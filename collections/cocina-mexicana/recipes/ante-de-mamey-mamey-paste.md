## Ingredients

- mamey
- clarified syrup
- clove
- cinnamon
- sesame

## Directions

1. After grinding the mamey well, mix it with syrup and let it take on the consistency of a paste
2. Add clove, cinnamon and sesame
3. Place it on layers of mamey soaked with syrup, and decorate the last paste layer with raisins, almonds and pine nuts, covering everything with cinnamon powder
