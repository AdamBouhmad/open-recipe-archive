## Ingredients

- veal loin
- salt
- ground pepper
- lard
- sherry wine
- water

## Directions

1. Take a veal loin and prepare it with salt and ground pepper; after two or three hours place it to brown in very hot lard
2. When already browned add a little sherry wine and place to cook over gentle heat with heat from above
3. When the wine has been consumed add a small cup of hot water and cover, leaving it with heat for another while
4. Afterwards let it cool to eat it
