## Ingredients

- Hard-boiled eggs
- Parsley (ground)
- Bread crumbs (soaked in vinegar)
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Grind a handful of parsley with a little bread crumbs soaked in vinegar
2. Add the necessary vinegar so it is neither too thick nor too watery
3. Add the corresponding salt, mitigating the acid with a little sugar
4. Bathe the cooked eggs, cut in half, with this sauce
