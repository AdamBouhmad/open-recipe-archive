## Ingredients

- jícama rallada (grated jícama)
- almendra molida (ground almond)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. To one pound of grated jícama add four ounces of ground almond and two pounds of sugar
2. The point is when it makes a thick wrinkle
