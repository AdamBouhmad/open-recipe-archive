## Ingredients

- Young rabbits
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Garlic
- Bay leaf
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Game essence
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Divide rabbits into pieces
2. Cook over two fires in oil with salt, pepper, nutmeg, garlic and bay leaf
3. When cooked, remove some oil, garlic and bay leaf from the casserole
4. Add truffles, mushrooms, a bit of game essence and tomato sauce
5. Continue cooking
