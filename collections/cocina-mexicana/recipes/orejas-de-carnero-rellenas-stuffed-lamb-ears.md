## Ingredients

- Lamb ears
- Picadillo filling
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Egg
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the ears as in the previous recipe
2. Drain well
3. Cut their upper extremities in a flower shape
4. Stuff with any picadillo
5. Soak in butter
6. Cover with bread crumbs, then cover again with more bread crumbs and egg
7. Fry in very hot butter
8. Serve with fried parsley
