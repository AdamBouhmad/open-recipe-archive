## Ingredients

- pollos
- agua
- vinagre
- sal
- ajos
- xitomate picado
- chiles verdes enteros
- jamón en dados
- azúcar
- clavo
- canela
- pimienta en polvo
- mantequilla
- harina

## Directions

1. Divide cleaned chickens into quarters and place in a pot with water, a little vinegar, salt, garlic and chopped tomato, whole green chiles, diced ham, a lump of sugar, clove, cinnamon and ground pepper, and butter instead of lard
2. Seal the pot by pasting the seams and place over the fire
3. Let simmer until the chickens are cooked, being careful to remove the chiles before they burst
4. Thicken the broth with flour browned in butter
