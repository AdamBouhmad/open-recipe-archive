## Ingredients

- A little milk
- One cup of starch
- One yellow lemon peel, well washed and finely chopped
- Sugar to taste
- Spun eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Dissolve one cup of starch in a little milk and add one yellow lemon peel, well washed and finely chopped
2. Sweeten to taste
3. Heat to a moderate consistency, neither too loose nor too thick
4. Pour into a serving dish and garnish with spun eggs
