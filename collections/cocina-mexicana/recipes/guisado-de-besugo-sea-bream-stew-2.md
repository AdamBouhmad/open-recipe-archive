## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Finely chopped onion
- Toasted bread ground into fine powder
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Broth

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the sea bream well.
2. Divide it widthwise into four or five pieces, or leave it whole if preferred.
3. Fry finely chopped onion slowly in a saucepan.
4. Place the sea bream, whole or in pieces, in the pan.
5. When browned on both sides, remove the fish and onion.
6. Add sufficient toasted and finely ground bread powder to form a paste.
7. Season with salt and pepper, or instead of pepper, use finely chopped and crushed parsley.
8. Dissolve this fried mixture with a little broth.
9. Pour this mixture over the sea bream.
