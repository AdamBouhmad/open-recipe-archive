## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- broth
- sugar
- cinnamon
- almonds

## Directions

1. Take fresh eggs and make a small hole to remove contents
2. Make a paste with yolks, broth from the pot, sugar, cinnamon and chopped almonds
3. Fill the eggshells with this paste using a small funnel
4. Seal the hole with a small piece of shell
5. Cook and serve
