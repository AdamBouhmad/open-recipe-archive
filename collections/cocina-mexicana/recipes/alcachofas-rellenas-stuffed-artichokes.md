## Ingredients

- artichokes
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- meat
- butter
- egg yolks
- hazelnut sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from artichokes
2. Boil briefly in salted water
3. Drain well on a sieve
4. Heat butter in a cazuela
5. Stuff the artichokes with a mixture of raw garlic, parsley, oil and breadcrumbs, or with finely chopped meat
6. Place in the cazuela over low heat
7. Cover with an iron lid with hot coals until toasted
8. If desired with sauce, pass through a beaten egg yolk mixture and add a hazelnut sauce
