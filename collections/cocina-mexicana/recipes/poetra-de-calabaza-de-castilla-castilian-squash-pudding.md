## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 coconut
- 1 pound squash (calabacita)
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar.
2. Grind one coconut.
3. Boil the squash, weigh one pound of it and grind it.
4. Once the syrup is thick, add the coconut to boil.
5. Then add the squash, stirring constantly until it separates from the pan.
6. Turn out onto a platter and sprinkle cinnamon powder on top.
