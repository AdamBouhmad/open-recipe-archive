## Ingredients

- Axolotls
- Salt
- Epazote
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Poblano chiles or tornachiles
- Green chiles (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the axolotls in salted water with epazote
2. Fry with minced onion and cooked ground tomatoes
3. When well fried, add broth from the axolotl cooking water
4. Incorporate deshredded poblano or tornachiles
5. May vary by grinding cooked green chiles with the tomatoes and omitting the deshredded chiles
