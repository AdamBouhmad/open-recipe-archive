## Ingredients

- onions
- melted lard
- one pound of lard per half azumbre of blood
- finely chopped herbs
- salt
- spices
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut onions into pieces and pass through melted lard without browning.
2. Mix with one pound of lard per half azumbre of blood, combining everything and adding finely chopped herbs, salt, spices, and cream.
3. With this mixture fill the intestines, having previously cleaned them well through a funnel.
4. Tie at one end and fill before tying, taking care not to make the tie too tight.
5. Cook in warm water until no blood comes out when pricked with a pin.
6. Then remove and let drain and dry, cutting pieces of varying lengths.
7. Note that the water should not boil as they would burst.
8. Place on grills or on a spit.
