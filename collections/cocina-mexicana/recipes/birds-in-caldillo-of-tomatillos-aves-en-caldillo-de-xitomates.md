## Ingredients

- tomatillos
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- garlic
- lard
- vinegar
- fine spices
- cooked onion
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind tomatillos and tomatoes with some green chiles, and adding a little crushed garlic, fry everything together in lard where vinegar will be added with fine spices, cooked and chopped onion, defoliated parsley and the necessary salt
