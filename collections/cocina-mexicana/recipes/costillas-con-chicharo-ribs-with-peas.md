## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- butter
- oil
- garlic
- onion
- cooked peas
- water
- bread crumbs
- aged cheese
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry the ribs in butter and oil. Once fried, add a regular amount of very finely minced garlic and very finely chopped onion; let it fry and add cooked peas to fry as well.
2. Once they are done, add sufficient water for them to cook. Remove and let the sauce thicken.
3. Separately coat a pan with butter and margarine, spread on top a layer of bread crumbs with aged cheese and sprinkle with oil; then place the sauce on top of this in which the ribs are, and on top of the sauce the ribs, which are covered with another layer of bread crumbs and cheese, and a little more oil.
4. Place over two fires, let them dry and serve.
