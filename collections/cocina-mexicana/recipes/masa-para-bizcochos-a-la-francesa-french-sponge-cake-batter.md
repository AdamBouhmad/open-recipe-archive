## Ingredients

- 12 eggs, separated
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- a little orange blossom water
- 1 pound flour
- butter for greasing molds

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks well with the powdered sugar and orange blossom water
2. Beat the egg whites until stiff and foamy like snow
3. Mix the whites with the yolks
4. Fold in the flour, stirring well
5. Pour into buttered molds, small boxes, or spread with a spoon on paper sheets
6. Bake in a gentle oven
