## Ingredients

- bobo
- limón
- sal
- cebolla
- jitomate
- perejil
- aceite
- clavo
- pimienta
- azafrán
- aceitunas
- alcaparras
- vinagre

## Directions

1. After scaling and cleaning, rub the whole bobo with lemon and salt to remove slime.
2. Chop plenty of onion, roasted and ground tomato and parsley; fry with plenty of oil.
3. Once fried, add the bobo to the broth, adding ground clove, pepper and saffron, olives, capers and a little vinegar.
4. Cook over two gentle fires.
