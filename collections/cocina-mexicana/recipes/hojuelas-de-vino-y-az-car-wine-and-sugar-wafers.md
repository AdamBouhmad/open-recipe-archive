## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- One pound of ground white sugar
- One quarter cup of white wine
- Seven egg yolks and four egg whites
- One piece of yeast the size of an egg yolk
- One pound of melted butter
- Water as needed for the dough

## Directions

1. Mix well two pounds of flour, one pound of ground white sugar, one quarter cup of white wine, seven egg yolks and four egg whites, one piece of yeast the size of an egg yolk, and one pound of melted butter, adding water as needed so the dough is not too hard
2. After mixing well, cook it in a comal
3. Once cooked, set it aside
4. Knead it again
5. Extend the hojuelas
6. Cut them with a pastry wheel
7. Fry them
8. Serve with or without sweetness
