## Ingredients

- 4 cuartillos milk
- 2 pounds sugar
- 4 ounces almonds
- 2 ounces rice
- 0.5 ounce pineapple
- cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Add sugar to the milk, then strain through cloth and boil with a cinnamon stick.
2. Wash the rice, grind it well, pour the milk into a pan and mix with the strained rice.
3. When it reaches half point, add the almonds not too finely ground.
4. When it reaches the proper point, add the pineapple to prevent the milk from curdling.
