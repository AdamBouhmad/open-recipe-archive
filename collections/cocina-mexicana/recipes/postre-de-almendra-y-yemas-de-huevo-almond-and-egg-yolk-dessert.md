## Ingredients

- sugar
- almonds, finely ground
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with one pound of sugar
2. Remove from heat and mix with another pound of finely ground almonds until well combined
3. Add sixteen beaten egg yolks
4. Return the pan to the heat and cook until the dessert begins to pull away from the sides of the pan
