## Ingredients

- six quartillos of milk
- six egg yolks
- lemon
- nine egg yolks
- white wine

## Directions

1. Mix six egg yolks with six quartillos of milk
2. Sweeten it and place on the fire
3. When it is boiling, squeeze a lemon into it
4. As soon as the milk curdles, remove from heat
5. Pour into a cloth that is tied, letting all the whey strain out
6. Reserve the requesón in the same cloth until the next day
7. Untie the cloth and slice the requesón
8. Pour nine strained egg yolks into the whey
9. Place on the fire and let it thicken
10. Arrange the slices of requesón on a plate
11. Pour the thickened whey on top
12. Add a little white wine
