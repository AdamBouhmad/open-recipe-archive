## Ingredients

- Chorizo
- String beans
- Salsify
- Carrots
- Fresh green beans
- Peas
- Chayotes
- Potatoes
- Zucchini
- Butter
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook string beans, salsify and chopped carrots with chorizo in salted water
2. Add tender fresh green beans and peas
3. When cooked, drain everything and when dry, add crumbled chorizo
4. Add chayotes and cooked and peeled potatoes cut into small pieces
5. Add zucchini that has been cooked with butter, onion, tomatoes, garlic and chopped parsley, which should be one third of the other ingredients and dry
