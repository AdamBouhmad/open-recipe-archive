## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- relleno fino (fine stuffing)
- tajadas de vaca (beef slices)
- lonjas de jamón muy delgadas (very thin ham slices)
- hojas de papel (paper sheets)
- caldo de sustancia de vaca ó de jamón (beef or ham broth)

## Directions

1. Prepare the squabs as in the previous article
2. Cover the belly with fine stuffing
3. Wrap them in beef slices, in very thin ham slices and in paper sheets, and when cooked, serve with loose broth of beef or ham substance
