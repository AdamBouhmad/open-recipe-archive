## Ingredients

- lamb meat, cleaned and cut into cubes
- sea salt
- egg
- water
- pepper
- Castilian vinegar
- broad chiles, deveined and washed
- garlic, cleaned
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the well-washed lamb pulp into four-finger sized cubes
2. Dissolve in a barrel or vessel with sufficient water and sea salt quantity so that an egg floats perfectly
3. Place the meat in this brine and keep it for three days
4. After three days, remove it, drain it slightly, and roll it in finely ground pepper so it is perfectly covered
5. Sprinkle with a little Castilian vinegar and put in the sun until well dried
6. Grind broad chiles deveined and well washed, with cleaned garlic and vinegar, forming a thick broth
7. Add the dried meat with the pepper and keep it there
8. Place on the grill with a comal on top with stronger fire, alternately brushing with green oil with garlic and with lemon juice
9. When it has formed a crust, serve with any of the sauces for roasted meats
