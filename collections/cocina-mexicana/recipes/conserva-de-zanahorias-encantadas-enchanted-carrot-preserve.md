## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Honey (3 cuartillos for every 3 pounds of carrots)
- Peeled and toasted almonds
- Walnuts
- Pine nuts
- Orange flower water
- Cinnamon
- Ginger
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots with a knife and cut them to the desired size, removing very well all the core without breaking or opening the tubes
2. Weigh them and keep them boiling in water for one hour
3. Remove from the water little by little with a skimmer, place in a small basket and cover with a cloth so they cool
4. Boil three cuartillos of honey for every three pounds of carrots
5. Once the honey is skimmed, strained and cold, pour it over the carrots that have already been strained in a pot, placing it over a moderate fire of wood and not charcoal
6. As soon as the carrots are well soaked and the honey reaches the proper point, remove the pot and take out some of the honey, which should be given another boil separately in a small pot
7. Add peeled and toasted almonds cut into small pieces and other pieces of walnuts and pine nuts, all toasted in advance
8. Give another boil immediately, taking care that the honey does not burn and that everything is well mixed and soft like an alfajor
9. Sprinkle this with orange flower water and dust with cinnamon, ginger, and cloves, all ground
