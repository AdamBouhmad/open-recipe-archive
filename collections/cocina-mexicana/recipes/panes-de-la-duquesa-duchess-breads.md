## Ingredients

- masa real (royal dough)
- confituras (preserves)

## Directions

1. Form the mixture from royal dough, ensuring it is not too soft
2. Give it a long shape of six to eight inches
3. Place in the oven
4. When cooked, open and place a spoonful of preserves inside
