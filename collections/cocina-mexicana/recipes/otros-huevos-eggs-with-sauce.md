## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- flour
- egg yolks
- water
- pepper
- parsley
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. Fry onion in large pieces and tomato without seeds or skin; when the base is cooked add a pinch of flour, fry until it turns white.
2. Grind some egg yolks and incorporate them into the fried base just until they are partly cooked; add a little water, a pinch of pepper, a handful of very finely chopped parsley, salt to taste and once seasoned and of regular thickness, add hard-boiled eggs split in half and cooking oil with the eggs; it should boil little.
3. In this same broth, meatballs of chicken breast or pork meat can be made.
