## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Sugar
- Almonds

## Directions

1. After peeling the peaches, cut them into thin slices
2. Cook in a pan with twelve ounces of sugar for each pound of fruit
3. When the preserve is near its setting point, add ground almonds mixed in
