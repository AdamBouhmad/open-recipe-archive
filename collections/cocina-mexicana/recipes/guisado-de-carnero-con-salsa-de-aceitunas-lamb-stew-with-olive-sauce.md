## Ingredients

- Lamb cuts
- Ham strips
- Lard
- Salt
- Ground garlic
- Vinegar
- Water
- Fried bread toasts
- Olives
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb cuts with ham strips.
2. Fry in lard with salt and ground garlic.
3. When fried, add sufficient vinegar and water to allow cooking.
4. When cooked, pit and grind the olives with fried bread toasts, using enough olives to form the sauce.
5. Grind clove and pepper with the olives and toasts.
6. Fry all the ground mixture.
7. Add the lamb with its broth to this mixture and let it boil until the sauce thickens.
8. When removing from heat, add oil.
