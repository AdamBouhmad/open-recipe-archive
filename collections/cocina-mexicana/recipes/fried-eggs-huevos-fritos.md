## Ingredients

- oil
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat oil and when it is very hot, break the egg into it.
2. With a flat iron spoon, splash the oil over the top so the yolk is covered.
3. Season with ground salt and serve.
4. Frying one at a time produces better results.
