## Ingredients

- Roasted young rabbit meat
- Mushrooms
- Spanish sauce
- Game essence (humillo de caza)
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Carefully preserve the meat from roasted young rabbits and cut into small thin pieces
2. Cut an equal volume of mushrooms equal to half the volume of meat
3. Place everything in a pan
4. Add Spanish sauce that has been reduced with game essence
5. Mix with a little butter
6. Fill the shells with this mixture
7. Sprinkle with grated bread
8. Brush melted butter with a feather or piece of paper
9. Place on a grill and brown under a bell oven
