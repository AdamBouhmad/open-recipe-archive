## Ingredients

- one pound sweet almonds
- ten egg whites
- a handful of bitter almonds
- grated zest of ten lemons
- twenty-four egg yolks
- twelve egg whites
- one pound sugar powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of peeled sweet almonds with ten egg whites so they do not become oily
2. Add a handful of bitter almonds
3. Mix with grated zest of ten lemons
4. Beat twenty-four egg yolks and twelve egg whites as for turrón
5. Mix with the ground almonds and one pound sugar powder
6. Pour into paper boxes lined with butter
7. Bake in the oven for a quarter hour
