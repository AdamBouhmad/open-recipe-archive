## Ingredients

- brains
- lard
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- flour
- tomato
- vinegar
- fine spices
- ground dried tomato skin
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook the brains, chop them very finely and fry them in lard.
2. But first fry onion, garlic and parsley, all finely chopped and then add the brains.
3. Separately, brown some flour, onion, garlic and chopped tomato, parsley and a little vinegar, fine ground spices and a little ground dried tomato skin.
4. Slice bread thinly, cover the slices with the brains and fry them.
5. When ready to serve, place the sliced bread on the platter and cover with the fried mixture.
