## Ingredients

- rabbit
- lard
- garlic cloves, ground
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- water
- bread

## Directions

1. Boil the rabbit so no hair remains; once clean, portion into rations and fry in lard
2. Remove and in the same lard fry ground garlic cloves, pepper, clove and cinnamon also ground
3. Return to the lard with a little water and to thicken fry some pieces of bread, grind them and add to the stew
