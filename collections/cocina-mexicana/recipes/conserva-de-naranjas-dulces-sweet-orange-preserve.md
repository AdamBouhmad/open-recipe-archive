## Ingredients

- Large Chinese oranges
- Water
- Sugar syrup (clarified, at low point)

## Directions

1. Select large Chinese oranges and peel the colored surface of the skin with the oil cells, leaving the white fleshy part
2. Make an incision at the crown and pierce the segments with a thin knife, leaving the juice without expressing it
3. Cook in water and when done, remove and cool
4. Gently express without damaging them and place in fresh water
5. Change the water twice daily until all bitterness is lost
6. Place in clarified sugar syrup at low point and gradually raise to preserve point over low heat
7. Repeat this operation three consecutive days
8. On the third day, add a little water to the syrup and boil with the oranges over regular heat until it finally reaches the high preserve point
9. Pour into jars, cover with a small pot of appropriate size, seal well with paste and paper to prevent air from entering
