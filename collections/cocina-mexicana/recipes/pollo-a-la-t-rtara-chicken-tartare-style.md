## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Tartare sauce or pepper sauce, or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the neck and legs from the chicken, open it along the belly, flatten it with a cleaver and first blanch it, then cook it in a saucepan with butter, chopped parsley and onions, salt and pepper.
2. A quarter hour before serving, roll it in breadcrumbs and put it on the grill over two fires.
3. Turn it so it browns on both sides, and serve with tartare sauce (see) or pepper sauce, or simply with lemon juice.
