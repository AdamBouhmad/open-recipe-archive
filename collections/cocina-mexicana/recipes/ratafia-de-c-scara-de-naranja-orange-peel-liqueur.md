## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of refined aguardiente
- six ounces of orange peel cuticles (the yellow crust of the orange separated from the white pulp of the peel)
- three ounces of white sugar for every two quartillos of liquor

## Directions

1. Put in infusion in eight quartillos of refined aguardiente six ounces of orange peel cuticles (the yellow crust of the orange separated from the white pulp of the peel)
2. The infusion should not last more than six to eight hours
3. After this time strain the liquor through cloth and add three ounces of white sugar for every two quartillos of liquor
4. The longer the infusion lasts, the spirit of wine acquires a more bitter and disagreeable taste
5. Sweeten to taste with well-clarified common syrup and place to filter through the sleeve to bottle
