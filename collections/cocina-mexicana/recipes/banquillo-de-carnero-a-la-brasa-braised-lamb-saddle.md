## Ingredients

- half a saddle of lamb
- lard or suet slices
- three parsnips
- four onions
- two cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley with small onions
- trimmings from the saddle
- good broth
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Cut the half saddle, which is the part of the lamb between the first rib and the leg
2. Bone it and season inside with salt and pepper
3. Roll it to form an oblong shape and tie it well
4. Place in a pan on slices of lard or suet
5. Add three parsnips, four onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme, a small bunch of parsley with small onions, and the trimmings
6. Pour over a ladle of good broth
7. Cover with a wheel of buttered paper
8. Cook over two fires for three hours or longer
9. When ready to serve, drain and untie, remove the skin and cover with gelatin
