## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- olive oil
- fine herbs
- ham
- breadcrumbs
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Fry the veal ribs as in the previous recipe, remove them moist from the oil.
2. Cover with a minced mixture made of fine herbs and ham, mixed with breadcrumbs.
3. Sprinkle with the oil in which they were fried.
4. Wrap in oiled paper and cook on the grill over a gentle fire.
