## Ingredients

- higaditos de gallinas
- ajos
- bizcochos tostados
- manteca
- agua
- clavo
- canela
- pimienta molida
- vinagre fuerte
- vino
- avellanas
- pasas
- almendras
- cebollas cocidas
- tostadas de pan

## Directions

1. Grind the hen livers with garlic and toasted biscuits in lard
2. Dissolve everything in water, fry and add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper, one quarter of strong vinegar and another of wine
3. Grind some hazelnuts and add to the broth with cleaned raisins and almonds
4. When well seasoned, add cooked onions prepared separately and the cooked hens
5. To serve, garnish with slices of bread fried in lard
