## Ingredients

- Hare
- ham strips
- hare bones
- two beef shanks
- good slice of beef
- butter

## Directions

1. Completely debone a hare and lard it with strips of well-seasoned ham.
2. Make an aspic with its bones, two good beef shanks and a good slice of beef, all well seasoned.
3. Render the hare in a pound of butter, and make with both things the stuffing of a cold pie.
