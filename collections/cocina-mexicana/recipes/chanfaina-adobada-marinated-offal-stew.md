## Ingredients

- asadura
- agua
- sal
- jamón
- chorizos
- longaniza
- chiles anchos
- ajo
- pimienta
- clavo
- cominos
- pan remojado en vinagre
- manteca
- vinagre
- caldo
- aceite
- chilitos
- aceitunas
- tornachiles curados

## Directions

1. Cook the offal as stated in the chanfaina con jamón article, with the same ingredients, chopping after cooking
2. Devein and soak some wide chiles and grind them with garlic, pepper, clove, cumin and bread soaked in vinegar
3. Fry everything in lard and season with salt, then add the chopped offal, ham, chorizo and longaniza
4. When all is fried, add vinegar and the broth in which the offal was cooked, straining first, and let it season over two fires until the sauce is consumed
5. Serve hot, adding oil when removing from heat, and garnish with small chiles, olives and quarters of cured tornachiles
