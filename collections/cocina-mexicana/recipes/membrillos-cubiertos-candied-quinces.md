## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Clarified syrup, one quarter measure per pound of quince
- Water in which the quinces were cooked

## Directions

1. Divide quinces into quarters
2. Remove the heart and peel
3. Parboil in boiling water for a few moments
4. Remove and let cool
5. Place in a pan with one quarter measure of clarified syrup for each pound of quince
6. Add part of the water in which they were cooked
7. Allow to boil a little on two different days, until well penetrated with the syrup
8. The syrup should reach the proper covering point after this time
9. Leave the quince quarters in it for two more days
10. After that, remove and dry on the drying rack
