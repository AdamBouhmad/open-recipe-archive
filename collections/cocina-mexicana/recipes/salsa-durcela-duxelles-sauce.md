## Ingredients

- Some mushrooms, washed and chopped
- Shallots
- Parsley
- Earthnut truffles if available
- 4 ounces of butter
- 4 ounces of scraped lard
- 1 bottle of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- A little garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- Nutmeg
- Spices
- 4 tablespoons of thick German sauce

## Directions

1. Rub some washed and chopped mushrooms with a cloth
2. Wash and rub shallots, parsley, and earthnut truffles if available
3. Place 4 ounces of butter and 4 ounces of scraped lard in a pot over heat
4. Add the rubbed and chopped things and fry with 1 bottle of white wine
5. Add salt, pepper, a little garlic, a bay leaf, nutmeg, and spices
6. Stir the sauce until it reduces to a gelatin
7. Add 4 tablespoons of thick German sauce
8. Transfer to a serving dish to be served with small papered pieces
