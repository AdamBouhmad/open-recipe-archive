## Ingredients

- lard
- onions
- garlic
- parsley
- spearmint
- ground tomatoes
- all spices
- lettuce
- chicken broth
- beet slices
- finely chopped carrots
- oil
- vinegar
- artichokes
- cauliflower
- pepper
- small chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. In a pot with lard, fry onions, garlic, parsley and spearmint
2. Add ground tomatoes, all spices and lettuce cut as for salad
3. Add the chickens with their broth, beet slices, finely chopped carrots, oil and vinegar
4. Cook until done
5. Garnish the plates or platters with cooked artichokes and cauliflower sprinkled with pepper, small chiles, olives and capers
