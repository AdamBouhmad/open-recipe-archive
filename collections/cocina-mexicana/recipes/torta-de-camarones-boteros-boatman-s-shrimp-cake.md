## Ingredients

- Garlic, chopped
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Lard
- Clove, ground
- Saffron, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Capers
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Salt
- Vinegar
- White sugar
- Parsley, leaves only
- Shrimp, cleaned and cooked
- Eggs, beaten
- Beet halves, cooked
- Tomatoes, sliced
- Onions, sliced
- Chile sauce

## Directions

1. Chop garlic and tomatoes, fry in lard, and add ground clove, saffron, cinnamon and pepper
2. When this sauce is seasoned, add capers, raisins, almonds, salt, vinegar, a lump of sugar, leafed parsley and clean cooked shrimp
3. When it reaches the right point, remove from fire and mix the paste with beaten eggs
4. Put everything in a buttered dish
5. On top place halves of cooked beets, and then sliced tomatoes and onions
6. Cover everything with the rest of the eggs and paste
7. Leave to cook at two fires
8. Can be served with chile sauce
