## Ingredients

- tomatoes (xitomates)
- onion, cooked
- avocado, peeled and sliced
- ground pepper
- tornachiles, sliced
- olives
- pepper powder
- oregano
- oil
- vinegar
- fish, cooked in slices

## Directions

1. Roast tomatoes and mash by hand
2. Mix with cooked onion, slices of peeled avocado, and ground pepper
3. Stir all together and pour into serving dish
4. Place portions of cooked sliced fish on top
5. Cover with sliced tornachiles, olives, pepper powder and oregano
6. Add oil and vinegar
7. Serve cold
