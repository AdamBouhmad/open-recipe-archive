## Ingredients

- Small sausages
- Ham essence
- Grated cheese

## Directions

1. Blanch small sausages in boiling water and drain the fat they release
2. Cook slowly in ham essence over low heat
3. In the serving dish, reduce the ham essence to form a small crust
4. Arrange the sausages on top, sprinkle with grated cheese and moisten with the sauce
5. Brown in the oven and serve dry
