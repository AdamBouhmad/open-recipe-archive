## Ingredients

- watercress
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- chiles
- onion
- lettuce hearts
- cooked beets

## Directions

1. Clean the watercress as described in the previous article
2. Chop it raw and mix with cloves, cinnamon and pepper, ground dry
3. Dress with oil and vinegar seasoned with salt
4. Mix with finely chopped chiles
5. Adorn on top with sliced onion, lettuce hearts, and slices of cooked beets
