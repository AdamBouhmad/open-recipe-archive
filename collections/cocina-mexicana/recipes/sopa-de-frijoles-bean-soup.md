## Ingredients

- Cold French bread, thinly sliced
- Onion
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Spices
- Lard and oil, equal parts
- Black beans
- Cooked eggs, sliced

## Directions

1. Slice the cold French bread very thin and fry it with onion, tomato, and garlic, adding some spices, using equal parts lard and oil.
2. Place the black beans to cook. Begin placing the bread in the baking dish, pouring the bean broth in place of pot broth at intervals, leaving a little space on top.
3. On the space left at the top, place slices of hard-boiled egg slightly covered so they do not dry out.
4. Place the baking dish on low heat with another heat source above it.
