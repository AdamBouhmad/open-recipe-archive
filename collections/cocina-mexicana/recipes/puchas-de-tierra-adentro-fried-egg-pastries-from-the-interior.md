## Ingredients

- thirty eggs
- a little aniseed
- salt
- lard, well heated
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat thirty eggs for frying, first the whites and then the yolks, adding a little aniseed and ground salt.
2. One person adds the well-heated lard while another stirs with their hand, gradually adding flour as needed.
3. Continue stirring so the dough does not become too firm.
4. Once prepared, cook in plain water.
5. Remove and once they are well dried, place in the oven, not too hot.
