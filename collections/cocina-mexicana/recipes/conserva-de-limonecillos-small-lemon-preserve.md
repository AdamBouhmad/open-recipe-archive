## Ingredients

- Green, fresh small lemons
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)
- Coconut paste or pine nut or almond paste for filling (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the small lemons partway through on one side
2. Place in a pot with water and give a single boil
3. Remove that water, squeeze slightly to release the seeds, rinse and return to water over heat for another boil
4. Remove and place in cold water, then carefully hollow them out and leave them in water
5. Change the water over heat to remove bitterness, maintaining and increasing their greenness
6. Once blanched and with a pot of fine syrup prepared, place them in it, cover and bring to a boil
7. Once well boiled, remove without uncovering, let cool, drain the honey and keep covered
8. Bring the syrup to proper point with finishing water and pour back over them
9. Cover them to prevent air from darkening them and lowering their color
10. When finally placing in the honey, it must already be cold
11. If they are to be filled with paste, coconut is most suitable; if lacking, use pine nut or almond paste
