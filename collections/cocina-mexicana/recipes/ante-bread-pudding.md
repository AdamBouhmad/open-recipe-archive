## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- pastille
- thin slices of sponge cake and cold bread
- comal

## Directions

1. Cook a good amount of milk and place it with sugar
2. Once it has boiled well, allow it to cool
3. Add plenty of egg yolks as needed
4. Have ground cinnamon, pastille, and sugar ready
5. Make thin slices of sponge cake and cold bread
6. Add cinnamon, sugar, and pastille to the bread
7. Pour the milk and yolk mixture very well over the top
8. It should be thick with egg so that it sets well
9. Place inside a pot with boiling water and put a comal on top
10. Once very well set without water dripping, remove from the pot
11. When it is time to put it on plates, have milk already cooked with sweetness ready and break eggs into it to place on top of the pudding
