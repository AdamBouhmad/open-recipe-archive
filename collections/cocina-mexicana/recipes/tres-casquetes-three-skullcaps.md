## Ingredients

- two ounces of flour
- water
- two ounces of sugar
- one egg
- lemon zest chopped very fine
- butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve two ounces of flour in a little water and add two ounces of sugar
2. Add one egg and a little finely chopped lemon zest
3. Mix everything together
4. Heat a little butter in a round pan and when hot, add the batter
5. Cook on two gentle heats
6. While this casquete cooks, lightly oil a concave round-shaped vessel
7. When the casquete is cooked, remove it from the pan with a cloth to avoid burning and place it in the oiled vessel to take its shape
8. Let it cool and remove from the vessel
9. Make two more casquetes of the same weight and composition
