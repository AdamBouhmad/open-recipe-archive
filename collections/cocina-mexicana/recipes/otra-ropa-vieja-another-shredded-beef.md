## Ingredients

- lard
- sliced garlic
- sliced onion
- tomato, finely chopped
- parsley, finely chopped
- spearmint
- all manner of spices
- a little saffron
- Castilian vinegar
- beef breasts

## Directions

1. Heat a skillet with lard very hot
2. Fry sliced garlic, sliced onion, finely chopped tomato, finely chopped parsley, spearmint, and all manner of spices with a little saffron and Castilian vinegar
3. Add beef breasts to this fry
4. When serving at table, add a little more vinegar
