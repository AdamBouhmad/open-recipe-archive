## Ingredients

- Cured herring
- Milk
- Melted butter
- Bay leaf and basil, finely chopped
- Two egg yolks
- Pepper and nutmeg
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Desalt the herring in water
2. Let them marinate for two hours in milk
3. Open them from the top and soak them well with melted butter mixed with finely chopped bay leaf and basil, two egg yolks, pepper and nutmeg
4. Place them on a gentle fire over the grill
5. Serve them with lemon juice
