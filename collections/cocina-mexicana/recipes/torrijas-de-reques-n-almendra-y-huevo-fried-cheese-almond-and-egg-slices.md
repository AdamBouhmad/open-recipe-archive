## Ingredients

- four ounces ground almonds
- half pound beaten ricotta cheese
- sifted sugar
- clove powder
- seven beaten egg yolks
- ground sponge cake as needed
- lard
- syrup
- almond slivers

## Directions

1. Mix together ground almonds, beaten ricotta, sifted sugar, clove powder, beaten egg yolks, and ground sponge cake to achieve proper consistency for forming torrijas
2. Fry in lard
3. Boil in syrup
4. Decorate with almond slivers to serve
