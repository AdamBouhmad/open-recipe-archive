## Ingredients

- eleven eggs
- capers
- oil
- lard
- onions
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- thyme
- water

## Directions

1. Cook eleven eggs and grind the yolks together with some capers
2. Fry them in oil and lard
3. Add onions in sections
4. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper and a bit of thyme, adding only as much water as necessary to grind the spices
5. Let everything fry well together
6. Then place the pompano over it, add plenty of oil and let it simmer a little
7. Decorate the platter with chiles, capers and olives
