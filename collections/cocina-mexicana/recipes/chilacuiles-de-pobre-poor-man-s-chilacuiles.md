## Ingredients

- bread
- lard
- ancho chile
- roasted tomatoes

## Directions

1. Brown bread in lard
2. Grind together with ancho chile and roasted, fried tomatoes
3. Once the sauce is mixed, add broth
4. Season and add cumin and avocados
