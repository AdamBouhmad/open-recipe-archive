## Ingredients

- half pound of flour
- three egg yolks
- four ounces of melted butter
- one and a half ounces of ground sugar
- minced meat

## Directions

1. Grind together three egg yolks with half pound of flour, four ounces of melted butter, and one and a half ounces of ground sugar
2. After everything is well incorporated, let the dough rest for a while
3. With half of it, form a small tortilla to cover the bottom of a pan and cook over two fires
4. Once cooked, place minced meat on top
5. Form another tortilla with the rest of the dough to cover the meat
6. Return to two fires until it browns, dusting sugar on top
