## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- beet
- green chile
- pear
- apple
- sweet potato
- peach
- peas
- fresh beans
- salt
- fine ground spices
- broth or water
- vinegar
- sugar or syrup

## Directions

1. Cut raw chickens into pieces and fry in a casserole with butter.
2. Make alternating layers of chicken and sliced onion, garlic, beet, green chile, pear, apple, sweet potato, and peach, with peas and fresh beans.
3. When all the chicken is arranged, add equal parts broth or water and vinegar.
4. Season with corresponding salt and fine ground spices.
5. Cook over two gentle fires until tender.
6. If the broth becomes too acidic, add a little sugar or syrup.
