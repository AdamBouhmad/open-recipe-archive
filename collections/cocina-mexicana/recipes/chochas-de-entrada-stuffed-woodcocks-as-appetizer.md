## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- ham, scraped, or a piece of butter with chopped parsley and onion
- salt

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcocks, singe them at the flame and cut them open at the top to empty them
2. Chop everything that is removed from the inside, excepting the gizzard which is discarded
3. Mix the chopped interior with scraped ham or with a piece of butter with chopped parsley and onion and a bit of salt
4. Stuff the woodcocks with this mixture and sew up the opening
5. Tuck in the legs and place on the spit, covered with slices of ham and wrapped in paper
6. When cooked, serve with sauce or light broth as one would with partridges
