## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef marrow bones
- pine nuts
- almonds
- hazelnuts
- four dozen eggs
- one quarter arroba of flour
- two pounds of lard
- ten egg yolks
- twelve ounces of ground sugar
- water
- eight pounds of sugar
- eight cuartillos of milk
- four dozen egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grind two pounds of beef marrow bones with sufficient pine nuts, almonds and hazelnuts, making a paste, adding four dozen eggs
2. Mix afterwards with one quarter arroba of flour, two pounds of lard, ten more egg yolks and twelve ounces of ground sugar and knead everything with a little water
3. Form the pie with this mass, which will be filled with a paste of egg threads made in this way: put eight pounds of sugar in eight cuartillos of milk, and the yolks of four dozen eggs
4. After everything is well dissolved, pass it through a threader or skimmer, making it fall into boiling well clarified syrup
5. Cover the pie with the dough said before, dust on top with panela, sugar and cinnamon, all ground and bake in the oven
