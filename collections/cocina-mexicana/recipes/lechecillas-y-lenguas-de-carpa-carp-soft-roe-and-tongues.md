## Ingredients

- carp soft roe and tongues
- salt
- vinegar
- flour
- oil for frying
- parsley for frying

## Directions

1. Take only the tongues and what is contained inside the carp, excluding the roe and intestines (tripe)
2. Soak them until no blood remains
3. Boil for a few moments in salt water and vinegar
4. Let cool and drain
5. Fry dusted with flour and serve with fried parsley
6. They can also be cooked on the grill in small boxes or as fricassee, after brushing with beaten egg
