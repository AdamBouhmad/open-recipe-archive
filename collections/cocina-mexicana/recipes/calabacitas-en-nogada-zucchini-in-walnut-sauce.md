## Ingredients

- fresh squash
- fresh walnuts or almonds if the nogada is to be false
- garlic
- black pepper
- bread crumb soaked in vinegar
- one or two fresh cheeses
- salt

## Directions

1. Well-peeled fresh walnuts or almonds if the nogada is to be false, soak for a long time
2. Grind them well in a very clean metate, and once the mass is kneaded, grind garlic and black pepper in regular quantity so that the nogada is somewhat loaded with it, bread crumb soaked in vinegar and one or two fresh cheeses according to the amount of nogada to be made
3. With everything ground separately, combine them by moistening with vinegar, and when brought to the table it will be seasoned with the necessary fine salt
4. With the squash whole and cooked in a pinch of salt, bathe them with the nogada
