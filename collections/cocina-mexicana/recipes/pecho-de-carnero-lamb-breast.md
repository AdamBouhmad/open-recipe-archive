## Ingredients

- Lamb breast
- Bacon slices
- Three onions
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Salt
- One cup of broth
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper
- Spicy sauce
- Escarole, celery or other vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot with bacon slices
2. Place the meat on top and cover with more bacon slices
3. Add three onions in chunks, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, salt, and one cup of broth
4. Cook over low heat for three hours with heat above and below
5. For first serving method: brown with melted butter, coat with bread crumbs, sprinkle with pepper and salt, place on grill and serve with spicy sauce
6. For second serving method: remove the sauce and serve garnished with escarole, celery or other vegetables
