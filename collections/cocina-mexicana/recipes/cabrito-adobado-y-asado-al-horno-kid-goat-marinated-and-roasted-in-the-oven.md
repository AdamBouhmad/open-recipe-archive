## Ingredients

- kid goat quarter
- wide chiles, deveined
- garlic
- cumin
- salt
- thyme
- oregano
- ginger
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade with deveined, soaked and ground wide chiles, garlic, cumin, salt, thyme, oregano and ginger if desired
2. Marinate the kid goat for one day and night
3. Remove from marinade and place in the oven with sufficient lard until well browned without burning
4. When ready to serve, pour all the marinade over it
