## Ingredients

- raw chicken breasts, minced
- salt
- onion, minced
- tomatoes, minced

## Directions

1. Mince raw chicken breasts
2. Season with salt
3. Mix with finely minced onion and tomatoes
4. Form meatballs
5. Fry in lard
