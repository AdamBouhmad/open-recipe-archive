## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- water
- salt
- orange flower water
- milk
- a little ground almond
- sugar
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- sponge cake (mamón)

## Directions

1. Cook chicken breasts with water and salt, and leave steeping in orange flower water overnight
2. The next day, remove and chop so finely that they seem ground, and put in proportional quantity of milk with a little ground almond and sufficient sugar
3. Put everything over heat until it thickens, then remove, and when cool, place over layers of sponge cake, the last being custard beaten with egg yolks
4. Make it set over two fires and finish with ground cinnamon sprinkled on top
