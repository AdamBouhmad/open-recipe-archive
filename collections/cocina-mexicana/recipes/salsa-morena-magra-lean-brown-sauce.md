## Ingredients

- butter
- 5 large carrots, sliced lengthwise
- 6 large onions, sliced
- 3 parsley roots
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 sprig of thyme
- 3 cloves
- 2 medium carp, divided into pieces
- 2 medium pike, in pieces
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 bottle white wine
- a little lean broth
- lean broth or white wine
- 1 pound butter
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Place in the bottom of a pot with butter: 5 large carrots sliced lengthwise, 6 large onions sliced, 3 parsley roots, 2 bay leaves, 1 sprig of thyme, 3 cloves, 2 medium carp divided into pieces, 2 medium pike in pieces, salt and pepper
2. Moisten with 1/2 bottle of white wine and a little lean broth
3. Let boil and stick to the bottom of the pot until it takes on a dark reddish color
4. Moisten again with lean broth or in its absence with 1/2 bottle of white wine to deglaze
5. Remove from heat and mix in 1 pound of butter, sliding it in little by little and stirring always so it does not turn into oil
6. Add a little vinegar and pass through cloth over a water bath
