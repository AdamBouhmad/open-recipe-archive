## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- ten eggs
- anise
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. To one cup of flour, ten eggs and anise, dissolve it, add salt and cook until it pulls away from the pot
2. Add a quarter pound of butter, and once it is lukewarm knead it and let it rest
3. Then fry in plenty of butter
