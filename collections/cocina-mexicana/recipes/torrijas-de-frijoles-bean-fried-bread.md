## Ingredients

- half a cup of beans
- water with tequesquite
- three cloves
- five peppercorns
- one hard sponge cake
- five beaten eggs
- lard
- orange flower water
- syrup

## Directions

1. Cook half a cup of beans in water with tequesquite, wash well and grind
2. Add three cloves, five peppercorns and one hard sponge cake, all ground, and mix with five beaten eggs
3. Using a spoon, form the fried bread pieces from this paste
4. Fry in lard, making them somewhat elongated with the spoon
5. Remove and sprinkle with orange flower water
6. Place in syrup
