## Ingredients

- lamb offal, cut into large, thick and square pieces
- lamb liver, cut into strips
- butter, melted over gentle fire
- parsley, chopped
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry in melted butter over gentle fire the offal cut into large, thick and square pieces
2. Cut the liver into strips and fry it also in the same butter
3. Drain half of the butter from them
4. Add chopped parsley, mushrooms, salt, pepper and lemon juice
5. Serve
