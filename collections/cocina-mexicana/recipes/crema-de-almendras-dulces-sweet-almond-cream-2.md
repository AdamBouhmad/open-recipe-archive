## Ingredients

- Sweet almonds
- Bitter almond (optional)
- Water
- Milk
- Egg whites
- Powdered sugar
- Orange blossom water
- Candied almonds

## Directions

1. Blanch a handful of sweet almonds, and if desired, one bitter almond, by putting them in boiling water for a few minutes
2. Crush them with a little water
3. Beat two egg whites and four ounces of powdered sugar in one quarter liter of milk
4. Put the milk over gentle heat until it reduces to a quarter
5. Add the almonds and let it boil for a few minutes
6. Add a tablespoon of orange blossom water, and when cold decorate with candied almonds
