## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon sticks
- sugar
- refined aguardiente

## Directions

1. Boil the milk well with a few cinnamon sticks
2. Sweeten more than usual
3. Strain it
4. Return to the fire and remove as soon as it begins to boil
5. Quickly add the refined aguardiente, which would curdle if mixed while the milk is still on the flame
