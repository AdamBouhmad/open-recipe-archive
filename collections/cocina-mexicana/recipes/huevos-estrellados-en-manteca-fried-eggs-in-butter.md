## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter or oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cilantro (dried and ground)
- Wide dried chiles or pasilla chiles

## Directions

1. Put a pan or skillet on the fire and add butter or oil
2. When it has heated well, remove from heat and break the eggs into the butter one by one
3. Baste them with a clean small spatula
4. When they are set, remove them
5. Put the skillet back on the fire, and when the butter or oil is very hot, lower the heat
6. In this way fry all the eggs needed
7. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top, or salt and dried ground cilantro
8. May be used to garnish soups, or with the salt and seasoning that has been prepared
9. If there is no seasoning prepared, some wide or pasilla chiles can be toasted whole in the butter, which will be distributed on the plate in which the eggs are served
