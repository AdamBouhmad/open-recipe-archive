## Ingredients

- a little gum tragacanth powder
- two quartillos of custard
- orange-flower water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Add a little powdered gum tragacanth to two quartillos of custard, adding some orange-flower water and sufficient powdered sugar
2. Beat this mixture with peeled white poplar twigs
3. When the cream has risen, let it rest to arrange with a skimmer, forming it into a pyramid shape in the center of a porcelain plate
4. Decorate with candied lemon or orange pieces
