## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- vinegar, double the amount of fish
- sherry wine, half the amount of vinegar
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaf
- oregano
- cumin
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves according to the amount of fish
2. Remove the garlic and add double the amount of vinegar and half that amount of wine
3. Grind pepper, cloves, bay leaf, oregano and cumin
4. Add all this with the fish so it cooks
5. Decorate with chiles and olives
