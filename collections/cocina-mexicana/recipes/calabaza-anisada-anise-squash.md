## Ingredients

- squash
- water
- salt
- butter
- spices
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut squash into pieces and boil for a quarter hour with water and salt, then drain.
2. Place a good piece of butter in a casserole and fry the squash in it, seasoning with spices and salt.
3. Place on a plate, covering the pieces with grated cheese so they brown, placing them between two fires.
