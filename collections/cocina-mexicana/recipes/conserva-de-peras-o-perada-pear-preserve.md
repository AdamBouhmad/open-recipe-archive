## Ingredients

- Bergamot pears
- Sugar (24 pounds for each arroba of pear)

## Directions

1. Peel the raw bergamot pear, grate it dry and weigh it
2. Make clarified syrup at the alfeñique point, which is when a little is dropped in cold water and becomes very hard, using 24 pounds of sugar for each arroba of pear
3. Mix the pear with the syrup and place over high heat without ceasing to stir until it reaches the wrinkle point, which is when taking a little with a spoon it forms wrinkles on top
4. Remove from heat, let cool in something spread out of clay, and then pour into glass jars or small boxes
