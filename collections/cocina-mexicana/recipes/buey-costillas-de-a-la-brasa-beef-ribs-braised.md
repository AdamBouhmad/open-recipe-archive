## Ingredients

- Beef ribs
- Thick strips of ham seasoned with garlic, clove, cinnamon, ginger, salt and pepper
- Slices of ham
- Slices of beef
- Four parsnips
- Five large onions, one of which studded with three cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Thyme
- Sprigs of parsley
- Two ladle-fuls of broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the rib by larding it with thick strips of ham seasoned with garlic, clove, cinnamon, ginger, salt and pepper
2. Line the pot with slices of ham, beef, and more beef
3. Arrange four parsnips and five large onions (one studded with three cloves) on top
4. Add two bay leaves, thyme, and sprigs of parsley
5. Tie the rib with twine and arrange it in the pot as described
6. Cover with ham and place the vegetable garnishes on top
7. After adding two ladle-fuls of broth, bring to a boil
8. Cook gently over low heat for three hours or less if the meat is tender
9. When cooked, drain and untie the meat
10. Pass some of the cooking liquid through a silk sieve, remove fat, and let it reduce
11. Bathe the rib with the reduced cooking liquid poured underneath
