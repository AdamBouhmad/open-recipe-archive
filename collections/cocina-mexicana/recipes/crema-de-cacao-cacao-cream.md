## Ingredients

- One pound of Caracas or Soconusco cacao, roasted, hulled, well-selected, and well-ground
- Half an ounce of vanilla pieces
- Twenty-four quartillos of brandy
- Three and a half pounds of sugar
- Twelve quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Leave one pound of well-prepared cacao and half an ounce of vanilla pieces in infusion for eight days in twenty-four quartillos of brandy
2. Distill the mixture
3. Dissolve three and a half pounds of sugar in twelve quartillos of water by heating, then filter through a strainer or bag
4. Mix the distillate with the sweetened syrup
5. Decant and bottle
