## Ingredients

- syrup
- egg yolks
- white wine
- sponge cake (mamón)
- almonds
- pine nuts
- raisins
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a medium-point syrup, set aside some to sprinkle on the sponge cake, and mix egg yolks into the remainder
2. Put over heat until it reaches not-too-high point, then pour over sponge cake sprinkled with the reserved syrup and white wine, studding the last layer with almonds, pine nuts, and raisins, sprinkled on top with ground cinnamon
