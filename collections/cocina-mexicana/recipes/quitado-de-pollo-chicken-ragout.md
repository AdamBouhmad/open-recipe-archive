## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Salt
- Lard
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Vinegar
- Mixed spices

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken in salted water, cut into quarters
2. When half cooked, fry in plenty of lard
3. Add the broth in which it was cooked
4. Fry a little flour
5. Add egg yolks beaten with vinegar and all spices
