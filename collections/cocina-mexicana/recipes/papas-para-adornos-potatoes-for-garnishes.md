## Ingredients

- The smallest and roundest potatoes
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest and roundest potatoes, peel them and put them to cook in beef broth and they serve to garnish the platter as convenient, or to put them in the body of some bird in place of onions, or mixed with them
2. When wanting to put them under a roasted leg of mutton or any other kind of meat on a spit, begin by cooking them in water or better in a stew, and place them in the dripping pan so they take on taste and color
