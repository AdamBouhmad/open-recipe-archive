## Ingredients

- 8.5 cuartillos of milk
- half a cuarta of fine sugar
- one stick of cinnamon
- sufficient ground almonds
- one camote or mamey, shredded and passed through the metate and strainer

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar and cook it with a stick of cinnamon until well sweetened
2. When the milk reaches medium point, add sufficient ground almonds
3. After the almonds cook for a short time, add the camote or mamey that has been shredded and passed through the metate and strainer
4. Cook until it reaches the proper consistency
