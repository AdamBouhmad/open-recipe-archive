## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one pound of ground almond
- egg yolks, as many as needed

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with three pounds of sugar and when it reaches the soft-ball stage in water, remove from heat
2. Mix in one pound of ground almond and as many egg yolks as are necessary for the dough to become yellow
3. After everything is well incorporated, return to the heat so that with a few boils the egg becomes cooked
4. Remove again and beat until the dough becomes manageable
5. Empty into a dish, gather together and cover with a napkin so it does not dry out
6. Form the mostachones in the style of round chocolate tablets
7. Roll in ground cinnamon and imprint with a mold of the desired pattern
