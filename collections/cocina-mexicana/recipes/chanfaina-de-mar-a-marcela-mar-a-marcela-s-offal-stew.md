## Ingredients

- asadura (offal)
- hígados de carnero (lamb livers)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion)
- peregil (parsley)
- orégano (oregano)
- tomillo (thyme)
- ajo (garlic)
- carne de puerco cocida (cooked pork)
- jamón (ham)
- chorizos (sausages)
- alcaparras (capers)
- pan (bread)
- clavo (cloves)
- canela (cinnamon)
- pimienta (pepper)
- azúcar (sugar)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cook the offal and lamb livers well
2. Chop the livers with a machete blow and cut the offal into small pieces with a knife
3. Fry plenty of onion, parsley, oregano, thyme and chopped garlic cloves in lard
4. Add the offal and livers along with cooked pork, ham, sausages and capers
5. Thicken the small broth with a slice of fried bread, browned and ground with clove, cinnamon and pepper
6. Add a little sugar and good vinegar to the stew, making the small broth thick
