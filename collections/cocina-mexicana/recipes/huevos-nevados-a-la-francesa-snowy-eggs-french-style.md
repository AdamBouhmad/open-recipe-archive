## Ingredients

- twelve egg whites
- powdered sugar
- orange blossom water
- egg yolks
- milk
- glazed sugar almonds or macaroons
- powdered sugar
- four cuartillos of milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites until they are stiff and white, adding powdered sugar and orange blossom water.
2. Dissolve the yolks in milk with glazed sugar almonds or macaroons and powdered sugar.
3. Bring four cuartillos of milk with sugar to a boil, and at the moment it is boiling, drop spoonfuls of the beaten egg white into it, leaving it there for a minute, repeating until all the egg white is used.
4. Set the milk aside, and when it stops boiling, mix it with the yolks, stirring everything continuously to pour over the eggs.
