## Ingredients

- Pears
- Cloves
- Cinnamon sticks
- Four quarts of milk
- Three pounds of sugar
- One pound of ground almonds
- One cup of cream
- Three ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, stud them with cloves and cinnamon sticks, and make them into a preserve.
2. Remove them from the syrup when it reaches the proper point and let them drain.
3. Sweeten four quarts of milk with three pounds of sugar, strain it, and place it on the fire, mixing in one pound of well-ground almonds, one cup of cream, and three ounces of butter.
4. When the mixture reaches medium point, remove the cloves and cinnamon from the pears and place them in the milk.
5. Remove them when the milk is about to reach the consistency of cajeta.
6. Place them then in a deep platter or compote dish, and when the milk, upon stirring, shows the bottom of the vessel, pour it over the pears, which are decorated as desired.
