## Ingredients

- half a pound of esperanza almonds
- thirty-six bitter almonds
- four ounces of sugar
- zest of a green lemon
- eight eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Grind all the almonds together, some whole, with the sugar finely ground
2. Use some egg whites when grinding the almonds to prevent them from becoming oily
3. Beat the egg yolks and add the sugar to them
4. After beating everything well, combine the egg whites, yolks, grated lemon zest, and almonds
5. Butter a pan and bake in the oven
