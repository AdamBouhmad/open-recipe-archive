## Ingredients

- chicken
- fat bacon
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- tomatoes
- lard
- twine

## Directions

1. Remove the wings and neck and place inside some pieces of fat bacon with pepper, salt, and crushed parsley, adding tomatoes.
2. Wrap in a sheet of kraft paper spread with lard, and roast, tying with twine so the paper does not fall.
