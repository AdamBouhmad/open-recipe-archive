## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- oil
- garlic
- bay leaves
- thyme
- fennel
- oregano
- bitter orange pieces
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and cut fish into portions
2. Dry the fish and roll in flour
3. Fry in oil
4. Crush whole garlic cloves
5. Make a cooking broth with garlic, bay leaves, thyme, fennel, oregano, and bitter orange pieces in sufficient strong vinegar
6. When all is cold, add the fried fish
7. Cover and can be used after eight days
