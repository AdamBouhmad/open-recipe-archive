## Ingredients

- chayotles
- jitomates (tomatoes)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- perejil (parsley)
- queso añejo (aged cheese)
- huevo (egg)
- harina (flour)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Slice and chop the jitomates, onion, garlic, and parsley; fry all this mixture
2. Once well fried, remove from heat and add a bit of aged cheese
3. Fill the chayotles with this mixture and cover with beaten egg as for stuffed chiles
4. Fry some flour with onions cut in rings and garlic cloves
5. Once the flour is golden, add sufficient water and let it season
6. Just before serving, place the stuffed chayotles in this sauce
