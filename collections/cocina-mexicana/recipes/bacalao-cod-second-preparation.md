## Ingredients

- oil
- lard
- ground garlic
- escabeche oil
- cod
- hot water
- wine

## Directions

1. Fry ground garlic in oil and lard
2. Once fried, add escabeche oil and then the cod that has been first passed in hot water
3. Let it simmer a little and before removing from heat, add wine and oil
