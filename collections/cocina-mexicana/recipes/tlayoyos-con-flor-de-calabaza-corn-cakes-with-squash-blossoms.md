## Ingredients

- Corn
- Squash blossoms
- Chilchotes
- Lard
- Epazote
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare corn as usual and make dough as for tortillas
2. Take the flowers, remove the stems, slice and wash
3. Cook in a pot
4. Toast chilchotes, remove skin and veins, slice
5. Put lard in a pot, fry the flowers and chile and dehojaded epazote
6. Add a little water and salt and let boil until dry
7. Make the tortillas, fill with this, fold in half and cook on a comal
