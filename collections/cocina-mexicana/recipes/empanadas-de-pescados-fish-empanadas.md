## Ingredients

- fish, cooked
- lard and oil
- lemon drops
- tomato
- onion, chopped
- garlic
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- one pound of fine flour
- hot water
- salt
- two raw eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. After the fish is cooked, remove bones and spines
2. In a pan, put a little lard and oil, a few drops of lemon, plenty of tomato, chopped onion and garlic
3. Place to fry adding clove, cinnamon and pepper
4. Once fried, add the fish and one tablespoon of the broth in which it cooked
5. Let it boil a little and set aside to cool
6. Knead one pound of fine flour with hot water and salt dissolved in the water
7. Add two raw eggs and a little lard, well kneaded
8. Wrap in a cloth for a while, then extend with a rolling pin so it is not too thin
9. Place the fish in the center and fold the other dough over, cut it giving it the shape of an empanada, crimping the edge so the broth does not escape
10. Coat a baking pan with lard, place a paper larger than the empanada and this is placed on the paper and put over two violent fires so they cook quickly and the dough does not become hard
11. Once done, sprinkle with sugar or sesame before serving
