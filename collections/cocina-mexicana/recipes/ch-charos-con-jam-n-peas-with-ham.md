## Ingredients

- green peas
- butter
- water
- ham
- broth or Spanish sauce
- parsley sprigs
- peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Mash the green peas with butter
2. Pour water over them and let soak for a quarter hour
3. While soaking, lightly fry thin pieces of ham in a proportionate amount of butter
4. Drain the peas and put them in another pot
5. Moisten with broth, ham pieces, parsley sprigs, and peppercorns
6. Add a little salt and bring to a boil over high heat
7. Reduce heat and let simmer very slowly until thoroughly cooked and the sauce is quite thick
8. Skim, remove the parsley, and serve
