## Ingredients

- fresh cheese
- half a pound of flour
- one ounce of lard
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind the fresh cheese on a clean metate
2. Mix with half a pound of flour and one ounce of lard
3. Knead well and put on a table
4. Spread with a rolling pin
5. Cut with a cup
6. Fry and put them in syrup
7. Serve on platters with cinnamon
