## Ingredients

- ground sponge cake
- milk
- sugar
- egg yolks
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Make a paste with ground sponge cake, soaked in well-sweetened milk and mixed with egg yolks
2. Coat with beaten egg and fry in butter or lard
3. Sprinkle with sifted sugar
