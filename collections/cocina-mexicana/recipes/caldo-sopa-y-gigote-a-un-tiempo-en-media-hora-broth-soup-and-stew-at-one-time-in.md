## Ingredients

- half a pound of meat, chopped
- one carrot
- one onion
- one turnip
- a little celery
- one clove
- two quartillos of water
- salt to taste
- rice, noodles, or semolina for the soup
- fine herbs fried in lard with flour
- the same broth

## Directions

1. Chop the meat, carrot, onion, turnip, celery, and clove
2. Place all in a cooking pot with two quartillos of water and salt
3. Bring to a boil and skim
4. After half an hour, pass through a sieve
5. Place rice, noodles, or semolina in a small bag in boiling water; when cooked, empty the bag into a soup tureen
6. Pour the strained broth over it
7. Add a few fine herbs fried in lard with flour, moistened with the same broth, letting it thicken slightly
8. Top with the chopped meat mixture, salt, pepper, and fried eggs
9. This way broth, soup, and stew are obtained at the same time in half an hour
