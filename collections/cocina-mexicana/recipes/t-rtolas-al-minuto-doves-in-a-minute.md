## Ingredients

- Twelve doves
- Lard
- Salt
- Chile
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Pluck, empty and arrange a dozen doves
2. Turn them in the casserole in lard and salt
3. When they have taken color, add chopped chile, garlic and parsley, a bit of flour, white wine and broth
4. Once the sauce begins to cook, remove the doves and serve with fried bread crusts
