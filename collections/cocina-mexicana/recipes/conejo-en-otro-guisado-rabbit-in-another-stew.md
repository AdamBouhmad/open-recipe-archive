## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Oil
- Bread
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Wide chiles
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Skin and cut the rabbit into portions
2. Heat oil in a pan; brown a good slice of bread, remove it and fry the rabbit portions
3. When it begins to brown, fry much chopped onion and garlic
4. When fried, grind the toasted bread with plenty of parsley and four or six wide chiles, deveined and soaked
5. Add this with sufficient water and salt, letting it boil until the rabbit is very tender and the broth is thick
6. Before serving add raw oil
