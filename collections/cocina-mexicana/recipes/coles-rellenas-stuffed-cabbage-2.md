## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- string
- broth
- Spanish sauce or butter and flour

## Directions

1. Boil a clean and washed cabbage in water for a quarter hour
2. Place in cold water and let cool
3. Remove the leaves one by one
4. Coat them and replace them as if the cabbage were whole, securing them with string
5. Boil again, then remove from heat
6. After cooling, remove the broth and squeeze lightly to release the fat
7. Place on a platter whole or cut in half
8. Pour over it strained broth, Spanish sauce, or butter and flour dissolved in the cabbage cooking broth
