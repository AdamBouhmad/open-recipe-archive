## Ingredients

- chickens
- water
- salt
- seasoning ingredients
- rice
- saffron water
- ripe tomatoes, chopped
- onions, sliced
- garlic cloves, chopped
- broth from cooking the chickens
- poblano chiles or tomachiles, roasted and peeled

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens according to the number of guests in water with little salt and the appropriate seasoning as for a pot, and after cooked divide into regular-sized portions
2. Clean and wash the rice, soak it in saffron water to take on color
3. In a pan over fire with lard, fry three or four ripe, well-chopped tomatoes, the same number of sliced onions, and chopped garlic cloves
4. Once everything is fried, add the well-drained rice so it also fries, then add the broth in which the chickens were cooked and let it boil in it
5. When the broth has reduced somewhat, add roasted and peeled poblano or tomachile chiles that are whole and remain on top
