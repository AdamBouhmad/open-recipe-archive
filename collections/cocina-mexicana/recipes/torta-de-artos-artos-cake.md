## Ingredients

- one pound artos (cleaned)
- half pound sugar
- starch (almidón)
- five egg whites
- five egg yolks
- five whole eggs
- one spoonful butter
- salt
- half pound butter (mantequilla)
- catarino biscuit powder
- butter for pan

## Directions

1. After cleaning, grind one pound of artos until it resembles flour and pass through a sieve
2. Grind half pound of sugar and sift it
3. Take what fits in a silver spoon of starch and after grinding mix it with the flour and sugar, place in a large pan
4. Beat five egg whites as for nougat and when very stiff add five egg yolks
5. To the flour mixture add five whole eggs, one spoonful butter, salt and half pound butter
6. Add the beaten eggs and mix with a hand or spoon
7. Prepare a pan greased with butter
8. Dust bottom generously with catarino biscuit powder and dust the sides
9. Pour batter in and dust with more biscuit powder
10. Cook in a small oven, then remove and leave in hot ashes with a covered pot with fire on top
11. Test with a straw; if it comes out clean, it is done
12. Remove from the mold so the biscuit does not stick
13. Note: if eggs are small, beat six instead of five
