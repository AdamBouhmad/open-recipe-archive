## Ingredients

- pigeons
- vegetables for macedoine

## Directions

1. Cook the pigeons as previously stated in the article on pigeons with peas
2. Divide them in half
3. Arrange them on a plate in the form of a crown
4. Place a macedoine of vegetables in the center, prepared as explained in the article on Macedoine of Vegetables
