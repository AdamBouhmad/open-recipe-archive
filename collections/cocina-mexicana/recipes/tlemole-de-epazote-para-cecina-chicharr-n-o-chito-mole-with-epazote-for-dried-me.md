## Ingredients

- cecina (dried salted meat)
- salt
- water
- lard
- epazote
- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- sesame seeds
- cooked tomatoes

## Directions

1. Soaked and well washed cecina, put to half cook in water with salt, lard and epazote
2. While cooking, fry in lard ancho and pasilla chiles and sesame seeds, all toasted and ground together with cooked tomatoes
3. Once everything is well fried, pour in the pot of cecina, which finishes cooking at the same time the broth thickens
4. Pork crackling and chito are added to this mole without cooking separately first
5. Can also be made with pork meat, following the same procedures as for cecina, and can finally omit the sesame seeds and add small squash
