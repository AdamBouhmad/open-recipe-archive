## Ingredients

- Mutton cuts
- Pot broth
- Vinegar
- A little water
- Ground saffron
- Whole pepper
- Salt
- Lard
- Cleaned walnuts
- Eyes (garlic cloves)
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook the mutton cuts in pot broth with vinegar, a little water, ground saffron, whole pepper, salt, and lard
2. When cooked, slice them
3. Coat with beaten egg and fry
4. Place in the platter
5. Cover with a sauce made of cleaned walnuts ground with garlic cloves
6. Fry the ground mixture in oil and add to it the broth in which the meat was cooked
