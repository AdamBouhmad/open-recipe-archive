## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- a little starch
- sugar
- a piece of lemon peel, dried
- ground almonds

## Directions

1. Mix milk with egg yolks until colored
2. Add a little starch and sugar, a piece of dried lemon peel, and ground almonds
3. Bring to a boil and when it reaches the consistency of jerícalla, remove from heat
4. Pour into a dish
5. Sprinkle sugar on top and burn with a hot iron
