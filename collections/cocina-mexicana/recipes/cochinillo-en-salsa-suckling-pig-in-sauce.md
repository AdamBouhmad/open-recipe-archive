## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- lard
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut into large pieces
2. After roasting, pass through lard and moisten with water or broth
3. Add salt, pepper, and a bay leaf
4. Make a mixture of egg yolks
5. When serving, sprinkle with lemon juice
