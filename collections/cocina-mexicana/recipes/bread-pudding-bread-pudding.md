## Ingredients

- crumbled bread
- eggs
- milk
- cinnamon powder
- clove
- other spices
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Mix crumbled bread with eggs and as much milk as is needed to only moisten it
2. Add cinnamon powder, clove, and other spices, and a little butter
3. Place in a glass or bowl
4. Leave to cook under a bell oven
