## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two birote bread cakes
- six eggs
- zest of one green lemon
- raisins
- almonds
- butter
- hard biscuit powder

## Directions

1. Soak two birote bread cakes in one quart of milk
2. Grind with six eggs, the zest of one green lemon, raisins and almonds
3. Spread the mold with butter, add hard biscuit powder, mix the dough and cook over two fires
4. When serving dust with sugar
