## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- water

## Directions

1. Put wheat flour in water, mix well and let it settle
2. Decant the water or remove it carefully without stirring the settled flour
3. Put the flour in a cloth napkin, tie it and hang it so that all the water drains out
4. When it stops dripping, put it to dry well in the sun
5. Grind it afterwards and sift it in a sifting sieve
6. Knead with lard, cooked egg yolks and sugar to taste, so that the dough becomes soft and can be ground
7. Make little tortillas from it on the metate, with which the turco is formed the same as in the previous articles
