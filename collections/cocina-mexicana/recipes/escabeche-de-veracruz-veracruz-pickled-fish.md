## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- garlic
- thyme
- bay leaf
- oregano
- lemon
- salt
- vinegar
- Tabasco pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and desalt if not fresh.
2. Cut into pieces small enough to fit in a crock.
3. Fry in oil, then set aside to cool.
4. In fresh oil, fry plenty of crushed garlic cloves, thyme, bay leaf, oregano, and lemon slices; add salt if the fish is not salted.
5. When well fried, add vinegar and whole Tabasco pepper.
6. Once well seasoned, set aside and let cool completely.
7. In the crock, layer fish with a bit of oregano, thyme, bay leaves, and fried lemon slices.
8. Cover with the herbs and oil.
9. Continue layering until the crock is full, finishing with raw oil.
10. Cover very well and after eight days it can be eaten.
