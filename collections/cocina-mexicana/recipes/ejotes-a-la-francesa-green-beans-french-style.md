## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- butter
- herbs
- pepper
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Choose green and tender beans
2. Trim both ends and remove the filaments on the sides
3. Blanch in hot salted water on a lively fire
4. When done and not overcooked, drain in a sieve
5. Dissolve a piece of butter mixed with finely chopped herbs, salt, and pepper
6. Add the beans that have been briefly in boiling water
7. Sauté in a pot
8. Mix in a piece of butter mixed with flour and lemon juice
