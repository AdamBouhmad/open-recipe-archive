## Ingredients

- Pork liver
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Pork loin
- Ham
- Garlic
- Thyme
- Pork caul
- Lard
- Salt
- White wine
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and stud it with clove, cinnamon, pepper, pork loin, ham, garlic, and thyme.
2. Wrap it in pork caul.
3. Place it in a pot or cooking vessel with lard.
4. Add salt, white wine, and vinegar.
5. Bake in the oven.
