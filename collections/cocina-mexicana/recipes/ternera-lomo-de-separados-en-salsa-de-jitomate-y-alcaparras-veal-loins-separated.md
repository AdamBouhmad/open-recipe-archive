## Ingredients

- Veal loins
- Lard
- Salt
- Green chiles, whole
- Onions, sliced
- Garlic, chopped
- Tomatoes, ground
- Capers
- Wide chile, deveined
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Water
- Vinegar
- Toasted bread fried in lard, ground

## Directions

1. Cut the loins and place in a pot with lard, salt, whole green chiles, sliced onions, chopped garlic, tomatoes ground together with capers, and one wide deveined chile which is ground only to give color, clove, pepper and cinnamon.
2. Add the necessary water and vinegar, and place the pot over the fire.
3. When the loins are cooked, thicken the sauce with toasted bread fried in lard and ground.
