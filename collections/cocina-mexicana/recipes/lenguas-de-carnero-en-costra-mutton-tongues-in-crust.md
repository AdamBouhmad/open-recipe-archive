## Ingredients

- Mutton tongues
- broth
- white wine
- parsley bundle
- onion
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- garlic head
- clove
- salt
- large pepper
- Spanish sauce
- bread crumbs
- butter or grated ham
- raw egg yolks
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues with a little broth, a glass of white wine, a bundle of parsley, onion, a bay leaf, a little thyme and basil, half a garlic head, two cloves, salt and large pepper
2. Let boil half an hour over low heat and pour Spanish sauce over them
3. For the crust, take a fireproof plate and pour on the bottom a filling the thickness of a finger, made with bread crumbs, a little butter or grated ham, two raw egg yolks, chopped parsley and onion, a little substantial broth or strained broth, a tablespoon of broth from the pot, salt and large pepper
4. Mix everything and pour into the plate, which will be placed over coals until the filling sticks
5. Then drain the butter from it and serve the tongues with their small broth on top of this crust
