## Ingredients

- xitomate bien cocido
- sal
- manteca
- cebollas
- ajos
- peregil
- caldillo
- chilitos enteros
- clavo
- pimienta
- azafrán
- vinagre bueno
- azúcar
- chapandongos
- aceitunas
- chilitos curados
- alcaparras

## Directions

1. Grind some well-cooked tomato, season with the necessary salt, and put to fry in a little lard
2. Add some onions in quarters, some chopped garlic and parsley, and once it no longer splashes much, add the well-seasoned broth with salt and some whole small chiles
3. When well boiled, grind a little clove, pepper and saffron in good vinegar and add it, seasoning with a lump of sugar
4. Add the chapandongos, made as previously stated, and as soon as the broth thickens a little, remove from heat
5. These chapandongos are made filled with cheese, brains and even with minced fish
