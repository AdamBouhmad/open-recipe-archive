## Ingredients

- Milk
- Gum arabic
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Put two quarter liters of good milk in the pan
2. Add a little gum arabic dissolved in a little orange blossom water
3. Beat with a wooden stick; when the cream is like snow, put it with a skimmer by spoonfuls on a platter
4. This preparation should be made a few minutes before serving and in the coolest place
