## Ingredients

- Seeds
- Syrup at good point
- Wine
- Spices
- Sponge cake

## Directions

1. Wash the seeds so that none of the green comes off
2. Grind them well
3. Add to the syrup at good point and stir like for bread that is not very thick
4. Add wine and plenty of spices
5. Arrange layers of sponge cake with all its decorations
