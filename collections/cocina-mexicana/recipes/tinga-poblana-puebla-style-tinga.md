## Ingredients

- pollos (chickens)
- jitomates (tomatoes)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- perejil (parsley)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo de los pollos (chicken broth)
- sal (salt)
- chupocles en vinagre (smoked chiles in vinegar)
- sardinas (sardines)
- lechuga (lettuce)
- aguacate (avocado)

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens, cut into portions, and cook
2. Chop plenty of tomatoes, onion, garlic, and parsley; fry all this in sufficient lard
3. Once well fried, add a little of the chicken broth
4. Add the meat with necessary salt and let season
5. When nearly done, add smoked chiles in vinegar cut into strips
6. Clean the sardines and add them as well
7. When almost dry, remove from heat and serve garnished with lettuce leaves and avocado on top
