## Ingredients

- Cooked duck quarters
- Eating oil, one cup
- Vinegar, sufficient
- Tomato, ground
- Garlic, ground
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Salt
- Duck broth

## Directions

1. Heat eating oil with sufficient vinegar in a pot.
2. Add ground tomato, garlic, clove, cinnamon, and pepper.
3. Season with appropriate salt.
4. Add the cooked duck quarters with a little of their broth.
5. Let everything season together and remove when it has thickened sufficiently.
