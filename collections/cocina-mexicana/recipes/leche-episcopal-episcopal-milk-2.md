## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar to taste
- flavoring to taste
- three egg yolks per cup
- ladyfinger biscuits (soletas)

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil
2. When it begins to thicken, add sugar and flavoring to taste
3. Measure into cups
4. Add three egg yolks to each cup
5. When cold, return to the fire until it thickens
6. Pour back into cups
7. Place whole ladyfinger biscuits inside
