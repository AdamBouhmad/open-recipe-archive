## Ingredients

- Bread pieces
- good oil
- excellent butter
- skate livers
- anchovy strips desalted
- oil
- parsley
- onion
- small garlic head
- capers
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut bread into toast-shaped pieces about one inch wide and long or a bit more
2. Pass them through good oil
3. Melt two blocks of excellent butter in a pot
4. Add the skate livers, some desalted anchovy strips, a little oil, parsley, onion, a small garlic head and capers, all finely minced, salt, pepper and a little fine spices
5. Stir and mix everything well
6. Form the canapés by making layers: first a layer of fine herbs when cooled, then a layer of skate livers cut the same way with anchovy strips
7. Make all the same way and finish with another layer of fine herbs
8. Sprinkle a little fine bread crumbs on top
9. Brown under a pot lid, griddle or sheet with fire and embers on top
10. Serve as an appetizer squeezing lemon juice over them
