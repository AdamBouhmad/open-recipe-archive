## Ingredients

- Beef udders
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Lard
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook the udders in water
2. Chop tomato, onion and garlic and cook them in lard until browned
3. In a separate pan, fry some garlic cloves
4. Grind the fried garlic with clove, cinnamon and pepper
5. Add the ground mixture to the lard in which the tomato mixture was fried
6. Add the ground spices and some of the broth in which the udders were cooked
7. Add some parsley, vinegar and oil
8. Add the sliced udders and let cook
