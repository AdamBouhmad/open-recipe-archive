## Ingredients

- mutton head
- broad chiles
- garlic
- cumin seeds
- ham
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- butter or oil
- paper

## Directions

1. Skin the mutton head and remove the cartilage to clean away the dirt
2. Grind broad chiles, two or three peeled heads of garlic and a few cumin seeds
3. Open the head, coat the center by inserting a slice of ham and a good portion of chopped parsley
4. Coat the inside with the ground chile and sew it closed
5. Spread the rest of the chile on the outside of the head and over the chile coat with butter or oil
6. Wrap in buttered paper and place in the oven at gentle heat, taking care to turn it, or it can be placed in a small pot with a double cover placed on top of the one placed below
7. Once well cooked, remove the paper and coat again with butter
