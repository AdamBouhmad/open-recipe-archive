## Ingredients

- sifted tamales without filling
- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make sifted tamales without any filling, let them rest for one day
2. The next day, grind them with cooked egg yolks, sugar, clove and cinnamon to taste
3. Make little tortillas on the metate and proceed as in the previous articles
