## Ingredients

- one pineapple
- sugar to taste
- clove and cinnamon powder to taste
- sponge cake or mamon toasted and ground, in the necessary quantity
- four well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cook a pineapple, peel it, grind it, and mix with sugar, clove and cinnamon powder to taste, and sponge cake or mamon toasted and ground in the quantity necessary to form the paste
2. Mix this with four well-beaten eggs so that it is not hard
3. Empty into molds or small cazuelas spread with lard and put to cook in the oven
