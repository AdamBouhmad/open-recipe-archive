## Ingredients

- Boneless pork meat
- Longaniza
- Chorizos
- Scrambled eggs
- Green chile
- Tortillas
- Cheese strips
- Burned lard

## Directions

1. Cook pork meat without bone, longaniza, and chorizos.
2. Once cooked, fry it until well browned and mash it coarsely.
3. Have the scrambled eggs ready with all their seasonings and green chile.
4. Dip the tortilla in the chile and place it on a platter.
5. Add a spoonful of the eggs and another of meat.
6. Wrap it and do the same with the rest.
7. Cover with the chile, cheese strips, and a little burned lard.
