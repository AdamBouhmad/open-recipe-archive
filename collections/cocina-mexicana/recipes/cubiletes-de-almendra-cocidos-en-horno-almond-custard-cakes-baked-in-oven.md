## Ingredients

- Fourteen egg yolks
- Six egg whites
- One pound of almonds, ground
- One fistful of sugar, ground
- Lard
- Syrup
- Raisins, almonds, and ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat fourteen egg yolks as for royal eggs, and separately beat six egg whites
2. Mix them together when well beaten, adding one pound of ground almonds and one fistful of ground sugar
3. Coat the custard cakes with lard and fill with the mixture
4. Put immediately in the oven to brown
5. When removed and cooled, give a brief boil in prepared syrup
6. Place on a platter, studded with raisins and almonds with ground cinnamon on top
