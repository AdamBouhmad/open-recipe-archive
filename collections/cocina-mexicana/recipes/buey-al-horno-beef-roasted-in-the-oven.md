## Ingredients

- beef slices, with half less fat
- lean ham pieces
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- garlic, all finely minced
- salt
- black pepper
- flour dissolved in a little vinegar
- 4 or 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the beef as desired or as most suitable into slices with half less fat than usual.
2. Put this meat in a casserole with small squared pieces of lean ham, parsley, onion, mushrooms, and garlic, all finely minced, salt, black pepper, and flour dissolved in a little vinegar.
3. Mix well all together and add 4 or 6 egg yolks.
4. Pour this mixture into a casserole or earthenware dish of appropriate size, lined with ham slices, pressing everything well down and covering with a lid, whose joints will be sealed.
5. Place in the oven to cook, leaving it there for three or four hours.
6. After this time, remove it; if it is to be served hot as a main course, remove the ham slices and degrease the sauce; but if it is to serve as an intermediate course, let it cool in the same state it came out of the oven.
