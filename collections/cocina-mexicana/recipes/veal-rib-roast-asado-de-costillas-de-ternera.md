## Ingredients

- Veal ribs
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion

## Directions

1. Fry in a pot with plenty of lard, salt, pepper and some onion tops
2. Leave to fry until they take on a roasted color
3. Then add sufficient water for the meat to cook
4. Cover the pot and leave on low heat
