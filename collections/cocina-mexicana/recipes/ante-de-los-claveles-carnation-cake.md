## Ingredients

- three to four well-cooked beets
- six pounds of sugar
- sesame seeds
- cloves
- cinnamon
- orange blossom water
- ground almonds or pumpkin seeds
- sponge cake
- carnation petals
- ash water

## Directions

1. Grind the well-cooked beets very well on a metate
2. Make a syrup with six pounds of sugar, strain after two boils, and add the beet
3. Add plenty of sesame, cloves, cinnamon, and orange blossom water
4. When it pulls away from the pan, add ground almonds or pumpkin seeds
5. Wash them well with ash water until they are very white and pound them to the size of an almond
6. Add the paste and let it cool
7. Once cold, place it on a platter and sprinkle sponge cake with the syrup
8. Add bread crumbs so it comes out well colored
9. After placing it on the platter, carve and decorate it
10. Have carnation petals stripped of white tips steeped in orange blossom water and scatter them on top
