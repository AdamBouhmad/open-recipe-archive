## Ingredients

- one pound of well-clarified sugar
- five or six tablespoons of virgin honey
- nine egg whites, well beaten
- a little squeezed lemon
- dragée and toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Put one pound of well-clarified sugar to boil until it reaches the consistency of a somewhat thick preserve
2. Remove from heat, let it cool a little, add about five or six tablespoons of virgin honey
3. Return the pan to heat and bring to the hard crack stage
4. When warm, add nine well-beaten egg whites that have been prepared beforehand
5. Beat and return to heat without stopping to stir
6. Then lower heat, add a little squeezed lemon, and keep stirring until it forms balls
7. Pour into a platter, and on top decorate with dragée and toasted sesame
