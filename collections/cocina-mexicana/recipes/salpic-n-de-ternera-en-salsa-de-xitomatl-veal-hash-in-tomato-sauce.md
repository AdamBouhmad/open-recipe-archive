## Ingredients

- cooked veal pulp
- roasted tomatoes in sufficient quantity
- finely chopped onions
- oregano
- vinegar
- plenty of oil
- pitted olives in halves
- pickled chili peppers, chopped
- strips of roasted poblano or cuaresmeño chiles
- capers
- whole olives for garnish
- preserved chili peppers for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the veal pulp well and shred it.
2. Grind the roasted tomatoes in sufficient quantity.
3. Mix the ground tomatoes with finely chopped onions, oregano, vinegar, plenty of oil, pitted olives in halves, chopped pickled chili peppers, strips of roasted poblano or cuaresmeño chiles, and capers.
4. Combine all ingredients with the shredded meat.
5. Place on a plate and garnish on top with whole olives, preserved chili peppers, and capers.
