## Ingredients

- Texocotes
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Follow the same procedures as the French-style ham jelly
2. Give the syrup the breaking point
3. Mix in the proportion of one arroba of sugar for every twenty-three cuartillos of texocote water
