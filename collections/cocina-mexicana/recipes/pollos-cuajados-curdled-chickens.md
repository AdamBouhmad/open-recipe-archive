## Ingredients

- chickens
- gizzards and livers
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- salt
- garlic cloves
- ripe tomatoes, ground
- green chiles
- parsley, chopped
- all spices, ground
- twelve egg yolks
- juice of four lemons
- broth
- raisins
- almonds
- caper berries
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens without getting them wet, and the next day cook them with the gizzards and livers, with ground clove, cinnamon, pepper, cumin, salt, and garlic cloves.
2. Separately fry ground tomatoes with green chiles and chopped parsley, then add all ground spices and sufficient salt.
3. Beat twelve egg yolks with the juice of four lemons, adding them gradually while stirring, and calm the foam with a little broth.
4. Let everything boil until it no longer produces foam, then place the chickens in quarters or whole.
5. Chop the gizzards and livers and let them cook over gentle heat until the sauce curdles, garnishing with raisins, almonds, caper berries, and parsley, then remove from heat.
