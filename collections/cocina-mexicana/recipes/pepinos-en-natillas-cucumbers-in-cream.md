## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- cream
- salt (fine)

## Directions

1. After peeling the cucumbers, divide them and remove all the inside
2. Chop them into cubes and place them to boil in water with salt
3. When they are almost cooked, remove and drain them
4. Place them in cold water and then dry them with cloth
5. Place them in cream seasoned with fine salt
6. When the cream is well-flavored, place them to finish cooking over low heat, taking care that they do not boil
