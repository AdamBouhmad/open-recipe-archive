## Ingredients

- fifty crabs
- crab meat
- crab shells
- noodles or rice
- broth
- sugar
- salt
- bread toasts
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash fifty crabs well in many waters and place dry in a casserole over very gentle heat
2. When cooked, separate the meat and keep the tails
3. Grind or crush the shells until they become paste
4. Pound together in the mortar or metate with the crab meat, noodles or rice cooked with broth
5. Place the mixture over the fire to boil
6. When it has boiled well, strain two or three times and add a little sugar and corresponding salt
7. Place the crab tails in a tureen with bread toasts fried in butter
8. Heat the bisque well, taking care that it does not boil again, and when serving add a good piece of butter
