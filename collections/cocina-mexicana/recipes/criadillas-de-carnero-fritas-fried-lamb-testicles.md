## Ingredients

- six pairs of testicles
- lemon
- salt
- pepper
- fresh parsley
- flour
- lemon slices
- salt-pepper
- grande sauce (or sauce at the point of half gelatin)

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from six pairs of testicles and divide each one into six pieces
2. Marinate with lemon, salt, pepper and fresh parsley
3. Let drain for a quarter hour before serving
4. Roll in flour
5. Fry twice so that they become well puffed
6. Serve with lemon slices on the side, with salt-pepper nearby, or with a grande sauce at the point of half gelatin
