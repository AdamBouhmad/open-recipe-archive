## Ingredients

- lamb blood
- onions in abundance
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut onions lengthwise in abundance, seasoning them with salt and pepper
2. When the lamb is slaughtered, stir the blood and mix it with the onion
3. Put everything in a roasting dish and place in the oven
4. When well roasted, remove and it can be eaten hot or cold
5. If you do not want to use the oven, roast it at home by placing the dish over strong heat with coals on the lid
