## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Salt
- Ham slices
- Eggs
- Bread
- Green chile
- Tomato
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Cilantro
- Hard-boiled egg slices
- Capers
- Tornachiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with salt, some ham slices, and eggs
2. Slice bread, toast it, and arrange it in a fireproof dish or platter
3. Fry green chile and tomato, finely chopped, seasoning with cloves, cinnamon, pepper, and toasted cilantro, all ground
4. Once fried, add the broth in which the chickens and ham were cooked, with the appropriate salt
5. Place a layer of toasted bread, then a layer of cooked chicken pieces, then a layer of hard-boiled egg slices, capers, tornachiles, and olives, and another layer of bread slices
6. Continue alternating and moistening the layers with the prepared broth until the necessary amount remains
7. Place the pot over the steam of a boiling pot until everything cooks properly
