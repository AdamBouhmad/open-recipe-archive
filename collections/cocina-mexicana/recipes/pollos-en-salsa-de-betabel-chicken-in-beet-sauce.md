## Ingredients

- lard
- salt
- cooked and ground beets
- chicken
- pieces of ham
- sliced chorizo, cooked
- raisins
- toasted sesame
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- capers
- sugar
- wine
- chicken broth
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry cooked and ground beets in lard with salt
2. Place chicken with pieces of ham and sliced cooked chorizo in this fat
3. Add raisins, toasted sesame, pine nuts, walnuts, capers, a bit of sugar, wine, and chicken broth
4. Season with ground cloves, cinnamon, and pepper, mixed with a bit of bread crumbs to enhance the color
5. Let the sauce simmer until it takes the consistency of sauce and is well seasoned
