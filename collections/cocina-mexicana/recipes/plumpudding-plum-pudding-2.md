## Ingredients

- six ounces of sugar
- six ounces of finely chopped veal tallow
- half pound of raisins without pits, chopped
- half pound of Corinth raisins
- four ounces of breadcrumbs
- four ounces of flour
- four eggs
- a little nutmeg
- salt
- three to five lemons
- one orange
- milk

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole six ounces of sugar and six ounces of finely chopped veal tallow, half pound of chopped raisins without pits and half pound of Corinth raisins, four ounces of breadcrumbs, four ounces of flour, four eggs, a little nutmeg, salt, three to five lemons and one orange, covered and chopped
2. Mix everything with milk and tie in a strong cloth moistened in cold water and well dusted with flour
3. Cook in boiling water and it must be six hours on the fire
4. Let cool before removing the cloth
