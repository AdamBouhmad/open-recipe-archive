## Ingredients

- sorrel
- three or four heads of lettuce
- a handful of chervil
- butter
- six egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of flour
- cream or three glasses of milk

## Directions

1. Prepare and season the sorrel as in the preceding article and cook it the same way
2. After it has been well fried in the butter, add two spoonfuls of flour filled to the brim mixed with cream or three glasses of milk over six egg yolks
3. Pour this composition over the sorrel
4. When this is dissolved, allow it to reduce on a somewhat ardent burner, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon
5. When done, pass it through a sieve and keep it hot for timely use
