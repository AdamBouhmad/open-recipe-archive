## Ingredients

- zucchini
- pork
- breadcrumbs
- oil
- ground pepper
- eggs
- parsley
- lard
- tomatoes
- onion
- garlic
- vinegar
- cloves

## Directions

1. After cooking the zucchini, remove a small crown and the insides, which can also be done before cooking
2. Chop very finely pork that is half-cooked
3. Mix with a little ground breadcrumbs, oil, ground pepper, three or four eggs, and minced parsley
4. Fill the zucchini with this mixture
5. Place in a lard-greased dish and bake between two fires until golden
6. Make the broth with roasted and ground red tomatoes, add minced onion, minced garlic and parsley, a little vinegar, pepper and ground cloves
7. Pour this broth over the zucchini and let simmer slowly
