## Ingredients

- onions, proportional in quantity to the substance to be made
- butter
- some spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- a little broth

## Directions

1. Halve the onions and after removing the beards and tails, slice them
2. Fry them in butter until they have browned well without burning
3. Add some spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and a little broth, and allow everything to reduce to half
4. Pass it through a sieve
5. When you wish to use this substance, it is necessary to heat it in a bain-marie
