## Ingredients

- Bobo fish
- Water
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Peppercorns
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak to desalt well and remove the scales
2. Divide into portions and place on the fire in a pot with water, not cooking all at the same time but one after another; the head should be the last
3. Place on a platter
4. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan; add a spoonful of peppercorns to fry a little and mix with the butter in which the bobo fried
5. Sprinkle the fish with this sauce, adding a little vinegar on top
6. This stew should be made three or four hours before eating
