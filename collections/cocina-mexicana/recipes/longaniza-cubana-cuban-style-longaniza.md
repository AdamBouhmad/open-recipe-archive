## Ingredients

- pork meat, marinated in adobo
- cooked onions
- a little parsley
- fine spices crushed
- a little garlic

## Directions

1. Marinate the pork meat in adobo as previously described
2. Take and chop cooked onions
3. Add a little parsley, fine spices crushed, and a little garlic
4. Place this filling in the intestines
5. Serve roasted or fried
