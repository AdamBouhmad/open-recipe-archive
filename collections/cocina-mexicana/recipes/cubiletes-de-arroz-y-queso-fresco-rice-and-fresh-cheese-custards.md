## Ingredients

- sixteen egg yolks
- sixteen egg whites
- rice ground dry
- sugar
- fresh cheese of any kind
- a piece of butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites separately
2. Incorporate with them the ground rice, sugar, fresh cheese, and a piece of butter the size of an egg, bringing everything to the point of being like a sponge cake batter
3. Fill the custard molds, already buttered, and place them to cook in the oven
