## Ingredients

- Sea bream, cleaned and scaled
- Onion, finely chopped
- Toasted bread, ground to fine powder
- Salt
- Pepper or fresh parsley, chopped and crushed
- Broth

## Directions

1. Heat oil or lard in a cazuela over low heat and fry the finely chopped onion
2. Add the sea bream, either whole or cut into four or five pieces widthwise
3. When the fish is browned on both sides, remove the fish and onion from the cazuela
4. Add enough toasted and ground bread to form a paste, seasoning with salt and pepper, or substitute the pepper with chopped and crushed parsley
5. Dilute this fried mixture with a little broth
6. Pour the mixture over the sea bream to serve
