## Ingredients

- tuna slices three fingers wide
- salt
- pepper
- juice of green grapes
- onions stuffed with cloves
- lemon juice
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tuna into slices three fingers wide
2. Season with salt and pepper, juice of green grapes, onions stuffed with cloves, and lemon juice
3. Fry
4. Serve with a good mustard sauce
