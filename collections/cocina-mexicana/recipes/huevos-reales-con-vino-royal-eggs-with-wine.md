## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- wicker baskets or beaters
- lard
- water
- tiles
- comal
- embers
- white wine
- syrup at high point
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. On a tray or other flat dish, beat the yolks with wicker baskets or beaters until very thick and forming small wrinkles on top
2. Place in a small pot greased with lard, and on the fire place another larger pot with water and a bed of tiles so the small pot sits there
3. Cover with a comal with embers on top
4. As soon as a straw shows the eggs are cooked, quickly empty the small pot onto cloths, and with the same speed cover the eggs so the air does not affect them and they deflate
5. Leave them until the next day, when they will be cut into quarters or slices
6. These, moistened with white wine, will be placed in syrup at high point, also with wine, and finally adorned with cinnamon, raisins and almonds
