## Ingredients

- equal parts pork meat and fat ham, finely chopped
- salt
- spices
- sheep intestines

## Directions

1. Chop equal parts of pork and fat ham finely
2. Add salt and spices
3. Fill sheep intestines with this mixture, tying them at the desired length
4. Before cooking on a grill, prick with a pin
5. May be fried in a pan
6. May also be made flattened with the same meats wrapped in caul fat
