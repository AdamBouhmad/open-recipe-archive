## Ingredients

- meat pieces
- salt
- bay leaf
- parsley
- tocino between fat and lean, fried
- garlic cloves
- pepper
- water
- tomatoes
- broth
- spices
- pomegranate juice

## Directions

1. Place the meat pieces in a pot with salt, bay leaf, parsley, and fried tocino
2. When braising, add garlic cloves crushed with pepper and dissolved in water
3. Add hot water
4. Fry tomatoes and add them
5. Add broth, salt and spices
6. Add pomegranate juice for additional flavor
