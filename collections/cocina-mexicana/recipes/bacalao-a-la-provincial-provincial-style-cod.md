## Ingredients

- young garlic
- crushed garlic
- parsley
- onion
- lemon in slices without peel or seeds
- peppercorns
- two tablespoons of oil
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- pepper

## Directions

1. After the cod is prepared as already indicated and cooked, take the platter on which it is to be served and put in it young garlic, crushed garlic, parsley, onion, lemon slices without peel or seeds, peppercorns, two tablespoons of oil, and a piece of butter the size of an egg
2. Put the cod on top, cover it with a layer of the same mixture and sprinkle with bread crumbs
3. Put the casserole over low heat so that it simmers little by little
4. Add lemon juice, a little pepper, and let it take color in the oven
