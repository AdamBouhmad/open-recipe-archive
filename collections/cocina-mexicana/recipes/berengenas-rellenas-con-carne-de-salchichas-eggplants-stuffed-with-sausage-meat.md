## Ingredients

- Ripe eggplants
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Parsley
- Herbs
- Onions
- Garlic
- Butter
- Bread
- Milk
- Sausage meat, half a pound

## Directions

1. Choose ripe eggplants and divide them in two lengthwise
2. Remove the flesh without damaging the skin and chop it
3. Place it in a concave vessel to drain a little
4. Add salt, pepper and a little vinegar and let it rest for one hour
5. Chop parsley and fine herbs, onions and half a head of garlic
6. Macerate everything in hot butter
7. Blanch the eggplant flesh in the same butter, squeeze it and mix with the preparation
8. Soak two egg-sized pieces of bread in broth or milk
9. Squeeze and crush them
10. Add half a pound of sausage meat, which is also crushed with the chopped eggplants
11. Mix everything well, season and crush a little more
12. Fill the eggplants with this mixture
13. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
14. Drizzle with butter
15. Cook at two heats
