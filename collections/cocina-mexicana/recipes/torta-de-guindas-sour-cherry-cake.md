## Ingredients

- Four pounds of pitted sour cherries
- One pound of sugar
- A little wine
- Three sheets of puff pastry dough
- Lard

## Directions

1. Place four pounds of pitted sour cherries in a pot with one pound of sugar and a little wine until they produce much juice and are well preserved
2. Simmer over low heat
3. Form the cake by placing three sheets of puff pastry dough in the bottom of a pot with lard
4. Arrange the preserved cherries on top
5. Cover with another layer of pastry dough
6. Bake in the oven
