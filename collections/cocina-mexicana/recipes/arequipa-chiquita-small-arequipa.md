## Ingredients

- 6 cuartillos milk
- 4 pounds sugar
- 4 ounces starch
- 6 almonds
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Combine six cuartillos of milk, four pounds of sugar, four ounces of starch, six almonds and orange blossom water.
