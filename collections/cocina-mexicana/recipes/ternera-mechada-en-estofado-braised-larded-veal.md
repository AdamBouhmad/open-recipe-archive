## Ingredients

- Veal loin or leg, well drained of blood
- Strips of ham
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Garlic head teeth
- Cinnamon
- Ginger
- Good vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. After the veal meat from the loin or leg is well drained of blood, lard it with strips of ham, black pepper, and cloves
2. Grind clove, pepper, the teeth of a head of garlic, cinnamon and ginger, and dissolve these spices in good vinegar with a little water
3. Place the meat there and leave it in infusion until the next day, taking care that the pot is well covered
4. The next day early, add sufficient water and salt, two or three onions in quarters and the teeth of a head of garlic chopped
5. Cover the pot with a cazuela, sealing the edges with paste and thick paper, and place it at a regular fire stirring the pot from time to time so it does not burn
6. After six hours of cooking, uncover the pot, turn it into a cazuela and add more good vinegar, adding cinnamon, clove and pepper in whole grains
7. Thicken with toasted and ground bread, and add small chorizos and pieces of ham, all cooked, olives, cured tornachiles, peeled almonds and raisins
8. Mitigate the vinegar with a piece of sugar to taste, and when the broth has thickened, loosen it by adding a glass of white wine and removing immediately from the heat
