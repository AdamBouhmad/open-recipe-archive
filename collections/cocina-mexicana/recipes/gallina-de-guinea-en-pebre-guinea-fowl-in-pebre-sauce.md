## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- lard
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- bay leaves
- raw oil
- water
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the guinea fowl and remove entrails
2. Place on a grill, anointing frequently with melted lard, lemon juice, salt and crushed garlic
3. When half roasted, place in a casserole with lard, chopped parsley, pepper, salt, two or three bay leaves, the fat left from roasting, half a cup of raw oil and enough water to cover it
4. Let it boil until tender
5. Remove the guinea fowl
6. Dissolve eight egg yolks in the broth, beating them well
