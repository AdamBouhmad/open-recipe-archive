## Ingredients

- Turkey giblets
- Lard
- Turnips
- Flour
- Broth
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as already stated, cook like pipila in adobo
2. Once cooked, fry some turnips in lard until they take on good color and remove them from the lard
3. In the same lard add a spoonful of flour, make a paste with them and dissolve it in the broth of the giblets, cook until it thickens, strain and let the turnips cook in this sauce
4. Add a lump of sugar, place the giblets on a platter, garnish and cover with the turnips, and pour the sauce over everything
