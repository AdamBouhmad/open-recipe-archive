## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- coconut, finely ground in the metate
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk
2. As it thickens, continue adding more milk until it reaches the proper consistency
3. Grind the coconut finely in the metate
4. Add the ground coconut to the rice and milk, letting everything boil together
5. Finally, add four egg yolks beaten in milk with the necessary sugar
