## Ingredients

- one hundred walnut rinds at two-thirds their size
- twenty-four cuartillos of good aguardiente
- half a dram of nutmeg
- half a dram of clove
- sugar as needed

## Directions

1. When walnuts have reached two-thirds of their size, cut them, selecting one hundred of the finest ones
2. Dry them, pierce them with a sufficiently thick pin
3. Flatten and crush them in a marble mortar
4. Leave them in infusion for two months in twenty-four cuartillos of good aguardiente, which is aromatized with half a dram of nutmeg and another half dram of clove
5. After straining all the aguardiente laden with bitter and aromatic substance through a cloth or sieve without pressing the pomace, dissolve the necessary sugar to sweeten it
6. Leave the infusion to continue for another two months
7. At the end of this time, filter the liquor again and store it in well-sealed bottles
