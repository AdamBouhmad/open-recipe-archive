## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- eight egg yolks
- half a pound of soaked and ground almond
- one large cooked and ground pineapple
- preserved pineapple slices
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Mix eight cuartillos of very sweetened milk with eight egg yolks and half a pound of soaked and ground almond dissolved in milk
2. Strain the mixture and place over heat
3. When close to reaching the proper point, add one large cooked and ground pineapple
4. Allow to boil until it has the proper consistency
5. Pour half into a platter
6. Place on top drained preserved pineapple slices, cut into quarters
7. Cover everything with the other half of the milk
8. When cold, dust with ground cinnamon
