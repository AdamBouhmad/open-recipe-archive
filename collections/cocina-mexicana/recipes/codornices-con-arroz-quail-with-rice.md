## Ingredients

- quail
- concentrated broth
- small chorizo
- rice
- fresh butter
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and gather the quail, which will be cooked with concentrated broth and small sausages.
2. Once everything is cooked, harden the small sausages with the rice, adding a little fresh butter.
3. Arrange the rice with the quail on top, let it set, and pour Spanish sauce over it.
