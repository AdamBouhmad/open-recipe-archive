## Ingredients

- Onions
- olive oil
- white beans
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut onions into slices and sauté in oil.
2. Add the beans with chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
3. After everything has cooked a while, place on a plate.
4. Pour a little vinegar that has been heated in a pot over the beans.
