## Ingredients

- ribs
- ham slices
- lard
- broth
- fried bread
- sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs leaving more meat
2. Lard them finely with very thin ham slices using larding needles
3. Pass them through lard to firm them up or brown them slightly
4. Let the lard drain
5. When cold, arrange the ends and place in a casserole with a little broth
6. Give them a couple of boils
7. Arrange with fried bread and serve with any sauce
