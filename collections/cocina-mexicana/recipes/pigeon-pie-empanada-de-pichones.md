## Ingredients

- fine dough
- pigeons
- beef slices
- ham slices
- fresh butter
- puff pastry or fine dough
- beef broth
- ham broth

## Directions

1. Make a base of fine dough and line a torte pan with it
2. Arrange the pigeons on top, pressing them down the same way as partridges in torte
3. Cover with beef slices, ham slices, and fresh butter
4. Place another sheet of the same dough or puff pastry on top
5. Complete the operation as for common empanadas, browning and baking in the oven
6. When cooked, remove the grease and take away the beef, ham, and filling
7. Pour clear strained beef and ham broth over the pigeons
8. Serve hot
