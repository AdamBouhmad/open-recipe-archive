## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- bread
- Spanish sauce or lard
- white wine
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the outer skin from the kidneys
2. Open them lengthwise and sauté them keeping them separated from each other, adding salt and pepper
3. Turn them until well cooked
4. Separately fry pieces of bread the size of the kidneys in equal quantity
5. Place on a platter with each piece of kidney alongside a fried piece of bread
6. Pour Spanish sauce over them, or if lacking that, a roux of lard moistened with half a glass of white wine
7. At the moment of serving add lemon juice or a few drops of vinegar
