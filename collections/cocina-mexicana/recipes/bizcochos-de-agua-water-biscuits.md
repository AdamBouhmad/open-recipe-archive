## Ingredients

- twenty-six egg yolks
- thirteen egg whites
- two ounces of ground sugar
- two ounces of cold lard
- a piece of leavening
- flour well sifted
- a little fine ground salt

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks and whites with the ground sugar and cold lard
2. Dissolve a piece of leavening with thirteen egg whites and mix with the sifted flour and fine ground salt
3. Mix everything together, beat perfectly
4. Increase the flour as necessary until the dough is of such consistency that rosquillas can be formed
5. Put them to cook in boiling water until they float to the surface
