## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- Spanish sauce (five spoonfuls)
- concentrated broth (two spoonfuls)

## Directions

1. Divide the cucumbers lengthwise into four parts
2. Clean them and give them a pleasing appearance
3. Scald them and let them drain on cloth
4. Arrange them in a pan and pour five spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and two of concentrated broth over them
5. Place the cucumbers over the fire
6. Half an hour before serving, bring them to boil and cook
7. Dress them immediately, pouring the liquid over them, and if it is too loose, let it reduce a little
