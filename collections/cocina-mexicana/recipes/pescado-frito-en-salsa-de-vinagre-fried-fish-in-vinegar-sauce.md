## Ingredients

- fish, cleaned and cut
- flour
- lard
- bay leaf
- clove
- pepper
- thyme
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- strong vinegar
- sugar
- oregano

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the fish, dry it and roll it in flour, fry it in lard until it browns.
2. Boil bay leaf, clove, pepper and thyme in equal parts water and vinegar with the necessary salt.
3. When it has boiled well, add a little more strong vinegar sweetened with a little sugar.
4. After it comes to a boil, remove from heat and place on hot coals until serving with a little oregano sprinkled on top.
