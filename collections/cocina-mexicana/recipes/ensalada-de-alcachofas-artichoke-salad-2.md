## Ingredients

- artichokes
- oil
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- Castilian vinegar
- chiles
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Wash the artichokes first
2. Remove the top leaves and cut the tail and tips
3. Cook until half done with salt and drain
4. Fry a little oil with garlic until it browns
5. Grind a little pepper, clove, and cinnamon and mix with the rest
6. In a regular pot place the artichokes face up
7. Cover with oil until it rises two fingers above the artichokes and add a spoonful of Castilian vinegar
8. Cover the pot with cloth
9. When serving decorate with vinegar, chiles, capers, and olives
