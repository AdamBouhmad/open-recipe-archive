## Ingredients

- Three pounds of syrup prepared at strong flower point
- Four pounds of ripe blackberries, whole

## Directions

1. Add the ripe blackberries to the syrup prepared at strong flower point
2. Give them a single slow boil so they do not break apart
3. Remove from heat and let rest in an earthenware basin with its syrup until the next day
4. Strain the syrup through a sieve
5. Cook the syrup alone to raise its point
6. When at the right point, return the blackberries to it
7. If the syrup is not sufficient, add a bit more prepared at the same point
8. Let cool and store
