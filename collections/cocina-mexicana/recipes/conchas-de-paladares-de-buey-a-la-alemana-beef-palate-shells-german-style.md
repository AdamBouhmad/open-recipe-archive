## Ingredients

- Beef palates
- German sauce (very thick)
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Parsley
- Lemon
- Bread crumbs
- Grated cheese
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut palates into dice and place in very thick German sauce with an equal amount of mushrooms, some butter, parsley, and lemon
2. Distribute everything in shells
3. Sprinkle with grated bread and cheese
4. Sprinkle with melted butter
5. Brown under a bell oven
6. Arrange and serve
