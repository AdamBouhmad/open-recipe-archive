## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 1 pound refined white sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 7 egg yolks
- a little water
- 1 cup butter

## Directions

1. Add to the flour the refined white sugar well ground, 2 egg whites and 7 egg yolks, a little water and 1 cup of butter
2. Mix everything gently without overworking the batter
3. When well incorporated, form the cake or cut the biscuits
4. Make some lines on top with the knife like pan de manteca (butter bread)
5. Bake in the oven
