## Ingredients

- dried apricots
- green lemon peels
- orange pulp
- orange marmalade
- cream
- milk
- rennet
- cream
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar some dried apricots, green lemon peels, orange pulp and marmalade in equal parts
2. Pass this mixture through a sieve and dissolve in cream mixed with milk
3. Warm and add the rennet
4. When the cheese is curdled, drain as is customary and serve with cream around it and fine sugar
