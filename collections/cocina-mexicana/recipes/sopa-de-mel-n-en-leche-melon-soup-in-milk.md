## Ingredients

- melon (peeled)
- milk
- sugar (to taste)
- cinnamon sticks
- bread (fried in butter)
- sweet macaroni
- almonds
- candied flowers
- bitter almond sweets

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled melon in milk, sweetening to taste and mixing in some small cinnamon sticks
2. Pass everything through a sieve and pour over slices of bread fried in butter
3. Decorate with sweet macaroni, almonds, candied flowers and bitter almond sweets
4. Serve without boiling once the bread is added
