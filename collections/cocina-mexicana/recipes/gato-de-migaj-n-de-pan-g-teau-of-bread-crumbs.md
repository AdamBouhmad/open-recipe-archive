## Ingredients

- bread crumbs from soft bread
- cream
- butter
- lemon peel
- sugar
- raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Crumble bread crumbs and throw them in boiling cream
2. Stir and turn, and let boiling continue
3. Then add a piece of butter, lemon peel, and sugar, and if desired also raisins
4. Finish the operation as for rice gâteau
