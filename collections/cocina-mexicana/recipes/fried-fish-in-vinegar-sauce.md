## Ingredients

- Fish
- Flour
- Lard
- Bay leaves
- Clove
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the fish, dry it and roll it in flour, fry it in lard until it browns
2. Boil bay leaves, clove, pepper and thyme in equal parts of water and vinegar with the corresponding salt
3. When it has boiled well, add another bit of strong vinegar, sweetened with a little sugar
4. Once it gives a boil, remove from heat and place it in the embers until serving it with a little of the sauce
