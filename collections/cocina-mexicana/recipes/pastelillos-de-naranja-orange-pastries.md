## Ingredients

- One pound of fine flour
- Seven egg yolks and one egg white
- Half a cup of refined brandy
- Half a cup of anise water
- Juice of one and a half oranges
- A little dissolved tequesquite
- Melted lard
- A little yeast

## Directions

1. Make the dough with flour, egg yolks and white, brandy, anise water, orange juice, tequesquite, melted lard, and yeast
2. Knead everything until it forms blisters or bubbles and let it rest
3. Form the pastries, fry them, and fill with sweet or milk cream
4. Dust with cinnamon or sugar
