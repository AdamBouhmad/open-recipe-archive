## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the milk until it becomes like rags, and in syrup at a point less than hard crack, well stirred until it cools
2. add the milk with egg yolks and stir over fire until it reaches the right consistency
3. place on papers with dragées on top, so that when cold they can be cut as desired
