## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- poblano green chiles
- lard
- water
- fresh cheese
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- bread for toasting
- butter

## Directions

1. Roast tomatoes and poblano green chiles well
2. Peel both, making the chiles red and removing the veins, and chopping the tomatoes very finely, removing the hard heart
3. Put a casserole on the fire with plenty of lard
4. Fry the tomatoes and chiles there, and when everything is well fried, add the necessary water, letting it boil on strong heat until it thickens a little
5. Then add the eggs one by one so they set and on top some slices of fresh good cheese and some hard-boiled egg yolks
6. Let it boil until the broth thickens well
7. Serve adorned with toast bread, fried in butter
