## Ingredients

- Young wild boar (milk-fed or suckling)
- Vinegar
- Sage
- Garlic
- Basil
- Laurel
- Thyme
- Salt
- Green coriander
- Juniper berries
- Pepper
- Clove
- Onion
- Parsley
- Paper, tripled and buttered

## Directions

1. Skin the young wild boar up to the shoulder blade
2. Cut the skin so that the head takes the form of a sea urchin
3. Twist the legs like those of a little pig
4. Compose and prick it below the knuckle of the shank
5. Let it marinate for twenty-four hours in vinegar
6. Season with sage, garlic, basil, laurel, thyme, salt, green coriander, juniper berries, pepper, clove, onion and parsley
7. Place in the roaster
8. Wrap the head with tripled paper that is buttered
9. Let it roast for three hours
10. Remove from the roaster and serve on a large plate with a good pepper sauce or pebre
11. Alternatively, it can be scalded up to the shoulder blades as with a young pig, then without marinating, it can be roasted and served with clear broth or gravy
