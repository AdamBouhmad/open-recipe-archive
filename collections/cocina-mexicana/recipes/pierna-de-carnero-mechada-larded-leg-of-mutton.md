## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- peeled garlic cloves
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- lard
- sufficient water
- salt
- herbs for flavoring
- fine ground spices
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the leg being careful not to damage it
2. Stud it with peeled garlic cloves, cloves, cinnamon, and pepper
3. Place in a pan with lard, sufficient water, necessary salt, aromatic herbs, fine ground spices, and a little vinegar
4. Cook over two fires
5. The broth should reduce until nearly dry
