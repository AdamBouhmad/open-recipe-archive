## Ingredients

- one pound of almond
- twelve ounces of sugar
- a little candied orange blossom or jasmine
- egg white

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds and mix with twelve ounces of sugar and a little candied orange blossom or jasmine
2. Pour everything into a vessel and moisten with enough egg white so that it can be spread with the blade of a knife onto metal sheets, prepared the same way as in the previous article
3. Spread the paste as thinly as possible, sprinkling finely chopped almonds mixed with sugar on top
4. Put them in the oven as indicated for German-style wafers in the preceding article, proceeding the same way
