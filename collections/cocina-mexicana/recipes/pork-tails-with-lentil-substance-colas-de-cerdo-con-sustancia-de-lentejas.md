## Ingredients

- Five or six pork tails
- lentils
- two onions
- one or two cloves
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare five or six tails, leaving the skin and cutting them seven to eight inches from the thickest end
2. Clean and char the tails over flames
3. Cook with lentils, two onions, one or two cloves and broth
4. Once the tails are cooked, place in a saucepan with a little broth
5. Strain the lentils through a sieve and place the substance in another saucepan
6. Skim and clarify, letting it reduce a little if too watery
7. Arrange the tails on a plate
8. Cover with the substance and serve
