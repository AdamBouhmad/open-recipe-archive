## Ingredients

- two ounces of powdered coffee
- four quartillos of milk
- two quartillos of custard
- three egg yolks
- four ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Boil two ounces of powdered coffee in four quartillos of milk with two quartillos of custard
2. After a few minutes, strain
3. Add three egg yolks, well dissolved, and four ounces of powdered sugar
4. Allow to reduce by half, strain, cool, and serve
