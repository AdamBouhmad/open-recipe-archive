## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- very thin bread slices

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to boil twice so it remains not too thick.
2. Sweeten with sufficient sugar.
3. When cold, pour over very thin bread slices in a dish or pot.
4. Place the pot over hot coals to rest.
