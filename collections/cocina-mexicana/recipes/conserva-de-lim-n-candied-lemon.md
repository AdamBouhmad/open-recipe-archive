## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Ash or tequesquite

## Directions

1. Cook the lemons with ash, or if lighter lemons are desired, with tequesquite, covering the pot with cooking straw
2. Change the water repeatedly, always hot, removing the pulp and keeping them in it until they lose their bitterness
3. Place them in clarified syrup and allow them to reach the proper stage
