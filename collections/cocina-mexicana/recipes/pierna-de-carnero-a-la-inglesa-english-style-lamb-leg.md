## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- salt
- parsnips
- turnips
- butter
- oil

## Directions

1. Wrap the leg in fine linen cloth
2. Boil in water for two hours with a handful of coarse salt
3. Cook parsnips and turnips in the same water
4. Unwrap the leg and arrange on a plate with the vegetables around it
5. Place a sauceboat on the table with melted butter and an oil cruet for each guest to choose as they prefer
