## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- Garlic cloves, unpeeled
- Tomatoes
- Parsley
- Water
- Castilian oregano
- Pepper
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb into pieces and wash it well, then fry with whole unpeeled garlic cloves.
2. When half fried, add tomatoes and finely minced parsley.
3. Once fried, transfer to a pot with sufficient water to cook.
4. Add a little rubbed Castilian oregano, ground pepper and clove, a little vinegar, cover the pot tightly and let it boil until well cooked.
5. Fry bread slices until they brown deeply, grind them, and add to the pot to thicken it.
