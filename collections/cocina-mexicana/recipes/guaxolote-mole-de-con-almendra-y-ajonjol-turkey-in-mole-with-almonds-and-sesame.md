## Ingredients

- Half pound ancho chiles, deveined
- Lard
- Handful of almonds
- Handful of sesame seeds
- Broth from the cooked turkey
- Salt
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Biscuit
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Turkey, cooked in water
- Piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Toast half a pound of deveined ancho chiles in lard.
2. Separately toast a handful of almonds and an equal handful of sesame seeds.
3. Grind everything and fry it.
4. Add broth from the turkey and appropriate salt.
5. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper, and biscuit.
6. Dissolve the ground mixture in equal parts wine and vinegar.
7. Add the pieces of turkey cooked in water, the ground spices, and a piece of sugar.
8. Let it thicken and serve.
