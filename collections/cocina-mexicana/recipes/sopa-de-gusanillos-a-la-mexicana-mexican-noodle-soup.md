## Ingredients

- Flour
- One egg
- Salt
- Milk
- Lard
- Chard
- Onions
- Xitomatl (tomatoes)
- Pot broth
- Grated cheese
- A little vinegar
- Butter
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Clove
- Hard boiled eggs
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Mix flour with an egg, add salt and knead with milk, moistening the hand with lard to soften the dough
2. Roll out this dough with a rolling pin forming strips two or three fingers wide
3. Cut the noodles very finely with a pasta maker or knife
4. Fry in lard the chard, onions and tomatoes, all chopped very finely
5. Season after frying with salt, saffron, pepper and clove
6. Add pot broth, grated cheese, a little vinegar and a little butter
7. Let the broth boil to bind it
8. Put the noodles in it, cover the pot and place it at the mouth of another pot to receive the steam so the noodles puff up
9. Serve this soup with slices of hard boiled eggs, chiles and olives
