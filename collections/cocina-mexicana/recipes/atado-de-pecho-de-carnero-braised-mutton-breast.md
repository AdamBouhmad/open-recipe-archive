## Ingredients

- Mutton breast
- Bread crumbs
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. The best way to prepare this dish is to boil it well first in a pot.
2. When it is sufficiently cooked, remove it from the pot, let it cool, pass it through warm water, and cover with bread crumbs.
3. Then let it cook on the grill only the time necessary for it to take color, turning it once or twice, and serve with Spanish sauce.
4. It can also be served with any type of purées.
