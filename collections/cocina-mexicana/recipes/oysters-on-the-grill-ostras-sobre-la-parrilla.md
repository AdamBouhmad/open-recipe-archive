## Ingredients

- two dozen oysters
- butter
- shallots
- pepper
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove two dozen oysters from their shells and place in a pan
2. Parboil them in their own water
3. Remove this water and add a piece of butter with chopped shallots and a little pepper
4. Make them jump without boiling
5. Place them back in their own shells
6. Add lemon juice
7. Bread them together with the scraping from the pan
8. Place on the grill
9. At the instant the oysters boil in their shells, remove and serve
