## Ingredients

- unripe grapes (agraz), slightly more ripe than the previous type but very green in color
- pincho (pointed tool)
- clarified syrup (at ball point)

## Directions

1. Choose unripe grapes somewhat more ripe than those in the previous article, but that retain their very green color
2. Remove the skin or peel and the pits with a very pointed tool
3. Make clarified syrup at ball point
