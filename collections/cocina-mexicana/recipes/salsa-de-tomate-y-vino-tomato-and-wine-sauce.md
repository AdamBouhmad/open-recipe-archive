## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- lard
- cloves
- pepper
- thyme
- white wine

## Directions

1. Chop tomatoes very finely and fry in lard
2. Add ground cloves and pepper mixed with thyme and white wine
3. Allow to season until it thickens properly and tastes good
4. This sauce is good for pork loin and young pigeons
