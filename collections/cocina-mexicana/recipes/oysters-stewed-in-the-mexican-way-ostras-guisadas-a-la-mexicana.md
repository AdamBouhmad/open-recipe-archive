## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- roasted tomatoes
- green chiles
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- capers
- oil
- vinegar
- oyster cooking water
- flour

## Directions

1. If fresh, cook with salt; but if in brine, desalt before cooking
2. Fry in lard chopped garlic and onions, roasted and ground tomatoes, and green chiles in strips
3. Season with salt, clove, pepper, saffron, and cumin, all ground
4. Add capers, oil, vinegar, a little of the warm water in which the oysters were cooked, and flour browned in lard
5. Add the oysters and let them boil until the sauce is seasoned and somewhat thick
