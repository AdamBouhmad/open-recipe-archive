## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Butter
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Water
- Cloves
- Garlic
- Shallots
- Small onions
- Parsley
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil for frying
- Fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Make a marinade with about half an egg of butter mixed with flour, half a glass of vinegar, a large glass of water, cloves, a head of garlic, shallots, small onions, parsley, bay leaf, thyme, basil, salt and pepper.
2. Warm this marinade, stirring the butter until it melts, then add the sweetbreads and remove from heat to marinate for an hour and a half to two hours.
3. Drain and pat dry with a cloth, roll in flour and fry, allowing them to take on a good color or become golden.
4. Serve with fried parsley around them.
