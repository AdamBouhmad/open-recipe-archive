## Ingredients

- cow brains
- water
- fine butter
- flour
- salad herbs
- salt
- pepper
- tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare two parts of the brain set, removing the third part called the knot
2. Place in hot water, remove the skin with two fingers without breaking the brains and place to soak in water
3. When free of blood, place in boiling water for five minutes
4. Refresh and cook in plain water for three quarters of an hour
5. When cooked, drain and arrange on the serving plate
6. Heat four ounces of fine butter with a scant regular spoonful of flour and a regular quantity of the herbs added to salads
7. Knead everything together with a wooden spoon, mixing salt, pepper, a splash of tarragon vinegar and a little water
8. Place the pan on the fire, stir the sauce turning it to bind it well, and cover the brains with it
