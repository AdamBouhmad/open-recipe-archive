## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one pound of white sweet potato, ground
- half a pound of peeled almonds, ground
- half a coconut, ground
- one small pot of custard

## Directions

1. Make clarified half-point syrup with three pounds of sugar
2. Add one pound of white sweet potato, half a pound of peeled and ground almonds, and half a coconut, all ground
3. As soon as this mixture begins to reach the point of cajeta, add one small pot of custard
4. Leave over heat until it reaches the point of separating from the pan, then remove to fill the candy molds
