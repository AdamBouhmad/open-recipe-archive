## Ingredients

- Frog legs
- Eggs for omelet
- Chile or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the frog legs by blanching and chilling in cold water
2. Make a common omelet with eggs
3. Mix the prepared frog legs into the omelet
4. Serve with chile or tomato sauce on the side
