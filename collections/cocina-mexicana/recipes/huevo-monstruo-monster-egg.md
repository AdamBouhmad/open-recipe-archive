## Ingredients

- eggs
- bladder

## Directions

1. Break a dozen eggs and separate yolks from whites
2. Take a clean bladder and fill it with well-beaten yolks
3. Tie very well so air does not enter
4. Suspend in boiling water until yolks become firm
5. Remove, cool and carefully break open
6. Taking another larger bladder, add the whites placing the already set yolks in the middle
7. Cook again and when ready, remove and break the bladder; the egg will be formed
8. Serve in poultry sauce or in slices
