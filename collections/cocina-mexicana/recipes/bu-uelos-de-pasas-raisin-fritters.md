## Ingredients

- Raisins
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten as for frying
- Lard for frying
- Orange blossom water
- Clarified syrup
- Grajea (sugar pearls)
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Cook the raisins in water and pit them
2. Grind well and mix with a little flour
3. Dissolve this mixture in beaten eggs as for frying
4. After mixing well, take spoonfuls with a small spoon and fry portions in lard
5. Remove and serve with a few drops of orange blossom water and clarified syrup
6. Garnish with grajea and toasted sesame seeds on top
