## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- eight egg yolks
- half a quarter of a quart of custard for each pound of sugar
- a little milk
- sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- syrup at high point
- mamey
- grated nutmeg
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make syrup of slightly more than medium point with one pound of sugar for every eight egg yolks
2. Add the yolks and, stirring very well, let it boil at low heat
3. Add then half a quarter of a quart of custard for each pound of sugar and bring it to the point of separating from the pan
4. Cook a little milk with sugar and cinnamon sticks
5. Mix with high point syrup
6. With this mixture wet the mamey to place layers of it, arranging on them the layers of custard
7. Sprinkle all with grated nutmeg and the last layer will be adorned with raisins and almonds
