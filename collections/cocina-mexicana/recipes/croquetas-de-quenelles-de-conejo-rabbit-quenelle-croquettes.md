## Ingredients

- Rabbit quenelles
- Velouté sauce
- Game essence
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Poach the rabbit quenelles
2. Let them drain
3. Pour somewhat reduced velouté sauce mixed with game essence over them
4. Bind with egg yolks
5. Once the mixture is cold, divide into portions
6. Coat with breadcrumbs
7. Bread by dipping in beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper
8. Bread again and fry
9. Serve with fried parsley
