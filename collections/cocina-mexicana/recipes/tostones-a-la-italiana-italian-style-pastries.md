## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- egg yolks
- butter
- water
- cooked filling
- good grated cheese
- black pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. For a quarter liter of flour, a little salt, four egg yolks, four ounces of butter and a little water make a dough that is not very stiff
2. Flatten it with a rolling pin until the leaves are thin
3. Fill them with some cooked filling and good grated cheese, some black pepper and nutmeg and two egg yolks
4. Proceed as for the previous articles
