## Ingredients

- Four fat hens
- Twelve ounces of lean ham, cut in pieces
- Two turnips, in pieces
- Three carrots
- One onion
- One dozen cloves

## Directions

1. Kill four fat hens the day before they are to be used. Clean them well and cook them in a clean pot with the following: twelve ounces of lean ham cut in pieces, two turnips in pieces, three carrots, one onion, and a dozen cloves placed in the onion.
