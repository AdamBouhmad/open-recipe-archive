## Ingredients

- Thirty eggs
- One pound of white sugar
- Six pounds of flour without yeast or salt
- Half a quart of white wine
- One pound of melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat the thirty eggs with one pound of white sugar
2. Mix with six pounds of flour without yeast or salt
3. Add half a quart of white wine and one pound of melted butter
4. From each pound of dough, make five rosquetes
5. After forming, strain them in boiling water until they float to the surface
6. Remove with a skimmer to drain
7. Place in an oven tempered as for previous rosquetes, with vents and opening uncovered
8. After cooking, remove and let rest about a quarter hour
9. Return to oven with vents and opening now covered
10. When hard, remove and coat with icing, then return to an almost cold oven
