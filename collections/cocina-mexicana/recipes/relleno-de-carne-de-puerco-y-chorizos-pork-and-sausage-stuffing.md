## Ingredients

- pork loin
- chorizo sausages
- salted water
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- green chile
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- cumin
- parsley
- broth
- almonds
- raisins
- pine nuts
- capers
- olives
- ham
- egg
- vinegar
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and mince pork loin
2. Form into balls and cook in salted water along with chorizo sausages
3. Remove from water
4. In a pan with lard, fry minced garlic, onions, tomatoes and green chile, all finely chopped
5. After frying this mixture, add the minced pork and the sausages with their casings removed and meat crumbled
6. Season with ground pepper, clove, saffron and cumin
7. Add finely chopped parsley, a little broth, almonds cut into quarters, raisins, pine nuts and capers
8. When the broth is consumed and the stuffing becomes dry, add halved and pitted olives, small pieces of ham and cooked egg
9. Add a little vinegar and oil
10. Ensure it has sufficient salt and is well seasoned and tasty
11. This stuffing is used for curdled cakes and to stuff pork loin, veal cutlets and other dishes, vegetables, etc.
