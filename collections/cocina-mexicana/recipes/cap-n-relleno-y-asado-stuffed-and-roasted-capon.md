## Ingredients

- 1 capon with its liver
- 12-15 cooked chestnuts
- small onions
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Make a hash with the capon's liver, 12-15 cooked chestnuts, small onions and parsley; season with salt and pepper and add 2 egg yolks, mixing everything well.
2. Stuff the capon with this hash and wrap it in buttered paper, place it on the spit.
3. When well roasted, remove the paper; gild it with egg, cover it with breadcrumbs so it takes color over a strong fire, and serve with spicy sauce.
