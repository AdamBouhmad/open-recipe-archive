## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- four egg whites, well beaten
- one pound of finely ground almonds
- a little sponge cake or biscuit
- butter for greasing
- ground almonds
- toasted sesame
- two cloves
- crumbled marquesote biscuit, ground and chilled
- white wine
- cinnamon
- raisins
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Beat twenty egg yolks until stiff
2. Mix with four egg whites beaten like for nougat
3. Mix with one pound of very finely ground almonds
4. Add a little sponge cake or biscuit and incorporate well
5. Pour the batter into a buttered pan placed over hot coals
6. Cover with a comal and place more hot coals on top
7. Cook until golden
8. Cut the cake into small pieces while hot and immerse in syrup
9. Allow to boil a few times in the syrup
10. Place on a platter and decorate with raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
11. Dust with ground grajea and cinnamon
