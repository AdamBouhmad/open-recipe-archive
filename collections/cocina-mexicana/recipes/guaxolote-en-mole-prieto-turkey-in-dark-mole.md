## Ingredients

- Chilhuacle chiles or mulato chiles (deveined)
- One tortilla
- Chile heads
- One tablet of chocolate
- Three or four roasted garlic cloves
- One roasted tomatillo
- Two handfuls of cooked tomatoes
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- Turkey meat
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Biscuit
- A pinch of sugar

## Directions

1. Toast the chilhuacle or mulato deveined chiles and also toast one tortilla
2. Burn some chile heads and add one tablet of chocolate, three or four roasted garlic cloves, one roasted tomatillo, and two handfuls of cooked tomatoes
3. Grind everything well, fry in lard, add the corresponding water and salt
4. Place the pieces of raw turkey fried into the sauce and let them boil until cooked
5. When done, add cloves, cinnamon, and black pepper, all ground with a little biscuit and a pinch of sugar
6. Let it boil until it thickens well like an adobe sauce
