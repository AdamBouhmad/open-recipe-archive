## Ingredients

- beef sirloin slices
- melted butter
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Arrange the sirloin slices as in the previous articles
2. Place in a sauté pan with melted butter, sprinkled with salt and coarse pepper
3. Make them jump at the moment of serving and arrange in the form of a crown
