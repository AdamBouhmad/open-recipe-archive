## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- butter
- onion slices
- wine
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Harden eggs in water, and when cold, slice them
2. Put a casserole with butter and fry onion slices in it
3. When well fried, add a little wine
4. Then put the egg slices and let them boil
5. When they have boiled a little, add three or four hard-boiled egg yolks dissolved in a little wine
6. Let them boil, and having thickened a little, season with salt and nutmeg
