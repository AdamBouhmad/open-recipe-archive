## Ingredients

- seven egg whites
- two ounces of flour
- ground and sifted sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix seven egg whites with two ounces of flour and ground, sifted sugar to taste.
2. Mix everything very well and strain.
3. In a baking dish, place a layer of sugar and another of mixture, continuing alternately.
4. Cook over two gentle fires and let brown.
