## Ingredients

- eighteen eggs, separated
- twelve ounces of powdered and sifted sugar
- one pound of powdered and sifted starch
- four ounces of sifted sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat eighteen eggs, keeping whites and yolks separate
2. While beating the yolks, gradually mix in twelve ounces of ground and sifted sugar
3. When thick, mix with the beaten whites
4. While beating together, dust with one pound of ground and sifted starch using a small brush until completely absorbed
5. Once all ingredients are well incorporated, place small portions on papers or baking sheets with a spoon
6. Quickly dust with four ounces of sifted sugar over the top
7. Put in a oven that is not too hot
