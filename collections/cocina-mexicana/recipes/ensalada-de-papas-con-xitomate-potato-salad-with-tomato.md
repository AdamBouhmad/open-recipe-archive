## Ingredients

- cooked potato
- onion, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- roasted and peeled tomatoes
- vinegar
- cooked onion
- chopped garlic
- avocado, peeled
- oil
- salt
- oregano

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked potatoes and stir in very finely chopped onion; sprinkle with salt, pepper and oregano; grind some roasted and peeled tomatoes, and dilute them with vinegar; remove the layers from some cooked onions and considerable chopped garlic, plenty of peeled avocado slices, stirring everything with the vinegar and tomato, and after stirring, add oil with sufficient salt; on top garnish with avocado quarters and sprinkled oregano.
