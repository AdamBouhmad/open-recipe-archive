## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of flour
- three egg yolks
- half fresh cheese
- small butter
- one cup of water with tequesquite
- half cup of melted lard
- salt
- filling of fresh cheese
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- syrup
- white wine
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Knead one and a half pounds of flour with three egg yolks, half fresh cheese, small butter, one cup of settled tequesquite water, half cup of melted lard, and a little salt
2. After kneading well, extend with a roller and cut out the fritters with an inverted hot cup
3. Fill with a paste of fresh cheese, powdered sugar, cinnamon, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
4. Cover the filling with another layer of dough, seal the edges and make a small indentation in the middle with the bottom of a cup
5. Fry and put in syrup boiled with white wine, cinnamon, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
