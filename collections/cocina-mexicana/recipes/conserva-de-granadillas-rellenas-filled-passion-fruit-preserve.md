## Ingredients

- Granadillas (China passion fruit)
- Lime water (agua de cal)
- Syrup (almíbar)
- Fig leaves
- Coconut paste

## Directions

1. Immediately after peeling, empty them through an opening made at the crown
2. Proceed as explained in the previous article for granadillas
3. When already preserved, fill them with coconut paste and finish the same way as with filled oranges
