## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks in cups without filling them, lined with butter
2. Add a little flour powder just to give it some body, and a little salt
3. Beat this very well and pour into molds
4. Place the molds in another pot with very hot lard that covers them
5. When fried, remove from molds and coat with syrup
