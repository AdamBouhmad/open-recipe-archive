## Ingredients

- Three pounds white peach
- Four pounds sugar
- Lemon

## Directions

1. After three pounds of white peach are well cooked, cut into slices and let drain
2. Make syrup with four pounds sugar, clarify it
3. When at medium point, remove and beat until it cools
4. Add the peach slices and return to heat until it reaches point
