## Ingredients

- hare
- hot water
- strong vinegar
- salt
- onion
- oil
- bread
- garlic
- parsley
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- capers
- olives
- tornachiles
- butter

## Directions

1. Skin the hare, wash it with boiling water, then with strong vinegar
2. Put to cook with salt and onion
3. When well cooked, remove and dry thoroughly
4. Put to fry in oil with salt; when it begins to take color, set aside
5. In a pot with butter, brown a slice of bread, then remove it
6. Fry plenty of onion, garlic and a little parsley, all chopped
7. Quench with water
8. Add cloves, pepper and cinnamon, ground together with the toasted bread, all dissolved in a bowl of good vinegar
9. Season with sufficient salt
10. Cut the hare into portions and add to the sauce
11. Add capers, olives and tornachiles
12. When the sauce is well thickened, add the oil in which the hare was fried
13. Set aside to rest in a warm place
