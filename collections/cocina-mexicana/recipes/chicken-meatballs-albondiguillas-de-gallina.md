## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- chicken fat
- soaked bread
- cooked egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- raw egg
- breadcrumbs
- poultry broth
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Take the breasts and mince finely with some chicken fat
2. Mix the minced meat with soaked bread and cooked egg yolks, salt, pepper, and ground cinnamon
3. Beat everything in raw egg until it has sufficient consistency
4. Form the meatballs into round shapes
5. Bread them all around
6. Fry them
7. Cook them in poultry broth
8. Serve with white sauce
