## Ingredients

- Rooster crests
- Rooster kidneys
- Water
- Salt
- Rich broth
- Lemon juice
- Flour
- Oil for frying
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the crests at the base of the head
2. Soak briefly in warm water to drain blood
3. Wash well
4. Boil in water until the skin can be easily removed with a knife without breaking
5. Dry and cook in rich broth
6. When nearly cooked, squeeze lemon juice into the broth
7. When nearly done, add the cleaned and soaked kidneys
8. When cooked, remove and cut into one-inch pieces
9. Roll in flour and fry in oil
10. Sprinkle with a little salt and pepper to serve as garnish
