## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar
- Water
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Divide quinces into quarters and remove the cores
2. Cook with their skin in water until well cooked
3. Pass through a sieve and weigh the quantity
4. Cook the cores separately in a little water
5. Mix one part of the resulting jelly with the quince
6. Make a syrup with sugar in proportion of one and a half pounds for each pound of quince
7. Clarify and cook until it reaches the perfect cracking point
8. Add the cooked quince, beating constantly in one direction only
9. Continue beating for a long time until the preserve feels heavy
10. Empty into molds
11. Alternatively, it can be made very white with raw quince, grated in water so it does not take color
12. Once drained, add to syrup that is left to boil until set, mixing in a little orange blossom water when removing from heat
