## Ingredients

- ground coconut
- biscuit
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Make the same as the previous recipe, omitting the egg yolks.
2. After it reaches the point of cajeta, cover it with ground biscuit and cinnamon.
3. Add melted butter on top and cover with a tin sheet or griddle with heat to brown it.
4. Dust with cinnamon powder afterward.
