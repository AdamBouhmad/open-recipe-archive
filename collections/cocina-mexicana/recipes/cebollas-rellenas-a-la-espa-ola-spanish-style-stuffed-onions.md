## Ingredients

- onions
- oil
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the onions and cut off the ends, then roast them in hot coals or under hot ash
2. When roasted, remove the first layer and hollow out a bit from the center, filling with any minced meat
3. Soak in oil, dust generously with breadcrumbs, and place in a pan over two gentle fires, cooking until the breadcrumbs brown
4. Alternatively, raw onions can be filled, rolled in breadcrumbs, moistened occasionally with cooking oil, and baked in the oven
