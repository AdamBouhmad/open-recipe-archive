## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- beaten eggs
- lard
- onions
- garlic
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- saffron
- cloves
- ground pepper
- chicken
- fish
- shrimp patties or roe
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- large capers
- olives
- chile peppers in quarters
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with salt so it remains whole and very dry
2. When cooked and fried, incorporate it with sufficient beaten eggs
3. In a cazuela coated with plenty of lard, pour half of the rice
4. Over it place a layer of seasoned sauce fried in lard made from chopped onions, garlic, tomatoes, and green chiles, seasoned with salt, saffron, cloves, and ground pepper
5. Over this sauce arrange quarters of chicken (raw if very small, cooked if larger), or pieces of cooked fish, or shrimp patties or roe
6. Add raisins, almonds, capers, large capers, olives, and chile peppers in quarters
7. When everything is well arranged, pour the other sauce over it
8. When leveled, add the other layer of remaining rice
9. Over it, when even, place another layer of beaten egg
10. Coat with lard and place over two fires
11. When set and golden, pour sugar on top
12. Another version is made the same way but with rice, sugar, and cinnamon before making the pie
