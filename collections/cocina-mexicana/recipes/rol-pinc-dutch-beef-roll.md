## Ingredients

- six pounds of beef, preferably from the ribs
- beef tripe
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take six pounds of beef, being better from the covered ribs, cut into pieces and mince everything together as for pastry filling, and season with salt, pepper, spices and nutmeg.
2. Prepare in advance a very clean beef tripe which is cut into square pieces of eight inches, more or less, and fill on the interior part with the minced meat; join the extremities of the wrapped pieces and sew them with a large needle.
3. Boil water with a good handful of salt and a quarter of vinegar in a very well-tinned pot, or better in a large pot where all the rolls fit comfortably, which are thrown into the boiling water and left to boil for the space of one hour; they are then drained on a clean cloth, and put in a jar of sandstone or in an unglazed pot, with the necessary vinegar to cover them; do not cover the jar until everything is perfectly cold, and these rolls can be used after fifteen days if you do not employ them all in that time, then leave the remaining ones in the vinegar only after that time.
