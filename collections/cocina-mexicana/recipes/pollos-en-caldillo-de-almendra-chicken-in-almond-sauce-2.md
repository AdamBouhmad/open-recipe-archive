## Ingredients

- garlic cloves, split
- toasted and ground almonds
- toasted bread
- salt
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- raisins
- pine nuts
- wine
- cooked chicken
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Fry split garlic cloves in lard until golden, then remove them
2. In the same fat, fry toasted and ground almonds mixed with toasted bread
3. Season everything with salt, cloves, pepper, and cinnamon, all ground
4. Add raisins, pine nuts, and wine
5. Place the cooked chicken pieces in this sauce with some of the broth in which they were cooked
6. Let simmer until the sauce thickens
