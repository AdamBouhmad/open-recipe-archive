## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic cloves (halved)
- Slices of blood sausage
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Málaga wine
- Cooked hen quarters
- Hen broth

## Directions

1. Fry split garlic cloves and slices of blood sausage in lard.
2. Grind both together with cloves, pepper, and cinnamon.
3. Dissolve the ground mixture in Málaga wine.
4. Return to the lard and add the cooked hen quarters and a little of their broth.
5. Let the broth boil until it reaches regular consistency.
