## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Garlic cloves
- Salt
- Oil
- Onion, chopped
- Black pepper powder
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from the green beans without breaking them and cook them with garlic and salt.
2. In a pot with oil, brown sliced garlic cloves and fry the green beans in the same oil.
3. Arrange them on a platter and add chopped onion and black pepper powder.
4. Add small chiles, olives, and lettuce halved.
