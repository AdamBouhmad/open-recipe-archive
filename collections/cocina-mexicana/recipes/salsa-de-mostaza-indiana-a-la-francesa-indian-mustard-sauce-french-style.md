## Ingredients

- dozen hard egg yolks
- good oil, in quantity equal to the yolks
- one spoonful of saffron
- small chiles, in quantity equal to the spoonfuls of oil
- one glass of vinegar
- coarse pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind a dozen hard egg yolks
2. Moisten them with good oil, adding it little by little in a quantity equal to that of the yolks, so that it mixes with them while continuously grinding
3. Add, without ceasing to grind, one spoonful of saffron, small chiles in quantity equal to the spoonfuls of oil, one glass of vinegar, coarse pepper, and salt
4. When everything is well ground, mashed, and mixed, pass this sauce through a cloth, which should have the appearance of a substance and be very thick
