## Ingredients

- torta pieces
- water
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- bread crumb
- vinegar

## Directions

1. All tortas can be braised, except those made with sweets
2. To braise them, cut into small long pieces, place in a casserole with water, salt, garlic, parsley and pepper, thickening the broth with a bread crumb mashed on the metate and dissolved in broth and a little vinegar
