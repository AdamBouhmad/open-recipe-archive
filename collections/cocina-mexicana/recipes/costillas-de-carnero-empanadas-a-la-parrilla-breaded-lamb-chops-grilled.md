## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- salt
- coarse pepper
- warm butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the previous recipe
2. Season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Bathe in warm butter
4. When well soaked, dust with bread crumbs on both sides, taking care that they are well covered
5. Arrange on a pot lid with bread crumbs on top and bottom
6. A quarter hour before serving, place on the grill over very active heat, taking great care that they do not cook too much and the bread does not burn
7. Dress with a clear sauce underneath
