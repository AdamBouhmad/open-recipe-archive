## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk, already boiled and sweetened to taste
- eight egg yolks
- three of fine sponge cake, crumbled
- cinnamon sticks
- chopped almonds
- whole pine nuts

## Directions

1. To four quartillos of boiled and sweetened milk, add eight egg yolks, three portions of fine sponge cake crumbled, and cinnamon sticks.
2. Return to boil, and when the milk begins to thicken, add chopped almonds and whole pine nuts.
3. When the paste becomes very thick, pour it onto a platter and let cool.
4. The next day, form meatballs from the paste, roll them in sponge cake, and place them on a grill over buttered paper at very gentle heat or over embers only to acquire a golden color.
5. Once cooled, arrange them on the serving platter and add honey at regular point, colored with egg yolks.
6. Add a little wine and a pinch of cinnamon powder, decorating the top with almond paste ornaments.
