## Ingredients

- aceite
- ajos rebanados
- peregil bastante
- higaditos cocidos
- clavo
- pimienta
- cominos
- pollos
- caldo
- lonjitas de jamón
- rebanadas de chorizo cocido

## Directions

1. Fry sliced garlic cloves in oil and remove when they are golden
2. Grind with plenty of parsley, cooked little livers, clove, pepper and cumin
3. Fry all the ground mixture in the same oil in which the garlic was fried
4. Place the chickens with their broth, strips of ham and slices of cooked chorizo
5. Let simmer until the sauce is consumed to the proper amount and reaches good consistency
