## Ingredients

- Eight cuartillos of milk
- One pound of ground rice
- Three pounds of sugar
- Twenty egg yolks
- Half an ounce or one quarter of broken cinnamon stick
- One lemon peel, cooked in small pieces

## Directions

1. Dissolve the ground rice, three pounds of sugar, and twenty egg yolks in eight cuartillos of milk
2. Add half an ounce or one quarter of broken cinnamon stick and one lemon peel cooked in small pieces
3. Heat over fire, and if it thickens too much when it begins to boil, add more milk
4. Allow it to cook to a point slightly higher than manjar blanco
5. Pour immediately into plates to cool
6. Cover with ground sugar and burn the surface with a hot iron or heated iron paddle
