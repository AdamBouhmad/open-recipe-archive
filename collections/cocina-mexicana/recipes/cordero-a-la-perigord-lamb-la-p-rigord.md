## Ingredients

- lamb
- butter or oil
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- mushrooms
- ham slices
- beef slices
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Prepare and fry the lamb with butter or preferably oil, parsley, onion, salt, pepper, and some finely chopped mushrooms if desired
2. Line the bottom of a pot with ham slices and some seasoned beef slices
3. Place the whole lamb or a half or quarter in the pot
4. Cover with ham and place lemon slices on top
5. Cook over very low heat
6. Serve with its own broth after removing grease and clarifying it
