## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- half a common tablespoon of flour
- salt
- black pepper
- a little water

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of butter, half a tablespoon of flour, salt, and black pepper in a saucepan
2. Mix everything together with a wooden spoon and a little water
3. Place the saucepan over heat, stirring the sauce until it is thickened, being careful not to let it boil so it does not taste like starch or glue
