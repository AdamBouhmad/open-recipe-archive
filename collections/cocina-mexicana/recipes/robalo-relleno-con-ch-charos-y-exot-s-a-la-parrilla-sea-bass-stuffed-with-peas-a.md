## Ingredients

- Sea bass, cleaned and desalted
- Pea mixture (as explained for stuffed chiles, page 264)
- Lard
- Paper
- String
- Flour
- Lard for frying
- Olives
- Small chiles
- Capers

## Directions

1. Clean and desalt the sea bass
2. Fill with the pea mixture explained for stuffing chiles
3. Tie with string
4. Wrap in paper well spread with lard
5. Place on grill, turning it when well roasted on one side
6. When roasted on both sides, untie and serve as is, or roll in flour and fry in lard
7. Garnish with olives, small chiles and capers
