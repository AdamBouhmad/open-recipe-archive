## Ingredients

- sugar
- rice
- milk
- eggs
- cinnamon
- clove
- butter
- coconut
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- candied citron
- syrup

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar, and when the syrup reaches the proper point, remove from heat and boil half a pound of well-washed rice
2. Remove the water and change it until it no longer has starch
3. Strain through a sieve and place in three portions of milk with sufficient sugar, cooking until very thick, and set aside for the next day
4. On the following day, beat twelve eggs as for frying and mix in the rice with cinnamon and very little clove
5. Butter a pot or pan, place in a row a layer with half the rice and egg and on top another of coconut
6. Add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, walnuts, pieces of candied fruit, and then place the other half of the rice
7. Bake when bread comes out of the oven, and after the cake is cooked, drown it in syrup, decorating as the others
