## Ingredients

- Lard
- Sliced onion
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Cooked chicken, quartered
- Salt
- Longaniza
- Sliced chorizo
- Strips of ham
- Pieces of sausage
- Pieces of citron
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Olives
- Ground clove, pepper, and cinnamon
- Slices of plantain, sweet potato, apple, pear, and peach quarters
- Pineapple
- Carrot
- Beet
- Chicken broth

## Directions

1. Fry sliced onion in lard
2. Add roasted and ground tomatoes
3. Put in the cooked chicken quartered with salt, longaniza, sliced chorizo, strips of ham, and pieces of sausage, all cooked
4. Add pieces of citron, raisins, almonds, capers, and olives
5. Add ground clove, pepper, and cinnamon
6. Add slices of plantain, sweet potato, apple, pear, and peach quarters, pineapple, carrot, and beet, all cooked and chopped
7. Finally add the chicken broth
8. Let everything simmer until the broth is almost completely consumed
