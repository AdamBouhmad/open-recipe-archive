## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Madeira wine or excellent white wine
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take twelve fresh egg yolks and four glasses of Madeira wine or excellent white wine, six ounces of sugar and a handful of cinnamon
2. Put everything in a pan over a lively fire, stir briskly with a chocolate whisk until the foam has filled the pan
3. Serve immediately without delay in small cream cups
