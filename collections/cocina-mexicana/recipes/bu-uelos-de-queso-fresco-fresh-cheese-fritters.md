## Ingredients

- Fresh cheese, crumbled
- Water
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Crumble the fresh cheese and soak in water for two or three hours, adding a little salt
2. Squeeze out all the water in a napkin
3. Grind and gradually mix in flour until the dough is able to form into very thin small tortillas
4. Fry these in well-tempered lard, continuously bathing them until they are golden
