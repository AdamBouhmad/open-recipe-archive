## Ingredients

- Corn, prepared as for tamales
- Egg yolks
- Lard
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the corn the same way as for tamales, rub it and after cleaning well, set it to drain in the sun
2. Grind it dry and sift it
3. Mix egg yolks and lard in proportion to the amount of dough, and beating well form the quesadillas
4. Place cheese in the middle of each quesadilla
5. Fry them in lard
