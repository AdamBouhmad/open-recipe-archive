## Ingredients

- Skate
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- parsley leaves
- black butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the skate as for white sauce
2. Clean and arrange it the same way
3. Fry parsley leaves and place them around the skate
4. Bathe the skate with black butter and serve
