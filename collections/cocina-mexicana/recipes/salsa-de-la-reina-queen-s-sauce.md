## Ingredients

- Roasted domestic chicken breasts
- Rice cooked in boiling water
- Broth
- Bread or other pastes

## Directions

1. Pound roasted domestic chicken breasts in a marble mortar with a sufficient amount of rice cooked in boiling water and well drained
2. Make a clear substance from the two things by adding broth and pass through a hair sieve
3. Gather whatever does not pass through with the bones, pound them also in the mortar
4. Put this second mixture over medium heat, leaving it for one hour
5. Remove from heat, strain, and pound with bread or other pastes
6. Do not add the first chicken breast substance until the moment of serving
