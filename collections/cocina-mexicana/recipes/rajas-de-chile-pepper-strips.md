## Ingredients

- Peppers
- Lard
- Oil
- Fresh cheese, sliced
- Water

## Directions

1. Devein the peppers raw, roast them, peel them and cut into strips
2. Rinse them
3. In a pot, heat lard and plenty of oil and fry the strips
4. When half-fried, add a little water and let it reduce; if needed add more water and more oil
5. When almost done, slice fresh cheese and add it
