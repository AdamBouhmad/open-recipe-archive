## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- sugar
- salt omitted
- water with a little yeast dissolved in it
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Like the previous recipe, with the difference that in the first heap omit eggs and salt, increase sugar slightly, and dissolve a little yeast in the water to be mixed with it
2. In the second heap place lard and oil
3. All can be mixed in a single heap, placing the lard without melting it
