## Ingredients

- veal rib rack
- fine bacon
- parsley
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- chiles
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- oil
- bread crumbs
- butter
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare a veal rib rack well. Lard the sirloin with very fine bacon. Place it to marinate for two or three hours with minced parsley, onion, thyme, bay leaf, chiles and garlic very finely chopped, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg and a little oil.
2. When it has taken on the flavors, place it on the roasting spit and pour all the marinade over it, which will be held in place with well-greased paper. Roast over low heat.
3. Afterward remove the paper, scrape off the herbs that have stuck to it, the same as to the meat, and place these in a saucepan with a little juice, some vinegar, butter kneaded with flour, salt and pepper. Make this sauce bind over the fire and pour it over the rib rack, after it has been covered with bread crumbs, with melted butter mixed with egg yolks.
