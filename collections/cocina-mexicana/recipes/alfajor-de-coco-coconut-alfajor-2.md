## Ingredients

- five pounds of sugar
- two grated coconuts
- two or three cups of toasted biscuit

## Directions

1. Dissolve five pounds of sugar in water over heat
2. Clarify the syrup and strain it while mixing with two grated coconuts
3. Leave over heat until it reaches the conserve stage
4. Add two or three cups of toasted biscuit and continue stirring after incorporating until it pulls away from all sides
5. Pour onto a table covered with wafer paper and do not cut until it is cool
