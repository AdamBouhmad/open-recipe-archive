## Ingredients

- nopales (cactus paddles)
- tequesquite
- lard
- oil
- epazote
- fresh cheese
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the nopales and cook in tequesquite water after cutting them lengthwise into slices
2. Wash well
3. Place a clay pot on the fire with a little lard and oil, and fry the nopales without adding any water
4. Add a little epazote and some fresh cheese in slices and corresponding salt
5. They should remain dry
