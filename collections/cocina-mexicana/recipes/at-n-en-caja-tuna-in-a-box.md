## Ingredients

- paper box
- tuna slices
- fresh butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make a paper box
2. Place tuna slices in it with fresh butter, fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top
4. Place the box in a roasting pan
5. Cook over two strong fires until it takes on good color, without keeping it that way for long
6. Serve immediately
