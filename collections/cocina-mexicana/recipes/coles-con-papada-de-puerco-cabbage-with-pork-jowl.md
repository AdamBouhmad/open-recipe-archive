## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Pork jowl
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onion
- Cloves
- Turnips
- Carrots

## Directions

1. After dividing the cabbage into quarters, wash and boil for a quarter hour in water
2. Add the pork jowl in pieces, without removing the skin
3. Remove the cabbage, place in cold water, squeeze, and tie with string
4. Cook over coals with a piece of pork jowl and the meat with which the cabbage is to be served, adding salt, pepper, a bundle of parsley with onion, cloves, and two or three turnips and carrots
5. When the meat and cabbage are cooked, remove them, dry the fat, and arrange on a plate with the pork jowl on top
