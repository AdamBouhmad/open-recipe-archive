## Ingredients

- lettuce
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- nutmeg powder
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Remove tough leaves and veins, wash well
2. Blanch in boiling salted water
3. Refresh in cold water
4. Drain and squeeze well
5. Chop as the previous recipe
6. Cook and prepare as for sorrel in milk
7. Generally nothing is placed on top
