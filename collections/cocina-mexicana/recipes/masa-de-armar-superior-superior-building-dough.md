## Ingredients

- Two quarts of flour
- Twelve ounces of butter
- Half an ounce of salt
- Six egg yolks
- One glass of water

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of flour on a table and make a hole in the middle
2. Put there twelve ounces of butter, half an ounce of salt, six egg yolks, and one glass of water
3. Mix the butter with the eggs, salt, and water, and gradually mix in the flour, until all is combined
4. Knead the dough so that it hardens well, and add water if it is too dry
5. Lower or pound it twice, because if kneaded more, there would be the risk of burning it, especially in summer or in months of great heat, and then it would not hold together and would break when assembled
6. If you want to make a large quantity, three pounds of butter, two ounces of salt, and ten egg yolks are necessary for six pounds of flour, taking care in any case that the dough is very firm in order to avoid any inconvenience
7. If it is intended for cakes, make it more soft
