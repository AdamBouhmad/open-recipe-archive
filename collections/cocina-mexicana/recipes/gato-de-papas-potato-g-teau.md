## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in steam
2. Having peeled them, grind them in a metate, adding butter and milk in which sugar has been dissolved
3. Let everything boil and pour into an earthenware dish where it is left to cool
4. Conclude everything as in the rice gâteau
