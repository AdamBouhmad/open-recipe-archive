## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- almonds
- sesame seeds
- cinnamon
- clove
- lard
- salt
- meat
- meat broth
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Toast the ancho chiles, almonds and sesame seeds, and grind them together with cinnamon and clove
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard, seasoning with salt, and then add the meats with the broth in which they were cooked and a little vinegar and sugar
3. Leave everything to boil until it reaches a good consistency and serve with toasted sesame seeds on top
