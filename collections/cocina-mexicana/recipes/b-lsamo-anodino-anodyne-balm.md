## Ingredients

- one ounce white soap
- two drams opium unprepared
- nine ounces rectified spirits of wine
- three drams camphor

## Directions

1. Combine white soap, opium, and spirits of wine
2. Keep at low heat for three days
3. Strain the liquid and add the camphor
4. Apply the balm to the affected part with a warm hand or apply a piece of linen soaked in the balm, renewing every three or four hours until the pain subsides
