## Ingredients

- Roncador (drum fish), cleaned and cooked
- Two handfuls of parsley
- One slice of soaked bread
- Half a large tomato (jitomate)
- Two cloves
- Ten peppercorns
- A little nutmeg
- Salt
- Chicken or mutton broth without saffron, or a little very hot water

## Directions

1. After the fish is cleaned and cooked, place it on a fish platter
2. Make a sauce by grinding together two handfuls of parsley, one soaked slice of bread, half a large tomato, two cloves, ten peppercorns, a little nutmeg, and salt
3. Fry all this mixture
4. Thin it a little with chicken or mutton broth without saffron, or a little very hot water
5. Pour the sauce over the cooked fish
