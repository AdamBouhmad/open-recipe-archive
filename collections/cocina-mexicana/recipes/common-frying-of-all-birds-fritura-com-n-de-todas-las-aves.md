## Ingredients

- birds
- lard
- water
- salt
- onions
- wide chile
- garlic
- vinegar
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. If the birds have fat, place in a pot with little lard and cover with water, adding the corresponding salt, two onions divided into quarters, a wide chile and two or three clean garlic cloves crushed
2. Let boil until they cook and then add a little vinegar with clove, pepper, and cinnamon in very small quantity
3. Place over two fires until all the broth consumes; brown in the remaining fat, and serve thus, or with some of the sauces or broths of which the following articles speak
