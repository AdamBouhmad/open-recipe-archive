## Ingredients

- tomatoes, cooked
- toasted sesame
- roasted xitomate
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- saffron
- lard
- meat (poultry, pork, or other)
- broth from cooking the meat
- vinegar
- whole toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked tomatoes with toasted sesame, a little roasted xitomate, clove, pepper, cinnamon, and saffron
2. Fry everything in lard and add the meat to be cooked with the broth in which it was cooked
3. Add a little vinegar and whole toasted sesame
4. Let it thicken as needed
