## Ingredients

- ground meat seasoned with spices
- beaten eggs
- pounded biscuit
- lard
- raisins
- almonds
- pieces of candied citron
- walnuts
- hazelnuts
- cooked ham
- cooked chorizos
- white wine
- ground sugar
- ground cinnamon
- garlic clove
- ground tomatoes
- olives
- small chilies
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Well season the ground meat with all spices and put a layer of it and pounded biscuit over another layer of beaten eggs in a pan oiled with lard
2. Arrange other layers as needed to fill the pan and decorate with raisins, almonds, pieces of candied citron, walnuts, hazelnuts, cooked ham and cooked chorizos
3. Sprinkle with white wine and put to cook over two fires, dusting when golden with ground sugar and ground cinnamon
4. A sauce can be made by frying a garlic clove in lard and then ground tomatoes, adding raisins, almonds, olives, small chilies, ham, chorizos, clove, cinnamon, pepper and sweet flavoring
