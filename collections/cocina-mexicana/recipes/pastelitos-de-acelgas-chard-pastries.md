## Ingredients

- best chard leaves
- salt
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- beaten eggs
- lard or oil

## Directions

1. Select the best chard leaves
2. Cook in salted water
3. When cooked, remove and squeeze out moisture
4. Chop well and mix with bread crumbs, sugar, well ground cinnamon and beaten eggs as for frying
5. Mix everything well and form into little strips or shapes
6. Beat other eggs as for frying
7. Dip in beaten egg and fry in lard or oil
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and serve
