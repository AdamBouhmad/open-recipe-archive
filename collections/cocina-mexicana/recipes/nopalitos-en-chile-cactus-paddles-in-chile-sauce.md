## Ingredients

- nopales (cactus paddles), cooked
- lard
- chiles (three-quarters wide chiles and one-quarter pasilla chiles)
- potatoes, cooked
- romeritos, cooked
- cilantro sprigs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the nopales as described in the previous article
2. Place a pot on the heat with lard
3. Devein the necessary amount of chiles (three-quarters wide and one-quarter pasilla)
4. Fry them in the lard until toasted without burning
5. Grind them well on a metate
6. Fry them again in a little lard
7. Add the nopales cut into small squares
8. Add cooked sliced potatoes, cooked romeritos, and some sprigs of fresh cilantro
9. Season with necessary salt
10. Let boil until the broth becomes very thick
