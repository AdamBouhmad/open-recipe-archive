## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- sugar
- orange blossom leaves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash the rice several times and place in the sun on a napkin
2. When well dried, remove it and cook the powder in a little water
3. Stir constantly so it does not settle
4. When cooked, add a little sugar, some orange blossom leaves, and if desired, some cinnamon sticks
