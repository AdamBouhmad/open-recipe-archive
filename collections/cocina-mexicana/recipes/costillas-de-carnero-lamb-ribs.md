## Ingredients

- Two lamb rib racks
- Bacon pieces
- Parsley
- Clear sauce

## Directions

1. Take two lamb rib racks from which sirloin and loin are removed.
2. Lard one rack with small pieces of bacon and the other with parsley.
3. Place on the spit, securing with skewers.
4. Three quarters of an hour is sufficient to roast them well.
5. Place on a plate with the sirloins on the outside and a clear sauce on top.
