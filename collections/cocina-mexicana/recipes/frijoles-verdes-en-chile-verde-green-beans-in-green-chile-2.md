## Ingredients

- green beans, not yet hardened
- salt from the earth
- fresh parsley sprigs
- tomatoes
- cooked green chiles
- fresh cilantro sprigs
- lard
- pork broth
- cooked pork, in pieces
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Take green beans while they have not yet started to harden; cook them in water with salt from the earth and some fresh parsley sprigs.
2. When they are cooked, place a pot on the fire.
3. Grind tomatoes, cooked green chiles, and sprigs of fresh cilantro (remove the veins from the chiles if desired).
4. Fry the ground tomato in a pot with lard, add the cooked beans, moisten with pork broth, and add the cooked pork, well-cooked and divided into pieces.
5. Season everything with fine salt and let it boil until the broth has reduced somewhat.
