## Ingredients

- white fish
- water
- salt
- marjoram
- thyme
- oregano
- bay leaf
- vinegar
- oil
- garlic
- lime peel
- onion, chopped
- oregano powder
- olives
- pickled chiles
- capers
- lettuce or escarole
- tomatoes, roasted and ground

## Directions

1. Boil water with salt, marjoram, thyme, oregano, and bay leaf
2. Let cool, then mix with strong vinegar
3. Fry split garlic cloves in oil
4. Fry the cleaned white fish in the same oil
5. Place fried fish in a pot with the herb water, herbs, fried garlic, and lime peel
6. Cover the pot and let rest for three or four days until fish is ready to eat
7. Serve with oil, vinegar, chopped onion, and oregano powder, garnished with olives, pickled chiles, and capers
8. Alternatively, serve drained over lettuce or escarole salad made with oil, vinegar, salt, roasted and ground tomatoes, and chopped onion, garnished with small chiles and olives
