## Ingredients

- equal quantities of liver and lard
- half that quantity of a piece of bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- four spices
- pork caul

## Directions

1. Chop together equal quantities of liver and lard and half that quantity of a piece of bread
2. Mix into the chopped mixture, stirring well, pepper, salt, nutmeg and a few of the four spices
3. Make sausages with it, flattening them
4. Wrap in pork caul
5. Serve roasted on the grill with some spicy sauce
