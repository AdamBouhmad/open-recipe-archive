## Ingredients

- cooked meats from preceding recipe
- concentrated broth from preceding recipe
- four ladles of Spanish sauce
- one bottle of Madeira wine
- butter
- red chile

## Directions

1. With the meats cooked as described in the preceding article, strain the concentrated broth
2. Season the broth with four ladles of Spanish sauce
3. Degrease and allow the sauce to reduce
4. Add one bottle of Madeira wine
5. Pass through cloth to a water bath
6. At the moment of serving, mix in a little butter kneaded with red chile
7. Arrange the olla-podrida the same as in the preceding article
8. Pour the sauce over it
9. Serve as hot as possible
