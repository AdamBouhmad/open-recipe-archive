## Ingredients

- Five pounds of wheat flour
- One pound of yeast
- One and one half pounds of sugar
- Three pounds of lard
- Eight egg yolks and four egg whites
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. This is not kneaded much, and in the rest, follow the same procedures as in the previous article
