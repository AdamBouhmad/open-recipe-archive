## Ingredients

- Chicken crests
- Chicken kidneys
- Stock
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the crests at their extremity and to clean them and remove any blood they may contain, wash several times in hot water and remove when the skin begins to loosen
2. Clean with a napkin without breaking them and cook in a pot with somewhat thick stock
3. Add lemon juice and do not add the kidneys until the crests are perfectly cooked
