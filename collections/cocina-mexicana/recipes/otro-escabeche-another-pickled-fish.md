## Ingredients

- fish
- lard
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- cilantro
- pepper
- cinnamon
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and slice raw.
2. Fry in lard until well cooked and browned.
3. Boil equal parts water and vinegar with salt.
4. Add toasted cilantro, pepper, and cinnamon, all ground and proportioned to the broth, plus some bay leaves.
5. Bring to a boil in a pot.
6. Once the fish is cold, arrange the slices in a crock or pot.
7. Add a pinch of the same spices that were added to the broth.
8. For each layer of fish add broth, bay leaf, and cover.
