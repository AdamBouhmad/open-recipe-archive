## Ingredients

- Beef tendons
- Meat jelly
- Mayonnaise
- Pickles
- Anchovies
- Capers
- Beets
- Meat jelly

## Directions

1. Prepare the beef tendons as in the previous article.
2. Let them cool in their jelly.
3. Arrange them in a crown shape.
4. Cover with mayonnaise, adorned with pickles cut in different ways, anchovies, capers arranged like pearl strings, beets, or meat jelly.
