## Ingredients

- fat and very fresh shad
- oil
- fine salt
- bay leaf
- parsley sprigs
- onions
- sorrel essence

## Directions

1. Choose a fat and very fresh shad, gut it through the gills, scale it and place to marinate in a little oil with fine salt, a bay leaf divided into four parts, some sprigs of parsley and some onions halved
2. Turn it in its marinade, and three quarters of an hour or an hour before serving, according to its size, place it on the grill
3. Baste with its marinade, turn it on both sides, and serve it on a good sorrel essence
