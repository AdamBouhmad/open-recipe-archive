## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- bacon
- flour, one spoonful
- broth
- parsley
- peas
- sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the squabs in butter with a little bacon in strips, add a spoonful of flour and moisten with broth, add parsley, and place peas on top, cooking everything over low heat.
2. A little sugar can be added inside the squabs before serving.
