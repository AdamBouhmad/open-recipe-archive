## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sugar
- virgin honey
- nine egg whites
- half a pound of peeled and ground almonds
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- sesame seeds, toasted
- raisins
- dragée

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar, clarify it, strain it and bring it to the point of setting in cold water
2. Remove from heat, add about a small cup of virgin honey, and beat until it becomes grainy
3. Add nine egg whites that have been beaten until stiff, after removing their oil, and when well incorporated, place the pan over covered coals to boil, continuing to beat gently until the egg is cooked, which is known if when tasted it does not taste raw
4. Remove from fire and mix in half a pound of peeled and ground almonds, split walnuts, clean pine nuts and toasted sesame seeds
5. Beat again until it acquires such consistency that when poured onto a plate it does not run, but not so much that it becomes hard
6. Pour into a platter and decorate with small balls of the same mixture, placing a raisin in the middle, and dusting with dragée
