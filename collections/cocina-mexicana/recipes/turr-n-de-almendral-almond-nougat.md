## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- six ounces of dried almonds
- sufficient sugar to make the paste manageable
- lemon essence

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Chop six ounces of dried almonds and add them gradually to the beaten whites
3. Gradually add sugar in small amounts until the paste is manageable
4. Flavor with lemon essence
5. Place in a mold and allow to dry
