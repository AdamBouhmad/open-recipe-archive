## Ingredients

- small fresh cheese
- twelve egg yolks
- three egg whites
- a box of sponge cake with a little more
- lard or butter
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- syrup

## Directions

1. Mix the fresh cheese with twelve egg yolks, three egg whites and a box of sponge cake with a little more, dissolved
2. Put everything to fry in lard or butter
3. When cooked, remove it, press it and put it in the syrup with raisins, almonds and pine nuts
