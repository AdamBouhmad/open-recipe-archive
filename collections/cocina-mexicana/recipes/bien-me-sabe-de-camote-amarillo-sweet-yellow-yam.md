## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- one taza or pot or pond of starch
- a quarter of almond
- two large good yellow yams
- four pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. For eight cuartillos of milk use one taza caldron or pond of starch, a quarter of almond, two large good yellow yams and four pounds of sugar, all ground and prepared as in the previous articles
