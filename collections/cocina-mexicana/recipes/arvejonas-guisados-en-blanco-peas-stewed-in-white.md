## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- water
- tequesquite water
- lard
- garlic
- tomatoes
- onion
- green chiles
- pork loin
- chorizos
- longaniza
- cumin
- fresh cilantro

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in water with salt and a little settled tequesquite water until half cooked
2. Set them aside and let cool naturally, as washing in hot water with cold water will harden them so they cannot be eaten
3. Once cooled, wash them and return them to the fire to finish cooking
4. Add sufficient water in lard where garlic and diced tomatoes have been fried, onion in quarters, and whole green chiles
5. Add pork loin, chorizos, and pieces of longaniza
6. Add ground cumin and sprigs of fresh cilantro, and season with the corresponding salt
7. Some add squash blossoms
8. They are also cooked with fruits as are chickpeas
