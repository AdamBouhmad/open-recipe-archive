## Ingredients

- 4 quartillos milk
- sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- cream

## Directions

1. Sweeten 4 quartillos of milk and mix in 12 egg yolks
2. After these are well dissolved, strain the milk and put it on heat adding some cinnamon sticks and some cream
