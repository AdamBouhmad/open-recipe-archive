## Ingredients

- almonds, peeled in boiling water
- one pound of almonds
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of rice
- two ounces of pine nuts

## Directions

1. Peel almonds in boiling water
2. Grind the almonds and pine nuts
3. Make the milk by diluting the mass with cold water and passing through a fine sieve or cloth
4. Place the milk on the fire in a new pot and add the rice that has been half-cooked with water and salt
5. Place the pot in infusion in a smaller pot and pour over the rice
6. When the rice is cooked, beat some egg yolks with milk and pour on top
7. Add sugar and cinnamon and keep some milk in case it hardens before serving
