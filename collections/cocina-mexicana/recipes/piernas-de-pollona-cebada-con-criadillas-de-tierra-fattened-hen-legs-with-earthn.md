## Ingredients

- hen legs
- pork meat, minced
- salpicon
- ham slices
- lemon or lime slices
- pan essence or pan drippings
- earthnut sweetbreads, cut in clove shapes
- substantial broth

## Directions

1. Bone the hen legs carefully without tearing the skin
2. Spread the meat on a clean cloth and fill the hollows with minced pork meat or salpicon prepared beforehand
3. Spread the skin and sew it so that each forms a ball or pellet, tucking the legs inside to about two-thirds their size
4. Place in a pot between ham slices and lemon or lime slices, moistening with pan drippings
5. Cook for about an hour until well done
6. After this time, set them aside to cool, remove the thread with which they were sewn and decorate with pieces of earthnut sweetbreads cut in clove shapes or inserted in the meat
7. Then heat them on a frying plate with a little substantial broth and arrange in the form of a crown, placing in the middle a ragout of mushrooms or earthnut sweetbreads
