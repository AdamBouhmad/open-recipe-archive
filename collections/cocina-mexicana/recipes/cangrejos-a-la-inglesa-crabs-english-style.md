## Ingredients

- Crabs
- Water
- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Criadillas de tierra (truffles)
- Fish broth
- Crab substance
- Egg yolks
- Sweet cream
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the crabs in water
2. Clean the tail and remove the small legs
3. Fry in fresh butter with mushrooms and truffles
4. Add a little fish broth and a little crab substance
5. Let soak on low heat
6. Thicken the broth with egg yolks dissolved in sweet cream
7. Sprinkle with chopped parsley
