## Ingredients

- Merlos (blackfish)
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onion
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- White wine

## Directions

1. After preparing them as explained in previous articles
2. Arrange them lined in a deep plate in which butter with parsley and chopped onion has been spread
3. Add a little salt, nutmeg, and coarse pepper
4. Sprinkle with melted butter
5. Moisten with white wine
6. Turn to the other side when half cooked
