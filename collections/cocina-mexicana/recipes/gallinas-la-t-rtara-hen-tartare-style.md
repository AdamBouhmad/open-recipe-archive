## Ingredients

- two hens
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- garlic cloves
- chervil
- oregano
- mustard
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare two hens, breaking the leg bones
2. Put a little lard in a casserole with salt and pepper and place the hens over two fires
3. A quarter hour before serving, remove them and place on a grill over low heat, changing their position so they take color
4. Cut two or three garlic cloves into pieces, a little chervil and oregano
5. Put everything in a casserole with mustard, a little vinegar, salt and pepper to taste
6. Add a small amount of oil, stirring constantly so it mixes; if it thickens too much add a little vinegar
7. Coat the hens with this sauce when serving
