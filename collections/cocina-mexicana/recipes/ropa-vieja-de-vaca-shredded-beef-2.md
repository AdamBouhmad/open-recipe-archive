## Ingredients

- onions
- lard
- flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- meat leftovers from boiled beef
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut onions and cook them in lard until done
2. Sprinkle flour and stir everything until it takes color
3. Add beef broth, salt and pepper
4. When the sauce is almost reduced, add the shredded meat pieces that generally remain from boiled beef
5. Sprinkle with a little vinegar and remove from heat once well combined
