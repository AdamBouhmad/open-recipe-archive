## Ingredients

- olives
- garlic cloves
- oil
- lard
- capers
- toasted and ground bread
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- jitomate, chopped
- bagre, fried beforehand
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Chop olives and a few garlic cloves and fry in oil and lard with some capers and toasted ground bread
2. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper and fry with chopped jitomate
3. Season and add the fish which should be fried beforehand
4. Decorate with olives and chiles
