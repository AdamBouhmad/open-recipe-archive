## Ingredients

- milk-fed suckling pig
- water
- vinegar
- six heads of garlic
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- oregano from China
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lard
- onion
- orange
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Sauté the suckling pig in equal parts water and vinegar with six mashed garlic heads, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, a little Chinese oregano, thyme and bay leaf
2. Once cooked, place on a platter spread with lard and then put over two fires and take to the oven
3. Garnish with onion, orange and lettuce
