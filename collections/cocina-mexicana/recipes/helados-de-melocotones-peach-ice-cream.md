## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Sugar in larger quantity than apricot ice cream
- Ice

## Directions

1. Make the same as apricot ice cream
2. Use sugar in greater quantity so they are soft to the taste
3. When you want to imitate the fruit with its fuzz, put in a covered glass that is surrounded on all sides with ice
