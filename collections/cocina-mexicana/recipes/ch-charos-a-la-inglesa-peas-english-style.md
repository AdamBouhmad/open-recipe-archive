## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bring a little water with salt to a boil.
2. When boiling, add the peas and cook over high heat.
3. When cooked, drain them.
4. Melt a little butter, mix in finely chopped parsley, and season to taste with fine salt.
5. Place the peas on a platter, bathe them with the butter, and serve.
