## Ingredients

- rice
- oil
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- turnip or cabbage
- broth from the pot
- pepper

## Directions

1. After washing well and draining the rice, fry it in oil and butter with a bit of chopped onion, garlic or parsley, turnip or white cabbage chopped fine
2. Once fried, add broth from the pot if you have it, with only a pinch of pepper and let it season
3. Keep it loose, as it is served this way
