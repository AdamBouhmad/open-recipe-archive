## Ingredients

- three onions
- three cloves of garlic
- lard
- one large tomato
- all kinds of spices
- saffron
- one handful of peeled almonds
- one handful of green peas
- bread for soup
- broth
- chopped parsley
- thyme
- marjoram
- burned butter
- oil
- slices of hard-boiled eggs
- lemon slices
- pomegranate flowers stripped of petals

## Directions

1. Chop three onions and three cloves of garlic, and while they fry in lard, roast one large tomato
2. Grind it with all kinds of spices, especially saffron, and add it along with one handful of peeled almonds and another of green peas to the lard where the garlic was fried, after removing the garlic
3. Grind the garlic with the almonds and add the ground mixture to the cooking pan
4. Let everything boil until the peas are cooked
5. Slice the soup bread and place it in a casserole
6. Pour the broth with the peas over it so it boils
7. Leave it over gentle heat until time to serve
8. Garnish with a little chopped parsley, thyme, and marjoram
9. Drizzle with burned butter and oil
10. Place slices of hard-boiled eggs, lemon slices, and pomegranate flowers on top
11. This soup should not be too thick
