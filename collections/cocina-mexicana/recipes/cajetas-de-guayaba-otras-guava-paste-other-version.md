## Ingredients

- guayabas (guavas)
- azúcar blanca (white sugar)
- almíbar (syrup)

## Directions

1. After grinding the guavas well as stated in the previous article, place them in very clarified syrup, made with six pounds of white sugar, thickening well
2. Place over heat and give it the proper point, then pour into the small boxes
