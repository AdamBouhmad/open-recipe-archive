## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Hot water
- Butter
- Pepper
- Parsley, chopped
- Milk
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scrape and after washing, boil in hot water
2. Cut into fillets and place in a casserole with butter, pepper and chopped parsley
3. After well sautéed, add a little milk, thickening this sauce with egg yolks, a little sugar and cinnamon
