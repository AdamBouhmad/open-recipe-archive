## Ingredients

- Thirteen eggs
- One pound of ground sugar
- One pound of ground and sifted starch
- Sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Put thirteen eggs in a pot and beat with a molinillo (whisk) until they are thick
2. Then add one pound of ground sugar and one pound of starch also ground and sifted
3. Pour this into paper boxes, sprinkle with sesame seeds, and put in a very hot oven
