## Ingredients

- One bottle of Rhine wine
- Two drachmas of vanilla
- Half a pound of sugar
- Eight egg yolks
- Cold wine

## Directions

1. Empty a bottle of Rhine wine into a pot with two drachmas of vanilla and half a pound of sugar
2. Place everything over a high fire
3. In a separate bowl, dilute eight egg yolks in cold wine
4. When the wine is boiling in the pot, add the egg yolk mixture
5. Let cook for several minutes, stirring the mixture constantly
6. Serve very hot in chocolate cups
