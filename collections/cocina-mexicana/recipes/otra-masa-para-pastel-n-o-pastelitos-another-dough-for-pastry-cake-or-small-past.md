## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two eggs (one with white and one yolk only)
- a small amount of cold lard
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a table
2. Add two eggs (one with white and one yolk only), a small amount of cold lard, salt and water
