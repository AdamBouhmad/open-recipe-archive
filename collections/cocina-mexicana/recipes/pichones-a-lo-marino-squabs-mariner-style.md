## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Lard
- Salt pork, cut into pieces
- Half broth and white wine
- Chiles

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs in lard with salt pork cut into pieces
2. Moisten with half broth and white wine
3. Add chiles
