## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- good vinegar
- oil
- fine salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the asparagus and tie them in bundles
2. Trim the base by cutting so they are even
3. Place a pot of water over the fire and when it boils, add necessary salt and asparagus bundles
4. After 15 to 20 minutes they will be well cooked; remove them
5. When cooled, remove from the pot and squeeze the bundles with a clean napkin without crushing them
6. When well drained, arrange them on a small plate without disturbing them
7. Dress with good vinegar, oil, fine salt and pepper, making a good salad
