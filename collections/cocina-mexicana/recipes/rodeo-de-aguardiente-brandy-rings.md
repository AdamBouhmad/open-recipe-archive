## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- twenty-one egg yolks
- a little anise water
- salt, the amount that fits between three fingers
- lard
- tequesquite water
- Catalan brandy
- egg white
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a little mound with the flour on a board
2. Open a hole in the middle and add the egg yolks
3. Knead with hands greased with lard so the dough does not stick
4. Gradually add teaspoonfuls of tequesquite water
5. Continue kneading and adding a little Catalan brandy until smooth
6. Pound on the table and roll with a rolling pin
7. Cut pieces and join the edges in the form of pastries with egg white
8. Pick with small tweezers
9. Fry in lard
10. Turn with a small stick
11. When golden, roll in sugar and cinnamon
