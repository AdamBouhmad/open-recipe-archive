## Ingredients

- chiles anchos
- pasilla chiles
- lard
- roasted bread
- toasted sesame seeds
- tripe (babillas)
- zucchini
- chile peppers
- olives

## Directions

1. Devein and toast equal parts wide chiles and pasilla chiles in lard
2. Grind the toasted chiles
3. Grind roasted bread and toasted sesame seeds
4. Fry the ground chiles again with the ground bread and sesame
5. Add the prepared and cooked tripe with its broth
6. Add sliced zucchini, preserved chiles, and olives
7. Season with salt to the proper consistency
