## Ingredients

- bobo
- sal
- jitomate
- aceite
- manteca
- pimienta
- clavo
- limón
- agua

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil with salt.
2. When half cooked, remove from heat.
3. Chop plenty of very red tomato, fry in oil and lard.
4. Add pepper and clove, squeeze half a lemon, add salt and the same water in which it cooked.
5. Place over heat until cooked, the sauce remaining of regular thickness.
