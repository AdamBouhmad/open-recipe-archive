## Ingredients

- Cooked pork feet
- Ground almonds
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Sugar
- Sesame seeds
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cook the pork feet, debone them and cut into small pieces
2. Mix with ground almonds and beaten egg yolks
3. Pour into a pan greased with butter
4. Spread more egg yolks on top mixed with sugar and sesame seeds
5. Cook over two fires, watching that it does not burn
6. When set, remove and add cinnamon powder on top
