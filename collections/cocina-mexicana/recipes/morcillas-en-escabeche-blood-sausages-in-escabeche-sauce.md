## Ingredients

- cleaned morcillas (blood sausages)
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- whole garlic heads
- many garlic cloves unpeeled
- whole black pepper
- whole cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the morcillas and place them in a pot with oil, vinegar, bay leaves, thyme, and whole garlic heads.
2. Place on the heat with a paper over the pot opening and a bowl with water on top.
3. Stir to prevent burning until all liquid is consumed.
4. In a separate pan with plenty of oil, when well heated, add many unpeeled garlic cloves and remove from heat as soon as they begin to brown.
5. Add bay leaves, thyme, whole pepper, whole cloves, salt, and vinegar.
6. Place the morcillas in a pot that has been used before and pour the oil in which they have been cooked over them.
