## Ingredients

- pheasant
- lamb offal
- ham
- lamb kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- lard
- onion, sliced
- tomato
- garlic
- parsley
- spearmint
- hard-boiled eggs
- chile peppers
- capers
- olives
- water
- vinegar
- bread crumb soaked in vinegar
- red chiles
- parsley ground with vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the pheasant and prepare the stuffing as follows
2. Cook cleaned lamb offal and chop coarsely
3. Cut ham into small pieces and add well-cooked lamb kidneys
4. Dust everything with salt and pepper and fry in oil until golden
5. Then fry in a casserole with lard, plenty of sliced onion, equal amount of tomato, chopped garlic, parsley and spearmint
6. Once the tomato is well fried, add some water and vinegar, stirring the mixture and adding chopped hard-boiled eggs, chile peppers cut into quarters, capers and olives
7. Stuff the pheasant with this mixture, sew it closed, and parboil in a casserole with some oil and lard
8. Cover with water, adding necessary salt, and bring to a boil over strong heat until well cooked
9. As the broth reduces, add a little parsley ground with vinegar and two or three red chiles, deveined and ground with a bread crumb soaked in vinegar
10. Let brown and then serve with chile or with one of the appropriate sauces
