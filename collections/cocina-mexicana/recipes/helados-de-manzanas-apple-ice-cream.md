## Ingredients

- 20 sweet-tart apples
- 12 ounces powdered sugar or syrup
- 2 lemons
- saffron yellow
- spinach cooking liquid
- carmine liquid

## Directions

1. Choose twenty sweet-tart apples, peel them, cut them into quarters more or less large; remove the core and seeds, place them on a gentle fire with a little water, and let them cook until they reduce to a fine paste
2. Pass them through a horsehair sieve, arranged over a pot so that it strains there, and mix with twelve ounces of powdered sugar or in syrup
3. Once the mixture is made with exactness, let it cool, squeeze two lemons into it and freeze it
4. At the time of putting it in the molds, which will have the shape of apples, add a little saffron yellow and spinach cooking liquid, then immediately with a fine brush and liquid carmine make the red spots more or less intense, in the same places where apples usually have them
