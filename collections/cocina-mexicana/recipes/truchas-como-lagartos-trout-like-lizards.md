## Ingredients

- trout
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- two or three bottles white wine
- onions
- clove
- nutmeg
- mixed herb bundle
- bread crust

## Directions

1. Select the best trout available
2. Scale, gut them and stuff the body with butter kneaded with fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Place in a fish pan with two or three bottles of white wine so that it rises about a finger's width after covering
4. Add salt, pepper, onions, clove, nutmeg, a mixed herb bundle and a bread crust
5. Cook over clear heat in such a way that the wine ignites
6. When the flame begins to diminish, add butter
