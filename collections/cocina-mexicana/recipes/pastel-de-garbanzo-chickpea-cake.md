## Ingredients

- cooked chickpeas
- salt
- melted lard
- chard
- onion
- garlic
- fried tomato
- oil
- grated cheese
- egg yolks
- egg white

## Directions

1. After the chickpeas are cooked, skin and grind them
2. Add salt and melted lard
3. Fill with chard seasoned with onion, garlic and fried tomato
4. Add oil, grated cheese, egg yolks and one egg white
