## Ingredients

- Four egg yolks
- Four tablespoons of good yeast
- One ounce of powdered sugar
- A little nutmeg zest
- Four ounces of warm butter
- One quarter of a pint of good milk
- One pound of flour
- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix together four egg yolks, four tablespoons of good yeast, one ounce of powdered sugar, a little nutmeg zest, and four ounces of warm butter in one quarter pint of good milk
2. Add little by little one pound of flour and make of it all a solid mass
3. Form it into an elongated stick or roller and cut into slices the thickness of two fingers
4. Arrange them in a baking dish and let the mass rise for a quarter hour at a gentle heat
5. When the dampfnudeln begin to rise, place the baking dish under the lit stove, put it in the campaign oven and let them cook
6. As soon as they take a beautiful color or brown, and it is known that they are cooked, pour over them a quarter pint of sweetened and boiling milk, which very soon will be absorbed
7. Let them puff up well, remove them still hot
8. Serve them dusted with sifted sugar and cinnamon, or with vanilla sauce
