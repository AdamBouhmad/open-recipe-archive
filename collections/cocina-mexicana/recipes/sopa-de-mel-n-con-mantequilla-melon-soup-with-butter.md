## Ingredients

- melón (peeled and emptied)
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- bread (toasted)
- pomegranate seeds

## Directions

1. Cut the melon into slices after peeling and removing the seeds, along the natural lines it has, and cut them in half
2. Put them to boil with water in a pan and with good butter, and season with salt, pepper and fine herbs
3. Pass them through a sieve with their broth and in this cook the toasted bread over low heat
4. Dress in the soup bowl and serve with fried slices of melon on top and pomegranate seeds
