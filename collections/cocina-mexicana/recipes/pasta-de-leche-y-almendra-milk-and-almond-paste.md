## Ingredients

- four enanillos of milk
- four pounds of sugar
- half a pound of soaked and ground almond

## Directions

1. Sweeten four enanillos of milk with four pounds of sugar and strain immediately
2. Add half a pound of soaked and ground almond afterward, incorporating it well
3. Place on the fire and without ceasing to stir so it does not stick, bring it up to point until the bottom of the pan is visible
4. Set aside, beat a little, and pour into the dishes in which it is to be served
