## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- four calderas cups of yeast
- two calderas cups of lard

## Directions

1. Mix eight eggs, half a pound of powdered sugar, four calderas cups of yeast, and two calderas cups of lard
2. Form the pastries
3. Place in the oven
