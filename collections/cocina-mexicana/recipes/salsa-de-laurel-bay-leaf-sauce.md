## Ingredients

- two glasses of vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- onion
- plenty of pepper
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Put two glasses of vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, onion, and plenty of pepper in a saucepan
2. Toast a little flour with butter and add it to the sauce
3. Let it boil for a quarter hour
4. Strain through a sieve
