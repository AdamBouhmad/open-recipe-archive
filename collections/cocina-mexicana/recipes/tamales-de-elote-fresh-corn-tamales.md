## Ingredients

- Fresh corn ears with husks
- Milk
- Cinnamon sticks
- Sugar
- Anise water

## Directions

1. Remove the husks carefully and save them.
2. Remove the kernels and grind with a little milk and strain.
3. Measure the cups of corn and add the same amount of milk.
4. Cook this atole with cinnamon sticks and when it has boiled a little, add sugar to taste.
5. Leave on the heat until you can see the bottom of the pot.
6. Boil the corn husks in advance in anise water.
7. Cover the husks with this atole carefully so they do not spill.
8. Place the cazuela over a pot with boiling water so they set.
9. Serve hot.
