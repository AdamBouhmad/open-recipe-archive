## Ingredients

- bitter or astringent pears
- sugar and brandy (optional)

## Directions

1. Crush the pears in a mill or cider press
2. Allow the juice extracted to ferment in barrels
3. Some add sugar and brandy to encourage fermentation, but it is better when it ferments without these ingredients, providing appropriate heat
4. This beverage strengthens the stomach and aids those who digest with difficulty better than cider
5. Wild pear wine can sometimes be stored for two or three years, though it is more flavorful than regular pear wine, it is not as wholesome
