## Ingredients

- tongue
- vinegar
- salt
- water
- garlic heads
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- black peppercorns
- fine pepper
- tomato
- Spanish oil

## Directions

1. Put the tongue to cook with vinegar, salt and water
2. Set aside in a pot a quarter liter of vinegar with garlic heads and two onions to which you will make a simple cut on top, bay leaf, thyme, oregano, black peppercorns and fine pepper whole, and salt
3. Cover the mouth of the pot with a cloth
4. Slice enough tomato and onion, heat Spanish oil well and fry the tomato and onion in it
5. Once done, skin the tongue, slice it and place in the fried mixture
6. To this add all the seasoning from the pot with the vinegar
7. Let cool and season
