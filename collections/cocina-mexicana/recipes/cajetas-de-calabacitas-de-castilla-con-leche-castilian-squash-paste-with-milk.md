## Ingredients

- calabacitas de Castilla (Castilian squash)
- leche (milk)
- almíbar clarificado (clarified syrup)

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe; but mix with milk, being careful that it does not curdle and become grainy
2. This way they are better, although they do not last long
