## Ingredients

- Hens
- Ham
- Garlic cloves
- Cloves
- Lard
- Salt
- Roasted and ground tomato
- Small whole onions or quarters of large onions
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Cloves (ground)
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Chopped pineapple
- Málaga wine or other generous wine
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean the hens and lard them throughout with ham, garlic cloves, and cloves.
2. Fry them in lard and salt in a pot.
3. Add roasted and ground tomato and small whole onions or quarters of large onions, allowing these to fry as well.
4. Add ground cinnamon, pepper, and cloves, almonds, raisins, and chopped pineapple.
5. Cover the hens with equal parts Málaga wine or other generous wine and water.
6. Cover the pot with a napkin and place a casserole with water on top.
7. Let the hens boil until they are perfectly cooked and the broth becomes thick.
