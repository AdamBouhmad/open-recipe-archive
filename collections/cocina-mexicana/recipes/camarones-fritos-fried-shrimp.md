## Ingredients

- cooked and cleaned shrimp
- oil
- garlic, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, finely chopped
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After being cooked and cleaned, fry the shrimp in oil with chopped garlic
2. Once golden, remove and sprinkle the cooked shrimp with salt and pepper
3. Put back in the oil to fry
4. Place on a platter and pour the fried garlic, finely chopped parsley, oil and vinegar seasoned with salt over the top
