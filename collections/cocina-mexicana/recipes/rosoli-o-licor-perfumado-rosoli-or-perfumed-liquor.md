## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- six cuartillos of water
- two tablespoons of orange blossom water
- one fresh egg
- good aguardiente in proportional quantity
- a few drops of amber or essence of hypocras (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil two pounds of sugar in six cuartillos of water until reduced to three-quarters of the original volume
2. Mix in two tablespoons of orange blossom water and allow to boil for only an instant
3. Break a fresh egg and separate the yolk; add the white and eggshell
4. Once the syrup begins to boil, remove from heat and filter several times through a cloth
5. When well clarified, mix in an appropriate quantity of good aguardiente to achieve desired strength
6. Add a few drops of amber or essence of hypocras if desired
