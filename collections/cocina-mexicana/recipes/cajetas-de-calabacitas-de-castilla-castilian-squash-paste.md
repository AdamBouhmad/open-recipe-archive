## Ingredients

- calabacitas de Castilla (Castilian squash)
- almíbar clarificado (clarified syrup)

## Directions

1. Peel these, remove the seeds, clean out the insides and grind them
2. Place them in clarified and ready syrup, and put over heat, stirring until they reach the proper consistency
