## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- water
- salt
- ham in strips
- longaniza, small pieces
- pork jowl, small pieces
- butter
- onion, chopped
- garlic, chopped
- green chiles, shredded
- tomatoes, roasted and ground
- saffron
- cilantro, toasted
- cumin
- black pepper
- cloves
- parsley, chopped
- oregano
- vinegar
- oil
- bread, fried and ground
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Pound the tongue and cook it in salted water with ham strips, pieces of longaniza, and pork jowl
2. Fry chopped onion and garlic in butter with shredded green chiles and roasted ground tomatoes
3. Add saffron, toasted cilantro, cumin, pepper, and cloves, all ground, along with chopped parsley and oregano
4. Place the cooked, skinned, and sliced tongue in this frying mixture with its cooking broth, ham, longaniza, and pork jowl
5. Add vinegar, oil, and fried ground bread
6. Let it boil until the broth reaches a good consistency
7. When serving, garnish with small chiles and olives
