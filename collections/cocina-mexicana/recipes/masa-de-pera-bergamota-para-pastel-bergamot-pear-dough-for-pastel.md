## Ingredients

- bergamot pears
- biscuit
- eggs

## Directions

1. Peel bergamot pears well and grind them with biscuit
2. Beat eggs as for frying and mix the pear mixture well into them
3. Alternate layers of pear mixture and filling in a pastel
