## Ingredients

- milk
- eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the milk until very reduced and well sweetened
2. Measure the milk in cups: for each cup, beat three egg yolks half-way
3. Heat honey to a gentle boil
4. Once the syrup reaches the proper point, remove from heat
5. Pour the cake into plates and garnish with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and powdered cinnamon
