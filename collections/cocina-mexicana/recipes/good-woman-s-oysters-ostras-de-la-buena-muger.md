## Ingredients

- oysters
- breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- parsley
- onion
- anchovies
- pepper
- salt
- fresh butter
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Parboil the oysters in their water
2. Drain and chop them very finely
3. Mix soaked breadcrumbs with finely chopped parsley, onion, and anchovies
4. Season with pepper and salt and add a little fresh butter
5. Mash everything together with the oysters, binding with some egg yolks
6. Place this sauce in the shells
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and put in the oven
8. Serve when they have taken on a beautiful color
