## Ingredients

- water
- fine salt
- pork leg cuts or lean meat

## Directions

1. Fill a barrel with water and salt it with fine salt until the water becomes very thick, so that an egg floats on top
2. Keep it in a cool and dry place
3. Add the pork leg cuts or, if preferred, only the lean meat, divided into pieces
4. After eight or ten days, it can be used, taking what is needed and leaving it for as long as desired
5. Take care to remove any mold that may form
