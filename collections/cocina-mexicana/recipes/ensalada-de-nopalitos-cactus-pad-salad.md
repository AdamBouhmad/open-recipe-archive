## Ingredients

- cactus pads
- onion
- oregano
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- small chiles
- olives
- chile strips

## Directions

1. Once cooked, leave whole or divide according to size
2. Leave a little of their broth, add chopped onion and oregano
3. Season with salt, oil and vinegar
4. Garnish with small chiles, olives and sliced chiles
