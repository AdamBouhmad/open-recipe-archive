## Ingredients

- garlic
- onion
- xitomate
- tomatoes
- parsley
- mint
- thyme
- fresh oregano
- lime peel
- salt
- pork blood
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Prepare the bowl with garlic, onions, xitomate, tomatoes, parsley, mint, thyme, fresh oregano, and lime peel, all chopped very finely
2. Season with salt and over it receive the pig's blood
3. Proceed as in the previous recipes
