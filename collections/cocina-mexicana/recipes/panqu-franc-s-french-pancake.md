## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of flour
- five egg yolks
- two egg whites
- a little sugar
- salt
- a few drops of orange blossom water
- two crushed sweet macaroons
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Put in a bowl two tablespoons of flour, five egg yolks, two egg whites, a little sugar, salt, a few drops of orange blossom water and two crushed sweet macaroons
2. Dissolve everything and finish dissolving with milk, being necessary that this batter remain watery
3. Put a deep small pan on the fire, heat it and dry it
4. Melt butter in a small pan
5. Lightly grease the small pan with it on all sides
6. Put in the small pan one tablespoon full of the batter and turn it on all sides in order to spread the pancake, which should be thin and equal on all parts
7. When it is cooked, turn the small pan and turn it out onto the plate in which it is to be served, spread it well and sprinkle it with sugar
8. Continue in the same way for the others until the batter is finished and then bathe all of them and serve
