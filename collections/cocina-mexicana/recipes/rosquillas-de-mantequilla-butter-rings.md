## Ingredients

- Half pound flour
- One pound butter
- Salt
- Eight egg yolks
- Sifted sugar as needed

## Directions

1. Knead half a pound of flour with one pound of butter, with salt, eight egg yolks, and as much sifted sugar as is necessary for the dough to be regularly sweet
