## Ingredients

- walnuts, cleaned
- fried bread
- wide chile, deveined and toasted
- lard
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the walnuts and grind them with fried bread and a little wide deveined chile toasted in lard, only to give color
2. Fry the ground mixture in oil and season with salt
3. Add ground pepper and clove
4. Add broth and vinegar
5. Place in this sauce what is to be served and let everything boil until it thickens
