## Ingredients

- Wild boar forequarters
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Garlic
- Fine herbs

## Directions

1. Place to marinate with salt, pepper, vinegar, garlic and all sorts of fine herbs
2. Cook on the roasting spit
3. Serve with a spicy sauce (see spicy sauce)
