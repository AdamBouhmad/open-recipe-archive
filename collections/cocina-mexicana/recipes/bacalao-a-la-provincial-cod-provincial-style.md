## Ingredients

- Cod
- Young garlic
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Onion
- Lemon
- Pepper
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. After prepared as already indicated and cooked, take the platter on which it is to be served, put in it young garlic, crushed garlic, parsley, onion, lemon in slices without skin or seeds, peppercorns, two spoonfuls of oil, and a piece of butter the size of an egg, put the cod on top, cover with a layer of equal mixture and sprinkle with bread crumbs, put the dish on gentle fire
