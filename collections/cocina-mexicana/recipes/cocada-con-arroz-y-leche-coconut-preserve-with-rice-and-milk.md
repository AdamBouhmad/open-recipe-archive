## Ingredients

- coconut paste from previous preparation
- three cuartillos of sweetened milk
- sixteen egg yolks
- one small measure of ground rice
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Prepare the coconut paste as in the previous recipes up to after it has boiled with the coconut, and set aside for the next day.
2. The next day, bring three cuartillos of sweetened milk to a boil with sixteen egg yolks and ground rice.
3. Add the coconut paste prepared the previous day and let it reach the point of cajeta.
4. Brown with heat from above and garnish with cinnamon powder.
