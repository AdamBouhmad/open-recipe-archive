## Ingredients

- shrimp
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- salt
- parsley
- water
- pan remojado
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- cumin
- saffron

## Directions

1. Remove the shells, feet and head from the shrimp and soak them in water from one day to the next
2. The following day, clean them again of any remaining shells and wash them in many waters
3. Cook in water until they are tender, taking care throughout all operations that they remain without breaking or crumbling
4. Fry in lard garlic, onion, tomatoes and green chiles, all chopped, seasoning with salt
5. Once these things are well softened in the lard, add chopped parsley and then add the shrimp with the water in which they were cooked
6. If the water is not sufficient, add warm water, seasoning the broth with salt, pepper, clove, cumin and saffron, all ground, and thickening with soaked and ground bread
7. Add oil and vinegar, and leave the cazuela over fire until the stew is well seasoned and the broth has the proper consistency, thicker than thin
