## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Fine salt
- Ground toasted cilantro

## Directions

1. Drop whole eggs into already boiling water at a rolling boil
2. Keep them on the fire for two or three minutes
3. Remove the pot from heat and keep them in hot water for the same amount of time or slightly longer
4. Place them in egg cups and serve with a short stick that fits in the egg without breaking it completely
5. Have ready fine salt and ground toasted cilantro on the side
