## Ingredients

- Rice
- Aged cheese
- Egg
- Butter
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Onion
- Salt
- Small chiles

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well and put it in the sun and once it is dry, grind it very fine into powder with aged cheese; beat the egg and add the rice there; put the skillet with butter and add it; once it is set, break into quarters and make the spice.
2. Grind the tomatoes and fry them with garlic and onion both chopped, fry the tomato very well, add salt and water, small chiles and once it is boiling, add the cake to boil for a good while.
