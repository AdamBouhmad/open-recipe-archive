## Ingredients

- tomatoes without seeds
- cumin
- garlic
- bread
- onions
- green chile slices
- lard
- cooked oysters
- salt
- vinegar
- olives
- capers
- parsley
- oregano
- water

## Directions

1. Grind tomatoes without seeds, cumin, garlic and a piece of bread
2. Chop onions and green chile slices
3. Fry these in lard
4. Once done, mix in the ground mixture
5. Add the cooked oysters and season with salt, vinegar, olives, capers, parsley and oregano
6. After they have turned once in the mixture, add water and let the sauce thicken a little
