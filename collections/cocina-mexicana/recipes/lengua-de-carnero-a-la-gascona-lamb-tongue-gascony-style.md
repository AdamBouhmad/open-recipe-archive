## Ingredients

- cooked lamb tongues
- Spanish sauce
- parsley
- onion
- half a head of garlic
- mushrooms
- salt
- black pepper
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked lamb tongues into strips
2. In a fire-resistant dish, place a layer of Spanish sauce at the bottom with parsley, onion, half a head of garlic, mushrooms, all chopped very finely, salt and black pepper
3. Place the tongue strips on top, seasoning them underneath
4. Cover everything with bread crumbs
5. Place small pieces of butter the size of a chickpea on top all around, which will nourish the dish and prevent the bread from toasting and turning black from the fire
6. Place the dish over gentle heat, cover it with the lid of a baking dish, and place fire on top
7. When the dish is well colored, serve with light sauce
