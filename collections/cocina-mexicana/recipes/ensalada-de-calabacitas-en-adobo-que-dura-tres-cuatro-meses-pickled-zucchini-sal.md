## Ingredients

- garlic
- fine pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- ginger
- oregano
- thyme
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- raw zucchini
- vinegar and water broth
- whole garlic heads
- aged cheese, crumbled
- oregano
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Pound garlic, fine pepper, clove, cinnamon, ginger, oregano, thyme and salt; dissolve this mixture together in equal portions of vinegar and water, and making two cuts on each raw zucchini without dividing it, place them in an earthenware jar lined on the inside, adding to the above the broth prepared for the endive salad, heavily loaded with vinegar, and several whole garlic heads.
2. Well covered, do not touch the jar for eight days; but if after that time the zucchini are still not well cooked and tender, leave them for four or five more days.
3. After that time the salad can be used, placing it on a platter and adorning the top with crumbled aged cheese, oregano, small chiles and olives. Be careful to remove it from the jar with a wooden spoon used only for this purpose.
