## Ingredients

- walnuts, ground
- mamón (sponge cake)
- cinnamon
- almíbar (syrup)
- beaten egg whites
- sugar
- lemon
- sesame (ajonjolí)
- toasted sesame
- raisins
- almonds
- citron pieces

## Directions

1. Grind the walnuts well and dry and form the cake in this way: place a layer of mamón with sufficient cinnamon, another thick layer of the walnut powder with cinnamon
2. Then put syrup until the mamón is well soaked, and cover all with beaten egg whites mixed with sugar, pouring on top a few drops of lemon and sesame
3. After the cake has been cooked over two fires, decorate with more toasted sesame, raisins, almonds and citron pieces
