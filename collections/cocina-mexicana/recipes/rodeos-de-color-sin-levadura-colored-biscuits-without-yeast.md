## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- One pound and two ounces of lard
- Six ounces of ground sugar
- Five egg yolks
- A little salt
- Three small cups of water

## Directions

1. Add to one pound of flour: one pound and two ounces of lard, six ounces of ground sugar, five egg yolks, a little salt and three small cups of water
2. Mix everything and flatten with a paddle to leave it very thin
3. Cut into small pieces of desired form and size
4. Place dry in a baking pan
5. Bake at two gentle heats
6. Serve with powdered sugar on top
