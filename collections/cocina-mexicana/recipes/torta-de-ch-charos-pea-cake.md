## Ingredients

- Small tender peas (Cambray type)
- Salt
- Hard bread
- Grated cheese
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook the small tender peas with salt
2. Drain them and mix with ground hard bread, grated cheese, and beaten eggs, so that the paste is not watery and does not fall apart when frying the portions
3. Take portions with a spoon for frying in butter
4. These small cakes can be served in tlemole or in adobo
