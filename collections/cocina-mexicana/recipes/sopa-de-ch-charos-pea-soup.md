## Ingredients

- puchero broth
- peas
- onion heads
- brains
- egg
- parsley
- nutmeg
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After skimming the puchero, set apart a quantity of broth and in that broth cook the peas with onion heads cut in wedges, making sure the peas do not fall apart
2. Take a small amount of brains and tear it so it is in little crumbs, well broken up
3. Mix with one raw egg, a little very finely chopped parsley, a grating of nutmeg and half a spoonful of butter and salt to taste
4. Let rest for about a quarter hour
5. Then make small meatballs that you drop into the broth, which should be boiling, and a little more butter, the necessary salt and let season for a while
