## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- white wine
- cinnamon stick
- cloves
- bay leaves
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. After soaking the ham for twelve or more hours, trim the shank and cook in water for at least an hour and a half
2. Remove and drain, then place in an appropriate pan with white wine to cover it
3. Add cinnamon sticks, half a dozen whole cloves and bay leaves
4. If sweetness is desired, add a pound of sugar
5. Can be served cold, in which case sugar powder should be sprinkled on top and browned with a hot iron plate
