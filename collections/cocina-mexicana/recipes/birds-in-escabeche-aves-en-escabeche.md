## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- fried bread
- spices
- garlic
- vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- lemon slices
- bay leaves
- olive leaves

## Directions

1. Roast some tomatoes and grind with fried bread and spices
2. Fry a little garlic, and in the same place add the ground tomato with a little vinegar, a bit of sugar and the corresponding salt
3. Cook apart in vinegar and salt some lemon slices, bay leaves and olive leaves, and stir afterwards with the fried tomato
4. Place the birds to cook in this escabeche, if they are small and tender; but if they are large, cook them apart, and fried in oil, or cooked, add them to the escabeche, to which water is added, if the large birds are to cook in it
