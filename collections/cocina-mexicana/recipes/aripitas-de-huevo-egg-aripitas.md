## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- four egg whites
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- one pound of lard
- flour as needed
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat together eight egg yolks and four egg whites
2. Add twelve ounces of powdered sugar and one pound of lard
3. Add flour gradually until the dough is not too stiff
4. Spread with a roller and form the aripitas
5. Fry in lard
6. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
