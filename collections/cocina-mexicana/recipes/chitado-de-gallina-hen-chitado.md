## Ingredients

- one hen, roasted
- lard
- chopped onion
- parsley
- broth
- wine or vinegar
- fresh lard
- spices
- chopped vegetables, particularly sorrel

## Directions

1. After roasting, cut the hen into quarters
2. Sauté with lard, chopped onion and parsley
3. When ready, add just enough broth to cover and a little wine or vinegar and fresh lard
4. Season with spices and if desired, add some chopped vegetables, particularly sorrel, which makes a good stew
