## Ingredients

- One pound of clarified sugar
- Six egg yolks
- One clean and well-ground coconut
- One sweet orange

## Directions

1. Add six beaten egg yolks to one pound of clarified sugar
2. When it is boiling add one clean and well-ground coconut
3. Squeeze a sweet orange into it
4. Give it the point of Portuguese eggs
