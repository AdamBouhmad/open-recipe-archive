## Ingredients

- Sponge cake of different colors
- Various preserves such as apricot jam and guava jam

## Directions

1. Cut fine slices of sponge cake of different colors
2. Arrange them symmetrically in a smooth mold
3. Make different compartments inside the mold
4. Fill them with different preserves of different colors such as apricot jam and guava jam
5. Cover the charlotte with sponge cake
6. Turn out onto the plate at the moment of serving
