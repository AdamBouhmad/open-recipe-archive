## Ingredients

- eggs
- cooked ham
- substance
- calf sweetbreads
- asparagus tips
- artichoke hearts
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Prepare as previously mentioned
2. Add diced cooked ham and a spoonful of substance; also made with calf sweetbreads, asparagus tips, artichoke hearts or cauliflower
