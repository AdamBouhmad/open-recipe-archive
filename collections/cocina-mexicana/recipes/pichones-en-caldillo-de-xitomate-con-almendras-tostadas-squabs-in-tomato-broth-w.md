## Ingredients

- squabs, cooked
- lard
- salt
- roasted tomatoes
- toasted almonds
- bread fried in lard
- pepper
- cloves
- capers
- cooked onions
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano
- broth from cooking the squabs

## Directions

1. After the squabs are cooked, fry them in lard with salt
2. Remove them from the frying and add roasted tomatoes, toasted almonds, bread fried in lard, pepper and cloves, all ground
3. Add afterwards capers, cooked onions stripped of their layers, oil, vinegar and oregano
4. Place the squabs on top, and pour over them the broth in which they were cooked
5. Let them boil until the broth is well seasoned and of regular consistency
