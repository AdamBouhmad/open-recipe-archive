## Ingredients

- fish glue
- deer horn or veal jelly

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the previous article and fill the molds desired with it, adding fish glue in proportion, and it should be noted that at least two sticks of fish glue are needed if the mold is of a medium size
2. Place in ice and let it freeze
3. It can be made with deer horn or with veal jelly
