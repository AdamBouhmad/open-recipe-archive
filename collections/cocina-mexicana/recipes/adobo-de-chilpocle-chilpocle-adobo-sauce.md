## Ingredients

- chilpocle
- tomatoes
- garlic
- cumin
- onion
- chile
- meat broth
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the chilpocle
2. Roast tomatoes and grind with garlic and cumin
3. Fry everything well
4. Parboil chopped onion in large pieces
5. Fry the chile
6. Add the meat broth with potatoes cut into quarters and cooked separately
