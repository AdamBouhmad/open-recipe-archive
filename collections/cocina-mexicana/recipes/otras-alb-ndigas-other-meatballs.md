## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- onion
- garlic
- fine spices
- bread, soaked
- vinegar
- butter
- egg
- onion
- butter
- oil
- tomato, two
- fine spices
- bread, fried
- eggs, cooked
- capers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop a bit of veal loin
2. Grind with onion pieces, garlic cloves, fine spices and soaked bread
3. Add vinegar in small quantity, butter and an egg
4. Mix well and make meatballs
5. Chop onion finely, fry in butter and oil
6. Grind two tomatoes with fine spices and a piece of fried bread
7. Incorporate cooked eggs with onion
8. When all is fried, add meatballs to cook without adding water
9. Cover and add capers with a bit of vinegar
