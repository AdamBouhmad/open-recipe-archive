## Ingredients

- milk
- flour
- oysters
- oyster broth
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. To three-quarters of a liter of milk add a small cup of flour, dissolving it in the same milk to prevent lumps and keep it very loose.
2. Place on heat and if it thickens add more milk.
3. Let it boil and when cooked remove the milk and pour one can of oysters with half their broth into the same pan.
4. Let it cook until thickened and place in a serving dish with slices of toasted bread.
