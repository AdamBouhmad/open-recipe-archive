## Ingredients

- Cooked onions
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Vinegar
- Onion, finely chopped
- Black pepper
- Oil
- Oregano
- Avocado slices
- Small chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking the onions with salt, if they are large, separate them into leaves; if small, leave them whole.
2. Roast some tomatoes and after removing the skin, grind them well with a little vinegar.
3. Add finely chopped onion, salt, and black pepper.
4. Add sufficient oil with salt and oregano rubbed between the fingers.
5. Mix everything well with the onions, adding avocado slices and small chiles in vinegar chopped finely.
6. Serve sprinkling more oregano on top.
