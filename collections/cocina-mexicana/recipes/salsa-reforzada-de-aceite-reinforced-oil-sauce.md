## Ingredients

- Curly sauce, cold
- Chervil
- Salad burnet
- Chives
- Tarragon
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Several tablespoons of vinegar
- The same amount of good oil

## Directions

1. Chop the same herbs from the previous article and add them to cold curly sauce
2. Add salt, whole pepper, several tablespoons of vinegar, and the same amount of good oil
3. Stir for a long time so the sauce becomes well bound
