## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted flour
- three ounces of butter
- two eggs (one with white and one yolk only)
- water as needed
- flour for dusting
- cold butter
- milk for filling
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of sifted flour with three ounces of butter, two eggs (one with white and one yolk only), and water as needed so the dough becomes tender
2. Once kneaded, dust the table with flour and place the dough on it
3. Extend with a rolling pin or bolillo
4. Coat with cold butter, roll like a cigar and extend again
5. Continue coating, rolling and extending for three or four times
6. The last time extend but not thin, rather somewhat thick
7. Cut out the pastries
8. Make a small hole in the middle of each with a finger and fill with some milk
9. Cover with another smaller circle
10. Place in the oven
11. After baking sprinkle sifted sugar on top
