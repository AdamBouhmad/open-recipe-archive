## Ingredients

- pork fat cut into small dice
- melted lard or butter
- small dice of lean ham
- mushrooms
- truffles if desired
- parsley
- onion
- flour
- substance or strained broth
- pepper and other spices
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs from a four-finger-long and three-finger-wide piece of soft bread
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Parboil pork fat cut into small dice and let it drain
2. Place melted lard or butter in a pan with small dice of lean ham and let them release their fat on gentle heat
3. Add mushrooms, and if desired truffles, chopped parsley and onion, and a little flour
4. Moisten everything with broth
5. Add the pork fat dice and season with pepper and other spices
6. Let it reduce on low heat until the sauce becomes thick
7. Remove from heat, add lemon juice and let cool
8. Make very thin toasts from soft bread four fingers long and three fingers wide
9. Cover them with the prepared mixture
10. Roll them in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs, and fry in lard until golden
11. Garnish and serve very hot
