## Ingredients

- salt cod
- water
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- lard
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Desalt the salt cod and place in a casserole with water to cover it
2. Put over heat to warm the water
3. Remove and take off the skins and bones
4. Cut lengthwise into slices
5. Chop garlic and parsley very finely
6. Put considerable oil and lard in a frying pan and medium-fry the garlic and parsley there, stirring so it does not brown, sprinkling with very finely ground pepper
7. Place the salt cod in another casserole and bathe it with the broth of oil and lard
8. Put another layer of salt cod on top and bathe it again with the broth and continue this way, with the broth being last
9. Add raw oil to the broth
10. Cover the casserole and place over two very low heats to cook slowly
11. This stew is especially recommended
