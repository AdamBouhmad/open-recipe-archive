## Ingredients

- chickens
- ham strips
- ancho chiles
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- cilantro
- garlic
- vinegar
- salt
- lard
- marjoram
- water
- lettuce leaves
- oil
- olives
- small chiles
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and lard them with ham strips
2. Place in a deep cazuela
3. Make an adobo of toasted, deveined, soaked and ground ancho chiles with cloves, pepper, cinnamon, toasted cilantro, garlic, vinegar and salt
4. Pour adobo over chickens
5. The next day, add lard, marjoram and water
6. Cook over two fires, turning them until well cooked and browned in their own adobo
7. Serve on lettuce leaves and top with oil, olives, small chiles and capers
