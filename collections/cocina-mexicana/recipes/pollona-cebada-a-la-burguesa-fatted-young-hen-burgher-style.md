## Ingredients

- One fatted young hen
- Good butter
- Four onions
- Two carrots or turnips
- A bouquet of fine herbs
- Salt
- White wine

## Directions

1. Place at the bottom of a saucepan a little good butter, two sliced onions and the fatted hen, passed through the flame, empty and cleaned, with the belly facing down.
2. Cover it with two more sliced onions, two carrots or turnips cut into thin strips, a bouquet of mixed fine herbs and a little salt, and let it cook under hot coals.
3. When it is half cooked, pour half a quart of white wine over it, and when its cooking is complete, serve it.
