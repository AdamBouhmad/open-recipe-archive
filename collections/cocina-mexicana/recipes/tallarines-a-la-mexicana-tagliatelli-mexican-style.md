## Ingredients

- flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 cup warm water
- fine salt
- small piece of yeast
- broth from the pot
- good grated cheese

## Directions

1. Mix flour with 4 egg yolks and one cup of warm water, stirring well and seasoning with fine salt while kneading, adding a small piece of yeast.
2. When the dough is tough, extend it with a rolling pin into very thin sheets, sprinkle with flour so they do not stick, and cut into thin strips.
3. Place these strips on a napkin in the sun, and when they have dried, place them in well-boiled pot broth.
4. After half an hour of cooking, pour into a soup tureen with good grated cheese sprinkled on top.
