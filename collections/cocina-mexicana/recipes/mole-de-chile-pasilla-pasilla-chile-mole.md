## Ingredients

- Chile pasilla
- Lard
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Pine nuts
- Walnuts
- Sesame seeds, toasted
- Almonds
- Chicken or pork, cooked
- Sugar
- Avocado leaf

## Directions

1. Remove veins from chile pasilla and toast it in lard to grind it with sufficient cinnamon, cloves, a handful of pine nuts, another of clean walnuts, another of toasted sesame seeds, and many almonds
2. Fry all this in lard
3. After frying, incorporate cooked chicken or pork meat, a lump of sugar, and avocado leaf
