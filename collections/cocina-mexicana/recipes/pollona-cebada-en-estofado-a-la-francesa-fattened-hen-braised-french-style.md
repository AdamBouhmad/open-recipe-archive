## Ingredients

- fattened hen
- thick ham slices
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- pork fat strips
- beef trimmings or scraps
- ham slices
- carrots, sliced
- onions
- cloves
- parsley
- lemon juice
- lard strips
- Madeira wine
- concentrated broth
- thick Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. After singeing and emptying the hen, stuff it with thick ham slices
2. Season with salt, pepper and spices, tuck the legs forward, tie it and tuck the tail into the body, giving it a graceful shape
3. In a pot, place pork fat strips, beef trimmings or scraps, two ham slices, some sliced carrots, two onions, two cloves and a bundle of parsley
4. Rub the hen with lemon juice, cover with lard strips and place on the bed in the pot with a paper wheel
5. Moisten with a glass of Madeira wine and several large spoonfuls of concentrated broth
6. Put on the fire and let it cook gently for about an hour
7. When cooked, strain its broth through a cloth, defatten it and reduce it with three spoonfuls of thick Spanish sauce
8. Drain the hen, untie it on the plate and cover with its sauce
