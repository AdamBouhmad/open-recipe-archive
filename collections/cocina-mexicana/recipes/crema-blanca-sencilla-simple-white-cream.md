## Ingredients

- 4 quartillos of milk, or 2 of cream
- A little rennet, dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Heat the milk or cream and let it boil until reduced to one-third
2. Allow it to cool until the finger can bear the heat without burning
3. Dissolve a little rennet in water with a common spoon and mix well with the cream
4. Strain everything through a sieve
5. Place the serving plate over hot coals
6. Pour the cream onto it and cover with a lid also heated with hot coals on top
7. Leave in this state until it has set
8. Transfer to a cool place and serve cold
