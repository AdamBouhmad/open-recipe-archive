## Ingredients

- pulp
- cloves
- pepper
- garlic
- ham in strips
- butter
- turnips
- bread, fried
- onion
- garlic
- wine
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the pulp with cloves, pepper, garlic, and ham in strips; once larded, fry in butter.
2. Peel some turnips, slice them, and fry until they brown and grind with a little fried bread.
3. Fry onion and garlic divided into cloves in a pot, add wine, vinegar, and the corresponding salt and let cook over gentle heat, trying that it remains of regular thickness.
