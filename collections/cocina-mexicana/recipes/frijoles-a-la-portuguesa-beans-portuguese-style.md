## Ingredients

- xitomate, chopped
- onion, chopped
- lard
- bean broth
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- saffron
- cooked beans
- cooked pork meat
- broth in which the pork cooked
- beans, ground
- salt
- lard and oil
- cheese
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of xitomate and onion and fry in lard.
2. Add a little bean broth with clove, pepper, cinnamon, and saffron, all ground.
3. Add the already cooked beans with cooked pork meat, a little of the broth in which it cooked, and a handful of ground beans to thicken.
4. Season with the corresponding salt and abundant lard and oil.
5. Let it thicken appropriately and serve with cheese, small chiles, and olives on top.
