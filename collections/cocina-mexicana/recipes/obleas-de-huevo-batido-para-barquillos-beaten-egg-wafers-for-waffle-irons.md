## Ingredients

- Three eggs (whites and yolks separated, then beaten together)
- Six ounces of sifted sugar
- Four ounces of flour
- Half a quarter-liter of milk with cream
- Four ounces of melted and cooled butter

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately, then beat them together
2. Mix in the sifted sugar and flour
3. When everything is well incorporated, add the milk with cream and the melted cooled butter
4. Stir everything together
5. When well incorporated, proceed as for making common waffle iron wafers according to the preceding articles
