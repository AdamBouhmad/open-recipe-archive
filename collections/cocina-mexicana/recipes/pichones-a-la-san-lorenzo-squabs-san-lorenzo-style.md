## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- pimienta gorda (whole pepper)
- sal (salt)
- mantequilla (butter)
- pan rallado (breadcrumbs)
- caldo de sustancia (beef broth)
- salsa de chalote (shallot sauce)
- agua (water)
- pimienta fina (fine pepper)
- chalotes (shallots)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. After passing them lightly through flames, tuck the feet under the belly, open along the back from the neck to the tail, empty and flatten the breast
2. Season with whole pepper and salt, warm a piece of butter in a pan, moisten the squabs with it, roll them in breadcrumbs, and when well breaded, half an hour before serving place them breast-side down on the grill at a gentle fire
3. Turn them appropriately and arrange them on a plate over loose beef broth or shallot sauce
4. Add half a glass of broth or water, salt, fine pepper, two common spoonfuls of very finely chopped shallots, three spoonfuls of good vinegar and one spoonful of breadcrumbs, give it two or three boils and pour the sauce over the squabs, tasting to make sure it is to good flavor
