## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- sugar to taste
- twenty-four egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- four ounces of soaked almonds
- four ounces of pinyon nuts
- ground biscuit
- sugar and cinnamon
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Sweeten eight quartillos of milk to taste
2. Strain the milk after sweetening and mix in twenty-four egg yolks
3. Once well dissolved, put the milk on the fire, adding a few cinnamon sticks
4. Once it thickens, add four ounces of soaked almonds and four ounces of pinyon nuts, both ground
5. Taste the mixture to see if it has enough sweetness and sweeten more if it lacks sugar
6. Let it boil until it reaches the consistency of cajeta (milk fudge)
7. When it reaches this point, pour it into dishes
8. Top it with ground biscuit mixed with sugar and cinnamon so that the surface is well covered
9. Bathe it with melted butter and place a griddle or comal on it
