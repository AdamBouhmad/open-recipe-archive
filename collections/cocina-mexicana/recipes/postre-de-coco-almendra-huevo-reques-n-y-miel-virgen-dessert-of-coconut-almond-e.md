## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- half a pound of almonds
- one coconut
- twelve egg yolks
- three regular tablespoons of virgin honey
- two calderas cups of cheese curds
- butter
- gragea (small sugar balls)
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point with three pounds of sugar
2. Away from heat, mix in half a pound of almonds and one coconut, both ground
3. Put on fire, and as soon as it thickens a little, add twelve egg yolks, allowing it to boil until the bottom of the pan is visible
4. Remove from heat and add three regular tablespoons of virgin honey and two calderas cups of cheese curds, beating well immediately after
5. Empty into plates that are buttered and browned with a metal sheet or comal with fire on top
6. Decorate with gragea and cinnamon
