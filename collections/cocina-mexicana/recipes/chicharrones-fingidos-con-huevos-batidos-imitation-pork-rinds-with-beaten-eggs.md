## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup milk
- one cup flour
- lard for coating the pan
- syrup (almíbar)
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs and mix with one cup of milk and one cup of flour
2. Ensure everything is well incorporated
3. Pour into a pan coated with lard
4. Cook over two fires until set
5. To check if well cooked, insert a straw; if it comes out clean, it is done
6. Remove from heat
7. Once cool, slice into the shape of pork rinds
8. Fry until golden
9. When serving, pour syrup, sugar, and cinnamon over them
