## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- Slices of ham
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Garlic cloves
- Vinegar
- Lard

## Directions

1. With the veal pulp skinned and beaten as in the previous article, lard with slices of ham, accompanying it in some holes with a grain of pepper, in another with clove, cinnamon in others, and in the rest with garlic cloves split in half
2. Being well filled with these accompanied lards, place in a small barrel or keg with vinegar and something heavy on top so it presses
3. After two days, boil that same vinegar in a cooking vessel with a little lard, and place the veal there
4. After cooking, cover to slice when cold
