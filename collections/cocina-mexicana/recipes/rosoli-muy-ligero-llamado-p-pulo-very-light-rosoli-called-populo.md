## Ingredients

- nine cuartillos of water
- three cuartillos of spirit of wine
- three cuartillos of clarified syrup
- half a glass of distilled essence of anise
- half a glass of essence of cinnamon
- very little musk and amber powder, prepared

## Directions

1. Boil nine cuartillos of water and let it cool
2. Add three cuartillos of spirit of wine, three cuartillos of clarified syrup, half a glass of distilled essence of anise, half a glass of essence of cinnamon
3. Add very little musk and amber powder, prepared
4. Do not cook the syrup for clarification as it hardens in rosolis and forms cloudiness
