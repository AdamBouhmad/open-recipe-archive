## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Garlic cloves
- Cooked tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Bread crumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes and remove the fuzzy center.
2. In a saucepan, fry whole, clean garlic cloves; once the garlic is fried, fry a good amount of cooked tomatoes and add sufficient clove and pepper.
3. When the tomatoes are well fried, arrange the artichokes there, adding some vinegar and bread crumbs with sufficient fine salt.
4. Place a grid over it with very little heat and remove from it when the broth is about to dry, so that they remain moist.
5. Serve hot.
