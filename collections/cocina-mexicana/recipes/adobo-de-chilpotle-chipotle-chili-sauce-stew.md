## Ingredients

- chipotle chile, boiled
- tomato
- pork meat
- garlic cloves
- cumin, ground
- salt
- plantain
- bread, fried
- onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Make the same as the first adobo, but use boiled chipotle instead of mulato chile
2. If you want it not to be spicy, you must devein and boil it from the day before, letting it soak all night
