## Ingredients

- Twelve pounds of pork loin with its fat
- Two pounds of wide chile, deveined
- Two large heads of garlic
- Good Castilian vinegar
- One bottle of good vinegar
- Fine salt
- One nutmeg
- Half ounce of cinnamon
- Twenty Tabasco peppers
- One spoonful of clove powder
- Clean bull casings

## Directions

1. Cut twelve pounds of pork loin with its fat into very small pieces without mincing
2. Devein two pounds of wide chile well and soak it to reduce heat
3. Peel two large heads of garlic and grind well with the chile and good Castilian vinegar
4. Season all with necessary fine salt and add one bottle of good vinegar
5. Place the meat in infusion for thirty hours, turning it occasionally from top to bottom
6. Grind one nutmeg, half ounce of cinnamon, and twenty Tabasco peppers
7. Gradually mix these spices into the meat so all partakes of them
8. When filling, mix in well one spoonful of clove powder
9. Stir the meat well and stuff into clean bull casings
10. Tie to form sausages of eight to ten fingers length
11. Use two ties leaving one finger of casing between each sausage so they separate when cooked without spoiling
12. Hang them in the air to dry well before using
