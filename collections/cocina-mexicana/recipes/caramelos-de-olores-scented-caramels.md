## Ingredients

- sugar syrup at caramel stage
- essential oil
- tincture of fruit flavor
- sweet almond oil

## Directions

1. When the sugar syrup is at the caramel stage, mix in a few drops of the desired essential oil and a little tincture that imitates the flavor of the fruit
2. Let it cool slightly on a plate oiled with sweet almond oil
3. Form small sticks and wrap them in paper
