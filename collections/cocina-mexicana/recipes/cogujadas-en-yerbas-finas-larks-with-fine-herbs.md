## Ingredients

- larks
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- ginger

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the larks and singe them at the flame
2. Put a good piece of butter in a pan with twelve or fifteen larks, salt, coarse pepper, a little clove, cinnamon, and pounded ginger
