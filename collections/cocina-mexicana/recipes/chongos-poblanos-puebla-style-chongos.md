## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- three egg yolks, beaten
- butter
- fine and cold sponge cake, sliced
- fresh cheese
- syrup
- thin slices of butter
- ground cinnamon
- pine nuts
- toasted almonds
- thin slices of fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as Chongos con Queso Fresco
2. With the difference that the garnish is placed over each layer rather than on the last layer only
