## Ingredients

- String beans, cleaned and chopped
- Salt
- Pieces of ham
- Pieces of longaniza sausage
- Lard for frying
- Chopped garlic
- Sliced onions
- Green chiles in strips
- Ground raw tomato
- Ground spices (all except cinnamon)
- Fried breadcrumbs, ground
- Chopped parsley
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Cooked potatoes, chopped (optional)
- Sliced fried blood sausage (optional)
- Fried pork ribs (optional)
- Sliced hard-boiled egg (optional)
- Olives (optional)
- Small chiles (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned, chopped string beans to cook in water with salt, pieces of ham and pieces of longaniza.
2. Fry chopped garlic in lard, and when browned add sliced onions, green chiles in strips and ground raw tomato.
3. When everything is fried, add to the frying mixture the string beans with the ham, longaniza and the broth in which they were cooked, with ground spices (all except cinnamon), fried and ground breadcrumbs, chopped parsley, oil and vinegar.
4. Let everything boil until the string beans are well seasoned and the sauce is thick.
5. If desired, add cooked, chopped potatoes and sliced fried blood sausage to garnish on top, as well as fried pork ribs, sliced hard-boiled egg, olives and small chiles.
