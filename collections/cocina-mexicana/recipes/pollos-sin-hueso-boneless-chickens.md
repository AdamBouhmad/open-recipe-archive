## Ingredients

- chickens
- tomatoes
- garlic
- green chiles
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- lard
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Once the chickens are cooked, remove the bones and cut them into good-sized pieces.
2. Fry tomatoes, garlic and green chiles, all chopped.
3. Season with salt, pepper, clove, cinnamon and saffron, all ground.
4. Coat a pot with lard, place a layer of the fried mixture with raisins, almonds, olives and chiles on top.
