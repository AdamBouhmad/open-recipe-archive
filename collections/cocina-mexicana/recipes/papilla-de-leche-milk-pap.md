## Ingredients

- lard
- anise
- salt
- milk
- flour
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Put a pot on the fire with plenty of lard, a little anise, salt and a little milk at half-cooked temperature and almost cold.
2. Dissolve a little flour as for poleadas and add it to the milk.
3. After it is well cooked and mixed with a little orange blossom water, serve it hot.
4. It can be made sweetened with sugar, and in that case omit the salt or add just one grain.
