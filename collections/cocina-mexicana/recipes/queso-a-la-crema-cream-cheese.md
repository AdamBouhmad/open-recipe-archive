## Ingredients

- three quarters of a quart of cream
- one quarter quart of milk
- rennet
- cream
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Warm three quarters of a quart of cream in one quarter quart of milk, add a little rennet, pass everything through a sieve and let it curdle over embers
2. When curdled, let it drain and serve with cream and fine sugar
