## Ingredients

- hare, boned and roasted
- cow udder, cooked
- bread crumbs soaked in broth
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- thyme
- laurel
- egg yolks
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Bone the roasted hare, removing all membranes and tendons.
2. Chop coarsely and grind all its meat together.
3. Add a cooked cow udder while grinding.
4. Mix with bread crumbs soaked in broth, adding salt, pepper, minced parsley, garlic, thyme and laurel.
5. Bind with egg yolks and grind together again.
6. Line a tin mold with ham slices.
7. Place the mixture on top, covering with more ham slices.
8. Cook in oven or in a bain-marie, that is, place the mold inside another vessel with boiling water.
9. When well cooked, remove and let cool.
10. To remove from mold, place in boiling water, then remove the ham slices and cover with bread crumbs.
