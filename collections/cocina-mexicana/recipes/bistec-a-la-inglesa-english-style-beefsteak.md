## Ingredients

- beef sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- parsley
- verjuice or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut against the grain the beef sirloin into equal slices the thickness of a finger
2. Remove the sinews and skins, flatten with blows of the machete on the flat side, and round them
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Bread them by coating with melted butter and rolling successively in breadcrumbs
5. Grill on the grill and serve on butter kneaded with chopped parsley and a little verjuice or lemon juice
