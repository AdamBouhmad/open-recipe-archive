## Ingredients

- red chile, ancho or pasilla, or a mixture of both
- tomatoes (optional)
- tortillas
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as white chilaquiles
2. Use red chile, ancho or pasilla, or a mixture of the two, with or without tomatoes
3. Ensure abundant lard and salt, since the tortillas have none and the chilaquiles would be bland without sufficient seasoning
4. Vary the garnishes to taste or according to leftovers available in the kitchen, such as roasted meats
