## Ingredients

- Cooked chayotes
- Bread
- Grated cheese
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked chayotes
2. Mix with grated bread and cheese, and beaten eggs, so that it has sufficient consistency to separate portions with a spoon
3. Coat the portions with more beaten egg
4. Fry in butter
5. Serve dry or stewed in pipián
