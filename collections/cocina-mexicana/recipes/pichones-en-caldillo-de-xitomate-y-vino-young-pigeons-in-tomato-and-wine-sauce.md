## Ingredients

- roasted tomatoes
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- fried bread
- raisins
- almonds
- toasted sesame
- Málaga or Parras wine
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- lard

## Directions

1. Fry in lard roasted and ground tomatoes with garlic and parsley and season with salt
2. Add clove, cinnamon and pepper, all ground with fried bread, raisins, almonds, toasted sesame, Málaga or Parras wine, and a little broth from cooking the pigeons
3. Place the pigeons in the sauce and let them boil until well seasoned
