## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Butter
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Raw oil
- Bay leaves
- Lemon or orange slices
- Garlic
- Broth
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges and coat with butter.
2. Roast them on grills.
3. Place in a pan with parsley, pepper, salt, raw oil, bay leaves, lemon or orange slices, pounded garlic, and enough broth to cover.
4. When cooked, remove them.
5. For each bird, add one beaten egg yolk.
