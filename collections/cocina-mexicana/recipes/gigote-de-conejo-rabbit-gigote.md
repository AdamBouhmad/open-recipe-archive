## Ingredients

- rabbits
- salt
- pepper
- oil or lard
- paper with lard
- vinegar
- bread slices
- water

## Directions

1. After cleaning the rabbits, coat with salt and pepper
2. Coat with oil or lard and wrap in buttered paper
3. Place on the grill, turning until half cooked
4. Remove the paper and coat with oil and vinegar, return to grill until well roasted
5. After roasting, chop the legs and loins, reserving one loin and the shoulder blades
6. Arrange bread slices in a pot, place the chopped meat on top
7. Arrange the heads and shoulder blades around the bread
8. Arrange the sliced loin in thin slices on top
9. Add a little water and sufficient salt
10. Sprinkle with a little pepper and add oil and vinegar
11. Place over two gentle fires until the broth is almost consumed
