## Ingredients

- crabs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- cream
- salt
- saffron
- egg yolks
- onion
- garlic
- tomato
- parsley

## Directions

1. Parboil the crabs and set the tails aside
2. Crush the bodies in a mortar and break them in a pot with twelve ounces of butter
3. Let them boil in the butter for about half an hour
4. Strain the substance through a cloth and add to this crab butter half a handful of breadcrumbs dissolved in cream
5. Season with salt, saffron and twelve egg yolks
6. Make small round pastry cases which are sewn or glued with beaten egg and buttered
7. Line with this mixture or stew
8. Add to them a minced mixture of onion, garlic, tomato and parsley, all finely chopped
9. Cover everything with the crab mixture mentioned
10. Put immediately in the oven which should be gently warm
11. When cooked, remove from the cases and serve dry
