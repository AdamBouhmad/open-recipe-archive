## Ingredients

- Young birds
- Lard
- Salt
- Thyme
- Pepper
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds and cut off the feet and half of the wings
2. Fry them without opening, with lard, salt, thyme, pepper and chopped parsley
