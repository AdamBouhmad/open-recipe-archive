## Ingredients

- six pomegranates with very red seeds
- two ounces of currant jelly or, if not available, apple jelly
- eight ounces of powdered sugar
- two cuartillos of common water or more if necessary

## Directions

1. Choose six pomegranates with very red seeds
2. Pound them in a marble mortar with two ounces of currant jelly, or apple jelly if not available, and eight ounces of powdered sugar
3. Make the mixture with the greatest possible care
4. Add two cuartillos of common water, or more if necessary
5. Strain through a very loose cloth or a sieve, pressing strongly
6. Freeze in the manner indicated for other mucilaginous substances that are not very fluid
7. Some prefer to put the cloth in a press to extract all the juice instead of expressing it
