## Ingredients

- pheasant
- butter or lard
- garlic
- sour orange peel
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- white wine
- good broth
- pheasant carapace and bones, ground
- oil
- pheasant liver
- fried bread toasts

## Directions

1. When the pheasant is almost cooked, remove from the fire and take off the wings and legs, dividing each piece in two
2. Remove all remaining meat and place in a casserole with butter or lard, very finely chopped garlic, sour orange peel, salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Add white wine and an equal amount of good broth in which the ground carapace and all the bones have been placed beforehand
4. Let thicken and at the moment of serving add a good spoonful of oil and the crushed liver
5. Garnish on top with fried bread toasts
