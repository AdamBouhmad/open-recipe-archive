## Ingredients

- 20 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- butter
- cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of joining in water with 20 ounces of sugar
2. Off heat, mix in 10 egg yolks, returning it to the stove with gentle heat so the egg cooks
3. When cooked, remove it and add 1 full cup of cream, beating everything so it incorporates well
4. Pour into dishes, grease with butter and brown with heat on top
5. After cooling, dust with cinnamon
