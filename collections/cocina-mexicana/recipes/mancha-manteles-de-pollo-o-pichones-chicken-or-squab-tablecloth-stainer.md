## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, soaked
- cooked apple
- peanuts
- toasted sesame
- pepper
- clove
- garlic
- lard
- salt
- poultry and its broth
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano
- large banana, sliced
- cooked onions

## Directions

1. Grind soaked ancho chiles, cooked apple, peanuts, toasted sesame, pepper, clove, and garlic
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. When ready, add the poultry, its broth, oil, vinegar, oregano, sliced large banana, and cooked onions
4. It will be like the others
