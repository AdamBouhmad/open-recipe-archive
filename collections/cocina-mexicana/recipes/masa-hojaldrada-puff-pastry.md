## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt
- two egg whites
- two spoonfuls of water
- one quarter pound of butter

## Directions

1. Place flour on a clean table forming a hole in the middle
2. Add salt, egg whites, water and butter to the hole
3. Gather the dough and let it rest for half an hour
4. Extend the dough with a rolling pin and cover with one pound of butter, well softened
5. If too warm, let rest again, then fold the two ends of the dough over the butter so it is well covered
6. Extend the dough with the rolling pin until it has only the thickness of a finger, fold in three layers and give four turns so what was at the side comes to the front
7. Roll the pin again - this is called one turn of dough
8. Repeat this operation as many times as necessary and let the dough rest until the oven is hot
9. Give two more turns before baking
10. Use as much butter as flour for five turns, and if there are three quarters butter to one part flour, six turns are needed
