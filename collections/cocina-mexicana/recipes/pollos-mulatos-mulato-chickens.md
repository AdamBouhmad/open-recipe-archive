## Ingredients

- cooked chickens
- toasted almonds
- oil
- hazelnuts
- chicken livers
- a little biscuit
- broth from the chickens
- wine
- clove
- cinnamon
- pimiento, ground
- salt

## Directions

1. After the chickens are cooked, lard them with toasted almonds and fry in oil.
2. Grind hazelnuts, chicken livers, and a little biscuit.
3. Dissolve this in some of the chicken broth and wine.
4. Season with ground clove, cinnamon, pimiento, and necessary salt.
5. Cook the chickens in this sauce until it thickens.
