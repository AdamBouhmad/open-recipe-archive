## Ingredients

- sponge cake (mamón), ground and chilled
- custard, well sweetened
- twelve egg yolks
- spice pastille
- orange blossom water
- butter or lard for greasing
- ground sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Grind the chilled sponge cake well
2. Mix with sweetened custard, twelve egg yolks, spice pastille, and orange blossom water
3. Place in a platter greased with butter or lard
4. Cook over low heat, basting with butter using feathers
5. After cooking, dust the cake with ground sugar and cinnamon on top
