## Ingredients

- bean sprouts
- peas
- shrimp
- lard
- onion
- green chiles

## Directions

1. Clean bean sprouts and peas and bring to boil
2. Clean shrimp, bring to boil and once all this is cooked put a cazuela with lard on the heat
3. Add a small amount of onion in chunks
4. Once fried add the bean sprouts and peas
5. Once seasoned add the shrimp together with the water they boiled in
6. Roast three or four green chiles and add in strips
7. Let boil a while and remove from heat
