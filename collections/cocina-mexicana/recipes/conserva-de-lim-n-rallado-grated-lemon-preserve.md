## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar) made with fine sugar
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)
- Honey

## Directions

1. Grate the lemons after removing only the yellow rind without taking any of the white interior
2. Pass through with a punch tool
3. Cook in water alone, then pass to other hot water
4. Leave in it; the next day, change to other hot water and repeat this morning and evening for six to eight days until they lose their bitterness completely and taste no longer bitter
5. Place over gentle heat in fine sugar syrup
6. Give one boil the next day, and another on the third day
7. Finish with orange blossom water and bring to honey point
