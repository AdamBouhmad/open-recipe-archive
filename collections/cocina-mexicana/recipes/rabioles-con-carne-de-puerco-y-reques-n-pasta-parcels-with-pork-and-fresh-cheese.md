## Ingredients

- pork loin from the head
- beef marrow
- fresh cheese curds
- spinach
- three or four raw eggs
- good grated cheese
- salt
- pepper
- good cooking oil
- flour
- warm water
- veal or beef pulp
- lard
- garlic heads
- whole onions
- ground pepper
- Jerez wine
- bay leaves
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Chop raw pork loin and grind with beef marrow, fresh cheese curds, and half-cooked spinach
2. Remove all skin and tendons from the meat and mix with three or four raw eggs, good grated cheese, sufficient salt and pepper, and a little good cooking oil
3. Once well mixed and the filling paste formed, moisten sufficient flour with warm water
4. Mix two or three eggs into the dough according to quantity and add a little salt
5. Mix everything well so the dough is soft and divide into three or four portions
6. Cover with a damp cloth and dust the work surface with dry flour
7. Extend each portion one by one with a rolling pin until it forms a sheet as thin as paper
8. Place balls of filling at equal distances on the sheet of dough
9. Fold part of the dough to cover them and press the edges of the covered dough balls with three fingers
10. Cut with a pastry wheel into small pastries or squares to form the rabioles
11. Bring salted water to boil in a pot and when boiling strongly, add the pastries in portions
12. Once they float, showing they are cooked, remove with a skimmer and drain without letting them stick
13. Cut veal or beef pulp into regular pieces and fry with lard in a pot until browned
14. Add a pair of garlic heads and whole onions, ground pepper and sufficient salt
15. Moisten with a quarter of Jerez wine and a few bay leaves
16. When the pot comes to a boil, increase the broth by adding water or meat broth
17. Cover with an adjusted pot and seal the edges with paste, putting on low heat until the broth is well seasoned
18. Shake the pot occasionally so it does not burn
19. In a platter, make a layer of the pastries, cover well with another layer of grated cheese
20. Bathe with some of the stewed broth and continue multiplying layers of rabioles and cheese
21. Moisten with the stewed broth and place the soup over the steam of a pot until ready to serve
