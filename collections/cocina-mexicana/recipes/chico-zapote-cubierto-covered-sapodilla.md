## Ingredients

- Sapodillas
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Peel in such a way that no bitter remains
2. Place to cook over gentle heat so the water does not boil
3. Prepare the syrup and add the sapodillas to give a boil
4. Remove and let rest in the sweet until the next day
5. Give another boil and rest until the next day when the final boil is given
6. Afterwards remove to dry and add to syrup that is at the proper point
7. Place on heat until it reaches the point of bladder stage
8. Then remove, drain, beat the syrup and pour it over them or roll them in honey so they are well covered with sweetness
