## Ingredients

- Eight cuartillos of milk
- Three ounces of starch
- A handful of wheat, ground
- A handful of almonds, ground
- One ounce of cinnamon
- Ingredients for other milk cakes

## Directions

1. Add three ounces of starch to eight cuartillos of milk and stir well until it dissolves
2. Add a handful of ground wheat and a handful of ground almonds
3. Add one ounce of cinnamon
4. Strain everything
5. Heat until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco (milk pudding)
6. Remove from heat and proceed as for other milk cakes
