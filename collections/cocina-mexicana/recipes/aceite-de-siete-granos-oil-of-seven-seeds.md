## Ingredients

- One ounce of green anise
- One ounce of dry anise
- One ounce of cumin
- One ounce of caraway
- One ounce of dill
- Three ounces of coriander
- Two ounces of fennel
- Sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Pound coarsely one ounce of green anise, one of dry anise, one of cumin, one of caraway, one of dill, three of coriander, and two of fennel
2. Put everything in infusion at atmospheric temperature in sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente
3. Proceed afterwards to distillation to obtain half of the liquor
4. Sweeten it the same way as in the preceding article
