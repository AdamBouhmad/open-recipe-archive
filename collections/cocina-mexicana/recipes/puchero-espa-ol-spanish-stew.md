## Ingredients

- 1 to 2 pounds veal or mutton
- 1 to 2 pounds chickpeas, soaked from the night before
- 1 good slice of raw lean ham
- poultry giblets
- salt
- chorizo
- tomatoes
- cumin
- vinegar
- parsley
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Add 1 to 2 pounds of veal or mutton to a pot with 1 to 2 pounds of soaked chickpeas in proportion.
2. Add a good slice of raw lean ham and poultry giblets.
3. When it boils, skim and add salt.
4. After boiling for half an hour, remove the necessary broth for the sauce you wish to prepare.
5. Add the legumes to the stew, which should be boiled separately.
6. Before everything is well cooked, add a chorizo.
7. When serving, place the meats in one dish and the legumes in another with tomato or parsley sauce.
8. For the tomato sauce: split three tomatoes and pass through a sieve, then place in a dish with the reserved broth, adding cumin, salt and vinegar. Let it boil a moment and pour into a sauce boat.
9. For the parsley sauce: grind very green parsley bound with bread crumbs, and place both in a dish with broth, salt and vinegar, letting it boil a good while and stirring with a wooden spoon until it comes together, then serve.
