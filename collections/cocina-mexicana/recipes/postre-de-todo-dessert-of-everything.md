## Ingredients

- Syrup made with eight pounds of sugar
- One pound of almond
- One pound of sweet potato, ground
- One pound of grated coconut
- A cuartillo of custard
- Twelve egg yolks
- Mamón or alone on a platter

## Directions

1. Make syrup with eight pounds of sugar
2. Add one pound of almond, one pound of ground sweet potato, and one pound of grated coconut
3. Let boil well
4. Then add a cuartillo of custard and twelve egg yolks, stirring the paste as they are added so they do not set
5. When it reaches the point of cajeta, empty into a platter alone or over sliced mamón
