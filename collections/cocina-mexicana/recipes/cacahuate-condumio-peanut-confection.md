## Ingredients

- Panela or unrefined cane sugar
- Peanuts, cleaned and ground

## Directions

1. Make a honey syrup from panela at the medium-ball stage of cooking
2. Add cleaned and ground peanuts to the syrup, allowing the mixture to reach the stage of cajeta (soft-ball stage)
3. Remove from heat and mix in two pounds of ground or toasted peanuts in halves
4. Return to the fire and allow to reach the cajeta stage again
5. Remove from heat and beat until it begins to set
6. Using a spoon, drop portions onto paper to cool
7. Once cooled and set, cut the candies into desired sizes and shapes
