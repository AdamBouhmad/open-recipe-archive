## Ingredients

- carrot
- aged cheese
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook the carrot, cut it and remove the core
2. Once cooked, squeeze it and mash in the dry metate
3. Grind aged cheese, beat the egg and add the carrot to the egg
4. Put the pan with lard, make small cakes with a spoon and add to the pan
5. Eat with chipotle sauce
