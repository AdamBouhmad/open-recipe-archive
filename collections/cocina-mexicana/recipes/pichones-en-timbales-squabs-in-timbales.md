## Ingredients

- Many squabs
- Water and salt
- Mushrooms
- Veal sweetbreads
- Oil
- Butter or lard
- 4 egg yolks
- Essence of sautéed vegetables

## Directions

1. Cook many squabs in salted water
2. Parboil mushrooms and veal sweetbreads, cutting them into dice-shaped pieces
3. Make the stew like the compote from previous recipes
4. Allow to cool
5. Make a dough with a little oil, butter or lard, and four egg yolks
6. Make as many dough bases as there are squabs
7. Place these dough bases in the timbales, add some of the stew, one squab, and cover with more stew
8. Cover everything with the dough
9. Seal the edges and bake in the oven
10. Serve with some essence or thinned essence
