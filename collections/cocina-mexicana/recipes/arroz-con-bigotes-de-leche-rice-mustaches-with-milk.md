## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- fine salt
- sugar syrup made from white sugar, well clarified
- flour
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon powder
- orange leaves

## Directions

1. After washing the rice two or three times so it does not break, put it to cook in milk with a little fine salt
2. Then add sugar syrup made from white sugar, well clarified until the rice cooks well
3. When cooked, let it reduce until it thickens greatly, pour into a platter
4. The next day, take portions of this rice and form whatever figures are desired
5. Roll in flour, then fry in butter until the surface is golden
6. Dust with sugar and cinnamon powder, place on a platter over orange leaves and cover with others
