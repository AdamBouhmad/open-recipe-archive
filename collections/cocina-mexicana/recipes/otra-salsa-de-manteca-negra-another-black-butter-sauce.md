## Ingredients

- Butter
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Place butter in a pan, which should be left until it also burns, without being black
2. Add a little parsley so it fries
3. Add vinegar, salt, pepper and bay leaf
4. Let it cook a little and remove the parsley and bay leaf with a skimmer
