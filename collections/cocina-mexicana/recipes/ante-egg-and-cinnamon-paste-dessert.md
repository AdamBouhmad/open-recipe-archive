## Ingredients

- Eggs, one pound
- Sugar, one pound
- Cinnamon, one ounce
- Syrup, three pounds, clarified
- Syrup at medium point
- Sponge cake
- Raisins and almonds
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Make syrup at the proportions mentioned and let it boil until the point of cajeta
2. In syrup at medium point, soak sponge cake and place one layer of sponge cake and another of eggs
3. Decorate with raisins and almonds
4. Add Sherry wine to the syrup in which the sponge cake is soaked
