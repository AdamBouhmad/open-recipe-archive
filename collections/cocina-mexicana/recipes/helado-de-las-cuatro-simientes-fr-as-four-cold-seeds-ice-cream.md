## Ingredients

- four ounces sweet almonds
- equal quantity of melon seeds
- pumpkin seeds
- cucumber seeds
- watermelon seeds with their shells
- five ounces sugar
- a strip of lemon or sour orange rind

## Directions

1. Blanch four ounces of sweet almonds
2. Unite with equal quantity of melon seeds, pumpkin, cucumber and watermelon seeds with their shells
3. Grind in a mortar, sprinkling with water, increasing it to about two quartillos
4. Strain through a cloth, squeezing well
5. Add five ounces of sugar and a strip of lemon or sour orange rind
6. Strain again through the cloth and freeze like the other beverages
