## Ingredients

- Flattened meat
- Chickpeas
- Ham
- Chard
- Tomatoes without seeds
- Spices
- Ham slices
- Capers

## Directions

1. Flatten the meat and fill it with chickpeas, ham, and chard
2. Knead with lard
3. Fry well
4. While frying, add tomatoes without seeds and spices so it cooks with a sauce
5. Serve with ham slices and capers
