## Ingredients

- A little butter
- Fine salt
- Milk
- Flour

## Directions

1. Put a little butter and fine salt with milk in a pot on the fire
2. When it is about to boil, add the flour, which is left to dry on the flame, as in the dough for cabbages
3. Immediately extend with a rolling pin as thin as possible
4. Cut into small squares
5. Fry in oil, bathe with sugar, and serve as a roasted dish or sweetmeat
