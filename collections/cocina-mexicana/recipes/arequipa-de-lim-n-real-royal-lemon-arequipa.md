## Ingredients

- lemons without skin or seeds
- 8 pounds prepared lemons
- 4.5 pounds sugar
- red coloring

## Directions

1. Take lemons without skin or seeds, sun-dry them and add water for eight days.
2. Cook and grate them.
3. To eight pounds of this preparation add four and a half pounds of sugar made into clarified syrup.
4. Color with red coloring.
5. Cook until it does not fall from the spoon.
