## Ingredients

- parsnips
- beets
- carrots
- turnips
- sweet potatoes
- potatoes
- chayote root or chayote itself if in season
- cooked lettuces
- broth from cooked roots
- fat or lean broth

## Directions

1. Boil and prepare parsnips, beets, carrots and turnips as for entry dishes
2. Prepare sweet potatoes, potatoes, and chayote root or chayote itself separately
3. Place cooked, drained, well-squeezed and pressed lettuces in the interior of the mold or pot in place of meat
4. Fill the pot or mold and keep it hot in a water bath
5. Boil the broth in which the roots were cooked, adding fat or lean broth as desired
6. Strain through a very fine cloth and reduce to half
7. Place the cartuja on a plate and pour the sauce over it
