## Ingredients

- brains
- artichokes
- chicken giblets
- lamb testicles
- tongue or beef tongue
- butter or margarine
- salt
- flour
- water
- egg yolks
- cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Compose the dish of brains, artichokes, chicken giblets, lamb testicles and tongue or beef tongue.
2. Cook everything separately and divide into medium-sized pieces that are put to fry in a pan with butter or margarine and the corresponding salt.
3. Dissolve a little flour in half a glass of water, sufficient so that the water is not clear, but like broth, and add it to the fricassee.
4. When it has boiled well and consumed what is necessary, remove from the heat while very hot and dissolve two or three egg yolks, which are stirred together with the rest, and adding cloves and pepper, serve immediately.
