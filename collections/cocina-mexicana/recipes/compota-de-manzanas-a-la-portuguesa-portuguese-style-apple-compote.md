## Ingredients

- apples
- ground sugar

## Directions

1. Core the apples needed to fill the compote dish or serving platter using a tin-leaf corer or knife
2. Place them in a baking dish or silver plate with ground sugar on the bottom
3. Put a small piece of sugar or powdered sugar in each apple
4. Bake them in the oven or cook them between two fires
5. Serve hot with ground sugar on top
