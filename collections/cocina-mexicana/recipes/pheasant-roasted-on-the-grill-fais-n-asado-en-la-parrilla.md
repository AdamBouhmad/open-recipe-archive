## Ingredients

- pheasant
- ham
- garlic cloves
- cinnamon
- orange leaves
- avocado leaves
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- buttered paper or banana leaves
- oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the pheasant well, lard all its meaty parts with ham, garlic cloves, cinnamon, orange leaves, and avocado leaves
2. Coat well with salt, pepper, and lard
3. Cover with buttered paper or banana leaves
4. Place on the grill over gentle heat with a griddle on top
5. Turn from time to time so it cooks evenly
6. When tender, remove the paper or leaves and coat with oil and lemon juice
7. Leave on the grill at two fires until toasted
