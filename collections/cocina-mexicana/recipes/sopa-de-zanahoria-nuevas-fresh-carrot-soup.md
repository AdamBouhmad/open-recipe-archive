## Ingredients

- fresh carrots
- broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots into long, thin strips
2. Blanch them
3. Place them in broth
4. When they are cooked, put them in the soup tureen, in which the bread will already have been soaked
5. Serve
