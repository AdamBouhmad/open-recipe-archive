## Ingredients

- Wide pork loins
- spices
- lard
- onion
- water
- flour
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the wide loins of pork into strips one finger thick
2. Strike them with the back of the cleaver or with the flat of the cleaver to thin them
3. Chop the remaining meat from the loins
4. Season the chopped meat with all spices except saffron, and place the chopped meat on the chops using the point of the knife
5. Wrap each strip and tie it in the middle with thread
6. Make a light broth by frying finely chopped onion in lard and adding water with very little flour so it does not thicken, and sufficient salt
7. Place the chops there to cook
8. When cooked, remove the thread and season the broth with all spices except saffron
9. In another pan, beat some egg yolks with a little lemon juice and finely chopped parsley
10. At the time of serving the chops and while they are boiling in their pan, remove them, add the beaten egg, shake the pan, and empty into a platter without putting a spoon in so they do not break
