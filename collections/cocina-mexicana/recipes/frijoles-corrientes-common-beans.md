## Ingredients

- beans
- tequesquite water
- lard
- cooking water
- onion
- epazote

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and cook in tequesquite water; once cooked remove from heat and let rest a while, empty into a cazuela to cool
2. Then place in a pot with a little lard and after some time add water from the same pot they cooked in and natural water
3. Place in the pot some onion tails, proportional lard and a branch of epazote
4. Bring to boil and after boiling let simmer on low heat to thicken
