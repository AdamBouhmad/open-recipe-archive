## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Roasted xitomate
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Oregano
- Cooked onions
- Oil
- Peeled avocado
- Cooked onion quarters
- Chopped tornachiles
- Small zucchini quarters
- Olives

## Directions

1. After the cauliflower is well cooked, divide it into its florets, slicing lengthwise those that are very large.
2. Place in a serving dish and cover with a sauce made as follows: grind roasted xitomate, dissolve it in vinegar, add sufficient salt and dust with oregano.
3. Roughly chop some cooked onions and mix in sufficient oil.
4. Cover the cauliflower with this sauce and garnish with slices of peeled avocado, quarters of cooked onion, chopped tornachiles, quarters of small zucchini, and olives.
