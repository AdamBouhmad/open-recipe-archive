## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Butter
- Good broth
- Onions
- Mixed herbs
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Sausages
- Bread

## Directions

1. Parboil the cabbage divided into four parts, having removed the core
2. Refresh in cold water, squeeze, tie, and place to cook with a piece of butter, good broth, onions, mixed herbs, salt, and whole pepper
3. When it begins to cook, add some sausages to complete the cooking
4. Fry in butter a piece of bread larger than the hand and place at the bottom of the serving plate
5. Arrange the quarters of cabbage around it with the sausages and onions, which should be well drained of fat
6. Reduce the sauce or broth in which the cabbage was cooked, mixing it with concentrated broth if available, and ensure it reduces so the sauce becomes light and good flavored, then pour over the dish
