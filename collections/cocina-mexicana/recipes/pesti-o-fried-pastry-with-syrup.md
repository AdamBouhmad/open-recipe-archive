## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- oil for frying
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Make a dough with flour and eggs
2. Cut the dough into small pieces
3. Fry in oil until toasted
4. Coat with clarified syrup that is brought to the proper point
