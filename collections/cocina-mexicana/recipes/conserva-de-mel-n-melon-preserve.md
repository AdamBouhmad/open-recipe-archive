## Ingredients

- ripe but firm melon
- water
- syrup

## Directions

1. Choose a ripe but firm melon
2. Divide it into slices, peel them, and cut them in half
3. Wash with water and add to the syrup
4. Once they are penetrated and tender, let the syrup thicken and remove from heat
