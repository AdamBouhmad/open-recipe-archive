## Ingredients

- six quartillos of milk
- three onions cut in quarters
- fresh cilantro
- parsley
- one whole small headed onion
- thyme
- laurel
- basil
- rabbit livers, chopped
- half pound of jowl cut in square pieces
- fine salt
- rabbit tripe, well cleaned
- two quartillos of fresh thick cream

## Directions

1. Bring six quartillos of milk to boil with three onions cut in quarters, fresh cilantro, parsley, one whole small headed onion, thyme, laurel, and basil.
2. Once the milk has reduced to one third, strain it and mix in plenty of chopped rabbit livers and half a pound of jowl cut in square pieces.
3. Empty everything into a deep basin or pan with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and two quartillos of fresh thick cream.
4. Mix everything perfectly and use this to fill the well-cleaned tripe.
5. Cook these puddings like the black ones: boil in milk, and after they are cold, grill them on paper over gentle heat.
