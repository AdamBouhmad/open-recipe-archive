## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- tomatoes
- sugar
- cheese

## Directions

1. After cooking the white cabbage, chop it and fry in butter with chopped onion, garlic, and tomatoes
2. Once done, add the cabbage to fry halfway
3. Add ground sugar to give it sweetness and plenty of grated large cheese or fresh ground cheese
4. When mixed, let it cool and add sufficient beaten eggs
5. Once well incorporated, pour into a buttered casserole, add more egg on top and place over two fires
6. Once set, sprinkle with ground sugar
