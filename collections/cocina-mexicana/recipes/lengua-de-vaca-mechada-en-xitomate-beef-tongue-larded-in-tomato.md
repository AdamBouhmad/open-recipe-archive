## Ingredients

- Lard
- Ham strips
- Chorizo slices
- Garlic cloves, peeled
- Roasted tomatoes
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Fry ham strips, chorizo slices, and peeled garlic cloves in lard
2. Once fried, remove them and grind the fried garlic with roasted tomatoes
3. Fry the ground mixture and add back the fried ham and chorizo along with cloves, pepper, and cinnamon
