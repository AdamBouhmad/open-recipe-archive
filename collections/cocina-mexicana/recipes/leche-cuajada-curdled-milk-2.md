## Ingredients

- milk
- pellicle from two chicken gizzards
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take milk with no water and cook until it thickens
2. Sweeten to taste
3. When cool, add the pellicle from two chicken gizzards ground to powder
4. Return to cook like jericalla
5. Sprinkle with cinnamon
