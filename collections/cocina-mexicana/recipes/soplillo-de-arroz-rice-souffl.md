## Ingredients

- two ounces of puffed and cooked rice
- sugar
- orange blossom
- two bitter macaroons
- four egg yolks
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Take two ounces of puffed and cooked rice and put it in a pot with sugar, orange blossom, two bitter macaroons, four egg yolks and six beaten egg whites
2. Follow the same procedures indicated for the other soufflés for cooking
