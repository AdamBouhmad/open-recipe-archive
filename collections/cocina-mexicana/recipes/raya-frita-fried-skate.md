## Ingredients

- Skate
- salt
- coriander
- mace
- pepper
- cloves
- garlic
- large onion sliced
- parsley
- chives
- butter kneaded with flour
- vinegar
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the skate
2. Cut into slices without separating the filaments
3. Place in a marinade with salt, a little coriander, mace, pepper, cloves, garlic, sliced large onion, parsley, chives, butter kneaded with a little flour, vinegar and fine herbs
4. Heat the marinade so the butter melts and leave the skate slices in it for about four hours
5. Drain and fry until they take on a beautiful color
6. Serve with fried parsley
