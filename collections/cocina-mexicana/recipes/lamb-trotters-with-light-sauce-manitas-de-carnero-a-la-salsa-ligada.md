## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- light white broth
- curled sauce
- concentrated broth
- egg yolks
- nutmeg
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb trotters in light white broth for four hours
2. Test for doneness by pressing with fingers until meat yields easily and feels tender
3. Remove and drain the trotters, trim the extremities so the feet remain whole and clean
4. Place in a saucepan and add six ladle-fulls of curled sauce and four of concentrated broth
5. Allow to reduce until only half remains
6. Just before serving, add a liaison of two egg yolks with a little nutmeg to the sauce
7. Once thickened, strain the sauce and pour over the trotters, which should be kept hot but not boiling
8. Add some black pepper
9. If curled sauce is not available, prepare an alternative broth: in a saucepan with butter, add finely minced garlic and parsley, cook until rendered, add one spoonful of flour with salt, black pepper, nutmeg and broth, bring to boil, thicken with egg yolks, and finish with lemon juice at service
