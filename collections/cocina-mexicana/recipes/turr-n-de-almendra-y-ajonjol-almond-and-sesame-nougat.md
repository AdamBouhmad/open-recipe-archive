## Ingredients

- six pounds of very white and fluffy sugar
- one cuartillo of virgin honey
- ten egg whites per pound of sugar
- toasted almonds
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Put six pounds of very white and fluffy sugar in a pan with one cuartillo of virgin honey, clarify both together, and after straining this syrup, return it to the heat until it rises in point, which is known by taking a little bit and throwing it in cold water to see if it breaks, and checking if the point is that of toffees, which is what it should have
2. If it is so, remove from heat and keep stirring until it cools
3. Have ready beaten and very fluffy egg whites, at the rate of ten for each pound of sugar, and add a few of them; beat them strongly in the syrup, and when these are consumed add more, increasing this way until all are added
4. Then place on heat always stirring the nougat so that it does not stick to the pan, and at the bottom it should be seen when stirring, to remove it from the heat and pour it onto the wafers already prepared of the desired size; with small spoons pour it into them, covering them with another without force so they do not fall
5. Add the toasted almonds and sesame when it is already at the right point
