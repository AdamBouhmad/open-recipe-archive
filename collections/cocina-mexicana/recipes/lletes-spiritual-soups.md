## Ingredients

- water
- one whole onion
- five mint leaves
- toasted coriander
- a little pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- one loaf of bread, crumbled
- one pound of sugar
- sesame
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- pieces of candied citron

## Directions

1. Put some water to boil with a whole onion and five mint leaves
2. Once the onion is cooked, remove it and also remove the mint
3. In the remaining water, add toasted coriander, a little pepper, clove and cinnamon, all ground, and a little salt
4. Add one crumbled loaf of bread, one pound of sugar, sesame, walnuts, pine nuts, and pieces of candied citron
5. Leave on the fire until the soup is thick
6. Serve with dragées and ground cinnamon on top
