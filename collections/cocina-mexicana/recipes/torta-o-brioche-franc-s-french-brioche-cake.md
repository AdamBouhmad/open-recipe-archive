## Ingredients

- one pound of fine flour
- two ounces of powdered sugar
- one dozen eggs
- one ounce of ordinary yeast, or brewer's yeast if available

## Directions

1. Form a dough with the flour, powdered sugar, and eggs
2. Add the yeast to the mixture
3. Leave the dough wrapped in a napkin for twelve to fourteen hours so that it rises or ferments
4. Shape into small rolls, cakes, etc.
5. Bake in the oven
