## Ingredients

- Six pounds of sugar
- Two egg yolks
- Three quartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Make syrup with six pounds of sugar and clarify with two egg yolks beaten in water.
2. Strain through a cloth and bring to the same point as in the previous article, at which state add three quartillos of milk and let it regain the point over the fire.
3. Conclude the operation the same as the previous article.
