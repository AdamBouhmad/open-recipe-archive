## Ingredients

- fresh shrimp
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- onion, chopped

## Directions

1. Put them to cook with salt
2. After cooking, clean them of all their shells, feet and head
3. If they are large, slice or cut them into pieces; if small, leave them whole
4. Serve with oil, vinegar, salt and chopped onion
