## Ingredients

- pound and a half of fresh cheese
- any preserved fruit except quince, drained and ground
- sifted sugar
- 4 ounces of toasted sponge cake
- cinnamon and cloves, ground together
- well-beaten eggs, whites and yolks separated then reunited
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix the fresh cheese with any preserved fruit except quince, drained and ground
2. If the batter does not have sufficient sweetness after mixing, add sifted sugar until it is pleasant to the taste
3. Mix in four ounces of toasted sponge cake and the ground cinnamon and cloves
4. Mix everything with well-beaten and risen eggs with whites and yolks separated then reunited
5. Pour into a small pan or skillet lined with lard
6. Bake the cake at two gentle fires
7. Place in syrup and decorate like the other cakes
