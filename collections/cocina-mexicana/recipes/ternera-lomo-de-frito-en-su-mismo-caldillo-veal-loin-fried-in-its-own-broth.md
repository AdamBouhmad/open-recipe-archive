## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Water
- Salt
- Garlic, ground
- Lard
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Make cuts in the loin with the knife that pass to the opposite side, and place to cook in a pot with water, salt, sufficient ground garlic, lard, thyme and laurel.
2. Burn lard in a casserole, and when the loin is cooked, empty the pot into it, removing the thyme and laurel, and let the broth reduce over two fires until it becomes completely dry and fried.
3. Serve thus or on lettuce leaves.
