## Ingredients

- white beans
- clean garlic
- sliced onion
- green cilantro
- lard
- chopped onion
- toasted and ground dried broad chile
- cooked beans
- salt
- grated fresh cheese or aged ground cheese
- sliced cheese
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. After washing, put the beans to cook in water with clean garlic, sliced onion, and green cilantro.
2. When cooked, fry more chopped onion in lard and a little toasted and ground dried broad chile to give it color.
3. Add the cooked beans, separating a handful to grind, and after grinding, add them with the broth in which they all cooked.
4. Season with necessary salt and grated fresh or aged ground cheese.
5. Let it boil until seasoned. To serve, decorate with sliced cheese, small chiles, and olives.
