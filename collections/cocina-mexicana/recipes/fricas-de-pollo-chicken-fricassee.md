## Ingredients

- Chicken, plucked and singed
- Butter
- Flour
- Water
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Parsley with small onions (bouquet)
- Three egg yolks
- Lemon juice or verjuice or vinegar
- Mushrooms (optional)
- Onions and artichoke hearts (optional)

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe over flame, and empty the chicken
2. Quarter it and soak in cold water for one to two hours so the meat becomes white
3. Drain the pieces
4. Put a little butter and a spoonful of flour in a saucepan and stir until the butter melts
5. Add a glass of water with salt, white pepper, nutmeg scrapings, and a bouquet of parsley with small onions
6. Put the chicken pieces in this broth and cook for three quarters of an hour
7. Make a separate sauce with three egg yolks and lemon juice, or verjuice, or a dash of vinegar
8. If adding mushrooms, do so when the chicken is more than half cooked
9. If mixing onions and artichoke hearts, add these to the broth at the same time as the chicken
10. Serve with crayfish, fried toast, and rooster combs around the plate
11. To keep the chicken meat white, rub with lemon and cover while cooking with a wheel of buttered paper placed on the fricassee inside the saucepan
