## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or similar cuts
- lard
- salt
- chopped tomato
- a little water
- wide chiles
- lard

## Directions

1. Fry lamb shoulder cuts in lard and season with salt
2. Add generously of wine such as Málaga and a little vinegar, roasted and ground tomato mixed with cloves, cinnamon and pepper, a little sugar, raisins and halved almonds
3. Cover the pot and let the meat cook
4. Empty into a cazuela and thicken the sauce without letting it dry completely
