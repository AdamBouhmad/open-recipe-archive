## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- onion puree
- Spanish sauce or broth
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook as the previous recipe
2. Drain and place in a pot with several spoonfuls of dark onion puree and the same of Spanish sauce
3. In the absence of Spanish sauce, use substantial broth or good pot broth, salt, and pepper
4. Sauté adding a good piece of butter
5. Sauté again and serve while hot
