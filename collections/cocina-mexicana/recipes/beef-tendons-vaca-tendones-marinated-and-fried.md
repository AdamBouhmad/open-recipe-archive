## Ingredients

- Beef tendons
- Ham or lard slices
- Marinade
- Batter for frying
- Oil for frying
- Fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the beef tendons to imitate oysters.
2. Place them in a casserole lined with slices of ham or lard and cover with more slices.
3. Moisten with a marinade and let cook for an hour and a half.
4. At serving time, drain and cover with batter for frying.
5. Fry in oil that should not be too hot.
6. Serve with a handful of fried parsley on top.
