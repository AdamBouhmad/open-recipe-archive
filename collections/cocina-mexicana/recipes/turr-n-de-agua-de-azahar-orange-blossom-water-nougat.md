## Ingredients

- sugar
- virgin honey
- egg whites
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Make it the same as the white spoon or foam nougat of the previous articles, omitting the almonds, walnuts, pine nuts and sesame
2. Add a little orange blossom water while beating
