## Ingredients

- Mulberries
- White wine
- Candied citron
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the mulberries
2. Once cooled, place them on a plate with white wine, candied citron, raisins, almonds cut in quarters, and powder of sugar and cinnamon
