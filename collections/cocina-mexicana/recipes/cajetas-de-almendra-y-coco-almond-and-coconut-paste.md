## Ingredients

- coco (coconut)
- almendras (almonds)
- azúcar (sugar)
- almíbar clarificado (clarified syrup)

## Directions

1. Shell and grind a coconut
2. Peel and pound half a pound of almonds
3. Place both in clarified syrup made with two pounds of sugar
4. Heat until it reaches the eye point and remove from heat
