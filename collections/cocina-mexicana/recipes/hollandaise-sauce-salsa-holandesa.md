## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- five or six egg yolks
- salt
- coarse pepper
- juice of three lemons
- half a glass of water

## Directions

1. Knead together the butter, flour, and egg yolks
2. Place this paste in a saucepan with salt, coarse pepper, the juice of three lemons, and half a glass of water
3. Heat everything, stirring without interruption until it has acquired sufficient consistency to cover vegetables or fish
4. Pour over the vegetables or fish to be served
