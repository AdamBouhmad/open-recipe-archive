## Ingredients

- turnips
- butter
- meat broth
- bread toast

## Directions

1. Cut and shape the turnips as for carrot soup
2. Fry them in butter until they brown and have a good color
3. Cook them in broth
4. Prepare toasted bread
5. Place the cooked turnips on top of the toast
