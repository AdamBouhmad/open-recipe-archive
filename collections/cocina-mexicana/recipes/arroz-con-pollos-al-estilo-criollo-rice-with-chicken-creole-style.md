## Ingredients

- 2 good chickens
- butter
- fine salt
- a bundle of herbs (see manojito surtido)
- 2 cloves
- 10 chiltipiquines, ground or crushed, or other hot chiles in their place
- a little saffron
- good broth
- 30 small round onions
- 1 pound of rice

## Directions

1. Choose 2 good chickens, cut them and arrange them as if to fry, and fry them in butter seasoned with fine salt, a bundle of mixed herbs (see manojito surtido), 2 cloves, 10 ground or crushed chiltipiquines or other hot chiles in their place, and a little saffron
2. Moisten the chickens with good broth, adding 30 small round onions that should be cleaned and cut as evenly as possible, being careful to remove the extremities and the heart
3. Fry separately until they are very golden; then drain and put to cook with the chickens, boiling everything over strong heat
4. Wash 1 pound of rice in six waters so no dust remains
5. Put to boil and discard the first water in which it boiled, and let it cook in another water so it does not break too much
6. Serve the chickens in one dish and the rice in another, being careful not to break the rice
