## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Onion
- Butter or fresh butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Broth

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a good handful of parsley and onion and sauté with butter or fresh butter, moderating the heat so the butter does not brown
2. Add a good spoonful of flour
3. Season with salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Add one cup of broth and let simmer slowly for ten minutes
5. Cut the meat from the boiled beef into thin slices, removing the fatty part, and let it simmer a moment in the sauce
