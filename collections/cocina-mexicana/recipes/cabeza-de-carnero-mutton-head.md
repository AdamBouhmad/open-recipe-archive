## Ingredients

- Mutton head
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Salt
- Thin ham
- Picante sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the head and cut it in half.
2. Lift the cartilages with a knife.
3. Inside, place chopped parsley, crushed garlic, pepper, salt and a little thin ham.
4. Tie with string.
5. Wrap in brown paper.
6. Roast and serve with a picante sauce.
