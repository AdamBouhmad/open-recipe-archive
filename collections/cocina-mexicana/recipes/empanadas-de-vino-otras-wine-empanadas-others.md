## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- four egg yolks
- salt water
- tequesquite
- butter

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour, add four egg yolks, a bit of salt water, a powder of tequesquite, and necessary butter to soften it.
2. Knead and let it rest covered for a while, then extend with a rolling pin.
3. Cut the dough to the desired size (smaller ones are better).
4. Brush with butter and roll into a ball again, then extend each one again and finish as the previous ones.
