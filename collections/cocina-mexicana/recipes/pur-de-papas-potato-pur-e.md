## Ingredients

- 4 pounds white potatoes, peeled and cut in quarters
- water
- salt
- butter
- broth from poultry

## Directions

1. Cook four pounds of white potatoes, peeled and cut in quarters, in water and salt.
2. Before they are completely cooked, remove all the water well.
3. In the same covered pot, place under the stove so they finish cooking and become mealy.
4. Stir with a spoon, adding a little butter and pass through a strainer; dissolve with poultry broth and pass through a sieve.
