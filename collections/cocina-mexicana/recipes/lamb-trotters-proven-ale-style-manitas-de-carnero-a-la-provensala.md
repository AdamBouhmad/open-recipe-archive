## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- onions
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the trotters as described for the light sauce version
2. Drain and clean them and place in a saucepan
3. Cut several onions in half and remove the heads, tails and centers, slicing crosswise so only semi-circular slices of the thickness of a line remain
4. Heat half a quarter-liter of oil in a saucepan over strong fire
5. Fry the onions stirring with a wooden spoon until golden
6. Remove from fire and pour off some of the oil
7. To the remaining oil add salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, the juice of three or four lemons and two full spoonfuls of broth
8. Bring the sauce to a boil and pour over the trotters, keeping them hot without boiling
9. Arrange in the serving dish, place the fried onion on top and sprinkle with lemon juice
