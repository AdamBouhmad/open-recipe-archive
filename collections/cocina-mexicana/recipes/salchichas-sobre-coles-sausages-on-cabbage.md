## Ingredients

- One cabbage
- Ham slice
- Melted lard
- Meat broth
- Common broth
- Salt and pepper
- Grilled sausages

## Directions

1. Divide cabbage into four parts, wash and blanch it, then plunge into cold water
2. Drain and cut into strips
3. In a pot, brown a ham slice and add the cabbage with melted lard
4. Moisten with meat broth and common broth, season with salt and pepper, and cook
5. Remove excess fat from the cooking liquid, reduce it slightly, and remove the ham
6. Arrange the cabbage on a plate with the grilled sausages on top
