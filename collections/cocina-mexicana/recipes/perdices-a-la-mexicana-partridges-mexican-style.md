## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- thin onion slices
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- pepper
- clove
- cumin
- bread toasts fried in lard
- oregano
- salt
- vinegar
- broth from cooked partridges
- capers
- partridges, cooked and fried
- garlic, ground
- olives
- cured tornachiles
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic and parsley in lard with thin onion slices and roasted ground tomatoes
2. Once done, add pepper, clove, cumin and bread toasts fried in lard, all ground, oregano, salt, vinegar, broth from the cooked partridges and capers
3. Place the partridges that have been cooked and fried in lard with ground garlic in this sauce
4. Let boil until the dish is well seasoned and of good consistency
5. When removing from heat, add olives, cured tornachiles and raw oil
