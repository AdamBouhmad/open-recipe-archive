## Ingredients

- beef loin
- ham strips
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter
- water
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Slice the loin into somewhat thick slices.
2. Lard with thick strips of ham.
3. After sprinkling with ground pepper, salt and flour, fry in butter.
4. Leave to cook for a quarter of an hour, adding water little by little until the sauce becomes thick.
5. Serve with olives and capers.
