## Ingredients

- olives
- capers
- grated cheese
- minced fish or shrimp

## Directions

1. At the lower part where the olives hung from the tree, cut away a slice to expose the pit
2. Using a specialized instrument like a small scoop, remove the pit
3. Fill with capers, or with grated cheese, or with minced fish or shrimp
4. Fit back the removed cap into the hole so it does not fall
5. Arrange on a plate and dress as for a salad
6. Sprinkle with pepper and pour oil over them, or cover with the cold seasoning of pickled squash, always adding some capers
