## Ingredients

- ancho chile
- vinegar
- oregano sprig
- beans
- peas
- small squashes
- carrots

## Directions

1. Devein ancho chile and soak it
2. Grind the well-deveined chile and beat with a little vinegar and an oregano sprig
3. Cook beans, peas, very small squashes and carrots, all chopped
4. Once well cooked, combine with the chile
