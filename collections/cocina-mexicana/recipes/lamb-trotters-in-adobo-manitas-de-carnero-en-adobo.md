## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- ancho chile
- garlic
- cumin
- lard
- broth or water
- cooked peas

## Directions

1. After boiling in water and simmering for half an hour as in the English style preparation
2. Fry in lard a deveined ancho chile that has been soaked and ground with garlic and toasted cumin
3. Add to the fried chile enough broth or water so the adobo has good consistency
4. Add cooked peas
