## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- flour
- parsley
- water
- fried onion
- spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After the salt cod is cooked, cut it into small pieces
2. Coat the pieces in flour and fry them in a casserole
3. Pour parsley and water over them until covered
4. Add a little fried onion, spices, and lemon juice
5. Cook until the sauce is consumed
