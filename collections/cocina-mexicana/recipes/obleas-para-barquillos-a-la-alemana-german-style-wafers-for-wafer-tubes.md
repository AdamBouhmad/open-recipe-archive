## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- a little candied orange blossom
- egg whites
- virgin wax
- oil

## Directions

1. Blanch one pound of sweet almonds and cut them into strips much thinner than for nogado
2. Place the cut almonds in a vessel with twelve ounces of powdered sugar and a little candied orange blossom, kneading everything with egg whites
3. Prepare the metal sheets by rubbing them with virgin wax and a little oil
4. Spread the paste on top as thinly as possible and put the sheets in a moderately hot oven
5. As soon as the wafers are half-cooked, remove them from the oven and cut them into very even squares
6. Return them to the oven for a moment and when removed, shape them into wafer tubes or rolls on a small stick prepared for this purpose
7. Once cold, place them on a sieve and keep them in a drying oven until serving time
