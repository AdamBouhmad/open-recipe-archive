## Ingredients

- venison loin
- ham strips
- pepper
- salt
- garlic
- clove
- cinnamon
- dried ancho chiles
- comino
- cilantro
- wine
- vinegar
- oregano
- lime
- lard
- onion
- green chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Let the venison loin bleed in water
2. Once well cleaned, lard with strips of ham seasoned with pepper and salt, garlic cloves, clove and cinnamon sticks
3. Place in a cazuela with toasted, deveined and soaked dried ancho chiles
4. Grind together the chiles with garlic, pepper, clove, cinnamon, comino and toasted cilantro
5. Dissolve the ground mixture with wine and vinegar and season with salt, oregano and lime slices
6. Coat the venison loins with this adobo and let them marinate until the next day
7. Transfer the cazuela to a pot and cook the loins in their adobo, adding necessary water and lard
8. When cooked, burn more lard in a cazuela, pour the pot with adobo and loins into it
9. Let them dry over two fires
10. Serve adorned with sliced onion, small green chiles and olives
