## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- leche (milk)
- mantequilla (butter)
- sal (salt)
- azúcar (sugar)
- canela (cinnamon)

## Directions

1. Cook the rice well washed in milk over the fire to cook it
2. When the rice is cooked, mash it well with a stick on the bottom and side of the pot so the rice does not break
3. Put to serve immediately and add the necessary butter, stirring well so the dish heats properly and taking care that the rice pot does not break
4. Some put two bay leaves in the milk before boiling it, and at the moment of serving add a dissolved egg yolk in warm water and some drops of orange blossom water
5. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top and make sure it takes color in a pan or with a shovel made hot to caramelize it on top
