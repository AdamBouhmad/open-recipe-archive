## Ingredients

- Square cut of beef
- milk
- fine salt
- nutmeg
- Bechamel sauce
- gelatin
- allspice
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and remove bones, skins and nerves from the sirloin
2. Soak the piece in milk for twenty-four hours
3. When ready to spit-roast, remove from milk and pat dry
4. Sprinkle with fine salt mixed with a little grated nutmeg
5. Spit the meat on a thick fork or spit that rests on the roasting spit
6. Two and a half hours before serving, place over the fire
7. Instead of basting with the fat released by the meat, baste with Bechamel sauce
8. When ready to serve, remove from the spit
9. Add to the remaining Bechamel sauce a piece of gelatin about the size of a walnut, some allspice and nutmeg
10. Place the beef square on the sauce and serve; the sauce need not be very thick
