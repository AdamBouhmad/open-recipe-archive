## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Brandy (cane, mescal, or Catalan)
- Roasted tomatoes
- Cooked lamb liver
- Oil or lard

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb pulp
2. Place in a pot with water, salt, a little vinegar, lard, thyme, marjoram, cloves, cinnamon, sugar, and brandy
3. Cover the pot
4. When the meat is cooked and the broth is reduced, pour into a casserole
5. Make a sauce with roasted tomatoes and cooked lamb liver ground together and fried
6. Let the meat simmer with the sauce until it is consumed and the meat becomes dry
