## Ingredients

- Cooked ham, sliced
- Ham fat and scraps
- Fine herbs
- Mollet bread dough

## Directions

1. Cut slices from cooked ham.
2. Mince the fat and scraps remaining after slicing with fine herbs to make a picadillo or filling.
3. Place mollet bread dough in a baking pan and layer in this order: a layer of dough, a layer of ham slices, a layer of picadillo, repeating this order and finishing with a layer of dough so that the ham is not visible.
4. When the oven is hot, turn the pan over onto a metal sheet and bake the bread for one to two hours depending on its thickness.
5. Serve cold.
