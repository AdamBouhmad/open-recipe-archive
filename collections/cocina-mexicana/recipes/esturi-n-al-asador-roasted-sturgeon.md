## Ingredients

- Sturgeon
- Ham, thin sliced
- Marinade

## Directions

1. Clean the sturgeon as explained in the previous article and lard it with thin ham.
2. Let it marinate for twenty-four hours.
3. After that time, arrange the sturgeon on the spit, baste it frequently with its marinade, which should be somewhat rich in fat.
4. Serve with a piquant sauce.
