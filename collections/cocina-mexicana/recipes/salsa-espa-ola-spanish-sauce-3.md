## Ingredients

- A certain quantity of broth
- Substance of small game and fowl
- Broth
- Equal parts substance and broth
- One glass of white wine
- A bunch of parsley
- One small onion
- One bay leaf
- One head of garlic
- Two cloves
- Two or three tablespoons of oil
- A bunch of cilantro
- One onion sliced into rings
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil and skim the foam from a quantity of broth
2. Add the substance of small game and fowl and broth
3. Remove the grease and pass through a sieve
4. Alternatively, use equal parts substance and broth, one glass of white wine, a bunch of parsley, one small onion, one bay leaf, one head of garlic, two cloves, two or three tablespoons of oil, a bunch of cilantro, and one onion sliced into rings
5. Boil everything together for two hours
6. Remove the grease
7. Add salt and pepper
