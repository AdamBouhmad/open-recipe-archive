## Ingredients

- Two egg yolks
- One tablespoon lemon juice
- Pepper powder
- Mashed garlic
- Oil

## Directions

1. To two egg yolks add one tablespoon of lemon juice, pepper powder and mashed garlic
2. Season and place over very low heat
3. Stir continuously and add a little oil
