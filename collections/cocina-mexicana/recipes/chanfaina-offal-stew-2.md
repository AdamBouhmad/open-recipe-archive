## Ingredients

- Sheep offal
- Half a pound of lard
- One large jitomate
- Six cloves of garlic
- One onion head
- Two red chiles
- A little clove and pepper
- Four eggs
- Pork meat
- Longaniza
- Chorizo
- One chicken
- Chiles in vinegar
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cook the sheep offal and then chop it into small pieces.
2. In a pot, add half a pound of lard, one large jitomate, six cloves of minced garlic, and one onion head and fry in the lard.
3. Once the ingredients are fried, add the offal.
4. Soak two red chiles without toasting, grind them, and add them along with a little clove and pepper.
5. While simmering, add four eggs, and when they are half cooked, scramble them.
6. Separately, fry pork meat, longaniza, sausage, chorizos, and one chicken very well browned and in portions, and add all of this.
7. To serve, add chiles in vinegar and olives.
