## Ingredients

- small squash
- raw oil
- parsley
- pepper
- verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take small squash and cut crosswise
2. Place in a dish with raw oil, parsley, pepper and verjuice or a few drops of vinegar
3. Roast between two fires
