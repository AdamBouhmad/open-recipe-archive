## Ingredients

- shrimp
- wide dried chiles, deveined and soaked
- oil
- garlic, ground
- water
- salt
- cooked lettuce, chopped
- garlic
- bread slices, fried in butter
- saffron
- cumin
- pepper
- clove
- parsley, chopped
- capers

## Directions

1. Grind the shrimp dry and then the wide chiles that have been deveined and soaked
2. Fry ground garlic in oil
3. Add the shrimp and chile and fry them
4. Add water and corresponding salt
5. When it comes to a boil, add cooked lettuce chopped with garlic and seasoned with salt
6. Let everything boil a little
7. In another pot, place bread slices fried in butter
8. Moisten with the shrimp broth
9. Add a layer of lettuce on top, alternating the layers
10. Let the soup cook over two fires, taking care it does not burn and is not too dry
