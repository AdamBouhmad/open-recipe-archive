## Ingredients

- smoked salmon slices
- oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the smoked salmon into slices, fry them by tossing in oil over very high heat.
2. Drain the oil afterwards, arrange on a plate or fish platter, and squeeze lemon juice over it.
