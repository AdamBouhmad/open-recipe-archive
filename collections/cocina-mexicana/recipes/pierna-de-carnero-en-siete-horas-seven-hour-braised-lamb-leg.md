## Ingredients

- lamb leg, boned to mid-shin
- salt
- coarse pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lard slices
- ham slices
- broken bones
- lamb meat slices
- four parsnips
- six onions
- three cloves
- parsley with small onions
- broth
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Bone the leg to the middle of the shin
2. Season the larding strips with salt, coarse pepper, thyme and bay leaf, all pounded
3. Lard the inner part of the leg without letting the strips protrude below
4. Once well larded, reshape the leg
5. Tie it so it does not appear boned
6. Place in a casserole lined with slices of lard and some ham
7. Add the broken bones, some slices of lamb meat, four parsnips, six onions, three cloves, a small bunch of parsley with small onions, and a ladle of broth
8. Place this over the leg, covering everything with lard slices and buttered paper on top
9. Cook seven hours if the leg is large, over low heat, with fire also on top on the casserole lid
10. When ready to serve, drain the leg, untie it, cover with gelatin made from its own reduced broth, and pour the rest on the plate
