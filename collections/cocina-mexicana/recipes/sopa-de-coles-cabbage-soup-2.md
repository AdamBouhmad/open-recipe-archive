## Ingredients

- two or three tight cabbages
- salt
- pepper
- bread toasted on a comal
- ham fat in very thin slices
- thin slices of carrots and onions
- broth from the pot
- all kinds of spices
- toasted cilantro powder
- lean ham shredded
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Boil two or three tight cabbages, and when they are half cooked, remove them from the water and hang them to drain well
2. Slice them across, sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. In a casserole, place a layer of bread toasted on a comal
4. Over it place another layer of very thin slices of fat ham, then another of very thin slices of carrots and onions, and over this another thick layer of the sliced cabbage
5. Repeat the same layers in this order until finishing with the last layer being ham, which is covered with fried toast
6. Moisten with broth from the pot seasoned with all kinds of spices
7. Put the soup over two gentle fires until the cabbage finishes cooking
8. Sprinkle toasted cilantro powder on top
9. Shortly before serving, cover with the shredded lean ham and very finely chopped parsley
