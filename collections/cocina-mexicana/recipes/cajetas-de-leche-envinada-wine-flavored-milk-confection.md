## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- ten cuartillos of milk
- one pound of not very finely ground almonds
- ten ounces of sponge cake or mamon, fried and ground
- one cuartillo of legitimate Málaga wine or any other generous wine that is not red

## Directions

1. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in ten cuartillos of milk, strain it and put it on the fire
2. Add one pound of almonds not very finely ground
3. When it is near reaching the point of cajeta, add, stirring well, ten ounces of sponge cake or mamon fried and ground, and one cuartillo of legitimate Málaga wine or any other generous wine that is not red
4. Once it has acquired the consistency of cajeta, remove from the fire and pour into cajeta molds
