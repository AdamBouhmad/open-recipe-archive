## Ingredients

- garden warblers
- ham slices
- ham, melted
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Wrap the birds with ham slices
2. Thread them on skewers that are fastened to the roasting spit
3. While cooking, baste with a little melted ham
4. Cook for seven or eight minutes until perfectly cooked
5. Serve hot with orange juice
