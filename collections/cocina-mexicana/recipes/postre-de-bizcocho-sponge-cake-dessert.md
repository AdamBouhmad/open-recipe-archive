## Ingredients

- flour
- sponge cake, ground, equal amounts to flour
- sesame seeds, toasted and ground
- raisins
- almonds in small pieces
- butter, fresh and melted
- eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Mix equal amounts of flour and ground sponge cake
2. Stir well and add toasted and ground sesame seeds, raisins and almonds in small pieces
3. Add fresh melted butter and beaten eggs
4. Butter a pan or cazuela
5. Pour the mixture in and leave to cook
