## Ingredients

- fattened hen, roasted
- béchamel sauce
- cream
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- fried eggs
- toasted bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. After the fattened hen is roasted and fried, remove the meat and mince it very finely
2. In a saucepan, add several spoonfuls of béchamel sauce and a little cream and butter with salt, pepper and grated nutmeg
3. Let this sauce boil and then mix it with the minced meat
4. Heat everything together without boiling
5. Arrange on a plate and top with fried eggs interspersed with toasted bread fried in butter and frosted
