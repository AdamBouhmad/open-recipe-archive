## Ingredients

- sugar
- rose petals

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup to reach the proper stage, and when very thick, dip a spoon and take a little, and as it boils more in the spoon itself, so much that it overflows, add rose petals stripped of their yellow.
2. Beat very briefly and let it boil again, making it recover the point it had.
3. Pour onto a damp board, placing paper underneath so it does not stick, dividing it into pounds.
