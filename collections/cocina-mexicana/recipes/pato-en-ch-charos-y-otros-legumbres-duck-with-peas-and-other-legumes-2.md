## Ingredients

- duck prepared and cooked as indicated for duck in sauce
- peas or other legumes for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the duck as indicated for duck in sauce
2. Serve the duck in the same way and cover with a dish of peas
3. Use the same method for duck with onions
