## Ingredients

- twenty-four cuartillos of refined brandy
- four cuartillos of water
- peels (stripped of flesh) of four grapefruits
- twelve cuartillos of water
- three pounds of clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Dilute twenty-four cuartillos of refined brandy with four cuartillos of water
2. Pour into a demijohn with the stripped peels of four grapefruits
3. Leave in infusion for several days, well sealed
4. Distill in a water bath
5. Mix the obtained spirit with twelve cuartillos of water and three pounds of clarified syrup
6. Filter the cream and bottle
