## Ingredients

- 2 ounces almonds
- 2 ounces flour
- 3 ounces sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- zest of green lemon
- 1 ounce butter
- salt
- 1 quart milk
- puff pastry for tartlet molds
- meringue batter
- coarse sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 2 ounces almonds and place in a pan with equal quantity of flour, 3 ounces sugar, 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg, zest of green lemon, 1 ounce butter and a little salt
2. Dissolve all this in 1 quart of milk
3. Place over heat to reach cream consistency
4. Pour into tartlet molds that have been lined with puff pastry
5. Place in oven on a baking sheet
6. When the fanchonetas are almost cooked, remove them and brush with meringue batter
7. Sprinkle with coarse sugar and return to oven to achieve a beautiful golden color
