## Ingredients

- oil
- butter
- garlic cloves
- cooked cauliflower
- salt
- ground garlic
- small hot peppers
- olives
- small whole cooked onions
- oregano powder
- avocado quarters

## Directions

1. In equal parts oil and butter, fry garlic cloves halved until they brown
2. Remove the garlic and add the already-cooked cauliflower divided into pieces to the same fat
3. Add salt and the garlic that was fried before, now ground
4. Fry the cauliflower, taking care to turn it so it browns on all sides
5. Remove and arrange on the plate
6. Add oil abundantly and garnish on top with small hot peppers, olives, whole small cooked onions, powdered oregano, and quarters of peeled avocado
