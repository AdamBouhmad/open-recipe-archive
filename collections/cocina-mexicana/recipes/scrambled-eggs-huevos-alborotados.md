## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- parsley
- fine salt
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumb
- lard
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Hard boil and halve the eggs as in the previous article
2. Knead the hard yolks with as many raw ones
3. Add very finely chopped parsley, seasoned with fine salt, and with this fill the hollows of the cooked yolks
4. Put the two layers of cooked whites, roll them in flour and bathe them in beaten egg with breadcrumb
5. Fry them in lard until they take good color
6. Can be put in tomato broth or another of the sauces that most please
