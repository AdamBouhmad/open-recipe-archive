## Ingredients

- venison ribs
- breadcrumbs
- good oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf
- game jelly
- pepper sauce

## Directions

1. After cutting and coating with breadcrumbs as done for lamb ribs, place in a pan with a little good oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and bay leaf
2. Just before serving, fry until cooked
3. When cooked, drain the oil and remove the bay leaf and garlic
4. Add a piece of game jelly and toss the ribs inside the pan and arrange on a plate
5. Boil a good pepper sauce in the pan so it becomes somewhat thick, finishing the operation with a little oil
6. Serve immediately
