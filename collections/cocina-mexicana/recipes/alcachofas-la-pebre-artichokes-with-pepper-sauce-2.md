## Ingredients

- small artichokes
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest artichokes and cut them into four pieces
2. Remove the outer leaves
3. Cut the upper part of the remaining leaves and the stem
4. Cook in water
5. Serve in cold water with vinegar, salt, and pepper
