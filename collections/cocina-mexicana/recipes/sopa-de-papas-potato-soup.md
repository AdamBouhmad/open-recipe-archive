## Ingredients

- Potatoes, peeled and cooked until soft
- Lard
- Onion, minced
- Salt
- Water in which potatoes were cooked
- Bread or rice for the soup

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the potatoes until they fall apart. Pass them through a sieve.
2. Reheat them with lard, minced onion, and salt, moistening them with the same water in which they cooked to make savory broth.
3. Use this broth to soak the soup, which can be made from bread or rice.
