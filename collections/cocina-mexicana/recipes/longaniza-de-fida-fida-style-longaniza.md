## Ingredients

- lean meat from stringy cuts and sirloin
- salt
- ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Choose lean meat from stringy cuts and sirloin
2. Cut very fine without chopping it
3. Season with salt and ground black pepper
4. Fill the longanizas as tightly as possible in thick pork intestines
5. Hang in a fresh and ventilated place until dry
6. Then store
