## Ingredients

- Broccoli rabe
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut off the thick leaves from the broccoli rabe and remove the skin with all the fibers, keeping the stems clean
2. Cook them together with the small florets at the tip with salt
3. When cooked, remove and wash in cold water and drain well until no water remains
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. Toss with finely chopped onion
6. Season with oil and vinegar, heavily salted
