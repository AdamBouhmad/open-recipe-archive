## Ingredients

- Twelve lamb tongues
- Grated cheese
- Thick Spanish sauce
- Egg white, lightly beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter, melted

## Directions

1. Cook twelve lamb tongues as described in the previous recipe.
2. When cool, divide into two halves.
3. Arrange the pieces on a plate on which grated cheese and thick Spanish sauce have been placed.
4. Sprinkle with the same sauce and powder with more grated cheese.
5. Coat with lightly beaten egg white.
6. Powder a second time with equal parts grated cheese and breadcrumbs.
7. Sprinkle with a little melted butter.
8. Place in the oven or under a domed cover to brown.
