## Ingredients

- Citron
- Salt water
- Natural water
- Clarified syrup
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the citron and clean of its bitter substances
2. Soak in salt water
3. Then place in natural water, rinsing thoroughly in many waters to remove the salt, repeating this operation for two consecutive days
4. Make into a preserve with clarified syrup, pouring into bowls or jars
5. Daily extract the syrup from there to cook it and return it cold over the citron
6. Add sugar as needed as the citron absorbs it
7. When the syrup has penetrated it perfectly and is at the proper high point, remove the citron and place to drain in the oven
8. Wash afterwards with warm water and return to syrup of a higher point than for egg preserves
9. Allow to boil until reaching the stage where, when a skimming spoon dipped in it is blown through, the syrup forms blisters or bubbles on the other side without breaking
10. Dry the candied citron and place to drain on esparto, wicker baskets placed upside down, or on the drying rack
11. The syrup being added to the citron when in the bowl or jars should always be cold
