## Ingredients

- Pork loin meat
- Salt
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Sesame seeds
- Anise
- Ginger
- Generous wine
- Intestines

## Directions

1. Mince the pork loin meat very well, removing all tendons and sinews
2. Add a little salt and let drain overnight in a place where blood can run off
3. Mix in abundant cinnamon, pepper, cloves, ground nutmeg, untoasted sesame, toasted anise, and a little ginger
4. Moisten with generous wine and mix very well
5. Fill intestines and tie them at short intervals
6. Hang at the chimney to cure
