## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- White salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Cooking oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the tips of the artichoke leaves with scissors, put them to boil in water, and after a couple of boils remove from the fire.
2. Bring water with white salt to a boil, and when it reaches a rolling boil, add the artichokes after cooling them in cold water.
3. When cooked, remove them and separate the center leaves until the fuzzy choke is exposed, remove it entirely.
4. Dust with breadcrumbs mixed with salt and pepper, bathe with cooking oil with salt, and with the leaves gathered together, place on a grill until golden.
5. When ready to serve, bathe with oil and vinegar seasoned with salt.
