## Ingredients

- three large cups water
- one large stick cinnamon
- one whole sweet orange peel
- six ounces sugar
- four ounces lard
- one pound well-washed butter
- one pound flour
- thirty egg yolks
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Cook in three large cups of water with one large stick of cinnamon and one whole sweet orange peel
2. Once cooked add six ounces of sugar, and dissolving it, strain everything and add four ounces of lard and one pound of well-washed butter
3. Put the pan on the fire and gradually add one pound of flour to the said broth, letting it take the necessary point so it comes away from the pan
4. Empty it into a napkin so the paste cools, and being cooled, spread it on a table where it will be kneaded with thirty egg yolks, incorporating them little by little until it has the consistency of spoon point
5. Make the puffs, fry them, and as they come out, sprinkle them with sugar
