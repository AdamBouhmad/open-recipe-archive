## Ingredients

- Cod
- Tomato juice
- Onions, finely chopped
- Garlic, finely chopped
- Chile, finely chopped and deveined
- Oil

## Directions

1. Soak the cod from the previous day, changing the water
2. The next day, place it in warm water until cooking time
3. Shred the cod and remove the bones and spine
4. Cook in oil with tomato juice, finely chopped onions and garlic, and finely chopped and deveined chile
