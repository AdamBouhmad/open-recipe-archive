## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Bacon strips
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Parsley
- Onions
- Veal slices
- Beef slices
- Organ meats
- Broth from the pot
- Pickles
- Red sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and place in boiling water for about half an hour
2. Transfer to cold water and let cool
3. Prepare strips of bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, spices, parsley, and finely chopped onions
4. Lard the tongue with this mixture
5. Place in a casserole with good seasoning and fine herbs, along with some bacon slices, veal slices, beef slices, organ meats, onions, and spices
6. Moisten everything with broth from the pot
7. Let the tongue cook over very low heat for four hours
8. When ready to serve, remove the skin from the top
9. Have strained red broth ready and add small pieces of pickles to it
10. Place the tongue on a platter and pour this last sauce over it
