## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Onion
- Garlic
- Lard
- Flour
- Water
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut raw chicken into pieces and place in a casserole with pieces of ham and chorizo, chopped onion, garlic cloves and a spoonful of lard
2. Brown well
3. Add flour and fry a bit more
4. Add water with chopped parsley, oregano and thyme
5. In a separate pan, fry garlic cloves in lard and grind with clove, cinnamon and pepper
6. Add this ground mixture to the casserole
7. When ready to serve, beat six to eight egg yolks with vinegar
8. Add lemon juice to the beaten eggs
9. Slowly add the egg mixture to the casserole while stirring constantly to prevent the eggs from curdling
10. The broth should remain thick
