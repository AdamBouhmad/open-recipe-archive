## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- milk
- a little lemon zest
- a little vanilla
- sugar to taste
- a little salt
- eight or ten egg yolks
- egg whites
- orange flower water
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Blanch and cook the rice in milk, adding more milk as it thickens
2. Add lemon zest, vanilla, sugar to taste, and salt
3. When the rice has fully burst, remove from heat and remove the lemon zest and vanilla
4. Add eight or ten egg yolks and beat the egg whites separately
5. Mix the beaten whites with orange flower water and mix everything well
6. Form batters that are dipped in beaten egg and sugar
7. Dust with grated bread
8. Repeat this last operation twice
9. Fry
