## Ingredients

- any of the explained creams
- 1 ounce fish glue (dissolved and clarified)

## Directions

1. Make any of the explained creams reach the proper point in a bain-marie, as all are good for the purpose
2. When cool, pass through a fine sieve or strainer with a wooden spoon
3. Incorporate 1 ounce of dissolved and clarified fish glue
4. Pour this composition into a molded form and when cold, bury it in ice for one hour
5. Turn it out onto the plate on which it should be served
