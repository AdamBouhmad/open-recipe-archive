## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- white wine
- garlic
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. After cleaning and removing the rancid part cut and soak for two days in cold water
2. Dry and rinse and then cook wrapped in white cloth with half wine and water, garlic, onions, thyme, bay leaf, clove and fine herbs
3. It is done when a skewer pierces it easily
