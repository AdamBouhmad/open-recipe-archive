## Ingredients

- One pound of beef
- Two partridges
- Two rabbits
- A piece of mutton or beef leg
- Two cuartillos of white wine
- Good broth
- Onions
- Carrots
- Thyme
- Basil
- Serpol
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Place one pound of beef, two partridges, two rabbits, and a piece of mutton or beef leg in a pot or large pan
2. Add two cuartillos of white wine and boil until the wine has evaporated
3. Fill the pot with good broth and add onions, carrots, thyme, basil, serpol, and cloves
4. Skim carefully and boil until the meats are cooked
5. Pass through a sieve
