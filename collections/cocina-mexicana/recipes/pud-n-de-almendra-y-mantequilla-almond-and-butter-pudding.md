## Ingredients

- three cuartillos of milk
- one pound of sugar
- six ounces of butter
- half a pound of peeled and ground almonds
- half an ounce of ground cinnamon
- eighteen beaten eggs
- raisins
- pine nuts
- pieces of walnuts
- ground biscuit as necessary

## Directions

1. Sweeten three cuartillos of milk with one pound of sugar and after straining, mix in six ounces of butter, half a pound of peeled and ground almonds, half an ounce of ground cinnamon, eighteen beaten eggs, raisins, pine nuts, pieces of walnuts and enough ground biscuit to form the paste
2. Once everything is well mixed, pour into a mold greased with butter, not filling it to the top
3. Place in the oven and cook at two fires, taking care that air does not reach it
4. When it is set, which is known by testing with a straw that comes out clean, remove and let it cool to turn out of the mold and serve
