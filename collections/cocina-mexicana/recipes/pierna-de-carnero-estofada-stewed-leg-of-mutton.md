## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- bacon
- spices
- salt
- onions
- carrots
- parsley
- aromatic herbs
- wine or water

## Directions

1. Bone the leg and lard it with bacon seasoned with spices and salt, so the strips do not protrude from the meat
2. Tie it with twine, striking the joint and cutting the bone so it takes up little space
3. Place in a casserole with six onions, three carrots, a bunch of parsley, aromatic herbs, salt, spices, a little bacon, and two glasses of wine or water
4. When it begins to boil slowly, place fire on top of the lid
5. When it has cooked sufficiently, serve with its defatted broth
