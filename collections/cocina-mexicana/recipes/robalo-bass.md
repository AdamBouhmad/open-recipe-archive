## Ingredients

- mulato chile
- pepper grains
- small onion
- robalo, desalted
- flour
- lard
- oil
- thyme
- bay leaves
- olives, chopped
- capers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Devein a little mulato chile and toast it over low heat, being careful not to burn it
2. Grind five grains of pepper
3. Slice a small amount of onion very finely
4. Remove salt from the robalo and cut into slices, dry with a cloth
5. Fry in oil and lard, adding a pinch of flour
6. Butter a baking dish and sprinkle with chile
7. Place a layer of fish and cover with the sliced onion
8. Add a little thyme, some bay leaves and more chile powder on top
9. Place another layer of fish and repeat the operation until finished, remembering to also add chopped olives and capers
10. Pour oil and vinegar on top, place over two low fires and do not stir with a spoon but by shaking the dish
