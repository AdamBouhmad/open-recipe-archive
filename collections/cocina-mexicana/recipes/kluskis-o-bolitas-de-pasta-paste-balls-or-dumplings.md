## Ingredients

- flour
- warm water
- eggs, slightly poached
- beer foam
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a somewhat thick dough with flour, warm water and eggs slightly poached, beer foam, salt and sugar
2. Let it rest in a warm place
