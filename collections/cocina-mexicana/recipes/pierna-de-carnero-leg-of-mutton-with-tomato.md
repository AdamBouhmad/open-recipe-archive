## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- onion, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- ham pieces
- oil
- juice of two lemons
- spices, ground in powder
- salt
- lard
- tomato, ground in quantity
- water
- salt, pepper, and cinnamon in powder
- chiles in vinegar
- olives
- capers and large capers

## Directions

1. In a small casserole, combine chopped onion and chopped parsley, ham pieces, oil, juice of two lemons, ground spices in powder, and a little salt
2. Mix everything well
3. Make some holes in the leg with a knife and fill them with this mixture
4. Put lard in another casserole or large pan and fry the leg there
5. Grind a good amount of tomato and add it to the meat
6. When well fried, add water, salt, pepper, and cinnamon in powder
7. Serve on a platter decorated with chiles in vinegar, olives, capers, and large capers
