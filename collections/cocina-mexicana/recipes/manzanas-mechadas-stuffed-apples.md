## Ingredients

- Apples with some tartness or pears
- Fine well-clarified syrup
- Quince hearts in boiling water

## Directions

1. Peel and core the apples and keep placing them in cold water
2. Make fine well-clarified syrup and in violent heat add the apples
3. As they soften remove them and place them on the platter in order
4. Keep the syrup cooking at violent heat until it reaches jelly point, aided with a little tejocotes jelly if available, or if not add quince hearts in boiling water and their mucilage is stirred into the syrup
5. When at the proper point place the apples on top and garnish
