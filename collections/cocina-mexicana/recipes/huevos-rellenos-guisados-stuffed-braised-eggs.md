## Ingredients

- eggs
- bread crumbs
- cream
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- fish

## Directions

1. Harden the eggs and cut in two halves
2. Remove yolks and grind with bread crumbs soaked in cream
3. Add an equal part of butter, one or two yolks, salt and pepper
4. Fill the eggs with this mixture and cover with bread crumbs moistened with butter
5. Pour the rest of the filling diluted with a little broth and let it simmer
6. May also be filled with chopped fish using oil
