## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Lard
- Water
- Onion
- Garlic
- Hazelnuts
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and place in a casserole with ham and chorizo slices and a spoonful of lard
2. Fry and add water
3. Chop onion and garlic and fry in a separate pan
4. Fry hazelnuts in lard, then grind them very well
5. Fry the ground hazelnuts in lard and add to the meat casserole
6. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and fried bread slices
7. Add oregano, thyme, raisins and almonds
8. When serving, add a little sherry wine
9. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers
