## Ingredients

- five pounds of clarified cold sugar
- one hundred egg yolks
- three pounds of ground almonds
- orange blossom water
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add the egg yolks to the cold sugar
2. Strain again and add the ground almonds
3. Place over gentle heat and stir constantly in all directions
4. Before it sets, add a little orange blossom water
5. Pour onto a platter and when cold sprinkle cinnamon on top
