## Ingredients

- Fifteen egg yolks
- Four quarts of pot broth

## Directions

1. Dissolve fifteen egg yolks in four quarts of pot broth, straining through a cloth
2. Pour everything into a vessel and bring to cook by placing the pot in hot water
3. When it has set well, take with a small spoon of that substance and arrange the mouthfuls in the soup tureen
4. Pour good broth afterward and sprinkle on top
