## Ingredients

- nine cuartillos of milk
- half a cup of peeled and ground chickpeas
- half a cup of pine nuts
- half a cup of small walnuts
- one cup of raw rice
- four ounces of almonds
- cinnamon sticks
- whole cloves
- one lemon peel
- orange leaves
- nine egg yolks
- a few grains of salt
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup and put it on the fire, stirring constantly
2. When it reaches medium point, add the almonds, walnuts and pine nuts
3. As soon as it pulls away from the pan, empty it onto a platter
4. Leave it from one day to the next
5. Sprinkle powdered sugar and cinnamon in abundance on top
6. Cover with a comal on the fire so that it forms a crust
