## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Two ounces of butter
- Proper salt
- Sifted powdered sugar
- Sufficient sour orange juice to soften the dough

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of flour with two ounces of butter, proper salt, sifted powdered sugar, and sufficient sour orange juice to soften the dough
2. Knead by fists, extend with a rolling pin, and form the pastry
