## Ingredients

- manteca
- aceitunas deshuesadas
- alcaparrones
- xitomate asado
- ajos
- peregil picado
- clavo
- pimienta en polvo
- aceite
- vinagre
- pollos
- caldo
- jamón cocido

## Directions

1. Fry olives pitted and ground with capers and a little roasted tomato in butter, after first frying garlic cloves in the same butter until they are golden, then remove them
2. Add to the frying chopped parsley, clove and ground pepper, oil and a little vinegar
3. Place the chickens with a little of the broth in which they were cooked and some strips of cooked ham
4. Let simmer until the sauce becomes somewhat thick
