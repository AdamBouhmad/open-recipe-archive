## Ingredients

- xitomate
- manteca
- ajos picados
- cebollas rebanadas
- rajas de chile verde
- sal
- clavo
- pimienta
- azafrán
- azúcar
- vinagre
- pollos cocidos
- caldo
- yemas de huevo cocidas y molidas

## Directions

1. Grind raw tomatoes and fry in butter with chopped garlic, sliced onions and strips of green chile
2. Season with salt, clove, pepper and saffron, all ground, sugar and vinegar
3. Place the cooked chickens in this sauce
4. Thicken the broth with cooked and ground egg yolks
5. Let simmer until well seasoned
