## Ingredients

- carp
- water and red wine in equal parts
- salt
- onion
- carrot
- garlic
- parsley
- thyme
- basil

## Directions

1. After cleaning the carp, boil it in equal parts of water and red wine with the necessary salt
2. Add sliced onion and carrot, garlic, parsley, thyme and basil, all chopped
3. When cooked, serve with chopped parsley on a napkin or plate
