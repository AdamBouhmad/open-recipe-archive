## Ingredients

- Lamb tongues
- White wine
- Marinesca sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb tongues over coals, moistening with white wine.
2. When cooked, drain them and remove the skins.
3. Arrange on a plate and cover with marinesca sauce.
