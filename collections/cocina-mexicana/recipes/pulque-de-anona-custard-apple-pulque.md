## Ingredients

- Twenty-five quartillos of fresh pulque
- Two or three custard apples
- Sugar (one pound)

## Directions

1. Peel the custard apples and remove the seeds
2. Mix the remaining pulp well with one pound of sugar and the pulque
3. Strain
