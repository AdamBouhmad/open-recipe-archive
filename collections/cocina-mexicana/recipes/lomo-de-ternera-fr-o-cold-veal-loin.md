## Ingredients

- veal loin
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. In the morning, prepare the loin after cleaning it, adding salt, pepper and vinegar, and keep it thus for the rest of the day
2. In the night, remove it and place it in a press, leaving it there until the following day
3. On that day, place it in a pan with moderate fire to parboil it there
4. When the meat is tender, remove it from the fire
5. Empty the pot into a dish, ensuring the loin finishes cooking over two fires
6. As the broth is consumed, add a little tomato and chopped parsley on top without stirring
7. When serving at the table, garnish with lettuce and fried toasts
