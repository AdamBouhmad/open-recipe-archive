## Ingredients

- calf rennet
- butter
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Parboil in butter heated over the fire, two pounds of rennet prepared as in the previous article, and remove it
2. Mince together a piece of the same rennet without parboiling, as thick as the fist of the hand, two heads of garlic, parsley, butter, pepper and coarse salt, and mix
