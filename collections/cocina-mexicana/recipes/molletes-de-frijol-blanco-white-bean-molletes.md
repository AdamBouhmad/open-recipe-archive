## Ingredients

- cooked and washed white beans
- cinnamon
- pepper
- milk
- two egg yolks
- sugar
- butter
- syrup
- pine nuts and almonds
- wine

## Directions

1. After cooking and washing the beans well, grind them with cinnamon and pepper
2. Beat in a pot with a little milk and two egg yolks, an appropriate amount of sugar
3. After beating, place in a pot with plenty of butter and drop with a spoon until golden
4. Fry until they burst, remove and place on a platter with its syrup
5. Decorate with pine nuts and almonds and sprinkle with wine on top
