## Ingredients

- Beef kidney
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onions
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cut a kidney into thin strips
2. Place on the fire with butter, salt, pepper, parsley, onions, and chopped garlic
3. After cooking, add a little vinegar and a little strained broth or broth from the pot
4. Do not cook further so they do not become tough
