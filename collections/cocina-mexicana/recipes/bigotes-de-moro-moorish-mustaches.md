## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef bone marrow
- half pound of almond
- eight or ten citrons
- a little cinnamon
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of beef bone marrow, let cool afterward and grind with half pound of almond, eight or ten citrons and a little cinnamon
2. Cut small pieces of this paste the size of a large walnut
3. Cover with beaten egg, roll twice in breadcrumbs
4. Fry in hot lard
