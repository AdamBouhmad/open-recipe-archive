## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- water
- skimmer

## Directions

1. Form small round, square, or other shaped figurines
2. Drop them into boiling salted water
3. Use a skimmer to stir the bottom so they rise and do not stick, keeping them separated so they do not destroy one another
4. When they rise on their own, remove them with the skimmer and place in very cold water
5. Once cooled, place them on a sieve or strainer to drain
6. Arrange on a tin sheet or paper greased with lard, placed on it and separated from one another
7. Place in an oven that is not very hot, taking care to close its mouth, and in twenty minutes they will be cooked
