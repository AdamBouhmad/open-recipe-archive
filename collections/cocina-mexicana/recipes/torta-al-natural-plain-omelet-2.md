## Ingredients

- eggs
- fresh water
- fine salt
- pepper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Beat some eggs according to the size of the omelet, and add half or one spoonful of fresh water, which greatly facilitates the intimate mixing of the whites and yolks
2. Season with fine salt mixed with a little pepper
3. Melt fresh butter in a pan, pour the beaten eggs into it and stir until the omelet begins to set
4. When it is set, lift it with the point of a knife and put another amount of fresh butter underneath
5. As soon as it has melted, turn the omelet by folding it over itself, which is done easily, leave it one or two minutes in the pan and place it on a platter to serve immediately
6. The flavor of the plain omelet can be varied infinitely by adding to the eggs, at the moment of beating them, grated cheese, minced fine herbs, cooked sorrel, sauce or meat juice; the procedure does not change in any of these cases
