## Ingredients

- three pounds sugar
- thirty egg yolks
- sponge cake (mamón)
- custard (natillas)
- syrup
- red pastille candy (gragea)

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of medium point with three pounds of sugar
2. When cool, add thirty beaten egg yolks
3. Return to low heat to cook well, stirring constantly until as thick as atole
4. Place a layer of sponge cake on a platter, then a layer of custard, alternating and marbling with the paste and sponge cake
5. On the final layer of custard, pour syrup and red pastille candy
6. Also called Ante de Huevo with custard
