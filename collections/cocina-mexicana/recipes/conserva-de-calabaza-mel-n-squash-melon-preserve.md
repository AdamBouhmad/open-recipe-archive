## Ingredients

- Squash melon
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Remove the first excessively thin skin, cut into wheels one finger width thick
2. Remove the seeds with the point of a knife
3. Place not in lime water but in clean water, stir by hand and move about like making soap lather so they release their foam and froth, upon which the sugar syrup is founded
