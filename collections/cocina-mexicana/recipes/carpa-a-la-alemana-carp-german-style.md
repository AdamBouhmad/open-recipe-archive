## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Fine pepper
- Ginger
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Divide the carp into pieces after washing it without emptying or removing the gills, removing only the blind intestine or cagalar
2. Place the pieces in a pan or large skillet with salt, coarse pepper, clove, cinnamon, fine pepper, ginger, and bread slices
