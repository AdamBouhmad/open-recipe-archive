## Ingredients

- turkey, cleaned
- ham slices
- fatty pork pieces
- pieces of beef knee and feet
- carrot
- onion
- fine herbs
- water or broth
- salt
- white wine

## Directions

1. After cleaning the turkey well and passing it through a flame to burn off the down, place it on a pan lined with ham slices
2. Surround it with pieces of fatty pork, pieces of beef knee and feet, carrot, onion and fine herbs
3. Cover with water or broth and add appropriate salt
4. Put to cook over gentle heat
5. When cooked, add a bottle of white wine and when it boils a little, remove it
6. Strain the broth and let it cool to remove the fat
7. Cover all the meat with this jelly and serve
8. If the turkey were deboned, it will turn out better
