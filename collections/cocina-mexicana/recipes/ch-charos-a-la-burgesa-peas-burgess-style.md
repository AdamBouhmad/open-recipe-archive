## Ingredients

- tender peas
- water
- butter (two ounces per cuartillo of peas)
- salt
- bunch of fine herbs
- piece of butter the size of a walnut
- flour
- egg yolks (optional)
- custard (optional)
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Put tender peas in a little water and add two ounces of butter for each cuartillo of peas, stirring well.
2. Drain the water, add salt and a bunch of fine herbs.
3. Fry and add boiling water; cook in it.
4. Add a piece of butter the size of a walnut mixed with flour, stir well, and serve.
5. Optionally, egg yolks can be added with a little custard, using less salt and adding sugar in that case.
