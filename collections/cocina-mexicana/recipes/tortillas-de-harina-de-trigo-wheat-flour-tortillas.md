## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- four ounces of butter
- three egg yolks
- two egg whites
- three ounces of sugar
- warm water

## Directions

1. Make the dough with the flour, butter, egg yolks, egg whites, sugar, dissolved with warm water
2. Form the tortillas
3. Cook in a comal or in the oven
