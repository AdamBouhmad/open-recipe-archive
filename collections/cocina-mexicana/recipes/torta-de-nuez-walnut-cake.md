## Ingredients

- Walnuts
- Lard
- Syrup
- Mamón
- Egg whites
- Ground sugar
- Lemon juice
- Dragée
- Sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Clean the walnuts and let them dry, then grind them
2. Take a layer of lard and another of walnut and continue this way until filling the pot
3. Pour syrup gently over this until the mamón is well moistened
4. Unite with egg whites, ground sugar, and a few drops of lemon
5. Spread on top, add dragée and sesame seeds and place in the oven or put a griddle with heat on top
