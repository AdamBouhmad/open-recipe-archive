## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- parsley
- onion
- lard
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- cream or milk foam
- eggs
- cheese, grated
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a hash with bread crumbs, parsley, onion, lard and egg yolks
2. Cover the bottom of a plate with this mixture
3. Place the eggs one by one on top
4. Place over a slow fire
5. Brown with a hot iron plate
6. Sprinkle with a little ground salt and a pinch of pepper and serve
7. Alternatively, place four spoonfuls of cream or milk foam in a plate and mix with ten eggs, taking care that the yolks do not burst
8. Add a little grated loose cheese and a pinch of pepper
9. Cook in a portable oven and serve soft
