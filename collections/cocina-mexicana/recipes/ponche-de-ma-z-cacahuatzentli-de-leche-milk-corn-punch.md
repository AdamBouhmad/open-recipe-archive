## Ingredients

- Black or dark blue corn (cacahuatzentli)
- Milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make it like the previous one with the difference that the mass after being ground is not dried with water but with milk
2. Do not mix bread or cochineal with it and use only black or dark blue corn
3. Let it reach a higher point so that it does not stick and can be removed from the dishes in which it is molded
