## Ingredients

- Small black beans with soft hulls
- Salt of the earth or fine salt
- Lard or oil
- Onion, sliced
- Garlic cloves, sliced
- Pork broth
- Pork meat, in medium pieces
- Onion, sliced
- Fine salt
- Wide chile seeds, cleaned (optional)
- Onion, raw (for serving)
- Aged cheese, crumbled (for serving)
- Eating oil (for serving)

## Directions

1. Select small black beans with soft hulls and cook them with salt of the earth or fine salt, the former being preferable.
2. Once cooked, wash them.
3. Heat a pot with lard or oil over the fire.
4. Fry sliced onion and garlic cloves in it.
5. When these have fried a little, add the beans to fry.
6. Once well seared in the lard, add pork broth and even pork meat in medium pieces.
7. Slice an onion over them, season to taste with the fine salt needed, and if desired, add some cleaned wide chile seeds.
8. Let them boil until they have thickened well.
9. Then serve them adorned, if desired, with raw onion and crumbled aged cheese, sprinkling a little eating oil over the top.
