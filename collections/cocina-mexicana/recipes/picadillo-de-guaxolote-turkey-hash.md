## Ingredients

- Carnes blancas de un guaxolote cocido al asador (white meat from a roasted turkey)
- Salsa bechamel (béchamel sauce)
- Caldo concentrado (concentrated broth)
- Caparazón del guaxolote (turkey carcass)
- Yemas de huevo (egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Take the white meat from a roasted turkey that has cooled
2. Carefully remove the skin and sinews and chop the meat
3. Mix the hash with béchamel sauce and heat
4. If no béchamel is available, make a red sauce moistened with concentrated broth in which the turkey carcass has been boiled
5. Bind this sauce with egg yolks and pour over the hash
