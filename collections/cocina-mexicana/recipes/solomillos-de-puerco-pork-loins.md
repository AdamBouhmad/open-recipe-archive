## Ingredients

- pork loins
- bacon strips
- bacon sheets
- veal slices
- carrots
- onions
- clove
- parsley sprig
- thyme
- bay leaf
- broth
- lettuce or spicy sauce or purée

## Directions

1. Wash the loins lengthwise and lard them with thin bacon strips, giving them a round shape
2. Place bacon sheets in a pan with some veal slices, carrots, onions, clove, a sprig of parsley, thyme and bay leaf
3. Place the loins on this seasoning and cover with two greased papers
4. Add a spoonful of broth and place on the fire
5. After one hour, place fire on top of the lid to give them color
6. When serving, remove them from the stew and place on the platter over lettuce or a spicy sauce or purée
