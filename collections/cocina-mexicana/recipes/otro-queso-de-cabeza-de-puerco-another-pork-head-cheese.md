## Ingredients

- pork head
- water
- salt
- whole peppercorns
- thyme
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- parsley
- onions
- garlic
- ground pepper
- ham

## Directions

1. Cook the head in a large pot with water and salt, whole peppercorns and thin ones, thyme, marjoram, bay leaf, parsley, onions, garlic and a little ground pepper
2. Before cooking clean it very well in hot water, scrape with a knife, cut off the snout and remove the teeth
3. When almost cooked, add one pound of ham to the pot so it also cooks
4. Cut both meats into small pieces, place in mold and press well
5. Should be left in the mold for twelve hours
