## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound and four ounces of butter
- twelve egg yolks
- twelve ounces of sugar
- one small cup of Jerez wine
- a little ground clove and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut and put in the oven
2. When removing them, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
