## Ingredients

- ducks
- straw
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- aromatic herbs in powder
- fried garlic
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the ducks with straw and cut off the tails
2. Clean and dry them with a cloth
3. Bone them and roll in flour
4. Fry in butter
5. Remove and place in layers in a pot
6. Between two layers of duck, add a layer of salt and pepper, aromatic herbs in powder, fried garlic, oil, and vinegar
7. Cover the pot and leave to infuse for three or four hours
8. Seal very tightly with a wooden lid and let sit for five or six days
