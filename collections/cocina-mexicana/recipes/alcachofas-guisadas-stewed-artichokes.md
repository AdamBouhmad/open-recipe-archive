## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Pepper
- Salt
- Garlic
- Onions
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Water
- Vinegar
- Capers
- Olives
- Pickled peppers
- Hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the artichokes as described initially
2. Divide them in half and fry in oil with breadcrumbs, pepper and salt
3. Remove them when fried
4. In the same pot in which they were fried, add chopped garlic and onions, roasted and ground tomatoes, to fry with the oil
5. Add water, vinegar and capers
6. When it boils, add the artichokes to boil a little as well
7. To serve, garnish with olives, pickled peppers and slices of hard boiled egg
