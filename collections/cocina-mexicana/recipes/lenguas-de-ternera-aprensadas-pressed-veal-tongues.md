## Ingredients

- veal tongues
- salt
- saltpeter

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape six tongues very well and place in the sun for two hours
2. Mix six pounds of salt with half a pound of saltpeter
3. Rub the tongues thoroughly with this mixture and place them in an appropriate vessel
4. Add the remaining salt and cover with cloth
5. Place a considerable weight on top to press the tongues and keep them in this state for eight days
6. On the ninth day, repeat the operation from the first day, omitting only the washing and sun-drying of the tongues
7. Add all remaining salt and press again for another eight days
8. After this time, remove from the press, dry, and place in a cool location where they will keep for a long time without spoiling
