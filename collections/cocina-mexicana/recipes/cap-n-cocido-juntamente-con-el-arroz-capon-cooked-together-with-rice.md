## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- good broth
- 1/2 pound well-washed rice
- parsnips
- onions
- clove spice

## Directions

1. After gathering the capon's legs and tying it, place it to cook in good broth with 1/2 pound of well-washed rice, some parsnips, onions and clove spice, letting them boil for 2 hours or less.
2. Untie the capon, place it in the serving dish with some whole pepper, pour the rice on top and serve without the vegetables.
