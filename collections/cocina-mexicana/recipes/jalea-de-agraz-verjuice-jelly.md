## Ingredients

- Verjuice (agráz)
- Clarified syrup (almíbar clarificado) at pearl stage
- One quarter (cuartillo) of syrup for another quarter of verjuice

## Directions

1. Prepare the verjuice the same way as for preserves
2. Pass it through a sieve
3. Add the verjuice to the clarified syrup at pearl stage
4. Let it boil until the syrup returns to the same degree of cooking
5. Place the verjuice in cloths with the skimmer, which should be dipped in it
