## Ingredients

- chilpocle (smoked chile)
- manteca (lard)
- aceite (oil)
- cebolla (onion)
- jitomate (tomato)
- montalaya (a type of ingredient)
- queso fresco (fresh cheese)
- aguacate (avocado)

## Directions

1. Cook the chilpocle, cut into small pieces, and wash well
2. Put the pot with lard and oil on the heat
3. Chop thick onion and tomato; fry and then incorporate the chilpocle and let everything fry together
4. Cook the montalaya, fry it, and add to the pot
5. Garnish with fresh cheese and avocado
