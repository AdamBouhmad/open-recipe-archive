## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- green chile
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- oysters
- parsley
- vinegar
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make a refrito of onion, tomato, and garlic with slices of green chile.
2. Halfway through frying add ground clove, pepper, and cinnamon.
3. When fried add the oysters and if they are dry add a little of their own water, making sure they are brothy.
4. Chop parsley and add to the stew with a little vinegar and breadcrumbs.
