## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- sixteen egg yolks, beaten
- four ounces of butter
- four large sponge cakes
- fresh cheese, mashed
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a watered honey with three pounds of sugar, let it cool and add sixteen beaten egg yolks and four ounces of butter, having set aside a little to coat the casserole
2. Return to the fire so that it boils and reaches the proper point
3. Coat a casserole with the separated butter
4. Slice four large sponge cakes and without frying them or doing any other operation, place them in layers with intermediate layers of mashed cheese and cinnamon
5. When all the sponge cake is in place, pour the syrup over it and let the chongos boil three or four times over two fires
