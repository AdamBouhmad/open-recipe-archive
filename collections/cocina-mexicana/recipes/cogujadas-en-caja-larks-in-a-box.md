## Ingredients

- larks
- forcemeat with poultry livers and truffles
- oiled paper
- ham slice
- buttered paper
- tomato sauce or fat

## Directions

1. Debone the larks
2. Fill them with forcemeat made with poultry livers and truffles
3. Make a small box of oiled paper for each lark
4. Put the same forcemeat in the bottom
5. Place the lark on it with a ham slice on top, covered with buttered paper
6. Cook between two fires or in a campaign oven
7. When ready to serve, drain the fat and pour a thick sauce over them, preferably fried tomato sauce
