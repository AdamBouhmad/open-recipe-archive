## Ingredients

- Desalted and half-cooked codfish
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Oil
- Tomatoes
- Good vinegar
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. After thoroughly desalting and half-cooking the codfish, cut into regular portions and form a paste with a little flour, two egg yolks, salt and a little oil.
2. Mix well so that it forms a liquid which when lifted with a spoon makes a thread; if too thick, add a little warm water.
3. Dip the codfish portions in this paste and fry in oil until golden.
4. In a pot with butter fry well-peeled and ground tomatoes; add good vinegar, a piece of sugar, and the fried slices of codfish, letting it season.
5. When the broth has thickened a little, serve.
