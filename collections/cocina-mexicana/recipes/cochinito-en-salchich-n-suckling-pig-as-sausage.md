## Ingredients

- Fat suckling pig
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Ginger
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Ham slices
- Sage
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Aromatic herbs
- Lettuce leaves
- Lime or lemon slices

## Directions

1. Choose a fat pig and slightly larger than those usually roasted.
2. Singe the bristles at the flame, wash well and scrape until very clean.
3. Cut off the feet, split it down the middle, and carefully open the meat to remove all the bones without separating them from the skin, both from the head and from the rest of the body.
4. Bleed it in water and drain it, extending it on a table with the meat exposed, and sprinkle it with salt so it is well salted.
5. Do the same with pepper, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg, all ground and well mixed, covering the meat with it, which by this operation should become darkened.
6. Roll it up very tightly, starting from the head and stretching the extremities strongly.
7. Tie it with strong thread, gathering the ends so it becomes curved, as is usually done with blood sausage.
8. Cook it with wine, vinegar, water, some ham slices, sage, thyme, marjoram, and other aromatic herbs, salt and plenty of spices.
9. When cooked, remove it, cut and remove the threads, divide it into round slices, and serve them cold with any of the sauces explained in their place, or on lettuce leaves, adorning on top with slices of lime or lemon.
