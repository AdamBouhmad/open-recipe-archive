## Ingredients

- veal meat portions
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- chorizo slices
- longaniza pieces
- ham strips
- ancho chiles
- garlic
- cloves
- cumin
- lard

## Directions

1. Wash and cut veal portions
2. Place in a pot with salt, water, vinegar, chorizo slices, longaniza pieces and ham strips, ancho chiles ground with garlic, cloves and cumin
3. Put over fire
4. Once the meat is cooked, finish as in the previous recipes with burned lard
5. When dry and ready to serve, garnish with ham, chorizo, longaniza and onion slices
