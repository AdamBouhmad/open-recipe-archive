## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- broth
- wine
- bacon
- veal knuckle

## Directions

1. Clean and remove the bones, place on low heat in a pot with salt, pepper, carrots, onions, parsley, broth and wine in equal parts.
2. Also add all the bones and half a veal knuckle, place strips of bacon above and below.
3. When all is cooked, separate the bones, pass the broth through a sieve, let the fat congeal in a platter, and cut into pieces to serve with the hare.
