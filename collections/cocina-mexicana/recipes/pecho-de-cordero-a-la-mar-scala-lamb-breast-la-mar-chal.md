## Ingredients

- lamb breasts
- good broth
- german sauce
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- butter
- aspic or gelatin

## Directions

1. Cook two lamb breasts over hot coals with good broth
2. Drain and press between two pot covers to cool
3. Arrange in the shape of a heart leaving a bone end like ribs
4. Soak in German sauce and roll in bread crumbs
5. Dip in four egg yolks beaten with two ounces of butter
6. Cover with bread crumbs three times
7. Place briefly on the grill
8. Serve with watered gelatin or medium-strength aspic
