## Ingredients

- One quarter pound rice
- Water
- Beaten egg
- Sugar
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook one quarter pound of rice in water until it opens
2. Prepare it as indicated for rice cake
3. Instead of placing it in a mold, form balls or small rolls
4. Dip them in beaten egg mixed with sugar
5. Bread them
6. Dip them again in egg
7. Pass them a second time through egg
8. Fry them well
9. Serve them very hot
