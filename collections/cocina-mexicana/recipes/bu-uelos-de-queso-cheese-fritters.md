## Ingredients

- four handfuls of flour
- half fresh cheese
- one cup of cold lard
- a little cold water

## Directions

1. Grind the flour and fresh cheese well together
2. Add one cup of cold lard and a little cold water
3. Knead everything very well
4. Make the fritters and cover them with syrup
