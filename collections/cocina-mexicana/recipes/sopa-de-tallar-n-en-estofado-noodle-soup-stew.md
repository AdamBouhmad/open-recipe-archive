## Ingredients

- one chicken
- fine spices, ground
- lard
- head of garlic
- onion, minced
- Málaga wine
- salt
- water
- ham
- noodles
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces, wash it and place in a pot
2. Add ground fine spices, a little lard, a head of garlic with cloves crushed with their skin, minced onion, and plenty of Málaga wine with corresponding salt
3. Place on heat, covering the pot opening well with paper and a small pot of water on top
4. Add a little water to the chicken to soften it and a little ham cut into small pieces
5. When it is near mealtime, boil the noodles in water with a little salt
6. Once cooked, remove them with a skimmer and fry in a pan
7. Pour in the chicken that is boiling in the pot, whose broth serves for the stew
8. Before serving, add a little vinegar to the stew
