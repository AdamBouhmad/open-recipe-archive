## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- wine (good Malaga or other generous wine)
- sponge biscuits (soletas)
- dragée (small candies)
- raisins
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at high point with one pound of sugar
2. Remove from heat and add twelve beaten egg yolks and a cup of good Malaga wine or other generous wine
3. Return the mixture to the fire and when it reaches the point of pulling away from the pan, remove it
4. Pour half into a serving dish
5. Place a layer of sponge biscuits on top, sprinkled with wine and syrup, over them pour the remaining half of the egg mixture
6. Decorate with dragée, raisins and cinnamon
