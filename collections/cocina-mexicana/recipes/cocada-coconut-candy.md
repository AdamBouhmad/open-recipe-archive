## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one grated coconut
- one quartillo of milk
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a syrup at medium point with one pound of sugar.
2. Add one grated coconut, letting it boil until it reaches a high point.
3. Add one quartillo of milk in which four egg yolks have been dissolved.
4. Bring back to a boil and let it reach the point of cajeta.
5. Pour into cajetas or a platter, browning with a hot comal placed over the top.
