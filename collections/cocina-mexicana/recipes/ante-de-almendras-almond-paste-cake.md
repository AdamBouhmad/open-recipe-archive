## Ingredients

- almonds, peeled and ground
- sugar made into clarified syrup
- egg yolks
- wine
- sponge cake (mamón)
- wine
- paste
- dragées

## Directions

1. To two pounds of peeled and ground almonds add four pounds of sugar made into well-clarified syrup at raised point
2. Cool slightly and add the almonds
3. Stir until incorporated
4. Place over low heat and add thirty egg yolks beaten with one cuartillo of wine without removing from heat
5. Stir until it releases from the pot
6. Layer sponge cake sprinkled with wine and paste
7. Top with dragées
