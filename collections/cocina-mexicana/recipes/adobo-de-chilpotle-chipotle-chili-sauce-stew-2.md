## Ingredients

- chipotle
- tomatoes
- garlic
- cumin
- onion, chopped
- pork meat broth
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the chipotle
2. Roast some tomatoes and grind with garlic and cumin
3. Fry everything very well
4. Blanch chopped onion in large pieces
5. Fry the chile and add the meat broth with potatoes cut into quarters and cooked separately
