## Ingredients

- turkey, plucked
- pork loin, chopped
- tomatoes
- raw ham
- clove
- cinnamon
- peppercorns
- saffron
- cumin
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- parsley, chopped
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- spices, ground
- garlic
- strong vinegar
- salt
- lard
- melted lard

## Directions

1. After plucking and passing through flames, cut the head, legs and opening
2. Through this opening remove the entrails, leaving the gizzard so it does not stretch
3. Do this with a stick and break the bones to be removed by hand through the same opening, keeping its shape as much as possible
4. Through the same hole fill it with a hash made of chopped pork loin, tomatoes, pieces of raw ham, clove, cinnamon, peppercorns, saffron and cumin, all ground, raisins, almonds, olives, a little chopped parsley, wine and vinegar when well mixed
5. After finishing filling, sew the hole closed with thread
6. Put the turkey so prepared to cook with water and ground spices from those that entered in the hash
7. After cooking, grind garlic with strong vinegar, a little salt and pepper and anoint the turkey
8. Also anoint a pot with lard, put in it the turkey anointed with this sauce, sprinkle with melted lard on top, and put in the oven or over two fires
9. Serve with the sauce that seems best
