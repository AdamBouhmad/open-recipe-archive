## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Stuffing made like picadillo for stuffed chiles
- Large tomatoes
- Sherry wine
- Castilian vinegar
- Creole vinegar
- Garlic
- Aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. When the chickens are plucked, make a small opening along the length that can be sewn closed after filling
2. Make the filling the same as picadillo for stuffed chiles
3. Blister large tomatoes
4. Fill the chickens and sew the opening so the filling does not come out
5. Wrap in tomato slices and arrange in the pan
6. Add a quarter measure of Sherry wine, another of Castilian vinegar and one of creole vinegar
7. Add garlic and aromatic herbs
8. Let it boil a few times in the broth
9. Turn carefully so the filling and tomato do not fall
10. Place a comal over the chickens and put one or two bricks on top so they are pressed down
