## Ingredients

- fifty oysters
- butter
- parsley
- tender onions
- flour
- white wine
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Place fifty oysters in hot water, and when it begins to boil, remove them and place in cold water.
2. Dry them and chop them finely; other fish meat may be added if available.
3. Place butter, parsley, and tender onions in a pan and sauté, then add a pinch of flour and half a glass of white wine.
4. Add this mixture to the oysters and let them simmer until all the liquid is consumed.
5. When about to serve, mix with three egg yolks dissolved in cream.
