## Ingredients

- bobo
- mostaza
- pan frito
- pan molido
- vinagre
- clavo
- pimienta
- nuez moscada

## Directions

1. Clean the bobo and cook it.
2. Grind mustard, washing it so it is not too sharp.
3. Mix in fried ground bread, vinegar, clove, pepper and nutmeg.
4. Coat the bobo with this sauce when serving.
