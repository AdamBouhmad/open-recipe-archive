## Ingredients

- Pámpano
- Parsley, chopped
- Garlic
- Onion
- Eggs
- Oil
- Requesón (fresh cheese)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Dried chile, ground and sifted

## Directions

1. After washing the pámpano and desalting it if it were salt-cured, chop plenty of parsley, garlic and onion
2. Mix three or four raw eggs and add oil, a piece of requesón, and salt-pepper, forming a mass that is not too loose
3. Give the fish a boil to be able to remove the bones
4. Well deboned, fill it with the prepared mass
5. Add salt-pepper, coat with oil on top
6. Sprinkle with dried chile, ground and sifted
7. Wrap in paper coated with oil and fry in oil with garlic
8. Serve with one of the sauces for fish
