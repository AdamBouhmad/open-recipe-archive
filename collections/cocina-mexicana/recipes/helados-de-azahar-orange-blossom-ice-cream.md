## Ingredients

- ten ounces of white sugar
- one and a half cuartillo of water
- half a pound of good, recently picked orange blossoms
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Dissolve ten ounces of white sugar in one and a half cuartillo of water over gentle heat
2. At the moment it begins to boil, pour over half a pound of good, recently picked orange blossoms
3. Add the juice of two lemons
4. Cover and leave in infusion for two, four or six hours
5. Strain through muslin, cool and freeze
