## Ingredients

- Two ounces each of tarragon leaves wilted in the shade, pimpinella leaves, chives, and cleaned shallots
- One and a half ounces each of fresh garlic and elderflower
- Stripped peels of two lemons
- Twelve crushed cloves
- Twelve cuartillos of the strongest good white Castilian vinegar
- Two and a half ounces of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Place in a bottle the tarragon leaves wilted in the shade, pimpinella leaves, chives, cleaned shallots, fresh garlic, elderflower, the stripped peels of two lemons, and twelve crushed cloves
2. Add twelve cuartillos of the strongest good white Castilian vinegar
3. Since the ingredients are very viscous and diminish the strength of the vinegar, add two and a half ounces of spirits of wine, with which an excellent vinegar will be obtained that keeps for a long time
4. Leave the ingredients to infuse for one month
5. Decant the vinegar as you would the tarragon vinegar
