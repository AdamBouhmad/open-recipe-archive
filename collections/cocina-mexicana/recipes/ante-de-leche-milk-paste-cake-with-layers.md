## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- orange blossom water
- aromatics
- sponge cake (mamón)
- lard
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add sufficient egg yolks to milk with sugar, cinnamon, orange blossom water and aromatics to taste
2. Layer sponge cake and soak well
3. Add all garnishes and cinnamon powder to every layer
4. First grease the pot with lard to place the layers
5. Once everything is assembled cook between two fires or in oven with cinnamon on top
