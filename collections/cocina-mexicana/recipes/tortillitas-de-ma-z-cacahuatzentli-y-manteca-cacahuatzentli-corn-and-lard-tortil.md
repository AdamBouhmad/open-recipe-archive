## Ingredients

- one and a half quartillos of prepared cacahuatzentli corn
- one pound of lard
- one pound of powdered sugar
- eight eggs
- a little settled tequesquite water

## Directions

1. Prepare one and a half quartillos of corn as in the previous article, grind it and mix with one pound of lard, one pound of powdered sugar, eight eggs and a little settled tequesquite water
2. Knead everything, form the tortillitas and cook them as in the preceding articles
