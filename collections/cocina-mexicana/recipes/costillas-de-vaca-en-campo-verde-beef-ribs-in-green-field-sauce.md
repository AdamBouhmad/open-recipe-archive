## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- butter
- mixed herbs bundle
- flour
- beef broth
- white wine
- salt
- black pepper
- chervil
- parsley
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the ribs in a pot with a piece of butter and a mixed herbs bundle, and parboil or half-fry over the fire
2. Add a little flour, moisten with broth and a glass of white wine, and season with salt and black pepper
3. Cook over gentle heat, remove excess fat, and let the sauce reduce
4. Add about a walnut of good butter kneaded with flour, and a good amount of chervil, parsley, and chopped parsley
5. Bind the sauce and add lemon juice or a dash of vinegar
