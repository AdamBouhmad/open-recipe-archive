## Ingredients

- 2 pounds chickpeas, soaked overnight with a little salt
- meat broth
- 1 onion
- bacon
- carrot
- bouquet of herbs

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of chickpeas that have been soaked overnight with a little salt in meat broth.
2. Add one onion, bacon, carrot, and a small bouquet of herbs.
3. Ensure the boil is gentle and gradual.
4. When cooked, remove the onion, carrot, and bacon.
5. Pass all through a sieve or strainer, pressing well.
6. Remove the fat, season, heat, and serve the purée.
