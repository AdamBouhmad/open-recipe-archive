## Ingredients

- lamb tripe
- toasted bread
- egg yolks
- Spanish sauce (salsa española)

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb tripe and grind with toasted bread and egg yolks
2. Mix this with Spanish sauce
3. Use as a base sauce for serving certain roasts or to season other meats
