## Ingredients

- Veal fillet
- Bone marrow from beef
- Salt
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Slice and flatten a veal fillet.
2. For each pound of meat, add a quarter portion of beef bone marrow, also sliced, and place it at the bottom of the pot.
3. Place the veal on top and add salt.
4. Put the pot on heat without adding any water.
5. When the meat is almost cooked, add small potatoes to cook with it and do not let it dry out.
