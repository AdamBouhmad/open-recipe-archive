## Ingredients

- 2 cuartillos good milk
- 2 cuartillos custard (natillas)
- 4 or 5 tablespoons powdered sugar
- egg whites from fresh eggs
- 1 tablespoon orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Combine 2 cuartillos of good milk with 2 cuartillos of custard and 4 or 5 tablespoons of powdered sugar
2. Let it boil until one-third has been consumed
3. Pour into beaten fresh egg whites when they are well risen
4. Stir continuously over the fire
5. After it has boiled a few times, add 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water
6. Finish and serve cold
