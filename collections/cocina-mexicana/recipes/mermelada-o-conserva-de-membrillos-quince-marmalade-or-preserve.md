## Ingredients

- most ripe quinces
- one pound of sugar per pound of juice

## Directions

1. Take the ripest quinces, cut into four pieces
2. Cook well and pass through a sieve
3. Put one pound of sugar per pound of juice in a vessel, clarify, add the juice and let it boil for a while before putting it in
