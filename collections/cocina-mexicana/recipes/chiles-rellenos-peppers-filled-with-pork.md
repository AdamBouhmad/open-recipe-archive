## Ingredients

- Pork loin pieces
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Candied citron
- Olives
- Apples
- Banana
- Small sausages
- Peppers
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Chop pork loin pieces and cook them
2. When cooked, chop garlic and tomato and fry them
3. Add raisins, almonds, capers, candied citron, olives, apples, banana and small sausages, all chopped
4. Add this mixture to the meat
5. When peppers are well cleaned and roasted, fill them with the mixture and fry them
6. Make a tomato sauce of your preference to serve with them
