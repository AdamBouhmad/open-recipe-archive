## Ingredients

- rabbit
- oil
- parsley
- garlic
- pepper
- hot water
- laurel leaves
- clove
- cinnamon
- capers or lime relish

## Directions

1. Skinned and cleaned without wetting, cut into pieces and place in a pot with raw oil, parsley, garlic and pepper to braise over low heat
2. Add hot water, and when half cooked add laurel leaves, clove and a pinch of cinnamon
3. Will be very good with capers or lime relish
