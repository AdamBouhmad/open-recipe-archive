## Ingredients

- purple tunas (dye tunas)
- pulque
- guava slices
- plantain slices
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Dissolve the peeled purple tunas in a little pulque
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Sweeten everything
4. Add slices of guava and plantain
5. Add small pieces of clove and cinnamon
