## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Lard
- Oil
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Black pepper
- Oregano
- Garlic
- Water
- Tomatoes

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning, and drying the chickens, fry them raw in lard mixed with oil and appropriate salt in a pot
2. Add vinegar instead of water with pepper, oregano, and garlic, all ground
3. After this has been well fried, add only the water strictly necessary for the chickens to cook
4. Cover the pot and let it boil over gentle heat until all the broth is consumed and the chickens remain with only the lard, which should dry
5. When serving, bring a fine tomato sauce to the table in sauce boats
