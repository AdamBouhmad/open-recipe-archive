## Ingredients

- cleaned and dried bobo fish
- oil or butter for frying
- paper, buttered
- mustard
- water
- cold bread, soaked
- salt
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning and drying the bobo fish with a napkin, fry it and wrap it in buttered papers to roast on a grill, being careful to turn it so it does not burn.
2. Grind the mustard in water and strain it, repeating this operation three times, changing the water to remove the pungency, and in the last operation add a piece of cold soaked bread which will also be ground.
3. Add salt, oil and vinegar so that it is thick and everything is mixed.
4. Remove the papers from the bobo fish, pour the sauce over it with more oil, once arranged on the fish dish.
