## Ingredients

- Beef shanks
- Pearl onions
- Turnips
- Carrots
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Mixed herbs bouquet
- Concentrated broth

## Directions

1. After rounding the meat around the bone of the shanks, arrange them with pearl onions, turnips, carrots, salt, pepper, cloves and a mixed herb bouquet.
2. Moisten with concentrated broth.
3. When cooked, arrange them surrounded by the vegetables that were cooked with them.
4. Strain the broth and let it thicken until it becomes a jelly.
5. Pour over the shanks and let set.
