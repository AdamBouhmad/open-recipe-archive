## Ingredients

- onions, chopped
- water
- salt
- lard from the offal
- blood
- pepper
- oregano
- optional: sweets or hot spices, crushed garlic, anise, fennel, cumin, cilantro, ginger, fresh pine nuts

## Directions

1. Chop some onions and place in clear cloth, let drain overnight
2. Just before making the morcillas, cook the onion in salted water without removing from cloth
3. When removed, let drain for a while
4. Chop the lard from the offal well and incorporate with the blood and onion, making the mixture somewhat thin
5. Season mainly with salt, pepper, and oregano
6. Optional additions may include sweets or hot spices, crushed garlic, anise, fennel, cumin, cilantro, ginger, and fresh pine nuts, choosing according to taste and regional style
7. Fill a long strip of pork or veal intestine
8. At intervals of half a palm, make ties, each forming a morcilla
9. Cook in a pot prepared for this purpose
