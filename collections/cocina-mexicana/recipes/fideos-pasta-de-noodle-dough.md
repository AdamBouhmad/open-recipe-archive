## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 4 egg yolks
- half a cup of melted butter
- 1 piece of yeast the size of an egg, dissolved in a cup of warm water
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the egg yolks, melted butter, and the yeast dissolved in warm water
2. Season with a little salt
3. Knead together thoroughly until it acquires the proper consistency
4. Place in molds through the concave part, pressing so that the noodles come out through the holes on the opposite side
5. As they reach a quarter in length or the desired size, roll them gently so they do not stick to one another
6. Spread to dry in the sun for three days on clean napkins
7. When completely dry, store for use
8. Do not accumulate a large quantity, as after a certain time they spoil and corrupt completely
