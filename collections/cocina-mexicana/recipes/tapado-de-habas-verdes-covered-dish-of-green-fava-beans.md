## Ingredients

- Lard
- Tomatoes, raw, sliced
- Onions, cut into quarters and blanched
- Green fava beans, raw and without skins
- Oregano
- Tomatoes, ground
- Bread, browned in lard
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Grease a cazuela with lard and place a layer of raw tomato slices, and another of blanched onion quarters
2. Add the raw, unskinned green fava beans
3. Place another layer of tomato, another of onion, and another of beans, adding a little oregano
4. Add tomato ground with bread browned in lard
5. When well fried, add sufficient water
6. When boiling well, squeeze lemon juice into it
7. Season with the necessary fine salt and leave to boil over low heat until everything is cooked
