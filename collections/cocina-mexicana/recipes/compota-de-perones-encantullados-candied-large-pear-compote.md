## Ingredients

- Large pears
- Cloves of spice, two per pear
- Cinnamon stick, three pieces per pear

## Directions

1. Proceed the same as for candied pear compote (see above), but do not fill them
2. When they are arranged on the platter, place two cloves of spice and three cinnamon sticks on each one
