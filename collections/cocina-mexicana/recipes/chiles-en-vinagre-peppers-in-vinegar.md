## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Water
- Salt
- Garlic head
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Put chiles in boiling water as they are
2. The next day when already spongy, carefully remove the stem so they don't break
3. Give them a boil with salt and a head of garlic
4. Remove the water again and place in a dry jar
5. Add oil, vinegar, salt and pepper, oregano, thyme, laurel, some lemon slices and whole pepper
6. Oil and vinegar must completely cover the chiles
7. Seal in this state and after eight days they can be eaten
