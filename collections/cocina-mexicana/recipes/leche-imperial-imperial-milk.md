## Ingredients

- Twenty egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of starch
- Four cuartillos of milk
- Sugar to taste
- Half a pound of ground almond

## Directions

1. Dissolve twenty egg yolks and two tablespoons of starch in four cuartillos of milk and sweeten to taste
2. Strain and add half a pound of ground almond
3. Heat until it reaches the consistency of burnt milk
