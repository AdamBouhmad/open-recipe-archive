## Ingredients

- good wine of deep or dark color
- cinnamon
- mace
- clove
- sugar dissolved in distilled rose water, orange blossom water, jonquil water, or jasmine water

## Directions

1. Mix together the wine, cinnamon, mace, clove, and sugar dissolved in the chosen distilled water
2. Pour the mixture into an alembic
3. Seal the joints well
4. Perform the distillation over gentle heat
5. If desired, amber flavor can be added by dissolving some grains in the liquor according to the quantity of the distillate, but only after distillation is complete
