## Ingredients

- Citron peels, defatted, finely cut, from two citrons
- Tarragon, fresh
- Mace
- Aguardiente
- Sugar, white
- Water
- Spirit of jasmine
- Orange flower water

## Directions

1. Cut the defatted citron peels very finely from two citrons and combine with one ounce of fresh tarragon and one dram of mace
2. Leave in infusion for several hours at atmospheric temperature in eight quarts of aguardiente
3. Proceed with distillation to extract four quarts of liquor
4. To this liquor add one and a half pounds of white sugar, dissolved in eight quarts of common water, half an ounce of spirit of jasmine, and one quart of orange flower water
5. Clarify with a cloth and store in well-sealed bottles
