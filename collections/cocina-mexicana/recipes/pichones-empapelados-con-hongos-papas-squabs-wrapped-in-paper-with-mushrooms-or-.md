## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- hongos ó papas cocidas (cooked mushrooms or potatoes)
- ajo (garlic)
- peregil (parsley)
- mantequilla (butter)
- sal molida (ground salt)
- papel aceitado (oiled paper)

## Directions

1. After removing the wings and splitting them lengthwise into two parts, dust with ground salt, fry in butter, and remove when they have taken on some consistency
2. In the remaining butter in the pot, add cooked mushrooms or potatoes, garlic and parsley all chopped
3. Once the sauce has reduced and taken on good flavor, place it on the squabs, dividing it for each half, and putting a slice of ham on the sides
4. Wrap in oiled paper and let cook on the grill
