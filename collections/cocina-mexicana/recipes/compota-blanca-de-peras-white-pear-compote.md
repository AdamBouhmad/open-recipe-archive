## Ingredients

- Pears
- Water
- Syrup
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Put the pears in boiling water
2. Before they are cooked, transfer to cold water
3. Peel immediately and scrape the stem
4. Return to cold water
5. Cook whole or in pieces in syrup with a slice of lemon
6. Let finish cooking
7. Serve hot or cold
