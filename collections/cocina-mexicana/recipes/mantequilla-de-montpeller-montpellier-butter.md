## Ingredients

- twelve anchovies
- twelve small pickles
- two spoonfuls of reinforced sauce
- two handfuls of capers
- twenty-four hard egg yolks
- salt and pepper
- six raw egg yolks
- vinegar
- two quarts of good oil
- spinach green for coloring

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar the anchovies, pickles, reinforced sauce, capers, hard egg yolks, salt and pepper until forming a paste
2. Add the six raw egg yolks
3. Continue pounding the mixture
4. Add vinegar and the oil little by little while pounding, not all at once, until the mixture reaches the consistency of butter
5. Pass this composition through fine cloth
6. Brighten the color with a little spinach green
7. Use principally for cold appetizers
