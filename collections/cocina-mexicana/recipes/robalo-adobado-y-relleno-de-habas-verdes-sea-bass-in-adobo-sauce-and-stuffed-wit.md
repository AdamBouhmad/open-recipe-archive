## Ingredients

- Sea bass, cooked
- Wide chiles, soaked and ground
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Fresh beans bound with egg
- Oiled papers
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Make an adobo with wide chiles soaked and ground with garlic, pepper, cloves, salt and a little vinegar
2. Spread this adobo on the fish after cooking
3. Fill with fresh beans bound with egg
4. Wrap in oiled papers
5. Fry in lard
