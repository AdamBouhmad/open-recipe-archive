## Ingredients

- chichicuilotes (small birds)
- water
- salt
- two or three onions
- lard
- tomatoes
- garlic
- a little pepper
- cilantro in powder
- flour or dried bread meal
- wine

## Directions

1. Clean the birds well
2. Cook with water, salt, and two or three onions
3. Once cooked, fry in lard
4. In the same pan add ground tomatoes, chopped garlic, a little pepper, cilantro in powder, and chopped onions
5. Add flour or dried bread meal meal, which should be browned to the degree needed according to the color desired for the sauce
6. Add the birds cut in half, so that one part keeps the neck and the other the rump
7. Add sufficient water, seasoned with salt, pepper, cinnamon, and a little wine
