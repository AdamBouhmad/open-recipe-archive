## Ingredients

- chiles
- papas fritas (fried potatoes)
- pan rallado (bread crumbs)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta molida (ground pepper)

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles the same way as previous recipes
2. Fill with fried potatoes mixed with bread crumbs, salt, and ground pepper
3. Prepare and serve according to the method for previous stuffed chile recipes
