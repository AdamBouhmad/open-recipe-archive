## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Partridges and quail
- White wine
- Onion
- Parsley
- Spices

## Directions

1. Fry bacon in pieces and remove it
2. In the remaining fat, fry the birds
3. Place in a pot and with the same fat fry chopped onion and parsley
4. Add a glass of white wine and spices
5. Cook slowly with the pot well covered
