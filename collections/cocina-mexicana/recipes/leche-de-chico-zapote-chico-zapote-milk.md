## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- one pound sugar
- one cup rice
- one cinnamon stick
- four chico-zapotes

## Directions

1. Grind the sugar and rice together
2. Add to the milk and strain
3. Place the pot on the heat with a cinnamon stick
4. Once it has thickened slightly, peel four chico-zapotes, grind them, and incorporate into the mixture
5. It should remain very thick
6. Once done, place in a serving dish
