## Ingredients

- Rooster crests
- Rooster kidneys
- Salt
- Ruffled or velvet sauce
- Poultry aspic
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook rooster crests and kidneys in salted water
2. Transfer to a saucepan with ruffled or velvet sauce and some poultry aspic
3. Let everything simmer gently for about ten minutes
4. Thicken the sauce
5. Add a little lemon juice
6. Arrange the crests for serving at the table
