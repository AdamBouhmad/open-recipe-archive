## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- garlic
- butter
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook with water and salt
2. Peel two large heads of garlic
3. Parboil in butter and oil and without removing the garlic add half a bottle of ordinary vinegar
4. Add some bay leaves, a little thyme and pepper powder
