## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook a cabbage for about a quarter hour
2. Place it afterwards in cold water
3. Remove the leaves one by one and coat them in batter
4. Reassemble it as if it were whole, fastening it with a cord or thread
5. Put it to cook and when serving, squeeze it, placing it on a platter and pour a parsley sauce over it
