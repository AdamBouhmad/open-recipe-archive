## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two ounces of sugar
- one tablespoon of orange flower
- one quartillo of milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve six eggs with two ounces of sugar, one tablespoon of orange flower and a quartillo of milk.
2. Place this in a very deep dish or plate in which it is to be served.
3. Leave to cook three-quarters of an hour in a bain-marie.
4. Dust with very fine sugar and then pass a hot iron plate over the top.
5. Can be served cold or hot.
