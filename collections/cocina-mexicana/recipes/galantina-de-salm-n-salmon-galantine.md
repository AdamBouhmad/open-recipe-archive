## Ingredients

- 1 large salmon, at least one and a half to two feet long
- Strips of anchovies
- Strips of marinated tuna
- Pickled gherkins
- Truffles
- Quenelles of any fish
- Light broth
- Aspic jelly
- Butter
- Fried aspic toast for garnish

## Directions

1. Choose one of the largest salmon or one that is at least one and a half to two feet long
2. Open it along the belly and remove the backbone
3. Extend it on clean linen and lard it with thick strips of anchovies and marinated tuna, with pickled gherkins and truffles
4. Spread quenelles of any fish over the entire surface of the flesh
5. Close the salmon to give it its natural shape
6. Wrap it in a napkin, place it to cook in light broth, and let it cool
7. Unwrap it, cover it with aspic jelly, garnish with butter and fried aspic toast, and serve
