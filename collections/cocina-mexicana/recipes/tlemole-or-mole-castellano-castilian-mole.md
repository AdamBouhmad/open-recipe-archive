## Ingredients

- pine nuts
- hazelnuts
- walnuts
- sesame seeds
- ancho chiles
- avocado leaves
- hard biscuit
- lard
- meat broth
- cooked meat
- pork pieces

## Directions

1. Toast pine nuts, hazelnuts, walnuts, sesame seeds, ancho chiles and avocado leaves, and grind everything with hard biscuit
2. Fry the ground mixture in plenty of lard and loosen it with the broth of the meat to be cooked in this mole
3. Put in it the cooked meat divided into regular portions with small pieces of cooked pork, and leave to boil until everything is well seasoned
4. When serving, sprinkle toasted or raw sesame seeds on top, as preferred
