## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- fifteen egg yolks
- half a pound of almonds
- cinnamon
- wine

## Directions

1. Make it exactly the same as the previous article, with the only difference being fifteen egg yolks and half a pound of almond for each pound of sugar
2. The cinnamon and wine in the same quantities as the preceding article
