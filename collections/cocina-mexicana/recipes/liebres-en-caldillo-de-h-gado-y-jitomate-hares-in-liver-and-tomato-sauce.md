## Ingredients

- Hare
- lard
- tomatoes
- almonds
- liver
- cilantro
- cumin
- clove
- pepper
- salt
- hare broth
- parsley
- oil
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry the quarters of hare as in the previous recipe, then in the same lard fry roasted tomatoes, toasted almonds, cooked livers, cilantro, cumin, clove and pepper, ground all together
2. Place in this mixture the already fried quarters of hare with salt, broth from the pot, chopped parsley, oil and a little vinegar
3. Let boil until the sauce is seasoned, adding when removed from heat small chiles and olives
