## Ingredients

- Two rabbits
- Ham
- Potatoes
- Carrot
- Onions
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Allspice
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Wine
- Broth or water
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Bone two rabbits well; lard one with ham and mince the other with an equal piece of ham coarsely
2. Season the minced meat with salt and chopped potatoes
3. Fill the whole rabbit with this forcemeat, interpolating strips of bacon, and when full, close it and wrap it in a clean thin cloth
4. Place it in a pan lined with ham, and arrange around it pieces of carrot, quartered onions, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, allspice, salt, and equal parts of wine and broth or water
5. Add the cracked bones of the rabbits and cover with slices of ham on top
6. Cook over two gentle fires, and when cooked, skim off the broth, let it cool and strain it well, degreasing it thoroughly
7. Clarify with an egg white, let it thicken and pour into dishes so it sets well
8. Cover the rabbit with this aspic, which will go with the slices of ham on top
