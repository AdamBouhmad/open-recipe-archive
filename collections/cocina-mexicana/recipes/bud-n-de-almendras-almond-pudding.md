## Ingredients

- half pound of almonds
- four ounces of sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- eight egg whites
- butter
- sponge cake of velorio

## Directions

1. Grind half a pound of almonds without adding water
2. Separately grind four ounces of sugar and sift it
3. Beat twelve egg yolks and separately beat very well eight egg whites that are mixed with the yolks and the almonds after beating
4. Once the almonds are well broken up, add the sugar, beat again and taste
5. If you want it sweeter, beat the mixture a lot
6. Grease a pan with butter and a little velorio sponge cake and do not fill the casserole too much because it will puff up
7. Place in the oven and when it browns on top put a paper on it so it does not burn
8. With a straw check if it is cooked; when the straw comes out clean it is done
9. A scraping of orange on top suits it very well
