## Ingredients

- squash seeds
- wide chile
- walnuts
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast the squash seeds and the wide chile needed only to give color
2. Grind both things with the walnuts
3. Fry everything in lard with salt
4. Complete the operation as in the preceding recipes
