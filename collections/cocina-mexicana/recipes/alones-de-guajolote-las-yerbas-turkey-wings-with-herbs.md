## Ingredients

- Turkey wings
- Bacon pieces
- Butter
- Herbs

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the wings
2. After plucking and cleaning, blanch lightly in boiling water
3. Cover with small pieces of bacon, passed through completely
4. Cook like pipila in adobo, covering with paper well soaked in butter
5. Once well cooked, place in a sauce or stew of herbs
