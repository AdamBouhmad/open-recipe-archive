## Ingredients

- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- lard
- oil
- capers
- olives
- oysters
- oyster broth

## Directions

1. Chop a good amount of tomato, onion, garlic, and parsley finely.
2. Heat lard and when well browned add oil.
3. When hot, add the chopped mixture with capers and chopped olives.
4. When all is fried, add the oysters with some of their own broth, ensuring the stew is somewhat thick.
5. Season with salt and allow to cook through.
