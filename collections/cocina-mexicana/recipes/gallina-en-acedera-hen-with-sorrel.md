## Ingredients

- one hen, roasted
- bacon
- chopped onion
- broth
- wine
- fresh lard
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- spices
- sorrel sauce

## Directions

1. After roasting, cut the hen into quarters
2. Sauté with bacon and a little chopped onion
3. Add broth with a little wine, fresh lard, salt and pepper, clove and other spices
4. After cooking, reduce the broth and add sorrel sauce
