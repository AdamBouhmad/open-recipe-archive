## Ingredients

- Pastry dough in sheets
- Apricot jam

## Directions

1. Make a little pastry dough in sheets and cut it into two round pieces
2. Place on each one the thickness of a hazelnut of apricot marmalade
3. Moisten and fold the edges
4. Place them on a pot cover from where they will slide into the frying oil
5. When they are golden, dust them with sugar
6. Remove them
