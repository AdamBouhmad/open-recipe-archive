## Ingredients

- Half a pound of finely ground sugar
- Twelve egg yolks
- Two quartillos of milk
- Red and fragrant pastille

## Directions

1. Dissolve in a pan half a pound of finely ground sugar and twelve egg yolks
2. Add two quartillos of milk and put the pan on the fire, stirring the mixture constantly so it does not burn
3. When it takes body, remove and pour into the platter over filled mamones, placing red and fragrant pastille on top of everything
