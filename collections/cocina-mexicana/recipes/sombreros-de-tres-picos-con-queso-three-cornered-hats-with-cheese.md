## Ingredients

- Fresh cheese, one pound and twelve ounces, well cleaned and desalted if very salty, or with a little salt if not salty
- Good flour, a handful, passed through a sieve
- Melted butter, four ounces
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Knead the cheese with the hands
2. Add a handful of good flour passed through a sieve and knead
3. Add the melted butter and knead a third time with eggs
4. Rest the dough
5. Shape into three-cornered hats
6. Cook in a fairly hot oven
