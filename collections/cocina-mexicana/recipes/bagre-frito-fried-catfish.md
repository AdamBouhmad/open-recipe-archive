## Ingredients

- Catfish
- Flour
- Oil or lard
- Garlic, chopped
- Sour orange or lemon slices
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sliced onion, deflegmed
- Oil and vinegar
- Oregano
- Chopped onion

## Directions

1. After washing the catfish, cut it into portions, dry it, roll it in flour
2. Fry in oil or lard in which chopped garlic has been fried until golden
3. When the fish is well browned, remove and serve alone or with sour orange or lemon slices, salt, pepper, and deflegmed sliced onion; or dress it with oil and vinegar with chopped onion, oregano powder, and salt
