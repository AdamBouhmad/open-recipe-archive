## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- chipotle chiles
- lard
- white broth or chicharrón broth
- guajes
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind sufficient tomatoes raw with small chipotles
2. Place a clay pot on the fire with lard and fry the tomato and the cleaned and minced chipotles
3. Then add the white broth or chicharrón broth with the same broth in which the chile was cooked and its corresponding salt
4. When it lacks little for being seasoned, grind the trimmed guajes so it only boils once
