## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- five egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- a pinch of cinnamon
- butter
- ground biscuit
- sliced ladyfingers

## Directions

1. Dissolve five egg yolks in a cup of milk with sugar to taste and a pinch of cinnamon
2. Prepare the pudding mold with butter and ground biscuit
3. Layer sliced ladyfingers and some of this milk until the mold is full
4. Cook and do not unmold until it is well cold, because if you do it breaks
