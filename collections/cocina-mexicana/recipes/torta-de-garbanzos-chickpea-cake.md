## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- salt
- toasted chickpea
- aged cheese
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas from the evening in warm water with a little salt
2. The next day cook them in water with ash, wash and remove skins
3. Grind with toasted chickpea and aged cheese
4. Mix with beaten eggs
5. Cook over two fires
