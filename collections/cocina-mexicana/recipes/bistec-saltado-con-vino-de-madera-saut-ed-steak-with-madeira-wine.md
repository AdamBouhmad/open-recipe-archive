## Ingredients

- slices of sirloin, prepared as in the previous articles
- melted butter
- salt
- black pepper
- one good glass of Madeira wine
- beef jelly
- Spanish sauce
- butter kneaded with pepper

## Directions

1. Place the sirloin slices in a sauté pan with melted butter
2. Sprinkle with salt and black pepper
3. Sauté at the moment of serving
4. Arrange in a crown shape when fried
5. Reduce one good glass of Madeira wine with a little beef jelly and Spanish sauce in the same pan from which the butter has been drained, preserving the fond
6. Pass this sauce through a sieve with a little butter kneaded with pepper if it is not sufficiently clear
7. Pour the sauce over the steaks
