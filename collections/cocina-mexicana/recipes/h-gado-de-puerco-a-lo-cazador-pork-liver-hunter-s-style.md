## Ingredients

- pork liver
- ham
- parsley
- onions
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- fine herbs
- chives
- flour
- juice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place on a platter and divide into two pieces without separating them
2. Extend on the platter and cut through the entire thickness without completely dividing the pieces
3. Mince together ham, parsley, and some onions, and cover the surface of the liver with this mixture
4. Dust with salt and pepper and bathe with oil
5. Let sit for one or two hours
6. Fold both halves of the liver back together, fill with lard strips, and place in a pot to cook slowly
7. When the liver is completely cooked, remove from the pot and place on a platter
8. Degrease the sauce
9. Add a few spoonfuls of juice and a few drops of vinegar when serving
