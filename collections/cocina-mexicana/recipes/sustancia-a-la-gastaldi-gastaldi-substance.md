## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Four tablespoons of curled sauce
- Three quartillos of cream with milk
- Sugar
- Flour (if curled sauce is not available)
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the onion as for onion substance and pass it over the fire in butter so it does not take color
2. When well dissolved, add four tablespoons of curled sauce, three quartillos of cream with milk, and a piece of sugar the size of a walnut
3. Let the substance reduce over high heat, stirring continuously
4. When it has thickened, pass it through a sieve
5. If curled sauce is not available, add a tablespoon of flour, the cream, salt and pepper, and complete the operation as described, placing it over gentle heat or in a water bath to prevent it from boiling
