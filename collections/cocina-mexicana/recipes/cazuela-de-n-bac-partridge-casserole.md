## Ingredients

- Four partridges, gutted with wings and legs cut off
- Partridge livers
- Meat
- Fat ham, double the weight of the meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Ginger
- Nutmeg
- Truffle scrapings
- Two pounds of truffles
- Lard for the bottom layer

## Directions

1. Fold the legs inward and tie the four partridges to perches after gutting them and cutting off the wings and legs
2. Scald in hot water to stiffen them slightly, dry, clean and lard with thick lardoons
3. Make a stuffing with the partridge livers and meat, and double the amount of fat ham, pounded or ground all together and mixed well
4. Season with salt, pepper, clove, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, all in powder form, and truffle scrapings
5. Wash, chop and season two pounds of truffles
6. Opening the partridges along the back, fill them with the chopped truffle mixture
7. Place a layer of the stuffing at the bottom of the casserole
8. Put the partridges on top, filling the spaces or interstices
