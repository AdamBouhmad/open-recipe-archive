## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon of melted lard
- a pinch of salt
- flour as needed
- egg white for sealing
- cold syrup
- optional: coconut paste filling with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, orange blossom water, and syrup

## Directions

1. Take two egg yolks, one tablespoon of melted lard, a pinch of salt, and enough flour to make a soft dough.
2. Once well beaten, extend with a palote, cut into small shapes, and stick the points together with egg white.
3. Let cool for about half an hour before frying.
4. After frying, brush with cold syrup so they do not fall apart.
5. If desired, fill with coconut paste made with all its components of raisins, almonds, and pine nuts, orange blossom water, and syrup on top.
