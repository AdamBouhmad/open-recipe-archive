## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 quarter of a cup of cream
- sugar
- nutmeg zest
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks and add them by turning and mixing into a quarter of a cup of cream seasoned with sugar, nutmeg zest, and orange blossom water
2. Cook in a water bath over a plate, giving it the consistency of cream and proceeding the same way
