## Ingredients

- Four egg yolks
- One cup of flour
- Salt
- Two cups of milk
- Lard

## Directions

1. Mix four egg yolks with one cup of flour and a little salt
2. Beat and incorporate with two cups of milk
3. After everything is well mixed and without lumps, pour into a frying pan greased with lard
4. Proceed as explained in the following article
