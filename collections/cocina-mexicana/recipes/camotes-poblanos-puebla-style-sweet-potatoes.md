## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- water
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. After the sweet potatoes are cooked, peeled, ground and sprinkled with water, pass them through a sieve
2. With an equal weight of sugar, make a clarified syrup to the point of setting in water
3. Then mix in the sweet potato, stirring well so it breaks down
4. Return everything to the fire until it reaches the point of coming away from the pan, adding a little orange blossom water
5. Once the paste is cold, shape the sweet potato candies, dusting with powdered sugar in your hand so they don't stick
