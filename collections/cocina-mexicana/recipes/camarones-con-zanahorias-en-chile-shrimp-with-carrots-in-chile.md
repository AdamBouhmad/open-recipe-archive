## Ingredients

- wide chile, soaked
- garlic, plenty of
- cooked and whole shrimp
- water in which shrimp were cooked
- raw carrots, cut into small pieces
- salt
- raw onion quarters, blanched

## Directions

1. Soak wide chile and grind it with plenty of garlic
2. Fry until it separates from the pan
3. Add the cooked and whole shrimp and the same water in which they were cooked
4. Add raw carrots cut into small pieces, that have been previously soaked in salt water and washed very well
5. Add quarters of raw onion, blanched
6. Let it all boil until it thickens as desired
