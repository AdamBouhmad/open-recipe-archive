## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ham strips
- Cooked egg quarters
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Kill the turkey and immediately debone it
2. Extend the meat on a napkin spread with lard, salt, and pepper
3. Place strips of ham and quarters of cooked egg on top
4. Add another dusting of salt and pepper
5. Roll the meat very tightly in the napkin
6. Tie and cook
7. When cooked, remove from the napkin, slice, and dress with oil and vinegar
