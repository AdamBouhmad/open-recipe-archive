## Ingredients

- guayabas (guavas)
- membrillo (quince)
- azúcar (sugar)
- canela molida (ground cinnamon)

## Directions

1. Cook the guavas and when they are done and have released their boil, remove and sieve through a fine cloth
2. Also cook quince and sieve it, then mix in the proportion of one pound of guava, half a pound of quince and one and a half pounds of well-crushed sugar
3. Place everything in a pan, let it boil over low heat, and when it reaches the proper point, pour into glasses that will be sun-dried for three or four days
4. One can add half an ounce of ground cinnamon for each arroba of sweet before putting it in the vessels and small boxes
