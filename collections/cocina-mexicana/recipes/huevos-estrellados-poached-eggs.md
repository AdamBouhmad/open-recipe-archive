## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Vinegar
- Fine salt
- Oil
- Cilantro (toasted and ground)

## Directions

1. Put a pan on the fire and add a little water
2. Add an equal amount of vinegar and season with fine salt
3. When boiling, carefully break the eggs into the pan, only as many as will fit
4. Take care that they do not join together
5. Immediately with a clean stick, gather the whites around the yolk so they do not spread too much, placing the white on top of the yolk
6. Once the top white is set, remove them without breaking
7. Serve allowing diners to season them with oil, vinegar, salt powder, and toasted cilantro, or any other sauce or seasoning prepared for this purpose
