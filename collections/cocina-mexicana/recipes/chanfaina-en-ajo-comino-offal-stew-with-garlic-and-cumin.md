## Ingredients

- Offal
- Abundant lard
- Three mulato chiles
- Three cloves of garlic
- A little bit of cumin
- Water
- Salt
- Fried bread slices
- Onion slices
- Radish slices
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Slice the offal raw and fry it in abundant lard.
2. Soak three mulato chiles and grind them with three cloves of garlic and a little bit of cumin.
3. Add this mixture to the same pot where the offal is cooking, adding also a little water and salt.
4. Leave it on the heat until the sauce thickens.
5. To serve, garnish with slices of fried bread, onion, and radish and lettuce.
