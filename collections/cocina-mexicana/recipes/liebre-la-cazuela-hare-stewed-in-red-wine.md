## Ingredients

- hare
- butter
- flour
- red wine
- water
- salt
- pepper
- bacon
- small onions

## Directions

1. After removing the skin and emptying the hare, cut it into pieces; if it has blood set it aside.
2. Melt butter in the pot and place over heat, adding a handful of flour; when the mixture is made, add the hare, wetting it with sufficient red wine and half water.
3. Season with salt and pepper; also add small square pieces of bacon and small onions.
4. Take care to skim and degrease it; when the hare is cooked place it on a platter.
5. If the sauce is too thin, reduce it, thickening it with the blood of the hare and its liver, pounded together, using it as ordinary sauces.
