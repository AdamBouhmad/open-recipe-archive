## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- one pound of finest wheat flour
- three and a half pounds of sifted sugar
- one pound of melted butter
- one tablespoon of brandy
- powdered sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately
2. Join them together and mix with the finest wheat flour and sifted sugar
3. Continue beating everything and once well incorporated, add the melted butter with all impurities removed and skimmed
4. Beat everything again, and once well beaten, add one tablespoon of brandy
5. Beat one final time perfectly
6. Pour into small dishes buttered with butter
7. Place in the oven, which should not be very hot
8. Sprinkle powdered sugar and cinnamon powder on top
