## Ingredients

- Toasted bread slices, well trimmed and good color
- Boiling broth

## Directions

1. Place sufficient toasted bread slices in a soup tureen
2. Pour a little boiling broth over them and let soak until serving time
3. Add the necessary broth so the bread floats easily
