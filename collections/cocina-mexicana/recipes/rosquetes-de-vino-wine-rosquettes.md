## Ingredients

- one pound of melted butter
- flour
- one-third of a cuartillo of white wine
- a little molasses
- salt
- powdered sugar to taste
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of melted butter with the flour it can moisten
2. Add one-third of a cuartillo of white wine, a little molasses, salt dissolved in warm water, powdered sugar to taste, and four egg yolks
3. Knead everything
4. Form rosquettes and bake in a not-too-hot oven
