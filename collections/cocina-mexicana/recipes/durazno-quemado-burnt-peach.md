## Ingredients

- peaches
- apples
- sugar
- ground almonds
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel peaches and apples, remove the pit from the peaches
2. cook them, grind them and sift through a cloth
3. use two pounds peach, one pound apple, four pounds sugar made into high-point syrup, half pound ground almonds, and eight egg yolks mixed with a little water
4. stir everything and bring to a boil
5. cook until it reaches cajeta point
6. pour into a platter
7. sprinkle powdered sugar and cinnamon on top
8. stud with split almonds
9. place a comal with heat on top to brown it
