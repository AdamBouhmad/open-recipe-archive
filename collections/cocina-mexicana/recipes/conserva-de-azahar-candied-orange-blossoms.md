## Ingredients

- Orange blossoms
- Sugar, half pound for each pound of orange blossoms
- Honey, virgin, two quartillos for each pound of orange blossoms

## Directions

1. Remove the petals from the orange blossoms, weigh them, and leave them in water overnight
2. The next day, boil in a pan with water until tender
3. Remove and place in fresh water, wash in three or four waters, squeeze well between the hands to release the bitterness, and pound gently in a mortar so as not to break the petals
4. Add gradually to water, and when everything is wet, wash in the same water, squeeze with the hands, and separate each petal individually
5. Make a syrup with half pound of sugar for each pound of orange blossoms; clarify it, and before it reaches stage, allow it to cool and mix immediately with the water from the orange blossoms and the prepared flowers as explained
6. Return to heat to boil everything until the sugar is absorbed into the orange blossoms, then add for each pound of these two quartillos of well-skimmed and cold virgin honey
7. Allow everything to boil until it reaches the proper stage for pouring into jars or vessels for storage
