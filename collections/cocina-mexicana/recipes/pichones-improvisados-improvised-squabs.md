## Ingredients

- young squabs
- melted ham
- butter
- oil
- lemon slices
- Champagne wine
- garlic heads
- mixed herbs (bouquet)
- ham slice
- ham pieces for dicing
- sauce as desired

## Directions

1. Fry young squabs in melted ham, butter, half a glass of oil, two slices of lemon, a glass of Champagne wine, three or four heads of garlic, a mixed herb bundle and a slice of ham
2. Separate some pieces of ham and cut into dice and poach in boiling water
3. Cook over high heat, stirring continuously for a quarter hour
4. Add slices of ham and let boil over gentle heat as if cooking on coals
5. Serve with sauce as desired
