## Ingredients

- Milk
- Rice
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. According to the quantity of milk, wash a little rice and grind it thoroughly and strain with the milk
2. Add sugar to taste
3. Gradually add egg yolks until it becomes yellow
4. Put on heat and when it reaches medium point add a little orange blossom water
5. Leave on heat until it reaches the proper point, which is when it is poured on a plate and rises up
