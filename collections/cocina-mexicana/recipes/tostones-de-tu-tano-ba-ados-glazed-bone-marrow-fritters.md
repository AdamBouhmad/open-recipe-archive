## Ingredients

- pastry cream equivalent to two eggs
- four ounces of bone marrow
- some orange blossom leaves
- caramelized sugar
- bitter almond biscuits

## Directions

1. Make a paste by pounding pastry cream, bone marrow, orange blossom leaves, caramelized sugar, and bitter almond biscuits together in a mortar
2. Fill the tostones with this mixture
3. Prepare them as all the other types as stated in the previous articles
