## Ingredients

- pulp cut into small pieces
- tablespoon of lard
- ham slices
- two or three tomatoes, diced
- four bunches of onion in sections
- two heads of garlic, crushed with skin
- oregano
- thyme
- bay leaves
- Mexican vinegar
- water
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- red wine
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Wash the meat pieces and put in a pot with lard, ham slices, diced tomatoes, onion in sections, crushed garlic heads with skin, oregano, thyme and bay leaves
2. Fry everything very well
3. Add a little Mexican vinegar and water
4. Grind cloves, pepper and cinnamon and add them, making sure the broth becomes thick
5. When serving, add red wine and garnish with olives and chiles
