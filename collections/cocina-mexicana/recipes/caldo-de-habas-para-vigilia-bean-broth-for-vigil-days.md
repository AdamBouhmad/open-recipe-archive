## Ingredients

- Dried beans
- Raw lard
- Garlic heads
- Mint leaves
- Cumin
- Saffron
- Fine salt
- Eating oil

## Directions

1. Soak the beans in cold water the day before.
2. The next day, peel them, and clean them.
3. Cook them in cold water without salt.
4. When they have broken down from boiling, add raw lard, whole garlic heads, mint leaves, ground cumin and saffron.
5. When they have thickened, remove from heat.
6. When serving, season to taste with fine salt and pour a little eating oil over each cup.
