## Ingredients

- Requeson (curdled milk cheese)
- Sugar
- Beaten eggs
- Ground cinnamon
- Lard
- Rosquete dough for base and lattice

## Directions

1. Pass the desired amount of requeson through a metate
2. Mix with necessary sugar and beaten eggs
3. Knead with ground cinnamon until everything is the color of cinnamon
4. Grease a cake pan or cooking pot with lard
5. Place a layer of rosquete dough in the bottom
6. Add the filling
7. Decorate the top with a lattice of the same dough used for the bottom
8. Bake in a not-very-hot oven
