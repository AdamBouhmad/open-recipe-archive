## Ingredients

- Capers
- Cooked chorizo
- Oregano
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Chopped parsley
- Clove
- Pepper
- Ground cumin
- Chicken broth
- Olives
- Chiles

## Directions

1. Grind the capers together with cooked chorizo
2. Fry the ground mixture
3. Add oregano, vinegar, oil, chopped parsley, clove, pepper, and ground cumin
4. Put in the chicken with its broth
5. Let everything simmer until the broth is seasoned and somewhat thick
6. Garnish with olives and chiles when serving
