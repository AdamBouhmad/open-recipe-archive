## Ingredients

- veal loin or pulp
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- salt
- wine
- vinegar
- cooked chorizos
- cooked ham
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the loin or veal pulp into thin slices.
2. Rub with ground clove, pepper, cinnamon, and salt, and sprinkle with wine and vinegar.
3. Prepare cooked and cleaned chorizos, cooked ham, sliced hard-boiled eggs, and plenty of chopped parsley.
4. Mix these ingredients and use to fill the roulettes.
5. When arranging, sprinkle again with the wine and vinegar and dust with the spices mentioned.
6. Tie very well with thread so they remain tight.
7. The next day, cook in salted water with wine, vinegar, and a tablespoon of lard.
8. Once cooked, remove from their broth if any remains, drain, and eat hot or keep to eat cold, lasting a week in good condition.
