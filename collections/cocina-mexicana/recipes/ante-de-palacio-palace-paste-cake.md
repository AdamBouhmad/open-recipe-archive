## Ingredients

- eggs
- sugar
- ground almonds
- sponge cake (mamón)

## Directions

1. Take fifty eggs and cook until hard
2. Sift the yolks through a hair sieve
3. Clarify one cuarta of sugar
4. Add one pound of ground almonds until the sugar reaches point
5. Remove all and add to syrup at point
6. Layer sponge cake and mixture
