## Ingredients

- Wide dried chile pepper (deveined)
- Dried cilantro (large portion)
- Lard
- Turkey broth
- Salt
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Turkey meat

## Directions

1. Toast the deveined wide chile pepper together with a large portion of dried cilantro
2. Grind both and fry in sufficient lard
3. Add the broth in which the turkey was cooked, with salt, cloves, and ground black pepper, and place the turkey in regular pieces
4. If the turkey is to be cooked in this sauce, fry it first in lard and use water instead of broth
