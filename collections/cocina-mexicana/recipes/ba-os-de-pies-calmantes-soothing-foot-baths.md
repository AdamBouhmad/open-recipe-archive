## Ingredients

- one pound bran
- a little mallow
- two or three handfuls mallow leaves
- a few handfuls pellitory

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of bran in a sufficient quantity of water with mallow, mallow leaves, and pellitory
2. Do not soak in this bath for a long time
