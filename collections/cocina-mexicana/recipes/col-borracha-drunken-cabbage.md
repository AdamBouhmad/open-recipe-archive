## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- loin slices
- chorizo
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- ground spices
- water
- wine
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- oregano

## Directions

1. Give a white cabbage one boil, let it cool
2. Insert slices of raw loin and chorizo between the leaves
3. Once filled, place it in a pot lined with lard
4. Add chopped tomato, onion and garlic with ground spices, water and double the amount of wine
5. Cover it very well and cook it
6. Garnish with chiles, olives, capers and sprinkled oregano
