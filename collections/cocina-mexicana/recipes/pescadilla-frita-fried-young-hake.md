## Ingredients

- Young hake (pescadilla)
- Flour
- Oil, butter, or lard

## Directions

1. Remove the scales, wash the fish, and clean it, leaving the liver.
2. Dry it, dust it with flour, and place it in very hot oil, butter, or lard.
3. Let it fry until it takes on a golden-brown color or becomes well browned.
