## Ingredients

- two parts parsley leaves
- one part radish leaves
- equal quantity of ripe tomatoes
- two garlic cloves
- salt
- chicken broth
- all spices except saffron
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- raisins
- small slices of ham
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Grind two parts parsley leaves and one part radish leaves with equal quantity of ripe tomatoes and two garlic cloves.
2. Fry this well.
3. Add the broth in which the chicken was cooked with necessary salt.
4. Place the chicken with all ground spices except saffron, sufficient vinegar, chiles, olives, capers, raisins, and small slices of ham.
5. When it has boiled and thickened reasonably, remove from heat and pour raw oil on top.
