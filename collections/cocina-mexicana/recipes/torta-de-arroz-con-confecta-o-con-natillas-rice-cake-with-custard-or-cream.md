## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- fresh butter
- sugar
- custard (optional)
- beaten eggs with more yolks than whites
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almonds
- pieces of candied citron
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well and spread it in the sun to dry
2. Cook it with milk, a little fresh butter and the necessary sugar
3. When well cooked and thick, remove from heat and leave in the same pot until the next day
4. If desired, mix with custard, which is very good though not necessary, and with beaten eggs having more yolks than whites, with raisins, pine nuts, almonds, pieces of candied citron and sesame seeds
5. Put everything in a baking pan or skillet coated with lard
6. Let it cook over two fires
