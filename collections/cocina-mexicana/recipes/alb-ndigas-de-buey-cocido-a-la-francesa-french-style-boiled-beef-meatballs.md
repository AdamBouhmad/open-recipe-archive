## Ingredients

- herbs
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- boiled beef

## Directions

1. Crush some fine herbs in butter with a little flour and a bit of broth, salt, pepper and nutmeg, letting it all boil until it thickens well
2. Mince cooked beef and mix with the said sauce, adding a little bread crumb to give it consistency
3. Form meatballs with this paste, roll in flour and fry
