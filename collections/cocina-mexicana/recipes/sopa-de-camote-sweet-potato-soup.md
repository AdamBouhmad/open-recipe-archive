## Ingredients

- 12 purple sweet potatoes
- 4 chicken breasts
- butter, size of two eggs
- 8 egg yolks, raw
- nutmeg
- 6 peppercorns

## Directions

1. Roast twelve purple sweet potatoes in embers until well cooked
2. Peel them, removing all hardness
3. Grind them and remove all fibers
4. Regrind with four chicken breasts
5. Mix the ground substance with a piece of butter the size of two eggs, eight raw egg yolks, a little nutmeg and six peppercorns, seasoning the mass with salt
6. When it sets on the spoon, remove it and pour onto slices of soft bread or crumbled cold bread
