## Ingredients

- panela honey
- whole roasted peanuts

## Directions

1. Let panela honey boil until it reaches the melcocha point
2. Add whole, clean, roasted peanuts and stir well with the syrup
3. Remove portions with a wooden spoon to form the brittle pieces
4. Cool on paper
