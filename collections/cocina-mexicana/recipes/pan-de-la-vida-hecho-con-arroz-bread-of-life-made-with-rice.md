## Ingredients

- four ounces of sifted rice flour
- four ounces of peeled almonds, finely ground
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- seven well-beaten egg whites
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix the sifted rice flour with the ground almonds, powdered sugar, and well-beaten egg whites
2. Stir everything together with a little water
3. Pour into greased glazed small dishes, not filling them too much because the dough rises when cooking
4. Place in the oven until golden
