## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- salt
- pepper
- ham
- whole peppers
- water
- vinegar
- Sherry wine
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- marjoram
- garlic
- oil
- vinegar
- tomatoes
- tender onions
- garlic

## Directions

1. Chop and grind veal pulp with salt and little pepper
2. On the same metate make a thick tortilla and add ham strips and whole peppers
3. Roll like a chorizo
4. Cook in a pot with water, vinegar and Sherry wine, bay leaf, thyme, oregano, marjoram and whole garlic cloves
5. Make sure it does not stick
6. When meat is cooked, let cool
7. Slice and dress with oil and vinegar or make a sauce of cooked tomatoes with tender whole onions and ground garlic, fry everything
