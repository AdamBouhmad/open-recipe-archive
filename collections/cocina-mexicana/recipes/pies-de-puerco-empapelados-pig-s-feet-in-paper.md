## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- butter
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- floret paper
- lard
- string beans
- oil
- garlic
- onion
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Split the feet lengthwise
2. Cook the day before with a piece of butter
3. The next day, wash with hot water and remove the long bone
4. Dry with a cloth and sprinkle with salt, ground clove and pepper, minced parsley and breadcrumbs
5. Take a sheet of floret paper and butter it
6. Place half a foot on each paper
7. Wrap like an empanada
8. Put on the grill with plenty of heat and turn until it crackles
9. Serve with the following salad: cooked string beans with strings removed and split lengthwise, dried and fried in oil with minced garlic cloves; once fried, remove from the pan and add minced onion, vinegar and salt
