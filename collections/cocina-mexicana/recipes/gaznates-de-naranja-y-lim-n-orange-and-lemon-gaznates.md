## Ingredients

- white tequesquite grain
- salt
- water
- half sour orange
- nine limes
- flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve a grain of white tequesquite and a little salt in very little water, squeeze half a sour orange and nine limes into it
2. Add all the flour needed so the dough can be extended with a rolling pin
3. Make the sheets, form and fill the gaznates as the previous ones
