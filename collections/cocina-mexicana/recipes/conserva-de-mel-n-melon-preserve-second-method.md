## Ingredients

- melon (neither ripe nor green, but large)
- water with very little lime
- clarified syrup (made with three libras of sugar)

## Directions

1. The melon should be neither ripe nor green, but large
2. Slice it, add the slices to water with very little lime, and place in clarified syrup made with three libras of sugar
3. Let it boil over slow heat until it thickens
