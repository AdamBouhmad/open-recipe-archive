## Ingredients

- ancho chile, deveined and toasted
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- peas
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare everything as for the previous adobo
2. Instead of adding meat, add the peas
3. Add oil and vinegar
