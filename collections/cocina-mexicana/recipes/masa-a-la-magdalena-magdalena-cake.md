## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- a little orange-blossom water
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Place flour, sugar, butter, orange-blossom water, and eggs in a bowl
2. Mix everything together and pour into a buttered mold
3. Bake in a gently heated oven
