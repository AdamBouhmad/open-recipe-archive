## Ingredients

- 8 pounds sugar
- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound camote
- 1 pound grated coconut
- 1 quarter of custard (natillas)
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with eight pounds of sugar
2. Add one pound of almonds, another of camote, all ground together, and another of grated coconut
3. Let it boil well
4. Then add a quarter of custard and twelve egg yolks, stirring the paste constantly as they are added so that they do not curdle
5. When it reaches the point of cajeta, empty it onto a single platter or over slices of sponge cake
