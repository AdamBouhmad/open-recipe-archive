## Ingredients

- quails
- veal (a slice)
- ham (a slice)
- carrots
- onion
- aromatic herbs (a bunch)
- bacon (slices)
- paper (greased)
- peas (cooked)
- ham

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot with a slice of veal and another of ham, carrots, onion and a bunch of aromatic herbs.
2. Cover immediately with slices of bacon and greased paper.
3. Put fire above and below.
4. After removing the broth, add cooked peas with ham.
