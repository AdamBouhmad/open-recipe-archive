## Ingredients

- cooked fat beans
- grated cheese
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. After cooking the beans, not so soft that they fall apart, open them in half with a knife.
2. Place a little grated cheese between the two halves, and coating with beaten egg, fry them.
3. Prepare them afterwards as the Portuguese style (see Frijoles a la Portuguesa) or in a xitomate broth.
