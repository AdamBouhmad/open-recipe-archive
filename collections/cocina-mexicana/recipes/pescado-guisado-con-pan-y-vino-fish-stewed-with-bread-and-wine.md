## Ingredients

- all seasonings, finely chopped
- parsley (abundant), finely chopped
- bread, toasted in lard
- pepper, ground
- cloves, ground
- bay leaves
- wine (abundant)
- oil (little)

## Directions

1. Chop all seasonings and abundant parsley very fine
2. Fry well
3. Add bread toasted in lard, ground pepper and cloves, bay leaves, abundant wine and little oil
4. Stew together
