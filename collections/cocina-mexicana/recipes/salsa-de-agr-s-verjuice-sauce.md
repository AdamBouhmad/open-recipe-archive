## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 tablespoons verjuice
- same amount of strained broth or broth from the pot
- salt
- pepper
- chopped shallot

## Directions

1. Put in a pot 2 or 3 tablespoons of verjuice, another amount of strained broth or broth from the pot, salt, pepper and chopped shallot, and it should remain clear
2. Heat and serve for grilled roasts
