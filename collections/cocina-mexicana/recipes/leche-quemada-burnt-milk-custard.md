## Ingredients

- one cup of ground and sifted starch
- eight quartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- one small cinnamon stick
- one lemon zest, well washed
- sugar to taste
- crushed sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Mix the ground and sifted starch with the eight quartillos of milk
2. Divide the entire mixture into two halves
3. In one half, beat the twelve egg yolks well
4. Add the other half of the milk and beat everything together
5. Strain the mixture
6. Add a small cinnamon stick and a washed lemon zest
7. Sweeten to taste
8. Heat to the desired consistency, some preferring it thick enough to cut with a knife, others preferring it looser
9. Pour into a platter and let cool completely
10. Sprinkle crushed sugar on top and burn it with a hot iron plate or a red-hot iron paddle
11. Alternatively, divide the platter into four parts: burn one part as described, cover two others, one with ground cinnamon and another with dragée, leaving the last in its natural color
