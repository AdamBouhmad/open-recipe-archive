## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- bacon finely sliced
- parsley
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- chiles
- garlic finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg grated
- oil
- oil-soaked paper

## Directions

1. Prepare a rack of veal well
2. Lard the tenderloin with finely sliced bacon
3. Marinate for two or three hours with parsley, onion, thyme, bay leaf, finely chopped chiles and garlic, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg and a little oil
4. When it has taken on the flavor, place on the spit and pour over all the marinade, which will be held with well-greased paper
5. Place over low heat; afterwards remove the paper, scraping off the herbs that may have stuck to it, and the same from the meat, and these will be placed in a saucepan
