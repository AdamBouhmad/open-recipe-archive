## Ingredients

- four pounds of ground corn flour cacahuazintle tamised
- half a pound of lard
- six egg yolks
- a little salt
- milk in place of water

## Directions

1. Put four pounds of ground corn flour cacahuazintle tamised into a pot, half a pound of lard, six egg yolks, a little salt, and milk in place of water
2. Beat all with a new spoon until it forms blisters
3. Make tamales, filling them with thick almond milk or any other paste
4. Cook them with the steam of water over grass or tapestle, arranged so that the water does not touch the tamales and the pot covered
5. When cooked, slice them, coat with egg, fry and arrange as in the previous article
