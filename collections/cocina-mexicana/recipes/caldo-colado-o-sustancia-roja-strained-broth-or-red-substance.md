## Ingredients

- small pieces of fat ham and beef pulp in proportion to the desired amount, one pound per quartillo
- two or three parsnips

## Directions

1. Place the ham and beef pieces in a cooking pan, well covered, over low heat to allow time for cooking
2. Pass the strained broth through a sieve or tamis
3. Keep the red substance for when needed
4. It should be well made if it is not too thick nor too watery, and if it has a beautiful cinnamon color
5. This broth can be made with all kinds of meats, but it is always necessary to add beef
