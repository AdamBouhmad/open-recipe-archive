## Ingredients

- Brown sugar
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Cilantro
- Sesame seeds
- Pine nuts
- Almonds
- Biscuit

## Directions

1. Make a honey from brown sugar and when ready, remove from heat
2. Add ground clove, cinnamon and roasted cilantro
3. Add roasted sesame seeds, pine nuts and almonds
4. Add crumbled biscuit and allow it to soak for about an hour
