## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- two onions
- parsley
- four to six garlic cloves
- pepper
- six peppercorns ground
- salt
- juice of three lemons or the juice of two oranges

## Directions

1. Heat a pan over the fire with half a pound of butter
2. When at good temperature, add two onions, a little parsley, and four to six garlic cloves, all finely minced
3. Add pepper and six ground peppercorns
4. Season with salt
5. Add the juice of three lemons or the juice of two oranges
6. This is a sauce of exquisite taste for roasted meats, and particularly good for basting roasted and toasted suckling pig
