## Ingredients

- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Dried fava beans, cooked and broken down
- Pork, cooked
- Broth in which the pork was cooked
- Cumin, ground
- Saffron, ground
- Spearmint
- Fresh cilantro
- Garlic head, whole (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the chopped garlic, onions, and tomatoes
2. Once fried, add the cooked and broken down fava beans
3. Add the cooked pork and the broth in which it was cooked
4. Add a few ground cumin and saffron, a sprig of spearmint, two sprigs of fresh cilantro, and if desired, another whole garlic head
5. Allow everything to boil until it reaches complete seasoning and the stew acquires the proper consistency
