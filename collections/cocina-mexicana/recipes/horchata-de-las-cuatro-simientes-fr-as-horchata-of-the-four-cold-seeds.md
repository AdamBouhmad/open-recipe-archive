## Ingredients

- melon seeds
- watermelon seeds
- squash seeds
- cucumber seeds
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind together the seeds of melon, watermelon, squash and cucumber
2. Strain them
3. Dissolve with water
4. Sweeten with sugar
