## Ingredients

- Four large mameyes
- A little cold bread
- Twelve well-beaten eggs
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Lard
- Syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Grind four large mameyes
2. Knead with a little cold bread
3. Mix with twelve well-beaten eggs
4. Knead everything together
5. Add sugar, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
6. Pour into a pan greased with lard
7. Let it set over two fires
8. Divide into pieces and place in syrup
