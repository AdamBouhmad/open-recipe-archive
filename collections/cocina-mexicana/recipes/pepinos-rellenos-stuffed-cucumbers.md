## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- picadillo (minced meat filling)
- turnip
- ham slices
- pot broth or tomato broth
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Clean the cucumbers and cut off the top
2. Hollow them out to fill with picadillo or other filling
3. Cover the opening with a turnip cut to size
4. Cover a pot with ham slices
5. Place the cucumbers on top
6. Pour pot broth or tomato broth over them, seasoned with necessary fine salt
7. Cook over low heat
8. When cooked, if there is too much broth, remove some of it, allowing the remaining broth to thicken over very low heat so that when served at the table they are bathed in it
