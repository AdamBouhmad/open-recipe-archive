## Ingredients

- mojarra fish
- flour
- oil
- lard
- tomatoes
- capers
- fresh parsley
- garlic
- salt
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean and wash them, pat dry whole and roll in flour for frying
2. Fry in oil and lard
3. Roast three or four tomatoes and grind with capers, fresh parsley, a whole peeled garlic clove and necessary salt
4. Place the mojarra on a platter and bathe with this sauce
5. Add chiles, olives, capers and plenty of oil and cook over low heat
