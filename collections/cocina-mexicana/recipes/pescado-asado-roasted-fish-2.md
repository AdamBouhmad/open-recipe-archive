## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Lemon
- Paper
- Lard
- Parsley

## Directions

1. After cleaning, make incisions from the head to the tail
2. Coat well inside and out with ground salt and pepper and a good quantity of oil in which a lemon has been squeezed
3. Once the fish is well coated, coat a paper with lard and oil
4. Add some chopped parsley to it, wrap the fish well, and let it cook over two gentle fires
