## Ingredients

- Plums
- Lemon covered with rind, sliced
- Puff pastry dough
- Beaten egg for glazing
- Sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Peel the plums and remove their pits
2. Line a pot with puff pastry dough
3. Place the plums on top, mixed with slices of covered lemon
4. Cover with another sheet of dough, glaze with beaten egg
5. Put the cake in the oven to bake
6. When done, coat in the ordinary way with sugar, decorate, and serve hot
