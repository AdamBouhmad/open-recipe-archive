## Ingredients

- Three pounds of sugar
- Three pitahayas

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup made with three pounds of sugar and bring it to the final point
2. Mix in three pitahayas that have been dissolved without water, returning to the fire so it recovers the point it had
3. It is known to be ready when a little dropped in water forms a ball that is not too hard and can be picked up
4. Then empty while still hot onto a small board, and cut with a little help by hand
