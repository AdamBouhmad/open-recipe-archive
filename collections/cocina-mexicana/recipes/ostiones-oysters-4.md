## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove oysters from their shells and give them a boil in the same water; remove from heat.
2. Toss them without boiling with a piece of lard, parsley, broken pepper, and lemon juice.
3. Place three or four in each shell, which must be well washed.
4. Put on grills and remove as soon as they have boiled.
