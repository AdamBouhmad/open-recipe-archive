## Ingredients

- almonds with shells
- syrup at strong flower point

## Directions

1. Place the almonds in a large pot without removing the shells, with syrup at strong flower point.
2. Stir vigorously with a wooden ladle until the syrup has completely adhered to the almonds and has acquired a brown color.
3. This operation requires strong heat to execute well.
