## Ingredients

- Beef tongue, larded
- Lard
- Fried bread
- Garlic
- Wine
- Cloves
- Black pepper, ground
- Small onions, cooked

## Directions

1. Lard the tongue as in the previous recipe
2. Partially cook it in lard and add fried and ground bread with garlic
3. Once the preparation is fried, add wine, cloves, and ground pepper along with cooked small onions
4. Let the broth reduce until it becomes thicker than in the preceding recipe
