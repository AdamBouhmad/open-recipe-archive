## Ingredients

- White cabbage
- Salt
- Garlic
- Onion
- Lard
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Tomatoes
- Olives
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage one day before making the salad and soak it after washing in two or three waters
2. The next day, wash it again and cook it with very little salt and a crushed garlic head
3. Once cooked, wash it well again so no odor from the cabbage or garlic remains
4. Chop onion finely and fry it in lard, and once fried add parsley
5. Press the cabbage well so no water remains and fry it with the onion and parsley
6. Once fried, add salt, vinegar, oil, cut tomatoes, olives and oregano in sprigs, then serve
