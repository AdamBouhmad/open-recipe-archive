## Ingredients

- beef ears
- cooked filling
- breadcrumbs
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the ears and cook them the same as in the previous article
2. Fill the ears with cooked filling, taking care to unite and press it well
3. Roll in breadcrumbs
4. Coat with beaten egg
5. Fry and serve with a liquid sauce on top
