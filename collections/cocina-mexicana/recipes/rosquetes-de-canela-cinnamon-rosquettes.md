## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks as for huevos reales (royal eggs)
2. Mix with the flour that the yolks can moisten
3. Knead everything together
4. Form rosquettes and fry them in hot butter as they are formed
5. Remove and let drain
6. Dip in syrup and cover with powdered sugar and cinnamon
