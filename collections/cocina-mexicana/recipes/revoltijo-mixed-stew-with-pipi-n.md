## Ingredients

- white beans
- peas
- plantains
- squash
- bean sprouts
- sesame
- shrimp
- dried wide chile
- egg

## Directions

1. Cook white beans, peas, plantains, squash and bean sprouts
2. Make pipián from toasted sesame and fry small pieces of shrimp, soaked and refreshed dried wide chile and fry when making the pipián
3. Fry the vegetables separately then combine everything with the pipián
4. Mix in beaten egg so you do not recognize the egg and it remains compact
