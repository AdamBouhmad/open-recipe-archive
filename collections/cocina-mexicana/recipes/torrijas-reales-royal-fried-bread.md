## Ingredients

- ten egg yolks
- lard
- orange flower water
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame seeds
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat ten egg yolks until they are well risen
2. Pour into a large cup greased with lard
3. Cover with a comal with coals and place over the steam of a boiling pot
4. Test with a straw; if it comes out dry, the yolks are cooked
5. Turn the cup over onto a napkin and cut into slices
6. Place in syrup over heat to give it one boil
7. Sprinkle with orange flower water and decorate with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, toasted sesame seeds and ground cinnamon
