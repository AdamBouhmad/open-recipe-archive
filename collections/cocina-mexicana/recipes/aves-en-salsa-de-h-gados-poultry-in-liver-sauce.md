## Ingredients

- poultry livers
- onions, cooked
- garlic, peeled
- tomatoes, cooked and peeled
- celery
- pepper
- meat broth
- fine sauce
- oil

## Directions

1. Grind cooked poultry livers with cooked onions, peeled garlic, cooked and peeled tomatoes, celery and pepper
2. Add meat broth to this mixture to thin it
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add the necessary fine sauce
5. Season with poultry meat over low heat, after it has been fried a little in oil
