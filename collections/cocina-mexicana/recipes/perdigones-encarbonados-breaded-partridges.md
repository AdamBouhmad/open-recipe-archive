## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Butter
- Earthnut truffles
- Salt
- Pepper
- Gelatin

## Directions

1. Divide the partridges in half
2. Fold the legs inward, making the extremity come out through a hole opened near the kidneys, so that each partridge half has the appearance of a small rib with the bone formed by the leg
3. Dip these halves in melted butter
4. Roll and cover them with earthnut truffles chopped like breadcrumbs, seasoning with salt and pepper
5. Put on the grill
6. Serve over half-set gelatin
