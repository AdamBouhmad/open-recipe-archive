## Ingredients

- rice cooked in milk
- sugar
- equal parts almonds and walnuts
- eggs
- butter
- raisins
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Take some rice cooked in milk, well sweetened and so thick that it forms a lump or ball
2. Grind equal amounts of almonds and walnuts
3. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately according to the quantity of cake desired
4. Mix into the rice one pound of butter or less according to the quantity of cake, the almond and walnut with half of the beaten egg, reserving the other half to put a little in the pan which will already be well buttered
5. Pour the rice into it and place the remaining egg on top
6. Cook the cake over two fires or in the oven
7. When cold, boil it in syrup and decorate with raisins and pine nuts
