## Ingredients

- pears in syrup conserve
- almond paste or custard or whipped cheese curds with sugar and cinnamon
- jelly

## Directions

1. Remove the pears from the syrup in which they were preserved and drain well
2. Remove the core and fill them with almond paste or custard or cheese curds beaten with sugar and cinnamon
3. Place them on a platter and cover with jelly
