## Ingredients

- carp
- flour
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Clean, empty and wash the carp
2. Make incisions or small cuts on the sides
3. Open it along the back and remove the roe and soft roe or eggs to fry separately
4. Coat well with flour and fry until golden
