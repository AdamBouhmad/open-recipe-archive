## Ingredients

- manteca
- aceite
- xitomates asados mondados y molidos
- cebolla picada
- sal
- agua
- tomillo
- orégano
- perejil picado
- yemas de huevo duro
- tostadas de pan
- alcaparras
- huevo duro

## Directions

1. Fry roasted, peeled and ground tomatoes mixed with manteca and oil, and chopped onion
2. Season everything with salt and add the necessary water
3. When this broth is boiling, add thyme, oregano and chopped parsley, two hard-boiled egg yolks crushed and a little more oil
4. Arrange the bread toasts in a pot and soak them with the broth
5. Having reduced it so the soup is loose, garnish with capers and slices of hard-boiled egg, adding a little more oil when serving
