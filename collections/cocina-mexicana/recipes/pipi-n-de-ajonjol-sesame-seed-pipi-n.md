## Ingredients

- sesame seeds
- fried toasts
- lard
- salt
- fruits, roots, herbs or meats

## Directions

1. After cleaning, toast the sesame seeds
2. Grind with fried toasts
3. Fry in lard with salt
4. Incorporate the fruits, roots, herbs or meats to be cooked in it
5. Proceed as in the previous recipes until finished
