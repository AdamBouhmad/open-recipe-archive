## Ingredients

- beets
- almonds
- coconut
- egg yolks
- syrup
- orange blossom water
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the beets
2. Grind almonds separately and coconut separately
3. Dissolve soft egg yolks in the syrup
4. Make each paste separately in good syrup at the proper point, with orange blossom water
5. Layer one layer of sponge cake and another of each paste
6. The last layer should be soft eggs with cinnamon on top
