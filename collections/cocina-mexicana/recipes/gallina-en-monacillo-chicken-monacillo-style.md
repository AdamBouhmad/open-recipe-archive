## Ingredients

- Beets
- Roasted tomatoes
- Chicken broth
- Salt
- Plantain slices
- Apple slices
- Sweet potato slices
- Pineapple slices
- All spices except cumin and saffron
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Toasted sesame seed

## Directions

1. Cook beets, and when cooked, peel and slice them
2. Grind them with some roasted tomatoes and fry
3. Add the chicken broth and season with salt
4. Add slices of plantain, apple, sweet potato and pineapple
5. Add the chicken in pieces
6. Grind all spices except cumin and saffron with a little vinegar and sugar, and add to the stew
7. Let it boil until it thickens properly
8. Serve with toasted sesame seed on top
