## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- ham pieces
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- olives

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and fry in butter with ham pieces
2. Remove the chicken and ham, brown a little flour, add one cup of broth, salt, pepper, and parsley
3. Pit the olives and wash with hot water
4. Return the chicken and ham to the sauce and add the olives five minutes before serving
