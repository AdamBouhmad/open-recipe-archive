## Ingredients

- Half arroba of superior white sugar
- Water equal in quartillos to the pounds of sugar
- Six eggs
- Three egg whites
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Oil

## Directions

1. Beat half a dozen eggs
2. Remove some of the water and in the remaining add the sugar, placing it on the heat until it begins to cook, adding the water that was previously set aside
3. When it has boiled three times and has the point of caramel, strain through cloth to another pan, placing it on heat until it reaches the regular string point
4. Beat three egg whites with sugar, stirring until it cools, adding a little lemon juice
5. Then place the sugar on the heat to take point, adding two or three drops of oil
6. Have some of the bath prepared, removing it from heat and beating it much
7. Place on a table some white sticks, removing it from the papers and cutting it with a small saw until finished
