## Ingredients

- 2 orange or lime peels, crushed
- 2 cuartillos (approximately 1 liter) water
- 6 ounces sugar
- juice of the oranges or limes

## Directions

1. Crush the peels of 2 oranges or limes in 2 cuartillos of water
2. Express the juice and mix everything with 6 ounces of sugar
3. Let rest for half an hour and strain through a cloth or fine napkin
4. The same method is used for limes
