## Ingredients

- Two or three onions, cut
- A little oil
- String beans, blanched and cooked
- Parsley, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut two or three onions and place them in a frying pan over the fire with a little oil.
2. When they begin to brown, add the string beans already blanched and cooked, letting them fry with the onion.
3. Chop parsley and some onions over the top and season with salt and pepper.
4. After two turns of the pan they will be ready, and then empty and arrange them on a plate.
5. Pour a splash of vinegar into the same pan and sprinkle the hot beans with it.
