## Ingredients

- pure milk without water
- sugar
- cilantro
- cinnamon stick
- chicken gizzards, ground
- eight gizzards for two cuartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Add adequate sweetness, cilantro and a cinnamon stick to pure milk
2. Bring to a boil until well cooked and thick
3. Add ground chicken gizzards in the proportion of eight for two cuartillos of milk and strain
4. Pour into the same platter in which it will be served
5. Stir everything and to set it well, place over the steam of a pot, covering with a bit of embers on top
