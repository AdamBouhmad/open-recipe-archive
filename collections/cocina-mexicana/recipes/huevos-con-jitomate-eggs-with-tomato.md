## Ingredients

- lard or oil
- tomatoes, finely chopped
- onion, chopped
- eggs, beaten
- ham, in pieces

## Directions

1. Fry the lard or oil first, then the finely chopped tomatoes, and if desired, first a little chopped onion
2. Add the perfectly beaten eggs and stir with the tomato
3. Can be served as an omelet, and if there are pieces of ham, they will form a good breakfast dish
