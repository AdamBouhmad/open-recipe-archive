## Ingredients

- squabs
- dried ancho chile, soaked and ground
- garlic
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- oregano
- lard
- flour
- eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs and divide them into quarters
2. Place them in a marinade made with ground ancho chile, garlic and cumin, with salt, vinegar and oregano
3. Let marinate until the next day
4. Add very little lard to the marinade and cook the squab quarters in it
5. When cooked, remove and let drain
6. Roll in flour, coat with beaten egg and fry in lard
7. Reduce the marinade separately
8. When the squabs are fried and arranged on a plate, pour the reduced marinade over them
