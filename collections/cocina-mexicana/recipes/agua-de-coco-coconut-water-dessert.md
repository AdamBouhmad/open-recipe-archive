## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of coconut water
- one cuartillo of syrup at half-point
- twelve egg yolks
- one handful of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Stir together one cuartillo of coconut water with another of syrup at half-point
2. Mix in twelve egg yolks and one handful of ground almonds
3. Place over heat, stirring occasionally, until the bottom of the pan becomes visible when stirred
