## Ingredients

- bread
- white sugar
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut equal slices of bread
2. Sprinkle them with a little white sugar and carefully place them on a grill to toast over low heat
3. When they are golden, place them on a plate or soup tureen, pouring hot milk with a grain of salt over them
