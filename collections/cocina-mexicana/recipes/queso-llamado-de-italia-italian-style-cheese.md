## Ingredients

- three pounds of beef or pork liver
- two pounds of lard
- half pound of fat
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- parsley
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- sage
- garlic
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Mince together three pounds of beef or pork liver, two pounds of lard and half pound of fat, seasoning with salt, pepper, and spices, and mix everything with parsley, onion, thyme, bay leaf, sage and minced garlic
2. Cover the bottom of a pot or mold with thin slices of ham and place on top a layer three fingers in height of the minced mixture, which is covered with ham slices and continue placing layers of filling or minced mixture in this order until the mold is full
3. Finally cover with ham slices and place in the oven where it will be left for three hours to cook
4. Then place to cool, and reheat slightly so that the cheese can be removed whole
5. Dress and garnish, if desired, with butter, aspic, fine herbs, hard-boiled egg yolks, etc., serving as a side dish
