## Ingredients

- eight or nine pears
- sugar powder
- four or five egg whites
- one tablespoon of rose water or orange blossom water
- aspic
- cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook eight or nine pears in boiling water until tender
2. Cool the pears and pass them through a sieve
3. Mix with good sugar powder
4. Beat four or five egg whites with one tablespoon of rose water or orange blossom water
5. Gradually mix the egg whites with the pears while continuing to beat until all becomes very light
6. Arrange this mousse or foam on a beautiful, thick, and tall aspic placed at the bottom of the plate, or on some cream (see vanilla cream, page 236)
