## Ingredients

- pork head
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a pork head with four feet with salt, an onion head, parsley and garlic
2. At half cooked, remove the bones and cut into small pieces
3. Mix with salt and pepper, finely chopped onion and stir everything together
4. Grease a mold with lard, add the meat and press it
5. The next day remove from the mold and place on a platter
