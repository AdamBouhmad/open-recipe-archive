## Ingredients

- ribs
- bacon cut into thin slices
- lard
- egg yolks
- parsley
- fresh garlic
- salt
- green chile
- acid

## Directions

1. Cook two or more good ribs in a saucepan with bacon cut into very thin slices and lard
2. After some time, transfer to another saucepan so they release the fat
3. When cooked, place on a plate
4. In the broth they have left, add a mixture of egg yolks, finely chopped parsley, fresh garlic, salt, a little green chile if the bacon is very fresh, and any acid
5. After this sauce has been on the heat for a while, pour it over the ribs
