## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Bay leaves
- Garlic
- Spices
- Brine
- Oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning, fry the partridges in oil
2. When half fried, add them to vinegar with bay leaves, crushed garlic and spices
3. Place in a vessel and add brine and oil on top
4. Cover hermetically and place in a cool location
