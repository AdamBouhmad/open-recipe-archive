## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 2 quartillos of milk
- ½ pound of refined butter
- optional: ½ pound of chocolate

## Directions

1. Mix 10 egg yolks, 2 quartillos of milk, and ½ pound of refined butter together perfectly
2. Place over low heat, stirring constantly without stopping until it reaches a regular thickness
3. Retire immediately and after a short time, freeze
4. If using chocolate, cook it with water first, then mix it in
