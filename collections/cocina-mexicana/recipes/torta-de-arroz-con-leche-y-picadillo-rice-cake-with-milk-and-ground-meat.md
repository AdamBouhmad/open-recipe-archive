## Ingredients

- one pound cleaned and soaked rice
- six handfuls of milk
- necessary sugar
- one and a half pounds of ground pork loin
- lard
- garlic cloves
- water
- ground cinnamon
- ground clove
- whole clove covered
- pine nuts
- pitted raisins
- almonds
- pounded biscuit

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of clean soaked rice and cook it with six handfuls of strained milk with necessary sugar until it reaches the consistency of white pudding
2. Cook one and a half pounds of pork loin and then pound it again, then fry it in lard in which garlic cloves have been fried, with a little water, ground cinnamon and clove, whole clove covered, pine nuts, pitted raisins, almonds and ground biscuit as needed to form a paste
3. Oil a baking pan, dust with ground biscuit, put a layer of the milk and rice mentioned at the beginning, dust with ground biscuit, put another layer of ground meat, dust the same way, and continue in this order until there are three layers of ground meat and four of milk, with the milk as the last layer, which will be covered with biscuit
4. Put the cake to cook over gentle fires or in the oven
5. Once it has set, sprinkle ground sugar and cinnamon on top
