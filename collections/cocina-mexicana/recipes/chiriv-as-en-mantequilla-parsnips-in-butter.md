## Ingredients

- parsnips, cut to the thickness of a cork
- butter
- one quarter liter of broth
- salt
- a small amount of sugar
- fine herbs
- one spoonful of frizzled sauce
- fried toast for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the parsnips to the thickness of a cork and parboil them for five minutes
2. Mix them with butter in a pot with one quarter liter of broth
3. Season with salt and a small amount of sugar
4. When you notice they are almost cooked, let the broth reduce to the point of gelatin
5. Add more butter, fine herbs, and one spoonful of frizzled sauce
6. Let it come to a boil so everything blends together
7. Serve adorned with fried toast
