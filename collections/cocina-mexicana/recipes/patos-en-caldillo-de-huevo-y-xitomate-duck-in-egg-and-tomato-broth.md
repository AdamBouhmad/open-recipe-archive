## Ingredients

- Garlic
- Onions
- Parsley
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Cooked egg yolks
- Fried bread toasts
- Ducks, cooked
- Pot broth
- Vinegar
- Cooked ham slices
- Oil
- Olives
- Chiles

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic, onions, and parsley in lard with roasted and ground tomatoes
2. Season with salt, pepper, and cloves ground together with cooked egg yolks and fried bread toasts
3. Add the cooked ducks to this mixture along with pot broth, vinegar, and cooked ham slices
4. Let simmer until the broth becomes somewhat thick
5. When removing from heat, add oil, olives, and small chiles
