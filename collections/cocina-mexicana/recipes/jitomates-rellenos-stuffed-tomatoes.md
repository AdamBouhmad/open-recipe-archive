## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- onion
- veal meat
- ham
- parsley
- butter
- bread crumbs
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the top cap from the tomatoes and scoop out the interior
2. Chop the onion, veal meat, ham, and parsley together
3. Mix everything well and fry thoroughly
4. Use this mixture to fill the tomatoes
5. Butter a baking dish or pan and place the filled tomatoes in it
6. Sprinkle the remaining chopped mixture with some bread crumbs over the top
7. Add a little broth and cook over two fires
