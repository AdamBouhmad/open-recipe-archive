## Ingredients

- ham
- wine
- pulque
- bay leaf
- oregano
- thyme
- cumin
- achiote
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the ham in a pot with wine, pulque, bay leaf, oregano, thyme, cumin, achiote, and vinegar
2. Cover the pot well and cook the ham over low heat
3. When the ham is cooked, remove it and drain it
4. Grind sugar and sprinkle it over the ham
5. Pass a hot iron over the top and both sides of the ham
6. Serve
