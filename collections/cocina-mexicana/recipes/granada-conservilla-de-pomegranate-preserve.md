## Ingredients

- pomegranate grain juice
- powdered sugar
- more sugar for syrup at blown stage

## Directions

1. Press the juice from the pomegranate grains and mix with powdered sugar
2. Heat until the sugar dissolves completely
3. Make a separate syrup with another piece of sugar at blown stage and set aside
4. Add the juice and sugar mixture to this syrup
5. When the jelly begins to form, arrange the preserve on a plate
