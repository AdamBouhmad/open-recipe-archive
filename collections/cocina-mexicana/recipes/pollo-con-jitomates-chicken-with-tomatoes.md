## Ingredients

- chicken
- lard
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- tomato

## Directions

1. Cut into four pieces and lightly fry in lard with salt, and in another pot fry a garlic clove, chopped onion and tomato, and when well fried return the chicken pieces to it leaving them a quarter hour over low heat so they take on the taste of the tomato.
