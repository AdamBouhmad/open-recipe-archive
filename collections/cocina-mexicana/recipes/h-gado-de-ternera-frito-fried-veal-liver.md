## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- veal liver
- flour
- mutton broth
- white wine
- salt
- black pepper
- fine herbs, chopped

## Directions

1. Melt butter or lard in a skillet.
2. Fry the liver cut into pieces, dusted with flour.
3. Once fried, moisten with some mutton broth and white wine.
4. Add salt, black pepper, and chopped fine herbs.
5. Let it cook and serve when done.
