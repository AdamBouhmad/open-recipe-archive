## Ingredients

- onion tails
- garlic cloves
- toasted bread
- clove
- pepper
- birds

## Directions

1. Fry onion tails, garlic cloves and toasted bread
2. Grind everything with clove and little pepper, and return to the pot with the birds and their broth
3. Season and let thicken as appropriate
