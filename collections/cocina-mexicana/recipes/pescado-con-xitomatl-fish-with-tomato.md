## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- garlic cloves
- oregano
- salt
- vinegar
- fish
- fish broth

## Directions

1. Roast ripe tomatoes and also garlic cloves.
2. Grind both and fry with a little oregano and necessary salt.
3. When well fried, add a little vinegar and the fish in pieces.
4. Add a little of the broth in which it was cooked, ensuring the sauce remains thick.
