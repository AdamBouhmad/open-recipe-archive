## Ingredients

- sugar
- egg yolks
- butter
- sponge cake
- aged cheese
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at soft-ball stage with one pound of sugar
2. Remove from heat and when cold, add four egg yolks and four ounces of butter
3. Return to heat so the egg cooks
4. Make thin slices of sponge cake and form a layer with them
5. Place on top another layer of grated aged cheese
6. Pour the syrup over it
7. Cover everything with a griddle with flame so it browns and heats
8. To serve, dust with ground cinnamon
