## Ingredients

- one pound of Mocha coffee or the best available
- eight to ten quartillos of refined aguardiente
- twenty ounces of ground white sugar

## Directions

1. Keep in infusion for eight days one pound of toasted and unground Mocha coffee or the best available in eight to ten quartillos of refined aguardiente
2. Strain
3. Add twenty ounces of ground white sugar
4. When well dissolved, filter the liquor and bottle
