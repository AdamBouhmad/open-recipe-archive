## Ingredients

- Quince, not green
- Salt water
- Fresh water
- Two pounds sugar for each pound of quince

## Directions

1. Choose quinces that are not green, peel, remove the core and divide into pieces
2. Add pieces to cold salt water and cook
3. After cooking, place in the sun in fresh water until they blanch
4. Drain and weigh the quince
5. Make syrup with two pounds sugar for each pound of quince, adding quince when syrup reaches medium point
6. Let boil until the syrup wrinkles on the spoon
7. Cool in an earthenware vessel, then transfer to glass jars
8. Place in the sun to preserve
