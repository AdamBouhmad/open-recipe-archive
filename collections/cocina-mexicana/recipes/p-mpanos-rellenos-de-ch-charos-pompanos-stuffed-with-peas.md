## Ingredients

- Pámpanos
- Peas, cooked
- Exotes (fresh green beans), cooked and chopped
- Onions
- Garlic
- Xitomates (fresh tomatoes), raw
- Salt
- Clove
- Pimienta (pepper), ground
- Eggs
- Thread
- Lard
- Harina (flour)
- Capers
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Fry in lard cooked peas and green beans chopped with onions, garlic and raw xitomates, all very finely, seasoning with salt, clove and ground pepper
2. When the mixture is done, add half-beaten eggs to it
3. When all is set, fill the cleaned and desalted pámpanos with this mixture
4. Tie with thread and wrap in well-buttered papers
5. Place immediately to roast on the grill, taking care to turn them on both sides
6. When cooked, remove the papers, untie and serve this way, or roll in flour and fry again, adorning with capers, chiles and olives
