## Ingredients

- Coconut conserve
- ricotta cheese
- sugar
- butter
- sesame seeds, raw
- raisins
- almonds
- alfeñique decorations

## Directions

1. Make coconut conserve following the procedure for roasted coconut conserve, and following the same procedures, make a paste of ricotta, regulating twenty ounces of this for each pound of sugar
2. Let both things rest, and the next day mix them in equal quantities
3. Put the dessert on a platter spread with butter, sprinkling raw sesame seeds on top
4. Put it in a very gentle oven so that it browns a little
5. Decorate afterwards with raisins, almonds and alfeñique decorations
