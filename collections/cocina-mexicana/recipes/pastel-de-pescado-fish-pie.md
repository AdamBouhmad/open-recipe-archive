## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- ground saffron
- eggs
- lard
- robalo or other fish
- tomatoes
- onions
- parsley
- water
- vinegar
- capers
- caperberries
- olives
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Mix flour with salt, ground saffron and eggs, knead strongly until it reaches the proper point
2. Divide into twenty parts, for example, of which ten are extended very thin in a baking dish coated with lard, puffing them with sufficient lard and flour
3. Next place the filling, which will be prepared as follows: After the fish robalo or other is well cleaned, dust it with flour and fry in oil
4. Also fry tomatoes, onions and parsley, all chopped, adding a little flour and a little water, vinegar, capers, caperberries and olives, clove, cinnamon, pepper and few cumin, all ground, sliced hard boiled eggs and the corresponding salt
5. After this is placed in the baking dish cover with the other ten remaining pieces of mass in the same manner as the first, and put the pie in the oven to cook
