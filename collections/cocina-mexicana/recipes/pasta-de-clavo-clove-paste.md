## Ingredients

- One pound of ground peeled almonds
- A little cinnamon
- Twenty-six to twenty-eight cloves of spice in powder form
- One and a half pounds of sugar at water-joining point consistency

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar at water-joining point consistency
2. Remove from heat and add the ground almonds, cinnamon, and powdered cloves
3. Return the mixture to the fire and stir constantly until the bottom of the pan becomes visible
4. Remove from heat
5. Pour into molds or small boxes
