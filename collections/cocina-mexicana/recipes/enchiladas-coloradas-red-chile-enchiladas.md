## Ingredients

- Equal parts dried ancho chile and pasilla chile, toasted
- Lard
- Small, soft tortillas
- Cheese
- Shredded fried meat
- Sliced onion
- Fried chorizo
- Fried longaniza or morcón
- Avocado quarters
- Chicken breast pieces
- Hard-boiled eggs, sliced

## Directions

1. Grind equal parts of toasted ancho and pasilla chile together
2. Heat lard in a cazuela
3. Dip the small, soft tortillas in the chile and fry them in the lard, frying no more than four at a time
4. Remove and arrange on a plate, stacking them on top of one another
5. On top of all of them place cheese, shredded fried meat, sliced onion, fried chorizo, fried longaniza or morcón, avocado quarters, chicken breast pieces and even sliced hard-boiled eggs
6. If the garnish is placed on each individual tortilla, they will be better
