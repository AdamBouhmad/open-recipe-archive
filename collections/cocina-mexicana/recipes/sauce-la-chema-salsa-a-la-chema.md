## Ingredients

- parsley
- small onions
- two tablespoons of flour
- half a pound of butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- two glasses of milk

## Directions

1. Chop a little parsley
2. Chop and wash an equal quantity of small onions
3. Place these two things in a saucepan with two tablespoons of flour, half a pound of butter, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Moisten everything with two glasses of milk
5. Let it boil for about fifteen or twenty minutes, stirring constantly
6. This sauce is placed over certain fish, such as codfish, turbot, etc.
