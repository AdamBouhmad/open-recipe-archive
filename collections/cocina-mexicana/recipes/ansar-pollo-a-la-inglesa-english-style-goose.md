## Ingredients

- one fat goose
- three large onions
- butter
- a handful of sage, finely chopped
- the goose liver
- pepper
- salt
- beef stock or concentrated red cattle broth

## Directions

1. Choose a fat goose and to ensure it is young and tender, try to break the upper part of the beak; if it breaks easily, it is proof of good quality
2. Proceed to separate the wings, empty it, pluck it, and singe it over flame, toasting the feet to remove the first skin, cut the nails and chop the liver last
3. Clean three large onions, cutting them into small dice; fry them in butter and let them cook gently, adding a handful of finely chopped sage and the liver with pepper and corresponding salt
4. Stuff the goose with this mixture, sew it closed and tie the feet lengthwise, then place it on the roasting spit to roast
5. While roasting, place it on a plate and serve with beef stock or concentrated red cattle broth
