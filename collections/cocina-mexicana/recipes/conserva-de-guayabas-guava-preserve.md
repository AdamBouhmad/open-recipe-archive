## Ingredients

- Guavas, preferably white ones, large, in good season, firm and without blemishes
- Syrup (almíbar) at low point
- Iron wire

## Directions

1. Select the largest white guavas in good season, firm and without blemishes
2. Peel them immediately after selection
3. Cook them, and when done, make a shallow cut at the crown or pierce them from top to bottom with iron wire, avoiding copper instruments
4. Place them in clarified syrup at low point and give them several boils
5. Set aside and let rest, returning to the fire the next day and letting them boil until the syrup reaches preserve consistency
