## Ingredients

- Pepitas seeds from Tierra Caliente
- Lard
- Lettuce
- Chilchotes zacapaleños
- Chicken (two hens)

## Directions

1. For two hens, use one almuد of pepita seeds from Tierra Caliente
2. Peel and brown them in lard and then grind them
3. Separately, grind lettuce and chilchotes zacapaleños to make it spicy
4. Add to the cazuela the pepita without frying, the lettuce, and the chilotes
5. Season everything together and when ready, add the hens
