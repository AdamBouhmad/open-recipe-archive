## Ingredients

- veal loin
- cumin
- garlic
- wide chile, toasted
- vinegar
- salt
- large tomatoes
- pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Two days after the veal is slaughtered, cut the loin and pound it so that it is well tenderized
2. Coat it all over with a paste made of cumin, garlic and one wide toasted chile, all ground
3. Place it in a pot with vinegar and salt, leaving it in this infusion for one day
4. The following day, place it to cook, and when the meat is tender, remove it from the fire
5. Chop some large tomatoes and fry them, seasoning with salt
6. Place the loin in this frying, and coating it well with the tomato, serve it sprinkled with pepper and clove
