## Ingredients

- two pork loins
- abundant salt
- two large garlic heads, peeled

## Directions

1. Coat two pork loins with abundant salt and leave it thus for two days, taking care to turn it
2. After these days, grind two large peeled garlic heads, grind them with a little salt and coat the loins abundantly with it so it penetrates well
