## Ingredients

- One quart or a little more of flour
- About one ounce of fine salt
- Twelve ounces of butter
- Three eggs

## Directions

1. Put one quart or a little more of flour on the table and make a hole in the middle
2. Put there about one ounce of fine salt, twelve ounces of butter, three eggs
