## Ingredients

- veal pulp, very aged
- spices of all kinds except saffron
- cumin
- cilantro
- salt
- bay leaves
- thyme
- oregano
- garlic heads

## Directions

1. Slice the very aged veal pulp across the grain so that the slices are quite thin
2. Grind together proportional spices of all kinds except saffron, with cumin and cilantro in lesser quantity than the others, until a paste is formed (or finely ground powder if a little water has not been added)
3. Layer in the barrel: veal slices and spices alternately, starting with spices and continuing successively until the barrel is full
4. On the spices add salt, bay leaves in small quantity, thyme, oregano, and crushed garlic heads
5. Cover the barrel and tighten the hoops
6. Fill through the hole in the lid with vinegar if the barrel is to travel to another location; but if it is only for use, there is no need to seal it, rather after placing the last layer of spices with everything else mentioned, pour vinegar over it until full, leaving the meat well bathed and covered with an overlaid lid
