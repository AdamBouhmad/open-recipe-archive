## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the flour in milk and sweeten with sugar.
2. This preparation is a nutritious food and easy to digest when it is well made and well cooked.
3. For a lighter meal for the stomach, take the precaution of subjecting the flour for some time to the heat of a moderately hot oven, then grind it and pass it through a sieve.
