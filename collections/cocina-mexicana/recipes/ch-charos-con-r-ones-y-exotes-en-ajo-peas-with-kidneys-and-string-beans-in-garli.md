## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- Peas
- String beans
- Salt
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Lard
- Dried ancho chiles
- Pasilla chiles
- Fried bread
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Cook the kidneys together with the peas and whole string beans in water with salt, ground garlic, and cumin
2. When cooked, remove from the broth and slice the kidneys and chop the string beans
3. Fry in lard with salt, dried ancho and pasilla chiles that have been deveined, soaked, and ground with garlic, cumin, and fried bread
4. Add the kidneys, peas, string beans, and the broth in which they were cooked
5. Add a little oregano and let it boil until the broth has the proper consistency
