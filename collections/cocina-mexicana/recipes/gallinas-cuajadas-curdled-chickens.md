## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Brains
- Testicles
- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Spices except saffron and cumin
- Four eggs
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Cook chickens, brains, testicles and cauliflower separately, each with its corresponding salt
2. Fry chopped tomatoes and garlic
3. Add the chicken broth, slice the brains and testicles, and cut the cauliflower in pieces
4. Break apart the chickens and add everything to the broth with ground spices, except saffron and cumin
5. When the broth is well seasoned, beat four eggs, squeeze a lemon over them after beating and add to the broth stirring well
6. Cover the casserole with a griddle over low heat until the egg sets, then serve
