## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Lard
- Onion
- Garlic cloves
- Turnips
- Water
- Ground clove
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Fried bread
- Thyme
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs
2. Place in a casserole with ham slices, chorizo, a spoonful of lard, plenty of onion, and garlic cloves
3. Peel turnips, cut into slices, fry in lard, and add to the casserole
4. When the meat and onion are fried, add water, ground clove, ground cinnamon, and ground pepper mixed with a piece of fried bread and thyme
5. The broth should be thick
6. Garnish like the previous recipe and add Jerez wine
