## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- yeast
- anise
- salt
- butter
- custard
- milk

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add six eggs with whites and two egg yolks, which makes one egg of yeast, its anise, a little salt, one butter and a plate of custard in place of water or milk
2. The consistency of the dough should be soft and is not left in the sun but rather fried right away
