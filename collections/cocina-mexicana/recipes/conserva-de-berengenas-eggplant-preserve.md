## Ingredients

- Eggplants
- Salt water
- Cold syrup
- Ground clove and cinnamon
- Almond and sesame (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut off the tips of the top calyxes and remove the spines
2. Cut in a cross at the crown and prick with a basting needle
3. Wash well with salt water
4. Add to a pot of boiling water, covering the mouth with straw so they boil without falling apart
5. Once cooked, strain in a basket
6. For each eggplant add a quart of cold syrup before it has reached point
7. Let finish cooking over gentle heat
8. Add ground clove and cinnamon, and if desired, almond and sesame
