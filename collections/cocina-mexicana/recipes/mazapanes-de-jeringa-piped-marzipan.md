## Ingredients

- 1 pound blanched sweet almonds
- 4 or 5 egg whites
- 1.5 pounds powdered sugar
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of blanched sweet almonds to dry in the stove and grind them, moistening them at the same time as they are being ground with 4 or 5 egg whites to prevent them from becoming oily
2. When well ground, add 1.5 pounds of powdered sugar and lemon zest
3. Beat everything well, making a workable paste that is put by parts into a piping bag
