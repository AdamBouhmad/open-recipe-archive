## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- grape leaves
- bread toasts
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and wrap these birds with grape leaves, if available, without emptying them
2. Place bread toasts underneath to catch what drips from them
3. Place on the spit, letting them cook for about half an hour and turning them well so that both their fat and everything that drips from them is received on bread toasts placed underneath
4. When ready to serve, remove the toasts and serve on these same toasts with lemon juice
