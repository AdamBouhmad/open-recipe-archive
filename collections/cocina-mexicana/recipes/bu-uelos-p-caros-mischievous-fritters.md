## Ingredients

- Six eggs
- One pound of flour
- Half caldron cup of melted lard
- One cup of aniseed water in which a piece of yeast the size of an egg is dissolved
- One quarter of a quartillo of Málaga wine
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs with the flour
2. Add the melted lard, aniseed water with dissolved yeast, wine, and salt
3. Beat everything very well until the dough shows eyes
4. Put it in a pot, cover well with a cloth, and place in the sun
5. When the dough has puffed well, form the fritters and fry them in lard
