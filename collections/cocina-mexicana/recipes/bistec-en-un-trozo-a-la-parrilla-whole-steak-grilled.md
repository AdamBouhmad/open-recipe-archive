## Ingredients

- sirloin
- salt
- black pepper
- warm butter

## Directions

1. Cut the sirloin in full
2. Beat, round, and skin it, taking care that the steak remains somewhat fatty
3. Season the prepared piece with salt and black pepper
4. Soak in warm butter
5. Grill at the moment of serving
6. It is necessary, above all, that the steak be cooked over high heat, that is, somewhat rare, so that the juices concentrate
