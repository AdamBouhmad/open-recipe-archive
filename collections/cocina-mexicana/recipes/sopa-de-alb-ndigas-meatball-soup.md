## Ingredients

- meat
- bread
- onion
- spearmint
- nutmeg
- vinegar
- rice
- broth

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and add a little soaked bread, considering the amount of meat, onion, and ground spearmint in small quantity and a scraping of nutmeg with vinegar
2. Make small balls with this mixture
3. Add a little rice to the broth
4. When the rice is cooked, add the meat balls
