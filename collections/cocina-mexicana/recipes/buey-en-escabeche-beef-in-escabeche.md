## Ingredients

- Beef pulp
- Fine ground salt
- Water
- Good vinegar
- Lard
- Garlic head
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Cumin

## Directions

1. Skin the meat and clean it well
2. Sprinkle with plenty of fine ground salt
3. Place in a dry pot and keep for eight days, taking care to turn it morning and night and remove the blood that runs from it
4. After eight days, place to cook in water and good vinegar with a little lard, a crushed garlic head, a pair of onions cut in quarters, bay leaves, a little ground thyme with black pepper, clove and cumin
5. Cover the pot with paper and place a well-adjusted plate on top with cold water inside
6. Place on gentle fire until the meat cooks well
7. Then remove from fire and the next day remove and cut into thick slices
