## Ingredients

- 18 cuartillos of milk
- 2 pounds of sugar
- 1 stick of cinnamon
- 24 eggs
- cold sponge cake, crumbled
- butter

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk with sugar and strain it
2. Add a stick of cinnamon and bring to a boil until it becomes so thick that when stirred, the bottom of the pan is exposed
3. Remove from heat and let cool
4. Mix in the beaten eggs as if for frying and blend them in
5. Add some cold crumbled sponge cake
6. Pour this mixture into molds that have been buttered and dusted with sponge cake
7. Bake in a moderate oven
8. When cooked, remove the cakes from the oven and turn out onto the serving plates
