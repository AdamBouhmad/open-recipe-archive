## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Fifteen raw egg yolks
- A little lard
- Sufficient salt
- Eight or ten egg yolks
- Half pound sugar
- Necessary wine

## Directions

1. Add to the flour fifteen raw egg yolks, a little lard and sufficient salt
2. Moisten with necessary wine to be able to spread the dough and mix everything very well
3. Make the dough hard and beat it, gradually adding sifted sugar until it is sufficiently sweet to the palate
4. When it becomes tough, extend it and form the empanadas and put them in the oven without coating with lard
