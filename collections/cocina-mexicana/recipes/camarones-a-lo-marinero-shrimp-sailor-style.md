## Ingredients

- raw shrimp, cleaned
- lard
- ripe tomatoes
- good vinegar or red wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the shrimp raw and put them to fry in lard until they begin to brown
2. Roast some ripe tomatoes, peel them and grind very well
3. Fry with the shrimp
4. Add a little good vinegar or red wine and a piece of sugar
5. Let it boil until the broth reduces
6. Serve cold removed from the lard, or hot on thin slices of bread, toasted in it
