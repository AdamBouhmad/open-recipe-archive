## Ingredients

- One pound almonds
- One pound sugar
- Orange blossom water
- Flour
- Eggs
- Lard
- Salt

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds with one pound of sugar, adding orange blossom water while grinding until the paste becomes very soft
2. Knead the flour with eggs, lard and salt
3. Form the empanadas with the almond filling and cook them in the oven
