## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Cheese, ground or fresh crumbled
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Tomatoes, roasted
- Water
- Onion quarters

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as previously described
2. Fill the chiles with cheese instead of scrambled eggs
3. Coat with flour and dip in beaten egg
4. Fry the chiles
5. Prepare the sauce the same way as for the eggs version
6. Place the stuffed chiles in the sauce and let it reach regular consistency
