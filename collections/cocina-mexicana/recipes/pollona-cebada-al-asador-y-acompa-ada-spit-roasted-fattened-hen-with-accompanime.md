## Ingredients

- fattened hen
- poultry breasts
- cooked ham
- pork fat
- cow udder
- mushrooms
- fine herbs
- spices
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs soaked in cream or milk
- vegetables for gardeners' stew
- larks
- squabs
- beef slices
- ham slices
- pork fat strips
- fine herbs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. After preparing and arranging the hen, open it along the back without removing any bones except those from the breast
2. Make a stuffing with poultry breasts, cooked ham, pork fat, cow udder, mushrooms, fine herbs, spices, egg yolks and bread crumbs soaked in cream or milk, chopping and crushing everything together
3. Spread this stuffing over the hen
4. Make a gardeners' stew with larks and squabs with necessary ingredients and broth, letting it simmer on low heat, then defatting and binding with concentrated beef or ham broth
5. After this stew cools, spread it over the stuffing on the hen, lightly covering with the same stuffing
6. Close and sew the hen
7. Wrap in beef slices, ham slices and pork fat strips with fine herbs, pepper and salt
8. Wrap the hen in paper leaves, tie it, place on the spit and let it cook on low heat
9. Serve by untying it with ham essence or cucumber stew if appropriate
