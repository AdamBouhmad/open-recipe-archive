## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- garlic cloves, fried
- asparagus, cooked in salted water, drained and chopped
- water from cooking the asparagus
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Proceed as in the previous recipe until the asparagus is half-fried, with the difference that the fried garlic is not removed from the fat
2. Add a little water from the cooking liquid and season with salt
3. When the broth is boiling, add eggs so they will set
