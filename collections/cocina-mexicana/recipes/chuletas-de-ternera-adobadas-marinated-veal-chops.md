## Ingredients

- veal pulp chops
- broad chiles
- oregano
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- sour orange juice
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- lard
- chiles
- onion

## Directions

1. Place the chops cut from veal pulp in a pot to marinate with broad chiles that have been soaked and ground with oregano, garlic, clove and pepper
2. Add sour orange juice and salt
3. Let marinate overnight
4. The next day add water, vinegar and lard, and place over heat
5. When cooked, empty the pot into a pot placed over fire to finish consuming the broth
6. When serving at table, top with the remaining marinade, with small chiles, garlics, and if desired onion slices
