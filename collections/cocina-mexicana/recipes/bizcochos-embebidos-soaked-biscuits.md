## Ingredients

- sixteen egg whites
- sixteen egg yolks
- three ounces of fine sugar
- one pound of melted butter
- three teacups of yeast
- one pinch of salt dissolved in a little warm water
- flour, as needed
- dragées or sugar

## Directions

1. Beat sixteen egg whites until they rise well
2. Add sixteen egg yolks and beat thoroughly when mixed
3. Add three ounces of fine sugar
4. Add one pound of melted butter, three teacups of yeast, and one pinch of salt dissolved in a little warm water
5. When everything is well mixed, vigorously stir in flour so the dough does not break, and when it reaches good consistency, add another half pound of cold butter
6. Mix well and form the biscuits
7. Place on papers, sprinkle on top with dragées or sugar, and put in the oven to bake
