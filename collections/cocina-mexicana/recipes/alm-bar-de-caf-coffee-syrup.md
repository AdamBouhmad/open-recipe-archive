## Ingredients

- eight ounces of coffee
- two quarts of water
- four pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Toast and grind eight ounces of coffee
2. Infuse it in two quarts of water and distill through a filter
3. Add four pounds of sugar
4. Cook until it reaches the proper point
5. This syrup can be used with milk and is preferable to a decoction
