## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Thin ham for larding
- Ham slices
- Veal pulp
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Broth
- Butter
- Chicory, cardoons, or tomato substrate

## Directions

1. Blanch and refresh the sweetbreads as described in previous articles.
2. Lard them with thin ham and place in a casserole lined with ham slices and other slices of veal pulp, some carrots, two onions, two cloves and two bay leaves.
3. Moisten with half a tablespoon of broth, so there is just enough liquid equal to half a sweetbread.
4. Cover with a round of buttered paper and cook over two fires for three quarters of an hour, being careful they do not take color.
5. Serve over chicory, cardoon, or tomato substrate, choosing whichever seems best.
