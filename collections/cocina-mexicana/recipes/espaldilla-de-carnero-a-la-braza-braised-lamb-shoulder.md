## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder, boned
- slices of ham
- parsnips
- onions
- two onions with two cloves
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- bones from the shoulder
- broth or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the boned lamb shoulder in a vessel suitable for braising
2. Add slices of ham, some parsnips, and onions, with one onion studded with two cloves
3. Add two bay leaves, a little thyme, the bones removed from the shoulder, and other bones if available
4. Pour in sufficient broth or water
5. Season with salt
6. Cook over low heat for three and a half hours
7. When ready to serve, drain the shoulder, untie it and remove the string
8. Add gelatin and serve on a plate
