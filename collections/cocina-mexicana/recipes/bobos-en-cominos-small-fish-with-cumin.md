## Ingredients

- Small fish (bobos)
- Cumin seeds
- Garlic cloves
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Ground pepper
- Chinese oregano

## Directions

1. Wash the cumin seeds well, dry them and toast thoroughly
2. Grind the cumin with garlic cloves and dissolve in water with plenty of salt
3. Add plenty of oil and vinegar, ground pepper and Chinese oregano
4. Place an oil-greased paper in a pot
5. Clean and gut the bobo well and place it on the paper
6. Cook between two fires
7. When all the water has been consumed, serve alone or with some fish sauce
