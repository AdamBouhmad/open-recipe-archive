## Ingredients

- three ounces of fresh butter
- twelve ounces of flour
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- three egg yolks
- four small glasses of water

## Directions

1. Melt three ounces of fresh butter and add twelve ounces of flour and the same amount of powdered sugar, three egg yolks and four small glasses of water
2. Remove from fire when the mixture is well heated
3. Make the wafers as indicated in the previous article and shape them into cornets with a brush
