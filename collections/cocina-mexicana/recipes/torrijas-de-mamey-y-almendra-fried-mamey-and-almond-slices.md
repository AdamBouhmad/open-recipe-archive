## Ingredients

- three large mameys
- four ounces toasted ground almonds
- sifted sugar
- toasted ground sponge cake
- beaten eggs
- lard
- syrup
- cinnamon
- clove
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Grind three large mameys removing all fibers and mix with four ounces toasted ground almonds, sifted sugar, and ground toasted sponge cake as needed for proper consistency
2. Form the torrijas, coat with beaten egg
3. Fry in lard
4. Boil in syrup with cinnamon and clove
5. Decorate with toasted sesame seeds to serve
