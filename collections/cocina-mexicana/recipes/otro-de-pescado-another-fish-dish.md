## Ingredients

- onion tails
- parsley
- lard
- garlic
- fried bread
- oil
- pepper
- water
- fish

## Directions

1. Chop a regular amount of onion tails and about half as much parsley.
2. Fry this in lard with garlic cloves.
3. Grind everything together with a piece of fried bread.
4. Re-fry all the ground mixture in a little lard and plenty of oil.
5. While frying, add a pinch of pepper.
6. Once well fried, add just enough water to season.
7. Add fish that has already been fried in oil.
8. Leave on the fire until only a rich sauce remains.
