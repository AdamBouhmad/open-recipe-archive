## Ingredients

- Veal ribs
- Lard
- Salt
- Wine
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Marjoram
- Strips of ham
- Small chorizos
- Pork jowl, cut in dice
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Picadillo from rabióles
- Capers
- Olives
- Pieces of ham
- Small chorizos
- Pork jowl
- Longaniza sausage
- Cooked egg
- Chili peppers in vinegar
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Break the bones symmetrically and cut the rib section to the desired size, wash well, and cook in a deep pot so that it lies flat, with lard, salt, wine, thyme, bay leaves, marjoram, strips of ham, small chorizos, pork jowl cut in dice, garlic, cloves, cinnamon, and black pepper, all ground
2. Once the ribs are cooked, remove from the pot, place on a table, carefully remove the bones, and fill with the same picadillo from rabióles, adding capers, olives, pieces of ham, small chorizos, pork jowl, longaniza, cooked egg, and chili peppers in vinegar, all chopped
3. Return to the pot and to the fire, being careful to turn it so it does not burn, and it should not be completely dry
4. Garnish for serving with all the things that were cooked with it, except the herbs, adding raisins and almonds
5. While it is being taken to the table, keep it hot over the steam of a pot
