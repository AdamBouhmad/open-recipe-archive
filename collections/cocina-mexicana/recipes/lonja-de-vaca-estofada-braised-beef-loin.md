## Ingredients

- Beef loin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Meat gelatin

## Directions

1. Debone the loin
2. Season the inside with pepper and salt
3. Tie it to maintain good shape
4. Put in a braising pan with butter only
5. Let cook for three hours, turning occasionally
6. Glaze and serve on meat gelatin
7. This piece can also be served cold with gelatin
