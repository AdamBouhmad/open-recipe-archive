## Ingredients

- Four ounces of lard
- White wine
- Calf brains
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Garlic
- Lard
- Beef puchero broth
- Parsley
- Juice of half a lemon
- Beaten eggs
- Flour

## Directions

1. Heat four ounces of lard in a pan on the fire until it reaches a regular temperature
2. Add a glass of white wine
3. Clean the brains with salt and vinegar and add them to cook
4. Fry some garlic in lard and add to the pan
5. Add a few cups of puchero broth, some parsley and the juice of half a lemon
6. After cooked, remove and cut into slices
7. Coat with beaten eggs and flour
8. Fry them
9. Pour them into the sauce in which they cooked
10. Coat with a little parsley and serve
