## Ingredients

- artichokes
- aged cheese
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- garlic
- oil

## Directions

1. Trim the artichokes and cook them
2. Grind aged cheese and dust it on the leaves with ground pepper and a little breadcrumbs
3. Pound some garlic and fry in oil
4. Place the artichokes there with tips pointing up
5. Put a griddle on top with very gentle heat and serve
