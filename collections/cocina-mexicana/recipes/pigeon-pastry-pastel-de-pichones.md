## Ingredients

- fine dough
- pigeons
- beef lardoons
- mushrooms
- beef marrow
- ground or pounded ham
- salt
- pepper
- beef substance
- lemon

## Directions

1. Make a base of fine dough
2. Place the pigeons on top, garnished with beef lardoons, mushrooms, beef marrow, and ground or pounded ham
3. Season everything appropriately
4. Cover and complete in the usual manner
5. Allow to cook in the oven for an hour and a half
6. Serve the pastry hot as an appetizer with beef substance and lemon
