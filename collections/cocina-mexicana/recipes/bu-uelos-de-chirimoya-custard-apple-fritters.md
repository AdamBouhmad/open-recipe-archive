## Ingredients

- Custard apple, solid and peeled
- Equal quantity of hard biscuit
- A pinch of sugar
- Ground clove
- Eggs
- Ground cinnamon
- Lard
- Thick syrup
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Pit the custard apple and grind it with an equal quantity of hard biscuit
2. Add a pinch of sugar and ground clove
3. Beat eggs well and add plenty of ground cinnamon
4. Mix everything perfectly
5. Place a pan with lard on the fire; when well heated, test a small spoonful of dough to see if it puffs well; if not, add more egg
6. Once it puffs well, form the fritters and fry them
7. Place them in thick syrup and decorate with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and cinnamon sticks
