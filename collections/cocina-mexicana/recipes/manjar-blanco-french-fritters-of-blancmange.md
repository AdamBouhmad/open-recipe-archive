## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of cream
- four ounces of flour
- a little salt
- a little lemon zest
- sugar
- four mazapanes
- two sweet pignolia nuts, crushed
- three eggs
- flour for dusting
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Dissolve in two cuartillos of cream, four ounces of flour, a little salt and a little lemon zest
2. Let the mixture cook for about three hours, stirring from time to time and after having covered the stove
3. When the blancmange is about to finish cooking, add sugar with four mazapanes and two crushed sweet pignolia nuts, and let it finish cooking
4. Incorporate three eggs one by one and make the paste bind together, which is spread on the cloth of a pan
5. Sprinkle with flour and let it cool
6. Divide into small square pieces and make small balls the size of grapes with them
7. At the moment of serving, heat butter in a pan, place in it the strainer, in which the fritters will have been placed, and shake the strainer frequently
8. As soon as the fritters have turned dark, remove them, let them drain, sprinkle them with sugar and serve
9. If desired, very finely chopped chicken breasts can be incorporated into the blancmange
