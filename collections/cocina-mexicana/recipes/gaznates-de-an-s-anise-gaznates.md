## Ingredients

- flour
- three egg yolks
- anise water
- tequesquite water
- egg white
- lard

## Directions

1. Knead a handful of flour with three egg yolks, a little anise water and tequesquite water until it does not stick to the hands
2. Beat it out and cut the gaznates with a cutter, sealing the edges with egg white
3. Let rest a little and fry, standing them in a pan where hot lard bathes them well
4. When fried on one side, turn to the other so both ends are well done
