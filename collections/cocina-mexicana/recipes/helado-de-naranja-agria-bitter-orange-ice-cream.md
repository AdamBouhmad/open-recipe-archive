## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sugar
- peels of two bitter oranges
- six bitter oranges
- four lemons

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at medium point with one and a half pounds of sugar
2. Infuse the peels of two bitter oranges
3. Add six more bitter oranges and four lemons for straining
4. Leave in infusion for one hour
5. Pass through a silk sieve to freeze it in the ice cream mold
