## Ingredients

- veal or cow kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- shallots, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- cooked mushrooms
- flour
- white wine
- Spanish sauce, thick
- fine butter
- lemon juice
- fried toast

## Directions

1. Slice the veal kidneys after removing the skins and fat
2. Place in a pan with butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg, chopped shallots and parsley, and cooked mushrooms
3. Fry them by sautéing over very high heat, and add a little flour, white wine, and some tablespoons of thick Spanish sauce
4. At the moment of serving, add a little fresh butter and lemon juice
5. The kidneys can also be cooked on the spit or in the oven, in which case it is necessary to leave them the fat, though they are usually only used as garnish or decoration for tarts, egg omelets, etc.
