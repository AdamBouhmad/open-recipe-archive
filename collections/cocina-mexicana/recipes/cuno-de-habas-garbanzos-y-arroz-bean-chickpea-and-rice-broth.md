## Ingredients

- Dried beans without skin
- Chickpeas without skin
- Rice
- Onion quarters
- Roasted and peeled garlic cloves
- Chopped cabbage
- Small squash in quarters
- Turnips
- Lard proportioned to the amount of broth
- Salt
- Cumin
- Roasted cilantro
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Clove

## Directions

1. Put a pot with water on the fire and add dried beans without skin, chickpeas without skin, and rice.
2. Add quarters of onion, roasted and peeled garlic cloves, chopped cabbage, small squash cut in quarters, and turnips.
3. Add lard proportioned to the quantity of broth you will make.
4. Season everything with salt and add ground cumin, roasted cilantro, saffron, a little pepper, and clove.
5. Let it cook until the seeds fall apart, according to whether you want the broth thick or thin, as this varies according to each person's taste.
6. When serving, do not forget the oil cruet on the table.
