## Ingredients

- Beet
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Clean toasted almonds
- Figs
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Good vinegar
- Wine, muscatel or other generoso

## Directions

1. Cook the beet with a lump of sugar and after peeling, cut it into small cubes.
2. Divide some raisins and remove the pits.
3. Chop clean toasted almonds and chop some figs as well.
4. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top of everything.
5. To a little good vinegar add enough sugar to cut the acidity and mix in all the chopped ingredients, ensuring there is not much broth.
6. Leave it in infusion for two or three days, and after placing it on a platter, when serving, bathe it with muscatel wine or other generoso and sprinkle more toasted sesame seeds on top.
