## Ingredients

- Pork tongues
- strips of ham
- garlic
- clove
- whole pepper
- oil
- salt
- vinegar
- ground clove
- ground pepper
- ground cumin
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- water
- lard
- xitomate
- roasted and ground garlic
- small whole onions
- flour
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Clean the tongues and lard with strips of ham, garlic, clove and whole pepper
2. Place in a pot with oil, salt and vinegar, clove, pepper and ground cumin, thyme, bay leaf and marjoram
3. Let marinate thus for twenty-four hours
4. The next day cook them in the same broth, adding water, lard, xitomate and roasted and ground garlic, and small whole onions
5. When the tongues are cooked, thicken the small broth with flour browned in lard
6. For serving, sprinkle with oil and decorate with small chiles and olives
