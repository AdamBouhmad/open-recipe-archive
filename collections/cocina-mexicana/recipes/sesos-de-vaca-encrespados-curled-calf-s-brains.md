## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- light broth
- six large round onions per brain
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- laurel
- curled sauce
- egg yolks
- pork cracklings

## Directions

1. Clean the brains and cook them in light broth (see light broth, page 132)
2. For each brain, cut six large round onions into small dice, blanch them and cook in butter with a little garlic, salt, pepper, nutmeg and laurel, without letting them brown
3. Pour curled sauce over them (see page 776) and let everything reduce until it forms a paste, then bind with egg yolks
4. Divide each brain in half and wrap each piece in the said paste, then cover with pork cracklings
5. Put on the grill and serve with clear Grand sauce underneath
