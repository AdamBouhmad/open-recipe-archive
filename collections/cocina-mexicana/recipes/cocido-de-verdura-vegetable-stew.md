## Ingredients

- meat
- bacon
- vegetables
- spices

## Directions

1. This stew differs from others only in that the vegetables are cooked with the meat and bacon.
2. Be careful to add the vegetables after the meat has cooked well.
3. Season with some spices, as is customary in Andalusia.
