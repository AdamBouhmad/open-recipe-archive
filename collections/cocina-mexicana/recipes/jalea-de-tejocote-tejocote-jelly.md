## Ingredients

- Tejocotes
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Cook and peel the tejocotes and then add to boiling water
2. Cover the pot and leave in the open air for two or three days
3. After which strain and sweeten with white sugar
4. Put to boil over low heat so it does not become reddish and give it the mirror glaze point
5. To one pound of sugar corresponds one cuartillo of jelly
