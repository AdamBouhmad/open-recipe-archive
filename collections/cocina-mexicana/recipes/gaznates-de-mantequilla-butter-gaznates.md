## Ingredients

- half pound flour
- twelve egg yolks
- salt
- butter
- sweet paste filling

## Directions

1. Knead half a pound of flour with twelve egg yolks, necessary salt and a little butter, without adding any water
2. Extend the dough into sheets with a rolling pin and form the gaznates by wrapping the sheet around a mold or reed like a tube
3. Remove the mold and fry in lard until golden
4. When fried, fill with some sweet paste
