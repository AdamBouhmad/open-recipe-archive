## Ingredients

- Grape juice that is neither too green nor too ripe
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Select grape juice that is not too green nor too ripe, such that the seeds can be easily removed by opening one side
2. Place the clean grape juice in cold water
3. Put in a pot over fire, being careful not to give it a strong boil, as it would turn into marmalade; it should be removed as soon as it rises to the surface of the water
4. Let it cool in this same water
5. When cool, put it over gentle fire to restore its green color, then return it to cold water
6. Drain over a sieve and add to syrup at low ball point
7. The next morning drain again and repeat the syrup point, adding the grape juice and repeating this operation five consecutive days
8. After five days, bring the syrup to large pearl point and add the grape juice for a single boil
9. Skim then and store in jars
