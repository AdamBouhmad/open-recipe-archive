## Ingredients

- capon
- giblets
- broth
- bread crumbs
- cream
- butter
- parsley
- green onions
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean out the capon
2. After cooking, mince all the giblets with broth containing cooked breadcrumbs with cream, a quarter pound of butter, parsley, very finely chopped green onions passed through butter, salt, pepper, and three egg yolks
3. Fill the interior with this stuffing, covering lightly with breadcrumbs
4. Brown the capon in butter and re-bread
