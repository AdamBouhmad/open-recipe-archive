## Ingredients

- butter
- breadcrumbs
- fat ham
- garlic
- onion
- broth
- chicken breast
- salt
- parsley
- thyme
- pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Place the pot on the fire with a piece of butter
2. Add some grated bread and pieces of fat ham to it
3. When already browned, add finely minced garlic and onion in the same butter
4. Then add broth and add a little minced chicken breast
5. Let it boil on a low fire with the necessary salt
6. When removing it from the fire, add coarsely chopped parsley and thyme, ground pepper and clove in small quantity
7. First let it thicken as necessary, and when in good condition, pour it into the sauce boat
