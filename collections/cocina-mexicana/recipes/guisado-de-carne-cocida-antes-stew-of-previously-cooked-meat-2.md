## Ingredients

- onions, sliced
- lard
- broth
- cooked meat, cut into pieces
- vinegar or mustard

## Directions

1. Sauté sliced onions in lard, adding a little broth
2. Add the meat cut into pieces and cook a little
3. Add a bit of vinegar or mustard and serve
