## Ingredients

- cebolla
- ajo
- perejil
- orégano
- manteca
- agua
- alcaparras
- pan
- manteca
- clavo
- pimienta
- cominos
- bobo

## Directions

1. Chop onion, garlic, parsley and oregano very finely and fry in lard.
2. Once fried add water so it is thin.
3. Grind a handful of capers and a slice of bread browned in lard, ground with clove, pepper and cumin.
4. Place the bobo in this broth to boil and transfer to a platter.
