## Ingredients

- pork meat
- ancho chiles, deveined and soaked
- tomatoes
- onion, in large pieces
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- clove
- oregano de Castilla
- banana slices
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry pork meat until well fried
2. Remove from heat
3. Grind well-deveined and soaked ancho chiles
4. Fry them well
5. Add red tomatoes in proportion to prevent the sauce from being too spicy
6. Add onion in large pieces, water, salt, vinegar, a little clove, and rubbed oregano de Castilla
7. Add the meat to cook with banana slices and a small piece of sugar
8. Let boil and the broth should remain regular
