## Ingredients

- two quartillos of good milk
- two quartillos of custard
- four or five tablespoons of powdered sugar
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Combine two quartillos of good milk with two quartillos of custard and four or five tablespoons of powdered sugar
2. Boil until one third has been consumed
3. Add to beaten egg whites from fresh eggs when they are thickened
4. Stir continuously over the fire
5. After it has come to a boil a few times, add one tablespoon of orange-flower water
6. Season and serve cold
