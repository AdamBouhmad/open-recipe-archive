## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp portions
- Small chorizos
- Lard
- Salt
- Small whole onions or quarters of large onions
- Wine
- Water
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon, ground
- Sugar (if using non-sweet wine)
- Flour browned in lard

## Directions

1. Fry lamb portions and small chorizos in the pot with lard and salt
2. Add small whole onions or quarters of large onions and fry them as well
3. Add wine and a little water with ground cloves, black pepper, and cinnamon
4. If the wine is sweet like Málaga wine, do not add sugar; if it is another type, add a small quantity
5. Cover the pot for the meat to cook
6. If necessary, thicken the broth with flour browned in lard
