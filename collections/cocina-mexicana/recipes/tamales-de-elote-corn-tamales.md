## Ingredients

- Tender corn ears
- Corn leaves
- Anise water
- Masa (corn dough)
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Select the most tender corn ears and remove the heads with a knife
2. Separate the best leaves
3. Cut the kernels and grind them well on the metate
4. Lower the ground corn from the metate with a little milk
5. Add sugar and salt to taste
6. Heat the mixture until it reaches the bottom of the pot
7. Cover the corn leaves that have been previously boiled in anise water with this atole
8. Place them carefully in a cazuela so they do not spill
9. Place the cazuela over a pot of boiling water so they coagulate
10. Serve hot
