## Ingredients

- one quarter of vinegar
- parsley, chopped
- shallots, chopped
- gherkins, chopped
- capers
- grapes or Corinth raisins
- pepper
- chili in powder
- nutmeg
- a little gelatin of meat

## Directions

1. Put into one quarter of vinegar chopped parsley, shallots, gherkins, capers, grapes or Corinth raisins, pepper, chili powder, nutmeg, and a little meat gelatin
2. Let everything boil
