## Ingredients

- Green chiles
- Epazote
- Green squash leaves
- Atole
- Salt
- Fresh corn kernels
- Corn in slices

## Directions

1. Grind green chiles with epazote and two or three green squash leaves
2. Mix this with the atole and sufficient salt
3. Add fresh corn kernels and corn slices
