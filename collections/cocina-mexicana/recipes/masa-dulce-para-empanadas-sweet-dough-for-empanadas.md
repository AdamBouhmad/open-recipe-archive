## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- 3 eggs
- sugar, tamised
- salt
- water
- lard, melted and cold

## Directions

1. Place two piles of flour on a surface with one pound each
2. Make holes in the middle of each for ingredients
3. In one heap place three eggs, a little tamised sugar, salt, and water sufficient for all to incorporate
4. In the other, only melted and cold lard
5. After well mixing the materials of each heap, unite the two and knead, adding lard until the dough becomes smooth
6. Extend with a rolling pin and cut the empanadas with a knife
7. Proceed as described in the previous articles
