## Ingredients

- lamprey
- bread crumbs
- egg
- salt
- pepper
- sauce à la tártara

## Directions

1. After the lamprey has been placed in water about to boil to remove the mud or slime, empty it through the gills, carefully removing the head near the knot so that the tail can be inserted there
2. Roll in breadcrumbs
3. Coat with beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Bread again with breadcrumbs
5. A quarter hour or about half an hour before serving, place the lamprey on the grill over a gentle fire, covered with a well-heated bell-shaped oven
6. Pour sauce à la tártara on a plate and place the lamprey on top
