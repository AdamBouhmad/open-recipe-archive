## Ingredients

- 2 pounds very ripe sour cherries
- 3 cuartillos (approximately 1.5 liters) water
- 8 ounces sugar
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Select 2 pounds of very ripe sour cherries, remove the pits and stems, and crush them in a mortar
2. Place the crushed cherries in a pot with 3 cuartillos of water, 8 ounces of sugar, and the juice of 1 lemon
3. Mix everything together with a spoon, strain through a sieve, and place in the ice garrafa (freezing vessel) or molds without filling completely
