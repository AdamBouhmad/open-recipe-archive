## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the artichokes into four parts and remove the skin, fry them in a little butter, rolling them first with fine salt.
2. Let them cook over two gentle fires.
3. When they are cooked and have taken on good color, place them on the plate with the head pointing upward, pouring over them afterwards the butter in which they cooked.
