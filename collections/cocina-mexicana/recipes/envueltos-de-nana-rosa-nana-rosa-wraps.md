## Ingredients

- Masa (corn dough)
- Flour
- Lard
- Picadillo (minced meat)
- Egg yolk
- Green or red chile
- Chorizos
- Longaniza
- Ribs
- Cheese or quesadillas

## Directions

1. Once the masa is ground, add a little flour and some lard and mix.
2. Make the tortillas and while raw, add the picadillo.
3. Brush the tortilla edge with a little egg yolk and wrap them.
4. Add them to very hot lard so they brown.
5. Place them on a platter and cover with fried green or red chile.
6. On top, add chorizos, longaniza, ribs, and slices of cheese or quesadillas.
