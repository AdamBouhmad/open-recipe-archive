## Ingredients

- butter
- ground cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter very well with cold water
2. Mix it with ground cinnamon and knead it with sugar, wetting the hand in cold water if it becomes hot
3. Once the dough is good, extract it through a syringe and go pouring it into cold water
4. Remove it from the water afterwards to form the rings, which are placed on papers or leaves with ground sugar on top
