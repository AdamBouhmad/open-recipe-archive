## Ingredients

- One fat small turkey
- Liver
- Truffles
- Parsley
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ham
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Choose a plump bird, singe it over the flame and empty it
2. Make a forcemeat with liver, truffles, parsley, onions, salt, pepper and ham, all chopped, bound with egg yolks
3. Fill the bird with this forcemeat, sewing it so the filling does not escape
4. Bard with ham and cook on the spit
