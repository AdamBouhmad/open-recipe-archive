## Ingredients

- six ounces of marshmallow root
- four pounds of water
- six pounds of sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook six ounces of marshmallow root in four pounds of water to extract the mucilage
2. Strain and mix in six pounds of sugar
3. Cook until it reaches the consistency of syrup, or if it is not to be used soon, better to cook it until the caramel point so it does not ferment
4. When it is to be used, dissolve it in orange blossom water
