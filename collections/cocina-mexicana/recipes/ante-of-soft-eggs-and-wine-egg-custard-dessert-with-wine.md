## Ingredients

- Medium-point syrup
- Egg yolks
- White wine
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Raisins
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a medium-point syrup and set aside a portion to sprinkle the sponge cake
2. Mix egg yolks with the remaining syrup
3. Place over heat until it reaches a not-too-high point
4. Pour over sponge cake sprinkled with the reserved syrup and white wine
5. Stud the top layer with almonds, pine nuts and raisins, dusted with ground cinnamon
