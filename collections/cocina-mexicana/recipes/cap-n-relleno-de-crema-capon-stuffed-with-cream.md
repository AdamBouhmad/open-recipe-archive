## Ingredients

- capon, roasted
- butter
- bread crumbs cooked with milk
- beef fat
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- bread crumbs for coating

## Directions

1. Remove the breast after roasting and mince it, mixing with a sauce made of bread crumbs cooked with milk, one quarter beef fat, parsley, onions, and mushrooms, all minced very fine and fried in butter, salt, pepper, and three egg yolks.
2. Stuff the capon with this forcemeat and reshape the removed breast with the same mixture.
3. Lightly coat with bread crumbs, brown with melted butter, and coat a second time with bread crumbs.
4. Brown under a camp oven at two fires.
5. Serve with one of the spicy sauces.
