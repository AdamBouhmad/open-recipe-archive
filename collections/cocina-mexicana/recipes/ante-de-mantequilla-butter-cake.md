## Ingredients

- thick slices of sponge cake
- very sweet milk
- chicken breasts
- cream from milk
- pastille
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- citron

## Directions

1. Make thick slices of sponge cake and moisten them with very sweet milk
2. Roast chicken breasts and chop them finely
3. On the sponge cake place a layer of milk cream and another of the chicken
4. The cream should be sautéed between the chicken layers
5. Sprinkle pastille on top and place over two fires until a crust forms
6. Finish by adorning with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and citron
