## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Pig liver
- Bread crumb, soaked in broth
- Fine herbs
- Egg yolks
- Spices
- Pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as previously mentioned.
2. Garnish it with a filling composed of pig liver and an equal quantity of bread crumb soaked in broth and dried afterward, of fine herbs, egg yolks and spices, all well mashed and mixed.
3. Place it on the roasting spit and serve with pepper sauce.
