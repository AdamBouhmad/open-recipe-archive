## Ingredients

- 1 and a half pounds of flour
- 2 ounces of ground sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 ounce of good oil
- 1 ounce of lard
- ground saffron for coloring
- necessary water
- 1 pound of sifted sugar (for icing)
- 3 egg whites, well beaten (for icing)
- juice of half a lemon (for icing)
- small candies (optional)

## Directions

1. To 1 and a half pounds of flour add 2 ounces of ground sugar, 1 egg yolk, 1 ounce of good oil, 1 ounce of lard, and saffron for coloring
2. Add necessary water and make the dough
3. Make pastries as described in previous recipes
4. For icing, if desired: make an icing with 1 pound of sifted sugar, 3 well-beaten egg whites, and the juice of half a lemon
5. Mix all ingredients together and brush onto pastries with feathers before putting in the oven
6. After baking, if desired, sprinkle with small candies and return to the oven for a short time
