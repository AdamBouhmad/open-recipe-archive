## Ingredients

- toasted sesame
- almonds
- Castilian walnuts
- crumb of bread
- water
- oil
- vinegar
- mustard
- oregano in powder
- salt
- oil
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind toasted sesame, almonds, walnuts, and moistened and squeezed bread crumb
2. Add water to make the sauce and bring to boiling
3. Beat well
4. Fry in a little oil
5. When it has boiled, add vinegar to taste, a little washed and ground mustard, and powdered oregano
6. Just before removing from heat, add salt and stir well so it does not break
7. Line the platter with this sauce
8. Place the fish on top and bathe with the same
9. Add raw oil, vinegar, olives, and other garnishes
