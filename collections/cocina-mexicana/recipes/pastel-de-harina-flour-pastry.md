## Ingredients

- half a pound of lard, well washed and beaten
- two egg yolks
- four ounces of well ground sugar
- salt (a pinch as much as can be taken with three fingers)
- one pound of flour
- three drops of oil
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound of lard and beat by hand until very light and full of air
2. Remove all the water
3. Add two egg yolks, four ounces of well ground sugar, salt very finely ground, and one pound of flour
4. Knead so it becomes smooth
5. Add three drops of oil
6. Place in a buttered pan and fry small tortillas until well covered
7. Cover with a griddle with fire on top
8. Once cooked, place over two slow fires
9. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top
