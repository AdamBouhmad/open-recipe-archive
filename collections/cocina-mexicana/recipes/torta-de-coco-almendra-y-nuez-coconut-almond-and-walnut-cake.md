## Ingredients

- One coconut
- Two pounds of sugar
- Twelve egg yolks
- Two dozen walnuts
- Four ounces of almonds

## Directions

1. Clean and grind the coconut
2. Dissolve it in clarified syrup of the proper consistency, along with four ounces of almonds that have also been ground
3. Put it on the heat until it becomes somewhat thick, then remove it and let it cool
4. Mix the paste with two dozen cleaned and ground walnuts and twelve beaten egg yolks
5. After everything is well incorporated, put it on the heat until it thickens a little more, remove it, let it cool, pour it into a platter, and apply fire above it until the cake browns well
