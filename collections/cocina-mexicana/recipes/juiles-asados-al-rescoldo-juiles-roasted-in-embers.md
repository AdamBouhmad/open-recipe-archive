## Ingredients

- juiles
- roasted garlic
- dried ancho chiles
- lard
- salt
- epazote
- cheese
- breadcrumbs
- corn husks
- vinegar
- oil
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the juiles, leaving the roe
2. Make a sauce of roasted garlic, soaked and deveined ground ancho chiles, melted lard and salt
3. Fill the fish with epazote, cheese and breadcrumbs
4. Coat with the made sauce
5. Cover with soaked corn husks
6. Place in embers or over a gentle fire and roast
7. When cooked, serve alone or with vinegar, oil and chopped onion
