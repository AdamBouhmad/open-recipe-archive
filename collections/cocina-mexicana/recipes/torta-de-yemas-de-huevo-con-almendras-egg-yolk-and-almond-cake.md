## Ingredients

- four ounces of almonds
- syrup
- twenty egg yolks
- ten egg whites, beaten
- a little cinnamon
- desired seasonings
- dragées
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Pound four ounces of almonds and add them to a little syrup with twenty egg yolks and ten beaten egg whites, a little cinnamon and desired seasonings
2. Cook, stirring constantly with a spoon
3. As the cake begins to set, press it with the same spoon to give it its form
4. Decorate the top with dragées and toasted sesame
