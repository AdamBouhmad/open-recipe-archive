## Ingredients

- beef leg, cut into small pieces
- bay leaves
- bacon, chopped (half fat, half lean)
- garlic
- pepper
- oil
- sufficient broth
- salt
- spices
- parsley
- tomato

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of beef leg into small pieces
2. Place them in a pot with salt and bay leaves
3. Chop bacon (half fat, half lean), fry it and add to the pot
4. Grind garlic with pepper, dissolve in a little water and add to the stew
5. Add oil and reheat gently
6. When convenient, add sufficient broth and season with salt, spices, parsley and tomato
