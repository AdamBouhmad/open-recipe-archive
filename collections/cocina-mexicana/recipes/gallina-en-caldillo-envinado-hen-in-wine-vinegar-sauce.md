## Ingredients

- garlic cloves, minced
- lard
- ground tomato
- toasted bread
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- hen
- water
- vinegar
- saffron, ground
- sugar
- Parras wine or Málaga wine
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Brown the minced garlic cloves in lard
2. In the same lard, fry the ground tomato mixed with a piece of toasted bread, clove, cinnamon and pepper
3. Place the hen that has been roasted raw into this mixture
4. Add equal quantities of water and vinegar, a little ground saffron, a piece of sugar, Parras or Málaga wine and toasted sesame
5. Let the hen cook well
6. When the wine-vinegar sauce has regular consistency, remove from heat and serve
