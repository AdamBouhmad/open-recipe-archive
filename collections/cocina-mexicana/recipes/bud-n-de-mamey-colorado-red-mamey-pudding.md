## Ingredients

- red mamey, filaments and skin removed
- four eggs
- panetela
- sugar
- salt
- clove
- spices
- cinnamon
- milk

## Directions

1. Remove the filaments and skin from the mamey
2. Knead the pulp with four eggs, panetela, sugar, a little salt, clove, spices, cinnamon and necessary milk
3. Form a small dough, place it in a pudding mold previously coated with butter so that the pudding does not stick
4. Cook and then sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
