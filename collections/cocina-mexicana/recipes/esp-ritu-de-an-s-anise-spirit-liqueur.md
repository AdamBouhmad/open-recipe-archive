## Ingredients

- Half a pound of anise
- Half a pound of angelica seeds
- Four pounds of common spirit of wine
- Two pounds of river water

## Directions

1. Crush half a pound of anise and half a pound of angelica seeds
2. Leave them to macerate for several hours in four pounds of common spirit of wine
3. Add two pounds of river water
4. Pour everything into the still for distillation
5. Collect four pounds of liquor from the distillation
