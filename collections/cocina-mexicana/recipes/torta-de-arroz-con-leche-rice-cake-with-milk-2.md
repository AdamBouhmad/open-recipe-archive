## Ingredients

- whole rice
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- egg whites
- egg yolks
- lard
- cinnamon
- minced meat
- hard-boiled eggs
- olives
- chile peppers
- capers
- onion

## Directions

1. Obtain whole rice and after washing and soaking it very well, dry it well in the sun
2. After drying, cook it in sufficient milk with necessary salt
3. If the milk is consumed or thickens too much while cooking, moisten with a little more and let it boil until the rice is cooked
4. Once cooked, add powdered sugar and stir, adding enough so the sweetness stands out
5. Remove from heat, set aside, and the next day beat egg whites until they have thickened well, then add the yolks and mix with the rice and milk, dusting with a little cinnamon
6. Grease a mold with plenty of lard and put in a thick layer of the rice and egg mixture
7. Next put another layer of minced meat made for filling chiles, adorning with slices of hard-boiled egg, olives, chile peppers, capers, and thin slices of onion head
8. Put on top another very thin layer of rice with egg, and another thick layer of minced meat, finishing filling the mold with a thick layer of the remains of rice and egg
9. Put the cake to set over two gentle heats, adding lard from time to time on the sides, and brushing a little on top with a feather
10. When well set, dust with dragées or sugar
