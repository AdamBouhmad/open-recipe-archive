## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- eggs
- bread

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk, strain and boil with cinnamon sticks
2. While boiling, break the eggs into it, frying them by spooning the same milk over them so the white coagulates over the yolk and the yolk remains covered
3. Remove the eggs and pour the milk over bread prepared for the soup
4. Boil until it reaches a good temperature
5. Serve dusted with cinnamon and decorated with the fried eggs
