## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 tomatoes, cut in quarters
- 5 onions, sliced
- parsley
- thyme
- 1 clove
- 4 ounces of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in a saucepan
2. Let everything boil together, taking care that it does not stick
3. After boiling for three quarters of an hour, pass through a hair sieve
4. Taste to see if it has sufficient salt
5. This sauce serves equally well for meat, vegetables, and fish
