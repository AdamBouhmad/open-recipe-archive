## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces the same way as for marmalade
2. Put to cook and the next day put a few in a cloth
3. Squeeze and continue doing this with the rest
4. Once squeezed, measure: for three cuartillos of jelly one pound of sugar
5. The syrup point the same as marmalade; at this point add the jelly
6. When it thickens on the spoon, if it rises remove from heat and fan with a fan
7. Remove the foam with a spoon, then pour out
