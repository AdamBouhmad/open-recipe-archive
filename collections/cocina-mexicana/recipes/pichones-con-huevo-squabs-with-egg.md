## Ingredients

- Lard
- Chopped garlic
- Ham, sliced
- Chorizo
- Vinegar
- Water
- Ground clove
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Capers
- Two or three cooked egg yolks
- Bread slices, toasted and fried
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Place chopped garlic and lard in a casserole
2. When the garlic is fried, add sliced ham and chorizo
3. When the meat is fried, add vinegar and water
4. Season with ground clove, cinnamon, pepper, and capers mixed with two or three cooked egg yolks
5. Grind well-toasted and fried bread slices and add to the meat
6. The broth should be thick
7. When bringing to the table, add a little oil
8. Garnish with chiles and olives
