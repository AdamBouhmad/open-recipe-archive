## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of lard
- five egg yolks
- one cup of salted water
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and egg yolks with the lard
2. Beat a lot
3. Add the salted water and continue beating until it puffs up and forms eyes
4. Add flour
5. Place the dough on a board over paper
6. Roll with a rolling pin
7. Form the biscuits with a mold
8. Pick with tweezers
9. Wrap in papers to take to the oven
