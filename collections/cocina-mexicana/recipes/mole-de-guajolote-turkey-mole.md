## Ingredients

- 1 large turkey
- 1.5 pounds chile; 1 pound mulato and half pound composed of pasilla and wide chile in equal parts
- Colored tomatoes
- Seeds of the three chiles
- A little roasted cilantro or sesame
- Toasted anise
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- 1 small garlic head
- Cooked peas cut into quarters
- Salt

## Directions

1. Grind everything very finely
2. Clean the turkey and parboil it with the cooked peas cut into quarters
3. Season to taste
