## Ingredients

- oysters
- lemon juice
- tomato paste
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Give the oysters a boil in a pan, drain them, and place in lemon juice.
2. Drain again, coat in tomato paste with salt and pepper, and fry with parsley.
