## Ingredients

- calabacitas
- pepper powder
- beaten eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked calabacitas
2. Add pepper powder
3. Beat six or seven eggs so they rise well
4. Pour these and the calabacitas into a pan with hot lard
5. Let cook and set the cake carefully so it does not burn
