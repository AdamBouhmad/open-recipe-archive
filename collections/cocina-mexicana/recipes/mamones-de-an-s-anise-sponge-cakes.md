## Ingredients

- eleven eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately then together
- one pound of sifted sugar
- ten ounces of sifted starch
- a little anise

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of eleven eggs separately until they harden, then together; mix in one pound of sifted sugar and beat again until the mixture becomes white and thick; then add ten ounces of sifted starch and a little anise, beating only so the starch incorporates; pour the mixture into boxes, taking care that they do not fill, and place in the oven for the mamones to cook, which can be served as is; but they are better if sliced or cut to the desired size, they are covered with the frosting from the puchas (see embetunadas puchas), and returned to the oven for a short while so the crust dries.
