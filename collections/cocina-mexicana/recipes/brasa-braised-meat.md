## Ingredients

- A piece of meat
- Slices of fat or ham, or meat
- A small assorted bunch of herbs
- Parsnips or xitomate
- Onions
- Spices
- One glass of white wine
- One glass of broth

## Directions

1. Cover the piece of meat you want to cook with slices of fat or ham, or meat
2. Add a small assorted bunch of herbs, parsnips or xitomate, onions and spices, with one glass of white wine and another of broth, according to the size of the piece to be cooked and bathed
