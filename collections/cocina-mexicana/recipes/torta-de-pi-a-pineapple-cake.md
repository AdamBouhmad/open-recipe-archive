## Ingredients

- pineapple
- salt water
- mamón, crumbled
- sugar
- spice pastille
- cinnamon
- eggs, beaten
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Slice the pineapple and soak it in salt water to remove its harshness
2. Wash it, grind it and mix with crumbled mamón, sugar, spice pastille and cinnamon, all ground
3. Add to beaten eggs as needed and incorporate well
4. Pour into a tortera or cazuela coated with butter
5. Cook the cake over two fires, and afterwards dust with ground sugar and cinnamon
