## Ingredients

- common bread roll
- pickled chiles
- olives
- aged cheese, crumbled
- oil
- salt
- dried oregano

## Directions

1. Split a common bread roll in half
2. Place pickled chiles with their stems removed and olives inside
3. Cover everything with crumbled aged cheese
4. Soak with oil and dust with salt and dried oregano
5. Join the two halves together and press firmly with the shopkeeper's press to incorporate everything well
