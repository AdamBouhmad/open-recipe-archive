## Ingredients

- one arroba of pork meat, one-eighth of which will be fat
- four ounces of pepper
- half ounce of clove
- half ounce of cinnamon
- two ounces of anise
- half ounce of cilantro
- salt

## Directions

1. Finely chop the meat and grind the spices separately (except the anise, which after toasting and cleaning is added whole to the chopped mixture)
2. Place each ingredient in its paper and mix with the meat half of each one with the corresponding salt so the mixture is well seasoned
3. Knead the meat, then add the other half of the spices that had remained and knead again
4. Set aside a small portion, fry it in a pan to test its seasoning, and if it lacks salt or needs more spice, add what is necessary to the whole mass of sausage
5. After filling the intestines, place them to cook in a pot with two or three
