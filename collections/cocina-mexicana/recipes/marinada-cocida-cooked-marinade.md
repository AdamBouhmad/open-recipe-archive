## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- three carrots
- four onions
- two bay leaves
- a little thyme
- two cloves
- sprigs of parsley
- small bulb onions
- two spoonfuls of flour
- one glass of vinegar
- two glasses of broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place butter in a pot over the fire with three sliced carrots and four sliced onions, two bay leaves, a little thyme and two cloves
2. Fry the root vegetables
3. Add sprigs of parsley and some small bulb onions, which are also partially fried or macerated
4. Add two spoonfuls of flour and stir with the butter
5. Add one glass of vinegar and two glasses of broth with the corresponding salt and pepper
6. Let everything simmer over a gentle fire for about an hour
7. After that time, pass the marinade through a sieve to use as needed
