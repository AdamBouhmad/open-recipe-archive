## Ingredients

- cooked cauliflower
- salt from the earth
- cheese
- breadcrumbs
- egg
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with salt from the earth
2. Separate the small stalks
3. Open them in half and fill with cheese and breadcrumbs, or any other chopped mixture
4. Join the two layers of the stalk
5. Press with a clean napkin
6. Dip in beaten egg, fry and serve to the table, or serve dry sprinkled with salt and pepper, or stew in adobo or spiced broth
