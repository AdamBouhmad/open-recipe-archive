## Ingredients

- cooked cauliflower
- salt from the earth
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- garlic
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. After cooking the cauliflower with salt, divide the small stalks
2. Dry them well
3. Roll in breadcrumbs
4. Add salt and pepper
5. Heat a pan with oil over the fire
6. Fry some minced garlic cloves
7. When they begin to brown, add the cauliflower stalks
8. Place on a platter and sprinkle grated cheese on top
9. Serve as is or in tomato broth or in adobo
