## Ingredients

- pork meat
- rice
- sugar
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- eggs
- butter
- sesame seeds
- tomatoes
- onions
- chorizo
- ham
- olives
- chile strips

## Directions

1. Mince the pork and cook as needed for filling.
2. Tie a little rice loosely in a napkin so it remains loose, and cook until it has swelled and filled the space in the napkin.
3. Remove, squeeze out, and let cool.
4. Grind sugar, clove, cinnamon, and saffron, and mix with beaten eggs in the necessary amount, mixing with the rice.
5. Pour everything into a pan lined with butter over layers of meat, setting aside a little of the mixture beforehand to place on top with toasted sesame seeds.
6. Cook the cake at two heats and serve with a sauce made of tomatoes, onions, chorizo, fried ham, olives, and chile strips.
