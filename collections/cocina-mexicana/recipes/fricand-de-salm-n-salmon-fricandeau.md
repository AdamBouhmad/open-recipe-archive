## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Ham lard

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into slices
2. Lard the slices
3. Cook the same way as the veal lardrecilla from the previous article, following the same procedures until finished
4. Serve with tomato sauce, pepper sauce, or Italian sauce
