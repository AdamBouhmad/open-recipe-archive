## Ingredients

- veal tripe
- chicken pieces
- pork loin with fat
- ham
- remaining tripe pieces
- pig feet
- chorizos
- chorizo sausages
- longaniza sausage
- garlic heads
- onion heads
- dried ancho chiles
- fine salt
- lard
- dried pork cecina
- thread for sewing
- lime (for cleaning)

## Directions

1. Open the veal tripe only as much as necessary to clean it well; wash with all its offal as described in the previous method.
2. Fill with the following: pieces of chicken and pork loin with its fat, ham, all remaining tripe pieces, pig feet, chorizos, large chorizos, longaniza, garlic heads, onion heads, dried ancho chiles, sufficient fine salt, plenty of lard, and pieces of dried pork cecina.
3. Mix everything well and fill the tripe.
4. Sew the opening well with strong thread and add backstitching so that the lard broth does not leak out.
5. Once well sealed, place the tripe in a wetted earthenware pot.
6. Dig a sufficiently deep hole and make a good fire of dry wood in it that will burn until the earth is hot like fire.
7. Empty the fire, place the pot in the hole, cover with a little earth, then add hot coals, and complete the covering with the excavated earth.
8. Place fire on top and after thirteen hours, remove the pot.
9. Open the tripe and serve with salt and pepper, adding competent garnish.
