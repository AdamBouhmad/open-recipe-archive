## Ingredients

- chicken
- oil
- salt
- lemon juice or vinegar
- green chile
- onions
- parsley
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with oil, salt, lemon juice or vinegar, green chile, onions and chopped parsley.
2. Once they have absorbed the substance of these ingredients, drain well and fry in oil.
3. Serve with a sauce of liquid javerine, salt, pepper, lemon wheels and very finely chopped parsley.
