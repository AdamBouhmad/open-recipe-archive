## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- pot broth
- nutmeg
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a lamb loin into thin slices and sprinkle salt and pepper on top
2. Fry well and place on a plate, covering with butter on top
3. Put a little flour in a saucepan with pot broth and a little nutmeg
4. Let cook stirring continuously
5. Add the slices, stir again, and serve garnished with fried parsley
