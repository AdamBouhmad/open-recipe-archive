## Ingredients

- sardines
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano powder
- roasted poblano chiles, shredded
- avocado slices
- capers
- olives
- cured small chiles
- roasted and ground tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry the sardines as described in the previous recipe
2. Cover the fried sardines in the serving dish with a sauce made from roasted and ground tomatoes combined with salt, oil, vinegar, powdered oregano, roasted and shredded poblano chiles, avocado slices, capers, olives and cured small chiles
