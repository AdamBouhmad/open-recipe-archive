## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- sixteen egg yolks
- one caldero cup of flour dissolved in milk
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Mix eight cuartillos of sweetened milk with sixteen egg yolks and one caldero cup of flour dissolved in milk
2. Strain and place over heat
3. Add cinnamon sticks, stirring to prevent sticking
4. Allow to reach the proper point
