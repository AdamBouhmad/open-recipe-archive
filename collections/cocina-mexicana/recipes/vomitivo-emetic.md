## Ingredients

- one scruple ipecac powder
- one ounce water
- one dram simple syrup
- optional: half grain or one grain of tartar emetic
- alternative: ten drams ipecac wine or half ounce of this mixed with equal quantity of scilla or sea onion syrup

## Directions

1. Mix the ipecac powder, water, and simple syrup
2. Those needing a stronger emetic may add half grain or one grain of tartar emetic
3. Those not wishing to use powders may take ten drams of ipecac wine or half ounce of this in equal quantity of scilla or sea onion syrup
