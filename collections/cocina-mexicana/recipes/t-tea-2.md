## Ingredients

- Water
- Tea

## Directions

1. Boil the water and after it has boiled for two minutes, remove it in the teapot to the table
2. There infuse the tea and after three minutes pour it into the cup, where it is sweetened
3. This way it does not lose its properties or aroma
