## Ingredients

- One and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar
- one quartillo of virgin honey
- a pinch of flavoring and sugar water
- one pound of hardtack biscuit or white bread biscuit, crushed
- one pound of fresh walnuts, shelled, peeled and ground
- half pound of shelled and toasted almonds ground with the walnuts
- half pound of toasted sesame
- half ounce of clove
- half ounce of cinnamon
- a small piece of ginger
- a small piece of nutmeg, ground to powder
- pieces of toasted walnut and almond
- raisins
- pine nuts
- preserved cherries
- grapes
- capulines
- preserved citron strips
- preserved fig
- slices of dried plantain

## Directions

1. Combine one and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar with one quartillo of virgin honey and bring to ball stage in water
2. Add a pinch of flavoring and sugar water
3. Add one pound of crushed hardtack biscuit or white bread biscuit and one pound of ground fresh shelled walnuts
4. Mix in half pound of shelled and toasted almonds ground with the walnuts
5. Add half pound of toasted sesame, half ounce of clove, half ounce of cinnamon, a small piece of ginger and a small piece of ground nutmeg
6. Place over heat, stir constantly and cook until it reaches the proper stage, then remove from heat
7. Stir in pieces of toasted walnut and almond, raisins, pine nuts, preserved cherries, grapes, capulines, preserved citron strips, preserved fig and slices of dried plantain
