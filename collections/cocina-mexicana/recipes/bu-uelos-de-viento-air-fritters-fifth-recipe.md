## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the flour as for poleadas, and once it is a little thick, add spoonfuls of butter
2. Beat the necessary eggs, mix them with the poleadas and little by little add them to fry, the butter should not be very hot
