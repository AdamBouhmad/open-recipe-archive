## Ingredients

- kidneys
- bread
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Boil the kidneys
2. When they begin to boil, drain the water and pour hot water over them
3. Let them cook and when done, slice them
4. Fry some rounds of bread and grind them with three cloves, three peppers, and a little cinnamon
5. Fry everything in lard and add a little water and the kidneys
6. Add salt and vinegar and let it season
