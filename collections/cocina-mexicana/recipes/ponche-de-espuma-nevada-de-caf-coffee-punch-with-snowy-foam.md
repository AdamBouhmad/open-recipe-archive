## Ingredients

- Four ounces of coffee
- Six egg yolks, very fresh
- Four cuartillos of double cream from milk
- Twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- Three cups of strong coffee

## Directions

1. Make coffee with four ounces, made as strong as possible, and filter it through a cloth or funnel
2. Dissolve six very fresh egg yolks in four cuartillos of double cream from milk with twelve ounces of powdered sugar
3. Add the coffee to this mixture, of which three cups suffice, making a little more or less than a cuartillo, as long as it is well strong
4. If it lacks sweetness, add sugar
5. Proceed as explained in the previous article for making the foam
