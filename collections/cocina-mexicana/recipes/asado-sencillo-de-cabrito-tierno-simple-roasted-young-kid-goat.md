## Ingredients

- young kid goat
- garlic cloves
- lard
- salt
- water
- clove
- pepper
- curly celery
- roman lettuce quarters
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard until browned, then remove from the pan
2. Add young kid goat divided into regular pieces to the lard
3. Blanch the meat over low heat in the lard with necessary salt
4. Once blanched, add a small amount of water, which should be consumed when the meat finishes cooking
5. Add the fried garlic ground with clove and pepper
6. Once the broth is consumed and the meat is cooked, leave it to fry in the same grease so it becomes somewhat toasted but not burned
7. When serving, garnish with small pieces of curly celery, quarters of roman lettuce, small chiles and olives
