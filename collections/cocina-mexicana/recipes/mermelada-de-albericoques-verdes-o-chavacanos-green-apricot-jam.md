## Ingredients

- Green apricots
- Water
- Sugar equal in weight to the prepared jam
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Rub the green apricots with cloth to remove the fuzz
2. Cook in water until very tender
3. Remove from water and place in cold water and drain
4. Crush and pass through a sieve
5. Place this mass over the fire to dry, turning constantly until it begins to stick to the pan
6. Weigh the jam and put an equal amount of sugar on the fire with a quarter liter of water
7. Bring to boil and skim, letting it boil until it has the perfect breaking point in water
8. Dissolve the jam in the syrup without boiling and pour into jars
