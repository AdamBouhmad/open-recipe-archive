## Ingredients

- 1 quartillo of liqueur
- 3 quartillos of water
- juice of 6 lemons
- 4 egg whites
- 10 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Pass through a sieve the liqueur, water, lemon juice, egg whites, and sugar
2. Let it rest for a while
3. Freeze in the same manner as sorbets
