## Ingredients

- Hares
- oil
- salt
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives
- caperberries
- oregano

## Directions

1. Roast the hares raw, fry them in oil and season with salt, adding a little vinegar
2. To serve, add oil and vinegar and decorate with small chiles, olives, caperberries and oregano
