## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of white sugar
- one coconut
- nine egg yolks
- one quarter of a cuartillo of white wine

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup made with the sugar and strain through a cloth.
2. Put it on the fire to give one boil.
3. Remove the brown shell from the coconut, grate it, and add it to the syrup to give several boils, then remove from heat and let cool.
4. Add the nine egg yolks without beating and stir until well incorporated.
5. Add the white wine and return to heat, letting it reach the point where it separates from the pan.
6. Pour onto a platter and place a griddle with heat on top to brown it.
