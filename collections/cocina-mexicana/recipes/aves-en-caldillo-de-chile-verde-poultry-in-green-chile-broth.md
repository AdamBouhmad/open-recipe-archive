## Ingredients

- green chile
- tomato
- garlic
- almond shells
- bread
- poultry broth
- oil
- vinegar
- pickled chiles
- olives
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop green chile, tomato and garlic very finely
2. Fry almond shells with a piece of well-toasted bread and grind them
3. Fry the ground mixture again and add the chopped tomato, chile and garlic
4. When everything is well fried, add poultry broth and let it boil until very thick
5. Once thick, add oil, vinegar, pickled chiles chopped, olives and the corresponding salt
6. Boil a little more and remove from heat, pouring over cooked or roasted poultry
