## Ingredients

- One quart of milk
- salt
- pepper
- butter kneaded with flour
- two sliced onions
- parsley root
- parsley bundle
- cloves
- garlic head
- bay leaf
- bread crumbs
- melted butter
- French mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Put in a pot one quart of milk, salt, pepper, butter kneaded with flour, two sliced onions, a parsley root, a small bundle of parsley, some cloves, a garlic head and a bay leaf
2. Stir everything until it boils
3. Place the skate slices in it and let them cook on a slow fire
4. Remove them and roll in bread crumbs
5. Bathe with melted butter, bread them again and grill them
6. Serve with French mustard sauce
