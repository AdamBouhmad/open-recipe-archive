## Ingredients

- veal loin
- salt
- cumin
- clove
- pepper
- garlic
- vinegar
- lard
- ham slices
- chorizo
- sausage
- chile peppers
- tomatoes
- onion
- poblano or cuaresmeño chiles
- sour orange juice
- thyme
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Make short incisions along the loin at short intervals, but so they do not pierce or divide it
2. Season these cuts on all sides with salt, cumin, clove, pepper and garlic all ground, and a little vinegar
3. Let the loin rest until the next day
4. Place in a pot with lard and fry without opening but keeping it compact in a deep and compact pot
5. Fry ham slices, sliced chorizo and chopped sausage along with the loin
6. After frying, add chopped garlic, roasted and ground tomatoes, onion slices and poblano or cuaresmeño chiles split
7. Add sufficient water, sour orange juice, thyme, two or three bay leaves
