## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- onions and carrots, cut in proportionate quantity
- broth
- bouquet of fine herbs

## Directions

1. Sauté onions and carrots cut in a pot with butter or lard in proportionate quantity.
2. After some time, add broth, putting in a small bouquet of fine herbs.
3. Let it simmer for a couple of hours over very low heat.
4. Pass through a sieve, season, and serve hot.
