## Ingredients

- Cooked sea bass
- Oil
- Garlic
- Onion
- Parsley
- Flour
- Oil or lard for frying

## Directions

1. After the sea bass is cooked, divide into regular portions
2. Fry in oil with ground garlic and onion and chopped parsley
3. When the oil is consumed and the fish portions are dry, roll in flour and fry again in oil or lard
