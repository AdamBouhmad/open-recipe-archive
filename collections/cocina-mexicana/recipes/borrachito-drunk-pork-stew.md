## Ingredients

- pork meat pieces
- salt
- manteca (lard)
- onion
- jitomate (tomato)
- garlic
- parsley
- pulque (fermented maguey beverage)
- water
- chiles in vinegar
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Put pieces of pork meat in a pot with salt and sufficient lard so that the pieces fry raw.
2. Chop onion, jitomate, garlic and parsley and add all of this to the pot.
3. Once everything is well fried, add for each pound of meat one measure of pulque and the same amount of water.
4. Bring everything to a boil until it thickens.
5. Add its corresponding salt.
6. To serve, decorate with chiles in vinegar, capers and olives.
