## Ingredients

- fresh fish
- water
- salt
- onion
- chile, or pepper if chile is not available
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the fresh fish in water with salt, onion, and chile, or pepper if chile is not available
2. Before removing from heat, add oil and vinegar, with oil in greater quantity
