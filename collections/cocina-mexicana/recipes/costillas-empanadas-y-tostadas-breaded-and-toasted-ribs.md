## Ingredients

- ribs
- oil
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- vinegar
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. After marinating in adobo for one or two hours with oil, fine herbs, salt, pepper, the juice of a lemon and a few drops of vinegar
2. Sprinkle with bread crumbs crumbled by hand
3. Toast over low heat
4. Serve with the sauce desired
