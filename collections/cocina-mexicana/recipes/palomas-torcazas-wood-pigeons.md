## Ingredients

- Wood pigeons
- Butter
- Salt
- Roasted hazelnuts, crushed
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Broth from the pot

## Directions

1. Plucked, cleaned and belly removed, sauté them in a casserole with butter and salt.
2. Half way through sautéing add crushed roasted hazelnuts, a little lemon juice, a pinch of pepper, all dissolved with two cups of broth from the pot.
3. Leave on the fire to finish sautéing and take on the flavor of the crushed mixture.
