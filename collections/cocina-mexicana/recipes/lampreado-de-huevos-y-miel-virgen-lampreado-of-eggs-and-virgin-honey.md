## Ingredients

- forty eggs
- four pounds of ground sugar
- pepper
- clove
- one ounce of cinnamon
- one caldron of virgin honey
- sesame
- pastille of scent
- one quarter of water
- one spoonful of butter
- common or market biscuit
- one spoonful of lard
- honey
- vinegar
- wine
- raisins
- almonds
- citron
- sesame
- hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Beat forty eggs with four pounds of ground sugar until they break up, and then add pepper, clove and one ounce of cinnamon, all ground, one caldron of virgin honey, sesame, pastille of scent and one quarter of water so that the dough becomes soft, and stirring everything well, put it in a pan with one spoonful of butter and common or market biscuit, ground, which will be prepared over the fire.
2. When this comes together and acquires the point of detaching from the pan, remove it and let it cool, and make some small cakes or buns that will be put in syrup, composed with one spoonful of lard, honey, vinegar, wine, raisins, almonds, citron, sesame and hazelnuts if desired.
