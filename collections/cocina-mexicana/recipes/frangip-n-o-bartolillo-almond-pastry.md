## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- eggs as needed
- four quartillos of milk
- two ounces of butter
- a little salt
- ten sweet almonds
- two bitter almonds
- three or four macaroons
- a little candied orange blossom in powder
- sugar ground and sifted
- or roasted, cleaned and split peanuts instead of almonds without orange blossom
- or pine nuts, hazelnuts or small walnuts

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of flour in a saucepan and dissolve it with as many eggs as needed, then add four quartillos of milk, two ounces of butter and a little salt
2. Put this mixture on the fire, stirring the paste constantly with a wooden spoon until it has boiled for about a quarter of an hour, taking care that it does not stick
3. When you know that the dough is cooked, remove it and let it cool
4. Split ten sweet almonds, two bitter ones and three or four macaroons, adding a little candied orange blossom in powder, mixing everything with the dough, which is sweetened with ground and sifted sugar
5. Stir and mix well the mixture with the wooden spoon, and form the frangipans or bartolillos, more or less thick, according to the quantity of eggs that you want to add
6. When you want to make them with peanuts, put these toasted, cleaned and split in place of the almonds and omit the orange blossom
7. They can also be made with pine nuts, hazelnuts or small walnuts
