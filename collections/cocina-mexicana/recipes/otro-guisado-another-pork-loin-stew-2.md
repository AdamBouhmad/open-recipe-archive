## Ingredients

- abundant onion, finely minced
- parsley
- ham strips, very thin
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- pork loin
- lard
- tomato, ground without seeds
- water
- Sherry wine
- olives

## Directions

1. Mince abundant onion very fine and some parsley, mix together
2. Cut strips of ham very thin
3. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper
4. Make deep holes in the loin with a knife and fill them with the onion and parsley
5. Dust the ham slices with the spices and place them in the holes
6. Once the entire loin is studded, put lard in a pan and in it the loin to fry
7. Once fried, add abundant ground tomato without the seeds and let it fry again; then add water and when the meat is cooked and the broth thick, sprinkle with Sherry wine and add olives
