## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- sugar
- sliced mamey fruit
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Cook the necessary milk with abundant cinnamon and the corresponding sugar
2. Allow to cool
3. In a large platter, place a layer of mamey slices
4. Bathe them with a spoon using the cooked milk
5. Stud each layer with abundant raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and walnuts
6. Continue placing layers in the same order until the platter is full
7. Place the platter in the oven to cook the pudding perfectly
8. Serve cold
