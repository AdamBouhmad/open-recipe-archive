## Ingredients

- Turbot, prepared as indicated
- One pound of salt
- Water

## Directions

1. After preparing the turbot as indicated in the first article, place about one pound of salt at the bottom of a kettle and fill it with water, giving it two or three boils
2. Remove immediately from the heat, let it rest
3. When it has become lukewarm, pour it over the turbot, being careful not to stir or throw in the pieces
4. Adjust the fish platter over a burner and let the turbot cook over low heat for one hour, without boiling
5. Serve afterwards hot in case it should be eaten thus with oil, or with butter sauce and cold
6. Serve at the same time with a silver spoon, gilded or not, because iron should never touch the turbot
