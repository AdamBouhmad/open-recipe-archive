## Ingredients

- German sauce
- mushrooms
- ground truffles
- beef brains
- beef sweetbreads
- nutmeg
- fresh butter
- cooked cow udder
- frying batter

## Directions

1. Reduce the necessary quantity of German sauce and mix in mushrooms and ground truffles, both cut into dice shapes
2. Continue cooking until reduced to a paste-like consistency
3. Add beef brains and beef sweetbreads cut the same way as the mushrooms and truffles
4. Add nutmeg and a little fresh butter and knead everything together
5. Allow to cool and mold into small sticks about two inches long
6. Cut small, very thin slices of cooked and cold cow udder and wrap the brain sticks in them
7. Coat or cover with frying batter
8. Fry, and serve garnished with fried parsley
