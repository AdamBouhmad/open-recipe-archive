## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- aromatic herbs (sufficient quantity)
- milk (one third to one half of the broth)

## Directions

1. Boil water, salt, and aromatic herbs together
2. Strain and clarify the broth
3. Add one third to one half milk to the broth
4. Place the small, flattened fish in the broth to cook for varying time over very gentle heat
