## Ingredients

- all seasonings
- biscuit
- eggs
- chorizo
- wine
- tomatoes
- bread
- garlic
- almonds
- olives
- raisins
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make hash with all the seasonings, mash biscuit, beat egg and mix with biscuit
2. Place a layer of egg and another of hash, add chorizos
3. Sprinkle with wine and place over two fires
4. For the sauce: grind tomatoes with a small piece of bread and fry with garlic, add almonds, olives and raisins, and powdered cloves and cinnamon
