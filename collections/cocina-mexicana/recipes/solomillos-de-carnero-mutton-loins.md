## Ingredients

- solomillos
- tocino
- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cumin
- parsley in sprigs
- diced onion
- wine
- sugar
- oil
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the solomillos with tocino and marinate for three days with vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, a little cumin, parsley sprigs and diced onion
2. When ready to serve, cook with wine and sugar until well reduced and taking on good color
3. Present hot with a sauce of oil, pepper, salt and vinegar
