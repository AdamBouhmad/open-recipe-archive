## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one cuartillo of juice from ripe lemons

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with three pounds of sugar
2. When it reaches the hard-crack stage, add one cuartillo of juice from ripe lemons
3. Once boiling stops, remove from heat and let cool
4. Pour into bottle
