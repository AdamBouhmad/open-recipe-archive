## Ingredients

- Purslane
- Chorizo
- Ham
- Longaniza (sausage)
- Cooked and peeled chickpeas
- Cooked egg yolks
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Butter
- Beaten egg for coating

## Directions

1. Cook the purslane as those made with jocoqui, but without it, somewhat dry and mixed with chorizo, ham, and longaniza, all cooked and in small pieces
2. Make a paste with cooked and peeled chickpeas and cooked egg yolks, grinding both and incorporating with sugar, cinnamon, and clove, all ground and to taste
3. In a pot greased with butter, put a layer of this paste, another of purslane, and the last of paste, well greased with butter
4. Put the cake to set over two fires and when golden, pour sugar and cinnamon powder over it
