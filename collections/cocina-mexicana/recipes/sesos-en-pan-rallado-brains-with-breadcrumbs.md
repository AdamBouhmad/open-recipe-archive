## Ingredients

- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- clove, ground
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- oil
- lemon juice
- cooked brains, sliced

## Directions

1. Mix breadcrumbs with pepper, ground clove, and salt; mince parsley very finely and mix with the above.
2. Coat a baking dish with butter and sprinkle some of the breadcrumb mixture on it.
3. Place the sliced brains, which should be well cooked, on top.
4. Cover with breadcrumbs and on top place a layer of butter, a little oil, and a few drops of lemon juice.
5. Place the baking dish between two fires so it browns.
