## Ingredients

- thrushes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- juniper berries

## Directions

1. Pluck the thrushes and singe them over flames to remove the down
2. Place them in a pot without emptying them, with butter, salt, pepper, and juniper berries
3. Cook over two fires
4. Arrange on a plate and pour over them the same broth or sauce in which they cooked
