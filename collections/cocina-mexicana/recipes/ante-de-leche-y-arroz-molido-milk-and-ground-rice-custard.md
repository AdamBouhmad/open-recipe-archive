## Ingredients

- rice
- eggs
- milk
- sugar
- sponge cake
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Grind the rice after it is cooked
2. Add eggs and milk cooked with sugar
3. Strain everything well and put over heat until it takes body
4. Lower heat and begin layering one layer of sponge cake and another of rice, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
5. Return to heat to set with a comal so that it forms a crust on top
