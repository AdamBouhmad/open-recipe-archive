## Ingredients

- water
- small firewood
- potatoes
- linen
- butter
- broth
- salt
- bread fried in lard

## Directions

1. Put a little water in a pot and add a little small firewood and make a small lattice of thin boards
2. Add potatoes and cover the pot with linen with a utensil fitted to the mouth on top
3. Put on the fire and when cooked, peel and grind in a metate adding broth
4. Pass through a sieve and put the substance in a pan with butter, adding more broth and seasoning with necessary salt
5. Put on the fire and when it has thickened, serve with toasts of bread fried in lard placed around the plate
