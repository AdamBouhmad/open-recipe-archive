## Ingredients

- two calf's brains
- broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- herb bouquet
- roots
- small round onions
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Soak two calf's brains in water and after cooking them with broth, white wine, salt, pepper, and an herb bouquet, serve them
2. Surround with a sauce or garnish made with roots, small cooked round onions and an herb bouquet
3. Season this garnish appropriately and bind with Spanish sauce (see Spanish sauce, page 765)
4. Another stew or any sauce can be substituted for the one just indicated
