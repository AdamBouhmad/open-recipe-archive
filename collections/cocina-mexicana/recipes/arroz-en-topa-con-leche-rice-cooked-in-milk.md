## Ingredients

- rice, well washed
- sufficient quantity of milk
- salt or sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the well-washed rice in a sufficient quantity of milk
2. Season with salt or sugar according to taste
3. Stir frequently
4. Serve immediately
5. Can also be made with almond milk using one quarter pound of bitter almonds for two quarts of milk
