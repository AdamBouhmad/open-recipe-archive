## Ingredients

- medium-sized raw potatoes
- beef or meatless broth
- water
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- roots
- some onions
- parsley in sprigs

## Directions

1. Take medium-sized raw potatoes, peel them and put to cook in beef or meatless broth and water with a little oil, salt, pepper, roots, some onions and parsley sprigs.
2. When they are cooked and have no more broth left, let them fry in the oil until they take on a good color.
3. Serve with oil sauce.
