## Ingredients

- one hen
- lard
- garlic bulb
- ham strips
- thyme
- bay leaf
- oregano
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- Jerez wine
- water

## Directions

1. Place the hen in a casserole with sufficient lard and a bulb of garlic, strips of ham, thyme, bay leaf, oregano and marjoram, salt and pepper
2. Once partially cooked, add one cup of vinegar and half a pint of Jerez wine
3. Let it take on a roasted color, then add one pint of water
4. Let it dry and decorate as desired
