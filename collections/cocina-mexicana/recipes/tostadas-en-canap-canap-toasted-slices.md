## Ingredients

- sweetbread or calf's thymus gland
- rooster crests
- artichoke bottoms
- mushrooms
- substance or broth
- beef marrow
- egg yolks
- sauce
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of sweetbread or calf's thymus gland, rooster crests and artichoke bottoms, cut into dice shapes
2. Cut mushrooms in the same form, fry them and moisten with substance
3. Add the mixture and cook everything with beef marrow
4. Season, and then bind with egg yolks and a little sauce
5. Let cool
6. Coat with beaten eggs and fry
7. Serve with the same beef or veal marrow
