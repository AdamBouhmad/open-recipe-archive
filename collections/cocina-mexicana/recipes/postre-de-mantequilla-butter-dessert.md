## Ingredients

- milk
- cornstarch
- sugar
- egg whites
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. To four quarts of milk, add three ounces of cornstarch and three pounds of sugar
2. Cook until thickened but not too thick
3. Beat four egg whites and let everything cook well together
4. Pour onto the butter (?), sprinkle cinnamon on top
