## Ingredients

- corn
- egg yolks
- lard
- cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the corn the same way as for making tamales, rub it and after cleaning well place it to drain in the sun
2. Grind it dry and sieve it, mix with egg yolks and lard in proportion to the quantity of dough and beating well form the quesadillas with cheese in the middle and fry them
