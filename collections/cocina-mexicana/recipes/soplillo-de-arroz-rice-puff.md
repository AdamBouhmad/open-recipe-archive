## Ingredients

- Half pound rice
- Cream
- Sugar
- Salt
- Six egg yolks
- Orange flower water
- Lemon zest
- A little lard
- Six egg whites
- Lard for the mold
- Paper

## Directions

1. Clean half pound of rice and cook it with cream, sugar and a little salt
2. When well cooked, pass it through a sieve
3. Place this puree in a saucepan with six egg yolks, some orange flower water, lemon zest with a little lard
4. Add six egg whites beaten until they form peaks
5. Heat it in a covered oven
6. Butter a mold and surround its edges with a strip of paper to support the puff as it rises
7. Pour the mixture into it
8. Place it over hot ashes
9. The oven should have moderate heat
