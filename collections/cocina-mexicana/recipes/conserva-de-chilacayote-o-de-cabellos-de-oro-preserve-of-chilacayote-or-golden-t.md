## Ingredients

- Chilacayote
- Ash water, settled
- Clarified syrup at very low point
- Chilacayote seeds, peeled
- Panela or piloncillo
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Peel the chilacayote with the point of a knife and scrape until white
2. Cut into small pieces so the threads do not become too long when boiling in the syrup
3. Keep in settled ash water for half an hour
4. Rinse in clean water and cook until perfectly soft
5. Place in clarified syrup at very low point so it only simmers with the peeled seeds
6. Repeat this operation several days until the threads are separated and the syrup reaches preserve point
7. It is more flavorful if panela or piloncillo is used instead of sugar
8. Top with toasted sesame seeds
