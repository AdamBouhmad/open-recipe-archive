## Ingredients

- 1 hen
- Half chile
- A portion of pasilla
- A portion of mulato
- A portion of wide chile
- 6 tomatoes and 1 jitomate, roasted
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- 1 roasted garlic head
- Pepper to taste
- 1 burned and ground tortilla
- Lard

## Directions

1. Remove veins very well from all chiles and toast them
2. Collect all the sticks and veins and burn them until they become charcoal so they do not become bitter
3. Add 6 roasted tomatoes and 1 roasted jitomate
4. Add clove, cinnamon, a roasted garlic head, pepper to taste, a burned and ground tortilla
5. Fry all this very well and incorporate into the chile
