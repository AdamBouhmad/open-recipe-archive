## Ingredients

- Roasted and cold lamb meat
- Beef udder
- Mushrooms
- Velouté sauce or velvety sauce
- Aspic
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted and cold lamb meat into small dice, removing any skin and sinews
2. Cut beef udder and mushrooms into the same small dice shape
3. Cook the mushrooms beforehand
4. Mix equal parts of velouté or velvety sauce with aspic
5. Reduce this mixture until very thick
6. Bind the sauce with egg yolks
7. Pour the sauce over the meat and mushrooms and let cool
8. Once cooled, take spoonfuls of the mixture and shape each with breadcrumbs to give it consistency
9. Coat the croquettes with beaten egg
10. Cover with breadcrumbs
11. Fry in very hot butter or lard
12. When perfectly browned, serve with fried parsley on top
