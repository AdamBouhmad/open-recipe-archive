## Ingredients

- Lamb breast cut in long, square or oval pieces
- Bacon pieces
- Ham slices
- Two carrots sliced into rounds
- Three or four onions sliced
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- One good tablespoon of broth
- Spinach, sorrel or chicory

## Directions

1. Place bacon pieces in a pot
2. Add ham slices and lamb breast pieces on top, covering with bacon
3. Add sliced carrots and onions, bay leaf, thyme, and one good tablespoon of broth
4. Let simmer slowly for about three hours with fire above and below
5. Drain and place on platter with spinach, sorrel or chicory in the middle
