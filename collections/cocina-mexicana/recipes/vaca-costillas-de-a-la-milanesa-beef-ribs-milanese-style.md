## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- butter
- garlic head
- shallots
- meat gelatin
- tomato sauce
- salt

## Directions

1. Well prepare the ribs and sauté with a little butter, a head of garlic, and some shallots until well fried on both sides
2. Reduce the heat, cover the sauté pan, place fire on top, and let the ribs simmer gently for a quarter hour
3. Remove the ribs and add a little meat gelatin and tomato sauce to the sauté pan, letting it thicken
4. Place the ribs in this jelly, let them simmer gently for a few minutes, and arrange on Neapolitan macaroni
