## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked, peeled and cut
- butter kneaded with flour
- cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After cooking, peeling and cutting, place in a pot a piece of butter kneaded with flour.
2. Dissolve with cream.
3. Season with salt and pepper and stir this sauce.
4. When it is about to cook, add the potatoes, sauté and serve hot.
