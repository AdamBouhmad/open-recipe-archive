## Ingredients

- veal tongues, cooked and skinned
- pork or chorizo filling
- egg, beaten
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Make wide, thin slices from the cooked, skinned tongues
2. Place pork or chorizo filling on each slice
3. Roll them well so the filling does not escape
4. Dip in beaten egg, roll in flour, and fry in butter
5. They serve as garnishes for roasts and can be served hot alone or with sauce, or eaten cold as cold meat
