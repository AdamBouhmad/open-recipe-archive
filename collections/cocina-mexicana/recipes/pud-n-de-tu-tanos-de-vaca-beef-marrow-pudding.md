## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of raw milk
- two large boxes of sponge cake
- one pound of beef marrow
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Crumble two large boxes of sponge cake into two cuartillos of raw milk
2. Beat the mixture well and pour into a dish greased with lard, adding one pound of beef marrow that has been cooked separately and cut into small pieces
3. Let it set at two fires and serve with sugar and cinnamon on top
