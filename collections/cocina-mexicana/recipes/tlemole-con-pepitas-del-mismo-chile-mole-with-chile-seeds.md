## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- chile seeds
- garlic
- clove
- sesame seeds
- salt
- turkey, chicken or meat
- lard
- water
- toasted tortillas

## Directions

1. Devein ancho and pasilla chiles in equal quantities, toast them and soak them
2. Toast and grind a few seeds from both types of chiles, then grind the chiles with garlic, a little clove and toasted sesame seeds
3. Season with salt and put the portions of turkey, chicken or meat to be cooked in this mole, and leave in a pot until the next day
4. Adding the necessary water and lard, put on the fire until the meats are cooked
5. Then thicken the mole with toasted tortillas ground at the fire and very finely ground, and when well seasoned, serve with toasted sesame seeds on top
