## Ingredients

- Barbel fish, well desalted
- Salt
- Oil
- Butter
- Onion
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Green chiles
- Water used to cook tomatoes
- Cumin
- Black pepper
- Eggs
- Oregano
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Small chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. After being well desalted, place the fish to cook in water with appropriate salt
2. Once cooked, cut into regular portions and fry well in oil
3. Fry butter, onion, tomato, garlic and green chiles, all chopped, and once golden, add a little water in which tomatoes have been cooked
4. Add ground cumin and pepper and some beaten eggs
5. Stir well until lumps form, then dilute them in cooking oil
6. Add the barbel, sprinkling with oregano and garnishing with slices of hard-boiled eggs, small chiles and olives
7. Serve with this sauce
