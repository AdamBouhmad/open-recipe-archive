## Ingredients

- capon
- butter or lard
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- mushrooms
- garlic
- parsley
- oiled paper
- ham slices
- sauce of choice

## Directions

1. Cook the capon in a casserole with butter or lard, salt, pepper, and fine herbs, turning it from time to time so it cooks evenly.
2. Remove the capon and in the same butter, fry chopped mushrooms, garlic, and parsley, which shall be placed over the whole capon.
3. When cooled, place half the herbs inside the capon and arrange it on four sheets of oiled paper.
4. Garnish with a slice of ham below and arrange the remaining herbs around it with another slice of ham on top.
5. Wrap with the paper, folding the edges so the folds do not meet, tie with twine and place in the oven to warm without toasting the paper.
6. Once hot, untie and make a square opening in the paper, wrapping the edges, and pour in a sauce of choice inside.
