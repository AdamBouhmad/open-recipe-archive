## Ingredients

- Spongy pork rind (chicharrón de espuma)
- Chorizo filling
- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Toasted tortillas
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Clove
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Cooked ham, small pieces
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the spongy pork rind in hot water and stretch it out
2. Cut it into pieces of one sixth (una sesma)
3. Place chorizo filling in the middle of each piece and wrap it
4. Tie with string
5. Fry garlic cloves in lard until golden, then remove them
6. In the same lard, fry toasted tortillas ground with cinnamon, pepper, and clove
7. Add chopped parsley and salt
8. Add small pieces of cooked ham, oil, and vinegar
9. Put the stuffed pork rinds in this sauce and let simmer until the broth reaches regular consistency
