## Ingredients

- Ducks, washed
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, whole
- Vinegar, strong
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Onion, minced fine
- Black pepper
- Olives
- Chiles
- Lettuce hearts

## Directions

1. Heat one quarter litre of oil per five ducks in a casserole.
2. Brown many whole garlic cloves in the oil.
3. Add abundant strong vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, and marjoram.
4. When about to boil, remove from heat, as boiling spoils it; pour into a pot.
5. Add the duck breasts, cooked as described in previous recipes.
6. Cover the pot very well; after three days, serve with minced onion, pepper, olives, and sliced chiles, garnished with lettuce hearts.
