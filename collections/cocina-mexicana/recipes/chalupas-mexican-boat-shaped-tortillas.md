## Ingredients

- masa (corn dough for tortillas)
- manteca (lard)

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as used for common tortillas and shape them into chalupas
2. Form and cook on a comal (griddle) as explained in the butter chalupas article
3. Fry in hot lard, leaving a hollow in the middle where sauce is placed
4. Alternatively, cook on a comal without frying, adding raw or cooked butter to the sauce and placing on a sheet of metal over the fire to soak in the butter
