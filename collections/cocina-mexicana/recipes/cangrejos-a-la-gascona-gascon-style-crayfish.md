## Ingredients

- crayfish
- parsley
- two heads of garlic
- one large onion
- cloves
- bay leaf
- two quartillos of Champagne wine
- half a quartillo of oil
- salt
- pepper
- slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Divide the crayfish in half and cook with chopped parsley, two heads of garlic (all finely minced), one large onion studded with cloves, a bay leaf, two quartillos of Champagne wine, half a quartillo of oil, salt, pepper and slices of lemon.
2. Let the sauce reduce, then remove the studded onion, bay leaf and lemon slices.
3. Serve.
