## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs
- Wine
- Salt
- Butter
- Almond paste for filling
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Make as the previous empanadillas, using wine in the dough instead of milk
2. Add orange blossom water to the almond paste filling
