## Ingredients

- guinea fowl, cleaned and eviscerated
- butter, melted
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic, crushed
- parsley, chopped
- black pepper
- bay leaves, two or three
- raw oil, half a cup
- water
- egg yolks, eight

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned guinea fowl on a grill, frequently brushing with melted butter, lemon juice, salt and crushed garlic.
2. When half-cooked, transfer to a pot and add butter, chopped parsley, pepper, salt, two or three bay leaves, the fat remaining from roasting, half a cup of raw oil and enough water to cover it.
3. Leave to boil until tender.
4. Remove the guinea fowl.
5. Dissolve eight egg yolks in the broth, beating them well so the broth thickens.
6. Let it boil a little, and pour over the guinea fowl, which will be on a platter, and serve.
