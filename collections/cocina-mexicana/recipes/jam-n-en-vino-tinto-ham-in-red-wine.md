## Ingredients

- ham leg
- water
- marjoram
- green rosemary
- sage
- savory
- laurel
- thyme
- cinnamon sticks
- nutmeg
- cloves
- lemon slices
- sugar
- red wine
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak a ham leg
2. Cook it in a pot with water, marjoram, green rosemary, sage, savory, laurel, thyme, cinnamon sticks, nutmeg, cloves, lemon slices, one pound of sugar and one quarter of red wine
3. Cover the pot until the ham is cooked, making sure the broth is consumed
4. Remove it and put it back on two fires with another pound of sugar and a quarter of wine without mixing water
5. Let it stay until the wine is consumed
6. Make a crust with egg yolks and a coating with sugar and cinnamon, ground, and egg whites
7. Once on the platter, decorate if desired with fried pastries and threaded eggs
