## Ingredients

- Thrushes
- Vinegar
- Bay leaves
- Spices
- Salt brine
- Fat

## Directions

1. Remove the heads and legs from the thrushes
2. Place them on a grill or spit, leaving them half-cooked
3. Place the half-cooked thrushes in vinegar seasoned with bay leaves and spices in sufficient quantity
4. Transfer them to a vessel or barrel
5. Pour hot salt brine over them
6. Cover with a layer of fat
7. Seal the vessel hermetically with a bladder or parchment
8. Store in a cool place in the shade
9. Prepared this way, the thrushes can be eaten all year without spoiling
