## Ingredients

- custard (natillas)
- egg yolks
- sugar, powdered
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. To a plate of custard, add a dozen raw egg yolks, half a pound of powdered sugar, and some bread crumbs
2. Beat everything cold
3. Form into a cake and place in syrup
