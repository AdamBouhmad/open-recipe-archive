## Ingredients

- squabs
- flour
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- cooked and drained lettuce
- capers
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- squab cooking broth

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs, coat them in flour and fry in lard
2. Remove from the fat
3. In the lard, fry chopped garlic, onions, tomatoes and green chiles, with cloves, pepper and ground cumin
4. When this mixture is fried, add cooked, drained and chopped lettuce, capers, salt, oil, vinegar and a little of the squab cooking broth
5. Let simmer until everything is seasoned and almost dry
6. Divide the mixture in half
7. Make a bed with one half on which to place the squabs with olives and cured small chiles
8. Cover with the remaining half of the mixture
9. After flattening slightly to even it out, brush with oil
10. Place the squabs over two gentle fires so the mixture finishes drying and becomes slightly golden on top
