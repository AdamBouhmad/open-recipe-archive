## Ingredients

- one quarter of good, clean almonds
- a little orange blossom water
- six eggs
- one caldero cup of custard
- a little fine sponge cake, ground
- sugar, ground to taste
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Grind one quarter of good, clean almonds, leaving some texture, and sprinkle with a little orange blossom water
2. Mix with six beaten eggs as if for cooking, incorporating at the same time one caldero cup of custard, a little fine ground sponge cake, and sugar ground to taste
3. Coat a cazuela with a little butter and dust it with a little ground sponge cake
4. Pour in the mixture and let it set over two fires
