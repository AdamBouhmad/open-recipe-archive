## Ingredients

- apples or peaches
- brandy
- sugar
- lemon peel
- flour

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit, cut into pieces and remove the pits or seeds
2. Place the apples or peaches in Catalan brandy with ground sugar and lemon peel
3. Once they have become well impregnated and penetrated with the aroma, drain them and roll in flour
4. Fry the pieces until they are well golden
5. Place on a serving plate and sprinkle with sugar
6. Use fruits that are not fully ripe, but somewhat firm
