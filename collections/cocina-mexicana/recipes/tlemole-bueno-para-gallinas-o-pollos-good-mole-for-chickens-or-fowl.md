## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- toasted tortilla
- garlic fried in lard
- sesame seeds
- cilantro
- cooked tomatoes
- clove
- pepper
- ginger
- salt
- chicken or fowl in quarters
- chicken or fowl broth
- pork jowl
- pork ribs

## Directions

1. Devein, toast and soak the ancho chiles and grind them together with toasted tortilla and garlic fried in lard, sesame seeds and toasted cilantro, cooked tomatoes, clove, pepper and ginger
2. Fry the ground mixture and season with salt, then add the chicken or fowl in quarters with the broth in which they were cooked, and cooked pork jowl and ribs, and leave to boil until the proper consistency
3. This mole can be made with any meats desired, and can be varied by putting peanuts in place of tomatoes
