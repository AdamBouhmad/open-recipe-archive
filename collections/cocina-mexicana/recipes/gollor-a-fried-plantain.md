## Ingredients

- Plantain
- Butter or oil for frying
- Egg (beaten, optional)
- Salt (if using egg coating)

## Directions

1. Slice the plantain lengthwise
2. Fry the sliced plantain in butter or oil
3. Alternatively, after slicing, dip the plantain pieces in beaten egg and fry them
