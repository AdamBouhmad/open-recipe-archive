## Ingredients

- butter
- milk
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Melt some butter in a pot over the steam of a boiling pot, remove the whey, and when clarified, mix in well-cooked milk and put everything in a pot where it will be well incorporated with a whisk
2. Mix in powdered sugar until it reaches good taste and place over layers of sponge cake, decorating the last layer as is customary
