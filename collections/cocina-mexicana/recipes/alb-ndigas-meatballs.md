## Ingredients

- butter
- two or three bunches of suet
- onion in wedges
- one or two chopped tomatoes
- chopped parsley
- flour
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- two or three garlic cloves
- whole zucchini

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a casserole with two or three bunches of suet
2. Add onion in wedges, chopped tomatoes, and chopped parsley
3. Fry until done, then add flour and fry together
4. Once fried, add water
5. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and two or three garlic cloves
6. Form meatballs and add them to the broth with whole zucchini
7. Let cook a little before serving
