## Ingredients

- French bread, cold
- butter
- garlic cloves, cleaned
- tomatoes, chopped fine
- parsley, chopped fine
- broth from the pot
- tongue meat, cooked
- cauliflower head, cooked with salt
- ham
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut cold French bread into very thin slices
2. Heat butter in a baking pan in the amount needed according to the quantity of soup
3. Fry the cloves from one head of garlic, cleaned and chopped, in the hot butter
4. When golden, remove from the butter and fry finely chopped tomatoes and parsley in it
5. When everything is well fried, add seasoned broth from the pot and let it boil once
6. Arrange alternating layers of toasted bread slices and slices of cooked cauliflower with salt in the pan
7. Bathe them with the prepared tomato broth
8. Put the pan over two gentle fires until the broth is consumed and the soup is neither too watery nor too thick
9. Pass to the tureen for serving, sprinkling pepper on top and adorning with ham and fried pieces
