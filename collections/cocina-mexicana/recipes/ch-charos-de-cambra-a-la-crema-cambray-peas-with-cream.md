## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of small tender cambray peas
- good piece of butter
- salt
- sugar
- bundle of parsley
- small onions
- hot water
- four egg yolks
- good cream

## Directions

1. Put peas in water with a good piece of butter.
2. Press and squeeze together with hands and put on a colander to drain perfectly.
3. Once drained, put in a pan and on a well-lit stove, fry them, taking care to toss frequently.
4. Add a little salt and a little sugar if desired, a small bundle of parsley and small onions.
5. Add boiling water and cook over high heat.
6. When the broth has almost been consumed, dissolve four fresh egg yolks in good cream, bind the peas with this and serve.
