## Ingredients

- Half arroba of veal meat
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Clove
- Garlic
- Ham
- Sour orange juice
- Castilian vinegar
- Carlon wine
- Wide chiles
- Garlic head
- Coriander, toasted
- Assorted spices
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Beat half an arroba of veal meat and stud it with cinnamon, pepper, clove, garlic and ham, and place in a suitable pot with one pot-full of sour orange juice, another of Castilian vinegar and another of carlon wine.
2. Soak four wide chiles in vinegar, which are then mixed with one head of garlic, a handful of toasted coriander, and all assorted spices in moderate quantity, and add to the meat everything ground with salt and sufficient water, so that the meat remains covered with the broth.
3. Keep it in this way for one day and one night, and the next day place over the fire and let it cook until the broth is consumed and the meat remains roasted and dry.
