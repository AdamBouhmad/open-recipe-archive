## Ingredients

- onions
- green chiles
- garlic
- xitomate
- oil or lard
- bread
- vinegar
- poultry or kid livers cooked
- fish broth
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop onions, green chiles, garlic, and xitomate finely
2. Fry in oil or lard
3. Soak bread in vinegar and grind with cooked poultry or kid livers
4. Fry this mixture and combine with the previous mixture
5. Add a little fish broth
6. Add ground pepper, cloves, and cinnamon
7. Season and add sufficient oil and vinegar with necessary salt
