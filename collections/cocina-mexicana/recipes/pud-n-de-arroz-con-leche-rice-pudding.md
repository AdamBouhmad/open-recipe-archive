## Ingredients

- rice pudding
- half a pound of butter
- egg yolks
- two or three egg whites
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Make rice pudding as is customary but thick; before it stops simmering add half a pound of butter, stirring well until incorporated
2. Let it cool and mix in egg yolks to make it very yellow; add two or three egg whites according to the quantity
3. Add a little finely chopped almonds
4. Pour into a not very deep dish greased with butter and lined with buttered paper
5. Place small pieces of butter on top and let it set at two fires to serve hot
