## Ingredients

- hare or rabbit, cut into pieces
- butter
- ham
- liver
- artichoke bottoms
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- white Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Divide the hare or rabbit into pieces and put in a pot with a piece of butter.
2. Add a bundle garnished with ham, the liver, artichoke bottoms and mushrooms, all passed through fire.
3. Add a little flour dissolved in broth, salt, pepper and a glass of white Champagne wine.
4. When all is cooked, before serving, add a liaison made with the animal's liver that was cooked in the same stew.
