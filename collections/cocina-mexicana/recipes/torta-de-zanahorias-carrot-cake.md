## Ingredients

- Cooked carrots
- Aged ground cheese
- Eggs
- Flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots, grind them, and squeeze out the liquid
2. Mix with aged ground cheese in sufficient quantity to flavor, and beaten eggs
3. Fry portions taken with a spoon
4. Serve dry or in spiced sauce
5. These cakes can also be made by adding a little flour, which varies their taste and appearance
