## Ingredients

- gobies
- butter
- parsley
- small onions
- mushrooms
- one or two shallots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- red wine
- water

## Directions

1. Remove scales, empty and dry the gobies without washing
2. Place butter with chopped parsley, small onions, mushrooms, shallots, thyme, bay leaf and basil, all finely minced, salt and coarse pepper in the bottom of the serving dish
3. Arrange the gobies on top
4. Season with the same ingredients placed below
5. Moisten with a glass of red wine
6. Cover the dish and bring the fish to a boil over good heat until only very little sauce remains
7. Cook for about a quarter hour until completely done
