## Ingredients

- Two cucumbers, not perfectly ripe
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- One onion

## Directions

1. Clean two cucumbers that are not perfectly ripe; if they have a bitter tip they are worthless and should be replaced with others; if not bitter, test by tasting
2. Cut into slices along the length, as thin as possible
3. Place in a serving dish with salt, pepper, vinegar, and an onion sliced as well
4. Let pickle for two hours
5. Remove some of the liquid
6. Serve with roasted beef
