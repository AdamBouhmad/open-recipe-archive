## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 pound peanuts, shelled and ground

## Directions

1. Heat clarified syrup made from sugar to the soft-ball stage (punto de juntar en el agua)
2. Add the ground peanuts to the syrup
3. To prevent the candy from releasing too much oil and spoiling quickly, do not grind the peanuts excessively; instead, only crush them lightly, and it would be better to grind them after roasting slightly
4. Place the mixture over the fire and bring it to the cajeta stage
5. Remove from heat and beat the mixture
6. Pour onto a wooden board (cajón) before it sets completely
7. Cut the candies into the desired size and shape
8. For a more flavorful result, substitute piloncillo or unrefined cane sugar for the refined sugar in making the syrup
