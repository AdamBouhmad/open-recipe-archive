## Ingredients

- una libra de arroz (one pound rice)
- seis cuartillos de leche bien endulzada (six quarters well-sweetened milk)
- media libra de mantequilla fresca (half pound fresh butter)
- un pedacito de panecillo o unas yemas de huevo desleídas (a small piece of bread or some egg yolks dissolved)

## Directions

1. Leave a pound of rice soaked overnight
2. The next day put it in six quarters of well-sweetened milk and put to cook
3. When it is near setting, add about half a pound of fresh butter and a small piece of bread or some egg yolks dissolved to give it color
4. Let it continue until it acquires the proper consistency
