## Ingredients

- six pounds of sugar
- a straw
- juice of one lemon
- one egg white
- sixty-four egg yolks
- four cuartillos of milk
- one pound of peeled and ground almonds
- two pounds of butter
- one mamón sliced
- ground cinnamon
- ground cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with six pounds of sugar, clarified with a straw, the juice of one lemon and one egg white
2. When at medium point, lower sixty-four egg yolks as for real eggs, and stir with four cuartillos of milk, then add to the syrup
3. Alternatively, put the sugar in the milk and let it boil on the fire; once cooled, add the yolks and mix one pound of peeled and ground almonds with two pounds of butter
4. Place a layer of sliced mamón in the dish, another of milk and another of almond mixture with butter, sprinkled well with ground cinnamon
5. Once the dish is filled in this manner, cover with beaten egg yolks and cook at two gentle fires, testing with a straw if the pudding is well cooked
6. To prevent sticking, brush constantly with butter using a feather
7. If desired, stud with peeled almonds dried in the sun
