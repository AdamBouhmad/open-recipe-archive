## Ingredients

- Zucchini, peeled and chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Green chile, chopped
- Salt
- Lard
- Cinnamon, ground
- Cloves, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Saffron, ground
- Dry cilantro, ground
- Vinegar, strong
- Pitted raisins
- Clean roasted almonds
- Olives
- Toma chiles in strips
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Peel and chop zucchini
2. Chop onions, tomatoes, garlic, and green chile
3. Cook everything with sufficient salt and abundant lard
4. When cooked, add a little cinnamon, cloves, pepper, saffron, and dry cilantro, all ground and dissolved in a small amount of strong vinegar
5. Add pitted raisins, clean roasted almonds, olives, and toma chile strips
6. After everything has boiled once, form the puff pastry dough and give it two or three turns so it has few layers
7. Form the pastries, filling them with the cold zucchini mixture
8. Fry in lard or place in the oven wrapped in papers
