## Ingredients

- Meat, raw then cooked
- Sugar
- Spices of all kinds except saffron
- Citron
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Butter
- Pounded sponge cake

## Directions

1. Chop the raw meat and then cook it
2. Add sugar, all spices except saffron, citron, raisins and almonds
3. Grease a pan with butter
4. Make a layer of pounded sponge cake
5. Add a layer of milk cream
6. Add a layer of the meat mixture
7. Top with a final layer of pounded sponge cake
8. With the pastry thus arranged, place it over two fires until it browns
