## Ingredients

- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- oregano
- lard
- water
- capers
- cumin
- pepper
- clove
- oil
- vinegar
- flour
- mojarra fish

## Directions

1. Slice the tomato, garlic and onion
2. Chop the parsley and oregano and fry in lard
3. Add a little water, capers, cumin, pepper and clove, oil and vinegar
4. Fry the mojarra separately, rolling them first in flour
5. Add them to the sauce shortly before serving
