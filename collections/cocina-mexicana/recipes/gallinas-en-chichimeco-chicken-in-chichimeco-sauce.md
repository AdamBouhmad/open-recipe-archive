## Ingredients

- Chicken livers
- Eyes
- Toasted biscuits
- Lard
- Water
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Strong vinegar
- Wine
- Hazelnuts
- Raisins
- Clean almonds
- Onion
- Chickens
- Bread toasts
- Lard

## Directions

1. Grind the chicken livers with eyes and biscuits toasted in lard
2. Dissolve everything with water, fry it, and add ground clove, cinnamon, and pepper
3. Add a quarter of strong vinegar and another quarter of wine
4. Grind some hazelnuts and add to the broth with raisins and clean almonds
5. When well-seasoned, add onion cooked separately and the cooked chickens
6. Serve adorned with bread toasts fried in lard
