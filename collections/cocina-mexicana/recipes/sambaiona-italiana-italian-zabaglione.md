## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks from fresh eggs
- four glasses of Madeira wine or excellent white wine
- six ounces of sugar
- a little powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pour the egg yolks, wine, sugar, and cinnamon into a saucepan
2. Place the saucepan over high heat and beat the contents with a whisk until the foam has filled the saucepan
3. Serve immediately in small cream pots or in coffee cups
