## Ingredients

- trout fillets
- salt
- coarse pepper
- parsley sprigs
- four medium onions
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- juice of two lemons
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut thin strips from the trout, slightly longer than a finger and roughly the same thickness
2. Place in a casserole with salt, coarse pepper, parsley sprigs, four sliced medium onions, two bay leaves, some thyme, and the juice of two lemons
3. Carefully stir the strips in their braise
4. When ready to serve, drain the strips onto clean linen, melt butter in a casserole until very hot
5. Dust flour over the trout strips, toss well in the same linen, shake off excess, and fry in the hot butter until golden
6. Arrange in a heap on a plate and serve with spicy sauce
