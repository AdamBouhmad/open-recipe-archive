## Ingredients

- recently made noodles
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Ensure the noodles are recently made and have no bad smell or rancid taste
2. Break them between the fingers
3. Drop into boiling broth
4. Stir with a wooden spoon so they do not stick together
5. Boil for half an hour
6. Serve immediately
