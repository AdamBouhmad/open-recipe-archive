## Ingredients

- trout
- two carrots
- four onions
- parsley
- small cauliflower heads
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- two cloves
- salt
- pepper
- three or four bottles of red wine
- four ounces of butter
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. After cleaning and gutting, tie the head and place in an oval casserole or fish kettle
2. Add two sliced carrots and four sliced onions, parsley, small cauliflower heads, two bay leaves, some thyme, two cloves, salt and pepper
3. Pour three or four bottles of red wine over it and let simmer for three quarters of an hour
4. Strain the broth through a sieve
5. Mix four ounces of butter with one tablespoon of flour in a casserole, adding the strained broth
6. Stir well to combine the butter and flour, reduce the sauce over high heat, and when it adheres to the spoon, remove from heat
7. Pour the sauce over the trout, which should be covered with it, tasting first to ensure proper seasoning
