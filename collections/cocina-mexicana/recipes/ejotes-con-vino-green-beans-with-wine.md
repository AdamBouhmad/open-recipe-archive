## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- onion
- butter
- wine

## Directions

1. Cook the green beans as described
2. Sauté a little onion in butter until it begins to take color
3. Mix in the green beans
4. Moisten with wine
5. Season with salt and butter
