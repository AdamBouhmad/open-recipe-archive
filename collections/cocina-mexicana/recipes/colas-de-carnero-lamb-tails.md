## Ingredients

- lamb tails
- bacon strips
- carrot
- onions
- herb bundle
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- broth
- lettuce
- escarole
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Place the lamb tails in a dish prepared with bacon strips, carrot, onions, an herb bouquet, salt, pepper, a glass of white wine and the same amount of broth
2. Let them cook over low heat
3. When ready, remove and let them drain to serve with lettuce, escarole, potatoes or any kind of vegetable
