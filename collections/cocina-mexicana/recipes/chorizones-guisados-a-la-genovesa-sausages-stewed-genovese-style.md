## Ingredients

- Onions
- 1 or 2 sprigs fresh mint
- Abundant parsley
- Lean ham
- Sausages
- Chicken or pork broth
- Toasted bread
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- White wine

## Directions

1. Chop onions, mint sprigs, parsley, lean ham, and sausages separately and very finely
2. First fry the onion, then the garlic and other herbs, mixing in the ham and sausages
3. When everything is well fried, add chicken or pork broth
4. Add ground toasted bread, clove, cinnamon, and pepper along with a little vinegar
5. When serving, add white wine
