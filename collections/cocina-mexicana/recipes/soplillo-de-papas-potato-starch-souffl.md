## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of cream
- six ounces of sugar
- six table spoonfuls of potato starch
- four egg yolks
- as much as one egg of butter
- a little grated lemon zest
- six egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Bring two cuartillos of cream to boil with six ounces of sugar
2. Dissolve six tablespoonfuls of potato starch with four egg yolks and the cream
3. Add as much as one egg of butter and a little grated lemon zest
4. Put this mixture over the fire, turning it until it has boiled a few times and let it cool
5. Add six egg yolks and mix with everything else, and if the soufflé has become very thick add one or two complete eggs
6. Beat four egg whites as for biscuit and gently mix them with the soufflé
7. Prepare it in a small silver pot and cook as the others
