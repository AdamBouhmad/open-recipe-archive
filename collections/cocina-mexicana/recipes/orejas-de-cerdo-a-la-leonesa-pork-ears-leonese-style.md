## Ingredients

- pork ears
- lard
- onions
- flour
- broth
- vinegar or lemon juice
- bread slices, fried (optional)

## Directions

1. In a sauce made with onions cut into pieces and sautéed in lard, place the ears cut into strips
2. Add a little flour
3. Simmer with broth and reduce
4. When serving, add a few drops of vinegar or the juice of a lemon
5. If desired, serve with fried bread slices
