## Ingredients

- sole fish
- flour
- butter or lard
- salt
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe and open them along the spine lengthwise
2. Dust well with flour and fry in butter or lard
3. Serve with finely ground salt sprinkled prudently on top, on a napkin or plate, accompanied by a halved lemon
