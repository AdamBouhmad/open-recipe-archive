## Ingredients

- oranges
- water
- syrup at light consistency

## Directions

1. Select the most beautiful oranges and lightly remove the thin yellow skin with a knife
2. Divide them into quarters and remove the seeds
3. Place carefully in cold water and blanch in boiling water, leaving them only as long as necessary
4. Return to cold water and when sufficiently tender or soft, remove and place in syrup at light consistency
5. After several boils, remove and allow to cool
6. Give several more boils and allow to cool again
