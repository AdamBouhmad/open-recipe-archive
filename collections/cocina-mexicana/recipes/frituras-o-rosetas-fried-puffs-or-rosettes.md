## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one cup of flour
- four eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil with a tablespoon of butter
2. Once it boils, gradually add one cup of flour, stirring constantly to prevent lumps
3. Once it pulls away cleanly from the pan, remove it and let cool
4. Mix in four eggs one by one, beating with a spoon until it reaches the proper consistency
5. Drop spoonfuls into hot lard
6. Dust the hot fried puffs with powdered sugar
