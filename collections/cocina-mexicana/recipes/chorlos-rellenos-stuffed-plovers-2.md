## Ingredients

- plovers (cleaned and plucked)
- bacon (a little)
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Open the plovers from below the tail when they are cleaned and plucked to be able to remove the intestines and giblets.
2. Chop everything extracted with a little bacon, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper.
3. Fill the plovers with this mixture.
4. Cover with thin slices of bacon.
5. Roast at low heat.
