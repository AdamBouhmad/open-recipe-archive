## Ingredients

- thick plantains
- lard for frying
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take thick plantains and slice them to the thickness of half a finger
2. With a tin tube, remove the core so they form little rings
3. Fry in lard until they brown slightly
4. Remove them well drained, dust with sugar and cinnamon, and serve hot
