## Ingredients

- raw chicken
- lard
- wine
- orange juice
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- cloves
- cinnamon, ground
- fried slices of toast, ground

## Directions

1. Fry raw chicken in lard
2. Add wine and orange juice, a bit of sugar, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
3. Add ground cloves and cinnamon
4. Let cook in this sauce
5. Thicken the sauce afterwards with fried and ground toast slices
