## Ingredients

- four pounds white sugar
- six martillos of milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the milk, then strain it
2. Put it to boil until it reaches the point of gathering in water and forms a perfect thimble on the finger
3. Remove from heat and beat until it forms a ball
4. Form into pastes like tablets
5. Work and carve with molds, giving them the desired shape
6. Place on clean napkins to dry
