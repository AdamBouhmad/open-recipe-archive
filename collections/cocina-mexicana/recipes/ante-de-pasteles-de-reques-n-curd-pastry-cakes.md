## Ingredients

- hot lard
- ground sugar
- hard biscuits
- curds
- melted lard

## Directions

1. Mix ground hard biscuits with hot lard and ground sugar
2. Dissolve the curds well with a little melted lard
3. Make a thin layer with the biscuit and lard, then add the curds, and above that another layer of biscuit
4. Place afterwards over two fires until they brown, then make small dishes or cups, and scatter sugar on top
