## Ingredients

- sixteen quartillos of aguardiente
- eight ounces of nebrina (juniper berries)
- one drachma of cinnamon
- one drachma of cilantro
- one drachma of angelica
- one drachma of clove
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Leave in infusion for thirty to forty days in sixteen quartillos of aguardiente, eight ounces of nebrina (juniper berries), with one drachma of cinnamon, another of cilantro, another of angelica and another of clove, after everything has been cracked
2. Pass the liquor through a sieve
3. Add two pounds of sugar
4. Filter again and store in well-sealed bottles
