## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes, peeled and sliced
- thin square pieces of bread
- butter
- potato flour broth

## Directions

1. Peel and slice into rounds the potatoes cooked in a stew.
2. Cut thin square pieces of bread.
3. Fry everything in butter.
4. Place in a deep dish and pour a broth made from potato flour over it.
5. Before serving, the stew can be browned by rolling it in sugar and putting it in a dome oven, or it can be browned with a hot iron plate.
