## Ingredients

- forty egg yolks
- four ounces of refined sugar
- melted lard up to four ounces
- half a cuartillo of mezcal wine
- one quarter ounce of tequesquite powder

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the refined sugar
2. When well thickened, gradually add the melted lard
3. Add the mezcal wine and tequesquite powder
4. Mix and blend in the necessary flour
5. Form the biscuits
6. Put them on papers and bake in the oven
