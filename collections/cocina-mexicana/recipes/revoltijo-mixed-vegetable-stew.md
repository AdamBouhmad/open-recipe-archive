## Ingredients

- potatoes
- clove
- cinnamon
- anise
- peas
- bean sprouts
- white beans
- common beans
- squash
- cooked shrimp
- egg
- shrimp powder

## Directions

1. Make a mole of potatoes with some cloves and a piece of cinnamon and what you can gather with three fingers of anise
2. Cook peas, bean sprouts, white beans and a few common beans but stewed
3. Cook squash separately and add cooked shrimp
4. Make small balls with egg and shrimp powder
5. Finally add beaten eggs
