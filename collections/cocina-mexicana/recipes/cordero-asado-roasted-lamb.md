## Ingredients

- quarter of lamb
- ham, sliced thin
- butter or lard, melted
- breadcrumbs
- fine herbs, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After having larded a quarter of lamb on the skin side with thin slices of ham
2. Brush on the opposite side with melted butter or lard, dusting it with breadcrumbs, chopped fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Wrap in buttered paper
4. When almost cooked on the grill, remove the paper and dust again with breadcrumbs on the side that had it
5. Allow it to brown on the same grill and serve sprinkled with lemon juice
