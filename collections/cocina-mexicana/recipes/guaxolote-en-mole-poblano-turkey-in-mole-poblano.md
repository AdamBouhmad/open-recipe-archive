## Ingredients

- Pasilla chile peppers (deveined)
- Mulato chile peppers (deveined)
- Field tomatoes
- Lard
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Cilantro
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Turkey meat

## Directions

1. Toast equal amounts of deveined pasilla and mulato chiles
2. Cook a good portion of field tomatoes, soak the toasted chiles a little, and grind them together with the tomatoes, ensuring everything is well ground
3. Fry this in lard
4. Grind cloves, cinnamon, and a little cilantro with a handful of toasted sesame seeds
5. Add everything to the chile frying and add the turkey
6. Let it boil until it reaches the right thickness and serve with toasted sesame seeds on top
