## Ingredients

- walnuts
- toasted biscuit (equal weight to walnuts)
- eggs
- cinnamon powder
- raisins
- almonds
- butter
- syrup

## Directions

1. Soak the walnuts, peel them, and grind them with an equal amount of toasted biscuit by weight
2. Beat eggs first the whites and then the yolks as if for frying
3. Mix the ground walnuts and biscuit into the beaten eggs, adding powdered cinnamon, raisins, and almonds to form a thick paste
4. Pour into a baking dish greased with butter
5. Bake at moderate heat until set
6. Serve with syrup and cinnamon on top
