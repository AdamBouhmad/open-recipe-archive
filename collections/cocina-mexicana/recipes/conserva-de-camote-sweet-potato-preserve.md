## Ingredients

- Sweet potatoes, white, purple or yellow varieties
- Lime water (agua de cal)
- Syrup (almíbar) at medium point

## Directions

1. Select sweet potatoes that are not crude and of regular size
2. Scald them in boiling water so the skin can be removed
3. When clean, cut them in the desired manner into pieces, wheels, stars, etc.
4. Place the pieces in water well-charged with lime but settled
5. Once they acquire consistency, remove and wash well in many waters
6. Prick them with a thick needle and place them in syrup at medium point
7. Let them boil on gentle fire for three or four hours daily
8. After three days, when the syrup has penetrated them well, let them boil until they reach preserve point
