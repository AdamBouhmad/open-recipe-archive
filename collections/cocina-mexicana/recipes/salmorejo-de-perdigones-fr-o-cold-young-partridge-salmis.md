## Ingredients

- Young partridges, broken apart
- Broth, as prepared in the first article of young partridge salmis
- Gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare the broth as described before in the first article on young partridge salmis
2. Let the sauce cool
3. Place the pieces of the birds in it
4. Arrange them mixed on a plate, whose edges are garnished with gelatin
