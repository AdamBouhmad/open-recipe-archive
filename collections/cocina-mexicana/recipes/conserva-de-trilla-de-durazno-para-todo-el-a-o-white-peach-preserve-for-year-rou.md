## Ingredients

- white peach
- salt
- cold water
- sugar
- one libra of sugar for each libra of peach

## Directions

1. Peel the white peach and remove the pit, placing it in cold salted water as you work
2. Cut into small pieces or make trilla, and cook
3. Place afterwards in baskets to drain, rinsing with a few glasses of cold water
4. Once well drained, weigh it
5. Make clarified syrup at more than medium point with one libra of sugar for each libra of peach
6. Add the peach, applying low heat until it reaches the proper point, which is known by the syrup forming wrinkles on a spoon
7. Then remove from heat, pour into glass jars or cups, and expose to the sun for three or four days
