## Ingredients

- Six quartillos of milk
- Three onions
- Fresh cilantro
- Parsley
- One small whole round onion
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Rabbit livers
- Half a pound of jowl

## Directions

1. Boil six quartillos of milk with three onions cut in quarters, fresh cilantro, parsley, one small whole round onion, thyme, laurel and basil
2. When the milk has reduced to one third, strain it
3. Mix into it plenty of chopped rabbit livers
4. Add half a pound of jowl cut into small square pieces
5. Add a bit of fine salt
