## Ingredients

- Water
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Ham slices
- Chorizos

## Directions

1. Put water in a pot with a little vinegar, sugar, pepper, cinnamon, and clove, all ground, sliced ham, and chorizos
