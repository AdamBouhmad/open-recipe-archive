## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- one pound of peeled ground almond
- sponge cake
- one chicken breast
- two hard biscuits
- a little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. To four pounds of sugar made into syrup at point, add one pound of peeled ground almond, sponge cake, one chicken breast and two hard biscuits, all ground
2. When it is ready to take the point of cajeta, add a little orange blossom water
