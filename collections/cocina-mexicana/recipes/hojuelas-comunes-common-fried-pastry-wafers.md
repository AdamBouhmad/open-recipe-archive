## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- Two egg yolks
- One egg white
- A little water of settled tequesquite
- A little dissolved yeast
- A little anise water
- The juice of one sour orange

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour with two egg yolks, one egg white, a little water of settled tequesquite, a little dissolved yeast, and a little anise water with the juice of one sour orange
2. Knead everything until it forms eyes
3. Let it rest
4. Extend it afterward with the roller
5. Cut the hojuelas immediately with the pastry wheel
6. Fry them
