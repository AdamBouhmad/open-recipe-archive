## Ingredients

- Lamb head, liver, lungs, feet, and heart
- Salt
- Spicy sauce
- Two egg yolks
- Butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and snout from the head
2. Cut the offal into slices
3. Drain the blood and wash well with hot water
4. Place in a pot and cook with salt
5. Just before serving, drain the meat
6. Except for the head, add a spicy sauce made with two beaten egg yolks, some butter, and lemon juice to the rest
7. Place the stew on a platter with the head in the middle
