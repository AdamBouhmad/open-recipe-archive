## Ingredients

- eight pounds of flour
- eight egg yolks and two egg whites
- two and a half pounds of butter
- three pounds of ground sugar
- one pound of yeast, or one and a half pounds if not strong
- orange blossom water
- wine
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together well
2. Form the biscuits
3. Bake in the oven
