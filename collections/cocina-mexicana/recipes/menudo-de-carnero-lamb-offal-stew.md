## Ingredients

- Cooked lamb offal
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Butter
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Broth from cooking the offal
- Lamb feet, cooked and boned
- Olives
- Oil
- Chiles
- Capers
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of tomato, onion and garlic together and fry in butter
2. Separately fry a handful of garlic and some ham slices, then grind with clove, cinnamon and pepper
3. Place ham strips and chorizos in the pot, when well fried add the offal cut into small pieces
4. Add some parsley, plenty of thyme, some broth in which the offal was cooked, and some lamb feet well cooked and boned
5. When the sauce is thick, place in platter with olives and oil, and garnish with chiles and capers
