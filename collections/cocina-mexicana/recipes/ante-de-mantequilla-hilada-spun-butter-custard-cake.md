## Ingredients

- butter
- sponge cake (mamón)
- high-point syrup
- cinnamon
- sugar
- flavoring pastille

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and spin it in cold water using a clean stocking or napkin, straining it over sliced sponge cake
2. Pour high-point syrup over it and sprinkle with ground cinnamon, sugar, and flavoring pastille
