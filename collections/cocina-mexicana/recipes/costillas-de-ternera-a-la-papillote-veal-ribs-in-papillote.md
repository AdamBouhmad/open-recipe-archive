## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- paper
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs quite thin and place them on papers cut for this purpose. Season with salt, pepper and finely chopped garlic, and small pieces of butter. Wrap them in the paper, leaving the bone outside, place them on the grill over low heat, and serve with the papers still on.
