## Ingredients

- Dried ancho chile
- Bread
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Small chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Grind soaked and well-deveined ancho chile with soaked bread, garlic and cumin, and fry in lard.
2. Add vinegar, small chiles and olives.
