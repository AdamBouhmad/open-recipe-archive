## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- half a pound of finely ground sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- fragrant red pastille

## Directions

1. In a pan, before putting it on the fire, dissolve half a pound of finely ground sugar and twelve egg yolks.
2. Add the two cuartillos of milk, place on the fire, and stir constantly to prevent burning.
3. As soon as it thickens, remove it and pour into serving dishes.
4. Add a fragrant red pastille.
5. Before pouring this milk, fill some sponge cakes and pour the milk over them.
