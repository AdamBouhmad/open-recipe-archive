## Ingredients

- six pounds of flour
- twelve egg yolks
- six egg whites
- one pound of sifted sugar
- one pound of fresh grated cheese
- twelve and a half pounds of butter
- hot water if needed

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with twelve egg yolks, six egg whites, one pound of sifted sugar, one pound of fresh grated cheese, and twelve and a half pounds of butter and knead everything together
2. If the dough is too stiff, add a little hot water until it becomes smooth
3. Dust a clean surface with flour, spread the dough on it with a rolling pin
4. Cut the biscuits, wrap each one individually in paper and bake in the oven
