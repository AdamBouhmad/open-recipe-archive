## Ingredients

- raw ripe tomatoes, very mature
- dried chile, soaked
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- water
- chicken or pork
- chorizo
- olives
- tornachiles made into strips
- vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- sweet potato
- plantain
- peanuts
- Castilian squash

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from very ripe raw tomatoes
2. Grind the tomatoes with soaked dried chile, cinnamon, clove and pepper
3. Fry everything together
4. Add warm water and chicken or pork
5. Add chorizo in pieces, olives, tornachiles made into strips, vinegar, salt and a piece of sugar
6. Add sweet potato, plantain, peanuts and Castilian squash
7. Simmer until well combined
