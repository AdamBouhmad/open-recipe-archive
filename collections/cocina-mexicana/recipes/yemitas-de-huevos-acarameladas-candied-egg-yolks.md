## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- caramel-stage syrup

## Directions

1. Knead or grind the cooked egg yolks with sugar and ground cinnamon.
2. Form small balls.
3. Dip them in syrup at caramel stage.
4. Allow them to dry.
5. Store or eat them.
