## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- a pound of ground almonds
- sixteen egg yolks
- cold water
- crumbled sponge cake (mamón)
- butter
- thick syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar and when at the honey stage, divide into two portions
2. In one portion, add a pound of ground almonds to form a paste and set aside
3. In the other pound, add sixteen egg yolks dissolved in a little cold water
4. Place over heat and when cooked, set aside
5. Mix with the almonds until it reaches the consistency of pita (a type of candy)
6. Remove from heat and dust with half of crumbled cold sponge cake
7. Butter a pot and pour the mixture into it to cook over two fires
8. To test if done, insert a straw and if it comes out clean, it is ready
9. Let cool in the same mold, turn out onto a round plate
10. Glaze on top with thick syrup
