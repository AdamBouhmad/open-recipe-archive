## Ingredients

- half cuartillo cooked and cold milk
- half pound good starch
- half ounce butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve half pound good starch in half cuartillo cooked and cold milk
2. Strain with a sieve and cook in a small pot, stirring constantly with a spoon
3. As soon as it starts to curdle add half ounce butter and cook until it becomes firm when beaten with the hand and does not stick
4. Set aside, put on a plate and save
5. The next day add 5 whole eggs and 5 more egg yolks, pass through a metate
6. Pipe with a piping bag, fry in a pan with plenty of warm butter until well golden
7. Then dip in boiling syrup and when on the plate, sprinkle with powdered sugar and syrup
