## Ingredients

- oysters
- water
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch the oysters in water over gentle heat without letting them boil
2. Remove and place in other cold water
3. Remove and dry with a napkin
4. Then place in anchovy sauce
5. Reheat and serve
