## Ingredients

- Two hind quarters of lamb, kept whole with the two legs joined together
- Thin strips of ham

## Directions

1. Take the two hind quarters of the lamb without separating them, keeping the two legs joined; lard both the legs and the loin with thin strips of ham. Keep on the spit for two hours and serve. If you do not wish to lard the roast, cover it with thin slices of ham. The larded parts can be covered with gelatin and served with a light broth.
