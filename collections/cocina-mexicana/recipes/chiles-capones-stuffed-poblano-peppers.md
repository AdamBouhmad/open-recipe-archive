## Ingredients

- Fresh poblano peppers
- Fresh cheese
- Epazote leaves
- Lard
- Oil
- Onion

## Directions

1. Roast and clean the peppers; remove the veins
2. Place a layer of fresh cheese and an epazote leaf on each pepper
3. In a pot, sauté lard and oil with a thick onion and a little flour until it takes color
4. Add water and salt, and when it comes to a boil, add the peppers
5. Turn them carefully with a knife so they do not burn
