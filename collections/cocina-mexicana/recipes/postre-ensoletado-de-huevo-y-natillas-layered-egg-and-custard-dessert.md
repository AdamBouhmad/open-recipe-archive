## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sugar
- eighteen egg yolks
- sponge biscuits (soletas) or mamón slices
- beaten custard (natillas)
- sifted sugar
- cinnamon powder
- ground almonds
- split almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of joining in water with a pound and a half of sugar
2. Remove from heat and mix in eighteen egg yolks outside the flame
3. When well dissolved, place the mixture with the syrup back on the fire
4. Cook to a point slightly lower than cajeta, then remove from heat
5. In serving dishes, make layers: one of this egg mixture, another of sponge biscuits or mamón slices, and another of beaten custard with sifted sugar and cinnamon powder
6. Continue in this order until the dishes are filled, ensuring the last layer is egg mixture
7. Decorate with ground cinnamon, split almonds and pine nuts
