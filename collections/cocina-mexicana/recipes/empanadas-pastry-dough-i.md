## Ingredients

- Half pound flour
- Six eggs
- Half cup lard
- A little white wine

## Directions

1. Mix everything well and let it rest for a quarter hour covered with a cloth
2. If the dough is too dry, add liquid or lard until the dough is neither too soft nor too hard
3. Form the empanadas and fry them in lard or butter
