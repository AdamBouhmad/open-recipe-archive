## Ingredients

- a little more than one quarter-pint of flour
- about one ounce of fine salt
- twelve ounces of butter
- three eggs (whites and yolks)
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Place flour on the table and make a hole in the middle
2. Add salt, butter, eggs, and water to the hole
3. Mix everything together and unite the dough without kneading it much or quickly
4. The dough should be neither very tight nor very soft
5. Let it rest and give it three and a half turns, or fold it in three and a half folds as for puff pastry
6. If it is too hard, add a little water; if too soft, add flour
