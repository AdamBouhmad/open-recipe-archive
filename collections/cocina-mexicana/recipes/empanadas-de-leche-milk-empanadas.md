## Ingredients

- two pounds flour
- half cup melted and cold butter
- half cup melted and cold lard
- six egg yolks
- half cup anise water
- half cup tequesquite water
- salt
- milk
- one pound ground almonds
- one pound sifted sugar
- coconut preserve or thick milk

## Directions

1. Combine two pounds of flour, half cup of melted and cold butter, half cup of melted and cold lard, six egg yolks, half cup of anise water, another half cup of settled tequesquite water, a bit of salt, and necessary milk to combine and knead well until bubbles form.
2. If it dries out, soften with more milk.
3. When ready, extend with a rolling pin and fill with almond paste made with one pound of ground almonds and one pound of sifted sugar mixed with either coconut preserve or some thick milk.
4. Fold, seal the edges, and fry in lard.
5. When removed, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon powder.
6. These are customarily made small.
