## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two egg yolks and one egg white
- water as necessary

## Directions

1. Make the dough with one pound of flour, two egg yolks and one egg white and the water necessary to make it tender
2. After kneading, place it on a floured table and extend with a rolling pin
3. Spread cold butter on it and roll it like a cigar
4. Extend again and repeat the other operations three or four times, leaving the dough thicker on the last time
5. Cut the small cakes and make a small hole in the middle of each with your finger, which will be filled with any of the creams or cooked milk preparations
6. Cover this filling with a small disc of dough and bake in the oven
7. They may also be made in the French style without covering the filling
