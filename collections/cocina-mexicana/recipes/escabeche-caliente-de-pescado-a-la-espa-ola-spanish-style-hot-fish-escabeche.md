## Ingredients

- garlic
- lard
- onion slices
- oregano
- clove
- ground pepper
- vinegar
- fish, fried in lard

## Directions

1. Fry some garlic cloves in lard
2. Crush the fried garlic in the same lard with a spoon
3. In the same lard, fry onion slices with oregano
4. Add clove, ground pepper, and vinegar
5. Pour this mixture over the fried fish
