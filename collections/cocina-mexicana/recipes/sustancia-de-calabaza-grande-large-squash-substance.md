## Ingredients

- large squash
- salt
- butter
- flour
- white pepper
- sugar
- egg yolks
- cream
- fried bread rounds

## Directions

1. Divide the squash into pieces, cook in water with salt, discard the water and pass through a strainer.
2. In a saucepan, place a piece of butter and a tablespoon of flour, not letting it brown.
3. Add the substance with white pepper and a little sugar, and let it cook for five minutes.
4. Bind with two egg yolks and a tablespoon of cream if available.
5. Serve on a plate surrounded by fried bread rounds.
