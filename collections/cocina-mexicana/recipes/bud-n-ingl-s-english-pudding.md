## Ingredients

- two pounds beef marrow or two pounds beef kidney fat
- one and a half pounds raisins
- three pounds bread crumbs
- one glass Málaga wine
- two small glasses Cognac brandy
- zest of half lemon
- candied citron
- flour
- salt
- eight whole eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of beef marrow, or if unavailable, two pounds of beef kidney fat.
2. Remove the skin and tendons, cut into small pieces and place in a large bowl.
3. Remove the pits from one and a half pounds of raisins and mix with the marrow or fat.
4. Add three pounds of bread crumbs passed through a sieve; one glass of Málaga wine, two small glasses of Cognac brandy, the zest of half a lemon chopped very fine, a handful of candied citron cut into small pieces, a handful of flour, sufficient salt, and eight whole eggs.
5. Moisten everything with milk, knead with the hands so it mixes well, and form a somewhat liquid paste.
6. Place in a capable pot to cook.
