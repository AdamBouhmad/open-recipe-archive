## Ingredients

- 6 ounces orange blossom jam
- 2 pounds sweet almonds
- 1 pound bitter almonds
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare 6 ounces of orange blossom jam, 2 pounds of sweet almonds, 1 pound of bitter almonds and 1.5 pounds of sugar
2. Peel the almonds and grind them until they form a fine paste
3. Clarify the sugar and let it cook to the low ball point and mix off the heat with the paste and jam
4. Stir the mixture over hot ashes until it takes the necessary consistency and let it cool to form the marzipans
