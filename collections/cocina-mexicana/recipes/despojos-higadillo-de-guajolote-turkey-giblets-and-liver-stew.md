## Ingredients

- Turkey giblets and liver
- Butter or lard
- Flour
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Parsley
- Green onions
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Chile peppers
- Fried potatoes
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Cut the giblets and liver into pieces and soak in cold water for one or two hours until the meat becomes white
2. Drain well and place butter and flour in a pot, stirring until the butter melts
3. Moisten immediately with a glass of water
4. Add salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, a small bundle of parsley and green onions
5. Place the giblets in this mixture and let cook for three quarters of an hour
6. Separately, beat three egg yolks and add lemon juice, verjuice or a splash of vinegar
7. When the giblets are almost cooked, add chile peppers in strips and continue cooking with the pot covered with paper
8. Garnish with fried potato slices and toasted bread slices
9. Pour the egg mixture into the sauce
