## Ingredients

- Lard
- Oil
- Flour
- Pepper
- Water
- Vinegar
- Laurel
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Heat lard and oil
2. Sprinkle with flour and let it brown halfway
3. Sprinkle with powdered pepper
4. Add water, vinegar, laurel, and oregano
5. Once seasoned, let it boil and add oil on top
