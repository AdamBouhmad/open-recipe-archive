## Ingredients

- 12 or 15 crested larks
- a good piece of butter
- salt
- allspice
- a little clove
- cinnamon
- ginger, crushed
- a scraping of nutmeg
- a spoonful of parsley, finely chopped
- the same amount of shallots, chopped the same way
- mushrooms, chopped the same way
- fine herbs
- 2 large spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- 1 spoonful of concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Pluck the crested larks, clean them, and singe them at the flame
2. Place a good piece of butter in a pan with 12 or 15 crested larks, salt, allspice, a little crushed clove, cinnamon, and ginger, and a scraping of nutmeg
3. Place over a strong fire and after sautéing them in the pan for 7 or 8 minutes, add a spoonful full of very finely chopped parsley and the same amount of shallots and mushrooms, also chopped the same way
4. Sauté everything, adding fine herbs, for another 7 or 8 minutes
5. Then moisten the sauté with 2 large spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and one of concentrated broth, stirring everything and mixing it over the fire
6. At the first boil, remove from heat and serve
