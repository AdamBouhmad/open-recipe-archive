## Ingredients

- potato flour (two spoonfuls per person)
- cold broth
- boiling broth

## Directions

1. Dissolve potato flour in cold broth (two spoonfuls per person)
2. Then pour everything into boiling broth, stirring continuously
3. Let cook for five minutes
