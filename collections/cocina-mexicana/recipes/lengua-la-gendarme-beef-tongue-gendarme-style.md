## Ingredients

- Cooked beef tongue
- Broth
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Egg yolks
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Simmer slowly in a casserole one cooked tongue, stripped of its skin and cut into thin slices, with one cup of broth and half a pint of white wine
2. Season moderately with salt and pepper
3. When the sauce is sufficiently reduced, remove the tongue slices from the casserole and place on a platter
4. Add to the sauce a handful of finely chopped parsley, two egg yolks dissolved in a little broth, and a few drops of vinegar
5. Pour this sauce over the tongue at the moment of serving
