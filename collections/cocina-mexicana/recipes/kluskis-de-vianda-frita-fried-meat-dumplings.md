## Ingredients

- one pound of lean ham, finely chopped
- bread soaked in wine and drained
- lemon peel
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Finely chop one pound of lean ham
2. Mix with bread soaked in wine and drained, lemon peel, salt and pepper
3. Make flattened balls with this paste
4. Roll them in breadcrumbs
5. Fry them
