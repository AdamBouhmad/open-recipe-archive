## Ingredients

- 4 cuartillos fresh custard (natillas)
- 8 tablespoons very white powdered sugar
- 2 fresh egg whites
- 1 teaspoon of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix 4 cuartillos of fresh custard with 8 tablespoons of very white powdered sugar, 2 fresh egg whites, and 1 teaspoon of orange blossom water
2. Beat everything with great lightness
3. As it rises, separate the risen portions and put them in a small basket lined with fine linen so it drains
4. Serve as soon as possible
