## Ingredients

- Two raw egg yolks
- A few drops of vinegar
- Very fine oil
- A few drops of lemon or vinegar

## Directions

1. Place two raw egg yolks and a few drops of vinegar in an earthenware dish
2. Mix well with a wooden ladle
3. Slowly add very fine oil in a continuous thin stream, almost drop by drop, stirring constantly
4. When the oil begins to bind with the yolks, add a few drops of lemon or vinegar without stopping stirring
5. Add oil and drops of acid alternately
6. If too thick when serving, soften with a little vinegar and season with salt and sometimes pepper
7. If the oil does not bind with the egg and they appear separated, place one or two yolks in another earthenware dish and slowly add what has broken, stirring constantly until it comes together
