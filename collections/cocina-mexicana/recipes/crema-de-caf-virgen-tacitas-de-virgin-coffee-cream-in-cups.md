## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of coffee
- 2 quartillos of milk
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Roast 2 ounces of coffee and pour it while still hot into 2 quartillos of milk that has already been boiled, with 4 ounces of sugar
2. Cover the vessel and allow the cream to cool
3. Strain to remove the coffee grounds
4. Put 6 egg yolks in a pot and dissolve them with the cream
5. Strain it four times through a sieve or fine strainer
6. Pour into the cups or jars, which are placed in boiling water
7. Allow them to set in a water bath
