## Ingredients

- Chito (hominy), white
- Pork meat
- Zucchini
- Jitomates (tomatoes)
- Chiltepín chile
- Lard

## Directions

1. Soak white chito from the day before
2. The next day, cook it together with pork meat and zucchini
3. Roast the jitomates, fry a little chiltepín, all of which is ground together and fried in a cazuela with lard
4. Add the meat and zucchini with the broth
