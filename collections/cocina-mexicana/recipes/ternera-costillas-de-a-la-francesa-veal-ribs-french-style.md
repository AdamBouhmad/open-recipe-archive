## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- olive oil
- fine herbs, chopped
- juice of a lemon or a little vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- sauce

## Directions

1. Place the veal ribs to fry one or two hours in oil, with all kinds of chopped fine herbs and the juice of a lemon or a little vinegar, salt and pepper.
2. Remove them, soaking them well in the oil.
3. Roll in breadcrumbs and place on the grill to cook over a very gentle fire.
4. When well cooked, serve with sauce.
