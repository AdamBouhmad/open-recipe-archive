## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Syrup of medium point
- Wine
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. After pitting, grind and mix with syrup of medium point, pound for pound
2. Keep on the fire until it has the consistency of paste
3. Arrange as the other pastes, sprinkling wine on the mamones or biscotto, dusting each layer with cinnamon, and decorating the top layer which will be of paste
