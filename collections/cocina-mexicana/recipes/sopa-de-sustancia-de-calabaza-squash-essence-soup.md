## Ingredients

- squash
- milk
- bread, toasted in butter

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into squares after peeling
2. Cook in water
3. Drain well and grind
4. Dissolve the ground substance in milk
5. Use this substance to soak toasted bread slices fried in butter
6. Put the soup over heat, and as soon as it boils a little, serve it, taking care that it is not too thick
