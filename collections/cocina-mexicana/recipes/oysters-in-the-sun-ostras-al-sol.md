## Ingredients

- oysters
- sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the oysters as for skewered oysters, with the only difference that the sauce should be more consistent
2. Incorporate it inside the oysters, and when they are firm and pressed, put them together two by two
3. Roll them in the omelette with a little melted butter and salt
4. Shape them like a flattened ball
5. Fry them like croquettes and garnish the same way with fried parsley in the middle
