## Ingredients

- Five and one quarter pounds of wheat flour
- One pound of yeast
- One and one half pounds of sugar, well ground
- Just over two pounds of lard
- Twenty-two egg yolks
- Salt, well ground
- Water

## Directions

1. Add one pound of yeast to five and one quarter pounds of wheat flour, one and one half pounds of well-ground sugar, just over two pounds of lard, twenty-two egg yolks, sufficient salt well ground, and a little water
2. Knead everything well
3. Flatten with a paddle and cut out biscuits with a mold
4. Place on papers and bake in the oven
