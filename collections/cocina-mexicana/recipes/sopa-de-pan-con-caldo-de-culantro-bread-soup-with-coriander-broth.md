## Ingredients

- Slices of cold bread
- Lard (manteca)
- Broth from the pot, seasoned
- Coriander (culantro), toasted and ground
- Cooked brains, seasoned with salt and pepper powder

## Directions

1. Brown bread slices on a griddle (comal)
2. Arrange in a casserole untied with lard
3. Pour seasoned broth that has been boiled with toasted and ground coriander in necessary quantity for the flavor to stand out
4. Place the casserole over hot coals with fire above
5. Once it has boiled sufficiently so the bread is neither raw nor falling apart, remove from heat
6. Garnish with cooked brains seasoned with salt and pepper powder
