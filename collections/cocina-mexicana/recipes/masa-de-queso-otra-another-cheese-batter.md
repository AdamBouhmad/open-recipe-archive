## Ingredients

- cabbage batter dough
- eggs as needed
- grated cheese or fresh cheese cut into small square pieces

## Directions

1. Make a cabbage batter according to that recipe
2. When it has dried, add as many eggs as necessary so the pieces are not too soft
3. Add a handful of grated cheese or, if using fresh cheese, cut into small square pieces
4. Mix everything well
5. Place pieces on a platform of the thickness desired
6. Brown lightly
7. One quarter hour before serving, bake in a gentle oven
8. If shaping the batter into a thicket or bramble, arrange it on a napkin
