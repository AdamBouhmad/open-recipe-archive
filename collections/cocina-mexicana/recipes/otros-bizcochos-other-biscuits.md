## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- two ounces of leavening
- a little warm water
- one and a half pounds of well-ground sugar
- thirty eggs
- seventeen ounces of lard
- a little salt

## Directions

1. The night before making, mix two pounds of flour, two ounces of leavening, and a little warm water
2. Beat very well, leaving the dough slightly liquid
3. Put it in a pot in a warm place
4. The following day, mix one and a half pounds of well-ground sugar, thirty eggs, seventeen ounces of lard, and a little salt
5. Beat very well and mix everything with the dough made the night before
6. Add the flour that it can absorb
7. Knead much and work it until it makes blisters
8. Let it rest for an hour
9. Form the biscuits in balls or of the desired size
10. Put them in the oven
