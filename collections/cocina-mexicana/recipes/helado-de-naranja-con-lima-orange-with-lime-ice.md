## Ingredients

- 22 ounces white sugar
- 2 cuartillos (approximately 1 liter) clear water
- 8 or 9 oranges
- 2 limes

## Directions

1. Dissolve 22 ounces of white sugar in 2 cuartillos of clear water
2. Select 8 or 9 oranges and 2 limes
