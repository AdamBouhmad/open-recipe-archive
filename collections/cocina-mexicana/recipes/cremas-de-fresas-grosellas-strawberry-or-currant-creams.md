## Ingredients

- strawberries or currants
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Squeeze the strawberries or currants very well and pass the juice through a sieve
2. Add whipped cream to the juice
3. Beat everything together and serve immediately
4. The same method applies for making it with capulines, sour cherries, etc.
