## Ingredients

- Veal loin, cleaned
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper, ground

## Directions

1. In the morning prepare the loin after cleaning it, adding salt, pepper and vinegar, and leave it thus for the rest of the day.
2. In the evening take it out and place it in a press leaving it there until the next day, when it is placed in a frying pan with oil.
3. It is cooked with vinegar, thyme, laurel, and marjoram, and when cooked it is dried, oil is spread on it, it is dusted with ground pepper and left to cool.
4. It is eaten cold with tomato or parsley sauce, and the loin of beef or cow can be prepared in the same manner.
