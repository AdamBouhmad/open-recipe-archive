## Ingredients

- green beans
- butter
- parsley
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter with chopped parsley in a pot
2. Add the green beans, which should be pre-cooked
3. Stir and reduce to sauce
4. Sprinkle with lemon juice or vinegar
