## Ingredients

- parsley
- vinegar
- sugar
- pepper
- egg yolks
- onion
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Grind plenty of parsley with a little vinegar, sugar and pepper
2. Fry the ground mixture
3. Mix in four cooked and ground egg yolks and finely chopped onion
4. Season with just enough salt and add oil
5. Serve with cold meats whether sliced or in a large piece
