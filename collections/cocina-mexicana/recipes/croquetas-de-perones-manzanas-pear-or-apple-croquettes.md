## Ingredients

- pears, prepared and cooked
- egg, beaten
- bread crumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare pears and cook as for pear gato
2. Mold and fry with egg, as with rice croquettes
3. After frying, dust with sugar
4. Serve
