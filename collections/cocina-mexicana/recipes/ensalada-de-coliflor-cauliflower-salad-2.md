## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- garlic
- oil
- butter
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- onion
- pepper
- Chinese oregano

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with salt
2. Fry chopped garlic in oil and butter
3. Add the cauliflower there and fry well
4. Place on a platter
5. Add oil, vinegar, olives, chiles in vinegar, chopped onion, a little salt, pepper, and a little Chinese oregano dusted on top
