## Ingredients

- grapes in small clusters
- egg whites, beaten
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the grapes into small clusters and dip them in beaten egg whites
2. Roll them in fine sugar and blow on them from above so that only the amount of sugar that will have stuck to the egg remains on them
3. Thread them on paper placed on a sieve, or on a wicker hurdle, or on a drying rack, and dry them at a gentle heat
