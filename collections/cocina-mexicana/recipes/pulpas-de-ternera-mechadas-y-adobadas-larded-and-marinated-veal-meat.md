## Ingredients

- veal meat portions
- adobo
- lard
- water
- beef bone broth
- ham
- chorizo
- bay leaf
- turnips
- onions
- salt
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Lard the veal meat portions as described in the previous recipes and place in adobo
2. The next day, cook in a pot with the little water they have and lard
3. Once the water is consumed, fry in their own adobo, being careful not to burn
4. Once well fried, add broth made from beef bones, ham, chorizos, bay leaf, turnips, whole onions and salt
5. Once the meat is cooked, burn lard in a cazuela
6. Empty the pot
7. Add ham in pieces, chorizos, onions stripped of layers, marrow already cooked in the other pot, a little fried and ground bread
8. Leave to dry over two fires
