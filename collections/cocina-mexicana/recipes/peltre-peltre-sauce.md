## Ingredients

- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- scallion
- pepper powder
- bread
- broth

## Directions

1. Put vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, scallion and pepper powder in a saucepan
2. Fry a little bread and dissolve it in broth
3. Add it to the sauce and let it boil for a quarter hour
4. Strain through a sieve
