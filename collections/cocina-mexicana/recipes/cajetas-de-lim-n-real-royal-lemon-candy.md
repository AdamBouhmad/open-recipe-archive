## Ingredients

- lemon flesh and white part of skin
- four and a half pounds of sugar for every eight pounds of lemon
- cochineal or insect dye
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Remove the insides and skins from lemons, keeping only the white and fleshy part
2. Sun-dry them, changing the water daily for eight days
3. Cook and grate the lemon
4. Put in clarified syrup made with four and a half pounds of sugar for every eight pounds of lemon
5. Color with cochineal dye
6. Allow to reach the point where the bottom of the pan can be seen through the paste
7. Add orange flower water when the paste is about to reach its point, so it does not evaporate
