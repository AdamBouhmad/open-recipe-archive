## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one egg
- lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Compose the dough of one pound of flour, one egg, lard, and sugar
2. Knead by striking on a metate, then pass to the table where it is beaten as for hojuelas
3. Make long strips that are wound with the hands and left to air
4. Fry in very hot lard and move with a stick so they form various designs
5. When removed from the lard, put them in syrup with raisins, almonds, and ground cinnamon
