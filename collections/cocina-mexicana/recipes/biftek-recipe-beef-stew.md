## Ingredients

- beef pulp
- water
- bay leaf
- oregano
- thyme
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice the beef and pound it with a wooden mallet
2. Prepare water with bay leaf, oregano, thyme, clove, pepper, cinnamon, a little vinegar, and salt
3. Cook until the meat is tender
4. Remove and partially cook it in butter
5. Place on a platter and garnish with vinegar, oil, sliced onion, and a dusting of oregano
