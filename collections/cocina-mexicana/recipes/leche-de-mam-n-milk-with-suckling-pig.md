## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- egg yolks
- a little starch
- suckling pig (mamón)
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Sweeten the eight quartillos of milk to taste
2. Add egg yolks until it turns yellow
3. Add a little starch
4. Strain and place in a pan over heat
5. Cook until it reaches a high point and remove from fire
6. Cut the suckling pig into slices
7. In dishes, alternate a layer of suckling pig and a layer of milk
8. Sprinkle with cinnamon and garnish with raisins and almonds
