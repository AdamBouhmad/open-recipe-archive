## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- egg
- mulato chile
- cumin
- garlic cloves
- water
- oil
- oregano
- bread, soaked

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower into florets and cook with salt
2. Once cooked, dry them well
3. Beat egg and fry the cauliflower
4. Soak mulato chile and grind with cumin in small quantity and two garlic cloves
5. Fry and add a little water and salt
6. Add a little oil and oregano
7. Once everything is boiling, add the cauliflower and a little soaked bread
