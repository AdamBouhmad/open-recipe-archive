## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Syrup
- Ground cinnamon
- Orange-flower water
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- White wine

## Directions

1. Cook the pineapple, grind it, and simmer in syrup
2. Flavor with plenty of cinnamon and orange-flower water
3. Place over layers of sponge cake moistened with syrup
4. Set the top layer to congeal
