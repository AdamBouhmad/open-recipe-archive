## Ingredients

- rolls
- butter
- pears or apples

## Directions

1. Take the rolls that are needed and slice them like beefsteaks; put the slices in a frying pan with butter, and give them five minutes of cooking over lively fire, being careful to turn them from time to time; sliced good pears or apples should be prepared in advance, which are fried the same way as the rolls and this dish is seasoned in the form of a crown, putting alternately one slice of each thing.
2. Serve as hot as possible.
