## Ingredients

- lamb blood
- tomato, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- garlic, a little
- clove, ground
- cinnamon, ground
- pepper, ground
- parsley, finely chopped
- oregano
- thyme
- oil
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the blood slices, or better yet parboil them, as frying them makes them tough
2. Finely chop plenty of tomato, onion and a little garlic and fry in the same pan in which the blood was fried
3. Add water and then add the blood
4. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper, and add a little finely chopped parsley, oregano and thyme
5. The broth must remain extremely thick
6. Add a little oil and garnish with chiles, olives and capers
