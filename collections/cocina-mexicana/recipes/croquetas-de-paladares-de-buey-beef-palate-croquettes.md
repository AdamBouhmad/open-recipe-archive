## Ingredients

- Beef palates
- Thick velouté sauce
- Egg yolks
- Fine butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the beef palates as described in the article on beef palates à la casera
2. Cut into small squares
3. Mix with thick velouté sauce
4. Bind the sauce with egg yolks and a little fine butter
5. Mix everything well
6. Form into small mounds on a flat surface or baking sheet
7. After cooling, shape as desired
8. Coat with breadcrumbs
9. Bread multiple times by dipping in beaten eggs and breadcrumbs
10. Season with butter, salt, and black pepper
11. Fry the croquettes and serve with fried parsley
