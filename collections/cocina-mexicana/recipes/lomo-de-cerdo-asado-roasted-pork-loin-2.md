## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Wide chile pepper, soaked
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Bay leaves
- Lard
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place the loin in a pot with an adobo of ground garlic, pepper, and soaked wide chile pepper.
2. Add a little vinegar, oregano, some bay leaves, and the corresponding lard and salt.
3. Leave it over fire until the broth is consumed.
4. Then roast the meat in the same pot over two slow fires.
