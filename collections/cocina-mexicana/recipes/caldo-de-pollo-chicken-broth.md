## Ingredients

- One common chicken, plucked and singed
- Five cuartillos of water
- One ounce of the four cold seeds (melon, watermelon, cucumber, and squash seeds)

## Directions

1. Tie with twine a common chicken that has been plucked, and has had its feet singed to remove the scaly skin, after having been disemboweled.
2. Place it in a pot with five cuartillos of water.
3. Crush in a mortar one ounce of the four cold seeds (melon, watermelon, cucumber, and squash seeds).
4. Make a small bundle by tying them in a small white cloth and put it in the pot.
5. Let it cook over low heat until one fifth of the broth is consumed and it is reduced to four cuartillos.
