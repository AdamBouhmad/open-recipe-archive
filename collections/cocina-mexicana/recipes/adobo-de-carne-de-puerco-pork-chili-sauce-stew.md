## Ingredients

- ancho chile, deveined and toasted
- garlic head
- roasted tomato
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- pork meat
- broth from cooking the meat

## Directions

1. Grind deveined and toasted ancho chile with a head of garlic and roasted tomato, clove, pepper, and cinnamon
2. Fry everything very well after grinding
3. Add the meat with the broth in which it was cooked
4. Leave it on the fire until it becomes dry
