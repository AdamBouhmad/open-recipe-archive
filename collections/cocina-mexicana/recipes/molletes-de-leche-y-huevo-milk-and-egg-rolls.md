## Ingredients

- four pounds of finest wheat flour
- twelve egg yolks
- six egg whites
- twelve ounces of ground sugar
- half a pound of lard
- one pound of yeast
- one quarter of a cuartillo of wine
- milk as necessary for kneading

## Directions

1. Knead four pounds of finest wheat flour with twelve egg yolks, six egg whites, twelve ounces of ground sugar, half a pound of lard, one pound of yeast, one quarter cuartillo of wine, and as much milk as necessary for kneading
2. Shape the molletes
3. Bake in the oven
