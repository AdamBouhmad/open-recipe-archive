## Ingredients

- Lard
- Pitted and ground olives
- Large capers
- Little roasted tomato
- Garlic
- Chopped parsley
- Clove
- Pepper powder
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Chicken broth

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard until golden, then remove them
2. Add pitted olives ground with large capers and a little roasted tomato
3. Add chopped parsley, clove powder, and pepper powder
4. Add oil and a little vinegar
5. Put in the chicken with a little of the broth in which it was cooked
6. Let everything simmer
