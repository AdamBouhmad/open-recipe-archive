## Ingredients

- Water
- Wine
- Onion slices
- Carrot slices
- Turnip slices
- A small bundle of fine herbs
- A piece of the best butter
- Pagel fish, gutted

## Directions

1. Boil water and wine in a saucepan with some slices of onions, carrots and turnips, a small bundle of fine herbs and a piece of the best butter.
2. Add the pageles, which should have been gutted in advance.
3. Let them cook, for which a quarter hour is sufficient.
4. Remove the skin and serve with any of the fish sauces.
