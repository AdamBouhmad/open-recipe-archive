## Ingredients

- Lard
- onions
- roasted tomatoes
- garlic
- dried wide chiles
- cumin
- clove
- pepper
- cooked hare
- broth
- plantain
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry sliced onions in lard and once golden, add roasted tomatoes, garlic, soaked wide chiles, cumin, clove and pepper, ground all together
2. When fried and seasoned, place the cooked portions of hare and a little broth, letting it boil until the sauce is of good consistency
3. Serve with fried slices of plantain, small chiles and olives
