## Ingredients

- Pagel fish, gutted but keeping the liver
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onions
- Water or light broth
- Nutmeg
- Capers

## Directions

1. Gut the fish reserving the liver and make some cuts in them.
2. Marinate with butter, salt, pepper, parsley and onions, these last two things chopped very fine.
3. Roast on both sides on the grill.
4. While roasting, remove the livers and grind them with a little water or light broth.
5. Make a sauce with them, mixed with good butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg and capers.
6. Serve by pouring this sauce, well heated, over the fish.
