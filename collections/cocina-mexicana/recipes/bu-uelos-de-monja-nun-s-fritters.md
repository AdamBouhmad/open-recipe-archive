## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- four ounces of sugar
- one cup of water
- scraped lemon peel
- flour
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a pot two ounces of butter, four ounces of sugar, one cup of water and scraped lemon peel
2. Add flour and boil until a paste forms, which is ready when it comes away easily from the pot
3. Mix in three eggs and stir everything very well
4. Spread on a plate with the handle of a kitchen spoon and cut into small pieces
5. Drop into hot oil and drain on a napkin
6. Serve dusted with fine sugar
