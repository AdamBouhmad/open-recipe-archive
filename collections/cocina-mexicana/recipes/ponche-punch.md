## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- ½ pound of piloncillo sugar
- a little essential oil of lemon
- about ½ quartillo of strong green tea infusion
- proportioned amount of celandine syrup
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 quartillos of superior brandy or rum

## Directions

1. Rub the peel of one lemon against a piece of piloncillo sugar weighing half a pound
2. Pour a little essential oil of lemon onto the sugar-impregnated piece
3. Add about ½ quartillo of a strong infusion of green tea with a proportioned amount of celandine syrup
4. Squeeze the juice of 2 lemons, removing the seeds first, and pour onto everything
5. Add 2 quartillos of superior brandy or rum
6. Set fire to it, agitate the flame with the punch ladle
7. When the lemon has reduced to two-thirds, extinguish the flame by blowing it out
8. Serve the punch hot in glasses
