## Ingredients

- twelve eggs (three eggs for each of four omelets)
- frangipan (see frangipan dough)
- fine powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make four omelets with three eggs each, making them as thin as possible
2. Decorate with frangipan (see frangipan dough)
3. Roll them in the form of envelopes
4. Cut off the extremities
5. Place on a plate
6. Dust with fine powdered sugar
7. Glaze and serve
