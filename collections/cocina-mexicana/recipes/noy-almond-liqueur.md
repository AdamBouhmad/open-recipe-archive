## Ingredients

- 8 quartillos brandy
- 0.5 pound almond kernels from apricots
- 4 ounces almond kernels from peaches and sour cherries
- 4 quartillos water
- 4 ounces orange blossom water
- 2.5 pounds crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds in water for twenty-four hours to loosen the skin.
2. Peel them, crack them and put them to infuse in the brandy for fifteen days.
3. After this, proceed to distillation.
4. When the sugar has been perfectly dissolved in the distilled water, add the orange blossom water, form the mixture and filter.
