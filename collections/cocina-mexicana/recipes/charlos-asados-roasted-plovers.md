## Ingredients

- plovers
- giblets (except gizzard)
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- bread (slices)
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Extract the giblets and chop them, except the gizzard, with bacon, salt, pepper, parsley and garlic.
2. Stuff the birds with this mixture.
3. Wrap in bacon and roast, basting them.
4. Catch the drippings on slices of bread.
5. Serve over these same slices with a spicy sauce.
