## Ingredients

- Six ounces of marshmallow root
- Four pounds of water
- Six pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Cook six ounces of marshmallow root in four pounds of water to extract the mucilage
2. Strain the mixture
3. Mix in six pounds of sugar
4. Cook until the consistency of syrup
5. If not to be used soon, cook until it reaches the caramel stage to prevent fermentation
6. When ready to use, dissolve in orange flower water
