## Ingredients

- Two fat teal ducks
- String
- Lemons, whole

## Directions

1. Choose two well-fattened teal ducks and pluck them without leaving any down
2. Cut the wings very close to the body and remove the necks
3. Draw the birds and singe them
4. Clean them and wring the legs
5. Tie them with string and rub all over with their livers
6. Place on a roasting spit and cook
7. When cooked, remove from the spit, untie and dress for serving with whole lemons
