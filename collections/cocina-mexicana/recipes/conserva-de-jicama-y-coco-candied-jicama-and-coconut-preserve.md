## Ingredients

- jicama
- coconut
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook the grated jicama in two boils and strain through a sieve
2. Clean and grate the coconut and combine with jicama
3. Rinse both with hot water and strain again
4. Place in good syrup, bring to proper consistency, and add orange blossom water
