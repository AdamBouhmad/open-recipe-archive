## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- yemas de huevos crudos (raw egg yolks)
- mantequilla derretida y fría (melted and cooled butter)
- leche cocida (cooked milk)
- sal fina (fine salt)
- azúcar en polvo (powdered sugar)
- hojas de maiz (corn husks)

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well and dry it in the sun on a napkin.
2. When it has dried well, grind it, and for each pound of this powder add four raw egg yolks and six ounces of melted and cooled butter.
3. Beat it, moistening the masa with cooked milk and adding the necessary fine salt, ground.
4. Continue beating, and when it begins to set, add powdered sugar to taste.
5. Beat it more until it reaches the proper point, which is known when a small piece of the masa floats on water.
6. Form the tamales in well-drained corn husks and they can be filled with any milk-based or fruit dessert.
7. Cook them with the opening upward so they do not leak, taking care that the boiling water does not reach them, but that they cook only by steam.
