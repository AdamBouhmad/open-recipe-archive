## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Salt
- All spices
- Parsley
- Fried garlic
- Saffron
- Soaked bread crumb
- Chile of the season
- Salt
- Water
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Potato slices
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the potatoes to fry, add water and salt, and when half cooked add all spices, parsley, fried garlic, saffron and soaked bread crumb, dissolve everything and add it to the potatoes, letting them cook for a while
2. Also put potatoes to fry with chile of the season and salt, add water, pound some garlic, cumin and some potato slices, dissolve with vinegar and let cook for a while
