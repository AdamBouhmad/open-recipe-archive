## Ingredients

- peas
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- hot water

## Directions

1. Put the peas to cook and take one part and grind it
2. Strain through a napkin
3. Fry the strained mixture in butter adding salt, pepper, and hot water
4. When seasoned, incorporate the whole peas
5. Let simmer a little and serve in broth
