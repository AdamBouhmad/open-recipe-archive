## Ingredients

- Beef tripe
- Onion rings
- Butter
- Olive oil
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley
- Verjuice
- Lemon

## Directions

1. After cooking, cut into small square pieces
2. Place onion rings with butter and one spoonful of oil in a casserole over high heat
3. Add pepper, salt, and parsley
4. Sauté the tripe, turning until it takes on a good brown color
5. Serve hot with verjuice and lemon juice
