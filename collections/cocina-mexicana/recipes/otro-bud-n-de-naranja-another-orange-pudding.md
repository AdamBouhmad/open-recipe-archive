## Ingredients

- four eggs
- six tablespoons of hard biscuit
- half tablespoon of orange zest
- half tablespoon of refined brandy
- raisins
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs as for frying and when they are very high add six tablespoons of hard biscuit, half tablespoon of orange zest and half tablespoon of refined brandy, chopped raisins and almonds
2. Grind everything and cook like the other puddings
