## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of water
- one quarter of butter
- small pieces of lemon peel
- one quarter of sugar
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan one cuartillo of water, one quarter of butter, some small pieces of lemon peel, one quarter of sugar and a little salt
2. Bring to boil, and before it begins to boil remove from heat and remove the lemon peel, adding flour little by little, as much as the water can absorb
3. Stir continuously and place on fire for five or six minutes, without stopping stirring
4. When the paste is ready it will be known because it separates from the pan; then transfer to a glazed bowl, and add eggs one by one until the paste sticks to the fingers
5. Butter and flour a platter, and place this paste on top in pieces the size of a walnut
6. Add chopped almonds with ground sugar, and put in the oven; this paste can be made into rolls, giving them whatever shape is desired; it should be baked in a moderate oven
