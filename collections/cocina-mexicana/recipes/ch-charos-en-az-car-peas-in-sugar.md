## Ingredients

- peas
- butter
- ham
- parsley
- onions
- garlic cloves
- flour
- water
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and fry them in a little butter in a pan
2. Slice the ham and make a parsley bouquet
3. Take two onions and two garlic cloves and begin to fry the peas again
4. Once fried, add half a tablespoon of flour
5. Add the ham, onions, garlic, and parsley and continue frying well
6. Pour in enough water for them to cook well
7. Add a little salt and four or five sugar cubes
