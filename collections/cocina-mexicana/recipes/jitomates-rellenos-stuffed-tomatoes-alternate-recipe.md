## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- parsley
- cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- anchovies
- salt
- oil
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the stem from the top of the tomato to extract the seeds with a coffee spoon
2. Squeeze out the water it contains with light finger pressure
3. Fill with a paste made of finely chopped parsley, cucumbers, hard-boiled eggs, and capers
4. Add small pieces of anchovy, salt, and oil
5. Once filled, wrap the tomato in bread crumbs
6. Place in an oven
