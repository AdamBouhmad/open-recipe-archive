## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds
- egg white
- a little orange blossom water
- 1 pound sugar syrup at flowering point

## Directions

1. Grind 1 pound of almonds in a metate, adding egg white from time to time
2. Put this paste in 1 pound of sugar syrup at flowering point and incorporate it well with a wooden spoon
3. Place in a pot on the fire and stir vigorously by hand until the mass becomes workable, taking care that it does not stick
4. Place on a sugar-dusted board, dust it as well, roll it, let it rest a little and form the desired figures or pass the mass through a piping bag
