## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- lard
- pepper
- flour
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage into thin slices
2. Blanch them in boiling water with a little salt, drain them, then chop them after refreshing
3. Put them on the fire in a cacerola with lard, salt and pepper
4. Add flour, which is scattered over the top while always stirring the cabbage
5. Add meat broth, stir always and let cook until the liquid decreases sufficiently
