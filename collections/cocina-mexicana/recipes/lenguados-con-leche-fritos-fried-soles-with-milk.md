## Ingredients

- sole fish
- milk
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Select two good soles and after emptying, washing and drying them well, open them along the spine
2. Soak them in milk, roll them in flour and fry them
3. When cooked through, drain them and serve with fried parsley
4. Before frying, one can or rather should remove the skin, which makes them more delicate and worthy of a finely set table
