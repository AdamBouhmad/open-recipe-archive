## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- less than 1/2 quartillo virgin honey
- lemon juice
- egg white for clarification
- 16 egg whites, beaten
- almonds, pine nuts, hazelnuts, walnuts, and sesame seeds, all toasted and in small pieces
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Make syrup with 2 pounds of sugar and less than 1/2 quartillo of virgin honey
2. Clarify with lemon and egg
3. Strain and put over fire
4. Reach the point where it hardens in water
5. Remove from fire
6. Beat until it begins to bread
7. Add a few drops of lemon
8. Add 16 well-beaten egg whites and beat until thick or it sounds hollow
9. Place the pan over low fire, stirring or beating carefully so it does not burn, until it reaches the proper point
10. The proper point is known by testing a little on the tongue without it sticking
11. Vary the quantities by adding 1 quartillo of virgin honey and 14 egg whites for every 4 pounds of sugar, or to taste
12. When the operation is complete, add almonds, pine nuts, hazelnuts, walnuts and sesame seeds, all toasted and in small pieces, and small cinnamon sticks
13. Pour into small boxes or dishes
