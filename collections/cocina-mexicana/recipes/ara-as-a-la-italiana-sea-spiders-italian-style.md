## Ingredients

- six sea spiders
- half bottle white wine
- onion slices
- chirivia or xitomate
- parsley sprigs
- bay leaf
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean six sea spiders as described previously
2. Cut off the head and tail
3. Do not cut along the sides
4. Place them in a casserole with half a bottle of white wine, some slices of onion and chirivia or xitomate, sprigs of parsley, a bay leaf and clove
5. Add pepper, cinnamon, a little vinegar and oil
6. Let the broth reduce until it reaches regular consistency
