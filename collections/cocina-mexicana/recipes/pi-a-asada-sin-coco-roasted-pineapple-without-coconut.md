## Ingredients

- cooked pineapple
- clarified syrup made with two pounds of sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Proceed as in the previous article, omitting the coconut and making the syrup with only two pounds of sugar
2. Let everything boil until it thickens
3. Remove from the fire so the pineapple browns
4. When browned, decorate with ground cinnamon on top
