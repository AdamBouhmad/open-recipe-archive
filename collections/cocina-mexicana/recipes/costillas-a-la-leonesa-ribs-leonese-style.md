## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- bacon
- gherkins
- anchovies
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- young garlic
- fine herbs
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- broth
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Lard the ribs with bacon, gherkins and strands of anchovies, placing them in marinade with oil, for at least one hour, with salt, pepper, young garlic, fine herbs and parsley, all chopped. Afterward coat them with slices of bacon and cook them in the marinade.
2. Put a little butter and a handful of flour in a pan, add fine herbs and chopped garlic, moistening with well-defatted broth. To serve, add the juice of a lemon or a few drops of vinegar.
