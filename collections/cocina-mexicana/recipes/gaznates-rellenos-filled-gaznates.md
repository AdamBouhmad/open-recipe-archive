## Ingredients

- three pounds flour
- eight egg yolks
- half small cup white wine
- two ounces melted lard
- sour orange juice
- tequesquite water
- sweet potato paste or coconut paste or almond paste

## Directions

1. Knead three pounds of flour with eight egg yolks, half a small cup of white wine, two ounces of melted lard, a little sour orange juice and tequesquite water
2. If these are not enough to moisten the flour well, add a little water, and when the dough blisters, extend with a rolling pin
3. Cut and form the gaznates, fry in lard, let cool and fill with sweet potato paste, coconut paste or almond paste
