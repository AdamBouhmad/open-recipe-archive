## Ingredients

- Pork ears
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook as previously directed for feet à la Santa-Menehould
2. Let warm slightly and open a little on the inner part to give them an agreeable shape
3. Moisten in their cooking liquid
4. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Fry and serve hot
