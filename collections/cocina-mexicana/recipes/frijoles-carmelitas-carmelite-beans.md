## Ingredients

- fat white beans
- tequesquite
- lard
- garlic cloves, chopped
- chilchotes, chopped
- xitomates, chopped
- water
- salt
- saffron
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- cooked onions
- beaten eggs
- oil
- a little vinegar
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Put fat white beans to cook with a little tequesquite, and when cooked, wash them.
2. Fry chopped garlic cloves in plenty of lard until they brown, and without removing them, fry chopped chilchotes and xitomates there as well.
3. Add a little water and season with sufficient salt.
4. Put the washed beans in this broth, and when they have seasoned by boiling in it, add saffron, clove, pepper, and cinnamon, all ground.
5. Add onions cooked separately, beaten eggs, oil, a little vinegar, and some fresh parsley.
6. Let it thicken appropriately.
