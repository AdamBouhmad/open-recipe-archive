## Ingredients

- Good portion of tomatoes
- Green chile peppers
- Fresh cilantro branches
- Turkey meat
- Lard
- Water or turkey broth

## Directions

1. Grind a good portion of tomatoes with some green chiles, deveining them if you want the mole to be less spicy
2. Also grind one or two branches of fresh cilantro
3. Fry the raw turkey in pieces
4. Separately fry the tomatoes with chiles and cilantro
5. Add the turkey and water or broth in which the turkey was cooked
