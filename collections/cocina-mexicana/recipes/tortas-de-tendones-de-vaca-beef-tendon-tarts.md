## Ingredients

- Beef tendons
- Dough
- Ham slices
- Butter
- Red substance
- Champagne wine or white wine

## Directions

1. Make them the same way as the previous article, with the only difference of briefly boiling the tendons in water, removing them from it immediately and putting them in cold water
2. When they are well drained, place them in the tart in the same way as the ribs
