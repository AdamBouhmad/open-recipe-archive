## Ingredients

- chile mulato (dried mulato chile)
- garlic heads
- cumin
- salt
- suckling pig
- manteca (lard)
- chilote (chile condiment)

## Directions

1. Soak the mulato chile, devein, wash and grind with two or three heads of garlic and some cumin and salt.
2. Wash the suckling pig, drain and dry it.
3. Cut it into strips and coat it with the chile.
4. Grease a large earthenware pot with lard in which the pig fits and send it to the oven; or if not, dig a pit in the earth about two or three quarters deep or as much as is necessary.
5. Put stones, gravel and much firewood in it, light it and let it burn until consumed.
6. In a new petate well sprinkled with water, wrap the pig, tie it and throw it in the hole, then a board and then earth.
7. It stays there for twenty-four hours.
8. Once the time has passed, remove it and serve with chilote.
9. Can be made with mutton, kid, venison, etc.
