## Ingredients

- flour
- anise water
- butter
- eggs
- salt
- syrup

## Directions

1. To two pounds of flour, cook a little anise water and put it back on the heat in a small pan
2. Add a spoonful of butter and boil until the butter is cooked
3. Remove from heat and stir in the two pounds of flour until it forms a thick paste and put it back on the heat and cook until it forms a ball and separates from the pan
4. Remove from heat and put in a tub until it cools enough that you can hold it in your hand; then gradually incorporate two by two the eggs with whites and yolks until it is in a state to pick up a little ball
5. Then add salt; the butter should be neither hot nor cold so they have room to puff and add syrup
