## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Salt
- Water
- Much onion
- Garlic
- Very ripe xitomates
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Plantain
- Sweet potato
- Pineapple
- Apple
- Tornachiles
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Lard
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Clove
- Sugar
- Olives
- Chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens in salted water
2. Divide into pieces
3. Slice much onion, garlic, and very ripe xitomates, a little parsley and oregano
4. Slice plantain, sweet potato, pineapple, and apple
5. Cut tornachiles into quarters, clean and chop almonds and raisins
6. Fry part of the xitomate, garlic, onion, and parsley in lard, and grease another casserole with it
7. Place a layer of xitomate, sliced onion, chopped parsley and garlic
8. Add some raisins, almonds, and capers and quarters of tornachiles
9. Alternate a layer of sliced fruits with another of chicken pieces, continuing in this order of layers until finishing with one of chicken
10. Over it pour the xitomate and all the rest that was fried
11. Pour chicken broth on top, and vinegar with ground cinnamon, pepper, and clove
12. Add sugar dissolved in water and put the covered dish to two heats, tasting the broth to reduce the acidity or sweetness so neither overshadows the other
13. When the broth is almost consumed, serve the covered dish, adorning the plate with olives, chiles in vinegar, and caperberries
