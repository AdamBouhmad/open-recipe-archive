## Ingredients

- hip bones
- breast bones
- one marrow bone
- two whole onions
- one head of garlic
- ground tomatoes
- two cloves
- two peppers
- two cinnamon sticks
- diced ham
- herbs, thyme and oregano
- carrots
- turnip
- Jerez or Malaga wine
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the hip bones, breast bones, one marrow bone, two whole onions, one head of garlic, ground tomatoes, two cloves, two peppers, and two cinnamon sticks, all ground
2. Add diced ham, herbs, thyme, oregano, carrots, and turnip to the caldillo
3. Place the pot over a gentle fire and add Jerez or Malaga wine and a little vinegar
4. Once seasoned, transfer to a saucepan and place on embers to give it a boil with the soup
5. If it lacks wine, add it before serving the soup
