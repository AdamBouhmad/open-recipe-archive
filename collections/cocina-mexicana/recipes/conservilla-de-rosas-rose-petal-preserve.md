## Ingredients

- white roses, very fresh
- sugar (one-half ounce of roses for each pound of sugar)

## Directions

1. Select very fresh white roses, remove the petals and chop them very finely.
2. Make a clarified sugar syrup; when it reaches the consistency of feather point, remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
3. Add the roses to the syrup and stir, turning the pan several times.
4. Complete the operation as is common for all small preserves (see FRENCH PRESERVES).
