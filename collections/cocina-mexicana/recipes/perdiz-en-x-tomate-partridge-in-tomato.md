## Ingredients

- partridges, cleaned
- lard and oil
- onions
- garlic cloves with skin
- tomato slices
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the cleaned partridges in a pot lined with lard and oil
2. Once fried, add quartered onions, unpeeled garlic cloves, tomato slices, whole clove, cinnamon, and pepper
3. Add sufficient salt and water
4. Cover the pot and maintain over low heat until the partridges are cooked
5. Season the broth well
