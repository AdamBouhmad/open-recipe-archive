## Ingredients

- Suckling pig, well cleaned
- Salt
- Ground black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Water
- Lard

## Directions

1. Clean the pig well and blanch it in water with plenty of salt and a handful of ground pepper.
2. Once well cured, remove it, drain it, and dry it thoroughly so no water remains.
3. Rub it inside and outside with another handful of pepper, cloves, and cinnamon ground with water, forming a somewhat thick paste.
4. Place it in a cazuela with a little lard and roast it in the oven.
