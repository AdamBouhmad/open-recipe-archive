## Ingredients

- fish
- lard
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- oil
- vinegar
- sliced onions
- small chiles in vinegar
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Fry the fish in lard.
2. Separately fry finely chopped onion and parsley with a pinch of salt, pepper and breadcrumbs.
3. When it browns, add plenty of oil and vinegar, and the portions of fish.
4. Remove from heat and serve with sliced onions, small chiles in vinegar, olives and capers.
