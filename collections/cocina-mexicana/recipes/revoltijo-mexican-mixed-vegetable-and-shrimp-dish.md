## Ingredients

- white beans
- peas
- plantains
- zucchini
- green beans
- toasted sesame seeds
- small shrimp portions
- dried ancho chile
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook white beans, peas, plantains, zucchini, and green beans
2. Make a pipián with toasted sesame seeds
3. Fry small portions of shrimp and soaked and refreshed ancho chile when making the pipián
4. Fry the vegetables separately
5. Combine everything with the pipián
6. Mix in beaten eggs so that the eggs are not visible and the mixture remains compact
