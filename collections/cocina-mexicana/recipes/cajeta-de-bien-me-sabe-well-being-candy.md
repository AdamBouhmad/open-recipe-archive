## Ingredients

- Four pounds of sugar
- One pound of blanched and ground almonds
- One chicken breast, well washed, cooked in coals wrapped in wet paper, shredded in orange blossom water
- One quartillo of milk
- A little finely ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Make syrup with four pounds of sugar at mirror point
2. Add the blanched and ground almonds
3. Add the prepared chicken breast mixed with one quartillo of milk and a little ground biscuit
4. Bring to cajeta point and set aside
