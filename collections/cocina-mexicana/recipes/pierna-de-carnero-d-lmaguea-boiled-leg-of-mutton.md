## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- cloth, very tightly woven
- water
- carrots
- turnips
- salt
- vegetables for garnish
- sauce
- melted lard

## Directions

1. Cook wrapped in a very tightly woven cloth
2. Place in a pot full of water with carrots, turnips, and salt
3. After two hours of boiling, remove from the cloth
4. Arrange vegetables around it for serving
5. Serve accompanied with sauce and melted lard
