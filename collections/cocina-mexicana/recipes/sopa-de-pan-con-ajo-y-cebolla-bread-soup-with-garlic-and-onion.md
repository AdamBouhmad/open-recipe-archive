## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic cloves
- pepper
- onion, sliced
- broth from the pot, seasoned
- parsley leaves
- marjoram
- sliced bread, toasted in a comal
- burnt lard
- parsley leaves
- fried brains

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard until they brown, remove and grind together with pepper
2. In the same lard, fry sliced onion, and when fried, add the ground garlic with pepper, seasoned broth from the pot, and parsley and marjoram leaves
3. Once this broth has boiled, brush a casserole with lard and place layers of toasted bread slices in it
4. Pour the broth over it and place the soup over gentle fire until cooked
5. Remove from heat, pour burnt lard over it and brown with a comal with fire
6. Decorate with parsley leaves and fried brains as in the previous recipe
