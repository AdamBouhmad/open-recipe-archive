## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- stock of cooked chard or spinach
- raw oil
- fried onion
- spices
- garlic cloves
- salt
- rice
- hazelnut sauce
- eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the chickpeas in stock of cooked chard or spinach
2. Once soaked, washed, and blanched, cook with a little raw oil
3. When cooked, add fried onion and all spices crushed with garlic cloves
4. Season with salt
5. They can be thickened by adding one-sixth part rice
6. Hazelnut sauce and eggs can also be added, but the most common method is to crush a spoonful of chickpeas with egg yolks
