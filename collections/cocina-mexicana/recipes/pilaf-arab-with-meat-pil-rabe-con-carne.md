## Ingredients

- rice
- mutton broth
- mutton meat

## Directions

1. Proceed the same as in the first pilaf article
2. The only difference is that when the rice begins to cook, place in the same pot the mutton that has already been cooked, divided into small portions
3. Cover the pot and proceed as in the preceding articles
