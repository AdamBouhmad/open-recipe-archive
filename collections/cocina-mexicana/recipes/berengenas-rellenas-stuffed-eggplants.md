## Ingredients

- eggplants
- marrow
- chicken breast or roasted lamb meat
- egg yolks
- hard bread crumbs
- salt
- nutmeg
- marrow

## Directions

1. Split the eggplants lengthwise
2. Remove part of the interior substance, leaving a good portion of pulp attached to the skin which should not be damaged
3. Prepare the filling separately by mincing the pulp with marrow and an equal quantity of chicken breast or roasted lamb meat
4. Mix egg yolks with very crumbled hard bread crumbs and unite this filling with the first by kneading well
5. Add salt and grated nutmeg, but not too much
6. Fill the eggplant halves with this filling, sealing the surface with a little egg yolk applied with feather barbs
7. Place on a platter put over the fire
8. Melt marrow and with it baste the eggplants
9. Cover the platter and place a live fire on the lid and gentle heat below
10. Serve on the same platter
