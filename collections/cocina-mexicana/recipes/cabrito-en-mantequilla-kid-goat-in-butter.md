## Ingredients

- kid goat, cleaned
- butter
- garlic cloves
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- water

## Directions

1. After cleaning, divide into regular-sized portions and fry in butter with whole garlic cloves and corresponding salt
2. Once the meat is fried, add water and the garlic cloves that were fried, grinding them with pepper and a few bay leaves
3. Be careful to turn the meat on all sides, and once the broth reduces the kid goat will remain in the butter
