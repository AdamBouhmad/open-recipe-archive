## Ingredients

- beef
- poultry substance or other
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very fine.
2. Placing it on the heat, add a little poultry substance or other.
3. Add a little broth and white wine to moisten it.
4. Leave on the heat until it is at the right point.
5. Serve immediately.
