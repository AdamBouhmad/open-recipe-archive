## Ingredients

- One cup whole rice
- Water
- Sugar
- Butter
- Tomatoes
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Candied citron
- Vinegar
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Cook one cup of whole rice in water with a piece of sugar; once cooked, set aside to cool and make a picadillo fried in butter with tomatoes, raisins, almonds, capers, candied citron and a little vinegar.
2. Beat four eggs, first the whites, and once they are very well beaten mix in the yolks and beat them; once done, mix in the rice leaving aside a little of the beaten egg.
3. Grease a pan very well with butter and put half the rice at the bottom, the picadillo in the middle and the rest of the rice on top of the meat.
4. Pour over it the bit of egg that was set aside and place in a small stove that has all the fire removed and only the embers left.
5. Do not fill the pan to the top but leave the cake about a finger below the rim, or half a finger so it puffs up; on top place a griddle with strong heat and take care that it is well distributed across the entire griddle so it browns evenly.
6. To know when it is done, insert a popote (drinking straw); when it comes out clean it is ready. When removed, sprinkle powdered sugar on top.
