## Ingredients

- Chiles anchos and pasillas
- Seeds from the chiles, toasted
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Sesame seeds, toasted
- Salt
- Turkey, in quarters or pieces
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove veins from chiles anchos and pasillas, toast them and soak
2. Grind a few seeds from each kind after toasting, and then the chiles, garlic, few cloves, toasted sesame seeds, and season with salt
3. In this adobo, place the turkey in quarters or pieces in a pot and the next day add water
