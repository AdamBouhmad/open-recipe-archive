## Ingredients

- sole fillets
- salt
- pepper
- haddock quenelles
- butter
- light fish broth
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the fillets, skin them, and prepare them so they remain well squared
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Spread them out lengthwise and place haddock quenelles on top
4. Roll them up starting from the tail and wrap them in paper well buttered, tying with string
5. Cook in light fish broth
6. When done, drain, arrange on a plate, and pour Italian sauce over them
