## Ingredients

- pompano, soaked and cleaned
- flour
- oil or lard
- onion, sliced
- tomatoes, roasted and ground
- garlic
- pepper
- water
- capers
- parsley, chopped
- bread, fried in lard and ground
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Soak and clean the fish thoroughly, then dry it
2. Roll in flour and fry in oil or lard
3. Remove from the pot and fry sliced onion and roasted ground tomatoes with garlic and pepper in the same pot
4. Add a little water, capers, and chopped parsley
5. Let the sauce come to a boil, then add the pompano
6. Boil until the fish is cooked
7. Thicken the sauce with bread fried in lard and ground
8. Serve adorned with small chiles and olives
