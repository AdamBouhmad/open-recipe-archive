## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- lemon
- butter
- eggs
- corn
- sugar

## Directions

1. To one quarter pound of flour add one quarter pound of milk and a lemon
2. Use flour that is not sweet, add the milk in small amounts, because if much is added at once it forms many balls
3. Then heat it in a little butter to put in the bottom of the pan, cook as the previous ones without ceasing to stir and removing the crusts that stick to the pan
4. Once the dough is cooked, begin to knead it
5. As soon as you can pick it up with your hand, begin adding eggs one by one; once four or five have been added, add the rest two by two and knead very well so the flour does not become lumpy
6. Add the corn and one ounce of ground sugar in powder so they come out whole
7. Then fry them but first let them rest a while
