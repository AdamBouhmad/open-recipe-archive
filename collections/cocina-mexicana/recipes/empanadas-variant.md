## Ingredients

- 1 pound sifted flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 ounce lard
- 3/4 cup good oil
- water as needed

## Directions

1. To 1 pound of sifted flour add 3 egg yolks, 1 ounce of lard, 3/4 cup of good oil, and water as needed.
2. Knead until soft and consistent.
3. Extend the dough with a rolling pin, cut to desired size, and finish as described in the previous recipe.
