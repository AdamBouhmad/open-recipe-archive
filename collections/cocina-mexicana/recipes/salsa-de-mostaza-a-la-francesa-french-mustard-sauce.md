## Ingredients

- mustard, in desired quantity
- shallot or herbs from the reinforced sauce of previous articles
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add a little shallot or herbs from the reinforced sauce of the previous articles to the mustard, along with salt and pepper
2. Thin everything with oil and vinegar, adding them little by little
3. Add some raw egg yolks immediately after
4. Stir everything until the mixture is perfect and the sauce is well bound
