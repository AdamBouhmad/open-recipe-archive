## Ingredients

- Poultry
- Vinegar
- Broth
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onion
- Garlic
- Bay leaf
- Flour
- Oil
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade with vinegar, broth, salt, parsley, onions, pounded garlic, and bay leaf.
2. Place pieces of boned poultry that was cooked the day before in the marinade.
3. Keep on warm ashes for two or more hours.
4. Remove and drain the pieces.
5. Wrap in flour.
6. Fry and garnish with parsley.
