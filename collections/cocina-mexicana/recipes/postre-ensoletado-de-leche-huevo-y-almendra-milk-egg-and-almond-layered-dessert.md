## Ingredients

- 4 quartillos milk
- sugar
- egg yolks as needed for yellow color
- 2 ounces soaked and cleaned almonds
- 2 ounces pine nuts
- biscuits or slices of sponge cake
- cinnamon powder for decoration

## Directions

1. Sweeten 4 quartillos of milk, heavily loaded with sugar, and add as many egg yolks as needed so it remains very yellow
2. When the yolks are well dissolved, strain the milk, put it on heat
3. When it is boiling, mix in 2 ounces of soaked and clean almonds and 2 ounces of pine nuts, both ground
4. Take care to keep stirring so it does not stick
5. When it is close to reaching the point, place in the dishes a layer of this cooked milk, another of biscuits or slices of sponge cake and on top another of the same milk
6. After it cools, decorate with cinnamon powder
