## Ingredients

- Cooked potatoes
- Sugar
- Loose grated cheese
- Butter
- Salt
- Eggs
- Bread flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. After cooking, grind the potatoes with a little sugar
2. Mix in loose grated cheese, a little butter, salt if needed, whole unbeaten eggs, and a powder of bread
3. Once well mixed, add to the skillet with hot lard and place to cook over two fires
