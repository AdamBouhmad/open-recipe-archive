## Ingredients

- manteca
- xitomates asados y molidos
- ajo
- peregil bastante
- pan remojado en vinagre
- clavo
- cominos
- sal
- alcaparras
- jamón cocido en lonjitas
- pollos
- caldo
- aceitunas
- chilitos

## Directions

1. Fry roasted and ground tomatoes with garlic, plenty of parsley, bread soaked in vinegar, clove and cumin in butter
2. Season with salt and add capers and strips of cooked ham
3. Place the chickens with their broth
4. Let simmer until the sauce is well seasoned
5. When removing from the heat, add olives and small chiles
