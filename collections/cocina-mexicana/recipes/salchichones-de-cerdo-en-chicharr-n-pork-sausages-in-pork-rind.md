## Ingredients

- pork loins
- chorizos
- ham
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds
- walnuts
- toasted sesame seeds
- clove
- cinnamon
- wine
- vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- tequesquite water
- pork rinds
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the loins into small pieces and mix them with chorizos, ham, pine nuts, raisins, almonds, walnuts, and plenty of toasted sesame seeds, clove, ground cinnamon, plenty of wine, vinegar, salt, and sugar.
2. Soak sponjed pork rinds in tequesquite water; wash them, and filling them with the said mixture so they have the shape of a pillow, put them to cook with lard and salt.
3. When the broth is consumed, add more red wine so the pork rinds toast, and cut into slices, serve them hot.
