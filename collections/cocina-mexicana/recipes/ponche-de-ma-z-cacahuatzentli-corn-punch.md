## Ingredients

- Black corn (cacahuatzentli), or also white
- Sugar
- A little dye made with small bread, carmine, or cochineal

## Directions

1. Prepare the black cacahuatzentli corn, although it can also be made with white, in the same way corn is prepared in nixtamal for making atole
2. Grind and toast it
3. Put it to boil with sugar to taste until it reaches such consistency that when cold it can be cut with a knife
4. Before pouring it into plates or dishes in the desired shape, mix it with a little dye made with small bread, carmine, or cochineal, so that it does not dye all the punch but forms veins, so that after being cut with a knife it creates a decorative effect
