## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- fat
- yeast
- anise
- salt
- ground almond

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add six eggs, a little fat, a piece of yeast, a little anise, salt and a little ground almond
2. Beat on a metate and make the fritters very thin
