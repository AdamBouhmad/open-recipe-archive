## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of walnuts
- half a pound of almonds
- eight egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point with two pounds of sugar
2. Away from heat, mix in one pound of walnuts and half a pound of almonds, both cleaned and ground
3. Put on fire, and before it reaches point add eight egg yolks, which are well incorporated, and allow it to reach the point of cajeta afterwards
4. Empty into the platter and brown and decorate with cinnamon, the same as in the previous articles
