## Ingredients

- half a veal hand
- piece of beef
- poultry
- pot broth
- garlic
- clove spice

## Directions

1. Place veal, beef, and poultry to cook with pot broth
2. Take care to stir and skim
3. Add more broth, garlic and clove spice
4. Cook on low heat for five hours
5. Strain through a sieve
