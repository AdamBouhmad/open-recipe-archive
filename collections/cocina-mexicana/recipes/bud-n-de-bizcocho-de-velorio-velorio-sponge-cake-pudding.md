## Ingredients

- half quart of milk
- three toasted bread slices
- three velorio sponge cakes
- three mamones
- six to eight eggs
- half lemon grated
- half a nutmeg
- three spoonfuls of Catalan

## Directions

1. To half a quart of milk add three toasted bread slices, three velorio sponge cakes and three mamones
2. Sweeten the milk to taste
3. Crush the sponge cake and mamones and add them to the milk with six to eight eggs, half lemon grated, half a nutmeg and three spoonfuls of Catalan
4. Grease the pudding mold with butter and pour the paste into it, crumbling the three toasted slices on top
5. Place over the fire
