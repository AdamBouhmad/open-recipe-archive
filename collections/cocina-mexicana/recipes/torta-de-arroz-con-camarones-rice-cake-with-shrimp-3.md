## Ingredients

- cleaned and cooked shrimp
- water or additional water if necessary
- rice
- salt
- beaten egg
- fried and seasoned mixture
- olives
- pieces of chiles in vinegar
- capers
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the shrimp and set aside, and in the water in which they were cooked, or with some more if necessary, cook the rice with salt
2. Once cooked and cooled, incorporate the beaten egg, the shrimp and a little fried and seasoned mixture like the previous one, adding olives, pieces of chiles in vinegar, and capers
3. Pour everything into a casserole lined with lard and finish as with the others
4. It can also be made with shrimp powder mixed into the rice after it is cooked in water
