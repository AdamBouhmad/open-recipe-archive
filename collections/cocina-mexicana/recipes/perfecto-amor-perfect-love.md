## Ingredients

- 6 quartillos brandy
- 1 ounce grapefruit peel
- 2 ounces lemon peel
- 0.5 drachm clove
- 3 quartillos distilled water
- 2.5 pounds crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Put the first ingredients to distill to obtain 4 quartillos of liqueur.
2. Dissolve the sugar in water, putting it on the fire.
3. When cold, mix together.
4. Unite with cochineal and filter.
