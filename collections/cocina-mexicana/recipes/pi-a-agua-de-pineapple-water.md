## Ingredients

- pineapple
- water
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind the pineapple and sift it through a sieve
2. Mix it with water and sweeten it
3. Pass it through a silk sieve
4. To serve, sprinkle the surface of the water with ground cinnamon
