## Ingredients

- ten ounces of butter
- twelve ounces of flour
- approximately half a small measure of refined Spanish brandy
- ten ounces of sifted sugar
- thirteen beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix and incorporate well ten ounces of butter with twelve ounces of flour and approximately half a small measure of refined Spanish brandy
2. Add ten ounces of sifted sugar
3. When well mixed with the flour and butter, gradually add and incorporate thirteen eggs, beaten as for common puffs, with the whites and yolks beaten separately and then combined
4. When the eggs are finished being stirred together, beat all the batter for about one hour without interruption
5. Then pour into buttered molds or small dishes without filling them
6. Put them to bake in an oven; the temperature of this should be the same as for common puffs and cover the mouth of the oven, taking care that the cake does not burn
7. The cake is known to be cooked if a straw is inserted into it and comes out clean
