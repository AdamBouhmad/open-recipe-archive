## Ingredients

- pork loin, sliced thin
- vinegar
- salt
- lard
- garlic, ground
- warm broth

## Directions

1. Slice the loin thin and flatten it like for beefsteak
2. Add a little vinegar and salt, and leave it thus
3. Half an hour before eating, place it to roast on the grill, brushing it with a little lard over very low heat
4. Turn it so it browns on both sides, and the little melted lard that drips is poured over it after putting it on the platter
5. When putting it to roast on the grill, first brush it with a little garlic ground with warm broth
