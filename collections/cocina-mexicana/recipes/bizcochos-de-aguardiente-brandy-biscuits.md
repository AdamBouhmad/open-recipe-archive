## Ingredients

- thirty eggs
- one pozuela of sugar
- one pozuela of butter
- one cup of refined brandy or distilled spirits
- a pinch of salt the size of three fingers
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix the thirty eggs with one pozuela of sugar, one pozuela of butter, one cup of refined brandy or distilled spirits, and a pinch of salt
2. Knead well with flour
3. Form the biscuits
4. Bake in the oven
