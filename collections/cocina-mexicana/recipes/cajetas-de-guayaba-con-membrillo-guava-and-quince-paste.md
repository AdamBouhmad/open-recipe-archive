## Ingredients

- guayaba molida en crudo (raw ground guava)
- membrillo cocido y cernido (cooked strained quince)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. To one pound of raw ground guava, add half a pound of cooked and strained quince and two pounds of sugar
2. Give it the proper point to be stored in glasses
