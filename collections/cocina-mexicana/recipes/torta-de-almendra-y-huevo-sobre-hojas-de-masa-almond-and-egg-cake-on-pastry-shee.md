## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of almonds
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Grind twelve ounces of almonds with half a pound of butter
2. When removed from heat, stud with toasted almonds and prick all over with the point of a knife
3. Fill these cuts with very thick syrup and dust with ground sugar and cinnamon
