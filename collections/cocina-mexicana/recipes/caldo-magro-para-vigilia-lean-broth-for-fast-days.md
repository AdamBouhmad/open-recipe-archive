## Ingredients

- six carrots, cut into wheels
- six turnips, cut into wheels
- six onions, chopped
- one large cabbage
- one celery root
- one glass of water
- four ounces of fresh butter
- one small bunch of parsley
- peas or green beans, optional
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put all vegetables in a pot with the water, butter, and parsley
2. Let it boil until all the water has evaporated
3. If using peas or green beans, add them with enough water to obtain sufficient broth
4. Let it cook for three hours
5. Season to taste and pass through a sieve
6. This broth can be used to make all the same dishes for fast days as one would make with meat broth
