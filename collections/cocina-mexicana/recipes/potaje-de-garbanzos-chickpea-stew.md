## Ingredients

- Chickpeas, well cooked with onion
- Oil
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water
- A little clove
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After the chickpeas are well cooked with onion, cook them in oil in which garlic and pepper have been fried
2. Add beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water
3. Season with a little clove and pepper
