## Ingredients

- half a pound sweet almonds
- half an ounce bitter almonds
- egg whites
- half a pound flour
- one pound powdered sugar
- a little butter
- salt
- grated lemon or citron peel
- eggs
- flour powder
- butter for pans

## Directions

1. Pound or grind together half a pound sweet almonds and half an ounce bitter almonds
2. Put in a bowl with egg whites and mix
3. Add half a pound flour and one pound powdered sugar, a little butter, salt, and grated lemon or citron peel
4. Add eggs in quantity needed so the dough is consistent
5. Knead until it hardens
6. Roll on flour powder and cut into pieces the size of a hazelnut
7. Round them by rolling and place on buttered baking pans
8. Brown and bake in a not-very-hot oven
