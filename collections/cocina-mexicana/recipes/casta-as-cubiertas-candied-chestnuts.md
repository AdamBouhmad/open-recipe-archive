## Ingredients

- Chestnuts with outer and inner shells removed and cooked
- Syrup at higher point

## Directions

1. After removing the outer and inner shells and cooking the chestnuts, make preserves as explained in its place
2. Cover them afterwards with syrup at a higher point, proceeding in the rest as for the other candied sweets
