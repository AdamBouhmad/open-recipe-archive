## Ingredients

- Syrup
- Egg yolks
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Sweetened milk
- Custard (natillas)
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare syrup as in the previous recipe, warming it without letting it cool completely
2. Add egg yolks as described in the other recipe
3. Place over heat, stirring constantly until it pulls away from the pan
4. Layer over sponge cake sprinkled with sweetened milk
5. Alternate with layers of custard beaten with sugar and cinnamon
6. Top with egg layer so it browns under two heats
7. Let cool before serving
