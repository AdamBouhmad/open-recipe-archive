## Ingredients

- beans
- tequesquite
- almonds
- lard
- hot water
- salt
- longaniza
- poultry

## Directions

1. Cook with plenty of tequesquite, wash and toast in lard
2. Grind with plenty of toasted almonds, add this with hot water, lard, salt, longaniza and poultry
