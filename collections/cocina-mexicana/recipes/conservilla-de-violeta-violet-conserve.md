## Ingredients

- violets, very clean
- syrup at blown point stage
- a little lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pound the violets very clean in a mortar and strain their juice through a cloth, pressing well
2. Pour into syrup at blown point stage (see page 25) after removing it from heat
3. Mix everything perfectly
4. Add a little lemon juice
5. Pour into molds and cut into desired shapes
