## Ingredients

- 12 ounces of sugar
- half a handful of bruised anise
- 4 quartillos of warm water

## Directions

1. Place in infusion with twelve ounces of sugar half a handful of bruised anise, in four quartillos of warm water
2. Do not let it take on too much flavor, because the ice cream will be acrid or pungent, and when it is known to be of good taste, pass it through a sieve, let it set in the sorbetera, turning it as for the other ice creams
