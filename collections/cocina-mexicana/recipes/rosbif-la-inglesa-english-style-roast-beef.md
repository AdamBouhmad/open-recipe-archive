## Ingredients

- Beef or lamb or mutton
- Butter

## Directions

1. Debone the extremity of the bone to four or six ribs
2. Place the piece on the spit
3. Turn it for three hours over good heat with equal care
4. Ensure the meat does not dry out
5. Serve with whole potatoes cooked in water or gravy
