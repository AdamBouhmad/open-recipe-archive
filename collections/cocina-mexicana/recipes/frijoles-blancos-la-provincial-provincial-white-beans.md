## Ingredients

- White beans
- broth
- 4 or 5 tablespoons olive oil
- butter
- 2 onions
- parsley
- goose leg or salted pork
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the beans that have been soaked, and place them in an earthenware pot with broth, 4 or 5 tablespoons of olive oil, a piece of butter, 2 sliced onions, chopped parsley, a goose leg or salted pork, pepper, salt, and nutmeg.
2. Cook for about 4 hours, so that when cooked through they are bound together thickly.
