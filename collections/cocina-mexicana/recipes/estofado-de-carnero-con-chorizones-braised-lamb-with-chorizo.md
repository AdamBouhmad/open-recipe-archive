## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp
- Slices of chorizo
- Lard
- Salt
- Roasted tomatoes
- Garlic
- Parsley
- White wine
- Vinegar
- A little sugar
- Water
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon, ground
- Flour fried and browned in lard

## Directions

1. Wash and cut the pulp as in the previous recipe
2. Fry in a pot with chorizo slices, lard, and salt
3. Add roasted tomatoes ground with garlic and parsley
4. Pour in white wine, a little vinegar, a little sugar, and water with ground cloves, black pepper, and cinnamon
5. Cover the pot
6. When the meat is cooked, thicken the broth if necessary with flour fried and browned in lard
