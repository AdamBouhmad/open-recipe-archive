## Ingredients

- whole peaches
- water
- sugar
- refined brandy

## Directions

1. Blanch whole peaches in boiling water and peel them
2. preserve them (see conserva de duraznos)
3. add an amount of syrup of refined brandy, ensuring both things mix well without boiling
4. use half a pound of sugar for each pound of fruit
