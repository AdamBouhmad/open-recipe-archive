## Ingredients

- Lentils
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- oil
- huauchinangos fish
- vinegar
- sugar
- celery
- wide chile
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils.
2. Once cooked, chop plenty of tomato, onion, and garlic.
3. Fry this mixture in butter and oil with some sliced huauchinangos fish until it browns well.
4. Add a tablespoon of vinegar and a little sugar.
5. Add some chopped celery to fry with the mixture.
6. Once the vinegar has reduced, add a wide chile soaked and ground with some peppercorns.
7. Let fry and then turn the lentils into it and let them season.
