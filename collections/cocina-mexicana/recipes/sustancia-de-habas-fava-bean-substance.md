## Ingredients

- Fava beans
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Broth or water
- Parsley
- Small onions

## Directions

1. Drain the fava beans and place them in cold water so they remain green
2. Drain again and place a piece of butter in a saucepan with salt, pepper, and a tablespoon of flour
3. Add the fava beans and moisten with broth or water
4. Add a small bunch of parsley and small onions and let the fava beans finish cooking
5. Pass the substance through a sieve and add a piece of butter before serving
