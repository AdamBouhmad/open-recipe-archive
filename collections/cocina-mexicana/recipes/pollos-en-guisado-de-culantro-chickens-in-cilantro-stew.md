## Ingredients

- two parts dried cilantro
- one part sesame
- lard
- green chile
- tomato
- ripe tomato
- garlic
- broth from the chickens
- salt to taste
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- toasted bread and lard
- oil
- vinegar
- capers
- caper berries
- slices of ham
- boiled onions

## Directions

1. Toast two parts dried cilantro and one part sesame, grind everything and strain.
2. Fry the ground mixture well in lard until the lard comes to the surface and set aside.
3. Separately fry finely chopped green chile, tomato, ripe tomato, and garlic, and when well fried, combine with the first mixture.
4. Add the chicken broth, sufficient salt, clove, cinnamon, pepper, and toasted bread fried in lard, all ground.
5. Place the chickens in the sauce and after one boil, add oil, vinegar, capers, caper berries, slices of ham, and separately boiled onions.
6. When the broth thickens, remove from heat and let rest.
