## Ingredients

- One quarter part bitter almonds
- Three parts sweet almonds, blanched
- Equal weight sugar
- Egg white, beaten

## Directions

1. Roast the bitter almonds in the stove and blanch all almonds
2. Reduce to paste by pounding and moistening gradually with beaten egg white
3. Take equal weight of sugar, clarify it, and bring to the boil
4. Add the almond paste to the clarified sugar and cook, stirring continuously until it adheres to the finger
5. Spread on a table dusted with powdered sugar until very thin
6. Cut into small pieces of desired shape
7. Place over heat to finish cooking and coat them
