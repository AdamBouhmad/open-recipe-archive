## Ingredients

- cacahuatzentli corn
- cooked egg yolks
- ground saffron
- melted lard
- sugar
- cinnamon powder
- sweet paste
- camote and pineapple paste
- coconut paste
- almond paste
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- citron

## Directions

1. Make nixtamal with cacahuatzentli corn, wash and trim it
2. Allow to dry, grind, sift, and add some cooked egg yolks, a bit of ground saffron, melted lard, sugar, and powdered cinnamon
3. Knead so that it is not too wet, and make tortillas on the metate
4. After toasting in lard they are called totoposcles (see tortillitas de metate, page 899)
5. Using them untoasted, line a lard-greased baking dish or pot
6. Place inside a sweet paste as in the previous article, or of camote and pineapple, or coconut, or almond, etc. (see SWEET PASTE, page 586), with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and citron
7. Cover with the same tortillas or masa memelas, making decorations or designs on top with the same dough
8. Allow to cook over two fires and sprinkle with ground sugar afterwards
