## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of syrup at point
- two cuartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Add two cuartillos of milk to two cuartillos of syrup at point
2. Bring to boil and test in water until it sets
3. Lower and beat well until half cooled
4. Turn onto a damp board and cut hot into desired sizes
