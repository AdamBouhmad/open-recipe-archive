## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 coconut, well ground
- 2 quarts milk
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a very clarified syrup with 1.5 pounds of sugar
2. Before it reaches the preserve stage, add the well-ground coconut to it
3. Allow it to reach the preserve consistency
4. Pour into a platter to cool
5. Mix in 2 quarts of milk and 12 egg yolks
6. Return to the fire to reach the same consistency as before
7. Pour again into a platter and keep for the next day
8. Place the platter on hot coals and cover with a comal with fire on top so that it browns
