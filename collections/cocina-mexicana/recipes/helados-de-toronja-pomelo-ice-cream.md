## Ingredients

- 3 pomelos
- 1.5 quartillos of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 12 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Place the scrapings of three pomelos on the fire with a quartillo and a half of milk, eight ounces of cream and twelve ounces of sugar, stirring everything until it begins to boil
2. Pass it then through a clean sieve, and letting it cool, place it to freeze
