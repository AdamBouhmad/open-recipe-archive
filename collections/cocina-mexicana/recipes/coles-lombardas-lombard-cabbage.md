## Ingredients

- cabbage
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After the clean cabbage has given a strong boil, remove from the fire, drain and chop into large pieces
2. Then put to cook with a little butter, adding salt and pepper
3. Stir frequently so that the butter incorporates
4. When well cooked, serve
