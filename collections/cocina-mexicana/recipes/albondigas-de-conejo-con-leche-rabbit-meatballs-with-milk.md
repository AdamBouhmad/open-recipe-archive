## Ingredients

- cooked rabbit
- rabbit bones
- beef scraps
- lean ham
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- lard
- milk

## Directions

1. Debone the cooked rabbit and remove membranes
2. Chop the meat very finely
3. In butter, reheat the mashed bones and scraps of beef with lean ham, salt, pepper, and a spoonful of flour sprinkled to prevent lumps, stirring well
4. Add sufficient milk to form a watery broth
5. Let boil for one hour with all these ingredients, stirring constantly
6. After this time, pass all through a sieve and reduce to sauce consistency
7. Mix with the chopped meat, keeping it hot without boiling
