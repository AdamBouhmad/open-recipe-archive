## Ingredients

- Mutton leg meat
- Fat bacon
- Parsley
- Green garlic
- Breadcrumbs
- Cheese
- Spices
- Salt
- Saffron
- Raw eggs
- Hazelnut
- Sour juice

## Directions

1. Chop the leg meat with fat bacon, parsley and green garlic if available, breadcrumbs and cheese.
2. Add spices, salt, saffron and raw eggs, mixing everything well.
3. Shape the meatballs in a small bowl or between two spoons.
4. Cook in broth.
5. When cooked, make a hazelnut sauce, thinning it with a little sour juice, and let it boil.
