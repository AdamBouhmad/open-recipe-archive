## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- pig liver
- ham
- capers
- anchovies
- fine herbs
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumb
- lard or milk
- sauce à la poulette

## Directions

1. Prepare as roasted suckling pig
2. Stuff the belly with the pig's own liver, chopped and cleaned of bile, ham, capers, anchovies, fine herbs, seasoned with pepper and salt, breadcrumbs soaked in lard or milk over the fire, an equal amount of lard, breadcrumbs pounded and dissolved in three egg yolks
3. Tie the belly closed
4. Place on the spit and roast as with the previous recipe
5. Serve accompanied with sauce à la poulette
