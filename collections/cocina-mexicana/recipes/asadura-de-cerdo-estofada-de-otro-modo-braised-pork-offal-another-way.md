## Ingredients

- pork offal
- xitomatl
- garlic
- parsley
- lard
- broth or water
- salt
- mixed spices
- four or six tornachiles
- creole vinegar
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and chop the offal into regular pieces
2. Chop xitomatl, garlic and parsley
3. Fry all this well
4. Add the raw offal and mix with the frying
5. Add broth or water and necessary salt
6. Grind all spices and add to the frying with lard
7. Cut four or six tornachiles into quarters and add with a little creole vinegar
8. Cover the casserole and let it boil until well cooked
9. When nearly done, dust with pepper and add lemon juice
10. Serve with little broth
