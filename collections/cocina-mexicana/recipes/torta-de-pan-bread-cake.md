## Ingredients

- bread
- lard
- picadillo
- chorizo
- hard-boiled eggs
- raisins
- almonds
- vinegar
- egg whites
- egg yolks
- comal
- lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into somewhat thick slices according to the size of cake desired.
2. Fry in lard on one side only and place in a baking dish, with the toasted side facing down.
3. On top of this layer of bread slices, put a layer of picadillo with sliced and fried chorizo on top, slices of hard-boiled egg, and some raisins and clean almonds.
4. Sprinkle with a little vinegar.
5. Alternate another layer of toasted bread and another of picadillo with its additions, continuing this way until ending with a layer of bread with the toasted side facing up.
6. Place the pan at the opening of a pot that is boiling and keep it there until midday.
7. Beat egg whites and when they have risen well, add the yolks.
8. Pour the beaten egg over the cake and place a comal with heat on top.
9. As soon as the egg has dried a little, brush the cake with lard and place the comal on again for a while.
10. Remove it and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
11. Put the comal back on top until the cake sets and forms a crust on top.
