## Ingredients

- Twelve egg whites
- Twelve egg yolks
- Four toasted and ground biscuits
- Half pound of sugar
- Washed butter
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Hazelnuts
- Cinnamon sticks
- Clarified honey

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and egg yolks separately as for marquesote
2. Combine them and beat again
3. Mix in the four toasted and ground biscuits with half pound of sugar
4. Grease a casserole well with washed butter, reserving a small piece to mix with the egg
5. Add toasted sesame seeds, raisins, almonds, hazelnuts, and cinnamon sticks
6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the cake
7. Place to cook between two gentle fires
8. Once cooked, break into small pieces
9. Pour clarified honey over it with raisins, almonds, walnuts, hazelnuts, cinnamon, and sesame seeds
