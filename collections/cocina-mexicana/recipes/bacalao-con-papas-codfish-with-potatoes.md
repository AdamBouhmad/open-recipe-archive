## Ingredients

- Desalted and cooked codfish
- Water
- Salt
- Potatoes
- Mayordomo sauce

## Directions

1. Desalt and cook the codfish in water, drain and dry.
2. Place on a plate garnished with whole potatoes cooked in salted water.
3. Cover everything on top with mayordomo sauce, in which a few drops of lemon will be squeezed.
