## Ingredients

- one arroba of flour
- a good piece of yeast dissolved in lukewarm water
- fifteen eggs
- three pounds of lard
- three pounds of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Knead well one arroba of flour and ferment with a good piece of yeast dissolved in lukewarm water, fifteen eggs, three pounds of lard, and three pounds of ground sugar, mixing everything together
2. Once the dough is fermented, make sticks from it, marking them with a knife according to the desired size
3. Place them on baking sheets and put in the oven to bake
4. When done, remove and break along the marks made, then put them back in a low-heat oven until they are well toasted
