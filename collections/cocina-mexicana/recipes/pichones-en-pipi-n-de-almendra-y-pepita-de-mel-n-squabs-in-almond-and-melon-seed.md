## Ingredients

- Almonds
- Bread
- Lard (manteca)
- Wide chiles (chiles anchos)
- Melon seeds (pepitas de melón)
- Water or broth
- Salt
- Squabs

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds and toast them
2. Brown a piece of bread in lard
3. Fry wide chiles after removing veins until toasted without burning and grind well
4. Grind melon seeds separately, strain well
5. Fry the chile in lard until well fried
6. Add the almonds and melon seed broth to fry as well
7. Add water or broth and squabs that have been cooked in salted water
8. Let boil until the pipián has thickened
