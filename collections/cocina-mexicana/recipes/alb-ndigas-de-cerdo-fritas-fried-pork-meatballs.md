## Ingredients

- pork meat
- onion, minced
- garlic, minced
- tomato, minced
- parsley, minced
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- beaten egg
- lard
- capers
- raisins
- fried and ground bread
- wine

## Directions

1. Mince and grind the pork meat as explained in previous articles
2. Mix with minced onion, garlic, tomato and parsley
3. Season with salt, add clove, pepper, cinnamon and beaten egg
4. Mix well and form meatballs
5. Fry in lard
6. Remove the meatballs and leave sufficient lard in the pan for the broth
7. Make the broth by frying minced onion in the lard, then add a little water, wine, clove, pepper, capers, raisins and a little fried and ground bread
8. Season with sufficient salt
9. Once boiling, add the meatballs and cook over two fires
