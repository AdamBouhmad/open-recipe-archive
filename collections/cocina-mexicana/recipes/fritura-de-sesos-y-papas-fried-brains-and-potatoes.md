## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- water
- eggs
- brains
- pepper
- butter
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook very well-peeled potatoes in very little water with salt, covered pot.
2. Once cooked, drain the water well and beat in a pan with a molinillo (hand mill).
3. Add beaten egg in proportion and beat everything together to mix well.
4. Have some brains already cooked, very well skinned and chopped with a knife; season with salt and pepper.
5. Place a skillet with plenty of butter, and once it is very hot let it cool and place it again over a very low heat.
6. With a spoon place the potato, make a hole in the middle and insert a ball of brains, cover and fry.
7. Make others the same way except that the brains are cooked in a stew of tomato, garlic, onion and parsley.
8. Others are made with the brains mixed with the potato and no egg is added but balls are made and they are dipped in egg like stuffed chiles.
