## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of ham
- Loin of veal
- Fat livers
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Artichoke hearts
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare equal quantities of ham, veal loin, fat livers, truffles, mushrooms, and artichoke hearts
2. Cook the meats and vegetables separately
3. Chop them in the form of small dice
4. To serve, add everything to Spanish sauce reduced to half
