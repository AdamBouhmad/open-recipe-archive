## Ingredients

- Four pounds of flour
- One and a half cups of melted butter
- Half cup of white wine
- A little syrup
- A little oil
- Four eggs
- A little yeast dissolved in water cooler than hot

## Directions

1. Knead four pounds of flour with one and a half cups of melted butter, half cup of white wine, a little syrup, a little oil, four eggs and a little yeast dissolved in water cooler than hot
2. When making the hojuelas, dust them with flour so they do not stick
3. Fry them in butter
4. With this dough, cones are made that are filled with any sweetened paste, and with the same, small empanadas of manjar blanco are also formed, which are served with ground sugar on top
