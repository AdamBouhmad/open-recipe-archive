## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Twenty egg yolks, well beaten
- Lard
- Pastry

## Directions

1. Incorporate the well-beaten twenty egg yolks thoroughly.
2. Place it in a pot greased with lard.
3. Bake in the oven, placing pastry on top as it comes out.
