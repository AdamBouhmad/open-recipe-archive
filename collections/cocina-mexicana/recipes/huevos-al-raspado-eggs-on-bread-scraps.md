## Ingredients

- bread scraps
- butter
- anchovy
- parsley
- onion
- tender garlic
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- bread crusts, fried

## Directions

1. In a casserole place bread scraps with butter, an anchovy, parsley, onion, a tender garlic, all well chopped and mixed with three egg yolks
2. Place over a slow fire
3. Break the eggs on top
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, seasoning with salt and pepper
5. Surround with fried bread crusts
