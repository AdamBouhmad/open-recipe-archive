## Ingredients

- salt
- egg yolk
- four or five ounces of oil
- vinegar
- nutmeg or pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Add salt to an egg yolk and whisk with a wooden spoon
2. While whisking, slowly pour in four or five ounces of oil
3. Sprinkle this sauce over fish or vegetables, adding another egg yolk, vinegar, and nutmeg or pepper to taste
4. This sauce is not heated over fire; it is enough that the dish in which it is made is a little warm
