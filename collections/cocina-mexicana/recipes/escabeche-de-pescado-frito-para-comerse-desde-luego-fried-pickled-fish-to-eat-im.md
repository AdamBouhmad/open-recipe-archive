## Ingredients

- Fish, washed and cut into portions
- Egg, beaten
- Lard and oil in equal parts
- Garlic cloves, cleaned and crushed
- Fine pepper
- Coarse pepper
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon, a little
- Pine nuts, cleaned
- Vinegar
- Whole pepper
- Bay leaves
- Chinese oregano
- Lemon slices, fried
- Fried garlic cloves
- Garlic cloves fried in oil
- Chile peppers in vinegar
- Olives
- Raw oil

## Directions

1. Wash and cut the fish into portions, bathe with beaten egg, and fry in equal parts lard and oil.
2. Remove the fish and fry cleaned, crushed garlic cloves in the same lard, then set aside.
3. Grind fine pepper, coarse pepper, clove, nutmeg, a little cinnamon, and cleaned pine nuts well.
4. Dissolve this mixture in good vinegar so it is neither too thick nor too thin.
5. Add the fish, incorporating whole peppers, bay leaves, Chinese oregano leaves, and fried lemon slices, adding the fried garlic.
6. Can be served immediately or after two days, garnishing with garlic cloves fried in oil, chiles in vinegar, olives, and raw oil on top, or without it.
