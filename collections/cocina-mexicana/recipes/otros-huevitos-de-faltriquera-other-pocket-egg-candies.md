## Ingredients

- two pounds sugar
- twenty-six beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup brought to turrón stage with two pounds of sugar
2. Mix with twenty-six previously beaten egg yolks
3. Return to heat and beat well so they cook
4. Beat until balls can be formed that do not stick to the papers in which they are to be wrapped
5. Wrap in paper
