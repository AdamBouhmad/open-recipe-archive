## Ingredients

- Onion
- Abundant lard
- Offal
- Salt
- Hot water
- Cloves of roasted garlic
- Pepper
- Clove
- Ginger
- Saffron
- Bread crumb
- Vinegar
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Chop onion and fry it in abundant lard.
2. When it is browned, add the chopped offal and stir.
3. Add salt and hot water.
4. Once it boils, grind the roasted garlic cloves, pepper, clove, ginger, saffron, and bread crumb.
5. Season with a little vinegar and chopped parsley.
