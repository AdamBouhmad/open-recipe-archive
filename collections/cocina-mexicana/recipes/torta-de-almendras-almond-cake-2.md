## Ingredients

- a pound of almonds
- egg
- a handful of bitter almonds
- a pound of sugar
- twenty-four egg yolks
- zest of a real lemon (lima real)
- butter
- caramel syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Grind the almonds with egg so they do not become oily
2. Separately, grind the bitter almonds and sugar
3. Add the ground mixture to the almonds in three parts to mix well
4. Add twenty-four egg yolks
5. Mix well and beat until well incorporated
6. Add a small cup of grated real lemon zest
7. Butter the mold and place the batter inside
8. Line with buttered paper
9. Bake in the oven
10. When cool, make caramel syrup and brush over the cake with a feather so it does not burn
