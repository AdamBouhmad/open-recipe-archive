## Ingredients

- hard-boiled egg yolks
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Mash hard-boiled yolks with vinegar, salt, pepper and oil which will be added drop by drop until a thick sauce is formed.
2. Cover the egg whites split into quarters with this sauce.
