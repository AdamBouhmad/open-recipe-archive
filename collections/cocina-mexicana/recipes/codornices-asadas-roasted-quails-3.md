## Ingredients

- quails
- butter
- salt
- breadcrumbs (a little)
- pepper (a little)
- egg yolks (one for every two quails)
- feathers (for basting)

## Directions

1. Give them a turn in the roaster first.
2. Have a pan ready on the fire with butter, salt, a little breadcrumbs, a little pepper and for every two quails one egg yolk.
3. Coat them with this mixture using feathers and turn them until they are golden.
