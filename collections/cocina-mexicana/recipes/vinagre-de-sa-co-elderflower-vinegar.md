## Ingredients

- Twelve cuartillos of good white Castilian vinegar
- One pound of elderflower (one third less than the amount of tarragon leaves used in the previous recipe)
- A small bundle of cloves
- Stripped peels of two lemons

## Directions

1. Prepare it the same way as the tarragon vinegar from the previous article, using one third less elderflower than the amount of tarragon leaves
