## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Broth without fat
- Beef tendons cooked over coals

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in water with salt, butter and a little broth without fat until almost done.
2. Crush with a wooden spoon and let cool.
3. Place half of this rice on a plate.
4. Place the tendons cooked over coals on top.
5. Cover with the other half of the rice.
6. Mold everything in the shape of a pastry.
7. Put in the oven to brown.
8. Lift the upper part of this pastry.
9. Add a stew or sauce inside.
10. Cover with its crust to serve.
