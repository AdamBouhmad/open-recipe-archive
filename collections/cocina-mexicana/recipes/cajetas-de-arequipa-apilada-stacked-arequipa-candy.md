## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- seven cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- cornstarch, sifted

## Directions

1. Put two pounds of sugar in seven cuartillos of milk with four ounces of peeled and ground almonds and sifted cornstarch
2. Place over heat and allow to reach the point of cajeta
3. Then add another pound of sugar and one pineapple, ground and strained through a napkin
4. Allow to boil until it reaches the point of cajeta
