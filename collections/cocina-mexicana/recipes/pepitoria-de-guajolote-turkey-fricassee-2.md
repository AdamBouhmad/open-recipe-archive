## Ingredients

- turkey
- diced bacon or butter
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- cloves
- toasted garlic
- hazelnuts
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After cleaning with a dry cloth, cut the turkey into pieces and sauté with diced bacon or butter, garlic, parsley, salt and a little pepper
2. Add some broth and make a sauce with cloves, toasted garlic, parsley, hazelnuts and an egg yolk, thinned with the same broth
3. Add this sauce to the pot and let cook a while
4. To keep the sauce from being too thick, add a couple of egg yolks
