## Ingredients

- chicken
- chicken broth
- fine spices except cilantro
- butter
- eyes (garlic or onion)
- bread
- oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken and cook it.
2. When almost cooked, remove and lard half of it.
3. Make a bread soup with broth from cooking the chicken, seasoned with fine spices except cilantro.
4. Pour this broth into a little butter in which some eyes have been fried, and with this and bread slices form the soup.
5. Set the chicken with the unlarded part on the soup.
6. Brush the larded part alternately with oil and lemon using a feather.
7. Cook over two gentle fires until the top part is roasted and the bottom part is cooked.
