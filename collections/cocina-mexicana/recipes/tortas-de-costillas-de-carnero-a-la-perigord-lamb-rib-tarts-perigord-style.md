## Ingredients

- One lamb rib rack, divided into small ribs
- Medium-sized truffles, cleaned
- Fine salt
- A little mixed spices
- Ham slices
- Butter, the thickness of a coin
- A good red substance
- One large glass of Champagne wine or good white wine

## Directions

1. Divide a lamb rib rack into small ribs, leaving each one only the bone that marks it
2. After lining the tart pan with dough as explained in the preceding articles, arrange the ribs on top, alternating them with an equal number of medium-sized cleaned truffles
3. Season everything on top with fine salt and a little mixed spices
4. Cover both with ham slices and spread over everything a layer of butter the thickness of a coin
5. Finish the tart like all the others and let it cook in the oven for at least three hours
6. When it is done, add a good red substance (see strained broth, page 132) to which a large glass of Champagne wine or good white wine will have been mixed
