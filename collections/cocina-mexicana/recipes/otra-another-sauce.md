## Ingredients

- oil
- large sections of onion
- olives
- pepper
- toasted bread
- parsley
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry large sections of onion in oil
2. Add olives with a pinch of pepper and toasted bread
3. Add parsley and vinegar
4. Grind everything together
