## Ingredients

- Small bobos fish
- Chile peppers seeds
- Red chiles with veins
- Garlic cloves, burned in oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Epazote
- Dried chile peppers
- Tamal leaves
- Onion
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. After gutting and washing the small bobos, toast a good portion of chile pepper seeds on the comal
2. Grind together with four or six red chiles with veins and plenty of garlic burned in oil
3. Dissolve everything in a little diluted vinegar and season with plenty of salt and pepper
4. Add a little oil and beat until somewhat thick
5. Dip the small bobos well in this sauce
6. Place in the center of each a sprig of epazote with a vein of dried chile
7. Gather three or four together and wrap in soaked tamal leaves
8. After covering well with the leaves, tie them and place at the embers or over gentle fire
9. When roasted, serve alone or with chopped onion and parsley, vinegar and oil
