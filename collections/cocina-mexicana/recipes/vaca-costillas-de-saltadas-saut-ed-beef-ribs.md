## Ingredients

- ribs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shallots
- melted butter
- Spanish sauce
- gelatin
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the ribs well, rounding the thick part of the meat and thinning the bone end
2. Place in a sauté pan with salt, pepper, minced parsley and shallots
3. Sprinkle everything with melted butter
4. Place the pan on very hot fire
5. When the ribs are cooked on one side, turn them over
6. Cook completely on both sides, which is known by pressing them slightly with your finger
7. Remove the butter used for cooking and replace with several spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and some gelatin
8. Heat this sauce and pour over the ribs
9. If there is no Spanish sauce, it will be necessary to keep the butter, add a little flour, and moisten as needed
