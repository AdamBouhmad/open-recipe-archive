## Ingredients

- oil
- butter
- garlic cloves
- onion
- chile strips
- mushrooms
- epazote
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Place a casserole on the fire with a little eating oil and butter; add garlic cloves to brown well, then add a handful of finely minced onion.
2. Once well browned, add plenty of chile strips; once everything is well fried, add the mushrooms to fry also, a sprig of epazote, a little water, sufficient salt, and more oil, and let season.
3. The mushrooms should be tested with garlic to see if they are good.
