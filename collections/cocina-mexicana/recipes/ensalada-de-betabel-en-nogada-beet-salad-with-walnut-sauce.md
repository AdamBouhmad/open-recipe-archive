## Ingredients

- almond
- large or small walnut, equal amounts
- roasted garlic cloves
- bread crumb
- cold water
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- cooked beet, very red and without fibers

## Directions

1. Grind equal amounts of almond and large or small walnut with some roasted garlic cloves and a bread crumb; stir everything well so it does not remain grainy, and dissolve it with a little cold water until it is like atole, adding considerable oil, pepper and the corresponding salt.
2. Also grind cooked beet, very red and without fibers; roast one or two in an oven or grill, make them into slices and mix them with the walnut sauce.
