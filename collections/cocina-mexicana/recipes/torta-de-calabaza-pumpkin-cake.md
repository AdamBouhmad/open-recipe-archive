## Ingredients

- cooked pumpkin
- panocha (unrefined sugar)
- cinnamon
- clove
- bread crumbs
- salt
- cheese
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Squeeze the cooked pumpkin a little and grind it with a piece of panocha, cinnamon and a little clove
2. Add some bread crumbs, a little salt and crumbled cheese
3. Mix everything with well-beaten and risen eggs
4. Pour into a casserole lined with butter
5. Let the cake cook and set over two fires
6. Serve dusted with powdered sugar
