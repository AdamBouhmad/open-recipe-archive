## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- its own broth
- cooked egg yolk
- crushed garlic
- pepper
- raw oil
- vinegar
- small cooked onions

## Directions

1. Place the cooked salt cod on a platter with a little of its own broth
2. Dissolve a cooked egg yolk and crushed garlic with a little pepper in the broth
3. Pour raw oil and vinegar over it
4. Add small onions that were cooked with the salt cod
