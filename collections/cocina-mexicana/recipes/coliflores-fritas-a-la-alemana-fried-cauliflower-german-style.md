## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- vinegar
- parsley
- frying batter
- butter

## Directions

1. Season the cauliflower first with salt, vinegar, and parsley
2. Cook it afterward as is customary
3. Cover immediately with frying batter
4. Fry in butter
