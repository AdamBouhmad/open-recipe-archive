## Ingredients

- One and one-half pounds of flour
- Three egg yolks
- One pound of lard
- A little salt
- Water
- Filling

## Directions

1. Mix flour with egg yolks, lard, salt, and enough water to make a soft dough
2. Let rest after kneading and then spread on a floured surface
3. Fold in four folds, also with flour
4. Roll out again, add filling, and brush the edges with melted lard using a brush
5. After covering the pastries, dust with ground sugar and bake in the oven
6. Scraps can be collected in a bowl and re-kneaded with water, salt, and lard
