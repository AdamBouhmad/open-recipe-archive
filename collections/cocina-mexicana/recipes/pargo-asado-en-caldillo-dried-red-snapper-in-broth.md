## Ingredients

- pargo asado (dried red snapper)
- sal (salt)
- cebollas (onions)
- xitomate cocido (cooked tomato)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajos (garlic)
- perejil (parsley)
- tornachiles en vinagre (chili peppers in vinegar)
- aceitunas (olives)
- alcaparras (capers)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Remove the stick that passes through the dried snapper and divide it into the largest pieces possible
2. Remove the bones and skin, then boil in hot water and wash well to remove any mold
3. Place in another very hot water and soak for twenty to thirty hours
4. Cook with salt and onions, then set aside
5. Chop cooked tomato without seeds and fry in lard
6. Add sliced onion and garlic, chopped parsley, chili peppers in vinegar cut in pieces, olives, capers, a little good vinegar, and sugar so it becomes slightly sweet
7. Add water from that in which the fish was cooked, and when the broth has thickened to the proper consistency and the fish is cooked, let it rest over very gentle heat
