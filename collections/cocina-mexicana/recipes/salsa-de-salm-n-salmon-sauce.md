## Ingredients

- pine nuts
- garlic clove
- pepper
- sage
- poultry offal
- oil
- sherry wine
- poultry broth or water

## Directions

1. Peel pine nuts, toast them and grind with a clove of garlic, pepper, royal sage and poultry offal
2. Dissolve the mixture with oil and add sherry wine
3. Add sufficient poultry broth or water
4. Use this sauce to cook poultry, after they have been cooked and fried in lard
