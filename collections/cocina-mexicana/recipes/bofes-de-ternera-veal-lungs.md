## Ingredients

- Veal lungs
- butter
- flour
- water or broth
- thyme
- garlic
- parsley
- eggs
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the veal lungs well with boiling water
2. Cut into small pieces
3. Put in butter, dust with flour
4. Stir well
5. Add water or broth
6. Season with thyme, garlic, and parsley
7. When cooked, add a beaten egg mixture and a few drops of vinegar
