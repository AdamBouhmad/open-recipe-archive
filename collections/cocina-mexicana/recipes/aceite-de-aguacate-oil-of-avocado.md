## Ingredients

- Seven ounces of avocado leaves
- Sixteen quartillos of aguardiente
- Well-clarified syrup
- Watercress tincture or spinach decoction

## Directions

1. Put in infusion for four to six days seven ounces of avocado leaves in sixteen quartillos of aguardiente
2. Distill until two-thirds of the liquor has been obtained
3. Sweeten with well-clarified syrup, quartillo for quartillo
4. Color it with watercress tincture or spinach decoction
5. Filter and bottle it
