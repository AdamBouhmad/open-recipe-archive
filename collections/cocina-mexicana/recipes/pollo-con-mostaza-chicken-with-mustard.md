## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Onion
- Garlic, minced
- Mustard
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread slices
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into quarters and add ham slices and chorizos with one tablespoon of lard
2. Fry well until browned
3. Add a little water
4. In another pan, fry some onion and minced garlic
5. Grind a little mustard, removing the sharp taste as for sauce
6. Grind well and add to the fried onion
7. Combine with the meat in the pot
8. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper and some fried bread slices
9. Add oregano and thyme
10. Before serving, add a little Sherry wine
11. Garnish the platter with chiles, olives and capers
