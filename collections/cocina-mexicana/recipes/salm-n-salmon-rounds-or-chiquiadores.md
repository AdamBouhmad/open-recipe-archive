## Ingredients

- one pound raw salmon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- half glass of wine
- German sauce

## Directions

1. Wash one pound of raw salmon, remove the skin, and cut into rounds the size of a coin or small disc.
2. Place these rounds in a pan with melted butter, season with salt and pepper, and add chopped parsley and half a glass of wine.
3. At the moment of serving, cook them over high heat.
4. Drain the butter, add one tablespoon of German sauce with a piece of fresh butter.
