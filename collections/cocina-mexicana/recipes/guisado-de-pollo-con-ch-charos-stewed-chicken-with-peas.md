## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Lard
- Onion, finely minced
- Thyme
- Flour
- Water
- Peas
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers
- Caperberries

## Directions

1. Put the chicken in a pot with a little lard, finely minced onion and a little thyme
2. Place the pot on the fire
3. When fried, add a pinch of flour and fry a bit more
4. Add water
5. Shell the peas and add them raw with cloves, cinnamon, pepper and a little Sherry wine
6. Garnish with chiles, olives, capers and caperberries
