## Ingredients

- cooked trout
- sauce

## Directions

1. When the trout is cooked, prepare its flesh the same way as turbot for croquettes (see croquetas de rombo o rodaballo, page 244), using a slightly more liquid sauce
2. Make pastry timbales and fill them with the ragout
