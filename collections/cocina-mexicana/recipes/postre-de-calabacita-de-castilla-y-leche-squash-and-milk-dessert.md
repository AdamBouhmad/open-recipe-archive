## Ingredients

- cooked and ground Castilian squash
- sugar
- milk
- butter
- ground biscuit
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of joining in water
2. Off heat, mix in cooked and ground Castilian squash in proportion of 20 ounces of squash for each pound of sugar
3. Put it on heat and let it reach the cajeta point
4. In this state, pour into cups and keep for when the dessert is to be made
5. Then dissolve this sweet proportioning 2 quartillos of milk for each cup of squash sweet and let it boil until it returns to its point
6. Pour into a dish greased with butter and with ground biscuit, sugar and cinnamon
7. Put more biscuit on top, bathe with melted butter and place over hot coals with heat from above to brown it
8. When cool, dust with ground cinnamon
