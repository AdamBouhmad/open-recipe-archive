## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound and two ounces of lard
- six ounces of ground sugar
- five egg yolks
- salt
- water
- four cuartillos of milk
- cinnamon stick
- two pounds of white sugar
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix with three pounds of flour one pound and two ounces of lard, six ounces of ground sugar, five egg yolks, salt and a small pot of water if needed
2. Knead with the fists and make a tortilla of regular thickness
3. Cut strips from it with the knife and form a lattice that serves to cover the pie
4. Cover the inside part with the same dough a baking dish and bake before putting the filling
5. Make the filling by boiling four cuartillos of milk with a stick of cinnamon until well cooked
6. With two pounds of white sugar make very clarified syrup, and when it boils put in it twelve egg yolks in a strainer
