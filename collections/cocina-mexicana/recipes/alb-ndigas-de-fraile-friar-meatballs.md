## Ingredients

- pork meat
- onions
- garlic
- parsley
- herbabuena
- marjoram
- thyme
- oregano
- ground bread
- egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Mince together pork meat, onions, garlic, parsley, herbabuena, marjoram, thyme, oregano, ground bread and egg
2. Add a little butter
3. Mix everything very well
4. Form meatballs larger than the usual size
5. Drop into boiling water
6. Add more of the same mixture to the broth as needed
