## Ingredients

- One young goose with white fat
- Salt

## Directions

1. Choose a young goose with white fat well developed. Pluck it and pass it through flames.
2. Remove the wings and nails, tie it leaving the legs, place it on the spit and roast it.
3. It is necessary to make the juice of the breasts come out by piercing it with a knife.
