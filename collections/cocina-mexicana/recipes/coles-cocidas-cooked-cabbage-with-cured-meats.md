## Ingredients

- pickled cabbage
- ham
- sausage
- chorizo
- broth
- pot fat

## Directions

1. Let the cabbage soak for two hours in cold water
2. After it has drained, put it in a pot with small slices of ham, sausage and chorizo
3. Moisten with broth and a little substance, or with pot fat
4. Let cook at low heat
5. Serve the cabbages, placing the ham, sausage and chorizos on top, removing the casing they were in
