## Ingredients

- almonds
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- lard
- wine
- vinegar
- birds

## Directions

1. Grind the almonds with clove, cinnamon, pepper and cumin, and place in a pot with some lard, wine and vinegar, adding the birds raw or cooked
2. Let everything boil until the perfect cooking of the meat
