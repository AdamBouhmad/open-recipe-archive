## Ingredients

- red carnations with calyces or green parts removed
- half drachma of clove for every two quartillos of aguardiente
- half drachma of cinnamon for every two quartillos of aguardiente
- three ounces of white sugar for every three quartillos of aguardiente

## Directions

1. Fill a crystal balloon or earthenware jug with red carnations, removing their calyces or all green parts
2. Add half drachma of clove and another half of cinnamon for every two quartillos of aguardiente and pour as much aguardiente as the vessel can contain
3. Leave in infusion for eight to fifteen days
4. Then strain with expression, adding three ounces of white sugar for every three quartillos of aguardiente
5. Filter and store
