## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Fine salt
- Powdered toasted cilantro

## Directions

1. Place whole eggs in water that is already boiling vigorously
2. Keep them on the fire for two or three minutes
3. Then remove the pot from the fire and keep them in the hot water another similar amount of time or a little more
4. Place them in egg cups and serve with a short spoon that fits in the egg without breaking it completely
5. Have ready fine salt and powdered toasted cilantro as condiments
