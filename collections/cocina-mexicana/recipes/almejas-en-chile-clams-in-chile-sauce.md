## Ingredients

- clams
- garlic
- salt
- lard
- onion
- wide chile peppers
- roasted garlic
- cumin
- mint leaves
- onion quarters

## Directions

1. After cleaned and prepared as described, cook the clams in water with garlic and salt.
2. Place a pot on the heat with lard, fry minced garlic and onion in it.
3. Toast wide deveined chiles, some clean roasted garlic, and a few cumin seeds.
4. Add everything to the pot where the garlic and onion were fried.
5. Once fried, add water, some mint leaves, onion quarters and the corresponding salt.
6. Add the clams there, and as soon as they are seasoned and the sauce has thickened a little, serve.
