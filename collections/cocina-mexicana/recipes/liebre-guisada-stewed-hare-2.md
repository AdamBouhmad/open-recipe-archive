## Ingredients

- hare
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaves
- nutmeg
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning, cut into pieces, which are placed in a casserole with oil, salt, pepper, garlic, some bay leaves, powdered nutmeg, and cook with heat below and above for a quarter hour.
2. Remove half the oil; add chopped parsley and remove the bay leaf and garlic.
3. Thicken the sauce with fried butter and flour, add lemon juice and serve.
