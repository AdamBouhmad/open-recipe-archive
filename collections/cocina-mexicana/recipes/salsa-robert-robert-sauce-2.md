## Ingredients

- eight to ten large onions
- four ounces of butter
- three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- two spoonfuls of broth
- one spoonful of mustard

## Directions

1. Cut eight to ten large onions into the form of thick cubes and place them in a pan over four ounces of butter at good heat so it does not weaken
2. When the onions have browned, add three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and two of broth
3. Allow to reduce and degrease the fat, and when it is somewhat thick, remove from heat
4. When about to use, add one spoonful of mustard and do not let it boil
