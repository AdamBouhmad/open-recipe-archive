## Ingredients

- lean meat
- fat bacon
- garlic
- parsley
- fried onion
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pine nuts
- spices
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make the filling from lean meat, fat bacon, garlic, parsley, fried onion, breadcrumbs, salt, pine nuts, spices and a little grated cheese
2. Remove the inner layers of the onions with a knife and fill them
3. Place in the pot without pressing them, cook and serve
