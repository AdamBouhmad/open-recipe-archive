## Ingredients

- eight pork feet, cooked
- cinnamon
- four cups milk
- twenty egg yolks
- lard

## Directions

1. Bone and shred the meat from eight well-cooked pork feet
2. Boil briefly with a small piece of cinnamon
3. Dry it in a napkin
4. Put it in four cups of milk with twenty well-beaten egg yolks, mixing well
5. Pour into a casserole greased with lard
6. Bake in the oven
7. Top with pastille when it comes out
