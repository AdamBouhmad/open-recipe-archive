## Ingredients

- four eggs
- half a small cup of starch
- two cloves
- a little cinnamon
- butter or lard
- clarified syrup
- orange flower water
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs and add half a small cup of starch, two cloves and a little cinnamon, all ground
2. Fry like a cake in butter or lard until cooked
3. Cut the fried bread into pieces
4. Place in syrup with orange flower water and sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top
