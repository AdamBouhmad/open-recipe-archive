## Ingredients

- solid beef
- ham
- garlic
- pepper
- cloves
- vinegar
- water
- Jerez wine
- oregano
- thyme
- almoraduz
- bay leaves
- lemon balm
- rosemary
- Tabasco pepper
- cumin
- cinnamon
- salt
- toasted ginger
- chile peppers
- tomato, chopped
- garlic heads
- onions
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- onion slices
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Take solid meat and lard with ham, garlic, pepper and cloves
2. Tie with twine
3. Place in a pot vinegar, water and Jerez wine
4. Mix oregano, thyme, almoraduz, bay leaves, lemon balm, rosemary, Tabasco pepper, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, salt, toasted ginger, chile peppers, chopped tomato, garlic heads and onions
5. Cover the pot with a plate and a cloth on top
6. Cook until it dries
7. Garnish with chiles, olives, capers, onion slices and lettuce leaves
