## Ingredients

- bagre, cleaned of scales and entrails
- oil
- onion heads
- lard
- vinegar
- salt
- bay leaf
- thyme
- garlic, crushed
- nutmeg
- cloves
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean of scales and entrails, slice and fry in oil until it takes color
2. Take some onion heads and fry in oil and lard in a pan
3. Once they take color add vinegar, salt, bay leaf, thyme, crushed garlic, nutmeg, cloves and pepper
4. Place the bagre in this sauce and let it cook
5. Before serving bathe it with oil
