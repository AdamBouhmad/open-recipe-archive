## Ingredients

- Two pounds clarified syrup
- One pound fresh ground walnut
- Orange blossom water
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add one pound of fresh ground walnut to two pounds of well-clarified syrup
2. Bring to the setting point in water
3. Add a little orange blossom water
4. Remove and beat
5. Place candies on paper with cinnamon on top if desired
