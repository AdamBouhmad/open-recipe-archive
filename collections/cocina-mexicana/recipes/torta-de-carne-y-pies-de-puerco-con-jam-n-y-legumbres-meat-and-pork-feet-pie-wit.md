## Ingredients

- pulp or loin of pork
- pork feet
- chorizos
- fat ham
- chicken livers
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- camote
- lettuce
- beets
- carrots
- hard boiled eggs
- beaten eggs
- ground biscuit
- butter
- olives
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop a pulp or loin of pork and cook it in a ball with pork feet, chorizos, fat ham, and chicken livers
2. When cooked, chop everything and season with clove, cinnamon, pepper and salt, stirring well
3. Mix with camote, lettuce, beets, carrots, hard boiled eggs all chopped, and enough beaten eggs as for frying, adding at the end a little ground biscuit
4. Butter a pie dish and pour all the chopped mixture there, cooking at two fires until the pie sets
5. Serve the pie adorned with olives and chiles in vinegar
