## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- onions
- garlic
- broth
- cooked egg yolks
- fried bread pieces
- cooked ham
- ham slices
- pork loin
- chorizos
- cooked egg whites
- parsley
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onions and three chopped garlic cloves in a pot with lard.
2. Place the chickens there with the broth in which they were cooked.
3. Grind cooked egg yolks with pieces of fried bread and a little cooked ham.
4. Dissolve the ground mixture in water and add it to the chickens with sliced ham and pork loin, chorizos, the cooked egg whites, finely chopped, a little chopped parsley, clove, cinnamon, pepper and a little saffron, all ground.
