## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon sticks
- sugar
- fresh butter
- three egg yolks per serving
- mamón (sponge cake)
- natillas (custard)
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cook milk with cinnamon sticks and sugar until the sugar rises to the top
2. Remove from heat and add a piece of fresh butter and three egg yolks for each serving, mixing well
3. In a pan or cake dish greased with butter, place a layer of sliced mamón that will be well soaked with the milk prepared as described, and another layer of natillas
4. Place to cook over two gentle fires, and on top cover the cake with beaten eggs, which will be browned with a griddle with flame
