## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- White salt
- Garlic heads, crushed
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, sliced
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Oregano
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Chopped onion

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with white salt and crushed garlic heads.
2. In a pan with oil, fry sliced garlic cloves until golden, then remove them.
3. Fry the cauliflower divided into its florets in that same oil.
4. When golden, dust with black pepper, salt, and oregano.
5. Garnish with small chiles, olives, and chopped onion.
