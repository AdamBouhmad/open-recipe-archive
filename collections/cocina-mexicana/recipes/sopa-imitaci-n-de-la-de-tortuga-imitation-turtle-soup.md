## Ingredients

- One veal head, well washed
- Onion, chopped lengthwise to the size of the meat from the head
- A small tied bundle of parsley, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram and a small stick of cinnamon
- Three crushed garlic cloves
- One whole onion studded with five cloves
- Thirteen to nine whole Tabasco peppers
- Fine ground pepper
- Some boiled and sliced eggs to the size of the meat
- Half a nutmeg
- Half a bottle of red wine
- Another half bottle of red wine
- Pieces of veal sweetbread, chopped

## Directions

1. Take a veal head, wash it very well until it is white, cook it in a pot in such a way that it does not fall apart.
2. Once cooked, remove it and chop it into small pieces the size of a hazelnut.
3. Chop onion lengthwise to the size of the meat from the head, cooking everything together in a covered pot, adding a small tied bundle composed of parsley, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram and a small stick of cinnamon, three crushed garlic cloves, one whole onion studded with five cloves, thirteen to nine whole Tabasco peppers, fine ground pepper, some boiled and sliced eggs to the size of the meat, and half a nutmeg.
4. Cook everything for a long time until serving time.
5. Add half a bottle of red wine at medium boiling and send another half bottle to the table when serving.
6. It will turn out better if you add some pieces of chopped veal sweetbread while it is on the fire.
