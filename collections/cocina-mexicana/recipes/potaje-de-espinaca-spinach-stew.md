## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Lard or oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut, peel and wash the spinach
2. Reheat with lard or oil, salt and pepper
3. Then add for the sauce oil or lard
4. Serve with fried bread crusts
