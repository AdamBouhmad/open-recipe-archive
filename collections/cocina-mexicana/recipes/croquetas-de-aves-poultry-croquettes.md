## Ingredients

- Poultry
- Bread crumbs
- Cream
- Egg yolk
- Egg
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the tendons from a prepared bird.
2. Mince finely with bread crumbs soaked in cream and egg yolk.
3. When well beaten and seasoned, form into small balls.
4. Wrap in bread crumbs.
5. Wet with beaten egg and bread again.
6. Fry.
7. Serve in pyramids with fried parsley underneath.
