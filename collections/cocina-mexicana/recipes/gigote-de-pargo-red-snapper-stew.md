## Ingredients

- pargo (red snapper)
- ajos (garlic)
- aceite o manteca (oil or lard)
- canela (cinnamon)
- clavo (cloves)
- alcaparras (capers)
- vino (wine)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- especias (spices)
- aceitunas (olives)
- tornachiles (chili peppers)
- cogollos de lechuga (lettuce hearts)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)
- pasas (raisins)
- almendras (almonds)

## Directions

1. Roast the red snapper over coals and place it in water to soak, then cook and chop it
2. Fry some garlic in oil or lard, preferably oil, and add the chopped red snapper
3. Add ground cinnamon, cloves, and capers, along with wine and vinegar
4. Once the broth has boiled and is seasoned, add spices, olives, chili peppers, lettuce hearts, egg yolks fried in lard, raisins, and almonds
