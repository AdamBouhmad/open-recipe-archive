## Ingredients

- hazelnuts
- olive oil
- parsley
- tarragon or taragontia
- small onions

## Directions

1. Make the same way as garlic butter, using hazelnuts instead of garlic
2. Add finely chopped parsley, tarragon or taragontia, and small onions
