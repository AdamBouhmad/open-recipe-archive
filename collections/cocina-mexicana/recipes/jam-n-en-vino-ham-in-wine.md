## Ingredients

- ham
- thyme
- laurel
- marjoram
- fresh rosemary
- mint
- parsley
- white wine
- Castilian vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the ham very well and round it
2. Cook it in water with a little thyme, laurel, marjoram, fresh rosemary, mint and parsley
3. Once cooked, remove it and put it back on the fire with white wine, Castilian vinegar and a piece of sugar
4. Once the broth has been consumed, let it cool
5. Scrape it on both sides
6. Place it on a comal by its lean side
7. Sprinkle sifted sugar on top
8. Place another comal with fire on top and maintain until it forms a crust
