## Ingredients

- Cold bread
- Six eggs
- Cheese, crumbled
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Place cold bread on a comal over fire to toast
2. Grind until very fine
3. Beat six eggs, first the whites and then the yolks as for frying
4. Mix the ground bread into the beaten eggs
5. Add crumbled cheese and cinnamon, keeping the mixture not too thick
6. Pour into a pan with lard
7. Cook over two fires
8. Slice the cake and place in syrup
