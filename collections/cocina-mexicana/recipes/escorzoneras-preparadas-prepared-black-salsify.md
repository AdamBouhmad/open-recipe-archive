## Ingredients

- Black salsify roots
- Water
- White vinegar
- Salt
- Butter
- White or red sauce

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots to remove the black skin without leaving any marks
2. As they are cleaned, place them in a bowl with water and white vinegar
3. Boil water in a pot with salt equal to the amount of an egg of butter and four tablespoons of white vinegar
4. When the water begins to boil, add the salsify roots and boil for one hour
5. After ensuring they are well cooked, drain them
6. Dress with white or red sauce and serve as a side dish
