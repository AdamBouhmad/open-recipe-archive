## Ingredients

- Tender fava beans with pods
- Salt
- Tequesquite
- Flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook tender fava beans with their pods in salted water with a little tequesquite
2. Once cooked, drain and dry with a cloth
3. Coat in flour until well covered
4. Fry butter in a pan and when very hot, place the fava beans separately to cover the bottom of the pan
5. Add a little more salt and turn them until well golden
6. As they are removed, place on a deep plate and cover with another so they do not cool and become tough
7. They are very appetizing
