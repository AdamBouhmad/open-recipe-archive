## Ingredients

- Rear part of a mutton
- Butter
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Break the bones of the legs and ribs
2. Strike the legs so they take a rounded form
3. Round the flanks, fixing them with a skewer
4. Arrange the rosbif on the spit, fixing it also with other skewers
5. Wrap in paper spread with butter
6. Let cook two hours and a half
7. A few moments before cooking is finished, remove the paper so it takes on a beautiful color
8. Serve with some clear substance
