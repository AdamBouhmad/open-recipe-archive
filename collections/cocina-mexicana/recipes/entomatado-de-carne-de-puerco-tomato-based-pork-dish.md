## Ingredients

- lard
- onions, chopped
- garlic, chopped
- tomatoes, cooked
- ground spices
- cooked onions
- vinegar
- pork meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onions and garlic in lard
2. In the same fat, crush the cooked tomatoes with a spoon so they do not break apart completely
3. Add ground spices, cooked onions set apart, a little vinegar, and the pork meat with the water in which it was cooked
4. Season with the corresponding salt
