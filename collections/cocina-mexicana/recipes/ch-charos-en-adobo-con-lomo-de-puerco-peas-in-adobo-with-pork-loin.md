## Ingredients

- wide dried chiles
- fried bread
- butter
- garlic
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- pork loin broth
- pork loin
- peas
- small sausages

## Directions

1. Devein and soak the wide chiles, then grind with bread fried in butter, garlic and cumin.
2. Fry everything, seasoning with salt.
3. Then add vinegar and the broth in which the pork loin was cooked.
4. Add the pork loin, peas and small sausages, all cooked beforehand.
5. Let simmer until the adobo reaches a regular consistency.
