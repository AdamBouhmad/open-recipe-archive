## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Water with tequesquite
- Crushed garlic
- Onion tails
- Water
- Salt
- Lard
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Garlic
- Cooked lamb liver
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pot broth

## Directions

1. Wash ducks with water of tequesquite and cook with crushed garlic, onion tails, water, and salt
2. Fry in lard and remove immediately
3. In the fat, fry chopped tomatoes, onion, and garlic
4. Season the mixture with salt and add cooked ground lamb liver, pepper, and ground cloves
5. Place the ducks in this sauce with plenty of oil, a little vinegar, and pot broth
6. Let simmer until the sauce reaches the proper consistency
