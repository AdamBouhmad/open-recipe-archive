## Ingredients

- Veal offal
- Veal feet
- Salt
- Chickpeas
- Tequesite
- Wide chile peppers, deveined
- Bread, fried in lard
- Garlic cloves
- Pepper
- Lard
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Grated cheese

## Directions

1. After washing the offal, cook it with salt along with the veal feet, very clean
2. When cooked, debone the feet and cut them with the offal into regular pieces
3. Cook the soaked chickpeas with a little tequesite
4. Toast the wide deveined chiles and grind them with bread fried in lard, garlic cloves, pepper and salt
5. Fry everything in a cazuela with lard
6. Add a little of the broth in which the offal cooked
7. Cut onions and tomatoes into quarters and add them with ham slices, chorizos and the cooked chickpeas
8. Put the offal and feet in the broth, add grated cheese, let it season
9. When it has boiled, add the necessary salt
