## Ingredients

- Two pounds of almonds with their skin, ground
- Water for rolling the almonds
- One pound of sugar
- One pound of good virgin honey
- One ounce of ground cinnamon
- A little nutmeg
- A little clove, also ground and sifted
- Flour for dusting
- Optional: a little fragrant water, candied lemon, or similar substance

## Directions

1. Grind two pounds of almonds with their skin, rolling them with water so they do not release their oil
2. Dry the almond paste in a pot over low heat so it has no remaining moisture
3. Take one pound of sugar and one pound of good virgin honey, and incorporate them together
4. Clarify and cook the syrup to the flower stage
5. Mix this syrup with the almond paste, adding one ounce of ground cinnamon, a little nutmeg and clove, also ground and sifted
6. Place everything over low heat and stir gently with a spoon or ladle so it does not stick
7. You will know it is ready if when touched with a finger it does not stick
8. Remove to a plate dusted with flour, where it will remain until it has cooled
9. Form the susamieles into an oval shape like mostachones, but without a point
10. These susamieles are not glazed
11. To give them a more exquisite taste, mix a little fragrant water, candied lemon, or a similar substance into the honey and sugar when it is combined with the almond paste
