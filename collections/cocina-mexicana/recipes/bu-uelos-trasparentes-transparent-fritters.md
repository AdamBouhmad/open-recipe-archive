## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Fifteen egg yolks
- Three egg whites
- Half pound aged cheese, ground
- A piece of leavening the size of an egg
- One cup anise water
- Salt
- Melted lard, as needed

## Directions

1. Mix one pound flour with fifteen yolks, three whites, half pound ground aged cheese, a piece of leavening, one cup anise water, salt, and enough melted lard so the dough is not very thick
2. Beat everything well
3. With a small spoon, fry spoonfuls in lard until golden
4. Remove and when cool, boil in syrup until they become transparent
