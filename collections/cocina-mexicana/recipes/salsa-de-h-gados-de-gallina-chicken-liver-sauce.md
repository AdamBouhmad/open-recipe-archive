## Ingredients

- chicken livers
- onions
- garlic
- cooked tomatoes
- celery
- pepper
- meat broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix chicken livers with peeled onions and garlic, cooked tomatoes without skin, celery and pepper
2. Grind everything and add meat broth
3. Dissolve the mixture well and pass through a sieve
4. Add necessary salt
5. Fry everything, and with more broth this sauce can be used to cook poultry, allowing it to season on gentle fire
