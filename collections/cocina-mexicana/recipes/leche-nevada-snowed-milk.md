## Ingredients

- milk
- fresh milk
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Three days before serving, cook milk thoroughly so that it creates a lot of cream, and let it sour slightly
2. After three days, give another boiling to fresh milk, then set it aside to cool
3. Once cooled, pour it into a pot with all the cream made before, the new cream, and white sugar
4. Mix everything with a spoon or small mill
5. Continue beating this milk and as it foams, pour it with a spoon into the plates, dusting with red tablet on top
