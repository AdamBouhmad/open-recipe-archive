## Ingredients

- masa (corn dough)
- manteca (lard)
- mole
- carne de puerco (pork)
- papada de puerco (pork jowl)
- hojas de plátano (plantain leaves)
- acuyo (herb)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Grind the masa well and prepare it as for other tamales.
2. Cook the masa with plenty of lard and set aside to cool.
3. If spoon tamales are desired, leave the masa more loose; if not, make it thicker.
4. When forming the tamales, fill them with well-fried and smooth mole with pork meat and pork jowl.
5. Pass the plantain leaves through running water to which a little acuyo has been added.
6. Once the tamales are formed, cook them with little hot salted water, covering the pot well.
