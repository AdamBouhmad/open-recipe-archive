## Ingredients

- lean ham
- cooked pork loin
- saffron
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- eggs
- lard
- much cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- vinegar
- toasted sesame
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Mince well on cold ham and cooked pork loin; season with saffron, pepper, clove and cinnamon, all ground; add raisins, almonds, pine nuts and eggs as for a small pastry, in fair quantity.
2. Fry with two or three layers of these in a pan with lard, which will be prepared over the fire, and taking it out from there, put it in syrup with much cinnamon, clove and pepper, all ground, a little vinegar, toasted sesame, raisins and almonds.
