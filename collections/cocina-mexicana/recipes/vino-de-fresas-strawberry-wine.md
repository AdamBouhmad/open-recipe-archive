## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- Sugar
- Aguardiente (optional)

## Directions

1. Crush the strawberries and add one pound of sugar for every six quartillos of juice
2. Let ferment for twenty-four hours
3. After this time place over fire and clarify, then let it ferment for eight days
4. Filter and bottle
5. Some add half a quartillo of aguardiente for every twelve of liquor, and do not bottle until four months after this mixture is made
