## Ingredients

- sirloin or loin of beef, sliced across the grain
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper powder
- parsley, finely chopped
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the meat of any nerve and flatten it with an iron or wooden mallet until it is the thickness of a finger
2. Soak the prepared meat in melted butter
3. Place on the grill at good heat without flame
4. Do not turn to the other side until it begins to brown
5. Once removed from heat, place on a warm platter where a sauce has been prepared consisting of butter, salt, pepper powder, finely chopped parsley, and a little lemon juice
6. Soak the beefsteak in this sauce on both sides
