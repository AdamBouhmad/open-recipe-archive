## Ingredients

- milk, four cuartillos
- rice, clean and well ground
- sugar syrup
- egg yolks, eighteen
- egg whites, six
- almonds, clean and pounded
- sponge cake, soaked in syrup

## Directions

1. To four cuartillos of milk add a little clean, well ground rice and the corresponding sugar syrup
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Add eighteen egg yolks and six egg whites with some clean pounded almonds
4. Stir much so that everything incorporates well
5. Place immediately over fire until it reaches the point of paste
6. Pour into a platter simply or over slices of sponge cake soaked in syrup
