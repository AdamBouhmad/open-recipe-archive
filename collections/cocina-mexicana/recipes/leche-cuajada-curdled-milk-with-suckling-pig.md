## Ingredients

- one quartillo of thick milk
- twelve egg yolks
- sugar
- a little starch
- orange flower water
- suckling pig (mamón)

## Directions

1. To one quartillo of thick milk add twelve egg yolks, sweeten to taste and add a little starch
2. Cook and when it thickens add a little orange flower water
3. When at the proper point, remove from heat
4. Arrange alternating slices of suckling pig and milk, with milk as the last layer
