## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound butter
- a little orange blossom water
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the flour, sugar, butter, orange blossom water, and eggs in a bowl
2. Mix everything well
3. Pour into a mold greased with butter
4. Bake in a gentle oven
