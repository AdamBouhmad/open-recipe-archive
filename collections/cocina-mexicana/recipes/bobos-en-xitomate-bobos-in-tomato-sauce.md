## Ingredients

- garlic, chopped
- large onions, chopped (same quantity as bobos)
- roasted and crushed tomatoes
- all ground spices except saffron
- alcaparras (capers), fresh and ground
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic and chopped large onions
2. Add roasted and crushed tomatoes with all ground spices except saffron
3. Cook the bobos, which should be cleaned and prepared, in this sauce
4. Add fresh ground capers and one or two tablespoons of vinegar
