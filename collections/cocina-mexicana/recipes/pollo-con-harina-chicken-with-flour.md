## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic cloves
- Thyme
- Flour
- Water
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Raw chorizos
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Put the chicken in quarters in a pot with one tablespoon of lard, ham slices and chorizos
2. Add chopped onion, garlic cloves and a little thyme
3. Fry everything very well
4. Add a little flour and fry a bit more
5. Add a little water
6. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper
7. Add a few raw chorizos to the broth so it becomes thick
8. Add a little Sherry wine
9. Garnish the platter with chiles, olives and capers
