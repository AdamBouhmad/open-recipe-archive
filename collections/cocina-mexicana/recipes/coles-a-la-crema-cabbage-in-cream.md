## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Butter
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Heavy cream

## Directions

1. After cleaning the cabbage well, boil it in salted water
2. Once cooked, let it cool and divide into large pieces
3. Place in a cooking vessel with butter over heat and season with salt, ground pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Once the cabbage is well parboiled or half-fried, add enough cream to cover the cabbage
5. Let it thicken over low heat, then remove from heat and serve with salt and pepper on top
