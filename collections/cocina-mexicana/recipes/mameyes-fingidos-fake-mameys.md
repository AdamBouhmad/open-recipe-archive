## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- One pound of mamey, ground and with fibers removed
- The heart of mamey
- Hard biscuit
- A little ground cinnamon
- Almond paste or covered almond

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of sugar with one pound of ground mamey, with fibers removed
2. Grind the heart of mamey with hard biscuit and add this to the rest so it takes body
3. Put everything on the fire and bring to the consistency of cajeta
4. Add a little ground cinnamon
5. Once the paste has cooled, form the mameys, trying to give them their shape
6. Imitate the pit with almond paste or covered almond
7. Roll them in well-ground cinnamon
