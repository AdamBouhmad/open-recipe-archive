## Ingredients

- six large shallots, finely chopped
- one good glass of vinegar
- one garlic head
- one bay leaf
- pieces of beef gelatin
- a little meat extract
- chili pepper butter
- one tablespoon of cooking oil

## Directions

1. Finely chop six large shallots, wash them and squeeze them in the corner of a napkin
2. Put them in a saucepan with a good glass of vinegar, a head of garlic, a bay leaf, and some pieces of beef gelatin
3. Let everything reduce together to a semi-gelatin consistency
4. Moisten with a little meat extract and finish by adding chili pepper butter and one tablespoon of cooking oil
