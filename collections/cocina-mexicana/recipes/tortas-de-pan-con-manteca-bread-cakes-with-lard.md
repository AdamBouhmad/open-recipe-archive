## Ingredients

- bread slices
- water
- lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices
2. Toast them on a grill
3. Dampen the bread in water
4. Fry the toasted slices in lard
5. When they come out of the pan, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Serve
