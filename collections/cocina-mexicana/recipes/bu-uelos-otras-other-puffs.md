## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- three large cups cold water
- one tablespoon lard
- anise water
- twelve egg whites
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dissolve one pound of flour in three large cups of cold water with one tablespoon of lard and a little anise water, taking care not to form lumps
2. Put on the fire until the dough forms and comes away from the pan
3. Let cool a little and beat twelve egg whites that will be mixed afterward with twelve egg yolks
4. Add them to the dough and proceed as in the previous article
