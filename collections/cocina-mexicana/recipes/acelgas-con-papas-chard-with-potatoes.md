## Ingredients

- chard
- potatoes
- lard
- onion
- garlic cloves
- ham
- meat from the pulp
- spices
- black pepper
- cloves
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the chard well and cook it with water until it has released all its color
2. Fry it in lard with a little minced onion, peeled garlic cloves, and minced or sliced potatoes until everything is well cooked
3. Add slices of fat and lean ham and meat from the pulp, minced after removing all moisture, having first cooked it on the fire in a pan without lard
4. Return to the fire and season with spices of all kinds, pounding the black pepper and cloves, and not forgetting garlic and salt
5. To give it consistency, mix in 2 or more eggs, according to the quantity
