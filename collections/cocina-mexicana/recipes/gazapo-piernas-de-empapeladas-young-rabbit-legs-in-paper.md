## Ingredients

- rabbit legs
- butter
- cinnamon
- cloves
- salt
- tomatoes
- garlic
- parsley
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Debone the rabbit legs
2. Fry them in butter until almost cooked
3. Dust with a little cinnamon, cloves, and salt
4. Wrap in buttered paper
5. Place on the grill until perfectly cooked
6. Finely mince tomatoes, garlic, parsley, and mushrooms
7. Fry in the remaining butter used for the legs
8. Mix a tablespoon of flour with a little broth or water and salt
9. Season well and pour this sauce over the rabbit legs and serve
