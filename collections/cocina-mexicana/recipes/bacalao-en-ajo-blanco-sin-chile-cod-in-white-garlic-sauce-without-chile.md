## Ingredients

- Bread
- Garlic
- Lard
- Black pepper
- Oil
- Garlic
- Cooked cod

## Directions

1. Toast a piece of bread in the fire until half burnt, and grind it with garlic browned in lard and a little pepper.
2. Burn oil in a pan and brown there some chopped garlic cloves, add the ground bread with everything else, and the already-cooked cod, cut into slices.
