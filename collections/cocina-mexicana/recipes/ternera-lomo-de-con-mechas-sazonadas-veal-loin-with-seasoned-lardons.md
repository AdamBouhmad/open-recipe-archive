## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Salt
- Garlic, ground
- Clove, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Vinegar
- Ham strips
- Water
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Coriander, toasted
- Chile pasilla, toasted and ground
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Lard

## Directions

1. Make a small paste with salt, garlic, clove and ground pepper and vinegar, and cover with it the ham strips with which the loin is larded.
2. Place the loin to marinate from one day to the next in a pot with water, vinegar, salt, garlic, pepper, clove, cinnamon, toasted coriander, a little toasted pasilla chile also ground and all mixed, oregano, thyme, laurel, and lard.
3. The next day place the loin to cook in this same adobo, and while cooking, empty it into a casserole, so that as the sauce is reduced over two fires, the loin remains roasted.
