## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 3 fresh eggs
- fine salt
- 2-3 egg yolks
- a little broken pepper
- meat stew

## Directions

1. Mix 6 three egg yolks and a little broken pepper, combining well all these substances until a mass is formed that will be seasoned with fine salt
2. To a pound of flour add three fresh eggs, and stir until forming a mass that is seasoned with fine salt
3. With a rolling pin form from it a sheet as thin as paper, dusting it with a little dry flour
4. Place short pieces of the prepared filling on half of the sheet at equal distance from each other
5. Moisten the other half of the flour sheet by sprinkling it with wine, and cover it pressing so it adheres at the edges of the filling
6. Cut equal and small squares, which will be placed for a little time over not very strong fire until they cook, which will be known when they float, then separate from the fire and place them drained of broth in another vessel
7. Take the soup tureen, and pouring in a little of the stew broth, place a layer of rabioles
8. On top add sufficient grated Flanders or La Barca cheese
9. Then a little melted butter, pouring on top more stew, which should be boiling
10. Thus multiply the layers of rabioles, cheese, butter and stew until filling the platter that will be served
