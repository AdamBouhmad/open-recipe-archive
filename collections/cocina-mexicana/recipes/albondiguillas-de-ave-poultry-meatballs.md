## Ingredients

- Poultry breasts
- Chicken fat
- Bread
- Cooked egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Raw egg
- Bread crumbs
- Poultry broth
- White sauce

## Directions

1. Mince poultry breasts finely with chicken fat.
2. Mix with bread soaked in milk, cooked egg yolks, salt, pepper, and cinnamon.
3. Beat with raw egg until it reaches the right consistency.
4. Form into round meatballs.
5. Bread them all around.
6. After frying, cook in poultry broth.
7. Serve with white sauce.
