## Ingredients

- Corn cooked as for tortillas
- Raw eggs
- Sugar
- A few of all spices

## Directions

1. Cook the corn as for tortillas, wash and remove the tips
2. Dry it in the sun
3. Grind it in a mortar until it becomes powder
4. Sieve it
5. Mix in raw eggs, sugar, a few of all spices
6. Grind everything, forming a dough as for tortillas
7. Form sheets with the same metlapil, lowering them on the metate
8. Form the pastry with these sheets
