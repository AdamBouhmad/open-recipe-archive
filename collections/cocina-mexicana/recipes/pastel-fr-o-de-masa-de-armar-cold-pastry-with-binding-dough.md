## Ingredients

- minced meat or thick sauce
- cooked meat from large animals, deboned
- cooked ham
- half-cooked fish, well deboned
- binding dough
- paper

## Directions

1. Prepare the minced meat or thick sauce
2. debone the cooked meats from large animals
3. cook the ham
4. prepare the fish by cooking it halfway and deboning it well
5. season all the meats before forming the pastry
6. form a ball, oval, or other shape from the dough
7. flatten it until it is about the thickness of a finger, slightly more or less
8. place it on paper the size of the pastry to be made
9. after giving it the desired shape, form a strip of dough of equal thickness to the base and as wide as desired for the pastry height
10. attach this ring or border well at the edges, making small pleats or fluting
11. spread the fine filling on the bottom
12. arrange the meats or fish, filling afterwards any gaps left by the coarse filling with the same fine filling that was placed at the bottom
13. flatten and press well so it binds together
14. if necessary, increase the height of the strip that surrounds the pastry, making it protrude from the edge at the top as a relief toward the outside
15. form a top cover that well covers the pastry and attach it to the edge border, forming a fold or chain pattern that gives it a good appearance
16. make a hole in the center where a rolled piece of cardboard or playing card is placed, which is removed when the pastry is halfway cooked, covering it with a small dough figurine that can cook in the short time remaining
17. form decorative figures on top as desired
18. sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
