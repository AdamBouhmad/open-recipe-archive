## Ingredients

- bread crusts
- butter (cow or pork)
- green pea puree
- puchero broth
- butter (cow)
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry bread crusts in butter until well browned
2. Make a puree of green peas, moisten with puchero broth until it is clear
3. Also melt a piece of butter in it
4. At the time of serving, add a little sugar
5. Pour over the fried bread crusts; the puree should be well seasoned
