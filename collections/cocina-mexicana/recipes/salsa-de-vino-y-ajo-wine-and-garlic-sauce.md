## Ingredients

- broth
- white wine
- salt
- parsley
- garlic
- lemon
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. Mix one quarter of broth with another quarter of white wine
2. Put on the fire until half is consumed
3. Season with salt
4. Add finely chopped parsley and garlic
5. After it has boiled for some time, remove from fire
6. Squeeze lemon juice into it at the moment of serving and add a little cooking oil
