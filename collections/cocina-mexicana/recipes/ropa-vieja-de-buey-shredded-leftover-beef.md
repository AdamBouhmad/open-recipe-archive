## Ingredients

- roasted beef leftovers or pieces of roasted beef
- flour
- butter
- water
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- mushrooms
- small onions, cooked

## Directions

1. Take the leftovers of roasted beef or coarsely chop the pieces of it.
2. Dust with flour, fry in butter, and once fried, add water, salt, pepper, and fine herbs.
3. Let boil until the broth is consumed, and when it has been consumed, beat some egg yolks, add a splash of vinegar, and add mushrooms and small onions, all cooked.
4. When it has thickened well, remove from heat and serve.
