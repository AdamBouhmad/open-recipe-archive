## Ingredients

- chicken
- offal (excluding tripe)
- ham
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- oil
- lard
- broth or water
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop the offal, excluding the tripe, into small pieces with a piece of ham, parsley, onion, and plenty of garlic and fry with oil and lard.
2. Parboil the chickens and then add them to the preparation with broth or water, clove, cinnamon and pepper.
3. Add a little oil if it lacks fat.
4. Leave loose if you want with sauce, or almost dry if not.
5. Cook over low heat and garnish with chiles and olives.
