## Ingredients

- Rice
- Broth or meat substance
- Salt
- Oil or fat
- Coals for toasting (optional)

## Directions

1. After washing the rice in warm water, cook it at low heat with broth or meat substance
2. When cooked, remove the fat from the top, taking care that it is neither too liquid nor too thick
3. If you wish it to be browned on top, place a lid with well-lit coals on it
4. On fasting days, break it in very hot water, add salt and oil
