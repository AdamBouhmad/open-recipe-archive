## Ingredients

- hare
- white wine
- butter or bacon
- spices
- garlic
- salt
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and soak in white wine, then fry with butter or bacon.
2. When done, pass to a pot, adding spices and two crushed garlic cloves and salt.
3. With the fat that remained, fry finely chopped onion and add to the pot with the wine in which the hare was soaked.
4. It must cook at least a couple of hours.
