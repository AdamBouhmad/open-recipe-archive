## Ingredients

- Sixteen egg yolks
- two lemon rinds descaled
- half an ounce of salt
- two ounces of sugar
- two quarts of cream
- four ounces of flour
- half an ounce of brewer's yeast
- warm water
- twelve ounces of flour
- four ounces of butter
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Break in a pot sixteen egg yolks, two descaled lemon rinds, half an ounce of salt and two ounces of sugar
2. Boil two quarts of cream and when boiling pour it over the yolks, stirring vigorously
3. Make it reach the proper point over the fire without letting it boil and pass through a cloth or sieve, letting it cool
4. Make yeast with four ounces of flour, half an ounce of brewer's foam and a little warm water, moistening it so it is somewhat soft
5. Let it ferment in a warm place
6. Soak another twelve ounces of flour with the cream mixture beaten with the yolks, adding four ounces of butter
7. Fold or gather the dough four or five times, add the yeast and fold two more times
8. Wrap the dough in a floured cloth, tie it firmly and let it rise or ferment in a warm place, taking about four hours to be in good condition
9. Then cut the pieces or little gifts the size of an egg, shaping them as desired by molding
10. Let them air or ferment more, about half an hour on a sheet
11. After browning put them in the oven
12. When cooked, open them on one side, remove a little of the crumb and put in their place butter kneaded with salt
13. Serve
