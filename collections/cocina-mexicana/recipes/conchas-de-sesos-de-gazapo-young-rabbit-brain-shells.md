## Ingredients

- Young rabbit brains
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- German sauce
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Bread crumbs
- Grated cheese
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Place the desired quantity of brains in water
2. Blanch in boiling salted water with vinegar
3. Clean and place in German sauce with mushrooms that have been previously cooked with butter and lemon juice
4. Place this stew in shells
5. Sprinkle with grated bread and cheese
6. Sprinkle with melted butter
7. Brown under a bell oven
