## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- parsley
- mint leaves
- veal
- ham
- Castilian vinegar
- water
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- Jerez wine
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Put lard in a cazuela and when well hot add sliced garlic and sliced onion, minced tomato, parsley and mint leaves
2. When the sauce is fried, add the meat in very thin slices and other slices of ham
3. Allow to fry and add Castilian vinegar with an equal part of water, then clove, cinnamon and powdered pepper
4. Before serving increase with Jerez wine
5. The broth should remain almost dry
6. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers
