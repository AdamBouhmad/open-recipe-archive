## Ingredients

- clams
- butter
- parsley
- onions
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks
- vinegar or agraz juice

## Directions

1. After washing them well, scrape their shells to remove sand and membranous ligaments that surround them.
2. Let them drain, dry them and place them dry in a pot over good heat to open them.
3. Remove one shell from each one and arrange them symmetrically on a plate.
4. Make the following sauce: fry in butter, chopped parsley and onions and a little flour, and moisten the frying with a little broth, and as it reduces, bind with egg yolks, adding a little vinegar or agraz juice.
5. Heat the plate a little and serve.
6. Alternatively, they can also be made by putting the clams in the pot with the butter, and seasoning the sauce together with them.
