## Ingredients

- large onions
- bread crumb
- milk
- hard egg yolks
- grated cheese
- cloves
- egg yolks
- flour
- egg yolk for coating
- oil for frying
- tomato sauce or lemon juice or green chile sauce

## Directions

1. Clean large onions and scald them; once well drained, hollow from the top to fill with the following:
2. Soak bread crumb in milk, squeeze well, add two hard egg yolks, two ounces of grated cheese, two cloves, and two beaten egg yolks; chop and mix everything very well.
3. Once the hollow is filled, flour the onions, coat with egg yolk, and fry; serve immediately, coating them with tomato sauce or with lemon juice; they can also be coated with green chile sauce.
