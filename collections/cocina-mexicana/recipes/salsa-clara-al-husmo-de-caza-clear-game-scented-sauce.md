## Ingredients

- essence of game
- water

## Directions

1. Proceed the same way as for making game essence, being able to make the sauce with the scraps or leftovers of rabbits and partridges
2. When the essence has been strained, pour it into a pan and bring to a boil
3. Place it in a corner of the stovetop, adding half a glass of cold water to skim and degrease it, which is done by carefully removing all the particles and skimming it with a spoon
4. Leave the essence to reduce until it has the consistency of a semi-gelatin, that is, until it has the consistency of syrup
