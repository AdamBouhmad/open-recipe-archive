## Ingredients

- verjuice
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- shallots
- cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Put two spoonfuls of verjuice juice and as much strained broth or essence into a pot, with salt, pepper, onions and shallots chopped very finely
2. Make sure the cooking liquid is very seasoned and let it heat
3. Add the cooked potatoes cut into very thin slices
4. After letting them become impregnated with the cooking liquid for a few minutes, remove them and serve
