## Ingredients

- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Green chiles
- Parsley
- Yerbabuena
- Lard
- Flour
- Fish broth
- Capers
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions, tomatoes, garlic, green chiles, parsley and yerbabuena, and fry them in lard
2. Also fry and brown a little flour
3. Mix it with the fried seasoning
4. Add a little of the broth in which the fish was cooked, if it is to be served cooked, and chopped capers; but if the fish is to be served roasted or fried, do not add water, and in either case add small amounts of vinegar
5. Pour this sauce or broth over the fish to take it to the table
