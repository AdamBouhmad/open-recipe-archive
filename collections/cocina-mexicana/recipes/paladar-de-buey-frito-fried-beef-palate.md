## Ingredients

- Beef palate
- Broth
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Butter
- Flour
- Oil for frying
- Fried parsley

## Directions

1. Divide a well-cooked and skinned palate into pieces the length of a finger
2. Place in a sauce composed of broth with an equal quantity of vinegar, salt, pepper, and a head of finely chopped garlic
3. Slightly heat this sauce after adding kneaded butter with flour, so the butter melts
4. Add the pieces, which must remain in this sauce for about three hours
5. After this time, remove and drain well, coat with flour, and place in very hot oil to fry
6. Serve accompanied by fried parsley
