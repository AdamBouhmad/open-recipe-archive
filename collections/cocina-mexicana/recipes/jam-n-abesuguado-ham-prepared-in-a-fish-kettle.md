## Ingredients

- ham
- meat slices
- onion slices
- carrot slices
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- water
- brandy
- veal reduction or powdered sugar
- legume stew

## Directions

1. Prepare and desalt as for ham in its natural state
2. Place in a fish kettle over slices of meat, onion and carrot strips, parsley, thyme, bay leaf, clove, etc.
3. Moisten with water
4. When half cooked, add a glass of good brandy
5. Do not cover the kettle again after adding brandy
6. When fully cooked, remove the skin and coat with either veal reduction or powdered sugar browned with a hot iron plate, or place in an oven to take on good color
7. Can be served over a vegetable stew of choice
