## Ingredients

- almonds, toasted
- sesame seeds, toasted
- ancho chiles, toasted
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- cooked turkey
- turkey broth
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Toast and grind together almonds, sesame seeds, and ancho chiles with clove and cinnamon
2. Fry this mixture in sufficient lard with salt until it releases its oil
3. Add the cooked turkey, its broth, a little vinegar and sugar
4. Simmer until it reaches a good consistency, neither watery nor too thick
5. Serve with toasted sesame seeds sprinkled on top
