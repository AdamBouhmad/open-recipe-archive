## Ingredients

- three regular potatoes
- a small cup of milk, well boiled with bay leaves, lemon peel, a little aniseed, a stick of cinnamon, and a piece of sugar
- three eggs
- lard
- sugar and cinnamon for coating

## Directions

1. Cook three regular potatoes and grind them.
2. Combine with a small cup of milk that has been boiled with bay leaves, lemon peel, a little aniseed, a stick of cinnamon, and a piece of sugar.
3. Beat all these ingredients with three eggs until loose.
4. Heat lard in a frying pan and spoon in small spoonfuls of the mixture.
5. Once fried, roll in sugar and cinnamon.
