## Ingredients

- potatoes
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- white beets or pickled gherkins, optional

## Directions

1. After washing, cook under ashes in an oven or put without water in a covered pot
2. Place the pot over very gentle fire and shake frequently without uncovering
3. When potatoes are cooked, peel them while still hot and cut into thin slices
4. Arrange in the salad bowl, hot or cold, and season like common salad, noting that they need much more oil, vinegar, and salt
5. Can add some very thin slices of white beets or pickled gherkins
