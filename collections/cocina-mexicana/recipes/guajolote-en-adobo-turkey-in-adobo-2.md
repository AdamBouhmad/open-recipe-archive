## Ingredients

- old turkey
- ham strips
- veal scraps
- veal leg
- white wine or brandy
- broth
- roots
- onions
- parsley
- herbs
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Pluck and empty an old turkey and tuck its legs into the body
2. Turn it over hot coals several times, cover with thick strips of well-seasoned ham and wrap in linen which is sewn closed
3. Place in a pot just large enough for it, add veal scraps, ham strips and a veal leg
4. Moisten with a quart of white wine or a measure of brandy, broth, roots, onions, a bouquet of parsley and fine herbs, salt, pepper, garlic and bay leaf
5. Cook over gentle heat and turn
6. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve and reduce until it becomes a jelly, let it cool and spread over the turkey
7. Place on a platter on a napkin, garnished with fresh parsley
8. It can also be served hot by reducing some of the jelly to sauce
