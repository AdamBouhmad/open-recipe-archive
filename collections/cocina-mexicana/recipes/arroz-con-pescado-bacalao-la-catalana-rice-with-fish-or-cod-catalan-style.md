## Ingredients

- oil
- butter
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- raw fish or cod
- rice
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry in a cazuela with oil and butter tomato, onion, parsley and garlic
2. When the mixture is almost fried, add the raw fish or cod to half-cook it; remove it and in the same broth add the rice unwashed and only clean of all debris
3. Once it is almost browned, add the fish or cod to finish browning
4. Add the necessary water, which must boil in bubbles and leave over very strong fire for twenty minutes or half an hour at most, after adding the proper salt, and with the cazuela covered
5. Remove from fire and let it rest a while before serving, keeping the cazuela very covered so the rice finishes cooking with its own steam
