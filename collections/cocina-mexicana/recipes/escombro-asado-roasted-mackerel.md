## Ingredients

- Mackerel, dried well
- Oiled paper
- Butter
- Fine herbs

## Directions

1. Dry the mackerel well and wrap it in oiled paper
2. Roast on both sides
3. Open along the back
4. Fill with butter mixed with fine herbs
5. Serve immediately, sprinkled with lemon juice
