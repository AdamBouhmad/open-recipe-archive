## Ingredients

- tench
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Place in boiling water and remove after one minute
2. Scale them
3. Stuff the body with butter mixed with fine herbs
4. Roast on a grill
5. Serve with some sauce of piquant and pleasant flavor
