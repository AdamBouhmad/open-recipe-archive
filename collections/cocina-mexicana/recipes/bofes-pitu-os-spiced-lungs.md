## Ingredients

- lungs
- water
- garlic, four cloves
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash well and cook the lungs in a pot, keeping the trachea outside the pot so it foams
2. Once cooked, cut into small pieces
3. Crush four garlic cloves with a little pepper, dissolve in vinegar and sprinkle over the lungs with oil and ground salt
4. Turn over low heat
