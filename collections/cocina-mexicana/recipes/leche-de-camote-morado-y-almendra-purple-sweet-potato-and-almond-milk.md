## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- sugar
- cooked and sieved purple sweet potato
- half a pound of almond, soaked and ground

## Directions

1. Sweeten eight cuartillos of milk with a heavy hand of sugar
2. Add cooked and sieved purple sweet potato as needed to color the milk
3. Allow to boil until it thickens
4. Add half a pound of almond, soaked and ground
5. Increase the sugar if needed for sweetness
6. When it reaches the proper point, pour into serving dishes
