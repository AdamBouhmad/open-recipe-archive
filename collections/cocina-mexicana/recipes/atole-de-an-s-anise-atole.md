## Ingredients

- atole
- ground cacao shell (well toasted), black corn and anise, or without black corn
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix with common atole the powder made from very toasted cacao shell and ground with black corn and anise (called prieto anise, or if less toasted and without black corn, called colorado anise)
2. Add a little sugar and let it boil for some time
