## Ingredients

- 1 quart water (more or less as needed)
- 3 ounces cheese
- 3 ounces butter
- a little salt and pepper
- flour, sifted
- eggs
- cheese slices for topping

## Directions

1. Put water, cheese, butter, salt, and pepper in a saucepan
2. Heat over fire and when the water boils, remove from heat
3. Incorporate sifted flour into the water as explained in the cabbage batter article
4. Proceed as before with whole eggs
5. Arrange the branches, etc. on wet platforms
6. Brown them lightly
7. Top each piece with a thin slice of cheese
8. Bake in a gentle oven
