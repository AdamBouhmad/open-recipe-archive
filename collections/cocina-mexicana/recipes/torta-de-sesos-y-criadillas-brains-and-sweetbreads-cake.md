## Ingredients

- Brains, sliced
- Sweetbreads, sliced
- Pork feet, minced
- Eggs
- Lard
- Tomato, coarsely chopped
- Garlic, sliced
- Onion heads, sliced
- Clove, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Vinegar
- Oregano, dried
- Water
- White sugar
- Olives
- Tornachile pieces
- Chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the brains and sweetbreads until done, then slice them
2. Cook the pork feet and mince them
3. Butter a baking dish
4. Layer in the following order: first a layer of sliced brains, then a thin layer of beaten egg; then a layer of sliced sweetbreads, and over it a thick layer of minced pork feet; alternate the layers in this manner
5. Cover finally with one or two beaten eggs
6. Place the cake at two gentle fires so it sets
7. For the sauce: coarsely chop a little tomato, slice clean garlic and onion heads, and fry all in a little lard
8. When well fried, season with ground clove, pepper and cinnamon in good vinegar
9. Add a little dried oregano, a little water and a lump of white sugar so it becomes sweet and sour
10. Add olives, pieces of tornachile and chiles in vinegar
11. Let it boil until it becomes thick
12. Serve the cake with this sauce
