## Ingredients

- one pound esperanza almonds
- five dozen bitter almonds
- water
- three tablespoons starch
- half pound sugar
- twelve eggs

## Directions

1. Soak one pound esperanza almonds and five dozen bitter almonds
2. Grind with a little water so they do not become oily
3. Mix with three tablespoons starch and half pound sugar, both sifted
4. Add twelve beaten eggs, egg whites as for turrón and egg yolks as for royal eggs
5. Incorporate everything well
6. Pour into paper molds lined with butter
7. Bake in the oven
