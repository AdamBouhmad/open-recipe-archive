## Ingredients

- Veal loin, cut into steaks
- Water
- Vinegar
- Ground cumin
- Garlic, ground
- Lard
- Thyme
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt
- Black pepper, ground
- Potatoes, cooked and fried
- Parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Cook the steaks in water with vinegar, ground cumin and garlic, lard, and a sprig of thyme
2. When cooked and the broth is consumed, remove from the pot
3. Roll them in breadcrumbs mixed with salt and ground black pepper
4. Fry in lard
5. Garnish for serving with slices of cooked and fried potatoes and parsley leaves
