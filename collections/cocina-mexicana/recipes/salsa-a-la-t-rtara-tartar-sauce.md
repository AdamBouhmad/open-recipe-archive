## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 shallots, finely chopped
- chervil, finely chopped
- tarragon, finely chopped
- mustard
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Put in a bowl 2 or 3 shallots, chervil and tarragon, all finely chopped, with mustard, salt, pepper and a splash of vinegar
2. Add oil and stir constantly
3. If the sauce thickens too quickly, add a little more vinegar
4. If it is too salty, add more mustard and oil
