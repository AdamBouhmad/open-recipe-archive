## Ingredients

- Lard
- Two or three garlic cloves
- Xitomatl (tomatoes)
- Onion
- Salt
- Rice
- Pot broth (or water in which fish has been soaked for vigil days)

## Directions

1. Burn in lard two or three garlic cloves and then fry tomatoes and considerable onion in proportion to the rice, both chopped
2. Season with salt
3. Add immediately the raw rice without soaking
4. Let it brown and then add pot broth or, if the soup is for a vigil day, water in which fish has been soaked
5. Let it thicken in both cases without stirring
