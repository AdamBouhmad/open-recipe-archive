## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Warm water
- Cold water
- Brush

## Directions

1. Place truffles in warm water and use a brush to remove all soil and sand as they are removed
2. Transfer to cold water
3. Clean again with the brush until nothing remains
4. Wash a third time and let drain
