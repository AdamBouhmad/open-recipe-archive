## Ingredients

- small sponge cakes
- butter, melted
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg, beaten
- milk
- toasted sesame seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Divide small sponge cakes in half and remove the crumb
2. Mix the crumb with melted butter, sugar, and cinnamon
3. Refill the cakes with this paste and join the two halves together
4. Cover with beaten egg and fry in butter
5. Boil milk with sugar and a stick of cinnamon
6. Place the cakes over two fires until the milk thickens, then add toasted sesame seeds, raisins, almonds, and cinnamon
7. Alternatively, they can be made without frying by placing them in the boiled milk after filling, in which case add eggs to the milk
