## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- four ounces of butter
- eight egg yolks
- water
- slices of pan de la vida
- ground cinnamon
- grated cheese
- butter for decoration

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar and when it reaches regular point, remove from heat
2. Add four ounces of butter and allow to cool
3. Add eight egg yolks dissolved in a little water and strained through a sieve
4. Return to heat for the mixture to thicken slightly
5. Soak slices of pan de la vida and place in a platter
6. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon and plenty of grated cheese
7. Place small pieces of butter and add a second layer, ensuring it is well covered with the paste
8. Proceed the same way for additional layers
9. Place heat on top
10. After cooling, decorate with butter flowers pressed through a fine sieve
11. Serve with a knife
