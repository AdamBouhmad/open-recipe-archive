## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- parsley
- ground pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the brains with sufficient salt
2. Remove the skin and veins and chop with garlic, onion, tomato and parsley
3. Add ground pepper and salt, stir everything and mix with beaten egg
4. Pour into a buttered dish and cook to set
5. Serve the pie dry and sprinkled with salt and pepper, or stewed in some broth
