## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- oregano
- thyme
- parsley
- salt
- lime peel strips
- salt
- pepper
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Marinate the ribs for two days with oregano, thyme, parsley, salt and lime peel strips
2. Remove and place on a wide grill over moderate heat
3. After turning and when done dust with salt and pepper
4. Serve with spicy sauce
