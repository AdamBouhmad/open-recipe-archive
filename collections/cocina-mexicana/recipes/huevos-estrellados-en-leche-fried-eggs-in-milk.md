## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Milk
- Fine salt
- Cinnamon sticks
- Sugar
- Cinnamon (powdered)

## Directions

1. Bring a sufficient quantity of milk to boil in a pan with a little fine salt and some cinnamon sticks
2. Add a good piece of sugar
3. Fry the eggs in this milk
4. When serving, sprinkle with a little powdered cinnamon
