## Ingredients

- Butter
- Oil
- Slices of cold bread
- Garlic cloves
- Two or three hard-boiled egg yolks
- Broth from the pot
- Chopped garlic and onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- A little vinegar
- Sliced capers

## Directions

1. Heat butter and oil in a pan
2. Fry slices of cold bread, garlic cloves, and two or three hard-boiled egg yolks in the pan
3. Grind everything together and dissolve in broth from the pot
4. Fry chopped garlic and onion
5. Add the broth seasoned with necessary salt and pepper, a little vinegar, and sliced capers
6. Let it thicken over gentle heat
7. Once thickened, strain into a sauce dish
