## Ingredients

- 50 frogs
- salt
- butter
- 8 to 10 large peppercorns
- nutmeg, amount that fits in three fingers, grated
- crumbled bread from a loaf
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the skins and heads from fifty frogs
2. Cook in water with a little salt, one tablespoon of butter, eight or ten large peppercorns, and grated nutmeg
3. When well cooked, put them in a mortar with the crumbled bread from a regular loaf of bread
4. Grind until a mass forms
5. Dissolve in strained broth from the same cooking liquid and a greater quantity of broth from the pot
6. Strain everything through a cloth
7. Put over gentle heat without boiling
8. Place bread slices in the tureen, moisten with a little broth from the pot
9. When serving the soup, bathe well with the frog substance, ensuring the broth is neither watery nor thick
