## Ingredients

- chickpeas, soaked from the day before
- white tequesite
- ancho chiles, soaked and desveined
- garlic cloves, cleaned
- piece of bread soaked in vinegar
- little bits of cumin
- lard
- pork broth
- fine salt
- dry oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soaked from the day before, put the chickpeas to cook with a bit of white tequesite.
2. Afterwards grind soaked and desveined ancho chiles, clean garlic cloves, a piece of bread soaked in vinegar, and a few bits of cumin, and fry all this in lard.
3. Add a bit of pork broth, and season with necessary fine salt.
4. Add a bit of dry oregano, and when well boiled, add a bit of vinegar and let it thicken on strong fire.
5. From there they will be put in the steam of a boiling pot.
