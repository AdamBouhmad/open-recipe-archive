## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- lemon juice
- folded dough or common dough
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a stew of mushrooms cut into dice shapes
2. Degrease it and bind it
3. Add lemon juice and let it cool
4. Make small leaves of the folded dough mentioned in the previous article or of common dough
5. Place the stew between two leaves
6. Seal the edges and fry the pastries in butter
7. Glaze like the previous ones
