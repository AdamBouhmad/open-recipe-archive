## Ingredients

- Powdered sugar
- Candied orange blossom
- Water
- Cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put powdered sugar in a copper pot or small pan, not tin
2. Melt without water over strong heat until it takes on a deep color
3. Add, according to the amount of caramel, more or less candied orange blossom previously dissolved in water
4. Add cream or milk in the same proportion
5. Strain everything after cooking and thicken over a bain-marie; serve cold like the others
