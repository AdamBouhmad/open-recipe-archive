## Ingredients

- Beef wheel or rump
- Lard or ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onion
- Shallot
- Garlic
- Pork or fresh ham rind
- Water
- Refined brandy

## Directions

1. Divide the beef wheel into slices and lard them with lard or ham
2. Season with salt, pepper, minced parsley, onion, shallot, and a little garlic
3. Also divide the pork or fresh ham rind into pieces
4. In a pot, make alternating layers of larded beef slices and pork rind
5. Moisten everything with one glass of water and another of refined brandy
6. Let cook four or five hours over hot coals
7. Serve like beef or beef à la mode (see page 92)
