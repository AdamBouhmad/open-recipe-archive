## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- water
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- 1 clove
- 3 large onions
- 1 parsnip
- fat ham
- 4 ounces rice
- broth from the pot

## Directions

1. After trussing the capon to maintain a graceful position while cooking, place it in a casserole where water almost completely covers it, seasoned with salt, very little thyme and bay leaf, 1 clove, 3 large onions, 1 parsnip and fat ham, letting it cook well in this small broth, turning it from time to time.
2. Set aside half of the same broth and then cook 4 ounces of rice in it, adding a little broth from the pot.
3. Take another small portion of the capon's broth and let it reduce to the point of gelatin to give color to the capon and the rice on top, pouring the rest of the broth into the same rice that will be cooking, letting it thicken.
4. Bind the rice with the gelatin and serve it over the capon.
