## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- half a pound of sugar
- raisins
- pine nuts
- pieces of almonds and walnuts
- ground cinnamon
- eight beaten eggs
- biscuit as necessary

## Directions

1. Sweeten two cuartillos of milk with half a pound of sugar and strain it
2. Mix in raisins, pine nuts, pieces of almonds and walnuts, ground cinnamon, eight beaten eggs and enough biscuit to make a paste
3. Once everything is well incorporated, pour into a mold greased with lard and cook as in the previous recipe
