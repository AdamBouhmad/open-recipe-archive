## Ingredients

- Mamón
- Well-sweetened custard
- Twelve egg yolks
- Scented pastille
- Orange blossom water
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Once the mamón is well ground on cold
2. Mix with well-sweetened custard, twelve egg yolks, scented pastille, and orange blossom water
3. Place in a platter greased with butter or lard
4. Let cook over gentle fire
5. Grease it with butter using feathers
6. Continue cooking as directed
