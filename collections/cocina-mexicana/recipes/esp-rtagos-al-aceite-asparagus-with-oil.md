## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of the white from all, tie equally in a bundle
2. Blanch in boiling water, then pass to cold water and then cook
3. Remove all the water and place on a plate in a single bundle
4. Serve with vinegar cruets
