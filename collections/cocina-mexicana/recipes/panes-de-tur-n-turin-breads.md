## Ingredients

- twelve tablespoons of flour
- six tablespoons of powdered sugar
- two or three eggs
- zest of one lemon
- two ounces of fine butter

## Directions

1. Make a manageable but firm dough with twelve tablespoons of flour, six tablespoons of powdered sugar, two or three eggs, the zest of one lemon, and two ounces of fine butter, beating or kneading with a wooden spoon in a bowl
2. Add a little flour and sugar if the dough becomes too soft
3. Turn onto a table and knead until it can be rolled easily by hand
4. Form small pastries of all kinds of designs, which should be the length of a finger
5. Glaze many times on tin sheets before placing in the oven, which should be hotter than for ordinary biscuit
