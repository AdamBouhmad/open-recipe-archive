## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Garlic
- Tomatoes
- Green chiles
- Salt
- Eggs
- Toasted bread
- Pieces of ham
- Longaniza
- Chorizo
- Cooked egg
- Capers
- Olives
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Parsley
- Cooked squabs
- Bread slices
- Black pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry in butter chopped onion, garlic, tomatoes and green chile, and once combined with salt, add eggs
2. Mix and when set, grease a pan with butter
3. Place a layer of beaten eggs, another of bread toasted in butter, another of the scrambled eggs
4. Add pieces of cooked ham, longaniza, chorizo, egg, capers, olives, raisins, almonds, chopped parsley, cooked squabs in quarters
5. Add another layer of bread slices and continue layering with beaten egg and black pepper between each layer
6. Make the last layer of beaten egg
7. Cook over two gentle fires and when set, sprinkle sugar on top
