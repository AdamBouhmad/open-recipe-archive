## Ingredients

- lard
- salt
- tomatoes
- pineapple
- toasted sesame
- jerez wine
- chicken broth
- flour browned in lard
- cloves
- pepper powder
- pineapple, chopped
- chicken
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry tomatoes, pineapple, and toasted sesame in lard with salt
2. Add jerez wine, chicken broth, flour browned in lard, cloves, and pepper powder
3. Add more chopped pineapple
4. Place the chicken, divided into quarters, with a bit of sugar in this sauce
5. Let simmer until the sauce thickens
