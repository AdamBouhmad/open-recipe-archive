## Ingredients

- Unripe grapes (agraz)
- Sugar
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make the fruit turn green as for preserve
2. Pass through a sieve pressing it strongly
3. Let it dry
4. Add to syrup at the cracking point in water
5. Heat only without boiling
6. Use as much sugar as the dried fruit
