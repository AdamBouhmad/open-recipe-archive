## Ingredients

- Pork loins
- Salt
- All spices except saffron
- Onion
- Water
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Lemon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the wide pork loins into strips one finger thick.
2. Pound them with a mallet so they become thin.
3. Finely chop the rest of the loins and season with all spices except saffron.
4. With a knife point, coat the strips with the chopped mixture.
5. Roll them one by one and tie with twine in the middle.
6. Fry well-minced onion, then add water and a little flour so it becomes thin, and sufficient salt.
7. Place the chops there to cook.
8. When cooked, remove the strings and season the broth with all spices except saffron.
9. In another pot, beat egg yolks with a little lemon and finely chopped parsley.
10. At mealtime, when the pot of chops is boiling, incorporate this mixture.
