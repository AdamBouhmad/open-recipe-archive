## Ingredients

- One onion
- One head of garlic, cleaned
- A little parsley
- One lettuce
- Lard or butter
- Broth from the pot
- Ground black pepper
- French bread slices
- Fried brains
- Dry roasted coriander powder

## Directions

1. Chop one onion, one cleaned head of garlic, a little parsley, and one lettuce, and fry all in lard or butter
2. After frying, add broth from the pot and a little ground black pepper
3. Place fried French bread slices in a serving dish
4. When the broth is seasoned, bathe the toast with it in the dish
5. Cover the dish with a griddle with coals on top and place on the fire
6. After seasoning the soup, garnish with fried brains and dry roasted coriander powder
