## Ingredients

- xitomates (tomatoes) roasted, peeled and seeds removed
- tomates (tomatoes) well cooked
- chile verde (green chile)
- queso (cheese)
- cebolla picada (chopped onion)

## Directions

1. Roast tomatoes, peel them and remove the seeds
2. Add well cooked tomatoes and grind with green chile
3. Place the mixture in the center of the chalupa with cheese and chopped onion
4. Follow the same method as for the previous chalupas
