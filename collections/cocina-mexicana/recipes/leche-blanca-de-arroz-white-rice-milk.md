## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of ground rice
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Sweeten six cuartillos of milk
2. Mix with four ounces of ground rice
3. Incorporate and strain
4. Place over heat and add cinnamon sticks
5. Allow to boil, stirring to prevent sticking
6. When it reaches the proper point, remove from heat and pour into serving dishes
