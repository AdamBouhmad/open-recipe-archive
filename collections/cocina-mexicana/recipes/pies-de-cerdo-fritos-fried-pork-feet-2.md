## Ingredients

- pork feet
- salt
- bread crumbs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the feet and cook them with salt
2. When well cooked, bone them without breaking them into pieces
3. Grate much bread and mix it with ground pepper
4. Roll the cooked feet well in it
5. Grease a casserole with lard
6. Place a layer of feet and dust with more bread crumbs and pepper
7. Place another layer of feet and sprinkle with a little ground salt
