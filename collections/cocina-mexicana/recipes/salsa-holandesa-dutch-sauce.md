## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Vinegar drops
- Half glass of water
- Salt
- Grated nutmeg
- Beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix a piece of butter with a little flour and some drops of vinegar, half glass of water, salt and grated nutmeg, with beaten egg yolks
2. Place over heat, stirring continuously and taking care that it does not boil, because it would curdle
