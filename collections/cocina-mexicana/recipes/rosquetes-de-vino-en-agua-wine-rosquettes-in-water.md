## Ingredients

- thirty eggs
- one pound of white sugar
- six pounds of flour without leaven or salt
- half a cuartillo of white wine
- one pound of melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat thirty eggs with one pound of white sugar
2. Mix with six pounds of flour without leaven or salt, half a cuartillo of white wine, and one pound of melted butter
3. From each pound of dough, make five rosquettes
