## Ingredients

- Beets with their stems
- Panela
- Fine syrup
- Pineapple sliced
- Cinnamon sticks
- Whole cloves

## Directions

1. Cook beets with their stems and panela
2. Clean and slice them
3. Place in fine syrup with sliced pineapple, cinnamon sticks and whole cloves so it takes on flavor
4. Preserve until it reaches the proper point, then set aside so only the beet remains
