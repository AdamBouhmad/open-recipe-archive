## Ingredients

- cooked roasted hen
- butter
- hen broth
- cloves
- cinnamon
- cilantro
- cream from cooked milk
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- sesame
- white wine
- sugar
- marquesote slices
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After the hen is cooked, roasted and cold, debone it and mince the meat
2. Put a pot on fire with butter and when hot add a little of the hen broth with cloves, cinnamon and toasted cilantro, all ground
3. Add the minced meat with cream from cooked milk so that it becomes like a paste
4. Add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, toasted sesame, a little white wine and a small piece of sugar
5. Line a pot with butter and place in it a layer of slices of marquesote
6. Arrange the minced meat mixture prepared as stated on this layer
7. Cover the top with another layer of marquesote slices
8. Cook over two fires and when done beat egg yolks and add them
9. Cover the pot again with a comal with fire so that the egg sets
