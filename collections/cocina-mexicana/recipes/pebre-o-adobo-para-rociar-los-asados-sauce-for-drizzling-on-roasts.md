## Ingredients

- Pork fat, chopped
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- Four tablespoons of oil

## Directions

1. Chop pork fat mixed with garlic and parsley
2. Add salt, pepper, one tablespoon of vinegar, and four tablespoons of oil
3. Beat everything together very well
