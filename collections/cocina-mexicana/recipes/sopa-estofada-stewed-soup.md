## Ingredients

- Lard
- Sliced onions
- Whole garlic cloves
- Mutton or beef in small pieces
- Some eggs
- Chopped ham
- Broth from the pot
- Tender peas, well cooked
- Some pieces of poultry
- Salt
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Well toasted bread slices
- Lean ham pieces
- Chicken breast
- Poultry giblets
- Longaniza sausage
- Whole chorizos

## Directions

1. Heat lard and fry sliced onions, whole garlic cloves, mutton or beef in small pieces, some eggs, and chopped ham
2. When it has taken on a roasted color, add broth from the pot, tender well-cooked peas, and some pieces of poultry
3. Season with salt and let everything cook over low heat
4. A little before removing from heat, add ground cinnamon and clove
5. Pass through a strainer
6. Place well-toasted bread slices in a serving dish
7. Pour the strained broth over the bread
8. Once it boils a little, the soup can be served with lean ham pieces, chicken breast, poultry giblets, longaniza sausage, whole chorizos
