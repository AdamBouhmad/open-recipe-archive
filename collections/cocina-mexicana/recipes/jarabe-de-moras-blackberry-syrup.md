## Ingredients

- very dark blackberries, slightly before reaching perfect maturity
- twenty-four ounces of blackberry juice
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Express very dark blackberries, slightly before reaching perfect maturity
2. Add twenty-four ounces of the juice to syrup at syrup stage, made with two pounds of sugar
3. Let boil for several minutes, remove from heat, let cool and store in dry, well-sealed bottles
