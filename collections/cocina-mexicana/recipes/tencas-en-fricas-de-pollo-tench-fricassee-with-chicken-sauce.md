## Ingredients

- tench
- butter
- assorted herbs
- broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the heads and cut into pieces
2. Fry in butter with an assorted bundle of herbs
3. Moisten with broth and white wine
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Cook over brisk heat
6. Bind the sauce with cream and egg yolks
