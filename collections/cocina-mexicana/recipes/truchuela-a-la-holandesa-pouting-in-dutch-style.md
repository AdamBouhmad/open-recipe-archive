## Ingredients

- pouting
- salt
- water
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the pouting well and cut into slices three fingers thick
2. Parboil by simmering two hours in a pot with two good handfuls of white salt and a glass of water
3. In a well-tinned pot prepared with water that should not boil until the moment of serving, add two more good handfuls of salt, ensuring the water is very salty
4. Cook the slices on a grill for five to seven minutes over high heat
5. Slide them into a slotted plate
6. Surround with parsley and let them cook in steam with salt and small rounded potatoes served separately
7. At the same time serve melted butter mixed with water in a sauce boat
