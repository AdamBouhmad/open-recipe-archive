## Ingredients

- Turkey offal
- Butter or lard
- Flour
- Broth or water
- Pepper
- Salt
- Bouquet garni
- Turnips
- Potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the offal into pieces and semi-fry or parboil in butter
2. Dust with flour and moisten with broth or water, adding pepper, salt and a mixed bouquet garni
3. Let cook and add turnips browned in butter
4. Before serving, remove the fat from the cooking liquid
5. Potatoes can be added if desired
