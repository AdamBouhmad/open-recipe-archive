## Ingredients

- ox loin
- oil
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- bay leaf
- buttered paper
- potatoes
- hot sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the loin well, removing all tendons and give it a few boils
2. Marinate in oil for twenty-four hours, adding salt, onion slices, parsley and bay leaf
3. Wrap in buttered paper and place on the roasting spit until it cooks
4. Shortly before removing from the fire, remove the paper so it browns
5. Serve with fried potatoes and hot sauce
