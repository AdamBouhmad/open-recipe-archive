## Ingredients

- 6 quartillos of milk mixed with very sweet cream
- 1 pound of refined and ground sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 1 quartillo of Maraschino of Zara

## Directions

1. Place on fire six quartillos of milk mixed with very sweet cream, with a pound of refined and ground sugar, and stir the mixture with a spatula until it has boiled twice
2. Pass it then through a horsehair sieve and pour it over six egg whites, beaten until snowy, continuing to stir until everything is incorporated
3. Add immediately a quartillo of Maraschino of Zara, and place the mixture in the sorbetera to freeze
