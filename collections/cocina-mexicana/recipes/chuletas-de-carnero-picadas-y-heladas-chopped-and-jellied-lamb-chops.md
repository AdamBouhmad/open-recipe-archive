## Ingredients

- Large lamb pieces
- ham
- lard
- gelatin
- broth
- buttered paper
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Cut large pieces and after larding them with ham, pass them through lard so they firm up
2. Let them drain and prepare them again with lard on the fire
3. Let them cool, trim and round them
4. Return them to the pan with a piece of gelatin and a little broth, wrapping them in buttered paper
5. When they are a good color and well jellied, dress them with fried croutons and serve with any sauce
