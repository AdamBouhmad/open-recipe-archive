## Ingredients

- Turkey wings
- Bacon pieces
- Herbs

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the turkey wings
2. After plucking well and removing quills, blanch lightly in boiling water
3. Cover with small pieces of bacon, passing them through
4. Cook like pipila in adobo, covering them with paper well soaked in lard
5. Once well cooked, place them in a sauce or herb stew
