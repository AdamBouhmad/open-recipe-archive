## Ingredients

- one pound of fine flour
- one tablespoon of hard butter
- sufficient water for kneading
- one pound of beef marrow
- chicken meat, stewed
- egg white and yolk for glazing

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of fine flour with one tablespoon of hard butter and sufficient water to make it workable
2. Cook the marrow to remove small bones and grind it with the kneading tool
3. Once clean, extend the dough and place the marrow on top, covering it by folding the dough
4. Work the dough by rolling it over the kneading tool, give it another fold and roll again, repeating this four or five times
5. Leave the dough at regular thickness and cut the pastry, placing strips of the same dough around the edge
6. Place paper in the middle to form a hollow, covering it with more dough to serve as a lid
7. Fill with stewed chicken meat or meat of your choice
8. Beat the white and yolk of one egg and coat the pasties on top with this mixture
9. Place in the oven
