## Ingredients

- flour
- catfish
- tomato
- garlic
- oil
- lard
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry the catfish in flour
2. Chop tomato and garlic and fry in oil and lard
3. Add clove, cinnamon and pepper
4. Once the broth is seasoned, add the fish slices and vinegar
5. Garnish with chiles and olives
