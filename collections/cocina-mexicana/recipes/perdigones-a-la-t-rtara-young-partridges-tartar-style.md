## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Warm butter
- Salt and pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Clean them and pass them through flames to burn the down
2. Tuck their legs, strike them as the previous ones and place them in a casserole with warm butter so that it covers them completely
3. Remove and sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Roll them in breadcrumbs so that they are well covered
5. A little more than half an hour before serving, place them on the grill over a very gentle fire
6. To bring to table, pour mustard sauce on a plate and arrange the young partridges on top
