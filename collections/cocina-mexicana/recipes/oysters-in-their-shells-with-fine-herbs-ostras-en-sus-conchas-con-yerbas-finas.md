## Ingredients

- oysters
- fine herbs, finely chopped
- mushrooms
- ham, grated
- butter
- oil
- powder of two peppers
- clove powder
- cinnamon powder
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Open and detach the oysters
2. Place them in a pan with their own water and heat without boiling
3. Add finely chopped fine herbs, mushrooms, grated ham, butter, a little oil, and powder of the two peppers, clove, and cinnamon
4. Put this mixture in the shells
5. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top
6. Place on the grill, covered with a camp oven
7. Serve when they have taken on a good color
