## Ingredients

- guayabas (guavas)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Remove only the heads, any spots, and the small seeds from the guavas
2. Grind them well, place in a pan with sugar, pound for pound, and put over heat stirring well and constantly so they do not stick
3. When they begin to reach the consistency of milk pudding, remove from heat, let cool, and with a wooden spoon pour into small boxes and glass vessels
