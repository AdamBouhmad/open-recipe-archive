## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- bay leaf
- thyme
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook and cut the tongue into more or less thick pieces
2. Cover with lard mixed with bay leaf and thyme, bread crumbs, pepper and salt
3. Cover with paper greased with lard and roast over moderate heat
