## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- salt
- wine
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- black pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- cloth

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the tongue well
2. Add sufficient salt and sprinkle with wine and vinegar
3. Fry four bay leaves and grind them with vinegar, fine pepper, and Tabasco pepper
4. Make the mixture as thick as possible and coat the tongue with it
5. Add more salt, wrap in cloth, and cook with sufficient vinegar
6. When done and cooled, unwrap and store for use
