## Ingredients

- six fresh egg whites
- half a pound of clarified syrup at the soft-ball stage, slightly warm
- lemon or orange zest
- sifted sugar for dusting
- nougat foam for filling

## Directions

1. Beat six fresh egg whites well.
2. Add them little by little to half a pound of clarified syrup at the soft-ball stage and slightly warm, with lemon or orange zest.
3. Stir everything constantly until the mixture reaches the proper consistency, which is known when a spoonful dropped onto itself stays on top without incorporating.
4. Distribute the meringues as desired on papers and dust with sifted sugar.
5. Place them in a bell-shaped oven with fire below and above, neither too strong nor too slow, and bake until they turn golden.
6. After baking, let them rest, then join each two meringues together, filling them with nougat foam.
