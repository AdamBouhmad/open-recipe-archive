## Ingredients

- venison breast and shoulder
- sauce base
- sugar
- water
- onions
- red wine
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- thyme

## Directions

1. Use only the breast and shoulder. Make a roux-based sauce as indicated on page 52, in which add a little powdered sugar.
2. Moisten with water and add the meat cut into pieces, finely chopped tender onions, a large glass of red wine, ham cut into small square pieces, salt, a pinch of pepper and thyme.
3. Cook everything and when serving remove the fat and serve. The darker the sauce, the more flavorful it will be.
