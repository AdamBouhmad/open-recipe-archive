## Ingredients

- lard
- xitomates
- onions
- clove
- cinnamon
- cumin
- desveined and toasted pasilla chiles
- cooked beans with a little water
- vinegar
- pork ribs
- longanizas
- chorizos
- oil
- olives
- raw chopped onion
- grated or aged cheese, ground

## Directions

1. Fry xitomates and onions in lard.
2. Grind clove, cinnamon, cumin, desveined and toasted pasilla chiles, and some cooked beans with a little water.
3. Fry this ground mixture, then add vinegar, fried pork ribs, longanizas, and chorizos.
4. Separately, fry fat beans with oil, olives, raw chopped onion, and a little grated or aged ground cheese.
5. Mix everything together with the fried ground mixture.
