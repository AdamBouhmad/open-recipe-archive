## Ingredients

- pig's head
- laurel
- thyme
- basil
- sage
- parsley
- spices
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon rind
- lemon juice
- jowl
- tongue in scarlet
- mushrooms
- pig's ears

## Directions

1. After completely deboning the pig's head, cut all the meat it contains into thin or thick strings, more or less long.
2. Separate the fat from the lean and cut the ears the same way.
3. Mix everything with laurel, thyme, basil, sage, and parsley, all chopped very fine; spices, salt, pepper, scraped nutmeg, the rind and juice of one lemon.
4. Spread the pig's head skin on a curing board, placing and introducing the strips of fat and lean meat, some jowl, some tongue in scarlet, mushrooms also cut into strips, and the strips of ears.
5. Wrap the skin and sew it very tightly.
6. Cook this cheese in a pot where it fits, drain it, remove it from the fire, and while still warm, put it in a mold of tin or tinplate to give it an agreeable shape.
