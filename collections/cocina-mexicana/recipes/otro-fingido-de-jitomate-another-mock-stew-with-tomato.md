## Ingredients

- onion
- thick tomato
- parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- oil
- broth
- thyme
- basil
- oregano
- powder of clove
- pepper
- meat from a stew or roast
- its own broth if it has any

## Directions

1. Fry onion, thick tomato, and finely chopped parsley; fry in butter and oil, add a little broth, thyme, basil, and oregano.
2. When it boils, add clove powder and pepper; when the boil is strong, add the meat which can be from a stew or roast, with a little of its own broth if it has any.
3. This is how the mock stew is cooked.
