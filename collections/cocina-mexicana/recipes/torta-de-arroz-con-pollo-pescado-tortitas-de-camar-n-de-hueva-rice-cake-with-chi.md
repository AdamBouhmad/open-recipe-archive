## Ingredients

- Rice
- Salt
- Beaten eggs
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Xitomates (tomatoes)
- Green chiles
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Clove
- Chicken
- Fish
- Shrimp cakes or fish roe cakes
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Caperberries
- Olives
- Cured pickled peppers
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with salt so that it remains whole, and after it is dry and cold, mix it with beaten eggs
2. In a cazuela lined with plenty of lard, put half of the rice thus prepared, and on top a layer of garlic, onions, xitomates and green chiles, all chopped very fine, fried and seasoned with salt, saffron, pepper and clove, all ground
3. On top place whole cooked chicken, or raw if small and tender, or portions of cooked fish, or cakes of shrimp or fish roe arranged as stated in previous articles
4. On top of the chicken, fish or cakes, place raisins, almonds, capers, caperberries, olives and quarters of cured pickled peppers, accommodating everything perfectly so that there are no gaps, and then add the remaining prepared rice, covering it with beaten eggs
5. Line with lard and put the cake to curdle at two gentle fires, serving when it is golden, with powdered sugar on top
