## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic, chopped
- green chiles, chopped
- fresh tomatoes, ground
- cooked nopal paddles
- cooked onions, separated into leaves
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- saffron
- bread fried in lard
- oregano, powdered or fresh and chopped
- vinegar
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic and green chiles in lard, then add ground fresh tomatoes.
2. Add the already cooked nopal paddles to this mixture and ensure they absorb the sauce well.
3. Add cooked onions separated into leaves, ground clove, pepper, cumin, saffron, bread fried in lard, and oregano.
4. Moisten everything with vinegar and oil, season with salt, and let simmer to achieve the desired consistency.
5. This dish can be varied by adding beaten or cut eggs, cooked shrimp, or cooked and sliced potatoes.
