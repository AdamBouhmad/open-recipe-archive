## Ingredients

- Mutton tongues
- Thin bacon
- Peber sauce
- Oil
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Blanch the tongues and lard them with thin bacon.
2. When half cooked, remove the skin.
3. Return to cook for another half hour on low heat.
4. Serve hot with a peber sauce, oil and parsley.
