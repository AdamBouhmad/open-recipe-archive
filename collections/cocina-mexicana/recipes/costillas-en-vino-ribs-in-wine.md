## Ingredients

- ribs
- tomato
- Jerez wine
- parsley
- ham
- lard
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the ribs with tomato, Jerez wine and chopped parsley
2. Parboil
3. Fry some sliced ham
4. Roast tomato and grind it
5. Put lard in a pan and add a handful of flour, then the tomato until well fried
6. Then add the ribs, ham and parsley to fry all together
7. Then add the wine to boil over low heat
8. Sprinkle with finely ground pepper and salt
