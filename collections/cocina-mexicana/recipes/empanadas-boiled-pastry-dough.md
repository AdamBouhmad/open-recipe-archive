## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Five raw eggs
- Necessary salt
- Two ounces powdered sugar
- A little hot water

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with five raw eggs, necessary salt, two ounces powdered sugar and a little hot water
2. Boil in water
3. After cooking, knead again and extend sheets with a rolling pin
4. Form the empanadas and fry them in lard
