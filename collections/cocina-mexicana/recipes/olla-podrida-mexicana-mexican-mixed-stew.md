## Ingredients

- water
- veal
- mutton
- pig's feet
- chicken
- meat of Toluca
- ham
- chorizo
- blood sausage
- chickpeas
- pork spine or meat
- garlic heads
- whole onions
- mint
- wide chile ground with cumin
- spices
- cilantro
- rice
- cabbage
- turnips
- green peas
- green beans
- broad beans
- vegetables
- Castilian squash
- sweet potato
- pears
- banana

## Directions

1. Once the water is boiling, add veal, mutton, pig's feet, chicken, meat of Toluca, ham, chorizo, blood sausage and chickpeas.
2. Once the pot is skimmed, add pork spine or meat, garlic heads according to the size of the pot, whole onions, mint, a wide chile ground with cumin, spices, cilantro, rice, and cover the pot as well as possible to boil until little broth remains.
3. Tie a cabbage with twine and add to cook along with some peeled turnips.
4. Once the cabbage is cooked, chop onion and garlic, fry them, and add the cabbage to this, adding green peas, green beans, broad beans and all kinds of vegetables.
5. Also add to the olla podrida Castilian squash, sweet potato, pears, banana, etc.
6. When the vegetables are being fried, add broth from the pot and a little pepper powder.
