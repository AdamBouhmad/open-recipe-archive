## Ingredients

- half chicken without fat
- quarter of a quart of water
- lettuce leaves
- chervil
- salt

## Directions

1. Place half a chicken without fat to boil in a quarter quart of water for one hour
2. Add some lettuce and chervil leaves
3. Season with salt
