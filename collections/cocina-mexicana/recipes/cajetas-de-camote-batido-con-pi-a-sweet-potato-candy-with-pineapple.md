## Ingredients

- sweet potato, cooked and peeled
- six pounds of sugar for every five pounds of sweet potato
- pineapple, ground (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook and peel the sweet potato, grind it and sift through a sieve
2. Make a syrup of less than half-point with six pounds of sugar for every five pounds of sweet potato
3. Mix together and allow to reach the wrinkle point on the spoon
4. When reaching that point, add ground pineapple if desired, though it is not necessary as the candies are very good with sweet potato alone
