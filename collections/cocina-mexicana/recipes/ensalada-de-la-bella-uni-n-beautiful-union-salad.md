## Ingredients

- Radishes
- Water
- Bergamot pear, cooked
- Raw grapes
- Pomegranate
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Calabash, cooked
- Peach slices
- Apple slices
- Candied citron
- Raisins
- Olives
- Pickled peppers

## Directions

1. Chop and cook radishes
2. Keep them in water for one hour
3. Remove and squeeze them
4. Place on a plate and add pieces of cooked bergamot pear, raw grapes, and pomegranate seeds
5. Add appropriate vinegar and oil
6. According to the season, also add cooked calabash, sliced peach and apple, cooked or raw
7. Decorate with candied citron, raisins, olives, and sliced pickled peppers
