## Ingredients

- Chickpeas, well cooked with onion
- Spinach, cooked separately
- Oil
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water
- A little clove

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas with onion
2. Cook spinach separately
3. Before adding the oil, add the cooked spinach to the cooked chickpeas
4. Add oil with garlic and pepper
5. Season with beaten eggs or bread soaked in water, clove and pepper
