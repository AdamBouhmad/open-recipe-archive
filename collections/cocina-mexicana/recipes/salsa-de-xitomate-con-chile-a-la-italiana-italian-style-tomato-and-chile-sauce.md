## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 onions, sliced
- thyme
- bay leaf
- 12 xitomates (small tomatoes)
- stock or a regular amount of butter
- salt
- 5 chiltepiquines, ground
- saffron powder

## Directions

1. Slice the onions in a saucepan, and add the thyme and bay leaf with the xitomates
2. Season with stock or butter, salt, ground chiltepiquines, and saffron powder
3. Heat everything, stirring from time to time, as this sauce is prone to stick
4. When it is somewhat thick, pass it through a cloth strainer like a substance
5. It should not remain too watery
