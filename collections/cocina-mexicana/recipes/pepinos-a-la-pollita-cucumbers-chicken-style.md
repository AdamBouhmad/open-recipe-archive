## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- butter
- flour
- cream
- broth
- egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry them in a pan with butter
2. Add a little flour
3. Moisten with cream and broth
4. Bind the liquid away from the heat with egg yolks
5. Add a few drops of vinegar before serving
