## Ingredients

- Salted cod
- Dried ancho chiles
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Oil
- Lard
- Bread
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Oregano
- Olives
- Chile strips

## Directions

1. From the evening before, soak the cod changing the waters, and also soak and devein dried ancho chiles.
2. The next day, grind the chiles with garlic, pepper and clove, frying in oil and lard, with more of the former, and add water with the raw cod, leaving it on the heat until it cooks well.
3. Thicken with soaked and ground bread, and when serving add oil, vinegar, cooked or raw chopped onion, oregano, olives and split chile strips on top.
