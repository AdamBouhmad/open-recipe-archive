## Ingredients

- Cooked mutton tongues braised
- Spanish sauce
- parsley
- onion
- garlic head
- mushrooms
- salt
- large pepper
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the tongues cooked braised into strips
2. In a fireproof plate, pour on the bottom a little Spanish sauce with parsley, onion, half a garlic head, mushrooms, all chopped very finely, salt and large pepper
3. Place the tongue strips on top, seasoning them underneath
4. Cover everything with bread crumbs, placing on top of it butter pieces the size of a chickpea
