## Ingredients

- two cucumbers, not perfectly ripe
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- one onion

## Directions

1. Clean two cucumbers that are not perfectly ripe; if they have a bitter tip, they are worthless and should be replaced with others; but if the tip is not bitter, which can be known by tasting them, cut them into wheels or slices along the length, as thin as possible
2. Place them in a serving dish with salt, pepper, vinegar, and a sliced onion
3. Let them pickle this way for two hours
4. Remove part of their liquid and serve with roast beef
