## Ingredients

- Twenty-five quartillos of pulque
- One pound of ground almonds
- Sugar (one and a half pounds)
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of ground almonds, one and a half pounds of sugar, and six egg yolks with twenty-five quartillos of pulque
2. Mix everything very well
