## Ingredients

- cabbage heads
- adobo sauce
- picadillo (minced meat mixture)

## Directions

1. Remove the crowns from the cabbage heads and replace them
2. Tie them so they do not fall apart and place them in a cazuela with water and salt that covers them well
3. Once cooked, place them to drain
4. Make the adobo
5. Open the cabbage heads leaf by leaf and coat with the adobo sauce
6. Add the picadillo, putting most of it in the center
7. Once well filled, gather the leaves and tie them
