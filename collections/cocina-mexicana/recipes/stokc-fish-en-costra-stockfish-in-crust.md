## Ingredients

- Stockfish, prepared and drained
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Three egg yolks
- Melted butter
- Bread crumbs
- Turnips or carrots, raw
- Butter for toasting

## Directions

1. Prepare the stockfish as indicated in the previous article but give the sauce a thicker consistency
2. Season the fish with salt, pepper and nutmeg and incorporate it into the sauce
3. Place this mixture on a plate, smooth it well on all sides with a knife, and shape it like a half orange
4. Beat three raw egg yolks with an equal volume of melted butter with a fork
5. With a brush, coat this mixture over the entire surface of the fish
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and remove any excess that falls on the plate edge
7. Make small supports with raw turnips or carrots and place them around the half orange to hold it up
8. Place in the oven for about half an hour until the crust is done
9. Remove the vegetable supports and replace them with slices of toast well browned in butter
10. Before cooking, drain off a bit of the melted butter so the crust acquires good color
