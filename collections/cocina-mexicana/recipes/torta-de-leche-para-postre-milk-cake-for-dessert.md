## Ingredients

- Milk, curdled like for cheese
- Egg yolks, enough to moisten the dough
- Sugar, in the appropriate amount
- A little cinnamon
- Butter, for coating the pan

## Directions

1. Curdle the milk as you would for cheese and press it
2. Pass it through the metate (grinding stone)
3. Add enough egg yolks to moisten the dough, with the corresponding sugar and a little cinnamon
4. Coat a skillet with butter
5. Pour the dough into the skillet and cook the cake over two fires
