## Ingredients

- peeled almond
- sugar syrup at high point
- orange blossom water
- tamal dough
- tamal leaves

## Directions

1. Make a paste like marzipan by boiling peeled almond, re-ground with sugar syrup at high point and a little orange blossom water
2. Wrap regular portions of this paste with tamal dough that will be prepared beforehand
3. Cover with leaves as if they were tamales and cook the same way as tamales
4. When done, remove the leaves and tamal dough and slice the paste
5. Pour onto a suitable plate over layers of mamón soaked in half-point sugar syrup, adorning the layers of milk with raisins and almonds
6. Give it a frosting on top and keep in the oven until it sets
