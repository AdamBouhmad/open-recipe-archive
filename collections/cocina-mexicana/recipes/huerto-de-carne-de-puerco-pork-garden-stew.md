## Ingredients

- lettuce
- salt
- carrots
- potatoes
- tomatoes, ripe
- garlic
- onions
- lard
- pork broth
- fine salt
- clove
- saffron
- pepper
- cumin
- vinegar
- oil
- pork meat
- pork loin
- pork ribs

## Directions

1. Trim and clean lettuce, cook in salted water
2. Clean and remove hearts from carrots, chop finely and cook with a good amount of potatoes
3. Chop ripe tomatoes, garlic, and onions finely and fry in lard
4. Remove lettuce, wash it, trim the stems and chop with the potatoes and carrots, then fry everything together
5. Moisten with pork broth and season with fine salt
6. Grind clove, saffron, pepper and cumin, add to the stew along with vinegar and oil
7. Add the cooked pork meat in loin slices and separated ribs
8. Let it season and when the broth is almost consumed, remove from heat and serve
