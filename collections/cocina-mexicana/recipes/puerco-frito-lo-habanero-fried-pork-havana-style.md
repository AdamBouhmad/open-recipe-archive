## Ingredients

- pork meat
- vinegar
- oregano
- salt
- ground pepper
- garlic
- lard
- green or ripe plantains
- white rice

## Directions

1. Prepare in a platter a marinade of vinegar, oregano, salt, a little ground pepper and some crushed garlic
2. Put the pork meat cut into pieces in this marinade
3. When it has well taken the taste of the marinade, remove the meat and fry it in lard
4. Serve with fried green or ripe plantains and white rice
