## Ingredients

- chard, spinach or endive
- raisins
- sugar and cinnamon in proportion
- one or two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cook any of these vegetables and after draining the water well, place in a casserole
2. Put on top raisins, sugar and cinnamon in proportion, and one or two beaten eggs
3. Place on low heat, covering with an iron lid with some coals on top
4. When you see that everything is bound together like an omelet, remove and serve
