## Ingredients

- 30 to 40 chestnuts
- butter
- consummate broth

## Directions

1. Remove the shell from 30 or 40 chestnuts and place them in a frying pan with a little butter, frying and tossing them over the fire until the second shell or thin skin covering them falls off by itself.
2. After cleaning them, cover them with consummate broth in a pot and let them cook for an hour and a half.
3. When well cooked, pass them through a sieve, being careful that the substance remains somewhat thick, and if it is too thick, thin it with a little cream.
4. At the moment of serving, add a little butter.
