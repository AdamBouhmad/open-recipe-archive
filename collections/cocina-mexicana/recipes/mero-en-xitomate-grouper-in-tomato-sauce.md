## Ingredients

- Grouper fish, salted or in brine
- Water
- Flour
- Equal parts oil and lard
- Garlic cloves
- Roasted tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Small chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Desalt the grouper if necessary, clean it and cook it with water
2. Divide into portions and dredge in flour
3. Put equal quantities of oil and lard in a pan and fry split garlic cloves until golden
4. Remove the garlic and fry the floured grouper portions in the fat until fried
5. Remove the grouper from the frying fat
6. Fry chopped onions in the same fat, then add roasted and ground tomatoes with the fried garlic, clove, pepper and cumin
7. Season with necessary salt
8. Put the fried grouper portions with the water in which it was cooked and a little vinegar into the sauce
9. Let boil until the sauce takes on regular consistency and is well seasoned
10. When removing from heat, add small chiles and olives
