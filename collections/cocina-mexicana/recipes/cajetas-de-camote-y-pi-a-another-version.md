## Ingredients

- one quarter arroba of sugar
- six pounds of cooked, peeled, and ground sweet potato without fibers
- one pineapple, ground without heart or skin

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with one quarter arroba of sugar
2. Add six pounds of cooked, peeled, and ground sweet potato without fibers and one pineapple, ground without heart or skin
3. Boil everything, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon so it does not stick to the pan, until when taken on a spoon and lifted, it does not fall when turned over
4. When it cools, fill the candy molds
