## Ingredients

- One pound of almonds
- Orange blossom water
- Eleven egg whites
- One pound of ground sugar
- Puff pastry dough
- Butter
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Ground cinnamon
- Dragée
- Pine nuts
- Walnuts
- Hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Weigh one pound of almonds and grind them so they remain grainy
2. Soak in orange blossom water
3. Mix with eleven well-beaten egg whites
4. Add one pound of ground sugar and mix well
5. Prepare and beat a small amount of puff pastry dough
6. Place one sheet of this dough in a casserole greased with butter
7. Pour the almond paste on top
8. Cover with another thin sheet of dough
9. Sprinkle with butter
10. Place the cake to cook between two fires
11. While cooking, add raisins, almonds, ground cinnamon, dragée, pine nuts, walnuts, and hazelnuts
