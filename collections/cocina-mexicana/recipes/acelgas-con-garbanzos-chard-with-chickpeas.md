## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- garlic
- salt
- chard cooking liquid
- onion
- bread
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Put the chickpeas in a pot with garlic and salt, and the green broth that the chard left when cooked, which should have been done separately
2. When the chickpeas are well cooked, mix everything together
3. Season with fried onion, peeled garlic cloves, a little soaked bread, and an egg yolk
4. Once they have thickened well, being careful that they do not boil too much so they do not burn, serve them on toasted bread or over any other dish
