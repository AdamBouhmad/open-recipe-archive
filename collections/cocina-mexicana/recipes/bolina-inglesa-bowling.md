## Ingredients

- One cold loaf of bread
- Milk
- Twelve beaten eggs
- Twelve ounces of melted butter
- One pound of well-ground sugar
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Citron
- Butter for coating cloth

## Directions

1. Soak one loaf of cold bread in milk, then remove when well soaked and break it apart
2. Mix with twelve beaten eggs and twelve ounces of melted butter as if for frying
3. Add to everything one pound of well-ground sugar, half an ounce of cinnamon, raisins, almonds and citron
4. Spread a cloth or napkin coated with hard butter, place the said dough on it, and joining the four corners or points, tie it and place in a pot with boiling water so it cooks
5. As soon as the dough becomes hard like tamale, remove it by cutting into slices, which will be covered when serving with ground sugar and cinnamon
