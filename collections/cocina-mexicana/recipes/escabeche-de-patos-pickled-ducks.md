## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Straw
- Flour
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Aromatic herbs
- Fried garlic
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooked with straw and tails removed, clean and dry with a cloth
2. Debone and roll in flour, fry in lard
3. Remove and place in layers inside a pot
4. Between two layers of duck put a layer of salt and pepper, aromatic herbs in powder, fried garlic, oil and vinegar
5. Cover the pot and leave in infusion for three or four hours
