## Ingredients

- loin or veal chuck
- water
- salt
- oil
- abundant lard
- garlic, chopped
- onions, thinly sliced
- tomato, finely chopped and in abundance
- pepper
- cinnamon
- clove
- hot water or broth from the pot

## Directions

1. Prepare a loin or veal chuck one day ahead, placing it to cook with water and salt.
2. Heat oil and abundant lard in a casserole, and fry plenty of chopped garlic in it until golden, then remove.
3. In the same casserole, fry many thinly sliced onions, cut into small rounds, allowing them to brown as well.
4. Add finely chopped tomato in abundance; before it finishes frying, season with salt, pepper, cinnamon, and clove in proportional quantities.
5. When the tomato juice has been consumed, add a little hot water or broth from the pot.
6. Place the meat in large slices or whole loins in the sauce, and allow to simmer gently over low heat.
