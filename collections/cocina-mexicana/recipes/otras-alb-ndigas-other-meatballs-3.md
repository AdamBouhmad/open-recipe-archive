## Ingredients

- loin and solid meat
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- aged cheese
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mince the loin and solid meat; put it in a bowl with two or three hard-boiled eggs with salt, clove, cinnamon and ground pepper, a little aged cheese grated, minced onion and parsley, mix well
