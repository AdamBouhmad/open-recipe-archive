## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges, between green and yellow in color
- Water
- Salt
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Select oranges between green and yellow, neither too green nor too ripe
2. Remove very thinly the colored outer skin, leaving the whitish flesh without the cells of essence, placing them in cold water as they are peeled
3. Divide them into two equal halves so that the segments are also split in half and squeeze them so no juice remains
4. Place them in boiling salted water, leaving them to cook in it
5. When cooked, remove and let cool in the same water they were cooked in
6. Change to other water with less salt and leave in the sun and open air, taking care to change them repeatedly to lightly salted water until they lose their saltiness
