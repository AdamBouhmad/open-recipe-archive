## Ingredients

- 2 quarters milk
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 pound cooked, ground and strained apple
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites, beaten
- Cinnamon, ground
- Butter
- Sliced biscuits
- Milk
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Butter pieces
- Candied citron pieces
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Cook 2 quarters of milk with a cinnamon stick and 2 pounds of sugar
2. When thick, let it cool and mix with 1 pound of cooked, ground and strained apple, 12 egg yolks and 6 beaten egg whites and a little ground cinnamon
3. Butter a platter
4. First place a little of the said paste
5. On top place sliced biscuits soaked in milk and squeezed
6. Then put more paste decorated with raisins, almonds, pieces of butter, candied citron and toasted sesame seeds
7. There should be three layers of biscuit
8. When the platter is full, bake in the oven at two fires with very gentle heat, being careful so the sugar does not burn
9. After the ante is cooked, decorate on top with almonds, raisins and grajea
