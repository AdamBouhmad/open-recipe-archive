## Ingredients

- oats
- milk
- cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the oats by removing the grain, cutting off both extremities and crushing
2. Cook with cow milk instead of goat or almond milk
3. Give it the consistency of cream
4. Add cloves for better flavor
