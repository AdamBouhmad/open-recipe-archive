## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs
- Fine salt
- Custard
- Cinnamon (fine, powdered)

## Directions

1. After melting butter in a skillet, add eggs seasoned with fine salt
2. Stir them much
3. When they are well stirred, add a glass of custard, which should also be stirred
4. Sprinkle with a little fine cinnamon, and if desired, a little juice can be added when serving
