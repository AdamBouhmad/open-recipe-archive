## Ingredients

- one pound of beef with skin and muscular fat removed
- two quartillos of water
- salt

## Directions

1. Mince the beef meat as if making morcillas
2. Add salt to the water and put the meat in it
3. Place over a slow fire so it maintains for two hours at a temperature near boiling but without actually boiling
4. After two hours, skim off the foam that has risen to the surface, stirring occasionally without uncovering
5. After skimming well, return to the fire, increase the heat somewhat and let it boil for about a quarter hour
6. Remove the pot from the fire and pass the broth through a very fine sieve
7. When the extract has cooled sufficiently, remove all the fat accumulated on the surface
8. This is the best meat extract for a convalescent
