## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, first beating the whites, and when these have thickened well, mix them with the yolks
2. Heat butter and pour in the egg mixture, allowing it to fry in the butter
3. The tortilla can be made with this substance alone and seasoned with salt, or mixed with onion, strips of green chile, small pieces of fried ham, longaniza sausage, chorizo, or any other substance that suits, and serve with chile macho sauce or fried tomato sauce
