## Ingredients

- truffles, cut into small strips
- lard, cut into small strips
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- parsley
- small onions
- garlic cloves
- paper

## Directions

1. Cut truffles and lard into small strips and roll them in salt, pepper, clove and cinnamon, all in powder
2. Mix with finely chopped parsley, small onions and garlic cloves
3. Lard the leg thoroughly with this mixture
4. Wrap in paper so that air cannot reach it
5. Leave thus for two days
6. After this time, place in a pan with its own juices, covered with slices of lard and ham
7. Cook until done
8. When cooked, degrease the sauce and add a spoonful of strained broth or substance
