## Ingredients

- pears, peeled
- cinnamon sticks
- cloves
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Arrange the peeled and larded pears (larded with cinnamon sticks and cloves) in a pan or pot with an almost flat bottom
2. Pour clarified syrup over them
3. Leave to simmer over gentle heat to the point of conserve, taking care that they do not break apart when turning
