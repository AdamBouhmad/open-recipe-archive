## Ingredients

- mutton leg
- ham
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- salt
- strong vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- Malaga wine

## Directions

1. Lard the leg of mutton with sufficient ham, clove, pepper and cinnamon
2. Place in a vessel covered with sufficient water
3. Add the necessary salt and a bottle of strong vinegar with sufficient bay leaves and thyme
4. Cook over two gentle fires
5. When it lacks little to be cooked, add two quartillos of Malaga wine
6. They keep without spoiling for a long time and can be eaten cold
