## Ingredients

- pork fat
- kidney fat
- thyme, bay leaf, and peppercorns (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the pork fats into small pieces, as well as the kidney fats.
2. Melt them over gentle heat in an iron or copper vessel, stirring constantly until some part has melted.
3. If desired, add thyme, bay leaf, and peppercorns in branches, but not salt, as it would impede the mixture.
4. Increase the heat and when the fat does not emit smoke, it is a sign that it is cooked.
5. Then strain it through a fine sieve, filling the jars, which should be of clay, to the brim.
6. Cover with two or three sheets of thick paper in a cool place.
