## Ingredients

- onions, minced
- tomatoes, minced
- herbabuena
- parsley
- capers
- pork meat, minced and ground
- salt
- ground saffron
- clove
- pepper
- chorizo meat, crumbled
- aged cheese, grated
- ham
- hard-boiled egg
- lard
- garlic, minced
- poblano or tornachile chiles, shredded
- onion slices
- roasted and ground tomato
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- fried and ground bread
- olives
- cured chiles

## Directions

1. Mince finely onions, tomatoes, herbabuena, parsley and capers
2. Mix with the minced and ground meat
3. Add salt, ground saffron, clove and pepper
4. Add crumbled chorizo meat and grated aged cheese
5. Mix well and form meatballs
6. Place ham and hard-boiled egg in the center of each
7. For the broth, fry minced garlic in lard, add shredded poblano or tornachile chiles and onion slices
8. Add roasted and ground tomato
9. Once the mixture begins to brown, season with salt
10. Add water, capers and the same spices used in the meat, vinegar and a small amount of oil
11. When boiling, add the meatballs
12. Thicken the broth with fried and ground bread
13. When serving, garnish with olives and cured chiles
