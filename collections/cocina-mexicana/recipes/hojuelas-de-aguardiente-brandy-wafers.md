## Ingredients

- Four ounces of finely milled flour
- One cup of water of settled tequesquite
- Four egg whites
- Six egg yolks
- One small cup of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix four ounces of finely milled flour with one cup of water of settled tequesquite
2. Beat four egg whites until they rise
3. Add six egg yolks
4. Mix everything with the flour
5. Add one small cup of brandy
6. Knead and work the dough well until it reaches point
7. Extend it with the roller
8. Cut the hojuelas
9. Fry them as all others
