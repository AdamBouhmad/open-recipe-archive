## Ingredients

- twelve mutton kidneys
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- mayordomo sauce, cold
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare twelve mutton kidneys by wetting them and opening them slightly on the side opposite the nerve
2. Remove the skins covering them and finish opening them without separating the parts
3. Thread them through, four at a time, on a wooden skewer or in its place a thick popote, so they cannot join or group together
4. Dip them in melted butter, cover with breadcrumbs, and place on the grill, taking care to turn them appropriately
5. When cooked, remove them from the skewers and arrange on a plate
6. Place on each one about half a walnut of cold mayordomo sauce
7. Heat the plate and squeeze lemon juice over it
