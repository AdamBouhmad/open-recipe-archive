## Ingredients

- One cuartillo of good lentils
- Beef broth
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Two cloves (spice)
- Bread crusts
- Butter

## Directions

1. Put lentils to cook in broth with two carrots, two onions, and two cloves
2. When cooked, pass through a sieve to make a clear substance
3. Bring this substance to a boil to degrease it
4. Pour over bread crusts fried in butter before serving the soup, so they have time to soak
