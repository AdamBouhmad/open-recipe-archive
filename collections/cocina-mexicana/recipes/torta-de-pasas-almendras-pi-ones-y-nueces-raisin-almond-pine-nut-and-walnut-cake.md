## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar, clarified
- toasted sesame
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- mamón, ground
- butter

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar, bring to the proper point and add to the syrup toasted sesame, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, walnuts and ground mamón
2. Pour everything into a cazuela coated with butter
3. Cook over two fires
