## Ingredients

- Cleaned walnuts
- A little wide red chile, deveined and soaked
- Cumin
- Cold bread
- Oil

## Directions

1. Grind cleaned walnuts with a little wide red chile that has been deveined and soaked, cumin, and cold bread
2. Fry everything
3. When it has thickened well, use this sauce to cover the peppers
4. Add oil on top
