## Ingredients

- sour texocotes
- water
- sugar
- virgin honey
- lemon
- rose juice

## Directions

1. Remove the crown and tails, wash them and put them to cook in water; as soon as they are cooked, peel them and remove the pits, then place in clean water, in which they will be kept four days, taking care to stir them with a wooden spoon daily.
2. At the end of this time and with the jelly being thick, strain through a napkin; mix very well and squeeze, grind in a metate and extract the juice in a washed cotton.
3. Make clarified syrup with a pound and a half of sugar, add a small amount of virgin honey, clarify again with a few drops of lemon, strain, add a cuartillo of rose juice and put on the fire so it takes a high point, taking care to remove the foam and to use the juice the same day it is extracted, because it spoils from one day to another.
