## Ingredients

- one pound of clarified sugar
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks as for torrejas
2. When the syrup reaches high point, remove the pan until it cools
3. Add the yolks, stir and place over gentle heat to give a few boils while stirring
4. Remove from heat and beat with a spoon
5. Pour into bowls and sprinkle cinnamon powder on top
6. The syrup should be boiled with cinnamon sticks
