## Ingredients

- crabs
- broth
- rice
- crab meat
- crab shells
- bread crust
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the crabs and put to cook in broth, dry them and remove the meat
2. Cook rice in broth, removing it without being very cooked and with broth still remaining
3. Take equal quantities of rice and crab meat, grind together and strain, loosening the ground mixture with the broth of the crabs but without making it too liquid
4. Pour over slices of bread crust that will have been soaked in good broth
5. At the moment of serving add butter, prepared with the shells of the crabs
