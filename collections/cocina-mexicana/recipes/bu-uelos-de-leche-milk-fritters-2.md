## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one cup of milk
- eight eggs (only two with whites)
- one cup of melted butter
- a little ground anise
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, milk, eggs, melted butter, anise and salt together
2. Fry the fritters
3. Cover with syrup on top
