## Ingredients

- artichokes
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut into four or six parts
2. Remove the hearts
3. Pass through butter in a saucepan, sprinkling with ground salt
4. Cook with heat above and below until done and colored
5. Place head down and cover with butter on top so they appear very white
