## Ingredients

- Fish, cleaned
- Lard for frying
- Lime slices
- Lemon slices
- Water
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and fry in lard until it browns, then set aside and let cool.
2. Cook lime and lemon slices in water.
3. Layer the fried fish in a barrel with a little of the cooled cooking water from the limes, add some clove, cinnamon, and pepper, some lime and lemon slices, and bay leaves.
4. Continue layering these ingredients, adding some of the lime water to each layer.
5. Cover the fish well with vinegar and seal very well.
6. The fish will not be good until it is ready, which takes a long time; when you begin to eat it by opening the barrel, it is necessary to use it immediately until finished, because it spoils quickly.
