## Ingredients

- salt cod
- water
- milk (two cups per half pound of salt cod)
- oil (half a cup per half pound of salt cod)
- butter the size of a pigeon's egg

## Directions

1. Place the salt cod in a casserole with water and cover it
2. Once cooked, cut into small slices
3. Measure two cups of milk for each half pound of salt cod, half a cup of oil, and a ball of butter the size of a pigeon's egg
4. Heat the butter until it browns
5. Add the salt cod to poach without frying
6. Gradually add milk and a few drops of oil, stirring until it thickens
7. Add milk and oil again until finished, making sure it ends up as thick as a dessert
8. Season and garnish with egg yolks
