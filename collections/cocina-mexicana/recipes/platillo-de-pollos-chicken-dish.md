## Ingredients

- three chickens, quartered
- one and a half quartillo of wine
- one and a half quartillo of vinegar
- two quartillos of water
- slices of ham
- clove and cinnamon, ground
- toasted almonds
- a little pepper and ginger
- salt to taste
- one tablespoon of lard
- tornachilos
- olives
- capers
- caper berries

## Directions

1. Cook the quartered chickens in one and a half quartillo of wine, the same amount of vinegar, and two quartillos of water.
2. Add slices of ham, clove and cinnamon ground together with toasted almonds and a little pepper and ginger.
3. Season with sufficient salt and add one tablespoon of lard.
4. Cover the pot as for a stew and cook over gentle heat.
5. When the chickens are very tender, uncover the pot and add tornachilos, olives, capers, and caper berries.
