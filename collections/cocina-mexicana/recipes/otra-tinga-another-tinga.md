## Ingredients

- carne de puerco (pork)
- longaniza (sausage)
- lechuga (lettuce)
- rábanos (radishes)
- aguacate (avocado)
- queso anejo (aged cheese)

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the previous tinga, with the difference that the meat must be well-fried pork with pieces of longaniza
2. Garnish with lettuce, well-arranged radishes, avocado, and aged cheese on top
