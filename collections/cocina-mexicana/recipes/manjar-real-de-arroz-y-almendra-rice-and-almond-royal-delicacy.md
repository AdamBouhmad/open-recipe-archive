## Ingredients

- one pound of soaked and well-ground rice
- eight quartillos of milk
- sugar as needed
- twenty-four egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- two cloves
- four ounces of clean ground almonds
- white wine

## Directions

1. Add the soaked and well-ground rice to eight quartillos of milk with the necessary sugar
2. Strain everything through a sieve
3. Add twenty-four egg yolks, some cinnamon sticks, and two cloves
4. Put over the fire without stopping stirring, and after a short time add four ounces of clean ground almonds
5. When it detaches from the pan while stirring, remove from the fire
6. Add a little white wine and empty into a platter
