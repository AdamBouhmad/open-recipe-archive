## Ingredients

- Milk
- Three egg yolks per cup of milk
- Marquesote (sponge cake), sliced thick
- Syrup at the proper consistency
- Cinnamon
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. For each cup of milk, place three egg yolks in each small pot
2. Cook over two fires, and when it reaches more than medium firmness, remove it from heat
3. Slice marquesote into thick pieces and place them in a platter in alternating layers with the jericalla until full
4. Put it in the oven to cook
5. After cooking, divide into pieces and pour syrup at the proper consistency over it in a platter
6. Decorate with cinnamon, raisins, and almonds
