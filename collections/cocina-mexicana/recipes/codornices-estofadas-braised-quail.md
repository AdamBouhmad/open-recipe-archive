## Ingredients

- quail
- burned lard
- sliced sausage
- salt
- onion
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- parsley
- raisins
- almonds
- Malaga wine
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the quail and parboil them in a pot with burned lard, sliced sausage and necessary salt.
2. Add chopped onion to brown, and once browned, add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper, parsley, raisins and chopped almonds and Malaga wine instead of water.
3. Cover the pot well, sealing the joints, until the quail are cooked.
4. Thicken the broth with flour browned in butter and pour over the quail to bring to the table.
