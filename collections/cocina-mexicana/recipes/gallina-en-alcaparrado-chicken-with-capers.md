## Ingredients

- Chicken
- A handful of capers
- Garlic
- A little fried bread
- A little onion
- Chicken broth
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Vinegar
- Raw oil

## Directions

1. Soak the capers, then grind them with garlic and a little fried bread
2. Put a casserole on the fire and fry a little finely chopped onion
3. Then fry the ground capers with the garlic and fried bread
4. Add a little of the chicken broth, ground pepper and cinnamon, and a little vinegar
5. Let it season, and when the broth has thickened a little, add raw oil on top
