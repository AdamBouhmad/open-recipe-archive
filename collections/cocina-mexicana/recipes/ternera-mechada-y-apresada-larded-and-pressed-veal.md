## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- Ham
- Tabasco pepper
- Fine pepper
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Water
- Ginger
- Oregano in branch
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Bay leaf
- Pepper
- Clove

## Directions

1. Skin the veal pulp and after beating it well, lard with ham, Tabasco pepper, fine pepper, and clove
2. Press in the press and leave for twenty-four hours
3. The following day, place to cook in vinegar mixed with water, adding ginger, oregano in branch, and a little thyme, marjoram, bay leaf, pepper and ground clove all together
4. Place the pot on the fire, cover hermetically so the steam does not escape
5. As soon as the meat is considered cooked, uncover, remove the veal and press again
6. After cooling, use as desired
