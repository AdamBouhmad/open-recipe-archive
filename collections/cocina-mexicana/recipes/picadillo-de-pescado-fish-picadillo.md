## Ingredients

- Fish of any kind, fresh or freshwater, with bones removed
- Parsley
- Onion
- Mushrooms (optional)
- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Bread crumbs soaked in milk
- Salt and seasoning

## Directions

1. Remove bones from fish of any kind
2. Make a picadillo with its meat that is mixed with parsley, onion, mushrooms if desired, butter, egg yolks and bread crumbs soaked in milk
3. Well seasoned, cook appropriately to eat as is, or in meatballs, in a cake, or as stuffing for other fish, cabbages or squabs
