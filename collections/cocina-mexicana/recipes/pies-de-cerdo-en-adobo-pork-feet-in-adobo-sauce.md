## Ingredients

- Cooked pork feet
- Wide chile peppers, deveined and well washed
- Garlic head, cleaned
- Cumin seeds
- Lard
- Salt
- Broth from cooking the feet
- Water
- Onions
- Oregano

## Directions

1. After cooking, remove the largest bones from the feet.
2. Fry them as in the previous recipes.
3. Grind the deveined chiles, cleaned garlic head, and cumin seeds well.
4. Fry everything, then add a little of the broth in which the feet were cooked and some water.
5. Season with necessary salt.
6. Add onions in quarters and sprinkle with oregano.
7. Boil everything together with the fried feet until seasoned.
