## Ingredients

- ham
- water for boiling
- oil for frying
- bread
- tomato sauce or eggs

## Directions

1. Before frying, give the ham a boil to desalt it
2. Cut into large or small thin slices
3. Fry so they do not harden or toast too much
4. Serve with slices of bread fried in the same fat, with tomato sauce or with fried eggs
