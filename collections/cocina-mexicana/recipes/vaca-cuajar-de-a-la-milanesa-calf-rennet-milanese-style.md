## Ingredients

- calf rennet
- bread croutons
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the rennet the same as in the previous article, cut it into strips and arrange it in layers on a deep plate, placing successively one of bread croutons, another of rennet and another of grated cheese
2. Let everything form a crust over gentle heat and serve
