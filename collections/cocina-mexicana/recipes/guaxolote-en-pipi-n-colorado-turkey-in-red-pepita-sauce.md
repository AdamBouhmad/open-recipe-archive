## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of ancho chile
- half pound of pasilla chile
- lard
- three or four handfuls of pumpkin seeds with shell
- water or broth
- cooked turkey in portions
- pork ribs, cooked, optional
- a little bit of clove
- toasted cilantro
- cinnamon
- salt
- caldo or hot water

## Directions

1. Devein one and a half pounds of ancho chile and half pound of pasilla chile
2. Toast them in lard so they do not burn
3. Toast three or four handfuls of pumpkin seeds with shell on a comal and grind them well
4. Form a ball like chocolate and put it to cook in boiling water
5. Meanwhile grind the fried chile, fry it in lard after grinding
6. Dissolve the pumpkin seed ball in a little broth or hot water and fry it with the chile
7. Add the necessary water or broth of the cooked turkey and the turkey itself in portions
8. If desired, add cooked pork ribs
9. Grind a little bit of clove, toasted cilantro and cinnamon, add it and let it season
10. When it has thickened well, season with salt because if added before it will break
