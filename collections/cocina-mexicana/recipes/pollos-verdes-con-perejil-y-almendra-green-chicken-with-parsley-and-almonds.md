## Ingredients

- Almonds
- Parsley
- Clove
- Pepper
- Lard
- Salt
- Chicken with its broth
- Cooked chorizo
- Cooked ham
- Oil
- Capers
- Olives
- Small chiles

## Directions

1. Grind almonds with plenty of parsley, clove, and pepper
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard with salt
3. Place the chicken with its broth in the pan
4. Add cooked chorizo, cooked ham, oil, and capers
5. Allow to simmer and season the broth
6. Remove from heat and add olives and small chiles
