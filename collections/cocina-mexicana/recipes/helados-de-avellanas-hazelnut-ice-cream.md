## Ingredients

- one pound of hazelnuts
- half a pound of sugar
- nine fresh egg yolks
- four cuartillos of custard

## Directions

1. Candy one pound of hazelnuts with half a pound of sugar
2. Roast them
3. After allowing them to cool, grind them to reduce to powder
4. Dissolve in a pan with nine fresh egg yolks and four cuartillos of custard
5. Allow to cook over very gentle heat
6. Pass the mixture through muslin to freeze it
