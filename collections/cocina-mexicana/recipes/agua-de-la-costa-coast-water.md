## Ingredients

- Cinnamon powder, not too fine
- Citron peels
- Dates
- Figs
- Aguardiente
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Put in infusion at atmospheric temperature for six to eight days: two ounces of cinnamon powder not too fine, the peels of two citrons, two ounces of dates and two ounces of figs in twelve quarts of aguardiente
2. Distill afterwards to obtain a little more than half the liquor
3. Mix with thirty-six ounces of sugar dissolved in six quarts of water
4. Filter everything and store
