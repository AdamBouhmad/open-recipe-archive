## Ingredients

- Chile pasilla
- Chile ancho
- Seeds from the chiles
- Peanuts
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Water
- Salt
- Cooked meat

## Directions

1. Toast, grind, and fry equal proportions of chile pasilla and chile ancho
2. Toast the seeds from the same chiles and grind them with peanuts, cloves, and cinnamon
3. Fry this mixture and dissolve the chile in it
4. Add hot water and salt
5. Add cooked meat and let it boil
6. Measure the water so it does not become watery
