## Ingredients

- Lettuces, tender parts only
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Ham slices
- Beef slices
- Two carrots, sliced
- Two onions
- Two cloves
- One bay leaf
- Broth
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Parboil the lettuces in salted water, keeping only the most tender parts and washing them well.
2. Boil for twenty minutes, then cool in cold water.
3. Squeeze out the water and place a little salt and coarse pepper in the heart of each lettuce.
4. After tying them, place them in a pot layered with ham slices, some beef slices, two sliced carrots, two onions, two cloves, and one bay leaf.
5. Cover the lettuces with more ham slices, add broth with a little of the pot liquid, and let them cook slowly for one hour.
6. When cooked, dry and press with a clean cloth to extract the broth.
7. Arrange on a plate in the form of a crown with toasted bread slices of similar size, pouring Spanish sauce underneath.
