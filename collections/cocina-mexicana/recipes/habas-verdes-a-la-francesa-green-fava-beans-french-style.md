## Ingredients

- Green fava beans
- Water
- Tequesquite
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat broth or water
- Parsley
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Cook the green fava beans in a little settled tequesquite water and some common water
2. After cooking, refresh them in cold water
3. Place a cazuela on the heat with butter seasoned with sufficient salt and pepper
4. Add the cooked fava beans and a little meat broth or water
5. Add a small bundle of parsley and another of oregano, seasoning with fine salt
6. Leave to boil and when they have thickened sufficiently, thicken with beaten egg yolks
