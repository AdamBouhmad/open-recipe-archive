## Ingredients

- cooked poultry livers
- mulato or pasilla chiles, deveined
- cumin
- vinegar
- lard
- oil
- oregano powder

## Directions

1. Grind cooked poultry livers with deveined mulato or pasilla chiles and a few cumin seeds
2. Thin the paste with vinegar
3. Fry well in lard
4. Place over roasted poultry with raw oil
5. Sprinkle with oregano powder on top
6. Can also be served separately in a sauce boat
