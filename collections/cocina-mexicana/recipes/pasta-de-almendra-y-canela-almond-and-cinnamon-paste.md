## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of sugar
- half a pound of soaked and ground almond
- half an ounce of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the preserve point with a pound and a half of sugar
2. Off the fire, mix in half a pound of soaked and ground almond
3. Return to the fire and stir constantly until the bottom of the pan is visible
4. Set aside and add half an ounce of ground cinnamon
5. Pour into small dishes when well incorporated and before it cools
6. If to be served in perforated papers, form the paste into balls, place in the papers, and glue them with gum
