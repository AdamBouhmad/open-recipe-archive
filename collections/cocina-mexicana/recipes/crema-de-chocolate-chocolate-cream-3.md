## Ingredients

- Chocolate
- Water
- Sugar
- Cream or milk
- Egg yolks
- Salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve chocolate in water in the proportion of one glass per pound
2. Add a little sugar and mix with cream, milk, and egg yolks in the proportion of six yolks per each unit of cream or milk
3. Add a grain of salt
4. Strain as the previous recipe and thicken over a bain-marie, taking care that it does not boil
