## Ingredients

- pineapples
- salt water
- one pound of sugar
- a little cinnamon
- desired seasonings
- eleven eggs, beaten
- butter
- one egg, beaten with seasonings

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapples and leave them whole overnight in salt water to remove harshness
2. Wash them well and cook them after removing the cores
3. Grind and mix with one pound of sugar and a little cinnamon, both ground, and desired seasonings
4. Mix the paste with eleven beaten eggs and pour into a pan or cazuela with hot butter
5. Cover with a tile with coals on top so the cake sets
6. Cover the top with another beaten egg mixed with seasonings
