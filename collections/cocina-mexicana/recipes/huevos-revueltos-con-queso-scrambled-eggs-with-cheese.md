## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Flanders cheese or other good cheese (grated)
- Butter
- Pepper (finely ground)

## Directions

1. Weigh the quantity of eggs needed and beat them in a pan
2. Add one-third the weight of the eggs in grated Flanders cheese or other good cheese, and one-sixth the weight of the eggs in butter
3. Put everything on high heat and stir continuously with a wooden spoon until the mixture has thickened well
4. Remove from the fire, mix in finely ground pepper, and serve
