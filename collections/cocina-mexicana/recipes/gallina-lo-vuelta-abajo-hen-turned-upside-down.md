## Ingredients

- one hen
- four ounces oil
- salt
- garlic cloves
- parsley leaves
- two chopped onions
- fine herbs
- fried plantains
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the hen into not very large pieces
2. Put in a casserole with four ounces of oil and season with salt
3. Fry until it takes a golden color, then add garlic cloves split in half, some parsley leaves, two chopped onions and some fine herbs
4. When the hen is fried, dry it and place on a plate with fried plantains
5. Make Italian sauce and pour it little by little over the hen
6. Add the oil that remained in the frying pan, decorate the plate with fried plantain cut in pieces and serve
