## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- sugar
- butter
- saffron
- salt
- eggs
- minced meat, cooked
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- capers
- fine spices
- recaudo

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with a little water, a piece of sugar, a spoonful of butter, saffron and salt
2. Let cool and grease a cake pan with butter
3. Beat eggs and mix with the rice, which should not be too soft
4. Layer rice in the pan, then the cooked minced meat with raisins, almonds, olives, capers, fine spices and recaudo
5. Top with another layer of rice
6. Pour beaten eggs on top, more than what is inside
7. Place over two fires until fully cooked
8. Dust with powdered sugar before serving
