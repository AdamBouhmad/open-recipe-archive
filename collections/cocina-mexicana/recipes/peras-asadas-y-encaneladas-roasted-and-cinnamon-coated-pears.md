## Ingredients

- large bergamota pears in good season
- clarified sugar syrup
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Select the largest bergamota pears in good season and roast them in the oven
2. Peel them and place them in clarified sugar syrup
3. Let them boil for eight or ten hours over low heat
4. Repeat this operation for four consecutive days
5. On the fifth day, when they are well penetrated with syrup, remove them and place them on hurdles or branches in the sun to dry
6. When completely dry, roll them in cinnamon powder
7. Wrap each one in paper and store them
