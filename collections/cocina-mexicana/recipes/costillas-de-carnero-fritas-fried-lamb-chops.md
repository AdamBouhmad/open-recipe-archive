## Ingredients

- lamb chops, from the fourth rib near the neck to the last
- butter
- Spanish sauce
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut the chops into pieces three fingers thick from the fourth rib near the neck to the last
2. Prepare by removing the skin and bones, except the middle rib bone which should be rounded on the loin side and cleaned on the chest side so it can be held with the fingers without touching the meat
3. Pound the loin meat with a mallet and go over it again to remove any meat that protrudes from the blows
4. Melt butter and place the chops in it
5. A quarter hour before serving, place over very high heat, taking care to turn when browned on one side
6. Arrange in a crown shape, placing a fried toast between each one
7. Drain the fat and keep the cooking substance, which is mixed with Spanish sauce and a little broth, stirring and turning to dress the little chops with it
8. Can be varied by serving on escarole, in tomato sauce, with fine roots or with earthnut truffles
