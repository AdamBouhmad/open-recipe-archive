## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- broth
- white wine
- parsley
- dried mushrooms (champignons)

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and fry in a good amount of butter
2. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and a spoonful of flour
3. When half-fried, add a little broth, white wine, chopped parsley, and dried mushrooms called champignons
4. Let simmer until the sauce reduces
