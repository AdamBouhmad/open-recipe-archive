## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of jitomates (tomatoes) and onions
- One bay leaf
- Crushed pepper
- One tablespoon or half, according to the quantity of jitomates, of saffron powder
- Broth with fat from the pot
- The necessary butter

## Directions

1. Use equal quantity of jitomates and onions, and season them with one bay leaf, crushed pepper, and one tablespoon or half of saffron powder, according to the quantity of jitomates.
2. Moisten the jitomates and onions with broth from the pot that has fat, and add the necessary butter.
3. It should cook over low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon so that it does not stick.
4. Strain through a sieve using a wooden spoon for this purpose.
