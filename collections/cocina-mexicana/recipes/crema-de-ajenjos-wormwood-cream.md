## Ingredients

- Half a pound of wormwood tops, large and small
- Peels of two lemons or better two oranges, finely chopped
- Sixteen quartillos of common brandy
- Four pounds of good sugar
- Eight quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Leave the wormwood tips and citrus peels in infusion in the brandy for ten hours in daylight
2. Distill to obtain only half of the liquor
3. Sweeten with four pounds of sugar dissolved in eight quartillos of water at syrup consistency
4. When cooled, clarify through a bag and store in well-sealed bottles
