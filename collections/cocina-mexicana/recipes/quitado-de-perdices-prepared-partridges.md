## Ingredients

- partridges (cleaned and washed)
- oil
- garlic (two heads, whole)
- parsley (chopped, substantial amount)
- cloves (six whole)
- peppercorns (six whole)
- sour orange juice
- ham (a slice)

## Directions

1. Brown the partridges on the grill on both sides.
2. When browned, put in a pot with oil, two whole heads of garlic, a substantial amount of chopped parsley, six whole cloves, six whole peppercorns and sour orange juice according to quantity, so it becomes half-sour, and a slice of ham.
3. Put on the fire to fry.
4. As soon as the parsley is fried, add necessary hot water to cook it.
5. At mid-cooking, add flour to thicken, stirring so it does not form lumps.
6. Serve thick.
