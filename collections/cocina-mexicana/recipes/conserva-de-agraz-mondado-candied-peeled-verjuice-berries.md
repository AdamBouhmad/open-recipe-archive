## Ingredients

- Verjuice berries, somewhat more mature but still very green in color
- Sugar, in quantity equal to the weight of prepared verjuice berries

## Directions

1. Select verjuice berries that are somewhat more mature than those for the previous recipe, but that retain their very green color
2. Remove the skin or peel and the stones with a very sharp pointed stick
3. Make a clarified syrup at the ball stage, with as many pounds of sugar as there are pounds of prepared verjuice berries
4. Add the verjuice to the syrup and allow it to boil a dozen times
5. Remove from heat, skim, and store in jars or bottles
