## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the artichokes and place them in cold water as the previous ones
2. Turn them at serving time, putting them in boiling water after removing the inner part
3. Place them in the serving dish, pouring white sauce in the hollow of the heart
4. You can also serve the sauce separately in a sauceboat
