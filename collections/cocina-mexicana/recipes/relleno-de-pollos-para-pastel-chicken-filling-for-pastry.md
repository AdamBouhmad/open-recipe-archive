## Ingredients

- chickens
- onion
- parsley
- flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- pitted olives

## Directions

1. Cut chickens into small pieces and remove the bones
2. Fry the meat together with chopped onion and parsley
3. Brown a little flour in butter and add to the chickens
4. Almost an hour before serving, add egg yolks mixed in a little vinegar and stir into the stew
5. Can be decorated with pitted olives
