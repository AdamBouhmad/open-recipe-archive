## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Salt
- Oil
- Water
- Yeast
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a dough following the previous method, but omitting eggs and salt from the first portion
2. Increase the sugar slightly
3. Dissolve a small amount of yeast in the water to be used for mixing
4. In the second portion, add butter and oil
5. All can be combined into one portion, adding butter without melting it
6. Once the dough is soft, extend with a rolling pin
7. Cut the empanadas with a knife and proceed as described in previous articles
