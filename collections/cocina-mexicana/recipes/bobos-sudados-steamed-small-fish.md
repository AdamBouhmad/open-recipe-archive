## Ingredients

- Small fish (bobos)
- Lard (manteca)
- Capers (alcaparras)
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Cooked onions
- Olives
- Tornachiles
- Oil

## Directions

1. Wrap the bobos well in papers filled with lard
2. Cook them between two fires, turning them from one side to the other until well cooked
3. Remove the papers and serve with the following sauce
4. Desalt the capers in water, grind them and fry with a little lard
5. Add salt if needed, a little vinegar, cooked onions, olives, tornachiles and plenty of oil
6. Serve the sauce over the bobos
