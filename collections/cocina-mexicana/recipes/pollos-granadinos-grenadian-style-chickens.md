## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- chicken broth
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- saffron
- raisins
- almonds
- chicken pieces
- salt
- toasted sesame seeds
- pomegranate seeds
- Málaga wine

## Directions

1. Choose ripe tomatoes, remove the seeds, grind well and fry
2. Add the broth in which the chickens were cooked and a little ground cloves, pepper, cinnamon and saffron
3. Add raisins and almonds
4. Add the chicken pieces, season with necessary salt
5. Let simmer over gentle fire for a long time until it thickens well
6. When serving, sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds, pomegranate seeds and a glass of Málaga wine
