## Ingredients

- sour apples
- a little sponge cake
- almonds
- sugar
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- egg yolks
- egg whites (double the number of yolks)
- butter or lard
- sugar and cinnamon, ground
- pine nuts
- almonds
- raisins
- syrup
- cinnamon, ground
- two drops of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Use sour apples, which are best; remove the skins and cores, and grind them
2. Mix with a little sponge cake, almonds, sugar, salt, pepper and clove, all ground
3. Beat some egg yolks and double their number in egg whites, and add them to the paste until it becomes thin
4. Pour into cazuelas coated with butter or lard
5. Cook over two gentle fires until the cake sets
6. When warm, dust with ground sugar and cinnamon, and decorate with pine nuts, almonds and raisins
7. Make a sauce with syrup, ground cinnamon, and two drops of orange blossom water, ensuring it is thick
8. Alternatively, add two cloves of spice to the syrup instead of orange blossom drops, and serve the cake in slices
