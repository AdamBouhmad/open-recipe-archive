## Ingredients

- testicles
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- clove
- flour
- broth
- wine
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into slices more or less thick
2. Place in a pot with butter, chopped onion and parsley, clove and a handful of flour
3. Moisten with equal parts broth and wine and the corresponding salt
4. Cook for half an hour over fire
5. Separate, arrange on a plate, and skim the sauce
6. Mix with strained broth, pour over and serve as an intermediate course
