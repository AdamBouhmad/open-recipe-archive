## Ingredients

- Any of four types of plums: green corazoncillo plums, black perdigon plums, yellow fleshy mirabel plums, or mirabolan plums
- Sugar equal in weight to the plum pulp
- Additional sugar in syrup of breaking point consistency

## Directions

1. Cook any of these four types of plums until they reach a jam consistency
2. Pass the jam through a sieve
3. Let it dry
4. Dissolve it in syrup made with an equal weight of sugar at breaking point consistency
5. Pour the paste into appropriate molds
6. Let it dry in a warming oven
