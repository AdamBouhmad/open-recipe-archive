## Ingredients

- butter
- salt
- fresh eggs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a platter that can withstand the action of fire with butter.
2. Sprinkle a little salt on it and break the fresh eggs in the platter, making sure the yolks remain whole.
3. Then sprinkle with fine salt mixed with pepper, placing a small ball of butter on top of each egg, and the platter over hot ashes.
4. Sometimes the eggs cook too much underneath and remain raw on top, but this inconvenience is avoided by passing over the eggs a spatula heated in the fire.
5. It is necessary to keep the spatula at a certain distance so that the yolks cook without hardening. This way of cooking eggs is more elegant than the ordinary way.
