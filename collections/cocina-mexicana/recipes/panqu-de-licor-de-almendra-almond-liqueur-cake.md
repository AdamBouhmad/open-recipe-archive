## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of good butter
- twelve egg yolks
- one pound of very finely ground sugar like powder
- twelve egg whites
- one cup of bitter almond liqueur

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with one pound of good butter
2. Beat twelve egg yolks and stir them together
3. Beat at the same time with one pound of very finely ground sugar like powder
4. In the same manner, beat the twelve egg whites afterward
5. Following the beating very slowly, add the eggs little by little to the mixture of flour and butter
6. Once everything is united, continue beating for two hours
7. At the end, add one cup of bitter almond liqueur and beat with it for another half hour
8. After this long and laborious operation, pour the beaten batter into one or several small dishes
9. Butter the edges of the small dishes so the cake does not stick
10. Put them to bake in an oven prepared and tempered as for sponge cakes
