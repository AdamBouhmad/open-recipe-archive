## Ingredients

- Loin
- Thick strips of ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Carrots
- Cabbage
- Beef broth
- White wine

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with thick strips of ham
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Fry in butter
4. Turn to brown on both sides
5. Place between two fires in a pot for two or three hours
6. Separately cook carrots and cabbage with broth
7. When the meat is cooked, place on a platter, alternating around it pieces of carrot with pieces of cabbage
8. To the sauce remaining in the pot add a cup of broth and a glass of white wine
9. Bring to a boil for a moment
10. Pour the sauce over the meat and vegetables when serving
