## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- water
- salt
- lard
- sliced garlic
- ground tomato
- broth from cooking the chops
- ground spices
- sugar
- vinegar
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Well trimmed and cut to regular size, cook the chops in salted water
2. Fry in a pan with lard, sliced garlic cloves and ground tomato
3. Add a little broth from cooking the chops and season with salt, ground spices, a pinch of sugar and a splash of vinegar
4. Place the chops in this sauce and let it reduce, taking care that they do not stick to the pan by stirring and turning from time to time
5. When the sauce is consumed, remove the chops and wrap in buttered paper, which is dusted with grated bread crumbs covering the chop on all sides, adding a little lemon juice
6. Fold the papers and place in a pan with lard over low heat until the little chops have good color
7. Can also be arranged the same way without wrapping in paper
