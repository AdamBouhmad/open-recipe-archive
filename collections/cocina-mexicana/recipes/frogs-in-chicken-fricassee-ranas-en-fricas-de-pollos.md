## Ingredients

- Frog legs, prepared
- Mushrooms
- Parsley bunch
- Onion
- Garlic head
- Cloves
- Butter
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place prepared frog legs in a pot with mushrooms, parsley bunch, onion, garlic head, cloves, and butter
2. Turn them over the fire two or three times
3. Add a little flour
4. Moisten with white wine and some broth
5. Season with salt and black pepper
6. Let cook for about a quarter hour until the broth reduces to a sauce
7. Thicken the sauce with egg yolks mixed with butter and chopped parsley
