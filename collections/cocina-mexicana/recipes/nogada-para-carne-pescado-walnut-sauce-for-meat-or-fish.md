## Ingredients

- A handful of toasted sesame seeds
- Cleaned almonds
- Small walnuts
- A little soaked and squeezed breadcrumb
- Water
- Hot lard
- Cooking oil
- A little vinegar
- A little mustard, well washed and ground (optional)
- Powdered oregano (optional)
- Salt
- Raw oil
- Olives

## Directions

1. Grind the toasted sesame seeds, cleaned almonds, small walnuts, and a little soaked and squeezed breadcrumb
2. Add water as necessary to make a sauce
3. Add boiling lard to this
4. Beat well
5. Fry everything in a little lard
6. As soon as it has boiled, add cooking oil, a little vinegar, and if desired, a little well-washed and ground mustard and powdered oregano
7. Almost at the end add salt and stir well so it does not curdle
8. Use this sauce to line the platter
9. Place the roasted bird or fish on it
10. Cover with the same sauce, adding raw oil and vinegar, olives and other garnishes
