## Ingredients

- Small white beans
- Milk
- Cinnamon sticks
- Ground almond
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the small white beans without tequesquite.
2. Add them to milk with cinnamon sticks, a little ground almond, and sufficient sugar to sweeten them.
3. Let them boil until they become thick.
4. Pour into a serving dish and garnish the top with cinnamon powder or dragée.
