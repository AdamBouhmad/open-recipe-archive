## Ingredients

- two quartillos of water
- a little sugar
- a small piece of butter
- lemon zest
- flour

## Directions

1. Put water, a little sugar, a small piece of butter, and lemon zest in a saucepan
2. Cook the water until it has taken on the smell of lemon
3. Remove the lemon
4. Put a large quantity of flour into the water with one hand while stirring everything with a spoon using the other hand
5. Continue sprinkling flour while stirring until the mixture becomes extremely thick and is entirely cooked, which is known when upon tasting it, no taste of flour is perceived
6. This takes at least half an hour, and the more cooked it is the lighter it becomes
7. Remove from the fire, add one egg and continue stirring until it incorporates into the paste
8. Add another egg immediately, and so on successively until it is manageable, always beating and stirring
9. Take it with the handle of a thick spoon, like a small nut, and make it fall into the hot fat by means of a strong jolt against the edge of the pan
10. The paste swells and the interior remains hollow
