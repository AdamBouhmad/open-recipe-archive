## Ingredients

- Beef marrow from one animal
- Two ounces of clean almonds
- Four or five candied citron pieces of common market size
- Eggs
- Ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Soak the beef marrow in water for twenty-four hours
2. Remove it, drain it and grind it
3. Grind together two ounces of clean almonds and four or five candied citron pieces of common market size in the same mortar
4. Mix everything and grind together
5. Form the mixture into balls or pears, or give it any other graceful shape possible
6. Bathe them with half-beaten eggs, mixing the whites with the yolks together
7. Roll them in ground biscuit
8. Fry them in lard
