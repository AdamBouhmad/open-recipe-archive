## Ingredients

- Pámpanos
- Papas (potatoes) or Acelgas (chard) or Espinacas (spinach)
- Oil
- Pan rallado (breadcrumbs)

## Directions

1. Prepare the potatoes the same way as for torta, or cook the chard or spinach as described in their respective articles
2. Fill the cleaned and desalted pámpanos with them
3. Do not tie with threads, because they are to be served in their papers
4. Wrap them after coating with oil and rolling in breadcrumbs in papers with much oil
5. Place to cook on the grill
6. When done, serve wrapped in their paper
