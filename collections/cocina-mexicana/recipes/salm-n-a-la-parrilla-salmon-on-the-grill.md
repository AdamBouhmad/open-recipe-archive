## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Oil
- Onion pieces
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Fry the salmon for one hour in oil with onion pieces and parsley
2. Grill it, basting with the remaining oil
3. Serve with any of the sauces appropriate for fish
