## Ingredients

- twelve cooked egg yolks
- two ounces of sponge cake, cold
- one ounce of cinnamon
- six ounces of clean almonds
- orange blossom water
- clove
- pepper
- toasted sesame
- powdered sponge cake
- beaten egg
- oil
- clarified syrup
- raisins
- toasted almonds
- toasted sesame
- citron pieces

## Directions

1. Grind twelve cooked egg yolks, two ounces of cold sponge cake, and one ounce of cinnamon
2. Pound six ounces of clean almonds and mix everything with a little orange blossom water, molded clove and pepper, and toasted sesame
3. Form the meatballs and arrange on a board with powdered sponge cake
4. Cover each one with two coats of beaten egg
5. Fry immediately and place in a prepared syrup made as follows: clarify as usual, add orange blossom water with raisins, toasted almonds, toasted sesame, and little pieces of citron
6. When it reaches medium point, add a little clove and a little pepper, both ground; when fully cooked, add the meatballs
7. Leave them over heat for a while
8. To serve, let cool and add white wine
