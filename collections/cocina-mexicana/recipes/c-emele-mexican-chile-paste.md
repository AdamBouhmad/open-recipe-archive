## Ingredients

- chile ancho, deveined and toasted
- cooked tomato
- head of garlic, toasted
- tortilla, toasted and then soaked
- pork meat
- epazote branch

## Directions

1. Grind together the deveined and toasted chile ancho, cooked tomato, toasted garlic head, and tortilla that has been toasted and then soaked
2. Fry the ground mixture with pork meat
3. Add an epazote branch and season to taste
