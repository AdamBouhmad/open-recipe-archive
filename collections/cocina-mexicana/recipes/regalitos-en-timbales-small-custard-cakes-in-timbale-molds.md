## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- half an egg's worth of good butter
- half a lemon rind, descaled and minced, or equal amount of orange blossom
- 4 egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- 1 full good glass of cream

## Directions

1. Place flour and powdered sugar in a suitable vessel
2. Add the butter, melted
3. Add the minced lemon rind or orange blossom
4. Mix everything together
5. Add the 4 egg yolks one at a time, mixing well and incorporating everything
6. Add a pinch of salt
7. Add the cream
8. Pour the mixture into small timbale molds that have been prepared in advance in the same manner as for puff pastry tartlets in timbales
9. Bake until cooked through
