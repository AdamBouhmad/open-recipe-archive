## Ingredients

- butter, plenty
- capers, desalted
- ground spices

## Directions

1. Cook the fish wrapped in papers well filled with butter, over two fires, turning them from one side to the other until they are well cooked.
2. Once cooked, remove the papers and serve with the following sauce: desalt capers in water, grind them and fry with ground spices.
