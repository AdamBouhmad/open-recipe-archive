## Ingredients

- solid meat
- pork loin
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- onion
- tomatoes
- parsley
- flour
- clove
- cinnamon
- garlic
- zucchini

## Directions

1. Mince solid meat and pork loin; once the meat is minced add two or three raw eggs and a little salt and pepper and grind well
2. In a saucepan put lard, two or three handfuls of onion in wedges, one or two minced tomatoes, parsley also minced; once it is fried add flour and let it fry all together more; once it is fried add water
3. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and two or three garlic cloves, form the meatballs and add them to the sauce with whole zucchini
4. Let it cook a little before serving
