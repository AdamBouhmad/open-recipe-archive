## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 pound flour
- half cup wine
- half pound melted butter
- half cup aniseed water
- a little warm water

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs and mix in 1 pound flour, half cup wine, half pound melted butter, half cup aniseed water, and a little warm water
2. Mix well and knead
3. Extend with a rolling pin and fry
4. Pour honey over them
