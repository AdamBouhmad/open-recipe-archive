## Ingredients

- Fresh eggs
- Milk
- Parsley or other fine herbs
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mix and beat some fresh eggs, add a little milk to soften them, add parsley or other fine herbs; sprinkle everything with salt, and pour the eggs thus prepared all at once into a skillet that contains a little well-heated butter.
2. As soon as it sets, grasp the skillet by the handle, give it a horizontal impulse forward extending the arm, and upon withdrawing it another impulse upward; the tender mass folds thanks to this movement in the shape of a half moon.
3. Correct with a spoon any imperfections in the edges; give another turn, remove and serve golden and steaming on a plate that should barely be moistened with a drop or two of clear fat as transparent as the juice of an orange.
