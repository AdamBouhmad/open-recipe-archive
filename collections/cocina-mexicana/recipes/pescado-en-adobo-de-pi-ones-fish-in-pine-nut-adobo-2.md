## Ingredients

- One ounce of cleaned pine nuts
- Twelve cloves
- Twelve grains of fine pepper
- Twelve grains of Tabasco pepper
- A piece of ginger
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Water from cooking sweet lime slices
- Fried fish with eyes

## Directions

1. Grind all the ingredients well and dissolve in vinegar
2. Strain and add necessary salt
3. Add a little water in which sweet lime slices have been cooked
4. Place the fried fish with eyes in this sauce and preserve for many days
5. When eating, one may add raw eating oil with salt if desired
