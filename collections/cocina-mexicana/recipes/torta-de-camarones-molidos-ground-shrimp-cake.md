## Ingredients

- Shrimp, cleaned and ground
- Biscuit, ground
- Egg, beaten
- Lard

## Directions

1. Grind the cleaned shrimp dry
2. Mix them with ground biscuit and bind with beaten egg as if for frying
3. Put in a buttered dish and let the cake set
4. Divide into pieces and cook in tlemole
5. Alternatively, make small tortillas from the same paste, frying each one separately, then add them to the tlemole
