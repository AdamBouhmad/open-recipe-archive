## Ingredients

- Good white wine
- Sugar
- Lemon zest
- Cinnamon
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Pour in a pan one quarter liter of good white wine, sugar, lemon zest and cinnamon, and bring to a boil
2. Break seven or eight eggs to use the yolks, which beat with a wooden spoon until well bound, binding them also with the wine, which will be poured little by little
3. Pass through a sieve and pour into small cups to take the bain-marie
