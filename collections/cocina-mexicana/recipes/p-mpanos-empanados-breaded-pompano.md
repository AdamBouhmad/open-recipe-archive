## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- wine
- egg yolks
- pompano, cooked
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a batter by beating flour with salt, wine, and egg yolks until somewhat loose
2. Coat the cooked pompano with the batter
3. Fry in lard
