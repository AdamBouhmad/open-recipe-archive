## Ingredients

- Partridge meat
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Spanish sauce
- Game essence

## Directions

1. Mix some egg yolks into the partridge meat and pass everything through a sieve
2. Pour into a mold buttered with butter
3. Place the mold in a bain-marie
4. Cook for one hour until done
5. Empty onto a plate
6. Pour Spanish sauce mixed with game essence over it
