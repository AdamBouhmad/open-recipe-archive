## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- water
- saffron
- cloves
- pepper
- egg whites
- egg yolks
- broth
- lard
- minced meat
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and soak the rice in two or three waters
2. After drying, moisten with water and cook, either steamed wrapped in a napkin or in a little water, seasoned with corresponding salt
3. Once cooked, set aside, and the next day drain well and mix with a little salt, or without it if desired
4. Moisten with broth or mix with a small amount of saffron, cloves, and ground pepper dissolved in water
5. Beat egg whites well, and when they have thickened add the yolks
6. Add the rice moistened with broth or with the spices already mentioned
7. Grease a mold with lard and put in a layer of rice and another of minced meat, alternating layers until the mold is full, finishing by pouring the beaten egg mixture on top
8. When it has set on the bottom, place a comal with little heat on top so the cake sets on both sides
9. Once set, it can be dusted with a little sugar
