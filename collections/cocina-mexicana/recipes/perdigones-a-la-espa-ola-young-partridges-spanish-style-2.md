## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Ham
- Its broth

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the young partridges as mentioned in previous articles
2. Place them in a casserole with ham
3. Pour the broth over the partridges along with its cooking liquid
4. When the broth has thickened, remove the fat as much as possible
5. Let it take on the consistency of a sauce
6. Ensure it has the necessary salt to taste well
