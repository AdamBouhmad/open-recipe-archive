## Ingredients

- partridges (plucked, emptied and prepared)
- lemon (slices without skin or seeds)
- bacon (slices)
- paper (greased)
- clear juices or lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. After plucking, emptying and preparing, cover the breast with slices of lemon without skin or seeds, and then with slices of bacon.
2. Wrap in greased paper and put on the roaster.
3. Serve with clear juice or lemon or orange juice.
