## Ingredients

- plovers
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- papers

## Directions

1. Chop much onion, parsley, a little garlic, and plenty of bread crumbs
2. Season with salt and ground pepper
3. Mix everything together
4. Wrap the plovers in this mixture, adding thyme inside
5. Wrap the plovers in papers
6. Put them on the grill and turn them over a gentle fire so they roast
