## Ingredients

- Pig blood
- Pig fat
- About thirty cloves of garlic, cleaned and crushed with a little salt
- One and a half ounces of anise
- One ounce of cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste
- One ounce of coriander
- Pig intestines
- Onion, chopped (optional, only if to be used fresh)

## Directions

1. Collect the blood, stirring constantly with a stick while it comes from the pig to prevent coagulation
2. Remove all fibrous matter, keeping only the liquid part
3. Add the pig fat, melting it in the blood as much as possible
4. Add the crushed garlic with salt, anise, cumin, salt, pepper, and coriander
5. Mix everything well to form the sausage mixture
6. Fill the pig intestines immediately, tying them at the ends
7. Boil in a pot of hot water, keeping them tied and stirring occasionally
8. Remove and prick with a pin; they are done when clear fat comes out from the holes
9. Hang them in the air in a place where they can be smoked for about one month, being careful that the fire is not so strong that it melts them
