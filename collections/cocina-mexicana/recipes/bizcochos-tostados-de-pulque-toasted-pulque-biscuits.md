## Ingredients

- five pounds of flour
- two cuartillos of pulque
- ten ounces of melted lard
- one panocha or panela of one pound weight (or better one or two piloncillos of equal weight) dissolved in water
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Mix everything and knead
2. Let rest for twelve hours
3. Add a little more lard to the dough
4. Cut the biscuits triangular (in half coconuts)
5. After another twelve hours put them in the oven
6. When baked, split them where the cut was made
7. Toast them
