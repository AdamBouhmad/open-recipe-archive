## Ingredients

- hen
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- clove
- bay leaf
- fifteen or sixteen small onions
- broth
- flour

## Directions

1. Sauté the hen in butter with salt, pepper, thyme, clove, and bay leaf
2. Add fifteen or sixteen small onions to the saucepan
3. Sauté them together
4. Add a little broth
5. Cook on low heat for five or six hours until the hen is very tender
6. Thicken the sauce with flour
7. Serve the hen surrounded by the onions
