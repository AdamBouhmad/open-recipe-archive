## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- salt
- fine pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- oregano
- vinegar or lemon juice
- bull's bladder
- strong thread
- clean cloth
- water
- tomillo
- thyme
- marjoram
- oil
- parsley sprigs
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Beat the tongue with a stick or against a stone and open it down the middle
2. Remove the nerves from the gullet, and after beating the cartilages well
3. Add to the tongue in the middle salt, plenty of pepper of both classes, and oregano, all in powder
4. Sprinkle the tongue afterwards with vinegar or lemon juice
5. Wrap very tightly and place in a bull's bladder, which is also tightened and tied with strong thread
6. Place under a stone with considerable weight on top, leaving it pressed for five days and taking care to turn it daily
7. Then cook wrapped in clean cloth, with water, vinegar, bay leaf, thyme and marjoram
8. Put back in the press, keeping it there one day, and the next day slice it, placing it on some salad
9. Cover with parsley sprigs, and for serving pour oil, vinegar and pepper powder, adorning with small chiles and olives
