## Ingredients

- Mutton feet
- Salt
- Water
- Chicken sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch and clean the feet carefully.
2. Remove the large bones.
3. Boil in salted water for four or five hours.
4. The feet are cooked when they are tender and the remaining bones come away easily.
5. Make a chicken sauce and simmer the feet in it gradually for half an hour so they take on the flavor.
6. Thicken the sauce.
7. Serve very hot.
