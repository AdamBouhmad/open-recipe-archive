## Ingredients

- ham slices
- cooked ham
- garlic
- water
- lard
- onion
- tomato
- pepper
- egg yolks
- parsley
- capers
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Toast ham slices that are neither too thick nor too thin
2. Between two slices place cooked ham chopped very finely and finely chopped garlic
3. Heat a little water and lard in a pot and add more garlic
4. When the water is consumed, add onion chopped very finely, in quantity about one third of the ham
5. When cooked, add ground tomato without seeds, browned garlic and a little pepper
6. When seasoned, add the ham and for each half pound add two yolks of hard-boiled egg ground, which should be mixed from the metate with very little water
7. Incorporate this into the fried mixture with a little fresh chopped parsley, capers, chopped olives and a little oil
8. Let it thicken and serve
