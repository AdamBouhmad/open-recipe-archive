## Ingredients

- hare or rabbit
- jitomate, chopped
- onion, chopped
- onion in segments
- garlic heads, whole and peeled
- salt
- pepper, whole
- ham, half pound sliced
- vinegar, half quartillo
- oil, half amount
- paper
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Skin the hare or rabbit, cut into pieces and place in a new pot with considerable chopped jitomate, chopped onion and onion in segments, whole peeled garlic heads, salt, whole pepper, half pound sliced ham, half quartillo of vinegar and half the amount of oil
2. Roast the liver separately, grind it and leave in vinegar then add to the pot
3. Cover well with kraft paper and a casserole with water placed on top
4. Place over gentle heat, shaking from time to time so it does not burn
5. Garnish with slices of fried bread
