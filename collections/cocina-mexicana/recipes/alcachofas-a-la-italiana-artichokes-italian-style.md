## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Lemon juice
- Water
- Fresh butter
- Fine salt
- Verjuice or lemon juice
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Divide the artichoke into four parts and remove the skin, brush well with lemon juice, and place to cook in a little water adding a piece of fresh butter and necessary fine salt, adding verjuice or lemon juice.
2. When cooked, remove and let drain, then serve on a plate with Italian sauce, which is made as follows: Place a saucepan on the heat with a piece of butter and butter, add proportionate flour, and when it begins to boil, reduce the heat leaving it gentle, and maintain the sauce there for three hours until it takes on a golden color.
3. Then moisten with a little meat broth, let it boil removing all the foam it produces, and when it no longer produces any, remove it; and letting it cool, strain it through a silk sieve.
