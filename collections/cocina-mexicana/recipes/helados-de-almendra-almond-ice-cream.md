## Ingredients

- five ounces bitter almonds
- ten ounces sweet almonds
- several spoonfuls of orange flower water
- eight ounces milk
- eight ounces custard
- twelve ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Soak five ounces of bitter almonds and ten of sweet almonds in cold water, not hot as is customary, for six or eight hours to remove the shells easily
2. Grind immediately in a marble mortar, adding some spoonfuls of orange flower water
3. Form the paste and dissolve it in eight ounces of milk with as much custard in a small pan
4. Stir continuously with a wooden spatula to dissolve twelve ounces of sugar
5. Mix the almond milk and when everything has the proper consistency, let cool and freeze as directed
