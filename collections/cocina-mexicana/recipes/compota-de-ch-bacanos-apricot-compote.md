## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into two pieces
2. Remove the stones
3. Arrange in a clay platter whose bottom has been dusted with grated sugar
4. Sprinkle lightly with water
5. Put over a slow fire to cook until almost complete reduction of the syrup
6. Dust again with sugar and cover with a lid
7. Serve cold
