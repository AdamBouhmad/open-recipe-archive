## Ingredients

- peaches that are slightly hard
- water
- clarified sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Select peaches that are slightly hard
2. Peel them and remove the pits
3. Put them to boil until they rise to the surface of the water and soften
4. Remove them and bathe in cold water
5. Place them in clarified syrup and boil them until they stop producing foam
6. Remove from the fire and serve hot
