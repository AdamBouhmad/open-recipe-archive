## Ingredients

- 6 cuartillos milk
- 3 pounds sugar
- 0.5 pound starch

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil.
2. After it boils, set aside one cuartillo and sweeten the rest.
3. Dissolve the starch in the cuartillo of milk that was set aside and mix everything together.
4. Strain and place over heat, stirring constantly.
5. It is done when, upon adding a little water, it can be picked up by hand.
