## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- onions
- mixed herbs bundle
- broth
- white wine
- veal bones
- veal knuckle
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Completely debone the hare
2. Place in a pot or pan with salt, pepper, carrots, onions, a bundle of mixed herbs, broth and white wine in equal parts
3. Add all the broken hare bones below, a veal knuckle and ham slices placed above and below
4. Let cook over gentle heat for an hour and a half
5. Once the hare is cooked, remove from heat and strain the sauce
6. Let the sauce thicken and serve hot
