## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove spices
- Paper
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Cinnamon
- Broth

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey and leave in the open air for twenty-four hours
2. Cook in a pot until half done
3. Place on a roasting spit with butter, salt and pepper
4. Insert clove spices
5. Wrap in paper and place over low heat
6. When roasted, make a sauce with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, pepper and broth
7. Pour the sauce over the turkey
