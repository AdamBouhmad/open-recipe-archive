## Ingredients

- artichokes
- garlic
- parsley
- onion
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After washing and cooking well, stuff with garlic, parsley, onion, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper
2. Place in a cazuela with oil and lemon juice between two gentle fires and serve
