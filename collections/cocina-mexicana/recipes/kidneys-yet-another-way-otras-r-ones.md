## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- Lard
- Butter
- Parsley
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Bread
- Capers
- Broad chile
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. After removing the fat and skin covering the kidneys, chop and fry in lard and butter
2. Chop parsley, tomato, garlic and onion and fry this mixture separately
3. Once the kidneys are well fried, mix them with the mixture in a pot
4. When they are cooked, add cloves, cinnamon, a piece of fried bread, capers, a broad chile and garlic, all ground together with a good piece of butter
5. Shortly before serving, add half a glass of Jerez wine
