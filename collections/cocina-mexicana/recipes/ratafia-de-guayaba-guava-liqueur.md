## Ingredients

- Ripe guavas
- Refined catalán brandy
- Guava leaves
- Cloves
- Common syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the hearts from ripe guavas and grind the peels
2. Put the ground peels in a cloth bag and press to extract the juice
3. To two quartillos of this juice, add four of refined catalán brandy, fifteen or twenty guava leaves, and six cloves made into powder
4. Pour this liqueur into a bottle and after fifteen days strain through a cloth
5. Sweeten to taste with common clarified syrup and bottle
