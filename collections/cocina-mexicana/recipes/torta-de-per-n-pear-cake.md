## Ingredients

- pears
- medium toasted and crushed sponge cake
- sifted sugar
- eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the pears and remove the cores and skins
2. Grind them and let them drain
3. When no water remains, mix with an equal volume of medium toasted and crushed sponge cake
4. Add sifted sugar as needed to make the paste flavorful
5. Allow six eggs for each pound of the mixture
