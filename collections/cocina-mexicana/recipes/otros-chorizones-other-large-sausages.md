## Ingredients

- one arroba of pork loin or spine meat
- one pound of soaked and well-ground ancho chile
- half pound of salt
- one head of garlic
- water

## Directions

1. The meat and chile must be well chopped; the salt and garlic well ground.
2. Mix well with a little water.
3. Leave in marinade for twelve hours and taste, adding what is needed.
