## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- xitomate
- roasted green chiles
- garlic
- hen livers cooked
- fresh fish broth
- fine spices
- capers
- oil
- vinegar
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop onion very finely, tomato, xitomate, and roasted and cleaned green chiles
2. Grind clean garlic
3. Mix all together
4. Grind cooked hen livers and dissolve in fresh fish broth
5. Add finely ground spices with capers
6. Add oil, vinegar, and olives
7. Bathe the fish with this sauce
