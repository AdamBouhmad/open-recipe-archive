## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- tomatoes
- garlic
- onion
- water
- salt
- rabbit in pieces
- parsley
- capers
- bread soaked

## Directions

1. Brown a little flour in butter
2. Fry tomatoes, garlic, and chopped onion in the same butter
3. Add water and sufficient salt with rabbit pieces
4. Once cooked, add a broth made from ground parsley, capers, and soaked bread
5. When thickened, remove from heat and let rest until serving
