## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Zucchini hearts
- Garlic
- Onion
- Tomato
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Aged cheese, ground
- Eggs, beaten
- Bread crumbs
- Lard
- Tomato sauce
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Divide the zucchini in half lengthwise and place them to cook in salted water
2. Remove and with a spoon extract the hearts or insides, chop them finely
3. Chop garlic, onion, tomato, and parsley, and fry everything together with the chopped zucchini hearts
4. Season the mixture with salt and pepper powder, and let it dry
5. Remove from heat, and when this picadillo is cold, mix it with ground aged cheese, beaten eggs, and bread crumbs
6. Fill the zucchini halves with this mixture, which will be placed in a casserole with lard, letting them brown over two gentle fires
7. Add a sauce of tomato and parsley, chopped and fried with a little vinegar and oil
