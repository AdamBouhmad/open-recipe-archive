## Ingredients

- Large ripe pineapples with healthy, leafy crown
- Water for cooking
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Choose pineapples in good condition, large, and with healthy and leafy crown
2. Peel them without removing the crown and remove all the black eyes until leaving them entirely clean
3. Put to cook immediately in boiling water
4. If they are to be candied in slices, cut them after the pineapple is cooked, and then the crown is unnecessary; but if it is to be candied whole, place it in already clarified and hot syrup, making it boil in it for some time daily
