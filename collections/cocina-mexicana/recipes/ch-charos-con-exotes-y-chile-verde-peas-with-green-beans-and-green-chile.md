## Ingredients

- peas
- whole green beans
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- broth
- cloves
- pepper
- capers
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cook peas together with whole green beans.
2. Then chop the green beans and fry both in butter after frying garlic, onions, tomatoes and green chiles all chopped in it.
3. Add a little broth with ground cloves and pepper.
4. Add some whole capers and let everything simmer until the broth is almost consumed.
5. Bind this with beaten egg and leave on the fire until it sets.
