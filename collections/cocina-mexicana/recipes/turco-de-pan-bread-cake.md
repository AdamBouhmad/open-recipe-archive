## Ingredients

- cold water bread or French bread
- egg yolks
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- lard

## Directions

1. Grind the cold water or French bread with egg yolks, salt, clove and pepper
2. Mix the necessary lard so that the dough forms
3. Make little tortillas with it and finish as with the others
