## Ingredients

- Cold French bread, thinly sliced
- Tomato, fried
- Garlic, fried
- Onion, fried
- Fine spices
- Flanders cheese, grated
- Egg yolks
- Broth from the cooked pot

## Directions

1. Take cold French bread and slice very thin. In the baking dish, place a layer of fried tomato, garlic, and onion with fine spices that should be prepared in regular quantity.
2. Over this fried sauce, place a layer of bread, then over this another layer of fried sauce, a pinch of grated Flanders cheese, and a little egg yolk prepared as if for a lamp.
3. Continue covering the baking dish in this manner until finished, leaving a little egg yolk on top.
4. The bread slices are broken apart with a little broth from the cooked pot so they are neither dry nor loose.
5. Place the baking dish on a small stove with low heat and on top place a griddle also with low heat.
