## Ingredients

- Twelve quartillos of milk
- Half pound of almonds
- Half pound of candied fruit
- One chicken breast, ground
- Twelve white egg yolks
- Two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Cook and bring to the point of cajeta
3. Add more sugar if needed
