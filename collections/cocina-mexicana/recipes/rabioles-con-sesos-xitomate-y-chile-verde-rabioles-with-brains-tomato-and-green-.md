## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- melted lard
- salted water
- tomato
- onion
- green chile deveined
- cooked chard
- cooked brains
- 2 peppercorns
- 2 cloves ground
- a little oil
- a little vinegar
- tomatoes
- half a wide chile toasted and deveined
- a little ground sugar
- 2 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- water or pot broth
- a little oil
- vinegar
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of flour with two egg yolks and one egg white, melted lard and salted water, and knead well until it forms small bubbles
2. For the filling, mix tomato, onion and deveined green chile all chopped very fine
3. Fry it with cooked chard and cooked brains also, adding 2 ground peppercorns and 2 ground cloves, a little oil and a little vinegar
4. Form and cook the rabioles following the same procedures of the previous articles
5. For the sauce, roast some tomatoes and squeeze them to remove all the seeds
6. Peel and grind them together with half a wide chile, toasted and deveined
7. Fry the ground mixture, adding a little ground sugar, 2 fine peppercorns and 2 cloves, water or pot broth, a little oil, vinegar and a little butter
8. Place the rabioles in the sauce
