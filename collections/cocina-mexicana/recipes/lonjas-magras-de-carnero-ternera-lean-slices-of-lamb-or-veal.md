## Ingredients

- Lamb or veal slices
- Salt
- Bacon
- Sugar
- White wine
- Laurel leaves
- Cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Cut the slices across the grain, not along it, and salt them in the afternoon.
2. The next morning fry with bacon.
3. Place in a casserole and add sugar, white wine, bay leaves, and a cinnamon stick, letting them cook for a while.
