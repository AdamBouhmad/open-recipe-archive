## Ingredients

- a dozen doves
- butter
- salt
- chile
- garlic
- parsley
- flour
- white wine
- broth
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean, and prepare a dozen doves
2. Give them a turn in a saucepan with butter and salt
3. When they have taken color, add chopped chile, garlic, and parsley
4. Add a pinch of flour, white wine, and broth
5. Once the sauce begins to cook, remove the doves
6. Serve with fried bread crusts
