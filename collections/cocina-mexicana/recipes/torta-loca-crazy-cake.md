## Ingredients

- pork feet
- tongue
- garlic
- carrots
- beets
- lettuce
- white cabbage
- green beans
- potatoes
- pork loin
- mutton
- butter
- eggs
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook pork feet and tongue with garlic cloves
2. Separately cook carrots, beets, lettuce, white cabbage, green beans and potatoes
3. Make a minced mixture of pork loin and mutton seasoned like picadillo for stuffing
4. Chop all the cooked vegetables
5. Line a casserole with butter, add a little beaten egg, and make a layer of picadillo and another of the chopped vegetables with the feet and tongue, covering with pine nuts, raisins and almonds
6. Begin again with another layer of picadillo, alternating the others in the indicated order until the casserole is full
7. Cover with more beaten egg and cook the cake over two gentle fires
8. Serve with tomato sauce called de Ángeles, adding small chiles, olives and sliced hard-boiled eggs
