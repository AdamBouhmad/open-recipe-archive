## Ingredients

- Suckling pig, scalded
- Pig liver, chopped
- Sancochado ham
- Criadillas de tierra (truffles)
- Mushrooms
- Radishes
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Fine herbs
- Tabasco pepper
- Sea salt
- Raw oil
- Orange sauce
- White pepper

## Directions

1. After scalding in boiling water and making the necessary preparations as mentioned in previous articles, fill it with its liver, chopped together with sancochado ham, criadillas de tierra, mushrooms, radishes, capers, anchovies, and fine herbs, seasoning the chopped mixture with Tabasco pepper and sea salt, and half-fried or sancochado in a saucepan.
2. Fill the belly with this chopped mixture, tie it with thread, place it on the roasting spit, and take care to baste it constantly with raw oil so it browns well.
3. To serve, accompany it almost always with an orange sauce, with salt and white pepper.
