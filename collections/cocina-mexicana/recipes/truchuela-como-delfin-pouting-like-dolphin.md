## Ingredients

- pouting
- salt
- water
- potatoes
- hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Scale a good-sized pouting
2. Empty it through the gills and twist the tail, passing a thread through it that ties to a skewer passed through both eyes
3. Cook in salted water, drain
4. Arrange on a napkin with boiled potatoes around it
5. Pour hollandaise sauce over it
