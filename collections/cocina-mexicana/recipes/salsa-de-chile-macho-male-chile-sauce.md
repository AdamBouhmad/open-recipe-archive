## Ingredients

- Wide chile
- Pasilla chile
- Pulque
- Chile seeds
- Butter
- Garlic
- Raw onion
- Aged cheese
- Oil

## Directions

1. Toast one part deveined wide chile and two-thirds parts pasilla
2. Break apart by hand in a little pulque
3. Add seeds from the same chile browned in butter
4. Add finely chopped raw garlic and onion
5. Add all necessary pulque
6. Dust with grated aged cheese
7. If desired, sprinkle with a little oil
