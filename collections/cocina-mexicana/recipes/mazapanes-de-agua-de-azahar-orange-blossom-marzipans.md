## Ingredients

- one pound of peeled and ground almonds
- egg white
- a little orange blossom water
- one pound of flower-point syrup
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and grind the almonds in the quantity of one pound
2. Add to the ground almonds some egg white and a little orange blossom water
3. Pour the paste into one pound of flower-point syrup, incorporating it well with a ladle
4. Place in a pot over heat and stir well with force until the mass becomes manageable, taking care that it does not stick
5. Place on a sugar-dusted board, dusting the mass itself as well, roll it, let it rest a little and form the figures desired, or pass the mass through a syringe
