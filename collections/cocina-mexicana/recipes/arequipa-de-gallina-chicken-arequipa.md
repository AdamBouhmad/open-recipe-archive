## Ingredients

- two quarts of milk
- one pound of almonds
- half pound of almonds
- chicken breast
- egg whites
- twelve pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. To two quarts of milk, add one pound of almonds and half pound of almonds
2. Add chicken breast
3. Add egg whites and twelve pounds of sugar
4. Cook until it reaches the desired consistency
