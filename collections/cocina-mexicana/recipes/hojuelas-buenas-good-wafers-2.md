## Ingredients

- Two pounds of milled flour
- A little butter
- Two eggs
- One piece of sugar
- One squeezed orange
- A few grains of salt

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of milled flour with a little butter, two eggs, one piece of sugar, one squeezed orange and a few grains of salt
2. Mix and knead everything until it is smooth
3. Then the hojuelas can be made, and they will turn out good
