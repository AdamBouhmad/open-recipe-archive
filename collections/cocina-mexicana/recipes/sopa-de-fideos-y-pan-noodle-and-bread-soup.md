## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- fideos (thin noodles)
- lard (manteca)
- 2 ripe tomatoes (jitomates)
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- broth from the pot
- chicken sweetbreads
- testicles (criadillas)
- ham
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread and toast them; set a few aside and fry them in lard along with the fideos so they turn golden, then remove from heat
2. Fry 2 ripe tomatoes, a little onion, garlic, and parsley, frying the garlic first then the rest
3. Once fried, add the broth from the pot
4. When seasoned, remove from heat and place the fideos in another pot
5. Take some chicken sweetbreads and testicles, cook them and make slices, placing them on top of the toasted bread that is not fried
6. Add the broth with a spoon and place over two low fires
7. Garnish with ham, slices of egg, testicles, and parsley leaves, ensuring it remains somewhat loose
