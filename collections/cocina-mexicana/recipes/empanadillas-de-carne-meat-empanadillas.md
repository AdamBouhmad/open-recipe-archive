## Ingredients

- Three pounds of flour
- One pound of melted and cooled butter
- A little salted water
- A little settled teqnesquite
- Ground meat filling
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of flour with one pound of melted and cooled butter
2. Add a little salted water and a little settled teqnesquite to soften the dough
3. Once all is well incorporated and the dough is manageable, extend the portions of each empanadilla separately with a rolling pin
4. Fill with ground meat
5. Fold and crimp the edges
6. Fry in butter
7. Remove from the oil and dust with powdered sugar
