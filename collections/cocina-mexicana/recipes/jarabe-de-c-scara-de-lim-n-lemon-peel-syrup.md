## Ingredients

- six ounces of fresh yellow lemon peels
- two quartillos of warm water at sixty degrees
- white sugar
- sugar lumps rubbed on lemon peel exterior

## Directions

1. Place six ounces of fresh yellow lemon peels in a glass vessel with two quartillos of warm water at sixty degrees
2. Leave it infusing for twelve hours at gentle heat
3. Strain it with expression and filter the strained liquid
4. Add double its weight in white sugar, which is dissolved in a covered vessel in a bain-marie, stirring from time to time
5. When the syrup is made and cooled, aromatize it by adding sugar lumps that have been rubbed on the exterior of lemon peel so they are impregnated with the aromatic essential oil
