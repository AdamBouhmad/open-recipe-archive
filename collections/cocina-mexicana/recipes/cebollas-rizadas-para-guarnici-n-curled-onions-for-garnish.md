## Ingredients

- ten large, round onions
- butter
- half an ounce of sugar
- salt
- one quarter pint of stock
- white wine

## Directions

1. Clean ten large round onions so that nothing is cut from the head so they remain whole
2. Place them side by side in a pan where butter has been melted over the fire
3. Add half an ounce of sugar, a little salt, and one quarter pint of stock, allowing them to cook over gentle fire
4. When they are nearly cooked, allow the stock to reduce to a jelly
5. When cooked and of good color, arrange them around a piece of beef or any other entrée
6. Pour over them the crust or drippings that will have stuck to the pan, loosening it with a little stock, or better yet, white wine
