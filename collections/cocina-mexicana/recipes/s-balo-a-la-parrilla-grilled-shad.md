## Ingredients

- shad
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- butter sauce
- capers
- sorrel essence

## Directions

1. After gutting and washing the shad, scale it, dry it and let it drain between two cloths
2. Place in a plate with salt, pepper and a glass of oil
3. Turn several times in this sauce for an hour before serving
4. Place immediately on the grill at gentle heat
5. At the moment of serving to the table, cover with butter sauce, sprinkled with capers on top, or with sorrel essence
