## Ingredients

- fat beans
- tequesquite
- pork meat pieces
- chicharrón de carne
- lard
- chopped onion
- dried broad chile, desveined, soaked and ground
- salt
- grated or fresh ground cheese
- bean cooking broth
- lettuce leaves, tender
- avocado, peeled and sliced
- radishes, chopped
- small chiles
- tornachiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Give the beans an initial cooking with tequesquite, and after washing, cook them again with pieces of pork meat and chicharrón de carne (which can be omitted if desired).
2. Fry chopped onion in lard, and when it browns, fry there the desveined, soaked, and ground dried broad chile, seasoning with salt.
3. Add the beans with the meat and chicharrón, letting everything refry, and then add grated or fresh ground cheese and the broth in which the beans and meat cooked.
4. To serve, decorate with tender lettuce leaves, peeled avocado slices, chopped radishes, small chiles, tornachiles, and olives.
