## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt dissolved in a little water
- half a pound of butter
- ten eggs, whites and yolks

## Directions

1. Make a well in the flour and add the dissolved salt, butter, and eggs
2. If the dough is not very soft, add more eggs
3. Combine the flour with the rest and knead with your fists
4. Collect and knead four times in succession
5. Place on a floured board or dish and leave for twelve hours
6. Then shape the escaldados
