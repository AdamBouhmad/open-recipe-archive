## Ingredients

- wings, sweetbreads, crests, necks, or livers of hen
- butter
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- parsley
- hazelnut sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry the wings, sweetbreads, crests, necks, or livers in butter with onion and tomato
2. Pound together garlic, pepper, salt, cloves, cinnamon, and parsley
3. When cooked and the sauce has reduced, add hazelnut sauce
4. Let it boil briefly
5. Add a little lemon juice
