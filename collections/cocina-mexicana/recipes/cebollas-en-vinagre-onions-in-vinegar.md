## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- vinegar
- herbs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean by removing the tail and head
2. Leave in plenty of salt for three days
3. Remove from the water they have released
4. Place in a glass jar with plenty of vinegar, herbs and pepper
5. Cover the jar carefully so that air does not enter
6. Leave for eight days after which they can be eaten
