## Ingredients

- large cooked potatoes
- good cheese, grated
- bread, grated
- pepper
- flour
- egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Peel cooked large potatoes
2. Cut off a little from the crown and hollow out with an appropriate instrument
3. Fill with good grated cheese mixed with a third part grated bread and a little pepper
4. Cover with the crown that was removed
5. Dust with flour and coat in beaten egg
6. Fry them
7. After frying, serve dry or stew in pipián, spiced sauce, or other broth
