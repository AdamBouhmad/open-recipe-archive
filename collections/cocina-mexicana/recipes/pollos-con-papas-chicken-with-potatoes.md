## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Potatoes
- Fried bread
- Water
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipes
2. Grind potatoes with egg and fried bread slices
3. Fry together with the meat
4. Add water, oregano, thyme, ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
5. When serving, add a little sherry wine
6. The broth should remain thick
7. Garnish as the previous recipes
