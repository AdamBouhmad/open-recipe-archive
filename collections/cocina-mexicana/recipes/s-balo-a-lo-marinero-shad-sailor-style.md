## Ingredients

- shad
- light broth
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the shad in light broth
2. Pass this broth through a silk sieve
3. Knead four ounces of butter with a little flour, and moisten with the broth
4. Place this sauce to cook, taking care to stir it
