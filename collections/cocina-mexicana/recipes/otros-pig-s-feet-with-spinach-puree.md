## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- sausage
- pork caul
- salt
- pepper
- spinach

## Directions

1. After cooking, debone the feet
2. Remove the casing from the sausage
3. Wrap the feet in pork caul
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Place on the grill
6. When cooked, serve with a spinach puree
