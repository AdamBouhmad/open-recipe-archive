## Ingredients

- clarified syrup of medium point with four pounds of white sugar
- two egg yolks
- two chicken breasts, ground
- three pounds of almonds, ground
- amber pastilles
- musk pastilles

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup of medium point with four pounds of white sugar
2. Add two egg yolks
3. Add two ground chicken breasts and three pounds of ground almonds
4. Add amber and musk pastilles in proportionate quantity
5. When it reaches the proper consistency, place the paste on layers of marquesote until the platter is filled
