## Ingredients

- corn flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Place the desired amount of corn flour in a pot and add the necessary milk so it is as liquid as atole and very thin
2. Put over fire and stir constantly with a stick until it cooks, which is known when the smell of raw corn is no longer perceived
3. Continue stirring until it forms a ball of firm dough, from which small loaves are made, which are cut with a thin string forming webs
