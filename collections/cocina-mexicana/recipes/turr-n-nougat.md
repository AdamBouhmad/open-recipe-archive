## Ingredients

- almonds, pine nuts, hazelnuts, walnuts and peanuts, or only one of these things, all toasted
- syrup and honey

## Directions

1. Toast the nuts
2. Mix them with syrup and honey brought to the proper point
3. The result is a solid and hard matter that is very savory
