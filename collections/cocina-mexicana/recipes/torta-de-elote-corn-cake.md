## Ingredients

- White tender corn
- Milk
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon
- Butter
- Four eggs
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Slice the white tender corn and cook the kernels in milk
2. After draining, grind it
3. Mix in a little sugar and ground cinnamon and the amount of butter desired
4. Beat four eggs as if for frying (this amount is for six to eight ears of corn)
5. Coat the baking dish with butter, add part of the beaten egg, then the corn atole and cover with the remaining egg
6. Cook over two fires and when half golden, add powdered sugar on top, letting it cook well
