## Ingredients

- chicken
- rooster combs and kidneys
- egg
- ham slices
- lemon juice
- veal slices
- onions
- carrots
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- concentrated broth
- rice
- good broth
- butter
- mushrooms
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Empty and prepare the chicken as in the previous article and fill it with a mixture of rooster combs and kidneys (see page 366), bound with egg.
2. Cover the extremities with ham slices so that the filling does not escape, and thus prepared, place in a casserole with lemon juice, slices of ham below and above, some slices of veal, three onions, two sliced carrots, two cloves, a bay leaf, a little thyme and two large spoonfuls of concentrated broth.
3. Place the casserole over fire, three-quarters of an hour being sufficient for cooking if it is small.
4. Pass the broth in which the chicken cooked through a silk sieve, and add one pound of very clean and well-washed rice, adding good broth if needed.
5. Let the rice boil for a quarter hour and place it to drain on a hair sieve.
6. Butter a casserole of appropriate size, place in the middle a wheel of dough with rice on top and arrange the chicken so it is not visible, filling the casserole with rice.
7. One hour before serving, make it take color as with the gâteau of rice (see page 370), placing fire on top of the lid.
8. At the moment of serving, turn over the rice cake, remove the dough wheel and empty the interior of the cake without disturbing the edges or bottom, putting inside a spoonful of rizzed sauce, mushrooms, the rest of the chicken stew, black pepper and a liaison of three eggs and as much butter as one egg.
