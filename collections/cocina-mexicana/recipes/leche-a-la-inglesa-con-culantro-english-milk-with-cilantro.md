## Ingredients

- Four cuartillos of milk
- A handful of cilantro
- One cinnamon stick
- One lemon peel
- Sugar to taste
- Four chicken gizzards in powder

## Directions

1. Bring four cuartillos of milk to boil with a handful of whole cilantro, one cinnamon stick, and one lemon peel
2. When it becomes slightly thick, remove from heat and let it cool to lukewarm
3. Sweeten sufficiently with sugar
4. Add four chicken gizzards in powder
5. Stir well and strain through a napkin
6. Place over the steam of a pot so it sets
