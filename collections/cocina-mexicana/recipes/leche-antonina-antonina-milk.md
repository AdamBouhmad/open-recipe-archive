## Ingredients

- Almonds
- Rice
- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Clean the almonds and toast them on a griddle until well browned, and do the same with the rice but without washing
2. For one pound of almonds use half a pound of rice, and grind everything very finely
3. Strain with the same milk and add sugar and the necessary milk
4. Grind a little cinnamon and mix it into the milk before putting it on heat
5. Once it reaches medium point, add orange blossom water and leave until it reaches high point
