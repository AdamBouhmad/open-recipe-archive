## Ingredients

- whole or halved shad
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Serve whole or in halves
2. If to be roasted, gut and do not scale
3. Place to cook in light broth like salmon
4. When cooked, serve on a napkin garnished with fresh parsley
5. If to serve as an appetizer, scale and serve with different sauces such as caper sauce, oil sauce, or Italian sauce
