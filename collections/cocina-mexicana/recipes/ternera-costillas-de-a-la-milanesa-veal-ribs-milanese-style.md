## Ingredients

- loins of young veal
- salt
- pepper
- lemon
- olive oil
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. From the loins of young veal, cut thin slices of meat that runs across the rib until reaching it, ensuring the slices are as large as possible.
2. Once cut, disengage them from the spine bone so that only the ribs remain.
3. Place the ribs on a table and flatten with an iron mallet, crushing the meat without breaking it.
4. Coat with salt and considerable pepper, squeeze lemon all over and leave soaking in raw eating oil until the next day.
5. Heat a pan on the fire and fry in a little oil until they have fried a bit, then remove.
6. Squeeze a little lemon on them, dust with pepper, and roll in ground biscuit.
7. Place on the grill until they form a crust.
8. Serve hot.
