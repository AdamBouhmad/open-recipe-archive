## Ingredients

- carrots
- water from cooking carrots
- breadcrumbs
- cheese
- egg

## Directions

1. Cook some carrots.
2. Once cooked, grind them; the paste that results is moistened with water from the same in which they were cooked.
3. Mix in a little breadcrumbs and cheese in the same manner, and once everything is well mixed, add the beaten egg that is necessary.
4. From this mixture, take spoonfuls that are put to fry in butter and then drained.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
