## Ingredients

- ruffled sauce
- essence of poultry in the proportion of four tablespoons of essence to one of sauce
- finely chopped parsley
- fresh butter
- coarse pepper
- half a lemon

## Directions

1. Combine a little ruffled sauce with a quantity of poultry essence in the proportion of four tablespoons of essence to one of sauce
2. Place the mixture over a brisk fire
3. When it has reduced to half and at the moment of use, add one teaspoon of very finely chopped parsley that has been blanched beforehand
4. Add a little fresh butter and some coarse pepper
5. Beat the sauce with a spoon, causing it to foam, and let it fall back into the saucepan without allowing it to boil
6. Pour immediately over the dish to be served
