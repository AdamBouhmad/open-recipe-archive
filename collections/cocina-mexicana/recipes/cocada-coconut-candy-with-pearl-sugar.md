## Ingredients

- one pound and a half of sugar
- one pound of grated coconut
- pearl sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound and a half of sugar.
2. Add one pound of grated coconut to the syrup.
3. Let boil until it reaches the proper consistency.
4. Pour onto a platter and dust with pearl sugar before browning.
