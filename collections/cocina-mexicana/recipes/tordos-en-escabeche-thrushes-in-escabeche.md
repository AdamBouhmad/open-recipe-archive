## Ingredients

- thrushes
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- spices
- brine
- salt
- fat
- bladder or parchment

## Directions

1. Remove the head and legs from the thrushes
2. Put them on the grill or roaster to half toast
3. Place them in vinegar with bay leaves and plenty of spices
4. Put them afterwards in a vessel
5. Pour hot brine over them
6. Cover with a layer of fat
7. Close the vessel with a bladder or parchment
8. Store in a cool place; they will keep more than a year in this manner
