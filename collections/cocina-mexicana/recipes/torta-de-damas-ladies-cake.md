## Ingredients

- feet and loin of pork
- lamb tongues
- squab pigeons
- all ground spices
- salt
- tomato
- garlic, fried
- beaten eggs
- lard in small pieces

## Directions

1. Cook and chop finely the feet and loin of pork, lamb tongues and squab pigeons
2. Season with all ground spices, salt, fried tomato and garlic
3. Mix with beaten eggs and lard in small pieces inside
4. Grease a cake pan or casserole with lard
5. Pour all the mixture into it and cook the cake over two fires
6. Serve on a platter
