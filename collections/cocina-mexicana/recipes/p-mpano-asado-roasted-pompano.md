## Ingredients

- Pámpano
- Parsley, chopped
- Garlic, ground
- Oil
- Orange juice
- Pimienta (pepper) powder

## Directions

1. Open the pámpano into slices
2. Between the slices place chopped parsley, ground garlic, and oil applied with feathers
3. Thus prepared, roast on a grill
4. Place afterwards in a pan or casserole and fry, adding also orange juice, chopped garlic and parsley
5. Fry the pámpano there and serve with a broth composed of water, orange juice and pepper powder, which is good for all roasted fish
