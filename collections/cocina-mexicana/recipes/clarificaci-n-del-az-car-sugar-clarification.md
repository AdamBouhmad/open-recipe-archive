## Ingredients

- One egg yolk
- Water
- Six to seven pounds of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Beat one egg yolk very well with a little water
2. Add gradually a regular glass of cold water
3. In a vessel, put six to seven pounds of fine sugar
4. Dissolve the sugar with half of the water prepared with the egg yolk, which should be very thick
5. Place the vessel on the fire
6. Before skimming, let the sugar rise twice
7. Add the other half of the prepared water gradually
8. It is ready when the foam comes out completely white
9. Remove from fire and add a glass of cold water to the sugar
