## Ingredients

- veal tripe and organs, cleaned
- water
- salt
- lard
- ancho chiles, soaked
- cooked tomatoes
- fried bread in lard
- cumin
- xoconostles, peeled (optional)
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Clean the veal tripe, feet and fat tripe well
2. Cook with water and salt until very well cooked
3. Fry in lard the ancho chiles soaked and ground with cooked tomatoes, bread fried in lard and cumin
4. When well fried, add peeled xoconostles if desired
5. Add the pot of tripe
6. Cut everything into small pieces and finish seasoning over two fires
7. It should be broth-like but not watery
8. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top when serving
