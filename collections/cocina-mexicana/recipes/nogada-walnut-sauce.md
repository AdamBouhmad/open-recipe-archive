## Ingredients

- cien nueces (one hundred walnuts)
- agua fría (cold water)
- medio queso fresco (half fresh cheese)
- cuatro onzas de pepita de calabaza (four ounces of pumpkin seeds)
- migajon de una torta de pan (crumb of bread loaf)
- una cabeza de ajo asada (one roasted garlic head)
- cuatro ó cinco clavos (four or five cloves)
- cominos (cumin)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Peel the walnuts overnight and soak them in cold water
2. The next day, grind them
3. Once ground, separately grind half fresh cheese, four ounces of well-washed pumpkin seeds (to remove the green skin covering them), bread crumb soaked in milk, one roasted garlic head, four or five cloves, and what can be gathered with two fingers of cumin
4. Mix everything together with the corresponding salt
5. Serve this sauce separately with chiles filled with picadillo, garnished with pomegranate seeds and leafy parsley
