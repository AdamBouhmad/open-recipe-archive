## Ingredients

- Small, tender green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Lettuce, chopped
- Oil
- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Trout or sea bass, floured, dipped in egg, and fried in lard (optional alternative to poached eggs)

## Directions

1. Follow the same procedure as the previous recipe, with the difference that no ham is used.
2. Add plenty of oil.
3. Instead of moistening with broth, add hot water to make the broth.
4. Alternatively, instead of poached eggs, you may add portions of trout or sea bass that have been floured, dipped in egg, and fried in lard.
