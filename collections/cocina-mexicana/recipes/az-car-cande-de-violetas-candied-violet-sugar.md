## Ingredients

- 1 pound of clarified syrup
- 2 ounces (half a pound) of violet flowers, cleaned
- Sugar powder, passed through a silk sieve
- A fork

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of clarified syrup until it reaches the pearl or ball point
2. When it is half-cooled, add half a pound of cleaned violet flowers
3. Leave the pot for two hours over hot ashes so the flowers release all their moisture
4. Strain through a sieve and remove the syrup from the flowers
5. Dust the flowers with powdered sugar that has been passed through a silk sieve, rubbing gently with your hand to dry them
6. Return to a sieve and place in the oven until the next morning
7. Sieve again to separate the sugar from the flowers
8. Boil three pounds of clarified syrup until it reaches the blown point, with the mold prepared
9. Pour the syrup into the mold when it reaches the said point, garnishing the surface with the flower sugar without making the layer too thick
10. Support it from above with a fork so the flowers take the sugar well, ensuring it is completely covered
11. Leave the mold five days in the oven without giving it a very strong temperature
12. After this time, strain to separate the syrup
13. When cooled, remove the candied sugar from the mold by turning it over forcefully onto a paper sheet on the table so the sugar falls out
