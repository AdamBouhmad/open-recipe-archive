## Ingredients

- fish such as barbel, carp or eel, cut into portions
- eight to ten crayfish without aperdigos, with legs removed
- small bulb onions, aperdigos and half-cooked
- mushrooms cut into small dice
- fried flour
- butter
- broth
- red wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a light sauce with fried flour in butter moistened with broth
2. Arrange the fish portions in a pot with small bulb onions, mushrooms and sprigs of fine herbs
3. Add the light sauce over the fish
4. Add red wine, salt, pepper and a piece of butter
5. Cook over a strong fire
6. Serve after pouring over the fried sediments from the pot
