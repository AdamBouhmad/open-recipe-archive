## Ingredients

- 6 ripe lemons
- 4 quartillos of common water
- 1 pound of martojada sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 4 quartillos of good red or white wine

## Directions

1. Cut very finely the peels of six very ripe lemons, and place them in infusion at atmospheric temperature for two hours, in four quartillos of common water
2. Mix with this water a pound of martojada sugar, stir until it is well dissolved and strain through a sieve
3. Beat four egg whites and pour them over the mixture, and incorporating everything exactly, add four quartillos of good red or white wine, and freeze
