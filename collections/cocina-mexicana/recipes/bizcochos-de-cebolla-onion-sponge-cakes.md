## Ingredients

- twelve egg whites
- twelve egg yolks
- twenty ounces of ground sugar
- twelve ounces of flour
- the zest of one whole lemon

## Directions

1. Beat well twelve egg whites, and separately beat twelve egg yolks with twenty ounces of ground sugar
2. Mix with twelve ounces of flour and the zest of one whole lemon
3. Once the batter is done, fill buttered molds with it and place in the oven
