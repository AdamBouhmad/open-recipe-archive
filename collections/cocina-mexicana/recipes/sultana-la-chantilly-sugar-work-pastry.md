## Ingredients

- Half a pound of royal sugar
- A little clear water
- As much virgin honey as a walnut
- A little lemon juice
- Cheese à la Chantilly, seasoned with powdered orange blossom sugar
- Official paste base (véase masa oficinal)

## Directions

1. Place the royal sugar in a small pot with a little clear water and as much virgin honey as a walnut
2. Bring the syrup to a boil, skim it, and let it reach the crack stage
3. When it reaches this degree of cooking, add a little lemon juice and stir the syrup well
4. Have ready a mold and an oil brush, as well as two silver forks joined together
5. Dip the handle of these forks in the syrup and make threads of syrup in the mold
6. Place the pot on embers and gradually form the sultana with the threads until it has enough consistency to detach from the mold
7. Once detached, add more threads of syrup to give it more strength
8. Prepare an official paste base with a pierced base decorated with small gatós in different ways
9. Place a cheese à la Chantilly in the middle, seasoned with powdered orange blossom sugar
10. Place the sultana on the edge of the small gatós and serve
