## Ingredients

- flour (as needed)
- 6 eggs
- 1 cuartillo milk (boiled with lemon peel)
- a little salt
- 4 ounces sugar
- a little butter
- a little orange blossom
- 4 egg yolks
- some macaroons and marzipans (crushed)

## Directions

1. Dissolve flour in 2 eggs; then add 4 more eggs and 1 cuartillo of milk that has been boiled with a bit of peeled lemon peel (remove the peel after boiling)
2. Add a little salt, 4 ounces of sugar, and a little butter
3. Cook this cream and when cooked, add a little orange blossom, 4 egg yolks, and some crushed macaroons and marzipans
4. Pour this composition onto a flat oiled mold and let cool
5. Cut into wheels, crowns, diamonds, or small cakes, coat with beaten egg and breadcrumbs, fry
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve
