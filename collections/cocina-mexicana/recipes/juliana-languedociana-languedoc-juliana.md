## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- kohlrabi or parsnips
- onions
- peas
- water or broth
- oil
- bread

## Directions

1. Prepare vegetables as in previous articles and sauté or lightly fry in a pan with half a quart of oil
2. Add them to pea substance, in water or in broth
3. Let cook two hours and pour over thin bread slices cut into strips
4. If water was used for cooking vegetables instead of substance or broth, add half a quart of raw oil when soaking the bread
