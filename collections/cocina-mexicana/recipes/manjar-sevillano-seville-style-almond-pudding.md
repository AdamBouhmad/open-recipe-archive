## Ingredients

- one chicken breast
- thirty quartillos of milk
- sixty egg yolks
- one pound of ground almonds
- seven pounds of sugar
- two and a half pounds of ground and sifted rice
- half a pound of fresh butter sliced

## Directions

1. With the above ingredients simmering add the fresh sliced butter
2. The consistency should be that of almond pudding and add orange blossom water
