## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- half a quarter-pint of white wine
- two ounces of powdered sugar
- four ounces of butter
- flour, as needed

## Directions

1. Mix twenty egg yolks, half a quarter-pint of white wine, two ounces of powdered sugar, and four ounces of butter
2. Knead everything with flour, adding flour gradually until the dough reaches regular consistency, so that it can be formed into biscuits and is neither hard nor soft
3. Form the biscuits and place on parchment papers
4. Sprinkle on top with dragées or sugar
5. Put in the oven to bake
