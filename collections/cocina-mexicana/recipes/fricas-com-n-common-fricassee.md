## Ingredients

- Mutton, pork, or chicken meat, raw
- Lard, butter, or ham
- Garlic
- Onions
- Parsley
- Mint
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- A little flour
- Broth
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Clove
- Pepper
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the raw meat into pieces
2. Put in a casserole or pan with lard, butter, or ham until it browns
3. Add dry garlic, onions, parsley, mint, thyme, marjoram, and a little flour
4. Brown everything
5. Add broth, cinnamon, nutmeg, clove, and pepper
6. When cooked, thicken with egg yolks
