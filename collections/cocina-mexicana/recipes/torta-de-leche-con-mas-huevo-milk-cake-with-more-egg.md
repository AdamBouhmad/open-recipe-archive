## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- small cinnamon sticks
- necessary sugar
- eight beaten eggs
- sponge cake (mamón), sliced
- butter for greasing
- ground sugar and cinnamon for topping or syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as Milk Cake with Egg, but use eight eggs instead of four
