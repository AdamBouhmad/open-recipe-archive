## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of turnip slices and potato slices
- Crusts of two loaves of bread
- Pepper
- Salt
- A good handful of chopped parsley
- Browned butter of very dark color

## Directions

1. Put in water equal quantities of turnip slices and potato slices, bread crusts, pepper and salt
2. Let it boil and cook
3. Pass through a strainer
4. Make a broth and add a good handful of chopped parsley
5. Remove the soup from the heat
6. At the moment of serving, pour over it browned butter of very dark color
