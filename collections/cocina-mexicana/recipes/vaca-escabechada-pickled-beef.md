## Ingredients

- three pounds of meat
- half a quartillo of oil
- two quartillos of good vinegar
- two heads of garlic roasted in the embers
- some crushed pepper
- thyme
- marjoram
- a little bay leaf
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice the meat and beat it well so that it remains the thickness of two fingers.
2. Fry the slices in oil, making sure they remain whitish and not toasted.
3. Put them in a pot with the vinegar, herbs, and the two whole, clean heads of garlic.
4. If the meat is cooked and the vinegar has not been consumed, distill it so that only the oil remains.
5. Add salt to taste and leave in the pot two or three days or more until it cools.
6. When serving, add more oil and Castilian vinegar.
7. Serve cold and garnish with olives, capers, and chiles.
