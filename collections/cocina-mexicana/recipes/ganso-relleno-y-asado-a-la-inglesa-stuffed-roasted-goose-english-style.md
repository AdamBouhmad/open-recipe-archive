## Ingredients

- One young goose with white fat
- The goose's liver, chopped
- Onions in small pieces
- Sage, chopped
- Butter
- Veal or beef juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous goose.
2. Fill the body with a mixture made with the goose's chopped liver, onions in small pieces and a pinch of chopped sage, all well mixed, sautéed in butter until it takes a golden color.
3. Place on the spit, and after roasting serve it with veal or beef juice.
