## Ingredients

- one cup water or milk
- anise
- butter
- one cup flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. To one cup of water or milk with a little anise and butter, once very hot add one cup of flour
2. When cold add the necessary eggs
3. Once cooked and kneaded, make them and drop into the butter and stir until it forms a ball
