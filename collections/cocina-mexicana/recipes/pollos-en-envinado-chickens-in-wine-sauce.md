## Ingredients

- ham
- lard
- chicken quarters
- wine
- vinegar
- salt
- water
- cinnamon
- cloves
- pepper
- saffron
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Chop ham into small pieces and fry well in lard
2. Partially cook the chicken quarters there
3. Add a portion of wine and another of vinegar with corresponding salt
4. When cooked, add water, ground cinnamon, cloves, pepper, saffron and a little sugar
5. Add whole raisins, almonds and toasted sesame seeds
6. Once boiling, taste to see if sweetness predominates; if so, add vinegar; if vinegar predominates, add sweetness
