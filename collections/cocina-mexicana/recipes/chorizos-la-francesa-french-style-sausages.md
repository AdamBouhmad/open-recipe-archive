## Ingredients

- marbled meat
- onions
- parsley
- garlic to taste

## Directions

1. Take a piece of marbled meat and chop it with onions, parsley, and a little garlic according to taste, seasoning appropriately.
2. Fill the intestines with this mixture, tying the ends.
3. Place in smoke in the chimney for several days.
4. When ready to eat, cook for two or three hours.
