## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- salt
- lard
- tocino
- garlic
- parsley, minced
- toasted garlic
- parsley
- pine nuts or almonds

## Directions

1. Take leg of mutton and cut into small pieces
2. Put in a pot with salt, lard, tocino, garlic and minced parsley
3. Before cooking, make a sauce with toasted garlic, parsley, and pine nuts or almonds
4. Let cook for a while
