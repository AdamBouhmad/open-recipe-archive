## Ingredients

- Four egg yolks
- One pound of ground sugar
- Two pounds of lard
- Two cups of water
- A pinch of salt
- Three pounds of flour
- Orange juice
- Oil
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- Abundant lard
- Fish broth
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers
- Candied citron
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Fish

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks in a bowl with one pound of ground sugar and two pounds of lard
2. Bring two cups of water to boil with a pinch of salt, remove from heat, and when the water is lukewarm, add the mixture and continue beating until it no longer sticks to the hand
3. Add three pounds of flour and knead everything together well for rolling out
4. Add orange juice and oil
5. Cut out the empanadas before filling them
6. For the filling, fry tomato, garlic, and onion with abundant lard, add a little fish broth, chiles, olives, capers, candied citron, almonds, and raisins
7. Add the fish with bones removed
8. Coat the empanadas with cold lard and cut with a mold before adding the filling
9. Fill, fold, and place in the oven
