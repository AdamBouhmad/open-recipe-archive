## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- ground sugar
- lemon zest or essence

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks well, and then add ground sugar in the proportion of about one ounce per yolk, a little lemon zest or essence of the same
2. When well mixed, form small mounds of this beaten mixture in the shape of yolks on a tin sheet; this should have sugar spread so they do not stick
