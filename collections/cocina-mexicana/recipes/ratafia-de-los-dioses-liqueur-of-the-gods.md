## Ingredients

- Refined catalán brandy
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Cloves
- Fine cinnamon
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one quartillo of refined catalán brandy with another of common water and one quarter of a quartillo of lemon juice
2. In this mixture infuse forty cloves and two adarmes of fine cinnamon
3. Put everything in a well-sealed bottle and keep for nine days
4. After this time, clarify one pound of very white sugar forming a high-point syrup and mix with the liqueur keeping the bottle well-sealed
5. After four to six days strain through a cloth and bottle
