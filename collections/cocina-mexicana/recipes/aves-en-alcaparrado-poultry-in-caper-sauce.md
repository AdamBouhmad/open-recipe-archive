## Ingredients

- Chiles, well roasted and seedless
- Raw garlic cloves
- Capers, ground
- Bread, toasted in lard
- Poultry broth or water
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Olives
- Chiles

## Directions

1. Grind well-roasted seedless chiles with raw garlic cloves
2. Fry well
3. Add a portion of capers ground with bread toasted in lard
4. Increase the sauce by adding poultry broth or water
5. Season with ground pepper, cloves, cinnamon and salt
6. Let thicken considerably
7. Add oil and vinegar
8. Increase with sliced olives and chiles
9. Serve thick
