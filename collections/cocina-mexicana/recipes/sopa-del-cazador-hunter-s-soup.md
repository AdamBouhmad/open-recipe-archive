## Ingredients

- one rabbit divided into pieces
- a piece of lard
- one cabbage
- some carrots
- onions
- parsley
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Put the rabbit pieces, lard, cabbage, carrots, onions, parsley, thyme, salt and pepper in a pot with water
2. Let it boil until everything is cooked
3. Moisten bread crusts with this broth, arranged as for natural soup
