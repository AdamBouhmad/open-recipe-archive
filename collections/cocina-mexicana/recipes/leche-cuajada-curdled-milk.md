## Ingredients

- Milk
- Egg yolks in the proportion of four per cup of milk
- Sugar to taste
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring milk to boil two or three times and remove from heat
2. Add egg yolks in the proportion of four for each cup of milk, sweetened to taste
3. Dissolve everything and strain through a sieve
4. Pour into a bowl and cook in a water bath, covering with a comal without fire so steam does not escape
5. When a straw comes out clean when tested, cover so it does not turn yellow, then sprinkle cinnamon on top
