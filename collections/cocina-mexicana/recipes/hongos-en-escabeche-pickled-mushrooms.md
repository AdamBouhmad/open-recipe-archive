## Ingredients

- large mushrooms
- oil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- garlic
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take the largest mushrooms, wash them well
2. Marinate in oil with salt and pepper
3. Keep there for the necessary time
4. Remove them and place on the grill, roast on both sides
5. When roasted, put them in a little oil in which garlic and chopped parsley have been fried
6. Place on a platter with that sauce
7. Dust with salt and coarse pepper
8. Serve
