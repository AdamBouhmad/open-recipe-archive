## Ingredients

- Carrots, cut in long wheels
- Onion
- Turnips
- Lettuce or escarole leaf
- Celery
- Sorrel
- Chervil
- Butter or oil
- Water
- Salt
- Two cloves
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut carrots, onion, and turnips into long wheels
2. Fry them with a lettuce or escarole leaf, celery, sorrel, and chervil in butter or oil
3. Cook everything in a quarter-liter of water, adding salt
4. When the liquid has been consumed, add more water and let it cook for a couple of hours
5. Season with two cloves and a little pepper
6. When the vegetables are cooked, strain the broth and moisten the soup, which can also be served without straining
