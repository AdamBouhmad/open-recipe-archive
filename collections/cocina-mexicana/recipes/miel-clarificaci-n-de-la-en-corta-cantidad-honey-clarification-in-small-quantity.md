## Ingredients

- one pound of honey
- one cuartillo of water
- one piece of burning charcoal or ember
- one piece of bread, well toasted on a grill

## Directions

1. Mix the honey with one cuartillo of water
2. Heat over gentle fire and remove the foam as it rises
3. If it boils too much, calm the boiling with a spoonful of water, using as little as possible
4. When no more foam appears, add a well-burning piece of charcoal or ember and a piece of well-toasted bread
5. Keep these two things in the honey for only five minutes, then remove with a skimming spoon, being careful not to leave any particles
6. Let the honey boil until it sticks to the fingers
7. For more perfect clarification, filter the honey through brown paper at the moment the charcoal has been removed, then return it to the fire to reach its final degree of cooking
