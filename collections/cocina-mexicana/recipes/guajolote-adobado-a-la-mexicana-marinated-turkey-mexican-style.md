## Ingredients

- guajolote
- laurel
- tomillo
- sal
- pimienta
- vinagre de Castilla
- ajos
- agua
- chile pesuilla
- chiltpotle
- manteca
- pulque
- chiles en vinagre
- queso añejo
- aceitunas

## Directions

1. Fry the raw turkey and cut into quarters
2. Once well fried, add to a pot with bay leaf, thyme, salt, pepper, Castilian vinegar and crushed garlic
3. Cook in little water, then remove if desired, or fry again and serve with chilote sauce
4. For the chilote sauce: toast pesuilla and chipotle chiles in lard, devein and set aside, then return the veins to the pan and add pulque, sliced chiles in vinegar, crushed aged cheese, olives and salt
