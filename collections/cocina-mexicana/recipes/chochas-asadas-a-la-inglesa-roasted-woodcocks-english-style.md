## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- ham, scraped in quantity about half the volume of the intestines
- parsley, chopped
- shallot, chopped
- salt
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Set aside the gizzard of the woodcocks, emptied by the back, and chop the other intestines
2. Mix with scraped ham in quantity about half its volume, a little chopped parsley and shallot, salt and pepper
3. Pass a skewer through the legs and fix it on both sides to the spit
4. Squeeze everything together and pass through a sieve or strain through a coarse strainer over the parts left in another pot with the breadcrumbs
5. Heat everything without letting it boil, add lemon juice and serve
6. When plating, place the toasts that received the fat, cut in the shape of a heart
