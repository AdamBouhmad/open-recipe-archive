## Ingredients

- Pork meat with fat
- Parsley, sufficient
- Onion, sufficient
- Garlic, sufficient
- Fine spices
- Cheese
- Pepper
- Salt to taste
- Clean casings

## Directions

1. Choose pork meat that has fat
2. Mince it with sufficient parsley, onion, and garlic
3. Season with fine spices and cheese, using pepper prominently
4. Add salt as needed
5. Fill clean casings with this mixture
6. Tie them at intervals of four to six fingers apart
7. Hang them in a chimney to smoke
8. Cook for four hours at a good boil
