## Ingredients

- One pound of well-worked veal pulp
- One pound of fat ham, cut into thick lards
- Onion slices
- One head of garlic, crushed
- Carrot slices
- A little vinegar
- Pork fat or lard
- Pepper
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Cooked and ground spinach
- Finely chopped onion
- Chopped garlic
- Salt
- Red wine
- Breadcrumb figurines

## Directions

1. Prepare a well-worked veal pulp, cutting it like a ball or wheel at the thickest part
2. Divide it in half and flatten with the flat of a machete
3. Cut one pound of fat ham into thick lards and lard the meat so the ends stick out the sides
4. Leave to soak in cold water
5. Cook with onion slices, crushed garlic head, carrot slices, and a little vinegar
6. When the meat is cooked, place in a casserole without disturbing the larding, on pork fat or lard so it does not stick
7. Finish cooking on two gentle fires or in an oven, having previously seasoned with a little pepper, clove, and vinegar
8. Fry cooked and ground spinach in lard, also frying finely chopped onion and chopped garlic in the same
9. Season with salt, add covered red wine
10. Serve the fricandó over this sauce, adorning the contour with breadcrumb figurines fried in lard
