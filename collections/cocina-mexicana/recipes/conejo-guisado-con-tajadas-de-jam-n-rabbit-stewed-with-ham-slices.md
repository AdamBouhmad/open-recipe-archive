## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Roasted tomato
- Good oil
- Sugar
- Ham slices
- Hot water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit as described, leave it for one day in vinegar, cut it into regular portions and brown it in butter with chopped tomato and garlic
2. When it reaches medium color, add a roasted and skinned tomato well ground, a little good oil, three or four ground sugar lumps, some ham slices and hot water, enough to cover the meat well
3. Cook over gentle heat, placing a comal with greater heat on top
4. When cooked, let the broth thicken and serve
