## Ingredients

- Ducks, cooked
- Butter
- Roasted tomatoes
- Garlic
- Toasted cilantro
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Fried bread toasts
- Cooked onions
- Salt
- Pot broth

## Directions

1. Fry the cooked ducks in butter and remove immediately from the fat
2. In the fat, place roasted tomatoes, garlic, toasted cilantro, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg, and ground fried bread toasts
3. When everything is fried, add cooked and separated onions, salt, and pot broth
4. Place the ducks in this mixture
5. Let simmer until the broth reaches good seasoning and is not too thick
