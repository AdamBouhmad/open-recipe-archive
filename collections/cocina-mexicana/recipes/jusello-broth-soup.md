## Ingredients

- meat broth
- parsley
- cheese
- eggs
- oil or lard

## Directions

1. Lightly fry or soften chopped parsley in oil or lard
2. Fry eggs in the same place
3. Before the eggs set completely, cover with grated cheese
4. Pour in immediately broth from the pot or other meat and let everything boil together
