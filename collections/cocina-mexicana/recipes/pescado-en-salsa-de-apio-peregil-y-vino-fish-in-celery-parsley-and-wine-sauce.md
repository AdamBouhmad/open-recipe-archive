## Ingredients

- celery
- parsley
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- capers
- jerez wine
- cooked fish

## Directions

1. Chop celery and parsley finely and fry in oil
2. Add pepper, salt, capers, and a little jerez wine
3. Add cooked fish when the sauce has thickened somewhat
4. Move to gentle heat
5. When well seasoned, remove and serve
