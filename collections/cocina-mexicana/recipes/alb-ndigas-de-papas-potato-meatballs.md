## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked and reduced to paste
- ground minced leftovers or meat scraps
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- small onions, chopped
- shallots, chopped
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- flour

## Directions

1. Reduce the cooked potatoes to a paste as in the German-style article.
2. Mix well with an equal quantity of ground minced leftovers or meat scraps.
3. Season with butter, salt, pepper, parsley, chopped small onions and shallots.
4. Bind everything with some egg yolks and form meatballs of regular thickness.
5. Coat them with beaten egg whites, roll them in flour and fry them.
6. They can be served adorned with parsley or with chile or tomato sauce.
