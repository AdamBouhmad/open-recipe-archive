## Ingredients

- Thistle plants
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Separate all the leaves
2. Cut the thistles six inches long, using the whitest parts
3. Round the stalk
4. Boil in water with sufficient salt
5. When cooked, let them cool
6. Remove all membranes and fibers
7. Serve with any of the explained sauces
