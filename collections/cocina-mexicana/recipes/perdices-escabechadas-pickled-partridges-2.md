## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Bay leaves
- Garlic
- Spices
- Brine

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges.
2. Fry them in oil.
3. When half-fried, place them in vinegar with bay leaves, pounded garlic, and spices.
4. Place in a vessel.
5. Pour brine over them and top with oil.
6. Seal hermetically.
7. Store in a cool place.
