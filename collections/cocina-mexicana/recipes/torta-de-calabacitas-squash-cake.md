## Ingredients

- cooked squash
- cheese
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. After the squash is cooked, remove from water, chop well and mix with some grated cheese, bread crumbs, and sugar
2. Fry this mixture in a pan with hot butter, letting the cake cook and set carefully so it does not burn
