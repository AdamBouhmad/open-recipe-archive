## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- orange blossom
- butter

## Directions

1. Break twelve eggs, separating yolks from whites.
2. Add twelve ounces of powdered sugar and a little orange blossom to the yolks, beating everything together.
3. Fry in a pan with a piece of butter.
4. Empty the omelet onto a silver plate placed over embers, covering it with a cloche oven.
5. Dust the omelet with powdered sugar and serve promptly when well puffed and of good color.
