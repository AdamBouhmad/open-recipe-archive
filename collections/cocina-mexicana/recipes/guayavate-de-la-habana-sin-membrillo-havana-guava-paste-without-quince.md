## Ingredients

- Ripe guavas
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Choose ripe and not overly rotten guavas
2. Peel and halve them raw
3. Remove the hearts and wash immediately so no seeds or pits remain
4. Pass the hearts through a fine sieve so only the paste passes without any pits
5. Have prepared and clarified a syrup of regular consistency, neither too thin nor too thick, measuring equal amounts of syrup and guava
6. Add the halved guavas to the cold syrup, which is then placed over heat with them and brought to boil until very thick
7. Mix in what was passed through the sieve and begin to beat lengthwise
8. Continue beating until the guava paste reaches the proper point, which is known by taking a little with a small spoon and cooling it; if it hardens and separates from the arm when struck, it is a sign that it has reached the proper consistency
