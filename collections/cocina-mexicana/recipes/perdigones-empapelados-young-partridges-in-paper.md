## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Salt
- Butter
- Garlic, potatoes, parsley and mushrooms
- Flour
- Broth
- White wine
- Ham

## Directions

1. Divide completely in half and sprinkle with salt
2. Fry in butter until cooked
3. Remove and fry garlic, potatoes, parsley and mushrooms (all chopped) in the remaining butter, dusted with flour
4. Add a little broth and white wine
5. When the broth thickens, pour it over the young partridges
6. Cover each half with a slice of ham
7. Wrap in oiled paper and place on a grill over gentle heat
