## Ingredients

- raw fish without bones
- fried seasoning base
- raw egg
- cooked egg
- lemon
- soaked bread
- oil
- parsley
- broth
- spices
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop raw fish without bones.
2. Place in a pot and add fried seasoning base, raw and cooked egg, lemon, soaked and ground bread in fine pieces, oil, and chopped parsley.
3. Beat everything together.
4. Form meatballs and drop into boiling broth.
5. Make the broth by frying all the seasoning base with the spices.
6. Once fried, add a little water and salt.
7. Once the meatballs are cooked, when removing them add a little oil, vinegar, and chopped parsley.
