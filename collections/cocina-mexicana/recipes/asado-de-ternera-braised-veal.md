## Ingredients

- Solid veal
- Garlic pieces
- Ham
- Lard
- Water
- Laurel
- Thyme
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Herbs
- Tomato slices
- Onion head
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take solid veal and lard it with pieces of garlic and ham.
2. Brown the meat in lard.
3. Once browned, add water, laurel, thyme, clove, cinnamon, pepper, loose herbs, tomato slices, an onion head, and a little vinegar.
4. Let it cook over good heat, ensuring it retains some sauce.
