## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- fine salt
- custard
- fine cinnamon
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a skillet
2. Add eggs seasoned with fine salt
3. Stir greatly
4. When already stirred, add a glass of custard, which is also stirred
5. Dust with a little fine cinnamon
6. If desired, when serving, a little lemon juice may be added
