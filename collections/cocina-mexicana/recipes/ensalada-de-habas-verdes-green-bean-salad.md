## Ingredients

- green beans
- spearmint leaves
- onion heads
- tender peas
- tequezquite
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- roasted chiles
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano

## Directions

1. Shell the beans over raw, adding spearmint leaves and onion heads
2. Cook beans in salted water
3. Cook tender peas with a little tequezquite, grind them and strain
4. When beans have thickened somewhat, mix in the pea substance
5. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
6. Let boil stirring so they do not burn until well thickened and beans are broken
7. Set aside from fire, empty into platters and let sit for ten or twelve hours until set
8. Once set, cut the mass into strips half a finger wide and two or three fingers long
9. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and let dry
10. When dry, chop roasted, peeled and deveined chiles or those cooked in salted water
11. Mix everything with oil and vinegar with salt, putting chopped onion and ground oregano on top
