## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Lard
- Ground garlic
- Finely chopped onion
- Vinegar
- Chicken broth
- Fresh pork blood, or if not available, a piece of blood sausage ground with salt
- Nutmeg
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken and quarter it
2. Fry it in lard
3. Add ground garlic and finely chopped onion
4. Once everything is fried, add vinegar and a little of the chicken broth
5. Add either fresh pork blood or a piece of blood sausage ground with salt, nutmeg, clove, cinnamon, pepper, and ginger
6. Add raisins and almonds
7. Let everything simmer until the broth is seasoned, taking care that it is neither too thick nor too thin
