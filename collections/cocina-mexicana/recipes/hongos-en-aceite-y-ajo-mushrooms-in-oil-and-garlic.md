## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- oil
- salt
- coarse pepper powder
- garlic
- parsley
- bread slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the mushrooms well, cut them into small pieces
2. Fry in a little oil with salt, coarse pepper powder and chopped garlic
3. When they take on color, add two handfuls of chopped parsley, toasts of mollete bread and the juice of one lemon
