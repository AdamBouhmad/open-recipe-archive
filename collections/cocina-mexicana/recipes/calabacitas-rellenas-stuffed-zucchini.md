## Ingredients

- zucchini
- salt
- picadillo
- lard
- tomato sauce
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- ham

## Directions

1. Cook zucchini with salt
2. Once cooked, remove the seeds from one side and dry
3. Fill with picadillo
4. Heat lard in a pot and cook them
5. Pour tomato sauce over the top
6. Place on heat and let the broth reduce until little remains
7. Add salt, sugar, vinegar, raisins, almonds, olives, and ham slices
