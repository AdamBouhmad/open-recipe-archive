## Ingredients

- ingredients same as chorizo
- anise cooked in white wine

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as chorizos but do not add as much hot pepper
2. Add a little anise cooked in white wine
3. Taste to know if well seasoned
4. Stuff as described for chorizos
