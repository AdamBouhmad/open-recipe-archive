## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- egg whites
- orange blossom water
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fatten half a pound of rice in milk and add a piece of butter
2. As soon as it is very thick, pour into an earthenware vessel to cool
3. Then add eight egg yolks and sugar in sufficient quantity, four well-beaten egg whites, and one or two spoonfuls of orange blossom water
4. Spread butter on a pot or pan, dust all over with bread crumbs
5. Already placed in the little stove or camp oven, pour the rice into it
6. Surround with heat, placing it also on the lid
7. Having taken good color, pour again into a platter
