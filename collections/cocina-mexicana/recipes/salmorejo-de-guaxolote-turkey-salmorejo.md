## Ingredients

- Guaxolote bien gordo (fat turkey)
- Vino (wine)
- Criadillas de tierra (truffles)
- Hongos (mushrooms)
- Anchoas (anchovies)
- Sal (salt)
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Caldo de sustancia de vaca (beef broth)
- Zumo de naranja (orange juice)

## Directions

1. Half-roast a fat turkey on a spit
2. Divide it into appropriate portions or pieces
3. Place them in a pan with wine, chopped truffles and mushrooms, some anchovies, salt, and pepper
4. When everything is cooked appropriately, bind the sauce with beef broth
5. After defatting it, serve the turkey with orange juice or ham sauce
