## Ingredients

- Scraps of fowl and veal
- Butter
- Two or three tablespoons of flour
- Pot broth
- One carrot
- Onion
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Pepper
- Salt
- Clove
- Half a bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Brown scraps of fowl and veal with butter
2. Add two or three tablespoons of flour and soak in pot broth
3. Add a carrot, onion, thyme, bay leaf, pepper, salt and clove
4. Boil and add half a bottle of white wine
5. After two hours remove from the fire and strain to separate the bones
