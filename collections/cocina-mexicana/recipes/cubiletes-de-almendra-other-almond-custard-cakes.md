## Ingredients

- Seven egg yolks
- Twelve egg whites
- Half pound of almonds, ground
- One fistful of sifted sugar
- Butter
- Syrup
- Cloves and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat seven egg yolks and twelve egg whites separately until stiffened
2. Mix together and combine with half pound of almonds and one fistful of sifted sugar
3. Pour the well-mixed dough into butter-lined molds
4. Bake in the oven until cooked, then unmold into syrup boiled with cloves and cinnamon or one of these two things at nearly full point
5. Let the syrup reduce on low heat
6. When serving, decorate as the previous ones
