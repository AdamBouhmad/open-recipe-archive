## Ingredients

- nopales (cactus paddles)
- salt
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- wide chiles
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove all spines from the nopales and two or three fingers of growth from their base
2. Cut them lengthwise or leave whole for certain dishes, or cut them into small squares
3. Cook them in water with necessary salt in a copper pot or with porous charcoal, as this helps release the viscosity well
4. When well cooked, brown chopped garlic in a little lard
5. Add chopped onion
6. Add necessary water, fine salt, and some whole wide chiles
7. When the broth has reduced and taken on a regular color, remove from heat
8. Add vinegar and oil to taste
