## Ingredients

- Lard
- Tomatoes, finely chopped
- Garlic, finely chopped
- Onion, finely chopped
- Strips of cooked ham
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- Peas
- Cooked artichokes
- Pepper
- Clove
- Saffron
- Flour browned in butter and darkened without burning
- Chicken broth
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped tomatoes, garlic, and onion in lard
2. Add strips of cooked ham, salt, chopped parsley, peas, and cooked artichokes
3. Add ground pepper, clove, and saffron
4. Add flour that has been browned in butter and darkened without burning
5. Put in the chicken with its broth
6. Let everything simmer until the broth is somewhat thick
7. When serving, add oil and garnish with chiles and olives
