## Ingredients

- 2 cuartillos of milk
- 4.5 pounds crushed sugar
- 6 ounces ground and sifted starch
- 1 chicken breast, ground
- orange blossom water
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add 4.5 pounds of crushed sugar to 2 cuartillos of milk
2. Strain and add 6 ounces of ground and sifted starch and 1 ground chicken breast
3. Place over fire and stir very carefully so it does not break
4. As soon as it comes off the sides of the cazo, add orange blossom water and let it boil with it
5. After cooling, dust the top with ground cinnamon
