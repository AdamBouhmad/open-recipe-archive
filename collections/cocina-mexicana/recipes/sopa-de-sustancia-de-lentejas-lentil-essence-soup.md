## Ingredients

- One quart of lentils
- Broth
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Two cloves
- Bread slices, toasted

## Directions

1. Cook one quart of lentils in broth
2. Add two carrots, two onions and two cloves
3. When well cooked, break them up and strain
4. With the resulting broth, soak the toasted bread slices placed in a tureen that will be put on the heat
5. Remove as soon as it begins to boil
