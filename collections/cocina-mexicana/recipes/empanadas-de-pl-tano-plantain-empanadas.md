## Ingredients

- semi-green plantain, cooked
- minced filling

## Directions

1. Cook the plantain semi-green, grind it dry and make tortillas from this dough on paper
2. Fill with minced filling as with chiles
3. Join the paper so it forms an empanada and fry afterwards in lard so they are very golden
