## Ingredients

- Fish
- Lard
- Garlic
- Ground cumin
- Ground pepper
- Tomato
- Green chile
- Onion
- Salt
- Cooked onion
- Oregano
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After the fish is cooked, fry it in lard with garlic, ground cumin and pepper, plenty of tomato, green chile and onion in large pieces, mashing the tomato and chile with the back of the spoon
2. After everything is fried well, and when the fish is ready to go to the table, add salt, cooked onion, oregano, oil and vinegar on top
