## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Salt
- Aged cheese
- Carrot flesh, cooked
- Beaten egg

## Directions

1. Well cleaned carrots, cook them with salt, and once cooked, split them in half and remove the heart
2. Grind a little aged cheese with some of the flesh of the cooked carrots, and with this mixture stuff them, covering the filling with the same pulp of the carrots
3. Bread them with beaten egg and fry them
4. Then serve them dry with salt and pepper powder on top, or stew them in sauce or chile with tomato
