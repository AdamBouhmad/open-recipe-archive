## Ingredients

- Quails
- Ham slices
- Grape leaves

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean and singe the quails at the flame.
2. Tie and cover each with a slice of ham and a grape leaf.
3. Place on the spit and arrange this on the roasting frame.
4. When cooked, untie and serve.
