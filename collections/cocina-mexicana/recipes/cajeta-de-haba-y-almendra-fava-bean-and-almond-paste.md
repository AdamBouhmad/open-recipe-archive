## Ingredients

- habas (fava beans)
- azúcar (sugar)
- leche (milk)
- almidón (starch)
- almendras martajadas (pounded almonds)

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of fava beans in warm water
2. Clean them, place in a napkin so they cook this way, and grind afterwards
3. Dissolve five pounds of sugar in five quartillos of milk and mix with one ounce of sifted starch, strain and cook with the beans
4. After cooking, add the rest: one pound of pounded almonds, and give it the corresponding point
