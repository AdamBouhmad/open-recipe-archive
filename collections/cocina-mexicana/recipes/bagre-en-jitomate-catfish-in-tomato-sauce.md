## Ingredients

- catfish
- ashes
- water
- lemon juice
- salt
- flour
- raw tomatoes
- cilantro
- pepper
- poblano chile
- oil
- parsley
- chiles
- olives
- cooked onions

## Directions

1. Take the catfish and place it in a basin with plenty of ashes and little water
2. After a long time has passed, rub with a small brush until it becomes soft
3. Rinse with clean water, dry and slice
4. Cook and then add lemon juice and salt
5. After enough time has passed, dry again
6. Coat in flour and fry until very golden
7. Once done, set aside for another day
8. Grind raw tomatoes, a little cilantro, pepper and a little clear, a poblano chile
9. Grind and fry all of this and add a little water
10. Once it has come to a boil, add the fish to boil again
11. Garnish with chopped parsley, small chiles, olives, cooked onions and plenty of cooking oil
