## Ingredients

- quinces
- two tablespoons of jelly
- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of quince
- one apple

## Directions

1. Cook quinces and remove the heads and let drain
2. Strain through clean cloth, rubbing with the hand
3. Add two tablespoons of jelly
4. To make the jelly, grind the quince cores, and to two pounds of sugar add one pound of quince and one apple strained through cloth
5. Bring the syrup to the hard crack stage, remove from heat and vigorously add the quince
6. Beat until it breaks up, add two tablespoons of jelly, pour into glasses and place in the sun for three days, after which it can be eaten
