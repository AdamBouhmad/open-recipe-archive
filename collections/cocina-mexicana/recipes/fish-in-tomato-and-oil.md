## Ingredients

- Fish
- Oil
- Garlic
- Hen
- Butter
- Tomato
- Pepper
- Water
- Salt
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Onion

## Directions

1. One day before eating the fish, soak it so it soaks well
2. The following day wash it and parboil it
3. Fry it in oil with much chopped garlic
4. Fry hen also chopped in equal parts of oil and lard
5. Then add cooked and ground tomato together with garlic and pepper
6. While frying, add water, salt and let it cook
7. Let it boil, and near the end add small chiles, olives and cooked onion
