## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- pine nuts
- clarified syrup
- egg yolks
- ground almonds
- wine
- orange blossom water
- sponge cake (mamón)
- raisins
- almonds
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas and remove their skins
2. Grind together with pine nuts
3. Sift the pine nuts and chickpeas through a sieve
4. Add to well-clarified syrup with egg yolks and ground almonds
5. Cook until it takes body
6. Add wine and orange blossom water
7. Layer this paste over sponge cake soaked in syrup and wine
8. Sprinkle with cinnamon powder
9. Garnish with raisins, almonds and pine nuts
