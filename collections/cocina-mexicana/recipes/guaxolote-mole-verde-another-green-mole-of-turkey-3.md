## Ingredients

- pumpkin seeds, peeled
- water and salt
- green chiles
- cumin seeds
- lard
- turkey broth
- cooked turkey in quarters or portions
- pork jowl, optional
- corn masa, optional

## Directions

1. After peeling the pumpkin seeds and squeezing them with your hands, put them in salt water
2. Remove from there, lightly brown them and regrind them dry with green chiles and cumin
3. Mix everything and fry in lard, adding a little turkey broth
4. Add the broth again after frying, then put the quarters or portions of cooked turkey and pork jowl if desired
5. Some add a little corn masa for thickening
6. Taste with a separate spoon from the one used to stir to avoid breaking it
7. Stir by putting the spoon to the bottom and passing it from one side to the other without removing it, the same way as dulce de cajetas
