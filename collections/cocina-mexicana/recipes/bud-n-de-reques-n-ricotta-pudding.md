## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- one and a half pounds of ricotta
- one quarter of ground biscuit
- butter
- one and a half pounds of syrup at high point
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almonds

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg yolks and add the whites
2. Cut into thin slices one and a half pounds of ricotta, grind one quarter of biscuit and grind everything together with the egg
3. Spread a pan or pudding mold with plenty of butter, pour the egg and cook over two fires, taking care it does not burn
4. Make one and a half pounds of syrup at high point and put the cake in it
5. To preserve it must boil little and over slow fire
6. Decorate on top with raisins, pine nuts and almonds
