## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- salt
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in small amount of water, allowing it to be consumed
2. Once cooked, remove and cut into slices
3. Place on a plate
4. Serve cold with oil, vinegar, and whole grains of salt
