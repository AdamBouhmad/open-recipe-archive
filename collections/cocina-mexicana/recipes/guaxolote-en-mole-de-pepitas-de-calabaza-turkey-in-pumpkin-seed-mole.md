## Ingredients

- Pumpkin seeds
- Salt water
- Lard
- Nuts
- Peanuts
- Chili seeds

## Directions

1. Sprinkle pumpkin seeds with salt water.
2. Toast them on a comal.
3. Grind them.
4. Sauté nuts, peanuts, and chili seeds in lard.
