## Ingredients

- Large fresh green peppers
- Fresh cheese, crumbled
- Epazote leaves, chopped
- Lard
- Thin tortillas
- Butter or lard, melted and seasoned with salt

## Directions

1. Select large green peppers, roast and peel them
2. Cut a line slightly below the stem about halfway down the pepper so the head does not come off, and remove the veins from the top
3. Crumble fresh cheese and chop epazote leaves, mix them together well
4. Fill the peppers through the head with the cheese mixture
5. Fry in lard; when cooked through, remove and drain
6. Bathe them in melted, seasoned butter or lard
7. Prepare very thin tortillas and wrap each pepper in one tortilla
8. Arrange on a platter
