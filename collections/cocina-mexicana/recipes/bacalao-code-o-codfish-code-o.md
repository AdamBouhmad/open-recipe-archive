## Ingredients

- bacalao (codfish)
- cebolla (onion)
- jitomate (tomato)
- ajo (garlic)
- chiles verdes (green chiles)
- aceite (oil)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo (broth)
- agua (water)

## Directions

1. Soak the codfish from one day to the next and cook in a well-covered pot
2. Chop onion, tomato, and garlic; roast green chiles and cut into strips
3. This mixture should be in double the quantity of the codfish, or at least in the following proportion: for one pound of codfish take eight chiles and twelve tomatoes that should not be chopped too much but remain in thick pieces
4. Put a tortera (clay dish) with oil and lard on heat, fry this mixture well, and then make a layer of fish, another of mixture, and finally add a little of the fish broth
5. Place on low heat
