## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Fish broths

## Directions

1. Empty the salmon through the gills and remove the roe
2. Wash in plenty of water, tie the head with a thread, and put to cook in a fish kettle or cooking vessel with one of the appropriate fish broths
3. Once cooked, remove it, let it drain and arrange on a platter or fish kettle covered with a napkin, surrounded with parsley
