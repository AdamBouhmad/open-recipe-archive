## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Fresh lard
- Puff pastry dough
- Egg wash

## Directions

1. After washing, drain truffles in a colander
2. Season and knead with finely chopped and ground fresh lard, divided into three portions
3. Use one portion to coat the truffles, another to grease and cover the surface of a puff pastry base
4. Place truffles on it in pyramid form
5. Use the third portion to cover the top, covering it with another layer of lard
6. Arrange the pastry pieces to perfectly adhere to the truffles stacked on top of each other, imitating the sinuosities of a rock formation
7. Glaze the piece (see gilding of pastry pieces), open a small hole in the cover and place in a hot oven for one hour
8. Remove from oven and trace the cover with a knife point to remove the lard coating
9. Replace the cover and serve the piece very hot as an intermediate course
