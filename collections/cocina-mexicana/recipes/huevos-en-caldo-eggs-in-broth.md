## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- two egg whites
- five large tablespoons of good concentrated broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix well in a pan six yolks and two whites with five large tablespoons of good concentrated broth.
2. Pass the mixture through a sieve or cheesecloth.
3. Lightly coat the molds with butter instead of freezing them.
4. Fill with the preparation described.
5. Allow to set.
6. Serve with good concentrated broth instead of sauce, allowing it to reduce a little.
