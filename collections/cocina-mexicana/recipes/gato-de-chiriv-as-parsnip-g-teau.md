## Ingredients

- twelve parsnips, the most yellow ones
- water
- a little salt
- one quarter liter of milk
- a little flour
- four whole eggs
- six egg yolks
- candied and ground orange blossom
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- four ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook twelve carefully scraped parsnips in water with a little salt in a pot
2. When cooked, dry them in a pan over the fire
3. After draining, pass through a sieve and discard the cores
4. Make a pastry cream with one quarter liter of milk, reinforced with a little flour
5. When cooked, incorporate the parsnip puree with a little candied and ground orange blossom and twelve ounces of powdered sugar
6. Add four whole eggs and six egg yolks one by one, mixing well with four ounces of melted butter
7. Fold in the six reserved egg whites that have been previously beaten
8. Three quarters of an hour before serving, pour everything into a pan or mold and bake in the oven
9. After the gâteau is cooked, turn it out onto a plate and serve
