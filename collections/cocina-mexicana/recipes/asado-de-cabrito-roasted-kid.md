## Ingredients

- kid meat (carne de cabrito)
- garlic (ajos)
- water
- vinegar
- onions, one or two whole
- small tomato (jitomate chico)
- herbs (yerbas de olor)
- lard (manteca)

## Directions

1. Brown the meat with some garlic; once it is browned, add sufficient water and a little vinegar, one or two whole onions, a small tomato or a piece of one, and herbs of flavor. Cover and let it dry in its own lard.
