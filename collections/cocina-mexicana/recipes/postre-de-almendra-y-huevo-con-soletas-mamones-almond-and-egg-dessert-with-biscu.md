## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 pound soaked and ground almonds
- clove
- cinnamon powder
- 12 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound cold biscuits or sponge cakes
- cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at medium point with 3 pounds of sugar
2. Off the heat, mix in 1 pound of soaked and ground almonds and a little clove and cinnamon powder just to flavor
3. Put it on the heat, and when it begins to thicken, add 12 egg yolks, stirring so they incorporate well
4. Let it boil until it is close to reaching the point, then add 1/2 pound of cold biscuits or sponge cakes
5. Remove from heat and when everything is well incorporated, pour into dishes covered with comales or tin sheets with heat on top to brown the dessert
6. After it cools, dust with cinnamon
