## Ingredients

- Xitomates (tomatoes), in good season but not ripe
- Ash water (agua de ceniza)
- Salt
- Clarified syrup (almíbar clarificado)

## Directions

1. Select tomatoes in good season but not ripe
2. Place them in settled ash water, leaving them for two or three days, changing the water daily
3. On the last day give them a boil in salted water
4. Peel them, empty them, place in fresh natural water
5. Change the water several times so they lose all sourness
6. Place them in clarified syrup and complete the operation as with other preserves
