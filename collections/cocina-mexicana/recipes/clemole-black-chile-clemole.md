## Ingredients

- black chiles, deveined and toasted
- black chile seeds, toasted on comal
- tortilla, toasted on coals
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- cooked chicken
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Devein black chiles and toast them in lard
2. Toast the black chile seeds on a comal
3. Toast tortilla on coals
4. Grind together the clove, cinnamon, and toasted ingredients
5. Fry the mixture in lard with salt
6. Add the cooked chicken and its broth
7. Simmer until it reaches a regular broth-like consistency
