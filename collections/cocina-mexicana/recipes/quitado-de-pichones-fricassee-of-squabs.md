## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Lard
- One or two tomatoes, chopped
- Three or four bunches of onion in wedges
- Two or three heads of garlic, crushed
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Creole vinegar
- Carlon wine
- Water
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs well
2. Place in a casserole with ham slices, chorizo, and a little lard
3. Add one or two chopped tomatoes, three or four bunches of onion in wedges, two or three crushed garlic heads, bay leaves, thyme, and oregano
4. Fry everything very well in the casserole
5. Add creole vinegar, carlon wine, and water
6. Season with ground cinnamon and pepper
7. The broth should be thick
8. When bringing to the table, add a little more wine
9. Garnish with chiles, olives, and capers
