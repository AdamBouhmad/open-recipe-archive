## Ingredients

- Meat
- Garlic
- Salt grains
- Onion pieces
- Parsley, chopped
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Capers
- Onion slices

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with garlic and salt grains.
2. Place it in a pot with onion pieces, plenty of chopped parsley, a tablespoon of lard, and a little vinegar.
3. Cover the pot and set to cook.
4. The sauce should be thick.
5. Garnish with many capers and sliced onion.
