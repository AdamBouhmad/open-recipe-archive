## Ingredients

- water
- vegetables necessary for the pot
- lean meat
- one hen

## Directions

1. Cook water with the vegetables necessary for the pot and add the defatted meat
2. Boil a hen until the meat acquires good color
3. Place it in the pot and leave it with the meat for eight hours
4. It must boil continuously but slowly
5. Reduce the water to half
6. This consommé is better and healthier than what is bought at great expense
