## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- one real of eggs (two with whites)
- a little salted water
- a little lard
- egg white for sealing
- filling of choice

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of flour, one real of eggs (two with whites), a little salted water, and a little lard.
2. Beat, cut into shapes, and brush with a little egg white.
3. Fill with desired filling.
