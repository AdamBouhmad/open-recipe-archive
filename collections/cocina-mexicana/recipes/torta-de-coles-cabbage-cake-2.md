## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Xitomates (tomatoes)
- White cabbage
- Sugar
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Fry in lard chopped garlic, onions and xitomates, and when done, add the cooked white cabbage to be recooked
2. Add powdered sugar in sufficient quantity so that the sweet stands out, and grated cheese or fresh cheese ground
