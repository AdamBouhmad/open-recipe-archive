## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges
- Salt water
- Sugar syrup (clarified, at low point)

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, removing the colored part of the skin while leaving the white flesh intact
2. Remove a small wheel from the crown
3. Pierce the segments with a thin knife without damaging the skin
4. Express all the juice before cooking
5. Cook in boiling salt water
6. Cool in the same water they were cooked in
7. Change to water with less salt and leave in sun and open air, changing the water frequently with lightly salted water until the bitter taste is lost but some subtle saltiness remains
8. Blanch in boiling water and after one boil, remove, cool, and change to clean water to completely desalt
9. Place in clarified sugar syrup at low point, bringing to boil until it reaches the preserve point
10. Set aside and complete the cooking the next day when the syrup reaches full preserve point
