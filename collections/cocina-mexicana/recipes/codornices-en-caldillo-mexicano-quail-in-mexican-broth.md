## Ingredients

- onions
- garlic
- parsley
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- pepper
- clove
- cumin
- toasted bread
- salt
- vinegar
- broth
- oregano
- capers
- quail
- raw oil
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onions, garlic and parsley in lard, and roasted and ground tomatoes.
2. Add pepper, clove, cumin and toasted bread fried in lard, all ground, salt, vinegar, broth, a little oregano and whole capers.
3. The quail, which will already be cooked and fried in lard with ground garlic, are added to the broth and left to simmer until completely flavored.
4. When serving, add raw oil, small chiles and olives.
