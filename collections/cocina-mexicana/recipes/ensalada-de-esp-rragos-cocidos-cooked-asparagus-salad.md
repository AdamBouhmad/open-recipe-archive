## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Onion

## Directions

1. Cook the bundles of asparagus and remove from the water
2. Squeeze gently without pressing hard
3. Untie them and arrange on the plate evenly
4. Dress with oil and vinegar seasoned with salt and pepper
5. Sprinkle finely chopped raw onion on top
