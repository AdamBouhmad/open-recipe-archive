## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- egg

## Directions

1. Give the puff pastry dough six turns
2. Make a base with it two lines thick
3. Cut twelve rounds from it with a pastry cutter that is joined or sealed, and brush them with egg
4. Soak another round pastry cutter in boiling water, much smaller than the first
5. Place it on the bases which have been placed on a sheet of tin
6. Push it in to two-thirds of the thickness of the dough so that when the pastries are cooked, the marked inner part can be removed
7. At the moment of serving, place in them a minced mixture of poultry
