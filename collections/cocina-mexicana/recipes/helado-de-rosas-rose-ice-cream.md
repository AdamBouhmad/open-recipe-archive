## Ingredients

- one drachm cochineal
- half a quartillo of verjuice
- one scruple of gum tragacanth, the whitest
- twenty-two ounces white sugar
- one and a half quartillos of water
- a little rose water

## Directions

1. Crush one drachm of cochineal and steep in infusion for twenty-four hours in half a quartillo of verjuice, keeping the vessel well covered and shaking the liquid every three hours
2. Prepare one scruple of the whitest gum tragacanth by crushing it in a marble mortar and moistening it from time to time with a little common water until it dissolves and forms a kind of mucilage
3. Pour twenty-two ounces of white sugar into one and a half quartillos of water, and when well dissolved, add the gum water and cochineal tincture, mixing everything and letting it sit for two hours
4. Strain the liquid through a cloth bag, add a little rose water, shake the mixture strongly and place in a cool place until you wish to freeze it
