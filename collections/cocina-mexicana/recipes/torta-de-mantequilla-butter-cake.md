## Ingredients

- sponge cake slices
- butter
- ground sugar
- egg whites
- grajea

## Directions

1. In one pan served with lard, place slices of sponge cake
2. In another pan melt butter with ground sugar which will be poured over the sponge cake in the baking pan, making sure it soaks well
3. Put over two gentle fires so it sets
4. When the cake is somewhat firm, remove from heat as it is already cooked
5. Beat some egg whites with ground and sifted sugar until very white and thick, then pour over the cake so it covers it
6. Dust with grajea and place a comal with fire on top so the egg sets
