## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter or lard
- salt
- black pepper
- cream of milk
- bread fried in oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as sorrel until putting it in the saucepan
2. Add the necessary salt, butter or lard and a little black pepper
3. When cooked add a little cream of milk and put some small pieces of bread fried in oil
4. Serve hot
