## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- four ounces of almond
- four ounces of rice
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten four quartillos of milk and put on the fire
2. Add four ounces of almond and four ounces of rice, both ground
3. When it reaches the right consistency, set aside, and before it cools, mix in cinnamon powder
