## Ingredients

- lentils
- water
- salt
- parsley
- two onion heads
- black pepper
- vinegar or egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in a little water with the necessary salt
2. Add a bundle of parsley, two onion heads cut into quarters, and a little black pepper
3. Let everything boil over very low heat in little water
4. When cooked, either add a little vinegar mixed with egg yolks, or remove the lentils from their cooking water and fry them in a little butter with the necessary salt
