## Ingredients

- thick and fresh mushrooms
- salt
- coarse pepper
- oil
- parsley
- shallots

## Directions

1. Choose thick and fresh mushrooms, clean them, wash them and remove the stems
2. Make incisions on the lower part
3. Dust with salt and coarse pepper
4. Marinate in oil
5. Cook on the grill on both sides
6. When they feel firm to the touch of the finger, they are cooked
7. Serve with good oil with chopped parsley and shallots, salt and coarse pepper
