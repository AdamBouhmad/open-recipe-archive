## Ingredients

- Whole chicken livers
- Stock or essence
- White wine
- Parsley
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take whole livers after carefully removing the bile and the bitter blood vessels
2. When cleaned for a few minutes in boiling water, put in a dish with sufficient essence or good stock and a glass of good white wine
3. Season with a bunch of parsley, scallions, a little garlic and pepper
4. After half an hour, remove the fat and serve as an entree
