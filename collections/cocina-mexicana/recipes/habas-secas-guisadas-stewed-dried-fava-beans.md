## Ingredients

- Dried fava beans
- Water
- Lard
- Garlic heads, whole
- Wide chile peppers, whole
- Spearmint sprigs
- Cumin, ground
- Saffron, ground
- Oil

## Directions

1. Soak the dried fava beans in cold water so they do not blacken
2. Remove the skins and place them to cook in cold water without salt
3. Once they have broken down, add raw lard, whole garlic heads, two or three whole wide chile peppers, one or two sprigs of spearmint, and small amounts of ground cumin and saffron
4. When serving, drizzle a few drops of oil on top
