## Ingredients

- simple slices of bread, either plain or toasted on a griddle and fried or not fried
- white panela honey

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the previous chongos
2. Use simple slices of bread (toasted on a griddle and fried or not fried) instead of sponge cake
3. Use white panela honey instead of syrup
