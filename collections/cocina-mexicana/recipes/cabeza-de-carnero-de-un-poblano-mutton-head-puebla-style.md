## Ingredients

- mutton heads
- lard
- onion
- garlic
- broth from head cooking
- pepper
- clove
- cumin
- cinnamon
- good vinegar
- avocado leaves
- salt

## Directions

1. Skin the heads and remove any cartilage if there is any dirt
2. Remove the brain cap to extract the brains whole
3. Cut the head well with a cleaver and cook in salted water until cooked
4. Remove and completely debone
5. Place a pot on the fire with lard and finely slice much onion and a little garlic
6. Fry this well in the lard and add sufficient broth from the water in which the head was cooked
7. Grind pepper, clove, cumin and cinnamon, add to the pot with a little good vinegar
8. Also add plenty of avocado leaves
9. Divide the meat from the head into regular pieces, skin the tongue, slice it and add to the broth
10. Add a little salt and cook over two gentle fires until the broth is well consumed and the meat is roasted
11. Serve with avocado leaves removed
