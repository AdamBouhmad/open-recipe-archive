## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- powdered sugar equal in weight to eight eggs
- a little salt
- rind of one lemon, finely chopped
- a little orange blossom
- eight egg whites
- flour equal in weight to five eggs, dried in the oven

## Directions

1. Separate the egg whites from eight eggs and place the yolks in a bowl with powdered sugar equal in weight to eight eggs, a little salt, finely chopped lemon rind, and a little orange blossom
2. Beat the yolks until white
3. Mix in the egg whites and beat until they are difficult to separate from the whisk
4. Sift and gradually sprinkle in flour equal in weight to five eggs, stirring gently until well mixed
5. Arrange the cakes on quarters of white paper with a common spoon
6. Dust with very dry powdered sugar passed through a silk sieve
7. Place immediately in a gentle oven so the cakes remain light
