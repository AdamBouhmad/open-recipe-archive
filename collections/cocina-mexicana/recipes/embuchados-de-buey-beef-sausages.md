## Ingredients

- beef intestines
- beef tripe and palates
- water
- onion
- butter
- six raw egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- nutmeg
- fat broth
- half bottle of white wine
- clove
- carrots
- celery

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the tripe as for pork sausages
2. Cut beef tripe and palates into strips, almost entirely cooked in water
3. Cut onion the same way and render in butter
4. Add six raw egg yolks, salt, pepper, spices and nutmeg
5. Fill the tripe with this mixture using a funnel
6. Cook the sausages in fat broth mixed with half a bottle of white wine
7. Add a seasoned bundle, clove, pepper, salt, carrots and onions
8. Serve as with pork sausages
