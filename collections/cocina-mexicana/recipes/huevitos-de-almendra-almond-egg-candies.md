## Ingredients

- sugar
- egg yolks
- four ounces ground almonds
- one quarter ounce cinnamon powder
- a little clove

## Directions

1. Make the same as the spiced pocket egg candies
2. Add four ounces of ground almonds at the time the yolks are added to the syrup
3. Add one quarter ounce of cinnamon powder and a little clove
