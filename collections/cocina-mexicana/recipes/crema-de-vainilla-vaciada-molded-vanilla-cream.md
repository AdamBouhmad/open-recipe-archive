## Ingredients

- Cream
- Sugar
- Vanilla
- 12 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs for each 4 quartillos of cream
- Butter for the molds

## Directions

1. Fill one or several molds with cream
2. Give them a few boils, add sugar and vanilla, and boil again for a few moments
3. Allow to cool immediately
4. Mix 12 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs for each 4 quartillos of cream into the cream
5. Strain this five times through a fine sieve or strainer
6. Pour into molds that have been buttered beforehand
7. Allow it to set in a water bath, taking care that the heat from the top is not too strong
8. Empty the mold or molds onto one or several plates
9. Return any remaining cream to the fire, stirring and turning as if it were a white sauce until it sticks to the wooden spoon
10. Remove from the heat, stir, and pour over the molded cream
