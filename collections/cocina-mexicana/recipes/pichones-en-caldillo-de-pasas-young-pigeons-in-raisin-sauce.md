## Ingredients

- cooked young pigeons
- breadcrumbs
- lard
- ground raisins
- hard sponge cake
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- sugar
- long plantain slices
- chorizo slices
- cooked ham strips

## Directions

1. After cooking the pigeons, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them in lard
2. When golden, remove them from the frying and add to the fat ground raisins mixed with a little hard sponge cake and a pinch of salt
3. Add then clove and cinnamon powder, a piece of sugar according to the amount of sauce and the number of pigeons, slices of long plantain and chorizo and strips of cooked ham
4. Once this sauce is well seasoned and thick, place the fried pigeons in it, removing the pan from the fire, which will be placed over the steam of a pot so that the pigeons stay warm without boiling
