## Ingredients

- Veal offal
- Garlic, ground
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Cooked ham, in strips
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Lard
- Pork caul fat
- Veal lungs
- Veal kidneys
- Veal liver

## Directions

1. After cleaning the offal well and removing the throat or windpipe, coat all parts with ground garlic mixed with vinegar and salt, and lard it entirely with strips of cooked ham rolled in salt, black pepper, cloves, thyme, and oregano, all in powder
2. In a pot coated with lard, spread a layer of pork caul fat, also coated with lard and sprinkled with the same spices and aromatics in which the ham lards were rolled
3. Arrange the offal on top with the lungs, kidneys, liver, and other parts separated
