## Ingredients

- Green fava beans, cooked
- Flour
- Lard
- Water
- Sour orange juice
- White wine
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the fava beans as previously described
2. Fry a small amount of flour in lard and when it has browned, add water
3. When it comes to a boil, squeeze sour orange juice into it
4. Once it boils a little, add the fava beans with a glass of white wine
5. Allow to season and when it has thickened sufficiently, remove from heat and serve
