## Ingredients

- oil
- lard
- crushed garlic
- oysters
- water
- fine pepper
- parsley
- lemon juice
- salt
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry crushed garlic in oil and lard
2. After being washed in their own water, add the oysters to fry, allowing the water they release to evaporate and ensuring they do not remain juicy
3. Remove from heat, dust with very fine pepper
4. Add parsley and lemon juice, tasting to ensure they are not too salty
5. After this, place them in the shells with bread crumbs on top
6. Then place them on the grill with fire on top
