## Ingredients

- six pounds of sugar
- one pound of grated coconut
- one pound of ground almonds
- one pound of sweet potato
- twenty egg yolks
- one pound of custard
- sliced mamey

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with six pounds of sugar and clarify it
2. Add one pound of grated coconut, one pound of ground almonds, and one pound of sweet potato
3. Let it boil and when it thickens, remove from heat
4. When cooled, add twenty egg yolks and one pound of custard
5. Return to heat until it acquires proper consistency
6. Pour into a platter over sliced mamey
