## Ingredients

- Minced meat
- Mutton tongues
- Chicken sweetbreads
- Chicken gizzards
- Chopped livers
- Sliced chorizo
- Butifarra
- Pieces of sausage
- Longaniza
- Strips of ham
- Whole eggs
- Oregano
- Broth
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Olives
- Eggs
- Butter
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a minced meat mixture
2. Add mutton tongues, chicken sweetbreads, gizzards, chopped livers, sliced chorizo and butifarra, pieces of sausage, longaniza, strips of ham, and whole eggs, all cooked
3. Add oregano and some broth and let it boil for a while to season
4. Add raisins, almonds and olives
5. When it is dry and fried, incorporate beaten eggs
6. Pour into a well-greased pan, even it out, and cover with more egg on top
7. Spread with butter and cook over two fires
8. Once set, sprinkle ground sugar on top
