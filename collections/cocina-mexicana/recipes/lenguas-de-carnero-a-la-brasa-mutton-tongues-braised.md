## Ingredients

- Mutton tongues
- water
- ham strips
- ham slices
- carrots
- onions
- herbs
- broth

## Directions

1. After washing well the tongues and bleeding them in water, parboil in boiling water, refresh in cold water and let drain
2. Prepare by removing the gullet, and lard with medium strips of ham
3. Cook with slices of the same ham, carrots, onions and a mixed bundle of herbs, moistening everything with broth
4. This cooking should be done over low heat for four or five hours
