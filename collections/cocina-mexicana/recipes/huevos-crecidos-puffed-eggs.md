## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- beaten egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook eggs and remove yolks
2. Coat yolks with beaten egg and fry in lard
3. Add more layers of beaten egg and fry again, continuing until they are large
4. Place in syrup or in sweetened milk that has been well boiled
