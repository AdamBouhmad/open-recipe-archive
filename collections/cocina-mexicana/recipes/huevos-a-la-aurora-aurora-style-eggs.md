## Ingredients

- ten or twelve hard eggs
- lean béchamel sauce
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Split ten or twelve hard eggs in two and remove the yolks.
2. Cut the whites into thin strips and add them to lean béchamel sauce.
3. Arrange them on the bottom of a plate.
4. Mix the yolks with a piece of butter, salt, and a little grated nutmeg.
5. Pass through a fine sieve or strainer and place over the whites already arranged on the plate.
6. Clean the edges and decorate with toast.
7. Place the plate on low heat under a covered oven.
8. At the moment of serving, remove the toast and replace with some fried garnishes.
