## Ingredients

- Wide chiles, deveined and washed
- Roasted garlic cloves
- Cumin
- Good vinegar
- Onion, finely chopped
- Salt
- Small zucchini, cooked
- Grated cheese
- Oregano powder
- Avocado slices
- Tornachiles in vinegar, chopped
- Olives
- Capers, desalted

## Directions

1. Devein and wash the wide chiles well and grind them with roasted garlic, cumin, and dissolve in good vinegar.
2. Chop onion very finely and mix into the sauce.
3. Add the necessary salt.
4. Arrange the small cooked zucchini whole and bathe them with the said sauce.
5. Sprinkle grated cheese, oregano powder, avocado slices, tornachiles in vinegar chopped, olives, and desalted capers on top.
