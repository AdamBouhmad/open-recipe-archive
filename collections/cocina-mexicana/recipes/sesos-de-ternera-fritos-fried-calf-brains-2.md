## Ingredients

- Calf brains
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Hot water
- Bacon
- White wine
- Beef broth or puchero broth
- Parsley
- Onions
- Lemon slices without peel or seeds
- Egg
- Flour

## Directions

1. After well prepared and with veins removed, wash the brains in hot water with salt and vinegar
2. Let them cool in the same water so they harden slightly
3. Cook them in a pan lined with bacon, white wine, double the amount of beef broth or puchero broth, parsley, onions, and some lemon slices without peel or seeds
4. After cooked, cut into pieces
5. Coat in egg and flour
6. Fry them
7. Serve garnished with fried parsley on top and underneath
