## Ingredients

- meat
- butter
- one or two garlic cloves
- one onion
- some carrots
- one turnip
- two or three cloves
- one bay leaf
- two or three large peppers
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Stud the meat with the larding needle.
2. Heat butter in a saucepan and brown it a little.
3. Add one or two garlic cloves split lengthwise, an onion split the same way, some carrots in pieces, a turnip also split, two or three cloves, a bay leaf, and two or three large peppers.
4. Parboil all this.
5. Once the meat is browned, add the corresponding water and cover the saucepan, placing it on low heat.
6. Before taking it to the table, strain the sauce so it remains clean.
