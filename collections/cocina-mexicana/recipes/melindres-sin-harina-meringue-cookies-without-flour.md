## Ingredients

- three egg whites
- one pound sifted sugar
- a little ground anise (optional)
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Beat three egg whites and mix them with one pound of sifted sugar, stirring enough so that it becomes well incorporated, adding if desired a little ground anise.
2. Form with this paste the small rings or little figures that seem best, sprinkling them with sesame seeds.
3. Place them on papers and put them in the oven to bake, which should be at a lower temperature than for bread.
