## Ingredients

- fat pullets in quarters, squabs whole or halved if large, or wings of turkey
- fine herbs
- salt
- spices
- small bunch of herbs
- piece of dough
- prepared meat cooled
- meatballs of ground beef picadillo
- ham slices
- dough for top layer
- water
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Scald and partially cook the chosen poultry in butter with fine herbs, well seasoned, adding a small mixed bunch of herbs
2. Place a piece of dough the thickness of a coin on the tart pan, beaten with the mallet
3. Arrange the cooked and cooled meat on top of the dough
4. Fill any gaps with little balls of ground beef picadillo
5. Cover the meat with ham slices
6. Place another sheet of dough on top, moistening it with water using a brush or scoring brush on the edges where the two sheets should join
7. Seal around the edges with the finger so they stay together and attached
8. Put a rounded border around the edge, pressing with the finger
9. Brush the entire upper part of the tart with beaten egg using a brush or feather
10. Bake in the oven
11. After a quarter hour, remove and make a hole in the center to release steam to prevent the lid from bursting
12. Return immediately to the oven
13. When cooked, remove the upper layer or dough lid by cutting around near the edge
14. Remove the grease inside and the ham slices
15. Add a good sauce prepared in advance, or if available a stew of beef sweetbread and mushrooms, well seasoned, which is better
16. Cover again with the cooked dough lid and serve
