## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Beets
- Wide dried chiles, devined
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cumin
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Oregano powder
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Avocado slices, peeled
- Cooked onions
- Olives
- Small chiles
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Cook both the carrots and beets
2. Chop them finely and marinate with wide devined chiles that are soaked and ground with little garlic, pepper, clove and cumin, and considerable cinnamon
3. Season with salt and oregano powder, incorporating everything well, and let sit until the next day
4. On the following day, remove the carrots and beets from the adobo without squeezing them, and place them on a plate arranged with oil, vinegar, peeled avocado slices, cooked and leafless onions, olives, small chiles, raisins, almonds and toasted sesame
