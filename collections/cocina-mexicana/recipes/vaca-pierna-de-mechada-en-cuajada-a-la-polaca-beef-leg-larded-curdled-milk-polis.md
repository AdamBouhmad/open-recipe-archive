## Ingredients

- beef leg
- lard or fatty ham
- good vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- whole cloves
- bay leaf
- lemon slices
- tarragon
- butter
- marinaded juices
- broth
- one quarter litre of curdled milk
- white substance

## Directions

1. Beat and clean the beef leg, removing all skins and nerves
2. Lard with lard or fatty ham
3. Place in a large earthenware dish or bowl
4. Pour over it a good glass of good vinegar that has been boiled for a quarter hour with salt, pepper, whole cloves, bay leaf, lemon slices and tarragon
5. Marinate in this way for one hour
6. Then place in a pot where it will take color with butter
7. Add half the juice from the marinade, some broth, one quarter litre of curdled milk, and a white substance made separately
