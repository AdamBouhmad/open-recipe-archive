## Ingredients

- One pound sweet almonds
- Four ounces bitter almonds
- One pound clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Peel one pound of sweet almonds and four ounces of bitter almonds
2. After they are well dried on a griddle, grind them
3. Clarify one pound of sugar and cook it lightly
4. Remove from heat and add the almond paste
5. Place the pan on heat stirring so the paste does not burn
6. When cooked, which will be when it does not stick to the hand, spread it on a table dusted with sugar, letting it cool
7. Then cut the marzipans in the shape desired and place them on a sheet of paper to bake in the oven
