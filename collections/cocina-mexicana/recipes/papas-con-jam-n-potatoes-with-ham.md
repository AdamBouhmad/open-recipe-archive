## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, mixed bunch
- fat and lean ham, cut in large dice
- raw peeled and cut potatoes

## Directions

1. Let butter brown in a pot over the fire and dissolve flour in it.
2. When this mixture is very dark in color, season with salt, pepper and a small bunch of mixed parsley.
3. Add ham, both fat and lean, cut in the form of large dice, and let it half-cook in the sauce.
4. Add the raw peeled and cut potatoes.
5. When they are cooked, remove the fat from the sauce to serve the potatoes and ham with it.
