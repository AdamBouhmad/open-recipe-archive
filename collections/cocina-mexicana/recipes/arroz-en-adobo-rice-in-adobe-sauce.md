## Ingredients

- rice
- sufficient amount of wide chiles
- garlic, peeled
- pork, chicken, or other meat
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the rice and put it in a cloth tied so the rice is loose
2. Boil a little water in a pot and hang the cloth with the rice by a string over it so the rice does not touch the water
3. Cover the pot so it cooks in steam
4. While this is being verified, devein and soak sufficient wide chiles in two or three waters
5. Remove them and grind with a portion of peeled garlic
6. Heat butter in a pot, add the pork, chicken, or other meat, and when it is fried, remove it from the pot
7. Fry everything in the butter in which the meat was fried, and when it is fried, return the meat to fry again with the tomato
8. Then add the well-washed rice and broth from cooking the meats
9. Let everything boil until the rice cooks, and as soon as it has very little broth, remove from heat and place in the steam of a boiling pot
