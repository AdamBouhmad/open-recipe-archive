## Ingredients

- half a quarter quart of milk
- nine ounces of starch
- salt
- twenty egg yolks
- lard

## Directions

1. In half a quarter quart of milk, dissolve nine ounces of starch
2. Strain it, add salt and cook until it forms a ball
3. Keep stirring constantly
4. Once cold, add twenty egg yolks and mix them well into the dough so there are no lumps
5. Fry small balls in lard that is not very hot so they burst, then put them in syrup
