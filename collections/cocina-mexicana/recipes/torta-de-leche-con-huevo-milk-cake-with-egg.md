## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- small cinnamon sticks
- necessary sugar
- four beaten eggs
- sponge cake (mamón), sliced
- butter for greasing
- ground sugar and cinnamon for topping or syrup

## Directions

1. Boil two cuartillos of milk with small cinnamon sticks and necessary sugar
2. Mix with four beaten eggs
3. Pour over layers of sliced sponge cake in a buttered platter or cake pan
4. Cook over two fires
5. Test doneness by inserting a straw; when it comes out clean, the cake is done
6. Remove from heat
7. Serve dry with ground sugar and cinnamon on top, or in syrup
