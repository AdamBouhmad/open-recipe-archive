## Ingredients

- one pound of clean, ripe pears
- two pounds and four ounces of sugar made into syrup
- the third part of a quarter ounce of cloves
- one ounce of cinnamon, whole
- sugar powder
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. To one pound of clean, ripe pears, add two pounds and four ounces of sugar made into syrup
2. To this add the third part of a quarter ounce of cloves and another ounce of cinnamon, kept whole
3. With the syrup prepared in these terms, place the pears in the syrup over low heat until they have been preserved and the sweet has penetrated them, leaving them until the syrup thickens considerably and remains like a covering
4. Begin removing the pears and placing them in a strainer and if you do not have one, place on a platter with sticks arranged like a grate so you can place them in the sun to dry well and not drip
5. After, pound together sugar and cinnamon; once mixed place in a mortar and in it go stirring the pears and pressing them with your hands
