## Ingredients

- Ham skin and covering, unbroken
- Ham feet
- All other skins and sinews separated from ham processing
- Spices as for Bologna sausages
- Salt

## Directions

1. When cutting ham for making other sausages, take care not to break or damage the skin or covering
2. Debone the ham feet and fill the hollow with all the other separated skins and sinews from the sausage preparation
3. Cut these into the form of thick dice and season as with Bologna sausages
4. Wrap everything in the ham skin and sew the ends
5. Keep in salt for eight days
6. Smoke and dry
7. When ready to use immediately, allow to dry for twenty-four hours
8. Cook on the fire for six hours
9. Serve as a relay course on a serviette
