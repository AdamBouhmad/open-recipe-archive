## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- salt
- peppercorns
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare, flatten and season the ribs with salt and peppercorns. Melt butter and dip each rib in it, then place them on a platter in which the bread crumbs will be. Turn them in the crumbs, place on the grill over very low heat so that the bread does not toast too much. Half an hour is sufficient for them to be well cooked.
