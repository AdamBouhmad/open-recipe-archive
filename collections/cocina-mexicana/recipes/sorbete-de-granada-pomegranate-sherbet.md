## Ingredients

- Twelve pomegranates with very red seeds
- Four ounces of currant juice
- Twenty ounces of sugar
- One azumbre of water

## Directions

1. Select pomegranates with very red seeds and crush in a stone mortar
2. Add four ounces of currant juice and twenty ounces of sugar
3. When all is well crushed and mixed, add one azumbre of water without stopping the stirring
4. Pass through a cloth, squeezing with force and freeze
