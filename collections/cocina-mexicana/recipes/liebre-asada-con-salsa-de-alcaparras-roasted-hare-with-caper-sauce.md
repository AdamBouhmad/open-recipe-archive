## Ingredients

- Hare
- oil
- onions
- capers
- pepper
- bread
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Golden chopped onions in oil and add ground capers with pepper and toasted bread fried, seasoning with necessary salt
2. Place the hare, already roasted raw, in this sauce with a little vinegar, and drying it sufficiently, serve the hare with this same sauce over it
