## Ingredients

- Lamb or kid goat heads
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolk
- Ham
- Butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the heads in half.
2. Prepare a filling: chop a little parsley and garlic, add salt, pepper, an egg yolk and ham, and give it all a turn in the pan with butter.
3. Fill the heads with this mixture.
4. Close them together.
5. Brush the outside with lemon juice and butter.
6. Tie with string and wrap in paper.
7. Place on the spit until tender.
