## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- green lemon zest, grated
- two bitter almond biscuits
- sugar
- orange blossom water
- puff pastry dough
- lean frying fat
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Clean, wash thoroughly and cook the spinach in a pot with a little water
2. Drain and let cool, then squeeze and press well
3. Grind in a mortar or mill on a metate
4. Add a little fresh butter, grated green lemon zest and two bitter almond biscuits with sugar and orange blossom water, grinding or pounding both together a second time
5. Make a base of thin puff pastry dough and divide into small pieces
6. Put about half a walnut of spinach filling in one corner of each piece and moisten and cover the toasts with dough
7. Round them with a knife and then fry in the lean frying fat
8. When well browned, drain and arrange neatly on a plate
9. Dust with sugar, pass a hot iron over them so they caramelize, and serve hot as a light course
