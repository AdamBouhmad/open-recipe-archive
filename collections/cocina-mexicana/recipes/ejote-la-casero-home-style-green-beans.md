## Ingredients

- Green beans
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the beans well and remove the strings.
2. Place water and salt in a pot and bring to boil, then add the beans.
3. When tender, remove and drain, then place in cold water.
4. Heat butter in a pan, add the drained beans, season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley.
5. Stir continuously and serve.
