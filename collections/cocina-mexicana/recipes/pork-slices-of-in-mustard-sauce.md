## Ingredients

- a pork loin, well-fatted
- thin and equal slices
- mustard sauce
- bread crusts, fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cut a pork loin into thin and equal slices
2. Place them in a mustard sauce prepared in advance
3. Serve with bread crusts fried in butter arranged around
