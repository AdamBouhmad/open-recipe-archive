## Ingredients

- ham, cleaned and trimmed of rancid parts
- water
- wine
- garlic
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning and removing the rancid parts, cut the ham and desalt it for two days in cold water
2. Dry and pat it
3. Cook it wrapped in white linen with equal parts wine and water, garlic, onions, thyme, bay leaf, clove, and fine herbs
4. Test doneness by pricking with a skewer
5. Remove and take out the central bone
6. When cold, remove the skin and cover the surrounding fat with a mixture of fine herbs and breadcrumbs
