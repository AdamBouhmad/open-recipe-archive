## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- sugar syrup at cracking point

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, cut them into quarters, and cook them like marmalade in water
2. Pass this marmalade through a sieve, pressing to extract it, and pour it into sugar syrup at cracking point
3. Bring to a boil and skim
4. When the jelly falls in flakes from the skimmer, pour it into jars
5. Use one quartillo of syrup for every one quartillo of pear substance
