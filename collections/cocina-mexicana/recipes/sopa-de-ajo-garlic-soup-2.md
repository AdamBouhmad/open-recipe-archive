## Ingredients

- Garlic, cut in small pieces
- Butter or oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Bread slices
- Eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the garlic pieces in butter or oil; when well fried but not burned, add salt and pepper
2. Add the necessary amount of water and let it boil for half a quarter-hour
3. If you wish to make it with eggs, place them on top of the bread slices, and pouring the boiling broth over them, they will turn out very good
