## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Broth from the pot
- Saffron, optional
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Red wine
- Bread slices, toasted

## Directions

1. After plucking the woodcock without washing them, roast them on a grill.
2. Then remove the innards and grind them with clove, cinnamon and pepper.
3. Chop plenty of onion and garlic, fry them, and once well fried add the ground innards and a little broth from the pot, before adding saffron if using it.
4. Place the woodcock in the fried mixture to cook.
5. Add a little thyme, a few bay leaves, plenty of red wine, a few slices of toasted bread and place them at the bottom of the platter with the woodcock on top.
