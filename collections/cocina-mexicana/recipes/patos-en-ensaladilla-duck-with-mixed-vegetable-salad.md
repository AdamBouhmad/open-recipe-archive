## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Water, settled with tequesquite
- Clean water
- Salt
- Raw tomatoes
- Pimiento
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Nutmeg, ground
- Duck broth
- Vinegar
- Carrot
- Beet
- Onion
- Olives
- Chiles
- Oil
- Lettuce
- Radishes

## Directions

1. Wash the ducks with water treated with tequesquite and let them sit overnight.
2. The next day, cook them in clean salted water.
3. Once cooked, leave them in this broth.
4. Grind raw tomatoes, pimiento, clove, cinnamon, and nutmeg, and fry this mixture in a little of the duck's own broth rather than in lard.
5. Once well fried, add a little more broth and some vinegar, and let it sit.
6. Separately, season a salad of carrot, beet, onion, olives, and chiles with oil and vinegar.
7. When serving, remove the duck from its broth, place on a plate, and top with the salad, then the tomato sauce, with lettuce and radishes around it.
