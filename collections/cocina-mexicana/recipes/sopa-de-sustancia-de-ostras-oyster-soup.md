## Ingredients

- twenty or more oysters
- broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound two dozen or more oysters in a mortar, depending on their size and the amount of soup to be made
2. Add them to broth
3. Cook over low heat for thirty to forty minutes
4. Soak fried bread cubes in butter with this broth
