## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp cut into regular-sized portions
- Lard
- Ground garlic
- Salt
- White wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Sugar in small quantity
- Oregano
- Black pepper
- Cloves, ground
- Toasted bread fried in lard, ground

## Directions

1. Cut lamb pulp into regular-sized portions
2. Fry in a pot with lard, ground garlic, and salt
3. Add white wine, a little vinegar, a little water, a small quantity of sugar, oregano, black pepper, and ground cloves
4. Cover the pot with a casserole of the same size as the pot mouth and seal the joints with paste
5. Let the meat cook covered
6. When cooked, remove from heat
7. If necessary to thicken the broth, add ground toasted bread fried in lard
8. Give it one or two more boils to ensure everything is well seasoned
