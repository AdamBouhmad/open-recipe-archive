## Ingredients

- twenty small pieces of loose pork fat
- twenty chipolata sausages
- twenty mushrooms
- twenty roasted chestnuts
- twenty quenelles
- twelve rounded earth truffles
- one glass of Madeira wine
- a little gelatin

## Directions

1. Put twenty small pieces of loose pork fat in a saucepan along with twenty chipolata sausages (prepared as described separately), and twenty mushrooms
2. Shape twenty roasted chestnuts and quenelles the same thickness as the sausages, and round twelve earth truffles
3. Cook everything together with a glass of Madeira wine and a little gelatin
