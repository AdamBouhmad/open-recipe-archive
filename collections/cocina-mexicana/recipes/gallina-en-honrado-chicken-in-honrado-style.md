## Ingredients

- four to six beets
- one large ripe tomato
- a few peppercorns
- clove spice
- two garlic cloves
- two wide chiles, deveined and well soaked
- butter
- chicken broth
- salt
- cooked chorizos
- chiles in vinegar
- olives
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- beet slices
- pineapple
- sweet potato
- apple or pear
- mild vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook four to six beets, then peel them.
2. Grind half of the beets with one large ripe tomato, a few peppercorns, clove spice, two garlic cloves, and two wide deveined and well-soaked chiles.
3. Fry this ground mixture in butter.
4. Add the chicken broth with sufficient salt, the chicken, and cooked chorizos.
5. Add chiles in vinegar, olives, raisins, almonds, capers, beet slices, and fruit such as pineapple, sweet potato, apple, or pear.
6. Let it boil until it thickens well.
7. At serving time, add a little mild vinegar.
