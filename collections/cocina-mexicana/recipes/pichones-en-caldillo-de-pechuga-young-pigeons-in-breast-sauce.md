## Ingredients

- cooked chicken breasts
- fried toast in lard
- clove
- cinnamon
- cooked egg yolks
- salt
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- wine

## Directions

1. After cooking, grind the chicken breasts with toast fried in lard, clove, cinnamon and cooked egg yolks
2. Fry all the ground mixture, season with salt, add broth from the pigeons and wine in sufficient quantity
3. Place the pigeons in this sauce
