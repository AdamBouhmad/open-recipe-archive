## Ingredients

- cabrajo (crab)
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- parsley, minced
- garlic, minced
- salt
- coarse pepper
- crab roe
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Break the shells of the crab and open it from the back of the head to the tail
2. Remove everything found inside the body with a spoon
3. Crush well with the back of a machete
4. Place in an earthenware dish with one tablespoon of mustard, minced parsley and garlic, salt, coarse pepper, and the roe commonly found under the tail
5. Dissolve everything with oil and vinegar, making a mustard sauce
6. Taste to ensure it is well-flavored
7. Separate and pour into a sauce boat, serving alongside the crab
