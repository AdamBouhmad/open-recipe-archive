## Ingredients

- 2 beef strips
- 1 pheasant or 4 partridges
- ham (amount equal to half a beef strip)
- 4 or 5 large carrots
- 5 onions (one studded with 5 cloves)
- 1 bottle dry Madeira wine
- 1 ladle of gelatin
- meat pieces from beef strips
- mushrooms
- bunch of parsley and onion
- some shallots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- sustancia roja (red substance)
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 2 beef strips, 1 pheasant or 4 partridges, ham equal to half a beef strip, 4 or 5 large carrots and 5 onions (one studded with 5 cloves) in a pan
2. Moisten the meats with 1 bottle of dry Madeira wine and 1 ladle of gelatin
3. Put the pan over high fire
4. When the broth has almost consumed, reduce to low fire
5. When the gelatin is a deep yellow, remove the pan from the fire and leave it for ten minutes so the gelatin can release well
6. Sweat the pieces of meat from the lower part of the beef strips, the same way as was done with these for the main sauce, and moisten that sauce with its broth
7. Well skimmed, dissolve a little red substance in it and pour it over the meat
8. Add mushrooms, a bunch of parsley and onion, some shallots, thyme and bay leaf
9. Once the sauce boils, place it in a corner of the stove so it only simmers gently until the meats are cooked
10. This sauce should have a beautiful color, neither too dark nor too light
