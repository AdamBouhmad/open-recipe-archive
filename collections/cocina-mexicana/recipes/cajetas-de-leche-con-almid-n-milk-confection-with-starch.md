## Ingredients

- very white sugar
- starch, washed in two waters and strained through a sieve
- half a pound of clean ground almonds

## Directions

1. Strain very white sugar and put it on the fire
2. When it has boiled a few times, add a small bowl of starch that has been washed in two waters and strained through a sieve with the third water
3. When it begins to reach the point of cajeta, add half a pound of clean ground almonds
4. Let it take on the proper consistency
