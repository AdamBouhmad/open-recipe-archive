## Ingredients

- cod
- onions
- tomato
- garlic
- pepper
- cinnamon
- oil
- clove
- green chile

## Directions

1. After soaking, cut into pieces and dry with a clean cloth
2. In a casserole put a good layer of onions cut into thick rounds, tomato, a few garlic cloves and a little pepper and cinnamon
3. Over them put another layer of cod pieces, over it another of onion, tomato and garlic, and so continue until the casserole is full
4. Add oil abundantly, a little clove and green chile and put on low heat, without adding broth until the juice from the tomato and onion has been consumed
