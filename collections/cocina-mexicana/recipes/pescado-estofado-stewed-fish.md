## Ingredients

- tomato slices (xitomate)
- onion slices
- bobo or other fish portions
- wide chiles, deveined and half-toasted
- cinnamon, ground
- cloves, ground
- pepper, ground
- oregano
- oil (one cup)
- good wine (one cup)
- or strong vinegar (some use in place of wine)

## Directions

1. Make a layer of thin tomato and onion slices in a large pot
2. Place fish portions on top
3. Cover with deveined, half-toasted and broken wide chiles
4. Sprinkle ground cinnamon, cloves and pepper and oregano over the chiles
5. Add one cup oil and one cup good wine (or strong vinegar instead)
6. Season to taste
7. Bury the pot in hot coals and cover with a comal with fire on top
8. Let cook in this manner
