## Ingredients

- Small turkey
- Oil
- Salt
- Diable sauce

## Directions

1. Gather the bird as described in previous articles
2. Place on the spit and roast, frequently turning it with oil and dusting with salt
3. When cooked, dress it and pour diable sauce over it
