## Ingredients

- strips of ham
- split garlic cloves
- cloves of spice
- strips of cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- cloves
- toasted cilantro
- saffron
- chopped parsley
- water
- vinegar
- almonds
- capers
- olives
- cured small chiles

## Directions

1. Stuff the chicken with strips of ham, split garlic cloves, cloves of spice, and strips of cinnamon
2. Fry in a pot with plenty of lard and necessary salt
3. Fry roasted and ground tomatoes with cloves, toasted cilantro, and saffron in the same fat
4. Add plenty of chopped parsley, water, vinegar, almonds, and capers
5. Cook the chicken in this broth
6. When it is cooked, add olives and cured small chiles
