## Ingredients

- Lean, chopped pork meat
- Beef leg meat equal in weight
- Ham cut into small cubes
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Broken peppercorns
- Saltpeter
- Beef or other large intestines

## Directions

1. Chop the lean pork meat and mix with beef leg meat equal in weight
2. Cut ham into small squares and mix with the minced meat
3. For six pounds of prepared meat, add five ounces salt, one dram of powdered pepper, one dram of broken peppercorns in grain, and three drams of saltpeter
4. Mix everything exactly and let rest overnight
5. Fill beef intestines or other large intestines, press the meat down firmly with a smooth stick, and tie like a bundle of tobacco
6. Leave in the curing room for eight days to cure well in salt mixed with equal quantity of saltpeter
7. Dry in the chimney or over smoke, coating with wine lees in which sage, thyme, bay leaf, and basil have been boiled
8. When dry, wrap in paper and store in ash
