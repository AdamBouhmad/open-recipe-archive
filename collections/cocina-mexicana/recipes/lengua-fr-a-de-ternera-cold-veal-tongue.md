## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- lemon
- salt
- water
- thyme
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Pound and wash the tongue well
2. Split it lengthwise and add lemon and salt
3. Place in the sun, adding more lemon if it dries too much
4. After four or five days, wash it well and cook in salted water with thyme
5. Let it cool
6. Serve dusted with pepper or without it
7. This tongue is also good for adding to a pot and serving with the cooked dish
