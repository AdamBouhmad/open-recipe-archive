## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Water
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch and put in cold water
2. Put to boil in a pot
3. When boiling, add apricots to cook
4. When they rise, remove and put in cold water with salt
5. Leave in water for about two hours, changing the water two or three times until no salt remains
6. For every pound of fruit, add one pound of sugar
7. Clarify so it is neither too thick nor too thin
8. Let cool and add the apricots
9. Bring to boil over gentle heat until it becomes preserve
