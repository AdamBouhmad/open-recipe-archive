## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit, cleaned
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- anchovies

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned rabbit lengthwise down the middle
2. Flatten it well with a mallet
3. Wrap it in buttered paper after coating it with a little vinegar, salt, and pepper
4. Cook over a moderate fire
5. When cooked, serve with anchovy butter
