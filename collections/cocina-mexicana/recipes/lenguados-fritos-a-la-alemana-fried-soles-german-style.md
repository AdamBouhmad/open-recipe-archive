## Ingredients

- sole fish
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- eggs
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Empty and clean them and wrap in cloth to dry the water
2. Cut into slices two fingers wide and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Coat in an egg batter in which a little melted butter has been added
4. Place in a good hot oil that is not too hot so the fish does not turn black before cooking
5. Serve on a napkin with two halves of lemon
6. This method of preparing sole is commonly preferred because it comes out better and is easier to distribute on a table as a roasted course
