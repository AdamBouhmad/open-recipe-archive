## Ingredients

- 12 fresh egg yolks
- 4 glasses Madeira wine or excellent white wine
- 6 ounces sugar
- a little cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Put in a pot 12 fresh egg yolks, 4 glasses of Madeira wine or excellent white wine, 6 ounces of sugar and a little cinnamon powder
2. Place the pot over high heat and beat the contents with a whisk until the foam fills the pot
3. Serve immediately without delay
