## Ingredients

- White fish
- Coarse salt
- Flour
- Oil or lard
- Tomatoes
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Green roasted chiles
- Onion
- Salt

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing, coat with coarse salt
2. Roll in flour and fry in oil or lard until well browned
3. Serve with a sauce of roasted and ground tomatoes seasoned with salt, oil, vinegar, oregano, roasted and peeled green chiles and minced onion
4. Alternatively, substitute this sauce with one of onion alone, seasoned with salt, oil and vinegar
