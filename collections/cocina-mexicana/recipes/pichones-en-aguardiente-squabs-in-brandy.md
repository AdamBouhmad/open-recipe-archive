## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Ham
- Half a quarter-liter of refined brandy
- Sugar, about the size of a walnut
- Strained veal broth
- Essence of sautéed vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot as for cooking à la brasa
2. Begin with gentle heat to make the contents sweat
3. Stir to create a crust
4. Remove the ham and degrease
5. Place the squabs breast-side down
6. Add half a quarter-liter of refined brandy and sugar about the size of a walnut
7. Allow to boil to the point of caramel
8. Add strained veal broth
9. Place a little essence of sautéed vegetables in a plate with the squab sauce
10. Arrange the squabs on top and serve hot
