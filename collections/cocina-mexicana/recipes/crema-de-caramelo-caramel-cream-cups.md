## Ingredients

- One lump of sugar the size of half an egg
- A little water
- A lemon rind
- Eight cups or small pots full of milk
- Four ounces of sugar
- A little lemon rind
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a lump of sugar the size of half an egg in a saucepan with a little water and a lemon rind, and let it boil until the caramel is formed, which should have a somewhat dark color
2. Measure eight cups or small pots full of milk and bring to boil with four ounces of sugar and a little lemon rind, then let cool
3. Dissolve six egg yolks in the cooled mixture and pass everything four times through a strainer
4. Boil water and place the cups in it, fill them with the said mixture and let them set in a bain-marie
