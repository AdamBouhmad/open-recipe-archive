## Ingredients

- Cooked chicken
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Tomatoes, finely chopped
- Salt
- Cooked and ground tomatoes
- Parsley
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Toasted cilantro
- Fried bread
- Vinegar
- Chicken broth
- Oil
- Olives
- Small chiles

## Directions

1. Fry the cooked chicken in lard and remove it
2. In the same lard, fry finely chopped garlic, onions, and tomatoes
3. Season with salt
4. Once fried, add cooked and ground tomatoes mixed with plenty of parsley, clove, pepper, cumin, toasted cilantro, and fried bread
5. Add a little vinegar
6. Place the chicken with its broth in the pan
7. Allow to simmer until fully seasoned
8. When removing from heat, add oil, olives, and small chiles
