## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- manteca (lard)
- ajo (garlic)
- cebollas (onions)
- peregil (parsley)
- jamón (ham)
- harina (flour)
- caldo (broth)
- clavo (clove)
- canela (cinnamon)
- pimienta molida (ground pepper)
- vino blanco (white wine)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- azúcar (sugar)
- pasas (raisins)
- almendras picadas (chopped almonds)
- alcaparras (capers)

## Directions

1. After cooking the squabs, fry garlic, onions, plenty of parsley and finely chopped ham in lard
2. Once this is fried, add some flour to brown it, salt and broth from the same water in which the squabs were cooked
3. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper, a glass of white wine, a splash of vinegar and a piece of sugar, raisins, chopped almonds and capers
4. Let the sauce thicken before serving
