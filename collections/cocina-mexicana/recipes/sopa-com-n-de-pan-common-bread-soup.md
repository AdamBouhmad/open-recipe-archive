## Ingredients

- Bread, sliced and toasted
- Broth from the pot
- Coriander (culantro), dried, toasted and ground
- Parsley
- Poultry giblets, chopped
- Fat ham pieces
- Lean ham shreds
- Chickpeas

## Directions

1. Slice bread and toast without burning
2. Place in a cooking pot
3. Add broth from the pot and place the pot over two gentle fires
4. Once it boils sufficiently, remove from heat
5. Add a little dried, toasted and ground coriander
6. Garnish with parsley leaves, chopped poultry giblets, fat ham pieces, or shreds of lean ham and chickpeas
7. Be careful not to boil too much as the soup becomes like paste
