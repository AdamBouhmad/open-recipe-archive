## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- raisins
- almonds
- sesame seeds
- orange blossom water
- white wine
- sugar
- beaten egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook eggs and remove yolks and grind them
2. Mix with raisins and almonds, then add sesame seeds and orange blossom water along with white wine and sugar to sweeten
3. Form into long pieces like mamones
4. Coat with beaten egg and fry in lard
5. Place in syrup
