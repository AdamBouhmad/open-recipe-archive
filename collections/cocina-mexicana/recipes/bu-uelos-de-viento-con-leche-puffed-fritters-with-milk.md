## Ingredients

- One caldron cup of milk
- Half cup of aniseed water
- A grain of tequesquite
- A little lard
- Salt
- One cup of fine flour
- Eight well-beaten eggs
- Sugar and cinnamon or syrup

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, aniseed water, tequesquite, lard, and salt together
2. Once boiling, add one cup of fine flour, stirring with a spoon until it forms a ball
3. Beat with the hand; when it no longer sticks, it is ready
4. Let it cool, then dissolve it in the eight well-beaten eggs, ensuring there are no lumps
5. Let rest for one hour
6. Take spoonfuls of the dough and fry in well-heated lard
7. Once golden, remove and dust with sugar and cinnamon, or dip in syrup and serve
