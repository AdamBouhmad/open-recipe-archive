## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- one pound of sugar
- a little cinnamon
- six ounces of butter
- twelve egg yolks
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- one real biscuit cake, grated and crumbled
- zest of one Chinese orange, grated
- six more ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Boil six cuartillos of milk with one pound of sugar and a little cinnamon, and let it cool
2. Mix in six ounces of butter, twelve egg yolks, raisins, almonds, pine nuts and one real biscuit cake that has been grated and crumbled
3. Add the grated zest of a Chinese orange
4. Pour into a dish greased with six ounces of butter
5. Let it set at two fires and stud afterwards
