## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- sixteen beaten egg yolks
- powdered sugar for rolling

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with one pound of sugar brought to medium ball stage
2. Let it cool
3. Add sixteen beaten egg yolks
4. Return to the fire, stirring continuously, until it reaches the point of pulling away from the pan
5. Remove from heat and form small candies with the paste
6. Roll or coat them with ground powdered sugar
7. Wrap each one in paper
