## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- twelve egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- a little butter
- cold water as needed

## Directions

1. Mix twelve egg yolks with one pound of flour, a pinch of salt, a little butter, and cold water as needed to bind the flour
2. Knead everything
3. Form rosquettes and fry in butter
4. Dip in boiling syrup and roll in sifted sugar mixed with powdered cinnamon
