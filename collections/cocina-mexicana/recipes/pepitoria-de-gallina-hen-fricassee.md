## Ingredients

- hen, cut into pieces
- butter
- parsley sprigs
- small onions
- flour (one tablespoon)
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the hen into pieces and blanch three minutes in boiling water
2. Drain and place in a saucepan with butter, parsley sprigs, and small onions
3. Sauté everything together
4. Add one tablespoon of flour and moisten with broth
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Cook until done
7. Remove the quarters of hen and reduce the sauce
8. Strain the sauce, bind with egg yolks without boiling
9. Pour over the pieces of hen with lemon juice
