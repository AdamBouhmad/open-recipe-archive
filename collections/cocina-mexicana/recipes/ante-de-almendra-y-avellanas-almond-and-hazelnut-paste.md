## Ingredients

- 1½ pounds sugar
- 1 pound almonds, cleaned and ground
- 1 pound hazelnuts, cleaned and ground
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of medium point with 1½ pounds sugar, well clarified and strained
2. Let it cool slightly
3. Mix in the ground almonds and hazelnuts
4. Stir everything together
5. Add a little orange blossom water
6. Place over the steam of a boiling pot so that with only that heat it reaches the fish-eye stage
7. Let it cool to make portions afterwards
