## Ingredients

- toasted almonds
- cooked egg yolks
- pasilla chiles, toasted and soaked
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- poultry and its broth
- vinegar
- sugar
- sliced apples
- cooked zucchini

## Directions

1. Grind toasted almonds, cooked egg yolks, and toasted and soaked pasilla chiles with clove, pepper, and cinnamon
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. Add the poultry, its broth, vinegar, sugar, sliced apples, and cooked zucchini
4. Finish like the others
