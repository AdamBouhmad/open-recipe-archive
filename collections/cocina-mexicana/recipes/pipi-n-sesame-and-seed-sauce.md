## Ingredients

- ajonjolí (sesame seeds)
- chile
- tomillo (thyme)
- bizcochos tostados (toasted biscuits)
- pepitas de calabaza (pumpkin seeds)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo (broth)

## Directions

1. Toast sesame seeds and grind them
2. Soak chile in water
3. Grind a little thyme, some toasted biscuits, and pumpkin seeds (which must be toasted first), making sure to have one plate of seeds
4. Incorporate everything together very well
5. Toast the chile in lard, then dissolve it in broth and season
