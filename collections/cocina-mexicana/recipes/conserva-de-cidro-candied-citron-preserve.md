## Ingredients

- citron
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Remove the outer skin with a knife
2. Place in water and set in the sun
3. Change the water daily and test until the citron no longer tastes bitter
4. Add to fine syrup, give one boil, and set aside
5. The next day and the day after, boil again
6. On the third day, boil with orange blossom water and test the syrup for proper consistency
7. This preserve can be made in sections, small pieces, or slices; for slices, after cutting them like lemon wheels with a knife, cut and remove a long thin strip, roll it up, and secure it with needle and thread so it does not unroll; at the end remove the thread and add orange blossom water
