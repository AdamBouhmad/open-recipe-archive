## Ingredients

- Lamb tripe
- Lemon
- Cooked tripe
- Tomato without seeds
- Six garlic cloves
- Bunch of onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- Ham pieces
- Whole chorizos
- Testicles sliced
- Capers
- Olives
- Chiles in vinegar
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Six peppercorns
- Six cloves
- Saffron threads
- Salt
- Water
- Cinnamon
- Sherry wine
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe very well, making sure the hole is not too large
2. Turn the tripe inside out to remove all impurities
3. Heat water, pour over the tripe and remove immediately
4. Scrape with a knife until clean
5. Take the intestines, turn them inside out after washing, wash inside by squeezing lemon juice in the water and rub well with lemon peel
6. Cook all together except the tripe with water and salt
7. When cooked, cut into small pieces
8. Chop tomato without seeds, six garlic cloves, a bunch of onion and some parsley, all very finely
9. Put this in a pot with butter, first add the garlic, before it browns add the onion, then the tomato and parsley
10. Let fry well, then add water and the intestines, ham pieces, whole chorizos, sliced testicles, capers, olives, chiles in vinegar, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
11. Grind six peppercorns, six cloves, and some saffron threads with salt, add to the pot and boil until completely dry like ground meat, then remove from heat
12. Take the tripe, fill it through the hole and sew it closed
13. Place a pot with butter, salt and water on the heat and add the tripe; the water should cover it, turning it so it does not burn
14. In another pot, cook ground tomatoes without seeds, fry well, and add to the pot with the tripe
15. Let reduce greatly, add clove, cinnamon, pepper and saffron all ground, thyme, chopped parsley, and some Sherry wine
16. The sauce should be thick and garnish with lettuce leaves
