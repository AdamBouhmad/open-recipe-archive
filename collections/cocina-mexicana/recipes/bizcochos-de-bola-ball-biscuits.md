## Ingredients

- ten and a half pounds of flour
- two and a half pounds of refined sugar
- eight eggs with whites and yolks
- three pounds of leavening
- a little warm water
- lard up to two pounds

## Directions

1. Put the flour in a trough and mix it with the refined sugar
2. Add the eggs and warm water
3. Knead everything, and at the same time gradually add lard underneath until the dough absorbs two pounds
4. Let it rest and rise until the following day
5. Knead again adding four more eggs and a little more lard as before
6. Let it rise again for half an hour
7. Cut the biscuits in the manner of molletes
8. Put them immediately to bake in the oven
