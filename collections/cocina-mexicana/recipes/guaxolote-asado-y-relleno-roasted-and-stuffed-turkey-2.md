## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Picadillo of various meats
- Sausages
- Roasted chestnuts (optional)
- Mushrooms
- Liver
- Parsley
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Watercress
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey with bacon and place it on the spit
2. Allow an hour and a half for it to roast perfectly
3. It can be stuffed with picadillo of all kinds of meats mixed with sausages, and if desired, with roasted chestnuts
4. Another filling can be made with abundant mushrooms, liver, parsley and onions, all chopped
5. Serve over watercress seasoned with salt and vinegar
