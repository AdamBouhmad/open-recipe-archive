## Ingredients

- Sugar, very white
- Water

## Directions

1. Make the same as the previous article, with the difference that the syrup should not be toasted
2. The syrup should be made from very white sugar
3. When it reaches the appropriate point, proceed to cut the honeycombs after beating the syrup as stated, finishing in the same manner
4. To make them rose-colored, add a little panecillo to the syrup when it begins to cook, according to the quantity needed
5. Note that in all honeycombs, care should be taken to stir the syrup continuously with a round spoon until it reaches the point of half caramel, which is the point it should have
