## Ingredients

- Hare
- hare liver
- bread crumb
- cream
- butter
- egg yolks
- onion
- sage
- ham
- oiled paper
- cherry jelly

## Directions

1. Empty the hare and leave it the paws, nails and ears, which will be scalded with boiling water.
2. Make a stuffing composed of the hare's liver, bread crumb soaked in cream, butter, egg yolks and a little onion and sage, all well crushed.
3. Stuff the hare's body with this, and sewing its belly, tie its paws with a thread so that it takes the posture of lying in its burrow.
4. Lard it with ham, wrap it in oiled paper and place it on the spit.
5. When it is cooked, remove the paper and ham, serving separately cherry jelly or another similar one.
