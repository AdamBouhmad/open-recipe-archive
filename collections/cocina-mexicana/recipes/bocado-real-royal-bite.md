## Ingredients

- One pound of ground almond
- One and a half pounds of ground sugar
- Orange-blossom water
- Scent pastille
- Toasted sesame
- Slices of sponge cake
- Beaten egg for frying
- Virgin honey
- A little white wine
- Orange-blossom water
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Citron
- Toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of ground almond with one and a half pounds of ground sugar
2. Add orange-blossom water, scent pastille and toasted sesame, leaving the paste soft enough to spread on slices of sponge cake
3. Spread the paste on each slice of cake and cover with another slice
4. Wrap both slices in beaten egg and fry like torrijas (French toast)
5. Make a compound syrup with virgin honey, a little white wine, orange-blossom water, raisins, almonds, citron and toasted sesame
6. When serving, arrange in layers on a platter, alternating layers of fried sponge cake and compound syrup
