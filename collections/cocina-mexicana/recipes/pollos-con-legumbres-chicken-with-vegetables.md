## Ingredients

- Hot lard
- Ground garlic
- Cooked chicken, quartered
- Cabbage
- Carrots
- Lettuce
- Green beans, chopped
- Peas
- Small onions
- Chorizos
- Strips of ham
- Capers
- Olives
- Chicken broth

## Directions

1. Heat lard and fry ground garlic in it
2. Add the cooked chicken quartered, along with cabbage, carrots, lettuce, chopped green beans, peas, small onions, chorizos, and strips of ham
3. Cook everything together
4. Add capers and olives
5. Pour in a little of the chicken broth
6. Season with clove, pepper, and salt
7. Let everything simmer until most of the broth has been consumed
