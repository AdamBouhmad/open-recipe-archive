## Ingredients

- milk, four cuartillos
- egg yolks, four
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- sponge cake, ground

## Directions

1. To four cuartillos of milk add four egg yolks, a little sugar and cinnamon
2. Place over fire to boil much
3. When at a very high point, butter a platter and lay a layer of ground sponge cake on top
4. Pour the milk which is dusted with more sponge cake and cinnamon
5. Leave to cook and set over two fires
