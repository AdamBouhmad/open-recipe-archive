## Ingredients

- Eels
- Some carp meat
- Fresh butter
- Parsley
- Onions
- Some shallots
- One head of garlic
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Some eggs
- Pork or beef intestines
- White wine
- Water
- Roots

## Directions

1. Clean the eels, break them up and mince the meat very finely with some carp meat
2. Add fresh butter, a little parsley and onions, both chopped, some shallots and one head of garlic
3. Season everything with salt and fine spices, mix with some eggs, and fill the pork or beef intestines with it, tying at proportional distances according to the desired size of the sausages
4. Put to smoke in the chimney for three days and then cook in white wine and a little water, with onions, roots and good seasoning
5. Serve as an intermediate course
