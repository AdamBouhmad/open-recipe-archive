## Ingredients

- Twelve egg yolks
- Two egg whites
- A pinch of tequesquite
- Four calderas of curdled milk (squeezed and ground)
- Two calderas of corn flour (prepared as in previous recipes)
- Half pound of powdered sugar
- One small bowl of melted lard

## Directions

1. Beat very well, as for marquesote, the twelve egg yolks and two egg whites with a pinch of tequesquite
2. Mix these with four calderas of squeezed and ground curdled milk
3. Add two calderas of corn flour prepared as in previous recipes
4. Add half pound of powdered sugar
5. Add one small bowl of melted lard
6. Proceed as previously described, with the oven somewhat hotter than for baking marquesotes
