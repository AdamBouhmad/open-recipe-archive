## Ingredients

- One fatted young hen
- Ham
- Coarse salt

## Directions

1. After passing it through the flame, empty and collect it, singe it a moment, place a slice of ham on the belly so it stays white, tie it with thread and put it to cook in a pot.
2. When it yields to the finger upon touching it at the leg, serve it with a little broth and coarse salt sprinkled over the top.
