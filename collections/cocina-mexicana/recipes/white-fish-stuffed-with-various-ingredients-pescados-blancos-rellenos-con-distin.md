## Ingredients

- white fish
- bread crumbs
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- capers desalted
- onion
- chard
- spinach
- peas
- paper or lard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare fish as in previous recipes
2. Stuff with one of the following mixtures: bread crumbs mixed with oil and seasoned with salt and pepper; desalted capers mixed with oil and chopped onion; cooked chard and spinach; or peas
3. Wrap in oiled or lard-coated papers with bread crumbs and pepper, or sprinkle with vinegar
