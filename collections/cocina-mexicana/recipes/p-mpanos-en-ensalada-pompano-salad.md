## Ingredients

- pompano, cleaned and cooked
- flour
- lard or oil
- lettuce leaves
- lettuce hearts
- olives
- pickled peppers
- capers
- tomatoes, roasted and ground
- green chiles, roasted and chopped into small pieces
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- oregano, dried

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the pompano
2. Roll in flour and fry in lard or oil
3. Arrange on a platter over lettuce leaves, surrounded by lettuce hearts
4. Decorate all around with olives, pickled peppers, and capers
5. Make a sauce by mixing roasted and ground tomatoes, roasted green chiles cut into small pieces, plenty of oil, and a little vinegar, seasoned with salt and sprinkled with dried oregano leaves without stems
6. Serve the sauce separately at the table
