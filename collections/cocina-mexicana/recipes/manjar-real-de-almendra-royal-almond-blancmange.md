## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one chicken breast, cooked without salt and ground
- one pound of peeled and ground almonds
- toasted almonds

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with two pounds of sugar, and add one chicken breast cooked without salt and ground
2. As soon as it has boiled, add one pound of peeled and ground almonds, and keep stirring until it reaches the point of separating from the pan
3. Pour into a platter and stud with toasted almonds
4. It is varied by mixing sesame with it, and placing it also on top with sesame
