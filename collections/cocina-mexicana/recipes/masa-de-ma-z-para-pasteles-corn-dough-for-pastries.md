## Ingredients

- Corn prepared as for tortillas
- Salt water
- Sugar
- Cloves and cinnamon
- A few hard egg yolks
- A little butter

## Directions

1. Wash the corn very well
2. Remove the tips
3. Let it dry
4. Break it apart when dry
5. Add to a little salted water
6. Add sufficient sugar, cloves, cinnamon, a few hard egg yolks, and a little butter
7. Grind everything very well to form a dough
8. Form sheets with the same metlapil, lowering them on the metate as tortillas are formed
9. With these sheets greased with butter, form the pastry
