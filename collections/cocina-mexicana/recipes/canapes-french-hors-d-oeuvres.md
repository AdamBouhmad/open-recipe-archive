## Ingredients

- Slices of bread crumb
- Oil for frying
- Anchovy strips
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Green seasoning (recaudo verde)
- Gherkins
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread crumb about the thickness of a finger
2. Remove the crusts
3. Fry in oil until they take on a beautiful color and drain
4. Chop the egg yolks separately and also chop green seasoning, gherkins and capers
5. Season everything with vinegar, pepper and salt
6. Garnish the toasted pieces with strips of anchovies and egg whites and the chopped mixture
7. Sprinkle with oil and serve on small supernumerary platters
