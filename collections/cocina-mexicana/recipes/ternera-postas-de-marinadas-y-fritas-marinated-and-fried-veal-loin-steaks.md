## Ingredients

- Veal loin, sliced
- Vinegar
- Cumin
- Garlic, ground
- Salt
- Water
- Tomato, ground
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Lard

## Directions

1. Make slices of the loin, bruise them slightly in the molcajete, and marinate in vinegar with a little cumin, ground garlic, and salt
2. The following day cook in sufficient water with ground tomato, onion, and bay leaves
3. When cooked, remove and fry in another pot with lard
4. The same can be made with beef or cow loins
