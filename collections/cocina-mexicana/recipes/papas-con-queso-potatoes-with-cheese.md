## Ingredients

- potatoes
- ripe tomatoes
- garlic
- allspice
- cheese, grated
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes and slice them
2. Grind separately ripe tomatoes, garlic, and a little allspice
3. In a baking dish, make a layer of potatoes, then a layer of grated cheese, then a layer of ground spices, and continue alternating layers until the dish is full
4. Place over two gentle fires until the potatoes are cooked
5. Serve with chopped parsley sprinkled on top
