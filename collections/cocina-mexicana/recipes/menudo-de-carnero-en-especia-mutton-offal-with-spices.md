## Ingredients

- Mutton offal
- Salt
- Garlic cloves
- Lard
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Chopped garlic
- Onion slices
- Green chiles
- Parsley, chopped
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Saffron
- Culantro, toasted
- Fried bread, ground
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. After thoroughly washing and cleaning the offal, cook it in water with salt and crushed garlic cloves
2. Divide the cooked offal into regular-sized pieces
3. Parboil the pieces in plenty of lard
4. Remove them from the cazuela and add roasted and ground tomatoes, chopped garlic, onion slices and green chiles divided lengthwise to the same lard
5. When this mixture has macerated a little, add much chopped parsley and season with salt, cloves, pepper, cumin, toasted saffron and culantro, all ground
6. Thicken with fried and ground bread
7. Add capers and a little vinegar
8. Add the offal with the broth in which it cooked
9. Let it boil a lot so that it seasons well
10. When removing from heat, add considerable oil and garnish with small chiles and olives
