## Ingredients

- shrimp
- lard
- dried chile
- vinegar
- garlic
- salt
- eggs
- potatoes

## Directions

1. With the shrimp prepared and cooked as described in previous articles, fry in lard some dried chiles that have been deveined, soaked and ground with garlic
2. Once done, add the shrimp with the water in which they were cooked, the corresponding salt and some eggs half-beaten, stirring well and letting it boil until the broth reaches a good consistency
3. If desired, cooked and sliced potatoes can also be added
