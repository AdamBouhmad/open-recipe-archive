## Ingredients

- pheasant
- ham
- garlic
- butter or lard
- potatoes, chopped
- salt
- thirty to forty roasted chestnuts
- minced meat mixture
- thin slices of beef or mutton
- ham slices
- Malaga, Jerez, or Carlon wine
- water
- roasted and ground chestnuts
- cooked and chopped potatoes

## Directions

1. Lard the pheasant with ham and garlic and parboil in butter or lard with chopped potatoes and sufficient salt
2. Set aside and let cool, then remove from the fat
3. Fry thirty to forty roasted chestnuts in the fat
4. Stuff the pheasant with the chestnuts alone or mixed with regular minced meat
5. Sew up the opening and wrap in thin slices of beef or mutton
6. Place in a casserole over slices of fat ham
7. Add Malaga, Jerez, or Carlon wine, a little water, and salt
8. Return to the fire until cooked
9. Skim the fat from the broth and thicken it over the fire with roasted and ground chestnuts and cooked and chopped potatoes
10. When thickened, pour over the pheasant to serve
