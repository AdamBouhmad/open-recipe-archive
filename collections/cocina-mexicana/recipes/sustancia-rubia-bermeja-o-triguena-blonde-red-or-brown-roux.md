## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one quarter (liquid measure) of flour, or more if the butter can absorb it

## Directions

1. Place the saucepan over somewhat lively fire and stir constantly until the substance becomes slightly yellow or brown
2. Then place the saucepan over gentle fire, covered with a little ash, and allow the substance to slowly acquire a beautiful blonde or reddish color
3. Only wheat flour should be used, because the others are not capable of binding well
