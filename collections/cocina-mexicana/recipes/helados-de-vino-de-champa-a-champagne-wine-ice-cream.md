## Ingredients

- 6 lemons
- 1 quartillo of water
- 8 ounces powdered sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 1 quartillo of white Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Scrape six lemons as finely as possible, and place the scrapings in a quartillo of water, where they will remain for two hours so that the water takes on their flavor
2. Divide the lemon pulps in half and squeeze the juice into the shell water, stirring everything with eight ounces of powdered sugar, stirring with a spatula or spoon, and letting the sugar melt perfectly
3. Pass it then through a sieve, beat four egg whites until reduced to snow, mixing with the lemon syrup or preserve and continuing to stir the mixture, until everything is well incorporated
4. Add in this case a quartillo of white Champagne wine, and place it in the sorbetera to freeze
