## Ingredients

- Plovers
- oil
- lard
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- capers
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- vinegar
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. After washing the plovers well, fry them with a little oil and lard
2. When they begin to take color, add water until they are covered in excess
3. Add the appropriate salt and let them cook until all the water is consumed
4. Add plenty of sliced onion, and once it is fried, add a little boiling water
5. Grind plenty of parsley and an equal quantity of capers, with cloves, cinnamon, and pepper
6. Add a little strong vinegar and a piece of sugar
7. Let them boil until the broth thickens well
8. Serve them rolled in the broth, when taking them to the table, with a little vinegar
