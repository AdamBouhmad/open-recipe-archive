## Ingredients

- blood from pork
- salt
- one cup of rice
- water
- regular amount of chopped and cooked onion
- two or three roasted garlic heads, ground with cumin, clove, and pepper, ground separately and dry
- little finely chopped parsley
- six regular tomatoes, finely chopped
- less than one cup of tomatoes, chopped the same way
- little mint, leaves removed
- little Chinese oregano
- six wide chiles, toasted and broken into pieces
- fat from the tripe, finely chopped, yielding about one and a half cups
- pork intestines, cleaned

## Directions

1. In the bowl where blood is collected when slaughtering the pig, add salt and let the blood rest until it cools, stirring as it breaks down
2. Cook one cup of rice with little water and salt until whole and well cooked
3. Cook a regular amount of chopped onion, drain well after cooking
4. Roast two or three heads of garlic and grind with cumin, clove, and pepper (each ground separately and dry)
5. Chop little parsley very finely
6. Chop six regular tomatoes finely
7. Chop less than one cup of tomatoes the same way
8. Remove mint leaves
9. Toast and break into pieces six wide chiles
10. Chop the fat from the tripe finely, yielding about one and a half cups
11. Mix everything with the blood and taste
12. Add whatever is lacking and let infuse for two hours
13. After washing the intestines, fill them, leaving space empty so they are not too crowded
