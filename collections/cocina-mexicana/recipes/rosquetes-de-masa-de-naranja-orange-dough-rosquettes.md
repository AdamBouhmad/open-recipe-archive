## Ingredients

- thirty eggs
- juice of three sour oranges
- two calderas of melted butter
- one pound of ground sugar
- one pozuelo of settled tequesquite water
- three calderas of leaven
- flour as needed to make the dough soft
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Mix thirty eggs with the juice of three sour oranges, two calderas of melted butter, one pound of ground sugar, one pozuelo of settled tequesquite water, and three calderas of leaven
2. Add flour until the dough is soft
3. Form rosquettes
4. Brush lightly with egg white just enough to make sesame seeds adhere
5. Place on parchment and bake in the oven
