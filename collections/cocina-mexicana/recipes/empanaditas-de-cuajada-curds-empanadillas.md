## Ingredients

- Milk
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Proceed as for Puff Pastry Empanadas with Sugar and Wine
2. For the filling, curdle four small cups of milk
3. Squeeze the curds well
4. Mix with two egg whites and four egg yolks, four ounces of sugar, and a little butter
5. Beat until well incorporated
6. If the mixture is too thin and not consistent enough, add a little flour
7. Form small empanadillas
8. Fry in butter instead of baking in the oven
