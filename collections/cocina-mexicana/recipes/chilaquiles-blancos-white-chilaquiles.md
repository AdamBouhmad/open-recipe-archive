## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- green chiles
- tortillas made the day before
- aged cheese
- longaniza sausage
- morcón
- chorizo
- pork ribs
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes and green chiles, adding a little water of ash if desired when very sour
2. Once cooked, wash in fresh water and remove the skin from the tomato
3. Grind everything, seasoned with necessary fine salt
4. Fry very well, and when done, add sufficient water
5. Let boil until the sauce reaches a regular consistency
6. Break up and add tortillas made the day before
7. Once they have boiled until they begin to dry, add a little grated aged cheese, mix it in, and remove from heat
8. Serve with fried longaniza, morcón, chorizo, pork ribs, and slices of fresh cheese
