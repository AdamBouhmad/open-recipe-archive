## Ingredients

- beef loin
- carrots
- onions
- bacon strips
- parsley bunch
- white wine
- pot broth
- salt
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe and place in a pot to cook with some carrots and onions, strips of bacon, a bunch of parsley, white wine, pot broth and a little salt.
2. Bring to a boil over good heat, cover the pot with heat on top and decrease the heat below.
3. When the meat is cooked, strain the sauce, take a quantity of stock, and reduce it with a little Spanish sauce.
4. Place in it the vegetables cooked beforehand, such as cucumbers, chicories, etc.
5. This loin can also be served with tomato sauce.
