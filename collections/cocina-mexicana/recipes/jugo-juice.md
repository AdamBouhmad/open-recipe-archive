## Ingredients

- medium onions
- medium carrots
- meat and poultry scraps
- half quart of water or broth
- clove spice
- salt

## Directions

1. Place onions and carrots cut into slices in a pot and on top place scraps of meat and poultry with half a quart of water or broth
2. Cook on high heat and when it has a golden color, add boiling water, a clove spice and salt
3. Cook everything for one and a half hours, taking care to squeeze the meat well so it releases its juice
4. Strain the juice through a sieve and pour it into another vessel, placing the meats in the pot to give it good flavor
