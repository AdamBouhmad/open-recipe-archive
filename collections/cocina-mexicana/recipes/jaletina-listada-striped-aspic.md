## Ingredients

- Two quarts of cream
- Nine egg yolks
- A little salt
- Half a pound of sugar
- One and a half ounces of isinglass dissolved
- Orange blossom
- Spinach green (see green officinal of spinach, page 322) with some crushed bitter macaroons
- Blanc mange
- Chocolate infusion

## Directions

1. Cook two quarts of cream
2. Pour into a saucepan and dissolve nine egg yolks in it
3. Add a little salt and half a pound of sugar
4. Place the saucepan over very gentle heat, avoiding boiling, and stir until it thickens
5. Pass through fine cloth or a silk sieve
6. Add one and a half ounces of dissolved isinglass
7. Divide this composition into four equal parts, pouring each into a separate glass
8. To one part add orange blossom
9. To another add spinach green with some crushed bitter macaroons
10. In the third add blanc mange
11. In the last add a chocolate infusion
12. Pour the first infusion onto the mold on the ice to the thickness of a finger
13. When it has set, pour the second in the same amount
14. Continue in this manner until the mold is full
15. Sprinkle salt on the ice
16. At the moment of serving, unmold the aspic
