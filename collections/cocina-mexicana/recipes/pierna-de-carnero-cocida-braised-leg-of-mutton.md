## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lard
- water
- garlic
- onions
- carrots
- salt
- flour or starch
- escarole

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the leg
2. Place in a casserole over low heat with lard to brown on both sides, turning with a little water
3. Add two garlic cloves, four whole onions, and two carrots
4. Let cook slowly for six hours
5. Season with salt without spices
6. Skim the fat from the broth and thicken with a little flour or starch
7. Spread the thickening across the surface with a small brush
8. Serve alone, placing raw escarole under the platter
