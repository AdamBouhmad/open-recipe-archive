## Ingredients

- chichicuilotes
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- chopped tomato
- clove
- pepper
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Coat a pan with lard
2. Add garlic, onion, chopped tomato, clove, pepper, and parsley
3. Half-cook the chichicuilotes whole or cut in two halves
4. Place the aforementioned mixture on top with more lard, butter, clove and pepper
5. Cook over two fires
6. Serve with broth added or with fried bread slices
