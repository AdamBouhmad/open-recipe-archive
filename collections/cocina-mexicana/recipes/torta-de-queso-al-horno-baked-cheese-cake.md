## Ingredients

- Two pounds of aged cheese
- A clean napkin
- Water
- Fifteen egg whites, beaten
- One pound of white sugar
- Paper boxes
- Dragées

## Directions

1. Grind two pounds of aged cheese
2. Wrap in a clean napkin
3. Soak in water until the salt is removed
4. Mix with fifteen beaten egg whites and one pound of white sugar
5. Pour the mixture into paper boxes
6. Top with dragées
7. Bake in the oven
