## Ingredients

- pulp of mutton
- tocino slices
- carrots
- onions
- bundle of fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- defatted broth

## Directions

1. Lard the pulp and tie with string
2. Place in a pot with several slices of tocino, carrots, onions, a bundle of fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Cover with defatted broth
4. Cook over high heat
5. When boiling strongly, cover the fire with ash and cover the pot, placing fire on the lid
6. Leave until well cooked
7. Serve bathed in the juices
