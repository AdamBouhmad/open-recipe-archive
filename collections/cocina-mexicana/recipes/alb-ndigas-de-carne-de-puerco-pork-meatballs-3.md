## Ingredients

- pork loin and solid meat
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- bread
- ham
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- capers
- olives
- flour
- lard
- garlic
- tomato
- broth
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Mince the loin and solid meat, put it in a bowl and add clove, cinnamon, pepper, salt, three or four raw eggs and a piece of soaked bread
2. Mix well and take portions of minced meat the size of a large empanada, spread it on a clean board and mix in strips of ham, pieces of egg, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, capers and chopped olives
3. Form the empanada
4. Put a saucepan with water and when it is boiling add the dough
5. Once it is cooked, remove it and slice it to regular thickness, roll it in flour and fry it like stuffed peppers
6. In a saucepan put lard with a minced garlic, let it fry and add ground tomato without seeds; fry it and add to the saucepan the broth in which the empanada was fried, with clove, cinnamon and pepper
7. Let it boil and when the sauce is thick add the fried slices
8. When bringing them to the table, sprinkle with Sherry wine
