## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove powder
- toasted and ground sponge cake
- eggs
- lard
- medium-point syrup

## Directions

1. Grind cooked egg yolks and mix with sugar, cinnamon, clove powder, and ground toasted sponge cake to form a paste that can be sliced
2. Coat the slices with beaten egg
3. Fry in lard
4. Place in medium-point syrup until it reaches conserve point
5. Place on plates and dust with cinnamon
