## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- ham strips
- chorizo slices
- garlic cloves
- roasted tomatoes
- cloves
- ground pepper
- ground cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- salt
- white wine

## Directions

1. Fry ham strips, chorizo slices, and peeled garlic cloves in lard
2. Remove and grind the fried garlic with roasted tomatoes
3. Fry the ground mixture and add the fried ham and chorizo
4. Add ground cloves, pepper, cinnamon, raisins, almonds, salt and white wine
5. Place the already-larded and cooked tongue in this sauce
6. Let the sauce reduce considerably before serving all together
