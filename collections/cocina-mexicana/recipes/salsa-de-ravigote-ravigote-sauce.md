## Ingredients

- Fine herbs: parsley, scallion, chervil, and celery
- One tablespoon of capers
- One or two anchovies (optional)
- One raw egg yolk
- Salt
- Pepper
- One tablespoon of oil
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- One cup of fat-free broth (for cooked version)
- Fresh butter (for cooked version)
- Half tablespoon of flour (for cooked version)

## Directions

1. Grind the fine herbs - parsley, scallion, chervil, and celery - in a mortar with one tablespoon of capers and one or two anchovies if desired.
2. When everything is made into a paste, add one raw egg yolk, salt, pepper, one tablespoon of oil, and one tablespoon of vinegar.
3. Serve this sauce with cold meat.
4. If you wish to cook it, boil it for a quarter of an hour with one cup of fat-free broth.
5. When cooked, add fresh butter in proportion, kneaded with half a tablespoon of flour, and add salt, pepper, and more drops of vinegar.
6. Remove from heat as soon as the butter is melted and serve.
