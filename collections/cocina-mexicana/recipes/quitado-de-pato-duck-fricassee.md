## Ingredients

- ducks, plucked and cleaned
- water
- straw
- butter
- ham slices
- onion, finely chopped
- garlic
- parsley, finely chopped
- thyme
- oregano
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- fried bread slices
- chiles
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the ducks and remove the tail
2. Place in a pot with water and straw and bring to a boil
3. Remove from heat and wash once or twice with hot water
4. Cut into quarters
5. Heat butter in a casserole and add the ducks with ham slices, plenty of finely chopped onion, and garlic
6. Let everything fry well
7. Add water and a little finely chopped parsley, thyme, and oregano
8. Grind cloves, cinnamon, and pepper together with some fried bread slices
9. Add this mixture to the pot
10. Garnish the platter with chiles, capers, and olives
