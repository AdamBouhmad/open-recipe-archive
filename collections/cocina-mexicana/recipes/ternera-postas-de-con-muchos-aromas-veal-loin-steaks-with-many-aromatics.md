## Ingredients

- Veal loin, cut into slices
- Lard
- Salt
- Garlic, ground
- Roasted tomato, ground
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Make slices of the loin
2. Fry them raw in lard with salt and ground garlic
3. After frying, add roasted and ground tomato with cloves and black pepper
4. When it begins to crust, add the necessary water for them to cook
5. Let the small amount of broth be consumed over two fires
