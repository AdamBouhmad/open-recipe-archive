## Ingredients

- apples
- walnuts
- chickpeas
- sugar
- salt
- beaten eggs
- lard
- granjea

## Directions

1. Cook and grind apples and walnuts
2. Cook chickpeas separately and grind also
3. Mix everything together and add sugar and a little salt
4. Mix with beaten eggs
5. Place over two fires in a casserole greased with lard
6. Add granjea on top at the end
