## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 6 eggs with whites and yolks
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- butter
- aniseed

## Directions

1. Beat two by two the eggs with whites and yolks until it reaches the state of being able to form a small ball
2. Add salt
3. The butter should be neither hot nor cold so that they have space to puff up
4. Add syrup
