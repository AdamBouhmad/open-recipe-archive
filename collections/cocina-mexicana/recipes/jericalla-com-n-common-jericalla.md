## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- egg yolks
- canela molida

## Directions

1. Sweeten milk to taste and bring to a boil with some cinnamon sticks
2. When it has acquired some consistency, remove it from heat and stir constantly as it cools to prevent cream from forming
3. Add egg yolks in the proportion of one for each half quart of milk
4. Once well incorporated, empty the mixture into a bowl or cups
5. Place these in a pan with boiling water, but the water should reach only halfway up the height of the cups
6. Cover with a comal with heat
7. The jericalla is done when a straw inserted in it comes out clean
8. Remove the bowl or cups and let cool
9. Add cinnamon powder on top
