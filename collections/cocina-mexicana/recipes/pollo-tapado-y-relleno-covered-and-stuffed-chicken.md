## Ingredients

- chicken
- reboltillo (egg mixture)
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- capers
- ham pieces
- lard
- ground garlic
- picadillo
- cooked egg sliced
- large capers
- small chiles
- beaten eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken and fill it with a reboltillo until set and dry
2. Add raisins, almonds, olives, capers, and ham pieces
3. Position the legs well so they do not stick, and keep the wings natural without being twisted
4. Fry in lard with ground garlic and when golden, remove it
5. In a deep, rounded cazuela well coated with lard, arrange the chicken
6. Around it and without touching the sides, begin placing picadillo
7. With a spoon, pour beaten eggs against the cazuela so everything is covered
8. Add to the picadillo olives, raisins, almonds, sliced cooked egg, large capers, and small chiles
9. Continue covering until the chicken is even and covered
10. Finish covering with more beaten egg so it unites with the surrounding egg
11. Place on coals with gentle fire above so it sets and browns
12. When done, remove it and pour powdered sugar on top
