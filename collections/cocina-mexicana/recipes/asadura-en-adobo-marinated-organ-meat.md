## Ingredients

- asadura
- manteca
- sal
- chiles anchos
- ajos
- cominos
- orégano
- vinagre
- limones
- aceite
- agua

## Directions

1. Cut the raw organ meat into portions that are not too small
2. Burn almost half a pound of lard for each portion
3. Fry with salt, and when well fried add deveined, soaked and ground ancho chiles with garlic and cumin
4. Once fried, add oregano, vinegar, juice of two lemons, oil, and enough water for it to be well cooked
5. Apply heat from above and be careful to stir it and add hot water if cooking is insufficient
6. Let it boil until completely dry
