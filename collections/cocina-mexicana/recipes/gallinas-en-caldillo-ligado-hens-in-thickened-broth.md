## Ingredients

- Ham with mostly fat
- Hen giblets
- Lard
- Ground tomatillo
- Finely chopped onion
- Raw hen quarters
- Hen livers
- Salt
- Clove
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Water
- Oil

## Directions

1. Chop ham and hen giblets and fry both in lard
2. Add ground tomatillo and very finely chopped onion
3. Once fried, add sufficient water and the raw hen quarters cut into pieces, whole hen livers, salt, clove, pepper and nutmeg ground together
4. When the livers are cooked, which will be soon, remove them and grind with parsley
5. Dissolve the ground mixture with vinegar and water and add to the broth
6. Let it boil until sufficiently thickened and the hens are cooked
7. When serving, drizzle with oil on top
