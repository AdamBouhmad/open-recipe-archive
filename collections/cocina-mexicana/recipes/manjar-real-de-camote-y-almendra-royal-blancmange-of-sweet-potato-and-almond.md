## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one chicken breast, ground
- half a pound of ground sweet potato
- one pound of ground almond

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with three pounds of sugar, and add one ground chicken breast, letting the syrup boil a little with it
2. Then add half a pound of ground sweet potato and let it boil again
3. After add one pound of ground almond, boiling it again until it reaches the point of soft eggs, and pour it onto plates over biscuits or lady fingers
