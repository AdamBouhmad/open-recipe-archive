## Ingredients

- hazelnuts
- almonds
- butter
- ground cinnamon
- white fish
- papers, buttered
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Toast the hazelnuts and almonds and grind them
2. Fry afterwards in butter, sprinkling with ground cinnamon and use this to fill the cleaned, gutted white fish without scales or spines
3. Wrap in papers buttered with bread crumbs, pepper, and salt and place on a grill or fry in lard, taking care to turn them so they brown on both sides
4. Serve immediately
