## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound almonds
- 1 pound sugar
- cinnamon powder
- wine in the same quantities as the preceding article

## Directions

1. Make it in the same way as the almond dessert of the preceding article, with the only difference being 15 egg yolks and 1/2 pound of almonds for each pound of sugar
2. Use cinnamon and wine in the same quantities as the preceding article
