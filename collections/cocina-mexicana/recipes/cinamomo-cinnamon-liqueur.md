## Ingredients

- twenty quartillos of refined aguardiente
- four ounces of broken cinnamon
- husked peels of one citron and one orange
- ten quartillos of river water
- four pounds of sugar
- cochineal for coloring
- cinnamon tincture for coloring (alternative method)

## Directions

1. Place four ounces of broken cinnamon and the husked peels of one citron and one orange in infusion in twenty quartillos of refined aguardiente for eight to ten days at a gentle heat.
2. After this time, distill in a bain-marie to extract ten quartillos of liqueur.
3. Mix these ten quartillos with ten more quartillos of river water in which four pounds of sugar have been dissolved.
4. Color with cochineal and filter to store in well-sealed bottles.
5. Alternatively, color this liqueur with a well-clarified cinnamon tincture made from a small portion separated from the original cinnamon amount for infusion.
