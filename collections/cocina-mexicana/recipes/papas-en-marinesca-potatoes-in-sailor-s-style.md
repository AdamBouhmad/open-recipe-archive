## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked and sliced
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- flour
- broth
- good wine

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked potatoes as described in other articles.
2. Place them in a pot with butter, salt, pepper, parsley and finely chopped onion.
3. Sprinkle everything with a little flour.
4. Moisten with broth and a sufficient quantity of good wine.
5. Let it boil until the sauce thickens.
