## Ingredients

- Ham prepared as in Jamón en Vino a la Francesa
- Two bottles of white wine
- Two pounds of salt
- Saltpeter
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Clove

## Directions

1. Place the prepared ham in a casserole with two bottles of white wine, two pounds of salt, a little saltpeter, thyme, laurel and clove.
2. Let it marinate for eight hours, turning it from time to time.
3. Remove the crust, wrap it in oiled paper and cook it on the spit.
4. Serve with a good sauce.
