## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one glass of boiling milk
- butter
- onions
- carrots
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter and dissolve a tablespoon of flour in it
2. Add little by little one glass of boiling milk to this mixture, stirring constantly until it boils
3. For a superior sauce, fry butter, sliced onions, carrots and parsley in a saucepan; brown well and then add boiling milk, stirring constantly
4. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg until it boils
5. Cook over low heat for half an hour
6. Strain
