## Ingredients

- Fish, cleaned and cut into portions
- Flour
- Egg
- Vinegar
- Water
- Sugar
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Ginger, toasted
- Salt, fine
- Tomatillos, cut in quarters
- Lime slices, cooked
- Green chiles, deveined

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and cut into portions, coat in flour and fry with egg.
2. Take equal parts vinegar and water and season with sugar.
3. Grind saffron, pepper, toasted ginger, and a little fine salt, and dissolve everything in the diluted vinegar, ensuring the broth is not too thick.
4. Add the fish and place on the fire to bring to a boil, adding tomatillos cut in quarters.
5. Remove from heat and add plenty of vinegar, with cooked lime slices and some deveined green chiles.
6. Serve cold or hot, and it is for the same day.
