## Ingredients

- four ounces of flour
- four ounces of fresh butter
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- three ounces of sweet almonds, crushed
- lemon zest or one to two tablespoons of orange flower water
- four to six well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Crush the sweet almonds
2. Mix the flour, butter, and powdered sugar
3. Add the crushed almonds
4. Add lemon zest or orange flower water
5. Add four to six well-beaten eggs
6. Mix everything in a mortar to form a paste
7. Butter the bottom of a cake pan
8. Cook over gentle heat with heat underneath and on top
9. Form the cake
10. Serve cold or hot, but always dusted with powdered sugar on top
