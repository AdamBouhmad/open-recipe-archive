## Ingredients

- one chocolate tablet
- water
- molinillo (chocolate beater)

## Directions

1. Place the chocolate tablet with water over the fire in a quantity slightly greater than what is needed to fill the cup in which it will be served
2. When it comes to its first boil, remove it, dissolve the tablet completely, and beat it with the molinillo so it incorporates with the water, then return it to the heat
3. When it gives two more boils and you wish to remove it, take it away a second time and beat it
4. Fill half the cup, return it to the heat, and then fill the cup completely, so that the surface is covered with foam
