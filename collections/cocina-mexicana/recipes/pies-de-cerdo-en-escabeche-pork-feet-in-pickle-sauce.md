## Ingredients

- Pork feet
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Garlic
- Lard
- Water
- Good vinegar
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Saffron
- Toasted bread
- Sweet lime slices, cooked separately
- Orange leaves
- Panela (unrefined cane sugar)
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. Wash the feet and cut them in half.
2. Cook them well and remove the bones, taking care not to remove any meat.
3. Roll them in flour, dip in beaten egg, and fry.
4. For the sauce: brown some garlic in lard, then add water and a little good vinegar.
5. Grind a little clove, pepper, and saffron.
6. Thicken with a little toasted bread ground in lard.
7. Add the spices, cooked sweet lime slices, orange leaves, and a small piece of panela, so that the vinegar stands out.
8. Once seasoned, place the fried feet in this sauce.
9. Serve sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds on top.
