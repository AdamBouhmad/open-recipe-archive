## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- butter
- ground cinnamon
- powdered sugar
- sponge cake (mamón) or marquesote
- syrup
- gragea
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. While cooking a syrup at medium point made with one pound of sugar, wash the butter very well in cold water, remove it from the water, add ground cinnamon and knead it with powdered sugar, wetting your hands in cold water if they heat up
2. When the dough is ready, put a layer of it and another of marquesote or sponge cake moistened in the syrup, repeating the operation until the plate is full, making sure the last layer is butter
3. Settle it with a silver spoon and decorate with gragea, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and cinnamon
