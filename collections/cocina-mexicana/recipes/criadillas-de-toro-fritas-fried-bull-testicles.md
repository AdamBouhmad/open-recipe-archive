## Ingredients

- bull testicles with their natural coverings
- salt
- water
- flour
- beaten egg
- lemon juice
- salt-pepper
- onion slices

## Directions

1. Cook with their natural coverings in salted water
2. Remove the first layers
3. Slice into rounds of half an inch thick, and if to be used for garnish of other dishes, divide the rounds into pieces of two inches in length more or less
4. Roll in flour and fry, or bread with beaten egg
5. Serve with lemon juice, salt-pepper and aperdigada onion slices
