## Ingredients

- Pheasant, plucked and cleaned
- Medium strips of ham
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- String

## Directions

1. Pluck the pheasant, clean it and singe it at the flame.
2. Tie the legs together.
3. Lard it throughout with medium strips of ham seasoned with salt, coarse pepper, clove, cinnamon and nutmeg, covering everything with a slice of ham and tying it with string.
