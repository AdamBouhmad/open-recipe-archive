## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- salt
- tomato sauce or gherkin sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs like veal ribs, leaving some fat, flatten them giving them a good shape, sprinkle with salt, and place on the grill. Serve with a tomato sauce or gherkin sauce.
