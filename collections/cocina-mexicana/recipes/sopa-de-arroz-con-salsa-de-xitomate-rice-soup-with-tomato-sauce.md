## Ingredients

- Rice
- Lard
- Tomato
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Hot water
- One or two eggs

## Directions

1. Wash rice in two or three waters, drain, and dry it
2. Make a sauce in a little lard with ground tomato, clove, and cinnamon, cooking it with hot water
3. When the rice is dry, mix it with one or two eggs, place it in a buttered serving dish, and add the prepared sauce
4. Bring to a boil until cooked, with heat on top, and let it thicken well
5. This soup may be served on days of meat abstinence
