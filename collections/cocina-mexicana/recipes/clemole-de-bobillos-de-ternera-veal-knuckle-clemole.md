## Ingredients

- veal hooves, cleaned
- water
- garlic
- salt
- cumin
- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- golden bread
- toasted sesame seeds
- lard
- cooked calabacitas, sliced
- olives
- small chiles

## Directions

1. Clean the veal hooves and cut into pieces
2. Cook in water with garlic, salt and cumin until well cooked
3. Remove and debone the hooves
4. Fry in lard the ancho and pasilla chiles, golden bread, and toasted sesame seeds, all ground together
5. When well fried, add the deboned hooves
6. Add the broth from cooking, cooked sliced calabacitas, olives and small chiles
7. Simmer until it reaches a proportionate broth-like consistency
