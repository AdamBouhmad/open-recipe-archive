## Ingredients

- pears
- clarified syrup
- high-point paste filling
- cinnamon
- butter
- warm water
- lemon drops

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the pears and place them in already-made, clarified, and cold syrup
2. Allow them to boil several times over high heat, then keep them at low heat for the rest of the day so they heat through well
3. The next day put them over low heat until they are soft and at the proper point, then remove and let them cool immediately
4. Cut out a small piece from the bottom and hollow them out there
5. Fill them with any high-point paste that has been prepared
6. Cover with the same small piece that was removed and return them to the syrup, which should be small, sprinkling cinnamon on top
7. Place them between two fires with low heat so they acquire color, then remove them
8. Arrange them on a platter with their syrup, garnished with butter washed in warm water and lemon drops, strained through a napkin
