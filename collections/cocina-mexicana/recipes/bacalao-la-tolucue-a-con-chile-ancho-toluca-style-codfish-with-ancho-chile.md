## Ingredients

- chile ancho (ancho chile)
- bacalao (codfish)
- ajos (garlic)
- pimienta (pepper)
- clavo (cloves)
- aceite (oil)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo (broth)
- pan (bread)
- perejil (parsley)
- aceitunas (olives)
- tornachiles (a type of chile)

## Directions

1. Soak ancho chile the night before in good quantity in proportion to the codfish and remove the veins
2. The next day, grind with garlic, pepper, and cloves
3. Fry this in oil and the rest in lard
4. When both are ready, incorporate them with the mentioned broth and add the raw codfish
5. Let it cook and thicken with softened bread that has been ground
6. Serve by pouring oil on top, chopped parsley, olives, and tornachiles cut into pieces
