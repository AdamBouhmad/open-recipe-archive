## Ingredients

- veal pulp chops
- jamón (ham)
- salt
- ground pepper
- garlic cloves
- cloves
- cinnamon sticks
- broad chiles
- oregano
- cumin
- cilantro
- wine
- vinegar
- lime
- veal bones
- chorizo
- turnips
- onions
- lard
- bread

## Directions

1. Ensure the pulps are well bled, washed and dried
2. Lard them with strips of ham dusted in salt and pepper powder, garlic cloves, cloves, cinnamon
3. Marinate in a pot with broad chiles that have been toasted, deveined and ground with garlic, clove, pepper, cinnamon, cumin and toasted cilantro
4. Dissolve all ground ingredients in wine and vinegar, season with salt
5. Add oregano and lime slices to the marinade
6. Let the meat marinate overnight
7. The next day, divide into regular pieces and cook in a pot with the little broth that remained
8. Fry in its own marinade, being careful not to burn
9. After frying, add to a broth made with veal bones, ham, small chorizos, one or two bay leaves, turnips, whole onions, salt and pepper
10. When the chops are perfectly cooked, burn lard in a pot, empty the pot into it and add pieces of the ham from the broth, the small chorizos, the onions separated, the cooked turnips, and a little fried and ground bread
11. Place to dry over two fires and serve hot
