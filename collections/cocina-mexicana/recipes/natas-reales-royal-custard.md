## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- sixteen egg yolks
- two handfuls of ground rice
- sugar as needed
- cooked custard from one basin of milk
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring four quartillos of milk to a boil with sixteen egg yolks, two handfuls of ground rice, and the necessary sugar
2. Just as it is about to reach the proper consistency, add the cooked custard from one basin of milk
3. Serve with ground cinnamon sprinkled on top
