## Ingredients

- tortillas chicas y muy delgadas
- chicharrón gordo
- pimientas gordas
- clavos de comer
- sal
- hebras de jamón magro
- huevos revueltos
- picadillo
- huevo batido
- manteca
- mole de chile colorado
- pipián

## Directions

1. Form some small and very thin tortillas
2. Take well-toasted fat pork skin, grind well with two or three large peppers and two or three cloves, seasoning with the necessary fine salt
3. Fry in a pot thin slices of lean ham and mix all the ground things with beaten eggs or with a picadillo like that of stuffed chiles
4. Add this filling to the tortillas, which with the filling are folded and dipped in beaten egg
5. Fry, and after draining, cook in red chile mole, prepared in any of the ways explained in the word MOLE, or better yet in the pipián way
