## Ingredients

- meat
- onion, cut coarsely
- tomato, ground
- water
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- four or five almonds with shell
- potatoes
- Sherry wine
- Catalan brandy

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and fry in a pot until the water it releases is consumed.
2. Add plenty of coarsely cut onion, let it brown, and add ground tomato to brown as well.
3. Once done, add the necessary water and a little vinegar, bay leaf, thyme, and oregano.
4. Cover the pot with a napkin and an inverted plate and place over very gentle heat.
5. Grind clove, cinnamon, and pepper with four or five almonds with shell and add to the meat.
6. When the meat is almost cooked, fry raw potatoes, add a little Sherry wine to the stew and a spoonful of Catalan brandy, and soon add the potatoes, then leave it almost without fire so it finishes cooking with the heat of the embers.
