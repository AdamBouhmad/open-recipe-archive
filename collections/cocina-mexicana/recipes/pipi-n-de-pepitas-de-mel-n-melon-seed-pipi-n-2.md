## Ingredients

- melon seeds
- almonds
- cooked chicken breasts
- oil mixed with lard
- salt
- ground clove
- birds or meats to cook

## Directions

1. Toast the melon seeds on a griddle
2. Grind them and strain through a sieve
3. Add almonds and cooked chicken breasts, grinding both together
4. Fry everything in oil mixed with lard
5. Season with salt and ground clove
6. Add the birds or meats to be cooked in this pipián with a little of the broth in which they were cooked
7. Let boil until the sauce becomes thick
