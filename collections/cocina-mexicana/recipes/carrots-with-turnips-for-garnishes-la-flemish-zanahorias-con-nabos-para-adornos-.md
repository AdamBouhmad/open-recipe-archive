## Ingredients

- Thirty thick carrots
- Thirty turnips
- Concentrated broth
- Sugar
- Thirty lettuces
- Thirty head onions
- Nivernese sauce
- Spanish sauce, thick

## Directions

1. Turn thirty thick carrots and as many turnips into the shape of batons, blanch them, and cook them in concentrated broth with a little sugar
2. Have thirty lettuces cooked on the coals with cabbage, which are drained, pressed and arranged
3. Dress everything on the plate in the form of a crown, interpolating one carrot and one turnip between each lettuce, and leaving the center of the plate free to place the meat that is prepared
4. Roll everything with thirty fried and frosted head onions
5. Once this dish of relay or entrance is arranged, bathe everything with well-reduced Nivernese sauce to gelatin and augmented with a little of the thick Spanish sauce
