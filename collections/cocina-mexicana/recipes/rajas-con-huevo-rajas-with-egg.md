## Ingredients

- chilchotes (dried chiles)
- cebolla (onion)
- huevos (eggs)
- agua (water)

## Directions

1. Toast the chilchotes, peel them, and cut into rajas (strips)
2. Chop plenty of onion and fry it
3. Once fried, add the rajas
4. Fry the eggs separately, then mix everything together
5. Add a little water
