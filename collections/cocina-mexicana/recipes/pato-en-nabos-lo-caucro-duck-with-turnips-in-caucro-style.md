## Ingredients

- Duck
- Lard
- Flour
- Turnips
- Broth from the pot
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After the duck is prepared as above, put some lard in the casserole
2. Turn the duck and some turnips in the lard, then remove them
3. In the lard, add a tablespoon of flour and make this mixture redden
4. Add this mixture with broth from the pot, return the duck and turnips with a sprig of parsley, salt and pepper
5. If the turnips are already tender, do not add them until the duck is almost cooked
6. Once the stew is done, let it reduce if necessary and serve everything together
