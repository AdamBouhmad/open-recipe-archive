## Ingredients

- rabbit
- onion tails
- garlic, crushed
- oregano
- thyme
- laurel
- salt
- vinegar of good quality
- water
- oil
- lard
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- roasted garlic
- cumin

## Directions

1. Disembowel the rabbit and place it whole with skin in a pot
2. Add onion tails, crushed garlic, oregano, thyme, laurel, salt, vinegar of good quality, and water
3. Once cooked, skin and portion into rations
4. Heat sufficient oil and some lard in a pan with crushed garlic, green oregano, thyme, and laurel, and fry the portions until they brown a little
5. When fried, add everything again so it browns; then add clove, cinnamon, pepper, roasted garlic and cumin, all ground without water but with vinegar
6. Let it boil once and remove from heat
7. In a used pot, layer meat and seasoning alternately
8. Once everything is arranged, add half a quartillo of good oil and half of Castilian vinegar and salt
9. Cover the pot with another, sealing the edges with yeast, and place over heat but not directly over flame so it simply receives the heat
10. After two days, uncover to check if it has sufficient salt and if the oil and vinegar cover it; if lacking, add what is necessary, cover again and leave for eight days, after which it can be eaten
