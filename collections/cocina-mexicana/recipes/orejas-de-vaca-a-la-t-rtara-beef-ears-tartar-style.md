## Ingredients

- four beef ears
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- stock
- verjuice
- minced shallots
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch four ears in boiling water
2. Open them at the thick part without breaking, just to keep them open
3. Skewer each one with a skewer
4. Dip in butter and cover with breadcrumbs
5. Grill, lightly sprinkling with remaining butter in which they were soaked
6. When they have good color, serve with a well-bound sauce made with a little stock, verjuice, minced shallots, salt and coarse pepper
