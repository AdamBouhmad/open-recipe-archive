## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- garlic
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take artichokes and cut the tip and head
2. Place to boil and when half cooked add salt
3. Add a head of garlic mashed and a little pepper powder
4. Place head up to add oil and a few drops of vinegar
5. Leave them in infusion for as long as desired
6. Note that this is an exquisite dish for some people and should not be missing from a formal table
