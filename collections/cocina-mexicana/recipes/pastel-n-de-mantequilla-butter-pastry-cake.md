## Ingredients

- one pound of good flour, well sifted
- water
- salt
- lard as needed for softening
- melted and cold butter
- minced filling (picadillo)
- pieces of cooked tender ham and chicken
- olives
- chili peppers in quarters
- capers
- large capers
- raisins
- almonds
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place one pound of good sifted flour on a table and mix with water and salt
2. Soften the mixture with all the lard necessary to work it
3. Let it rest covered for a while
4. Gradually extend it into very thin sheets and coat with melted and cold butter
5. Roll as thick as desired and flatten with a rolling pin
6. Cut the dough to the size the pastry cake should be
7. Butter a serving dish and place as base and filling a minced mixture with pieces of cooked tender ham and chicken in pieces, olives, chili peppers in quarters, capers, large capers, raisins and almonds to a proportionate height
8. Cover with another layer of dough equal to the bottom, either in one piece or in three or four
9. Stack pieces overlapping at the sides so that when cooked it forms like a cloud
10. If desired woven, cut the dough into strips two fingers wide, make a crosshatch pattern, and with another large strip cover at the edge of the dish all the points of the weave
11. Place in a well-tempered oven so it is not undercooked and flattened, nor burned
12. When done, sprinkle sifted sugar so it adheres
