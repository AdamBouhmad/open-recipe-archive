## Ingredients

- Corn
- Water
- Lime (calcium oxide)
- Corn for grinding

## Directions

1. Soak corn in proportion to the amount of atole desired, adding water in which a little lime has been slaked
2. Let it settle until the corn turns yellow
3. Place over fire with its own water
4. Remove from fire to remove the skin
5. After cooling completely, wash in plenty of water
6. Rub by hand so it becomes very clean and grind very fine
7. Dissolve the mass in water and pass through a sieve
8. Put in a pot over fire and let it thicken as needed, stirring frequently so it does not burn
9. Stir with a wooden spoon that has no grease, but is not new, as a metal spoon cuts the atole and a new wooden spoon with grease gives an unpleasant flavor
