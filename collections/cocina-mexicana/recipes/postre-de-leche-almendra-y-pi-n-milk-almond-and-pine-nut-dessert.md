## Ingredients

- milk
- almonds
- pine nuts, ground
- egg yolks
- butter
- marquesote, sliced
- pastille or ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook a little milk well and let it cool
2. Mix with ground almonds, pine nuts and egg yolks until it becomes yellow
3. Butter a cazuela
4. Lay down a layer of sliced marquesote, removing the black part
5. Pour the mixture on top
6. Dust with pastille or ground cinnamon
7. Leave to set over two gentle fires
