## Ingredients

- Much butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley sprigs
- Lean and fatty bacon, cut into pieces
- Raw potatoes, peeled and cut

## Directions

1. Fry much butter, dissolve flour in it
2. When this mixture takes on a deep color, season with salt, pepper and parsley sprigs
3. Add lean and fatty bacon cut into pieces and let it half cook in the mixture
4. Then add the raw potatoes after peeling and cutting them
5. When cooked, skim and serve promptly
