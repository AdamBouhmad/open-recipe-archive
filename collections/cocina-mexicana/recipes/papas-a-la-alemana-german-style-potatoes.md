## Ingredients

- twelve large potatoes
- water
- four ounces of butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley, chopped
- three eggs
- butter, melted (for cooking)

## Directions

1. Clean twelve large potatoes and cut them into very thin slices.
2. Put them to cook in water over strong heat.
3. When they appear ready to fall apart in substance, drain them.
4. Return them to the same pot over the fire, stirring strongly with a wooden spoon.
5. Add four ounces of butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg and chopped parsley.
6. Break three eggs into them without ceasing to stir this mixture until it becomes solid.
7. Let it cool then.
8. Have melted butter ready in a large sauté pan and put the potatoes into it as if they were thick quenelles.
9. Make them take color over strong heat and turn them so they brown equally on all sides.
