## Ingredients

- spinach with roots
- oil
- garlic cloves
- vinegar
- water
- toasted pine nuts
- raisins

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach very well and wash it, but leaving the roots on it
2. Fry in oil with some clean garlic cloves that have been chopped
3. Once fried, add a little vinegar diluted with boiling water
4. Add toasted pine nuts and pitted raisins
5. Let it season and serve hot
