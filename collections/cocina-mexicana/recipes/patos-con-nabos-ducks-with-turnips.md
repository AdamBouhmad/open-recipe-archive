## Ingredients

- Ducks, prepared
- Butter or lard
- Meat or poultry broth
- Salt
- Black pepper
- White wine
- Bundle of aromatic herbs
- Turnips, browned in butter

## Directions

1. Fry the prepared ducks in butter or lard.
2. Add meat or poultry broth, salt, pepper, white wine, and a bundle of aromatic herbs.
3. When the ducks are nearly cooked, add turnips that have been browned in butter.
4. When completely cooked, remove the ducks from the broth and let the broth cool.
5. Skim the fat from the broth, thicken it, bathe the ducks with it, and serve.
