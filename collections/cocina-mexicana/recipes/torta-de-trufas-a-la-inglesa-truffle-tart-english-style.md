## Ingredients

- two pounds of good truffles
- six slices of ham
- a little carrot peel
- one large onion sliced
- one bay leaf
- a little powdered aromatic spices
- sufficient salt

## Directions

1. Wash and brush two pounds of good truffles
2. Clean them and put them in a pot with six ham slices, a little carrot peel, one large onion sliced, one bay leaf and a little powdered aromatic spices
