## Ingredients

- bread or sponge cake slices
- fresh cheese
- sugar
- gragea
- ground cinnamon
- beaten egg
- butter
- syrup
- raisins

## Directions

1. Between two slices of bread or sponge cake, place some fresh cheese mixed with sugar or gragea and ground cinnamon
2. Coat with beaten egg
3. Fry in butter
4. Place in syrup and decorate with raisins and other garnishes if desired
