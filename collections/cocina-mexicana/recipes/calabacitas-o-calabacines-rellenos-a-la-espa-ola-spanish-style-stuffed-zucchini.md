## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Picadillo or cheese
- Turnip
- Ham
- Broth
- Tomato sauce or tomato salsa

## Directions

1. Hollow out both ends with the end of a larding needle or kitchen spoon
2. Fill with picadillo or cheese, and cover the openings with pieces of turnip cut like cork stoppers
3. Place in a casserole lined with ham slices, pour broth over them, and let them cook over low heat
4. Serve with tomato sauce or tomato salsa
