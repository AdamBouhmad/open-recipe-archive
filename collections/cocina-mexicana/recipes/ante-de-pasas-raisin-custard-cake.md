## Ingredients

- red wine
- sugar
- pitted raisins
- sponge cake (mamón)
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Sweeten red wine a little with sugar, add pitted raisins
2. When well soaked, place on a plate over sponge cake with ground cinnamon sprinkled on top
