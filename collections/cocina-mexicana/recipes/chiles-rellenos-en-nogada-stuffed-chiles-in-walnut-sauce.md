## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Pork picadillo
- Nogada sauce
- Flour
- Beaten egg

## Directions

1. Fill the chiles with pork picadillo as previously described
2. Fry the chiles
3. Arrange them on a plate
4. Cover with nogada sauce as explained for zucchini or any other nogada
