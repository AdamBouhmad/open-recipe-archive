## Ingredients

- ribs
- oil
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaves
- onion
- pickled gherkins
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare and flatten the ribs
2. Place them in an adobo with oil, salt, a bunch of parsley, bay leaves, and an onion cut into rings
3. After twenty-four hours, place on a grill over high heat
4. Turn them and remove when done
5. Serve with their own sauce or with a spicy sauce made with pickled gherkins in vinegar, chopped
