## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 13 egg yolks, beaten
- eggs, beaten
- syrup

## Directions

1. For one pound of sugar, prepare thirteen beaten egg yolks
2. Cook until separating from the pan, and on top beaten yolks to form a crust
3. Place over two fires, then cut into slices
4. Fry with beaten eggs and boil in syrup
