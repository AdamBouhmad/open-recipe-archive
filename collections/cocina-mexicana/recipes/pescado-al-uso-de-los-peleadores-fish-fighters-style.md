## Ingredients

- raw fish slices
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- saffron threads
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Place raw fish slices in a pot.
2. Add chopped garlic, onion, tomato, cumin, Castilian pepper, and saffron threads ground with a little water and vinegar.
3. Cover the pot well and cook on two fires.
4. Add oil and sufficient salt.
5. Let cook.
