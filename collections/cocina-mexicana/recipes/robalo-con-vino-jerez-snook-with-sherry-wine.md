## Ingredients

- ajos
- pescado
- vinagre
- vino
- pimienta
- clavo
- laurel
- orégano
- cominos
- chiles
- aceitunas

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves according to the amount of fish.
2. Remove and add twice as much vinegar and half as much wine.
3. Grind pepper, clove, bay leaf, oregano and cumin.
4. Add all of this with the fish so it cooks.
5. Garnish with chiles and olives.
