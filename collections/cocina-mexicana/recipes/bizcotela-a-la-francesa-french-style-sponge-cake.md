## Ingredients

- syrup at the feather stage
- flour, approximately equal in quantity to the syrup
- one egg white
- orange blossom water
- a little amber

## Directions

1. Mix syrup at feather stage with approximately equal quantity of flour to form a paste
2. Spread and knead on a table dusted with sugar
3. When firm, pound in a mortar or grind in a metate with one egg white, orange blossom water, and a little amber until well incorporated
4. Form the mixture into small balls
5. Drop into boiling water in a pot or pan, removing them only when they float to the surface
