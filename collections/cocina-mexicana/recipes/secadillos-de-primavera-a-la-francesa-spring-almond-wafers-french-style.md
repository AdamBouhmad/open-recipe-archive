## Ingredients

- sweet almonds
- spinach green (see official spinach green)
- sugar
- syrup at the point of breaking

## Directions

1. Cut sweet almonds as in the previous article and, rubbing them with spinach green and sugar, set them to dry
2. Prepare the secadillos as described in the following article
3. Dip them one by one in syrup at the point of breaking, then immediately in the almonds so they adhere
4. Arrange and serve
