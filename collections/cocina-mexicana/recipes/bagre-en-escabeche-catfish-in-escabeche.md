## Ingredients

- Catfish portions, fried and cooked
- Lemon or lime slices
- Orange leaves
- Lard or oil
- Garlic, chopped and fried
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Cooked lime slices
- Good vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the catfish portions and cook lime or lemon slices with one or two orange leaves
2. In a pot, place a layer of fried catfish slices and add a little of the water in which the lime slices were cooked, chopped and fried garlic in lard or oil, a little clove, cinnamon and pepper ground, some bay leaves, some slices of cooked lime, and some good vinegar
3. Multiply these layers until the pot is full and cover well
4. Serve after six or eight days, and continue using it afterwards, otherwise it will spoil
