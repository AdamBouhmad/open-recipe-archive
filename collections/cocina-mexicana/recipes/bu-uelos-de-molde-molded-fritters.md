## Ingredients

- Three egg whites
- Thirty-two egg yolks
- One pound of flour
- One pocilio of raw milk or Jerez wine
- A little salt
- Lard

## Directions

1. Beat three egg whites, then add thirty-two yolks and beat again
2. Mix in one pound of flour, stirring well so it does not form lumps
3. Add one pocilio of milk or Jerez wine and a little salt
4. Heat the mold in the lard that is prepared for frying
5. When hot, quickly dry the mold with a cloth and dip it in the beaten egg mixture until coated exactly
6. Immediately place in well-heated lard so the egg cooks, shaking the mold so the fritter does not stick
