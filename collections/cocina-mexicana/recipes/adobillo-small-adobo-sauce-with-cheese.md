## Ingredients

- squashes
- salt
- vinegar
- potatoes
- beans
- ancho chile
- bread
- garlic
- cumin
- oil
- aged cheese
- thyme

## Directions

1. Cook squashes with salt and a little vinegar
2. Cook potatoes and beans separately
3. Grind soaked ancho chile with a piece of bread, garlic cloves and cumin with corresponding salt
4. Add squashes, potatoes and beans to this adobillo
5. Add oil, grated aged cheese and thyme
