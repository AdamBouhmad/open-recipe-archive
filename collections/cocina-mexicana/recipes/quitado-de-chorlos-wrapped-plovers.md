## Ingredients

- onion (much, chopped)
- parsley (chopped)
- garlic (a little, chopped)
- breadcrumbs (substantial amount)
- salt
- pepper (ground)
- thyme
- paper

## Directions

1. Chop much onion, parsley, a little garlic and a substantial amount of breadcrumbs with salt and ground pepper.
2. Mix everything together.
3. Wrap the plovers in this mixture, adding thyme inside.
4. Wrap the plovers in papers and place on a grill.
5. At low heat, turn them so they roast evenly.
