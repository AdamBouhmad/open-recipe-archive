## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- tequesquite
- cream from cooked milk
- ten to twelve hard biscuits
- sugar
- ten eggs
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the pumpkin with tequesquite and grind half of it with a plate of cream from cooked milk
2. Grind separately ten or twelve hard biscuits and some sugar, and mix everything with five beaten eggs
3. Line a casserole with butter, add considerable bread crumbs and beat five other eggs, pouring half of them into the casserole
4. Place the pumpkin mixed with the other eggs and biscuits on top, pouring over all of this the remaining beaten egg
5. Put the casserole in the oven at a moderate temperature
6. After the cake is cooked, serve dusted with sugar
