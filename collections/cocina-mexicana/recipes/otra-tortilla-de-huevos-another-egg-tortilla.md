## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Break the eggs and pour them into a pitcher
2. Season with the necessary fine salt and beat only until well mixed
3. Pour into hot butter to fry, lowering the omelet pan or skillet from the heat so it sets
4. Then flip it in the air and return it to the heat until it is cooked
5. Serve with a powder of ancho chile, made from chiles dried by heat without toasting them, being careful to remove the veins beforehand and to remove all the husks
