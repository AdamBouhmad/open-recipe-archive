## Ingredients

- one pound of shelled sweet almonds
- four or five egg whites
- one and a half pounds of powdered sugar
- lemon zest
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Dry one pound of shelled sweet almonds in a stove
2. Grind them, moistening at the same time with four or five egg whites as you grind to prevent them from becoming oily
3. Once well ground, add one and a half pounds of powdered sugar and lemon zest
4. Mix everything well to form a manageable paste
5. Put the paste in parts into a pastry bag with a star tip
6. Arrange these fragments on sheets of paper dusted with sugar, long enough to form rings
7. Place the rings on tin sheets covered with white paper
8. Let them dry
9. Put them in a very hot oven to bake
