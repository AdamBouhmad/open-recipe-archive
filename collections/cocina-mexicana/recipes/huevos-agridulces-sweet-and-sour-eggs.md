## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- sesame seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook eggs until hardened
2. Cut in half and remove yolks
3. Knead yolks with a little vinegar, sugar, ground cinnamon, pepper, clove, sesame seeds, raisins and almonds
4. Fill the hollows of the eggs left by the yolks with this mixture
5. Fry afterwards and place in a broth of vinegar and water with a little salt, pepper, clove, toasted sesame seeds, raisins, almonds and sugar
