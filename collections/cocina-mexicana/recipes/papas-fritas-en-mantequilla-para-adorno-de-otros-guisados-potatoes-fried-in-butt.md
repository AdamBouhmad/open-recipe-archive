## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fine butter
- veal gelatine

## Directions

1. Clean and wash potatoes and cut into slices lengthwise, although it is better to leave them whole
2. Put in a pan and pour melted fine butter over them
3. Use one pound of butter for every three dozen medium-sized potatoes
4. Place over very lively fire with fire also from above on the pan cover
5. After a few minutes, use only gentle fire both below and above
6. Leave the potatoes until they have taken on a beautiful golden color, taking care to stir them from time to time
7. When well toasted, drain and toss in fresh butter, adding a little veal gelatine
