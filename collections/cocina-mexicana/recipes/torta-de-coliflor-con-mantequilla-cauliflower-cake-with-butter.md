## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Grated cheese
- Eggs
- Butter
- Sugar
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with salt
2. Chop it and mix with grated cheese and beaten eggs
3. Pour into a casserole dish lined with butter
4. Cover with more egg, butter the top again
5. Let the cake set over two fires
6. Serve with sugar and cinnamon powder sprinkled on top
