## Ingredients

- one small pot of rice
- three cuartillos of milk
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- eight egg whites
- twenty-four egg yolks
- two crumbled marquesote biscuits
- butter for greasing
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- grajea
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash and grind one small pot of rice
2. Mix with three cuartillos of milk, dissolving well
3. Cook with one and a half pounds of sugar until it thickens
4. Remove from heat and allow to cool
5. While cooling, beat eight egg whites well
6. Mix with twenty-four egg yolks and beat again
7. Crumble two marquesote biscuits and mix everything, beating by hand
8. Pour into a buttered pan or pot
9. Cook over two fires
10. When done (test with a straw that comes out clean), remove from heat
11. Cut into pieces and immerse in half-point syrup
12. Allow to boil a few times
13. Place on a platter and decorate with raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
14. Dust with grajea and ground cinnamon
