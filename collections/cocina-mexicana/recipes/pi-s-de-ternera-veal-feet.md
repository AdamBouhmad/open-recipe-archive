## Ingredients

- veal feet
- salt
- lard
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- cilantro
- cumin
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the veal feet very well and cook with salt until well done.
2. Remove the bones and cut into regular pieces.
3. Place a casserole on the heat with lard.
4. Crush plenty of tomatoes on the metate, put in water and wash well.
5. Grind green chiles, some cilantro sprigs, and a little cumin.
6. Fry all this in the lard and moisten with water.
7. Add salt, let it season and the sauce thicken, and serve.
