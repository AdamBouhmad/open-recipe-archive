## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- broth
- white wine
- bundle of parsley
- salt
- pepper, ground
- butter and flour paste
- aged cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Open the chickens along the back from the neck to the tail.
2. Flatten them well with a mallet and fry in butter.
3. Once well fried, add two parts broth and one part white wine, a bundle of parsley, salt, and ground pepper.
4. Let cook over gentle heat, and once cooked, strain the sauce, add a little butter kneaded with flour, and let it thicken well.
5. Place the chickens on a fire-resistant platter, bathe them with the sauce, and sprinkle grated aged cheese on top.
6. Place over two gentle fires.
