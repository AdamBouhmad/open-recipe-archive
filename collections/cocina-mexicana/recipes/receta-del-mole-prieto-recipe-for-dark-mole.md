## Ingredients

- Cooked turkey or hen meat
- Chile proportioned to the amount of meat
- Seeds of the same chile
- 1 garlic head
- A piece of bread
- Jitomate
- Raw tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- Ground cinnamon
- Avocado leaf
- Santa leaf
- A piece of sugar
- Salt to taste
- A little bizcocho de velorio or catarino
- Sesame

## Directions

1. After the turkey or hen meat is cooked, burn an amount of chile proportioned to the quantity of meat
2. Burn some seeds of the same chile, a garlic head and a piece of bread
3. After everything is burned, grind it well adding jitomate, raw tomatoes, clove, pepper and ground cinnamon
4. Once fried, add the meat
5. Add to this broth an avocado leaf and another called Santa, a piece of sugar and salt to taste
6. Once seasoned, remove from fire and crumble a little bizcocho de velorio or catarino, and add it with sesame on top
