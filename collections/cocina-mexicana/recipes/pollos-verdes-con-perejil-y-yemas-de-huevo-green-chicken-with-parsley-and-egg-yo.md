## Ingredients

- Chicken with its broth
- Lard
- Chopped onions
- Ground parsley
- Fried bread toasts
- Cooked egg yolks
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Olives

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onions in lard
2. Then fry ground parsley mixed with fried bread toasts, cooked egg yolks, and garlic
3. Season with salt, pepper, and clove
4. Add oil, vinegar, and a little oregano
5. Place the chicken with its broth in the pan
6. Allow to simmer until fully seasoned
7. When removing from heat, add olives
