## Ingredients

- One quart chickpeas
- One pound ham or pork fat
- One and a half pounds beef pulp
- Two or three carrots
- Two or three onions
- Two cloves of spice
- Broth
- Skimmed sauce
- Spinach green

## Directions

1. Wash one quart of chickpeas and put them in a medium pot with one pound of ham or pork fat, one and a half pounds of beef pulp, two or three carrots and the same number of onions, one of which should be studded with two cloves of spice
2. Fill the pot almost entirely with broth and let the chickpeas cook
3. When done, put them in a strainer, let them drain and dry, and remove the ham, beef and vegetables
4. Pass the chickpeas through the strainer, moistening them little by little with broth from the same pot in which they were cooked, making the substance thick, as it is easier to liquefy than to thicken afterwards
5. Put it in a pot with four skimming spoonfuls of skimmed sauce (see page 766)
6. After boiling and skimming, degrease it
7. When sufficiently thick, empty it into another pot, and at the moment of serving, mix in spinach green (see page 322)
