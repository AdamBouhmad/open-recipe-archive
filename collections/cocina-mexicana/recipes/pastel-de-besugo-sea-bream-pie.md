## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Salt
- Raisins (pitted)
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Almonds (blanched and crushed)

## Directions

1. Clean, gut, and scale the sea bream
2. Cook it in water with salt
3. Remove the bones and skin and mince the meat finely
4. Add pitted raisins to the minced meat, and if desired, a little garlic and pepper
5. Incorporate everything with six beaten eggs and a few blanched and crushed almonds
6. Fill the pastry with this mixture, form it, and cook in the usual manner or in a mold
