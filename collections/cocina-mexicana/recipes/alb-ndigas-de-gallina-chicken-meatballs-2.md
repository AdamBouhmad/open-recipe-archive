## Ingredients

- chicken breasts, raw and chopped
- salt
- onion, finely chopped
- tomatoes, finely chopped
- lard
- roasted and ground almonds
- toasted and ground bread
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Chop raw chicken breasts and season with salt
2. Mix with finely chopped onion and tomatoes
3. Form the meatballs and fry them in lard
4. For the broth, fry roasted and ground almonds and toasted and ground bread
5. Add chicken broth
6. Place the meatballs in the broth to finish cooking, without allowing the broth to thicken too much
