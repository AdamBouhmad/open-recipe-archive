## Ingredients

- niztamal ground like atole
- milk
- anise
- sugar
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Grind niztamal like atole, leave in a pot until the next day
2. Remove the water and put the dough in a small pot with two cups of milk, a little anise and a piece of sugar, two spoonfuls of butter
3. Cook until it pulls away and smells like tamale dough
4. Pour into a bowl and when cold add three eggs with white and three without whites
5. Once it is hard and beaten, add the fritters to not-very-hot butter, pouring the butter over them so they open; drop them in the syrup that has orange blossom water and granulated sugar on top
