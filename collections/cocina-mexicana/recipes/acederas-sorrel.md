## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- good butter or olive oil
- salt
- black pepper
- parsley
- onions

## Directions

1. Wash the sorrel leaves well, removing the stems
2. Leave them one or two hours in fresh water
3. Remove and dry them, then chop finely with a knife on a board
4. Put them in a saucepan with good butter or olive oil, salt, black pepper, parsley and onions, all finely chopped
5. When fried, remove from heat and serve with the aforementioned dishes
