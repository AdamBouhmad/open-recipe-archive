## Ingredients

- pork meat
- peas
- squash
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- oregano
- cooking oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Put pork meat to cook, when it is half cooked add peas and squash
2. Chop the raw tomato, chopped onion, and garlic, fry all of this in a cazuela with the meat
3. Season it afterwards with the broth with the peas and squashes
4. Add dried oregano and a little cooking oil and its salt
