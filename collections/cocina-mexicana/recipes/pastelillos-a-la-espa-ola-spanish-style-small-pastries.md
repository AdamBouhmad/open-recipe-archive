## Ingredients

- Ham
- Beef
- Chicken breast
- Garlic
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mince ham, beef, and chicken breast together
2. Season the mixture and grind it in a mortar with a little garlic
3. Fill the prepared pastry shells with this filling and finish them in the usual manner
