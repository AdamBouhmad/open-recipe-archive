## Ingredients

- White beans
- water with tequesquite
- water
- butter
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- oil
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the white beans with a little tequesquite water.
2. When cooked, wash them well, and also wash the pot they were cooked in.
3. In the same pot add clean water, butter, and salt, place over heat.
4. Chop tomato without seeds and a little onion.
5. Heat butter in a pan, fry the onion, then the tomato, then the beans.
6. Let simmer until thickened and serve with oil, chiles in vinegar, etc.
