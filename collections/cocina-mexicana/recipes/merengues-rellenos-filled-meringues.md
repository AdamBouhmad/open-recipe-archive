## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- three ounces powdered sugar
- zest of one lemon
- fine sugar for dusting
- candied or preserved fruit or raisins for filling

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites until they thicken.
2. Add three ounces of powdered sugar and lemon zest.
3. Stir very well until the mixture becomes completely liquid.
4. Drop spoonfuls of it at half-inch intervals on papers arranged on sheet metal, forming meringues of the desired shape and leaving a hollow in the middle.
5. Dust with fine sugar and bake at a gentle heat.
6. When they are well risen and have taken on good color, remove from the fire.
7. Place some candied fruit or raisins in the middle of each, and cover one meringue with another.
