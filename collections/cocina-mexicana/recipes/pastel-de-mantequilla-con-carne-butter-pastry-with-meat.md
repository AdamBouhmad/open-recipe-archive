## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- water
- a bit of salt
- one pound of butter
- beaten eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Knead gently one pound of flour with water and a bit of salt until it rises when you press your finger on it
2. Extend with a rolling tool and place one pound of butter on it
3. Give it four folds like a napkin, extend again with the rolling tool and fold in half afterwards
4. Repeat this operation seven times
5. Then form the pastry and place on a tray moistened with water
6. Coat the pastry on the outside with beaten eggs
7. When it is half cooked, sprinkle sugar powder on top
