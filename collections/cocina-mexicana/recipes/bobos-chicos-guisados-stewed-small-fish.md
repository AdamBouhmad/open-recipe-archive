## Ingredients

- Small bobos fish
- Lard (manteca)
- Onion, chopped
- Water
- Salt
- Dried chiles
- Dried chile pepper veins
- Lime juice

## Directions

1. Fry plenty of chopped onion in lard
2. Add water, sufficient salt and some whole dried chiles
3. Let the broth boil well and add the cleaned small bobos to cook
4. If desired, add dried chile pepper veins and a little lime juice
