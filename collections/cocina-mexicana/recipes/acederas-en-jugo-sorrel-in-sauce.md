## Ingredients

- sorrel, finely chopped
- good handful of chervil
- white chard, also chopped
- water
- salt
- butter proportioned to quantity
- flour
- meat stock, beef juice or other stock
- hard boiled eggs or fried eggs or meat or fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Chop the sorrel very finely, add a good handful of chervil and white chard, also chopped
2. Put everything to cook in water with a little salt
3. When cooked, remove and drain well
4. Then fry in the saucepan with butter proportioned to its quantity
5. Dust with flour without ceasing to stir it
6. Moisten with meat stock, beef juice or any other stock
7. When it has cooked for a quarter hour, put on the platter and on top whatever seems best: pieces of hard boiled eggs, fried eggs, meat or fried bread crusts
