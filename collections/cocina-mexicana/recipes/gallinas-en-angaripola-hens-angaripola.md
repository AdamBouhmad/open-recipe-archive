## Ingredients

- pork feet
- tongue
- ham
- chorizos
- hens
- lard
- tomato slices
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cilantro, toasted
- small chiles
- olives
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- onion slices
- oil
- vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the pork feet, tongue, ham, chorizos and hens
2. In a pot buttered with cold lard, arrange a layer of round tomato slices
3. Add a layer of cooked pork feet with ground clove, cinnamon, pepper and toasted cilantro
4. Add small chiles, olives, raisins, almonds, pine nuts and another layer of onion slices
5. Place the hen with another layer of tomato slices
6. Add a layer of chorizos and ham with the same spices, pine nuts, raisins and almonds
7. Make the last layer of onion, tomato and parsley
8. Place over heat and remove when the onion is soft
9. Then add plenty of oil and vinegar, taking care not to add water or broth either before or after
