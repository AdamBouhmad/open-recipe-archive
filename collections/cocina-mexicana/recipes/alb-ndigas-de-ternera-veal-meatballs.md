## Ingredients

- Veal, half a pound
- pork, half a pound
- pepper
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- potatoes
- chorizo
- capers
- egg, cooked
- parsley
- garlic, half a head
- tomato, three large
- water

## Directions

1. Grind veal and pork with pepper and salt
2. Chop tomato, onion and garlic very finely and fry well in butter
3. Cook potatoes and chorizo separately and simmer in the mixture
4. Add capers, a cooked egg and a bit of parsley
5. Make meatballs with the ground meat and fill with some of the picadillo mixture
6. Fry in butter
7. Make a sauce with half a head of fried garlic, three large roasted and ground tomatoes, and capers
8. Grind everything without removing the dark parts of the tomato and fry well in butter
9. Add water and then the meatballs
10. Let cook
