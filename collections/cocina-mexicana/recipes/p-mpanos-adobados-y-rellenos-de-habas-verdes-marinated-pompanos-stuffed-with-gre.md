## Ingredients

- Pámpanos
- Chiles anchos, deveined and soaked
- Garlic
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Habas verdes (green broad beans)
- Egg
- Lard

## Directions

1. Grind wide chiles, deveined and soaked, with garlic, pepper and clove
2. Dissolve the ground mixture with vinegar and season with salt
3. Marinate in this broth the pámpanos, already cleaned and desalted if not fresh
4. Fill with green broad beans bound with egg
5. Wrap in oiled papers and fry in lard, or cook on the grill
