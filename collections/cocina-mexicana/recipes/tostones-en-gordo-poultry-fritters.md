## Ingredients

- poultry minced
- fried and well seasoned
- soaked breadcrumbs in cream
- two raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a minced preparation of poultry, fried and well seasoned, thickened with breadcrumbs soaked in cream and two raw egg yolks, all well pounded in a mortar
2. Place a small piece of this filling the size of a walnut in each tostón
3. Complete the operation as with the other types
