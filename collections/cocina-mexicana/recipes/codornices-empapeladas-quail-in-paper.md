## Ingredients

- quail
- sauce durcelle
- paper
- fat ham slice
- lean ham slice

## Directions

1. Coat the quail in durcelle sauce, having first divided them in half after roasting and letting them cool.
2. Wrap them in paper the same way as beef ribs, garnished on one side with a slice of fat ham and on the other with a very thin slice of lean ham.
