## Ingredients

- oil
- garlic cloves
- small pieces of pickled fish
- raw oil
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper powder
- capers
- pickled capers
- thyme powder
- salt
- vinegar
- egg whites
- egg yolks
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Fry clean garlic cloves in oil in a pan until golden, then remove them.
2. Add small pieces of pickled fish to the oil and fry.
3. Once fried, drain well.
4. Add the fried fish to a little raw oil with small amounts of clove, cinnamon, and pepper powder, finely chopped capers and pickled capers, thyme powder, salt, and a splash of vinegar.
5. Return to the heat and half-poach, then remove.
6. Beat egg whites, and when well thickened, add double the amount of egg yolks, mix and beat with a little flour.
7. Mix the escabeche with the eggs.
8. Place everything in a pan lined with butter.
9. Cook at two gentle heats.
10. Serve the cake dry with chilote sauce, or cut into pieces in escabeche broth.
