## Ingredients

- Purslane
- Pork meat in pieces
- Salt
- Chilpocles (dried chilies)
- Garlic cloves
- Broth from cooking the meat

## Directions

1. Cook the purslane separately
2. Cook pork meat in pieces with salt
3. Soak the chilpocles and grind them with garlic cloves
4. Fry the meat and remove it
5. Fry the ground chilpocle mixture in the same pan
6. Drain the purslane, chop it, and add it to the fried chilpocle with the meat and some of the meat broth
