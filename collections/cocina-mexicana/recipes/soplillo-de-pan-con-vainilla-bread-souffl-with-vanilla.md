## Ingredients

- cream, two quarts
- vanilla stick
- sugar, six ounces
- bread mollete, one pound
- butter, equivalent to two eggs
- whole eggs, two
- egg yolks, four
- egg whites, four

## Directions

1. Boil two quarts of cream and add a vanilla stick and six ounces of sugar
2. After three or four boils, remove from heat
3. Remove the crumb from one pound of mollete bread and soak it in the cream while still very hot
4. Leave until all has cooled
5. Place the bread in clean linen and press and squeeze to extract the cream
6. Put in a mortar with the vanilla and grind well
7. Add butter equivalent to two eggs, two whole eggs and four yolks
8. When well combined, pass through a sieve or quenelle sieve, pressing from above with a wooden spoon
9. Put this bread mixture in a pot
10. Beat four egg whites as for sponge cake and mix with the bread crumb
11. Place the soufflé in a silver pot and put in a gentle oven, or place over hot coals with a dome oven on top
12. Serve immediately when cooked
