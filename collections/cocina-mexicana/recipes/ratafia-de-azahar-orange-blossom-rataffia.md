## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- twelve ounces of orange blossoms, recently picked
- sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of the consistency of jarabe with two pounds of sugar, and before removing it from the fire, add twelve ounces of recently picked orange blossoms
2. Let it cool and add sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente
3. Place it in an unglazed sandstone jug to leave it for twenty days in infusion at atmospheric temperature and in a mild place
4. After this time, filter and store
