## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Butter
- Flour
- Water or broth
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook and drain the carrots
2. Place in a saucepan a piece of butter and two tablespoons of flour
3. Moisten with water or broth after adding salt
4. When the broth is thick, add the carrots and two lumps of sugar
5. Let boil a little and serve
