## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- broth

## Directions

1. Turn or cut the potatoes with special spoons or knives as previously described.
2. Cook them in boiling salted water for the necessary time.
3. Place them in cold water before they begin to break apart.
4. Then heat them with well-reduced broth.
