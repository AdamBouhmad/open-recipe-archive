## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Carrots
- Large onions
- Turnips
- Parsley
- Fresh butter
- Defatted broth
- Salt
- Bread crumb
- Milk

## Directions

1. Chop coarsely mushrooms, carrots, large onions, turnips, and parsley
2. Fry everything in a little fresh butter
3. After frying, add sufficient defatted broth
4. Season with salt and let boil for one hour
5. Remove from heat and pass through a sieve
6. When serving, add roux made with bread crumb that has been cooked in milk, ground and also passed through a sieve
