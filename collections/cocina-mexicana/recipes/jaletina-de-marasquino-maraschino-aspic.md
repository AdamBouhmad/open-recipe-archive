## Ingredients

- Two ounces of isinglass
- Clarified syrup made with twelve ounces of sugar
- Five glasses of maraschino from Zara
- One small glass of kirschwasser
- One glass of filtered water

## Directions

1. Cook the isinglass with clarified syrup made from twelve ounces of sugar as indicated for orange jelly
2. When the syrup and isinglass are almost cool, add five glasses of maraschino from Zara and one small glass of kirschwasser
3. Pass everything through a silk sieve
4. Add one glass of filtered water
5. Fill molds with this composition
6. Set the aspic on crushed ice
