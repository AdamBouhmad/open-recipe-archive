## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- Water
- Salt
- Thyme branches
- Bay leaves
- Wide chiles
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Ginger
- Good vinegar
- Sour lime slices
- Orange leaves
- Avocado leaves
- Raw onion
- Olives
- Small chiles
- Pickled small chiles
- Hard boiled eggs
- Oregano powder

## Directions

1. After well bleeding the veal pulp, place to cook in water with salt, some thyme branches and some bay leaves
2. When very cooked, set aside and wrapped in clean linen press so it drains well
3. Grind plenty of wide chiles well deveined and washed, with clean garlic, a little oregano, cumin and ginger, and loosen everything with good vinegar, seasoning with necessary salt
4. In small barrels or kegs place a layer of slices of the cooked meat and another of the marinade sauce somewhat thick, with some bay leaves and a little powdered thyme, and on top slices of sour lime and orange and avocado leaves
5. Over it place another layer of meat, then another of marinade with the additions of the previous one, and thus they go alternating until the meat desired is pickled
6. After eight days the marinade will be good, which is garnished on the plates with raw sliced onion, olives, small chiles and pickled small chiles, slices of hard egg and oregano powder
