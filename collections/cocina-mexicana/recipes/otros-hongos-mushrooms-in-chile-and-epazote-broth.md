## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- garlic heads
- mulato chiles
- raw garlic
- salt
- masa
- epazote

## Directions

1. Remove the stems and wash as before; place in a pot with sufficient water, two heads of mashed garlic, two or three mulato chiles ground with raw garlic according to the quantity of mushrooms, and salt.
2. Cook and once they are almost cooked, season with a little masa and a sprig of epazote.
