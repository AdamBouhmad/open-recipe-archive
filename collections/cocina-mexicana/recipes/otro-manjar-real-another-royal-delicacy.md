## Ingredients

- twelve cuartillos of milk
- forty egg yolks
- half pound very finely ground and dissolved rice
- sweetener to taste
- whole cloves
- cinnamon sticks
- half pound almonds
- one cup orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Heat the milk on the stove
2. When it begins to boil, add whole cloves and cinnamon sticks
3. Add the ground and dissolved rice, sweetener, half pound almonds, and one cup orange blossom water
4. Bring to the point of manjar blanco
