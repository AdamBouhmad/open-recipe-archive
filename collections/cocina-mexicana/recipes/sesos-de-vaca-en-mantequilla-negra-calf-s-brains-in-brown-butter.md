## Ingredients

- four calf's brains
- salt
- vinegar
- brown butter
- parsley for frying

## Directions

1. Remove the skins and fibers from four calf's brains, clean them well and soak them for many hours
2. In a pot with boiling water, add a handful of salt and half a glass of vinegar, and blanch the brains in this water for about five minutes
3. Remove from heat and leave in the same water so they become firm and compact, then cook for three-quarters of an hour in a good marinade (see MARINADE, page 510)
4. At the moment of serving, pour brown butter over them and surround with fried parsley
