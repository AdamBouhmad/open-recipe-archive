## Ingredients

- pork sirloin meat
- salt
- pepper
- a small amount of clove
- cinnamon, all finely ground and sifted
- a little generous wine

## Directions

1. Mince the pork sirloin meat perfectly with a knife
2. Add salt, pepper, a small amount of clove, cinnamon, all finely ground and sifted, and a little generous wine
3. Knead well and leave in this state for three or four days
4. After that stuff into narrow intestines, usually from lamb
5. Tie them at the desired length
6. Put them to dry in the air in a cool place
