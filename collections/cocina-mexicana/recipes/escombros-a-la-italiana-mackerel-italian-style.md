## Ingredients

- Mackerel, cleaned and heads and tails removed
- White wine
- Onion slices
- Carrot peelings
- Parsley branches
- Bay leaf
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the heads and tails from four cleaned mackerel
2. Place in a pan with half a bottle of white wine, some onion slices, carrot peelings, parsley branches, a bay leaf, and plenty of salt
3. Cook
