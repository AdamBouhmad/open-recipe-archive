## Ingredients

- Apples, peeled and without seeds
- Syrup
- Wine

## Directions

1. Cook peeled apples without seeds
2. Grind them and mix with syrup
3. Put over fire until it takes the consistency of paste
4. Soak the sponge cake with syrup and wine
5. Proceed as in the other antes
