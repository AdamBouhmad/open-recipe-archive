## Ingredients

- Five quartillos of milk sweetened and cooked
- five pounds of fine sugar
- one cinnamon stick
- one pound of mamey passed through a metate and strainer

## Directions

1. Cook five quartillos of milk with five pounds of fine sugar and one cinnamon stick until sweetened
2. Strain it
3. Mix in one pound of mamey passed through a metate and strainer
4. Simmer while stirring until it reaches its proper stage
5. Add orange blossom water and flavoring
