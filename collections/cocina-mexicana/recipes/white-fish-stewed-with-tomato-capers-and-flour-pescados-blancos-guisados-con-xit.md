## Ingredients

- oil
- tomatoes, roasted and ground
- garlic
- parsley, chopped
- capers
- onions, cooked
- ground pepper
- salt
- flour, browned
- water
- white fish

## Directions

1. Fry roasted and ground tomatoes with garlic in oil
2. After frying, add chopped parsley, capers, cooked onions, ground pepper, salt, and browned flour dissolved in the water the onions were cooked in
3. Season the broth well and before it thickens, add the cleaned white fish to cook
4. For serving, add oil and garnish with large capers, small chiles, and olives
