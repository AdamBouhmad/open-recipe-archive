## Ingredients

- potatoes, peeled
- water
- salt
- cod
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- onions, chopped
- shallots, chopped
- verjuice or vinegar
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the raw potatoes and put them to cook in water with a little salt.
2. When they lack only a quarter of their cooking time, add a piece of cod and let it cook perfectly.
3. Drain both things.
4. Dress the cod in a plate that can withstand fire, placing the potatoes around it and dividing them in two halves if they are very thick.
5. Add a piece of butter with chopped parsley, onions and shallots, a little verjuice or a splash of vinegar and coarse pepper.
6. Place the plate on the trivet over gentle heat and stir frequently.
7. Serve this stew very hot as soon as it is seasoned.
