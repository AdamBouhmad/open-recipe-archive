## Ingredients

- thick parsley roots
- water
- salt
- perch
- butter sauce
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Scrape thick parsley roots as you would parsnips
2. Boil a good handful of them in four quartillos of lightly salted water
3. Prepare the perch and place them in this water
4. Remove and reserve the parsley roots to place over the fish later
5. Boil the perch for about six to eight minutes
6. Serve in a deep dish with the cooking liquid poured over
7. Add the parsley roots and warm butter sauce
8. Serve potatoes separately to accompany the perch
