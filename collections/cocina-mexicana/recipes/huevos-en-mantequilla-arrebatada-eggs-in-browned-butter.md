## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Break eggs in the plate as before, taking care not to burst the yolks
2. Fry butter in a skillet until almost burned
3. Skim and strain it, then reheat
4. Pour over the eggs
5. Drain the eggs with the butter in the skillet, place over hot ashes to finish cooking
6. Place on a plate and sprinkle with vinegar heated in the skillet; the vinegar must be almost boiling
