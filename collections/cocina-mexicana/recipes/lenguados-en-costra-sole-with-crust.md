## Ingredients

- butter
- fine herbs
- shallots
- mushrooms
- sole fish
- fine bread crumbs
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Spread a piece of butter on a silver plate
2. Cook fine herbs, shallots, and mushrooms finely minced in butter separately and then place on the silver plate
3. Arrange the sole on top
4. Cover lightly with very fine breadcrumbs and sprinkle with some drops of butter
5. Moisten with white wine and let cook over low heat under a cloche oven so that the crust forms gently
6. Check that they are well seasoned, add lemon juice, and serve very hot
