## Ingredients

- potatoes
- parsley in sprigs
- browned butter

## Directions

1. Cook in the ordinary way.
2. Peel and cut into pieces.
3. Place on a platter surrounded with parsley sprigs.
4. Pour over them a sauce of browned butter.
5. Serve promptly.
