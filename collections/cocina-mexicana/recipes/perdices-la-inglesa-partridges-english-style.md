## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Oil
- Pepper sauce or other sauce

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning, and singing the partridges, split them open from the tail to the crop.
2. Turn them inside out through the legs.
3. Flatten them with a mallet.
4. Dip them in an oil marinade.
5. Place them on grills over a hot fire.
6. Serve with a pepper sauce or another sauce of choice.
