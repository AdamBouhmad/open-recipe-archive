## Ingredients

- Pears
- Cinnamon sticks
- Cloves
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. In a pan or pot with an almost flat bottom, arrange the peeled pears that have been studded with cinnamon sticks and cloves.
2. Pour clarified syrup over them and let them simmer over low heat until they reach the consistency of a preserve, being careful to turn them without breaking them.
3. When the pears are cooked and the syrup has reached the consistency of a jelly, place them on a plate and cover them with their jelly, which should have been well skimmed so that it is clean and smooth over the pears.
