## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- lard or butter
- salt
- salt and pepper or sauce such as chile or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes either by putting them in water and putting them on the fire until they boil well, or by putting them in a pot with little water, covering the mouth with a cloth, and then with a well-fitted lid or pot
2. Peel the cooked potatoes, slice them and fry them in lard or butter, adding the corresponding salt
3. When they are ready to brown, serve them alone, or with salt and pepper, or accompanied by some sauce, such as chile or tomato sauce
