## Ingredients

- Three pounds white sugar
- Egg whites
- Two cuartillos skim milk

## Directions

1. Make syrup with three pounds of white sugar
2. Clarify with egg whites
3. When at mirror point, add two cuartillos of skim milk
4. Bring to the proper point where a drop in cold water forms a ball
5. Remove from fire and beat until very white
6. Pour into a board of desired height and cut out candies with tin molds
