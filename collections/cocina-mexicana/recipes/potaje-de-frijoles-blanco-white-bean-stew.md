## Ingredients

- White beans
- Water
- Fried onion
- Crushed garlic
- Saffron
- A leaf of spearmint
- Bread crumbs, cheese or rice (for thickening, optional)
- Raw oil, pepper, parsley and spearmint (alternative preparation)
- Oil and vinegar (alternative preparation)

## Directions

1. Put beans to cook
2. After the first boil, change the water
3. When well cooked, season with fried onion, crushed garlic, saffron and a leaf of spearmint
4. If desired to thicken, do so with a little bread crumbs, cheese or rice
5. Alternatively, beans can be prepared with raw oil, pepper, parsley and spearmint, reheating over low heat
6. Others eat them with only oil and vinegar
