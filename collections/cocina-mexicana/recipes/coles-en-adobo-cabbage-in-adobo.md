## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- wide chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- bread
- vinegar
- butter
- pork broth
- pork ribs
- onions

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage and put to cook with salt
2. When cooked, remove from the fire and when cold, chop coarsely
3. Devein wide chiles, and after washing very well, grind them with garlic cloves and a few cumin seeds, adding a piece of bread soaked in vinegar
4. Fry all this in a little butter, and put the cabbages there, adding a little water or pork broth
5. Also add well-cooked pork ribs, seasoning with necessary salt, adding whole cooked onions and let cook at low heat
