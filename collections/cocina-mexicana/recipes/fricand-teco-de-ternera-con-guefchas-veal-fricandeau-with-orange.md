## Ingredients

- veal pulp
- orange juice
- lemon
- ham
- water
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- garlic crushed
- spices
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- butter
- carrot slices
- oil
- quellites
- onion
- cumin

## Directions

1. Take veal pulps and shape them like bones, place in orange juice and leave for a whole day
2. The next day, coat with lemon, stud with ham and place in a saucepan with equal parts water and vinegar with bay leaves, thyme, crushed garlic, plenty of spices, cinnamon, clove and pepper, and a spoonful of butter and carrot slices
3. When cooked, coat with oil and butter, and place over two fires until half-browned
4. Should be brought to the table whole, on a sauce of cooked quellites seasoned with garlic, onion and a little cumin
5. They should remain dry when served and are adorned with carrot slices
