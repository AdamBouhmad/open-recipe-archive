## Ingredients

- Dried fava beans
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Saffron
- Mint, bunch of
- Water
- Rice, clean
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fava beans well and scald them before adding to the pot
2. Cook and remove the foam because there are always small worms in them
3. When cooked, grind garlic, pepper, saffron and a bunch of mint and dissolve all with a little hot water
4. For every four handfuls of fava beans add one handful of clean rice
5. Season with salt, stir everything, remove from heat and let rest a while
