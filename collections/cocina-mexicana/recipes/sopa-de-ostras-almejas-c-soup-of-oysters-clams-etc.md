## Ingredients

- oysters or clams
- cleaned and chopped onions
- fresh butter
- flour
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters or clams in their own water for two or three minutes
2. Cook cleaned and chopped onions in fresh butter
3. Add a little flour and moisten everything with the water released by the oysters or clams
4. Put the oysters or clams and toasts fried in butter into this broth
5. Add a liaison of egg yolks
6. Proceed the same way for soups of any kind of shellfish
