## Ingredients

- Pieces of curdled milk
- Clarified syrup at medium point
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. With a spoon, place pieces of curdled milk into clarified syrup at medium point
2. Allow to boil over gentle fire until the syrup reaches a point higher than ordinary preserves
3. With great care, place the pieces of curdled milk in the dish
4. Pour the syrup over them and dust with ground cinnamon
