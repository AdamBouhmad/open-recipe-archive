## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cheese
- Lard
- Salt

## Directions

1. The number of eggs should be proportionate to the number of people eating; the cheese should be one-third of the egg weight and the lard one-sixth
2. Break and beat the eggs in a pan
3. Add the lard and cheese (grated or cut into small pieces)
4. Place the mixture over the fire, stirring with a spoon until it thickens
5. Add salt and form the pastry in the usual manner
