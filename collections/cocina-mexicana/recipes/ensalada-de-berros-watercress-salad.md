## Ingredients

- watercress
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Remove the green leaves and skin from the watercress
2. Cook with salt
3. After drying, dress with oil, vinegar
4. Some add small chiles and olives
5. It is good for relieving scurvy
