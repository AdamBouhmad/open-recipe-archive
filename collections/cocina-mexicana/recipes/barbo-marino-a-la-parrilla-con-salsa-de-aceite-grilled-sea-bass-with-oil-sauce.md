## Ingredients

- Sea bass
- Oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the scales, gut the fish, and wash it
2. Drain well and dry it thoroughly
3. Open it along the back
4. Marinate it in oil with salt and coarse pepper
5. Place it on the grill, taking care that it does not stick or burn
6. Baste it with oil and turn it frequently
7. Ensure that the white side takes on a good color
8. When well cooked, arrange it on a plate
9. Garnish with slices of seedless lemon
10. Serve with oil sauce
