## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove outer leaves and blanch the artichokes
2. Cook in boiling water
3. Place in a cazuela with seasoned broth with salt and pepper
4. Fry onion and add a handful of flour to the oil until it browns
5. Pour this over the artichokes to make a sauce
6. Turn the artichokes two or three times
