## Ingredients

- chiles anchos (wide dried chiles)
- relleno (filling)
- harina (flour)
- huevo batido (beaten egg)
- manteca (lard)

## Directions

1. Prepare the wide dried chiles the same way as for capon chiles
2. Fill them the same way as previous recipes, but not with minced pork
3. Coat with flour
4. Coat with beaten egg
5. Fry in lard
6. Place in the appropriate broth for each type of filling, as previously explained
