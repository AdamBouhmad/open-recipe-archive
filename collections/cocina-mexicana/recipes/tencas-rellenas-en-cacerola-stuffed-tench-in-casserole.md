## Ingredients

- tench
- fish forcemeat
- butter
- fine herbs
- onions
- salt
- black pepper
- breadcrumbs
- crayfish substance

## Directions

1. Soak in water and clean
2. Open along the back
3. Debone and stuff with a good fish forcemeat
4. Coat a plate with butter and add fine herbs, onions, salt and black pepper
5. Arrange the stuffed tench on top
6. Season with the same preparation and spices
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
8. Bake in the oven until golden
9. Serve dry or with a vegetable stew or crayfish substance
