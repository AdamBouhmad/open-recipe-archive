## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds, cut into dice
- one portion of candied orange blossom
- two egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Dry the almonds in the oven or candy them
2. Mix with one portion of candied orange blossom in a royal glaze made with two well-beaten egg whites
3. Place in boxes and bake in the oven like the previous recipe
