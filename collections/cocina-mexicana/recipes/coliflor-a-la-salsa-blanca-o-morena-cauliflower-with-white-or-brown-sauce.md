## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- butter
- white sauce
- strained broth

## Directions

1. Clean and wash well
2. Place to cook in water with a little salt and butter
3. After cooked place on a platter, trying to give them the shape of a single cauliflower, and pour white sauce or strained broth over them
4. It can also be put in the same sauce and turned a few times over the fire, though this way they do not look as good but taste better
