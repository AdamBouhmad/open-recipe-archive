## Ingredients

- twelve or more eggs
- bladder for cooking

## Directions

1. Break twelve or more eggs, separating the whites from the yolks
2. Remove the skins from the yolks, and gathering all of them in a clean bladder, tie it with a thread so that it forms a round ball
3. Put water to boil in a pot and throw the bladder there, hanging it from the thread so it does not stick to the bottom
4. When it is considered that the yolks have set well, remove them, and as soon as they are cold, remove the skin of the bladder and there will remain a large yolk that will be accommodated in another bladder, putting the whites there, ensuring that the yolk remains in the middle and the bladder is oval-shaped
5. Cook again in hot water, removing it when it has set, and when cold, remove the bladder, and there will remain an enormous egg, which can be served whole or in halves, or in slices that partake of the white and the yolk, presenting it with a sauce corresponding to hard-boiled eggs, or in oil and vinegar, adorned with sliced onion, oregano and chopped parsley
