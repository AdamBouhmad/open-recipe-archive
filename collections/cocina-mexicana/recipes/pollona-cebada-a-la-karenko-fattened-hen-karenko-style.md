## Ingredients

- fattened hen
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- garlic
- mushrooms, raw
- parsley, minced
- tomato sauce
- thick Spanish sauce
- aspic
- earthnut sweetbreads, sliced
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Singe and empty the hen and divide into portions as for fricassée
2. Heat oil in a pot and place the portions there, parboiling with salt, pepper, nutmeg, a little garlic and fifteen raw mushrooms
3. Fry the hen until each portion turns yellow and well browned and all are well cooked
4. Drain the oil and add a little minced parsley, one spoonful of tomato sauce, two of thick Spanish sauce and a piece of aspic the size of a walnut
5. Let everything boil on low heat together with some small slices of earthnut sweetbreads
6. Arrange the hen on its plate, drizzle with lemon juice and cover with the sauce
