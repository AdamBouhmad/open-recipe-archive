## Ingredients

- ten to twelve pounds quince (peeled and divided into pieces)
- nine to ten jars of good claret must

## Directions

1. After peeling and dividing the quinces into pieces, weigh ten or twelve pounds of this fruit and add to nine or ten jars of good claret must
2. Let ferment in a barrel for thirty days
3. After this time strain the liquor and pour into a barrel or other vessel, which should be sealed exactly
