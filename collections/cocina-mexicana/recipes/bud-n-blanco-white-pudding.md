## Ingredients

- Raw fish meat
- Chicken breasts
- Cow sweetbreads
- Calf brain
- Carp roe
- Breadcrumb cooked in milk
- Butter
- Cow udder cooked in white broth
- Rice cooked in milk
- White onion broth
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop together raw fish meat, chicken breasts, cow sweetbreads, calf brain and carp roe
2. Grind them very fine or crush in a metate or mortar
3. Add breadcrumb cooked in milk until it dries
4. Add as much butter as the amount of meat and breadcrumb used
5. Add a cow udder previously cooked in white broth and chopped
6. Add a quarter part of rice cooked in milk
7. Add the same amount of white onion substance or broth
8. Add five or six egg yolks, each thing separately, taking care to grind and crush one before adding the other
9. Fill the intestines, taking care not to fill them too much so they don't burst when cooking
10. Tie them at both ends
11. Place them in boiling water for a quarter of an hour
12. To make sure they are cooked, remove them with a skimmer and prick with a pin
13. If blood no longer comes out but only fat, it is a sign they are cooked
14. Let them cool then to roast them on a grill when you want to serve them
