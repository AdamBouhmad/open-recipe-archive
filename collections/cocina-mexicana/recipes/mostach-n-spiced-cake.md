## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted sugar
- ten yolks and five whites
- thirty ground cloves
- one pound of flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the eggs in the mixing bowl, mix the sugar and beat with the hands
2. Add the finely ground cloves
3. When the dough rises, add the flour and mix well
4. Once consistent, spread butter on the palms of the hands and make small balls of regular size
5. Wrap them in paper to take to the oven
6. When cooked, remove the papers and place them in a clean pot and cover well so they stay white
