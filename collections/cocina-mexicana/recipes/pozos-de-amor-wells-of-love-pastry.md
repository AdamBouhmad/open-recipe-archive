## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Sugar
- Preserves or pastes for filling

## Directions

1. After the puff pastry dough has been given all its turns and folds, make a base of two lines thickness and cut it with a fluted mold (that is, with the fluted pastry cutter)
2. Place this first part on the baking sheet or platform, and then cut with another pastry cutter of the same class but smaller, another small base that is placed on top of the first
3. With another even smaller and round pastry cutter, dipped in boiling water, cut another sheet that is placed on top, pressing or sinking it down a bit
4. Glaze the wells and put them in the oven
5. When they are almost cooked, dust them with fine sugar and put them to snow, that is, make the sugar melt or dissolve and coat them
6. Take them out, hollow them, and fill them with the preserves or pastes that are most pleasing
