## Ingredients

- One pound of blackberries
- One pound and ten ounces of medium sugar
- Tejocote jelly
- Sugar for tejocote season

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of blackberry and leave for three days in the open air and shade
2. Stir daily with a spoon, strain on the third day through a serviette
3. Clarify one pound and ten ounces of medium sugar to the ball stage
4. Incorporate the hot blackberry broth, as cold it would lose color
5. Let it take the point of jelly although it may not be firm; but in tejocote season add a cuartillo of jelly and two pounds of sugar so it becomes firm
6. Place afterwards in glasses
