## Ingredients

- lamb pulp or pork loin
- all spices
- chopped raisins
- chorizo sliced
- chicken divided in pieces
- potatoes cut
- beets chopped finely
- alcaparras
- olives
- tornachiles
- whole almonds
- plenty of parsley
- beaten eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a picadillo of lamb pulp or pork loin
2. Add all spices, chopped raisins, sliced chorizo, chicken divided in pieces, cut potatoes, finely chopped beets, alcaparras, olives, tornachiles, whole almonds and plenty of parsley
3. Mix all with beaten eggs in sufficient quantity
4. Pour into a casserole with hot lard
5. Cook over embers covered with a comal with fire on top
6. When the cake is cooked, lift with a knife
7. Add pepper on top before serving at the table
