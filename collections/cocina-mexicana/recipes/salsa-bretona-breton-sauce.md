## Ingredients

- Some large headed onions
- A good piece of butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Thyme
- Laurel
- A little sugar
- Vinegar
- A little tomato sauce
- 1 cup of well-cooked white beans, mashed
- A good piece of very fresh butter

## Directions

1. Remove the beards and tails from some large headed onions and slice them thin
2. Place them in a pot with a good piece of butter, salt, pepper, garlic, thyme, and laurel
3. Fry everything over moderately high heat until the onion has browned
4. Move the pot to a low fire, cover it, and place a little heat or embers on the lid
5. When the onion is cooked, put it back over a more intense fire and add a little sugar and vinegar
6. Let everything reduce until the onion begins to curdle
7. Add a little tomato sauce and 1 cup of well-cooked white beans that have been mashed
8. Pass everything through a strainer and let it simmer over low heat with a good piece of very fresh butter
9. This sauce is commonly served with beans or green beans
