## Ingredients

- pork tongues
- salt
- broad chiles
- lard
- garlic cloves
- tomatoes
- onion
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- broth
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Put the tongues to cook in a pot with salt, sliced and cooked
2. Remove from heat, devein two or three broad chiles and fry them in lard with garlic cloves and ground tomatoes
3. Put all this in a cazuela with lard, two or three bunches of onion in wedges, and when well fried add the meat and a little broth in which the tongues cooked
4. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper and add it; the gravy should be thick
5. Garnish with chiles and olives
