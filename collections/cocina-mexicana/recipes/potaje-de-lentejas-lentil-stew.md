## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Salt
- Oil or lard
- Chopped parsley
- Fried bread crusts (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook lentils with salt
2. Place in a pot with oil or lard and chopped parsley
3. Reheat a little
4. Then add water
5. Serve with fried bread crusts or without them
