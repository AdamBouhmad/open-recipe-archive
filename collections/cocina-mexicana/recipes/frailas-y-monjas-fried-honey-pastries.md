## Ingredients

- three loaves of sugar of a pound and a half each
- half a pound of butter
- eight eggs
- ten or twelve hard biscuits
- a little ground clove

## Directions

1. Make a honey mixture with three loaves of sugar of a pound and a half each and half a pound of butter
2. While it comes to a boil, beat eight eggs, first the whites and then the yolks
3. Mix the beaten eggs with ten or twelve hard biscuits that have been crushed and a little ground clove
4. When the honey and butter mixture is boiling, remove it from the heat and place it on the embers so that one person stirs it constantly while another adds the eggs
5. Finish cooking over two fires
