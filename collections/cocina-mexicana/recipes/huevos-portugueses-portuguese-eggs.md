## Ingredients

- syrup
- wine
- egg yolks
- mamones
- dragée

## Directions

1. Mix a quartillo of good syrup, another of wine, and thirty egg yolks
2. Beat everything well and cook
3. Pour over layers of mamones, sprinkling the top with dragée
