## Ingredients

- lamprey
- wine
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Heat water and when it begins to boil, place two lampreys in it, then remove them shortly after to clean off the mud or slime they are covered with
2. Empty the lampreys through the gills, being careful to remove the head near the knot so that the tail can be inserted there
3. Set aside the blood separately
4. Divide into pieces, separating the head and tail extremity
5. Make a red sauce with flour browned in butter in a pan
6. Place the lamprey pieces in the sauce, moisten with red wine
7. Add small whole onions browned in butter, mushrooms, a mixed bundle of herbs studded with clove, a little salt and pepper
8. Let the lampreys cook in this stew
9. Remove the grease from the broth
10. When ready to serve, bind the sauce with the lamprey blood that was set aside
11. Taste the stew to ensure it is well seasoned, finish and serve with fried toast and crayfish or shrimp
