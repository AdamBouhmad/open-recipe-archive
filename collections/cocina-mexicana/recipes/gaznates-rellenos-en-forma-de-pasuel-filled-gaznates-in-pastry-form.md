## Ingredients

- thirteen egg yolks
- one and a half ounces ground sugar
- one and a half ounces melted butter
- half small cup refined Spanish brandy
- flour
- salt
- tequesquite powder
- egg white

## Directions

1. Beat thirteen egg yolks until it hardens and the beaten becomes white
2. Incorporate with a small spatula one and a half ounces of ground sugar passed through a sieve, another ounce of melted butter, half a small cup of refined Spanish brandy and as much flour as can be moistened with the egg
3. Add a little salt and a little tequesquite powder, ensuring the mixture is not hard
4. After well incorporated, place on a table and give it a light kneading, adding more flour if the dough is still sticky
5. If it is soft, extend on flour powder with a rolling pin and cut the gaznates with a cutter of the desired size
6. Shape with appropriate iron or tin pincers, moisten the edges to be joined with egg white, making some in the common form
