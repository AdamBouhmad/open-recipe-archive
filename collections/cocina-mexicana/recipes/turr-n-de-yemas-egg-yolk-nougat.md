## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- twelve egg yolks, well beaten
- a mamón
- a little wine

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup and give it the hard crack point
2. To one pound of sugar add twelve well beaten egg yolks, mix and beat like nougat
3. Return to the fire to heat it and when hot, remove and dust with mamón
4. Return to the fire until it is like nougat
5. Add a little wine and place on a platter or tray and then eat
