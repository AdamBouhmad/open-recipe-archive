## Ingredients

- One dram of clove
- One adarme of mace
- Twelve quartillos of aguardiente
- Three pounds of sugar
- Six quartillos of common water
- Half a pound of orange blossom water
- Another half pound of double rose water distilled
- Two drams of jasmine spirit
- Two drams of amber spirit

## Directions

1. Put in infusion for ten or twelve hours at atmospheric temperature one dram of clove and one adarme of mace in twelve quartillos of aguardiente
2. Proceed immediately to distillation in a water bath, from which two-thirds of the liquor should be obtained
3. Dissolve three pounds of sugar in six quartillos of common water and half a pound of orange blossom water
4. Mix with the distilled liquid
5. Add another half pound of double rose water distilled, two drams of jasmine spirit and two of amber spirit
6. Filter the liquor in a covered funnel
7. Keep in well-sealed bottles because its perfume is so fugitive that it would evaporate completely
