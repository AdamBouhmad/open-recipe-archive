## Ingredients

- two young rabbits
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- truffles
- mushrooms
- shallots
- parsley

## Directions

1. Partially cook two young rabbits divided into pieces in a casserole with butter, salt, pepper, spices, truffles, mushrooms, minced shallots and parsley and a few ground or crushed aromatics
2. Let this stew cool
3. Coat a mold or casserole with butter and cover
