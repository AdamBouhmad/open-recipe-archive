## Ingredients

- coconut milk
- sugar
- egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. To each cup of coconut milk add half pound of sugar and three egg yolks
2. Grate the coconuts, squeeze them by hand and to each large cup of milk dissolve three egg yolks
3. If it becomes too thick add a little water
4. Strain through cloth, make clarified syrup, and when at a high point add the milk
5. Stir with the back of a wooden spoon and after being over heat a short time, remove as soon as it boils
