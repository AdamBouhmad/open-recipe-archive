## Ingredients

- Almond paste
- One third part by weight of desired fruit, crushed and sieved

## Directions

1. Prepare as the preceding recipes
2. After the paste is mixed into the clarified sugar, add one third part by weight of the desired fruit, crushed and passed through a sieve
3. Conclude as previously described
