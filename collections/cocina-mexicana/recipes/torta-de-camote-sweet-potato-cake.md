## Ingredients

- Sweet potato (camote)
- Hard biscuits
- Orange blossom water
- Sugar
- Beaten eggs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Grind the sweet potato with hard biscuits
2. Add orange blossom water and necessary sugar
3. Mix in beaten eggs
4. Stir everything together
5. Pour into a cooking pot or cake pan with lard
6. Set the cake over two fires
