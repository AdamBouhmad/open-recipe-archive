## Ingredients

- powdered sugar, passed through silk sieve
- one egg white, beaten
- two portions of candied orange blossoms

## Directions

1. Make a glaze that is neither too liquid nor too dry by mixing powdered sugar, passed through a silk sieve, with one beaten egg white separated from the yolk
2. When the glaze reaches the proper consistency, add two portions of candied orange blossoms
3. Fill small paper boxes, four times smaller than ordinary ones, halfway with this mixture
4. Place in a gentle but sufficiently hot oven so that the cakes do not collapse
5. Remove when golden brown
