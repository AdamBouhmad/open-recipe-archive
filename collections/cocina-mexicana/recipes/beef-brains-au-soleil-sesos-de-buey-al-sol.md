## Ingredients

- beef brains
- thick German sauce
- marinade from the brains
- fine herbs
- beaten egg
- grand sauce

## Directions

1. Place cooked brains, divided in halves, on a platform
2. Add to a thick German sauce some of the brains' marinade and fine herbs arranged as for papillotes
3. Coat and cover the brains with this sauce
4. Once cooled, coat twice with beaten egg and fry
5. Arrange in crown form at the moment of serving and pour the grand sauce (not too thick) over them
