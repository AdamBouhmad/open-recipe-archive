## Ingredients

- pork blood
- fine salt
- hot water
- cloth napkin
- a regular weight
- pork lard
- grated bread
- ground and sifted sugar
- ground black pepper
- ground clove
- ground cinnamon
- ground anise
- ground ginger
- a few grains of ground cumin

## Directions

1. When slaughtering the pig, collect the blood in a strainer or similar object to filter it
2. Avoid moving it and let it cool until it coagulates
3. Then place it in boiling water to cook and become firm
4. Remove from water, wrap in a cloth napkin, and place a regular weight on top to drain thoroughly
5. Once drained and cooled, grate it
6. Mix with a portion of grated bread and ground and sifted sugar
7. Skin a small amount of pork lard and, once cleaned of veins and foreign parts, grind it in a mortar
8. When very smooth, knead the grated blood with it
9. Mix in powder form the black pepper, clove, cinnamon, anise, ginger, and a few grains of cumin
10. Once everything is well mixed, fill the intestines with this mixture
11. Tie and place to cook
12. Note: These blood sausages spoil in a short time, and for this reason it is necessary to eat them immediately
