## Ingredients

- eggs
- cream of vanilla, lemon, chocolate or assorted
- almond cream or gelatin
- fish glue
- ice

## Directions

1. Make a small hole in an egg with a knife point
2. Loosen the yolk with a thick needle so it can be emptied
3. Once removed, fill the shell with desired cream using a funnel
4. Place the egg or eggs in egg cups for a pot where eggs can be bathed halfway
5. Give them a water bath, wash them and serve on a napkin like eggs passed through water
6. They can also be filled with almond cream or any gelatin that can be closed with fish glue, placing the eggs over ice
