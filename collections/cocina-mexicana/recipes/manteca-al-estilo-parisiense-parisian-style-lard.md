## Ingredients

- pork fat (sain)
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Choose white pork fat
2. Remove the skin
3. Beat well with a stick
4. Place in a pot or pan with a little water
5. Leave to cook over gentle heat and simmer for a long time so the lard is well cooked and can be preserved
6. Ensure it is well cooked
7. Let cool
8. When only warm, pass through a sieve
9. Place in a cool location if you wish to store it
