## Ingredients

- carp soft roe
- butter
- mushrooms
- ham
- lemon juice
- fine herbs
- good broth

## Directions

1. Let the soft roe cook for some time over low heat in a saucepan with butter, mushrooms, a slice of ham, the juice of one lemon and a small bunch of fine herbs
2. Add a little good broth
