## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley, chopped
- cream sauce, well reduced
- bread crumbs
- egg for coating
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs and chop the whites very finely
2. Mash the yolks and add chopped parsley and well-reduced cream sauce
3. When everything is seasoned form small meatballs
4. Roll them in bread crumbs to soak them a second time with egg and fry them
5. Serve covering them with fried parsley
