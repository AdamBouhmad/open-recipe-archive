## Ingredients

- oil
- quarter pounds onion
- green chiles
- tomatoes
- garlic
- lard
- vinegar
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- coriander toasted and ground
- ginger
- nutmeg
- mint
- parsley
- coriander green
- broth from cooked capons
- flour toasted on a griddle
- lard for casserole
- cooked and roasted capon quarters
- pork meat
- sausage
- ham fried
- white wine
- pickled chiles
- capers
- raisins
- almonds
- saffron

## Directions

1. Fry in oil quarters of onion, green chiles, tomatoes and garlic, all minced and in sufficient quantity, adding a little lard and a bit of vinegar.
2. Add clove, cinnamon, pepper, cumin, toasted and ground coriander, ginger and nutmeg, and add mint, parsley and green coriander, all green, and fry everything together again.
3. Then add the broth in which the capons were cooked and sprinkle everything with a little flour toasted on a griddle until it has turned reddish.
4. When everything is very thick with no broth remaining, place in a casserole greased with lard a layer of this frying and another of quarters of cooked and roasted capon with pork meat, sausage and ham, all fried (the ham is cooked first and fried afterwards in slices).
5. Fill the casserole in this manner, finally adding a little white wine, pickled chiles, capers, raisins, almonds and saffron.
