## Ingredients

- Lard
- Two cloves of garlic
- Almonds with shell
- A small piece of bread
- Hard egg yolks
- Chicken broth
- Salt
- Chicken

## Directions

1. In a little lard fry two cloves of garlic and also fry some almonds with shell and a small piece of bread
2. Grind all this with some hard egg yolks
3. Add broth from the chicken in which it was cooked
4. Season with necessary salt
5. Add the whole chicken or in pieces
6. Let it boil until the broth has thickened
