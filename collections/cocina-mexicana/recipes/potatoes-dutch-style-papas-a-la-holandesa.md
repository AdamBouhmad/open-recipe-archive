## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped herbs
- beef juice
- egg yolks, beaten
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Make a paste of potatoes as in the preceding recipes.
2. Sauté it, seasoning with salt, pepper and finely chopped herbs.
3. Moisten with a little beef juice.
4. Form into balls.
5. Roll in beaten egg yolks.
6. Fry and serve garnished with fried parsley.
