## Ingredients

- larks
- ham
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- pie dough

## Directions

1. Empty the larks and place everything removed from them, except the gizzard which is discarded, with ham at the bottom of the pie
2. Remove the legs and heads from the larks and place them on top
3. Heat them in a pan with good butter, parsley, onion, and mushrooms, all chopped
4. Let cool
5. Make and finish the pie as explained in the article on pies
