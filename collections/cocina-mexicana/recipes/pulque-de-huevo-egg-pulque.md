## Ingredients

- Pulque
- Egg yolks
- Sugar to sweeten
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with the pulque, more or less according to taste
2. Sweeten in the same manner
3. Pour pulque into glasses and sprinkle ground cinnamon on top
