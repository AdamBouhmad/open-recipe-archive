## Ingredients

- four quartillos of double, very fresh cream
- half pound of powdered sugar
- gum adragant or gum arabic in powder, the amount that can be taken with three fingers
- equal quantity of candied orange blossom, ground
- three drops of bergamot essence
- six pounds of snow
- saltpeter
- two egg whites (if needed)

## Directions

1. Place the cream, powdered sugar, gum adragant or gum arabic in powder, candied orange blossom ground, and bergamot essence in a stoneware vessel
2. Once the sugar has dissolved, crush six pounds of snow and place in a basin with saltpeter
3. Position the vessel containing the cream on the snow to cool it
4. Beat the cream with the instrument used for beating egg whites until it turns to foam more quickly
5. As the cream turns to foam, remove it with a skimmer, comb, or rake, and place in a sieve positioned over a basin
6. If the cream does not foam as needed, add two egg whites to help it along
7. Once all the beaten cream has been placed in the sieve, if it is not sufficient, collect what has passed through the sieve, beat it again, and combine with the other
8. Typically these foams are placed in gilded or silver cups made for this purpose; if not available, use glass or crystal cups placed in a kind of tin cave made for this purpose, which has a grill in the shape of the cups to place them on
9. Place crushed snow mixed with saltpeter underneath and also on top of the cave cover, which should be in the manner of the cover of a bell oven, with a kind of channel or gutter for water to drain
10. These foams can hold for two or three hours before serving
