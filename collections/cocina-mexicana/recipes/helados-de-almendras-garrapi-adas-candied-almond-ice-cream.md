## Ingredients

- eight ounces of sugar
- one and a half cuartillo of water
- the same quantity of almonds prepared as in the previous article
- orange blossom water
- remaining sugar
- milk and custard

## Directions

1. Dissolve eight ounces of sugar in one and a half cuartillo of water
2. Add the same quantity of almonds prepared as described in the previous article
3. After allowing them to become candied, set them aside
4. When cold, grind them by rolling with orange blossom water
5. Continue the operation as described in the previous article
6. Add the remaining sugar to the milk and custard
7. Freeze as instructed
