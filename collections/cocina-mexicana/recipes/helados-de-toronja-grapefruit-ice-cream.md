## Ingredients

- Zest of three grapefruits
- One and a half quartillos of milk
- Eight ounces of cream
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Put the grapefruit zest on the fire with one and a half quartillos of milk, eight ounces of cream and twelve ounces of sugar
2. Stir everything until it begins to boil
3. Pass through a clean sieve
4. Allow to cool
5. Put in the sorbetera and freeze
