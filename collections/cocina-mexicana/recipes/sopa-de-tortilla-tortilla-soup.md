## Ingredients

- tortillas
- lard
- garlic cloves, chopped
- broth
- epazote leaves

## Directions

1. Wrap the tortillas and slice them into very thin wheels and save them for the next day
2. In a pan with lard over fire, fry three or four chopped garlic cloves, then immediately the tortilla strips
3. When well fried, add sufficient broth with some epazote leaves
4. When the broth has reduced so it is not dry, serve decorated the same way as bread soup
