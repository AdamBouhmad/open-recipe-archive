## Ingredients

- Wild boar leg
- Fat ham
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Garlic
- Shallots
- Parsley
- Small onions
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Ham slices
- Sliced onions
- Carrot skins
- Large turnips
- Parsley and small onions bunch
- Two garlic heads
- Four cloves of spice
- Two laurel leaves
- Thyme
- Basil
- Salt and coarse pepper
- Half cuartillo of refined Spanish brandy
- Two cuartillos of white wine
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cover a wild boar leg on all sides with fat ham
2. Season it with salt, fine spices, garlic, shallots, parsley, small onions, thyme, laurel and basil, all chopped very fine
3. Place it in a pot of the same size with some slices of ham, sliced onions, carrot skins, large turnips, a good bunch of parsley and small onions, two garlic heads, four cloves of spice, two laurel leaves, thyme, basil, salt and coarse pepper
4. Let it sweat for half an hour over gentle fire
5. Moisten with half cuartillo of refined Spanish brandy, two cuartillos of white wine and broth
6. Make it sweat again over gentle fire for six or seven hours
7. Let the leg cool in its own cooking liquid
8. Serve as a grand cold dish with its sauce, which should be light and of good taste
