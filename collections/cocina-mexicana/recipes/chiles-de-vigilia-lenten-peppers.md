## Ingredients

- Swiss chard
- Peas
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Oil
- Lard
- Chiles
- Fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Cook chard and peas separately, each with salt
2. Once cooked remove from water and drain
3. Fry tomatoes in a skillet with half oil and half lard with chopped chard
4. Toast, peel and devein peppers of the season and fill them with the fried chard
5. In a baking dish place a layer of chard and peas and another of sliced fresh cheese and so on successively until the last layer is of chard
6. Add oil and place over two low fires until they are seasoned
