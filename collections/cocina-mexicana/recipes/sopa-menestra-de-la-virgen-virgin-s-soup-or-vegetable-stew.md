## Ingredients

- two ounces of bread crumbs
- meat broth
- two or three breasts of roasted fowl
- six shelled sweet almonds
- six cooked egg yolks
- one glass of cream
- bread crusts
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Boil two ounces of bread crumbs in meat broth for several minutes
2. Pound two or three breasts of roasted fowl, six shelled sweet almonds, and six cooked egg yolks in a mortar
3. Mix everything together and strain through a sieve or clean napkin
4. Add one glass of cream and season appropriately
5. Keep hot in a bain-marie
6. Soak bread crusts in meat broth
7. Just before serving, pour the fowl substance over the crusts and let simmer briefly
