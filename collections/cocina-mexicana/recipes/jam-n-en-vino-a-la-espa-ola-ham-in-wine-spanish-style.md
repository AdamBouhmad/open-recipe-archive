## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- fresh rosemary
- thyme
- mint
- parsley
- laurel
- white wine
- Castilian vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. In a pot put water with fresh rosemary, thyme, mint, parsley and laurel
2. Cook the ham there
3. Clean it afterwards, scrape it with a knife and dry the water
4. Then put it back on the fire with white wine and Castilian vinegar until the liquid is consumed and the ham forms a crust, which is the sign of being ready
5. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top, ground
