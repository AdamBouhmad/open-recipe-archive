## Ingredients

- 2 ounces chestnut flour or 25 roasted and ground chestnuts with a small quantity of milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 cuartillos milk
- 1 piece of butter the size of an egg
- 4 ounces powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put 2 ounces of chestnut flour or 25 roasted chestnuts ground with a small quantity of milk into a saucepan
2. Add 2 egg yolks, 2 cuartillos of milk, a piece of butter the size of an egg, and 4 ounces of powdered sugar
3. After it has boiled for a few minutes, pass through a sieve
4. Finish and let cool
