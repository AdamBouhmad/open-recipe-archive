## Ingredients

- Sixteen eggs, separated
- Rice, ground dry
- Sugar
- Fresh cheese of any kind
- A piece of butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and egg whites separately
2. Incorporate them together with dry ground rice, sugar, fresh cheese of any kind, and a piece of butter the size of an egg, reaching the consistency of mamón
3. Fill the butter-lined custard cakes and place to bake in the oven
