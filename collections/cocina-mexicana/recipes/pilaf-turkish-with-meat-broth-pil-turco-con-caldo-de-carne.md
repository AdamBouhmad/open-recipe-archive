## Ingredients

- one measure rice
- three measures good broth
- saffron

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well in lukewarm water
2. Put it in a vessel with three measures of good broth
3. Close the vessel hermetically and place over a very hot fire
4. When it begins to boil, dissolve a little saffron in a cup with broth and pour into the rice vessel
5. Let it boil vigorously, always keeping the pot tightly covered so the rice bursts, becomes compact, and takes on consistency
6. Empty and serve on a plate in the form of a pyramid
7. This operation well executed takes one hour, or at most an hour and a half
