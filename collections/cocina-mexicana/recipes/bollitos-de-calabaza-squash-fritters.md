## Ingredients

- One cup plantain, ground and cooked
- One cup squash, well dried and cooked
- Two cups pasta and one cup remilled dough
- A little anise
- Lard
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of ground cooked plantain with one cup of well-dried cooked squash
2. To two cups of pasta add one cup of remilled dough
3. Unite the ingredients
4. Add a little sprinkled anise
5. Form the fritters
6. Fry them in lard
7. When serving, dust them with powdered sugar
