## Ingredients

- Two fat chickens
- Mutton loin, equal amount to chicken meat
- Truffle criadillas (earth truffles)
- Ham
- Lard
- Nata (cream) of milk, a little
- Four raw egg yolks
- Fine herbs
- Spices
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from two fat chickens without tearing it, keeping it whole if possible
2. Mince the chicken meat with an equal amount of mutton loin, earth truffles, ham, and lard
3. Incorporate all the minced meat together
4. Add a little cream of milk, four raw egg yolks, fine herbs, spices, salt, and pepper to the mixture
5. If the chicken skin came off whole, fill it with the prepared mixture; if divided, wrap the mixture in the skin pieces
6. Tie it appropriately and cook over white coals (brasa blanca)
7. If serving as an entrance course, add a good bound essence to the cooking liquid
8. If serving as an intermediate course, season the coals more strongly and serve the mortadella cold at the table
