## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the milk well with a little cinnamon, then add sugar to sweeten it.
2. Measure the milk with a cup measure and for each cup of milk use three egg yolks, beaten well.
3. Strain through a sieve so no membrane from the yolks remains.
4. Place over steam in a pot that is boiling, cover with a small griddle with hot coals on top.
5. When it is well set, insert a straw, and when it comes out clean, it is well cooked.
