## Ingredients

- half pound of ground sugar
- four quarters of a quart of cream
- lemon zest
- orange flower water
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add half pound of ground sugar to four quarters of a quart of cream with lemon zest and orange flower water
2. Bring to a boil and let it reduce by half
3. Bind afterwards with some egg yolks without boiling, curdle over embers, and drain in a basket or small strainer with thin cloth
4. Serve with sugar
