## Ingredients

- Thirty crabs
- Twelve almonds
- Half a pound of beef pulp
- A piece of ham
- One onion
- Carrots
- Large turnips
- Flour
- Butter
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Basil
- Parsley
- Onions
- Bread crusts
- Mushrooms (optional)
- Earth truffles (optional)
- Fish broth (if made without meat)

## Directions

1. Wash thirty crabs in several waters and cook them in plain water
2. Clean them of their shells, which are set aside separately to be pounded and mixed with twelve almonds and the meat of the same crabs
3. Slice half a pound of beef pulp and a piece of ham, doing the same with one onion, some carrots and large turnips
4. Place this over the fire in a pot and when thick, add some flour and butter
5. Turn it several times, stirring continuously and add broth, salt, pepper, clove, basil, parsley, onions and bread crusts, being able to add also mushrooms and earth truffles
6. Let the substance cook slowly and remove the beef, which will be shredded with what was pounded from the crabs, and strain or pass through a sieve
7. If made without meat, substitute butter for the beef and ham, and moisten with fish broth
