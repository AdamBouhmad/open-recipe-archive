## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Gizzards
- Liver
- Lard
- Ham pieces
- Onion
- Garlic
- Red wine
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Pluck and open the woodcock and gather the entrails, gizzards and liver
2. Heat lard in a casserole and add the woodcock with ham pieces, plenty of finely chopped onion, and a little chopped garlic
3. After the entrails, gizzards and liver are washed and ground, fry them and add to the casserole with a little red wine, clear broth, cinnamon, pepper and a little thyme
