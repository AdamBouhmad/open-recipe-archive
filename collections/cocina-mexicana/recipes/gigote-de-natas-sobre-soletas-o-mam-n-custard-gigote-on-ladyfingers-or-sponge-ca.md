## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- ladyfingers or sponge cake (soletas o mamón)
- custard (natillas compuestas)
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken breasts without salt
2. Chop coarsely and put to boil in milk with sugar and cinnamon
3. When it has thickened somewhat, place on a platter over ladyfingers or sponge cake
4. Cover with a layer of prepared custard
5. Fill the platter in this same order, with the final layer being custard
6. Cover with a griddle with fire to make it set
7. Serve with cinnamon on top
