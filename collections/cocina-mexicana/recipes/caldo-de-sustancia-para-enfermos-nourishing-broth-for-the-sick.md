## Ingredients

- pieces of lamb neck
- one lamb head
- four lamb feet, well cleaned
- one hen
- a handful of chickpeas
- fine salt
- one head of garlic
- one head of onion
- one sprig of mint
- rice

## Directions

1. The day before making the broth, soak pieces of lamb neck, one head, four lamb feet well cleaned, one hen, and a handful of chickpeas
2. Put them in a pot with water on the fire with a little fine salt
3. Remove all the foam that forms
4. When it no longer produces foam, add one whole head of garlic and one whole head of onion, with a sprig of mint
5. Then add the rice and serve the broth without any other addition
