## Ingredients

- espaldilla (shoulder)
- jamón en tiras (sliced ham)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta (pepper)
- hilo (string)
- tajadas de jamón (slices of ham)
- chirivías (parsnips)
- cebollas (onions)
- clavos de especia (cloves)
- hojas de laurel (bay leaves)
- tomillo (thyme)
- huesos (bones)
- caldo o agua (broth or water)
- gelatina (gelatin)

## Directions

1. Debone the shoulder up to the elbow
2. Lard with thick slices of seasoned ham
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, round it and tie with string so it does not fall apart during cooking
4. Place in a braising vessel with slices of ham, some parsnips, onions (one studded with two cloves), two bay leaves, a bit of thyme, the bones removed from the shoulder with others if available, and sufficient broth or water
5. Season with salt and cook slowly for three and a half hours
6. When ready to serve, drain the shoulder, untie it and remove the string
7. Add gelatin and serve on a plate
