## Ingredients

- hare
- wine
- cinnamon powder
- small zucchini
- oil
- salt
- cooked potatoes
- cooked cauliflower
- onion
- capers
- tornachiles
- pepper
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Roast the hare as described in the previous article and chop it
2. Add a glass of wine and a little cinnamon powder
3. Place on the fire, stirring so it does not stick; after a couple of boils, remove from heat and let rest
4. Fry slices of small zucchini in oil with a pinch of salt; remove without falling apart
5. Fry slices of cooked potatoes in the same oil, then cooked cauliflower stems
6. Make a bed of zucchini slices on a platter
7. Drain the wine from the hare and mix with finely chopped onion, capers and finely chopped tornachiles
8. Dust with a little salt and pepper
9. Place another bed of the hare mixture on the zucchini
10. Form another bed of fried potatoes on top, then another of hare, then another of cauliflower sprinkled with salt and pepper
11. Place the remaining hare on top
12. Dust with dried oregano and bathe with a little sauce of oil, vinegar and salt
13. Top with raisins and chopped almonds, olives and strips of tornachiles
