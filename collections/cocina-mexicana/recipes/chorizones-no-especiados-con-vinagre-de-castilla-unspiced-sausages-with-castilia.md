## Ingredients

- 4 pork loins, finely chopped
- 12 ounces dried red chile
- 4 or 5 heads of garlic
- Salt to taste
- Castilian vinegar to taste
- A little water
- Cured or dried cow intestines

## Directions

1. Grind twelve ounces of dried red chile without toasting and four or five heads of garlic
2. Incorporate everything together, seasoning with necessary salt and Castilian vinegar to taste
3. Add a little water and mix
4. Repeat the mixing for three consecutive days
5. On the third day, fill cleaned cured or dried cow intestines with the mixture
6. Prick the intestines to release air and tie them at convenient distances according to the desired size of the sausages
