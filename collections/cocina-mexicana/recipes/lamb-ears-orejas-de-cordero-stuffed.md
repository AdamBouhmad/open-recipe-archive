## Ingredients

- Filling (see relleno)
- Beef broth
- Breadcrumbs
- Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley, chopped and fried

## Directions

1. Bleed the ears in water and singe them; dry them afterwards and singe over the flame; cook in white broth; drain and stuff with some cooked forcemeat (see filling); soak in good thick meat broth, and roll in breadcrumbs; coat on all sides with eggs beaten with salt and coarse pepper, and roll again in bread; fry until golden, then remove and drain on clean cloth; serve with chopped and fried parsley.
