## Ingredients

- seven cuartillos milk
- ground rice
- four ounces ground almonds
- six sweet potatoes of regular size
- two large mameys
- chirimoya or cooked ground guava

## Directions

1. Boil the milk a little with ground rice
2. When cool mix in the ground almonds and return to fire to boil a little more
3. Cool a second time and mix in six sweet potatoes of regular size that are ground and without fibers
4. Put over fire where it cooks until it begins to thicken
5. Mix in two large ground mameys and continue on fire until it reaches the consistency of cajeta or pulls away from the pot
6. Empty into a serving dish
7. The mamey may be replaced with chirimoya or cooked and ground guava
