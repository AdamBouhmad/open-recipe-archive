## Ingredients

- Pagel fish
- White wine
- Butter
- Carrots
- Onion slices
- A mixed small bundle of herbs
- Garlic
- Clove of spice
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. Gut the fish and remove the gills.
2. After washing them, add them to a watered or light broth made with white wine, butter, carrots, sliced onions, a mixed small bundle, garlic and clove of spice, letting everything boil a little.
3. When this broth is ready, put the pageles to cook in it.
4. While cooking, arrange them on a plate and pour caper sauce over them.
