## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic
- Onion
- Tomato
- Parsley
- Pork broth or cooking stock
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Flour
- Zucchini, raw
- Butter

## Directions

1. Fry in lard garlic, onion, tomato, and parsley, all chopped
2. Add broth in which pork meat has been cooked, or from the pot before spices have been added, with pepper, cinnamon, and clove, all ground, and a little flour browned in the lard
3. In a casserole place a layer of slices of raw zucchini, and another of the fried mixture, until the casserole is filled, which with another little broth will be placed over two fires to cook
4. Move the casserole from time to time so the zucchini do not stick, and when cooked and somewhat dry, remove from heat and pour melted butter on top
