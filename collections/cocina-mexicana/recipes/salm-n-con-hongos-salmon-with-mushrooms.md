## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Red wine
- Broth
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt
- Spices
- Nutmeg, grated
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon in equal parts red wine and broth
2. Add chopped mushrooms, garlic and parsley, salt, spices and grated nutmeg
3. Once well cooked, remove the salmon from the cooking vessel
4. To the liquid remaining in the pot, add a little butter kneaded with flour
5. Let it cook after straining, and seasoning to taste, serve poured over the salmon
