## Ingredients

- twenty onions of the same size
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- one quarter pint of water
- a piece of sugar the size of half an egg
- an equal amount of butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Select twenty onions of the same size and clean them carefully so that the tails and heads are not cut too close to the root
2. Butter the bottom of a pan and place the onions with the head side down so that the tail part is facing up
3. Add a little salt and coarse pepper, one quarter pint of water, a piece of sugar the size of half an egg, and an equal amount of butter
4. Place a wheel of buttered paper on top
5. Leave on the stove at a fairly strong fire until the stock reduces to half, then move to another stove with gentle fire
6. At the moment of serving, allow them to cool and arrange on a plate with Spanish sauce
