## Ingredients

- Bergamota pears
- sugar
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Peel the bergamota pears from top to bottom, cutting the tip of the stems, and place them in fresh water
2. Cook them until they feel soft to the touch, then remove them with a skimmer and place them in fresh water again
3. Let them drain well
4. For every fifty pears, add one pound of sugar dissolved in four quartillos of water
5. Once the sugar is dissolved, place the pears in it and let them soak for two hours
6. Remove them and arrange them on hurdles or branches with the stems facing up
7. Place them in an oven at moderate heat for one night, like the heat remaining after bread is removed
8. The next day, bathe the pears again in the sugar water and repeat the oven operation in the same way
9. Continue this procedure for four days, with the last time leaving them in the oven until they are completely dry
10. Store them in dry places for as long as desired
