## Ingredients

- nopal paddles, cleaned
- salt
- tomatoes
- garlic cloves, cleaned and roasted
- cilantro, toasted
- lard
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned nopal paddles lengthwise to the width of a finger and cook them in a copper pot with sufficient salt.
2. Once cooked, cook some tomatoes and grind them together with roasted garlic cloves and a little toasted cilantro.
3. Place a pan with lard over the fire and fry the nopal paddles there.
4. Add the ground tomato mixture with the garlic and cilantro.
5. Once everything is well fried, remove from heat, add raw oil, and serve the nopales.
