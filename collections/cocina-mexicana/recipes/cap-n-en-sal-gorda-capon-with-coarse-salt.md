## Ingredients

- capon
- butter
- broth
- beef juice
- coarse salt

## Directions

1. Prepare as the preceding recipe
2. Turn several times in the pan with butter and place to cook in broth
3. When well-cooked, place on a platter, drizzle with beef juice, and sprinkle the breast with coarse salt grains
