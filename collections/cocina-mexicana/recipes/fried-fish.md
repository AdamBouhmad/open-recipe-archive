## Ingredients

- Lard
- Onions
- Garlic
- Clove
- Fine pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- White bread
- Vinegar
- Water
- Fish
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry onions and garlic, chopped finely, in lard with clove, fine pepper and Tabasco pepper
2. Separately fry a piece of white bread soaked in vinegar, grind it and dissolve it in water with plenty of vinegar
3. Join everything and add the fish cut into pieces
4. Once it has boiled, remove from heat, and if the fish is not salted, season with the necessary salt
