## Ingredients

- Ancho chile (or milder if unavailable)
- Cilantro seeds
- Nutmeg
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Salt
- Aged cheese
- Onion
- Parsley
- Lard
- Tortillas

## Directions

1. Prepare ancho chile, and if not available, use a milder one, along with cilantro seeds, a piece of nutmeg, clove, cinnamon, pepper, a little thyme, and the necessary salt; grind all of this and fry it together.
2. The sauce should be thick; add water when it is almost ready.
3. Grind aged cheese and chop plenty of onion in thin rounds and parsley.
4. Put a frying pan with lard on the heat and vigorously fry the tortillas, which should be neither thick nor thin.
5. Remove them from the lard and dip them in the prepared sauce, which should be kept hot.
6. Once they are well soaked, remove them and place them on a platter.
