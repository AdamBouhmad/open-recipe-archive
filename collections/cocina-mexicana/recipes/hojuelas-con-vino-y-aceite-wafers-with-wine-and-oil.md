## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- Four eggs
- Four ounces of butter
- The juice of one lime
- A little water of settled tequesquite
- One piece of yeast the size of an egg
- Half a small cup of white wine
- A little oil

## Directions

1. Knead everything together and work the dough well
2. Extend the hojuelas with the roller
3. Cut them
4. Fry them
5. Serve with syrup
