## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Cumin
- Pot broth

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes by putting them in boiling water for a while so they release their skin easily
2. Make them into a paste
3. Dissolve this paste in pot broth and add some ground cumin
4. If the tomato has been preserved dry, it will be necessary to put it in water and place the dish over a pot that is on the fire so that it heats with the steam, being careful to shake it from time to time so that it dissolves perfectly, and in this state it can be served
