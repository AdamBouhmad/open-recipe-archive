## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Two raw egg yolks
- A little butter or buttermilk
- Sufficient salt
- Grated zest of two or three lemons
- Powdered sugar and cinnamon to taste
- Generous wine, as much as needed to make the dough soft

## Directions

1. Mix the two raw egg yolks with the flour, butter or buttermilk, salt, grated lemon zest, powdered sugar and cinnamon, and wine
2. Knead very well
3. Extend with a rolling pin into sheets
4. Form the pastry
