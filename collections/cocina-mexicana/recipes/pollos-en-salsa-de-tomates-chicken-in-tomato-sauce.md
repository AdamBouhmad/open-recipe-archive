## Ingredients

- burned lard
- chopped garlic cloves
- cooked and ground tomatoes
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- chicken
- chicken broth
- sliced chorizo
- raisins
- almonds
- cooked and chopped beet
- parsley
- fried and ground bread
- white wine

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic cloves in burned lard
2. Add cooked and ground tomatoes with cloves, pepper, and cinnamon
3. Place the chicken with its broth in this mixture
4. Add sliced chorizo, raisins, almonds, cooked and chopped beet with parsley, fried and ground bread, and white wine
5. Let simmer, taking care that the sauce does not become too thin
