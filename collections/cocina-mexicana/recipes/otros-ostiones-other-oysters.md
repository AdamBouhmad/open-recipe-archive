## Ingredients

- oil
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- ground tomato
- oysters
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- toasted flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry garlic, onion, parsley and ground tomato in oil
2. Once well fried, add the oysters and a pinch of cinnamon, clove and pepper
3. Add saffron, a little toasted flour, parsley and vinegar
