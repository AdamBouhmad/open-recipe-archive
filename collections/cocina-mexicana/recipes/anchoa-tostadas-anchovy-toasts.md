## Ingredients

- bread slices
- oil for frying
- fine oil
- vinegar
- black pepper
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut bread slices with precision to the size and thickness of a finger
2. Fry them in oil and arrange on a plate
3. Make a sauce with fine oil, vinegar, black pepper, parsley and onion, all chopped
4. Pour this sauce over the toasts
5. Arrange with the anchovies to adorn and partially cover the roasts
