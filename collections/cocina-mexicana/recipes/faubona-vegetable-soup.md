## Ingredients

- Same vegetables as Juliana soup
- Sorrel or lamb's lettuce
- Lettuce
- Root vegetables
- Leeks
- Herbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the vegetables into the form of dice
2. Mash or pound the sorrel or lamb's lettuce and lettuce
3. Fry the roots, leeks, and herbs in butter
4. Moisten them afterward with broth and serve
