## Ingredients

- strained broth
- pot broth
- parsley
- poultry livers
- anchovies
- capers
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. In a pot, combine a little strained broth with the same amount of pot broth, a little chopped parsley, two cooked poultry livers, an anchovy and capers, everything chopped very finely
2. Add about half an egg's worth of good butter, salt and pepper
3. Let the sauce bind on the fire and use as needed
