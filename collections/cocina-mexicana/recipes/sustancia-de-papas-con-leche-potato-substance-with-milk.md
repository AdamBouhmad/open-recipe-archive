## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 2 ounces fresh butter
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes under hot ashes, peel them and pass them through a strainer.
2. Place them in a saucepan with 2 ounces of fresh butter, pepper and salt.
3. Stir and moisten with milk until the substance reaches the proper consistency.
4. Bring to a boil for a moment without letting it stick.
5. Sugar can be added, omitting the pepper.
6. Serve as an intermediate course.
