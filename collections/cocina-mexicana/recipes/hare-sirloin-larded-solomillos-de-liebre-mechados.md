## Ingredients

- Hare
- thin ham
- butter

## Directions

1. Once the hare is emptied and cleaned, insert the knife along the length of the spine, from the shoulder blade to the leg, separating the sirloin with it.
2. Insert the fingers between the bone and the sirloin, separating it so that its thick end remains hanging from the leg.
3. Insert the knife along the edge of the sirloin side so that it meets the thumb without cutting the skin.
4. Then stretch the sirloin towards the person cutting it, and leaving the nervous skin, the sirloin is found separated and prepared at once, lightly removing the skin if any remains.
5. In this state, lard it with thin or fine ham, giving it the shape desired, whether long, round or any other; place some slices of ham in a saucepan, some slices of ham and onion, and a little thyme and laurel.
6. Arrange the sirloin spread with butter and let it cook over two fires.
