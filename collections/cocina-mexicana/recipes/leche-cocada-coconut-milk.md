## Ingredients

- one pound of clarified sugar
- one ground coconut
- two quarts of milk
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Heat the clarified sugar to a high point
2. Add the ground coconut and incorporate without removing from heat
3. When it reaches the consistency of bocadillo, add two quarts of milk and continue boiling until it pulls from the pan
4. Remove from heat and while it cools slightly, beat twelve egg yolks
5. Add them near the fire and return to low heat, stirring well until the egg sets
6. Place a griddle with coals on top to brown
