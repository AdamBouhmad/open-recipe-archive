## Ingredients

- one pound of good sifted flour
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Place one pound of good sifted flour on a table, mix with water and salt, and soften with all the butter necessary to work it
2. Once done, leave it covered for a while
3. Then begin extending it in very thin sheets coated with butter
