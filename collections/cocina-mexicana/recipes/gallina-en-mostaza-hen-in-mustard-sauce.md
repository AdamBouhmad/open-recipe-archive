## Ingredients

- hen
- mustard, well washed
- vinegar
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- sugar
- saffron
- egg yolks, beaten

## Directions

1. Grind one cup of very well washed mustard until it is black, diluting it from the metate with vinegar and adding clove and cinnamon
2. Put it on the fire with lard, sugar and saffron
3. When it has boiled a little, add the hen which has been cooked and browned in lard
4. Remove from heat and add a little oil, ham slices, cooked eggs, chiles, olives and capers
