## Ingredients

- bird
- lard
- butter or ham
- garlic
- cumin
- vinegar
- pepper
- oil
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the bird in pieces in raw state, and place in a pot with lard, butter or ham, letting them brown
2. Cook apart some cumin and garlic crushed together with vinegar and add pepper, oil and nutmeg
3. Pour this over the roasted birds
