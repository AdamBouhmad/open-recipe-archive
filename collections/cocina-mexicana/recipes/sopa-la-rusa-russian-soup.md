## Ingredients

- equal quantities of ham, fat, and beef sirloin and beef loin
- Madeira wine
- fresh butter
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- small carrots
- small onions
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the ham, fat, beef sirloin, and beef loin into small equal square pieces
2. Put everything in a pot with Madeira wine, fresh butter, nutmeg, salt and pepper
3. Let it cook until the wine is exhausted and the meat is reduced to gelatin
4. Moisten this preparation with pot broth, or better with concentrated broth
5. Let it cook for at least four hours
6. Prepare separately small carrots and small onions in equal quantity, frying them in butter
7. Let them finish cooking in substantial broth
8. When the soup is cooked, arrange the vegetables on top and add a little chopped parsley
