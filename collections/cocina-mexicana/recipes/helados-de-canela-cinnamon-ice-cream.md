## Ingredients

- three-quarters of an ounce of select cinnamon
- a little sugar
- thirty ounces of common milk
- ten ounces of custard
- fifteen ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Grind three-quarters of an ounce of select cinnamon in a marble mortar with a little sugar
2. Bring to boil with thirty ounces of common milk, ten ounces of custard and fifteen ounces of sugar
3. When this begins to thicken, strain through a sieve or a piping bag
4. Allow to cool and freeze
