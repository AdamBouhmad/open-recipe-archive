## Ingredients

- Squabs (young pigeons)
- Scrambled eggs
- Fried toast
- Ham pieces
- Longaniza pieces
- Chorizos
- Cooked egg
- Chopped parsley
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Pickled peppers
- Beaten eggs
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make it exactly the same as the scrambled egg cake, adding over the scrambled eggs cooked squabs cut into quarters
