## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges
- Salt
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar), freshly clarified
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous ones, make their small crown and fill with salt
2. Pass through from side to side with a punch tool, giving them four punctures
3. Blanch for nine days, changing water daily and gently squeezing each time
4. Cook and remove bitterness by placing them in tepid waters
5. Once without bitterness, in freshly clarified sugar syrup, boil very slowly over the course of three days, in the manner previously described
6. On the last day, add orange blossom water and bring the honey to mirror point
