## Ingredients

- betabeles
- zanahorias
- chiles anchos
- ajo
- comino
- clavo
- canela
- sal
- orégano
- aceite
- vinagre
- aguacate
- cebollas cocidas
- aceitunas
- chilitos
- pasas
- almendras
- ajonjolí tostado

## Directions

1. Cook the beets and carrots, then mince finely
2. Marinate with deveined and ground ancho chiles, little garlic, cumin and clove; but considerable cinnamon
3. Add salt and oregano and incorporate well
4. Leave in the adobo, and the next day remove without pressing
5. Add sufficient oil, vinegar, avocado, cooked onions separated into leaves, olives, small chiles, raisins, almonds and toasted sesame
