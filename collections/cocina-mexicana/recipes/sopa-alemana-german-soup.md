## Ingredients

- one quarter liter or a little more of broth
- three egg yolks
- one and a half tablespoons of potato starch
- one tablespoon of grated cheese
- black pepper
- one egg
- cream or double cream

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil in a saucepan
2. In a bowl, prepare three egg yolks with one and a half tablespoons of potato starch, one tablespoon of grated cheese, and a little black pepper
3. Mix everything and add one egg and a little cream or double cream
4. The preparation should be sufficiently liquid to pass through a skimmer
5. Pass it quickly over the boiling broth
6. It takes only five minutes to cook in it
7. Pour the soup into the tureen and serve grated cheese separately at the same time
