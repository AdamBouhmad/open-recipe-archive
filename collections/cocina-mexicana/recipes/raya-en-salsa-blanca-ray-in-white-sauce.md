## Ingredients

- Ray fish
- Light broth
- Vinegar
- White sauce
- Capers
- Pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. After removing the gall and washing it very well, put it to cook in light broth to which a glass of vinegar is added
2. Once cooked remove the slime, membrane or skin from both sides and clean it well
3. Arrange it on a plate, cover it with white sauce and decorate the top with capers and pickled gherkins cut in the shape of dice
