## Ingredients

- Eight quartillos of milk
- Four pounds of sugar
- One ounce of starch

## Directions

1. Dissolve four pounds of sugar and one ounce of starch in eight quartillos of milk
2. Place over fire, stirring constantly, until you can see the bottom of the pan
3. When it reaches the proper consistency, pour it into a platter or shells
