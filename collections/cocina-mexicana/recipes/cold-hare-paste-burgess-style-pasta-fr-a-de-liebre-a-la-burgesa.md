## Ingredients

- Hare
- thick ham strips
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- brandy
- hare blood

## Directions

1. Empty the hare and save the blood.
2. Separate its limbs and lard them on all sides with thick strips of ham, rolled in salt, pepper, parsley, onion and minced garlic.
3. Then put it in a small pot with a glass of brandy and a piece of butter.
4. Put it to cook over a gentle fire, and when all the sauce has been consumed, add the blood, which will be left to heat without boiling.
5. Arrange the hare in the dish in which it is to be taken to the table, and serve everything together so that it appears only one cold intermediate piece.
