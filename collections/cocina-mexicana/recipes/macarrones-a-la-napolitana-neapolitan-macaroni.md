## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- Parma cheese or best available cheese
- braised broth
- butter, one pound for every two pounds of macaroni

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni in water with salt only
2. Arrange in a tureen by successively placing a layer of macaroni and another of Parma cheese or the best available
3. Pour braised broth over them immediately
4. Add melted butter on the last layer, in the proportion of one pound for every two pounds of macaroni
