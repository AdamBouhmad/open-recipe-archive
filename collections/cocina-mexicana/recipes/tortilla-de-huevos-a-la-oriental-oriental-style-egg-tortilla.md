## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- water
- parsley (optional)
- onion (optional)
- butter

## Directions

1. Break and pour the eggs into a pot or any other vessel
2. Season with ground salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Add a little water, and if desired, a little finely minced parsley and onion
4. Melt excellent butter in a pan over high heat
5. Pour the eggs there and flip the tortilla from one side to the other until cooked, taking care that it does not stick, which is achieved by moving the pan from side to side
