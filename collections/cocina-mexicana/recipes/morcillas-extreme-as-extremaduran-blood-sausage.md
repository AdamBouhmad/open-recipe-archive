## Ingredients

- blood from pork, with serum removed
- melted lard
- chopped onion
- tomatoes
- yerba buena (mint)
- thyme
- oregano
- ground spices
- one cup of milk
- handful of crushed rice
- fat from the tripe
- salt
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- bay leaf water

## Directions

1. Remove the serum from the pork blood and add melted lard, chopped onion, tomatoes, yerba buena, thyme, oregano, and ground spices
2. Add one cup of milk and a handful of crushed rice
3. Add the fat from the tripe, salt, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and walnuts
4. Beat everything so it becomes fluffy
5. Fill the intestines very loosely because they will expand later
6. Parboil in bay leaf water
7. Do not prick them so the lard does not escape
