## Ingredients

- rabbit
- straw
- hot water
- tomatoes
- garlic
- onion, chopped
- oregano
- spices, ground
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the rabbit with straw and wash with hot water
2. Cook tomatoes, break them down and fry with garlic, chopped onion, considerable oregano and ground spices
3. Before removing from heat add oil
