## Ingredients

- Garlic cloves
- Butter
- Cold bread
- Almond shells
- Roasted tomato
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- White wine

## Directions

1. Slice garlic cloves and brown in butter with a piece of cold bread and almond shells
2. Grind everything with a little roasted and peeled tomato
3. Dissolve in a little water seasoned with salt and pepper
4. When thickened, remove from heat
5. When serving, add a little white wine
