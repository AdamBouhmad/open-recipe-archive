## Ingredients

- beef tongues
- lard
- thin onion slices
- garlic
- tomatoes
- green chile strips
- salt
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- capers
- Málaga or Parras wine

## Directions

1. In a casserole with lard over heat, add thin onion slices, minced garlic and tomatoes, and green chile strips with salt, heating and frying without over-browning
2. In another lard-coated casserole, make a layer of the fried mixture, then a layer of thin slices of cooked and skinned tongue
3. Dust with cloves, pepper and cinnamon, and decorate with raisins, almonds, olives and capers
4. Continue alternating layers until the casserole is full
5. Carefully pour Málaga or Parras wine over the top so all layers are well soaked
6. Place in the oven or over two fires until the sauce is consumed, being careful not to burn
