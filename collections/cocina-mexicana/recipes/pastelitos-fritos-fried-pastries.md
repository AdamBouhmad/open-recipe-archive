## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- starch flour
- lard
- pasta filling of choice
- egg threads
- very well clarified syrup
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and add a powder of starch flour, mix this well
2. Dip the mold in this, and thus coated introduce it into a small pot with sufficient lard so that the pastry fries
3. After frying, fill it with the pasta desired, and on top place egg threads, which are made by pouring through a skimmer egg yolks into very well clarified syrup that is boiling
4. The skimmer should have small holes, and the syrup should not be at very high point and should have orange flower water
