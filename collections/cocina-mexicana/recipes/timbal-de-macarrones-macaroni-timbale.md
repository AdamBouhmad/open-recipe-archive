## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one egg
- half a pocillo of salted water
- four ounces of lard
- five ounces of butter
- half an ounce of cinnamon
- powdered sugar
- one young hen
- two large handfuls of chopped onion
- cloves, pepper, and salt as needed
- one pound of hollow macaroni
- three handfuls of grated Flemish cheese
- hard biscuit for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a dough with the flour, egg, salted water, lard, and butter, sweetened with powdered sugar
2. Fry a young tender hen and cut it into small pieces
3. Mix the hen with two large handfuls of chopped onion and sufficient cloves, pepper, and salt
4. Cook one pound of hollow macaroni in salted water, cut into small pieces, and drain through a sieve
5. Mix the macaroni with the hen and onion mixture
6. Remove from heat and mix in three handfuls of grated Flemish cheese
7. Butter a pan, dust it with hard biscuit, and line it with the dough
8. Add the filling and cover with the dough spread with a rolling pin
9. Put in the oven and it is done when it pulls away cleanly from the pan
