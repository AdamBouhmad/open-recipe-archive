## Ingredients

- one quart of raw milk
- two boxes of cold medium ladyfingers
- one or two ounces of butter
- sugar to taste
- almonds
- citron
- pine nuts
- cinnamon
- twenty egg yolks
- half with whites and half without whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve two boxes of cold medium ladyfingers in one quart of raw milk with one or two ounces of butter, sugar to taste, almonds, citron, pine nuts and cinnamon
2. Cook everything and let cool
3. Add twenty egg yolks, half with whites and half without whites, beaten well
4. Spread the pan with butter and put to curdle between two fires
5. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
