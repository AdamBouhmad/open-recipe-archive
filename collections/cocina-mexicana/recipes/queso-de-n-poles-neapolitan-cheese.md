## Ingredients

- Four cuartillos of milk
- Two ounces of ground almond
- Half a pound of white sugar
- Sixteen egg whites
- Butter

## Directions

1. Heat the milk and then stir in the almond and sugar
2. Put to boil until it thickens like a dessert
3. When it reaches the proper point, pour it out to cool well
4. Beat sixteen egg whites and mix well with the milk
5. Butter the mold and cook like jerícalla in water with heat from above
