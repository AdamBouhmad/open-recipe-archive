## Ingredients

- capon
- five eggs
- half pound sugar
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- hard biscuit or hard biscuit for cakes, ground
- raisins
- almonds
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a dough or stuffing with five eggs, half pound of sugar, pepper, clove, cinnamon, and saffron, mixed with ground hard biscuit or hardtack, raisins and almonds.
2. Incorporate everything and fry in lard where it is seasoned.
3. Stuff the capons with this mixture and cook them trussed.
4. Place in a casserole greased with lard and put in the oven.
