## Ingredients

- Codfish
- Onions
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice onions crosswise so they form rings, fry in butter until golden, and removing them, let the same butter also brown, which strained is poured over the fried onions, with salt, pepper and a regular amount of vinegar.
2. Cook the codfish as in the previous recipe, drain, dry and arrange on a plate.
3. Pour the browned onion and butter mixture on top and serve.
