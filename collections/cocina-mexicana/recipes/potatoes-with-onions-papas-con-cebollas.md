## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- potatoes, half-cooked
- pot or meatless broth

## Directions

1. Fry onion in butter.
2. Cut potatoes at half-cooked stage and finish cooking in the fried onion.
3. Moisten with pot or meatless broth.
4. Cook very slowly.
