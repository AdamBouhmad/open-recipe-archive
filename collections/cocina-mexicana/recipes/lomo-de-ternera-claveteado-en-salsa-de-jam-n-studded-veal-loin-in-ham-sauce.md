## Ingredients

- veal loin
- salt
- fine pepper of Tabasco
- vinegar or juice of sour orange
- whole Tabasco pepper
- raisins
- almonds
- garlic
- ham
- cotton cloth
- water
- bay leaf
- lean ham, ground
- roasted and ground tomato
- fried slices of onion, tomato and garlic
- red wine
- vinegar
- little oil

## Directions

1. Pound the loin, make small cuts with the tip of the knife and add fine salt and ground Tabasco pepper
2. Sprinkle with vinegar or sour orange juice
3. Stud it with whole Tabasco pepper, raisins, almonds, garlic and ham, and let it marinate in this manner for two days
4. Wrap it afterwards in cotton cloth, tie it and place it to cook in water with salt, vinegar and bay leaf
5. The following day, untie and unwrap it, and serve it with a sauce made from ground lean ham, roasted and ground tomato, fried slices of onion, tomato and garlic
6. Fry everything together and add red wine, vinegar and a little oil
