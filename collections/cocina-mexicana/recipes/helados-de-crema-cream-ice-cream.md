## Ingredients

- 1.5 quartillos of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 12 ounces of sugar
- lemon peel scrapings

## Directions

1. Place to boil a quartillo and a half of milk and eight ounces of cream with twelve ounces of sugar and the scrapings of a lemon peel, stirring continuously until it begins to thicken
2. Remove it immediately from the fire, and pass it through a cloth over a glass, where it is left to cool and then poured into the sorbetera so that it freezes
