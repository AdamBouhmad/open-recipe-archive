## Ingredients

- two quartillos of fresh milk
- six ounces sugar
- a little lemon rind
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix two quartillos of fresh milk with six ounces of sugar, a little lemon rind and a little cinnamon
2. Let steep in infusion for half an hour
3. Strain through a cloth
4. Freeze in the ordinary manner
5. Alternatively, the milk may be cooked first before adding the ingredients
