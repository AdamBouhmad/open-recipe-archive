## Ingredients

- white sugar
- lemon
- sweet almond oil

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from very white sugar and clarify it with lemon
2. Let it boil without stirring or inserting a spoon until it reaches the hard-crack stage when a drop is placed in cold water
3. Oil a metate with sweet almond oil and pour the syrup carefully with a silver spoon so it does not granulate
4. Cut the caramels and wrap them in powdered sugar
