## Ingredients

- onions
- tomatoes
- garlic
- raisins
- almonds
- ham
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper, ground
- salt
- dried oregano
- lard
- broth
- oil
- vinegar
- olives
- tornachilos
- capers
- caper berries

## Directions

1. Cut onions into long slices.
2. Chop tomatoes, garlic, raisins, almonds, and ham.
3. Add ground clove, cinnamon, pepper, a little salt, and dried oregano.
4. Coat a cazuela with lard and make a layer of this mixture and another of chicken quarters, alternating layers until ending with a layer of mixture.
5. Place the cazuela over two very gentle fires, and as it dries, gradually add meat broth, adding lard occasionally if needed, until the chickens are cooked.
6. Once cooked, let them dry, and when serving add plenty of oil and vinegar, and garnish with olives, tornachilos, capers, and caper berries.
