## Ingredients

- Cooked artichokes
- Garlic cloves, peeled
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Tender cooked peas
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the center leaves forming the spiny part from the cooked artichokes.
2. Place a pot on the fire with oil and fry a large quantity of peeled garlic cloves until they turn golden.
3. Remove the garlic and grind it with black pepper, salt, and an equal portion of tender well-cooked peas.
4. Fill the artichokes with this mixture and replace the leaves that were removed.
5. Bathe them with the oil in which the garlic was fried and roll them in breadcrumbs.
6. Place on the fire until they turn golden brown.
7. When serving, drizzle with oil and vinegar with salt.
