## Ingredients

- 5 cuartillos milk
- 4 pounds sugar
- 5 ounces starch
- 6 almonds
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Combine five cuartillos of milk, four pounds of sugar, five ounces of starch, six almonds and orange blossom water.
