## Ingredients

- veal loin jerky
- wide chiles, soaked
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Spread out the jerky cut from the veal loin, and coat it with adobo made with soaked wide chiles and ground with garlic, pepper, clove and cinnamon, the ground mixture seasoned with salt, and diluted with vinegar
