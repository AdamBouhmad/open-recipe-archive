## Ingredients

- Gobies (cleaned, gutted, and dried)
- Butter
- Parsley
- Small onions
- Mushrooms
- One or two shallots
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- One glass of red wine

## Directions

1. Place butter with finely chopped parsley, small onions, mushrooms, one or two shallots, thyme, laurel, and basil at the bottom of the serving dish
2. Season with salt and black pepper
3. Arrange the cleaned gobies on top
4. Season the gobies with the same herbs and seasonings
5. Moisten with one glass of red wine
6. Cover the dish
7. Bring the fish to a boil over good heat until only very little sauce remains, which takes about a quarter hour for complete cooking
