## Ingredients

- trout
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the trout
2. Stuff the body with butter kneaded with fine herbs
3. Season to taste
4. Let marinate and then grill on the grill
5. Serve with French mustard sauce
