## Ingredients

- Fish
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Spanish oil
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pickled chiles
- Olives
- Capers
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. For six pounds of fish, grind twenty large tomatoes with plenty of ground garlic
2. Fry the garlic separately until it burns in Spanish oil and lard
3. Once done, add the ground tomatoes
4. When the boiling begins, place the fish slices in the pan after scaling and drying well with a cloth
5. Add ground salt and pepper, pickled chiles, olives, capers, and vinegar
6. Let the water released by the tomato reduce so that only the lard and oil remain
