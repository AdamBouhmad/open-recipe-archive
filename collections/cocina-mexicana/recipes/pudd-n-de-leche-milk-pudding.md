## Ingredients

- one teacup of hot milk
- three egg yolks
- sufficient sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. To one teacup of hot milk add three egg yolks for jericalla, sufficient sugar
2. Place to curdle in a small bowl of boiling water with a small griddle of hot coals on top
3. Pour into a large cup, sprinkle cinnamon on top and place in the oven
