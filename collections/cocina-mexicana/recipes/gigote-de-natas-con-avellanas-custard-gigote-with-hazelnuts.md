## Ingredients

- one roasted chicken breast
- half a pound of roasted and ground hazelnuts
- ladyfingers (marquesotes)
- custard (natillas)
- milk
- ground sugar
- candied fruit (grajea)

## Directions

1. Chop the roasted chicken breast and mix with the roasted and ground hazelnuts
2. Layer in alternating fashion: ladyfingers, then the chicken and hazelnut mixture, then custard
3. Sprinkle everything with milk and sprinkle ground sugar on top
4. Repeat the layers to the desired height
5. Set to curdle using two fires (top and bottom heat)
6. Top with candied fruit before serving
