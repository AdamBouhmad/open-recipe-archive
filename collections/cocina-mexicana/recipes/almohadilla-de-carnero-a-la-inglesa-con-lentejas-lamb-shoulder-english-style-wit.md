## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Lentils (one pound)
- Stock or broth
- Salt
- Mixed herbs bundle
- Bread crumbs
- Crustless bread slices

## Directions

1. Cut the shoulder along the ribs
2. Place to cook in stock with a little salt and a mixed herb bundle
3. Transfer to a casserole on the fire with half of the stock or broth produced by cooking one pound of lentils, which should not be too watery
4. Cover on one side with slices of bread crumbs toasted on the grill
5. Place the casserole in the oven for one hour
6. When ready to serve, add the other half of the lentil stock
