## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon of very dry, very white flour
- 1/2 pound of sugar
- A little salt
- A little infusion of orange blossom, vanilla, or other aromatic substance
- 2 quartillos of milk with cream

## Directions

1. Put in a pot the flour, sugar, salt, and aromatic infusion
2. Add 2 quartillos of boiling milk with cream
3. Leave it over the fire to reach the proper point
4. Pour into a concave plate as soon as it begins to boil
5. When cold, make a border or other garnish with the following composition: mix the same aroma used for the cream with a little sugar and 4 cooked egg yolks
6. Pass this mixture through a sieve
7. With this, arrange the fringe or ornament with which to border the plate for serving
