## Ingredients

- Large intestines of pig
- Mulato or pasilla chiles, deveined
- Pig blood
- Raw pig fat, chopped
- Salt
- A little sugar, ground
- Plenty of onions
- Walnuts
- Almonds
- Citron candy
- Pine nuts, cleaned
- Raisins
- A little ginger, ground

## Directions

1. Wash the large pig intestines and place in hot water
2. Toast mulato or pasilla chiles without letting them burn, devein them
3. When cool, break them well with your fingers and mix in the pig blood
4. Chop raw pig fat from the same pig and mix with sufficient salt into the blood
5. Grind a little sugar and mix in so it stands out slightly
6. Chop plenty of onions, walnuts, almonds, and citron
7. Clean pine nuts and raisins and mix these also in the blood
8. Grind a little ginger and mix everything well
9. Fill the intestines, tie the ends, and boil until cooked
