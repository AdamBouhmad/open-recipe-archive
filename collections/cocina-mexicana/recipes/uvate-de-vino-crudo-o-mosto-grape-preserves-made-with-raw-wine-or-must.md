## Ingredients

- ripe grapes
- best pears (optional)

## Directions

1. Express the juice from degrained grapes, pour it into a cauldron and let it boil gently, skimming and stirring it frequently so that it does not stick to the bottom and the uvate does not acquire a burnt taste
2. As the juice cooks and thickens, gradually reduce the fire, and when it is reduced to a third, pass it through a strainer, let it finish cooking, and then pour it into very clean jars
3. To give this preserve consistency and a particular taste, add the best pears that can be found, cut in quarters and mixed with it
