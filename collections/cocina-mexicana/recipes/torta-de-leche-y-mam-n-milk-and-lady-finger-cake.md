## Ingredients

- one cup milk
- fifty egg yolks
- lard
- lady fingers (mamón)
- cinnamon
- sugar
- paper

## Directions

1. Mix fifty egg yolks with milk and sweeten to taste
2. Grease a pan with lard and cover with paper
3. On the paper, place a layer of sliced lady fingers
4. Pour the milk mixture over so the lady fingers soak well
5. Dust with cinnamon powder
6. Fill the pan and place over two fires
7. Test doneness by inserting a straw; if it comes out clean, it is done
8. Dust with sugar and cinnamon
