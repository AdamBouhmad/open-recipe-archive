## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- twelve egg yolks, well beaten

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup and bring it to the soft-crack stage
2. To one pound of sugar add twelve well-beaten egg yolks, mix and beat as for nougat
3. Return to the heat to warm it, and when hot, remove from heat and dust with a biscuit
4. Return to the heat until it reaches the consistency of nougat
5. Add a little wine and place on a platter or serving dish, then serve
