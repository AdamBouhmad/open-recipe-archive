## Ingredients

- two onions
- some tomatoes
- a handful of green chiles
- a cucumber
- a head of garlic
- parsley
- celery
- bread
- salt and pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Chop the onions, tomatoes, green chiles, cucumber, garlic, parsley and celery very finely and place in a bowl
2. Add bread broken into pieces, forming double the volume of the other ingredients
3. Season everything with salt, pepper, oil and vinegar as if making a salad
4. Complete the gazpacho with more or less water to form the broth
5. Serve as a cold soup, eaten with a spoon
