## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Oregano
- Eggs
- Hardtack powder
- Oil
- Lard

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the fish into pieces
2. Remove the bones, salt it, and let it drain
3. Leave it for three or four hours
4. One hour before eating, place it in lemon juice with oregano
5. Beat eggs with hardtack powder without letting it set
6. Roll the fish in this sauce
7. Fry in oil and lard
