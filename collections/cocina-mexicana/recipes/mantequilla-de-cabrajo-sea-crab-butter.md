## Ingredients

- fine butter, amount equal to one egg
- sea crab eggs from the interior and under the tail

## Directions

1. Pound together an amount of fine butter equal to one egg with the eggs found in the interior and under the tail of a sea crab
2. Pass through a silk sieve
3. Remix the butter on a plate until it becomes a beautiful red color
4. Use as needed
