## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Thin ham slices
- Anchovies
- Truffles or ground tubers
- Rendered ham

## Directions

1. After larding the chickens with thin ham and well-seasoned anchovies, fill them with a fine hash of truffles or ground tubers and put them to cook in the roasting spit.
2. When almost cooked, drip rendered ham over them, taking care that they do not blacken, and serve with hot French mustard sauce (see).
