## Ingredients

- Coconut with almond
- Syrup
- Dry wine
- Sponge cake
- Egg yolks
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. First remove the skin that the coconut has attached to the almond
2. Grate the almond
3. Place in slightly clear syrup and bring to the heat to give it the proper point
4. Add sponge cake soaked in dry wine and beaten egg yolks
5. Leave a short while on the heat and stir well
6. Remove on plates and dust with cinnamon
