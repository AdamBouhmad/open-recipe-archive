## Ingredients

- roasted tomatillos
- cooked tomatoes
- very few cooked green chiles
- roasted garlic
- pineapple
- ham pieces
- chopped pineapple
- cooked pork loin
- water from cooking the pork
- salt
- cooked sausages
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- sugar
- ground pineapple

## Directions

1. Grind roasted tomatillos, cooked tomatoes, very few cooked green chiles, roasted garlic, and pineapple together
2. Fry all the ground mixture, adding pieces of ham and chopped pineapple
3. Place the cooked pork loin in the pot with the water in which it was cooked
4. Season with sufficient salt and bring to a boil several times
5. Add the cooked sausages, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and a small piece of sugar
6. Thicken the sauce with more ground pineapple
7. Serve garnished with small chiles and olives
