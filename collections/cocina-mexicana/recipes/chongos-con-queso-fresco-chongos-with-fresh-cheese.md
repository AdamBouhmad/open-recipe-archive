## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- three egg yolks, beaten
- butter
- fine and cold sponge cake, sliced
- fresh cheese
- syrup
- thin slices of butter
- ground cinnamon
- pine nuts
- toasted almonds
- thin slices of fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of medium point with two pounds of sugar
2. Strain and cool the syrup, then mix it with three beaten egg yolks
3. Butter a casserole dish well
4. Layer sliced cold sponge cake alternating with fresh cheese, spooning syrup over each layer so everything is well covered
5. Top the last layer with thin slices of butter
6. Place the casserole at the heat of the stove with some hot coals, cover with a griddle with a little flame
7. When the chongos are well soaked (observe by tilting the casserole slightly to see if the syrup has been consumed), remove from heat
8. When lukewarm, dust with ground cinnamon and garnish with pine nuts, toasted almonds, and thin slices of fresh cheese
