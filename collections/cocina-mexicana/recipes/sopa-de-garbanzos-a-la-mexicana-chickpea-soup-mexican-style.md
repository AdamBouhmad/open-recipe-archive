## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- milk
- cold bread, very thin
- raisins
- citron
- almonds
- pine nuts
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook some chickpeas and grind them well
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Mix and dissolve in milk until it becomes like atole
4. Put to cook until it takes body
5. Place thin slices of toasted cold bread on a platter
6. Set aside a little of the milk and pour the rest over the bread
7. Add raisins, citron, toasted almonds and pine nuts (the last two toasted and all three cut into pieces), sugar and ground cinnamon
8. Place over hot coals with fire on top, adding the reserved milk as it cooks
