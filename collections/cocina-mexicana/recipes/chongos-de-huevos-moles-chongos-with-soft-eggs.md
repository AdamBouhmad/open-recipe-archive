## Ingredients

- soft cold sponge cakes
- clarified syrup
- butter
- fresh cheese, crumbled
- soft eggs

## Directions

1. Cut some soft cold sponge cakes in half
2. Soak them in well-clarified syrup
3. On a platter coated with butter, place layers of these sponge cakes and of fresh crumbled cheese
4. The last layer will be of soft eggs, and let the chongos set over gentle heat, then placing the platter over the steam of a pot
