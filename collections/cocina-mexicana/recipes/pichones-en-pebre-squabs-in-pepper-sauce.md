## Ingredients

- squabs
- lard
- salt
- raw green chiles, deveined
- cooked tomatoes
- garlic
- cumin
- pepper
- broth from cooking the squabs
- capers
- vinegar
- oil
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry lard with salt, raw deveined green chiles, cooked tomatoes, garlic, cumin and pepper, all ground together
2. Add the squabs with their cooking broth, capers and a little vinegar
3. When removing from heat, add oil and olives
