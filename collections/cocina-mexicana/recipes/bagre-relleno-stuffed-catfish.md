## Ingredients

- green chili peppers
- garlic
- xitomate (tomato)
- tomato
- parsley
- cooked egg yolks
- clove
- pepper
- cooking oil
- salt
- lard
- paper

## Directions

1. Fry green chili in a small amount, garlic, xitomate, tomato and parsley, all chopped; dissolve cooked egg yolks in this frying, and adding clove, pepper and oil with the necessary salt, fill the small catfish or portions of the desired size with this.
2. Wrap them in paper that has already been prepared with lard, pepper, chopped parsley, breadcrumbs and salt.
3. Place them to cook on the grill, taking care that they do not burn, and serve them alone or with the sauce from the previous recipe, or another fish sauce.
