## Ingredients

- chickens
- ham
- garlic
- cloves
- pepper
- parsley
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- saffron
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- capers
- flour
- olives
- cured tornachiies
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and make many incisions all over with the knife tip
2. Insert pieces of ham into some incisions, split garlic cloves into others, and ground clove and pepper powder into some
3. Cover all incisions with parsley leaves
4. Place the chickens in a pot with water and vinegar, seasoning with salt and ground saffron
5. After cooking, remove the chickens from the broth and fry them in lard with ground garlic
6. When well browned, set aside and fry roasted and ground tomatoes in the same lard
7. Mix in the broth in which the chickens cooked, flour browned in lard, capers, and pickled caperberries
8. When seasoned and of regular consistency, add olives, cured tornachiies, and oil
