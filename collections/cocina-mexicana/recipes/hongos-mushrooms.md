## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- salt
- oil
- onion
- hazelnut sauce
- parsley
- pepper
- mint

## Directions

1. Wash mushrooms well so they do not taste of earth and cook in water and salt.
2. Once cooked, drain and fry in oil, and halfway through frying add chopped onion so they finish frying with it.
3. Place in a casserole, make hazelnut sauce, add a handful of parsley, pepper, and mint and let everything simmer together; if you do not want sauce, the onion will suffice.
