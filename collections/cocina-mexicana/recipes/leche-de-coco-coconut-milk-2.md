## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- three pounds of sugar
- two pozuelos of well-washed and ground rice
- grated coconut
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place a pan over heat
2. Add the milk, three pounds of sugar, two pozuelos of well-washed and ground rice and grated coconut
3. Cook until it reaches such a point that when drops of water are added they form little balls
4. Empty into dishes and make designs with cinnamon powder
