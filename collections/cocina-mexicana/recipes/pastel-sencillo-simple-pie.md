## Ingredients

- prepared pastry dough
- flour
- hake, turbot, mushrooms or other filling

## Directions

1. Fill the pastry with flour for cooking
2. Remove and discard the flour
3. Fill the pie with a stew of hake, turbot, mushrooms or whatever is desired
4. Bake in the oven or over hot coals
