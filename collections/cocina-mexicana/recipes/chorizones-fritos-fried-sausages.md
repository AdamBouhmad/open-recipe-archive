## Ingredients

- Sausages
- Lard
- Bread crusts
- Vinegar (optional)
- Salt (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the sausages in lard
2. When they are well cooked and half toasted, serve them with crispy bread pieces fried in the same lard
3. Some add a little vinegar while cooking and a pinch of salt to make the sausages smell good
