## Ingredients

- rabbit
- lard
- oil
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- oregano
- vinegar
- rabbit cooking broth
- rabbit portions
- burnt bread
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- ham slices
- chorizos
- small zucchini
- tomatillos
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry onion, clean garlic, and plenty of parsley and oregano all chopped in two parts lard and one part oil
2. Add a little vinegar
3. Increase with broth from cooking the rabbit and rabbit portions
4. Grind burnt bread toasted at the fire with sufficient clove, cinnamon and a little pepper
5. After some boils, add ham slices and chorizos
6. Add small zucchini and tomatillos in quarters
7. When the broth is almost thickened, add considerable oil
8. When seasoned, remove from heat
