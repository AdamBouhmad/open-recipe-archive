## Ingredients

- Any of the previously explained creams
- 1 ounce of fish gelatin, dissolved and clarified

## Directions

1. Heat the mixture and bring it to the setting point in a water bath
2. Allow it to cool
3. Pass it through a fine sieve or strainer with a wooden spoon
4. Incorporate 1 ounce of fish gelatin, dissolved and clarified
5. Pour this composition into a mold
6. Once cold, bury it in snow for one hour
7. Turn it out onto the serving plate
8. If the cream does not release from the mold, soak the mold in hot water for 2 or 3 seconds
