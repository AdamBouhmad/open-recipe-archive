## Ingredients

- water
- veal breast bones
- ancho chile, ground
- pasilla chile, ground
- salt
- epazote branch
- calabacitas

## Directions

1. Heat a pot of water and bring to a boil
2. Add the veal breast bones and equal amounts of ground ancho and pasilla chiles
3. Do not skim the pot
4. Let simmer over gentle fire after adding the appropriate salt
5. When almost cooked, add an epazote branch and calabacitas
