## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half a pound of melted butter
- six ounces of liquid yeast
- four ounces of ground sugar
- grease
- paper

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with half a pound of melted butter, six ounces of liquid yeast, and four ounces of ground sugar
2. Form the biscuits immediately when these are combined
3. Let rest a while until they sweat
4. Top with grease and wrap in paper
5. Bake in the oven
