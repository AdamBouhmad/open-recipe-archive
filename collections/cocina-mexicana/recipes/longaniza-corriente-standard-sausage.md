## Ingredients

- pork meat with fat
- dried chiles
- chiles, wide
- cumin seeds
- vinegar
- salt
- pork intestines, thin and clean

## Directions

1. Take pork meat that has enough fat and cut into medium pieces
2. Devein and wash wide chiles in several waters
3. Grind clean chile eyes and a few cumin seeds
4. Dissolve these in good vinegar and season with sufficient salt
5. Put the meat in this adobo and let it infuse for eight to ten hours
6. Fill the thinnest, well-cleaned intestines and hang them in the air to dry
