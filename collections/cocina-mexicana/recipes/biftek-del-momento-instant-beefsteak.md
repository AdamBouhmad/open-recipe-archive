## Ingredients

- veal, sliced very thin
- lemon
- oil
- butter
- olives
- capers
- onion, sliced
- garlic in vinegar
- thyme
- oregano
- lemon or orange slices
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Slice the veal very thin and beat it well
2. Coat it with lemon
3. Partially cook it in oil and butter
4. To serve, place on top olives, capers, sliced onion, garlic in vinegar, thyme, oregano, slices of lemon or orange, and grated cheese
