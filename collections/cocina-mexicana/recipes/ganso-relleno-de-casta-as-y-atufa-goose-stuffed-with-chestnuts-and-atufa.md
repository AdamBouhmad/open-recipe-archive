## Ingredients

- Goose
- Goose liver
- Lard
- Onion
- Chestnuts

## Directions

1. Prepare as stated
2. Sauté the chopped liver in lard with an onion in small pieces
3. Add a sufficient quantity of chestnuts prepared as stated for chestnut pear
4. Season this mixture to good taste and fill the body of the goose with it
5. Tuck the tail feathers inward and sew up the belly and the bladder
6. Place on the spit three quarters of an hour before serving
7. Observe the same degree of roasting as if it were not stuffed
