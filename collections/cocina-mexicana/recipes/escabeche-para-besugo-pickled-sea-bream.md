## Ingredients

- Sea bream, head removed and scaled
- Oil for frying
- Garlic cloves
- Juice of twelve sour oranges
- Vinegar, a quarter portion
- Water, two portions
- Clove
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Salt
- Garlic, roasted in lard

## Directions

1. Remove the head from the sea bream and scale it, cut into portions and fry in oil with a few garlic cloves until golden, then set aside and let cool.
2. Take the juice of twelve sour oranges and add a quarter portion of vinegar and two portions of water.
3. Grind clove, saffron, pepper, and salt, form the broth with this and place on the fire ensuring it does not boil.
4. When seasoned, remove from heat, and once it cools, add the sea bream pieces, keeping them well sealed.
5. Serve cold or hot without adding anything else except garlic roasted in lard.
