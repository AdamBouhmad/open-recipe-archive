## Ingredients

- Beef palate
- Butter
- Onion
- Broth
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Clean carefully and cook in water
2. Cut into strips
3. Brown onion in butter
4. When half fried, add the palate
5. Moisten with broth and add a bundle of parsley, salt, and pepper
6. Let the sauce reduce and serve with a little mustard
