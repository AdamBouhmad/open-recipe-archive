## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Orange
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut the tips of the leaves and the stem, brush with orange so they do not turn black.
2. Loosen them a little and add inside oil, pepper, and salt.
3. Place them in a saucepan with water so they cook, in such a way that all the water is consumed and only the oil remains.
4. Serve with sauce or without it.
