## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- three ounces of ground rice
- twelve egg yolks
- pulp of custard apple
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Mix six cuartillos of very sweetened milk with three ounces of ground rice and twelve egg yolks
2. Incorporate everything well and strain through a sieve
3. Place over heat, stirring to prevent the rice from settling
4. When it reaches the proper point, add custard apple pulp that has been ground, using only the interior and whitest part to avoid bitterness
5. Add as much as needed for the milk to taste of custard apple, and add sugar if needed
6. Allow to boil more to recover the proper point
7. Pour into serving dishes, and when cooled, decorate with cinnamon powder
