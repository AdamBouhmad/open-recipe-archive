## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of sugar
- cinnamon stick
- twenty-four egg yolks
- a pound of ground almonds
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar and cook to half-hard stage
2. Add a cinnamon stick to give it flavor
3. Beat the twenty-four egg yolks
4. When the syrup is cold, add the yolks
5. Place over heat and add the pound of ground almonds
6. Stir until it reaches the consistency of cajeta (spreadable candy)
7. In a pan buttered, place to cook over two fires
8. Top with sugar and cinnamon
