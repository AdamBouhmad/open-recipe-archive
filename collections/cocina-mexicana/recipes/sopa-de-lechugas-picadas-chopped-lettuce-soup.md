## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- lard
- broth with all its spices
- bread
- oil

## Directions

1. Chop the lettuce leaves up to where the white begins
2. Fry them in lard
3. When the liquid has been consumed, add broth from the pot with all its spices
4. Let everything boil until the lettuce is completely cooked
5. Soak toast bread in this same broth, interspersing the lettuce
6. Put the soup over gentle heat until the broth thickens slightly
7. Remove from heat to rest and serve
