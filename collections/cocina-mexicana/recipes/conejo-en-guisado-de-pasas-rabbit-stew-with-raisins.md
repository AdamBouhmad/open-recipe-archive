## Ingredients

- large portion of raisins
- lard
- roasted and peeled tomatoes
- oil
- water
- salt
- cooked or roasted rabbit portions
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown a large portion of raisins in lard
2. Grind the raisins with enough roasted and peeled tomatoes
3. Fry the mixture in oil
4. Add water and sufficient salt along with the rabbit portions
5. When the broth is thick, add considerable vinegar
6. After a good boil, remove from heat
