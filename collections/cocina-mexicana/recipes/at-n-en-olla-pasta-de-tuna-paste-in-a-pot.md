## Ingredients

- tuna meat
- red butter
- white wine
- green lemon
- salt
- pepper
- mushrooms
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Chop the meat of a piece of tuna
2. Put it in a pot with red butter, white wine, green lemon, salt, pepper and mushrooms
3. Let it cook
4. Serve with fried bread
