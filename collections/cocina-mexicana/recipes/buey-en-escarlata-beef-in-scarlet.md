## Ingredients

- beef
- thyme
- oregano
- basil
- bay leaf
- cloves
- garlic, finely minced
- onion, finely minced
- cloth
- onions
- aromatic herbs
- red sauce

## Directions

1. Take a piece or vessel of clay of appropriate size, add plenty of thyme, oregano, basil, bay leaf, and cloves; add finely minced garlic and onion, cover the vessel well with a cloth to prevent air from entering, and leave for five days without moving.
2. After this time, turn it and leave another five days; after this, place to cook with onions and all kinds of aromatic herbs, and serve with red sauce.
