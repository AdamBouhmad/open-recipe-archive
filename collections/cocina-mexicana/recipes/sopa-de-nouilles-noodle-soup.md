## Ingredients

- Five or six very fresh eggs
- Half a cuartillo of flour
- Water
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Break five or six very fresh eggs into half a cuartillo of flour and add a little water, salt, and coarse pepper, making a very consistent mass
2. Extend the mass with a rolling pin until it is reduced to the thickness of a line
3. Cut from it small pieces, strips, scallops, etc., which are dusted with flour so they do not stick together
4. Drop them into boiling broth to cook in it
5. This soup needs a quarter of an hour of cooking
