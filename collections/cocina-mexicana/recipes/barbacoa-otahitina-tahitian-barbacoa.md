## Ingredients

- Animal meat
- Pebbles or guijarro
- Dry wood
- Coconut leaves and husks
- Green banana leaves
- Fruits or roots such as potatoes, camotes, chayotes

## Directions

1. Make a shallow hole the size of the piece to be cooked
2. Cover the bottom with pebbles
3. Light a fire with dry wood, leaves and coconut husks
4. When the pebbles are well heated, remove the coals outside
5. Cover the hole with green coconut leaves or others
6. Place the animal to be cooked on them after wrapping it whole or in quarters in banana leaves
7. Cover with the embers and place some fruit or roots such as potatoes, camotes, chayotes, etc., equally wrapped in leaves
8. Cover everything with the rest of the embers, ash and part of the hot pebbles that were removed
9. Throw many coconut leaves on top and cover everything with earth to concentrate the heat
10. After a time proportionate to the animal that was cooked, open the hole and remove the meat, so well roasted, so tender and so juicy
