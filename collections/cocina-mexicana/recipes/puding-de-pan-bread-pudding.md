## Ingredients

- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- milk
- cinnamon
- clove
- spices
- butter

## Directions

1. Make with breadcrumbs, eggs, and milk in small quantity only to moisten it
2. Add a powder of cinnamon, clove and spices, and butter
3. Place in a dish which should be cooked over great heat
