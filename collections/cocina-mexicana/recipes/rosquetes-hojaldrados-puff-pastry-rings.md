## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Butter
- Cinnamon
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Take the desired quantity of puff pastry dough and extend it well on a table and spread with butter
2. Fold into several folds, all spread with butter
3. Make small sticks from it, which are divided into small pieces, and extend with the rolling pin so that they are not too thin
4. With a small cup cut out some wheels, and with its bottom cut out the bite from the middle, which results in the rosquetes being formed
5. Fry them in lard, which should not be excessively hot, because then they would toast on top while remaining raw inside, nor so cold that they do not puff up and burst
6. After frying, throw them into hot syrup, boiled with cinnamon, and once it penetrates them, remove them, drain them and arrange them on a plate, decorating with dragées and ground cinnamon
