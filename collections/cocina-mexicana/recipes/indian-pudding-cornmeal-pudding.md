## Ingredients

- cornmeal flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Dilute cornmeal flour, butter, salt, and pepper together
2. Place the mixture in a linen sack or bag
3. Put the sack in a pot of boiling water
4. Leave it there for four consecutive hours
