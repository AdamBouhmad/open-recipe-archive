## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- throat
- salt
- fine pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- oregano powder
- vinegar or lemon juice
- bull's bladder
- strong thread
- heavy tiles
- clean linen cloth
- bay leaf
- thyme
- marjoram
- parsley
- oil
- ground pepper
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Beat the tongue well and split it lengthwise
2. Stuff with the throat (well beaten, with nerves removed), salt, fine pepper, Tabasco pepper, and oregano powder
3. Sprinkle with vinegar or lemon juice
4. Wrap tightly on itself and place in a bull's bladder, tying it securely with strong thread
5. Place under heavy tiles for five days, turning daily
6. After five days, cook wrapped in clean linen cloth with vinegar, bay leaf, thyme and marjoram
7. After cooking, place again under tiles for twenty-four hours
8. Slice into thick or thin slices as preferred
9. Serve on French salad, covered with parsley, drizzled with oil, dusted with ground pepper, and decorated with chiles and olives
