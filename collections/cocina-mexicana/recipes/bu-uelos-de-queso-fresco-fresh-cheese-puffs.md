## Ingredients

- three pounds flour
- half Ixtapan cheese
- three egg yolks
- one egg white
- half bowl anise water
- one pound melted lard

## Directions

1. Grind half Ixtapan cheese with three egg yolks, one egg white, half bowl of anise water and one pound of melted lard for three pounds of flour
2. Knead everything well and knead until the dough makes eyes
3. After, dusting the dough with flour, extend thin sheets with the rolling pin, and with a cup or some circle of tin foil, cut and fry in very hot lard, bathing them on top with it until they brown
