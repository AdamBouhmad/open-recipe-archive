## Ingredients

- eight pounds of flour
- one pound of sugar
- half pound of lard
- one quarter of wine
- twenty-four egg yolks
- eight egg whites
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Knead eight pounds of flour with one pound of sugar, half pound of lard, one quarter of wine, twenty-four egg yolks, eight egg whites and as much water as needed.
2. When the dough becomes smooth, spread the papelinas with a rolling pin to the desired size on a smooth board.
3. Place them to air on cloths, and arrange them on a comal that should already be hot over the fire.
4. When half cooked, wrap them in the shape of gaznates and put them back on the comal until they are perfectly cooked.
