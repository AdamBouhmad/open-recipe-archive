## Ingredients

- beef
- parsley
- potatoes
- eggs or other garnishes

## Directions

1. Cook the beef in water (see OLLA or COCIDO)
2. Debone a hindquarter and tie it giving it a round shape so that when placed on the plate it bulges
3. At grand tables serve this piece of beef with parsley around it
4. At common or home tables decorate with pasta, sometimes with fried cabbage, or with ham, turnips, carrots, cabbage or onions, or with fried or soft-boiled eggs around it
