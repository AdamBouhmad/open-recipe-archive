## Ingredients

- Tomato, finely ground
- Garlic, finely minced
- Onion, finely minced
- Water
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Fried bread, one piece
- Vinegar
- Duck quarters
- Sugar, one piece
- Carrots, minced
- Beets, minced
- Potatoes, minced
- Eating oil
- Olives
- Capers
- Small chiles in vinegar
- Sesame

## Directions

1. Fry much ground tomato, finely minced garlic, and onion.
2. Add water, ground clove, cinnamon, pepper, and a piece of fried bread, and moisten well with vinegar.
3. Add the duck in quarters.
4. Once it has boiled, add a piece of sugar, minced carrots, beets, and potatoes.
5. Let season well, and when done, add eating oil, olives, capers, small chiles in vinegar, and sesame.
