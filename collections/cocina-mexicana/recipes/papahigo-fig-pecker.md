## Ingredients

- papahigos (birds)
- ham
- bread crumbs
- salt
- verjuice or white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and feet without emptying the birds and thread them onto skewers with strips of ham
2. While they cook, dust them with bread crumbs mixed with salt
3. Serve with verjuice in grain or with white pepper
