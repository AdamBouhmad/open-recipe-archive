## Ingredients

- Turkey, killed two days prior and cleaned
- Salt
- Half pound ancho chile, deveined
- Half pound pasilla chile
- Lard
- Small tomatoes
- Turkey broth
- One ounce cinnamon
- Half ounce pepper
- Twenty cloves
- Half pound more lard
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned turkey in salted water.
2. Devein half a pound of ancho chile and the same of pasilla.
3. Toast the chiles in lard without burning them.
4. Cook small tomatoes and grind them together with the fried chiles, ensuring the chile is well ground.
5. Fry everything in lard.
6. Add broth from the turkey and place the turkey in regular pieces.
7. Add one ounce of ground cinnamon, half ounce of ground pepper, and twenty ground cloves.
8. Add half a pound more of lard.
9. Season to taste with salt.
10. Boil until the broth thickens regularly.
11. When serving, place a little separated lard on top of the plates and sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds.
