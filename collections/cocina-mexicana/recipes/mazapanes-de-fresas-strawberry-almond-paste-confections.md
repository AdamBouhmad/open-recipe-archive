## Ingredients

- One pound sweet almond
- Six ounces sugar
- Six ounces strawberries, crushed and sieved

## Directions

1. Reduce to paste one pound of sweet almond
2. Add six ounces of sugar and six ounces of strawberries, crushed and passed through a sieve
3. Conclude as described in previous recipes
