## Ingredients

- Veal
- Carrots
- Garlic
- Ham
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Water
- Vinegar
- Herbs
- Serviette
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Laurel leaves

## Directions

1. Lard the veal with carrots, garlic, ham, cloves, and pepper
2. Place to cook with water, a little vinegar, garlic, and a bouquet of aromatic herbs
3. When well cooked, remove and roll in a serviette, squeezing tightly
4. Leave in press for a full day
5. After this, slice and place in oil and vinegar of which about a quarter will be with ground thyme and marjoram, plenty of laurel leaves, and abundant remaining ingredients
