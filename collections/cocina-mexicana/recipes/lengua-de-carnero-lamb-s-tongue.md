## Ingredients

- lamb's tongue
- thin bacon
- pebre sauce
- oil
- parsley
- bay leaf
- pimento
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Scald the lamb's tongues and lard them with thin bacon
2. When they are half-cooked, remove the skin
3. Return to cook for half an hour over low heat
4. Serve hot with a sauce made of pebre, oil, parsley, bay leaf, pimento and vinegar
