## Ingredients

- desalted ham leg
- white wine
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a desalted ham leg and soak in white wine for ten to twelve hours
2. Place in the roaster and roast for six hours over moderate heat, continuously basting with the drippings
3. When done remove the skin and dust with bread crumbs
4. When serving add lemon juice
