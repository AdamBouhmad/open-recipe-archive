## Ingredients

- strained broth
- one glass of white wine
- another glass of broth from the pot
- onion
- parsley sprigs
- garlic
- clove
- one bay leaf
- two tablespoons of oil
- a pinch of cilantro
- one sliced onion

## Directions

1. Put the strained broth, white wine, and broth from the pot in a saucepan with onion, parsley sprigs, garlic, clove, bay leaf, oil, cilantro, and sliced onion
2. Cook everything together over low heat for two hours
3. Skim it immediately
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Season with salt and pepper
