## Ingredients

- hejoles (beans)
- queso (cheese)
- huevo (egg)

## Directions

1. Cook the beans until done
2. Slice some cheese
3. Make a layer of beans, then a layer of cheese, then more beans on top
4. Fry with egg as for chiles rellenos (stuffed chiles)
5. Make a sauce as for chiles and place the beans in it
