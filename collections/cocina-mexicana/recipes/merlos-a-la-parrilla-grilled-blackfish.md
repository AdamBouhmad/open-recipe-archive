## Ingredients

- blackfish
- oil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- butter sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. After emptying and cleaning the blackfish, make incisions on both sides.
2. Drizzle with oil, salt, and coarse pepper.
3. Half an hour before serving, place on the grill over fairly lively fire.
4. When well roasted, cover with butter sauce or, preferably, tomato sauce.
