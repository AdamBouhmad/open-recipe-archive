## Ingredients

- raw chicken breasts
- lard
- cooked egg yolks
- hard biscuit
- cinnamon
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wrap raw chicken breasts in paper well-coated with lard
2. Roast on a grill
3. Chop and grind with cooked egg yolks, a little hard biscuit, and just a pinch of cinnamon for flavor
4. Place the grill over gentle heat with paper on top well-coated with lard
5. Arrange the meatballs on it, turning carefully when they are roasted on one side
6. Serve with powdered sugar and cinnamon on top
