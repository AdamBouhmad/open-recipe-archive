## Ingredients

- Good white Castilian vinegar
- Clean and dry rose petals, the same quantity as would be used of tarragon leaves
- A string of well-dried roots of Florentine iris

## Directions

1. Proceed the same way as for the wilted tarragon vinegar, putting in its place the same quantity of clean and dry roses
2. Instead of the bundle of cloves, use a string of well-dried roots of Florentine iris, which can be used again by drying them after they have been removed from the vinegar when the operation is complete
