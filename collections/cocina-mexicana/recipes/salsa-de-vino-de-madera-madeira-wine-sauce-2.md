## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 glass dry Madeira wine
- a few tablespoons concentrated broth
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- candied or preserved green lemon, divided into small pieces
- 1 good piece of fine butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 tablespoon flour in 1 glass of dry Madeira wine
2. Add a few tablespoons of concentrated broth, a little butter, salt, nutmeg and candied or preserved green lemon divided into small pieces
3. Bring everything to a boil over high heat for about twenty minutes
4. Remove from heat and add 1 good piece of fine butter
5. Stir the sauce carefully so that the butter does not turn into oil
