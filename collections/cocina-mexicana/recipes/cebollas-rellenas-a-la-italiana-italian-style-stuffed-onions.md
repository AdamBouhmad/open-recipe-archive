## Ingredients

- onions
- bread crumb
- milk
- hard egg yolks
- cheese (grated)
- almonds (peeled)
- egg yolks (beaten)
- salt
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- flour
- eggs
- broth
- lemon juice and zest
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and arrange as in the previous recipe
2. Soak a bread crumb in milk and squeeze it out
3. Mix with two hard egg yolks, two ounces of grated cheese, five peeled almonds, two beaten egg yolks, salt and a little cinnamon, pepper and powdered saffron
4. Chop everything and mix, filling the onions with this
5. Roll in flour, coat with beaten egg and fry
6. Serve immediately with a sauce made with a little strained broth, lemon juice and zest, or with a spicy sauce, or tartare sauce, or tomato sauce
