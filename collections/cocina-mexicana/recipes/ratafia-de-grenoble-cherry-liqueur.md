## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of selected cherries
- twenty quartillos of juice
- two pounds of sugar
- twenty quartillos of common aguardiente
- one drachma of cinnamon
- twenty-four crushed cloves
- eight ounces of peach leaves
- half pound of crushed and cleaned kernel almonds from apricot pits

## Directions

1. Crush selected cherries in a sieve by pressing with a skimmer after removing the stems, so that the pulp and stones separate completely
2. Break the stones
3. Put everything in an earthenware vessel over gentle and continuous fire, stirring with a wooden spatula
4. After a few moments of boiling, empty into a basin and let cool
5. Place in the press and extract twenty quartillos of juice
6. Dissolve two pounds of sugar in the juice
7. Rectify twenty quartillos of common aguardiente with one drachma of cinnamon, twenty-four crushed cloves, eight ounces of peach leaves and half a pound of crushed and cleaned kernel almonds from apricot pits
8. Mix everything exactly
9. Filter through the sleeve and store in well-sealed bottles
