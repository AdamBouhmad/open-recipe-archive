## Ingredients

- Yellow and sour quinces
- Sea salt water
- Water
- Two and one-half pounds sugar for each pound of quince slices

## Directions

1. Peel yellow and sour quinces, ensuring no black marks remain
2. Make slices that do not reach the core, as the grainy part would spoil the preserve
3. Add to sea salt water and leave for twelve to fifteen hours
4. Remove and place in a pot with boiling water until well cooked
5. Add to fresh water where they remain until the next day
6. Transfer to highly clarified syrup at alfeñique point for boiling
7. Make syrup with two and one-half pounds sugar for each pound of well-drained quince slices
