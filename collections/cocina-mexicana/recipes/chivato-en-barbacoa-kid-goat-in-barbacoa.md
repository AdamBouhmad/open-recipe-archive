## Ingredients

- Kid goat
- Chile ancho, ground
- Garlic, ground
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ginger

## Directions

1. Kill and skin the animal, then gut it.
2. Clean the stomach and intestines thoroughly, then return them to the stomach and place it back in its original position.
3. Rub the entire body inside and out with a paste made from ground ancho chile, ground garlic, salt, pepper, and ginger.
4. Cook in barbacoa according to the different methods explained under barbacoa.
