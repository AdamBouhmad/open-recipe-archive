## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- cinnamon
- clean almonds
- beaten eggs
- lard
- syrup
- wine
- almonds
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Grind cooked egg yolks with cinnamon and clean almonds
2. Form this mixture into tortillas
3. Wrap with beaten eggs and fry carefully in lard
4. Place in syrup made with wine, almonds, pine nuts, toasted sesame seeds and ground cinnamon
