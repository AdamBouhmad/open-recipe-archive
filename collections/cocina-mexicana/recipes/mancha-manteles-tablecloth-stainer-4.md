## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic cloves
- ancho chiles, deveined and soaked
- roasted tomatoes
- clove
- salt
- cooked hen, in quarters
- broth
- apples, sliced
- pineapple, in small pieces
- small onions, all cooked

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard
2. Grind together with deveined and soaked ancho chiles, roasted tomatoes, and cloves
3. Fry everything in lard with salt
4. Once done, add the cooked hen in quarters, broth, sliced apples, pineapple in small pieces, and small cooked onions
5. After boiling well, it will be in a somewhat thick broth
