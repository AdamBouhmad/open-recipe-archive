## Ingredients

- one catfish
- salt
- bitter orange or lemon juice
- pepper powder
- oil
- lard
- xitomate (tomato), chopped
- parsley, chopped
- breadcrumbs
- four or five olives
- roasted xitomates
- fried parsley
- fried garlic
- vinegar
- chorizo
- longaniza sausage
- onion
- broth
- fine and coarse pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- Cologne water

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the catfish, add salt, bitter orange or lemon juice and pepper powder, letting it air-dry this way.
2. Grease some papers with oil, at the same time coating them with lard, and putting on top of them finely chopped pepper, xitomate and parsley, and breadcrumbs; place the fish on them and wrap them in the papers, placing them to cook at gentle heat on the grill.
3. For the sauce: grind four or five olives according to the amount of sauce you are making; roasted xitomates, a little fried parsley, fried garlic soaked in vinegar, fried chorizo and longaniza and dissolve the ground mixture with oil and juice of raw tomato.
4. Fry a fine soffritto separately with onion and parsley in greater quantity; when fried, add the ground mixture so that it also fries, adding broth, fine and coarse pepper, clove and cinnamon, vinegar, and three or four drops of Cologne water; season with sufficient salt, and when bringing it to the table place chili peppers and olives on it.
