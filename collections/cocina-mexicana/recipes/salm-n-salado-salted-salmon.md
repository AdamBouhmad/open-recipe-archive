## Ingredients

- salted salmon
- water
- salt
- salad

## Directions

1. Soak and desalt the salted salmon in cold water.
2. Place in a saucepan with clean water, and skim when it begins to boil.
3. Remove the saucepan from the heat, cover it with a cloth, and after five minutes, drain and serve with salad.
