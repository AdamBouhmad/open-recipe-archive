## Ingredients

- Mulberries, slightly green
- Syrup at the blown-sugar stage
- Fine sugar, one pound per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Take slightly green mulberries and give them one strong boil, covered, in syrup at the blown-sugar stage
2. Remove from heat and skim
3. Keep in the syrup for twenty-four hours in the stove so they absorb it
4. After this time, remove them and let them drain on a sieve
5. Arrange them on slate or tiles and dust with fine sugar
6. Turn them over to the other side and repeat the operation when the first side is dry
7. Store them for future use
