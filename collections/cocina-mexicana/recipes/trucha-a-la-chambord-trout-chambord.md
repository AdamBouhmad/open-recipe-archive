## Ingredients

- gutted trout
- large truffle cloves
- wine marinade
- veal sweetbreads
- squabs
- carp quenelles
- orange juice
- crayfish
- financière ragout

## Directions

1. Blanch the gutted trout by plunging into boiling water, remove all skins perfectly, wash thoroughly in several waters, drain, and lard with large truffle cloves shaped like dominoes
2. Cook in a good wine marinade
3. At the moment of serving, drain and arrange on an oval plate or fish kettle
4. Garnish with four larded veal sweetbreads, four cooked squabs, eight quenelles with sour orange juice, and eight good crayfish
5. Pour a good financière ragout in place of sauce (see FINANCIERA)
