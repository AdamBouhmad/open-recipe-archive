## Ingredients

- veal offal prepared as above
- ham strips
- chorizo slices
- raisins
- almonds
- salt
- spices
- pork caul
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- garlic
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare the offal as in the previous recipe
2. Divide into small pieces and mix with cooked strips of ham and slices of chorizo
3. Mix in raisins, almonds cut into quarters, salt and the same spices as the larded offal
4. Wrap in a sheet of pork caul that has been coated with plenty of oil and dusted with breadcrumbs
5. Place in a casserole dish with ground garlic and sufficient lard
6. Place in the oven to cook, finishing the operation as in the preceding recipe
