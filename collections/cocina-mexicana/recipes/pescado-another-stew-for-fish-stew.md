## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- lard
- pepper
- cloves
- fish cooking broth
- oil
- salt
- oregano in powder
- chiles in vinegar
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry thin slices of bread in lard until golden
2. Grind the fried bread with pepper and cloves
3. Dissolve the ground mixture in the cooking broth of the fish
4. Add oil and sufficient salt
5. Stir well so the broth becomes thick
6. Pour over the cooked fish
7. Place the dish over steam from a pot
8. When serving, add oil and powdered oregano
9. Decorate with chiles in vinegar and olives
