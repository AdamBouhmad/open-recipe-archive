## Ingredients

- eight to ten onions
- salt
- four ounces of lard
- a little salt pork and roasted poultry meat
- six egg yolks
- six or seven grains of ground pepper
- a little breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook eight to ten onions with salt.
2. Squeeze them well.
3. Sauté in four ounces of lard.
4. Chop a little salt pork and roasted poultry meat.
5. Mix with six egg yolks, six or seven grains of ground pepper, a little breadcrumbs, and salt.
6. Sauté this mixture with the onions.
7. When cooked, remove and introduce into well-cleaned intestines, tying at both ends.
8. Place to cook for about half an hour.
9. When ready to eat, roast or fry, adding the sauce desired.
