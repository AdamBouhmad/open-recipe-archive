## Ingredients

- Lard
- Roasted and chopped tomatillos
- Roasted garlic
- Onion slices
- Salt
- Grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry roasted and chopped tomatillos and roasted garlic in lard with onion slices
2. Season with salt and grated nutmeg
