## Ingredients

- ham
- chorizos
- roasted tomatoes
- fried bread
- garlic
- lard
- fresh herbs
- water from cooking the ham
- fried bread
- hard-boiled egg
- chiles
- oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook ham with chorizos
2. When cooked, grind roasted tomatoes, fried bread, garlic browned in lard and fresh herbs
3. Fry everything well and add a little of the water in which the ham was cooked
4. Let it boil and incorporate the chorizos with sliced ham
5. The broth should remain thick
6. Garnish with slices of fried bread, hard-boiled egg, chiles, oil and chopped parsley
