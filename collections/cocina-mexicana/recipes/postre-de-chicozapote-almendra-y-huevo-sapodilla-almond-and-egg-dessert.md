## Ingredients

- sugar
- sapodillas, large
- almonds, ground
- egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at hard-crack stage with two pounds of sugar
2. When removed from heat and cooled, add six large peeled and ground sapodillas, one pound of ground almonds, and twelve beaten egg yolks
3. Add a cinnamon stick
4. Return to heat and simmer until it reaches the proper consistency
5. The next day, brown the surface with a hot metal sheet or comal
