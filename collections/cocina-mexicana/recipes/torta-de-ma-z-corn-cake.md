## Ingredients

- cacahuazentle corn
- sugar
- three eggs
- almonds
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Buy cacahuazentle corn, grind and sift it
2. Grind sugar and weigh three eggs
3. Weigh the same amount of corn, the same of sugar, the same of butter as the eggs weigh, and four ounces of almonds
4. Grind the almonds with a little milk
5. Beat together the sugar, corn, and butter
6. Separately, beat the yolks as for royal eggs and the whites as for nougat
7. Mix everything together and beat until foamy
8. Butter the mold and pour everything into it
9. Send to the oven to bake. Must beat a lot.
