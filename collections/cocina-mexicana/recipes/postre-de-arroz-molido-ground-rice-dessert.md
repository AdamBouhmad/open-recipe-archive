## Ingredients

- rice, one pound
- milk, six cuartillos
- sugar
- egg yolks, ten

## Directions

1. Wash the rice first with cold water and then with hot water
2. Cook it, grind it and add to six cuartillos of milk
3. Sweeten appropriately
4. Place over fire and without stopping to stir, add ten beaten egg yolks
5. Keep it there until it reaches a point lower than manjar blanco
