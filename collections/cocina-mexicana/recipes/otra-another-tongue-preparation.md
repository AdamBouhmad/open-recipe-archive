## Ingredients

- tongue
- ham
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- onion
- carrot
- thyme
- oregano
- bay leaf
- butter
- Castilian vinegar
- Carlon wine
- water
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- bread, fried

## Directions

1. Stud the tongue with ham, garlic, pepper and clove
2. Place in a pot with plenty of sliced onion and carrot pieces, thyme, oregano and bay leaf
3. Fry in butter and when done add Castilian vinegar, the same amount of Carlon wine and water in small quantity
4. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper with fried bread slices
