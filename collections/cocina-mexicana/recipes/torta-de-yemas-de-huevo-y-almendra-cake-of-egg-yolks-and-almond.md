## Ingredients

- two pounds sugar
- thirty egg yolks
- one pound ground almonds
- cinnamon, molded

## Directions

1. Make clarified and loose syrup with two pounds of sugar
2. When it has cooled, break up thirty egg yolks in it and one pound of ground almonds, mixing everything well to put it on the fire, where it will remain until it reaches the point of detaching from the pan
3. Remove then, let cool and put afterwards in a cake pan or casserole greased with butter, dusting the cake on top with molded cinnamon
4. Put the pan in the oven and cover with a griddle with flame until the cake sets and forms a crust, taking care that it does not burn
