## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Butter
- Garlic, parsley, onion and mushrooms
- Salt and pepper
- Ham
- Broth
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the young partridges well
2. Tuck the leg ends into the body through small cuts made with a knife on each side
3. Parboil over heat in a saucepan with a little butter, garlic, parsley, onion and mushrooms (all finely chopped and seasoned with salt and pepper)
4. When the birds have absorbed the flavor of the marinade, wrap each in a slice of ham and then in paper moistened slightly so it does not burn
5. Bury in hot ashes
6. When cooked, serve with rich broth and lemon juice, or with the sauce preferred
