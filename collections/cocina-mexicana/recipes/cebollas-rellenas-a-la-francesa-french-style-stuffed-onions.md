## Ingredients

- twenty large onions
- quenelles
- ham slices
- water or broth
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean twenty large onions, being careful not to scrape the heads or their first layer or skin
2. Blanch in hot water and immediately refresh in cold water as for turnips, then drain on clean cloth
3. Remove the interior part with the instrument for hollowing apples, or with a thin knife, without breaking or damaging the outer skin
4. Fill with quenelles and arrange in a flat pan so they remain at the same height, forming a bed
5. Cover with ham slices, add water or broth, and a little sugar and salt
6. Let it come to a boil over strong fire, and when cooked, let the liquid reduce and serve as a garnish for large meat dishes
