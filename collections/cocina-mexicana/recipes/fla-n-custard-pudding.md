## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of cream
- a piece of cinnamon
- four ounces of sugar
- five egg yolks
- butter
- flour
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Boil four cuartillos of cream with a piece of cinnamon and four ounces of sugar
2. After cooling, mix in five well-beaten egg yolks
3. Return to fire, stirring until it becomes somewhat thick, taking care not to let it boil
4. Set aside and let cool
5. Butter a clean piece of linen and dust it with flour
6. Pour the flaón into it and tie at the four corners so the flaón cannot escape
7. Place in a pot with water and let boil for three quarters of an hour
8. Remove from fire with great caution so it does not break
9. Let cool in an appropriate vessel
10. Untie the linen and arrange the flaón on a plate
11. Dust with sugar and serve cold with a sauce made of equal parts melted butter and Madeira wine
