## Ingredients

- 12 cuartillos of milk
- 0.5 pound ground rice, passed through a sieve
- 1 pound sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 caldron cup of orange blossom water
- a little musk

## Directions

1. Add 0.5 pound of ground rice passed through a sieve, 1 pound of sugar, and 4 egg yolks to 12 cuartillos of milk
2. Cook everything together
3. When it reaches medium point, add 1 caldron cup of orange blossom water and a little musk
4. Let it boil until it is so thick that when a drop is placed on a plate it comes off
