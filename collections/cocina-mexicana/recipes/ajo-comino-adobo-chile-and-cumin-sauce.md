## Ingredients

- Wide chiles, deveined
- Cumin seeds
- Bread crumb
- Good vinegar
- Finely minced onion
- Aged cheese pieces (optional)
- Oregano (optional)

## Directions

1. Devein the wide chiles, soak them, and grind with a few cumin seeds and a bread crumb
2. Dilute this mixture with good vinegar
3. Add finely minced onion and serve in a sauce dish
4. Some add small pieces of aged cheese and oregano
