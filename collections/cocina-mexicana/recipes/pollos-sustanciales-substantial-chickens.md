## Ingredients

- chickens
- water
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- caraway
- nutmeg
- dried cilantro
- twelve cooked egg yolks
- salt
- lemon juice
- nutmeg scraping
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put the chickens to cook in water with clove, pepper, cinnamon, caraway, nutmeg, dried cilantro, twelve cooked egg yolks and necessary salt, all ground.
2. Once cooked, fry this sauce well and add the chickens.
3. When the sauce is very thick, serve, pouring lemon juice on them, nutmeg scraping and chopped parsley on top.
