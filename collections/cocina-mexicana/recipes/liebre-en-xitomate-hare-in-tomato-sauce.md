## Ingredients

- Cooked hare
- lard
- salt
- ham
- garlic
- tomato
- onion
- broth
- vinegar
- clove
- pepper
- capers
- oil
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Cook the hare and divide it into regular portions
2. Fry in lard with salt, slices of ham and ground garlic
3. Add raw tomato ground with onion, and when everything is rendered, season with salt and add broth, vinegar, clove, pepper and capers
4. Let boil until completely seasoned, and when removing from heat add oil, small chiles and olives
