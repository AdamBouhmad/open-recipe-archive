## Ingredients

- pineapple
- salt
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Clean and slice lengthwise in pieces like melon slices
2. Remove the core and cook with salt
3. Give three or four boils and let cool
4. Pass to fresh water for twenty-four hours
5. Defleme, rinse, and add to good syrup
6. Give one boil each day for the space of three days; on the last day bring the syrup to proper consistency and add orange blossom water
