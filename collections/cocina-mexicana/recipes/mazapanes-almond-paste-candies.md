## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one to two egg whites
- one pound of sugar
- wafers
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds on a metate, wetting it with one or two beaten egg whites so it does not become oily
2. Clarify one pound of sugar until it reaches hard-ball stage
3. Add the ground almonds to the clarified sugar
4. When the mixture pulls away from the pot, set it aside until the next day
5. Form small balls with wet hands and place them on wafers
6. Flatten slightly and make a cross in the center with a knife dipped in water
7. Send to the oven
8. For glazed mazapanes: clarify honey until it reaches a high hard-ball stage, scrape in green lemon zest, dip the mazapanes in and out of the honey, and dry on a platter while rolling them in powdered sugar
