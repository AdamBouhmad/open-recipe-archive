## Ingredients

- Cooked bird
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Lettuce
- Pickled cucumbers
- Hard boiled eggs
- Anchovy fillets
- Poultry stock

## Directions

1. After the bird is cooked, remove the meat and chop it coarsely
2. Put in an adobo with salt, pepper and vinegar
3. After a few moments, dress with lettuce, pickled cucumbers, hard boiled eggs, all chopped, and strips of anchovies, pouring the stock over the top
4. Others cut everything into strips
