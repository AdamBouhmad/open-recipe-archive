## Ingredients

- saffron water
- salt
- flour
- cooking oil
- filling: jitomate
- garlic
- onion
- cooked chard
- brains
- powdered cheese
- spices
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- a small piece of sugar
- butter
- puchero broth
- green and dry oregano
- parsley
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Make dough with saffron water, salt, flour and a little cooking oil so it is soft, good flavored, yellow and loose to roll out
2. Extend with a rolling pin as thin as possible
3. Make filling balls with half the dough, and cut the other half with a knife or cutter into little squares that you spread on tablecloths
4. Be careful to turn them over after two hours so they do not stick
5. Save them for the next day when you pass them through butter so they brown
6. Make the sauce with jitomate, garlic and onion, all finely chopped, add puchero broth, green and dry oregano, parsley, clove, cinnamon and pepper
7. The filling is made with jitomate, onion, garlic, cooked chard without veins and chopped, brains, powdered cheese, spices, vinegar, oil, salt and a small piece of sugar
8. Throw the rabioles into the boiling sauce to cook
9. When serving, add Jerez wine
