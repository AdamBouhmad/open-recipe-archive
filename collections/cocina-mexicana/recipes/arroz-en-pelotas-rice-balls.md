## Ingredients

- 1 pork tongue
- 4 pork feet
- 1 real of pork shoulder
- crushed black pepper
- 2 whole heads of garlic
- rice
- 4 large loaves of cold breast bread, grated
- 1 real of cured ham, finely chopped
- fine salt
- fine spices
- eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. The night before, put in a pot a pork tongue, four pork feet, a real of pork shoulder, a little crushed black pepper and two whole heads of garlic
2. Cover the pot and let it boil all night with sufficient water so it does not burn
3. The next day, strain the broth, clean it, and cut the tongue and the rest into pieces
4. Grate four large loaves of cold breast bread; finely chop a real of cured ham and mix with the bread, salt and fine spices until it tastes like chorizo
5. Beat eggs as for a torta and mix with the mass until balls can be made
6. Give them the shape of chocolate bars or sugar loaves and cook them in the pot
7. When they are cooked, remove them and cut them in half, returning them to the pot to cook inside
8. When the rice is finishing drying, place the half-cone shapes on top, covering everything well with beaten egg, and let it brown over two fires
