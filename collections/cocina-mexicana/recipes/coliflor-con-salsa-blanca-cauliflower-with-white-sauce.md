## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- water
- white sauce
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put to boil with a handful of salt
2. When the water is boiling add the cauliflower after cleaning it well
3. Fifteen minutes of boiling are sufficient
4. Drain and cool by placing it at the edge of the hearth in a covered pot
5. Prepare a white sauce bound with two or three egg yolks
6. At the moment of serving, take them one by one, drain them and place in a pot with the flower facing down, then turn them on a platter so the flower faces up
7. Pour the white sauce very hot over them, but without boiling
