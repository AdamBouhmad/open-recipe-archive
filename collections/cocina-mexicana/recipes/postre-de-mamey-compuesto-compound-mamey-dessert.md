## Ingredients

- 2 well-ripened and colored mamey fruits
- 5 beaten eggs
- sifted sugar to taste
- toasted sesame
- pine nuts
- ground biscuit as needed
- butter
- raisins and ground cinnamon for garnish

## Directions

1. Choose 2 well-ripened and colored mamey fruits and remove their rinds, pits and all the fibrous part near them
2. Grind them and mix with 5 beaten eggs, sifted sugar to taste, toasted sesame, pine nuts and ground biscuit in the necessary quantity so the paste is not too hard and can set
3. Pour into a dish greased with butter and set it at two gentle heats
4. When set, remove it and decorate with raisins and ground cinnamon
