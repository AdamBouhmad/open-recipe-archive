## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sugar
- one large coconut
- one quart of milk
- four egg yolks
- four ounces of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Make syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar
2. After straining, clarifying and at low point, mix one large grated and well ground coconut
3. Let it boil
4. When it has thickened, add one quart of milk, four egg yolks and four ounces of ground almonds
5. Stir everything without stopping moving so it does not stick
6. When the bottom of the pot is seen and the paste does not jump or sound, remove from heat
7. Beat it a little and as soon as it sets before it dries out, extract it through a syringe on a napkin and make the rings
