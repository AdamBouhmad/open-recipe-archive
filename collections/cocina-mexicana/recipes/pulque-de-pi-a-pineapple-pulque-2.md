## Ingredients

- Pineapples
- Pulque
- Sugar to sweeten
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Clove

## Directions

1. Peel and grind the pineapples
2. Then also grind them with the skins
3. Strain with the pulque through a sieve
4. Sweeten, adding a few bits of cinnamon, pepper and clove
5. The greater the quantity of pineapple, the better the pulque turns out
