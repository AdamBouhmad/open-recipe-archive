## Ingredients

- dos onzas de almendras dulces (two ounces of sweet almonds)
- tres onzas de almendras amargas (three ounces of bitter almonds)
- azahar garapiñado (candied orange blossom)
- cuatro cuartillos de nata hirviendo (four quarters of boiling cream)
- ocho onzas de azúcar (eight ounces of sugar)
- cuatro yemas de huevo (four egg yolks)
- dos huevos enteros (two whole eggs)

## Directions

1. Grind two ounces of sweet almonds and three of bitter almonds, both candied, with a little candied orange blossom
2. Pour all into four quarters of boiling cream
3. Pass immediately through a sieve
4. Add eight ounces of sugar, four egg yolks and two whole eggs
5. Stir everything very well
6. Sieve again
7. Pour into small cups or small bowls
8. Curdle in a bain-marie
