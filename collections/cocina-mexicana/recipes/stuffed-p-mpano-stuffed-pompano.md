## Ingredients

- pámpano fish
- eggs
- onion
- tomato
- oil
- lard
- parsley
- pepper
- tomatoes
- oregano
- thyme
- cinnamon
- clove

## Directions

1. Soak the pámpano to desalt it
2. Once desalted, pat dry
3. Cook eggs, peel and chop into large pieces
4. Chop a little onion and tomato, fry in oil and lard and fry well
5. Add the chopped egg, a little parsley and powdered pepper; this stuffing should be dry
6. Stuff the pámpano and sew shut with thread
7. In a pan with lard and oil, fry the whole pámpano without removing the tail
8. Roast tomatoes and grind with a little fresh parsley, a little oregano and thyme
9. Fry this where the pámpano was fried
10. Add onion sections and plenty of oil
11. Once well fried, add a little water
12. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper and add it
13. Place the pámpano in a pan or earthenware dish where it fits whole and spread out
14. Pour this sauce over it, which should be thick, and decorate with chiles, capers and pickled capers
