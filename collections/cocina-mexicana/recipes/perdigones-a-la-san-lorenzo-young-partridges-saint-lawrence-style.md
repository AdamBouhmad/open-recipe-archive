## Ingredients

- Young rabbit
- Salt and pepper
- Oil
- Spanish sauce
- Lemon juice
- Lemon rind scrapings

## Directions

1. Tuck the legs of the rabbit inward and flatten it by striking it on the belly
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Partially cook it in a casserole with a little oil
4. Then place it on the grill over a very lively fire
5. Remove and dress on a plate
6. Pour Spanish sauce over it to which lemon juice and scrapings of lemon rind have been added
