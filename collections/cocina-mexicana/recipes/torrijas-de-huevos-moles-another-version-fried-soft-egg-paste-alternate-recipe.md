## Ingredients

- clarified syrup at conserve point
- one and a half pounds sugar
- ten egg yolks
- ground sponge cake
- beaten eggs
- butter
- raisins
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix off heat the clarified syrup at conserve point made with sugar and egg yolks half-beaten and ground sponge cake
2. Return to heat to achieve cajeta consistency
3. Remove from heat and mix with ground sponge cake, beating frequently until well incorporated and of proper consistency to form small sticks the thickness of a cigar or slightly more
4. Coat with beaten eggs
5. Fry in butter
6. Boil in clarified syrup until it reaches conserve point
7. Place on plates to serve and decorate with raisins and dust with cinnamon
