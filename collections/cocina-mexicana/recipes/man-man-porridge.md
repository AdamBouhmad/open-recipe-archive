## Ingredients

- nine cuartillos of milk
- half a cup of peeled and ground chickpeas
- half a cup of pine nuts
- half a cup of small nuts (kernels removed)
- one cup of raw rice
- four ounces of almond (all ground and the almond strained)

## Directions

1. For nine cuartillos of milk, use half a cup of peeled and ground chickpeas, half a cup of pine nuts and half a cup of small nuts; these and the pine nuts are placed in warm water and their skins removed; one cup of raw rice, four ounces of almond (all ground and the almond strained).
