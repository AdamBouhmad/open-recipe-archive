## Ingredients

- Red wine, one quarter pot
- Sugar
- Pitted raisins, half pound
- Sponge cake
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add a little sugar to the red wine and add half a pound of pitted raisins
2. Slice sponge cake and soak each layer in the mixture until well moistened
3. Sprinkle with cinnamon
