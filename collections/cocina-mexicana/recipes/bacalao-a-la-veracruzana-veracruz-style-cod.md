## Ingredients

- Cod
- Water
- Garlic cloves
- Breadcrumbs
- Broad chile, ground
- Parsley
- Olive oil
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cod into regular pieces and place in a pot with water for about eight hours until the salt is released
2. Place on a platter and drain the water well
3. Remove the bones
4. In a frying pan, spread the cod well extended
5. Add peeled garlic cloves split in half, breadcrumbs, a little ground broad chile and minced parsley
6. Place another layer of cod also extended on top
7. Pour four ounces of olive oil, water and salt until it covers the fish
8. Place on the fire well covered and over low heat
9. When no broth remains, it can be served
