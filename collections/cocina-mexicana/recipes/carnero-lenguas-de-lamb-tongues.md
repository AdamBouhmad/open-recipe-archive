## Ingredients

- lenguas (tongues)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- cebollas (onions)
- ajos (garlic)
- peregil (parsley)
- hongos cocidos (cooked mushrooms)
- harina (flour)
- pimienta (pepper)
- caldo (broth)
- vino de Málaga (Málaga wine)
- limón (lemon)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cook tongues in salted water
2. Once cooked, remove the thick skin covering them and split lengthwise into two or three parts
3. Chop some onions, garlic, parsley and cooked mushrooms
4. Sprinkle everything with flour and fry
5. Add sufficient salt and pepper, a little of the broth in which the tongues cooked
6. Add the tongues to the frying mixture with a quarter of Málaga wine
7. Let simmer until everything has thickened well
8. Just before removing from heat, add lemon juice or a splash of vinegar mixed with water if it is too strong
9. Give another boil and remove from heat
