## Ingredients

- four ounces of sweet almonds
- grains of verjuice
- four cuartillos of freshly expressed verjuice
- sixteen ounces of purified verjuice
- pound and twelve ounces of white sugar

## Directions

1. Before making the syrup, it is necessary to prepare and purify the verjuice, and for this, pound in a marble mortar four ounces of sweet almonds with some grains of verjuice; when the almonds have been reduced to a very fine paste, place this in a bowl or cup of sandstone, and dissolve there with four cuartillos of freshly expressed or extracted verjuice, and pass through a washed cotton until the liquor comes out clean and without any color.
2. Thus purified, pour the verjuice into glass bottles, well sealed and covered even on top of the seal, and thus it can be preserved without alteration for many years.
3. Mix, then, sixteen ounces of purified verjuice with pound and twelve ounces of white sugar, placing immediately on the fire, until it takes the consistency of syrup.
