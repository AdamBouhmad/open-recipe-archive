## Ingredients

- tongue
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- water
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- Sherry wine
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Slice raw, place in a cazuela with plenty of chopped tomato, onion and garlic
2. Fry everything very well and add water, chopped parsley, oregano and thyme
3. Add cloves, cinnamon and pepper; the gravy should be thick
4. Before serving, add Sherry wine and garnish with chiles, olives and capers
