## Ingredients

- meat cut into small pieces
- minced tocino and onion
- salt
- pepper
- hazelnut sauce
- spices
- pomegranate juice from two or three cooked and squeezed pomegranates

## Directions

1. Put the meat pieces in a pot along with minced tocino and onion, salt and pepper
2. Braise over gentle heat
3. When somewhat more than halfway cooked, add hazelnut sauce
4. Add all spices when well cooked
5. Add the juice of two or three cooked and squeezed pomegranates
