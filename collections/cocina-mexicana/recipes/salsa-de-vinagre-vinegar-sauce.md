## Ingredients

- equal quantity of broth and vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- chopped garlic
- plenty of parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Put equal quantities of broth and vinegar in a saucepan with salt, pepper, chopped garlic, and plenty of chopped parsley
2. Place over fire and give it a couple of boils
