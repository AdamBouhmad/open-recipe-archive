## Ingredients

- xitomates maduros (ripe tomatoes)
- chiles anchos (wide dried chiles)
- manteca (lard)
- picadillo (minced meat)
- sal (salt)
- clavo (clove)
- canela molida (ground cinnamon)
- vino (wine)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- aceitunas (olives)
- chilitos en vinagre (small peppers in vinegar)
- alcaparras (capers)
- alcoparrones (large capers)
- orégano seco (dried oregano)

## Directions

1. Cook ripe tomatoes and squeeze out the seeds
2. Crush the tomatoes well
3. Soak wide dried chiles and remove the veins thoroughly
4. Grind the chiles and fry in lard
5. When the chile is well fried, add some tomato, salt, and some minced meat
6. When thickened slightly, add ground clove and cinnamon, wine, and a little vinegar
7. When the broth has thickened properly, remove from heat
8. Garnish with olives, small peppers in vinegar, capers, large capers, and a little dried oregano
