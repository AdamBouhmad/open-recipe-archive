## Ingredients

- Three pounds of cake flour
- Forty egg yolks
- One and one-half pounds of melted butter
- One quarter of a quart of white wine

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of cake flour with forty egg yolks, one and one-half pounds of melted butter, and one quarter of a quart of white wine
2. Beat the mixture and knead well
3. Form the rosquetes
4. Drop them into a pot of boiling water
5. Remove them from the water and place in a very hot oven to bake
6. When they come out of the oven, coat them with clarified and candied syrup made from five pounds of sugar, or glaze them with egg on top
