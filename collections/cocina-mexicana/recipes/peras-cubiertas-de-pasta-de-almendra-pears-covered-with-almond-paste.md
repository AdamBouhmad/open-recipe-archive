## Ingredients

- pears in syrup
- almonds
- biscuit
- cinnamon powder
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the pears from the syrup in which they were preserved and let them drain
2. In a little of the same syrup, add ground almonds, ground biscuit, and cinnamon powder, placing over heat
3. Make a paste from these ingredients, stirring continuously so it does not burn, until it reaches the point of cajeta
4. Place the pears on a platter and cover them with this paste
5. Dust with ground cinnamon
6. Half a pound of almonds and two ounces of biscuit are sufficient for one dozen pears
