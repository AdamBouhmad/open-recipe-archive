## Ingredients

- Green fava beans
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter
- Ham, sliced

## Directions

1. After peeling the green fava beans, cook them with salt
2. Once cooked, drain and coat in flour
3. Fry thin slices of ham in the same butter and add the fava beans
4. Add salt and let them soften fry together
5. Serve hot
