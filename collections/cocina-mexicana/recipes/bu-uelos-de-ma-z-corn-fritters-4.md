## Ingredients

- dried corn, ground
- water
- anise
- butter
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Grind corn dried, boil water with anise, butter and salt
2. Once it crumbles, pour into a bowl to cool
3. Add as many eggs as needed for it to rise
4. Fry in not-very-hot butter
5. Cook in syrup
