## Ingredients

- lamb, cleaned and bled
- water
- vinegar
- fine salt
- broad chiles, deveined and washed
- cumin
- ginger
- garlic, cleaned
- oregano
- thyme
- lard
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned and bled lamb in water with a little vinegar and corresponding fine salt
2. Devein and wash broad chiles, grind with cumin, ginger and cleaned garlic
3. Add a little oregano and thyme, and fry everything with considerable lard
4. Once well fried, add sufficient vinegar, and if too strong, mitigate with a very small piece of sugar
5. Add the meat and place over two fires until perfectly consumed
6. Serve with the sauce called chilote
