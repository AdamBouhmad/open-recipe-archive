## Ingredients

- Rice
- Salt
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Ground saffron
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Green chile, chopped
- Butter
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Olives
- Capers
- Sliced chorizo
- Ham, in small pieces
- Pork loin, cooked

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with salt, and once it is cooked it will be thick; when it cools, mix into it cloves, pepper, cumin, ground saffron and onion, garlic, tomatoes and green chile, all chopped very finely and fried in butter with salt, raisins, almonds, olives, capers, slices of chorizo, small pieces of ham and loin of pork already cooked.
