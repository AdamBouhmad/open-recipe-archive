## Ingredients

- veal lungs
- hot water
- lard
- flour
- water or broth
- thyme
- garlic
- parsley
- eggs
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning well with hot water, cut into small pieces and place with lard, dusted in flour, stir much, adding water or broth, season with thyme, garlic, parsley, and when cooked add a beaten egg and a few drops of vinegar
