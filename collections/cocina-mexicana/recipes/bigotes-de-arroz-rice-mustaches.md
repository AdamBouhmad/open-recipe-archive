## Ingredients

- rice
- very little sugar
- beaten eggs
- picadillo
- ground bizcocho

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with very little sugar, and when the water is consumed, remove from heat and stir with beaten eggs
2. Make balls putting picadillo inside, and roll in ground bizcocho
3. Fry until golden
4. Place on a platter and sprinkle with ground sugar, and put a sprig of parsley on each one
