## Ingredients

- butter
- garlic clove
- milk
- bean flour
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a pot with butter and a crushed garlic clove on the fire
2. Add a quarter of milk and gradually add bean flour as needed to thicken
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Serve white, or give it color with two egg yolks
