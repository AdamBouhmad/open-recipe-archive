## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 15 egg yolks, cooked
- syrup
- candied fruit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar
2. Mix with fifteen cooked egg yolks until a paste forms
3. Arrange in layers over candied fruit or sponge cake
4. Once three layers are covered, pour syrup over them until soaked
5. Place over two fires or bake in the oven to set
6. Sprinkle with dragée on top
