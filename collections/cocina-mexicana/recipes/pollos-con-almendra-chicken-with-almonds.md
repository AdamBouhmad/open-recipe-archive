## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Lard
- Water
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Garlic
- Almonds with shell
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Fried bread
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and place in a casserole with ham and chorizo slices and a spoonful of lard
2. Fry well and add water
3. Chop two or three tomatoes, onion and garlic, fry in a separate pan
4. Toast almonds with shell and grind them well
5. Fry the almonds very well with the tomato mixture
6. Add to the chicken casserole
7. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper
8. Add oregano and thyme
9. Fry bread slices in lard, grind them and add to the casserole
10. When serving, add a little sherry wine
11. The broth should be thick
12. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers
