## Ingredients

- media libra de arroz (half pound rice)
- agua (water)
- leche (milk)
- azúcar (sugar)
- ocho yemas de huevo (eight egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound of rice with warm water and put it in a clean napkin washed without soap, tie it and let all the milk drain
2. The next day put water to boil in a pot and when it comes to a boil put the rice inside the napkin, letting it boil two full hours to cook
3. After that take the rice out of there, put it in the milk and break it up with a spoon
4. Return to the heat and as soon as it is boiling add the sugar
5. When it is starting to set, dissolve eight egg yolks in a little milk, sweeten it and add it to the rice so it finishes acquiring the proper consistency
6. Empty it then into the dish in which it is to be served and at the time of serving it with ground cinnamon
