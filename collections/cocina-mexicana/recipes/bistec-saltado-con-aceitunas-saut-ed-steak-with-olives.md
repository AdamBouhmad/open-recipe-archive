## Ingredients

- beef steak, prepared as in the previous article
- pitted olives, turned

## Directions

1. At the moment of serving, add the pitted olives to the steak
2. Fry them there, making them sauté
3. Arrange on a plate
4. Pour the jelly or consumed broth with the olives over the top
