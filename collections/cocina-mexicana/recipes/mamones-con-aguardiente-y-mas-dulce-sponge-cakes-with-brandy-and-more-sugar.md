## Ingredients

- eggs with whites beaten
- fourteen ounces of sugar
- twenty ounces of starch
- one small glass of refined Spanish brandy

## Directions

1. Make also as the mamones from the previous articles, with the difference that fourteen ounces of sugar and twenty ounces of starch will be added, and when the whites are beaten stiff, mix in a small glass of refined Spanish brandy.
