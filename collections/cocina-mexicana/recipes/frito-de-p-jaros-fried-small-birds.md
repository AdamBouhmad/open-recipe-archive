## Ingredients

- Small birds
- Butter
- Salt
- Thyme
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds.
2. Cut off the legs and half of the wings.
3. Fry them whole in butter with salt, thyme, pepper, and chopped parsley.
