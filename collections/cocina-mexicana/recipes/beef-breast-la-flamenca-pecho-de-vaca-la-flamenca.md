## Ingredients

- beef shoulder or breast
- slices of ham
- carrots
- onions
- mixed herbs bouquet
- beef consommé
- sugar
- lettuce
- cabbage
- large onions

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat by stuffing the interior with mint and folding into a ball shape, tying with thread
2. Place in a braising pan with slices of ham, carrots, onions, and a mixed herb bouquet
3. Moisten with beef consommé and cook until done
4. Drain the meat and strain the cooking liquid, removing fat
5. Reduce the liquid to a semi-gelatin consistency
6. Place the meat back in the liquid and simmer gently with heat from above and below
7. Serve garnished à la Flamenca style: surrounded by carrots and onions cut into stick shapes and cooked in concentrated broth with a little sugar, and lettuce and cabbage cooked in a braising pan, arranged in a crown shape
8. Garnish the top with fried and golden large onions
