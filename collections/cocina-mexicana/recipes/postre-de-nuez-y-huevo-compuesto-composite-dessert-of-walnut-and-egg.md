## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one pound of ground walnuts
- ten egg yolks
- sugar, sifted to taste
- butter
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- pieces of candied citron
- toasted sesame
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Make syrup with one pound of sugar
2. Mix away from heat another pound of ground walnuts
3. Return to fire and as soon as the paste becomes quite thick but without allowing it to reach point, remove it
4. Beat ten egg yolks until they harden, mixing with sifted sugar to taste
5. In a platter well buttered place a layer with half of the beaten yolks
6. Place on top another layer of the walnut paste, which is decorated with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, pieces of candied citron, toasted sesame, and cinnamon powder
7. Cover this layer with the remaining walnut paste, and on top of all pour the remaining half of the beaten yolks from the first layer, sprinkling sesame on top after everything is well flattened
8. Put at two gentle fires to brown, and as soon as it cools, powder with cinnamon
