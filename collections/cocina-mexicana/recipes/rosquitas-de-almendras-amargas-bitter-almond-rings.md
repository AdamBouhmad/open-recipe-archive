## Ingredients

- one handful of ground bitter almonds
- two egg whites
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one handful of ground bitter almonds with two egg whites and sugar, making a small dough
2. Form the rings or balls and place them on paper
3. Bake them in a domed oven or between two fires
