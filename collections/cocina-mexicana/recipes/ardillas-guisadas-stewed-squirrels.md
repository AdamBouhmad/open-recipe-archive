## Ingredients

- squirrels with skin
- water
- salt
- garlic
- onions
- parsley
- xitomate
- raisins
- bread fried in lard

## Directions

1. Open the squirrels without removing their skin, disembowel, clean and wash them on the inside
2. Place them to cook in salted water, leaving the skin on
3. When the meat is tender, skin them and clean them
4. Cut the meat into small pieces
5. Fry minced garlic, onions, parsley and xitomate very finely
6. Add the meat to the fried ingredients with a little water or broth
7. Add raisins and bread fried in lard, both ground together
8. Add clove, pepper and cinnamon with a little vinegar and oil
9. Let the broth reduce until it reaches regular consistency
