## Ingredients

- three or four pounds good sugar
- good apple jelly
- good olive oil

## Directions

1. Clarify three or four pounds of good sugar to which good apple jelly is added to give it paste-like consistency and prevent it from crumbling
2. Put only about half a pound at a time in the pan
3. Cook until it becomes caramelized
4. Pour in small quantities onto a marble slab rubbed with good olive oil
5. Form small pieces like hazelnuts
6. While the mass is still hot, form a ball that is lengthened
7. Using a glass tube half a foot long and the thickness of a goose feather, oiled at its end, shape the bottles
