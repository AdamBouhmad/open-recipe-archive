## Ingredients

- one egg yolk
- pepper
- salt
- a few drops of vinegar
- olive oil
- one tablespoon

## Directions

1. Place one egg yolk, pepper, salt and a few drops of vinegar in a small bowl
2. Stir, turn and mix everything well
3. Add one tablespoon of olive oil drop by drop without ceasing to stir
4. Once the sauce is curdled and in sufficient quantity, add vinegar gently without stopping to turn the sauce
5. This is very delicate but patience is necessary as it takes a quarter hour or more to be well made and well turned
6. It serves to cover all kinds of cold birds and can be made green by adding parsley or tarragon when adding the eggs
7. If made by placing the bowl on crushed snow, it takes a more convenient consistency
