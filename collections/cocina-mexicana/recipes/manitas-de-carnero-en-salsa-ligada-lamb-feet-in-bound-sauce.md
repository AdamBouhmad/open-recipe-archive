## Ingredients

- lamb feet
- white light broth
- bound sauce
- concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Well scalded lamb feet should be deboned up to the joint
2. Parboil, refresh and remove the tuft of hair in the cleft
3. Place to cook over low heat in white light broth for four hours
4. Test to see if they are cooked, which is known when the meat yields easily to finger pressure or feels soft and tender
5. When done, remove and place to drain and arrange the extremities so the feet remain whole and clean
6. Place in the pan and add six ladle-fulls of bound sauce and four of concentrated broth
7. Let consume until only half remains
