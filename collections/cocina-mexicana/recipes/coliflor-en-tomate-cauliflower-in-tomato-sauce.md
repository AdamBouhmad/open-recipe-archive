## Ingredients

- ham slices
- butter
- chorizo slices
- longaniza slices
- cloves
- pepper
- saffron
- parsley
- broth
- salt
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- cooked cauliflower
- oil
- small hot peppers
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry ham slices in the butter they release over heat to make cracklings
2. Add more butter and fry already-cooked sliced chorizo and longaniza
3. Add ground cloves, pepper, saffron, and chopped parsley
4. Moisten with broth and season with salt
5. Add beaten egg yolks cut with vinegar, and as soon as it comes to a boil, place the already-cooked cauliflower in this sauce
6. Allow to boil until the dish is completely done
7. When removing from heat, add oil with small hot peppers and olives
