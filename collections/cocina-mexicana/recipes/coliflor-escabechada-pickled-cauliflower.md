## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- parsley, minced
- onion
- bread crumbs
- fresh cheese
- pepper
- salt powder
- vinegar
- oil
- butter
- olives, minced
- capers
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower the day before and drain it the next day
2. Mince finely the parsley and onion
3. Add to it bread crumbs, fresh cheese, pepper, powdered salt
4. Add a cup of vinegar and oil
5. Grease a paper with butter and place it inside a pan
6. Place a layer of cauliflower, another of cheese and bread, the oil and vinegar, salt, pepper, minced olives, capers, chiles in vinegar in thin slices, parsley
7. Add a layer of all this on top and place on two slow fires
8. When serving, place chiles, olives and capers on top
