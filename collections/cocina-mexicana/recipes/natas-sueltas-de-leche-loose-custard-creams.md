## Ingredients

- sixteen egg yolks
- eight quartillos of milk
- one pound of sugar
- two ounces of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sixteen egg yolks in the eight quartillos of milk with the pound of sugar and two ounces of cornstarch
2. Strain all of it and place over fire until it reaches the proper consistency
