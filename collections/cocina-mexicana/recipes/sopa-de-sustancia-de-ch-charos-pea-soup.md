## Ingredients

- peas
- good broth
- carrots
- onions
- bread crumbs
- butter
- spinach juice

## Directions

1. Cook peas in good broth with some carrots and onions
2. Pass the cooked peas through a sieve to make a substance
3. Fry bread crumbs cut into cube shapes in butter until golden
4. Pour the pea substance over the fried bread cubes
5. To preserve the green color, add juice extracted from squeezed blanched and chopped spinach
