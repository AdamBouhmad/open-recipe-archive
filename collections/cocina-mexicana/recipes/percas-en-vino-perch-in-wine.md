## Ingredients

- perch
- water
- white wine
- broth
- bay leaf
- garlic clove
- parsley bouquet
- cloves
- salt
- butter
- flour
- black pepper
- nutmeg
- anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Scald the perch with boiling water
2. Remove the gills
3. Place in a pot with equal parts white wine and broth, a bay leaf, a garlic clove, a parsley bouquet, two cloves, and salt
4. Let cook
5. Pass the cooking liquid through a sieve
6. In a pot, knead together a little butter with flour
7. Moisten with the perch cooking liquid, stirring the sauce until it is bound and cooked
8. Add black pepper, a little grated nutmeg, and anchovy butter (about the size of a pigeon egg)
9. Drain the perch, place them in this sauce and serve
