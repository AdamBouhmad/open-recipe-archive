## Ingredients

- Pure cream from milk
- Egg yolks
- Powdered sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mix pure milk cream with egg yolks and the necessary powdered sugar to achieve good sweetness
2. Beat everything together until the mixture becomes thin
3. Pour it into a baking dish rubbed with butter to prevent sticking, leaving it one finger thick
4. Place it on the stove with little heat and let it set
5. After it has cooled, cut it with a knife and form strips, being careful not to break them
