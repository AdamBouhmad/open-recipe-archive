## Ingredients

- sheep brains
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- onion

## Directions

1. Take sheep brains and two hard-boiled eggs; cook the brains with salt, break them apart, and clean the hard-boiled eggs which are chopped.
2. Fry onion and add all the chopped ingredients to it, fry a little, make balls of the desired shape, bread them and fry them.
