## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic
- Almonds
- Egg yolk
- Two to three ancho chiles
- Chickens
- Chicken broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry a clove of garlic in lard and remove it
2. In the same lard fry some almonds and an egg yolk well ground with two or three ancho chiles
3. Add the chickens and the broth in which they were cooked
4. Season with corresponding salt
