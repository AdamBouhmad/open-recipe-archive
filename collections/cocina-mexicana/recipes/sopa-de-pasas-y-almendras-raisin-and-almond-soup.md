## Ingredients

- Lard
- Twenty-five to thirty almonds
- Twenty-five to thirty large raisins
- Three raw tomatoes
- Onion, finely chopped
- Water
- Thin bread toasts

## Directions

1. Fry twenty-five to thirty almonds and as many large raisins in lard, remove them, and grind with three raw tomatoes
2. Fry a little finely chopped onion and add the ground mixture to fry as well
3. Add sufficient water and place thin bread toasts in a serving dish
4. Bathe them with the almond broth and bring to a boil until the soup thickens appropriately
5. Take care not to stir it
