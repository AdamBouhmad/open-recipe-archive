## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of cooked and cooled cream
- toasted and crushed sponge cake as needed
- 12 egg yolks and 6 egg whites
- butter
- clarified syrup
- cinnamon
- orange leaves
- pound and a half of sugar
- cinnamon powder
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Mix the cooked and cooled cream with enough toasted and crushed sponge cake to form a very thick paste
2. Add twelve yolks and six whites beaten separately until hard
3. Pour everything into a skillet lined with butter
4. Bake at two gentle fires
5. When set, pierce to the bottom with a popote and pour over clarified syrup boiled with cinnamon and orange leaves, made with a pound and a half of sugar
6. When the syrup has been consumed and the cake is dry and well penetrated, arrange on a plate
7. Sprinkle with cinnamon and decorate with pine nuts
