## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Virgin honey
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Biscuit

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with sugar and virgin honey
2. Cook until it reaches the soft candy stage, which is when threads form when stretched between the fingers
3. Add ground cinnamon, clove, pepper and crushed biscuit that can absorb the syrup
4. Make the alfajores between wafers or in paper cones, decorating as appropriate
