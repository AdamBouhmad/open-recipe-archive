## Ingredients

- raw chicken killed the previous day
- butter
- onion, minced
- garlic cloves
- flour
- milk
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- parsley
- eggs
- breadcrumbs or ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Roast raw a chicken killed the previous day, and after it is cool, remove all the bones and mince the meat very finely.
2. Fry in butter a little minced onion, two garlic cloves and a small amount of flour, then adding a quarter liter of milk and letting everything boil until it thickens.
3. Put the minced chicken, and season with salt, cloves and cinnamon, adding a little minced parsley.
4. Let the fricassee cool and mix in two eggs, stirring them in.
5. Take portions with a spoon, or give the paste the shape of balls, pears or other forms, which rolling in breadcrumbs or ground biscuit and coating with beaten egg, are fried and served.
