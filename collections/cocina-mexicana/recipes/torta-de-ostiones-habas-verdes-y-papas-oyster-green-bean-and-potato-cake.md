## Ingredients

- oysters
- green beans
- xitomates
- potatoes
- beaten eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the oysters
2. Cook separately green beans, xitomates and potatoes
3. Chop all ingredients
4. Mix with beaten eggs
5. Place over two fires with a little salt
6. When the cake sets, serve dry or with sauce
