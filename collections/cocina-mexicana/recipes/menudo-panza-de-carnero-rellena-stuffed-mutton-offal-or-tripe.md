## Ingredients

- Mutton offal
- Cooked chorizos
- Ham pieces
- Tongue pieces
- Pork jowl pieces
- Capers
- Mutton tripe, clean
- Lard
- Ground garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thread
- Water
- Thyme
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook the offal the same way as in the previous recipe and let the broth reduce
2. Add cooked chorizos, ham pieces, tongue pieces, pork jowl pieces and capers, mixing everything and making sure it is well seasoned
3. Fill another clean mutton tripe with this mixture, spreading it inside with lard, ground garlic, salt and pepper
4. Roll it so that the filling does not escape and forms a kind of pillow or roll
5. Tie it with thread and cook it in a suitable pot with water, salt, lard, garlic, thyme and marjoram
6. When well cooked, remove it, untie it and slice it
7. Serve the hot slices, sprinkled with pepper
