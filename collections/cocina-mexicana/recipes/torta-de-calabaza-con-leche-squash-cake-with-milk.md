## Ingredients

- One squash
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Milk
- Two quartillos of milk
- Orange blossom
- Handful of well-ground almonds
- Butter
- Mamón (sponge cake slices)
- Beaten egg
- Cinnamon and almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Cook a squash prepared like the previous recipe with sugar, cinnamon, and milk
2. Let it boil on a gentle fire until it breaks down and incorporates with the milk
3. Continue stirring constantly
4. Once cooked, strain through fine cloth
5. Separately cook two quartillos of milk with sugar, cinnamon sticks, orange blossom, and a handful of well-ground almonds
6. Remove from heat, strain
7. In a pan greased with butter, place a thin layer of squash
8. Sprinkle with milk and beaten egg
9. Then place another layer of thin mamón slices
10. Moisten them also with milk and cover with a thin layer of butter
11. Alternate the layers until finishing with a layer of squash
12. Cook over two fires
13. Serve hot with almonds and cinnamon on top
