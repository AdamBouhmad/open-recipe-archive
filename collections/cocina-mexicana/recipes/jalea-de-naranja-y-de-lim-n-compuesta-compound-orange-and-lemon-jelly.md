## Ingredients

- Three orange peels
- Ten oranges
- Three lemons
- Half pound of sugar
- Three strips of fish glue
- Water

## Directions

1. With a knife remove in small portions and very superficially the peels of three oranges and place them in a saucepan
2. Squeeze the juice of three peeled oranges and seven more along with three lemons over them
3. Clarify half a pound of sugar
4. Place over the orange juice the peels and the clarified syrup at the breaking point
5. Let warm the orange juice with the syrup and strain through fine cloth
6. Mix gradually with the infusion of three strips of fish glue
7. After beating a strip of fish glue, add it little by little to a saucepan with one and a half cuartillo of water
8. Let it dissolve on low heat for two hours and filter through muslin without squeezing or pressing it
9. Then mix with the warm syrup preparation, ensuring the fish glue incorporates well with the orange juice
10. Pour the jelly into small jars or cups placed on snow or in a cool place
11. When the jelly has set well, remove from the snow
