## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of fresh butter
- fourteen ounces of powdered sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- twelve egg whites beaten separately
- a little refined brandy

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a pot that has no grease
2. Beat for one hour and a quarter with a large wooden spoon
3. Add a little refined brandy
4. Continue beating for another quarter hour
5. Make paper boxes, butter them and gradually pour in what is being beaten, without filling them
6. Put them in an oven tempered as for sponge cakes
