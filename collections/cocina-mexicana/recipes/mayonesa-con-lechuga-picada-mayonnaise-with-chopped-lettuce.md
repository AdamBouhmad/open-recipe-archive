## Ingredients

- lettuce
- twelve egg yolks
- half a quarter liter of golden olive oil
- vinegar
- salt
- birds or fish

## Directions

1. Chop a little lettuce as for a salad and let it drain
2. Beat twelve egg yolks and while beating incorporate half a quarter liter of golden colored olive oil, not green
3. From time to time sprinkle with a little vinegar, continuing to beat until it curdles like custard
4. Season the sauce with the corresponding salt
5. Arrange on plates as follows: place a bed of mayonnaise on a platter, another of chopped lettuce and on top place the birds or fish already prepared
6. If the fish are white, they need only be boiled in water, and removing them without breaking, dry them well with a napkin and place them on the bed of chopped lettuce
7. Cover with mayonnaise and arrange the beds in this order as desired, the last always being egg
