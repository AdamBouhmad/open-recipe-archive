## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- minced meat filling

## Directions

1. After the tomatoes are cooked or roasted, remove the thin skin that covers them, remove the seeds with a small spoon, and fill them with any minced meat (see MINCED MEAT and FILLING sections), concluding the operation as for those in the previous article.
