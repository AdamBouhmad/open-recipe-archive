## Ingredients

- Twelve beaten eggs
- A pound and a half of cheese curds
- A pound and a half of coconut paste
- Two fine biscuits, crumbled
- Half a pound of butter
- Egg whites and yolks, beaten separately then mixed together
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Mix together the twelve beaten eggs, cheese curds, coconut paste, crumbled biscuits, and butter
2. Mix everything with egg whites and yolks beaten separately and then mixed together
3. Add raisins and almonds
4. Pour everything into a skillet coated with butter and put it in the oven
5. After cooking, simmer it in clarified syrup
6. Decorate with raisins and pine nuts to serve
