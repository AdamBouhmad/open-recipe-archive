## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked and sliced
- good oil
- parsley, chopped very fine
- onions, chopped very fine
- garlic, chopped very fine
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice or vinegar
- anchovies, desalted (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked potatoes into not very thick slices.
2. Place them in a pot with good oil, parsley, onions, and a little garlic, all chopped very fine.
3. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice or a splash of vinegar.
4. Let them heat and serve. This dish can be prepared with some desalted anchovies.
