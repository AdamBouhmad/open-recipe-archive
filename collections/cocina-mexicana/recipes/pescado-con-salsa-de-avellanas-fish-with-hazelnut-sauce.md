## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- water or white wine
- pine nuts or hazelnuts
- parsley
- garlic
- bread

## Directions

1. After cleaning, choose a good piece from the middle or tail.
2. Cook it with salt in water or white wine.
3. Soak pine nuts or hazelnuts, some parsley, a small garlic clove, and some toasted bread.
4. Grind all of this and pour it into the pan where the fish is cooking, thinning this mixture with broth, wine, or water.
5. Serve after bringing it to a boil.
