## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- one ounce of fine powdered chocolate
- four ounces of flour
- ten ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat and mix well in a mortar six fresh eggs, one ounce of fine powdered chocolate, four ounces of flour, and ten ounces of powdered sugar
2. Arrange the paste on paper or in molds
3. Bake in the oven
