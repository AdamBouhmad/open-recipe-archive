## Ingredients

- cod, desalted and cooked
- lard
- garlic, chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- tomato, finely chopped
- fine pepper
- oil
- chopped parsley
- flour
- beaten eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Desalt and cook the cod without letting it fall apart, clean of bones, and cut into regular slices
2. Fry chopped garlic, onion, and tomato in lard, adding fine pepper in powder
3. Add the fish and then good oil and plenty of chopped parsley
4. After some time on the heat, remove and mix with a little flour
5. Incorporate everything with beaten eggs and season with salt according to how salty the cod is
6. With a silver spoon, take spoonfuls to form balls or patties and fry in heated lard in a pot
7. After frying, serve either as is or with one of the appropriate sauces for cod
