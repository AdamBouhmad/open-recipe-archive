## Ingredients

- Four regular pork legs
- Six pounds of lard
- Three pounds of unfried butter
- One pound of deveined and soaked red chile
- Three large heads of garlic, cleaned
- Three ounces of toasted cilantro
- Half ounce of pepper
- Half ounce of claro
- Three or four quarts of good white wine
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mince the four pork legs into a coarse mixture
2. Chop the lard and unfried butter in the same manner
3. Grind together one pound of deveined and soaked red chile with the clean cloves from three large heads of garlic, three ounces of toasted cilantro, half ounce of pepper, and half ounce of claro
4. Mix all ingredients with the meat
5. Add three or four quarts of good white wine
6. Add salt as needed
7. Leave the mixture in infusion for three days, turning the meat two or three times daily
8. Take clean, not-too-thick casings and fill them with this meat mixture
9. Tie them well at distances of four fingers apart
10. Prick them with a needle so that air escapes from places that were not well filled
