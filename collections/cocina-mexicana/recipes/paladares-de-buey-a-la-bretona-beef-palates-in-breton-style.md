## Ingredients

- beef palates
- onion broth
- toasted bread slices
- gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare, cook and drain as in the previous recipe.
2. Serve on an onion broth or stock, placing a fried toast between each piece of palate, and covering everything with gelatin.
