## Ingredients

- pheasant
- ham strips, medium-sized
- salt
- black pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- ham slice
- string
- vegetables of all kinds
- herb bouquet
- beef slices
- half bottle of dry Madeira wine

## Directions

1. After plucking, emptying, and singeing the pheasant over a flame, gather the legs and tie them together
2. Lard the pheasant all over with medium-sized strips of ham seasoned with salt, black pepper, clove, cinnamon, and nutmeg
3. Cover everything with a slice of ham and tie with string
4. Line a braising pan or pot with all kinds of vegetables and a well-stocked herb bouquet, along with some slices of beef
5. Place the pheasant on top
6. Cover with other slices of beef so it braises well
7. Add half a bottle of dry Madeira wine
8. When cooked (which you will know by pressing your finger on top), remove the pheasant and place on a plate
9. Arrange it well and pour over it as sauce the same cooking broth, after letting it reduce a little and straining it to clarify
