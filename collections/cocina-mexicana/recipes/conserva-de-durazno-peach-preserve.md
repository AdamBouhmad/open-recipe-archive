## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Hot water
- Cold water
- Clarified syrup with lemon

## Directions

1. Peel peaches and add to hot water to give them a boil
2. Place in cold water
3. Remove and add to clarified syrup with lemon until they penetrate well and reach good point
