## Ingredients

- spinach
- earth salt
- lard
- garlic cloves
- tomato, chopped
- onion, in quarters
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- bread soaked in vinegar
- fine salt
- cooked and peeled chickpeas
- chopped potatoes
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- good vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the spinach well with earth salt, remove and wash in cold water
2. Squeeze out the water in a cloth
3. Place a pot on the fire with lard and brown the garlic cloves in it
4. Add plenty of chopped tomato and quartered onion
5. When everything is well fried, add the finely chopped spinach and fry it as well
6. Grind pepper, clove, saffron, and bread soaked in vinegar and add it with necessary fine salt
7. Add cooked and peeled chickpeas, chopped potatoes, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
8. Season with good vinegar and a little sugar
9. Let it cook and reduce until dry and with plenty of lard
