## Ingredients

- wine
- good vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- saffron
- ground spices
- lard
- whole chickens
- olives
- tornasolé peppers
- lettuce hearts

## Directions

1. Make a small sauce with wine and good vinegar, a little sugar, salt, saffron and other ground spices and lard
2. Put whole, well-cleaned chickens in it
3. Let them cook and roast over two fires
4. When serving, decorate with olives, tornasolé peppers and lettuce hearts, divided in halves or quarters
