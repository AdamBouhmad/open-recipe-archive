## Ingredients

- mutton tails
- concentrated broth
- sugar
- parsnips
- turnips
- celery roots
- small onions
- pork fat
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. After blanching, cook the tails in concentrated broth with a little sugar, parsnips and turnips well turned, celery roots and small onions
2. Cook separately and at the braise the mutton tails with pork fat, cut in small cubes, then drain the tails
3. Put the pork meat and vegetables in thick Spanish sauce
4. Dress these on a plate, place the tails on top, and cover with gelatin made with the Spanish sauce
