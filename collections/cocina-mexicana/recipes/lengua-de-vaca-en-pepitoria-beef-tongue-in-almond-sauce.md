## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- water
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- parsley, finely chopped
- onions, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Clean well and put in boiling water for about half an hour
2. Pass to cold water
3. Once cold, prepare thin bacon strips seasoned with salt, pepper, spices and finely chopped parsley and onions
4. Lard the tongue with this mixture and prepare to cook
