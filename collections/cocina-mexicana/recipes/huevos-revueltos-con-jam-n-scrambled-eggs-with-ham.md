## Ingredients

- eggs
- ham, cut into small square pieces
- bread, fried in pieces

## Directions

1. Add sufficient amount of ham cut into small square pieces to the eggs
2. Proceed as with the previous recipes
3. Serve surrounded with small pieces of fried bread
