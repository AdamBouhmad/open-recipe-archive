## Ingredients

- oil
- garlic, minced
- onions, minced
- parsley, minced
- water
- white wine
- ground spices except saffron
- toasted and ground bread crust for thickening
- almoradux or mejorana
- rosemary
- whole capers
- olives
- tornachiles
- cooked cauliflower
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry in oil minced garlic, onions and parsley.
2. Add a little water and the bobo fish, which will be left to cook over gentle heat.
3. Add oil and white wine in the proportion of half a quarter of each for three fish, ground spices except saffron, and a toasted and ground bread crust for thickening so that it is neither thick nor watery.
4. Add almoradux or mejorana, rosemary, whole capers, and olives and tornachiles when serving.
5. The fish platter can also be adorned with cooked cauliflower, fried in oil with salt.
