## Ingredients

- Tripe
- Mulato chiles with a little ash
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Piece of soaked bread
- Lard
- Broth from cooking the tripe
- Cooked chickpeas

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe very well and then put it to cook
2. Soak mulato chiles with a little ash to enhance their color and grind them with garlic, pepper and a piece of soaked bread
3. Fry all the ground mixture in plenty of lard
4. Once well fried, season with a little broth from the tripe cooking
5. Add the tripe and add cooked chickpeas and let season well
6. If you want the dish to be dry, omit only the bread and add the fried chickpeas to the sauce after frying
