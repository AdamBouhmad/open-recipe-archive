## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- olive oil
- black pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean well and cook in water until perfectly tender
2. Remove the water and place in a pot with olive oil, salt, and black pepper
3. After turning a few times, add a little broth from the cooked dish
