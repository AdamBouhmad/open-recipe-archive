## Ingredients

- beef shoulder
- lard or fine minced fat
- scarlet tongue
- salt
- pepper
- ham slices
- carrots
- onions
- mixed bouquet
- reduced broth

## Directions

1. Debone a shoulder
2. Lard it with minced fat or lard and scarlet tongue
3. Add salt and pepper on the inside
4. Fold in the shape of a ball and tie with threads
5. Place in a braising pan with ham slices, carrots, onions, and a mixed bouquet
6. Moisten with reduced broth
7. When cooked, let drain
8. Strain and degrease its broth
9. Reduce to semi-gelatin
10. Place the shoulder there, rolling it with the gelatin and making it simmer gently with fire above and below
11. Serve garnished à la Flemish, that is, surrounded with carrots and onions cut in the form of small sticks and cooked
