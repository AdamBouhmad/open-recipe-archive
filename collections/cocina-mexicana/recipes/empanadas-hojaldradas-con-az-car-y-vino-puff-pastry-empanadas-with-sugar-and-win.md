## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Five egg yolks
- Wine
- Salt
- A little butter
- A small amount of sugar
- Sufficient water
- Sweetened and thick milk
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a table and add five egg yolks, wine, salt, a little butter, a small amount of sugar so it is not too sweet, and sufficient water to mix and knead
2. After kneading well, extend with a rolling pin
3. Spread butter on it and fold in half
4. Repeat this operation six times
5. Finally extend the dough and cut the empanadas in different shapes
6. Fill with sweetened thick milk
7. Cover with a layer of dough and seal, not at the edge as in common empanadas, but half an inch inward so the puff pastry separates at the edge
8. Decorate on top with small figures or ornaments of the same dough
9. Place on papers on baking sheets
10. Bake in the oven
11. When removed from the oven, dust with powdered sugar
