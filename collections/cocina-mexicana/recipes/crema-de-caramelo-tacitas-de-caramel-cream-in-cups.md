## Ingredients

- A lump of sugar the size of half an egg
- A little water
- A lemon peel
- 8 small cups or jars full of milk
- 4 ounces of sugar
- A little lemon peel

## Directions

1. Put in a pot a lump of sugar the size of half an egg with a little water on a lemon peel
2. Let it boil until the caramel is formed, which should have a somewhat dark color
3. Measure 8 small cups or jars full of milk and bring them to a boil with 4 ounces of sugar and a little lemon peel
4. Allow to cool
