## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- two quartillos of custard
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- three egg yolks
- one tablespoon of caramel-point syrup

## Directions

1. Bring two quartillos of milk and two quartillos of custard to a boil with four ounces of powdered sugar, three egg yolks, and one tablespoon of caramel-point syrup
2. Allow it to reduce by half
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Season and let cool
