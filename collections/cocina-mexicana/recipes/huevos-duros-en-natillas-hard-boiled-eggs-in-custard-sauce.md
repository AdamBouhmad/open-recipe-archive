## Ingredients

- Butter
- Parsley (finely chopped)
- Salt and pepper
- Custard
- Flour
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter in a pan and add finely chopped parsley and the necessary salt and pepper powder
2. Add a glass of custard in which a tablespoon of flour has been dissolved
3. When the sauce is of good consistency, bathe the hard-boiled egg slices with it
