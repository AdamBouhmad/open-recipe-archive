## Ingredients

- pumpkin seeds, ground to ten ounces
- one pound of sugar
- four ounces of ground coconut
- wafers

## Directions

1. Shell the pumpkin seeds, removing also the green inner skin
2. Grind them in such quantity that ten ounces result after grinding
3. Make a clarified syrup with one pound of sugar
4. Let it boil until it reaches the point of setting in water
5. Remove from heat and outside the fire add the ground seed with four ounces of ground coconut
6. Incorporate everything and return to the fire to boil a few times
7. Remove a second time and beat until it is about to set
8. Pour into small boxes with wafers, or onto wafers placed on a table so that the marzipans can be cut in whatever shape is desired
9. If the paste becomes too hard, sprinkle with water and return to the fire so it softens
