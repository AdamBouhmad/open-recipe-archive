## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- lard
- onion
- tomato

## Directions

1. Slice the ham to regular thickness
2. Put to boil in little water in a pan and place the slices in for three or four minutes
3. Remove and drain them
4. Heat lard and half fry the slices, then remove them
5. In the same lard add a little finely chopped onion and plenty of raw chopped tomato
6. When well fried, incorporate the ham slices and let them season
