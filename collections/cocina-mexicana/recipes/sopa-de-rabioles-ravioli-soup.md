## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon of lard (manteca)
- 2 times as much butter (mantequilla)
- a little salt
- water
- spinach or chard, cooked
- lard and butter
- onion
- celery
- grated cheese (Flemish if available, or aged cheese)

## Directions

1. Add the egg, a teaspoon of lard, twice as much butter, a little salt, and clear water to the flour and knead well until it forms eyes
2. Let it rest a moment, cover with a serviette, then extend it with a roller to make the ravioli
3. For the filling: fry cooked spinach or chard with lard, butter, and onion; grind a little celery and add grated cheese (Flemish or aged, if available), all to taste
4. Form small balls and place them at intervals on one layer of dough
5. Cover with another layer of dough and cut with a roller
6. Leave until the next day
