## Ingredients

- Twenty egg yolks
- One spoonful of sifted starch
- Lard for coating the mold
- Sugar powder (if to be served dry)
- Half-point syrup
- White wine or orange blossom water (if not dry)

## Directions

1. Beat twenty egg yolks as for royal eggs, and when well beaten, add one spoonful of sifted starch and beat again; put two spoonfuls of this in each mold, greased with lard, and place in the oven, hot and tempered as for sponge cake. As soon as they are well cooked, remove them and shape the crown by inserting a clove of spice; noting that if they are to be served dry, add sugar powder to the yolks while beating; but if not, proceed as stated, and after removing them cooked from the oven, place them in half-point syrup to boil with white wine or orange blossom water.
