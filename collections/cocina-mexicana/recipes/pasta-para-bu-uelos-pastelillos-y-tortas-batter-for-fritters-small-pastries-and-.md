## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt
- one quarter pound of butter or lard
- six egg yolks
- half a cuartillo of water

## Directions

1. Spread flour on a very clean table and make a hole in the middle
2. Add salt, butter or lard, egg yolks and water to the hole
3. Mix everything until it incorporates with the flour and knead
4. Add a little water - adjust the consistency harder or softer according to its use
