## Ingredients

- beef breast
- beef broth
- white wine
- mixed herbs bouquet
- salt
- pepper
- German sauce
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. After larding the breast, place to cook whole with a little beef broth, half a glass of white wine, a mixed herb bouquet, salt and pepper
2. When cooked, arrange on a plate, turning the skin over the ribs to expose the tendons
3. Top with German sauce and simmer briefly
4. Sprinkle with a little peppercorn
