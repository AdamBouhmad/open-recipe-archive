## Ingredients

- Squash
- Toasted sesame
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper in powder
- Grated cheese
- Sifted sugar
- Toasted and ground biscuit as needed
- Twelve eggs
- Lard to grease the pan

## Directions

1. Cook, peel, and clean the squash
2. Grind it and when well drained, measure two calderas cups of it
3. Mix with a little toasted sesame, clove, cinnamon, and powdered pepper
4. Add grated cheese and sifted sugar in quantities sufficient to taste
5. Add toasted and ground biscuit as necessary for the paste to be very thick
6. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately until well risen
7. Incorporate everything together
8. Pour into a greased pan or pot
9. Let it set over two fires, taking care it does not burn
10. When cool, arrange on a plate with sugar and ground cinnamon
