## Ingredients

- chayotes
- eggs
- butter
- ground cinnamon
- toasted biscuits
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. After cooking the chayotes, split them lengthwise
2. Remove the pit and pouch, and with a silver spoon scrape them so the flesh comes out and the shells remain whole like little cups
3. For eight chayotes, beat ten eggs, first the whites and then the yolks
4. Beat the chayote flesh well with a large spoon until soft
5. Add the beaten egg (reserving slightly less than half), a little butter, plenty of ground cinnamon, well-ground toasted biscuits, salt, and a little sugar
6. When well mixed, fill the chayote shells and place them in a buttered baking dish
7. Add some of the reserved egg to each one, spreading it well
8. Place over two fires until the egg on top is cooked and browned
