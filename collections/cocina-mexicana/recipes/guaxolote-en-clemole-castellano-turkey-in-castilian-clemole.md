## Ingredients

- Pine nuts
- Hazelnuts
- Enclosed walnuts
- Avocado leaves
- Wide dried chile pepper (deveined)
- Toasted biscuit
- Lard
- Turkey broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Toast pine nuts, hazelnuts, enclosed walnuts, and avocado leaves
2. Fry the deveined wide chile pepper until it reaches a high toast, and grind it all together with a little toasted biscuit
3. Fry well in lard, and then add the broth in which the turkeys were cooked with the appropriate salt
4. Let it boil until it seasons and the broth thickens
