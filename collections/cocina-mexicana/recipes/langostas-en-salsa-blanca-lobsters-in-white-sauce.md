## Ingredients

- langostas (lobsters)
- mantequilla blanca (white butter)
- peregil picado (chopped parsley)
- caldo de pescado (fish broth)
- sustancia de garbanzos (chickpea substance)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta (pepper)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)
- agraz (verjuice)
- caldo colado de hongos (strained mushroom broth)

## Directions

1. Put the lobsters to cook and fry them in white butter, that is, without letting it burn, with chopped parsley, fish broth, chickpea substance, salt and pepper.
2. Let them season over low heat.
3. Bind the broth with egg yolks dissolved in verjuice.
4. Add a strained mushroom broth and serve.
