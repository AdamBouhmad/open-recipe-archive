## Ingredients

- Codfish
- Salt
- Water
- Oil
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Cooked tomato (xitomate)
- Pickled chiles
- Broth from cooking the codfish
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After soaking for twenty-four hours, put to cook with two boils in a little salted water, dry and debone.
2. Fry in a pot with a little oil, abundant sliced onion, garlic and minced parsley, cooked peeled and ground tomato, and several pickled chiles in quarters.
3. Once everything is cooked in the oil, add sufficient broth from the water in which the codfish cooked; add pepper and the salt it needs.
4. Put on a very low fire, and when well seasoned, serve.
