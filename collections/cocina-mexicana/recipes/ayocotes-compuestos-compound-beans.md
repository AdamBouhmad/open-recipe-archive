## Ingredients

- ayocotes (large beans)
- tequesquite
- lard
- bay leaf
- thyme
- salt
- onion
- clove
- cinnamon
- pork scraps
- longaniza sausage
- poultry
- chorizo
- chorizones
- fresh cheese
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the ayocotes with tequesquite and then wash them
2. Season in the pot and set aside a few to grind
3. Place the rest in the pot with a little lard, bay leaf, thyme, and salt
4. Once seasoned, grind the set-aside beans and fry with onion, and add them to the pot
5. While frying, add a little ground clove and cinnamon
6. Separately, cook pork scraps and fry them, frying a piece of raw longaniza, poultry, chorizo, and chorizones, all fried
7. Mix everything into the ayocotes, noting that all scraps must boil with the ayocotes; the rest is placed on top when about to serve
8. Garnish with crumbled cheese, chipotle in vinegar, and rebozado onion and they will be very good
