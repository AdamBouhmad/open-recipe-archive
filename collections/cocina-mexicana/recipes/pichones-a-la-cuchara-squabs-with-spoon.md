## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Ham slices
- Sartén essence
- Crayfish
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Pass the squabs through flames and clean them
2. Place in a pot with butter, lemon juice, salt, and coarse pepper
3. Make them crackle in this seasoning
4. Place in a pot between strips of ham with the butter in which they were fried
5. Add a sartén essence or sauté essence
6. A little more than a quarter hour before serving, place over heat
7. At the moment of taking to the table, drain and arrange on the plate
8. Place a crayfish between each squab
9. Pour hollandaise sauce underneath them
