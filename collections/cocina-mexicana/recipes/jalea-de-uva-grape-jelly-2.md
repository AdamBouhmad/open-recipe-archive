## Ingredients

- Grapes
- Grape seeds
- Grape skins
- Whole grapes
- Preserved or candied sour cherries
- Figs
- Small lemons
- Tejocotes or pieces of candied fruit

## Directions

1. Do the same leaving some little seeds that are pleasing, some little skins that give taste, whole grapes, sour cherries in preserve or covered, figs, small lemons and tejocotes or pieces of candied fruit
