## Ingredients

- butter or oil
- eggs
- salt
- tomato, ham, or sorrel (optional)

## Directions

1. When the butter or oil is at the right temperature, break the eggs and add one after the other.
2. When fried, serve with salt on top or with a little tomato, ham, or sorrel.
3. Generally they turn out better fried in a pot than in a pan, because in the latter they can burn and lose much.
