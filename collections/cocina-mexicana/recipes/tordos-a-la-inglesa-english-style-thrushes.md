## Ingredients

- thrushes
- paper
- butter
- breadcrumbs (a little)
- salt (ground)
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning, coat the thrushes covering them with paper and put to roast.
2. Once they are tender, coat them with butter so they brown.
3. Add a little crumb of bread and ground salt.
4. Serve with spicy sauce.
