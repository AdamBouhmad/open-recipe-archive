## Ingredients

- hare, skinned
- vinegar
- salt
- water
- oil
- garlic
- clove
- cinnamon
- ham
- vinegar
- water
- spices
- white wine
- bread

## Directions

1. Soak the skinned hare in vinegar with a little salt and wash it well.
2. Pass it to boiling water to finish blanching.
3. Clean and dry well.
4. Cook in oil with garlic until it takes a golden color.
5. Remove and dust with salt, clove and cinnamon.
6. Place on the grill and turn until toasted.
7. Cool, remove all meat and cut into small pieces.
8. Cut ham into sufficient quantity and cook in little water with the minced hare.
9. When well cooked, drain the water and boil in equal parts vinegar and water.
10. Add all kinds of spices except saffron and let the broth reduce.
11. When almost dry, add a quarter or more of white wine.
12. Let rest a little and serve with slices of bread toasted in lard.
