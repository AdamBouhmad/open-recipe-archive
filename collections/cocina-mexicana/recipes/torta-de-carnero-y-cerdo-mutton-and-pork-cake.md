## Ingredients

- Minced mutton and pork
- Eggs
- Butter
- Pieces of tongue
- Pork feet
- Strips of ham
- Sliced chorizo
- Pieces of longaniza
- Pieces of sausage
- Pieces of egg
- Pieces of cooked cauliflower
- Capers
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the minced meat as previously described
2. Mix with sufficient beaten eggs
3. In a pan greased with butter, place a layer of the minced meat
4. Add a layer of pieces of tongue and pork feet, strips of ham, sliced chorizo, pieces of longaniza, sausage, egg and cooked cauliflower
5. Continue layering with capers, almonds, raisins, chiles and olives
6. Make the last layer of egg
7. When it has set, remove and sprinkle ground sugar on top
