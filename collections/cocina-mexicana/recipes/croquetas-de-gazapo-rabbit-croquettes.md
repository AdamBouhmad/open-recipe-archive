## Ingredients

- Young rabbits, roasted
- Béchamel sauce
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Nutmeg
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Roast young rabbits
2. Remove the strips and solid meat from the thighs
3. Cut the meat into dice, separating skin and sinews
4. Place in a saucepan
5. Reduce a medium ladle of béchamel sauce to half
6. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg and dissolve in the sauce without heating
7. Pass through a strainer so it falls over the cut rabbit meat
8. Season with black pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg
9. Mix the meat well with the sauce, which should be somewhat thick
10. When cold, form small mounds the size of a pigeon's egg with a spoon
11. Dust with breadcrumbs and shape as desired
12. Bread by dipping in beaten egg and seasoning with salt and pepper
13. Bread again and fry
14. Serve with fried parsley
