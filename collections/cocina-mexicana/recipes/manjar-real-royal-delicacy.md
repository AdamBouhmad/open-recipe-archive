## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- water
- clarified sugar syrup
- ground almonds

## Directions

1. Cook chicken breasts in water without salt
2. Once cooked, shred into threads and place to boil in clarified sugar syrup
3. Add plenty of ground almonds, bring to proper point, and remove from heat
