## Ingredients

- Young rabbit
- White wine
- Broth
- Parsley
- Onion
- Garlic head
- Cloves
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Basil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. When gutting the rabbit, leave the liver after removing the bile
2. Debone only in the middle of the loin and make a hole in the skin
3. Pass the front half of the rabbit through the hole so it is turned and shaped like a turtle
4. Tie it in this position with thread
5. Cook with a glass of white wine, some broth, parsley, onion, a head of garlic, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, salt and pepper
6. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve
7. Degrease it, mix in a little strained broth to bind it, and let it reduce to the consistency of sauce
8. Pour the sauce over the rabbit to serve
