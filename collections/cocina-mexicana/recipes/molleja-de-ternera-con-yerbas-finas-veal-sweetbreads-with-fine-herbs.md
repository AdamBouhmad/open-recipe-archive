## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Green chiles
- Butter
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Bacon slices
- White wine
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Chop very finely parsley, garlic, and green chiles
2. Mix everything with butter, salt, and peppercorns
3. Place with the sweetbreads in a pot with bacon slices on top, half a glass of white wine and the same amount of meat broth
4. Cook over low heat so it simmers slowly
5. When well cooked, skim the sauce and add a little clarified strained broth
6. Use this sauce to baste the sweetbreads
