## Ingredients

- 1 bottle white wine
- 1/4 quartillo vinegar
- 6 garlic heads
- 6 cloves
- 1/4 ounce nutmeg
- 2 bay leaves

## Directions

1. Place wine and vinegar in a saucepan over the fire
2. Add garlic heads, cloves, nutmeg, and bay leaves
3. When everything is about to boil, remove from the fire
4. Leave it on the coals for 6 or 7 hours
5. Strain the essence through a sieve
6. Preserve in well-sealed bottles for use in small doses as needed
