## Ingredients

- Three pounds of sugar
- One egg white for clarifying
- Two egg whites, very well beaten
- Lemon juice, from half a lemon
- A handful of rose leaves, tips removed and dried

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with three pounds of sugar, clarify it with one egg white and let it settle until the next day
2. Decant and strain through clean cloth, then place on the fire so it reaches the breaking point
3. At this point remove it, beat it quickly and without stopping beating, mix in two egg whites, also very well beaten, that have been prepared beforehand, the juice of half a lemon, and a handful of dried rose leaves with tips removed
4. Pour immediately into the pieces in which you want to mold the honeycombs, on a napkin, not removing them until they have cooled well
5. Decorate on the outside by attaching to the napkin with a little egg white, fresh rose leaves, pomegranate flowers and borage flowers
