## Ingredients

- Rice
- Sugar
- Toasted biscuit
- Eggs
- Lard
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook rice with sugar until well sweetened, ensuring it remains cooked but whole
2. Place it in a clean small basket to drain so no water remains
3. Form small pieces like little potatoes, pressing them firmly
4. Roll them in toasted biscuit
5. Beat eggs with egg whites
6. Roll the fritters in the beaten eggs
7. Fry them in lard
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
