## Ingredients

- kid (cabrito), one quarter
- lard (manteca)
- flour
- boiling water, two to three cups
- pepper
- salt
- bouquet garni (ramillete)
- tender onions (cebollas tiernas)
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Scald a quarter of kid. In a casserole, add a piece of lard and a spoonful of flour; when this dissolves well, gradually add two or three cups of boiling water, so that it becomes clear and well mixed. Place the quarter of kid inside, seasoning it with pepper, salt, a decorated bouquet garni, and tender onions. Let it cook. Half an hour before serving, remove the fat, thicken it with an egg yolk, and present it at the table.
