## Ingredients

- spinach, cleaned
- butter
- salt
- marjoram
- thyme
- mint
- onion, minced
- cloves
- sugar
- raisins
- bread, toasted

## Directions

1. Place cleaned spinach in a pot with sufficient water
2. Add butter, salt, a small bundle of marjoram, thyme and mint, minced onion and a few cloves
3. Bring to a boil
4. When spinach is half-cooked, add sugar to taste, a handful of raisins and a good amount of bread slices toasted by the fire
5. Let boil until well cooked and remove from heat
6. Serve very hot over slices of bread arranged in a small bowl
