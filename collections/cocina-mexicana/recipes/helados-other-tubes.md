## Ingredients

- thirteen egg yolks
- half a handful of ground and sifted rice
- a few ground almonds
- milk

## Directions

1. Beat well thirteen egg yolks, half a handful of ground and sifted rice and a few ground almonds in milk
2. Strain everything, sweeten and cook until it is as thick as custard
3. Let cool and fill the tubes
