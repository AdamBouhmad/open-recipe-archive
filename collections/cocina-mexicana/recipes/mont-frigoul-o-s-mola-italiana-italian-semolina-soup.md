## Ingredients

- four very fresh eggs
- half a pound of finest wheat flour
- half a pound of corn flour
- four ounces of superior cheese, grated
- one piece of cooked milk (lechecocida)
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. On a clean table, mix four very fresh eggs with half a pound of finest wheat flour and the same amount of corn flour, four ounces of grated superior cheese, and one piece of cooked milk
2. Make a very consistent dough and cut into pieces
3. Dust the pieces with a little flour
4. Divide with a chopping knife until as fine as semolina
5. Pass through a colander
6. Dry on sheets of paper
7. At the moment of serving, drop into boiling broth
8. Stir with a spoon to prevent sticking
9. It needs only two minutes of cooking
10. Pour into the soup tureen and bring to table with grated cheese separately
