## Ingredients

- Hens
- Ham
- Garlic cloves
- Cloves
- Lard
- Salt
- Slices of chorizo
- Slices of sausage
- Small pieces of pork loin
- Roasted and ground tomato
- Cloves
- Ground cinnamon
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Jerez wine
- Water
- Sugar
- Fried and ground toast (if needed for thickening)

## Directions

1. Lard the hens the same way as in the previous recipe.
2. Fry them in lard with salt, slices of chorizo, slices of sausage, and small pieces of pork loin.
3. Add roasted and ground tomato to fry as well.
4. Add a little cloves and plenty of ground cinnamon, raisins, almonds, equal parts Jerez wine and water, and a little sugar.
5. If the broth lacks good consistency when the hens are cooked, thicken it with fried and ground toast.
