## Ingredients

- Equal parts green pumpkin seeds, peeled and ground
- Sugar syrup at the thread stage

## Directions

1. Mix equal quantities of ground green pumpkin seeds and sugar syrup that has been removed from the fire.
2. Beat well and return to the fire, stirring constantly until it reaches the point of cajeta.
3. Remove from heat but continue stirring, and pour into a small box, filling it only halfway with white paste.
4. Quickly tint the remaining paste in the pan with cochineal or brick, and fill the box with it.
