## Ingredients

- eggs
- water

## Directions

1. Take the freshest eggs possible
2. Bring water to a boil
3. When it boils vigorously, add the whole eggs carefully so they do not break
4. Leave for two or three minutes depending on desired doneness
5. Remove from fire, leaving eggs inside one minute so the white sets well
6. Remove and serve
