## Ingredients

- quails
- grill
- butter
- salt
- broth
- garlic
- onions
- parsley
- dry wine
- fine spices

## Directions

1. After cleaning and drawing, slightly over-roast on a grill at very low fire for a short time
2. Put in a saucepan with butter and salt; fry
3. In the broth they left, fry six cloves of garlic, onions, and parsley, all chopped
4. Add a glass of dry wine and fine spices
5. Add the quails and let them braise and boil over low fire for about half an hour
6. Serve afterwards
