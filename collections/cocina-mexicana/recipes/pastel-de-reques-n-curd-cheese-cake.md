## Ingredients

- ricotta cheese from cow's milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon powder
- raisins
- almonds
- walnuts
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Form the cake with any of the doughs that seem best
2. Fill with ricotta cheese from cow's milk, beaten with egg yolks to give it color, and mixed with sugar and cinnamon powder so it tastes good, with raisins, almonds, walnuts and pine nuts
