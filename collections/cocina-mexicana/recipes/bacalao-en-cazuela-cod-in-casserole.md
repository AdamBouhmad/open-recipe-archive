## Ingredients

- cod
- bread crumbs
- garlic
- parsley
- oil
- chile
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the cod into pieces, putting it in a casserole so that it covers the bottom
2. Put over it a thick layer of bread crumbs, garlic and parsley, then another layer of cod, and so successively until the casserole is full
3. Pour raw oil, garlic, chile and water that covers everything over it
4. Cover the casserole and let it cook on low heat until it is almost dry, serving it in the same casserole
