## Ingredients

- 1 pound of ripe strawberries
- ½ pound of currant juice
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 pound of sugar
- ½ quartillo of water

## Directions

1. Choose ripe strawberries and extract the juice by passing them with pressure through fine linen
2. Unite the strawberry juice with half a pound of currant juice and the juice of two lemons
3. Add 1 pound of sugar and ½ quartillo of water
4. Dissolve everything well
5. Freeze
