## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of fine sugar in milk or water
- 28 cooked egg yolks passed through a sieve
- 6 ounces of ground almonds
- 10 raw egg yolks
- olor

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar well in milk or water
2. Pass the 28 cooked egg yolks through a sieve
3. Combine the egg yolks, 6 ounces of ground almonds, and 10 raw egg yolks
4. Mix everything together and cook until it reaches a point where it sets in water
5. Add olor
