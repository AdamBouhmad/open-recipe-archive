## Ingredients

- garlic
- oil
- tomato
- salt
- cucumbers
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry garlic with oil in a pan
2. In this pan add chopped tomato with the corresponding salt
3. Add slices of peeled cucumbers, letting them cook and fry until done
4. Transfer to a platter with all the frying oil, adding more oil and vinegar, more salt if needed, and a little ground pepper
