## Ingredients

- squabs, roasted
- adobo sauce, cooked
- egg whites
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted squabs in half lengthwise and simmer slowly in a cooked adobo sauce.
2. When ready to fry, drain and coat in a light batter made with beaten egg whites and place in hot oil.
3. Serve as is typical for such dishes, with fried parsley on top or underneath, as preferred.
