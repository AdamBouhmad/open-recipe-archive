## Ingredients

- oil
- lamb blood, freshly obtained
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat oil in a frying pan until boiling
2. Once the blood is fresh, drop it in by spoonfuls
3. It will come out like fritters and is served with pepper and salt
