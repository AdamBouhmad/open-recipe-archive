## Ingredients

- Fish roe
- Biscuit or toasted bread
- Aged cheese
- Eggs, beaten
- Lard
- Bread, ground
- Vinegar
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Lime, sliced
- Orange leaves
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Toast the roe at the fire, grind it with biscuit or toasted bread and a little aged cheese
2. Mix with beaten egg as if for frying and set the cake in a buttered dish
3. This cake is served dry or in spice broth, chile broth, or escabeche made with ground bread, vinegar, clove, pepper and cumin, adding slices of lime, orange leaves and a little sugar
