## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Steam the potatoes
2. Peel them
3. Mash them, adding lard and milk in which sugar has been dissolved
4. Let everything boil
5. Pour into an earthenware vessel and let cool
6. Continue the same way as for rice cake
