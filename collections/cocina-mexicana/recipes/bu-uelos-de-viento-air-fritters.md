## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- one and a half cups water
- one cup water
- half cup butter
- anise
- ten eggs
- five whole eggs
- five egg yolks only
- a small pot of water with anise
- salt
- tequezquite

## Directions

1. Dissolve one pound of flour in one and a half cups of water
2. Put a pot on the fire with one cup of water and half cup of butter and gradually add the flour with a little anise
3. Beat at the same time until the dough is well cooked
4. Set aside and once it is cold beat it with eggs added little by little; these will be ten, nothing more than five whole and five without whites
5. Also cook a small pot of water with anise, a grain of salt and what fits in three fingers of tequezquite; add two spoonfuls of this
6. Once the dough is well beaten, place it near the fire for about an hour
7. Then make the fritters in plenty of butter so they puff up
