## Ingredients

- Old poultry
- Water
- Ash
- Butter

## Directions

1. Place the poultry in cold water for twenty-four hours, into which some handfuls of ash have been added
2. After this time, wash, pluck, and gut the poultry
3. Leave them in this state for another twenty-four hours
4. Then place them in a pot to boil for a quarter of an hour
5. After this, lard and spit-roast them, rolling them in very hot butter when they are half-cooked
