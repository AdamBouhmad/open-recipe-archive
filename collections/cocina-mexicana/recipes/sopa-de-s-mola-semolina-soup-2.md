## Ingredients

- broth from the pot or meat substances
- semolina

## Directions

1. Cook the broth from the pot or meat substances and add the semolina
2. Observe that this pasta is more prone to clumping, so it must be stirred more continuously than other pastas
3. Half an hour is sufficient for it to cook
4. For semolina in milk, do the same, only using milk instead of broth
