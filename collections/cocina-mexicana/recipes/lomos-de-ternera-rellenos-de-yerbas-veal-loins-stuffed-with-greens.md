## Ingredients

- veal loin pieces
- chard or lettuce, cooked and fried
- spices
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- hard-boiled eggs
- candied citron (optional)
- clean twine
- roasted and ground tomato
- bread toasted in lard
- minced onion
- parsley
- oil
- small chiles
- olives
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Make pieces of meat from the veal loin to slice, and fill them with cooked and well-fried chard or lettuce, seasoned with spices and mixed with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, chopped hard-boiled eggs, and if desired, pieces of candied citron
2. After filling the loins, secure them with very clean twine and place them to cook in a pot until they become very tender
3. Then place them in a sauce made with roasted, ground tomato and fried in lard, with bread toasted in the same to turn it the color of cinnamon, very finely minced onion and parsley
4. When serving at the table, add a spoonful of oil to the sauce, and decorate the plate of loins with small chiles, olives and hard-boiled eggs cut into small pieces
