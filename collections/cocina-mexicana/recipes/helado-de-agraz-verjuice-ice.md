## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound degrained verjuices
- 3 cuartillos (approximately 1.5 liters) water
- 0.5 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Choose half a pound of degrained verjuices and express their juice in a pot, straining through a cloth
2. Add 3 cuartillos of water and half a pound of sugar to the juice
3. Strain again after having rested for half an hour
4. Freeze using the common method
