## Ingredients

- cream
- candied lemon
- double cream
- cream
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. First make a cream cheese (see previous articles)
2. Then pound a candied or covered lemon, which is dissolved in double cream
3. Mix this with the first cheese and let it drain again in a basket or small strainer to serve with cream and fine sugar
