## Ingredients

- foie gras
- earthnut truffles
- mushrooms
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- coarse pepper
- ground spices
- nutmeg
- butter
- Spanish sauce
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Poach foie gras in proportion to the number of shells to be served
2. Cut into thin slices
3. Do the same with some earthnut truffles and mushrooms
4. Add chopped parsley, salt, coarse pepper, ground spices, nutmeg, and a piece of butter
5. Half-fry everything in a pan over the fire
6. Moisten with a little Spanish sauce, thickened or reduced
7. Let the dish take on the proper consistency
8. Place in shells
9. Cover with grated bread
10. Spread with butter
11. Coat with breadcrumbs again
12. Brown in a covered oven until golden before serving
