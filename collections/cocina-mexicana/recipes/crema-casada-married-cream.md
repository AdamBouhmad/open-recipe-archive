## Ingredients

- A short handful of flour
- Finely chopped green lemon
- A little candied orange blossom, crushed in a mortar
- A piece of sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 quartillo of cream or 2 of milk

## Directions

1. Put in a pot the flour, lemon, orange blossom, and sugar
2. Dissolve all with 8 egg yolks (setting the whites aside in a clean bowl)
3. Dissolve the yolks in 1 quartillo of cream or 2 of milk
4. Cook this cream for half an hour over the fire
5. Beat the egg whites until well raised
6. Mix the whites with the cream
7. Pour into the serving plate and dust the top with sugar to cover well
8. Cook in the oven, which should not be too hot, or under a dome cover with heat on top
9. Serve when well raised and baked
