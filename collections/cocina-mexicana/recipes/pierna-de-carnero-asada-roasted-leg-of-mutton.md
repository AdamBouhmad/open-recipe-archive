## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lard or fresh butter

## Directions

1. After letting the leg air for one or two days in summer and two or three days in winter, beat it strongly on both sides with a stick to tenderize it
2. Place it on the roasting spit
3. The fire must be very vigorous at the beginning, and if the roast is progressing well, turn the leg frequently
4. Baste with lard or fresh butter and season to taste
5. Generally served with pan gravy, which can be omitted if not desired
6. When it begins to take on color, moderate the fire and continue cooking until you are certain that the exterior is well browned but some parts remain reddish inside, and when cutting the leg it is neither too raw nor too bloody
7. On average the cooking should last an hour and a half
8. Serve immediately upon removing from the spit
9. Serve the juices separately as gravy
