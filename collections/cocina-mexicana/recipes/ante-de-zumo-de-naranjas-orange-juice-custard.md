## Ingredients

- ripe oranges
- brandy
- powdered sugar
- sponge cake slices
- threaded eggs or red pastille

## Directions

1. Squeeze ripe oranges
2. In the juice add brandy and powdered sugar so that it becomes sweet and sour
3. Pour over slices of sponge cake
4. Top with threaded eggs or red pastille
