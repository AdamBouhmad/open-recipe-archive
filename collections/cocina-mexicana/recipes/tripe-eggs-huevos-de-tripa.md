## Ingredients

- onions
- custard
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut onions into pieces
2. When they are already fried, add a glass of custard
3. When well cooked, place slices of hard-boiled egg there
4. Leave until they are well heated, avoiding them boiling
5. Then they can be served
