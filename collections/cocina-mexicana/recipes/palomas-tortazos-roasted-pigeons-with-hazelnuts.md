## Ingredients

- pigeons
- butter
- salt
- toasted hazelnuts
- lemon juice
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean, and remove the belly from the pigeons
2. Brown them in a saucepan with butter and salt
3. When half browned, add crushed toasted hazelnuts and a little lemon juice
4. Add a pinch of pepper
5. Dissolve everything with two cups of broth from the pot
6. Leave on the fire to finish browning and take on the flavor of the ground mixture
