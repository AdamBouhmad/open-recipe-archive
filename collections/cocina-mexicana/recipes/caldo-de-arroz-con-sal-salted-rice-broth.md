## Ingredients

- Rice
- Onion quarters
- Garlic heads
- Fine salt
- Water
- Pepper
- Clove
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cut onions into quarters and crush two or three garlic heads.
2. Put them in a pot with fine salt and water.
3. When the rice has cooked, season with ground pepper, clove, saffron, and cumin.
4. Add a little lard and boil until it is seasoned without remaining very thick.
