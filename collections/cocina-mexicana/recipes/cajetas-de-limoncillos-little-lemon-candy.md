## Ingredients

- green lemons, halved
- salt water
- a small bundle of straw
- one pound of sugar for every pound of ground lemon
- clarified syrup at half-point
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Cut green lemons in half and cook in cold water
2. Once they come to a boil, remove the water and replace with hot water prepared with a little sea salt and a small bundle of straw
3. Repeat this operation three times after it comes to another boil
4. Remove from heat and change only the hot water until all the bitterness is removed
5. Grind the lemon and put in clarified syrup at half-point made with one pound of sugar for every pound of ground lemon
6. Mix well so it remains very dissolved
7. Add orange flower water and place over heat to boil until the point where the bottom of the pan can be seen through the paste
8. Although the lemons are yellow, cooking will make them green
