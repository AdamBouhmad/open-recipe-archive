## Ingredients

- 4 duck breasts
- oil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- onion
- parsley
- gelatin
- orange sauce

## Directions

1. Separate the breasts of four ducks, leaving the skin on
2. Marinate in oil with salt, coarse pepper, an onion and sprigs of parsley
3. One hour before serving, thread on skewers that are adjusted to the spit and cook for about half an hour
4. Remove from the skewers, cut into slices, leaving the skin on and place in hot gelatin without letting it boil
5. At the moment of serving, arrange on a plate in the form of a crown over orange sauce.
