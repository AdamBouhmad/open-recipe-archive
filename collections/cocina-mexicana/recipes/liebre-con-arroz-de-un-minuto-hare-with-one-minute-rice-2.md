## Ingredients

- hare
- butter or oil
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- black peppercorns
- cloves
- hot pepper
- rice
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the hare
2. Put a pot with butter or oil, salt, garlic, a little onion, black peppercorns, cloves and crushed hot pepper
3. Add the rice after rinsing it well with water and rinsing it again
4. When fried, add water so that the rice remains whole
