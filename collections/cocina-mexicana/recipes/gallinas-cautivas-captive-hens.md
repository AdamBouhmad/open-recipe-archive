## Ingredients

- hens
- livers
- sweetbreads
- chorizos
- ham
- sweet potatoes
- tomatoes
- cloves
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- cilantro
- vinegar
- pineapple
- sesame
- olives
- chiles
- saffron

## Directions

1. Cook the hens and chop in the broth the livers, sweetbreads, chorizos and ham.
2. Grind the sweet potatoes and tomatoes and add to the broth along with cloves, cinnamon, pepper and a little cilantro.
3. Once well seasoned, add vinegar, pieces of pineapple, sesame, olives, chiles and saffron.
