## Ingredients

- sugar (cooked to nougat stage)
- egg yolks (12 for each half pound of sugar)
- vino de madera (aged wine) (1 small glass per 12 yolks)
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the syrup to the nougat stage
2. Lower the pan from the fire and let it cool
3. Once cooled, add egg yolks in proportion of 12 for each half pound of sugar
4. Beat everything together the same way as for making nougat until it detaches from the pan
5. While beating the yolks with the syrup, gradually add aged wine at the rate of 1 small glass for each 12 yolks
6. Pour into small cups and sprinkle ground cinnamon on top before serving
