## Ingredients

- beef pulp, sliced
- lard
- ham slices
- chopped onion
- garlic
- chopped parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- mint leaves
- culantro
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- fried bread slices
- oil
- herbs

## Directions

1. Slice the pulp and place in a casserole with plenty of lard and ham slices
2. Add chopped onion, garlic, chopped parsley, oregano, thyme, mint leaves and culantro
3. Fry everything and then add water
4. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper and fried bread slices, add all together
5. The broth should be somewhat thick
6. Before serving, add a little oil and garnish with herbs as is customary
