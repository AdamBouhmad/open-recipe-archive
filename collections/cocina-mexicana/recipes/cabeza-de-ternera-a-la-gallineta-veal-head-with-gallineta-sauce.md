## Ingredients

- Veal head
- Gallineta sauce ingredients
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the veal head as in the previous recipe
2. Make a gallineta sauce and add the pieces of veal head before binding it
3. Heat slowly for a moment
4. Bind with egg yolks without letting it cook
5. Serve with lemon juice
