## Ingredients

- oil
- butter
- one head of garlic
- two onions
- four tomatoes
- salt
- water
- bread toasts
- aged grated cheese
- clove
- cinnamon
- onion

## Directions

1. In a pot, fry one minced head of garlic, two onions and four tomatoes in oil and butter, seasoning with salt.
2. Add a little water.
3. In another pot, place a layer of toasted bread, add some of the broth made in the first pot, and sprinkle with aged grated cheese and a little clove and cinnamon with minced onion.
4. Continue layering in this manner, cooking over two fires until the broth is consumed.
5. Serve with eggs fried in butter on days of abstinence.
