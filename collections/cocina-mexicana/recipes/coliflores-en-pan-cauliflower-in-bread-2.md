## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- cold water
- ham slices
- beef or butter fat
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning carefully, parboil in cold water
2. Drain immediately in a colander
3. Line the bottom of a casserole with ham slices, matching the bottom of the platter in which the cauliflowers will be served
4. Place the cauliflowers on top with the tails pointing up
5. Make a filling with beef pulp, fat or butter from the same, parsley, onion, mushrooms, all chopped; salt and pepper, without any cream or broth
6. Mix this chopped mixture well and introduce with a spoon into the holes that remain between the cauliflowers, so that no space remains uncovered
