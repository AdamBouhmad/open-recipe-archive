## Ingredients

- Ducks, cooked
- Lard
- Roasted tomatoes
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Saffron
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Marjoram
- Thyme
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Flour browned in lard

## Directions

1. Cook the ducks as mentioned and fry in lard
2. Add roasted tomatoes, garlic, cumin, saffron, cloves, and pepper, all ground
3. After everything is fried, add chopped parsley, sprigs of marjoram and thyme, capers, vinegar, and flour browned in lard
4. The broth should not be very thick
