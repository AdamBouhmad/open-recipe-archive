## Ingredients

- egg whites
- half a pound flour
- one pound powdered sugar
- a good handful glazed orange blossoms, reduced to powder
- a piece of butter the size of a walnut
- a little salt
- egg yolk for basting

## Directions

1. Mix in a bowl with egg whites half a pound of flour, one pound powdered sugar, a good handful of glazed orange blossoms reduced to powder, a piece of butter the size of a walnut, and a little salt
2. Make a consistent dough
3. Pass through a pastry funnel and drop onto buttered baking pans
4. As the dough comes out of the funnel, cut into buttons with a knife moistened in egg white
5. Bake in a gentle oven, brushed with egg yolk
