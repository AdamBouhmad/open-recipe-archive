## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- white wine
- syrup
- ground cinnamon
- candied fruit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Make a syrup at medium point with two pounds of sugar
2. When clarified and cold, mix with sixteen egg yolks beaten halfway
3. Place over heat and beat until it separates from the pan
4. Remove and place over layers of sponge cake moistened with white wine and a little syrup that was set aside before adding the yolks
5. The final layer will be of yolks with the syrup, and sprinkle with ground cinnamon
