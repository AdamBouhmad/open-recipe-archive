## Ingredients

- manteca
- cebollas
- ajos
- peregil
- xitomates molidos
- asadura
- agua
- sal
- vinagre
- lonjas pequeñas de jamón
- choricitos
- longaniza
- azafrán
- clavo
- pimienta
- cominos
- hígado cocido
- alcaparras
- tomillo
- hoja de laurel
- aceite

## Directions

1. Fry in lard the onions, garlic and parsley, all chopped, with ground raw tomatoes
2. Add the chopped offal that will have been cooked in water with salt, a little vinegar, small slices of ham and pieces of small chorizo and longaniza, allowing these meats to soften or render in the lard
3. Then add the broth in which the offal was cooked, having first seasoned it with saffron, clove, pepper, cumin and cooked liver, all ground together
4. Add whole capers, thyme, a bay leaf and oil, letting everything boil together until the sauce thickens and acquires a regular consistency
