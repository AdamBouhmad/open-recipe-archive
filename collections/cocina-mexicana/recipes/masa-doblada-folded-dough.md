## Ingredients

- flour
- butter in small pieces
- one ounce of salt
- sufficient quantity of water

## Directions

1. Spread the flour on the table and make a hole in the middle
2. Place one quarter of flour with five parts butter in pieces, one ounce of salt and sufficient water to make a fairly thick dough
3. Mix in small portions and then knead with all possible force
4. Use the dough no more than two hours after making
