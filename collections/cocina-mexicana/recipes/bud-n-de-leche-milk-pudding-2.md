## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- sugar
- a small cinnamon stick
- salt
- half loaf of bread
- doblen
- whole raisins
- toasted almonds

## Directions

1. Cook one quart of milk with its necessary sugar, a small cinnamon stick and a little salt
2. Add half a loaf crumbled
3. Then add doblen dissolved and add whole raisins, toasted almonds, a little
