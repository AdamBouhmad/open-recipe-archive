## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- small amounts of pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and ground biscuit
- thirty egg yolks
- optionally, very finely ground almond

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with four pounds of sugar
2. Add a small amount of pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and finely ground biscuit
3. Let boil until it has body
4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly
5. Add thirty egg yolks
6. Return to heat and stir until the mixture pulls away from the pan
7. Pour into a flat dish or platter
8. Leave until the next day
9. Shape into dates, wrapping them in cinnamon
10. Place on a board and turn them frequently so they do not stick or flatten
11. If desired, very finely ground almond may be added
