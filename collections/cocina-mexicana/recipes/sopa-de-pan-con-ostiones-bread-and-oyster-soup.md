## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- whole chile
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- wide chile
- oysters
- bread
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a soffritto with onion, tomato, and garlic, adding a whole chile with fine spices of clove, cinnamon, and pepper with a piece of wide chile to give it color
2. Wash the oysters in their own water and add them to the soffritto dry, because they themselves release much water after cooking
3. Place thin slices of bread in a baking dish
4. Add a layer of oysters and another of bread until finished with the oysters, leaving these on top
5. Be careful to add parsley
