## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- fresh parsley
- pepper
- clove
- capers
- oil
- vinegar
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Brown the bread in butter
2. Grind it with two or three hard-boiled egg yolks, fresh parsley, pepper, clove and capers
3. Add a little oil and vinegar
4. Dissolve this mass well in broth from the pot and season with salt
5. Bring to a boil until it thickens
