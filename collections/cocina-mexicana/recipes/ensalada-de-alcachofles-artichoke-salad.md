## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- raw onion
- oregano
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in salted water and remove all the leaves, leaving only the hearts
2. Put the hearts whole or chopped in a platter or salad bowl
3. Dress with raw chopped onion and oregano powder
4. Add salt, oil and vinegar
