## Ingredients

- Calf liver
- Flanders cheese or other good cheese
- Pot broth
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a calf liver well, let it cool and grate it so it becomes very fine
2. Add an equal amount of Flanders cheese or other good cheese also grated
3. Sprinkle with black pepper now and then while stirring
4. Dissolve everything in pot broth
5. Bring to a boil and when it has boiled a few times, arrange in bread toasts
6. Bathe the toasts well soaking them with that broth and put the soup on two fires until it browns
7. Afterward adorn as other soups and serve
