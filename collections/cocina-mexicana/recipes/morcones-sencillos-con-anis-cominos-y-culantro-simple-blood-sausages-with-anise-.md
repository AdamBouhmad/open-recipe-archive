## Ingredients

- salt
- garlic
- anise
- cumin
- toasted cilantro
- pork blood
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Prepare the bowl with salt, garlic, anise, cumin, and toasted cilantro, all ground
2. Receive the pig's blood in it and proceed as in the previous recipe
