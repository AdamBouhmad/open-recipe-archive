## Ingredients

- Duck
- Straw
- Flour
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Aromatic herbs in powder
- Fried garlic
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After the ducks are cooked with straw and the tail feathers are cut, clean and dry them with a cloth
2. Debone them and roll them in flour
3. Fry them in lard, then remove and place them in layers inside a pot
4. Between each layer of duck, add salt, pepper, powdered aromatic herbs, fried garlic, oil and vinegar
5. Cover the pot and leave to infuse for three or four hours
6. Cover very tightly with a wooden lid and leave for five or six hours
