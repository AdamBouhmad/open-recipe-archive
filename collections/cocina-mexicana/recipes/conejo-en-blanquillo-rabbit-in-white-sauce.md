## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Water
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter or lard
- White wine
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- Black pepper
- Egg yolks
- Lemon or good vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning the rabbit, divide it into good portions and boil in water with plenty of salt
2. After a couple of boils, remove it and let it drain; dust it with flour and fry in butter or lard without letting it brown
3. Add equal parts broth and white wine; add a bundle of fine herbs, a good handful of mushrooms, salt and pepper
4. When it has boiled sufficiently, remove it, let the broth cool, strain it degreasing it well and put it back on the fire until it cooks
5. Cooked, add a liaison of egg yolks beaten with lemon or good vinegar, stirring constantly so it does not curdle
6. When it stops foaming, remove it and serve
