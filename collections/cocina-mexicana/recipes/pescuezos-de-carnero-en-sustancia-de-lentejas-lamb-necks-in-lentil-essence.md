## Ingredients

- Lamb necks
- Onions
- Parsnips
- Warm butter
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lentil essence

## Directions

1. Proceed in all respects as previously described.
2. At the moment of serving, place the necks over lentil essence.
