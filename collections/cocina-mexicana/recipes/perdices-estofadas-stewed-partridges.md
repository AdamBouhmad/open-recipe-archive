## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Raw oil
- Bay leaves
- Lemon or orange slices
- Garlic
- Water or wine

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges.
2. Brown them on grills.
3. Arrange in a pan with parsley, pepper, salt, raw oil, bay leaves, lemon or orange slices, garlic, and enough water or wine to cover.
4. Cook until done.
5. Remove and serve.
