## Ingredients

- a dozen chichicuilotes
- salt
- pepper
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Clean a dozen chichicuilotes
2. Skewer them on brochettes and arrange on the spit
3. Place toasted bread underneath to catch the fat that drips from the birds
4. Once cooked, dust with salt and pepper
5. Serve on the toasted bread with the drippings
