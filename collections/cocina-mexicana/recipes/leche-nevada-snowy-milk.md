## Ingredients

- One cuartillo of milk
- One cuartillo of natillas
- Sugar to taste
- Sliced membrillo
- Gragea for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix one cuartillo of milk with one cuartillo of natillas and sweeten to taste
2. Beat with a whisk until it makes much foam
3. Pour this foam into a serving dish over a layer of membrillo slices of desired height
4. Sprinkle gragea on top
