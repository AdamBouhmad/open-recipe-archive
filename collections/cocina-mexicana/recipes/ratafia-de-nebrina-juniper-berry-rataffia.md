## Ingredients

- sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente
- eight ounces of nebrina (juniper berries)
- one dram of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Leave eight ounces of nebrina (juniper berries) in infusion for thirty to forty days in sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente with one dram of cinnamon
