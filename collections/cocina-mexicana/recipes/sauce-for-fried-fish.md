## Ingredients

- Flour
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onion
- Raw tomatoes
- Oil
- Water
- Fried fish
- Parsley
- Cooked onion

## Directions

1. Brown flour in lard with a little garlic and plenty of finely chopped onion
2. Squeeze raw tomatoes there, and when everything is well fried, add oil and plenty of water, letting it boil until the broth becomes fairly thick
3. Then remove from heat and place in the broth the fish already fried in flour and lard, chopped parsley and cooked onion
