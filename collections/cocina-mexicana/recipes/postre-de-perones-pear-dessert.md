## Ingredients

- Syrup made with eight pounds of sugar
- Twelve peeled pears
- A cuartillo of custard
- Half cuartillo of milk
- Half pound of clean ground almonds
- Powdered sugar
- Comal with fire

## Directions

1. Make syrup with eight pounds of sugar and clarify it
2. Set aside a little of the syrup, and in the rest, when it has risen in point, add twelve peeled pears
3. Remove the pears once they are penetrated with the sweet and let drain
4. In the syrup left behind, put a cuartillo of custard, half cuartillo of milk, and half pound of clean ground almonds
5. Make the paste with these ingredients and let it take the proper point
6. Once the pears are drained, arrange on a platter with the reserved syrup
7. Cover with the paste and let cool
8. Then sprinkle powdered sugar on top and cover with a comal with fire until a crust forms
