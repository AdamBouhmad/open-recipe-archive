## Ingredients

- Jitomates (tomatoes)
- Onion in wedges, cooked
- Cooked chickpeas
- Capers
- Olives
- Chiles in vinegar, chopped
- Fat olives
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Fried fish
- Oil
- Ground garlic (for cod)

## Directions

1. Roast some jitomates and wash them as for green chile; break them up on a plate and add cooked onion in wedges, cooked chickpeas, capers, olives, chopped chiles in vinegar, the same with the olives which should be fat ones, oregano, thyme, powdered pepper, vinegar, and oil.
2. Stir the fried fish into all of this.
3. If the stew is of cod, after it is cooked, clean it, heat oil in a pan with plenty of ground garlic to fry, and in this the cod is parboiled, which together with the oil is added to the sauce.
