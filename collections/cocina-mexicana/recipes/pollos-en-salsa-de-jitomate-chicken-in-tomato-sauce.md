## Ingredients

- chicken
- lemon
- ham
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. After singeing and removing the wings, neck and breastbones, place inside the cavity left in the bird some thin lemon slices.
2. In this state place in the pan, cover with pieces of ham, and cook over two fires for an hour.
3. When cooked serve with tomato sauce.
