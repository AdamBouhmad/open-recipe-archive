## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the trotters in water and clean them
2. Remove the large main bone while keeping them whole
3. Place in a saucepan with a good piece of butter, minced onion and garlic, salt and pepper
4. Cook until almost no sauce remains
5. Stir and remove from heat so they do not stick
6. Once cooled, moisten with the remaining sauce
7. Cover with breadcrumbs and place on the grill
8. Serve with piquant sauce that is not thick
