## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- butter
- thin strips of lard or ham
- fine herbs
- stuffing
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Flatten the ribs well and coat with breadcrumbs
2. Dust with salt and place with a piece of butter in a sauté pan
3. Cook slowly over low heat
4. When nearly cooked, wrap in paper leaves covered with very thin slices of lard or ham and with the thickened sauce
5. Add chopped fine herbs, some stuffing, and mushrooms
6. Place immediately on the grill over very gentle heat so the paper does not burn and does not become too dark in color
