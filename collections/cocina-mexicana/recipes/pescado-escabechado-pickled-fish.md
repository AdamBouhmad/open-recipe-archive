## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- vinegar
- lemon
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, cut into strips, and fry in oil.
2. When it is already cold, place it in a large, clean earthenware pot that is not glazed.
3. Fill it halfway with water and strong vinegar, add strips of lemon and green bay leaves.
4. After twenty-four hours in this brine it can be served or left until needed.
