## Ingredients

- loin
- ham
- tomato
- ground spices
- raisins
- almonds
- sesame
- walnut
- candied citron
- wine
- parsley
- hard-boiled eggs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook loin and ham and slice everything, fry tomato, ground spices, raisins, almonds, sesame, walnut, candied citron, wine, parsley, hard-boiled eggs sliced
2. Beat eggs as if to fry as many as are necessary, mix everything and place over two fires
