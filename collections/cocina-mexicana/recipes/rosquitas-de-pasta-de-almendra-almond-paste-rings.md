## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of almonds

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds from one day to the next
2. Grind them and add them to clarified syrup at mirror point, in the ratio of one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of almonds
3. Stir both things over the fire until the paste does not stick to the hand
4. Set aside and cover with a napkin, letting it rest a while
5. Form the rings with it, which if desired can be gilded with gold leaf
