## Ingredients

- Eighteen pounds of sugar
- One large jug of milk
- One pound of blanched and ground almond
- One pound of sweet potato prepared the same way

## Directions

1. Dissolve eighteen pounds of sugar in a large jug of milk, strain and bring to a boil, keeping it on the fire for twelve hours
2. Separately, dissolve one pound of blanched and ground almond in a little milk, and do the same with one pound of sweet potato in another portion of milk
3. Once the milk has boiled, add the dissolved almond first and let it boil again
4. Then add the sweet potato and stir constantly
5. When foam appears on the sides, add about one and a half quartillos of water on those same sides
6. Finally bring to cajeta point, which is when stirring reveals the bottom of the pan, at which time the candy will have turned the color of a braid (dark brown)
