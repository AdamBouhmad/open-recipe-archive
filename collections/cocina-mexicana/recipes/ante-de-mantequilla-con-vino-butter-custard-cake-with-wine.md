## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- six ounces of butter
- two ounces of ground almond
- a little ground cinnamon
- a little white wine
- sponge cake (mamón)
- butter
- water
- lemon drops
- white sugar
- gragea

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of sugar with six ounces of butter, two ounces of ground almond, a little ground cinnamon, and a little white wine, making a medium-point syrup
2. Layer sponge cake and sprinkle with syrup and wine mixed together
3. Wash a little butter well with water and a few lemon drops until very white, mix with very finely ground white sugar
4. Press it through a fine cloth to produce threads, which are placed over the sponge cake and sprinkled with gragea on top
