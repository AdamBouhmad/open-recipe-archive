## Ingredients

- papas (potatoes)
- cebolla (onion)
- longaniza (sausage)
- chorizo (chorizo)
- carne de puerco (pork)
- chilpocles en vinagre (smoked chiles in vinegar)
- aguacate (avocado)

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, peel and chop them
2. Fry onion, longaniza, chorizo, solid pork meat, and chorizos
3. Add the potatoes and let everything fry well together
4. Once fried, serve with chilpocles in vinegar, avocado, and sliced onion
