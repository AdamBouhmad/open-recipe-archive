## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Pork fat
- Anchovies, well-rinsed
- Coarse pepper
- Two garlic cloves
- Parsley
- Small onion
- Three leaves of tarragon or dragon herb
- Three leaves of basil powder
- White wine (one quarter liter)
- Broth (one quarter liter)
- Butter (size of walnut)
- Flour (small amount)

## Directions

1. Prepare the shoulder by removing the lower flaps or skins beneath the sirloin
2. Cut into strips for larding the sufficient amount of pork fat, mixed with well-rinsed anchovies that have been kneaded with coarse pepper, two garlic cloves, parsley, a small onion, three leaves of tarragon or dragon herb, and three leaves of basil powder
3. Lard all the meat with the fat and anchovies
4. Place the garnished shoulder in a pan
5. Moisten with one quarter liter of white wine and the same amount of broth
6. Let cook on low heat
7. Degrease the sauce, to which add a piece the size of a walnut of butter kneaded with a little flour
8. Let the sauce thicken and serve over the shoulder
