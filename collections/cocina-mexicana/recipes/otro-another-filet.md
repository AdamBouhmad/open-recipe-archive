## Ingredients

- one filet
- a piece of pork meat
- thyme powder
- pepper
- nutmeg
- two garlic cloves
- butter
- a handful of finely chopped onion

## Directions

1. Open the filet like a book.
2. Chop and grind a piece of pork meat, to which add some thyme powder and pepper and a grating of nutmeg.
3. Fill the filet with this meat.
4. Once filled, sew it.
5. Brown two garlic cloves in butter, remove them, and add to the butter a handful of very finely chopped onion.
