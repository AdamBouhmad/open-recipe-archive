## Ingredients

- two ounces dry aniseed
- three ounces green aniseed
- half ounce cilantro
- half ounce fennel
- sixteen cuartillos of brandy
- eight cuartillos distilled water
- three pounds two ounces white sugar

## Directions

1. Roughly crush two ounces dry aniseed, three ounces green aniseed, half ounce cilantro and half ounce fennel (referring to seeds not plants)
2. Infuse for two to three days in sixteen cuartillos of brandy
3. Distill everything in a bain-marie to extract eight cuartillos of liquor
4. Mix with eight cuartillos of distilled water in which three pounds two ounces of white sugar have been dissolved beforehand
5. Filter and store in well-sealed bottles
6. During distillation take care not to let any extractive matter pass through, which would make the liquor cloudy and milky
