## Ingredients

- goat udders (ubres)
- lard (manteca)
- oil (aceite)
- onion, one cup chopped fine, per udder
- parsley, half cup chopped, per udder
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot, make slices, fry in lard until golden, chop them, and add a little breadcrumbs for frying. Put oil and lard in a frying pan, and for each udder one cup of finely chopped onion and half a cup of finely chopped parsley. First fry the onion with a pinch of pepper, then combine all the ingredients, and then the udders which are left to steam in the pot, for if they heat too much they are ruined.
