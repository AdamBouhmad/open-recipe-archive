## Ingredients

- Two cooked beef palates
- Butter
- Whole pepper
- One ounce of grated Parma cheese
- Bread crumbs
- Grated cheese
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut two well-cooked beef palates into strips in the shape of macaroni
2. Place in a pan with butter, whole pepper, and one ounce of grated Parma cheese
3. Fry well
4. Divide equally among six shells after sprinkling with grated bread and cheese
5. Sprinkle with melted butter
6. Brown under a bell oven
7. Arrange on a plate and serve
