## Ingredients

- goat udders (ubres)
- water
- tomato (jitomate)
- onion (cebolla)
- garlic (ajo)
- lard (manteca)
- clove (clavo)
- cinnamon (canela)
- pepper
- parsley (perejil)
- vinegar (vinagre)
- oil (aceite)

## Directions

1. Cook the udders in water. Chop tomato, onion, and garlic and add to lard. Brown them. Separately, grind some garlic cloves with clove, cinnamon, and pepper and add to the lard in which the previous mixture was fried. Add the ground mixture and a little of the broth in which the udders cooked, a little parsley, vinegar, and oil, the sliced udders, and let them cook.
