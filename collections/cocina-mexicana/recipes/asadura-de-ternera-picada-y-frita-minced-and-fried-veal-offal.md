## Ingredients

- veal offal
- oil
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the offal
2. Mince it and fry in oil with ground garlic, clove, pepper and the minimum necessary salt
3. Mix in breadcrumbs, incorporating them well
4. Serve completely dry
