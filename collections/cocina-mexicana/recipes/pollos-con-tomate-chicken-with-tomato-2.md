## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Tomatoes
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Peppermint
- Garlic
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipes
2. Grind cooked tomatoes with some parsley, oregano, thyme, peppermint and garlic cloves, all well ground
3. Add to the casserole where the meat is
4. After frying, add water
5. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper with fried bread slices
6. The broth should remain thick
7. Garnish as the previous recipes
