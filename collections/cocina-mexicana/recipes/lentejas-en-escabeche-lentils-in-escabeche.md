## Ingredients

- lentils
- salt
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- water
- vinegar
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- biscuit
- chiles
- banana slices
- apple slices
- sugar
- egg

## Directions

1. Soak the lentils from the day before. On the day they are to be cooked, place them to cook with salt from the earth.
2. When cooked, drain them.
3. Place a pot with butter on the heat and in it fry clean, chopped garlic until it turns golden.
4. Add two or three onion heads cut into quarters and rolled in the butter.
5. Add a little water and some good vinegar.
6. Season with the necessary fine salt and grind in a little vinegar cloves, pepper and cinnamon with a biscuit, and add all of this to the lentils.
7. Then add chiles and slices of banana and apple, and let it boil with a piece of sugar.
8. When it has seasoned, beat an egg slightly, pour it over and stir well.
9. Drain immediately and serve.
