## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar
- peach sauce

## Directions

1. Cook in a pot with charred grass on the bottom and tequesquite water
2. When cooked, peel and weigh the pears
3. For each pound of pear, use five pounds of sugar
4. For longer storage, add two pounds of sugar clarified with a few spoonfuls of peach sauce, which is enough to bring it to proper consistency
5. Add the pears and bring to proper consistency over two days
6. The same can be done with bergamot pears
