## Ingredients

- one young hen
- butter
- egg yolk
- the hen's liver
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- breadcrumbs
- half a cuartillo of broth
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stuff the young hen as in the previous article, using butter in place of grated ham and adding a pinch of chopped garlic
2. Put on the spit to roast
3. When cooked, sprinkle with a little butter in which an egg yolk has been dissolved
4. Cover with breadcrumbs and brown at the fire
5. For the sauce: put half a cuartillo of broth in a pot with a little vinegar, as much as half an egg of butter kneaded with a good amount of flour, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
6. Let it thicken over the fire and serve with the hen
