## Ingredients

- veal pulp
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- chorizo slices
- longaniza pieces
- ham strips
- broad chiles
- garlic
- cloves
- cumin
- onion

## Directions

1. Wash and cut the veal pulps
2. Place in a pot with water, vinegar, salt, chorizo slices, longaniza pieces, ham strips, broad chiles that have been deveined and ground with garlic, cloves and cumin
3. Put to cook until done
4. Conclude the operation as in the preceding article, adorning the chops for serving with the ham, chorizo and longaniza that were cooked with them, and with onion slices
