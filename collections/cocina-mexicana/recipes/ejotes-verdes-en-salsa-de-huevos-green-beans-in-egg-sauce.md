## Ingredients

- green beans
- butter
- parsley
- egg yolks
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter with chopped parsley in a pot
2. Add the green beans
3. Stir and reduce the sauce
4. Before serving, add a beaten egg yolk mixture, diluted with lemon juice or a little vinegar
