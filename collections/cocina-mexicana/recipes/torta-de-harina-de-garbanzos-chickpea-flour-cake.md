## Ingredients

- ground chickpea
- cold water
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Grind the chickpea and sift it
2. To each pound of powder add two quartillos of cold water and season with the corresponding salt
3. Mix well so that no lumps remain, adding water or powder as needed so it is neither too thick nor too watery
4. Let infuse all night
5. The next day place a casserole on the fire with half a quartillo of oil
6. Stir the chickpea well
