## Ingredients

- one pound of veal loin
- ham
- four ounces of butter
- water
- dry generous wine, about half a small glass
- one or two bay leaves
- a little pepper powder
- salt
- two boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Take one pound of veal loin; make some small slits to put inside some pieces of ham; place on fire in a pan with four ounces of butter; brown over very low heat, trying it remains covered; put a small amount of fire on top and leave it until it has taken on a half-golden color.
2. Then add a little water, dry generous wine about half a small glass, one or two bay leaves, a little pepper powder; season with salt, and try to boil little by little for about one or two hours.
3. Add two boiled potatoes dissolved well in a little water, leave it a short while, and serve. It is a stew of good taste and substantial.
