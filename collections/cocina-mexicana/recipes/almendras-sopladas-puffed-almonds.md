## Ingredients

- one pound of shelled almond
- egg white
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces one pound of shelled almond.
2. Place in egg white with twelve ounces of powdered sugar.
3. Beat until snow-like.
4. Spread on sheets of white paper and place in the oven.
