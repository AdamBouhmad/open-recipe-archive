## Ingredients

- peas
- fava beans
- green beans
- oil
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cheese
- pork loin slices
- garlic cloves
- roasted tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cook peas, fava beans and green beans
2. When cooked and chopped fry in oil and butter with salt, pepper powder and a little cheese
3. When nearly done fry well and use to fill slices of pork loin
4. Tie with string to keep tight and filling in
5. Cook in little water with a bit of salt
6. Fry garlic cloves in butter and oil, grind with roasted tomatoes
7. Fry the seasoning until very reduced
8. Add the meat to parboil with the seasoning and a little water to season
