## Ingredients

- lard
- salt
- chopped beet, cooked
- chopped lettuce
- chicken
- chicken broth
- additional ingredients from prior recipe
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Prepare similarly to the Beet and Apple recipe
2. Use chopped instead of ground beet
3. Use chopped lettuce instead of apple
4. Chop the lettuce as if for salad
5. Sprinkle toasted sesame on top before serving
