## Ingredients

- Chervil
- Salad burnet
- Tarragon
- Chives
- Parsley
- Spanish watercress
- Mexican watercress or nasturtium plants
- A little cold German sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch in plenty of water over very high heat equal quantities of chervil, salad burnet, and tarragon, a little chives, parsley, Spanish watercress, and Mexican watercress or nasturtium plants
2. Refresh these herbs in plenty of water and squeeze them
3. Pound them in a mortar, adding a little cold German sauce
4. When everything forms a kind of paste, pass it through a strainer
