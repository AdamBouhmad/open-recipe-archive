## Ingredients

- Tight and compact cabbage
- Salt
- White salt
- Beef or mutton fat
- Oil
- Garlic cloves
- Cooked onions
- Cooked small squashes
- Cooked garlic
- Lard
- Green chiles
- Red chiles
- Warm water
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Dried chile pasilla
- Butter

## Directions

1. Select tight and compact cabbage, clean of large leaves and cook in salted water with a piece of beef or mutton fat
2. When well cooked, drain and chop into large pieces
3. Place a pot on the fire with oil and fry plenty of garlic cloves until well browned, then remove the garlic and fry the cabbage pieces until golden
4. Have cooked onions, small squashes and garlic
5. Fry eight or ten green chiles and the same number of red chiles in lard, then pass them in warm water and peel them
6. Fry them in the oil in which the cabbage was fried, place the cabbage in the platter and mix with the fried garlic and the oil in which the chiles were fried
7. Over this pour vinegar seasoned with salt and pepper, garnish the platter with the cooked onions, small squashes and garlic, sprinkling oregano and adding on top some dried chile pasilla toasted in butter
