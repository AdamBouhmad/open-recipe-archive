## Ingredients

- 3 pounds white sugar
- 2 chicken breasts, pre-boiled without salt
- 1 pound cleaned almonds, coarsely ground

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with 3 pounds of very white sugar, clarified until it is as clear as water
2. Add the 2 chicken breasts that have been ground, having been previously half-cooked without salt
3. Once it begins to boil and no longer releases white foam, add 1 pound of cleaned almonds, coarsely ground (not very finely ground)
4. Cook to the desired thickness, high or low according to taste
