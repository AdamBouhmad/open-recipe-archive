## Ingredients

- one dozen egg whites
- powdered sugar
- orange blossom water
- egg yolks
- milk
- orange blossom in syrup

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites, add powdered sugar and orange blossom water
2. Dissolve the yolks with milk and orange blossom in syrup and powdered sugar
3. Heat two quartillos of milk with sugar until it boils
4. At the moment it boils take some of the beaten eggs in a spoon and drop into the milk, letting it sit for a minute
5. Repeat until all is used
6. Remove the milk and then add the yolks stirring constantly to pour everything over the eggs
