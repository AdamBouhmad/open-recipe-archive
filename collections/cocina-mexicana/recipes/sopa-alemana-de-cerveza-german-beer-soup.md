## Ingredients

- Half a pound of white bread, crumbled
- Fresh butter
- One quarter strong beer
- One quarter red wine
- Chopped lemon peel
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Sugar
- Bread slices fried in butter

## Directions

1. Toast the crumbled white bread in fresh butter
2. Add one quarter strong beer and one quarter red wine
3. Add chopped lemon peel, cinnamon, clove and sugar
4. Let it boil lightly
5. Pour everything over bread slices fried in butter
