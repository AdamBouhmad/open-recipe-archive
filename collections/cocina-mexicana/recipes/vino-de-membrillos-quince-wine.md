## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar
- White wine
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean away the fuzz, cut into pieces and remove the seeds, crush well and press to extract all the juice
2. Clarify the juice over fire after adding two pounds of sugar, one cup of white wine, and two quartillos of water for every four of juice
3. Meanwhile make a syrup, incorporate it with half its weight of aguardiente, and with this mixture give strength to the fruit wine, which should be allowed to ferment beforehand
