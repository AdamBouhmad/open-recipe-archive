## Ingredients

- 20 healthy peaches nearly ripe
- 2 pounds sugar
- sufficient water
- brandy of 25 degrees equal to two-thirds the weight of the syrup

## Directions

1. Choose twenty healthy peaches that are very near ripe.
2. Remove the fuzz with a linen cloth and prick them all over to the pit with a large pin.
3. Dissolve two pounds of sugar in sufficient water, put it to cook until it acquires the consistency of syrup, then add the peaches.
4. Stirring them constantly, let them cook until they soften slightly and yield to finger pressure.
5. Remove them and place in jars; set the syrup aside.
6. When cold, add two-thirds of its weight of brandy of 25 degrees.
7. Shake the mixture well, pass it through a filter, pour this liqueur into the jars with the peaches until covered, cork with a cork, and on top a wet parchment, which is tied well around.
