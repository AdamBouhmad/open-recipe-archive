## Ingredients

- Sea bass, cleaned and desalted
- Cooked spinach, squeezed and chopped
- Toasted bread, ground
- Beaten eggs
- Salt
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Clean and desalt the sea bass
2. Fill with spinach cooked, squeezed and chopped, mixed with ground toasted bread and beaten eggs, seasoned with salt and pepper powder
3. Can also add grated cheese and cooked and chopped potatoes to the filling
4. Wrap in oiled papers
5. Fry in lard
