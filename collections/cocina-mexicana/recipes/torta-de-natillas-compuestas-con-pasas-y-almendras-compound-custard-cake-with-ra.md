## Ingredients

- Mamón soaked in milk
- Sweetened custard
- Raisins and almonds broken into pieces
- Cream
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place a layer of mamón soaked in milk
2. Add a layer of sweetened custard
3. Add a layer of raisins and almonds broken into pieces
4. Top with a layer of cream
5. Cook the cake and while cooking, remove from fire to add some egg yolks
6. Soak the mamón in the egg yolks and immediately place in the oven to set or cook over two fires
