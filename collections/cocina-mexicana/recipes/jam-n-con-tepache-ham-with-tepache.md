## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- tepache or pineapple water
- Jerez wine
- mixed ground spices
- oregano
- bay leaves
- orange leaves
- sugar
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. After desalting and cleaning, place to half cook in water
2. When half cooked, discard the water and immediately add a little tepache or pineapple water, a quart of Jerez wine, all mixed ground spices, oregano, bay leaves, orange leaves and half a pound of sugar
3. When well cooked, remove and let cool
4. Remove the skin, dust with powdered sugar
5. Burn with a very hot iron plate and garnish as desired
