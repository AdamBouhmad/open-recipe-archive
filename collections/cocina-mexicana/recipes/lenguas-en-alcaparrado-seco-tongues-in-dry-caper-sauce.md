## Ingredients

- butter
- capers, ground
- garlic
- cloves
- cumin
- black pepper
- veal tongue, cooked, skinned, and sliced
- salt
- ham strips
- small whole onions, cooked
- vinegar
- broth from cooking the tongue

## Directions

1. Fry ground capers with garlic, cloves, cumin, and pepper in butter
2. Add the cooked, skinned, and sliced tongue
3. Season with salt and add ham strips and cooked small whole onions
4. Add a little vinegar and broth from cooking the tongue
5. Let it reduce well over two fires until dry
6. Serve as the roasted tongues
