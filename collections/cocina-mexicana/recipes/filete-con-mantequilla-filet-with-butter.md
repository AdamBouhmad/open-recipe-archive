## Ingredients

- one filet
- salt
- pepper
- lemon
- melted butter
- fried or toasted potatoes

## Directions

1. Take one filet and flatten it whole until it is the thickness of a finger, and sprinkled with salt, pepper, and lemon, leave it until mealtime.
2. When the soup is served, place it on the grills and brown it, turning it from one side to the other.
3. Once browned, have melted butter and mix it with the substance that has distilled from the filet while roasting, and coat it on top, breading it on one side and the other.
4. Serve with fried or toasted potatoes.
