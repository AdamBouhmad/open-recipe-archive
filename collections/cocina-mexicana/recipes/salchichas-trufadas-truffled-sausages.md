## Ingredients

- beef pulp and fresh pork meat in equal quantities
- beef marrow cut into dice shape
- five earthtruffles cooked in Champagne wine
- fine salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Chop finely together beef pulp and fresh pork meat according to the number of sausages desired
2. Cut equal amount of beef marrow into dice shape
3. Chop five earthtruffles cooked in Champagne wine the same way
4. Mix everything together and season with fine salt and spices of the same class
5. Form these sausages from pieces of curled preparations or in intestines
6. Place on a grill over gentle fire, serving with lemon juice
