## Ingredients

- chicken
- oil
- veal
- wine
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. With a well-fattened chicken, after cleaning lightly fry over active heat in a pan with oil, generally with a little veal cut into thin slices.
2. Then add wine and when the bird is done, strain the sauce through a sieve, adding a little flour dissolved in butter.
