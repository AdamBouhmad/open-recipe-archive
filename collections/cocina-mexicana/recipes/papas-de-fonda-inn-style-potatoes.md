## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- butter
- broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes and slice them
2. Season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. Fry in butter and add a little broth
4. When it has thickened a little, add lemon juice
5. When it has thickened well, remove from heat
