## Ingredients

- four chickens
- half pound pork shoulder
- one quarter pound almonds
- a piece of bread
- four well-colored tomatoes
- lard
- chopped onions
- two or three garlic cloves
- parsley
- ground pepper
- ground cloves
- ground cinnamon
- chicken broth
- sherry wine

## Directions

1. Brown half a pound of pork shoulder in lard
2. Add one quarter pound of almonds, a piece of bread and four well-colored tomatoes; grind all
3. Fry chopped onions, two or three garlic cloves and a little parsley
4. Add all the ground mixture and fry it too
5. Add the chickens with their broth, ground pepper, cloves and cinnamon
6. Once boiling, add sherry wine
