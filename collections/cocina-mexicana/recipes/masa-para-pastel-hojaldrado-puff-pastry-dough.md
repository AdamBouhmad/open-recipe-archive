## Ingredients

- Flour
- Hot water
- A little sour orange juice
- Two raw egg whites
- A little butter or drops of edible oil

## Directions

1. Moisten the flour with hot water
2. Add a little sour orange juice, two raw egg whites, and a little butter or drops of edible oil
3. Knead well until the dough is very smooth
4. Extend with a rolling pin into very thin sheets
5. Stack the sheets one on top of another, greased with butter so they don't stick, using eight or ten sheets or as many as desired
6. Roll them up afterwards
7. The next day, extend and cut them while still joined together to form the pastry
