## Ingredients

- Chicken meat
- Bread crumbs
- Egg white
- Egg yolk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes and tendons from the chicken meat
2. Chop with bread crumbs moistened in egg white and yolk
3. When everything is well incorporated, season and form into balls
4. Coat with bread crumbs, dip again in egg, and fry
5. Serve with parsley prepared in the same manner
