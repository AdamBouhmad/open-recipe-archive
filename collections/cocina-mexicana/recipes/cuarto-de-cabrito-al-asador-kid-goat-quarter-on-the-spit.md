## Ingredients

- two hind quarters of kid goat
- onion quarters
- thyme
- laurel
- basil
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Join the two hind quarters, which are the best
2. Remove the main bones and membranes that cover them
3. Place in marinade or escabeche with quarters of onion, thyme, laurel, basil, parsley, garlic and salt for three or four days
4. Place on the spit and serve with pepper or tomato sauce
