## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- sea salt
- tequezquite

## Directions

1. Once cleaned, soak for three or four hours in sea salt and a little tequezquite
2. Hang them in the oven and light a small fire below; when well lit, throw dried wood so that the meat receives all the smoke
3. After two or three hours, put to cook in clean water
4. When they have cooked a little, let them drain
5. Place on a board and put much weight on top to press them
6. Leave them three or four days and they will be good
