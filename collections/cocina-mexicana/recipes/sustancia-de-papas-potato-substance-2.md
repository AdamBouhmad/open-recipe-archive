## Ingredients

- 12 to 15 raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel 12 to 15 raw potatoes, wash and slice them, and place them in a saucepan with a glass of water, a little butter, salt, and nutmeg.
2. Let them boil and cook over a stove at two fires for half an hour.
3. When cooked, mash them with a wooden spoon away from the heat.
4. Return to the fire and reduce as needed, and finally add a good piece of butter and a smaller piece of sugar.
