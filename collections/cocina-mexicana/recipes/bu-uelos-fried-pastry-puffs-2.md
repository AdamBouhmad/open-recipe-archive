## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 6 eggs with whites
- 2 egg yolks
- aniseed
- salt
- butter
- 1 plate custard, in place of water or milk

## Directions

1. The dough should be soft
2. Do not let it sit in the sun but fry immediately
