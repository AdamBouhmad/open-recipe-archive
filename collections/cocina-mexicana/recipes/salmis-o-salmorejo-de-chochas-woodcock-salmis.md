## Ingredients

- three or four woodcocks
- salt
- coarse pepper
- bread slices
- pan drippings
- bread crumbs
- half a quarter of white wine
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- two good spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and empty three or four woodcocks and place them on the spit
2. Finely chop the intestines with salt and coarse pepper, and arrange this mixture on toasted bread slices
3. Place the toasted bread beneath the woodcocks while they are cooking so they receive the fat that drips from them
4. After cooking and once cooled, divide them by separating the wings, breast, and thighs, which are placed in a pot with bread crumbs
5. Mash the remaining parts well and form a paste with them, then place this paste in another reduced pot with half a quarter of white wine, coarse pepper, nutmeg, and two good spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
6. Let everything boil together and pass it through a strainer or fine sieve over the pieces that were left in the other pot with the bread crumbs
7. Heat everything without boiling, add lemon juice, and serve
8. When arranging on the plate, place the toasted bread slices that received the fat, cut in heart shape
