## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- six egg yolks and four egg whites
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- one and one-half quarts of milk
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Melt four ounces of butter until it rises
2. Dissolve six egg yolks and four egg whites into it gradually, with salt, pepper, a little grated nutmeg and one and one-half quarts of milk
3. Add flour in sufficient quantity to make a dough
4. Take portions with a spoon dipped in warm water to form medium-sized balls, as they puff up greatly in boiling water
5. Drop them into boiling water as they are formed
6. Leave them in the water for about eight or ten minutes
7. Remove with a skimmer, arrange on a plate, garnish with fried potatoes and serve with sauce or fried bread toasts with crumbs
