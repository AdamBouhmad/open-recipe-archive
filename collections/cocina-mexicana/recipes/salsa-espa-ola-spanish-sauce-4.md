## Ingredients

- Strained broth
- One glass of white wine
- One glass of pot broth
- Onion
- Parsley sprigs
- Garlic
- Clove
- One bay leaf
- Two tablespoons of oil
- A pinch of cilantro
- One onion sliced into rings

## Directions

1. Place strained broth, one glass of white wine and one glass of pot broth in a saucepan
2. Add onion, parsley sprigs, garlic, clove, one bay leaf, two tablespoons of oil, a pinch of cilantro and one onion sliced into rings
3. Cook everything together at low heat for two hours
4. Skim immediately
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Season with salt and pepper
