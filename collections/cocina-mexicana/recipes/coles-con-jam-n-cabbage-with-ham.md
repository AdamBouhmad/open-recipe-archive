## Ingredients

- cabbage
- tequesuete
- anise
- salt
- ham pieces
- salchichón
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage and remove the first leaves, put to boil with a little settled tequesuete and anise
2. After boiling a quarter hour, remove from the fire
3. When cold, cut into quarters and put to cook in another water with a little salt, pieces of ham and salchichón
4. As soon as it has boiled enough, reduce the heat, making it cook well at low heat
5. Remove and place on a platter with slices of the ham and salchichón it cooked with
6. In the broth in which it cooked, add a little flour mixed with butter, and when the sauce has thickened a little, bathe the cabbage with it, dusting with salt and pepper
