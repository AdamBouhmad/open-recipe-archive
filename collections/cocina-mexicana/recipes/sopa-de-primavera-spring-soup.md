## Ingredients

- Same ingredients as recipe number 3
- Asparagus
- Small blanched onions
- Small radishes
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as recipe number 3, adding asparagus and small onions blanched beforehand, and small radishes
2. To remove the harshness of these vegetables, add a little sugar to the broth
