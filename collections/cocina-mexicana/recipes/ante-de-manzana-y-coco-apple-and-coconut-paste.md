## Ingredients

- Apples
- Coconut, grated
- Sugar
- Sesame seeds, toasted
- Cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the cores
2. Place in syrup, cooking them and preventing them from breaking by removing them in time
3. Place separately on plates
4. Pour over them high-point syrup mixed with grated coconut in the proportion of 1 pound of coconut to 3 pounds of sugar
5. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds
6. Dust with ground cinnamon
