## Ingredients

- A small bunch of fresh parsley
- A little water or pot broth
- Vinegar
- One crushed garlic clove
- Cumin

## Directions

1. Take a small bunch of fresh parsley and chop it well with a knife or crescent-shaped chopper on a board, or grind in a molcajete until it becomes a paste
2. Dissolve this paste in a little water, or better in pot broth
3. Add a small amount of vinegar, one crushed garlic clove and some cumin
