## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- one and one-half pounds of almonds, finely ground
- half pound of rice
- chicken breast, finely ground
- sugar
- fourteen egg yolks

## Directions

1. To four quartillos of milk add one and one-half pounds of finely ground almonds, half pound of rice, finely ground chicken breast, and sugar to taste
2. Cook and when at the proper point remove from heat
3. Add fourteen well-beaten egg yolks, stir and beat again like turron (nougat)
4. Return to heat without ceasing to beat it
5. When at the proper point, empty into a serving platter and serve
