## Ingredients

- one pound of fine flour
- eight eggs
- melted lard
- water
- aguardiente
- salt
- syrup

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of fine flour with eight eggs, a little melted lard, a little water, a little aguardiente, and necessary salt
2. Beat the dough until it makes blisters, then strike it on a metate or board, divide the dough into equal pieces, and place a clean napkin on the knee
3. Over it spread the fritters, being careful that the edges are not thick
4. As they are made, fry in very hot lard, and as soon as they brown, remove them with a clean stick and hang them over the lard until they drain well
5. Serve with syrup on top
