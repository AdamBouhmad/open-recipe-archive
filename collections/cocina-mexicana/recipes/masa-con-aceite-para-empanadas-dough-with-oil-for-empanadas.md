## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 ounce of lard
- a small cup of oil
- water in sufficient quantity

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with three egg yolks, one ounce of lard, a small cup of oil, and water in sufficient quantity
2. Knead all well and when it becomes smooth and consistent at the same time, extend with a rolling pin
3. Proceed as with the previous empanadas
