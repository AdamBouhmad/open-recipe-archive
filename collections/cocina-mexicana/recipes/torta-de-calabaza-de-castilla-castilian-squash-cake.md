## Ingredients

- One medium ripe Castilian squash, peeled and cleaned
- Water
- Sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon sticks
- Six egg yolks
- Butter
- White wine
- Syrup at half point
- Ground cinnamon
- Beaten egg
- Mamón (sponge cake slices)
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into pieces and cook in very little water with sugar to taste, cloves, and cinnamon sticks
2. Stir frequently until it breaks down
3. Once cooked, strain through fine cloth and beat with six egg yolks
4. Grease a cake pan with butter
5. Spread a thin layer of squash on the bottom
6. Sprinkle with white wine, dust with cinnamon powder, and cover with beaten egg
7. Place another layer of thin mamón slices
8. Sprinkle with wine and half-point syrup, add cinnamon powder and a thin layer of butter
9. Alternate layers until finishing with a layer of squash
10. Cover the pan with a griddle and cook the cake over two moderate fires
11. Serve hot dusted with cinnamon powder, almonds, and toasted sesame seeds on top
