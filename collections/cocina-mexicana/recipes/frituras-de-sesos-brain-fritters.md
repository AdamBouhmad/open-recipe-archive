## Ingredients

- two cooked brains
- one egg
- one tablespoon flour
- one tablespoon minced parsley
- two tablespoons minced onion
- ground pepper
- bread crumb
- cooking oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Place two cooked brains in a casserole, incorporate just one egg broken, one tablespoon of flour, another of minced parsley, two of minced onion, sufficient ground pepper, a small piece of bread crumb, a little cooking oil, salt to taste
2. Mix everything perfectly and place in a spoon to make small cakes that are fried in lard
