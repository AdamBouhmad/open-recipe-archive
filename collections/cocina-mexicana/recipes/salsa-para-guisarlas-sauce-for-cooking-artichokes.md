## Ingredients

- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- fresh parsley
- butter
- oil
- water
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop a little garlic and onion very finely
2. Chop fresh tomato without seeds and fresh parsley
3. Heat butter and oil in a pan and fry the garlic
4. Then fry the onion until both are parboiled
5. Add the tomato and parsley on top
6. Fry this well
7. Add a little water and salt, pepper powder, and thyme
8. Once seasoned add a little oil and vinegar
9. Remove from heat and arrange artichokes upright in a cooking vessel
10. Spoon the sauce over them and add more oil
11. Place over two gentle fires so they do not burn
12. Shake the vessel frequently
13. Make sure the heat from above is low so the artichokes do not brown
14. The little broth should remain thick, and serve
