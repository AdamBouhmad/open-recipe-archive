## Ingredients

- beef
- bacon pieces
- lard
- sorrel cooked

## Directions

1. Soak the meat after removing the skins well
2. Place small pieces of bacon at intervals inside
3. Put lard in a pot and when hot add the meat
4. The meat should not be stirred
5. Cover with an iron lid
6. Place over low heat, also placing hot coals on the lid
7. Prepare a sauce of cooked sorrel
8. Add the sauce to the sides of the meat, not on top
9. Serve, taking care not to let the juices evaporate
