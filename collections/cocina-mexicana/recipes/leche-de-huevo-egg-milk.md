## Ingredients

- two pounds of clarified sugar
- caramel
- four quartillos of milk
- five egg yolks
- two ounces of butter

## Directions

1. To two pounds of clarified sugar at caramel point add four quartillos of milk and five egg yolks
2. Cook until it reaches the proper point
3. When it reaches a high point add two ounces of butter
4. Empty as soon as it is cold
5. Place a comal with coals on top to brown it
