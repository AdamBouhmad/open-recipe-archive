## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- ten hard-boiled egg yolks
- ground sugar to taste
- butter

## Directions

1. Grind the flour and hard-boiled yolks together
2. Mix in ground sugar until the mixture is to taste
3. Gradually add butter until the dough is well compact, so it can be thinned much with the palms of the hands
4. Do not add water
5. Cut the biscuits giving them the desired shape
6. Send them to the oven
