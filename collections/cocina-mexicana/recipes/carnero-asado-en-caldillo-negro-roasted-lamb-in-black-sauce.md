## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- water
- salt
- lard
- tomatoes
- onions
- bread
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- parsley
- garlic
- fine salt
- vinegar
- capers
- caper berries

## Directions

1. Cook the unwashed meat with only water and salt over two fires
2. When cooked, remove the broth and brown it with lard, placing more fire above than below
3. Grind several tomatoes and onions, fry well in lard
4. Toast a piece of bread well and grind it with a little clove, pepper and cinnamon
5. Add finely chopped onion, parsley and garlic, and dissolve in water, frying everything with the tomato
6. Add corresponding fine salt, a little vinegar, and finely chopped capers and caper berries
7. Serve the meat alone or with this sauce
