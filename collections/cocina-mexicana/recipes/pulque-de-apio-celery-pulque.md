## Ingredients

- Twenty-five quartillos of fine, strong pulque
- Celery
- Sugar (one and a half pounds)

## Directions

1. Grind sufficient celery on the metate with a little pulque and no water until it is a good green color
2. Mix the ground celery into the pulque
3. Add one and a half pounds of sugar
4. Stir everything well to incorporate
5. Let it settle
