## Ingredients

- Exotes (green beans)
- Butter
- Garlic heads
- Spanish sauce
- Flour

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan with a piece of butter one or two garlic heads cut into small dice shapes and the cleaned exotes
2. Place the saucepan in the oven
3. When the onions begin to brown, moisten with Spanish sauce, and if not available, add a handful of flour
4. Continue frying until the flour and onions are done
