## Ingredients

- water
- cooked blood, sliced
- lard
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon, ground
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme

## Directions

1. Put water in a pot on the fire and when boiling, add the cooked blood cut into slices
2. In a pan, put lard and oil and fry the slices well with plenty of tomato, onion and a little garlic
3. When well fried, add sufficient water with cloves, pepper, ground cinnamon, parsley, oregano and thyme in small quantity
4. Put on the fire until the sauce becomes thick
