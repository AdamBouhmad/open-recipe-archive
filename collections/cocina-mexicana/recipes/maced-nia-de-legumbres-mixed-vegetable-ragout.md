## Ingredients

- peas
- green broad beans
- green beans cut into strips
- dried beans
- cauliflower
- artichoke hearts
- turned mushrooms
- cucumbers or peppers
- asparagus tips
- small onions
- turnips
- German sauce
- gelatin from vegetables and roots
- sugar
- fine butter

## Directions

1. Parboil legumes of all kinds such as peas, green broad beans, green beans cut into strips, dried beans, cauliflower, artichoke hearts, turned mushrooms, cucumbers or peppers, asparagus tips, and small onions
2. Turn and parboil turnips separately in equal quantity
3. Fifteen minutes before serving, scald all the legumes and drain them on a clean cloth
4. Prepare a good German sauce in a saucepan, quite thick
5. While it is boiling, add the gelatin from the vegetables and roots with a little sugar and a little fine butter
6. Mix everything together until the legumes stick together and are coated in the sauce
7. Use this as a garnish for soup courses, entrées, pastries, and intermediates
