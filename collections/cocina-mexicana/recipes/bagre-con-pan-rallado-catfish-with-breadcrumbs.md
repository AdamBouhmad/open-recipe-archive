## Ingredients

- one catfish, cut into portions
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- clove
- thyme
- marjoram
- onion
- parsley
- lard
- paper
- vinegar
- cooking oil
- olives
- pickled chili peppers

## Directions

1. After washing the catfish well and cutting it into portions, roll it in breadcrumbs.
2. Grind pepper, clove, thyme and marjoram; chop onion and parsley very finely, and place a casserole greased with lard with paper on the bottom.
3. Form a layer of catfish portions, sprinkle with chopped onion and parsley, another of fish rolled in breadcrumbs with spices on top, and thus continue multiplying the layers, the last one being onion and parsley.
4. Place at two fires, and when it is cooked, sprinkle it with vinegar, adding plenty of oil and decorating it with olives and pickled chili peppers.
