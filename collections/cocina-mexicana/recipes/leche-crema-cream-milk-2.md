## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk
- four cups cornstarch
- rice as for manjar blanco
- lime or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Soak the lime or lemon peel in water until the water turns yellow
2. Add this to the milk
3. Add the cornstarch and rice and bring to a point not very high
4. Grind a little sugar, sprinkle on top, and with a hot iron spoon, burn and caramelize so it becomes edible
