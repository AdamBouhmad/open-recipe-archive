## Ingredients

- tomato
- garlic
- onion, very finely chopped
- lard
- water
- cooked offal
- dried chile, three or four
- cumin, a little
- salt
- plantain, fried

## Directions

1. Finely chop tomato, garlic and onion very finely and fry in lard with a little water
2. Chop cooked offal and let it dry
3. When ready, grind three or four dried chiles that have been soaked, add a little cumin and add water with the corresponding salt
4. Cook over two heats until it dries
5. Garnish with fried plantain
