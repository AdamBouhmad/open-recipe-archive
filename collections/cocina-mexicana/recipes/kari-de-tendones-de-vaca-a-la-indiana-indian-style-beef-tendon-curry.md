## Ingredients

- beef tendons, prepared and blanched
- good portion of butter
- saffron
- bay leaf
- many finely chopped hot chiles
- good piece of fatty pork, cut into small, square, flat pieces
- flour
- broth with mushrooms
- artichoke hearts, cut and turned
- small pearl onions
- one pound of rice, more or less
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare and blanch the tendons as for cooking in white broth
2. Place them in a saucepan with a good portion of butter, a little saffron, bay leaf, many finely chopped hot chiles, and a good piece of fatty pork cut into small, square, flat pieces
3. When everything is well rendered in the butter, add a few spoonfuls of flour and broth with mushrooms
4. Add the artichoke hearts (cut and turned) and small pearl onions successively and in the indicated order so that everything cooks at the same time
5. Parboil separately about one pound of rice, more or less according to the quantity and strength of the curry
6. Butter well the bottom and sides of a saucepan, add the rice after it has been drained
7. Cook it over two fires so that it takes on good color on all sides
8. Skim the stew and pour it onto a deep plate
9. Arrange the rice bread on another plate and serve both things at the same time
