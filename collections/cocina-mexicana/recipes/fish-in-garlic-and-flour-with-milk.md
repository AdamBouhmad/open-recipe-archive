## Ingredients

- Fish
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onion
- Oil
- Flour
- Milk

## Directions

1. Let the lard burn, and once it has done so, fry garlic and onion, chopped very finely, in it, mixing oil at the time of frying
2. Then add flour, and before it takes color add milk and place the fish there
