## Ingredients

- Beef sirloin, with fat and sinew removed
- Breadcrumbs
- Spanish sauce, somewhat reduced and boiling
- Black pepper
- Two eggs
- Breadcrumbs and cheese, grated
- Butter or clarified butter for frying
- Thick tomato broth
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Mince the beef sirloin as finely as breadcrumbs
2. Add the minced meat to Spanish sauce that is somewhat reduced and boiling
3. Add black pepper, two eggs, grated breadcrumbs and cheese
4. Mix everything thoroughly
5. Allow to cool
6. Form large balls from this mixture
7. Coat with beaten egg and breadcrumbs mixed with cheese
8. Fry in hot butter or clarified butter
9. Drain the balls
10. Cook gently in thick tomato broth
11. Arrange on a plate with tomato sauce underneath
