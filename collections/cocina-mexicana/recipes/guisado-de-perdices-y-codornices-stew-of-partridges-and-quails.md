## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Partridges and quails
- Onion
- Parsley
- White wine
- Spices

## Directions

1. Fry bacon pieces and remove them.
2. In the rendered fat, fry the birds.
3. Place in a pot.
4. In the same fat, fry chopped onion and parsley.
5. Add one glass of white wine and spices.
6. Cook over low heat with the pot tightly covered.
