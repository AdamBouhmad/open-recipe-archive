## Ingredients

- partridges, cleaned and drawn
- ham
- salt
- onions
- wine
- vinegar
- capers
- large capers
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- parsley, ground
- 2 carrots
- 2 turnips

## Directions

1. Cook whole cleaned and drawn partridges with ham, salt, and onions, adding wine, vinegar, capers, large capers, clove, cinnamon, pepper, and ground parsley
2. Add carrots and turnips
3. Season everything with pepper and cover with more ham
4. Cook over low heat
5. Drain the partridges over a cloth, pressing slightly to remove all liquid
6. Cover the pot and let cook, serving with oregano on top
