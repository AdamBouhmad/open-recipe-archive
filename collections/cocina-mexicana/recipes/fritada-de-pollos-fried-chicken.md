## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Oil
- Lemon juice
- Chopped parsley
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Tarragon

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chickens, quarter them and fry them raw in oil with lemon juice, chopped parsley, and quartered onions.
2. Sprinkle with sufficient salt and pepper and let them cook, adding a little water.
3. When cooked, fry them again in oil alone.
4. Arrange them in the serving dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper powder, and chopped parsley and tarragon.
