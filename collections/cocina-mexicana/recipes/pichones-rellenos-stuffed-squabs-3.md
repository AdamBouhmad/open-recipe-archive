## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Veal
- Poultry breast
- Ham
- Onion, fried
- Eggs
- Parsley
- Ground pepper
- Ground cumin
- Kraft paper

## Directions

1. Remove the wings and necks from the squabs
2. Trim the tail
3. Make a fine hash of veal, poultry breast, ham, and fried onion
4. Add eggs, parsley, ground pepper, and ground cumin
5. Stuff two squabs with this mixture
6. Cross the legs over the top to prevent the filling from falling out
7. Wrap in kraft paper
8. Roast in a casserole between two low fires
