## Ingredients

- Two ounces of rose petals, with sepals removed, choosing those of the most vivid color
- One quartillo of boiling water
- Sugar equal to double the weight of the filtered liquid, very white

## Directions

1. Place the rose petals in a gourd
2. Pour one quartillo of boiling water over them
3. Let infuse for twelve hours
4. Strain with expression
5. Filter the strained liquid
6. Dissolve sugar equal to double its weight at gentle heat in the filtered liquid
