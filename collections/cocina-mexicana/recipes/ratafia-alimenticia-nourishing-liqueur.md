## Ingredients

- Spirituous cinnamon water
- Egg yolks
- Fine flour

## Directions

1. In a glazed earthenware pot that has never held lard, pour six ounces of spirituous cinnamon water
2. Beat the water with a clean mill, and at the same time gradually and without ceasing to beat add four egg yolks
3. Continue beating and adding ingredients as specified for the preparation
