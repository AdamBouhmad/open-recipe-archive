## Ingredients

- pork loin portions of regular size
- garlic cloves
- ham pieces
- cloves
- pepper
- parsley leaves
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- roasted and ground tomato
- raisins
- halved almonds
- capers
- whole spices
- lard
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Divide the pork loin into regular-sized portions and stud with garlic cloves, ham pieces, cloves and pepper, sealing the holes on top with tightly pressed parsley leaves
2. Place in a pot with wine, little vinegar and water, roasted and ground tomato, raisins, halved almonds, capers, whole spices of the same kinds used for studding, and a little lard
3. Season with the appropriate salt and a little sugar
4. Cover the pot and let the meat cook, which should result in a thick broth
