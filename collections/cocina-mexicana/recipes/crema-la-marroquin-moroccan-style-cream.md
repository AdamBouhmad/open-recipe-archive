## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eggs
- Cream
- Sugar
- Salt
- Toasted and crushed orange blossom
- Lemon zest

## Directions

1. Dissolve flour in eggs, both yolks and whites together
2. Dissolve this paste in the cream in the proportion of seven and a half quarter liters and two eggs per tablespoon of flour
3. Add a good piece of sugar, some grains of salt, toasted and crushed orange blossom, scraped lemon zest or in pieces
4. Cook everything for half an hour, stirring constantly
5. Remove from heat, let cool and coat
