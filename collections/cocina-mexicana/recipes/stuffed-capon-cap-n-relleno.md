## Ingredients

- capon
- giblets
- broth
- breadcrumbs
- cream
- butter (one quarter pound)
- parsley
- green onions, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the capon and cook it
2. Chop all the giblets with a broth containing breadcrumbs cooked with cream, a quarter pound of butter, parsley, finely chopped green onions sautéed in butter, salt, pepper, and three egg yolks
3. Fill the interior with the stuffing, covering it lightly with breadcrumbs
4. Brown the capon in butter
5. Bread it again
6. Brown it in the oven
7. Serve with spicy sauce
