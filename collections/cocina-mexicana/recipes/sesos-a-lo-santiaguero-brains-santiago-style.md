## Ingredients

- sesos (brains)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- caldo del puchero (pot broth)
- caldo de gallina (chicken broth)
- perejil (parsley)
- ajos (garlic)
- cebollas (onions)
- zumo de limón (lemon juice)
- huevos duros (hard boiled eggs)

## Directions

1. Wash the brains first with salt and vinegar
2. Place them in a casserole with about four cups of pot broth and two cups of chicken broth
3. Add some parsley, garlic and onions, all chopped, about eight or ten drops of lemon juice
4. Season to taste
5. Cut four hard boiled eggs into slices
6. Let everything cook for the regular time and you will have an exquisite dish
