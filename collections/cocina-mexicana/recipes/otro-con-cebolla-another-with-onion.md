## Ingredients

- cebolla
- ajo
- clavo
- pimienta
- pan blanco
- vinagre
- pescado
- chiles
- alcaparras

## Directions

1. Chop onion and garlic finely and fry together with clove and pepper.
2. Separately fry a little white bread and soak in vinegar, grind and add to what is frying.
3. The fish should already be cooked, break into pieces and add to the above.
4. Let it season and garnish with chiles, capers, etc.
