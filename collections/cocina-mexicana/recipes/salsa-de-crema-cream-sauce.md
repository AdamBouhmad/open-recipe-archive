## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- a good amount of finely chopped parsley
- one onion cut into pieces
- salt
- ground pepper
- grated nutmeg
- a bit of cream or milk

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together
2. Add finely chopped parsley and onion cut into pieces, salt, ground pepper, grated nutmeg and a bit of cream or milk
3. Stir a lot, letting it boil for a quarter hour
