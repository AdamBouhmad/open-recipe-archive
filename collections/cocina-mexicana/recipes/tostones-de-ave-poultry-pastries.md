## Ingredients

- puff pastry scraps
- cooked minced poultry
- water
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Extend the puff pastry scraps with a rolling pin until the leaves are as thick as a peseta coin, or thinner if possible
2. Moisten the edges with a brush or gilding brush dipped in water
3. Place small portions the size of a grape of cooked minced poultry on them
4. Give them the shape of small slippers, cutting them like half-moons with a pastry cutter or knife, taking care that the edges are well sealed
5. Flour the lid of a pan and place the pastries on it
6. Keep in this state until it is certain the hour of serving, at which moment put them to fry
7. As soon as they acquire good color or are well gilded, dress them with a small bundle of fried parsley and serve immediately
