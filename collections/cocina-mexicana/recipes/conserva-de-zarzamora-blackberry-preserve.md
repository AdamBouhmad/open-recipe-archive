## Ingredients

- One pound blackberries
- Salt
- Two pounds sugar
- Cinnamon stick
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place blackberries in water with a little salt
2. The next morning change the water giving it two or three rinses
3. Clarify two pounds of sugar and let it cool
4. Once the blackberries are drained add a cinnamon stick
5. As soon as it is boiling add a few drops of lemon juice and carefully remove the seeds with a small spoon so they do not break
6. Test the point on your fingers until the soft ball stage is reached
