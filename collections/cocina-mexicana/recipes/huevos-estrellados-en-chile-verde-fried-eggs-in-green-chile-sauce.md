## Ingredients

- Green chiles
- Tomatoes
- Salted water
- Fine salt
- Water

## Directions

1. In a little salted water, cook green chiles and tomatoes
2. When they are cooked, wash them in cold water
3. Grind both together well
4. Fry everything and season with fine salt
5. Add the necessary water
6. Fry the eggs in this sauce
