## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- a little milk
- half a pound ground almonds
- milk totaling four quartillos with the yolks
- sugar in excess

## Directions

1. Dissolve twenty egg yolks in a little milk
2. Strain through a sieve and mix with the remaining milk in which half a pound of ground almonds will have been added, the two together making four quartillos
3. Sweeten so that the sugar is prominent, so that after being frozen it remains regular
4. Cook this mixture in a very clean pan, stirring constantly until it has boiled very well
5. Empty into a basin, let cool and fill the tubes
