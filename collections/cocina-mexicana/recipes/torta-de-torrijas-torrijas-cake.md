## Ingredients

- Half pound of beef marrow
- Half pound of butter
- One pound of fine flour
- Ten eggs
- One pound of ground sugar
- Butter
- Syrup (almíbar)
- Beaten egg for coating

## Directions

1. Melt half pound of beef marrow with half pound of butter
2. Mix with one pound of fine flour
3. Beat ten eggs with one pound of ground sugar
4. Combine the flour mixture with the beaten eggs
5. Pour into a cake pan greased with butter
6. Cook well over two fires
7. Cut the cake into proportional pieces and place in syrup
8. For more exquisite torrijas, fry the slices in butter after coating with beaten egg, then place in syrup
