## Ingredients

- white apples
- one quarter sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the apples in half and remove the core
2. Place them in a pan and add one quarter sugar and enough water for them to cook
3. Once cooked on one side, turn them over to the other side
4. When the syrup is sufficiently thick, place them in the compote dish and pour over them the syrup in which they cooked
5. Serve cold or hot
