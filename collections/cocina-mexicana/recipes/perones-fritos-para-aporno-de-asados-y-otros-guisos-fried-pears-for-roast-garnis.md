## Ingredients

- pears
- a good handful of flour
- two or three raw egg yolks
- a little good edible oil
- sufficient salt
- a little water
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Remove the core from the pears using a corer, passing through with a round hole without opening them
2. Remove the crowns from both ends and cut into thin slices
3. Form the batter from a good handful of flour, two or three raw egg yolks, a little good edible oil, and sufficient salt
4. Loosen the batter with a little water and beat until a spoonful poured from a height forms a thread that does not break
5. Coat the pear slices with this batter
6. Fry in lard until golden colored
