## Ingredients

- sorrel
- butter
- broth
- bread
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put in a pot with butter a handful of cleaned and washed sorrel, chopped into pieces
2. When well softened, add the amount of broth that is needed
3. Once it is about to boil, add the bread, leaving it on low heat
4. Pour into the soup tureen with a beaten mixture of egg yolks when it is to be served
