## Ingredients

- Beef rib
- Oil instead of butter
- Twenty large onions
- Four ounces of good oil
- Vinegar
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a beef rib as previously described, using oil instead of butter
2. When cooked, place on a plate
3. Cut twenty large onions in half and slice them so the slices form semicircles
4. Fry the onion slices in four ounces of good oil until they take on a beautiful color
5. Add vinegar, broth, salt and pepper to the onions
6. Pour this sauce over the rib
