## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Fat ham
- Oil
- Cooked hens
- Broth from cooking the hens
- Ground saffron

## Directions

1. Chop together tomatoes, garlic, parsley and fat ham finely
2. Fry these ingredients in oil
3. Add the already cooked hens and fry until well browned
4. Remove the hens and add a little of their cooking broth and ground saffron to the pan
5. Let the sauce thicken and pour it over the hens
6. Serve hot
