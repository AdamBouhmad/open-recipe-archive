## Ingredients

- Capers
- Cooked livers
- Garlic browned in lard
- Lard
- Salt
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Vinegar
- Chicken broth
- Large capers
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Grind together capers, cooked livers, and garlic browned in lard
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard
3. Season with salt, clove, pepper, oregano, and vinegar
4. Put in the chicken with its broth, large capers, and oil
5. Let everything simmer until the broth is well seasoned
6. When serving, garnish with chiles and olives
