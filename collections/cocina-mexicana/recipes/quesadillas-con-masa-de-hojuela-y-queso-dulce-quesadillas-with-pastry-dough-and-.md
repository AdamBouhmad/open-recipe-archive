## Ingredients

- Pastry dough
- Fresh cheese, ground
- Powdered sugar
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. Extend the dough with a rolling pin so it is neither too thin nor too thick
2. Cut rounds using a medium cup
3. On each round place a paste made from ground fresh cheese, powdered sugar, cloves, cinnamon, and toasted sesame seeds
4. Cover the round with another piece of dough
5. Flatten the edges, fold or crimp them, and place a small decorative shape made from the same dough on top
6. Bake in a regularly tempered oven
7. When removed from the oven, dust with sugar
