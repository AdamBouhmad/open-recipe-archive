## Ingredients

- veal
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- herbs

## Directions

1. Place the veal in a cazuela with a quarter liter of oil, salt, pepper and a handful of herbs
2. Cook over low heat and stir often so it takes on color
3. When well cooked, serve with Italian sauce, adding the rest of the first cooking liquid
