## Ingredients

- three cuartillas of cream
- sugar
- glazed almonds
- candied lemon
- marzipan
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Boil three cuartillas of cream with sugar and finely chopped glazed almonds, candied lemon, and marzipan.
2. Beat the whites and yolks of eight eggs separately.
3. Taking portions of the beaten whites with a spoon, put three spoonfuls at a time into the cream, forming eggs without yolks; let them drain and place one upon another until they form eight eggs on the serving plate.
4. Put the cream on the fire to reduce it to sauce consistency.
5. When ready to serve, add the eight beaten yolks, letting them bind over the fire but without boiling so the yolks do not spill.
6. Pour the sauce over the whites arranged as described.
