## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Butter
- Olive oil
- Pepper
- Salt
- Garlic
- Laurel
- Parsley
- Carrots
- Onions
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Piece a chicken as you would for fricassee, and blanch it in a mixture of butter and olive oil over a very lively fire, so that all the parts of the chicken brown perfectly.
2. Then add pepper, salt, garlic, laurel, parsley, sliced carrots and onions, and a few spoonfuls of Spanish sauce (see).
3. When the chicken is half cooked, cover the fire with hot coals, and let it simmer gently for half an hour.
