## Ingredients

- Ten egg yolks
- One tablespoon lard
- One tablespoon sifted sugar
- One and a half pounds flour
- Sugar and cinnamon for rolling

## Directions

1. Beat ten egg yolks very well with a chocolate mill, and at the same time gradually mix in one tablespoon of lard and another of sifted sugar
2. Then add one and a half pounds of flour, increasing it if necessary, until the dough is of the proper consistency to form the rosquillas with it
3. Fry them in very hot lard
4. Roll them in ground sugar and cinnamon
