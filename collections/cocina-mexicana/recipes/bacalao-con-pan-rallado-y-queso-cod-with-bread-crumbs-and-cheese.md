## Ingredients

- Oil
- Onion, finely chopped
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Tomato, roasted and ground
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Grated cheese
- Fish in large portions
- Oil
- Small chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion and parsley very well in oil
2. Add roasted and ground tomato, bread crumbs, pepper, oregano, and grated cheese
3. Cook the fish in portions in this sauce with oil, small chiles, and olives
