## Ingredients

- Catfish portions, cooked and fried
- Oil
- Garlic, chopped
- Very ripe tomatoes, roasted and peeled
- Capers
- Bread, toasted in lard
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Olives
- Small chiles

## Directions

1. After cooking and frying the catfish portions with oil and chopped garlic, grind very ripe roasted and peeled tomatoes and the garlic with which the fish was fried
2. Also grind plenty of capers with bread toasted in lard
3. Pour everything into the oil in which the catfish portions were fried and make a sauce, adding ground clove, cinnamon, and pepper
4. Place the catfish in it, and when the sauce is quite thick, add oil, vinegar, olives, and small chiles
