## Ingredients

- six quartillos of brandy
- one quarter pound of green anise
- lemon peel
- a little cinnamon
- three quartillos of water
- one and a half pounds of clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Infuse one quarter pound of green anise, the peel of a lemon, and a little cinnamon in six quartillos of brandy
2. Leave in this infusion for one month
3. Strain
4. Add three quartillos of water and one and a half pounds of clarified sugar
5. Filter and put in bottles
