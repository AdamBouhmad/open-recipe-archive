## Ingredients

- requesón (curd cheese)
- water of orange blossoms
- powdered sugar
- virgin honey
- pastille (candy)
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash the requesón in a little orange blossom water
2. Add powdered sugar and virgin honey, alternating one and the other while beating
3. Beat continuously
4. Leave it sweet to each one's taste
5. Empty into a platter
6. Place pastille and ground cinnamon on top
