## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- crayfish
- curled sauce
- herb sauce
- spinach green

## Directions

1. Once the brains are cooked as the butler's brains, arrange them placing a crayfish between each one
2. Pour a little curled sauce over a herb sauce (see pages 766 and 774) and heighten the color with spinach green (see spinach green, page 322)
3. Cover the brains with this sauce and the curled can be replaced with white substance (see page *)
