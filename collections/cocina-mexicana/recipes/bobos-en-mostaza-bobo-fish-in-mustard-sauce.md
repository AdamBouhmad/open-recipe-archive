## Ingredients

- mustard, ground and sieved
- almonds, ground
- cinnamon
- clove
- oil
- vinegar
- capers
- olives
- tornachiles
- cooked bobo fish

## Directions

1. Grind and sieve mustard to remove its pungency.
2. Also grind almonds, cinnamon and clove, and mix everything with oil and vinegar in abundance, capers, olives and tornachiles.
3. After cooking the bobo fish, add the mustard.
4. Serve cold.
