## Ingredients

- lard
- oil
- bobo fish, desalted and in slices if not fresh
- clean toasted and ground almonds
- broad chiles, deveined, fried in lard and ground
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- olives
- tornachiles in strips

## Directions

1. Coat a casserole with lard and add considerable oil.
2. Place a layer of desalted bobo fish in slices if not fresh.
3. Cover with clean toasted and ground almonds, broad chiles that have been deveined, fried in lard and ground with a little pepper, clove and cinnamon.
4. Add another layer of bobo fish following the same method, adding salt if necessary.
5. Cook over two fires and serve with olives and split tornachiles.
