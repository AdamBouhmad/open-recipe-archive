## Ingredients

- Two raw egg yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spanish oil, one tablespoon per egg yolk
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat two raw egg yolks in a bowl, well seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Add Spanish oil almost drop by drop, in the proportion of one tablespoon per egg yolk.
3. Beat the mixture for a long time, no less than a quarter of an hour so that it turns out well.
4. When the oil is well incorporated into the egg yolks, add little by little the proportionate amount of lemon juice, or in its absence, vinegar.
