## Ingredients

- coles dough
- butter (less than standard)
- flour
- egg for moistening
- egg yolk for gilding
- syrup at crack point
- candied orange blossom

## Directions

1. Make the same as the zampabocas franceses described in the previous article
2. After coating with syrup, pass over candied orange blossom that has been spread on paper beforehand
