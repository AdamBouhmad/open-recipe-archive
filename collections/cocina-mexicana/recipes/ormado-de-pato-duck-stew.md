## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Straw
- Lard
- Ham slices
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Chiles
- Capers
- Olives

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the ducks, and after washing well and removing the tail, put them in a pot with water and straw, and once they have boiled a few times remove from the fire
2. Give them one or two washes with hot water, then quarter them
3. Put lard in a pan and there put the ducks with some slices of ham and plenty of chopped onion and garlic and let everything fry well
4. Then add water and a little chopped parsley, thyme and oregano, and grind clove, cinnamon and pepper with some slices of fried bread
5. Garnish the platter with chiles, capers and olives
