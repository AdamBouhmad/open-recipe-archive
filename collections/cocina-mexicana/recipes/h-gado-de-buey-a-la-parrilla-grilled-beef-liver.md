## Ingredients

- beef liver
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into thin slices and place them on the grill
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and turn to the other side doing the same
3. Let them cook very little
4. Serve the slices two by two, one on top of the other, placing between them a small ball of butter kneaded with parsley
