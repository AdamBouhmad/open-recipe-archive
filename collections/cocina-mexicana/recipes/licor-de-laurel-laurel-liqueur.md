## Ingredients

- 8 quartillos brandy
- 6 ounces myrtle leaves and flowers
- 6 ounces laurel leaves
- 0.5 broken nutmeg
- 0.5 drachm clove
- 4 quartillos distilled water
- 3 pounds crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Mix and filter.
