## Ingredients

- young rabbit
- bacon pieces
- salt
- roasted hazelnuts
- bread
- garlic
- parsley
- spices

## Directions

1. Fry with small pieces of bacon and a pinch of salt, then place in the pot to brown.
2. Serve with the following sauce: pound some roasted hazelnuts with a slice of moistened bread, garlic cloves, parsley and spices, ensuring the mixture is not too thick.
