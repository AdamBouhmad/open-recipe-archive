## Ingredients

- twelve and a half quartillos of milk
- nine pounds of sugar
- one large grated and ground coconut
- cinnamon sticks
- orange blossom leaves
- four ounces of soaked, ground and dissolved almond
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten twelve and a half quartillos of milk with nine pounds of sugar and strain
2. Mix with one large grated and ground coconut, cinnamon sticks and orange blossom leaves with the precaution of taking care not to make the milk too green
3. Put on the fire, let it boil, and when it is well thickened, remove the orange blossom leaves and add four ounces of almond that has been soaked, ground and dissolved with a little milk
4. Make it boil again, and when it reaches the right consistency, set aside, remove the cinnamon sticks, and pour into plates that are adorned with cinnamon powder
