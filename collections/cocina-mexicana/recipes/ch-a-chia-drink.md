## Ingredients

- Chia seeds
- Water
- Lemon juice (optional)
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Add the chia seeds to water, stirring well to prevent lumps from forming when they coagulate.
2. Leave in the water until each grain develops a sphere of coagulated water around it.
3. Sweeten to taste.
4. Add a few drops of lemon juice to each glass, or add whole lemons to the infusion according to quantity.
