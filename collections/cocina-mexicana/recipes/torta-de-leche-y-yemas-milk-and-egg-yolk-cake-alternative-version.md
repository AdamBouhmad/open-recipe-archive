## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk, fat removed
- one fresh cinnamon stick
- eighteen egg yolks
- necessary sugar
- sponge cake (mamón), crumbled and trimmed

## Directions

1. Bring four cuartillos of milk to three boils, removing fat
2. Set aside half a cuartillo
3. Allow the rest to boil gently with one fresh cinnamon stick until it thickens
4. When cold, mix with eighteen egg yolks and necessary sugar
5. Strain the yolks with the milk before sweetening
6. Mix everything with crumbled sponge cake with top and bottom crusts removed
7. Pour into a buttered pan
8. Cook over two fires
