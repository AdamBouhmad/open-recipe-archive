## Ingredients

- dos cuartillos de leche (two quarters milk)
- medio de natillas (half custard)
- ocho yemas de huevo (eight egg yolks)
- un poquito de arroz bien lavado, remojado y molido (a little rice well washed, soaked and ground)
- dulce (sweetener) to taste

## Directions

1. Boil everything except the custard
2. Add the custard when the rice is near perfect doneness
