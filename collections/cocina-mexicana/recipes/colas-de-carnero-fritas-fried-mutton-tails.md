## Ingredients

- mutton tails
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- lard
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the tails at the braise, leave them to cool and bread them in the ordinary manner
2. Coat them successively with beaten egg and crumbled or grated breadcrumbs, which is repeated twice
3. Fry them afterwards in lard and serve with fried parsley on top
