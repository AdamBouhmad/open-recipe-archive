## Ingredients

- cooked and cleaned cod
- one cup peeled garlic for two pounds of fish
- about twice as much crumbs from descaled bread
- six to eight peppercorns
- one clove
- butter
- plenty of oil

## Directions

1. Cook and clean the cod
2. For two pounds of fish, grind one cup peeled garlic
3. Grind this with about twice as much crumbs from descaled bread
4. Grind everything perfectly with six to eight peppercorns and one clove
5. Fry all of this in a little butter and plenty of oil, but do not fry much as it turns black
6. When half-cooked, add a little water
7. Begin placing the cod in the sauce
8. When done, add a little oil on top and let it thicken to taste
