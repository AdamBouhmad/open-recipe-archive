## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Green rind of covered lemon
- Sugar
- Fresh butter
- Salt
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach and wash in plenty of water
2. Blanch in a pot with water on the stove
3. Remove and let drain
4. Squeeze and press between the hands
5. Pound in a mortar with green rind of covered lemon, necessary sugar, a piece of fresh butter, and a little salt
6. When everything is well pounded, line a baking pan with puff pastry dough
7. Spread the spinach mixture in it
