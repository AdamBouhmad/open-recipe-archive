## Ingredients

- squash
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- egg yolks
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into equal square pieces
2. Cook in water with salt and a little butter
3. Place in a saucepan with butter, minced parsley, salt and pepper
4. Let simmer
5. When the butter has been consumed, dissolve egg yolks in cream or milk and combine with the squash
