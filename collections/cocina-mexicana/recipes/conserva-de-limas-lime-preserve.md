## Ingredients

- Yellow limes
- Salt
- Sugar (1 pound for every 6 limes)

## Directions

1. Select yellow limes and peel them without removing the stem or hollowing them out, only cutting the skin and leaving them in their original shape
2. Place them to cook with a little salt
3. Once cooked, remove them and place in another pot of hot water without salt
4. Keep them in water for two days, changing the water twice daily, ensuring the water is warm until the limes have cooled
5. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar for every six limes, and after clarifying and cooling it well, add the limes also cooled
6. Let them take on the proper point over low heat
7. The syrup should have enough water so that it does not reduce more than necessary and need to be added later
