## Ingredients

- lard
- oil
- garlic cloves
- pieces of fat chicharrón (fried pork belly)
- tips of a bundle of well-cooked asparagus
- artichoke heart bases
- chopped mushrooms
- eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put lard and oil in a casserole, and when both are hot
2. Brown some garlic cloves
3. Add some pieces of fat chicharrón and the tips of a bundle of well-cooked asparagus, pieces of artichoke heart bases and chopped mushrooms, all cooked
4. Add the eggs with broken yolks and seasoned with salt
5. Moving the casserole away from the heat, stir everything until the egg sets well
6. Place on a platter and dust with salt and pepper
