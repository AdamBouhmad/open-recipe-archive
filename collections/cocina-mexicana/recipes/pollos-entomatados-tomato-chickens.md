## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- all spices, ground
- onion
- garlic
- parsley, chopped
- plenty of cooked and ground tomatoes
- lard

## Directions

1. Parboil the chickens in lard.
2. Transfer them to a pot with their water and sufficient salt.
3. Add a little vinegar, all ground spices, onion, garlic, and chopped parsley, plenty of cooked and ground tomatoes, and a little lard.
4. Cover the pot and seal the edges.
5. Cook the chickens over gentle heat, stirring occasionally with the pot, and once they are considered cooked, uncover them.
6. Once uncovered, remove from heat as soon as the sauce is somewhat thick.
