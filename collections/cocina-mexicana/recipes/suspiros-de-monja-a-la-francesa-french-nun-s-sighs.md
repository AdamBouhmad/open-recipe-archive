## Ingredients

- Duchess paste (masa de la duquesa)
- Lard, butter, or oil for frying
- Sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Spread the duchess paste on a baking sheet
2. Cut it and form small balls from it
3. Fry them in lard, butter, or oil until well browned
4. Drain them
5. Dust with sugar and serve
