## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Jícama
- Syrup of medium point
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. After removing the cores from the carrots, grate them
2. Grate jícama in equal parts
3. Place in syrup of medium point
4. Leave over fire until they take the consistency of paste
5. In the rest as with the other antes
6. Sprinkle the last layer with toasted sesame seeds
