## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley, chopped
- ground pepper
- bread crumbs
- white fish
- oil
- salt
- lemon, sliced
- vinegar
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook eggs and remove yolks, mixing them with chopped parsley and ground pepper; chop the whites
2. Fill the cleaned and gutted fish with this mixture
3. Spread paper sheets on a table and sprinkle with bread crumbs mixed with chopped parsley and ground pepper
4. Place fish in rows on the paper
5. Cover with more bread crumbs, parsley, and pepper
6. Drizzle with plenty of oil and fold the paper at the four sides so liquid does not escape
7. Place on a grill at medium heat and cook, taking care they do not burn
8. Serve as is or with salt-pepper and lemon slices, or with oil and vinegar poured over, or with a sauce boat of tomato sauce or another sauce
