## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- two onions
- parsley
- clove
- thyme
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Divide several tomatoes into pieces
2. Cook them with a little butter, two sliced onions, parsley, clove, thyme, salt, and pepper
3. When everything is cooked, strain through a hair sieve
