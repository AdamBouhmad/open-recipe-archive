## Ingredients

- squabs
- lemon juice or vinegar
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Place a little lemon juice or a splash of vinegar in a small dish
2. When killing the squabs, let the blood fall into it
3. Prepare them as for compote
4. Use the blood as a binder, adding two or three egg yolks and two or three spoonfuls of milk, all passed through a sieve
