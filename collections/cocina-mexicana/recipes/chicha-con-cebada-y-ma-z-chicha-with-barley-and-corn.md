## Ingredients

- Two large pineapples with their skins, ground raw
- One ounce of barley
- One ounce of dark corn cacahuatzincle
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Water

## Directions

1. Grind the two pineapples with their skins raw.
2. Add one ounce of barley, one ounce of dark corn cacahuatzincle, nutmeg, a little cinnamon, and a little clove.
3. Place everything in a jar full of water, covered lightly with dried corn husks.
4. Leave in the sun and open air for nine days, stirring daily.
5. After nine days, strain and season with ground cloves, pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
6. Sweeten to taste.
