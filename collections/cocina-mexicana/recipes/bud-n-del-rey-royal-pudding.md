## Ingredients

- half pound of flour
- half pound of powdered sugar
- half pound of butter in pieces
- four eggs without beating
- two ounces of almonds in quarters
- two ounces of Corinth raisins

## Directions

1. Put everything in a casserole and beat until the butter is dissolved
2. Then add two ounces of almonds in quarters and two ounces of Corinth raisins
3. Stir and pour into the pudding mold that will be spread with butter and dusted with ground biscuit
4. Cook over two slow fires like all puddings
