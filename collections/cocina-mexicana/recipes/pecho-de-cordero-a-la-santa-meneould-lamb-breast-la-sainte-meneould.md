## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- ham slices
- beef pulp slices
- parsnips
- onions
- clove
- bay leaf
- thyme
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Line a pot with ham slices and beef pulp slices
2. Place the breast in the pot with two parsnips, two onions, clove, bay leaf, thyme, and a ladle of broth
3. Cook two hours over low heat
4. Drain and sprinkle with salt and coarse pepper
5. Cool between two pressed pot covers
6. Debone and arrange
7. Soak in warm butter and roll in bread crumbs
8. Brush with more butter using a feather brush
9. Sprinkle more bread crumbs
10. Place on the grill over low heat for a quarter hour covered with a dome oven
11. Serve over a light broth
