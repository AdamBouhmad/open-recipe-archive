## Ingredients

- turnips
- carrots
- kohlrabi
- radishes
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the turnips, carrots, kohlrabi, and radishes into small strips
2. Place them to cook in salted water
3. When these vegetables are cooked, add the noodles with other things in the same broth
4. Skim and stir from time to time so that balls or lumps do not form
5. Serve seasoned with salt and coarse pepper
6. This dish is often placed on the table as a soup
