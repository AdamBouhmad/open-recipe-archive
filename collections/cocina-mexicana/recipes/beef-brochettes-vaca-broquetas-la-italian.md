## Ingredients

- Beef liver
- Ham
- Pepper
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cut the beef liver into small pieces.
2. Alternate pieces of ham and liver on skewers.
3. Sprinkle with pepper and salt.
4. Cover with breadcrumbs, moistening them with oil.
5. Cook on the grill.
6. Serve as soon as they are well browned.
