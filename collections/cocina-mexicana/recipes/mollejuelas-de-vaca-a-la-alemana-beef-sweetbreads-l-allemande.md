## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Butter
- Semi-gelatin of meat
- German sauce
- Truffles
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. After soaking the sweetbreads, blanch and refresh them in cold water.
2. Cut into small, thin, round pieces and cook in a semi-gelatin of meat with a little butter.
3. Once cooked, add them to hot German sauce with cooked truffles and mushrooms, prepared in the same manner.
4. At the moment of serving, add a little fresh butter and lemon juice.
