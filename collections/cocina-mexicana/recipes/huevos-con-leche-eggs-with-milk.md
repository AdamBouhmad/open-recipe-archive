## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- flour

## Directions

1. To the dissolved butter add finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper and a glass of cream in which a spoonful of flour has been previously dissolved
2. When the sauce is ready place in it the cooked eggs cut into slices
