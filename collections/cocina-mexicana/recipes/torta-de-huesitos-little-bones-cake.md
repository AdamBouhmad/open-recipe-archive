## Ingredients

- Pork loin, common and head meat
- Ripe tomatoes, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Lard
- Ground spices
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Pork feet, six or eight, cooked
- Eggs, beaten
- Chile sauce

## Directions

1. Chop pork loin and head meat, cook well with salt, then chop again
2. Chop ripe tomatoes, garlic and onions, fry in lard and add the meat to fry together with the tomato
3. Add all ground spices, a little vinegar and a lump of sugar
4. When the mixture is seasoned, cut six or eight well-cooked pork feet into short pieces
5. Mix with the mixture and dissolve everything in beaten eggs as if for frying
6. Put all the mass in a buttered baking dish and place at two fires
7. As soon as the cake is set, serve with chile sauce
