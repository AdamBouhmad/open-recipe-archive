## Ingredients

- Purple tunas (prickly pears, called dye tunas)
- Pulque
- Sugar to sweeten
- Guava slices
- Guineo banana slices
- Bits of clove
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. After peeling, dissolve the purple prickly pears in a little pulque
2. Strain through a sieve and sweeten everything
3. Add slices of guava and guineo banana and bits of clove and cinnamon
