## Ingredients

- barley
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare as with peeled barley
2. Strain immediately through fine cloth or a sieve
3. If desired to be more nutritious, allow it to thicken over the fire after straining, which will make it as nourishing as peeled barley in equal quantity
4. Milk can be mixed into these preparations to make them more agreeable to taste
