## Ingredients

- Fresh mackerel
- Crabs
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Onions
- Butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Oiled paper
- Crab substance

## Directions

1. Select two or three very fresh mackerel
2. Parboil the crabs in boiling water, clean them, crush the shells and chop the tails
3. Mix the crab with mushrooms, parsley, and onions
4. Bind everything together with good butter, salt, and pepper
5. Stuff the mackerel with this mixture
6. Wrap in oiled paper and cook on a grill over moderate heat
7. Serve with crab substance
