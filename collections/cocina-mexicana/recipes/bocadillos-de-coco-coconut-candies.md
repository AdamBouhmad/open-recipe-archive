## Ingredients

- Coconut
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water
- Dragée

## Directions

1. Grate or grind the coconut on a clean metate
2. Pour into prepared syrup that is somewhat thick
3. Place on the fire until it pulls away from the pot
4. Pour into small boxes, adding orange blossom water and dragée on top
