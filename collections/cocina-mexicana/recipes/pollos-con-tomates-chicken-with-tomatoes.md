## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Onion
- Lard
- Flour
- Water
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Bay leaves
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and place in a casserole with ham and chorizo slices, chopped tomatoes and a little chopped garlic and onion with a spoonful of lard
2. Fry well
3. Add flour and fry a bit more
4. Add water, thyme, oregano and bay leaves
5. Grind clove and cinnamon
6. Add oil before serving
7. The broth should be thick
8. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers
