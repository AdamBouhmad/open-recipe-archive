## Ingredients

- Melon, between green and ripe
- Water with lime (agua de cal)
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)

## Directions

1. Peel the melon and cut eight slices from each melon
2. Give it a light cooking
3. Place in the sun for a morning, changing lime waters
4. Place in cold sugar syrup and with vigorous boiling bring to soak and take point
5. Let cool and add orange blossom water
