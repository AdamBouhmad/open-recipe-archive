## Ingredients

- Manteca (lard)
- Equal quantity of wide chiles and pasilla chiles, toasted and ground
- Cooked and ground tomatoes
- Cecina (dried pork), soaked and washed
- Water
- Manteca
- Epazote
- Salt
- Optional: small squashes or soconoxcles
- Optional: toasted and ground sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Place a cazuela (cooking pot) over the fire with lard and fry equal quantities of toasted and ground wide chiles and pasilla chiles along with cooked and ground tomatoes
2. Empty into the cazuela a pot in which cecina has been previously prepared by cooking with water, lard, epazote and salt (if the cecina has become desalted, it should first be soaked and washed well before cooking)
3. Let everything boil until the clemole reaches a regular consistency, neither watery nor very thick
4. Optional: if desired and the season permits, add very small squashes or soconoxcles
5. Optional: for variation, sesame seeds (toasted and ground) may be added to the chiles, but in this case omit the squashes or soconoxcles
