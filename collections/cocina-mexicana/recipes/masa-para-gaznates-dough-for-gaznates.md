## Ingredients

- seven egg yolks
- tequesquite powder
- sugar
- brandy
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat well seven egg yolks with a little tequesquite powder, another of sugar and a little brandy
2. Mix all with the flour that can be moistened by it and form the gaznates
