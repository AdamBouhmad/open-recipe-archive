## Ingredients

- Fish
- Lard
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- Parsley
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Capers
- Ground pepper
- Ground clove
- Ground cinnamon
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Coat a pot with lard and place a layer of chopped tomato, garlic, onion and parsley, small chiles, olives and capers, with ground pepper, clove and cinnamon mixed with vinegar, all fried separately
2. Place the parboiled fish on top, whichever kind it is, and cover with another layer of the said seasoning, loading it more with parsley
3. To the last layer add oil and breadcrumbs, having seasoned it with the corresponding salt
4. Let it cook over two fires
