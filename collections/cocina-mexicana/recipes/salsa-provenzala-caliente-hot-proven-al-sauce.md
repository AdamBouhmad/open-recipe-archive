## Ingredients

- 2 raw egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon of German sauce
- A little crushed garlic
- Chiltepiquin powder
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Olive oil
- Herb sauce or greens from herbs or spinach or blanched parsley, optional

## Directions

1. Place 2 raw egg yolks in a small pot with 1 teaspoon of German sauce, a little crushed garlic, chiltepiquin powder, and the juice of 2 lemons
2. Let this sauce thicken in a bain-marie over embers, stirring carefully until it gains some body
3. Remove from heat and add olive oil, stirring gently so it does not curdle
4. Serve this sauce as prepared, or add herb sauce or greens from herbs or spinach or blanched parsley
5. This sauce is commonly used for poultry or fish dishes
