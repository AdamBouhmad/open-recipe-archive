## Ingredients

- butter
- sponge cake (mamones)
- butter pieces
- pepper
- pine nuts
- candied citron
- raisins
- almonds
- milk cream (natas de leche)
- milk

## Directions

1. Grease a cooking vessel with butter
2. Layer sliced sponge cake with butter pieces on top
3. Make the gigote by sprinkling with pepper and small pieces of pine nuts, candied citron, raisins and almonds
4. Layer milk cream on top
5. Add another layer of sponge cake on top, taking care to toast it
6. Fill the spaces with the milk from the gigote, which should be served cold
