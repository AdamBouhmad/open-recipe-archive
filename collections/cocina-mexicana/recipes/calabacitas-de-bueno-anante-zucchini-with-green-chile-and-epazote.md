## Ingredients

- tender young squash
- lard
- salt
- fresh green chiles
- epazote
- pork ribs
- cooked chicharrones

## Directions

1. Chop the tender squash finely and put to cook with lard alone and salt
2. When they have cooked a little, grind some fresh green chiles and fried epazote, which you add to the squash, adding small pork ribs and cooked chicharrones
3. Leave on low fire until they are well cooked
