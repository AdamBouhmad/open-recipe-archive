## Ingredients

- Garlic
- Lard
- Onion, chopped
- Good roasted tomatoes
- French bread slices, thin
- Tomato broth

## Directions

1. Fry a little garlic in lard, remove it, and after the lard is burned, add chopped onion and good roasted tomatoes ground with the garlic
2. Remove the pot from the fire
3. In another pot, place thin, well-toasted French bread slices and bathe them with the tomato broth
4. Put on the fire until the soup thickens as needed
5. Without stirring it at all, it can be served at the table
