## Ingredients

- sixty egg yolks
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- half a cuartillo of oil
- flour as necessary
- almibar of half point mixed with a little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, powdered sugar, and oil
2. Add the necessary flour so that everything is absorbed
3. Knead until the point where the biscuits can be made
4. Give them the desired form
5. Put them in the oven
6. When baked, coat them with almibar mixed with a little orange blossom water
7. Let them dry before serving
