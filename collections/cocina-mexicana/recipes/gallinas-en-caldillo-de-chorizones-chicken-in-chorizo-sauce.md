## Ingredients

- cooked chicken
- lard
- salt
- chorizos
- roasted tomatoes
- garlic
- chicken broth
- ham
- flour
- cloves
- pepper
- vinegar
- olives
- small chilies
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry quarters of cooked chicken in lard with a little salt
2. Once browned, set aside and fry slices of chorizos in the same lard
3. Set the fried chorizos aside and fry roasted and ground tomatoes with garlic in the lard
4. Add the fried chicken quarters, the cooking broth, pieces of cooked ham, flour browned in lard, and the ground fried chorizo slices with cloves and pepper and a little vinegar
5. Let everything boil together
6. When serving, add olives, small chilies, and more oil
