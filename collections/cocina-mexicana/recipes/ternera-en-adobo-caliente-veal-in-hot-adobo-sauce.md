## Ingredients

- veal, bled and cleaned
- salt
- wide chiles, washed and deveined
- two heads of garlic, cleaned
- oregano
- cumin
- a piece of bread soaked in vinegar
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Divide the bled and cleaned veal into regular portions.
2. Cook in water with salt, then remove from heat.
3. Grind the washed and deveined wide chiles with two clean heads of garlic, a little oregano, cumin, and a piece of bread soaked in vinegar.
4. Fry all this and then dissolve with a little vinegar, seasoning with necessary salt.
5. The acidity of the vinegar can be mitigated with water.
6. Add the pieces of meat and leave until the broth has thickened to a regular consistency.
7. Serve adorned with olives and cured small chiles.
