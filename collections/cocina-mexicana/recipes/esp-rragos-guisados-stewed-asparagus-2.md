## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- garlic cloves, minced
- asparagus, cooked in salted water, drained and chopped
- toasted bread, ground
- pepper
- clove
- water from cooking the asparagus
- small whole onions, cooked
- vinegar
- oregano
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry minced garlic cloves in butter or lard until golden, then remove them
2. In the same fat, add the cooked and chopped asparagus
3. When half-fried, add ground toasted bread mixed with the garlic that was removed from the fat, pepper, clove, a little water from the cooking liquid, the whole cooked onions, vinegar and oregano
4. When most of the broth has been reduced, add oil and serve
