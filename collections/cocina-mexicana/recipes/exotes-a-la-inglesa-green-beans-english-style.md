## Ingredients

- Exotes (green beans)
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice or good vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the exotes and when cooked, stir them, allowing the sauce to reduce over low heat
2. When removing from heat, add a binding of egg yolks beaten with lemon juice or good vinegar
