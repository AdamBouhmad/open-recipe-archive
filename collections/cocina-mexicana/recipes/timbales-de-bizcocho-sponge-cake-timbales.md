## Ingredients

- six eggs
- sugar equal to the weight of six eggs
- flour equal to half the weight of six eggs
- clarified butter
- flour mixed with sugar

## Directions

1. Place sugar in a bowl with the egg yolks and set the whites aside in a small pot
2. Beat the yolks and sugar with two spatulas, incorporating the desired flavoring at the same time
3. Add the flour mixed with the beaten whites, stirring everything lightly and the batter should be sufficiently mixed but not overworked
4. Prepare a very clean mold, coated with clarified butter and heated over the fire with flour mixed with sugar, and fill it with the said batter
5. Place in the oven at a temperature somewhat less than moderately hot, and although it browns on top, do not worry about the rest of the interior part
6. Remove when cooked
