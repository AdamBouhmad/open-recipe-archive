## Ingredients

- cream or milk, enough to fill twelve small cups
- six ounces of sugar
- one pound of sweet almonds
- eight bitter almonds, very finely ground
- one and a half sticks of fish glue, reduced to small pieces and dissolved
- one cuartillo of water

## Directions

1. Heat enough cream to fill twelve small cups with six ounces of sugar
2. Dissolve one pound of sweet almonds and eight bitter almonds, very finely ground, in the hot cream
3. Strain everything through a fine cloth, wringing it
4. Beat one and a half sticks of fish glue, reduced to small pieces and dissolved, in one cuartillo of water, letting it cook two hours over low heat
5. Pass through a strainer and add it to the blancmange, which should be lukewarm
6. Fill the small cups with this mixture, place them in ice or in a cool place, and serve when they have coagulated
