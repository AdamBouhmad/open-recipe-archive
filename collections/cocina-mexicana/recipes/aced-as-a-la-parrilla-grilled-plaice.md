## Ingredients

- Plaice
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the plaice
2. Soak them in oil
3. Sprinkle with salt and then with pepper
4. Place on a grill to roast
5. Dress them afterwards by pouring Italian sauce over them
