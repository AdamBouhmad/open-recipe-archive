## Ingredients

- Xitomatl (tomatoes), roasted or cooked
- Ten dried ancho chiles per eight xitomatl
- Lard
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Ten peeled walnuts
- Some pine nuts
- Hot water
- Whole sesame seeds
- Candied fruit (acitrón)
- Chiles
- Cinnamon
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Roast or cook the xitomatl and press out the seeds, removing the veins
2. Brown ten ancho chiles in lard for each eight xitomatl
3. Grind the chiles together with a measure of toasted sesame seed
4. Fry everything in lard
5. Add ten peeled walnuts and some pine nuts, all ground together
6. After frying well, add hot water with the poultry to be stewed
7. Add some whole sesame seeds, candied fruit, chiles, cinnamon, vinegar and salt
8. Stew until cooked through
