## Ingredients

- prepared nixtamal from common corn
- twelve ounces of melted butter
- a little cinnamon, according to taste
- five ounces of aged cheese
- four eggs
- two pounds or more of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the nixtamal as for common tortillas, rub it well, remove the head, and put it to dry
2. Grind it afterwards, sift through a sieve and put it in a very clean batea or box
3. Mix with twelve ounces of melted butter, a little cinnamon according to taste, five ounces of aged cheese, and four eggs, and beat the dough well until the bowl is clean when mixing or beating
4. Then add two pounds or more of powdered sugar, and when well incorporated, make the tortillitas which are put to cook on a comal
5. Place very clean little stones of iron on top so the tortillitas do not stick
6. Turn them and lift them when cooked with little spatulas so they do not break apart
