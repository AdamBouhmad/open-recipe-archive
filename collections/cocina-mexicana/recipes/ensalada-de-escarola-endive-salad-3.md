## Ingredients

- endive
- soaked chiles
- roasted garlic cloves
- cumin
- vinegar
- grated cheese
- onion
- oregano
- salt
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Soak the endives in water to remove their bitterness, and change the water until they no longer taste bitter; divide them into four parts or chop coarsely.
2. Make a sauce from soaked chiles, roasted garlic cloves and cumin, all ground and dissolved in vinegar; pour it over the endives, adding grated cheese, finely chopped onion, oregano, seasoning everything with sufficient salt, and pouring raw oil over the top.
