## Ingredients

- Best piece of beef pulp from the leg
- Fat ham
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Parsley
- Celery
- Nutmeg
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Four onions
- Four carrots
- Mixed bouquet
- One cow's foot
- Water
- Brandy
- White wine

## Directions

1. Choose the best piece of beef pulp from the leg and lard it with fat ham
2. Season with black pepper
3. Place in a well-sealed casserole with four onions, four carrots, a mixed bouquet, one cow's foot and a good glass of water
4. Place on fire and let the broth reduce to aspic, basting it so it does not burn, taking great care of this
5. When it has congealed well, stop moistening it with water
6. Add half a glass of brandy and a good glass of white wine with a little salt
7. Place on two fires to cook slowly for four or five hours
