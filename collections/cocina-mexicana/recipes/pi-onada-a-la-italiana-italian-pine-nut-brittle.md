## Ingredients

- one quarter of fresh shelled pine nuts
- one quarter of toasted and cleaned peanuts
- one pound of syrup prepared at flower point
- ground and sifted cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix the fresh shelled pine nuts and toasted cleaned peanuts with one pound of syrup prepared at flower point
2. Remove from heat and let everything rest until lukewarm
3. As the piñonada begins to soften, dust with ground and sifted cinnamon
4. Remove with a fork and spread on a clean sheet of paper
5. Dust a second time with cinnamon of the same kind
6. After approximately half an hour, once the sugar has set on the piñonada, cut into pieces of desired shape and arrange as convenient
