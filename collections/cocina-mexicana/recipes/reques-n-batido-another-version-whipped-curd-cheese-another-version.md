## Ingredients

- requesón (curd cheese)
- syrup
- virgin honey

## Directions

1. Place the requesón in a bowl with syrup
2. Work and stir so that it incorporates well
3. Add virgin honey in quantity equal to the syrup
4. Continue beating until it becomes very white
5. Serve in layers on top of other layers of sponge cake
