## Ingredients

- cebollas (onions)
- xitomates (tomatoes)
- habas verdes cocidas (cooked green beans)
- calabacitas chiquitas de milpa (small squash)
- ejotes muy tiernos (tender green beans)
- caldo de carnero o menudo (lamb or offal broth)
- sal (salt)
- clavo (clove)
- azafrán (saffron)
- canela (cinnamon)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- azúcar (sugar)
- carnero cocido o menudo (cooked lamb or offal)
- plátano (plantain)
- manzana (apple)

## Directions

1. Fry a competent quantity of coarsely chopped onions and tomatoes
2. When well fried, add cooked and peeled green beans, small squash, and very tender green beans, all cooked separately
3. Add broth from cooking the lamb or offal, season with sufficient salt and ground clove, saffron and cinnamon
4. Add a little vinegar and a bit of sugar
5. Add the cooked lamb or offal in small pieces
6. Add slices of plantain, apple and other fruits if desired
