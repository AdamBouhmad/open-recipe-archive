## Ingredients

- melon seeds
- watermelon seeds
- squash seeds
- cucumber seeds
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind together the melon seeds, watermelon seeds, squash seeds, and cucumber seeds
2. Strain the mixture
3. Dissolve in water
4. Sweeten with sugar
