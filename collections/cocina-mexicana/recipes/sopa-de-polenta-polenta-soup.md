## Ingredients

- corn (cacahuatzentl) ground into very fine flour
- water or milk
- salt
- cheese from Flanders or other good cheese, grated
- butter, melted
- braised broth or rabioles broth

## Directions

1. Grind corn into very fine flour and pass through a sieve
2. Pour into a pot with a little water or milk with sufficient salt and place over fire, stirring continuously with a wooden stick until all moisture is consumed and it loses the taste of flour
3. Form the resulting dough into a cake on a clean napkin and let cool
4. Cut into slices using a thread
5. In the serving dish, arrange a layer of grated Flanders or other good cheese with a little melted butter, then a layer of polenta slices, then another layer of cheese and butter, alternating until all is placed
6. Pour braised broth or rabioles broth very hot over it and place the soup tureen in the steam of a pot
7. Also place a comal with gentle fire on top, ensuring it is neither too thick nor too thin
