## Ingredients

- eight ounces of quince juice
- one pound of white sugar

## Directions

1. Extract eight ounces of quince juice by grating it, wrapping it in linen and pressing it in a press
2. Boil the pulp with sufficient water and strain it by pressing
3. Combine everything and add one pound of white sugar
4. Put it on the fire until it has the consistency of syrup
5. Let it cool, filter it so it is very clear and keep it for use
