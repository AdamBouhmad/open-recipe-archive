## Ingredients

- little less than a pound and a half of flour
- little less than one quarter pound of butter
- half a pound of peeled almonds, well ground
- two chicken breasts, very finely ground
- a few drops of cooking oil
- a little warm water with settled tequesquite

## Directions

1. Knead everything together, adding liquid if it is very hard
2. Form the pastelón from this dough
