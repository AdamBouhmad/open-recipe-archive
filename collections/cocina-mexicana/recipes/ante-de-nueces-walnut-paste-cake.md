## Ingredients

- walnuts
- almonds
- pine nuts
- clarified syrup
- orange blossom water
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the walnuts and remove their bitter skins
2. Take equal parts walnuts, almonds and pine nuts
3. Grind all together
4. Mix into well-clarified syrup
5. Cook with orange blossom water
6. Layer sponge cake sprinkled with syrup and cinnamon
7. Add walnut mixture between layers with all required garnishes and cinnamon
