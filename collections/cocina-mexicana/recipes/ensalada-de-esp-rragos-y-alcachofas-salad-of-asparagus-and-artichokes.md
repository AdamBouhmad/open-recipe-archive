## Ingredients

- cooked asparagus
- cooked artichokes
- salt
- oil
- garlic cloves
- poblano chiles
- vinegar
- pepper
- butter
- small onions

## Directions

1. After the asparagus are cooked as described in previous sections, take well-cooked artichokes in salted water
2. Remove all the soft substance from both the asparagus and artichokes, trying to extract the artichoke hearts as whole as possible
3. Heat oil in a pan over the fire, brown whole garlic cloves and remove them
4. Roast and peel poblano chiles that have been deveined and well-cleaned, break them into pieces and fry in the oil where the garlic was fried, which should also be removed
5. In that same oil fry the substance extracted from the asparagus and artichokes, adding a splash of vinegar
6. Once fried, set aside and sprinkle the artichoke hearts with salt and pepper, then fry them in butter with small cooked onions
7. When golden, set them aside
8. Place the fried asparagus substance on a platter and top with the chiles and garlic that were fried, the onions, and the artichoke hearts
9. Serve hot or dressed with oil, vinegar, and salt
