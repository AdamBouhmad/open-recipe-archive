## Ingredients

- parsley
- almonds
- garlic
- tomatoes
- hard egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- capers
- olives
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a bunch of parsley, then remove it
2. Grind it with a few almonds, clean garlic cloves, tomatoes and two or three hard egg yolks
3. Dissolve this mixture in oil and vinegar
4. Season with salt
5. Add capers, olives and chiles in vinegar
