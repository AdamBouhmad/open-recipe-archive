## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- three eggs (two with whites and one yolk only)
- a small amount of leavening dissolved in a small amount of tequesquite water
- less than half a cup of butter
- one cup of melted lard
- half a small cup of generous wine
- sifted sugar
- juice of two sour limes
- water as needed
- half a pound of soaked almonds without shells
- half a pound of pine nuts
- one pound of sifted sugar
- one large cup well filled with custard
- six egg yolks
- one quarter of cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Mix flour with three eggs (two with whites and one yolk only), a small amount of leavening dissolved in tequesquite water, less than half a cup of butter, one cup of melted lard, half a cup of generous wine, sifted sugar, juice of two sour limes and water as needed
2. Knead and work until smooth
3. Make tortillas as in the previous recipe
4. For the filling, grind half a pound of soaked almonds without shells with half a pound of pine nuts
5. Incorporate this with one pound of sifted sugar, one large cup well filled with custard, six egg yolks and one quarter of cinnamon powder
6. Assemble and bake as in the previous recipe
