## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk
- a stick of cinnamon
- a green lemon peel
- twelve egg yolks
- one pound of sugar
- six ounces of sugar for caramel

## Directions

1. Boil four cuartillos of milk with a stick of cinnamon and a green lemon peel
2. Let it thicken until it resembles atole and cool
3. Beat twelve egg yolks until very stiff
4. Mix with the milk, one pound of sugar, beat again and strain
5. Grind six ounces of sugar and place in a pan to form caramel
6. When it foams heavily, pour into a tinplate mold to cover all sides well
7. When cooled, pour the milk mixture and place to cook in a water bath
8. Place fire on top until it browns and is well cooked
