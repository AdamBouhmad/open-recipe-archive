## Ingredients

- Young hare shoulders
- Young hare head
- Young hare neck
- Young hare rear section with loins
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Laurel
- Garlic
- Breadcrumbs
- French mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Separate the shoulders with hands, the head and neck of a young hare
2. Cook the rear section united with the loins with butter, salt, pepper, laurel, and a little garlic
3. Allow it to cool
4. Roll in breadcrumbs
5. Cook on the grill
6. Arrange on French mustard sauce
