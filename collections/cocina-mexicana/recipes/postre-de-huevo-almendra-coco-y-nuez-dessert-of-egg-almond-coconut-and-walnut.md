## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- six ounces of almonds
- four ounces of walnuts
- three ounces of coconut
- forty egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point with three pounds of sugar
2. Away from heat, mix in six ounces of almonds, four of walnuts, and three of coconut, all ground
3. Put on fire, and when it has thickened a little, remove it
4. Add forty egg yolks, and after well incorporating them, return to fire leaving it to reach the point of cajeta
5. Empty into the platter and brown with fire from above, and after cooling decorate with ground cinnamon
