## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- sugar
- strong tea infusion
- milk

## Directions

1. Butter the bread slices with which you line a soup tureen
2. Sprinkle them with sugar
3. Pour over them a glass of strong tea infusion and double the amount of milk
