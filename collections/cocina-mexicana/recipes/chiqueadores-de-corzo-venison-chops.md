## Ingredients

- two venison shoulder cuts
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf
- butter (three tablespoons)
- pepper sauce (concentrated)
- game jelly

## Directions

1. After lifting the meat from two venison shoulders, remove sinews and round them, making them the same size
2. Place on a pan with salt, coarse pepper, garlic, and bay leaf
3. Put on a stove with somewhat strong heat just before serving
4. When the chops resist when touched with a finger, turn them and add butter, three tablespoons of thick pepper sauce, and a little game jelly
5. Mix and bind everything by stirring vigorously
6. Serve with fried bread croutons around it
