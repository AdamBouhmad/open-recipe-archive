## Ingredients

- onion
- garlic
- xitomate
- parsley
- clove
- black pepper
- cinnamon
- broad chiles
- thyme
- pork mesentery
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- salt
- pork blood
- pork intestines
- lime

## Directions

1. Prepare a bowl with onion, garlic, xitomate, parsley, clove, black pepper, cinnamon, and deveined and toasted broad chiles, all ground
2. Add thyme in powder form if dry, or chopped if fresh
3. Add small pieces of pork mesentery, raisins, sliced almonds, and pine nuts
4. Season everything with the necessary salt
5. Receive the pig's blood over this seasoning mixture, stirring well with it before it cools so that everything becomes well incorporated
6. Add more salt if needed
7. With this mixture fill the pork intestines after washing them well, preparing them with salt and rubbed with lime and rinsed afterwards
8. Tie at the convenient distance according to the desired size of the sausages
9. Immediately place them to cook in boiling water
