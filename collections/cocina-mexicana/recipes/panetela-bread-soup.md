## Ingredients

- broth
- grated bread
- sugar

## Directions

1. After soaking the bread crumb, grind it, strain it, and dissolve some egg yolks into it, sweetening with sugar
2. Add a small stick of cinnamon
3. Put it on the heat, letting it boil until it reaches the consistency of atole
