## Ingredients

- chiles
- picadillo de carne de puerco (minced pork)
- nogada (walnut sauce)
- granos de granada (pomegranate seeds)

## Directions

1. Fill the chiles with minced pork as previously described
2. Fry the chiles
3. Arrange on a plate
4. Cover with nogada sauce (see pages 122 and 124) or any other nogada
5. Garnish with pomegranate seeds
