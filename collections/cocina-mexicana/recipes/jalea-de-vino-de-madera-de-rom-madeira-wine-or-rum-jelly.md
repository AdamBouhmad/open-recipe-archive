## Ingredients

- five cups of liquor
- one ounce of fish glue
- sugar in proportion

## Directions

1. For eight jars or cups of jelly, use five cups of liquor, one ounce of fish glue and sugar in proportion.
2. Follow the same procedures as in the preceding article.
