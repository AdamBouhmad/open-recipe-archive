## Ingredients

- Milk
- Sweetener
- Spices
- Egg yolks, three per cup
- Biscuits

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to boil and when it begins to thicken add sweetener to taste and spices
2. Measure into cups, and for each one add three egg yolks
3. When cool put back on heat until it thickens and pour back into the cups with whole biscuits inside
