## Ingredients

- chicken or mutton
- chickpeas (optional)
- ham (optional)
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make regularly with chicken or mutton, adding some chickpeas and ham if it is to be sustaining.
2. The broth should be cooked before serving.
3. If you want it to have more substance, grind the chicken in the metate with bread crumbs and dissolve in broth, then strain, and seasoned place on the fire, but without letting it boil.
