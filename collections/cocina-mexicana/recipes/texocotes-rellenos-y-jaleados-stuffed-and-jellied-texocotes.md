## Ingredients

- large texocotes
- boiling water
- clarified syrup
- coconut preserve or almond paste or other sweet paste

## Directions

1. Choose the largest texocotes and remove the crown
2. Place in boiling water and cook until tender
3. Remove and let cool
4. Peel them and remove the pits with a suitable small spoon made of tin or iron
5. Dry them and place in clarified syrup that has been prepared in advance
6. Let them boil until the jelly forms
7. Before the jelly cools and sets, remove them carefully without handling roughly, using small pointed sticks shaped like a fork
8. As they drain, fill them with a small spatula or spoon of coconut preserve or almond paste or other sweet paste
9. Arrange them on a platter and pour their syrup or jelly over them, having let it reduce more
