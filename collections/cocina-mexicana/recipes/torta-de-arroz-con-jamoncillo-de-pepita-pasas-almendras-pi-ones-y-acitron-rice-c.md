## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- one heaping cup of sifted rice
- jamoncillos (small hams)
- almonds
- raisins
- citron
- pine nuts
- butter
- three pounds very white sugar
- raisins, almonds and pine nuts for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs, first the whites and then the yolks, and mix in a heaping cup of sifted rice, some well crumbled jamoncillos, almonds, raisins, citron and pine nuts
2. All together fry in a cake pan with plenty of butter, and when the cake is cooked, add syrup of medium point, made with three pounds of very white sugar, and let it boil until the cake is thick
3. Decorate for serving with raisins, almonds and pine nuts on top
