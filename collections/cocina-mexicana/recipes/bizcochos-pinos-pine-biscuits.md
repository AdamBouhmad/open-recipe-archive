## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- twelve ounces of ground sugar
- twelve ounces of butter
- sixteen eggs
- one quarter ounce of salt
- half a pound of yeast

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well
2. Form the biscuits
3. Toast in the oven
