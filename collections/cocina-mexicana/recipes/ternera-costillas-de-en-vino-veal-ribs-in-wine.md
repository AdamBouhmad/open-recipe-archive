## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- meat broth
- Málaga wine
- salt
- Madeira wine or Málaga wine

## Directions

1. Place the ribs to cook in equal parts meat broth and Málaga wine with salt.
2. When cooked, remove from the heat.
3. Return the broth to the fire, and when it has thickened add a little Madeira or Málaga wine.
4. Remove from heat, degrease, return to the fire, and as soon as it boils, pour over the cooked ribs.
5. Serve hot.
