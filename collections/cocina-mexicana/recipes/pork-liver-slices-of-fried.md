## Ingredients

- pork liver
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaf
- mustard sauce
- parsley
- shallot
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into very thin slices
2. Fry in butter with salt, pepper, garlic, and bay leaf
3. Once the liver is cooked, remove it from the pan and arrange on a plate
4. Remove the butter from the pan and replace with a few spoonfuls of mustard sauce
5. Add a little chopped parsley and shallot to the sauce
6. Let it reduce as needed
7. Add a little fresh butter
8. Pour over the liver slices
