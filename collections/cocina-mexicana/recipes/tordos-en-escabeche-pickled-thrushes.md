## Ingredients

- thrushes
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- spices (substantial amount)
- brine (hot)
- fat

## Directions

1. Remove the head and legs.
2. Place on grills or roaster to half-toast them.
3. In this form put in vinegar with bay leaves and abundant spices.
4. Place afterwards in a vessel, adding hot brine and covering with a layer of fat.
5. Close the vessel with a bladder or parchment and put in a cool place.
6. In this way they will keep for more than a year.
