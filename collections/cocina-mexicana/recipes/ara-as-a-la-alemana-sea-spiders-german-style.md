## Ingredients

- six sea spiders
- anchovies
- eels
- butter
- half bottle white wine
- basil
- bay leaf or clove
- parsley
- onion
- chirivia or xitomates
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare six sea spiders by removing their heads and tail extremities
2. Stuff them with strips of anchovies and eels
3. Place in a casserole with some butter, half a bottle of white wine, a little basil, a bay leaf or clove in its place, parsley, onion and chirivia or xitomates
4. Let them cook
5. Once cooked, remove them from the broth, which will be strained through a sieve into a casserole
6. Add a little butter mixed with flour, let it cook and reduce until it reaches the consistency of sauce
7. Add lemon juice at the moment of serving
8. Drain the sea spiders, arrange them on plates, pour this sauce over them and serve
