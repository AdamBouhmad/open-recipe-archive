## Ingredients

- Beef leg wheel, at least six inches high
- Scarlet tongue (see pages 98 and 468), cut in strips or long lards
- Carrots
- Onions
- Mixed herbs bouquet
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Concentrated broth (page 131)
- Spanish sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut from the thickest part of the leg
2. Remove the bone from the middle
3. Lard with scarlet tongue cut in strips or long lards
4. Fix with forks or skewers to maintain shape
5. Tie with string
6. Put to cook for four hours in a proper braising vessel with carrots, onions, mixed herbs bouquet, nutmeg, salt and pepper
7. Moisten everything with concentrated broth
8. After the leg is cooked, remove fat from the broth
9. Mix in some spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
10. Let thicken, strain through cloth and mix with lemon juice
11. Pour this sauce over the wheel which will have been jellied
