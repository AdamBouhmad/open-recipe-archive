## Ingredients

- scraps of small game like partridges, hares, birds and veal feet
- half bottle of white wine
- two onions
- salt
- pot broth
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Place scraps in a pot with half a bottle of white wine and two onions
2. Season everything and cook until it reduces to a jelly
3. Moisten with a few spoonfuls of pot broth so it does not stick
4. When everything is well cooked, strain and clarify with two egg whites
