## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sugar
- One coconut of regular size, ground
- Carmine dye
- Wafers

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with two pounds of sugar to the thread stage and remove from heat.
2. Mix off the heat with one ground coconut of regular size.
3. Return the pan to the heat until the syrup regains its original point.
4. Remove from heat again and beat on one side only so it does not become grainy.
5. Before it sets, place on white wafers, tint part of the paste with carmine so that the two colors mixed imitate ham, and cut as desired.
