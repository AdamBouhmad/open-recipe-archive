## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- broth
- saffron
- clove
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and soak the rice in two or three waters
2. Once dry, moisten with water and cook either by steam tied in a napkin or in little water, seasoned with corresponding salt
3. Once cooked, set aside and the next day drain well and mix with a little salt or without it
4. Moisten with broth or mix with small amounts of saffron, clove, and ground pepper dissolved in water
5. Beat well
