## Ingredients

- tongue
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- butter
- ham
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- bread, fried
- thyme
- oil
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Boil the tongue in a pot
2. Chop plenty of onion, garlic and a bit of parsley
3. Put butter in a pot and add this mixture with ham slices
4. When fried, add the tongue cut in slices
5. Fry a bit more and add the same broth in which the tongue boiled
6. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper with fried bread slices and a bit of thyme
7. To bring to the table add oil, chiles, olives and capers
