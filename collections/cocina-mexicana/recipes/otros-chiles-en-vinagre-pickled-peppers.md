## Ingredients

- Large chiles
- Vinegar
- Castilian vinegar
- Salt
- White panela
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Rosemary
- Yerba Santa María

## Directions

1. Take the largest chiles and split them through the middle with a knife making it pass through the other side
2. Take a pot and add much regular vinegar and a little Castilian vinegar, sufficient salt and a piece of white panela
3. Take a head of garlic and grind it with skin and a few cumin seeds, cinnamon, clove, pepper and all this ground add to the vinegar, also fine and regular oregano, thyme, laurel, a little rosemary, two or three leaves of yerba Santa María, two or three crushed heads of garlic and as many whole ones
4. Put all this in the pot to cook the garlic
5. When the garlic is half cooked add the chiles
