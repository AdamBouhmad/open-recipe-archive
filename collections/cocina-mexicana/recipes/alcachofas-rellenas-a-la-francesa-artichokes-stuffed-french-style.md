## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Cooked mushrooms
- Fresh parsley
- Tender garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes and remove the fuzzy center as in previous recipes.
2. Fill them with a mixture of cooked mushrooms, raw parsley, and tender garlic, all finely chopped and seasoned with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Place this mixture in a little butter and oil, leaving it to half-fry; stir everything together in the butter and oil to mix well, and use this to fill the artichokes.
4. Place a covered dish with slices of ham, arranging the artichokes in it and pouring over them what remains of the butter and oil, moistening the dish with white wine and water, or meat broth.
5. Place over gentle heat and cover the dish with a grid that has low heat.
6. When well seasoned, they can be served.
