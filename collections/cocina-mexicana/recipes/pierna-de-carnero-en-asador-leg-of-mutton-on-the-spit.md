## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- oil
- pimiento
- onion
- parsley
- garlic

## Directions

1. Let the leg hang for one day to air
2. Beat it lightly so it becomes more tender
3. Place it in a marinade for one day with oil, pimiento, onion, and parsley
4. Insert whole garlic cloves in various parts of the meat
5. Place on the spit over very high heat so it roasts quickly
6. Turn it frequently during cooking, rolling it with its drippings and the marinade
7. It should be on the fire for at least an hour and a half
