## Ingredients

- Four cuartillos of double cream from milk
- Half a pound of powdered sugar
- A pinch of gum tragacanth, in powder
- A little candied orange blossom
- Three drops of bergamot essence
- Six pounds of snow
- Saltpeter

## Directions

1. Pour the double cream, powdered sugar, and powdered gum tragacanth into a stone or earthenware vessel, adding a little candied orange blossom and three drops of bergamot essence
2. When the sugar has dissolved, crush six pounds of snow and place it in another vessel of the same material with saltpeter
3. Place the bottom of the vessel containing the cream on the snow to cool it
4. To make it foam more quickly, beat it with the same instrument used for beating egg whites
5. As the cream turns to foam, remove it with a skimmer, rake, or comb and pour it into a sieve placed over a basin
6. If the cream does not foam as much as needed, help it with two egg whites
7. If there is not enough foam after all has been beaten, take what has passed through the sieve, beat or whip it again, and mix it with the other
8. These foams are usually placed in large tin or silver goblets made expressly for this; when these are not available, use glass ones placed in tin containers made for this purpose with a grill in the shape of the goblets
9. Place crushed snow mixed with saltpeter underneath and also spread over the lid of the containers, which should be made like the upper part of a campaign oven with a gutter for water to drain
10. This precaution is essential to keep the foams fresh, which can be preserved two or three hours before serving
