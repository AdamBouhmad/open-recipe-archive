## Ingredients

- pork head
- fresh pork meat scraps
- salt
- whole pepper
- ground spices
- parsley
- onions
- parsnips
- bay leaves
- thyme
- basil
- parsley with chives
- cloves of spice
- salt
- pork or other meat scraps
- water

## Directions

1. Bone the head and include with it any scraps of fresh pork meat available
2. Season everything with salt, whole pepper, ground spices, parsley, and chopped onions
3. Place in a suitable vessel and leave for nine or ten days
4. Remove and dry
5. Gather the pieces of meat and refill the head so it recovers its original shape
6. Sew closed the opening where it was boned and tie so it maintains its shape while cooking
7. Wrap in clean linen cloth tied at the ends
8. Place in a brazier or pot with the bones and skins that were removed, nine or ten parsnips, as many onions, seven or eight bay leaves, as many branches of thyme and basil, a good bundle of parsley with chives, seven cloves of spice, and a good handful of salt, along with any scraps of pork or other meat
9. Add water until the head is well submerged
10. Let it cook over low heat for nine or ten hours
11. When cooked, remove and leave for two hours in its broth
12. Remove from there with another clean cloth
