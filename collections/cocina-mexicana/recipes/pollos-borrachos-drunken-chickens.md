## Ingredients

- cooked chickens
- egg
- wine
- cinnamon
- sugar
- almonds
- posas
- citron
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame seeds
- bread

## Directions

1. After cooking, fry the chickens with egg
2. Add wine, cinnamon and sugar to the same pot
3. Decorate on top with almonds, posas, citron, pine nuts, toasted sesame seeds and bread
4. Keep the pot warm over the steam of a boiling pot until serving time
