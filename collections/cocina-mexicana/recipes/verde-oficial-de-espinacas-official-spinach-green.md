## Ingredients

- spinach leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After washing a sufficient quantity of spinach leaves, grind them in a mortar
2. Press with a cloth or within a cotton fabric to extract the juice
3. Pour this juice into a pan that will be placed on the fire so the spinach boils there for a few moments
4. Then strain in a sieve and use both the residue and juice as needed
