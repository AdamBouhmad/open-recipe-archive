## Ingredients

- rabbit
- rabbit bones, crushed
- veal offal
- lean bacon
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Remove bones and membranes from rabbit and mince what remains very finely
2. Braise the crushed bones with veal offal, lean bacon, salt and pepper in lard, dusting everything with a little flour
3. Stir well and add milk, and once it has boiled for one hour, remove and pass through a sieve, reducing to the consistency of a cooked dish, putting with it the minced rabbit, which will be thickened solely by this
