## Ingredients

- coconut
- syrup at medium point
- raisins
- almonds
- aromatics
- sponge cake (mamón)
- pastille
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Remove the brown skin from coconut
2. Grate or grind it
3. Add to syrup at medium point
4. Preserve it
5. Add raisins, almonds and aromatics
6. Layer this mixture and sponge cake
7. Top with pastille and sesame seeds
