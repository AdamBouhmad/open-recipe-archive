## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- potato flour
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes and cut them into thin slices or into long quarters
2. Coat them in a frying batter, preferring potato flour for this
3. Fry them in butter until they acquire a beautiful color
4. Before serving, dust them with white salt
