## Ingredients

- two onions
- eight carrots
- two turnips
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Peel, wash, and cut two onions, eight carrots, and two turnips
2. Place in boiling water for a quarter hour over low heat
3. Refresh and dry them
4. Sauté with lard or butter, stirring from time to time so they do not burn
