## Ingredients

- Double jonquil flowers
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Take double jonquil flowers and cut the stem a quarter of an inch from the flower
2. Place in clarified syrup so it only gives one boil in it
3. Remove from fire and leave for a quarter hour to release its moisture
4. Strain and process according to the methods described for violet and jasmine candied sugars
