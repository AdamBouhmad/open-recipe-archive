## Ingredients

- Lard
- Parsley
- Roasted tomatoes (ground)
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cilantro (toasted and ground)
- Nutmeg (grated)
- Capers
- Wine
- Cooked hen quarters
- Hen broth
- Flour (browned in lard, if needed)

## Directions

1. Fry plenty of parsley and roasted tomatoes in lard, ground so that the green color predominates.
2. Add ground cloves, pepper, and toasted cilantro, grated nutmeg, capers, and wine.
3. Add the cooked hen quarters and a little of their broth.
4. Let boil until completely seasoned.
5. If the broth is too thin, thicken with flour browned in lard.
