## Ingredients

- Beef sirloin
- Butter (size of half an egg)
- Water or broth

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter the size of half an egg in a pot
2. Place the sirloin there and partially cook over lively fire
3. Add a little water or broth
4. Let it finish cooking over low fire
