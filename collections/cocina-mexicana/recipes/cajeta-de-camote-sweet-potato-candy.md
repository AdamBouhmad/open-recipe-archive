## Ingredients

- Three pounds of fine clarified sugar
- two pounds of white sweet potato, ground and sifted

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of fine clarified sugar with two pounds of ground and sifted white sweet potato
2. Cook, stirring, until it pulls away from the pot
3. Add orange blossom water
4. Cool and fill small molds or form into small candies
5. Roll in sifted sugar
6. Air dry and dry in the sun until they take on color
