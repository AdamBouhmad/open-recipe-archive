## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- lard
- onion
- tomato
- garlic cloves
- green chiles

## Directions

1. After cooking, divide into slices and remove the bones, being careful not to leave the pieces too small
2. Fry onion in lard
3. Add well colored tomato, garlic cloves and green chiles, all chopped
4. The entire mixture should be substantial in volume
