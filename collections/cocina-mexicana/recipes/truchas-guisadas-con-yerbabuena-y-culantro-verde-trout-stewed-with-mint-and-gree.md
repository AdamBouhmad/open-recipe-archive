## Ingredients

- trout
- butter
- salt
- onion
- mint
- green cilantro
- parsley
- bread
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- water
- small chiles
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the trout well and divide into portions
2. Fry the portions in butter with salt, then remove them
3. In the same fat, add finely chopped onion, mint, green cilantro and parsley, all ground
4. When this mixture is fried, add soaked bread ground with cloves, pepper and cinnamon, vinegar and water
5. When the sauce comes to a boil, add the trout portions and let them boil once
6. When removing from heat, add small chiles, olives and oil
