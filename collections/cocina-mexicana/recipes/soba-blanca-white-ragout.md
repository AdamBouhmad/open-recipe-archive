## Ingredients

- leftover veal from the previous day
- broth from other meats and poultry
- broth from the pot
- egg yolks
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover veal into small pieces
2. Clarify and reduce the broth from other meats and poultry with broth from the pot
3. Bind the sauce with egg yolks
4. Add a little butter and parsley
