## Ingredients

- Quails or partridges
- Butter
- Salt
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the birds.
2. Give them a turn on the spit.
3. Heat a pan with butter over the fire.
4. Mix salt, a little bread crumbs, a pinch of pepper, and one egg yolk per two quails.
5. Baste the birds with this mixture using a feather, turning them until well browned.
6. Prepare partridges in the same manner.
