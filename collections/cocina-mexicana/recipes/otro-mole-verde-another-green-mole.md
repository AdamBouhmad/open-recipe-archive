## Ingredients

- Tomatillos (green tomatoes)
- Garlic cloves
- Pork meat
- Broth in which meat was cooked
- Cooked zucchini
- Green chiles
- Yerba santa (holy herb)

## Directions

1. Take green tomatoes and garlic cloves, grind them and fry
2. Add pork meat with the broth in which it was cooked and some cooked zucchini
3. Once seasoned, add ground green chiles and yerba santa and let it boil
