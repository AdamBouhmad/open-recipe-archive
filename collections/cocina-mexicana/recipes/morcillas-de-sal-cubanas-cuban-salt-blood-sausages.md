## Ingredients

- pig intestines
- cooked pork blood
- ground and toasted spices
- small pieces of fatty meat
- salt water
- fried tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the intestines to be filled, tying them with a small cord at the tip.
2. Pour in the cooked pork blood with the ground and toasted spices.
3. Add small pieces of fatty meat cut into very small pieces.
4. Mix everything together.
5. After filling, tie another small cord at the mouth.
6. Place in salted water to cook.
7. Taste and remove, cutting into pieces.
8. Fry the pieces and add fried tomato sauce.
