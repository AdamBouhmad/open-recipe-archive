## Ingredients

- butter
- one onion, finely chopped
- one glass of water
- potatoes, peeled
- pepper
- salt
- mint, parsley, tarragon and other herbs in a bunch

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter and a finely chopped onion in a pot and let it fry until it takes on a good color.
2. Add a glass of water and the peeled potatoes with pepper, salt, and a small bunch of mint, parsley, tarragon, etc.
3. Let everything cook slowly.
4. Before serving this stew, remove the herb bundle.
