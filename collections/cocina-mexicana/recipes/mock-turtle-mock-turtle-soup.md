## Ingredients

- piece of upper part of calf's head, partially cooked and prepared
- parsley
- thyme
- marjoram
- savory
- onions
- bay leaf
- mushrooms
- lean ham
- cloves
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- butter
- flour
- Madeira wine
- meat stock
- veal pâté with brains
- artichokes in small balls
- whole yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- lemon juice
- Tabasco pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Half-cook a piece of the upper part of calf's head, already partially prepared, in salted water
2. Cut into small dice
3. Combine with parsley, thyme, marjoram, savory, onions, bay leaf, mushrooms, lean ham, cloves, pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Macerate or render all in butter
5. Remove these ingredients from the pot and make a red roux with flour in the same pot
6. Return all the prepared ingredients to the pot and add the necessary amount of water for the soup, which should have the consistency of a substantial or strained broth
7. Let boil and skim for two hours, then pass through a sieve
8. Add Madeira wine and meat stock if desired, veal pâté with brains and artichokes in small balls, whole yolks of hard-boiled eggs, lemon juice, Tabasco pepper, and salt if the ham is not too salty
9. Serve the pieces of calf's head, mushrooms with the balls and yolks in this broth, without any bread
10. Serve boiling, in individual cups to keep it warmer longer
