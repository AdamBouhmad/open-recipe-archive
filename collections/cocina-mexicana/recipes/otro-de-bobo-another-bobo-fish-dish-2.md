- vinagre de Castilla
- aceite
- manteca
- papel
- hilo

## Directions

1. Chop parsley with plenty of garlic.
2. Grind dry clove, cinnamon, pepper, oregano, marjoram and bay leaf.
3. Clean the bobo and dry with a napkin, soak with Castilian vinegar and stuff with the seasoning.
4. Coat with oil, oil a paper with lard and wrap the bobo in it, tying with string.
5. Place in a cooking pot over two fires.
6. Make a small sauce with finely chopped parsley, onion, oil, vinegar, capers, salt and pepper.
7. Bathe the bobo with this sauce when placing on the platter to serve.
8. Garnish with olives and capers.
