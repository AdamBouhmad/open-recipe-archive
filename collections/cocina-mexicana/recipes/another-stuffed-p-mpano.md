## Ingredients

- pámpano fish
- tomato without seeds
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- thyme
- oil
- lard
- capers
- olives
- chile in vinegar
- almonds
- raisins
- hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Desalt the pámpano and pat dry, making sure it has no holes
2. Chop a little tomato without seeds and a little onion and garlic with a little parsley
3. Once partially cooked, add the parsley and tomato and fry well
4. Everything should be fried in oil and lard
5. Add a little pepper and thyme
6. Once everything is fried well, add capers, olives, strips of chile in vinegar, almonds, raisins and little strips of hard-boiled egg
7. With all of this stuff the fish, making sure it is tightly packed
8. Sew closed with thick thread
9. In a pan with oil and lard, fry the pámpano
10. Turn carefully so it does not fall apart and fry well on both sides
11. Remove from heat and leave in the same pan
