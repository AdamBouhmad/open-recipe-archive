## Ingredients

- pork loin portions
- a little lard
- abundant wine
- ham pieces
- butifarra slices
- longaniza pieces
- poblano chiles, whole but deseeded
- roasted eye and tomato
- toasted sesame
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- a little vinegar
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Place pork loin portions in a pot with a little lard
2. Add abundant wine, ham pieces, butifarra slices, longaniza pieces, whole but deseeded poblano chiles, roasted and ground eye and tomato, toasted sesame, cloves, pepper, cinnamon, a little vinegar and a little sugar
3. Cover the pot and let the meat cook, taking care to add more wine if the broth is consumed before the meat is finished cooking
