## Ingredients

- Beef oxtail
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Hot sauce

## Directions

1. After cooking in the pot and cooling, melt butter seasoned with salt and pepper
2. Soak the oxtail well in it for half an hour
3. Then dust with breadcrumbs, repeating this twice
4. Place on the grill and serve with hot sauce
