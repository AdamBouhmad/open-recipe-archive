## Ingredients

- Green fava beans
- Water
- Tequesquite
- Lard
- Ripe tomatoes, well ground
- Garlic, chopped
- Pork broth
- Bread, fried and ground with cumin
- Green chiles, whole

## Directions

1. Remove the fava beans from their pods and cook them in water with a little settled tequesquite
2. Remove the small heads and fry them in lard with well-ground ripe tomatoes and chopped garlic
3. Once fried, moisten with pork broth, adding a little fried bread ground with cumin and some whole green chiles
4. Once the broth has thickened, remove from heat and serve
