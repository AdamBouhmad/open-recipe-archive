## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds cheese, buttered, lightly salted, and 10-12 days old
- 1.5 pounds fresh good butter
- salt
- 8 or 9 eggs
- 1 glass of water or milk
- 3 pounds 12 ounces flour
- 2 pounds 4 ounces additional flour

## Directions

1. Knead 1.5 pounds of buttered, salted cheese that is 10-12 days old on a table with your hands until it becomes a lump-free mass
2. Add 1.5 pounds of fresh good butter, salt, and 8 or 9 eggs
3. Spread this mixture on a table and add 1 glass of water or milk
4. When moistened and as liquid as beaten egg, incorporate first 3 pounds 12 ounces of flour and then another 2 pounds 4 ounces, setting aside 2 handfuls of this flour for later
5. When the dough is well bound, dust with some of the reserved flour and knead 2 or 3 times, but gently, over the course of half an hour
6. Then spread and let rest in this state for no more than a quarter hour
7. Roll lengthwise and cut the small pastries to the desired size and thickness of two fingers
8. Place on paper greased with fresh butter
9. Arrange them, lift the edges, and bake in the oven
10. With the oven well covered, half an hour is sufficient for them to cook
11. Watch from time to time to ensure they do not burn
