## Ingredients

- clarified syrup at panochita point
- pine nuts
- almonds
- toasted sesame
- small bread or cinnamon powder (optional)

## Directions

1. Clarify syrup and bring to panochita point
2. Add pine nuts, almonds, toasted sesame, and if desired, small bread or cinnamon powder for coloring
