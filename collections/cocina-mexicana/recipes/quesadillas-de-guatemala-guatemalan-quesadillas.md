## Ingredients

- One pound rice, sifted through a sieve as for making tamales
- One pound powdered sugar
- One pound butter
- Eight eggs, beaten
- One egg, unbeaten
- A little salt
- One tablespoon lard

## Directions

1. Mix one pound sifted rice, one pound powdered sugar, one pound butter, eight beaten eggs, one unbeaten egg, a little salt, and one tablespoon lard
2. Beat the dough the same way as the dough prepared for sifted tamales, leaving it at the point that it floats in water
3. Empty it into paper boxes lined with butter or lard
4. Place them to bake in an oven that should be at the same temperature as for mamones
