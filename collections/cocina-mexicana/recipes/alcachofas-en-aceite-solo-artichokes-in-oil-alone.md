## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Orange
- Oil
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the tips of the leaves and the stem
2. Rub with orange so they do not turn black
3. Loosen them a little and add oil, pepper and salt inside
4. Put them in a pot with water so they cook, in such a way that all the water is consumed and only the oil remains
5. Serve with sauce or without it
