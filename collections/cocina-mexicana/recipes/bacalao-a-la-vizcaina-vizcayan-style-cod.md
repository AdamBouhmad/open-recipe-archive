## Ingredients

- cod
- tomatoes
- onion
- oil

## Directions

1. After soaking and cutting into pieces, bring to a boil while roasting some tomatoes on a grill
2. Remove the skin from the tomatoes and break them down well with a wooden spoon on a plate
3. Chop much finely minced onion and put it in oil to sauté and when it is still without taking color add the tomato and finish sautéing
4. Put the cod in a casserole in an orderly manner
5. Pour the tomato, onion and oil in which it was fried over it
6. Leave on low heat until the cod is well cooked
7. Be careful to stir the casserole often so that the cod takes the sauce and it is always best to do it on low heat
