## Ingredients

- eggs
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- parsley
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil eggs and remove the yolks
2. Grind the yolks in a metate, mixing them with bread crumb soaked in milk and a little parsley for color
3. Add a very small amount of water
4. Beat very well
5. Partially cook in butter
6. Use this sauce to cover the previously fried mojarra
