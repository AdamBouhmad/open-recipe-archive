## Ingredients

- lean veal from loin or leg
- butter
- onion
- white wine
- garlic cloves
- salt
- parsley
- spices
- bay leaves
- water

## Directions

1. Cut lean veal into small pieces and fry in butter
2. Place in a pot with the fat that remains
3. Fry finely chopped onion in this fat and add to the meat
4. Add white wine, crushed garlic cloves, salt, parsley, spices, and bay leaves
5. Place the pot over low heat, covering it with paper and a cup of water on top to prevent evaporation
6. Let cook for two hours and remove to serve
