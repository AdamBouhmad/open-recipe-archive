## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- five pounds of yeast
- two pounds of butter
- one pound of ground sugar
- salt as needed (the amount that fits in three finger widths)
- hot water

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour with five pounds of yeast, two pounds of butter, one pound of ground sugar, and salt dissolved in hot water
2. Knead everything very well
3. Let the dough rise for two or three hours
4. Form the breads from the dough
5. Bake in the oven at regular heat so they do not burn
