## Ingredients

- Guans
- two whole garlic heads
- two mulato or ancho chiles, well destemmed
- salt to taste
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- capers, desalted and ground
- handful of parsley leaves
- a little kid goat or veal liver, grated
- broth from cooking the guans
- a little ground clove and cinnamon

## Directions

1. After cleaning the guans well, put them to cook with two whole garlic heads, two mulato or ancho chiles very well destemmed, corresponding salt, some water and the same amount of vinegar.
2. When cooked, remove the broth and let cool.
3. When cold, skim it to collect the fat that has released.
4. Put this fat in a casserole with a bit of oil and fry a portion of capers, desalted and ground, with a handful of parsley leaves, a bit of grated kid goat or veal liver, adding it with broth from that in which the guans were cooked and a little ground clove and cinnamon.
5. When it is almost thickened, season with corresponding salt and serve.
