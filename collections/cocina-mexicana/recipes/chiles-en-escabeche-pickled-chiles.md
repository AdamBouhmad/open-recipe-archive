## Ingredients

- cien chiles cuaresmeños (one hundred quaresmal chiles)
- ajos (garlic)
- tomillo (thyme)
- mejorana (marjoram)
- laurel (bay leaf)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre muy bueno (very good vinegar)
- aceite francés (French oil)

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from one hundred chiles without toasting them
2. Prepare loose garlic cloves, thyme, marjoram, and bay leaf
3. In a tin can with a lid, place a little salt and then alternating layers of herbs and chiles
4. The last layer should be double herbs and whole garlic heads
5. Pour two bottles of very good vinegar and two bottles of French oil over everything and seal tightly
6. Every third day, shake the can carefully without letting air in
7. After fifteen days they can be eaten, adding salt if needed
