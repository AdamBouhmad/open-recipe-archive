## Ingredients

- a good handful of parsley
- onion
- butter or fresh buttermilk
- one good tablespoon of flour
- salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- one cup of broth
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mince very finely a good handful of parsley and onion, and sauté with butter or buttermilk, being careful to moderate the heat so the butter does not brown.
2. Add one good tablespoon of flour; season with salt, pepper, and a little grated nutmeg.
3. Add one cup of broth and let simmer slowly for about ten minutes.
4. Cut the meat from the boiled beef into thin slices, removing the fatty parts.
5. Let the meat simmer for a moment inside the sauce.
6. Before serving, add two beaten egg yolks, stirring them in so they incorporate well.
