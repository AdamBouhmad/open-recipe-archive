## Ingredients

- slices of sirloin
- butter or oil
- vinegar
- salt
- butter kneaded with parsley
- lemon juice or verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Arrange the sirloin slices as described for English-style steak
2. Parboil in butter or oil with a little vinegar, or with strong vinegar alone and necessary salt
3. Grill until they have dried a little
4. Serve on a hot plate with butter kneaded with parsley and lemon juice or verjuice or a dash of vinegar, which is called à la Maître d'hôtel
5. If desired, garnish with slices of chorizo or raw potatoes fried in butter
