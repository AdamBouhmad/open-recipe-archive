## Ingredients

- six table spoonfuls of water
- one complete egg and four yolks
- two cuartillos of cream or milk
- as much as one egg of butter
- six common spoonfuls of powdered sugar
- two bitter macaroons and three sweet ones
- one toasted biscuit
- one common spoonful of candied orange blossom
- four egg yolks, or more if the mixture is very thick
- five egg whites

## Directions

1. Place six tablespoonfuls of water in a pot with one complete egg and four yolks dissolved in it
2. Add two cuartillos of cream or milk and as much as one egg of butter
3. Put everything over the fire, stirring and turning constantly
4. When this composition is cooked, let it cool and add six common spoonfuls of powdered sugar, two bitter macaroons and three sweet ones, and one toasted biscuit, all of which should have been ground and left very fine
5. Add one common spoonful of candied orange blossom and mix everything
6. Add four egg yolks, or more if the mixture is very thick
7. Beat five egg whites as for biscuits and add them to the composition
8. Place the soufflé in a silver pot and conclude the operation as in the preceding articles
