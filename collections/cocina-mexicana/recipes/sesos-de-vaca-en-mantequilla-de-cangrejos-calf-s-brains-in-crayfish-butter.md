## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- crayfish butter
- thick curled sauce
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the brains as the butler's brains, drain and arrange on a plate at the moment of serving
2. Mix an amount of crayfish butter (see page 507) equal to a pigeon egg with four spoonfuls (of skimming spoons) of thick curled sauce (see page 760), taking care that the butter melts well and binds with the sauce
3. Stir and mix well without letting it boil, add a little black pepper and pour over the brains
