## Ingredients

- Chilchotes
- Lard
- Oil
- Salt
- Fresh cow cheese

## Directions

1. Toast the chilchotes, peel and cut into strips
2. Put a skillet on the fire with lard and oil
3. Fry the strips with salt and when almost done add slices of fresh cow cheese
