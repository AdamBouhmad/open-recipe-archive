## Ingredients

- one duck, prepared as described
- butter
- turnips
- a tablespoon of flour
- broth from the pot
- a small sprig of parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place a little butter in a saucepan
2. Turn the duck and some turnips a few times in the butter
3. Remove them and in the butter add a tablespoon of flour
4. Brown this mixture and add broth from the pot
5. Return the duck and turnips to the pot with a small sprig of parsley, salt, and pepper
6. If the turnips are already tender, do not add them until the duck is almost cooked
7. Once the dish is done, let it reduce if necessary
8. Serve everything together
