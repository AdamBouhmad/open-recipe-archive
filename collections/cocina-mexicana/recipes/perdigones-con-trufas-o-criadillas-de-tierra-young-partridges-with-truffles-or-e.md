## Ingredients

- Three young partridges
- Half a pound of earthnuts
- Butter
- Salt and spices
- Four ounces of ham and poultry scraps
- Onions, carrots, mixed herbs
- Broth

## Directions

1. Empty three young partridges through the crop and pass through flame to singe the down
2. Wash earthnuts weighing half a pound, clean and cut into hazelnut-sized pieces
3. Macerate in a saucepan with a little butter, salt and spices for five minutes
4. Pound about four ounces of ham and poultry scraps along with the trimmings from the earthnuts
5. Mix everything together and fill the young partridges with this mixture
6. Arrange so the filling cannot escape
7. Decorate with onions, carrots, mixed herbs, salt and poultry scraps
8. Cover with ham and a wheel of paper and moisten with two quarts of broth
9. When cooked, untie, arrange and serve with earthnut sauce
