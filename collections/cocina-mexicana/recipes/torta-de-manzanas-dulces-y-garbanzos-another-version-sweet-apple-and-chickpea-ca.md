## Ingredients

- sweet apples
- cooked chickpeas
- salt
- a pinch of sugar and cinnamon
- eggs, well beaten and risen
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and grind them raw with some cooked chickpeas
2. Add salt and a pinch of sugar and cinnamon
3. Mix the paste with well-beaten, risen eggs and pour into a cazuela coated with butter
4. Cook over two fires until the cake is done
5. When served dry, dust with ground sugar on top
