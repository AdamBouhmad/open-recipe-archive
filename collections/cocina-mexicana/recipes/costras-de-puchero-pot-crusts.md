## Ingredients

- pan frío (cold bread)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo sin desengrasar (broth without removing fat)
- caldo caliente bien desengrasado (hot broth well defatted)

## Directions

1. Make some thin coscorrones of cold bread, thinner than the common ones
2. Fry them in lard until they are well browned
3. Let them drain and place them in a silver or porcelain bowl that resists fire
4. Dampen them with broth without removing the fat
5. Let them boil on gentle heat until the broth has been consumed and the toasts begin to stick together
6. Add a little hot well-defatted broth
7. Serve
