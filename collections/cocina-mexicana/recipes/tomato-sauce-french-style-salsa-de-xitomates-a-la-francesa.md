## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- four or five tablespoons of ruffled sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the tomatoes in a saucepan with a little broth, salt, and coarse pepper
2. Let them reduce until the broth becomes thick
3. Pass through cheesecloth as a substance
4. If after this the sauce is too thin, return it to the saucepan and let it reduce a little more
5. Mix in four or five tablespoons of ruffled sauce
6. At the moment of serving, add as much butter as an egg, letting it melt in the same sauce
