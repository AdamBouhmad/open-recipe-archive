## Ingredients

- Two quarts of milk
- One and a half pounds of sugar
- Half a pound of ground walnuts

## Directions

1. Sweeten two quarts of milk with one and a half pounds of sugar
2. Strain the mixture
3. Mix in half a pound of ground walnuts
4. Place over heat at a higher point than for cajeta
5. Remove the paste
6. Beat it
7. Pour into molds
