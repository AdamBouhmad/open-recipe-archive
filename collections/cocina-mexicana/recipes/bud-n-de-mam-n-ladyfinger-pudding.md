## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- five egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- a pinch of cinnamon
- butter
- ground biscuit
- slices of ladyfingers

## Directions

1. Dissolve five egg yolks in a cup of milk with sugar to taste and a pinch of cinnamon
2. Spread the pudding mold with butter and dust with ground biscuit
3. Layer slices of ladyfingers and bath them with this milk until the mold is full
4. Cook and do not unmold until it is cold so it does not break
