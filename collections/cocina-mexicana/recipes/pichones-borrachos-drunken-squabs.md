## Ingredients

- xitomates bien colorados (well-colored tomatoes)
- almendras con cáscaras (almonds with skins)
- pan (bread)
- ajo (garlic)
- peregil molido (ground parsley)
- vino tinto (red wine)
- caldo (broth from squabs)
- pasas (raisins)
- pedacitos de acitrón (candied citron pieces)
- almendras (almonds)
- aceitunas (olives)
- azúcar (sugar)
- canela (cinnamon)
- pimienta (pepper)
- clavo (clove)

## Directions

1. Roast well-colored tomatoes and fry them with a handful of almonds with skins and a piece of bread
2. Peel the tomatoes and grind everything together in a metate
3. Fry small garlic cloves separately with a little ground parsley, and add red wine, all the broth in which the squabs were cooked, raisins, candied citron pieces, almonds, olives, a little sugar, cinnamon, little pepper and clove
4. Boil the squabs in this sauce and serve
