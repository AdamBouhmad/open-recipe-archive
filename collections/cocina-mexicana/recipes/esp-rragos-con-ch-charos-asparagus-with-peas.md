## Ingredients

- small asparagus
- butter
- salt
- whole pepper
- sugar
- flour
- water or stock
- two egg yolks
- hard boiled eggs or fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest asparagus, cut into pieces the size of a chickpea
2. Cook as the previous ones; after cooked remove from boiling water to put in cold water
3. Place in a colander so they release all the water, then on a clean napkin
4. After well dried put in the saucepan with butter, salt, whole pepper and a little sugar
5. Put on high heat, stirring without ceasing
6. Dust lightly with flour and add a few drops of water or stock
7. An instant later remove from heat and while hot mix with two egg yolks
8. Serve surrounded by hard boiled eggs in slices or fried bread crusts
