## Ingredients

- milk, four cuartillos
- egg yolks, sixteen
- rice, ground, two handfuls
- sugar
- cream, two cuartillos
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. To four cuartillos of milk add sixteen egg yolks, two handfuls of ground rice, and sugar to taste
2. Bring everything to a boil together
3. When near the proper point, add two cuartillos of cream
4. Let it take consistency
5. Serve with cinnamon on top
