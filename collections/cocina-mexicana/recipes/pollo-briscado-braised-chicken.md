## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Lard in abundance
- Onion, finely minced
- Potatoes, cut in quarters
- Parsley
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into quarters and fry in abundant lard with finely minced onion and potatoes cut in quarters
2. Fry until the chicken is very white
3. Add a large handful of parsley to barely cook it there
4. Add a little water to form sauce and finish cooking the chicken
