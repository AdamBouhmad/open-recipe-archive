## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley and onion minced
- A little flour
- Beef or vigil broth
- A glass of wine

## Directions

1. Cook in water, peel and cut into strips, put in a saucepan with butter, salt, pepper, minced parsley and onion with a little flour
2. Add beef or vigil broth and a glass of wine, serving with little sauce
