## Ingredients

- String beans, cleaned
- Fritter batter

## Directions

1. Clean the string beans and let them cook almost completely.
2. Place them afterwards to dry between two pieces of linen.
3. Divide them in half lengthwise.
4. Cover them with fritter batter.
5. Fry them until well browned and serve as a side dish with fried parsley, which should be very green and well dried before frying.
