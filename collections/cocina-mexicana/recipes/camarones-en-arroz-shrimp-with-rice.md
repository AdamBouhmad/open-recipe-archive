## Ingredients

- Large shrimp
- strong vinegar
- lard
- roasted tomato
- good vinegar
- sugar
- rice
- soaked and well-washed rice
- ripe tomatoes
- onion
- parsley
- poblano chiles
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- butter
- salt
- comal

## Directions

1. Select the largest shrimp and clean them well, then soak them in strong vinegar for ten or twelve hours
2. Drain them well and fry in lard until cooked in a pot
3. Once cooked, fry roasted tomato that has been well ground, a little good vinegar, and a piece of sugar in the same pot
4. Leave to boil over a strong fire, and when all the broth has reduced, fry the shrimp well and remove them
5. Place a cazuela over the fire and fry soaked and well-washed rice in it with lard
6. When the rice begins to brown, fry two or three ripe tomatoes that have been well chopped, a portion of sliced onion, and a little parsley
7. Add water and the shrimp, roast and clean poblano chiles and add them whole
8. Let boil for a while, and when the rice has puffed up well, grind clove, cinnamon and saffron, which are added with more lard if necessary, as it should have plenty, and add salt to taste
9. Place a comal over it and serve immediately
