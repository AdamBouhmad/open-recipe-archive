## Ingredients

- 1 lamb loin
- peeled criadillas de tierra (earth truffles)
- bacon strips
- butter the thickness of a silver coin
- salt, pepper, and cloves, ground
- good meat stock seasoned to taste
- 1/2 cuartillo of the best white wine

## Directions

1. Take a lamb loin and cut small chops without leaving more bone from the rib than necessary to recognize them.
2. Line the pie dish with dough as described in the previous article.
3. Place the chops on the dough.
4. Take some peeled earth truffles and place them between the chops.
5. Dust everything with ground salt mixed with pepper and cloves.
6. Place strips of fat bacon over the chops and truffles.
7. Cover everything with a layer of butter the thickness of a silver coin.
8. Finish the empanada as prescribed.
9. Bake for three hours in the same manner.
10. After baking, lift the dough top carefully, remove the bacon and excess butter, scrape away any raw dough inside the edges.
11. Pour in good meat stock seasoned to taste mixed with 1/2 cuartillo of the best white wine.
12. If earth truffles are not available, make the empanada with chops alone and omit the white wine from the sauce.
