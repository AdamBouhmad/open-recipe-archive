## Ingredients

- Dried ancho chiles
- Garlic
- Bread
- Water
- Cooked cod
- Oil

## Directions

1. Grind soaked dried ancho chiles, garlic and soaked bread.
2. Fry all of it and add water in which the fish was cooked.
3. Once it has boiled, add the slices of already-cooked cod and a little oil, then remove from heat immediately.
4. This same sauce and method can be used for any fish desired.
