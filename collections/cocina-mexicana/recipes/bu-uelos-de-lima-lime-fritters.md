## Ingredients

- Two pounds flour
- Fourteen eggs
- Half pound lard
- Juice of two large limes
- A little tequesite dissolved in water
- Water, as needed

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds flour with fourteen eggs, half pound lard, juice of two large limes, and a little tequesite dissolved in water
2. Add more water if the dough is too thick
3. Beat everything very well
4. Fry by spoonfuls with a small spoon until golden
