## Ingredients

- one tender young hen
- white onion bulbs with heads and tails
- broth with substance
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose a very tender young hen and cook it on the spit or over coals as for the between-two-plates preparation
2. Pour its sauce into an onion stew to give it body
3. For the onion stew: cut the heads and tails from small headed white onions
4. Boil them a quarter hour in boiling water
5. After cooling, remove the first skin
6. Cook them in broth
7. Drain them and put them in well-seasoned meat broth to take on good flavor
8. Let them simmer over a burner for a few moments
