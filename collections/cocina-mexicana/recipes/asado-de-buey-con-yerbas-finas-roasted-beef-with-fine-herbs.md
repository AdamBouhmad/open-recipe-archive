## Ingredients

- Beef pulp
- Water
- Fat ham
- Lard
- Fine herbs
- Meat broth
- Butter
- Flour
- Lemon juice
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. After bleeding the beef pulp, dry it well
2. Lard with fat ham
3. Fry in lard with a portion of coarsely chopped fine herbs
4. Once fried, place on the spit
5. Cover with the remains of the frying and wrap in well-buttered paper
6. Place on fire, and when cooked remove the paper and herbs
7. Place in a pot with meat broth and butter kneaded with flour and lemon juice
8. Let boil until the sauce has thickened much
9. Then coat the meat with beaten egg and breadcrumbs
10. Brown on high fire
11. Serve hot with any of the sauces mentioned in their place
