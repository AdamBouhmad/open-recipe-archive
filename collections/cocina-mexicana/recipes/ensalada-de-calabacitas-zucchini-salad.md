## Ingredients

- Small zucchini
- Salt
- Small onions, cooked
- Cooked peas
- Cooked green beans, peeled
- Avocado slices, peeled
- Oregano powder
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the small zucchini with salt and cut them into quarters.
2. Add one or two cooked small onions separated, cooked peas, and cooked and peeled green beans.
3. Add avocado slices and oregano powder.
4. Pour the mixture of oil, vinegar, and salt.
5. After mixing everything, garnish with avocado quarters, chiles in vinegar, and olives.
