## Ingredients

- Veal tongue
- Water
- Salt
- Chorizo
- Bread
- Eggs
- Butter
- Garlic
- Roasted tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Capers
- Small cooked onions
- Flour
- Broth
- Coriander
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue with water, salt, and chorizo
2. Remove the skin and chop it
3. Mix with the chorizo meat, season as for picadillo, mix with grated bread and beaten eggs
4. Take portions with a spoon and fry in butter
5. For the sauce: fry garlic cloves in butter, and when golden remove them and grind with roasted tomatoes, clove, and pepper
6. Pour the ground mixture into the butter in which the garlic was fried, fry it, season with salt
7. Add vinegar, oil, capers, cooked small onions, flour browned in butter, and broth
8. When well boiled, add the small cakes and let them boil a bit more in the sauce
9. When removed from heat, add coriander and olives
10. Serve dry or stewed in this sauce
