## Ingredients

- chicken
- chicken liver
- bacon
- parsley
- onion
- anchovies
- pepper
- ham or veal
- lard

## Directions

1. Crush and mix the livers with bacon, parsley, onion and anchovies; mix in a little pepper, introduce this mixture between the skin and the meat of the chicken, cover with loin slices, and a sheet of paper spread with lard, and thus place in the roasting pan.
2. Serve with a sauce of ham or veal, and place the anchovies cut in small pieces.
