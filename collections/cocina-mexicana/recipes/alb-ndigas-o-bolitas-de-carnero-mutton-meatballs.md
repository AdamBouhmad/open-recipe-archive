## Ingredients

- Piece of mutton pulp
- Sausage meat, one quarter the weight of the mutton
- Bread crumb soaked in milk
- Two or three egg yolks
- Fine herbs, finely minced
- Cooked and ground potatoes
- Ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of mutton pulp
2. When done, mince it and add one quarter its weight in sausage meat
3. Add a bread crumb soaked in milk, two or three egg yolks, finely minced fine herbs, and some cooked and ground potatoes
4. Mix well and form small balls
5. Roll in ground biscuit and fry
6. Serve with any sauce, principally spicy ones
