## Ingredients

- Sifted sugar
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix sifted sugar with egg yolks until a dough forms that releases from the fingers when handled
2. Form small shapes on papers
3. Place in a casserole at the embers, covered with a comal with fire on top
4. Cook until done
