## Ingredients

- Finely chopped onion
- Mint
- Parsley
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cumin
- Salt
- Pig intestines

## Directions

1. Chop onion finely, along with mint and parsley
2. Add clove, pepper, cinnamon, cumin, and a little salt
3. Fill the pig intestines with this mixture
4. Tie at the ends so they do not empty
5. Place in boiling water to cook until, when pricked, only clear water comes out
