## Ingredients

- oyster shells
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place the shells near heat to open easily.
2. Remove the black vein that appears when opening and detach the oyster from the shell with a knife.
3. Add breadcrumbs, very finely chopped parsley, oil, salt, and pepper.
4. Close the shells and place near the coals until the oyster is cooked.
5. Serve in the same shells.
