## Ingredients

- eel loin
- thin ham
- oil
- minced garlic
- vinegar
- salt
- oregano
- thyme
- laurel
- broth

## Directions

1. Stuff the entire eel loin with thin ham
2. Tie it with a buttered string at the ends so it forms a wheel and secure in this position
3. Place in a pan, add oil, finely minced garlic, vinegar, salt, oregano, thyme and laurel
4. After frying, add broth and place in the oven or over two fires until the sauce is consumed
