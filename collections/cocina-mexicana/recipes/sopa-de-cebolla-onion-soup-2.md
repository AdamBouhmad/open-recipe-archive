## Ingredients

- onion
- fresh butter
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Take an onion, chop it very finely, and fry it in fresh butter until very browned
2. Add a little flour and let it fry until it becomes almost black
3. Then add enough water for the broth, salt, and a little pepper, letting it boil for six minutes
4. Strain it and soak the bread, which should be toasted crust and no crumb
