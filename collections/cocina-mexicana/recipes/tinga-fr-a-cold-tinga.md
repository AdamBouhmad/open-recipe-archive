## Ingredients

- chilpocles en vinagre (smoked chiles in vinegar)
- cebolla (onion)
- aceite (oil)
- aguacate (avocado)

## Directions

1. Take smoked chiles in vinegar and cut into small pieces
2. Remove the veins and add sliced onion, oil, and avocado
