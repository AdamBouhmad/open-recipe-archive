## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Butter
- Chiles
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Flour
- Broth
- White wine
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. After removing membranes and tendons and coating them, roast the partridges.
2. Mince the meat very finely.
3. Reheat the minced meat in a saucepan with butter, chiles, parsley, and garlic.
4. Add one tablespoon of flour.
5. When everything is well mixed, add broth and white wine.
6. Place the minced mixture in this sauce and cook without boiling.
7. Serve with slices of toasted bread.
