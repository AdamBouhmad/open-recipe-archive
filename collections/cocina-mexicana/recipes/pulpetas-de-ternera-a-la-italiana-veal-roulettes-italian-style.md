## Ingredients

- veal meat
- veal pulp
- fat ham
- lean ham
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- bread
- eggs
- grated cheese
- lard
- onion
- carrot
- broth
- egg yolks
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces and pound them until thin.
2. Chop other veal pulps with fat and lean ham and mix this chopped meat with chopped parsley, ground salt, bread soaked and very well squeezed until no water remains, two or three eggs depending on the amount of chopped meat, and grated cheese.
3. Spread a little of this filling on each roulette and roll them up.
4. Place a casserole on the heat lined with lard and layer the bottom with very thin slices of onion and carrot.
5. Place the wrapped roulettes on top and let them cook on low heat, covered, with more heat on top than below.
6. When they begin to stick, add a little broth, as they should cook slowly.
7. Once cooked, add egg yolks with sour orange juice, let cook a little more and stir constantly so the egg does not curdle.
