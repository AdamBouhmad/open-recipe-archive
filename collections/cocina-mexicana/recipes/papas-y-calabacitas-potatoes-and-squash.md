## Ingredients

- wide chiles
- toasted bread
- cumin
- lard
- salt
- vinegar
- small onions
- oregano
- small squash
- peeled and sliced potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak wide chiles and grind them with toasted bread and cumin
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard with salt and add vinegar, the water in which small onions will have been cooked, the same small onions, oregano, small whole squash and peeled and sliced potatoes, all cooked
3. Once the cooking liquid becomes seasoned and boils until it becomes thick, remove the stew and serve
