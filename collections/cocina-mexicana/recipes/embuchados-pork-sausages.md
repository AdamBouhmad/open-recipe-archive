## Ingredients

- thick pork intestines or tripe
- pork meat cut into sticks
- pork jowl, chopped into small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- aromatic herbs, crushed

## Directions

1. Select the thickest intestines or tripe from the pork
2. Wash and clean them well
3. Soak them for twenty-four hours in cold water
4. Drain and dry them
5. Cut the meat into sticks and the jowl into small pieces
6. Mix with salt, pepper, and crushed aromatic herbs
7. Fill other tripe with this mixture
8. Make them to a convenient size
9. Tie both ends and place in the salt cellar
