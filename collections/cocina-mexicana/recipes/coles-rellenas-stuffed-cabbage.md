## Ingredients

- large cabbage
- ground meat
- sausages
- chestnuts
- ham slices
- parsnips
- ripe tomato
- onions
- meat broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take a large cabbage and remove the green and thick leaves, put to boil whole for a quarter hour
2. Remove from the fire and when cooled, remove a little from the interior part, and fill with a mixture of ground meat, sausages and chopped chestnuts, or with any other chopped filling or stuffing like that of chiles
3. Then cover the part well with some of its leaves, tie with string, and place in the center of a shallow dish or pan whose bottom is covered with slices of ham
4. Cover on top with other slices of ham, arranging around it the cabbage leaves that were removed from the center, parsnips, ripe tomato and chopped onions
5. Moisten with meat broth, and when the broth has reduced a little, remove from high heat, add a quarter liter of white wine and put at two low heats until it reduces
6. When serving, dust with sufficient salt and pepper or take to the table with some other thin sauce
