## Ingredients

- four pounds of beef pulp
- four old hens
- one hip of beef
- two beef shanks
- water or broth
- parsnips
- turnips
- onions
- two or three cloves

## Directions

1. Place the beef pulp, hens, hip, and shanks in a pot and cover with water or broth
2. Bring to a boil and skim
3. Refresh this broth three or four times to bring up the scum well
4. Let it simmer gently, adding parsnips, turnips, onions, and two or three cloves
5. When the meats are cooked, pass the broth through a fine cloth to clarify it well
6. Season to taste
