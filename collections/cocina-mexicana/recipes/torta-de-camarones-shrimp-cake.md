## Ingredients

- Shrimp cleaned of head, feet and tail
- Almonds
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Sugar
- Cooking water

## Directions

1. Clean the shrimp of head, feet and tail, soak them, cook and remove all skin
2. Prepare everything as in the previous recipe with the difference of using plenty of breadcrumbs
3. If the shrimp are very large, cut them in half
4. Use the water they were cooked in instead of plain water
