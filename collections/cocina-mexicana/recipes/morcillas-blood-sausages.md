## Ingredients

- fat from the belly
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- blood

## Directions

1. Mince the fat taken from the belly into small pieces and place in a large earthenware bowl, along with minced onion, salt, pepper, clove, and cinnamon
2. Mix everything so that the spices blend well
3. Add the blood little by little, stirring with a wooden spoon
4. After mixing, fry a little in the pan
