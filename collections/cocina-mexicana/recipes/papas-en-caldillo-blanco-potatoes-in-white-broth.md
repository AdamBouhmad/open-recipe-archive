## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- potato starch
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- anchovies, optional
- capers, optional

## Directions

1. Cook and cut potatoes as in the previous article and keep them as hot as possible
2. Dissolve potato starch with a piece of butter, broth, salt, and pepper over gentle fire
3. As soon as this sauce has thickened sufficiently, add chopped anchovies and capers if desired
4. Pour over the cooked potatoes
5. Serve as hot as possible
