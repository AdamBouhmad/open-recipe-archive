## Ingredients

- sugar, two pounds
- butter, two pounds
- clove
- cinnamon
- sesame seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- marquesote, sliced
- egg whites
- sugar, very white, ground and sifted

## Directions

1. Make clarified sugar syrup with two pounds of sugar and when at point, set aside
2. Add two pounds of butter, composed with a little clove, cinnamon, sesame seeds, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
3. In a platter, lay slices of marquesote and pour the compound syrup over them
4. On top place egg whites beaten with very white sugar, ground and sifted
5. When they have thickened in such a way that this layer covers the entire sponge cake well and fills the plate, garnish with sesame seeds, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
6. Cover with a comal with embers so that it browns
