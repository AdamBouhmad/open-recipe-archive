## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- seeds from the chiles, toasted
- garlic cloves
- few cloves
- toasted sesame seeds
- salt
- turkey
- water
- lard
- toasted tortillas

## Directions

1. Devein the ancho and pasilla chiles, toast them, and soak them
2. Toast the seeds from both types of chiles and grind them
3. Grind the chiles, garlic cloves, few cloves, and toasted sesame seeds together
4. Prepare an adobo with this mixture and salt
5. Place turkey cut into quarters or pieces in a pot with the adobo
6. The next day, add the necessary water and lard
7. Place over fire and when cooked, add toasted tortillas that have been well ground to thicken the sauce
8. When ready to serve, sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top
