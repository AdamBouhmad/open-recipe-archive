## Ingredients

- six ounces cold bread
- milk
- three eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon powder
- raisins
- almonds
- sponge cake or bread

## Directions

1. Soak six ounces cold bread in milk and drain
2. Mix with three beaten eggs, sugar, cinnamon powder, raisins, and almonds divided in halves to form a thick paste
3. Add ground sponge cake or bread as needed for consistency
