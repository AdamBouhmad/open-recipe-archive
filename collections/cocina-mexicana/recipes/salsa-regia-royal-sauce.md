## Ingredients

- Thin slices of fat ham
- Salt and pepper
- Slices of carrot
- Slices of onion
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Cloves of clove spice
- Meat ready to cook
- Good dry wine
- Broth

## Directions

1. In a pot, arrange thin slices of fat ham sprinkled with salt and pepper on the bottom
2. Make slices of carrot and onion and arrange them also on top of the ham
3. Add thyme, bay leaf, and some cloves of clove spice
4. Place meat that is ready to be cooked there
5. Add a good amount of dry wine
6. Cover the meats with broth until they are covered in the pot
7. Place over gentle heat for a long time and cover as much as possible to prevent evaporation
8. If the bird or any meat that has been put in becomes fried and even browned, the sauce may be allowed to reduce by stirring so it does not stick
