## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- butter, half the volume of the chestnuts
- powdered sugar
- six egg yolks or more according to the quantity of chestnuts
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Simply peel the chestnuts in water
2. Then put them to cook in water also, adding the flavoring desired
3. Once cooked, drain them on a clean cloth, dry them well and put them in a mortar where they are ground much and then passed through a quenelle sieve
4. Put them back in the mortar with half their volume of butter, adding powdered sugar
5. After grinding everything well together, add six egg yolks or more according to the quantity of chestnuts, with which they are also ground
6. If the mass is very liquid, pour it from the mortar into a pot
7. A little more than half an hour before serving, beat six egg whites, and when they rise as for biscuits, gently mix them with all the ground mixture
8. Then pour it into a silver pot and cook the soufflé as the others
