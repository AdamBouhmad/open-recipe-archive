## Ingredients

- two handfuls of pitted and cleaned raisins
- two biscuits
- a little beef marrow
- some cooked egg yolks
- half citron
- egg yolks mixed with biscuit
- ground biscuit
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Grind in a mortar two handfuls of pitted and cleaned raisins, two biscuits, a little beef marrow, some cooked egg yolks, and half citron
2. Mix the dough well and form small cigars
3. With other egg yolks mixed with biscuit, make small balls
4. Roll everything in ground biscuit
5. Fry in lard or butter
6. Serve with syrup and wine
