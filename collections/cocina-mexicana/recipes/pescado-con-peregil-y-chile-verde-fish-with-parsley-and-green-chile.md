## Ingredients

- plenty of parsley
- green chile
- tomato
- garlic
- spice powder
- salt
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of parsley, green chile, tomato and garlic.
2. Fry very well, add spice powder minus saffron, add necessary salt, plenty of oil and a little vinegar.
3. Bathe the fish and serve.
