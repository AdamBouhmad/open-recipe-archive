## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Fine salt
- Water or milk
- Butter

## Directions

1. Break into a pan the eggs that are necessary, season with fine salt and add a little water or milk, and beat everything together.
2. The more beaten they are, the lighter the omelet will be.
3. Melt butter in a skillet, without heating it; pour the eggs into it, beating them always, and place on the fire, shaking the skillet strongly and frequently to loosen them.
4. When they are set and well colored, add a little butter underneath.
5. Turn the omelet if you believe it necessary or fold it in half, placing it on the plate; in this way it will be less dry and consequently more delicate.
