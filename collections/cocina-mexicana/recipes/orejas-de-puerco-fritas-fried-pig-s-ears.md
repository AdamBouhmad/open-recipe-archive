## Ingredients

- pig's ears
- wine
- water
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and cook with one part wine and two parts water
2. Make an adobo with pepper, garlic, parsley, thyme, bay leaf, salt and a little vinegar if desired
3. Serve very hot
4. To fry, coat with a batter of beaten eggs and flour and dust with sugar
