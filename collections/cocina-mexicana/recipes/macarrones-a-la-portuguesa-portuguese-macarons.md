## Ingredients

- one pound of peeled sweet almonds
- twelve egg whites
- twenty ounces of powdered sugar
- five ounces of potato flour

## Directions

1. Pound one pound of peeled sweet almonds in a mortar
2. Beat twelve egg whites separately until they rise and harden like snow
3. Combine with the almonds, adding twenty ounces of powdered sugar and five ounces of potato flour
4. Beat everything very well
5. Pour the mixture into molds like Savoy sponge cake or paper boxes like common sponge cakes
6. Bake in the oven the same way
