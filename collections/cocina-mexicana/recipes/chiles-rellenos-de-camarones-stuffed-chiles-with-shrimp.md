## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Shrimp, prepared according to specia, cuajados or albondigas recipes
- Shrimp, ground with hardtack as for shrimp cake
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard
- Onions, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Hot water
- Saffron
- Cilantro, toasted
- Pepper
- Clove
- Bread, fried in lard
- Small cured chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as previously described
2. Fill with preparations of shrimp in specia, cuajados, or shrimp meatballs recipes, or with ground shrimp mixed with hardtack
3. Ensure shrimp are broken into pieces and dried
4. Coat with flour and dip in beaten egg
5. Fry the chiles
6. Fry chopped onions, garlic, tomatoes and parsley in lard
7. Add oil, vinegar and salt to season the frying well
8. Add hot water with saffron, toasted cilantro, pepper, clove and fried bread in lard, all ground
9. Let the sauce boil briefly
10. Place the stuffed chiles in it and keep over heat until they reach regular consistency
11. Shortly before serving, add small cured chiles and olives without letting the sauce boil to avoid off-flavors
