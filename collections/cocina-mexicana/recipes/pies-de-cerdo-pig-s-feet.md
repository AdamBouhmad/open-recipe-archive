## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- oil
- lard
- sugar
- cinnamon
- hazelnuts

## Directions

1. After cleaning, singe the feet and soak overnight
2. The following day, cook well
3. Remove the bones without breaking them into many pieces
4. Make a paste of flour and eggs, or if not, breadcrumbs
5. Coat and fry in oil or lard, covering the pan or pot so they don't splatter
6. Serve with sugar and cinnamon
7. If you want to serve with sauce, make one of hazelnuts that is thinned with the water where the feet were cooked
