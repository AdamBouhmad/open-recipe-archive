## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- bread crumbs
- milk
- fresh lard
- onion, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Cut hard boiled eggs in two lengthwise
2. Mash the yolks with an equal amount of bread crumbs soaked in milk and wrung out
3. Add the same amount of fresh lard, chopped onion and parsley, salt, pepper and fine spices
4. Mash this mixture and soften with raw egg yolks
5. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
6. Put the rest on a heat-resistant plate forming a layer about a finger thick
7. Arrange the eggs on top
8. Place the plate on the stove and cover with a comal with coals so they take on color
