## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sugar
- One pound of almonds, ground
- One chicken breast, ground
- Two quartillos of cooked milk
- Orange flower water
- A small piece of panelillo

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at medium point with two pounds of sugar
2. Away from the fire, mix in one pound of ground almonds and one ground chicken breast
3. Return to the fire and add two quartillos of cooked milk
4. Allow it to reach the point of cajeta, stirring continuously
5. When it is about to reach the right consistency, add a little orange flower water and a small piece of panelillo to make it a rose color
6. The correct point is reached when a small amount dropped in cold water forms a ball
