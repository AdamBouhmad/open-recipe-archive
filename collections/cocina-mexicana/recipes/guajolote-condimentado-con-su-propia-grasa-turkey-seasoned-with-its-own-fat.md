## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Veal slices
- Bacon strips
- Broth
- Pepper
- Salt
- Bundle of herbs

## Directions

1. After singeing and gutting the turkey, twist its legs and pass through butter
2. Place veal slices on the bottom of a pot and lay the turkey on top, breast down
3. Cover with bacon strips
4. Add broth, seasoning with pepper, salt and a bundle of herbs
5. Cook over low heat
6. When cooked, strain the gravy through a sieve, remove the fat and serve poured over the turkey
