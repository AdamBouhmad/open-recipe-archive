## Ingredients

- Four ounces of ground sugar
- Two ounces of corn flour
- Two ounces of common flour
- Four ounces of very fresh butter
- A little grated lemon peel or orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix four ounces of ground sugar with two ounces of corn flour, two ounces of common flour, four ounces of very fresh butter and a little grated lemon peel or orange blossom water
2. Grind everything in a mortar until it has the consistency of dough
3. Flatten this with a paddle on the table to leave it very thin
4. Cut into small pieces of desired form and size
5. Place dry in a baking pan
6. Bake at two gentle heats
7. Serve with powdered sugar on top
