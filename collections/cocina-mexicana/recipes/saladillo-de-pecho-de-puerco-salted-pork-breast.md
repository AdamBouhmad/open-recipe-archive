## Ingredients

- pork breast
- fine salt
- saltpeter

## Directions

1. Divide the pork breast into medium-sized pieces and rub them with fine salt
2. Add a little saltpeter and pile the pieces one on top of another in a jar or pot, taking care to press them well to prevent air between them from giving them a rancid taste
3. To prevent this inconvenience, fill the gaps with salt, cover the vessel with a double clean cloth, and seal it as hermetically as possible
4. After eight or ten days the salted pork can be eaten, well dried, with the substance of chickpeas, peas, or lentils, or with turnips, or well with some stew of cabbages or other legumes
