## Ingredients

- turbot fish, cut into small cubes
- light broth
- béchamel sauce
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the flesh of the fish into small cubes
2. Cook in light broth
3. Let cool and place in a saucepan with béchamel sauce
4. Put over heat to warm and season well
5. Cool again
6. Proceed as for poultry croquettes: form into croquettes, coat with beaten egg and bread crumbs
7. Fry until golden
