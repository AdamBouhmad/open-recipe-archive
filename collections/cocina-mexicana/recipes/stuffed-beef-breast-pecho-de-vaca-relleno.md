## Ingredients

- beef breast
- onions
- carrots
- mixed herbs bouquet
- water or broth
- meat trimmings and bones
- Spanish sauce
- veal pulp
- lard
- salt
- spices
- mushrooms
- parsley
- truffles

## Directions

1. Remove the protruding bone and bone ends
2. Make an incision to separate the meat from the bones and insert the filling inside
3. Sew the opening closed so the filling cannot escape, tie the meat and place in a pot with two onions, two carrots, and a mixed herb bouquet
4. Moisten with water or broth and add meat trimmings, bones and other meat scraps
5. Remove the vegetables as they finish cooking
6. When half-cooked, remove from the pot, strain the cooking liquid and remove fat
7. Reduce until it reaches a gelatin consistency and return the breast to cook
8. Ensure it is positioned on the side that will be served
9. Untie, extend the gelatin with a brush over any parts that lack it, and serve with thick Spanish sauce
10. For the filling: mince together one pound or half a pound of veal pulp and the same amount of lard as finely as possible, season with salt and a few spices
11. Mushrooms and chopped parsley may be added, and if available, truffles
12. Lightly fry the filling in butter until all is well incorporated and then place in the breast which is cooked as described
