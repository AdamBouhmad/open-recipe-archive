## Ingredients

- one young turkey, cleaned and prepared
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- chiles
- garlic
- bacon slices
- salt in grains
- pepper
- one glass of white wine
- one glass of broth from the pot
- or half a glass of good brandy if white wine is not available
- additional strained broth to thicken the sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning and preparing the turkey, flatten the breast slightly and turn the legs inward
2. In a saucepan, place butter, parsley, onion, chiles, and garlic, all finely chopped
3. Turn the turkey a few times in this mixture on the grill
4. Transfer the turkey to another saucepan of appropriate size
5. Cover the breast with bacon slices and season well with salt in grains and pepper
6. Moisten with one glass of white wine and one glass of broth from the pot; if white wine is not available, replace with half a glass of good brandy
7. Cook over low heat
8. Add a little strained broth to thicken the sauce
