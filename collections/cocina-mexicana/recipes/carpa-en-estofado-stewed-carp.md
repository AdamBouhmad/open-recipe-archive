## Ingredients

- water and white wine in equal parts
- mace
- coarse pepper
- salt
- onions
- fine herbs
- grated horseradish
- carp

## Directions

1. Pour water and white wine in equal parts into a saucepan along with mace, coarse pepper, salt, some onions, fine herbs and grated horseradish
2. Place the carp pieces in the saucepan
3. Cover the saucepan and let the broth simmer for one hour over very gentle heat
4. Remove the carp and let it drain
5. In another saucepan, pour half a quartillo of white wine, two chopped anchovies, one onion, a little lemon juice, four ounces of butter mixed with flour, a little good cream and one large cup of the broth in which the carp was stewed
6. Let everything boil for a few minutes
7. Add to the sauce two egg yolks mixed with a little cream and squeeze this into the sauce
8. Serve the carp in this sauce
