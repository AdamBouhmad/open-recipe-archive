## Ingredients

- Twelve cuartillos of water
- Five parsnips
- Three turnips
- Four large onions
- Three cloves
- One lettuce with a little parsley inside, blanched in boiling water beforehand
- One hen
- Two pounds of beef shank
- Three pounds of beef pulp
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook slowly in a pot with twelve cuartillos of water five parsnips, three turnips, four large onions, three cloves, one lettuce with a little parsley inside (blanched in boiling water beforehand), one hen, two pounds of beef shank, three pounds of beef pulp, and the corresponding salt.
2. When the viands are completely cooked, pass the broth through a silk sieve, and take it or make with it whatever soup is desired.
