## Ingredients

- beef kidneys
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of the kidneys, dust with flour and fry in the pan with butter, salt, pepper, chopped parsley and onion.
2. Gradually moisten little by little with white wine and let them give a simmer, by which they will be cooked.
