## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Jicama
- Syrup made with two pounds of sugar
- Two ounces of ground almond
- A little orange blossom water
- Mamón

## Directions

1. Empty the lemons and cook as for preserve, deflesh as necessary, and grate
2. Also grate a jicama
3. Put in syrup made with two pounds of sugar
4. Add two ounces of ground almond
5. When the paste has taken point, add a little orange blossom water
6. Put over layers of mamón, and after decorating and studding as is customary, sprinkle with syrup and orange blossom water
