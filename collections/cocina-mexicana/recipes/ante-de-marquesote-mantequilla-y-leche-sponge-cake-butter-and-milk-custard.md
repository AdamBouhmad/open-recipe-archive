## Ingredients

- butter
- two sponge cakes
- twelve egg yolks
- four cuartillos of milk
- one handful of ground almonds
- sugar
- syrup at medium point

## Directions

1. Butter a dish well and add two crumbled sponge cakes, twelve egg yolks, four cuartillos of milk, one handful of ground almonds, and sugar to taste
2. Beat very well with a beater, although some crumb of the sponge cake may remain, and place between two fires
3. As it consumes, pick at it and coat with melted butter until it detaches from the dish
4. Remove to cool and add a little syrup at medium point with its flavorings
5. Serve cold or warm with sugar and cinnamon on top
