## Ingredients

- Peas
- Pork jowl, cut into dice
- Longaniza sausage
- Water
- Salt
- Small round onions (cebolli­tas cabezonas)
- Dried ancho chiles
- Pasilla chiles
- Garlic
- Roasted tomatoes (xitomates asados)
- Cumin
- Clove

## Directions

1. Cook the peas together with diced pork jowl and pieces of longaniza in water with salt and small onions
2. Toast equal quantities of ancho and pasilla chiles
3. Remove the veins and grind them with garlic, roasted tomatoes, cumin, and clove
4. Add the peas, pork jowl, longaniza, and small onions with the broth in which they were cooked
5. Let everything boil until the broth becomes somewhat thick
