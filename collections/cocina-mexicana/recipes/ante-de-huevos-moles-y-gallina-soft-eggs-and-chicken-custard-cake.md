## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- chicken breast
- one pound of almond
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup
- soft eggs

## Directions

1. Make a medium-point syrup with two pounds of sugar, and when cool, add a chicken breast cooked without salt the previous day and ground with one pound of almond, mixing very well with a spoon
2. Put over heat until it reaches proper consistency and pour over sponge cake sprinkled with syrup, adding soft eggs on top made from regular loin
