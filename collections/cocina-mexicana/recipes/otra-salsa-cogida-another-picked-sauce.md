## Ingredients

- Vinegar
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Onion
- Pepper powder
- Bread
- Broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place in a pot vinegar, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, onion and pepper powder
2. Fry a little bread, which is dissolved in broth, then add salt
3. Let it boil for a quarter of an hour, then pass it through a sieve
