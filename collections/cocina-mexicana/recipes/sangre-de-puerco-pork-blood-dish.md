## Ingredients

- fresh pork blood
- salt
- fine spices
- sugar
- fried ripe plantains

## Directions

1. When the pig is slaughtered, place a wide-mouthed pot or very clean wooden bucket under the wound.
2. Collect the warm blood.
3. Continuously turn it without ceasing, always in the same direction, to prevent it from coagulating.
4. Once collected, put the blood to cook.
5. Add necessary salt and fine spices.
6. When cooked and seasoned, a little sugar may be added.
7. Serve with fried ripe plantains.
8. To prevent blood from coagulating when it falls from the slaughter wound, place some corn husks in the pot or bucket, which is sufficient to avoid mishap.
