## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- garlic cloves, peeled
- ham pieces
- pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and salt
- white wine, half a bottle
- lard, half a pound
- water

## Directions

1. Make small incisions with a knife on all sides of the leg
2. Insert peeled garlic cloves and ham pieces into the holes
3. Once studded, rub the entire leg with a mixture of ground pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and salt
4. Place in a casserole
5. Add half a bottle of white wine, half a pound of lard, and a small quantity of water
6. Cover the casserole and cook over two fires until well browned
