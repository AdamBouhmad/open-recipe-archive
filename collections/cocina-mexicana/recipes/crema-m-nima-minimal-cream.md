## Ingredients

- eggs (broken into pieces)
- milk (sweetened and boiling)
- egg yolks
- orange blossom
- a little flour
- a little olive oil

## Directions

1. Break eggs into boiling sweetened milk
2. Drain the broken eggs and add egg yolks, orange blossom, a little flour, and a little olive oil to the milk
3. Heat this mixture
4. When the broken eggs are arranged on a plate, pour the mixture over them and serve
