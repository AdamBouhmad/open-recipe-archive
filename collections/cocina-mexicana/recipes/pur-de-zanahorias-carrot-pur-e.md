## Ingredients

- 10 to 15 carrots, sliced
- sufficient butter
- good broth

## Directions

1. Place in a pot with sufficient butter ten or fifteen carrots cut into slices.
2. Moisten with good broth when well sautéed.
3. Cook until done.
4. Strain, reduce the purée, and cook again for four hours.
5. Serve over any kind of soup.
