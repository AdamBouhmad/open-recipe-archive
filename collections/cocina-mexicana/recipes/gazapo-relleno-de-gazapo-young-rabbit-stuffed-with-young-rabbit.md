## Ingredients

- 2 young rabbits, deboned
- ham
- papas (potatoes)
- salt
- carrots
- small whole onions
- parsley
- thyme
- peppercorns
- wine
- broth or water
- egg white

## Directions

1. Lard one deboned rabbit with ham
2. Coarsely chop the other rabbit with an equal piece of ham
3. Season the chopped mixture with salt and diced potatoes
4. Stuff the whole rabbit with this mixture and close it
5. Wrap in clean, thin linen
6. Place in a pan lined with ham
7. Arrange around it pieces of carrot, small whole onions, parsley, thyme, peppercorns, salt, and equal parts wine and broth or water
8. Add the broken bones from the rabbits
9. Cover with ham slices on top
10. Cook over two gentle fires
11. When cooked, let cool and strain the broth, degreasing it well
12. Clarify with egg white
13. Let it thicken and pour into dishes to set completely
14. Cover the rabbit with this aspic, topped with ham slices
