## Ingredients

- lamb hands
- water
- butter
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Cook in water alone and remove the large bones
2. Place them immediately in a saucepan with butter, finely chopped herbs, salt and pepper
3. Thicken the broth, removing the hands so they cool
4. Put them in the sauce and immediately coat them in bread crumbs
5. Place to roast over low heat and serve with a spicy sauce
