## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. After peeling, use special cutting spoons or knives to remove the fleshy part of the potatoes, forming stars or fillets.
2. Place them in very hot butter so they are completely covered or swimming in it.
3. Fry until done, being careful to serve them immediately after removing them, drained and sprinkled with very fine salt.
