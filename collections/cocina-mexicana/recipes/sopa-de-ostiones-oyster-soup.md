## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- white broth without saffron or chile
- egg yolks
- oysters
- French bread
- lemon

## Directions

1. Chop a few garlic cloves very finely and a little parsley, fry them in a little butter
2. When it turns golden, add a little flour and stir so it does not brown too much
3. Remove white broth without saffron or chile from the pot and let it come to a boil
4. Beat some egg yolks and pour them into the broth, taking care that they do not cook
5. Add a jar of oysters with all their broth
6. Cut pieces of French bread and brown them
7. Place them in the soup tureen and pour the prepared broth over them, squeezing a little lemon to taste
