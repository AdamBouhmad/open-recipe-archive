## Ingredients

- cardoons
- salt
- lard
- onion
- butter
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and all the fibers from the cardoon stalks
2. Cut into pieces four fingers long and put to cook in salted water
3. Once cooked, set aside and let cool
4. Put a little lard in a pot over the fire and fry finely chopped onion without letting it brown
5. Add the well-drained cardoon, sufficient butter and a pinch of pepper
6. Add a little water, let it season, and when ready serve hot
