## Ingredients

- Two tablespoons of flour
- Three tablespoons of powdered sugar
- Good butter
- Half a lemon zest or its equivalent of orange blossom
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put in a vessel two tablespoons of flour, three tablespoons of powdered sugar, a piece of good butter the size of half an egg which will be melted, and half a lemon zest chopped or its equivalent of orange blossom
2. Mix everything together, adding four egg yolks which are added gradually
