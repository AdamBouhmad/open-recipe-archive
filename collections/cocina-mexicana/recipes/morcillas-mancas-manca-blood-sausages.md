## Ingredients

- poultry breasts
- lard in equal quantity to the breasts
- onions cut in small pieces
- broth
- salt
- spices
- breadcrumbs cooked in cream
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix poultry breasts and lard in equal quantity, chopping well.
2. Add onions cut in small pieces and cooked in broth, salt, spices, and breadcrumbs cooked in cream.
3. Chop everything together again so it is well mixed.
4. Add egg yolks and cream.
5. Fill the intestines and cook in water with half milk.
6. When done, chop and place to toast in a paper box lined with lard.
