## Ingredients

- 4 cuartillos good milk
- 2 cuartillos custard (natillas)
- 1.5 ounces sugar
- a little rennet dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Boil together and reduce to two-thirds: 4 cuartillos of good milk, 2 of custard, and 1.5 ounces of sugar
2. Let cool and add a little rennet dissolved in a spoon with a bit of water
3. Mix everything exactly and pass the cream through a sieve
4. Put immediately over hot coals and cover with a lid or griddle with fire on top
5. When it reaches the proper point, cool it and leave in a cool place
