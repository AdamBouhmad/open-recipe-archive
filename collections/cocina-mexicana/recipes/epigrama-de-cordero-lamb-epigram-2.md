## Ingredients

- quarter forequarter of lamb
- bread crumbs
- parsley sauce

## Directions

1. Take a whole quarter forequarter and remove the shoulder to roast on a spit
2. Separate the six ribs
3. Cook the breast over hot coals and cut into six pieces in the shape of bread slices
4. Roll in bread crumbs and place on the grill
5. Cook the ribs in a frying pan and prepare them placing a piece of breast on a fried toast
6. Pour parsley sauce on top, mixed with the shoulder cut into thin slices, adding mushrooms if desired
