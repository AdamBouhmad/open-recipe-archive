## Ingredients

- Three or four ounces lard
- Cold water
- Anise, enough to fill the palm of the hand
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Heat the lard in a pan
2. When very hot, add cold water and anise and salt
3. Begin stirring and gradually add flour until the mixture thickens
4. Continue stirring until the mixture begins to toast on the bottom of the pan
5. Remove from heat and mix in eggs until the paste is softer than stiff and spoonfuls can be taken from it
6. Fry spoonfuls over low heat until they brown and burst well
7. Dust with sugar and cinnamon and serve
