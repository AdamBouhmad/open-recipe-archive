## Ingredients

- Sweet apples
- Cinnamon sticks
- Cloves
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Place sweet apples to cook without peeling, taking care that they do not fall apart
2. Once cooked, remove the core with a knife or with a tin sheet corer made for this purpose
3. Stud the exterior with small cinnamon sticks and cloves
4. Add them to the prepared syrup to give them a boil, taking care that they do not fall apart, neither when cooking nor when handling, nor when boiling with the syrup, nor when arranging in the serving dish or preserve container
