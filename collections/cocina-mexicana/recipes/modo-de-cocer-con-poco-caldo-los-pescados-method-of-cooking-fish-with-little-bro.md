## Ingredients

- fish
- vinegar
- salt
- bay leaves
- onion
- water

## Directions

1. After scaling and cleaning, bathe with boiling vinegar and immediately cover the platter where it is placed with a lid and damp cloth so the steam does not escape.
2. After a quarter of an hour, remove and throw them into a pot of boiling water with a little salt, bay leaves, and onion.
3. After the fish is cooked, remove from heat and add a little cold water, leaving it thus until it is time to serve, which is done by placing it on a folded napkin so it retains its color. This method is called au court-bouillon or short broth.
