## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Four egg yolks
- A little salted water
- A little tequesquite powder
- Necessary butter

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with four egg yolks, a little salted water, a little tequesquite powder, and necessary butter to make the dough crispy
2. After kneading well, cover and let rest
3. Extend with a rolling pin
4. Cut portions for each empanadilla
5. Spread with butter
6. Knead separately (each portion is kneaded apart and extended a second time)
7. Proceed as for the previous ones
