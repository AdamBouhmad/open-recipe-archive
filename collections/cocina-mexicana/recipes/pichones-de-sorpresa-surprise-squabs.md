## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Lettuces, firm or budding
- Veal picadillo
- Cold strained broth
- Sartén essence
- Orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the squabs appropriately and cook à la brasa
2. Prepare as many firm or budding lettuces as there are squabs
3. Lard the lettuces, drain them, and remove their hearts
4. Place a squab covered with veal picadillo in place of the heart
5. Add cold strained broth
6. Tie the lettuces with string
7. Cook in the same brasa in which the squabs were cooked
8. Once the lettuces are cooked and drained, soak them in sartén essence
9. Serve with orange or lemon juice
