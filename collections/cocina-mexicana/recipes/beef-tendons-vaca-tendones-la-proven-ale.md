## Ingredients

- Beef tendons
- Oil
- Onions
- Garlic cloves
- Vinegar
- Spanish sauce
- Dry chile powder

## Directions

1. Prepare the tendons the same as à la Milanese.
2. Cook them in oil with onions cut in strips and garlic cloves.
3. Drain and simmer gently in a glass of vinegar with a little Spanish sauce and a bit of dried chile powder.
4. Arrange the tendons in a crown shape.
5. Cover with the onion prepared this way.
