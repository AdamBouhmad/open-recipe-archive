## Ingredients

- camote (sweet potato)
- almendra (almond)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. To one pound of ground sweet potato add one pound of almonds, all ground, and one pound twelve ounces of sugar made into syrup
2. Give it the same point as marmalade
