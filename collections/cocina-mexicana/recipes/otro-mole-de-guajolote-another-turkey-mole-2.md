## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- 2 knuckles of ham at one real each
- 1/2 pound mulato chile
- 1/2 pound pasilla chile
- 8 to 10 chilpocles
- About half tomatoes by measure
- 1 cup sesame
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds with their shells
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- 1 roasted garlic head
- A little toasted anise
- A little cilantro, toasted
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Before toasting the chile, remove the veins and seeds
2. Toast without letting it burn, and toast the chilpocle until slightly overdone
3. Measure about half tomatoes and cook them
4. Grind the chile and then the tomatoes, one cup of sesame and half cup of pumpkin seeds with shells
5. These two things together are mashed with a knife and without cleaning the grinding stone, grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, one roasted garlic head, a little toasted anise and a little cilantro the same way
6. Cut the turkey into pieces, wash it, the same with the head and giblets, as well as the knuckles
7. Put in a large cazuela with water enough to cover the meat
8. Put on the fire and add the chile and tomatoes
9. Once it boils add the spices and seeds
10. If the meat is not cooked add more water and continue boiling until it is tender
