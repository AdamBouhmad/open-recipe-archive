## Ingredients

- three-quarters of an ounce of Ceylon cinnamon
- forty grains of clove
- twenty grains of ginger
- one and a half ounces of fragrant rose water
- eight ounces of good white wine
- one pound of Mancha sugar

## Directions

1. Place the aromatic substances in infusion in a vessel with the rose water and white wine for thirty to thirty-six hours
2. Filter the liquid and dissolve the sugar in it using the heat of a water bath
