## Ingredients

- lamb leg, cut into portions
- small tomatoes, one caldron measure
- tomatoes, another equal measure
- assorted spices, except cumin
- cumin, regular quantity
- ham slices
- chorizo
- olives
- peeled almonds
- salt
- lard, one pound
- garlic cloves
- oil
- Málaga wine, one glass
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Cut a lamb leg into portions and place in a pot
2. Toast a caldron of small tomatoes and cook another equal measure in water; add both to the pot
3. Grind assorted spices except cumin and add a regular quantity
4. Add ham slices, chorizo, olives, and peeled almonds
5. Add sufficient salt and one pound of lard
6. Cover the pot with an adjusted bowl and seal the rim with paste and paper
7. Place over gentle fire until the meat is cooked and the broth is consumed
8. Remove and place a bowl with lard to burn, fry clean garlic cloves in it, and once the lard is burned, add a little oil
9. Fry the meat until dry
10. Once dry, add a glass of Málaga wine, sprinkle toasted sesame on top
