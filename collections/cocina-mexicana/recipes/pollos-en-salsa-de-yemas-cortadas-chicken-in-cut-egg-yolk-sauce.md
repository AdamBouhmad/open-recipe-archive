## Ingredients

- lard
- chopped onion
- chopped parsley
- salt
- cooked chorizos
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon powder
- raisins
- almonds
- chicken broth
- wine
- fried flour
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion and parsley in lard and season with salt
2. In the same fat, fry cooked chorizos and add pepper, cloves, and cinnamon powder
3. Add raisins, almonds, some chicken broth, wine, fried flour, and egg yolks cut with lemon juice
4. Place the chicken in this sauce
5. Let simmer until it takes the consistency of sauce and is well seasoned
