## Ingredients

- about one quarter liter of flour
- a piece of butter about half the size of an egg
- four eggs with whites and yolks
- a little salt
- four ounces of fine sugar
- six ounces of well-ground almonds

## Directions

1. Place about one quarter liter of flour on a table and make a hole in the center
2. Place a piece of butter about half the size of an egg, four eggs with whites and yolks, a little salt, four ounces of fine sugar, and six ounces of well-ground almonds in the hole
3. Knead everything together
4. Form the gâteau in the usual manner
5. Bake in the oven
6. Brush with sugar and pass a hot shovel over the top
