## Ingredients

- thyme
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- onion
- tomato
- bread
- offal
- spices
- garlic clove
- egg
- oil
- parsley
- capers
- cooked egg
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cook the offal with thyme, marjoram and bay leaf
2. Fry onion and tomato, when the seasoning is well fried add a little bread to brown it but not too much
3. Add the chopped offal, separating some pieces to grind with spices and a garlic clove
4. Mix the ground mixture with an egg and add it when heating for serving with a little oil
5. Decorate the platter with chopped parsley, capers, cooked egg and fried bread
6. Rinse the offal in warm water when finished cooking, and fry the bread separately
