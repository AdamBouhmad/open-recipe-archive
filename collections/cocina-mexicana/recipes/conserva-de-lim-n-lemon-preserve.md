## Ingredients

- Green lemons that are firm and porous
- Water

## Directions

1. Choose the greenest lemons that are firm and porous
2. Put in water
3. Rub on a pumice stone, trying to remove only the outer skin but keeping the green color
4. Open in a cross so the juice comes out
5. Put in the oven for a short time
6. At the second boiling, the insides can be removed so they lose their bitterness more quickly
7. Do this carefully to avoid damaging them
8. Note that there should be many boilings but short ones so the bitterness is completely removed
9. Each time do this, put in cold water to shock them
10. In syrup give only one or two boilings, as this is sufficient
