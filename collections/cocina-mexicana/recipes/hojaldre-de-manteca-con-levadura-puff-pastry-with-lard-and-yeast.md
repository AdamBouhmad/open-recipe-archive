## Ingredients

- six pounds of flour
- one pound and a quarter of lard
- one pound of ground sugar
- one and a half pounds of yeast
- eighteen egg yolks
- ten egg whites
- necessary water
- cold lard for filling the holes
- sugar, cinnamon, and dragée, all ground
- pieces of citron and almonds, if desired

## Directions

1. Knead six pounds of flour with one pound and a quarter of lard, one pound of ground sugar, one and a half pounds of yeast, eighteen egg yolks, ten egg whites, and necessary water
2. The dough should be neither very soft nor very hard
3. Fill the holes in the dough with cold lard
4. Place in the sun on buttered papers
5. When the pastries have risen, sprinkle over them at the mouth of the oven ground sugar, cinnamon, and dragée, and pieces of citron and almonds, if desired
