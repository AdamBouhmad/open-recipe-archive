## Ingredients

- a loaf of cold bread
- garlic cloves
- butter
- four hard biscuits, quite stale
- clove
- sugar
- white wine
- orange blossom water
- sesame seeds, toasted
- beaten eggs
- pine nuts
- walnuts, cleaned

## Directions

1. Soak a loaf of cold bread the night before
2. Fry some garlic cloves in butter and remove them
3. Add the squeezed bread crumb to fry well until it separates from the pan
4. Mix this with four hard, quite stale biscuits, a little clove and the necessary sugar, all ground
5. Add a little white wine and some orange blossom water
6. Mix everything well and put in a casserole with toasted sesame seeds on top, cover with a napkin
7. Leave until the next day, then stir in beaten eggs, pine nuts and cleaned walnuts
8. Incorporating everything, put it over two fires and serve with sugar and cinnamon powder on top
