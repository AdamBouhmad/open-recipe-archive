## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- beef broth
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice or verjuice

## Directions

1. Cook the green beans in salted water
2. Pour boiling into a pot
3. Moisten with a spoonful of beef broth
4. Add butter and chopped parsley and peppercorns
5. Bind by stirring much and quickly
6. Add lemon juice or verjuice and serve
