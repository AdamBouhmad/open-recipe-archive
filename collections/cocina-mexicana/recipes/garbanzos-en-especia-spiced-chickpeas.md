## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- garlic
- onions
- lard
- cooked chickpeas
- salt
- vinegar
- green chiles
- plantain
- apple
- pineapple
- pork (optional)

## Directions

1. Grind some ripe tomatoes
2. Mince some clean garlic
3. Slice some onions
4. Fry everything in lard
5. Add cooked chickpeas as described in previous articles
6. Add the necessary water seasoned with salt
7. Add a little vinegar and a piece of green chiles whole
8. Add pieces of plantain, apple, and pineapple
9. If desired, add pork, and if not, serve them alone, letting them season well
