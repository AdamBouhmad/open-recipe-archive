## Ingredients

- seven ounces of good coffee
- ten ounces of custard
- thirty ounces of milk
- fifteen ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Roast seven ounces of good coffee until it acquires a beautiful cinnamon color
2. Grind it and keep in a pan
3. Bring ten ounces of custard to boil with thirty ounces of milk and fifteen ounces of sugar
4. When everything begins to thicken, pour over the coffee
5. Stir and agitate with a spoon
6. Cover, allow to cool and strain through a sieve or thin cloth
7. Freeze in the usual manner
