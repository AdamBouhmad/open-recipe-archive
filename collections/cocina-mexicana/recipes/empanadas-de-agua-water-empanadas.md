## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt dissolved in water
- half an ounce of beef lard
- 10 eggs
- cream
- cold water

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of flour on a table, making a hole in the middle
2. Pour into it half an ounce of salt dissolved in water, half an ounce of beef lard, and ten eggs
3. Knead, and if the paste is not soft enough, add eggs and knead five or six times
4. Place the dough on a table sprinkled with flour and leave for about twelve hours
5. After this time, form the empanadas and boil them in water mixed with cream, stirring the pot to make them rise to the surface and returning them with a skimmer
6. When they have consistency, remove them and place in cold water for two hours
7. Drain and bake in a campaign oven
