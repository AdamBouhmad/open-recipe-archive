## Ingredients

- popped corn kernels
- sugar syrup at caramel stage

## Directions

1. Form roses from the popped corn kernels
2. Drop them into the syrup when it is at the caramel stage
3. Leave over heat for a short time
4. Pour onto a clean cloth, and when cool cut into the desired shape, though generally it is made in the shape of alfajor
5. The same method is used to prepare sesame seed alegría
