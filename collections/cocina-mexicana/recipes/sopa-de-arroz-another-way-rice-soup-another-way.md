## Ingredients

- rice
- broth or meat essence (caldo o sustancia de carne)
- salt
- oil (for fasting days)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in warm water
2. Cook slowly over low heat with the broth or meat essence
3. Remove the fat from the top when cooked
4. Ensure it is neither too liquid nor too thick
5. If you wish it golden on top, place a cover with well-lit embers
6. On fasting days, break the rice in very hot water, add salt and oil
7. If desired, you may add essence of chickpeas, peas, lentils, etc.
