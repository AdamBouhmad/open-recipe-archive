## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Water
- Salt
- Half pound lard
- Eighteen egg yolks
- Eight egg whites
- Clarified syrup
- Grajea

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of flour in water with salt and half pound of lard, stirring continuously until it sticks to the fingers and crackles when stirred
2. Remove from heat and add eighteen beaten egg yolks with eight whites
3. Beat until the mixture reaches the right consistency
4. With a small spoon, drop spoonfuls into very hot lard, making sure they do not stick together
5. When golden, remove and place in well-clarified syrup
6. When the syrup reaches the right consistency, serve with grajea on top
