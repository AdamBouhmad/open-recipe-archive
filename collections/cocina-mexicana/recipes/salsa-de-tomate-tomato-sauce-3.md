## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- vinegar
- oil
- oregano
- green chiles

## Directions

1. Cook the tomatoes and grind them
2. Add salt, pepper, minced onion, vinegar, oil and powdered oregano
3. Ground green chiles can be added for those who like spice
