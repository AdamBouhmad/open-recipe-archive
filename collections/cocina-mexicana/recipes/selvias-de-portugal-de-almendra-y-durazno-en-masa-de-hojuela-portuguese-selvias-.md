## Ingredients

- almond paste, a little
- peach cajeta
- cinnamon, ground
- cloves, ground
- pepper, ground
- wafer dough (see WAFERS, page 408)
- lard for frying
- syrup at high point
- powdered sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix a little almond paste with peach cajeta
2. Add a little ground cinnamon, cloves and pepper
3. Extend any of the wafer doughs with a rolling pin, making the sheet not too thin
4. Cut into triangles or in the shape of a small flag
5. Place a little of the paste on top
6. Cover with another sheet of dough and seal at the edges
7. Fry in lard
8. Place in syrup at high point
9. Remove with a skimmer
10. Roll in powdered sugar and cinnamon
11. Eat the selvias cold
