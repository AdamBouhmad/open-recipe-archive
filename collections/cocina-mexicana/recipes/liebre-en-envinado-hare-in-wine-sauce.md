## Ingredients

- hare, cleaned and cooked
- roasted tomatoes
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- oil
- salt
- broth
- parsley, minced
- vinegar
- seedless raisins
- sliced peppers
- olives
- banana slices
- pineapple slices
- Parrots wine or Málaga wine

## Directions

1. After the hare is cleaned, cooked and boneless without cutting into small pieces, roast tomatoes.
2. Grind with cinnamon, pepper, clove and saffron.
3. Fry all of this well.
4. Add a little broth and competent salt.
5. Add the meat, minced parsley, vinegar, seedless raisins, sliced peppers, olives, banana slices and pineapple slices.
6. Cook at two mild fires until consumed.
7. Add Parrots or Málaga wine.
8. Let rest and serve.
