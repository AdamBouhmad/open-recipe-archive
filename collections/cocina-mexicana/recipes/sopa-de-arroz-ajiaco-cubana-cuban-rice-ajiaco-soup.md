## Ingredients

- rice, well washed
- broth
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the well-washed rice to boil with the necessary broth
2. Separately, fry garlic with a little onion and tomato in oil
3. Add a few drops of lemon juice
4. Mix this with the rice after removing it from heat
5. Stir it very well and after letting it rest a while, it can be eaten
