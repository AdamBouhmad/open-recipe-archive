## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- spinach or Swiss chard
- cooked chickpeas
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- bread
- small cooked onions
- oil
- vinegar
- ham (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry minced garlic, onions, tomatoes, and some green chiles in lard
2. Then fry the spinach or Swiss chard, already cooked, minced, and squeezed, in the same place
3. Add the cooked and hulled chickpeas with the broth in which they were cooked
4. Season with salt, clove, pepper, saffron, and cumin, ground spices, soaked and ground bread, small cooked onions, oil, and vinegar
5. Let everything boil until completely seasoned
6. Serve with fried toasted bread
7. If not for a fasting day, sliced raw ham may be added
