## Ingredients

- Butter
- Garlic
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- White cabbage, cooked
- Ground sugar
- Grated or fresh ground cheese
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic, onions, and tomatoes in butter
2. Once fried, add the cooked white cabbage so it wilts
3. Add ground sugar in sufficient quantity so the sweetness stands out, and grated or fresh ground cheese
4. Let everything cool and mix in beaten eggs, incorporating well
5. Pour into a buttered casserole dish, cover with more beaten eggs
6. Place to set the cake over two fires
7. Serve with ground sugar sprinkled on top
