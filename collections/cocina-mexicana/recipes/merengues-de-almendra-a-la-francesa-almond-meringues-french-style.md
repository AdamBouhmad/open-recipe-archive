## Ingredients

- Six egg whites
- Four ounces powdered sugar
- Four ounces sweet almonds, reduced to paste
- Fine sugar

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites with four ounces of powdered sugar and evaporate them over embers, stirring continuously
2. Add four ounces of sweet almonds reduced to paste and mix everything
3. Form the meringue round or oval, the size of a spoon, leaving it empty inside
4. Sprinkle with fine sugar and put them in the oven
5. When they have risen, remove them, split them in half, fill them with whipped cream or some preserves and cover them again
