## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of ground rice
- six egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- orange leaves
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten six cuartillos of milk
2. Mix with four ounces of ground rice and six egg yolks
3. Incorporate everything and strain
4. Place over heat and add cinnamon sticks and orange leaves
5. Allow to boil, stirring to prevent sticking
6. When it reaches the proper point, remove from heat
7. Pour into serving dishes and decorate with cinnamon powder
