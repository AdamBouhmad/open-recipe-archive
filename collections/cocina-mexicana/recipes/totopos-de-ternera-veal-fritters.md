## Ingredients

- Veal meat
- Egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion slices
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Chop the veal meat and grind it, removing the sinews so it is well ground
2. Break an egg and mix it with the meat
3. Add salt and pepper
4. Form the totopos
5. Fry them
6. Serve with onion slices, olives, and capers
