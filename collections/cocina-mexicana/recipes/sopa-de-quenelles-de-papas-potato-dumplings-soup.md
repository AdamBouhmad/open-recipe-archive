## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lettuce
- butter
- egg yolks
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- broth

## Directions

1. Steam the potatoes
2. Peel and pound the potatoes in a mortar
3. Pound roasted lettuces cooked on the spit
4. Mix together the ground ingredients
5. Add a piece of butter, egg yolks, pepper, salt and nutmeg
6. Make a paste firm enough to form into small balls by pounding again
7. Cook these dumplings in sufficient quantity of broth and let them cook for one hour
