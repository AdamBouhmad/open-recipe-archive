## Ingredients

- turkey offal
- butter
- flour
- broth or water
- pepper
- salt
- herbs
- turnips

## Directions

1. Cut the offal into pieces and sauté with butter
2. Dust with flour and moisten with broth or water, seasoning with pepper, salt and a bouquet of herbs
3. Cook, adding two turnips that have been browned in butter until they take on color
4. Drain and garnish with fried potato slices
