## Ingredients

- twenty-six eggs
- one pound of starch
- ten ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Make the same way as the mamones from the previous article; but the quantities of ingredients are as follows: twenty-six eggs, one pound of starch, and ten ounces of sugar.
