## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk
- two pounds sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- two egg yolks
- one cup milk
- eight egg yolks
- one pound sugar
- one pound butter

## Directions

1. Boil four cuartillos of milk with two pounds sugar and cinnamon sticks until well dissolved
2. Grind and let cool
3. Dissolve two egg yolks in a small portion of milk and strain through a serviette
4. Place on heat to reach a higher point than cajeta
5. Beat eight egg yolks with one pound sugar and one pound butter, beating the yolks first
6. Combine everything in the pot and beat until it shows eyes
7. Fill serving cups
