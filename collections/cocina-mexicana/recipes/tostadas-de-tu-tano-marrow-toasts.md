## Ingredients

- almond paste
- cream with very delicate marrow
- egg, beaten
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make almond paste bases in the shape of bread toasts with a small border around the edge
2. Place in a baking dish
3. Cover with a little cream mixed with very delicate marrow
4. Brush with beaten egg
5. Dust with sugar
6. Bake until set
7. Serve hot
