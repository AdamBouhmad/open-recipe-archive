## Ingredients

- squabs
- lard
- ham pieces
- broth
- white wine
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Fry the squabs in lard with ham pieces
2. Add equal parts broth and white wine
3. Add mushrooms
4. Let cook until well seasoned and ready to serve
