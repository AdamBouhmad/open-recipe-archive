## Ingredients

- hen
- good olive oil (about half a pound)
- salt
- parsley bundle
- fine herbs
- Italian sauce
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut the hen as for pepitoria
2. Place it in a saucepan with almost half a pound of good olive oil and salt
3. Brown it well
4. Just before it is completely browned, add a bundle of parsley and fine herbs
5. When everything is ready, place the quarters of hen on a platter
6. In another saucepan, make Italian sauce
7. Pour in the oil from frying the hen little by little, stirring constantly
8. When the sauce is ready and tastes good, pour it over the hen that has been kept warm
9. Finish by decorating with fried bread crusts
