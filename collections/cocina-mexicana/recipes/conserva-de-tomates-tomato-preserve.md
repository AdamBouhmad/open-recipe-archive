## Ingredients

- Large, fleshy tomatoes
- Ash water (agua de ceniza)
- Salt
- Syrup (almíbar) at high point

## Directions

1. Select the largest and fleshiest tomatoes
2. Prick them with a needle and place them to deflame in ash water for two or three days, changing the water twice daily
3. Give them a light boil in the last water when it is boiling
4. Remove, peel and place in fresh water for three days
5. After this time, well drained, place them in syrup at high point, letting them rest to penetrate
6. The next day place them on gentle fire to complete the seasoning
7. If they are to be filled, do not peel them
