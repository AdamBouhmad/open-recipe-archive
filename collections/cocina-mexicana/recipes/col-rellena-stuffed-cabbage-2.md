## Ingredients

- cabbage
- loin slices
- green beans
- carrots
- chorizo
- longaniza
- ham
- salt
- water
- wide chiles
- butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Look for one that is somewhat hollow and remove the heart
2. First place slices of loin of beef at the bottom and on top of this green beans cut into pieces, small carrots, chorizo, longaniza
3. For a regular cabbage use four ounces of sliced ham and salt
4. Tie the cabbage once filled with small ropes and place in a pot with water until it covers the cabbage
5. Fry separately two wide chiles in enough butter, turn them with the butter into the pot where the cabbage is
6. Let everything cook and when it lacks little add three large potatoes
7. To serve untie it, place on a platter carefully, bathe in its broth and place the potatoes around it
