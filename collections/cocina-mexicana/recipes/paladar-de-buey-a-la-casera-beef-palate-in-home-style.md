## Ingredients

- four beef palates
- warm water
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- pork fat
- linen cloth
- sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch four beef palates in warm water and remove the hard, black skin; if it resists and cannot be removed well, soak for a moment in boiling water.
2. Wash afterwards in many hot waters, then in cold water.
3. Divide into pieces three fingers long and place in a pot or casserole with salt, pepper, onions, thyme, bay leaf and pork fat.
4. Let them cook on a gentle fire for six or seven hours, and they should remain very tender.
5. Place to drain on a cloth and arrange on the plate in the form of a crown, pouring the desired sauce in the middle.
