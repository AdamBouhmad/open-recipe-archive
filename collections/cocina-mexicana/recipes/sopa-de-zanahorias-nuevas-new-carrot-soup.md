## Ingredients

- fresh carrots
- water
- meat broth
- bread toast

## Directions

1. Cut and shape fresh carrots into small sticks about one inch long, all of the same size
2. Blanch them in boiling water
3. Drain them and cook in broth
4. Prepare toasted bread
5. Place the cooked carrots on top of the toast
