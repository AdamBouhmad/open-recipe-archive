## Ingredients

- mutton meat
- membranes and tendons removed
- cooked potatoes or roasted chestnuts
- fine herbs
- lard
- flour
- broth
- bread
- eggs

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes and tendons
2. Chop the meat very well with cooked potatoes or roasted chestnuts
3. Pass fine herbs through lard with one tablespoon of flour, which is left to toast
4. Add broth and add the minced meat
5. When it is ready, serve with slices of fried bread around it
6. The sauce should be thick, and fried bread can have fried eggs on top
