## Ingredients

- Fried bread
- Garlic
- Cumin
- A grain of clove
- A few strands of saffron
- Broth
- Bread crumb

## Directions

1. Grind fried bread, garlic and cumin, a grain of clove and a few strands of saffron
2. Dissolve in broth
3. Thicken with bread crumb
4. Give a few boils
