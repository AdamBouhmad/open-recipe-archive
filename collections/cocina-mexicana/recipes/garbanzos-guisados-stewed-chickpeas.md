## Ingredients

- large chickpeas
- clove
- cinnamon
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- pork meat stock
- pork ribs
- ham slices
- chorizos
- sugar
- vinegar
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook large chickpeas and when done, peel them
2. Grind a few chickpeas very fine with clove and cinnamon
3. Mince tomato, onion, and garlic, fry well and incorporate chickpeas to fry with the tomato
4. Add pork stock, pork ribs, ham slices, chorizos, the remaining whole chickpeas, a piece of sugar, and vinegar
5. Garnish with chiles in vinegar
