## Ingredients

- grapefruit or bitter orange
- ash lye or tequesquite
- sugar

## Directions

1. Either peel or grate the yellow skin without wasting the flesh
2. Cook until soft in strong lye of ash or tequesquite
3. Rinse well and soak in fresh water
4. Change the water daily or twice a day until no bitter taste remains
