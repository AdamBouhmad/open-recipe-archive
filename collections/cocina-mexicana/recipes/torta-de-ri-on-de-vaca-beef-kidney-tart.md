## Ingredients

- One beef kidney, roasted on a spit
- The fat that covers it
- Frangipane

## Directions

1. Chop part or all of a beef kidney roasted on a spit, with part of the fat that covers it, according to the size of the tart to be made
2. Incorporate this chopped mixture with the frangipane and proceed in everything as indicated ahead in the article of frangipane tart
