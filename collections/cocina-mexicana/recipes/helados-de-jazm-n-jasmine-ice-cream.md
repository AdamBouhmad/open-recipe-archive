## Ingredients

- ten ounces of white sugar
- two cuartillos of common water
- four ounces of recently picked jasmine flowers

## Directions

1. Dissolve ten ounces of white sugar in two cuartillos of common water over gentle heat
2. Pour while warm over four ounces of recently picked jasmine flowers placed at the bottom of a porcelain bowl or clay vessel
3. After four hours of infusion in the covered vessel, remove and strain through cloth or sieve
4. Freeze in the ice cream mold
