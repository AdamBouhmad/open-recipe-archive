## Ingredients

- Goose
- Goose liver
- Onions
- Sage
- Lard
- Veal or beef juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as above and fill the body with a mixture made from chopped liver, onions in small pieces and a bit of chopped sage, all well mixed and sautéed in lard until it takes on a golden color
2. Place on the spit and after roasting, serve with veal or beef juice
