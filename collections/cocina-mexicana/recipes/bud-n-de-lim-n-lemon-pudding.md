## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- two egg whites
- half pound of almonds not very finely ground
- more than half pound of sifted sugar
- four or five grated lemons
- butter
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg yolks with two egg whites as if making royal eggs
2. Once it is that way, add half pound of almonds not very finely ground, more than half pound of sifted sugar, four or five grated lemons, until everything mixed well tastes of sufficient lemon
3. Grease the pudding mold with butter and on top of it sprinkle sufficient sponge cake powder to form a crust
4. Pour this paste into the said mold, remove the fire from the stove, and place it there
5. Then place small pieces of butter on top, and put a griddle with fire on it so it has two fires
6. To know when it is cooked, test with a straw; when you insert it and it comes out clean and dry, it is a sign that it is done
