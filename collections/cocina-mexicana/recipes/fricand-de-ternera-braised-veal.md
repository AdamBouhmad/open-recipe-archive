## Ingredients

- veal pulps
- pieces of fat ham
- cracked peppercorns
- cloves in small pieces
- water
- salt
- butter
- pieces of carrot
- whole clean onions
- very finely chopped onion
- flour
- all spices except saffron and cumin

## Directions

1. Divide the veal pulps into pieces two fingers thick
2. Lard them with small pieces of fat ham, cracked peppercorns and cloves in small pieces
3. Put them to cook in water with salt, butter, some pieces of carrot and whole clean onions
4. When these and the carrots are cooked, brown them in butter
5. Chop the onion very finely, sprinkle it with flour, fry the onion in a little butter, and as soon as it is browned add a little of the broth in which the meat was cooked, and season with all spices except saffron and cumin
6. Fry the cooked meat, add it to the seasoned broth, and when it has thickened a lot, remove from heat and serve, placing the fried carrots and onions on top, and you can add other garnishes of fried herbs and vegetables
