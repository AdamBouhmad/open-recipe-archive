## Ingredients

- beef liver sliced very thin
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- oil
- flour
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the very thin slices of liver with salt and pepper
2. Coat them with eggs beaten with a little oil
3. Then coat them in flour so they are covered with it
4. Fry in oil
5. Arrange in the form of a crown and place tomato sauce in the middle
