## Ingredients

- pumpkin-melon, raw
- lime water
- clarified syrup (made with three libras of sugar)

## Directions

1. Make round slices of this fruit in its raw state and remove the rind and seeds
2. Place them in well-settled lime water and keep them there for three or four minutes, then remove and dry them
3. Place afterwards in clarified, watered, and cold syrup made with three libras of sugar, and let it thicken over gentle heat
