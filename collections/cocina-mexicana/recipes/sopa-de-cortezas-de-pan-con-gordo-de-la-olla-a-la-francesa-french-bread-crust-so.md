## Ingredients

- bread crusts with little crumb
- meat broth
- pot fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove bread crusts with little crumb and place in a deep dish
2. Pour broth and pot fat over them
3. Place over fire until they form a crust
4. Prepare three layers of bread with crumb removed, leaving only the crust
5. Soak the crusts in the pot fat
6. Season with salt
7. When serving, drain the fat as the soup should be dry
8. Serve broth separately so each guest can add as much as desired to their plate with the bread and crust
