## Ingredients

- pumpkin seeds
- raw tomatoes
- green chiles, soaked
- a few cumin seeds
- lard
- cooked turkey
- chicken, cooked, optional
- pork jowl, optional

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin seeds and toast them, then grind them together with the shells
2. Then grind raw tomatoes, soaked green chiles and a few cumin seeds, proportioning the amounts so the mole barely stings
3. Fry everything in lard and add the cooked turkey
4. You can add chicken, also cooked, and pork jowl
