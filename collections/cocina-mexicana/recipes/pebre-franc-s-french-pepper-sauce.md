## Ingredients

- Good handful of parsley leaves
- Onions
- Two bay leaves
- Thyme
- Fine pepper
- One glass of vinegar
- Butter
- Two ladlefuls of Spanish brown sauce
- One ladleful of broth

## Directions

1. Place parsley leaves, onions, bay leaves, thyme, fine pepper, vinegar, and butter in a cooking pot
2. Let the vinegar reduce until little remains in the pot
3. Add two ladlefuls of Spanish brown sauce and one ladleful of broth
4. Let reduce to the proper point and pass through a sieve without pressing
