## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Onions, cut into quarters
- Water
- White salt
- Oil
- Lard
- Tomato
- Xilomate
- Garlic
- Parsley
- All spices, ground, except saffron
- Oregano powder

## Directions

1. After thoroughly cleaning the cabbage of all green or tough leaves, put it to cook with onions cut into quarters, water, and white salt.
2. In a pan with equal parts oil and lard, fry the tomato, xilomate, garlic, and parsley, all chopped, and all spices ground, except saffron.
3. When everything is well fried, add the cooked cabbage and onions so they also fry.
4. Once fried, serve hot with oil and vinegar, seasoned with salt and oregano powder.
