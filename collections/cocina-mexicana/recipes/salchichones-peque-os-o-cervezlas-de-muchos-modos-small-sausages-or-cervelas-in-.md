## Ingredients

- Ground pork and other ingredients as in previous sausage articles
- Ground truffles
- Chopped shallots
- Onions
- Butter

## Directions

1. Proceed with composition as in the previous sausage articles, and additionally add ground truffles and chopped shallots according to what you want the sausages to be made of
2. If they are to be onion sausages, chop the onions and parboil them over high heat in a pot with a little butter that will have been melted beforehand
3. When the onions are almost cooked, incorporate them into the minced mixture and proceed as indicated for the cervelas or sausages of this class
