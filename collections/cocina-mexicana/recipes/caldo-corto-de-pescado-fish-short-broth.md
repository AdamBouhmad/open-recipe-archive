## Ingredients

- butter
- carrots
- onions
- celery
- turnips
- fish
- water
- salt
- herbs

## Directions

1. Place a pan over heat with butter, carrots, onions, celery, turnips, and other vegetables cut finely.
2. Add the fish in pieces, moisten with a little water, and when it begins to simmer, add boiling water with salt and a bouquet of herbs.
3. Pass everything through a silk sieve for necessary uses.
