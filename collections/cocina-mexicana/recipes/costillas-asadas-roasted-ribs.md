## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- bread crumbs
- flour
- white wine
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Cut, prepare and flatten the ribs
2. Place on the roaster with a little butter
3. While roasting cover with bread crumbs mixed with salt, pepper and fine herbs
4. Remove when done
5. Add to the butter bread crumbs, a little flour and a glass of white wine
6. Let reduce a little and add sliced gherkins
