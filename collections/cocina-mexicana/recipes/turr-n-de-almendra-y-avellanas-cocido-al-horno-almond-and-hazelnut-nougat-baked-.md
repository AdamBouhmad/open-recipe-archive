## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound almonds
- 1/4 pound hazelnuts
- 1 piece of green lemon peel
- sufficient powdered sugar
- 2 or 3 egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Prepare almonds and hazelnuts as in the previous article
2. Divide into small parts together with a green lemon peel
3. Dry well in a pot over fire
4. Mix with sufficient powdered sugar and 2 or 3 well-beaten egg whites
5. Make a workable paste with all these ingredients
6. Distribute on sheets of paper
7. Bake in the oven until done
