## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- roasted chile poblano
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes and grind them in the molcajete.
2. Fry them with plenty of butter and with strips of roasted chile poblano.
3. Once they are well fried, add the eggs there, let them cook scrambled and when removing them add slices of fresh cheese.
