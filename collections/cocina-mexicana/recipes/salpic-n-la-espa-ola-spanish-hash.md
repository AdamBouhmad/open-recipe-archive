## Ingredients

- Beef
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Onions, sliced

## Directions

1. Cook the meat, which is usually beef
2. Chop it and season with pepper, salt, vinegar, and sliced onions
3. Mix everything together and eat cold
