## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- salt
- cod
- oil
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- egg yolks
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. After soaking the chickpeas from the previous day, cook them in water with salt and a little cod
2. When already cooked, add plenty of oil
3. Let them boil more
4. Shortly before eating, stew them with fried onion, roasted garlic, parsley, and egg yolks
5. Add all of this away from the heat, stirring much to serve them at the table very hot with toasted bread
