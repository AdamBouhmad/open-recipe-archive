## Ingredients

- four and a half pounds of very white sugar
- one pound of ground sweet potato
- one pound of ground almonds
- one pound of grated coconut
- one pound of custard
- sliced mamey
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with four and a half pounds of very white sugar and clarify it
2. Add one pound of ground sweet potato and let it boil
3. When the paste is near the point of cajeta, add one pound of ground almonds, one pound of grated coconut, and equal quantity of custard
4. Let it boil again and give it high or low point if it is to cover various layers of mamey
5. Pour over slices of mamey and decorate on top with raisins, almonds and pine nuts
