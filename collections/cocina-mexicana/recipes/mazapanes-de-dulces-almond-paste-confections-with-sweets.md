## Ingredients

- Almond paste
- Sweet or marmalade, equal weight to the almond paste

## Directions

1. Prepare almond paste as in the preceding recipe
2. Instead of using fruit, add an equal weight of sweet or marmalade
3. Cook and finish as previously described
