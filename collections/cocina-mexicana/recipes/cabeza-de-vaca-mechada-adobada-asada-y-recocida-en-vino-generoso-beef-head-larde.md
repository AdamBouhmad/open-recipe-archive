## Ingredients

- Beef head, prepared as in preceding recipe
- Lard
- Ground garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon powder
- Fortified wine, such as Málaga or Parras wine
- Lettuce leaves
- Celery
- Water
- Olives
- Small chiles
- Capers
- Caper berries

## Directions

1. Roast or fry the head as described in the previous article
2. Cut and divide into pieces, arranging them well in a casserole coated with lard
3. Between each layer of meat and head parts, place ground garlic, salt, pepper, cloves, and cinnamon powder
4. Cover everything with fortified wine such as Málaga or Parras wine
5. Let it boil over two fires until fried and dry
6. Serve on lettuce leaves, adorned with more lettuce hearts in quarters, peeled and curled celery in water, olives, small chiles, capers, and caper berries
