## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Onion
- Garlic
- Broth from the pot
- Saffron
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Red wine
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. After the woodcock are plucked without washing them, roast them on a grill
2. Remove the entrails and grind them with clove, cinnamon and pepper
3. Chop plenty of onion and garlic, fry it, and when well fried add the ground entrails and a little broth from the pot
4. Add saffron if desired
5. Place the woodcock in the fried mixture to cook
6. Add a little thyme, some bay leaves, plenty of red wine, some slices of toasted bread and place them at the bottom of the platter with the woodcock on top
