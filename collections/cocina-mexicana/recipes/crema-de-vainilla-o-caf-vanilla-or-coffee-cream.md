## Ingredients

- vanilla
- milk
- cream (for whipping)
- one tablespoon of coffee or one teaspoon for the other variety

## Directions

1. For vanilla: boil a little vanilla in milk for a few minutes, strain the milk, and add to the cream before beating
2. For coffee: add one tablespoon of coffee at siesta time
