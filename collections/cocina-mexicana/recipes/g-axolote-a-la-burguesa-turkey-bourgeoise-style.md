## Ingredients

- güaxolote (turkey)
- butter or melted ham
- parsley, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- garlic, a little, finely chopped
- salt
- coarse pepper
- ham slices
- white wine
- broth
- broth, strained

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe in the flame and clean the turkey.
2. Flatten it a little over the belly and gather in the legs.
3. Parboil it in a pan with butter or melted ham, with finely chopped parsley, onion, mushrooms and a little garlic.
4. Transfer to another pan with its seasoning, salt and coarse pepper.
5. Cover the belly with slices of ham, moisten with equal parts white wine and broth, and let it cook slowly.
6. Then remove the fat and add to the sauce a little strained broth to bind it.
