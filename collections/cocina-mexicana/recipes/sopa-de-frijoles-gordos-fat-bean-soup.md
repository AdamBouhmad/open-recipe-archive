## Ingredients

- frijoles gordos cocidos
- agua
- tortera
- manteca
- aceite
- cebollas picadas
- caldo sazonado
- pan dorado en manteca
- orégano en polvo
- queso rallado
- comal
- huevos estrellados en manteca

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked fat beans and dissolve them in water, then strain
2. Put a pot on the fire with manteca and oil, frying three or four finely chopped onions there
3. When they are browned, add the seasoned broth, letting it boil until it thickens somewhat
4. Then add slices of bread browned in manteca, a little more oil and powdered oregano
5. Let the soup cook over two fires and when it is ready to remove from the fire, sprinkle grated cheese on it, putting the griddle back on it until it should be served
6. To take to the table, add a little more oil on top and garnish with eggs fried in manteca
7. This soup will serve for days of abstinence if it is not pot broth in which it was made
