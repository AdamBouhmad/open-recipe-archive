## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter, half the weight of the potatoes
- salt
- spices
- chopped parsley
- four egg yolks
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in water or preferably in hot ashes
2. Grind and sieve them
3. Add half their weight in butter with salt, spices, chopped parsley, and four egg yolks
4. Pound everything perfectly
5. Form into small balls
6. Bread and fry
