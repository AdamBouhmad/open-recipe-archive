## Ingredients

- purslane leaves
- salt
- lard
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- pork broth
- fine salt
- comino
- bread
- pork meat

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the purslane stems and separate them well
2. Cook with salt until well done, which is known when they fall apart when rubbed with the fingers
3. Drain and set aside
4. Put a cazuela with lard on the fire
5. Finely chop raw tomato and garlic, and cut onion heads into quarters
6. Fry all this in the lard until well fried and the inner skins of the tomato have curled
7. Remove the purslane from the pot and wash well in cold water
8. Drain, squeeze well and fry with the tomato
9. When well fried, add pork broth seasoned with fine salt and some ground comino mixed with bread crumb soaked in broth
10. Add the cooked pork meat and let the purslane season
