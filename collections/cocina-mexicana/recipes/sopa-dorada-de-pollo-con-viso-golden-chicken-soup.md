## Ingredients

- tender chickens, roasted
- bread, sliced
- chicken pieces
- ham strips
- chorizo, sliced
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- sesame, toasted
- syrup of less than half point
- cloves
- cinnamon
- wine, sufficient

## Directions

1. After roasting tender chickens, disjoint them and debone the breasts and thighs
2. Slice the breasts
3. In a pot make layers alternating sliced bread and chicken pieces with ham strips, chorizo slices, raisins, almonds, pine nuts and toasted sesame, with bread as the final layer
4. Moisten all with syrup of less than half point boiled with cloves, cinnamon and sufficient wine
5. Cook the soup over two fires until tender
