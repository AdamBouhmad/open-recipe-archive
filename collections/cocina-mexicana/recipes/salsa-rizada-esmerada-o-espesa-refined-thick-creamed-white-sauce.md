## Ingredients

- Beef shank bones
- Chickens
- Carrots
- Onions
- Parsley and small onions
- Concentrated broth
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the Spanish sauce
2. Allow it to thicken by letting it reduce
3. Remove the fat carefully and skim it well
4. Take great care to keep this sauce very white
5. Add the mushrooms
6. Pass it through a fine sieve
7. Keep it warm in a water bath
