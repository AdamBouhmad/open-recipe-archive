## Ingredients

- duck
- poultry forcemeat
- pork meat forcemeat
- chestnuts
- white wine
- Spanish sauce
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. After emptying the ducks through the crop, bone them by turning and gradually removing all the bones
2. Fill them halfway with forcemeat of poultry and pork meat
3. Tie and cook over coals
4. When cooked, wipe away the fat and serve with a good sauce or with a sauce of chestnuts cooked with a cup of white wine, some Spanish sauce and a little fresh butter.
