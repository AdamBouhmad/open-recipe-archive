## Ingredients

- partridge (cleaned)
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- aromatic herbs
- garlic (two handfuls)
- sour orange (cut in wheels)

## Directions

1. Once the partridge is cleaned, simmer in a pan with butter.
2. Remove the partridge and to the same butter in which it fried add broth, salt, pepper and all herbs, and let boil again.
3. Put the partridge and let it cook.
4. While frying apart two handfuls of garlic and in another small pan a sour orange cut in wheels.
5. A quarter hour before serving mix everything so it finishes cooking.
