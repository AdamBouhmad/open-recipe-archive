## Ingredients

- squabs
- lard
- salt
- chorizos
- ham strips
- onions in quarters
- garlic
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- water
- peas
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- vinegar
- fried and ground bread
- oil
- olives
- caperberries
- capers

## Directions

1. Divide the squabs in half and fry in lard with salt, chorizos and ham strips
2. Add quartered onions, garlic and roasted ground tomatoes, and fry everything
3. Add the necessary water, peas, cloves, pepper and ground cumin
4. Add a little vinegar and fried ground bread for thickening
5. When the squabs are cooked and the sauce is well seasoned, remove from heat
6. Add oil, olives, caperberries and capers
