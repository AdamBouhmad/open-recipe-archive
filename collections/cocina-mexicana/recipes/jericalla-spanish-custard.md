## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk and strain it
2. Add cinnamon sticks and five egg yolks for each cup of milk
3. Pour into a bowl and set to curdle in a water bath with heat from above, or in the steam of a pot, always covered with a griddle with coals
4. Serve with cinnamon sprinkled on top
