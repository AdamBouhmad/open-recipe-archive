## Ingredients

- 18 ounces of apricot or peach kernels
- 1 drachm of cinnamon
- 12 cuartillos of spirits
- 3 pounds of sugar
- 4 cuartillos of water
- 1 cuartillo of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Soak the kernels in cold water for 10 to 12 hours to remove their shells
2. Roughly pound them in a marble mortar with 1 drachm of cinnamon
3. Let this macerate for 2 hours in 12 cuartillos of spirits
4. Proceed immediately to distillation in a bain-marie, to obtain two-thirds of the liquid
5. Dissolve 3 pounds of sugar in 4 cuartillos of water, adding another cuartillo of orange flower water
6. Mix everything together, clarify in a cloth strainer, and store in well-sealed bottles
