## Ingredients

- 4 regular melons, ground
- 3 pounds of clarified sugar, warm
- 6 egg yolks
- half a pound of not very finely ground almonds

## Directions

1. Add the ground melons to the clarified warm sugar
2. Add the egg yolks and half a pound of not very finely ground almonds
3. Place everything on the heat and stir until it reaches the consistency of cajeta
