## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove
- toasted and ground sponge cake
- beaten egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Grind cooked egg yolks and mix with sugar, cinnamon and clove in powder form
2. Mix with toasted and ground sponge cake, making a paste that can be sliced
3. Coat the slices with beaten egg
4. Fry in lard
