## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Meat for picadillo or chard and potatoes for filling
- Chard
- Potatoes
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Garlic
- Lard

## Directions

1. Open a pork loin carefully so that the cut does not go all the way through, forming a pocket like a bag
2. Fill either with raw picadillo as for chiles, or with cooked chard and cooked potatoes
3. If filling with chard, cook the chard and potatoes first, then chop them together
4. Fry a mixture of tomato, onion and garlic all chopped, and add the chard and potatoes to this mixture
5. After filling, sew the opening closed
6. Cook over fire with the sauce described, or with one made from roasted tomatoes ground with spices, added to the roast with a little vinegar, letting it season
7. Serve with salad and fried potatoes
