## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- water
- butter or olive oil
- salt
- black pepper
- parsley
- onions

## Directions

1. Wash the sorrel leaves well, removing the stems first
2. Leave in fresh water for one or two hours
3. Remove and dry, then chop finely with a knife on a board
4. Place in a saucepan with good butter or olive oil, salt, black pepper, parsley and onions, all finely chopped
5. After frying, remove from the fire and serve
