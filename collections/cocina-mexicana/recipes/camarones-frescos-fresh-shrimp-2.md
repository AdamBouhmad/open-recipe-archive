## Ingredients

- shrimp
- salt
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- cloves
- lemon
- cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Once cleaned, place the shrimp to cook with a bit of salt
2. Fry them in oil
3. Add breadcrumbs, pepper and cloves, a few drops of lemon and cooked potatoes cut into pieces
4. Add sufficient water and let them boil
5. The sauce should be neither thick nor watery
