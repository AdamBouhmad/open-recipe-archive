## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- toasted sesame
- wine
- white fish
- salt
- coating batter for fruit
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Grind cooked egg yolks and season with clove, cinnamon, pepper, toasted sesame and wine, leaving a consistency appropriate for stuffing fish
2. Wash the fish and rub with salt
3. Stuff the fish with the prepared mixture
4. Cover with the coating batter for fruit
5. Fry in lard and serve
