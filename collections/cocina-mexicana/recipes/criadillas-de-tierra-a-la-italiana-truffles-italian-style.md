## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Butter
- Italian sauce or Spanish sauce
- One glass of good white wine
- Toasted bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the truffles as thinly as possible
2. Cut into slices and fry in a saucepan with a piece of butter
3. Add Italian or Spanish sauce and a glass of good white wine
4. Skim the broth and let it reduce until of good consistency
5. Serve the truffles decorated with fried toasts
