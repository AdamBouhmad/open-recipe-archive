## Ingredients

- Twelve eggs
- One and a half mamón of fresh cheese
- Ground cinnamon
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Lard
- Syrup of medium point
- Powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs and dissolve one and a half mamón of fresh cheese in them
2. Add ground cinnamon, raisins, and almonds
3. Coat a pot with lard, add everything and place over two fires
4. Cut into quarters
5. Dip in syrup of medium point until it comes to a boil, with powdered cinnamon on top
