## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- 2 quarts of boiling water
- 1 measure of tea
- 1 good measure of sugar

## Directions

1. Choose three of the best lemons and remove the peels very lightly, keeping them as thin as possible
2. Separate all the white pith, which is useless, and discard it
3. Cut the juicy part into slices, removing the seeds
4. Put these slices and the lemon peels into two quarts of boiling water with a good measure of tea
5. Leave to infuse for five minutes
6. Strain through a serviette
7. Add two quarts of aguardiente and twelve ounces of sugar
8. Put on the fire and remove when it begins to boil
9. Pour into the punch bowl to serve at table
