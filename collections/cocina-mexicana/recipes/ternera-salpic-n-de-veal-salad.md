## Ingredients

- veal pulp
- ham with fat
- salt
- chopped onion
- salt
- pepper
- capers
- chopped parsley
- onion
- avocado slices
- oil
- vinegar
- olives
- small chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the veal pulp well with interspersed ham in large pieces and salt.
2. After both are well cooked, shred them, mixing together.
3. Place on a plate.
4. Add chopped onion, salt, pepper, capers, chopped parsley, onion and avocado slices, oil and vinegar as for salad.
5. Garnish on top with olives and small chiles in vinegar.
