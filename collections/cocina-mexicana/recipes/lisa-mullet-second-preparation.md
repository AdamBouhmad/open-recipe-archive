## Ingredients

- mullet
- oil
- garlic
- ground spices
- vinegar
- Chinese oregano

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the mullets
2. Place them in a pan with oil and a crushed head of garlic
3. Let them fry
4. Once fried, grind spices with vinegar
5. Add them to the pan with a bit of Chinese oregano and plenty of vinegar
6. Let them simmer over low heat
