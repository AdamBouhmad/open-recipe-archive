## Ingredients

- Plaice
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- White wine or water
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Place some plaice on a plate spread with butter
2. Add salt, pepper, and crushed parsley
3. Pour a glass of white wine or water over them
4. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top
5. Place the plate in an oven and cover it
6. Let it simmer for ten minutes
7. Serve once the fish is cooked
