## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one small cup of virgin honey
- ten egg whites, unbeaten

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar and honey together and bring to the ball stage
2. Cool and add ten unbeaten egg whites
3. Place over heat for a short time until it reaches the proper consistency
4. Pour onto a platter and decorate with toasted hazelnuts and almonds, raisins, and a dusting of cinnamon
