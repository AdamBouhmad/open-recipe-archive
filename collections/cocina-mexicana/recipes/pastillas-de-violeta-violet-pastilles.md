## Ingredients

- four pounds sugar
- one pound violet flowers without calyces or stems
- one quarter pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Prepare four pounds of sugar as described
2. Place one pound of violet flowers without calyces or stems in an alembic
3. Pour one quarter pint of boiling water and seal the alembic hermetically
4. Place in a stove for twelve hours
5. Pass the infusion through cloth, squeezing well to extract all the juice
6. Dissolve the sugar with the juice and make the paste
7. Continue the rest of the preparation as previously described
