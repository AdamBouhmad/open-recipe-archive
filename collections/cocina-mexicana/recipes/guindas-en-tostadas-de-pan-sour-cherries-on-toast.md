## Ingredients

- One tin of sour cherry preserve
- Small slices of bread
- Butter
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the tin of sour cherry preserve into a silver saucepan and heat it.
2. Fry small slices of bread in butter.
3. Once fried, roll them in powdered sugar.
4. When removed from the sugar, decorate with the confiture by pouring it on top.
5. Serve hot.
