## Ingredients

- Ground truffles
- Parsley
- Onion
- Butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fresh, thick mackerel
- Grape leaves
- Ham slices
- Lemon juice
- Double paper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Chop some truffles with parsley and onion
2. Mix this with good butter, salt, and pepper
3. Stuff two selected, thick, fresh mackerel with this mixture
4. Wrap them in grape leaves with ham slices
5. Make a double paper box, oil it on the outside, and place the mackerel inside with a bit of butter on the bottom
6. Place an oiled paper sheet on a grill and arrange the box on top
7. Cook the fish over low heat
8. Serve in the same box with lemon juice
