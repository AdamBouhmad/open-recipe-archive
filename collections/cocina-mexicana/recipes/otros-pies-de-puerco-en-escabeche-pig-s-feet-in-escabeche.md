## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- eggs
- biscuits
- lard
- garlic
- water
- clove
- cinnamon
- sour lime
- vinegar
- lime leaves
- sesame

## Directions

1. Fry the feet with eggs as for stuffed chiles
2. Grind trimmed biscuits until they resemble sesame for pipian
3. Fry minced garlic in lard, then fry the biscuit in the same pan
4. Add water, clove and ground cinnamon
5. Add sliced cooked sour lime, vinegar and lime leaves
6. Add the pig's feet to season
7. When serving, sprinkle with toasted sesame
