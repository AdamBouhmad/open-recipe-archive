## Ingredients

- two coconuts
- four ounces of almonds
- four ounces of pumpkin seeds
- four ounces of walnuts
- all ground
- twenty egg yolks, well beaten
- butter
- toasted and ground sponge cake
- pine nuts
- almond strips
- dragées
- sesame

## Directions

1. Prepare two coconuts as for cajeta, and when at the right point, mix with four ounces of almonds, four ounces of pumpkin seeds and four ounces of walnuts, all ground
2. Cook this over heat, and when at the right point, mix with twenty well-beaten egg yolks and pour into a pan coated with butter and dusted with toasted ground sponge cake, adding more sponge cake on top
3. Cook over two fires, and when the cake is half-cooked, decorate with pine nuts, almond strips, dragées and sesame, then continue cooking until done
