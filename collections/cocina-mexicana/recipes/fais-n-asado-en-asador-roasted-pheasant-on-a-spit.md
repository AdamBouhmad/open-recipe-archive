## Ingredients

- Pheasants
- Thin ham for larding
- Paper
- Salt
- Pepper
- Verjuice or orange sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck the pheasants, clean them, singe them and clean them perfectly.
2. Lard them with thin ham and place them on the spit, where they are left to cook over gentle heat, covered with a piece of paper.
3. When they are almost cooked, remove the paper and let them take on a beautiful golden color.
4. Remove from the spit and serve hot with verjuice or orange sauce with salt and pepper.
