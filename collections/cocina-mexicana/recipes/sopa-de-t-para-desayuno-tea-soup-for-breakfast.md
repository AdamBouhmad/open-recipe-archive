## Ingredients

- Bread slices
- Butter
- Sugar
- Strong tea infusion
- Milk (double the quantity of tea)

## Directions

1. Butter the bread slices with which the soup tureen is lined
2. Sprinkle them with sugar
3. Pour over them a glass of very strong tea infusion and double the quantity of milk
