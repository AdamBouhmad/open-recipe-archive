## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- ham slices
- thin lemon slices
- braised broth

## Directions

1. Pluck the capon, singe it, clean it and empty it: bend the legs back over the haunches, and thread the body with a special needle strung with twine or hemp cord so that the haunches are secured against the body, tightening the knot and making the belly firm so that the legs do not decompose while cooking.
2. Place slices of ham in a casserole and arrange the capon over them, covering with very thin slices of lemon and other slices of ham on top of everything: pour braised broth over it and let it cook for five-quarters of an hour.
