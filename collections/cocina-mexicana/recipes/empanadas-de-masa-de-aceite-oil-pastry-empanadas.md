## Ingredients

- Six pounds flour
- Two pounds lard
- Half pound powdered sugar
- Eight egg yolks
- One cup cooking oil
- Sufficient salt
- Hot water
- Sesame seeds
- Ground saffron

## Directions

1. Soak the flour with the lard and powdered sugar, egg yolks, cooking oil and sufficient salt
2. Dissolve in a little hot water and knead
3. While kneading, add toasted sesame seeds and ground saffron for good color
4. Form the empanadas
