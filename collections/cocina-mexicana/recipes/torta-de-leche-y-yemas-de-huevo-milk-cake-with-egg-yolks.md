## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- necessary sugar
- sponge cake (mamón), sliced
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Mix one cuartillo of milk with twelve egg yolks and necessary sugar
2. Pour over sliced sponge cake placed in a buttered cake pan
3. Cook over two fires
