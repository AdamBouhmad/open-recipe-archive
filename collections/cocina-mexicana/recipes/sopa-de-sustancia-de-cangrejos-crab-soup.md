## Ingredients

- fifty crabs
- salt
- coarse pepper
- vinegar
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the crabs well
2. Remove the two middle claws from each crab's tail to extract an intestine
3. Cook the crabs at high heat with salt, coarse pepper and vinegar
4. Open them immediately to extract the interior part of the belly
5. Mash bread crumbs fried in butter
6. Heat this substance over low heat, being careful it does not boil
7. Serve over bread cubes fried in butter that have been prepared in advance
