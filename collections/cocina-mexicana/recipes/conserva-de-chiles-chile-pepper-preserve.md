## Ingredients

- Poblano or tornachile peppers
- Salt water
- Clear water
- Clarified syrup
- Orange blossom water
- Sesame

## Directions

1. Gently remove the crowns from the poblano or tornachile peppers
2. Remove the veins and seeds, rinse
3. Add to salt water, changing it the next day with water that has less salt and is settled
4. Leave in this water for about half an hour, then return to salt water
5. Repeat daily until the peppers no longer are spicy
6. Give them a boil, and if they still retain some heat, place in clear water until they lose it entirely
7. Preserve over gentle heat in clarified syrup
8. Add orange blossom water and sesame
