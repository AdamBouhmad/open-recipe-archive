## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- paper
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs quite thin
2. Place them on paper cut for this purpose
3. Season with salt, pepper, parsley and garlic, all finely chopped, and small pieces of lard
4. Wrap them in the paper, leaving the bone outside
5. Place on a grill over low heat
6. Serve with the papers still on
