## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Onion
- Salt
- Oil or melted butter
- Pepper
- Salt in grains
- A little vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and cook whole potatoes with onion and salt
2. When cooked but not falling apart, remove and put them on a plate to cool
3. Cut them into large pieces and arrange them on the platter where they are to be served
4. Dress them with oil or melted butter, a pinch of pepper, salt in grains and a little vinegar
