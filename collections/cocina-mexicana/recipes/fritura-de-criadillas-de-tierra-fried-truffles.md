## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning the truffles well, peel them, round them, and cut them into slices two lines thick.
2. Melt butter in a pan and place the truffles on top with a little salt and coarse pepper.
3. Put the pan over a fairly brisk fire, and after the truffles have boiled a few times in the butter, turn them over.
4. Three minutes are sufficient for them to cook perfectly.
5. When cooked, drain them and put them in refined Spanish sauce, and arrange them on the plate to serve as an intermediate course.
