## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- salt
- water
- butter
- capers, ground
- garlic, browned in butter
- cloves
- black pepper
- saffron, ground
- small whole onions, cooked
- apple slices
- sweet potato slices
- plantain slices
- broth from cooking the tongue
- flour, browned
- vinegar
- oil
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Pound the tongue, then cook it in salted water
2. Skin the tongue, slice it, and fry in butter
3. Fry ground capers with garlic browned in butter in the same pan
4. Add cloves, pepper, and ground saffron
5. Add the cooked whole onions, sliced apple, sweet potato, and plantain
6. Pour in the broth in which the tongue was cooked and the browned flour
7. Add vinegar and oil
8. Let everything season until the broth reaches a good consistency
9. Garnish with small chiles and olives when removing from heat
