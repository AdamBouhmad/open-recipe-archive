## Ingredients

- 20 egg yolks
- butter
- water
- syrup
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. For one conserving cup, twenty yolks are needed, which are placed in a new pan and beaten with a spoon until very thick
2. Butter the conserving cup and pour the beaten yolks into it
3. Heat a pot of water over the fire, and when it is boiling, place the cup in it, which will be covered with a small comal with embers when the eggs begin to set
4. From time to time insert a straw to know if they are cooked, which is known by it coming out clean
5. Remove the cup from the water and empty it
6. Cut the eggs into slices, which are placed in syrup, and finished with raisins, almonds, pine nuts and ground cinnamon
