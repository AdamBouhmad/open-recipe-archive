## Ingredients

- fresh cheese
- salt
- chile-ajo (chile and garlic paste)

## Directions

1. Prepare with fresh cheese that is already being crumbled, which is ground with a little salt and pressed as is customary, covering it with a layer of chile-ajo and letting it dry in the press.
