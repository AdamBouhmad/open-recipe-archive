## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- four ounces of lard
- four ounces of sugar
- two egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- juice of two sweet oranges
- five or six cloves
- cinnamon, pinched with three fingers

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and form biscuits
2. Cook in the oven
3. When cooked, sprinkle sugar on top
