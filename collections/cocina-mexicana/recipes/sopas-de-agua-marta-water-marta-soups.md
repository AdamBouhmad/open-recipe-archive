## Ingredients

- Water
- One onion
- Half an ounce of lard
- Toasted and ground biscuit
- A little saffron
- Dry roasted cilantro
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. In a small pot with water, put an onion cut in a cross at the top to give it flavor and let it boil twice
2. Remove the onion and add to the boiling water half an ounce of lard and toasted and ground biscuit, adding little by little
3. When dissolved, add a little saffron and a little dry roasted cilantro, both ground separately, and sugar
4. To serve, sprinkle ground cinnamon over it
