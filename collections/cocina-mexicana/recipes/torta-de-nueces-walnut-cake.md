## Ingredients

- walnuts
- syrup
- butter
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds
- candied citron
- sesame
- cinnamon
- clove
- egg yolks
- biscuit

## Directions

1. Split the walnuts, soak them, skin them and toast them without burning
2. Grind them and place in syrup at the proper point that will have been prepared, dissolving in it so that a paste forms
3. In a buttered plate or pot, place a layer of walnut with syrup on a sheet of paper
4. On top place another layer of pine nuts, raisins, almonds, candied citron and toasted sesame, cinnamon and clove, and cover with beaten egg yolks and a little powdered biscuit
5. In this order alternate the layers desired, with the last being of egg yolks, and cook the cake in the oven or between two fires
