## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- ham slices
- concentrated broth
- coarse salt
- meat essence or substance

## Directions

1. Arrange the capon as mentioned, cover it with slices of ham and cook it in concentrated broth.
2. It will be known to be well cooked when the wing is pinched; and being so, untie it and serve with whole coarse salt sprinkled on top and meat essence or substance underneath.
