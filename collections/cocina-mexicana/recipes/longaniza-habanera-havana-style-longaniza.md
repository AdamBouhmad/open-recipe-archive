## Ingredients

- vinegar
- oregano
- salt
- ground pepper
- crushed cilantro
- chopped garlic
- four bay leaves
- pork meat from the loin or muscle

## Directions

1. Prepare an adobo composed of vinegar, oregano, salt, ground pepper, crushed cilantro, chopped garlic, and four bay leaves
2. Add pork meat from the muscle or loin
3. Let it take on the flavor of the adobo well for about an hour and a half
4. Chop the meat into very small pieces
5. Place in intestines and tie as if eight finger-widths apart
6. This sausage is always served by frying the longanizas
