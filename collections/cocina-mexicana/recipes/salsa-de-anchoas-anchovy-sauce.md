## Ingredients

- two anchovies washed in vinegar
- ham or veal broth or substance
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the anchovies in vinegar and remove the bones
2. Chop them finely
3. Put them in a small pan with ham or veal broth, pepper and salt
4. Boil for a while and strain
5. When serving, add a little lemon juice
