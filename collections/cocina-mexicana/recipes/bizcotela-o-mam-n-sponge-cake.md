## Ingredients

- twenty egg whites
- twenty egg yolks
- one pound of ground sugar
- one and a half pounds of cornstarch, sifted

## Directions

1. Beat twenty egg whites well
2. Add the twenty yolks with one pound of ground sugar
3. Continue beating with one hand near the oven, which should already be hot
4. With the other hand, gradually sprinkle in one and a half pounds of sifted cornstarch
5. To test if the oven is at the right temperature, put a small portion of the batter in a small box and place it in the oven: if it burns, cool the oven slightly; if it does not rise, heat it more
6. When at the right temperature, place the bizcotelas in the oven, dusting them with ground sugar on top
