## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- One small cup of white wine
- Four ounces of butter
- Four ounces of sugar
- Water of salt
- Eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Knead well two pounds of flour with one small cup of white wine, four ounces of butter, four ounces of sugar, water of salt and eight egg yolks
2. Roll out the dough and cut the hojuelas
3. Brown them on both sides on a hot comal
4. Serve with syrup
