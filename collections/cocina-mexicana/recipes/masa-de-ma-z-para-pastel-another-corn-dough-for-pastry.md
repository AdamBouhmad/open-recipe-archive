## Ingredients

- Nixtamal
- Portion of white bread
- Cloves, cinnamon, and saffron
- Sugar
- Some raw egg yolks
- A little butter

## Directions

1. Wash and remove the tips from the nixtamal
2. Grind it with a portion of white bread
3. Add cloves, cinnamon, saffron, sugar, some raw egg yolks, and a little butter, so as not to remove the elasticity from the dough
4. With cloths lowered from the metate, as has been said, form the pastries
