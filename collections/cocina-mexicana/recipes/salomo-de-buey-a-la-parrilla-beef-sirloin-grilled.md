## Ingredients

- Beef sirloin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Parsley
- Lemon juice or verjuice
- Fried potatoes or spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the nerves and cut the beef two fingers thick
2. Flatten with the back of a machete
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Place on the grill over lively fire
5. When cooked, serve with butter mixed with parsley and a little lemon juice or verjuice, and with fried potatoes or any spicy sauce
