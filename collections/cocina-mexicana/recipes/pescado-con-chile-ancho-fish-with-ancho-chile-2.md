## Ingredients

- Ancho chile
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Onion
- Water
- Bread
- Oregano
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind soaked ancho chile with garlic, pepper, clove, and cinnamon
2. Fry in lard with onion
3. Once half fried, dilute with water, a little ground and moistened bread
4. Let the raw fish cook in that broth
5. Add powdered oregano and vinegar
