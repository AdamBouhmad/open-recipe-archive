## Ingredients

- oysters
- lard
- oil
- ground garlic
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Add the oysters to lard and oil
2. Add ground garlic and pepper and let boil until dry, as no water should be added
3. Season with salt and vinegar
