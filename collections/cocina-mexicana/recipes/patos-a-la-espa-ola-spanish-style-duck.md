## Ingredients

- Ducks, divided into quarters
- Lard
- Garlic heads, crushed
- Vinegar
- Water
- Onions, in quarters
- Clove
- Pepper, ground

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks and divide into quarters.
2. Fry crushed garlic heads in lard, then fry the meat.
3. Add vinegar with a little water, onions in quarters, ground clove, and pepper.
4. Once cooked, remove and serve.
