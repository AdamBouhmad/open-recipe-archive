## Ingredients

- Four cuartillos of double cream
- Twelve ounces of powdered sugar
- One good glass of maraschino

## Directions

1. Pour twelve ounces of powdered sugar into four cuartillos of double cream and add one good glass of maraschino
2. When everything is well dissolved and mixed, beat it to form the foam
3. Do the rest as in the preceding articles
