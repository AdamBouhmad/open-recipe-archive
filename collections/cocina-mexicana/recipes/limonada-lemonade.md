## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, water, and lemon juice in quantities according to taste and preference
2. The resulting liquid is a pleasant and very refreshing beverage called lemonade
3. If aguardiente is added to it, it is called punch and is served either cold or hot according to taste and circumstances
