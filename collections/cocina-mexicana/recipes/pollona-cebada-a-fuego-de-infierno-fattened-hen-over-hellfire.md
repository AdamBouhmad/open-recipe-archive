## Ingredients

- roasted fattened hen
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- thick substantial broth

## Directions

1. Divide a roasted fattened hen into portions and marinate them in oil with salt, pepper and lemon juice
2. Place on the grill over a very hot fire
3. Serve with thick substantial broth poured over
