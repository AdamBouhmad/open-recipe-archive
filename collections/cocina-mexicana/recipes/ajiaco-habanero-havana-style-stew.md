## Ingredients

- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Green beans, cooked and shelled
- Small squash, small and cooked separately
- Very tender green beans, cooked separately
- Broth from the cooking of mutton or included
- Salt
- Cloves
- Saffron, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Mutton or offal, cooked
- Plantain slices
- Apple slices
- Other fruits, if desired

## Directions

1. Fry onions and tomatoes cut coarsely in a competent quantity of lard until well fried
2. Add cooked and shelled green beans, small squash, and very tender green beans, all cooked separately
3. Add broth from the cooking of mutton or included
4. Season with sufficient salt and with ground cloves, saffron, and cinnamon
5. Add a little vinegar and a lump of sugar
6. Add the mutton or cooked offal in small pieces
7. Add slices of plantain, apple, and other fruits if desired
