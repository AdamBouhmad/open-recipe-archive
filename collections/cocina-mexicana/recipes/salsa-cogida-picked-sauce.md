## Ingredients

- Butter
- Finely chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. In a plate place a piece of butter, very finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper
2. Mix and blend everything well
3. Then add a little vinegar
