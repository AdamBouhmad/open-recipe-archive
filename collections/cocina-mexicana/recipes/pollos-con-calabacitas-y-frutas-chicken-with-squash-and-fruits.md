## Ingredients

- manteca
- pan
- ajos
- tomates asados
- sal
- azafrán
- clavo
- pimienta molida
- peregil picado
- aceite
- vinagre
- calabacita pequeña cruda
- pera
- durazno
- cebolitas cocidas
- membrillo
- pollos sanconchados
- caldo

## Directions

1. Fry bread slices and garlic in butter until they are golden; remove both
2. Grind roasted tomatoes with the toasted bread, seasoning with salt, saffron, clove and ground pepper
3. Add chopped parsley, oil, vinegar, slices of small raw squash, pear and peach, cooked small onions and strips of quince
4. Place the partially cooked chickens and the broth in which they were cooked
5. Let simmer until the things that were put in raw are well cooked and everything is well seasoned
