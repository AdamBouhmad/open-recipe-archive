## Ingredients

- Pork tongues
- Pork manilas (a cut)
- Pork shoulder blades
- Chickens (optional)
- Lettuce, raw
- Onions, blanched
- Radish
- Salt
- Avocados
- Vinegar
- Olive oil
- Tamales with salt (optional)
- Olives (optional)
- Chiles in vinegar (optional)
- Capers (optional)
- Romaine lettuce hearts
- Radishes
- Blanched onions in water

## Directions

1. Cook tongues, manilas, and shoulder blades of pork, and if desired, add chickens.
2. Remove bones and divide into small pieces.
3. Slice raw lettuces.
4. Slice blanched onions.
5. Chop a radish.
6. Put everything on a platter.
7. Sprinkle the necessary salt on top and distribute the pieces of meat.
8. Slice peeled avocados.
9. Over everything pour a regular amount of vinegar and sufficient raw oil.
10. Some add small tamales with salt, olives, chiles in vinegar, and capers.
11. Garnish the platter with romaine lettuce hearts, radishes cut into quarters, and blanched onions in water.
