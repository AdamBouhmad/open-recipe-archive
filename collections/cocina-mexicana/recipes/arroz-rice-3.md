## Ingredients

- rice
- saffron
- water
- all the usual ingredients for a sofrito
- powder of pepper
- powder of clove
- chicken
- fresh wide chile
- boiling water

## Directions

1. From the day before, wash the rice and put it to dry on a board
2. Dissolve a bit of saffron in hot water and dye the rice, letting it dry again
3. Make a sofrito of all the usual ingredients, adding a pinch of pepper and clove powder
4. Add the chicken in quarters so it softens a bit and then add the rice with one wide fresh deveined chile and boiling water to cover the contents
5. Put over violent fire; when the water has dried, move to low fire so it does not fall apart
