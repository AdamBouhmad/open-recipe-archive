## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- garlic cloves
- oil
- lard
- bread
- ground pepper
- bread powder
- thyme
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Desalt the cod very well
2. Put it in a casserole full of water and place on the fire until it heats, but before it boils remove it from the fire so that the cod does not fall apart
3. Take the cod out of the water, remove the skin and take out the bones
4. Peel some garlic cloves, crush them with oil and lard and grind them
5. Soak a piece of bread and grind it
6. Put oil and lard in a casserole, add the garlic and bread, add ground pepper, bread powder also and thyme with a little more oil
7. Then add the cod without water
8. Garnish with olives and capers
