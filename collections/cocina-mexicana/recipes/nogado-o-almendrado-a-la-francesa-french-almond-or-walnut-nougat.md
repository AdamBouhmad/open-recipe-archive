## Ingredients

- almonds or walnuts, one pound
- sugar, twelve ounces, pulverized

## Directions

1. Blanch, wash, and let drain one pound of sweet almonds or walnuts
2. Divide the almonds into five thin strips each and the walnuts into small pieces
3. Toast them in a very gentle oven until golden
4. Melt the pulverized sugar in a pan over low heat
5. When melted, add the hot almonds or walnuts, mixing perfectly with the dissolved sugar after removing the pan from heat
6. Place the paste on oiled molds, lifting it around the mold edge with the help of a lemon, pressing the almonds or walnuts with it
7. Make the paste as thin as possible
8. Remove the molds, arrange the piece, and serve
