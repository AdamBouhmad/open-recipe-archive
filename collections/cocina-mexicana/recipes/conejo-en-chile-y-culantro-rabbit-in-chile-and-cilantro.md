## Ingredients

- lard
- green chiles sliced
- cilantro
- cumin toasted
- clove
- pepper
- rabbit cooking broth
- rabbit portions cooked
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry sliced green chiles in lard with ground toasted cilantro, cumin, clove and pepper
2. Moisten with a little of the broth in which the rabbit meat was cooked
3. Add the rabbit portions already cooked
4. Add oil and vinegar and let season
