## Ingredients

- small handful of chervil
- half as much pimpinella
- half as much tarragon
- half as much small chives
- salt
- coarse pepper
- one glass full of mustard
- half glass of oil
- two to three raw egg yolks
- four to five spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Gather a small handful of chervil, the same amount of pimpinella, tarragon, and small chives
2. Blanch all these herbs and, pressing them well, mash them
3. Add salt, coarse pepper, and one glass full of mustard, and grind everything together again
4. Add half a glass of oil that will be mixed exactly with the rest
5. When everything is well thinned, add two or three raw egg yolks and four or five spoonfuls of vinegar
6. Stir everything and pass through a cloth
7. If the green color is not pronounced enough, it can be enhanced with spinach green
