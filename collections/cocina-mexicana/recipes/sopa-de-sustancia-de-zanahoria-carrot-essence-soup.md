## Ingredients

- Thirty to forty carrots
- One quarter lard
- Pot broth
- Sugar, about half an egg's weight
- Pan toasts

## Directions

1. Chop thirty to forty carrots
2. Fry them in one quarter lard and moisten with pot broth, adding a piece of sugar about half an egg's weight
3. Let them cook on low heat for at least two hours, taking care to add broth to them
4. Separate from heat and use this substance to make the soup, and if not sufficient augment with pot broth
5. Place bread toasts in a tureen and pouring the carrot broth there, bring to a boil taking care to remove the foam the soup gives off and that it not remain thick
