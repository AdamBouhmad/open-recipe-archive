## Ingredients

- Red mullets
- Onion slices
- Parsley
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 cloves
- Salt
- Black pepper
- 1 or 2 bottles white wine
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning the mullets, tie their heads
2. Place them in a pot with some onion slices, parsley, two bay leaves, two cloves, salt, black pepper, and one or two bottles of white wine, ensuring they are well covered
3. Let them cook over low heat for a little more than a quarter hour
4. Drain them and arrange on a plate over Italian sauce
