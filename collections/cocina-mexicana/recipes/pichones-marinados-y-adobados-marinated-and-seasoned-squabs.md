## Ingredients

- squabs
- vinegar
- ground garlic
- salt
- bay leaf
- oregano
- water
- flour
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- garlic
- wide chile, deveined and soaked
- cloves
- pepper
- toasted bread
- small chiles
- olives
- onion, thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Clean and divide the squabs in half or into quarters
2. Marinate the squabs in vinegar with ground garlic, salt, bay leaf and oregano overnight
3. The next day, cook them in the same marinade with a little water added
4. Remove the cooked squabs and coat each piece in flour
5. Fry in lard until done, then remove from the fat
6. In the same lard, fry roasted tomatoes, garlic, deveined soaked wide chile, cloves, pepper and toasted bread, all ground together
7. Add the squabs and their marinade, removing the bay leaf
8. Let simmer until the sauce is well seasoned
9. Serve garnished with small chiles, olives and very thin onion slices
