## Ingredients

- Three or four glasses of good broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- One tablespoon of mustard

## Directions

1. Reduce three or four glasses of good broth with salt, pepper and garlic to half its volume
2. Add one tablespoon of mustard
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Serve very hot
