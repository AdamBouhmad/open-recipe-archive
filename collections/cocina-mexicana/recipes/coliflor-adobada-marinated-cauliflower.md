## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. After washing the cauliflower well, boil it for a quarter hour
2. Remove and drain well
3. Place in hot eating oil
4. When cooled, extract from oil and place in vinegar loaded with salt, with a handful of chopped parsley
5. Keep for some days
6. After this time it can serve for all the stews mentioned, especially for stuffing with a compound mixture, or with hazelnuts, or walnuts, or ground almonds mixed with breadcrumbs, salt and pepper, and fried in oil
