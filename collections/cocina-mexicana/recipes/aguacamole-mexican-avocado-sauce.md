## Ingredients

- ripe tomatillos
- green chiles or tornachiles
- good vinegar
- cured and chopped small chiles
- cooked and broken up onions
- avocado
- salt
- abundant oil
- cured tornachiles in strips
- olives
- avocado slices
- oregano powder

## Directions

1. Cook ripe tomatillos and green chiles, or if out of season, roast tornachiles, peel them and cut into strips, then mix with the rest
2. Peel both tomatillos and chiles and grind them
3. Loosen the ground mixture with good vinegar
4. Mix with chopped cured small chiles and broken up cooked onions
5. Place clean avocado in abundant slices, seasoning with salt and much oil
6. Decorate the platter on top with cured tornachiles in strips, olives, other sliced avocado slices, and oregano powder
