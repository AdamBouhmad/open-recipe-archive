## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- eight bitter almonds, very finely ground
- twenty ounces of cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place in a pan one pound of sweet almonds with eight bitter almonds, very finely ground
2. Heat twenty ounces of cream with sugar in another pan
3. Dissolve the ground almonds in the boiling cream
4. Pass everything through a fine strainer, pressing it
5. A quarter hour before serving, place over fire, stirring with a wooden spoon and let it thicken until it sticks to the spoon
6. Empty it then into a silver pan and serve hot
