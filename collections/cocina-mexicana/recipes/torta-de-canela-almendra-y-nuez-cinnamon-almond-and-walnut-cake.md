## Ingredients

- One pound of almonds
- One pound of sugar
- One pound of butter
- Fourteen eggs
- Three ounces of cinnamon
- A little pastille

## Directions

1. Beat everything well, having ground the almond and sugar
2. Make a very fine dough of small rings and with it line the cake pan
3. Put the cake on top and decorate with strips of the dough
4. Put it in the oven to cook, and when the decorations are golden, remove it
