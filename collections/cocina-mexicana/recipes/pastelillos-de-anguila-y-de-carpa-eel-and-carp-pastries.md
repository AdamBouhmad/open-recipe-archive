## Ingredients

- Eel meat
- Carp roe
- Mushrooms
- Small onions
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Lemon or verjuice

## Directions

1. Mince eel meat, carp roe, and mushrooms (all partially cooked), small onions, and fine herbs, and season
2. Lightly fry in butter equal to the amount of the minced mixture
3. Form pastries from puff pastry dough and bake in the oven or in pie pans
4. When cooked, add lemon juice or verjuice in grains
