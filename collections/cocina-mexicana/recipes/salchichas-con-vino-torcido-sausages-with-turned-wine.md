## Ingredients

- pork meat
- ground garlic
- ground pepper
- whole pepper grains
- turned wine
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Chop pork meat very finely
2. Mix with ground garlic according to the quantity of meat, ground pepper, some whole pepper grains, and wine, preferably turned wine
3. Stir all together and leave to infuse until the next day
4. Fill intestines and tie at short intervals, or leave at the desired size for the sausages
