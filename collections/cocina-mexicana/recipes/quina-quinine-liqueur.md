## Ingredients

- superior quinine powder
- refined brandy
- orange essence
- clarified simple syrup

## Directions

1. After grinding superior quinine into fine powder, place it to infuse in refined brandy and separating a little tincture to later give color to the liquor, distill in a bain-marie
2. Mix to the product two drops of orange essence for each quarter quart and after a day of resting, sweeten with clarified simple syrup, more or less concentrated in sugar, according to the desired sweetness and oiliness of the liquor; the common measure is to use one quarter quart of simple syrup for each quarter quart of recovered brandy
3. Then decant into stoppered large bottles and bottle immediately for use
