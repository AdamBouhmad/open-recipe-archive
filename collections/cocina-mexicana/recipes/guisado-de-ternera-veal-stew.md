## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- orange or lemon juice
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- pepper
- ham
- tomato
- carrot
- water
- clove
- cinnamon
- wine
- olives
- chiles
- vinegar
- capers
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets, wash and place in a cazuela with orange or lemon juice and salt for one hour
2. After that time, wash them
3. In another cazuela fry peeled onion and parsley with oil, powdered pepper and ham, and with this lard the meat which is parboiled in lard
4. Add tomato without seeds, the meat and carrot slices, add water, then clove, cinnamon and pepper all together
5. It should remain with little broth
6. When bringing to the table add wine, olives, chiles with vinegar, capers, raisins and almonds
