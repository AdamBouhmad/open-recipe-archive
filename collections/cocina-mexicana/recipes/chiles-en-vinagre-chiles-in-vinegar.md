## Ingredients

- Green chiles without spots or bites
- Bay leaf
- Lemon slices
- Salt
- Water
- Castilian vinegar
- Eating oil
- Onion slices
- Crushed garlic
- Chinese oregano

## Directions

1. Select the greenest chiles without spots or bites.
2. In a barrel, layer bay leaf, chiles, and lemon slices with salt, repeating until the barrel is full.
3. Pour water to the brim and cover tightly.
4. Let rest for fifteen to twenty days.
5. Remove chiles one by one, open with a knife, and remove the veins.
6. Prepare a frying of eating oil, onion slices, crushed garlic, and bay leaf.
7. Place the chiles in a jar with Castilian vinegar, the frying mixture, and a little Chinese oregano.
8. After some time, they are ready to use.
