## Ingredients

- pork knuckles
- butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- white wine
- flour
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the knuckles and place on the roaster with butter
2. While roasting cover with bread crumbs mixed with salt, pepper and fine herbs
3. When done add a glass of white wine, a little flour and bread crumbs
