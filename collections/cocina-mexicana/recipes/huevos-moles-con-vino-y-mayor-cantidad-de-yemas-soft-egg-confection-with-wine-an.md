## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- twelve well-beaten egg yolks
- white wine
- syrup
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- candied fruit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Make the syrup with one pound of sugar
2. The yolks will be twelve and well beaten
3. Moisten the sponge cake with wine and the final layer is adorned with raisins, almonds and pine nuts
