## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Egg yolks
- Butter, melted
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads
2. Parboil them
3. Put them to cook in a semi-gelatin
4. Let them cool and drain
5. Beat egg yolks with melted butter
6. Coat the sweetbreads with this egg mixture twice, covering successively with bread crumbs
7. Roast on a grill
8. Dress on a semi-gelatin
