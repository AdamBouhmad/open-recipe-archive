## Ingredients

- cleaned mameys, the most colorful ones
- syrup of thread point
- ground cinnamon
- raisins
- toasted almonds

## Directions

1. Grind the clean mameys, always choosing the most colorful ones
2. Add them to thread point syrup so that the paste forms
3. Place it in layers sprinkled with ground cinnamon
4. Top with toasted raisins and almonds
