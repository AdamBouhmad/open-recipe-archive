## Ingredients

- 4 quarters of a cup of cream
- 1 piece of cinnamon
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Bring 4 quarters of a cup of cream to boil with a piece of cinnamon and 4 ounces of sugar
2. After it cools, mix in 5 well-beaten egg yolks
3. Return to the fire, stirring until it becomes slightly thick and taking care not to let it boil
4. Remove from heat and when it has cooled again, butter a piece of clean linen
5. Dust the butter with flour and pour the custard into it
6. Tie at the four corners so the custard cannot escape
7. Place in a pot of water and let it boil for three-quarters of an hour
8. Remove the custard from the fire
