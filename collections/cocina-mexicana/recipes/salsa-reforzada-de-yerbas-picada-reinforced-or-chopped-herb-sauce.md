## Ingredients

- Chervil
- Salad burnet
- Chives
- Tarragon, somewhat more than the other herbs
- Curly sauce
- Vinegar
- Whole pepper
- A small piece of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Chop equal quantities of chervil, salad burnet, and chives, and somewhat more tarragon
2. Heat the curly sauce and add vinegar and whole pepper
3. At the moment of serving, add the chopped herbs with a small piece of fresh butter and stir so everything is well mixed
