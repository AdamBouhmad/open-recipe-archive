## Ingredients

- eight camotes of yuca
- eight regular camotes
- two pounds of sugar
- four cuartillos of milk
- one pound of peeled almonds

## Directions

1. Put eight camotes of yuca, eight regular camotes, and two pounds of sugar, all ground, to cook with four cuartillos of milk
2. Cook without reaching a very high point
3. Strain and add one pound of peeled almonds when it is cold
