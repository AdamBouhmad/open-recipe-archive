## Ingredients

- A handful of pine nuts, well washed and cleaned
- A few grains of garlic
- Cumin
- One cooked egg yolk
- White broth or water

## Directions

1. Take a handful of pine nuts well washed and cleaned
2. Grind them together with a few grains of garlic, cumin and one cooked egg yolk
3. Dissolve in white broth or water
4. Bring to a boil and it is ready to serve
