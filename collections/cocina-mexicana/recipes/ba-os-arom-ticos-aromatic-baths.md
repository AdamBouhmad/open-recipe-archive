## Ingredients

- bay leaf
- thyme
- rosemary
- lavender
- marjoram
- oregano
- sage
- basil
- wallflower
- mint
- brandy

## Directions

1. Boil the herbs
2. Pass the decoction through a cloth
3. Add a few drops of brandy
