## Ingredients

- Onion
- Lard
- Parsley
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Chicken broth
- Ham pieces

## Directions

1. Mince onion and fry in lard
2. Remove and fry parsley
3. When fried, grind with cloves, cinnamon, pepper and a piece of fried bread
4. Add the broth in which the chicken was cooked and pieces of ham
