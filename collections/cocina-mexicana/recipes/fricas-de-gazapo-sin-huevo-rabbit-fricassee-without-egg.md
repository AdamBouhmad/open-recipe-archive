## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Spices
- Two carrots
- Four onions
- Two turnips
- Three stalks of celery
- One mixed bouquet

## Directions

1. Divide the rabbit into pieces
2. Cook in broth that covers them completely, seasoning with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and spices
3. Add two carrots, four onions, two turnips, three stalks of celery, and one mixed bouquet
4. When the vegetables are cooked, remove them and reduce to substance
5. If the rabbit is already cooked, strain the broth through a sieve; otherwise let the meat cook more
6. After straining the broth, make a somewhat thick sauce with it and the vegetable substance
7. Pour over the rabbit to serve
