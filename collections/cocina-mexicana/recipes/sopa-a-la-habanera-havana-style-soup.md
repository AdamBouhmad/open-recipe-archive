## Ingredients

- bread
- garlic
- parsley
- grated cheese
- almonds or hazelnuts, toasted
- broth from the pot
- cloves or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare the bread by cutting it into long, thin strips
2. Toast the strips
3. Place them in a cooking pot in layers, mixing minced garlic, parsley, and grated cheese between the layers
4. Crush some toasted almonds or hazelnuts and add pot broth
5. Bring to a boil and add cloves or cinnamon
6. When cooked, place half the mixture in the soup and leave it simmering
7. After a short time, remove from heat and add the other half of the sauce
8. Place hot coals on the lid so it toasts on top
