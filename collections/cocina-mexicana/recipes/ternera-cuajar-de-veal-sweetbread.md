## Ingredients

- veal sweetbread
- onions
- carrots
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- salt
- peppercorns
- water
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the sweetbread well, then cook with onions, carrots, parsley, thyme, bay leaf, clove, salt, peppercorns and sufficient water.
2. Once cooked, let drain and cut into pieces four fingers long.
3. Cover immediately with butter kneaded with parsley, onion, a little finely chopped garlic, salt and pepper.
4. Serve with a spicy sauce.
