## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- bread
- oil or fat for frying
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and roast the ribs according to previous instructions
2. Place them on a platter with fried bread toasts in each interval
3. Drizzle with tomato sauce that has been passed through a sieve
