## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 16 egg yolks, cleaned
- syrup
- 1/2 cup grated coconut
- almonds
- pine nuts
- cinnamon
- dragée
- candied fruit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Make a syrup at medium point with two pounds of sugar
2. Strain through a cloth and let cool
3. Remove sixteen egg yolks from eggs, cleaning them well of the white
4. Place in a jar and remove all membranes with a fork
5. Add a little cold syrup to the jar and beat the yolks with a mill until very dissolved
6. Pour into the cooled syrup in the pot
7. Heat without ceasing to stir so they do not become greasy, and let boil until the syrup reaches the point of thickening
8. Add half a cup of grated coconut
9. Give a few boils and when ready, remove and let cool
10. Place over layers of sponge cake, and the final layer of syrup with egg is adorned with almonds, pine nuts, a little cinnamon and dragée
