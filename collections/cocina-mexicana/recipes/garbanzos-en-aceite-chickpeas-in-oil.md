## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- ash
- dried wide chiles
- garlic
- oil

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas with a pinch of ash; the next day remove that water and put to cook in clear water
2. When well cooked put to cool and when well cooled shell them
3. The next day put a pot with half a quarter liter of oil
4. Soak three dried wide chiles, grind with a clove of garlic and add to the oil to fry in the same oil
5. When well seasoned add the chickpeas to boil once more and set aside to serve
