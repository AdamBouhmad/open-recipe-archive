## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of good milk
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- cilantro
- breadcrumbs
- a quarter of lard, cut into small pieces
- a dozen finely ground sweet almonds
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- five raw egg yolks
- ham strips
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Boil two cuartillos of good milk with thyme, bay leaf, basil, and cilantro until reduced to half
2. Pass the remaining milk through a sieve
3. Add a little breadcrumbs and put over fire until the bread has absorbed the milk
4. Remove from heat and grind in a quarter of lard cut into small pieces, a dozen very finely ground sweet almonds, salt, grated nutmeg, and five raw egg yolks
5. Pour this mixture into the body of the hen (passed through flame, emptied and perfectly cleaned)
6. Sew it closed so nothing escapes
7. Cook it wrapped in strips of ham, moistened with milk and parboiled with salt and a little cilantro
8. When cooked and well drained of its fat, serve with salsa a la reina
