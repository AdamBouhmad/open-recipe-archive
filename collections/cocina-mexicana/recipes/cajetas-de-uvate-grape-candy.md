## Ingredients

- Grapes
- Water
- Syrup at medium point

## Directions

1. Boil the stemmed grapes in a little water
2. Remove and pit them
3. Add them together with the water in which they were boiled to the syrup
4. When everything is at medium point, set aside and store for the next day
5. The next day, return it to the fire to bring it to jelly point and pour it into the molds
