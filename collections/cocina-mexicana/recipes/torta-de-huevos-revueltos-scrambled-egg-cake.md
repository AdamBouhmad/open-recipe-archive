## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Green chile
- Lard
- Fried toasts
- Ham pieces
- Longaniza pieces
- Chorizos
- Cooked egg
- Chopped parsley
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Pickled peppers
- Beaten eggs
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Scramble and cook the eggs, adding to them a little chopped green chile
2. In a cazuela lined with lard, put a few beaten eggs, place on top a layer of fried toast and another of the scrambled eggs
3. Add, if not already present, small pieces of ham and longaniza, chorizos and cooked egg, chopped parsley, raisins, almonds, capers, pickled peppers and olives
4. Place another layer of fried bread and another of scrambled eggs with their decorations, alternating in this way until the cazuela is full, putting between them a little beaten egg and a pinch of pepper
5. Cover everything with beaten egg and let the cake curdle at two gentle fires, serving it with powdered sugar on top
