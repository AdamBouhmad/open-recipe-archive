## Ingredients

- Hare
- oil
- chorizo
- ham
- garlic
- tomatoes
- clove
- bread
- salt
- oregano
- small onions
- olives
- pickled chile peppers

## Directions

1. Fry slices of chorizo and slices of ham in oil, removing them when fried
2. In the same oil, fry garlic cloves that, when golden, are ground with cooked tomatoes, clove and toasted bread, putting everything to fry again
3. Add salt, oregano, cooked small onions and the water in which they were cooked, the cooked hare, and the fried chorizo and ham
4. Let everything boil together until the sauce is of good consistency, and when removing from heat add olives and pieces of pickled chiles
