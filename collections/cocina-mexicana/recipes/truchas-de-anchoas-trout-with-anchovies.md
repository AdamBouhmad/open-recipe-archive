## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- fine oil
- bread crumbs
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Scale and gut the trout and make an incision on the side
2. Let marinate with salt, pepper, garlic, parsley, chopped onions and mushrooms, thyme, bay leaf and basil powder, and fine oil
3. Place in a pan with its marinade
4. Cover with breadcrumbs and bake in the oven
5. Serve with anchovy sauce
