## Ingredients

- Oil
- Lard
- Parsley
- Onions
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Bread
- Poblano chile
- Desalted and cooked cod
- Oil
- Toast bread

## Directions

1. Fry in oil and lard parsley, onions and garlic, all chopped very fine.
2. Add a ground tomato with cold bread and poblano chile, after the tomato and bread have been fried and browned in lard.
3. Then add the desalted and cooked cod, adding the oil necessary so the dish becomes rich with it.
4. Once it has given a few boils, remove and let rest, decorating the platter when serving with toasted bread pieces arranged around it.
