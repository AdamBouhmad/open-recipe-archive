## Ingredients

- Hot lard
- Ground garlic
- Ground capers
- Clove
- Pepper
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Chicken broth
- Strips of cooked ham
- Oil
- Olives
- Chiles or cured tornachiles

## Directions

1. Heat lard and fry ground garlic in it
2. Fry ground capers in the same lard with clove and pepper
3. Season everything with salt and add vinegar
4. Put in the chicken with its broth and strips of cooked ham
5. Let everything simmer well, ensuring the broth is not too thin
6. When removing from the heat, add oil and garnish with olives and chiles or cured tornachiles
