## Ingredients

- tongue
- herbs
- garlic
- onion
- vinegar
- cloves
- cinnamon
- whole peppercorns
- lard
- oil
- flour
- roasted tomatoes
- powdered pepper
- broth
- cooked peas
- red wine

## Directions

1. Wash very well and put to cook with herbs, mashed garlic, two onion wedges, vinegar, cloves, ground cinnamon and whole peppercorns
2. Slice cooked onion and skin the tongue
3. Fry onion wedges in lard and oil
4. Once fried, add flour and let it take color
5. Grind roasted tomatoes and fry them in the same place
6. Add powdered pepper and let fry well
7. Once well fried, add broth from that in which the tongue cooked and mix with the seasoning
8. Add cooked peas and let the broth dry
9. Before serving, add red wine
