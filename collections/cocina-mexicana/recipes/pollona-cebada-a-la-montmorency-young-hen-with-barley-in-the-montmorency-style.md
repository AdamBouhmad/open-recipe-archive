## Ingredients

- one young hen
- the hen's liver, cut into dice
- pork fat
- egg

## Directions

1. Pass the hen through flame and empty it
2. Lard it on top and stuff it with liver cut into dice, pork fat, and egg
3. Sew it closed and cook by fricassee, letting it set the same way
