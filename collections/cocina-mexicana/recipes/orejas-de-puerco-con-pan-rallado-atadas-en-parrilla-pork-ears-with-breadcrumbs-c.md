## Ingredients

- pork ears
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- salt and pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the ears over hot coals after passing them through the flame
2. Wash, clean, and scald them
3. When tender and cooled, coat in breadcrumbs
4. Dip in beaten eggs
5. Pass again through breadcrumbs
6. Cook on grills
7. Serve dry or with clear juice
