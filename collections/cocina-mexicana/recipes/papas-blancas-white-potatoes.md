## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked and cut
- parsley, chopped
- onions, chopped
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook and cut the potatoes as described in previous articles.
2. Place them in a pot with chopped parsley and onions.
3. Let them macerate and moisten with milk, taking care to turn them well.
4. Serve before they come to a boil.
