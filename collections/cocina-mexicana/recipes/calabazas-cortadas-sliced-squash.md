## Ingredients

- squash, not too firm
- pepper
- salt
- tomatoes
- raw oil
- breadcrumbs
- mint
- onion

## Directions

1. Choose squash that is not too firm and cut into thin slices.
2. Place at the bottom of a casserole dish and sprinkle with pepper, salt, and chopped tomatoes that have had their skin removed by placing them on the coals.
3. Arrange additional slices the same way, drizzle with raw oil and place over two fires.
4. It is good to add breadcrumbs on top, a little mint, and finely chopped onion.
