## Ingredients

- good desalted and cleaned salt cod
- pepper
- nutmeg
- onions in slices
- parsley sprigs
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- tarragon
- flour
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut finger-length strips or slices from the salt cod without removing the skin
2. When all are ready, place in a marinade in a casserole with pepper, nutmeg, sliced onions, parsley sprigs, oil, vinegar, bay leaf, and tarragon, stirring from time to time
3. At serving time, dry the salt cod
4. Pass through flour, then through beaten egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry well in good oil
6. Place on a plate after draining and garnish with fried parsley
