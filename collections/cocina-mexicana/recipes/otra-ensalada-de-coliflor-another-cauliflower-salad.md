## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- garlic, two large heads
- butter
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- pepper powder
- chiles
- olives
- capers
- caperberries

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with water and salt
2. Peel two large heads of garlic
3. Sauté them in butter and oil
4. Without removing the garlic, add half a bottle of vinegar
5. Add some bay leaves, a little thyme and pepper powder
6. On top add the cauliflower and keep stirring so it incorporates and does not burn
7. Remove from heat, add more Castilian vinegar and oil
8. Place on a platter and decorate with chiles, olives, capers and caperberries
