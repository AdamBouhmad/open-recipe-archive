## Ingredients

- Cheese
- Bread crumbs
- Sugar
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Eggs
- Lard
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind cheese and mix with bread crumbs
2. Sweeten and add almonds and pine nuts
3. Beat with eggs
4. Pour into a pot greased with lard
5. Cook over two fires
6. When set, sprinkle with ground sugar and cinnamon on top
