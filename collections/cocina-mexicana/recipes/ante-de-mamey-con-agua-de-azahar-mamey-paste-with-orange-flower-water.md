## Ingredients

- the most colorful mameys
- clarified syrup
- one ounce of cinnamon, ground
- one quarter ounce of clove, ground
- orange flower water
- dried mamey
- raisins, pitted

## Directions

1. Select the most colorful mameys and remove the skins, pits and all fibrous parts near the heart and pits
2. Grind them and add them to clarified syrup to make a paste
3. Add one ounce of ground cinnamon and one quarter ounce of ground clove
4. Add a little orange flower water
5. Let it thicken over heat
6. Layer it in alternating layers with dried mamey, decorating the last layer which will be of paste with pitted raisins
