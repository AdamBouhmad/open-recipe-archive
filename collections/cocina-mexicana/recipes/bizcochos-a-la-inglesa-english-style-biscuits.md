## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh butter
- one pound of flour
- fourteen egg whites
- egg yolks
- one pound of finely ground sugar
- one quarter of a cuartillo of refined brandy

## Directions

1. Melt one pound of fresh butter and mix with one pound of flour
2. Beat fourteen egg whites well and mix in the yolks beaten separately
3. Add one pound of finely ground sugar
4. Mix the flour mixture with the eggs
5. Add one quarter of a cuartillo of refined brandy and stir very well
6. Pour into molds greased with butter
7. Bake in an oven at the proper temperature for biscuits
