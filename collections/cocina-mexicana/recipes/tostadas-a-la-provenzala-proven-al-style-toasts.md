## Ingredients

- baguette slices
- good quality oil
- anchovies, desalted
- ground pepper
- fine oil
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Fry baguette slices in good quality oil
2. Arrange on a plate
3. Split them open
4. Place one desalted anchovy on each
5. Sprinkle ground pepper on top
6. Drizzle with fine oil
7. Serve as an intermediate course with a little orange juice
