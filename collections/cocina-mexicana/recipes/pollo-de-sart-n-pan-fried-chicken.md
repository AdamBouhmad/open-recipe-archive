## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- salt
- black pepper
- lemon juice
- ham slices
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the chicken over flame; empty it through the crop and remove the breastbone.
2. Knead a piece of butter with salt, black pepper, and lemon juice and fill the chicken with this mixture.
3. Cut the neck and tie it by passing thread with a needle from leg to leg, filling the crop with skin so that the butter does not escape.
4. Place the chicken in a casserole between slices of ham, and pour over it a panful of essence (see essence of pan), one hour being sufficient for its complete cooking.
5. To serve, drain it, untie it and put tomato sauce over it.
