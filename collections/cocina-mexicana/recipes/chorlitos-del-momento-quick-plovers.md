## Ingredients

- Plovers
- A good piece of butter
- Shallots, chopped
- Nutmeg, grated
- Salt
- Pepper
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Half a cuartillo of white wine
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Gather the thighs of the plovers, singe them at the flame and clean the birds
2. Place them in a pan over high heat with a good piece of butter, chopped shallots, a little grated nutmeg, salt, and pepper
3. Fry them for seven or eight minutes, shaking the pan
4. Add to the frying the juice of two lemons, half a cuartillo of white wine, and a little bread crumbs
5. Let the sauce come to a boil, remove the plovers and serve
