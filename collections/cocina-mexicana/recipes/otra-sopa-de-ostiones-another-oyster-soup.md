## Ingredients

- French oil and butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- very finely chopped onion
- broth without chile or cabbage
- milk
- oysters
- pepper
- parsley
- egg yolks
- bread

## Directions

1. Fry three tablespoons of flour in French oil and butter, and once it is browned, fry a little very finely chopped onion and add the broth, which should have neither chile nor cabbage
2. Once the flour is well mixed, add the milk and the oysters with all their broth, and let it come to a boil
3. Before serving, mix in a little pepper, parsley, and two egg yolks beaten with milk
4. Toast the bread in slices or small square pieces fried in butter or butter
