## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- half pound sugar
- half pound butter
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Grind the rice and sugar together
2. Melt the butter
3. In a bowl mix the rice, sugar, melted butter, egg yolks and one unbeaten egg white
4. Beat well
5. Once at the proper consistency, fold in seven beaten egg whites, beaten as for nougat (turrón)
6. Beat a little more
7. Grease a mold with butter
8. Pour all into it and place over two fires or bake in the oven
