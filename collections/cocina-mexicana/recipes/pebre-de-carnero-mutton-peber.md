## Ingredients

- Raw mutton
- Onion
- Garlic
- Raw tomato
- Water
- Spices
- Pepper or Tabasco
- Clove
- Oregano
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cut the raw mutton into pieces and fry it until well browned.
2. Chop onion and garlic, and brown them in the same fat.
3. Add chopped raw tomato to what is frying.
4. Once everything is well fried, add water so the meat can cook, but it should not become watery.
5. Add spices, pepper or Tabasco, clove, oregano and oil.
6. Serve.
