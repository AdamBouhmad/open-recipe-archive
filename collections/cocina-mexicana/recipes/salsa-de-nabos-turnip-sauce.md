## Ingredients

- Garlic cloves
- Butter
- Crumbled bread
- Chopped onion
- Pieces of cooked turnip
- Broth from the pot
- Clove
- Pepper
- Pieces of fat ham

## Directions

1. Roast some garlic cloves and clean all the teeth well
2. Pound the garlic and fry in butter
3. Add crumbled bread, chopped onion, and pieces of cooked turnip
4. Add broth from the pot seasoned with clove and pepper
5. When thickened, add small pieces of fat ham that have been browned in butter
