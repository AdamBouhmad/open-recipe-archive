## Ingredients

- half-point syrup
- ground camote (sweet potato)
- ground almonds
- egg yolks
- peeled cinnamon

## Directions

1. In a little half-point syrup, add well-ground camote and put it on the fire until it returns to the half-point stage.
2. Then add ground almonds, egg yolks and peeled cinnamon, and let it reach the point of cajeta.
3. Place it over mashed mamón (a fruit), sprinkled with wine and a little flavoring.
