## Ingredients

- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- A handful of chopped parsley
- Some onions
- A few drops of vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Knead a piece of butter with salt, pepper, a handful of chopped parsley, some onions, and a few drops of vinegar or lemon juice.
2. Once everything is prepared, add it to vegetables cooked in water or to fish roasted on grills or simply cooked.
3. It is necessary that these dishes are very hot so that the butter melts, otherwise it is necessary to put them in the fire again.
