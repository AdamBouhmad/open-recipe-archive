## Ingredients

- onions
- water
- a sprig of mint
- salt
- plenty of sugar
- cold bread
- cinnamon
- clove
- saffron
- red wine
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Boil onions with sufficient water according to the amount of breadcrumbs desired
2. Boil a sprig of mint with the onions
3. When both have boiled well, remove them and add the corresponding salt to the water
4. Add plenty of sugar, cold bread soaked and rehydrated, cinnamon, clove, and saffron, all toasted
5. Remove from heat and add a little red wine, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and walnuts
