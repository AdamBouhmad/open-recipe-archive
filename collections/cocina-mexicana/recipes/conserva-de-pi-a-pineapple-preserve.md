## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Peel it so that nothing black remains, slice it and cook until tender
2. Add to cold water while the syrup is being made, clarified to low point
3. Once made, place the pineapple there so that little by little it absorbs the syrup until it reaches the proper point
