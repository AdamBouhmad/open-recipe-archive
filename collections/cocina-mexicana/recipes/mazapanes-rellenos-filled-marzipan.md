## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted sugar
- one pound of shelled and coarsely ground almonds
- orange blossom water
- sugar or cinnamon for dusting
- gragea

## Directions

1. Make a paste with one pound of sifted sugar, one pound of shelled and coarsely ground almonds, and a little orange blossom water
2. Form small hollow pieces with it, like small plates, cups, cubes, etc., that can hold the filling
3. Make them of regular thickness so they do not break or fall apart when moistened with what will be put inside
4. Let them air dry
5. Fill them with any of the pastes that imitate fruits, being very good for this the almond paste, adding to it egg yolks beaten with wine, or mixed with custard and cinnamon
6. Once the marzipan pieces are filled with the aforementioned pastes, dust them with sifted sugar or cinnamon, or with gragea, according to the filling paste
7. Put them in a not very hot oven for a short time
