## Ingredients

- medium potatoes, peeled
- fat or lean broth
- water
- good oil
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- parsley, mixed bunch
- raw oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel raw medium potatoes and put them to cook in fat or lean broth and water with a little good oil and some salt and pepper.
2. Add some carrots, turnips, onions and a small bunch of mixed parsley.
3. When they are cooked and the broth has been consumed, let them fry a moment in the oil.
4. As soon as they take on a beautiful color, serve them immediately with raw oil, vinegar, salt and pepper.
