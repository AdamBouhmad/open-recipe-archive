## Ingredients

- good butter
- two onions, sliced
- one young hen, passed through flame, emptied and trussed
- two more onions, sliced
- two carrots or turnips, cut into strips
- a small bunch of assorted fine herbs
- salt
- half a cuartillo of white wine

## Directions

1. Put a little good butter in the bottom of a pot
2. Add two sliced onions and the young hen (passed through flame, emptied and trussed, belly-side down)
3. Cover with two more sliced onions, two carrots or turnips cut into strips, a small bunch of assorted fine herbs, and a little salt
4. Cook over hot coals
5. When half-cooked, add half a cuartillo of white wine
6. When cooking is complete, degrease the sauce, pass through a sieve, mix with a little strained broth or meat stock, and serve over the hen
