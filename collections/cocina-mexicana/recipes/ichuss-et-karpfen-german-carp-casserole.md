## Ingredients

- carps, scaled, cleaned and cut into pieces
- salt
- four ounces fresh butter
- four chopped anchovies
- half an ounce fine powdered sugar
- one ounce capers
- peppercorns
- clove
- mace
- lemon slices
- one quarter of red wine
- one quarter of vinegar

## Directions

1. Scale, clean and divide the carps into pieces
2. Dust with salt and let sit for one or two hours to acquire consistency
3. Put four ounces of fresh butter at the bottom of a casserole
4. Add four chopped anchovies and place the carp pieces on top
5. Add half an ounce of fine powdered sugar, one ounce of capers, peppercorns, clove and mace
6. Cover everything with lemon slices
7. Pour one quarter wine and one quarter vinegar on top
8. Cover the casserole hermetically, sealing the joints with paste or rice flour paste or corn dough
9. Let cook over low heat
10. Serve as an entry in the same casserole
