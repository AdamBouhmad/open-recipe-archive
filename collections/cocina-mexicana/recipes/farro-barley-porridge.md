## Ingredients

- Barley, crushed or half-ground
- Sweet almonds, peeled and ground
- Meat broth or lean broth
- Butter
- Bread toasted in butter

## Directions

1. After crushing or half-grinding the barley grains, remove the husks
2. Wash three times in warm water
3. Cook in meat broth or lean broth as desired
4. When half-cooked, add some sweet almonds, peeled and ground, to whiten the farro
5. Sweeten it and allow it to boil once or twice more
6. Alternatively, the farro can be made with almond milk, which is clean almonds ground and dissolved in water
7. Place the farro over toasted bread fried in butter and serve
