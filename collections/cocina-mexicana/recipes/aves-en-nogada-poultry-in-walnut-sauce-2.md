## Ingredients

- Equal parts walnuts and almonds
- Bread soaked in vinegar
- Raw garlic cloves
- Oil
- Salt

## Directions

1. Grind equal quantities of walnuts and almonds together with a piece of bread soaked in vinegar and raw garlic cloves
2. When everything is well ground, fry in oil with plenty of salt
3. Pour over the poultry at the time of serving
4. Top with chopped parsley, chiles, olives and other garnishes
