## Ingredients

- Tender green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Oil
- Eggs, raw and half-beaten

## Directions

1. Since these beans are served dry, cook them with little salt so they are not too salty after the broth is consumed.
2. Choose very tender beans and add oil when cooking them.
3. When they are cooked and the broth is almost consumed so that only the oil remains, add raw eggs that are half-beaten.
4. As soon as they are bound, serve them.
