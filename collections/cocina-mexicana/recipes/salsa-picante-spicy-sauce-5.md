## Ingredients

- half of a quarter liter of vinegar
- chopped shallot
- a pinch of fine pepper
- one bay leaf
- a little thyme
- a little broth

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar, chopped shallot, fine pepper, bay leaf, and thyme in a saucepan
2. Reduce to half
3. Add a little broth and let it thicken until it becomes a clear paste
