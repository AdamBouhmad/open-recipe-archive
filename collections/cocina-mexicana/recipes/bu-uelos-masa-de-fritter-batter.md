## Ingredients

- little more than half a caldero cup of flour
- two raw egg yolks
- one scant tablespoon of oil
- salt to taste
- a little warm water (if needed)

## Directions

1. Mix everything well
2. If it becomes too thick, add a little warm water
3. Beat everything very well
4. When a little of the batter is taken with a spoon and poured out, it should form a thread that does not break, then it will be ready
5. Cover fruits with it and fry in lard, basting them with it and keeping them there until they brown
