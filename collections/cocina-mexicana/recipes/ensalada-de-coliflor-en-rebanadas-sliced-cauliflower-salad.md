## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Garlic cloves, crushed
- Parsley
- Raisins (optional)
- Cooked zucchini (optional)
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Olives
- Small chiles
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Remove the flower before cooking and leave the stem clean, remove the skin and cut into slices.
2. Put these slices to cook with two or three crushed garlic cloves.
3. When well cooked, wash until they have no bad smell, then fry with chopped garlic and parsley.
4. If desired, raisins and zucchini cooked separately may be added.
5. Always dress with vinegar and oil seasoned with salt, olives, small chiles, and oregano.
