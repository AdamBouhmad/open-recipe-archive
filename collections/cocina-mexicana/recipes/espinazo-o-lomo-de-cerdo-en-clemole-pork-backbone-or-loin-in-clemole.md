## Ingredients

- pasilla chiles
- garlic
- bread, fried
- toasted peanuts
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- lard
- pork loin or backbone, in regular portions
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Devein and toast pasilla chiles and grind them with garlic and fried bread, toasted peanuts, clove, pepper and cumin
2. Fry everything together in a pot with lard
3. Add sufficient water so the loin or backbone can cook, adding it in regular portions with necessary salt
4. Let the clemole boil until the meat is cooked and the sauce is appropriately thick
