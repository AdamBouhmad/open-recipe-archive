## Ingredients

- Dark beans
- butter
- kitchen seasonings
- cloves
- cinnamon
- chiles
- olives
- chorizo
- longaniza
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the dark beans without tequesquite.
2. After cooked, place in a pan.
3. Take a pan or skillet and fry with plenty of butter and all kitchen seasonings.
4. Grind cloves and cinnamon together with chiles, olives, chorizo, longaniza, and a little vinegar, and mix with the beans.
