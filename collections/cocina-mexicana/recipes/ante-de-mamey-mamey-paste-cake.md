## Ingredients

- mamey fruit
- syrup
- white wine
- aromatics
- raisins
- almonds
- sesame seeds
- sponge cake (mamón)

## Directions

1. Grind the mamey
2. Add to syrup and boil six times
3. Add white wine, aromatics, raisins, almonds and sesame seeds
4. Place over sponge cake
