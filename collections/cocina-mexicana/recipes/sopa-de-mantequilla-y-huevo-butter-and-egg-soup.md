## Ingredients

- sugar, one pound
- egg yolks, six or eight, well cooked with spices
- marquesote, sliced
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar in a small pot
2. Add six or eight well-cooked egg yolks with spices
3. When it reaches the right consistency, add sliced marquesote with raisins and almonds
