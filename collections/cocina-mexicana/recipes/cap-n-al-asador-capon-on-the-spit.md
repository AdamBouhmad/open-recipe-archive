## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- ham slices
- watercress
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Arrange the capon as in the previous article until it is covered with slices of ham, put it on the spit and baste it with its own juices.
2. When cooked, plate it and serve with watercress seasoned with salt and vinegar.
