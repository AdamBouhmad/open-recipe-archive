## Ingredients

- half a pound of crushed sugar
- three and a half quartillos of aged red wine
- a splash of hipocrás essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the wine
2. Pass the wine five or six times through a cloth or felt filter
3. Once clarified, add a splash of hipocrás essence
4. Stir well with a spoon
5. Taste to check if it is strong enough, and if not, add more essence
6. Bottle immediately and seal so it does not spoil
