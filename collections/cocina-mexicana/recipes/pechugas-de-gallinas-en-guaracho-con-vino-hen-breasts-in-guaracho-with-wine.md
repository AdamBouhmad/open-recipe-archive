## Ingredients

- Casserole
- Lard
- Tomatillo
- Garlic
- Onion
- Pineapple slices
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Sweet potato slices
- Pear slices
- Apple slices
- Capers
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Ground saffron
- Hen breasts
- Wine

## Directions

1. Coat a casserole with butter and lard and make a layer of chopped tomatillo, garlic and onion
2. Add pineapple slices, ground clove, cinnamon and pepper, ham slices, chorizos, sweet potato, pear and apple slices, capers, raisins, almonds and ground saffron
3. Place the hen breasts on top and cover them with the same mixture that was put below
4. Pour wine until everything in the casserole is covered
5. Cook over two fires until the breasts are well cooked and the broth seasoned, then remove to serve immediately
