## Ingredients

- String beans, cleaned
- A piece of butter
- One or two heads of garlic, cut into small dice
- Spanish sauce or flour
- Broth with substance
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Place the string beans in a saucepan with a piece of butter and one or two heads of garlic cut into small dice.
2. Place the saucepan in the oven.
3. When the garlic begins to brown, moisten with Spanish sauce, or if you do not have it, add a handful of flour, letting it fry until both the flour and garlic are well browned.
4. Add a ladleful of substantial broth, seasoned with salt and coarse pepper.
5. Cook this sauce over gentle heat, mixing it with the string beans, which should already be blanched and cooked.
6. Once they are well flavored, arrange them on a plate and serve.
