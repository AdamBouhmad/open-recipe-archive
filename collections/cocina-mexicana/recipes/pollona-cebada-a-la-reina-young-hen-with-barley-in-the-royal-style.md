## Ingredients

- one young hen
- ham strips
- small wooden skewers
- clarified rizada sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the young hen in a pan and let it cool
2. Remove the breasts and make a cooked hash with them
3. Use this hash to stuff the hen, giving it its original shape
4. Cover completely with strips of ham held in place with small wooden skewers
5. Put in a baking dish in the oven or place over gentle heat with a hot campaign oven above for about an hour
6. When serving, remove the ham strips covering the hen
7. Dress it on the plate with a little clarified and well-seasoned rizada sauce
