## Ingredients

- twenty ounces of sugar
- one pound of ground guava without skin
- four ounces of cleaned and ground calabash seed

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of gathering in water with twenty ounces of sugar
2. Off the fire, mix with one pound of ground guava without skin and four ounces of cleaned and ground calabash seed
3. Place the mixture on the fire and bring it to a higher point than that of cajeta
4. Set aside and beat a little before pouring
