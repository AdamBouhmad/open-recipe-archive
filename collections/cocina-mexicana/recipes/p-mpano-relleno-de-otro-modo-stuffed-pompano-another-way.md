## Ingredients

- Pámpano
- Xitomate (fresh tomato), raw with seeds removed
- Onion, minced
- Garlic
- Potatoes
- Almonds, chopped
- Parsley
- Capers
- Lard
- Vinegar, diluted
- Sugar
- Oil
- Lemon

## Directions

1. After desalting the pámpano, chop raw xitomate with seeds removed
2. Chop fine onion and garlic, add chopped potatoes and almonds, parsley and capers
3. Fry everything well in lard, add a little diluted vinegar and a piece of sugar
4. Once seasoned forming a thick mass, fill the fish
5. Place paper on the bottom of a casserole, add oil, coat the fish also with oil
6. Cook at two fires until done
7. Removing the oil in which it cooked, coat with lemon and put back with the paper until it browns on both sides
8. Then stew in some broth or serve with one of the sauces mentioned in the previous article
