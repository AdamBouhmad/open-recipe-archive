## Ingredients

- sixteen pounds of flour
- three pounds of butter
- thirty egg yolks
- fifteen egg whites
- three pounds of sugar
- one cup of orange blossom water
- a little yeast
- water as needed by the flour

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough with the flour, butter, egg yolks and whites, sugar, orange blossom water, yeast, and water as needed
2. Place in paper boxes prepared in advance
3. Bake in the oven
