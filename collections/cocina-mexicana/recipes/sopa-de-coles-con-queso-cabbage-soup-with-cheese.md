## Ingredients

- One head of cabbage
- Water
- Lard or ham slices
- One onion
- Some carrots
- Beef broth
- Butter
- Bread slices
- Good grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage into pieces and parboil for half an hour in boiling water
2. Plunge into cold water and let drain until no water remains
3. Put in a pot lined at the bottom and sides with slices of ham or lard, adding one onion and some carrots
4. Place over gentle heat and moisten from time to time with broth
5. When the cabbage is cooked, put a piece of butter in another pot and place on top a layer of bread slices powdered with good grated cheese
6. Alternately place other layers of bread and cabbage, making sure to powder them all with cheese
7. Moisten everything with broth and place the pot over gentle heat so the soup can sizzle without boiling for about half an hour
8. Empty into the soup tureen and add the necessary broth, as it should be soupy
