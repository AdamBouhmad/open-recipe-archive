## Ingredients

- beef brains
- water
- lard or ham slices
- bay leaves
- onion slices
- carrot slices
- parsley
- green onions
- wine
- onions
- butter
- flour
- mushrooms
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the brains the same way as in the previous recipe, being able to replace white wine with red wine if desired.
2. After cooking, pass the broth through a sieve.
3. Clean some onions and sauté them in hot butter until they brown.
4. Sprinkle them with flour, moisten with wine of the same type used to cook the brains, add some mushrooms and cook everything together.
5. Once done, arrange the brains on a plate after draining them, taste the stew to check if it is well salted, and pour it over the brains.
