## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sugar
- 1/2 pound almonds soaked in cold water and ground
- 1/2 pound grated coconut
- 1/2 pound cooked and sifted camote
- 2 cups custard (natillas)
- 8 egg yolks
- butter
- sponge cake, ground
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with three pounds of sugar, that is clarified and strained
2. Add to it off the fire half a pound of almonds soaked in cold water and ground, half a pound of grated coconut, and another half of cooked and sifted camote
3. Put over the fire, and when it is about to take the point, add two cups of custard and eight egg yolks
4. Make everything incorporate and bring it to the point of cajeta, or of pulling away from the pan which is the same, and empty it into a platter buttered with butter, browning it with a griddle with gentle fire, or making it form a crust with ground sponge cake, sugar and cinnamon
