## Ingredients

- four pounds of ground sugar
- two pounds of corn flour
- two pounds of wheat flour
- two pounds of lemon or orange flower water

## Directions

1. Mix and grind until it reaches the consistency of dough
2. Extend with a rolling pin until thin
3. Cut into pieces according to the desired shape
4. Place in a baking pan
5. Bake at two low heats
6. Once cooked, dust with sugar
