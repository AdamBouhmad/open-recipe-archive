## Ingredients

- Milk
- One pound of sugar for each cuartillo
- Castilian squash, cooked and ground without skin or seeds, in proportion of one pound per cuartillo of milk

## Directions

1. Sweeten milk with one pound of sugar for each cuartillo
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Add cooked and ground Castilian squash without skin or seeds, in proportion of one pound for each cuartillo of milk
4. Heat so it boils and becomes thick
