## Ingredients

- robalo, washed and desalted
- flour
- oil
- lard
- six bundles of onions
- half bottle of vinegar
- pepper
- thyme
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. After washing and desalting, cut into portions and wrap in flour
2. Fry in oil and lard
3. Slice six bundles of onions
4. Heat oil and lard in another pan and add the onion, stirring vigorously
5. Add half a bottle of vinegar to make broth
6. Add a little pepper and thyme
7. Remove from heat and place the fish in a baking dish
8. Cover with this sauce
9. Decorate with olives, capers and oil
