## Ingredients

- very lean bacon shanks
- pork loins
- saffron
- pepper
- cinnamon
- toasted clove
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- eggs
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- wine
- vinegar
- toasted sesame
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Choose very lean bacon shanks, put them to desalt a little, and cook them with pork loins; as soon as they are well cooked, remove them, let them cool and mince them.
2. Grind saffron, pepper, cinnamon, of each thing the proper amount, and a little toasted clove; incorporate everything with the paste, add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and beat the paste with eggs in proportional quantity.
3. Make some small pastries and fry them with eggs, which will be well beaten, giving them two or three layers; put them in the syrup, which will have cinnamon, pepper, little clove and wine. If desired, a little vinegar, toasted sesame, raisins and almonds can be added.
