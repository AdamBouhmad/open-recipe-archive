## Ingredients

- lettuces
- cheese
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- cold butter
- cooked and ground egg yolks
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Make them in all respects as the previous article, but the filling is formed with grated cheese and bread crumbs, salt, pepper, cold butter, cooked and ground egg yolks and chopped parsley.
