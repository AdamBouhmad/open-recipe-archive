## Ingredients

- butter
- thin bread slices, fried and toasted
- raisins
- almonds
- candied citron pieces
- candied orange pieces
- broth
- white sugar
- butter
- saffron
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- toasted cilantro
- salt
- two cooked egg yolks

## Directions

1. Coat a pot or earthenware dish with butter.
2. Make layers of thin fried and toasted bread slices, raisins, almonds, candied citron, and candied orange until the dish is full.
3. Moisten everything with broth sweetened with white sugar and composed with butter, saffron, cinnamon, pepper, toasted cilantro, and salt.
4. Add two cooked egg yolks.
5. When the bread slices are well-soaked, place the soup to set between two fires.
6. Can also add cooked chorizo, chicken breasts, and pieces of ham in the layers, or fried if desired.
