## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- lard
- pepper
- salt
- spices
- ham
- butter
- lard slices
- veal slices
- roots
- spice cloves
- bay leaf
- parsley
- scallions
- concentrated broth
- gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare the ribs as described in the sautéed ribs article
2. Lard them with fine lard, well seasoned with pepper, salt, and spices, and with ham
3. Place them over heat on a sauté pan with butter, and when partially cooked, transfer to a pot
4. Line the bottom of the pot with lard slices, some sliced veal, roots sliced lengthwise, two cloves of spice, a bay leaf, and a bundle of parsley and scallions
5. Cover with more lard slices and a wheel of paper buttered, adding a full spoonful of concentrated broth
6. Cook over gentle heat for an hour and a half
7. When serving, drain and glaze with good gelatin, arranging nicely on a plate with Spanish sauce, gherkins, white onion substance, tomato sauce, etc.
