## Ingredients

- ham center cut (landrecilla)
- water
- onions
- cloves
- herbs
- bacon
- lean veal
- white wine
- fresh peas

## Directions

1. Desalt the landrecilla for twenty-four hours
2. Place it in a pot filled with water, with two onions studded with two cloves of spices and a bouquet of herbs
3. When the water begins to boil, skim the pot and leave the landrecilla in it for two hours
4. Remove it and the broth is very good for soup
5. Cover the bottom of the pot with two or three slices of bacon and a piece of lean veal, on top of which place the landrecilla with the onions and herbs and add a little white wine, letting it all cook over a low fire
6. Cook fresh peas separately
7. When they are cooked, remove the landrecilla from the pot and soak it well in the juice after having removed the fat
8. If the sauce is too thin, reduce it and add the peas, which are left to cook for a quarter hour
9. Place them on a platter and serve the landrecilla on top of the peas, very hot
