## Ingredients

- Lamb head
- Lamb liver
- Lamb heart
- Lamb tripe
- Lamb feet
- Broth
- Butter
- Herb bouquet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Verjuice

## Directions

1. Remove the jaws and snout from the head and divide the remaining offal into pieces
2. Blanch everything briefly and then cook over gentle heat with broth, good butter, a mixed herb bouquet, salt and pepper
3. Let the broth thicken over the heat and then add a little verjuice
4. Arrange the head in the middle of the serving plate, exposing the brains, placing the rest of the offal around it and pouring the broth over
