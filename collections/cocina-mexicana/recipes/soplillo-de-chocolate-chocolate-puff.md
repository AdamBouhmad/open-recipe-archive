## Ingredients

- One quarter pound milk
- Three ounces chocolate
- One tablespoon potato starch
- Four fresh eggs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Take one quarter pound of milk and three ounces of chocolate
2. Cook the chocolate in the milk
3. Add the starch
4. Cook for three minutes
5. When the mixture is cool enough that it will not cook the eggs, add the well-beaten yolks
6. Beat the egg whites from the same eggs until they are somewhat firm
7. Mix them with everything
8. Pour it into a well-prepared mold that has been heated after being well buttered
9. Place it over two fires
10. Serve in the mold without delay
