## Ingredients

- butter (less than standard)
- flour
- coles dough (see coles dough, page 516)
- egg for moistening
- egg yolk for gilding
- small figurine decorations
- syrup at crack point

## Directions

1. Reduce the butter and strengthen the coles dough with flour
2. When the dough has dried well, moisten it with egg
3. Place small pieces of dough on the platform about the size of half an egg
4. Gild them and using a finger moistened in the gilding, make a small hole in the middle to place a small figurine
5. Bake them and after baking, coat one by one with syrup at crack point
6. Press them together and serve
