## Ingredients

- pheasant
- ham slices
- breast fat or raw brains
- leftovers
- carrots
- onions
- herb bouquet
- cabbage
- broth
- black pepper
- salt
- game sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the pheasant as described previously for stew
2. Line the braising pan or pot with ham slices, breast fat or raw brains, leftovers, some carrots, onions and a well-stocked herb bouquet
3. Parboil the cabbage separately, tie it, and place with the pheasant in the braising pan or pot with broth, a little black pepper and no salt, as the broth and ham are already seasoned
4. Let everything cook over gentle heat for two hours
5. At serving time, drain the cabbage, place the pheasant in the middle of the plate, arrange the fat, cabbage and brains around it, and pour game sauce over and around it
