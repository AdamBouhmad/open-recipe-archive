## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of virgin honey
- five egg whites
- one pound of ground almond

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with two pounds of sugar and one pound of virgin honey, clarify it, and when it reaches the soft ball stage, let it cool slightly and mix in little by little five well-beaten egg whites that have been prepared in advance
2. At the same time as the egg is mixed with the syrup, beat everything perfectly until it turns white and reaches the soft ball stage again
3. Add then one pound of ground almond and let it rise again to the point where small balls can be formed
