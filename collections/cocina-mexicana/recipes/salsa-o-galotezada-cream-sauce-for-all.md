## Ingredients

- Fresh butter or lard
- Flour
- Hot cream
- Salt or sugar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of fresh butter or lard in a pot and heat
2. Once the butter is melted, add a tablespoon of flour
3. Stir well for a while
4. Add a quarter measure of hot cream and let it reduce, being careful to turn and stir frequently so it does not stick
5. Gradually add other portions of cream up to two quarter measures
6. When it reaches the right consistency, remove from heat and season with salt or sugar
7. Serve for all types of fish and vegetables
