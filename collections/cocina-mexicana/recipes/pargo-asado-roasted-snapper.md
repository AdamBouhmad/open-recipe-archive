## Ingredients

- pargo (snapper)
- silver coin
- fried oil
- peppercorns, crushed
- garlic cloves, cleaned and crushed
- lemon or vinegar juice

## Directions

1. Place this fish in a wide pan with a silver coin so it does not stick.
2. Brush it with fried oil that has been prepared, with crushed peppercorns and cleaned and crushed garlic cloves.
3. Roast over two slow fires, and serve with the same broth it has released, mixed with a few drops of lemon or vinegar juice.
