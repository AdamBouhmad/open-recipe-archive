## Ingredients

- Bergamot pears
- Biscuit
- Eggs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Peel the bergamot pears well
2. Grind them with biscuit
3. Beat eggs as if for frying
4. Mix the pear dough well into the eggs
5. Alternate layers of pear dough and filling in a lard-greased baking dish
