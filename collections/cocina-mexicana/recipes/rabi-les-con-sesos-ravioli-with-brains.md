## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- tequesquite (mineral salt)
- fatty ham
- beef marrow
- brains
- salt
- small black pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- grated Flemish cheese
- flour
- two or three eggs
- melted lard

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of spinach with a little settled tequesquite water
2. Form a filling by mixing the spinach with some fatty ham, an equal amount of beef marrow, and an equal amount of brains, all cooked separately
3. Add the necessary fine salt
4. Add small black pepper, cloves, and cinnamon, all in powder form
5. Add grated Flemish cheese
6. Once everything is well mixed, make a dough with flour, two or three eggs, and fine salt
7. Extend this dough on a table spread with a little melted lard
