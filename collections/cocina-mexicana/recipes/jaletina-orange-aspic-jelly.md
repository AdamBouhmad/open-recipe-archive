## Ingredients

- veal feet
- water
- sweet oranges
- sugar
- eggs
- cinnamon
- orange liqueur
- Catalan brandy

## Directions

1. Cook three well-cleaned veal feet in sufficient water until they fall apart
2. Remove the fat from the broth by passing it through a sieve
3. Measure eight cuartillos and strain through new linen as many times as necessary to remove all fat
4. Peel three sweet oranges, cut the peels into pieces and place in a pot with the juice of the same oranges, one and one-quarter pounds of very white sugar, four eggs only broken, one ounce of cinnamon, half a cuartillo of orange liqueur and another half of Catalan brandy all mixed together
5. Place on heat until it boils vigorously
6. Pour into a flannel bag to distill
