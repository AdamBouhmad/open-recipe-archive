## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk, boiled and sweetened
- eight egg yolks
- three ounces of fine biscuit, crumbled
- cinnamon sticks
- yolk and flour coating

## Directions

1. To four boiled and sweetened quartillos of milk, add eight egg yolks, three ounces of crumbled fine biscuit, and cinnamon sticks
2. Heat again and prepare the syrup and garnishes as in the previous article
