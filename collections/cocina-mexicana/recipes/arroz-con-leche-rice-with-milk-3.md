## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one quarter pound of refined sugar
- one quarter pound of rice
- a small piece of cinnamon
- lemon or orange peels
- sugar and cinnamon powder for serving

## Directions

1. Heat the milk in a new pot with a small piece of cinnamon and lemon or orange peels tied with strong thread for removal
2. When the milk begins to boil, add the rice that has been half-cooked with water and salt and drained
3. Stir constantly with one hand without stopping to prevent the milk from curdling
4. Serve with sugar and ground cinnamon
