## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- syrup
- orange blossom water
- white wine
- 30 eggs
- 4 ounces ground almonds
- marquesote biscuits, sliced
- ground cinnamon
- toasted sesame seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- dragée

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup made with two pounds of sugar
2. Add orange blossom water and let it reach medium point, forming a thread between the fingers
3. Add a cup of white wine and give a few boils, then remove to cool
4. Beat thirty eggs very well and gradually add to the syrup, removing the pot a bit while another stirs very quickly
5. Place the pot over gentle heat until it reaches the point of separating from the syrup
6. Then add four ounces of ground almonds, dissolved in the syrup that was set aside
7. Place sliced marquesote biscuits on a platter, which will be moistened with clarified syrup made of one quarter of it mixed with orange blossom water and another quarter of white wine
8. Moisten the marquesotes well with this
9. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon, toasted sesame seeds, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
10. On top of everything place the soft eggs, which will also be adorned with raisins and almonds, sprinkling with a little ground cinnamon so the yellow of the eggs stands out, and dragée
