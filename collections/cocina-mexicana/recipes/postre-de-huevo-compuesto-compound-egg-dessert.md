## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 24 egg yolks
- clove
- cinnamon powder
- virgin honey (optional for variation)

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at conserve point with 2 pounds of sugar
2. Off heat, mix in 24 beaten egg yolks, a little clove and cinnamon powder
3. Pour into a dish covered with comal or tin sheet with heat on top to brown it
4. After it cools, dust with cinnamon
5. It can be varied before it sets by mixing in a bowl of virgin honey, making it very flavorful
