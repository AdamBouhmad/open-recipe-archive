## Ingredients

- Veal
- Potatoes, raw
- Tomatoes, minced
- Onions, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Lard
- Bread crumbs
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Place a piece of veal to cook in the pot and once it is half-cooked (more tender than tough), mince it into small pieces.
2. Cut equal-sized pieces from raw potatoes in the same quantity as the meat. Mince tomatoes, onions, and garlic as if making another type of soup.
3. Put sufficient broth from the pot into a casserole over heat, adding the minced garlic, tomatoes, and onions, a regular spoonful of lard, the meat, and potatoes.
4. Bring to a boil and when well-cooked, add bread crumbs to thicken it, but not in such quantity that it becomes like atole.
5. If the broth becomes somewhat loose when placing it in the soup bowl, skim off the lard. Of spices, only add a little black pepper.
