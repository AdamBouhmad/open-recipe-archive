## Ingredients

- 6 cuartillos milk
- 3.5 pounds sugar
- 0.5 ounce starch

## Directions

1. Dissolve half an ounce of starch in one cuartillo of milk.
2. Add sugar and remaining milk.
3. Cook over gentle heat.
