## Ingredients

- clean chickens
- lard
- water
- salt
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- parsley
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- peas
- small squash
- small chiles

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chickens, let them dry until they have no moisture
2. Fry them in lard, and after boiling a little, add salted water to cook them
3. Meanwhile, finely chop garlic and onions
4. Chop plenty of tomatoes and parsley very fine
5. When the garlic and onion are fried, fry the tomato and parsley for a long time
6. Add the chickens with their broth
7. Add cooked onion, peas, small squash and small chiles
8. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper and season with these spices and sufficient salt
