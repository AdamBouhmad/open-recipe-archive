## Ingredients

- ordinary sea salt
- water

## Directions

1. Boil sea salt in water
2. Skim off foam when removed from heat
3. Strain and clarify
4. The water will be sufficiently saturated without need to measure salt carefully, as only the salt that water can dissolve remains suspended, with excess settling at the bottom of the vessel
