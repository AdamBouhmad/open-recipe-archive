## Ingredients

- eggs
- capers
- oil
- lard
- onions
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- thyme

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs and grind the yolks together with some capers
2. Fry them in oil and lard
3. Add onions cut into sections
4. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and a little thyme
5. Do not add more water than necessary to grind the spices
6. Let everything fry very well and then gradually pour the pámpano over it
7. Add plenty of oil and let it simmer a little
8. Then decorate the platter with chiles and capers
