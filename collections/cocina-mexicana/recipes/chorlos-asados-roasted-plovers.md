## Ingredients

- plovers
- giblets
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- bread slices
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Extract and chop the giblets, except the gizzard, with bacon, salt, pepper, parsley, and garlic
2. Fill the birds with this mixture
3. Wrap them in bacon
4. Roast them, basting with the juices that run off
5. Catch the juices in slices of bread
6. Serve on these same slices with a spicy sauce
