## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- Grand sauce

## Directions

1. Cook as the brains à la mayordoana; pour seven or eight lines of thickness of Grand sauce (see Grand sauce, page 765) into the bottom of a mold and let it congeal there
2. On the jelly, arrange the brains cut into pieces so they are transparent and form the pattern well
3. Pour a little of the same Grand sauce, almost cold, on top of the brains and put the mold in the snow so everything congeals
4. At the moment of serving, rub the mold with a hot cloth or cotton to release the jelly and invert onto a plate, taking care not to damage the jelly and placing the plate on ice immediately
5. If some gelatin remains on the plate, suck it out with a hollow tube or straw of feather, etc.
