## Ingredients

- ribs
- tongues

## Directions

1. After cooking the ribs, fry them until they are in good condition
2. Before serving, cut slices of one or more tongues that have been stewed beforehand
3. Place them in the middle and in the intervals of the ribs
4. This is merely a method to differentiate them and enhance this dish
