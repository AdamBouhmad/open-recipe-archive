## Ingredients

- three or four pounds sugar
- two ounces good jasmine spirit
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Pound and pass three or four pounds of sugar through a sieve as previously described
2. Place the sugar in an earthenware vessel with two ounces of good jasmine spirit
3. Use the spirit to dissolve and make the sugar into paste
4. Add natural water, strain, and form the pastilles
5. Use equal quantity of spirit of each plant for each pound of sugar for similar pastilles of tuberose, jonquil, and others
