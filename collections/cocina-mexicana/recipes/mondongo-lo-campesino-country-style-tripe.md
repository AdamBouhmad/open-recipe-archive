## Ingredients

- tripe
- hot water
- vinegar or lemon juice
- water
- salt
- veal
- ham
- sausage
- chickpeas
- lemon juice
- garlic heads
- chile
- bread
- saffron
- garlic
- cumin
- cilantro
- tripe broth

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe in hot water, then drain well
2. Wash again with good vinegar or lemon juice
3. Cut into small pieces and place in a pot with water and salt, a veal cut without bones, ham cut into pieces, a good sausage, some Castilian chickpeas, the juice of half a lemon, two whole garlic heads, and chile
4. Grind a little bread soaked in saffron with three garlic cloves, some cumin, and cilantro with some of the tripe broth
5. Add this to the stew and let it thicken a little
6. Serve always very hot
7. If using tripe, turn it inside out when washing
