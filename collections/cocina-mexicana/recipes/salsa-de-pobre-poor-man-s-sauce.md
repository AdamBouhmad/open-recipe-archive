## Ingredients

- broth or fat
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make it with broth or fat, one tablespoon of vinegar, salt, pepper and parsley
2. Let it boil
3. It is used to reheat roasted or boiled meat left from the previous day
