## Ingredients

- thick sole fish
- oil
- onions
- parsley
- butter
- egg yolk
- salt
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Select the thickest fish and after scraping them on both sides, clean them through a small opening made under the roe
2. Cut off the beards, soak them, wash them and dry them well between two cloths
3. Fry in oil with onions and parsley
4. Pass a spit along the spine and fix its extremities
5. Leave to roast basting with oil
6. When almost cooked through, melt butter over gentle heat, mixing it with an egg yolk and salt
7. Cover the sole with this mixture and then with crumbled cold bread crumbs
8. Fry in butter until golden and serve with the sauce you prefer
