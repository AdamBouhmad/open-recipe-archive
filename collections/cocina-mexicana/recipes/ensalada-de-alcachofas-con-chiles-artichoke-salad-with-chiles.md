## Ingredients

- Green poblano chiles
- Artichokes, cooked
- Garlic cloves
- Onion
- Oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place green poblano chiles to ripen among straw and in the sun for one or two days until they turn red.
2. Roast them and after peeling, remove the veins or place them in salt water to desflame, changing the water two or three times.
3. After cooking the artichokes, disassemble them, carefully removing the hearts without destroying them.
4. Remove all the tender parts from the leaves with a knife.
5. Chop a little onion finely and also chop the chiles.
6. Place a pot on the fire with oil, burn some garlic cloves in it, and fry the chile with the onion and all that was removed from the artichoke leaves, adding salt and black pepper.
7. Once well fried, add the artichoke bottoms to fry without breaking them apart.
8. Remove from the fire and serve hot with salt, vinegar, and oil.
