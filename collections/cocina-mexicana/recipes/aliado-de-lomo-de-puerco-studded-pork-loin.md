## Ingredients

- pork loin
- hot water
- lemon pieces
- garlic cloves
- salt water
- lard
- paper
- twine

## Directions

1. Prepare the loin from one day to the next, wash with hot water and stud with lemon pieces and garlic cloves.
2. Place in salt water infusion for twenty-four hours.
3. Remove and drain, coat well with lard, wrap in paper and tie with twine.
4. Place on the grill to roast over low heat; if necessary, apply another coating of lard.
5. Leave until well roasted, garnishing as desired.
