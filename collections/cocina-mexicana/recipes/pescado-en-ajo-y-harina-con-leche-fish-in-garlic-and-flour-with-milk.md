## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- oil
- flour
- milk
- fish

## Directions

1. Burn the lard
2. Once burnt, fry finely chopped garlic and onion in it, mixing in oil while frying
3. Add flour
4. Before it takes color, add milk and place the fish in it
