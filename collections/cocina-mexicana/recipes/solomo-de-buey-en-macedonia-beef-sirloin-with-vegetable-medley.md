## Ingredients

- Beef sirloin
- Thick strips of ham
- Vegetable medley

## Directions

1. Flatten the sirloin and lard it with thick strips of ham
2. Cook on hot coals
3. Strain the cooking liquid and serve both the meat and sauce over a medley of vegetables
