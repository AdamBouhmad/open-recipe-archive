## Ingredients

- sole fish
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- mushrooms
- white wine
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. In a fire-resistant platter lined with butter, parsley, onion and garlic, and if desired mushrooms, all chopped very finely, place the sole
2. Cover it with the same ingredients, adding a glass of white wine
3. Dust with bread crumbs or crumbled cold bread
4. Sprinkle with melted butter so it takes on a beautiful golden color
5. Leave to cook over two fires, the lower one more gentle than the upper one
