## Ingredients

- less than half a cuartilla of flour
- two pounds of butter
- one pound of beef fat
- six egg yolks
- sufficient quantity of very hot water

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and fat
2. Skim well before incorporating into the flour
3. Add the six egg yolks and sufficient quantity of very hot water
4. Knead for a long time and roll out many times
