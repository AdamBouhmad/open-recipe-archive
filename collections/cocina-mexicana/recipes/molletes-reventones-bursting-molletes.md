## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh yeast
- two ounces of cold lard
- one cup of warm water
- several eggs
- nine ounces of finely ground sugar
- three and a half pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Place one pound of fresh yeast, two ounces of cold lard, and one cup of warm water in a bowl
2. Mix with the palm of the hand until well kneaded
3. Beat several eggs in a pot with a whisk until whites and yolks are well incorporated
4. Add the eggs to the yeast mixture and stir a little with the palm of the hand
5. Add nine ounces of finely ground sugar and three and a half pounds of flour, with one person adding flour while another stirs and mixes well
6. When well incorporated, beat the dough well
7. When it pulls away from the hands and the container, place in a bowl, cover with a cloth, and set at the side of the brasero
8. The next day, form molletes by weighing them at one ounce each and placing them on greased baking sheets
9. Cover again and after three hours, place in a moderate oven
