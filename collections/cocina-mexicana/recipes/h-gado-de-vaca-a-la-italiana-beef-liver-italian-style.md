## Ingredients

- beef liver
- salt
- black pepper
- fine herbs (parsley, small onions, mushrooms, half a head of garlic, two shallots)
- half a bay leaf
- thyme
- basil powder
- good oil
- small piece of butter
- flour
- verjuice
- a dash of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into very thin strips
2. Place a layer of strips in the bottom of a medium saucepan, seasoning with salt, black pepper, good oil and fine herbs composed of parsley, small onions, mushrooms, half a head of garlic and two shallots, all chopped very fine, and adding half a bay leaf, thyme and basil powder
3. Place another layer of liver strips seasoned the same way, and continue this way until all is used, with the last layer being seasoning
4. Let cook for one hour over gentle heat and remove immediately from the saucepan with a skimmer
5. Skim the fat from the broth and add a small piece of butter worked with flour, with half a spoonful of verjuice or a dash of vinegar
6. Thicken the sauce over heat stirring with a spoon, adding a little substantial broth if it becomes too thick, and heat the liver in it
7. Serve arranged on a plate
