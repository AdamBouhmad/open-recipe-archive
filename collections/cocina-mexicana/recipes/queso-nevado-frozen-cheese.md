## Ingredients

- four types of ice cream
- two cream-based
- two fruit-based
- snow
- saltpeter

## Directions

1. Prepare four types of ice cream, two of cream and two of fruits
2. Fill a mold for frozen cheeses placing one quarter of each of the prepared ice creams
3. Put the mold in a container with crushed snow and saltpeter
4. When you wish to prepare this cheese, wet the mold in warm water, dry it, remove the lid, place a plate on top and then turn it over with the mold, which being removed immediately, leaves the cheese placed on the plate
