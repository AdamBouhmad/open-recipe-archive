## Ingredients

- Duck, plucked and cleaned
- Ham slices
- Lemon juice
- Spanish sauce
- Black pepper
- Sour orange juice or lemon juice
- Orange or lemon peel

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning, and singeing the duck over flame to remove down, tuck the legs between the thighs and tie with string.
2. Insert the tail inside the body, securing it with a needle and thread, forming a rounded, compact shape.
3. Rub the breast with lemon juice.
4. Line a casserole with slices of ham and place the duck on top, covering it with more ham slices.
5. Place everything in a pan to cook; one hour before serving, put it over the fire and let it cook until ready to serve.
6. Drain, untie, and arrange on a plate.
7. For the sauce: heat three tablespoons of Spanish sauce in a small pot over the fire with some black pepper and the juice of a sour orange or lemon, with a bit of its peel.
8. At the first boil, pour the sauce over the duck.
