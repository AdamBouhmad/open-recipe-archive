## Ingredients

- broth
- vinegar
- parsley
- garlic cloves
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. In a pot, combine broth and vinegar in sufficient quantity
2. Add a handful of chopped parsley with eight or ten cloves of garlic, salt and pepper
3. Put on the fire until the garlic is perfectly cooked
4. Use this sauce or broth to boil leftovers of any meat or fish
