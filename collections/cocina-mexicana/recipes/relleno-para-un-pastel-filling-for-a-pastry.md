## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, well cooked with seasonings
- 4 or 5 dozen meatballs
- good stew or braised stock
- macaroni or thick noodles
- salt
- Flanders cheese, grated
- cinnamon
- egg yolk, beaten

## Directions

1. Prepare in advance: have 1 chicken well cooked with seasonings, deboned and cut into small pieces
2. Have 4 or 5 dozen meatballs (the size of a one-ounce bullet) prepared and cooked as usual
3. Have ready a good stew or braised stock that should be thick, colored and substantial
4. Boil macaroni or thick noodles in salted water until not too soft
5. Drain through a colander
6. In the prepared cooking pot, layer the ingredients: a few noodles with grated Flanders cheese, some meatballs, some cooked chicken, a pinch of cinnamon, and some of the stew stock
7. Continue layering until the pot is filled
8. Cover with the dough layer and seal it at the edges so the pastry is well closed
9. Brush the top surface with a feather using beaten egg yolk
10. Sprinkle sugar on top
11. Put in a very hot oven; when almost done (which can be seen from the covering dough), cool slightly and turn onto a round platter to serve
