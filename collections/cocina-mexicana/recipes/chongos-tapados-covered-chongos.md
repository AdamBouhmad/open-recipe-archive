## Ingredients

- butter
- cold sponge cake, sliced
- fresh cheese
- syrup
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Coat the casserole generously with butter
2. Place a layer of sliced cold sponge cake and another of fresh cheese, adding syrup as if making capirotada
3. Coat the last layer with some feathers of egg yolks
4. Cover with a comal so that the chongos set on gentle heat
