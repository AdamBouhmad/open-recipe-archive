## Ingredients

- half a pound of almonds
- juice of three Chinese oranges
- peelings of three Chinese oranges, cleaned and chopped
- two ounces of starch
- six egg yolks
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel and grind almonds weighing half a pound
2. Mix the ground almonds with the juice of three Chinese oranges and their cleaned, chopped peels, placing in a bowl
3. Mix in two ounces of starch, six egg yolks, and half a pound of powdered sugar
4. Fold in the well-beaten egg whites
5. Pour the dough into a long paper box that has been buttered
6. Bake in the oven until cooked
7. When the gâteau is cooked, cut into small square pieces
8. Cover with royal icing sugar and dry in the oven
