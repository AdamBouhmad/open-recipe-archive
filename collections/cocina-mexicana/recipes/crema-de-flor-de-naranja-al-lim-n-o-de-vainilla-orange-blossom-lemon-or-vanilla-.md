## Ingredients

- Orange blossom, lemon, or vanilla
- Cream or milk
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make all creams as coffee or tea cream, by infusion in the cream or milk of the ingredient with which you wish to flavor it
2. Cook and bring to the proper point equally over a bain-marie and serve cold
