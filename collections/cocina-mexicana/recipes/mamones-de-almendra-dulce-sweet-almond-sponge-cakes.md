## Ingredients

- one pound peeled almonds
- egg white
- twenty-four egg yolks
- one pound ground sugar
- egg whites
- one tablespoon ground starch

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of peeled almonds with egg white so they do not become oily
2. Beat twenty-four egg yolks with one pound ground sugar until the mixture shows eyes
3. Beat the egg whites as for frying
4. Combine everything and add one tablespoon of ground starch
5. Beat quickly at the mouth of the oven
6. Pour into paper boxes
7. Bake in a well-tempered oven so they only brown
