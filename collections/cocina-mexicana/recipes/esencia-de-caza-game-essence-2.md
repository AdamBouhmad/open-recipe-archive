## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef
- 2 partridges
- 2 rabbits
- 1 piece lamb or beef leg
- 2 quartillos white wine
- onions
- carrots
- thyme
- basil
- serpol
- cloves
- good broth

## Directions

1. Place beef, partridges, rabbits, and lamb or beef leg in a pot or marmite
2. Add 2 quartillos of white wine and let it boil until the wine has been consumed
3. Fill the pot with good broth
4. Add onions, carrots, thyme, basil, serpol, and cloves
5. Skim thoroughly
6. Let everything boil until the meats are cooked
7. Strain the essence through a sieve
8. Store for use
