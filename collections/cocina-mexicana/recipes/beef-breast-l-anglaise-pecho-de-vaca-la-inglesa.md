## Ingredients

- beef breast
- veal kidney fat
- bread crumb
- parsley
- sage leaves
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- meat sauce
- pear or apple jelly

## Directions

1. After preparation, make an incision between each rib
2. Mince together one pound of veal kidney fat, the same amount of bread crumb, a handful of parsley and some sage leaves
3. Continue mincing while adding egg yolks, salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. Place this filling in the incisions made in the breast
5. Sew and roast on the spit
6. When cooked, arrange over meat sauce and serve pear or apple jelly separately
