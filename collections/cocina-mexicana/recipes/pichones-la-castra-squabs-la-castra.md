## Ingredients

- squabs
- ham, fat slices
- meat broth
- parsley
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Empty and scald the squabs, turn the feet inward and leave them to cool in cold water.
2. Place them in a pot between two slices of fat ham with meat broth and a bunch of parsley and fine herbs.
3. When cooked, skim the broth and add it to a sauce, which is clarified and thickened.
