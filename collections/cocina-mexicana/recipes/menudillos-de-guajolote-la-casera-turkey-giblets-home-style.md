## Ingredients

- turkey giblets: wings, feet, neck, and gizzard
- butter
- a small bunch of parsley
- onion
- garlic cloves
- two cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- a good tablespoon of flour
- broth from the pot or meat broth
- salt and pepper
- turnips

## Directions

1. Scald and clean all the giblets thoroughly
2. Place in a saucepan with butter, a small bunch of parsley, onion, garlic cloves, two cloves, thyme, and bay leaf
3. Place over heat and add a good tablespoon of flour
4. Add broth from the pot or meat broth
5. Season as needed
6. Fry turnips in a frying pan until they take on a golden color, then add them to the pot
7. Let cook; if the sauce is too thin, let it reduce over the heat
