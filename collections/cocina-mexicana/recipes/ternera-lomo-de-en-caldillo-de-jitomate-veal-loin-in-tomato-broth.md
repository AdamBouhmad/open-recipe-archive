## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Lard
- Garlic, ground
- Salt
- Tomatoes, roasted and ground
- Broth from the pot
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin, ground
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. After the loin is cooked, fry it in lard with ground garlic and salt.
2. Then add roasted and ground tomatoes which will be fried together with the loin.
3. When it is almost dry, add broth from the pot with clove, ground pepper and cumin, thyme, laurel, vinegar and sugar.
4. Let it cook a bit more over two fires.
