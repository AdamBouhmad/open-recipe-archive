## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- very finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter, very finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper on a plate
2. Mix and stir everything well
3. Then add a little vinegar
