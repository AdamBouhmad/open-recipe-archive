## Ingredients

- pargo (snapper)
- garlic
- oil or lard
- cinnamon, ground
- cloves, ground
- capers, ground
- wine
- vinegar
- spices
- olives
- tornachiles (a type of chili)
- lettuce hearts
- egg yolks fried in lard
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Roast the pargo over coals and place it in soak to cook afterwards and chop it.
2. Fry some garlic in oil or lard, although oil is better, and add the already chopped pargo with ground cinnamon, cloves and capers, wine and vinegar.
3. Once the broth has boiled and is seasoned, add spices, olives, tornachiles, lettuce hearts, egg yolks fried in lard, raisins and almonds.
