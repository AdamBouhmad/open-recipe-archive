## Ingredients

- burned lard
- garlic cloves
- roasted tomatoes
- pineapple
- fried bread
- cinnamon
- clove
- wine
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- cooked young pigeons
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry in burned lard some garlic cloves cut in pieces and remove them from the fat as soon as they brown
2. Place in the fat roasted and ground tomatoes with pineapple, fried bread, cinnamon and clove, and when everything is fried, add wine, broth from the pigeons, the pigeons themselves and a small piece of sugar
3. Once the sauce has the proper consistency, remove from heat and serve
