## Ingredients

- Corn masa as for regular tamales
- Pork skirt meat
- Water
- Salt
- Mulato chile
- Pasilla chile
- Cooked tomatoes
- Toasted sesame
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Three garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare the masa as for regular tamales.
2. Fry a piece of pork skirt meat cut into small pieces with water and salt.
3. Toast and devein mulato and pasilla chiles.
4. Grind the chiles with cooked tomatoes, toasted sesame, clove, cinnamon, black pepper, and roasted garlic cloves.
5. Add this mixture to the frying meat until it becomes thicker than regular mole.
6. Make the tamales and fill each with a small piece of meat using a spoon.
7. Wrap and cook as directed.
