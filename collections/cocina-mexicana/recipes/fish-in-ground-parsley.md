## Ingredients

- Garlic
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Lard
- Parsley
- Bread
- Cooked or parboiled fish
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind garlic, pepper and cumin and fry them in lard
2. Grind parsley with a piece of soaked bread, and straining it, add it to the fried ingredients, where the cooked or parboiled fish will be put to boil with the necessary salt
3. When removing from heat, add oil and vinegar
