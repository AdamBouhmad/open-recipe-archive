## Ingredients

- Two or three leeks or wild garlic
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Cut very finely two or three leeks or wild garlic, which will be fried with a little butter
2. Add a quarter of a glass of vinegar, which will be left to reduce
3. Then add a ladle of Spanish sauce
4. Let it all cook over low heat
