## Ingredients

- 1 cup fine starch
- 2 cups milk
- salt
- aniseed powder
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 cup fine starch in 2 cups milk
2. Add a grain of salt and a little aniseed powder
3. Strain through cheesecloth and cook with 1 tablespoon butter
4. When well cooked, set aside to cool
5. Add 6 egg yolks and 2 egg whites, mix with both hands until there are no small pieces
6. Using a spoon, put in a piping bag and pipe into warm butter
7. To prevent them from bursting, remove and dip in syrup pot
8. When at soft-ball stage or after two or three boils, remove carefully with a stick and put on plates with thick syrup
