## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fennel, chopped
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in water until done
2. Cut the cooked potatoes into round, equal slices
3. Boil a handful of chopped fennel in broth for a quarter hour
4. Pour this broth over the potatoes that have been placed in the soup tureen, ensuring there are enough potatoes to serve in place of bread
