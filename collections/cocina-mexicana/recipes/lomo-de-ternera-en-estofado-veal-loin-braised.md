## Ingredients

- bacon pieces
- veal loin
- carrots
- onions
- herb bundle
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- vinegar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Place bacon pieces in a pot, and on top veal loin with carrots, onions, a bundle of herbs, salt, pepper and a little broth, a few drops of vinegar and lemon in slices without peel or seeds
2. Place to cook with a little fire from above, and to serve skim off grease and reduce
