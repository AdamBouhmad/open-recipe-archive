## Ingredients

- forty-two cuartillos of milk
- eight pounds of sugar
- two ounces of starch
- half a pound of pounded almond

## Directions

1. Dissolve in forty-two cuartillos of milk, eight pounds of sugar and two ounces of starch
2. Strain it, place to boil, and when at medium point, stir in half a pound of pounded almond
3. Give it the point of cajeta and the color desired
