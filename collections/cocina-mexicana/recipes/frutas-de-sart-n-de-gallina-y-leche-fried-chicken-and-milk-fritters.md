## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Onion
- Garlic cloves
- Flour
- One quartillo of milk
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Parsley
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Kill the chicken and let it sit from one day to the next
2. Roast it over coals
3. Remove all the bones and mince the meat very finely
4. Fry a little onion and two minced garlic cloves in lard
5. When they are browned, add a little flour to fry it as well
6. Pour in one quartillo of milk and let everything boil until it thickens
7. Mix in the minced chicken with cloves, cinnamon, salt and chopped parsley
8. Mix everything very well with two eggs and let it cool
9. Form the fritters, roll them in breadcrumbs and coat them with egg
10. Fry them and serve
