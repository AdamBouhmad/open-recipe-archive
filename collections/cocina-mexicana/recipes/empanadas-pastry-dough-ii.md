## Ingredients

- One arroba of flour
- Two and a half pounds powdered sugar
- Five pounds lard
- Thirty-five egg yolks
- Four or five egg whites
- Two ounces yeast
- Hot water as needed

## Directions

1. Add the powdered sugar to the flour
2. Add the lard, egg yolks, egg whites, and yeast
3. Add hot water until the dough is somewhat stiff
