## Ingredients

- Ground truffles and lard in small strips
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- parsley
- small onions
- garlic cloves
- chicory slices
- leftover roasted leg meat from the previous day
- concentrated broth
- Spanish sauce
- butter
- fried bread croutons

## Directions

1. Cut ground truffles and lard into small strips and roll them in salt, pepper, clove, cinnamon all in powder form
2. Mince parsley, small onions, and garlic cloves very finely
3. Lard the leg of mutton with these preparations on all sides and wrap it in paper so that no air can reach it
4. Leave it thus for two days
5. After that time, place it in a pot with its own juices, covered with chicory slices
6. Make chicory slices ordinarily with the remains of a roasted leg from the previous day by slicing the meats and placing them in a pot
7. After having blanched the chicory, mince it separately, render it in butter and let it cook in equal parts of concentrated broth and Spanish sauce
8. When well cooked and thick, pour it over the meat slices, mix everything together and dress on a plate in the shape of a hill with fried bread croutons in butter around it
