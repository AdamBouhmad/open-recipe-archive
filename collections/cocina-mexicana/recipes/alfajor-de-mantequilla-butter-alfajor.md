## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- two quartillos of virgin honey
- toasted or toasted bread, crushed
- a little butter
- powder of pepper
- clove
- ginger
- nutmeg
- toasted sesame seeds
- pieces of almond
- pieces of walnut

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with the sugar and virgin honey at high point, well clarified
2. Away from the heat, mix with toasted bread or pan, crushed one or the other, and adding a little butter return it to the heat so that it incorporates throughout the paste, which is achieved in little time
3. Remove from the fire, add powder of pepper, clove, ginger and nutmeg to taste, let it rest for some hours
4. Thicken the paste with bread or pan if it is too liquid, or with hot syrup if it is too hard
5. Add toasted sesame seeds and pieces of almond and walnut if making cartouches; if pouring into a platter or boxes, only decorate on top with these things and with cinnamon powder
