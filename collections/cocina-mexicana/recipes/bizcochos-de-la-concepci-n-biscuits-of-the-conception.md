## Ingredients

- six pounds of flour
- eight egg yolks
- two pounds of melted lard
- one and a half pounds of ground sugar
- twelve ounces of leavening
- one cuartillo of water

## Directions

1. Mix everything
2. Knead well
3. Make the biscuits
4. Put them in the oven
