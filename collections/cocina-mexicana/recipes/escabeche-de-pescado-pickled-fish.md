## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic
- vinegar
- oil
- Castilian pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- cloves
- bay leaf
- oregano
- cumin
- fish

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard, using a proportion matching the amount of fish.
2. Remove the pot from the heat.
3. Add two parts vinegar and one part oil, Castilian and Tabasco pepper in greater quantity, cloves, bay leaf, oregano, and cumin.
4. Let steep a while.
5. Once the fish is fried, place it in the broth.
