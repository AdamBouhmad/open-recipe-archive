## Ingredients

- Rice
- Salt water
- Eggs
- All spices except cumin

## Directions

1. Soak the rice for ten or twelve hours in salt water
2. Put it to boil
3. Before it is completely cooked, remove it, press it in a cloth
4. Mix in some eggs and all spices except cumin
5. Knead well
6. Form sheets from it
7. Make pastry by greasing all sheets with butter or buttermilk, or by having added this fat when making the dough
