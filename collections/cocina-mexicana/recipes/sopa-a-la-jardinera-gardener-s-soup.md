## Ingredients

- Thin strips of carrots and turnips
- Lettuce
- Sorrel
- Chervil, chopped
- Butter or oil
- Broth from the pot
- Handful of peas
- Some asparagus heads
- Bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut thin strips of carrots and turnips
2. Have chopped lettuce, sorrel, and chervil
3. Sauté everything with butter or oil
4. Add broth from the pot, adding a handful of peas and some asparagus heads
5. Once these vegetables are well cooked, skim them and pour over bread crusts
