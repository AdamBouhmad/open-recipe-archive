## Ingredients

- one pineapple
- six cuartillos of water
- some tamarinds
- two cloves
- one thick cinnamon stick
- twelve peppercorns
- pineapple peels

## Directions

1. Grind one pineapple and place in a pot with six cuartillos of water, some tamarinds, two cloves, one thick cinnamon stick, twelve peppercorns, and the crushed pineapple peels
2. Cover the pot well and place in the sun and night air for three days
3. Strain on the day it is to be consumed and keep in a cool place
