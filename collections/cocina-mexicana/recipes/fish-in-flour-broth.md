## Ingredients

- Fish
- Lard
- Onions
- Pepper
- Flour
- Water

## Directions

1. After the fish is roasted or cooked, fry onions and very finely chopped garlic and pepper in lard
2. Sprinkle flour over it, and dissolved with a little water, add it to the onion
3. When taking the fish to the table, pour the broth over it
