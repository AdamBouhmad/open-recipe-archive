## Ingredients

- Preserved peaches in syrup
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Hazelnuts
- Peanuts
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Ground clove and cinnamon
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Syrup and wine

## Directions

1. Make a paste from preserved peaches in syrup
2. Add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, hazelnuts, peanuts, toasted sesame seeds, and ground clove and cinnamon
3. When the paste is well thickened, layer it over slices of sponge cake
4. Moisten the sponge cake with syrup and wine
