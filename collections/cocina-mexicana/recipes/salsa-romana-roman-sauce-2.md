## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef
- 1/2 pound ham
- 2 chicken legs
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaf
- cloves
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- butter (amount equal to the ham)
- 12 egg yolks per pound of beef, cooked and ground
- 3 quartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Cut the ham, beef, and chicken legs into small dice according to the proportions given
2. Add carrots, onions, bay leaf, cloves, basil, salt and pepper
3. Add butter in amount equal to the ham
4. Heat everything gently over mild fire, then remove from heat
5. Add the cooked and ground egg yolks
6. Mix everything well to incorporate as much as possible
7. Moisten with 3 quartillos of milk, adding it gradually so the egg yolks dissolve well
8. Return the pan to the fire and bring the sauce to a boil, stirring constantly
9. Finally, strain through cheesecloth and it is ready to serve
