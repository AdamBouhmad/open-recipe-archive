## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Peas
- Swiss chard
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Salt
- Oil
- Lard
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Deflem the chiles
2. Cook the peas and chard
3. Mix them with tomato, onion and chopped garlic, adding the necessary salt
4. Fry all this in two-thirds oil and one-third lard
5. Fill the chiles
6. Once filled, place them in a pot and put cheese and oil on top
7. Cover with a comal with fire on top so the cheese toasts a little
