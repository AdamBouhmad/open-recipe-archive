## Ingredients

- Ducks, plucked and cleaned
- Tequesquite water
- Onion tails, crushed
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Lard
- Dried guajillo chiles, soaked
- Cumin
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Onion, minced raw

## Directions

1. Remove the opening and small point between the tail and spine with a knife.
2. Pass the ducks over flame to remove all down.
3. Soak them in warm tequesquite water with crushed onion tails for ten to twelve hours.
4. Cook with abundant garlic, pepper, and salt.
5. Grind cooked garlic with pepper and fry in lard.
6. Add some of the broth in which the ducks were cooked and place the ducks in it.
7. Leave over the fire until the broth is consumed and the ducks brown in the fat.
8. Serve with a sauce made from soaked guajillo chiles, garlic, cumin, vinegar, oil, and minced raw onion.
