## Ingredients

- Two parts mutton pulp
- One part pork loin
- Piece of bread soaked in vinegar
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Cumin seeds
- One or more raw eggs
- Hard boiled egg or cooked brains seasoned with fine herbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mince very well two parts of mutton pulp and one part pork loin
2. When minced, add a piece of bread soaked in vinegar and grind everything together, removing tendons and skins that resist the grinding stone
3. Grind separately clove, pepper, cinnamon, saffron and a few cumin seeds
4. Mix everything into a dough with one or more raw eggs according to the quantity of meat
5. Form walnut-sized balls with a piece of hard boiled egg or cooked brains seasoned with fine herbs in the center
6. Cook in already boiling water, leaving at gentle heat for a long time
7. When cooked, stew in spices, in a stew, or with clemole
