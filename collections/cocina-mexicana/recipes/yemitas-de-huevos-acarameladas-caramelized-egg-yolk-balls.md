## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- syrup

## Directions

1. Knead or grind cooked egg yolks with ground sugar and cinnamon
2. Make into small balls
3. Dip in syrup at caramel consistency
4. Let dry and store or eat
