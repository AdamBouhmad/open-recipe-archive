## Ingredients

- Poultry livers
- Egg yolks
- Poultry blood
- Rice
- Broth
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Take poultry livers, egg yolks, and blood, and fry them.
2. When done, combine with rice and let it brown.
3. Add broth and a little Spanish sauce.
4. Pound one liver and let everything thicken slightly.
