## Ingredients

- pears
- butter
- eggs
- tomatoes
- onions
- chicken quarters
- chorizo
- raisins
- almonds
- ham
- dried chiles
- olives
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop or slice the pears, fry them a little in butter and set aside
2. Line a casserole with butter, add a little beaten egg, and make a layer of half-fried pear, another of thinly sliced tomatoes and onions, and another of cooked chicken quarters and cooked chorizo, filling the gaps with raisins, almonds, ham pieces, cured dried chiles and olives
3. Begin again with another layer of pear, alternating the others in the indicated order
4. Cover with beaten eggs, which are dusted with sugar and cinnamon
5. Let the cake set over two fires
6. Serve dusted with sugar and cinnamon powder
