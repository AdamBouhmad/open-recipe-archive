## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Equal parts oil and lard
- Onion, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Tomatoes, minced
- Whole black pepper
- Water
- Salt
- Turnips, cleaned and sliced
- Bread, well toasted
- Garlic cloves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks and fry them raw in equal parts oil and lard.
2. Once fried, add minced onion, garlic, and tomatoes, whole black pepper, water, and sufficient salt.
3. When the ducks are almost cooked, add cleaned, sliced turnips.
4. Once cooked, grind very toasted bread in lard with two cloves of garlic in a little vinegar, add to the ducks, and keep over heat until the broth thickens well.
