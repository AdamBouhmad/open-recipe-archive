## Ingredients

- hare
- butter
- onions
- garlic
- salt
- cinnamon
- cloves
- oil
- vinegar
- sour lime slices
- orange leaves
- sugar
- bread
- Malaga wine or white wine
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the hare in its skin, then remove the skin and debone it
2. Fry it in a large portion of butter with chopped onions and garlic
3. When it reaches medium color, remove it and wrap in papers greased with butter, having dusted it with salt, a little cinnamon and cloves
4. Cook on a grill; when done, unwrap and oil it, return to the grill until well roasted
5. Remove from heat and when cold, chop it finely
6. Fry in a little oil, turning only once
7. Add a little diluted vinegar, a stick of cinnamon and three or four cloves
8. Add slices of sour lime, orange leaves cooked in water and a piece of sugar
9. Let simmer over low heat until seasoned, then remove from heat and let rest
10. To serve, heat well and place golden bread slices fried in butter on a platter
11. Pour a glass of Malaga or white wine over the gigote and place on the bread slices
12. Remove the cinnamon sticks, cloves and lime leaves and wheels
13. Garnish with chopped hard boiled eggs and dust lightly with cinnamon over the platter
