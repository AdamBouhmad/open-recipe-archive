## Ingredients

- Cooked calf's tongue
- salt
- water
- ham
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- xitomate
- tomato
- onion
- garlic

## Directions

1. After cooking in salted water, peel it and lard with ham, pepper, clove and cinnamon
2. Place in a pot with lard, xitomate, tomato, onion and garlic, all chopped
3. As soon as the condiment is rendered, add a little water
4. Let the tongue cook until the broth is consumed and it is fried there itself
