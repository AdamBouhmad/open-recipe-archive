## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of beef from a cow's leg, cut into thick cross-wise pieces
- necessary salt
- 4 ounces of lard
- half a glass of dry wine
- double quantity of water
- 3 or 4 small whole onions
- a pair of bay leaves
- a small stick of cinnamon
- 6 cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Cook the beef pieces in a pot with necessary salt
2. Once cooked, remove and fry in a pan with 4 ounces of lard
3. When browned, add half a glass of dry wine and double quantity of water
4. Add 3 or 4 small whole onions, a pair of bay leaves, a small stick of cinnamon, and 6 cloves of garlic
5. Let it boil sufficiently, and if it becomes dry, add a little water
