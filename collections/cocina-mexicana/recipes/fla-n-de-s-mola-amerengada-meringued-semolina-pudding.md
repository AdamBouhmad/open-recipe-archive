## Ingredients

- pastry dough
- semolina
- milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- orange blossom or grated lemon peel
- bitter almond macaroons
- marzipan
- salt
- butter
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare pastry dough as for hot pastry crust
2. Pour semolina into boiling milk like beaten eggs and let drain
3. Add egg yolks, sugar, orange blossom or grated lemon peel, crushed bitter almond macaroons and marzipan
4. Mix everything with a little salt and one egg's worth of melted butter
5. Beat half of the egg whites lightly and fold in with the other ingredients
6. Pour the semolina into the flaón three quarters of an hour before serving
7. Bake in a gently heated oven
8. Let it rise well, then beat the remaining egg whites
9. Mix with powdered sugar and a little candied orange blossom
10. Lightly knead and cover the flaón with this mixture
11. Brush with granulated sugar
12. When golden in color, serve
