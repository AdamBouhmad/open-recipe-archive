## Ingredients

- 10 quail
- ham slices
- brains from a cow's head
- 12 small chorizo (chipolata)
- salt
- pepper
- wooden wine
- concentrated broth
- paper
- sauce à la tolosa

## Directions

1. Gather or tuck the quail like chickens.
2. Singe them at the flame and place them in a pot among some slices of ham, with the brains of a cow's head divided in two halves, a dozen small sausages called chipolata, and a mixed bunch of herbs.
3. Season with salt and pepper and moisten with a good glass of wooden wine and the same amount of concentrated broth.
4. Cover the quail with slices of ham and a round of paper and let them cook this way.
5. Once cooked, drain the quail and the brains.
6. Remove the casing from the small sausages and arrange them in the middle of the plate.
7. Place the quail around them, the brains on top of the small sausages, and cover everything with sauce à la tolosa.
