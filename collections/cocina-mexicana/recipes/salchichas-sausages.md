## Ingredients

- sauce made liquid with beaten eggs
- sugar
- salt
- cloves and cinnamon powder
- meat with enough fat
- cream or breadcrumb soaked in milk cream

## Directions

1. Liquefy the sauce with some beaten eggs, with or without sugar
2. Season with salt in addition to cloves and cinnamon powder
3. The meat, which will have plenty of fat, must be finely chopped so that with the eggs it forms a homogeneous and well-united mass
4. Some, to make them more exquisite, add a little cream or breadcrumb soaked in milk cream
5. These sausages are for eating soon
