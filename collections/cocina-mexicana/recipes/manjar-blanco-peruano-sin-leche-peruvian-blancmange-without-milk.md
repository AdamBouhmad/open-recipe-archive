## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground almond
- 1 chicken breast
- 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste with 1 pound of ground almond and 1 chicken breast
2. With 2 pounds of sugar, make a syrup at a point somewhat lower than that of marmalade
3. Remove from the fire and stir the paste into it
4. Return to the fire and let it reach the point of cajeta, not too high
