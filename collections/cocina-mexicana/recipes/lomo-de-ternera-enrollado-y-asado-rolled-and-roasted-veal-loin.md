## Ingredients

- veal loin jerky
- salt
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- cooked lean ham

## Directions

1. Once the jerky of the loin is made, rub it with salt, garlic, clove and pepper, all ground
2. Place on one edge enough cooked lean ham of the size that the jerky has lengthwise
3. Roll it very tightly, and tie it with thread or twine
4. Place it to cook the same as the loin with embedded bacon (see a bit before), and conclude the operation in the same manner
5. When roasted, serve it whole or in slices arranged with avocado quarters, olives and cured chiles
