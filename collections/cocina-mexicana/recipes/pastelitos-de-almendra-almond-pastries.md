## Ingredients

- Three pounds sugar
- One pound almonds
- Paste filling

## Directions

1. Grind three pounds of sugar with one pound of almonds, regrinding both until they form a smooth paste
2. Form the tops and bottoms of the pastries with this dough, either by hand or better with molds to make a pleasing figure
3. Let dry for one day
4. The next day, fill with a sweet paste filling such as the following: two quarter litres of thick milk, one and one quarter pounds of sugar and eight egg yolks
5. Heat everything, and when it comes loose from the pan as you stir it with a spoon, remove from heat
