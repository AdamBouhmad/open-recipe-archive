## Ingredients

- cooked meat slices
- large onions
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- thyme
- bay leaves
- oil
- raw oil
- chopped onion
- oregano powder
- small chiles
- olives
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place slices of cooked meat in a pot with large, peeled onions
2. Season with salt, pepper, cloves, cinnamon, a sprig of thyme, and one or two bay leaves
3. Sauté everything with oil
4. Cover the pot very well and leave on low heat until the meat is well penetrated with the spiced oil
5. Before serving, add raw oil, chopped onion, powdered oregano, small chiles, and olives
6. Add a little vinegar if desired
