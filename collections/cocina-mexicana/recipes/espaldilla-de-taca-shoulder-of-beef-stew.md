## Ingredients

- butter
- shoulder of beef, deboned
- water or broth, about half a small glass
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- a few drops of brandy

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter and place the deboned shoulder inside.
2. Turn it several times over very active heat.
3. Add about half a small glass of water or broth, salt, pepper, fine herbs, and a few drops of brandy.
4. Cook everything over low heat.
