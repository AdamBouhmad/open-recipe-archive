## Ingredients

- Pears
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the pears and cook them in water
2. When cooked, let them cool and change the water in small quantity
3. Mash them by hand and let them rest for one day
4. The next day pour them over a sieve to drain
5. Take one quarter of the drained liquid to mix off the fire with perfectly clarified syrup made with one pound of sugar at the mirror point without breaking
6. Make the mixture as quickly as possible
7. Return to fire so it recovers the mirror point that breaks with a spoon
8. Remove from fire, let rest a little, remove all the foam, and pour into molds or dishes in which it is to be served
