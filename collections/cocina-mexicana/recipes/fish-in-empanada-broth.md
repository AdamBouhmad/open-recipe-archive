## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Onions
- Ground saffron
- Raw fish
- Chopped parsley
- Ground pepper
- Oil
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Fry chopped tomatoes and onions with a little ground saffron
2. Put this mixture over the raw fish, adding chopped parsley, ground pepper, oil, salt and a little water
3. Cover and place on fire until the fish is well cooked
4. This broth can be varied by also adding fried garlic with the tomato, and then adding a little clove and pepper
