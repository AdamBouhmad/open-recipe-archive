## Ingredients

- squabs
- lard
- salt
- roasted tomatoes
- toasted almonds
- fried bread in lard
- pepper
- cloves
- capers
- cooked and shredded onions
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano
- squab cooking broth

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs and fry them in lard with salt
2. Remove from the frying and add roasted tomatoes, toasted almonds, fried bread, pepper and cloves, all ground
3. Add capers, cooked shredded onions, oil, vinegar and oregano
4. Place the squabs on top
5. Pour their cooking broth over them
6. Let simmer until the sauce reaches a good consistency and regular texture
