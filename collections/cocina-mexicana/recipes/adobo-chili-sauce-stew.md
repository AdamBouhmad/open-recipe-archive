## Ingredients

- mulato chile
- tomato
- pork meat
- garlic cloves
- cumin, ground
- salt
- plantain
- bread, fried
- onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Devein and soak mulato chile
2. Grind it with a little tomato
3. Fry it and add a little water and salt
4. Add the meat, garlic cloves, and ground cumin
5. Let it season until it is almost dry
6. Garnish with fried plantain, fried bread, and sliced onion
