## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- hoja de parra (grape leaf)
- jamón (ham)

## Directions

1. After emptying and passing through flames, not only to burn the down but also to make the meat somewhat firm, clean and tie them
2. Place a grape leaf on them, if available, with a ham slice, and put them in the oven, half an hour being enough for their complete cooking
