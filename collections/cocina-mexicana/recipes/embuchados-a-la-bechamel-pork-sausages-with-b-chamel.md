## Ingredients

- butter
- ham slice
- three shallots
- garlic head
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- two cuartillos of cream
- milk
- breadcrumbs or grated bread
- chicken breast strips
- pork lard
- fatty pork meat
- beef suet
- six raw egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Sweat butter, ham slice, three shallots, garlic head, parsley, thyme, bay leaf and basil in a pan for a quarter hour
2. Add two cuartillos of cream and reduce by half
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Boil milk with a handful of breadcrumbs until absorbed
5. Mix with strips of breast, lard, fatty pork meat and beef suet, six raw egg yolks, salt, pepper, spices and nutmeg
6. Fill tripe with this mixture
7. Form the sausages
8. Cook in equal parts milk and broth with a bundle of herbs
9. Proceed as described for pork sausages
