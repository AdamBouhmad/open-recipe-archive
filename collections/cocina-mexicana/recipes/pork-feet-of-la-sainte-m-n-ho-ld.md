## Ingredients

- pork feet
- long linen ribbon
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsnips
- onions
- clove
- parsley
- brine
- white wine
- warm butter
- coarse pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bind the pork feet with a long linen ribbon to prevent them from falling apart while cooking
2. Place in a casserole with thyme, bay leaf, parsnips, onions, clove, parsley, some brine, and about half a bottle of white wine or less, as they must remain on the fire for a long time and need plenty of broth
3. Cook over low heat for twenty-four hours without interruption
4. Let them cool in their own broth
5. Carefully unbind the ribbon and leave untouched until the next day
6. Shortly before serving, coat with warm butter, season with coarse pepper, and roll in breadcrumbs
7. Put on the grill over very gentle heat
8. Serve without sauce
