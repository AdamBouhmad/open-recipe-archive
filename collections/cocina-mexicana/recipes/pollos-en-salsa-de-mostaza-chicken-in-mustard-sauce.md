## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Garlic head
- Green chiles
- Parsley
- Mustard
- Ripe tomatoes
- Bread
- Lard
- Onion
- Salt
- Cloves
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with a peeled head of garlic, some green chiles, and a bunch of parsley
2. Break up a cup of mustard and wash it well so it is not too pungent
3. Grind it with ripe tomatoes and a slice of bread browned in lard
4. Chop onion finely and fry it well
5. Add all the ground mixture with appropriate salt
6. Once fried, add broth from the chickens' cooking and the chickens themselves
7. Cook over gentle heat so the sauce thickens well
8. Add a little ground cloves and pepper
9. Leave on the fire for another while
