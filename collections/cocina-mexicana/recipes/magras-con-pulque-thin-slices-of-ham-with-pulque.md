## Ingredients

- ham slices
- wine
- pulque
- bay leaf
- oregano
- thyme
- cumin
- garlic
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the ham slices in a pot with wine, pulque, bay leaf, oregano, thyme, cumin, garlic and vinegar
2. Cook the ham in the pot, covering it very well at low heat
3. When cooked, remove and drain
4. Grind sugar and dust the ham
