## Ingredients

- milk
- butter
- sponge cake, sliced and fried in butter
- curd cheese, crumbled
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almonds, cleaned and broken
- walnuts, cleaned and broken
- toasted sesame seeds
- syrup
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of less than medium point with milk instead of water
2. Butter a casserole dish
3. Alternate layers: one of sponge cake sliced and fried in butter, and another of crumbled curd cheese with raisins, pine nuts, almonds, walnuts, toasted sesame seeds, a bit of syrup and melted butter
4. Continue alternating until the last layer, which should be curd cheese
5. Cook over fire until done, so they remain very soft and not too dry
6. After they have rested, place a griddle with flame on top to brown them
7. Garnish on top the same way as was done internally on the curd cheese layers
8. Can also be made with fresh cheese in the same manner, or with syrup in water, following the same procedures
