## Ingredients

- quail
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- German sauce
- breadcrumbs
- grated cheese
- egg
- pepper
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Empty the quail and fill them with butter kneaded with lemon juice and salt.
2. Coat first in German sauce and cover with grated bread and cheese.
3. Roll in beaten egg and season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover again with grated bread and cheese.
5. Let them cook over low heat in butter.
6. Serve with tomato sauce.
