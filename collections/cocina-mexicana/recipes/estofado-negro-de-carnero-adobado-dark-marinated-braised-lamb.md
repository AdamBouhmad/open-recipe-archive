## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp portions
- Ground garlic
- White wine
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Almonds, ground
- Wine

## Directions

1. Stud the lamb portions as in the previous recipe
2. Place in a casserole with ground garlic, white wine, a little vinegar, and sufficient salt
3. Marinate from one day to the next
4. Cook
5. Serve with ground almonds dissolved in wine
