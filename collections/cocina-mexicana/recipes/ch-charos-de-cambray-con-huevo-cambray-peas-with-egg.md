## Ingredients

- cambray peas
- broth
- fried bread slices
- eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in broth, and when well cooked and tender, add fried toasted bread slices.
2. Break some eggs over them and season with salt and pepper.
3. Put over two gentle fires so they finish cooking evenly.
4. Serve as an intermediate course.
