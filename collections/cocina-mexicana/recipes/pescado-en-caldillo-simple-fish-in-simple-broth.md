## Ingredients

- white or red wine, or more frequently vinegar
- butter
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- fine pepper
- rosemary
- thyme
- bay leaf
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Prepare broth with wine or vinegar, butter, salt, cloves, cinnamon, black pepper, fine pepper, rosemary, thyme, bay leaf and fine herbs
2. Cook somewhat voluminous fish in the broth
3. Allow fish to cool
4. Place cooled fish on a napkin folded in many folds
5. Serve immediately with oil and vinegar or other sauces
6. Alternatively, boil all ingredients for several minutes to give more flavor and to soak the fish, especially if it is tender and cooks quickly
