## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges
- Salt water
- Sugar syrup (clarified, at low point)
- Coconut paste or almond paste

## Directions

1. Proceed as for whole bitter orange preserve
2. Cook the small wheel removed from the crown together with the oranges so it can be used to cover them after filling
3. Remove the segments whole, being careful not to damage the orange
4. When ready to serve, remove carefully from the syrup and fill with coconut paste, almond paste, or other suitable filling
5. Cover with the wheel cap, securing it with small cinnamon sticks
6. Place on a serving plate and pour the syrup over top
