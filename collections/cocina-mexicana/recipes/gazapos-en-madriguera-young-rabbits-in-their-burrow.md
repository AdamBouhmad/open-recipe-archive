## Ingredients

- 2 young rabbits
- rabbit livers
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- peppercorns
- concentrated broth
- white wine
- bouquet garni

## Directions

1. Stuff two rabbits with their own livers, parsley, onion, and mushrooms, all chopped
2. Mix with a piece of butter and season with salt and peppercorns
3. Sew closed and tuck the legs under the belly and the front legs under the neck, securing with skewers
4. Cook with concentrated broth, white wine, a bouquet garni, salt, and peppercorns
5. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve, skim fat, add a little broth or meat extract
6. Reduce to sauce consistency
7. Arrange the rabbits as if they were in their burrow
