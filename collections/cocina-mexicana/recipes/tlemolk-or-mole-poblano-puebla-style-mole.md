## Ingredients

- half a pound of ancho chiles
- half a pound of pasilla chiles
- lard
- about a handful or slightly less of cilantro
- half a quarter-liter of cooked tomatoes
- half a head of roasted garlic
- some pepper
- some clove
- some toasted sesame seeds
- meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast in lard half a pound of ancho chiles and another half pound of pasillas, taking care that they do not burn
2. Toast also in the lard about a handful or slightly less of cilantro
3. Grind both things together with half a quarter-liter of cooked tomatoes, half a head of roasted garlic and some pepper, clove and toasted sesame seeds
4. Fry the meat raw and then add it to the mole
5. Season with salt and leave to boil over regular heat until the meats are cooked
