## Ingredients

- chícharones (pork rinds)
- water
- lard
- onion
- garlic
- salt
- mulato chile
- ajo

## Directions

1. Clean and cook in plain water
2. Once cooked, drain the water and mince
3. Place in a clay pot with lard, finely chopped onion, garlic, and sufficient salt, being careful that they remain thick
4. Make a sauce with mulato chile and garlic, and eat with it
