## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- lard
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cook and fry the same as the stuffed trotters, with the difference that lard is used instead of butter
2. Serve garnished with fried parsley
3. The stuffed version can also be served this way, but without the filling inside in place of the bones, instead coating with it before dipping in the beaten egg
