## Ingredients

- cooked young pigeons
- lard
- thin onion slices
- garlic
- parsley
- sprig of marjoram
- salt
- flour
- clove
- cinnamon
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- Parras or Málaga wine
- raisins
- capers

## Directions

1. After cooking the pigeons, fry them in lard and remove them
2. Add to the fat thin slices of onion, chopped garlic and parsley, a sprig of marjoram and the corresponding salt
3. Once the mixture is fried, add flour browned in lard, clove and cinnamon powder, the pigeons and the broth in which they were cooked
4. Let them boil until somewhat dry and then add Parras or Málaga wine, raisins and capers, removing from the fire once it boils three or four times
