## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Thin ham for larding
- Thick ham for larding

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweetbreads as described in the preceding article by larding them with thin ham on top and thick ham underneath.
2. Place them in a casserole with the same ingredients as the previous article.
3. When almost cooked, remove the vegetables and let the broth simmer with the sweetbreads over high heat until it reduces to aspic.
4. Remove the meat and detach the aspic with a few spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, degreasing it.
5. Place the aspic underneath the larded sweetbreads, which should be jellied, arranging the vegetables around them.
6. Carefully prepare them with aspic to serve with any type of sauces or garnishes.
