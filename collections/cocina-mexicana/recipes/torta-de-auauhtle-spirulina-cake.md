## Ingredients

- Auauhtle (spirulina)
- Biscuit, ground
- Salt
- Eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Toast and grind the auauhtle according to the amount of cake desired
2. Mix with about one quarter part of ground biscuit
3. Season with appropriate salt and mix well with beaten eggs as if for frying
4. Cook in common mole or serve dry with sliced onion and a little lemon juice
5. Can also be mixed in small pieces with romeritos scramble
