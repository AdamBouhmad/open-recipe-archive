## Ingredients

- Blackberries
- Water
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Aguardiente

## Directions

1. Crush the berries as with any other fruit and measure the juice produced
2. Add for every quartillo one of water, two drams of cinnamon, one ounce of sugar, and half a cup of aguardiente
3. After one week filter and bottle
