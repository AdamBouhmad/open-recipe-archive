## Ingredients

- tortilla dough
- chicharrón (fried pork rind), ground
- chile pasilla (dried pasilla chile), toasted
- epazote (aromatic herb)
- salt
- manteca (lard)

## Directions

1. Make with tortilla dough and fill generally with ground chicharrón, toasted pasilla chile, a little epazote and salt.
2. Once the filling is placed on half of them, fold the other half on top and seal the edges.
3. Once done, fry in lard.
4. It is necessary to eat them hot so they do not become hard.
