## Ingredients

- raw young pigeons
- lard
- salt
- white wine
- sugar
- clove
- black pepper
- cinnamon
- garlic cloves
- ground almonds
- raisins

## Directions

1. Fry the raw pigeons in lard and add salt, white wine, a small piece of sugar, clove, pepper and cinnamon and let them boil until cooked
2. In a pot fry in lard some garlic cloves cut in pieces, remove them as soon as they brown, and place in the fat ground almonds
3. Pour over the pot of pigeons with its broth, to which raisins are added and let it thicken somewhat to serve
