## Ingredients

- 2 cuartillos custard (natillas)
- 2 cuartillos milk
- 5 ounces powdered sugar
- a little rennet dissolved in 3 tablespoons of milk
- 1 tablespoon orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Gently boil, stirring constantly, 2 cuartillos of custard, 2 cuartillos of milk, and 5 ounces of powdered sugar
2. Remove from fire and add a little rennet dissolved in 3 tablespoons of milk with 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water
3. Mix exactly, pass through a sieve, and pour the cream into a porcelain bowl set over hot coals
4. To make it velvety, cover with a lid or griddle with fire on top
5. Let cool and serve
