## Ingredients

- One pound of pineapple, ground and passed through a strainer
- one pound of clarified sugar at medium stage
- one pound of sweet potato

## Directions

1. Ground and pass pineapple through a strainer
2. For each pound of pineapple, use one pound of clarified sugar at medium stage
3. Keep the preserved pineapple separate, then mix with the sweet potato
4. Cook until it reaches a very high stage so it does not weep from the bottom of the molds where it is poured
