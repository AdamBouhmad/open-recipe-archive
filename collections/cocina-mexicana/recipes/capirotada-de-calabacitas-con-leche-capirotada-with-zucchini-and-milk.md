## Ingredients

- small zucchini
- water
- salt
- butter
- onion
- milk
- clove
- cinnamon
- bread toasts
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Slice small zucchini and cook in salted water.
2. Remove and dry with clean cloth, then drain in a sieve.
3. Make a sauce by frying thin onion slices in butter and adding milk seasoned with salt and a pinch of ground clove and cinnamon.
4. In a buttered pot, place a layer of toasted bread, then a layer of cooked zucchini slices covered with the fried onion and milk sauce, and on top another layer of sliced fresh cheese.
5. Continue layering in this order to fill the pot, with the final layer being cheese.
6. Cook over two fires and when thickened, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and brown again.
7. Can also be made with butter and a little sugar, omitting the salt.
