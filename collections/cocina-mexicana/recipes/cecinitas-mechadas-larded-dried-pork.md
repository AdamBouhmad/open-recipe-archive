## Ingredients

- Small pieces of cecina (dried pork)
- Water for soaking
- Fat ham
- Water, boiling
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Lard
- Almonds
- Bread
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cut small pieces of cecina and soak them, washing very well
2. Lard the pieces with fat ham
3. Place the cecinitas in a cazuela over the fire with boiling water, garlic, pepper and lard
4. When the cecinitas are tender, form a sauce with almonds and bread that have been ground, fried in lard, and seasoned
