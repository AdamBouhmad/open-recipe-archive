## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Tomatillos
- Roasted and peeled green chiles
- Aged cheese (grated)

## Directions

1. Make these eggs the same way as the previous ones, with the only difference being that instead of tomatoes, tomatillos are cooked and ground
2. If desired, add strips of roasted and peeled green chile
3. When serving, sprinkle with some grated aged cheese
