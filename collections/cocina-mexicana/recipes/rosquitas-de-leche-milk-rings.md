## Ingredients

- eleven quarts of milk
- two and a half pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Sweeten eleven quarts of milk with two and a half pounds of sugar
2. Strain it and put it on the fire until it reaches the point of candy
3. Remove it and extract through a syringe, immediately forming the rings
