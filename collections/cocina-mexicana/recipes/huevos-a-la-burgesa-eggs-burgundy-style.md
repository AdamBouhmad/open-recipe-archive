## Ingredients

- butter
- eight to ten eggs
- thin slices of fresh bread crumb
- thin slices of Gruyère cheese or other good fresh cheese
- salt
- nutmeg
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Spread butter the thickness of a knife blade on the bottom of the plate in which the eggs will be served.
2. Place very thin slices of fresh bread crumb all over, and other smaller and also very thin slices of Gruyère cheese.
3. Break eight or ten eggs over this.
4. Season with a little salt, nutmeg, and black pepper.
5. Cook at gentle heat on a stove.
