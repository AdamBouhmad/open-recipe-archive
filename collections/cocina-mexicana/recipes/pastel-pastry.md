## Ingredients

- four pounds of flour
- six eggs (three with whites and three yolks only)
- one cup of tequesquite water
- a small amount of Jerez wine
- a small amount of salt
- half of butter
- flour for dusting
- small pieces of butter
- minced filling with all its accompaniments

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, six eggs (three with whites and three yolks only), one cup of tequesquite water, a small amount of Jerez wine, a small amount of salt, and half butter very well
2. Extend with a rolling pin
3. Make a circle according to the size of the pan
4. Dust with flour and small pieces of butter and one sheet of dough
5. Continue layering until six sheets, with the minced filling and all its accompaniments on top
6. Place other sheets of dough on top and around the edges make pinches like an empanada
7. Place in the oven
