## Ingredients

- four regular hog legs, chopped coarsely
- six pounds of lard, chopped the same way
- three pounds of lard not fried, chopped the same way
- one pound of red chile, deveined and soaked, ground
- teeth of three large garlic heads, cleaned
- three ounces of toasted cilantro
- half ounce of pepper
- half ounce of claro

## Directions

1. Chop four regular hog legs to make a coarse chopped meat
2. Add six pounds of lard and three pounds of unfried lard, chopped the same way
3. Grind one pound of red chile (deveined and soaked) together with the cleaned cloves from three large garlic heads, three ounces of toasted cilantro, half ounce of pepper and half ounce of claro
4. Mix everything with the meat
5. Add three or four quartillos of wine
