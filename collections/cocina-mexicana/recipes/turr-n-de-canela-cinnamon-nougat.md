## Ingredients

- sugar
- virgin honey
- egg whites
- almonds
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- ground cinnamon
- hazelnuts
- raisins
- almonds for decoration

## Directions

1. Make it the same way as the white spoon nougat, with the difference that no sesame is added
2. When mixing the almonds, walnuts and pine nuts, add ground cinnamon in the amount necessary so that it has the smell, color and flavor of cinnamon
3. Do not beat as much as the other, hazelnuts can be added, and decorate with raisins and almonds
