## Ingredients

- manteca
- perejil picado
- cebollas picadas
- caldo de puchero
- tostadas de pan frio francés
- tomillo
- vino blanco

## Directions

1. Fry chopped parsley and onions in manteca
2. Add the necessary broth from the pot
3. When it begins to boil, arrange toasted slices of cold French bread
4. Add broth as the toasts are arranged
5. Then add a little thyme and sufficient white wine
6. This soup is served loose
