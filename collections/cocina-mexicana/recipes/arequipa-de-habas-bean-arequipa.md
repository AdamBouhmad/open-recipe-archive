## Ingredients

- 1 pound dried beans
- 5 pounds sugar
- 5 cuartillos milk
- 1 ounce starch
- 1 pound almonds

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of dried beans in warm water, clean them and place in cloth to cook.
2. Grind them well.
3. Dissolve five pounds of sugar in five cuartillos of milk and add one ounce of sifted starch.
4. Strain and cook with the beans.
5. After cooking, add one pound of almonds not too finely ground and cook to the proper point.
