## Ingredients

- Onion, sliced
- Garlic, crushed
- Tomato, sliced
- Pepper
- Clove
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Oregano
- Lamb ribs
- Salt
- Lemon
- Ham
- Red wine

## Directions

1. Fry sliced onion, crushed garlic, sliced tomato, pepper, clove, thyme, laurel, and oregano.
2. Prepare the ribs with pepper, salt, and lemon, fry them separately until just white on both sides.
3. Grind the fried mixture, pass through a fine sieve, and add the sauce where the ribs are frying along with a piece of ham and a small glass of red wine.
