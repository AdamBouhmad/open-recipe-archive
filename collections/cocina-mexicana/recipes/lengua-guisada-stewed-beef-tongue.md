## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- onion
- roasted garlic head
- peppercorns
- cumin
- parsley
- bread piece, soaked
- lard
- oil
- green chile slices
- water
- vinegar
- cooked beef tongue

## Directions

1. Grind tomatoes, onion and a roasted garlic head with peppercorns, cumin, parsley and soaked bread
2. Fry everything in lard and oil, adding green chile slices
3. Once fried, add a little water and vinegar
4. Add the cooked, peeled and sliced tongue to cook
