## Ingredients

- chayotes
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked and peeled chayotes
2. Season with salt, ground pepper and dry oregano powder
3. Put in plates and add corresponding oil and vinegar
