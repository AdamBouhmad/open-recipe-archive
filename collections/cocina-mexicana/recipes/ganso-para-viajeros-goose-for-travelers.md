## Ingredients

- Goose
- Butter
- Garlic, crushed
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Warm aguardiente

## Directions

1. To roast them, brush with a sauce made of butter, crushed garlic, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
2. After roasting, sprinkle them with warm aguardiente.
