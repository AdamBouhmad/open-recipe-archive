## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- one plate of finely ground rice powder
- ground sugar, as needed for sweetening
- egg yolks, as many as needed for color
- one handful of ground starch, molded and sifted
- ground almonds
- cinnamon stick
- a pinch of salt
- one or two lemon zests, washed

## Directions

1. Mix the finely ground rice powder and the necessary ground sugar with four quartillos of milk
2. Stir until the sugar dissolves
3. Dissolve as many egg yolks as needed for color in the same mixture
4. Add a handful of ground and sifted starch
5. Strain everything
6. Add ground almonds, cinnamon stick, and a pinch of salt
7. Place over heat and if it thickens too much when it begins to boil, add plain milk
8. Let it cook and add one or two washed lemon zests after it has boiled
9. Before it reaches the proper consistency, taste to see if the lemon flavor stands out and if sweetness is lacking, then add or omit lemon zest and sugar as needed
10. Allow it to reach a higher consistency than manjar
