## Ingredients

- Flour that fills both hands joined together, taken twice
- Six egg yolks
- A little salt
- Butter for frying
- Syrup for serving
- Sweetened paste for filling the cones

## Directions

1. Take flour that fills both hands joined together, taken twice
2. Mix it with six egg yolks and a little salt, until the dough softens
3. Roll out the cones or hojuelas to the size desired
4. Fry them in very hot butter
5. Serve them sprinkled with syrup
6. Fill the cones with sweetened paste that is most pleasing
