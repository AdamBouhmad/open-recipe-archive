## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- three cuartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with two pounds of sugar
2. Boil separately three cuartillos of milk and when it is very reduced, set aside and let cool
3. Once the syrup is also cold, stir one with the other
4. Return all to fire so that it boils and takes half the point of cajeta
5. Layer over sponge cake and adorn on top with dragée
