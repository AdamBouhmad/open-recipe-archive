## Ingredients

- one and one half pounds of almonds
- egg whites
- one and one half pounds of powdered sugar
- zest of one lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pound one and one half pounds of almonds and set to dry
2. The next day, grind them again in a mortar, adding egg white from time to time
3. Once they form a soft paste, beat it with egg whites, one and one half pounds of powdered sugar, and the zest of one lemon peel
4. Spread the paste on a wide flat spatula and form macarons the size of a small walnut
5. Bake in the oven at moderate heat like sponge cake, and glaze in the same manner
