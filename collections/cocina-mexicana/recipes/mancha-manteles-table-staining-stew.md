## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, ground without toasting, deveined and soaked
- peanuts
- lard
- salt
- cooked poultry or meat
- meat broth
- chorizo, sliced
- large plantain, sliced
- cooked onions
- fried and ground bread

## Directions

1. Grind together untoasted deveined and soaked ancho chiles with peanuts
2. Fry this mixture in lard with salt
3. Add the cooked poultry or meat, its broth, sliced chorizo and large plantain, cooked onions and fried ground bread
4. Let it simmer until it becomes somewhat thick
