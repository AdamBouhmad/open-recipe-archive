## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- fourteen eggs
- two pounds of sugar
- a little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Wash one pound of rice, put it in the sun, grind it and sift it through a bakery sieve
2. Once sifted, beat the egg whites first, then mix in the yolks and the rice
3. When everything is well mixed, pour it with a spoon like torrejitas into hot lard
4. Once they are cold, prepare two pounds of sugar made into a thin syrup with a little orange blossom water
5. Put the fritters in the syrup and let them boil
6. Set aside and let them settle until the next day, then return them to the heat so they reach the proper point
7. If they are dry and have absorbed all the honey, add more until it reaches the consistency of a preserve
8. Set aside, and when plating, sprinkle cinnamon on top
