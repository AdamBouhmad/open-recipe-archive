## Ingredients

- beef leg larded with fat ham
- garlic cloves
- cloves
- six large onions
- six dried pasilla chiles
- nutmeg
- Madeira wine or Jerez wine
- meat broth
- fine salt
- chicken breast
- Flemish or de la Barca cheese
- cooked borage
- fresh cheese curds or very thick cooked milk
- cinnamon
- two or three egg yolks
- broken pepper
- flour
- three fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stew of beef leg larded with fat ham, garlic cloves, and cloves
2. Place in a pot with six large onions, six dried pasilla chiles, grated nutmeg, a bottle of Madeira or Jerez wine, and sufficient meat broth seasoned with fine salt
3. Boil over high heat until it thickens well; then place over two very low heats until it darkens without burning
4. Add more broth and give it a couple of boils, then remove from heat
5. When cooled, strain through a cloth to degrease it
6. Form a filling with chopped chicken breasts, grated Flemish or de la Barca cheese, bundles of cooked and chopped borage, fresh cheese curds or very thick cooked milk, grated nutmeg, ground cinnamon, two or three egg yolks, and a little broken pepper
7. Mix all these substances well until a paste forms and season with fine salt
8. To a pound of flour add three fresh eggs and mix until forming a dough seasoned with fine salt
9. Form with a rolling pin a sheet as thin as paper, dusting with some dry flour
10. Place portions of the prepared filling on half the sheet at equal distance
11. Moisten the other half of the flour sheet by sprinkling it
