## Ingredients

- Sour cherries, pitted
- Juice of additional sour cherries or currants, for every twenty pounds of prepared cherries
- Half pound sugar or panela for each pound of the mixture

## Directions

1. Place pitted sour cherries in a vessel, taking care not to damage them and not to lose their juice
2. Add juice of other sour cherries or currants if available, proportioning for every twenty pounds of prepared cherries
3. Add half pound of sugar or panela for each pound of the mixture
4. Cook over high heat, stirring gently
5. When cooked and at proper consistency, remove from heat, let cool, and transfer to preserving vessels
