## Ingredients

- trout
- finely chopped parsley
- salt
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Remove strips from the trout, prepare them, and remove the skin from the scale side
2. Cut the strips into small pieces the size of a peseta or smaller, ensuring all pieces are the same length and thickness
3. Arrange in a sauté pan and sprinkle with finely chopped and well-washed parsley, salt, coarse pepper, and nutmeg
4. Melt a piece of butter and pour over the trout strips
5. At the moment of serving, fry over high heat, tossing constantly
6. Once golden and crispy on one side, turn the pieces, leaving them only an instant on the fire
7. Arrange in a heap on the plate and serve with thick or lean Italian sauce (see ITALIANA, page 430)
