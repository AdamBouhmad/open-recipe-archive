## Ingredients

- Pork loin, two pieces
- Pork head meat, one piece
- Pork feet, six to eight, cleaned
- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Tomato, chopped
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Clove, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Sesame seed
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Cook two pieces of pork: one of common loin and another of head meat, as well as six to eight cleaned pork feet
2. Once cooked, debone the feet and cut into thick pieces
3. Chop the cooked meat again and mix with the pieces of feet
4. Chop garlic, onions and a little tomato, fry them and add the chopped meat with a little vinegar and salt
5. Add ground clove and pepper, sesame seed and very finely chopped parsley
6. Leave everything to season over the fire until it becomes almost dry
7. Mix the seasoned mixture with beaten eggs
8. Put it in a buttered baking dish and place it at two fires until the cake sets
9. Serve with the sauce from the previous recipe
