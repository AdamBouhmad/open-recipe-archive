## Ingredients

- bagre
- aceite
- chiles anchos
- piñón
- almendras
- rabanitos
- cebolla
- aceitunas
- tornachiles

## Directions

1. Prepare the catfish by removing the skin and fry in oil
2. Remove and fry soaked and ground ancho chiles, pine nuts and almonds
3. Once this is done and not too thick, add the portions of catfish and cover with the adobo
4. Add oil and cook over two fires
5. Once the liquid has reduced, remove carefully and place on a plate covered with all the adobo
6. Garnish with chopped radishes, sliced onion, olives and quartered small chiles
