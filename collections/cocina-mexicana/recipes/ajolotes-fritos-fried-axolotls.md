## Ingredients

- Axolotls
- Salt
- Flour
- Oil or lard
- Vinegar
- Parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Skin the axolotls, wash them well and clean their innards
2. Cook them in salted water
3. Rinse and coat in flour
4. Fry in oil or lard
5. Serve with oil, vinegar, salt and sprigs of parsley
