## Ingredients

- roasted fowl
- béchamel sauce
- cream or broth
- fried eggs
- fried bread rounds

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the partridge substance, substituting the meat of other roasted fowl.
2. If after passing through the sieve it is too thick, a little boiled cream can be added.
3. Serve also with fried eggs on top and fried bread rounds around it.
4. It can be made with broth instead of cream, but then it will be less white.
