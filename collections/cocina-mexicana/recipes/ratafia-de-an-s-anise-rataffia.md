## Ingredients

- sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente
- four ounces of green anise
- one quarter ounce of dried anise, crushed
- two pounds of sugar
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of green anise and one quarter ounce of dried anise, crushed, in infusion for thirty days in sixteen cuartillos of aguardiente
2. Decant the liquor, strain it, dissolve two pounds of sugar in a sufficient quantity of water
3. Mix this syrup exactly with the aguardiente loaded with anise
4. Let it rest for a few more days, strain or filter through a cloth, and store in well-sealed bottles
