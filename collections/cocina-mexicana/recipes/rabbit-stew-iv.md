## Ingredients

- rabbit portions
- lard
- ham pieces
- onion, chopped
- garlic
- chorizo
- water
- thyme
- oregano
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- fried bread
- wine carlon
- olives
- chiles
- capers

## Directions

1. Once portions are clean, place in lard in a casserole with meat, ham pieces, chopped onion, garlic and chorizo
2. Fry and add water, thyme and oregano
3. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and a piece of fried bread, mix with the previous and let season
4. When serving sprinkle with wine carlon and garnish with olives, chiles and capers
