## Ingredients

- veal liver
- parsley
- small onions
- lard
- vinegar or wine
- spices
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut into slices and place in the pan with parsley, chopped small onions and lard; stir a little, and then add a spoonful of vinegar or wine, spices, pepper and salt
2. Let cook for a quarter hour and serve
