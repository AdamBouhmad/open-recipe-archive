## Ingredients

- eight wide chiles
- ox head
- ham
- fine salt
- chiles
- cumin
- bread crumb
- vinegar
- garlic
- cured beef fat
- thread

## Directions

1. Devein eight wide chiles and soak them, changing the water
2. Clean and dehorn an ox head well, turn it with jaws facing upward
3. Open with a knife the meat that joins the jaws to expose the tongue
4. Stuff the tongue with ham and coat the entire center of the mouth with fine salt
5. Grind the soaked chiles, adding cumin and a bread crumb soaked in vinegar
6. Dissolve this paste in good vinegar, add plenty of salt
7. Coat the entire head inside and out with this sauce, repeating two or three times as it dries
8. Leave it until the next day
9. On the day to be eaten, make a thicker sauce similar to the first
10. Cut the head meat with a knife, coat again inside and out with the sauce
11. Grind clean garlic and coat the head with the garlic paste, filling the openings
12. Cut thin slices of desalted cured beef fat, place some slices inside the mouth, cover all the meaty parts with the rest
13. Tie the slices with clean thread and put in the oven to cook slowly over gentle heat
14. When cooked, remove the cured beef fat slices and serve with a chilote sauce
