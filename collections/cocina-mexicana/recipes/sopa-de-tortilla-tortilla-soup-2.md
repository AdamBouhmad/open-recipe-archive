## Ingredients

- Lard
- Three garlic cloves, crushed
- Tortilla
- Tomato
- Onion, chopped
- Sufficient broth
- One sprig of epazote
- Pieces of ancho chile

## Directions

1. Heat lard with three crushed garlic cloves and fry the tortilla in it until it becomes stiff.
2. Remove the tortilla, fry chopped tomato and onion, and return the tortilla there with sufficient broth, one sprig of epazote and pieces of ancho chile on top.
