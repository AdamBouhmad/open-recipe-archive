## Ingredients

- pineapple
- roasted tomatillos
- garlic
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cilantro
- saffron
- vinegar
- sugar
- water from cooking the birds
- poultry
- salt
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Divide a peeled pineapple into two halves
2. Cook one half
3. Grind the other half with roasted tomatillos
4. Fry the ground mixture with minced garlic, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, cilantro, and saffron all ground together
5. Add the cooked pineapple in pieces, a little vinegar, sugar, and the water in which the birds were cooked
6. Cut the birds into quarters and place them in this sauce
7. Season with salt and thicken with toasted bread
