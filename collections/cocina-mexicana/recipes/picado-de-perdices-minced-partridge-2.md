## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Butter
- Chiles
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Flour
- Broth
- White wine
- Toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Remove membranes and tendons, then roast
2. Mince the meat very finely
3. Brown in a pan with butter, chiles, parsley and garlic
4. Add a spoonful of flour
5. When well mixed, add broth and white wine
6. Cook the mixture without boiling
7. Serve with slices of toasted bread
