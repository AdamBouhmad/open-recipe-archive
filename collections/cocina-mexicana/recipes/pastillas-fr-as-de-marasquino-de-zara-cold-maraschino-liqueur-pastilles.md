## Ingredients

- one pound superfine sugar
- four ounces very white powdered gum arabic
- good maraschino spirit of Zara

## Directions

1. Pound the sugar and pass through a silk sieve into a porcelain vessel
2. Dissolve the gum separately in hot water
3. When cold, pour over the sugar which will be further dissolved with good maraschino spirit of Zara
4. Dust slate slabs with sugar passed through a silk sieve
5. Drop the paste onto the slates in the shape of small sticks and cut into round pieces
6. When the slates are full, shake them against the table so the pieces spread and take the form of pastilles
7. Place in a stove with moderate heat
8. The next morning turn them with a thin knife to finish drying
9. Store in well-closed boxes
