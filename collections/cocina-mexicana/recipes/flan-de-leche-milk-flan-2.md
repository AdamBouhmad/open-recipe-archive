## Ingredients

- sixteen egg yolks
- one pound of white sugar
- one tablespoon of flour or starch
- one quarter pound of sugar for caramel
- one cuartillo of good milk
- lemon peel shavings or lemon essence
- optional: coffee for flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat sixteen egg yolks very well
2. Add one pound of white sugar and one tablespoon of flour or starch, beat until well mixed
3. Melt one quarter pound of sugar in a pot without adding water
4. When at the point of clear caramel, coat the interior of the pot with it
5. Add the yolks, one cuartillo of good milk and mix with the egg and sugar very slowly
6. Stir much with a wooden spoon
7. When well mixed, add lemon peel shavings or a few drops of lemon essence
8. Place on fire with a cover filled with embers on top
9. When set, remove and let cool, then place on the serving plate
10. If desired with coffee flavor, cook the milk first with a small bag of coffee, then remove it
11. To know if the flan is ready, insert a straw and upon removing it you will know how it is
