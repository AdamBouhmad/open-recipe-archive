## Ingredients

- one pair of panela (unrefined cane sugar)
- a little anise
- some cloves
- sufficient water
- twenty cuartillos of pulque

## Directions

1. Boil one pair of panela with a little anise and some cloves in sufficient water
2. Mix all in twenty cuartillos of pulque
