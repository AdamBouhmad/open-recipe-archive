## Ingredients

- blackfish
- parsley leaves or root
- two or three small whole onions
- bay leaf
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the blackfish, then cut off the head and tail.
2. Place them in a pot to boil gently for a quarter hour with parsley leaves or root, two or three small whole onions, a bay leaf, salt, and water.
3. After arranging them on a plate, take some of the good broth in a pot, tear apart many parsley leaves, and let it boil a few times.
4. Serve the blackfish by pouring this broth over them.
