## Ingredients

- one female duck
- her own liver
- two whole lemons

## Directions

1. Choose a female duck
2. Pluck and singe over paper flame
3. Remove the wings and neck
4. Cut the ends of the feet
5. Turn them outward and tie before placing on the spit
6. Rub with her own liver and wrap in paper
7. Remove the paper when almost roasted
8. Serve very hot with two whole lemons
