## Ingredients

- Three pounds flour with bran removed
- One and a half pounds of this amount
- Half hot cup caldron of yeast
- About a quarter cup cooking oil
- One ounce powdered sugar
- A little salt
- Two eggs
- Melted lard, not hot
- Rest of the flour

## Directions

1. Mix half the flour with the yeast, cooking oil, powdered sugar, a little salt and two eggs
2. Mix everything beating constantly
3. Add melted lard that is not hot and the rest of the flour
4. Soften with lard until the dough is very soft
5. Form the empanadas and put them in an oven heated as for baking sponge cake
6. Alternatively, they can be fried in lard and dusted afterwards with sugar
