## Ingredients

- wide chiles
- ripe tomatoes
- four tablets of chocolate
- cinnamon and clove
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame
- salt to taste
- water
- lard
- raw chickens

## Directions

1. Devein and wash wide chiles well, and grind them with ripe tomatoes.
2. Separately grind four tablets of chocolate and a little cinnamon and clove.
3. Combine this with the ground tomato mixture and add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, toasted sesame, and sufficient salt.
4. Increase this sauce with necessary water and pour it all into a cazuela coated with lard.
5. Fry the raw chickens in lard, add them to the sauce, and cook over two gentle fires until done, stirring occasionally with the cazuela.
