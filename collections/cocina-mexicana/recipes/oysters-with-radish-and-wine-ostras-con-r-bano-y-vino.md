## Ingredients

- oysters
- pungent sauce
- lard
- parsley
- mushrooms
- radishes
- flour
- broth
- wine
- breadcrumbs
- beef butter

## Directions

1. Open and detach the oysters from their shells
2. Cook for a few minutes in their own water, draining them immediately
3. Place in pungent sauce
4. Put a pan with lard over heat and add chopped parsley, mushrooms, and radishes
5. Add a tablespoon of flour and moisten with equal parts broth and wine
6. Let the sauce reduce as necessary
7. Select and clean the largest shells
8. Place four to six cooked oysters in each
9. Add the sauce and cover with breadcrumbs
10. Pour melted beef butter over them
11. Place on the grill over moderate heat, giving them color with the hot shovel
