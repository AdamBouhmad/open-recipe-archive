## Ingredients

- Small squash
- Raw oil
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose it small and beat it
2. Place in a casserole with raw oil, parsley, pepper, salt and a little lemon juice or in its absence a little vinegar
3. Place over two fires so the moisture is consumed
