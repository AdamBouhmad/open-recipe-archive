## Ingredients

- ham slices
- garlic
- onion
- tomato (xitomate)
- lard (manteca)
- flour
- pork broth or other meat broth
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- parsley
- mint (yerbabuena)
- pork or other meat for stewing

## Directions

1. Fry thin slices of ham in lard
2. Add minced garlic, onion, and tomato
3. Add flour browned in lard
4. Pour in the broth in which the meat was cooked
5. Season with ground clove, pepper, and saffron
6. Add minced or ground parsley and mint
7. Add the meats to be stewed
8. Let everything boil until the broth reaches the proper consistency
