## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads and thymus glands, portioned
- mushrooms
- ham
- butter
- mixed herbs
- substance and broth
- red sauce
- egg yolks
- beaten eggs
- essence (optional)

## Directions

1. Divide the portioned veal sweetbreads and thymus glands in half
2. Fry in butter with a mixed herb bundle
3. Moisten with substance and broth
4. When half cooked, bind with red sauce
5. Let the broth reduce until everything is dry and stir in three egg yolks
6. Place the mixture on each toast
7. Top with small egg pieces
8. Coat in beaten eggs
9. Fry until golden
10. Serve dry or with some essence
