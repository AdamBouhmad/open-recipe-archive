## Ingredients

- eel, divided into six-inch pieces
- thin ham
- marinade
- bread slices
- butter

## Directions

1. Divide the eel into six-inch pieces and stuff with thin ham
2. Place in a marinade and let drain
3. Fix on a spit, arranging a bread slice of the same size between each piece of eel
4. Roast while basting with butter
5. Serve when well cooked, with pepper sauce or without stuffing
