## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- two pounds of ricotta, ground
- twelve hard egg yolks
- pine nuts
- ground cinnamon
- mamey
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make medium point syrup with three pounds of sugar
2. Add two pounds of ricotta ground with twelve hard egg yolks and the pine nuts, also ground, necessary to form the paste
3. Place this over heat until it reaches point
4. Remove it then and mix with ground cinnamon
5. Place it on mamey, decorating with raisins, almonds and pine nuts
6. It turns out better if made with two pastes, the said one and another of almond, alternating both with the mamey
