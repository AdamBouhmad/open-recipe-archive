## Ingredients

- Boned and cooked poultry from the day before
- Vinegar
- Broth
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onions
- Garlic, crushed
- Bay leaf
- Flour
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare an adobo with vinegar, broth, salt, parsley, onions, crushed garlic and bay leaf
2. Place the pieces of boned and cooked poultry in the adobo
3. Keep the pieces in adobo for two or more hours over hot ashes
4. Remove and drain the pieces
5. Wrap them in flour
6. Fry them and serve with parsley
