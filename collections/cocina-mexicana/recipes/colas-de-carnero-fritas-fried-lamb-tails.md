## Ingredients

- lamb tails
- butter
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare as previously described and cut into pieces more or less thick
2. Pass through butter and then through eggs
3. Fry and surround with fried parsley
4. Instead of bread crumbs, it can be dipped in frying batter
