## Ingredients

- Veal leg muscles
- Strips of ham
- Garlic teeth
- Cloves of spice
- Lard
- Salt
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Lump of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the veal muscles of a proportional size
2. Separately lard them with strips of ham, garlic teeth and clove spices, with everything remaining very well packed
3. Place them in a pot with lard, salt, wine in sufficient quantity, little vinegar, water, and a lump of sugar
