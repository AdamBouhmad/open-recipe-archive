## Ingredients

- one quartillo of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- sweetener to taste
- orange blossom water
- a little cornstarch
- sponge cake
- toasted sesame
- almonds
- candied fruit
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Combine milk, yolks, sweetener to taste, orange blossom water and cornstarch stirring
2. Cook until thick like atole
3. On the platter place a layer of sponge cake and another of toasted sesame
4. Top with almonds, candied fruit and cinnamon
