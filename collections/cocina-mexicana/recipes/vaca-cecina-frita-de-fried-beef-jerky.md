## Ingredients

- beef jerky (cecina)
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- capers
- cooked lemon
- cooked chonzón meat
- flour
- butter
- small onions
- celery
- egg yolks
- broth
- parsley
- shallot

## Directions

1. Soak the jerky in water to desalt it
2. Rinse it well and soak again briefly in salted water with vinegar
3. Remove and dry with a clean cloth
4. Coat with oil and dust with ground cloves, pepper, and cumin
5. Decorate on one side with a row of capers, another of strips of cooked lemon, and another of cooked and crumbled chonzón meat
6. Roll very tightly, tie with thread, and place to cook in water with salt, vinegar, small onions, a piece of celery, and butter
7. Once cooked, slice the meat, coat the slices in flour, and fry in butter
8. In the broth left behind, whisk a mixture of three egg yolks, broth, parsley, and chopped shallot, and deglaze what may have stuck to the pot, leaving it to thicken over the fire
9. Add a splash of vinegar, some whole pepper and salt if the lard is not sufficiently salted or if ham has not been used in its place
10. Pour this sauce over the meat
