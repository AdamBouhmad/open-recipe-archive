## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- garlic
- onion
- pork meat
- bread
- clove
- cinnamon
- cooking oil
- oregano
- chipotles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the tomatoes very well and chop them finely together with garlic and onion and put to fry
2. Parboil some pieces of pork meat and add them
3. Toast a piece of bread, clove and cinnamon, grind and add it all together with a little cooking oil, a pinch of oregano and some chipotles in vinegar
