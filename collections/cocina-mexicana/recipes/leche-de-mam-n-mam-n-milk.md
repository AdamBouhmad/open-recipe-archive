## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- egg yolks
- cornstarch to taste
- mamón cake

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk to taste with egg yolks until it becomes yellow
2. Add a little cornstarch, strain, and place in a pot on heat
3. Bring to high point and remove from fire
4. Slice the mamón cake and layer alternately with the milk in serving plates while both remain
