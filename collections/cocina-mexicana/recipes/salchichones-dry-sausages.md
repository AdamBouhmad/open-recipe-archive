## Ingredients

- one arroba of pork meat
- a handful of marjoram
- thyme
- rice
- laurel leaves
- ginger
- nutmeg
- oregano
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- three quartillos of wine
- salt

## Directions

1. Take one arroba of pork meat and mince it well
2. Add a handful of marjoram, thyme, rice, laurel leaves, ginger, nutmeg, oregano, pepper, clove, cinnamon, three quartillos of wine, and salt
3. Let it infuse from one day to the next
4. Fill well-cleaned bull tripe
5. Tie them, hang them and let them dry
