## Ingredients

- kid goat (prepared as stuffed roasted kid goat)
- kid goat liver, cooked
- toasted almonds
- butter
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- ginger
- wine

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as stuffed roasted kid goat
2. After cooking as described, add a sauce made with the cooked liver of the same kid goat, ground with toasted almonds
3. Fry in butter, season with salt, clove, pepper, cinnamon and ginger, and moisten with wine
4. Return to the oven afterwards
