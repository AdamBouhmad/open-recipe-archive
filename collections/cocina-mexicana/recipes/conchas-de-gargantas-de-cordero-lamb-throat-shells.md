## Ingredients

- Lamb throats
- Water
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Bread crumbs
- Parmesan cheese
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak lamb throats in water to remove blood
2. Blanch them and cook in butter with lemon juice
3. Cut and prepare in blanquillo style (see cordero en blanquillo)
4. Place this stew in shells, sprinkling on top with bread crumbs mixed with a little Parma cheese
5. Sprinkle with melted butter
6. Brown under a bell oven
