## Ingredients

- Good healthy Muscat grapes
- Sugar
- Aguardiente
- Spirit of mace
- Nutmeg distilled
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg grain

## Directions

1. Select good healthy grapes, squeeze and crush them and press the juice in a fine clean cloth
2. Filter this juice through a cloth and dissolve sugar in it at the rate of four ounces for each two quartillos of liquor
3. Mix with as much aguardiente as there is grape juice
4. To aromatize it add a little spirit of mace and nutmeg distilled with a little cinnamon
5. Let the mixture rest for a long time before clarifying it, and if you want to give it more perfume add a grain of nutmeg
