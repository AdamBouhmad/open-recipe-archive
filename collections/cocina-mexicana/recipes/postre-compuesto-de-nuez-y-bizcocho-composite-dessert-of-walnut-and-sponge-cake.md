## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of ground walnut
- ten egg yolks
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- egg white
- cinnamon powder
- toasted and ground sponge cake
- butter

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with two pounds of sugar and mix with one pound of ground walnut, making a paste on fire like those of the previous articles
2. Before it is at point, remove it and add ten egg yolks, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, egg white, and cinnamon powder, and toasted and ground sponge cake as much as necessary to thicken, the same as if it had been brought to point on the fire
3. Put in a platter well buttered, which is moved in all directions so the sweet is flattened, bathe with more butter on top and put at two fires to brown
4. As it cools, powder with ground cinnamon
