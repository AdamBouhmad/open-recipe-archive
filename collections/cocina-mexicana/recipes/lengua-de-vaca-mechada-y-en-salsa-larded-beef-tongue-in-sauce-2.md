## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- cold water
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- red sauce or blonde sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in cold water for 24 hours, changing the water several times
2. Scald in boiling water and scrape to remove the skin
3. Lard with bacon strips seasoned with pepper, salt and chopped parsley
4. Let cook slowly for about 4 or 5 hours
5. Remove from heat, open it in the middle without dividing it, and place in the dish
6. Remove the fat from the cooking liquid and thicken with red sauce
7. Let thicken, or it can be replaced with a blonde sauce or tomato sauce
