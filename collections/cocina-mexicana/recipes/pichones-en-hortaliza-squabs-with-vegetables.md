## Ingredients

- squabs
- water
- salt
- ham pieces
- longaniza pieces
- sausage pieces
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- chopped parsley
- cooked and ground tomatoes
- cauliflower
- carrot
- beet
- small zucchini
- artichokes
- cloves
- pepper
- fried and ground bread
- oil
- capers
- caperberries
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs in salted water with ham pieces, longaniza and sausage
2. Fry them in lard, then remove from the fat
3. In the same lard, fry garlic, onions and a little chopped parsley
4. Fry cooked ground tomatoes, then add cooked and chopped cauliflower, carrot, beet, small zucchini and artichokes
5. Place the squabs with their broth in this mixture, add cloves, pepper and fried ground bread
6. When removing from heat, add oil and garnish with capers, caperberries, small chiles and olives
