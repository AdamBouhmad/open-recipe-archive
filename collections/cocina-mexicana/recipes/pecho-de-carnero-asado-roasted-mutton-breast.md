## Ingredients

- mutton breast
- ham slices
- onion quarters
- carrot slices
- fine herbs
- pepper
- salt
- bones

## Directions

1. Line a cooking pot with slices of ham
2. Place the mutton breast on top
3. Add quarters of onion and slices of carrot, a small bundle of fine herbs, pepper and necessary salt
4. Cook over gentle fire
5. When cooked, remove all the bones and serve
