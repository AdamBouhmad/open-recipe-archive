## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Onions
- Peas
- Green beans
- Cabbage
- Butter
- Sausage
- Sausages
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Turn carrots and turnips to the size of the mold in which the dish is to be made, ensuring they are all the same thickness
2. Parboil the partridges and then cook them in concentrated broth
3. Cook the onions in the same manner
4. Cook the partridges as explained in the article on partridges with cabbage
5. Prepare the mold or dish by buttering it
6. Form a design on the bottom with the turnips and carrots cut into strips so they can be arranged, as well as with small onions and peas, and green beans according to taste
7. Arrange alternately on the sides of the mold carrots and turnips
8. Put a light layer of cooked and drained cabbage over the entire mold
9. Arrange the partridges in the middle on the breast, surrounding them with sausage pieces and sausages at intervals
10. Add more cabbage on top until the mold is full
11. Two hours or more before serving, heat the charterhouse in a bain-marie with hot coals around it
12. When bringing to the table, press gently so it drains, pressing it with a lid and taking care not to break it
13. Turn carefully onto the plate in which it is to be served, pouring very little sauce over it
14. If any of the design has broken, repair it as best as possible; some cooked lettuce may be mixed with the cabbage if desired
