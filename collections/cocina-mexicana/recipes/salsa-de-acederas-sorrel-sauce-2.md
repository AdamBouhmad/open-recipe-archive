## Ingredients

- Some sorrel leaves
- A small piece of fried bread
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Bread or toasted flour
- Pepper
- Clove

## Directions

1. Take some sorrel leaves, a small piece of fried bread, parsley, garlic and cumin and grind
2. After being well ground and made into a paste, dissolve in pot broth
3. Thicken with bread or toasted flour
4. Some also add pepper and clove to this sauce
5. Give everything a few boils
