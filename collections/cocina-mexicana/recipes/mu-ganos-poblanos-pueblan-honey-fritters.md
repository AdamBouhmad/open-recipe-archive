## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- thirty egg yolks
- anise powder, the amount that fits between three fingers
- half a small cup of settled tequesquite water
- three tablespoons of brandy
- lard
- three pounds of sugar
- one and a half pounds of sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Put one pound of flour in a pan with thirty egg yolks
2. Add the amount of anise powder that fits between three fingers
3. Add half a small cup of settled tequesquite water and three tablespoons of brandy
4. Knead and if it remains loose, add flour until it does not stick to the hand
5. Beat a lot on a metate
6. Make long thin sticks that are wound in the hand
7. Fry in lard, making the sticks on a table
8. Make a syrup with three pounds of sugar and one and a half pounds of sugar
