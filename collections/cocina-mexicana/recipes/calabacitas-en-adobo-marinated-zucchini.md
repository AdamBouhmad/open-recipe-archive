## Ingredients

- calabacitas
- agua
- sal
- chiles anchos
- ajos
- cominos
- vinagre
- orégano
- cebolla

## Directions

1. Cook small zucchini in salted water
2. Marinate with well-deveined, soaked and ground ancho chiles with garlic and cumin, loosened with vinegar, seasoned with salt and oregano
3. Coat the zucchini with the adobo, leaving it thick
4. Add onion if desired
