## Ingredients

- twelve egg whites
- sixteen egg yolks
- one pound flour

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites and sixteen egg yolks until the mixture is white and thick
2. Add one pound of flour little by little so that it does not become lumpy
3. Shape the mamones into long shapes like ladyfingers or round
4. To make a crust, beat eighteen eggs and pour over them with ground sugar
5. Put in the oven until they toast slightly
