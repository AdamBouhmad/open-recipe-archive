## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Lard
- Butter
- Eating oil
- Onions
- Garlic cloves
- Bread
- Red wine
- Peppercorns
- Whole cloves

## Directions

1. Kill and clean the squabs in the dry
2. Prepare a pan with lard, butter, and eating oil
3. Cut squabs into quarters
4. Cut onions into wedges and separate garlic cloves
5. Brown these together with the squabs
6. Brown a piece of bread separately in only lard
7. Grind the bread together with the browned onion and garlic cloves
8. Dissolve this mixture in the pan where the squabs were browned
9. Add a little red wine to make a sauce, which should be neither thick nor watery
10. Place the squabs on top of the sauce
11. Add whole peppercorns and whole cloves
12. Leave at two low fires until cooked
