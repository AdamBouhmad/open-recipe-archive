## Ingredients

- pork loin
- onions
- garlic
- ham pieces
- peppercorns
- water
- vinegar
- orange juice
- wine
- bay leaves
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- raw onion
- oil
- ground black pepper

## Directions

1. After washing, lard the pork loin with onions, garlic, small pieces of ham, and peppercorns, all raw
2. Place in a pot with little water, vinegar, orange juice, wine, bay leaves, peppercorns in grain, and little lard
3. Cover the pot with a comal until the broth has been consumed, turning from time to time so it browns evenly
4. Serve in slices with cold sauce made from roasted and ground tomato, finely minced raw onion, vinegar, oil, and a little ground pepper
