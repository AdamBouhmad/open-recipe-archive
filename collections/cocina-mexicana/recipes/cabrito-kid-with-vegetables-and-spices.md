## Ingredients

- kid (cabrito)
- lard (aceite)
- onions (cebollas)
- carrots (zanahorias), whole
- thyme (tomillo)
- marjoram (mejorana)
- water
- salt
- flour
- pepper
- cinnamon (canela)
- egg yolks, raw

## Directions

1. Fry the kid in lard and add to the meat at the same time onions and whole carrots, thyme, marjoram, water, and salt. When it is half-cooked, dilute flour in water with a little pepper and cinnamon and add it to a casserole. When already cooked, add some raw egg yolks which should be stirred for a while.
