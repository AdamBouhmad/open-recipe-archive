## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Green chiles
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Lard
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Olives
- Tornachiles
- Red wine

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens, then debone them and cut into good-sized pieces
2. Fry tomatoes, garlic, and green chiles, all chopped
3. Season with salt, pepper, cloves, cinnamon, and saffron, all ground
4. Grease a pot with lard
5. Make a layer of the fried mixture with raisins, almonds, olives, and tornachiles
6. Place another layer of chicken meat on top
7. Continue alternating layers until finishing with a layer of the fried mixture
8. Cook over two gentle fires until much of the broth is consumed
9. Shortly before finishing, add a quarter liter of red wine
