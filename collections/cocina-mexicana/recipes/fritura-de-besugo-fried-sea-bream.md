## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Fine salt
- Flour
- Egg
- Lard or oil
- Optional: oil and flour for light pebre sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning and gutting the sea bream well, rub it with fine salt and hang it in the shade for twenty-four hours.
2. The next day, remove the bones and cut it into small pieces.
3. Roll the pieces in flour, coat with beaten egg, and fry in lard or oil.
4. The fry can also be made in a light pebre sauce, made of the oil in which the fish is fried and a little flour incorporated into it.
