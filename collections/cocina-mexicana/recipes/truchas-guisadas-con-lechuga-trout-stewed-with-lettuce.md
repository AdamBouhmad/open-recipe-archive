## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- water
- flour
- oil
- onion
- parsley
- cooked lettuce hearts
- toasted bread
- cloves
- pepper
- hot water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the trout and divide into portions
2. Parboil the portions in salted water, remove them, coat in flour and fry in oil
3. Remove from the fat and set aside
4. In the same oil, add finely chopped onion, ground parsley and cooked, chopped lettuce hearts
5. When this mixture is fried, add toasted bread ground with cloves and pepper, hot water and a little vinegar
6. Let the sauce boil and season; then add the trout portions to boil once in it
