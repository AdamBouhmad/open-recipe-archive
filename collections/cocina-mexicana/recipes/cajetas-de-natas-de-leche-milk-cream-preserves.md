## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of clarified sugar at medium point
- 1 pound of white camote, ground
- half a pound of fine almonds
- half a coconut
- one real of milk cream (natas de leche)

## Directions

1. Add the ground white camote, fine almonds, and half coconut to the clarified sugar at medium point
2. Once everything has reached the consistency of cajeta, add one real of milk cream
3. When it reaches the point of detaching from the pan, remove from heat and fill plates or cajeta molds
