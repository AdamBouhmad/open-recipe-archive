## Ingredients

- one full tablespoon of chopped parsley
- half a tablespoon of shallots
- mushrooms (equal amount to shallots)
- half a bottle of white wine
- butter equal to one egg
- two ladle-fulls of curled sauce
- one ladle-full of concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one full tablespoon of chopped parsley, half a tablespoon of shallots, and another equal amount of finely chopped mushrooms
2. Add half a bottle of white wine and butter equal to one egg
3. Let everything boil until much has been consumed
4. When the saucepan begins to dry, add two ladle-fulls of curled sauce and one of concentrated broth
5. Let the sauce boil over a rather strong fire
6. Skim and degrease the sauce carefully
7. When it has been reduced to a good point, meaning when it is as thick as a light broth, remove from fire
8. Pour into another saucepan and keep hot in a water bath
