## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Cooking broth
- Lard
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Capers
- Tornachiles
- Water
- Vinegar
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens and break into pieces in a casserole with a little of the broth in which they cooked
2. Add lard, clove, cinnamon, a little pepper, capers, tornachiles, a little water, and a little vinegar
3. Let boil so everything incorporates
4. Add six beaten egg yolks for each chicken, stirring until the egg is cooked, without adding the whites
