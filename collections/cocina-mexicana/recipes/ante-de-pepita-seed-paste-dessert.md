## Ingredients

- Seeds
- Syrup at medium point
- Almonds, crushed
- Wine
- Spices
- Sponge cake
- Pastille topping

## Directions

1. Clean the seeds and wash them very well
2. Grind the seeds well
3. For one pound of seeds, use four ounces of crushed almonds and the seeds finely ground
4. Make syrup at medium point and cook until thickened as desired
5. Add wine and all spices
6. Alternate layers of sponge cake and this paste
7. Top with pastille
