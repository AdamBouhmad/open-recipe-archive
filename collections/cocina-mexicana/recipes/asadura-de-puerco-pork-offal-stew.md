## Ingredients

- pork offal
- water
- onion
- garlic
- tomato
- parsley
- butter
- ham pieces
- pork ribs
- oregano
- thyme
- clove
- cinnamon
- cumin
- vinegar
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut the offal into small pieces and soak in water to remove grease
2. Chop onion, garlic, tomato and parsley very finely
3. In a casserole put everything together with butter, ham pieces and pork ribs and parboil
4. Add oregano, thyme powder and ground spices of clove, cinnamon and cumin
5. When fried add sufficient water to cook and Castilian vinegar
6. Can be left in broth or adobed
7. Serve with chiles in vinegar, olives and capers
