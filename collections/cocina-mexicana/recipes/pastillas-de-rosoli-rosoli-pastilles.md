## Ingredients

- one pound superfine sugar
- four ounces powdered gum arabic
- good rosoli spirit
- a little powdered carmine

## Directions

1. Perform the same operations as for the maraschino pastilles
2. Dissolve the powdered sugar with good rosoli spirit
3. Add a little powdered carmine to give the pastilles a rose color
