## Ingredients

- habas (fava beans)
- leche (milk)
- azúcar (sugar)
- agua de azahar (orange blossom water)

## Directions

1. Soak the fava beans, peel and cook them
2. Drain and grind them
3. Sweeten milk with one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of fava bean and strain
4. Then mix the ground fava bean and leave over heat until it reaches the paste point, which consists of the pan being clean when stirred, which must be done continuously when close to reaching the cooking point
5. When removed from heat add a little orange blossom water
