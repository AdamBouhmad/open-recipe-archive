## Ingredients

- Eggplants
- Cheese, grated
- Butter or oil
- Bread crumbs
- Egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Open the eggplants and remove the hearts
2. Chop the hearts very finely and mix them with grated cheese
3. Blanch the hollow eggplants in butter or oil
4. Fill them with the cheese mixture
5. Roll them in bread crumbs
6. Cover them with beaten egg
7. Fry them
8. Serve them as is or with broth or tomato sauce
