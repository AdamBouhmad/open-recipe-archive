## Ingredients

- Artichoke hearts, half-cooked
- Mushrooms, cut into pieces
- Livers, cut into pieces
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onion
- Garlic head
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Broth or white wine
- Egg yolk and cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a casserole with butter, a bunch of parsley, onion, garlic head, salt, pepper, and a handful of flour.
2. Moisten with broth or white wine.
3. After half an hour of cooking, skim off the fat and serve.
4. If instead of broth an egg yolk and cream liaison is added, the fry becomes very tasty.
