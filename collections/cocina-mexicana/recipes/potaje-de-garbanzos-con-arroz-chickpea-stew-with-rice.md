## Ingredients

- Chickpeas, well cooked with onion
- Oil
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Rice (one-sixth part in proportion to the chickpeas)
- Beaten eggs or bread soaked and dissolved in water
- A little clove
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickpeas as in the previous recipe
2. After cooking, add one-sixth part of rice in proportion to the chickpeas
3. Add rice before thickening the stew
4. Season with beaten eggs or bread soaked in water, clove and pepper
