## Ingredients

- Veal loin of twelve pounds
- Salt of cattle and salt
- Castilian vinegar
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Garlic cloves
- Laurel
- Peppercorns
- Cloves
- Onion
- Sour lemons
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Parsley
- French mustard
- Ground pepper

## Directions

1. Take a twelve-pound veal loin
2. Mix half a pound of cattle salt and one pound of salt, grind together
3. Rub the meat well and with a larding needle puncture the meat and introduce salt into all incisions made
4. Place the meat in a deep pot with a bottle of Castilian vinegar, thyme, marjoram, whole peeled garlic cloves, laurel, whole peppercorns of different sizes, cloves, whole onion, and half a dozen sour lemons cut in half
5. Cover the pot with cloth so the aroma does not evaporate
6. Leave for nine days, turning the meat several times
7. At nine days remove the meat and place sticks on the bottom of the pot with the meat on top so it does not touch the pot, add water and cook
8. When cooked, serve with salad or with the following sauce: boil eggs and chop very finely, also chop onion very finely and mix, add oil, vinegar, chopped parsley, French mustard, salt, and ground pepper
9. This meat keeps for three or four months
