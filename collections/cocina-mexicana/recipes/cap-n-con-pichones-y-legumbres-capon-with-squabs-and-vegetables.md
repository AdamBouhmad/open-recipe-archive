## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- good broth
- 3-4 pounds beef brisket
- 1-2 squabs
- lettuce
- roots

## Directions

1. Arrange and tie the capon, then place it to cook in good broth made with a piece of beef brisket of 3-4 pounds and 1-2 squabs, following common broth procedures.
2. Once the capon and squabs are cooked, serve them surrounded by lettuce and roots, cooked the same way as for lettuce soup or potage à la Chantilly.
3. Arrange the vegetables forming a little mound or thicket over the capon, and serve at the same time in a separate dish the broth in which the capon and meats were cooked.
