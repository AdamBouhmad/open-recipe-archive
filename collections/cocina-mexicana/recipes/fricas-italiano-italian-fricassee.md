## Ingredients

- Lard or butter
- Flour
- Clove
- Diced ham
- Garlic
- Onions
- Mutton or poultry meat, raw
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- Nutmeg
- Lemon or sour orange juice
- Egg yolks
- Warm broth

## Directions

1. Put lard or butter, flour, clove, and diced ham in a casserole
2. Brown the flour
3. Fry garlic and onions
4. Add broth
5. Add raw mutton or poultry meat and let cook on a gentle fire, seasoning with cinnamon, clove, nutmeg, and salt
6. When everything is cooked, thicken as follows: put chopped parsley and nutmeg in a pan with lemon or sour orange juice
7. Beat in the necessary egg yolks by hand, adding warm broth
8. When diluted, pour into the fricassee casserole and put on low fire, stirring
9. When ready, cover the fire well and add the cooked meat
10. For each pound of meat use six yolks and two lemons
