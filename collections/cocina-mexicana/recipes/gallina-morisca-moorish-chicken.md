## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Fatty ham
- Chopped onion
- Potatoes
- Lettuce hearts
- All spices
- Chicken broth
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Lard

## Directions

1. Roast a chicken and then cut it in quarters
2. Fry small pieces of fat ham with finely chopped onion
3. Add potatoes and lettuce hearts, and season with all spices
4. In the casserole put a layer of chicken and another of the mixture
5. Add enough of the broth in which the chicken was cooked to cover, with a little wine, vinegar and lard
6. It should boil twice and remain somewhat sour
