## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- orange blossom water
- lemon rind zest
- salt
- water
- apricot jam

## Directions

1. Make a kind of folded dough with flour, butter, orange blossom water, lemon rind zest, a little salt and water
2. Form small leaves with it
3. Place a little apricot jam between two of these leaves
4. Gild the pastry, fry in butter and glaze with sugar, passing a red-hot shovel over it
