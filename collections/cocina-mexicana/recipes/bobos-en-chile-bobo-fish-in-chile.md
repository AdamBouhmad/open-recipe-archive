## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic
- stale bread, crumbled
- ground chiles
- cooked fish
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry garlic in lard and add crumbled stale bread and ground chiles.
2. Add the fish cooked separately.
3. When serving, add oil and vinegar.
