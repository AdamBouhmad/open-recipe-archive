## Ingredients

- Beef palate
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Onion
- Parsley
- Hot sauce

## Directions

1. After cooking, drain well and let cool
2. When cold, place in a cold sauce made of oil, salt, pepper, one garlic clove, very finely minced onion, and a handful of chopped parsley
3. Leave for two hours in this sauce so it absorbs well, turning it frequently to absorb the flavor better
4. Then place on the grill like a beefsteak and serve with hot sauce
