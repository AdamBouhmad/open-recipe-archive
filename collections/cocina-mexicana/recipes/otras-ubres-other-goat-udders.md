## Ingredients

- goat udders (ubres)
- tomato (jitomate)
- onion (cebolla)
- garlic (ajo)
- parsley (perejil)
- clove (clavo)
- cinnamon (canela)
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar (vinagre)
- oil (aceite)
- chiles
- capers (alcaparras)

## Directions

1. Cook and slice them. Chop a little tomato without seeds, onion, garlic, and parsley also chopped. Fry first the tomato with the parsley, then the onion and then the garlic. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, and salt with a little vinegar. In this broth put the udders to boil, leaving them with very little broth. Add oil and garnish with chiles and capers.
