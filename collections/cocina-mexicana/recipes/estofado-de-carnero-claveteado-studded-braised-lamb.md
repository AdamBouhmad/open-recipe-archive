## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp portions
- Cinnamon strips
- Garlic cloves
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Ham
- Lard
- Strong vinegar
- Mixed ground spices (except cumin)
- Salt
- White wine
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Stud the lamb portions with cinnamon strips, garlic cloves, cloves, black pepper, and ham
2. Place in a pot with lard, strong vinegar, all ground spices except cumin, and necessary salt
3. Cover the pot and let the meat cook
4. When cooked, turn the pot into a casserole
5. Thicken the broth with toasted bread
6. Add a little white wine and give it one boil
7. Serve
