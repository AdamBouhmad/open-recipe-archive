## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- salt
- roasted tomatoes
- garlic
- parsley
- poblano chiles or Cuaresmeño chiles
- cloves
- pepper
- saffron
- water
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- oil
- cooked cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cut raw chickens into quarters and fry in lard with a little salt
2. Add roasted and ground tomatoes and chopped garlic and parsley
3. Let the seasoning macerate a little
4. Add sufficient water with ground cloves, pepper, and substantial saffron, adding whole poblano or Cuaresmeño chiles to cook in the same broth
5. Remove the chiles as soon as they are cooked before they burst
6. When the chicken is also cooked, mix egg yolks cut with vinegar into the broth
7. Stir constantly with a spoon so no lumps form
8. After a brief boil, add the cooked chiles, oil, and pieces of cooked cauliflower
9. Let it boil once more before removing from heat
