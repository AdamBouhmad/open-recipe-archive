## Ingredients

- pork head
- tongue
- salt
- herbs
- cloves
- pepper
- nutmeg
- vinegar
- wine of Jerez

## Directions

1. Cut the head into large pieces removing a piece of jowl if it were very large
2. Cook everything with the tongue with salt and aromatic herbs
3. When cooked but not too much so it does not fall apart, set aside, remove the bones and cut into small pieces
4. Do the same with the tongue
5. Grind well twenty-five cloves and thirty thin peppercorns, a regular piece and a small ground nutmeg
6. Mix from the metate with one cup of Castilian vinegar and another of wine of Jerez
7. Mix everything with the head already cut, and place in a pan, adding more salt if needed, ground
8. Parboil everything well and while hot place in a cloth bag a quarter in circumference so it comes out large, tie as tightly as possible and place to press with considerable weight on top so the fat comes out, making sure it comes out well rounded and the tied part remains in the middle
9. When cold, unsew the bag to remove it
10. If desired the feet can be added, only add more spices, wine and vinegar in proportion to the amount
