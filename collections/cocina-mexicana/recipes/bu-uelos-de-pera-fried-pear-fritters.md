## Ingredients

- pears, not green
- aguardiente (brandy)
- sugar
- frying paste or batter
- lard for frying
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take pears that are not green and remove the tops and bottoms
2. With a corer, remove the cores, peel and slice them to half a finger thickness
3. Soak in an infusion of aguardiente with sugar for some hours
4. Remove and dry with a clean cloth
5. Coat in the batter, fry like the plantains, and serve hot, dusted with sugar and cinnamon
