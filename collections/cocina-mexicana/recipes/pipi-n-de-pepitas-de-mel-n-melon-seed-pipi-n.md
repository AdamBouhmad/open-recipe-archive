## Ingredients

- melon seeds
- almonds
- cooked chicken breasts
- oil
- lard
- salt
- ground clove
- birds or meat

## Directions

1. Toast the melon seeds in a comal (griddle), grind them and strain through a sieve
2. Add almonds and cooked chicken breasts, grinding both together
3. Fry everything in oil mixed with lard
4. Season with salt and ground clove
5. Add the birds or meats to be cooked in this pipián, with a little of the broth in which they were cooked
6. Let them boil until the sauce becomes thick
