## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds
- water
- sugar
- bread

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of sweet almonds, leave them in water for an hour, blanch them, and crush them on the metate little by little so they are well ground
2. Dissolve them in two cuartillos of water and add sufficient sugar, which is a little more than half a pound, and once well dissolved, strain through a sieve
3. Soak the soups of good spongy bread, which are cooked over low heat
4. When serving them, sprinkle with a little cinnamon
