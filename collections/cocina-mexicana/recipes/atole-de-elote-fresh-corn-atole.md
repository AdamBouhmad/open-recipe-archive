## Ingredients

- fresh corn (elotes), raw and shelled
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Instead of dried corn, shell and grind raw fresh corn so that the kernel remains almost whole
2. Cook with water and a pinch of salt until it reaches the consistency of atole
