## Ingredients

- milk
- two egg yolks per cuartillo
- orange leaves
- clove
- cinnamon powder
- Malaga wine or other generous wine

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk and mix in egg yolks in the proportion of two per cuartillo
2. Strain and place over heat
3. Add orange leaves and allow to boil until it thickens
4. Add clove for flavor and cinnamon powder for color
5. Remove the orange leaves and add a little Malaga wine or other generous wine
6. When the milk returns to the proper point, pour into serving dishes
