## Ingredients

- onion
- garlic
- cloves
- pepper
- white bread
- vinegar
- fish, cooked beforehand
- chiles
- capers

## Directions

1. Mince onion and garlic finely
2. Fry cloves and pepper together with them
3. Separately fry a little white bread and soak it in vinegar
4. Grind it and combine with what is frying
5. The fish should be cooked beforehand; cut it into pieces and add it to the mixture
6. Let it season and decorate with chiles, capers, etc.
