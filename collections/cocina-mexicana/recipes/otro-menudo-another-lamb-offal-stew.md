## Ingredients

- Cooked and cut lamb offal
- Butter
- Two tomatoes
- Three bunches of onions in cloves
- Parsley
- Flour
- Offal broth
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Saffron threads
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. With the offal well cut and cooked, chop it and place in a pot with butter
2. Chop two tomatoes and three bunches of onions separated into cloves and fry
3. Add chopped parsley, a little flour and fry again
4. Add some offal broth
5. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper with some saffron threads and cook
6. The sauce should be thick
7. Garnish the stew with chiles, olives and capers
