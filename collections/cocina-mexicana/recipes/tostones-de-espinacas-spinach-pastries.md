## Ingredients

- cooked spinach
- fresh butter
- lemon rind
- bitter almond biscuits
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar the cooked spinach with fresh butter, lemon rind, some bitter almond biscuits, sugar and orange blossom water
2. Fill the pastries with this and make them like the others
