## Ingredients

- tender squash
- water
- salt
- bread crumb
- milk
- hard egg yolks
- grated cheese
- peeled almonds
- cloves
- beaten egg yolks
- spices
- butter
- juice

## Directions

1. Choose the most tender squash, cook for a quarter hour in water and salt, then remove the insides.
2. Soak bread crumb in milk, squeeze well, and add two hard egg yolks, two ounces of grated cheese, five peeled almonds, two cloves, two beaten egg yolks, salt, and spices.
3. Chop and mix everything together, pass the well-filled squash through butter and serve with some juice.
