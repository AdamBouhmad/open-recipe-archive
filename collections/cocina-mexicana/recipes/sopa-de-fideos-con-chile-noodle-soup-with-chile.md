## Ingredients

- chile ancho
- ajo
- pimienta
- clavo
- cominos
- fideos
- caldo suficiente
- queso en polvo

## Directions

1. Devein a little ancho chile and soak it to reduce the spiciness
2. Grind it with garlic cloves, pepper, clove and what fits in two fingers of cumin
3. When everything is ground, fry the chopped garlic cloves and the other ground things with sufficient broth
4. When the noodles have cooked, place them in the steam of a pot, garnishing and adding the powdered cheese
