## Ingredients

- tuna
- lard
- very ripe tomatoes
- a little vinegar
- good vinegar
- a piece of sugar or piloncillo
- water
- ground cinnamon
- wine from Parras or Muscatel
- parsley
- four to six cooked egg yolks
- pepper
- garlic
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash the tuna and desalt if it was salted
2. Debone as much as possible
3. Cut into portions two to three fingers thick
4. Fry in lard until it begins to brown
5. Roast very ripe tomatoes and grind with a little vinegar
6. Add the ground tomato to the frying tuna
7. Once it has fried a little, add good vinegar and a piece of sugar or piloncillo
8. Add a little water and ground cinnamon
9. Let boil until all the tomato broth is consumed and everything is fried in the remaining lard for a while
10. Add a little wine from Parras or Muscatel
11. Serve cold or hot with the following sauce: grind together a handful of parsley, four to six cooked egg yolks, and a little pepper
12. Fry peeled and chopped garlic in oil until browned and set aside
13. In the same oil, fry the parsley and all the ground mixture
14. Add water in which some fish has cooked, a little good vinegar, and sufficient salt
15. When the sauce has seasoned and is thick, pour over the tuna
16. Place the fried garlic and raw oil on top
