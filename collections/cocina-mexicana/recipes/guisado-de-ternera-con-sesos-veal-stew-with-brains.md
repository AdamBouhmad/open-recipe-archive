## Ingredients

- carne de ternera (veal)
- mantequilla (butter)
- cebolla rebanada (sliced onion)
- zanahoria picada (chopped carrot)
- tiras de jamón (strips of ham)
- laurel machucado (crushed bay leaf)
- clavo (clove)
- pimienta (pepper)
- limón (lemon)
- mejorana (marjoram)
- tomillo (thyme)
- caldo del puchero (pot broth)
- sesos (brains)
- alcaparras (capers)
- aceitunas (olives)
- chiles en vinagre (chiles in vinegar)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and coat a frying pan with butter
2. Add the meat with sliced onion, chopped carrot, strips of ham, crushed bay leaf, clove, pepper and a few drops of lemon, marjoram and thyme
3. Once everything is in the pan place little balls of butter on top
4. Add pot broth and as it dries add more little by little until it has enough to cook and remain dry
5. Cook some brains separately, chop them and add them to the stew to serve
6. Garnish with capers, olives and chiles in vinegar
