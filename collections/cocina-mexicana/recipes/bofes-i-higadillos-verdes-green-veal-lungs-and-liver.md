## Ingredients

- Veal lungs
- liver
- salt
- butter
- one large onion
- parsley
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- two or three green chiles
- a piece fried
- broth
- two or three egg yolks
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the veal lungs and liver with salt and butter
2. Chop finely
3. Slice a large onion and fry until not too toasted
4. Remove and add the chopped lungs and liver to the butter
5. Grind together parsley, pepper, clove, cinnamon, two or three green chiles and a fried piece
6. Fry everything in broth with two or three egg yolks and a bit of oil
7. The broth should be between thick and watery
