## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Milk
- Sugar
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks in milk until it has more or less color, as desired
2. Sweeten well so it is pleasant to the taste
3. Strain and put to boil
4. Add a little fresh butter
5. Remove once it has the consistency of atole or porridge
6. Allow to cool and put in the sorbetera to freeze
