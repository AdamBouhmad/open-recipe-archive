## Ingredients

- milk
- carrot slices
- butter or margarine
- flour
- cooked chicken breasts, finely minced
- thyme
- salt

## Directions

1. Bring milk to a boil in a quantity proportioned to the boxes to be filled, with carrot slices, which are removed from the milk as soon as they have imparted their flavor, then removed from the heat.
2. Place a pan on the fire with butter or margarine, and add flour in proportioned quantity, and before it browns, gradually incorporate the milk and very finely minced cooked chicken breasts that have been prepared beforehand, taking care that they remain quite white.
3. Add a little crumbled thyme with the necessary salt, and let everything cook together.
4. When this béchamel is well seasoned, fill the fried boxes with it, which covered with their lids are brought to the table on a platter.
