## Ingredients

- ricotta cheese
- sifted sugar to taste
- raisins
- almonds
- walnut pieces
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame
- 9 egg yolks for each pound of ricotta
- ground biscuits as needed for body
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the ricotta with sifted sugar to taste and mix in raisins, almonds, walnut pieces, pine nuts, and toasted sesame
2. Make a clarified syrup at high point with 3 and 1/2 pounds of sugar, and off heat mix in 1 pound of ground ricotta, 7 cooked and ground egg yolks, 1 cup of clean pine nuts, cinnamon and clove to taste
3. Beat so it thickens a little and pour into a dish
4. Weigh the resulting paste to add 9 well-beaten egg yolks for each pound of ricotta
5. Add ground biscuits in the necessary quantity to give it body
6. Pour into a dish greased with butter
7. Stir in all directions so the dessert flattens and evens out
8. Grease with butter on top and set it to set and brown at two gentle heats
9. After it cools, dust with ground cinnamon
