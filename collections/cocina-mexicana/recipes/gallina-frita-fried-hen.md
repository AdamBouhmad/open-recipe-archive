## Ingredients

- hen
- lard
- salt
- wide chile
- onions
- garlic with skin
- optional: sherry and ground fine spices, less saffron and cumin

## Directions

1. Clean the hen and fold the feet in the customary way
2. Fry it raw in a small amount of lard
3. Once well parboiled, pour water into the pot until the hen is completely covered
4. Add a little salt, one wide chile, a couple of whole cleaned onions, and one garlic clove with skin
5. Let it boil at gentle heat, taking care to add a little hot water if much is consumed, until it is cooked
6. When cooked, let all the water be consumed and brown it in the fat left in the pot, being careful to remove the garlic, onion and chile when it is finishing cooking
7. If desired, when it is cooking down, a little mild sherry and ground fine spices can be added, with less saffron and cumin
