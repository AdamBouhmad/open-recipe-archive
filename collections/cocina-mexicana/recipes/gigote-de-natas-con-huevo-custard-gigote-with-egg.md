## Ingredients

- two chicken breasts
- two quarts of milk
- eight egg yolks
- butter
- sponge cake (mamón)
- custard (natillas)
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg yolks beaten with sugar
- cinnamon
- bread

## Directions

1. Roast the two chicken breasts raw
2. Mix two quarts of milk with eight egg yolks
3. Chop the chicken breasts finely and add to the milk
4. Grease a platter with butter and layer: sponge cake, then chicken and milk mixture, then custard composed with sugar and cinnamon, and finally chicken again
5. Place over hot coals and cover with a griddle with more hot coals on top until the gigote sets
6. If desired, decorate with egg yolks beaten with sugar, ground cinnamon and bread
