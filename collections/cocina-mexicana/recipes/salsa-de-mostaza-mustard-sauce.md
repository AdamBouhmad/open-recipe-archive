## Ingredients

- mustard
- water
- bread crumb
- fine salt
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pound the mustard after cleaning it of all foreign matter
2. Place it in water, and after washing it, grind it with a little water
3. When it is well ground, add a small piece of moistened bread crumb and a few grains of fine salt
4. Moisten it again by lowering it with vinegar, adding all the vinegar necessary so that it is neither too thick nor too thin
5. Add a piece of sugar until the palate recognizes that the acidity of the vinegar has been mitigated
6. If you wish to reduce the pungency of the mustard, pound it a second time and wash it in two or more waters
7. This sauce is placed in a sauce boat so that those who eat the stew may take the necessary amount
