## Ingredients

- bobo
- clavo
- culantro
- pimienta
- cominos
- hojas de aguacate
- pepextle
- vino
- agua
- vinagre
- servilleta
- aceite
- aceitunas
- alcaparras
- chiles

## Directions

1. Well washed, slice widthwise without separating.
2. Fill the slices with ground clove, cilantro, pepper and cumin.
3. Wrap with avocado leaves and tie.
4. In a pot over heat, prepare pepextle as for tamales and place the bobo in it, adding wine, water and vinegar in equal parts.
5. Cover the pot with a plate and tie a napkin at the mouth of the pot.
6. Once the bobo is cooked, place on a platter and sprinkle with its own broth, adding oil.
7. Garnish with olives, capers and chiles.
