## Ingredients

- Young squabs, no more than eight days old
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Ham slices
- White wine
- Concentrated broth
- Mixed herbs
- Rooster combs
- Crayfish
- Toulouse-style sauce

## Directions

1. Choose young squabs no more than eight days old
2. Pass through flame and prepare them
3. Parboil in butter with lemon juice
4. Place in a pot lined with ham slices
5. Add the butter in which they were parboiled
6. Moisten with white wine and concentrated broth
7. Add a mixed bundle of herbs
8. Cover with buttered paper
9. Cook at two fires
10. Prepare a Toulouse-style sauce
11. Arrange the squabs over the sauce with rooster combs and crayfish
