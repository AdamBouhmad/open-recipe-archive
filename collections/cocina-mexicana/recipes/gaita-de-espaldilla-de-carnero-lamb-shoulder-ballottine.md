## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder, boned to the third joint
- tongue en escarlata
- beef or pork forcemeat from sausages or chorizos
- broth

## Directions

1. Bone the lamb shoulder to the third joint
2. Lard it with tongue en escarlata
3. Break the knuckle with the cleaver about one-third of the way through
4. Fill the shoulder with meat from sausages or chorizos
5. Tie with string to give it a globe shape
6. Cook as for shoulder à la braise
7. Reduce the broth to gelatin
8. Serve over an arrangement of small root vegetables
