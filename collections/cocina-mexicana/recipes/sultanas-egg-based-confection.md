## Ingredients

- Four fresh eggs (yolks and whites)
- Sugar, ground and sifted, equal to the weight of the four eggs
- Flour, equal to half the weight of the four eggs
- A little fragrant water

## Directions

1. Blend everything well together
2. Add a little fragrant water
3. Once everything is well incorporated, distribute the mixture on papers in various shapes
4. Sprinkle with ground and sifted sugar
5. Note that they extend too much in length, so there should be sufficient distance between one and another so they do not touch or stick together
6. Place them to cook in a small oven with fire on top
7. Once cooked, remove from the fire, first moistening the paper and passing it quickly over the fire to remove them
