## Ingredients

- Ham fat
- Paper greased with butter

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb forequarter with fat ham from the shoulder blade area to the end of the breast, and pass a skewer between the ribs and shoulder, tying the ends to the spit; use forks or skewers to secure it to the spit without piercing holes; cover with slices of ham and buttered paper on top, which should be removed shortly before it finishes cooking so it browns; it should cook for two hours.
