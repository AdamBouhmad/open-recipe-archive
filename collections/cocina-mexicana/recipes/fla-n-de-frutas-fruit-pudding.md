## Ingredients

- armoring paste
- apricots, peaches or avocados, pitted
- powdered sugar
- almonds
- syrup

## Directions

1. Line a mold no more than two inches high with armoring paste, ensuring the paste takes the shape of the mold well
2. Place pitted apricots, peaches or avocados in a bowl and toss with powdered sugar
3. Arrange in the molded crust
4. Bake the flaón in a hot oven
5. Crack the pits and clean the almonds
6. Place the almonds over the fruit and drizzle with a little syrup
