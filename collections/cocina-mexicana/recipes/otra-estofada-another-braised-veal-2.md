## Ingredients

- veal in pieces
- butter
- lean ham
- onion
- garlic crushed
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaves
- spices
- white wine

## Directions

1. Fry veal pieces in butter with a little lean ham and onion
2. Transfer all to a pot with crushed garlic, salt, parsley, bay leaves and spices
3. Cover well and when it begins to cook, add a little white wine
4. The broth should reduce to half for it to be properly done
