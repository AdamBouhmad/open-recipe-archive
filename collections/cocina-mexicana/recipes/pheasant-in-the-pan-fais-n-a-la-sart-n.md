## Ingredients

- pheasant
- carrots
- onions
- herb bouquet
- ham slices
- white wine
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- sauce

## Directions

1. After larding it, place in a casserole or pan with carrots, onions, and a small bouquet of fine herbs
2. Cover with small slices of ham
3. Add equal parts white wine and broth
4. Season and let cook over gentle heat
5. To serve, add a sauce that is not chile-based
