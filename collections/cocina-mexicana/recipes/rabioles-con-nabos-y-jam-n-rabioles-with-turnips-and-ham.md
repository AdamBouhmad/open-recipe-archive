## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 3 ounces butter
- 1 ounce lard
- 2 eggs
- salt
- water as needed
- cooked turnips
- cooked ham
- salt
- pepper
- grated cheese
- butter
- a little minced onion
- water
- salt
- saffron
- clove
- ground pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Make the dough with a pound of flour, 3 ounces of butter, 1 ounce of lard, 2 eggs, salt and necessary water
2. For the filling, make it with cooked turnips and ham, chopped very fine, seasoned with salt and pepper and mixed with grated cheese, all to taste
3. For the sauce, fry a little minced onion in butter and add water, salt, saffron for color, clove and pepper, all ground
4. Brown flour in butter letting it boil until well seasoned
5. Follow the same procedures of the previous articles for forming and cooking
