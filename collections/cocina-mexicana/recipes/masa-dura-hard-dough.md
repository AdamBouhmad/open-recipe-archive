## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- slightly less butter
- a little cold water
- sufficient quantity of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the butter
2. Add a little cold water and sufficient salt
3. Knead with the rolling pin and ensure it becomes very hard
