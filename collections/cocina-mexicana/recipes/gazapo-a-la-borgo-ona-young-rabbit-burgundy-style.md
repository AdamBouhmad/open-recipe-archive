## Ingredients

- Young rabbits
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Garlic
- Bay leaf
- Demi-glace sauce
- White wine
- Mushrooms
- Egg yolks
- Parsley
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Divide young rabbits into pieces
2. Heat butter in a casserole and add the rabbit portions after cleaning away blood with a sponge or drying with clean cloth
3. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, garlic and bay leaf
4. Cover the casserole with the lid and much fire on top; a quarter hour is enough for the rabbit to be well cooked
5. Moisten with a spoonful of demi-glace sauce and a glass of white wine
6. Let the sauce reduce somewhat and add turned mushrooms
7. Bind with egg yolks, a bit of butter, parsley and lemon juice
