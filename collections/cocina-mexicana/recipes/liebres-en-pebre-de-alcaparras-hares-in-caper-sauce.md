## Ingredients

- Hares
- water
- salt
- lard
- capers
- bread
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- oil
- vinegar
- onions
- parsley
- hare broth
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the hares in salted water
2. Divide them and fry the quarters in lard, also with salt
3. Remove the fried quarters and in the same lard fry ground capers with toasted bread, clove, pepper, saffron and cumin, so that everything fries also
4. Add oil, vinegar, cooked onions, chopped parsley, the fried quarters of hare and broth from the pot, allowing it to season and the sauce to thicken
5. When serving, add small chiles and olives
