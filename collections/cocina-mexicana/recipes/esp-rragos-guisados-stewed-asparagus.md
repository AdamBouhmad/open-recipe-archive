## Ingredients

- asparagus
- lard
- garlic cloves
- salt
- water
- bread
- pepper
- cloves
- small onions

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic cloves in lard and set aside when browned
2. In the same lard, add asparagus cooked in salted water, drained and chopped
3. When half-fried, add toasted and ground bread mixed with the garlic removed from the lard
4. Add a little pepper and clove and a little water from the same water in which the asparagus was cooked
5. Add small cooked onions
