## Ingredients

- pork loin
- ancho chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- oregano
- thyme
- water
- lard
- onion slices
- olives
- tomatillos

## Directions

1. After washing, make slices three to four fingers thick without cutting all the way through
2. Place in a deep cazuela and adobo with ancho chiles that have been deveined, soaked and ground with sufficient garlic, cumin, salt, vinegar, oregano and thyme
3. The next day or the same day if necessary, add necessary water and lard
4. Cook over two fires, turning to cook evenly
5. Let consume until it fries in its own adobo and lard, being careful not to burn
6. When ready to serve, place on plate and garnish with onion slices, olives and tomatillos in quarters
