## Ingredients

- eighteen guinea bananas
- thirty egg yolks
- one pound of sugar
- competent amount of milk
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind the bananas very well
2. Strain everything together and bring to a boil with cinnamon
3. Cook until it reaches regular consistency
