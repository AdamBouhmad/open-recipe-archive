## Ingredients

- Chicken, divided into quarters
- Water
- Lard
- Salt
- Ground garlic
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Sausages

## Directions

1. Divide the chicken into quarters and place in a casserole over the fire with water, lard, little salt, and ground garlic
2. Take care to turn them
3. When cooked, allow all the broth to be consumed so they fry in their own fat
4. Cover the casserole with a griddle, baking dish, or sheet of tin with heat over it
5. Allow the broth to be consumed so they fry in their grease and serve with the remaining fat poured over
