## Ingredients

- Apples
- Lime water
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Half-point honey, well clarified
- Milk
- Ground almonds
- Few egg yolks
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Deseed and peel the apples, and place them in lime water until they form a crust; wash them and stud with cinnamon and cloves, and boil them in half-point honey well clarified, until it reaches the right point; place them in a well-drained platter, and in the honey in which they were cooked add the milk, reduced with ground almonds, a few egg yolks, and cinnamon powder; make a paste with all this, and pour it over the apples.
