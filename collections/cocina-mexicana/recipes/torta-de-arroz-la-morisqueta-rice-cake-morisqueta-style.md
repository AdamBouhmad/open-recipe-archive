## Ingredients

- Rice, soaked and washed
- Pork meat for picadillo
- Spices, cloves, cinnamon and cumin
- Butter
- Eggs
- Powdered sugar
- Sesame seeds
- Tomatoes, cooked and ground
- Garlic, chopped
- Onion, sliced
- Ham, sliced
- Olives
- Chiles in vinegar
- Raisins
- Almonds, sliced
- Apple slices
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak and wash the rice and set it to cook; make a picadillo of pork meat with all its trimmings as for stuffed chiles, with spices, cloves, cinnamon and cumin.
2. Cook the rice so that it remains whole; mix it with powdered sugar, grease the skillet with butter, add a little egg and then the rice to cover the bottom of the skillet; add the picadillo, break some eggs over it and on top put all the rice and more egg.
3. On top add a little sesame and place over two fires; to see if it is cooked insert a popote so it comes out clean and on the plates sprinkle powdered sugar on top.
4. For the sauce: cooked tomatoes and ground, are fried in butter with chopped garlic, sliced onion, sliced ham, olives, chiles in vinegar, raisins, sliced almonds, apple slices, parsley, a little vinegar and sugar, taking care that it is not too loose.
