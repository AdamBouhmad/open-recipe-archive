## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of virgin honey
- five egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with two pounds of sugar and one pound of virgin honey, which is clarified
2. When it reaches the soft ball stage, let it cool and gradually mix in five well-beaten egg whites that have been prepared with proper anticipation
