## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- mushrooms
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- fish broth
- wine
- bread
- oil or butter for frying

## Directions

1. Fry onion in butter
2. Add mushrooms, a small bunch of fine herbs, salt, pepper, and a little bay leaf
3. Add equal parts fish broth and wine
4. Serve on a platter with slices of bread fried in oil or butter
