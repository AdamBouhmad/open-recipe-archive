## Ingredients

- potatoes
- aged cheese
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, peel and mash them, and grind aged cheese
2. Beat the egg and add the potato
3. Put the pan with lard and make the cake, divide into quarters and eat dry with sauce or salad or in mole
