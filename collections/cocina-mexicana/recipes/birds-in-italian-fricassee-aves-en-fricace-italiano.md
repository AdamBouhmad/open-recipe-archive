## Ingredients

- birds
- lard or butter
- flour
- clove
- ham
- garlic
- onions
- broth
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Brown flour in lard or butter with clove and small square pieces of ham
2. Fry garlic and onions there, and then add broth with the raw birds cut up which are left to cook over gentle fire, seasoning with cinnamon, clove, nutmeg and salt
3. As soon as the birds are well cooked, place chopped parsley in a pan or pot and beat it with egg yolks as many as necessary, gradually adding butter when they are diluted, then place in another pot that will be put over gentle fire: stir and when it reaches a simmer, join it with the cooked meat
