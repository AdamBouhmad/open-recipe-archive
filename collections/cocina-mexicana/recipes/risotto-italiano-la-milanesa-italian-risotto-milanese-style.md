## Ingredients

- half a large onion, chopped
- butter
- one pound of rice
- broth
- saffron

## Directions

1. Chop half a large onion and fry it gently in a pan with butter until it browns
2. Add one pound of rice and the broth as it is needed to make it burst and serve it of a certain consistency, stronger than for ordinary soup
3. Add a little saffron, taking care that it does not stick
4. When the rice has burst according to the particular taste of each (the Milanese cook it in a certain way)
