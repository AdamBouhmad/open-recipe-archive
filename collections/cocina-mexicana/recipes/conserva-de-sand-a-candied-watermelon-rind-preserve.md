## Ingredients

- watermelon
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Remove the thick outer skin with some of the red flesh
2. Clean away the hard cortex
3. Place in the sun, changing the water daily until very white
4. Add to good syrup, pricking it so the syrup penetrates; to test if it is done, prick with a thin straw
5. Do not add cold water as it spoils and causes spotting, but do add orange blossom water and give one violent boil, as if done slowly it turns black
