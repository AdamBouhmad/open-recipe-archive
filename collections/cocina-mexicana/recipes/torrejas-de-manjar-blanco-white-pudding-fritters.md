## Ingredients

- white pudding
- eggs
- flour
- raisins
- almonds
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make the white pudding very thick, add no water to the milk
2. Cool for two or three days and slice it
3. Beat some eggs and add a little flour to fry them and squeeze with a napkin so they do not fall apart
4. Having placed the syrup, add them
5. If desired, stud them before frying with raisins and almonds and let them boil briefly in syrup
6. Place in a platter with powdered cinnamon on top
