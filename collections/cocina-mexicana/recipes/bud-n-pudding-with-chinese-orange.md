## Ingredients

- one grated Chinese orange
- raisins
- almonds
- citron
- milk
- sugar
- six eggs
- butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak in well-boiled milk one grated Chinese orange, raisins, almonds and citron
2. Chop everything very fine and mix with plenty of sugar
3. Beat six eggs very well and spread a pan with plenty of butter
4. Heat the burner, put the pan adding more butter on top and also sugar and powdered cinnamon and a comal with embers
