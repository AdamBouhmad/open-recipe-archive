## Ingredients

- Pork leg
- water
- wine
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- herbs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove any rancid parts from the leg
2. Cut and place in cold water for two or three days to desalt
3. Drain and dry well
4. Wrap in clean linen
5. Cook in equal parts wine and water with onions, thyme, bay leaf, clove and a mixed bouquet of herbs
6. Test for doneness by piercing with a skewer, which should pass easily through when cooked
7. Remove and take out the center bone
8. Let cool
9. Lift the skin and cover the fat around it with breadcrumbs mixed with fine herbs
