## Ingredients

- One quartillo of pine nuts
- Two hundred small walnuts
- One drachma of cinnamon
- Syrup made with one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Grind one quartillo of pine nuts, two hundred small walnuts and one drachma of cinnamon
2. Mix everything in clarified syrup of medium point made with one pound of sugar
3. Arrange over mamones soaked with syrup and orange blossom water, sprinkling wine and decorating the top layer
