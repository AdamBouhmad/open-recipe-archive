## Ingredients

- lamb or kid testicles
- water
- salt
- oil
- egg yolks
- flour
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Open the testicles and remove the first and second membrane
2. Cook with water and salt
3. After draining, fry in oil then drain again
4. Beat egg yolks with a powder of flour and salt, dip the testicles and fry again in very hot oil
5. Serve with cinnamon and sugar
