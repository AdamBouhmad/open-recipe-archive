## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt
- three quarters of butter or lard
- six egg yolks
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Pile two pounds of flour on a clean, smooth table
2. Make a hollow in the middle and add salt, butter or lard, egg yolks, and water
3. Mix the yolks with the butter or lard, the water with the salt
4. Incorporate everything little by little with the flour
5. Then knead with your fists, adding a little water
6. Take care not to work the dough more than twice if it is somewhat firm, otherwise it will break when arming it
7. If preparing for making cakes, make it a little softer or more tender
