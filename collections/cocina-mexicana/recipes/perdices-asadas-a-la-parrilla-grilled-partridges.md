## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place the raw partridges on the grill, rolling them with oil.
2. Roll them in breadcrumbs and return to the grill so they brown.
3. Serve with lettuce salad, unpicked.
