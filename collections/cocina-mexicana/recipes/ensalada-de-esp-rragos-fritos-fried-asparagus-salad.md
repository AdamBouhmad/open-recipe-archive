## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Onion
- Oil
- Garlic
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Form bundles with the asparagus and cook in salted water
2. When well cooked, remove all the tough part of the stems and toss with finely chopped onion
3. Place a pot on the fire with oil and fry finely minced garlic until golden
4. Add the asparagus with the oil to fry there with a very short splash of vinegar
5. Once fried, serve hot, moistened with oil and vinegar
