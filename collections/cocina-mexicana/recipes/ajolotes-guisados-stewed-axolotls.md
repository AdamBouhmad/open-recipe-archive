## Ingredients

- Axolotls, skinned and washed
- Lard
- Onion
- Parsley
- Ground biscuit
- Oil
- Dried chile (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the skinned and washed axolotls in a little lard with some onion
2. Add the axolotls with chopped parsley and ground biscuit
3. Add water and a good branch of epazote
4. Let them cook with the proper amount of salt
5. To serve, add a little oil, and those who like spice can add some veins of dried chile to the broth
