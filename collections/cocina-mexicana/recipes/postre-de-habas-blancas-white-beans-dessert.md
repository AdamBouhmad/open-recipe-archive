## Ingredients

- white beans, one handful
- milk, four cuartillos
- sugar
- egg yolks, eight

## Directions

1. Toast well a handful of white beans in a comal
2. Soak them in hot water
3. Peel and grind in a metate
4. Strain with four cuartillos of milk
5. Grind the chaff again and strain it also
6. Sweeten so that the milk becomes somewhat loaded with sugar
7. Dissolve eight egg yolks into it
8. Place over fire until it reaches the convenient point
9. Pour into a platter and dust with ground cinnamon on top
