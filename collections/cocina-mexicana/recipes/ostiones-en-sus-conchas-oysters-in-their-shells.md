## Ingredients

- four dozen oysters
- butter
- tender onions
- parsley
- flour
- broth
- white wine
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Open four dozen oysters, remove them from their shells, and give them a boil in the same water they have released.
2. Remove from heat and place a little butter in a pan with very tender chopped onions and parsley.
3. Sauté and add a tablespoon of flour, dissolving everything with broth and half a glass of white wine.
4. Cook and reduce this sauce by adding the oysters to it.
5. Set aside the largest and cleanest shells and after washing well, place three or four oysters with appropriate sauce in each, covering with breadcrumbs and a little butter.
6. At the moment of serving, place on grills covered with a griddle full of coals and leave for a few minutes.
