## Ingredients

- Ham
- Pure saltpeter
- Spirit of wine
- Ground or crushed juniper berries

## Directions

1. Separate the hams from the pig's body
2. Salt them sufficiently with pure saltpeter
3. Place them under a press and leave for eight days
4. Soak them with spirit of wine in which ground or crushed juniper berries have been infused
5. Smoke and dry them over juniper wood smoke in a suitable location
