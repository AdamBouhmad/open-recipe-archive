## Ingredients

- Capers
- Garlic
- Fried bread
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. For two pounds of meat, grind a quarter pound of capers, six cloves of garlic and a quarter part of fried bread soaked
2. Add this to the prepared broth
3. At half-cooked stage when everything is together, let season until the sauce is thick
