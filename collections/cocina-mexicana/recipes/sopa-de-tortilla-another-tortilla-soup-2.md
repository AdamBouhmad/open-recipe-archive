## Ingredients

- Tortilla, cut into small squares
- Chard
- Onion, finely chopped
- Garlic, finely chopped
- Butter
- Lard
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pork loin
- Chorizo, sliced
- Aged cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the tortilla into small squares, cook some chard and chop it.
2. Chop onion and garlic finely, grind a small quantity of the chard, fry the onion, garlic and ground chard in butter and lard, and this is what makes up the sauce, season with a little oil and vinegar.
3. Fry the tortilla in butter and lard, coat the pot with butter and layer tortilla, then pork loin, chorizo in slices and chopped chard and so on until the pot is full.
4. To serve at table, pour the hot sauce over it and garnish with aged cheese on top.
