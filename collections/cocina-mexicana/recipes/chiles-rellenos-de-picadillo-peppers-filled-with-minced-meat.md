## Ingredients

- Peppers
- Pork loin from the middle and pork loin from the head, or solid veal
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Ham, cooked
- Egg
- Candied citron pieces
- Pitted raisins
- Peeled almonds
- Lard
- Garlic
- Saffron
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Cumin
- Sugar
- Castilian vinegar
- Picadillo broth
- Olives
- Flour
- Eggs for frying

## Directions

1. Devein peppers raw, then roast them and wrap in cloth to sweat, peel and soak in cold water
2. Take pork loin from the middle and head, or solid veal, chop with a machete until it is coarsely chopped
3. Cook with appropriate salt
4. When cooked, chop red tomatoes; for half the amount of meat use two tomatoes
5. Cook the ham together with the picadillo meat, cut into small pieces the size of hard-boiled eggs
6. Cut candied citron into small pieces, use pitted raisins and peeled almonds
7. Chop the meat a second time but carefully so it does not become as finely minced as sawdust
8. Burn lard, add garlic first, then tomato, then meat
9. Add a little saffron, four or five cloves, cinnamon and some cumin, all ground very fine
10. Add to the meat and tomato
11. Add a little sugar, Castilian vinegar and a little picadillo broth
12. Let it boil; when cooked and drying, add the ham, almonds, candied citron, raisins and olives
13. Ensure the taste of sugar and vinegar stands out
14. Let the picadillo dry taking on a golden color
15. When cooled, fill the peppers and roll in flour
16. Beat eggs and fry the peppers
