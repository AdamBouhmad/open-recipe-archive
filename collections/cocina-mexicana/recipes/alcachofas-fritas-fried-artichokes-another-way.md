## Ingredients

- tender artichokes
- handful of flour
- two eggs
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Choose the most tender artichokes; after cutting them into pieces, wash them well and dry them, put them for some time in a batter made with a handful of flour, two eggs, a bit of vinegar, the same amount of oil, salt and pepper
2. Fry and serve with fried parsley
