## Ingredients

- mojarra fish
- oil
- lard
- bread
- tomatoes
- onion
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash them very well, then remove from water, pat dry and remove the roe
2. In a pan with oil and lard, fry the mojarra carefully so it does not break
3. In the same lard, fry the roe after slicing it very thinly
4. Remove it and in the same lard fry bread slices
5. Remove these and still in the same lard fry minced tomatoes and finely minced onion
6. Grind half of the fried roe together with the bread and mix with the tomato and onion
7. Add a pinch of pepper, a little vinegar and a good amount of oil
8. Let it simmer and when ready, moisten the mojarra with this sauce inside and out
9. Place on a platter and top with the remaining roe sliced together with sliced fried bread
