## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- syrup

## Directions

1. Separate yolks from whites and clean yolks well
2. Press through cloth and pour through a shell or perforated spoon or strainer into syrup at medium consistency when it has a good boil
3. Remove and arrange over mamones or moistened sponge cake with syrup
