## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit
- liver
- white wine
- broth
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. When gutting the rabbit, leave in the liver and remove the bile
2. Debone only in the middle of the back and make a hole in the skin
3. Pass the front half of the rabbit through the hole so it is turned inside out in the shape of a turtle
4. Tie in this position with thread
5. Cook with a glass of white wine, a little broth, and a little parsley and onion
