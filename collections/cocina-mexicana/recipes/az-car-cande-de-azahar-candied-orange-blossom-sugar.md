## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- half pound of orange blossoms, white, fresh and clean
- quarter of a cuartillo of good wine spirit

## Directions

1. Make syrup with four pounds of sugar and clarify it as is customary
2. Let it boil until it reaches the blown stage
3. Add half a pound of orange blossoms that have been previously prepared, white, fresh and clean
4. Let it give twelve boils
5. Strain through a sieve and return the strained liquid to heat to reach the blown stage again
6. After skimming and removing from heat, add a quarter of a cuartillo of good wine spirit
7. Pour into a pot, cover it and place in the stove where it will be kept for eight days, maintaining equal heat
8. After that time, uncover the pot to drain the syrup, leaving the candied sugar stuck to the bottom
9. Heat the pot and detach any remaining sugar
10. The orange blossoms used can be dried in the stove with sugar powder and sifted to obtain candied orange blossoms
