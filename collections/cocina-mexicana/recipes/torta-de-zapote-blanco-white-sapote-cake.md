## Ingredients

- Ripe white sapotes
- Hard biscuits, ground
- Pine nuts
- Cinnamon
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Beaten eggs
- Butter
- Syrup (almíbar)
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Peel and grind ripe white sapotes
2. Mix with ground hard biscuits, pine nuts, cinnamon, toasted sesame seeds, and beaten eggs
3. Pour into a pan greased with butter
4. Set the cake over two fires
5. Divide into quarters or slices
6. Place in syrup and decorate with raisins, almonds, and ground cinnamon
