## Ingredients

- One fresh ham
- Two whole pork loins
- One bottle of wine
- Half quart of vinegar
- Garlic cloves from one or two heads, ground
- One ounce of cinnamon
- Clove, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Ginger, to taste
- A little oregano
- Bull casings

## Directions

1. Soak a fresh ham for three days
2. Remove the skin
3. Mince it together with two whole pork loins
4. Mix one bottle of wine, half quart of vinegar, ground garlic cloves from one or two heads, one ounce of cinnamon, clove, pepper, ginger to taste, and a little oregano
5. Mix everything and let cure for three days
6. Fill bull casings with this mixture
7. Put to dry over the smoke of straw
