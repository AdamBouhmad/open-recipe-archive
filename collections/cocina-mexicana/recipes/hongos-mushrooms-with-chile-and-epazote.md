## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- salt
- mashed garlic
- garlic
- onion
- chile peppers
- epazote
- chile seeds

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from mushrooms, wash them, and cook in water, salt, and mashed garlic.
2. Fry plenty of chopped garlic and onion; some chile seeds and two or three chiles, torn in pieces, chilpotle or piquín; fry everything very well, and when the seeds are golden color, add plenty of chopped epazote to just simmer so it does not turn black.
3. Add water and salt and let that little broth season, which should remain a bit watery, and then add the mushrooms.
