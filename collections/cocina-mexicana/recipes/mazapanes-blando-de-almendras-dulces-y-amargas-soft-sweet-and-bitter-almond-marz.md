## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sweet almonds
- 1 pound bitter almonds
- egg white
- 3 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Soak 3 pounds of sweet almonds and 1 pound of bitter almonds, peel them and put them to dry
2. Crush them in a mortar, grinding them well so that they form a very fine paste, adding egg white from time to time
3. Make a sugar syrup with 3 pounds of the best sugar, clarify it and let it boil until it reaches the low ball point
4. Remove the pan from the fire and pour the almond paste into the syrup
5. Place the pan on embers and stir constantly so that it does not burn or stick, knowing that the paste is well made if when a little is placed on the back of your hand it can be removed without sticking
6. Pour onto a sugar-dusted table, let it cool and extend to the thickness desired, cutting it immediately into different shapes made with molds or tin cutters
7. Place on paper sheets and bake at moderate heat after glazing with sugar like biscuits
