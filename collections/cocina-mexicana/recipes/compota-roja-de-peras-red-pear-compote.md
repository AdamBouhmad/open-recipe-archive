## Ingredients

- Pears
- Water
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Tin

## Directions

1. Remove the eyes from the pears and scrape the stems
2. Peel if desired
3. Put on the fire in a clay vessel with water, sugar, a little cinnamon, and a piece of tin
4. This compote is generally served hot
