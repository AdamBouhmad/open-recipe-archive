## Ingredients

- two pineapples with skin
- twelve cuartillos of water
- one plateful of barley
- one toasted bread cake
- one plateful of toasted corn
- two cuartillos of sour atole
- eight peppercorns
- eight crushed cloves
- two corn husks
- lemon syrup (optional)
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind two pineapples with their skin.
2. Bring to boil in twelve cuartillos of water the amount of barley that fits in a soup plate.
3. When the barley has burst from boiling, remove from heat and pour into a large jar.
4. Add the ground pineapple, one toasted bread cake, one plateful of toasted corn, two cuartillos of sour atole, eight peppercorns, eight crushed cloves, and two corn husks.
5. Cover the opening with a cloth and place in the sun for fifteen days, stirring with a stick every third day.
6. After this time, strain and sweeten if desired with lemon syrup.
7. Add a little clove and cinnamon.
8. Bottle and seal tightly; it will be ready to use on the third day.
