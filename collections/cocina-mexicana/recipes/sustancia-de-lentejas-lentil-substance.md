## Ingredients

- One and a half quarts lentils
- One pound pork fat
- One and a half pounds beef pulp
- Two carrots
- Three onions
- Two cloves of spice
- Broth
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. After washing one and a half quarts of lentils, put them in a pot with one pound of pork fat, one and a half pounds of beef pulp, two carrots and three onions, one of which is studded with two cloves of spice
2. Fill the pot with broth and let the lentils cook
3. When done, remove the vegetables, fat and beef, and pass them through a strainer
4. When the broth has been wrung out, put the substance in a pot with four skimming spoonfuls of Spanish sauce (see page 795)
5. More broth is needed in this substance than in that of chickpeas, because it is necessary for it to boil a long time to take color, taking care to skim and degrease it
6. Be especially careful that it is not too salty, as when it reduces it would become bitter and acrid
7. After reducing sufficiently, reserve it in another pot for the uses intended
