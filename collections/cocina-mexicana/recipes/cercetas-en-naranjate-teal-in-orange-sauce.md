## Ingredients

- Four small teal ducks, moderately hung
- Teal livers
- Machete

## Directions

1. Select four small teal ducks, moderately hung
2. Draw them carefully, singe them lightly so that only the down is burned, and clean them carefully so that no quills remain that might conceal feathers
3. Crush the livers with the back of a machete to make a light stuffing, mixing them with a good piece of (recipe cuts off)
