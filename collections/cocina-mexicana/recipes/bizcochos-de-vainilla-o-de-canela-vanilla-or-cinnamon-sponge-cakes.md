## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- one ounce of vanilla or cinnamon powder
- four ounces of flour
- ten ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make the same as chocolate bizcochos, substituting chocolate with vanilla or cinnamon powder
