## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- broth or water
- bread

## Directions

1. After cleaning, cut into pieces and place in a pan, frying them in oil, chopped parsley, and a garlic clove.
2. Simmer over low heat, season well with ground salt, and add broth or water.
3. To thicken the sauce, crush a piece of toasted bread and add it.
