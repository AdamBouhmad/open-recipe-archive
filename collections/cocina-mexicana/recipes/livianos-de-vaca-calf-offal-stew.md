## Ingredients

- calf lungs, liver, heart, and spleen
- butter
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Put the offal to bleed in cold water and parboil for a moment in boiling water
2. Divide into pieces and place in a pot with a piece of butter and a handful of flour, moistening everything with broth
3. When the stew is cooked and well seasoned to taste, add three egg yolks dissolved in a little milk
4. Let them mix over the fire, and as the sauce thickens, serve
