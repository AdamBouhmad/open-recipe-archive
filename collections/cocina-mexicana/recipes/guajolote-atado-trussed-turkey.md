## Ingredients

- turkey
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- sugar
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning, let it hang in the open air for twenty-four hours
2. Cook in a pot and when half-cooked, remove and place on a roasting spit with butter and a dusting of salt and pepper
3. Insert some cloves into the breast and legs, wrap well in brown paper, tie securely and roast over gentle heat
4. When well roasted, make a sauce with a little sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, pepper and broth, and drizzle over the turkey
