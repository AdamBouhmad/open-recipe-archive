## Ingredients

- shrimp
- garlic
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- lard
- thyme
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron threads
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Soak the shrimp, clean them and remove the shell
2. Chop half a head of garlic very fine, a bit of tomato without seeds, a bit of onion and parsley
3. Fry the garlic and onion in oil and lard
4. Add a bit of thyme, salt, cloves, cinnamon and pepper with some saffron threads, all ground
5. Once fried, add the shrimp to the mixture along with chiles, olives and capers
