## Ingredients

- dough as prepared above
- meat prepared with salt and pepper
- good butter
- bacon strips
- egg

## Directions

1. Take the quantity of dough sufficient to make them the size desired
2. Pass a wooden roller over this dough to flatten it and place it in a baking dish
3. On top of this dough place the meat that has been prepared with salt and pepper
4. In all the gaps add good butter
5. Cover the meat with wide and thin strips of bacon, which in the art are called lardoons
6. Cover everything with a covering of dough passed with a roller of the same thickness as that which has served as the base for the empanada
7. Lower it a little around its circumference to join the edges of the upper dough with the lower
8. Moisten these edges with a little water so they adhere well
9. For the same purpose press the upper edge on the lower with convenient force
10. Conclude by making a border somewhat raised with the thumb all around the dough
11. Take an egg, beat well its yolk and white, and with a feather coat the entire upper surface of the empanada
12. Bake in the oven for three hours
13. A quarter hour after the empanada is in the oven, remove it and make a hole in the middle so the vapor that forms in its interior may escape, which would make it watery and bad-tasting
