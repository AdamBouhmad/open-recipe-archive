## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- garlic
- oregano
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Roast plenty of tomatoes and garlic
2. Grind with a little oregano
3. Fry everything well
4. Add a little vinegar and salt
5. The sauce should remain thick
