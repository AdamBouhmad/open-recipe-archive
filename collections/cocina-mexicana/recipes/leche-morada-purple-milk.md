## Ingredients

- milk sweetened to taste
- purple sweet potato
- a little rice
- one stick of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk to taste
2. Cook the purple sweet potato and grind with a little rice
3. Mix into the milk and strain with a colander
4. Cook with a cinnamon stick until it reaches the proper consistency
