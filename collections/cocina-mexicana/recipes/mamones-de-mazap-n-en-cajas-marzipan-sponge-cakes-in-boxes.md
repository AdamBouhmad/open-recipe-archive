## Ingredients

- half a pound of ground almond
- half a pound of fresh adobera cheese, fresh and soft grated, also ground
- four beaten eggs, whites and yolks
- powdered sugar according to taste
- egg yolks with butter
- lard for coating boxes

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of ground almond, another half pound of fresh adobera cheese, fresh and soft grated, also ground, four beaten eggs (whites and yolks), and powdered sugar in more or less quantity according to each one's taste; when everything is incorporated, gradually add more egg yolks with butter until the mass becomes soft, then pour into boxes coated with lard, without filling them, and place in the oven; when the mamón is half cooked, remove the boxes, quickly coat the mamón with butter, dust with ground sugar, and put the boxes back in the oven for the mamones to finish cooking.
