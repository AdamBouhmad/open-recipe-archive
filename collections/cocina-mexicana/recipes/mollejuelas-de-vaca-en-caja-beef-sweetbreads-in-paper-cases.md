## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Oil or melted lard
- Parsley
- Small onions
- Mushrooms
- Shallots
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Paper for cases
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak and blanch the sweetbreads, remove the cartilage, and cut into thin slices.
2. Make a marinade with oil or melted lard, parsley, small onions, mushrooms and chopped shallots, salt and coarse pepper.
3. Place the sweetbreads in the marinade.
4. Make seven or eight paper cases three fingers long and coat the bottom with oil.
5. Fill them with the sweetbreads and their marinade, place on the grill over oiled paper, and cook over hot coals for half an hour, being careful not to let the paper catch fire by spreading the flames with a shovel if necessary.
6. Once the sweetbreads are cooked, add a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar.
