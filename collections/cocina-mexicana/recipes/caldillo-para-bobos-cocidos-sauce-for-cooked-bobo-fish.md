## Ingredients

- cebolla
- perejil
- ajo
- orégano
- yemas de huevo
- pan frito
- pan molido
- clavo
- pimienta
- cominos
- caldo
- bobo
- aceitunas
- chile

## Directions

1. Chop onion, parsley, garlic and oregano; grind and fry with egg yolks and slices of fried ground bread, clove, pepper and cumin.
2. Add broth from the same in which the bobo cooked and mix.
3. Garnish with ground olives and a chile.
4. Bathe the cooked bobo with this sauce.
