## Ingredients

- Exotes (green beans)
- Butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fine herbs, chopped
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the exotes and let them drain well
2. Then fry them in butter with salt, black pepper and chopped fine herbs
3. As soon as they are fried, serve them as is or add the juice of a lemon
