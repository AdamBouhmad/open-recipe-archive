## Ingredients

- lard
- lamb liver
- garlic, four cloves
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. When the lard is hot, add the liver cut into thin, long slices
2. Prepare the same stew as for lungs: crush four garlic cloves with a little pepper, dissolve in vinegar and sprinkle over with oil and ground salt, turning over low heat
