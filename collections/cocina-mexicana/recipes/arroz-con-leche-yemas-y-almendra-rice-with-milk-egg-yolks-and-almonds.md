## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- leche (milk)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- azúcar (sugar)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)
- almendras molidas (ground almonds)

## Directions

1. Soak the rice in milk
2. The next day put it to cook with water and a grain of salt
3. When it is half cooked, add the milk
4. When it has finished cooking, add the sugar, egg yolks and ground almonds so that their flavor stands out, with quantities proportional to the amount of rice
5. Empty into a platter and when taking it to the table sprinkle ground cinnamon on top
