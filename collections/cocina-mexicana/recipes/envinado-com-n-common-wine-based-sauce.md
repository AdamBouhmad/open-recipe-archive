## Ingredients

- lard
- chopped garlic
- roasted and ground xitomates
- toasted bread
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- poultry or meat
- broth from cooking the meat
- vinegar
- ground saffron
- sugar
- sweet wine
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic in lard
2. When frying, add roasted and ground xitomates with toasted bread, clove, pepper, and cinnamon
3. Place the poultry or meat to be cooked in this mixture
4. Add equal amounts of the broth in which they were cooked and vinegar
5. Add a little ground saffron, a little sugar, sweet wine, and toasted sesame
