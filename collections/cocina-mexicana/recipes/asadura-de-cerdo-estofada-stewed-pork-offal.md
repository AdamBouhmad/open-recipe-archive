## Ingredients

- pork offal
- ham
- spices
- thyme
- salt
- vinegar
- pork caul
- pumpkin seeds (peeled, toasted, and ground)

## Directions

1. Lard this as you would stew meat, with ham and spices, adding thyme, salt, and vinegar on top.
2. Sprinkle a pork caul with powder of pumpkin seeds that have been peeled, toasted, and ground, and wrap the offal in it.
