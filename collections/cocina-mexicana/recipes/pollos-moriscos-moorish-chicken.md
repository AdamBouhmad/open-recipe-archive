## Ingredients

- garlic
- onions
- oregano
- parsley
- spearmint
- cilantro
- sesame
- cumin
- lard
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- saffron
- wine
- vinegar
- chorizos
- criadillas
- capers
- caperberries
- acitrón
- oil

## Directions

1. Mince garlic and plenty of onions, oregano, parsley, spearmint, and green cilantro
2. Grind sesame and cumin
3. Fry garlic separately in plenty of lard and when half-fried add parsley, oregano, and cinnamon ground separately
4. Dissolve this with egg yolks, saffron, wine, and vinegar and add the chickens to this mixture
5. Partially cook the chickens
6. Add chorizos, sliced criadillas, capers, caperberries, powdered oregano, acitrón, and oil
