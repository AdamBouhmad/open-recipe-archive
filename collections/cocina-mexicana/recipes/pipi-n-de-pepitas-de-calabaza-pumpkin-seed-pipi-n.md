## Ingredients

- necessary portion of pumpkin seeds
- wide dried chiles
- lard
- chile seeds
- water
- salt
- vegetables and legumes or meat as desired

## Directions

1. Toast the necessary portion of pumpkin seeds
2. Grind them thoroughly until they resemble chocolate and form into a ball
3. Boil the ball strongly in water
4. Meanwhile, devein the necessary wide dried chiles
5. Fry the chiles well in lard
6. Add a portion of the chile seeds, fry and grind them well together with the chiles
7. When the pumpkin seed ball has cooked, remove it from water and dissolve it in the chiles
8. Place a pot on the fire with lard and fry the ground chiles and dissolved seeds
9. Add water and the vegetables, fruits or meats desired to make the pipián
10. Do not season with salt until the very end, as it causes the sauce to break
11. This pipián can be made with all oily seeds, always toasting and frying them, taking care not to add salt until the last moment
