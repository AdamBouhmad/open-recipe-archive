## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of fresh cheese
- four ounces of butter
- six eggs
- six additional egg yolks
- one pound of yeast
- one pound of flour
- six ounces of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Grind twelve ounces of fresh cheese and mix with four ounces of butter
2. Add six eggs and six more egg yolks
3. Add one pound of yeast and one pound of flour
4. Add six ounces of ground sugar
5. Knead all together until of regular consistency
6. Form the biscuits on paper
7. Bake in the oven until golden
