## Ingredients

- Broth from the pot or other substances
- Butter
- Lemon juice or verjuice

## Directions

1. Reduce the quantity of broth from the pot or other substances to half by boiling
2. Then add a little butter, always cooking over strong heat
3. When it has taken body without being too thick, remove from heat and add a little lemon juice or verjuice
