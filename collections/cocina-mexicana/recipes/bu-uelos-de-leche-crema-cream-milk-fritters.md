## Ingredients

- Egg whites and yolks
- Flour
- Candied citron, chopped
- Cinnamon sticks
- Grated lemon peel
- Milk
- White wine
- Flour
- Eggs with whites
- Lard

## Directions

1. Make a cream with egg whites and yolks, and more than the usual amount of flour
2. Before it finishes cooking, add chopped candied citron, thin cinnamon sticks, and grated lemon peel
3. Do not remove from heat until it has the consistency of manjar-blanco
4. Drop portions like small sandwiches onto a floured board
5. When cold, coat with a glaze made of milk, white wine, flour, and eggs with whites
6. Fry in lard and serve dry, dusted with flour
