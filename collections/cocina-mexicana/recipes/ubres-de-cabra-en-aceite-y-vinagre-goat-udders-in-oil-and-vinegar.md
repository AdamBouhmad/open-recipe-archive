## Ingredients

- goat udders (ubres de cabra)
- bread rusks (pan abizochado)
- pepper, six grains
- salt
- parsley (perejil)
- oil (aceite)
- vinegar (vinagre)
- onion slices (rebanadas de cebolla)
- chiles
- olives (aceitunas)
- capers (alcaparras)

## Directions

1. Cook and slice them. Grind a little bread rusk, six grains of pepper, and necessary salt. Fry a little parsley and toast this with the udders. After rinsing and rolling in bread, place them on a platter with oil, vinegar, slices of onion, chiles, olives, and capers.
