## Ingredients

- three pounds sugar
- clarified syrup
- two pounds ground or whole peanuts
- toasted sesame seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with three pounds of sugar and let it reach the point of gathering in water
2. Remove from heat and away from the flame, mix in two pounds of ground or whole peanuts
3. When well incorporated, return to heat and beat the mixture
4. Remove portions with a spoon, place on papers, and let cool before serving
5. The candy made with ground peanuts can be sprinkled on top with toasted sesame seeds if desired
