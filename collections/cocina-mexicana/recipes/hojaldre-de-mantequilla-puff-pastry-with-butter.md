## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- about half an ounce of fine salt
- one egg white
- one good glass of water
- as much butter as one egg
- half a pound of butter for layering

## Directions

1. Pile two pounds of flour on a table and make a hollow in the middle
2. Add about half an ounce of fine salt, one egg white, one good glass of water, and as much butter as one egg
3. Mix everything together by hand, kneading it lightly and being careful that no lumps form
4. The dough should be neither very hard nor very soft, and knead as quickly as possible so it does not heat up from the warmth of the hands
5. After a quarter hour, flatten the dough slightly with a rolling pin or stick, placing half a pound of butter on top and flattening it as well
6. Fold the two ends of the dough, folding one over the other so that the butter is enclosed in the interior, and let rest for half an hour
7. Give it two turns, let twenty minutes pass, and then give it two more turns
8. Form the pastries and place them in the oven to bake, or use the dough for pastries or whatever is needed
