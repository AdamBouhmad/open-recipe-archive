## Ingredients

- rice
- oil
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- tomato
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Sauté the rice in oil with onion, chopped garlic and parsley, a little tomato.
2. When well prepared, add water in good proportion.
3. A little before it is completely cooked, remove it and let it cool for half an hour.
4. The grains should remain loose, without broth, and not be broken apart.
