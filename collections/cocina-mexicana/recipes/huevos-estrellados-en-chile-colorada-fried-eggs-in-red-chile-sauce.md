## Ingredients

- Pasilla chiles (deveined)
- Wide chiles (deveined)
- Water
- Roasted tomatoes
- Fine salt
- Butter
- Aged cheese (grated)

## Directions

1. Devein some pasilla chiles and add to them one-third as much wide chiles, also deveined
2. Toast all of them
3. In a little water, break them apart with your fingers or grind them with a few roasted tomatoes
4. Add the necessary fine salt
5. Put a pan with butter on the fire and fry well the chile sauce
6. Fry the eggs in it
7. When removing from heat, sprinkle with some grated aged cheese
