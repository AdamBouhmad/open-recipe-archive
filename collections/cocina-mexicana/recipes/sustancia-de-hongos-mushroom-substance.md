## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Curried sauce
- Concentrated broth
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the mushrooms after having cut them and remove the end of the stem
2. Sauté them in a little water with lemon juice
3. Chop them and wring them out in a clean cloth
4. Fry them afterwards in butter with lemon juice until the butter turns to oil
5. Add a few spoonfuls of curried sauce and an equal amount of concentrated broth (see pages 766 and 131)
6. Allow to reduce and add a good amount of black pepper
