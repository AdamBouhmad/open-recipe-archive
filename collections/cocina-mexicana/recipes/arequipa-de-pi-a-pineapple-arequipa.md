## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sugar
- 1 pineapple, ripe
- 0.5 pound almonds
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clarify three pounds of sugar and bring it to half point.
2. Grind one ripe pineapple until very fine.
3. Grind half a pound of almonds, not too finely, leaving some pieces.
4. Add to the syrup to cook.
5. Beat twelve egg yolks, bring to a boil in a pan, remove from heat as soon as boiling stops, and add the egg slowly while another person stirs constantly.
6. Return to heat until it reaches the proper point.
