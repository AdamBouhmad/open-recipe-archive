## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Five ounces of sugar in powder
- Half a pound of ground and sifted starch

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with five ounces of powdered sugar until they become pure foam
2. Then mix into the beaten mixture half a pound of ground and sifted starch, and beat another little with the hand
3. Grease the molds and pour in the beaten mixture in the necessary quantity
4. Place in the oven at two fires to cook
