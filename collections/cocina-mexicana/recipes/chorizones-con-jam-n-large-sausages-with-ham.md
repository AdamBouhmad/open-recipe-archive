## Ingredients

- Pork loins, deveined
- Ham, one pound for each loin
- Strong vinegar, half quart per loin
- Salt, to taste
- Garlic, to taste
- Cumin, to taste
- Saffron, to taste
- Ginger, to taste
- Clove, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Wide chile, deveined and soaked
- Casings

## Directions

1. Devein pork loins and mince them
2. Mix with minced ham, using one pound of ham for each loin
3. Add half quart of strong vinegar per loin
4. Season with salt, garlic, cumin, saffron, ginger, clove, pepper, and wide soaked and deveined chile, all ground
5. Test the flavor and let rest for twenty-four hours
6. Proceed as for the previous sausage article
