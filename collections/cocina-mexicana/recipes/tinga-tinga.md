## Ingredients

- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- chilpocles en vinagre (smoked chiles in vinegar)
- longaniza (sausage)
- carne de puerco (pork)
- aceite (oil)
- queso fresco (fresh cheese)

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of onion and garlic, and smoked chiles in vinegar; fry all this very well
2. Once well seasoned, add fried longaniza and pork meat
3. Remove from heat and add a spoonful of oil and some slices of fresh cheese
