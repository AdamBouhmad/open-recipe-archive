## Ingredients

- squash seeds
- lard
- wide chile
- bread
- water
- salt
- meat

## Directions

1. Fry the seeds in lard with the wide chile needed to give color and bread, without letting it brown too much so the pipián does not become bitter
2. Grind everything on a metate with a brazier underneath, as is done with cacao for chocolate
3. Or, when the mass is soft, remove it and dissolve in hot water and fry in lard with salt
4. When it has boiled, put in this pipián the meats to be cooked in it, already cooked
5. Complete the operation as in the other recipes
