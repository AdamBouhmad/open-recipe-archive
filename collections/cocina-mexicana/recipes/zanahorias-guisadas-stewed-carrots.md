## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Boiling water
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley, chopped
- Milk
- Egg yolks
- Bacon, sliced
- Broth

## Directions

1. After scraping and washing well, blanch in boiling water
2. Cut into strips and place in a casserole with a piece of butter, pepper, salt and chopped parsley
3. Cook, moistening with milk, and when ready, bind with egg yolks
4. If prepared for a meat meal, place in a casserole with bacon slices, parsley, salt and pepper, moisten with broth and reduce until almost a jelly
