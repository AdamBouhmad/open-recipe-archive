## Ingredients

- four ounces of coffee
- two quartillos of cream or boiling milk
- one egg yolk per cup (if making in individual cups)
- sugar
- a grain of salt

## Directions

1. Brown four ounces of coffee in a saucepan
2. When it has a deep color, pour it into two quartillos of boiling cream or milk
3. Cover the mixture to allow the infusion to occur
4. If making in individual cups or bowls, place one egg yolk, sugar, and a grain of salt in each according to taste
5. If making in a medium saucepan or concave dish, maintain the same proportions
6. Allow the cream to set its point in a water bath with heat from above, taking care that it does not boil
7. Strain all cream preparations through cheesecloth or a horsehair sieve
