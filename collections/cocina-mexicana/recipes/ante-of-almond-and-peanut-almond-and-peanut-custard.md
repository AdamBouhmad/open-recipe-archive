## Ingredients

- One pound and a half sugar
- Medium-point syrup
- One pound peeled almond
- Toasted peanuts without shells
- Half ounce ground cinnamon
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Syrup and wine
- Egg threads (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Make medium-point syrup with one pound and a half of sugar
2. Mix in one pound peeled almond, roasted peanuts without shells, and half ounce ground cinnamon
3. Place over heat until it reaches cajeta point
4. Pour over sponge cake moistened with syrup and wine
5. Garnish the top with egg threads
