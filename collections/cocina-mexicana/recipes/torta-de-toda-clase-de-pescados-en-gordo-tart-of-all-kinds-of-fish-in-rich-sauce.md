## Ingredients

- Fish, prepared and cut according to its size and type
- Lard or grated ham
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish and cut it according to its size and type; spread it over grated lard or ham, and follow the same procedures as for the other tarts
2. When these are done, glaze it with sugar and serve hot or cold
