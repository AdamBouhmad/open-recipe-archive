## Ingredients

- one quartillo of cacahuatzentli corn
- twelve eggs
- half a pound of butter
- a medium fresh cheese, grated
- one pound of well-ground sugar

## Directions

1. Make nixtamal with one quartillo of cacahuatzentli corn the same way as with common corn for atole, and after cooking wash it well until it becomes very white
2. Put it to dry, grind it well afterwards, sift through a sieve and put it in a very clean batea or box
3. Mix with twelve eggs, half a pound of butter, a medium fresh cheese grated that is not very buttery, and one pound of well-ground sugar
4. Beat well like nougat until the dough forms eyes
5. Put it in small paper boxes spread with butter without filling them, because the tortillas swell when cooking, which is done in a moderate oven
6. In the absence of an oven, heat a stove, place an extended cazuela on it where the small boxes are arranged, and cover with a comal with fire on top
