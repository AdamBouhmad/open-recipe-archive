## Ingredients

- ham
- lard
- roasted tomato
- fine vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Once the ham is cleaned, slice it to the thickness of a finger or a little more
2. Fry it in a little lard until it begins to brown
3. Add roasted tomato, well ground
4. When it is well fried, add fine thinned vinegar or native vinegar without water, a piece of sugar
5. Let it consume over strong fire, frying the ham well
6. Serve it cold or hot
