## Ingredients

- one regular small pot of milk
- a little more than one ounce of starch
- sufficient sugar

## Directions

1. To one regular small pot of milk add a little more than one ounce of starch and sufficient sugar
2. Place to curdle until at a very high point
3. Let cool and pour into a platter for a day or night
4. Fry with eggs like chiles
5. Sprinkle cinnamon on top
