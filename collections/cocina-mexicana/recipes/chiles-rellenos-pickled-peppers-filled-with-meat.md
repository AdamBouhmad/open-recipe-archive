## Ingredients

- Peppers
- Good vinegar
- Onion, sliced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Garlic, crushed
- Green beans, cooked
- Zucchini, cooked
- Chorizo pieces
- Cooked ham
- Sardines
- Bread crumbs
- Grated cheese
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Beets
- Avocado

## Directions

1. Roast peppers, peel them without tearing them
2. Place in good vinegar with onion slices, salt, pepper, ground cloves, crushed garlic, cooked green beans and cooked zucchini
3. The next day, remove from vinegar, drain and devein on one side
4. Fill with equal parts chopped chorizo, cooked ham and sardines
5. Roll in grated bread and cheese
6. Place on a platter
7. Cover with another layer of the same filling and another of grated bread and cheese
8. Add oil and vinegar
9. Decorate with beets, avocado and onion slices
