## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- wide chiles
- almonds
- hazelnuts
- chile seeds
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast the wide chiles and remove the veins
2. Grind almonds, hazelnuts, chile seeds, clove, cinnamon and pepper
3. Fry the mixture
4. Add to the feet
5. Cook in salt
