## Ingredients

- pasilla chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- bread
- vinegar
- lard
- fish broth
- oil

## Directions

1. Destem and soak four ounces of pasilla chiles
2. Wash well and grind with clean garlic, a handful of cumin, and bread soaked in vinegar
3. Fry in a little lard
4. Add broth from cooking fresh fish
5. Add sufficient oil
6. Once it has boiled well, remove and let rest
7. Bathe cooked or roasted fish with this sauce
