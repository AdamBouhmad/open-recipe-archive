## Ingredients

- cooked poultry livers
- mulato chiles
- cumin
- vinegar
- lard
- raw oil
- oregano

## Directions

1. Grind cooked poultry livers with deveined mulato chiles and a scruple of cumin
2. Dissolve in vinegar
3. Fry well in lard
4. Add raw oil and powdered oregano
