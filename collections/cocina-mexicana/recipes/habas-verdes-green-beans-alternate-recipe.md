## Ingredients

- beans
- salt
- oil
- garlic
- hazelnuts
- bread
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. When small, remove only the ends; when large, peel them entirely
2. Boil in water and salt, then drain
3. Add oil with fried garlic
4. Make a sauce with toasted hazelnuts, a little toasted bread soaked in water and vinegar, pepper, and eggs to match the sauce
5. Dilute the sauce with seasoned water and pour over the beans
6. Let them boil on gentle heat
