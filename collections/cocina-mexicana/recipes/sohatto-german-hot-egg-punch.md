## Ingredients

- four whole eggs with yolks and whites
- four additional egg yolks
- one bottle of old wine
- half pound sugar
- a little ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slowly dissolve four whole eggs and four additional egg yolks in a bottle of old wine
2. Add half a pound of sugar and a little ground cinnamon
3. Pour everything into a deep pot or earthenware jug and place over heat
4. Beat with a molinillo whisk, and as soon as it rises, pour the mixture into coffee or chocolate cups
5. Serve very hot
