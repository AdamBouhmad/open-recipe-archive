## Ingredients

- trout
- blanched almonds
- olives
- cured chile peppers
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- capers
- oil
- vinegar
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the trout
2. Prepare a filling with blanched and split almonds, olives, cured chile peppers, onion and parsley, all chopped and seasoned with salt, pepper, oregano, capers, oil and vinegar
3. Stuff the trout with this mixture
4. Cover with bread crumbs, pepper and salt, sprinkle with oil
5. Wrap in papers coated with butter
6. Grill on a grill, turning carefully, or fry in butter, serving in their papers
