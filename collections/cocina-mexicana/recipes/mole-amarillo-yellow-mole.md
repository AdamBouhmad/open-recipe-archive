## Ingredients

- Yellow chile, soaked
- Roasted garlic head
- Cooked tomato
- Meat
- String beans or zucchini
- Epazote

## Directions

1. Take soaked yellow chile, a roasted garlic head, and cooked tomato
2. Grind everything
3. Add the meat desired, string beans or zucchini, and epazote
4. Season
