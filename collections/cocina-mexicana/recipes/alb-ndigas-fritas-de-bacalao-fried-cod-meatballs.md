## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Bread crumbs
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Black pepper grains
- Cloves
- Saffron
- Beaten eggs
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Bread
- Egg yolks
- Spices
- Black pepper grains
- Whole clove
- Parsley
- Fried onion

## Directions

1. Cook and prepare the cod, remove the bones and chop very well, then mix and knead with bread crumbs, peeled garlic, chopped parsley, grains of black pepper, cloves and ground saffron, beaten eggs and salt.
2. When the mixture is well seasoned, make the meatballs with a spoon, which should be moistened for this in vinegar kept in a cup for that purpose, so the meatball paste does not stick to it, and fry them in very hot oil.
3. The broth or sauce is made with a little fried bread, egg yolks, ground spices, grains of black pepper, whole clove, chopped parsley and a little very finely fried onion.
