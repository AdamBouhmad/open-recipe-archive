## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt
- cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Dissolve butter in a pot and add eggs
2. Season and stir frequently with three or four bundles of straw tied together
3. Add a little cream and at serving time add a bit of lemon juice
