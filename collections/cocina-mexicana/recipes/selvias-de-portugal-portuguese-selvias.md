## Ingredients

- hazelnuts, peeled
- almonds, peeled, equal quantity to hazelnuts
- flour
- sugar
- syrup at high point
- twelve egg yolks, beaten
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel hazelnuts and almonds in equal quantity
2. Toast them with flour and rub with a cloth to remove the skin
3. Grind with a little sugar
4. Place in a small pot with a little syrup at high point
5. Cook until they acquire consistency
6. Add twelve beaten egg yolks, a little pepper, cloves and cinnamon
7. Let everything cook a bit more, but without it becoming hard
8. Make a dough thicker than for wafers
9. Extend and cut in the shape of a ribbon
10. Place them on top of each other, two by two, joining with the fingers
11. Cut with a wheel cutter
12. Fry and then place in syrup at high point
13. Heat briefly and remove one by one with a slotted spoon or skimmer
14. Before they cool, dust with sugar and ground cinnamon
