## Ingredients

- twenty-four well-beaten egg yolks
- one pound very finely ground almonds
- one pound white sugar
- lard
- ground sugar

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-four well-beaten egg yolks with one pound of very finely ground almonds and one pound of white sugar
2. Beat everything again and put in a pan with lard
3. Dust on top with ground sugar and let it cook over two fires
