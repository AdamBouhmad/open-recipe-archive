## Ingredients

- at least half a pound of domestic poultry, fish, or beef
- bread crumb (prepared bread)
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Finely mince at least half a pound of any of these meats
2. Pass through a quenelles sieve
3. Add prepared bread crumbs
4. Grind everything together on a metate with butter, salt and pepper
5. Beat two or three egg yolks and incorporate them
6. Beat egg whites with a wooden spoon and fold in
7. Heat broth or water with butter and salt until boiling
8. Fill a spoon with the prepared filling, smoothing it with a knife dipped in hot water
9. Place the spoon over lukewarm water and empty it onto white paper
10. When the paper is full, bring the broth to a boil and gently place the paper with the quenelles in it
11. Remove quenelles as they detach themselves
12. Move the pot to the edge of the stove and cook for ten minutes
13. Remove the quenelles from the broth
