## Ingredients

- cabbage
- ancho chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- meat broth
- meat scraps
- ribs
- chorizo
- cooked chickpeas
- lard
- tomato
- onions

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbages and put them to cook
2. Once cooked, drain them
3. Grind ancho chile with garlic and cumin, fry it very well and add meat broth, scraps, ribs and chorizo, cooked and clean chickpeas in small quantity
4. Place the cazuela with lard so the cabbages fry and add them to the adobo so they become thick
5. This can also be made with spices, chopped tomato and garlic, large onions and ground cumin
