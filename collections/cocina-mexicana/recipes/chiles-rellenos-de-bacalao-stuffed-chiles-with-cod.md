## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Cod picadillo for meatballs
- Flour
- Beaten egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as previously described
2. Fill with cod picadillo prepared according to the cod meatballs recipe
3. Coat with flour and dip in beaten egg
4. Fry the chiles
5. Prepare the sauce the same way as in previous recipes
