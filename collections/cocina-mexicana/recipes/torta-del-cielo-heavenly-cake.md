## Ingredients

- one pound of rice, cleaned and soaked
- six cuartillos of milk, strained
- sugar as needed
- half a pound of solid pork meat, chopped
- garlic cloves
- lard
- water
- cinnamon
- ground cloves
- white wine
- pine nuts
- pitted raisins
- almonds
- hard biscuits, ground
- ground biscuit for dusting

## Directions

1. Grind the cleaned and soaked rice
2. Cook it with the strained milk and necessary sugar until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco
3. Cook the solid pork meat and chop it
4. Chop it again finely
5. Fry garlic cloves and add the ground meat to lard with a little water, cinnamon, ground cloves, white wine, pine nuts, pitted raisins, almonds, and some ground hard biscuits
6. Butter a cake pan and dust it with ground biscuit
7. Lay a layer of milk and dust with more biscuit
8. Add a layer of the ground meat mixture with biscuit, similar to the previous layer
9. Continue until you have three layers of milk and three of meat mixture, in the order described, with the final layer being milk
10. Sprinkle more biscuit on top
11. Cook between two gentle fires or in an oven
12. Once set, place powdered sugar and ground cinnamon on top
13. Serve cold
