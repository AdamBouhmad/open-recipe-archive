## Ingredients

- quince pulp
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. After the juice has drained, mix the residue remaining in the sieve with powdered sugar
2. Knead and flatten with a roller
3. Cut with the rim of an inverted glass
4. Place these circles on paper and roast in the oven
5. After twelve hours in the oven wrap in paper and place in jars
