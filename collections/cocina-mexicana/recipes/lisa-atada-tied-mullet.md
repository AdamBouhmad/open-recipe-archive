## Ingredients

- bunch of onions
- garlic heads
- oil
- oregano
- thyme
- cloves
- pepper
- lemon juice
- capers
- salt
- lard
- tomatoes
- mullet
- comal
- vinegar
- chiles

## Directions

1. Chop a bunch of onion with three heads of garlic very fine
2. Heat a quarter liter of oil in a casserole and fry the chopped ingredients adding oregano, thyme, cloves and pepper, the juice of a lemon, a handful of capers and salt
3. Grease an earthenware dish with lard and cover the bottom with sliced tomatoes and onions
4. Make incisions along the length of the mullets on both sides
5. Once prepared, place them over the layer of onion and lard in the dish and cover with all the prepared mixture
6. Put the dish on the fire and cover with a comal with plenty of heat on top
7. It should remain with very little broth and like roasted
8. Pour oil on top and add chiles in vinegar
