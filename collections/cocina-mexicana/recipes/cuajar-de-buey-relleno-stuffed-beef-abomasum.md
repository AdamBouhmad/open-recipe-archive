## Ingredients

- two pounds beef abomasum
- red butter
- red butter without melting the size of a fist
- two garlic heads
- parsley
- common butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- three cuartillos water
- three tablespoons vinegar
- one garlic head

## Directions

1. Parboil two pounds of abomasum in red butter and remove
2. Take a piece of red butter without melting the size of a fist, two clean garlic heads, parsley, common butter, pepper and salt, and chop everything together
3. Place this filling in the piece of abomasum and sew around it
4. Make a red broth with a good tablespoon of flour, three cuartillos of water, three tablespoons of vinegar and one garlic head
5. After this sauce has boiled for a quarter hour, cook the stuffed abomasum in it for a long time on low heat, taking care that it does not stick
