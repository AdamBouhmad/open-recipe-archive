## Ingredients

- eight foie gras
- earthnut truffles
- mushrooms, turned
- butter
- salt
- coarse pepper
- Spanish sauce
- Madeira wine
- ice

## Directions

1. Poach eight foie gras and cut into very round buttons
2. Melt butter in a pan
3. Arrange the buttons in the pan with sliced earthnut truffles and turned mushrooms
4. Sprinkle everything with salt and coarse pepper
5. Reduce a little Spanish sauce mixed with half a glass of Madeira wine and a piece of ice the size of a walnut
6. At the moment of serving, fry the buttons by tossing them, drain them
7. Add to the sauce and arrange on a plate
8. Garnish with croutons or fried toast
