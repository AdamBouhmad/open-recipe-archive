## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- water
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled potatoes in a little water, covering the pot with a plate that also has water so the steam finishes cooking them.
2. Beat as for fritters until they form eyes.
3. Beat in a bit of milk and a piece of butter, adding milk little by little until it reaches the desired thickness.
4. After this, do not return it to the heat.
