## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- tomatoes
- parsley
- garlic
- broth
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- vinegar
- breadcrumbs
- cheese

## Directions

1. Pound the tongue, clean it well and then cook it
2. Once cooked, slice it, chop tomatoes, parsley and garlic very finely and fry
3. Once well fried, add to it the broth in which the tongue cooked, straining it first
4. Add ground cloves, cinnamon and pepper, a little vinegar and then add the tongue prepared as stated
5. Let boil quite a bit over slow fire
6. Once they are almost done, dust with breadcrumbs and ground cheese just to give one boil
7. Garnish with capers and chiles
