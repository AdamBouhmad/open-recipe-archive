## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- egg
- sesame
- garlic
- water
- ground biscuit
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- vinegar
- sugar
- lime
- orange leaves

## Directions

1. Clean the feet well and let them cook the night before the day they are to be prepared
2. Remove the broth and cover with beaten egg as if for chiles
3. Fry sesame with minced garlic and when golden, add water to boil vigorously, then add the feet
4. Add ground biscuit mixed with clove, cinnamon and pepper, a little saffron, vinegar and sugar
5. In a separate pot, boil lime and orange leaves
6. After it has boiled a few times, add the broth
