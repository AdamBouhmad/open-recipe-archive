## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cream
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- eggs, beaten
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in hot coals
2. Once cooked, peel and grind them with a little cream
3. Pass through a sieve and add butter, chopped parsley, and beaten eggs
4. Form into balls over bread crumbs
5. Fry them
6. Sprinkle with sugar and orange blossom water
