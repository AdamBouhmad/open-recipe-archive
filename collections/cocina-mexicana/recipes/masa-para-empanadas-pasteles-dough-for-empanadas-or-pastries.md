## Ingredients

- five cuarterones of lard or good butter
- one quartilla of flour
- one ounce of salt
- water

## Directions

1. Put flour on a very clean board
2. Make a hole in the middle of the flour to put the lard in several portions and the salt
3. Add water judiciously and prudently, as too much would make the dough very soft and lacking consistency
4. Unite the lard with the water as well as possible
5. Little by little mix with the flour
6. Once the flour has absorbed all the water, knead well with force, it does not matter that this dough is very stiff as long as it is well sticky and contains no lumps
7. Make this dough at least two hours before using so it does not have time to rest and rise
8. With this dough, all kinds of empanadas are made, such as veal, rabbits, hares, poultry and fish
