## Ingredients

- Whole cabbage
- Chestnuts
- Sausages
- Butter-based sauce

## Directions

1. Parboil a whole cabbage and refresh it afterwards
2. Press well with the hands without breaking the leaves
3. Placing on a table, carefully remove the leaves, removing the core and placing chestnuts and sausages in its place
4. Reassemble all the leaves so it is not known that they contain something inside
5. Place the cabbage to cook over light coals
6. While cooking, let it drain and serve with some sauce that contains butter
