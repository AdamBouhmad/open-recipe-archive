## Ingredients

- Two quartillos of milk
- Two pounds of sugar
- Wafers

## Directions

1. Sweeten two quartillos of milk with two pounds of sugar while cold.
2. Strain the milk and bring it to the fire, letting it boil until it reaches a point higher than the setting point in water.
3. Remove from heat and beat, tinting part of the paste to resemble ham.
4. Before it sets, place on wafers, stacking one paste on top of the other, and cut the sweets as desired.
5. While the paste is over the heat, keep stirring constantly if you want it to be very white and not to burn; but if you want to imitate Westphalian hams, let it burn a little.
