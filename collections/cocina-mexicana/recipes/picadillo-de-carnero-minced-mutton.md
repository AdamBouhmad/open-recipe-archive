## Ingredients

- leftover mutton
- grease
- fine herbs
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. To use leftover meat from one day to another, put it in a frying pan with a little grease, fine herbs, a glass of white wine, salt and pepper
2. Add a little broth and only let it heat without boiling
