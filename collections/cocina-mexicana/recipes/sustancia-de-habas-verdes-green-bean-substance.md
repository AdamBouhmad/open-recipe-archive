## Ingredients

- Large fresh beans
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Broth or water
- Parsley
- Small onions

## Directions

1. Shell the large beans and bring water with sufficient salt to a boil in a pot
2. Put the beans in it and keep them there for a quarter hour
3. Drain them and put them in cold water so they remain green
4. Drain them again and put a piece of butter in a pot with salt, pepper and a spoonful of flour
5. Put the beans in it and moisten them with broth or water
6. Add a small bunch of parsley and small onions, and let the beans finish cooking
7. Pass the substance through a strainer and add a piece of butter to serve
