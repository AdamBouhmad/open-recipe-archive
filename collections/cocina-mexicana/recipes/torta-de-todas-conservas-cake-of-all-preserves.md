## Ingredients

- Slices of marquesote
- Watered honey
- Wine
- Cloves
- Preserves of almonds, citron, and sweet potato, all ground
- Egg yolks
- Betún de puchas (icing)

## Directions

1. Soak marquesote slices in watered honey with a little wine and boiled with two or three cloves
2. Place in alternating layers with preserves, especially almonds, citron, and sweet potato, each layer of different preserve
3. Once the pan or casserole is full, pour beaten egg yolks on top
4. Place in the oven
5. As soon as the cake sets, cover with betún de puchas in decorative patterns
6. Return to the oven for a short time
