## Ingredients

- Four ounces of pasilla chiles
- Cumin, a handful
- A piece of bread soaked in vinegar
- Lard
- Fresh fish broth
- Oil

## Directions

1. Devein and soak four ounces of pasilla chiles
2. After washing very well, grind them with a clean mortar, a handful of cumin, and a piece of bread soaked in vinegar
3. Fry this in a little lard and then add broth from the same in which fresh fish has been cooked and oil
4. Once it has boiled well, remove from heat and let rest
5. Bathe the cooked or roasted fish with this sauce
