## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Lard
- Chicken broth
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Tornachiles
- Pineapple
- Sugar
- White wine
- Vinegar
- Sesame
- Cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Roast well-colored tomatoes, remove the skin, grind them, and fry in lard
2. Add the broth in which the chickens were cooked and season with cloves, cinnamon, saffron, raisins, and almonds, all ground
3. Add tornachiles and pineapple in small pieces, a lump of sugar, white wine, and a little vinegar
4. Boil the cooked chickens in this sauce
5. When well seasoned, sprinkle toasted sesame on top
