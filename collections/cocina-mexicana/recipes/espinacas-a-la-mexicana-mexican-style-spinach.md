## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- garlic, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- lard
- cooked spinach, chopped
- cooked chickpeas, with tequesquite removed and husks removed
- cooked potatoes, sliced
- vinegar
- fine salt
- sugar
- fried bread toasts

## Directions

1. Roast ripe tomatoes well, then grind them thoroughly
2. Add sufficient finely chopped garlic and onion
3. Fry everything in lard
4. When fried, add the cooked and chopped spinach
5. Add cooked chickpeas prepared with a little tequesquite and with husks removed, and sliced cooked potatoes
6. Moisten with vinegar and season with necessary fine salt and a little sugar
7. Serve with fried bread toasts
