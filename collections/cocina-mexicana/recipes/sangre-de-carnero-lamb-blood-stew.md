## Ingredients

- cooked lamb blood
- oil
- lard
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Boil water in a casserole and when it boils add the cooked and sliced blood
2. Put oil and lard in a frying pan and fry the blood slices well
3. Separately chop plenty of tomato and onion with a little garlic and fry it
4. When well fried add the blood that was fried separately
5. Add water in small quantity, clove, pepper, ground cinnamon, parsley, oregano, and thyme
6. Cook it. It should remain with little broth
7. Garnish with chiles and olives
