## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half an ounce of salt
- one egg (white and yolk)
- one good glass of water
- four ounces of oil
- up to one quartillo of oil total

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of flour on a table with a hollow or hole in the middle
2. Add half an ounce of salt, one egg (white and yolk), one good glass of water, and four ounces of oil
3. After moistening the dough, let it rest for two hours
4. Flatten it afterwards, making it very thin, using the rest of the oil, up to a total of one quartillo, adding it to the dough as it becomes absorbed into it
5. It is necessary that all of the oil be consumed
6. This dough is suitable for meatless puff pastries or for fish
