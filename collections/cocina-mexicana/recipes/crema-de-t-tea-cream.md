## Ingredients

- 1/4 ounce green tea (dry, green in color)
- 4 cuartillos milk
- up to 1 ounce tea depending on quality and strength

## Directions

1. Boil 1/4 ounce of green tea with 4 cuartillos of milk, or up to 1/2 to 1 ounce depending on the quality and strength of the tea
2. Follow the remaining procedures explained for tea cream
