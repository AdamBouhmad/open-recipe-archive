## Ingredients

- pipila (young turkey)
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- chiles
- garlic
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning and preparing, slightly flatten the breast and turn the legs
2. In a pot place butter, parsley, onion, chiles and garlic, all finely chopped
3. Turn the bird several times on the grill in this mixture and transfer to another suitable pot
4. Place the bird with its breast covered with bacon strips and well seasoned with salt and pepper
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine and the same amount of broth
