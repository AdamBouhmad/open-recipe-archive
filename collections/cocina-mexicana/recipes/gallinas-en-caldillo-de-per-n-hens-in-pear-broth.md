## Ingredients

- Hens
- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Roasted tomato
- Pears or sour apples (cooked)
- Hen broth
- Málaga wine
- Small whole onions (cooked)
- Ham (pieces)

## Directions

1. Cook the hens and divide into quarters.
2. Fry the quarters in lard, then remove.
3. Fry some garlic cloves in the same lard.
4. Grind the fried garlic with roasted tomatoes and a greater quantity of pears or cooked sour apples.
5. Fry all the ground mixture.
6. Add the hen quarters with equal parts of their cooking broth and Málaga wine, cooked small whole onions, and small pieces of ham.
7. Let the broth season and reduce on moderate heat to the appropriate level for serving.
