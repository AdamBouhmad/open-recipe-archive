## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- sugar
- milk
- red tablet paste

## Directions

1. Dissolve twelve egg yolks with half a pound of sugar in a pan
2. Add two quartillos of milk
3. Place on heat and stir the yolks constantly so they do not burn until everything thickens
4. Pour into a plate over slices of mamones with red tablet paste, well ground and dissolved
