## Ingredients

- Garlic
- Oil
- Bread
- Black pepper powder
- Cooked cod slices
- Oil
- Parsley
- Olives
- Chile strips

## Directions

1. Fry some garlic in oil, and there add soaked and ground bread, and pepper powder.
2. Being everything fried, add the slices of already-cooked cod, and when serving add more oil, chopped parsley, olives and chile strips on top.
