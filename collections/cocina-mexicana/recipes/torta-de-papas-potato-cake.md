## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cooked and peeled
- Biscuit, ground
- Aged cheese
- Eggs, beaten
- Lard

## Directions

1. After cooking and peeling the potatoes, chop some and grind others in a metate with biscuit and aged cheese
2. Mix everything with beaten egg as if for frying
3. Set to coagulate in a buttered baking dish
4. Small tortillas can also be made from the same paste and cooked in spice or in tlemole
