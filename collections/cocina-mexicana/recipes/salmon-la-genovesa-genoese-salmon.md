## Ingredients

- salmon
- red wine
- butter
- flour
- fish or beef stock

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon as in the previous recipe, moistened with red wine covered, and add the same seasoning as before without salting it much.
2. In a saucepan, place a good piece of butter with two tablespoons of flour from a skimming spoon, well mixed.
3. Pass the salmon's cooking liquid through a silk sieve and pour it into the saucepan with the butter, stirring.
4. Place over heat and turn the sauce until it boils, letting it reduce to half.
5. Skim and remove fat, then pass through cheesecloth.
6. Keep hot without boiling.
7. When the fish is to be served, drain it, arrange it on a fish platter, and cover with the sauce.
