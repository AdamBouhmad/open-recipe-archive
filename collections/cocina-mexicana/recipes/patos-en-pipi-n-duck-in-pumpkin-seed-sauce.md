## Ingredients

- Pumpkin seeds, good portion
- Chile anejo, two-thirds
- Mulato or pasilla chile, one-third
- Lard
- Duck quarters, cooked
- Water
- Salt
- Duck broth

## Directions

1. Toast a good portion of pumpkin seeds on a griddle, grind well, and form into a ball like chocolate, cooking it in boiling water.
2. Devein two-thirds anejo chile and one-third mulato or pasilla chile.
3. Brown them in lard, and also brown seeds from the same chiles.
4. Grind with the chiles and fry this mixture well in lard.
5. Dissolve the pumpkin seed mixture in some of the water the ducks were cooked in, fry with the chile, and add more duck broth.
6. Once it has boiled somewhat, add the cooked duck quarters, and before removing from heat, season with sufficient salt.
