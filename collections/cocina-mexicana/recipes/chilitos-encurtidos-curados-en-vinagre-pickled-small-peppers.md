## Ingredients

- chilitos (small peppers)
- agua del pozo (well water)
- vinagre bueno (good vinegar)
- sal de la tierra (earth salt)

## Directions

1. Prepare the barrels by removing one end and loosening the hoops slightly
2. Fill with small peppers packed very tightly, using a man standing on a round board placed over them
3. Cap the barrel with its end and tighten the hoops
4. Through the bung hole, pour with a funnel until full a broth made of two-thirds well water, one-third good vinegar, and one quarter arroba of earth salt for each barrel
5. After two or three days when the peppers are cured, refill the barrels with the same broth
6. Repeat this operation from time to time to prevent the broth from being consumed and the peppers from spoiling
7. Use as garnish for stews and salads
