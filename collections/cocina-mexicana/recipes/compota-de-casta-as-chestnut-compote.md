## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Ash lye
- Sugar, one quarter
- Water, half cuartillo
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts in ash lye, placed in a linen bag
2. Peel them and place them in a pot with one quarter of sugar and half cuartillo of water
3. Let them cook over low heat for a quarter of an hour
4. Before serving, squeeze some lemon juice into the syrup and add ground and sifted sugar
