## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- red wine
- pepper
- ginger
- nutmeg
- cooked small onions
- raisins
- thyme
- marjoram
- butter
- water from cooking onions
- olives
- capers
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the trout, divide into pieces
2. Place pieces in a cooking vessel with salt, red wine, pepper, ginger and nutmeg all ground together, cooked small onions, raisins, a sprig of thyme and another of marjoram, butter and a small amount of the water in which the onions were cooked, using only as much water as necessary
3. Cook the trout in this sauce, turning it carefully, until it is completely dry
4. Serve on fried bread slices, decorated with olives and capers
