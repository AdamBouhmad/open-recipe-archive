## Ingredients

- quince
- salt
- orange juice or sour orange juice
- pineapple
- sugar
- cinnamon
- white wine
- raisins
- almonds
- citron
- pomegranate

## Directions

1. Peel and chop the quince
2. Soak in salted water for twelve hours
3. Remove and drain
4. Place in a platter and add orange or sour orange juice, small chopped pieces of pineapple, sugar and ground cinnamon, white wine, raisins, almonds, citron, and crumbled pomegranate
