## Ingredients

- four ounces of hazelnuts
- four ounces of bitter almonds
- egg white, beaten
- three more egg whites
- two egg yolks
- two ounces of powdered sugar
- half an ounce of flour sifted through silk sieve
- two ounces of very finely ground sugar
- flour and very fine sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Blanch four ounces of hazelnuts and four ounces of bitter almonds by boiling briefly in water
2. Cool and grind them, adding a little beaten egg white to prevent them from releasing oil
3. Beat three egg whites well and mix with two egg yolks beaten separately and two ounces of powdered sugar
4. Stir the mixture well until perfectly incorporated
5. Sprinkle with half an ounce of flour sifted through silk sieve and two ounces of powdered sugar, stirring everything together
6. Fill small paper boxes with this mixture
7. Sprinkle the top with flour and very finely ground sugar
8. After bread has been removed from the oven or at that heat level, bake the biscuits, and hold burning straw at the entrance to give them color
