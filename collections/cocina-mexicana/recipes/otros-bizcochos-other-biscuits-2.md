## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of lard
- half a pound of sugar
- two ounces of sifted wheat starch
- one quarter liter of milk
- a little orange flower water
- eight egg yolks
- leavening

## Directions

1. Knead very well with eight egg yolks and leavening
2. Depending on the weather, four ounces of lard can be reduced and replaced with butter
