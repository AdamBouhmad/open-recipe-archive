## Ingredients

- Zapalote plantains without heart
- Clarified syrup
- Ground cinnamon
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Choose zapalote plantains that have no heart and put them to cook
2. Have clarified syrup ready on the fire so it is boiling when the plantains burst as they cook
3. Peel them as quickly as possible and place them in the syrup
4. Give the syrup a very high point as soon as it is recognized that the plantains are well penetrated
5. Place these on wires or frames with thin rods so they drain
6. Make another clarified syrup at high point and put the plantains in there, then remove and place again on the wires to dry
7. Once well dried, roll them in ground cinnamon and wrap each one separately in papers for storage
