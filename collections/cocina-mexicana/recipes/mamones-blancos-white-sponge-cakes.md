## Ingredients

- twenty egg whites
- eight egg yolks
- two bowls of sugar
- one pound of starch

## Directions

1. Make as the mamones from the previous articles; but the quantities are: twenty egg whites and eight egg yolks, two bowls of sugar, and one pound of starch.
