## Ingredients

- one pound of pine nuts
- milk
- six eggs
- six ounces of sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of pine nuts in milk and grind them
2. Continue adding milk while grinding
3. Dissolve everything in about two cuartillos of milk
4. Beat six eggs and incorporate with the milk, mixing well
5. Add six ounces of sugar and a little salt
6. Cut the molds somewhat wide
7. Coat with lard and fill
8. Prick with a knife so they do not form blisters
9. Send to the oven
