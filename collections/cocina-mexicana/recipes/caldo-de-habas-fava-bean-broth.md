## Ingredients

- fava beans
- garlic
- spearmint
- onion
- cabbage
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the fava beans overnight
2. Crush them in a metate to help remove the skins more easily
3. Wash them very well
4. Place them in a pot with cold water and bring to a boil
5. When they begin to fall apart, add garlic, spearmint, onion, cabbage, lard, and salt
6. Let them cook until the mixture becomes thick
