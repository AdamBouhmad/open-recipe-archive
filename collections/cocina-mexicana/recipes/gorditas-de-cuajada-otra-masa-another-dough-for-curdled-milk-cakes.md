## Ingredients

- Two calderas of nixtamal (cleaned and rubbed)
- Six calderas of curdled milk (neither too squeezed nor too pressed)
- Six egg yolks
- One egg white
- Four ounces of melted lard
- Sifted sugar (to taste)

## Directions

1. For two calderas of cleaned and rubbed nixtamal, take six calderas of curdled milk that is neither too squeezed nor too pressed
2. Add six egg yolks and one egg white
3. Add four ounces of melted lard and sifted sugar to taste
4. Follow the same procedures as described in the previous articles
