## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- One ounce sifted starch
- Salt
- A little clean anise
- Twelve eggs
- Half pound lard

## Directions

1. Mix one pound flour with one ounce sifted starch, salt in powder, a little anise, twelve eggs, and half pound lard
2. Stir everything without any water
3. Place the dough in a pot over gentle heat
4. After a while, remove and form the fritters and fry them
