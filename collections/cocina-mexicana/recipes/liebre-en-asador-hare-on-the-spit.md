## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon strips
- liver
- butter
- garlic
- broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Empty and clean well a hare, from which the blood will be collected.
2. Cut off the front legs, leaving only the hind part called the loin, which will be larded with bacon strips, and placed on the spit about an hour.
3. When cooked, serve with a sauce made with the pounded liver, which will be browned in butter with chopped garlic, moistened with broth and white wine.
4. Add also salt, pepper, vinegar and its own blood.
