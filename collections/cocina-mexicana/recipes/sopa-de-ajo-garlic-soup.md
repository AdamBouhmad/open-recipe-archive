## Ingredients

- lard or oil
- garlic cloves from one large head
- water or broth
- thick bread toasts
- oil for eating

## Directions

1. Place a pan with lard or preferably oil over the fire
2. Chop the garlic cloves from a large head and once they have browned, add sufficient water or broth
3. Arrange thick bread toasts as needed
4. Once it has boiled a little, add eating oil if the garlic was fried in lard
5. Once it has reduced somewhat, remove from heat and serve
