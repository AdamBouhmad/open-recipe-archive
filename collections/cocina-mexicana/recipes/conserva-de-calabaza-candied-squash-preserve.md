## Ingredients

- squash
- lime water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into regular sized pieces
2. Soak pieces for twenty-four hours in infusion of settled lime water
3. Test the water to ensure it coats the tongue, then rinse
4. Cook, testing with a thin straw; if the straw passes through easily it is done
5. Place in cold water and then in fine clarified, strained syrup at medium point and hot
