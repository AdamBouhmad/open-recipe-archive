## Ingredients

- Broth or fat
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Make with broth or fat, add one tablespoon of vinegar, salt, pepper and parsley
2. Let it boil and then serve to reheat the roast or cooked food that remained from the previous day
