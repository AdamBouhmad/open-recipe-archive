## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- egg
- bread crumbs
- oil
- sugar

## Directions

1. After cooking, moisten the salt cod in egg
2. Wrap it in bread crumbs
3. Fry in oil
4. At serving time, sprinkle a little sugar on top
