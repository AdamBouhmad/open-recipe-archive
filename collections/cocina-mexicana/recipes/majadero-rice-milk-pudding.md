## Ingredients

- Ten quartillos of milk
- One pound soaked rice and half pound dry rice
- Two ounces sifted starch
- Egg yolks
- One chicken breast shredded
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook the rice and starch with well-sweetened milk
2. When it is about to reach the proper point add egg yolks so it becomes yellow
3. Beat these with a little water and a shredded chicken breast and orange blossom water
4. Bring to the consistency of white custard
