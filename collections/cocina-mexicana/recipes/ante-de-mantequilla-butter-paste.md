## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one pound of butter
- four egg yolks
- toasted and ground almond
- mamey
- ricotta
- scented pastille

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup of full point with one pound of sugar
2. Add one pound of butter and when it cools, mix with four egg yolks
3. Return it to heat so that these cook
4. Then add a little toasted and ground almond
5. Place layers of this paste on others of mamey, and if desired, the last can be ricotta with scented pastille, placing it at two heats so that it sets
