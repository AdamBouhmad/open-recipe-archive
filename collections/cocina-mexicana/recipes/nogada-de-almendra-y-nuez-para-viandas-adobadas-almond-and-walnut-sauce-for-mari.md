## Ingredients

- garlic
- cumin
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- meat or poultry
- lard
- walnuts
- almonds
- bread
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Make the adobo (marinade) with ground garlic, cumin, pepper, clove, and cinnamon, dissolved with vinegar and water, and seasoned with salt
2. Place the meats or poultry in the adobo and let them pickle or marinate from one day to the next
3. The next day, cook them in the same adobo, adding lard
4. When the meats are cooked, set them aside without removing them from their adobo
5. Grind equal quantities of walnuts and almonds (soaked and cleaned) with bread soaked in vinegar
6. Fry the ground mixture in lard
7. Place the marinated birds or meats with their broth in this frying
8. Let everything boil until the nogada thickens
9. When removing from heat, add raw oil
