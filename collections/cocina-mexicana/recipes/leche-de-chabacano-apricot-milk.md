## Ingredients

- one quart of milk sweetened to taste
- nine apricots, the ripest and sweetest
- a little rice

## Directions

1. Grind the apricots together with a little rice
2. Mix into the sweetened milk and strain through a sieve
3. Cook to regular consistency
4. If making cajeta, do not strain, only regrind
