## Ingredients

- Veal meat
- Orange
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Avocado leaves
- Rosemary sprigs
- Orange leaves
- Chinese laurel
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Marjoram
- Thyme
- Lemon balm
- Almonds
- Sage
- Olives
- Ham
- Sausages
- Onion
- Oregano
- Chiles in vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into pieces and rub with orange and salt
2. Leave for three days
3. Then bathe well with vinegar and leave for another three days, draining
4. When dry, press and leave for another three days
5. Coat a pot with butter and place the meat with wine, vinegar, avocado leaves, rosemary sprigs, orange leaves, Chinese laurel, garlic, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, ginger, marjoram, thyme, lemon balm, almonds, sage, olives, ham, and sausages
6. Cover the pot very well and cook
7. When the meat is cooked, remove the pot to open air, remove the meat, slice thinly, and place on a platter
8. When serving, top with onion, oregano, thyme, olives, vinegar, oil, and chiles in vinegar
