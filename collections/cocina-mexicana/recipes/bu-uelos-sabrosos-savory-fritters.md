## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Eight egg yolks
- Four egg whites
- A small piece of yeast dissolved in milk
- A lump of sugar
- Small amount of butter, melted
- Whole aniseed
- A little lard
- Warm water
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites as if for frying
2. Mix in the flour and yolks
3. Add the yeast dissolved in milk
4. Add the sugar lump and melted butter
5. Sprinkle with whole aniseed
6. Add a little lard and warm water
7. Knead everything well
8. Let the dough sit in the sun for one hour, turning it occasionally so it does not form a crust
9. Stretch out the fritters, fry them, and serve with syrup
