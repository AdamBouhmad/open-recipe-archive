## Ingredients

- almonds with shells, toasted
- salt
- clarified butter
- egg yolks
- flour
- clean and ground almonds
- romanitas
- artichokes in quarters
- capers

## Directions

1. Toast almonds with shells, grind them and fry with salt in clarified butter.
2. After cleaning the bobo fish, stuff them with this mixture and cook over gentle heat with butter.
3. Beat egg yolks with a little flour to cover the bobo fish, and after frying well, place them on a platter or fish dish.
4. Pour over them a thick broth made of clean ground almonds, fried in butter with salt.
5. If desired, decorate the fish platter with romanitas, artichokes in quarters and capers.
