## Ingredients

- pork lard, four ounces per two pounds of beef
- bay leaves
- parsley
- garlic, four or five cloves
- breadcrumbs, two tablespoons
- cooked beef
- capers

## Directions

1. Prepare the lard in a casserole with bay leaves, parsley, garlic and breadcrumbs
2. Add previously cooked beef, cut into small pieces
3. Sauté with low heat
4. Cover with an iron lid and place coals on top so the stew fries evenly
5. Cook about one hour
6. Season and add capers, leaving on the heat a short while
7. Serve hot
