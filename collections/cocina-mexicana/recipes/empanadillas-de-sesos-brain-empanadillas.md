## Ingredients

- Brains
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Cold bread slices
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook the brains well and remove all fibers and membranes
2. Add sufficient salt and ground pepper
3. Place between slices of cold bread, pressing so they adhere well
4. Fry in very hot butter
5. When golden, remove and serve
