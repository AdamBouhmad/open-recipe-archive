## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- ham
- capers
- all spices (whole or ground)
- broad chile powder
- salt
- oregano
- wine
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Carefully debone the suckling pig so it does not break
2. Cut the meat into strips and fill the intestines with ham, capers, and all spices either whole or ground, broad chile powder, salt and oregano
3. Sprinkle the meat with wine, add raisins, almonds and pine nuts
4. Tie it up so nothing escapes
5. Place in a cazuela with three parts wine, vinegar and water, over two fires and take cold and dry
6. If you want to take them hot, thicken the broth with toasted bread or flour
