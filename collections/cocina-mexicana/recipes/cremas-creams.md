## Ingredients

- half cream
- half milk
- half sugar
- a little rennet dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Take half cream, half milk and half sugar, place on fire and let reduce by a third
2. Separate from fire and when almost lukewarm add a little rennet dissolved in water, pour everything into a bowl after passing through a sieve
3. Make it thicken with very gentle fire on top and below, bathe it and serve cold
4. It can also be thickened in a water bath, especially if it is to be served in small cups
