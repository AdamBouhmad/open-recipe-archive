## Ingredients

- Large catfish, cleaned and deboned
- Cinnamon strips
- Garlic pieces
- Avocado or orange leaves
- Vinegar
- Clove powder
- Cinnamon powder
- Pepper powder
- Saffron
- Cooking oil
- Generous wine

## Directions

1. After cleaning a large catfish, remove the bones and stuff it with cinnamon strips, pieces of garlic and avocado or orange leaves
2. Make a sauce of vinegar with clove, cinnamon, and pepper powder
3. Add a little ground saffron and a few drops of cooking oil
4. Mix everything well, dissolve it in generous wine
5. Place the fish in infusion from one day to the next
