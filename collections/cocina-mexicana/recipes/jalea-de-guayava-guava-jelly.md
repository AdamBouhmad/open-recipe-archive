## Ingredients

- Guavas
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds and cores from the guavas
2. Use only these with the pulp
3. Make the jelly by mixing two cuartillos of guava juice with one pound of clarified sugar syrup
4. Proceed as for the jellies in the previous articles
