## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- garlic
- pepper
- cumin
- ground capers
- a little roasted flour
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan over the fire with three tablespoons of butter
2. Add mashed garlic, pepper and cumin, and when half fried add ground capers
3. Add a little roasted flour, necessary salt and water
