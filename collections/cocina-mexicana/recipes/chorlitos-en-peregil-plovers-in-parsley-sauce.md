## Ingredients

- Plovers
- Oil
- Lard
- Salt
- Water
- Onion, sliced
- Parsley
- Capers
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Strong vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. After washing the plovers well, fry them with a little oil and lard
2. When they begin to take color, add water until they are covered with excess
3. Add salt and let them cook until all the water is consumed
4. Add plenty of sliced onion, and when it is fried, add a little boiling water
5. Grind abundant parsley and an equal quantity of capers with clove, cinnamon, and pepper
6. Add a little strong vinegar and a piece of sugar
7. Let them simmer until thickened
