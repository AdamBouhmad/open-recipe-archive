## Ingredients

- rice
- a little warm water
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the rice, set it to dry, grind it, sift it like flour
2. Then put it in a small pan with a little warm water and salt, dissolving it like atole
3. Put it to cook and keep stirring without stopping so it does not become lumpy
4. Once well cooked, remove it, put it on a platter and once cold, add eggs until it dissolves
5. Make them in the shape of wind fritters, and if they need more eggs, add them
