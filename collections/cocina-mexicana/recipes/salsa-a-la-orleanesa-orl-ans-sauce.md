## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 small tablespoons of vinegar
- fine pepper
- shallot
- about half an egg's worth of butter
- 4 or 5 tablespoons of refined brown sauce
- 4 or 5 small pickles, cut in dice
- 3 hard-boiled egg whites, cut in dice
- 4 or 5 anchovies
- 1 cooked carrot, cut in dice
- 1 tablespoon of whole capers

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar, fine pepper, and shallot in a saucepan with the butter
2. Let everything reduce a little and add the refined brown sauce
3. At the moment of serving, put the pickles, egg whites, anchovies cut into small squares, carrot, and capers in the sauce
4. Place over the fire for an instant without needing to boil
