## Ingredients

- Calf sweetbread
- Cold defatted broth
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Finely chopped herbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade with cold defatted broth and an equal amount of vinegar, adding salt, pepper and finely chopped herbs
2. Cook the sweetbread thoroughly
3. Let it cool
4. Soak it in the marinade for one hour
5. Drain well
6. Cut into pieces the thickness of a finger
7. Fry them
8. Serve surrounded by parsley
