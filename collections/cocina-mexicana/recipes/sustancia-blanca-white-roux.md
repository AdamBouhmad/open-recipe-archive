## Ingredients

- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter and flour as described in the preceding article
2. Place the saucepan on a burner with fire not too ardent
3. Stir without removing it until it has heated well, and do not allow it to take color, because this substance is more beautiful the whiter it is
4. It serves to bind the curled sauce and others
