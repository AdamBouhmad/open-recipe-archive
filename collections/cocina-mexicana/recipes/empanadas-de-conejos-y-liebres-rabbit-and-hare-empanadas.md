## Ingredients

- rabbit
- hare

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into quarters and even smaller portions, and break the bones a bit with the back of a machete or kitchen knife.
2. Blanch it, give it a boil, and place it in fresh water before putting it in the empanada.
3. For the hare, remove all the bones, keeping only the meat, and proceed in the same manner: the bones can be used to make a stew, as they always retain some meat attached.
