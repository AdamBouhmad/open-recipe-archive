## Ingredients

- Medium-sized cabbage
- Four lettuces
- Slices of ham
- One sausage
- Two turkey legs or four chicken legs
- Broth, not very salty
- Bundle of parsley
- Two chopped onions
- Two cloves
- Thin bread slices
- A puree of choice

## Directions

1. Boil a medium-sized cabbage with four lettuces and drain them
2. Place them in a pot with slices of ham, a sausage, and two turkey legs or four chicken legs
3. Cook everything in not very salty broth to which a bundle of parsley and two chopped onions with two cloves are added
4. Drain the ham and vegetables separately
5. Strain the broth and degrease it until it is clear
6. Make a crown with thin bread slices in a hollow dish, intermixing the bread, ham, and vegetables, all cut into quarters
7. Fill the middle of the crown with a puree of choice
8. Place the legs on top and the sausage slices around
9. Leave everything over a gentle fire and serve with the broth separately, clarified and as hot as possible
