## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Parsley, chopped
- Onion, tender and chopped
- Pepper in peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cut carrots with a knife into pieces like bottle corks
2. Cook in water with salt and butter
3. Place in a casserole with a piece of butter, parsley and tender chopped onion, salt and peppercorns
4. Add the drained carrots, fry a little and serve
