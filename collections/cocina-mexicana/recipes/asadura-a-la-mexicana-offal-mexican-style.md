## Ingredients

- offal
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- chile ancho strips
- sausages
- ham
- dried chile ancho
- garlic, raw
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the offal with bay leaf, thyme and oregano
2. Fry chopped garlic, onion and tomato and fry with some chile ancho strips, frying well
3. In a separate pot, cook sausages with a piece of ham
4. Once the ham is cooked, cut into strips and parboil in the seasoning with the broth it cooked in
5. Cut the offal into pieces, grind dried ancho chile that has been soaked with raw garlic, clove, cinnamon and pepper, and add everything to the seasoning, except the sausages which are fried separately and placed on the platter over the stew
