## Ingredients

- Apples, peeled and cored
- Water
- Clarified sugar syrup
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Beef marrow
- Bread
- Butter
- Eggs, raw
- Raisins, pitted
- Salt
- Gragea sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and core the apples
2. Boil them briefly in water
3. Remove and drain them
4. Clarify a little sugar syrup and boil the apples in it until they form a paste
5. Before it thickens completely, add sufficient clove, cinnamon and pepper so their flavor stands out
6. Remove from heat
7. Wash beef marrow well and boil it two or three times
8. Remove from water and grind with sufficient bread and butter
9. Add raw eggs, pitted raisins and appropriate salt
10. Grind everything well together
11. Grease a pan with butter
12. Remove sheets of dough, thinned on the metate as for corn pastries
13. Line the entire pan with these sheets
14. Place the apple filling in the center
15. Cover with other sheets of the same dough
16. After covering well, small figures of the same dough can be formed on top
17. Place the pastry over two fires
18. Once cooked, sprinkle with gragea
