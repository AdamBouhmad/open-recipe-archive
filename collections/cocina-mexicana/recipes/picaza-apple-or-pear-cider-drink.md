## Ingredients

- Apples or pears
- Water

## Directions

1. Crush or grind the apples or pears before or during their ripeness
2. Put them into a barrel through its hole
3. Fill immediately with water to a distance of two fingers from the hole to allow room for fermentation
4. As the picaza is drunk, replace it with water until the liquor becomes too weak to drink
