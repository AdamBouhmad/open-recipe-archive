## Ingredients

- media libra de arroz limpio (half pound of clean rice)
- tres cuartillos de leche (three quarters milk)
- rajitas de canela (cinnamon sticks)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Soak half a pound of clean rice the night before
2. Put it in three quarters of milk with some cinnamon sticks and sugar according to taste
3. Let it boil, stirring continuously with a wooden spoon so it does not stick or burn
4. Thicken according to taste
