## Ingredients

- twelve to fourteen large wide dried chiles
- half pound of almonds without removing the skin
- chile seeds
- lard
- water
- pepper
- cloves
- salt
- meats or vegetables for cooking as desired

## Directions

1. One night before serving, devein twelve to fourteen large wide dried chiles
2. Place them to soak in cold water overnight and reserve the seeds
3. The next day, toast half a pound of almonds without removing the skin and the chile seeds
4. Grind both together like chocolate until no skin remains
5. Grind well the chiles that were soaking
6. Fry both ground mixtures in lard
7. When everything is fried, add water and let it boil until it thickens somewhat
8. Then add spices such as pepper and clove, but not cumin
9. Add the meats or vegetables that are to be cooked in the pipián, previously cooked with the necessary salt
10. When ready to remove from the fire, add salt to season
