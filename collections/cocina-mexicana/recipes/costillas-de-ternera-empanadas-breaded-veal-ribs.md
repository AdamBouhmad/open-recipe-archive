## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- oil
- fine herbs
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice or vinegar
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Place the ribs in marinade for two hours with oil, fine herbs, pepper, salt, lemon juice or vinegar. Afterward wrap them in bread crumbs and toast over low heat, serving them dry or with the sauce desired.
