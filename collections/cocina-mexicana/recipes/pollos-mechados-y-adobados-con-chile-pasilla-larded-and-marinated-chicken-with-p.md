## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Thin lard strips
- Pasilla chiles, toasted with their seeds
- Garlic
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Oregano
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Water

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the previous recipe with the difference that pasilla chiles are used instead of ancho chiles, ground with their seeds after toasting
2. Use cumin in place of toasted cilantro among the ground spices
3. Use oregano in place of marjoram
