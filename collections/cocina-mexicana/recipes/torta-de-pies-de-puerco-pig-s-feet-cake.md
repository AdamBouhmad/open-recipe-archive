## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- pork
- wide chile
- cumin
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- wine
- vinegar
- olives
- pickled chiles
- capers
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook and debone the feet
2. Mince pork as for picadillo
3. Grind wide chile, cumin, garlic, pepper, clove and cinnamon
4. Fry everything together
5. Add wine, vinegar, olives, pickled chiles and capers
6. Beat two eggs, butter a cake pan with lard
7. Pour half the beaten eggs into the pan and add the picadillo
8. Cover with the remaining egg and cook at two heats
