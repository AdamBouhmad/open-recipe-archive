## Ingredients

- one coconut
- two pounds of sugar
- a little jicama
- nine egg yolks
- one and a half quarter measures of milk
- half a cup measure of almonds

## Directions

1. Place the coconut with the sweet and the almond to reach half stage
2. Once there, add the cooked and strained milk and the egg yolks
3. Combine everything together and bring to high cajeta stage
4. Have ready a baking dish rubbed with lard or butter with paper on top
5. Pour the sweet and place in a hot oven with a griddle on top with heat until it reaches consistency
