## Ingredients

- Walnuts
- Syrup
- Cinnamon
- One or two red prickly pears

## Directions

1. Clean the walnuts and soak to remove the skin
2. Grind them
3. Mix in clarified syrup with cinnamon, orange blossom water and one or two red prickly pears strained through a sieve
4. When the paste reaches point, arrange over mamones soaked with syrup and orange blossom water
5. Decorate the top layer as is customary and brown it with a griddle with fire on top
