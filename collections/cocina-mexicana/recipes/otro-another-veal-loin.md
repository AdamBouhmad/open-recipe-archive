## Ingredients

- loin
- ham
- pepper
- fine spices
- vinegar
- oil
- water
- garlic head
- onion
- parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Prick the loin on both sides larding it with ham and pepper; grind fine spices washing the metate well with vinegar, coat the loin with oil, adding the spices and leaving it a while in infusion
2. Then place it in the pan to brown in lard, then add water, a crushed head of garlic, onion pieces, the ground spices in which it was in infusion, parsley leaves, and let everything remain until it releases the water leaving only the little sauce
