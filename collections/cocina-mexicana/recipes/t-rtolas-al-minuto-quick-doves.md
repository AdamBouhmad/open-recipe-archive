## Ingredients

- One dozen doves
- Butter
- Salt
- Chile
- Garlic, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth
- Bread crusts, fried

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean and prepare one dozen doves.
2. Give them a turn in the pan in butter and salt; when they have taken color, add chile, garlic and chopped parsley, a pinch of flour, white wine and broth.
3. Once the sauce begins to cook, remove the doves and serve them with fried bread crusts.
