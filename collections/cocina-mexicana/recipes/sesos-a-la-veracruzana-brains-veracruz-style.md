## Ingredients

- sesos (brains)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- huevos (eggs)
- manteca derretida (melted lard)
- nuez moscada (nutmeg)
- pimienta (pepper)
- pan rallado (breadcrumbs)
- salsa de jitomate (tomato sauce)
- plátanos fritos maduros (ripe fried plantains)

## Directions

1. After cleaning the brains well, cook them in water and salt
2. Cut them into small slices and bathe them in a batter prepared beforehand, composed of three eggs, melted lard, salt, nutmeg, pepper and breadcrumbs
3. Fry them little by little, and when they appear golden, remove them and place them on a plate
4. With a spoon add a little clear tomato sauce and ripe fried plantains
