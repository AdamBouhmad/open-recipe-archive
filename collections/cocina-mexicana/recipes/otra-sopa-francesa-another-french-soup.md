## Ingredients

- ground veal loin from inside
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- egg
- flour
- water with salt
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- flour
- French broth
- bread pieces or cooked rice

## Directions

1. Grind veal loin from inside, mixing it with nutmeg, pepper and salt in powder
2. Make small meatballs the size of a ball
3. Roll them in egg and then in flour
4. Cook them in boiling salted water
5. Separately fry plenty of onion, garlic and parsley, all chopped, and a little flour
6. To this add French broth and the meatballs
7. Add some pieces of bread or cooked rice
8. The meatballs can also be cooked in the same broth, but skimming it well
