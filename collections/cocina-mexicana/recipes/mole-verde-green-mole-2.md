## Ingredients

- lard or comal
- squash seeds
- tomatoes
- cooked green chiles
- epazote
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- hairy squash seeds

## Directions

1. Heat in lard or toast on a comal the squash seeds, which are then ground and fried alone so the mole does not break
2. Fry separately tomatoes, cooked green chiles and grind them with a little epazote, clove, pepper and cinnamon
3. Once everything is fried, mix these things with the squash seeds and fry everything together, without adding any cold water at all
4. The quantity of tomatoes should be sufficient so that the mole becomes green
