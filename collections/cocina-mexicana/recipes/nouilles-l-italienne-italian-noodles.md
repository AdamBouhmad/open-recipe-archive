## Ingredients

- pasta dough (same as for ravioli)
- concentrated turtle soup broth
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make the pasta dough the same as for ravioli, rolling it out thin and flat.
2. Let it dry slightly on a clean cloth or napkin until it can be folded without tearing.
3. When slightly dry, roll it up with great care and cut the nouilles, lifting them slightly with the tip of a knife.
4. Spread them on a table and let them dry until ready to use.
5. To serve, bring them to a boil in the same concentrated broth from turtle soup, leaving them in for ten minutes.
6. Once they rise to the surface, they are cooked; transfer them to the soup tureen and serve with grated cheese on the side.
