## Ingredients

- Frog legs
- Flour
- Oil, butter, or lard for frying
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the frog legs by blanching in boiling water and then placing in cold water
2. Pat dry the frog legs
3. Roll them in flour
4. Fry in oil, butter, or lard until well browned
5. Serve very hot, sprinkled with lemon juice
