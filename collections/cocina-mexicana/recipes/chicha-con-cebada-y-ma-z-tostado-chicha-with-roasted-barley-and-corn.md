## Ingredients

- dark corn (as much as fits on one plate)
- barley (as much as fits on eight plates)
- sugar
- twenty cuartillos of boiling water
- two white corn cobs
- one quarter ounce of cinnamon
- one nutmeg
- twenty cloves

## Directions

1. Toast the dark corn and barley.
2. Crush both toasted ingredients with a little sugar.
3. Pour everything into a pot with twenty cuartillos of boiling water and add two white corn cobs.
4. Grind one quarter ounce of cinnamon, one nutmeg, and twenty cloves.
5. Tie this ground mixture in a clean cloth and place in the pot.
6. Cover the opening with a cloth, place in the sun and exposed to the night air.
7. After eight days, remove carefully without stirring so it does not become turbid.
8. Strain and sweeten, adding spices again if needed.
