## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of flour
- three tablespoons of powdered sugar
- a piece of good butter, about the size of half an egg
- half a lemon rind, chopped, or its equivalent of orange blossom
- four egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- about one and a half quartillos of good cream

## Directions

1. Put the flour, powdered sugar, melted butter, and lemon rind or orange blossom in a vessel
2. Mix everything together
3. Add the four egg yolks one by one, incorporating them well with the other ingredients
4. Add a pinch of salt
5. Add about one and a half quartillos of good cream
6. Pour this mixture into small timbales, prepared as for pastry pies (see PASTELES)
7. Bake until set
