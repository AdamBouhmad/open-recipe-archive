## Ingredients

- blood from beef
- fat from beef, or pork fat if insufficient
- sweet and hot pimentón

## Directions

1. In places where beef cecina is made for yearly provision, make morcilla with the blood and fat of the animal
2. If fat is lacking, add a portion of pork fat
3. Season the same as the preceding morcillas, including both sweet and hot pimentón
4. Stuff and cook like the previous version
