## Ingredients

- robalo, desalted and cleaned
- oil
- lard
- three ancho chiles
- ash water
- garlic head
- cumin
- onions, cooked
- vinegar
- capers
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- oregano
- oil

## Directions

1. Desalt and clean the fish, dry it and cut into portions
2. Fry in oil and lard
3. When all is fried, soak three ancho chiles in ash water, remove veins
4. Grind a head of garlic and some cumin
5. In the same pan where the fish was fried, add a little water to form a thick broth
6. Add cooked onions separated into sections, a little vinegar, capers, olives, chiles in vinegar, oregano and plenty of oil
7. Put the fish in the broth, which should boil very little
8. Decorate the platter with olives
