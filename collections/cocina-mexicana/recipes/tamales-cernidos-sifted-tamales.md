## Ingredients

- peeled corn
- lard, melted
- broth

## Directions

1. After peeling the corn, rub it well to remove the skin
2. Place in the sun to dry
3. Grind on a metate and pass through a fine ayate
4. To each libra of this powder add half a libra of melted lard and a little broth to help it loosen
5. Do this in a pot so it can be beaten
6. Beat until it sounds like turrón (nougat candy)
7. Prepare the picadillo as for stuffed chiles
8. Wrap and assemble the tamales
