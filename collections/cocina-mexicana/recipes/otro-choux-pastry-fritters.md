## Ingredients

- milk
- butter
- flour
- eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Bring a cup of milk to a boil with a tablespoon of butter.
2. Once it comes to a boil add one equal cup of flour little by little, stirring constantly so it doesn't form lumps.
3. Once it pulls away from the pan well, remove from heat, and when it is cold gradually mix in four eggs, one by one, beating with a spoon until it forms eyes and drop it into the butter with a spoon, rolling them hot with ground sugar.
