## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1/2 ounce salt
- 4 or 5 eggs
- 6 ounces butter
- a small amount of saffron powder
- 6 ounces candied grapes
- 2 ounces Corinth or muscatel raisins
- a little yeast
- warm water

## Directions

1. Put the flour on the table and make a hole in the middle
2. Place the salt, eggs, butter, saffron powder, candied grapes, raisins, and yeast in the hole
3. Mix and blend with a little warm water, leaving the dough soft and well bound
4. Place the dough in a buttered pot
5. Let it rest for 5 or 6 hours in winter
6. When it has risen, bake in the oven as you would with rolls
