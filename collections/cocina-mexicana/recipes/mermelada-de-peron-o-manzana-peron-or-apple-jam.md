## Ingredients

- Perons, peeled
- Water
- 20 ounces sugar per pound of thickened fruit

## Directions

1. Peel the perons and cook in water
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Dry what has been strained over the fire until it acquires some consistency
4. Dissolve in syrup at high feather stage, which is heated without boiling
