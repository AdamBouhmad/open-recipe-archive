## Ingredients

- prepared pastry dough
- meat such as turkeys, hares, game birds, rabbits, ducks, squabs or partridges
- ham
- filling

## Directions

1. Pass the meat through butter - bone game birds like turkeys, hares, game birds and rabbits but leave ducks, squabs and partridges whole
2. Cook the ham beforehand
3. Lard everything except the ham with pieces of varying size and seasoned
4. Take a piece of prepared pastry and form a ball or oval by flattening and reducing it to the thickness of a finger on two sheets of wrapping paper
5. Leave a border of three or four fingers on the pastry
6. Place the meats inside filling the gaps with filling
7. Press with the hands to make everything a single mass and cover with slices of ham
8. Form a cover with the pastry with an opening in the middle, insert a rolled piece of cardboard in the opening
9. Bake over high heat where it must remain for at least three hours
10. Remove the cardboard and close the opening with a little of the same pastry
