## Ingredients

- loins
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- strong vinegar
- bay leaf
- thyme
- marjoram
- water
- oil
- powder pepper
- olives
- tornachiles

## Directions

1. Washed and drained loins, leave them lengthwise without the cuts penetrating to the other side, and well rubbed with salt powder, pepper and crushed garlic, cover the cuts with chopped parsley, and arrange the meat in a deep pot with the cuts facing upwards
2. Cover with strong vinegar, bay leaf, thyme and marjoram, and leave for four days in infusion with the pot well covered
3. After four days add water to cook it, and as soon as the broth is consumed, remove the pot from the heat, drain the meat and press it between two boards after wrapping it in cloth and placing weight on top
4. The next day add oil, vinegar, ground pepper, olives and tornachiles
