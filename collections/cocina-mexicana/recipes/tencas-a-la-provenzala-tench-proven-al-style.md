## Ingredients

- tench
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- olive oil
- white wine
- onions
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place in boiling water and remove immediately
2. Clean them
3. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and chopped parsley
4. Place on a plate with good olive oil and half a measure of white wine
5. Bake in the oven
6. Cut six large onions into rings and fry in oil
7. Once cooked and well browned, drain them
8. Form a ring around the tench with the onions
9. Serve with lemon juice
