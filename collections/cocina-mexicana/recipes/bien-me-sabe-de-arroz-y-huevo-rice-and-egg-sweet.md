## Ingredients

- twelve cuartillos of milk
- half a pound of ground almond
- four ounces of rice, also ground
- twenty egg yolks
- one stick of cinnamon

## Directions

1. To twelve cuartillos of milk add half a pound of ground almond, four ounces of rice also ground, twenty egg yolks and one stick of cinnamon
2. The point is to curdle; when a little is thrown in cold water it should set
