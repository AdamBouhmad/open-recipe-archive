## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- milk
- flour
- ginger powder
- refined Catalan brandy
- salt
- butter
- rum
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat six to eight egg yolks in one-quarter cuartillo of milk, mixing until the beaten mixture is thick
2. Gradually add flour that has been mixed with milk (prepared beforehand), stirring and mixing constantly
3. Add two tablespoons of ground ginger, one cuartillo of refined Catalan brandy, and a little salt, stirring and mixing well
4. Make sure the pan is well washed and clean, place a piece of butter the size of a nut in it
5. Pour the batter so it covers the bottom, making it three times thicker than the French crêpes
6. Sprinkle the pancakes on plates with rum and sugar; they are dry and crusty
