## Ingredients

- One oto (bustard), gutted
- Ham, loose cut, closer to meat than skin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Nutmeg, scraped
- Laurel

## Directions

1. After gutting the oto, beat the belly and break the hip bones.
2. Cover with loose ham, the part closest to the meat rather than to the skin, seasoned with salt and pepper, adding clove, scraped nutmeg and laurel.
3. Put much pounded ham in the pastry and other whole slices because this bird has very dry meat.
4. Form the pastry in the common manner and leave it in the oven at least four hours for it to cook.
