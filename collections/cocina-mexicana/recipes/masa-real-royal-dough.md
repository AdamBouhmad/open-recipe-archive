## Ingredients

- two glasses of water
- four ounces of fresh butter
- four ounces of sugar
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- salt
- flour
- one or two eggs

## Directions

1. Place water, fresh butter, sugar, finely chopped lemon zest, and salt in a pot or skillet
2. Put over fire, and when it begins to boil, move the skillet to the edge of the stove
3. Remove the lemon zest
4. Add flour little by little, as much as the water can absorb, stirring constantly and without ceasing
5. Place over fire for five minutes and remove when it reaches the right point, which will be known when the dough pulls away from the skillet
6. Then empty it into a cold earthenware vessel, adding one or two eggs until the dough sticks between your fingers
