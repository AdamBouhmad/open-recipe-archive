## Ingredients

- syrup at large feather point stage
- violets
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- half a pound of violets

## Directions

1. Make syrup at large feather point stage (see page 26)
2. After removing from heat and when half warm, dissolve in it the violets pounded in a mortar and passed through a sieve
3. One and a half pounds of sugar are needed for half a pound of violets
