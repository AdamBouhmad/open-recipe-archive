## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- egg whites
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Lightly roast the chestnuts on a grill over gentle heat so that the shells can be removed
2. Once clean, coat them with beaten egg whites
3. Roll them over powdered sugar
4. Place them on a sieve to dry in the oven
