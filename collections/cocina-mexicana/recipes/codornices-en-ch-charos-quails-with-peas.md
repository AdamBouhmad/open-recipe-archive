## Ingredients

- quails
- veal slice
- ham slice
- carrots
- onion
- aromatic herbs
- bacon strips
- greased paper
- cooked peas with ham

## Directions

1. Cook in a saucepan with a slice of veal and a slice of ham, carrots, onion, and a bunch of aromatic herbs
2. Immediately cover with strips of bacon and greased paper
3. Put fire above and below
4. After removing the broth, add cooked peas with ham
