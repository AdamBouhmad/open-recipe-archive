## Ingredients

- meat
- ham
- garlic cloves
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Stud the meat with ham, garlic cloves, cloves, cinnamon, and pepper and add salt.
2. Leave thus for four days, turning it each day.
3. After, place one entire day between two boards.
4. Cook in water and vinegar.
5. Once cooked, press again between the boards and wrap in a napkin, ready to eat.
