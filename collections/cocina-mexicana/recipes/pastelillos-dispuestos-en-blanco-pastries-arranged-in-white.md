## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- cooked poultry
- fat
- cow udder
- ham
- breadcrumbs
- cream
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- white strained broth or cream

## Directions

1. Form the pastries as in the previous article
2. Fill them with a minced mixture of cooked poultry, fat, cow udder, parboiled ham, breadcrumbs soaked in cream, onions, salt and pepper, all chopped and mixed and bound with five egg yolks
3. After completing and brushing the pastries with egg, put them in the oven
4. When cooked, remove the filling and dilute it in white strained broth
5. Heat without boiling
6. Put it back into the pastries
7. This operation can be done with cream and egg yolks in place of strained broth
