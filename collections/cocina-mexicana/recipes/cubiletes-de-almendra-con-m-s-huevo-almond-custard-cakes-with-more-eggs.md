## Ingredients

- Fifteen eggs
- Half pound of almonds, ground
- One and a half fistfuls of sifted sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make the same as the previous recipe, with the only difference being fifteen eggs and one and a half fistfuls of sifted sugar
2. Do not boil in syrup
