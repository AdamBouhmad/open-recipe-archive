## Ingredients

- lengua cocida
- adobo del lomo de cerdo
- manteca
- caldo de la lengua
- perejil
- aceitunas
- chilitos
- huevo cocido

## Directions

1. Using already cooked tongue, peel it and slice into pieces
2. Marinate the slices with the adobo for pork loin
3. Burn lard in a pan and fry all together
4. Add broth from the tongue and finish cooking over two fires until the adobo and lard are consumed
5. Garnish with parsley leaves, olives, small chiles and quarters of hard-boiled egg
