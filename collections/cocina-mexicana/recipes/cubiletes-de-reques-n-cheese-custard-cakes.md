## Ingredients

- Two eggs, separated
- Ricotta cheese, enough to fill one cup
- Toasted and ground mamón in necessary quantity
- Syrup at half point
- Cinnamon
- Almonds, walnuts, raisins, pine nuts, and hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately until stiffened
2. Mix them with ricotta cheese and toasted ground mamón to form a dough of proper consistency
3. Fill molds and place in the oven
4. When cooked, remove from molds and make small cuts so the syrup penetrates
5. Place in syrup at half point, boiled with cinnamon, leaving until syrup is consumed
6. When dry, decorate with almonds, walnuts, raisins, pine nuts and hazelnuts in small pieces
