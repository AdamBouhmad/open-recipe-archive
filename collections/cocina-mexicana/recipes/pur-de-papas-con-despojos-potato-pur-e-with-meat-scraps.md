## Ingredients

- 6 onions
- 6 medium carrots, sliced
- meat and poultry scraps
- 1 glass water or broth
- 1 bouquet garnished with cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Place in a pot six onions and six medium carrots made into slices, and on top throw the scraps of meat and poultry with a glass of water or broth.
2. Place over high heat.
3. When all has taken on a nice color, dampen with boiling water, adding a bouquet garnished with cloves and a little salt.
4. Leave to cook for an hour and a half over moderate heat, pressing and compressing the meats during cooking so they release their juice.
5. Pass through a sieve and when cold pour slowly into the pot or serving dish.
6. The meats are put in the pot and communicate good flavor to it.
