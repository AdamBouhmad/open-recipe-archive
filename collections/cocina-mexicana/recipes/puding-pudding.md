## Ingredients

- three cuarterones of biscuits
- cream
- milk
- six ounces of bone marrow
- eight ounces of powdered sugar
- fresh butter
- some raisins
- Malaga wine
- four ounces of macaroni
- eight egg yolks
- one glass of rum

## Directions

1. Take three cuarterones of biscuits and break them into small pieces
2. Soak them in cream
3. Cook with milk, adding it little by little as the bread thickens
4. When it forms a broth not too thick, remove from heat
5. Clarify six ounces of bone marrow and add to the pot
6. Add eight ounces of powdered sugar with fresh butter, some raisins, Malaga wine and four ounces of macaroni
7. Boil this mixture for twelve minutes, stirring constantly
8. When at the right point, let it cool in a porcelain dish
9. Add eight egg yolks and one glass of rum
10. Mix everything and place in a mold and cook like a rice cake, but over good heat
