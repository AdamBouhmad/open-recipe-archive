## Ingredients

- Veal pulps
- Strips of ham
- Garlic cloves
- Cloves
- Lard
- Salt
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Sugar
- Sausage pieces
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Ground cinnamon
- Roasted and pressed tomatoes
- Almonds in quarters
- Raisins

## Directions

1. Cut the veal pulps into proportional sizes
2. Lard them separately with strips of ham, garlic cloves, and cloves, ensuring everything is very compact
3. Place in a pot with lard, salt, sufficient wine, little vinegar, water, and a lump of sugar according to the amount of broth
4. Add pieces of sausage, slices of ham, sliced chorizo, plenty of ground cinnamon, roasted and pressed tomatoes, almonds in quarters, and raisins
5. Cover the pot with a proportional small cooking vessel, sealing the joints with dough
6. Place on the fire, handling occasionally with your hands in the air so the meat does not stick
7. When the meat is recognized to be cooked, set aside
8. If it has not yet cooked and the broth has reduced, add wine and return to the fire until it cooks through
