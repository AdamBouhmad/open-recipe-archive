## Ingredients

- chicken
- lard
- onion
- spices
- salt
- white wine
- water
- chicken livers
- garlic

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, put lard in the pan and lightly fry the chickens.
2. Then place them in the pot where they are to cook and in the remaining fat fry some onion and add it over the chickens with all the spices.
3. Next add a little salt and a glass of white wine, and put over low heat with a small pot of water on top, and a cloth or paper to avoid evaporation of the substances, and it should be like this for about an hour.
4. Lightly fry the chicken livers, crush them with a clove of garlic, mix them with the broth and well seasoned, cover again and let it boil a couple of times, with which an excellent sauce comes out.
