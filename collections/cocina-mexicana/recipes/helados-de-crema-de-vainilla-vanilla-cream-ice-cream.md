## Ingredients

- 0.5 ounces of vanilla
- sugar
- 1.5 quartillos of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 12 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Open and cut into small pieces half an ounce of vanilla, then bruising it in a marble mortar with a little sugar, until pulverized
2. Place it then in a vessel on the fire with a quartillo and a half of milk, eight ounces of cream and twelve ounces of sugar, letting everything boil, until it has regular consistency
3. Pass this composition through a cloth, and being cold, pour it into the sorbetera to freeze
