## Ingredients

- Chicken quarters
- Lard
- Toasted and ground roasted tomatoes
- Roasted and ground cloves
- Black pepper, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Cilantro, ground
- Vinegar
- A little sugar
- Parras wine
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Ham pieces

## Directions

1. Fry roasted and ground tomatoes and eyes in lard
2. Add ground cloves, pepper, cinnamon, and cilantro
3. Add vinegar, a little sugar, and Parras wine
4. Place chicken quarters in this sauce
5. Beat egg yolks with lemon juice and add to the stew
6. Add ham pieces
7. Let everything simmer together over gentle heat
