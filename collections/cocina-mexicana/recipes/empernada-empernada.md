## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Two egg yolks
- Lukewarm water with salt
- Abundant lard
- Veal
- Pork
- Salt
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with two egg yolks and knead strongly until the dough is smooth, adding a little lukewarm salted water
2. When well kneaded, roll out with a rolling pin and place about one tablespoon of filling on each empanada
3. Fold the dough and cut with a wheel, giving it the customary shape
4. Heat abundant lard until very hot and fry the empanadas until golden
5. For the filling, cut veal and pork into large pieces and cook with salt
6. When well cooked, chop fine until very ground and fry with tomato, onion, and garlic that have been well fried beforehand
7. When half fried, add a little vinegar and let dry until the meat turns very golden
