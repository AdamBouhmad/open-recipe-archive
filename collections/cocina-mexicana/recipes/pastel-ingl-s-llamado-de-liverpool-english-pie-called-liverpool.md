## Ingredients

- half a cuartillo of pure milk
- three handfuls of flour
- salt
- a piece of butter
- one quarter pound of Gruyère cheese
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Put the milk in a pot over heat and when it is warm gradually add three handfuls of flour, stirring constantly
2. When it no longer forms small balls add some salt, a piece of butter and one quarter pound of Gruyère cheese cut into very thin slices
3. Continue stirring the mixture until everything is perfectly mixed
4. It is done when stirred with a spoon it comes away easily from the pan
5. Remove from heat and add three perfectly beaten eggs and keep hot until serving
6. Put a buttered pan over heat, place over moderate but lively heat and beat for ten minutes so the pie remains very pleasant to the eye and tasty to the palate
