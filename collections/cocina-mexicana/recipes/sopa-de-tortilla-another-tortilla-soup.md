## Ingredients

- Half a dozen small tortillas, divided into quarters
- Lard for frying
- One onion, chopped
- One tomato
- A quarter measure of cooked chard, ground
- Three fried garlic cloves
- Three large peppers
- Chorizo
- Pork loin
- Ham
- One tablespoon of oil
- Half of vinegar
- A handful of grated cheese
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Take half a dozen small tortillas, divide them into quarters and fry them in lard.
2. Chop one onion and one tomato, grind a quarter measure of cooked chard, fry three garlic cloves and three large peppers.
3. Fry everything together, cook chorizo, pork loin and ham, cut into small pieces and fry.
4. Use the water in which all this was cooked for the sauce.
5. Add to it the fried mixture with one tablespoon of oil, half of vinegar, a handful of grated cheese and salt to taste.
6. Pour a little broth in the casserole, a layer of tortillas, one of cheese and another of chorizo, ham and loin and continue thus until the casserole is full and place it over two fires.
