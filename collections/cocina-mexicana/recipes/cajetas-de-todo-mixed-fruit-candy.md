## Ingredients

- Pears
- Thirty-six pounds of sugar for eighteen pounds of pear
- One pound of cooked pineapple, in small pieces
- Seven quartillos of peach jelly
- Six quartillos of apple jelly
- Eight pounds of white sweet potato, sifted
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook pears, peel and slice them
2. Place in syrup at breaking point made with thirty-six pounds of sugar for eighteen pounds of pear
3. Add one pound of cooked pineapple in small pieces
4. When this reaches point, add seven quartillos of peach jelly and six of apple jelly, letting it boil until it recovers its point
5. Set aside and add eight pounds of sifted white sweet potato, and when it is well dissolved return the mixture to the fire so it reaches jelly point
6. Finally add orange blossom water, set aside and pour into the molds
