## Ingredients

- three cuartillos of milk
- sufficient sugar
- two orange leaves
- seven egg yolks
- one egg white

## Directions

1. Boil three cuartillos of milk until reduced to two
2. Sweeten sufficiently and let boil another while
3. Add two orange leaves and give two boils, remove and stir so it does not form lumps
4. When lukewarm, mix with seven egg yolks and one egg white that have been beaten beforehand
5. Strain everything through a colander
6. Prepare a pot with powdered sugar made into light-colored caramel by means of fire
7. Pour the preparation into this pot and cook in a water bath
