## Ingredients

- sorrel, proportional in quantity to the substance to be made
- three or four heads of lettuce
- a handful of chervil, very clean
- a good piece of butter
- chopped mushrooms
- shallots
- parsley
- four spoonfuls of curled sauce, more or less according to the quantity
- or one tablespoon of flour as an economical alternative
- broth from the pot
- pepper and salt
- five or six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop the sorrel together with the lettuce heads and chervil and squeeze to extract the juice
2. Put a good piece of butter in a saucepan with chopped mushrooms, shallots and parsley that are fried in it
3. Place the sorrel on top of the herbs and allow it to cook
4. When all ingredients are cooked to the proper point, add four spoonfuls of curled sauce, or one tablespoon of flour if using the economical method
5. Moisten the substance with broth from the pot, adding pepper and salt
6. When it has consumed the proper amount, strengthen it with five or six egg yolks, pass it through a sieve, and place it in a saucepan for use as needed
