## Ingredients

- picadillo
- lard
- masa dough (like ravioli dough)
- wine
- sugar
- chicken
- flour
- artichoke hearts
- cauliflower
- cooked eggs
- parsley leaves
- large capers
- olives
- chile peppers
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a picadillo and coat a cazuela with lard
2. Place in it a not-too-thin sheet of dough made like ravioli dough, adding wine and sugar so it extends from the sides
3. Over this arrange half of the picadillo and all the garnishes of previous recipes
4. Over this place the cooked chicken in quarters, coated in flour and fried, with artichoke hearts and cooked cauliflower
5. Add the same garnishes as below
6. Follow with the other half of the picadillo
7. Over it place another sheet of the said dough
8. Join the two sheets making the seal upward and add some decorative pieces from the same dough
9. Coat well with lard, place in the oven taking care it does not burn
10. When the dough sheets are known to be cooked, remove it and before it cools, pour sugar mixed with powdered cinnamon on top
