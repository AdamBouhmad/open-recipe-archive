## Ingredients

- guavas
- sugar
- tejocote jelly
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel the guavas and put them on the fire until they come to a boil; remove the insides and grind well
2. Pass the insides through a sieve or cloth and chop everything finely
3. Make a syrup at ball point with one and one-quarter pounds of sugar for each pound of guava
4. Once ready, add the paste with half a cuartillo of tejocote jelly for each pound of paste
5. Reach the point where a paddle moistens and enters; when it comes out clean, beat much until it cools, then add three egg whites beaten and strained for every five pounds of guava
