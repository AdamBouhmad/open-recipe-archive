## Ingredients

- ripe mameys
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- beaten egg
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut some ripe mameys into quarters, and add raisins, almonds and pine nuts; cover two or three times with beaten egg, fry and put them in the sauce of the previous articles, with plenty of cinnamon.
