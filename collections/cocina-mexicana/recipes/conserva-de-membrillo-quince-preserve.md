## Ingredients

- Quinces with skin
- Water
- Syrup at regular point
- Sugar

## Directions

1. While the quinces are cooking in water with their skin, prepare syrup at regular point
2. Once the quinces are cooked, peel and cut into slices
3. Add to the syrup and let boil until it reaches point
