## Ingredients

- tripe
- water
- lime
- sour orange (naranja agria)
- onion
- sour orange peel

## Directions

1. Empty the tripe of dirt, soak in a little water, and wash well.
2. Turn the stomach and all the tripes.
3. Put the stomach in boiling water, then after a time remove and clean by scraping with a knife.
4. Once well scraped, dissolve a piece of lime in a little water, place the stomach in it, and keep it there for half an hour.
5. Then rinse well and transfer to a pot together with the tripes, also rinsed.
6. Squeeze sufficient sour orange over these offals, crush some onion roots, and with the sour orange peels that were squeezed and the resulting broth, clean all the tripes and stomach.
7. When they have turned white, transfer to clean water.
8. Leave them there to form braids from the tripes, and if necessary, cut up the remaining offal pieces.
