## Ingredients

- ripe xitomates (tomatoes)
- garlic
- lard
- water from cooking fish
- poblano chiles

## Directions

1. Roast ripe xitomates and remove the skin
2. Add a clean head of garlic
3. Grind everything well
4. Fry the xitomate mixture in a little lard
5. Add a little water from the cooking of the cod or other fish
6. When the sauce has boiled well, roast poblano chiles
7. Shred the chiles
8. Finish seasoning the sauce and remove from heat
