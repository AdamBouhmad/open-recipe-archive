## Ingredients

- 1 and 1/2 pounds sugar
- 1 pound ground ricotta
- 7 cooked and ground egg yolks
- 1 cup clean pine nuts
- cinnamon to taste
- clove to taste
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of joining in water with 1 and 1/2 pounds of sugar
2. Off heat, mix in 1 pound of ground ricotta, 7 cooked and ground egg yolks, 1 cup of clean pine nuts, and cinnamon and clove to taste
3. Beat so it thickens a little and pour into a dish
4. Immediately grease with butter and set it to set and brown at two heats
5. After it cools, dust with ground cinnamon
