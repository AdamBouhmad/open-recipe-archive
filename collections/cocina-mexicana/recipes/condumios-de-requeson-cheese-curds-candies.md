## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 16 cooked egg yolks
- 21 ounces cheese curds (requeson)
- 2 ounces almonds, soaked
- 3 drams ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with the sugar and bring it to the soft-ball stage (punto de juntar en el agua)
2. Remove from heat
3. Mix the cooked egg yolks with the cheese curds and soaked almonds, grinding them together well
4. Combine this mixture with the syrup
5. Return the pan to the fire and bring the mixture to the hard-ball stage (punto de cajeta bien marcado)
6. Remove from heat and beat well
7. Mix in the ground cinnamon
8. Pour into a wooden mold lined with paper and wafers (oblea)
9. Cut into desired shapes
10. Alternatively, the paste can be poured into small colored paper boxes
