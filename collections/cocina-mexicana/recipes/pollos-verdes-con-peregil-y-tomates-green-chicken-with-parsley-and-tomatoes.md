## Ingredients

- pollos
- manteca
- ajos
- cebollas
- xitomates
- sal

## Directions

1. After the chickens are cooked, fry them in butter, then remove them
2. Fry garlic, onions and tomatoes, all finely chopped, in the same butter
3. Season with salt and continue the preparation
