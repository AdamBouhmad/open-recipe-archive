## Ingredients

- Chile mulato
- Cooked tomatoes
- Pork meat
- Cooked potatoes
- Water
- Salt
- Peppercorns, ground

## Directions

1. Toast and grind chile mulato and cooked tomatoes
2. Fry and add pork meat
3. Add cooked potatoes in pieces
4. Add water and salt with ground peppercorns
