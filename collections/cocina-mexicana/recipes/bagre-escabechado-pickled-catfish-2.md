## Ingredients

- catfish
- lemon
- flour
- oil
- lard
- clove
- pepper
- bay leaf
- orange leaf
- oregano
- thyme
- vinegar (half local, half Castilian)
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash the catfish very well with lemon, and once clean, dry it with a napkin
2. Coat it with flour, fry it in plenty of oil and lard
3. Layer the catfish in a pot, and on top place a layer of the lard in which it was fried
4. On top of this sprinkle ground clove and pepper and some bay leaves, orange leaf, oregano and thyme
5. Fill the pot with vinegar that is half local and half Castilian, with oil
6. Cover the pot and let it sit for three days, after which remove the catfish and place it on a platter to serve
