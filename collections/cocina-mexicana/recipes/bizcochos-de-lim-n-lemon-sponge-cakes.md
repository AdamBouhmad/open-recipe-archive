## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- the zest of one lemon
- four ounces of flour
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a batter with six fresh eggs, the zest of one lemon, four ounces of flour, and twelve ounces of powdered sugar
2. Place the batter on papers to bake in the oven
