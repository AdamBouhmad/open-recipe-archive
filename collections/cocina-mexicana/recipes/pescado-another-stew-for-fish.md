## Ingredients

- lard
- thin bread slices
- pepper
- clove
- fish broth
- oil
- salt
- chiles in vinegar
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry thin slices of bread in lard, and when golden, grind with pepper and clove.
2. Dissolve in broth in which the fish was cooked, add oil and sufficient salt.
3. Mix well so the broth is thick, and pour over the cooked fish, placing the plate in the steam of a pot.
4. When serving, add oil and oregano powder, garnishing with chiles in vinegar and olives.
