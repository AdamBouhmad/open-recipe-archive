## Ingredients

- puff pastry strips
- confiture pieces of choice

## Directions

1. Extend the puff pastry strips with a rolling pin until very thin
2. Moisten the dough and place pieces of preserves at certain distances
3. Fold the leaves to form a kind of slipper or cap shape
4. Press the edges well together
5. Arrange the tostones on a baking sheet until shortly before serving
6. Fry them so they acquire good color and are well golden
7. Drain them, place on a platter, dust with sugar and let them glaze under a bell oven
8. Garnish and serve
