## Ingredients

- jitomate
- cebolla
- aceite
- manteca
- aceitunas
- cominos
- bobo
- pan rallado
- pan molido

## Directions

1. Chop tomato and onion and place in a casserole with oil and lard to fry.
2. When almost fried, add split olives and cumin to finish frying.
3. Add the bobo, dusted with breadcrumbs and ground bread.
4. Fry the bobo well, and when serving add oil, olives and capers.
