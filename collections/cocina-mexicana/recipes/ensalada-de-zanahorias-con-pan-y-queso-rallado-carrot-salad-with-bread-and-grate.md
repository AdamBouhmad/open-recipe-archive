## Ingredients

- carrot
- bread
- grated cheese
- oil
- potato, chopped
- cooked onion
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- oil
- vinegar
- avocado

## Directions

1. After chopping the carrots as in the previous article, roll them in bread and grated cheese mixed together; fry in oil, and when they have browned a little, remove them and stir with chopped potatoes and cooked onions broken apart.
2. Sprinkle competent salt, pepper and oregano; stir everything with oil and vinegar, and garnish the salad with avocado in quarters.
