## Ingredients

- ground meat
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- parsley
- garlic
- prunes

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of ground meat and ham with salt, pepper, a couple of eggs, parsley and garlic, and add prunes
2. Fry this mixture in butter and use it to stuff the turkey
3. Roast the turkey
