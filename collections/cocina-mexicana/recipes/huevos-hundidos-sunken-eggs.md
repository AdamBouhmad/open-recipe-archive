## Ingredients

- grated cheese
- melted butter
- black pepper
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. In a bowl, combine grated cheese with a little melted butter and black pepper.
2. Add egg yolks and mix well.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold into the preparation.
4. Mix gently with a wooden spoon.
5. Add the remaining beaten whites, incorporating gently with constant stirring.
6. Prepare the sunken eggs in boxes prepared beforehand, filling them only halfway.
7. Put to cook in the oven like sponge cakes.
8. Serve as soon as they have finished cooking.
