## Ingredients

- Bread, sliced very thin
- Ham slices
- Chicken liver
- Chicken gizzards
- A handful of hazelnuts
- Chicken broth
- Hard boiled eggs, sliced
- Fried brains
- Chorizo
- Roasted coriander powder
- Parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Slice bread very thin and toast it on a griddle
2. Make slices of cooked ham, chicken liver, and chicken gizzards
3. Toast a handful of hazelnuts, clean them, grind them, and put them in a pot with chicken broth to boil
4. In another pot, layer bread, then liver, then bread, then ham, then bread, then gizzards, filling the pot this way
5. Pour sufficient hazelnut broth over each bread layer, cover the pot, and let it boil
6. For serving at table, garnish on top with sliced hard boiled eggs, fried brains, chorizo, roasted coriander powder, and parsley sprigs
