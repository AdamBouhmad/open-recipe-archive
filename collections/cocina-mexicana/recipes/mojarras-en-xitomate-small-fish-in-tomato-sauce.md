## Ingredients

- cleaned mojarras (small fish)
- equal parts oil and lard
- very ripe tomatoes
- small onions
- garlic
- parsley
- fish broth or water
- salt
- ground pepper
- oregano

## Directions

1. Place a cooking vessel on the fire with equal amounts of oil and lard
2. Roast very ripe tomatoes and slice them well
3. Slice small onions and finely chop competent amounts of garlic and parsley
4. Fry the onion alone first
5. Add the roasted and well-ground tomato, parsley, and garlic
6. When everything is fried, add fish broth or water
7. Add competent salt and ground pepper, and place on a gentle fire
8. Add the mojarras
9. When the broth has reduced somewhat, add plenty of oil and oregano
10. Place a comal (griddle) with little fire on top
11. When considered cooked, remove and let season in the heat until serving
