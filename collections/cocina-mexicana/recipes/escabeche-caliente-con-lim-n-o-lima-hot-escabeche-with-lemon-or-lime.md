## Ingredients

- fish, fried
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin, ground
- sour lime or lemon slices, without skin
- capers
- small chili peppers
- olives

## Directions

1. After frying the fish, prepare a broth with water, salt, and vinegar
2. Add ground clove, cinnamon, pepper, and cumin
3. Place the fish in the broth with lime or lemon slices without skin
4. Let it boil
5. Add capers, small chili peppers, and olives
