## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls of rose petals separated from the flowers
- Clarified syrup
- Sugar powder, sieved
- Carmine diluted in clarified honey

## Directions

1. Candy two handfuls of separated rose petals in clarified syrup by boiling with the rose until it reaches a strong blown point
2. Remove from fire and cover the petals with powdered sugar
3. Sieve to separate them from the sugar and rub with your hand to spread the flower
4. Place on another sieve to dry in the oven
5. Add a little carmine diluted in clarified honey to the syrup before covering it with the rose, so the candy acquires a slight rose tint
6. Prepare the mold and bring the syrup to the blown point as described for jonquil and violet
7. Pour into the mold and cover with the dried rose petals as described for other candied flowers
