## Ingredients

- six sea spiders
- oil
- parsley
- salt
- lemon juice
- pepper
- caper sauce or butter sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare six sea spiders by removing the spines from the gills and back
2. Disembowel and wash them well
3. Make a cut on each side
4. Place them in a little oil with parsley and salt
5. A quarter hour before serving, roast them on the grill, turning them with their drippings
6. If desired, add lemon juice and pepper to the drippings
7. Cook on both sides and serve with caper sauce or butter sauce
