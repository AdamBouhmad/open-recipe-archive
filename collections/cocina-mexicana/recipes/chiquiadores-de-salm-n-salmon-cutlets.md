## Ingredients

- 1 pound raw salmon
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Chopped parsley
- 1/2 glass wine
- 1 tablespoon German sauce
- Fresh butter
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Fried toast for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the salmon, remove the skin, and cut into rounds the size of a peso or tostón coin
2. Place these cutlets in a pan with melted butter, season with salt and black pepper, add chopped parsley and half a glass of wine
3. At the moment of serving, cook over high heat
4. Drain the butter from them, add one tablespoon of German sauce (see among the sauces) with a piece of fresh butter and the juice of a lemon
5. Arrange on a plate garnished with fried toast and serve
