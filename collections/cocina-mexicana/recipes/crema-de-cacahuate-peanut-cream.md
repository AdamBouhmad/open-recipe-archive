## Ingredients

- six ounces of toasted and cleaned peanuts
- two tablespoons of orange-flower water
- four egg yolks
- three ounces of lemon and orange peel
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- four quartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Crush or grind six ounces of toasted and cleaned peanuts on a metate
2. Gradually add to them two tablespoons of orange-flower water, four egg yolks, three ounces of lemon and orange peel, and four ounces of powdered sugar
3. Mix everything exactly and dissolve in four quartillos of milk
4. Cook over low heat, stirring with a spoon or spatula
5. When the cream is cooked, pour into pots or cups, or into a deep plate
