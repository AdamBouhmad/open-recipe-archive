## Ingredients

- Bread, very thin sliced
- Butter
- Milk
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Sesame
- Dragée
- Pine nuts
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Slice bread very thin and fry in butter
2. Cook milk with sugar, and once it has the proper consistency, arrange over layers of the fried slices with raisins, almonds, sesame, dragée and pine nuts
3. Form on top its puff pastry with beaten egg white and powdered sugar, and put on the fire with a griddle
