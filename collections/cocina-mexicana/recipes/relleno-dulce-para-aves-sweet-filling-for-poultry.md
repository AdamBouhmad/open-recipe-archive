## Ingredients

- Biscuit
- cheese
- cinnamon
- pepper
- coriander, toasted
- saffron
- sugar
- eggs
- butter
- raisins
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Grind the biscuit with a little cheese and add plenty of cinnamon and pepper, a little toasted coriander, saffron and sugar, all ground
2. Beat everything together with eggs
3. Put a pan with butter on the heat, and as soon as it is well hot, add the paste there so that it cooks
4. Once it forms grains, lower the heat and pour it into a bowl so that it cools
5. Add raisins and pine nuts to it
6. Fill the poultry with this mixture
7. In a pot with boiling water, place the filled birds so that they parboil
8. Once well parboiled, remove them and roast them in an oven or between two fires
