## Ingredients

- A quarter ounce of cinnamon
- A quarter ounce of mace
- Peel of three citrons
- Twelve quartillos of brandy
- Three pounds of sugar
- Four quartillos of water
- Half a pound of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Leave the cinnamon, mace, and citron peel in infusion in brandy at room temperature for five or six days
2. Distill until half the liquor is obtained
3. Dissolve three pounds of sugar in four quartillos of water by heating
4. After cooling, add half a pound of orange blossom water
5. Filter through a bag and store in well-sealed bottles
