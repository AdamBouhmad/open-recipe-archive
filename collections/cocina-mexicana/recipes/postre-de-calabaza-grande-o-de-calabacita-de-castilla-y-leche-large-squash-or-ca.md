## Ingredients

- One squash
- Crushed sugar
- Milk
- Mamón
- White wine
- Cinnamon
- White almond confection

## Directions

1. Make a hole in the middle of a squash and remove the insides and seeds
2. Fill with crushed sugar and cover well, put to cook in the oven
3. The next day, remove the skin and grind it
4. Put in a little milk and put on the fire to reach the point of paste
5. Arrange over layers of mamón sprinkled with white wine, alternating with layers of white almond confection and ground cinnamon
