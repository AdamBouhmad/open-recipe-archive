## Ingredients

- three-quarters of an ounce of cloves
- two ounces of sugar
- thirty ounces of milk
- ten ounces of custard
- thirteen ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Coarsely pound three-quarters of an ounce of cloves and two ounces of sugar in a marble mortar
2. Bring to boil with thirty ounces of milk, ten ounces of custard and thirteen ounces of sugar
3. Once it acquires the proper consistency, strain through muslin
4. Cool and freeze
