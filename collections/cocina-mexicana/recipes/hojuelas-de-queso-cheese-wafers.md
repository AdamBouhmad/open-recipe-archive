## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two eggs (whites and yolks)
- two tablespoons of lard
- two tablespoons of butter
- one cup of refined Spanish brandy
- the juice of one sour orange
- a little tequesquite powder
- half ounce of fresh, ground adobera cheese
- a little salt
- sifted sugar to taste
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with two eggs, two tablespoons of lard, two tablespoons of butter, one cup of refined Spanish brandy, the juice of one sour orange, a little tequesquite powder, half ounce of fresh ground adobera cheese, a little salt, sifted sugar to taste, and the necessary water to bind the flour
2. Knead well so that it is smooth, adding lard if necessary
3. Extend the dough on the table dusted with flour, and cut and fry the wafers like those in the previous article
