## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of flour
- powdered sugar
- one tablespoon of cooking oil
- two tablespoons of orange blossom water
- egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Dissolve sufficient flour in water with a little powdered sugar, one tablespoon of cooking oil and two tablespoons of orange blossom water
2. Beat well
3. At the time of use, mix with a few egg whites that are very well beaten
4. Ensure it reaches a regular thickness so that fruit becomes well covered with one immersion
5. Fry the fruit in very hot lard, basting it with the fat and removing it as soon as it has browned
