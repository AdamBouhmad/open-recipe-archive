## Ingredients

- a pound of clean ground almonds
- a pound of finely ground sugar
- six eggs beaten as for frying
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix the ground almonds with the ground sugar and beaten eggs
2. Butter a pan and add the mixture
3. Bake in the oven or cook over two fires until done
