## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- basil
- onion slices
- whole scallions
- parsley sprigs
- five or six cloves
- juice of two lemons or vinegar
- butter
- flour
- water
- fine butter or oil for frying
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon slices into strips and place in a marinade made with salt, pepper, bay leaf, basil, onion slices, whole scallions, parsley sprigs, five or six cloves, and the juice of two lemons or a bit of vinegar.
2. Mix everything and let the fish marinate for two hours.
3. The marinade can also be heated, in which case add a little butter kneaded with flour, vinegar, and water.
4. Once the salmon strips are well marinated, dry them between two cloths, roll them in flour, and fry them in fine butter or oil.
5. Serve garnished with fried parsley.
