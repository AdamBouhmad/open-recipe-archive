## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- broth
- vinegar
- capers
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- chervil
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut a cooked beef tongue into very thin slices.
2. In the same plate in which it is to be served, place at the bottom a little broth and a splash of vinegar, capers, parsley, onion, garlic and chervil, all finely chopped; salt, pepper and breadcrumbs.
3. Place the tongue slices on top, which are covered with the same things that formed the base, with breadcrumbs on top of everything.
4. Place the plate in the oven on a gentle fire: let it simmer until a crust forms at the bottom of the plate, and add a little broth when serving.
