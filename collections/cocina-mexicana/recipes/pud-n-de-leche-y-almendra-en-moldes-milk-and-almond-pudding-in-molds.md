## Ingredients

- five cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of almonds
- sugar to taste
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- citron
- cinnamon
- twenty egg yolks
- ten egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix five cuartillos of milk with four ounces of almonds, cold mamón, twelve ounces of butter, sugar to taste, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, citron and cinnamon
2. Cook everything and once cold, add twenty egg yolks and ten egg whites
