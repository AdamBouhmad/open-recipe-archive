## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Ham
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- White wine
- Spanish sauce
- Mixed herbs

## Directions

1. Empty the young partridges and prepare as stated in previous articles
2. Place in a saucepan with ham, butter, lemon juice and pepper
3. Once parboiled in butter, add half a bottle of white wine and the same amount of Spanish sauce, adding a mixed herb bundle
4. Let cook for about an hour
5. Remove the young partridges and untie them
6. Let the broth thicken, degrease, strain and place the young partridges on top
