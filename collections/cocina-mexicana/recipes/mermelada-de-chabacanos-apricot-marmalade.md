## Ingredients

- two pounds of ripe apricots
- one pound of clarified sugar
- half of the pits

## Directions

1. Choose two pounds of ripe apricots, remove the pits and cut into pieces
2. Cook over fire for three quarters of an hour
3. Add one pound of clarified sugar and then half of the pits
4. When it reaches a state of consistency let it cool and put in jars
