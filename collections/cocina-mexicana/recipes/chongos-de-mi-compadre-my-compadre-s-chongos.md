## Ingredients

- pan de la vida
- butter
- syrup with wine and ground cinnamon
- cheese
- almonds
- raisins
- walnuts
- peanuts
- pine nuts
- beaten eggs
- syrup

## Directions

1. Slice the pan de la vida thinly and fry in butter until golden
2. Dip in syrup with wine and ground cinnamon
3. Butter a cooking vessel and begin layering: one layer of golden bread and one layer of cheese, almonds, raisins and walnuts (all chopped), with corresponding peanuts and pine nuts
4. Add a little syrup and continue building layers until the last layer of beaten eggs
5. Place over two fires and prevent burning by adding syrup without disturbing the egg layer
6. Lift the edges with a knife to help it concentrate
