## Ingredients

- Roasted chicken
- Egg yolks
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Clove, powdered
- Flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Roast the chicken as in the two preceding articles
2. Cover with a batter made by beating egg yolks until hardened and mixing with a little saffron, pepper, and powdered clove, and with enough flour to make a thick paste that can be spread with feathers on the chicken
3. Place to cook on the spit over gentle heat
4. When the paste dries, which will be brief, take care to turn them and bathe them with lard, then coat again with the egg paste
5. Repeat these operations until the chicken is well cooked, covered, and golden
