## Ingredients

- good portion of tomatoes
- green chiles
- one or two sprigs of fresh cilantro
- turkey, raw and in regular pieces
- water or broth in which pork has been cooked
- salt
- a little bit of clove
- small green chiles
- small zucchini, cooked
- sprigs of cilantro

## Directions

1. Grind a good portion of tomatoes with some green chiles, and devein these if you want the mole not to be spicy
2. Grind also one or two sprigs of fresh cilantro
3. Fry the raw turkey in regular pieces
4. Separately fry the tomato with chile and cilantro
5. Add the turkey and water or broth in which pork has been cooked
6. Bring to boil with necessary salt until it cooks
7. Grind a little bit of clove and add it
8. Add small green chiles and small cooked zucchini, and sprigs of cilantro
