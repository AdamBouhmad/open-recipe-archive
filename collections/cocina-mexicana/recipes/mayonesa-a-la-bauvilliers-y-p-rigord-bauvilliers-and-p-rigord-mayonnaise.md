## Ingredients

- two raw egg yolks
- one cooked egg yolk
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- good olive oil

## Directions

1. Place the two raw yolks and one cooked yolk in a bowl with one tablespoon of vinegar, salt and pepper
2. Pour good olive oil over these ingredients, stirring and turning constantly
3. Add more oil while turning for about twenty minutes until the desired thickness is achieved
