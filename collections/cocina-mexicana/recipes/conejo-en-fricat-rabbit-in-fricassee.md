## Ingredients

- rabbit
- fresh butter
- bacon, in squares

## Directions

1. Skinned, emptied and cleaned, cut into pieces
2. Place a good piece of fresh butter in a pan, through which pass square pieces of bacon; remove immediately
3. Place two spoonfuls of flour in the butter and knead
