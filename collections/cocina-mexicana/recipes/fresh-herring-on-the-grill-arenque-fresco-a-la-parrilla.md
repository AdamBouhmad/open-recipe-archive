## Ingredients

- Fresh herring
- Oil or butter for grilling
- Fish sauces or broths

## Directions

1. Remove the gills and intestines from the herring
2. Wash them well and marinate them as you would mackerel
3. Grill them on the grill grate
4. Serve them with any of the broths or sauces for fish
