## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Butter
- Four additional ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with butter until it comes together
2. Then add four ounces of melted butter
3. Beat everything well and pound it
4. As soon as it has risen, wrap it in a napkin and keep it covered
5. After two hours, remove the dough
6. Stretch the hojuelas with a hand coated with butter
7. Cut them with a pastry wheel
8. Brown them in butter
9. Serve with syrup
