## Ingredients

- four ounces rice
- one cuartillo milk
- five or six macaroni pieces
- marzipans, broken
- citron or lemon peel, chopped very fine
- four ounces sugar
- a little salt
- glazed orange blossoms
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- four egg yolks
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and parboil four ounces of rice
2. Put to cook in one cuartillo of milk, adding more milk as it is consumed, until the rice bursts
3. Season with five or six broken macaroni pieces, broken marzipans, very finely chopped citron or lemon peel, four ounces sugar, a little salt, and glazed orange blossoms
4. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg
5. Bind everything with four egg yolks without letting it boil
6. Spread the mixture on a plate and let it cool
7. When cool, cut into small equal pieces and form into balls
8. Cover immediately with beaten eggs and roll in bread crumbs
9. Shortly before serving, fry in moderately hot oil
10. When the croquettes have acquired a beautiful color, drain them, dust with sugar, arrange on a plate, and serve
