## Ingredients

- Catfish, cleaned and divided into portions
- Salt
- Boiling water
- Flour
- Lard
- Wine, twice the amount of vinegar
- Vinegar, strong
- Ginger
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Orange leaves
- Lima slices

## Directions

1. Parboil the catfish portions in boiling salted water
2. Remove the skin
3. Coat thoroughly in flour and fry in lard
4. Leave in this state until the next day
5. Arrange in a barrel or pot and add twice as much wine as vinegar
6. Add enough liquid until the fish is covered
7. Add ground ginger, black pepper, cloves, cinnamon, orange leaves, and lima slices
8. After eight days it can be eaten, but it becomes superior after fifteen days in cold or mild climates
