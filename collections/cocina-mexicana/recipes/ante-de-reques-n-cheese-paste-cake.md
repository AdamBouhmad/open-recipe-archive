## Ingredients

- fresh cheese (requesón)
- syrup
- egg yolks
- aromatics
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup

## Directions

1. Grind the cheese
2. Add to syrup with egg yolks and aromatics
3. Cook
4. Place over sponge cake soaked in syrup
5. Top with all garnishes
