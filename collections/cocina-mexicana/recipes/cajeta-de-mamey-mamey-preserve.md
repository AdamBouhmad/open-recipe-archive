## Ingredients

- camote, pineapple, and mamey, peeled and each ground separately in equal proportion
- 2 pounds of clarified sugar per pound of fruit paste
- 2 ounces of ground almond per pound of fruit paste

## Directions

1. Peel camote, pineapple, and mamey and grind each fruit separately, keeping them in equal proportion
2. Per pound of this paste, add 2 pounds of clarified sugar and 2 ounces of ground almonds
3. As they begin to reach the proper point, stir until it detaches from the pan
