## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- water
- vinegar
- garlic heads, crushed
- pepper, ground
- salt, ground
- lard
- breadcrumb crumbs, ground

## Directions

1. Put the lamb meat to cook in water with vinegar, some crushed garlic heads, ground pepper and salt, and a little lard
2. When well cooked, remove it and add ground breadcrumb crumbs, a little of the broth in which it cooked, and additional salt if needed
3. Place over two fires until the broth has reduced and the meat is browned
