## Ingredients

- thrushes
- salt
- water
- onion
- garlic cloves
- chiles
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the thrushes
2. Place them in a pot with sufficient salt and water
3. Add one or two onions cut into quarters, two or three garlic cloves, and two whole chiles
4. Cook until done
5. Sprinkle with a little oil and vinegar
6. Allow to reduce and brown in the remaining fat
