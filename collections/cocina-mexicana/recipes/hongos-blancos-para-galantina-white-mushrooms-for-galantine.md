## Ingredients

- lemon
- water
- mushrooms
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Squeeze a lemon in water
2. Add the mushrooms to it as they are being turned
3. Put them afterwards in a saucepan with lemon juice and butter over the fire
4. Let them boil for five minutes
5. Keep them afterwards in an earthenware or ceramic vessel
6. Use them when needed
