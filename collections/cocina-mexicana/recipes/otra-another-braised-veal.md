## Ingredients

- veal
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- garlic
- ham in strips
- butter
- turnips
- fried bread
- onion
- wine
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with clove, cinnamon, pepper, garlic and ham strips
2. Fry the larded meat in butter
3. Peel turnips and fry them until golden
4. Grind everything with fried bread
5. In a pot, fry onion and garlic divided in cloves
6. Add wine, vinegar and salt
7. Add water and let cook over two gentle fires
