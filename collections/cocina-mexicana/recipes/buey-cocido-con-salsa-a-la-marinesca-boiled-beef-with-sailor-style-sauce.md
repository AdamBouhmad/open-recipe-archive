## Ingredients

- butter
- small onions
- flour
- red wine
- broth
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- thyme

## Directions

1. Brown some small whole onions in a pan with good butter
2. Add a spoonful of flour, which is tossed with the onions
3. Add a glass of red wine and half a glass of broth with some mushrooms, salt, pepper, a bay leaf and a little thyme
