## Ingredients

- Breadcrumbs
- Oil or fresh butter
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sorrel

## Directions

1. Lard the outer part with thin strips; on the inner part rub with oil or fresh butter, covering it completely with plenty of grated bread on the oiled part; wrap in oiled paper and place on the spit. Before it finishes cooking, remove from the fire, apply new breadcrumbs mixed with chopped parsley and salt to the previously breaded part, and let it take color over a very high fire; sprinkle with a little vinegar and place over a bed of sorrel, commonly called ox tongue. Oil can be used instead of butter, or lard.
