## Ingredients

- Oil in proportion to the fish
- Garlic
- Onion, chopped
- Green chiles, whole
- Tomato, roasted, peeled and mashed
- Salt
- Wide chile, toasted in lard
- Bread slices, toasted in lard
- Bread crumbs, soaked in vinegar and ground
- Cooked and deboned cod
- Fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Fry garlic in oil until burnt, then remove
2. Add chopped onion and whole green chiles to the oil
3. When the onion turns golden, add roasted, peeled and mashed tomato in the proportion of eight large ripe tomatoes for each fish, salt, pieces of toasted wide chile in lard, and bread slices similarly treated, with a little bread crumbs soaked in vinegar and ground
4. When everything is well fried, add the cooked and deboned cod and let it simmer a little
5. Serve on a platter over fried bread slices not browned, with the sauce poured over
