## Ingredients

- Small zucchini
- Butter
- Onion
- Garlic
- Large green chile
- Epazote
- Longaniza sausage, small pieces

## Directions

1. Choose small zucchini, chop finely, wash well
2. Place in a pot with a little butter
3. Grind an onion and a large garlic clove and put to cook with this
4. Grind a large green chile and a little epazote
5. Fry this with small pieces of longaniza in considerable butter
6. When all this is well fried, turn in the zucchini and let it season over low heat
