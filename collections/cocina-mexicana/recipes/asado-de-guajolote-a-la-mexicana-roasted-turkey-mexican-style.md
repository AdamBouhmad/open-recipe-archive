## Ingredients

- guajolote
- chile ancho
- cebollas
- orégano
- sal
- vinagre
- manteca
- mollejas
- hígado
- corazón

## Directions

1. Soak untoasted ancho chile in water; mix fifteen chiles with two large onions, little oregano, salt and vinegar
2. Coat the turkey with half of this sauce after cooking, and place in the oven after putting whole onions in the belly, bracing the sides of the bird with a stick so they do not come together
3. When turning the turkey, add the other half of the sauce
4. When grinding the chile, also grind the gizzards, liver and heart, all already cooked
5. If the turkey is lean, before coating with sauce give it a coating of lard
