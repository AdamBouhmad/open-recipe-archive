## Ingredients

- fine powdered sugar
- egg white
- paper
- twelve ounces of sugar
- four ounces of clean jasmine flowers

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little fine powdered sugar in egg white so that it is not very liquid, leaving it in the same plate where the sugar was dissolved
2. Make a paper mold of the desired size for the gató
3. Make high feather-point syrup with twelve ounces of sugar for each four ounces of clean jasmine flowers
4. Add the flowers to the syrup, stirring continuously over the heat
5. When it begins to rise, add the sugared egg white
6. Empty into the mold and keep the bottom of a hot pan or skillet above it at a certain distance
