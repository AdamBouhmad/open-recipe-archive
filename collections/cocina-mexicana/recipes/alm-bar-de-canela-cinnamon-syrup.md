## Ingredients

- six pounds of water
- eight ounces of fine cinnamon
- eight ounces of the distilled water

## Directions

1. Infuse eight ounces of fine cinnamon in six pounds of water
2. Keep it thus for twenty-four hours
3. Proceed with distillation by alembic, from which approximately half the water will come out
4. Put eight ounces of this water in a large glass, which is covered with parchment punctured with a pin
5. This glass is introduced in hot water of seventy to seventy-five degrees
6. When everything is mixed, remove from heat and let cool
7. Strain through a sieve and bottle
