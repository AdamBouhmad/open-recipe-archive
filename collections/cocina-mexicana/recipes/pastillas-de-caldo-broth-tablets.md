## Ingredients

- four pounds of veal hands
- twelve pounds of beef leg
- ten pounds of lamb loin
- three pounds of veal kidney
- sufficient water
- five or six egg whites
- salt
- a few spices

## Directions

1. Take four pounds of veal hands, twelve pounds of beef leg, ten pounds of lamb loin, and three pounds of veal kidney
2. Cook everything with sufficient water
3. After well skimming the pot, press the meat to extract the juice and return to cook on gentle heat with fresh water
4. Once it has boiled, combine both broths and when cool remove the fat
5. Reheat the liquid and clarify it with five or six egg whites
6. Add corresponding salt and a few spices
7. Strain the liquid and evaporate it in a bain-marie until it reaches the consistency of jelly
8. Pour into molds or cut into small pieces and store in wide-mouthed glass bottles
