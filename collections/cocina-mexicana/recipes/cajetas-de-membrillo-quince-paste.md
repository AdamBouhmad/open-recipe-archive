## Ingredients

- membrillo (quince)
- azúcar (sugar)
- agua tibia (warm water)

## Directions

1. Wash the quince on the following day, cook it and place it in a pot with warm water, leaving it until the next day
2. Drain it in a basket
3. Stir it well afterwards, pass it through a very fine cloth, and place it in clarified syrup at the ball stage, made with twenty-nine pounds of sugar for each arroba of quince
4. The point is known when it makes a boil with smoke
5. When it reaches this point, remove from heat, beat in the pan and fill the small boxes
