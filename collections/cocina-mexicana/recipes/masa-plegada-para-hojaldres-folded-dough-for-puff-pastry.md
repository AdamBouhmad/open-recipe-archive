## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- pound and a half of butter or lard
- half an ounce of salt
- four eggs
- two glasses of water

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of flour on a table and form a hollow in the center
2. Add butter or lard, salt, eggs, and water to the hollow
3. Mix everything little by little with the flour and gather without kneading
4. Let rest and then form the puff pastries
