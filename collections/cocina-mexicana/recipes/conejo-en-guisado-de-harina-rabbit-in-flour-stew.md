## Ingredients

- Flour
- Butter
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- Water
- Salt
- Rabbit
- Broth

## Directions

1. Brown a little flour in butter; fry tomato, garlic and chopped onion there, add sufficient water and salt and the rabbit in pieces
2. When cooked, add a broth
