## Ingredients

- fatty pork sirloin
- mustard sauce
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut a fatty pork sirloin into thin and very equal slices
2. Place them in mustard sauce prepared with anticipation
3. Serve with croutons of bread fried in butter around them
