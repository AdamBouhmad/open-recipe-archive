## Ingredients

- French bread, sliced
- Lard and oil
- Two slices of fried bread
- Two garlic cloves
- Roasted cumin seeds
- Salted fish, a small piece
- Fresh fish broth, cooked with onion and salt
- Fried eggs
- Black pepper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Slice French bread and toast it in lard and oil
2. Grind two slices of fried bread with two garlic cloves, a few roasted cumin seeds, and a small piece of salted fish
3. Thin this mixture with a little fresh fish broth cooked with onion and salt
4. Place the bread slices in a serving dish and bathe them with this broth
5. Give a few boils over strong heat, then dry the soup over two low fires
6. Dress with fried eggs, a little black pepper, and oil
