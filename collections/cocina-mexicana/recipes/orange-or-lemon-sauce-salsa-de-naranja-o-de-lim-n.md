## Ingredients

- peel of one orange or one lemon, well descarnated
- game essence

## Directions

1. Cut the well-descarnated peel of an orange or lemon into small, thin strips
2. Blanch and refresh them in cold water
3. Use them by mixing with game essence as with bigarrade sauce
