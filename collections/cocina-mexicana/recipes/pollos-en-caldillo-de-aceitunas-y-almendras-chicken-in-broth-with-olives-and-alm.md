## Ingredients

- Cooked chicken
- Oil
- Salt
- Pitted and ground olives
- Toasted almonds
- Chicken broth
- Strips of ham
- Sliced chorizo
- Vinegar
- Cured tornachiles

## Directions

1. Cook and quarter the chicken
2. Fry it in oil with salt, then remove it
3. Fry pitted olives ground with toasted almonds in the same oil
4. Put the chicken back in the mixture
5. Add the broth in which the chicken was cooked
6. Add strips of ham and sliced cooked chorizo
7. Add a little vinegar
8. Let everything simmer until the broth is somewhat thick
9. Garnish with pieces of cured tornachiles when serving
