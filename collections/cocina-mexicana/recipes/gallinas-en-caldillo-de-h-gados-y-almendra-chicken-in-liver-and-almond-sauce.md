## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic
- onion
- mutton liver
- almonds
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- oil
- vinegar
- cooked chicken
- chicken broth
- capers
- pickled caperberries
- olives
- small chilies

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic in lard and when browned, also fry chopped onion
2. Add cooked and ground mutton liver mixed with an equal volume of almonds, cloves, pepper, and cumin
3. Add oil and vinegar, the quarters of cooked chicken and its cooking broth
4. Boil as long as necessary so the sauce is not too thick
5. Remove from heat and add more oil, capers, pickled caperberries, olives, and small chilies
