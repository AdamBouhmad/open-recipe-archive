## Ingredients

- half pound fine flour
- water
- salt
- butter
- eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take half pound of fine flour, dissolve in cold water as for making communion wafers and a little salt
2. Heat a little butter in a pan and add it there stirring
3. Beaten as for an egg omelet, with a ladle in the pan over hot coals, it will come to make a waxy little dough, which cooks well, so it does not stick and form a crust, because everything depends on this dough coming out good and well cooked
4. Put it in a mortar with the necessary eggs; half with whites and half without them
5. Once they are soft, fry them and pass them through syrup; on the plates add cinnamon
