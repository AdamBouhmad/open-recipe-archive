## Ingredients

- ciento de nueces (one hundred walnuts)
- cuarterón de queso fresco de vaca (quarter pound of fresh cow cheese)
- jamón gordo (fat ham)
- dientes de ajo (garlic cloves)
- sal (salt)
- dientes de granada (pomegranate seeds)
- perejil fresco (fresh parsley)

## Directions

1. Clean the walnuts thoroughly and grind them in the metate, which must be very clean so the walnuts do not break
2. Grind a piece of fat ham and garlic cloves in the same metate
3. Add the corresponding salt and mix everything together
4. Once ready, place at the edge of a pot so it does not become grainy
5. Cover the chiles with this sauce, pomegranate seeds, and fresh chopped parsley (foreign parsley is better)
