## Ingredients

- squabs, cleaned and plucked
- butter
- onion
- carrot
- ham
- bay leaves
- thyme
- black pepper
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- water
- peas
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Split the squabs in half lengthwise and fry in sufficient butter with onion in sections, carrot, pieces of ham, bay leaves, thyme, powdered pepper, chopped parsley, salt and a little water, adding more as it is consumed so the squabs cook well.
2. Cook the peas, add a little salt to keep them green, and once cooked, fry in butter and buttermilk separately.
3. Fry a little flour until it is white, not golden; dissolve it in the broth in which the squabs were cooked, which should be strained first, making a thick sauce.
4. Add the peas which will have cooked somewhat with the buttermilk, then the well-cleaned squabs, adding a little chopped parsley; place on the fire and season, taking care that the peas remain only parboiled.
