## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- flour, one portion
- almonds, ground
- bread or sponge cake, sliced and toasted

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with sugar, cinnamon and one portion of flour
2. Strain and return to the fire to boil a little more with ground almonds
3. Pour over toasted slices of bread or sponge cake
4. Cook the soup, letting it boil so that it remains somewhat loose
5. Serve dusted with cinnamon
