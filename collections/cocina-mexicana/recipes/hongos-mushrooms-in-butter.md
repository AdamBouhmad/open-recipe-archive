## Ingredients

- garlic
- butter
- water
- onion
- parsley
- mushrooms
- oil
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of garlic very fine and cook in butter and a little water; once the water is consumed and only butter remains, add chopped onion about one third the amount of mushrooms and let it cook in that butter (not fry).
2. Add a little finely chopped parsley and the mushrooms, which should already be cooked and well drained so they have no water, giving them just a brief simmer and adding a little oil and a pinch of pepper.
3. Separately fry some bread crumbs in oil and incorporate them.
