## Ingredients

- veal leg
- bacon strips
- vegetables
- parsley bundle
- onions
- stock or broth
- sorrel

## Directions

1. Wash the pulp of a veal leg, ensuring it is covered with fat that envelops it
2. Pound it between two cloths
3. Lard with bacon strips and place in a casserole with vegetables and a bundle of parsley and onions
4. Moisten with stock or broth and let cook four hours
5. Skim the sauce and strain it, returning it to the meat, and let it reduce over low heat
6. Serve, adorning the platter with sorrel
