## Ingredients

- Veal loin, cut into steaks
- Ham
- Water, boiling
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Lard
- Almonds, ground
- Bread, ground

## Directions

1. Make the steaks and lard them with ham
2. Place them in a pot of boiling water with garlic, black pepper, and lard
3. When cooked and tender, arrange on a plate and cover with almonds and bread, both ground and passed through lard
