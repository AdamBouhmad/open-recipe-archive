## Ingredients

- green chiles
- raw tomatoes
- garlic
- lard
- nopales
- onion
- cilantro
- pepper
- cumin
- bread fried in lard
- oregano
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince green chiles, abundant raw tomatoes, and garlic, and fry in lard
2. Separately, cook the nopales
3. Once cooked, add them to the fried mixture and let them absorb the substance well
4. Add very finely minced onion, cilantro, pepper, and cumin, all ground together with powder of bread fried in lard and a pinch of oregano
5. Pour oil and vinegar over the top and let it boil so it seasons properly
