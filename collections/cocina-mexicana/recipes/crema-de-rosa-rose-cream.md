## Ingredients

- Cream
- Sugar
- Rose essence
- Liquid carmine
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Take two quarter liters of good cream and bring to a boil
2. When it rises, place at the edge of an oven and sweeten it
3. Add a quarter drop of rose essence and liquid carmine for color
4. While this mixture infuses, break six or eight eggs, using only the yolks, which beat with a wooden spoon until well bound
5. Pour into a tureen or onto a deep platter or in small cups and give them a bain-marie as with vanilla cream
