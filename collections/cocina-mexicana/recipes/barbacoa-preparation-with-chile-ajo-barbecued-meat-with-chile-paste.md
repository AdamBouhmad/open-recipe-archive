## Ingredients

- chile ancho (dried broad chile)
- cumin
- garlic cloves
- manteca (lard)

## Directions

1. Soak the broad chile.
2. Once soaked, grind it with cumin and garlic cloves.
3. This sauce is called chile-ajo.
4. Coat the meat well with lard and on top coat it well with the chile-ajo.
5. Wrap in the petate.
