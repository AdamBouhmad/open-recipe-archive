## Ingredients

- two coconuts
- three pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Grate two coconuts and then grind them
2. Add the ground coconut to a clarified syrup made with three pounds of sugar
3. Bring to a boil and leave until it reaches the point of cajeta, or until the bottom of the pan is visible when stirred
