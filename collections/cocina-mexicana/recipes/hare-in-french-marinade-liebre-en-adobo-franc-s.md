## Ingredients

- Hare
- medium strips of ham
- ground aromatics
- salt
- pepper
- parsley bouquet
- small onions
- laurel leaves
- thyme bundle
- carrots
- cloves
- broth

## Directions

1. Once the hare is emptied and cleaned, lard it with medium strips of ham seasoned with ground aromatics, salt and pepper.
2. When the legs and loins are larded, place some slices of ham in a saucepan with a small bouquet of parsley and onions, two laurel leaves, another bundle of thyme, two or three carrots and two or three cloves.
3. Moisten everything with broth, put it to cook, and while cooking, arrange it on a plate and serve hot.
