## Ingredients

- two and a half pounds white sugar
- one pound pounded almonds
- sugar for grinding
- orange blossom water
- twenty-four beaten egg yolks
- grajea
- almond strips

## Directions

1. Clarify two and a half pounds of white sugar without lemon and strain the syrup through cloth
2. Put on the fire until it reaches medium point
3. Lower to the ground and when it cools slightly, add one pound of pounded almonds after having ground a little sugar so the almonds do not stick to the grinding stone, taking care to sprinkle with orange blossom water
4. Then add twenty-four beaten egg yolks without stopping to stir the syrup, which will then be put on the fire until it reaches the point of coming away from the pan without letting it go from your hand
5. Dust a dish with ground grajea and pour the paste on top, which is covered with the same grajea, studding it with almond strips
