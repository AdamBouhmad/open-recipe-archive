## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- one cup of milk
- four eggs
- lard for greasing

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of flour with one cup of milk first, then add four eggs.
2. Once all is well mixed, grease a frying pan with lard and place it in a pot of boiling water.
3. Pour the mixture into the hot pan to the thickness of a cigar end and cover with a small dome with a little fire so it sets.
4. Then empty the pan to continue the process.
