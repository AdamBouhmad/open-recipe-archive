## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds veal loin
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- water
- tomato, chopped
- onion, sliced
- garlic cloves, crushed
- oregano
- thyme
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- potato sauce

## Directions

1. Take 1.5 pounds of veal loin, lard it and place it to soak in vinegar, salt and pepper for one hour
2. Put a pan with very hot lard on the fire
3. Add water little by little until it releases its broth
4. Add chopped tomato, sliced onion, crushed garlic cloves, oregano, thyme, bay leaf, and peppercorns
5. Fry everything until it becomes dry
6. Add a potato sauce
