## Ingredients

- well-cooked chickens
- onion
- tomatoes
- garlic
- bergamot pear
- cooked ham
- cinnamon
- pepper
- garlic cloves
- cumin
- butter
- flour
- four egg whites
- broth or water
- ground sugar
- lamb or beef tongue
- vinegar
- salt
- peppercorns
- brains
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens well, then cut into quarters.
2. Mince onion, tomatoes, garlic, and bergamot pear.
3. Fry some sliced cooked ham.
4. Grind a little cinnamon, pepper, garlic cloves, and a few cumin seeds.
5. Grease a casserole with butter and make a layer of the minced vegetables and ham.
6. Add a layer of chicken pieces, sprinkle with some spices dissolved in water and dust with flour.
7. Continue alternating layers of vegetables, chicken dusted with flour, and spices, concluding with a final layer of vegetables and ham.
8. Cook over two gentle fires, adding a little broth or water.
9. When the broth is consumed and the vegetables are cooked, remove from heat.
10. Beat four egg whites with a pinch of flour, and pour over the dish.
11. Place a griddle with some heat on top to brown it, then dust with ground sugar.
12. Serve the chickens in the same dish without disturbing it.
13. Prepare lamb or beef tongue slices cooked in equal parts water and vinegar with salt, plenty of garlic, onion, and peppercorns, cooking overnight on gentle heat.
14. Cook brains with salt, slice them, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry with a little salt and pepper.
15. Arrange the tongue and brain slices on top of the casserole and scatter blanched and finely minced onion over all.
