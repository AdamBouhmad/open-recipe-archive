## Ingredients

- peeled almonds
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cut peeled almonds into small strips
2. Make them glaze for an instant like orange blossoms
3. Place in a bowl with egg whites
4. Once this mixture has enough consistency to be handled, set the macarons on paper
5. Bake in the oven
