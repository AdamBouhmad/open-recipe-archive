## Ingredients

- Lamb sweetbreads from a roasted leg
- Duxelles sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the lamb sweetbreads from a roasted leg
2. Place them in duxelles sauce as described in the article on ribs à la Maitenom
3. Follow the same procedures as indicated there
