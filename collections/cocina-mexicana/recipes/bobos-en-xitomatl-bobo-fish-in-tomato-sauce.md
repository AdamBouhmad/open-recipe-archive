## Ingredients

- garlic, minced
- large onions, minced
- roasted and mashed tomatoes
- all ground spices except saffron
- fresh capers, minced
- one or two tablespoons of strong vinegar
- butter
- thyme
- almonds
- parsley, finely minced
- olives
- tornachiles in strips

## Directions

1. Fry minced garlic, minced onions in quantity equal to the number of bobo fish, and roasted and mashed tomatoes with all ground spices except saffron.
2. Cook the cleaned and prepared bobo fish in this sauce, adding fresh minced capers and one or two tablespoons of strong vinegar according to the quantity of fish.
3. Shortly before removing from heat, add butter, thyme and almonds.
4. When serving, sprinkle finely minced parsley, olives and tornachiles in strips on top.
5. Take care that the bobo fish do not fall apart.
