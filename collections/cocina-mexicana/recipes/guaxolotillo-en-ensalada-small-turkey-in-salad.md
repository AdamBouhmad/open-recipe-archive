## Ingredients

- Small turkey
- Lettuce hearts
- Anchovies
- Capers
- Olives
- Pickles
- Oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as for mayonnaise
2. Surround with lettuce hearts and decorate the salad with anchovies, capers, olives and pickles
3. Serve with a cruet of oil
