## Ingredients

- Lard
- Tender onion slices
- Stale bread, torn by hand into pieces
- Three to four wide chile peppers, deveined and washed
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place a cooking pot on the fire with lard and let it burn
2. Fry slices of tender onion in it
3. Add pieces of stale bread torn by hand
4. Moisten with a broth made from three to four wide chiles, deveined, washed and ground, which are dissolved in sufficient water
5. Season with salt and let the soup come to a boil
6. Remove from heat, let the covered pot rest, and serve hot
