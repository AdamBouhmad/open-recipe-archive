## Ingredients

- Two cuartillos of milk
- Six ounces of butter
- A little bread crumb
- One cinnamon stick
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Citron
- Almonds, all chopped

## Directions

1. Mix two cuartillos of milk with six ounces of butter, a little bread crumb, and one cinnamon stick
2. Add egg yolks and sufficient sugar as for jericalla
3. Mix everything well and strain
4. Add chopped raisins, citron, and almonds
5. Pour into a buttered serving dish
6. Place over hot coals and cover with a comal with fire on top until it sets well
