## Ingredients

- one arroba of sugar
- one arroba and two cuartillos more of virgin honey
- six egg whites
- almonds

## Directions

1. After clarifying one arroba of sugar, mix with another arroba and two cuartillos more of virgin honey, choosing the clearest; strain through fine cloth and place on heat to reach the soft ball stage, taking care to remove with a small broom what sticks around the edge of the pan
2. After it has reached the point, remove and cool with a paddle, adding six well-beaten egg whites, and beating everything with the paddle by force of arms until it turns white, having repeated four times the operation of adding six egg whites
3. As soon as it is white and thick, remove with a finger a thimbleful of nougat, which when immediately immersed in cold water should break
4. If the fire is very strong, diminish it somewhat by covering with some bricks or sherds, and add as much almond as it can hold
