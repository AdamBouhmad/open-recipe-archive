## Ingredients

- old partridges, plucked, emptied and singed
- ham strips and lardoons
- salt
- pepper
- aromatics
- ham slices
- beef slices
- leftover meats
- onions
- carrots
- herb bundle
- broth
- white wine
- butter
- paper
- Spanish sauce
- essence of game

## Directions

1. Pluck, empty and singe the partridges
2. Lard them with medium lardoons of ham, well seasoned with salt, pepper and ground aromatics
3. Gather their legs and tie them to maintain a nice form
4. Place in a pot with ham slices, beef slices, leftover meats, some onions and carrots and a mixed herb bundle
5. Moisten with broth and white wine
6. Cover well with a wheel of paper coated with butter
7. Once the partridges are cooked, remove them from the braise, let drain, untie and arrange
8. Pour thick Spanish sauce mixed with a little essence of game over them
