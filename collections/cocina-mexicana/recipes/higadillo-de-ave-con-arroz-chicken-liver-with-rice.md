## Ingredients

- Chicken livers
- Chicken yolks
- Chicken blood
- Rice
- Poultry stock
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Take the chicken livers, yolks and blood from the birds and fry them
2. When cooked, combine with the rice and let it braise
3. Add stock and a little Spanish sauce
4. Afterwards, crush one liver and let it cook until it thickens somewhat
