## Ingredients

- substance (meat broth)
- eggs
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Place a little substance in the plate in which the eggs will be served.
2. Place on gentle heat on the stove.
3. Carefully break the eggs over it so that the yolks remain whole and do not burst.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Cook from above with a shovel made red hot and serve half-soft.
