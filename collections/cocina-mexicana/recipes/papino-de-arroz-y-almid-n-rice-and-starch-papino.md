## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- starch, sifted
- six quarters of milk
- sugar
- ten egg yolks
- orange blossom water
- manjar blanco (milk-based cream)
- carmine
- sea salt
- mamón

## Directions

1. Wash one pound of rice in cold water and then in hot water.
2. Dry it and then grind it.
3. When ground, weigh it and complete one pound with sifted starch, replacing what the rice has lost.
4. Mix everything with six quarters of milk, sweetened well with sugar, and having mixed everything in the milk, strain through a sieve without ceasing to stir everywhere.
5. Once it is boiling, beat ten egg yolks and add them to the milk with a little orange blossom water.
6. Give it the half-point of manjar blanco and add a little carmine, enough to give it a pink color.
7. Before sweetening, add two grains of sea salt, and it can be served as ante over layers of mamón.
