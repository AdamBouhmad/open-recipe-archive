## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls of clean jasmine flowers
- Clarified syrup
- Sugar powder

## Directions

1. Follow the same procedures as for candied violet sugar
2. Use two handfuls of clean jasmine flowers in place of half a pound of violet flowers
