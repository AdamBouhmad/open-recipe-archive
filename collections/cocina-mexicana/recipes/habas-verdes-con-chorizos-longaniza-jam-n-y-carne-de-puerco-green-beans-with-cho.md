## Ingredients

- Small, tender green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Ham, in small pieces
- Chorizos, whole
- Longaniza, in pieces
- Pork, in small portions
- Garlic
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Green chiles
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Clove
- Pepper
- Fried bread toasts, ground
- Capers
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Olives

## Directions

1. Choose small and tender beans and cook them in salted water with pieces of ham, whole chorizos, pieces of longaniza, and small portions of pork, all together.
2. In a separate pot, fry finely chopped garlic, onions, tomatoes, and some green chiles.
3. Add this frying to the pot of beans.
4. Add saffron, cumin, clove, and pepper, all ground, along with fried bread toasts, capers, oil, and vinegar.
5. Allow the broth to thicken.
6. When serving at table, add olives, which would give a bad flavor to the stew if it boiled with them.
