## Ingredients

- almond cake in preserve
- beaten egg yolks
- butter
- syrup
- garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the almond cake already in preserve into slices
2. Coat with beaten egg yolks
3. Fry in butter
4. Place on plates and cover with syrup of good consistency
5. Decorate like the previous recipe
