## Ingredients

- Half a pound of chocolate
- One glass of water
- Six egg yolks
- Four cuartillos of double cream
- Twelve ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pound of chocolate in a glass of water over gentle heat
2. Beat it with a chocolate whisk until it is well dissolved and has reduced slightly
3. Remove from heat and mix six egg yolks into it, incorporating them together with four cuartillos of double cream and twelve ounces of powdered sugar
4. Pour everything into a jug and when it has cooled, complete the operation as in the previous articles
