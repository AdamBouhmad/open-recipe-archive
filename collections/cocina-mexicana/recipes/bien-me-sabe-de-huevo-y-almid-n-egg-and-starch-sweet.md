## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- twelve ounces of starch
- half an adarme of saffron in box, well washed

## Directions

1. Dissolve in four cuartillos of milk twelve egg yolks and twelve ounces of starch
2. Add half an adarme of saffron in box, well washed
