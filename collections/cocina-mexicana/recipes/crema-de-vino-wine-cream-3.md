## Ingredients

- eight to ten egg yolks
- powdered sugar
- one bottle of Valdepeñas or Málaga wine, sweetened and aromatic

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with sufficient powdered sugar
2. Gradually add the wine to the beaten mixture while stirring constantly
3. Cook everything while stirring until the cream is perfectly thickened
