## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Raw chicken breast
- Fat ham
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Egg yolks
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cumin
- Nutmeg
- Fried garlic broth
- Egg yolks
- Lemon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes and remove the fuzzy center as in previous recipes.
2. Chop raw chicken breast, fat ham, garlic, and parsley all together.
3. To this raw meat add egg yolks, and add clove, pepper, cinnamon, a few cumin seeds, and nutmeg: grind all this and mix it with the chopped meat, and fill the artichokes with this.
4. Arrange them in a dish and moisten with broth of fried garlic, increasing it as it is consumed, until they are well cooked.
5. When cooked, bathe them with beaten egg yolks mixed with lemon and parsley, being careful not to stir the egg yolks except with the same serving plate, so they do not break.
