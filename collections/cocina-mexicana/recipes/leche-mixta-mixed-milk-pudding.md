## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- one and a half pocilios of rice
- half a pound of almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak one and a half pocilios of rice, wash it and mix it together
2. Stir everything in a pot and place it on the fire
3. Clean half a pound of almonds, toast them in a pan and grind them again
4. Strain with the milk and add to the pot
5. Continue grinding the strainings until nothing remains
6. Bring to the point of white pudding
7. Pour into bowls
8. Top with toasted almonds split in half
9. Cover with powdered sugar
10. Place in a small oven without fire and put it above on a grid
11. Remove once well browned
