## Ingredients

- Nixtamal (corn prepared with lime)
- Nine and a half ounces of sugar
- One pound of lard
- Salt
- Warm water
- Anise
- Water for cooking
- Corn leaves

## Directions

1. Wash the nixtamal well and dry it with a cloth
2. Grind and sift well
3. Add nine and a half ounces of sugar, one pound of lard, salt, warm water and anise
4. Beat everything well
5. Place a little water in a pot and form a wooden grate so the tamales do not get wet when placed on it
6. Cook with the steam from the water
