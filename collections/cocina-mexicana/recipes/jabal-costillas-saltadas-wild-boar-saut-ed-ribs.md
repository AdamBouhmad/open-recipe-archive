## Ingredients

- wild boar ribs, cut and prepared
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- one glass of white wine
- one glass of Spanish sauce
- flour toasted in lard and concentrated broth (if Spanish sauce is not available)

## Directions

1. Fry the cut and prepared ribs, making them jump in butter with salt and pepper over a very high fire
2. When cooked on both sides, arrange them on the plate in the form of a crown
3. Into the pan in which they were fried, pour one glass of white wine and another glass of Spanish sauce
4. Let it reduce as necessary and pour this sauce over the ribs
5. If Spanish sauce is not available, it can be replaced with a light broth made with flour toasted in lard and concentrated broth
