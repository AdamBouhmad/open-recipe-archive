## Ingredients

- three eggs
- salt
- one quarter quart of milk
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs in a bowl with salt
2. Pour one quarter quart of milk over it and stir well
3. Add sifted flour gradually and stir well
4. Beat with your hands until you have a dough like common fritters, neither too hard nor too soft
5. Once well beaten, make the fritters with holes and fry them over moderate heat
