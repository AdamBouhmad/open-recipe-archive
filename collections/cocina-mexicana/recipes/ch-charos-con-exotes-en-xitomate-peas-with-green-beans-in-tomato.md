## Ingredients

- peas
- whole green beans
- chorizo slices
- longaniza pieces
- butter
- onions
- garlic
- tomatoes
- saffron
- pepper
- cloves
- fried bread pieces
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook peas with whole green beans, chorizo slices and longaniza pieces together.
2. Once cooked, fry onions, garlic and tomatoes all chopped in butter.
3. Chop the green beans and add to the mixture with the peas, chorizo, longaniza and the broth in which they were cooked.
4. Add saffron, pepper, cloves, pieces of bread fried in butter all ground, and a little oil.
5. Let simmer until most of the broth is consumed.
