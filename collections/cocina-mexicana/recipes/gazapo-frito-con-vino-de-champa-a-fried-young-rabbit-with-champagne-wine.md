## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit
- butter
- ground spices
- flour
- champagne wine
- salt
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the rabbit the same way as the previous recipe with the same seasonings
2. Add a small tablespoon of flour
3. Mix the flour with the rabbit off the heat
4. Add a good glass of champagne wine
5. Place the pan on the fire and stir well so the sauce thickens without boiling
6. When it reaches good consistency, serve the stew
