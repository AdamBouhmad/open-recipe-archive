## Ingredients

- Sweet oranges, cut into pieces
- Lemon juice
- 1 pound sugar per half pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Cut the oranges into pieces and remove the segments
2. Cook in boiling water with a little lemon juice until tender
3. Remove from the water and place in cold water
4. Drain and press in a napkin
5. Crush and pass through a sieve
6. Place this jam in syrup at high feather stage and let it boil for a few moments
