## Ingredients

- one young hen, roasted on the spit and then fried
- several spoonfuls of béchamel sauce
- a little cream
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the young hen on the spit and then fry it
2. Remove the meat and chop it very finely
3. Put several spoonfuls of béchamel sauce in a pot with a little cream and butter, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Let this sauce boil and then mix it with the chopped meat
5. Heat everything together
