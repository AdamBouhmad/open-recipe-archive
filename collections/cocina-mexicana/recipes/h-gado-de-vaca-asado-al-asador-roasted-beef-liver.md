## Ingredients

- beef liver
- thick lard for larding
- pork fat
- oil
- salt
- bay leaves
- onion slices
- parsley
- iron spit or wooden skewer
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. The liver can be larded on the inside with thick lard and on the outside with pork fat
2. Place it afterwards to marinate in oil with salt, one or two bay leaves, onion slices and parsley, leaving it in this marinade for six or more hours
3. At the moment of putting it on the spit, check all over so nothing remains from the different things it was marinated in
4. Pass an iron spit or wooden skewer through the middle lengthwise, and then introduce other smaller skewers to secure it to the largest one with twine
5. Secure it so it does not move, wrap it in paper spread with butter, which is removed before it finishes cooking so it takes on good color
6. It needs two hours to be well cooked, but can be removed once no more blood drips, which is a good sign of being perfectly cooked
7. Remove the skewers and serve with Italian sauce or French pepper sauce
