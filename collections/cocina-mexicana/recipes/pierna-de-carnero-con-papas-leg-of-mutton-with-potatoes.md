## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- lard
- broth
- herbs for seasoning
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from near the rump and fold the meat
2. Cut the leg bone very short
3. Fry the meat in lard
4. Moisten with a little broth and season by adding a bundle of herbs
5. Cook over low heat
6. Serve with potatoes; it can also be served with green beans or other vegetables as deemed appropriate
