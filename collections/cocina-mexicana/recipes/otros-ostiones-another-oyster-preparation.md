## Ingredients

- seasoning mix
- spices
- oil
- lard
- raw oysters
- parsley
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Chop all the seasoning and grind all the spices
2. Fry everything in oil and lard
3. Add the raw oysters without water
4. When they are a little fried, add finely chopped parsley
5. Coat a cazuela with lard, place the oysters together
6. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top and put to cook over two fires
