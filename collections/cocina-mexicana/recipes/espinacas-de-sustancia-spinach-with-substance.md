## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- salt
- bread, fried

## Directions

1. After washing and briefly placing in hot water, drain and fry in butter, dusted with a little flour
2. When well fried, add beef broth and season with necessary salt
3. Let cook and serve with fried bread toasts
