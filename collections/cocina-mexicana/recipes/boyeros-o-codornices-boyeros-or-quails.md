## Ingredients

- boyeros or quails (cleaned and eviscerated)
- butter
- salt
- garlic (six cloves, chopped)
- onion (chopped)
- parsley (chopped)
- dry wine (a glass)
- fine spices

## Directions

1. After cleaning and eviscerating, slightly over-roast on a grill at very low heat for a short time.
2. Put in a pan with butter and salt.
3. Fry them and remove.
4. In the broth they left, fry six chopped garlic cloves, onion and parsley.
5. Add a glass of dry wine and fine spices.
6. Put the quails and let them simmer and boil at low heat for about half an hour.
7. Serve afterwards.
