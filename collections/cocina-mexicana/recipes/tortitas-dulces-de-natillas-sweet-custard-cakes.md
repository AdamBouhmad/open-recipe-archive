## Ingredients

- two large cups of custard
- sifted sugar to taste
- toasted and ground malton
- split almonds
- raisins
- pine nuts
- eight egg yolks, beaten and hardened

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of custard with sifted sugar to taste and toasted and ground malton, and beat until a paste forms
2. Add split almonds, raisins, pine nuts and eight egg yolks beaten and hardened
3. When everything is well incorporated and the paste is not too dry, put it in small molds or small cazuelas spread with lard and put to cook in the oven
