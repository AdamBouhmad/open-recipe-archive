## Ingredients

- lamb
- ham
- garlic
- pepper
- lard
- oil
- onion
- tomato
- bay leaf
- parsley
- thyme
- oregano
- mint
- roasted garlic head
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- flour

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb as for a stew, with ham, garlic and pepper
2. Parboil with lard and oil, sliced onion and tomato
3. Once parboiled and in the pot, grind bay leaf, parsley, thyme, oregano, mint, one roasted garlic head, cumin, salt and sufficient vinegar
4. Cover the pot and thicken the meat in the cazuela with a little flour browned in lard
5. It should not be too dry
