## Ingredients

- A piece of squash
- Butter
- Salt
- Milk
- Sugar
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Take a piece of squash, remove the skin and seeds, cut it into small pieces and place it on the fire in a saucepan with water.
2. When it is broken down like marmalade and the water has been consumed, add a little butter and salt, letting it cook a bit.
3. Cook milk with sugar and pour this over the squash.
4. Place bread slices in a platter, moisten with part of the squash, and covering it, place on a gentle fire for a quarter hour, taking care it does not boil, and at the time of serving finish pouring the rest of the hot broth.
