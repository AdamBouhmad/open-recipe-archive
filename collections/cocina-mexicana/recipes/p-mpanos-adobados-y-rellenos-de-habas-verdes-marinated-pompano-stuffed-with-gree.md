## Ingredients

- broad chiles, deveined and soaked
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- vinegar
- salt
- pompano, cleaned and desalted if not fresh
- green beans, bound with egg
- oiled papers
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Grind the broad chiles (deveined and soaked), garlic, pepper, and clove together
2. Dissolve the ground mixture with vinegar and season with salt
3. Marinate the cleaned and desalted pompano in this sauce
4. Stuff the pompano with green beans bound with egg
5. Wrap in oiled papers and fry in lard, or cook on the grill
