## Ingredients

- bread crumb box
- minced meat and cooked herbs
- calf rennet
- fine herbs
- breadcrumbs or grated bread
- melted butter
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Fry in oil a box made of bread crumbs, whose bottom is garnished with minced meat and cooked herbs, and fill it with alternating layers of rennet and fine herbs
2. Cover the last layer of herbs with breadcrumbs or grated bread, and pour a little melted butter on top
3. Put the box on a silver plate over gentle heat, and cover it with a bell oven
4. When the crust begins to form and the box has taken on a good color, pour a little Spanish sauce over it and serve
