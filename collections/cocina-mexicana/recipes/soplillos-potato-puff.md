## Ingredients

- Four tablespoons potato starch
- One quarter pound cream
- Four ounces sugar
- Four ounces lard
- Scraped lemon zest
- Four egg yolks
- Six egg yolks
- Four egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve four tablespoons of potato starch in one quarter pound of cream
2. Add four ounces of sugar, four ounces of lard, scraped lemon zest and four egg yolks
3. Bring everything to a boil until it becomes consistent
4. Remove it from the fire and let it cool
5. Mix in six more egg yolks and four well-beaten egg whites
6. Take small pieces of this mixture and place them on paper, separated from each other
7. Place a thin iron plate over very hot ashes on top
8. Serve immediately as they begin to rise
