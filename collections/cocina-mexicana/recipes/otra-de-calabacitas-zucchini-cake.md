## Ingredients

- zucchini
- cinnamon powder
- eggs
- bread
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Chop the zucchini and stew until cooked and dried
2. Cool them and mix with plenty of cinnamon powder and sufficient beaten eggs
3. In a buttered casserole, place a layer of toasted bread from the comal and ground, another of zucchini, and the last layer of bread
4. Add plenty of butter and place over two gentle fires
5. When it has set and browned, remove it and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
