## Ingredients

- Water
- Cooked blood, sliced into strips
- Lard
- Oil
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Clove
- Pepper
- Ground cinnamon
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Olives
- Chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Put water in a casserole over the fire and when it is boiling add the cooked blood, cut into strips.
2. In a pan, heat lard and oil, and fry the blood strips well with plenty of tomato, onion, and a little garlic.
3. Once well fried, add to the pan sufficient water with clove, pepper, ground cinnamon, parsley, oregano, and thyme in small quantity.
4. Put over the fire until the broth becomes thick.
5. When serving, add olives and chiles in vinegar.
