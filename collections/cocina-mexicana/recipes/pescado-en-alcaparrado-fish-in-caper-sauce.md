## Ingredients

- Capers
- Almonds
- Bread fried in lard
- Toasted cilantro
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Wine
- Cooked fish portions

## Directions

1. Grind capers with some almonds, bread fried in lard, toasted cilantro, clove and cinnamon
2. Fry all the ground mixture in lard
3. Add wine and let boil
4. Place portions of cooked fish in this sauce
5. Let boil a little longer and remove from heat so the caper sauce is not too thick
