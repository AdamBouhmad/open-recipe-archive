## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Sugar
- Aguardiente
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Separate the outer part of the lemon peel with a knife, cut the fruit and squeeze it
2. Place the separated peel inside the juice for twenty-four hours, together with twelve ounces of sugar per quartillo of juice
3. Three days later add half a quartillo of aguardiente for every four of mixture and let ferment and clarify for fifteen days before filtering and bottling
