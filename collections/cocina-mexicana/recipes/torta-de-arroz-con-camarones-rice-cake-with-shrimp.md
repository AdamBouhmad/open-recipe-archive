## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- garlic
- salt
- lard
- shrimp
- tomatoes
- onions
- parsley
- cured chile peppers

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in water after washing and sun-drying it
2. When cooked, add cloves, cinnamon, pepper, saffron, and two or three roasted garlic cloves, all ground
3. Add a small amount of lard, season with salt, and once all the broth is consumed, set aside for the next day
4. Grind raw shrimp and fry in a pot with lard, tomatoes, onions, garlic, parsley, and cured chile peppers, all well chopped and in abundance
