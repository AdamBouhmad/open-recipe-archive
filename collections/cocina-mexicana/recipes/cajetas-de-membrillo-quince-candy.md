## Ingredients

- quince, peeled and cored
- clarified syrup
- two pounds and a half of sugar for every pound of quince

## Directions

1. Peel and core the quince, cook and grind, sifting through a thin cloth
2. Make clarified syrup with two pounds and a half of sugar for every pound of quince
3. Put over heat and allow to reach the point of separating from the pan
