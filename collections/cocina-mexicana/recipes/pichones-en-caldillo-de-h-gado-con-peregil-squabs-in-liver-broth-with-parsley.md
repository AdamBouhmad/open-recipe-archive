## Ingredients

- cooked livers
- fried bread
- lard
- chopped onion
- salt
- chopped parsley
- oregano
- saffron
- pepper
- cloves
- squabs
- squab cooking broth
- raw oil

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked livers with fried bread
2. Fry chopped onion in lard, then add the ground liver mixture and fry
3. Season with salt and add chopped parsley, oregano, and ground saffron, pepper and cloves
4. Place the squabs with their cooking broth in this mixture
5. Let simmer and season properly
6. When removing from heat, add raw oil
