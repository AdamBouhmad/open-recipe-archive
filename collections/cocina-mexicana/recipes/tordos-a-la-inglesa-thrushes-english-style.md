## Ingredients

- thrushes
- lard or ham
- paper
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck the thrushes and tuck the legs in
2. Thread them on a skewer and fasten to the spit, wrapping them in paper
3. Place a piece of lard or ham at the end of another skewer wrapped in paper
4. Light the fat and let it drip over the thrushes, after removing their paper
5. Dust with salt and breadcrumbs
6. Allow them to take on good color
