## Ingredients

- six pounds sugar
- straw for clarifying
- juice of one lemon
- one egg white
- sixty-four egg yolks
- four quarts milk

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with six pounds of sugar, clarified with a piece of straw, lemon juice, and one egg white
2. When at medium point, add the sixty-four egg yolks as you would for royal eggs
3. Mix with four quarts of milk
4. Pour everything into the syrup
