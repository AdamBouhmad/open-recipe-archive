## Ingredients

- venison ribs
- ham slices
- broth
- onions
- bouquet garni
- carrots

## Directions

1. After preparation, lard with ham slices and cook with two spoonfuls of broth, onions, a bouquet and carrot slices, placing over two fires.
2. After cooking, allow to form aspic and serve, surrounded with a piquant sauce.
