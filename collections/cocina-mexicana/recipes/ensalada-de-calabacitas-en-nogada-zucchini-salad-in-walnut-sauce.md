## Ingredients

- Walnuts, large
- Almonds
- Garlic cloves
- Black pepper
- Bread crumb, soaked
- Fresh cheese
- Salt
- Oil
- Cooked zucchini
- Pomegranate
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Soak in warm water the quantity of large walnuts needed and remove the skin.
2. Also soak almonds, in less quantity than the walnuts, and peel them.
3. Grind both on a very clean metate with garlic and black pepper so that the pepper flavor stands out, soaked bread crumb, and fresh cheese.
4. Ensure everything is well ground with the necessary salt.
5. Add sufficient oil and arrange the zucchini that have been previously cooked with salt.
6. Garnish the salad with pomegranate broken into seeds and lettuce on top.
7. Serve cold.
