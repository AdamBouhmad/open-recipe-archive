## Ingredients

- shrimp
- ancho chiles
- ají
- lard
- water
- salt
- eggs
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the shrimp from the day before and remove the heads, tails and feet
2. Wash them well in two or three waters and cook them without salt
3. Clean them again of all remaining skin and fry them in lard with soaked and ground ancho chiles with ají
4. Add the shrimp to the frying mixture along with the water they were cooked in, salt and half-beaten eggs, stirring constantly when adding so they do not become lumpy
5. After they have boiled well, they should remain in a broth that is neither thick nor watery
6. Add cooked potatoes in small pieces
