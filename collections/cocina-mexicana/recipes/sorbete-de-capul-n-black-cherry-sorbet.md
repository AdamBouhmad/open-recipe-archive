## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of black cherries
- juice of currants or strawberries
- ½ quartillo of water
- ½ pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Drain the juice from the black cherries and currants or strawberries through fine linen
2. Mix with ½ quartillo of water and ½ pound of sugar
3. Stir everything well
4. Freeze
