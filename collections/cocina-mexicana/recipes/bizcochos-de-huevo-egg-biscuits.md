## Ingredients

- twenty-four egg yolks
- three ounces of raw birote bread, finely ground
- seven ounces of ground sugar
- three ounces of melted butter
- two tablespoons of orange juice
- salt
- flour of the finest quality, up to one pound

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-four egg yolks until they form very large eyes
2. Dissolve three ounces of raw birote bread, finely ground in the metate, into them using your fingers
3. With a wooden spoon, gradually incorporate seven ounces of ground sugar, three ounces of melted butter, two tablespoons of orange juice, a pinch of salt, and flour of the finest quality
4. Mix little by little until the dough becomes smooth but capable of being worked
5. Cover and let it sit overnight exposed to the air
6. The next day, shape the biscuits and place them on papers greased with butter
7. Bake in an oven, ensuring the heat is even
