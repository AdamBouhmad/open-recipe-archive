## Ingredients

- three quartillos of milk
- ten egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook three quartillos of milk until reduced to two
2. Set aside from the fire and when cold, mix with ten egg yolks and the corresponding sugar
3. Return to the fire, and as it thickens, pour over sliced mamón in a suitable platter
4. Having filled it with alternating layers of mamón and milk, put it in the oven to set
