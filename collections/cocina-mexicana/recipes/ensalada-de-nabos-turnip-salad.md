## Ingredients

- Turnips
- Salt
- Syrup at medium point
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- White wine
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Walnuts
- Pine nuts
- Sesame seeds, toasted
- Candied citron

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips and cut them into small pieces
2. Soak them, changing the water two or three times before cooking
3. Cook them with salt so they are neither hard nor overcooked
4. Wash them sufficiently to remove the odor
5. Place them in syrup at medium point with cloves, cinnamon, and ground pepper
6. Give them a half boil and remove from the fire, covering well
7. After two days, drain the syrup
8. On the serving plate, dress them with white wine, sugar and ground cinnamon, raisins, almonds, walnuts, and pine nuts, all clean, toasted sesame seeds, and candied citron
