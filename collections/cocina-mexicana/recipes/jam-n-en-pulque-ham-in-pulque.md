## Ingredients

- ham, soaked and cleaned
- pulque
- cilantro, dried and whole
- panela
- bay leaf
- Tabasco pepper
- Jerez wine
- sugar
- catalán liqueur

## Directions

1. After thorough soaking, washing and scrubbing with a brush, cook in pulque with dried whole cilantro, panela, a little bay leaf and plenty of Tabasco pepper
2. Once cooked, drain and let cool, then round it
3. Place Jerez wine in a pan and partially cook the ham
4. Place on a platter with the lean side down
5. Dust with sugar on top and sides
6. Coat well with superior catalán liqueur and ignite
7. Should be brought to the table while the catalán is still burning
