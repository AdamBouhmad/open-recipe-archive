## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Cooked oysters
- Béchamel sauce or butter (as much as two eggs)
- Flour
- Black pepper
- Oyster water
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Take a handful of puff pastry dough and flatten it with a rolling pin until it is the thickness of a line
2. Cut circles with a large pastry cutter
3. Fit these bases into small copper timbales or cups as best as possible
4. Remove the beards from the cooked oysters; if large, divide them in half, or leave whole if small
5. Add the oysters to béchamel sauce, or if unavailable, make a sauce with butter, flour, and coarse pepper
6. Place this mixture over the fire and add oyster water, stirring continuously and adding more oyster water if the sauce is too thick
7. Add the oysters to the sauce and bind everything with two or three eggs
8. Fill the pastries with this mixture, cover with a pastry lid, glaze, and bake in the oven until cooked, then remove from the timbales to serve
