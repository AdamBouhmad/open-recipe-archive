## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 2 eggs, one with white and one without
- a little cold lard
- salt
- water
- 20 ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Place 1 pound of flour on the table and add 2 eggs (one whole and one yolk only), a little cold lard, salt, and water
2. Knead until the dough becomes tough and forms blisters
3. Extend the dough, brush with butter and fold
4. Continue brushing with butter, folding and extending until 20 ounces of butter have been incorporated
5. Proceed as with the previous pastries
