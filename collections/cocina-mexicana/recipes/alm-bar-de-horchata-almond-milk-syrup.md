## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- four ounces of bitter almonds
- two pounds of sugar
- one azumbre of water
- two ounces of orange blossom water
- rind of one lemon

## Directions

1. Leave the almonds in cold water for the proper time so that the skin separates easily, and should not be placed in hot water as is customary
2. Pound the almonds in a mortar, adding scrapings of lemon rind
3. Dissolve everything in half of the water, press, strain and cook until it takes on sufficient consistency
4. Bottle and be sure to shake it every two or three days
