## Ingredients

- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- cooked potatoes, cut like salad
- milk

## Directions

1. Place in a pot chopped parsley and onion with the cooked potatoes cut like a salad.
2. Sauté them.
3. Moisten with milk, taking care to stir them well.
4. Serve before they cook.
