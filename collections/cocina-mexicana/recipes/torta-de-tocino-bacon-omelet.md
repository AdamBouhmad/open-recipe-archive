## Ingredients

- Desalted ham, rind removed
- Butter
- Eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut into small square pieces a piece of desalted ham with the rind removed; brown it in the skillet with a little butter and add the eggs well beaten and seasoned; finish and serve like the plain omelet.
