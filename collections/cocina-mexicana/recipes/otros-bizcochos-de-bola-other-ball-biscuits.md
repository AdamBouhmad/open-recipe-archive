## Ingredients

- twenty egg whites
- twenty egg yolks
- one pound of ground sugar
- one pound of melted lard
- five pounds of flour
- six large cups of leavening
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites
2. Mix in the egg yolks and beat until the foam is very thick
3. Add the ground sugar and melted lard
4. Add the flour and leavening
5. Mix everything and knead until it takes on good consistency
6. Form balls of about four ounces each
7. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top
8. Bake in the oven or over two fires
