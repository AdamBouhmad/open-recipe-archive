## Ingredients

- Good quality ancho peppers
- Large cheese, ground
- Lard
- Small cooked onions
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Warm the ancho peppers gently so they can be deveined on one side without tearing them or removing the stem
2. Soak the deveined peppers, then rinse and let them dry
3. Fill them with ground cheese, keeping them flat and not bulging
4. Fry in sufficient lard until cooked but not burned
5. In the same lard, partially fry cooked small onions that have been cooked in water and salt
6. When they have fried a bit, add their water, and when it comes to a boil, add the peppers carefully so they do not break open
7. They should remain in broth; use only enough water for them to soften so that when serving, the skin can be easily removed when eating
