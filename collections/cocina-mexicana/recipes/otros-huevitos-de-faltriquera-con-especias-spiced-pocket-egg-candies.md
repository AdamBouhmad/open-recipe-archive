## Ingredients

- sugar
- sixteen half-beaten egg yolks
- fifty ground cloves
- cinnamon powder to taste
- sifted sugar for rolling
- decorative cut papers

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup brought to soft ball stage in water
2. Mix with sixteen half-beaten egg yolks, fifty ground cloves, and cinnamon powder to taste
3. Place the mixture over low heat so the egg cooks without stopping beating
4. Continue moving, then remove from heat
5. Beat until it forms a ball
6. Empty onto a plate dusted with sugar
7. Form small balls, which should be rolled in sifted sugar
8. Wrap in decorative cut and curled papers
