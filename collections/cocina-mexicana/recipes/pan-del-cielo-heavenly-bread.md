## Ingredients

- four handfuls of flour
- twelve eggs
- one and a half cups of melted butter
- one and a half cups of milk
- sugar as needed

## Directions

1. Take four handfuls of flour
2. Add twelve eggs beaten with a wooden spoon, one and a half cups of melted butter, one and a half cups of milk, and the necessary sugar
3. Spread the dough on papers
4. Place in the oven for perfect cooking
