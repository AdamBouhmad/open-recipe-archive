## Ingredients

- five pounds of sugar
- two grated coconuts
- two or three cups of toasted and crushed biscuit

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in water and on the fire
2. Clarify the syrup and strain it
3. Mix it with what is produced from two grated coconuts
4. Leave on the heat until it reaches the point of conserve
5. Add two or three cups of toasted and crushed biscuit, according to what is needed
6. Do not stop stirring after it is incorporated, until it detaches from all sides
7. Pour onto a table covered with wafers, smooth it immediately
8. Cut into desired size, but do not divide the alfajores until after the paste has cooled
