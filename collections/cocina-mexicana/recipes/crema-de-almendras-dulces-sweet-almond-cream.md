## Ingredients

- seis almendras dulces (six sweet almonds)
- agua hirviendo (boiling water)
- dos claras de huevo (two egg whites)
- dos cuartillos de leche (two quarters of milk)
- cuatro onzas de azúcar en polvo (four ounces of powdered sugar)
- una cucharada de agua de azahar (one tablespoon of orange blossom water)
- almendras encarameladas (caramelized almonds)

## Directions

1. Keep six sweet almonds in boiling water for a few minutes and remove the skin
2. Grind them immediately with a little water so they do not release oil
3. Beat two egg whites in two quarters of milk with four ounces of powdered sugar
4. Bring to boil on gentle heat until it reduces by a quarter
5. Add the ground almonds
6. Let boil for a few more minutes
7. When removed from heat, add one tablespoon of orange blossom water
8. When cold, decorate with caramelized almonds
9. Serve
