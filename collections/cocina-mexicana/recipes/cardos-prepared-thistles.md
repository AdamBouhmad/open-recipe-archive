## Ingredients

- Tenderest and whitest thistle stalks
- White sauce
- Concentrated meat broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Select the tenderest and whitest stalks that are full and firm inside
2. Clean and cut all pieces to the same size
3. Blanch in boiling water
4. Refresh in cold water
5. Cook in white sauce
6. Boil in good concentrated broth
7. Remove and arrange on a plate
8. Let them absorb the broth and pour it over them
