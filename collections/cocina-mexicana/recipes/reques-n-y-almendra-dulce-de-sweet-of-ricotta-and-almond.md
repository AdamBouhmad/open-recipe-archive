## Ingredients

- Ricotta cheese
- cooked egg yolks
- pine nuts
- almond paste
- almond
- clarified syrup
- biscuit
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make the ricotta paste with the difference of using only one pound of ricotta, and adding four ounces of cooked egg yolks and one ounce of pine nuts, all ground separately
2. Make another almond paste that should be somewhat loose, regulating for each pound of almond two pounds of clarified syrup at the point of gathering in water
3. Put in a platter a layer of biscuit soaked in milk cooked with sugar and cinnamon, and slightly squeezed with the hands, another of ricotta paste, another of biscuit, another of almond paste, another of egg yolk paste, another of pine nuts and in this manner until the plate is filled, the last layer being ricotta, and concluding everything as stated in the preceding article
