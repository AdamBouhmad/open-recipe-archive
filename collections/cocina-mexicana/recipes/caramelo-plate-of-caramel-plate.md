## Ingredients

- sweet almond oil
- cinnamon pieces
- orange blossom
- peanuts
- small sour cherries
- sugar syrup at caramel stage

## Directions

1. On a plate oiled with sweet almond oil, spread small pieces of cinnamon with a little orange blossom, shelled peanuts cleaned, toasted and cut into pieces, and small sour cherries
2. Once everything is placed symmetrically on the plate, pour the sugar syrup at caramel stage over the fruit, proceeding very slowly and letting it fall in a thin stream
3. Turn the substances over and repeat the same process on the other side, without loading the plate with more syrup than necessary to cover the parts to be candied
