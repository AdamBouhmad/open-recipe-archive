## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Butter
- Garlic cloves
- Clove
- Cumin
- Broth
- Flour
- Parsley
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the artichokes as initially described and cut them in half
2. Fry chopped garlic cloves in butter
3. Then add the artichoke halves, separating the garlic which will be ground with clove and cumin
4. Add this mixture over the artichokes before they finish frying
5. Add broth and flour browned in butter, chopped parsley and necessary salt
6. Allow the broth to reduce greatly so that the artichokes are served almost dry
