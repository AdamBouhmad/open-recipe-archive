## Ingredients

- six carrots
- six turnips
- six onions
- one cabbage
- a little celery
- one glass of water
- four ounces of fresh butter
- a bunch of parsley
- a handful of peas and green beans, or some potatoes or one spoonful of dissolved potatoes in sufficient water

## Directions

1. Cut six carrots in small slices, the same number of turnips and very small onions, one cabbage, a little celery
2. Put in a pot, adding one glass of water, four ounces of fresh butter and a bunch of parsley
3. Bring to a boil until the water evaporates
4. Then add a handful of peas and green beans, or some potatoes or one spoonful of dissolved potatoes in sufficient water so it has regular broth
5. Leave in infusion for three hours after seasoning appropriately
6. Pass through a sieve
