## Ingredients

- tripe
- vinegar
- lemon
- salt
- bay leaf
- cornflower cut into wheels
- thyme
- parsley
- toasted bread crumbs
- garlic
- bacon
- spices
- chile

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing the tripe thoroughly with two or three waters, drain and leave in an adobe made of vinegar, lemon, salt, and a bay leaf branch for about two hours
2. Remove and drain the tripe
3. Cut into pieces and place to cook with cornflower cut into wheels, thyme, and chopped parsley
4. When almost cooked, make a sauce with toasted bread crumbs, garlic, bacon, spices, and chile
5. Finish cooking in a pot, stirring well, and serve
