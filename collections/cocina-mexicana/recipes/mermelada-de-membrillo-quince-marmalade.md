## Ingredients

- quinces
- apples
- jelly from quince cores
- one ounce of apple per pound of quince
- less than one small cup of jelly
- two pounds of clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Cut quinces into quarters and remove the cores, cook one part of the other
2. When cooked, pass through a sieve
3. Separately pass apples through a sieve and strain the jelly from the cores
4. For each pound of quince put one ounce of apple, a little less than one small cup of jelly to two pounds of clarified sugar
5. Boil everything except the quince over regular fire
6. It is ready when dropping a few spoonfuls in water forms a ball
7. Then incorporate the quince, remove from heat to break it up
8. When cooled, empty the quince; once they cook remove the water so they set quickly
