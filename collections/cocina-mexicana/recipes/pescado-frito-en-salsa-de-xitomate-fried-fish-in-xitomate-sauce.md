## Ingredients

- xitomates
- garlic
- oil
- fish cooking water
- green chile

## Directions

1. Roast xitomates and grind with roasted garlic cloves
2. Fry well
3. Moisten with a little water from cooking the fish
4. Add strips of roasted and cleaned green chile
5. Once the chile is cooked, remove from heat
6. Bathe the fried fish with this sauce
