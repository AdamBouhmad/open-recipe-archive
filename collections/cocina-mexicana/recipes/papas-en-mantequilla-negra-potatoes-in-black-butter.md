## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- parsley
- black butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes cooked in the ordinary manner into pieces
2. Place them on a plate and surround them with sprigs of fried parsley
3. Sprinkle with black butter (see black butter, p. 607)
4. Serve promptly
