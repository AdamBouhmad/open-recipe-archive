## Ingredients

- various fruits that can be cut
- aguardiente (brandy)
- sugar
- orange blossom water
- lemon rind
- frying batter
- butter or lard or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Make fritters from all kinds of fruits that can be cut, whether in slices, quarters or wheels, and serve as supplementary dishes
2. After removing the skins, seeds or pits from the fruits, cut them
3. Put them in an infusion for some hours in aguardiente with sugar, orange blossom water and lemon rind
4. Drain them well afterward, cover them with some frying batter or fry them without covering until they take on good color, whether in butter, lard, or as is used in some places, in oil
5. Give them a glaze of sugar by passing a hot iron palette over them, if it is not preferred to simply dust them with sugar
