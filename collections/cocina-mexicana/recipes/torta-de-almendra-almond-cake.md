## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of sugar
- a pound of ground almonds
- bitter almonds to taste
- six egg yolks beaten
- twelve egg whites beaten very well
- six egg yolks
- butter
- high-stage syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Make syrup from the sugar at high stage
2. Add the ground almonds and bitter almonds, then six beaten egg yolks
3. Fold this into twelve well-beaten egg whites and six egg yolks
4. Butter a pan and add the batter
5. Cook over two fires, not in the oven as it will settle
6. When cooked, coat the top with high-stage syrup
7. Repeat until it forms a frosting (betún)
