## Ingredients

- Meat with skin on
- Dry firewood

## Directions

1. Open a hole one vara deep and of the size of the piece to be cooked
2. Form a fire in it with well-dried firewood
3. After it has heated much, remove all the fire and throw the piece there without removing the skin
4. Cover it with hot embers without flames
5. Over this embers form a fire of dry firewood that is kept going until the following morning
6. In the morning, remove the cooked meat
