## Ingredients

- beef
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- parsley, chopped
- lemon juice
- red wine
- nutmeg
- burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Fry the meat in butter with salt and pepper
2. Brown flour well in the butter remaining in the pan
3. Dissolve in broth
4. Add a dusting of pepper, chopped parsley, lemon juice, red wine, and nutmeg
5. Add the steaks to this sauce
6. If the sauce is not caramel colored, add a little burnt sugar
