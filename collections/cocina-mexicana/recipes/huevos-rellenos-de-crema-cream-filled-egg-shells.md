## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- chocolate cream
- orange cream or coffee cream
- flour
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Make a small hole at both ends of twelve eggs.
2. Introduce a straw through one hole to break the yolk.
3. Blow through one end to empty the eggshells.
4. Rinse the shells in water until completely clean.
5. Let drain and dry in air.
6. Plug one of the two holes with flour mixed with egg yolk.
7. When dry, fill six shells with chocolate cream using a small funnel.
8. Fill the other six shells with orange or coffee cream.
9. Plug all holes and cook the eggs in hot water without letting them boil.
10. Remove the paste from both ends that plugged the holes and dry the eggs.
11. Serve as an intermediate course on a folded napkin.
