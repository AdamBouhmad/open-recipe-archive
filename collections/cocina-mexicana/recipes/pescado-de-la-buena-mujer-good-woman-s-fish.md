## Ingredients

- fish, cleaned and cut
- lard
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the fish, cook it in lard with onions cut into pieces.
2. When the fish is cooked, add ground salt and pepper, oregano and a little vinegar.
