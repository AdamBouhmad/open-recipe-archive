## Ingredients

- green beans
- butter
- onion
- flour
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Sauté chopped onion in butter
2. Dust with flour
3. Add a little broth
4. Reduce the sauce
5. Add the green beans, which have been pre-cooked with salt
