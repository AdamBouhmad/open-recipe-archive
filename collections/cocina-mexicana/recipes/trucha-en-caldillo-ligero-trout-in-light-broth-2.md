## Ingredients

- trout
- onion slices
- clove
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Gut the trout without opening the belly
2. Wash and dry very well
3. Tie the head with a string and place in a fish pan
4. Add onion slices, four cloves, four bay leaves, some sprigs of thyme, a handful of parsley in branches, and salt
