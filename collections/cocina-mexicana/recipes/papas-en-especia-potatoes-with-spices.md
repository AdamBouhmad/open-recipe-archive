## Ingredients

- potatoes
- tomatoes
- bread
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- green chiles
- parsley
- salt
- shrimp or cooked pork scraps
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- saffron

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes and slice them
2. Grind a little tomato and soaked bread
3. Put a pot with lard on the fire and fry some chopped garlic in it and then the ground tomato
4. Add two or three onions divided into quarters, whole green chiles and a little chopped parsley
5. Add the necessary fine salt and then add the chopped potatoes and some shrimp or cooked pieces of pork meat with the water in which they were cooked
6. Let them boil on high heat
7. When the broth has thickened a little, add clove and pepper with a few bits of cumin and saffron, all ground
8. As soon as the broth is well seasoned and thickened, they can be served at the table
