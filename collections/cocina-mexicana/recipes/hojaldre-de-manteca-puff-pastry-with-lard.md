## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one quarter of an ounce of salt
- one egg (white and yolk)
- four ounces of lard
- lard up to one pound total, half-melted for gilding the dough

## Directions

1. For one pound of flour, add one quarter of an ounce of salt, one egg (white and yolk), four ounces of lard, and the rest up to one pound of lard, half-melted, to gild the dough as it becomes absorbed
2. Follow the same procedures as for puff pastry with oil (see above)
