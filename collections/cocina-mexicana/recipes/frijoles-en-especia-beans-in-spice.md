## Ingredients

- Beans that are neither black nor white
- Water with tequisquite or without it
- Lard
- Ripe tomatoes, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- Pork meat, in pieces
- Broth from the same in which the meat was cooked
- Ground clove
- Ground black pepper
- Fine salt
- Little chiles in vinegar (for serving)
- Olives (for serving)

## Directions

1. Cook beans that are neither black nor white in water with tequisquite or without it.
2. Heat a pot with lard over the fire.
3. Chop plenty of ripe tomatoes, garlic, and onions.
4. Fry them in the lard, and then add the beans to fry with the tomato.
5. Once fried, add pork meat in pieces and the broth from the same in which the meat was cooked.
6. Season with ground clove and pepper, and with the necessary fine salt.
7. Let them boil until they thicken.
8. When serving, add little chiles in vinegar and olives.
