## Ingredients

- crumbs of cold bread
- powdered sugar
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the crumbs of cold bread into small thin squares
2. Sprinkle them with powdered sugar
3. Place them on a grill or under a camp oven until they are lightly browned
4. Boil the milk and thicken it with egg yolks as described in the milk soup article
5. Pour it over the bread
