## Ingredients

- chicken in quarters
- lard
- ham
- raisins
- almonds
- sesame seeds
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- cilantro
- garlic
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- olives
- pequin chiles

## Directions

1. Grease a casserole with lard and make layers of chicken quarters alternating with ham, raisins, almonds, and sesame seeds until the casserole is full
2. On top sprinkle powdered cloves, cinnamon, pepper, cumin, toasted cilantro, and garlic cloves
3. Add wine and vinegar with little water
4. Cook and garnish with olives and pequin chiles
