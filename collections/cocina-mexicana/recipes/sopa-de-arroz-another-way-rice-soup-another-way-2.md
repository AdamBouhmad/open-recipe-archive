## Ingredients

- rice
- lard (manteca)
- onion
- tomato (jitomate)
- broth from boiled meat (caldo del cocido)

## Directions

1. Sauté the rice with lard, onion, and a little tomato
2. Add the broth from boiled meat (or water for fasting days)
3. The rice should cook in one hour
4. Move it aside a little before it is fully cooked
5. Serve it a quarter hour later
