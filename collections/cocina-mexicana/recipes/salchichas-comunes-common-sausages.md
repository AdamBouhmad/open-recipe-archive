## Ingredients

- equal parts lean pork and fat pork, finely chopped
- vinegar
- pepper
- ginger
- ground fennel
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop equal quantities of lean and fat pork very finely
2. Add vinegar, which if very strong may be tempered with a little water
3. Add pepper, ginger, ground fennel and sufficient salt
4. Fill thin pork intestines and tie at intervals of one quarter
5. Bring salted water to a boil
6. Once boiling, add the sausages
7. When the intestine is cooked, remove and hang in the air to dry
