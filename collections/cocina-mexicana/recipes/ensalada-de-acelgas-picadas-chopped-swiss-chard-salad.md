## Ingredients

- Swiss chard
- oil or lard
- ground garlic
- cooked onions
- oregano

## Directions

1. After cooking the Swiss chard, drain and chop it
2. Render in oil or lard with ground garlic
3. Add cooked onions and oregano powder
4. Season like other salads
