## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- Bread
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Bring the kidneys to a boil and once they have boiled for a while, drain the water and add fresh hot water
2. Let them cook and then slice them
3. Fry slices of bread and grind with three cloves, three peppercorns and a little cinnamon
4. Fry this in lard
5. Add water to the mixture and then the kidneys, salt and vinegar
6. Let cook
