## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Salt
- Syrup
- Orange flower water

## Directions

1. Clean and cook with not much salt
2. Remove to cold water and change it daily until they completely lose their bitterness
3. Put in syrup, boil and remove
4. Do this same process for three days and on the last day add orange flower water
