## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- one ounce of well-ground sugar
- six egg yolks
- cornmeal flour
- one ounce of melted lard

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites with the well-ground sugar
2. When the foam has risen well, mix in the six egg yolks which are also beaten
3. Mix in the cornmeal flour and melted lard
4. Knead everything well, increasing the flour until the dough reaches a regular consistency
5. Form the biscuits
6. Put them on papers and bake in the oven
