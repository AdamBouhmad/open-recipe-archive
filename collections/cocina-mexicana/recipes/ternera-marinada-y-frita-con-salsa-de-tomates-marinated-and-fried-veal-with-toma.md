## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Good vinegar
- Garlic heads
- Oregano
- Pepper
- Clove
- Bay leaf
- Avocado leaves
- Water
- Salt
- Flour
- Oil
- Lard
- Tomatoes
- Wide chiles
- Veal cooking broth
- Dry oregano
- Raw oil
- Boiled egg slices
- Small chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Slice the veal loin from the day before and place in a pot with sufficient good vinegar, two or three crushed garlic heads, a little oregano, more pepper than clove, a little bay leaf and avocado leaves
2. The next day add water and necessary salt, and place to cook with the pot well covered
3. When cooked, set aside from the fire and drain the broth well
4. Roll in flour and fry in oil with a little lard until golden
5. Arrange the slices on the platter and bathe with the following sauce
6. Chop tomatoes well and fry in oil, being careful not to break them with the spoon, with wide chiles deveined, well soaked and chopped also
7. Moisten with a little of the broth in which the veal cooked, and with the hot sauce pour over the fried slices
8. Sprinkle with dry oregano and drizzle with raw oil
9. Garnish on top with slices of boiled egg, small chiles, and olives
