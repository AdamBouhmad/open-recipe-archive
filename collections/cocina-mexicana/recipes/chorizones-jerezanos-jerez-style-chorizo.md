## Ingredients

- three pounds of pork loin cut into small pieces
- two pounds of pork fat
- twenty wide chiles
- two bundles of peeled garlic
- cinnamon
- cloves
- pepper
- toasted anise
- Castilian vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the loin and fat into small pieces and place in a dish
2. Grind twenty wide chiles and two bundles of peeled garlic
3. Add to the meat and separately add in powder form: cinnamon, cloves, pepper, toasted anise, and Castilian vinegar
4. Mix everything together
5. Leave in infusion for three days, stirring frequently
6. After three days, fry a little and taste to add what is missing
7. Fill the intestines and before tying them, prick with a pin so air escapes
8. Hang over a brazier so they receive heat and a little smoke
9. They should be there for five days, then hang them in a cool place
