## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Potatoes, fried
- Bread, grated
- Salt
- Pepper, ground
- Flour
- Beaten egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as previously described
2. Fill with fried potatoes mixed with grated bread, salt and ground pepper
3. Coat with flour and dip in beaten egg
4. Fry the chiles
5. Prepare the sauce the same way as in previous recipes
