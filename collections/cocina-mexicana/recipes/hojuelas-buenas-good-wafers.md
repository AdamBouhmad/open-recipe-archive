## Ingredients

- two pounds of fine flour
- a little lard
- two eggs
- a lump of sugar
- one squeezed orange
- some grains of salt

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of fine flour with a little lard, two eggs, a lump of sugar, one squeezed orange, and some grains of salt
2. Stir everything together and knead until smooth
3. Then the wafers can be made and they will turn out good
