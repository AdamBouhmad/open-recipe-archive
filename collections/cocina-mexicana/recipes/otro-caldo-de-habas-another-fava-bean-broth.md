## Ingredients

- fava beans
- chickpeas
- lard
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- spearmint
- chipotle pepper
- eating oil

## Directions

1. Peel the fava beans raw and wash them
2. Place them in a pot with sufficient water so there is no need to add more, as this would break them down
3. Add chickpeas, lard, and salt at the same time
4. Let them boil; when they are half cooked, add garlic, onion, spearmint, and a raw chipotle pepper
5. Alternatively, fry the chipotle in lard, cut it into small pieces so each person can serve themselves according to their taste
6. Add a little eating oil
