## Ingredients

- Two pounds of beef stomach, prepared
- Butter
- A piece of beef stomach, uncooked, as thick as the fist
- Two garlic heads
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Coarse salt
- One good spoonful of flour
- Three glasses of water
- Three spoonfuls of vinegar
- One garlic head

## Directions

1. Brown two pounds of prepared beef stomach in butter over the fire and set aside
2. Finely chop together a piece of uncooked beef stomach as thick as the fist, two garlic heads, parsley, butter, pepper, and coarse salt
3. Stuff the main piece with this mixture, sewing it closed all around
4. Make a base with one good spoonful of flour, three glasses of water, three spoonfuls of vinegar, and one garlic head
5. Bring to a boil for a quarter hour, then place the stuffed stomach in it
6. Cook for a long time over gentle heat, being careful that it does not come apart
