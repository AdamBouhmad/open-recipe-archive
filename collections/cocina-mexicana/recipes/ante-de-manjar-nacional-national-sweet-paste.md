## Ingredients

- four pounds of white sugar
- two egg yolks
- two chicken breasts, ground
- three pounds of almonds, ground

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half point with four pounds of white sugar
2. Add two egg yolks, two ground chicken breasts, three pounds of ground almonds
