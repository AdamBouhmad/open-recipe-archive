## Ingredients

- Cooked ham, sliced
- Carrots
- Onions
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Thyme
- Laurel
- White wine
- Concentrated broth
- Spinach
- Butter
- Spanish sauce
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Render carrots, onions, parsley, garlic, thyme and laurel in butter.
2. Moisten everything with white wine and concentrated broth.
3. When the roots are almost cooked, pass the broth through a sieve, add the ham to it and cook over two gentle fires for two hours.
4. Prepare the spinach separately by blanching it, frying it in butter and moistening it with a little of the ham broth and some Spanish sauce.
5. Add a little grated nutmeg, salt and coarse pepper.
6. Arrange the spinach on the serving plate, place the ham on top and brown it immediately.
