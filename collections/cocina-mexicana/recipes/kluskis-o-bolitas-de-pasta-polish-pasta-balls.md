## Ingredients

- flour
- warm water
- eggs, lightly poached
- beer foam
- salt
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a somewhat thick batter with flour, warm water and lightly poached eggs
2. Add beer foam, salt and sugar
3. Let rest in a warm place to rise by fermentation
4. Pinch off small pieces with fingers to form balls
5. Let the balls rest again on the table to puff up further
6. Drop into boiling salted water, where they will puff up even more
7. Once all are cooked, brown them in hot melted butter
8. Serve
