## Ingredients

- One beef head
- Beef brains
- Veal pulp
- Beef fat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onion
- Half a bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Two spoonfuls of spirits
- Three egg yolks
- Three egg whites
- Beef tongue
- Beef cheeks
- Beef eyes

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the head, clean and blanch it, being careful not to cut it, and debone it to separate the brains, tongue, eyes, and cheeks
2. With these brains, veal pulp, and beef fat, make a fine forcemeat seasoned with salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley, onion, half a bay leaf, thyme, and basil until they are like powder
3. Add two spoonfuls of spirits and bind the stuffing with three egg yolks unbeatten and three beaten egg whites
4. After parboiling in boiling water, clean very well the tongue, cheeks, and eyes, removing all the dark parts, and cut into strips or dice, mixing with the stuffing
5. Place the unparboiled skin in a pan, leaving the ears on top, and fill with the aforementioned forcemeat
6. Then sew it closed by folding or pleating it like a bag and tie it all around
