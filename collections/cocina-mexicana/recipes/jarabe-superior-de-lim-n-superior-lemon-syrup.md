## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of filtered and purified lemon juice, freshly expressed
- two pounds of white sugar, crushed
- sufficient quantity of lemon spirit

## Directions

1. Place one cuartillo of filtered and well-purified expressed lemon juice in a silver, thick glass, porcelain, or earthenware vessel with two pounds of crushed white sugar
2. Place the vessel over very gentle heat, stirring continuously until the sugar is dissolved
3. Remove from heat, let cool, pass through cheesecloth and aromatize with sufficient quantity of lemon spirit
4. Preserve in dry bottles well sealed, keeping away from heat and light
