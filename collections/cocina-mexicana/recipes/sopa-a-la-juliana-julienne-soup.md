## Ingredients

- Equal quantity of carrot, celery, lettuce, sorrel, peas, and tender beans
- Butter
- Slices of onion
- Broth from the pot
- Thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of carrot, celery, lettuce, sorrel, peas, and tender beans
2. Sauté them in butter with some slices of onion
3. Add broth from the pot afterwards
4. Cook at low heat
5. Pour over thin slices of bread
