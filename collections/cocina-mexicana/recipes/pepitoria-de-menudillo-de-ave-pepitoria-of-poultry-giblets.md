## Ingredients

- Poultry wings
- Sweetbreads
- Crests
- Necks
- Livers
- Butter
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Parsley
- Hazelnut sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a pepitoria with the wings, sweetbreads, crests, necks, and livers of poultry by frying them in butter with onion and tomatoes.
2. Pound garlic, pepper, salt, cloves, cinnamon, and parsley.
3. When cooked and the sauce has reduced, add the hazelnut sauce, which will darken the dish.
4. Add lemon juice.
