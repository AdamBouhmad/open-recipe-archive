## Ingredients

- half a cuartillo of milk
- one tablespoon flour
- three egg yolks
- a grain of salt
- two ounces of clarified butter
- powdered sugar to equal the butter
- chocolate powder or vanilla, or three ounces of roasted coffee beans

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil and place in a saucepan with flour, egg yolks, and salt
2. Dissolve everything by moistening it a little with cream
3. Place this mixture over the fire, stirring until it reaches a suitable consistency
4. Remove from the fire and add two ounces of clarified butter and the same amount of powdered sugar
5. Return to the fire and stir until the cream has taken body
6. Add perfume as desired: two ounces of chocolate powder or a little vanilla powder
7. If making coffee flavor, steep three ounces of roasted coffee beans in the boiling milk, cover, and when the infusion is lukewarm, strain it, then proceed as stated
8. This cream is intended to garnish cakes for desserts
