## Ingredients

- Zucchini, chopped
- Onion
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Green chile
- Corn kernels
- Salt
- Milk
- Butter
- Sausage slices
- Longaniza
- Small sausages
- Pork loin slices
- Pork ribs, cooked
- Lard
- Fresh cheese, crumbled
- Aged cheese slices
- Fried pork loin
- Roasted peppers, peeled and shredded
- Avocado quarters

## Directions

1. Chop zucchini as mentioned previously, place in a pot with onion, garlic, tomato, and green chile, all chopped, corn kernels, and salt
2. Add milk instead of water and butter instead of lard, letting it cook over a fire so it does not burn
3. In a separate casserole fry with lard slices of sausage, pieces of longaniza, small sausages, slices of pork loin and small ribs of pork, all cooked
4. When these items are well fried, add the cooked zucchini stew, and mixing with crumbled fresh cheese, let it come to a boil
5. When serving, garnish on top with slices of cheese and fried pork loin and roasted peppers, peeled and shredded, and quarters of avocado
