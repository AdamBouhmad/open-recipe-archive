## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one pound of butter
- four egg yolks
- a little toasted and ground almond

## Directions

1. Heat one pound of sugar, add one pound of butter, and as soon as it cools, mix it with four egg yolks
2. Return to the heat so that the yolks cook, then add a little toasted and ground almond
3. Layer this paste alternately with sponge cake (mamón), and if desired, the last layer can be fresh cheese (requesón) with flavoring pastille
4. Put over two fires so that it sets
