## Ingredients

- raw garlic
- oil
- cumin
- pepper
- salt
- bread
- fresh water
- vinegar
- cucumber, cucumber slices
- tomato slices
- onion slices

## Directions

1. Rub a soup bowl with raw garlic
2. Add oil, cumin, pepper and salt and beat until well mixed
3. Place bread broken into round or square pieces
4. After stirring it in the soup bowl, add fresh water
5. Let it sit a while until the bread swells
6. At the moment of serving, add vinegar so the bread does not sour
7. Some slices of cucumber, tomato, pepper and onion can be added
