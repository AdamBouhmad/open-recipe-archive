## Ingredients

- beef sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- olives
- jelly or sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as in the English-style beefsteak article
2. At the moment of serving, add the beefsteak to small pieces of pitted olives or what amounts to the same thing, pitted olives
3. Fry making it jump and arranged on a plate pour over it the jelly or reduced sauce with the olives
