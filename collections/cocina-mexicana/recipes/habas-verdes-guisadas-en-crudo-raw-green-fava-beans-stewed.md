## Ingredients

- Green fava beans, raw
- Lard
- Tomato, roasted and ground
- Onion
- Garlic
- Green chile

## Directions

1. Fry the raw green fava beans in lard
2. While fried, add roasted and ground tomato mixed with onion, garlic, and green chile
