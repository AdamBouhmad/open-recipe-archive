## Ingredients

- large pears, neither green nor overripe
- 2 pounds of pear
- 1 pound of white ground sugar
- musk flavoring (olor de almistle)

## Directions

1. Cook the pears over a wood fire with enough boiling water to cover them completely
2. Once they are tender, peel them and cut into thin slices without seeds
3. Place in a earthenware bowl and weigh 2 pounds of pear and 1 pound of white ground sugar
4. Place on the fire with a good wood fire, stirring with a stick
5. When a little white film appears on the bottom of the pan and it looks as if it is frying around the edges, it is done
6. Remove from heat and add musk flavoring
