## Ingredients

- membrillo (quince)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Cut the quince in half, remove the core, cook it and strain through a sieve
2. Make clarified syrup at the highest point possible, with one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of quince
3. Place the quince there and let it reach the point of wrinkles
4. Remove from heat, beat slightly, and pour into glasses or small boxes
