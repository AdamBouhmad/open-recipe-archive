## Ingredients

- seven to eight beef ears
- ham slices
- pan essence
- butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Soak and blanch seven to eight beef ears, parboil them, refresh, pass through flames
2. Make some cuts and trim the upper part to the middle
3. In a pot bottom, place ham slices and arrange ears on top
4. Cover ears with more ham slices
5. Add a pan-full of pan essence
6. Cover inside the pot with buttered paper
7. Cook ears on low heat for two and a half hours
8. Drain after cooking and arrange on a plate
9. Pour Italian sauce over top
