## Ingredients

- fish roe
- water
- salt
- cloth
- oil
- lard
- garlic
- bread
- pepper
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place the roe in a cloth to desalt it
2. Once the water feels not very salty, drain it until no more water comes out
3. Dry it in a clean cloth and fry it in a large pan so it can be turned without breaking
4. Once fried and removed from the pan, take a piece and grind it with some fried garlic cloves and a piece of fried bread
5. Before grinding it, fry it in lard and oil with some peppers
6. In the same pan where the roe was fried, fry this mixture adding more oil
7. Once seasoned, without adding water, place the roe on a platter and coat it with this sauce
8. Add sliced onion and chopped parsley
