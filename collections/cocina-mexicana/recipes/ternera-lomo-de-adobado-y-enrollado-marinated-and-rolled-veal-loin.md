## Ingredients

- Veal loin, sliced thin
- Wide dried chiles, soaked and ground
- Garlic
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Raisins
- Peeled and chopped almonds
- Capers
- Chopped parsley
- Oregano
- Wine
- Water
- Lard
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Chili peppers in vinegar
- Olives

## Directions

1. Extend the thinly sliced veal loin and spread it with an adobo made by grinding soaked wide dried chiles with garlic, black pepper, cloves, and cinnamon, seasoning the ground mixture with salt and diluting it with vinegar
2. Place on top a good amount of raisins, peeled and chopped almonds, capers, chopped parsley, and oregano
3. Roll tightly and tie securely with twine
4. Cook in a deep, narrow pot with wine, water, salt, lard, thyme, and bay leaves, making sure it does not run out of water so it does not burn
5. Once cooked, remove from heat, untie, and leave over two fires until the broth is completely consumed
6. Serve whole or in slices, pouring over the adobo that remains in the pot, and garnish with chili peppers, olives, and capers
