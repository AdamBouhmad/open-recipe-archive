## Ingredients

- one quarter of flour
- twenty ounces of good butter
- about one ounce of salt
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Place the flour on a very clean table and make a hole in the middle
2. Put salt, butter in small pieces, and necessary water in the hole
3. Knead the butter well with the water
4. Gradually add the flour in small quantities
5. When the flour has absorbed all the water, knead vigorously with strength
6. The dough does not need to be very hard as long as it is well bound
7. Prepare at least two hours before use to allow time for fermentation
