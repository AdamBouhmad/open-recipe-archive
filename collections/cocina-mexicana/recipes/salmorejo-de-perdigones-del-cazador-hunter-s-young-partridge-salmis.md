## Ingredients

- Young partridges, roasted
- Oil
- Wine
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the young partridges and break them apart as mentioned before
2. Place their pieces in a casserole with a little oil and the same amount of wine, with salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Make them jump in this sauce
4. Serve
