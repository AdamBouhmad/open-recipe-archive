## Ingredients

- tripe
- lime water
- lemon
- pork feet
- tripe
- chickpeas
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- green chiles
- ancho chile
- jitomates
- cooked chickpeas
- oregano
- chorizo
- ham
- longaniza
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe well with lime water and rinse with squeezed lemons so it is whiter and the odor dissipates
2. Place to cook adding pork feet and tripe, all very clean
3. Cook the feet separately
4. Also cook chickpeas in regular quantity and peel them
5. Fry plenty of onion and garlic in good quantity of butter with green chiles
6. Grind a piece of ancho chile with some comino, roasted jitomates and a few cooked chickpeas
7. Add to the fried mixture the pork feet with their broth, the tripe and offal without water, after having cut them into pieces for cooking
8. Add Castilian oregano, whole chickpeas, chorizo, ham, longaniza and salt, and let everything cook together
