## Ingredients

- turkey blood
- seasoning mixture for blood sausages of recado
- salt
- onion
- garlic
- xitomate
- parsley
- mint
- thyme
- oregano
- lime peel

## Directions

1. Prepare a bowl with the same seasoning as for blood sausages of recado, but in a lesser quantity proportionate to the blood of a turkey
2. For the turkey, pull the head from the neck in one motion by hanging it, or if there is not enough strength or skill, hold it by the feet with the left hand
3. If the turkey was not hanging, hang it immediately after separating the head
4. Pluck and lightly strip the skin near the head and remove the head so the blood runs into the bowl of seasoning that has been carefully positioned at the convenient point
5. Mix it so that everything becomes well incorporated
6. When no more blood comes out, pluck the entire skin of the neck and, separated from the body, fill it with the seasoned blood
7. Tie at the ends and cook in boiling water
