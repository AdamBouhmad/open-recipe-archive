## Ingredients

- rabbit
- garlic
- tomato
- onion
- pepper
- saffron
- toasted bread or rice
- oil
- vinegar
- mint leaves

## Directions

1. After cooking, divide the rabbit into small pieces and fry with clean mashed garlic
2. Add chopped tomato and onion with ground pepper and saffron
3. Once this is well fried, add toasted and ground bread or rice
4. Add a little oil and the same amount of vinegar
5. Add some mint leaves
6. Let boil a little over strong fire and remove from heat
