## Ingredients

- Walnuts, gathered at the end of May or beginning of June when soft
- Clarified syrup, less than half stage
- Nutmeg
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon, a small amount

## Directions

1. Gather walnuts at the end of May or beginning of June when they are soft
2. Pierce with a basting needle to verify they are soft
3. Cook with a small amount of straw on top
4. Gently peel and place in water, changing it daily until the bitterness is gone and the astringent quality has been removed, which can be tested through the small holes
5. Place in clarified syrup at less than half stage and bring to a boil over very high heat
6. The next day, place over heat again, and on the third day, also over heat, add nutmeg, clove, pepper, and a small amount of cinnamon, but do not allow to boil
7. Allow to cool and store in jars so they do not crack
