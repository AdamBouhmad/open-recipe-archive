## Ingredients

- clams
- 1 head of garlic
- a little parsley
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the clams well and put them in a pot with 1 head of garlic and a little parsley
2. Place over high heat, shaking them from time to time until they are open, then remove the shells
3. Reserve and clarify the water they release
4. Make a butter sauce and add the clams to it
5. Keep well heated until serving time
