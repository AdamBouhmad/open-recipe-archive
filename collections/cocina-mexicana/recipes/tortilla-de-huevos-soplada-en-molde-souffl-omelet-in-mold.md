## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three tablespoons fine powdered sugar
- one tablespoon potato starch
- four crushed macaroons
- a little salt
- a little candied orange blossom in powder

## Directions

1. Crack the six eggs, separating the whites from the yolks
2. Add three tablespoons fine powdered sugar to the yolks, one tablespoon potato starch, four crushed macaroons, a little salt and a little candied orange blossom in powder
3. Mix and stir everything together
4. Grease and bread the mold as for the rice cake (see rice cake, page 370)
5. Beat the egg whites when the entries are about to be served
6. Mix them then with the yolks and pour everything into the mold, without filling it completely
7. Put the omelet into a moderately hot oven
8. When cooked, turn it over onto a plate and serve
9. It should be of a beautiful color and movable or trembling
