## Ingredients

- One pound and four ounces of sugar
- six pitayas
- one pound of sweet potato

## Directions

1. Prepare one pound and four ounces of sugar at medium stage syrup
2. Prepare one pound of sweet potato in the manner previously described
3. Mix the prepared ingredients in the sugar syrup at medium stage
4. Strain the pitaya if desired, as the seeds do not look bad
5. Mix this pitaya juice with the sweet potato
6. To prevent it from being too green, add a small piece of brown sugar
