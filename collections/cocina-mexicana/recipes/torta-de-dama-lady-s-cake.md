## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- one pound almonds
- orange blossom water
- seven eggs
- butter or lard
- sesame seed tablets (pastillas de ajonjolí)

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of sugar and one pound of almonds together
2. While grinding, gradually add orange blossom water
3. Beat seven eggs with their whites together with the almonds and sugar
4. Grease a pan generously with butter or lard
5. Cook between two fires or in the oven
6. Top with sesame seed tablets
