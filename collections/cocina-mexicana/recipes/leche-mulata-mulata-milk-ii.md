## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- one and one-half pocillos of rice
- half pound of almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak one and one-half pocillos of rice for eight quartillos of milk
2. Wash and mix it; stir everything in a pan and place over heat
3. Clean half pound of almonds, roast in a pan and grind finely
4. Strain with the milk and add to the pan
5. Grind the strainings again until nothing remains
6. Cook to the consistency of manjar blanco (white delicacy)
7. Pour into dishes
8. Top with roasted almonds broken into pieces
