## Ingredients

- Sea barbel fish
- Oil
- Salt
- Whole black pepper
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove the scales, gut and wash the fish
2. After draining well and drying, open it along the back
3. Place in a marinade of oil with salt and whole black pepper
4. Place on the grill, being careful that it does not stick or burn, basting it with oil and turning it frequently, ensuring it takes on a good color on the white side
5. Once well cooked, arrange on a plate, garnishing with slices of pitted lemon and serving with oil sauce
