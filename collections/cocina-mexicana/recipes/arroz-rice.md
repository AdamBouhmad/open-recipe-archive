## Ingredients

- rice
- costillas (ribs)
- salt
- oil
- garlic
- parsley
- tomato
- onion
- pepper
- ham
- water
- clove

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with salt, then fry them in oil with garlic, parsley, tomato, a little onion, pepper and ham.
2. Rub the dry rice in a cloth to remove dust and add it to the pan, stirring.
3. Add water, keeping in mind that each cup of rice absorbs about two and a half cups of water.
4. Add a pinch of pepper and clove.
5. Leave over the fire until the rice is cooked and each grain is whole.
6. Remove from the fire and cover with a lid with embers.
