## Ingredients

- onion quarters
- water
- salt
- wide chile, deveined, soaked and ground raw
- fish portions
- cloves, ground
- cinnamon, ground
- pepper, ground
- garlic, ground
- wide chiles, toasted, deveined and ground
- carrot pieces
- capers
- oregano
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry onion quarters
2. Add water, salt and raw ground chile
3. Add fish portions with ground cloves, cinnamon, pepper and garlic
4. Simmer and add toasted, deveined and ground wide chiles
5. Add carrot pieces and capers
6. Sprinkle oregano on top
7. When removed from heat, add olives and oil
