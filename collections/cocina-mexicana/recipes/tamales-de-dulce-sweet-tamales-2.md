## Ingredients

- nixtamal (corn kernels)
- powdered sugar
- lard
- manjar blanco (milk and rice-based filling)

## Directions

1. Boil and wash the nixtamal, grind it dry and sift through an ayate (fiber cloth)
2. Grind the sugar to powder
3. Put the masa in a casserole with the sugar and cold lard
4. Beat the mixture
5. Form the tamales and fill them with manjar blanco
6. Prepare the manjar blanco by boiling milk with sugar and ground, strained rice until it thickens
