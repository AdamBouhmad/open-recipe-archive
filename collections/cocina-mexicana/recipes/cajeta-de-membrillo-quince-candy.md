## Ingredients

- White quinces without blemishes or yellow spots
- one pound of syrup for each pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Select white quinces without blemishes or yellow spots
2. Cook them, peel and place in cold water after removing the cores
3. Desalt for two or three days so they do not lose all their tartness
4. Change the water so they do not turn red
5. Grind them and for each pound of fruit use one pound of syrup
6. Mix, place over heat and cook to a high stage so it has a good cut
