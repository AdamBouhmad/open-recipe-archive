## Ingredients

- Hens
- Water
- Salt
- Lard
- Chopped tomatillo
- Whole small headed onions
- Toasted ground cilantro
- Pepper
- Clove
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cook the hens in water, salt and lard
2. Once cooked, add chopped tomatillo and whole small headed onions, allowing them to brown over two fires
3. When they have taken on good color, add cilantro toasted and ground with pepper and clove
4. After removing from the heat, sprinkle with oil and vinegar and garnish with chiles and olives
