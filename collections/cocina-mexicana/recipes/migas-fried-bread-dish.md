## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- butter or oil
- bread, crumbled and soaked
- water
- salt
- anise seeds

## Directions

1. Fry some garlic cloves in butter or oil
2. Add crumbled and soaked bread with a little water
3. Season with salt and anise seeds
