## Ingredients

- pork meat, cooked and minced
- tomatoes, minced
- garlic
- chiles
- capers
- raisins
- almonds
- candied citron
- parsley, leaves removed
- eggs, beaten in proportion to the meat
- olives
- cured tornachilos
- vinegar
- saffron
- clove
- cinnamon, ground
- ham, in small pieces

## Directions

1. Cook and mince pork meat, and place it in a frying mixture composed of minced tomatoes, garlic, and chiles with capers, raisins, almonds, candied citron, and parsley with leaves removed.
2. Place beaten eggs in proportion to the meat there as well, adding olives, cured tornachilos, vinegar, saffron, clove, and ground cinnamon and ham in small pieces, leaving it on the fire until the mass is well cooked and bound.
3. Afterward, make some cartouches like cigar papers or larger, and fill them with the minced mixture explained, tying with twine and the paper well coated with lard on the same metate, adding salt and small pieces of ham.
4. Roast on the grill. Some tomatoes are roasted and ground, and fried in lard. Ground spices, capers, sweets, and fruit are added. In this broth or sauce are served the large meatballs, which wrapped in paper as said before, are placed to cook in a pot with plenty of lard, and when you know they are cooked, remove the paper and slice them.
5. Serve with tomato sauce or chile sauce.
