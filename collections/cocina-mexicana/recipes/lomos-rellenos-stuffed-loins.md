## Ingredients

- pork loin
- chard
- onion
- garlic
- tomato
- clove
- pepper
- lard
- oregano
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the loin and flatten well until very wide.
2. Cook chard and fry with onion, chopped garlic, tomato, clove and pepper; fill the loins with this and tie with twine.
3. Fry a whole tomato and some garlic cloves in lard and mix.
4. First fry the raw loin and when fried, add the mixture.
5. Add a little water and vinegar, oregano and salt, ensuring the broth is not too thick.
