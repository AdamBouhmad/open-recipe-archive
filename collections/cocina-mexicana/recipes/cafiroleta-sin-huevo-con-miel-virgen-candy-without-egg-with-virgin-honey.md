## Ingredients

- sugar
- toasted mamón
- toasted and pounded almonds
- virgin honey
- clove

## Directions

1. Make the syrup at a high point
2. Remove from the fire
3. Add broken toasted mamón, toasted and pounded almonds (so it has grain texture), virgin honey, and clove
