## Ingredients

- fowl
- salt
- onions
- garlic
- pepper
- cloves
- green chile
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut raw fowl and fry with salt
2. Cut onions into large pieces
3. Take a head of garlic with skin partially ground, a little pepper and cloves, all ground, and green chile in pieces, and add everything to the pan with the meat to fry
4. Once the fowl is fried, add vinegar and then sufficient water
5. The broth should remain thick
