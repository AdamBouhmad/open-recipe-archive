## Ingredients

- Liver
- broth
- white wine, half a glass
- parsley
- small onions
- garlic, one clove
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the bile from the liver
2. Blanch the whole liver with boiling water
3. Put broth, white wine, parsley, small onions, garlic, pepper and salt in a pot
4. Add the liver
5. Cook for a quarter of an hour
