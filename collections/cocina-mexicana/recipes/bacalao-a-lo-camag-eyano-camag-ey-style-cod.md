## Ingredients

- Cod
- Peppercorns
- Parsley
- Garlic cloves
- Toasted hazelnuts
- Breadcrumbs
- Vinegar
- Nutmeg
- Cold water
- Fried olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. After preparing as described, dry the cod and place it to cook
2. When cooked, drain well and place in a pot
3. Grind six peppercorns, a little parsley, six garlic cloves, eight toasted hazelnuts, breadcrumbs, a little vinegar and grated nutmeg
4. Dissolve everything with a cup of cold water
5. Pour into the cod pot, adding fried olive oil with some garlic, a few drops of lemon juice and necessary salt
6. Place on the fire to cook a little and serve
