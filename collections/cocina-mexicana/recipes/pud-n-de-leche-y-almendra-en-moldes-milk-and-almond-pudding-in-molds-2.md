## Ingredients

- Five quartillos of milk
- Four ounces of clean ground almonds
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Clove powder, according to taste
- Raisins
- Pine nuts
- Pieces of walnut
- Ten beaten eggs until firm
- Toasted and ground mamón in the quantity necessary to form the paste
- Butter for coating the mold

## Directions

1. Mix five quartillos of milk with four ounces of clean ground almonds, sugar, cinnamon, and clove powder according to taste
2. Add raisins, pine nuts, pieces of walnut, ten beaten eggs until firm, and toasted and ground mamón in the quantity necessary to form the paste
3. Mix everything well
4. Pour into a mold coated with butter without filling it completely
5. Cook as indicated in the previous articles
