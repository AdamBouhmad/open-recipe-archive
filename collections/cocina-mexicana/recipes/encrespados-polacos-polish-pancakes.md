## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk or cream
- fresh butter
- nutmeg
- lemon rind
- sugar
- salt
- flour
- Corinth raisins or muscatel raisins
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix and beat eight eggs with three-quarters of one cuartillo of milk or cream, two ounces of melted fresh butter, grated nutmeg, lemon rind grated with sugar, a little salt, and ten ounces of flour
2. Place butter or lard in the pan in the same quantity as for the previous pancakes and pour the batter in the same manner
3. As soon as the batter is poured, sprinkle Corinth raisins (or muscatel raisins if the other is unavailable) on it
4. Flip the pancake, let it cook, place it on a plate, dust with sugar, roll it with a carving fork with two long tines, and serve very hot
