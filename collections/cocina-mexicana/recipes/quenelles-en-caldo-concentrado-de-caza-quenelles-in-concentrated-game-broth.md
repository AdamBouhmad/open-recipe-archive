## Ingredients

- quenelles (prepared as for poultry)
- concentrated game broth
- egg (for clarifying)

## Directions

1. Form quenelles in spoons as with poultry quenelles
2. Do not poach them until the moment of serving
3. Clarify the concentrated game broth with an egg
4. Strain the broth through a napkin
5. Pour the boiling broth into a silver serving dish
6. Place the quenelles in the broth, removing them from the water in which they were poached
