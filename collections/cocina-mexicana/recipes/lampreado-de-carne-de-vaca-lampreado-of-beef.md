## Ingredients

- three pounds of beef
- one pound of dark sugar
- one quarter ounce of ground cinnamon
- one small glass of red wine
- good vinegar
- a few powdered spices
- raisins
- clean almonds
- pieces of citron
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- sesame
- lean and fatty ham
- a little toasted bread called alfajor
- biscuit

## Directions

1. Cut three pounds of beef into strips, press it for one night, place it in the sun on the following day and keep it in the sun another day; then leave it for two days in water, and after that cut it across.
2. Preserve in a syrup made with one pound of dark sugar, adding one quarter ounce of ground cinnamon, one small glass of red wine, good vinegar so that its flavor stands out with respect to the wine, a few powdered spices, raisins, clean almonds, pieces of citron, pine nuts, walnuts, sesame, lean and fatty ham and a little toasted bread that they call alfajor, crushed.
3. After removing it from the fire, add a few more powdered spices and crushed biscuit.
