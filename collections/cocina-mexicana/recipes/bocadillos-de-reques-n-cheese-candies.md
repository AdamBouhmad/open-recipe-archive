## Ingredients

- Requesón cheese
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Grind the requesón very well on a metate
2. Crush sugar in greater quantity than the requesón and place on the fire
3. When it reaches medium point, add the requesón
4. Let it boil until it pulls away from the pot
5. Pour into small boxes or on wafers
