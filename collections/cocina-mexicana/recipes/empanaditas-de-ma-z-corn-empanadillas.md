## Ingredients

- Corn
- Salt
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- A little saffron
- Ground meat filling or other filling
- Sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook corn as for tortillas
2. Wash it, removing all husks and trimming well
3. Grind again on the metate as for tortillas
4. Form small dough balls and place in boiling salted water
5. When cooked, grind very well again
6. Add salt, raw egg yolks, butter, and a little saffron
7. Once ground and well mixed, form the empanadillas
8. Fill with unsalted ground meat or other filling
9. Seal well at the edges
10. Fry as they are filled until golden
11. When the butter drains, dust with sugar and cinnamon
