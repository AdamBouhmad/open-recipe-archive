## Ingredients

- eggs
- egg yolks
- raisins
- almonds
- white wine
- sugar
- beaten egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook eggs in desired quantity and remove yolks and grind them
2. Mix with raisins and almonds cut into small pieces, sufficient white wine and ground sugar to sweeten
3. Form into long pieces like mamones (a type of sponge cake)
4. Coat with beaten egg and fry in lard
5. Place in syrup
