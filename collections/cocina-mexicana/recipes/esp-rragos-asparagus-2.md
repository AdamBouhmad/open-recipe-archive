## Ingredients

- tender asparagus
- salt
- water
- eggs
- fried oil
- garlic
- pepper
- spices
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the most tender asparagus in water and cook with salt in half or less water
2. Add eggs so they set without becoming hard
3. For serving, put on a platter with little water and pour over it fried oil with garlic, pepper, spices and a few drops of vinegar if desired
4. Asparagus can also be served simply cooked with salt, placed in bundles, accompanied with vinegar cruets
