## Ingredients

- trout
- eel strips
- anchovy strips
- fresh butter
- trout flesh
- mushrooms
- criadillas de tierra (truffle-like fungi)
- parsley
- small onions
- fresh butter
- fine herbs
- spices
- salt
- pepper
- crayfish ragout

## Directions

1. Lard the trout with strips of eels and anchovies
2. Assemble the pie and brush with fresh butter
3. Prepare a ragout with trout flesh, mushrooms, criadillas de tierra, parsley, small onions, fresh butter, fine herbs, spices, salt and pepper
4. Pour the ragout over the trout and cover with more butter, completing the operation in the usual manner
5. Bake the pie, remove excess fat after cooking, add a crayfish ragout, and serve hot
