## Ingredients

- five pounds of sugar
- three pounds of almonds
- two ounces of toasted bread
- one ounce of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with five pounds of sugar
2. Let it boil until it reaches the consistency of a preserve
3. Add three pounds of almonds, blanched in hot water, dried, roasted and coarsely ground
4. Let the syrup boil again until it thickens
5. Add two ounces of toasted and ground bread, bringing it to the point of cajeta, which consists in seeing the bottom of the pan when stirring
6. Remove from heat and mix in one ounce of ground cinnamon
7. Beat until it is about to set
8. Pour into tin boxes with wafers or into paper boxes, which can be colored with scalloped edges
