## Ingredients

- eight ounces of good chocolate, grated
- thirty ounces of milk
- ten ounces of custard
- ten ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix and bring to boil until they have the proper consistency: eight ounces of good grated chocolate, thirty ounces of milk, ten ounces of custard and ten ounces of sugar
2. Stir and agitate everything with a spatula
3. Pour into a bowl, allow to cool and freeze in the ice cream mold
