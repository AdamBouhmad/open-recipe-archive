## Ingredients

- Suckling pig loin
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Raw oil
- Lemon juice
- Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. If there remained any whole loin of suckling pig, prepare it by marinating it with salt, black pepper, oil, and the juice of a lemon.
2. At the moment of serving, place it on the grill over very strong fire, and bring it to the table with tartar sauce underneath.
