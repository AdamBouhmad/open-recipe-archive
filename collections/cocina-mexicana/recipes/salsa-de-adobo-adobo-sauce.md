## Ingredients

- pasilla chiles
- garlic head
- cumin
- bread soaked in vinegar
- lard
- water
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from the pasilla chiles, soak them, wash in different waters
2. Grind the chiles together with a peeled head of garlic, a handful of cumin, and a piece of bread soaked in vinegar
3. Fry everything in a little lard
4. Add water and a little cooking oil
5. Let it all boil until well seasoned
