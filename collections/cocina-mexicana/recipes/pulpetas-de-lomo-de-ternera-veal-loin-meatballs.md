## Ingredients

- veal loin meat
- vinegar
- ground pepper and salt
- ground garlic
- powder of bay leaves
- pork fat or thin slices of fatback
- boiling water
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- lard
- flour
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Prepare the meatballs from veal loin meat
2. Sprinkle them with vinegar and dust with ground pepper and salt
3. Spread ground garlic and powder of bay leaves on them
4. Place thin slices of pork fat or fatback on top of the meatballs
5. Add a little more vinegar and wrap the meatballs, tying them with strong thread
6. Heat water in a pot with garlic, a bay leaf, a little thyme and lard
7. Place the meatballs there to cook, covering the pot
8. When cooked and the broth has been consumed and they are well browned, remove from heat and serve coated with a sauce made from flour half-fried in butter or lard
