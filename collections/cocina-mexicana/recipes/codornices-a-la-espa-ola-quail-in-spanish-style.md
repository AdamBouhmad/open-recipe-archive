## Ingredients

- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- quail
- ham slices
- sartanada sauce
- white wine
- broth
- fried bread croutons
- Spanish sauce
- aspic

## Directions

1. Knead a piece of butter with lemon juice, salt and pepper, and fill the quail with it, which will have been emptied through the crop.
2. Tie the haunches of the quail with a needle and thick twine, leaving the legs free.
3. Line a pot with slices of ham, place the quail on top, and cover them also with slices of ham.
4. Pour a sartanada sauce dissolved with broth and white wine in equal quantities.
5. A little more than half an hour before serving, place over the fire.
6. When removing them, drain and untie the quail, placing them on fried bread croutons of their same size that will have been prepared.
7. Serve with Spanish sauce thinned out a bit, under which a small piece of aspic the size of half a walnut will have been placed.
