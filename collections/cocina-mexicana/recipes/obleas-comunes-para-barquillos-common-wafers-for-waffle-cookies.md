## Ingredients

- flour
- syrup at soft-crack stage or virgin honey
- water

## Directions

1. Dissolve a proportional quantity of flour in syrup at soft-crack stage or in virgin honey
2. Leave the batter liquid so that when poured into the center of the mold, it spreads when the two hot iron plates close
3. After cooking, the batter should be as thin as an ordinary wafer with figures embossed in relief from the mold
4. Remove the waffle iron from the heat, open it, take out the wafer, and shape it into a horn or cone form
