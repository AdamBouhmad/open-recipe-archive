## Ingredients

- pork loin
- sage
- vinegar
- clove spices
- lemon rind
- defatted broth
- salt
- pepper
- one or two onions
- some carrots

## Directions

1. Remove the fat covering the loin, leaving only a small layer.
2. Roll the meat and cover with sage branches, tying firmly with string.
3. Place the meat in vinegar for two or three days with some cloves and lemon rind.
4. Remove from vinegar and place in a pot with half of the vinegar it was soaked in, one cup of defatted broth, salt and pepper, one or two onions and some carrots cut into pieces.
5. Cook for two or three hours over brisk heat at first, then moderate.
6. Place on a platter and serve with the defatted cooking sauce.
