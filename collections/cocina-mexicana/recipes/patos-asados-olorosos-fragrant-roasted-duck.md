## Ingredients

- Raw duck
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Water
- Marjoram
- Thyme
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Lettuce leaves
- Olives
- Chiles
- Celery

## Directions

1. Divide raw ducks into quarters and fry in lard
2. Once golden, add vinegar and water with sprigs of marjoram and thyme, salt, pepper, cloves, and ground cinnamon
3. Let cook until all the broth is consumed and the ducks are dry
4. Serve on lettuce leaves, garnished with olives, chiles, and celery
