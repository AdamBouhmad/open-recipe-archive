## Ingredients

- Three or four merlos (blackfish)
- Flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Select three or four of the best merlos
2. Scale them and cut off the extremity of the tail and gills
3. Empty and wash them
4. Return the liver to the body
5. Make slits on both sides
6. Cover with flour
7. Fry in butter until they take on good color
8. Drain immediately and dust with a little fine salt
9. Place a napkin on the serving plate and arrange the fish on top
