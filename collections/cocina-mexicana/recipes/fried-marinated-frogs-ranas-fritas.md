## Ingredients

- Raw frogs
- Vinegar
- Water
- Parsley
- Onion slices
- Garlic heads
- Whole onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Basil
- Flour
- Oil for frying

## Directions

1. Marinate raw frogs for one hour in equal parts vinegar and water with parsley, onion slices, garlic heads, whole onions, cloves, bay leaf, thyme, and basil
2. Drain the frogs
3. Roll them in flour
4. Fry them
5. Serve adorned with fried parsley
6. Alternatively, instead of flouring, soak them in a batter made of flour mixed with a spoonful of oil and a glass of white wine, seasoned with salt, until the batter reaches the right consistency to form a thick thread when poured
