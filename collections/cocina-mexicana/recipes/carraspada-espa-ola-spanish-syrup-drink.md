## Ingredients

- half a quarter of virgin honey
- half a quarter of water
- a little cinnamon powder
- a little clove powder
- one quarter of rough red wine or covered carlon wine

## Directions

1. Mix half a quarter of virgin honey with another half a quarter of water
2. While stirring, add a little cinnamon and clove powder
3. Mix this composition again, quarter by quarter, with rough red wine or covered carlon wine
