## Ingredients

- flour
- requesón (fresh cheese)
- butter
- salt
- water
- egg

## Directions

1. Take enough flour according to the desired size and mix with an equal quantity of requesón and as much butter as the other two things weigh
2. Add salt and water
3. Place the flour on a table and make a hollow or hole in the middle, putting water and butter there
4. Knead well tightly and then extend with a rolling pin
5. Put the crumbled requesón on top and fold into four folds
6. Extend and fold again, which is repeated several times
7. Form the gato, gild with egg, and put in the oven to cook
