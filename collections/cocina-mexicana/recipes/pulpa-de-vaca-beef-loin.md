## Ingredients

- beef loin, sliced
- lard
- onion
- chorizos
- ham
- garlic
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- cilantro
- water
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- bread fried in lard
- oil
- capers
- olives
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the loin
2. In a pot put a tablespoon of lard, onion, chorizos, ham and garlic
3. Chop the onion with parsley, oregano and thyme
4. Add green cilantro
5. Fry everything together
6. Add water, clove, pepper, cinnamon, and slices of bread fried in lard, all ground
7. Make sure it becomes thick for serving at the table
8. When eating, add a little oil, capers, olives and chiles in vinegar
