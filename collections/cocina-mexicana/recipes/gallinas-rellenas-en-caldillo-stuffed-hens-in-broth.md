## Ingredients

- Hens
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onion
- Tomato
- Green chile (chopped)
- Ham (pieces)
- Longaniza (pieces)
- Chorizo (pieces)
- Pork loin (pieces)
- Hen livers and gizzards
- Cooked egg
- Olives
- Cured chiles
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Capers
- Saffron
- Cloves
- Pepper (ground)
- Salt
- Roasted and ground garlic
- Roasted and ground tomato
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Cinnamon (ground)
- Málaga wine

## Directions

1. Cook the hens whole after cleaning.
2. Prepare the filling: fry garlic, onion, tomato, and chopped green chile in lard.
3. Add pieces of ham, longaniza, chorizo, pork loin, hen livers, gizzards, and cooked egg.
4. Add olives, cured chiles, almonds, raisins, and capers, all chopped.
5. Season with ground saffron, cloves, and pepper, and with salt.
6. Cook sufficiently and stuff the hens with this mixture.
7. Fry the stuffed hens in lard until golden, then remove.
8. Fry roasted and ground garlic and tomato in the same lard.
9. Add raisins, almonds, pine nuts, ground cloves, pepper, and abundant ground cinnamon.
10. Place the hens in this mixture and cover with Málaga wine and broth from the hens' cooking.
11. Let boil until the broth is well seasoned and of good consistency.
