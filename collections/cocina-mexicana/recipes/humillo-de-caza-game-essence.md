## Ingredients

- one bottle white wine
- four or five partridges or rabbits, or half of each
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- clove
- bay leaf
- concentrated broth or game essence
- a few tablespoons Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Put four or five partridges or rabbits, or half of each, in a pan with one bottle of white wine
2. Season with carrots, onions, thyme, clove and bay leaf
3. Let everything reduce until it becomes gelatine
4. Moisten then with concentrated broth, or better with game essence
5. Let everything boil until the game is cooked
6. Skim off fat immediately
7. Strain this essence through clean cloth
8. Add a few tablespoons of Spanish sauce
9. Use as needed
