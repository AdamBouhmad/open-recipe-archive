## Ingredients

- Unripe grapes
- Sugar, one quarter
- Water, half cuartillo

## Directions

1. Remove the small grains from the unripe grapes and place them in a sieve with one quarter of sugar and half cuartillo of water
2. Cook everything over low heat, and as soon as the grapes are very green and the syrup is thick, place it in the compote dish or platter
3. Serve cold
