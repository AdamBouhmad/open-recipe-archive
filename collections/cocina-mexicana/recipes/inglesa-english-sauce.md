## Ingredients

- two hard-boiled egg yolks
- anchovies
- capers
- one quarter of good broth
- salt
- whole pepper
- butter equal to half an egg
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Chop the two hard-boiled egg yolks and set half aside in a saucepan
2. Add chopped anchovies and capers to the saucepan
3. Add one quarter of good broth, a little salt, and whole pepper
4. Add butter equal to half an egg, kneaded with a little flour
5. Let the sauce bind over the fire
6. Pour the sauce over the desired dish
7. Sprinkle the remaining chopped egg yolks over the food
