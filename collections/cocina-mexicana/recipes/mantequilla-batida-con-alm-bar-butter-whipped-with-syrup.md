## Ingredients

- fresh firm butter
- high thread cold syrup

## Directions

1. Select fresh firm butter and clean it of all foreign matter
2. Put it in a pan with cold high thread syrup
3. Beat both together by hand, adding the syrup little by little and not all at once, in the quantity necessary for the butter to remain agreeably sweet
4. Continue beating with a spoon rather than by hand until it hardens well
5. Empty onto a moistened napkin prepared on a table
6. Place spoonfuls of the compound butter on the napkin, flattening and rounding them slightly with the spoon
7. Or extract with a pastry syringe, making letters or rings as the butter comes out
8. Leave on the table in a cool place, bringing out to the night air, frequently moistening the napkin so that water does not fall directly on the butter
9. Lift from the napkin with a knife and arrange on the serving platter in layers, decorated with cinnamon powder and dragée
10. Good for accompanying chocolate, but these butters melt easily and last little time if not kept in a cool place
