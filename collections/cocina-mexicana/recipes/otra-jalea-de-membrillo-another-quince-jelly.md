## Ingredients

- water
- quinces without skin or core, halved
- fourteen cuartillos of the resulting jelly
- 1 arroba of sugar (if the jelly is too watery)

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil and add the halved quinces without skin or core
2. After they are cooked, remove them from the heat and take them out of the pan with a wooden spoon
3. Know when they are done by touching them with your fingers; if they are already slimy, place them in a basket to drain the water
4. Strain the water through a sieve and measure fourteen cuartillos of this jelly
5. If it is too watery, add one arroba of sugar
6. Clarify everything together and strain
7. Place on the heat as soon as it begins to jump like marquesote
