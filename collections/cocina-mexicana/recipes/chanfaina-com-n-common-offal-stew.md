## Ingredients

- asadura (offal)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- hígado (liver)
- chile ancho (dried wide chile) desvenado y remojado
- clavo (cloves)
- pimienta fina (fine pepper)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion) rebanada
- ajos (garlic) picados
- caldo (broth)
- orégano (oregano)
- tomillo (thyme)
- chiles verdes (green chiles)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Remove fat from the offal, divide it and cook in water with salt
2. When cooked, remove from water and chop into small pieces
3. Grind a piece of liver together with a deveined and soaked wide chile and a little clove and fine pepper
4. Put a pot on the fire with a little lard and fry plenty of sliced onion, some chopped garlic and the ground liver with spices
5. Moisten with the broth in which the offal was cooked, add the offal and let it boil
6. Add oregano, thyme, some green chiles and vinegar
7. When the broth is seasoned well, it is ready to serve
