## Ingredients

- Teal ducks
- Ham, thick
- Turnips
- Lard
- Sustancia (meat essence)
- Strained broth

## Directions

1. Cook the teal ducks on a roasting spit or over coals larded with thick ham
2. Cut the turnips into the shape of dice or rounded like olives
3. Fry them in lard until they brown
4. Drain them and let them cook slowly in a good sustancia mixed with strained broth
5. When done, pour this stew over the teal ducks arranged on a platter
