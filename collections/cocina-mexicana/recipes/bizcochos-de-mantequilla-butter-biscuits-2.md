## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- six egg yolks
- powdered sugar to taste
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients until compact
2. Extend with a rolling pin
3. Make the biscuits
4. Put them in the oven
5. When removing them, sprinkle powdered sugar on top
