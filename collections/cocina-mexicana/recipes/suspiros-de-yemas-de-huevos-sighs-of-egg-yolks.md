## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- ground and sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks with ground and sifted sugar, stirring so that small figures or cakes can be made by hand
2. Let rest six to eight hours
3. After this time put in a moderate oven where they will cook and burst very quickly
