## Ingredients

- Celery
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Onion

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the celery and peel the stalk as with broccoli rabe
2. Divide into two or four parts lengthwise and cut into two-finger-sized pieces
3. Soak in cold water to blanch
4. After four to six hours, drain well
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and dress with oil and vinegar seasoned with sliced onion
