## Ingredients

- pig's head
- salt
- broth
- garlic
- onion
- chickpeas
- nixtamal
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break up the head and wash it thoroughly.
2. Put it to cook with salt, and as soon as it is tender, remove it and debone it, cutting into small pieces.
3. Let the broth in which it cooked settle; pour it into another vessel and add the meat with plenty of garlic, sliced onions, chickpeas, and a little nixtamal that has been trimmed, washed, and mashed, clove, cinnamon, and pepper.
