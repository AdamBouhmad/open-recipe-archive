## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes and slice them
2. Season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. Fry in butter and add cream instead of broth
4. Allow to thicken and remove from heat
