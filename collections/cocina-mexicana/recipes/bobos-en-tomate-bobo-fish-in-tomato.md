## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic, minced
- onions, minced
- tomatoes, minced
- all spices except saffron
- onions, cooked

## Directions

1. Fry in lard minced garlic, onions and tomatoes with all spices except saffron, and cook onions separately.
2. Coat a casserole with lard and place a layer of sliced bobo fish if they are large, or whole if small.
3. Add another layer of the fried mixture with spices, and another layer of cooked onions.
4. Add a little of the water in which the fish was cooked, and a bit of vinegar.
5. Place the casserole over two fires until the dish is seasoned and the broth has reduced.
6. Then add capers, olives, tornachiles in pieces and dry oregano.
