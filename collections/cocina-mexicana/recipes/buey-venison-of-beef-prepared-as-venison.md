## Ingredients

- Beef pulp
- Water
- Garlic cloves
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Ham
- Good vinegar
- Water
- Ground black pepper
- Chinese oregano
- Avocado leaves
- Rosemary
- Bay leaves
- Marjoram
- Fine salt
- Olives
- Turnips
- Onion slices
- Bitter orange slices
- Lettuce hearts
- Raw oil (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the beef pulps and slice them thickly across the grain
2. Soak in cold water until well bled
3. Remove the scraps
4. Stud with clean garlic cloves, black pepper, clove, cinnamon and pieces of ham
5. Drain well
6. Take a pot and spread a bed of chopped straw at the bottom
7. Place a slice of meat on top
8. Add a quarter of good vinegar, a little water, ground black pepper, Chinese oregano, avocado leaves, rosemary, bay leaves and marjoram
9. Then place slices of meat until they well cover the liquid
10. Add another layer of the aromatic herbs mentioned
11. Add the same amount of vinegar and water
12. Continue this way until all the meat is arranged
13. Season with fine salt
14. Seal the mouth of the pot with paste and thick paper
15. Place the pot on fire, and after two hours of fairly strong fire, leave it for eight or ten hours on gentle fire
16. After this time, uncover the pot and remove the meat to see if it is cooked, and if it still needs cooking, add vinegar and cover again continuing the fire until it cooks
17. Remove from fire and serve cold, adorning it with olives and turnips, onion slices and bitter orange slices, salt, black pepper and oregano, lettuce hearts, and if desired raw oil
