## Ingredients

- one quarter liter of water
- a little powdered sugar
- two ounces of butter
- a little finely grated green lemon zest
- a little salt
- flour as needed
- eggs
- sweet macaroons, crumbled or crushed
- candied and ground orange blossom
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil a quarter liter of water with a little powdered sugar, two ounces of butter, finely grated green lemon zest, and a little salt
2. Add enough flour to make a well-bound dough
3. Stir over the fire until the dough pulls away from the pan
4. Remove from heat and add one whole egg while the dough is hot, stirring well to incorporate
5. Continue adding eggs one by one until the dough runs between the fingers
6. Return briefly to the fire, then remove again
7. Continue adding eggs one by one as many as the dough can absorb, along with crumbled or crushed sweet macaroons, candied and ground orange blossom, and lemon zest
8. Form gâteaux of desired size and cook separately
9. Top with roasted and cleaned peanuts mixed with fine sugar and egg white, then dry briefly in the oven
