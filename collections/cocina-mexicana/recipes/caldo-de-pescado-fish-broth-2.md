## Ingredients

- butter (a little)
- carrots
- onions
- celery
- turnips
- other vegetables as available
- fish scraps or fish cut into small pieces
- water (small quantity)
- salt
- herb bouquet

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a pot and add finely sliced carrots, onions, celery, turnips and other vegetables
2. Add fish scraps or cut fish with small quantity of water
3. When it begins to set, add more boiling water
4. Add salt and herb bouquet
5. When cooking reaches the proper point, pass through a silk sieve
6. For thinner consistency, use for white sauce, etc.
7. For thicker consistency, reduce further to obtain substance for fish prepared dried in Spanish style
