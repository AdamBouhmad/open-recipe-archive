## Ingredients

- flour or crushed wheat
- salt
- almond milk or goat milk
- wheat grains cooked
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook flour or crushed wheat in water with a little salt
2. Once cooked, add almond milk or goat milk
3. Add cooked wheat grains
4. Sweeten with sugar to taste
5. Add cinnamon in small pieces
