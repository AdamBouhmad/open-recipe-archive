## Ingredients

- pork loin, quarter-sized cut
- ham, cooked in strips
- eggs, cooked in pieces
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- capers
- olives, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- water
- tomato, ground without seeds
- onions
- broth from cooking the loins
- clove
- cinnamon
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cut the loin the size of a quarter-yard, place it on a cutting board and cut it in such a way that it goes widening and the cuts do not pass to the other side, hitting the meat hard to flatten it
2. Fill with strips of cooked ham, pieces of cooked egg, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, chopped capers and olives and a dusting of salt and pepper
3. Then roll and tie it
4. Place in a pot a little water with corresponding salt and cook the meat
5. Once cooked, grind abundant tomato without seeds and fry in a small pan with whole onions
6. Add a little broth from the one in which the loins were cooked; grind clove, cinnamon and pepper, add to the loins and when the broth is thick, add a little Sherry wine
