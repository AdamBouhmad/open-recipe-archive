## Ingredients

- Thoroughly soaked codfish
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. Thoroughly soak the codfish and cook without removing the skin.
2. Fry in oil, in proportion of twelve ounces for each pound of fish, the cloves of garlic and abundant parsley, all well minced, with a little pepper.
3. Place the already cooked codfish there with the skin facing down, stir with a spoon or something else.
4. Add as much milk as oil was used, stirring constantly for the broth to thicken.
