## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- green chiles
- vinegar
- pickled chiles
- cooked onions
- avocado
- salt
- oil
- olives
- oregano

## Directions

1. Cook ripe tomatoes and green chiles
2. Peel both and grind
3. Dissolve in good vinegar, add chopped pickled chiles and crumbled cooked onions
4. Chop avocado into pieces and add with the appropriate salt and plenty of oil
5. Place in a platter and adorn on top with pickled chiles divided into quarters, olives, plenty of slices of peeled avocado, and powdered oregano
