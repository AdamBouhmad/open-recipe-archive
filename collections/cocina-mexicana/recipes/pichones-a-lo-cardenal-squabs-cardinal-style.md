## Ingredients

- squabs
- lemon juice
- lard
- ham
- paper
- crab meat
- crab sauce

## Directions

1. Rub the squabs with lemon juice to whiten them
2. Brown them in lard without letting them brown too much
3. Place them in a dish prepared with ham
4. Pour the lard in which they were browned over them
5. Cover with more slices of ham and a sheet of paper
6. Let cook at two gentle fires
7. Serve mixed with crab and crab sauce on top
