## Ingredients

- chickens
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- cold lard
- raw ham
- garlic head
- good vinegar
- lard
- oil
- parsley
- ground almonds
- hard egg yolks
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pluck the chickens dry and beat them well with a stick, especially on the breast
2. Evaporate and wash them
3. Rub inside and out with lemon juice, salt-pepper and cold lard
4. Insert inside a large piece of raw, clean ham, a peeled and half-cooked head of garlic and a little good vinegar
5. Place in a pot and put in the oven at gentle heat, turning them from time to time so they do not burn
6. Baste them alternately with lard and lemon juice, salt and pepper, repeating until cooked
7. Give them the final coating with oil, lemon, salt and pepper
8. Form a sauce with ground almonds and hard egg yolks beaten with a little vinegar, pepper and salt
9. Pour this sauce over the chickens
10. Top with plenty of oil and chopped parsley
