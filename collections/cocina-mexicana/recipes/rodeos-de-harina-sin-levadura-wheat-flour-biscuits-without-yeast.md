## Ingredients

- Flour
- Eight egg yolks and one egg white
- One pound of well-ground and sifted sugar
- One pound of lard
- Warm water
- Sugar, panecillo or cinnamon, ground, for topping

## Directions

1. Make a mound of flour on a clean table with a hole in the center
2. Stirring continuously with the hand, add eight egg yolks and one egg white, one pound of well-ground and sifted sugar, and one pound of lard, gradually taking flour from around the edges that the mixture absorbs
3. Moisten with warm water until smooth
4. Shape the biscuits and place on papers
5. Top with ground sugar, panecillo or cinnamon
6. Bake in the oven
