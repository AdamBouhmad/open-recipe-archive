## Ingredients

- mustard
- almonds
- toasted bread
- lard
- onion
- parsley
- white wine
- vinegar
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- cooked pork loin
- cooked chicken
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash a cup of mustard well and grind it with almonds and toasted bread.
2. Fry a good amount of chopped onion and parsley in lard and when the onion begins to brown, add the ground mustard with the almonds.
3. Once fried, add plenty of white wine and a splash of vinegar.
4. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper and sufficient salt.
5. Slice cooked pork loin and place it with the chickens in pieces, also cooked, in the sauce, letting it boil until it has thickened appropriately.
6. When serving, pour oil on top.
