## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- flour
- egg

## Directions

1. As you scrape and clean the salsify, put them in a bowl filled with water and vinegar
2. Remove and cook in abundant water, boiling with a little salt and a spoonful of flour and beaten egg
3. Fry and let brown
