## Ingredients

- Four pounds of ground sugar
- Thirty-two eggs
- One pound of ground almond
- A little water to dilute the almond from the metate
- Four ounces of walnut

## Directions

1. Mix four pounds of ground sugar with thirty-two eggs, one pound of ground almond, a little water with which to dilute the almond, and four ounces of walnut
2. Place on the fire and bring to cajeta point
