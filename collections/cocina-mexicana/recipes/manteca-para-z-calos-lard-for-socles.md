## Ingredients

- three pounds of very white kidney tallow from mutton
- six pounds of lard
- powdered Prussian blue
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from three pounds of very white kidney tallow from mutton
2. Chop this fat very fine
3. Set to melt like the pork fat in the previous article
4. When melted and almost cooked, add six pounds of lard
5. Let them melt together
6. Pass the mixture through new linen over a basin
7. Cool by beating with a wicker brush the same way egg whites are beaten
8. When it takes on some body, stop using the brush
9. Beat with two spatulas
10. Add a little powdered Prussian blue and the juice of two lemons
11. This lard is used to model a socle to serve under a galantine, a ham, etc.
12. For other colors, spinach green, carmine or cochineal, saffron, etc. can be used
