## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, deveined, soaked and ground
- tomatoes
- lard
- salt
- poultry
- ham in strips
- sausage in pieces
- chorizo, sliced, all cooked with the poultry
- broth
- cooked onions
- apple
- peach
- clove
- ground cinnamon
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind deveined, soaked ancho chiles with tomatoes
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. When well fried, add the poultry, ham in strips, sausage in pieces, and chorizo sliced, all cooked with the poultry, its broth, cooked onions, apple, peach, clove, and ground cinnamon
4. Add vinegar and sugar
5. It will be like the previous ones
