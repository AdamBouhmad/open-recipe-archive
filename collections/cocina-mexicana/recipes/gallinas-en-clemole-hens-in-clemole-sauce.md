## Ingredients

- day-old hens
- wide chiles
- pequín chiles
- sesame, toasted
- almonds with shell, toasted
- cilantro seeds, toasted
- cooked tomatoes
- xitomates
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper

## Directions

1. Kill the hens a day before, plucking them dry and cleaning them while cold, taking care not to let a drop of water touch them so the meat does not break and they are tender the next day
2. Devein three dozen wide chiles and toast them at the embers
3. Soak six other chiles
4. Toast the pequín chiles without any vein
5. Reground twelve ounces of sesame, four ounces of almonds with shell and a little more than half an eggshell of toasted cilantro seeds
6. Grind two jícaros of cooked tomatoes and two of xitomates, adding cinnamon, clove and pepper
