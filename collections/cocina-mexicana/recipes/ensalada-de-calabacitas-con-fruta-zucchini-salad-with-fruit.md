## Ingredients

- tender zucchini
- salt
- peach without skin, in small slices
- plantain
- oil
- vinegar
- olives
- chile peppers, split
- capers
- avocado
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook, chop and wash the tender zucchini; then add salt, peach without skin and in small slices, plantain, oil, vinegar, olives, split chile peppers, capers, avocado and a little pepper when serving.
