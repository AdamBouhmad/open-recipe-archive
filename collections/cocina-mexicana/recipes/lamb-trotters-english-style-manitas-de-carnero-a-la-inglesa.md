## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- broth
- verjuice
- salt
- pepper
- parsnip root
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- bread crumbs
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. After boiling in water, simmer for half an hour to develop flavor with broth, one spoonful of verjuice, salt, pepper, a stuffed parsnip root, some onion quarters and a head of garlic
2. Drain and remove the bones
3. Place in their place bread crumb pieces of the same size and thickness, which have been browned in butter
4. Arrange on a plate and cover with piquant sauce
