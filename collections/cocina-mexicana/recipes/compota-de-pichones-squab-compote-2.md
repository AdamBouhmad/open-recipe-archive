## Ingredients

- Young squabs
- 2-3 truffles or earthen mushrooms
- Mushrooms
- Several poultry livers
- One small veal sweetbread, larded and divided into four parts
- A small bunch of parsley
- Onion
- One head of garlic
- 2 cloves
- Basil
- A piece of good butter
- Flour
- Equal parts common broth and meat essence
- 1 glass white wine
- Salt and coarse pepper
- Lemon juice or white vinegar

## Directions

1. Scald the young squabs and tuck their feet under their bodies
2. Lard them and remove the neck and wings
3. Clean them well
4. Place in a pot with truffles or earthen mushrooms, mushrooms, poultry livers, veal sweetbread, parsley, onion, garlic head, cloves, basil, and butter
5. Place over heat, add a little flour and moisten with equal parts common broth and meat essence and one glass of white wine
6. Season with salt and coarse pepper
7. Allow the squabs to cook and the sauce to reduce to the consistency of sauce
8. Carefully remove the fat and add lemon juice or a splash of white vinegar before serving
9. Ensure everything is well cooked and properly salted
