## Ingredients

- Lamb breast
- Bacon slices
- Onions
- Carrots
- A bouquet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Place meat in a pot prepared with bacon slices, onions, carrots, a bouquet, salt, pepper, and broth
2. When cooked over low heat, remove the bone
3. Sprinkle with pepper and fine salt
4. Brown with melted butter
5. Coat with bread crumbs and place on grill to serve with spicy sauce
6. Alternatively, can be cooked in a pot and served later with all kinds of legumes, substances, or condiments
