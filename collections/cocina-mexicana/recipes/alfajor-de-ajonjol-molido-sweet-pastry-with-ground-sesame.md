## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Virgin honey
- Almonds
- Hazelnuts
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Cinnamon
- Sesame seeds
- Biscuit

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with sugar and virgin honey and clarify it
2. Cook until the stage where it adheres to the fingers when a little is taken
3. Add ground almonds, ground hazelnuts, clove, nutmeg, cinnamon, ground sesame, and toasted biscuit cakes
4. Pass everything through the metate and then through the sieve
5. Add whole sesame seeds and let the syrup reach the point of setting in water
