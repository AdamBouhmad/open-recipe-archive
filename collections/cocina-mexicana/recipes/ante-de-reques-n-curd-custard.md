## Ingredients

- syrup at medium point
- curds
- virgin honey
- orange blossom water
- sponge cake
- dragée

## Directions

1. Make syrup at medium point
2. When it is cold, pour a little into a dish and beat the curds with a spoon
3. Add a little virgin honey until it becomes smooth, and orange blossom water
4. Layer one layer of sponge cake and another of curds with dragée on top
