## Ingredients

- Onion
- butter
- green beans
- salt
- pepper
- verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown the onion in butter until it has good color.
2. Add the cleaned, cooked, and drained beans.
3. Sauté and season to taste, then sauté again so the seasoning is absorbed.
4. Add a little verjuice or vinegar and serve.
