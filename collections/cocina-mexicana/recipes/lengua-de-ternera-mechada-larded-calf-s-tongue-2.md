## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Salt
- Cooked ham
- Small cooked onions
- Cooked sausage slices
- Cooked chorizo
- Parsley
- Clove, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Oregano
- Flour, browned in butter
- Capers
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Generous wine
- Broth from cooking the tongue

## Directions

1. Pound the tongue against a stone or with a stick
2. After washing well, cook it in water with salt
3. When cooked, remove the skin and divide into portions for serving one or two per plate
4. Lard with cooked ham
5. Place in a pot with small cooked onions, slices of cooked ham and sausage, cooked chorizos, and stripped parsley
6. Dust with ground clove, pepper, and cinnamon and oregano
7. Add flour browned in butter, capers, raisins, almonds, generous wine, and some of the broth in which the tongue was cooked, until the tongue is completely covered
8. Cook on two fires and let the broth thicken
