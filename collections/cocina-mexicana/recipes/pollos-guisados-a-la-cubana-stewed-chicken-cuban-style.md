## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- onions
- garlic cloves
- oregano
- fine spices
- vinegar
- dry wine
- salt
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and cut into quarters
2. Place in a casserole with lard, onions cut into four pieces, whole garlic cloves, oregano, fine spices, a little vinegar, and half a glass of dry wine
3. Season with salt
4. Heat and when half-cooked, add three chiles in vinegar
5. Let cook over low heat until ready to serve
