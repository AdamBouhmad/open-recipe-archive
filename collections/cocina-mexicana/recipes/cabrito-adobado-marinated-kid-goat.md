## Ingredients

- kid goat portions
- lard
- salt
- garlic
- ancho chile
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger
- water
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- onion slices
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry kid goat portions in lard with salt
2. Once fried, add sufficient crushed garlic
3. When garlic is fried, add ground ancho chile that has been deveined, mixed with cloves, pepper, cumin and ginger
4. Add necessary water, vinegar, bay leaves and a bundle of thyme
5. Once cooked, burn lard in a cazuela
6. Remove thyme and bay leaves from the pot and pour into the lard
7. Finish cooking over two fires until dry
8. Garnish with onion slices and olives
