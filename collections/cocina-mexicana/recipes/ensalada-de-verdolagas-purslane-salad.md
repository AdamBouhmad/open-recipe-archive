## Ingredients

- purslane
- salt
- garlic
- butter or oil
- onion
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in salted water and drain afterwards
2. Transfer to a pan where garlic has been fried in butter or preferably oil
3. Place in a platter and season with oil and vinegar, adding chopped raw onion
