## Ingredients

- water
- vinegar (substantial amount)
- oil (a little)
- salt
- pepper (ground)
- cinnamon (ground)
- onion (chopped)
- garlic (chopped)
- parsley (chopped)
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil a little water with a substantial amount of vinegar, a little oil, salt, pepper and cinnamon all ground together.
2. Let it boil until only the fat remains.
3. While it is frying, add chopped onion, garlic and parsley.
4. Before serving, add breadcrumbs.
