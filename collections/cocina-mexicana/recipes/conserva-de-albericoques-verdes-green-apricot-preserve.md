## Ingredients

- Green apricots, undamaged
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Cold water
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Select undamaged green apricots
2. Prick them at the stem with a needle
3. Place them in the center of a clean napkin with salt powder proportioned to the quantity of apricots
4. Shake them side to side and roll them with vinegar until the fuzz falls off
5. Place in cold water, then in a pot with sufficient clarified syrup
6. Give one boil
7. Skim and transfer the fruit with syrup to a glazed earthenware dish so the fruit remains always covered with syrup
8. Let rest briefly
9. Pass through a sieve to strain the syrup and separately give it a dozen boils
10. Return the cooled apricots and syrup to the dish
11. Repeat this operation for two or three days, adding syrup of strong flower point in proportion to what the fruit has absorbed, ensuring the syrup covers the fruit
12. Store the preserve in appropriate vessels
