## Ingredients

- pork, cooked
- tomatoes without seeds or pits
- raisins
- almonds
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- pomegranate
- sesame seeds, toasted
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind tomatoes without seeds or pits according to the amount of meat
2. Fry the ground tomatoes until the liquid is thick
3. Add the cooked pork with raisins, almonds, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, and saffron (all ground except the meat)
4. When serving, add salt and top with pomegranate and toasted sesame seeds
