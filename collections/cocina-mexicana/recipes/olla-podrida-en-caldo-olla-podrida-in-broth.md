## Ingredients

- Veal
- Lamb
- Chicken
- Salt
- Water
- Soaked chickpeas
- Fat ham
- Chorizos
- Longaniza sausage
- Garlic (roasted and cleaned)
- Onions (roasted and cleaned)
- Rice
- Mint
- Small squash or Castilian squash
- Turnips
- Kohlrabi
- White cabbage
- Carrots
- Tender green beans (shredded and tied in bundles)
- Bergamot pears, brown pears or milk pears
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Roasted cilantro

## Directions

1. Clean and divide veal, lamb and chicken into regular portions
2. Place them in a proportioned pot with salt and enough water so it does not reduce too much when consumed, which would ruin the taste
3. Add soaked chickpeas and put on the fire, skimming when necessary
4. After the broth is skimmed, add fat ham and continue cooking
5. When the meats are halfway cooked, add chorizos and longaniza in pieces, roasted and cleaned garlic and onions, rice, mint, small or Castilian squash, turnips, kohlrabi, white cabbage, carrots, tender green beans shredded and tied in bundles, and bergamot pears, brown pears or milk pears
6. Then add ground clove, pepper, saffron, cumin and roasted cilantro
7. Ensure there is enough fire so the meats are well cooked
