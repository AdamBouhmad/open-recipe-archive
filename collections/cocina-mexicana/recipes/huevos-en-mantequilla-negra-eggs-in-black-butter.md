## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Break the eggs onto a plate and season with a little salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Burn the butter until it is almost black and pour some of it well-heated over the surface of the eggs
3. Immediately slide or slip the eggs from the plate into the pan of hot butter and put it back over the fire
4. Perform the same operation to pass them from the pan to the plate, pouring over them a little vinegar that has been heated beforehand
5. If the eggs on the top side are not yet well cooked, it will be necessary to pass a shovel made hot from above over them so they are
