## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- a little tender onion
- agraz or lemon juice or a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter with chopped parsley, salt, pepper, nutmeg and a little tender onion on a plate
2. Stir everything well together until well mixed
3. Then add agraz or lemon juice or a little vinegar
