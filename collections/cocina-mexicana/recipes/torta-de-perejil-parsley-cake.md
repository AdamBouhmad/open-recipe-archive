## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Make thin cakes of three eggs each, seasoned with parsley, salt, and pepper
2. When making them, spread them over the cover of a saucepan, roll them very tightly, and then cut each one in two parts
3. Coat with well beaten egg and bread with very fine ground breadcrumbs
4. Fry until they have good color in a pan with fresh lard and serve garnished with parsley
