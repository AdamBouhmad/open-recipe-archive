## Ingredients

- folded pastry dough
- filling
- egg

## Directions

1. Extend sheets of folded pastry dough with a rolling pin
2. Line a tart pan or skillet with these sheets
3. Place the filling in the center
4. Cover with a layer of the same dough
5. Press well on the edges to seal
6. Brush the top with beaten egg
7. Bake in a hot oven or between two fires
8. After baking, open it and add a good sauce before serving
