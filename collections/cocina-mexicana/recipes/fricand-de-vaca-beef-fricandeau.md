## Ingredients

- Slice of beef pulp or wheel, two fingers thick
- Pork fat
- Broth
- Mixed herbs bouquet
- Clear broth (page 132)
- Pot broth

## Directions

1. Lard a slice of beef pulp or wheel with pork fat
2. Parboil briefly in boiling water
3. Put to cook with broth and mixed herbs bouquet
4. When cooked, remove from the pot to remove fat from the broth
5. Strain the broth through a sieve into another pot
6. Let thicken over fire until almost nothing remains
7. Put the cooked meat in to glaze
8. When glazed on the side where it was larded, arrange on the serving plate
9. Loosen what remains in the pot over fire, adding a little clear broth and some pot broth
10. When it tastes good, serve over the fricandeau
11. This can equally be served over chicory, sorrel or spinach, etc.
