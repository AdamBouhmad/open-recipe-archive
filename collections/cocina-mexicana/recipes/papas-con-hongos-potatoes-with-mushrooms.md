## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onions
- mushrooms
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- black pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice or verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the potatoes cooked in salted water, or preferably as indicated for French-style salad potatoes
2. Place them in a pot with chopped onions and mushrooms and a good piece of butter
3. Brown them over the fire and add a little flour
4. Turn them well on all sides and moisten with broth, seasoning with salt and black pepper
5. Let them cook and once the cooking liquid has sufficiently reduced, bind it with egg yolks
6. At the moment of serving, add lemon juice or verjuice, or in their absence, a few drops of vinegar
