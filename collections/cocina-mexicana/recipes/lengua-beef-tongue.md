## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- tomato
- onions
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- oregano
- lard
- capers
- Sherry wine
- vinegar
- olives
- pickled chiles

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue and remove the skin
2. Cut it in half and open it
3. Grind plenty of tomato, onions, clove, cinnamon, pepper, thyme, bay leaf and oregano
4. Fry everything together in lard until cooled
5. Put half the mixture in a pan with capers at the bottom, then the tongue and on top the other mixture with more capers
6. Add a small cup of Sherry wine and a little vinegar
7. Cook on two fires so it becomes like roasted
8. Decorate with olives and pickled chiles
