## Ingredients

- veal jerky or ribs
- pasilla chiles
- mulato chiles
- cooked tomatoes
- cloves
- lard
- cecina broth
- salt
- epazote
- veal udder
- veal breast

## Directions

1. From the day before, desalt the meat.
2. On the day of cooking, cook at high heat.
3. When well cooked, remove from heat.
4. Devein some pasilla and mulato chiles, toast them well without burning, and grind with cooked tomatoes and a few cloves.
5. Fry the ground mixture in lard and moisten with the cecina broth.
6. Add salt and cut the meat into regular pieces.
7. Place in the mole, adding some epazote sprigs.
8. Let it boil until the sauce thickens to the proper consistency.
9. The same preparation and cooking applies to cecina, which becomes very tasty in this mole, particularly the udder and breast of veal or beef.
