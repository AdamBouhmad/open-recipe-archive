## Ingredients

- dried anchos chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Take a dozen ancho chiles, remove the veins and place to deflamer so they are not spicy; then grind dry with half a head of garlic and a few cumin seeds with sufficient salt.
2. Lower this from the metate with a little water and add everything to a pot.
