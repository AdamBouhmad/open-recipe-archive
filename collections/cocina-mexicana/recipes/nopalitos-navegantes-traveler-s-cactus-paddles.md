## Ingredients

- nopal paddles, cleaned and cut lengthwise into strips
- salt
- lard
- eye of meat, chopped
- onion heads, cut into quarters
- water
- dried pasilla chiles, whole
- cooking oil
- epazote

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned nopal paddles, cut lengthwise into strips, with salt in a copper pot.
2. Place a cazuela over the fire and fry the nopales in lard with a little chopped eye of meat.
3. When browned, add onion heads cut into quarters, sufficient water, the nopales, and a sprig of epazote.
4. Add two or three whole dried pasilla chiles and a little cooking oil.
5. Let simmer until the broth is seasoned, then remove from heat, add necessary salt and plenty of oil.
