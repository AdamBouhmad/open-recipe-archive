## Ingredients

- papaya
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Peel and thoroughly clean of internal parts and seeds, then cook
2. Wash and to whiten, spread on a board in the sun all day until night
3. Then place in water, and the next day in fine sugar syrup well clarified and at medium point
4. Preserve in it as described and add flavoring like all preserves
