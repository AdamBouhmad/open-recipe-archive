## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- butter
- flour
- broth
- coriander (dried, toasted, and ground)

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the cucumbers into dice shapes, place them to cook with salt
2. When they have come to a boil a few times, drain and dry them in cloth
3. Fry them lightly in butter
4. Remove them from the butter and sprinkle with flour
5. Pour broth over them, which is seasoned with a little dried coriander, toasted and ground
6. Let them cook over low heat until done
7. When the broth has been consumed appropriately, strain it and pour it over the cucumbers when serving
