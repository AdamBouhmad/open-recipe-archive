## Ingredients

- six large carrots
- six large onions
- four thick turnips
- four leeks
- three celery stalks
- butter
- half an egg's worth of sugar
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the carrots, onions, turnips and leeks
2. Place them in a pot with sufficient butter and as much as half an egg of sugar
3. Fry the vegetables in white, meaning do not let them brown or take any color when frying
4. When they begin to color, moisten them with broth and let them simmer over low heat
5. Pass the substance through a sieve and clarify it, not making it too thick
6. Soak bread in good broth and pour the substance over it
