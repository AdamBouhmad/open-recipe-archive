## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- onion
- tomato
- parsley
- eight eggs
- milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked potatoes well
2. Fry the chopped onion, tomato, and parsley
3. Beat eight eggs, mixing the whites with the yolks, adding a cup of milk and some finely ground salt and pepper
4. Mix the potatoes with the eggs
5. Pour everything into the pan with the fried seasoning, letting the cake set carefully so it does not burn until it is well cooked and set
