## Ingredients

- lard
- strips of ham
- sliced chorizo
- chopped garlic cloves
- cooked and ground beet
- cooked and ground apple
- salt
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- capers
- cooked small onions
- chicken
- chicken broth
- wine
- cured tornachilesm
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry strips of ham and sliced chorizo in lard and remove them
2. In the same fat, fry chopped garlic, cooked and ground beet and apple with appropriate salt
3. Season with ground cloves, pepper, and cinnamon
4. Add capers and cooked small onions
5. Place the chicken with its broth, the fried ham and chorizo, and a bit of wine
6. When well simmered and seasoned, add cured tornachilesand olives before serving
