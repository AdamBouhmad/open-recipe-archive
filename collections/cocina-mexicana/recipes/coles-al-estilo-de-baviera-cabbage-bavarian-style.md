## Ingredients

- Red cabbage
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mixed herbs
- Three cloves
- Two onions
- Sausage

## Directions

1. Cut a red cabbage and parboil it
2. Wash and place to cook over coals with broth, salt, pepper, a mixed herb bundle, three cloves, and two onions
3. Cook a sausage divided into two halves which has been parboiled and tied separately in the same broth
4. When everything is cooked, remove excess fat and serve the sausage in the middle of the plate with the cabbage around it, pouring a light flavorful sauce over top
