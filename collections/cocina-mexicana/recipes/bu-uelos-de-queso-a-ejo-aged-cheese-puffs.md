## Ingredients

- two ounces aged cheese well ground
- four ounces flour
- three egg yolks
- one egg white
- butter

## Directions

1. Form the dough with two ounces of aged cheese well ground, four ounces of flour, three egg yolks and one egg white
2. Knead everything with plenty of butter and follow in all the same procedures as in the previous ones
