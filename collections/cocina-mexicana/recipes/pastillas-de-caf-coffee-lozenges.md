## Ingredients

- three pounds of superfine sugar
- three ounces of good powdered coffee
- one quarter measure of water
- a small piece of fish glue

## Directions

1. Prepare three pounds of superfine sugar as described.
2. Boil three ounces of good powdered coffee in one quarter measure of water.
3. Strain the decoction through a cloth in which a small piece of fish glue has been placed to remove the sediment.
4. When the liquid is cold and clear, mix it with the sugar to form the lozenges.
5. Observe not to put in the pan more than what can be kept hot while it is being poured, because if it cools, it would damage the operation.
