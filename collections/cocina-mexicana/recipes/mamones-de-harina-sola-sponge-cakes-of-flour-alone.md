## Ingredients

- fifteen eggs
- six ounces of sugar
- half a pound of fine flour

## Directions

1. Make the same way as the mamones from the previous article, and the ingredients are put in this proportion: fifteen eggs, six ounces of sugar, and half a pound of fine flour.
