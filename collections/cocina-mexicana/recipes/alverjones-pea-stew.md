## Ingredients

- Alverjones (large peas)
- Water
- Tequesquite
- Salt
- Pork meat in pieces
- Onion slices
- Chilpocles (dried chilies)
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Put the alverjones to cook in water with a little tequesquite
2. Remove this water and cook them like beans, putting them in fresh water with salt
3. Cook pork meat in pieces separately
4. Fry the cooked pork with onion slices
5. Add the alverjones with their broth, fried chilpocles, and a little thyme
