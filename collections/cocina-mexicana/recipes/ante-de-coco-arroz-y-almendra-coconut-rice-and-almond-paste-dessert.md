## Ingredients

- Rice cooked with milk
- Almond paste
- Coconut paste
- Eggs
- Butter
- Syrup
- Raisins

## Directions

1. Make rice cooked with milk the day before
2. Then make almond paste and coconut paste
3. Beat eggs and grease a cazuela with butter
4. Layer rice, then coconut, then almond, and egg between each layer
5. Add as many layers as desired and cook at two heats
6. Add syrup, raisins, and whatever else is desired
