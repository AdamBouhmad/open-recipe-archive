## Ingredients

- Four pounds of sugar
- One glass of clarified lemon juice
- Six quartillos of rack or tea

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with egg white and at the breaking point using four pounds of sugar
2. Add one glass of clarified lemon juice
3. Stir and agitate the mixture until it acquires a closed boil
4. Remove from heat and empty into a basin
5. Let cool
6. Mix in six quartillos of rack or tea
7. When prepared with rum or any other pleasant spirituous liquor, it can be preserved a long time
8. To make punch, add only the necessary amount of hot water or more or less concentrated tea infusion
