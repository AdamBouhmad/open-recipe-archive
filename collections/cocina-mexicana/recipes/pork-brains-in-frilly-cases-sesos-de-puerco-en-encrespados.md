## Ingredients

- pork brains
- pork fat
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- aromatics
- pork caul

## Directions

1. After cleaning the brains, chop them mixed with pork fat
2. Season with salt, pepper, spices and powdered aromatics
3. Wrap this mixture in pork caul pieces
4. Arrange and cook at moderate heat
