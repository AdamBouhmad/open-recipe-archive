## Ingredients

- ten pounds of beef rump
- one sheep breast
- one veal tendon
- a proportionate quantity of desalted ham
- one hen
- two squabs
- two old partridges
- one duck
- one pound of pork fat
- one raw sausage
- eight sausages with red chile inside
- two pounds of chickpeas
- six very hot chiles
- four cloves
- a little nutmeg
- a little mace
- four cabbages
- ten lettuces
- thirty carrots
- thirty turnips
- one dozen artichoke hearts
- twenty-four blanched onions
- green beans
- green beans
- cucumbers or gherkins
- peas
- gelatin
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and tie ten pounds of beef rump with twine
2. Prepare one sheep breast, one veal tendon, and a proportionate quantity of desalted ham
3. Prepare two old partridges cut like stuffed chicken, one duck, one hen, and two squabs
4. Soak two pounds of chickpeas in warm water since the day before
5. Arrange all prepared meats in a braising vessel with the soaked chickpeas
6. Moisten everything with great broth and bring to a boil
7. Add six very hot chiles, four cloves of spice, a little nutmeg, and a little mace wrapped in clean cloth
8. Allow everything to continue boiling until completely cooked, removing the most tender chickpeas
9. Prepare four cabbages, ten lettuces, thirty carrots, and thirty turnips, shaping them with the greatest evenness possible
10. Parboil everything and arrange in a covered saucepan with slices of ham
11. Moisten with the defatted broth from the braising vessel
12. Prepare one dozen artichoke hearts, trimmed and rounded, cooking them in white broth with twenty-four blanched onions
13. Shape carrots and turnips in the form of olives and cook them in concentrated broth with a little sugar
14. Prepare green beans, green beans, cucumbers or gherkins, and peas, parboiling each article and setting each aside
15. Strain the broth from the braising vessel, degrease it, clarify it, strain again through a cloth, and keep it boiling
16. Squeeze the cabbages and lettuces and arrange them on a large platter in the following order: a piece of cabbage, a carrot, a lettuce, and a turnip, covering the circumference of the platter in a circle, placing the chickpeas in the spaces
17. Arrange the meats in order in the middle of the platter and the twelve artichoke hearts on the border of the vegetables at equal distance, placing the blanched onions between them
18. Heat the small vegetables well, sauté them with gelatin and butter, and arrange them like bouquets in the artichoke hearts
19. Finally cover all the meats with gelatin
20. Serve everything very hot with the concentrated broth served separately
