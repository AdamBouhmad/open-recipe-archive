## Ingredients

- offal
- lard
- salt
- tomato
- warm water
- garlic
- spices

## Directions

1. After boiling, cut into small slices and sauté with lard and salt, taking care to skim it well
2. Then fry with tomato, and before serving, stew with warm water, garlic and spices
