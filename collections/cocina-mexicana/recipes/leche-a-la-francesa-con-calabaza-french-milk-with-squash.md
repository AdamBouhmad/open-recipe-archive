## Ingredients

- Two cuartillos of milk
- A little ground rice
- Egg yolks
- Sugar to taste
- One stick of cinnamon
- A piece of cooked and ground squash
- A little orange blossom water
- Almonds and ground cinnamon for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix two cuartillos of milk with a little ground rice, egg yolks, and sugar to taste
2. Strain through a sieve and heat with a cinnamon stick
3. After it has boiled well, add a piece of cooked and ground squash
4. Stir until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco
5. Add a little orange blossom water
6. Pour into a serving dish and garnish on top with almonds and ground cinnamon
