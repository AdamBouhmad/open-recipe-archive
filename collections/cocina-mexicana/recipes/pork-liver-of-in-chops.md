## Ingredients

- pork liver
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- ground spices
- pork slices

## Directions

1. Chop the pork liver with fat
2. Season with salt, pepper, and ground spices
3. Wrap this chopped mixture in pork slices
4. After flattening them, let them cook over moderate heat
