## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Lard
- Salt
- Finely chopped onion
- Fried bread
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Kill the chicken so that only the head is separated from the neck
2. Bend the neck backward and hang the chicken so that all the blood drains into it
3. Extract the blood and place it in a new cloth tied at the corners, or in the skin itself, and cook it
4. Grind the cooked blood with fried bread
5. Fry finely chopped onion in lard with salt
6. Add the cooked and ground blood
7. Put in the cooked chicken, a little of its broth, olives, and capers
8. Let everything simmer until the broth is well seasoned
