## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit, divided into pieces
- good quality broth
- lean ham
- assorted herb bundle (manojito surtido)
- black pepper
- lentils
- onions
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Divide the rabbit into pieces and cook in good broth with lean ham, an assorted herb bundle, and black pepper
2. Cook lentils separately in unsalted broth with some onions browned in butter
3. When the lentils are cooked, pass them through a sieve with their broth
4. Remove the rabbit and ham from their cooking broth and decrease the broth by incorporating it with the lentil puree
5. Heat the lentil mixture in a cooking pot on hot coals, allowing it to take on a good color
6. Just before serving, decrease the sauce further and allow it to reduce sufficiently
7. Add a lump of sugar if the sauce is too sharp
8. Arrange the rabbit (kept warm) in a saucepan on the serving platter and pour the lentil sauce over it
9. Place the ham that cooked with the rabbit around the dish
10. Serve ordinarily in a silver saucepan or in an earthenware or porcelain dish
