## Ingredients

- Cooked and cold turbot
- Lettuce hearts
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Gherkins
- Corn if convenient
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. With the turbot cooked and cold, cut it into small slices, which are arranged one over the other on a plate, so that they form a small mound
2. Place lettuce hearts around it and hard-boiled eggs in quarters or chopped, separating the yolks from the whites
3. Decorate the salad with capers, anchovies, gherkins, or corn if this is convenient to taste
4. When adding the oil mixed with vinegar and salt, be careful not to disturb the plate, losing the symmetry
