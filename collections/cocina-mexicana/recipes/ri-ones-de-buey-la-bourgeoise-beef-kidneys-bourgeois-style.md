## Ingredients

- beef kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion, finely minced
- garlic, finely minced
- vinegar or verjuice
- strained broth

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into thin strips
2. Stew them over heat with butter, salt, pepper, finely minced onion and garlic
3. Once cooked, add a dash of vinegar or verjuice and a little strained broth
4. Be careful not to let them boil so they do not shrink and toughen
