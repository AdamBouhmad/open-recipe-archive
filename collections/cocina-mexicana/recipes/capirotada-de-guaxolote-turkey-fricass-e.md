## Ingredients

- Guaxolote cocido al asador (roasted turkey)
- Mantequilla (butter)
- Harina (flour)
- Hongos (mushrooms)
- Peregil (parsley)
- Chalote (shallot)
- Vino blanco (white wine)
- Caldo (broth)
- Tostadas fritas (fried toasts)
- Cuajadas (curds)

## Directions

1. Roast the turkey on a spit and when cool, break it apart
2. Prepare an Italian sauce with butter, a spoonful of flour, chopped mushrooms, parsley, and a little shallot
3. Moisten with equal parts white wine and broth
4. When defatted and properly seasoned, add the turkey portions
5. Leave to cook over low heat for about half an hour
6. Skim the fat from the sauce again
7. Add some fried toasts and curds
