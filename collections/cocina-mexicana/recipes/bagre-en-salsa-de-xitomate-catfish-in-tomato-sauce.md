## Ingredients

- one catfish
- wood ash
- flour
- lard
- ripe xitomates (tomatoes)
- pepper
- garlic
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning the catfish well with ash so that it becomes white, remove the bones and the bones from the center of the head; dry it well with cloth so that it is dry.
2. Roll it in flour, place it in a pan that has sufficient capacity, add lard and at a low heat that does not burn the lard much, fry it and leave it until it is well browned on the bottom side.
3. Then turn it carefully so that it does not break, and when it is already browned on both sides, roast some ripe xitomates, grind a little pepper and a bit of garlic, peel the xitomates very well, add the ground pepper and garlic on top and hot lard that is not the one in which the catfish was fried.
4. Once they have fried a lot with that lard, add a little water, letting it boil until the xitomate breaks down well, and when it has seasoned, pour it over the catfish, and on top raw cooking oil, and let it rest in the heat until time to serve it.
