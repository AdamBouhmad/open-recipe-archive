## Ingredients

- chalupas base
- equal parts ancho and pasilla chile, toasted and ground
- salt
- manteca (lard)
- hebras de carne frita (strands of fried meat)
- cebolla picada (chopped onion)

## Directions

1. Make chalupas as in the butter chalupas article
2. In the center add a little chile made from equal amounts of ancho and pasilla, toasted and ground, seasoned with salt
3. Fry in lard and before they brown, remove them
4. Decorate with strands of fried meat, chopped onion or other garnish
