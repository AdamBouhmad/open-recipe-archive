## Ingredients

- duck
- cabbage
- ham
- sausage
- cooked roots
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the duck as for pan-fried duck
2. Parboil the cabbage
3. Cook the cabbage together with the duck, a piece of fat ham, a sausage and other ingredients
4. When cooked, drain and arrange on a plate
5. Drain the cabbage and squeeze by hand, forming it into a cord that is placed around the duck
6. Arrange the sausages, ham and sausage slices and cooked roots that have been carefully trimmed on top
7. Bind everything together, pour thick Spanish sauce over it and serve.
