## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound of yeast
- twenty-four egg yolks
- fourteen egg whites
- twelve ounces of melted lard
- one quarter liter of milk
- aniseed
- syrup

## Directions

1. Knead three pounds of flour with one pound of yeast, twenty-four egg yolks, fourteen egg whites, twelve ounces of melted lard, one quarter liter of milk, and a little aniseed
2. Once the dough is very soft, stretch the fritters as the ordinary ones, fry in lard and serve with syrup
