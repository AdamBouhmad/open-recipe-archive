## Ingredients

- Beans not yet hardened
- Water
- Salt of the earth
- Green parsley branches
- Tomatoes
- Green chiles, cooked
- Green cilantro sprigs
- Lard
- Pork meat broth
- Pork meat, cooked and divided into pieces
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Take beans when they have not yet begun to harden and cook them in water with salt of the earth and some green parsley branches.
2. Once cooked, heat a pot over the fire.
3. Grind tomatoes, cooked green chiles, and green cilantro sprigs (removing the veins from the chiles if desired).
4. Fry the ground tomato in a pot with lard.
5. Add the cooked beans.
6. Moisten with pork meat broth and add this meat, well cooked and divided into pieces.
7. Season everything with fine salt.
8. Let it boil until the broth has reduced somewhat.
