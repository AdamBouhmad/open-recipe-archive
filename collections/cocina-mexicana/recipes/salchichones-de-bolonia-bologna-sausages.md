## Ingredients

- Lean meat from two hams
- Two pork shoulders
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Cilantro
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Bay leaf
- Fresh, sound fat lard cut into large cubes
- Beef intestines
- Saltpeter
- Oil
- Wood ash

## Directions

1. Separate the lean part from two hams and combine with two pork shoulders
2. Scrape with a knife and carefully remove all sinews
3. Mix the prepared meat with salt, whole pepper, cilantro, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, and bay leaf
4. Choose fresh, sound fat lard and cut into large cubes
5. Mix everything together and fill beef intestines, tying at both ends
6. Place in a vessel with saltpeter, cover well, and leave for eight days
7. Remove, drain, and tie between two sticks like a bundle of tobacco
8. Dry and smoke
9. When dry, untie, rub with a mixture of oil and wood ash, and hang in a suitable place to serve when desired
