## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Pork loin
- Ham
- Sausage
- Chiles in vinegar
- Lard
- Onion
- Ground chile
- Meat broth
- Fried and ground fish
- Spices
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Parsley
- Olives
- Capers
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Quarter the chickens and cook them with pork loin, ham, and sausage.
2. Devein the chiles in vinegar and wash them so they do not bite.
3. Heat lard in a cazuela and fry onion in pieces.
4. Add the ground chile and fry until half done.
5. Add the meat broth, pork and chicken meat, and fried ground fish.
6. Add spices, clove, cinnamon, loose parsley, olives, chiles in vinegar, capers, and almonds.
7. Leave to cook over low heat.
