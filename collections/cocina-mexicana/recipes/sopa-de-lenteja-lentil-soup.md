## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Lard
- Broth from the pot
- Sorrel
- Fried bread, in pieces

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils with lard and then crush them through a strainer so they come out like broth.
2. Add the broth from the pot to this mixture.
3. Mince the sorrel and add it to the broth to cook.
4. Serve with fried bread in small pieces.
