## Ingredients

- Mixture from the article that follows (not specified in this excerpt)
- Kirschwasser
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pour the mixture into a basin
2. Place the basin on ice mixed with saltpeter
3. Add kirschwasser and lemon juice to the mixture
4. Beat with a wooden spoon until the mixture becomes firm and white like beaten egg white
5. Pour into molds and proceed as with other aspics
