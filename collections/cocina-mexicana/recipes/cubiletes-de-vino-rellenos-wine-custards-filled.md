## Ingredients

- fifteen egg yolks
- two ounces of ground sugar
- two ounces of melted lard
- a little ground tequisquite
- more than half a small pot of white wine
- flour
- chocolate
- nougat or other paste
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the fifteen egg yolks as if for sponge cake
2. Add two ounces of ground sugar, two ounces of melted lard, salt, a little ground tequisquite, and more than half a small pot of white wine
3. Mix well with flour that will be moistened, and when it reaches the point of forming bubbles, roll it very thin
4. Cut it with a chocolate cup
5. Fill with nougat or any other paste
6. From this same batter, cinnamon biscuits can be made by frying them in not very hot lard and rolling them in ground sugar mixed with cinnamon
