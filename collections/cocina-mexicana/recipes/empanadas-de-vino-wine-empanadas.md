## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- wine
- almonds
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. The dough will be as the previous one, with the difference that instead of milk, wine is used.
2. The filling will be paste of soaked and ground almonds mixed with sifted sugar in equal amounts, orange blossom water, and otherwise made the same way.
