## Ingredients

- egg whites, per half dozen
- two ounces of rose sugar
- goat's or sheep's milk

## Directions

1. For each half dozen egg whites, take two ounces of rose sugar and beat until very foamy.
2. Heat goat's or sheep's milk in a new saucepan or pot.
3. When hot, drop the foam by spoonfuls to cook a little.
4. Remove with a skimmer so the milk drains out.
5. Dust with cinnamon.
