## Ingredients

- two well-ripened coconuts
- six beaten eggs
- one and a half cups of white sugar
- two sticks of ground cinnamon
- three tablespoons of butter
- one panetela
- butter for coating
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Choose two well-ripened coconuts, open them and extract the meat inside
2. Express the coconut, adding water so that it forms a kind of milk
3. Strain and add six beaten eggs, one and a half cups of white sugar, two sticks of ground cinnamon, three tablespoons of butter and one panetela
4. Make a dough and place it in a pudding mold, coating it inside with butter and breadcrumbs to prevent the dough from sticking
5. Place the dough in the mold and cook it between two fires, or if preferred in the oven
6. When it is believed to have set, sprinkle it with cinnamon and sugar
