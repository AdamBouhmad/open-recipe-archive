## Ingredients

- ox head
- salt
- onion, minced
- pork jowl
- wine
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Remove horns and skin from the head
2. Cook in water with sufficient salt
3. Meanwhile, mince much onion and cut pork jowl into small cubes
4. Fry the onion and pork jowl together
5. When well fried, add wine and a little vinegar
6. Grind all the spices and add with fine salt
7. When the head is well cooked, remove it and while very hot disassemble it removing all the bones
8. Pour the prepared hot sauce over it and serve
9. This method can be used for cooking heads of lamb and other animals
