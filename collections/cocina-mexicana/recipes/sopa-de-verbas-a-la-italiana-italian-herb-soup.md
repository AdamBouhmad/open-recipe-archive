## Ingredients

- Sorrel
- Spinach
- Lettuce
- Chard
- Chervil
- Butter
- Ham or bacon loin
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put in a pot sorrel, spinach, lettuce, chard, chervil, butter, and a loin of ham or bacon with salt and pepper
