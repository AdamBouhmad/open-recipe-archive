## Ingredients

- less butter
- flour
- cabbage paste (see cabbage paste)
- egg
- syrup at the point of breaking

## Directions

1. Use less butter and reinforce with flour the cabbage paste
2. When well dried, moisten with egg and place small pieces on the platform about the size of half an egg
3. Brown them, and wetting the tip of your finger in the browning, make a small hole in the middle to place a small figure in it
4. Cook them, and after cooking, coat them one by one with syrup at the point of breaking
