## Ingredients

- thin-sliced bread
- butter
- milk
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- sesame seeds
- dragée
- pine nuts
- egg white
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Slice bread very thin and fry in butter
2. Cook milk with sugar until it reaches the proper consistency
3. Place the milk mixture on top of beds of fried bread slices
4. Scatter raisins, almonds, sesame seeds, dragée, and pine nuts over the bread
5. Form a puff pastry on top with beaten egg white and powdered sugar
6. Place over heat with a comal with fire on top so that the egg cooks
