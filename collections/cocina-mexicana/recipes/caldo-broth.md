## Ingredients

- fresh, healthy meat
- beef
- poultry and game scraps
- salt
- roots
- garlic

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of beef and scraps of poultry and game in a sufficient quantity of water
2. Season with salt, roots, and garlic
3. Place on low heat and leave until everything is well cooked
4. Strain the broth whenever needed
