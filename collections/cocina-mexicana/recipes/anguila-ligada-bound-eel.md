## Ingredients

- eel, cut into pieces
- marinade
- water
- salt
- butter
- two tablespoons flour
- white wine
- mushrooms or potatoes
- fine herbs bundle
- egg yolks
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut eel into pieces and soak in marinade
2. Cook in salted water and drain
3. Fry in butter with two tablespoons flour
4. Add water and a glass of white wine, mushrooms or potatoes, and a bundle of fine herbs
5. Skim and degrease the sauce
6. When thickened and of good taste, mix in a liaison of egg yolks and lemon juice or a little vinegar
7. Do not allow to boil
