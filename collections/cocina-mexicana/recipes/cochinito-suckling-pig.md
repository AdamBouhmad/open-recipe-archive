## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- garlic
- cumin
- cinnamon
- cloves
- pepper
- lard
- salt
- lettuce hearts
- onion
- lemon or orange
- olives
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Kill the suckling pig catching the blood in a cazuela
2. Wash with boiling water, open it, and wash with cold water
3. It must remain whole and place on an iron platter without being pressed
4. Grind garlic, cumin, cinnamon, cloves and pepper, mix everything with lard and salt
5. Spread the suckling pig all over and place on the fire covering the platter, taking care to keep basting the suckling pig so it does not burn
6. The fire should be slow, as the stew will have only the water from the spices
7. Garnish with lettuce hearts, onion, lemon or orange, olives and chiles in vinegar
