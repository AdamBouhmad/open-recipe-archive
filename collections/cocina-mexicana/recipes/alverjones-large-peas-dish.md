## Ingredients

- alverjones (large peas)
- water
- tequesquite
- salt
- pork scraps
- onion
- chipotle chiles
- thyme

## Directions

1. Cook in water and a little tequesquite
2. Remove this water and cook them like beans, placing them in fresh water with salt
3. Then cook pork scraps
4. Once cooked, fry with onion wedges and add the alverjones with their broth
5. Add fried chipotles and a little thyme
