## Ingredients

- 10 ounces dried orange peels
- 2 quartillos common brandy
- 1 pound sugar
- equal quantity of water

## Directions

1. Let the dried orange peels macerate in the sun for fifteen days in a well-sealed bottle with two quartillos of common brandy, taking care to shake the bottle every day.
2. After this time, put it on the fire and dissolve one pound of sugar in an equal quantity of water, letting it become caramel.
3. Pour this into the brandy saturated with orange peel essence.
