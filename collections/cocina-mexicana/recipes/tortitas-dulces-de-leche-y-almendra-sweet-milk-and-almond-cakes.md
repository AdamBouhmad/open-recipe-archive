## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- half a cup of parboiled rice
- four ounces of mortajada almonds
- a little scraped lemon zest
- six egg yolks, well beaten
- point syrup for bathing
- pine nuts and split almonds for garnishing
- cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Sweeten eight quartillos of milk more than usual and mix half a cup of parboiled rice with it
2. Put the milk on the fire, and as it thickens add four ounces of mortajada almonds and a little scraped lemon zest
3. Let it reach a very high point and being cold after being removed, add six well-beaten egg yolks
4. Pour into small molds or cazuelas spread with lard without filling because these cakes swell
5. Put to cook in the oven and when set remove them, arrange on platters, bathe with point syrup and decorate with pine nuts and split almonds, dusting with cinnamon
