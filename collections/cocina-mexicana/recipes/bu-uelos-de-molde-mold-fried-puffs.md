## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 22 eggs with whites and yolks
- 3 fingers of salt
- 1 tablespoon Jerez wine
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Put all in a deep bowl, beat with a spoon until no lumps remain
2. The butter should not be very hot
3. For them to turn out fine, heat the mold in the butter and dip in the batter, return to the butter with violence
4. Pour syrup over them
