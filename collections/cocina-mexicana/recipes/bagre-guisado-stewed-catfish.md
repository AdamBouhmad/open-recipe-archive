## Ingredients

- Catfish portions, fried
- Oil
- Garlic, finely chopped
- Onion, finely chopped
- Ground tomato
- Tomatillos, quartered
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. After frying the catfish in portions, put a pot with oil on the heat
2. Fry finely chopped garlic and onion in it, and when they have browned, add ground tomato, tomatillos in quarters, and a little clove, cinnamon and pepper ground
3. Add a little water and the portions of catfish, let the sauce season and serve hot
