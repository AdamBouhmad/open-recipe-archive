## Ingredients

- syrup at point
- orange flower water
- toasted sesame
- ground cinnamon
- ricotta
- mamey
- milk
- custard
- ground sugar
- butter flowers
- gragea
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. When the syrup is at point, add orange flower water, toasted sesame and ground cinnamon until it turns a little brown
2. Dissolve the ricotta there so that it forms a loose paste
3. Place on a platter a layer of mamey sprinkled with milk, another of custard prepared and beaten with ground and sifted sugar, and another of said ricotta
4. On top decorate with butter flowers drawn through a napkin, some covered with ground cinnamon, others with gragea and others with powdered sugar
