## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Butter
- Chiles
- Spices
- Minced and pounded poultry meat
- Partridges

## Directions

1. Cut the sweetbreads into large pieces.
2. Brown them in butter with chiles and spices.
3. Mix with minced and pounded poultry meat.
4. Stuff the partridges with this mixture.
5. Cook and serve with a sweetbread sauce.
