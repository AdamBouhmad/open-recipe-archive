## Ingredients

- hens
- raw meat loins
- onions
- tomatoes
- garlic
- pineapple
- butter
- parsley
- cloves
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- vinegar
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- chiles
- olives
- candied citron
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut the hens into quarters and slice the raw meat loins with onions, tomatoes, garlic and pineapple.
2. Grease a pot with butter, and place a layer of the said mixture, adding parsley, then another layer of hen, loin and ham, all raw, alternating this way until the pot is full.
3. To each layer add a pinch of cloves, cinnamon, plenty of pepper and vinegar.
4. Place the pot over two fires until it is cooked through.
5. Garnish the stew with pine nuts, raisins, almonds, capers, chiles, olives, candied citron and oil.
