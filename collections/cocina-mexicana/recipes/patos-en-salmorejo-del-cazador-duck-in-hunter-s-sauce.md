## Ingredients

- Duck breasts and legs, roasted
- Duck bones and skeletons
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- One glass of Bordeaux wine
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts and legs from roasted ducks and divide the bones and skeletons into pieces
2. Place all in a cooking pot with salt, pepper, and a little oil, one glass of Bordeaux wine, and the juice of one lemon
3. Fry everything, making it jump together
4. Serve
