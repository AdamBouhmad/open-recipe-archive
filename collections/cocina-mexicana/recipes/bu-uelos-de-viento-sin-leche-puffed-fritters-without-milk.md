## Ingredients

- Twelve caldron cups of water
- Salt
- Aniseed
- One pound of sifted flour
- Eight egg yolks
- Eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Put water with salt and aniseed in a small pot; when hot, add one pound of sifted flour and let it cook well
2. Remove from heat and add eight yolks and eight whites of egg, beating everything well together
3. Form the fritters and fry them
