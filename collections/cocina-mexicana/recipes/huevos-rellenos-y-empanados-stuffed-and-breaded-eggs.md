## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- bread prepared as for quenelles
- butter
- raw egg yolks
- salt
- spices
- bread crumb
- melted butter
- curly sauce

## Directions

1. Cut twelve eggs lengthwise slightly above the middle.
2. Remove the yolks and crush them.
3. Mix with bread (prepared as for quenelles) and butter in equal portions of each.
4. Add two or three raw yolks, salt, and a few spices.
5. Fill the egg whites with this paste.
6. Join the two parts of each egg to give them the form of whole eggs.
7. Once well joined, dust with bread crumb and sprinkle with a little melted butter.
8. Place the remaining filling on the bottom of a plate.
9. Arrange the eggs on top and put in the oven or under a covered oven to brown.
10. Serve with a few drops of curly sauce.
