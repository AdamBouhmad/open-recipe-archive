## Ingredients

- purslane
- salt
- water
- dried ancho chiles
- tomato
- garlic
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the purslane as described in previous recipes and wash it
2. Prepare a chile-garlic sauce or clemole with dried ancho chiles and tomatoes
3. Cook the purslane in this sauce
