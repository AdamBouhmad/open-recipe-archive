## Ingredients

- Wings
- Sweetbreads
- Crests
- Necks
- Livers of poultry
- Lard
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Fry the wings, sweetbreads, crests, necks and livers in lard with onion and tomatoes
2. Crush garlic, pepper, salt, cloves, cinnamon and parsley
3. When cooked and the sauce reduced, add the crushed mixture
