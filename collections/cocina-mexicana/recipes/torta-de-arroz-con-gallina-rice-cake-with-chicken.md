## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- sugar
- chicken
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- parsley
- pepper
- cumin
- vinegar
- salt
- small chiles
- olives
- six eggs

## Directions

1. After one pound of rice has been cooked and dried, place it to cool in a casserole
2. Dust with sugar so it is not too sweet
3. Cook the chicken, which should also be cooked and dried, with minced garlic, onions, tomatoes and parsley, little pepper and ground cumin, and a bit of sugar, moistening slightly with vinegar and adding salt, small chiles and olives
4. Let the seasoning rest, and when cold, in a pan prepared with butter and six well-beaten eggs, add the rice and mix with them
5. Make a layer of this and another of the chicken stew, covering with another layer of rice and any remaining egg
6. Let the cake set over two fires
