## Ingredients

- two cakes of cold biscuits
- one quart of very sweetened milk
- two tablespoons of butter
- raisins
- almonds
- citron
- five eggs

## Directions

1. Cook two cakes of cold biscuits in one quart of very sweetened milk and put the biscuits in the milk in which two tablespoons of butter, raisins, almonds and citron pieces are dispersed by hand
2. Before the milk thickens with all this it is boiled and then everything is added
3. Spread a cake pan with butter, beat five eggs as for cake and mix with the milk leaving a little egg separate
4. Then pour everything into the cake pan and on top the little egg that was set aside and put in a burner only with embers, and on the cover very strong fire so it browns
5. Put a straw in it and when it comes out clean it is ready
