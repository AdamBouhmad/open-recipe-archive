## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- water with salt
- one pound of butter
- pork meat, cooked and fried raw
- garlic, minced
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. Mix, knead, and beat one pound of flour with a little salted water.
2. When well extended, place one pound of butter in the middle and fold the dough over it, covering it well.
3. Beat slowly again, taking care to cover with flour if the dough tears.
4. After folding and beating several times, extend to the thickness of a peso coin and cut circles with a cup.
5. Make a filling with pork meat that has been cooked and fried raw, with plenty of minced garlic, onion, pepper, salt, and nutmeg.
6. Place a little filling on each circle, cover with another equal circle, and mark with a smaller cup so a small border remains.
7. Brush with egg yolk diluted in water using a feather and bake in the oven.
