## Ingredients

- Lamb necks
- Onions
- Parsnips
- Warm butter
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Small root vegetables

## Directions

1. Proceed in all respects as in the previous recipe.
2. At the moment of serving, place the necks over small root vegetables.
