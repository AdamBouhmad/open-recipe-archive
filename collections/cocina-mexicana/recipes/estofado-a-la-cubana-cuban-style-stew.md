## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef or veal mass, well washed
- four ounces of butter
- four small onions, minced
- six garlic cloves
- three raw garlic cloves
- a little parsley
- one small cinnamon stick
- a little saffron in threads
- two cloves
- some peppercorns
- half a bottle of dry or good red wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of beef or veal mass well washed; place on grills to roast, and when judged roasted, cut into pieces the size of a chestnut.
2. Prepare a pan with four ounces of butter, mince four small onions and six garlic cloves; fry them and add three raw garlic cloves, a little parsley, a small cinnamon stick, a little saffron in threads, two cloves, and some peppercorns, all well minced and crushed, placing it on top of the fried base, as well as the meat made into pieces.
3. Let it fry for about a quarter of an hour, and add half a bottle of dry or good red wine; cover the pan with an iron lid, on which a little fire is placed and after sufficient time for it to be cooked, which will be about half an hour, add a few drops of lemon juice.
