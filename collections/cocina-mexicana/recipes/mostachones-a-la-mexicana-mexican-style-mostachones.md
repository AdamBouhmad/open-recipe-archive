## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of syrup
- one cuartillo of virgin honey
- one pound of raw almonds with skin
- twenty hard biscuits
- half an ounce of clove
- one ounce of cinnamon
- thirty egg yolks
- four ounces of butter
- a little wine

## Directions

1. Mix the syrup and virgin honey together and let boil until it reaches the soft-ball stage
2. Add the raw almonds with skin, hard biscuits, clove and cinnamon, all ground
3. Add the egg yolks and butter
4. Place on the heat and stir until it pulls away from the pan
5. Let cool and form into small figures and dust with cinnamon
6. If glazed is desired, make a white icing with beaten egg whites, plenty of ground sugar, and a few drops of lemon
7. Coat the figures on one side with the icing, and when set, turn over and coat the other side
8. Place on a comal or in a warm oven
