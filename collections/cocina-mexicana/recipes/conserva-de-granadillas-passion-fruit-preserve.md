## Ingredients

- Granadillas (China passion fruit)
- Lime water (agua de cal)
- Syrup (almíbar)
- Fig leaves

## Directions

1. Remove the hard outer shell from the granadillas without damaging the white inner part, leaving the stem
2. Place them in lime water and leave them for two or three hours to acquire consistency
3. Rinse them thoroughly and scald them in boiling water
4. When removed, prick them with a thick needle so the syrup penetrates
5. Place them immediately in syrup, giving them several boils together with one or two fig leaves
6. The next day boil them more until the syrup reaches preserve point
7. Remove the fig leaves, let cool and place in preserve containers
