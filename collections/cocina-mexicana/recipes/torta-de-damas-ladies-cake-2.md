## Ingredients

- One cup of milk cream, ground after cooling
- One pound of almonds
- Half an ounce of cinnamon, also ground
- A tiny bit of clove
- A little orange blossom water
- Six well-beaten eggs
- Sufficient powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Incorporate in a pot the ground milk cream, almonds, cinnamon, clove, orange blossom water, well-beaten eggs, and sufficient powdered sugar
2. Make a gaznate dough and with it line the plate or pot
3. Pour the mixture in and put it in the oven moderately hot
4. When the cake sets, coat it with a glaze made of a little sugar with ground cinnamon
