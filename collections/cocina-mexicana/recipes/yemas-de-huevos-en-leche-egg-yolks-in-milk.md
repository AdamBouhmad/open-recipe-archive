## Ingredients

- sugar
- egg yolks
- milk
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make syrup with six pounds of sugar, clarify it and bring to coating consistency
2. Beat apart twelve egg yolks with six quartillos of milk, which after straining through a sieve is added to the syrup
3. When boiling, add half a pound of clean almonds crushed with a little water
4. Let everything boil, and returning to coating consistency, remove from heat to beat again
5. If too stringy, return to heat so it reaches high consistency
