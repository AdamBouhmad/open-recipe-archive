## Ingredients

- Lamb ribs
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Salt
- Oil
- Fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take well-aged lamb ribs, skin and remove the fat.
2. Beat well with the flat side of a machete.
3. Remove from the spine and extract the ribs, leaving one with a long handle to hold it.
4. Flatten again with the machete and coat with lemon juice, sprinkling generously with pepper and a little salt.
5. Let dry for an hour, then place in oil infusion.
6. The next day, remove and wrap the meat in paper.
7. Place on the grill over low heat.
8. When well cooked, remove the paper, roll in very finely ground bread crumbs.
9. Add a little lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
10. Place on the grill until the crust browns, then serve alone or with any preferred sauce.
