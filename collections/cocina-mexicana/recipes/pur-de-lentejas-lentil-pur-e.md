## Ingredients

- 1 pound lentils
- broth from the pot
- a little bacon
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions, thinned and sautéed in butter
- 2 egg whites
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of lentils in broth with a little bacon, two carrots, two onions thinned and sautéed in butter, and two egg whites.
2. When all is cooked, pass through a strainer.
3. Thin the purée with broth.
4. Cook in a pot to skim it, and serve over fried bread crusts.
5. This purée can be made for fast days by substituting oil for bacon.
