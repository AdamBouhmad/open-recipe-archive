## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- medium fresh cheese
- half a box of sponge cake
- raisins
- almonds
- cinnamon
- orange blossom water
- lard
- ground cinnamon for garnish
- grajea or small sweet bread for coloring

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs as for frying and mix with a medium crumbled fresh cheese, half a box of sponge cake, raisins, almonds, cinnamon and orange blossom water
2. Put everything in a well-oiled baking pan with lard
3. Let it cook over two fires
4. For serving, dust with ground cinnamon
5. For the striped version, before putting the batter in the pan, tint one part of the dough with cinnamon, another with grajea or sweet bread, and leave the other white; carefully layer the mixtures so that when cut it forms stripes of various colors
