## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley, chopped (a little)
- flour (a little)
- cloves, ground
- cinnamon, ground
- wine
- fish, cooked and cut into pieces
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a pan or casserole
2. Add chopped parsley and flour, stirring constantly
3. Add ground cloves and cinnamon and wine
4. Place cooked and cut fish in the pan
5. Pour the mixture over the fish
6. Garnish with slices or halves of hard-boiled eggs
