## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- ham slices
- beef marrow or fat or butter
- beef
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- good broth
- butter

## Directions

1. After cleaning the cauliflower carefully, parboil it in cold water and drain it in a colander
2. Line the bottom of a cooking pot with ham slices, shaped to fit the bottom of the serving plate
3. Place the cauliflower on top with the stems pointing down
4. Make a filling with beef marrow, fat or butter from beef, chopped parsley, onion, and mushrooms, all minced; season with salt and pepper without cream or broth
5. Mix this filling well and introduce it with a spoon into the hollows between the cauliflower florets, ensuring no empty spaces remain
6. Cook in good broth, seasoning the dish well so it has good flavor
7. When cooking is complete and all the broth is consumed, gently turn the pot over onto the serving plate so the cauliflower florets face up and the stems face down, covered with the ham slices that lined the pot
8. Remove these ham slices and pour good strained broth with a little butter over the dish
9. Serve as an appetizer course
