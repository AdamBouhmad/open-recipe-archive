## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- sheep's feet
- pig's ears
- salt
- cinnamon
- sugar
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Take four pig's feet, four sheep's feet and two pig's ears
2. Clean well and cook with salt
3. Once cooked, mince very well and fry with cinnamon and sugar
4. Beat eggs in the proportion for making a cake
5. Add the minced mixture and mix well
6. Pour into a cake pan buttered with lard
7. Cover and put fire on the lid
8. To check if it is done, insert a straw in the center of the cake which should come out clean
