## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Onion, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Water
- Walnuts
- Lard
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread slices
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into quarters and add ham slices and chorizos with one tablespoon of lard
2. Mince onion and garlic and fry everything together
3. Add water
4. Clean the walnuts and grind well
5. Heat lard in a pot and fry the walnuts very well
6. When fried, add to the pot with the meat
7. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper and some fried bread slices
8. Garnish the platter with chiles, olives and capers
9. The broth should be thick
