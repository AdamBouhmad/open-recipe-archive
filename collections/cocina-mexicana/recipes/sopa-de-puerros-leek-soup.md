## Ingredients

- leeks
- butter
- meat broth
- bread soup

## Directions

1. Cut the leeks into strips about one inch long
2. Wilt them in butter until they brown
3. Cook them over gentle heat in a small amount of broth
4. Place on prepared bread soup
