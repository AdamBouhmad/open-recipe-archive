## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- orange flower water
- ten egg yolks
- mamey
- cream
- ground cinnamon
- red pastille

## Directions

1. Make well-clarified syrup with one pound of sugar
2. Add orange flower water and ten egg yolks, beating everything like turron
3. Place a layer of mamey, another of egg and another of cream, all sprinkled with ground cinnamon and red pastille
4. Arrange the layers in this order as desired
5. Cover them with a comal with heat so that the last layer browns a little
