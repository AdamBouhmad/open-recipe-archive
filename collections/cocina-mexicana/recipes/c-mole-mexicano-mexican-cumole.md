## Ingredients

- mulato chile
- tomatoes
- mutton breast
- calabacitas

## Directions

1. Devein and toast the mulato chile
2. Cook tomatoes
3. Grind the chile and tomatoes together
4. Cook with mutton breast
5. When the mutton is cooked, fry it
6. Add calabacitas in pieces
7. Let season over gentle fire
