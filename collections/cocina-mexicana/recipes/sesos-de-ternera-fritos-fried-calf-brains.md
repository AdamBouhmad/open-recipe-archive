## Ingredients

- sesos de ternera (calf brains)
- agua caliente (hot water)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- tocino (bacon)
- vino blanco (white wine)
- caldo de sustancias o del puchero (meat broth or pot broth)

## Directions

1. After well preparing the brains and removing the veins, wash them in hot water with salt and vinegar, letting them cool in the same water so they harden a little
2. Cook them in a casserole lined with bacon, white wine, double the amount of meat broth or pot broth
