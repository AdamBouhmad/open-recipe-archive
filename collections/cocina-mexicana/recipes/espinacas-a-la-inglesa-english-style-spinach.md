## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- hot water
- bread, fried in butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and chop the spinach coarsely
2. Fry in butter seasoned with salt, pepper and grated nutmeg
3. Let cook on low heat
4. When done, add more butter and a little hot water
5. Remove from heat and serve with toasted bread fried in butter
