## Ingredients

- Clarified syrup
- Three pounds sugar
- One libra of clean almonds
- Pine nuts
- White wine
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Syrup and wine for moistening

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at medium-point with three pounds sugar
2. Add one libra of clean almonds and pine nuts, beating until mixed
3. Place over heat until the smell of cooked egg is perceived
4. Stir to prevent burning
5. Pour over sponge cake moistened with syrup and wine
6. Place in oven to brown
