## Ingredients

- Chayotes, cooked
- Cheese, grated if aged, or crumbled if fresh
- Egg, beaten
- Lard
- Tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked chayotes into slices.
2. Place a little grated or crumbled cheese between two slices.
3. Coat with beaten egg.
4. Fry in lard.
5. Serve as is or with tomato sauce.
