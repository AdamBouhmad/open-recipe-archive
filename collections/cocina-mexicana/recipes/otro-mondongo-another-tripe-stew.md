## Ingredients

- tripe
- lemon water
- chickpeas
- ham
- garlic head
- onion
- spices
- vinegar
- cebolletas
- ancho chile
- ham
- oregano powder
- vinegar
- olives
- capers
- pickled chilies

## Directions

1. After washing well, leave in lemon water for a good while and drain
2. Cut into pieces and place to cook with chickpeas and a piece of ham, a garlic head, a piece of onion in quarters, whole spices and a little vinegar
3. The next day make a sofrito of all the prepared ingredients with finely ground cebolletas and a soaked ancho chile
4. Pour the tripe with a little broth, adding by each portion ham, oregano powder, vinegar, olives, capers, and pickled chilies
