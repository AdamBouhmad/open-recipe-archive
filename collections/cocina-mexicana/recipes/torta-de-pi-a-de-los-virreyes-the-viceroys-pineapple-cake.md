## Ingredients

- pineapples
- clarified syrup (almíbar)
- half a pound of rice
- eggs
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Remove the hearts of the pineapples, chop them, and cook them
2. When done, place in clarified syrup
3. When they reach the proper stage, remove and drain well
4. Cook half a pound of soaked rice in a napkin hung inside a pot so it cooks
5. When well cooked, remove and let drain so no water remains
6. Beat eggs in proportion, about eight per pineapple
7. Add the drained pineapple, rice, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts to the eggs
8. Mix everything very well
9. Butter a pot and pour everything in and cook over two fires
10. When cooked, let the cake cool
11. Make small quarter-sized pieces and place in syrup
