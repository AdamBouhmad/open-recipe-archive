## Ingredients

- Lard
- Roasted and chopped tomatoes
- Roasted and chopped garlic
- Sliced onion
- Salt
- Nutmeg, grated
- Cooked chicken quarters
- Small walnuts, toasted and ground
- Cilantro, toasted and ground
- Vinegar
- A pinch of sugar

## Directions

1. Fry roasted and chopped tomatoes and garlic in lard
2. Add sliced onion
3. Season with salt and grated nutmeg
4. Place cooked chicken quarters in the pan
5. Add small ground toasted walnuts and cilantro
6. Add vinegar and a pinch of sugar
7. When everything is well seasoned, remove the chickens
8. Pour the sauce over the chickens and serve
