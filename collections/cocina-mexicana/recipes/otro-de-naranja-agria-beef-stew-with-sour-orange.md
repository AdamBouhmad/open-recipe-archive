## Ingredients

- beef, cut into thin slices
- lard
- sour orange
- vinegar
- salt
- ham slices
- chorizos
- garlic cloves
- sliced onion
- thyme
- oregano
- bay leaf
- water
- ground cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- fried bread slices
- Jerez wine
- chiles
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut beef into thin slices and place in a casserole
2. Coat with lard and squeeze a sour orange over them
3. Add a bit of vinegar and salt
4. Leave in this infusion for one day
5. Remove, dry and fry in lard with ham slices, chorizos, whole garlic cloves, sliced onion, thyme, oregano and bay leaf
6. Fry everything together and add water and vinegar, ground cloves, cinnamon, pepper and fried bread slices
7. The broth should be thick
8. Before serving, add Jerez wine and garnish with chiles, capers, etc.
