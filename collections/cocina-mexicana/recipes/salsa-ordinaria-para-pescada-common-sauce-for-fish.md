## Ingredients

- Jitomates (tomatoes)
- Onions in slices
- Parsley
- Ground spices
- Garlic
- Pickled chiles
- Vinegar
- Water
- Cooked fish slices

## Directions

1. Make a fry of jitomates, onions in slices, parsley, and ground spices, garlic, pickled chiles, vinegar, and the necessary water.
2. Add the slices of already cooked fish and finish seasoning.
