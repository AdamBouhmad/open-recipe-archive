## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, whole
- One bottle of wine
- One bottle of good vinegar
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper, ground
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Duck giblets (excluding tripe, heart, and tail)
- Water
- Salt
- Lemon, sliced
- Bread, fried and toasted

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks and cut off the tail.
2. Heat oil in a pot and fry whole garlic cloves.
3. Fry the ducks in this oil.
4. Add one bottle of wine and one bottle of good vinegar, ground clove, cinnamon and pepper, bay leaf, thyme, and duck giblets (excluding tripe, heart, and tail).
5. Add water and salt, and let reduce.
6. Brown them in the remaining fat and serve with salt, pepper, lemon slices, and fried bread toasts.
