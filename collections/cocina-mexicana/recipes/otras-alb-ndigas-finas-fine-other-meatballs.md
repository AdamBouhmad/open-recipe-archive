## Ingredients

- solid meat
- bunches of onion and parsley
- one or two eggs
- clove
- pepper powder
- salt
- soaked bread
- ham
- egg

## Directions

1. Chop solid meat and remove the skin
2. Fry separately bunches of onion and parsley
3. In the casserole mix the meat with one or two eggs, clove, a powder of pepper, salt and soaked bread
4. Grind everything well and form meatballs
5. Place a piece of ham and a piece of egg inside each one
6. Cook in broth
