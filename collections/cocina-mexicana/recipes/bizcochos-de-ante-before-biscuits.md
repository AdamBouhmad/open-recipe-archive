## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- half a quarter liter of tequezquite water
- one pound of butter
- ante (a type of paste)
- two pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Put in a pan twelve egg yolks, half a quarter liter of tequezquite water, one pound of butter and the amount of ante obtained with three fingers
2. Mix everything well and vigorously
3. Add two pounds of flour to absorb all the liquid
4. Once well kneaded, form the biscuits by spreading the dough on a board
5. Cut into shapes like cocolitos, wrap each one in paper and put in the oven
