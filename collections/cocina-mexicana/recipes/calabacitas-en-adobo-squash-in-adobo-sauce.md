## Ingredients

- wide chiles
- pasilla chiles
- garlic heads
- lard
- calabacitas (squash)
- pork broth
- salt from the earth
- cloves
- black pepper
- cumin
- pork ribs
- longaniza sausage
- lomo (loin)
- epazote
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Toast a portion of wide chiles and one quarter of pasilla chiles at the fire.
2. Grind them well with clean garlic heads.
3. Place a pot with lard on the fire and fry the chiles and garlic there.
4. When well fried, add the calabacitas cut into small quarters.
5. Add a little pork broth and salt from the earth.
6. When nearly finished cooking, add cloves, black pepper, and cumin.
7. Also add cooked pork ribs, longaniza sausage, and loin slices with a sprig of epazote.
8. When well cooked, remove from fire and serve, seasoning with a little more oregano and vinegar.
