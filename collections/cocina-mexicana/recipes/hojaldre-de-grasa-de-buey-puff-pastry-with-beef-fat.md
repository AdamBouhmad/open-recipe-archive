## Ingredients

- half a pound of clean kidney fat or beef suet
- another half pound of cow fat

## Directions

1. Clean half a pound of kidney fat or beef suet and another half pound of cow fat well
2. Pound them in a mortar
3. Pass them through a tamis or quenelles sieve
4. Knead them in cold water
5. Dry them with clean linen
6. Use this fat in place of the butter from the first puff pastry, following the same procedures otherwise (see puff pastry with butter above)
