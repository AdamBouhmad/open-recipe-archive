## Ingredients

- Six ounces of myrtle flower, or in its absence, leaves of the same shrub
- One ounce of peach leaves
- A quarter of a nutmeg in coarse powder
- Sixteen quartillos of brandy
- Four pounds of white sugar
- Eight quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Mix six ounces of myrtle flower (or leaves) with one ounce of peach leaves and a quarter nutmeg in coarse powder
2. Leave in infusion at room temperature in brandy for a very short time
3. Distill in a bain-marie to obtain half the liquor
4. Dissolve four pounds of white sugar in eight quartillos of water over heat, then cool
5. Mix with the distilled liquor and filter
