## Ingredients

- two ounces lard
- two or three tomatoes
- two tender onions
- six garlic cloves
- one hen
- one and a half jars of water
- salt
- saffron threads
- pepper
- one and a half pounds rice

## Directions

1. Prepare a casserole with two ounces of lard
2. Heat and fry two or three tomatoes, two tender onions, six garlic cloves
3. Add the hen cut into pieces and when it has softened, add one and a half jars of water
4. Season with salt and let boil until almost cooked
5. Add a few saffron threads and a pinch of pepper
6. Add one and a half pounds of rice
7. Let boil until the water is consumed and the rice is almost cooked
8. Set aside and after a quarter hour serve
