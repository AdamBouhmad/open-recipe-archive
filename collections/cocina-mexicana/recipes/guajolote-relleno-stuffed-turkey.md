## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- Ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Prunes
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of ground meat and ham with salt, pepper, a couple of eggs, parsley and garlic
2. Also add prunes
3. Fry in butter and use to stuff the turkey
4. Roast the turkey after stuffing
