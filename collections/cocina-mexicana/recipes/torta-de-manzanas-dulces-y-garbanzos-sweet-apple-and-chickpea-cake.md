## Ingredients

- sweet apples
- equal portion of chickpeas
- syrup
- cinnamon
- a little pepper
- salt
- saffron
- eggs, well beaten
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the apples, and do the same with an equal portion of chickpeas
2. Combine both and add syrup, cinnamon, a little pepper, salt and saffron, all ground
3. Mix everything with well-beaten eggs and pour into a cazuela or tortera coated with butter
4. Cook the cake over two fires and serve with ground sugar and cinnamon sprinkled on top
