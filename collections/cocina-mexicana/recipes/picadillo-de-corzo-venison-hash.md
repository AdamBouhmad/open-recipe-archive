## Ingredients

- roasted venison meat
- cooked herbs
- pepper sauce (thick)
- butter
- fried bread croutons

## Directions

1. Debone the roasted venison meat and chop it well, together with cooked herbs
2. Put everything in a thick pepper sauce or gravy with a little butter
3. Heat without boiling
4. Serve with fried bread croutons arranged around it
