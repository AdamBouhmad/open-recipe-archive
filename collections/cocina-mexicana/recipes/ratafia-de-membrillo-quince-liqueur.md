## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- two quartillos of juice
- four quartillos of refined Catalan aguardiente
- four cloves
- four adarmes of powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove skin and core from well-ripe quinces
2. Crush in a mortar
3. Place in the press to extract the juice
4. To two quartillos of juice add four of refined Catalan aguardiente, four cloves and four adarmes of powdered cinnamon
5. Place in a well-sealed jug for a month
6. After this time sweeten to taste with well-clarified common syrup and place to filter through the sleeve to bottle
