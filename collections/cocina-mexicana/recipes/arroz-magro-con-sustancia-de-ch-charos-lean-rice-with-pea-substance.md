## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- caldo magro (lean broth)
- sustancia de chícharos (pea substance)

## Directions

1. Clean the rice and wash it in four or five warm waters
2. Put it in lean broth and let it boil for about an hour and a half
3. When it is cooked, an hour and a half before serving, add the pea substance, doing so that it binds well to the rice and is neither too liquid nor too thick
