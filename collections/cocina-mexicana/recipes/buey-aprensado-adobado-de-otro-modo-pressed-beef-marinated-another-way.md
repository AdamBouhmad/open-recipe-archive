## Ingredients

- beef pulp
- water
- cloves
- fine pepper
- cinnamon
- Tabasco pepper pods, cascaras (husks)
- vinegar
- garlic
- wide chile
- more water
- vinegar
- avocado leaves
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Soak the beef pulp until it bleeds out; when it is already white, prick it and coat it with the following ground spices: cloves, fine pepper, cinnamon, and Tabasco pepper husks.
2. Grind raw garlic cloves and a wide chile in refined vinegar, spread this thick paste all over the meat, and place it in a pot with a very covered mouth.
3. After twenty-four hours, turn the meat and cover the pot mouth again.
4. After another twenty-four hours, add water and vinegar into the same pot until they cover the meat well, add avocado and bay leaves, and cover the pot mouth with a casserole, sealing the edges well with flour paste (engrudo).
5. Place over a gentle fire for many hours until it cooks; once cooked, remove it and, wrapped in clean cloths, press it.
