## Ingredients

- butter
- squabs
- flour
- vinegar
- meat broth, a small cup

## Directions

1. Fry a little butter and in it turn the squabs; add a little flour dissolved in vinegar and leave everything until the bird is well browned; then add a small cup of broth from the pot, leaving it over low heat until this little sauce is reduced by half.
