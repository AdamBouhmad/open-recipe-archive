## Ingredients

- beef pulp
- cloves
- cinnamon
- fine pepper
- water
- good vinegar, a large amount
- onion heads, 3 or 4
- ground pepper
- mint branches
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Pierce the pulp with plenty of cloves, cinnamon, and fine pepper.
2. Then place in a cooking pot with sufficient water, a large amount of good vinegar, three or four onion heads, ground pepper, some mint branches, and competent fine salt.
3. Place over two fires, and when it has consumed the broth, keep it to take from it as needed.
