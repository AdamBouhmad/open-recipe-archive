## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- salt
- clean garlic
- water
- lard
- garlic cloves
- onion
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- cooked cabbage, chopped
- chickpea broth
- saffron
- cumin
- clove
- pepper
- cooked and cleaned shrimp
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the chickpeas from one day to the next, and the following day wash and put to cook with salt and clean garlic in plenty of water, so there is no need to add it as it reduces.
2. When well cooked and tender, fry in lard garlic cloves, onion, tomatoes and some green chiles, chopped, adding afterwards cooked cabbage, also chopped, the chickpeas with the broth in which they were cooked, salt, saffron, cumin, ground clove and pepper, cooked and cleaned shrimp or toasted and ground shrimp, a bit of oil and another of vinegar.
3. Let everything boil, and if after being well seasoned the small broth is too watery, it will be thickened with chickpeas of the same ones, which will be removed to grind, and will be added after grinding to the stew.
