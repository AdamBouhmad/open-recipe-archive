## Ingredients

- selected chestnuts
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. While chestnuts are cooking over coals, make a syrup that is clarified and boiled to the point of pearl
2. Add the peeled chestnuts one by one
3. As they are removed with a spoon, place them in cold water, which will instantly crystallize or freeze the syrup
