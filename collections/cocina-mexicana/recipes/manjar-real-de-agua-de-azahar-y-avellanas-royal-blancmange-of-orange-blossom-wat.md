## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- two chicken breasts
- orange blossom water
- one pound of ground almonds or hazelnuts
- ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Make syrup with four pounds of sugar at the point of the ladle degree, add two well-cooked chicken breasts disintegrated in orange blossom water two hours before adding them, and one pound of ground almonds or hazelnuts
2. Let boil until it separates from the pan and pour into the platter
3. But if it is to serve for small pastries, as soon as the syrup has boiled with the chicken breast and almond, remove it from heat, and as it cools, add ground biscuit, beating it until it is recognized as having enough consistency to pour into the pastries, which will be adorned on top with almonds and pine nuts
