## Ingredients

- Roasted garlic
- Onions
- Capers
- Parsley
- Oil
- Flour
- White wine
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Saffron, ground
- Cooked calf's tongue
- Thin slices of bread
- Banana slices
- Butter

## Directions

1. Grind roasted garlic, onions, capers, and parsley together
2. Fry all of this in oil and remove when fried
3. In another pot with oil, brown some flour and add a glass of white wine with ground clove, cinnamon, and saffron
4. Mix the first fried mixture into this broth
5. Add the cooked tongue in slices
6. When the broth has thickened sufficiently, remove from heat and serve with thin slices of fried bread and banana fried in butter
