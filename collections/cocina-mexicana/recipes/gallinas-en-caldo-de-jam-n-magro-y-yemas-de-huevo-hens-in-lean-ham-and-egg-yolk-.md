## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Parsley (chopped)
- Hen quarters (cooked)
- Cooked egg yolks
- Lean ham
- Raisins
- Salt
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry garlic, tomato, and chopped parsley in lard.
2. Place the cooked hen quarters in this mixture.
3. Grind together cooked egg yolks, lean ham, and some raisins.
4. Add this mixture to the fried ingredients, seasoning with necessary salt, cloves, and pepper.
5. Add oil and vinegar.
6. Once everything is well bound together, remove from heat and serve.
