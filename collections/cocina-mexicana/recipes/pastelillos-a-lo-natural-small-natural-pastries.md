## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- minced filling
- parsley
- onion
- egg

## Directions

1. Flatten the puff pastry so that it is no more than two lines thick
2. Cut rounds from it with a pastry cutter or appropriate instrument like a round cutter
3. Place on each round about a hazelnut amount of minced filling mixed with finely chopped parsley and onion
4. Cover with other rounds of dough of equal size to those below
5. Brush with a feather brush dipped in beaten egg
6. Put in the oven and remove after half an hour
