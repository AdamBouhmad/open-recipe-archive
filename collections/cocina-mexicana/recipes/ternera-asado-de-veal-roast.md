## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- Vinegar
- Garlic cloves, crushed
- Water
- Salt
- Lard
- Garlic cloves, chopped
- Tomatoes
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cumin
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Broth
- Bread
- Eggs, cooked
- Olives
- Lettuce leaves
- Celery stalks

## Directions

1. After beating the veal pulp, cook it from one day to the next with vinegar, some crushed garlic cloves, and sufficient water and salt
2. The next day fry some chopped garlic cloves in lard, and when they have browned, add tomatoes and a greater quantity of cooked and ground tomatoes, letting them become very fried
3. Then season with salt, cinnamon, cloves, black pepper, and a little cumin, all ground, and add some bay leaves, thyme, marjoram, and a little broth
4. Slice the veal and add it to the said sauce, letting it almost dry over a very low fire
5. For serving, garnish the roast with slices of bread browned in lard, cooked eggs, and if desired, olives and white lettuce leaves or thin celery stalks curled in water
