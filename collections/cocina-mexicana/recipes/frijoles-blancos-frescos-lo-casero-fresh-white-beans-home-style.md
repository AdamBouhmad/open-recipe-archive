## Ingredients

- White beans
- boiling water
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- vinegar or verjuice, or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Add beans to boiling water with salt and a little butter.
2. Refresh frequently with cold water.
3. When cooked, drain them.
4. Place in a pan with a good piece of butter, sauté, and add chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and a little vinegar, verjuice, or better yet, lemon juice.
