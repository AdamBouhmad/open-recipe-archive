## Ingredients

- Ten to twelve red tunas or pitayas
- Water
- Cloves (two or three)
- Peppercorns (two or three)
- Cinnamon
- Twenty-five quartillos of pulque
- Sugar (one and a half pounds)

## Directions

1. Peel the red tunas or pitayas
2. Boil them with a little water, ground cloves, peppercorns, and cinnamon
3. When well boiled, remove from fire and cool
4. Add to twenty-five quartillos of pulque with one and a half pounds of sugar
5. Stir everything well
6. Strain
