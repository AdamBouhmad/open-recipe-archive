## Ingredients

- Wide chile peppers
- Tomatillos, cooked
- Lard
- Pork broth
- Salt
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Cumin
- Onion
- Oregano
- Pork meat
- Sugar
- Sweet potato slices
- Apple slices
- Pineapple slices
- Banana slices

## Directions

1. Devein wide chile peppers and lightly toast them over the fire, being careful not to burn them.
2. Soak them and grind with cooked tomatillos.
3. Fry this in lard, then add the pork broth.
4. Season with necessary salt and a little ground clove, cinnamon, and cumin.
5. Add an onion in quarters and a little sprinkled oregano.
6. Add the pork meat and after boiling a little, add sugar so it becomes slightly sweet.
7. Then add slices of sweet potato, apple, pineapple, and banana, letting it boil until it has taken on a regular consistency.
