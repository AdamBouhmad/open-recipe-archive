## Ingredients

- Three pounds of fine clarified sugar
- twenty-eight cooked and sifted egg yolks
- six ounces of ground almond
- two raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clarify three pounds of fine sugar
2. Cook and sift twenty-eight egg yolks
3. Combine the sugar with the egg yolks and six ounces of ground almond
4. Mix in two raw egg yolks
5. Cook until it sets in water
6. Add orange blossom water and flavoring
