## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- ginger
- thyme
- oregano
- wine
- vinegar
- pork loin
- lard
- water
- olives
- small chiles

## Directions

1. Make adobo of deveined, soaked and ground ancho and pasilla chiles with sufficient garlic, cumin, ginger, thyme, oregano, wine and vinegar
2. Marinate the pork loin, whole or in portions
3. The next day, add water and lard
4. Cook over two fires as in the previous recipes
5. When ready to serve, garnish with olives and small chiles
