## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- celery
- broth
- salt
- clove
- ground pepper
- lard
- flour
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry in a pan with butter, minced parsley, onion and celery
2. Add a little broth and season with salt and a little ground clove and pepper
3. Place the chops there and let them cook slowly over moderate heat
4. In another pan, prepare the cream by frying lard and a little flour so it does not brown
5. Add sufficient milk, seasoned with ground clove and pepper and necessary salt, stirring until it thickens like atole
6. In this case add three egg yolks for each quart of milk used
7. Cover the chops with this sauce, which should be rolled in bread crumbs
8. When about to serve, fry in very hot lard
