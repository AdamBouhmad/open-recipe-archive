## Ingredients

- three pounds of clarified sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- wine
- cinnamon
- clove

## Directions

1. Add the egg yolks to the clarified sugar
2. The syrup should be thick and cold with wine, cinnamon and clove mixed in
3. Add this to the syrup and boil again
4. When thick place a layer of sponge cake and another of eggs
5. Then stud with cloves
