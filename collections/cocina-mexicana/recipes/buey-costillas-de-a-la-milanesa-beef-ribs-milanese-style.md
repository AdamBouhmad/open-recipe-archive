## Ingredients

- Beef ribs or loin
- Ham strips heavily seasoned with spices for larding
- One quarter of Madeira wine
- One quarter of broth
- Silk sieve for straining

## Directions

1. Prepare the rib or loin as in the previous recipe, taking care to use more spices in the ham strips used for larding
2. Add one quarter Madeira wine and one quarter broth
3. Allow to cook
4. Pass the cooking liquid through a silk sieve, removing all fat
5. Let it reduce so only one glass remains to pour over the rib
