## Ingredients

- trout
- flour
- butter
- salt
- bread slices
- cooked lettuce hearts
- pepper powder
- orange juice
- small chiles
- olives
- large capers

## Directions

1. Clean the trout and divide into portions
2. Coat in flour and fry in butter with salt; also fry bread slices in the same fat
3. Remove both and set aside
4. Fry cooked, chopped lettuce hearts in the fat and remove
5. On a platter, make a layer of fried bread slices, then a layer of chopped lettuce, then a layer of trout portions, ensuring each piece rests on a bread slice
6. Season each with pepper powder and orange juice, and decorate with small chiles, olives and large capers
