## Ingredients

- Hare
- hare liver
- parsley
- onion
- fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- ham

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing like that of the previous article and add the hare's liver, which will have been crushed separately and passed through a sieve.
2. Put this stuffing in a mold lined with ham slices, and let it cook in a bain-marie; that is, inside another vessel with water, taking care to cover the mold and to put fire on top of the lid.
3. When the hare loaf is cooked, it will be arranged by placing a plate on the mold for removal and turning it over afterwards.
4. It sets on the front part and is served on game essence.
