## Ingredients

- black cacahuatzentli corn
- lard
- salt or sugar
- warm water
- rice and milk filling, made in the ordinary way but somewhat more loaded with sweetness
- corn husks

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the tamales in the preceding articles, with the difference that the cacahuatzentli corn must be black, and that they are filled with rice and milk prepared in the ordinary way, though somewhat more loaded with sweetness
2. Form and cook the same as the others
