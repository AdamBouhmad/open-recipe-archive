## Ingredients

- Four and a half pounds of blackberries
- Mace
- Aguardiente
- Sugar
- River water

## Directions

1. Crush four and a half pounds of blackberries and infuse the juice with half a dram of mace in nutmeg and six quartillos of aguardiente for fifteen to eighteen days
2. Then dissolve three and a half pounds of sugar in two and a half quartillos of river water
3. Decant this honey and mix with the aguardiente
4. Filter the liqueur and bottle it
