## Ingredients

- thirty pits of large black wild cherries, pulp removed but unwashed
- four quartillos of spirit of wine
- two and a half pounds of the finest sugar
- four quartillos of water
- two quartillos of rum

## Directions

1. Place the thirty cherry pits in infusion for one month in four quartillos of spirit of wine
2. After the infusion time, filter the liquor through blotting paper
3. Sweeten it with clarified syrup made with two and a half pounds of the finest sugar
4. Add four quartillos of water and two quartillos of rum
5. Shake the mixture well
6. When it is perfectly blended, bottle it
