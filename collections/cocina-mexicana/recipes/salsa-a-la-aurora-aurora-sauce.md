## Ingredients

- thick curled sauce
- 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
- black pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a saucepan with thick curled sauce, add the lemon juice, pepper, and grated nutmeg
2. Pass the hard-boiled egg yolks through a colander or skimmer to form a kind of noodles
3. At the moment of serving, when the sauce is very hot, add these yolks to it, being careful that it does not boil and that it has the necessary salt
