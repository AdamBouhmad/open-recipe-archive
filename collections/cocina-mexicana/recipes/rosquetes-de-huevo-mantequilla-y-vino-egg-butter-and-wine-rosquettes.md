## Ingredients

- half a cuartillo of wine
- three ounces of butter
- forty-two egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- one-eighth of a cuartillo of aguardiente
- about three pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Heat the wine with three ounces of butter
2. Beat forty-two egg yolks and mix with a pinch of salt and one-eighth of a cuartillo of aguardiente
3. Combine the mixtures
4. Add flour until about three pounds is incorporated
5. Knead this dough thoroughly until small holes appear when cut with a knife
6. Form rosquettes and bake immediately in the oven
7. Once baked, cover with syrup at the point of coating and dust with cinnamon
