## Ingredients

- Calf sweetbread meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Cloves
- Vinegar
- Vinaigrette sauce

## Directions

1. Pass the meat through boiling water after salting it lightly
2. Cool it in fresh water
3. Cook with salt, pepper, a few onions, some cloves and a glass of vinegar
4. Cook thoroughly for about three hours
5. Serve hot from the pot with ravigote sauce
6. At the table, place vinaigrettes, mustard and finely chopped herbs so each person can adjust the sauce to their taste
