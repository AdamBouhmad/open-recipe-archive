## Ingredients

- four beef brains
- white wine marinade
- mushrooms
- German sauce
- parsley bouquet
- four ounces fine butter
- lemon juice
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Clean four brains, blanch them and cook in a white wine marinade
2. Divide into sixteen pieces, removing two from each half
3. Arrange in crown form on a plate
4. At the moment of serving, bring mushrooms to a boil in thickened German sauce
5. Add a bouquet of parsley, four ounces of fine butter, stirring well in the sauce so it does not turn to oil
6. Add a good amount of lemon juice and black pepper
7. Serve very hot
