## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of fresh butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt
- pepper
- a little water
- a little vinegar or lemon juice
- parsley, finely chopped
- a little nutmeg
- one or two egg yolks (optional, for thickening)

## Directions

1. Put the butter kneaded with flour, salt and pepper in a saucepan
2. Dissolve it with a little water and stir until it is bound, without letting it boil
3. When removed from the fire, add a little vinegar or lemon juice, chopped parsley, and a little nutmeg
4. If a thicker sauce is desired, bind it with one or two egg yolks
