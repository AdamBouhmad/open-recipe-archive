## Ingredients

- common puchero
- old chicken
- piece of lean beef
- piece of veal
- 2 carrots
- onions
- leeks
- bundle of aromatic herbs
- spice clarified
- water

## Directions

1. Add to the common puchero an old chicken, a piece of lean beef and another of veal, two carrots, onions, leeks, a bundle of aromatic herbs, clarified spice, and cook until reduced to one third.
2. Remove the fat.
