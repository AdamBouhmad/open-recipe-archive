## Ingredients

- Beef pulp
- Fat ham
- Fine salt
- Carrots
- Onions
- Fine spices
- Broth
- White wine

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with fat ham
2. Place to cook with necessary fine salt, clean carrots and onions
3. Add fine spices
4. Moisten with broth and white wine
5. Cover the mouth of the pot
6. Leave to cook on gentle and prolonged fire
7. Once sufficiently cooked, remove from fire
8. Pass the broth through a sieve and serve
