## Ingredients

- lard
- mulato chiles
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- fried bread
- fish

## Directions

1. Fry two mulato chiles in lard and grind them with the same lard
2. Fry garlic until transparent, then onion and tomato
3. Add the chile, a piece of fried and ground bread to thicken
4. Place the fish in this sauce
