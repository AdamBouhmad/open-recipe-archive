## Ingredients

- one pound of soaked and finely ground rice
- eight cuartillos of milk
- sugar as needed
- twenty-four egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- two cloves
- four ounces of clean pounded almonds
- a little white wine

## Directions

1. Add one pound of soaked and finely ground rice to eight cuartillos of milk with the necessary sugar
2. Strain everything through a sieve, and adding twenty-four egg yolks, some cinnamon sticks and two cloves, place over heat without ceasing to stir and shortly after add four ounces of clean pounded almonds
3. When it separates from the pan as you stir it, remove it from heat, add a little white wine and pour it into a platter
