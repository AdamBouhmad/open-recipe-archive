## Ingredients

- quince, peeled, sliced, and cored
- salt water
- two guavas for every pound of quince
- one pound and four ounces of sugar for every pound of quince

## Directions

1. Peel the quince, slice it, remove the heart, and soak in salt water then cook
2. For each pound of quince, mix in two guavas
3. Squeeze and sift the mixture
4. Put in clarified syrup made with one pound and four ounces of sugar for every pound of quince
5. Leave over heat until it reaches the proper point
