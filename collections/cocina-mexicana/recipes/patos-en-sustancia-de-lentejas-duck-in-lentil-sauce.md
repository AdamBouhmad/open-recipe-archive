## Ingredients

- duck
- ham slices
- beef pulp slices
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- small onions
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the duck as for pan-fried duck
2. Place ham slices at the bottom of a casserole with the duck on top, other slices of beef pulp, two carrots, three onions, two cloves, a bay leaf, some sprigs of thyme and a bundle of parsley and small onions
3. Cover the duck with the slices, add a ladleful of broth and cook
4. Three quarters of an hour is sufficient for complete cooking if the duck is tender
5. At the moment of serving, drain, untie, arrange on a plate and cover with lentil sauce.
