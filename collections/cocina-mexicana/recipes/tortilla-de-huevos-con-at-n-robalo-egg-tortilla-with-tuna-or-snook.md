## Ingredients

- two fish roe from carp for six people, or eight from juil if carp roe is not available
- fresh tuna, or in its absence well-desalted snook
- minced garlic cloves
- butter to taste
- parsley
- onion
- lemon juice
- twelve fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Lightly cook the well-washed roe, placing them in already boiling slightly salted water for five minutes
2. Take a piece of fresh tuna and minced garlic cloves very finely
3. Mince the roe and the fish so that it mixes well with the rest
4. Put everything in a pot with a little butter and let it sauté until well melted
5. Take another bit of butter to taste and mix it with parsley and onion, place in a fish baking dish sprinkling it with lemon juice, then place immediately on hot ashes
6. Beat twelve fresh eggs with the sautéed fish and egg mixture; stir until the mixture is perfect, and make the tortilla in the customary manner
7. Ensure three things: first, it is necessary to sauté the roe and fish when frying them, avoiding boiling so they do not harden; second, the fish baking dish should be deep so that the sauce can be served with a spoon; third and last, heat the fish baking dish that will receive the dish and place it on the table so it can retain its temperature for a long time without the butter setting from cold
