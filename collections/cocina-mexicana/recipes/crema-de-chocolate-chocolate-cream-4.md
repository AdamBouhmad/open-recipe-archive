## Ingredients

- chocolate
- water
- sugar
- cream
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dissolve chocolate in water in the proportion of one glass per pound
2. Add a little sugar and mix with cream, milk and egg yolks in the proportion of six for each one of cream or milk
