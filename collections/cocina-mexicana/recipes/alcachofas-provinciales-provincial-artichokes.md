## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Salt
- Oil
- Garlic cloves
- Bread
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After trimming the artichokes and cleaning them well, place them to cook with the corresponding salt.
2. When well cooked, remove and let cool, take out the center leaves, and then extract the fuzzy center well.
3. Rearrange the leaves back, and place a saucepan on the heat with sufficient oil, fry in it some clean garlic cloves.
4. When ready, remove them from the oil and arrange the artichokes in it.
5. Grind the garlic with a piece of bread soaked in vinegar, and this mixture dissolve in a little oil and add it to the saucepan.
6. When the oil in the saucepan is consumed, moisten the artichokes with a bit of vinegar, sprinkling them from time to time with bread crumbs.
7. Once the artichokes have taken on color, remove them, and placed on a small plate with the bases facing upward, sprinkle them with salt and pepper, then roll them in the lard or oil in which they were fried.
