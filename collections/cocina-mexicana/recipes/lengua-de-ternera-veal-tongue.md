## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- butter
- ham
- chorizo
- broth
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Cook the veal tongue with corresponding salt
2. Chop plenty of tomato, onion and garlic
3. Fry with plenty of butter in a pot with ham slices and chorizo
4. When well fried, slice the tongue and add to the pot
5. Add some of the broth in which the tongue cooked, a bit of parsley, oregano and thyme
6. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
7. The sauce should be thick and garnish with chiles and olives
