## Ingredients

- one quarter ounce of vanilla, cut into small pieces
- three egg yolks
- two quartillos of good milk
- two quartillos of custard
- four ounces of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Cut a quarter ounce of vanilla into small pieces
2. Dissolve three egg yolks in two quartillos of good milk, adding the same amount of custard and four ounces of ground sugar
3. Combine everything and cook over low heat, stirring with a spatula or wooden spoon
4. When the cream reaches the proper point, strain and serve
