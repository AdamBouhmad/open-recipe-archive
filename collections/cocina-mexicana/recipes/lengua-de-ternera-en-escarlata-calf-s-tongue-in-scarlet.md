## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Saltpeter, powdered
- Clove
- Whole black pepper
- Basil
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Salt, in abundance
- Water
- Carrots
- Onions
- Whole pepper
- A little brine

## Directions

1. Prepare and parboil the tongue in boiling water
2. Dry it and rub it thoroughly with powdered saltpeter until well impregnated
3. Place it in an earthenware vessel with clove, whole pepper, basil, bay leaf, and thyme
4. Pour very salty boiling water over it
5. Let it soak there for six days
6. After this time, remove it and cook it in eight quartillos of water with carrots, onions, thyme, basil, bay leaf, whole pepper, clove, and a little brine
7. When done, transfer it to another earthenware pot with the broth and let everything cool together
