## Ingredients

- 1 pound meat
- 3 cuartillos water
- salt
- carrot
- turnip
- parsley
- clove
- garlic
- onion
- sugar (optional)
- poultry giblets (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of meat in 3 cuartillos of water with salt, letting it cook slowly and skimming well.
2. Then add some carrot, turnip, parsley, clove, garlic and a toasted onion, or alternatively a little burnt sugar to give color to the broth.
3. Cook five hours over low heat.
4. With the broth, after straining, make the soup.
5. Some poultry giblets may also be added.
