## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour (1 pound per portion)
- 3 eggs
- some sifted sugar
- some tequesquite
- water as needed
- melted and cooled lard

## Directions

1. In one portion of 1 pound flour, add 3 eggs, some sifted sugar, some tequesquite, and water as needed.
2. In another portion of 1 pound flour, mix with only melted and cooled lard.
3. Combine both portions and knead together, gradually adding lard until soft, workable, and able to be extended with a rolling pin.
4. When extended to regular thickness, cut with a knife to desired size.
5. Fill with meat or fish and finish as the previous recipes, adding lattice patterns made from the same meat.
