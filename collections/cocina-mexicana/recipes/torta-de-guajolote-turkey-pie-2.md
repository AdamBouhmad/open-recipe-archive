## Ingredients

- guajolote (turkey)
- water
- salt
- garlic
- laurel
- thyme
- lard
- ground garlic
- picadillo
- capers
- olives
- chile peppers in quarters
- chorizo slices
- ham strips
- pepper
- cooked egg quarters
- parsley leaves
- large capers

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey and cook it whole in water with salt, crushed garlic, laurel, and thyme
2. Once cooked, remove it and carefully peel off all the skin on a table, starting from the neck, except for the wings
3. Make slices from the breasts and thighs
4. Coat a cazuela well with lard and place the turkey skin with its exterior facing the center
5. Coat it with burned lard and ground garlic, and arrange the thigh slices on it
6. Over these place the picadillo, adding capers, olives, chile peppers in quarters, chorizo slices, ham strips, all cooked, and pepper
7. Over this place the breast slices, well soaked in the lard with ground garlic
8. Cover everything with the same turkey skin that should remain outside the cazuela
9. When covered, coat well with the same lard and place in a moderate oven or over two gentle fires, taking care it does not burn
10. When done, decorate with cooked egg quarters, parsley leaves, large capers, olives, and chile peppers in quarters
