## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of almond
- four ounces of rice
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten four cuartillos of milk and place over heat
2. Add four ounces of almond and four ounces of rice, both ground
3. When it reaches the proper point, remove from heat
4. Before it cools, mix in cinnamon powder as needed for color
5. Pour into serving dishes
