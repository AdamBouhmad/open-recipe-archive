## Ingredients

- ribs
- picadillo
- wine
- vinegar
- lard
- garlic
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- parsley
- paper

## Directions

1. Cut pieces that have two ribs each
2. Remove the bone from one and leave the bone in the other
3. In the thickest part of the meat, make a hollow with a knife and fill with well-seasoned picadillo
4. Sew it so the picadillo does not fall out
5. Coat the whole piece with wine, vinegar, and lard in which garlic has been fried
6. Cover with bread crumbs mixed with ground garlic, salt, pepper, clove, cinnamon and finely chopped parsley
7. Wrap each piece in a paper spread with lard
8. Place in a baking dish also spread with lard
9. Put in the oven
