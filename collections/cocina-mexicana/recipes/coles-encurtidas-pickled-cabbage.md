## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- juniper berries
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Layer the cabbage in thirds
2. Cover all with whole leaves, a cloth on top and a wooden cover pressed down with a stone or other very heavy object
3. Four or five days after the first operation, remove the spigot and let the brine drain to renew it, repeating the same procedure until it comes out clear and without any smell
4. The barrel of pickled cabbage should be accommodated in a place of moderate temperature all year; and from the moment you begin to use the pickle, remove the brine every month at the latest to add new brine, taking above all great care to keep the barrel covered by means of the cover
5. Some add juniper berries and caraway seeds, mixing them at the time of adding the salt with the cabbages
