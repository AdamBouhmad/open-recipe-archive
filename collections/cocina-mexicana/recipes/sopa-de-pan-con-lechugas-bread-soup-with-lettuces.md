## Ingredients

- Common bread soup base
- Lettuces
- Ham pieces
- Whole onions
- Carrots
- Butter
- Cooking oil
- Clean, chopped garlic
- Fat broth
- Good vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare common bread soup
2. Clean the lettuces well and without removing the crown, blanch them in boiling water and refresh in cold water
3. Remove, squeeze out water, tie with string so they do not fall apart, and cook with pieces of ham, whole onions and carrots
4. Remove and squeeze again, taking care they do not fall apart
5. Put some butter and cooking oil in a pot where clean, chopped garlic will be fried
6. Add the lettuces to fry, moistening them with a little fat broth to which a bit of good vinegar has been added
7. Remove from heat before they fall apart, arrange in bundles on the soup plate, adorning it with the cooked onions, carrot skins and the lean part of the ham
