## Ingredients

- syrup at medium stage
- clove
- cinnamon
- cilantro, toasted
- vinegar
- very thin bread slices, cold
- butter or lard
- raisins
- almonds
- fried ham slices

## Directions

1. Clarify a syrup at medium stage.
2. Add clove, cinnamon, and toasted cilantro; grind everything together.
3. Add a little vinegar and remove from heat.
4. Place very thin slices of cold bread, golden in butter or lard, in a dish.
5. Add syrup, raisins, almonds, and fried ham slices on top.
6. Let season at two fires.
7. Serve with more syrup.
