## Ingredients

- about half a pound of fresh cheese
- two handfuls of flour
- two eggs
- hot lard
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Crumble about half a pound of fresh cheese, soak it in water to desalt it, and after straining grind it
2. Mix it with two handfuls of flour and two eggs
3. Form the rings with this dough
4. Fry them in hot lard, making them take the color desired, dark or light
5. Drain them and put them in hot water, drying them by hand when removed from it
6. Put them in clarified syrup that has been prepared
7. Let it boil with them until it has the consistency or point of preserve
8. Remove from heat, put the rings on a plate and serve them dusted with cinnamon
