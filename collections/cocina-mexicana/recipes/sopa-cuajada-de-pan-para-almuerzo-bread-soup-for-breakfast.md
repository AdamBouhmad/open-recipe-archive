## Ingredients

- bread slices fried in butter
- cinnamon
- seasoned broth from the olla
- egg yolks, beaten
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place bread slices fried in butter in a pot
2. Sprinkle with cinnamon
3. Add seasoned broth from the olla as needed so the bread becomes well sponged
4. Cover with beaten egg yolks
5. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top
6. Place the pot in the steam of an olla, covering it with a comal with hot coals on top
