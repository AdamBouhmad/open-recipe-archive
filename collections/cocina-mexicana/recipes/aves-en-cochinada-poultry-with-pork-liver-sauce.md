## Ingredients

- pork liver
- roasted almonds with skin
- toasted bread soaked in broth
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Mash the pork liver with roasted almonds with skin and toasted bread soaked in broth
2. After mashing well, strain it
3. Season with clove, cinnamon, pepper and nutmeg
4. Give it a boil with sugar and lemon
5. It is ready to be served with any roast, especially poultry
