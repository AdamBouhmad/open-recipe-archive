## Ingredients

- dried wide chile
- garlic
- onion
- eggs
- dark beans
- white beans
- squash
- bean sprouts
- peas
- quelites
- chard
- potatoes
- shrimp
- bread
- cheese
- lard

## Directions

1. Soak dried wide chile, devein and grind with garlic
2. Fry onion and add the chile to fry
3. Scramble some eggs and when cooked add stewed dark and white beans, cooked squash, stir everything
4. Add bean sprouts, peas, quelites, chard and chopped cooked potatoes
5. Make small tortillas of shrimp or bread and cheese and add to the stew
6. Burn some lard and pour on top
