## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- butter
- grated cheese (Parmesan, Flemish, or from the Astillero)
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower as described in the previous recipe
2. Make a sauce with butter in which incorporate a handful of good grated cheese
3. Arrange the cauliflower on a plate and coat evenly with this sauce, covering completely
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and an equal amount of grated cheese
5. Place in the oven to brown
