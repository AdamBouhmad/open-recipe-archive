## Ingredients

- A little less than a pound and a half of flour
- A little less than a quarter pound of butter
- Half a pound of peeled almonds, well ground
- Two chicken breasts, very finely ground
- A few drops of edible oil
- A little lukewarm water with settled tequesquite

## Directions

1. Mix everything well, adding liquid if the dough is too stiff
2. Form the pastry dough from this mixture
