## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- Castilian vinegar
- cloves
- pepper
- garlic
- ham pieces
- oil
- flour
- lard
- bay leaves
- thyme
- lemon juice
- salt
- thick paper
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak the meat in cold water for eight to ten hours, changing the water several times so it becomes well desalted
2. Sprinkle with flour
3. Pound the meat strongly with the flat side of a cleaver
4. Place a cooking pot with sufficient lard and fry the leg until it browns
5. Once browned, cover the leg with water
6. Grind cloves, pepper and cinnamon, and add along with three or four bay leaves and a little vinegar to the pot
7. Place fire in a comal and add two bruised heads of garlic, placing the fire over it and letting it boil, turning the meat from time to time
8. When the broth is consumed, let the meat brown and serve with tender lettuce leaves and one of the sauces explained in its place
