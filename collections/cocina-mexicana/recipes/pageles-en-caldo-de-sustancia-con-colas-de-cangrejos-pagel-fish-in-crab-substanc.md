## Ingredients

- Pagel fish
- Crab substance
- Crab tails
- Salt
- Broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the pageles as in the previous article.
2. Prepare the crab substance.
3. Cook the crab tails in salted broth.
4. Drain the pageles and arrange them on a plate, placing the cooked tails over them, and on top of all the crab substance.
