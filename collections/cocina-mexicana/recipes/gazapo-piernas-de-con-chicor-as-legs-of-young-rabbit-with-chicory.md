## Ingredients

- Rabbit legs
- Ham
- Leftovers of roasted meats
- Onions
- Carrots
- Bouquet of herbs
- Broth
- Gelatin

## Directions

1. Debone the rabbit legs and replace the bones with ham
2. Sew the meat and cook in a casserole with slices of ham, leftovers of roasted meats, onions, carrots and a bouquet of herbs
3. Moisten everything with broth, cover with a wheel of buttered paper, and let it simmer over a fairly lively fire for two hours
4. Drain the legs afterward, cover with gelatin and serve over chicory substance
