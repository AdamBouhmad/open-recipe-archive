## Ingredients

- Ripe pomegranates
- Water
- Two pounds of sugar for every seventeen ounces of pomegranate liquid

## Directions

1. Take ripe pomegranates and extract the grains
2. Crush the grains
3. Put them in a proportionate amount of river water and cook for several minutes
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Let settle and extract the liquid by decantation
6. For every seventeen ounces of pomegranate liquid, add two pounds of sugar
7. Cook, skimming the foam
