## Ingredients

- Exotes (green beans)
- Water
- Black butter
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Parboil the exotes in boiling water and remove them to drain in a colander
2. Arrange them on a plate seasoning with salt and black pepper
3. Pour black butter over them
4. Alternatively, brown a little butter in the saucepan, add the well-drained exotes and sauté them over high heat
5. Season before arranging on the plate and sprinkle with a little vinegar
