## Ingredients

- Monacillo flowers
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the green calyx and the small stem from the middle, remove the petals
2. Make syrup at the same point and add some monacillo flowers without petals so it gives color and they remain
3. In a small amount set apart boil the remaining monacillos
4. These are squeezed with a serviette in the remaining syrup and boil to the point of jelly with its flavorings
