## Ingredients

- prepared and cooked lamb tongues
- fine herbs
- ham
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the lamb tongues over coals
2. Divide them in half lengthwise
3. Place them on a plate
4. Pour finely chopped and fried fine herbs over them
5. When everything is cold, wrap the half tongues with their seasoning in oiled paper
6. Place a small slice of ham underneath the tongue and on each side
7. Fold the papers so the seasoning cannot escape
8. Place over fire to brown them
9. Arrange to serve in the form of a crown
