## Ingredients

- hens
- ham slices
- clove
- garlic
- pepper
- cinnamon
- wide chiles
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- lard
- hen broth or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pluck the hens dry and clean them
2. Lard the breasts with slices of ham, whole cloves, garlic, pepper and cinnamon
3. After one or two days of being killed, they will be cooked in the following broth
4. Devein and wash very well six wide chiles for each hen
5. Clean garlic, and grind it very well with the chiles, clove and pepper
6. Fry the ground mixture in a little lard with two pieces of pork loin and some large chorizos
7. Then add hen broth or water with salt
8. When it boils a little, add the hens, remove from heat, and add olives and small chiles
9. Accompany this sauce to the hens, which will be adorned with slices of the chorizo and pork loin that were fried with them and with minced parsley, whole lettuce hearts and sliced hard boiled eggs
