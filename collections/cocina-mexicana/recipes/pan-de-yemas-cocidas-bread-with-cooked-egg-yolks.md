## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- eight cooked egg yolks
- salt to taste
- sugar to taste
- melted lard

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with eight cooked egg yolks, salt and sugar to taste
2. Knead with melted lard until it does not stick to the hands
3. Extend in a casserole dish greased with butter
4. Cover the casserole with a comal with fire on top to cook
5. Once done, add the minced filling of your choice
6. Cover with the dough and put between two fires to cook
