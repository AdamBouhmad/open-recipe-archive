## Ingredients

- foie gras
- ground sweetbreads
- one pound beef udder, cooked
- soaked bread
- five eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Partially fry the foie gras and let cool
2. Place one pound of cooked beef udder inside
3. Pound everything together in a mortar, adding soaked bread
4. Pass everything through a fine sieve
5. Add five eggs to the mixture
6. Butter a round mold resembling a cheese mold
7. Line the bottom with a buttered paper round exactly the size of the mold
8. Pour the sieved filling into the mold
9. Press well by dropping the mold firmly on a table
10. Cover with another buttered paper round
11. Cook in a bain-marie with high heat on top for one and a half hours before serving
12. Unmold onto a plate
13. Serve with earthnut truffle sauce
