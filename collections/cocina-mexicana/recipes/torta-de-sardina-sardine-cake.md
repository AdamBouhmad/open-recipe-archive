## Ingredients

- Ripe tomato
- Onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- Sardines
- Black pepper
- Capers
- Eggs
- Lettuce
- Onion slices

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a ripe tomato, onion, and parsley and fry in butter
2. Remove the bones and skin from the sardines and cut into pieces
3. Add them to the tomato mixture with black pepper and capers
4. Beat eggs and add to the mixture, stirring until incorporated
5. Heat butter in a pan and fry the cake, turning it on both sides
6. Serve on a plate decorated with lettuce and onion slices
