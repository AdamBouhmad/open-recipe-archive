## Ingredients

- veal pulp
- lard
- onion
- chorizos
- ham
- garlic
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- cilantro
- water
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- bread
- oil
- olives
- chiles
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut the pulp into slices and place in a cazuela with a spoonful of lard, minced onion, chorizos, ham, garlic, parsley, oregano, minced thyme and a little fresh cilantro
2. Fry everything and add water, grind clove, pepper, cinnamon and some slices of bread fried in lard
3. It should be brought to the table with thick broth, adding beforehand a little oil
4. Garnish with olives, chiles and capers
