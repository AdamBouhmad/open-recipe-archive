## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- One and one-half quarts of milk
- Twenty-four ground almonds
- Cornstarch (what can be taken with three fingers)
- A little flour
- One stick of cinnamon
- Eight egg yolks
- Half a pound of powdered sugar
- Orange tree leaves

## Directions

1. Make the pastry with puff pastry dough (as for common pastries)
2. In the milk, add the ground almonds, sifted cornstarch, a little flour, cinnamon stick, egg yolks, and powdered sugar
3. Beat everything together well in a small pan and cook over low heat for about fifteen minutes
4. Add two orange tree leaves when the first boil appears
5. Use this filling for the pastry
