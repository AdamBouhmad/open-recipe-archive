## Ingredients

- claveles deshojados (carnation petals)
- azúcar (sugar)
- limón (lemon)

## Directions

1. Cut off the white tips from one pound of carnation petals and grind them after cleaning
2. Place in clarified syrup made with three pounds of sugar and boil until it reaches the paste point
3. Then add the juice of one lemon, being careful that the seeds do not fall in
