## Ingredients

- wide chiles, soaked
- garlic
- cumin
- lard
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- water
- bagre, skin removed

## Directions

1. Grind soaked wide chiles with garlic and cumin in greater quantity
2. Fry in lard with salt and once it is done add oil, vinegar and water
3. After it has boiled add the bagre already prepared and skin removed
4. Let it boil a little and it should remain in broth
