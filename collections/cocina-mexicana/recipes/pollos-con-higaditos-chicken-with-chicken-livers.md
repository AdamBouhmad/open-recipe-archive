## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Onion
- Garlic
- Water
- Chicken livers
- Parsley
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Lard
- Oil

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe and add water
2. Chop onion and garlic and cook in a separate pan
3. Grind chicken livers and mix with the onion
4. Fry until cooked
5. Add to the meat
6. Add chopped parsley, ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
7. Fry bread slices in lard and grind them
8. Add to the casserole
9. When serving, add a little oil
10. Garnish as the previous recipe
