## Ingredients

- juiles
- butter or lard
- tomato
- green chile
- onions

## Directions

1. After washing, coat with butter or lard
2. Place on a grill
3. When cooked, serve with a sauce of chopped fried tomato, strips of green chile, and cooked onions
