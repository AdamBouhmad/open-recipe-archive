## Ingredients

- one pound of beef or veal
- onion
- bacon
- half glass of water
- salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces
2. Reheat in a pot with onion, bacon and half glass of water until it begins to stick
3. In that case, add boiling water and season with salt
4. Cook three quarters of an hour and then strain through a sieve
