## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, deveined and soaked
- clove
- garlic
- tomatoes
- onion, sliced
- cooked hen, in pieces
- ham, in pieces
- papada and longaniza, all cooked with the hen
- broth
- oregano

## Directions

1. Grind together deveined and soaked ancho chiles, cloves, garlic, and tomatoes
2. Fry sliced onion; once ready, add the ground mixture
3. When ready, add the hen, pieces of ham, papada and longaniza, all of which will have been cooked with the hen
4. Add broth and oregano
5. Boil until in good condition
