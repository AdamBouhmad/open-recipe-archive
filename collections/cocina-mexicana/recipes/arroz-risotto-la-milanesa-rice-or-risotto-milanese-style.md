## Ingredients

- one onion
- butter
- one pound rice
- broth from the pot
- grated cheese
- white pepper powder
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop an onion and fry it in a pot with butter until it browns
2. Then add one pound of rice and broth from the pot, letting it cook until soft, or somewhat underdone, as the Milanese prefer it
3. Add grated cheese and a bit of butter, remove from fire after stirring well, and serve seasoning beforehand with white pepper powder and nutmeg
