## Ingredients

- Cooked tripe
- Butter
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Cook and cut into pieces
2. Sauté in butter without salt, together with abundant chopped onion
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Add broth so the sauce is not too thick
5. When serving, dissolve one spoonful of mustard into it
