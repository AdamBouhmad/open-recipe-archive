## Ingredients

- one chocolate tablet
- milk
- molinillo (chocolate beater)

## Directions

1. Place the chocolate tablet with milk over the fire in a quantity slightly greater than what is needed to fill the cup in which it will be served
2. When it comes to its first boil, remove it, dissolve the tablet completely, and beat it with the molinillo so it incorporates with the milk, then return it to the heat
3. Give it only one boil the second time you place it on the fire so it does not become too thick
4. Fill half the cup, return it to the heat, and then fill the cup completely, so that the surface is covered with foam
