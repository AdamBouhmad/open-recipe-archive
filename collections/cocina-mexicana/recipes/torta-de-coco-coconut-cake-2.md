## Ingredients

- one grated coconut
- twelve eggs
- crumbled sponge cake (mamón)
- half a pound of lard (manteca)
- syrup (almíbar)
- Jerez wine
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the twelve eggs as if for frying
2. Mix with crumbled sponge cake
3. Heat half a pound of lard until very hot
4. Pour the batter into the hot lard
5. Cook the cake over very low heat
6. When cooked, add syrup, Jerez wine, and ground cinnamon
