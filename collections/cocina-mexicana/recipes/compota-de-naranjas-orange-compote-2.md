## Ingredients

- oranges
- water
- sugar for syrup at low point stage

## Directions

1. Select the most beautiful oranges and lightly remove with a knife the thin yellow skin
2. Divide them into quarters and remove the seeds
3. Place them carefully in cold water and blanch in boiling water, leaving them only as long as necessary to blanch
4. Return them to cold water, and when they are sufficiently tender or soft, remove them to place in syrup at low point stage
5. After several boils, set them aside and let them cool
6. Give them another boil and let them cool again
7. Remove the oranges from the syrup and place them in compote dishes, pouring the syrup over them
8. The syrup should be boiled a little more after removing the oranges, and let it cool before pouring into the compote dishes
