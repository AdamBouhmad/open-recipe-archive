## Ingredients

- young rabbits
- ham fat and lean
- scarlet tongue
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- good olive oil

## Directions

1. Bone a dozen young rabbits and lard them with fat and lean ham and scarlet tongue
2. Season with salt, pepper and fine spices, roll like a sausage and tie with string
3. Cook for one to two hours in a pan with salt, pepper, garlic, bay leaf, thyme and good olive oil
4. Drain and let cool, divide into pieces like tuna or pompano
5. Put in earthen pots or jars with oil to serve when desired as supplementary dishes
