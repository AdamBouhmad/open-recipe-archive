## Ingredients

- pulpa de tamarindo (tamarind pulp)
- agua (water)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Cook the pulp in water and then let it cool.
2. Strain it and sweeten with sugar.
3. For a milder version, make the tamarind as an infusion without cooking it.
