## Ingredients

- tender peas
- green beans
- salt
- raw onion
- avocado
- lettuce
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook tender peas and green beans with salt
2. Shell the beans and unite with the cooked peas
3. Add raw finely chopped onion, chopped avocado and a little chopped lettuce
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and mix well
5. Moisten with oil and vinegar and place avocado pieces on top
