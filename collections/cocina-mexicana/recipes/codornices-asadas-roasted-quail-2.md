## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 cloves of garlic, cleaned
- 1 pasilla chile, deveined
- cooking oil
- salt
- pepper
- butter-coated paper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the quail.
2. Grind three or four cloves of cleaned garlic and one pasilla chile deveined, thinning it at the metate with a little cooking oil.
3. Coat the quail with this mixture, dusted with sufficient salt and pepper.
4. Wrap them in butter-coated paper and place on a grill over very low heat until they cook completely.
5. Remove the paper, coat with lemon juice, and return to the grill, taking care that when they dry, to coat them with a feather dipped in the same oil used to coat them raw.
6. They may be served with a broth made of ground livers mixed with toasted bread and fried in oil with chopped onion, adding clove, cinnamon, pepper and toasted cilantro ground with a little strong vinegar and sufficient cooking oil.
