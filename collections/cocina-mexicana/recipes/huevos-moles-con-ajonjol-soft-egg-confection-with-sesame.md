## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- syrup at medium point
- 12 egg yolks, well beaten
- toasted sesame seeds
- candied fruit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at medium point with one pound of sugar
2. Add twelve well-beaten egg yolks and toasted sesame seeds
3. Arrange in layers alternating with layers of sponge cake, with the last layer being eggs
4. Place a comal with embers on top
