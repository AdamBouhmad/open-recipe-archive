## Ingredients

- cebolla picada
- manteca
- caldo del puchero
- perejil picado
- clavo
- pimienta molida
- fideos
- queso molido o rallado

## Directions

1. Chop a little onion and fry it in manteca
2. Add broth from the pot, a little chopped parsley, clove and ground pepper
3. When everything is boiling, add the noodles
4. As soon as you can tell they are cooked, remove them and place them in the steam of a pot, being careful they do not thicken too much
5. This soup can be garnished like others, adding at the time of serving a little ground or grated cheese, sprinkled on top
