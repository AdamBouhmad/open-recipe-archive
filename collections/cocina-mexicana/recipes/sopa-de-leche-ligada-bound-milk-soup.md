## Ingredients

- milk
- salt
- sugar
- egg

## Directions

1. Heat the milk
2. Sweeten it and add a little salt
3. When ready to serve, add one beaten egg for each quarter liter of milk
4. Return to the fire, stirring it much with a wooden spoon
5. When it thickens on the spoon, remove it and pour onto slices of soft bread or crumbled cold bread
