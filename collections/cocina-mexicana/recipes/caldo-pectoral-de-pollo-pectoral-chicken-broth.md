## Ingredients

- Six cuartillos of water
- One chicken, prepared as described
- Two ounces of pearl barley
- Two ounces of rice
- Two ounces of virgin honey

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of pearl barley, an equal amount of rice, and an equal quantity of virgin honey in six cuartillos of water with the chicken prepared as previously described.
2. Skim the broth and let the chicken cook over low heat until one third of the broth is consumed and it is reduced to four cuartillos.
