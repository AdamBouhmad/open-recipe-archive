## Ingredients

- pichones (squabs)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- manteca (lard)
- almendras (almonds)
- pan (bread)
- peregil (parsley)
- orégano fresco (fresh oregano)
- cebollitas (small onions)
- especias finas (fine spices)
- clavo (clove)
- rebanaditas de jamón (thin slices of ham)

## Directions

1. After cooking the squabs well in salted water, brown several almonds and bread slices in lard with a handful of parsley and another of fresh oregano
2. Grind everything well, fry it, and add the squabs with small onions cooked separately
3. Grind a few fine spices in which clove stands out, and season everything with sufficient salt
4. Add thin slices of ham cooked separately, and when the sauce has thickened, serve the squabs
