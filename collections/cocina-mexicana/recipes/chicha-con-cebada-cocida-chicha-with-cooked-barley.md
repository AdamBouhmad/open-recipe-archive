## Ingredients

- Two pineapples with skin, ground
- One heaping soup-plate of barley
- Twelve cuartillos of water
- One toasted bread cake
- One plate of toasted corn

## Directions

1. Grind two pineapples with their skin.
2. Boil the amount of barley that fits in one heaping soup plate in twelve cuartillos of water.
3. When the barley has burst from boiling, remove from heat and place in a jar.
4. Add the ground pineapple, the toasted bread cake, and the toasted corn to the jar.
