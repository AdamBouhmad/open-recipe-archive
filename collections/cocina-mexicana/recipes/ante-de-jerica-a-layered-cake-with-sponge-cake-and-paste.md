## Ingredients

- Syrup at cold point
- egg yolks
- sliced sponge cake (mamón)
- paste
- syrup
- colored pastille, ground

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks over syrup at cold point
2. Cook until they are well thickened
3. Layer sliced sponge cake
4. Top each layer with a layer of paste
5. Continue filling the dish, sprinkling each layer with syrup and ground colored pastille
