## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 2 pounds powdered sugar
- additional egg whites as needed

## Directions

1. Proceed the same as for bitter almond macaroons, with the difference that only 2 pounds of sugar are needed for each pound of almonds
2. Place 1 pound of sweet almonds on a clean cloth and rub them to remove dust
3. Grind them in a marble mortar with 4 egg whites, being careful not to grind them too much and to prevent them from turning into oil
4. Transfer the ground mixture to a bowl and incorporate 2 pounds of powdered sugar
5. If the paste is too dry, add egg whites, ensuring it is neither too liquid nor too thick
6. Arrange small portions the size of a walnut on sheets of paper
7. Bake the macaroons in the oven at a very gentle and steady heat
