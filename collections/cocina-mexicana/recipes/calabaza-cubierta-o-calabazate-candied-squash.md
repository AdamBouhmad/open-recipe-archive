## Ingredients

- Squash
- Salt water or settled lime water, depending on the type of squash
- Natural water
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the rind, interior fibers, and seeds from the squash
2. Divide into pieces of the desired size
3. Soak in salt water if the squash is compact and consistent by itself; use lime water if the squash is soft and somewhat spongy, so it takes on consistency
4. After this operation, place in natural water, rinsing thoroughly with many waters to lose the salt or lye flavor
5. Parboil in boiling water, return to cold water
6. Add to clarified syrup and allow to boil somewhat
7. Pour into bowls or jars and follow the same procedures already explained for candied citron and other candied sweets
