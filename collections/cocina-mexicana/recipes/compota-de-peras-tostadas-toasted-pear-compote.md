## Ingredients

- pears not very ripe
- water
- cinnamon stick
- one quarter sugar

## Directions

1. Select pears not very ripe and place them in a well-lit oven until the skin is burnt, turning them so they toast evenly
2. Remove the skin by rubbing them in water
3. Cut them in half and remove the seeds
4. Wash them well in several waters
5. Cook them in a pot with one and a half quarter liter of water, a cinnamon stick, and one quarter sugar
6. Cover the pot and let them cook until they feel soft to the touch and the syrup is consumed
7. Serve cold or hot
