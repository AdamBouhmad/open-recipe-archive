## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- broth
- white wine
- parsley bunch
- salt
- ground pepper
- flour
- aged cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Open the chickens along the back, from the neck to the tail.
2. Flatten them well with a mallet and fry them in butter.
3. Once well fried, add two parts broth and one part white wine, a bunch of parsley, salt and ground pepper.
4. Let them cook over low heat.
5. When cooked, strain the sauce and add a little butter mixed with flour and let it thicken well.
6. Place the chickens on a heat-resistant platter, bathe them with the sauce and sprinkle grated aged cheese on top.
7. Place over two gentle fires, and when the broth has reduced and taken color, remove and serve.
