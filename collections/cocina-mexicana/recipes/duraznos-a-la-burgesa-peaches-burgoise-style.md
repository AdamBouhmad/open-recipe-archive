## Ingredients

- almost ripe peaches
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Divide the peaches in half
2. place them to cook in a little water with sugar over low heat so they do not fall apart
3. when they are soft and yield to the touch of a finger, remove them
4. let their syrup cook a little longer and pour it over them
