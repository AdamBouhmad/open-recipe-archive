## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- agua (water)
- leche hervida y caliente (hot boiled milk)
- sal (salt)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. After arranging the rice as described, put it to cook with a little water
2. Moisten with hot boiled milk, add salt and a little sugar
3. When it is cooked and a little thick, put water in a plate with sugar which is made to reduce to the point of caramel
4. When it has taken on a caramel color, pour it over the rice
