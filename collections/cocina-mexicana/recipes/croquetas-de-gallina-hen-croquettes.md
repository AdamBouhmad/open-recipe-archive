## Ingredients

- one hen
- hen livers
- mushrooms
- lard
- egg yolks
- butter
- bread crumbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook a hen and let it cool
2. Cut the meat without skin and sinews into small pieces with some livers and mushrooms
3. Put a regular amount of lard in a casserole, remove from heat when half melted
4. Add three egg yolks and a little butter, mix everything in a saucepan
5. Let cool and divide into equal portions of desired size
6. Prepare bread crumbs on a board, coat the croquettes with them and then with beaten eggs as for making an omelet, forming the mass compact
7. Just before serving, place them in hot lard and remove as soon as they take good color
8. Place on the platter with sprigs of parsley
