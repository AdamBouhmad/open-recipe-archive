## Ingredients

- morcones (blood sausage) well cooked
- cebolla picada (chopped onion)
- chiles verdes rebanados (sliced green chiles)
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the morcones well and fry them
2. Add plenty of chopped onion and sliced green chiles
3. Fill the chalupa with this mixture
4. Add the corresponding salt
