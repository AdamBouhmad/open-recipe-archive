## Ingredients

- One and one-half pounds of flour
- Twenty-four egg yolks and two egg whites
- Four and one-half ounces of ground sugar
- Four ounces of butter
- One quarter of a quart of Spanish brandy
- A small amount of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites first until they rise well and thicken
2. Mix in the flour, ground sugar, butter, Spanish brandy, and salt
3. Knead all together until it makes a crackling sound
4. Form small rosquetes
5. Arrange on buttered paper or tin sheets
6. Bake in the oven
