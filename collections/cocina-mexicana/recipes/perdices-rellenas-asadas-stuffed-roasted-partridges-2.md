## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Partridge liver
- Scraped bacon
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onions
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing with the liver, scraped bacon, salt, chopped parsley and onions
2. Stuff the partridge with this filling
3. Fold the legs over the breast
4. Brown in a pan with butter
5. Place on the spit to roast
6. Serve with the same sauce as the preceding recipe
