## Ingredients

- Forty egg yolks
- Four ounces of ground sugar
- Four ounces of hot melted butter
- One quarter quart of refined mescal
- Half ounce of white tequesquite
- White salt
- Cake flour as needed

## Directions

1. Put forty egg yolks and four ounces of ground sugar in a bowl and beat by hand until the mixture, when lifted from a raised hand, forms a nipple-like shape when it falls
2. Add four ounces of hot melted butter, stirring quickly by hand so the egg does not cook
3. Prepare the mescal the day before: combine one quarter quart of refined mescal with half ounce of white tequesquite and a little white salt, and let settle
4. Add the settled mescal to the beaten mixture without incorporating the sediment
5. After mixing all ingredients well, gradually add cake flour until the dough is quite soft
6. Transfer the dough from the bowl to a clean table or board and knead it a little
7. Cut the rosquetes or puchas to the desired size, being careful not to handle them too much and placing them seam-side down
8. Bake in the oven
