## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- fine biscuits
- sugar
- butter
- sesame
- raisins
- almonds
- hazelnuts
- cinnamon
- clarified honey
- canela

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites as for sponge cake, and twelve yolks apart
2. After everything is well beaten, combine and beat again
3. Toast four large fine biscuits and grind them, also grinding half a pound of sugar
4. Once everything is ground, mix with the eggs, adding one and a half pounds of butter that will have been washed
5. Separate a portion with which to butter a pot or pan
6. Place in it a layer of the said paste and on top another of toasted sesame, raisins, almonds, hazelnuts and cinnamon pieces, covering with another layer of paste sprinkled with toasted sesame
7. Place in the oven over two fires, and when the cake is cooked, cut into pieces that are placed in clarified honey with raisins, almonds and cinnamon
