## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- orange zest
- cognac to taste

## Directions

1. After the milk is cooked, sweeten to taste and mix with egg yolks until well yellow
2. Strain and let thicken a little
3. Before removing from fire, add one tablespoon of orange zest
4. When it has boiled once, remove from fire
5. When serving, add cognac to taste and bring to the fire beating much with a chocolate whisk
6. Pour hot into glasses
