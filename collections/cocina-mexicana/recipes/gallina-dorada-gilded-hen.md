## Ingredients

- one hen
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- spices
- bacon slices
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the hen briefly in a pot or in broth with salt, pepper, onion and spices
2. Wrap in bacon slices and place on the spit at low heat
3. Remove the bacon slices and let the bird until it takes color
4. Then brush with beaten egg yolks
5. To serve, make a sauce with a little sugar and cinnamon
