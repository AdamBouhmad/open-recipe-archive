## Ingredients

- one good piece of beef
- bacon strips
- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- fine spices
- white wine
- bacon in small pieces
- garlic, chopped very finely
- small whole onions
- carrot slices
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the beef
2. Lard with bacon strips
3. Add chopped parsley and onion, salt, pepper and fine spices
4. In the pot put white wine, bacon pieces, finely chopped garlic, small whole onions, carrot slices, peppercorns, and a little salt
5. Place the meat over these ingredients
6. Cover the pot well
7. Place over low heat for five or six hours
8. Serve when cooked through
