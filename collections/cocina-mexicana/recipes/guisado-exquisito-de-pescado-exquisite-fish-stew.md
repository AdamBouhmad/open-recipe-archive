## Ingredients

- Garlic, finely minced
- Onions, finely minced
- Oil or lard
- Fish, raw, cleaned and cut into pieces
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Pepper
- Orange juice, in considerable amount
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Finely mince garlic and onions and fry them in a cazuela with oil or lard
2. Add the raw, cleaned fish cut into pieces
3. Add chopped parsley, oil, and pepper
4. Add considerable orange juice and the corresponding amount of salt with a little water
5. Place over two fires and leave until the fish is perfectly cooked
