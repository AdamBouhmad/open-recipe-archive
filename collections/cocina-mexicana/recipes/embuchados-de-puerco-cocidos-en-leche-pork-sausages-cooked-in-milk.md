## Ingredients

- thick pork intestines
- water
- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- pork lard
- lean ham
- salt
- fine spices
- anise
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash the thick pork tripe well and cut to desired sausage size
2. Soak in three parts water and one part vinegar with thyme, bay leaf and basil to remove the tannery taste
3. Separate one part of the tripe and cut the other into strips
4. Cut pork lard and lean ham into strips
5. Combine all and season with salt, fine spices and a little anise
6. Fill the separated tripe two-thirds full with this mixture so they do not burst when cooked
7. Tie both ends
8. Cook in milk mixed with water, salt, thyme, bay leaf, basil and a little lard
9. Cool the cooked sausages in their own broth
10. Grill and serve as supplementary dishes
