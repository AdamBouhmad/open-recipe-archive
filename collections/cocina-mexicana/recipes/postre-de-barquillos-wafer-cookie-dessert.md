## Ingredients

- curd cheese
- sugar
- red scent tablet or sponge cake
- cinnamon
- almonds
- hazelnuts, toasted
- raisins
- citron pieces
- orange blossom water
- egg
- lard for frying
- sugar
- high-point syrup
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- citron pieces
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Knead very well a little curd cheese with sugar, plenty of red scent tablet or sponge cake, cinnamon, almonds and toasted hazelnuts, all ground
2. Add raisins, citron pieces and orange blossom water, and well mixed, fill the wafer cookies with it, which covered with beaten egg are fried in lard
3. Then roll them in sugar and put them in syrup with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, citron pieces and toasted sesame
