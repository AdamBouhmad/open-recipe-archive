## Ingredients

- guayava cocida, mondada y sin huesitos (cooked, peeled guava without seeds)
- leche (milk)
- zumo colado de tuna morada ó colorada (strained juice of purple or red prickly pear)

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the previous recipes
2. Place cooked, peeled guava without seeds in place of the cherimoya or orange
3. It may be colored, if desired not yellow, with strained juice of purple or red prickly pear, according to taste
