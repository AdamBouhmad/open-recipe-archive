## Ingredients

- cod
- onions
- tomatoes
- chiles
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces and put in a casserole with a layer of onions cut into thin and round slices, tomatoes and chiles of the season chopped
2. Over this layer put another of cod well arranged, and so successively as needed, ending with a layer of tomato, chile, etc.
3. Pour plenty of oil over it and put with low heat from above and below, letting it cook well until it is ready to serve
