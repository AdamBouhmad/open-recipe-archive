## Ingredients

- chicken meat, cleaned of tendons
- bread soaked in milk
- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- raw egg

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken meat of tendons and mince very finely
2. Mix with bread soaked in milk and egg yolk
3. Season properly and beat well
4. Form into small balls
5. Coat with breadcrumbs
6. Dip in beaten raw egg
7. Coat again with breadcrumbs
8. Fry them
9. Serve in pyramids with fried parsley
