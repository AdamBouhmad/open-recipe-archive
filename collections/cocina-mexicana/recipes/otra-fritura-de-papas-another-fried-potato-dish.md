## Ingredients

- cooked potato
- aged cheese
- salt
- egg
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Mash the cooked potato by hand, mixing in plenty of aged cheese and sufficient salt.
2. Form balls that are rolled in egg and then in flour.
3. Brown them in very hot lard.
