## Ingredients

- Two pounds of butter
- Puff pastry dough
- Sugar

## Directions

1. The day before, wash two pounds of butter in water and leave it in clean water overnight in the open air
2. The following day, remove the butter from the water, cut into small pieces, and place on puff pastry dough
3. Proceed with everything else as for common pastries
4. Dust with sugar for baking
