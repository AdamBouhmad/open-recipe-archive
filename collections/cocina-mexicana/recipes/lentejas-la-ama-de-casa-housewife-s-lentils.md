## Ingredients

- Lentils
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in slightly salted water.
2. Drain them and place in a pan with a good piece of butter, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
3. Sauté for a few minutes.
4. Place on a platter, garnishing with fried bread crusts, and serve very hot.
