## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of red carnations
- one drachm of crushed clove spice
- twelve ounces of boiling water
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Remove buds from twelve ounces of red carnations and place the petals in a water bath with one drachm of crushed clove spice
2. Pour twelve ounces of boiling water over them and let macerate for fifteen hours at a slightly elevated temperature
3. Strain the liquid, pressing the pomace in a press and filter everything
4. Make syrup with two pounds of sugar, bring it to syrup consistency, add the strained carnation liquid, let boil for several minutes
5. Once cool, store in dry, well-sealed bottles
