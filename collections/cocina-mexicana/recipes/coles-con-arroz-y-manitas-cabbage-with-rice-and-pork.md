## Ingredients

- cabbage
- tequesuete
- anise
- fine salt
- rice
- ham slices
- lamb or pork meat
- butter
- ripe tomato
- garlic
- onion
- water
- green poblano chiles

## Directions

1. Remove the thick and green leaves from the cabbage, put to boil strongly with tequesuete and anise
2. Remove, chop into fairly large pieces, and put to cook with fine salt, rice, slices of ham and of lamb or pork meat well cleaned
3. When all this has cooked, remove from the fire, put a pot on it with butter and fry appropriate amounts of ripe tomato, garlic and onion chopped, adding sufficient salt
4. Pass the cabbage and all the meat there and add the corresponding water
5. Let season at low heat, and add two or three green poblano chiles, roasted and peeled
6. When this sauce is ready to dry, remove and serve
