## Ingredients

- Lettuce, finely chopped
- Onion, finely chopped
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Lettuce hearts
- Radishes
- Cooked egg yolks
- English mustard
- Cooked beets

## Directions

1. Chop the lettuce very finely and add a small amount of finely chopped onion
2. Sprinkle with pepper and moisten with vinegar and oil, adding sufficient salt
3. Garnish as with the previous salad, with lettuce hearts and radishes soaked and cut into quarters
4. It can also be covered with cooked egg yolks, crumbled and seasoned with salt, oil, a little vinegar and peeled English mustard
5. Garnish also with small radishes and cooked beets carved into small figures
