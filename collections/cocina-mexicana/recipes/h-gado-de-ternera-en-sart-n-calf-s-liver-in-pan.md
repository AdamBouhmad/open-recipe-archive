## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- tender garlic cloves
- parsley chopped
- onions chopped
- butter
- flour
- red wine
- salt
- pepper
- agraz
- broth or stock

## Directions

1. Cut a calf's liver in slices and place in the pan with tender garlic cloves, chopped parsley and onions and a piece of butter
2. Place over high heat, adding a pinch of flour
3. When the liver appears to become hard, turn it and moisten with red wine
4. Season with salt and pepper, adding a little agraz and when about to boil remove from heat
5. If the sauce is too thick, add a little broth from the pot or stock
