## Ingredients

- cabbage, large and very white
- salt
- garlic

## Directions

1. Take a large and very white cabbage, remove the stems and first leaves
2. Hollow it on top like a watermelon, tie this hollow with a little rope
3. Take a pot proportioned to the cabbage so that it does not fall apart, with enough water, salt, crushed garlic
