## Ingredients

- Ground biscuit
- Milk, well sweetened
- Egg yolks
- Beaten egg
- Butter or lard
- Sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste with ground biscuit soaked in well-sweetened milk and mixed with egg yolks
2. Coat with beaten egg
3. Fry in butter or lard
4. Dust with sifted sugar after frying
