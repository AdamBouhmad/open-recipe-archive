## Ingredients

- Lamb tripe
- Lamb feet
- Lamb kidneys
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Ripe tomato
- Lard
- Broth from cooking the tripe
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cumin
- Saffron
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe and cut the belly into squares of four or five fingers.
2. Add pieces of the entrails from the same tripe, forming small bundles and tying them at the ends with the same tripe.
3. Cook these bundles with the lamb feet and all other tripe parts, including kidneys but not the lung, cut into regular pieces with sufficient salt.
4. Chop plenty of onion, garlic, parsley, and ripe tomato and fry all of this well in a pot with sufficient lard.
5. Add sufficient broth from the cooking of the tripe and add the tripe as well.
6. Grind all spices with a little cumin and saffron and add to the broth.
