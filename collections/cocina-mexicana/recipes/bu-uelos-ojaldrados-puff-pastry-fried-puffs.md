## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 egg yolks
- half pound flour
- cold water
- a little yeast
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a dough like puff pastry with 2 egg yolks, half pound flour, cold water and a little yeast and butter
2. Stretch like pastry sheets and butter them, then place one on top of another up to twenty
3. Then roll all together and let rest briefly
4. With a knife cut them crosswise
5. With your hand shape the puffs and fry them
