## Ingredients

- Curdled milk (squeezed dry)
- One razada of nixtamal (cleaned and rubbed)
- Salt (as much as can be taken with three fingers)
- Three egg yolks
- Two egg whites
- Three tablespoons of melted lard
- Ground sugar (to taste)

## Directions

1. Squeeze the curdled milk in a napkin until dry
2. Measure four calderas of the dried curdled milk and one razada of cleaned and rubbed nixtamal
3. Grind both ingredients together
4. Mix in salt measured with three fingers
5. After grinding everything well, begin kneading
6. Add three egg yolks, two egg whites, and three tablespoons of melted lard
7. Add the lard gradually as the dough absorbs it, along with ground sugar to taste
8. Continue until the dough reaches the proper consistency (same as previous recipe)
9. Complete the operation the same way as described in the previous recipe
