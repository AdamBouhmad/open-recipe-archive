## Ingredients

- one pound of well-sifted corn flour
- six and a half ounces of lard
- six cooked egg yolks
- one raw whole egg
- five ounces of ground sugar
- half a small cup of orange blossom water
- a little ground salt

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of well-sifted corn flour with six and a half ounces of lard, six cooked egg yolks, one raw whole egg, five ounces of ground sugar, half a small cup of orange blossom water, and a little ground salt
2. After everything is well mixed, form the tortillitas
3. The next day, pass them two or three times through the metate
4. Form them a second time and put them in the oven after the white bread has come out, which is necessary for them to have good flavor and good cooking, being careful that they do not burn and that they all brown equally
