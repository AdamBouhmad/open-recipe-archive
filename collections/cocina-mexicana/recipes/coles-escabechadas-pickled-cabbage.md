## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- water
- tomato
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- garlic heads
- black pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- cooked ham
- chorizones
- chorizos
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbages and put them to cook with salt
2. When they have boiled a little, remove this water and add another hot water so they finish cooking with more salt
3. Put the montalayas to parboil and in the same pot add chopped tomato and a few whole onions
4. Once parboiled, remove and place to drain, chop very finely and put back with more tomato in the same place
5. Once this is well fried, wash the cabbages with another hot water and put them in the cazuela with corresponding hot water
6. Add a little bay leaf, thyme, oregano and the onions that were fried first, garlic heads, loose black pepper, and a little oil and vinegar to taste
7. Add the cooked ham in slices with chorizones and chorizos
8. Season everything with the cabbages and to bring to the table, garnish with chiles and olives
