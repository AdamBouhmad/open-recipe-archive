## Ingredients

- butter
- large sections of onion
- ground tomato without seeds
- pepper
- broth from the metate
- minced parsley
- cooking oil
- Sherry wine
- cooked or roasted tongue slices

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a pan
2. Add large sections of onion
3. Add ground tomato without seeds and a pinch of pepper
4. Use only the broth that comes from the metate
5. Let it fry for a long time over low heat
6. Add a handful of finely minced parsley
7. Add a little cooking oil and some Sherry wine
8. Add the cooked or roasted sliced tongue
