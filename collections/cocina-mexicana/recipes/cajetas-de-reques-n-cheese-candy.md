## Ingredients

- Half of a small cheese (requesón)
- One pound of sugar
- Four pounds of huacamote
- Three pounds of white sweet potato
- One pound of almond, all ground
- Six quartillos of milk
- Clarified syrup at mirror point, one pound per pound of paste

## Directions

1. Grind all solid ingredients
2. Dissolve in six quartillos of milk until no lumps remain
3. Mix with clarified syrup at mirror point in the proportion of one pound per pound of paste, stirring and breaking up everything so it does not become lumpy
4. Place on the fire and bring to cajeta point
