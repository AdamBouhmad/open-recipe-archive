## Ingredients

- Small turbot
- Mayordomo sauce, raw
- Two large onions
- Butter
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Nutmeg
- Lemon
- Half bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Choose a small turbot, empty it, wash it, let it drain, open it along the back, separate the flesh from the spine, and place a good raw Mayordomo sauce between the spine and the flesh
2. Chop two large onions and place them on a silver plate the size of the small turbot, with a piece of butter
3. Season with salt, coarse pepper, thyme, bay leaf in powder, chopped parsley and a little grated nutmeg
4. Arrange the small turbot over the onions, dust with salt, add lemon and melted butter, and moisten with half a bottle of white wine
5. Place the plate on a small burner and cover with a dome oven, letting it cook over very gentle heat
6. While it cooks, baste it frequently
