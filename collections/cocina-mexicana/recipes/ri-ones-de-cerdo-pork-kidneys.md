## Ingredients

- pork kidneys
- lard or butter
- parsley
- garlic
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- white wine
- bread

## Directions

1. After cutting them into slices, fry them in lard or butter and add parsley, garlic, and mushrooms, chopped very fine.
2. Add sufficient salt and pepper, and as soon as everything is well fried, sprinkle flour on them and add a glass of white wine.
3. Leave them on low heat until they cook, without letting them boil because they would harden, and serve them with toasted bread fried in lard.
