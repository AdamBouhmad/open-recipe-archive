## Ingredients

- raw tomatillos
- vinegar
- sea salt
- sugar
- water
- fresh parsley
- oregano
- onion
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from four or six raw tomatillos
2. Mince them very finely
3. Place them in fine vinegar and season with sea salt
4. Soften the acid with a piece of sugar and some water
5. Add finely chopped fresh parsley, dust with a little oregano and minced onion, and add sufficient cooking oil
