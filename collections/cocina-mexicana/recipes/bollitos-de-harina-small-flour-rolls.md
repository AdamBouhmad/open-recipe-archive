## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- Five of yeast
- Two of butter
- One of ground sugar
- A pinch of fine salt

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour with five of yeast, two of butter, one of ground sugar, and a pinch of fine salt dissolved in hot water
2. Knead everything very well and let the dough rise or ferment for two or three hours
3. Form the small rolls with it, which will be cooked in a moderately hot oven so they do not burn
