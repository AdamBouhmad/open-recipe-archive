## Ingredients

- one pound sweet almonds
- three dozen bitter almonds
- three tablespoons cornstarch
- half pound sugar
- eighteen eggs

## Directions

1. Grind the sweet almonds and then the bitter almonds, clean them along with the cornstarch and sugar
2. Once well incorporated, pour into a mold or paper box greased with lard
3. Place in the oven at two very low fires
4. Test for doneness with a straw—if it comes out clean, it is done
