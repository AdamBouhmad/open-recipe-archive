## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one egg yolk
- six ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Knead the flour with warm water
2. As soon as it comes together, add the melted lard and butter
3. Work it like washing clothes
4. Wrap it in a napkin and remove it to stretch with lard, not hot, but at a good temperature
5. Drain the wafers well
