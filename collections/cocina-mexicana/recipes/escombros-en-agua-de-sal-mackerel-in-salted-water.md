## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- Salt
- Boiling water
- Thickened mayordomo sauce

## Directions

1. Empty the mackerel through the head without removing the liver
2. Dry and open along the back
3. Place in boiling salted water
4. Remove after twenty minutes
5. Serve with thickened mayordomo sauce poured over
