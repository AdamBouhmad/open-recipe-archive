## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Oil
- Garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After the cauliflower is well cooked, divide it into florets.
2. Heat a pan with oil over the fire and fry clean garlic cloves in it.
3. When very golden, dust the cauliflower stems with salt and pepper, roll them in a little ground bread, and remove the fried garlic.
4. Brown them in that oil, adding a few drops of vinegar, or serve them thus, dusting again with salt and pepper.
5. Alternatively, grind the golden garlic cloves that were removed from the oil, dissolve them in the oil, and mixed with a little vinegar, use it to bathe the cauliflower.
