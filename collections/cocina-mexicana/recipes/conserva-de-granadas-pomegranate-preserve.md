## Ingredients

- Fresh, greenish pomegranates
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Coconut paste for filling (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the hard shell without breaking the white inner calyx, leaving the stem at all of even size and in good proportion
2. Open through the crown enough to empty them if they are to be filled and if this is done with coconut paste which are more delicious or with another
3. After peeling and emptying, or without emptying, give a gentle boil
4. Place in the sugar syrup and conclude as all the previous ones
