## Ingredients

- grapes
- small pits that grace it
- small skins that give flavor
- whole grapes
- preserved or candied sour cherries
- figs
- small lemons
- tejocotes or pieces of candied fruits

## Directions

1. Make the same way as the previous recipe
2. Leave some small pits to grace it
3. Add some small skins that give flavor
4. Include whole grapes, preserved or candied sour cherries, figs, small lemons and tejocotes or pieces of candied fruits
