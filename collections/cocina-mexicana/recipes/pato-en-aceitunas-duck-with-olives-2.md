## Ingredients

- Duck
- Bacon pieces
- Half pound of olives

## Directions

1. After the duck is well prepared and arranged, turn it in a casserole with pieces of bacon
2. Add half a pound of olives to the casserole where the duck is and leave for a few minutes
3. Serve the duck surrounded by the olives
