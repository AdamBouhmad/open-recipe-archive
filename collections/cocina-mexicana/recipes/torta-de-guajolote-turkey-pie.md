## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- pastry dough
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey and place it whole to cook in water with salt, crushed garlic, bay leaf, and thyme.
2. Once cooked, drain it, and on a table carefully remove all the skin, starting from the neck, except the wings.
3. Make slices from the breasts and thighs.
