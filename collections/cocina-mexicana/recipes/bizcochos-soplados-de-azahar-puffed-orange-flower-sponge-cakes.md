## Ingredients

- powdered sugar passed through a silk sieve
- one egg white, beaten
- two pinches of candied orange flowers

## Directions

1. Make a glaze that is neither too liquid nor too dry by mixing powdered sugar, passed through a silk sieve, with one beaten egg white separated from the yolk
2. When the glaze reaches the right consistency, add two pinches of candied orange flowers
3. With this composition, fill halfway some very small paper boxes, four times smaller than ordinary ones
4. Put them in a mild oven, but sufficiently hot
