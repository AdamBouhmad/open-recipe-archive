## Ingredients

- Lard
- Wide chile peppers, soaked
- Cooked tomatoes
- Cumin
- Bread fried in lard
- Xoconoxcles, peeled (optional)
- Lengua-de-vaca leaves (optional)
- Mutton offal
- Salt
- Water
- Sesame, toasted

## Directions

1. Fry soaked and ground wide chiles in lard with cooked tomatoes, cumin and bread fried in lard
2. Add peeled xoconoxcles or lengua-de-vaca leaves if desired
3. Pour the offal, which should be cooked in regular pieces in salted water, and its broth over this mixture
4. Let everything finish seasoning over two fires
5. It should remain brothy but not watery
6. Serve with toasted sesame sprinkled on top
