## Ingredients

- small soft tortillas
- chile

## Directions

1. Fry small soft tortillas with chile or prepare them as explained, serving them for light breakfasts and for consuming with pulque, which is the only beneficial drink to have with them, as water would make them indigestible and fermented liquors would make them harmful
