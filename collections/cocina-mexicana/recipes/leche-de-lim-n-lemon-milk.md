## Ingredients

- Milk
- Pounded sugar in the proportion of one pound per cuartillo

## Directions

1. Sweeten milk with pounded sugar in the proportion of one pound for each cuartillo
2. Dissolve the sugar well, strain through a napkin, and heat until it reaches the thread stage (when a little dropped in cold water forms a ball)
3. Remove from heat and beat well, adding some drops of lemon juice
