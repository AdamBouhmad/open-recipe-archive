## Ingredients

- pulp of mutton
- loin of pork
- pork head
- piece of ham
- garlic
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- ground cumin
- vinegar
- butter
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- chile

## Directions

1. Cook together the pulp of mutton with loin of pork, pork head, and a piece of ham
2. Chop with plenty of garlic and celery
3. Season everything with salt, pepper and ground cumin, sprinkle well with vinegar, and fry in a little butter
4. Add beaten egg as for frying and stir everything, breaking up the chopped mixture in the egg
5. Add a little breadcrumbs and cook to set at two gentle fires
6. Serve the pie whole and dry, or in medium pieces, stewed in chile sauce or chile broth
