## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- garlic
- onions
- flour
- broth
- salt
- ground spices

## Directions

1. Fry the chickens in lard with finely minced garlic and onions.
2. Very quickly sprinkle flour on them, stirring them constantly at the same time, taking care that the flour does not brown.
3. Add the broth in which they were cooked and salt.
4. Add ground spices of all kinds, except saffron and cumin, and let them season until the sauce thickens.
