## Ingredients

- Panela (cane sugar) - one pair unit
- Anise
- Cloves
- Water
- Twenty quartillos of pulque

## Directions

1. Boil panela with a little anise and cloves in sufficient water
2. Mix everything in twenty quartillos of pulque
