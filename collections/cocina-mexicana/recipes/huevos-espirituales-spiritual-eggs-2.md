## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- warm water sweetened
- or syrup
- or wine
- or brandy
- or milk
- or atole

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks in warm sweetened water, or in syrup, or in wine, or in brandy, or in milk, or in atole, and these are the spiritual eggs.
