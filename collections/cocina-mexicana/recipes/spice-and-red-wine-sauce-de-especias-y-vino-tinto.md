## Ingredients

- Pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Garlic
- Red wine
- Onion
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind six peppercorns, four cloves and a small piece of cinnamon
2. Add to the roast at half-cooked stage with a head of garlic peeled and only crushed, half a pocillo of red wine and an onion cut into eight pieces
3. Add a little vinegar and let season
