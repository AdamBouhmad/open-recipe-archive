## Ingredients

- beaten fresh cheese made from whey
- egg yolks to give color
- sugar to taste
- ground cinnamon to taste
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Beat the fresh cheese made from whey with egg yolks to give it color
2. Add sugar and ground cinnamon to taste
3. Add pine nuts, raisins, almonds and broken walnuts
4. This will be the filling for the pastry, which will be made in all respects like the pastry of almonds, pine nuts and custard
