## Ingredients

- three cuartillos of cambray peas
- four ounces of butter
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Proceed as in the previous recipe, without omitting the sugar, until the broth is almost consumed.
2. When this is the moment to serve the peas and they are simmering, remove them from heat and toss with new butter off the heat until well coated.
3. Arrange in a mound, making sure they are well seasoned and have necessary salt.
