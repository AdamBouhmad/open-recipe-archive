## Ingredients

- Tomato
- Chopped garlic
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Spices
- Saffron
- Capers
- Pitted raisins
- Almonds
- Small chiles
- Fish broth
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Fry tomato with chopped garlic; separately fry onion with tomatoes
2. Join everything and add spices without forgetting saffron, capers, pitted raisins, almonds, small chiles, fish broth, oil, vinegar, oregano and toasted sesame
