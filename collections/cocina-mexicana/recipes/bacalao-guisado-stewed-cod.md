## Ingredients

- Cod, cut in pieces
- Onion
- Tomato
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and soak in water until desalted, changing the water often
2. Cook and drain on a board
3. Fry onion and tomato in abundance and arrange the pieces in a proportionate dish and spread over it
4. Pour the onion and tomato with parsley, pepper and crushed garlic over them, continuing thus with the remaining pieces until the vessel is full
5. Pour water over and when it covers, bring to a boil a couple of times and season with salt, no other spice
