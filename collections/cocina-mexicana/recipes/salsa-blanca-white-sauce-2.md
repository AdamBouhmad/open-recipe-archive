## Ingredients

- One quarter pound of fresh butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- A little water

## Directions

1. Place one quarter pound of fresh butter in a saucepan, kneaded with one tablespoon of flour, salt and pepper
2. Dissolve with a little water
