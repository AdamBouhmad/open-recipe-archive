## Ingredients

- one jarro of milk
- four pounds of sugar
- beaten eggs
- lemon peel
- two ounces of butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put one jarro of milk in a pot to cook over gentle heat, and as soon as it boils, add four pounds of sugar and stir well, not leaving the heat until it is as thick as atole
2. When cool, add beaten eggs and lemon peel, return to heat, stir well, and add two ounces of butter
3. When it looks like sleet, remove from heat and place over layers of sponge cake, covering the last layer with ground sugar and cinnamon
