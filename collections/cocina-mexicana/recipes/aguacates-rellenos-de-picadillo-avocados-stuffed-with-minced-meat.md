## Ingredients

- picadillo of pork loin with all types of spices
- roasted tomatillo
- cooked beets
- cooked onions
- pomegranate
- cinnamon sticks
- small walnuts
- sugar
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- small walnuts seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- dragées

## Directions

1. Prepare the picadillo of pork loin with all types of spices, roasted tomatillo, cooked beets and onions, and pomegranate, seasoned as is customary
2. Divide the two halves of the avocado
3. Place small cinnamon sticks on them
4. Cover with a layer of small walnuts seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
5. To grind the walnuts, soak them first so that the brown skin covering them can be easily removed, then sprinkle with vinegar, loosening the ground mixture with very little water
6. Decorate the dish on top with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and dragées
