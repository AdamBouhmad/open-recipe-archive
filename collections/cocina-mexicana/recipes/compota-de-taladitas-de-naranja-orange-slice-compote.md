## Ingredients

- oranges
- water
- sugar for syrup at low point stage

## Directions

1. Cut some oranges into small, very thin strips after removing the first colored skin
2. Blanch them until they feel soft to the touch
3. Pour them into cold water, drain them, and give them several boils in syrup at low point stage
4. Give them a second boil the next day and serve with the same syrup
