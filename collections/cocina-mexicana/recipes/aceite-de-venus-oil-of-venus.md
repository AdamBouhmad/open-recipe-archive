## Ingredients

- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- One adarme of mace
- Descaled rinds of four citrons
- Half an ounce of carmine
- Half an ounce of anise
- One ounce of coriander
- Half an ounce of benzoin
- Half an ounce of storax
- A small handful of walnut leaves
- Aguardiente
- Four and a half pounds of sugar
- Eleven quartillos of water
- Saffron tincture

## Directions

1. Pound together in a mortar the cinnamon, mace, citron rinds, carmine, anise, coriander, benzoin, storax, and walnut leaves
2. Leave this in infusion in a jar of aguardiente for eight days
3. Distill it after this time
4. Put four and a half pounds of sugar in eleven quartillos of water and leave it on the fire until it reaches the point of syrup to sweeten the distilled liquor
5. Color it with saffron tincture and filter it
