## Ingredients

- Fifteen apples
- Five ounces butter
- Half pound powdered sugar
- Cinnamon sticks
- Bread crumb

## Directions

1. Cut apples into quarters and remove seeds and peels
2. Make very thin slices from the quarters
3. Put in a saucepan with five ounces butter, half pound powdered sugar, and cinnamon sticks
4. Cook on high heat, being careful not to reduce to marmalade
5. When cooked, remove cinnamon
6. Make very thin slices of bread crumb
7. Soak in butter and line a smooth copper mold
8. Place apples on top and cover with more buttered bread slices
9. Cook over embers or in the oven
10. When it has taken on a beautiful color, turn out onto the serving plate
