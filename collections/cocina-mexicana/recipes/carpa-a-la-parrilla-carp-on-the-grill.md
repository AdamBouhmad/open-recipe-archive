## Ingredients

- carp
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the carp as for the previous recipe
2. Without flour, place it on the grill after rubbing and soaking it with oil, salt and pepper
3. Serve with anchovy butter
