## Ingredients

- Squash
- Lime water
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Clean the squash and place in lime water for one full hour
2. Have a pan with boiling water ready and place it there
3. As soon as it cooks, remove it and place in cold water, leave until the next day
4. Make the syrup and place it there to give three boils, let cool in the syrup
