## Ingredients

- White beans
- Garlic
- Onion
- Fresh cilantro
- Lard
- Ancho chile
- Salt
- Fresh cheese or aged cheese
- Cheese slices for garnish
- Small chiles for garnish
- Olives for garnish

## Directions

1. After washing the beans, cook them in water with clean garlic, sliced onion, and fresh cilantro.
2. When cooked, fry in lard with more chopped onion and a little toasted and ground ancho chile to give it color.
3. Set aside a handful of the cooked beans to be ground.
4. Add the cooked beans to the lard mixture, along with the ground beans and the broth in which they were cooked.
5. Add necessary salt and fresh grated cheese or aged ground cheese.
6. Let it boil until it is seasoned.
7. To serve, garnish with cheese slices, small chiles, and olives.
