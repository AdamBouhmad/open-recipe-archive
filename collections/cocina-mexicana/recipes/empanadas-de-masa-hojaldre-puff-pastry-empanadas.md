## Ingredients

- One and a half pounds flour
- Two egg yolks and one white
- A short piece of lard
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Mix everything with cold water
2. Knead well
3. When the dough reaches a regular temperature, form the puff pastries
4. Put them in the oven or cook them with two heats
