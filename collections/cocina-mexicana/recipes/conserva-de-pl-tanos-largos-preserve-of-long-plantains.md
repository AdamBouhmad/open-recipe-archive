## Ingredients

- Well-rounded plantains without heart, ripe but not overripe
- Clarified syrup at low point

## Directions

1. Select well-rounded plantains that have no heart and are ripe but not overripe
2. Cook them with skin, and when the skin begins to crack, remove and peel them
3. Cut into pieces of desired size or leave whole if small
4. Place in clarified syrup at low point
5. Allow to infuse with the syrup over three days over gentle fire
6. After three days, bring the syrup to preserve point
