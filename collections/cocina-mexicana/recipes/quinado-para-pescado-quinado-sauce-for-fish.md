## Ingredients

- parsley
- green chile
- tomato
- garlic
- cinnamon powder
- salt
- cooked chickpeas
- cooking oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Finely chop plenty of parsley, green chile, tomato, and garlic.
2. Fry very well.
3. Add a little ground cinnamon, proper amount of salt, cooked chickpeas, cooking oil, and a bit of vinegar.
4. Serve.
