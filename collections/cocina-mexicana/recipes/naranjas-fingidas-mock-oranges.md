## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- butter
- syrup at conserve point
- orange blossom water
- sugar pearls
- powdered sugar (if to be served dry)

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks without any mixture
2. Pour the beaten mixture into small buttered coffee cups
3. Cook in a water bath with fire above—place in a large saucepan with boiling water and cover with a comal with fire on top
4. Test the cooking with a straw; when cooked, remove from heat
5. Place in syrup at conserve point with a little orange blossom water and sprinkle with sugar pearls
6. If they are to be served dry, add ground sugar to the yolks when beating them
