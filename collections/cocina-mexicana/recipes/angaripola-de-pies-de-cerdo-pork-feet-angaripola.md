## Ingredients

- pork feet
- salt
- pork pulp
- chorizos
- chicken
- lard
- xitomatl
- garlic
- parsley
- capers
- chiles in vinegar
- olives
- saffron
- pepper
- clove
- culantro, toasted

## Directions

1. Cook the pork feet with necessary salt
2. The next day, cook pork pulp, chorizos and chicken
3. Debone the feet and shred all this meat
4. Fry in a casserole greased with lard
5. When well fried, cover the meat with slices of xitomatl, chopped garlic, parsley, capers, chiles in vinegar and olives
6. Add well-ground saffron, pepper, clove and toasted culantro
7. Add necessary salt and a little broth
8. Cook over two fires until the broth is consumed
