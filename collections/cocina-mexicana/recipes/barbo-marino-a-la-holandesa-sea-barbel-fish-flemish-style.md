## Ingredients

- Sea barbel fish
- Salt
- Parsley
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Boil water with sufficient salt for the fish
2. Once it has boiled sufficiently for the salt to dissolve, remove from heat and let rest
3. Strain so it is very clear and does not blacken the barbel
4. Place the fish in the salt water so it is covered or bathed exactly
5. Heat over fire and as soon as it reaches its first boil, move to gentle heat so it maintains its warmth without boiling
6. Once cooked, drain and arrange on a fish platter with parsley around it
7. Serve with hollandaise sauce separately
