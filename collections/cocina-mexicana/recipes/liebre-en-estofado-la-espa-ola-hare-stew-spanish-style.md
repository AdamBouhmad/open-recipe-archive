## Ingredients

- hare
- white wine
- onion
- oil
- pepper
- clove
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the hare into pieces, clean with a napkin and soak in white wine for eight or ten hours.
2. Remove and brown with fried onion in oil.
3. Add pepper and clove and add all the wine in which it was soaked and some bay leaves.
4. After cooking, remove the bay leaves and serve.
