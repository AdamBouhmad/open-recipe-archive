## Ingredients

- nixtamal from half a bushel of corn
- water
- lard
- aniseed
- twenty-eight egg yolks
- syrup
- pastille

## Directions

1. Grind the nixtamal that results from half a bushel of corn, as if to make atole, and let it settle
2. Bring a little water to a boil with lard and aniseed, and add the settled dough to it, stirring continuously until it releases from the pan
3. Let it cool, then add twenty-eight egg yolks, beating well
4. Fry in lard and serve with syrup and pastille on top
