## Ingredients

- equal parts walnuts and almonds
- a piece of bread soaked in vinegar
- raw garlic
- oil
- salt
- parsley, chopped
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind equal amounts of walnuts and almonds with a piece of bread soaked in vinegar and raw garlic until everything is very finely ground
2. Fry in oil with plenty of salt
3. Pour over the poultry at the time of serving
4. Top with chopped parsley, chiles, olives and other garnishes
