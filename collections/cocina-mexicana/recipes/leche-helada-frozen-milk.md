## Ingredients

- Four cuartillos of fresh milk
- Two beaten egg yolks
- A little cinnamon
- One pound of white sugar

## Directions

1. Cook four cuartillos of fresh milk with two beaten egg yolks
2. Cook a little cinnamon and dissolve it in one pound of white sugar
3. Stir without stopping until it thickens and comes to a boil
4. Strain through a sieve and pour into the freezing vessel
