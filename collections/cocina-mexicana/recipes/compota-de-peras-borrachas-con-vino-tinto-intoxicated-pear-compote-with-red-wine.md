## Ingredients

- Pears, not very ripe
- Water, one glass
- Cinnamon stick
- Cloves, two
- Sugar, two ounces
- Red wine, one glass

## Directions

1. Select pears not very ripe and place them whole in a pot with one glass of water, a stick of cinnamon, two cloves of spice, and two ounces of sugar
2. Cook well covered over embers, and when half cooked, add one glass of red wine
3. After thoroughly cooked, remove the pears from the syrup and thicken the syrup greatly, as this compote requires it
4. Serve hot
