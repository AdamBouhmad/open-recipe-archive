## Ingredients

- Catfish, cleaned
- Butter
- Lettuce, finely chopped
- Green chile, chopped
- Tomato, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- Ground spices including cumin
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the cleaned catfish in butter
2. Cook the finely chopped lettuce
3. In a cazuela with butter, fry the green chile, tomato, garlic, and onion all chopped
4. Once fried, add the lettuce with ground spices of all kinds, including cumin, oil and vinegar
5. Place the catfish in this sauce until well seasoned
