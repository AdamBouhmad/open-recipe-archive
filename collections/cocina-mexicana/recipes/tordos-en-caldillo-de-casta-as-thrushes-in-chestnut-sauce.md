## Ingredients

- thrushes
- salt
- water
- onion
- lard
- flour
- roasted chestnuts
- cilantro
- cinnamon
- clove

## Directions

1. Clean the thrushes and cook them with salt, water, and onion tops
2. In a pot with lard, fry a good amount of chopped onion
3. Add flour until golden
4. Grind roasted chestnuts and add them to the pot
5. Add some of the broth in which the thrushes were cooked
6. Add toasted and ground cilantro, a little cinnamon and powdered clove, and sufficient salt
7. When the sauce thickens, skim it and serve the thrushes in it
