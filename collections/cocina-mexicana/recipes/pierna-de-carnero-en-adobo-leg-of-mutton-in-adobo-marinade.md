## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- oil
- pepper
- onion
- parsley
- garlic cloves, whole

## Directions

1. Let it air for one day
2. Beat it lightly so it comes out more tender
3. Marinate for one day in adobo with oil, pepper, onion, and parsley
4. Insert a whole garlic clove in various parts of the meat
5. Place on the roasting spit over a very vigorous fire so it browns quickly
6. During cooking, turn it frequently, rolling it in its fat and the adobo
7. It should be on the fire for at least an hour and a half
