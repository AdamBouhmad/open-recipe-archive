## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- peppercorns
- salt
- hot water

## Directions

1. Place a pot with four or six ounces of fresh butter on a low burner
2. Add two raw egg yolks, one spoonful of lemon juice, six or eight crushed peppercorns and a little fine salt
3. Stir continuously, agitating it much so the egg does not curdle
4. When it has thickened greatly, add a little hot water
