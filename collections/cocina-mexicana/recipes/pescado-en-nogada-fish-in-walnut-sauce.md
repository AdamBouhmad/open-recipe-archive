## Ingredients

- fish
- walnuts with hard shell removed
- bread
- water
- salt
- cooking oil
- vinegar
- oregano
- onions
- epazote
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Remove the hard shell from the walnuts and boil briefly, then remove the thin skin.
2. Toast lightly and grind with a piece of bread.
3. Loosen with water and season with necessary salt.
4. Add cooking oil, vinegar and sprinkled oregano.
5. Cook the fish in water with salt, two or three onions cut into quarters and one or two sprigs of good epazote.
6. Bathe the fish with the sauce and garnish with olives and broken chiles and raw oil.
