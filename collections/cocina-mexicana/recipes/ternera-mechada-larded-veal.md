## Ingredients

- veal leg
- bacon strips
- lard
- salt
- flour
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- sorrel

## Directions

1. The best meat is from the leg, which is larded with strips of bacon that penetrate well, and seared in lard with salt
2. Then add a little flour, pepper, garlic, parsley and let it cook for two hours
3. If desired, sorrel may also be added, which should be cooked separately with salt and lard, and a little before serving add the sorrel
