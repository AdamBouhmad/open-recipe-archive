## Ingredients

- Large intestines of pig
- Finely chopped onion, in quantity
- Fine salt
- Pig blood
- Pig fat in pieces
- One nutmeg, ground
- Fine pepper
- Clove

## Directions

1. Place the large intestines in hot water
2. Chop onion very finely and grind the necessary fine salt
3. Mix these with the pig blood
4. Cut the pig fat into pieces in sufficient quantity and mix with the blood
5. Grind nutmeg, fine pepper, and clove, and mix slowly into the blood so everything is well combined
6. Fill the intestines that were left in hot water, tying the ends so they do not empty
7. Place on a stick and put in boiling water until cooked, which is known when pricked with a pin if only water comes out
