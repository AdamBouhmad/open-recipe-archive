## Ingredients

- hen, cleaned and without innards
- melted butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic, crushed
- parsley, chopped
- pepper
- bay leaves (2-3)
- olive oil (half cup)
- water
- eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned hen on a grill and baste frequently with melted butter, lemon juice, salt, and crushed garlic
2. When half roasted, place it in a casserole with butter, chopped parsley, pepper, salt, 2-3 bay leaves, the fat remaining from roasting, half a cup of raw olive oil, and enough water to cover it
3. Let it boil until tender
4. Remove the hen and dissolve eight egg yolks in the broth, beating well to thicken
5. Let it boil a little
6. Pour over the hen placed on a platter and serve
