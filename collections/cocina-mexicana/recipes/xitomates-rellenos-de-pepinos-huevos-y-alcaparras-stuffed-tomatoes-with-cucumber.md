## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- parsley
- cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- pickled peppers
- anchovy pieces
- salt
- oil
- breadcrumb scraping

## Directions

1. The tomatoes are prepared as in the previous articles, and are gently squeezed with the fingers so that the water that they usually contain when very ripe comes out; fill them with parsley, cucumbers, hard-boiled eggs, capers and pickled peppers, all chopped very finely, small pieces of anchovy, salt and oil; cover with breadcrumb scraping and cook under a camp oven.
