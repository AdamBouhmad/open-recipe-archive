## Ingredients

- young pigeons
- thin lards of jowl
- garlic cloves
- clove
- salt
- water
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- parsley
- cinnamon
- black pepper
- capers
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- vinegar
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Lard the pigeons with thin lards of jowl, garlic cloves and clove, and place them to cook in salted water
2. After cooking, fry them in lard, remove them and then add roasted and ground tomatoes and chopped parsley
3. After frying this mixture, add salt, clove, cinnamon and pepper, all ground, capers, the already fried pigeons, the broth in which they were cooked and a little vinegar
4. Let them boil until the sauce is somewhat thick, and when removing from heat add olives and oil
