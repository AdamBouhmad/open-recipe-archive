## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- salt
- five egg yolks
- wine
- butter
- sifted sugar
- water
- thick milk or paste

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a table, add salt, five egg yolks, wine, a bit of butter, sifted sugar to make it slightly sweet, and necessary water to combine.
2. Knead well and extend with a rolling pin, brush with butter and fold again.
3. Repeat this process five or six times.
4. Finally extended, cut into squares or as is customary, fill with some paste or thick milk.
5. Place the top layer, seal well not at the edge but about a finger's width inside so the pastry pulls back around the edge.
6. Place some figure of the same dough on top, set on papers and tin sheets, put in the oven.
7. When they come out, sprinkle sifted sugar on top.
