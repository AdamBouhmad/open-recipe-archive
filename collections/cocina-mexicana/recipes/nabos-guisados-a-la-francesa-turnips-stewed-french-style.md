## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- common broth
- substance broth
- fine herbs bouquet
- butter
- curled sauce
- four tablespoons
- calf broth
- allspice
- sugar the size of an egg
- one tablespoon substance broth

## Directions

1. After peeling and cutting the turnips with exactitude, let them have one boil in water and then place them to cook with common broth and substance broth and a small bouquet of fine herbs
2. When they are cooked, seasoned and of good taste, degrease the stew
3. To prepare them in another manner, after they have been golden in the butter, add one tablespoon of flour, sugar and broth, letting them cook as the others
4. Serve with their sauce
