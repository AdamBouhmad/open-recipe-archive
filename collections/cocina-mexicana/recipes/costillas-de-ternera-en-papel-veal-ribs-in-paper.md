## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- bacon
- paper
- oil

## Directions

1. Coat the ribs on both sides with a mixture of bread crumbs, butter, parsley and onion, all very finely chopped, pepper and salt, covering them with thin slices of bacon. Once done, wrap them in white paper coated with oil or butter, place on grills to roast over low heat and serve in the same paper.
