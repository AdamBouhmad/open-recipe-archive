## Ingredients

- fifty oysters
- water
- lard
- parsley
- tender onions
- flour
- white wine

## Directions

1. Add fifty oysters to hot water, and when the boil is about to break, remove and place in cold water
2. Dry afterwards and chop finely, and other fish meat can be added if available
3. Place lard, parsley and tender onions in a pan, reheat and add a pinch of flour and half a glass of white wine
