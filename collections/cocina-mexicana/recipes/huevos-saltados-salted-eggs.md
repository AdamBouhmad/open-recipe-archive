## Ingredients

- egg yolks, cooked
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Mash the cooked egg yolks with vinegar, salt, pepper and oil
2. Add oil drop by drop until a thick sauce forms
3. Cover the egg whites cut into quarters with this sauce
4. Garnish the platter with sprigs of curly parsley
