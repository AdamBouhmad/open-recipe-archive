## Ingredients

- pork sirloin
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the pork piece in a square shape with one finger of fat remaining, leaving the square well covered
2. Make some cuts in the fat that covers it and place on a spit
3. Two hours are sufficient for perfect cooking
4. Serve as a roast or as an entree, with a spicy sauce or with whatever sauce seems best
