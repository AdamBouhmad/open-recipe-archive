## Ingredients

- a piece of cold roasted veal
- butter
- flour
- broth
- cream
- ground pepper
- 25 to 30 asparagus pieces
- mushrooms
- green parsley
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cold roasted veal, cut it into large cubes, and make a butter sauce: let a piece of butter the size of an egg become warm, add a spoonful of flour, let it brown slightly, fill it with broth, add a spoonful of cream and some ground pepper.
2. Once the sauce is ready, add the veal pieces, also 25 to 30 pieces of asparagus and mushrooms, which should first be braised in butter and green parsley; let everything braise together well until no sauce remains.
3. Arrange it in a dish in the shape of a mountain, and place a wreath of puff pastry around it. If asparagus is no longer available, cauliflower may be used instead, which should first be boiled in salt water and cut into pieces.
