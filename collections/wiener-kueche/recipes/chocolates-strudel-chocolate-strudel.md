## Ingredients

- Strudel dough
- 16 deca chocolate
- 12 deca beaten butter
- 16 deca finely ground vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 deca bread crumbs, softened in milk and well pressed out
- 12 deca finely ground almonds
- 6 egg whites
- canary milk or cream for serving

## Directions

1. Treat this strudel exactly as the Ham Strudel (No. 1229), with the difference that it is filled with the following chocolate mass instead of ham
2. Place 16 deca chocolate on a baking sheet in the oven and leave until the chocolate is soft enough to be pressed in with a finger
3. Beat 12 deca butter and mix smooth with the warmed chocolate
4. Mix with 16 deca finely ground vanilla sugar
5. Stir in 8 egg yolks gradually
6. Mix well with 8 deca bread crumbs softened in milk and well pressed out, and 12 deca finely ground almonds, one after another
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
8. Cut the strudel into desired pieces
9. Arrange in a bowl and serve with canary milk or cream
