## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter, beaten
- 20 deca vanilla sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 28 deca finely diced rolls soaked slightly in milk
- 14 deca raisins
- 3 tablespoons fine rum
- 14 deca finely diced suet
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca butter with 20 deca vanilla sugar until mixed
2. Beat in 12 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
3. Add 28 deca finely diced rolls soaked in milk, 14 deca raisins, 3 tablespoons fine rum, and 14 deca finely diced suet
4. Mix well
5. Lightly fold in the snow from 10 egg whites
6. Fill a buttered and floured mold with a cylinder in the center with this mixture
7. Steam covered for 3/4 to 1 hour
8. Turn out onto a platter
9. Fill the center cavity with 10 to 12 deca finely pounded sugar and pour 1/16 liter rum over it
10. Light on the table
11. Serve immediately with desired fruit juice or Chaudeau
