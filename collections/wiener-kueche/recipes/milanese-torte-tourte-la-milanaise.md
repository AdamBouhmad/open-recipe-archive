## Ingredients

- 2 Deka orange blossoms
- 6 tablespoons cold water
- 30 Deka blanched almonds
- 28 Deka beaten butter
- 16 Deka lemon sugar
- 1 knife tip cinnamon
- 8 egg yolks
- 30 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Soak orange blossoms in cold water for approximately 2 hours
2. Strain through a sieve and grind very finely with blanched almonds
3. Mix beaten butter with lemon sugar
4. Add the ground almonds, cinnamon, and egg yolks one by one
5. Stir for 1/2 hour
6. Mix in flour
7. Proceed as in the 'Viennese Torte' (no. 1486)
