## Ingredients

- white turnips
- 5 dkg butter
- 6 pieces sugar cubes
- salt
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips and cut them into thin slices
2. In 5 dkg hot butter, brown 6 sugar cubes
3. Add the turnips and salt
4. Deglaze with a little water or broth and cover
5. Braise for 1/2–1 hour without stirring much
6. Dust lightly before serving
