## Ingredients

- one corncrake
- water
- leeks
- yellow turnips
- cabbage
- celery
- parsley
- various seasonings
- butter
- baked potato dumplings

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the corncrake and put it on the fire with water, following the same proportions as for beef
2. Cook it with leeks, yellow turnips, cabbage, celery, parsley and various seasonings like a beef soup
3. Strain it off, make a thin roux from butter
4. Let it simmer well with this soup
5. Put baked potato dumplings or something similar in the soup pot and serve the soup over them
