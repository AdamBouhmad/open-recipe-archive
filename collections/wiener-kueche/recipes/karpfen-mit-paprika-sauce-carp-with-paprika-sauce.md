## Ingredients

- 10 decilitres butter
- 1 piece finely chopped onion
- approximately 1 kilo cleaned carp, cut into 2-finger wide and 1-finger long pieces
- 5 sprigs parsley tied together
- approximately 1/2 litre sour cream
- paprika to taste
- capers or lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Place 10 decilitres butter in a casserole, add a piece of finely chopped onion
2. Add approximately 1 kilo of cleaned carp, cut into 2-finger wide and 1-finger long pieces, and 5 sprigs parsley tied together
3. Let this roast covered for approximately 15 minutes
4. Pour approximately 1/2 litre sour cream over it
5. Let this simmer covered for 1/4 hour
6. Add paprika to taste, or a tablespoon of capers or lemon juice as desired
7. Let it come to a boil
8. Carefully remove the fish so it does not fall apart
9. Serve with potatoes, noodles, or similar
