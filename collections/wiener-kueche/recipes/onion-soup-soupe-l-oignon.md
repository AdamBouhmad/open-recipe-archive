## Ingredients

- 16 Deca butter
- 4 heads of onion
- 2 liters water
- Flour
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Parsley

## Directions

1. In 16 Deca butter, add 4 onion heads sliced into half rings about two fingers wide
2. Let them roast somewhat
3. Dust with the necessary flour and let them roast until golden
4. Pour in 2 liters water and simmer slowly for 1 hour
5. Season with salt, a little nutmeg, and spices if desired
6. Sprinkle a spoonful of finely chopped parsley over and serve with boiled bread slices in the soup pot
7. The soup can also be passed through a sieve before serving
