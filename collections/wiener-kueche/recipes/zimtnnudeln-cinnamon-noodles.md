## Ingredients

- 1 Vierding (about 4 oz) butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/2 Seidel (about 1/4 liter) lukewarm cream
- 4 spoonfuls beer yeast
- 1 pound fine flour
- 1 Loth (about 0.5 oz) cinnamon
- 1 Vierding (about 4 oz) sugar
- butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, then stir in the 5 whole eggs and 5 egg yolks
2. Pour in the lukewarm cream and 4 spoonfuls of beer yeast
3. Add 1 pound of fine flour
4. Beat the dough well, take it out onto a floured board, and roll it finger-thick
5. Cut the dough out with a spoon or biscuit mold
6. Pound 1 Loth cinnamon and 1 Vierding sugar finely sifted together
7. Coat a pan with butter and layer the noodles in it, filling it up
8. Sprinkle each layer of noodles strongly with the cinnamon-sugar mixture
9. Do not allow them to rise too much and bake them slowly and carefully
