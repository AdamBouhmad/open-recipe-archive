## Ingredients

- 12 boiled potatoes, small variety
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- Sorrel, diced finely
- 1/2 liter cream
- 3 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Peel 12 boiled small potatoes and slice them thinly.
2. Mix with 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped parsley and some salt.
3. Butter a casserole, dust with flour, and line the bottom with the sliced potatoes.
4. Sprinkle with hard-boiled eggs cut into slices and finely diced sorrel.
5. Beat 1/2 liter cream with 3 whole eggs and pour some of this mixture over the eggs and sorrel.
