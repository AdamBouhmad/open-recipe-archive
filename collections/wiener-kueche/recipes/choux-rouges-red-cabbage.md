## Ingredients

- 1 red cabbage, cored and ribs removed
- vinegar
- salt
- 12 decagrams fat
- 1 head of onion, finely cut
- 6 decagrams sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds
- 1/2 liter red wine
- 1/2 liter broth
- approximately 1/2 liter butter sauce

## Directions

1. After coring and removing the ribs from the cabbage, roll it together and cut finely into noodle-like strips.
2. Pour some vinegar over it, mix well with salt and let stand 1/2 hour.
3. In 12 decagrams fat, roast 1 finely cut head of onion.
4. Add the cabbage to the onion with 6 decagrams sugar and 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds.
5. Cover and braise 15 minutes, then stir well.
6. Add 1/2 liter red wine and 1/2 liter broth and continue braising covered until the cabbage is completely soft.
7. Add approximately 1/2 liter butter sauce, stir well and let boil thoroughly.
8. If the cabbage is not sour enough, add the necessary vinegar and salt and let boil again.
9. Serve with smoked meat and sausages.
