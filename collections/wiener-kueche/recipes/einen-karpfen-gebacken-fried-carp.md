## Ingredients

- Carp
- salt
- flour
- water
- breadcrumbs
- fat for frying
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the carp and cut it into pieces
2. Salt the pieces well and let them sit in salt for a while
3. Turn each piece in flour, dip it quickly in fresh water, and coat it again well with fine breadcrumbs
4. When all pieces are prepared, fry them golden brown in hot fat (which must always be very hot; if not hot enough they will be greasy and not crisp)
5. Serve the fish warm, sprinkled with green parsley
6. Fish of all kinds are prepared for frying in this manner
