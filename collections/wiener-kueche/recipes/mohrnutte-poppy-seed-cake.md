## Ingredients

- 18 ounces butter
- 18 ounces sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 18 ounces finely ground poppy seeds
- 10 ounces finely chopped citron
- 6 egg whites
- 18 ounces blanched and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 18 ounces butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 18 ounces sugar for 1 hour
3. Gradually add 6 egg yolks together with 18 ounces finely ground and ground poppy seeds
4. Add 10 ounces finely chopped citron, then gently fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 6 egg whites together with 18 ounces blanched and ground almonds
5. Pour the mass into a desired lined mold and bake in a not very hot oven for 1 hour
6. Sprinkle with sugar or cover with chocolate glaze and treat further as the Nebrücken
