## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter red whipped cream
- 1/2 liter Rhine wine
- 1/4 kilogram sifted sugar
- lemon sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. In a porcelain dish, add 1/2 liter red whipped cream, 1/2 liter Rhine wine, 1/4 kilogram sifted sugar and lemon sugar to taste
2. Place on ice, but must be well covered
3. After approximately 1 hour, add the mixture to a whisk bowl in which it is beaten with the whisk until it becomes foamy
