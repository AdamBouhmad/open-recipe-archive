## Ingredients

- 1 strong tablespoon flour
- 6 tablespoons egg
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter boiling wine
- some cinnamon
- 24 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 1 strong tablespoon flour with 6 tablespoons egg, beat 8 egg yolks in
2. Beat this with 1 liter boiling wine together with some cinnamon and 24 deca sugar on the fire with a whisk in a snow bowl or pass through a sieve into the soup pot as soon as it begins to boil
3. The soup must not boil and, if desired, the flour and cinnamon can be omitted
4. If the soup is not sweet enough, add sugar as needed
