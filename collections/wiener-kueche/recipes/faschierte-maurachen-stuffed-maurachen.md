## Ingredients

- butter
- half pound veal
- several handfuls of maurachen
- parsley
- salt
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- semmel (bread roll) soaked in milk
- cream
- 2 egg yolks
- large maurachen
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Let a piece of butter warm in a casserole; add half a pound of veal and several handfuls of maurachen, which have been washed well beforehand, and let it stew down
2. When stewed, chop it fine with some parsley, add salt, ground pepper and a little nutmeg, along with half a semmel soaked in milk and well pressed out, and pound the whole thing finely in a mortar, to which add a few spoonfuls of cream and 2 egg yolks
3. Take large, well-washed maurachen, salt them, and let them sit in salt for a while; then dry them well, remove the stem, and clean them well from inside
4. Fill them with the minced meat, cut off the stem, and press it back in; then put small pieces of butter and some fresh parsley in a pan, place the maurachen in it, pour several spoonfuls of fresh cream over them, add a good handful of breadcrumbs, and a few small pieces of butter, apply heat from below and above, and let them bake slowly
