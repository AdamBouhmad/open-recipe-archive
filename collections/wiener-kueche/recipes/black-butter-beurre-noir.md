## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams chocolate
- 8 decagrams butter

## Directions

1. Roast the chocolate on a baking sheet in the oven
2. Place the roasted chocolate in a saucepan
3. Stir in 8 decagrams butter and when both are well mixed, place it on ice
