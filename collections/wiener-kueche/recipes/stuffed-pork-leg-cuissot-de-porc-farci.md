## Ingredients

- pork leg
- salt
- pepper
- capers
- shallots
- parsley
- egg
- fat
- root vegetables
- onions
- broth
- vinegar
- glaze or butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bone the pork leg, remove the thick skin (rind) and cut out the meat from the inside where it is crossed by sinews
2. Free the cut meat of sinews, chop it finely together with capers, capers and shallots using a chopping knife, beat in a whole egg, mix well with necessary salt and pepper
3. Spread this mixture on the inside of the prepared leg, roll it up tightly and tie it with twine
4. Meanwhile, place some fat, thin-sliced root vegetables and onions in a casserole, add the tied meat and let it braise uncovered until tender
5. After 1½ hours, pour in some broth and vinegar and let it boil well
6. Remove the meat, untie the twine, brush it with dissolved glaze or coat with butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs if desired and let it roast in the oven until it takes on a golden color
7. Cut the meat into desired pieces, arrange on a platter, garnish with braised rice, turnips or chestnut purée etc. and serve with caramel sauce or the meat's own juices
