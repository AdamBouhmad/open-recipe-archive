## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets (carbonades)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Fat

## Directions

1. After the cutlets (carbonades) have been cut, the meat is held away from each individual bone, the rib bone is scraped and the meat pushed back.
2. The cutlets are beaten flat with a meat mallet or knife handle without damaging the meat.
3. They are salted, and if desired, sprinkled with a little pepper, lightly dipped in flour on both sides and fried on both sides until golden yellow in very hot fat.
4. Each rib bone is then covered with a papillote.
