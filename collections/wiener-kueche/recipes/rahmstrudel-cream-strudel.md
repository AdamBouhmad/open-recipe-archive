## Ingredients

- 20 dkg Flour
- 1 Egg
- Salt
- Butter (walnut-sized)
- Almost 1 dl lukewarm water
- ½ l Cream (for filling)
- 6 dkg Butter (for filling)
- 3 Egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons Sugar
- 2 tablespoons Breadcrumbs
- 3 Egg whites
- 1 l Cream
- ¼ l Milk (for basting)

## Directions

1. Make strudel dough from flour, egg, salt, butter, and lukewarm water
2. After resting, stretch thinly on floured cloth
3. For filling: cream butter, add egg yolks and sugar, mix in cream and breadcrumbs, fold in beaten egg whites (whites should be beaten while dough is resting)
4. Spread filling on dough and roll up
5. Place in buttered baking pan and bake until light brown
6. Baste frequently with hot milk during baking
7. Preparation method is the same as for Potato Strudel
