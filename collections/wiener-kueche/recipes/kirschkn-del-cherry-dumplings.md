## Ingredients

- strudel dough, potato dough or yeast dough
- fresh cherries, 4 to 5 per dumpling

## Directions

1. Prepare as plum dumplings, according to choice of strudel dough, potato dough, or yeast dough
2. Instead of one plum, put 4 to 5 fresh cherries in each
