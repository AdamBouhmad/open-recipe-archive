## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 1 measure cream
- quarter pound sugar
- lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg yolks into a casserole
2. Stir well with 1 measure cream
3. Add quarter pound sugar rubbed with a lemon and a piece of cinnamon
4. Stir on not too fast fire until it comes to a boil once
5. Slowly draw in the snow from the egg whites
6. Arrange the cream on a plate
7. Pour over a cup of currant jelly and marble with a spoon
8. Place the plate in a warm oven; the cream should become only gold yellow, not brown
9. Serve warm
