## Ingredients

- Pigeons, cleaned, dressed, and larded
- Speck (bacon), sliced in thin strips
- Root vegetables
- Onion
- Whole spices
- 5 crushed juniper berries
- Broth
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Black bread, sliced thin
- Sour cream (optional)
- Lemon slices
- Puff pastry or butter paste for garnish

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with speck, thinly sliced root vegetables, onion, whole spices, and crushed juniper berries.
2. Place the prepared, dressed, and larded pigeons in the casserole and let them braise covered for approximately 1/4 hour.
3. After about 1/4 hour, pour some broth, wine, and vinegar over them and let the pigeons braise covered and softly with thinly sliced black bread.
4. Remove the pigeons and cut them lengthwise down the middle.
5. Arrange on a platter and pour the skimmed and strained sauce over them.
6. If desired, add some spoonfuls of sour cream to the strained sauce and bring to a boil again.
7. Pigeons prepared this way can also be filled with liver forcemeat.
8. Arrange lemon slices on the pigeons and garnish with fallen or puff pastry.
