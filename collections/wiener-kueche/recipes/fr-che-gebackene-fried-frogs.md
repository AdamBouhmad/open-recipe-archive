## Ingredients

- Beautiful, large frog legs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Parsley
- Onions
- Good wine vinegar
- Beer batter or eggs and breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the frog legs
2. Marinate them for some time in salt, pepper, fine herbs, parsley, onions and good wine vinegar
3. Dry them thoroughly
4. Coat in beer batter and fry in lard, or alternatively coat in beaten eggs and grated breadcrumbs
5. Serve with fried parsley as garnish
