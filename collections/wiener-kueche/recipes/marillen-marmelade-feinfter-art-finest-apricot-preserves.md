## Ingredients

- 4 kilograms fresh ripe apricots
- 1 kilogram powdered sugar
- half of the apricot kernels, blanched and dissolved

## Directions

1. Break the ripe soft apricots in half and remove the kernels.
2. Place 1 kilogram of apricots with 1.25 kilograms of powdered sugar in a saucepan over heat and cook rapidly while stirring constantly for 1/4 to 1/2 hour.
3. When the apricots are completely cooked and liquid, pass them quickly through a very clean sieve into an earthenware or porcelain bowl.
4. Return the passed mixture immediately to the saucepan on heat and cook for approximately 1/4 hour while stirring.
5. A few minutes before filling the preserves into jars, fold in the blanched almonds made from half of the available apricot kernels.
6. Put only 1 tablespoon of hot preserves into each prepared jar first to warm them, then fill gradually.
7. Place the jars in an airy location and after 8 days cover with perforated parchment paper.
8. Do not skim the preserves while cooking. When cooking the strained mixture, be very careful that it does not become too dark but maintains a beautiful yellow color.
9. The preserves should drip from a spoon.
