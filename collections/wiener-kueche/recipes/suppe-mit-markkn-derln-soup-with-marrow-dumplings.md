## Ingredients

- 30 dkg beef brisket
- 5 dkg marrow
- 1 bread roll
- 1 egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- 3 dkg bread crumbs
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Melt the finely cut marrow, strain through a sieve into a bowl and let solidify
2. Beat until fluffy
3. Mix with 1 egg, 1 bread roll that has been trimmed, soaked in water, and well squeezed
4. Add salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley, and 3 dkg bread crumbs
5. Let stand a while
6. Form small dumplings
7. Place in strained, boiling beef broth
8. Cook slowly for 5 minutes
