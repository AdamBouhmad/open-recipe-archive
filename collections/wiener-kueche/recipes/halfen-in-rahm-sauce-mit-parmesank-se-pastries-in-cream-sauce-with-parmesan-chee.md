## Ingredients

- flour
- pressed yeast
- milk
- melted butter or lard
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- salt
- 4 deca butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 liter cream
- 1/3 teaspoon grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Ordinäre Halfen (Nr. 1275), only without sugar
2. When the halfen are finished baking, lay 2 together with 1/3 teaspoon grated Parmesan cheese between them and arrange on a plate
3. Put 4 deca butter and 2 tablespoons flour in a saucepan and pour in 1 liter cream while stirring constantly, stir on the heat until it boils
4. Pour the boiling cream sauce over the arranged hot halfen, which must be completely covered with sauce, and put them in the hot oven
5. After 10 minutes remove the dish, place on another plate and serve as an entree after the soup, or in the evening for supper or tea
