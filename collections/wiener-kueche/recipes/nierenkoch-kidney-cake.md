## Ingredients

- one whole kidney
- the soft part of a small roll soaked in milk
- one-half quarter pound butter
- one whole egg and two yolks
- finely chopped lemon zest
- fresh parsley
- butter for the sheet
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Chop one whole kidney and the soft part of a small roll that has been soaked in milk and squeezed out
2. Beat one-half quarter pound butter until very fluffy
3. Beat in one whole egg and two yolks
4. Add finely chopped lemon zest and fresh parsley
5. Stir everything together well
6. Make a rim on the sheet and spread both with butter
7. Put the kidney mixture into it
8. Apply heat both above and below
9. Bake it until golden brown and dust with sugar
