## Ingredients

- 1 pineapple
- 8 lemons
- Sugar
- Three quarter pound finely clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the outside of the pineapple on sugar and scrape this onto a plate
2. Cut the pineapple itself into pieces and press the juice through a hair sieve well
3. Do the same with 8 lemons
4. After cooling, stir three quarter pound finely clarified sugar into the mixture
5. Pour into the freezing pan and process
