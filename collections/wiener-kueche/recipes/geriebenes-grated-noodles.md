## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- salt
- 2 whole eggs
- approximately 2 liters beef broth

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 liter flour on a board, make a well in the middle
2. Add salt and 2 whole eggs
3. Knead into a very firm dough
4. Rub through a grater, spreading grated pieces on board with flat hand to dry before cooking
5. In approximately 2 liters boiling beef broth, cook grated dough with constant stirring
6. Cook 8-10 minutes
