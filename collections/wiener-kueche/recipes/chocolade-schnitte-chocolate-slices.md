## Ingredients

- 16 Deca butter
- 16 Deca sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 Deca almonds, blanched, chopped and sieved
- 16 Deca grated chocolate
- 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 12 Deca flour
- transparent chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Place 16 Deca butter in a deep bowl and stir very little
2. Mix in gradually 16 Deca sugar, 6 egg yolks, 16 Deca blanched chopped and sieved almonds, and 16 Deca grated chocolate
3. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites from 6 eggs with 12 Deca flour
4. Spread on a butter-brushed baking sheet knife-back width thick and proceed as with the Genua-Schnitte
5. Cover heavily with transparent chocolate glaze
6. This dough must not be stirred long like the Genua-Schnitte dough, or it will collapse when baking
