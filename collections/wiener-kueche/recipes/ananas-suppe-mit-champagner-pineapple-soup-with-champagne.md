## Ingredients

- 1 large peeled pineapple
- 8 deca crushed sugar
- 16 deca sugar
- 3/4 liter water
- 8 deca sugar
- 1 bottle Champagne
- 1/2 liter Rhine wine
- 16 deca sugar
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the flesh of a large peeled pineapple into small pieces, scatter these with 8 deca crushed sugar and set on ice.
2. Place the skin and remains in a bowl, mix 16 deca sugar with it and strain the crushed remains through a sieve to the cut pineapple.
3. Place it in a saucepan, let it simmer with 3/4 liter water with 8 deca sugar, strain it once more through a sieve and when it has cooled, pour in 1 bottle Champagne, 1/2 liter Rhine wine with 16 deca sugar and the juice of 1 lemon, mix everything together and then set it on ice in a bowl for several hours.
