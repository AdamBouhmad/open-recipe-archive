## Ingredients

- fine green peas
- salt water
- butter
- green parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook fine green peas uncovered in salt water until tender
2. Drain the water thoroughly
3. Arrange the peas on a platter
4. Place a piece of butter mixed with finely chopped green parsley and some salt on top of the peas in small pieces
5. Serve
