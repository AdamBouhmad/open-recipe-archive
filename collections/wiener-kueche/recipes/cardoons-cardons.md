## Ingredients

- cardoon leaves
- water
- ham slices
- broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the stalk one by one, cut them to finger length
2. Cook with plenty of water and ham slices until the fine skin can be rubbed away
3. Drain and rub each piece lightly with the fingers so they appear fleshy
4. Cook them gently in some strained broth with wine, but only enough to cover the cardoons, covered
5. Remove from broth onto cloth, arrange nicely and cover with ham slices that were cooked in the broth
