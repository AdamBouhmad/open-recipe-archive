## Ingredients

- half a pound of sago, rinsed several times
- three seidels of boiled milk
- an arbitrary amount of sugar
- some cinnamon
- eggs

## Directions

1. Add sago which has been rinsed several times to three seidels of boiled milk together with an arbitrary amount of sugar and some cinnamon
2. When the sago has swollen well in the milk and cooked thick, bind it with eggs
3. Serve it cold or warm
4. Be careful that the milk does not curdle, but with this attention you will get a very good soup
