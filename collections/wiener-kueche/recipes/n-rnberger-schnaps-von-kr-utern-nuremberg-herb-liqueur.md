## Ingredients

- 2 Quentchen each of flowers of Brandoſt (Origanum vulgare), wormwood buds, rosemary leaves, sage leaves, strawberry leaves and roots, betony leaves (Betonica officinalis), leaves and flowers of milk thistle (Carduus Mariam), red rose petals, lichen saxatilis
- 1 Quentchen each of the root of iris (Iris german.), calamus root, white Diptuna, and red gentian

## Directions

1. Gather these plants around St. John's Day
2. Clean and wash them
3. Allow the brandy to infuse with them for 14 days in moderate sun or oven heat
4. A second infusion can be made
