## Ingredients

- two small chickens
- good cream
- lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well, wash several times with warm water, salt them
2. Cut into usual pieces and let sit in salt for a quarter hour
3. Put good cream in a casserole
4. Cut a lemon into thin slices and carefully remove the pits
5. Put half the lemon slices in the cream, then half of the chicken pieces
6. Continue layering: cream, remaining lemon slices, remaining chicken
7. Top with cream and three to four Loth of butter in small pieces
8. Note: shake the dish rather than stir it, and do not cook longer than until done, or the chickens will lose their appearance and good taste
9. When serving, arrange the chickens on a dish, place the lemon slices on top, and pour the sauce over
