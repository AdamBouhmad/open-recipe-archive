## Ingredients

- 1½ liters flour
- ½ liter milk
- 12 deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 deca sugar (vanilla or regular)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ liter sour cream
- 24 deca passed poppy seeds or 24 deca breadcrumbs soaked in milk and pressed out, or ½ liter soft breadcrumbs
- 8 egg whites
- white breadcrumbs
- ¼ liter hot milk
- 2 egg yolks
- cold milk
- ⅛ liter milk
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare a strudel dough from 1½ liters flour and let it rest for ½ hour
2. Beat together 12 deca butter and 8 egg yolks gradually
3. Mix in 12 deca sugar (vanilla or regular), ½ teaspoon salt, and ½ liter sour cream
4. Blend in 24 deca passed poppy seeds or breadcrumbs (either 24 deca soaked in milk and pressed out, or ½ liter soft breadcrumbs)
5. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 8 egg whites
6. Stretch the strudel dough as described in the Young Strudel recipe, spread it evenly with the filling, and sprinkle with white breadcrumbs
7. Roll up and treat as described in the Ham Strudel recipe
8. If desired to prevent dryness, pour ¼ liter hot milk over it halfway through baking
9. After baking, cut into desired pieces, place on a serving dish using a pastry server, and sprinkle with finely crushed sugar
10. Beat 2 egg yolks with a few spoonfuls of cold milk, pour into ⅛ liter of hot milk, sweeten to taste, and serve separately as a sauce in a sauceboat
11. This milk is called 'Canary Milk'
