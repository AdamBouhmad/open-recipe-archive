## Ingredients

- approximately 1/8 liter lingonberries
- red wine
- cinnamon as desired
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Use the same procedure as for the quince sauce, but the lingonberries must be crushed and red wine used
2. You can also add 1 pinch of cinnamon
