## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg nuts
- 30 dkg flour
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 8 dkg hot butter
- 5 dkg sugar

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 30 dkg flour, 3 eggs, and salt
2. Cut into wide noodles
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook for 5 minutes
4. Drain and pour cold water over them
5. Place in a casserole with 8 dkg hot butter
6. Heat in the oven and arrange on a round platter
7. Mix the grated nuts with 5 dkg sugar and sprinkle over the noodles
