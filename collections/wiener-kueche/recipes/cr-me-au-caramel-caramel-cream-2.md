## Ingredients

- 12 deca crushed sugar
- 1/4 liter cold water
- 1/2 liter cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 6 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Put 12 deca crushed sugar in a saucepan and let it brown
2. Gradually pour 1/4 liter cold water onto it
3. Let it boil well and then cool
4. Meanwhile, beat 1/2 liter cream with 4 egg yolks and 6 deca sugar
5. Put this in a snow kettle
6. Add the cooled caramel sugar
7. Beat over heat until the mass begins to thicken
8. Use immediately
