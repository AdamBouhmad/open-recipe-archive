## Ingredients

- 4 spoons milk
- 7 dkg vanilla sugar
- 4 dkg grated chocolate
- 14 dkg grated nuts
- pinch cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook 4 spoons milk with 7 dkg vanilla sugar, 4 dkg grated chocolate, 14 dkg grated nuts and a pinch of cinnamon in a small pot for a few minutes
2. Let it cool
