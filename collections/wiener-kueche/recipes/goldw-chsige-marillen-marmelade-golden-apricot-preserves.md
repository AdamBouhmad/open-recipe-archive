## Ingredients

- ripe soft apricots
- sugar equal in weight to the apricots without kernels

## Directions

1. Break the ripe soft apricots in half and remove the kernels.
2. Place the apricots with sugar equal to their weight in an earthenware dish, stir well together, and let stand covered overnight.
3. The next day place them in a saucepan over heat and cook while stirring constantly until the preserves reach the correct consistency, being very careful that it does not lose its yellow color and become dark through overcooking.
4. Fill hot into jars as indicated in the Finest Apricot Preserves recipe.
