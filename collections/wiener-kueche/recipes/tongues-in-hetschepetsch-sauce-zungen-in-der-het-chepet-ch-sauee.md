## Ingredients

- tongue
- salt water
- 4 to 5 spoonfuls of preserved hetschepetsch
- sugar
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in salt water until tender and cut it into thin slices.
2. Thin 4 to 5 spoonfuls of preserved hetschepetsch with some sugar and white wine to make a sauce, pour it on the bottom of the dish, and arrange the sliced tongue in two rows on the sauce, but in such a way that you leave an empty space in the middle of the dish so that the sauce is visible.
