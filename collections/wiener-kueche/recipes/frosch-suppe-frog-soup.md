## Ingredients

- frogs, cleaned
- butter
- finely chopped green parsley
- flour
- pea water
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- cream
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Braise the cleaned frogs in a piece of butter with finely chopped green parsley
2. Dust with a couple spoonfuls of flour
3. Braise again
4. Fill with pea water
5. Add a little salt and nutmeg flower
6. When the soup has reduced and the frogs are soft, add 2 spoonfuls of cream
7. Serve over toasted bread
