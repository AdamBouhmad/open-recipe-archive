## Ingredients

- 28 Deca caster sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites
- 24 Deca fine flour
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Lightly fold in the snow from 12 egg whites simultaneously with 24 Deca fine flour
3. Fill a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour with this mixture
4. Bake at low heat for 3/4 to 1 hour
5. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and let it cool
6. Place it on a sheet of writing paper and cover with glaze of your choice
7. Optionally, after baking, cut the cake into layers, spread with jam, layer them together, and cover with glaze
8. Optionally, the stiffly whipped snow from 3 egg whites can be mixed with 12 Deca finely sifted sugar, 2 squares of grated chocolate, or 2 tablespoons of strained strawberries or raspberries, and this can be used instead of jam to spread between the layers
