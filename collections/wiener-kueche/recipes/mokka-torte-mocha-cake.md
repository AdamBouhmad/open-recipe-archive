## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- flour
- sugar
- eggs
- 12 dkg tea butter
- 8 dkg sugar
- strong black coffee
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons black coffee
- 8 dkg butter
- swelled chopped almonds
- sugar for dusting
- for glaze: sugar
- black coffee

## Directions

1. Make a biscuit torte from 6 egg yolks
2. For cold stirred filling: stir together 12 dkg tea butter with 8 dkg sugar and mix in several tablespoons of strong black coffee
3. For warm beaten filling: beat 7 dkg sugar with 2 egg yolks and 4 tablespoons black coffee over steam until thick, cool, and stir in 8 dkg butter
4. Fill the torte with either filling
5. The torte can be spread with cream inside and outside and piped with rosettes using a pastry bag
6. For this you need about twice as much cream as the basic amount
7. If the torte is only spread with cream on the outside, sprinkle swelled chopped almonds on top; if filled only inside with cream, cover with coffee glaze
8. For the glaze: add several tablespoons of hot black coffee to the sugar instead of rum and water, and stir well
9. The sugar must be very finely sifted if piping the torte
