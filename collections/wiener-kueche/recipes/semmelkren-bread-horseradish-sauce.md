## Ingredients

- a little flour
- a piece of butter or skimmed fat
- one handful breadcrumbs
- broth as needed
- grated horseradish
- sugar
- two spoonfuls milk cream
- a little saffron

## Directions

1. Let the flour in a piece of butter or skimmed fat run slightly brown
2. Toast it with one handful of breadcrumbs for a few moments
3. Pour in as much broth as necessary to achieve proper consistency
4. Finally add grated horseradish, some sugar, two spoonfuls of milk cream, and a little saffron
