## Ingredients

- pike
- lard
- anchovies
- wood chips
- thin spit
- paper
- fat
- anchovy sauce
- potatoes
- lemon slice or parsley

## Directions

1. After scaling, opening and cleaning the pike, skin it by making a cut on the back between flesh and skin from tail to head, making another similar cut on the belly, then pulling the skin down toward the tail
2. Dry the fins and tail somewhat
3. Skewer the pike alternately with lard and anchovy pieces on both sides in two rows in a slanted direction toward the head
4. Sprinkle the pike with wood chips
5. Place it on a thin spit, bind it with triple paper that has been brushed with fat, and roast it at strong heat for approximately 1 hour
6. Remove the paper 1/2 hour before serving so the lard and fish take on a nice color
7. Remove from spit and arrange in a long dish
8. Serve with anchovy sauce and potatoes
9. Place a lemon slice or sprig of parsley in the fish's opening
