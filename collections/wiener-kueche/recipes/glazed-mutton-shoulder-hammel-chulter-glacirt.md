## Ingredients

- Mutton shoulder
- Bacon
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- Poultry forcemeat
- String
- Sauce-Hash or truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Separate all bones from the shoulder
2. Lard it with fine bacon
3. Turn it repeatedly in melted butter, salt, pepper, finely chopped herbs, mushrooms, and truffles
4. Cover it with fine poultry forcemeat
5. Roll it up and bind it with string, giving it a nice shape
6. Cook it like a fricandeau
7. Serve with a sauce-hash or truffle sauce
