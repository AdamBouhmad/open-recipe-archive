## Ingredients

- 8 kohlrabi
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 6 ounces butter
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- White soup
- Butter sauce
- Meat for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi and cut them into pieces of your choice
2. Cook them tender in salt water
3. Place the drained kohlrabi in a casserole with 6 ounces of butter and 1 tablespoon of parsley
4. Pour enough cold water over them so they are covered and braise covered until tender
5. If the liquid evaporates and the kohlrabi are not yet tender, add the necessary white soup
6. Pour a thin butter sauce over them and bring to a boil again
7. Cook the fine greens from the kohlrabi in salt water, cut finely, and mix with butter sauce into the kohlrabi
8. Garnish with fried or braised meat
