## Ingredients

- 8 pounds ripe apricots
- apricot kernels from the pits
- 5 pounds 8 loth sugar

## Directions

1. Take 8 pounds of very ripe apricots
2. Remove the pits
3. Press through a fine sieve until nothing remains but the skins, which are discarded
4. Place the pressed apricots over low charcoal heat
5. Allow the liquid to evaporate
6. Shell and cut the apricot kernels lengthwise
7. Add a handful of these kernels to the marmalade
8. Stir well and set aside
9. Clarify and cook 5 pounds 8 loth of sugar as described
10. Carefully stir in the marmalade
11. When it begins to thicken and drops cleanly from a spoon, set it to cool
12. Fill into jars or boxes
