## Ingredients

- yeast dough as per recipe 1253
- 1/3 liter finely pounded poppy seed
- 1/4 liter boiling milk
- 12 deca sugar
- poppy seed filling as specified

## Directions

1. Prepare the yeast dough from recipe 1253 and let it rise
2. Roll out the risen dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a knife back
3. Prepare filling: stir finely pounded poppy seed into boiling milk and cook with sugar
4. Add raisins as desired to the filling
5. Spread the filling on the dough
6. Fold the longer side of the dough 3 finger widths over, brush this fold with poppy seed filling
7. Roll up the dough completely, place on a butter-greased baking sheet
8. Cover and let rise in a warm place
9. Brush the strudel strongly with beaten egg and bake in the oven
