## Ingredients

- One beaver tail
- Bacon
- Truffles
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Roots
- Dried herbs
- Bay leaves
- Spices
- Lemon slices
- Salt
- Oyster or truffle sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Take the tail from a beaver, lard it finely with bacon and truffles
2. Place in a casserole over heat
3. Add wine, vinegar, roots, dried herbs, bay leaves, spices, lemon slices and salt
4. Cook until tender
5. Serve with oyster or truffle sauce if desired
