## Ingredients

- 6 washed and separated tomatoes
- Some onion
- 16 Deca red rice
- 8 Deca hot butter
- 2 liters water
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Stew the tomatoes with some onion
2. Add the red rice to 8 Deca hot butter
3. Pour in 2 liters water
4. Simmer uncovered for about 1.5 hours slowly
5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese
6. The soup can be served passed or unpassed; if passed, add cut bread starch
