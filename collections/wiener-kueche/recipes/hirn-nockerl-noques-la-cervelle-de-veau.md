## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter
- 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
- 20 Deka peeled, diced, milk-soaked breadcrumbs
- 1/2 pound veal brains, cooked in salted water and passed through a sieve
- some salt
- 1 knife point pepper
- 6 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deka butter until creamy
2. Stir in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
3. Mix in the breadcrumbs and veal brains
4. Add salt, pepper and flour
5. Form into Nockerl as with the preceding recipe
