## Ingredients

- 16 Deka beaten butter
- 16 Deka powdered sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 16 Deka blanched, very finely ground almonds with egg white
- 4 Deka trimmed rolls soaked in milk
- 10 egg whites
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold

## Directions

1. Beat 16 Deka butter with 16 Deka powdered sugar.
2. Add 10 egg yolks one after another and stir.
3. Mix well with 16 Deka blanched, finely ground almonds and 4 Deka trimmed rolls soaked in milk and well drained.
4. Lightly fold in the snow of 10 egg whites.
5. Fill a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour with this mass, cover and bake for 1/2 hour in steam or in the oven.
6. If it is baked in steam, any desired fruit juice is added.
