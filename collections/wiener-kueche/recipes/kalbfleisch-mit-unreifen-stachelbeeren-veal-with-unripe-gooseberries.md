## Ingredients

- Veal
- Warm water
- Salt
- Unripe gooseberries
- Bread slices, lightly toasted
- Meat broth
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Set the meat with warm water on the fire, salt it, skim it clean, and let it simmer until soft
2. Meanwhile, place unripe gooseberries with a pair of lightly toasted bread slices in a pot
3. Pour meat broth and a little vinegar over them and let them simmer until very soft
4. Press them through a sieve
