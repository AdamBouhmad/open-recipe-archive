## Ingredients

- 6 bread rolls
- ½ liter milk
- 1–2 eggs
- ½ kg apples
- sugar
- raisins
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice the bread rolls into leaves, place in a bowl, pour over with milk beaten with 1–2 eggs and let stand for 15 minutes.
2. Butter a casserole dish well, add a layer of bread, then a layer of peeled apples sliced into leaves.
3. Sprinkle with sugar and raisins, drizzle melted butter over.
4. Fill the form this way in layers, close with bread rolls, drizzle with butter, bake in the oven for 30–45 minutes.
5. Serve in the form.
