## Ingredients

- Cabbage heads
- Salted water
- Onions
- Flour
- Butter
- Caraway
- Broth
- Wine vinegar
- Mutton cutlets

## Directions

1. Let the cabbage heads cut in halves cook tender in salted water, then chop them finely, let finely cut onions and some flour brown in butter, add the cabbage with a little caraway, let it steam so, pour some broth and wine vinegar to it, let it boil up in it, and garnish it when serving with the mentioned cutlets.
