## Ingredients

- 1 piece of meat
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 large finely chopped onion
- broth or water
- potatoes, diced
- carrots, cut into noodle-like strips
- celery
- green peas
- mushrooms
- salt water for cooking vegetables
- cream
- red wine

## Directions

1. Pound the meat, wash it, salt and pepper it, and lard it.
2. In 6 dkg hot fat, brown 1 large finely chopped onion until dark brown.
3. Add the meat and pour in broth or water.
4. Let it braise covered for 2 to 2½ hours, turning often and adding liquid as needed.
5. 45 minutes before serving, add the diced potatoes, noodle-cut carrots, celery, green peas, and mushrooms that have been pre-cooked in salt water.
6. Let them braise until tender.
7. Dust the sauce lightly with flour, add cream and red wine, and bring to a boil.
8. Serve the meat and vegetables on a long platter with the sauce.
