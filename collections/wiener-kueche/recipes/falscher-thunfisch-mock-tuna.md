## Ingredients

- meat
- salt
- root vegetables
- 1 onion ring
- 5 peppercorns and spice seeds
- 1 bay leaf
- approximately 1 dl vinegar
- 3 egg yolks
- butter
- 1 anchovy, boned and passed through a sieve

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, lightly salt it, roll it, and bind it.
2. Cut the root vegetables into leaf-like slices.
3. Put them in a pot with the onion ring, peppercorns, spice seeds, bay leaf, vinegar, and salt in plenty of cold water.
4. Add the meat and cook for 1 to 1½ hours.
5. Strain 1 liter of the broth into a snow pot and let it cool.
6. Beat it with 3 egg yolks over steam (over a pot with hot water) until thick.
7. Stir in butter melted in thin threads, but not hot.
8. At the end, mix in the boned and passed anchovy.
9. Remove the meat, unbind it, slice it, arrange it on a long platter, pour the sauce over it, and serve immediately.
