## Ingredients

- 3 hard egg yolks
- 2 rolls, rind removed and soaked in water
- cold water
- vinegar
- oil
- mustard
- salt
- 4 h chives, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Pass the hard egg yolks, then the squeezed-out rolls.
2. Mix with some cold water, vinegar, oil, mustard and salt.
3. Finally stir in the finely chopped chives.
4. The sauce can also be served with cold meat.
