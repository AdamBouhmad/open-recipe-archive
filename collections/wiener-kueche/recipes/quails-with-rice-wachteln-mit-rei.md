## Ingredients

- cleaned quails
- bacon slices
- veal meat
- green parsley
- chives
- salt
- half a glass white wine
- several spoonfuls of good broth
- rice for garnish

## Directions

1. Put cleaned quails, some bacon slices, a piece of veal, a little green parsley, chives, some salt, half a glass white wine, and several spoonfuls of good broth into a casserole
2. Cook everything together on a gentle coal fire
3. When cooked, remove the quails
4. Clean the sauce of fat and strain through a sieve
5. Lay the quails in the middle of the dish
6. Pour the small sauce over them
7. Surround them with a rice border
