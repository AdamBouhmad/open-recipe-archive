## Ingredients

- beautiful meaty chickens
- piece of veal
- lard
- bread roll soaked in milk
- two eggs
- nutmeg
- salt
- parsley roots
- yellow turnips
- beef broth
- mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and soak them briefly in warm water so they become white
2. Cut them open lengthwise along the back and remove the legs, keeping the thighs and wings attached
3. Take a piece of veal, remove the skin and all fibrous parts
4. Chop the veal together with some lard into small pieces
5. Soak a bread roll in milk, squeeze it well, beat two eggs into it, and stir over heat until very thick
6. Pound the meat and bread together in a mortar until very fine
7. Add nutmeg and salt
8. Dry the chickens with a cloth, fill with the stuffing
9. Sew the skin back together on the back and shape properly
10. Cover with thin slices of lard
11. Place in a casserole with parsley roots and yellow turnips
12. Pour one or two ladles of beef broth over
13. Braise slowly for half an hour and prepare a mushroom sauce
14. When serving, remove the lard from the chickens, place on a dish, and pour the sauce over
