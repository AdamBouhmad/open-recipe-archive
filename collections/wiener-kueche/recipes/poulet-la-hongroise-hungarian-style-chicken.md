## Ingredients

- 8 deciliter lard
- finely cut onions (to taste)
- 2 cleaned and dressed chickens
- 1 liter soup
- raw potatoes, cut into desired pieces
- paprika (to taste)

## Directions

1. In a casserole with 8 deciliter lard, add finely cut onions to taste
2. Place 2 cleaned and dressed chickens inside and braise covered for 1-2 hours
3. Pour 1 liter soup over the chickens
4. Add raw potatoes cut into desired pieces
5. Braise everything together until tender, season with paprika
6. Serve as paprika chicken
