## Ingredients

- veal shank
- cooked smoked tongues
- sardelles
- bone marrow
- butter
- onions
- cloves
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- wine
- broth
- capers
- cream

## Directions

1. Flay a medium shank, wash and salt it.
2. Prepare cooked smoked tongues, sardelles, and bone marrow; cut all into lengthwise pieces.
3. Make holes in the shank with a narrow knife and lard it alternately with tongues, sardelles, and marrow.
4. Line a casserole with butter, three onions stuck with cloves, lemon peel and juice.
5. Place the shank on top, pour a seidel of wine and half as much broth.
6. Cover well, apply heat above and below, and let braise slowly for two hours, turning several times.
7. When completely braised and tender, chop a handful of capers.
8. Place the shank in another casserole, strain the sauce over it, add the capers and some spoonfuls of cream.
9. Let it braise a little longer, arrange the shank on a dish, pour sauce over it, and serve.
