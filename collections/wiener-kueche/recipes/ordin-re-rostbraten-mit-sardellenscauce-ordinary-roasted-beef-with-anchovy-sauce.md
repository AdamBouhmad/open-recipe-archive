## Ingredients

- Beef from the rib
- Salt
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Beef broth
- Anchovies
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Take the beef designated for this from the rib, cut as much as needed from it, salt it, knock it, and turn it in butter and bread crumbs
2. Roast it sufficiently on the grill
3. Then place it in a casserole, add some beef broth, anchovies, and the juice of a lemon
4. Let it reduce briefly and then serve
