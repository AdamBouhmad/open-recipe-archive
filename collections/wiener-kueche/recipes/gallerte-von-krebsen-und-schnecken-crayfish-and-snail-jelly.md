## Ingredients

- 30 crayfish
- 30 snails
- One loth of fresh butter
- Two cleaned and finely chopped veal feet
- 8 pounds (two and a half measures) of spring water
- Half a quench of ground nutmeg
- A handful of chervil herb

## Directions

1. Clean the crayfish and snails as if they were to be eaten
2. Pound them small
3. Set them with the fresh butter on a gentle fire
4. Add the chopped veal feet and spring water
5. Let them cook together until only one pound of broth remains
6. Throw in the ground nutmeg and handful of chervil herb
7. Let it boil once more and press it through linen cloth
8. Set it in the cellar so it congeals like a jelly
9. This amount provides food for four to five days
10. A wasted patient who digests well takes two spoonfuls each morning, midday and evening, dissolved in about six spoonfuls of unsalted chicken broth
