## Ingredients

- About 1 pound bitter almonds
- 1 quarter pound sweet almonds
- 2.5 pounds sugar
- White of about 20 eggs

## Directions

1. Proceed as described for sweet macaroons
2. Shape the bitter macaroons into a round form
