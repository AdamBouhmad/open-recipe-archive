## Ingredients

- cleaned and peeled horseradish
- 3 tablespoons hot broth
- 2 tablespoons oil
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper (if desired)
- finely cut chives (if desired)
- crushed paprika (if desired)

## Directions

1. Grate one spoonful of cleaned and peeled horseradish, place it in a bowl and pour 3 tablespoons of hot broth over it, set it aside and let it cool
2. Then stir the horseradish with 2 tablespoons oil, some salt and the necessary vinegar together well
3. When serving, add some pepper on top, if desired finely cut chives and some crushed paprika
4. This horseradish is also used for turnips, strudel and all fish dishes
