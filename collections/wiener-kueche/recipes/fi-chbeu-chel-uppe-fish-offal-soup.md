## Ingredients

- 1 roe fish offal
- 16 dkg vegetables
- 1 bread roll
- fish head
- salt water
- vinegar
- 5 dkg butter or fat
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and slice the vegetables into leaves.
2. Roast them with 1 onion, 5 peppercorns and spice kernels, 1 bay leaf, a trace of thyme in 5 dkg hot fat until dark.
3. Wash the halved fish head and cook it in salted water.
4. Pour this water over the vegetables, season with vinegar and braise for 1-1.25 hours, stirring frequently.
5. Strain the soup and add a dark roux made from 5 dkg butter or fat.
6. Season with salt and vinegar.
7. Add the offal, which has been cooked separately in a small pot for 15-20 minutes.
8. Cook together well and serve with bread slices.
