## Ingredients

- Beautiful ripe apricots
- Crushed sugar
- Fruit brandy
- Wine batter

## Directions

1. Select beautiful ripe apricots, remove the skin, cut into two pieces, then marinate with crushed sugar and fruit brandy.
2. Remove them from the marinade, dry them off, and coat them in wine batter.
3. Bake them quickly, glaze them lastly with sugar, and serve them to the table.
