## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 30 deca Perugia sugar
- 10 deca finely ground and sifted almonds with skin
- 26 deca finely grated chocolate
- 1 knife tip cinnamon
- equal amount finely chopped lemon peel
- 1 ground clove
- 5 egg whites
- 10 deca flour

## Directions

1. Gradually beat 5 egg yolks into 16 deca beaten butter
2. Add 30 deca Perugia sugar and beat for 1/2 hour
3. Mix in 10 deca finely ground and sifted almonds with skin, 26 deca finely grated chocolate, 1 knife tip cinnamon, equal amount finely chopped lemon peel, and 1 ground clove
4. Lightly mix in the snow of 5 egg whites with 10 deca flour
5. Fill into a buttered and floured form and bake approximately 1 hour in a moderately hot oven
6. Remove from oven and let stand in the form for 1/4 hour, then turn out and dust with finely ground sugar when completely cool
7. Alternatively, bake this mass in 4 layers. In this case, lightly mix the stiffly beaten snow of 3 egg whites with 10 deca finely ground sugar and 2 tablets of grated chocolate, spread this on 3 baked layers, stack them so that a spread side meets a dry side, place the fourth dry layer on top and cover the whole with chocolate or desired glaze. Baking in several layers is advisable as it easily becomes greasy in the middle otherwise
