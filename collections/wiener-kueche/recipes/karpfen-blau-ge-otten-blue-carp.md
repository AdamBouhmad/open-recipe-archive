## Ingredients

- 1.2 kilograms carp
- 6 decagrams root vegetables
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Water for court-bouillon

## Directions

1. In a large casserole, cook a court-bouillon (see explanations) with plenty of salt and vinegar for 30 minutes
2. Prepare the fish and halve it
3. Cut into three-finger-width pieces
4. Place these pieces in the boiling court-bouillon
5. Withdraw from heat immediately and let rest for 8-10 minutes
6. Lift onto a long platter
7. Serve warm with boiled potatoes, or cold with vinegar and oil, or with sauce tartare
