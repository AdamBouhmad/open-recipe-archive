## Ingredients

- 1/2 l milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- 12-15 dkg flour
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Whisk the milk with 1 egg, salt and flour until smooth.
2. Pour some melted fat into a Palatschinken pan, let it get hot, pour it back, pour in a small ladle of dough, let it spread thin and round, and fry on both sides until light brown.
3. Add fat to the pan again, let it get hot and repeat.
4. Roll the finished Fridatten, cut into noodle-like strips and serve warm.
