## Ingredients

- 1 liter beans, soft-cooked in salt water and drained
- 16 ounces butter
- 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Shake the drained beans with 16 ounces butter, 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley, salt, white pepper, nutmeg, and the juice of 1 lemon until the butter is incorporated
2. Arrange them heaped on a platter and garnish with roasted breadcrumbs or with any desired meat
