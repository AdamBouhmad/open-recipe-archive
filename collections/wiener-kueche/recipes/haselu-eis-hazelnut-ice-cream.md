## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 6 dkg hazelnuts
- 5 dkg sugar
- butter for coating the sheet
- 3 dl milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 piece vanilla
- 9 dkg sugar

## Directions

1. Roast 6 dkg hazelnuts in a pan with 5 dkg sugar until dark, place on a buttered sheet and cool.
2. Roughly crush them in a mortar and then grind them finely in the mortar with some milk.
3. Mix them into the whipped vanilla cream (3 dl milk, 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1 piece vanilla, 9 dkg sugar).
4. The sugar used for roasting the hazelnuts must be deducted from the vanilla ice sugar because the mass will otherwise not freeze.
5. Pass the whole thing through a hair sieve, cool it and freeze like Vanilla Ice Cream.
