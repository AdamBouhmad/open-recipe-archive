## Ingredients

- 1 heaping plate of strawberries
- 1 1/2 liters wine
- 1/2 liter wine
- 1 liter water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- baked bread slices

## Directions

1. Let 1 heaping plate of strawberries braise with 1 1/2 liters wine until the strawberries fall apart, then pass through a sieve
2. Pour 1/2 liter wine and 1 liter water onto it, add sugar and cinnamon, stir together well
3. Serve with baked bread slices in the soup pot
