## Ingredients

- 14 deka flour
- 1/4 liter milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg whites (snow)
- 8 deka hot butter

## Directions

1. Place 14 deka flour in a deep bowl
2. While stirring constantly, pour in 1/4 liter milk
3. When smooth, add 5 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon salt, one after another
4. Stir well together
5. Gently fold in the snow from 5 egg whites
6. Pour the dough into a casserole containing 8 deka hot butter
7. Proceed further as described for the kleine Kaiserschmarn
8. The sugar may also be omitted
