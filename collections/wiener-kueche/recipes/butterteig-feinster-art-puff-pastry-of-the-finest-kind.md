## Ingredients

- Cold and firm butter
- Finest flour
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Lay butter on the flour and beat them together widely with the butter.
2. Turn the dough so that the two open sides lie right and left, tap lightly with the rolling pin on the middle of the dough and push the butter through rolling until toward the edge.
3. Once the dough is rolled out lengthwise into a strip, fold it from right to left again so that it lies in 3 equal parts over one another.
4. Place it on a tray dusted with flour and let it rest covered at a cold place.
5. After about 1/4 hour, place the dough again on the flour-dusted board so that the open sides lie right and left, roll it out and proceed as before.
6. Repeat this three times, after which the dough can be used in various ways, for strudel, pastries, etc.
