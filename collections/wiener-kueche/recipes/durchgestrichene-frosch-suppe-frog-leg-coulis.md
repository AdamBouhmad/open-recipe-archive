## Ingredients

- 20 frog legs
- 8 decagrams butter
- 1 piece onion
- 4 pieces cleaned, thinly sliced champignons
- lemon juice
- 1 1/4 liters wine
- 8 hard-boiled eggs
- 14 decagrams butter
- flour
- 3 liters broth

## Directions

1. Wash the frog legs, dry them with a cloth, and remove the toes
2. Roast them in 8 decagrams of butter with onion and champignons
3. Add lemon juice and 1 1/4 liters of wine and braise gently until tender
4. Pound this mixture with 8 hard-boiled eggs very finely
5. Heat 14 decagrams of butter and add flour to make a roux
6. Roast the roux and pour on 3 liters of broth
7. Cook slowly for 1 hour, removing the fat as it rises
8. Add the braised frog legs and pass through a fine sieve into a casserole
9. Bring the soup to a boil before serving, season it, and serve with fried bread slices, rice, noodles, or other soup garnishes
