## Ingredients

- 0.25 liters water
- 7 dekagrams butter
- salt
- 11 dekagrams flour
- 3-4 small eggs
- 1 spoon sugar
- whipped cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a choux dough from the water, butter, salt, flour, eggs and sugar.
2. Using 2 small spoons, form oblong pastries on a buttered sheet.
3. Brush with egg white or beaten egg.
4. Bake at high heat until light brown and cool.
5. Cut them open on the side.
6. Fill with sweetened whipped cream.
7. Place on a glass platter and cover with chocolate sauce.
