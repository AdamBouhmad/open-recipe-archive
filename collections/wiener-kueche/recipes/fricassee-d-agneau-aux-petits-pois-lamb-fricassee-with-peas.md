## Ingredients

- 8 ounces butter
- 1/2 liter fine peas
- Lamb meat, cut into pieces
- Flour with finely chopped green parsley
- Broth as needed

## Directions

1. Put butter into a casserole with 1/2 liter fine peas and the lamb meat cut into pieces
2. Let it braise covered
3. When the peas are tender, add some flour mixed with finely chopped green parsley
4. Pour the necessary broth over it
5. Let it braise until the meat is tender
6. Arrange the meat and sauce on a platter and serve
