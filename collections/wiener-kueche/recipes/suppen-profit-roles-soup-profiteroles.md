## Ingredients

- 6 rolls, crust removed and cut into finger-thick slices
- milk
- 4 whole eggs
- breadcrumbs
- hot lard or butter for frying
- roasted brain for filling
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the rolls into finger-thick slices and lightly dip them in milk.
2. Place a filling of roasted brain between each two pieces.
3. Dip these profiteroles into 4 beaten eggs, coat in breadcrumbs, fry in hot lard, and place on blotting paper.
4. Serve them on a serviette with the soup.
5. Instead of breadcrumbs, they can also be coated in flour.
6. The profiteroles can be served as a garnish with vegetables.
