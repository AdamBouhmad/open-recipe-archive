## Ingredients

- noodles
- 2 tablespoons semolina
- 12 decagrams hot butter
- 1 tablespoon cold water or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the noodles as described in 'Abgescbmalgene Nudeln'
2. Lightly roast 2 tablespoons semolina in 12 decagrams hot butter
3. Mix well with 1 tablespoon cold water or milk
4. Add the drained noodles that have been rinsed with cold water to the semolina
5. Salt to taste and roast while stirring often
6. Arrange on a dish and serve
