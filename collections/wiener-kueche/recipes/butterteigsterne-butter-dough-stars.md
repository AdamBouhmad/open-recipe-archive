## Ingredients

- 28 Deka flour
- eggs
- marmalade as desired
- sugar

## Directions

1. When you have prepared a butter dough from 28 Deka flour and rolled it out to knife-back thickness, cut finger-long and finger-wide strips with slanted ends
2. Take 6 such strips, of which the two lowest are the longest, the two middle ones somewhat shorter and the two uppermost the shortest, and layer them crosswise to form a star
3. Before placing one strip on another, brush the middle of each dough strip with beaten egg so that one holds the other
4. Press the top strip strongly in the middle with the finger, which creates a depression
5. Place these stars on a sheet dampened with water using a knife, make several incisions in each strip with a knife, brush the stars with beaten egg and bake them at fairly strong heat in the oven
6. When this is done, remove them from the sheet with a thin knife, fill the depression made with the finger in the middle with desired marmalade, sprinkle with finely ground sugar and serve
7. They can also be baked in hot lard
