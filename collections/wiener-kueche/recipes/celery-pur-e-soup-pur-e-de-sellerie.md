## Ingredients

- 16 ounces butter
- 1 thinly sliced Spanish onion
- 3 thinly sliced mushrooms
- some finely chopped green parsley
- 2 cleaned celery heads, thinly sliced
- approximately 2 liters beef soup
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- pepper
- salt
- roasted bread

## Directions

1. In 16 ounces butter, roast together 1 thinly sliced Spanish onion, 3 thinly sliced mushrooms and some finely chopped green parsley
2. Add 2 cleaned celery heads, thinly sliced to the roasted mixture
3. Pour in approximately 2 liters of beef soup and let it cook until soft
4. Pass through a fine sieve with 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
5. Add pepper and the necessary salt and bring to a boil
6. When serving, add roasted bread
