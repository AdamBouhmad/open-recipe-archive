## Ingredients

- 1 whitefish weighing 1-1.2 kilograms
- 1/2 kilogram new potatoes
- 2 lemons
- 1/4 liter vinegar
- 8 spoonfuls salt
- root vegetables
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- thyme
- hot butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Scale and wash the whitefish, then tie at the opening
2. Tie very firmly at the head and tail, loosely in the middle (so the fish cooks through at the middle)
3. In the fish poacher, bring to a boil 1/4 liter vinegar, 8 spoonfuls salt, root vegetables, 1 onion, 5 peppercorns, 2 bay leaves, and thyme for 30 minutes
4. Lower the fish in, pull the pan back (the fish must not boil or it will fall apart) and let the fish poach without boiling for 8-10 minutes
5. Remove the fish and untie it
6. Quickly place on the fish platter, drizzle with hot butter, scatter with chopped parsley
7. Garnish with the potatoes
8. Serve hot butter or Hollandaise sauce separately (see Appetizers: Cauliflower with Hollandaise)
9. The potatoes are sliced and arranged on the edge of the platter
