## Ingredients

- 1 liter cold cream
- 12 deca butter
- 15 deca flour
- 4 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg yolk (if needed to thin the dough)

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter cold cream and 12 deca butter into a saucepan
2. When this boils, add 15 deca flour while stirring constantly
3. Stir the dough quickly over the fire until it is completely dry and pulls away from the sides of the pan
4. Place the dough in a deep bowl and after it has completely cooled, gradually stir in 4 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
5. If the dough is too thick, thin it with 1 egg yolk
6. Fill the dough into a metal pastry bag with a small finger-thick opening
7. Hold the pastry bag over a buttered baking sheet, press continuously on the plunger so the dough comes out, and make 2 finger-long strips 2 fingers apart on the sheet
8. Brush them with beaten egg, sprinkle with pearl sugar mixed with finely chopped almonds, pistachios or pine nuts, and bake in a fairly hot oven until golden
