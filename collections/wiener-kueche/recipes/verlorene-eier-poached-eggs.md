## Ingredients

- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Eggs
- Pepper
- Herbs

## Directions

1. Put water, some vinegar and a little salt into a shallow casserole.
2. When boiling, crack the desired number of eggs into it and stir gently for 2-3 minutes until the egg white is completely set.
3. Remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and serve on a plate on puree, pache, vegetables or sauce.
4. The eggs can then be sprinkled with a little pepper and finely cut herbs as desired.
