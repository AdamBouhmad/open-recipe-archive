## Ingredients

- 12 Loth (about 6 oz) fine flour
- egg yolks
- lukewarm milk
- 2 spoonfuls beer yeast (Germ)
- 4 spoonfuls crayfish butter
- salt
- crayfish butter for spreading
- cut crayfish tails
- crayfish butter for the pan
- boiling milk with sugar

## Directions

1. Put 12 Loth fine flour in a bowl, beat in egg yolks, and pour in some lukewarm milk
2. Add 2 spoonfuls of beer yeast and 4 spoonfuls of crayfish butter, salt the mass slightly
3. Beat the dough well until it releases from the spoon
4. Dust a noodle board with flour and pour the dough onto it
5. Roll it out to the thickness of a knife back, spread crayfish butter on it, and sprinkle cut crayfish tails on top
6. Cut wide strips and roll them together
7. Coat a cooking pan with crayfish butter, lay the noodles in it
8. After they have risen for a quarter hour, pour boiling milk with some sugar into the noodles
9. Provide heat from below and above, and they are finished in a quarter hour
