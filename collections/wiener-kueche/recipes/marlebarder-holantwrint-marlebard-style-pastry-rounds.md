## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams butter
- 4 decagrams finely ground sugar
- 1 knife-point of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 decagrams pressed yeast dissolved in 6 tablespoons lukewarm milk
- 54 decagrams flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy and mix well with the finely ground sugar and salt
2. Gradually stir in the egg yolks
3. Add the pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk and the flour, stirring well together
4. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon, then cover and let it rise in a warm place
5. When the dough has risen about 3 fingers higher, place it on a flour-dusted board and roll out to the thickness of a knife back
6. Cut out round discs using a doughnut cutter, spacing them 2 fingers apart
