## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound fine refined sugar
- 1/2 measure fresh water
- a little egg white
- a few drops lemon juice
- chestnuts

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound sugar to the crack stage: take 1 pound fine refined sugar and 1/2 measure fresh water, clarify it with a little egg white, and cook it over strong heat in a copper unlined casserole
2. Test the sugar by dipping a smooth stick first in cold water, then in the sugar, and quickly back into the water; bite it between your teeth—if it cracks and does not stick to your teeth, it is ready
3. Add a few drops of lemon juice to the sugar
4. Lightly roast and peel the chestnuts beforehand and stick them on wooden skewers
5. Dip them in the sugar, let them drip off, and turn them slowly until they have cooled somewhat
6. When the sugar begins to cool, warm it again by shaking it frequently
