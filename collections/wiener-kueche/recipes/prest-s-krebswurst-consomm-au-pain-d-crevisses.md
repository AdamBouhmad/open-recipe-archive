## Ingredients

- 12 pieces crayfish
- salt water
- 14 deca butter
- crayfish shells
- pepper
- parsley
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 6 deca breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook 12 pieces crayfish in salted water
2. Remove claws and tails and set aside
3. Pound the shells with 14 deca butter in a mortar
4. Put in a casserole and toast slightly
5. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
6. Add some salt, a pinch of parsley, then gradually 4 egg yolks
7. Mix in the removed, finely cut claws and tails with the snow from 4 egg whites and 6 deca breadcrumbs
8. Treatment same as for potato dumplings
