## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 4 deca lemon sugar
- 1 knife point salt
- 3 deca fresh yeast
- 1/8 liter warm cream
- 40 deca flour
- 3 egg whites
- butter for dusting

## Directions

1. Mix 14 deca butter with 4 deca lemon sugar and 1 knife point salt
2. Add 3 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter warm cream and 40 deca flour
3. Stir well and beat the dough from the spoon until it releases from the spoon
4. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 3 egg whites
5. Roll out this dough on a flour-dusted board to finger thickness and cut out round discs the size of an ordinary plate
6. Press the dough in the middle with your fingers so that it forms a finger-wide and slightly raised rim
7. Cover these cakes and let them rise at a warm place until the dough has risen somewhat
8. Meanwhile prepare the following fillings: Cheese filling: 20 deca sifted curd is sweetened to taste and mixed well with 1/2 knife point finely cut lemon peel and finely cut toasted almonds
9. Poppy seed filling: 1/8 liter finely ground poppy seed and 1/2 deca vanilla sugar are placed in a saucepan, 1/8 liter boiling milk is poured in and stirred over the fire until it thickens, then mixed well with 1/2 knife point finely cut lemon peel as desired and 1 toasted spoonful
