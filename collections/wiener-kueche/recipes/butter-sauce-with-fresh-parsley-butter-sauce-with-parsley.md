## Ingredients

- Butter-Sauce flavored with lemon juice (from recipe 191)
- 1 tablespoon parsley leaves, plucked
- water

## Directions

1. Add 1 tablespoon plucked parsley leaves to the previous Butter-Sauce flavored with lemon juice, which have been briefly boiled in simmering water and then drained
2. This should only be done when serving, otherwise the beautiful green color of the boiled parsley will be lost
