## Ingredients

- 10 Deca powdered sugar
- 1 egg white
- 16 Deca blanched almonds, very finely chopped with 1 egg white
- 8 Deca finely chopped citron
- wafer strips
- glaze of any desired kind

## Directions

1. Stir 10 Deca powdered sugar with 1 egg white and 16 Deca blanched almonds, very finely chopped with 1 egg white for 1 hour
2. Mix well with 8 Deca finely chopped citron
3. Place this mixture finger-long on wafer strips and dry on a baking sheet in the very hot oven
4. When done, remove the slices and after cooling completely, cover with glaze of any desired kind
