## Ingredients

- 16 deca blanched almonds
- some milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 liters lukewarm milk
- 4 egg yolks
- 8 deca sugar
- some cinnamon or vanilla
- some cold milk

## Directions

1. Blanched almonds are, so they do not become oily, finely ground with some milk
2. Stir them with 1 tablespoon flour into 2 liters lukewarm milk and let cook under constant stirring until well cooked
3. In a pot which is twice as large as the mass, beat 4 egg yolks, stir in 8 deca sugar, some cinnamon or vanilla and a few spoonfuls cold milk
4. Pour the boiling milk through a sieve into it, beat on the fire until it becomes foamy and serve the soup
