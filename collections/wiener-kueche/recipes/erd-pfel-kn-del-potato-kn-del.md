## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 50 deca cooked potatoes
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/16 liter milk
- equal amount of flour
- some salt
- 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until creamy.
2. Stir in the cooked potatoes that have been hot-sieved, egg yolks, whole eggs, milk, flour, salt, and green parsley.
3. Let stand covered for 1/2 hour.
4. Form knödel from the mixture.
5. Cook them in boiling salted water for 1/4 hour.
