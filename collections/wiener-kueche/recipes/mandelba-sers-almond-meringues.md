## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 3 stiff egg whites
- 17 dkg soaked grated almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the stiff egg whites and soaked grated almonds
2. Make small mounds with 2 spoons onto a buttered baking sheet
3. Dry them in the oven
