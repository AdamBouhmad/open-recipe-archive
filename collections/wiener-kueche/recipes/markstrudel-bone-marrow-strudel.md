## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- butter
- 6 loth finely pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- quarter pound bone marrow, finely diced (removing the small bones carefully)
- 4 loth citron (Citronat), finely cut
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare and draw out the strudel dough finely, and when it has dried, brush it with butter
2. Beat 6 loth finely pounded sugar with 6 egg yolks well together
3. Stir in the finely diced bone marrow and 4 loth finely cut citron
4. Last, fold in the snow (stiff peaks) from 3 egg whites
5. Spread the filling on the strudel dough, roll it together, place it on a butter-brushed bowl, add a little cream on top
6. Bake the strudel beautifully in the oven
7. When baked, sprinkle it with sugar and serve at table
