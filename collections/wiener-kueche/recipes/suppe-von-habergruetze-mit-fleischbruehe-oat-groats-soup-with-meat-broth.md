## Ingredients

- One handful oat groats
- Water
- A good piece of butter
- Thin meat broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- One egg yolk

## Directions

1. Set one handful oat groats in water over the fire and let it cook fairly thick
2. When thick enough, stir in a good piece of butter
3. Pour on sufficient thin meat broth
4. Add a little salt and some nutmeg
5. Beat the yolk of one egg and add it just before serving
6. This soup is very beneficial for consumptive and dry natures
