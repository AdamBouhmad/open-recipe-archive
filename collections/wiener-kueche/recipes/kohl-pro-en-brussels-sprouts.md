## Ingredients

- brussels sprouts
- salted water
- 3 dkg hot butter

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the sprouts.
2. Place in a pot with cold salted water and cook uncovered for 15-20 minutes.
3. Drain and place in a saucepan with 3 dkg hot butter, toss in the butter.
