## Ingredients

- 12 deka flour
- 12 deka butter
- 4 deka sugar
- 5 lightly cooked, sieved egg yolks
- 1 knife point finely cut lemon peel
- beaten egg white for brushing
- 16 deka vanilla sugar
- 8 deka egg butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 0.25 liter cream
- citron glaze

## Directions

1. Put 12 deka flour, 12 deka butter, 4 deka sugar, 5 lightly cooked sieved egg yolks, and 1 knife point finely cut lemon peel on a board and work together into a dough
2. Roll out knife-back thick and cut out round shapes with a small cutter
3. Brush the edges with beaten egg white
4. Roll out the dough scraps into thin noodles and place them on the brushed edges
5. Place the tartlets on a butter-brushed baking sheet with a knife
6. In a mixing bowl, combine 16 deka vanilla sugar, 8 deka egg butter, 1 tablespoon flour, and 0.25 liter cream, stir together at moderate heat until thick, then set aside
7. Once completely cool, fill the tartlets using a piping bag, brush the dough edges with egg white, and bake in the oven
8. Once done, remove the tartlets from the sheet, glaze with citron glaze leaving the edges free, and place a spoon handle in the middle of each one
