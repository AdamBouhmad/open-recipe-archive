## Ingredients

- garlic
- vinegar
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- beef soup
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. To remove the unpleasant taste from the garlic, simmer it first in vinegar
2. Roast the garlic, finely chopped, until golden in butter
3. Mix in breadcrumbs roasted in butter
4. Pour in as much beef soup and vinegar as needed
5. Season as desired and let simmer slowly
6. Before serving, add one piece of butter
