## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Head lettuce
- Lobster meat scraps
- Mayonnaise
- Whipped mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Treat the lobster as described in recipe 959
2. Cut head lettuce into oblong pieces
3. Mix the lobster meat scraps and some spoonfuls of mayonnaise together
4. Stir well and fill the shells and claws with this mixture
5. Arrange the cut lobster meat neatly on top
6. Decorate with whipped mayonnaise using a pastry bag with optional decorative designs
7. Serve additional mayonnaise in a sauce boat
