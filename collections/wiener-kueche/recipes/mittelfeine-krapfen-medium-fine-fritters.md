## Ingredients

- 60 Decaliter dried, warm, sifted, fine flour
- 4 Decaliter sugar
- 5 grams salt
- 1 liter milk, divided into 3 equal parts
- 3 Decaliter fresh yeast
- 12 Decaliter butter or 10 Decaliter beef lard
- 6 egg yolks
- Marmalade or jam for filling
- 6-8 Decaliter beef lard
- 7/8 Decaliter pork lard of better quality

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as Fine Pastry Fritters, using only 6 egg yolks instead of 10, and using 12 Decaliter butter or 10 Decaliter beef lard instead of 14 Decaliter butter in the dough.
