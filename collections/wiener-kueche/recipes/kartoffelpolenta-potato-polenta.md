## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram round potatoes
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 3 dekagrams Parmesan
- 15 dekagrams flour

## Directions

1. To the creamed 7 dekagrams butter, stir in 3 eggs, salt, 1/2 kilogram cooked, passed, cooled potatoes, 3 dekagrams Parmesan and 15 dekagrams flour
2. Place the mixture in a well-buttered napkin and proceed as with the Serviettenknödel
3. Pour melted butter over the arranged slices
