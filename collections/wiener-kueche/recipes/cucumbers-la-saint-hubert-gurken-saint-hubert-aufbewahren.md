## Ingredients

- Cucumbers with small seeds
- salt
- vinegar (see No. 1901)
- oil

## Directions

1. Choose a good variety of cucumbers with small seeds.
2. Cut them into leaves, lengthwise strips, or as desired after splitting.
3. Sprinkle with salt, lay on cloth, and dry them with the cloth.
4. Place the cucumbers in jars and pour cooled salted vinegar (see No. 1901) over them.
5. Pour a finger's-breadth of oil on top of the vinegar.
6. Seal the jars, which must not be completely full, with beeswax and corks, and store in a cool, airy place.
