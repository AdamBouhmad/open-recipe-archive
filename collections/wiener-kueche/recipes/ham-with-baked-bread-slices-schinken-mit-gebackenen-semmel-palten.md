## Ingredients

- small cut and well-soaked pieces of ham
- one seidel of broth
- one glass of good white wine
- small cut green parsley
- chives
- bread slices (semmeln)
- lard (schmalz)
- stewed potatoes or mushrooms for garnish

## Directions

1. Lay small cut and well-soaked pieces of ham in a brine or casserole
2. Pour one seidel of broth and one glass of good white wine over it
3. Add a little small cut green parsley and chives
4. Simmer everything until soft and only very little sauce remains
5. Note that the ham must be well soaked of salt and the broth only lightly salted
6. Cut bread slices (semmeln) of the same size as the ham pieces
7. Fry them in lard
8. Lay the ham on top and pour the small sauce over it
9. Around the rim of the dish, form a border of stewed potatoes or mushrooms
