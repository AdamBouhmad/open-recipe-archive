## Ingredients

- 16 deka butter cut into pieces
- 24 deka flour
- 16 deka vanilla sugar
- 1 whole egg
- beaten egg white for brushing
- crystal sugar mixed with coarsely cut almonds

## Directions

1. Rub 16 deka butter cut into pieces finely with 24 deka flour, work together with 16 deka vanilla sugar and 1 whole egg into a dough
2. After it rests covered in a cold place for 12 hours, cut into nut-large pieces
3. Roll each individual piece pencil-thick noodle-fashion, form a pretzel, place it on a butter-brushed baking sheet
4. Brush with beaten egg white and sprinkle as desired with crystal sugar mixed with coarsely cut almonds, bake at low heat in the oven until pale yellow
