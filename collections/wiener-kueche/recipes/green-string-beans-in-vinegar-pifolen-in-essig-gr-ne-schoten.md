## Ingredients

- Green string beans
- salted water
- cold water
- vinegar (prepared as cucumber vinegar)
- oil

## Directions

1. String the green beans and either cut into noodle-like strips, break into pieces, or leave whole if small.
2. Cook them in boiling salted water until half-tender, then place in cold water and drain.
3. Fill jars with the beans and pour over them cooled, boiled vinegar prepared as for pickled cucumbers.
4. Pour a finger's-breadth of fine oil over the vinegar.
5. Seal the jars, which must not be completely full, with glass covers and corks.
6. Store in a cool, airy place.
