## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- 7 pounds butter
- 7 pounds Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in salted water until soft
2. Cool in cold water and drain well
3. Place in a pan and add 7 pounds butter and 7 pounds Parmesan cheese per pound of macaroni
4. Proceed as with rice
