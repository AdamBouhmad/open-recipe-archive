## Ingredients

- 8 crayfish
- Butter
- Crayfish meat and claws
- Butter sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a crayfish butter from 8 crayfish
2. Shortly before serving, add the extracted meat and claws to the cooked, strained butter sauce
3. Stir together and bring to boil once
