## Ingredients

- 2 pounds butter
- 3 pounds veal tails, cut into finger-length pieces
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/4 liter rice
- parsley, tied in sprigs
- 1 piece cleaned mushroom
- 1 onion spiked with a clove

## Directions

1. Put 2 pounds butter with 3 pounds veal tails, which have been cut into finger-length pieces, into 1/2 liter water and let them cook.
2. When they begin to become soft, add 1/4 liter rice along with some sprigs of bound parsley, 1 piece of cleaned mushroom, and a small onion spiked with a clove.
3. Braise this until the rice is soft.
4. Before serving, remove the onion and parsley, and place the rice with the pieces on a platter to serve.
