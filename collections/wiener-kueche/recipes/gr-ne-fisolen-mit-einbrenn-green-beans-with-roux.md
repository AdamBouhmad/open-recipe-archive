## Ingredients

- ½ kg green beans
- salt
- 5 dkg fat
- bean water or broth
- pepper
- vinegar
- sugar (optional)
- cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the beans by cutting off the ends and removing the strings
2. Wash the beans and cut into noodle-like pieces or into 3-4 pieces
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook uncovered for 45 minutes
4. Make a roux from 5 dkg fat and mix with bean water or broth
5. Add the cooked beans to the roux
6. Season with salt, pepper, and vinegar; sweeten if desired
7. Simmer for 20 minutes
8. Cream can be added if desired
