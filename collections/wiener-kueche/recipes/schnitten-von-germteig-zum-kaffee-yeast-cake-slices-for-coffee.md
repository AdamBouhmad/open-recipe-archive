## Ingredients

- 14 Deca butter
- 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
- 2 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 4 tablespoons lukewarm milk
- 16 Deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, then gradually stir in the egg yolks and whole egg
2. Add the yeast dissolved in milk and flour, mix well
3. Beat until the dough pulls away from the spoon
4. Spread the dough on a buttered baking sheet to a finger-thickness
5. Let rise in a warm place until doubled in height
6. Brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with coarse sugar mixed with finely chopped almonds
7. Bake slowly in a moderately hot oven
8. Cut diagonally into pieces 2 fingers wide and finger-long
9. Serve warm or cold with tea, coffee, chocolate, or wine as desired
