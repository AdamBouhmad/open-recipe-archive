## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter
- 12 Deka granulated sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- Snow from 4 egg whites
- 12 Deka breadcrumbs
- Butter for forms
- Flour for forms
- Finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 16 Deka butter until creamy and combine with 12 Deka granulated sugar
2. Add 8 egg yolks, one after another
3. Finally fold in the snow from 4 egg whites simultaneously with 12 Deka breadcrumbs
4. Fill butter-greased, flour-dusted almond molds to the halfway point
5. Bake 1/4 hour in the oven
6. Arrange elevated on a bowl and sprinkle with finely sifted sugar or serve with desired glaze
