## Ingredients

- One boiled calf's liver, finely grated
- One fatty goose liver, cut into small cubes
- Half pound of marrow
- Some finely chopped onions
- Half pound of butter
- 8 eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- Half pound of small raisins
- Finely cut lemon zest
- One pound of grated breadcrumbs, soaked in cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate the boiled calf's liver finely on a grater
2. Sauté the fatty goose liver cut into cubes, the marrow, and some finely chopped onions in half pound of butter
3. Gradually stir in 8 eggs, 8 egg yolks, and half pound of small raisins
4. Add finely cut lemon zest
5. Gradually combine with one pound of grated breadcrumbs soaked in cream, along with the liver and marrow, to make a mass
6. Season with salt and nutmeg
7. Prepare as other puddings that are cooked
8. When serving, garnish with lemon slices and pour caper sauce over it
