## Ingredients

- Canned sardines in tin
- Lemon wedges
- Green parsley
- Serviette (napkin)

## Directions

1. Open the tin box containing the sardines with a sharp knife (or sardine opener if available), placing the tip of the knife along the edge of the lid, striking with a hammer and cutting through both narrow and one broad side
2. If lacking the strength to cut through with the knife, place the knife blade at the edge of the lid and tap carefully with a hammer on the back of the knife
3. Bend the opened lid backwards
4. Place the tin in a bowl covered with a serviette and garnish with lemon wedges
5. Alternatively, place a bouquet of green parsley in the center of a serving bowl, remove the sardines from the tin with a fork, arrange them so they lie on their belly with the head side touching the green parsley, fill the empty space between them with halved lemon slices, and cover everything with green parsley
