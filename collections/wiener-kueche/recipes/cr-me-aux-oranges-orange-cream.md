## Ingredients

- 12 deca sugar
- 1 orange
- Cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter cream

## Directions

1. Rub 12 deca sugar on an orange and let it dissolve in a few spoonfuls of cream
2. Meanwhile, beat 4 egg yolks with 1/2 liter cream
3. Put this in a snow kettle
4. Add the dissolved sugar
5. Beat with a whisk over heat until the mass begins to thicken
6. Use immediately
