## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cold water
- 40 drachms sugar
- peel of 1/4 orange
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 1.5 liters Rhine wine
- juice of 3 oranges
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1/4 liter rum or brandy

## Directions

1. In a pot, dissolve 40 drachms sugar in 1/2 liter cold water
2. Add the peel of 1/4 orange, 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks, whipping them in
3. In a snow bowl, place 1.5 liters Rhine wine, pour in the whisked mixture, place it on the fire and beat with a whisk until the mixture is hot, but it must not boil
4. After adding the juice of 3 oranges, 2 lemons and 1/4 liter rum or brandy, strain the punch through a fine sieve, beat it again hot and serve it either immediately or cold
