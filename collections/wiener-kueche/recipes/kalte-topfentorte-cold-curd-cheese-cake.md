## Ingredients

- 15 dkg flour
- 15 dkg butter
- 6 dkg softened grated almonds
- 4 dkg sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 dkg sugar
- curd cheese, passed through a sieve
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly dough from flour, butter, almonds, sugar, and egg according to recipe for Teige (doughs)
2. After resting, roll out dough on floured board not too thin, cut to size of cake base (medium form)
3. Place dough sheet in ungreased cake form
4. Form half of remaining dough into a finger-thick roll, press it flat somewhat at the edge in the form
5. Bake at high heat until light brown, let cool completely
6. Spread filling on top
7. Form thin rolls from remaining dough, lay them in a lattice pattern over the filling
8. Brush with egg, bake at higher heat until light brown
9. Remove from form, cool completely, dust with sugar, serve
10. Prepare filling by stirring together 3 egg yolks with 5 dkg sugar until foamy
11. Mix in passed curd cheese
12. Gently fold in 3 egg whites at the end
13. Bake cake the day before serving
