## Ingredients

- 1 quart grated semolina breadcrumbs
- 24 drams butter
- 2-4 liters beef or meat broth or water
- salt
- nutmeg
- 4 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Toast the grated semolina breadcrumbs slowly in butter until light brown
2. Pour in 2-4 liters of beef or meat broth or water
3. Simmer slowly for about 1/2 hour
4. Season with salt and a little nutmeg
5. Strain well and serve
6. If desired, the soup can be thickened with 4 egg yolks
