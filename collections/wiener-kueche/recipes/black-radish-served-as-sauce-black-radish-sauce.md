## Ingredients

- about 3 pieces black radish
- salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- 2 tablespoons oil
- vinegar
- bread (optional)
- finely cut chives (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel about 3 pieces of black radish, wash them, dry them with a cloth, grate them quickly on a plate and stir together with some salt, 1 pinch of pepper, 2 tablespoons oil, and vinegar as needed
2. If desired, you can add some grated bread on top and finely cut chives when serving
3. Note: This sauce should be prepared only a few minutes before serving
