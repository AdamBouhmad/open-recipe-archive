## Ingredients

- 1 kilo dried plums
- 1/2 kilo dried pears (prunes) or twice as much plums
- 1/2 kilo grapes
- 20 pieces hazelnut kernels if available
- 1/4 kilo dried apple pieces
- some wine or liqueur of choice
- 1 tablespoon schnapps
- finely cut peel of 1 lemon
- 2 kilos black bread dough
- black flour to achieve proper consistency

## Directions

1. Prepare the mixture as for the 'Fine Christmas Bread' (recipe 1310), but do not wrap the formed loaf or strip in dough.
2. Instead dust with flour and place in a baking vessel or form also well dusted with flour.
3. Let it rise for 1.5 hours at a warm place lightly covered.
4. Lay buttered paper on top and after transferring to a sheet with this paper, bake slowly.
