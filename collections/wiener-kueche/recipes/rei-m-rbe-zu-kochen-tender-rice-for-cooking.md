## Ingredients

- two to four drams of rice
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Obtain a tin ball with an opening at the top that holds at most six to eight drams of rice
2. Clean the ball well and wash it inside and out before each use
3. Place two or four drams of rice inside, as it constantly boils up during cooking
4. Place this ball in the pot where meat broth is cooking
5. Let it cook for approximately two hours and skim it
6. When it has cooked enough and swelled properly, remove the ball
7. Alternative: if you do not have a tin ball, place the rice in a cloth or napkin, tie it so that two-thirds space remains, and it cooks just as well in the cloth as in the ball
