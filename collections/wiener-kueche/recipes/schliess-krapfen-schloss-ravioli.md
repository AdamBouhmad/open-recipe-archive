## Ingredients

- 30 deca flour
- approximately 3 whole eggs
- salt
- 8 deca butter or fat
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- 4 deca white bread crumbs
- 10 deca finely chopped cooked or roasted meat
- salt and spices to taste
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 liters beef broth or salt water

## Directions

1. Mix 30 deca flour with approximately 3 whole eggs and some salt using a spoon, then work it with both hands or a wide knife on a board
2. Cut the whole dough into pieces and form small balls from it, rolling them out very finely
3. While working on rolling out one piece, the others must remain covered
4. In 8 deca butter or fat, roast 1 teaspoon of finely chopped green parsley and add 4 deca white bread crumbs
5. Mix 10 deca finely chopped cooked or roasted meat with salt and spices to taste, and stir well with the above, then add 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk
6. On the very finely rolled out dough, place a row of hazelnut-sized balls of this filling, at a distance of 2 finger-widths from each other
7. Brush them around with beaten egg and fold over the empty dough so that the filling is covered
8. Using an upside-down pastry cutter, press the dough around the filling tightly and round, then scallop it or cut it out with a pastry cutter in a half-moon shape
9. Add the Schloss ravioli to boiling beef broth or salt water, let them simmer covered for 8-10 minutes, remove them with a wooden spoon, and serve them in the beef broth
