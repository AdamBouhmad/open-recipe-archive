## Ingredients

- Quince apples or pears
- Lemon juice
- Lemon water
- 80 Deca sugar
- Cold water
- Cochineal or elderberry juice (for red color, optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the quince apples or pears into arbitrary pieces and remove the seed cavities
2. Peel each piece and rub very strongly with lemon juice
3. Throw into cold, then boiling lemon water and cook until they feel soft
4. Quickly place into cold water and then onto a cloth to dry
5. Transfer to preservation jars and pour over enough cold, boiled sugar water (80 Deca sugar per liter of water) so that the cracks are covered
6. Bind the jars and cook them like the Muscatel pears (No. 1842) for 3/4 hours in steam
7. If you want the quinces to be red, add a few drops of cochineal or elderberry juice when filling the jars
