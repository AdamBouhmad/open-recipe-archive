## Ingredients

- 30 dkg flour
- 10 dkg butter
- salt
- 2 spoons sugar
- 1 whole egg
- warm milk as needed

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a mixing bowl, rub in the 10 dkg butter, add salt, 2 spoons sugar, 1 whole egg, the risen starter, and warm milk as needed.
2. Beat the dough until it releases from the spoon. It should be firm; if needed, knead further on a board.
3. Roll out into a rectangle, brush with fat, brush edges with egg white.
4. Spread the cooled filling on top, roll up the strudel, let it rise on a greased sheet near the oven, brush with beaten egg, and bake at lower heat until light brown.
5. Cut, sprinkle with sugar, and serve cooled.
