## Ingredients

- 24 deka apples (10 pieces)
- 1/4 liter milk
- 5 whole eggs
- 1/4 liter milk
- 4 deka sugar
- 12 deka hot butter

## Directions

1. Slice the apples thinly and place them in a casserole
2. Pour 1/4 liter milk over them
3. Beat together 5 whole eggs, 1/4 liter milk, and 4 deka sugar
4. Pour this mixture over the apples
5. Place the mixture in a casserole containing 12 deka very hot butter
6. Let it steam in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes
7. If desired, mix lingonberries and raisins into the mixture before baking
8. When finished steaming, arrange the Schmarn on a plate using a pastry server
9. Sprinkle with vanilla sugar
