## Ingredients

- 16 loth fresh lemon juice
- 56 loth Jamaica rum
- 1 pound fine sugar
- 1 pound water
- egg white (optional)

## Directions

1. Shake 16 loth fresh lemon juice and 56 loth Jamaica rum together
2. Let stand quietly
3. After about 48 hours when the liquid has cleared, pour it off
4. Strain the sediment through blotting paper
5. Dissolve 1 pound fine sugar in 1 pound water (optionally clarify with egg white)
6. Mix both together to make an extract
7. Use the extract to make punch by combining with warm water or tea water
8. Sugar can also be added afterwards, which is even better
