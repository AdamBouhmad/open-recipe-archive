## Ingredients

- Ripe peaches, melons, or apricots
- 80 Deca sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Choose fairly ripe peaches, melons, or apricots and cut them, freed from pits and skin, into arbitrary pieces
2. Place them in a porcelain pot
3. Cook 80 Deca sugar with 1/2 liter water until heavy-cooked (No. 1859)
4. Pour lukewarm over the fruit and let it stand covered
5. On the second day strain and proceed further like the heavy-cooked quince slices (No. 1861)
6. The same applies for excess juice
