## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 20 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 12 teaspoons butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 teaspoons vanilla sugar
- 1/2 hour beating time
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan

## Directions

1. In 1/2 liter of boiling milk, whisk in 20 teaspoons of ground cinnamon with constant stirring and let it cook briefly
2. Add 12 teaspoons of butter, stir well and set aside
3. Meanwhile, beat 6 egg yolks and 12 teaspoons of vanilla sugar for 1/2 hour, add the cooled mixture, stir well together
4. Finally, lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Fill half of this mass into a butter-greased, flour-dusted pan, place in the oven, let it set slightly, then cover with desired cooked filling, add the second half of the mass and bake covered for 3/4 hour in a water bath or in the oven
