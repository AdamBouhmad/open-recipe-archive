## Ingredients

- milk
- 1 spoonful flour
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 piece vanilla
- 2 spoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. In a snow kettle, stir some milk with 1 spoonful of flour.
2. Add ½ l milk, 2-3 egg yolks, 1 piece of vanilla, and 2 spoonfuls of sugar.
3. Whip everything with a whisk over steam until light cream forms.
