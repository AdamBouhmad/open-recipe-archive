## Ingredients

- 10 dkg lean rolled ham
- ½ l milk
- 4 dkg hot butter
- some flour
- salt
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- breadcrumbs for breading
- hot lard for frying
- spinach or cooked lettuce for serving

## Directions

1. In hot butter, lightly brown some flour
2. Pour in the milk and stir
3. Add salt, 2 hard-boiled chopped eggs, and the chopped ham
4. Form croquettes on a floured board
5. Bread them and fry in hot lard
6. Drain and serve with spinach or cooked lettuce
