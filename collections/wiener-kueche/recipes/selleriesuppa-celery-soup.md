## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- 4 deca butter
- 1/2 liter pea water
- 2 heads celery, peeled and cut into dice
- 12 deca butter
- flour
- 2 liters pea water
- salt
- spices
- 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Roast 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley in 4 deca butter
2. Pour in 1/2 liter pea water, strain well, and add
3. Add 2 heads of celery cut into dice and braise gently
4. Make a pale yellow roux with 12 deca butter and flour
5. Pour in 2 liters pea water as for a thickened soup, stirring well
6. Add the braised celery, salt, spices and 1/4 liter cream
7. Let simmer well
