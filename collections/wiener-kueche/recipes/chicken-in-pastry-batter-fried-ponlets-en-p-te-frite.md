## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Wine or egg batter
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare as indicated for 'Chickens braised in their own juices' (No. 822). After they are cooked tender, set them aside in their liquid and after they have cooled completely, remove them, dry them, cut them into pieces as desired, dip them in wine or egg batter and fry them in hot fat.
2. Remove them with a skimming ladle and treat them as the preceding ones. They can also be served after the soup.
