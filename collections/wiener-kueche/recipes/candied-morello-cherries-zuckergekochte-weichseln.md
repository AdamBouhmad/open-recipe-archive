## Ingredients

- 1 kilo Morello cherries
- 80 Deca sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. After removing the stems, cook 80 Deca sugar with 1/2 liter water until heavy-cooked
2. Pour lukewarm over the Morello cherries and let them stand in a warm place for several hours
3. Strain the juice and add 4 Deca sugar
4. Cook quickly so the beautiful color is not lost and add the Morello cherries
5. Let them cook a few minutes and then fill into jars as hot as possible
6. Place them unclosed in a breezy location
7. After several days bind them with writing paper, which you pierce with a needle
8. Treat excess juice as indicated for heavy-cooked quince slices
