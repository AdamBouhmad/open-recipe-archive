## Ingredients

- 5 lemons
- 2 dl spun sugar (approximately 18 dkg cube sugar cooked with 1 dl water for 15 minutes)
- cold water

## Directions

1. Squeeze the lemons and mix with 2 dl spun sugar and cold water until the immersed scale (see Strawberry Ice Cream) reads 19°.
2. Peel 2 lemons very thinly, place the peels in the solution, let stand for 45 minutes, strain through a hair sieve and freeze (see Vanilla Ice Cream).
