## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter fresh water
- juice of 3 lemons
- 20 deciliters sugar

## Directions

1. In 1/2 liter fresh water, add the juice of 3 lemons and 20 deciliters sugar
2. When the sugar is dissolved, add 1/4 liter fresh water
3. Mix and serve the lemonade
