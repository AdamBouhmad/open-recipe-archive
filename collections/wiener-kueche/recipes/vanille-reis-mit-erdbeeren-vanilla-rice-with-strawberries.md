## Ingredients

- 24 Deka rice, washed multiple times with hot water
- 8 Deka vanilla extract
- 1 liter boiling cream
- 1/4 liter strained strawberries
- 16 Deka sugar
- 1/4 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. Add 24 Deka rice, which has been washed multiple times with hot water, with 8 Deka vanilla extract to 1 liter boiling cream, cover and let it cook
2. Fill the rice into a mold lined with milk and set in a bowl
3. Put 1/4 liter strained strawberries into a freezing container, mixed with 16 Deka sugar and 1/2 liter whipped cream, place the mold in ice and stir the mass for 1/2 hour, after which arrange it decoratively in the center of the already prepared rice and serve
4. Instead of strawberries, raspberries, currants, apricots, peaches, etc. can be used
