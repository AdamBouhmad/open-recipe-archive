## Ingredients

- One and a half measures sweet cream
- 1 pound pistachios
- Fresh water
- Lemon zest rubbed on sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- Three quarter pound clarified, cooled sugar
- Spinach drops

## Directions

1. To one and a half measures sweet cream add 1 pound pistachios, whose skins must be cleaned, and whose kernels are soaked in fresh water for a while
2. Drain and dry them thoroughly
3. Pound them fine in a mortar with some cream and lemon zest rubbed on sugar
4. Beat 10 egg yolks well in a pot and stir in three quarter pound clarified, cooled sugar
5. Stir the remaining cream with the whole mixture over gentle charcoal fire, stirring constantly until it begins to thicken slightly
6. Then work in a little spinach drops, remove from fire, press everything through a fine hair sieve
7. Stir until cooled and finally pour into the freezing pan
