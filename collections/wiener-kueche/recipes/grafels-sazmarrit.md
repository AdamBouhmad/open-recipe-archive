## Ingredients

- bacon or lard
- potatoes
- salt
- pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Finely cut bacon is roasted golden in butter or lard
2. Softly cooked, leaf-like sliced potatoes seasoned with salt and, if desired, pepper are roasted in it until they have a brown crust
3. Pile them up on a serving dish and serve immediately
