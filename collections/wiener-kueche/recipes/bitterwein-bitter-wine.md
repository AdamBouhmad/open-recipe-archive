## Ingredients

- one handful Kardobenedikten (blessed thistle) herb
- one handful Tauſendguldenkraut (centaury) herb, both stripped from stems
- 1 loth mustard leaves without stems
- half loth Moskovitic rhubarb
- one handful young wormwood
- outer skin of 2 bitter oranges, cut very thinly
- 1 loth fennel seeds
- 9 Maß of the best old wine

## Directions

1. Cut all ingredients very finely together
2. Place them in a bottle with a large opening
3. Pour 9 Maß of the best old wine over it
4. Stopper the bottle and expose it to the sun for 1 or 2 days, or place it on a warm hearth
5. Shake it frequently during this time
6. Draw the wine off into small bottles and stopper them well
