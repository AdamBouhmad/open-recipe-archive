## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 eggs' weight sugar
- 2 eggs' weight flour
- butter
- flour for dusting
- anise seeds

## Directions

1. Beat 3 whole eggs with 3 eggs' weight of sugar for 15 minutes
2. Mix in 2 eggs' weight of flour
3. Using two spoons, make small discs on a buttered, floured baking sheet
4. Sprinkle with anise seeds
5. Bake in a moderately hot oven until light brown
6. Carefully lift with a knife and immediately curl over a wooden spoon handle while still warm, as they cannot be bent once cooled
