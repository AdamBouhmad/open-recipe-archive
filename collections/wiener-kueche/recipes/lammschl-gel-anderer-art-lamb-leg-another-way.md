## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- anchovies
- green parsley
- butter
- lamb leg, boned and trimmed
- fat
- mushrooms
- root vegetables
- onion
- whole spices
- red wine
- broth
- glace

## Directions

1. Cut mushrooms, anchovies and green parsley very finely and brown in butter.
2. A boned and trimmed lamb leg is stuffed with the roasted mixture, placed in a casserole in which there are fat, mushrooms, root vegetables, onion and whole spices, and braised covered until tender.
3. After about 2 hours, add red wine and broth and let the meat braise covered again.
4. When this is done, remove the meat, cut it into desired pieces, arrange on a platter, cook the strained, defatted juice with dissolved glace and pour it over the arranged meat.
