## Ingredients

- Beef
- Potatoes
- Caraway seeds
- Paprika
- Salt
- Onion
- Fat

## Directions

1. The preparation is as above, only with the difference that raw, cube-cut potatoes are added to the half-tender meat and braised tender with the meat, after which it is first salted.
