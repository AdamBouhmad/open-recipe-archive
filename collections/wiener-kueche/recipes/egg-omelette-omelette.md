## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Pepper
- 10 ounces butter or lard
- Optional fillings: fried calf's brain, crawfish tails, mixed herbs, asparagus tips, truffles, cooked ham, or braised mushrooms

## Directions

1. Beat 12 whole eggs with 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper to taste until well combined.
2. Heat 10 ounces butter or lard in a sauté pan until hot.
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and bake quickly while gently moving and pushing the set portions.
4. When no liquid remains on the surface, lift the omelette from the pan into a bowl using two serving spoons.
5. If desired, sprinkle with fried, diced calf's brain, or extracted crawfish tails, or finely chopped mixed herbs, or asparagus tips cooked in salted water, or finely diced truffles, or cooked, diced ham.
6. Fold the omelette once over, arrange in a bowl, and sprinkle with the same ingredient used for filling.
