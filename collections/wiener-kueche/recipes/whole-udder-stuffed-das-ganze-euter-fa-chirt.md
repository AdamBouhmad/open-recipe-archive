## Ingredients

- udder
- fine forcemeat
- egg yolks
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. This udder must consist of one or two large whole pieces. After it has been boiled soft and the skin removed, cut it lengthwise in slices, but not through to the bottom; then make a good, very fine forcemeat, mix it with a couple of egg yolks, and fill this forcemeat between each such cut.
2. Then press the udder back together into shape, pour over it a seidel of sour cream stirred with a couple of egg yolks, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and bake it in the oven a little until it takes on a nice color.
3. Then serve it with the sauce that has formed.
