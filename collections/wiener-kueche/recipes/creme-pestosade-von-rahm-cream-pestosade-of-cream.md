## Ingredients

- one tablespoon fine flour
- 1 seidel cream
- 16 egg yolks
- one and a half quarter pound sugar
- one lemon
- one piece whole cinnamon
- egg whites from the above eggs
- 4 loth finely chopped, sugared orange peels

## Directions

1. Stir one tablespoon fine flour with 1 seidel cream gradually until clear
2. Stir 16 egg yolks into a casserole, pour in the cream, grate one and a half quarter pound sugar on a lemon and mix it under, also add one piece whole cinnamon
3. Stir everything over not too strong a fire until it boils
4. Beat the whites from the above eggs into very stiff snow and stir it under the mass while it is still warm
5. With a tablespoon, spoon it onto a serving dish so it forms a mountain
6. Sprinkle 4 loth finely chopped, sugared orange peels over it and serve cold
