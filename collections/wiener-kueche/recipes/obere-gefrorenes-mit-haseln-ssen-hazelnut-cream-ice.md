## Ingredients

- 12 deca hazelnuts
- 12 deca hazelnut kernels
- 3/4 liter cream
- 1 1/4 liter cream
- 1 roasted coffee bean
- 8 egg yolks
- 20 deca fine sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 12 deca hazelnuts and 12 deca hazelnut kernels very finely with 3/4 liter cream
2. Add to 1 1/4 liter cream in a saucepan and stir over heat until the mixture is very hot, then add 1 roasted coffee bean and let cool covered
3. In a saucepan beat 8 egg yolks with 20 deca fine sugar
4. Press the cream through cloth and gradually add to the eggs, beating over heat until it begins to thicken, but it must not boil
5. Treat further as Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720)
