## Ingredients

- Calf brain to be fried
- Wafers of double the size of the brain slices
- Water
- Beaten eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Very hot fat, about 2 fingers deep

## Directions

1. Dip wafers in water
2. Quickly fill the wafers with the brain
3. Dip in beaten eggs and coat with breadcrumbs (panate)
4. Fry in very hot fat
