## Ingredients

- 24 peaches
- 1 pound peach puree
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar
- 2 small glasses Muscat wine
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Zest of 1 lemon rubbed on sugar
- Warm wine

## Directions

1. Peel 24 peaches and very carefully remove the flesh from the pits and press through a hair sieve
2. For 1 pound peach puree take three quarter pound clarified sugar, 2 small glasses Muscat wine, and the juice of 3 lemons; rub the zest of 1 lemon on sugar
3. Stir everything well together with a spatula
4. Pour a glass of warm wine into the hair sieve over the remainder of the peaches and press through
5. Then place everything in the freezing pan, pouring the wine in very slowly
