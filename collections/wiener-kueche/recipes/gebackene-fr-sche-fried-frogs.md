## Ingredients

- Frogs
- Salt water
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten and salted
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard or butter, very hot
- Vegetables (optional)

## Directions

1. Use only the thighs of the frogs
2. Peel off the green skin
3. Remove the lower legs
4. To separate the two legs, remove the hip bone
5. Salt the frog legs
6. Dredge in flour
7. Dip in beaten, salted eggs
8. Roll in breadcrumbs
9. Fry on both sides in very hot lard until golden
10. Remove with a slotted spoon
11. Arrange in a serving bowl and serve with vegetables
