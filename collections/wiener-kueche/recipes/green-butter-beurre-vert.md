## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams butter
- 1 tablespoon spinach thick juice
- 1 knife point white pepper
- some salt

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a mortar with the spinach thick juice, white pepper, and salt
2. Pound all together and pass through a fine sieve
3. With a wooden spoon, take the strained mixture from the sieve in long strips, place it on a plate, and let it cool
