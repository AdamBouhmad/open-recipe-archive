## Ingredients

- Calf brain
- Fine thinly sliced kidneys
- Fat
- White soup

## Directions

1. Prepare as for brain with truffles, but roast finely sliced thinly cut kidneys together with the brain at the same time
2. If desired, before serving add several spoonfuls of white soup and braise for a few minutes
