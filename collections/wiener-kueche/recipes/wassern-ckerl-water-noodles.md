## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 liter cold water

## Directions

1. In a deep bowl, combine 1/2 liter flour and 1/2 teaspoon salt
2. Gradually pour in approximately 1/4 liter cold water while stirring constantly
3. Beat the dough until it falls from the spoon
4. Place dough on a board and proceed as indicated in the Holzbackerzöferln recipe
