## Ingredients

- 3 eggs worth of butter
- 3 eggs worth of sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 eggs worth of flour
- ½ kg cherries

## Directions

1. Cream together butter weighing the same as 3 eggs with sugar weighing the same as 3 eggs
2. Gradually add 3 whole eggs
3. Mix in flour weighing the same as 3 eggs
4. Pour the mixture into a cake form
5. Top with pitted and wiped cherries
6. Bake at moderate heat
7. Turn out and serve cooled without powdered sugar
