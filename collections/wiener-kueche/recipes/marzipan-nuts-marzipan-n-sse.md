## Ingredients

- marzipan dough
- finely ground cinnamon
- flour
- water and sugar glaze
- compote of choice
- arabic gum solution

## Directions

1. Take a piece of marzipan dough and knead it thoroughly with finely ground cinnamon
2. Stretch the mass with a rolling pin to the thickness of half a finger
3. Cut into small pieces and fill forms (of pear wood) in which two nutshell halves with their markings and ridges must be carved
4. Dust the forms beforehand with finest flour
5. Press the dough firmly into them and scrape off excess with a knife
6. Turn the dough out of the forms and continue with the remaining mass
7. Lay these marzipan nutshells hollow side down on paper and let dry for 4 days
8. Then place in a weakly heated oven for a quarter hour
9. Coat with a glaze of water and sugar and let dry for a couple minutes in the oven
10. Fill the interior of each nutshell with compote of choice
11. Brush the edges with a solution of arabic gum and join them two by two together
