## Ingredients

- ½ liter milk
- ½ liter whipped cream (Schlagobers)
- 5 dag sugar
- 7 dag butter
- 7 dag flour
- 2 dl boiling milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. In a pot, brown the sugar, then quickly mix with the butter and flour
2. Pour in the boiling milk, bring to a boil, remove from heat, and stir until the mixture is cold
3. Fold in the egg yolks and beaten egg whites
4. Fill into a pudding mold and let set in a water bath for 1 hour
5. Turn out, cool, pour the following crème over it, and garnish with sweetened whipped cream
