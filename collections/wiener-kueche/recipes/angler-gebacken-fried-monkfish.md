## Ingredients

- 60 dkg Fisch (fish)
- Salt
- Schmalz (lard)
- Zitronenſcheiben (lemon slices)
- Sauerkraut or Kartoffelsalat (sauerkraut or potato salad) for serving

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the fish, clean it, wash it and place it in milk water.
2. Either remove and cut the fillets from the sides or divide the fish widthwise into cutlets.
3. Salt them and let them rest for 30 minutes.
4. Wipe them with a cloth, bread them and fry them in hot lard, then let them drain.
5. Serve on a long platter, topped with lemon slices, with sauerkraut or potato salad as a side dish.
