## Ingredients

- pike
- finely chopped onions
- shallots
- butter
- basil
- thyme
- marjoram
- milk
- semmels (rolls)
- 8 to 10 eggs
- salt
- spices
- melted butter
- fatty pork intestines

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and wash the pike; cut out the flesh and remove the bones
2. Soften finely chopped onions and shallots in butter
3. Cook basil, thyme, and marjoram in milk along with some semmels soaked in the milk
4. Pass the pike flesh with the onions and shallots in butter
5. Chop it finely with them, salt and season properly
6. Beat 8 to 10 eggs and stir everything together well in a bowl
7. Pour in as much melted butter as needed, and gradually add the herb milk until the mixture is not too stiff
8. Fill into fatty pork intestines
9. Cook the sausages in milk
10. When serving, braise them in paper casings with butter
