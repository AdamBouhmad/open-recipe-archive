## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh berries
- one Seidel of water
- one quarter pound of sugar
- juice of one lemon (for strawberry water only)

## Directions

1. Take one pound of fresh berries for one Seidel of water
2. Press them in the water
3. Add one quarter pound of sugar
4. Stir everything together well
5. When the sugar is completely dissolved, let the water run through a filter cloth
6. Keep it in a cool place
7. For strawberry water, press in the juice of one lemon (this is not necessary for the other fruits)
