## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- A handful of blanched almonds
- Sugar
- Provençal oil
- Good wine vinegar
- Finely chopped chives
- Parsley
- Anchovies or boned herrings (optional)

## Directions

1. Pound the egg yolks from 6 hard-boiled eggs together with a handful of blanched almonds and some sugar in a mortar until very fine.
2. Mix on a sauce plate with a couple spoonfuls of Provençal oil and good wine vinegar.
3. Add finely chopped chives and parsley, and if desired, some finely chopped anchovies or boned herrings.
4. Serve with beef or cold roasts.
