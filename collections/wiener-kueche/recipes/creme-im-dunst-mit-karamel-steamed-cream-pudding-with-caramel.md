## Ingredients

- 20 deca sugar for caramel
- 6 tablespoons water
- 1 liter cream
- 12 deca sugar
- salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. Brush a mold with butter and dust it with flour. After 20 deca sugar has already been cooked into caramel (see No. 1420), pour one third of it into the mold. To the remaining sugar add 6 tablespoons water and when it has dissolved, boil it with 1 liter cream, 12 deca sugar and, if desired, 1 pinch of salt. Meanwhile stir together in a saucepan 8 egg yolks and 1 whole egg, gradually pour in the prepared cream, place it on the fire for a few moments while constantly stirring and pass it through a sieve. Then the mass is filled into the mold, this is placed into the water bath and treated further as indicated under No. 1424.
