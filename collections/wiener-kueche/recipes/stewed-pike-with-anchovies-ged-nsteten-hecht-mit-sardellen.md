## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onions
- shallots
- lemon peel
- anchovies
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as before
2. Cut it whole or into pieces
3. Place in a pot with salt and pepper
4. Add a piece of butter, finely cut onions, shallots and lemon peel
5. Stew it well
6. Before it is completely cooked, add some finely cut anchovies with parsley and a little lemon juice
7. Arrange the pike with this sauce
8. It can also be cut into pieces, fried with butter, anchovies and lemon peel
