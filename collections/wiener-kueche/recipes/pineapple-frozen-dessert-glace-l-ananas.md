## Ingredients

- 1 small pineapple
- 12 deca grams sugar
- 12 deca grams sugar
- 1/4 liter cold water
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel 1 small pineapple and cut it into slices
2. Pound it with 12 deca grams sugar in a stone mortar
3. Dissolve 12 deca grams sugar in 1/4 liter cold water, mix this with the juice of 1 lemon into the pounded pineapple
4. Weigh the mass to 22 degrees, pass it through a fine sieve, and proceed further as with the 'Ordinary Strawberry Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1746)
