## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 21 dkg sugar
- 21 dkg strained hard curd cheese
- 6 egg whites
- 21 dkg blanched grated almonds
- 7 dkg breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks, 21 dkg sugar, 21 dkg sieved hard curd cheese, 6 egg whites together with 21 dkg blanched grated almonds and 7 dkg breadcrumbs into the beaten 21 dkg butter.
2. Fill the mass into the buttered, floured cake form.
3. Bake at higher heat until light brown.
4. Remove from form when cooled and serve dusted with powdered sugar as a sweet dish.
