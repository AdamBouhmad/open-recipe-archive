## Ingredients

- butter
- white wine
- lemon juice
- mussels, well cleaned
- butter sauce
- 3 egg yolks
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Put some butter, white wine, and lemon juice in a casserole
2. Add well-cleaned mussels and let them steam covered until they open
3. Remove the empty half-shell
4. Pass the liquid through a sieve into a casserole
5. Make a butter sauce with this liquid and cook it well
6. Beat 3 egg yolks with some sour cream and stir into the sauce
7. Arrange the mussels on a serving dish and serve the sauce in a sauce boat
8. Must be served very hot
