## Ingredients

- egg whites from 8 eggs
- 1 seidel good cream
- 1 seidel Spanish wine
- sugar
- lemon peels

## Directions

1. Carefully beat off the whites from 8 eggs and pour in 1 seidel good cream and 1 seidel Spanish wine
2. Sweeten as desired, beat it with a clean small whisk, and add some lemon peels
3. When the snow is strong enough, take some from the top with a spoon, pile it on a plate, and serve
