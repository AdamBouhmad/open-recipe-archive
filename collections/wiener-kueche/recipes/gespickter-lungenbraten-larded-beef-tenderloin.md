## Ingredients

- one beef tenderloin
- salt
- one part vinegar
- two parts water
- onion pieces
- yellow turnips
- lemon peel, cut
- two bay leaves
- whole pepper
- lard for larding
- one measure of cream milk

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful beef tenderloin, cleanly remove the skin, cut off the legs and salt it
2. Make a marinade from one part vinegar and two parts water, add onion pieces, yellow turnips, cut lemon peel, two bay leaves, and some whole pepper
3. Let it boil well and pour it hot over the beef tenderloin, cover it with a lid and let it stand for twelve to twenty-four hours in a cool place
4. Take the beef tenderloin and lard it in three or four rows
5. Remove the onions and yellow turnips from the marinade and lay them in a pan, place the beef tenderloin on top
6. Add heat above and below and let it braise slowly for two hours until it takes on a yellowish-brown color
7. Pour one measure of cream milk over the beef tenderloin, let it braise for another quarter hour until the sauce has a beautiful yellow color
8. Place the beef tenderloin on the dish and serve the sauce underneath
