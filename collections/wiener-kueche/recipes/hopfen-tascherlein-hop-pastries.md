## Ingredients

- noodle dough
- flour
- 16 hectograms hop filling
- 1 egg
- salt
- eggs for brushing
- salted water
- butter-roasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare noodle dough and roll out very thin on a flour-dusted board
2. Mix 16 hectograms prepared hops with 1 egg and necessary salt
3. Distribute the hop mixture along the dough at 3-finger intervals in rows, leaving 3-finger-wide empty dough borders between rows
4. Brush the dough around the hop mounds with beaten eggs
5. Fold the empty dough over the filling, press gently, and cut half-moons between each pair of mounds using a pastry cutter
6. Place in boiling water and cook covered
7. After 8-10 minutes, remove with a slotted spoon, place in a pot with butter-roasted bread crumbs, stir well, and arrange on a plate
