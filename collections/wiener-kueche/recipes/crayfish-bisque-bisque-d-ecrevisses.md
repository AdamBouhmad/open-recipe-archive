## Ingredients

- 10 drams butter
- sliced onion
- carrots
- celery
- parsley
- mushrooms
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- caraway
- 20 cleaned live crayfish
- 1/2 liter cream
- 40 drams rice
- good broth or water
- crayfish claws and tails
- crayfish shells
- 2 liters broth
- glass of Cognac or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Toast finely sliced onion, carrots, celery, parsley, and mushrooms (2 pieces of each) in butter until light yellow
2. Add salt, a pinch of cayenne pepper, a pinch of caraway, 20 cleaned live crayfish, and 1/2 liter of cream
3. Close the casserole tightly and steam everything
4. Separately, steam 40 drams of rice in good broth or water until soft
5. Remove the steamed crayfish from the broth
6. Remove and set aside the claws and tails in the soup pot
7. Pound the shells with the root vegetables and steamed rice
8. Press through a fine sieve into a casserole
9. Dilute with 2 liters of broth
10. Bring to a boil again, strain once more
11. Add a glass of Cognac or Madeira wine
12. Serve in the soup pot with the crayfish tails inside
