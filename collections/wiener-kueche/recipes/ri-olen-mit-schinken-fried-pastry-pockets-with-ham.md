## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- egg yolk
- salt
- milk
- flour
- milk
- 10 decagrams chopped ham

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough the same way as Riſolen mit Hirn
2. For the filling: In 4 decagrams hot butter, let some flour begin to brown, pour in 1/4 liter milk, let the mixture cook down thick, season with salt, and add 10 decagrams chopped ham
3. Proceed as with the other Riſolen
