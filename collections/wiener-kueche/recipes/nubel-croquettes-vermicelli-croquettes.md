## Ingredients

- Noodle dough made from 1 egg
- 0.5 liter boiling milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 egg yolks
- Sour milk
- Salt
- Flour for dusting
- Eggs for dipping
- Breadcrumbs
- Hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. If noodle dough has been made from 1 egg, roll it out finely and cut into fine noodles
2. Place these in 0.5 liter boiling milk with 2 tablespoons butter and let them cook thick covered, then set aside
3. Beat 3 egg yolks with a few spoonfuls of sour milk and pour into the cooked, cooled noodles
4. Season with salt and mix well
5. Place this mixture on a flour-dusted board and after completely cooling, form small sausages 2 fingers thick and 1 finger long from it
6. Dip these in beaten eggs, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in hot lard
7. When they have a nice golden color, remove with a slotted spoon and place on blotting paper
8. Serve these croquettes as garnish for boiled beef or roasted lungs
9. They can also be served as a flour dish
