## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- sugar syrup
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, cut them in half, and blanch in water like pears.
2. Once completely soft, cook them again strongly in syrup and lemon juice.
3. Once cooked through, arrange on a compote dish and pour the syrup over them, which must be cooked down to thickness.
