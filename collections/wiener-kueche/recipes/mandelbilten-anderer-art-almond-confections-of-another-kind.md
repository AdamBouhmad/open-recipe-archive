## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, firmly beaten
- 16 decagrams sugar
- 16 decagrams peeled, finely cut almonds
- wax
- fine pounded sugar
- ice

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites firmly and stir with 16 decagrams sugar for 1/2 hour
2. Add 16 decagrams peeled, very finely cut almonds and stir again for 1 1/2 hours
3. Spread knife-back-thick with almond mass on a wax-coated baking sheet
4. Sprinkle with fine pounded sugar and dry in the oven
5. Once done, immediately cut into pieces that are 2 fingers wide and finger-long
6. Place on a warmed, bent baking sheet and after complete cooling, cover with desired ice
