## Ingredients

- Cleaned Rhine fish
- Salt water
- Gravy or sauce
- Egg yolks
- Lemon slices
- Potatoes
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned Rhine fish into pieces as desired and place in a casserole.
2. Pour boiling salted water over it and let it come to a boil once.
3. Set aside and let it stand covered in the broth.
4. When cold, remove the fish, pull off the skin, place in a casserole and pour over gravy which has been thickened with egg yolks.
5. Bring to a boil several times.
6. Arrange the Rhine fish with the sauce in a bowl.
7. Alternatively, after cooking in salted water, cut into pieces as desired, arrange in a bowl and serve with lemon slices and potatoes.
8. Or, once the fish has cooled in its own broth, serve with vinegar and oil.
