## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter peas
- water
- 10 deca butter
- flour
- diced onions
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 liter peas in water until soft and pass through a sieve
2. Make a light roux with 10 deca butter and flour
3. Roast finely chopped onions in the roux
4. Pour in 2 liters water and add the passed peas with salt
5. Stir well, let simmer, and pass through a sieve
