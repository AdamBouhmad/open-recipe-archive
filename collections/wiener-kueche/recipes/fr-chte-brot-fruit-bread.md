## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 24 drams brown sugar
- 15 egg whites
- 24 drams flour
- 4 drams powdered almonds
- 4 drams finely cut citron
- 4 drams currants
- 4 drams raisins
- 4 drams candied peel
- 4 drams candied lemon peel
- butter for greasing the baking form

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 24 drams brown sugar for 1/2 hour
2. At the same time, fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 15 egg whites with 24 drams flour, 4 drams powdered almonds, 4 drams finely cut citron, 4 drams currants, 4 drams raisins, 4 drams candied peel, and 4 drams candied lemon peel
3. Pour the mixture into a baking form greased with butter and dusted with flour
4. Bake at moderate heat for approximately 1 hour (baking time depends on how full the form is)
5. Turn out onto a sieve or board and slice the bread into thick pieces the following day
