## Ingredients

- 8 whole young lettuce heads
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Forcemeat (farce)
- 8 drams hot butter
- Fresh parsley, finely cut
- ½ liter broth

## Directions

1. Wash eight whole lettuce heads, place them in boiling salt water for a few minutes, then in cold water, and lay them out on a cloth; once the water has drained sufficiently, separate the leaves and place a spoonful of forcemeat in the center of each head.
2. Close the leaves and restore the heads to their original form.
3. In 8 drams hot butter, toast some finely cut fresh parsley, place the lettuce heads in it, pour ½ liter broth over them, and cook uncovered for about ½ hour.
4. When serving, remove them with a slotted spoon and pour a little butter sauce over the salad.
