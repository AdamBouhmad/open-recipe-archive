## Ingredients

- 3 cutlets à 25 dkg
- 5-6 mushrooms
- 1 onion
- parsley
- broth or water
- salt
- pepper
- fat for searing

## Directions

1. Prepare the cutlets the same way as the Rhineland fillets and quickly sear on both sides
2. In the remaining fat, roast 1 finely chopped onion and parsley, add the meat, pour in broth or water and braise covered under frequent basting for 2 hours
3. 1 hour before serving, add the cleaned, washed mushrooms, finely chopped or cut into strips, and braise them together
4. Dust the sauce lightly and serve unpassed with the meat
