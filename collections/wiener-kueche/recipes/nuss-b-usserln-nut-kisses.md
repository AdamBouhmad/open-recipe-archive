## Ingredients

- 16 deca ordinary pounded nuts
- 10 deca fine pounded, blanched hazelnuts
- 1 pinch grated coffee
- 16 deca fine pounded sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix 16 deca ordinary pounded nuts, 10 deca fine pounded blanched hazelnuts, 1 pinch grated coffee, and 16 deca fine pounded sugar together well
2. Lightly fold in the whipped whites of 2 eggs
3. Using a teaspoon, place nut-sized pieces on a baking sheet covered with wafers at finger-width intervals, marked with the handle of a second teaspoon
4. Sprinkle with fine pounded sugar
5. Dry slowly in a cool oven
6. When the kisses are dry outside and soft inside, cut them from the wafers
