## Ingredients

- Pigeons, capons, veal or lamb meat
- Butter or bacon
- Flour or semolina
- Spices
- Wine
- Lemon peel
- Herbs

## Directions

1. Beat the meat well, salt it, and dust it with flour or semolina
2. Roast it in browned butter or bacon until golden
3. Cook it slowly in meat broth
4. Add spices, wine, lemon peel and good herbs as desired during cooking
