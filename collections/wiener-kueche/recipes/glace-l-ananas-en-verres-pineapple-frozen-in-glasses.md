## Ingredients

- 1 liter Pineapple-Frozen, prepared, firmly frozen and finely worked
- Stiffly beaten snow from three egg whites

## Directions

1. Once you have 1 liter of Pineapple-Frozen prepared, firmly frozen and finely worked, fold the stiffly beaten snow from three egg whites well into it.
2. Fill the mass into glasses and serve it.
