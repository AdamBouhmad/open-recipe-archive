## Ingredients

- 25 good, ripe, juicy apricots
- 1 Maß brandy
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Cut 25 apricots into small pieces
2. Crack the pits, peel them, and crush them roughly
3. Place everything in a large glass
4. Add 1 Maß brandy and 1.5 pounds sugar
5. Bind the glass well
6. Let stand for three weeks, stirring twice daily
7. Filter, fill into bottles, cork well, and store in a cool place
