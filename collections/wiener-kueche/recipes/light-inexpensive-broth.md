## Ingredients

- 8 pork bones
- 6 pig's feet
- 1 ox foot
- 1⅔ kg ham bone
- 4 whole onions
- ½ piece celery
- yellow turnips
- parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- ammonia
- butter herbs
- 10 grains each of spice varieties
- 1 liter white wine
- 2½ liters vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Hack the bones and feet into small pieces, rinse them, place in a pot with 6 liters cold water
2. Bring to boil, carefully remove foam and fat
3. Add the whole onions, celery, yellow turnips, parsley, bay leaf, ammonia, butter herbs, and spices
4. Simmer slowly for 5 hours
5. Remove fat completely, add 1 liter white wine, 2½ liters vinegar, and salt
6. Simmer another hour
7. Strain through fine sieve into earthenware vessel, let stand overnight
8. Treat as with the previous broth
9. Yields 3 to 4 liters
