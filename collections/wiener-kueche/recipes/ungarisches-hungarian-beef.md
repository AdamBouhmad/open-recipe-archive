## Ingredients

- A well-marbled piece of beef from the rib piece
- Kernel fat, finely chopped
- Onion, sliced
- Salt
- Turkish pepper (paprika)
- Flour

## Directions

1. Skin a well-marbled piece of beef from the rib piece and cut it into pieces somewhat longer than one finger joint and broad
2. Place very little finely chopped kernel fat in a casserole, as well as a couple of sliced onions
3. Add the cut meat and let it braise in its own broth
4. Salt it properly, season it only with Turkish pepper (paprika), but proceed moderately as it is very strong, and highly peppered meat might not appeal to a German as much as to a Hungarian
5. Finally, dust it with a little flour
