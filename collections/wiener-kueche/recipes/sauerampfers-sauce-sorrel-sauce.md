## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons hot butter
- 1 plate full of washed sorrel leaves
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter soup
- Equal amount of sour cream

## Directions

1. Add 1 plate full of washed sorrel leaves to 8 tablespoons hot butter
2. Cover and braise until soft
3. Dust with 8 tablespoons flour, pour with 1/4 liter soup and equal amount of sour cream
4. Let it simmer and pass through a sieve
5. One can also cut raw sorrel leaves finely, but then do not pass the sauce through a sieve
6. If necessary, some tablespoons of sour cream can be added
