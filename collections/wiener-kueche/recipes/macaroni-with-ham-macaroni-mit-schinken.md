## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter of sour cream
- 40 tablespoons of cooked, lean, finely sliced ham
- 24 tablespoons of cooked, drained macaroni
- salt as needed
- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Place 4 egg yolks in a deep bowl
2. Add 1/2 liter of sour cream gradually
3. Add 40 tablespoons of cooked, lean, finely sliced ham
4. Add 24 tablespoons of cooked, drained macaroni
5. Add salt as needed
6. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 4 eggs
7. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
8. Bake in the oven for 3/4 hour
9. Remove from oven, turn out onto a platter and serve the macaroni hot
