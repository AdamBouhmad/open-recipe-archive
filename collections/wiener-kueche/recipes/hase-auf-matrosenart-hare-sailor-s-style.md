## Ingredients

- one hare, almost completely roasted
- half a measure of small onions
- half a pound of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one slice of lean ham
- one measure of white wine
- one measure of broth
- bay leaves
- one lemon, sliced
- several crushed cloves
- blood from poultry
- small chopped anchovies (optional)

## Directions

1. When the hare is almost completely roasted, let it cool and cut it into pieces approximately two fingers wide
2. Cut half a measure of small onions into four parts and sweat them soft in half a pound of butter
3. Add to this one tablespoon of flour, one slice of lean ham, one measure of white wine, and as much broth, along with several bay leaves
4. Let this sauce cook through several times, then add one lemon cut into slices along with several crushed cloves
5. Lay the hare in it and let it cook very slowly for half an hour
6. When serving, this sauce can still be finished with one teacup full of blood from poultry; one can also add some finely chopped anchovies if desired
