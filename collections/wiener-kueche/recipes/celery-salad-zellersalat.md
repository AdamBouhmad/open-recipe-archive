## Ingredients

- 1 celery root
- cold water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 cubes cube sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the celery
2. Place in a small pot with cold water, vinegar, salt, and 2 cubes of sugar
3. Cook for 30 minutes, drain
4. Dress with vinegar and oil
5. Use to garnish the potato salad
