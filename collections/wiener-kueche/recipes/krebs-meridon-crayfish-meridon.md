## Ingredients

- 1 mug cream
- crumbs from 2 rolls
- 5 loths crayfish butter
- tails and claws from 40 to 50 crayfish, cut only down the middle
- 1 mug very young green peas, previously braised in butter
- salt
- 2 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Pour 1 mug cream over crumbs from 2 rolls
2. Beat 5 loths crayfish butter until fluffy
3. Add the squeezed-out roll crumbs and the removed tails and claws from 40 to 50 crayfish, which are cut only down the middle
4. Add 1 mug very young green peas that were previously braised in butter but retained their color
5. Add some salt, then gradually stir in 2 whole eggs and 5 egg yolks, beating until fluffy
6. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 5 eggs
7. Brush a mold with crayfish butter and line it decoratively with whole crayfish tails, green peas, or asparagus heads
8. Place in steam for one hour
