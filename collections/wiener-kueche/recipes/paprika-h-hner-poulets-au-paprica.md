## Ingredients

- 8 Decilit hot lard or bacon
- finely diced onions (to taste)
- 2 cleaned and dressed chickens
- flour
- 1/8 liter soup
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- salt
- paprika (to taste)

## Directions

1. In a casserole containing 8 deciliter hot lard or bacon, add finely diced onions to taste
2. Place 2 cleaned and dressed chickens inside and braise covered
3. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, dust the chickens with flour
4. Pour 1/8 liter soup over them and braise covered until tender
5. Pour 1/2 liter sour cream over the chickens, salt and season to taste with paprika
6. Let come to a good boil
7. Remove the chickens, cut them into desired pieces, arrange on a platter
8. Strain the sauce and pour over the chickens
9. Serve with noodles, turnips, or stuffed onions
