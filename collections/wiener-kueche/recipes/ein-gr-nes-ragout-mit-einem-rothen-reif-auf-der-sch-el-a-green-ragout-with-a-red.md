## Ingredients

- one quarter pound fresh butter
- crayfish butter
- four small rolls
- milk
- four egg yolks
- one whole egg
- salt
- nutmeg
- crayfish tails, finely cut
- stewed veal or lamb meat, very finely cut
- parsley sauce

## Directions

1. Make a beautiful crayfish butter from a quarter pound fresh butter
2. Peel the rind from four small rolls and soak them in milk
3. When soft, squeeze them out and blend with the crayfish butter
4. Stir in four egg yolks and one whole egg
5. Add proper salt and nutmeg
6. Mix in finely cut crayfish tails
7. Butter a platter and form a ring from this mixture as in the previous recipe
8. In the middle of the ring place stewed veal or lamb meat in very small pieces in parsley sauce
9. When well cooked, place it in the middle of the ring
10. Brush the ring again with crayfish butter
11. Place it for half an hour in a moderately hot oven
12. Before serving, remove the skin from the top of the stew and serve
