## Ingredients

- cold pastry dough
- pastry filling of choice
- poultry or game
- bacon slices
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Take as much cold pastry dough as needed for the lid, make it a finger's thickness
2. Work the remaining dough together like a round loaf
3. Press a hole into it with your fist, shaping a pot the size of a medium flowerpot, a finger's thickness or thinner as you prefer
4. First place the dough on a sheet of paper
5. Press the remaining dough inward with your fist on the bottom
6. Using a crimping iron, crimp a border on the outside of the bottom
7. Line the inside of the pastry with filling of choice
8. Arrange poultry or game inside, filling the spaces with farce
9. Cover everything with bacon slices
10. Brush the outer edge 2 finger-widths with egg yolk
11. Lay the lid over the pastry and press it firmly onto the brushed side
12. Cut off the excess
13. Crimp the pastry all around with the named iron
14. Make a hole in the lid the size of a seidel bottle
15. Set a small chimney on top
16. Brush the pastry with beaten eggs and bake in a not too hot oven for 3 to 4 hours slowly, depending on whether the pastry is larger or smaller
17. The pastry can also be decorated with flowers cut from the same dough, but these must first be brushed with egg
