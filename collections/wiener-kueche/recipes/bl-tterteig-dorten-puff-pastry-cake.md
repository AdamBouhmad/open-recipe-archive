## Ingredients

- 56 Deka flour for butter dough
- egg wash
- apricot jam
- gooseberry jam
- chopped almonds
- pearl sugar
- finely chopped candied citron
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare butter dough from 56 Deka flour and roll out knife-back thickness
2. Cut 6 round discs using a cake pan or bowl
3. Lay them on a water-dampened baking sheet at 2-finger width apart
4. Make several cuts with a knife and brush with egg wash, keeping the edges knife-back width dry
5. Roll out the dough scraps thinly and cut out small shapes with forms
6. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with pearl sugar mixed with chopped almonds, finely chopped citron and pistachios
7. Bake all 6 plates at fairly strong heat in the oven
8. When done, remove from oven and cool
9. Spread one plate with dissolved apricot jam, cover with a second disc and coat with gooseberry jam, and continue until all 5 plates are used
10. On top, place the plate decorated with dough, sprinkle the whole thing with finely ground sugar and serve in a bowl
11. Instead of jam, apple puree, egg cream, vanilla, chocolate, hazelnut, wine or any desired cream may be used
