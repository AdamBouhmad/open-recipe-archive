## Ingredients

- peas
- cold salt water
- 5 dkg butter
- parsley
- pea water
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and place them in a pot with cold salted water.
2. Cook for 20-30 minutes.
3. Make a parsley roux with 5 dkg butter and pour in pea water.
4. Add the peas, season with salt and sugar.
5. Cook together for 20 minutes.
6. For very young peas: place them in a casserole with 5 dkg butter, pour in a little water, season with salt and sugar, and braise for 20 minutes, dust slightly, and cook a few more minutes.
