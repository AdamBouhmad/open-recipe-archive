## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 0.25 liter cold water
- 1 kilogram peeled pineapples, cut into slices
- 0.5 kilogram finely powdered sugar
- 1.5 liters water

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 kilogram of sugar, 0.25 liter of cold water, and 1 kilogram of peeled pineapples cut into slices into a stone mortar.
2. Pound this very finely into a dough, then place in a saucepan on heat with 0.5 kilogram of finely powdered sugar and 1.5 liters of water.
3. Cook while stirring constantly for approximately 10 minutes, then pass quickly through a sieve.
