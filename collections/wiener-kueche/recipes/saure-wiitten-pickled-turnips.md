## Ingredients

- white turnips
- salt
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the white turnips
2. Cut noodle-like, as fine as desired, on a turnip slicer or tooth scraper
3. Place in a tub the same way as cabbage
4. Sprinkle with some salt and caraway seeds
5. Stamp down, weight, and treat further like cabbage
