## Ingredients

- 1 Orange-Gefrorenes (No. 1753)
- 1/2 liter Champagne wine
- 1 1/8 liters Rum or Arrack

## Directions

1. Prepare an Orange-Gefrorenes (No. 1753) and work it together with 1/2 liter Champagne wine and 1 1/8 liters Rum or Arrack.
2. Serve in glasses.
