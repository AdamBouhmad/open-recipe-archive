## Ingredients

- 8 deca of bacon or butter
- onion
- root vegetables
- whole spices
- white herbs
- poularde
- peeled lemon wedges
- bacon slices
- 1/2 liter of vinegar or 1/2 liter of wine
- 1 liter of broth

## Directions

1. Place bacon or butter, onion, root vegetables, whole spices, and white herbs in a casserole
2. Lay a flambeed, cleaned, trussed, and seasoned poularde on the root vegetables, covered with peeled lemon wedges and bound with bacon slices
3. Braise covered until the root vegetables are brown
4. Pour in 1/2 liter of vinegar or 1/2 liter of wine and 1 liter of broth and let the poularde braise covered until tender
5. Remove the poularde, remove the bacon and lemon wedges, cut the poularde into desired pieces, arrange on a platter and serve the defatted, strained juice in a sauce boat
