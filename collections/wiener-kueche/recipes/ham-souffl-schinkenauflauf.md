## Ingredients

- 8 dkg ham
- ⅛ liter cream
- 6 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- pinch of salt
- pepper
- chopped ham
- cream
- 40 dkg cooked, peeled potatoes, sliced thinly
- breadcrumbs
- 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix the 6 dkg butter rub with 2 egg yolks, pinch of salt, pepper, chopped ham, cream and 40 dkg cooked, peeled, thinly sliced potatoes
2. Fill half of the mixture into a buttered soufflé dish lined with breadcrumbs
3. Drip melted butter on top, add breadcrumbs and 1 hard-boiled egg sliced
4. Add the other half of the mixture on top, sprinkle with breadcrumbs again, drip with butter
5. Bake at moderate heat for 20-30 minutes
6. Can also be served as an evening dish
