## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 10 dkg butter
- 1 whole egg
- 7 dkg sugar
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 2 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- 1 spoon water or milk
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 8 dkg chopped butter
- pralines

## Directions

1. Make sponge cake from egg yolks
2. Cut through twice
3. Fill with either cold or warm whipped cream
4. Glaze brown on top and decorate with pralines
5. For cold filling: Beat 10 dkg butter, stir in 1 whole egg, 7 dkg sugar, 5 dkg grated chocolate
6. For warm filling: Beat 2 egg yolks, 7 dkg sugar, 1 spoon water or milk over steam until thick, cool, stir in 5 dkg grated chocolate and 8 dkg chopped butter, can make more cream to coat outside and sprinkle with swollen chopped almonds
