## Ingredients

- 25 cooked snails
- finely cut green parsley
- onion
- lemon peel
- 4 drams semolina breadcrumbs
- butter
- cream
- 2 whole eggs
- toasted semolina slices

## Directions

1. Clean the cooked snails and cut them finely
2. Mix with finely cut green parsley, onion, and lemon peel
3. Toast semolina breadcrumbs in butter and add along with some cream
4. Simmer well
5. Add 2 whole eggs, stir well together
6. Immediately spread on toasted semolina slices
7. Place the slices in a casserole whose bottom is covered with butter and keep warm covered
8. Before serving, pour good pea soup over them
