## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter, beaten
- 16 Deka vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 20 Deka roasted or cooked and sieved chestnuts
- 8 egg whites
- Optional: 1 knife-tip grated nutmeg and 6 pieces blanched, finely ground almonds

## Directions

1. Beat butter gradually with vanilla sugar, egg yolks, and roasted or cooked and sieved chestnuts
2. After ½ hour, lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
3. Fill a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold with this mixture
4. Bake for ¾ hour in the oven
5. If desired, nutmeg and blanched, finely ground almonds can be stirred in with the chestnuts
6. Serve the chestnut souffle with water-chocolate sauce and whipped cream
