## Ingredients

- fat
- onion
- root vegetables
- whole spices
- lamb leg, trimmed and larded
- wine
- broth
- soup sauce

## Directions

1. In a casserole, add fat, onion, root vegetables, whole spices and the trimmings from the lamb leg, place the trimmed and larded leg with the larded side up and let it braise covered.
2. When the forming juice is almost evaporated, add some wine and broth and let it braise covered again.
3. Remove the meat, place it in a pan, pour half of the strained broth over it and roast it in the oven with frequent basting until the fat has taken on a golden color.
4. Glaze the meat with dissolved soup sauce, cut it into desired pieces, reassemble them on a platter and pour jus-sauce at the side.
