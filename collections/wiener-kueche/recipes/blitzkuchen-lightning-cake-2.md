## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 14 dkg flour
- 14 dkg melted, almost boiling butter

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar, 2 egg yolks, and 1 whole egg for 15 minutes.
2. Very quickly mix in 14 dkg flour and 14 dkg melted, almost boiling butter.
3. This must be done quickly as the butter will otherwise stiffen and the mass will be hard to spread.
4. Quickly spread fingerthick onto buttered, floured sheet and bake at higher heat until light brown.
5. Cut into pieces or strips while still warm, but remove from sheet only when cooled.
6. The slices can be brushed with marmalade, put together, and dusted with powdered sugar.
