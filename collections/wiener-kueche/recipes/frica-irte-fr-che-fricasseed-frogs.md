## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Onion
- Pea broth
- Lemon peel
- Frogs
- Milk cream
- Egg yolks
- Cold water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Warm a piece of butter in a pan
2. Add a spoonful of flour and half an onion
3. Let it cook only a moment until it does not brown
4. Fill with pea broth
5. Add some lemon peel and let it cook for a while
6. Place the frogs in the sauce
7. Add a spoonful of milk cream and let simmer somewhat more
8. In a small pot, take two or three egg yolks, half a spoonful of cold water and the juice of a lemon
9. Strain the sauce into this mixture
10. Heat constantly at a coal fire while stirring
11. Pour over the frogs on a platter
12. Sprinkle with lemon peel
