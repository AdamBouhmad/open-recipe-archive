## Ingredients

- venison loin
- vinegar
- onion
- bacon for larding
- paper
- marinade (beitze) and cream for basting

## Directions

1. Prepare the loin like the leg, namely marinated in vinegar and onion, and let it lie in the marinade for one or two days
2. Strip the skin cleanly
3. Lard it with bacon
4. Bind it with paper
5. Roast it slowly on the spit
6. Baste it several times with marinade and cream
7. When well roasted, arrange it on a platter and garnish like venison leg
