## Ingredients

- 12 1/2 dkg butter
- 12 1/2 dkg flour
- 12 1/4 dkg sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 stiffly beaten egg whites
- 5 dkg raisins
- bread crumbs
- blanched almond halves

## Directions

1. Soften the butter without melting it and beat it
2. Stir in the flour spoonful by spoonful and beat the mass well and fluffy
3. If the mixture is too thick, place it on the stove and warm it with constant stirring until it becomes pliable
4. In another bowl, beat the sugar with 4 egg yolks until frothy and mix this mass well with the first
5. Finally, very lightly fold in 4 stiffly beaten egg whites and 5 dkg raisins
6. Fill the mass into a well-buttered or greased form sprinkled with bread crumbs and lined with blanched almond halves
7. Bake at a fairly sharp fire until light brown
8. Let the cake cool somewhat in the form after baking, turn it out and dust it with sugar immediately
