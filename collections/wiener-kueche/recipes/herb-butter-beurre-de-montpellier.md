## Ingredients

- tarragon leaves
- chives
- green parsley
- chervil
- sorrel
- burnet
- watercress
- salad burnet
- 8 sprigs each
- 8 small onions
- 1 piece rocambole
- 24 teaspoons butter
- lemon juice
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Finely cut tarragon leaves, chives, green parsley, chervil, sorrel, burnet, watercress, and salad burnet (8 sprigs each), 8 small onions, and 1 piece of rocambole
2. Pound very finely in a mortar with 24 teaspoons of butter, some lemon juice, a little salt, and the same amount of pepper
3. Pass through a sieve and arrange very neatly on a plate
