## Ingredients

- 18 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- Jam for topping

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from flour, butter, egg yolks, and sugar without letting it rest
2. Shape into a roll
3. Cut equal pieces
4. Form balls
5. Place on unbuttered sheet and indent the top
6. Bake at medium-high heat until light brown
7. Top with jam
8. Work quickly as the dough becomes stiff
