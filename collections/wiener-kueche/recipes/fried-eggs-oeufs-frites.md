## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard or clarified butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Heat lard or clarified butter to a finger's height in a casserole until very hot.
2. Break eggs into the hot fat and fry for a few minutes.
3. When serving, sprinkle with a little salt and pepper and serve with sauces, cabbage salad, or similar dishes.
