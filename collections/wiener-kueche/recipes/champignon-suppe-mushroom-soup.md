## Ingredients

- fresh cleaned, washed and chopped mushrooms
- fresh water
- butter
- finely minced onions and parsley
- pepper and salt
- some mace
- strong stock or pea soup
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Place the fresh, cleaned, washed and chopped mushrooms temporarily in fresh water so they stay white
2. Let finely minced onions and parsley simmer somewhat with butter in a casserole over charcoal fire
3. Add pepper, salt and some mace, fill with strong stock or pea soup
4. Let the mushrooms cook well in it
5. Before serving, thicken the mushrooms and add toasted bread slices to the soup
