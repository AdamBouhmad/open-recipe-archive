## Ingredients

- one spoonful fat
- one piece butter
- 6 to 7 onions
- one spoonful flour
- broth to achieve proper consistency
- one tablespoon vinegar
- 3 cloves
- lemon peel, finely cut

## Directions

1. Heat one spoonful fat in a small pan with a piece of butter until it becomes a nice golden-brown color
2. Cut 6 to 7 onions into fine strips and add to the pan
3. Add one spoonful of flour
4. Pour in enough broth to achieve the proper consistency
5. Finally add one tablespoon vinegar, 3 cloves, and finely cut lemon peel
6. Let everything cook thoroughly together
