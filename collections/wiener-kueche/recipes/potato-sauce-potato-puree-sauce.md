## Ingredients

- about 4 large potatoes
- 2 tablespoons oil
- salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sugar (if desired)
- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish or chives (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook about 4 large potatoes until soft and pass them through a sieve
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons oil, some salt, 1 pinch of pepper, vinegar to taste and if desired 1 teaspoon sugar
3. If desired, you can add 1 tablespoon grated horseradish or chives
