## Ingredients

- Pig's head, cleaned
- Water
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Root vegetables
- Parsnip, small amount
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic, crushed (optional)
- Rocambole or shallots, finely chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned pig's head gently in 1 1/2 liters water, 1/4 liter vinegar with onion, root vegetables and some parsnip
2. Remove the meat from the broth, cut it into finger-length pieces, and arrange on a platter
3. Remove the fat from the broth and add the necessary salt and pepper
4. The flavor must be pleasantly sour
5. If desired, add some crushed garlic, or finely chopped rocambole and shallots, mix well together
6. Pour over the cut pig's head and let it stand in a cool place until completely stiff
7. Serve either with the platter, or cut into pieces and serve with vinegar and oil
8. Pig's feet can also be used instead of pig's head
