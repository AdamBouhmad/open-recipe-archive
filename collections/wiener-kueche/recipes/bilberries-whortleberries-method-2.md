## Ingredients

- 1 kilo selected dried bilberries
- 1 kilo fine sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the bilberries and sugar together lightly
2. Cover and place on the hot plate
3. Stir the bilberries frequently with a wooden spoon and bring to a boil
4. Once they have come to a boil, remove from the stove
5. Stir well and treat further as described above
6. Note that the bilberries must not boil longer than just once
