## Ingredients

- 30 deca butter
- 30 deca peeled almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 30 deca lemon sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- 30 deca flour
- cherries or morello cherries

## Directions

1. Beat 30 deca butter until fluffy
2. Mix with 30 deca peeled almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites
3. Stir in 30 deca lemon sugar and 10 egg yolks one after another
4. Finally lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 10 egg whites simultaneously with 30 deca flour
5. Fill 2 fingers high into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
6. Allow cherries or morello cherries, 2 and 2 one upon the other, to sink into the dough
7. Bake for 3/4 hours in an adequately hot oven
8. Further treatment is as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
