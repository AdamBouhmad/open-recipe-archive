## Ingredients

- 5–6 mushrooms
- salt
- water or broth
- 4 dkg butter
- parsley roux
- lemon juice
- 4–5 spoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms, slice them into leaves, cook them 20–30 minutes in salted water or broth.
2. Make a parsley roux with 4 dkg butter and pour mushroom water over it.
3. Add the cooked mushrooms, season with lemon juice and salt.
4. Add 4–5 spoonfuls of cream and cook for 20 minutes.
