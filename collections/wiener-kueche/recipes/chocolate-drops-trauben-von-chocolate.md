## Ingredients

- 22 deca sugar
- 1 table spoon grated chocolate
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, chocolate, and lemon juice well in a deep bowl
2. Lightly fold in the snow from 1 egg white
3. With a coffee spoon, take equally large pieces and loosen with the handle of a second spoon
4. Place in small heaps at finger-width distance on a baking sheet lined with wafers
5. Sprinkle with finely cut almonds and finely pounded sugar
6. Dry at low heat for approximately 2 hours in the oven
7. Remove from the oven once completely dry
