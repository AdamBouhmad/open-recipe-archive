## Ingredients

- one softly cooked calf's head or ear, or together with cooked calf's sweetbread
- 12 Deciliter butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 pieces of thinly sliced mushrooms
- 1.5 teaspoon chopped parsley
- approximately 2 liters soup
- toasted rolls, small diced calf's head, or bread crumbles

## Directions

1. Cook a softly cooked calf's head or ear, or together with cooked calf's sweetbread
2. Place in cold water and then cut into arbitrary small pieces
3. Heat 12 Deciliter butter in a saucepan and add 4 tablespoons flour
4. When it foams, add 2 pieces of thinly sliced mushrooms and 1.5 teaspoon chopped parsley
5. Let it roast
6. Pour over with approximately 2 liters soup in which the calf's head and sweetbread were cooked
7. Stir over heat until it comes to a boil and let it simmer slowly for approximately 1.5 hours
8. Remove from heat, strain through a sieve, return to the saucepan
9. Add the diced calf's head and sweetbread, bring to a boil again
10. Serve with toasted rolls, small diced calf's head, or bread crumbles
