## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of butter
- peeled onions, each cut in two parts
- bouillon or water
- salt and pepper
- bread slices, diced and browned

## Directions

1. Sauté the peeled and halved onions in butter in a casserole over charcoal fire until they turn brownish
2. Fill with bouillon or water and cook for half an hour
3. Finally salt and pepper
4. Serve over diced and browned bread slices
