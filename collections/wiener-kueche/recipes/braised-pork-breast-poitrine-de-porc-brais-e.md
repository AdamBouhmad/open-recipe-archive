## Ingredients

- pork breast
- caraway
- bay leaves
- Spanish onions
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- broth or water
- herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the breast into strips two fingers wide and place in a casserole with ½ teaspoon of caraway, 2 bay leaves, 2 Spanish onions pierced with a few cloves
2. Season with necessary salt and pepper and let it braise uncovered for a short time
3. After about ¼ hour, pour in 1½ liters of white vinegar and enough broth or water so that it covers the meat, add some tied herbs and let it braise covered until the meat is tender
4. During this time the sauce should reduce to about a sixth of its volume
5. Place the meat on a platter and serve the defatted sauce separately
6. In the same manner one uses the neck and belly meat of pork
