## Ingredients

- 6 pieces cleaned anchovies
- some parsley
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 8 deca bread crusts, soaked in water and pressed out
- vinegar
- water
- 2 tablespoons oil
- pinch of pepper
- salt
- 1/2 coffee spoon sugar as desired

## Directions

1. Pound together the cleaned anchovies, parsley, hard-boiled egg yolks, and bread soaked in water and pressed out
2. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
3. Stir with some vinegar and water to make a thick sauce so that an inverted spoon stays covered
4. Add 2 tablespoons oil, a pinch of pepper, and some salt
5. Mix well
6. If desired, add 1/2 coffee spoon sugar before serving
7. You can also add some onion to the anchovies
