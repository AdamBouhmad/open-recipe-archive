## Ingredients

- hazel hens
- bacon
- butter
- oysters
- potatoes
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the hazel hens, open them, wash them, and salt them
2. Lift the breast and insert a small piece of bacon into it
3. Lard them and roast them to a golden-brown color
4. Make a short sauce from butter, some oysters, potatoes, lemon peel, and lemon juice
5. Arrange the roasted hazel hens on a dish and pour the sauce over them
