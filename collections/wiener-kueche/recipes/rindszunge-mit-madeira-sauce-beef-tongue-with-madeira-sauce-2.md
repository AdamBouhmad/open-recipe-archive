## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. After the beef tongue is first washed clean, it is placed in 4 liters of water with 1 handful of salt and cooked gently covered for approximately 2 hours.
2. The thick skin will then easily peel off and after this is done, the tongue is returned to the broth and cooked completely tender. (A beef tongue takes approximately 4 hours cooking time in total.)
3. Then cut it lengthwise in half and arrange the pieces, cut obliquely into leaves, in approximately 1 finger long and 2 fingers wide strips on a platter, so that they form again a whole tongue.
