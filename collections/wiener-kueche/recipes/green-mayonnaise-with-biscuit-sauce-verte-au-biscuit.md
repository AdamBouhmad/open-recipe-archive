## Ingredients

- 1 handful spinach
- 10 sprigs green parsley and chervil
- 1 teaspoon finely cut tarragon
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 12 teaspoons biscuit soaked in water and well squeezed
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- vinegar to taste
- 4 tablespoons oil

## Directions

1. Cook spinach with 10 sprigs of green parsley and chervil in salted water, place in cold water, drain the water, press the cooked mixture strongly and finely pound it together with 1 teaspoon finely cut tarragon, 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, and 12 teaspoons of biscuit soaked in water and well squeezed in a mortar
2. Pass through a sieve and only before serving mix with a little salt, the necessary pepper, some vinegar, and 4 tablespoons of oil to make a sauce
3. The mixing of vinegar and salt happens only before serving, as otherwise the green color would be lost
