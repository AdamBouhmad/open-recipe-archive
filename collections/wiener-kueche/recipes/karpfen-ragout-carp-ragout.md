## Ingredients

- 16 decilitres butter or fat
- 1 piece onion
- some root vegetables, finely chopped
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 sprigs thyme
- 5 grains of each type of spice
- 1 fish head, chopped
- 1 kilo carp, cut into 2-finger wide and 1-finger long pieces
- 1/3 litre white or red wine
- some meat broth or water
- 8 decilitres black bread

## Directions

1. In a casserole, place 16 decilitres butter or fat, then a piece of onion and some root vegetables, finely chopped, 1 bay leaf, 5 sprigs thyme, and 5 grains of each type of spice
2. Lay the chopped fish head on top and let it simmer covered for approximately 1/4 hour
3. Add 1 kilo carp cut into 2-finger wide and 1-finger long pieces and 1/3 litre white or red wine, some meat broth or water, and 8 decilitres black bread
4. Let this simmer covered and gently for 1/2 hour
5. Arrange the fish pieces going around on a platter, strain and degrease the sauce
6. Adjust the seasoning with vinegar or lemon and salt, and let the sauce come to a boil
7. Pour over the fish
8. In the centre, serve a ragout of crayfish, mushrooms, artichoke hearts, and similar items
