## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound butter
- one-quarter pound ground almonds
- the soft part of a small roll soaked in milk and squeezed out
- one-half pound sliced veal liver
- two loth citron
- the zest of half a lemon, finely chopped
- sugar to taste
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat one-quarter pound butter until very fluffy
2. Stir in one-quarter pound ground almonds, the soft part of a small roll that has been soaked in milk and squeezed out, one-half pound sliced veal liver, two loth citron, the finely chopped zest of half a lemon, and enough sugar to make it sweet
3. Add a bit of nutmeg
4. Stir everything together for half an hour
5. Make a rim of short pastry on a sheet pan and bake it
6. Spread the sheet and rim with butter
7. Pour the liver mixture into it
8. Bake it slowly for three-quarters of an hour and serve it sweetened
