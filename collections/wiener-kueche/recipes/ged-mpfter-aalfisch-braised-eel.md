## Ingredients

- eel
- butter
- onion
- root vegetables
- thyme
- mixed vegetables
- bay leaf
- red wine
- fish broth
- flour

## Directions

1. After skinning and cleaning the eel, roll it up snail-fashion so that the tail is twisted inward first and the head is directed outward. To keep the eel in this position, pierce it with small skewers, tie it to the bottom of a fish pan and salt it. Into the fish pan put meanwhile 16 pieces butter, 1 small onion, some root vegetables, all cut into leaves, 5 bunches of thyme, from each kind of vegetable 5 grains and 1 bay leaf; let this simmer somewhat, then lay the eel on it, pour over it with 1/2 liter red wine and as much broth that the liquid covers the fish. Let it braise covered for approximately 3/4 to 1 hour. Then lift it out with the bottom, place it on a cloth, cover it with a second cloth so that the fish fat soaks into it, remove the twine and place the eel on the platter. From the broth make a sauce with some butter and flour, strain this and degrease it, if you have some add Madeira wine, bring to a boil and serve in a sauce boat.
