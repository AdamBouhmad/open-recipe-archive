## Ingredients

- About 10 apples (Pearmain or Casseler apples)
- Lemon juice
- Cold water
- 1/8 liter boiling water
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 10 deka sugar
- Wine, as desired
- 1 stick lemon peel
- Cinnamon, as desired
- Redcurrant or desired marmalade
- Gelée cut into strips

## Directions

1. Halve the apples and remove the seed cavity, then peel them
2. Rub the apples with lemon juice and immediately place them in cold water with lemon juice to prevent discoloration
3. In a saucepan, combine 1/8 liter boiling water with the juice of 1 lemon, 10 deka sugar, and wine as desired
4. Add the apples to this mixture and cook covered until fairly tender
5. Remove the apples and let them cool on a covered dish
6. Cook the remaining liquid uncovered with 1 stick of lemon peel and cinnamon as desired until the syrup begins to thicken
7. Strain the syrup through a cloth into a dish and let it cool
8. Arrange the apples on a compote dish in a crown shape or as desired
9. Pour the syrup over the apples and decorate with redcurrant or desired marmalade and gelée cut into strips
