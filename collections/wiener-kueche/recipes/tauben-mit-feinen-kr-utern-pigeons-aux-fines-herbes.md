## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Butter
- Chervil, finely cut
- Green parsley, finely cut
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth

## Directions

1. After removing the entrails and cleaning the pigeons in water, cut them apart, remove the breast and back bones, and beat the meat with the flat side of a meat cleaver.
2. Heavily butter a shallow casserole, lay the meat in it, sprinkle with finely cut chervil, green parsley, salt, and pepper, and quickly brown on both sides.
3. Remove the pigeons and arrange on a platter.
4. Dust the butter remaining in the casserole with flour, roast it, pour in white wine and some broth, stir well, and let it simmer.
5. Pour this sauce over the arranged pigeons.
