## Ingredients

- Half a pound sugar
- One stick vanilla
- 6 egg yolks
- Whites of the eggs
- 4 Loth fine flour

## Directions

1. Pound and finely sieve half a pound sugar and one stick vanilla together, stir with 6 egg yolks to a foam
2. Beat the egg whites to snow and fold in slowly together with 4 Loth fine flour
3. Otherwise bake for a quarter hour at gentle oven heat
