## Ingredients

- 50 dkg calf's liver
- ½ liter cream
- 6 dkg bacon
- 5 dkg hot fat
- salt
- pepper
- water
- fried potato pancakes or dumplings

## Directions

1. Prepare the liver, cut into slices, salt, pepper, and lard with bacon strips.
2. 30 minutes before serving, place in a pot with 5 dkg hot fat, brown lightly.
3. Pour in a little water and simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Strain the sauce and pour cream over it, finish simmering.
5. Serve with fried potato pancakes or dumplings.
