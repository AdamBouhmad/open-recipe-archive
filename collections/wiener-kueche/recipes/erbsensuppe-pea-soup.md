## Ingredients

- 1 Seidel large, clean peas
- 3 Seidel soft water
- Butter
- Flour
- Green parsley
- Salt
- Flour dumplings

## Directions

1. Put cleaned peas with soft water on the fire and cook until very soft
2. Let a piece of butter become hot, add a good spoon of flour so it turns golden yellow
3. Add green parsley and let it simmer briefly
4. Pour the broth with the peas over it
5. Let it cook down a little, then push through a sieve
6. Return to the pot, salt, and serve over carved flour dumplings
