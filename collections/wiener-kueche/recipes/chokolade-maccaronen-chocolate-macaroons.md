## Ingredients

- Sweet macaroon dough
- Half a pound chocolate
- About 2 spoonfuls of almond dough
- A spoonful of finely pounded cinnamon or a teaspoon of vanilla, or both as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweet macaroon dough according to instructions
2. Place half a pound chocolate on a baking sheet and soften it over charcoal fire
3. Pour it onto a plate and add about 2 spoonfuls of the almond dough
4. Stir the mixture well together
5. Bring it into the mortar with the remaining dough mass
6. Add a spoonful of very finely pounded cinnamon or a teaspoon of vanilla, or both as desired
7. Work everything thoroughly through
8. Take careful precautions when baking
