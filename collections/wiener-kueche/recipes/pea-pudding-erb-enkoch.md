## Ingredients

- fresh, clean peas
- water
- butter
- roll, roasted in butter
- ten eggs
- eight egg yolks
- one pound sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Cook fresh, clean peas in water until half done, so that the skins come off by themselves
2. Remove the skins and let the peas dry
3. Pound them finely and smooth in a mortar
4. Rub in a mixing bowl a piece of butter under them, as well as roll roasted in butter
5. Beat ten eggs in it, always stirring very tender and smooth
6. Add eight egg yolks and stir everything well together again
7. Finally pour in one pound sifted sugar and stop stirring until it becomes completely smooth and tender
8. Put this batter in the dish and mold coated with butter, let it bake, and serve it well garnished and warm, for all fat-made puddings must be eaten warm
