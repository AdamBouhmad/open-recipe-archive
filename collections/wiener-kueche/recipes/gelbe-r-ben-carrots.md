## Ingredients

- Carrots, same quantity as for kohlrabi
- 8 ounces butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- Water
- Butter sauce
- Parsley, finely cut

## Directions

1. Peel the carrots, cut them into pieces of your choice or scoop them out
2. Place them in a casserole with 8 ounces of butter and 1 tablespoon of sugar
3. Pour water over them and cook the carrots tender
4. Instead of stirring, shake the casserole from time to time to avoid breaking the carrots
5. Before serving, add some butter sauce, sprinkle a teaspoon of finely cut parsley over them, and let them cook a little longer
