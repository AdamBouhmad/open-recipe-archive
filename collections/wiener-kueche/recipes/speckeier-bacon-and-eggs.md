## Ingredients

- 8 dkg bacon
- 6 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into thin slices, line an omelet pan with them.
2. Lightly roast the bacon and break the eggs into the pan next to each other.
3. Salt, pepper, and sprinkle with a little vinegar.
4. When the eggs are set, either turn them with a spatula or let them brown a bit more, then lift onto a plate and serve immediately.
