## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- ¼ liter semolina
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- ½ liter milk cream
- 4 egg whites
- ½ liter flour
- 16 deca raisins
- ½ liter milk

## Directions

1. Gradually mix 12 deca butter with 4 egg yolks
2. Blend in ¼ liter semolina, 16 deca vanilla sugar, and ½ liter milk cream well
3. Let this mixture rest for ½ hour so the semolina can swell
4. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 4 egg whites
5. Prepare strudel dough from ½ liter flour and stretch it finely as described in the Young Strudel recipe
6. Spread with the semolina filling above
7. Sprinkle with 16 deca raisins
8. Roll together lightly and treat as described in the Ham Strudel recipe
9. When the strudel is half-baked, pour ½ liter mixed milk over it
10. Finish baking and cut into desired pieces, arrange on a serving dish, and serve
