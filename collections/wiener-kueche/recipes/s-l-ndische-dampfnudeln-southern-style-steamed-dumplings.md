## Ingredients

- 60 Deka flour
- 4 Deka sugar
- 5 grams salt
- 2 Deka yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter uncooked milk
- 4 Stercks butter
- 1 whole egg
- 1/8 liter uncooked milk
- 10 Deka melted butter or 8 Deka lard
- Lukewarm milk for cooking
- Melted butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix together in a deep bowl 60 Deka flour, 4 Deka sugar and 5 grams salt
2. Dissolve 2 Deka yeast in 1/4 liter uncooked milk with constant stirring and keep it on the warm stove until the milk becomes lukewarm
3. Beat 4 Stercks butter and 1 whole egg together with 1/8 liter uncooked milk and pour this at the same time with the dissolved yeast into the middle of the flour, stir into a dough and beat finely until it detaches from the spoon
4. Again stir in 10 Deka melted butter or 8 Deka lard until the dough is combined with the butter
5. Let the dough rise covered beside the stove in a warm place until it has risen about halfway
6. In a butter-brushed casserole, pour in enough lukewarm milk to cover the bottom
7. Using a spoon, place large pieces of the dough next to each other in the casserole, each time brushing them with melted butter
8. When the casserole is filled, let it stand covered in a warm place until the dough has risen somewhat
9. Drizzle it once more with melted butter and bake for 3/4 hour in a fairly hot oven
10. After this time, turn out the casserole, separate the dumplings from one another, arrange them on a platter and serve at table with custard or cream
