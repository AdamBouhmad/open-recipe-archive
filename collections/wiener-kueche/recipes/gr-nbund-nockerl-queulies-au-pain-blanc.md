## Ingredients

- 10 Deka butter, beaten
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- some salt
- 20 Deka white breadcrumbs moistened with 4 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Beat 10 Deka butter until creamy
2. Stir in 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg and some salt
3. Mix in the white breadcrumbs moistened with milk
4. Form and cook as with the other Nockerl recipes
