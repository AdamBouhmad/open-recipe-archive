## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- 1/2 liter tarragon vinegar
- Soup stock
- Tarragon sprigs
- Chervil sprigs
- Green parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned, skinned brain in equal parts water and vinegar
2. When the brain has completely cooled in this liquid, remove it and place on a cloth
3. Mix 1/2 liter of tarragon vinegar with good soup stock, season with salt, pepper and a bay leaf, and cook well
4. Cook tarragon sprigs, chervil sprigs and green parsley in salt water briefly, then drain well and chop very finely with a knife
5. Place this chopped mixture with the brain in a casserole, pour the prepared vinegar over it, let it come to a boil once and serve in a dish
