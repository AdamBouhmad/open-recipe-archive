## Ingredients

- 24 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites, beaten to snow
- 12 ounces fine flour
- Optional: 6 pieces blanched pistachios, 10 pieces pignoli, 5 shelled hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Beat 24 ounces sugar with 10 egg yolks for 3/4 hour
2. Gently fold in the snow from 5 egg whites simultaneously with 12 ounces fine flour
3. If desired, finely chop 6 blanched pistachios, 10 pignoli, and 5 hazelnuts together and fold in with the snow
4. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
5. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour
