## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 21 dkg flour
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg
- water
- 8-1 kg small Maschanzker apples
- 12-14 pieces cube sugar
- 1 scoop water

## Directions

1. Make a butter dough and roll out thin
2. Cut into squares and place a half or whole over-stewed apple in the middle
3. Brush corners with egg white and press crosswise over the apple
4. Bake on an unbuttered sheet at high heat until light brown
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve cooled
