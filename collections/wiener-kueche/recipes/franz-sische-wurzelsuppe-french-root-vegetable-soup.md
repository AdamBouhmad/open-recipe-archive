## Ingredients

- yellow turnips
- celery
- parsnip
- leeks
- onion
- beef lard
- cabbage
- head lettuce
- chervil
- sorrel
- good meat broth
- consommé
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Clean yellow turnips, celery, parsnip, leeks, and onion and cut lengthwise
2. Let them simmer soft in beef lard
3. Add cabbage, head lettuce, chervil, and sorrel cut lengthwise
4. When everything is soft together, fill with good meat broth (or better, half consommé if available)
5. Let it come to a boil with it
6. Before serving, add nicely browned bread slices
7. Let them boil with these for a couple more minutes
8. Serve moderately salted at table
