## Ingredients

- 1 pickled cucumber
- 2 anchovies
- 4 h capers
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls Betramesfig vinegar
- 1 spoonful lemon juice
- 1 spoonful water
- salt
- mustard
- several spoonfuls oil
- parsley
- onion
- shallots
- chervil herb

## Directions

1. In a snow kettle, beat 6 egg yolks with 2 spoonfuls Betramesfig vinegar, 1 spoonful lemon juice, and 1 spoonful water on the hot stove plate until thick.
2. Let them cool, then mix them with some salt, mustard, and several spoonfuls of oil.
3. Mix in parsley, onion, shallots, chervil herb, ½ pickled cucumber, the deboned anchovies, and the capers, all finely chopped.
4. The sauce can be served with all cold fish and cold meat.
