## Ingredients

- 8 blanched almonds
- 1 egg yolk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 16 tablespoons sugar of choice
- 1 tablespoon starch
- 2 tablespoons milk cream
- 32 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Mix the blanched almonds with 1 egg yolk to a very fine dough.
2. Beat 8 tablespoons butter until fluffy, stir in the ground almonds and then 16 tablespoons sugar of choice, 1 tablespoon starch, 2 tablespoons milk cream and 32 tablespoons flour.
3. Let this dough stand for some time in a cool place after it is well mixed, then place it on a board dusted with flour, roll it out to the thickness of a knife back, and cut strips that are finger-length and 3 fingers wide.
4. Tie a cord of approximately 1 foot length around the barrel of the mold and pull the cord through it as well.
5. After heating the mold very hot in lard, place a cut dough strip on it, hold the cord lightly on the dough with your index finger and wrap it with the cord, not too tightly, then lead the end of the cord back to the handle and hold it there.
6. Bake the dough wrapped in this way, whose ends should point upward, until light brown in very hot lard.
7. Remove the mold from the lard, hold it over a plate, carefully loosen the cord and carefully remove the baked, arch-shaped fritter from the mold, where the cuts from the cord should run at equal distances.
8. Place the fritter on a dish and sprinkle with sugar.
9. Continue until all the dough is used, then serve the fritters with dissolved marmalade, any warm cream or Chaudeau, or whipped cream or chocolate.
