## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 8 deca blanched almonds
- 3 egg yolks
- 14 deca sifted sugar

## Directions

1. To the stiffly beaten snow of 4 egg whites, add 8 deca blanched almonds finely ground with 3 egg yolks, along with 14 deca sifted sugar and mix everything well together
2. Cover a baking sheet with white paper and divide
