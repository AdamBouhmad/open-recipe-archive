## Ingredients

- orange peels
- strong brandy

## Directions

1. Cut the orange peels into long narrow pieces like sauerkraut, after cleaning away the inner white skin
2. Throw them into a glass bottle
3. Pour strong brandy over them
4. Set in the sun until ready
