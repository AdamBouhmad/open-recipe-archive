## Ingredients

- flattened beef roast pieces
- raw potatoes, cut into large pieces

## Directions

1. Prepare as described above
2. When the roast is half tender, add raw, large-cut potatoes to the side of the casserole in the cooking liquid
3. Braise them together with the meat until tender
4. Arrange and serve together
