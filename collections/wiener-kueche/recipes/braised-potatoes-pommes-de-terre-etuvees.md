## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram raw peeled potatoes
- 10 ounces butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon finely cut parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes very finely into dice and put them in butter with some salt and pepper, cover this dish and braise slowly.
2. When the potatoes are soft, sprinkle them with finely cut parsley and serve them heaped up.
