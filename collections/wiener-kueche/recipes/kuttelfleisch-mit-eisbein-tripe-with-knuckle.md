## Ingredients

- 2 kilos tripe
- butter
- green parsley
- onion
- salt
- breadcrumbs soaked in broth
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cooked green peas
- puff pastry
- butter
- flour
- grated Parmesan cheese
- rice

## Directions

1. After cleaning, 2 kilos of tripe are slowly cooked until tender, then cooled and cut into noodle-like strips.
2. In a casserole containing hot butter, add finely cut green parsley and finely cut onion, let these roast somewhat, then add the cut tripe, salt to taste and let braise covered for a while.
3. Then add breadcrumbs softened in broth, well-pressed and passed through a sieve, with a pinch of pepper, some nutmeg and 1/4 liter of cooked green peas, stir well and set aside.
4. After completely cooling, a buttered and floured mold is lined with knife-back-thick rolled-out puff pastry, a layer of the above mixture is placed on it, this is sprinkled with grated Parmesan cheese, this is covered with several spoonfuls of piled rice, another layer of the above mixture is placed on top and this is continued until everything is used up, then it is baked.
