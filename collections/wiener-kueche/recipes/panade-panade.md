## Ingredients

- 6 crusts removed, softened in cold water bread rolls
- 16 spoonfuls melted butter
- 2 liters boiling beef broth
- 0.25 liter cream
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Squeeze 6 bread rolls (crusts removed, softened in cold water) well
2. Add to 16 spoonfuls melted butter and stir over heat with a cooking spoon until it becomes a smooth dough that shines with fat
3. Gradually pour 2 liters boiling beef broth over this dough, stirring constantly
4. When the soup has cooked for 1/2 hour over moderate heat, mix with 0.25 liter cream in which 3 egg yolks have been beaten
5. Serve after that
