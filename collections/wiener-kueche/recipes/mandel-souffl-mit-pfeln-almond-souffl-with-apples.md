## Ingredients

- 12 Deka beaten butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 Deka powdered sugar
- 12 Deka finely ground and passed almonds
- 1 1/2 liter cream
- 8 egg whites
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold
- pippin apples
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deka butter with 8 egg yolks, 16 Deka powdered sugar, 12 Deka finely ground almonds, and 1 1/2 liter cream gradually and stir.
2. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites.
3. Core pippin apples, peel them and fill the resulting hollow with marmalade.
4. Place them in a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour at finger-width distance and pour the mass over.
5. Bake in the oven at low heat for 1 hour.
6. When this is done, remove the cake mold, discard the rim and arrange the baked cake on the cake plate in a bowl and serve.
