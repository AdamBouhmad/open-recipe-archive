## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram sweet chestnuts
- 10 to 12 decagrams fine sifted sugar
- hot praline sugar
- candied chestnuts
- melted chocolate
- whipped cream mixed with sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the sweet chestnuts crosswise and place them in a casserole with enough cold water to cover them
2. Cook for approximately half an hour until the interior of the chestnuts is soft and mealy
3. Remove the chestnuts with a spoon and pass them through a sieve into a deep bowl
4. Mix the sifted sugar with the passed chestnuts thoroughly
5. Form walnut-sized balls from this mixture
6. Using a piping needle, pick up each ball individually, dip it in hot praline sugar, and lay two rows of balls side by side in a predetermined bowl
7. Cover this with a second double row also dipped in hot praline sugar
8. Form somewhat smaller balls, dip them individually in hot praline sugar, and place them on the center of the two double rows
9. Build the crown by placing proportionally large crown points made of smaller chestnut balls dipped in sugar
10. When the crown is complete, dust it with melted chocolate
11. Fill the empty space created in the middle of the crown with whipped cream mixed with sugar, to which you may add any desired flavor
