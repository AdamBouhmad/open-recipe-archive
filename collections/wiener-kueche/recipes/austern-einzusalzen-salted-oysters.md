## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- whole cinnamon
- green fennel, crushed in summer

## Directions

1. Remove the oysters from their shells
2. Place them in large earthenware pots such that the bottom is sprinkled with some salt and pepper
3. Lay some bay leaves, whole cinnamon, and green fennel (crushed in summer) on top
4. Then lay a layer of oysters, and again a layer of the aforementioned spices, then oysters and so forth until the vessel is full
5. You can then eat them raw or use them cooked for various dishes
