## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- eggs
- sugar
- grated lemon zest
- salt

## Directions

1. First stir a small spoonful of flour into a little melted butter
2. Beat as many eggs as needed
3. Add sugar, grated lemon zest, a little salt, and about one quarter-seidel of butter for every 8 eggs
4. Stir everything together well
5. Pour into a dish lined with cold butter
6. Place the dish in a baking pan on salt or sand, level it so it sits straight, and bake with heat from below and above
7. Alternatively, for no more than 8 eggs, place the dish over a pot of boiling water and cook it until set
