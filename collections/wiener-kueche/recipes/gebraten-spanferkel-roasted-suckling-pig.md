## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Root vegetables
- Vinegar
- Red wine
- Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut open the suckling pig and remove the entrails
2. Wash the suckling pig thoroughly, let it soak in water for some time, then remove it and dry it with a cloth
3. Cut it into desired pieces
4. Cook it like boiled meat with root vegetables in vinegar-flavored liquid and red wine
5. When the meat is sufficiently tender, remove it with a slotted spoon onto a platter
6. Pour some broth over it and garnish with horseradish or butter sauce or vinegar and oil
7. Be very careful so that the meat does not boil too much
