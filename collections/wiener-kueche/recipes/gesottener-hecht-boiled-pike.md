## Ingredients

- pike
- water
- salt
- 1 bay leaf

## Directions

1. Scale, open and clean the pike
2. In a fish pan, add necessary water and salt with 1 bay leaf
3. When the water boils, place the pike in (the water must cover the pike)
4. As soon as the water returns to boiling, remove from heat
5. Let the pike stand approximately 3/4 hour while the water maintains simmering temperature but must not boil
6. When serving, place the pike on a suitable platter or on thin board covered with a napkin that must be sewn together on the underside of the board
7. Arrange plucked green parsley around it and garnish the fish with diced potatoes boiled in salt water
8. Serve with melted butter, Hollandaise sauce, thin anchovy sauce, or strained mustard sauce
9. The cuts in the fish need not necessarily be made
10. The pike can also be divided into 2-finger-wide pieces, each cut multiple times (scored)
