## Ingredients

- a piece of thin breast
- salt
- white beer
- wine
- beef broth
- vinegar
- peeled and sliced almonds
- raisins
- cloves
- ginger
- pepper
- grated lebkuchen (spice cake)
- sugar
- a pair of bay leaves
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take a piece of thin breast, chop it into pieces one and a half fingers thick and beat it well
2. Sprinkle it with salt and brown it lightly on a grill
3. Layer it in an earthen pot and pour over it white beer, wine, beef broth and a little vinegar
4. Place coals around the pot, cover it and let the meat braise for quite some time
5. Then add peeled and sliced almonds, raisins, cloves, ginger, pepper, grated lebkuchen, sugar and a pair of bay leaves
6. If the broth is insufficient, add more wine, beef broth and vinegar
7. Stir it often so it absorbs the flavors well and keep adding coals until it is properly tender
8. When serving, sprinkle it with lemon peel
