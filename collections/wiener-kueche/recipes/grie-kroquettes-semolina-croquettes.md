## Ingredients

- 1/4 litre milk
- 16 dkg coarse semolina
- hot lard
- grated chocolate or sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 16 dkg coarse semolina spoonwise into 1/4 litre boiling milk until thick
2. Let cool
3. Form equal-sized pieces into croquettes
4. Bread them
5. Fry in hot lard
6. Drain
7. Roll in grated chocolate or sugar and serve warm
