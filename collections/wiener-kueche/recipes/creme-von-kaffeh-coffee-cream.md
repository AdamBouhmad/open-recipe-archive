## Ingredients

- One Seidel water
- 8 Loth coffee
- Half a Seidel good cream
- 16 whole eggs
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook 8 Loth coffee in one Seidel water as usual
2. Add half a Seidel good cream lukewarm to it
3. Beat 16 whole eggs in a basin, and with constant stirring add the sweetened coffee to the cream
4. Arrange the cream on a plate according to the method given above
5. Serve the cream cold at table
