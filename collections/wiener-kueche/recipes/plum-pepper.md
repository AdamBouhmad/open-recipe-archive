## Ingredients

- dried plums
- clove spice
- lemon peel
- sugar
- red wine
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare this dish like dried plums in sauce
2. The only difference is that the softly cooked plums are finely cut with a sharp knife
3. These are then added to the butter sauce with crushed clove spice, some finely cut lemon peel, and as much sugar and red wine as desired
4. Let simmer
