## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 4 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Fold 3 egg yolks and a pinch of salt gently into the stiff snow of 3 egg whites
2. Mix in 4 dkg flour
3. Fill the mixture into a schöberl pan and bake at moderate heat until light brown
4. Loosen the schöberl, turn it out, cut it, and serve warm
