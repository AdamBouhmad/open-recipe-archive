## Ingredients

- 21 deka butter
- 5 deka flour
- 14 deka flour
- 4 deka fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 7 deka cooled but not cold butter
- 5 deka sifted sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 pinch finely cut lemon peel
- 35 deka flour
- 1/4 liter lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Work 21 deka butter with 5 deka flour by hand into a dough on a board
2. Roll flat with a rolling pin and let rest in a cool place
3. In a deep bowl, combine 14 deka flour and in its center pour 4 deka fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
4. Lightly stir and cover, let stand in a warm place
5. When this yeast has risen sufficiently, add to it 7 deka cooled but not cold butter, 5 deka sifted sugar, 3 egg yolks, 1 pinch finely cut lemon peel, and 35 deka flour
6. Mix well with 1/4 liter lukewarm milk, and beat the dough until it releases from the spoon
7. Roll out on a flour-dusted board to finger thickness
8. Lay the above butter on it and fold together with the dough as described for butter dough of finest type (No. 1045)
9. After folding 3 times and rolling out to finger thickness, cut into 3 finger-wide and equally long pieces
10. Place on a butter-greased and flour-dusted sheet, making a small indentation with the finger in the center of each piece
11. Cover the sheet with a cloth and place in a warm spot
12. When the pieces have risen about halfway higher, brush with beaten eggs
13. Fill the indentation made with the finger with desired filling and bake at fairly strong heat
14. These cakes require very careful attention
