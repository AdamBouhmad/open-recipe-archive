## Ingredients

- raspberry pulp
- 12 dkg vanilla sugar
- whipped cream
- 1 dkg dissolved gelatin

## Directions

1. Mix the raspberry pulp with vanilla sugar, whipped cream, and dissolved gelatin.
2. Fill a pudding mold rinsed with water.
3. Bury in ice for 2–3 hours.
4. Loosen, hold in hot water for a moment, unmold.
5. Garnish if desired with whipped cream and serve.
