## Ingredients

- 4–6 pork cutlets, about 20 dkg each
- caraway seeds, crushed
- salt
- 4 dkg fat or butter
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe the cutlets clean, tap them lightly.
2. Rub both sides with crushed caraway seeds and salt.
3. Heat 4 dkg fat in a large casserole, add the cutlets and brown on both sides.
4. Pour in a little water and braise them covered for 30–45 minutes.
5. Turn them frequently and pour in a little hot water if needed.
6. The sauce should be a nice brown color.
