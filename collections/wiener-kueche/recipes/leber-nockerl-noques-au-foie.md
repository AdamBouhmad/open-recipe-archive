## Ingredients

- 150 Deka scraped liver
- 1/4 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley roasted in butter
- 1 knife point marjoram
- 30 Deka finely chopped marrow
- 20 Deka peeled, diced, milk-soaked breadcrumbs
- 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
- some salt, pepper and spices as desired
- 6 Deka breadcrumbs
- boiling salted water

## Directions

1. Combine the liver with the parsley, marjoram, marrow, breadcrumbs, eggs, salt, pepper and spices
2. Pound and press through a sieve into a deep bowl
3. Stir in 6 Deka breadcrumbs
4. Let rest covered for 3/4 hour
5. Place Nockerl into boiling salted water and cook covered for 8-10 minutes
6. Remove with a skimming spoon
7. Note: 2 tablespoons finely chopped onions can also be roasted with the parsley if desired
