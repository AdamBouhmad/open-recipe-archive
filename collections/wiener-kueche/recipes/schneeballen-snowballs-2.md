## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 5 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 dkg sugar
- 1 spoon cream
- 2 spoons wine or rum
- hot lard
- sugar
- raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Make a dough on the board from the flour, butter, egg yolks, sugar, cream, and wine or rum.
2. Divide it into equally large pieces, roll them into thin sheets, and cut finger-wide strips lengthwise without going over the edge.
3. Cover them with a cloth and let them rest for one hour.
4. Thread the sheets onto a cooking spoon handle, alternating one strip on top and one on the bottom.
5. Hold them in hot lard, bake them until light brown, and serve them sprinkled with sugar, with or without raspberry juice.
