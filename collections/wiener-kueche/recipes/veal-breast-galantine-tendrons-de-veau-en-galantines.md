## Ingredients

- veal breast
- veal forcemeat
- ham
- lard
- truffles
- goose liver
- blanched pistachios
- onion
- root vegetables
- bay leaf
- thyme
- pepper
- broth
- white wine
- aspic
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal breast as described for steamed veal breast, filling it with forcemeat, ham, lard, truffles, goose liver, and pistachios.
2. Roll tightly and bind securely with kitchen twine.
3. Line a casserole with thinly sliced lard, place a piece of onion, thinly sliced root vegetables, a bay leaf, and some thyme.
4. Place the bound meat on top, cover with lard strips or pour fat over, cover with double-folded paper, fit with a suitable lid, and braise for about 0.5 hour.
5. Add broth and some white wine and braise covered for another hour.
6. Allow to cool completely, then remove the meat, discard the top lard, and untie the string.
7. The meat may be wrapped in cloth and stored in a cool place for several days if not used immediately.
8. Before serving, cut into half-finger-thick slices, arrange neatly on a platter, garnish with chopped aspic, and serve with any mayonnaise.
