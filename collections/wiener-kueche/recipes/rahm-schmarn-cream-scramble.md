## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter flour
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites (snow)
- 8 deka hot butter

## Directions

1. Place 1/4 liter flour in a deep bowl
2. Gradually stir in 1/4 liter sour cream until smooth
3. Mix in 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt
4. Stir in 8 egg yolks one after another
5. Gently fold in the snow from 8 egg whites
6. Pour this dough into a casserole containing 8 deka hot butter
7. Treat further as described for the kleine Kaiserschmarn
