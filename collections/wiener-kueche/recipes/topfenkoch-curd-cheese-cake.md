## Ingredients

- 1 maß good milk
- 6 whole eggs
- juice of 1 lemon
- bread crumbs from 1 small roll
- 12 loth butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- zest of 1 lemon, finely diced
- 3 loth citron, finely cut
- 1 loth pistachio nuts, finely cut
- 4 loth almonds, finely ground

## Directions

1. Put 1 maß good milk in a pot with 6 whole eggs and juice of 1 lemon
2. Beat everything together well
3. Strain through a sieve into a ramekin
4. Set over heat and stir constantly until it becomes a not too firm curd cheese
5. Strain through a sieve and let cool
6. Pound it in a mortar with bread crumbs from 1 small roll
7. Beat 12 loth butter in a bowl until fluffy
8. Beat in 6 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks, each well beaten, then the curd cheese
9. Add finely diced lemon zest, finely cut citron, finely cut pistachio nuts, and finely ground almonds
10. Stir everything constantly until baking time
11. Butter a metal dish and ring mold well
12. Fill the mixture in and bake in a cake pan
13. Sprinkle with sugar
