## Ingredients

- meat from a roasted woodcock
- 10 decagrams fresh butter
- 5 juniper berries

## Directions

1. Pound the meat from the roasted woodcock with 10 decagrams fresh butter and 5 juniper berries very finely in a mortar
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Let it cool
4. In the same way, butter can be prepared from all kinds of game, not only wild game but also domestic poultry
5. With wild game meat, a piece of truffle braised in Madeira wine can be added to the butter
