## Ingredients

- 3 dag sugar
- 1 dl water
- ½ liter milk
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 dag sugar

## Directions

1. In a pot, toast the sugar until dark, then deglaze with water and bring to a boil
2. Pour into a mixing bowl, mix with the milk, egg yolks, and sugar, and beat over a water bath until thick
