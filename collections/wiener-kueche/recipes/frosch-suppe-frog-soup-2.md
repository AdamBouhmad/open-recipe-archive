## Ingredients

- 16 peeled and finely cut turnips
- 1 liter pea water
- 4 cleaned, thinly sliced champignons
- 5 decagrams butter
- 10 whole frogs or 20 frog legs
- 2 liters pea water or broth
- frog bones
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream
- Madeira wine, white table wine, or champagne (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the turnips in 1 liter of pea water
2. Roast the champignons in 5 decagrams of butter
3. Add the whole frogs or frog legs to the champignons and cook until tender in 2 liters of pea water or broth
4. Remove the meat from the frog bones and set aside
5. Pound the frog bones with the cooked turnips and champignons
6. Stir this mixture together well with the soup
7. Season and bind with 4 egg yolks and 1/4 liter of cream mixed together
8. Pass through a sieve
9. Add the frog meat back to the soup
10. Serve with fried turnips
11. Optionally add Madeira wine, white table wine, or champagne
