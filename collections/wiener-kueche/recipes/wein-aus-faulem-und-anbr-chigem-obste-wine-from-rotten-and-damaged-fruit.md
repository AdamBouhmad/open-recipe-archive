## Ingredients

- rotten or damaged fruit
- water, as much as needed
- one third hopfen (hops)

## Directions

1. Cut the fruit into pieces
2. Put it with water and one third hopfen into the kettle
3. Boil it for one and a half hours
4. Strain it
5. Let it ferment
6. Draw it off into bottles
7. You will have a beer that is wine-like in character
