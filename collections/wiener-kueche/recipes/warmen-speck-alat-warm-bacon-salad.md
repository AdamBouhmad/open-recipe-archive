## Ingredients

- Salad (endive or Bologna lettuce preferred)
- One quarter pound bacon, diced
- Wine vinegar
- Salt
- Butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the salad thoroughly
2. Cut the bacon into small dice
3. Let it turn golden in a pan
4. Pour wine vinegar and salt over it
5. Let it come to a boil with the bacon
6. Pour the boiling mixture over the salad
7. The vinegar can be strained and brought to a boil again if desired
8. Alternatively, the salad can be made warm with butter
