## Ingredients

- 6 pieces beef roast
- fat
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- 1 1/2 liter brown broth
- garlic
- 1 bay leaf

## Directions

1. Pound the beef roast pieces, season them, and quickly brown them in fat
2. Transfer them to another casserole without the cooking fat
3. Pour 1/2 liter sour cream and 1 1/2 liter brown broth over them
4. Season with garlic and 1 bay leaf
5. Braise them covered over strong heat until tender
6. The sauce should be thick and light brown in color
7. Pour the sauce over the roast when serving
