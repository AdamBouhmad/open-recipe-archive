## Ingredients

- honey
- spices
- 1/2 kg flour
- 1 whole egg
- 4 dkg butter
- not quite 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Directions

1. Let the honey cook with the spices
2. Mix it on the board with the flour and remaining ingredients
3. Work the dough well
4. Let it rest covered for one day
5. Roll it out and cut various shapes
6. Place filling in the middle
7. Lay a sheet of dough on top
8. Press the edges firmly together
9. Brush with egg
10. Top with half an almond
11. Place the gingerbread on an unbuttered baking sheet
12. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
13. Let them rest for 4-5 days before serving
