## Ingredients

- 7 deka gum tragacanth
- 1.3 liters water
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 kilos and some peppermint oil
- various desired shapes

## Directions

1. Dissolve 7 deka gum tragacanth in 1.3 liters water. The dissolution takes 12 hours.
2. Pass the gum tragacanth and slowly stir with 4 egg yolks on a marble slab.
3. Gradually add 2 kilos and some peppermint oil while stirring.
4. Cut out desired shapes and dry them slowly.
5. For tragacanth dough for figures and various decorations such as leaves and flowers, make the same way, only with the difference that only 2 egg yolks, 1/8 kilo sugar, and 1/2 kilo powdered flour are added.
