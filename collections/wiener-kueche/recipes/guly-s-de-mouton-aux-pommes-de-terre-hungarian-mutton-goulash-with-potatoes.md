## Ingredients

- mutton shoulder
- fat or bacon
- onions, finely cut
- rocambole
- cumin
- salt
- seasonings
- paprika
- broth
- potatoes, raw and diced
- flour
- sour cream
- parsley sauce (optional)
- rice or noodles (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton shoulder into fairly large, cube-like pieces.
2. In a casserole, add some fat or bacon, finely cut onions and rocambole, and let these brown.
3. Add the meat with some cumin, salt, seasonings if desired, paprika and some broth, and braise gently until somewhat tender.
4. Before the goulash is completely tender, add diced raw potatoes at the side of the casserole and braise them together with the meat.
5. Stir some flour into sour cream, bring it to a boil with the sauce, remove from heat and combine everything well.
6. Braised rice or noodles can also be served with it, or parsley sauce can be stirred into the broth instead of sour cream.
