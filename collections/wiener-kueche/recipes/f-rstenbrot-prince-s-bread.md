## Ingredients

- Sugar equal in weight to 2 eggs
- 2 whole eggs
- Flour equal in weight to 2 eggs
- Raisins equal in weight to 2 eggs
- Softened almonds, cut into sticks

## Directions

1. Beat sugar equal to 2 eggs with 2 whole eggs for 15 minutes
2. Mix in flour, raisins, and softened, stick-cut almonds
3. Spread in a long, not-too-wide form on buttered sheet
4. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
5. Slice thin only the next day
