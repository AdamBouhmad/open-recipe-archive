## Ingredients

- 60-80 dekagrams pork shoulder
- 1 celery root
- 10 liters Kremser mustard
- 6 liters cream
- 5 dekagrams fat
- 1 onion
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it, optionally bind it
2. In a roasting pan, brown a finely chopped onion in 5 dekagrams fat until light golden
3. Add the finely sliced celery, the mustard and the meat
4. Roast for 1-2 hours under frequent basting with its own juice and additional liquid
5. Defat the juice, strain it, pour it onto 1/2 liter thick cream, cook well and serve with the meat
