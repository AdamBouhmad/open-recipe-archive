## Ingredients

- 20 live, washed crayfish
- water to cover
- salt
- 1 tablespoon caraway
- 10 sprigs parsley tied together

## Directions

1. Place 20 live, washed crayfish in a casserole dish
2. Pour water over them until they are covered
3. Salt them well
4. Add 1 tablespoon caraway and 10 sprigs of parsley tied together
5. Cook covered for approximately 15-20 minutes until they turn red
6. Arrange them on a platter piled neatly (pyramid-shaped) on a clean napkin while hot
7. Sprinkle lightly with fresh parsley on top
8. Ensure all claws are directed upward; arrange some crayfish at the bottom of the dish with claws facing the edge
9. Alternatively, one can place the live crayfish directly into boiling water and wrap the caraway in a cloth napkin instead
