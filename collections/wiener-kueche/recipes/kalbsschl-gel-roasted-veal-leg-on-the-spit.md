## Ingredients

- small veal leg
- silver handle or paper band
- butter
- salt
- white soup
- 3 tablespoons dissolved soup aspic

## Directions

1. For a small veal leg, remove the outer skin, hack off the knee bone at the so-called joint above the knee disc, separate the meat from the bone and push it back.
2. The bone must be trimmed such that when serving a silver handle or paper band can be attached.
3. After the leg has been completely cleaned and skinned, salt the meat well on all sides and let it rest for a while.
4. Then wrap the meat with several sheets of paper well coated with butter, bind these with twine and roast the meat on a spit at moderate heat for approximately 2 hours.
5. Then remove the paper, dust the meat again with salt, baste it with melted butter and white soup and let it roast another approximately 15 minutes until the meat has a light brown color.
6. Then remove it from the spit, cut it into pieces as desired and arrange these on a platter so that the meat retains its former shape.
7. The bone is provided with a paper band or silver handle.
8. To the sauce remaining in the pan add 3 tablespoons of dissolved soup aspic, let it come to a boil together and serve it separately with the meat.
