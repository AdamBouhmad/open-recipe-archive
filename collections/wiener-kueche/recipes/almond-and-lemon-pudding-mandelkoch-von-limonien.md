## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound ground almonds
- three-quarters pound sugar
- one large lemon
- six whole eggs
- ten egg yolks
- butter for the mold

## Directions

1. In a mixing bowl, take three-quarters pound ground almonds and the same amount of sugar
2. Grate a large, beautiful lemon on a grater well
3. Let the peel simmer, as well as a couple of eggs, strain it, press it out well, and mix it under the almonds and sugar
4. Stir everything well together
5. Take six whole eggs and ten egg yolks, beat them in gradually, and stir for a whole hour
6. Coat the mold with butter, fill in the stirred mixture, bake it for a whole hour slowly, and it is done
