## Ingredients

- pike
- pike flesh
- breadcrumbs soaked in milk

## Directions

1. Choose any desired size pike, scale it, cut open the belly, remove the entrails and rinse the fish quickly
2. Dry it well with a clean cloth, sew the belly which was opened with a strong thread (unbleached twine)
3. Trim the swimming fins and lay the fish on its belly on a water-dampened board
4. Cut the skin on the back from the tail toward the head and separate it 2 fingers to the side of the head
5. Do the same on the other side of the fish
6. Carefully pull the skin from the flesh and spread it completely on the board
7. Using a small knife, remove all the flesh from the skeleton, but do not injure the head and skin
8. Leave the fish lying with the spread skin sprinkled with some salt and cover with a cloth dipped in cold water
9. Meanwhile make the following forcemeat: 0.5 deca pike flesh, 10 deca rind-removed breadcrumbs, soaked in milk and well pressed out
