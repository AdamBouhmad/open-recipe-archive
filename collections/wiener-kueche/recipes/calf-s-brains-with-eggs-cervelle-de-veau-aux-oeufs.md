## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 2 whole eggs
- Salt
- Finely cut green parsley
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Place the peeled brain in 8 tablespoons of hot butter
2. When it has completely contracted, beat it lightly apart with a serving spoon
3. Mix in 2 whole eggs, some salt, finely cut green parsley and some pepper
4. Alternatively, instead of stirring the eggs with the brain, serve fried eggs on top of the roasted brain when plating
