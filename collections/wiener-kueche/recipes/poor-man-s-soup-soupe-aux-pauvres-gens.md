## Ingredients

- 10 Deca butter or 12 Deca lard
- Flour as needed
- 0.33 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 liters broth

## Directions

1. In 10 Deca butter or 12 Deca lard, add flour as needed and let brown considerably
2. Add 0.33 teaspoon cinnamon, roasting it somewhat
3. Pour in 2 liters broth and stir as for burnt flour soup
4. Add spices if desired
5. Let everything cook, pass through a sieve, and serve
6. The soup should have a nice brown appearance
