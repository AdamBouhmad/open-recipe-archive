## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled, strained egg yolks
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 1 3/4 liters oil in ice
- juice of 2 lemons or 2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Stir 4 hard-boiled, strained egg yolks with 4 raw egg yolks in a deep bowl for 15 minutes on ice
2. After this time, stir in 1 3/4 liters of oil in ice, the juice of 2 lemons or 2 tablespoons of vinegar drop by drop gradually
3. However, one can begin adding the vinegar or other acid only after half of the oil is mixed with the egg yolks
4. One must be very careful and precise with this treatment so the mayonnaise does not separate (become curdled)
5. Stir in pepper and salt at the end
6. Keep the mayonnaise covered on ice
7. If it has curdled, put 2 raw egg yolks in a very cold bowl set in ice and slowly beat the mayonnaise back together with it
