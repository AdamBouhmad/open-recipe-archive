## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter or lard
- 1 tablespoon crushed sugar
- 2-3 scraped, finely grated yellow carrots
- Stock
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- Crushed nutmeg

## Directions

1. To 8 tablespoons butter or lard, add 1 tablespoon crushed sugar
2. When this is brown, add about 2-3 scraped, finely grated yellow carrots and let them braise
3. Dust the braised carrots with the necessary flour, pour with 1/4 liter stock
4. Add 1/2 teaspoon finely cut green parsley and some crushed nutmeg and bring to boil
