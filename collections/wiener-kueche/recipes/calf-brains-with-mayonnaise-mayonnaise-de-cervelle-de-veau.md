## Ingredients

- Root vegetables and onion, sliced thin
- Lemon zest
- Parsley sprigs
- Spices
- Water
- Vinegar
- Calf brain
- Mayonnaise
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Green salad

## Directions

1. Put thinly sliced root vegetables, onion, a little lemon zest, parsley sprigs and spices in a saucepan
2. Add water and vinegar and cook until soft
3. Strain through a sieve and cook the brain in the strained broth until completely shrunken
4. Set aside with the broth until ready to use
5. Using a serving spoon, remove the brain, place on cloth
6. Once the water has drained sufficiently, cut into pieces of choice
7. Arrange piled on a platter, cover with mayonnaise
8. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs and green salad
