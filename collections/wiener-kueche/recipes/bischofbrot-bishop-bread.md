## Ingredients

- 15 arg raw almonds, cut lengthwise
- 5 dkg chocolate
- 5 dkg citron, diced
- 15 dkg sugar
- 8 whole eggs
- 15 arg flour
- 15 dkg raisins

## Directions

1. Cut 15 arg raw almonds lengthwise and 5 dkg chocolate into pieces; dice 5 dkg citron
2. Beat 15 dkg sugar well with 8 whole eggs
3. Mix in 15 arg flour, almonds, chocolate, citron, and 15 dkg raisins
4. Fill into a buttered, floured biscuit mold
5. Bake slowly until light brown
6. Cool, turn out, and cut into thin slices only the next day
