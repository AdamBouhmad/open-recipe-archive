## Ingredients

- young chickens
- young peas
- butter
- sugar
- flour
- good bouillon
- parsley
- pepper herb
- yellow lettuce
- 4 pounds butter
- yellow turnips
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare a couple of young chickens and cook them in consommé until half done
2. Take two seidel of young peas and sweat the finest ones with a piece of butter in a casserole, to which you may add some sugar if desired
3. Add one tablespoon of flour, stir this with half a measure of good bouillon until clear
4. Add a bunch of parsley and pepper herb, as well as the young chickens, and let everything cook until tender
5. Sweat the larger peas with pepper herb, yellow lettuce, 4 pounds butter and some flour
6. Fill with the necessary bouillon and cook slowly for an hour
7. Add some yellow turnips and finally strain through a hair sieve
8. When the young chickens are tender, place them in the soup pot and arrange the green peas and the other soup over them, adding a little salt and nutmeg
