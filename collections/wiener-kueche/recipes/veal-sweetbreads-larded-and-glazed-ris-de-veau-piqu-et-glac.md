## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Water
- Salt
- Bacon
- Butter
- Broth
- Aspic glaze
- Mushroom purée
- Braised lettuce
- Sorrel purée

## Directions

1. Let the veal sweetbreads lie in lukewarm water for one hour, then remove and boil for a few minutes in salt water until it slightly yields to finger pressure.
2. Place in cold water, remove, and lay on a board, cover with another board, and weight it down.
3. When completely dry, lard it with thin, half-finger-long strips of bacon.
4. Place the sweetbreads in a casserole lined with butter, moisten with some broth, and braise quickly.
5. When completely tender, cut into pieces, arrange circularly on a plate, and brush with dissolved aspic glaze.
6. In the center place mushroom purée, braised lettuce (as a vegetable) or sorrel purée.
