## Ingredients

- 30 drams fine sifted sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon anise
- 36 drams flour
- butter for greasing the baking form

## Directions

1. Stir 30 drams fine sifted sugar with 12 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs for 3/4 hour
2. Lightly mix in 1 tablespoon anise with 36 drams flour
3. Pour into a baking form greased with butter and dusted with flour
4. Bake at moderate heat for 1 to 1 1/4 hours
5. Turn out onto a sieve and let cool
6. Cut into slices the thickness of a knife back and dry these in the heated oven as desired
7. The anise can be omitted and vanilla sugar used instead of ordinary sugar, giving it the name Vanille-Brot
