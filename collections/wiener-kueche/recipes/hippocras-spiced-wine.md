## Ingredients

- 4 bottles good Rhenish or white wine
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 loth cinnamon
- some white whole peppercorns
- peel from 1 lemon
- 1 grain ambergris
- sugar

## Directions

1. Combine 4 bottles good Rhenish or white wine with 1 pound sugar, 2 loth cinnamon, some white whole peppercorns and peel from 1 lemon
2. Cover well and let stand for 24 hours
3. Place 1 grain ambergris (pounded fine with sugar and wrapped in linen) in a filter bag
4. Strain the wine through several times
