## Ingredients

- Various types of turnips
- Salt water
- Soup
- Butter
- Green peas
- Sprouts
- Green beans, sliced diagonally
- Asparagus, cut into pieces
- Cold water
- Cauliflower
- Red beets, cut into pieces or scooped out
- Sugar
- Meat for garnish, or fried or baked fish

## Directions

1. Cook all varieties of turnips, each according to its type, in salt water
2. Place them in a casserole and braise them with some soup and butter
3. Cook green peas, sprouts, sliced green beans, and asparagus pieces separately in salt water until tender
4. Place the cooked vegetables in cold water and drain
5. Add the drained vegetables to the braised turnips
6. Pour some butter sauce over them, mix well together, and arrange heaped on a serving dish
7. Top with a cauliflower cooked in salt water
8. Pour more butter sauce over the arranged dish
9. For enhanced appearance, add red beets that have been braised separately in butter, sugar, and water
10. Garnish the dish with meat, or fried or baked fish
