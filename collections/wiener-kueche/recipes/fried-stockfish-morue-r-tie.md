## Ingredients

- Cooked stockfish
- Flour
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Hot fat

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked stockfish into desired pieces.
2. Dip these pieces in flour, dunk in whisked, beaten eggs, roll in breadcrumbs.
3. Fry on both sides until golden in very hot fat.
4. Remove with a slotted spoon, place on blotting paper and arrange in a bowl, sprinkled with blanched, chopped parsley and garnished with lemon wedges.
5. The fried stockfish can also be served with vegetables or mustard sauce.
