## Ingredients

- 32 Deca flour
- Butter
- Flour
- Jam
- Punch essence or liqueur

## Directions

1. Prepare a biscuit mixture from 32 Deca flour
2. Fill a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour with this mixture
3. Bake at moderate heat for approximately 1 hour in the oven
4. After this time, turn the mixture out and when it has cooled completely, cut it in the middle to obtain 2 round slices
5. Cut one slice into cubes of equal size
6. Cover the bottom of the same cake form (in which the biscuit was first baked) with a sheet of writing paper greased with unsalted bacon
7. Arrange the cubically cut biscuit, with the cubes alternately dipped in punch essence and various colored liqueurs, close together in checkerboard pattern into the cake form to cover the bottom exactly
8. When this is done, cover the biscuit with boiling hot jam
