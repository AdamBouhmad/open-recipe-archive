## Ingredients

- 6 deca butter
- 18 deca cooked, pressed potatoes
- 1 teaspoon fine Gries
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg whites
- butter for greasing
- flour for lining

## Directions

1. Beat 6 deca butter until fluffy
2. Mix in 18 deca cooked, pressed potatoes
3. Add 1 teaspoon fine Gries, some salt, 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg, mixing well together
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 2 egg whites
5. Grease small molds with butter and line with flour
6. Fill halfway
7. Cook in steam and serve with brown sauce
