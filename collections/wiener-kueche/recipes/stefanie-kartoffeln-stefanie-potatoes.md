## Ingredients

- 14 deca blanched almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 14 deca sifted sugar
- 8 egg whites
- 14 deca sugar
- 4 egg whites for cream filling
- 14 deca sugar for cream
- Chocolate, coffee, vanilla or other flavorings for cream

## Directions

1. Pound the 14 deca blanched almonds very finely with 4 egg yolks
2. Stir in 14 deca sifted sugar
3. Beat 8 egg whites to stiff peaks and mix with 14 deca sugar
4. Fold the pounded almonds into the egg white mixture
5. Fill a pastry bag with the mixture and pipe small, equal-sized puffs onto a paper-lined baking sheet
6. Sprinkle with sugar and dry in a hot oven for 1/4 hour
7. While still warm, remove from the paper
8. Prepare the cream filling: beat 4 egg whites to stiff peaks and mix with 14 deca sugar, adding desired flavoring such as chocolate, coffee, or vanilla
9. Once completely cooled, fill the puffs with cream, pair two together, brush with thin warm marmalade, and glaze
10. Alternatively, fill with whipped cream and do not glaze
