## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Flour
- Water
- Vinegar
- Warm water
- Butter
- Good broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. These roots must be scrubbed very quickly, as they lose their color and turn red through prolonged handling in the hand; to prevent this, put some flour with water in a bowl, stir it well, add some vinegar and warm water, and as soon as a root is scrubbed, place it immediately in this mixture.
2. In 8 ounces of hot butter, let an equal amount of flour roast, then pour it with good broth, add the roots cut into desired pieces (which have been removed from the scrubbing solution and rinsed with fresh water), and let them cook gently with the necessary salt.
