## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Pork fat, equal amount to meat
- Salt
- Crushed bay leaves
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Pepper
- Garlic, optional
- Water
- Thin intestines, cleaned and soaked

## Directions

1. Finely chop the pork meat and pork fat together with a mincing knife.
2. Season the mixture with salt, crushed bay leaves, thyme, marjoram, pepper, and optionally garlic.
3. Thin the mixture with some water.
4. Fill the cleaned and soaked thin intestines with the mixture and twist into desired lengths.
5. Shortly before use, place lard in a shallow pan and lay the sausages in it.
6. Brush the sausages with fat, fry them, and turn them during cooking.
7. Serve immediately, as they lose flavor with longer standing.
8. Serve with mustard or desired vegetables.
