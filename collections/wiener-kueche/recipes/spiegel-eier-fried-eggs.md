## Ingredients

- Butter or lard
- Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put a hazelnut-sized piece of butter or lard in each indentation of a muffin mold.
2. When hot, crack an egg into each indentation, dust the eggs with salt and pepper and cook until the egg white is completely set.
3. Remove the eggs with 2 metal spoons and serve on puree, vegetables or sauces.
