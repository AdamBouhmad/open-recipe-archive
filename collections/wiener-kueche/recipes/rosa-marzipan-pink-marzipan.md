## Ingredients

- Marzipan dough
- Sugar
- Cochineal
- Rose essence
- Rose water
- Fine, narrow, thread-like cut candied orange peel
- Diamond-shaped citron leaves
- Colored, sugar-preserved fruits

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as taught above for White Marzipan.
2. Add a little cochineal and a few drops of rose essence to the sugar glaze.
3. Use rose water instead of orange blossom water.
