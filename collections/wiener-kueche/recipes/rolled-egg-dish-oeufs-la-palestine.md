## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 4 ounces butter
- Preserved cherries
- Sugar
- Optional: vegetables, peas, ham, truffles, ragout, Parmesan cheese, other meats, snails, or mushrooms

## Directions

1. Beat 12 eggs with 1/2 teaspoon salt until well combined.
2. Heat 4 ounces butter in a sauté pan and pour in a serving spoon of the beaten eggs.
3. When the egg sheet becomes slightly firm, lift it from the pan with the aid of two serving spoons into a bowl.
4. Continue with remaining egg mixture, baking successive sheets and removing each to the bowl.
5. Immediately after baking, each sheet is topped with preserved cherries, the sheets are rolled lightly together, arranged in a bowl, sprinkled with sugar, and placed in the hot oven for a few minutes so the eggs warm through.
6. Alternatively, instead of cherries, the egg sheets can be filled with fine vegetables, green peas, finely chopped ham, truffles, fine ragout, or grated Parmesan cheese, finely chopped other meats, breaded and fried fish, roasted snails, or finely chopped mushrooms braised in butter.
