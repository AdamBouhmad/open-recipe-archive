## Ingredients

- 1 pound marzipan dough
- 4 loth chocolate
- hot water
- cinnamon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Take 4 loth chocolate for 1 pound marzipan dough.
2. Grind the chocolate with a little hot water over the fire into a paste.
3. Pour this paste over the marzipan dough.
4. Add cinnamon or vanilla.
5. Work the mass well together and spread it on cut wafer sheets.
6. This confection also requires only moderate oven heat.
