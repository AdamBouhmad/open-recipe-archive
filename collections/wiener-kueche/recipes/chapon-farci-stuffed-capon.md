## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams veal meat
- 14 decagrams bacon
- 3 whole eggs
- 6 decagrams crusts removed, softened in milk, well pressed rolls
- 4 tablespoons meat cream
- 4 cleaned, washed, finely diced anchovies
- 12 crayfish tails or 6 shelled oysters
- 8 decagrams finely sliced truffles
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the veal meat and bacon very finely with a sharp knife
2. Place in a mortar and pound with 3 whole eggs, 6 decagrams of crusts removed, softened in milk and well pressed rolls, and 4 tablespoons of meat cream until fine
3. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
4. Add 4 cleaned, washed, finely diced anchovies, 12 crayfish tails or 6 shelled oysters and 8 decagrams of finely sliced truffles
5. Salt and season to taste and mix well together
6. Truss the capon as for chickens, salt it, fill it with the above forcemeat, truss it, bind it with bacon and roast it on a spit or in the oven
7. When finished roasting, cut it into pieces of choice, reassemble it on a platter and garnish it with watercress and with lemon and orange wedges
8. Serve the capon with any salad desired and the resulting juices
