## Ingredients

- 6 deca udder
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 whole eggs
- 1/3 liter oysters
- some salt
- some nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir 6 deca udder, 3 egg yolks, and 4 whole eggs very finely
2. Mix in 1/3 liter oysters, some salt, and some nutmeg
3. Fill a form greased with butter and dusted with flour with this mixture
4. Cook in a water bath for 3/4 hour
