## Ingredients

- 24 deca peeled almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 16 deca powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 6 deca flour

## Directions

1. Put 24 deca peeled almonds into a mortar and pound them together with 2 egg whites into a dough. Meanwhile, stir 16 deca powdered sugar together with 8 egg yolks. Mix this well with the very finely pounded almonds and lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites together with 6 deca flour. Bake as described for 'Waffles from Batter' (No. 1648).
2. When the waffle is golden baked, remove it with 2 forks, place it on a plate and set this in a warm place. Continue baking waffles until the batter mixture is used up, then shortly before serving sprinkle them with sugar and serve with fruit sauce or any warm glaze.
3. The heating of the iron must not happen too strongly, but must be repeated frequently. When you have finished baking the waffles, the iron must not be washed but only cleaned with blotting paper.
