## Ingredients

- 28 Deka beaten butter
- 28 Deka orange sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 whole eggs
- 8 Deka blanched almonds
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1.5 knife tips cinnamon
- 6 hard-boiled and passed egg yolks
- 30 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Mix beaten butter with orange sugar
2. Add egg yolks and whole eggs one by one
3. Add ground almonds, cinnamon, and hard-boiled egg yolks
4. Stir for 1/2 hour
5. Lightly mix in flour
6. Brush round or square paper sheets finger-high with the mixture
7. Proceed as in the 'Viennese Torte' (no. 1486)
