## Ingredients

- One whole loin roast, skinned
- Several small rolls
- Fresh bacon
- Salt
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Sour cream
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Skin a whole loin roast well and cut it crosswise into small schnitzel about as thick as a knife-back
2. Peel the crust off several small rolls and cut them lengthwise into very thin slices
3. Take fresh bacon and cut it into fine schnitzel of the same thickness as the roll and meat schnitzel
4. Sprinkle the meat schnitzel with a little salt and arrange them properly on top of each other in this order: one schnitzel of meat, one schnitzel of bacon, and one schnitzel of roll, continuing until everything is well fitted together
5. Insert two small skewers through them and press firmly together so that it returns to the shape of a loin roast
6. Bind it with a roasting spit above and below and roast for 2 hours very slowly
7. Before roasting, brush with butter and vinegar, and lastly with sour cream
8. Before serving, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over it and seek through a couple of minutes with strong fire to give it a beautiful crust
9. When serving, place the drained sauce on the dish and lay the roast on it
