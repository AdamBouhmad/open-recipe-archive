## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 12½ dkg sugar
- 4 stiff egg whites
- 12½ dkg flour
- butter and flour for pan
- 10 dkg finely crushed cube sugar for caramel

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with sugar for 20 minutes
2. Fold in stiff egg whites and flour
3. Butter and flour a baking sheet, draw a circle from cake form
4. Spread one quarter of batter to form thin layer, bake light brown
5. Place cake form on baking sheet, cut dough layer round and loosen from sheet
6. Continue baking batter in layers this way
7. Spread filling on each layer, leaving one layer unfilled
8. Stack all but one filled layer, surround with filling, top with caramel-coated layer
9. For filling: Beat 8 dkg butter with 1 egg, mix with 8 dkg sugar and 1 dkg cocoa
10. For caramel: Melt and brown 10 dkg finely crushed cube sugar in small pot, stirring until no lumps remain, pour quickly on smooth side of prepared dough layer, smooth with buttered knife and let cool momentarily, mark individual cake portions with buttered knife and cut through when glaze is completely stiff
