## Ingredients

- 4 large or 8 small heads of cabbage
- salt
- water
- 16 deciliters hot fat
- finely chopped onion
- a small piece of crushed garlic
- 16 deciliters flour
- 1.25 liters broth
- pepper
- 1/3 teaspoon marjoram

## Directions

1. Cut the stems from the cabbage heads, remove the leaves, and split them along the core, then place in cold water
2. Wash the leaves well and place in boiling salted water, letting them cook until soft
3. Heat 16 deciliters of fat, add finely chopped onion and if desired a small piece of crushed garlic, letting this brown slightly
4. Add 16 deciliters of flour and let it brown again with frequent stirring
5. Pour in 1.25 liters of broth while stirring constantly and let it thicken
6. Add the drained cabbage, broken up with a wooden spoon, along with pepper and 1/3 teaspoon of marjoram
7. Stir everything together well and let it cook
8. If the cabbage is too thick, thin it with some broth
