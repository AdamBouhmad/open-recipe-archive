## Ingredients

- rolls according to number of persons (8 rolls or 32 Loffel), finely diced and mixed with 1/2 liter milk
- 16 Loffel butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 20 Loffel finely diced bacon
- salt
- cloth

## Directions

1. For 8 rolls (or 32 Loffel), finely diced and mixed with 1/2 liter milk, take 16 Loffel butter and beat it fluffy
2. Beat in 6 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
3. Mix well with the rolls and 20 Loffel finely diced bacon
4. Add salt and put it in a cloth dipped in cold water, well wrung out and spread over a colander
5. Tie the cloth tightly with twine or gather the dough together
6. Put it in boiling salted water and let it cook covered for 1 1/2 hours
7. When serving, remove the cloth from the knödel, place it on a dish and pour browned roll crumbs over it and serve immediately
