## Ingredients

- ¾ litre cold water
- 80 decilitres finest sugar

## Directions

1. Put ¾ litre cold water and 80 decilitres finest sugar into a very clean copper pot
2. Boil it for 10 minutes, removing the foam that forms
3. Pour the syrup into an earthen vessel and use it in a lukewarm state
