## Ingredients

- 4 deca butter
- 5 sprigs parsley
- equal amount tarragon leaves
- some white pepper
- some cayenne pepper
- 3 pieces anchovies
- 5 juniper berries
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 handful shallot
- 1 piece starfruit
- 1 piece pickled cucumber
- 3 raw egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons French mustard
- 1 tablespoon rose hip marmalade as desired

## Directions

1. Pound finely in a mortar: 4 deca butter, 5 sprigs parsley, tarragon leaves, white pepper, cayenne pepper, 3 pieces anchovies, 5 juniper berries, 1 tablespoon capers, 1 handful shallot, 1 piece starfruit, and 1 piece pickled cucumber
2. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
3. Mix the strained mixture with mayonnaise made from 3 raw egg yolks, 2 tablespoons French mustard, and optionally 1 tablespoon rose hip marmalade
4. Mix well together
