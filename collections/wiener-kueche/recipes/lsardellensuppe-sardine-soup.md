## Ingredients

- 10 deca butter
- flour
- 2 liters water
- some parsley root
- 10 deca cleaned finely chopped sardines
- some celery
- garlic
- finely chopped lemon peel
- 1.5 liters cream
- salt
- ginger

## Directions

1. Make a light roux with 10 deca butter and flour
2. Add 2 liters water and some parsley root to 10 deca cleaned finely chopped sardines, some celery and garlic, and finely chopped lemon peel
3. Let simmer well and pour the roux with this and 1.5 liters cream over, add salt and ginger
4. Let simmer well
5. Can be served passed or unpassed
