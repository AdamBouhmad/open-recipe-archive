## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- 32 decagrams sugar
- 32 decagrams finely ground and sifted almonds
- 1 1 decagrams finely diced citron
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold in 32 decagrams sugar, 32 decagrams finely ground and sifted almonds, and 1 1 decagrams finely diced citron, mixing lightly.
2. Fill a buttered cake form dusted with flour with this mixture and bake at very low heat for 1 1/2 hours in the oven.
