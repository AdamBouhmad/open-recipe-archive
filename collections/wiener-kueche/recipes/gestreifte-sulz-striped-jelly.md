## Ingredients

- Orange jelly mass
- Spinach juice or other coloring agents

## Directions

1. Prepare an orange jelly mass
2. Divide it into several equal parts and color them as desired with spinach juice to make green, with alkermes juice to make red, with violet flowers to make gray, etc.
3. These differently colored jelly masses are poured into the mold alternately, 2 fingers high, and each time the poured jelly is left to freeze before more jelly mass is poured and so continued until the mold is full
4. One must be careful to pour the same amount each time so the mold will be properly filled
