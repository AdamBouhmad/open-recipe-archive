## Ingredients

- Well-washed potatoes of equal size
- Cold water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place well-washed potatoes of equal size with their skins in a pot with cold water, which should not completely cover the potatoes.
2. If they are somewhat large, pour in cold water as soon as they begin to cook, and continue to do so repeatedly until they are cooked through after about half an hour or three-quarters of an hour.
3. Drain the water and set the pot back over the fire until all moisture has evaporated.
4. Add some salt during cooking.
