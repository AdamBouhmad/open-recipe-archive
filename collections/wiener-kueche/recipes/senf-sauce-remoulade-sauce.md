## Ingredients

- 1 handful tarragon leaves
- equal amount sorrel
- some parsley
- some burnet
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 5 tablespoons oil
- 2 tablespoons French mustard
- salt
- spice
- 1/8 liter oil
- 3 tablespoons tarragon vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil tarragon leaves, sorrel, parsley, and burnet in salted water until tender
2. Pound with 4 hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar
3. Dilute with 5 tablespoons oil
4. Add 2 tablespoons French mustard, necessary salt, and some spice
5. Pass through a fine sieve into a deep bowl
6. Place on ice
7. Gradually stir in 1/8 liter oil and 3 tablespoons tarragon vinegar, or juice from a whole lemon
