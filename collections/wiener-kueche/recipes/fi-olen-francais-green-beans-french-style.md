## Ingredients

- 5 dkg butter
- a little flour
- cooked green beans
- vegetable water or broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat 5 dkg butter in a saucepan, add a little flour, add the cooked and drained green beans, pour in some vegetable water or broth, season with salt, and let cook down for 10 minutes.
