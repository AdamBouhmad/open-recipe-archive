## Ingredients

- parsley
- sorrel
- borage, 10 sprigs each
- 4 pieces cleaned anchovies
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 5 pieces small pickled cucumbers
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 deca bread soaked in water and pressed out
- 1 spoon French mustard
- 4 tablespoons oil
- pinch of pepper
- salt
- vinegar or lemon juice as needed

## Directions

1. Boil parsley, sorrel, and borage in salted water
2. Put in cold water, drain, and press out well
3. Cut finely with 4 pieces cleaned anchovies, 1 tablespoon capers, and 5 pieces small pickled cucumbers
4. Pound together in a mortar with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, 4 deca bread soaked in water and pressed out, and 1 spoon French mustard
5. Pass through a sieve into a bowl
6. Place on ice
7. Stir in approximately 4 tablespoons oil, 1 pinch pepper, and some salt
8. If it is not sour enough, adjust with vinegar or lemon juice
