## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar in small pieces
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 oranges
- 1/4 liter rum
- 1/8 liter wheat grains
- 1 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Like Gesundheits-Punch mit Reis, but instead of rice, stir 1/8 liter wheat grains in 1 liter cold water
2. Boil slowly for 1/2 hour
3. Shock with some cold water
4. Allow the grains to settle
5. When the grain water becomes clear, strain it into the punch components
