## Ingredients

- A beautiful tenderloin roast
- Glass of Bordeaux wine or red wine
- Aspic
- Herb mayonnaise
- Green parsley or watercress

## Directions

1. Prepare and lard a beautiful tenderloin roast the same way as for braising.
2. Braise it in the same manner, with the difference that you add a glass of Bordeaux wine or red wine when braising.
3. Once the meat is completely tender, let it rest in the braising liquid and set in a cool place.
4. Once the fat begins to solidify, remove the roast from the pan, place it on a flat dish, cover it with a strip of white paper dipped in the braising liquid, place a long board on top, and weight it down so the tenderloin remains in a beautiful flat form.
5. Trim it neatly and arrange it in desired pieces decoratively on a dish.
6. Garnish the dish with diced aspic, spread herb mayonnaise on top of the meat, and decorate the dish neatly with green parsley or watercress.
7. Serve the remaining mayonnaise in a separate sauce boat.
