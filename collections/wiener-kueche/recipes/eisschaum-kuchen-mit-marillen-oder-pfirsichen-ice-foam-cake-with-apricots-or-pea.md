## Ingredients

- 30 deca caster sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- 24 deca flour
- halved and stoned apricots or peaches

## Directions

1. Beat 30 deca caster sugar with 10 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 10 egg whites with 24 deca flour
3. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Add halved and stoned apricots or peaches, arranged so that 3 and 3 pieces come one upon the other
5. Bake at low heat for 1 1/2 to 3/4 hours
6. Treat as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
