## Ingredients

- Half a pound of fine noodles
- One seidel of cream, boiled
- Equal amount of milk
- Half pound of butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 4 pounds of crushed sugar
- Zest of one lemon
- Noodles

## Directions

1. Stir the fine noodles into a seidel of boiled cream and equal amount of milk, let them swell and cool
2. Beat half pound of butter with 12 egg yolks until foamy
3. Stir in 4 pounds of crushed sugar, the zest of one lemon, and the noodles
4. Fill the mixture into a casserole brushed thickly with fresh butter
5. Cover with netting soaked an hour before
6. Let it bake slowly in the oven for 3 hours, pouring boiling water as needed so the water reaches the height of the mass
7. Serve with a cream sauce poured over it
