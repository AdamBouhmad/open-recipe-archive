## Ingredients

- bacon or lard
- goose liver
- salt
- Madeira or white wine
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. In a casserole, add some bacon or lard, lay a cleaned goose liver sprinkled with salt and basted with some lard on top.
2. Let this braise covered for 8 minutes.
3. Add approximately 1.5 liters of Madeira or white wine and let the liver braise for another 1.5 hours covered.
4. Place the liver on a board and cut finely with a carving knife.
5. Return to the ragout in the casserole and add salt, pepper, and wine as needed.
6. Braise once more and use this ragout, which can be made from goose liver leftovers, for small pies or puff pastry in pastry form and for various dishes.
7. A few spoonfuls of butter sauce can also be added.
