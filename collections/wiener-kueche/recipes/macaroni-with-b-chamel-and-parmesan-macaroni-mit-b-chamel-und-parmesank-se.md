## Ingredients

- macaroni prepared as 'Faded Macaroni' (No. 1206)
- 8 tablespoons of butter
- 8 tablespoons of flour
- 1 liter of milk
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- cold milk
- grated Parmesan cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and cool the macaroni as described in 'Faded Macaroni' (No. 1206)
2. Make a roux from 8 tablespoons of butter and 8 tablespoons of flour
3. Whisk in 1 liter of milk and cook well
4. Add 4 egg yolks mixed with some cold milk and mix with grated Parmesan cheese
5. Spread some sauce on the bottom of a buttered platter or casserole
6. Layer with a portion of the cooked but cold macaroni and continue alternating
7. Top with remaining sauce, sprinkle strongly with grated Parmesan cheese and drizzle with melted butter
8. Place the casserole in the oven for 1/4 hour
9. Remove from oven, transfer to a serving platter and serve hot
10. If using a casserole, turn out onto a platter before serving
