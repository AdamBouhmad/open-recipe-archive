## Ingredients

- 12 Deka shrimp butter
- 3 eggs and 3 egg yolks
- 1 Deka trimmed, finely diced rolls soaked in milk
- 1 1/8 liter milk cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 knife point of parsley
- 3 egg whites for snow
- 1/2 liter flour for dough
- finely chopped shells and meat of 15 crayfish
- 8 Deka bread crumbs
- 1/4 liter cooked green peas

## Directions

1. Whip 12 Deka shrimp butter until fluffy
2. Stir in eggs and yolks one by one
3. Add diced rolls in milk, milk cream, salt, pepper, and parsley
4. Fold in snow from 3 egg whites
5. Spread on finely stretched strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
6. Scatter crayfish shells and meat, bread crumbs, and green peas over it
7. Roll lightly and treat as lung strudel
