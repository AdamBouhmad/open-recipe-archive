## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, passed through a sieve
- 14 deca butter
- 14 deca powdered sugar
- 10 deca flour

## Directions

1. Pass the 6 hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve and mix finely with 14 deca butter
2. Place this mixture on a board and work it with 14 deca powdered sugar and 10 deca flour into a dough
3. Cover the dough and let it rest in a cold place
4. Once the dough has become stiff, cut it into nut-sized pieces
5. Treat further as specified in the Vanilla Puffs recipe
