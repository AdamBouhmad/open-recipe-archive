## Ingredients

- 15 dkg grated hazelnuts, nuts, or raw grated almonds
- 15 dkg sugar
- 1-2 egg whites
- Jam for topping

## Directions

1. Mix grated nuts, sugar, and egg white(s) in a bowl
2. Form small balls
3. Place on buttered sheet
4. Make an indent on top
5. Bake in oven at low heat
6. Top with jam and sprinkle with sugar
