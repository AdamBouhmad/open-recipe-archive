## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- water
- vinegar
- 1/2 onion
- 6 dkg fat
- salt
- sugar
- bread slices or macaroni

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and break the tomatoes
2. Stew with water, some vinegar, and 1/2 onion for 30 minutes
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Make a roux with 6 dkg fat and pour water or soup over it
5. Add the strained tomato mixture
6. Season to taste with salt, sugar, and vinegar
7. Boil well and serve with bread slices or macaroni cooked in salted water, cut small
