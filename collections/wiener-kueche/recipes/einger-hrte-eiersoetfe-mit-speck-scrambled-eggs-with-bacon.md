## Ingredients

- 12 deca grams bacon, finely diced
- 12 whole eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chives, finely cut

## Directions

1. Dice 12 deca grams bacon finely, put it in a skillet or egg dish pan and roast until it looks glassy.
2. Meanwhile, whisk 12 whole eggs well, season to taste with pepper and add to the roasted bacon and cook with constant stirring and lifting of the eggs as they begin to set.
3. They are then arranged as desired firm or more liquid with a spoon in a warm bowl and sprinkled with finely cut chives.
4. It can also be served in the egg dish pan, which is placed on a bowl.
