## Ingredients

- Eel
- Breadcrumbs
- Hot fat
- Mushrooms
- Onions
- Butter
- Madeira sauce
- Maitre d'hôtel butter

## Directions

1. Salt the eel, cut lengthwise in 3 places, sprinkle with breadcrumbs
2. Fry golden on both sides in hot fat on a pan
3. Arrange in a bowl and cover with prepared mushrooms
4. Finely chop mushrooms and onions, roast in butter, pour on Madeira sauce and bring to a boil
5. Pour this sauce over the fried eel, cover with large pieces of Maitre d'hôtel butter
6. Place in the oven for 1/2 hour, then serve
