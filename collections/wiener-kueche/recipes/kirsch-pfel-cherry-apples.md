## Ingredients

- Cherry apples
- Apple corer
- Lemon water (cold and hot)
- Sugar water (made from sugar syrup as described in recipe 1841)

## Directions

1. Core the apples with an apple corer
2. Peel them neatly
3. Place each apple immediately in cold lemon water
4. Then place all apples together in hot lemon water where they should cook for 5 minutes
5. Remove the apples and quickly immerse them three times in fresh cold water
6. Place them in the preserving jars which should already contain some sugar water
7. Pour the remaining sugar water over them and proceed as already explained
