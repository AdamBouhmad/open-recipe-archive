## Ingredients

- 1½ liters beans (fassolen)
- cold water
- 2 tablespoons oil
- salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sugar (if desired)

## Directions

1. Place the beans with cold water on the fire and let them cook until soft
2. Strain and pass them through a sieve
3. Stir in 2 tablespoons oil, some salt, 1 pinch of pepper, vinegar to taste and if desired 1 teaspoon sugar
