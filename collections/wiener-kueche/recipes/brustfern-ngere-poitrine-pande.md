## Ingredients

- Breast of beef
- Salt
- Pepper
- Marrow if desired
- Fat
- Bacon
- Root vegetables
- White wine
- Broth
- String

## Directions

1. Pound the breast of beef, wipe it with a cloth, remove the bones, and rub it thoroughly with salt, pepper, and marrow if desired.
2. Roll the thicker part inward and roll the meat against the thinner part, then bind with string.
3. Place a piece of fat, some bacon, and root vegetables in a casserole, lay the breast on top so that the end of the rolled meat comes to lie on top.
4. Cover the casserole and let the breast brown slightly, then baste it with as much white wine and broth as will cover the meat.
5. The meat must braise covered in the oven for approximately 2 hours.
6. Remove from the casserole and, after removing the string, serve. Pass and degrease the juices and serve them separately.
7. This roast can also be served cold and sliced.
