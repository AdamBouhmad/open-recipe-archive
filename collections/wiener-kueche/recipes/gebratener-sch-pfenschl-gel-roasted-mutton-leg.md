## Ingredients

- Mutton leg
- Bacon
- Mutton meat
- Green parsley
- Basil
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Aromatic herbs
- Ginger
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Gooseberry juice
- Later: grape juice
- Capers
- Wine
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin as thinly as possible
2. Lard the leg with bacon and roast as usual
3. Chop another piece of mutton meat finely
4. Mix it with green parsley, basil, thyme, marjoram, and other fragrant herbs as desired
5. Add ginger, a little nutmeg, butter, a couple spoonfuls of expressed gooseberry juice (or later expressed grape juice), capers, and wine
6. Let it cook together
7. Before serving, chop a couple of eggs finely, mix breadcrumbs into them, add to the broth, and let it boil once more
8. Baste the leg on the spit several times with this broth
9. Remove from spit, place on a plate, and pour the broth over it
