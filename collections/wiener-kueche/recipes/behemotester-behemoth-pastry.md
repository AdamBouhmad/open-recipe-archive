## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- some salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 3 decagrams pressed yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 54 decagrams flour
- melted butter for brushing
- finely ground sugar mixed with cinnamon
- finely cut almonds, raisins, and lingonberries

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, gradually stir in the sugar, salt, egg yolks, and whole egg
2. Add the pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk and the flour, stirring well together
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon, then cover and let it rise in a warm place for 1/2 hour
4. Place the dough on a flour-dusted board, roll it out to the thickness of a knife back, and cut into strips 3 fingers long and 1 finger wide
5. Sprinkle the strips with finely ground sugar mixed with cinnamon and finely cut almonds, raisins, and lingonberries, then roll up together
6. Lay the rolls side by side in a butter-greased casserole so that the end of the rolled dough faces upward, and brush with melted butter where rolls touch
7. Cover with a cloth and let rise in a warm place until the dough has risen again
8. Brush with melted butter and bake 3/4 to 1 hour
9. Remove the casserole, turn it out, separate the pastries from each other, arrange on a plate, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
