## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- flour equal to the volume of the eggs
- 1 liter boiling milk or cream
- 12 tablespoons sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 tablets grated chocolate or 3 tablespoons strong black coffee (optional)
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying
- zest of oranges or lemons (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare a noodle dough from 2 whole eggs and as much flour as they will hold
2. Treat it as described in the noodle soup recipe
3. Cut the dough into knife-back thick, equally wide, and half-finger long noodles
4. Add them to 1 liter of boiling milk or cream and cook into a thick mush, stirring frequently
5. Add 12 tablespoons sugar and 1 pinch salt during cooking
6. Pour the cooked mass into a deep bowl
7. After it has cooled somewhat, gradually stir in 6 egg yolks and optionally 2 tablets of grated chocolate or 3 tablespoons of strong black coffee
8. Continue treatment as described for Rice-Croquettes (No. 1136)
9. Optionally, rub the required sugar with orange or lemon zest (using only the outer yellow skin) to flavor the dish
