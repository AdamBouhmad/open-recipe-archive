## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams flour
- 12 decagrams spice sugar (vanilla, orange, or lemon)
- 1 liter milk
- 6 egg yolks
- butter for greasing mold and dusting

## Directions

1. Mix flour and spice sugar in a deep bowl
2. Gradually stir in 1 liter milk and 6 egg yolks
3. Pour into a buttered mold dusted with flour
4. Cook in steam until the mass is completely firm, which will take approximately 1/2 to 3/4 hour
5. Turn out onto a flour-dusted board and let the mold rest briefly over it
6. When the cream is completely cooled, cut into desired pieces (finger-long and finger-wide strips, square pieces, or use a round cutter)
7. Wrap each piece in very thin, finest puff pastry
8. Brush with beaten eggs
9. Sprinkle with bread crumbs as desired
10. Fry in hot fat
11. Sprinkle with desired spice sugar when serving
