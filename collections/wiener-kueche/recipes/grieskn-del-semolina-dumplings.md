## Ingredients

- 1 measure cream
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound semolina
- 12 egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Make 1 measure of cream with 1/4 pound butter boiling
2. Then add 1/2 pound semolina and stir on the fire until it detaches from the pan
3. When it has cooled sufficiently, gradually work in 12 egg yolks with a little salt
