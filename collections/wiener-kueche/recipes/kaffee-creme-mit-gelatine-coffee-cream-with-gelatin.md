## Ingredients

- 1.25 liters strong black coffee essence
- 16 Deca sugar
- 0.5 liters water
- 2 Deca gelatin
- 0.5 liters heavy cream

## Directions

1. Add 16 Deca sugar to 1.25 liters strong black coffee essence and heat until very hot, but do not boil.
2. Dissolve 2 Deca gelatin in 0.5 liters water, then pour the dissolved and steaming hot gelatin (but not boiling) into the sweetened hot coffee and stir in a deep bowl until lukewarm.
3. Fold in 0.5 liters heavy cream to this mixture.
4. Immediately transfer into a mold brushed with fine oil, which has been buried in ice 1 hour earlier.
5. Proceed as directed for Rice with Vanilla Cream (No. 1689).
6. Note: Work very quickly when preparing this cream. If ice is not available, very cold water may be used. For 2 Deca gelatin, the total liquid should be 1 liter.
