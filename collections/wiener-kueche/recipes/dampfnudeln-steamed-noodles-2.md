## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- 6 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 spoonfuls sugar
- yeast
- warm milk
- 1 liter milk
- 5 dkg almonds, softened and chopped
- Canary milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Warm 25 dkg flour in a mixing bowl, rub in 6 dkg butter
2. Add 3 egg yolks, salt, 3 spoonfuls sugar, the risen yeast starter, and lukewarm milk as needed
3. Beat the dough until it pulls away from the spoon
4. Let it rise near the stove
5. Place it on a floured board, form round noodles, dip them in melted butter
6. Lay them in a baking pan in which 1 liter milk has been warmed
7. Sprinkle with 5 dkg softened, chopped almonds, possibly add another layer of noodles, sprinkle with almonds again
8. Let them rise again, bake at moderate heat
9. Arrange on a round platter, pour Canary milk over them and serve sprinkled with sugar
