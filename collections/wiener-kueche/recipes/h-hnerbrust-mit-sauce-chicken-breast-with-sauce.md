## Ingredients

- Poultry
- Juniper berries
- Lard
- Wine
- Broth
- Black bread
- Olives
- Cream
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Lay the dressed and browned poultry on juniper berries
2. Cover with lard and braise covered briefly
3. Pour wine and broth over so the liquid covers the poultry
4. Add black bread cut into strips and braise until tender
5. Remove the poultry and degrease
6. Pass the sauce through a sieve
7. Add peeled olives and cream to the sauce
8. Adjust seasoning with lemon juice if needed
9. Serve the sauce with the poultry
