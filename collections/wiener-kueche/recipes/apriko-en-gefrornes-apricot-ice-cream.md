## Ingredients

- Apricots (Marillen), same quantity as for pear ice cream
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Take as many apricots as for pear ice cream
2. When they are well pressed through, stir in three quarter pound clarified sugar until everything is well mixed
3. Pour into the freezing pan
