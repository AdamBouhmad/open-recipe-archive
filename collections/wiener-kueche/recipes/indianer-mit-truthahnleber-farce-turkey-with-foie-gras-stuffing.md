## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned turkey
- Liver stuffing
- Truffles, sliced into leaves

## Directions

1. Except that the turkey is filled with liver stuffing instead of bread stuffing, the treatment is otherwise exactly the same as described for 'gefüllter Indianer' (stuffed turkey).
2. Thinly sliced truffles are added.
