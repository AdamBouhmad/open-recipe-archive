## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg beef (ox tail)
- 6 h bones
- 4 h liver
- 4 h soup greens (parsley root, yellow turnip, celery, leek)
- ½ kg tomatoes
- 25 dkg rice
- Salt
- Vinegar
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 pepper and spice grains
- 2 cloves
- ½ onion
- 4 dkg fat for tomato sauce
- Sugar
- Red wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the broth: Beat the meat, rinse it thoroughly along with the bones and liver in cold water. Place all in cold water to start. If desiring good, juicy meat, place it in boiling water to quickly close the pores so the protein does not cook out. For 1 kg meat take 2½-3 liters water. If placing meat in cold water, the broth will be better. Do not skim off the foam that forms when boiling—this is the protein, the nutrient. After boiling, add 1-2 spoonfuls of salt, after a few minutes add the cleaned, washed and cut greens, a piece of cabbage, 1 onion ring roasted in a small pan in some fat until dark yellow, several mushrooms (fresh or dried), 5 spice grains, some ground pepper and caraway. Let the broth cook slowly for 2-2½ hours. It should only boil on one side and must not be stirred. To clarify it, add some clean eggshells during cooking and if it boils too vigorously, add a spoonful of cold water. Strain it through a fine sieve or napkin.
2. Prepare the tomato sauce: Wash the tomatoes, remove the stems, divide them, and stew for 30 minutes with very little water, salt, vinegar, 1 bay leaf, 5 pepper and spice grains, 2 cloves, and ½ onion in a pot. Pass them through a sieve, add to a light roux made from 4 dkg fat, pour on broth or water, cook for 15-20 minutes, sweeten to taste and optionally sour with red wine.
3. Prepare the rice: Select the rice carefully and place in a pot with cold water to cook.
