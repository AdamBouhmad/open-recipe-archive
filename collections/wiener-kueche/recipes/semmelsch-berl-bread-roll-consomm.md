## Ingredients

- 12 Deca butter
- 16 Deca peeled, diced bread rolls soaked in milk
- 8 egg yolks
- Salt
- A knife-point of finely chopped parsley
- Snow from 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deca butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 16 Deca peeled, diced bread rolls soaked in milk along with 8 egg yolks, some salt, and a knife-point of finely chopped parsley
3. Mix in the snow from 6 eggs
4. Fill and bake as with Kaiserschöberl
