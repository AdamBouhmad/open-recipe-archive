## Ingredients

- ¼ liter green peas
- ½ kg cabbage sprouts
- 1 small head cauliflower
- 1 bunch carrots
- some green beans (optional)
- 3 mushrooms
- ¾ liter milk
- 5 dkg butter
- 4 dkg flour
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the peas, cleaned and washed cabbage sprouts, cauliflower florets, and diced carrots and beans in salted water for 25-30 minutes.
2. Drain and let drip dry.
3. In a pot, make a roux with 5 dkg hot butter and 4 dkg flour until light golden, pour in the milk, stir continuously until thick, and let cool.
4. Mix the cooled sauce with 3 egg yolks, salt, the vegetables, thinly sliced mushrooms sautéed in butter, and 3 beaten egg whites.
5. Fill into a buttered soufflé dish, bake at moderately high heat for 30-45 minutes.
6. Serve immediately in the baking dish.
