## Ingredients

- 12 deciliters fat
- 10 deciliters bacon
- 1 head onion finely chopped
- 1 heaped liter sauerkraut
- broth
- 1/8 liter butter sauce
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- salt
- vinegar or white wine

## Directions

1. Let 10 deciliters bacon brown in 12 deciliters fat and let 1 finely chopped head of onion brown with it
2. Add 1 heaped liter sauerkraut to the browned mixture and let it braise uncovered for 10 minutes
3. Pour broth over the sauerkraut so it just covers it
4. Let it braise gently covered, stirring occasionally
5. When done and braised, add 1/8 liter butter sauce, 1 tablespoon sugar, some salt and if necessary some broth and vinegar
6. Instead of vinegar, white wine can be used
7. Stir together and let it braise well
8. The sauerkraut should be pleasantly sweet and sour
