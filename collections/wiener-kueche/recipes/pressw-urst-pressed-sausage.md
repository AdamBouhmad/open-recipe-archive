## Ingredients

- 1 Pfund good ham
- 1 Pfund bacon
- 2 Pfund good fatty pork
- pork ears
- several onions
- a couple pieces of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- basil
- thyme
- a pig's stomach
- half vinegar and half water
- bay leaves
- cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the 1 pound ham, 1 pound bacon, 2 pounds pork, and pork ears uncooked into small diced and oblong pieces
2. Add several finely cut onions and a couple pieces of finely cut garlic
3. Season well with salt and pepper, and mix well with basil and thyme
4. Fill this mixture into a pig's stomach and tie it well on both sides
5. Place it in a casserole or pot, add half vinegar and half water so that it completely covers the stomach
6. Add several bay leaves and cloves to the liquid
7. Let it cook for a full 2 hours in this broth
8. Remove the stomach and place it between two bowls so it is compressed somewhat
9. Let the broth congeal into jelly, and when the stomach has cooled well, slice it and pour the jelly over it
