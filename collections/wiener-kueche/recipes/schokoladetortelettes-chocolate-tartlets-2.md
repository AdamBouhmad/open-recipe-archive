## Ingredients

- 19 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 7 dkg sugar
- 10 dkg grated chocolate
- milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 spoonful flour
- 2 spoonfuls sugar
- 7 dkg grated chocolate for filling

## Directions

1. Make a dough of 19 dkg flour, 14 dkg butter, 1 egg yolk, 7 dkg sugar, and 10 dkg grated chocolate on the board
2. Roll out and cut into rounds
3. Bake on a lightly buttered baking sheet in a moderately hot oven
4. For the filling: beat ½ milk, 2 egg yolks, 1 spoonful flour, 2 spoonfuls sugar, and 7 dkg grated chocolate over steam in a snow basin until thick
5. Fill the baked tartlets and optionally ice or sprinkle with sugar
