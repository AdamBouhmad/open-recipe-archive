## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 kilo dried plums, half-soft cooked and finely cut
- 16 decagrams blanched, finely ground almonds
- 8 decagrams sugar
- 8 decagrams crusts, soaked in water and well pressed out rolls
- finely cut lemon zest
- wafer cookies
- water
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Beat 8 decagrams of butter until fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 4 egg yolks
3. Mix in 1/4 kilo of dried, half-soft cooked, finely cut plums
4. Add 16 decagrams of blanched, finely ground almonds
5. Add 8 decagrams sugar
6. Add 8 decagrams of crusts that have been soaked in water and well pressed out
7. Add finely cut lemon zest
8. Mix everything well together
9. Form finger-thick strips on a flour-dusted board
10. Wrap these in wafers that have been moistened with water
11. Dip in beaten eggs
12. Roll in bread crumbs
13. Fry in very hot fat
