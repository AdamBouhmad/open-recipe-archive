## Ingredients

- pigeons
- butter
- mushrooms
- green parsley
- beef broth
- wine
- mace

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeons into four pieces, let them roast in hot butter, add some mushrooms and green parsley, pour beef broth and wine over them, and let everything come to a boil together.
2. Finally, add some mace and a small piece of butter.
