## Ingredients

- Fried or braised fish, cut into small pieces
- Piquant sauce with wine, especially Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Prepare these like the 'Poultry-Pasteten' (No. 1075), with the difference that they are filled, instead of with hache, with hard-boiled eggs or with any fried or braised fish cut into small pieces, to which one adds several spoonfuls of piquant sauce with wine, especially Madeira wine.
