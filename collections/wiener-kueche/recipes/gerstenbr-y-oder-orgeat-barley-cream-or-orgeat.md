## Ingredients

- peeled barley, soaked and rubbed by hand, cooked for seven or eight hours
- sweet almonds, blanched
- sugar
- silver or earthen dish
- glowing coals
- milk (optional)

## Directions

1. Take the prepared barley and place it in a stone mortar
2. Rub it together with blanched sweet almonds and press it through
3. Add sugar according to taste
4. Place the mixture in a silver or earthen dish on glowing coals and let it cook very gently until finished
5. Some prefer it thinner, others thicker; if you cook the pressed mixture again it becomes thicker and more nourishing; if you do not cook it, it remains thinner and is preferred by many, though it nourishes less
6. It may be taken 2 to 3 or 4 times daily depending on whether it is thicker or thinner
7. Sometimes milk is added toward the end of cooking, which makes it more pleasant, but is not suitable for those who have a fever
