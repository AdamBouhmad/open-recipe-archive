## Ingredients

- 1 large or 2 small heads of cabbage
- approximately 1 1/4 liters vinegar
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Cut 1 large or 2 small heads of cabbage finely into noodles
2. Pour approximately 1 1/4 liters vinegar and 1 liter water over it, add 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
