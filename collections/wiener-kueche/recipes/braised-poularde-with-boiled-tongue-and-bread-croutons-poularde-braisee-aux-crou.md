## Ingredients

- poularde
- finely cut bacon
- cooked ox tongue
- bread croutons

## Directions

1. Clean and truss a plucked poularde, season it, and lard it with finely cut bacon, and treat it like the braised poularde
2. When done, remove the poularde, cut the twine, pull it out, and cut the poularde into desired pieces
3. Arrange pieces of beef tongue two fingers wide and one finger long alternately with the poularde pieces in a bowl
4. Make a jus sauce from the juice in which the poularde was braised and serve in a sauce boat
5. Border the bowl with imitated woodcock roe
