## Ingredients

- 4 ounces truffles, cut into leaves or noodle shapes
- strained Butter-Sauce
- Madeira wine
- dissolved soup glace

## Directions

1. Add truffles cut into leaves or noodle shapes to the strained Butter-Sauce
2. Cover and braise with several spoonfuls of Madeira wine and, if available, some dissolved soup glace
