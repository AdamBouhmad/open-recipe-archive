## Ingredients

- 16 ounces butter
- 1/2 liter fresh peas, thoroughly cleaned and washed
- some parsley
- 1 piece butter
- 1 whole onion
- a small piece of raw, lean ham
- 1/2 liter beef soup
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Braise 1/2 liter of fresh peas in 16 ounces butter with some parsley, 1 piece butter, 1 whole onion, a small piece of raw lean ham and 1/2 liter beef soup
2. When the peas are soft, remove the onion and ham
3. Stir the peas well and pass through a sieve
4. Thin this purée with the necessary beef soup and let it come to a boil before serving
5. Season with salt and if desired add a knife-tip of white pepper
6. Alternatively, instead of fresh peas, 1/4 liter of dried peas can be used, in which case use only 2 ounces of sugar instead of 4
