## Ingredients

- 6 dkg butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 12½ dkg sugar
- 37 dkg flour
- Lemon zest
- ½ packet Oetker baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat butter in a bowl for a few minutes
2. Mix in 2 whole eggs, sugar
3. Later add flour, lemon zest, and baking powder
4. Place dough on floured board and knead
5. Roll out in portions
6. Cut with cake cutter
7. Place on lightly buttered sheet
8. Bake at moderate heat until pale yellow
9. If no cake cutter available, use other cutters and prick with a needle so they rise better
