## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 8 dkg grated chocolate
- 7 egg whites, beaten stiff
- 14 dkg grated raw almonds
- 15 dkg butter
- 15 dkg vanilla sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 8-10 dkg grated chocolate for the cream
- 5 dkg dark roasted, coarsely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 7 egg yolks for 30 minutes, stir in 8 dkg grated chocolate and fold in 7 beaten egg whites and 14 dkg grated raw almonds.
2. Fill the mixture into the form and bake slowly.
3. The next day, cut through the cake, fill with cream, put together, brush inside and around with cream, and sprinkle with 5 dkg dark roasted, coarsely chopped almonds.
4. For the cream: beat 15 dkg butter, stir in 15 dkg vanilla sugar, 1 whole egg, and 8-10 dkg grated chocolate.
