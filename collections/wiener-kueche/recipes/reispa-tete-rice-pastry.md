## Ingredients

- crayfish butter
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- rice cooked in milk
- veal sweetbreads
- crayfish tails
- asparagus
- mushrooms
- crayfish butter
- beef broth
- egg yolk
- egg white

## Directions

1. Beat a piece of crayfish butter and mix in 4 egg yolks
2. Add the beaten snow (egg whites) from the 4 eggs and a quarter pound of rice cooked thick in milk and cooled
3. Stir everything together well
4. Make a ragout from veal sweetbreads, crayfish tails, asparagus, and mushrooms, cutting everything into dice
5. Let it cook well in warm crayfish butter
6. Pour in 2 egg yolks mixed with cold beef broth
7. Add milk cream and butter
8. Continue until the casserole is full
9. Make a lid and brush with egg white
10. Let it bake slowly in the oven
