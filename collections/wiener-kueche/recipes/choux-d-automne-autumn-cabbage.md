## Ingredients

- 1 large or 2 small heads of cabbage
- approximately 1/4 liter vinegar
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 12 decagrams butter
- 1 finely cut onion
- 10 decagrams flour
- white beef broth
- cabbage broth
- 1/4 liter cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage finely into noodle-like strips.
2. Cook with approximately 1/4 liter vinegar, 1 liter water, 1 teaspoon caraway seeds and the same amount of salt until soft.
3. Meanwhile, make a light golden roux from 12 decagrams butter, 1 finely cut onion and 10 decagrams flour.
4. Pour the roux with a ladle of white beef broth and some of the cabbage broth.
5. Add the strained cabbage to the roux and stir well together.
6. If desired, add 1/4 liter cream.
7. If too thick, thin with the cabbage broth.
