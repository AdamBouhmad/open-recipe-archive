## Ingredients

- 32 deca peeled almonds
- 1/8 liter water
- 32 deca finely sifted sugar
- Juice of one lemon
- 32 deca finely sifted sugar (second addition)
- 1 tablespoon finely cut citron
- 1 tablespoon finely cut candied orange peel
- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 1 heaped tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Pound almonds with water to a paste
2. Stir in first amount of sugar and lemon juice over heat until dough is dry and releases from the spoon
3. Pound dough finely again in mortar
4. Place on plate and let rest in cool place
5. Transfer to bowl and mix with second amount of sugar, citron, and candied orange peel
6. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites and flour
7. Knead well on board
8. Roll out to knife-back thickness
9. Cut into desired figures
10. Place on dry baking sheet
11. Brush with beaten eggs
12. Dust with sugar
13. Bake golden in well-heated oven
