## Ingredients

- 16 h root vegetables
- 1 sliced bread roll (Schneidſemmel)
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 piece black bread crust
- vinegar
- hot water
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and cut the root vegetables into thin slices
2. Roast 1 onion, the root vegetables, 5 peppercorns and spice berries, 1 bay leaf and a piece of black bread crust in a little fat until dark
3. Splash with vinegar, pour on hot water and stew gently for 1½ hours until soft
4. Pass through a sieve, place in a casserole, pour on beef broth
5. Season if needed with more vinegar and cook through
6. Serve with bread slices
7. If too thin, add a light roux and let cook well
