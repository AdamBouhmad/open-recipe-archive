## Ingredients

- Gudgeon
- Water
- Salt
- Potatoes
- Hot butter
- Sauce à la Hollandaise or other sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the fins from all around, pull off the skin from the gills toward the head on both sides, cut off the head with the entrails
2. Wash the fish and salt it
3. Place the cleaned fish in boiling salted water, bring to a boil once, then arrange in a bowl
4. Garnish with potatoes and serve with hot butter, Sauce à la Hollandaise or any desired sauce
