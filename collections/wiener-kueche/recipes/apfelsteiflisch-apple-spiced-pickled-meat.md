## Ingredients

- 1 piece of meat from the brisket (beef tongue part), about 4 kilos
- about 5 tablespoons salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 piece of onion cut in leaf-like pieces
- some garlic
- a few bunches of mugwort
- wintergreen and basil if desired
- some rosemary
- 5 juniper berries
- various spices
- salt water
- about 3 tablespoons saltpeter (if using within 3 days)
- water
- 1/2 liter wine
- 1/4 liter vinegar
- soup or water
- 16 decagrams bacon
- yellow turnips and celery cut in leaf-like pieces
- 1 piece of black bread
- 1/2 liter cream
- capers
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Grind all spices very finely.
2. Rub the meat thoroughly with the spice mixture.
3. Place in an earthen vessel, cover with a wooden lid, and weight down with a stone.
4. Store in a cool place for 14 days.
5. If using within 3 days, add about 3 tablespoons of saltpeter.
6. After pickling, boil the meat in water and serve with any piquant sauce or French mustard.
7. Alternatively, braise it covered with 1/2 liter wine, 1/4 liter vinegar, and some soup or water (enough to come up over the meat), 16 decagrams of bacon, some yellow turnips and celery cut in leaf-like pieces.
8. When half-braised, add 1 piece of black bread.
9. When the meat is tender, remove it, skim fat from the sauce, pass through a sieve, return the meat to the sauce, and pour 1/2 liter of cream with some capers over it.
10. Let braise for another 1/2 hour.
11. When serving, garnish with green parsley and capers.
