## Ingredients

- Kaiser pears (not too soft)
- Lemon juice
- Lemon water (cold and hot)
- Sugar water (made from sugar syrup as described in recipe 1841)
- Cinnamon stick (optional, to replace missing stems)

## Directions

1. Cut the pears in half lengthwise through the stem
2. Remove the core with an apple corer or knife
3. Peel the pears and rub with lemon juice
4. First place in cold lemon water, then in boiling lemon water
5. When they begin to soften, quickly immerse them three times in fresh cold water
6. Place in the already prepared preserving jars containing some sugar water
7. Fill with the remaining sugar water and treat as the Meusel-Cateller or Kelsen pears (recipe 1842)
8. If any pears are missing stems when finishing as compote, they may be replaced with a piece of cinnamon stick as desired
