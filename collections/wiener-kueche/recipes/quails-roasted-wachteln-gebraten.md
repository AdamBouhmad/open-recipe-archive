## Ingredients

- quails
- vinegar
- wine
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Clean the quails by plucking, washing, and salting them
2. Let them sit in salt for a while
3. Add half vinegar and an equal amount of wine
4. Add cinnamon and some cloves
5. Let them lie in the brine overnight
6. Roast them well in the juices
7. A small sauce can also be made from the brine
