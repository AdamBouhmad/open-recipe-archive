## Ingredients

- beef tongue (steamed, cooked, smoked or poached)
- grated horseradish
- scraped horseradish
- beet juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. One can use a steamed, cooked, smoked or poached tongue
2. Cut the tongue weakly into finger-thick slices and arrange in a circle on a plate
3. Place some grated horseradish in the center
4. Place small piles of scraped horseradish around the edge
5. Color 1 teaspoon horseradish in beet juice and place a small amount of this red-colored horseradish on each pile of white horseradish around the plate
6. Garnish the plate with finely plucked green parsley
7. Ensure the tongue comes to table quite hot
8. Serve with vinegar or vinegar horseradish
