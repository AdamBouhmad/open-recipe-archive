## Ingredients

- 60 dkg haddock
- 8 dkg root vegetables
- 1/2 kg small round potatoes
- 1 onion
- 8 peppercorns and spice berries
- much vinegar
- salt
- milk water
- brown roasted butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare a brine consisting of root vegetables, 1 onion, 8 peppercorns and spice berries, much vinegar and salt, and boil for 30 minutes
2. Remove the skin from the fish, clean it, wash it and place in milk water
3. Place it in the boiling brine, pull back, and let it poach for 10-15 minutes depending on size
4. Transfer it to a platter, pour brown roasted butter over it and serve warm with small potatoes which have been tossed in butter and parsley
