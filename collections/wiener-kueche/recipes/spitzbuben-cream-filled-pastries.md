## Ingredients

- 21 ounces butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 30 ounces flour
- 8 whole eggs
- eggs for brushing
- flour for dusting
- Vanilla chocolate or cream for filling

## Directions

1. Add salt to the butter and flour, stirring constantly over heat for 30 minutes until the dough is dry and separates from the pan
2. Empty the dough into a deep bowl and after complete cooling, stir in the 8 eggs gradually
3. Using a tablespoon, remove dough pieces of desired size and spread them on a buttered and floured baking sheet at 3-finger width intervals
4. Brush with beaten eggs and bake in a fairly hot oven until golden
5. Remove with a thin knife, cut them open at the side to the middle, and fill as desired with vanilla chocolate or cream
6. Arrange elevated on a dish and serve warm or cold
7. Alternatively, Spitzbuben can be made in very hot lard instead of on the sheet: place dough pieces with a tablespoon into hot lard, but not too many at once as they double in size during frying
8. When brown on both sides with occasional shaking of the pan, place briefly on blotting paper, arrange elevated on a dish, sprinkle with sugar, and serve with cream of choice
