## Ingredients

- Six pounds beef from the leg, taken while still warm from the ox
- Six Seidel of water
- Six measures of salt
- Cleaned beef intestines

## Directions

1. Remove skin and all sinews from the warm beef leg
2. Beat it very fine with a wooden mallet
3. While beating, add six Seidel of water and salt with six measures of salt
4. Let stand overnight
5. The next day, salt it a bit more
6. Fill it firmly into cleaned beef intestines
