## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 3 lemons
- 7 tablespoons isinglass in 1 liter water
- 40 tablespoons clarified sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- white wine
- 1 small piece orange peel

## Directions

1. Press the juice from 4 oranges and 3 lemons through cloth, ensuring it is completely clear.
2. Pour this juice into 1/4 liter measure, filling the remaining space with white wine.
3. Cook 7 tablespoons isinglass in 1 liter water and reduce to 1/4 liter liquid as previously described.
4. Pour 40 tablespoons clarified sugar (prepared according to No. 1542 in 1/2 liter water) to which a finger-length piece of orange peel has been added during clarification, to the isinglass liquid.
5. Let the sugar and isinglass together simmer approximately 10 minutes and strain through fine, tightly woven linen cloth.
