## Ingredients

- 1 bowl-shaped pastry case of hard dough
- Béchamel sauce made with butter
- 2 or more eggs
- Large macaroni, thoroughly cooked and strained
- 2 spoonfuls of good butter
- A knife-tip of cinnamon
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make a bowl-shaped pastry case from hard dough as described previously
2. Prepare a Béchamel sauce with butter and add a couple more eggs than usual
3. Cook the large macaroni thoroughly, strain it well
4. Stir the macaroni into the Béchamel sauce along with a couple spoonfuls of good butter, a knife-tip of cinnamon, and a little salt
5. Fill the open pastry case with this mixture
6. Bake uncovered in a not-too-hot oven for one hour
7. Serve immediately to the table
