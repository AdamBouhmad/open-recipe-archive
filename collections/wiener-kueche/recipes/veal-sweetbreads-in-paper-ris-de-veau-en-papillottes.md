## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Broth
- Mushrooms
- Paper
- Fine herbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal sweetbreads as indicated for Escalopes, cooking tender in white broth with mushrooms.
2. After completely cooling, cut into half-finger-thick slices.
3. Wrap these like Veal Cutlets with fine herbs in paper and grill them like Cutlets over a grate.
