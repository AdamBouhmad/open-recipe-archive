## Ingredients

- half Pfund marrow
- finely cut onions
- shallots
- half Maß good pork or calf blood
- half Maß sweet cream
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- narrow casings
- warm water
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of marrow into small dice and let it brown in a pot with finely cut onions and shallots
2. Add to half a Maß of good pork or calf blood half a Maß of sweet cream
3. Mix together with salt, pepper, and marjoram
4. Fill into narrow casings and let it simmer in warm water for a while until the blood coagulates
5. Before use, turn them in butter and roast them on the grate
6. They serve as well for side dish as for vegetables
