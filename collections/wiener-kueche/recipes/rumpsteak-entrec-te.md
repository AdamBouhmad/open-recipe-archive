## Ingredients

- Rump steak cut finger-thick
- Pepper and salt
- Hot lard for frying
- Cream and gravy or soup
- Maître d'hôtel butter or parsley or tarragon butter
- Optional: lemon slices
- Optional: fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Lightly beat finger-thick cut rump steaks so they do not become too wide but retain their height
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
3. Quickly fry on both sides in hot lard
4. Place on a serving spoon, remove fat from the drippings and add some cream and gravy or soup, bring to a boil and pour to the side of the rump steak
5. Place a piece of Maître d'hôtel butter or parsley or tarragon butter in the middle of each piece of meat
6. Alternatively, you can add sliced lemon
7. Rump steak can also be served with the defatted own sauce and with fried potatoes
