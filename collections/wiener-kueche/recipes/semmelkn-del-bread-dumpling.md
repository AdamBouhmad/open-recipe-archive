## Ingredients

- Bread rolls, cut into cubes
- 6 dkg fat
- 1/4 finely chopped onion
- Parsley
- 2 dl milk
- 1 egg
- Salt
- 3-5 tablespoons flour
- Bread crumbs roasted in fat

## Directions

1. Cut bread rolls into cubes and place in a bowl
2. Toast the finely chopped onion and parsley in 6 dkg hot fat
3. Pour over the bread and let stand a while
4. Beat together 2 dl milk, 1 egg, and salt
5. Pour over the bread
6. Mix lightly with 3-5 tablespoons flour
7. Form dumplings
8. Place in a casserole with boiling salt water
9. Cook slowly for 8-10 minutes
10. Transfer to a round platter
11. Pour roasted bread crumbs in fat over them
