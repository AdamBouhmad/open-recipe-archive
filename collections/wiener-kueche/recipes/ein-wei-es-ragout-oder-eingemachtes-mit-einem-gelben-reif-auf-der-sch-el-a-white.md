## Ingredients

- six to eight pieces of beautiful dark yellow turnips
- good meat broth
- butter
- bread crumbs
- four egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- stewed veal or lamb meat
- white butter sauce
- carrots or cauliflower

## Directions

1. Clean and wash six to eight pieces of beautiful dark yellow turnips
2. Cook them in good meat broth until completely soft
3. Strain the broth and press the yellow turnips through a sieve
4. Take the pressed turnips and blend them with butter and a handful of bread crumbs
5. Mix in four egg yolks, a little salt and nutmeg
6. Form a ring from this mixture on a buttered platter
7. In the middle place a stewed veal or lamb meat ragout with white butter sauce
8. You can also add some carrots or cauliflower
9. The stew can also be made with chicken
10. Leave it for half an hour in a warm oven or tube and serve
