## Ingredients

- Beautiful herrings
- Herring kidney fat
- Bacon
- Shallots
- Parsley
- Lemon peel
- Egg yolks
- Grated bread
- Dried herbs
- Butter
- White paper

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings and separate them.
2. Remove all bones.
3. Chop the meat of some herrings very finely with some kidney fat.
4. Scrape bacon into the mixture.
5. Chop shallots, parsley, and lemon peel very finely.
6. Mix everything together with a couple of egg yolks, grated bread, and dried herbs.
7. Pound together well in a mortar.
8. Spread the farce onto the side of each herring where the bones were removed, so it looks like a whole herring again (the head must be cut off).
9. Cut white strong paper into approximately the shape of the herring, only slightly longer and wider.
10. Spread butter on the paper, place the herring on it, and wrap the edges firmly around it.
11. Fry on a grill for about a quarter hour over brisk heat.
12. Turn very frequently.
