## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- milk
- butter
- plums

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough made with milk and let it rest for 3/4 hour
2. On a flour-dusted board, roll out the dough very thin
3. Cut into squares corresponding in size to plums
4. Wipe fresh plums with a cloth
5. Place one plum on each square of dough
6. Distribute the dough evenly over it
7. Place the dumplings on a flour-dusted board
8. 1/4 hour before serving, put the dumplings in boiling salted water and cook covered
9. Meanwhile, in a saucepan, toast 8 ounces of breadcrumbs in 10 ounces of hot butter
10. Add the drained plum dumplings, shake lightly, arrange on a plate and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
