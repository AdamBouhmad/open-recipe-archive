## Ingredients

- One cleaned calf brain, freed of skin, cooked in salted water
- Flour
- Beaten eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Very hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Once the calf brain is completely cooked, divide it in half and slice each half lengthwise
2. Dip the pieces in flour, then in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs (panate)
3. Shortly before serving, fry them in very hot fat
