## Ingredients

- 2 pieces of carp or pike
- 4 lots of butter
- 2 small bread rolls soaked in milk
- 2 tablespoons of cream
- 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks
- mace and salt
- beef lard
- pea broth
- bread slices

## Directions

1. From 2 pieces of carp or pike remove the bones, peel off the skin and chop it finely
2. Stir 4 lots of butter fluffy with 2 small bread rolls soaked in milk, 2 tablespoons of cream, 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks, mace and salt
3. Give the finely chopped fish into this batter and mix well, then make small dumplings from it
4. Cook the dumplings in beef lard or cook them in pea broth
5. When serving, add a previously made strained pea soup on top, in which one can also serve baked or water-cooked bread slices
6. If the farce is pounded and driven through a hair sieve, it turns out much better
