## Ingredients

- 24 Deca rinsed and dried rice
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 12 Deca butter
- 1 pinch of salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 14 Deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg whites (snow)

## Directions

1. Stir 24 Deca rinsed and dried rice into 1/2 liter boiling milk and cook until thick, then add 12 Deca butter and 1 pinch of salt, mix well and set aside
2. Meanwhile stir 8 egg yolks with 14 Deca vanilla sugar for 1/2 hour, mix this well with the cooled rice and finally fold in lightly the snow from 8 egg whites
3. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour and cook covered for 3/4 hours in steam or bake in the oven
4. In the first case the soufflé can be poured with hot jam shortly before serving, in the other case it can be sprinkled with finely pounded sugar
