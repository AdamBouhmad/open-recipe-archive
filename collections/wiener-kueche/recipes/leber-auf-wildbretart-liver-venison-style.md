## Ingredients

- 50 dkg calf's liver
- 4 dkg root vegetables
- 4 dkg bacon
- ½ liter cream
- 5 dkg hot fat
- ½ onion
- 3 peppercorns and spice berries
- salt
- pepper
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. In hot fat, roast the cleaned, washed root vegetables cut into thin slices with ½ onion, peppercorns, and spice berries until dark golden.
2. Add diced bacon.
3. Add the cleaned, washed, salted, and peppered liver.
4. Pour in a little water and vinegar, cover, and simmer for ½-1 hour.
