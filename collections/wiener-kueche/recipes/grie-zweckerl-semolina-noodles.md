## Ingredients

- 30 dkg flour
- 3 eggs
- salt
- water
- 10 dkg fat
- 18 dkg semolina
- ½ 1 milk

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough and cut into larger fleckerln
2. Cook in boiling salted water for 5 minutes, drain and shock
3. In a casserole, let the semolina turn light brown in hot fat, pour in with the milk
4. Add the fleckerln, salt them and warm in the oven while stirring frequently
5. Serve as a sweet course or as a side dish
