## Ingredients

- good consommé
- 3 pounds veal from the neck or bones
- 3 pounds lean beef
- beef foot
- couple calf feet
- old hen
- parsley
- parsnip
- yellow turnips
- 1 pound ham

## Directions

1. Put good consommé in a casserole, add 3 pounds veal from the neck or bones, 3 pounds lean beef, a beef foot, a couple calf feet, and an old hen
2. Add parsley, parsnip, and yellow turnips, a couple pieces of each, along with 1 pound ham
3. Simmer for 4 hours
4. Strain it clean and serve in bowls to drink
