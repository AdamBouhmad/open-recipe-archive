## Ingredients

- 30 egg yolks
- tablespoon flour
- 1 measure Malaga wine
- sugar
- 2 lemons
- fine chopped pistachios

## Directions

1. Stir in a pot with 30 egg yolks a tablespoon flour clearly
2. Pour in 1 measure Malaga wine
3. Beat well with a whisk for half a quarter hour
4. Add only as much sugar as needed for rubbing 2 lemons, so the cream does not become too sweet
5. Beat all ingredients over gentle fire with the whisk until it becomes a thick cream and comes to a gentle boil once
6. Arrange on a dish
7. Sprinkle with quarter pound fine chopped pistachios
8. Serve cold
