## Ingredients

- fruit scraps of any kind
- wild apples and pears

## Directions

1. Collect all fruit scraps of whatever kind
2. Throw them into a clean barrel that must stand in a frost-free place, such as a cellar
3. Continue adding fruit scraps unworriedly until the barrel is full, regardless of how long it takes
4. After the end of winter, when there is no more fruit to add to the barrel, press out the fruit
5. Let the obtained juice stand in a clean container and skim off what settles as impure matter on top until everything becomes completely clean
6. Place the mass in the necessary heat
7. In a few weeks you will have a vinegar that is completely equal to ordinary wine vinegar and will have retained nothing of the rotten taste
8. For wild apples and pears: let them lie in the open in heaps, or on the floor if that is not possible, until they become soft, then treat them the same way as described for cultivated fruit
