## Ingredients

- ½ kg potatoes
- 1 egg
- salt
- 3 dkg butter
- 2 spoonfuls cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a dough from potatoes, egg, salt, butter, cream, and flour (see potato dough)
2. Form into a roll
3. Cut into equal-sized pieces and shape into short thick noodles
4. Place in a casserole with boiling salted water
5. Cook slowly for 4-5 minutes
6. Carefully transfer to a platter
7. Pour over with breadcrumbs toasted in fat and serve
