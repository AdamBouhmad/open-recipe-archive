## Ingredients

- pineapple juice
- orange peel
- 2 lemons
- refined sugar
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as Orange Jelly (No. 1660), with the difference that orange peel is added to the sugar, juice from only 2 lemons is used, and pineapple juice is used instead of wine.
