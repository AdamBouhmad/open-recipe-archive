## Ingredients

- 2 egg spoonfuls of semolina
- 12 tablespoons of hot butter
- 1 egg spoonful of cold water or milk
- salt to taste
- noodles prepared as described in 'Faded Noodles' (No. 1201)

## Directions

1. Toast 2 egg spoonfuls of semolina in 12 tablespoons of hot butter for a short time
2. Mix in 1 egg spoonful of cold water or milk thoroughly
3. Add the drained noodles that have been rinsed with cold water to the semolina
4. Season to taste and toast with frequent stirring
5. Arrange on a platter and serve hot
6. Alternatively, the noodles can be browned with breadcrumbs: toast 8 tablespoons of breadcrumbs in 16 tablespoons of hot butter, add the noodles, and stir frequently while heating
