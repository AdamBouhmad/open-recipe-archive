## Ingredients

- 30 deca beaten butter
- 12 deca vanilla sugar
- 36 deca flour
- marmalade
- finely pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 30 deca beaten butter with 12 deca vanilla sugar and 36 deca flour well together.
2. Roll out in finger-thick sheets and bake in cake pans.
3. After 1/2 hour remove from the oven and after completely cooling, spread with marmalade of choice.
4. Layer them together and sprinkle the top with finely pounded sugar.
