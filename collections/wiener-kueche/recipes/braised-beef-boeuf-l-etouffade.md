## Ingredients

- Well-aged beef loin of approximately 3 kilograms
- Bacon slices
- Onion
- Thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 piece of garlic (if desired)
- Ginger, pepper, nutmeg, and cloves (as desired)
- 1.5 liters white wine
- 1.5 liters broth

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with bacon slices, some onion, and thyme; add 1 bay leaf and, if desired, 1 piece of garlic
2. Season the meat with salt, place it on the bed of aromatics
3. Season with ginger, pepper, nutmeg, and cloves as desired
4. Braise covered for approximately 1 hour
5. Pour 1.5 liters white wine and 1.5 liters broth over it
6. Braise for approximately 2 hours; if it reduces too much, add broth as needed
7. When serving, garnish it neatly and serve the skimmed sauce in a sauce boat
