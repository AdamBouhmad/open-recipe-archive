## Ingredients

- 8 deca coffee
- 1/2 liter hot cream
- 16 deca sugar
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. Roast 8 deca coffee and pour 1/2 liter of hot cream over it and cover it so that it absorbs the coffee aroma. After approximately 1/4 hour, strain it through a sieve, place it on the stove and let it boil with 16 deca sugar. Then stir together in 4 tablespoons cream 8 egg yolks and 1 whole egg, gradually pour in the prepared cream, place it on the stove for a few moments while constantly stirring, pass the mass through a sieve, fill it into the mold, and proceed further as indicated in the preceding number 1424 for cooking the cream.
