## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- salt
- 1/2 Seidel (about 1/4 liter) cream
- flour
- 1 Mass (about 2 liters) boiling milk
- 1/2 pound butter for finishing
- crushed sugar
- 4 or 5 egg yolks for the sauce
- sugar for the sauce

## Directions

1. Beat 1/4 pound butter with 4 eggs until clear, add a little salt and 1/2 Seidel cream
2. Stir in as much flour as needed so the dough can be worked
3. Using a knife on a floured sheet, form oblong small sausages
4. Boil them in 1 Mass of boiling milk
5. Meanwhile, let 1/2 pound butter in a cassole become very hot and layer the small sausages from the milk into it in layers
6. Sprinkle crushed sugar over them and set a lid with coals on top
7. When the noodles are golden baked, stir the remaining milk with 4 or 5 egg yolks, add some sugar, and serve separately with the noodles
