## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- veal forcemeat (about 1/2 pound)
- boiled ham or tongue, cut into pencil-thick pieces
- lard, cut similarly
- truffles
- 1 small goose liver, roasted and cut into pieces
- lard strips
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from a veal breast and cut out the cartilage.
2. Pound the meat with the flat of a cleaver and season with salt and pepper.
3. Spread veal forcemeat on the inner side from which the bones were removed.
4. Arrange pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of cooked ham or tongue, similarly cut lard, truffles, and small roasted goose liver pieces on top.
5. Roll the meat very tightly and bind securely with kitchen twine.
6. Place in a casserole lined with lard, cover the top with lard strips, and roast for about 1.5 hours.
7. Remove the meat, untie the string, cut into thick slices forming round pieces, and serve with defatted sauce.
