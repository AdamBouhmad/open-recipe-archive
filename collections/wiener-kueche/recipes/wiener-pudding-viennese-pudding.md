## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter, beaten
- 12 deca vanilla sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 18 deca blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter with 12 deca vanilla sugar until mixed
2. Gradually add 7 egg yolks and 18 deca blanched, finely pounded almonds, one after the other
3. Beat this mixture for 1/2 hour
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
5. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
6. Steam covered for 3/4 hour
7. Turn out onto a platter and serve with Chaudeau
