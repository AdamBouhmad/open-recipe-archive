## Ingredients

- ½ l white wine
- 16-20 pieces of cube sugar
- 4-6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a chaudeau pot, let the cube sugar dissolve in ½ l white wine.
2. Add 4-6 egg yolks and whip on a hot stove plate until it rises and becomes thick.
3. Alternatively, whip everything in a snow kettle over steam until thick and foamy.
