## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram greengages
- 0.5 kilogram powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the ripe greengages, remove the kernels, and simmer 1 kilogram of greengages with 0.5 kilogram of powdered sugar in a covered saucepan.
2. After approximately 10 minutes, pass quickly through a sieve, cook in the same way, and fill hot into jars like Apricot Preserves.
3. The greengages should not be cooked too long as they will otherwise lose their green color.
