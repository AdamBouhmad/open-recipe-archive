## Ingredients

- morels
- asparagus
- butter
- fresh parsley
- butter sauce
- milk cream
- asparagus water

## Directions

1. Cut off the stems of the morels
2. After washing the heads, cut them into rings
3. Braise these in butter with fresh parsley
4. Prepare a butter sauce and dilute it with milk cream and some asparagus water
5. Reduce it well
6. Pour this sauce over ordinarily cooked asparagus and serve with the morels
