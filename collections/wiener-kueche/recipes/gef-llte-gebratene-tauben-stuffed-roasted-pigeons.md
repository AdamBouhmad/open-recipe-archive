## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Veal breast forcemeat

## Directions

1. The pigeons are feathered (plucked), flamed, gutted, washed, and the neck, head and feet are cut off. The leg bone is pushed back, the thighbone is tucked inward and with the index finger from behind toward front the skin is separated from the meat. The pigeons are salted inside and outside and filled with the forcemeat of veal breast, namely the mass is spread between the skin and breast down to the thighs and the wings are turned inward against the back over the skin of the neck so that the skin is held secure by this. The pigeons are then placed in a pan, covered with hot fat and roasted in the oven for about 1/2 hour with frequent basting. After this time they are removed, the string is cut, pulled out, each pigeon is cut lengthwise in the middle and arranged on a dish with any salad served alongside. The pigeons can instead of breadcrumb forcemeat be filled with liver forcemeat.
