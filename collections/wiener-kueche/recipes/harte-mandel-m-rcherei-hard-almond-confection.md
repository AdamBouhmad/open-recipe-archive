## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams peeled almonds
- 1 egg white
- 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar
- flour
- marmalade
- glaze

## Directions

1. Pound peeled almonds very finely with 1 egg white and 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar into a dough
2. Place on a flour-dusted board and spread knife-back-thick
3. Cut into two parts
4. Spread marmalade on one part and cover with the second part
5. Press together and cut into desired pieces
6. Place on a baking sheet and bake in the oven until maize-yellow
7. Remove from oven and after completely cooling, spread with dissolved marmalade
8. Once dry, cover with desired glaze
