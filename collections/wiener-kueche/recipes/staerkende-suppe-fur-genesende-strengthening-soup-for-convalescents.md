## Ingredients

- One egg
- Wine or sugar and cinnamon, or meat broth
- Nutmeg
- Yellow-toasted bread rolls or white bread slices

## Directions

1. Boil one egg until somewhat hard and press it through
2. Mix either with wine, or sugar and cinnamon, or with meat broth into which a little nutmeg has been added
3. Prepare this broth over yellow-toasted bread roll or white bread slices
4. Give the patient this soup to eat every morning
5. Alternatively, omit the egg and spices, soak the bread slice in some wine or lemon juice, and pour thin meat broth over it
