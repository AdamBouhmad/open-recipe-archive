## Ingredients

- 80 drachm raw, boned meat of any kind
- 60 drachm fat (beef fat or bacon), finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 8 drachm crusts of bread, soaked in water and squeezed dry
- 1 bay leaf
- sprigs of thyme
- salt and pepper as needed

## Directions

1. Finely chop together the meat and fat
2. Pound in a mortar with 2 whole eggs, squeezed bread crusts, 1 bay leaf, and sprigs of thyme
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. With a spoon, take portions of this forcemeat and place on a water-moistened board
5. Smooth with a knife and shape as desired, like fish cakes or sausages
6. Dust with flour
7. 10 minutes before serving, place in a shallow casserole containing hot butter
8. Brown on each side for approximately 4 minutes with gentle movement
9. Pour off the fat, cover the patties with brown soup and an equal amount of cream
10. Add a little lemon juice if desired and bring to a boil once
11. Serve with rice, macaroni, or puree
