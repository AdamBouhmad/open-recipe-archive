## Ingredients

- scraps of cut-out dough
- one third of the pastries
- 8 dkg cube sugar
- 6-8 spoons rum
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 spoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the scraps of cut-out dough and one third of the pastries in a bowl
2. Cook 8 dkg cube sugar with 6-8 spoons rum, 1/2 liter water and 1 spoon lemon juice for a few minutes
3. Mix with the scraps to a thick porridge
4. Alternatively, bakery, torte and biscuit scraps can be used for the filling
