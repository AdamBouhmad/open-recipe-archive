## Ingredients

- Finished strawberry frozen dessert (Recipe 1746)
- 1/4 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. As desired, fold 1/4 liter whipped cream into the finished strawberry frozen dessert (Recipe 1746)
2. Keep it in the container until serving, for about seven days
3. The mixing of whipped cream can be done with any other desired fruit frozen dessert in the same manner as with strawberry frozen dessert
