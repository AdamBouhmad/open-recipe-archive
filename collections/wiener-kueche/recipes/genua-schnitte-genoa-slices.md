## Ingredients

- 18 Deca butter
- 18 Deca sugar
- 4 whole eggs
- 18 Deca flour
- 1 tablespoon rum
- marmalade as needed
- transparent glaze

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a deep bowl and stir only slightly so it does not become fluffy
2. Add 1 whole egg, 1 tablespoon flour and 1 tablespoon sugar, mix together
3. Repeat this process until all 4 eggs are used
4. Add the remaining flour and sugar simultaneously, mix well and fold in 1 tablespoon rum
5. Spread this mixture knife-back width thick on a baking sheet brushed with butter and bake light yellow in the oven for a short time
6. Remove the sheet from the oven and cut the dough with a long thin knife into two layers
7. Spread one layer with dissolved marmalade and cover with the second layer so the edges lie evenly
8. Spread the upper dough also with marmalade, and after it has cooled completely, cover with a transparent glaze
9. Cut the whole into finger-long and 2 finger-width wide pieces
10. Can also be served warm with Chaudeau
