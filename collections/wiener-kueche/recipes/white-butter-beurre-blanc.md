## Ingredients

- 10 decagrams butter
- salt
- white pepper, finely ground
- 3 tablespoons fine oil

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the butter with salt and finely ground white pepper
2. If the butter is hard, pound it in a mortar until creamy and gradually mix in 3 tablespoons of fine oil
3. If the butter is soft, stir it in a bowl with a wooden spoon and place it on ice
