## Ingredients

- 1 kilo sugar
- 1 liter water
- very thinly cut peel of 2 sweet oranges
- very thinly cut peel of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place 1 kilo sugar, 1 liter water and the very thinly cut peel of 2 sweet oranges and 2 lemons
