## Ingredients

- Lard
- Root vegetables, finely cut
- Onion
- 1 bay leaf
- Whole spices
- Water
- Vinegar
- Prepared frog legs
- Grated bread

## Directions

1. Place lard and finely cut root vegetables, onion, bay leaf, and whole spices in a casserole
2. Pour in equal amounts of water and vinegar
3. Cook covered
4. When the root vegetables are soft, add the prepared frog legs
5. Let them stew covered until tender
6. Remove the legs and place in the center of a serving bowl
7. Garnish with the cooked root vegetables and grated bread
8. Mix egg yolks with some cold soup, pour the hot broth over this, and serve the sauce in a sauce boat with the frog legs
