## Ingredients

- 6 dag butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 dag grated Parmesan
- ½ kg cooked, passed, and cooled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and beat in the egg yolks, salt, and grated Parmesan
2. Mix in the cooled mashed potatoes
3. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a small finger on a floured board and cut into rectangles or squares
