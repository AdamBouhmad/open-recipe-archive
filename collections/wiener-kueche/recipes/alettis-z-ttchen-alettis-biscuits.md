## Ingredients

- 16 deka finely sifted sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 16 deka fine flour
- blanched, finely cut almonds
- finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Beat together 16 deka finely sifted sugar, 8 egg yolks, and 2 whole eggs for 1/2 hour
2. Quickly mix in 16 deka fine flour
3. Spread this mass on a butter-greased sheet
4. Sprinkle with blanched finely cut almonds
5. Dust with finely sifted sugar
6. Bake in the oven at moderate heat until golden
7. Cut the baked biscuit into 2 finger-wide and finger-long pieces
