## Ingredients

- flattened and salted beef roast pieces
- bacon
- onion
- garlic (optional)
- 1/4 liter crumbs of black bread
- meat broth
- wine
- seasoning
- raw, peeled potatoes

## Directions

1. Place the flattened and salted beef roast pieces with bacon, onion, and garlic if desired in a casserole
2. Braise until brown
3. Sprinkle 1/4 liter of black bread crumbs over them
4. Add meat broth and wine, in total enough so the liquid barely covers the meat
5. Add seasoning to taste and raw, peeled potatoes
6. Braise covered until tender
7. Arrange the roast with the potatoes on a serving dish
8. Pour the defatted sauce over them
