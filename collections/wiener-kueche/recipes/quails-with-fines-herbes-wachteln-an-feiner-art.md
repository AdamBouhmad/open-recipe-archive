## Ingredients

- Quails
- Salt
- Pepper
- 2 cleaned, sliced mushrooms
- 2 pieces of shallots
- 5 sprigs of parsley
- Butter (12 teaspoons)
- Flour (2 teaspoons)
- White wine (approximately 1/2 liter)
- Macaroni or steamed rice for serving

## Directions

1. Clean the quails with a cloth and draw the legs inside
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Finely cut the mushrooms, shallots, and parsley
4. Place the quails with these ingredients in a casserole with butter
5. Cover and roast for 10 minutes
6. Dust with flour and roast briefly
7. Pour approximately 1/2 liter white wine over it
8. Stir constantly until a thick sauce forms
9. Arrange the quails with the sauce on a plate
10. Serve with macaroni or steamed rice
