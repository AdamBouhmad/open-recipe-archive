## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deca flour
- 4 deca finely pounded sugar
- 1 knife-tip salt
- 1/4 liter whipped cream
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca butter until fluffy, add 6 egg yolks one after another and then mix this with 16 deca flour, 4 deca finely pounded sugar and 1 knife-tip salt, stirring for another 1/4 hour. Meanwhile, pour 1/4 liter whipped cream into a mixer and beat it with the snow whisk to a thick foam, give this with the snow from 6 egg whites to the batter mentioned above and fold together lightly.
2. When you have brushed both parts of the hot waffle iron with melted butter or unsalted bacon, add 1 tablespoon of the above batter, close the iron firmly and bake as described for 'Waffles from Batter' (No. 1648).
