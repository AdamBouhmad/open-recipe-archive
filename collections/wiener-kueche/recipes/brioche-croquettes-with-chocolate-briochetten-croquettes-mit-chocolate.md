## Ingredients

- 3 eggs (for brioche dough)
- melted chocolate cream
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare as Brioche-Croquettes, with the difference that they are spread with melted chocolate cream instead of jam
