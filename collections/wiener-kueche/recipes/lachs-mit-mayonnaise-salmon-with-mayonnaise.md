## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Mayonnaise
- Aspic (various colors)
- Lemon slices
- Butter
- Crayfish
- Truffles
- Calf's combs

## Directions

1. Place the completely rested, blue-cooked salmon carefully with the insert on a platter
2. Decorate on the back with silver skewers on which one puts crayfish, truffles, and calf's combs
3. Arrange around the fish on the platter: crayfish, variously colored, chopped or neatly cut-out aspic, lemon slices, colored butter and similar items
4. Place a lemon slice in the fish's belly instead of wood
5. Ensure that everything looks tastefully and neatly arranged
6. Serve Mayonnaise separately in a sauce boat
