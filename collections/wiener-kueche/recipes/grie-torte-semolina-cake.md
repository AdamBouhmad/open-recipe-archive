## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 20 dkg sugar
- lemon zest
- 15 dkg raw grated almonds
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 10 dkg finest semolina

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and sugar
2. Add lemon zest and raw grated almonds
3. Fold in stiff egg whites together with finest semolina
4. Fill into cake form
5. Bake slowly
6. Cut cake in half, coat with jam, reassemble
7. Cover with rum or lemon ice or serve with warm wine
