## Ingredients

- 1½ kg tenderloin
- 10 dkg bacon
- salt
- pepper
- very hot fat
- 3 dkg butter
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Remove all bones, fat, and skin from the meat.
2. Salt and pepper it and lard it.
3. Place it in a pan with very hot fat, skin side down.
4. Sear on the stovetop, then place in a hot oven.
5. Roast for 30-45 minutes at high heat, basting frequently.
6. When the top is brown, turn it over.
7. Transfer to a plate and place in an open oven.
8. Make a sauce by pouring off the fat, browning 3 dkg butter in the pan, and adding a little broth or water. Bring to a boil and serve separately.
9. Slice the meat very thin (it should be pink inside) and arrange on a long platter with garnishes.
