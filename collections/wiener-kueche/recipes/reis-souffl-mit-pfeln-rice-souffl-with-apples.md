## Ingredients

- rice mass (as per recipe 1373)
- apples, thinly sliced
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as described for the ordinary Rice Soufflé (No. 1373)
2. Fill half of this mass into a buttered form dusted with flour, put it in the oven, and as soon as the mass has drawn together, cover it with finely and thinly sliced apples, sprinkle with finely pounded sugar and cover with the second half of the rice mass
3. Put it in the oven and bake for 3/4 hours
4. Instead of apples the mass can be covered with any compote or sprinkled with 2 tablets of grated chocolate
