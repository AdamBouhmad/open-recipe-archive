## Ingredients

- 10½ dkg butter
- 10¼ dkg sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 14 dkg roasted hazelnut kernels, grated
- 3 egg whites
- 1 spoon breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 10½ dkg butter first alone, then with 10¼ dkg sugar and 3 egg yolks until well beaten.
2. Stir in 14 dkg roasted, shelled, grated hazelnuts, 3 egg whites, and 1 spoon breadcrumbs.
3. Spread the mass onto buttered, floured sheet and bake at medium heat.
4. Cut the cake into pieces while still warm, or cut out with a round cutter.
5. Dust with powdered sugar or brush with marmalade, stack together, white ice, and decorate the top with a hazelnut or candied cherry.
