## Ingredients

- 2 Deca gelatin
- 0.25 liters water
- 0.5 liters Maraschino, orange water, or fruit liqueur
- green almonds, black walnuts, Angelica, whole raisins, and pineapple pieces, 12 Deca total
- 16 Deca sugar
- 0.5 liters heavy cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 Deca gelatin with 0.25 liters water and add 0.5 liters Maraschino, orange water, or fruit liqueur of choice.
2. Cut various finely prepared fruits (green almonds, black walnuts, Angelica, whole raisins, and pineapple pieces), 12 Deca total, into small pieces.
3. Heat the fruit liqueur with 16 Deca sugar and add the cut fruits to the dissolved gelatin.
4. The liquid should not exceed 0.5 liters total.
5. Transfer to a deep bowl and stir until it cools.
6. Fold in 0.5 liters whipped cream.
7. Transfer to a mold brushed with oil, which has been buried in ice 0.5 hour earlier.
8. From this mass, as from any other cream mass, one can prepare Cabinet Pudding as follows: Line a mold or cake plate with thin sliced baked biscuit or fine baked dough, cover thickly with cream, then alternate layers of biscuit and cream until the mold is full, cover and bury in ice for 2 hours, turn out onto a platter, and garnish as desired with sugar, fruit, marmalade, or whipped cream.
