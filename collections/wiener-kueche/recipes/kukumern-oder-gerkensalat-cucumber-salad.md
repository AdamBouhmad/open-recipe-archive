## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Young radishes (optional)
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers if they are not yet too large and cut them into fine thin slices
2. If desired, some young radishes can be cut into thin slices and mixed in
3. Salt them and dress immediately with vinegar, oil and pepper
4. Alternatively, some let them sit in salt for some time and then press the water out thoroughly, though this method is said to be unhealthy because they become tough when pressed and are much harder to digest
5. Serve as a salad
