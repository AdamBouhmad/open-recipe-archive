## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Butter
- Flour
- Dried peas, beans, or bran
- Beaten eggs
- Fruits or whipped cream
- Strawberries, roasted chestnuts, or custard creams

## Directions

1. Follow the same preparation as the preceding Timbale
2. After the timbale is completely cooled, instead of filling with meat, fill with desired fruits or whipped cream mixed with strawberries, roasted chestnuts, or desired cream sauces
