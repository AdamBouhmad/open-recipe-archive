## Ingredients

- 20 Maschanzger apples
- One quarter pound sugar
- Water
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Hollow out the cores from 20 Maschanzger apples, then peel them and throw them in fresh water.
2. Make a caramel from one quarter pound sugar, and as soon as it takes on a yellowish color, remove it from the fire.
3. Lay the apples next to one another in the casserole, pour a small glass of water to it, add a lemon peel, and let it cook slowly and well covered until the apples are soft and glazed.
4. Let them cool and arrange them on the compote dish.
5. From what remains in the casserole, cook a syrup with one quarter pound sugar and some water, pass it through a hair sieve, and when it is cold, pour it over the apples.
