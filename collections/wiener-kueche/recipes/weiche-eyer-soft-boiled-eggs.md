## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cold water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread, finely cut
- Butter

## Directions

1. Place eggs with cold water over the fire, and remove them when the water begins to boil.
2. Serve with finely cut bread, salt, pepper, and butter (in some regions, butter is served separately to melt in them).
