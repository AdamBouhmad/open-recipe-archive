## Ingredients

- Meat
- Salt
- Caraway
- 5 dkg fat
- Water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat
2. Rub with salt and caraway
3. Bind if desired
4. Heat 5 dkg fat in a casserole until hot
5. Place meat in and brown on all sides
6. Add a small amount of water
7. Cover and braise for 2 hours, turning often and adding water as needed
8. Slice and serve with the brown sauce on a long platter
