## Ingredients

- Mutton cutlets
- Braising sauce (recipe no. 189)

## Directions

1. Make a braising sauce following recipe no. 189 and let it brown.
2. Strain the sauce.
3. Braise the cutlets in the strained sauce.
4. Serve with vegetables of choice or purée.
