## Ingredients

- Pike
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- paper
- onion
- parsley root
- pike head and bones
- flour
- oil or fat
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons cream
- juice of 1 lemon
- fine dumplings

## Directions

1. Skin the pike, remove the rib bones and other remaining bones, and cut the flesh into pieces 2-3 fingers wide, 1 finger long, and half-finger thick
2. Butter a casserole with knife-back thickness of butter
3. Arrange the cut pieces next to each other, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and cover with a round-cut piece of paper brushed with butter
4. 1/4 hour before serving, place the casserole on fire, remove the paper, and fry the pieces 2-3 minutes on each side turning them over
5. While the pieces sit covered, make the following sauce: put 16 deca butter, some finely sliced onion and parsley root, the broken pike head and bones into a casserole
6. Let roast for a short time, dust with flour, add 1/4 liter wine and equal amount stock and some salt and let come to a boil
7. Strain everything and may be finished as desired with 3 egg yolks, 3 tablespoons cream and juice of 1 lemon, and pour over the pieces
8. Serve with any fine dumplings
9. Alternative preparations: pieces may be prepared like half-schnitzels and either dipped in flour and fried in finger-high fat, or treated like breaded schnitzels
10. Garnish with lemon slices and serve salad with it
