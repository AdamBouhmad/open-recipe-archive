## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds each of quitch grass and grass roots and the roots of Pfaffenröhrlein
- two Maß of water
- six Quentchen of polychrest salt
- one half pound of oxymel

## Directions

1. Take quitch grass and grass roots and the roots of Pfaffenröhrlein, one and a half pounds each
2. Cook them for two hours in two Maß of water until one Maß remains
3. Press strongly through a cloth
4. Add six Quentchen of polychrest salt and one half pound of oxymel
5. Take half a tea cup full every two hours for fourteen days or three to four weeks in succession
