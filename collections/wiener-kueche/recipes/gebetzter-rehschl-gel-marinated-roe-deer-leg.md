## Ingredients

- Roe deer leg, skinned and spitted
- Marinade (as described in No. 703)
- Butter or fat
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. After skinning and spitting the leg, but not salting it, baste it with the marinade specified in No. 703
2. Cover it with a sheet of paper brushed with butter or fat and let it stand covered in a cool place
3. Every day the meat must be turned in the marinade, but on the third or fourth day the marinade is reheated and poured back over the leg strained
4. Do not let it stand longer than eight days
5. Then treat it further as you would the marinated loin
6. Note that if the leg or game in general is very large, naturally cook more liquid with the marinade
