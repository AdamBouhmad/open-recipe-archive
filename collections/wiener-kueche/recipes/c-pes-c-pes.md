## Ingredients

- cèpes
- butter
- green parsley

## Directions

1. From the peeled cèpes, remove both the lower part of the stem and any worm-damaged portions
2. Cut the cèpes into thin slices
3. Place them in butter with roasted green parsley and braise them until tender
4. Use them as a vegetable or as a garnish for various meat dishes
