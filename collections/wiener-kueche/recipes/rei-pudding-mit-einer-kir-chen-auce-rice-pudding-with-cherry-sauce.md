## Ingredients

- One pound of rice
- Boiling water
- Boiling milk
- Half a measure of cream
- One measure of milk
- Whole cinnamon
- One pound of butter

## Directions

1. Select the rice, rinse it, and blanch with boiling water
2. Fill with boiling milk and cook over slow heat until thick, allowing the rice to swell well while remaining grainy
3. Cook the prepared rice with half a measure of cream, one measure of milk, and some whole cinnamon
4. Let it cool
5. Beat one pound of butter
