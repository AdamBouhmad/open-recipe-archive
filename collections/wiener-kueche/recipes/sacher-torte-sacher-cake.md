## Ingredients

- 16 deca chocolate
- 16 deca butter
- 16 deca finely ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- apricot jam, boiling hot
- chocolate glaze, warm or cold

## Directions

1. Soften 16 deca chocolate on a baking sheet in the oven
2. Beat 16 deca butter well
3. Add the softened chocolate and stir until cold
4. Gradually add 16 deca finely ground sugar with 6 egg yolks and stir
5. Gently fold in the snow (stiffly beaten egg whites) from 6 egg whites with 16 deca flour
6. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour with this mixture
7. Bake for approximately 1 hour in the oven
8. Remove the ring and let the cake cool on a sieve
9. If desired, brush the cooled cake with boiling hot apricot jam
10. Once dry, cover with warm or cold chocolate glaze
