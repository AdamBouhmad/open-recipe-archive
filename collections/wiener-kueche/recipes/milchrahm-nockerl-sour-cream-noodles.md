## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 liter flour
- salted water
- 1/8 liter toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 1/4 liter sour cream, 4 egg yolks, and 1/2 teaspoon salt well together
2. Gradually pour into 1/2 liter flour in a deep bowl while stirring constantly
3. Beat the dough until it falls from the spoon
4. Using a spoon kept in hot water, place noodles of desired size into boiling salted water
5. Cover and cook for approximately 10 minutes
6. Pour into a colander and rinse with cold water
7. Place in a pot containing 1/8 liter toasted bread crumbs
8. Stir well together and serve hot as a garnish for roasted lungs, paprika chicken, etc.
