## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage
- 2 bread rolls
- 0.5 deciliter milk
- 4 dekagrams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and cut the cabbage into noodle-like strips.
2. Braise it in 4 dekagrams of butter over frequent stirring for 25-30 minutes until soft and cool it.
3. Make a roux of 4 dekagrams butter and stir in 2 egg yolks.
4. Soak 2 bread rolls in milk, squeeze them well and add them to the roux.
5. Add the cooked cabbage, salt, pepper and 2 beaten egg whites.
6. Fill the mixture into a schöberl pan and bake at moderate heat.
7. Loosen the schöberl, turn it out, cut it and serve warm.
