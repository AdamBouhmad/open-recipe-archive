## Ingredients

- 30 dkg rice
- 2 dkg Parmesan
- 4 dkg flour
- hot lard
- hash filling
- spinach or cooked salad

## Directions

1. Braise the rice until very soft, place on a floured board, mix with Parmesan and 4 dkg flour and roll out two fingers thick.
2. With a round cutter, cut out slices and bread them.
3. With a small cutter, mark a lid on top, bake the crusts in hot lard, lift out the lid, fill with hash and place the lid back on.
4. Serve with spinach or cooked salad.
