## Ingredients

- plums
- sugar (equal weight to the plum juice)
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the kernels from the plums.
2. Place them in a newly glazed pot with a lid tied securely on top.
3. Place the pot in a vessel of boiling water and keep it there for a good hour, ensuring no water enters the pot.
4. Remove the pot from the water.
5. Pass the plums through a fine sieve.
6. Measure the juice and add an equal weight of clarified sugar.
7. Treat as with the preceding marmalades.
