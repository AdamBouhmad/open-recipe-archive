## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/4 liter milk
- Juice of 3 lemons
- 7 decagrams isinglass, still hot

## Directions

1. Clarify 40 decagrams sugar with 1/2 liter water and let it cool covered
2. Add to 1/4 liter milk the juice of 3 lemons
3. Place this on the fire, whereby it becomes curdled and strain through cloth
4. Mix the strained whey with 7 decagrams of still hot isinglass
