## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg boned veal shoulder or front rib
- salt
- water
- 6 dkg butter
- veal broth
- lemon juice
- 1 small head of cauliflower
- 2 mushrooms

## Directions

1. Wash the veal and place in cold salted water
2. Cook for 1 hour and remove
3. Slice the meat
4. Make a parsley roux with 6 dkg butter and pour veal broth over it
5. Add the meat and cook well
6. Sour with lemon juice
7. Add the cauliflower and mushrooms cut into leaves, which were cooked separately in salted water
8. Do not stir much
9. Serve on a round platter with the dumplings
