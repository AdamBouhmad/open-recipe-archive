## Ingredients

- 50 dkg calf's liver
- 4 h root vegetables
- 4 dkg bacon
- 1/2 l cream
- 5 dkg hot fat
- 1/2 onion
- 3 peppercorns and spice seeds
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. In 5 dkg hot fat, brown the cleaned, washed, thinly sliced root vegetables with 1/2 onion, 3 peppercorns and spice seeds, and the diced bacon until dark golden.
2. Add the skinned, washed, salted, and peppered liver, pour in some water and vinegar, cover and braise for 1/2 to 1 hour.
3. Remove the liver and cut into slices. Strain the sauce, add cream, let it reduce, put the liver back in and simmer a few more minutes.
4. Serve with rice, noodles or potato pancakes.
