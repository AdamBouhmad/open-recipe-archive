## Ingredients

- half a measure good meat soup
- 4 pounds rice
- salt
- nutmeg
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Pour half a measure of good meat soup over 4 pounds rice for 6 persons, which should first be washed with cold water, then lukewarm, and finally hot water until nothing white comes off anymore (because it often takes on a stale smell from lying in barrels)
2. Let it cook salted, without stirring, until tender if you want to serve it with brown soup; you can also stir it often once you have fricasseed it with eggs
3. Since the poured soup will be drawn into the rice, put it in a dish and pour a measure of beef soup colored with consommé over it, salt it and serve it to the table, and you may also present grated Parmesan cheese on a separate plate
4. If you do not want the soup this way, stir 3 egg yolks whisked with a spoonful of water into the beef soup to be added until it becomes thick, then serve it with the rice, and sprinkle both this and the brown soup with nutmeg flower
