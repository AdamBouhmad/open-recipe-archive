## Ingredients

- Pork kidneys
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys thinly
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
3. Let them go in butter with finely chopped shallots and parsley over high heat until the kidneys begin to stiffen
