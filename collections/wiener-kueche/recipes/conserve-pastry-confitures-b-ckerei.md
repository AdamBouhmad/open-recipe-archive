## Ingredients

- 15 deca finely sifted powdered sugar
- 10 deca flour
- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Lightly mix powdered sugar and flour with stiffly beaten egg whites
2. Fill into syringe or paper cone
3. Pipe mixture in desired figures onto baking sheet lined with parchment
4. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar
5. Dry at low heat in open oven
6. Remove and loosen from baking sheet with fine knife
7. Let cool
