## Ingredients

- 17 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 17 dkg sugar
- 17 dkg raw grated almonds
- 5 stiff egg whites
- 17 dkg buckwheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat butter alone, then with egg yolks and sugar well
2. Mix in raw grated almonds
3. Fold in stiff egg whites together with buckwheat flour
4. Fill into form
5. Bake slowly
6. When cooled, cut in half, fill with jam
7. Glaze brown or sprinkle with sugar
