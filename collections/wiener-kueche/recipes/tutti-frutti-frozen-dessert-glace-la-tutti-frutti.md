## Ingredients

- Firm, finely worked pineapple, orange, or lemon frozen dessert
- Small diced, preserved fruits such as pineapple, figs, grapes, apricots, peaches, quinces, pears, and similar

## Directions

1. Into firm, finely worked pineapple, orange, or lemon frozen dessert, slowly mix small diced preserved fruits such as pineapple, figs, grapes, apricots, peaches, quinces, pears, and the like
2. Serve this heaped on cups or in glasses
