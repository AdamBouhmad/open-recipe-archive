## Ingredients

- 26 deca blanched almonds
- 1 egg white
- 20 deca vanilla sugar
- 2 tablets grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Pound 26 deca blanched almonds very finely with 1 egg white
2. Place in a deep bowl with 20 deca vanilla sugar and 2 tablets grated chocolate
3. Mix well together
4. Lightly fold in the whipped whites of 2 eggs
5. Prepare as directed for the Nuss-Böusserln (recipe 1627)
