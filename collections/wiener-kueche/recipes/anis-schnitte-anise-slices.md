## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 16 Deca finely chopped sugar
- 12 Deca potato flour
- 12 Deca regular flour
- finely frozen sugar mixed with flooded anise

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites from 8 eggs until stiff
2. Mix gradually with 16 Deca finely chopped sugar, 12 Deca potato flour and 12 Deca regular flour
3. Spread this mixture finger-high on wafers, sprinkle with finely frozen sugar mixed with flooded anise
4. Cut into strips of any desired size and bake on a baking sheet at low heat in the oven
5. When half baked, that is still soft, place on a warmed sponge form and dry completely in the very hot oven
