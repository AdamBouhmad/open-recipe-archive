## Ingredients

- 15 spoonfuls semolina
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Combine 15 spoonfuls semolina and 2 eggs together with hands until crumbly
2. Spread the semolina on a board and let dry completely
3. Stir into 2 liters boiling broth
