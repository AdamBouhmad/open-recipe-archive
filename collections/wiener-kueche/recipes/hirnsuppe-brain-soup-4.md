## Ingredients

- 30 dkg beef marrow bones
- 1 calf brain
- 3-4 mushrooms
- 1 roll
- 5 dkg butter
- parsley
- 2 tablespoons flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Wash the brain, remove the skin, blanch it, and pass through a sieve
2. Clean, wash, and finely chop the mushrooms
3. In a saucepan, roast chopped parsley in 5 dkg hot butter, add the mushrooms and stew for 15 minutes
4. Add 2 tablespoons flour and the brain, season with salt and pepper
5. Pour in strained beef broth, simmer for 20 minutes, and serve with bread slices
