## Ingredients

- Beef filet with bones and sinews removed
- 16 Deca bacon
- 6 anchovies
- 1 strong tablespoon capers
- 1 small onion
- 4 sprigs parsley
- Fat
- 1 onion
- Some root vegetables
- 12 Deca black bread
- 5 grains spice
- 8 sprigs marjoram
- Bones and scraps from the filet

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from a small beef filet, trim it, and carefully cut it from the thinner side so deep that you can unfold it without dividing it into two pieces
2. Finely chop 16 Deca bacon, 6 anchovies, 1 strong tablespoon capers, 1 small onion, and 4 sprigs parsley on a cutting board with the back of a knife
3. Spread this mixture on the inner, already salted side of the filet
4. Roll the filet together and wrap it with twine
5. In a casserole, add fat, 1 onion, some root vegetables, 12 Deca black bread (all cut into thin slices), 5 grains spice, 8 sprigs marjoram, and the bones and scraps removed from the filet
