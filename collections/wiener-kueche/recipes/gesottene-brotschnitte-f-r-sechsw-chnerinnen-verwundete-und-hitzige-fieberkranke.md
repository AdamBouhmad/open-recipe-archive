## Ingredients

- thin slices of rolls or white bread
- melted butter
- warm water

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a bowl with thin slices of rolls or white bread
2. Pour a little melted butter over it
3. Lay another layer of thin bread on top
4. Pour more butter over it
5. Continue in this manner until the bowl is full
6. Pour a little warm water over it, but only so much that no water is visible
7. Cover it well
8. Let it boil gently over a mild fire
9. The dish is then ready
