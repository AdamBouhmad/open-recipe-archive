## Ingredients

- 12 ounces bacon, diced
- 12 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chives, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut 12 ounces of bacon into fine dice and place in a sauté pan or egg pan, roasting until it becomes translucent.
2. Meanwhile, beat 12 eggs well, season to taste with salt and pepper, and pour over the roasted bacon.
3. Cook over gentle heat, stirring and scraping, until the eggs begin to set.
4. Serve as desired, either firm or more liquid, with a serving spoon in a warm bowl, sprinkled with finely chopped chives. It can also be served in the egg pan, which is placed on a hot plate.
