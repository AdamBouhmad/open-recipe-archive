## Ingredients

- small trout
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- lard or fat

## Directions

1. Use the smallest trout
2. Scale and clean the trout
3. Make small slits on both sides
4. Let them stand in a cool place for some time
5. Dry them thoroughly
6. Roll them on both sides in flour
7. Then roll them in beaten egg
8. Coat well with breadcrumbs
9. Fry them light brown in very hot lard for ¾ hour before serving
10. Arrange on a suitable platter
11. Garnish with fried green parsley and lemon slices
12. Larger trout should be cut into finger-length pieces and prepared the same way
