## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 0.33 liter millet
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 1 pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Bring 1 liter of milk to a boil
2. Stir in 0.33 liter of washed millet under constant stirring and cook well
3. When the mixture begins to become thick, add 12 tablespoons butter, 8 tablespoons sugar, and 1 pinch of salt
4. Stir well together
5. When the millet is soft and cooked thick, arrange in a serving dish dusted with finely ground sugar and grated gingerbread (Lebkuchen) and serve
