## Ingredients

- fat
- veal kidneys
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- broth or white wine
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Put some fat in a casserole and roast finely cut onions in it.
2. Meanwhile, skin the kidneys, cut them into thin slices, and add them to the roasted onions.
3. Dust with some flour, salt and pepper them, stir well together, roast for 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Pour broth or white wine over them, add a pinch of marjoram, stir everything well together and serve in a bowl.
