## Ingredients

- 7 egg yolks
- 14 dkg sugar
- 5 dkg breadcrumbs
- 4 dkg citron
- 8 dkg orange peel
- 2 ground cloves
- 2 allspice berries
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 7 stiff egg whites
- 14 dkg grated almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 7 egg yolks with 14 dkg sugar for 20 minutes
2. Mix in 5 dkg breadcrumbs, 4 dkg citron, 8 dkg orange peel (finely cut), 2 ground cloves, 2 allspice berries, 1 pinch cinnamon, and lemon zest
3. Fold in 7 stiff egg whites together with 14 dkg grated almonds
4. Fill the batter into the torte form and bake
5. Turn out and cut through
6. Spread with jam, dust with sugar or frost white, and decorate with half swelled almonds and angelica
