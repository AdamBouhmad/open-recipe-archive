## Ingredients

- 5 bitter almonds
- 20 sweet almonds, blanched
- 1/2 liter cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 deca sugar
- 8 deca dissolved warm isinglass
- 1/3 liter cold water
- 1/8 liter reduction
- 2 deca dissolved gelatin
- Coffee Cream with Gelatin No. 1090
- 1/2 liter whipping cream

## Directions

1. Pound 5 bitter and 20 sweet blanched almonds very finely
2. Cook with 1/2 liter cream and press through a cloth
3. Stir with 5 egg yolks and 10 deca sugar over the fire until thick
4. Pass the mass through a hair sieve into a deep bowl
5. Add 8 deca dissolved warm isinglass (prepared with 1/3 liter cold water and reduced to 1/8 liter) or 2 deca dissolved gelatin (Coffee Cream with Gelatin No. 1090) to the cream
6. Stir until it begins to thicken and is completely cold
7. 1 hour earlier, place 1/2 liter whipping cream in a snow bucket on ice
8. Beat the cream to stiff foam and fold in the cream
9. Coat a desired mold with fine oil
10. Bury in ice and fill with the prepared cream
11. Let stand several hours
12. Shortly before serving, dip the mold briefly in hot water, wipe dry quickly and turn out onto a platter
13. Garnish the cream with meringues or Spanish wind
