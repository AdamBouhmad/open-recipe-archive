## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 7 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 1 spoon wine or rum
- 1 spoon cream
- hot lard
- sugar
- Chaudeau or raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from the flour, butter, egg yolks, salt, wine or rum, and cream.
2. Roll it out thinly and cut it into three- or four-sided pieces.
3. Let them rest covered for 30 minutes.
4. Bake them in hot lard, let them drain.
5. Sprinkle them with sugar and serve them still warm with Chaudeau or raspberry juice.
