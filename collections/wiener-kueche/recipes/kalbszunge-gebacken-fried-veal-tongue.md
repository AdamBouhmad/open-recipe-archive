## Ingredients

- 1 veal tongue
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- lard for frying
- spinach, cooked lettuce, or head lettuce for serving

## Directions

1. Wash the tongue, place in cold salt water and cook 1-2 hours.
2. While still warm, peel off the skin.
3. Slice the tongue, salt it, bread it, fry in hot lard.
4. Serve with spinach, cooked lettuce, or head lettuce.
5. Alternatively, cook and slice the tongue and place in finished Polish sauce (see Menu 40: Karpfen schwarz) and serve as an evening dish.
