## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 14 dkg sugar
- 12 dkg raw grated almonds
- 8 egg whites
- 14 dkg grated chocolate
- 5 dkg rum-moistened bread or roll crumbs
- jam
- optional: mulled wine

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 14 dkg sugar for 20 minutes
2. Mix in 12 dkg raw grated almonds
3. Fold in 8 stiff egg whites together with 14 dkg grated chocolate and 5 dkg rum-moistened bread or roll crumbs
4. Fill the mixture into the form and bake
5. Brush inside and outside with jam and brown glaze, or fill only inside and pour warm mulled wine over it
