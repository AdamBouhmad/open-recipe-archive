## Ingredients

- One seidel of good butter
- Crumbs of one and a half semmel (rolls)
- 4 loths of swollen, finely pounded almonds
- 4 loths of butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 loths of citronat, cut into large cubes
- Preserved green walnuts
- Green prunes
- Pitted morello cherries
- Almonds (of all these equal parts)
- Egg whites
- Good rum

## Directions

1. Pour one seidel of good butter over the crumbs of one and a half semmel
2. Heat on low coals with 4 loths of swollen, finely pounded almonds until it boils, then let it cool
3. Beat 4 loths of butter, 2 whole eggs, and 3 egg yolks gradually until fluffy and stir in
4. Add cut into large cubes: 3 loths of citronat, preserved green walnuts, green prunes, pitted morello cherries, and almonds in equal parts and in such quantity that little of the semmel is visible
5. Beat the whites of the 3 eggs to stiff peaks and fold in so nothing is visible
6. Take a mold with a thick post in its center, brush with butter, fill with pudding, and place in steam for one hour
7. When serving, turn it out; in the moment it is brought to the table, fill the opening with good rum, place some pieces of sugar in it, light it, and bring it flaming to the table
8. The bottom of the mold should be artfully lined with citronat cut into half moons and swollen, split almonds
