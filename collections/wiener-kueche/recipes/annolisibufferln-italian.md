## Ingredients

- 28 deca pignoli
- 2 egg whites
- 16 deca fine sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 28 deca pignoli with 2 egg whites
2. Add 16 deca fine sifted sugar and pound everything together into a dough
3. Using a teaspoon or piping bag, place small mounds on a baking sheet lined with wax paper
4. Sprinkle with fine pounded sugar
5. Dry in a cool oven
