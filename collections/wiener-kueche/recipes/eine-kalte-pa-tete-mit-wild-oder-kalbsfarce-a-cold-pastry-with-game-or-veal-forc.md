## Ingredients

- butter
- water
- flour
- eggs
- salt
- venison or hare
- bacon
- anchovies
- pepper
- shallots
- onions
- lemon peel
- red wine
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of butter in half a measure of water
2. Make a dough from 4 pounds of flour with this mixture and 2 to 3 eggs on a board and salt it
3. The dough must be as firm as possible, so all the water may not be necessary depending on flour quality
4. When the dough is like bread dough, i.e., very fine, wrap in cloth and let it rest
5. Meanwhile make a fine forcemeat from the flesh of a venison loin or freshly shot hare, a quarter pound of bacon, the same amount of cleaned anchovies, pepper, shallots, onions, and lemon peel
6. The forcemeat must be well strained, add a glass of red wine and 4 to 5 egg yolks
