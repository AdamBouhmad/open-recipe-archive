## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Butter
- Flour
- Dried peas, beans, or bran (for filling)
- Beaten eggs
- Salmi of game or other meat filling (optional)
- Sautéed vegetables, rice, macaroni, or sauce (for filling)

## Directions

1. Brush a casserole or mold with butter and dust with flour
2. Cut a square of finger-thick puff dough to fit the size of the casserole
3. Fold the four corners lightly together in the middle and place in the casserole
4. Spread the four corners apart over the sides of the casserole
5. Distribute the dough in the casserole so it forms no wrinkles and trim any excess hanging over the edge
6. Fill the inner empty space with dried uncooked peas, beans, or bran and cover with a finger-thick dough disc fitted to the size of the casserole
7. Make several cuts in the dough cover with a knife
8. Place in the oven and bake at fairly strong heat for approximately 1 hour until the dough is golden yellow
9. Remove the casserole and loosen the dough cover around the edges
10. Place the dough cover on a plate, cover the casserole with a spoon, and holding both together firmly, invert the casserole quickly
11. Let stand for a few moments, then carefully remove the casserole and let the timbale cool slightly
12. Carefully place in a bowl, remove any remaining peas or beans with a spoon or fork
13. Fill as desired: brush with hot game salmi and garnish with sautéed game or other meat, or fill with sautéed cabbage mixed with meat, sautéed rice, macaroni, or hare or partridge salmi
14. Cover again with the lid and serve hot at the table
