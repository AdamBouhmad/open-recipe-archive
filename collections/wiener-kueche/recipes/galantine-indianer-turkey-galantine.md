## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- Stuffing (triple the amount needed for a normal stuffed turkey)
- Cold pâté stuffing (optional)

## Directions

1. The treatment is exactly the same as described in recipe 854 'Galantine von Poularbe', with the difference that for a turkey you need triple the amount of stuffing.
2. Alternatively, the turkey can also be filled with cold pâté stuffing and served as a galantine.
