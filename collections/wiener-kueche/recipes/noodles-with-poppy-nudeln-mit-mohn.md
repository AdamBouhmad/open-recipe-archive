## Ingredients

- noodles prepared as faded noodles (No. 1201)
- 1/8 liter of finely ground poppy seed
- sugar as needed
- honey as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare noodles as described in faded noodles (No. 1201)
2. Mix 1/8 liter of finely ground poppy seed with the necessary sugar
3. Stir the noodles with honey as desired
4. Arrange heaped on a platter and serve hot
