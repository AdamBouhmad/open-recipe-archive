## Ingredients

- ½ kg dried or ½ kg fresh plums
- 10-12 pieces of cube sugar
- Water
- Butter (walnut-sized)
- 1 tablespoon Cinnamon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Wash plums, remove pits
2. Cook with cube sugar and enough water to cover, covered, stirring occasionally, for 30-45 minutes
3. In a pot, mix melted butter with cinnamon and flour until smooth
4. Add to plums and cook for 10 minutes
5. Serve warm
