## Ingredients

- plums
- stems cut short
- lemon juice
- 60 decilitres sugar
- 1 litre water

## Directions

1. Cut the stems of the plums somewhat short
2. Peel the plums and rub with lemon juice
3. Treat as the preceding fruit
4. Only let them cook a moment
5. Use 60 decilitres sugar in 1 litre water for the sugar water
