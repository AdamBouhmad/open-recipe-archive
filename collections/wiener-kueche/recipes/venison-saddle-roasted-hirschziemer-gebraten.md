## Ingredients

- venison saddle
- brine/marinade
- bacon
- butter
- capers

## Directions

1. Wash the venison saddle and remove the skin
2. Pound it until it becomes tender, salt it well, and let it lie in brine for two or three days
3. Roast it in a roasting pan in the oven, cover it with a paper brushed with fat so the bacon does not burn, and pour brine over it frequently
4. When well roasted, place it on a dish and pour caper sauce over it
