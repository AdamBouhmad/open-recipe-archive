## Ingredients

- Wild duck
- Bacon or marrow
- Onion
- Root vegetables
- 5 crushed juniper berries
- 5 grains each type of spice
- 5 sprigs of thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- Bacon slices
- Black bread (8 teaspoons, thinly cut)

## Directions

1. Let the duck hang in its feathers for 2-3 days until tender, but not longer to avoid bad odor
2. Place some bacon or marrow, onion, and root vegetables (all thinly cut) in a casserole
3. Add 5 crushed juniper berries, 5 grains each of spices, 5 sprigs of thyme, and 1 bay leaf
4. Place the dressed duck on top
5. Cover with bacon slices
6. Let roast covered
7. Then add thinly cut black bread
