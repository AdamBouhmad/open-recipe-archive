## Ingredients

- 32 deka flour
- 12 deka butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/8 liter sour cream
- 2 deka sugar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put 32 deka flour on a strudel board, cut 12 deka butter into it, and work this with the flour
2. Make a hollow, crack 3 egg yolks into it, add 1/8 liter sour cream, 2 deka sugar, and some salt
3. Work this into a dough until blisters become visible, then let it rest for an hour in a cool place
4. Roll it out knife-back thick, cut from it finger-long and finger-wide strips, whose both ends are cut at an angle
5. Six such strips, of which the two lowest are the longest, the two middle ones somewhat shorter, and the two topmost the shortest, are now laid cross-wise over each other so that the resulting figure forms a star
6. Each dough strip is dotted in the middle with beaten eggs before the next one is laid on it, which makes one strip hold the other; on the last, topmost strip, press strongly in the middle with the finger so that a hollow forms
7. These star fritters are baked in hot fat until pale yellow, shaking the saucepan somewhat; then they are sprinkled with vanilla or desired spice sugar, and in each hollow something put in the middle of each groove
8. Some marmalade is put and served this way. You can also serve sauce with it
