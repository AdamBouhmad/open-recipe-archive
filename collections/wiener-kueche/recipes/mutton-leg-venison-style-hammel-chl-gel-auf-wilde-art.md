## Ingredients

- Fat and tender mutton leg
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Wine
- Onions
- Coriander
- Lemon
- Butter
- Pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Take a fat and tender mutton leg and beat it well
2. Skin it like venison
3. Lard it
4. Marinate it for several days in vinegar, salt, pepper, nutmeg, wine, onions, coriander, and a lemon with the peel cut off
5. If you need it immediately, heat the marinade and pour it hot over the meat
6. Skewer the mutton leg
7. Baste it with the same marinade and butter which you place in the roasting pan
8. Roast it on the platter
9. Serve it with a pepper sauce or with the drippings from the roasting pan, from which the fat must be skimmed
