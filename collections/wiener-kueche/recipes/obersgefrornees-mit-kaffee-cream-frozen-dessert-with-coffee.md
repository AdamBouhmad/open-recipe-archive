## Ingredients

- 16 deca fine roasted coffee
- 1 liter boiling cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 24 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Roast coffee until light brown
2. Pour hot into 1 liter boiling cream and cover
3. Let stand 1/4 hour
4. Place egg yolks and sugar in a snow-beater
5. Beat with whisk, gradually adding the strained cream
6. Beat over fire until it begins to thicken, but do not boil
7. Remove from fire and beat until lukewarm
8. Pass through a hair sieve
9. When completely cooled, fill into the freezing box
