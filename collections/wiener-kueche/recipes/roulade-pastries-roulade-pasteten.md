## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Eggs
- Filling (not specified)

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and place the filling in the middle of strips brushed with beaten eggs
2. Fold the dough finger-width overlapping, with a cut side facing up
3. Brush the roulades with beaten eggs, leaving a knife-back-width dry border where the dough overlaps
4. Place on a water-moistened baking sheet with the top edge cut-side facing the back wall of the oven
5. Bake in the oven at fairly strong heat until golden yellow
6. Remove with a knife from the sheet, arrange in a bowl, and serve warm as an appetizer, side dish, or for supper or tea
