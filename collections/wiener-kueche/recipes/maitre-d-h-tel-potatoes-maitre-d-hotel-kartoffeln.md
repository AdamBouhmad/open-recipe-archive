## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- 6 dkg hot butter
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and cut them nicely
2. Place them in cold salted water
3. Cook for 20 minutes and drain
4. In a saucepan, let finely chopped parsley run in 6 dkg hot butter
5. Pour in the potatoes and toss them in the butter
