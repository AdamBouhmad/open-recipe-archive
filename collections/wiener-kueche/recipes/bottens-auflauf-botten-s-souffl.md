## Ingredients

- mass from baked mushrooms recipe
- 12 teaspoons sugar
- 1/4 liter milk
- egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. From the same mass as given for 'Baked Mushrooms' (No. 1380), make croquettes and cut them fine
2. In a deep bowl, add 12 teaspoons of sugar, pour in 1/4 liter milk mixed with egg yolks with constant stirring and mix well with the cut croquettes
3. Finally, lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
4. Fill a butter-greased, flour-dusted form with the above mass and cook covered in a water bath for 3/4 to 1 hour
5. After this time, remove the form, wipe it, cover with a plate, turn it out and carefully remove the form
6. Serve the soufflé with desired chaudeau, fruit juice or cream
