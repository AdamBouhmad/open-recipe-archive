## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 pound carp, cleaned
- smoked salmon, half the amount of carp
- semmel (roll), finely diced
- 1 sidel (about 0.5 liter) white wine
- onions, one handful
- rocambole (shallot-like plant)
- butter
- sausage herbs, finely pounded
- sardelles (anchovies), cleaned (one-half pound)
- capers (3 drams)
- lemon juice
- pork intestines
- paper casings
- butter

## Directions

1. Properly clean a 3 to 4 pound carp and cut out the fine good flesh
2. Cut half the amount of flesh from a smoked salmon
3. Finely dice one semmel and mix together with a sidel of white wine
4. Soften one handful of onions and some rocambole in butter and add to the above
5. Add finely pounded sausage herbs
6. Finely chop one-half pound of cleaned sardelles and 3 drams of capers together
7. Mix with the juice of one lemon under the rest
8. Fill the mixture into pork intestines, but not too firmly, otherwise they will burst
9. Divide into desired pieces and tie with twine
10. Place in paper casings brushed with butter
11. Roast slowly on a grill
