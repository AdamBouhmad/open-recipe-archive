## Ingredients

- One pound very fine sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- Zest of one lemon
- Juice of half a lemon
- Whites of 14 eggs
- 28 Loth fine flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. In an earthen dish, stir one pound very fine sugar with 16 egg yolks and the zest of one lemon for a good hour
2. Press the juice of half a lemon and stir it for another quarter hour
3. Beat the whites of 14 eggs into stiff snow and fold slowly into the mixture
4. Finally stir in 28 Loth fine flour, brush a regular torte pan lightly with butter, and fill in the mixture
5. The pan should only be half full and must be baked very slowly
6. When the torte rises two finger-widths above the pan and has some cracks on top, it is done
