## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilo sugar cut into small pieces
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 sweet oranges
- 1/4 liter rum
- 2 drachms Russian tea
- 1 liter boiling water

## Directions

1. Place the sugar cut into small pieces with the juice of 1 lemon and 4 sweet oranges and 1/4 liter rum in a porcelain bowl
2. Let it stand covered in a warm place
3. In a second porcelain bowl, place 2 drachms Russian tea and pour 1 liter of boiling water over it
4. After 15 minutes, pour this to the dissolved sugar, stir well and serve immediately
5. A bottle of rum is placed on the table
6. Alternatively: place the very thinly cut peel of 1 orange and 1/2 kilo sugar that has been boiled in 1 liter water in a porcelain bowl, pour the syrup over it and cover
7. Press the juice from 5 oranges and 2 lemons, remove the peel, add the juice, add 1/4 liter rum and the same amount of tea, then serve the punch hot
