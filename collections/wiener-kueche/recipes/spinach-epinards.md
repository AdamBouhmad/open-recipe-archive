## Ingredients

- 8 handfuls fresh, cleaned spinach
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 8 drams butter
- 8 drams flour
- Small, finely cut rolls
- Garlic, crushed (if desired)
- ½ liter broth
- Salt
- Pepper (if desired)

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned spinach in boiling salt water; cook it uncovered so it retains its green color; drain it well, place it in cold water, press it thoroughly, and chop it finely.
2. Make a roux from 8 drams butter and 8 drams flour; stir in some finely cut rolls and, if desired, a little crushed garlic.
3. Add the spinach, pour ½ liter broth over it; season to taste, stir, and let it cook until it boils.
