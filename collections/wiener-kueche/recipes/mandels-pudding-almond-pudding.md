## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter, beaten
- 16 deca sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 18 deca blanched almonds, finely pounded with 2 egg whites
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter with 16 deca sugar and 8 egg yolks for 1 hour
2. Add 18 deca blanched almonds finely pounded with 2 egg whites
3. Mix well
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
6. Steam covered for 1 hour
7. Serve with desired fruit juice
