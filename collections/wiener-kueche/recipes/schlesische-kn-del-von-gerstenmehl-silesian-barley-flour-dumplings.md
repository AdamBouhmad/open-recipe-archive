## Ingredients

- Barley flour
- Salt
- Cold water
- Browned lard or cracklings
- Lard or onion

## Directions

1. Make a soft dough from barley flour, salt, and cold water, as for rolls.
2. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a finger and sprinkle it generously with browned lard or cracklings.
3. Roll it up like a strudel and cut it into pieces 3 fingers wide.
4. Cook the pieces in salted water.
5. Sprinkle with fried, finely diced lard or, if desired, with fried onion.
6. Serve with boiled meat or sauerkraut.
