## Ingredients

- 1/6 liter cold water
- 36 deca grams sugar
- Juice of 4 oranges
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Yellow zest of 2 oranges, finely cut

## Directions

1. Dissolve 36 deca grams sugar in 1/6 liter cold water
2. Add the juice of 4 oranges and 1 lemon to this
3. Add the yellow zest of 2 oranges, finely cut with a sharp knife
4. Let this liquid sit covered for about 1 hour
5. Weigh the mass to 22 degrees, pass it through a sieve, and proceed further as with the 'Ordinary Strawberry Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1746)
