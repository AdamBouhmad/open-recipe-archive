## Ingredients

- beautiful pineapple
- pineapple skin
- 60 decilitres sugar
- cold sugar water

## Directions

1. Peel a beautiful pineapple and place the skin immediately in preserving jars
2. Cut the pineapple into slices knife-back thick or as desired and place also in preserving jars
3. Pour cold sugar water (of 60 decilitres sugar) over both the pineapple and the skin so that it covers them and only a small finger-height space remains empty in the jar
4. Bind and cook as the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842) for 3-4 hours in steam
