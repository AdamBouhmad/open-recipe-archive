## Ingredients

- same base as white bread knödel
- 1 pound cooked and sieved or diced calf brains
- some white pepper
- 3 deca white bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the base mixture as for white bread knödel.
2. Boil the calf brains in salted water, sieve or cut into pieces, and stir into the mixture.
3. Add white pepper and white bread crumbs.
4. Form and cook knödel as directed in the preceding recipe.
