## Ingredients

- 8 Deca butter
- finely cut mushrooms
- parsley, finely cut
- 8 Deca flour
- soup stock as needed
- juice of one lemon
- white wine, if desired
- salt
- pepper
- finely cut, cooked or braised veal meat, approximately one plate full
- puff pastry, as desired
- eggs à la Minute or fried eggs
- bread slices, fried in butter

## Directions

1. In butter, roast some finely cut mushrooms and parsley equally
2. Dust this with 8 Deca flour and let it roast
3. Pour the necessary soup stock on it
4. Add the juice of one lemon and some white wine if desired
5. Salt and season it
6. Stir it well together and let it cook together with the finely cut, cooked or braised veal meat, approximately one plate full
7. Arrange it on a serving dish and garnish it with puff pastry prepared as desired and eggs à la Minute or fried eggs and bread slices fried in butter
8. Pour the defatted juice over it
