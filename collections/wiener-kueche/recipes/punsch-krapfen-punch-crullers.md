## Ingredients

- Spanish biscuit base
- punch-dipped biscuit pieces
- punch preserve glaze

## Directions

1. Prepare as indicated for Indian Crullers (recipe 1632), only with Punch Crullers the hollowed out Spanish biscuit is filled with a smaller piece of biscuit dipped in punch
2. Glaze them with punch preserve glaze instead of chocolate glaze
