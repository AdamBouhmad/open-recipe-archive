## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls
- 0.25 liter strong black coffee
- 16 decagrams whipped butter
- 16 decagrams finely pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 14 decagrams almonds with skins, finely pounded and passed
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold
- cream or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soften 16 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls in 0.25 liter strong black coffee
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Beat 16 decagrams whipped butter with 16 decagrams finely pounded sugar and 6 egg yolks gradually
4. Add 14 decagrams almonds with skins, finely pounded and passed, with the coffee bread
5. Mix well
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
7. Fill a buttered mold dusted with flour
8. Bake covered in steam for 0.75 hour
9. Turn into a dish and serve with whipped cream or cream
