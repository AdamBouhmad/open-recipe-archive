## Ingredients

- one Groſchen worth (or somewhat more) of grated stag's horn
- 2 Maß of the same beer

## Directions

1. For a properly fermented barrel of beer that is several days old, take grated stag's horn
2. Boil it several times with 2 Maß of the same beer
3. Fill it warm and carefully into the barrel
4. Let it rest undisturbed overnight
5. Fill the beer into bottles and it will be clear as ruby
