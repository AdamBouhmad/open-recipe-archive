## Ingredients

- Cauliflower, soft-cooked in salt water
- Egg or bread batter
- Hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Divide the soft-cooked cauliflower into small rosettes
2. Dip them in egg or bread batter
3. Fry them in very hot lard until golden
4. Place on blotting paper and serve hot on a platter arranged in a heap
