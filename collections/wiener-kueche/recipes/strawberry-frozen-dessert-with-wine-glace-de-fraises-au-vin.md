## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter passed strawberry puree
- 56 deca grams sugar
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 liter good white wine

## Directions

1. Stir 56 deca grams sugar into the 1/2 liter strawberry puree
2. Mix in the juice of 1 lemon and 1/2 liter white wine
3. Fill this liquid into the freezing container and proceed as with the 'Family Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1720)
4. Serve in goblets
