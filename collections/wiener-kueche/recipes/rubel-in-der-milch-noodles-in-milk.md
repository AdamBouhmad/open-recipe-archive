## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 liter boiling milk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- sugar for sprinkling
- cinnamon for sprinkling (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare noodles from 1/2 liter flour and 2 whole eggs as described for Abgeschmalzene Rubel (No. 1201)
2. Add them to 1 liter boiling milk while stirring constantly
3. Cook them thickly with 8 tablespoons butter and the same amount of sugar
4. Arrange on a platter, sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and optionally with cinnamon, and serve
5. If the noodles have cooked down too much, thin with boiling milk as needed
