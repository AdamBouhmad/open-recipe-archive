## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 14 deca lemon sugar
- 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks
- 14 deca blanched almonds finely pounded with 1 egg white
- 14 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy
2. Gradually add 14 deca lemon sugar, 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks
3. Mix in blanched almonds finely pounded with egg white and flour, working into a dough on a board
4. Let rest covered in a cool place for approximately 2 hours
5. Roll out dough to finger thickness and cut according to the size of the baking sheet
6. Roll up the dough on a noodle roller and unroll on a butter-greased baking sheet
7. Brush with beaten eggs and bake pale yellow at low heat in the oven
8. Cut the baked product into rows with a round cutter, layering them 2 by 2 with boiled jam between each
