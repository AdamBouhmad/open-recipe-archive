## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons ground almonds
- 8 tablespoons raisins
- 1 clove spice
- 8 tablespoons currants
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 1/2 liters wine
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour mixed with 4 tablespoons breadcrumbs
- optional: 4 tablespoons black bread or equal amount rose hips
- optional: 2 tablespoons raisins, equal amount rose hips, and equal amount cooked young turnips diced finely

## Directions

1. Put ground almonds, raisins, 1 clove, currants, and cinnamon in a saucepan
2. Pour in 1 1/2 liters wine and let it boil thoroughly
3. Meanwhile put 6 tablespoons butter in a saucepan and let it become hot
4. Add 4 tablespoons sugar to this
5. When this becomes brown, let 4 tablespoons flour mixed with 4 tablespoons breadcrumbs roast in it
6. Add what was cooked with the wine, stir it well together and serve this sauce with freshly formed veal cutlets and also with sliced fish
7. You can also cook 4 tablespoons black bread or equal amount rose hips with it, and if desired, also 2 tablespoons raisins, equal amount rose hips, and equal amount cooked, finely diced young turnips and serve them with it
