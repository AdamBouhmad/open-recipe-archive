## Ingredients

- 20 h spring herbs (chervil, blessed thistle, ground ivy, sorrel, spinach, yarrow)
- 6 dkg butter
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the herbs
2. Place in a pot with cold salted water and cook for 20 minutes
3. Strain and chop finely
4. Make a roux with 6 dkg butter, pour on beef broth and add the herbs
5. If desired, thicken lightly and serve with bread slices
