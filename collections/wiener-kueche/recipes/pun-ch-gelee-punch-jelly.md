## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound of hartshorn
- 2 measures of water
- half a bottle of arrack
- three-quarters pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare like lemon jelly, but set the three-quarters pound of hartshorn with 2 measures of water instead of 3 halves to obtain more liquid to replace the wine missing here
2. When the jelly comes from the fire, pour in half a bottle of arrack
3. Use three-quarters pound of sugar instead of half a pound
