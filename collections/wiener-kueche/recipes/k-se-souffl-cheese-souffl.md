## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 16 Deca flour
- 10 Deca butter
- 1/2 liter cream
- some salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 10 Deca grated Parmesan cheese
- 9 egg whites (snow)

## Directions

1. Put 3 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, 16 Deca flour, 10 Deca butter, 1/2 liter cream and some salt into a saucepan and stir over the fire until it has turned into a thick mass, then empty it into a deep bowl
2. After it has cooled, stir in gradually 6 egg yolks and 10 Deca grated Parmesan cheese and finally fold in lightly the snow from 9 egg whites
3. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mass, which is then baked for 1/4 hours in the oven
4. This soufflé is served at table immediately, sprinkled with grated Parmesan cheese
5. This mass can also be baked in paper cups and served in these
