## Ingredients

- Sour cream (Schmette)
- Butter, in small pieces
- Anchovies, finely chopped and boned
- Eggs, as many as needed
- Salt

## Directions

1. Coat a dish with sour cream.
2. Add small pieces of butter and finely chopped, boned anchovies, and let the butter melt.
3. Break eggs into the dish so they remain whole, as many as needed.
4. Salt them and let them come to a simmer.
