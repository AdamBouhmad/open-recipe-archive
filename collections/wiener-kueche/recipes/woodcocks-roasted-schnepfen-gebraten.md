## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- entrails (intestines and liver)
- onion
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean the woodcocks by plucking and singeing them
2. Remove the intestines and liver, salt them, and dress them well
3. Bend the feet and wings, pass the beak through the right wing
4. Roast them on a spit
5. Finely chop the intestines and liver together with some onion, toast some breadcrumbs in butter, add the chopped mixture, and cook it briefly
6. Fry some thin slices of bread in lard, place them on a dish, spread the woodcock paste on top, and serve with the roasted woodcocks on the table
