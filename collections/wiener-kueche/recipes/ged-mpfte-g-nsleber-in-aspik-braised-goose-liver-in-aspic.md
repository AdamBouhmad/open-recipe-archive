## Ingredients

- goose liver
- aspic
- truffles
- tongue
- hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. When the goose liver is braised, let it stand covered in the casserole until the liver is completely cold.
2. Remove and trim away the fat, wipe clean with a cloth.
3. Cut into strips the thickness of a knife blade and of any desired width.
4. Place a form in ice and pour a finger-high layer of cooled aspic into it.
5. When this has set, decorate with truffles, nicely cut-out tongue, hard-boiled egg white, and lettuce leaves.
6. Add some more aspic and when set, add the goose liver pieces.
7. Continue this way with aspic and liver pieces until the form is full, with aspic on top.
8. After this has stood 1 hour in ice, treat it as the "goose liver cake" with aspic.
