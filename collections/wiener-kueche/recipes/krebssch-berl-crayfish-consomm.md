## Ingredients

- 14 Deca crayfish butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 20 Deca diced bread rolls soaked in milk
- Claws and tails from 10 crayfish
- Snow from 6 eggs
- Green peas soaked in crayfish water (optional, 1/8 liter)

## Directions

1. Beat 14 Deca crayfish butter until fluffy, gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
2. Add 20 Deca diced bread rolls soaked in milk and the extracted claws and tails from 10 crayfish
3. Stir everything well together and lightly mix in the snow from 6 eggs
4. Can optionally mix in 1/8 liter green peas soaked in crayfish water
5. This schöberl can be steamed or baked and then added to the soup
