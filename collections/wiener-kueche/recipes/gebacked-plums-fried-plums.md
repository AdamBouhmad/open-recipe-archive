## Ingredients

- Softly cooked dried plums
- Blanched almond kernels
- Egg or egg batter
- Fat
- Sugar
- Optional: grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Place softly cooked dried plums on a cloth and dry well
2. Remove the pits and insert blanched almond kernels in their place
3. Dip in egg batter or egg and flour batter
4. Prepare as for Gebacked Apple Slices (recipe 1169)
5. When fried, they may be rolled in grated chocolate mixed with finely ground sugar, after which they are called Schlosserbubchen
