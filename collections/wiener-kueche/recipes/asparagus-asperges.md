## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- butter
- bread crumbs
- Maitre Sauce

## Directions

1. When cleaning asparagus, hold the stalk in your hand and use a knife to remove the thin, closely attached, scale-like leaves from the top downward
2. Lightly scrape the stem for over half its length from the top
3. Lay it in cold water
4. Tie the asparagus into bundles and cut the bottom even
5. Place it in boiling salted water and let it cook soft
6. Lay a napkin on a platter and arrange the asparagus on it
7. Serve hot butter with toasted bread crumbs in one sauce boat and Maitre Sauce in another
8. If placing the asparagus on an uncovered platter, pour the butter sauce or butter with bread crumbs over it
9. If two bundles are cooked, arrange them on the platter so the heads face each other and the stems lie outward at the edge of the platter
