## Ingredients

- 1 pound young pork
- small piece of bacon
- handful of washed rice
- salt
- ground pepper
- 2 whole eggs
- sauerkraut leaves
- thin light yellow roux (made from fat and flour)
- onion, finely cut
- half a measure of sour cream
- ham, sliced

## Directions

1. Chop together 1 pound young pork and a small piece of bacon
2. Place in a mixing bowl and add a handful of washed rice, some salt, ground pepper, and 2 whole eggs
3. Mix everything well together
4. Remove the ribs from the sauerkraut leaves
5. Fill the leaves with the meat mixture
6. Roll up tightly together
7. Place in a pot with finely cut sauerkraut and set to cook for a couple of hours
8. Make a thin light yellow roux, add finely cut onion and let it brown slightly
9. Add the sauerkraut to the roux and let it cook for at least another hour
10. Before serving, add half a measure of sour cream
11. Serve garnished with the stuffed rolls and sliced ham on the side
