## Ingredients

- fresh, fine green kidney beans
- salt water
- fresh butter
- green parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the fresh, fine green kidney beans as described, cook tender in salt water
2. Drain well and arrange raised on a platter
3. A piece of fresh butter mixed with finely chopped green parsley and some salt is placed on the hot kidney beans in pieces and served
