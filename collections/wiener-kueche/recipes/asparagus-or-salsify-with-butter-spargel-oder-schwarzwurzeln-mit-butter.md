## Ingredients

- 1 bunch asparagus
- ½ kg salsify
- salt
- hot butter
- breadcrumbs
- vinegar
- oil
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the asparagus carefully toward the head, cut off the base and wash
2. Tie the asparagus plants loosely together
3. Place them in an asparagus pan or skillet in cold salted water
4. Cook for 20-30 minutes
5. They are done when they pierce easily with a knife
6. Lift them carefully with a skimmer onto a long platter
7. Pour over breadcrumbs browned in hot butter
8. Can also be cooled, served as a salad with vinegar and oil, or warm with Hollandaise sauce
9. Salsify is scraped, washed and quickly placed in milk water to keep white
10. Then cook like asparagus in salted water for 15-20 minutes
11. Serve with butter and breadcrumbs or as a salad
