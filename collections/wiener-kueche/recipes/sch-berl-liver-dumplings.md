## Ingredients

- 1½ white rolls
- 12 dkg beef liver
- 2 white rolls
- a small piece of finely chopped onion
- parsley
- 6 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak and squeeze out 1½ white rolls
2. Scrape the liver and pass through with the squeezed rolls, onion, and parsley
3. Mix with creamed butter, egg yolks, salt, and pepper
4. Lightly fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from 2 eggs
5. Fill into a dumplings pan and bake over low heat
6. Loosen, turn out, and cut into pieces
