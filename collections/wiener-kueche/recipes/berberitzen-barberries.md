## Ingredients

- beautiful red, ripe barberries (Seinschafl, Sauerberen, Essigberen)
- 60 decilitres sugar
- 1 kilo sugar
- 1 litre water

## Directions

1. Pluck beautiful red, ripe barberries from their stems
2. Treat further as the cherries (Nr. 1845), only use 1 kilo sugar for 1 litre water instead of 60 decilitres
