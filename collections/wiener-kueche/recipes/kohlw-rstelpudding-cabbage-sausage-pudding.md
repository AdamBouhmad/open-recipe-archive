## Ingredients

- 2 smaller cabbage heads
- 25 dkg salted pork
- 1/2 cup cream
- 10 dkg Parmesan
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the cabbage of cores and bad leaves, wash it, place in boiling salted water for 5 minutes, drain it, and lay the leaves one by one on a board.
2. For the filling: Mix 2 eggs with half the cream, salt, pepper, and the chopped ham.
3. Fill the cabbage leaves and roll them into sausages.
4. Butter and flour a pudding mold. Mix 2 eggs with the grated cheese.
5. Fill the mold with a portion of eggs and cheese, place cabbage sausages on top, then alternate layers of eggs with cheese and sausages until all is used.
6. Cook the pudding 1 hour in steam, loosen it, turn it out, and serve with hot butter and grated cheese.
