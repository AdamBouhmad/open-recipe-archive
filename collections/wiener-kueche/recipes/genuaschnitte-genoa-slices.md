## Ingredients

- 18 dkg butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 18 dkg sugar
- 1 tablespoon rum
- 18 dkg flour
- Jam for filling

## Directions

1. Beat butter until soft
2. Gradually add 4 whole eggs, sugar, and rum, stirring well
3. Gradually mix in flour by spoonfuls
4. Spread on buttered, floured sheet (not to the edges)
5. Bake at medium-high heat until light brown
6. While still hot, cut into rounds or squares
7. Cool, remove from sheet
8. Spread with jam, sandwich together
9. Ice or sprinkle with sugar as desired
