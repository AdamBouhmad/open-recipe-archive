## Ingredients

- Eel
- sage leaves
- bacon
- butter
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Kill the eel by driving a nail through its head and nailing it to a table
2. Cut the skin off around the head and pull it off the entire eel
3. Hold it on both sides over glowing coals until you can pull off any remaining skin
4. Salt and lard it completely from above with sage leaves and bacon interspersed
5. Roll it up into a round shape, cut off the head, and place the eel on a sheet or casserole lid
6. Pour butter over it and place it in an oven or baking oven
7. Roast it, basting it frequently with butter and lemon juice during cooking
8. When roasted, coat it with glaze or sprinkle with breadcrumbs as desired to give it color
9. Serve it whole on a dish with anchovy sauce poured over it
