## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg round potatoes
- 5 dkg butter
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- 2 spoons cream
- flour
- 1/2 kg topfen or powidl (plum butter)
- 12 dkg butter or fat for roasting
- breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a potato dough from the potatoes, 5 dkg butter, 2 eggs, salt, 2 spoons cream, and flour
2. Roll out thinly and cut into rounds
3. Place filling in the center of each round
4. Brush the edges with egg white and fold together, pressing to seal
5. Place the pockets in a pot of boiling salted water and cook slowly for 5–8 minutes
6. Remove and drain
7. Toss in a large pot with breadcrumbs roasted in 12 dkg hot butter or fat
8. Transfer to a round platter, dust with sugar, and serve warm
