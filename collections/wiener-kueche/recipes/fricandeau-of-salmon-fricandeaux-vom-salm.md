## Ingredients

- salmon
- bacon
- ham
- braise
- glace
- sauce royale
- thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the salmon thoroughly
2. Cut it into wide pieces
3. Lard with bacon and ham
4. Cook slowly in a braise
5. Brush each piece with glace
6. Cook a sauce royale very briefly
7. Pull some thick cream into the sauce
8. Arrange the sauce on a plate
9. Place the fricandeau in it and serve
