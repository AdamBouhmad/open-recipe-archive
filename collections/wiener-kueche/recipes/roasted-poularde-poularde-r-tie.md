## Ingredients

- poularde
- bacon

## Directions

1. After plucking, flambe the poularde and hold the feet especially in the flame so the scaly skin can be removed
2. The wings can be cut off entirely or the feathers removed from them
3. Open the poularde, clean it in cold water, dry it inside and out, and truss it like a pheasant
4. Lard the breast with fine bacon or cover with bacon slices
5. Wrap the poularde with a sheet of paper brushed with butter and bind with twine
6. Put the poularde on a spit and roast it at moderate heat for approximately 3/4 to 1 hour
7. Remove the paper and roast the poularde over bright fire for another 1/4 hour so it becomes a beautiful brown color
8. Remove it, cut the twine, pull it out, cut the poularde into desired pieces, arrange them in a bowl, cover with bacon slices and pour the defatted juice over it
9. Alternatively, the poularde can be placed in a pan with fat and roasted in the oven, basting frequently with its own juice
