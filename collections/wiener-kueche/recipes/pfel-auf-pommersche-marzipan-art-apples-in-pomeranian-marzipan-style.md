## Ingredients

- Good portion of black bread
- Finely crushed sugar
- Cinnamon
- Grated lemon peel
- One quarter pound butter
- Apple marmalade
- Large raisins
- Apricot marmalade
- Finely cut candied citron

## Directions

1. Grate a good portion of black bread and mix it with much finely crushed sugar, cinnamon and grated lemon peel.
2. Let one quarter pound butter melt in a round dish and sprinkle in as many bread crumbs that the cake is as high as a small finger.
3. Then a thick layer of apple marmalade is laid on top, into which large raisins, apricot marmalade, and finely cut candied citron are mixed, and afterwards this layer is covered equally thickly with the grated bread mentioned above.
4. Finally sprinkle it everywhere finely with melted butter and bake it three quarters of an hour slowly in the oven.
