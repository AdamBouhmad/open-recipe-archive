## Ingredients

- green beans
- salted water
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Trim the ends of the green beans and remove the strings.
2. Cut the beans diagonally into thin pieces.
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook uncovered for 25-30 minutes.
4. Drain and arrange.
5. Top with small pieces of fresh butter.
