## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Salt
- Pache or puree
- Goose liver
- Frog cutlet
- Cock's comb
- Bread slices in butter

## Directions

1. Put eggs with a spoon as needed into a casserole with boiling water and cook covered for 7 minutes.
2. After this time, remove them quickly, place in cold water and shell them.
3. Once done, place them again in salted, lukewarm water where they are then arranged slightly off-center in a bowl and garnished with pache or puree.
4. Or place the pache in the center of the bowl, garnish it with the eggs and place between each egg a piece of goose liver, or a frog cutlet, or a cock's comb, or bread slices roasted in butter.
