## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Scale and skin the salmon
2. Cut 2 knife-back thick round slices from the flesh
3. Lay these slices next to each other in a casserole brushed with butter
4. Dust with salt and pepper
5. Pour melted butter over them and cover with a round sheet of buttered paper
6. Shortly before serving, place on medium heat and fry 2 to 3 minutes
7. Turn each piece and fry the other side equally long
8. Arrange the slices in a circle on a platter
9. Put Hollandaise, Mustard, or Butter sauce with lemon juice in the center
