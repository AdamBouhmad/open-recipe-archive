## Ingredients

- 1 pound raw lean ham
- beautiful veal meat in equal amount to ham
- onions
- celery
- parsley roots
- 2 pounds butter
- extraction flour (wheat flour)
- white bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the pound of raw lean ham and equal amount of beautiful veal meat into thin slices, as well as onions, celery, and parsley roots, adjusting quantities as desired for larger or smaller amounts
2. Melt 2 pounds of butter, then stir in extraction flour with a wooden spoon so that it becomes neither stiff nor too thin, but can be stirred comfortably
3. Stir this on gentle heat until it begins to foam white
4. Stir in the aforementioned ham, veal meat and vegetables
5. Add first some white bouillon and stir it smooth (if too much bouillon is added at once to the sweated flour, it becomes lumpy)
6. Then pour in as much white bouillon as needed for the quantity of coulis desired
7. Cook on gentle heat from the side until it reaches proper thickness
8. During cooking, remove all rising fat and foam with a spoon
9. Pass through a sieve without pressing through with a spoon, otherwise it will take on a blackish appearance from the cooked meat and vegetables
