## Ingredients

- three-quarter pound butter
- egg yolks
- half a pound pounded almonds
- half a pound sifted sugar
- two lots pounded cinnamon
- three-quarter pound flour
- canned morello cherries, currants, etc.

## Directions

1. Stir the butter until it is quite foamy
2. Add egg yolks gradually
3. Add the pounded almonds, sifted sugar, and pounded cinnamon
4. Stir this mixture for a whole hour
5. Add three-quarter pound of flour
6. Brush a tart sheet with the rim
7. Make the bottom the thickness of half a knife back
8. Fill it with canned morello cherries, currants, etc.
