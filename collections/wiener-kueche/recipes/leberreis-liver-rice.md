## Ingredients

- 10 dkg liver and spleen together
- 2 bread rolls (Schneidemmels)
- a small piece of finely chopped onion
- 1½ bread rolls soaked in water
- 4 dkg butter or fat
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- a little marjoram
- 6 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and spleen, scrape them, and pass them through a sieve with a small piece of finely chopped onion and 1½ bread rolls soaked in water and well pressed out.
2. Mix the whole thing with a mixture of 4 dkg butter or fat, 1 egg, salt, pepper, chopped parsley, and a little marjoram.
3. Instead of breadcrumbs, use flour and press the mass through the reverse side of a grater into the broth.
