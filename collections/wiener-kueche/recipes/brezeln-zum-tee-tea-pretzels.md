## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 7 deca vanilla sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deca compressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm cream
- 32 deca flour

## Directions

1. Mix the beaten butter, vanilla sugar, and salt well together
2. Add 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks one after another, stirring them in
3. Add the compressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm cream and the flour to the beaten butter, stir well and beat the dough until fine
4. Let the dough rise covered in a warm place until it has risen somewhat
5. Place the dough on a flour-dusted board and form pretzels, rings, twists, or other desired shapes
6. Place these on a baking sheet covered with buttered paper
7. Let them rise lightly covered for some time in a warm place
8. Brush with beaten egg and bake slowly in a fairly hot oven
