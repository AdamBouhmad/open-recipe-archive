## Ingredients

- Cleaned beans
- Green parsley, finely cut
- Salt
- Wine vinegar
- Butter
- Flour
- Onion, finely cut
- Lemon peel
- Roasted bread rolls

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned beans very soft in a pot
2. Skim off the broth frequently
3. Add finely cut green parsley, salt, and a little wine vinegar
4. Give the soup the proper thickness with a roux made of butter, flour, and finely cut onion
5. Add a little lemon peel
6. Serve over roasted bread rolls
