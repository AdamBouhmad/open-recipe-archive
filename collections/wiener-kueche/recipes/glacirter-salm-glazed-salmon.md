## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Bacon
- Butter
- Fifteen small onions
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Coulis
- Bouillon
- A glass of white wine
- Juice of a lemon
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the salmon, skin it, and lard it beautifully with bacon, and season it well.
2. In a cassole, sweat in butter fifteen small onions, truffles, and mushrooms over charcoal until golden, let the salmon brown in it, then briefly bring to a boil with some coulis and bouillon.
3. Add a glass of white wine and the juice of a lemon.
4. Do not salt it strongly; when it is nicely dressed and glazed on the plate, pour the sauce over it.
