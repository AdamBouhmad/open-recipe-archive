## Ingredients

- 22 decagrams butter
- 1 tablet grated chocolate
- juice of 1 lemon
- snow from 1 egg white
- finely cut almonds
- fine pounded sugar
- wafers

## Directions

1. Stir together butter, grated chocolate, and lemon juice in a deep bowl
2. Mix lightly with the snow from 1 egg white
3. Using a coffee spoon, take out equal-sized pieces and release them with the handle of a second spoon in small heaps at finger-width distance on a baking sheet lined with wafers
4. Sprinkle with finely cut almonds and fine pounded sugar
5. Dry for approximately 2 hours in the oven at low heat
6. When completely dry, remove from oven, cut each piece off individually while keeping the wafer attached
