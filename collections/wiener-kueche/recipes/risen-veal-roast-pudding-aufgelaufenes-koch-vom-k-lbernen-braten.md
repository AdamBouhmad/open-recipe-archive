## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound butter
- one-quarter pound soaked and finely ground almonds
- two whole eggs
- eight egg yolks
- veal roast, baked
- crumb of one small roll soaked in milk
- marrow
- one-quarter pound finely sifted sugar
- lemon peel, finely cut

## Directions

1. Beat one-quarter pound butter fluffy in a mixing bowl
2. Stir in one-quarter pound soaked and finely ground almonds
3. Add two whole eggs and eight egg yolks, each well beaten
4. Cut the veal roast finely with a knife and pound it well with the crumb of a small roll soaked in milk in a mortar
5. Mix it under the above
6. Take about two kreuzer's worth of clean prepared and diced marrow
7. Finally add one-quarter pound finely sifted sugar and finely cut lemon peel
8. When everything is mixed, stir for a quarter hour
9. Coat a dish and mold with butter, put the pudding in, bake it slowly in a cake pan, sprinkle with sugar, and serve it
