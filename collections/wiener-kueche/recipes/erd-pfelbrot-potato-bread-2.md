## Ingredients

- 40 dkg flour
- salt
- 2 spoonfuls sugar
- 1/2 kg cooked, passed, still warm potatoes
- 1 egg
- 2 dkg yeast
- milk
- 10 dkg butter, cut into pieces
- 5 dkg raisins

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a bowl, add a pinch of salt, 2 spoonfuls sugar, 1/2 kg cooked, passed, still warm potatoes, 1 egg, the yeast and several spoonfuls of milk, and beat the dough until it releases from the spoon
2. On a board, knead it with 10 dkg cut-up butter until the butter is completely mixed
3. Knead in 5 dkg raisins and divide the dough into 2 parts
4. Shape each part into loaves, place them on a greased baking sheet near the stove to rise, brush with beaten egg yolk
