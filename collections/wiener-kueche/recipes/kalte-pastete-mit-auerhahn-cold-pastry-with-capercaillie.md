## Ingredients

- 1 capercaillie, cleaned and gutted
- Pepper
- Salt
- Crushed cloves
- Fragrant herbs
- Hard dough pastry case
- Pastry forcemeat
- Good raw ham

## Directions

1. Cut the capercaillie open along the back and remove the bones
2. Rub it overnight with pepper, salt, crushed cloves, and fragrant herbs
3. Fill a hard dough pastry case with forcemeat
4. Place the marinated capercaillie in the middle
5. Cover it completely with the forcemeat and top with thin slices of good raw ham
6. Bake with a properly made lid
7. The remaining bones and carcass can be used to make a jelly or aspic to serve with the pastry
