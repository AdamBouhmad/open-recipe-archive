## Ingredients

- 20 crayfish
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- sprigs of green parsley
- salt water
- 16 drams butter
- flour
- 8 drams rice
- 2 liters pea water or water
- 3 Madeira apples or 3 spoonfuls of Madeira marmalade (optional)
- salt
- 12 drams rice (cooked)
- crayfish tails
- 1/4 liter cooked green peas

## Directions

1. Place crayfish with caraway and green parsley in boiling salt water and simmer until they turn red
2. Remove and set aside the claws and tails in the soup pot
3. Pound the shells and fat in a mortar and steam them in a casserole over water
4. Press them through a sieve into another casserole
5. Heat the crayfish butter, add flour, and toast it
6. Add 8 drams of rice and pour in with 2 liters of pea water or water
7. Bring to a boil with constant stirring
8. If desired, add 3 Madeira apples torn apart or 3 spoonfuls of Madeira marmalade
9. Simmer slowly for 1 hour
10. Strain through a sieve into a casserole and season with salt
11. Serve with 12 drams of cooked rice, crayfish tails, and 1/4 liter of cooked green peas, or only with toasted semolina bread and crayfish tails
