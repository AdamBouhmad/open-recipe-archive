## Ingredients

- 20 dkg macaroni
- 4 dkg tongue or rolled ham
- 3 dkg Parmesan
- 5 dkg butter
- beef broth or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Wipe the noodles, cut them into small pieces, cook in boiling salted water for 30 minutes, stirring often, drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Make a butter roux with 5 dkg butter and thin it with cold beef broth or milk.
3. Add the noodles, chopped tongue, part of the grated cheese, and salt to the sauce and braise for a while.
4. The mixture must be thick.
5. Fill into buttered shells, sprinkle cheese on top and drizzle hot butter over it.
6. Place the shells on a baking sheet, bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes until golden, and serve on a plate with a folded napkin.
