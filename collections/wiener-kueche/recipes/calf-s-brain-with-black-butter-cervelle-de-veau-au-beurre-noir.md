## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the brain as in the vinegar recipe
2. Arrange around a dish and pour black butter over it
3. To prepare the black butter: stir butter over low heat until the rising foam disappears and the butter is chocolate brown
4. Mix the browned butter with necessary salt, some pepper and a little vinegar
5. Bring to a boil once and strain through a fine sieve over the brain
