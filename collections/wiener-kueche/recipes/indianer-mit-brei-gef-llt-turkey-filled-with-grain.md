## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- Grain stuffing (double the amount used for chicken)

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as in recipe 823 ('Hühner mit Brei gefüllt'), with the difference that for a turkey you must make double the amount of stuffing used for chicken.
