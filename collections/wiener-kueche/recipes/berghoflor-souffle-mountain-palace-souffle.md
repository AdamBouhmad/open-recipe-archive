## Ingredients

- 9 hectograms flour
- 1.5 liters boiling milk
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 hectograms sugar
- 9 egg whites
- butter
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Place 9 hectograms flour in a casserole, stir well with 1.5 liters boiling milk, then continue stirring over the fire until the mixture becomes thick
2. Transfer to a bowl and continue stirring until it is cool
3. Gradually add 12 egg yolks and 12 hectograms sugar and stir well for half an hour
4. Fold in the snow from 9 egg whites lightly, put the whole into a casserole coated with butter and let bake for 3/4 hour
5. However, before it is done, coat it with dissolved marmalade
6. Mix the snow from 3 egg whites with 12 hectograms sugar, place on the baked mixture, dust with sugar, and dry in the open oven
