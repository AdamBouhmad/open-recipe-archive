## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cherries
- 16 egg yolks
- strong glass Madeira wine
- whole cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 2 pounds cherries together with the stones quite small
2. Pass through a hair sieve
3. Beat 16 egg yolks in a pot
4. Add the cherry juice, a strong glass Madeira wine, a piece of whole cinnamon, and some sugar to taste
5. Let come to a boil once over charcoal fire with constant stirring, so it becomes a thick foam
