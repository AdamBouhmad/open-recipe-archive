## Ingredients

- flattened and salted beef roast pieces
- flour
- hot fat or lard
- fat or bacon
- 1 piece onion, sliced leafy
- finely chopped anchovy fillets
- parsley
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/4 liter broth

## Directions

1. Coat the flattened and salted beef roast pieces in flour
2. Quickly brown them on both sides in hot fat or lard
3. Meanwhile, add fat or bacon to a casserole
4. Add 1 piece of leafy sliced onion, finely chopped anchovy fillets, and parsley
5. Place the roast pieces (6 pieces) on top
6. Pour 1/2 liter cream and 1/4 liter broth over them
7. Braise covered until tender
8. Transfer to a serving dish
9. Thin the sauce if necessary, which can also be strained
10. Pour the sauce over the roast
