## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter rolled barley
- 2 liters water
- 12 Deca butter
- 0.25 liter sour cream
- Vinegar
- Salt
- 1 clove

## Directions

1. Boil the rolled barley in 2 liters water with 12 Deca butter for about 2 hours slowly
2. Sour with 0.25 liter sour cream and vinegar as needed
3. Add salt and 1 clove, and spices if desired
4. Simmer for some time
5. Stir well and pass through a sieve
6. The soup should have a pleasant sour taste
