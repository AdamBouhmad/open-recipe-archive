## Ingredients

- 64 Deka butter
- 64 Deka lemon sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- blanched spice cloves
- cinnamon
- white of 15 eggs
- 64 Deka flour
- butter for coating

## Directions

1. Cream 64 Deka butter until fluffy
2. Add 64 Deka lemon sugar and 20 egg yolks one after another
3. Stir in 1 teaspoon nutmeg, an equal amount of blanched spice cloves and cinnamon
4. Lightly mix in the whites of 15 eggs with 64 Deka flour
5. Stretch the hollow brügel (a rod that is wide at one end and tapers at the other) onto a spit and secure well with small wooden pegs
6. When the brügel is warmed through, brush it with paper coated with butter and secure this with twine, which is fastened at both ends of the wood at the ends
7. Make a bed of charcoal in the base of the spit
8. Place a long pan under the spit to catch dripping batter
9. Turn the spit over the fire until the paper is yellow, then drizzle with clarified butter
10. When it begins to foam, pour the dough mixture with a ladle while rotating the spit evenly until the form is evenly covered
11. Continue rotating the spit evenly so the batter forms in a point
12. Let the poured batter turn yellow, then pour the same amount of batter again
13. Repeat this process until all batter is used
14. When done, cut the cake evenly at both ends, loosen the twine, remove the spit, carefully remove the cake, place on a plate
15. After one or two days, sprinkle with various icing, garnish with small sticks and decorate the top with flowers
