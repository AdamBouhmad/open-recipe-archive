## Ingredients

- 14 decagrams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 pinch nutmeg
- 30 decagrams cooked or fried and mashed potatoes
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Gradually mix 14 decagrams butter with 4 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon salt, a pinch of nutmeg and 30 decagrams cooked or fried and mashed potatoes
2. Lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
3. Fill a buttered form lined with breadcrumbs with the potato mixture
4. Cook covered in steam for 3/4 hour
5. Remove the form and turn out the pudding
6. Cut into desired pieces and arrange on a dish
7. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, pour hot butter over it
8. Serve with beef, tenderloin, game or cold roast
