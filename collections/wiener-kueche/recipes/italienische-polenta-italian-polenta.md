## Ingredients

- 3 halves salted water
- polenta or corn meal (similar to semolina but yellowish)
- butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Heat 3 halves salted water in a pan until it boils
2. Stir in polenta or corn meal until a very thick dough forms
3. Work the dough with a spoon as finely as possible
4. Shape into a round ball on a board, wrap in a napkin, and let sit for one hour
5. Cut into slices with a thick string or knife back
6. Thickly butter a cassole and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
7. Layer slices of polenta alternately with grated Parmesan cheese and butter pieces until all is used, with the last layer covered with cheese
8. Bake in strong heat for one hour
9. When serving, drain off the fat, turn out after loosening the edges with a knife, and serve
10. The polenta should have a crisp crust and light brown appearance
