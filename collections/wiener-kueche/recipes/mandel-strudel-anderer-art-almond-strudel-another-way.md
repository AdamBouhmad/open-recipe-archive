## Ingredients

- 12 deca beaten butter
- 12 deca finely ground vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deca blanched almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter with 12 deca finely ground vanilla sugar
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix gradually with 16 deca blanched almonds finely ground with 2 egg whites and 1/4 liter milk cream
4. Lightly fold in the snow from the remaining 4 egg whites
5. Stretch strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour thin, as indicated for Lung Strudel (No. 75)
6. Brush with the almond filling
7. Treat further as for Rice Gries Strudel (No. 1236)
