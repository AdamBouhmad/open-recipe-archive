## Ingredients

- 32 deca butter
- 2 pieces thinly chopped onions
- 2 cooked turnips
- 1 small piece celery
- 1 parsley root
- 1/3 kilo carp
- 1/2 kilo pike
- 1/2 kilo tench or 1/2 kilo bream
- some frogs and 1 crayfish (if available)
- 1 liter cold water
- spices (optional)

## Directions

1. Put 32 deca butter, 2 pieces of thinly chopped onions, 2 cooked turnips, 1 small piece of celery, and 1 parsley root in a casserole and heat
2. Clean and cut into pieces the fish (carp, pike, tench or bream, frogs and crayfish) and add to the vegetables
3. Let roast covered, then add 1 liter of cold water and braise covered
4. Spices may be added if desired
5. Pour in 2 liters cold water and let simmer slowly for 2 hours
6. Set the soup aside, remove the fat carefully, strain through a sieve, and use
