## Ingredients

- Approximately 1 kilogram potatoes
- 8 ounces butter
- 3 whole eggs
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Finely cut parsley
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Hot lard

## Directions

1. Roast the potatoes in glowing ashes or in the oven, pass them through a sieve, and then stir in 8 ounces of butter and 3 whole eggs, to which add also salt, some nutmeg, and finely cut parsley.
2. Pass this mixture through and let it cool. When the whole thing is cold and firm, form quenelles with a spoon, roll them in eggs and bread crumbs, bake them in hot lard and serve immediately. This is used as a garnish for roast beef and for various meat dishes.
