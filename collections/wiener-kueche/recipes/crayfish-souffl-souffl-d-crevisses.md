## Ingredients

- 10 crayfish, cooked in salted water
- 20 ounces butter
- 8 ounces blanched almonds
- 12 ounces crusts of bread, softened in milk
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 6 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Cook crayfish in salted water, remove tails and claws, pound shells with butter to prepare crayfish butter
2. Blanch and finely pound almonds, then pound again with softened bread crusts and hard-boiled egg yolks
3. Beat the crayfish butter until fluffy, add raw egg yolks one by one
4. Mix in the pounded almond mixture, cayenne, nutmeg, and the crayfish tails and claws
5. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites
6. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
7. Cover and steam for 1/2 hour or bake in the oven
8. Turn out onto a serving dish and serve with sweet cream or béchamel sauce as desired
