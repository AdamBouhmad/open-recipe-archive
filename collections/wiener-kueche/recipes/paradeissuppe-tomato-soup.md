## Ingredients

- ½ kg tomatoes
- 1 bread roll
- water
- vinegar
- ½ onion
- 6 dkg fat
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and break the tomatoes
2. Stew with water, some vinegar and ½ onion for 30 minutes and pass
3. Make a roux from the fat, add water or broth
4. Add the strained tomatoes, season with salt, sugar and vinegar to taste
5. Boil well and serve with bread slices or with small cut macaroni cooked in salted water
