## Ingredients

- 2 Pfund butter
- 1 Pfund fine dry flour
- 16 egg yolks
- 1/2 Pfund sugar
- zest of two lemons
- 1 Seidel sweet cream
- 1/2 Pfund small pitted raisins
- 3 Pfund large pitted raisins
- 10 egg whites
- fine-cut candied citron
- currants
- linen napkin

## Directions

1. Beat 2 pounds butter to foam
2. Stir in 1 pound fine dry flour and 16 egg yolks, adding several spoonfuls of flour after each egg is added, finishing with both together
3. Dissolve 1/2 pound sugar, over which the zest of two lemons has been grated, in 1 Seidel sweet cream
4. Stir this under the mass, along with 1/2 pound small and 3 pounds large pitted raisins
5. Fold in the snow of 10 eggs
6. Spread the butter-greased napkin over a suitable casserole or other dish, strewn with fine-cut candied citron and currants
7. Bind the mass in loosely so it can swell
8. Let it cook for 2 hours without interruption
9. When serving, pour a thick wine sauce over it
