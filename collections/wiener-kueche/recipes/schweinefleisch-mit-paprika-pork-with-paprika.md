## Ingredients

- Pork neck, shoulders, or breast pieces, cut into thumb-width cubes
- Diced bacon
- 1 onion, finely minced
- 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds
- Salt
- Paprika
- Broth
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the diced bacon in a casserole dish.
2. Add the cut pork pieces and finely minced onion.
3. Mix in 1/2 teaspoon of caraway seeds and allow to braise covered.
4. When the onion has become slightly roasted, add enough broth and vinegar so the liquid does not completely cover the meat.
5. Season with salt and a knife-tip of paprika.
6. Allow the meat to braise covered until tender.
7. Arrange the meat on a platter with the defatted braising liquid and serve with noodles, turnips, or similar accompaniments.
8. Optionally, peeled potatoes cut into pieces can be added at the same time and braised together with the meat.
