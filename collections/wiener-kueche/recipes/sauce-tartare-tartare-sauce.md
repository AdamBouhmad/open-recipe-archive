## Ingredients

- 4 ounces butter
- 5 sprigs parsley
- the same amount of tarragon leaves
- some white pepper and some cayenne pepper
- 3 pieces capers
- 5 juniper berries
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 shallot
- 1 piece macaroni and 1 piece pickled cucumber

## Directions

1. Pound the capers, juniper berries, macaroni, and pickled cucumber finely in a mortar and pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
2. Mix this passed mixture with a mayonnaise prepared from 3 raw egg yolks (see there) and with 2 tablespoons French mustard and if desired 1 tablespoon peppery marmalade, stirring well
3. Except for the juniper berries, the ingredients mentioned above can instead of being pounded be cut very finely, in which case no butter is used
