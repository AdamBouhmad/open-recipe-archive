## Ingredients

- about 1/2 Mass (about 1 liter) good milk
- 2 spoonfuls fine flour
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar
- a nutmeg-sized piece of butter
- orange blossom water or lemon peel or some orange water
- bread crumbs
- lard for frying
- sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Cook about 1/2 Mass of good milk down and set it aside
2. In a bowl, beat 2 spoonfuls of fine flour with 4 eggs, then mix this with the milk very well so it does not become grainy
3. Let it come together on gentle charcoal heat, but do not boil
4. While constantly stirring, add 1/4 pound of finely sifted sugar, a nutmeg-sized piece of butter, and a few drops of orange blossom water
5. Place it back on the charcoal and stir constantly until it becomes thick
6. Coat a cake sheet with butter and pipe the cream a thumb-thick onto it
7. When it has cooled, cut it into oblong or square pieces, coat them with bread crumbs, and fry them nicely in lard
8. After sprinkling with sugar, glaze them in the oven
