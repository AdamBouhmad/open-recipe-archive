## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Red roe of the lobster
- Oil
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Mayonnaise
- Diced aspic
- Olives
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Head lettuce

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster
2. Remove and finely pound the red roe in a mortar
3. Pour oil over it, stir, pass through a sieve and set aside in a cool place
4. Cut the large meat pieces neatly, sprinkle with salt and pour vinegar and oil over them
5. Mix the prepared lobster roe with mayonnaise, which becomes pale red
6. Place diced aspic in a dish, arrange the lobster meat around it in a circle
7. Cover with mayonnaise, then add more lobster pieces covered with the rest of the mayonnaise
8. Garnish this salad very neatly with variously colored aspic, olives, hard-boiled eggs, head lettuce and similar items
