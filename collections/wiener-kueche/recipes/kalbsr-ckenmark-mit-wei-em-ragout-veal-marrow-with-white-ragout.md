## Ingredients

- Veal marrow
- Salt water
- Hen marrow
- Hen combs
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Poach the removed loin marrow in boiling salt water for approximately 2 minutes
2. Remove and add to a white ragout of hen marrow, hen combs and prepared mushrooms
3. Stir carefully together so it does not break and serve hot on a platter
4. This ragout can also be used mixed with other ragouts
