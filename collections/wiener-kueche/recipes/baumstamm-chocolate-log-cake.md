## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 8 dkg sugar
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 3 dkg rum-moistened breadcrumbs
- 3 egg whites
- 8 dkg raw grated almonds
- 12-15 dkg butter for cream
- 5 dkg sugar for cream
- 4 dkg grated chocolate for cream
- 1 egg yolk for cream
- swollen chopped pistachios for topping

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg with 8 dkg sugar for 15 minutes
2. Stir in 5 dkg grated chocolate and 3 dkg rum-moistened breadcrumbs
3. Fold in 3 stiff egg whites together with 8 dkg raw grated almonds
4. Fill the mixture into a buttered and floured round log pan
5. Bake at moderate heat
6. Cut the cake lengthwise through the middle
7. Fill and brush the outside with cream
8. Mark the bark with a fork dipped in hot water by stroking from top to bottom
9. Sprinkle with swollen chopped pistachios
