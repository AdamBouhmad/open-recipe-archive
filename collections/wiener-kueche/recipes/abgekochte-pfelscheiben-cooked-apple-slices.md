## Ingredients

- Apples
- Small round cutter
- Flour for dredging
- Eggs for coating
- Breadcrumbs for rolling
- Lard for frying
- Powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Using a small round cutter as described in the Fried Apples recipe, remove the core house and peel the apples.
