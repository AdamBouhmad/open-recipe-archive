## Ingredients

- Some onion
- Green parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 large peeled, diced gherkin
- Stock
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut dill herb
- 1/4 liter cream
- Salt

## Directions

1. Some onion and green parsley are roasted in 2 tablespoons butter and 1 large, peeled, diced gherkin is braised with some stock until the gherkin pieces are tender
2. A roux is made from 6 tablespoons butter and the same amount of flour, the braised gherkins are cooked well with 1/2 teaspoon finely cut dill herb, 1/4 liter cream, and some salt
3. Stir together and, if desired, strain
