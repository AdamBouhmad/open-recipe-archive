## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons washed rice
- 0.5 liter boiling milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 knife point of salt
- Flour for rolling
- Hot lard for frying
- 4 tablespoons sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the washed rice in 0.5 liter boiling milk and let it cook soft and thick covered
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons butter and let the rice cool
3. Mix in 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk with 1 knife point of salt
4. Place the dough on a flour-dusted board, form small sausages or dumplings from it
5. Roll these in flour and fry in very hot lard
6. Remove with a slotted spoon and use as garnish for boiled beef, roasted lungs or the like
7. They can also be served as a flour dish at table, but then the dough must be seasoned with 4 tablespoons sugar
8. When serving, add any desired cream
