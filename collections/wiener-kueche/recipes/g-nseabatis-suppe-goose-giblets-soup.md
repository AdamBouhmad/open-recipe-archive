## Ingredients

- one cleaned goose head with eyes removed and throat removed
- cleaned goose stomach
- skinned goose feet
- goose wings with quills removed

## Directions

1. Clean a goose head with eyes and throat removed
2. Clean the goose stomach, skinned feet, and wings with quills removed
3. Wash thoroughly and cook tender in meat broth
4. Prepare an Estragon soup from this
5. Lay up the goose head, cut into neat pieces
6. Continue as with the Ragout Soup
