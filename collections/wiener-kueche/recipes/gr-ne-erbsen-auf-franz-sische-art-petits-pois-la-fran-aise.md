## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter fine green peas
- salt water
- 5 ounces butter
- 2 ounces sugar
- salt
- toasted bread cubes (Croutons)

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 liter of fine green peas in salt water for 5 minutes uncovered
2. Drain the water thoroughly
3. Place the peas with 5 ounces of butter and 2 ounces of sugar in a casserole dish
4. Cover and braise until the butter has dissolved
5. Stir and adjust seasoning with sugar and salt as needed
6. Heap on a platter and garnish with toasted bread cubes
