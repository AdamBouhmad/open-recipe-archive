## Ingredients

- Young cabbage or turnip plant leaves
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 18 drams butter
- 18 drams flour
- Garlic (if desired)
- Onion (if desired)
- ½ liter broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the stems, wash them, and cook them uncovered in salt water.
2. Just before serving, make a roux from 18 drams butter and 18 drams flour; if desired, add some garlic and onion; pour the roasted flour with ½ liter broth over it, season with salt, and stir for 5 minutes.
3. Once this has boiled well, add the cooked, chopped plant leaves, let them boil again, and serve.
4. May also be prepared like Spinach No. 296.
