## Ingredients

- 28 Deka flour
- 16 Deka cinnamon or orange sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 Deka peeled almonds
- 3 egg whites
- 5 egg whites for snow

## Directions

1. Make a butter dough from 28 Deka flour as described in the butter dough recipe (No. 1045) and let it rest in a cool place
2. Meanwhile, put 16 Deka cinnamon or orange sugar in a deep bowl, stir in 8 egg yolks one after another
3. Stir this mixture for half an hour with 16 Deka peeled almonds finely pounded with 3 egg whites
4. Lightly fold in the snow (beaten egg whites) from 5 egg whites
5. Roll out the rested butter dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a knife back, cut into two equal parts
6. Distribute the almond filling evenly along the middle of both pieces lengthwise
7. Brush the edges of both narrow sides with beaten egg and treat further as described for the Apple Strudel made with butter dough
8. Place a dampened wooden spoon on both sides of the strudel so that the filling cannot push the dough apart during baking
