## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- water
- half a measure of milk
- 4 eggs
- butter
- salt
- hollandaise sauce or rosehip broth

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of rice several times in boiling water
2. Cook it a couple more times with water
3. Add half a measure of milk and let it cook until thick
4. Mix in 4 eggs, some butter, and salt
5. Place everything in a cloth, tie it tightly
6. Place in a pot of boiling water and cook for two hours
7. Make a hollandaise sauce or rosehip broth and pour over before serving
