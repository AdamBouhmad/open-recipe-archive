## Ingredients

- Spider crab
- Salt water
- Salt
- Green parsley (optional)
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon juice
- Cold oil

## Directions

1. Cook the spider crabs like crayfish in salt water until the water begins to foam
2. Remove them and take off their legs
3. Remove all meat from the shell and upper limbs
4. Discard the eggs, paired organs and anything black as unusable
5. Fill the shell, sprinkle with salt, optionally with some green parsley and breadcrumbs
6. Drip some lemon juice and cold oil on top
7. Place the shells on the grill, put in the oven for 5 minutes and arrange to serve
8. Note that spider crabs are especially fatty at full moon
