## Ingredients

- 32 deka finely sifted sugar
- 1/10 liter clarified cold sugar
- 1 teaspoon Maraschino

## Directions

1. Put 32 deka finely sifted sugar in a deep bowl
2. Gradually stir in 1/10 liter of clarified cold sugar and then 1 teaspoon Maraschino
3. After beating this finely for approximately 1 hour, a thick flowing mass should form (the icing should not be so thick that it drips heavily from the spoon)
4. Coat a cake knife-back thick with this icing, if necessary dry it for a few minutes in a lukewarm oven, and then set it cold
5. Use a knife for coating the cake
