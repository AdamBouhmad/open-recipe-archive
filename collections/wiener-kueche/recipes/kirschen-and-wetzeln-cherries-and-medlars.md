## Ingredients

- large, dry, black cherries
- 2-3 fingers high of sugar water
- sugar for lightly inserted preserved fruit (as in Nr. 1841)
- 60 decilitres sugar
- medlars
- stems

## Directions

1. Remove stems from large, dry, black cherries and place them in preserving jars
2. Pour in 2-3 fingers high of sugar water (sugar for lightly inserted preserved fruit, Nr. 1841) with 60 decilitres sugar
3. Proceed further as with the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842)
4. For medlars, treat them similarly, only cut the stems somewhat shorter and leave them on, and boil 80 decilitres sugar in 1 litre water
