## Ingredients

- 28 deca flour
- 28 deca butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 8 deca sugar
- 1/16 liter white wine

## Directions

1. Put 28 deca flour and 28 deca butter on a board, work both together finely with the hands
2. Make a depression in the middle and add 2 egg yolks, a little salt, 8 deca sugar, and 1/16 liter white wine
3. Mix everything well with a knife and work into a smooth dough
4. Place on a flour-dusted board and let rest covered in a cold place
5. Then roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a small finger, cut the edges evenly
6. Roll the dough onto a roller, unroll onto a buttered baking sheet and prick several times with a pastry fork
7. Cover the dough thickly with cherries or any other desired fruit, which must always be placed skin-side down, but only so that on all 4 sides a 2-finger-wide empty space remains, which is folded over the cherries
8. Brush with beaten eggs and bake at fairly strong heat in the oven
9. When done, dust the cake with finely pounded sugar, cut into desired pieces, arrange on a platter and serve warm or cold
