## Ingredients

- Stiff snow from 3 eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 6 Deca fine flour
- 1 pinch salt
- Nutmeg (if desired)

## Directions

1. Mix 3 egg yolks, 6 Deca fine flour with a pinch of salt and if desired a bit of nutmeg lightly into the stiff snow from 3 eggs, without stirring the snow
2. Pour into a shallow casserole dish greased and dusted with flour to a finger-height
3. Bake at moderate heat and use as with Kaiserschöberl
