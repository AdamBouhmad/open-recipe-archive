## Ingredients

- 1 glass wine vinegar
- Fine shallots
- Pepper
- Butter
- Brown coulis
- Salt
- Small cucumbers
- Cooked egg whites
- Cooked yellow turnips
- Cleaned anchovies
- Capers

## Directions

1. In a casserole, briefly cook a glass of wine vinegar with fine shallots, pepper, and a small piece of butter.
2. Add several spoonfuls of brown, properly salted coulis.
3. Let it cook clear and skim well.
4. Prepare separately: small cucumbers, the whites of cooked eggs, cooked yellow turnips, and cleaned anchovies cut into small dice.
5. When serving, mix all these with a spoonful of fine capers into the hot sauce.
6. The sauce must not boil after this.
