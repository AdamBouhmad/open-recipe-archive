## Ingredients

- apricots (quantity not specified)
- sugar equal in weight to the apricots without kernels

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the Finest Apricot Preserves (recipe 1870), with the difference that only as much sugar is used as the apricots without kernels weigh.
2. Omit the dissolved apricot kernels.
