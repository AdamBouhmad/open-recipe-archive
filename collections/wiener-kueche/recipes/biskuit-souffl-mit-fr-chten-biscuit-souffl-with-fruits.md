## Ingredients

- 16 Deka powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 14 Deka flour
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold
- various fruits or sponge fingers

## Directions

1. Put 16 Deka powdered sugar in a deep bowl, add 8 egg yolks one after another and beat for 1/2 hour.
2. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites together with 14 Deka flour.
3. Line a cake mold with sponge fingers or any desired fruits, as indicated for the 'Apfel Strudel Auflauf' (recipe 1372).
4. Pour the biskuit mass over and bake at low heat for 1 hour in the oven.
