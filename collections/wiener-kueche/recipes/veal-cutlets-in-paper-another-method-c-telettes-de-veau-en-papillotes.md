## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Tarragon
- Shallots
- Butter
- Bacon
- Oil or butter
- Fine herbs
- Bouillon glace or Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. When the meat has been scraped from the rib, the cutlets are beaten flat and salted.
2. Mushrooms, green parsley, chervil, tarragon if desired and shallots are cut very finely and fried with the cutlets in hot butter for 3-5 minutes.
3. They are then set aside and, when completely cooled, are spread on both sides with the fine herbs and covered on both sides with thin bacon slices, into which small incisions are made with a knife.
4. The wrapping of the cutlets with paper and the further handling is done in the same way as described for the preceding cutlets.
