## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 1 tablespoon finely pounded sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 60 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter gradually with sugar, salt, egg yolks, and whole eggs
2. Add the yeast dissolved in milk and flour, mix well
3. Beat the dough until it lifts from the spoon
4. Cover and let rise in a warm place until risen and doubled in volume
