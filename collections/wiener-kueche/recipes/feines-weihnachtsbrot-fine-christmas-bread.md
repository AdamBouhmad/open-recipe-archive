## Ingredients

- 12 deca ground, ground almonds
- 12 deca hazelnut kernels
- 12 deca currants
- 12 deca dates
- 12 deca figs
- 6 deca citron
- 1 kilo dried pears (prunes)
- 1 kilo dried plums
- or if no pears available, twice as much plums (both soft-cooked and cut with a knife)
- 12 deca juniper berries
- 12 deca raisins
- 24 deca sugar
- 4 tablespoons rum
- 2 deca melted pistachios
- 4 deca pine nuts (finely cut in leaves)
- 1/2 kilo fresh grapes or Malaga grapes
- finely cut peel of 1/2 orange and 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 kilo black bread dough
- 12 deca black flour
- yeast dough as for milk bread

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well and moisten with 4 tablespoons rum, let it stand overnight.
2. The next day add melted pistachios, pine nuts, fresh grapes, citrus peels, and black bread dough.
3. Add 12 deca black flour and work everything together well.
4. Form into small loaves or hand-wide, 3-inch high and any length strips.
5. Wrap the formed loaves or strips in dough similar to the mixture or yeast dough as for milk bread.
6. Place them on a sheet lined with buttered paper with the overlapped dough seam-side down, brushed with egg.
7. Let them rise at a warm place for approximately 1.5 hours lightly covered.
8. Brush with egg and bake approximately 1.5 hours slowly in the oven.
9. When removed, immediately wipe them with a cloth moistened in water.
