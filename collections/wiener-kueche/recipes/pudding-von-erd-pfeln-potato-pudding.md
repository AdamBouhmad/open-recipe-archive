## Ingredients

- 16 teaspoons beaten butter
- 46 teaspoons passed potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- finely cut green parsley
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 8 teaspoons finely cut ham or other filling
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan

## Directions

1. 16 teaspoons of beaten butter are stirred little by little with 46 teaspoons of passed potatoes, 1 teaspoon of salt, some finely cut green parsley and 6 egg yolks and finally lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
2. Fill half of this mass into a butter-greased, flour-dusted cake form, coat with 8 teaspoons of finely cut ham or other filling, but only so much that a finger-width border remains all around and cover this evenly with the second half of the potato mass
3. Place the pudding in the oven and bake it at low heat for 3/4 hours
