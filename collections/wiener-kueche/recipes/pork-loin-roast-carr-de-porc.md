## Ingredients

- pork loin (carré)
- salt
- caraway
- garlic
- vinegar
- fat

## Directions

1. Salt the carré well, salt it, rub with caraway and if desired with some crushed garlic, sprinkle with vinegar
2. Place in a pan containing fat and roast in the oven, basting frequently with the accumulated juices
3. When the rind has taken on a light-brown color, cut it in a grid pattern (score it) with thumb-width intervals and finish roasting
4. Cut off the ribs and arrange them in a platter, serving the defatted juices with them
