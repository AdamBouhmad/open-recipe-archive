## Ingredients

- 6-8 mushrooms or champignons
- 4 dkg butter
- salt
- parsley
- 8 dkg butter
- water or broth
- ½ liter cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms, cut into thin slices
2. Place in a pot with 4 dkg hot butter and braise for ½ hour, stirring occasionally
3. Make a parsley roux with 8 dkg butter and moisten with water or day-old broth
4. Add the mushrooms, season with salt, cook well
5. Optional: add a few tablespoons of cream to the soup
