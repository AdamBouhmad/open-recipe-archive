## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour for pasta dough
- salt
- butter
- ham
- milk cream

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 2 eggs
2. Roll it into thin sheets and cut each sheet into about six pieces
3. Cook well in salted water
4. Drain in a sieve and rinse with cold water
5. Let dry on a cloth
6. Make a short pastry and line a buttered casserole with it
7. Lay a layer of pasta pieces on top
8. Beat a piece of butter with 3 eggs and 8 spoonfuls of milk cream together well
9. Pour some of this egg and cream mixture onto the pasta layer
10. Sprinkle three-quarters of a pound of boiled, finely cut ham
11. Alternate with another layer of pasta pieces until the casserole is full
12. Cover with a sheet of short pastry and bake brushed with egg white for one hour in the oven
