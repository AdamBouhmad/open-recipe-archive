## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- 3/4 liter sour cream
- 1 tablespoon capers

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue as described above
2. Prepare the Madeira sauce as above, but with the difference that instead of Madeira wine, add 3/4 liter sour cream and 1 tablespoon capers and heat through
3. Pour over the sliced tongue
4. Alternative: use 1/8 liter vinegar and 3/8 liter soup, or 1/4 liter ordinary white wine instead of the sour cream
