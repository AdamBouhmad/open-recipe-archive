## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons washed rice
- 1.5 liters cream or milk
- 16 tablespoons granulated sugar
- juice and zest of 1 orange
- 3 egg whites
- powdered sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Cook 16 tablespoons of washed rice in 1.5 liters of boiling cream under constant stirring until soft and thick
2. Add 16 tablespoons granulated sugar and the juice and zest of 1 orange
3. Stir well together
4. Arrange on a serving dish
5. Cover evenly with meringue made from 3 egg whites
6. Dust heavily with finely ground sugar
7. Bake in an open oven for 10 minutes on low heat
8. Remove the dish and garnish with marmalade, stewed fruits or apples, or compote of choice
9. Place on a second serving dish and bring to table
