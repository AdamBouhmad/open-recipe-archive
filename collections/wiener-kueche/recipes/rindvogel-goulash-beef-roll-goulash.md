## Ingredients

- Beef
- Onion
- Marjoram
- Caraway seeds
- Paprika
- Salt
- Stock

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as for goulash, with the difference that the onion is finely diced.
2. When it is golden roasted, add the meat finely sliced leafy with marjoram and proceed as with goulash.
