## Ingredients

- 1 pork head or ½ kg pork belly from a young pig
- 8 dkg root vegetables
- 10 dkg horseradish
- 1 onion
- 8 peppercorns and spice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- thyme
- salt
- vinegar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the pork or head 2-3 times very well.
2. Place in cold salted water with vinegar, cleaned root vegetables cut into thin slices, onion, peppercorns, spice berries, bay leaves, and thyme.
3. Cook for 1-1½ hours.
4. Place the head on a dish, stuff the snout and ear with curled parsley, garnish the head with vegetables and horseradish.
5. Slice the meat, arrange on a long dish, garnish similarly, and serve with boiled potatoes.
