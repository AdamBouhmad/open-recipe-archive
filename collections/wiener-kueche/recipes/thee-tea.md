## Ingredients

- Weak black tea
- Hot water
- Boiling water
- Cold cream
- Sugar
- Rum or cognac
- Lemons

## Directions

1. Fill the teapot with hot water to warm it, then pour out the water after a few minutes.
2. Add tea (for 1 person use 1 teaspoon full; for several persons use somewhat less per individual).
3. Pour boiling water over it, but fill the pot only half full.
4. After 5 minutes, add the remaining water, also boiling, and after the same length of time serve the tea.
5. Always provide boiling hot water, either in a pot or kept boiling hot in the teapot by a spirit lamp.
6. Simultaneously serve unboiled cream, 1 bottle of rum or cognac, and lemons so that the tea can be drunk as desired.
7. Pour the tea through a strainer into cups, but do not fill the cups completely with tea, always leave about 1 finger's width empty.
