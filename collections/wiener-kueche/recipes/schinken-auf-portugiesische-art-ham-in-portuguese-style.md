## Ingredients

- Ham
- Water
- Beef broth, good quality
- Red wine
- Onions
- Yellow turnips
- Parsley roots
- Peppercorns
- Cloves
- Cinnamon, whole
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Remove the rind and anything inedible, and soak overnight in water
2. Place the ham in a casserole
3. Pour half a measure of good beef broth and the same amount of red wine over it
4. Add many onions, yellow turnips, parsley roots, peppercorns, cloves, and whole cinnamon
5. Cover well and cook on weak fire for four to five hours until tender
6. Let it cool in the broth
7. Remove it from the broth and skim all fat from the sediment
8. Clarify with a little egg white
9. Place a hand-breadth border of hard dough on the serving dish
10. Bake it in the oven
11. Place the ham in the middle and fill the space with the clarified sediment as it begins to gel
