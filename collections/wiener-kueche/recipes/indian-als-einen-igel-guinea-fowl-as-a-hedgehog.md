## Ingredients

- guinea fowl
- ragout made from various preferred ingredients
- small spits of boxwood or other wood
- fat livers
- veal sweetbread
- ham
- egg white
- breadcrumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. After the guinea fowl is gutted, split the breast from one end to the other
2. Remove the breastbone
3. Fill it with a good ragout made from various preferred ingredients
4. Sew it closed again
5. Cook it in a common braise until done
6. Once this is done, cut small spits from boxwood or other wood
7. Stud them with pieces of fat livers, veal sweetbread, ham and similar items
8. Dip these in egg white
9. Sprinkle them with breadcrumbs
10. Fry them in lard
11. Place the guinea fowl with the breast facing down in the serving dish
12. Leave the neck attached, which serves as a tail
13. Cover the back with the spits, of which you must have enough so the guinea fowl is sufficiently covered
