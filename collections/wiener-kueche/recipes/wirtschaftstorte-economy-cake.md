## Ingredients

- 6 stiff egg whites
- 15 dkg sugar
- 15 dkg grated hazelnuts
- 3 dkg grated chocolate
- marmalade
- brown icing or powdered sugar

## Directions

1. With a wooden spoon, gradually stir 15 dkg sugar, 15 dkg grated hazelnuts, and 3 dkg grated chocolate into 6 stiff egg whites.
2. Fill the mass into the cake form and bake weakly.
3. The next day, cut it straight, brush the top with marmalade, and brown ice or dust with powdered sugar.
4. Make this cake only when you have egg whites available.
