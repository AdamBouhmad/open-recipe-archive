## Ingredients

- 1 liter semolina
- 1/8 liter roll crumbs roasted in 1 1/2 Loffel lard or finely diced bacon
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut parsley roasted in 10 Loffel lard
- salt
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- parsley bunch for cooking

## Directions

1. Mix under 1 liter semolina 1/8 liter roll crumbs that have been roasted in 1 1/2 Loffel lard or finely diced bacon
2. Pour heated 1/2 teaspoon finely cut parsley roasted in 10 Loffel lard over the semolina
3. Add salt and stir well
4. Pour 3/4 liter boiling water over it and stir until it forms a firm dough
5. Form knödel of desired size and cook in salted water with a bunch of parsley, but not too many in one pot
