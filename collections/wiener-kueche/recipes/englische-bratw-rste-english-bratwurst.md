## Ingredients

- one pound young pork meat, fat or lean
- one pound veal meat
- one pound kidney fat
- half pound bread rolls
- milk
- six fresh sage leaves
- lemon zest
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- sausage herbs
- Madeira or Rhine wine (about one cup)

## Directions

1. Scrape skin and nerves from all the meat
2. Chop finely together with the milk-soaked bread rolls, sage leaves, lemon zest, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and sausage herbs
3. If desired, work in about one cup Madeira or Rhine wine for fine flavor
4. Wet the bread board with fresh water, roll out the mass, and form bratwursts of equal length
5. Grill them, lay on paper brushed with butter on a roasting rack, and let them take color, or fry them in a pan with some butter
6. Alternatively, fill the sausage forcemeat into pork caul, mix in diced truffles that have been passed through minced bacon and finely chopped onions, form equal crepinettes, and grill on both sides on a roasting rack
