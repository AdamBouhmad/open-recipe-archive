## Ingredients

- 16 deca sago
- water
- 1 1/4 liters water
- approximately 1/2 liter red wine
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook 16 deca sago in water, but not too soft
2. Strain off the water, pour 1 1/4 liters water to the sago according to the strength of the wine, and approximately 1/2 liter red wine
3. Add sugar so it is sweet enough
4. Let cook until the sago is transparent
5. Before serving some cinnamon can be added and stirred in well
