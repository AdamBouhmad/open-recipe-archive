## Ingredients

- 10 small cabbages
- Veal forcemeat
- Twine
- 8 ounces butter or lard
- Finely cut green parsley
- 1/2 liter soup
- Salt and spices

## Directions

1. Hollow out 10 small cabbages like sauerkraut cabbages
2. Fill with veal forcemeat
3. Fold the leaves over the filling and wrap with twine
4. Roast finely cut green parsley in 8 ounces butter or lard
5. Add the cabbage
6. Pour 1/2 liter soup over it, salt and spice it, and braise covered for about 3/4 hour
7. Loosen the twine
8. Cut the cabbages into 4 pieces
9. Thin the cabbage broth with some cream and serve with the cabbage
