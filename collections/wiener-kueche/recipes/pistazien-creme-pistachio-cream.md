## Ingredients

- one pound pistachios
- one good spoonful brandy
- 1 seidel cream
- the yolk of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Grind one pound pistachios with one good spoonful brandy finely in a mortar
2. Put them in a casserole, pour in 1 seidel cream, beat in the yolk of 2 eggs, and whisk vigorously
3. Stir it over charcoal fire until it has an oblong thickness, only it must not boil
4. When cold, it can be served as cream
