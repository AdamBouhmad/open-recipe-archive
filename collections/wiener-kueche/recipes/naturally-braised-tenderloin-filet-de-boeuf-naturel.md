## Ingredients

- Tenderloin, the piece between the spine and the flank
- Salt and pepper
- Lard from the tenderloin or bacon sheets
- Lard or bacon strips for larding

## Directions

1. Remove the bone and thick skin from the tenderloin first, then cut off all fat
2. With a small sharp knife, cut between the second very thin skin and the meat and, under gentle movement while holding the blade against the meat, separate the skin paper-thin from the meat from one end to the other
3. After salting and peppering, lard the smoothly skinned side, more or less heavily according to taste, with equally long, needle-like cut, salted lard in regular rows
4. Line a casserole or pan with lard from the tenderloin or bacon sheets, place the tenderloin and the separated skin on it and place lard or bacon on top
5. Braise in its own juice with frequent basting
