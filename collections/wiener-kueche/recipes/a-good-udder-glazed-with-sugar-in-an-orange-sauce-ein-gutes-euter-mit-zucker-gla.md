## Ingredients

- two cow udders
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- white wine
- sugar
- orange zest
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Take two cow udders, cook them soft, then remove them from the broth, remove the skin, and form them completely round.
2. Make a sauce from a piece of butter and a spoonful of flour, let it turn slightly yellow, add beef broth, a glass of white wine, a piece of sugar rubbed on orange peel, and the juice of the same, let the sauce cook well, strain it through a sieve onto the dish, place the udder on top, sprinkle it well with sugar.
3. Burn this sugar with a glowing iron so that the udder gets a glaze, and serve it.
