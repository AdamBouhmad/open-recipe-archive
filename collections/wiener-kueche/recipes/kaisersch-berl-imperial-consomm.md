## Ingredients

- 12 Deca butter
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 pinch salt
- Snow (egg white) from 6 eggs
- 16 Deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deca butter until fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 3 tablespoons milk
3. Add 6 egg yolks and 1 pinch of salt
4. Mix in the snow from 6 eggs along with 16 Deca flour at the same time into the mixture
5. This must be done carefully so the snow does not get beaten down too much
6. Pour into a shallow casserole dish greased with butter and dusted with flour to a finger-depth
7. Bake at moderate heat, turn out onto a board and let cool
8. Can be cut into pieces of any size or cut out with a cutter before adding to soup
9. Add to the soup only a few minutes before serving, as it otherwise absorbs too much soup and becomes too soft
