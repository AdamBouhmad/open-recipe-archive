## Ingredients

- About 15 small carrots
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 8 tablespoons butter
- Equal amount of flour
- Finely chopped green parsley or tarragon
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- Vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel about 15 small carrots, cut them finely into thin slices
2. Cook in salt water, then place in cold water
3. Make a light roux from 8 tablespoons butter and equal amount of flour
4. Add finely chopped green parsley or tarragon, let roast a bit, stir in with 1/2 liter soup
5. Season with salt and nutmeg and let simmer well
6. Add the strained carrots with 1/4 liter sour cream and cook once more
7. As desired, one can season this sauce with vinegar or lemon juice
