## Ingredients

- One measure of very sour wine vinegar
- Two measures of country wine

## Directions

1. Let one measure of very sour wine vinegar and two measures of country wine stand in a container made of two well-fired clay vessels for several days in sun or oven warmth
2. Fill it up again with two measures, and continue thus until the container is full
3. Let it sour for at least eight days
4. Filter two measures from it and pour into the other container
5. Fill the first one again, and gradually fill up the second as well
6. When you need to use from the first, refill it from the second, and fill the second again with wine
7. In this way you always have good wine vinegar
8. If you throw in some pieces of sugar from time to time, the vinegar becomes much more spirited
