## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- water
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut peeled apples into thin slices
2. Braise with some water, several spoonfuls of wine, and as much sugar as desired
3. Make a butter sauce
4. Stir it with the juice from the apples
5. Add the apples to it
6. Let simmer
