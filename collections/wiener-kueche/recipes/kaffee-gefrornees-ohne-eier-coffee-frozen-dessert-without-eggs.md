## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter strong coffee
- 1 liter double-strength unsweetened cream
- 20 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients cold and stir together
2. Place in the freezing box and treat further as indicated for Gefrorne mit Vanille (No. 1720)
