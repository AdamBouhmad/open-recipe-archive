## Ingredients

- a beautiful piece of beef from the rib or shell, cooked soft
- good beef fat
- three to four large split onions
- yellow turnips
- parsnip
- celery, cut
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful piece of beef from the rib or shell and cook it soft.
2. In a casserole, take good beef fat.
3. Add three to four large split onions, some yellow turnips, parsnip, and celery, cut, to the fat and let them braise.
4. Before serving, add the meat and a little beef broth to the braised vegetables.
5. When plating, place the meat on the plate and arrange the vegetables with the little sauce that surrounds them around it.
