## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 28 drams of fine sugar
- juice of a lemon
- 3 tablespoons of finely strained strawberry juice

## Directions

1. Whisk 2 egg whites to stiff snow, add 28 drams of fine sugar, juice of a lemon and 3 tablespoons of finely strained strawberry juice and stir this for 3/4 to 1 hour.
2. Then put this mass on the cake, spread it with a knife dipped in hot water, sprinkle it with finely sifted sugar and let the cake dry in the open oven for about 10 minutes.
3. This can be made from any fruit desired.
