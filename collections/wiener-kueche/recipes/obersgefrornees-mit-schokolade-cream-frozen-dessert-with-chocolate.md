## Ingredients

- 28 deca vanilla chocolate
- 1 liter boiling cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 20 deca finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Soften chocolate on a board in the oven
2. Place in a saucepan and pour 1 liter boiling cream over it, stirring constantly
3. Stir smooth and let cool
4. Place egg yolks, sugar, and completely cooled chocolate in a snow-beater
5. Proceed further as indicated for Gefrorne mit Vanille (No. 1720)
