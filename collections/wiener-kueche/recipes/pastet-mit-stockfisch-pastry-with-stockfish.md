## Ingredients

- About 5 pounds well-soaked stockfish
- Cold water
- Fresh water
- Puff pastry
- Egg yolk
- Pike farce balls
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Cream sauce
- Good cream

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the soaked stockfish
2. Cut away the skin and divide it into pieces two fingers wide
3. Place it with cold water in a casserole over the fire until after half an hour it begins to draw
4. Pour off the water and pour fresh water over it
5. Repeat this once more until it finally comes to a gentle boil
6. Lay it out and set aside to cool
7. On a prepared tin plate lay puff pastry rolled to a small finger's thickness and cut to size
8. Arrange the stockfish and balls made from pike farce in layers on the same pastry
9. Sprinkle salt, pepper and nutmeg on the stockfish, and between it some small pieces of butter
10. Brush the edge with egg yolk and make a lid that is not too thick over it
11. Pour a cream sauce into it beforehand, for which good cream must be used
