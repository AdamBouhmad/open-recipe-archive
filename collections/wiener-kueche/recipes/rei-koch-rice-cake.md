## Ingredients

- not quite one-quarter pound rice
- cream
- one-quarter pound butter
- two whole eggs and four yolks
- a bit of egg white foam
- sugar and cinnamon to taste

## Directions

1. Take not quite one-quarter pound rice and cook it in cream until white and thick
2. Add one-quarter pound butter and stir it together
3. Allow it to cool
4. Beat it until fine
5. Beat in two whole eggs and four yolks, stirring each one well
6. Add a bit of egg white foam, and sugar and cinnamon to taste
7. Serve it on a sheet pan with a rim or bake it in a cake pan
