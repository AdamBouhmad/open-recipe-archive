## Ingredients

- calf mesentery
- vinegar
- salt water
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- butter sauce
- vinegar or wine or lemon juice

## Directions

1. The calf mesentery is cleaned in sour water, then cooked in salt water, removed, cut into desired pieces, combined with finely cut green parsley, some salt, nutmeg and a little pepper in a well-made butter sauce and brought to a boil.
2. The sauce can be soured to taste with some vinegar, wine or lemon juice.
