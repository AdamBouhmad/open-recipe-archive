## Ingredients

- Pieces of glowing wood charcoal
- 0.5 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Instead of toasted bread, throw some pieces of glowing wood charcoal into 0.5 liter of cold water.
2. Let it stand uncovered and strain it after some time.
