## Ingredients

- Braised or fried veal
- Fried capon or guinea fowl breast
- Bread roll, softened in milk
- 3 eggs
- Fresh parsley
- 4 lothes butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs
- 2 spoonfuls good cream

## Directions

1. Cut braised or fried veal and fried capon or guinea fowl breast small. Soak bread roll in milk and press out. Make a mixture from 3 eggs and chop fresh parsley finely. Beat 4 lothes butter fluffy, stir in the chopped ingredients, and add 3 eggs one after another. Mix salt, pepper, some bread crumbs, and 2 spoonfuls of good cream well together and add to the mixture. Butter a cloth, place the mixture on it, tie together and simmer in boiling salt water for one hour. After removing, place in soup pot and pour brown soup over it.
