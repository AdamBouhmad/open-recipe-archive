## Ingredients

- pancakes
- pork kidneys
- 5 dkg hot fat
- ½ scallion
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Skin, wash, and slice the kidneys thinly
2. 15 minutes before serving, lightly brown ½ scallion in 5 dkg hot fat
3. Add the kidneys and roast them quickly over high heat
4. Add a few drops of vinegar and water
5. Let roast a few more minutes and salt
6. Spread on the pancakes
7. Roll the pancakes with the closed side down on a flat dish
8. Make cuts on top so the filling is visible
