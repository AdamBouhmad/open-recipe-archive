## Ingredients

- Plums (Zwetschken)
- Wheat bran

## Directions

1. Pick the plums before they detach from the stem, thus with the stem still attached
2. Lay them in a dry chamber for 2-3 days
3. Then layer them in wheat bran, but without them touching one another
4. They will keep themselves for several months
5. When they are to come to the table, hold them for some time over water vapor so they swell up and look fresh
