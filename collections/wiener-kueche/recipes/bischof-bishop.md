## Ingredients

- 8 pieces beautiful bitter oranges
- 4 bottles good Pontac
- 2 pounds sugar
- Toasted dark bread crust, cinnamon and cloves (optional)

## Directions

1. With a knife, lightly score all sides of 8 beautiful bitter oranges with small cuts into the peel.
2. Roast them on a grate over very gentle charcoal fire.
3. Make deep cross-shaped cuts in them.
4. Place in an earthenware pot and pour 4 bottles of good Pontac over them.
5. Cover the pot well and set on hot ashes for 4 to 6 hours so the oranges infuse well.
6. You can press them several times with a spoon, or better yet, let them stand overnight.
7. Press through a napkin and sweeten with 2 pounds sugar.
8. The Bishop can be drunk either warm or cold.
9. You can add a roasted dark bread crust along with some cinnamon and cloves to infuse with it, but the pure orange aroma or taste is always preferable.
