## Ingredients

- ½ kg maccaroni
- 5 dkg butter or fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Wipe the maccaroni, break in 3-4 pieces
2. Place in much boiling salted water and cook ½-¾ hour while stirring frequently
3. Drain and shock in cold water
4. Place in a casserole with butter or fat, salt it and warm in the oven while stirring frequently
