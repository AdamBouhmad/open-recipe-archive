## Ingredients

- a bundle of asparagus, boiled in salted water and cut into oblong pieces
- 20 crayfish tails, removed and each cut in half
- twelve eggs
- salt
- pepper, pounded
- a piece of crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Take a bundle of asparagus that has been boiled in salted water and cut into oblong pieces, and from 20 crayfish remove the tails and cut each in half.
2. Beat twelve eggs in a pot, salt them and stir them together, add the asparagus and crayfish tails to the eggs, add some pounded pepper, and stir everything well together.
3. Let a piece of crayfish butter become warm, add the whole mixture into it, stir it often, and break it into small pieces.
4. When serving at table, arrange it on a small round dish; pile it high in the middle, and arrange crayfish tails around the edge.
