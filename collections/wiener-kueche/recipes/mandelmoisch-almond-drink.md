## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram sweet almonds
- 1 deciliter bitter almonds
- boiling water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 kilogram sweet and 1 deciliter bitter almonds with boiling water, skin, wash several times and pound finely with some cold water
