## Ingredients

- beef tongue cooked in salt water
- Madeira sauce with lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a freshly cooked beef tongue into finger-thick slices
2. Prepare as cutlets in paper (papillotes)
3. Serve with Madeira sauce with lemon juice in a sauce boat
