## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 16 tablespoons washed rice
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 1 pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Bring 1 liter of milk to a boil
2. Stir in 16 tablespoons of washed rice under constant stirring
3. Allow it to cook well until the rice is soft
4. Add 8 tablespoons sugar, 8 tablespoons butter, and 1 pinch of salt
5. Stir well together and let the rice cook covered until done
6. Arrange the rice on a serving dish and serve dusted with sugar and optionally with cinnamon or grated chocolate
7. If the rice is too thick, thin it with boiling milk
