## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound calf's liver
- 3 parts of a bread roll
- 2 eggs
- lard
- good broth
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Bake 1/4 pound calf's liver, 3 parts of a bread roll, and 2 eggs in lard
2. Pound everything finely together in a mortar
3. Put it in a pot
4. Fill with good broth to which nutmeg has been added
5. Let it cook for half an hour
6. Press through a sieve
7. Return to the pot
8. Let it cook a bit longer
