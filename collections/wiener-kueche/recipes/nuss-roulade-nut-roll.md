## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 16 drams sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 14 drams flour
- white wax for greasing
- tissue paper
- hazelnut filling or candied peel filling
- breadcrumbs
- 3 eggs for brushing
- pearl sugar for sprinkling
- butter for the hot plate

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 16 drams sugar for 1/2 hour
2. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 6 egg whites with 14 drams flour
3. Grease a warm baking sheet with white wax, wipe lightly with tissue paper
4. Spread the batter the thickness of a knife back on the sheet
5. Bake at low heat until pale yellow
6. Remove from the baking sheet with a thin long knife
7. Brush with hazelnut filling or dissolved candied peel, and if using the latter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs
8. Fold the dough 2 fingers wide on the longer side, brush with the same filling, then roll up completely
9. Brush with beaten eggs, sprinkle with pearl sugar, and dry for a few more minutes in the oven on the hot plate
10. Cool completely and cut into pieces of desired size
