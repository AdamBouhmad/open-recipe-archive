## Ingredients

- sparrows or other birds
- butter
- onion
- 2 liters water
- 16 decagrams black bread cut in thin slices
- salt
- 3 beef marrow
- ¼ liter wine

## Directions

1. Pluck and flambe the birds, then pluck again and let cool
2. Fry them quickly with fat and thinly sliced onion in a saucepan
3. Pound them with the onion in a mortar and place this mixture in a casserole with 2 liters water and 16 decagrams thinly sliced black bread
4. Simmer about 1 hour with salt and the fat in which the birds were fried
5. Strain and serve the soup with diced bread rolls
6. Can add 3 beef marrow spoonfuls sprinkled with ¼ liter wine
