## Ingredients

- 3 spoonfuls rice flour
- the yolk of 5 eggs
- 3 spoonfuls water
- 2 spoonfuls orange blossom water
- 1 seidel good cream

## Directions

1. Stir together 3 spoonfuls rice flour, the yolk of 5 eggs, 3 spoonfuls water, and 2 spoonfuls orange blossom water well
2. Boil 1 seidel good cream and pour it in
3. Let this mixture come together over charcoal fire under constant stirring until it is as thick as required for cream
4. Serve in small cream cups
