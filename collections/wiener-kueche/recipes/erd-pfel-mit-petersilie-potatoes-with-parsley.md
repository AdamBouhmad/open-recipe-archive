## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- cream
- cooked potatoes
- 1 strong tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as for potatoes with ham, except instead of ham add 1 strong tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
