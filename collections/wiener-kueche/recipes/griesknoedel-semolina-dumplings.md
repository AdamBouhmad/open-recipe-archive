## Ingredients

- 1 measure cream
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound semolina
- 12 egg yolks
- salt
- 4 loth sugar
- grated lemon zest
- milk for cooking
- brown butter for serving
- prune compote for serving

## Directions

1. Bring the cream and butter to a boil
2. Add the semolina and stir over the fire until it pulls away from the pan dryly
3. Let cool sufficiently
4. Gradually stir in 12 egg yolks with a little salt, 4 loth sugar, and grated lemon zest
5. Using a spoon, form dumplings on a flour-dusted board
6. Cook the dumplings in simmering milk
7. Serve with brown butter and prune compote
