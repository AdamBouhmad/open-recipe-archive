## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- Butter
- Flour
- White soup
- Cauliflower water

## Directions

1. Cut off the stems of the cauliflower and peel the fine skin
2. Place the cauliflower in cold water for 1/4 hour, then place in boiling salt water and cook until soft
3. Make a roux with butter and flour, pour in some white soup and cauliflower water, and bring to a boil
4. Carefully remove the cauliflower from the salt water, place it heaped on a platter, pour the butter sauce over it, and serve without further additions
5. The sauce can also be made with milk instead of soup
