## Ingredients

- 5 pounds stockfish
- 1/2 pound butter, beaten to a foam
- 10 eggs
- finely chopped onion
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch 5 pounds stockfish, which should only draw, not boil, lay it out when cooked so it drains well, free it from bones, and chop it finely.
2. Add it to 1/2 pound butter beaten to a foam, along with 10 eggs, some finely chopped onion, salt, nutmeg, and a piece of sugar, and stir everything well together.
3. Cook this mass for one hour in a pot of simmering salt water according to the method above.
4. Serve with a butter sauce poured over it.
