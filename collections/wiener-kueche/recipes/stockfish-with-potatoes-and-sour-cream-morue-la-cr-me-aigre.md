## Ingredients

- 1 kilo prepared stockfish
- Anchovy butter
- Potatoes
- 1 liter cream
- 2 egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the stockfish and then cut it into neat pieces.
2. Spread a bowl with anchovy butter, line it with blanched, finely sliced potatoes.
3. Add a layer of the cooked stockfish on top and sprinkle with blanched, finely diced anchovies.
4. Whisk 1 liter of cream with 2 egg yolks and pour some of this mixture over the stockfish.
5. Repeat this process until all stockfish is used.
6. On the final layer, add again boiled, finely sliced potatoes, cover with some cream, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and cover with anchovy butter.
7. Place the arranged bowl in the oven and let it sit for approximately 2 to 3 hours so that the dish turns golden and then serve.
