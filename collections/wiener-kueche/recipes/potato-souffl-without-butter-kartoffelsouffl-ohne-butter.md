## Ingredients

- 12 ounces granulated sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 50 ounces cooked, passed, pitted potatoes
- 8 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Beat the granulated sugar with egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Add the cooked, passed, and pitted potatoes
3. Gently fold in the whipped egg white snow
4. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
5. Bake for 3/4 hour in the oven
6. Turn out onto a serving dish and sprinkle with sugar
