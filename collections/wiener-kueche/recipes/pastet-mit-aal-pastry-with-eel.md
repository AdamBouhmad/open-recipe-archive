## Ingredients

- One large eel
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Dry herbs
- Egg yolk
- Pike farce
- Coal fire
- Puff pastry
- Anchovy sauce or sauce made from the fond
- Spices

## Directions

1. Cut the head off a large eel
2. Skin it and remove the entrails
3. Remove the bones from it
4. Cut it into pieces two fingers wide
5. Score them once
6. Marinate them in vinegar, salt, and dry herbs
7. Remove them after one hour and dry them off
8. Brush the cuts in the eel with egg yolk
9. Fill them with pike farce
10. Braise it in dry herbs over charcoal fire for a quarter hour
11. Make a base of puff pastry in the previously mentioned manner
12. Lay the eel with balls formed from pike farce on top
13. Brush the pastry base
14. Lay a lid over it and form it nicely round
15. Brush again
16. Bake for three quarters of an hour
17. Pour an anchovy sauce or sauce made from the fond into it, which must be piquant
