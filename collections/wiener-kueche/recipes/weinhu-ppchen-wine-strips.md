## Ingredients

- 14 eggs
- 1 pound sugar
- zest of one lemon
- three-quarters pound fine flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Whip the whites of 14 eggs to foam
2. Pound 1 pound sugar, over which lemon zest has been rubbed
3. Gradually stir in three-quarters pound fine flour
4. Spread this mass in 2 finger-widths strips on a butter-rubbed sheet
5. Bake quickly for a quarter hour
6. Cut and while still warm wind over a finger-thick round stick
