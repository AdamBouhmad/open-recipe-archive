## Ingredients

- Finely sifted sugar
- Cold firm marmalade
- Water

## Directions

1. Spread finely sifted sugar on board
2. Make depression in center
3. Add enough cold firm marmalade to make stiff dough
4. Moisten hands with water and form into desired figures such as apples and pears
5. Place on white writing paper
6. Dry in well-heated oven
