## Ingredients

- Kohlrabi, neatly cut
- Yellow turnips
- Cabbage
- Artichokes
- Cherries
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Goose fat
- Thin bacon slices
- Cooked pickled tongues, thinly cut

## Directions

1. Stew the neatly cut kohlrabi, yellow turnips, cabbage, artichokes and cherries separately in a little butter until soft
2. Dust each with a little flour, add a small amount of broth and shake well so they do not fall apart
3. Coat the bottom of a mold with goose fat and line with thin bacon slices
4. Add a layer of cabbage, then a layer of cooked pickled tongues cut into thin pieces
