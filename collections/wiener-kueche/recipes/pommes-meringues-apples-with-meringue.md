## Ingredients

- 10 Maschanzger apples
- Egg white
- Fine sugar

## Directions

1. Make a marmalade from 10 Maschanzger apples, which must be thick, arrange it on a dish.
2. Beat egg white with fine sugar into a meringue and spread it over the apples.
3. Let them take on a yellowish color in the oven and serve them.
