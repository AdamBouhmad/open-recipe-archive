## Ingredients

- various dark, light and yellow vegetables
- butter
- sugar
- broth
- white vegetables
- salt water
- eggs
- veal or fish forcemeat
- sausage

## Directions

1. Punch various dark, light and yellow vegetables with desired forms and braise soft with butter, sugar and the necessary broth.
2. With a vegetable cutter knife, cut white vegetables into beautiful forms, cook them in salt water and roast in butter and sugar.
3. Similarly, cook sprouts, green beans, green peas, asparagus tips and artichokes in salt water, each cooked separately without covering, then drain the water.
4. Butter a mold well, place a peeled, halved potato in the center so the flat side lies on the bottom; the remaining bottom space and the side walls are lined according to taste with the various vegetables.
5. Cover the layer finger-thick with veal, pâté or fish forcemeat and fill the remaining space with the rest of the mixed vegetables.
6. Brush with forcemeat, layer with thinly sliced sweetbread, tongue or sausage, add more forcemeat, press well together, place the mold in a steam bath and let it stand 1 hour covered.
