## Ingredients

- 4 pieces cleaned anchovies
- Some onion
- Green parsley
- 8 tablespoons butter or lard
- Equal amount of flour
- 1/2 liter soup
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut 4 pieces cleaned anchovies, some onion and green parsley finely
2. Make a dark roux from 8 tablespoons butter or lard and equal amount of flour
3. Add the finely chopped herbs, pour 1/2 liter soup over
4. Salt, simmer well and pass through a sieve
