## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters flour
- 1.5 liters milk
- 3 whole eggs
- beef and pork lard, approximately 1.5 kilos combined

## Directions

1. In a deep bowl, place 1.5 liters flour and gradually pour in 1.5 liters milk while stirring constantly until the dough is completely smooth.
2. Beat in 3 whole eggs and let it rest somewhat.
3. Heat beef and pork lard in a small baking pan until very hot.
4. Dip the mushroom mold into the hot fat, let it become very hot, remove it, turn it over so all fat drips off, quickly dip it halfway into the dough, then quickly place the mold back in the hot fat and hold it there until the mushroom has a beautiful brown color.
5. Carefully remove the baked mushroom with a spoon (as it is very fragile) and place it on a sieve lined with blotting paper, which will absorb any excess fat.
6. Fill these mushrooms either with soup or with ragout.
7. Work quickly to finish the mushrooms.
