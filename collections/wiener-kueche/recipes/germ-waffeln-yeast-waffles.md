## Ingredients

- 30 Deca butter, beaten
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 Deca orange or lemon sugar
- 4 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter milk
- 30 Deca flour
- 5 egg whites, beaten
- butter or unsalted bacon fat for oiling the waffle iron
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Gradually beat into 30 Deca beaten butter: 6 egg yolks, 4 Deca orange or lemon sugar, and 4 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter milk, mixing them one after another.
2. Lightly fold in the beaten egg whites from 5 eggs together with 30 Deca flour.
3. Let the dough rise covered in a warm place until it has increased by a third in volume.
4. Oil both sides of the waffle iron with melted butter or preferably unsalted bacon fat.
5. Place 2 spoonfuls of the dough in the iron, close it well, place in coals, and after several minutes turn it to the other side and place in the coals for an equal time.
6. Remove the waffle with two forks, place on a plate, and keep in a warm place.
7. Continue baking waffles until the dough is used up.
8. Shortly before serving, sprinkle with sugar and serve with fruit sauce or desired warm sauce.
9. Note: The first baked waffles usually do not turn out well. Grease the iron moderately but repeatedly. When finished baking, do not wash the iron but only clean it with paper.
