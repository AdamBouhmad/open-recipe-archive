## Ingredients

- 3 dkg butter
- salt
- 1 egg
- 4-5 dkg semolina
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Cream the butter with a pinch of salt
2. Stir in the egg and semolina, let stand for a while
3. Using a spoon dipped in hot broth, form small dumplings and drop into simmering beef broth
4. Let them cook slowly for 5 minutes
5. If the broth boils too vigorously, add a ladle of cold water to reduce the heat
6. Alternatively, use 2 eggs: add the yolks to the creamed butter and fold in the beaten whites together with the semolina; proceed as before
