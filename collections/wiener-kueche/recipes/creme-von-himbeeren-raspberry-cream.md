## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh raspberries
- 16 egg yolks
- 2 glasses champagne wine
- quarter pound sugar

## Directions

1. Select 2 pounds fresh raspberries well and pass through a hair sieve
2. Beat 16 egg yolks into a pot
3. Add 2 glasses champagne wine, quarter pound sugar, and the raspberry juice
4. Let sputter on charcoal fire until everything becomes a thick foam
5. Pile the cream on a plate like a mountain
6. Serve cold
