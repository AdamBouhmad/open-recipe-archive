## Ingredients

- 32 deca butter
- finely chopped sorrel
- finely chopped chervil
- green parsley
- celery
- lamb's lettuce
- salt
- nutmeg
- 2 liters water
- toasted bread rolls
- cream and egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. In 32 deca butter, slowly braise the finely chopped sorrel, chervil, green parsley, celery, and lamb's lettuce
2. Add salt and nutmeg
3. Pour in 2 liters water and let simmer for 3/4 hour
4. Pass the soup through a sieve and serve with toasted bread rolls
5. Some cream whisked with egg yolks can be added if desired
