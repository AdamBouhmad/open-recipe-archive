## Ingredients

- hot butter
- flour
- rose hip sauce or whole rose hips
- broth
- red wine
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. In hot butter add flour
2. If you have rose hip sauce, add 1/4 liter to the roux and pour it with some broth and red wine
3. If using whole rose hips, crush approximately 1.5 liters and add to the sauce
4. Pour with the necessary broth and some wine
5. Season with sugar and a little salt
6. Let it cook well and strain it
7. Instead of broth, you can use the stock from roasted game when pouring the roux
