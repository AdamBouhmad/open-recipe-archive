## Ingredients

- 8 deca butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 10 deca bread crumbs
- 1/2 deca cooked, finely cut meat
- some green parsley
- 1 mushroom
- 1 piece of rhubarb if desired
- some salt
- 5 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy.
2. Stir in egg yolks and whole eggs.
3. Add bread crumbs that have been peeled, cut into dice, and soaked in milk.
4. Add finely cut cooked meat, green parsley, finely cut and roasted mushroom and rhubarb if desired.
5. Add the roasted ingredients with some salt and flour to the mixture.
6. Mix well together.
7. If the dough is too soft, add 1 tablespoon of white bread crumbs.
8. Form knödel of any desired size.
9. Cook them in beef broth and serve in soup.
