## Ingredients

- three quarters taffet or fine herrings apples
- one quarter winter radish
- onion
- pair of herring, deboned
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil
- herring milk (if using soft roe herring)

## Directions

1. Peel three quarters of the taffet or fine herrings apples and one quarter of the winter radish
2. Cut them, removing the seed casings and all pithy parts, into slices
3. Finely chop these slices together with an onion and a pair of deboned herrings
4. Season with salt, pepper, vinegar, and oil
5. If using soft roe herrings, the salad gains greatly in flavor if one first curddles the milk in vinegar and then pours it over
