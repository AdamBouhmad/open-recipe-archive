## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fine sugar
- 12 lemons
- 1 seidel water
- 1 loth fine tea
- 1 measure old Rhine wine
- 1 measure Burgundy
- 1 measure arrack
- Half a bottle good Maraschino
- 1 bottle Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Break 2 pounds of fine sugar into small pieces and put in a soup pot.
2. Pour the juice of 12 lemons and 1 seidel of water, in which 1 loth of fine tea has been steeped, through a napkin over the sugar.
3. As soon as the sugar has melted, pour in 1 measure of old Rhine wine, the same amount of Burgundy and arrack, then half a bottle of good Maraschino and 1 bottle of Champagne wine.
4. Stir everything well together.
5. Serve this punch cold.
