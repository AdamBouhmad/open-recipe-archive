## Ingredients

- 1 large ham
- 5 crushed juniper berries
- 1 bay leaf
- sprigs of thyme
- cold water
- finger-thick, black bread dough

## Directions

1. Place the large ham in a casserole with 5 crushed juniper berries, 1 bay leaf, and several sprigs of thyme
2. Fill the casserole with cold water and simmer the ham slowly for about 1 hour
3. Remove the ham, dry it with a cloth, and wrap it the next day in finger-thick, black bread dough
4. Make several cuts so the dough does not burst during baking
5. Bake the ham for 3 hours in a baker's oven or baking oven
6. Allow the ham to cool in the bread dough and it can be stored in it for a longer time
7. Note: The cooking time before wrapping in bread dough depends on the size of the ham - a large ham requires 1 hour, a smaller ham only 1/2 hour, and a very small ham is wrapped in bread dough and baked without precooking
