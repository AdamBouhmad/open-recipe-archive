## Ingredients

- mushrooms of arbitrary size
- mushroom stems
- green parsley
- onion
- butter
- salt
- white pepper
- breadcrumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Break off the stems from mushrooms and peel them
2. Place them in cold water and then in a strainer
3. Finely chop the mushroom stems, remaining trimmings, green parsley, some onion and one whole mushroom
4. Roast this mixture in butter, then stir in the necessary salt and some white pepper to make the stuffing
5. Arrange the hollowed out mushrooms in a well-buttered casserole with the stuffing facing upward
6. Fill these with the paste, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, pour cream over them and braise in the oven for 10 minutes
7. Serve with any meat dishes
