## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- one small bottle of good aged wine
- one small stick of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks in a pot, pour one small bottle of good aged wine to it, place a small stick of vanilla in it
2. Whisk wine and eggs over gentle charcoal fire until it is about to boil once
3. Immediately pour it onto a plate, remove the vanilla, and serve warm
