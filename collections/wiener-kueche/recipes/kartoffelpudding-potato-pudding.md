## Ingredients

- 7 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 6 bread rolls (Semmeln)
- ½ liter milk
- 18 dkg cooked and passed potatoes
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream together 7 dkg butter
2. Mix in 3 egg yolks and salt
3. Add 6 bread rolls (trimmed, soaked in milk, and well drained)
4. Mix in 18 dkg cooked, passed, and cooled potatoes
5. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Fill into a pudding mold and steam for ¾ hour
7. Loosen, turn out, pour melted butter over, and serve warm
