## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper (if desired)
- Semolina biscuits or bread

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach as described above, pass it through a sieve, and bring it to a boil with 4 drams butter, the necessary salt, and if desired, pepper, stirring constantly.
2. Serve as a side dish or on a plate with toasted bread slices (Croutons).
