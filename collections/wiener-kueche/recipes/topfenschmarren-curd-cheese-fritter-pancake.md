## Ingredients

- curd cheese, pressed
- 3 egg yolks
- cream
- salt
- lemon zest
- 4 dkg raisins
- 3 egg whites, beaten stiff
- 14 dkg flour
- 6 dkg hot butter or fat

## Directions

1. Mix the pressed curd cheese with 3 egg yolks, cream, salt, lemon zest, 4 dkg raisins, and 3 beaten egg whites
2. Fold in 14 dkg flour
3. Pour into a pan with 6 dkg hot butter or fat
4. Prepare and serve like Semmelschmarren
