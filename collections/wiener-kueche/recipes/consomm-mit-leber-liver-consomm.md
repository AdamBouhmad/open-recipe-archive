## Ingredients

- 10 dkg beef liver
- 2 bread rolls
- milk
- 4 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the liver, pass through sieve
2. Cream together butter, egg yolks, salt, and pepper
3. Mix in the liver, bread rolls that have been soaked in milk and well squeezed out, and pass through sieve, then fold in 2 egg whites
4. Cook mixture in consommé form for 30 minutes
5. Loosen, turn out of form, slice, and serve warm
