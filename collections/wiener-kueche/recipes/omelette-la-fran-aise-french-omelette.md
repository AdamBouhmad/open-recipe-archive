## Ingredients

- Egg whites (4, stiffly beaten)
- Flour, fine (1 tablespoon)
- Sugar (1/2 tablespoon)
- Salt (a pinch)
- Egg yolks (4)

## Directions

1. Add flour, sugar, salt and egg yolks to the stiffly beaten egg white and stir lightly
