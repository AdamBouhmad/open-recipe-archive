## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk
- 14-25 dkg flour
- 2-3 eggs
- salt
- fat for frying
- marmalade or topfen filling

## Directions

1. In a pot, beat together 1/4 liter milk with 14-25 dkg flour, 2-3 eggs, and salt until smooth
2. Heat some melted fat in the palatschinken pan and pour it back out
3. Pour in a small ladle of batter and let it run thin and round
4. Fry lightly on both sides and place on a flat plate
5. Repeat with the remaining batter
6. When all palatschinken are done, spread with marmalade, roll, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm
7. Or spread with topfen filling, sprinkle raisins on top, roll, place in a well-buttered baking pan, and bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes under frequent basting with hot milk
