## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams butter
- 4 decagrams finely ground sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 decagrams pressed yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 30 decagrams flour
- melted butter for brushing
- cinnamon mixed with finely ground sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, mix in the finely ground sugar and salt, then gradually stir in the egg yolks
2. Mix in the pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk and the flour, stirring well together
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
4. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in height
5. Lay the dough on a flour-dusted board, roll it out to the thickness of a knife back, and cut it into strips 3 fingers long and 2 fingers wide
6. Sprinkle the strips with finely ground sugar mixed with cinnamon, roll them together, and lay them side by side in a butter-greased casserole with the ends of the rolled dough facing upward
7. Brush the areas where the rolls touch each other with melted butter
8. Cover with a cloth and let rise in a warm place until slightly higher
9. Brush with melted butter, pour 1/2 liter of boiling milk mixed with 2 tablespoons sugar over them, and bake slowly for 1 hour
10. Turn out the casserole, separate the rolls, arrange on a plate, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
