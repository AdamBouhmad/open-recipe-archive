## Ingredients

- ½ l milk or cream
- 1 dl strong black coffee
- 5 egg yolks
- 12 dkg sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk the milk with 5 egg yolks, 12 dkg sugar and the black coffee over gentle heat until slightly thickened.
2. Strain it and freeze like Vanilla Ice Cream.
