## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 11 dkg butter
- risen yeast starter (Dampfl)
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 spoons sugar
- 1 egg
- finely chopped lemon zest
- 1 spoon lukewarm milk
- poppy seed or nut filling
- egg white for brushing
- beaten egg for brushing before baking

## Directions

1. On a board, rub together 21 dkg flour with 11 dkg butter
2. Add the risen yeast starter, 1 pinch salt, 2 spoons sugar, 1 egg, finely chopped lemon zest and 1 spoon lukewarm milk
3. Knead into dough and let rise under a warm casserole for 1 hour
4. Roll out thinly, cut into rectangles
5. Brush the edges with egg white, place filling in the middle and press edges together
6. Form into crescent shapes, place on greased baking sheet
7. Set again near the oven to rise
8. Before baking, brush with beaten egg and bake at moderate heat until light brown
9. Sprinkle with sugar and serve cooled
