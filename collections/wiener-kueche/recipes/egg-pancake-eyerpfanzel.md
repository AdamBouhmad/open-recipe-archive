## Ingredients

- 6 loth butter
- ten hard-boiled eggs
- a bread roll soaked in milk
- fresh parsley
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- half a seidel of cream
- salt
- pounded pepper

## Directions

1. Beat 6 loth butter until fluffy, chop ten hard-boiled eggs finely together, take a bread roll soaked in milk and fresh parsley, chop everything finely and add it to the butter.
2. Stir in two whole eggs and two yolks, and add half a seidel of cream along with a little salt and pounded pepper.
3. Butter a casserole dish, add the mixture, and bake until golden.
4. When baked, turn it out onto a dish and serve.
