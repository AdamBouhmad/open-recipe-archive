## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Flour
- Water
- Vinegar
- Warm water

## Directions

1. This root must be cleaned very quickly, as it loses color through prolonged contact with the hand and turns red
2. To prevent this, put some flour with water in a bowl, stir it well, add some vinegar and warm water, and as soon as a root is cleaned, place it immediately in this mixture
