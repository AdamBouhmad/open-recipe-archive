## Ingredients

- 6 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- Pinch of salt
- 4 egg whites
- 3 dkg flour
- 4 dkg butter
- 3 tablespoons milk
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Cream together 6 dkg butter with 4 egg yolks, 7 dkg sugar, and a pinch of salt
2. Fold in 4 beaten egg whites along with 3 dkg flour
3. Heat 4 dkg butter and 3 tablespoons milk in an omelet pan
4. Pour batter in and smooth
5. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes until light brown
6. Using a spoon, cut out dumplings and place on a platter
7. Sprinkle with sugar
