## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned poularde (hen)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Small truffles
- Veal farce (cold pâté farce)
- Thin slices of bacon
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. Cut the poularde along the back and lay it open; carefully pull out the bones while leaving the flesh attached to the skin.
2. Sprinkle the poularde with salt and pepper, lay small truffles upon it, spread with veal farce, add another layer of truffles, spread again with farce, and continue until the poularde is completely filled and has regained its natural form.
3. Prepare the butter dough and form the pâté as described in the 'Goose Liver Pâté' recipe (No. 1057).
4. Line the inside of the pâté with thin slices of bacon.
5. Spread the bottom 2 fingers high with veal farce, sprinkle with finely diced truffles, and place the poularde with its back upon it.
6. Spread the poularde with veal farce, fill the pâté with cut truffles and chopped goose liver, spread again with veal farce, and cover with a slice of bacon that has been scored several times beforehand.
7. Proceed as described in the 'Goose Liver Pâté' recipe.
8. Bake for 1½ to 2 hours.
9. Use ⅕ kilo of veal farce for the poularde and for filling the pâté.
