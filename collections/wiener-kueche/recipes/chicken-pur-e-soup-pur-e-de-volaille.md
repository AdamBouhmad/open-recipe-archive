## Ingredients

- butter
- bouquet of green parsley
- 3 pieces of mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 1 whole chicken
- 1/2 liter white soup
- 2 liters soup
- 8 ounces soft peas
- 4 hard egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Place butter, a small bouquet of green parsley and 3 pieces of thinly sliced mushrooms in a casserole
2. Add 1 whole chicken and roast it
3. After roasting, add 1/2 liter of white soup and let it braise gently
4. Remove the chicken, skin it, and remove the bones
5. Pound the bones into the casserole where the chicken was braised
6. Add 2 liters of soup and boil well
7. To the removed chicken meat, add 8 ounces of soft peas and pound this together very finely in a mortar
8. Place the pounded mixture in a casserole, pour the chicken soup over it, stir in the necessary salt and let it boil well
9. Pass it through with 4 hard egg yolks, place the pot in hot water so the soup does not boil, but stir it frequently with a serving spoon
10. The soup is usually served with braised rice or fried bread
11. You can also add chicken breast cut into pieces and peas boiled in salt water
