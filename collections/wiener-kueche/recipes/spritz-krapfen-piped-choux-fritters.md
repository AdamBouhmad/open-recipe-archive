## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk
- Lard for frying
- Powdered sugar for serving
- Cream or warm marmalade for serving

## Directions

1. Use choux pastry made from 1/4 liter milk, fill it into a metal piping syringe with a star-shaped opening.
2. Using the wooden pestle, pipe the dough from the syringe in cross or feather shapes into the hot lard.
3. When the fritters are browned on one side, turn them with a skimming spoon and fry them equally on the second side, shaking the pan frequently during this time.
4. When the fritters have puffed up to twice their height and have a brown, crispy crust, remove them with a skimming spoon, place on paper towels for a few moments, then arrange elevated in a dish, sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and serve hot.
5. Cream or warm marmalade can be served with them as desired.
