## Ingredients

- 20 dkg grated hazelnuts
- 20 dkg sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix 20 dkg grated hazelnuts, 20 dkg sugar and 2 egg whites in a bowl
2. Place the mass on a board sprinkled with sugar
3. Knead it to a dough
4. Roll it out square
5. Brush with thick glaze
6. Cut into oblong pieces
7. Place on a buttered baking sheet
8. Bake at low heat
