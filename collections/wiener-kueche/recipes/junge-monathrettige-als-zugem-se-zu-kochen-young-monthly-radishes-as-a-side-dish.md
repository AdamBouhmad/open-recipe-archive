## Ingredients

- small, equal, young monthly radishes
- sugar
- fat or lard
- radishes (blanched beforehand)
- flour
- meat or brown soup

## Directions

1. Take small, equal, young monthly radishes
2. Scrape and wash them clean
3. Set a small piece of sugar with fat or lard on heat and let it become chestnut brown
4. Add the radishes, which must have been blanched beforehand
5. Let them braise gently until tender
6. Sprinkle a little flour over them
7. Add some meat or brown soup
8. Let it reduce briefly
9. Serve to table. You can also lay bratwurst or fried foods on top as garnish
