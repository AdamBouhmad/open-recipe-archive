## Ingredients

- ½ kg spinach
- salt
- 5 dkg butter
- spinach water or broth
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and stems from spinach, wash thoroughly
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook uncovered for 20 minutes
3. Drain and pass through a sieve
4. Make a light roux with 5 dkg butter and moisten with spinach water or broth
5. Add the spinach, season with salt and pepper, cook well
6. Alternative: moisten the spinach with milk instead of broth or water
