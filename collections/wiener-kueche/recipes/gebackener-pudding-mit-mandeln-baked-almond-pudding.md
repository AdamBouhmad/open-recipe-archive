## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pfund almonds
- finely soft-boiled lemon peel
- 4 Pfund sugar
- several spoonfuls rose water
- 4 Pfund butter
- 8 eggs
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Pound 1/2 pound almonds with finely soft-boiled lemon peel, 4 pounds sugar, and several spoonfuls of rose water in a mortar
2. Melt 4 pounds butter
3. Beat 8 eggs and 4 egg whites very thickly
4. Mix the remaining ingredients together
5. Grease a rimmed baking dish with butter
6. Add the mixture and bake slowly for one hour
