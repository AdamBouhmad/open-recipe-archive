## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk puree
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- caraway
- 1 1/4 liters boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir together milk puree with 3 tablespoons flour, some salt and caraway
2. Stir into 1 1/4 liters boiling water, cook and pass through a sieve
3. The vessel in which this is cooked must however be twice as large as the mass occupies, so that it does not boil over while cooking
