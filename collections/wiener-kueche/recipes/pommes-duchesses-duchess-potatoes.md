## Ingredients

- 6 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 dkg grated Parmesan
- ½ kg cooked, passed, cooled potatoes
- flour
- 1 egg, beaten
- butter for the baking sheet

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter with 2 egg yolks, salt, grated Parmesan, and the cooked, passed, cooled potatoes.
2. Roll out the dough on a floured board to the thickness of a small finger and cut into rectangles or squares.
3. Press a grid pattern into the top with a knife.
4. Brush with beaten egg.
5. Place on a buttered, floured baking sheet and bake at moderately high heat until light brown.
6. Keep warm until serving.
