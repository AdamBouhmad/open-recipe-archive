## Ingredients

- one seidel of dried well-sorted peas
- 3 halves of pure river water
- celery
- parsley
- yellow turnip roots
- some baked bread slices
- some whole pepper and mace

## Directions

1. Fill a pot with the peas and water and let it cook
2. Add celery, parsley, yellow turnip roots, some baked bread slices, some whole pepper and mace
3. Let it simmer slowly, then strain the broth and use it as needed
