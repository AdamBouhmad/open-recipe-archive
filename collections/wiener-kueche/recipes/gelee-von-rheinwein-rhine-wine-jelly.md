## Ingredients

- twelve scalded calf's feet
- 2 measures of water
- one bottle of Rhine wine
- half a pound of sugar
- zest of one lemon
- juice of one lemon
- egg white from 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Slit the calf's feet at the hoof and divide them apart, remove the long middle bone and discard it
2. Cook the feet with 2 measures of water for 4 hours, skimming often but without salting
3. Pass them through a hair cloth
4. Set them again on charcoal fire to reduce to half and become a thick stock
5. Add one bottle of Rhine wine, half a pound of sugar with the zest of one lemon rubbed into it, and the juice, without letting it boil so the fine flavor does not escape
6. Clarify the jelly with the foam from 6 eggs
7. Filter it
8. Fill into the mold
9. Set in a cool place
