## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 1 egg white
- 7 dkg grated chocolate
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 1 egg white and 7 dkg grated chocolate in a wide mixing basin until thick
2. Roll out on a sugar-dusted board
3. Cut out rings
4. Place on buttered baking sheet and dry in the oven more than bake
