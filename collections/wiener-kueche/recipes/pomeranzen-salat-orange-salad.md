## Ingredients

- fresh oranges
- sugar
- preserved sour cherry flesh

## Directions

1. Slice fresh oranges thinly, leaf by leaf
2. Arrange them neatly on a plate
3. Sprinkle sugar over them so they are sweet enough
4. Place preserved sour cherry flesh on each leaf
5. Serve
