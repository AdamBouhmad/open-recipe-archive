## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Veal forcemeat
- Duck livers
- Mushrooms
- Anchovies
- Fine dry herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bacon slices
- Sauce Haschee

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the ducks properly and loosen the skin from the breasts
2. Fill the breasts with a well-prepared veal forcemeat to which you must add the duck livers, mushrooms, anchovies, fine dry herbs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Pound all this together in a mortar and use it as mentioned above
4. Bind the ducks carefully so the forcemeat does not fall out
5. Also bind the ducks with bacon slices and roast them over not too strong fire on the spit
6. When serving, give a sauce haschee underneath and glaze the ducks nicely with it
