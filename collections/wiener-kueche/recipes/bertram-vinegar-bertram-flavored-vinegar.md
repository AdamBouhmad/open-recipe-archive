## Ingredients

- Picked leaves from 5 bunches of Bertram
- 5 liters of good vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the picked leaves from 5 bunches of Bertram in a bottle
2. Pour 5 liters of good vinegar over them, but leave a space 3 fingers wide at the top of the bottle
3. Cork the bottle and bind it with linen cloth
4. Set it in the sun or a warm place for several weeks
5. Strain the vinegar through a linen cloth
6. Fill it into a bottle, cork it well and seal it
