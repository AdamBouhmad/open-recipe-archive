## Ingredients

- 8 ounces butter
- 8 ounces flour
- 1/2 liter white beef broth

## Directions

1. Put 8 ounces butter in a saucepan and stir in 8 ounces flour
2. Gradually pour in 1/2 liter white beef broth and stir
3. Let it cook until boiling, then pass through a sieve
4. Use for various sauces and vegetables
