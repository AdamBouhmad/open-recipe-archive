## Ingredients

- 16 Deka Butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- 30 Deka semolina
- 1/2 liter flour for dough

## Directions

1. Whip 16 Deka butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 5 whole eggs, 1/4 liter milk cream into 30 Deka semolina
3. Let stand covered for 2 hours
4. Spread this filling on stretched dough made from 1/2 liter flour
5. Proceed as with lung strudel
