## Ingredients

- lung roast
- salt
- wine
- fine spice herbs
- whole pepper
- ginger
- must (or shriveled grapes)
- a piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Take a lung roast, wash it out and salt it
2. Let it braise covered in a casserole with a little wine, fine spice herbs, whole pepper and ginger until it becomes completely tender
3. Pour must over it, or in the absence of must, press shriveled grapes through a clean cloth
4. Add a piece of sugar and let the sauce simmer until it becomes thick
5. When the meat is plated, strain the sauce over it
