## Ingredients

- 1 eel
- 2 liters court bouillon
- 2 liters seasoned lenten root vegetable soup

## Directions

1. Lay the eel on a board and make a strong cut two fingers above the tail on the broad side with a sharp knife so the blood can run out
2. To skin the eel, hang it by the head on a hook, cut the skin around, loosen it carefully with a cloth, and pull it off quickly
3. Alternatively, pierce the eel on a spit and singe on all sides over fire or spirit flame until the skin forms a crust, then quickly wipe the skin and fat off with a rough cloth
4. Roast and bone an eel weighing 1 kilo, cut the flesh carefully from the bones into thin slender pieces
5. Cook gently in 2 liters court bouillon
6. Put the eel pieces in the soup pot and pour over them 2 liters of seasoned lenten root vegetable soup in which the eel was cooked
