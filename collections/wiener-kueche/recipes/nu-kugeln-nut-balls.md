## Ingredients

- 15 dkg cube sugar
- water
- 1/2 kg grated nuts
- 1/2 kg raw grated almonds

## Directions

1. Cook 15 dkg cube sugar with a small scoop of water to a thick consistency
2. Pour over the 1/2 kg grated nuts and 1/2 kg raw grated almonds mixed with part of the sugar
3. Stir together
4. Form balls with wet hands
5. Roll in grated chocolate
6. Dry them
7. Serve in paper cups
