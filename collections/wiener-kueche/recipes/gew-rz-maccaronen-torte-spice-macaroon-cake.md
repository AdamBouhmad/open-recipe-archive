## Ingredients

- Wafer sheets
- Spice macaroon dough
- Sugar (about 8 to 12 loths)
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Glue together as many wafer sheets as required for the size of the cake
2. Place the dish intended for serving on the wafer sheet and cut it to size
3. Cover it to a finger's thickness with the aforementioned spice macaroon dough
4. Push it into the oven
5. While the cake bakes, put about 8 to 12 loths of sugar into a pan and cook it with orange blossom water until it shows high bubbles when stirred with a skimming spoon
6. When the cake is baked light brown, pour the sugar mass over it
7. Return it to the oven for a couple of minutes so the glaze dries
8. These cakes are delicious in aroma and taste
