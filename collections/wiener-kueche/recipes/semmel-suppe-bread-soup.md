## Ingredients

- 6 crusts removed bread rolls, diced
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- approximately 2 liters boiling beef broth

## Directions

1. Place 6 diced bread rolls (crusts removed) in a casserole
2. Add 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks, stir well together
3. Pour approximately 2 liters boiling beef broth over it without stirring much
4. It must boil only from one side and is served after 3/4 hour
5. Be very careful that no lumps form during cooking
