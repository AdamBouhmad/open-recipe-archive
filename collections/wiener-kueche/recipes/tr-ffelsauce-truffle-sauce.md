## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Shallots
- Butter
- Truffles
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 good glass white wine
- Several spoonfuls espagnole or brown coulis
- Lemon juice
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Finely chop parsley and shallots and sweat in butter.
2. Peel and chop the truffles and add them.
3. Add salt, pepper, 1 good glass of white wine, and several spoonfuls of espagnole or brown coulis.
4. Let everything reduce slowly together.
5. Remove scum and fat.
6. When serving, add lemon juice and nutmeg.
