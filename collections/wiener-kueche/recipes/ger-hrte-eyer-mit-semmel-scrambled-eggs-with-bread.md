## Ingredients

- Bread crumbs (Semmelbrösel)
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Lemon peel, chopped
- Fresh egg yolks
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk for about 2 hours until very soft, then press through a fine sieve.
2. Add a little salt and sugar and chopped lemon peel.
3. Beat in as many fresh egg yolks as needed and mix with the bread.
4. Beat the egg whites to stiff snow and fold in.
5. Coat a casserole with butter, put the mixture in it, and bake in the oven or under a cake cover with heat from above and below.
6. Turn it out onto a dish, dust with sugar, and glaze it with a hot shovel.
