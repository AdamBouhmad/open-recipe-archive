## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- 2 spoons sugar
- finely chopped lemon peel
- 2 spoons cream
- quark (curd cheese), passed through a sieve
- 3 dkg currants

## Directions

1. To the mixture of 4 dkg butter, add 1 egg, 2 spoons sugar, finely chopped lemon peel, 2 spoons cream, the passed quark, and 3 dkg currants.
