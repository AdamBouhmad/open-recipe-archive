## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 16 deca finely ground sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 16 deca almonds, finely ground with skin and passed
- 16 deca grated

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca butter gradually with 16 deca finely ground sugar and 4 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Add 16 deca finely ground and passed almonds and 16 deca grated
