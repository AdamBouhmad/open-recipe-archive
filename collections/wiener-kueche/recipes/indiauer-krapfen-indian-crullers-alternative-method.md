## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- 16 deka sugar
- 10 deka egg butter
- 24 deka flour
- thin warm vanilla marmalade
- chocolate glaze
- whipped cream mixed with sugar or cream

## Directions

1. Add 16 deka sugar, 10 deka egg butter, and 24 deka flour to stiff snow from 10 egg whites, stir lightly and quickly
2. Using a piping bag, pipe uniform large heaps onto a baking sheet lined with white paper
3. Bake in the oven at low heat slowly for 1 hour until golden
4. Remove from the sheet and hollow them out with a fine knife on the underside
5. Join every two together, brush them with a brush using thin warm vanilla marmalade, and cover with chocolate glaze
6. Only when you use them are they taken apart on the side and filled with whipped cream mixed with sugar or cream and served
