## Ingredients

- 40-50 dkg beef or veal roast remnants
- 1 bread roll soaked in water
- a piece of onion
- parsley
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- hot lard for frying
- cooked lettuce, spinach, or sauerkraut

## Directions

1. Soak a bread roll in water, squeeze out well.
2. Mince the beef or veal with the squeezed bread roll, onion, and parsley twice.
3. Mix with 1 egg, salt, and pepper.
4. Form into patties, roll in bread crumbs.
5. Fry in hot lard.
6. Serve with cooked lettuce, spinach, or sauerkraut.
