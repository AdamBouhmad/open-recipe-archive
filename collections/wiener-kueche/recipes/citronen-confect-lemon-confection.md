## Ingredients

- marzipan dough
- egg white
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare like the cinnamon confection, but note that when dissolving the marzipan dough, use the white of the egg, not the yolk.
2. Instead of cinnamon, season this confection with grated lemon peel.
3. Cut various shapes from wafer sheets, spread the mass on them, and bake in moderate heat.
