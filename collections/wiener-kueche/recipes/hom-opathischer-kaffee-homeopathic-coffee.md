## Ingredients

- roasted ground coffee
- roasted pounded acorns
- cold water
- cream and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. For homeopathic coffee, take a mixture of roasted ground coffee and roasted pounded acorns
2. Steep 1 spoonful of this in 1/2 liter water covered, but only for a few minutes
3. Add some cold water and let the coffee stand at the side until the coffee has settled to the bottom
4. Pour the clear coffee into the pot, place this in hot water and serve the coffee with cream and sugar
