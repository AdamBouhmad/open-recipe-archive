## Ingredients

- 8 ounces truffles, very finely chopped
- 12 ounces fish or other meats, diced small
- Butter
- Cream or soup
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 5 eggs
- 2 yolks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread rolls, baked in butter

## Directions

1. Cut 8 ounces of truffles very finely, and cut 12 ounces of fish or other meats into small dice.
2. Braise these in butter and add a few spoonfuls of cream or soup.
3. Beat 2 tablespoons milk, 5 eggs, and 2 yolks together, season with salt and pepper.
4. Heat 8 ounces of butter until hot, stir in the beaten egg mixture.
5. When it begins to thicken, fold in the chopped truffles and meat mixture.
6. Serve with bread rolls baked in butter.
