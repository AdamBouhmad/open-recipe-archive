## Ingredients

- 16 loth ordinary white bread
- half a measure of water

## Directions

1. Cook 16 loth of ordinary white bread in half a measure of water for one hour
2. Strain it through a sieve
3. Let the water evaporate over gentle heat until it becomes thick
4. Let it cool
