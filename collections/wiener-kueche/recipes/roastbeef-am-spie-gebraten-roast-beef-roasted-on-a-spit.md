## Ingredients

- A piece of beef as described above
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Paper
- String
- Fat for basting

## Directions

1. After salting and peppering the meat, let it rest for 1 hour.
2. Thread it lengthwise through the center onto a spit so it maintains even rotation.
3. Brush two sheets of paper laid together heavily with butter, wrap the meat with this paper, bind it with string, and roast it at strong heat for 1.5 hours, basting frequently with fat.
4. The paper prevents it from forming a heavy crust immediately.
5. Remove the paper 0.5 hour before the roasting is complete, increase the heat to give the roast a beautiful color, and continue roasting for another 0.5 hour under frequent basting on the spit.
6. Serve in its full juices and well-roasted. Serve the juice in a separate sauce boat.
