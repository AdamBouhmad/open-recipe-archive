## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 6 dkg butter
- salt
- 1 egg
- 2 spoonfuls cream
- flour
- fresh apricots or plums
- sugar
- salt water

## Directions

1. Make a potato dough from the potatoes, 6 dkg butter, salt, 1 egg, 2 spoonfuls cream and flour (see doughs section).
2. Form into a roll and cut into equal-sized pieces.
3. Stretch these pieces apart, wrap a wiped apricot or plum in each and form into dumplings.
4. Instead of the stone, place a small piece of sugar in the fruit.
5. Place the dumplings in a casserole in boiling salt water and let them cook slowly for 8 minutes.
6. Carefully remove them with a slotted spoon, let them drain.
7. Toss them in a casserole where 10 dkg hot butter or fat has been used to toast breadcrumbs until dark.
8. Place on a round dish and serve sprinkled with sugar.
