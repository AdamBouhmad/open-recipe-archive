## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg table piece or oxtail
- 6 h root vegetables
- 1 onion ring
- 1 bay leaf
- thyme
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 5 dkg fat
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the cleaned, washed root vegetables into thin slices
2. Roast them dark with the onion ring, bay leaf, thyme, peppercorns and spice berries in the fat
3. Beat and wash the meat, salt and pepper it, add to the vegetables
4. Pour in some water and vinegar, cover and braise for 2-2½ hours, turning and adding water frequently
5. Remove the meat, strain and pass the sauce, season with vinegar or wine to taste, bring to a boil again and serve separately or with the meat
