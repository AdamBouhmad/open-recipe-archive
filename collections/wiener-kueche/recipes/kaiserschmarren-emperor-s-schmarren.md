## Ingredients

- ¾ l Milk
- 18 dkg Flour
- 3 Egg yolks
- 6 dkg Sugar
- Salt
- 6 dkg melted butter
- 3 Egg whites
- 8 dkg Butter
- Sugar
- Compote

## Directions

1. Mix milk with flour, egg yolks, sugar, salt, and melted butter until smooth
2. Fold in beaten egg whites
3. Heat butter in a large flat pan
4. Pour in mixture not too thick and bake on the stove without stirring until light brown
5. Turn over and bake evenly light brown on the other side
6. Divide into small pieces with a fork and roast a little longer
7. Arrange on a round platter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm with compote
8. Note: if the pan is too small and the mixture is poured too thick, it will not bake through
