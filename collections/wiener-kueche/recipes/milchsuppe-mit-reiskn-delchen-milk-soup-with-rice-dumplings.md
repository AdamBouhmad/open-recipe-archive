## Ingredients

- 8 loth rice
- water
- 5 eggs
- sugar
- a teacup of melted butter
- crushed cinnamon
- grated lemon peel
- butter
- milk
- crushed almonds
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Soak 8 loth rice in pure water overnight, drain it
2. Cook the rice very soft and stiff, then let it cool
3. Stir in 5 eggs, sugar according to taste, a teacup of melted butter, crushed cinnamon and grated lemon peel
4. Form small smooth dumplings and bake them in a butter-greased cake pan until brown
5. Prepare milk cooked with crushed almonds, egg yolks, sugar and cinnamon
6. Serve the milk soup over the dumplings after the soup has formed a skin
7. Dust with cinnamon
