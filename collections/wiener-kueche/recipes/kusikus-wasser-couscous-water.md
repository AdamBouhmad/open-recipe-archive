## Ingredients

- 16 deka washed couscous
- 1 liter water
- Finely pounded sugar
- Several egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil 16 deka washed couscous in 1 liter of water for about half an hour.
2. Strain it through a fine sieve, add finely pounded sugar as desired, and sprinkle in several egg yolks.
