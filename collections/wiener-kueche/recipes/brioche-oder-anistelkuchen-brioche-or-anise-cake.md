## Ingredients

- 28 Deca flour
- 4 Deca fresh yeast
- Some dissolved fresh yeast
- Butter
- Eggs
- Salt
- Caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board and make a well in the center
2. Crumble the yeast into the well and add some dissolved yeast
3. Mix with a knife until a smooth, fairly firm dough forms
4. Cover and let rest for some time
5. Form the dough into 2-finger-long and thin rods or pretzels
6. Place on a buttered baking sheet, brush with beaten egg
7. Sprinkle with finely crushed salt mixed with caraway seeds
8. Bake until golden in the oven
