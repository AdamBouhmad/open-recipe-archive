## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar, clarified (see recipe 1542)
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Punch essence or tea with rum, instead of wine, lemon and orange juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
2. Instead of wine, use punch essence or tea with rum
