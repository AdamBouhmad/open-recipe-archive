## Ingredients

- Perch
- Salt water (boiling)
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the blue-cooked salmon
2. Instead of vinegar, pour only boiling salt water over it
3. Serve with Hollandaise, Mustard, or Butter sauce with lemon juice
