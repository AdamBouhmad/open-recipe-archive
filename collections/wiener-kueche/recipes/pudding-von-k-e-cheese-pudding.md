## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter cream
- 7 decagrams Parmesan cheese
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 9 decagrams flour
- 3 egg whites
- 4 decagrams grated cheese
- peas
- chopped ham
- butter

## Directions

1. In a small pot, beat the cream with 3 egg yolks, a pinch of salt, and 9 decagrams flour until smooth
2. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites together with 4 decagrams grated cheese
3. Fill the mixture into a buttered, floured pudding mold
4. Steam for 45 minutes until set
5. Turn out onto a flat plate
6. Garnish with fresh green peas cooked and lightly stewed in butter, and chopped ham
7. Serve with hot butter and grated cheese on the side
