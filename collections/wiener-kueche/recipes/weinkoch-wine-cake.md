## Ingredients

- one egg-sized portion of good lard
- fine bread crumbs, finely grated
- two parts wine and one part water
- one-quarter pound butter
- five whole eggs and five yolks
- raisins, cleaned and washed
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Let one egg-sized portion of good lard become hot in a cake pan
2. Roast finely grated bread crumbs golden yellow in it
3. Let two parts wine and one part water simmer together and pour it over the bread crumbs
4. Stir it like a child's pudding and allow it to cool well
5. Sweeten it well with sugar
6. Beat one-quarter pound butter until fluffy
7. Stir in five whole eggs and five yolks, each stirred well, and the cooled pudding
8. Add cleaned and washed raisins and some ground cinnamon
9. Stir everything for half an hour
10. Spread a sheet pan and rim or a cake pan with butter
11. Pour the pudding into it and bake it slowly
