## Ingredients

- 28 deca blanched, very finely ground almonds
- 1 liter cream
- 30 deca vanilla sugar
- 4 deca flour
- 24 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put blanched, finely ground almonds in a saucepan
2. Pour 1 liter cream over them
3. Stir over heat until it begins to boil
4. Strain through a sieve and let cool
5. Meanwhile, gradually stir 30 deca vanilla sugar and 4 deca flour with 24 egg yolks
6. Combine this mixture with the cooled cream
7. Fill into a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour
8. Cook covered for 3/4 hour in a water bath
