## Ingredients

- 28 deca flour
- 8 deca butter
- 1 whole egg
- salt
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. When 28 deca flour has been finely worked with 8 deca butter, make a depression in the middle
2. Add 1 whole egg, a little salt, and 1 teaspoon salt to it
3. Work into a dough and use as desired
