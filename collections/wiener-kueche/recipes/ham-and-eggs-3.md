## Ingredients

- 8 dkg ham
- 6 fresh eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. In an omelet pan, let a piece of butter become hot and fry the ham on both sides.
2. Break the eggs individually into a small bowl, then let them slide onto the ham.
3. Salt and pepper them.
4. Place in the oven for approximately 8 minutes until the eggs are set.
5. Sprinkle them with chopped chives.
6. Serve in the pan or on a plate.
