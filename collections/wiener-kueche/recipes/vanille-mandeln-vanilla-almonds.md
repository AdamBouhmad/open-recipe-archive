## Ingredients

- Dough as for almond pastries
- 8 Deka butter
- 8 Deka granulated sugar
- Pinch of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter cream
- Snow from 4 egg whites
- 8 Deka flour
- Candied pineapple (4 pieces per almond)

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough and treat as described for almond pastries (No. 1116)
2. Instead of almond filling, fill with the following mixture:
3. Beat 8 Deka butter fluffy
4. Add 8 Deka granulated sugar, pinch of salt, 4 egg yolks and 3/4 liter cream, one after another
5. Finally lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites simultaneously with 8 Deka flour
6. Stick 4 pieces of candied pineapple in each almond
7. Bake as described for almond pastries
