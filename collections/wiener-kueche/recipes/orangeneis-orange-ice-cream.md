## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 3 lemons
- 30 dkg cube sugar
- 1 dl water
- 2½ lemons

## Directions

1. Rub 30 dkg cube sugar on the wiped oranges, add 1 dl water and cook-spin for 15 minutes.
2. Squeeze the oranges and 2½ lemons, mix with the spun sugar and 2 dl cold water, weigh with the sugar scale (19°), strain through a hair sieve and freeze like Vanilla Ice Cream.
