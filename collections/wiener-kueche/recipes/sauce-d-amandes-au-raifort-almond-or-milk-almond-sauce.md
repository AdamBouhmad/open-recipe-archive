## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- About 3 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 liter boiling milk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- Sugar to taste
- 2 tablespoons grated horseradish
- 4 peeled almonds, moistened with milk, finely crushed

## Directions

1. 1/4 liter lukewarm milk and about 3 tablespoons flour are stirred smooth, then gradually added to 3/4 liter boiling milk with constant stirring
2. Add 8 tablespoons butter, sugar to taste, and 2 tablespoons grated horseradish and let everything boil well together
3. For the preparation of almond sauce, one must use 1 tablespoon less flour and replace it with 4 peeled almonds, moistened with milk and then finely crushed
4. Proceed further as indicated above
5. The horseradish goes in at the end and must not boil
