## Ingredients

- orange peel
- unripe small pomegranates (optional, one-third or one-fourth of the amount)
- wine spirit

## Directions

1. Cut the orange peel and pomegranates quite small, or crush the latter
2. Pour two to three times the weight of wine spirit into a bottle with a somewhat wide mouth
3. Do not fill the bottle completely
4. Seal with a bladder
5. Place in the sun or, if necessary, in gentle oven heat until everything has drawn out
6. Transfer the liquid to another bottle, including the expressed residue of the peel
7. Store in corked glass bottles, which preserves it for a long time
