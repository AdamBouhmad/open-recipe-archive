## Ingredients

- brains from 2 veal heads and 2 pork heads
- onions, finely chopped (approximately one-half pound)
- scraped bacon
- 2 pounds pork belly meat
- 6 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- semmel (roll), soaked in milk and squeezed out
- sausage herbs
- milk
- fatty pork intestines
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Clean and soak the brains well, cook them, chop finely, and set aside
2. Chop about one-half pound of onions very finely and soften in scraped bacon
3. Cook 2 pounds of pork belly meat until soft, cut very finely, and place in a grindstone
4. Add the bacon and onions along with 6 eggs and stir together
5. Add some salt, nutmeg, and a milk-soaked and squeezed out semmel
6. Work everything well together
7. Finally stir in the brains
8. Cook the sausage herbs in milk and pour this into the mixture, which should not be too thin
9. When well stirred, fill into fatty pork intestines and tie securely
10. Cook the sausages in bouillon
11. Braise in brushed paper casings on a grill
