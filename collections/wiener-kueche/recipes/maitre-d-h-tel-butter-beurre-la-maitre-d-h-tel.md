## Ingredients

- 15 teaspoons butter
- 1 tablespoon finely cut green parsley
- some chervil optionally
- salt to taste
- white pepper to taste
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Finely pound 15 teaspoons of butter with 1 tablespoon of finely cut green parsley, some chervil (if available), a little salt, white pepper, and the juice of 1 lemon in a mortar and crush it
2. Add to steaks or cutlets
