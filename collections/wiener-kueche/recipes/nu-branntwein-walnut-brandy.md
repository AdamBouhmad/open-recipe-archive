## Ingredients

- 1 liter good brandy
- 9-13 halved green walnuts
- 1 piece clove
- 1 very small piece cinnamon
- 5 pieces juniper berries
- 1/2 liter water
- 16 tea cups sugar
- optional: some vanilla

## Directions

1. Place 1 liter good brandy in a bottle
2. Add 9-13 halved green walnuts (this must always be done before the end of June)
3. Add 1 piece clove, 1 very small piece cinnamon and 5 pieces juniper berries to the bottle
4. Seal well and let stand approximately 3 weeks, shaking the bottle frequently during this time (it can also stand in the sun)
5. After this time, strain the brandy into another bottle and dilute the drained liquid with sugar water if desired
6. To make the sugar water: boil 1/2 liter water with 16 tea cups sugar and optional vanilla for about 1/4 hour, cool and mix with the drained brandy
7. Pour brandy again over the walnuts remaining in the bottle
