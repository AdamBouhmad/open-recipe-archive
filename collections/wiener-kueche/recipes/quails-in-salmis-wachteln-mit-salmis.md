## Ingredients

- Gutted, cleaned quails
- Bacon slices
- String
- Bacon (for lining)
- Root vegetables
- Onion
- Whole spices
- Red wine
- Soup
- Flour (8 teaspoons)
- Butter (8 teaspoons)
- Madeira wine
- Truffles (finely cut, optional)
- Semolina slices for crust

## Directions

1. Wrap the cleaned, seasoned, and dressed quails with bacon and bind with fine string
2. Line a casserole with bacon slices, root vegetables, onion, and whole spices
3. Place the quails inside and let roast covered briefly
4. Pour red wine and soup over them
5. Let simmer gently covered until tender
6. Remove the quails and set aside
7. Roast flour in butter, pour the strained cooking liquid over it
8. Mix well, add a few spoons of Madeira wine and finely cut truffles if desired
9. Bring to boil again
10. Remove bacon from quails and arrange them on a plate
11. Pour the salmis sauce over them
12. Line the edge of the plate with toasted semolina slices (croutons)
