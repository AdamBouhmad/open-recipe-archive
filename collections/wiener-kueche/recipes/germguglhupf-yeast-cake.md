## Ingredients

- 10 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- a pinch of salt
- 35 dkg warmed flour
- yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast
- some spoonfuls lukewarm milk
- 5 dkg currants

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until very fluffy
2. Mix in 7 dkg sugar, 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg thoroughly
3. Add a pinch of salt, 35 dkg warmed flour, yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast, and some spoonfuls lukewarm milk
4. Mix the whole, beat the dough well and fold in 5 dkg currants
5. Fill the prepared mold
6. Let it rise uncovered near the oven until the mold is full
7. Bake the cake in moderate heat until light brown
