## Ingredients

- 4-6 fresh eggs
- 3 anchovies
- 1½ rolls
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 4 dkg capers
- 6 dkg butter
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs for 12 minutes, cool completely, and peel
2. Cut in half lengthwise or widthwise and remove yolks
3. Pass the yolks through a sieve with the cleaned anchovies
4. Press through the deringed milk-soaked, well-pressed rolls
5. Stir into 6 dkg butter creamed
6. Add salt, pepper, cream, and mustard
7. Fill the halved eggs with this mixture
8. Garnish with parsley, capers, or caviar
9. Alternatively, fill using a pastry bag, piping the remainder into the center of a glass dish and arrange the eggs around it
