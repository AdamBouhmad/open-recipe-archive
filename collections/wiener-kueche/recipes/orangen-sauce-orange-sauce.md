## Ingredients

- butter sauce
- approximately 6 tablespoons sugar
- some finely cut orange zest
- juice of 1 lemon
- flesh of 1 orange
- peeled diced orange

## Directions

1. To a cooked, strained butter sauce add approximately 6 tablespoons sugar and some finely cut orange zest with the juice of 1 lemon
2. Remove the flesh of 1 orange, cut the peeled orange into dice, add it to the above
3. Let everything boil well
