## Ingredients

- 2 pieces cooked turnips
- 3 pieces onion
- 2 carrots
- 1 head of celery
- 32 deca butter
- 1/2 liter dried peas
- some sorrel
- chervil
- parsley
- 3 liters cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the turnips, onions, carrots, and celery very finely and place in a casserole with 32 deca butter
2. Roast until golden yellow in color
3. Add 1/2 liter dried peas along with some sorrel, chervil, and parsley
4. Pour in 3 liters cold water and let simmer slowly for approximately 2 hours
5. Let it rest covered for about 1/2 hour but do not cook further
6. Strain through a sieve into an earthenware pot and set aside until needed
