## Ingredients

- 4 ounces butter
- 12 ounces lemon sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons cold butter or custard
- snow from 3 egg whites
- 16 ounces almonds
- butter for greasing the molds
- flour for dusting the molds

## Directions

1. Beat 4 ounces butter until fluffy
2. Mix in 12 ounces lemon sugar
3. Stir in 6 egg yolks gradually
4. Add 3 tablespoons cold butter or custard
5. Gently fold in the snow from 3 egg whites together with 16 ounces almonds
6. Butter the molds, dust with flour, and fill half-full
7. Cook covered in steam for 15-20 minutes, or bake in the oven
8. Turn the cakes out and serve elevated on a platter with chocolate sauce of choice
