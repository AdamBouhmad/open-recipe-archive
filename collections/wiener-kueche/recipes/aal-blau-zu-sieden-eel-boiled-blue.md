## Ingredients

- Eel
- wine vinegar
- butter
- bay leaves
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Strike an eel on the head with a piece of wood until it is dead
2. Open it, wash it out, and spray wine vinegar on it
3. Set wine vinegar with a small piece of butter, several bay leaves, whole cloves, salt, and pepper on the fire
4. When it boils, add the eel with its skin, cover it well, let it cook through completely
5. Serve it with a little fresh wine vinegar and decorated with green parsley
