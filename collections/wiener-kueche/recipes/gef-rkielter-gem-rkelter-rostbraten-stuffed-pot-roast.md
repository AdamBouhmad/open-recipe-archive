## Ingredients

- 5 Rostbraten (pot roasts)
- 16 Deca Speck, fein geschnitten (16 deca bacon, finely cut)
- Zwiebel und Petersilie, fein geschnitten (onion and parsley, finely cut)
- 6 Deca rohes Reichfleisch (6 deca raw meat)
- Salz (salt)
- Optional: Pfeffer (pepper)
- Fett (fat)
- Fein geschnittenes Wurzelwerk (finely cut root vegetables)
- Zwiebel (onion)
- Mehl (flour)
- 3/4 Liter Suppe (3/4 liter broth)
- Optional: braune Suppe (brown broth)
- Optional: Rahm (cream) und Kapern (capers)
- Optional: Erdäpfel (potatoes), Sardellen (anchovies)
- Optional: sauren Rahm (sour cream)

## Directions

1. Take 5 pot roasts and pound out 4 of them flat.
2. Finely mince the 5th piece with 16 deca bacon, some onion and parsley using a chef's knife.
3. Mix this filling with 6 deca raw meat, some salt, and pepper if desired.
4. Divide the filling into 4 portions and spread knife-back thickness on each pounded pot roast.
5. Roll each piece up and tie lightly with twine.
6. In a casserole add some fat, finely cut root vegetables and some onion.
7. Place the breaded, rolled pot roasts on top, let them braise briefly, then pour 3/4 liter broth over them.
8. Cover with a lid and braise gently, which takes 1 to 1.5 hours.
9. Place on a platter, remove the twine and slice neatly.
10. Add some brown broth to the sauce, let it come to a boil briefly and pour over the pot roasts.
11. Alternatively: degrease the sauce, add some cream and capers, let it boil and pour over.
12. Or: add finely diced raw potatoes and 6 pieces of cleaned and cut anchovies to the filling instead of meat. Before the rolled pot roasts are fully braised, degrease the sauce, add sour cream and finish braising.
13. Or: finely chop a small head of cabbage, braise it in butter, and mix with finely cut pot roast, some finely chopped ham, some caraway, and pepper if desired; proceed as above.
14. Or: pound together 8 deca bacon, 1 softened and well-pressed roll, some onion, 9 raw parsley sprigs, anchovies, capers, some lemon peel, the 5th piece of pot roast and 2 whole eggs; season with salt and spices, sieve and spread on the pot roasts, then proceed as above.
15. For any of these preparations you can substitute 4 tablespoons of finely cut cooked ham for the anchovies.
16. Or: mix the 5th piece of finely cut pot roast with the filling of 16 deca bratwurst, which is also highly recommended for cabbage-filled pot roasts.
