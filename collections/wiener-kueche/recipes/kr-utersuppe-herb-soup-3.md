## Ingredients

- leek
- chervil
- parsley
- spinach
- sorrel
- young onions
- garden cress
- flour
- butter or good beef fat
- good beef broth
- egg yolks
- cold water
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Wash leek, chervil, parsley, spinach, sorrel, young onions, and garden cress clean and chop everything finely together with a chopping knife.
2. Let some flour cook with butter or good beef fat in a pot until lightly browned, then braise the chopped herbs in it for a while.
3. Fill with good beef broth and let it come to a boil, but not for too long, otherwise the herbs will lose their color.
4. Before serving, for about 3 measures of soup, beat 6 egg yolks in a pot with some spoons of cold water.
5. Pour this under constant stirring into the soup, stirring until the soup begins to thicken.
6. Serve over toasted bread.
