## Ingredients

- half pound marzipan dough
- 4 to 5 egg whites
- quarter pound farina sugar
- half pound sweet almonds, cut into fine long pieces
- crushed cinnamon

## Directions

1. Dissolve half pound marzipan dough in 4 to 5 egg whites
2. Add quarter pound farina sugar, half pound sweet almonds which have been cut into fine long pieces, and some crushed cinnamon
3. Stir everything well together
4. Arrange the mass in small heaps on paper
5. Bake in moderate oven heat
