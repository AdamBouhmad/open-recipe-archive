## Ingredients

- 28 deca flour
- 22 deca firm butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 whole egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 6 deca finely pounded sugar
- 1 teaspoon finely cut lemon peel
- approximately 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Put 28 deca flour on a board
2. Cut 22 deca firm butter into small pieces and work it fine with the flour using both hands
3. Make a depression in the middle, add 1 egg yolk, 1 whole egg, 1 teaspoon salt, 6 deca finely pounded sugar, 1 teaspoon finely cut lemon peel, and approximately 1/4 liter cream
4. Mix everything well with a knife and work it as quickly as possible into a smooth dough until no cracks are visible
5. Put the dough on a board dusted with flour and let it rest 1/2 hour in a cold place
6. After this time, roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board, fold it together, and let it rest another 1/2 hour
7. Then roll out the dough thick as a small finger and use as desired
