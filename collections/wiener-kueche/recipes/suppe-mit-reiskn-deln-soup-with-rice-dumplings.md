## Ingredients

- 1 pound washed rice
- meat broth
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- 4 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Stir 1 pound washed rice cooked in meat broth, but not too soft, together well with breadcrumbs fried brown in butter and 4 whole eggs
2. Cut out dumplings and cook them in the soup
