## Ingredients

- 4 beautiful tomatoes
- 3 sprigs parsley
- 1/2 liter cold water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter wine
- Stock
- Sugar
- Salt
- Or 3/4 liter preserves instead of fresh tomatoes
- Vinegar (alternative to wine)

## Directions

1. 4 beautiful tomatoes are washed, broken apart from each other, placed with 3 sprigs parsley in a cassette dish
2. Poured with 1/2 liter cold water and braised covered with 2 tablespoons butter
3. Meanwhile, make a butter sauce from 8 tablespoons butter and 8 tablespoons flour
4. Pour it with the braised tomatoes, 1/4 liter wine, and as much stock as needed
5. Add the necessary sugar and some salt, let boil, and strain
6. If using about 3/4 liter preserves instead of fresh tomatoes, place it in a butter sauce of 8 tablespoons butter and the same amount of flour
7. Add 1/4 liter white wine and the necessary stock, sugar, and some salt, stir, and boil well
8. Instead of wine, one can also give the necessary acidity with vinegar, or it can also remain natural
