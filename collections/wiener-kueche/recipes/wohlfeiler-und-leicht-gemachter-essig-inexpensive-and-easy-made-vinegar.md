## Ingredients

- 1 measure of wine
- approximately the same amount of water
- 6 loth of freshly baked rye bread

## Directions

1. Mix 1 measure of wine and approximately the same amount of water
2. Throw 6 loth of freshly baked rye bread into the mixture
3. In summer, place the vinegar in the sun; it will be ready for use in 3 days
4. In winter, place it near oven heat; it will be ready for use in 8 days
