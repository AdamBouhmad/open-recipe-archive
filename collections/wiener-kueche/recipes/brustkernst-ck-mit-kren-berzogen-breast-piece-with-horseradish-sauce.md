## Ingredients

- a beautiful piece of breast meat, six to seven pounds, boiled soft
- horseradish sauce, slightly thicker than usual for beef
- four egg yolks
- melted butter
- fine breadcrumbs
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful piece of breast meat of six to seven pounds, boiled soft in broth, remove it and strain it.
2. Make a horseradish sauce, only slightly thicker than customary for beef.
3. Stir four egg yolks into the horseradish sauce.
4. Coat the breast piece one finger thick with this mixture.
5. Pour melted butter over it and sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs.
6. Before serving, place it in the oven and let it become a beautiful breadcrumb color.
7. Garnish neatly with fresh parsley and serve.
