## Ingredients

- turkey (Indian)
- salt
- bacon for larding
- semmel (bread roll) soaked in milk
- butter for basting

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and cut it open
2. Remove the breastbone, wash it thoroughly several times, and salt it well
3. Lard the breast neatly with bacon
4. Place a milk-soaked semmel in the head
5. Roast it over steady heat
6. If the turkey is large, it will take nearly two hours to roast; during roasting baste it with butter
7. Serve with the drippings from roasting
