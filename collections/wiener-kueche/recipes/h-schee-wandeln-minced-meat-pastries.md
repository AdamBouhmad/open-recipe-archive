## Ingredients

- roasted, finely chopped veal
- butter
- pepper
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- butter pastry dough
- egg white

## Directions

1. In a pan or casserole, put roasted, finely chopped veal with a piece of butter, some pepper and lemon peel, let it stew down
2. Add lemon juice and 2 egg yolks, stir well together, and let it cool on a plate
3. Make a butter pastry dough, roll it out, cut out small pieces with a small pastry cutter
4. Put one piece on a board, fill a teaspoon of minced meat into it, place another piece on top, press them together slightly, and continue until all are done
5. Brush with egg white, place in a pie pan, and bake until cool
