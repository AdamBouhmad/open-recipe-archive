## Ingredients

- Green beans
- small young yellow carrots
- small onions
- cauliflower florets
- shallots
- rocamboles
- artichoke bottoms
- small cucumber slices
- mushrooms
- asparagus
- vinegar
- water
- clove
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare the vinegar one or two days ahead: boil 2 parts wine vinegar with 1 part water, 1 clove, and some nutmeg for about half an hour, removing foam that forms, then cool in an earthenware, glass, or porcelain vessel.
2. Vegetables to be pickled are: green beans, young small yellow carrots, small onions, cauliflower florets, shallots, rocamboles, artichoke bottoms, small cucumber slices, mushrooms, and asparagus.
3. All vegetables must be very young.
4. Cook each type separately in salted water until half-tender, then place in cold water and drain on a sieve or cloth.
