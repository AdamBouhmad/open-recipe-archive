## Ingredients

- One fat goose
- One pound chestnuts
- Goose liver
- Some veal
- Parsley
- Onions
- Butter
- Clear sauce or sauce pikante

## Directions

1. Clean and wash a fat goose well, dry it thoroughly inside and outside, and salt it
2. Roast a pound of chestnuts and cut them into pieces
3. Prepare a well-seasoned forcemeat with the finely chopped goose liver, some veal, parsley, onions, and the chestnuts
4. Let this forcemeat half cook in butter over gentle charcoal fire, stirring frequently, and set aside to cool
5. Fill the goose with it, dress it well so nothing falls out, roast it nicely on the spit or in the oven
6. When the fat is separated, serve it with clear sauce or sauce pikante
