## Ingredients

- plucked suckling pig
- crushed juniper berries
- vinegar
- red wine
- stock
- Bearnaise sauce or lemon sauce or vinegar and oil

## Directions

1. Let a plucked suckling pig rest for about 2 hours
2. Rub it with crushed juniper berries and burn off the hair with a glowing iron (this should be done by a blacksmith)
3. Cut open the belly and remove the entrails
4. Wash the suckling pig thoroughly, let it rest in water for a time, then remove it and dry it with a cloth
5. Cut it into desired pieces and cook it like venison with root vegetables in a vinegar and red wine mixture
6. Once the meat is sufficiently soft, remove it with a slotted spoon to a platter, pour some stock over it, and serve with Bearnaise sauce, lemon sauce, or vinegar and oil
7. Take great care so the meat does not become overcooked
