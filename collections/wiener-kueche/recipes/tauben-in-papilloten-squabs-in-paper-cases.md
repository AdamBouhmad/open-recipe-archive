## Ingredients

- Four squabs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine ground herbs
- Butter
- Scraped bacon
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Shallots
- Seasonings
- White thick paper
- Oil

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the four squabs, remove the claws, tuck in the feet, and cut through the back
2. Flatten them with a knife and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and finely ground herbs
3. Pass a piece of butter and scraped bacon with the squabs and fine herbs over charcoal fire until it has drawn together, then remove to a plate
4. Throw finely chopped mushrooms, parsley, shallots, and some seasonings into the same butter, let it sweat for a while, and pour everything over the squabs
5. Set the prepared squabs aside to cool
6. Make capsules from white thick paper, each holding half a squab, grease the capsules inside with oil, and place the squab inside
7. At the moment of serving, place them on a grate, grill them very slowly, and serve clear sauce underneath
