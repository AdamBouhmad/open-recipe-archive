## Ingredients

- 2.5 pounds strawberries
- 5 loth red currants (ribes)
- 1 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Pass 2.5 pounds strawberries and 5 loth red currants through a fine sieve
2. Clarify 1 pound sugar
3. After cooling, stir it in until it is time to pour everything into the freezing container
