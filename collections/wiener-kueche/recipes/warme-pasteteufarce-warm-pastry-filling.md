## Ingredients

- 4 pounds veal from the leg
- 1 pound bacon
- 1 pound ham
- 1 pound kidney fat
- 4 large onions
- mushrooms or truffles
- dried herbs
- 1 glass good wine
- white bread soaked in milk or water, rubbed and squeezed out
- 6 whole eggs
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Remove all sinews from 4 pounds veal from the leg
2. Cut the veal into slices, then into cubes, along with 1 pound bacon, 1 pound ham, and 1 pound kidney fat
3. Sweat all of this with 4 large onions, mushrooms or truffles, and some dried herbs over gentle charcoal fire for half an hour
4. Pour in 1 glass of good wine
5. Finely chop the meat and bacon together with the above mixture and white bread that has been soaked in milk or water, rubbed, and squeezed out
6. Let the sauce that results from this reduce briefly
7. Rub everything together finely in a mortar with 6 whole eggs
8. Season with salt, pepper, and spices as needed
9. This filling can be used for all warm pastries
