## Ingredients

- 20 deca fine sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter good, unboiled cream
- several spoonfuls Maraschino

## Directions

1. Add to a saucepan 20 deca fine sugar, 8 egg yolks and 1 liter good, unboiled cream
2. Beat over heat until it begins to thicken (without boiling), remove from heat and beat until cool
3. Pass this mixture through a hair sieve into a bowl, stir in several spoonfuls Maraschino and proceed further as with Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720)
