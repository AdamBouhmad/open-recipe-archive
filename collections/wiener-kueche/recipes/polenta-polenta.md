## Ingredients

- 1 liter salt water
- 1 tablespoon Polenta flour
- 1/2 kilo Polenta grain or flour
- hot butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Place 1 liter of salt water in a rather large casserole
2. Add 1 tablespoon Polenta flour
3. When the water boils, pour in 1/2 kilo Polenta grain or flour
4. Make a cross in the flour with a knife, cut through it, and boil uncovered for 1/4 hour
5. Remove the Polenta with a spoon, place it in a bowl
6. Pour hot butter over it and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, which is served separately
