## Ingredients

- dried peas
- salt water
- onions
- butter for roasting
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the peas as described in the Böhmische Erbsen recipe until tender and remove the skins
2. Pour into a shallow casserole dish so the peas are about 2 fingers deep
3. Spread 1 head of onion, cut lengthwise and roasted until golden, over the peas
4. Sprinkle heavily with bread crumbs
5. Bake uncovered in the oven for half an hour until a crust forms
6. Serve in the casserole or arrange on a platter so the crust is not destroyed
7. Can be served with meat, roasted poultry or pigeons as indicated above
