## Ingredients

- Butter, a small piece
- Flour
- Parsley water or weak pea broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Mushrooms (Champignons), finely cut
- Asparagus, optional

## Directions

1. Melt a small piece of butter in a saucepan and roast a little flour in it so it barely takes on a yellow color.
2. Pour parsley water or weak pea broth on it, salt it, season with nutmeg, add finely cut mushrooms, and let it simmer.
3. Follow the procedure for poached eggs, pour the prepared sauce into a dish, place the eggs in it, and let them simmer once more.
