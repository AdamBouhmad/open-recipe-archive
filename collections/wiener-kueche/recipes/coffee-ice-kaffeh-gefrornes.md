## Ingredients

- cream
- egg yolks
- clarified sugar
- 6 loth roasted good coffee
- 3 cups water

## Directions

1. Take the same amount of cream, egg yolk and clarified sugar as for vanilla ice and proceed as above
2. Pour 6 loth roasted good coffee with only 3 cups of water and filter
3. Add the coffee to the above mixture
4. Stir until cooled
5. Pour into the freezing container to process
