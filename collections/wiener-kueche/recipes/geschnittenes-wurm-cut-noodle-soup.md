## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/3 liter flour
- 2 liters beef broth

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from the eggs and flour
2. Work this dough and then cut it very finely with a sharp knife
3. Rub it with both flat hands several times
4. Pass it through a sieve so that it looks like fine barley
5. Continue cutting and sieving the remaining dough until it is all processed
6. In 2 liters of boiling beef broth, add this fine noodle with constant stirring and let it simmer for 8-10 minutes
7. Remove any foam that forms, strain the soup, and serve at table
