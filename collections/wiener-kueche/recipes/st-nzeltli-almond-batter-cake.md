## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 24 ounces powdered sugar
- 24 ounces flour
- beaten eggs for brushing
- coarsely chopped almonds
- sugar mixed with almonds

## Directions

1. Stir 8 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs with 24 ounces powdered sugar for 1 hour
2. Lightly mix in 24 ounces flour
3. Spread this mass finger-thick on a buttered sheet
4. Brush with beaten eggs, sprinkle with sugar mixed with coarsely chopped almonds, and bake in a moderate oven until golden
5. Cut into desired size pieces immediately while warm
