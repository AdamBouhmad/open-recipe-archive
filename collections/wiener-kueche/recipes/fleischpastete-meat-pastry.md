## Ingredients

- leftover roasted veal or other meat scraps
- 4 softened rolls without crusts, wrung out well
- 60 decagrams meat
- 16 decagrams butter
- 6 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- 8 decagrams breadcrumbs
- green peas
- 1/4 liter cream
- sugar sauce (sauce tartare)

## Directions

1. Cut leftover roasted veal or other meat scraps very fine
2. Add 4 softened, wrung-out rolls without crusts and chop everything very fine with a chef's knife
3. With 60 decagrams of meat, beat 16 decagrams of butter until fluffy, add 6 eggs one by one, stir in the meat, salt, pepper, 8 decagrams breadcrumbs and mix very well
4. Brush a mold with butter and dust with flour
5. Place a layer of the beaten mixture in the mold, then a layer of braised green peas, then meat mixture again and so forth
6. Once everything is used up, cook the cake in steam, turn it out of the mold, pour 1/4 liter of cream over it and place it in the oven so the cream thickens, or serve with sugar sauce (sauce tartare) instead
7. One may also use thinly sliced truffles instead of green peas, or thinly sliced eggs with finely cut mushrooms
