## Ingredients

- corn salad, washed
- white boiled cauliflower rose
- hard boiled eggs
- soft boiled beets
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned corn salad on a plate
2. Set a white boiled cauliflower rose in the center
3. Take hard boiled eggs and soft boiled beets
4. Remove the yolks from the eggs and finely cut the whites and yolks of the eggs as well as the beets separately
5. Use these three components to neatly arrange the salad as desired, then it is finished
