## Ingredients

- 30 deca butter
- 30 deca orange sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 30 deca flour
- cherries or morello cherries

## Directions

1. Stir 30 deca butter with 30 deca orange sugar and 10 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 8 egg whites with 30 deca flour
3. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Allow cherries or morello cherries to sink into the dough as desired
5. Bake for approximately 1 hour at low heat in the oven
6. Treat as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
