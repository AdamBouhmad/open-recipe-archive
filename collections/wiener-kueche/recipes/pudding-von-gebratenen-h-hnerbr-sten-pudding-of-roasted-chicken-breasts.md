## Ingredients

- 1 Pfund crawfish butter
- 8 whole eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 Pfund grated bread rolls soaked in milk and squeezed out
- finely chopped breast meat from chickens or capons
- 2 small handfuls flour
- 4 Loth sugar
- zest of one orange
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pound crawfish butter to foam, or stir other butter to foam
2. Beat in 8 whole eggs and 8 egg yolks, one after the other, stirring until foamy
3. Add 2 pounds grated bread rolls that have been soaked in milk and then squeezed out
4. Add finely chopped breast meat from chickens or capons
5. Add 2 small handfuls flour
6. Add 4 Loth sugar over which the zest of one orange has been grated
7. Add salt
8. Handle the mass as usual
9. When serving, add a Dutch sauce with wine
