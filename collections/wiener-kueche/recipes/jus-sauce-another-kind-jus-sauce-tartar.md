## Ingredients

- 8 ounces fat
- some beef and marrow, cut into leaves
- 5 carrots
- 1 small chopped veal bone
- 1/2 liter broth
- 1/3 liter broth
- 1/4 liter wine
- 8 ounces black bread
- 8 ounces butter
- 8 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Put 8 ounces fat, beef and marrow (cut into leaves), and 5 carrots into a saucepan
2. Add a small chopped veal bone and cook it with 1/2 liter broth
3. When the root vegetables are well cooked, add another 1/3 liter broth, 1/4 liter wine, and 8 ounces black bread
4. Let it cook for 1 hour and pass through a sieve
5. In 8 ounces butter, toast the same amount of flour, pour in the strained mixture, and stir until it boils
6. If bread is added, omit the butter and flour
