## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- pepper
- sage or bay leaves
- lemon
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. After preparing the eel as already described, cut it into 3-finger wide pieces; these are skewered crosswise on a small spit; after each piece put either 2 sage or 2 bay leaves. Dust them with salt and pepper and tie them to a large spit, roast the eel pieces quickly (approximately 20 minutes) and serve them immediately with lemon pieces. You can also, when the fish is half roasted, sprinkle it with breadcrumbs and finish roasting it this way. Instead of on the spit, you can roast the eel in the same way in a flat pan in the oven.
