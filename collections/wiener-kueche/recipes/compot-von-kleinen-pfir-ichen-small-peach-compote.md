## Ingredients

- Small peaches
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take these as desired, throw them in boiling water, remove from fire and leave them in until the skin can be pulled off.
2. Then cook them with sugar and some cinnamon, the sugar must always be clarified, and arrange them.
