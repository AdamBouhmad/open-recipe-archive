## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads, cleaned and cut into large pieces
- Water
- Onions
- Green parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned and cut veal sweetbreads in the necessary water until completely tender.
2. Remove them and place them in an earthenware casserole in which onions and green parsley with some pepper and salt have been first roasted in butter.
3. Cover them and braise with the same water in which they were boiled, basting spoonfully until the pieces become somewhat brown and the sauce becomes somewhat thick.
