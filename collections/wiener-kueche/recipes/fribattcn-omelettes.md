## Ingredients

- dough made from 1.5 liters flour, 1.5 liters milk, and 3 whole eggs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Heat some lard in a casserole until very hot.
2. Add a small amount of lard to a flat pan with a handle and let the pan become very hot.
3. Pour the fat back into the casserole and add approximately 2 tablespoons of dough to the pan, spread about paper-thin.
4. Place the pan back on the fire and add some fat from the side.
5. Flip the omelette with a pastry spoon and add a little more fat.
6. Place the browned omelettes on blotting paper and continue until the dough is finished.
7. The omelettes can be cut into strips or decorative shapes as desired.
8. Note: It is especially important that the lard for frying is very hot, otherwise the mushrooms and omelettes will fall apart.
