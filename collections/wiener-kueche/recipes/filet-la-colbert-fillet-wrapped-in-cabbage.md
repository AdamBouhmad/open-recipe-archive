## Ingredients

- 1 to 1.20 kg tenderloin
- 10 dkg bacon
- 1 large or 2 small heads of cabbage
- 3 mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- fines herbes
- breadcrumbs
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the cabbage.
2. Wash the good leaves and place in plenty of boiling salt water.
3. Cook for 5-8 minutes, then drain and lay flat on a board.
4. Prepare and roast the English Fillet as directed.
5. Brush the cooked fillet with fines herbes.
6. Place it skin side down on the cabbage leaves.
7. Fold the leaves over the meat on both sides until completely wrapped.
8. Place in a roasting pan, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, add a little broth or water nearby, and braise at high heat for 20-25 minutes more.
9. Slice thinly and serve on a long platter with the pan sauce.
