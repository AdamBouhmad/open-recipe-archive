## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh butter
- 12 egg yolks
- zest of one lemon
- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 12 egg whites
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Melt 1 pound fresh butter
2. Gradually stir in 12 egg yolks, lemon zest, and 1 pound finely sifted sugar
3. Whip the whites of the 12 eggs in a shallow bowl to stiff peaks
4. Mix the egg white into the mass
5. Finally stir in 1 pound flour by the spoonful
6. Brush small tin forms with melted butter
7. Fill them half-full with the mass
8. Place on a baking sheet
9. Bake for half an hour
10. Each batch yields 60 to 70 tarts of ordinary size
