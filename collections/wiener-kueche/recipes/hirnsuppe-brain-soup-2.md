## Ingredients

- 1 calf's brain
- beef broth
- 15 to 20 mushrooms of medium size
- parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- 1/2 cooking spoon of flour
- pepper, ground
- nutmeg
- bread rolls, baked

## Directions

1. Cook a calf's brain in beef broth
2. Press the cooked brain through a sieve
3. Coarsely chop 15 to 20 mushrooms of medium size
4. Put finely chopped parsley in a casserole with a piece of warmed butter
5. Let the mushrooms simmer in the butter for a while
6. Dust with half a cooking spoon of flour
7. Let it simmer a bit longer
8. Add the brain
9. Fill with good beef broth
10. Season the whole with ground pepper and nutmeg
11. Let it simmer well together
12. Serve over baked bread rolls
