## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams raw beef fat
- 8 decagrams marrow fat
- 1 head of onion cut in slices
- 2 pieces each of carrots, parsley, and celery
- 1 kilo cooked veal bones
- 1 kilo beef bones
- 1/2 kilo shin bones
- 4 liters broth or water
- 5 peppercorns
- 1 spice clove
- 1 small piece nutmeg
- 10 decagrams potato starch
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 1/8 liter Madeira or white wine
- paradise apple puree or whole paradise apples
- 3 truffles braised in wine
- 3 braised champignons
- 1 piece soft-cooked calf's head
- calf's brain, diced

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a casserole with 8 decagrams raw beef fat and 8 decagrams marrow fat
2. Add sliced onion head, carrot, parsley, and celery pieces
3. Place on these 1 kilo cooked veal bones, 1 kilo beef bones, and 1/2 kilo shin bones
4. Seal the casserole well and braise the contents until the fat becomes clear
5. Pour off the fat and add 4 liters of broth or water
6. Add 5 peppercorns, 1 spice clove, and 1 small piece of nutmeg
7. Cook slowly covered for 3 hours
8. Remove all fat, strain through cloth, and bring to a boil again
9. Mix 10 decagrams of potato starch with 1 pinch of white pepper and thin with 1/8 liter of Madeira or white wine until flowing
10. Stir this into the boiling soup using a whisk, stirring constantly
11. Add paradise apple puree or whole paradise apples
12. Cook slowly for 1 hour with frequent skimming
13. Before serving, add 3 truffles braised in wine, 3 braised champignons, 1 piece of soft-cooked calf's head, and diced calf's brain
