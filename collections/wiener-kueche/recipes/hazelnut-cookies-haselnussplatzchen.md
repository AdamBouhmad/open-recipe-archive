## Ingredients

- 15 deca beaten butter
- 8 deca sugar
- a pinch of finely cut lemon zest
- 6 hard-boiled, passed egg yolks
- 24 deca flour

## Directions

1. Mix the beaten butter with sugar, lemon zest, and gradually add the hard-boiled, passed egg yolks
2. Add flour and mix well
3. Work the dough on a flour-dusted board and let it rest covered in a cool place
4. After approximately half an hour, roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a knife back
5. Cut out rounds with a cookie cutter, and make a small hole in the center with a small cutter to create thumb-width rings
6. Work the resulting scraps back into a dough, let rest covered in a cool place for half an hour, roll out to the thickness of a knife back, and treat as described above
7. Place the cut rings on a butter-greased baking sheet using a knife, brush with beaten eggs, sprinkle with hail sugar mixed with coarsely cut almonds, and bake pale yellow at low heat
