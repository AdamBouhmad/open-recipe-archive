## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams butter or lard
- 1 piece onion, finely sliced
- some root vegetables, finely sliced
- the chopped carp head
- 10 pieces blanched raisins
- 10 pieces raw dried prunes, pitted
- 16 decagrams finely sliced black bread
- 8 decagrams dark and 8 decagrams light raisins
- 5 sprigs thyme
- 5 sprigs fresh parsley
- 2 bay leaves
- 5 grains each of spice
- 1 spice nail
- 1 very small piece of cinnamon
- 4 decagrams sugar
- 1.5 liters beer
- 0.25 liter red wine
- 0.5 liter vinegar
- carp blood (if available)
- blanched almonds
- fish broth if needed
- one carp of approximately 2 kilos

## Directions

1. In a pot place 12 decagrams butter or lard, 1 piece onion, some root vegetables, all finely sliced, the chopped carp head, 10 pieces blanched raisins, 10 pieces raw dried pitted prunes, 16 decagrams finely sliced black bread, 8 decagrams dark and 8 decagrams light raisins, 5 sprigs thyme, 5 sprigs fresh parsley, 2 bay leaves, 5 grains each of spice, 1 spice nail, 1 very small piece of cinnamon and 4 decagrams sugar
2. Let this braise covered for 15 minutes, then pour it with 1.5 liters beer, 0.25 liter red wine and 0.5 liter vinegar and let it braise covered
3. If the carp blood is available, stir it with vinegar and add it to the sauce and stir
4. If the sauce is too thick, add some fish broth and pass it
5. As soon as this is done, add blanched raisins, currants, some raisins and peeled finely cut almonds and let it come to a boil once more
6. This sauce must be sweet-sour and bitter and have a pleasant spicy taste
7. If it is too thin, add some butter sauce; if it is not dark enough, stir in a few spoonfuls of caramel sugar
8. The fish of approximately 2 kilos is meanwhile cut and prepared in the same way as indicated for the 'Sour Boiled Carp with Root Vegetables'
9. Arrange it with some sauce on the dish and serve desired dumplings or semolina strudel with it
10. The remaining sauce is served in a sauce boat
11. Any kind of fish can be prepared in this way
