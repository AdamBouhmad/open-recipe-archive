## Ingredients

- some onion
- 8 tablespoons butter or lard
- 2 large boiled, passed potatoes
- 6 pieces finely cut anchovies
- 1/2 liter broth
- several spoonfuls sour cream

## Directions

1. Let some onion roast in 8 tablespoons butter or lard
2. Add 2 large boiled, passed potatoes and 6 pieces finely cut anchovies
3. Pour in with 1/2 liter broth and several spoonfuls sour cream
4. Let it boil thoroughly
5. Pass it through a sieve or serve unpasseed as desired
