## Ingredients

- 12 quails
- 1/2 veal forcemeat
- hare forcemeat, equal amount (see Cold Pâté Forcemeat No. 1035)
- butter dough

## Directions

1. The preparation and execution of this pâté is the same as specified for the 'Pâté of Poulardy,' with the difference that instead of 1/2 veal forcemeat, equal amount of hare forcemeat is used, and instead of the poulardy, 12 quails are used.
2. The quails, after the back has been cut open and the bones carefully loosened, must be filled with a small, cleaned truffle and the required hare forcemeat.
3. If desired, instead of one large pâté, 12 small ones can be made from the butter dough, which must always be shaped to correspond to the size of each individual quail.
