## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound ground sugar
- 15 eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Discard half of the egg white
2. Whip the eggs in a clean pot for half an hour until foaming
3. Add the sugar
4. Add the flour and finely sifted vanilla
5. Beat the dough until it begins to thicken
6. Brush some forms with melted butter
7. Fill them only half full
8. Bake
