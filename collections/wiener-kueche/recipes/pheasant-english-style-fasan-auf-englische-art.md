## Ingredients

- one beautiful pheasant
- forcemeat/farce
- bacon
- ham
- lemon peel
- butter
- ham
- cloves
- bay leaves
- mushrooms
- flour
- brown broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from a beautiful pheasant
2. Fill it with forcemeat and reshape it
3. Bind it with bacon, ham, and lemon peel in paper
4. Place it on a casserole lid brushed with butter and put it in the oven
5. For the sauce: let some butter become warm in a pan, add ham, cloves, bay leaves, and finely cut mushrooms
6. Dust some flour over it, pour some brown broth on top, and let it simmer
7. Finally add some lemon juice, let it boil, skim off the fat, and pour this short sauce over the pheasant
