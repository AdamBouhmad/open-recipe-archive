## Ingredients

- Small pieces of pineapple, citron, nuts, cherries, pignoli, almonds or similar fruits
- 24 deca chocolate
- 24 deca fine sifted sugar
- 1 egg white
- Colored pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Have ready small pieces of pineapple, citron, nuts, pitted cherries, pignoli, almonds or similar fruits
2. In a double boiler, melt 24 deca chocolate
3. Quickly stir in 24 deca fine sifted sugar to form a dough
4. While the dough is still warm, form hazelnut-sized balls, placing a piece of the prepared fruit in the center of each
5. Place on a sugar-sprinkled paper
6. Dip the balls in stiffly beaten egg white
7. Roll in colored pearl sugar
8. Dry in air and store
9. If the chocolate mass becomes too stiff, add a few drops of warm water
