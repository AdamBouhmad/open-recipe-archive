## Ingredients

- 6 pounds pears (such as Isenbart or Bergamotten variety)
- 4 pounds sugar
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, cut them into four pieces, remove the cores, and place in fresh water.
2. Cook the pears in the water for some time until they become soft.
3. Pour off the water and press the pear pulp through a sieve into a soup pot.
4. Clarify the 4 pounds of sugar, cook it, and skim off the foam.
5. Stir the pear pulp into the sugar.
6. Place on charcoal fire and stir constantly until it reaches the proper thickness.
7. Once cooled, fill into appropriate containers or jars.
