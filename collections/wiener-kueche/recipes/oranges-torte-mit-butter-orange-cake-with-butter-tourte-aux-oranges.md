## Ingredients

- 14 decagrams butter, beaten
- 24 decagrams orange sugar
- juice of one orange
- 8 raw egg yolks
- 8 hard-cooked egg yolks, sifted
- 4 egg whites
- 8 decagrams flour
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 decagrams butter with 24 decagrams orange sugar and the juice of one orange until well combined, then stir for 1/2 hour.
2. Add 8 raw and 8 hard-cooked, sifted egg yolks, stir for another 1/2 hour, then lightly fold in the beaten snow of 4 egg whites mixed with 8 decagrams flour.
3. Fill a buttered cake form dusted with flour with this mixture and bake for 1 hour in a well-heated oven.
4. Once baked, place the cake on a sieve to cool completely, then cover with a glaze of choice.
