## Ingredients

- 1 pound of cacao beans
- 1 loth of cinnamon
- 1 quenchen of vanilla (optional)
- 2 measures of strong brandy
- 1 half pound of roasted cacao beans
- 1 half loth of cinnamon
- 1 half quenchen of vanilla
- 1 half quenchen of cloves
- 2 pounds of sugar
- 2 pounds of water

## Directions

1. Pound 1 pound of cacao beans and 1 loth of cinnamon (and 1 quenchen of vanilla if desired)
2. Pour over with 2 measures of strong brandy and let stand for 4 days, stirring meanwhile
3. Distill 1 measure from it
4. Take 1 half pound of roasted cacao beans and the same amount of cinnamon and vanilla as before, plus 1 half quenchen of cloves
5. Bring it with 2 measures to the remaining liquid
6. Let stand for 8 to 10 days in gentle heat
7. Filter it and mix with the distilled spirits
8. Dissolve 2 pounds of sugar in 2 pounds of water and add to the mixture
