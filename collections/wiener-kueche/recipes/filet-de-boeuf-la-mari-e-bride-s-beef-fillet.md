## Ingredients

- one whole fillet
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- coarse bacon
- fine breadcrumbs
- mushrooms
- parsley
- onions
- veal sweetbread
- basil
- three egg yolks
- eggs
- strong braise

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and fat from a whole fillet
2. Split it lengthwise and flatten it well
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and spices
4. Lard it with coarse bacon
5. Make a fine farce from finely grated bread, crushed mushrooms, parsley, onions, bacon or veal sweetbread, salt, pepper, basil and three egg yolks, well combined
6. Spread this farce lengthwise on both pieces of the fillet
7. Cover them with beaten eggs
8. Roll them up
9. Bind them in a clean cloth
10. Cook them in a strong braise until done
11. When serving, remove the cloth, untie it
12. Glaze the fillet nicely
13. Serve a truffle sauce underneath
