## Ingredients

- Dough as for cherry cake
- 12 Deka butter
- 12 Deka finely sifted sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 32 Deka pressed curd cheese (Topfen)
- 8 Deka raisins
- 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described for cherry cake
2. Instead of cherries, cover the dough with the following curd filling:
3. Beat 12 Deka butter fluffy
4. Mix in 12 Deka finely sifted sugar
5. Stir in 1 whole egg
6. Mix in 32 Deka pressed curd cheese, 8 Deka raisins and 1/4 liter cream, one after another
7. Bake as for cherry cake
