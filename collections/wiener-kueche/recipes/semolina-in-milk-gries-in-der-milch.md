## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 0.125 liter semolina
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 1 pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Pour 1 liter milk into a saucepan with 8 tablespoons sugar and 1 pinch of salt if desired
2. Stir in 0.125 liter semolina under constant stirring and let it cook thick
3. Arrange in a serving dish and serve dusted with finely ground sugar and optionally with cinnamon
4. Optionally cook with 4 tablespoons butter or before serving, pour with 4 tablespoons very brown butter
