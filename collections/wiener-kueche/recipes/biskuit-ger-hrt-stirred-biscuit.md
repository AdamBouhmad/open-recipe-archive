## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- juice and zest of ½ lemon
- 4 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
- 11 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 5 egg yolks for 30 minutes
2. Add juice and zest of ½ lemon
3. Lightly fold in 11 dkg flour together with 4 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
4. Fill into a buttered, floured biscuit mold
5. Bake
6. The biscuit may be brushed with jam and iced, or baked further
