## Ingredients

- about twenty-five crayfish
- milk-soaked bread rolls
- egg yolks
- butter
- cream
- two to three hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- mace
- fresh marjoram
- cleaned pork intestines

## Directions

1. Break the live crayfish and chop the tails very finely
2. Soak bread rolls in milk and add to the chopped crayfish tails along with egg yolks
3. From the shells, cut off the heads and remove the bitter parts
4. Pound the shells with a good piece of butter in a mortar until fine
5. Sweat the pounded shells covered over coals for a while
6. Add cream proportionally and let it come to a boil several times
7. Strain through a fine cloth into the crayfish mixture
8. Add two to three hard-boiled egg yolks, salt, mace, and a little fresh marjoram
9. Work the entire mass well together until it is neither too thin nor too thick
10. Fill into cleaned pork intestines and cook in milk
