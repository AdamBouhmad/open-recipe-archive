## Ingredients

- Beef loin
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Lemon peel
- Bay leaf
- Onions
- Cloves
- Whole pepper
- Capers
- Lemon
- Anchovies
- Bacon
- Green parsley
- Onion
- Butter
- Milk cream
- Pepper
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and membranes from the beef loin, wash it well, and salt it.
2. Prepare a pickling brine from a little wine, vinegar, water, lemon peel, a bay leaf, and a couple of onions studded with cloves.
3. Boil the brine and pour it hot over the beef loin, add some whole pepper, and let it stand overnight.
4. The next day, place it on the heat and let it stew until tender.
5. Take a handful of capers, place them on a board, and chop them together with half a lemon, a couple of washed anchovies with bones removed, a small piece of bacon, a little green parsley, and a small onion from the above brine.
6. Let this mixture cook in butter, place it with the meat, add half a seidel of milk cream, a little butter and pepper, and let it stew a little.
7. Skim off the fat, arrange the beef loin on the platter, and garnish with some lemon slices.
