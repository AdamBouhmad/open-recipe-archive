## Ingredients

- pork leg from a young pig
- salt
- caraway
- fat
- broth

## Directions

1. Salt the pork leg well with the skin (rind) attached and rub with caraway
2. Place in a pan containing fat, cover the meat with fat and roast in the oven with frequent basting using broth and the accumulated juices
3. When the rind develops a glassy appearance during roasting, cut it in a grid pattern with a sharp knife at thumb-width intervals and continue roasting it brown and crispy by frequently basting with the juices
4. Cut the meat into desired pieces, arrange them on a platter so that the leg resumes its original form, and serve it with braised salad, sauerkraut, pea purée or similar
