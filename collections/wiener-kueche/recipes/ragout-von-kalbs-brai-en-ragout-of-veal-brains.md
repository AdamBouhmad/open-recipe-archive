## Ingredients

- ham
- veal brains
- beef palate, boiled and cleaned
- morels
- mushrooms
- onions, finely chopped
- bay leaves
- salt
- roux

## Directions

1. Cut ham into thin slices as much as needed and cook them properly
2. Wash the veal brains well, let them become firm again in warm water, clean them
3. Place them with the ham slices in a casserole
4. Cut boiled, well-cleaned, and cooled beef palate into small pieces along with morels and mushrooms
5. Put this in the casserole with finely chopped onions, bay leaves and salt
6. Make a somewhat brown roux, pour a little meat broth to it
7. Pour this over the ragout and let it cook together
