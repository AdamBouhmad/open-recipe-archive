## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams powdered sugar
- 16 decagrams peeled almonds
- 1 egg white
- fine sifted sugar
- wafers

## Directions

1. Stir together for 1 hour powdered sugar, peeled almonds pounded very finely with 1 egg white, and 1 egg white
2. Using a coffee spoon or piping bag, place nutmeg-sized heaps on a wafer-covered baking sheet
3. Sprinkle with fine sifted sugar and dry in a full oven
4. Cut down from the wafer
