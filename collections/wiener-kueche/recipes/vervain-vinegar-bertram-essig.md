## Ingredients

- Leaves from 5 bunches of vervain
- 5 liters of good vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the plucked leaves from 5 bunches of vervain into a bottle
2. Pour 5 liters of good vinegar over them, but a space three fingers wide must remain empty in the bottle
3. Cork the bottle and bind it with linen cloth
