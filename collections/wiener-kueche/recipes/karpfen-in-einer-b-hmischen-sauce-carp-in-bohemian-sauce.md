## Ingredients

- Carp
- wine vinegar
- beer
- onions
- lemon peel
- parsley roots
- celery
- butter
- bread crust
- sugar

## Directions

1. Open the carp as large as needed, remove the insides, carefully remove the gallbladder, take out the blood and add a couple drops of wine vinegar to it
2. Cut the carp into pieces as desired
3. Place half vinegar and half beer on the fire in a pan or casserole, add some cut onions, lemon peel, some pieces of parsley roots, and finely cut celery
4. Boil this together well, then add the carp pieces with the bundle and a piece of butter, and let everything cook well and slowly
5. You can also let some bread crust cook with it if desired
6. Before serving, add the blood and a piece of sugar, let it boil a little more with these additions, and arrange the carp with the vegetables on the table
