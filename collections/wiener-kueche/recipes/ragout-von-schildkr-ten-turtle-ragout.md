## Ingredients

- Turtle
- Salt water
- Butter
- Pea water
- Green parsley
- Half a Seidel of young green peas
- Twelve finely chopped mushrooms
- Fifteen to twenty crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Boil the turtles in salt water until soft, break the shells in two parts, remove the cooked turtles, peel off the black skin from the feet and other parts, and carefully remove the entrails and liver so the gall does not burst.
2. Cut the turtles into four parts, make a small butter sauce, and fill it with pea water.
3. Take a piece of butter, some green parsley, half a Seidel of young green peas, twelve finely chopped mushrooms, fifteen to twenty crayfish tails, and let them braise.
4. When everything is well braised, add it to the turtles and let everything cook well together.
5. It should have only a little sauce when it is served at table.
