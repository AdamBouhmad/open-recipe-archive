## Ingredients

- Strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
- peeled pears, cut into thin slices
- raisins
- currants
- finely ground sugar
- cinnamon, as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough and let it rest
2. Stretch the dough until thin
3. Fill instead with pears as indicated for Apple Strudel (No. 1238), cut into thin slices
4. Treat further as for Apple Strudel (No. 1238)
