## Ingredients

- 4 dkg ham, finely chopped
- 4 dkg tongue, finely chopped
- 4 dkg Veronese salami, finely chopped
- 3 dkg grated Emmental cheese
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 anchovy, passed
- 6 dkg butter
- salt
- bread or roll slices
- parsley for decoration
- butter balls

## Directions

1. Chop ham, tongue, and salami very finely
2. Mix with grated Emmental, hard-boiled egg, and passed anchovy
3. Stir into 6 dkg butter and salt
4. Spread on bread or roll slices
5. Arrange slices in a wreath shape on a platter
6. Place butter balls in the center
7. Decorate with parsley
