## Ingredients

- 20 deca rice
- hot water
- 1/4 liter boiling milk
- 8 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Wash 20 deca rice in hot water
2. Place on sieve to drain well
3. Stir into 1/4 liter boiling milk and let cook
4. Stir in 8 deca butter and place rice in a deep bowl
5. When completely cooled, stir in 4 egg yolks, some salt, and nutmeg
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
