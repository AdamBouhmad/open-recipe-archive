## Ingredients

- 16 Deka sugar
- 18 Deka violet flowers with all green parts removed
- 3 tablespoons Maraschino
- some dissolved indigo
- 2 Deka dissolved gelatin (gelatin solution No. 1687)
- 1/2 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. Heat 16 Deka sugar and add to it while still hot 18 Deka violet flowers from which all green parts have been removed, cover the sugar and let it sit for 7 hours over the violet flowers so that it takes on their scent
2. The flowers must be picked before sunrise
3. When the sugar is still lukewarm, stir in 3 tablespoons Maraschino and some dissolved indigo, mix this all into 2 Deka dissolved, still hot gelatin (gelatin solution No. 1687), strain it through cloth, stir the strained mixture while still warm to 1/2 liter whipped cream and proceed further as with Coffee Cream with Gelatin (No. 1690)
