## Ingredients

- 16 ounces flour
- 16 ounces granulated sugar
- 1/3 liter cold milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Put flour and granulated sugar in a casserole, slowly add cold milk while stirring
2. Heat and stir until the mixture is well cooked
3. Pour into a deep dish and cool completely
4. Add egg yolks one by one
5. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites
6. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
7. Bake at low heat for 3/4 hour in the oven or bake in the casserole itself
8. Turn out onto a dish, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
