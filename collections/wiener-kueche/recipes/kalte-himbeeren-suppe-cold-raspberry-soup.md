## Ingredients

- 2 liters raspberries
- 1 kilo sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 liters white wine
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Place 2 liters raspberries in very fresh water, then immediately lay them on a cloth and select with a needle approximately one-third of the most beautiful berries, which are placed in a large bowl, scattered with sugar, and set on ice.
2. Let 1 kilo sugar boil well in 1/2 liter water, pour this hot over the remaining raspberries and let it sit covered for approximately 3 hours.
3. Strain this through a sieve, pour 2 liters white wine and the juice of 1 lemon into it, strain it once more through a sieve into a porcelain bowl.
4. Mix in the whole raspberries and serve the soup at table.
