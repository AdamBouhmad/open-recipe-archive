## Ingredients

- 28 teaspoons butter
- 28 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 pinch finely cut lemon peel
- 8 egg whites
- 28 teaspoons flour, or semolina, potato flour as desired

## Directions

1. Beat 28 teaspoons butter with 28 teaspoons powdered sugar and 8 egg yolks gradually and mix.
2. Stir for 1/2 hour with the juice of 1/2 lemon and 1 pinch finely cut lemon peel.
3. Lightly fold in the stiff snow of 8 egg whites with 28 teaspoons regular flour, or semolina or potato flour as desired.
4. Fill this mass into a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour and bake for 1 hour in a well-heated oven.
5. Once the form's edge has been removed and the cake cooled on a sieve, cover it with egg glaze, sprinkle with peeled finely cut almonds and sugar and serve on a flat plate once dry.
