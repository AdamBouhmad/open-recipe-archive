## Ingredients

- 1 pound kidney fat, cut into slices
- several large onions, cut into slices
- 4 pounds veal meat and bones
- 4 pounds beef
- one old hen
- 3 measures water
- bouillon
- celery
- parsnip
- yellow turnips

## Directions

1. Cut the kidney fat and onions into slices
2. Place 4 pounds veal meat and bones, 4 pounds beef, and one old hen in a casserole
3. Set it over charcoal fire and pour 3 measures water into it
4. Let it draw until it turns yellow
5. Fill it up with bouillon
6. Cut celery, parsnip, and yellow turnips into it
7. Skim it frequently and continue cooking until everything is quite soft
8. Skim off the fat
9. Strain the consommé through a fine napkin to preserve it for further use
