## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram boned hare or venison meat
- 66 decagrams cut bacon
- 5 juniper berries
- 2½ decagrams bacon
- 25 decagrams smoked, formed tongue
- equal amount of truffles
- salt and spices to taste
- butter
- flour
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Pound hare or venison meat finely in mortar with 66 decagrams cut bacon and 5 juniper berries
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Cut the 2½ decagrams bacon, tongue, and truffles into small cubes
4. Mix cubed ingredients with the pâté, salt, and spices
5. Brush a mold with butter, line with flour, and cover with thin bacon slices
6. Press the pâté in and cover with bacon slices that have been cut several times
7. Place the mold in boiling water and cook covered for 2 hours, with ¾ of the mold in water
8. Refill evaporated water as needed
9. After cooking, turn out and let cool covered with the bacon slice
10. Prepare a cleaned mold in ice and pour finger-high aspic into it
11. When set, garnish with cooled tongue, side of a hard-cooked egg, and cut truffles
12. Pour finger-high aspic over these additions
13. When fully cooled, trim the cake in the mold and carefully place in the mold
14. Fill the space between mold and cake with melted aspic
15. After standing in ice for 1 hour, remove the mold, dip in hot water for a moment, dry, turn out onto a dish, carefully remove the mold, and border the dish edge with neatly cut aspic
