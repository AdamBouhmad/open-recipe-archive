## Ingredients

- Baked biscuit, diced (24 drams)
- Cream (1/4 liter)
- Egg yolks (6)
- Vanilla sugar (8 drams)
- Marmalade
- Sugar, finely crushed (20 drams)
- Egg whites (6)
- Chocolate, grated (1 tablet)
- Spinach juice (1/2 teaspoon)
- Almond extract (several drops)
- Orange juice (1/4 teaspoon, mixed with 1 egg yolk)

## Directions

1. Cut baked biscuit into cubes and arrange in a butter-coated dish
2. Whip together cream, egg yolks and vanilla sugar and pour spoonwise over the biscuit
3. Cover with dissolved marmalade
4. Mix finely crushed sugar with the snow of egg whites
5. Divide into 5 parts
6. Color one part with grated chocolate, the second with spinach juice, the third with almond extract, the fourth with orange juice (mixed with egg yolk), leave the fifth white
7. Put each colored portion into a paper cornet and pipe decoratively onto the biscuit
8. Sprinkle with sugar and bake in an open oven for 3/4 hour
