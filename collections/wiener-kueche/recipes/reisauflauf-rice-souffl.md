## Ingredients

- milk
- 17 dkg rice
- vanilla
- 8 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls sugar
- 4 egg whites
- apples or grated chocolate (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook milk with 17 dkg rice and a piece of vanilla in a pot for 30-45 minutes, stirring frequently, until thick, and cool
2. Stir in creamed butter, egg yolks, and sugar
3. Lightly fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Fill half the mixture into a well-buttered baking dish
5. If desired, add a layer of stewed apples or grated chocolate, then add the remaining mixture
6. Bake light brown over moderate heat
7. Cut into portions, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm
