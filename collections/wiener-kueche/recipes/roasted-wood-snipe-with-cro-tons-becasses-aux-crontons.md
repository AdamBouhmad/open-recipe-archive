## Ingredients

- Plucked and flamed woodcocks, not opened
- Lard slices, finely cut
- Strong broth bread slices
- Lard

## Directions

1. Pluck and flame woodcocks without opening them
2. Remove the eyes
3. Dress them by turning the feet inward so the claws clasp the thighs and the knee lies under the breast
4. Bend the long beak either into the thigh at the side or into the breast forward, or turn the head so the back of the head sits firmly on the breast with the beak pointing forward
5. Salt the dressed woodcocks and wrap them with finely cut lard slices and bind them
6. Shortly before serving, place them on a spit and roast for 15-20 minutes
7. During roasting, place strong thick bread slices cut into the pan below which is lined with lard, so that the escaping juice drips onto them
8. After this time, remove the woodcocks from the spit, loosen the lard, divide the woodcocks into 4 parts but reassemble them, arrange on a platter with the lard and place the bread slices at the edge of the platter
