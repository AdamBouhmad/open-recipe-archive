## Ingredients

- 28 Deca butter
- 28 Deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 28 Deca fine flour
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy
2. Mix in the vanilla sugar
3. Stir for 1/4 hour
4. Gradually add the 6 egg yolks and stir for another 1/2 hour
5. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites simultaneously with 28 Deca fine flour
6. Proceed as with the Biscuit-Torte (No. 1498)
