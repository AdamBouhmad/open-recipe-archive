## Ingredients

- Not fully cooked potatoes of arbitrary amount
- Mehl (one-sixteenth the weight of the potatoes)
- Salt
- Spice herbs
- Pepper
- Ground pickled beef or pickling
- Fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Take an arbitrary amount of not fully cooked potatoes and grate them finely.
2. Mix in one-sixteenth of their weight in flour, as well as salt, spice herbs, and a little pepper, also grated pickled beef or pickling, and add some fried bread cubes (which should be grated or crushed finely).
3. Form the dumplings from this mixture.
4. Cook them until they swim on top of the water.
