## Ingredients

- One goose
- Salt
- Saltpeter
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Fatten the goose very well, especially from the time it begins to lay on its keel
2. Provide oats, grain, bran, carrots and parsnips; give it as much movement as possible
3. Feed it clean good feed at night and provide fresh clean water, sometimes also river water
4. Pluck the goose only gently, taking only as many feathers as naturally come away in the hand
5. By Martinmas you will see a very different goose than usual
6. After slaughter and removing the entrails and everything, dissolve the bones
7. Rub the goose inside with salt, saltpeter, cloves and pepper
8. Rub it outside with salt
9. Fold the two halves of the goose with the inside facing each other, tie with twine, and press slightly between two boards placed at an angle in a dish
10. Baste the goose alternately with drippings, today on one side, tomorrow on the other
11. After ten days in the drippings, once it has dried a little, wrap in paper and bring into weak smoke
12. Cut into slices like salmon
