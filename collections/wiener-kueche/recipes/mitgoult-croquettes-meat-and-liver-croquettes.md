## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons of any braised, finely cut meat
- 16 tablespoons finely diced goose liver
- 1 teaspoon of green parsley
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- Salt
- Pepper to taste
- Butter sauce (if needed)
- Sour cream (if needed)
- Flour for dusting
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the meat, goose liver, parsley, egg yolks, whole egg, salt and pepper well together
2. If the mixture is too dry, mix it well with some butter sauce or sour cream
3. Place the mixture on a flour-dusted board, form small sausages 1 finger long and 2 fingers thick
4. Dip them in beaten, whipped eggs, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them quickly until golden in very hot lard
5. Remove with a slotted spoon, arrange them nicely in a bowl and serve as garnish with vegetables and purees or with any desired hot dish
6. They can also be served as a dish by themselves
