## Ingredients

- 28 teaspoons weighed sago
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 teaspoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs
- 5 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan

## Directions

1. Place 28 teaspoons of sago on a sieve, and once the water has drained well, whisk it into 1/2 liter of boiling milk with constant stirring and let it cook briefly
2. Set aside and let cool
3. Meanwhile, beat 6 egg yolks and 12 teaspoons of sugar for 1/2 hour, add the cooled but cooled sago, mix well and lightly fold in the snow from 5 egg whites along with 2 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
4. Fill a butter-greased, flour-dusted form with this mass and bake in the oven for 1 hour at low heat
