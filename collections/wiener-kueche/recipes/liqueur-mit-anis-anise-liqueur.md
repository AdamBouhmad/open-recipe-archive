## Ingredients

- 1 and one-quarter pounds sugar
- 1 glass water
- 3 ounces anise
- half seidel water
- 3 seidels brandy

## Directions

1. Let 1 and one-quarter pounds sugar cook with 1 glass water until the sugar is completely and well dissolved
2. Scald 3 ounces anise with half seidel of boiling hot water
3. Cover and let stand for half an hour
4. Pour under the boiling sugar
5. Remove from fire and pour in 3 seidels brandy
6. Mix well together and pour into a stone jug
7. Set in the sun, or in winter on the stove, and let stand tightly stoppered for three weeks
8. After three weeks, filter and fill into bottles and cork well
