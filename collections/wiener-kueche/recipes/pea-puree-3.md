## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg dried peas
- cold water
- baking soda
- 6 dkg fat
- onion
- salt

## Directions

1. Sort and wash 1/2 kg yellow or green peas
2. Place in a pot with twice as much cold water and a little baking soda
3. Cover and cook for 1½ hours, stirring occasionally
4. Add a light onion roux made with 6 dkg fat
5. Pass through a sieve, dilute with water or broth if necessary, season with salt
6. Cook thoroughly
7. Arrange on a round platter, make steps with a round knife, and pour a browned onion rings in fat into the center so it runs down the steps
