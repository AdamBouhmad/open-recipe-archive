## Ingredients

- One pound of the finest wheat flour
- Three quarters pound good unsalted butter, or butter freed of salt by multiple washings
- Two ripe eggs
- One spoon full of good French brandy
- One tablespoon full of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Lay half of the flour on a smooth, clean table, preferably on a marble plate, make a depression in it
2. Put the eggs, brandy, and cream into the depression
3. Work this all together into a firm dough so that it can be rolled out
4. Take half of the butter, lay it on the rolled out dough, fold the dough over the butter
5. Sprinkle with a portion of the remaining flour, choose a new spot on the table, then turn the dough mass so that the upper side lies on the flour-dusted spot
6. Roll the entire mass out and together several times, then take the other half of the butter and do the same as with the first, until all the flour is used
7. Then roll out the dough fairly thin and cut it according to the form you wish to give the pastry
8. Finally, lay the dough in layers one upon another to the height desired
9. Between each layer brush a little egg yolk, and brush it on the outside as well so that the pastry obtains a beautiful, golden-brown color
