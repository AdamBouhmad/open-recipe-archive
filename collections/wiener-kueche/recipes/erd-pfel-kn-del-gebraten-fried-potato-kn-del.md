## Ingredients

- 8 deca beaten butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- some salt
- a knife point of finely cut green parsley
- medium-sized fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until creamy.
2. Stir in whole eggs, egg yolks, salt, and green parsley.
3. Remove the marrow from medium-sized fried potatoes and push it through a sieve.
4. Add to the beaten butter and mix well together.
5. About 1 hour before mealtime, shape small knödel from the potato mixture and fry them in lard.
6. Place them on blotting paper and add them to the soup.
