## Ingredients

- 2-3 sliced rolls (Schneidssemmeln)
- 0.5 liter milk
- 25-30 decagrams flour
- 1 egg
- Salt
- Fat for roasting
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. In a basin, pour a small amount of milk (which has been whisked with 1 egg and salt) over the flour
2. Stir to a firm dough
3. Mix this dough with the rolls cut into cubes
4. Let it rest for a while
5. Using a slotted spoon, scoop out dumplings and place them in plenty of boiling salt water for 8 minutes
6. Lift them onto a round platter
7. Pour over them breadcrumbs roasted in fat
