## Ingredients

- Yellowish-brown roux
- Red cabbage
- Water
- Good broth, 2 spoons
- Salt
- Sugar
- Roasted chestnuts
- Bratwurst

## Directions

1. Add the red cabbage (cleaned, blanched twice with boiling water, cooked soft, drained and finely cut) to a yellowish-brown roux
2. Stew for a while, add 2 spoons of good broth, salt and a piece of sugar
3. Stew a while longer, then add roasted chestnuts as desired
4. Arrange neatly on the dish when serving and garnish with bratwurst
