## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal
- 1.5 pounds ham
- Yellow turnips
- Small onions
- 2 cloves
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- 0.5 pound butter
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 measure (Maß) cream
- Milk (if needed)
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound veal and 1.5 pounds ham into small pieces.
2. Place in a casserole with some yellow turnips, small onions, 2 cloves, bay leaves, thyme, and 0.5 pound butter.
3. Let everything draw slowly on low coals, stirring often to prevent the meat from sticking to the bottom or darkening.
4. Stir 5 tablespoons flour into the casserole.
5. Once well mixed, pour in 0.5 measure of cream.
6. Stir with a cooking spoon until the sauce begins to boil.
7. Simmer gently for 1.5 hours until it reduces properly.
8. If it becomes too thick, add some milk.
9. Do not salt heavily as ham is already present.
10. The sauce should bind and look like porridge.
11. If still too thick when serving, add some cream or good milk, strain through cheesecloth, and use as needed.
