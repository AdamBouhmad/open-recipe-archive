## Ingredients

- 4-6 young kohlrabi
- hashé of 25 dkg roasted veal
- 1/2 cup cream
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the top off each kohlrabi, peel, and hollow out.
2. Cook the kohlrabi with their tops in salted water for 20-30 minutes, drain, and cool.
3. Fill with hashé and replace the tops.
4. Place them in a buttered omelet pan, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, dot with butter.
5. Bake for 10-15 minutes in the oven, basting with cream during baking.
6. Serve on a round plate as a first course or with fine garnish.
