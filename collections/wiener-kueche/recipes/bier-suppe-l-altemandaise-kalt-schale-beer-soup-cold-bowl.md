## Ingredients

- 2 liters beer
- 1/2 kilo sugar
- Lemon peels, finely cut
- Cinnamon, a small amount
- 1 pinch salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon cold milk
- 1/4 liter black bread rolls
- Small raisins and currants, together 12 deca

## Directions

1. Bring 2 liters beer to a boil with 1/2 kilo sugar, finely cut lemon peels, some cinnamon and 1 pinch salt.
2. Stir in 8 egg yolks which have been whisked with 1 tablespoon cold milk.
3. Strain through a sieve into a bowl and set cold.
4. Toast 1/4 liter black bread rolls in the oven.
5. Clean and search through small raisins and currants, together 12 deca, simmer in 1/2 liter water, drain and lay the raisins and currants on a cloth.
6. Add these and the dried bread rolls to the beer soup, mix well together and place the bowl on ice for several hours.
