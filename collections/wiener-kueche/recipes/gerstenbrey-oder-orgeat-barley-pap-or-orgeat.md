## Ingredients

- hulled and soaked barley
- sweet almonds with skin removed
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take hulled barley that has been soaked
2. Rub it with the hands
3. Cook it for seven or eight hours
4. Place it in a stone mortar
5. Rub it with blanched sweet almonds and press it through
6. Add sugar
7. Place it in a silver or earthen bowl on glowing coals
8. Let it cook very gently, then it is ready
9. Those who prefer it thicker may cook the pressed mixture again; those who prefer it thinner may leave it uncooked
