## Ingredients

- crayfish as needed
- truffle, thinly sliced
- salt
- Madeira wine to cover

## Directions

1. Place the needed quantity of crayfish in a casserole
2. Add thinly sliced truffle
3. Season with salt
4. Add enough Madeira wine to cover the crayfish
5. Cover with a fitting lid
6. Cook for 7.5 hours
7. Let stand covered until serving
8. Arrange the crayfish on a dish, cover with a lid or napkin
9. Serve the sauce in a sauce boat, seasoned with butter and lemon juice if desired
