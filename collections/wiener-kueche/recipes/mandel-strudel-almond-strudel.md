## Ingredients

- 10 deca sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 16 deca blanched, finely ground almonds
- 2 egg whites
- Strudel dough
- melted butter
- selected raisins

## Directions

1. Place 10 deca sugar in a deep bowl
2. Stir in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks gradually
3. Mix with 16 deca blanched, finely ground almonds
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 2 egg whites
5. Brush stretched strudel dough with melted butter
6. Distribute the mixture on top
7. Sprinkle with 16 deca selected raisins
8. Roll dough together
9. Treat as for Rice Gries Strudel (No. 1236)
