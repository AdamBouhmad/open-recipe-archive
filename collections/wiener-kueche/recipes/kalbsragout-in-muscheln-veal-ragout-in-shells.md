## Ingredients

- 50 dag veal shoulder or rack
- 1/2 head cauliflower
- 2 mushrooms
- Parmesan cheese
- 5 dag butter
- 5 dag butter, parsley and flour for roux
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, cut into small squares, salt it, place in a casserole with 5 dag hot butter, pour in a little water, cover and braise 45 minutes.
2. Remove the meat to a plate and pour the juice over it.
3. In the casserole, make a roux from 5 dag butter, parsley and flour, add the meat with its juice, let it reduce, add lemon juice, separately cooked cauliflower separated into florets, and sliced mushrooms.
4. Stir minimally.
5. Fill the ragout into buttered shells, place on a baking sheet, sprinkle with grated cheese and breadcrumbs, drizzle melted butter over it, gratinée in the oven 15-20 minutes.
6. Serve the shells on a cup with a folded napkin.
