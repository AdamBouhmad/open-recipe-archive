## Ingredients

- 3 pounds black sweet cherries
- 3 pounds raspberries
- 3 pounds strawberries
- brandy (half measure per measure of juice)
- sugar (half pound per measure of juice)

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries and crush the raspberries and strawberries
2. Put all together in a clean pot
3. Cover and let stand for 5 to 6 hours in a cool place
4. Press through thin cloth
5. For each measure of juice, add half measure brandy and half pound roughly crushed sugar
6. Put together in a large glass
7. Seal well with a bladder
8. Shake vigorously 2 to 3 times daily
9. After standing for four weeks, filter the liqueur
10. Fill into bottles, cork well, and let stand in a cool place for at least eight months
