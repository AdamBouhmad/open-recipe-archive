## Ingredients

- Veal forcemeat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon peel
- Flour
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Fat

## Directions

1. The veal forcemeat, as specified in the recipe, is salted, seasoned, then passed and carefully mixed with finely cut lemon peel added with a knife point.
2. On a board sprinkled with flour, this forcemeat is rolled out finger-thick with a rolling pin which is also lightly sprinkled with flour, and cutlets are cut out with a cutter form and laid on a flour-sprinkled board.
3. They are then spread with beaten eggs, sprinkled with bread crumbs, and by turning them with the help of a knife or pastry server, this is repeated on the other side.
4. Shortly before use, the fillets are placed in a casserole in finger-thick, very hot fat, fried light yellow on both sides and removed with a pastry server.
5. In the same way, these fillets (cutlets) of fish forcemeat can be prepared for fast days. Ribs for the cutlets are formed from a piece of parsley or cinnamon bark and covered with paper papillotes. They are served with fine vegetables, fine ragout or a piquant sauce.
