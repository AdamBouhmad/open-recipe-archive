## Ingredients

- Small sprouts
- Water, lightly salted
- 12 Loth butter
- Salt
- White pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the small sprouts of their coarser leaves
2. Cook them soft in plenty of lightly salted water, such that only the outer leaves are easily crushed; the interior of the small sprout must remain somewhat firm
3. Drain the cooked sprouts well
4. Season one measure of the sprouts in 12 Loth butter with salt and a little white pepper
5. Stew over strong heat for a quarter hour, such that the accumulated juice evaporates
6. Serve with an accompaniment of bratwurst, pickled tongues, or mutton cutlets, etc. as a vegetable
