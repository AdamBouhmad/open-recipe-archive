## Ingredients

- 16 ounces flour
- 0.5 liter cold cream
- 10 ounces blanched almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 ounces sugar
- 0.5 liter cream
- 6 egg whites, whipped
- baked biscuit

## Directions

1. Gradually stir flour into cold cream until smooth
2. Add blanched almonds finely pounded with 2 egg whites, then egg yolks, sugar, and cream one by one
3. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites
4. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold, insert baked biscuit as desired
5. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour or bake in the oven
