## Ingredients

- 16 decilitres butter or fat
- 1 piece finely chopped onion
- very little finely chopped root vegetables
- 12 decilitres flour
- 1/4 litre vinegar
- 1/2 litre water (or as much liquid as needed for sauce)
- 3 sprigs thyme
- 2 crushed juniper berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 grains of each type of spice
- 5 sprigs parsley tied together
- 1 small piece of lemon peel
- approximately 1 kilo carp, cut into 2-finger wide pieces
- carp blood
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 1/4 litres sour cream
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. In 16 decilitres of butter or fat, add 1 piece of finely chopped onion and very little finely chopped root vegetables
2. Allow this to roast covered for a short time
3. Add 12 decilitres flour and let it brown
4. Add 1/4 litre vinegar, 1/2 litre water (or as much liquid as needed for sauce), 3 sprigs thyme, 2 crushed juniper berries, 1 bay leaf, 5 grains of each type of spice, 5 sprigs parsley tied together, and 1 small piece of lemon peel
5. Let this simmer for 1/2 hour
6. Place approximately 1 kilo of carp, cut into 2-finger wide pieces, in a casserole
7. Pour the sauce over and let it simmer covered for another 1/2 hour
8. Arrange the fish elevated on a platter
9. Mix the carp blood with 1 tablespoon vinegar, then with 1 1/4 litres sour cream and 3 egg yolks, stirring well
10. Stir this mixture into the sauce
11. Pour part of this sauce over the fish and serve the rest in a sauceboat
12. Serve with dumplings, noodles, or potato dumplings
