## Ingredients

- 8 deca of fat
- root vegetables
- onion
- whole spices
- 1 bay leaf
- white herbs
- poularde
- 1/4 liter of white wine
- 1/4 liter of broth
- 1/4 liter of cream
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon of capers

## Directions

1. Place fat, root vegetables, onion, whole spices, bay leaf, and white herbs in a casserole
2. Lay a cleaned, trussed, and seasoned poularde on top and let it braise covered
3. When the resulting juice has evaporated, pour in 1/4 liter of white wine and 1/4 liter of broth and let the poularde braise covered until tender
4. Pour 1/4 liter of cream over it, add the juice of a lemon, and cook this together well
5. Remove the poularde, cut it into desired pieces, and arrange in a bowl
6. Pass the sauce through a sieve into a casserole, add 1 tablespoon of capers, bring to a boil once, and serve with the poularde
7. The poularde can be garnished with various shellfish dishes cooked in salt water
