## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Salt water
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. After preparing and plucking the calf's head as indicated, cook it very tender in salt water
2. Place in cold water, then remove and clean with a cloth and dry
3. Cut into pieces three fingers wide and one finger long
4. Sprinkle with some salt
5. Dredge in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat in breadcrumbs
6. Fry in very hot fat two fingers deep
7. Fry the pieces on both sides
