## Ingredients

- 10 unsalted boiled and prepared crayfish
- 2 pounds crayfish butter
- 10 egg yolks
- crayfish meat
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg flowers
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Finely chop the meat from 10 unsalted boiled crayfish that have been prepared.
2. Beat 2 pounds crayfish butter into a foam, beat in 10 egg yolks, and mix well with the chopped crayfish meat, salt, pepper, nutmeg flowers, and enough breadcrumbs so that it becomes a ball.
3. Proceed with this mass as already described.
