## Ingredients

- good yellow mustard
- very strong vinegar, strengthened by frost
- marjoram, 1 loth per measure of vinegar
- tarragon (Drachenkraut), 2 loth per measure of vinegar
- thyme, 1 loth per measure of vinegar
- sage, 1 loth per measure of vinegar
- rocambole onions (Roccombolenzwiebeln), 2 loth per measure of vinegar
- mustard powder, 2 pounds
- pounded sugar, 1 pound or more
- cinnamon, 12 loth
- ginger, 12 loth
- allspice (Neugewürz), 12 loth
- fennel seeds (Fenchelſamen), 1 quenchen
- vinegar, as needed

## Directions

1. Pound good yellow mustard in a mortar and warm it in a pan until the oil can be pressed out with the fingers
2. Press it strongly and pound it into fine powder
3. To one measure of very strong vinegar that has been strengthened by frost, add 1 loth marjoram, 2 loth tarragon, and 1 loth each of thyme and sage, all dried and finely chopped, then add 2 loth rocambole onions
4. Let this steep in the sun for 5 to 6 days, then strain it through
5. Take 2 pounds of the mustard powder, 1 pound of pounded sugar or more, 12 loth each of cinnamon, ginger, and allspice (all pounded), and 1 quenchen of fennel seeds
6. Mix everything well and make it into a paste with as much vinegar as necessary
