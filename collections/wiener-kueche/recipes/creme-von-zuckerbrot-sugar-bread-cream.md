## Ingredients

- 20 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons fine flour
- 8 loth biscuit
- 1 seidel Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Stir 20 egg yolks, 3 tablespoons fine flour, 8 loth biscuit, and 1 seidel Rhine wine together thoroughly
2. Let it boil once over constant stirring
3. It must become quite thick and is served cold
