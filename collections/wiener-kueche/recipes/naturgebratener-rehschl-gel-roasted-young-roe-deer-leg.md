## Ingredients

- Young roe deer leg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Paper for wrapping
- Glace with piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the leg and rub it with salt and pepper, then spit it
2. Wrap it with 2 sheets of paper brushed with butter and tie it
3. Place on the spit and roast for 1 hour at medium-strong heat
4. Half an hour before serving, remove the paper, increase the flame so the meat takes on a beautiful brown color
5. When serving, cover the bone with a paper frill and serve with some glace with piquant sauce
6. Alternatively, if roasting in the oven, put some fat in the casserole, lay the meat on 2 tablespoons, cover the top with bacon slices, roast for approximately 1⅓ hours and baste it with its own juices
7. Cut as desired and add the strained juice
8. Garnish with orange or lemon slices and serve with compote or sweet salad
