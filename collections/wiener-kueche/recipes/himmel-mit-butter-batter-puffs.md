## Ingredients

- 14 tablespoons fine flour
- 14 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons rum
- 1/2 liter cream
- 3 tablespoons melted, clarified butter

## Directions

1. Place 14 tablespoons fine flour, 14 tablespoons vanilla sugar, and 1 pinch salt into a deep bowl.
2. Gradually add 2 whole eggs, 2 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons rum, and 1/2 liter cream, stirring very well.
3. Mix in 3 tablespoons melted, clarified butter until well combined.
4. The mixture should flow in a thin stream from a spoon.
5. Prepare Hoblthuppen (cream puffs) using this batter as described in No. 1655.
