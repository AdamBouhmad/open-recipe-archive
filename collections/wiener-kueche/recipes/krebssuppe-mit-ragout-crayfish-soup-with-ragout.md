## Ingredients

- 30 crayfish
- pike heads, 2 baked
- bread rolls roasted in drippings
- pea broth
- nutmeg flower
- ground pepper
- asparagus heads, parboiled
- roach (Maurachen), braised
- pike liver
- pike, shredded
- crayfish tails
- crayfish butter
- flour
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Parboil 30 crayfish
2. Free 15 of them from the bile, which is then pounded
3. From the remaining crayfish, remove the tails and claws and set them aside
4. Pound 2 baked pike heads with some bread rolls roasted in drippings, to which you add good pea broth and let simmer
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Add some nutmeg flower and ground pepper
7. Prepare parboiled asparagus heads, braised roach, pike liver, shredded pike, and crayfish tails
8. Put a little crayfish butter in a casserole, dust with a little flour and stir in the above ingredients
9. Pour the strained crayfish soup over it and bring to a boil again
10. Serve over toasted bread slices
