## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- 8 dekagrams butter or fat
- 1 large onion
- 2 knife tips paprika
- salt
- hot water
- 1 deciliter sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the chickens, quarter them and salt them.
2. In a casserole, heat 8 dekagrams of butter or fat and lightly brown 1 large onion until light yellow.
3. Add 2 knife tips of paprika to the onion.
4. Place the chicken pieces skin-side down into the casserole.
5. Pour some hot water beside them and braise them covered, turning occasionally and adding water as needed, for 1-1.5 hours depending on the size of the chickens.
6. Place the cooked chicken on a plate and strain the cooking liquid.
7. Return the liquid with the meat to the casserole.
8. Stir in the sour cream spoonful by spoonful and braise for another 10 minutes.
9. Serve the chicken on a round dish with the sauce.
