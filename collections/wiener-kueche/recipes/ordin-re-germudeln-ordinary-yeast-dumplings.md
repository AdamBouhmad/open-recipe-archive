## Ingredients

- 50 Deka flour
- 4 Deka yeast dissolved in 4/8 liter lukewarm milk
- 1/2 liter milk
- 3 Stercks butter
- 1 whole egg
- 1 heaping tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Filling of choice

## Directions

1. Place 50 Deka of flour in a deep bowl, make a well in the middle, pour in 4 Deka yeast dissolved in 4/8 liter lukewarm milk into this well
2. Stir it together somewhat and let it rest covered for a short time
3. Beat together 1/2 liter milk, 3 Stercks butter, 1 whole egg, 1 heaping tablespoon sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt until frothy and pour it into the yeast mixture
4. Stir well and beat the dough until it detaches from the spoon
5. Let it rise covered in a warm place until doubled
6. Using a tablespoon, form pieces of dough of desired size, place them on a flour-dusted cloth and spread them apart with floured fingers
7. Place 1 teaspoon filling of choice in the middle of each dumpling, fold the dough over the filling and press together with 4 fingers
8. Place these dumplings in a butter-brushed casserole, brush them on the sides where they lie next to each other with melted butter and treat them further as described for 'Gewöhnliche Buchteln' (Nr. 1262)
