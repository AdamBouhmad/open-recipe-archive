## Ingredients

- Blue grapes
- 1 kilo grapes with 1/4 kilo powdered sugar
- Ground cinnamon and clove nuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Blue grapes are stripped from the bunch
2. 1 kilo of them with 1/4 kilo powdered sugar are boiled for approximately 15 minutes in a saucepan
3. Then like the apricot jam filled hot into jars
4. One can cook ground cinnamon and clove nuts with it
