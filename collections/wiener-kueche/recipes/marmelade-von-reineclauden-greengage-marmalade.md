## Ingredients

- 6 pounds ripe greengages
- 4 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Select 6 pounds of beautiful ripe greengages
2. Cut open and remove the pits
3. Pass through a fine sieve
4. Place on a charcoal fire until thick and the watery liquid is absorbed
5. Clarify and cook 4 pounds of sugar as described
6. Slowly stir in the fruit mass
7. Continue cooking over the charcoal fire until it reaches the proper thickness
8. After cooling, fill into boxes or jars
9. Plum (Zwetschken) and mirabelle marmalade are prepared the same way
