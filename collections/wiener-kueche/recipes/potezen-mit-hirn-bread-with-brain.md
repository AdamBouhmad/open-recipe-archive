## Ingredients

- 4-5 sliced rolls
- 1 pork brain or 1/2 calf brain
- 3/4 liter milk
- salt
- breading

## Directions

1. Cut the rolls into slices so that every 2 remain connected together.
2. Spread the inner side with roasted brain, dip in salted milk, bread them, and bake them.
