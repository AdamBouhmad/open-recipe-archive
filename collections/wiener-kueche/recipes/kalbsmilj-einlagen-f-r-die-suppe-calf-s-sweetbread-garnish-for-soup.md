## Ingredients

- 1 calf's sweetbread
- 12 decigrams butter
- 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 knife point of nutmeg
- 1 knife point of white pepper
- 16 decigrams white breadcrumbs, softened in milk

## Directions

1. Blanch the calf's sweetbread.
2. Beat 12 decigrams butter until fluffy.
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk and stir this into the prepared sweetbread.
4. Stir in a teaspoon of finely chopped green parsley, a knife point of nutmeg, and the same amount of white pepper.
5. Mix it with 16 decigrams white breadcrumbs, softened somewhat in milk, into a dough.
6. In a greased, floured mold, cook it in a steam bath.
