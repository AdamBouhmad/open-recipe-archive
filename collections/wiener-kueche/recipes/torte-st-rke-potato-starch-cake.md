## Ingredients

- 24 Deka lemon sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- juice of 1 whole lemon
- whites of 12 eggs
- 12 Deka potato starch
- 12 Deka finest arrowroot flour

## Directions

1. Stir together 24 Deka lemon sugar and 12 egg yolks with the juice of 1 whole lemon for 1 minute
2. Lightly fold in the whites of 12 eggs with 12 Deka potato starch and 12 Deka finest arrowroot flour
