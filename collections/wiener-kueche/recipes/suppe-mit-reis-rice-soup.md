## Ingredients

- 60 spoonfuls cleaned rice
- approximately 1.5 liters white broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in warm water to remove any staleness
2. Heat the rice with approximately 1.5 liters white broth and let it cook covered evenly
3. Before serving, thin with as much strong broth as needed
4. Alternatively, cook 16 spoonfuls rice in 0.5 liter white broth with salt, covered, slowly. The rice will absorb the broth, remain white and whole
5. Add approximately 1.5 liters white or brown broth to the pot with the cooked rice
