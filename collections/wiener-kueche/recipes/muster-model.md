## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 8 decagrams peeled, finely cut almonds
- 8 decagrams almonds cut very finely with a sabre knife
- oil

## Directions

1. Put 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar and juice of 1 lemon in a bowl
2. As soon as the sugar foams yellow, mix with finely cut and sabre-knife-cut almonds
3. Fill oil-coated molds or other forms with this warm mass
4. Press firmly with a whole lemon into the form so that the decoration on the form is firmly impressed into the mass
5. Turn the impressed mass out (knock out) into a bowl and continue in this manner until the mass is used up, keeping it warm throughout
