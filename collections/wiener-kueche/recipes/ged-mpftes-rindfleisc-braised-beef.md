## Ingredients

- a beautiful piece of beef (hip-shank)
- salted and elongated cut tongues
- bacon
- salt
- fresh bacon
- onion
- yellow turnips
- parsley root
- celery, sliced in leaves
- lemon peel
- whole pepper
- belly herb (Kuttelkraut)
- half wine
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Lard a beautiful piece of beef (hip-shank) with salted and elongated cut tongues and bacon, and salt it.
2. In a braising pan, put fresh bacon, onion, yellow turnips, parsley root, and celery, sliced in leaves.
3. Add lemon peel and whole pepper, and belly herb.
4. Lay the beef on top, add half wine, some vinegar and water, and let it stew well covered.
5. When soft enough, remove the meat and skim off the fat.
6. Let the root vegetables cook down well.
7. Before plating, put the meat back in the pan and let it braise a little more.
8. Place it on a plate, pass one portion of the sauce through a sieve over it, and serve the other portion of sauce in a bowl.
