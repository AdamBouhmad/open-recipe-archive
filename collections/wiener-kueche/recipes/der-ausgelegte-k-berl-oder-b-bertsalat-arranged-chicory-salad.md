## Ingredients

- salad, washed
- chicory
- soft boiled salsify root, peeled
- aspic jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the salad and place it on a plate
2. Take a chicory and make a neat bouquet from it, placing it in the center of the salad
3. Take a soft boiled peeled salsify root, cut it into leaf-like pieces
4. Arrange these leaves neatly around the bouquet
5. Take aspic jelly, cut it into small pieces, and arrange them around the edge of the plate, then it is finished
