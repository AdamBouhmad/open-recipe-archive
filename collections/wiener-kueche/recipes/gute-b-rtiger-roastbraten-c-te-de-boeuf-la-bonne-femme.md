## Ingredients

- Roastbraten, cut finger-thick
- Salt
- Flour
- Fat
- Vinegar
- Caraway
- Broth
- Parsley butter with lemon juice
- Bacon pieces (6-7 per piece, finger-long, seasoned with pepper and salt)

## Directions

1. Cut the roastbraten finger-thick, pound them but not too thin, dust with salt, dip them in flour, and fry them in a pan in some fat until they take on a golden-brown color.
2. Pour the juice in which the roastbraten were previously roasted into a casserole and pour some vinegar, some caraway, and enough broth over it to cover the roastbraten.
3. Cover and let them braise until tender, then lay them on a plate and place in the center of each piece some parsley butter with lemon juice.
4. Serve the degreased juice of the roastbraten in a sauce boat.
5. Alternatively, each piece of roastbraten can be threaded through with approximately 6-7 pieces of bacon, finger-long and thick, seasoned with pepper and salt, which gives the juice a good flavor.
