## Ingredients

- 12 deca crayfish butter
- 16 deca powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 deca blanched almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites
- ½ liter rum
- 4 egg whites
- ½ liter flour
- cooked crayfish claws and tails
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mix 12 deca crayfish butter and 16 deca powdered sugar well together
2. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks and blend this mixture well
3. Add 12 deca blanched almonds (finely ground with 2 egg whites), ½ liter rum, one after another, mixing well
4. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 4 egg whites last
5. Prepare strudel dough from ½ liter flour and stretch it finely as described in the Young Strudel recipe
6. Spread with the prepared filling above
7. Sprinkle with crayfish claws and tails (cut finely) and crayfish shells, then with breadcrumbs
8. Treat further as described in the Sweet Semolina Strudel recipe
