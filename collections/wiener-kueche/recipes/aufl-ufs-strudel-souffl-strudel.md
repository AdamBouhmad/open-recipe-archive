## Ingredients

- ½ liter flour
- ¼ liter flour (for filling)
- ½ liter milk (for filling)
- 12 deca butter
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough as described in the Rice Strudel recipe with the difference that the following mass is used instead of rice filling
2. In a saucepan, put ¼ liter flour, gradually pour in ½ liter milk while stirring constantly
3. Stir this on the fire until it becomes thick, then pour into a deep bowl
4. Stir cold with 12 deca butter and 16 deca vanilla sugar
5. Gradually stir 6 egg yolks one after another into this now-cooled mass
6. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 6 egg whites last
