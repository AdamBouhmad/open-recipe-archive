## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Meat glaze
- Espagnole

## Directions

1. Beat and trim the chops
2. Turn them in butter, salt, pepper, and fine herbs
3. A quarter hour before serving, place them on strong charcoal fire
4. Turn them quickly on both sides
5. Pour off the butter
6. Add a piece of glaze and let it dissolve while shaking
7. Dress the chops neatly
8. Add a little worked espagnole in the roasting pan
9. Pour it in the middle of the platter and serve
