## Ingredients

- Neck, breast, and shoulder of mutton
- Bacon
- Onion, cut into leaves
- Flour
- Soup stock
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- White pepper
- Thyme
- White turnips, neatly cut

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces 2 fingers wide.
2. Let these brown with bacon and thinly sliced onion over fairly strong heat.
3. Remove the meat pieces and place in another pan.
4. In the remaining fat, stir in a few spoonfuls of flour and toast.
5. Deglaze with some soup stock.
6. Season with 1 bay leaf, salt, white pepper, and some thyme.
7. Pass through a sieve after boiling.
8. Pour over the meat pieces.
9. Add neatly cut white turnips.
