## Ingredients

- Puff pastry (No. 1045) without sugar
- Beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Roll out the puff pastry (No. 1045) without sugar on a flour-dusted board 2.5 knife-back widths thick, and as desired cut out round, leaf-like, crescent-shaped, or square shapes.
2. Place them with a spoon in three-finger distance apart on a board moistened with water, brush with beaten eggs while keeping the edges knife-back-wide dry, and bake yellow at strong heat in the oven.
3. With a thin knife, loosen the puff pastry from the board and use it as garnish for game dishes.
