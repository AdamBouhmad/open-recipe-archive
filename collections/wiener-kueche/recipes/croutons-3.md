## Ingredients

- 1¼ Schneidemmels (sliced bread rolls)
- 6 dkg butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the bread rolls first in half, then into leaf-like slices.
2. Melt the butter in a small pot.
3. Dip the bread slices in the melted butter, then turn them on both sides in grated Parmesan.
4. Lay them in an omelet pan and bake them in the oven at moderate heat until light brown.
5. Serve them separately.
