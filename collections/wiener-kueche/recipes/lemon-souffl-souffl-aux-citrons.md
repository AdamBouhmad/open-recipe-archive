## Ingredients

- 12 tablespoons softened butter
- 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 tablespoons blanched almonds, finely ground, with 1 egg white
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, passed through a sieve
- 10 tablespoons finely diced citron
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the softened butter with vanilla sugar until well combined
2. Add 8 egg yolks one after another, stirring
3. Gradually mix in the blanched almonds (ground with 1 egg white), hard-boiled egg yolks, and finely diced citron
4. Lightly fold in the snow of 6 egg whites
5. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
6. Cover and cook 3/4 to 1 hour in a water bath or bake in the oven
7. Turn out onto a serving dish and dust with finely ground sugar and serve at table
