## Ingredients

- turkey (Indian)
- poultry livers
- truffles or mushrooms
- mushrooms or truffles, chopped
- young onions, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- oil or butter
- butter and flour
- good fond
- white wine
- lemon juice
- garlic (optional)
- bacon sheets and paper

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the turkey for roasting
2. Make a forcemeat from some poultry livers, truffles or mushrooms, well seasoned
3. Fill the turkey with this and prepare it, threading it on the spit
4. When it takes color, wrap it with bacon sheets and paper, roast it slowly, and frequently baste with hot butter
5. Meanwhile finely hack some cleaned mushrooms or truffles, young onions, and parsley, pass through oil or butter, then add a piece of butter mixed with flour
6. Add some good fond and a small glass of good white wine and let the sauce cook well when seasoned
7. Garlic can sometimes be added, but it can easily be omitted
8. Finally squeeze lemon juice into it, remove the bacon sheets, arrange the turkey on the platter, and serve this sauce hot underneath
