## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter wine
- 8 Deca sugar
- 1 clove
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 12 Deca sliced rolls (Semmeln), cut in flakes
- 1 Deca butter, worked
- 16 Deca finely pounded sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites (snow)

## Directions

1. Pour 1/4 liter wine with 8 Deca sugar, 1 clove and 1 piece cinnamon into a saucepan, bring to a good boil and pour through a sieve over 12 Deca sliced rolls, cut in flakes
2. Stir over the fire until it has cooked well, then set it aside and let it cool
3. Meanwhile work 1 Deca butter with 16 Deca finely pounded sugar well together, stir in 8 egg yolks one after another, then mix this well with the cooled mass and finally fold in lightly the snow (whipped egg whites) from 8 egg whites
4. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mass, cover and cook for 3/4 hours in steam or bake in the oven
