## Ingredients

- 1 kg cleaned calf's feet
- water
- black bread
- garlic
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place cleaned calf's feet in a casserole, cover with water about 3 fingers above the meat
2. Simmer until soft
3. Cut thinly sliced black bread and roast with some garlic in desired fat in a deep bowl
4. Place the soft-cooked calf's feet, which must be cut finely, with the bread and pour seasoned and peppered broth from the calf's feet over it
5. Instead of garlic, finely cut roasted onion can be used
