## Ingredients

- cabbage
- 1/4 hectogram fat
- salt
- flour
- salted water

## Directions

1. Cut, salt, and squeeze out the cabbage as indicated in the Kraut-Strudel recipe
2. Place it in a pot containing 1/4 hectogram fat
3. Braise covered with frequent stirring until soft
4. Season to taste
5. Add noodles made from flour, cut and boiled in salted water
6. Let them become hot with frequent stirring
7. Heap onto a plate and serve
