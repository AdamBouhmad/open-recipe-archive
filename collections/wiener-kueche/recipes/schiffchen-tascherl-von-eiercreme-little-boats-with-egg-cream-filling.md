## Ingredients

- 8 deca butter
- 6 deca fine powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 2 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk or cream
- 32 deca flour
- beaten eggs for brushing
- 1 tablespoon cream, custard or jam filling

## Directions

1. Beat 8 deca butter with 6 deca fine powdered sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt until fluffy, add 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg one after the other, add the yeast mixture and 32 deca flour, stir well and beat the dough until it separates from the spoon, let stand covered in a warm place
2. When the dough has risen somewhat, put it on a floured board, roll out strongly to the thickness of a knife back, cut into squares of desired size, brush the corners with beaten eggs
3. Put 1 tablespoon of cream, custard or jam filling in the middle of the squares, fold up the 4 corners in the middle, place on a buttered sheet and let stand lightly covered in a warm place
4. When risen after some time, brush with beaten eggs, sprinkle with blanched, finely cut almonds and bake at fairly high heat in the oven, then remove with a very thin spatula, arrange on a plate and serve sprinkled with sugar
