## Ingredients

- 16 spoonfuls fine semolina
- approximately 2 liters boiling broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Add 16 spoonfuls fine semolina under constant stirring to approximately 2 liters boiling broth
2. Let cook for 1/2 hour, salt, and serve
