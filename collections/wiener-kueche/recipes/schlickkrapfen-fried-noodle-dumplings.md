## Ingredients

- 15 dkg flour
- 1 egg
- some lukewarm water
- salt
- 30 dkg ground beef (hachee)

## Directions

1. Make a firm strudel dough from the flour, egg, lukewarm water and salt
2. Let it rest for ½ hour under a warmed casserole
3. Roll it out thin and cut out shapes with a cutter
4. Place hachee in the middle, brush edges with egg white, and press together over the filling
5. Cook in strained, boiling beef broth in a casserole
