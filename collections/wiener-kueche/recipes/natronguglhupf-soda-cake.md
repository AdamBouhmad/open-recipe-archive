## Ingredients

- 14 dkg butter
- 14 dkg sugar
- 4 whole eggs
- 28 dkg flour
- lemon zest
- ½ aborted, lukewarm milk
- 1 spoonful rum
- 5 grams baking soda
- 10 grams cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Beat the butter for 15 minutes
2. Gradually mix in 14 dkg sugar and 4 whole eggs
3. Mix in spoonfuls of 28 dkg flour, lemon zest, ½ boiled, lukewarm milk and 1 spoonful rum
4. Mix 5 grams baking soda and 10 grams cream of tartar together
5. Lightly fold this mixture into the batter
6. Immediately fill into the mold and bake at sharper heat
