## Ingredients

- one capercaillie
- salt
- crushed rowan berries
- basil
- rosemary
- wine
- vinegar
- onions
- bay leaves
- lard for larding
- melted lard for basting
- lemon peel for garnish
- anchovy or caper sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck the capercaillie, wash it well, salt it thoroughly and rub it with crushed rowan berries, basil and rosemary
2. Let it lie for one hour, then make a marinade from wine, vinegar, onions and bay leaves and pour it over hot
3. If it is old, it must be marinated for two to three days and poured over with hot marinade twice daily; if it is young, marinate it for only one day
4. Then lard it beautifully with lard, roast it slowly on a spit, baste it frequently with melted lard
5. Make a quick anchovy or caper sauce from the drippings and the marinade
6. Arrange it on a dish, garnish it with lemon peel and serve the sauce in a bowl alongside
