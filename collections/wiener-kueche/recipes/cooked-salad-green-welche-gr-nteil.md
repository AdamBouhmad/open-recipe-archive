## Ingredients

- Young salad greens
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Herb butter or toasted bread crumbs or Dutch sauce or butter sauce

## Directions

1. After these have been cleaned and washed, boil them gently in salt water; arrange them in a heap on a plate and pour over them either herb butter, toasted bread crumbs, a Dutch sauce, or an ordinary butter sauce.
