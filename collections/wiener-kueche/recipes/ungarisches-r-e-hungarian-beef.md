## Ingredients

- well-marbled beef from the rib section
- very little finely chopped suet
- a pair of onions, sliced
- salt
- Turkish pepper (paprika)
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Skin a well-marbled beef from the rib section and cut it into pieces somewhat longer and wider than one finger joint
2. Put very little finely chopped suet in a casserole, along with a pair of onions sliced into rounds
3. Add the cut meat and let it braise in its own broth
4. Salt it properly and season it only with Turkish pepper (paprika), but do so moderately as it gives a strong flavor and heavily peppered meat may not please Germans as much as Hungarians
5. Finally dust it with a little flour to thicken the sauce and do not let it cook too soft
