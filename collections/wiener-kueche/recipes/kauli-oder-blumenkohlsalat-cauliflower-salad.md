## Ingredients

- Beautiful white cauliflower
- Salt water
- Fresh water
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook beautiful white cauliflower in whole florets in salted water
2. When the florets are soft enough, drain off the water
3. Rinse them with fresh water so they remain beautifully white
4. Salt them slightly
5. Arrange them neatly on a serving dish
6. Serve with vinegar and oil
