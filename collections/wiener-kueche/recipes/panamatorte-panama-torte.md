## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 8 dkg grated chocolate
- 7 egg whites
- 14 dkg raw grated almonds
- 15 dkg butter
- 15 dkg vanilla sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 8–10 dkg grated chocolate
- 5 dkg dark roasted, coarsely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 7 egg yolks for 30 minutes
2. Stir in 8 dkg grated chocolate
3. Fold in 7 egg whites and 14 dkg raw grated almonds
4. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake slowly
5. The next day, cut the torte and fill with crème
6. Assemble the pieces and spread crème on the outside and sides
7. Sprinkle with 5 dkg dark roasted, coarsely chopped almonds
8. For the crème: beat 15 dkg butter until creamy, stir in 15 dkg vanilla sugar, 1 whole egg, and 8–10 dkg grated chocolate
