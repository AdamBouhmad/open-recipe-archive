## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, firmly beaten
- 16 decagrams sugar
- 12 decagrams peeled, finely cut almonds or pignoli

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites firmly
2. Mix very well with 16 decagrams sugar and 12 decagrams peeled, finely cut almonds or pignoli
3. Form into peaks on a wax-coated baking sheet using a paper cone or piping bag
4. Dry in an oven at full heat
