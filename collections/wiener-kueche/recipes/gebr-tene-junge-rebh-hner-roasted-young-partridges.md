## Ingredients

- young partridges
- fine lard
- lard slices
- twine
- fat
- salt

## Directions

1. After the partridges have been plucked, stuffed, opened and cleaned, dress them as indicated for Capon No. 735.
2. Thread the breasts with fine lard or wrap them with lard slices, bind these with fine twine and roast the birds over strong fire for approximately 1/4 hour on the spit.
3. After this time, remove the partridges, cut the twine, carefully pull it out, divide the birds lengthwise down the middle, arrange them on a platter, cover with the lard slices and pour the defatted juice over them.
4. Alternatively, the partridges can be roasted in the oven by placing them in a pan in which there is some fat, and basting them frequently during roasting with the resulting juice.
