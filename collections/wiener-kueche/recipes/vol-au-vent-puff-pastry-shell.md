## Ingredients

- butter dough, approximately 1/2 kilogram
- eggs

## Directions

1. A chilled butter dough of approximately 1/2 kilogram is rolled out 2 fingers thick and a round plate is cut out.
2. Butter-brushed paper is placed on a baking sheet, the round dough plate is placed on it, brushed with beaten eggs so far that a strongly knife-back-width margin remains, then the dough is cut with a knife a finger's breadth deep in the rounding so that a 2-finger-wide margin is obtained all around.
3. The pâté is then placed in the oven and baked at strong heat for 3/4 to 1 hour.
4. To prevent the pâté from becoming too dark, it is covered with paper once it is yellow.
5. The pâté, now twice as high, is then removed from the oven, the cut plate is carefully loosened with a knife, removed, and the soft and fatty dough inside is carefully removed with a spoon without injuring the crust at the bottom.
6. If this is done as quickly as possible, place the pâté in a bowl, fill it shortly before serving with any desired hot ragout, cover it again with the lid, and serve the pâté to the table.
