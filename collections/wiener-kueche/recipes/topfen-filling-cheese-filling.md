## Ingredients

- 5 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- 5 dkg sugar
- topfen (cheese)
- cream
- lemon zest, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Beat 5 dkg butter until creamy
2. Stir in 1 egg, 5 dkg sugar, the strained topfen, a few spoons of cream, and finely chopped lemon zest
