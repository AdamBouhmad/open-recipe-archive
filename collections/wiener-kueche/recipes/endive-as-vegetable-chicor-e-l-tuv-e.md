## Ingredients

- Endive (Chicorée)
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth or cream
- Salt

## Directions

1. Finely clean the endive, wash it, place it in boiling salt water, and cook it uncovered until tender; place in cold water, press it, and chop it finely, then place in a casserole.
2. Blend a piece of butter with some flour, add this to the endive, season with salt, and pour broth or cream over it, or pour a butter sauce over it and let it boil.
3. Serve this with Calf Cutlets, fricandeau, etc.
