## Ingredients

- 8 heads Romaine lettuce (Kopfsalat)
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 12 drams butter
- 12 drams flour
- Broth
- Salt
- Green peas (4 spoonfuls, if desired)

## Directions

1. Take about 8 heads of Romaine lettuce (Rundsalat), divide each into 2 parts, remove the coarse stems, wash the salad, boil it in salt water, and place in cold water.
2. In 12 drams butter, make an equal amount of flour into a roux; add the pressed and somewhat chopped salad, pour ½ liter broth over it, season with salt, stir it, and let it boil well while stirring frequently.
3. If desired, add 4 spoonfuls of green peas.
