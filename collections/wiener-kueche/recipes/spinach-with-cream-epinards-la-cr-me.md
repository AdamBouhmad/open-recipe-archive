## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 8 drams hot butter
- Cream
- Salt
- Pepper (if desired)
- Bread rolls

## Directions

1. Boil the same amount of spinach as above in salt water, then pour cold water over it; pass it through a sieve once the water has drained sufficiently.
2. Place the strained spinach in a casserole with 8 drams hot butter, the necessary cream, some salt, and if desired, a pinch of pepper; let it boil well while stirring frequently.
3. Cut bread rolls into desired pieces or hollow them out, toast them in butter, and serve alongside.
