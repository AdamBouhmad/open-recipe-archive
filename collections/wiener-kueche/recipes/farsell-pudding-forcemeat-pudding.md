## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- flour as needed
- 1/3 liter boiling milk
- 12 deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare a firm dough from 2 whole eggs and the needed flour
2. Grate the dough on a grater
3. Stir into 1/3 liter boiling milk and cook thoroughly
4. Add 12 deca butter and mix well, then set aside
5. Beat 8 egg yolks with 16 deca vanilla sugar for 1/3 hour
6. Slowly add the cooled dough mixture and mix well
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites
8. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
9. Steam covered for 3/4 hour
