## Ingredients

- 2 cleaned chickens
- fat
- 1 head onion, thinly sliced
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 sprigs thyme
- ¼ liter dry or light wine
- ¾ liter cream
- potatoes or turnips
- 2 tablespoons capers or 1½ teaspoons chopped green parsley or chervil

## Directions

1. Cut each of 2 cleaned chickens into 4 pieces with the shoulder joint cut through the back
2. Place in a casserole containing fat, thinly sliced onion, bay leaf, and thyme
3. Cover and braise
4. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, pour in ¼ liter wine
5. Let some of this evaporate and pour ¾ liter cream over
6. Cover and braise briefly
7. When chickens are half-tender, add finely diced potatoes and braise together
8. Remove chickens, arrange in a serving dish, garnish with potatoes, and pour sauce over
9. Optionally stir in 2 tablespoons capers or 1½ teaspoons finely chopped green parsley or chervil and bring to a boil once
10. Instead of potatoes, diced turnips or macaroni cooked in salted water can be used to garnish
