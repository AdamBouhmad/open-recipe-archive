## Ingredients

- 14 decagrams flour
- 12.5 decagrams butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 spoonful milk
- 1 spoonful sugar
- Lemon peel
- Salt
- Apples, peeled and cored
- 8-10 sugar cubes
- Lemon peel
- Water
- 3 decagrams raisins
- Egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from 14 decagrams flour, 12.5 decagrams butter, 1 egg yolk, 1 spoonful milk, 1 spoonful sugar, lemon peel, and salt (see doughs)
2. After resting, roll out thinly and cut into a rectangle
3. Place on an ungreased baking sheet
4. Put filling down the middle lengthwise
5. Fold the dough over from both sides
6. From the dough scraps, cut narrow strips, brush with egg white, and lay them crosswise on the strudel
7. Brush the strudel with beaten egg
8. Bake at high heat until light brown
9. Cool, cut into equal pieces
10. Sprinkle with sugar and cool before serving
11. For the filling: Peel, core, and quarter the apples; stew gently with 8-10 sugar cubes, some lemon peel, a little water, and 3 decagrams raisins; the filling must be dry and only used when cooled
