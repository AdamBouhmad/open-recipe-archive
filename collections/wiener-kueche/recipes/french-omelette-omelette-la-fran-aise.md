## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff
- 1 tablespoon fine flour
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- a little salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon hot butter
- 1 tablespoon marmalade
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Mix the stiff egg whites with the flour, sugar, salt, and egg yolks lightly.
2. Pour 1 teaspoon hot butter into a hot frying pan.
3. Spread the prepared mixture somewhat and bake in the oven for approximately 8 minutes.
4. Spread 1 tablespoon marmalade on top.
5. Fold the omelette together using a wooden spoon, slide onto a dish, dust with sugar, and serve.
