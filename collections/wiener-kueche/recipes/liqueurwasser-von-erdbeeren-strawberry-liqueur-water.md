## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries picked before sunrise
- 1 large coffee spoon of lemon juice
- 8 ounces of fine sugar
- 6 to 8 ounces of spirits

## Directions

1. Pick ripe strawberries before sunrise or before the sun shines on them
2. Clean them of stems and leaves
3. Crush them with a wooden club in a stone mortar
4. Add 1 large coffee spoon of lemon juice
5. Stir gently with a wooden stick and bring to a glass vessel, where the mixture stands covered for some hours
6. Weigh 8 ounces of fine sugar and bring it to a stone pot
7. Pour the mixture over the sugar
8. When the sugar has dissolved, add 6 to 8 ounces of spirits
9. Stir everything together, strain through a filter bag, and keep in a cool place
