## Ingredients

- 6 fresh eggs
- 8 dkg bird lettuce or 2 heads lettuce
- 1 pickled cucumber
- 2 anchovies
- 4 dkg capers
- 2 dkg chives
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs for 12 minutes, halve them, and remove yolks
2. Fill with warm or cold mayonnaise
3. Garnish with anchovy strips
4. Sprinkle with chives
5. Arrange eggs in the center of a glass dish
6. Surround with prepared green salad
7. Place chopped yolks and lemon and cucumber slices between
8. Decorate to taste with capers or caviar
