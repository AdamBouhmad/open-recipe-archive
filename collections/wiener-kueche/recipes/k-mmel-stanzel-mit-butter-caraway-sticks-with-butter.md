## Ingredients

- 10 Deca flour
- 8 Deca butter
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 6 tablespoons cold milk

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board and rub in the butter finely
2. Make a well in the center, add salt and dissolved yeast
3. Mix and work into a smooth, firm dough
4. Cover and let rest for some time
5. Form into sticks or pretzels and proceed as for the Salt-Sticks of Yeast Dough (recipe 1297)
