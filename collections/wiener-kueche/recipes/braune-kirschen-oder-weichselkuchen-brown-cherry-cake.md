## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- 12 deca honey
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- cherries or morello cherries

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy, gradually add honey
2. Gently mix in 2 tablets grated chocolate and 12 deca honey
3. Fill this mixture into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Allow cherries or morello cherries to sink into the dough as desired
5. Bake for approximately 1 hour at low heat in the oven
6. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and serve the following day
