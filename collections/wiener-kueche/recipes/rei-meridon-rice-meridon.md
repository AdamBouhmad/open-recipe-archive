## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice
- milk or good broth
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- tails and claws from 30 crayfish
- 2 or 3 soft-boiled stomachs of guinea fowl or geese
- some cooked calf's head, cut lengthwise
- salt
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound rice in milk or good broth and let it cool
2. Beat a piece of butter the size of an egg until fluffy
3. Add the rice to the butter, then gradually stir in 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
4. Add the removed tails and claws from 30 crayfish, 2 or 3 soft-boiled stomachs of guinea fowl or geese, and some cooked calf's head cut lengthwise
5. Add some salt and finally fold in the beaten snow of 3 egg whites
6. Line the bottom of a cassole brushed with butter entirely with white paper and also brush the paper with butter
7. Arrange crayfish tails and asparagus heads boiled in salted water decoratively
8. Pour in the rice mixture and let it cook in steam for a full hour
9. Carefully turn it out onto a platter, remove the paper, and serve immediately
