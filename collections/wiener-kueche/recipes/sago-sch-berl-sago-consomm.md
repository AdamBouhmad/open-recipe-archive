## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters boiling milk
- 4 Deca washed sago
- 8 egg yolks
- Salt
- 4 spoons white bread crumbs
- Snow from 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook 4 Deca washed sago in 1 1/2 liters boiling milk, then pour it into a deep bowl and let it cool
2. Beat in 8 egg yolks, add some salt and 4 spoons of white bread crumbs and lightly mix in the snow from 8 eggs
3. Further handling is as with Griesschöberl
