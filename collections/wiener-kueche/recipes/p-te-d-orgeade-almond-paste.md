## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 4 loths bitter almonds
- a little water
- 1 pound finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch 1 pound sweet and 4 loths bitter almonds in boiling water until the skin can be removed
2. Wash them
3. Pound them very finely with a little water
4. Add 1 pound finely sifted sugar
5. Toast together over the fire with constant stirring until when you strike it with the flat of your hand, nothing of the mass sticks to it anymore
6. Remove the mass and when it has cooled, store it either in earthenware containers or form it into round sticks, roll in paper, and store in a cool place, not a damp one, until use
7. To make Orgeade, dissolve 1/4 pound of the mass in 1 measure of pure spring water
8. Let it steep for about an hour, then press through a napkin with some orange blossom water
