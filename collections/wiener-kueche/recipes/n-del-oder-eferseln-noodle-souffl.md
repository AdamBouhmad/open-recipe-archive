## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 12 teaspoons butter
- 12 teaspoons vanilla sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare a noodle dough from 2 eggs and roll it thin, cut into noodles or eferl
2. Cook in 1/2 liter of boiling milk and let it cook briefly
3. Add 12 teaspoons of butter, stir well and set aside
4. Meanwhile, beat 12 teaspoons of vanilla sugar and 4 egg yolks for 1/4 hour, add the cooled mass, stir well together and finally lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
5. Proceed as with the 'Brick Soufflé' (No. 1375)
