## Ingredients

- 1 liter wine
- 28 deciliters rum or arrack
- 20 deciliters sugar

## Directions

1. In an earthenware bowl, add 1 liter wine and 28 deciliters rum or arrack in which to dissolve it
2. Break 20 deciliters sugar into pieces, dip one after another in rum or arrack, place each on a perforated spoon, light the sugar and let it melt into the wine
3. After all 20 deciliters are burned, mix the liquid and serve it
