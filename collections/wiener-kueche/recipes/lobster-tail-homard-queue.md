## Ingredients

- Lobster tail
- Aspic or mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut the tail open on the side
2. Remove the upper cover
3. Remove the meat carefully
4. Cut into neat pieces
5. Arrange neatly in the shell
6. Decorate with aspic or mayonnaise
