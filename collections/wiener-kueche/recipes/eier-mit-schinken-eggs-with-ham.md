## Ingredients

- 6 fresh eggs
- 10 dkg rolled ham
- salt
- hot butter or fat
- parsley for garnish
- spinach or cooked lettuce for serving

## Directions

1. Scramble the eggs with salt and mix with chopped ham
2. In a loaf mold, add some hot butter or fat
3. Fill with some of the egg mixture
4. Let cook until set, then turn out onto a plate
5. Garnish with parsley
6. Serve with spinach or cooked lettuce
