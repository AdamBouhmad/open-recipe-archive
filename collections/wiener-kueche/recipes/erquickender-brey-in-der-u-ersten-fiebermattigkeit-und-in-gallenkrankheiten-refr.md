## Ingredients

- two drams of freshly pressed juice from mulberries, currants, or cherries, or in a season when these fruits are not fresh, two drams of pressed pap from these fruits
- egg yolk
- one scruple of lemon juice
- two drams of white wine
- finely rubbed bread and sugar

## Directions

1. Take two drams of freshly pressed juice from mulberries, currants, or cherries, or pressed pap if fresh fruit is unavailable
2. Add the egg yolk
3. Add one scruple of lemon juice
4. Add two drams of white wine
5. Add finely rubbed bread and sugar until it becomes a thin pap
