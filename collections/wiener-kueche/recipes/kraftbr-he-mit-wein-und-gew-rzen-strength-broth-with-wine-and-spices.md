## Ingredients

- one well-crushed capon
- twelve live crayfish
- half a pound of white bread, soaked in milk overnight
- two drams of small raisins (currants)
- twelve pieces each of bugloss berries and sebesten
- half a dram each of cardamom, cubeb, nutmeg flower, and cinnamon
- one handful each of endive and lettuce
- one measure of water
- one and a half seidel of malmsey wine

## Directions

1. Crush the capon well and prepare the crayfish
2. Soak the white bread in milk overnight
3. Crush all ingredients small
4. Place everything in a large tin bottle
5. Pour one measure of water and one and a half seidel of malmsey wine over it
6. Seal the bottle tightly
7. Set it in a large kettle with water
8. Let it cook for six hours
9. Press it through a cloth
10. Skim off the fat
11. Allow the remainder to become thick at a cool place
12. Each morning and evening, give several spoonfuls in thin meat broth or warm water
