## Ingredients

- 24 drams butter
- 28 drams brown sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- 30 drams flour
- 2 to 3 tablets chocolate
- butter for greasing the baking form

## Directions

1. Beat 24 drams butter until light and fluffy
2. Gradually add 28 drams brown sugar and 12 egg yolks and beat well
3. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 10 egg whites with 30 drams flour
4. Pour into a baking form greased with butter and dusted with flour
5. Soften 2 to 3 tablets of chocolate on a baking sheet so that a finger can be pressed in
6. Cut the chocolate into pieces the size of a small finger
7. When completely softened, insert these pieces into the raw batter in the form as desired
8. Bake slowly at moderate heat for approximately 1 hour
