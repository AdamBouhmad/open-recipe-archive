## Ingredients

- 1 head of red cabbage
- 8 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion
- 8-10 pieces of sugar
- salt
- caraway seeds
- vinegar
- water
- red wine
- 1 apple (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and stalks from cabbage, divide into quarters, wash, and cut into fine strips
2. In a casserole, roast 1 onion dark yellow in 8 dkg hot fat
3. Brown 8-10 sugar cubes in the fat
4. Add the cabbage, then salt, caraway seeds, vinegar, and enough water to cover
5. Stew covered for 2-2½ hours, stirring frequently
6. Season further if needed with salt and sugar
7. Sour with red wine
8. An apple cut into cubes may be stewed with it
