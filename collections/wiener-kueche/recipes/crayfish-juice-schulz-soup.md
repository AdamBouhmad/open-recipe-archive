## Ingredients

- 10 crayfish boiled in salt water
- 20 drams butter
- 4 drams toasted semolina slices
- 2 heads of onion
- 2 halves roasted or baked fish heads
- finely sliced white and yellow roots
- flour
- pea water
- salt
- pepper
- 12 drams rice cooked in milk

## Directions

1. Remove the gall from the boiled crayfish
2. Pound the crayfish and place in a casserole with butter
3. Add toasted semolina slices, onion heads, roasted or baked fish heads, finely sliced white and yellow roots, and flour
4. Steam everything until tender
5. Pour the steamed mixture with 2 liters of pea water
6. Bring to a boil, strain, and season with salt and pepper
7. Add 12 drams of rice cooked in milk to the soup
8. You can also add less rice and instead use toasted semolina slices
