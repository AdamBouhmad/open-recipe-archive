## Ingredients

- salt
- water
- artichokes

## Directions

1. Sprinkle salt on the bottom of a clean barrel, place the cut stems of the artichokes upright, reverse the following ones and position them with the tips between the tips of the lower ones, and continue until the barrel is full.
2. Have the cooper seal it after first boring a hole in the upper bottom.
3. Take a good portion of flowing water and salt, let it boil for quite a long time, and let it cool again, and pour it through the hole into the artichokes.
4. When the barrel is full, seal the hole firmly with a stopper, place it in the cellar, and shake it occasionally; this way they will keep for a very long time.
5. Alternatively, before placing them in the barrel, you may cook them in the manner of asparagus, but they must lie in the warm water for 2 hours and dry for the same length of time before being placed in the barrel.
