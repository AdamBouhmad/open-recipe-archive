## Ingredients

- fresh, peeled and finely chopped black truffles
- butter

## Directions

1. Sauté the fresh, peeled and finely chopped black truffles in butter
2. Pound them very finely in a mortar
3. Press them through a hair sieve
4. Mix them into the desired forcemeats
