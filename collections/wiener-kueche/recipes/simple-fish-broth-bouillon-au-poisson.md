## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams butter
- onion cut in thin slices
- 1 kg cleaned, opened, rinsed fish
- ¾ liter cold water
- 2 liters clear pea water

## Directions

1. Place butter and thinly sliced onion in a casserole, add the cleaned fish cut in pieces with the head divided in 4 parts
2. For those not adhering strictly to fasting time, add 16 decagrams thinly sliced beef or calf liver to the onion and roast covered until brown
3. When brown, pour ¾ liter cold water over and braise covered carefully so it does not scorch
4. Pour the brown mixture with 2 liters clear pea water and simmer slowly about 2 hours
5. Remove fat, strain soup through fine sieve and use as desired
