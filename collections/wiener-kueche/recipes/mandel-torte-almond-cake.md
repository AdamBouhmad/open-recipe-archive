## Ingredients

- 24 Deca orange or vanilla sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 30 Deca peeled almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 8 egg whites
- 8 Deca fine flour
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the egg yolks for 1/3 hour
2. Add the almonds (which have been peeled and finely ground with 4 egg whites) and mix well
3. After stirring for another 1/4 hour, lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 8 Deca fine flour
4. Fill a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour with this mixture
5. Bake at low heat for 3/4 to 1 hour in the oven
6. After this time, turn the cake out onto a sieve and let it cool
7. Once cooled, place the cake on a sheet of writing paper so that the former bottom side faces up
8. Cover the cake with glaze of your choice and only serve to table once completely dry
9. For the glaze: beat the stiffly whipped snow from 2 egg whites with 3 tablespoons of strained strawberries or raspberries and 10 Deca sugar for 1/2 hour
10. Apply to the cake, distribute evenly with a knife (dipped several times in hot water), dust with finely ground sugar, and dry in the overfilled open oven for approximately 10 to 15 minutes
