## Ingredients

- breadcrumbs
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- candied lemon zest
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak breadcrumbs in milk for about 2 hours until they absorb the liquid well
2. Press through a cloth
3. Add salt, sugar, and finely chopped candied lemon zest
4. Add egg yolks and beaten egg whites and mix together
5. Pour into a casserole well-coated with butter
6. Bake in the oven or under a cake cover with heat from above and below
7. Finally turn the cake out onto a serving dish
8. Glaze with a hot shovel
9. Adjust the quantity of breadcrumbs and eggs according to the number of people to be served
