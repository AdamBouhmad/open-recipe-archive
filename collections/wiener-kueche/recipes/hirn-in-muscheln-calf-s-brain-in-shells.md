## Ingredients

- 1 calf's brain
- 5 mushrooms
- 3 dkg Parmesan
- 2 anchovies
- several spoonfuls white wine
- 6 dkg hot butter
- 1 finely chopped onion or shallots
- parsley
- 1½ trimmed, water-soaked, squeezed rolls
- egg
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Wash the brain and remove the membrane.
2. In hot butter, let finely chopped onion or shallots, parsley, and cleaned mushrooms come to a boil.
3. Add the trimmed, water-soaked, squeezed rolls and the brain (blanched or unblanched).
4. Braise for a few minutes with the addition of wine and salt to a thick paste.
5. Fill this into buttered shells.
6. Pour a little beaten egg over it.
7. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
8. Top with anchovy strips (these can also be omitted).
9. Place the shells on a sheet and let them gratinate in the oven for 10-15 minutes.
10. Serve the shells on a dish or cup with a folded napkin.
