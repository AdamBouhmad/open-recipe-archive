## Ingredients

- 3 Loth sugar
- Peel of one lemon
- One Obertaſſe water
- 8 Obertaſſen water
- One Vierding sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take 3 Loth sugar, the peel of one lemon, and one Obertaſſe water in a casserole
2. Let it reduce to caramel in the casserole until it becomes beautifully brown
3. Pour in 8 Obertaſſen of water and add one Vierding sugar
4. After bringing all to a good boil, let it cool
5. Stir 6 egg yolks well, pour in the cold mixture, and pass it a couple of times through a fine sieve
6. Fill into cups, place them in a casserole full of boiling water, and cover with a lid with coals on top
7. The cream will be ready in ten minutes and is served cold
