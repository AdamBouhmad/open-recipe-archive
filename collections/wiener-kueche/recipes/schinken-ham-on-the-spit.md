## Ingredients

- Ham
- White wine
- Breadcrumbs, fine
- Green parsley
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Remove the rind from the ham and clean it well below
2. Soak it in lukewarm water
3. Pour one measure of white wine over it and let it soak for ten to twelve hours, or overnight until roasting
4. Thread it onto a spit and wrap it with paper
5. Pour the wine it was soaked in into the roasting pan and baste it frequently during roasting
6. When it is almost tender enough, remove the paper
7. Sprinkle it with fine breadcrumbs and green parsley and let it brown nicely
8. Remove it from the spit, let it cool, arrange it on a white napkin placed over the dish, and garnish with green parsley
