## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams flour
- 4 decagrams butter
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- 1 spoonful milk
- 1/2 calf brain

## Directions

1. Rub together 16 decagrams flour with 4 decagrams butter
2. Add 1 egg yolk, a pinch of salt, and 1 spoonful milk; knead into dough
3. Let rest, then roll out thinly
4. Cut out rounds
5. Place filling in the center of each round
6. Brush the edges with egg white
7. Press the dough together over the filling
8. Place on an unfloured baking sheet
9. Fry in hot lard until golden
10. Drain and serve warm with cooked lettuce or spinach
11. For the filling: Wash the brain, remove the membrane, blanch it. In 3 decagrams hot butter, let chopped parsley begin to brown, add the brain, season with salt and pepper, let it stew for a few minutes while stirring. When cooled, fill the pastries. If the brain is too thin, thicken it with flour.
12. Serve on a flat plate on parchment paper, garnished with parsley
