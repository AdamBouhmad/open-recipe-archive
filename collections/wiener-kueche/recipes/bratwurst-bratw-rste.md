## Ingredients

- 50-60 dkg bratwurst
- fat
- potato hash

## Directions

1. Place 50-60 dkg bratwurst in a saucepan with not too hot fat
2. Fry slowly brown on both sides for 10-15 minutes
3. Serve with potato hash
