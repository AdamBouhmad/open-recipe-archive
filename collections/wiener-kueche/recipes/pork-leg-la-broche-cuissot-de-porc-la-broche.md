## Ingredients

- pork leg from a young pig
- salt
- root vegetables, sliced thin
- onions
- shallots
- pepper
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Choose the pork leg from a young pig, remove the thick skin (rind) and salt it well
2. Place thin-sliced root vegetables, onions, shallots and some pepper in a casserole, lay the salted leg on top and let it marinate for about 3 days, turning it frequently
3. After this time, thread it on a spit, wrap it with double-layered paper well coated with butter, tie it with twine and roast slowly for 1½ to 2 hours
4. Half an hour before the end of roasting, remove the paper and roast the meat over high heat so that it takes on a golden-brown color
5. Remove from the spit, cut into desired pieces, arrange on a platter and serve with onion purée or desired vegetables or salad
