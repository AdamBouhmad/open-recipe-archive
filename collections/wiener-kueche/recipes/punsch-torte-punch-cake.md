## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- sugar
- water
- rum or punch
- lemon juice
- 10 sugar cubes
- jam

## Directions

1. Prepare a biscuit torte
2. Cut it apart the next day and fill it
3. If you have bakery or torte scraps, use them for the filling; or make a torte from half of the chocolate torte batter
4. In a small pot, cook several tablespoons of water, the same amount of rum or punch, lemon juice, and 10 sugar cubes for a few minutes
5. Pour over the scraps or chocolate torte, stir into a paste, and use as filling
6. Frost the torte red
