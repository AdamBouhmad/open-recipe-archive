## Ingredients

- Orange-Frozen (No. 1753)
- 1/2 liter Champagne wine
- 1/8 liter Rum or Arrack

## Directions

1. Prepare an Orange-Frozen (No. 1753) and then fold it well with 1/2 liter Champagne wine and 1/8 liter Rum or Arrack.
2. Serve it in glasses.
