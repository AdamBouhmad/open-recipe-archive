## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- coulis
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- Madeira wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and scrape them well
2. Grill them
3. When they become reddish, put them in a casserole with some coulis, a little bouillon, salt, pepper, and a small glass of Madeira wine
4. Press the juice from half a lemon into it
5. Let the ragout cook for half an hour
6. Arrange it then
