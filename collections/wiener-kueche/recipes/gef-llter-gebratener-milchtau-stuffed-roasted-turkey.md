## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned and plucked turkey
- Same stuffing as used for 'gefüllte Halbsbrust' (recipe 611)
- 1 piece of bread roll or 1 roasted chestnut
- Butter
- Paper

## Directions

1. Let the plucked and cleaned turkey rest for 1 hour after salting.
2. Prepare the same stuffing as specified for 'gefüllte Halbsbrust' (recipe 611).
3. Fill the crop skin with this stuffing up to the thighs and close the crop opening with a piece of bread roll or a roasted chestnut.
4. Truss the stuffed turkey, place it on a spit, wrap in buttered paper, and roast slowly.
5. After approximately 1 3/4 hours, remove the paper and roast the turkey at bright fire until brown and crispy.
6. Alternatively, place the stuffed turkey in a pan with butter and roast it in the oven, basting frequently with the pan drippings.
7. Cut the roasted turkey into desired pieces, reassemble them, place in a bowl, and serve with salad or compote of choice.
