## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- hot butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Break macaroni into smaller pieces
2. Add to boiling salted water and cook covered until soft
3. Drain in a colander and rinse with cold water
4. In a casserole with hot butter, add the drained macaroni
5. Stir well together and arrange hot on a platter
6. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese
