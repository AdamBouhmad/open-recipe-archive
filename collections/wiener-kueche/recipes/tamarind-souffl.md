## Ingredients

- 8 Deka semolina
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cold milk
- 16 Deka powdered sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites
- butter for coating the bowl

## Directions

1. Put 8 Deka semolina, 4 egg yolks, and 1/4 liter cold milk in a snow bowl and beat with a whisk at low heat until it forms a thick mass.
2. Stir in 16 Deka butter well, pour it into a deep bowl and let it cool.
3. Beat 16 Deka powdered sugar and 5 egg yolks for 1/2 hour, then stir into the cooled semolina mass.
4. Lightly fold in the snow of 5 egg whites.
5. Line a bowl brushed with butter with the mixture and bake for 3/4 hour at low heat in the oven.
6. Remove the bowl from the oven, turn it out onto a second bowl, sprinkle the cake with finely ground sugar, and serve immediately.
