## Ingredients

- 1 veal knuckle
- salt
- lard in pencil-thick, half-finger-length pieces
- lard, thinly sliced
- 12 deca of raw ham, as desired
- soup stock
- butter or Béarnaise sauce

## Directions

1. After removing the skin from the veal knuckle, salt it and thread it with pieces of lard
2. Place thinly sliced lard and as much raw ham as desired in a casserole, lay the knuckle on top, pour some soup stock over it, and let it braise gently covered
3. Remove the lard from the cooking liquid, then bind a lighter butter or Béarnaise sauce with the cooking liquid
