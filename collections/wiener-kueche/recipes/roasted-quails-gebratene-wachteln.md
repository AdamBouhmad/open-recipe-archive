## Ingredients

- Quails
- Salt
- Grape vine leaves, cut
- Thin bacon slices
- String
- Defatted cooking liquid

## Directions

1. After plucking, singeing, and cleaning the quails with a cloth
2. Sprinkle inside and outside with salt
3. Wrap with cut grape vine leaves and thin bacon slices
4. Bind with string
5. Shortly before serving, roast on a spit for approximately 10-15 minutes over strong bright fire
6. Remove from spit, cut the string and remove it
7. Arrange the quails on a plate with the grape vine leaves and bacon
8. Serve with defatted cooking liquid
9. Alternatively, the quails can be roasted in a pan over strong heat with frequent basting in the oven
10. Or the quails can be placed on a spit with 1 semolina slice and bacon between each quail
