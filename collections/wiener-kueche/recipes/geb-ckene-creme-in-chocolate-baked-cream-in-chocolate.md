## Ingredients

- cream mass from baked cream in crusts recipe
- wafer cookies
- water
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying
- grated chocolate mixed with sugar

## Directions

1. Cut pieces from the steamed cream mass using a round cutter
2. Wrap in moistened wafer cookies
3. Dip in beaten eggs
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. Fry in hot fat
6. Roll in grated chocolate mixed with sugar
