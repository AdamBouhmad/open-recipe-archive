## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deca rind-removed, finely cut rolls
- milk
- parsley
- 1 champignon
- butter for roasting
- 16 deca roasted, finely cut meat
- salt
- nutmeg
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter until fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
3. Mix in 16 deca rind-removed finely cut rolls soaked in milk
4. Add some finely cut parsley and 1 champignon roasted in butter
5. Add 16 deca roasted finely cut meat, some salt, and nutmeg
6. Mix everything well together
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
8. Cook in steam or bake
