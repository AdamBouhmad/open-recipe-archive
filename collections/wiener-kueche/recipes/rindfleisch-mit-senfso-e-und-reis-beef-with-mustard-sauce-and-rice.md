## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg beef (Rieddeckel)
- 1 pair sausages
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 finely chopped onion
- a few pieces of bread cut into noodle shapes
- strained beef broth
- 5 dkg fat
- 1¼ tablespoons French mustard
- a few tablespoons cream
- salt

## Directions

1. 20 minutes before serving, in a saucepan heat 6 dkg fat and roast 1 finely chopped onion and a few pieces of bread cut into noodle shapes
2. Pour the strained beef broth over this
3. Add the sausages which have been washed and cut into leaf-like pieces and cook together for a few minutes
4. Optionally whisk in 1 egg, but the soup must not boil after this
5. For the sauce: Make a roux from 5 dkg fat and mix with broth or water
6. Add 1¼ tablespoons French mustard and a few tablespoons cream
7. Season with salt and cook well
