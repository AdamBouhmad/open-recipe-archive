## Ingredients

- rissole dough
- any stewed fruit
- sugar for dusting
- optional: beaten egg whites, egg, and breadcrumbs for alternate filling

## Directions

1. Prepare rissole dough as in the recipe for Rissoles mit Farce
2. Fill with any desired stewed fruit
3. Cut out and fry as described in the basic rissole recipe
4. Dust with sugar and serve as a dessert
5. Alternative filling: use beaten egg whites, egg, and breadcrumbs, using 6 ounces of each
