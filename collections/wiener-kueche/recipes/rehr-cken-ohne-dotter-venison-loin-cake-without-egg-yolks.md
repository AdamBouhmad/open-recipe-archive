## Ingredients

- 14 dkg butter
- 12 dkg sugar
- 12 dkg softened chocolate
- 5 stiff egg whites
- 12 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Cream together 14 dkg butter
2. Gradually stir in 12 dkg sugar
3. Mix in 12 dkg softened chocolate and 5 stiff egg whites together with 12 dkg flour
4. Fill the batter into a buttered, floured form and bake and finish as the previous recipe
