## Ingredients

- small trout
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- caraway
- onion
- clove

## Directions

1. Kill and clean the trout as described for blue boiled trout
2. Wrap the cleaned trout in a clean cloth dipped in the water in which they lie, wringing it out so no air can enter
3. In a fish kettle, add 1½ liters water, ½ liter vinegar, 1 tablespoon salt, ¾ teaspoon caraway, and a small piece of onion spiked with 1 clove
4. Boil this mixture for approximately ¼ hour
5. Pour the broth through a sieve over the boiling hot trout, which should be in a fish kettle or flat pan
6. Proceed as with the blue boiled trout
7. The trout will have a very pleasant flavor, though not as beautiful a blue color as the blue boiled trout
8. The broth should cover the trout only just barely
9. Can be served with a white wine sauce or with champagne instead of vinegar, or simply in salt water and caraway or white wine
