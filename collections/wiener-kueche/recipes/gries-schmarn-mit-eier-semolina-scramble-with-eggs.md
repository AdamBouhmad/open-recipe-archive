## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter Gries (semolina)
- 1/2 liter Milch (milk)
- 4 Eidotter (egg yolks)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (finely ground)
- 1/4 liter cream
- 24 deka butter or lard
- 1/8 liter boiling milk (for finishing)
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 liter Gries into a deep bowl
2. While stirring constantly, add 1/2 liter milk
3. Mix in egg yolks gradually, combined with finely ground salt
4. Mix in 1/4 liter cream thoroughly
5. Cover and let stand for 2 hours until the semolina has swollen well
6. Heat 24 deka of butter or lard in a pan until hot
7. Add the Gries mixture and let it steam in the oven for 1 hour
8. A few minutes before serving, pour 1/8 liter of boiling milk over it
9. Stir well and let it steam a little more
10. Arrange the Schmarn in a bowl and serve at table sprinkled with sugar
