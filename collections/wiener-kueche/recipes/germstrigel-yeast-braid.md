## Ingredients

- ½ kg flour
- 8 dkg butter, cut into pieces
- a pinch of salt
- 2-3 spoonfuls sugar
- 1 egg
- yeast leaven from 2 dkg yeast
- some spoonfuls lukewarm milk
- 5 dkg currants
- beaten egg for brushing
- swollen, chopped almonds or pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a mixing bowl, rub in 8 dkg cut butter
2. Add a pinch of salt, 2-3 spoonfuls sugar, 1 egg, yeast leaven from 2 dkg yeast, and some spoonfuls lukewarm milk
3. Mix the dough and beat it well
4. On the board, knead it for 20-30 minutes until quite fine
5. Gradually knead in 5 dkg currants
6. Divide the dough into 9 parts: 4 large, 3 medium, and 2 small
7. Form the 4 large parts into fairly long rolls and braid them together
8. Place on greased baking sheet
9. Form the remaining parts correspondingly smaller, roll and braid them
10. Press the four-strand braid in the middle, place the three-strand braid on top, press again, and place the two-strand braid over it
11. Let the braid rise near the oven
12. Brush with beaten egg
13. Sprinkle with swollen, chopped almonds or pearl sugar
14. Bake in weaker heat until light brown
