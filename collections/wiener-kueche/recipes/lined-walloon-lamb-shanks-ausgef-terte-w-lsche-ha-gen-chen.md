## Ingredients

- Lamb shanks
- Truffles
- Poultry livers
- Calf sweetbreads
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Mushrooms, finely chopped
- Shallots
- Onions
- Basil
- Scraped bacon
- Egg yolks
- Bacon strips
- Sauce italienne

## Directions

1. From several lamb shanks, separate the bones leaving a short piece attached
2. Prepare a good salpicon from truffles, poultry livers, and calf sweetbreads
3. Add salt, pepper, spices, finely chopped mushrooms, shallots, onions, basil, and scraped bacon to the salpicon
4. Stir in a couple of egg yolks
5. Fill the shanks with this mixture and sew them closed, maintaining their shape
6. Cover them with bacon strips
7. Cook them in a strong but short braise until tender, and they should have a beautiful white appearance
8. When serving, place sauce italienne underneath
