## Ingredients

- 80 drachms sugar in small pieces
- 1 liter water
- juice of 5 lemons
- 1/4 liter rum or brandy
- 1/2 liter Champagne

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place 80 drachms sugar in small pieces and 1 liter water
2. Let this boil, then add the juice of 5 lemons, 1/4 liter rum or brandy and 1/2 liter Champagne
3. Let it become hot, strain the punch through a sieve into a soup pot or tureen and serve it hot
