## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- fine skin removed
- meat beaten
- very fine bacon
- butter
- finely sliced root vegetables
- mushrooms
- peas
- spices
- white stock
- brown sauce
- dissolved meat glaze
- fine vegetables or puree or ragout or pasta

## Directions

1. Remove chicken breasts as indicated for braised chicken breasts, remove the fine skin, beat the meat and lard it with very fine bacon (spitchcock)
2. In a casserole lined with butter, finely sliced root vegetables, mushrooms, peas and spices, place the larded breasts
3. Let them braise covered briefly
4. Pour white stock over them and braise covered until tender
5. Remove them and place in a shallow casserole containing some brown sauce
6. Braise them in the oven until the bacon has a golden color
7. Brush the breasts with dissolved meat glaze and arrange them in a circle on a platter
8. Pour the remaining juices over the breasts and place fine vegetables, puree, ragout or pasta in the center of the platter
9. Serve sauce separately
