## Ingredients

- same as Chocolate Glaze (No. 1556)
- 4 drams of butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the 'Chocolate Glaze' (No. 1556), with the difference that about 4 drams of butter are allowed to boil with it.
2. This glaze is only suitable for use if the cake is eaten the same day, because over time it becomes dull from the butter.
