## Ingredients

- 10 deca butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 60 deca strained curd cheese
- Salt
- 1/2 liter flour
- 12 deca white breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 10 deca of butter until fluffy.
2. Add 5 whole eggs, 60 deca of strained curd cheese, some salt, 1/2 liter of flour, and 12 deca of white breadcrumbs, one after another, and stir well.
3. Form medium-sized dumplings from this dough and treat them as directed in the recipe for curd cheese dumplings with semolina.
