## Ingredients

- rail hens
- brine/marinade
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the rail hens
2. Let them soak in water for one hour, then place them in brine for one day
3. Before putting them on the spit, salt and dress them, then roast them well
4. During roasting, baste them several times with the brine, and finally with cream and butter
5. When roasted, serve them on the table with the sauce poured over them
