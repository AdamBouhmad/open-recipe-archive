## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg loin chops, thickly sliced
- 2 anchovies
- Salt
- Pepper
- 6 dkg fat
- 1 large finely chopped onion
- 1 small teaspoon paprika
- Cold water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove skin from meat
2. Cut first into strips, then into diagonal cubes
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Heat 6 dkg fat in a casserole
5. Toast 1 large finely chopped onion
6. Add 1 small teaspoon paprika
7. Deglaze with a splash of cold water
8. Add the meat
9. Pour in some vinegar and water
10. Cover and braise for 2 hours, adding water occasionally
11. After 30 minutes, add the boned and finely chopped anchovies
12. Let braise together
13. Serve on a round or rectangular platter
