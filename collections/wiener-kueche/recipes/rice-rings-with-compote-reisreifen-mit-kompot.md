## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter milk
- 16 tablespoons washed rice
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons orange sugar
- 1 pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Bring 0.5 liter of milk to a boil
2. Stir in 16 tablespoons of washed rice under constant stirring and cook covered until soft and thick
3. Add 4 tablespoons butter, 8 tablespoons orange sugar, and 1 pinch of salt
4. Stir well together
5. When the rice is fairly thick, mound it on a serving dish
6. Press a round spoon into the center and use a tablespoon to form an even ring
7. Fill the center with applesauce or compote as desired
