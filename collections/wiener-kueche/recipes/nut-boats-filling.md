## Ingredients

- 10 dkg grated nuts
- 5 dkg sugar
- 2 dkg fine breadcrumbs
- 2 spoons honey
- pinch cinnamon
- 3 dkg grated chocolate
- 2-3 spoons hot milk

## Directions

1. Mix 10 dkg grated nuts, 5 dkg sugar, 2 dkg fine breadcrumbs, 2 spoons honey, a pinch of cinnamon, 3 dkg grated chocolate and 2-3 spoons hot milk to a thick porridge
