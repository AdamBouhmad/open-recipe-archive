## Ingredients

- 2 dl milk
- 4 dkg butter
- salt
- 6 dkg semolina
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Bring 2 dl milk to a boil with 4 dkg butter and salt.
2. Stir in 6 dkg semolina spoonful by spoonful to make a thick mixture and let cool.
3. Fold in 2 egg yolks and 2 beaten egg whites.
4. Fill the mixture into a consommé mold.
5. Let it set in a steamer for 30-45 minutes.
6. Loosen, turn out, cut into pieces, and serve warm.
