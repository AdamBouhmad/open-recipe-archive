## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 1/2 measure cream
- 6 tablespoons fine flour
- 1 pound raw kidney fat
- 1 pound large raisins
- 1 pound small raisins
- 2 loths citron
- 1 pound grated sugar
- 1 glass brandy

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg yolks and 6 egg whites together in half a measure of cream.
2. Take 6 tablespoons fine flour, 1 pound raw kidney fat, 1 pound large raisins (freed from pits), and 1 pound small raisins (carefully washed).
3. Add 2 loths citron, 1 pound grated sugar, and 1 glass brandy to the mass.
4. When everything is well stirred together, cook it as already described.
