## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- crayfish forcemeat
- crayfish tails, cut
- egg yolk
- crayfish butter
- crayfish ragout

## Directions

1. Place small pastry cases made from puff pastry in small molds, about the size of a walnut
2. Fill each with crayfish forcemeat and some cut crayfish tails
3. Brush with egg yolk and cover with a lid
4. Bake brushed with crayfish butter until nicely golden in the oven
5. Remove the lid and score the forcemeat
6. Pour good crayfish ragout into each pastry
7. Replace the lids and serve warm
