## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar
- 1/4 liter very ripe, picked, cleaned barberries
- 1 finger-length piece of lemon peel
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- 1/2 liter white wine

## Directions

1. Clarify 40 decagrams sugar and add to it the barberries and lemon peel
2. Bring to a boil and strain through cloth
3. Pour the strained liquid to 7 decagrams of hot dissolved isinglass
4. Boil together for 10 minutes
5. Strain immediately through fine, thin linen cloth into a deep bowl
6. Add 1/2 liter white wine to this liquid, so that 1 liter of liquid results in total
7. Stir and proceed further as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
