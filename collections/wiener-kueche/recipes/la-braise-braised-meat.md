## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lean ham, cut into pieces
- 1 pound good kidney fat, cut into pieces
- 1 measure fatty bouillon
- one bay leaf
- some cloves
- pepper
- mace
- several large onions
- celery
- yellow turnips
- one bundle parsley
- 2 pounds bacon

## Directions

1. Place the ham, kidney fat, bouillon, bay leaf, cloves, pepper, mace, onions, celery, yellow turnips, and parsley bundle on the fire
2. Cut the bacon into broad slices to cover the piece of meat or poultry to be prepared
3. Use a casserole with a very tight-fitting lid for the braise
4. Place glowing coals on top of the lid and beneath it so that it cooks as little as possible rapidly
5. Even if it only bubbles occasionally, the meat, which should not be too soft anyway, will certainly become much more tender than if it cooked rapidly
6. Note that the piece of meat or whatever it may be that is to provide this braise must fill the casserole exactly, which must not be too large
