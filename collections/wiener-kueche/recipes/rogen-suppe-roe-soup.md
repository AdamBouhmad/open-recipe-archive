## Ingredients

- cooked roe
- pea broth
- pepper
- ginger
- nutmeg
- butter
- bread, cubed and toasted in butter

## Directions

1. Stir together the cooked roe
2. Let it come to a boil in strained pea broth
3. Add a little pepper, ginger, nutmeg and a small piece of butter
4. Serve the soup over bread that has been cubed and toasted in butter
