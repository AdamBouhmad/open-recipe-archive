## Ingredients

- 10 deca sugar
- 2 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 16 deca very finely ground, sifted hazelnut cream

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and stiffly beaten egg whites for 1.5 hours
2. Add finely ground hazelnut cream and mix well
3. On baking sheet lined with wafers, place walnut-sized pieces at 3 finger-widths apart
4. Press indentation in center with spoon handle and spread mixture around edge to form small wreaths
5. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar
6. Dry in well-heated oven
7. Remove small wreaths from wafers
8. Before serving, fill center indentation as desired with jam or small pieces of pineapple, citron, and the like
