## Ingredients

- less beautiful goose livers
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parchment paper

## Directions

1. Use less beautiful goose livers for this dish
2. Cut them into oblong strips, half finger thick, 3 fingers wide
3. Butter a flat glazed casserole well, arrange the liver pieces in it at intervals, dust with some salt and pepper, and cover with buttered parchment paper
4. 10 minutes before serving, place the casserole on the fire and move it lightly for several minutes, then turn each liver slice piece by piece, move them over the heat again for the same length of time
