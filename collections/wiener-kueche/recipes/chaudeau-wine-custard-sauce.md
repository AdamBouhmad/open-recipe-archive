## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter white or red wine
- 12 egg yolks
- 24 deca sugar

## Directions

1. 1/4 hour before serving, pour 1/2 liter white or red wine into a snow kettle
2. Beat in 12 egg yolks
3. Add 24 deca sugar
4. Whip over heat with a whisk until the mass rises and becomes thick, but do not boil
5. Use immediately
6. For red chaudeau, stir in a few drops of almond juice shortly before serving
7. Alternatively, use a tall, narrow pot over heat where the mass bubbles
