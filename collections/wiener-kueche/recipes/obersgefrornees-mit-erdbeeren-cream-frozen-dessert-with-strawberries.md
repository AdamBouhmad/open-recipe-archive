## Ingredients

- 50 deca sugar
- 1/2 liter strained strawberry puree
- 1 liter cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve 50 deca sugar in 1 liter cold water
2. Stir 1/2 liter strained strawberry puree well into it
3. Freeze as Vanille-Gefrornees (No. 1720)
4. Meanwhile beat 1 liter cream to foam
5. Add this foam to the frozen strawberries in the box
6. Mix well with the mixture and distribute it, so it does not become grainy
7. Let freeze completely covered
