## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 7 dkg butter
- 7 dkg raw grated almonds
- 7 dkg sugar
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- 1 crushed clove
- 1 spoon milk

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, butter, raw grated almonds, sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice, cinnamon, crushed clove and milk on the board to a dough
2. After resting, roll it out thin
3. Cut it into rectangles (like cards)
4. Place the cards on an unbuttered baking sheet
5. Brush them with beaten egg
6. Top them with halved soaked almonds
7. Bake until light brown
