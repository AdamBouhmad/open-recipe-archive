## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- Flour
- 1 liter simmering milk
- 12 Deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- Snow from 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 3 eggs and flour and cut noodles from it
2. Cook the noodles in 1 liter simmering milk and let them cook through
3. Beat 12 Deca butter until fluffy and beat in 8 egg yolks
4. Add the prepared noodles, stir well together and lightly mix in the snow from 8 eggs
5. Further handling is as with Griesschöberl
6. Instead of noodles, one can also cut leeks
