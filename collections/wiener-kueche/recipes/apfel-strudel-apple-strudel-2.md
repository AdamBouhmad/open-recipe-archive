## Ingredients

- ½ liter flour
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- peeled apples
- raisins
- currants
- sugar
- cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from ½ liter flour for baking
2. After stretching it finely, sprinkle with breadcrumbs toasted in butter
3. Lay peeled apples cut into thin slices on top
4. Sprinkle with selected raisins, currants, and finely crushed sugar, and optionally with cinnamon
5. Treat further as described in the Ham Strudel recipe
6. Cut into desired pieces, arrange on a serving dish, and serve sprinkled with sugar
