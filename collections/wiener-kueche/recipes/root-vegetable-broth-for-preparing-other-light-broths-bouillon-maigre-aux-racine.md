## Ingredients

- 2 pieces yellow turnips
- 3 pieces onion
- 2 leeks
- 1 head celery
- 32 decagrams butter
- ½ liter dried peas
- sorrel
- chervil
- parsley
- 3 liters cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the turnips, onion, leeks, and celery very well, cut all thinly and place in a casserole with 32 decagrams butter
2. Roast until it has a golden yellow color
3. Add ½ liter dried peas with some sorrel, chervil, and parsley, pour with 3 liters cold water
4. Simmer slowly about 2 hours
5. Let stand covered about ½ hour but do not boil further
6. The soup will clarify from the quiet standing
7. Strain through sieve into an earthenware pot and set aside for further use
