## Ingredients

- one barrel (half ohm) of good and strong French white wine
- 30 pounds yellow cane sugar
- 16 pounds large raisins, freed from stems and torn by hand

## Directions

1. Fill the barrel not quite full with good and strong French white wine
2. Add the yellow cane sugar and the large raisins (which must be of superior quality; small raisins give a particularly good Hungarian flavor)
3. Once the sugar has dissolved and everything is well shaken together, in summer bring the wine to the ground where the hottest days are most suitable, but in winter place it in a heated room of 13-14 degrees Reaumur
