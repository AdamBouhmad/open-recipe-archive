## Ingredients

- pork leg from a young pig
- salt
- caraway
- fat
- broth

## Directions

1. Salt the pork leg well with the skin (rind) attached and rub it with caraway
2. Place it in a roasting pan containing fat
3. Cover the meat with melted fat and roast it in the oven at moderate heat for about 2 hours, basting frequently with the juices that accumulate
4. Remove the meat, cut it into desired pieces, arrange on a platter and serve the sauce separately in a sauceboat
5. The sauce can be soured and the fat removed as desired
