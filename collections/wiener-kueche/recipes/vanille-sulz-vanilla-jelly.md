## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar, clarified (see recipe 1542)
- 1 vanilla bean cut into pieces
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Cherry spirit or maraschino, as much as needed
- Total liquid of 1 liter with dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Clarify 40 decagrams sugar (see recipe 1542) and add the vanilla bean pieces at the same time
2. After straining the clarified sugar through cloth, add it hot to 7 decagrams boiling isinglass
3. Mix both, strain the mass through fine, thin linen cloth
4. Add the clear juice of 2 lemons and as much cherry spirit or maraschino so that the entire mass with the dissolved isinglass does not exceed 1 liter
5. Proceed further as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
