## Ingredients

- ½ kg Kipfler potatoes
- 10 h mixed greens (lamb's lettuce or chicory)
- 4 Kiel sprats
- 1 milk herring
- 1 smoked herring
- 8 anchovies
- 1 piece of eel
- 4 dkg salmon
- 2 lemons
- 2 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the sprats and anchovies; cut off heads.
2. Remove heads from herring and smoked herring; also remove the backbone, wash inside and out, and cut into pieces.
3. Boil the potatoes and arrange them in a salad.
4. On a glass platter, pile the potato salad in a mountain shape.
5. Around it arrange alternately the greens, hard-boiled egg slices and lemon slices (garnished with caviar if desired), cut eel, herring, and smoked herring.
6. Place the sprats and other small fish crosswise on top of the potato salad.
7. Top with a lemon basket, garnished with caviar if desired.
8. The potato salad may be omitted and the platter filled only with fish, though more fish will be needed.
