## Ingredients

- Slices of bread or rolls
- 1 liter boiling water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Toast some slices of bread or rolls in the oven or before the fire until brown.
2. Put them in a pot, pour 1 liter of boiling water over them, and let it cool uncovered.
3. It is strained and drunk cold with or without sugar.
