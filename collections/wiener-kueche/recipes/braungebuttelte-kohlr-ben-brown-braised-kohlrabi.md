## Ingredients

- 8 large kohlrabi
- Salt water
- 8 ounces beef fat
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Onion, finely cut
- Soup, enough to cover the kohlrabi
- Butter sauce or white soup with some sugar
- Kohlrabi leaves
- Cold water
- Meat for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi, cut them into cubes, and boil them half-tender in salt water
2. In 8 ounces of beef fat, brown 1 tablespoon of flour, then brown some finely cut onion in it
3. Add the drained kohlrabi and pour enough soup over them so they are covered
4. When the kohlrabi are tender, add some butter sauce or white soup with sugar
5. Boil the kohlrabi leaves separately in salt water without covering them, then place in cold water and press well
6. Cut the leaves finely and mix them with the kohlrabi
7. Garnish with meat of your choice
