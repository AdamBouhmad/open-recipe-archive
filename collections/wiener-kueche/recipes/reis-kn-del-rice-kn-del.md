## Ingredients

- 18 deca rice
- 1/2 liter milk
- 12 deca butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 deca bread crumbs
- some salt
- 1/16 liter cooked green peas
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in warm water.
2. Boil it in 1/2 liter milk until swollen and let it cool.
3. Beat the butter until creamy.
4. Stir in 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks.
5. Add the cooled rice with 8 deca peeled bread crumbs cut into dice and soaked in milk, and salt.
6. When ready to cook, add 1/16 liter fine cooked green peas and 2 tablespoons flour.
7. Form knödel of any desired size.
