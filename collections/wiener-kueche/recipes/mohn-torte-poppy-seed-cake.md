## Ingredients

- 18 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 16 dkg grated poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Beat 18 dkg sugar with 6 egg yolks well
2. Fold in 6 stiff egg whites together with 16 dkg grated poppy seeds
3. Fill into the form and bake slowly
4. Cool, dust with sugar or frost with white glaze
