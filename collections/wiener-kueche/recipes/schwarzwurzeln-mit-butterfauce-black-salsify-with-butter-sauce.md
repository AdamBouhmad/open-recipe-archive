## Ingredients

- black salsify (scorzonera)
- cold water
- flour
- vinegar
- bouillon
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Once the black salsify is cleaned, cut it into finger-length pieces
2. Immediately throw them into cold water with a little flour and some drops of vinegar
3. Set a casserole with bouillon and some butter on coal fire
4. Lay the dry roots in it
5. Add pepper and salt
6. Let them cook until they are soft
7. Then make a butter sauce with lemon juice over them
