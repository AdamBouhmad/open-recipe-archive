## Ingredients

- About 1.5 kilos well-browned fish
- Some pieces of dark bread
- Some marrow roasted in fat
- Parsley and celery
- 2 liters pea water or water

## Directions

1. Pound the browned fish, dark bread, roasted marrow, parsley, and celery finely together
2. Pour in 2 liters pea water or water
3. Simmer well and let brown
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Simmer a bit more
6. Garnish with boiled bread slices
