## Ingredients

- a pair of veal feet
- a piece of ham
- cloves
- pepper
- mace
- root vegetables and herbs uncut
- one bundle parsley
- a glass of white wine
- some vinegar

## Directions

1. This is almost the same as what one understands by braise, except that for a daube, if it is to become a strong aspic which is given to cold pastries and similar dishes, one also adds a pair of veal feet, a piece of ham, cloves, pepper, mace, root vegetables and a bundle of parsley (all uncut), a glass of white wine, and some vinegar
