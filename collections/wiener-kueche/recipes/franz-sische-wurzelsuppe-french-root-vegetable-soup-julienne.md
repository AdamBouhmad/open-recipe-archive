## Ingredients

- 1 piece white turnip
- 1 dark and 1 light yellow turnip
- 1 piece parsnip
- 1 piece celery root
- 2 carrots
- 1 small head celery
- 1 piece Spanish onion
- 2 mushrooms
- 2.5 liters beef broth
- salt and spices as needed

## Directions

1. Peel and wash all vegetables, then cut them finely into small pieces.
2. Let them cook gently in 2.5 liters of beef broth.
3. Once salt and spices have been added, serve the soup with roasted bread roll or braised rice, or Pasch dumplings.
4. If desired, 4 to 6 tablespoons of cooked butter sauce can be stirred into this soup.
