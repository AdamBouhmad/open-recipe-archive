## Ingredients

- Strawberry puree, passed through a fine sieve
- 1/4 liter cold sugar syrup
- Juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix the cold sugar syrup with the juice of 2 lemons into the strawberries
2. Pass the entire mixture through a fine sieve again and stir well together
3. Weigh the prepared strawberry juice with a saccharometer until it shows 22 degrees sugar content
4. If the juice does not reach this degree, add the necessary sugar dissolved in some water; if it exceeds this, add some water and lemon juice
5. Pour the well-stirred mixture into the freezing container and proceed as explained at the beginning for frozen desserts (Recipe 1746), but for fresh fruit frozen desserts, bury the container in ice 1 hour earlier and sprinkle with a larger quantity of salt or, as with à la minute frozen desserts, also with a mixture of coarsely crushed saltpeter and sal ammoniac
