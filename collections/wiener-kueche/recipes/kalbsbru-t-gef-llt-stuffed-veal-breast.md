## Ingredients

- 1½ kg veal breast
- 4-5 bread rolls
- 6 dkg melted butter
- salt
- 1-2 eggs
- finely chopped parsley
- breadcrumbs as needed

## Directions

1. Have butcher undercut the veal breast
2. Wash and salt inside and outside
3. Fill with prepared filling, spread filling slightly apart
4. Fasten opening with skewer or sew closed
5. Place in roasting pan with skin side down
6. Place piece of butter or fat on top, add some water
7. Braise at first moderate, then higher heat for 2-2½ hours, basting frequently with own juices and adding more liquid
8. Turn when brown on top
9. Remove from pan, take out skewer, cut out ribs, divide meat into pieces
10. Serve with pan juices that have been brought to boil with a piece of butter
11. For filling: soak bread rolls in water, squeeze out well, mix with melted butter, salt, eggs, and finely chopped parsley, thicken with breadcrumbs if needed
