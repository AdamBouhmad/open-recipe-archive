## Ingredients

- 8 dkg Speck (bacon)
- 6 Eier (eggs)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into thin slices and line an omelet pan with it.
2. Lightly brown the bacon and break the eggs into the pan side by side.
3. Season with salt and pepper and spray with a bit of vinegar.
4. When the eggs are set, either turn them with a spatula or let them fry a bit more on the bottom, then lift onto a plate and serve immediately.
