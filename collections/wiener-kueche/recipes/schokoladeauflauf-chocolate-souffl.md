## Ingredients

- 8 dkg butter
- 8 dkg sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 bread rolls
- milk
- 3 egg whites
- 8 dkg grated chocolate
- butter for form
- sugar for form

## Directions

1. Cream 8 dkg butter and stir in 8 dkg sugar and 4 egg yolks
2. Soak 2 bread rolls (with crusts removed) in milk, squeeze out well, and add
3. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites together with 8 dkg grated chocolate
4. Fill into a well-buttered and sugared soufflé form
5. Bake at lower heat for 20-30 minutes
6. Sprinkle with sugar and serve in the form
7. This amount serves 4 persons; for 6 persons add 2 more eggs and proportionally more of the other ingredients
