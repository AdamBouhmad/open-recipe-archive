## Ingredients

- baked crepes
- marmalade
- Spanish almond paste
- 4 egg whites
- 6 hectograms fine powdered sugar
- butter
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Fill the baked crepes with any marmalade, roll together and divide each one into two parts
2. Lightly mix the firm snow from 4 egg whites with 6 hectograms finely powdered sugar
3. Spread some Spanish almond paste on a round bowl coated with butter, place the filled crepes cut into pieces on top, cover these again with the paste and continue alternately until everything is used and the bowl is arranged heaped
4. The last layer is paste, dust with finely powdered sugar, then let dry in the open oven for 0.5 hour
5. While baking, turn the bowl in the oven several times so that the almond paste gets an even color
6. When a yellow crust has formed, take the bowl from the oven and sprinkle the crust with warm marmalade
