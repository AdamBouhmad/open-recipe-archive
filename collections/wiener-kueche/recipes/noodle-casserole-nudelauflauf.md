## Ingredients

- 20 dkg flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- ¼ liter milk
- 5 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 dkg sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from flour, eggs, and salt
2. Cut into soup noodles
3. Bring ¼ liter milk to a boil in a pot and cook the noodles until thick, then cool
4. Cream 5 dkg butter together with 3 egg yolks and 5 dkg sugar
5. Fold in the cooled noodles and the beaten egg whites
6. Pour into a well-buttered casserole form
7. Bake at moderate heat for 30-45 minutes
8. Serve dusted with sugar in the casserole
