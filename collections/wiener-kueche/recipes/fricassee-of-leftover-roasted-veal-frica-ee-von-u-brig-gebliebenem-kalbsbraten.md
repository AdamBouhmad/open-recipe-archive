## Ingredients

- leftover roasted veal
- butter
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- half a small onion
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the brown surface from the roast, cut it into pieces, sweat it in a casserole with butter, add beef broth, salt, pepper, mace, and a half small diced onion, let everything cook together for a short time.
2. Then stir a couple of egg yolks with a little vinegar, add some finely chopped parsley to it, and pour it into the veal, after which let it become thick over the fire.
