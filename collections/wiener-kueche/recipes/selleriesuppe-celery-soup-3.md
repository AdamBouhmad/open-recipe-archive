## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Flour
- Cream
- Celery, sliced
- Roasted bread rolls
- Butter
- Pea broth

## Directions

1. Pour eggs stirred with flour in cream over sliced celery, roasted bread rolls, and a piece of butter in boiling pea broth
2. Season to taste
