## Ingredients

- small monthly radishes
- butter
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Peel small radishes and place in a baking dish with butter and sugar
2. Pour water over them until it reaches them
3. Braise until tender
4. Pour in several tablespoons of butter sauce, stir well together and serve as garnish
