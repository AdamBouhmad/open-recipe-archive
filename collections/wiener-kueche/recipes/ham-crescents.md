## Ingredients

- 16 dkg flour
- 16 dkg butter, cut into pieces
- a pinch of salt
- 1 egg yolk
- yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast
- 10 dkg cooked ham
- 1½ dkg grated Parmesan
- 1½ spoonfuls cream
- egg white for brushing

## Directions

1. On the board make a yeast crumb dough from 16 dkg flour, 16 dkg cut butter, a pinch of salt, 1 egg yolk, and yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast
2. After kneading, let it rise under a warm pot for 1 hour
3. Roll out thin and cut into squares
4. Place filling in the center of each square
5. Brush the edges with egg white
6. Press together over the filling and form into crescents
7. Place on greased baking sheet near the oven to rise
8. Before baking, brush with beaten egg or egg yolk
9. Bake in sharper heat
10. Serve cold with tea or warm with spinach or mushroom sauce
11. For filling: mix 10 dkg cooked ham with 1½ dkg grated Parmesan and 1½ spoonfuls cream
