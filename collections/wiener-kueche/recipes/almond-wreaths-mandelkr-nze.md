## Ingredients

- half pound marzipan dough
- 2 to 5 egg whites
- crushed cinnamon
- a little flour
- coarsely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Take half pound marzipan dough, 2 to 5 egg whites, crushed cinnamon, and a little flour
2. Knead the mixture into a dough
3. Scatter coarsely chopped almonds on the work table
4. Form finger-long sausages from the dough
5. Roll them in the chopped almonds
6. Join the two ends together to form a wreath
7. Place on paper
8. Slide into a well-heated oven and let bake until light brown
