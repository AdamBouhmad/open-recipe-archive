## Ingredients

- prepared orange or Rhine wine jelly
- sugar-dried fruits: mirabelles, reineclodes, apricots, grapes, and orange slices

## Directions

1. Pour approximately 2 fingers' height of finished orange or Rhine wine jelly into a mold
2. If possible, let it set on ice for a while
3. Arrange sugar-dried fruits symmetrically in it: mirabelles, reineclodes, apricots, grapes, and orange slices
4. Pour cooled jelly over these fruits
5. When sufficiently set and the fruits are well covered with jelly, arrange more fruits on top
6. Fill the mold completely with jelly and let it set well
7. When using, place the mold in hot water and immediately turn it out onto the plate
8. Do not leave the mold in hot water too long so that too much of the jelly does not melt
