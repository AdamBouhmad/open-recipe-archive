## Ingredients

- partridges
- juice of unripe grapes
- lemon juice
- salt
- cloves
- shallots
- bay leaves
- flour
- white wine
- egg yolks
- melted bacon fat
- parsley

## Directions

1. Marinate the partridges in the juice of unripe grapes and lemon juice with salt, cloves, shallots and bay leaves
2. Let them soak for approximately three hours
3. Make a thin batter from flour, white wine, and egg yolks
4. Dip the partridges into it
5. Bake them in melted bacon fat
6. Arrange them with fried parsley
