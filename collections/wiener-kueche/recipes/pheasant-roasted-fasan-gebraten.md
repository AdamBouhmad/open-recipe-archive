## Ingredients

- one pheasant
- butter
- lard/bacon
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Clean the pheasant thoroughly by plucking it
2. Remove the entrails and wash it well
3. Salt it well
4. Lard it neatly with finely cut bacon
5. Roast it frequently basting with butter
6. When roasted, garnish it with lemon peel and serve it basted with the juices on the table
