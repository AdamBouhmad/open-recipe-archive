## Ingredients

- beautiful, fatty, cleaned pork intestines
- wine
- pork fat
- pork meat
- sausage herbs
- thyme
- basil
- marjoram
- pepper
- nutmeg
- bouillon or braise

## Directions

1. Cut clean pork intestines into desired pieces
2. Season properly and marinate with some wine
3. Cut equal amounts of pork fat and pork meat the same size as the intestines
4. Mix everything together with sausage herbs, thyme, basil, marjoram, pepper, and nutmeg
5. Add the marinated intestines and work everything together well
6. Fill into pork intestines, not too firmly but rather loosely so they do not burst
7. Bind both ends well with twine
8. Cook in bouillon or braise
9. When cold, use in various ways: grill them as they are, or bread them with grated bread and grill, or keep them for a long time in winter
