## Ingredients

- Bread rolls
- Light broth
- Salt
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from bread rolls and cut into flakes
2. Cook well in a pot with a ladle of light broth
3. Season with salt
4. Just before serving, mix in cleaned grated horseradish
5. Serve warm
