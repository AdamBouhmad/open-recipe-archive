## Ingredients

- 1 liter fat broth
- 1 tablespoon polenta flour
- 1/2 kilo polenta groats or flour
- hot butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter of fat broth into a fairly large casserole
2. Add 1 tablespoon polenta flour
3. When the broth boils, add 1/2 kilo polenta groats or flour
4. Make a cross in the flour with a knife, cut through it
5. Cook uncovered for 1/4 hour
6. Remove the polenta with a spoon and put it into a dish
7. Pour hot butter over it and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top, which is also served separately
