## Ingredients

- fat or marrow from beef roast
- 1 large onion
- 1 small yellow turnip
- salt
- seasoning
- flattened beef roast pieces
- black bread
- vinegar or wine and broth or water
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1 tablespoon capers
- anchovy fillets

## Directions

1. Place fat and minced beef bones in a casserole
2. Add 1 large onion and 1 small yellow turnip, both sliced leafy
3. Add salt and seasoning to taste
4. Place the flattened beef roast pieces on top
5. Braise covered until they have reduced
6. Add a piece of black bread and enough vinegar or wine and broth or water so the liquid rises just above the meat
7. Braise covered for 1 hour
8. Transfer the roast to another casserole
9. Remove fat from the sauce and strain it over the roast
10. Add 1/2 liter cream
11. Add 1 tablespoon chopped capers and anchovy fillets, as many pieces as there are roast pieces
12. Braise these gently until completely tender
13. 1/2 liter cream is sufficient for 6 roast pieces
