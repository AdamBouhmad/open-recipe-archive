## Ingredients

- horseradish, cleaned and scraped
- breadcrumbs, grated on the same grater as the horseradish
- meat broth as needed
- one piece butter
- nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. In a small pot, simmer the carefully scraped and grated horseradish with meat broth as needed
2. Add the necessary breadcrumbs grated on the same grater as the horseradish, to remove any remaining horseradish pieces
3. Simmer for about a quarter hour
4. Finally add one piece of butter and a strong knife-tip of grated nutmeg
