## Ingredients

- 1/2 maß cream
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound curd cheese
- 1/4 pound ground almonds
- 6 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 pound sugar
- 6 loth chocolate

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 maß cream into a pot
2. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
3. Beat well and set over heat until it becomes a curd cheese
4. Bind in a cloth and hang overnight to let the water run out
5. Beat 1/4 pound butter in a bowl
6. Pound 1/2 pound curd cheese and 1/4 pound ground almonds together well
7. Put into the beaten butter
8. Beat 6 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks in a bowl
9. Beat egg whites from the eggs to peaks
10. Alternately stir in a pair of eggs and a little peak until it takes half an hour total
11. Add 1 pound sugar and 6 loth chocolate
12. Grease the ring mold with butter
13. Fill the beaten mixture in and bake slowly
