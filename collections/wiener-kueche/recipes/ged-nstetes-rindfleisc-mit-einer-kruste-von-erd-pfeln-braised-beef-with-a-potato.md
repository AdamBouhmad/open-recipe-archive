## Ingredients

- beef, cooked soft
- bacon slices
- onion
- yellow turnips
- flour
- good broth
- red wine
- kohlrabi
- yellow turnips
- three large potatoes
- butter, about the size of half an egg
- fresh green parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the beef soft, then place it in a casserole with bacon slices, onion, and yellow turnips, turning it often so it browns on all sides.
2. Remove it, dust a spoonful of flour on it, and when the flour has browned, fill halfway with good broth and halfway with red wine.
3. Once it has cooked through, strain it.
4. Using a hollow iron, form small balls from kohlrabi and yellow turnips, place them in the sauce, and cook them soft.
5. While this happens, cook three large potatoes and peel them; once cold, grate them on a grater.
6. Beat a piece of butter the size of half an egg, and mix well with the grated potatoes, some finely chopped fresh parsley, salt, two whole eggs, and two egg yolks.
7. Coat the upper part of the meat evenly one finger thick with this mixture and bake it in a hot oven until the crust is beautifully brown.
8. When plating, place the meat in the middle of the plate, the small brown sauce on the bottom of the plate, and form a wreath around it with the braised kohlrabi and yellow turnips.
