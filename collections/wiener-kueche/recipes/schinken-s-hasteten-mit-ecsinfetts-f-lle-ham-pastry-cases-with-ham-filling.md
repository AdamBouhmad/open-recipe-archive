## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 16 deca finely cut ham
- 3 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter sour cream or sweet cream
- Salt
- 3 egg whites
- Butter
- Flour
- Butter dough, knife-back thickness

## Directions

1. Beat 2 egg yolks, cut them into small squares.
2. Mix finely cut ham with 3 egg yolks, mix well with 3/4 liter sour cream or sweet cream, the cut egg yolks, and some salt, then fold in the snow from 3 egg whites.
3. Line small sandal molds buttered and dusted with flour with knife-back thick squares of butter dough.
4. Fill to a finger-wide edge with the ham mixture given above, fold the 4 corners slightly over each other in the center, brush with beaten eggs, but leave all edges knife-back width dry.
5. Place the molds on a baking sheet in the oven and bake the pastries at moderate heat for about 1/2 hour.
6. After this time, lift them out of the molds with a fork, arrange on a platter, and serve as a main course, flour dish, or for supper or tea.
