## Ingredients

- 70 dkg Knurrhahn (gurnard)
- Oil
- Petersilie (parsley)
- Sardelle (anchovy)
- Salt
- Milchwaſſer (milk water)

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, wash it and place in milk water for 30 minutes.
2. Make an incision on the side, salt it and let it rest for 30 minutes, then wipe it off.
3. Heat some spoons of oil in a pan, lay the fish in it, fry on both sides for 10-15 minutes and lift onto a platter.
4. Let finely chopped parsley heat in the oil, add possibly 1 strained anchovy and pour it over the fish.
