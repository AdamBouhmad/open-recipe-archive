## Ingredients

- 4 loth lard or butter
- egg
- 2 egg yolks
- milk
- salt
- semolina
- beef soup or pea broth

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of 4 loth lard or butter beaten very fluffy, a whole egg, 2 egg yolks, a couple spoonfuls of milk, the necessary salt, and as much semolina as needed
2. Let a piece of lard in a pan become hot, put the mixture in, let it brown on top and bottom from the heat, and then simmer it in beef soup (on fast days in pea broth)
3. When serving sprinkle it with nutmeg flower
