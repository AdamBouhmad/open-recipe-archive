## Ingredients

- Approximately 1 kilogram raw potatoes
- Boiling salt water
- 20 ounces butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Short pastry dough
- Ham
- Sausage or goose liver

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut them into pieces, and pour boiling salt water over them (only enough so the water goes 1/2 finger width above the potatoes), add 10 ounces of butter and cook them soft until they can be stirred into a puree, then stir in another 10 ounces of butter, salt, and some pepper.
2. Lightly brush the bottom and sides of a casserole or desired form with butter and line with short pastry dough. This can be rolled the thickness of a cord and arranged in the form of a spiral.
3. Put the stirred potatoes 1/2 finger high on the bottom of the form, cover them with smoked ham that has been cooked soft and cut into small pieces, fried sausages, or fried goose liver, add again as much potato as before, and continue until the casserole is full.
4. Then cover the opening with dough and bake the cake for about 1 hour at moderate heat.
