## Ingredients

- Beaver tail with part of the backbone attached
- Bacon sheets
- Butter
- Onions
- Cloves
- Dried herbs
- Red wine
- Good bouillon
- Sauce Espagnole
- Capers
- Truffles
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Leave part of the backbone attached to the beaver tail
2. Blanch the tail repeatedly with boiling water to remove scales
3. Line a long dish with bacon sheets, butter, onions, cloves and dried herbs
4. Place the beaver tail in the dish and sear it quickly over heat to color it
5. Add a glass of red wine and good bouillon
6. Let it cook slowly until done
7. Remove the tail and skim off fat
8. Mix the cooking liquid with Sauce Espagnole
9. Reduce briefly
10. Add capers, truffles and lemon juice
11. Serve with the sauce
