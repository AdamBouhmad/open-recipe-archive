## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- peas broth
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Hard boil as many eggs as needed
2. Cut them in half
3. Remove the yolks to a bowl and mash well with a spoon
4. Chop parsley very fine
5. Toast it with a little breadcrumbs in butter
6. Mix into the egg yolks
7. Beat in a few fresh eggs as needed to make a thick filling
8. Stir everything together well
9. Fill the egg white halves with the mixture, heaping it up like a small hill
10. Bake in a dish brushed with butter and dotted with small pieces of butter
11. Prepare a green sauce by pounding parsley in a mortar
12. Mix with some peas broth
13. Add butter and seasoning
14. Let the sauce simmer only briefly so it does not lose its color
15. Pour the green sauce over the baked eggs
