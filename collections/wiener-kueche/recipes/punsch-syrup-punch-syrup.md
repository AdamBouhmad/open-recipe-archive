## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine sugar
- 2 pounds lemon juice
- 1.5 bottles rum or arrack

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar
2. Add lemon juice and rum or arrack
3. Place on gentle charcoal fire and cook while stirring constantly, not for too long
4. Let cool
5. Pour rum in slowly
6. Stir well
7. Fill into bottles
