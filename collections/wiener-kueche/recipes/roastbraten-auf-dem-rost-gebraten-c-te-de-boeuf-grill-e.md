## Ingredients

- Roastbraten pieces (from the loin to the shoulders, one piece per person)
- Butter
- Parsley butter with lemon juice, or tarragon butter, or shallot butter with vinegar
- Salt
- Spices

## Directions

1. Roastbraten is the part found from the loin to the shoulders. Take as many pieces as there are people at table, remove the ribs, pound and salt the roastbraten, dip them in melted butter, and broil them quickly over strong heat on a grill.
2. After 2-3 minutes, turn them and broil them equally on the other side.
3. Arrange them on a plate and place in the center of each piece some parsley butter with lemon juice, or tarragon butter, or finely cut shallots cooked in butter with several spoonfuls of vinegar, some salt and spices, and pour this on the side of the plate or serve the juice in a sauce boat.
