## Ingredients

- 2/3 liter black millet
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 12 decagrams hot lard

## Directions

1. Put 2/3 liter black millet and 3/4 teaspoon salt in a casserole
2. While stirring, heat it
3. Make a hollow in the middle and gradually pour about 1/2 liter boiling water into it
4. Stir well and let it boil for a few minutes
5. If the mass is too thick, add the necessary hot water
6. Cook until it becomes a fairly dry flour dough
7. Divide the dough into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon
8. Pour 12 decagrams hot lard over it and roast covered for a few minutes
