## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams peeled, diced Mischantscher apples
- 0.5 ounce water
- 0.5 ounce coffee
- 16 decagrams sugar
- 14 decagrams whipped butter
- 16 decagrams finely sifted sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 6 decagrams white bread crumbs
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Place 16 decagrams peeled, diced apples in a saucepan
2. Add 0.5 ounce water, 0.5 ounce coffee, and 16 decagrams sugar
3. Stew until soft and set aside to cool
4. Beat 14 decagrams whipped butter with 16 decagrams finely sifted sugar well
5. Add 6 egg yolks one by one and beat
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites simultaneously with 6 decagrams white bread crumbs
7. Butter a mold and dust with flour
8. Spread a portion of the butter mixture in the mold
9. Layer with cooked apples, cover with more butter mixture
10. Continue alternating until all ingredients are used
11. Pour the juice from the stewed apples over the top
12. Bake at moderate heat for 1/2 hour in the oven
