## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 15 dkg sugar
- 12 dkg coarsely chopped hazelnuts or nuts
- 5 dkg finely diced citron

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites with 15 dkg sugar over steam until thick
2. Add coarsely chopped hazelnuts and finely diced citron
3. Form small mounds using 2 small spoons on buttered sheet
4. Bake at moderate heat
