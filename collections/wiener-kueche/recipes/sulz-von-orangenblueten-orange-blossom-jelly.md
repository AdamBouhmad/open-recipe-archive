## Ingredients

- 4 decagrams fresh picked orange blossoms
- 40 decagrams boiling, clarified sugar
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Clear juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as Violet Sulz (see recipe 1677)
2. Use 4 decagrams fresh picked orange blossoms instead of violet flowers
3. Instead of lemon and orange juice, use other juice
