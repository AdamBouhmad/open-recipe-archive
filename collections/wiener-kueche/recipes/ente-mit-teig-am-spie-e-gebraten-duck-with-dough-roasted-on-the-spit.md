## Ingredients

- One young fat duck
- Salt
- Pepper
- Dry fine herbs
- Good poultry forcemeat
- Butter
- Bacon slices
- Thin pâte brisée
- Buttered paper
- Sauce pikante

## Directions

1. Clean and gut a young fat duck, remove the breastbone and thigh bones, rub with salt, pepper, and dry fine herbs
2. Fill it with good poultry forcemeat, sew it up again, and dress as for roasting
3. Let butter melt in a casserole and pass the duck in it, also bind bacon slices around it, and wrap it entirely in a thinly rolled out pâte brisée
4. Around this wrap buttered paper and tie it with string
5. Roast the duck at moderate fire on the spit, and when roasted, remove the paper, dress it on the plate, and serve sauce pikante underneath
