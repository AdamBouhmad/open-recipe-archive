## Ingredients

- 32 drams of finely sifted sugar
- 1 1/6 liter of very strong hot coffee without sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 32 drams of finely sifted sugar and 1 1/6 liter of very strong hot coffee without sugar together for 1 hour.
2. It must be so thick that it drops heavily from the spoon.
3. Pour the glaze onto the cake, bread, Gugelhupf or the object in question and spread it with a knife dipped in very hot water; also stroke the running glaze back up with the knife as long as it holds.
4. If the glaze is too thick, add coffee; if it is too thin, add sugar.
5. This cannot be determined because the sugar is not uniform.
