## Ingredients

- 5 bunches chervil
- 5 bunches parsley
- chervil herb
- thyme
- 1 piece mushroom
- pike fillets
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop very finely with a knife: chervil, parsley, chervil herb, thyme (5 bunches each) and 1 piece mushroom
2. Place the pike pieces described in the previous recipe in the butter-brushed casserole, sprinkle with the chopped herbs and fry as indicated before
3. Prepare sauce as indicated in the pike fillets recipe and either pour over the arranged pieces or serve in a sauceboat
4. Serve with any fine dumplings or potatoes
