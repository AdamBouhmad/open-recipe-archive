## Ingredients

- 8 teaspoons hot lard butter
- 8 teaspoons flour
- 1/4 liter boiling cream or milk
- 9 egg yolks
- 16 teaspoons sugar
- crayfish claws and tails, finely cut
- 4 egg whites
- 8 teaspoons white breadcrumbs
- crayfish butter

## Directions

1. In 8 teaspoons of hot lard butter, add 8 teaspoons of flour, stir both well together, once it begins to foam, add 1/4 liter of boiling cream or milk, stir it at the stove until the dough leaves the spoon and set it aside
2. Meanwhile, beat 9 egg yolks with 16 teaspoons of sugar for 1/2 hour, slowly pour this to the cooked but cooled mass, mix well with finely cut crayfish claws and tails and finally lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites with 8 teaspoons of white breadcrumbs
3. Fill a mold lined with crayfish butter and greased with this mass, which is then cooked covered in a water bath for 3/4 hours
