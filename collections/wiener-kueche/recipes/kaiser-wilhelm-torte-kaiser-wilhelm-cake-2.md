## Ingredients

- 11 dkg butter
- 15 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 11 dkg softened chocolate
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 9 dkg flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 4 dkg grated chocolate
- 6 dkg butter
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Cream together 11 dkg butter with 15 dkg sugar gradually
2. Add 6 egg yolks one by one
3. Add 11 dkg softened chocolate
4. Fold in 6 stiff egg whites together with 9 dkg flour
5. Fill the batter into the form and bake slowly
6. For the filling: beat 2 egg yolks with 7 dkg sugar and 1 tablespoon water over steam until thick
7. Cool the filling and gradually stir in 4 dkg grated chocolate and 6 dkg butter until smooth
8. Fill and frost the torte with brown glaze
9. Sprinkle with pistachios
