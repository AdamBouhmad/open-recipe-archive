## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- 8 dkg fat
- 1 onion ring
- 5-8 pieces sugar cubes
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Caraway
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and stalks from cabbage
2. Cut into quarters, wash, and cut into fine noodle-like strips
3. Heat 8 dkg fat in a casserole
4. Toast 1 onion ring until dark yellow
5. Brown 5-8 sugar cubes in the fat
6. Add the cabbage
7. Season with salt, vinegar, and caraway
8. Add water until cabbage is covered
9. Braise covered for 2-2.5 hours, adding water occasionally
10. Season to taste with salt, sugar, and vinegar
