## Ingredients

- Fresh paradise apples
- Fresh water

## Directions

1. Wrap fresh paradise apples in vine leaves
2. Place them in a small jar and fill it completely with fresh water, which should be replaced once or twice daily to keep them fresh
3. It is best to use a jar with a tap so that the water can be drained easily
4. The jar must be completely filled with paradise apples
