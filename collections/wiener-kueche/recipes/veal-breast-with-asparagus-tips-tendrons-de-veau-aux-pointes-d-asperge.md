## Ingredients

- veal breast
- asparagus tips
- salted water

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as described for braised veal breast.
2. Cook asparagus tips in salted water until tender.
3. Drain the asparagus, place in a casserole, pour the thickened sauce over, stir well, and serve with the veal breast.
