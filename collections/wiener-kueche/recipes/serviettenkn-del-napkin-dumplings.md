## Ingredients

- 6 bread rolls
- 1/2 liter milk
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 6 dekagrams melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the rolls into small cubes and place in a bowl
2. Pour over with milk mixed with 3 eggs, salt and 6 dekagrams melted butter
3. Let stand for 1 hour
4. Place on a well-buttered napkin, form into a roll, bind tightly in the napkin, wrap with string
5. Place in a pan with plenty of boiling salted water and cook for 1 hour
6. Unwrap the roll, cut into slices and arrange in steps on both sides of the meat
