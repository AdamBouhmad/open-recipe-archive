## Ingredients

- 1 mass and 3 seidel milk
- one piece cinnamon
- 15 whole eggs
- one quarter pound bitter almonds
- three eighth pound sugar
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Let 1 mass and 3 seidel milk with one piece cinnamon boil several times
2. Beat 15 whole eggs in a pot with one quarter pound bitter almonds finely grated on a grater, three eighth pound sugar equally finely grated, and the zest of one lemon also grated, and whisk for a quarter hour
3. Pour the hot milk to it while stirring very strongly
4. Pour the cream onto a plate and let it become firm in a lukewarm oven
5. It can be served cold or warm, or the plate can be set on a casserole with boiling water, covered with a fitting lid, and the cream allowed to become firm in this way
6. All creams must not stand too hot, otherwise they become blistery, which is not good
