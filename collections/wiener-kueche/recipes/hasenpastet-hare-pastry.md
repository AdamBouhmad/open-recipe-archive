## Ingredients

- Fine puff pastry
- Egg yolk
- Hollowed semolina bread
- Paper
- Good ragout of hare

## Directions

1. Roll fine puff pastry to the thickness of a small finger for the bottom base of the pastry
2. Place it on a baking sheet and on top place a round hollowed-out semolina bread wrapped in paper in the shape of a mountain
3. Brush the pastry all around with egg yolk
4. Lay a lid rolled from the same pastry of the same size over it
5. Cut it away all around the pastry so that you can press circles with the back of a knife in a row around it to give it a more handsome form
6. Brush with egg yolk and bake for half an hour
7. Once it is beautifully yellow, cut out a lid to remove the paper together with the semolina bread
8. Fill with a good ragout of hare
9. Serve hot
10. Various poultry can also be used for this pastry
