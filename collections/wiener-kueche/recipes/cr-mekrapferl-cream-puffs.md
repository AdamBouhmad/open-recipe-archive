## Ingredients

- choux pastry made from: 2 dl water, 7 dkg butter, salt, 14 dkg flour, 3-4 small eggs
- filling cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare choux pastry from water, butter, salt, flour and eggs (see Teige section).
2. With a small spoon, pipe small puffs onto a buttered sheet.
3. Bake at higher heat until light brown.
4. They should rise high and be hollow inside.
5. Make a test puff first to check if the dough is the right consistency.
6. If the dough is too thin or too stiff, add flour or an egg accordingly.
7. Do not open the oven door too often during baking or they will collapse.
8. Cut the puffs on the side, fill with cream, sprinkle with sugar and serve.
