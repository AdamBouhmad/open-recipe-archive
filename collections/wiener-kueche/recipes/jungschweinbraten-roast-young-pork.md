## Ingredients

- pork with skin
- salt
- caraway seeds
- hot water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat
2. Rub with salt and caraway seeds
3. Place skin-side down in a roasting pan with hot water
4. Roast in the oven for 30 minutes
5. Remove and score the skin by cutting small squares into it with a sharp knife
6. Place back in the pan skin-side up
7. Roast for another 1-1½ hours at higher heat, basting frequently with its own juices
8. Serve as above
