## Ingredients

- apples
- preserves (such as gooseberries or others)
- good butter dough
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the apples as above.
2. Fill with preserves.
3. Wrap in a piece of good butter dough.
4. Arrange on a tart pan.
5. Bake slowly in the oven.
6. Glaze with sugar.
7. The same can also be made with pears.
