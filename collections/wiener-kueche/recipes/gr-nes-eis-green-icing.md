## Ingredients

- prepared white icing
- spinach leaves
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix a little spinach leaves into the prepared white icing, having first finely rubbed them with powdered sugar
2. This gives the icing a beautiful color, though the taste is not particularly pleasant, so it is used only for decoration
