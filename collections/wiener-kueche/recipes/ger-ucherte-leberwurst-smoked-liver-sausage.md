## Ingredients

- Fatty raw or uncooked liver, finely chopped
- One quarter of the broth from the previous recipe
- Cleaned intestines

## Directions

1. Use the ingredients from the previous recipe, but make it somewhat fattier
2. Add raw or uncooked liver, finely chopped
3. Add only one quarter of the aforementioned broth
4. Fill into cleaned intestines
5. Hang for two to three days in smoke
6. Can be eaten raw and uncooked; best served cold
