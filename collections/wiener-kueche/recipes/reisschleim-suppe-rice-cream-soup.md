## Ingredients

- 16 spoonfuls rice
- 1 liter broth
- approximately 1.5 liters white or chicken broth

## Directions

1. Cook 16 spoonfuls rice in 1 liter broth until soft
2. Pour approximately 1.5 liters white or chicken broth over it, stir well together
3. Press through a fine sieve
