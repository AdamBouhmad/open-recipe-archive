## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- 1/2 kilo sugar per liter of clear juice

## Directions

1. Put an arbitrary quantity of raspberries into a deep bowl
2. Crush them completely and let them stand for 24 hours, until the juice separates from the flesh and fermentation has begun
3. Press the crushed raspberries in small portions through a strong cloth, fill the juice into a bottle, bind this with a muslin cloth and let it stand for 8-10 days until a rose-colored sediment has formed and the juice is fairly clear
4. Pour off the clear juice and filter the rest through filter paper
5. To every liter of clear juice add 1/2 kilo sugar, namely put the sugar and the respective quantity of raspberry juice in a saucepan, boil it for 1/4 hour under constant rapid stirring, strain it through flannel and fill it into bottles only when it has cooled
6. In the preparation of raspberry syrup neither iron nor enameled dishes may be used, as the juice would become blue or black from it
7. Instead of flannel one can also use a finer linen cloth
