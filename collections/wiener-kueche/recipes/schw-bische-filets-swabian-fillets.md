## Ingredients

- 3 cutlets à 25 dkg
- 4 dkg bacon
- 2 anchovies
- 4 tablespoons capers
- 1 pickled gherkin
- some broth or water
- cream
- salt
- pepper
- fat for searing

## Directions

1. Wipe the cutlets, remove the skin, pound them, salt and pepper them, lard them, make a small cut at the edge, quickly sear them on both sides in hot fat and place on a plate
2. Pour part of the remaining fat into a casserole
3. Finely chop 1 onion, the gherkin, the capers, the boned anchovies, 1 bay leaf and the bacon scraps and roast them in the hot fat in the casserole
4. Add the fillets, pour in some hot water and cover
5. Braise them under frequent basting for 2-2½ hours
6. Remove the fillets, dust the sauce, pass it through a sieve, season with vinegar, add cream and cook well with the meat
7. Serve on a long platter with the sauce
