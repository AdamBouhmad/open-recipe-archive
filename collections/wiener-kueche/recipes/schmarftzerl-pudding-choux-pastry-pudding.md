## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk used to prepare Schmarftzerln (recipe 1345)
- 5 egg yolks
- 8 ounces sugar
- 5 egg whites, beaten to snow

## Directions

1. Prepare Schmarftzerln from 1/2 liter milk
2. Cook them in 1/2 liter boiling milk and stir over heat until they are well cooked through
3. Set aside and let cool
4. Beat 5 egg yolks with 8 ounces sugar for 1 hour and 15 minutes
5. Slowly combine with the cooled Schmarftzerln
6. Gently fold in the snow from 5 egg whites
7. Line a buttered mold with flour, lay the cut Schmarftzerln, fill with the mixture above
8. Cover and steam for 1 hour and 15 minutes
