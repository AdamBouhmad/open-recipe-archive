## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- juice and zest of ½ lemon
- 5 stiff egg whites
- 7 dkg potato flour

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and egg yolks fluffy for 15 minutes
2. Add lemon juice and zest
3. Fold in stiff egg whites together with potato flour
4. Fill into form
5. Bake slowly
6. Cut in half, fill with jam, coat with jam on top and glaze white, or fill only inside and sprinkle with sugar
