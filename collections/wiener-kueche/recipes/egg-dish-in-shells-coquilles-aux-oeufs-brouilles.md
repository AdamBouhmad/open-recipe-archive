## Ingredients

- Scrambled egg ragout (from recipe 1015)
- Shells
- Bread rolls, baked in butter

## Directions

1. Prepare scrambled eggs with ragout as in recipe 1015.
2. Fill warmed shells with the egg mixture.
3. Top with baked bread rolls or milk bread pieces and serve.
