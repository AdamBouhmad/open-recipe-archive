## Ingredients

- One chicken, cut into small pieces
- A piece of lean ham
- Some slices of veal
- Finely chopped bone marrow
- One cup of bouillon
- One glass of strong white wine
- A little coulis
- Finely chopped shallots
- Mushrooms
- A bundle of dried herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place the chicken, ham, veal, bone marrow, and one cup of bouillon in a casserole over moderate heat.
2. Once it has begun to render, pour in a glass of strong white wine, a little coulis, and the remaining bouillon.
3. Add finely chopped shallots, mushrooms, and a bundle of dried herbs.
4. Let the sauce reduce to half its volume.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
6. Remove the various solids that were used to make the sauce.
7. Pass the sauce through a fine sieve.
8. Skim off the fat and foam, and use as desired.
