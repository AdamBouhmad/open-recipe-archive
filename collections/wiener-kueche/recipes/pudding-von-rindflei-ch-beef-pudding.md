## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of lean beef, cut into long narrow strips then fine cubes
- Half pound of smoked bacon, cut the same way
- 8 well-beaten eggs
- Finely chopped onions
- Finely cut lemon zest
- Salt
- Mace
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Dried and finely grated basil
- 3 or 4 spoonfuls of hot butter

## Directions

1. Cut 3 pounds of lean beef into long, narrow strips, then into fine cubes
2. Cut half pound of smoked bacon the same way
3. Put into a bowl with 8 well-beaten eggs, finely chopped onions, finely cut lemon zest, salt, mace, cloves, pepper, and dried and finely grated basil
4. Pour 3 or 4 spoonfuls of hot butter over
5. Stir everything together well
6. Prepare as all other puddings
7. Cook for 4 hours
8. For the sauce: brown flour in butter until golden, let cool
9. Pour meat broth onto it
10. Add mushrooms, lemon slices, capers, mace, and pepper
11. Let it cook and pour over the pudding
