## Ingredients

- 2 pounds dry fine flour
- half pound butter
- 5 whole eggs
- egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons good beer yeast
- 4 loth sugar
- half Mass good milk
- nutmeg
- preserved raspberries, currants, or apricots
- jam with almonds, sugar, and cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Warm the milk so butter melts in it
2. Beat the eggs very smooth
3. Add milk with butter and remaining ingredients plus a little nutmeg
4. Strike the flour into this
5. Beat the dough with a cooking spoon until it detaches
6. Transfer to table and work in a little more flour so it rolls out easily
7. Roll out straw-stalk thickness
8. Lay a row of small heaps of jam filling mixed with almonds, sugar, and cinnamon across the width, not too close together
9. Fold the outer side over
10. Cut puffs with a pastry wheel
11. Let rise in warmth
12. Fry golden yellow in lard
