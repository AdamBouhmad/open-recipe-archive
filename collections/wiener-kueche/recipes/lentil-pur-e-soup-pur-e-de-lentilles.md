## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter lentils, cleaned and washed
- 1 parsley root
- 1 yellow turnip
- 1 onion
- some parsley and leek roots
- 10 ounces butter
- 2.5 liters beef soup
- white pepper
- salt
- bread croutons
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 liter of cleaned, washed lentils until soft, strain and pass through a sieve
2. Clean and peel 1 parsley root, 1 yellow turnip, 1 onion and some parsley and leek roots, slice finely
3. Roast this in 10 ounces butter, pour in 2.5 liters of beef soup and let simmer 1/2 hour slowly
4. Pass the vegetable soup through a sieve, remove the fat cleanly and thin the lentil purée with it
5. Bring to a boil again and remove scum and fat once more
6. Season with salt, white pepper and serve with bread croutons
7. If desired, this soup can be soured with vinegar or lemon juice
