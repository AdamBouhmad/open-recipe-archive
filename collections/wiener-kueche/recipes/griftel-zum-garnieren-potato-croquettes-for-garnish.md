## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes and cut them egg-shaped or into strips
2. Cook them covered in salted water
3. Drain them, place a piece of butter on top and let it melt
4. Sprinkle with parsley and mix with the potatoes
5. Serve as garnish for beef, venison or fish
