## Ingredients

- strong beef broth
- well-plucked and cleaned partridge or woodcock
- egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a particularly strong broth and cook it well, then clean it of all fat.
2. Strain it through a cloth, add necessary salt, and keep it hot.
3. Take a well-plucked and cleaned partridge or woodcock and pound it very finely raw in a mortar, removing the entrails from the partridge but not from the woodcock.
4. Add one serving spoon of broth and 4 egg yolks to it.
5. Pound everything together well, then strain it through a hair cloth.
6. If the strained mixture is too thick, add some cooled broth.
7. When the soup is hot and seasoned with salt and grated nutmeg to taste, pour it into the soup pot and add the strained mixture while constantly stirring.
