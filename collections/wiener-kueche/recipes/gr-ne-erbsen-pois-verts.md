## Ingredients

- 1 liter fine green peas
- salt water
- 12 ounces butter
- flour as needed
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook 1 liter of fine green peas in boiling salt water until tender
2. Meanwhile, prepare a butter sauce with 12 ounces of butter, flour as needed, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and salt, cooking well
3. Add the drained peas to the butter sauce
4. Cover and keep warm until ready to serve
