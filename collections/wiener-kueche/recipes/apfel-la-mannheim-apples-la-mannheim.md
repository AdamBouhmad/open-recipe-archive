## Ingredients

- 6 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 bread rolls soaked in water
- 3 stiffly beaten egg whites
- 7 dkg swelled, grated almonds
- stewed cooled apples
- currants

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter, egg yolks, sugar, squeezed bread rolls, egg whites and almonds
2. Fill half the mass into a buttered baking pan
3. Spread the stewed cooled apples on top, scatter currants over them
4. Spread the remaining batter on top and bake in the oven at moderate heat
5. Cut into pieces, remove with a spatula, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm
