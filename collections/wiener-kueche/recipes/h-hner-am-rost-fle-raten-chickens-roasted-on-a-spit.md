## Ingredients

- Plucked, cleaned chickens
- Butter
- Bound sauce
- Breadcrumbs
- Eggs
- Lemon slices
- Ragout or other garnish (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens lengthwise down the middle, remove the breast and back meat, cut the legs or thighs below the knee, push the meat of the thighs back slightly, and tuck the wings under the thighs.
2. Flatten the chickens with a meat pounder, salt them, place in a casserole brushed with butter, cover with butter and roast for 15-20 minutes over medium heat.
3. Remove from heat, let them cool in the butter, then place in a bowl.
4. Brush the now completely cooled chicken pieces on both sides with bound sauce, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, dip in the butter in which the chickens were roasted, and coat again with breadcrumbs.
5. Place a paper brushed with butter on a roasting rack, arrange the chicken pieces side by side on it, brown on both sides, then cut each piece in half, arrange these pieces in a circular pattern in a bowl, place desired ragout or similar garnish in the center, and garnish the rim of the bowl with lemon slices.
6. The chickens can alternatively be roasted in a shallow casserole with some fat instead of on a spit.
