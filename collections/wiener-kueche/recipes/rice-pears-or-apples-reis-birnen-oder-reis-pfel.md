## Ingredients

- rice mass from recipe 1136
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying
- 1 clove per piece
- cinnamon stick pieces

## Directions

1. Form the same mass as the Rice-Croquettes (No. 1136) into pear or apple shapes
2. Roll in flour, dip in beaten egg, and roll in breadcrumbs
3. Fry in very hot lard
4. Remove with a slotted spoon, insert a clove on one side and a piece of cinnamon stick at the other side where the stem would be
5. Arrange in a bowl
