## Ingredients

- Dough as for Gebacked Pies with Plums (recipe 1178)
- 16 Deca rinsed currants (or raisins)
- Fat, very hot
- Sugar, finely ground

## Directions

1. When the dough has risen about one-third higher, mix 16 Deca washed currants into it
2. Using a teaspoon, form pieces of desired size that are blunt at the tip of the spoon and taper into a string at the opposite end
3. Place in very hot fat and let fry evenly, shaking the pan frequently
4. Remove with a slotted spoon, place briefly on blotting paper, arrange in a bowl, sprinkle with finely ground sugar, and serve with a sauce
