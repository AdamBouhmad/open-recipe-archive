## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 21 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- water
- egg white for brushing
- apples
- 8-10 cubes cube sugar
- water
- lemon peel
- 3 dkg raisins

## Directions

1. Prepare a butter dough from the flour, butter, egg, salt, vinegar, and water
2. Roll out into a rectangle and place on an ungreased baking sheet
3. Fill down the middle lengthwise
4. Fold the dough over from both sides to cover the filling
5. Brush with egg white
6. Cut narrow strips from the leftover dough and place them crosswise over the strudel
7. Brush with beaten egg and bake
8. Cut diagonally, dust with sugar, and serve cooled
9. For the filling: peel, core, and quarter the apples; braise with cube sugar, water, lemon peel, and raisins until soft; cool completely and use without the liquid
