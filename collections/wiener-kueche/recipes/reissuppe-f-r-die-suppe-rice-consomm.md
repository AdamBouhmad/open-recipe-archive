## Ingredients

- 20 Deka rice
- hot water
- 1/4 liter boiling milk
- 8 Deka butter
- 4 egg yolks
- some lard
- nutmeg
- 4 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in hot water and strain it well
2. Stir it into 1/4 liter of boiling milk and let it simmer
3. Stir in 8 Deka butter and transfer the rice to a deep bowl
4. Once completely cooled, stir in 4 egg yolks, some lard and nutmeg
5. Gently fold in the beaten egg whites
6. Fill into a mold and cook in a water bath or bake like Kaiserschmarren
