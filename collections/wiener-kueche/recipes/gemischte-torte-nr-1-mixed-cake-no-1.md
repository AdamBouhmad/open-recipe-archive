## Ingredients

- 6 decagrams butter, beaten
- 10 decagrams granulated sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 8 decagrams almonds with skin, finely ground and sifted
- 6 egg whites
- 1 bar grated chocolate
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Gradually mix 6 decagrams beaten butter with 10 decagrams granulated sugar and 6 egg yolks, stirring for 1/2 hour.
2. Add 8 decagrams finely ground and sifted almonds with skin, mix well, and lightly fold in the beaten snow of 6 egg whites mixed simultaneously with 1 bar of grated chocolate.
3. Fill a buttered cake form dusted with flour with this mixture and bake at moderate heat for 1/2 hour in the oven, then place on a sieve to cool.
