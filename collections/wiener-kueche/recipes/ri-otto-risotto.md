## Ingredients

- 30 decagrams rice
- 1 piece beef marrow
- cooked meat from 1 chicken or 1 goose
- tomato paste
- 5 decagrams Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 kilogram green peas
- 10 decagrams lard
- 1 spring onion
- chicken broth
- salt

## Directions

1. In 10 decagrams hot lard in a casserole, let 1 spring onion and the beef marrow begin to brown
2. Strain through a sieve and return the fat without the onion to the casserole
3. Add the cleaned rice and let it hiss for 3-4 minutes, but no longer
4. Pour in strained chicken broth so that it covers the rice by a finger's width
5. Stir and let the broth evaporate
6. Add salt and tomato paste, pour in more broth, and let the rice stew for approximately 30-45 minutes
7. Just before serving, add the grated cheese, the separately cooked peas, and the diced chicken meat
8. Let it stand for 5 more minutes
9. Serve immediately, scattered with cheese, as an appetizer or evening dish on a round plate
10. Instead of chicken broth, the rice can be cooked with goose broth and diced goose meat added
