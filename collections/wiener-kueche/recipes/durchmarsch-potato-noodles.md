## Ingredients

- 6 medium potatoes
- 12 hectograms hot fat
- salt
- 1/4 liter flour
- salted water

## Directions

1. After potatoes are boiled soft and peeled, crush them into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon
2. Add to 12 hectograms hot fat
3. Season to taste and stir well
4. Add noodles made from 1/4 liter flour, cut and boiled in salted water
5. Stir well together
6. When hot, place on a plate and serve immediately
