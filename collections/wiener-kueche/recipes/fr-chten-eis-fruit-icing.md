## Ingredients

- white icing base
- fruit juice or marmalade

## Directions

1. Prepare as white icing, only using any desired fruit juice instead of lemon juice, namely the sieved pulp of fresh fruit or marmalade dissolved with some lukewarm water
2. If the color of the icing is not beautiful enough, one can add alkermes or cochineal color for red, or egg yolks for yellow if needed
