## Ingredients

- 12 drams flour
- 1 liter cold cream
- 14 drams butter, beaten
- 16 drams sugar
- 14 egg yolks
- 6 blanched, finely ground bitter almonds
- 9 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Pour 1 liter cold cream slowly over 12 drams flour.
2. Stir over heat until it has thickened considerably.
3. Set aside and let cool.
4. Mix 14 drams beaten butter with 16 drams sugar thoroughly.
5. Stir in the 14 egg yolks one after another.
6. Mix this with the 6 blanched, finely ground bitter almonds and the cooled flour mixture.
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 9 egg whites.
8. Fill a buttered mold, dusted with flour.
9. If desired, insert biscuits, crushed macaroons, or small pastries.
10. Bake at low heat for 3/4 hour in the oven.
