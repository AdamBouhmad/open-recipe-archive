## Ingredients

- Veal, about 10 cutlets of knife-back thickness
- Bread
- Capers
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Egg
- Onion
- Carrot
- Mushrooms
- White wine or vinegar
- Soup

## Directions

1. Cut about 10 knife-back thick cutlets from a veal leg
2. Take 2 cutlets and pound them in a mortar or pass through a meat grinder with 8 ounces of bread softened in water and well pressed out, and 4 capers
3. Meanwhile, roast in 4 ounces of butter 1 tablespoon of finely cut green parsley, some onion, and finely cut mushrooms
4. Add the roasted mixture to the pounded veal in the mortar
5. Pound again with one whole egg, strain
6. Spread this paste knife-back thick on the pounded and salted cutlets
7. Roll up these cutlets and tie them with string
8. Place 1 finely cut head of onion, 1 small piece of carrot, and 2 mushrooms, thinly sliced, in a casserole with fat
9. Lay the cutlets side by side in the casserole on the vegetables
10. Cover with a paper bag brushed with butter
11. Let stew covered for 15 minutes
12. After this time, pour in 1/2 liter of white wine or 1/2 liter of vinegar and some soup
13. Stew until completely tender
14. Cut the cutlets into desired pieces, arrange heaped on a serving dish
15. Pour the defatted, strained sauce over them
16. Note: the cutlets must remain well covered during stewing, and there must always be 2 fingers depth of sauce in the casserole
