## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons butter
- 24 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 50 tablespoons cooked, pressed, cooled potatoes
- 8 egg whites
- finely sifted sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat 16 tablespoons butter until fluffy
2. Gradually beat in 24 tablespoons powdered sugar and 8 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix in 50 tablespoons cooked, pressed, cooled potatoes
4. Lightly fold in the beaten egg whites from 8 eggs
5. Fill the mixture into a form brushed with butter and dusted with flour
6. Cover and steam for 3/4 hours or bake in the oven
7. Turn the soufflé out onto a platter, sprinkle with finely sifted sugar, and serve
8. Optionally, 12 tablespoons finely sifted and pressed hazelnuts or 12 tablespoons blanched almonds finely sifted with 1 egg white can be mixed in with the potatoes
