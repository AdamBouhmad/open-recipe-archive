## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- 1/2 liter millet flour
- 12 decagrams hot lard or rendered fat

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter water in a fairly large pot and when it boils, add 1/2 liter millet flour
2. Cook covered for 10 to 15 minutes
3. Turn the flour mass with a wooden spoon so the lower side comes to the top
4. Cook covered again for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring through the flour mass with a wooden spoon
5. Pour off the water that has not evaporated
6. Stir the flour mass well together
7. Put it in a flat casserole, pour 12 decagrams hot lard or rendered fat over it
8. Let it stand covered over low heat for 1/4 hour
9. Divide the Sterz into smaller pieces with a broad spoon
10. Arrange raised on a dish, pour hot lard or fat cracklings over it
11. Serve if desired with beef broth, mushroom broth or boiled milk, or serve with tea
