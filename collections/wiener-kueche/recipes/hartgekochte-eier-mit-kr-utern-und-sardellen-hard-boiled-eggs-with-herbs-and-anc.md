## Ingredients

- Hard-boiled shelled eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Grated herbs
- Sardelles

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled shelled eggs into 4 parts, place in a bowl, dust with salt and pepper, pour vinegar and oil over and sprinkle with grated herbs.
2. Then clean sardelles, separate them, remove the bones, wash them and roll them snail-shaped.
3. Arrange them on the bowl between the eggs.
