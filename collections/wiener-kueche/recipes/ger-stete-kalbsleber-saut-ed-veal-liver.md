## Ingredients

- calf liver
- salt
- flour
- fat
- onion
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After freeing the calf liver from its skin and cutting it into strips the thickness of a knife back and finger length, it is lightly salted, dredged on both sides in flour and fried in very hot fat.
2. If one desires onion with the liver, finely cut onion is added alongside the liver and allowed to brown with it.
3. The roasted liver is then neatly arranged on a platter, the remaining juices are soured with a little vinegar, somewhat reduced, poured over the liver and garnished with the fried onion.
4. However, the frying of the liver should only occur shortly before serving, as the liver becomes tough through longer standing.
5. This liver can be fried in its natural state, without vinegar and onion.
