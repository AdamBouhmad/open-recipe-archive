## Ingredients

- 32 deca calf liver
- a small piece of bacon, optional
- 1/4 teaspoon finely cut onion
- some green parsley
- a knife point of marjoram
- 16 deca bread crumbs
- 16 deca butter or bacon fat
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 1/4 teaspoon salt
- a knife point of white pepper
- 16 deca bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Finely scrape the calf liver and optional bacon with a knife.
2. Roast the finely cut onion with some green parsley in a small amount of fat, pressing the fat through a sieve so that the onion remains behind.
3. Add this fat to the liver.
4. Add the bread crumbs that have been soaked in water and well pressed out.
5. Add marjoram, whole egg, egg yolk, salt, white pepper, and bread crumbs.
6. Mix everything well together, cover, and let stand for 1 hour.
7. Form knödel of any desired size and treat as the preceding knödel.
