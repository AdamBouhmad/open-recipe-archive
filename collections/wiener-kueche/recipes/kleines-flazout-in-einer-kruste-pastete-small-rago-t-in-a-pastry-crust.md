## Ingredients

- Butter dough, strong knife-back thickness
- Butter
- Flour
- Dried raw peas
- Ragoût of any kind
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. Cut strong knife-back thick butter dough into squares matching the size of the sandal molds.
2. Line sandal molds brushed with butter and dusted with flour with the squares, then fill with dried raw peas, cut away the dough hanging over the edge of the mold all around, place the sandal molds on a baking sheet and bake the pastries at quite strong heat in the oven.
3. When they have a golden color, remove the molds from the oven, cover with a plate, and holding both together firmly, quickly turn the mold and carefully remove it after letting it sit for a few moments.
4. Carefully place the baked shells on a platter, remove the remaining peas inside with a fork, fill the shells with any ragoût, cover with a lid formed from the same dough as the shells and baked to match, place in a platter covered with a napkin and serve hot.
