## Ingredients

- 1 pound marzipan dough
- egg yolks, as needed
- 1 loth finely ground cinnamon
- wafer sheets

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 pound marzipan dough with as many egg yolks as required to make a mass that can be easily stretched with a knife.
2. Add 1 loth finely ground cinnamon and stir everything well together.
3. Cut various shapes from wafer sheets.
4. Spread the mass knife-back high onto the shapes and bake in moderate heat.
5. To give this confection an even more beautiful appearance, it can be glazed.
