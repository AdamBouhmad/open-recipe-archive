## Ingredients

- 2 young cleaned chickens
- veal forcemeat, chicken forcemeat or liver forcemeat
- green peas
- asparagus peas
- finely cut crayfish tails (if available)
- 8 deciliter butter
- onions
- root vegetables
- mushrooms
- whole spices
- soup

## Directions

1. Flame 2 young chickens, singe them, wash and prepare them
2. Make a forcemeat by mixing veal forcemeat, chicken forcemeat or liver forcemeat with green peas, asparagus peas and finely cut crayfish tails if available
3. Stuff the chickens with this forcemeat and dress them
4. In a casserole, place 8 deciliter butter, onions, root vegetables, mushrooms and whole spices
5. Lay the chickens inside and braise covered
6. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, pour the necessary soup over them
7. Braise covered until tender
8. Remove the chickens, make a butter sauce with the liquid, let it reduce well and remove the lard completely
9. Halve the chickens, place in a platter and serve the sauce alongside
