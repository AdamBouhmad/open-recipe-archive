## Ingredients

- veal leg
- marrow, cut
- truffles, cut (optional)
- bacon
- butter
- thinly sliced onion
- mushrooms
- approximately 1/2 liter white wine
- white soup
- salt

## Directions

1. After the leg has been trimmed, skinned and salted, lard it with cut marrow and, if desired, cut truffles and bacon.
2. Put butter, thinly sliced onion and mushrooms into a casserole, place the leg on top and pour approximately 1/2 liter white wine and white soup over it, but altogether only enough so that the liquid does not cover the meat, and braise until the meat is soft.
3. Then remove it, place it on a platter, skim the fat from the sauce and pour the strained sauce over the meat.
