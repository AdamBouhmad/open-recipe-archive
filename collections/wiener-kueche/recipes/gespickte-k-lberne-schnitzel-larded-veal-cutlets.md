## Ingredients

- veal shank
- bacon
- salt
- fat
- onion
- cloves
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- broth
- lemon juice
- lemon peel
- sardelles
- nutmeg
- mussels
- wine

## Directions

1. Cut two fingers wide and four fingers long cutlets from a veal shank, not too thick.
2. Lard them with finely cut bacon, salt them slightly, and place in hot fat.
3. Stick an onion with cloves and place it with them, let turn golden brown.
4. Take some flour and breadcrumbs, sprinkle on the cutlets, and braise until it turns brownish.
5. Pour a ladle full of broth over, add juice of half a lemon, some cut lemon peel, two finely chopped sardelles, and some nutmeg.
6. Wash fresh mussels with wine, add them, and let come to a boil. The mussels can also be shelled.
