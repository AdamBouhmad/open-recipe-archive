## Ingredients

- 12 tablespoons butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 1/2 liter semolina
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1.5 liters boiling milk
- sugar for sprinkling
- cinnamon for sprinkling (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat 12 tablespoons butter until fluffy
2. Gradually beat in 3 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs one after another
3. Mix in 1/2 liter semolina and 1/2 teaspoon salt thoroughly
4. Let this mixture rest for 1 hour so the semolina can swell
5. Using a spoon, place dumplings of desired size into 1.5 liters of boiling milk
6. Let them come to a boil covered
7. Place in the oven to give them a golden color
8. Arrange on a platter, sprinkle with sugar and optionally with cinnamon, and serve
