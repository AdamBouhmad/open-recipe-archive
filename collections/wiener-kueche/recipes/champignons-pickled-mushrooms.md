## Ingredients

- Mushrooms, both large and small
- Strongly salted water
- Sharp vinegar
- Mace
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Melted mutton fat

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, both large and small, separate them from their stems, then split them
2. Place them in boiling, strongly salted water and let them come to a boil once or twice
3. Let them drain in a colander, and also press them out
4. Make sharp vinegar hot with mace, allspice, cloves, pepper and bay leaves
5. Throw the mushrooms in and let them come to a boil
6. After everything has cooled as quickly as possible, pour it into glass jars
7. After three days, pour the purest melted mutton fat over it so that it covers the whole about several lines thick
8. Bind the glass bottles tightly with strong paper and place them in cool locations
