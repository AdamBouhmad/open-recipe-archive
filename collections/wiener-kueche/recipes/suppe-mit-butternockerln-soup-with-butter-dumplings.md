## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg shoulder and tail beef
- 5 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 2 egg whites
- 6 dkg flour

## Directions

1. To the creamed butter, stir in 2 egg yolks and salt
2. Mix in the stiff snow of 2 egg whites together with 6 dkg flour
3. Let this stand for a while
4. Form small dumplings with a spoon dipped in hot soup
5. Place them in the strained boiling beef soup and let them cook slowly for 5 minutes
6. If the dough is too loose, add more flour as needed
