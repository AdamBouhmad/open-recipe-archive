## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- juice of 1 lemon
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 ounces bread crusts, softened in milk
- 16 ounces butter, beaten
- 7 egg yolks
- 8 ounces blanched almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 5 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Put cream and lemon juice in a casserole, beat in 3 egg yolks, cover and simmer gently until curdled
2. Strain and press lightly to remove all liquid
3. Pass the curdled mixture through a sieve with the softened bread crusts
4. Beat the butter and add 7 egg yolks one by one
5. Mix in the blanched pounded almonds with the 2 egg whites and the passed bread mixture
6. Gently fold in the 5 whipped egg whites
7. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
8. Cover and steam for 1/2 hour or bake in the oven
9. Turn out into a dish, pour hot marmalade over it, and serve immediately
