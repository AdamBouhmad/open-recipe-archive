## Ingredients

- 28 dkg flour
- 21 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon rum
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 2 dkg sugar
- 5 dkg butter for filling
- 2 egg yolks for filling
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- finely chopped lemon zest
- ½ kg quark (Topfen)
- 3 dkg raisins
- 1 egg for brushing
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from 28 dkg flour, 21 dkg butter, 2 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon rum, 2 tablespoons cream, and 2 dkg sugar
2. After resting, divide into 2 parts and roll each to baking sheet size
3. Place one part on an unbuttered baking sheet and spread the filling on it
4. Sprinkle 3 dkg raisins on top
5. Place the second dough part on top and press edges together
6. Brush with beaten egg and bake at higher heat until light brown
7. Cut into pieces, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and serve cooled
8. For the filling: Beat 5 dkg butter until creamy, mix in 2 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar, finely chopped lemon zest, and strained quark
