## Ingredients

- ½ kg potatoes
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash potatoes and place in cold water
2. Cook for 25-30 minutes, drain, and cool completely
3. Peel and slice thinly
4. Mix with salt, vinegar, and oil
