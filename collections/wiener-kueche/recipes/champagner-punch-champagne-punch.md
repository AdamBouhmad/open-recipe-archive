## Ingredients

- 80 deciliters sugar in small pieces
- 1 liter coffee
- juice of 5 lemons
- 1/2 liter rum or arrack
- 1 1/2 liters champagne

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, add sugar and coffee and let it boil
2. Add the juice of 5 lemons, 1/2 liter rum or arrack, and 1 1/2 liters champagne
3. Let it heat, strain the punch through a sieve into a soup pot or bowl
4. Serve it hot
