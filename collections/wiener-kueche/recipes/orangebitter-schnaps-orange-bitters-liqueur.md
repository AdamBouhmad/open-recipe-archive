## Ingredients

- 2 Maß strong and pure corn brandy
- 4 Loth green bitter oranges
- 4 Pfund white candy sugar
- 2 spoonfuls Cremor Tartari

## Directions

1. Pour the corn brandy over the green bitter oranges, candy sugar, and Cremor Tartari
2. Seal the vessel with a bladder through which a needle is inserted
3. Place it in the sun or at a moderately warm location
4. Shake the mixture several times daily
5. Pour it off when the brandy has completely clarified
