## Ingredients

- 1 carp
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- flour
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Scale the carp well (see recipe 870)
2. Gut it, clean it well, split it, then cut it into 3-finger wide pieces
3. Make an incision on the skin side of each piece
4. Sprinkle with salt and let it stand covered in a bowl for 1/2 hour before serving
5. 1/2 hour before serving, coat the carp pieces well with breadcrumbs mixed with flour, or coat them until they are dry and fry from hot fat until light brown
6. Lay them on blotting paper so the excess fat is absorbed
7. Instead of mixing flour with breadcrumbs, you can add 1 tablespoon semolina to the flour, which makes the fish pieces crisp when frying
8. Or coat them only with flour or treat them like fried chicken
9. Serve any salad with it
