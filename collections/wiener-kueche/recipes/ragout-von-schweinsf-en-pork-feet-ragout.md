## Ingredients

- Pork feet
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Onions, finely cut
- Meat broth
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Salt
- Vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the feet in water, let them remain until cold, then cut them into pieces.
2. Place a piece of butter in a casserole, brown a little flour and finely cut onions in it, add the cut feet as well, pour good meat broth over them, season with nutmeg, pepper, whole cloves and salt, and let them cook.
3. Before serving, add two spoonfuls of vinegar or squeeze the juice of a lemon into it, and let it come to a boil once more with it.
