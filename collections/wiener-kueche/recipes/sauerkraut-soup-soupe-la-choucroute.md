## Ingredients

- 1 liter sauerkraut
- 10 ounces fat
- 1/8 liter flour
- 1 finely sliced onion
- 1 liter good beef soup
- approximately 1 liter good soup

## Directions

1. Cook 1 liter sauerkraut in water until soft
2. Place 10 ounces fat in a casserole
3. Add 1/8 liter flour and 1 finely sliced onion and roast the flour with the onion
4. Pour in 1 liter of good beef soup, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming
5. Strain the cooked sauerkraut and pass the soup through a sieve
6. Add the sauerkraut to the soup and pour in approximately 1 liter of good soup, mix very well together
