## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pfund fine noodles
- 1 Seidel cream
- milk
- 1/2 Pfund butter

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound fine noodles into 1 Seidel boiled cream and an equal amount of milk
2. Let them swell and cool
3. Beat 1/2 pound butter
