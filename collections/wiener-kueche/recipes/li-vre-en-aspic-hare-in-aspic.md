## Ingredients

- Salmis (hare ragout)
- Aspic jelly
- Truffles
- Hare back and legs

## Directions

1. Pour a finger-high layer of cooled aspic into a mold set in ice
2. Once set, cover with some truffles and pieces of hare and steamed goose liver
3. Add a finger-high layer of salmis on top
4. Layer with hare back pieces
5. Pour another finger-high layer of cooled aspic on top
6. Continue alternating layers until the mold is full
7. Pour aspic on top as the final layer
8. Cover and set in ice for 1 hour
9. Remove the mold, dip briefly in hot water, dry with a cloth, and turn out onto a platter
10. Garnish the edge of the platter with neatly cut aspic
11. Serve as an entrée or side dish
