## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 12½ dkg sugar
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 15 dkg good butter
- 4-5 Karlsbader wafers
- 8-10 dkg butter
- 10 dkg vanilla sugar
- 4 dkg grated chocolate
- 2 egg yolks
- 5 dkg grated hazelnuts
- swelled chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 3 whole eggs with 12½ dkg sugar and 5 dkg grated chocolate over steam with the whisk until thick
2. Cool and stir in 15 dkg good butter until the mass is smooth
3. Spread this cream on 4-5 Karlsbader wafers
4. The torte can be spread with cream on top and around and sprinkled with swelled chopped almonds; or make less cream and frost it brown
5. Alternatively, use a cold stirred cream: cream together 8-10 dkg butter with 10 dkg vanilla sugar, 4 dkg grated chocolate, 2 egg yolks, and 5 dkg grated hazelnuts
