## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 3 tablespoons fine powdered sugar
- 1 knife point of salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 3 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter milk
- 28 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy, add 3 tablespoons fine powdered sugar, 1 knife point of salt, 8 egg yolks and 1 whole egg one after the other, mix well with the yeast mixture and 28 deca flour, beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
2. Brush almond molds with butter, dust with flour and fill halfway with the dough, let stand covered in a warm place
3. When the dough has risen to double height, put the almond molds in the oven where the dough bakes slowly
4. When finished, turn out the almond cakes, arrange on a plate and serve sprinkled with sugar
