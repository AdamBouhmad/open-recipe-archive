## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- Sugar
- 4 lemons
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 measure Rhine wine
- 1 seidel arrack
- 1 seidel water
- 12 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Rub the peel of 2 lemons onto sugar.
2. Put this sugar, along with the juice of 4 lemons, 1 pound sugar, 1 measure Rhine wine, 1 seidel arrack, 1 seidel water and 12 whole eggs in a kettle.
3. Set it on a charcoal fire and beat it with a white whisk until the foam rises high.
4. Serve this punch in drinking glasses.
