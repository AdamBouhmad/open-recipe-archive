## Ingredients

- 1 beautiful, large goose liver
- fine bacon for larding
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- 2 pieces finely sliced mushrooms
- 5 bunches of parsley tied together
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- approximately 4 tablespoons soup
- 1 tablespoon capers

## Directions

1. Clean the goose liver as described above, lard it lengthwise with fine bacon, and dust with salt and pepper
2. Put some butter in a casserole, place the larded liver on top, cover it with a thin, sliced bacon slice, and let it braise covered
3. After 10 minutes, baste the goose liver with 1/2 liter sour cream and approximately 4 tablespoons of soup and let it braise covered for 1.5 hours
4. Remove it to a serving dish and reduce the sauce slightly, which can also have 1 tablespoon of capers added to it
5. Serve the sauce with the goose liver, garnished with steamed rice or macaroni
