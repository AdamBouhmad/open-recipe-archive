## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 4 egg yolks
- 11 dekagrams sugar
- 5 dekagrams grated nuts, hazelnuts or almonds
- 5 dekagrams flour
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Stir the 4 egg yolks, 11 dekagrams sugar, 5 dekagrams grated nuts into the stiff snow of 4 egg whites and lightly fold in 5 dekagrams flour
2. Line a baking sheet with buttered paper
3. Spread the mixture finger-high on it
4. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
5. Turn out onto a board, quickly remove the paper
6. Spread the filling on it, quickly roll up the roulade, sprinkle with sugar, chill immediately and later cut on the diagonal
