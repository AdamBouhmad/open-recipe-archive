## Ingredients

- 12 tablespoons peeled bread rolls, cut into flakes
- 0.25 liter milk
- 16 tablespoons softened butter
- 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- butter
- flour
- sugar or jam

## Directions

1. Cut 12 tablespoons of peeled bread rolls into flakes, place in a saucepan, pour 0.25 liter of milk over them and stir over the fire until it forms a thick mixture
2. Set aside and let cool
3. Meanwhile, mix 16 tablespoons of softened butter with 16 tablespoons of vanilla sugar
4. Add 10 egg yolks one after another, stirring
5. Mix this well with the cooled mixture
6. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites
7. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
8. Cover and cook 1/3 to 3/4 hours in a water bath or bake in the oven
9. Turn out onto a serving dish, dust with sugar or pour dissolved jam over it and serve at table
