## Ingredients

- Young fresh salad
- Garden cress
- Young chervil
- Fine onion tubes or small young onions
- Blue borage flowers
- Violets (March violets, cowslips, yellow and blue)
- Monthly flowers
- Bertram leaves
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Good Provençal oil
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the salad, cress, chervil, and onions together thoroughly
2. Pick the flowers and add to the salad: blue borage, March violets, cowslips, yellow and blue violets, monthly flowers, and Bertram leaves
3. Mix all together
4. Hard-boil some egg yolks, crush them very fine
5. Pour good Provençal oil, vinegar and sugar over them
6. Dress the salad with this mixture and serve at table
7. Herb vinegar can also be used, and those who dislike sugar can use salt instead
