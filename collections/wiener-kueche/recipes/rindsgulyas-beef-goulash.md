## Ingredients

- 60-80 dkg shank or rear roasting meat
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 large onion
- 2 knife-tips paprika
- cold water
- 1 piece dark bread crust
- 1 parsnip (optional)
- caraway
- marjoram
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. In a roasting pan, in 6 dkg hot fat, let 1 large onion turn dark golden.
2. Add 2 knife-tips paprika, pour in a scoop of cold water, add the washed, cut into strips then diagonal cubes, salted and peppered meat, 1 piece of dark bread crust, optionally a parsnip, some caraway, marjoram and vinegar.
3. Braise covered for 2 hours, adding water and stirring frequently.
4. Serve on a round dish. Side dishes: rotkohl, noodles, rice.
