## Ingredients

- Veal chops (cutlets)
- Flour
- Fat

## Directions

1. After the chops have been cut, the bone is held for each individual one, the rib bone is scraped, and the meat is pushed back
2. The chops are pounded flat with a meat pounder or back of a knife without damaging the meat
3. Salt them, dust with pepper if desired
4. Lightly dip both sides in flour
5. Brown both sides in very hot fat until golden
6. Cover each rib bone with a papillote
