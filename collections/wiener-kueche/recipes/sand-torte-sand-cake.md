## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 21 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon rum
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 21 dkg potato flour

## Directions

1. Beat 21 dkg butter for 20 minutes
2. Gradually stir in 21 dkg sugar and 6 egg yolks and beat again for 20 minutes
3. Mix in 1 tablespoon rum and 6 stiff egg whites together with 21 dkg potato flour
4. Fill into the form and bake very slowly
5. Fill with jam, dust with sugar, or spread jam on top and frost white
