## Ingredients

- Fresh truffles
- Onions, cut into cubes
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Good broth (Schü)
- Wine
- Poultry meat
- Finely chopped truffles

## Directions

1. Wash the truffles carefully.
2. Sauté the truffle skins with diced onions in butter.
3. Stir in a little flour and salt.
4. Fill with good broth and cook together.
5. Cut the truffles themselves into slices.
6. Fill with broth and a glass of wine.
7. Cook properly and add the first sauce.
8. Make a farce from poultry and finely chopped truffles.
9. Form small dumplings from the farce and place them in the sauce.
