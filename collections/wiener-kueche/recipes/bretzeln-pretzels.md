## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 12 dkg butter
- 3½ dkg sugar
- lemon zest
- 2 hard passed egg yolks
- egg white
- coarse sugar or chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Make a dough of 14 dkg flour, 12 dkg butter, 3½ dkg sugar, lemon zest, and 2 hard passed egg yolks on the board
2. Form into a roll and cut into equal-sized pieces
3. Shape each into a long thin roll and form into a pretzel
4. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and bake in a sharp fire until light brown
5. Once cooled, brush with egg white and dip one side in coarse sugar or chopped almonds
