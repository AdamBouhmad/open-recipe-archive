## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage
- 20 dag smoked meat
- 8 dag bacon
- 5 dag fat
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage but do not separate, wash it, place in boiling salt water and cook uncovered 4-6 minutes depending on size, remove and drain, cool it.
2. Fold back the leaves, brush with filling, press together so the head regains its former shape and wrap lightly with twine.
3. In a casserole, heat 5 dag fat, let a finely chopped onion lightly brown, lay some bacon slices, then the cabbage on top, let it braise in the oven under frequent basting with broth or water for 1 hour.
4. Remove the twine, serve the cabbage on a round dish.
5. For the filling: finely chop the smoked meat and mix with a water-soaked, well-squeezed roll, salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
