## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilo bacon, cut into strips
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 kilo cleaned truffles, cooked in Madeira wine, finely sliced

## Directions

1. Treat the capon as described above
2. In 1/4 kilo of bacon cut into strips, add the necessary salt and, if desired, some pepper, 1/2 kilo of cleaned truffles cooked in Madeira wine and finely sliced, and stuff the capon with this mixture as described above
3. Prepare the poultry in the same manner
