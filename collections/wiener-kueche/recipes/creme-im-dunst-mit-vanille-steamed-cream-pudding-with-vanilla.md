## Ingredients

- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1/2 liter very cold good cream

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan 16 deca vanilla sugar, 8 egg yolks and 1 whole egg and stir it together with 1/2 liter very cold, good cream, put it into a snow-beating bowl and beat it for some minutes over the fire with the whisk until it is lukewarm, it must not boil. Then pour it through a sieve into a deep dish and from there into any desired mold, buttered and dusted with flour, or into cups, and proceed further as indicated above (No. 1424).
