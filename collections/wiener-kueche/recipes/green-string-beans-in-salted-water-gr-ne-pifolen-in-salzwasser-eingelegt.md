## Ingredients

- Green string beans
- grape leaves
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from both sides of each bean.
2. Cover the bottom of a small barrel or earthenware dish with grape leaves.
3. Add a two-finger-high layer of beans, sprinkle with salt, and repeat until the vessel is full.
4. Cover with a lid and weight it down with a stone or similar.
5. When the beans stored in a cool place are to be used, first wash them, soak until tender, and use for salad or vegetable dishes.
