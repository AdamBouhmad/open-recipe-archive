## Ingredients

- Veal loin, finger-thick slices
- Bacon
- Fat
- Sour cream
- Soup
- Anchovies
- Mixed vegetables
- Soup or salt water

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Emperor Cutlets, but add finely cut anchovies instead of lemon juice
2. Garnish with dark and light yellow turnips and other mixed vegetables, which are cut as desired and cooked tender in soup or salt water
