## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams flour
- 18 dekagrams butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 2 spoons vinegar or wine
- some water
- vanilla cream

## Directions

1. The butter dough must be started the evening before.
2. Divide the finished butter dough into 2 parts.
3. Roll each part thinly to the size of a baking sheet.
4. Bake on the sheet and cool completely.
5. Spread vanilla cream on one part.
6. Divide the second part into equal-sized pieces and lay them close together on the cream.
7. Divide the lower part according to the cuts, place on a platter and sprinkle with sugar.
