## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 14 dkg raw almonds, half grated and half cut into thin flakes
- 6 dkg grated chocolate
- 1½ egg whites
- sugar for dusting
- icing if desired

## Directions

1. Blend 14 dkg sugar, 14 dkg raw almonds (half grated, half cut into thin flakes), and 6 dkg grated chocolate with 1½ egg whites in a wide mixing basin
2. Place on a sugar-dusted board and knead together
3. Form into a sausage, wrap in paper, and let lie for 1-2 days
4. Slice diagonally, or alternatively roll out into a square, let dry, ice white or red, and cut into narrow slices
