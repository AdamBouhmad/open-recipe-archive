## Ingredients

- peel of 1/2 pineapple
- 80 drachms sugar
- juice of 2 oranges
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1/8 liter water
- 1/2 liter good red wine
- 1/2 liter white wine
- Champagne wine
- 1/8 liter brandy or rum
- 1/4 liter maraschino

## Directions

1. After rubbing the peel of 1/2 pineapple on 80 drachms sugar, press its juice together with the juice of 2 oranges and 2 lemons
2. In a soup pot, place the sugar broken into pieces, pour 1/8 liter water to it and let the sugar dissolve
3. To this, pour 1/2 liter good red wine, the same amount of white wine, Champagne wine, 1/8 liter brandy or rum, and 1/4 liter maraschino and mix everything well
4. Fill this punch into bottles, place on ice and then serve cold
