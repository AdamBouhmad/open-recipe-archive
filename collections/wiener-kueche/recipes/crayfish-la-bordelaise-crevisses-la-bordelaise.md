## Ingredients

- 12 deciliters fresh butter
- 5 leaves each of onion, carrot, parsley, leek, celery
- 8 deciliters lean, raw meat
- 2 deciliters soup glace
- 4 spoonfuls Paradise apple purée
- 4 spoonfuls cognac
- 15 crayfish
- salt
- pepper
- 3/4 liter white Bordeaux wine

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a deep casserole
2. Arrange the onion, carrot, parsley, leek, and celery leaves on top
3. Add the lean raw meat
4. Braise everything covered together
5. Add the soup glace, Paradise apple purée, and cognac
6. Place 15 crayfish on top so they rest on their legs
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Add 3/4 liter of white Bordeaux wine
9. Cook covered until the crayfish turn red
10. Transfer the crayfish to a dish and cover to keep them warm
11. Reduce the sauce, strain it, and serve in a sauce boat with the hot crayfish
