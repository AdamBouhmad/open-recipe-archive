## Ingredients

- 7 decagrams isinglass
- 1 liter water
- 40 decagrams sugar
- 1/2 liter water for clarifying sugar
- 12 decagrams selected watercress
- Juice of 3 lemons
- 1/4 liter milk
- 1/2 liter whey, in warm state

## Directions

1. Boil 7 decagrams isinglass in 1 liter water and reduce to 1/2 liter
2. Clarify 40 decagrams sugar in 1/2 liter water
3. Pound 12 decagrams selected watercress and press through cloth
4. Add this juice and the juice of 3 lemons to 1/4 liter milk
5. Place this liquid on heat until it becomes curdled
6. Strain this through fine cloth
7. Pour the hot, clarified sugar to the boiling isinglass, and also mix in the strained whey (1/2 liter) in warm state
8. Stir everything, let run through fine linen cloth and proceed further as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
