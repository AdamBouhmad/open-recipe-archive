## Ingredients

- 40 drachm roasted meat from boned capon breasts
- 40 drachm cooked calf's udder, finely chopped
- 32 drachm bread crusts, soaked in water and squeezed dry
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
- equal amount of finely chopped mushrooms
- 3 egg yolks
- 10 drachm butter

## Directions

1. Place bread crusts in a casserole with 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley, equal amount of finely chopped mushrooms, 3 egg yolks, and 10 drachm butter
2. Cook over heat, stirring constantly, until a thick mass is formed
3. Set aside and let cool completely
4. Beat in 2 more egg yolks and stir over heat until the dough pulls away from the spoon
5. Transfer the dough to a deep bowl and set aside to cool
6. Pound this cold bread dough and the chopped meat together finely in a mortar
7. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
8. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
9. Use as forcemeat filling
