## Ingredients

- 7 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- 12 dkg grated chocolate
- 3 egg whites
- 7 dkg raw grated almonds
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dkg butter until light, then add 4 egg yolks and 7 dkg sugar gradually.
2. Mix in 12 dkg grated chocolate.
3. Fold in 3 egg whites together with 7 dkg raw grated almonds.
4. Fill the mixture into a pudding mold and cook in steam for 1 hour.
5. Loosen, cool, turn out onto a platter, and decorate with sweetened whipped cream before serving.
6. If serving warm without whipped cream, keep in steam until serving, then loosen and immediately turn out onto a porcelain platter.
