## Ingredients

- Butter, a small piece
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Eggs, as many as needed
- Egg yolks
- Sour cream (Schmette)
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Melt a small piece of butter in a saucepan and let a little flour turn yellowish in it.
2. Pour vinegar on it and let it cook in the roux.
3. Break eggs into it one beside the other without stirring them.
4. Beat a few egg yolks with sour cream, strain, add some of the sour sauce, pour it over the eggs, and let it simmer together.
5. Ensure the eggs do not become hard.
6. Before serving, dust with sugar and cinnamon.
