## Ingredients

- one orange
- 4 oranges
- 8 loth sugar
- half seidel water
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the zest from one orange very thinly and place it in a casserole
2. Squeeze the juice from 4 oranges into the casserole, mix in 8 loth sugar and half seidel water
3. Beat the whites of 5 eggs into snow and stir slowly into the casserole
4. Set it over not too strong a charcoal fire and stir the mass in it until it becomes thick
5. Pass everything through a hair sieve and let it become cold
