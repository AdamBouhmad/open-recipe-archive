## Ingredients

- duck
- brine/marinade
- lemon peel
- butter
- flour
- duck liver
- cream
- capers

## Directions

1. Pluck the duck, singe it slightly, and remove the entrails
2. Salt it well and let it lie in brine for one day
3. Lard it with lemon peel, roast it on a spit to a golden-brown color, and baste it several times with brine and cream
4. Let some butter become hot, add some flour and let it brown, then add the finely cut liver of the duck and let it stew
5. Pour in some of the brine and the drippings from the duck during roasting, along with a couple spoonfuls of cream and some capers
6. Place the duck on a dish, pour the sauce over it, and serve
