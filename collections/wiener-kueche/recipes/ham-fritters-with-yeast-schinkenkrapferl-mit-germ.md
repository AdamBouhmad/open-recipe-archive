## Ingredients

- 10 dkg cooked, passed, still warm potatoes
- 1 dkg yeast
- several tablespoons milk
- 10 dkg ham
- 1-2 anchovies
- 2 dkg Parmesan
- 14 dkg flour
- 1½ dkg melted butter
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- lukewarm milk
- egg white for brushing
- hot lard for frying
- grated cheese
- mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dkg flour on the board with the potatoes, yeast, 1½ dkg melted butter, 1 egg yolk, salt and some lukewarm milk
2. Knead smooth
3. Let the dough rise under a warm saucepan for 30 minutes
4. Roll out thinly and cut with a fritter cutter
5. Put filling in the middle, brush the edges with egg white, lay a second sheet of dough on top
6. Press the dough together
7. Place the fritters near the oven to rise
8. Fry in hot lard
9. Drain and sprinkle with grated cheese, serve with mushroom sauce
