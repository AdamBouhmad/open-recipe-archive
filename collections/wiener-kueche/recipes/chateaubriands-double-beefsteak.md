## Ingredients

- 1 skinned tenderloin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Hot fat
- 1 piece raw or cooked truffle, cut into leaves
- 4 spoonfuls Madeira wine
- Demi-glace or meat stock gravy

## Directions

1. From a skinned tenderloin, cut 4 finger-thick slices.
2. Flatten these with the flat of a knife, press lightly together so that they have the proper height and rounding.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, lightly dip both sides in flour.
4. Place in a pan containing hot fat and fry 5-7 minutes on each side, about 1/4 hour in total.
5. For each Chateaubriand, place 1 piece of raw or cooked truffle cut into leaves in a casserole, pour 4 spoonfuls of Madeira wine into it and let it boil.
6. Add some demi-glace or gravy made from 2 tablespoons of meat stock and pour this with the truffles over the Chateaubriands arranged on a platter.
