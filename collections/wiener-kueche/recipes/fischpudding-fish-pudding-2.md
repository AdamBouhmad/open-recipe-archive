## Ingredients

- ½ kg Angler or Schellfish (monkfish or haddock)
- ½ l Weißwein (white wine)
- 2 Schneidsemmeln (bread rolls)
- 2 dkg Parmesan (parmesan)
- 2 Sardellen (anchovies)
- 1 Zitrone (lemon)
- 2 ganze Eier (whole eggs)
- 5 dkg zerlassene Butter (melted butter)
- Mehl (flour)
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove bones and chop 30 dkg of the prepared fish, pass it with the bread rolls (soaked in water and well pressed out) along with 2 whole eggs, grated Parmesan, 5 dkg melted butter, salt, pepper and some flour, mixing well.
2. Remove bones from the remaining fish, cut into cubes, salt and pepper.
3. Place a layer of the filling in a well-buttered, breadcrumbed pudding mold, then a layer of fish on top, alternating filling and fish, ending with filling.
4. Cook the pudding for 1 hour in a water bath, remove, loosen, unmold, pour the following sauce over it and serve.
5. For the sauce: In 4 dkg butter, let finely chopped onion turn light yellow, add flour to make a light, light roux, pour in with water or broth, add 3 peppercorns and spice berries and the deboned, finely chopped anchovies and let the sauce cook well. Strain it, pour in the wine, bring to a boil again and finally mix in 2 egg yolks (which must first be stirred with some cold water so they do not curdle). After the binding, the sauce must not boil anymore.
