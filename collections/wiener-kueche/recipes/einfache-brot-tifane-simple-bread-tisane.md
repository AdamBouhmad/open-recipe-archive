## Ingredients

- 16 loth ordinary white bread
- half a measure water

## Directions

1. Take 16 loth of ordinary white bread
2. Cook it with half a measure of water in a well-closed earthen pot for one hour
3. Add back as much water as was lost through cooking
4. Strain through a sieve
