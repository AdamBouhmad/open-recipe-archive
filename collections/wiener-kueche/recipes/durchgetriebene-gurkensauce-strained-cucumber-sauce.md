## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Onions
- Cucumbers
- Green parsley
- Beef broth
- Vinegar
- Sour cream
- Seasonings

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from butter, flour, and onions.
2. Cut cucumbers into noodle-like pieces and add to the roux.
3. Add green parsley.
4. After this has roasted for a while, pour in beef broth, a little vinegar, and sour cream.
5. Season to taste.
6. Let cook well.
7. Pass through a sieve into the sauce boat.
