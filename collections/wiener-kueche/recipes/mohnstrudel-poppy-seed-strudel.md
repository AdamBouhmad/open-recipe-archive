## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- 8 egg yolks
- spoonful pounded sugar
- just over half seidel cream
- 3 spoonfuls good yeast (Germ)
- salt
- half mass flour
- half seidel clarified honey
- half mass finely pounded poppy seeds
- milk
- small piece butter
- finely pounded vanilla
- finely cut lemon zest

## Directions

1. Beat half pound butter fluffy
2. Beat in 8 egg yolks one by one, well stirred
3. Add a spoonful of pounded sugar, just over half seidel cream, 3 spoonfuls good yeast, some salt, and half mass flour
4. Mix everything well, beat the dough finely, and let it rise a little
5. For the filling, take a strong half seidel of clarified honey and half mass finely pounded poppy seeds
6. Place it on a gentle coal fire and add enough milk so it can be spread, also a small piece of butter, finely pounded vanilla, and finely cut lemon zest
7. When soft enough, set it aside and let it cool
8. Take the dough on a flour-dusted board, roll it two knife-backs thick
9. Spread the filling equally thick on it, roll the strudel together, place it on a butter-brushed baking sheet, let it rise well again, and bake it with sustained heat
