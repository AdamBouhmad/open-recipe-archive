## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- salt
- spices
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- fried parsley
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked tongue into finger-thick slices that are 1 finger long and 2 fingers wide
2. Dust with salt and spices, dip in melted butter and breadcrumbs, and fry on a grill
3. If no grill is available, fry quickly in a shallow pan with very hot fat about a finger-deep
4. Arrange the tongue in a star shape on a plate; place fried green parsley in the center and border the plate with lemon slices
