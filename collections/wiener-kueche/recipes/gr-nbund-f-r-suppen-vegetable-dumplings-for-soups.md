## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter, beaten
- 6 whole eggs, added one by one
- 26 Deka finely chopped, roasted or boiled meat leftovers
- some lard
- 1 knife point of white pepper
- 1 teaspoon of fresh parsley
- 14 Deka breadcrumbs
- 1/2 liter milk
- butter for greasing the cloth

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deka butter until creamy
2. Add 6 eggs one by one
3. Add the finely chopped meat leftovers with some lard, white pepper, and parsley
4. Moisten 14 Deka breadcrumbs with 1/2 liter milk and mix well into the preceding mixture
5. Butter a cloth in the middle, place the mixture on it, bind tightly with twine
6. Place in boiling salted water and cook covered for 3/4 to 1 hour
7. Remove the cloth bundle, place on a platter and serve with the soup, or cut into pieces and add to the soup
8. Garnish with small baked potato dumplings and roasted small bread pieces if serving whole, or sprinkle with finely chopped chervil if desired
