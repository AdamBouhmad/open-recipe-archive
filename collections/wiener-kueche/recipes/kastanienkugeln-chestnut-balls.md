## Ingredients

- 10 dkg cube sugar
- Water
- ½ kg chestnuts
- Grated chocolate

## Directions

1. In a small pot, cook 10 dkg cube sugar with a few spoonfuls of water until thick
2. Wash chestnuts, make a cut on the flat side
3. Cook in a pot for 1 hour
4. Strain, peel, and press through a sieve
5. Mix with the sugar to make a paste
6. Form into small balls
7. Roll in grated chocolate
8. Let dry
