## Ingredients

- softly cooked ham
- 1/4 pint Madeira wine
- equal amount of broth
- Madeira sauce
- truffles (optional)
- paper or silver foil

## Directions

1. Cut the skin carefully from the softly cooked ham
2. Remove excess fat from the ham
3. Place the ham in a suitable casserole with 1/4 pint Madeira wine and an equal amount of broth
4. Braise for about 1/4 hour
5. Cut the ham into leafy pieces and arrange them in a bowl so it looks whole
6. Serve with Madeira sauce and truffles if desired
7. Attach a paper or silver foil to the wine
