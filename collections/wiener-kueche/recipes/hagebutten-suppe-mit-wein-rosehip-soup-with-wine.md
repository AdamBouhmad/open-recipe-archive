## Ingredients

- rosehips, about 1 and a half pounds for 12 persons
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- sugar
- wine, 1 measure
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Put the rosehips in a 3-seidel pot, add a piece of cinnamon and the peel of a lemon
2. Cook until soft
3. Strain through a fine hair sieve
4. Add sugar as desired
5. Bring to a boil again with 1 measure of wine
6. Serve with toasted bread slices
