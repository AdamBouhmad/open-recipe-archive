## Ingredients

- 3-4 whole eggs
- 4 dkg ham
- salt
- butter or fat
- parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and chopped ham in a small bowl.
2. Heat a nutmeg-sized amount of butter or fat in an omelet pan until hot, pour in the mixture.
3. Break it up slightly with a fork so it can brown without burning.
4. When it is set, loosen it with a spatula, fold it together, lift it onto a plate, and garnish with parsley.
