## Ingredients

- Butter sauce
- 1 strong tablespoon mustard
- Some roasted parsley

## Directions

1. Add 1 strong tablespoon mustard with some roasted parsley to a well-simmered butter sauce
2. Let it simmer well and pass through a sieve
3. If desired, add some gherkin
