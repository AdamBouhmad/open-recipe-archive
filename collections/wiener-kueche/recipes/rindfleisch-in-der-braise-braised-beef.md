## Ingredients

- A beautiful piece of beef from the rib or shank
- Good beef fat
- 3 to 4 large split onions
- Several yellow turnips
- Parsnip
- Celery
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Soften a beautiful piece of beef from the rib or shank by cooking it
2. Heat good beef fat in a casserole
3. Add 3 to 4 large split onions, several yellow turnips, parsnip, and celery cut into pieces to the fat
4. Let them braise
5. Before serving, add the meat and a little beef broth
6. Place the meat on the serving dish and pour the vegetables together with the small amount of sauce that is present over it
