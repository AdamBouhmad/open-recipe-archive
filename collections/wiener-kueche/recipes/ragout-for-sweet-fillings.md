## Ingredients

- Calf sweetbreads
- Fricasseed meat
- Crayfish tails
- Asparagus tips
- Truffles (finely diced or cut in thin slices, as desired)
- Cooked chicken meat (roasted or braised) and some mushrooms
- Butter sauce (well-cooked)
- Wine and lemon juice (as needed)
- Egg yolks
- Cold cream
- Breadcrumbs or Parmesan cheese (if desired)

## Directions

1. Cook each ingredient separately
2. Dice the meat, crayfish tails, mushrooms, and other ingredients finely
3. Add to well-cooked butter sauce
4. Add wine and lemon juice as needed
5. When well-cooked, mix in egg yolks beaten with cold cream
6. Use as filling for baked mushrooms, pastries, baked scallops or mussels
7. For mussels: fill the ragout, sprinkle with breadcrumbs or Parmesan cheese, place on a sheet pan, bake in the oven, and serve as an appetizer or side dish
