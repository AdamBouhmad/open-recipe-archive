## Ingredients

- cooked organ meat
- onion
- butter
- egg yolks
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- water
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. When the organ meat is cooked and finely chopped with an onion, braise it in a pan with butter
2. Add a couple of chopped egg yolks, finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper, and let it cool
3. Take flour on a board, salt it a little, break a whole egg into it, add some butter, make the dough with lukewarm water, and work it by hand until it detaches itself from the board
4. Roll it out with a rolling pin and help with the hand until it is as fine and thin as possible
5. Brush the rolled out dough with a beaten egg, place the organ meat mixture here and there with a spoon as much as you need dough for one dumpling, then cut them out with a dumpling wheel, and fold the empty dough over them
6. Set water on the fire in a casserole, when it boils put the dumplings in, let them come to a boil a couple of times, remove them with a slotted spoon, and let the water drain well
7. Arrange them on a dish, carefully pour the soup over them with a spoon so none tear, sprinkle them with finely chopped green parsley, pepper and grated nutmeg, and finally with breadcrumbs browned in butter
