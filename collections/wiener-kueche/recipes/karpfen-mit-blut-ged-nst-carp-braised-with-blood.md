## Ingredients

- Carp
- salt
- butter
- onion
- wine vinegar
- pepper
- cloves
- lemon peel
- bay leaves
- pea broth
- fat
- sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Open the carp so you get the blood cleanly, scale it, and cut it into pieces like the previous recipe
2. You can also keep the bundle if the gallbladder has been removed from it
3. Sprinkle the carp with a little salt
4. Put a piece of butter and a little cut onion on the bottom of a pot, place the carp on top
5. Add a little wine vinegar, pepper, several cloves, lemon peel, and bay leaves
6. Braise the carp with these, turn the pieces once, and when it is almost braised through, add a little pea broth
7. Make a brown roux with a piece of fat, a piece of sugar, and a spoonful of flour, add it to the pot
8. Let it boil a little more with these additions
9. Before serving, add the carp blood, let it come to a boil once more, arrange the carp on a dish, and pour the sauce over it
