## Ingredients

- 8 old kipferl (croissant-like pastries)
- ½ liter milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- ½ kg apples
- raisins
- 5 dkg sugar
- 5 dkg melted butter

## Directions

1. Slice the kipferl into fine leaves, pour over with ½ liter milk beaten with 2 eggs and let stand for 30 minutes.
2. In a well-buttered casserole form, place first a layer of kipferl, then a layer of peeled apples sliced into leaves, raisins and sugar.
3. Fill the form this way, pour over with ¼ liter milk beaten with 2 egg yolks and 5 dkg sugar.
4. Drizzle with 5 dkg melted butter, bake in the oven for 30–45 minutes and serve in the form.
5. Instead of apples, pitted cherries can be used, in which case omit the raisins.
