## Ingredients

- bread rolls, sliced into leaves
- milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- salt
- 6 dkg butter or fat
- 5 dkg raisins
- sugar for sprinkling
- compote or raspberry juice for serving

## Directions

1. Slice the bread rolls into leaves and place in a bowl
2. Whisk together milk, 2 eggs, 2 tablespoons sugar, and salt
3. Pour the milk mixture over the bread and let stand for 30 minutes
4. Heat 6 dkg butter or fat in a casserole
5. Add the soaked bread and 5 dkg raisins
6. Let them stew first on the stovetop, then in the oven, stirring frequently, until they are grainy yet soft
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with compote or raspberry juice
