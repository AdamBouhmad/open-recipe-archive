## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams beaten butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 decagrams pressed yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 56 decagrams flour
- melted butter for brushing
- filling of choice

## Directions

1. Mix the beaten butter with sugar, salt, and gradually stir in the egg yolks
2. Add the pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk and the flour, stirring well together
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon, then cover and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in height
4. Using a spoon, place pieces of dough on a flour-dusted board and press them apart with floured fingers
5. Fill the center of each piece with 1 teaspoon of filling, and press the dough together over it with 4 fingers
6. Place in a butter-greased casserole, brush the sides where rolls touch with melted butter, and cover with a cloth
7. Let rise in a warm place until the dough has risen again
8. Brush with melted butter and bake for 1 hour
9. Turn out, separate the rolls from each other, arrange on a plate, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
