## Ingredients

- 3 eggs' weight butter
- 3 eggs' weight sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 eggs' weight flour
- ½ kg currants, washed and drained
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat together 3 eggs' weight butter with 3 eggs' weight sugar and 3 whole eggs gradually
2. Mix in 3 eggs' weight flour
3. Spread the batter finger-thick on a buttered, floured baking sheet
4. Top with ½ kg currants that have been rubbed and washed and drained
5. Sprinkle generously with sugar
6. Bake at higher heat until light brown
7. Cut into pieces, sprinkle with sugar, and serve cooled
