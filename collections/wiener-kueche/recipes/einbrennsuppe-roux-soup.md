## Ingredients

- finely cut onions
- several cloves
- a little caraway
- a piece of lard
- 3 spoonfuls of flour
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a very dark brown roux from a piece of lard and 3 spoonfuls of flour
2. Add finely cut onions, several cloves and a little caraway
3. When everything is very brown, pour in as much boiling water as needed
4. Salt it and let it cook well until quite thick
5. Before serving, strain it through a sieve and serve over small diced bread or house bread in a bowl
