## Ingredients

- 14 deka beaten butter
- 12 deka lemon sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 16 deka blanched, finely chopped almonds
- 1 egg white, very finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon grated chocolate
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- 8 deka flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deka beaten butter gradually with 12 deka lemon sugar and 2 egg yolks
2. Mix in 16 deka blanched finely chopped almonds, 1 tablespoon grated chocolate, 1 pinch cinnamon, and 8 deka flour
3. Roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a small finger
4. Cut into desired pieces and place on a butter-greased sheet
5. Brush with egg
6. Bake, then cover with white glaze and dry in a hot oven
7. Optionally, sprinkle the cut pieces with blanched finely cut pistachios after glazing, which gives the appearance of finely cut chives
