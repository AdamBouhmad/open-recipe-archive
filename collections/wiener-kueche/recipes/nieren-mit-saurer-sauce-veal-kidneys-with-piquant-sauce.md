## Ingredients

- horseradish, finely sliced
- vinegar
- water
- butter
- flour
- sour cream
- roasted kidneys

## Directions

1. Finely sliced horseradish is well cooked in vinegar and water.
2. Then pass it through a sieve, pour this over a roux made from butter and flour, and let it simmer with sour cream.
3. Shortly before serving, add roasted kidneys, stir everything well together and serve on a platter garnished with fried eggs or poached eggs.
