## Ingredients

- a couple of calf's sweetbreads
- 1 udder
- butter
- small fish finely chopped
- 3 egg yolks
- milk
- nutmeg
- salt
- square wafers
- egg white
- eggs, beaten
- fine bread crumbs
- lard
- white pressed soup

## Directions

1. Boil a couple of calf's sweetbreads and an udder briefly and chop finely into cubes
2. Warm a piece of butter
3. Add the sweetbreads and finely chopped fish and braise
4. Beat 3 egg yolks in a little milk
5. Whisk it well
6. Pour it over and stir in with nutmeg and salt
7. Coat square wafers with egg white
8. Put 3 spoonfuls of the ragout on each
9. Shape it nicely round like a dumpling
10. Roll in beaten eggs
11. Then roll in fine bread crumbs
12. Fry hot in lard
13. Place in the soup pot
14. Pour white pressed soup over it and serve
