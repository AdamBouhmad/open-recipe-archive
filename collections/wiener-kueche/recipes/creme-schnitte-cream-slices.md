## Ingredients

- Butter pastry dough prepared as for fruit slices
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream
- 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Prepare as fruit slices, but fill with the following cream mass instead of jam
2. Place 2 tablespoons flour in a mixing bowl with 16 deca vanilla sugar and 6 egg yolks
3. Whip this with the whisk over the fire until it becomes a thick mass
4. Remove from fire and after complete cooling, lightly fold in the snow from the 6 egg whites
