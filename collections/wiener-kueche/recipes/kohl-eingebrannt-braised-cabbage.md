## Ingredients

- 2-3 heads cabbage
- 4 dkg fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean, separate, and wash the cabbage.
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook uncovered for 25-30 minutes.
3. Drain and chop coarsely.
4. Make a roux with 4 dkg fat, add the cabbage water or broth, add the cabbage, season with salt and pepper, and cook down for 20 minutes.
