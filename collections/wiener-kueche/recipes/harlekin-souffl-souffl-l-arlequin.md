## Ingredients

- 1 Béchamel soufflé mixture
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare a Béchamel soufflé mixture
2. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with half of the mixture
3. Mix the second half of the soufflé with 2 tablets of grated chocolate and add on top
4. Cook 3/4 to 1 hour covered in a water bath or bake in the oven
5. Turn out onto a serving dish and serve with chocolate sauce, cream, or whipped cream
