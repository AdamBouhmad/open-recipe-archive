## Ingredients

- Knife-back-thick butter pastry dough
- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 12 deca fine sifted sugar
- Fine sugar for dusting
- Strawberries or currants or raspberries (optional)
- Whipped cream, sweetened and well-drained (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut 3-finger-wide strips of butter pastry dough from the knife-back-thick pastry, of desired length
2. Place on a baking sheet
3. Make zigzag marks with a knife
4. Brush with egg and bake
5. Lightly fold 12 deca finely sifted sugar into the stiff snow of 4 egg whites
6. Pipe 2 fingers high onto one part of the slices
7. Dust with fine sugar and dry in the oven for 5 minutes
8. Place another baked sheet on the dried snow and dust with sugar
9. Cut into pieces of choice and serve
10. Strawberries, currants, or raspberries can be folded under the snow
11. Alternatively, fill the slices instead of with snow with sweetened, whipped, well-drained cream, but these must not be dried in the oven, but rather served immediately
