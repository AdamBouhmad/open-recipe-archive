## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 liter ripe crushed gooseberries
- necessary broth
- some white wine

## Directions

1. Make a roux of 6 tablespoons butter with equal amount flour
2. Add 3/4 liter very ripe crushed gooseberries
3. Pour in with the necessary broth and some white wine
4. Season to taste and let it boil thoroughly
5. This sauce can be passed or served unpasseed
