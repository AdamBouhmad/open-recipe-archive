## Ingredients

- Poor and unripe fruit from bad years
- Plums with crushed stones
- Small apples that seem worthless
- Few ripe grapes
- Slightly spoiled and cut fruit
- Hips, sloe berries, rowan berries and similar
- Various spices and culinary herbs (sage, savory, lavender flowers, anise and the like) in moderate measure
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Crush all the ingredients together
2. Bring it into fermentation through heat and yeast, moderating the fermentation
3. Distill it
4. If a second distillation seems necessary, add various roots, herbs and spices according to desired flavor
