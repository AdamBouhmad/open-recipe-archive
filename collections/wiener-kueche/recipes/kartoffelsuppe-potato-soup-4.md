## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg round potatoes
- salt
- caraway
- marjoram
- water
- 5 dkg fat
- 6 tablespoons root vegetables (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the potatoes
2. Cut them into cubes
3. Set them to cook with salt, caraway, marjoram, and water for 30 minutes
4. Make a light onion roux from 5 dkg fat
5. Pour the potato water over it
6. Add the potatoes and cook well together
7. Optionally, add separately cooked root vegetables cut into cubes
