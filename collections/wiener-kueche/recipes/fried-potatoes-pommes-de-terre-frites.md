## Ingredients

- Raw potatoes
- Round cutter
- Salt
- Hot lard

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes, cut them out with a round cutter so they all have the same form, cut them into slices, salt them slightly, bake them in hot lard, remove them with a wooden spoon as soon as they have a beautiful golden color, and serve them immediately with various meat dishes. They can also be served on a napkin at tea.
