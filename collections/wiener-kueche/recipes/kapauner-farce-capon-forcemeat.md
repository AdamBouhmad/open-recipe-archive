## Ingredients

- 40 drachm raw, boned meat from capon breasts
- 32 drachm bacon
- 32 drachm bread crusts, soaked in wine and squeezed dry
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- brandied dough made from 1/2 liter milk
- salt and spices as needed

## Directions

1. Finely chop together the capon meat and bacon with a kitchen knife
2. Place in a mortar and pound together with bread crusts soaked in wine, 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, and brandied dough
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Season to taste with salt and spices
5. Use for stuffing or fill a loaf mold and cook in steam
