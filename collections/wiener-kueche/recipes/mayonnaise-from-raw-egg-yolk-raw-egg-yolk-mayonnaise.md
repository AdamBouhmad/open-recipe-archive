## Ingredients

- 6 raw egg yolks
- ¼ liter oil in ice
- juice of 1 lemon or 2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- 2 pinches pepper

## Directions

1. Place 6 raw egg yolks in a deep bowl and stir them over ice
2. Then stir in dropwise and gradually ¼ liter of ice-cold oil with the juice of 1 lemon or 2 tablespoons of vinegar
3. When vinegar and oil are well mixed, add the necessary salt and 2 pinches of pepper and let this mayonnaise stand covered on ice until use
4. This mayonnaise is served with boiled cold chicken, likewise with roasted brain and with delicate fish dishes
