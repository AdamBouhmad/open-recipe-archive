## Ingredients

- morels
- butter
- parsley
- béchamel sauce
- cream
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned morels into rings
2. Braise them in butter and parsley
3. Add some béchamel sauce with cream, salt and nutmeg
4. Mix everything well together, season and serve
