## Ingredients

- 22 drams peeled semolina rolls, cut into flakes
- 2 liters cold water
- 16 drams butter
- 5 strands saffron, grated
- salt
- spices (optional)
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Place the sliced semolina rolls in a casserole with cold water, butter, and grated saffron
2. Bring to a boil while constantly stirring
3. Season with salt and spices if desired
4. Allow to simmer
5. Mix egg yolks with 1/4 liter of cream, strain well
6. Add the egg yolk and cream mixture to the soup while constantly stirring
7. This produces a somewhat thick but flavorful soup
