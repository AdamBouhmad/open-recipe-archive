## Ingredients

- 1/2 maß milk
- 1/4 pound washed rice
- 1/4 pound lard
- 6 loth finely ground almonds
- 6 loth sifted sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 5 eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 maß milk in a pot
2. Add 1/4 pound washed rice and cook it in the milk
3. Let it simmer slowly until cooked
4. Transfer to a dish and let cool
5. Pound it in a mortar
6. Beat 1/4 pound lard in a bowl until fluffy
7. Add the rice, 6 loth finely ground almonds, 6 loth sifted sugar, lemon zest
8. Add 5 eggs and 6 egg yolks, each well beaten
9. Stir for another hour
10. Pour into a sealed ring mold
11. Sprinkle with sugar and bake slowly in a heated cake pan
