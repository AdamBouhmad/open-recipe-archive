## Ingredients

- milk
- 12 dkg rice
- vanilla
- whipped cream
- sugar
- 1 dkg gelatin
- cold water
- raspberry juice or whipped cream and candied cherries for serving

## Directions

1. Bring milk to a boil with 12 dkg rice and a piece of vanilla
2. Cook while stirring for ½ hour and cool
3. Mix with whipped cream and add sugar to taste
4. Slowly pour in dissolved gelatin (see instructions) and stir well
5. Fill into a pudding mold rinsed with cold water
6. Place in ice or in a container of cold water for 2-3 hours
7. Carefully loosen, hold the mold briefly in hot water, turn out the cream
8. Serve with raspberry juice or decorated with whipped cream, candied cherries, and stewed fruit
9. Chopped candied fruit can also be mixed with the rice
