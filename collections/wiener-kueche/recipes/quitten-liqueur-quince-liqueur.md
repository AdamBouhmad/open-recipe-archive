## Ingredients

- the largest and finest quinces
- 1 Maß brandy
- 3 halves quince juice
- 1 pound sugar per half of juice

## Directions

1. Take the largest and finest quinces
2. Peel them and remove the core house
3. Crush them in a large mortar
4. Press through a strong cloth
5. For 1 Maß brandy take 3 halves quince juice, and for each half take 1 pound sugar
6. Place everything together in a large glass, cover well
7. Let stand for 12 days
8. On the thirteenth day, filter through filter paper
9. Fill into bottles, cork well, and let stand for 6 months
