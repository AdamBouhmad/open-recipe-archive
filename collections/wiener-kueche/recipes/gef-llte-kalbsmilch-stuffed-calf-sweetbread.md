## Ingredients

- calf sweetbread
- breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- green parsley
- marjoram herb
- basil
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg
- egg yolk
- salt water

## Directions

1. The larger part of a calf sweetbread is sliced open and the interior is scraped onto a clean cutting board with a knife back.
2. To this add breadcrumbs softened in milk, some green parsley, a small sprig of stripped marjoram herb, and if desired basil and bacon.
3. Cut all of this finely together, place in a deep bowl, salt it, season with some pepper and if desired nutmeg, stir in 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk.
4. With this mixture, fill the sweetbread, sew it shut and cook gently in salt water.
5. Then remove it and either roast it at the fire or fry it with butter in a pan, then cut the sweetbread into desired slices and serve it with fine vegetables, or serve it in the soup pot.
