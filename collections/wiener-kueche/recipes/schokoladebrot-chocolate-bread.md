## Ingredients

- 11 dkg sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 14 dkg raw grated almonds
- 6 dkg grated chocolate
- 2 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
- 7 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Beat 11 dkg sugar with 4 egg yolks for 15 minutes in a wide mixing basin
2. Mix in 14 dkg raw grated almonds, 6 dkg grated chocolate, 2 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and 7 dkg flour
3. Spread in an oblong, not too wide shape on a buttered baking sheet
4. Bake in a moderately hot oven
5. Cut into slices only the next day
