## Ingredients

- 2 kilos deboned tenderloin
- 12 decagrams bacon
- 1 mushroom
- Some parsley
- 1 small head onion
- 12 decagrams diced, leafy-cut bread crusts, soaked in water and well pressed out
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- Salt
- Spices
- Flour
- Butter or fat

## Directions

1. Finely chop 2 kilos of deboned tenderloin with 12 decagrams of bacon.
2. With a chopping knife, mince 1 mushroom, some parsley, and if desired a small head onion.
3. Mix all this well together.
4. Add 12 decagrams of diced, leafy-cut bread crusts that have been soaked in water and well pressed out, then 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk, salt and spices.
5. Mix well and chop once more with the chopping knife.
6. Spread the mass on a flour-dusted board, form into thick pieces or 4 beefsteaks, making them 1-2 fingers high and of desired size.
7. Dust them on both sides with flour and fry them in very hot butter or fat for 12-15 minutes.
8. To make all beefsteaks uniform in size, you can use a cookie cutter to mark out the shape beforehand, or first form equal-sized knots from the mass from which you then shape the beefsteaks.
9. Serve with potato purée, potato hash, vegetables, rice, vegetables, or a Suprême or any piquant sauce.
