## Ingredients

- 50 deca sugar
- 1 liter cold water
- 1/2 liter sieved strawberry pulp
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve 50 deca sugar in 1 liter cold water
2. Stir in 1/2 liter sieved strawberry pulp and freeze as described in Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720)
3. Meanwhile beat 4 egg whites to stiff peaks
4. Add this to the freezing bowl with the frozen strawberries, mix well with the mixture
5. Tablet this so it does not become grainy and let it finish freezing covered
