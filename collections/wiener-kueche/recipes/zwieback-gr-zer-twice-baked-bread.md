## Ingredients

- 16 Deca flour
- 15 Deca finely sifted sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- blanched, finely cut almonds
- butter for coating the baking sheet

## Directions

1. On a board, work together 16 Deca flour, 15 Deca finely sifted sugar, 1 whole egg, and 1 egg yolk to form a dough.
2. Shape this dough into a long roll and place it on a buttered baking sheet.
3. Brush with crushed egg white and sprinkle with blanched, finely cut almonds if desired.
4. Bake in a moderate oven for 1/2 to 3/4 hour.
5. Remove the roll, cut it into pieces as thick as the back of a knife, and dry these pieces in a moderately warm oven.
