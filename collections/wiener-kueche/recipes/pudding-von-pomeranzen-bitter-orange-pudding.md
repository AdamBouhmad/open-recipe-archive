## Ingredients

- 8 soft-cooked fresh bitter oranges
- 2 pounds finely cut white bread, soaked in cream
- 1/2 pound sweet almonds
- 2 pounds finely crushed sugar
- 1/4 pound finely cut citron
- 1 pound butter
- 18 egg yolks
- 12 eggs (for snow)
- wine sauce with red wine and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Press 8 soft-cooked fresh bitter oranges through a hair sieve.
2. Stir in 2 pounds finely cut white bread that has been softened in cream, 1/2 pound sweet almonds, and 2 pounds finely crushed sugar, along with 1/4 pound finely cut citron.
3. Beat 1 pound butter with 18 egg yolks into a foam, then gradually fold in the mass, and finally the snow from 12 eggs.
4. Cook the pudding in the usual manner and serve with a wine sauce made from red wine and cinnamon.
