## Ingredients

- mutton from the lower part of the back
- thin slices of ham
- bacon strips seasoned with pepper and shallots
- thin twine
- paper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Carefully loosen the skin from the lower part of the back with a knife so it remains whole and attached at the bottom
2. Lay some thin slices of ham and bacon strips seasoned with pepper and shallots on it so the entire back is covered
3. Draw the skin back over it
4. Wrap it with thin twine
5. Wrap the back with paper
6. Roast it on a spit
7. When it is almost done roasting, remove the paper
8. Sprinkle it with breadcrumbs
9. Let it roast completely until tender
10. You can make a brown sauce with capers and olives, or a ragout to serve with it
