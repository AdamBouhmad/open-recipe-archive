## Ingredients

- 7 egg whites
- 25 dkg sugar
- 25 dkg softened, finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 7 egg whites with 25 dkg sugar over steam until thick
2. Spread softened, finely chopped almonds on a pastry board
3. Take small pieces of the beaten mixture, roll them in almonds
4. Form into small crescents
5. Place on buttered sheet
6. Bake at low heat until light brown
