## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Butter or some veal broth
- White flour
- Egg yolk
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the crayfish and remove the fleshy tail pieces, setting them aside
2. Pound the rest, bring it with butter or some veal broth to the fire
3. Pass it finally through a sieve and bind the passed-through mixture with some white flour stirred in, one egg yolk, a little lemon juice
4. Season gently with salt and let the prepared crayfish tail pieces become warm in this sauce
5. Then fill them into the patties
