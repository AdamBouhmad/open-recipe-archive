## Ingredients

- 0.5 seidel ordinary, somewhat strong tea
- 0.5 seidel milk
- 6 egg yolks
- cold milk
- Syrop Capilaire

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 seidel ordinary, somewhat strong tea with 0.5 seidel milk
2. Beat 6 egg yolks with some cold milk
3. Stir the beaten egg yolks into the tea mixture
4. Sweeten sufficiently with Syrop Capilaire
5. Serve hot
