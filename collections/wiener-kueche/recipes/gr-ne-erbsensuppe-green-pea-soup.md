## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter green or yellow peas
- 1 pair sausages
- salt
- baking soda
- 5 dkg fat
- onion

## Directions

1. Sort and wash the peas
2. Place to cook with twice the height in cold water and a little baking soda for 1¼ hours, stirring occasionally
3. Drain the peas
4. Add to a light onion roux made from 5 dkg fat
5. Pour the pea water over it
6. Cook well together and press through a sieve
7. Return to the casserole and season with salt
8. A few minutes before serving, add the washed sausages, cut into slices, and let them cook together
9. Serve with bread slices
