## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo calf forcemeat
- spinach juice to color green
- cochineal to color red
- white part left uncolored
- salted water
- lard

## Directions

1. Divide the forcemeat into 4 equal parts.
2. Color one part green with spinach juice, another part red with cochineal, and leave the third part white.
3. Form walnut-sized knödel from each of these parts.
4. Before serving, cook the green, red, and white knödel in salted water for 15 minutes.
5. Fry the knödel made from the fourth part of the forcemeat in lard until they have a beautiful golden color.
6. Add all the knödel to a soup pot and serve with white, hearty soup.
7. Note: Cochineal can be replaced with 1 teaspoon grated carrot.
