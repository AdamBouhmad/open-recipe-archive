## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound sago
- water
- mass milk
- seidel cream
- cinnamon
- quarter pound fresh butter
- equal amount sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago, cook it several times with water, and cool it on a hair sieve with fresh water
2. Heat a mass of milk and a seidel of cream with some cinnamon and add the sago to it so it swells and cooks well
3. Watch carefully as it easily sticks to the bottom
4. When well swollen and short, stir in quarter pound fresh butter and an equal amount sugar and let it cool
5. Stir in 12 egg yolks and the whites in the same way
