## Ingredients

- 1 large head of cabbage
- ½ kg rice
- 3 dkg Parmesan
- 3 dkg butter
- 2 dkg butter or fat
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and stalks from the cabbage, wash and cut into noodle-like pieces
2. Heat 3 dkg butter in a casserole, add the cabbage, salt it and roast under frequent stirring and adding very little broth or water for 30 minutes
3. Braise 8 dkg rice with a scoop of water, salt and 2 dkg butter or fat covered, without stirring much, until soft for 30 minutes
4. Mix the grated Parmesan with the cabbage and rice, put into the soup pot and pour the strained beef broth over it
