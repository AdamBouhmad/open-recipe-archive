## Ingredients

- Root vegetables, leafed-sliced
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- Pinch of thyme
- 5 decagrams hot fat
- Fish head, halved
- Salt water
- Vinegar
- Dark roux of 5 decagrams butter or fat
- Beuschel (fish offal), cooked separately 15-20 minutes
- Bread slices for serving

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and slice the root vegetables into leaves
2. In a pot, roast the root vegetables with 1 onion, 5 peppercorns and spice berries, 1 bay leaf, and a pinch of thyme in 5 decagrams hot fat until dark
3. Wash the halved fish head and cook it in salt water
4. Pour this water over the roasted root vegetables
5. Season with vinegar and stew for 1-1¼ hours, stirring frequently
6. Pass the mixture through a sieve
7. Add a dark roux made from 5 decagrams butter or fat
8. Season with salt and vinegar
9. Add the beuschel which has been cooked separately in a pot for 15-20 minutes
10. Cook well together
11. Serve with bread slices
