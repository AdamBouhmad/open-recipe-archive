## Ingredients

- pears
- cold water
- lemon juice
- sugar
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, cut them in half, and remove the kernels.
2. Place immediately in cold water with lemon juice to keep them white.
3. Cook them in syrup until very soft, also adding some lemon juice.
4. Once the syrup is cooked down to short, arrange the pears on the compote dish and pour the syrup over them.
