## Ingredients

- 8–10 mushrooms
- 3 bread rolls
- 5 dkg hot butter
- parsley, finely chopped
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- ½ egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms.
2. Cut out the stems and hollow out the heads.
3. Finely chop the stems and the scooped-out flesh.
4. Stew in hot butter with finely chopped parsley for a few minutes.
5. Mix with breadcrumbs, salt, and beaten egg.
6. Fill the mushroom heads with this mixture.
7. Place in a buttered omelet pan, add a little soup, and place in the oven for 10–12 minutes.
8. Meanwhile, cut thick bread slices and fry them brown in hot lard.
9. Place on a platter, set one mushroom on each crouton, and garnish with parsley.
