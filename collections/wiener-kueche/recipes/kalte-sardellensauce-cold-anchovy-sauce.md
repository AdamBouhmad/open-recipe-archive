## Ingredients

- 2-3 anchovies
- 3 hard egg yolks
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- mustard
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the anchovies
2. Press the anchovies with 3 hard egg yolks through a sieve
3. Mix with some water, vinegar, oil, mustard, and salt to taste
4. The sauce can also be served with cold meat
