## Ingredients

- Pike
- salt
- butter
- lemon juice
- Pike liver

## Directions

1. After the pike is cleaned and well salted, thread it lengthwise onto a spit
2. Roast it very slowly over fire, brushing it frequently with butter, and continually baste it with lemon juice
3. Meanwhile, take out the liver, brush it with butter, and fry it on both sides on the grate in its juices
4. When the pike is roasted, place it on a serving dish, insert the liver into its mouth, pour melted butter over it once more, and serve it at table
