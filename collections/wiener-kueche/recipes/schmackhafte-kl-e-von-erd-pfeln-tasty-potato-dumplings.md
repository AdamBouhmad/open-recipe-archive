## Ingredients

- Not quite fully cooked potatoes, quantity as desired
- Flour, one-sixteenth the weight of the potatoes
- Salt
- Herb seasonings
- Pepper
- Grated pickled beef or pickling
- Fried bread cubes, grated or pounded fine

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes finely and mix with flour equal to one-sixteenth their weight, salt, herb seasonings, and a little pepper
2. Add grated pickled beef or pickling and some fried bread cubes, grated or pounded fine
3. Form dumplings from this mixture
4. Cook them in water until they float on the surface
