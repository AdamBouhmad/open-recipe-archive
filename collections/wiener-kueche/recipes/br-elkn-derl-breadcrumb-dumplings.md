## Ingredients

- 4 bread rolls
- water
- 5 dkg butter or fat
- 1 egg
- salt
- parsley
- 12-15 dkg breadcrumbs
- salt water

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from bread rolls and place in water
2. After about 5 minutes, remove and squeeze out very well
3. Cream 5 dkg butter or fat and stir in 1 egg, salt, finely chopped parsley, the bread rolls, and about 12-15 dkg breadcrumbs
4. Let stand for a while
5. Form small dumplings
6. Place in a casserole in boiling salted water
7. Cook slowly for 5 minutes
