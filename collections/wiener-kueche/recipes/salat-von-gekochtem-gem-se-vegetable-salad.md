## Ingredients

- Various cooked vegetables
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook various types of vegetables in salt water until soft
2. Drain the vegetables
3. Add vinegar, oil, some pepper and finely chopped fresh parsley
4. Mix everything well just before serving
5. Optional: mix 2 hard-boiled egg yolks with 4 tablespoons oil and necessary vinegar for better flavor
