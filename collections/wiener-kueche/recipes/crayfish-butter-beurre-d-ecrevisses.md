## Ingredients

- 15 crayfish cooked in salted water
- 20 teaspoons butter

## Directions

1. From 15 crayfish cooked in salted water, remove the meat from the shell and pound the red shell very finely with 20 teaspoons of butter
2. Place this in a casserole and let it roast somewhat
3. Pour the roasted mixture through a sieve laid over a bowl with cold water and push it through so the butter becomes cold
4. When it is stiff, remove it from the cold water, give it a desired form and use it as needed
