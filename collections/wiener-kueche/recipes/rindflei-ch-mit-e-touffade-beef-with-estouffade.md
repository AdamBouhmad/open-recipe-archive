## Ingredients

- a good piece of beef from the tenderloin
- spice and fine spice herbs
- salt
- bacon, sliced
- a piece of ham
- four small onions
- yellow turnips
- parsley roots
- celery
- lemon, sliced
- seasonings as desired
- four slices of baked bread
- half a measure of red wine
- one measure of good vinegar
- black dough for sealing

## Directions

1. Beat a good piece of beef from the tenderloin thoroughly
2. Mince spices with fine spice herbs, cut holes in the meat and insert the mixture
3. Salt it
4. In a cooking vessel place sliced bacon, a piece of ham, four small onions, yellow turnips, parsley roots, celery, sliced lemon, seasonings, and about four slices of baked bread
5. Lay the meat in the vessel, pour over it half a measure of red wine and one measure of good vinegar
6. Cover and seal the vessel around the rim with black dough
7. Place it on coals and let it steam gently for about six hours
8. Plate the meat and strain the sauce through a sieve over it
