## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar, clarified
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Wine of choice, instead of wine and fruit juices

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
2. Instead of orange and lemon juice, use wine of choice
