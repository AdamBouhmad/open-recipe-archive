## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram nuts
- 1/2 liter whipped cream
- 7 dekagrams sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 7 dekagrams grated nuts
- 2 egg whites
- 4 dekagrams breadcrumbs
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream together 7 dekagrams sugar with 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs until foamy
2. Stir in 7 dekagrams grated nuts and with 2 egg whites at the same time 4 dekagrams breadcrumbs
3. Spread the mixture finger-thick on a buttered, floured baking sheet
4. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
5. Cut into larger rectangles while still warm and cool
6. Join every 2 together with sugared whipped cream, either sprinkle with sugar or coat with white glaze and decorate with half nuts
