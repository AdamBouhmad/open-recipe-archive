## Ingredients

- 20 pieces sweet almonds
- 5 pieces bitter almonds
- Cream

## Directions

1. Blanch 20 sweet almonds and 5 bitter almonds
2. Grind with some cream
3. Treat further as in the Hazelnut Cream recipe
