## Ingredients

- 7 egg yolks
- 16 deca sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 12 deca semmel crumbs
- small fruits such as gooseberries

## Directions

1. Beat 7 egg yolks with 16 deca sugar for 1/4 hour
2. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 6 egg whites with 12 deca semmel crumbs
3. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Allow desired small fruits, such as gooseberries and the like, to sink into the dough
5. Treat as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
