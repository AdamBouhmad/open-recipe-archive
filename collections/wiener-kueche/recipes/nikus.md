## Ingredients

- 2 bottles Pontac
- 1 seidel water
- 2 lemons
- 1 pound sugar
- Grated nutmeg
- 3 loths Bishop essence

## Directions

1. Put 2 bottles Pontac, 1 seidel water, the rubbed-off zest and juice of 2 lemons, 1 pound sugar and a little grated nutmeg in a tureen.
2. Set on charcoal fire until a white foam appears.
3. Add 3 loths of Bishop essence.
4. Let it steep a while longer.
5. Serve in customary punch glasses.
