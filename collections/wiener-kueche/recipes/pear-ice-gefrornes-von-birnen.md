## Ingredients

- 30 ripe bergamot or butter pears
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel about 30 ripe bergamot or butter pears
2. Cut into 4 pieces
3. Remove the cores
4. Cook with some water to a marmalade
5. Pass through a fine sieve into a terrine
