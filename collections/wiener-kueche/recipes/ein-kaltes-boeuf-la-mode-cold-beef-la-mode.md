## Ingredients

- A large piece of lean beef
- Wine vinegar
- Water
- Onions
- Aromatic herbs
- 4 veal or beef feet, finely chopped (if the meat weighs 18 to 20 pounds)

## Directions

1. Lard and season a large piece of lean beef
2. Place it in a deep pot or braising vessel
3. Cover the meat halfway with wine vinegar and water
4. Add onions and aromatic herbs
5. If the meat weighs 18 to 20 pounds, add 4 veal or beef feet finely chopped
6. Cover well and braise slowly in an oven, or place in a baking oven overnight until tender
7. Remove the meat and strain the sauce
8. Clarify the sauce and let it aspic
9. Slice the meat and chop the aspic
10. Pour the aspic over the meat and serve cold for supper
