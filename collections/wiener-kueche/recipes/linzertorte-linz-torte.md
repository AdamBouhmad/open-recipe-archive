## Ingredients

- Three-quarters pound butter
- Three-quarters pound sugar
- Three-quarters pound flour
- Three-quarters pound almonds
- Zest of one lemon
- Rheinwein
- Preserved fruit, such as cherries, apricots, etc.
- Egg white foam

## Directions

1. Take butter, sugar, flour, and almonds, three-quarters pound each
2. Also take the zest of one lemon, pound the almonds with a little Rheinwein, scatter the flour on the pastry board, and make a dough
3. Brush paper with butter, roll out a piece of the dough finger-thick on it, and cut out a base
4. Spread preserved fruit on it as desired, such as cherries, apricots, etc.
5. From the remaining dough that is rolled out, make a lattice over the torte, brush this with egg white foam, and bake for a good hour
