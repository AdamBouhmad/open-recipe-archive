## Ingredients

- Beef from the tail shank
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter
- Vinegar
- Water
- Onion
- Capers
- Lemon peels

## Directions

1. Take beef from the tail shank kept in a cool place (cold kitchen), lard it, salt it, season with pepper
2. Coat thickly with flour and then brown it with butter on both sides until the flour is brown
3. Place it in a casserole and add vinegar and water
4. Add onion, capers, and lemon peels
5. Let it cook until the meat is tender and the sauce is reduced and thickened, then serve
