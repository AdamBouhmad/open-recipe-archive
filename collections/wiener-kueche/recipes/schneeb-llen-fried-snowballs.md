## Ingredients

- 32 deka flour
- 20 deka butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- salt to taste
- 1/16 liter milk
- wine or a few tablespoons rum

## Directions

1. Put 32 deka flour and 20 deka butter on a board, crumble these together with both hands
2. Make a hollow in the middle and add 3 egg yolks, 1 teaspoon sugar, some salt, 1/16 liter milk, and wine or rum
3. Work this into a dough like for strudel, which must have the same consistency, otherwise the snowballs will not rise properly
4. When the dough is sufficiently worked so that blisters become visible, let it rest in a cool place
5. Then form small balls from it, roll these out knife-back thick, and either cut round discs with a large round cutter or make them with a needle
6. Cut 5 lines through these at equal distance, finger-width apart, but so that the edge remains whole
7. Then grasp every other strip with a spoon handle, push the dough together on the stick, and draw the first grasped strip over the others, so that it now lies under the stick at the end, whereby a kind of loaf is formed
8. Hold this in a saucepan with very hot fat and pull out the spoon handle only when the dough remains together in the intended form
9. When the snowballs are baked light brown under frequent shaking of the saucepan on a baking sheet, turn them and bake them until done
10. Put them on baking paper and from there coat them in cinnamon and sugar or as desired
11. When serving, any desired marmalade can be served with it
