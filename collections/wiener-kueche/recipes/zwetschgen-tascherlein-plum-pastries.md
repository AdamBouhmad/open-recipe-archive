## Ingredients

- noodle dough
- flour
- plums
- salted water
- toasted bread crumbs
- nuts
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Hopfen Tascherlein, using plums instead of hop filling
2. Alternatively, instead of coating with toasted bread crumbs, sprinkle with finely ground nuts mixed with cinnamon and bread crumbs
3. Mix well and serve
