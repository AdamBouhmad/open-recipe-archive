## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound sugar
- zest of one lemon
- 15 eggs

## Directions

1. First stir butter to foam
2. Add one egg yolk after another
3. Mix sugar and flour together
4. Finally fold in the peaks of 3 eggs
5. Bake the tart slowly in a form that has a hole in the middle
