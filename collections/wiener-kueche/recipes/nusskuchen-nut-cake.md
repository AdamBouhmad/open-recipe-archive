## Ingredients

- 10 dkg butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 14 dkg sugar
- 14 dkg flour
- 10 dkg grated nuts
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 2 whole eggs, 2 egg yolks, 14 dkg sugar, and 14 dkg flour into the beaten 10 dkg butter.
2. Fill the mass into the buttered, floured small cake form.
3. Stir 10 dkg sugar, 10 dkg grated nuts, and 2 egg whites into a paste.
4. Spread this on the dough and bake slowly.
5. Serve with coffee or tea.
