## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- 3 slices chocolate (Zelten)
- quarter pound blanched almonds, finely cut
- melted butter
- 5 spoonfuls good cream (Obers)

## Directions

1. Make a strudel dough, draw it out finely, and let it dry
2. Grate 3 slices chocolate, finely cut quarter pound blanched almonds
3. Pour some spoonfuls of melted butter over the dough, sprinkle it with the chocolate and almonds
4. Pour 5 spoonfuls of good cream over it, roll it together
5. Pour a good inch of cream into a ring mold, place the strudel in it, apply heat above and below, and let it bake beautifully crisp
