## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter groats
- 2 whole eggs
- 24 tablespoons butter
- 4.5 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 liter boiling water

## Directions

1. In a casserole, add 1/2 liter of groats and 2 whole eggs and stir on a hot plate with a spoon until it is dry.
2. Add 24 tablespoons butter and 4.5 teaspoons salt to 1/2 liter of boiling water.
3. Loosen the groats with this and stir constantly for approximately 3 minutes over strong heat.
4. Remove from heat and let stand covered for 5 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, coat a ring mold generously with butter, press the groats into it, place it uncovered in the oven where it must bake slowly for 1 hour.
6. Before serving, turn the groats out onto a platter, pour some hot butter over it and garnish it like the Kugelhupf with bratwurst and fried goose liver.
7. Serve very hearty brown broth or sauerkraut soup with it.
