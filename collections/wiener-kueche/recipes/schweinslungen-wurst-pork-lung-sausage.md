## Ingredients

- Pork lung or offal, as much as needed
- Calf offal and mesentery, optional for multiple sausages
- Fatty pork or fat
- Water for cooking
- Four bread rolls (Semmeln) per two offal pieces and a couple pounds of meat
- Three to four onions
- A piece of garlic
- One lot powdered marjoram
- One lot pepper
- Salt
- Pork intestines for casing

## Directions

1. Precook the pork lung or offal in water until not quite soft
2. Remove all glands and cartilage carefully
3. Cut everything into small pieces and chop together to desired fineness
4. Cut four bread rolls into pieces and place them in the broth in which the meat was cooked
5. Once the bread rolls are soft, add them to the mixture along with three to four finely cut onions, a piece of finely cut garlic, one lot powdered marjoram, one lot pepper, and salt well
6. Mix thoroughly and add one measure of the aforementioned broth to thin
7. Mix again and fill into cleaned pork intestines
8. Do not fill too full, tie closed, place in boiling water
9. Leave in the water until slightly set but not fully cooked, approximately a quarter hour
10. Remove and store for future use
