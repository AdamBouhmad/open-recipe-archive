## Ingredients

- A couple of floury potatoes
- Wine
- Meat broth
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- Fine spices
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes raw and cook them in wine until soft enough to press through smoothly.
2. If the sauce is too thick, add some meat broth.
3. Season with sugar, lemon juice, and fine spices.
4. Add a piece of butter.
5. Let everything cook together.
