## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt water
- lemon juice
- butter sauce
- white wine or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as mentioned above, but cut the leaves more, closer to the white flesh, and cook them until soft as above
2. Before serving, pour strong butter sauce over them, which has been cooked with lemon juice or good white wine
