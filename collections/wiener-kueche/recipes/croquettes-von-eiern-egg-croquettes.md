## Ingredients

- 12 hard-boiled, shelled, thinly sliced eggs
- 3/4 liter thick béchamel sauce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Flour for dusting
- Eggs for dipping
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the hard-boiled eggs with the thick béchamel sauce
2. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. After completely cooling, treat as the Ragout Croquettes
4. Dust with flour, dip in beaten eggs, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in hot lard
