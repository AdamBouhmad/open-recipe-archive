## Ingredients

- 1½ dl boiling milk
- 6 dag butter
- 6 dag sugar
- 1 dl strong black coffee
- 12 dag flour
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 dag sugar
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rapidly stir the butter, sugar, coffee, and flour into the boiling milk
2. Remove from heat and stir until the dough is cold
3. Fold in the egg yolks, sugar, and beaten egg whites
4. Fill the mixture into a pudding mold and let it set in a water bath for 1 hour
5. Turn out, cool, coat with the following crème, and serve
