## Ingredients

- pancakes
- 3 large mushrooms
- 4 dkg hot butter
- chopped parsley
- some broth or water
- salt
- lemon juice
- some cream
- ½ l milk
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and finely chop the mushrooms
2. In 4 dkg hot butter, lightly fry chopped parsley
3. Add the mushrooms and pour in some broth or water
4. Add salt, lemon juice, and a few tablespoons cream
5. Let stew for 8-10 minutes (should not be very wet)
6. Spread the mushroom mixture on the pancakes and roll
7. Place in a buttered dish
8. Pour milk beaten with 1 egg yolk over
9. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes
