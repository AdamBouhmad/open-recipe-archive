## Ingredients

- 15 apricots
- 1 kilo sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 liters white wine or Rhine wine
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel 15 apricots, divide them in half, cut one-third into slices, scatter these with sugar and set on ice.
2. Remove the kernels from the apricot pits, crush them, and place them with the remaining apricots in a bowl and pour over with 1 kilo sugar which has been well boiled in 1/2 liter water, let sit covered for 1/2 hour.
3. After this time, strain it, pour it with 2 liters white wine or better Rhine wine and the juice of 1 lemon, mix in the cut apricots and let sit on ice in a deep porcelain or glass bowl for approximately 4 hours.
