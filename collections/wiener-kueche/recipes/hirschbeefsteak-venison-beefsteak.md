## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram venison filet
- 5 dekagrams bacon
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- hot fat
- butter
- parsley
- Maitre d'hôtel butter

## Directions

1. Remove skin, bones and fat from the filet and cut into two-finger-wide pieces.
2. Lightly pound with the back of a knife.
3. Lard the pieces.
4. Place on a plate, drizzle with melted butter, cover with paper dipped in butter and let stand until ready to cook.
5. Just before cooking, salt and pepper them.
6. Coat one side with flour.
7. Place flour-side down in a flat pan with very hot fat.
8. Quickly brown on both sides for 4-5 minutes.
9. Arrange on a long platter.
10. Place a piece of Maitre d'hôtel butter on each beefsteak.
11. Garnish with curly parsley and serve the drippings alongside.
