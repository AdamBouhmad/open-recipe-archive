## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 16 drams vanilla sugar
- 8 egg whites
- 14 drams fine wheat flour
- unblanched almonds for greasing the baking sheet
- fine powdered sugar for dusting
- 1 egg white
- 1 tablespoon fine sifted sugar
- 1 tablespoon fine powdered almonds
- candied peel for filling

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 16 drams vanilla sugar until pale
2. Fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 8 egg whites mixed with 14 drams fine wheat flour
3. Using a pastry bag or syringe, pipe the mixture onto a baking sheet greased with unblanched almonds in biscuit-like shapes
4. Dust finely with powdered sugar and bake at low heat in the oven until pale yellow
5. Remove from the baking sheet with a thin knife and place on a sieve
6. Alternatively, form heaps of any desired size from this mixture, dust with fine powdered sugar, and bake at low heat until pale yellow
7. When cooled completely, pair 2 and 2 together, brushing between them with warm candied peel
8. The stiffly beaten snow of 1 egg white mixed with 1 tablespoon fine sifted sugar and 1 tablespoon fine powdered almonds can be used to fill the biscuits instead of candied peel
