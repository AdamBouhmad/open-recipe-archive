## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- bacon strips
- bread (semmeln)
- lard (schmalz)
- good broth
- red wine
- woodcock entrails
- green parsley
- onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the woodcocks and remove and reserve the entrails
2. Wrap the woodcocks in bacon strips and roast them
3. Cut out the finest pieces from the breast
4. Pound the rest of the woodcocks together with bread cut into pieces that have been fried in lard in a large mortar until it becomes like a thick sauce
5. For two woodcocks use somewhat less than one whole bread loaf
6. Pass it through a fine hair sieve into a casserole
7. Fill it half with good broth and the other half with red wine
8. Cook until it becomes like a thick sauce
9. Meanwhile finely chop the woodcock entrails and add a little green parsley and very little finely cut onion
10. Sauté with butter
11. Cut large triangles from a bread loaf, fry in lard, and coat thickly with the sautéed mixture
12. Lay the breast pieces of woodcock on the bottom of the dish
13. Pour the salmi over it
14. Arrange the coated bread triangles in neat order around the rim of the dish
