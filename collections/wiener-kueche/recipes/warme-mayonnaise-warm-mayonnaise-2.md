## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 spoonful vinegar
- 1 spoonful cold water
- 1 spoonful lemon juice
- mustard
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. In a double boiler, whisk 4 egg yolks with 1 spoonful vinegar, 1 spoonful cold water, and 1 spoonful lemon juice over heat until thick
2. Cool and mix with mustard, salt, vinegar, and oil
3. If the mayonnaise curdles, slowly add 1 spoonful of cold water at the edge while stirring constantly
