## Ingredients

- Roasted, ground coffee
- Roasted, crushed hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of roasted, ground coffee and roasted, crushed hazelnuts.
2. For 1 liter of water, use 1 tablespoon of this mixture, covered in boiling water for a few minutes only.
3. Add several spoonfuls of cold water and let the coffee settle on the side until the grounds have settled to the bottom.
4. Pour the clear coffee into the pot, place this in hot water, and serve the coffee with cream and sugar.
5. Figs, almonds, and chicory coffee are additions that can be added to ordinary coffee.
