## Ingredients

- 32 deca beef liver
- 16 deca bread crumbs
- some finely cut green parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon onion
- 8 deca bacon fat or butter
- a knife point of ground pepper
- salt
- 18 deca broth
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a pinch of marjoram

## Directions

1. Finely chop the beef liver.
2. Place the bread crumbs that have been soaked in water and well pressed out in a deep bowl with some finely cut green parsley.
3. Roast the onion in 8 deca bacon fat or butter, then press this fat through a sieve so the onion remains behind and add to the liver.
4. Add ground pepper, salt, broth, flour, and marjoram.
5. Mix everything well together, cover, and let stand for 1/4 hour.
6. Form knödel of any desired size.
7. Treat as with the preceding knödel.
