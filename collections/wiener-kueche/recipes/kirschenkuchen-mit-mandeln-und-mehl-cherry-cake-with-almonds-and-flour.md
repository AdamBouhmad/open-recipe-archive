## Ingredients

- 28 deca butter
- 24 deca caster sugar
- 8 deca peeled almonds
- juice of one lemon
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 28 deca flour

## Directions

1. Mix 28 deca butter with 24 deca caster sugar until well combined
2. Add 8 deca peeled almonds, finely ground with lemon juice, and 8 egg yolks
3. Stir for 1/2 hour
4. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 8 egg whites with 28 deca flour
5. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
6. Further treatment is as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
