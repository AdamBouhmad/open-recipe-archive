## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter millet
- water
- 12 decagrams hot butter
- onion
- lard cracklings

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 liter washed millet in a casserole
2. Pour so much cold water over it that it comes finger-high above the millet
3. Cook covered until soft and thick
4. When no liquid remains, pour 12 decagrams hot butter over it, in which onion has been roasted if desired
5. Let it stand covered over low heat
6. Put the Sterz on a dish, sprinkle with lard cracklings and serve hot
