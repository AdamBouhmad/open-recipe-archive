## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salted water
- Fresh water
- Beautiful yellow head lettuce
- Wine vinegar
- Provençal oil
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the asparagus as usual and tie it in bundles
2. Cook it until tender and drain the water off completely
3. Arrange it on a serving dish so that the heads point inward and the asparagus becomes completely cold
4. Take beautiful yellow head lettuce, wash it clean
5. Arrange it star-shaped in the dish with the asparagus
6. Salt it and pour over it a mixture of good wine vinegar with Provençal oil about a quarter hour before serving at table
