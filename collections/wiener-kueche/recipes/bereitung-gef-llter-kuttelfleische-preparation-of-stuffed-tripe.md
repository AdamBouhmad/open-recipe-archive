## Ingredients

- tripe
- water
- onion
- green parsley
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the well-cleaned and cut into larger pieces tripe in the necessary water completely tender.
2. Then remove it, place it in an earthenware casserole where onion, green parsley with some pepper and salt have previously been roasted in butter.
3. Here it is braised covered.
