## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- 5 egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- some milk cream
- 1 liter boiling milk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 liter flour on a board, make a well in the center, add 5 egg yolks, a pinch of salt, and some milk cream
2. Mix with a spoon and work into a smooth, firm dough
3. Let rest covered for 1/2 hour
4. Cut the dough into walnut-sized pieces and roll them into pencil-thick noodles with your hands
5. Cook them in 1 liter boiling milk, stirring well together
6. Steam with 8 tablespoons butter and 4 tablespoons sifted sugar in the oven or on the stove
7. If using these noodles as a garnish for braised meat or stew, omit the sugar
