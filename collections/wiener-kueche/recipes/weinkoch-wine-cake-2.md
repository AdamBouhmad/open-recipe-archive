## Ingredients

- 1 egg-sized portion of good lard
- finely grated bread crumbs
- 2 parts wine
- 1 part water
- sugar
- 1/4 pound butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- cleaned and washed currants
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Heat the lard in a pan until hot
2. Toast the finely grated bread crumbs in it until golden yellow
3. Let 2 parts wine and 1 part water boil together and pour into the bread crumbs
4. Stir it like a milk pudding and let it cool completely
5. Sugar it well
6. Beat 1/4 pound butter until fluffy
7. Stir in 5 whole eggs and 5 egg yolks, each well beaten, one by one
8. Stir in the cooled cake mixture
9. Add some cleaned and washed currants and some ground cinnamon
10. Stir everything for half an hour
11. Butter a metal dish and ring mold or ramekin
12. Pour the mixture in and bake slowly
