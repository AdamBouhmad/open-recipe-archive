## Ingredients

- Chicken drumsticks with skin
- Herb forcemeat
- Butter
- White wine
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the drumsticks carefully with the skin, lay them on a moistened board and cut them apart, then remove the thighbone while keeping the knucklebone attached.
2. Flatten the drumsticks slightly with the flat of a knife, salt and pepper them, fill each with a teaspoon of forcemeat of choice, then sew them closed so that the bones protrude slightly and each drumstick forms a cutlet shape.
3. In a casserole add some fat, lay in the prepared drumsticks, and after they have braised somewhat, pour white wine and broth over them and let them braise covered until tender.
4. Remove the drumsticks, arrange them neatly formed side by side in a shallow casserole, pour the defatted cooking liquid over them, cover with paper, place a somewhat smaller lid on the paper so the drumsticks are pressed, and let them stand in a warm place.
5. The roasted chicken breasts are arranged with the drumsticks, arranged in a circle in a serving dish, the defatted liquid is poured over and served with fine vegetables.
