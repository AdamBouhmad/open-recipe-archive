## Ingredients

- 24 deca chocolate
- 12 deca sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 3 deca sugar
- 1 teaspoon fine pounded and sifted almonds, pistachios or pignoli
- Optional: hot marmalade

## Directions

1. Prepare a dough from 24 deca chocolate and 12 deca sugar as directed for Chocolates-Kugeln
2. Spread the warm dough on a sugar-sprinkled paper and roll out knife-back thickness with a sugar-dusted rolling pin
3. Have previously prepared stiffly whipped whites of 2 eggs mixed with 3 deca sugar and 1 teaspoon fine pounded and sifted almonds, pistachios or pignoli
4. Spread this mixture knife-back thickness on the rolled chocolate dough
5. Fold the dough once so the edges lie evenly together on all sides
6. Spread the folded dough again knife-back thickness with the egg white mixture
7. Cover this by folding the dough once more as indicated
8. Cut the dough into thumb-width strips, then cut these into finger-width squares
9. Dry in air
10. Work as quickly as possible as the chocolate mass becomes stiff when cooled
11. If this happens, return the chocolate mass to the double boiler and warm through with a few drops of water
12. Alternatively, hot marmalade may be used instead of the egg white mixture
13. The chocolate dough may also be filled into paper cases half full, topped with a little marmalade, then filled to the top with warm chocolate mass and dried in air
