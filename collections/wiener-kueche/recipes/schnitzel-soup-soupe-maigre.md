## Ingredients

- 1 kilo cleaned schnitzel or pike meat
- 10 Deca butter
- 8 Deca rice
- 2.5 liters broth
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. Braise the meat
2. Place the braised meat in a casserole
3. Add 10 Deca butter, 8 Deca rice, and 2.5 liters broth
4. Add 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
5. Simmer together
6. Mix well and pass through a sieve
7. Season with salt and spices
8. Place the casserole in hot water and stir the soup frequently with a spoon
