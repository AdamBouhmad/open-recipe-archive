## Ingredients

- Calf's mesentery
- Butter
- Finely chopped mushrooms
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Good bouillon
- Fine oil
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the mesentery well, wash it, and cook it in a good braise.
2. Remove it and cut it into filets.
3. Sauté onions in butter with finely chopped mushrooms over gentle heat for a while.
4. Dust with a little flour and add salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
5. Pour in good bouillon and a tablespoon of fine oil.
6. When it has cooked briefly, place the cut mesentery pieces in the sauce and let them simmer a little.
7. Press lemon juice into it and serve very hot.
