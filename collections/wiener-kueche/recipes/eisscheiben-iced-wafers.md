## Ingredients

- 19 dkg flour
- 17 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 18-20 dkg sugar for icing
- Jam for filling

## Directions

1. On the board, make a crumbly dough from flour, butter, sugar, and egg yolks
2. Let it rest
3. Roll out and cut rounds or rings
4. Place on unbuttered sheet
5. Bake at medium-high heat until light brown
6. Brush with icing made from 2 egg whites beaten with 18-20 dkg sugar
7. Place back on sheet and let icing dry in open oven
8. Brush one side with jam and sandwich together
