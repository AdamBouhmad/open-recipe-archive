## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 15 dkg cooked, passed, cooled potatoes
- 4-6 dkg flour
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. To the beaten butter, stir in 1 egg, salt, the cooked and cooled potatoes, and flour.
2. Form small dumplings with a spoon.
3. Place them in hot lard and fry until light brown.
4. Drain and serve warm separately.
