## Ingredients

- 4 whole eggs
- yolks from 4 eggs
- 1 measure Rhenish wine
- 12 loth sugar
- pieces of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place 4 whole eggs and yolks from 4 eggs in a casserole
2. Gradually stir in 1 measure of Rhenish wine
3. Strain through a hair sieve so that the egg membranes remain behind
4. Add 12 loth sugar and some pieces of cinnamon
5. Place on the fire and beat continuously with a whisk until it rises in a thick foam
6. Serve in cups
