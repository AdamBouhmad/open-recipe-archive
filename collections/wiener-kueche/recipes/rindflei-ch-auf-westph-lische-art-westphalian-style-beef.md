## Ingredients

- well-marbled beef from the ribs, about the size of a hand
- proper seasoning
- one whole green onion
- some marjoram
- bread crumbs, a handful
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Chop a well-marbled piece of beef from the ribs into pieces about the size of a hand
2. Put them in a spacious pot, skim them well, and cook them with proper seasoning, a whole green onion and some marjoram, reducing to about two fingers' width
3. Seal the pot with a lid and place it by the fire so the meat can cook gently
4. When you notice the meat is nearly cooked through, open it and skim the fat from the broth
5. Add a handful of bread crumbs and lemon peel, bring it to a boil once more, and serve it
