## Ingredients

- one fresh, large tongue
- bacon
- anchovies
- butter
- half a seidel of cream
- breadcrumbs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cook a fresh, large tongue until tender, then remove the skin; it should not be taken from the pot until it is ready to be placed on the spit; it must also be served from the spit to the table.
2. Once the skin is removed, lard it beautifully and neatly with two parts bacon and one part anchovies, place it on a thin spit, let a piece of butter melt, add half a seidel of cream to it, and baste it three to four times during roasting, sprinkle it with breadcrumbs, squeeze lemon juice over it, and apply the sauce once more.
3. The tongue must be roasted quickly; then remove it from the spit, place it on a dish, and pour the sauce over it.
