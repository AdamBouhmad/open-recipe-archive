## Ingredients

- Chicken breast fillets with wing bone attached
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Separate the two breast parts lengthwise from the carcass so that the wing bone remains attached.
2. Flatten it with a flat knife.
3. Handle them as the fried chickens.
