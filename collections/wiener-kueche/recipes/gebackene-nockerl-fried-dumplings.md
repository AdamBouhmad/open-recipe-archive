## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 6 dkg butter or fat
- 1 egg
- Pinch of salt
- 30-40 dkg flour
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cream together 6 dkg butter or fat with 1 egg, a pinch of salt, and the milk
2. Stir in 30-40 dkg flour
3. Beat the batter well
4. Using a spoon dipped in hot lard, drop small dumplings into plenty of hot lard
5. Fry until light brown
6. Let drain
7. Keep warm in the open oven until serving
