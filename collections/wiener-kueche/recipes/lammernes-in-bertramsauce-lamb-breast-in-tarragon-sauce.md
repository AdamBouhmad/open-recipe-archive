## Ingredients

- breast of lamb
- butter
- flour
- onion
- tarragon (Bertram)
- broth
- vinegar
- cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Blanch the lamb breast and cut into pieces.
2. Let a piece of butter become warm, add a spoonful of flour and let it brown slightly.
3. Add an onion cut into four parts and a bundle of tarragon.
4. Fill with broth, add some vinegar and two spoonfuls of cream.
5. Add the lamb pieces and let simmer until tender.
6. Before serving, arrange the lamb on a dish and strain the sauce.
7. Let the sauce come to a boil again, whisk two egg yolks in a pot, add the sauce to them, and pour over the meat.
