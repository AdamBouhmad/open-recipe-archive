## Ingredients

- 30 dekagrams neck pot roast
- 16 liters root vegetables
- 1 small head of cabbage
- 1/4 kilogram fresh green peas
- 1/4 kilogram green beans
- 1/4 kohlrabi
- 1/2 head cauliflower
- 2 mushrooms
- 6 dekagrams butter
- 1 onion
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and cut the root vegetables, cabbage, kohlrabi and beans into fine noodle-like pieces
2. 1½ hours before serving, brown a finely chopped onion in 6 dekagrams hot butter until light golden
3. Add the cut vegetables except the cabbage, sauté for a while
4. Pour in hot water and braise until soft
5. After 1/2 hour, gradually pour the strained beef broth on top, salt
6. 20 minutes before serving, add the cabbage, separately boiled mushrooms, cauliflower florets and peas
