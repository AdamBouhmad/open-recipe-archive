## Ingredients

- Aspic, melted
- Red beets
- Truffles
- White from hard-boiled eggs
- Cooked green peas
- Cooked green beans
- White salad
- Fish varieties for garnish
- Colored butters
- Caviar
- Olives
- Fresh parsley or watercress

## Directions

1. Pour about finger-thick melted aspic into a mold brushed with oil
2. Layer with red beets, truffles, whites of hard-boiled eggs, green peas and green beans, carefully arranging with a larding needle on the cooled aspic without damaging the pattern
3. Cover carefully with melted aspic without disturbing the design
4. Pour another finger-thick layer of melted aspic and when completely cooled, add a layer of white salad
5. Repeat this layering process until the mold is filled
6. The top layer must be melted aspic
7. Cover the mold with a plate and cloth and let stand in ice for 2 hours
8. Remove the mold, dip briefly in boiling hot water, dry with a cloth, cover with an appropriate plate and invert quickly
9. Carefully remove the mold, garnish the molded salad with various fish varieties, colored butters, caviar, olives and decorate with fresh parsley or watercress
10. If the mold develops a hollow in the middle, fill the empty space with remaining white salad after turning out
11. Small molds can also be used for this salad
