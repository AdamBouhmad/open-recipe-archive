## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter white wine
- 12 egg yolks
- 32 deca sugar
- 4 deca dissolved warm isinglass
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/8 liter reduction
- 2 deca gelatin
- 1/4 liter water
- 1/2 liter whipping cream
- drops of alkermes juice

## Directions

1. Place 1/4 liter white wine, 12 egg yolks and 32 deca sugar in a snow bucket
2. Put on the fire and beat until the mass begins to rise
3. Remove from fire and continue beating until cold
4. Stir in 4 deca dissolved warm isinglass (prepared with 1/2 liter water and reduced to 1/8 liter) or 2 deca gelatin dissolved in 1/4 liter water
5. When this is full but not cold, fold in 1/2 liter cream whipped to foam on the ice
6. Fill into a desired mold and proceed as with Whipped Bitter Almond Cream (No. 1713)
7. If desired, color the cream pink with a few drops of alkermes juice
