## Ingredients

- a piece of beef lard
- a piece of sugar
- a pair of spoonfuls of flour
- crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- 2 measures of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Make a brown roux from a piece of beef lard and a piece of sugar, which must become chestnut brown in the lard
2. Add a pair of spoonfuls of flour, some crushed cinnamon and cloves
3. Add 2 measures of boiling milk and let it cook together for a while
4. The sweetness of the sugar should be slightly noticeable
5. If desired, mix in 3 egg yolks and pour over toasted bread slices
