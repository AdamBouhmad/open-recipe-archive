## Ingredients

- 1 piece onion
- 1 piece from each root vegetable
- some pieces of dill (dill herb)
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 finger-width piece of lemon peel
- 1 chopped carp head (if available)
- approximately 2 liters cold water
- vinegar
- 10 deca fat
- flour
- 1/2 liter cream (if desired)
- salt
- cleaned carp milt and roe

## Directions

1. Cut into a casserole 1 piece onion, 1 piece from each root vegetable, some dill, 1 bay leaf, 1 finger-width piece of lemon peel, and if available, 1 chopped carp head
2. Pour in approximately 2 liters cold water, add as much vinegar as needed, and let cook 1 hour
3. Make a dark roux with 10 deca fat and flour and pour into the boiling liquid, stirring frequently so no lumps form
4. Add 1/2 liter cream and necessary salt if desired, let simmer well
5. Strain through a sieve into another casserole and cook again
6. Add the cleaned and well-washed carp milt and roe to the boiling soup and let simmer approximately 1/2 hour
7. Remove the roe pieces, cut into pieces, and put in the soup pot, then pour the soup over them; it should have a pleasant sour taste and brown color
8. Serve with toasted rolls
