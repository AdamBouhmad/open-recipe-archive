## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites
- 32 deca fine pounded sugar
- 18 deca flour

## Directions

1. Whip stiffly the whites of 8 eggs and lightly fold in 32 deca fine pounded sugar
2. Using a paper cornet, pipe in arbitrarily large pointed mounds on a paper-lined baking sheet
3. Sprinkle with fine pounded sugar
4. Dry in a cool oven
5. Prepare a biscuit with 18 deca flour baked knife-back thickness
6. After cooling completely, cut into round finger-thick slices using a pastry cutter
7. From the completely dried and cooled Spanish dough, cut out the center of the bottom and hollow out almost to the point with careful care
