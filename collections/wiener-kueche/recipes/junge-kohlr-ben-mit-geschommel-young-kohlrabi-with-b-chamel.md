## Ingredients

- 1 peeled kohlrabi
- Butter
- Pepper
- Sugar
- Oil
- Milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. Using a thin corer, scoop small kohlrabi from a peeled kohlrabi
2. Braise them uncovered in butter slowly and season with pepper and sugar during cooking
3. Drain the butter and replace it with béchamel sauce by mixing oil into the drained butter, letting it brown slightly, adding milk, and seasoning with a little salt
4. Pour this sauce over the kohlrabi
