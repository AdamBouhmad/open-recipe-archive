## Ingredients

- Head of a carp or other fish
- 8 Deca finely sliced rolls
- One piece of marrow
- A whole egg
- 8 Deca fat
- 8 Deca flour
- 2.5 liters beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish head into pieces
2. Coat in flour and breadcrumbs
3. Fry in fat with the sliced rolls, marrow, and egg
4. Pound the fried mixture finely
5. In a casserole, heat 8 Deca fat and 8 Deca flour, stirring to toast
6. Add the pounded fish mixture
7. Pour in 2.5 liters beef broth or water
8. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring frequently
9. Pass through a sieve into a casserole
10. Skim off the fat
11. Pour into soup pot and garnish with boiled rolls or fried bread cubes
