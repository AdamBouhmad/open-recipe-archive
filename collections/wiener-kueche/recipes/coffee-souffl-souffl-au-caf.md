## Ingredients

- 16 drams flour
- 1/2 liter cold coffee cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 24 drams sugar
- 16 drams butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Place 16 drams flour in a casserole and pour with 1/2 liter cold coffee cream.
2. Add 3 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, and 24 drams sugar.
3. Stir over heat until the mixture thickens considerably.
4. Add 16 drams butter and stir in.
5. Set the thickened mixture aside.
6. After cooling completely, mix in 10 egg yolks one after another.
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites.
8. Fill a buttered mold, dusted with flour.
9. Bake for 3/4 hour at low heat in the oven.
