## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- 21 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg finely sieved sugar
- 9 dkg apricot or raspberry marmalade
- 2 stiff egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from 25 dkg flour, 21 dkg butter, and 7 dkg sugar.
2. After resting, divide it into four parts.
3. Roll each part out to cake form bottom size and place on unbuttered sheet.
4. Bake at higher heat until light brown.
5. After cooling, brush the layers, stack them together, and dust with powdered sugar.
6. For the filling: stir 7 dkg finely sieved sugar with 9 dkg apricot or raspberry marmalade and fold in 2 stiff egg whites.
7. The filling is better if made 2-3 days before use.
