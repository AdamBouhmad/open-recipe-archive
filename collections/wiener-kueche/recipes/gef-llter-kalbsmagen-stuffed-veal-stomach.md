## Ingredients

- small cabbage head
- salt water
- green parsley
- onion
- cabbage, finely cut
- beef or veal fat
- breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- savory
- caraway
- eggs
- egg yolk
- veal stomach
- flour
- cream
- capers

## Directions

1. The leaves from a small cabbage head are separated, cooked in salt water, removed, dried with a cloth and finely cut with a sharp knife.
2. In fat, roast finely cut green parsley and some onion, add the cut cabbage with rendered, milk-softened, well-squeezed breadcrumbs, salt it, season with savory and some caraway and let it braise covered.
3. Then set it aside and when it is completely cooled, stir in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks.
4. The stomach from a calf, after being carefully cleaned, is filled with this mixture, then sewn shut and cooked gently in salt water for about 2 hours.
5. Lay the stomach in a casserole containing hot butter, roast it on both sides until golden and then cut it into desired pieces.
6. In a casserole put 1 tablespoon of flour, gradually pour in, while constantly stirring, 1/3 liter of cream and stir this until it begins to boil, then add 1 tablespoon of capers, let it boil once and serve with the prepared veal stomach.
