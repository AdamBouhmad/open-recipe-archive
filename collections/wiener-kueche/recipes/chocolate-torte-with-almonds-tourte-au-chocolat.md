## Ingredients

- 28 deca powdered sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 16 deca almonds, finely ground with skin and passed
- 28 deca grated chocolate
- 12 egg whites
- 12 deca black flour
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- glaze

## Directions

1. Stir 28 deca powdered sugar with 16 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Add 16 deca finely ground and passed almonds and 28 deca grated chocolate
3. Gently fold in the snow (stiffly beaten egg whites) from 12 egg whites with 12 deca black flour
4. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. Bake for 1 hour at low heat
6. Remove the ring and once completely cooled, cover with glaze of choice
