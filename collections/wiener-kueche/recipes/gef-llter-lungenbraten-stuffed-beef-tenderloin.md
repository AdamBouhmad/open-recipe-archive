## Ingredients

- one beef tenderloin
- 16 deca of bacon
- 6 shallots
- 1 strong tablespoon capers
- 1 anchovy
- 4 sprigs parsley
- fat
- 1 onion
- some root vegetables
- 12 deca black bread
- 5 grains spice
- 8 sprigs parsley
- bones from the tenderloin
- salt
- beef broth
- 1/4 cream
- 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Carefully cut a small beef tenderloin lengthwise from the thinner side so that it can be opened but not divided into two pieces
2. Finely chop 16 deca of bacon, 6 shallots, 1 strong tablespoon capers, 1 anchovy, and 4 sprigs parsley on a cutting board with a knife
3. Spread this mixture on the inner, cut side of the tenderloin
4. Roll the tenderloin together and wrap it with twine
5. In a casserole, place fat, 1 onion, some root vegetables, 12 deca black bread, all cut into thin slices, 5 grains spice, 8 sprigs parsley, and the bones removed and broken from the tenderloin
6. Place the meat on top so that the open side faces upward
7. Braise covered for 1/2 hour
8. Add enough broth so the meat is almost covered and braise covered for about 1.5 hours in the oven until tender
9. Remove the meat and skim the fat from the sauce
10. Add 1/4 cream to the sauce and let it boil
11. In a second casserole, place the skimmed fat, then the meat freed from the twine, cover with 1/4 liter cream and roast in the oven until it gets a brown crust
12. Strain the sauce, let it boil somewhat and pour it over the meat cut into desired pieces
13. The sauce must be thick and piquant and can also be served in a sauce boat
14. Garnish the tenderloin with potatoes, macaroni, small cucumbers and the like
