## Ingredients

- 1 capon, prepared as described
- bacon slices
- finely cut black bread
- 1/4 litre cream
- capers (optional)
- finely cut gherkins (optional)
- pitted olives (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the prepared capon with the sauce in a casserole, lay bacon slices on the capon and braise it with finely cut black bread until tender
2. Remove the capon and place it in a shallow pan, pour 1/4 litre of cream over it and let it brown for approximately 10 minutes
3. Meanwhile, pass the sauce through a sieve, remove the fat, pour the cream from the capon into the sauce, stir it well together and bring it once to a boil
4. If desired, some capers, finely cut gherkins and pitted olives can be stirred into the sauce
5. Cut the capon into pieces, reassemble it and arrange it on a platter, garnish with puff pastry and serve with the sauce
