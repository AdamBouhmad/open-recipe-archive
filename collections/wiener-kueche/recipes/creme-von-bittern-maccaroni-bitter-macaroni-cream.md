## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- one spoonful fine flour
- 2 seidel milk
- one quarter pound sugar
- one lemon
- half pound bitter macaroni
- half pound small raisins

## Directions

1. Stir 12 egg yolks with one spoonful fine flour and 2 seidel milk clear
2. Add one quarter pound sugar onto which a lemon has been grated, half pound bitter macaroni which is cut several times, and half pound small raisins
3. Boil once over constant stirring and stir cold again
4. Draw the egg white snow under the cream, pile it up on a plate, bake it in an oven that is no longer hot, and serve warm
