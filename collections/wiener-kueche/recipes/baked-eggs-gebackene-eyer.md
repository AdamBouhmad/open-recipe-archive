## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- chopped fresh parsley
- chopped anchovies
- a handful of bread crumbs
- several spoonfuls of cream
- salt
- pounded pepper
- beaten eggs
- half bread crumbs and half flour for coating
- lard for frying
- lemon juice
- anchovies
- fresh parsley
- chives
- pea water

## Directions

1. Take hard-boiled eggs and cut off a small piece from the bottom so they stand on the dish.
2. Cut off the top as a lid, remove the yolks, and pound them fine in a mortar.
3. Beat a piece of butter until light, add finely chopped fresh parsley, some finely chopped anchovies, a handful of bread crumbs, several spoonfuls of cream, salt and some pounded pepper, and stir everything well together.
4. Fill the eggs with this mixture, dip the filled eggs in beaten egg, coat them in half bread crumbs and half flour, then fry them in hot lard.
5. Make a small sauce of lemon juice, anchovies, fresh parsley and chives, which all go into a thin yellowish roux, and is extended with pea water.
6. Add several spoonfuls of cream and let the whole reduce to a thin sauce.
7. Place the eggs on a dish, pour the sauce over them, and they are ready.
