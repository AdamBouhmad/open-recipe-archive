## Ingredients

- Calf brain
- Beef broth
- 15 to 20 mushrooms of medium size
- Parsley, finely cut
- Butter
- Half a cooking spoon flour
- Good beef broth

## Directions

1. Cook calf brain in beef broth. Push through a sieve. Coarsely chop 15 to 20 medium-sized mushrooms and place with finely cut parsley in a casserole with a piece of warm butter. Let the mushrooms simmer a while, dust with half a cooking spoon of flour, let simmer a bit more, add the brain and fill with good beef broth.
