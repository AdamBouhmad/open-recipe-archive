## Ingredients

- 2 ounces anise
- 1 ounce fennel
- 2 measures purified brandy
- 1 measure water
- 4 pounds sugar
- 3 measures water
- 1 seidel brandy

## Directions

1. Crush 2 ounces anise and 1 ounce fennel
2. Pour 2 measures purified brandy and 1 measure water over the mixture
3. Let it stand for 8 days
4. Distill the mixture
5. Dissolve 4 pounds sugar in 3 measures water to make sugar water
6. Add the sugar water to the distillate
7. Mix in 1 seidel brandy
8. Filter the liquid
