## Ingredients

- two Pfund finely cut marrow
- two Pfund pure pork lard
- two Loth cloves
- two nutmegs
- 2 Pfund finely grated Parmesan cheese
- two Loth cinnamon
- pepper
- four Loth salt
- clean casings

## Directions

1. Mix two Pfund finely cut marrow, two Pfund pure pork lard, two Loth cloves, two nutmegs (both finely ground), 2 Pfund finely grated Parmesan cheese, two Loth cinnamon, and pepper together well with four Loth salt
2. Fill into clean casings and make each sausage half a pound
3. When traveling in a place where good soup is not available, take a pot of boiling water of two to three Maß, add one such sliced sausage to it, let it only simmer, and serve it on toasted bread for the best soup
