## Ingredients

- Small green salted cucumbers
- Salt
- 1 teaspoon bitter mustard seeds per jar
- 1 teaspoon sweet mustard seeds per jar
- Vinegar (recipe 1901)

## Directions

1. Peel small green salted cucumbers
2. Cut them in half lengthwise and carefully remove the seeds
3. Salt them well and let them stand covered in an earthenware vessel overnight
4. The next day, spread them on a hair sieve or cloth so they lie on the cut side to drain the water well
5. Pack them tightly in half-liter jars or similar sized containers standing upright lengthwise
6. In each jar add 1 teaspoon bitter mustard seeds and 1 teaspoon sweet mustard seeds
7. Fill with vinegar (recipe 1901)
8. Bind with covers and let them boil for half an hour in a water bath
