## Ingredients

- 1 kilo fine large dried bilberries
- 1/2 kilo fine sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Place the bilberries in a casserole that has never contained fat
2. Mix thoroughly with the fine sifted sugar, but carefully so the bilberries do not get crushed
3. Place the casserole on the warm stove and let the bilberries warm while stirring constantly until they reach the proper degree of cooking, without however boiling
4. When they have reached the proper stage, set them aside and while still hot, place them in an earthen pot or glass
5. Let the bilberries sit for 3 days, after which time cover them with paper, pricking this paper several times with a needle
6. Each time bilberries are used, stir them well beforehand
7. Remove any excess liquid that forms over time - this is very good for use as a beverage mixed with soda water, ice water, etc., or for sauces
