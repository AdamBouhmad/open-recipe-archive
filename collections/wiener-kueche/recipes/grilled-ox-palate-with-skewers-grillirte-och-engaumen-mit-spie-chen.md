## Ingredients

- ox palate
- calf's udder
- braising liquid
- beef lard
- truffles
- mushrooms
- parsley
- onions
- flour
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- white bread, grated
- butter
- clear broth or Sauce Espagnole or clear piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the ox palate and remove the skin
2. Cook it soft with calf's udder in a braise
3. Cool until it can be removed and cut into large, even cubes
4. Put a quarter pound beef lard in a casserole
5. Sauté thinly sliced truffles and mushrooms with finely chopped parsley and onions
6. Dust with proportional flour
7. Fill with the braising liquid
8. Cook briefly and bind with 6 or 8 egg yolks so the sauce becomes quite thick
9. Set the diced palate pieces into it
10. Draw off with lemon juice and set it cold again
11. On a silver skewer thread the palate pieces, alternating with truffle and udder and mushrooms
12. Coat evenly with sauce
13. Roll in fine, white grated bread and continue until everything is well covered with bread
14. Melt a good piece of butter and stir it with some egg yolk
15. Dip each skewer in it and coat thickly with grated bread
16. Fry on three sides on a grate, and give the fourth side color with a glowing shovel
17. Serve with clear, strong broth or Sauce Espagnole or clear piquant sauce
