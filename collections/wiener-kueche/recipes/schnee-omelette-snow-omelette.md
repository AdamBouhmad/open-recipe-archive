## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks with 4 tablespoons sugar for 1 hour
2. Lightly fold in the firmly beaten snow from 6 egg whites with 1 heaping tablespoon flour
3. Put 1 tablespoon hot fat on a very hot omelette pan and then evenly put in 2-3 tablespoons of the above-mentioned mixture
4. Bake on the lower side only until it forms a light brown skin, then strike the omelettes from both sides together and place them side by side in a bowl
5. Put the bowl briefly in the oven and let the omelettes bake through, then sprinkle with preserved fruit as desired and serve
