## Ingredients

- small meaty chickens
- salt
- onion
- yellow turnips
- green parsley
- pepper
- allspice
- three or four cloves
- water
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well, salt them slightly, and dress them nicely
2. Place in a casserole with onion, yellow turnips, green parsley, pepper, allspice, and three or four cloves
3. Fill with three parts water and one part vinegar
4. Let simmer until the chickens are half cooked
5. Warm a piece of butter, add two spoonfuls of flour, let it brown slightly
6. Strain the sauce onto it
7. Add two or three spoonfuls of cream
8. Return the chickens to the sauce
9. Let them cook completely through
