## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter liquefied caramel sugar
- cream
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as Vanille-Gefrornees (No. 1720), with the difference that instead of vanilla, add 1/4 liter liquefied caramel sugar to the snow-beater with the cream, egg yolks, and sugar
