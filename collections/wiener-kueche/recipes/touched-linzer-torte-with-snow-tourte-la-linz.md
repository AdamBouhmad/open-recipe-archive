## Ingredients

- 32 Deka blanched almonds
- juice of 1 lemon
- 32 Deka butter
- 32 Deka caster sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1.5 knife tips finely chopped lemon zest
- snow from 8 egg whites
- 32 Deka powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Grind blanched almonds very finely with lemon juice
2. Mix well with butter and caster sugar
3. Add egg yolks and lemon zest gradually
4. Stir for 1/4 hour
5. Fold in the snow of egg whites mixed with powdered sugar
6. Proceed as in the 'touched Linzer torte' (no. 1483)
