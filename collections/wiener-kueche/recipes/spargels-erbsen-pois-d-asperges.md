## Ingredients

- asparagus
- cold water
- salt water
- butter sauce
- green parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut asparagus into pea-sized pieces
2. Place in cold water
3. Cook in salt water until tender
4. Drain and place in cold water
5. In a butter sauce, roast some green parsley and add sugar to taste
6. Add the drained asparagus peas, bring to a boil and serve
