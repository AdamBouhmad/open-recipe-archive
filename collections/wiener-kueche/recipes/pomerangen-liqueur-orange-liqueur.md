## Ingredients

- half Maß brandy
- 2 bitter oranges
- three-quarter pound sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the peel of one orange on sugar
2. Add the whole other orange to the brandy
3. Add three-quarter pound sugar to half Maß brandy
4. Bind the glass well and stir twice daily
5. On the twenty-first day, filter, fill into bottles, cork well, and let stand for 6 to 7 months
