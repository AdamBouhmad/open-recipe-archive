## Ingredients

- 32 deka finely sifted sugar
- white of 1 small egg
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Put 32 deka finely sifted sugar into a deep bowl
2. Stir this using a wooden spoon meant only for this work, with the white of a small egg and the juice of 1 lemon for approximately 1 hour until well beaten
3. If the mixture is too thick, add some water; if too thin, add more sugar, and stir for another 1/4 hour
4. The icing must form a brilliantly white and glossy mass that flows thickly from the spoon
5. This icing, which contains egg white, must be used on the same day, as it becomes hard if it sits longer
