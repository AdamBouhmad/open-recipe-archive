## Ingredients

- ½ kg fingerling potatoes
- 1 small celery root
- white beans
- ¼ l lentils
- 1 head lettuce or 10 dkg lamb's lettuce
- 1 red beet
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Prepare potatoes as potato salad
2. Sort and wash beans and lentils, cook separately in cold water with some baking soda for 1½ hours more steaming than boiling
3. Drain beans and lentils, dress while still warm with salt, vinegar, and oil
4. Prepare celery root as salad
5. Wash red beet, cook in plenty of water for 1½-2 hours, cool, and slice thinly
6. Clean and wash lettuce or lamb's lettuce, cut head lettuce into noodle-like pieces, dress with salt, vinegar, and oil
7. Heap potato salad in center of glass bowl, arrange beans and lentils alternately around it, place lettuce between
8. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into 8 pieces, celery, and red beet
