## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour
- cream
- sliced celery
- roasted bread slices
- a piece of butter
- pea broth

## Directions

1. Stir eggs mixed with a bit of flour in cream into the sliced celery, roasted bread slices and a piece of butter in pea broth
2. Pour the boiling soup over and season to taste
