## Ingredients

- 28 Deca caster sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites
- 24 Deca fine flour
- 16 Deca grated chocolate
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. When the sugar and egg have been stirred for 1/2 hour, mix in 16 Deca grated chocolate
3. Lightly fold in the snow from 12 egg whites simultaneously with 24 Deca fine flour
4. Fill a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. Bake at low heat for 3/4 to 1 hour
6. Turn out and cool before glazing
