## Ingredients

- cooked rabbit meat
- soft side parts of rabbit
- a pair of bread rolls
- milk
- one pound pork fat
- onions
- sausage herbs
- salt
- spices
- sweet cream
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. From cooked rabbit, scrape the fine meat from the nerves and chop finely together with the soft side parts
2. Soak bread rolls further in milk, then press them out
3. Cut one pound pork fat into cubes
4. Sauté a handful of finely chopped onions in the fat
5. Stir in the rabbit mixture and add the bread rolls, sausage herbs, salt, and spices
6. Stir everything together with sweet cream and six egg yolks
7. Proceed as with white boudin sausages
