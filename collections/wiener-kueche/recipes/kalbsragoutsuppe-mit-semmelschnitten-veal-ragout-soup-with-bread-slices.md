## Ingredients

- 20 h veal bones or ½ veal head
- 1 small head cauliflower
- 2 mushrooms
- 1 bread roll
- 8 dkg butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the veal head or bones, place in cold salted water and cook for 1 hour.
2. Remove the meat from the bones and cut into small pieces.
3. In a saucepan, make a parsley roux with 8 dkg butter, add the veal bone broth, add the meat, season with salt, and cook well for 15 minutes.
4. Before serving, add the divided cauliflower and the thinly sliced mushrooms, which have been cooked separately in salted water for 20 minutes.
5. Serve the soup with bread slices or bread dumplings.
