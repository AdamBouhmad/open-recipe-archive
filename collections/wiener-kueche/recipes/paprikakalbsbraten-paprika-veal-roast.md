## Ingredients

- 60-80 dkg boned veal shoulder
- 1 liter cream
- 4 dkg fat
- water
- 5 dkg fat
- 1 finely chopped onion
- 2-3 knife tips paprika
- broth or water
- veal roasting juices

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it, and bind it if necessary.
2. Heat 4 dkg fat in a roasting pan, add the meat, brown it on all sides, add very little water, and let it roast in the oven for 1 to 1½ hours, basting frequently and adding more liquid.
3. Meanwhile, in 5 dkg fat, roast 1 finely chopped onion until light yellow.
4. Add 2-3 knife tips of paprika, pour in broth or water, and let it cook well.
5. Dust it with flour, strain it through a sieve, pour it up with the cream, bring it to a boil again, mix it with the roasting juices, and pour it over the sliced meat.
