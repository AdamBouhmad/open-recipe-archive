## Ingredients

- 8 ounces grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- some vinegar
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 4 tablespoons broth
- some salt
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- finely cut chives (optional)

## Directions

1. Pass 8 ounces of grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out together with 3 hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve into a bowl
2. Stir together with some vinegar, 2 tablespoons oil, 1 pinch white pepper, 4 tablespoons broth, some salt and ½ teaspoon sugar into a thick sauce
3. You can also add finely cut chives on top
