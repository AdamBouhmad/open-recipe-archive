## Ingredients

- 12 loth butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- finely chopped parsley
- 12 loth finely grated bread

## Directions

1. Stir 12 loth butter to foam
2. Gradually add 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks with salt, nutmeg, and finely chopped parsley
3. Fold in 12 loth finely grated bread
4. Form small dumplings
5. Cook slowly in bouillon for a quarter hour
6. Serve under young chickens, pigeons, veal, and lamb, or with crayfish sauce
