## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls orange blossom water
- 3 spoonfuls good cream
- 4 loth sugar
- some nutmeg blossom
- 3/4 pound grated potatoes
- lard for frying
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter with 3 whole eggs and 5 yolks to a foam
2. Add 3 spoonfuls orange blossom water and the same amount of good cream, along with 4 loth sugar and some nutmeg blossom
3. Stir in 3/4 pound grated potatoes and add more so the mass becomes stiff enough to make small round dumplings
4. Fry them golden in lard and serve with a cream sauce
