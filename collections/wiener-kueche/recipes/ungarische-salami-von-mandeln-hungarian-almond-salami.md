## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar
- 1 egg white
- 16 decagrams peeled almonds
- 1 egg white
- 2 decagrams tragacanth gum
- 1/2 liter lukewarm water
- 1 tablespoon almond milk
- fine pounded sugar
- 8 decagrams coarsely cut almonds
- 8 decagrams cubed citron
- 8 decagrams finely cut brunelles
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- nutmeg
- crushed spice cloves
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Put 16 decagrams fine sifted sugar, 1 egg white, peeled almonds pounded very finely with 1 egg white, and tragacanth gum dissolved in 1/2 liter lukewarm water (strained through cloth) in a bowl
2. Stir over the fire until the thick boiled mass releases from the bowl
3. Mix with 1 tablespoon almond milk
4. Spread on a board dusted with fine pounded sugar
5. Work in coarsely cut almonds, cubed citron, finely cut brunelles, cinnamon, nutmeg, and crushed spice cloves into a dough
6. Form the dough into sausage shapes
7. Roll in fine sifted sugar mixed with grated chocolate
8. Dry for 6 to 8 hours in a very cool oven
