## Ingredients

- 12 pounds ripe cherries
- 6 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the cherries and remove the stems
2. Pit them with a pointed stick
3. Place in a casserole over a charcoal fire
4. Let the liquid cook down somewhat, stirring frequently with a wooden spoon until reduced to half
5. Clarify 6 pounds of sugar and cook until it reaches the small crack stage (petit casse), which is when a drop in cold water cracks between the teeth
6. Add the cherries to the sugar
7. Stir well over the charcoal fire until it reaches the proper consistency, which is when you can see the bottom of the casserole when stirring
8. When half-cooled, pour into jars or boxes
