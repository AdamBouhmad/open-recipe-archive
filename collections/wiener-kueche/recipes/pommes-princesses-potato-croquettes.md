## Ingredients

- ½ kg round potatoes
- 8 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- salt
- pepper
- 3 dkg grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cream together 8 dkg butter with 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, salt, pepper, and 3 dkg grated Parmesan.
2. Fold in ½ kg cooked, mashed, and cooled potatoes until well combined.
3. Fill a pastry bag fitted with a star tip.
4. Pipe small croquettes onto a buttered and floured baking sheet.
5. Brush with beaten egg.
6. Bake at high heat until light brown.
7. Serve warm.
