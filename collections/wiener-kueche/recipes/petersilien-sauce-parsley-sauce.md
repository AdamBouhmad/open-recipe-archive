## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- Butter-Sauce (from recipe 186)

## Directions

1. Shortly before serving, add 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley to the strained Butter-Sauce
2. Stir well and bring to a boil once
