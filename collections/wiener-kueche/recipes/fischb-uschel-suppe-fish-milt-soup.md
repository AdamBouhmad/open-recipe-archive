## Ingredients

- clean fish milt, cut into pieces
- a good portion of roe
- water, 1 measure
- vinegar, 2 seitels
- bay leaves, 2
- lemon peel
- tripe greens (Kuttelkraut)
- onion, 1 head cut into 4 pieces
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- pepper
- saffron
- butter or good beef drippings
- yellow roux
- cream

## Directions

1. Put cleaned and cut milt and a good portion of roe into a pot with 1 measure of water and 2 seitels of vinegar
2. Add 2 bay leaves, lemon peel, tripe greens, onion cut into 4 pieces, salt, nutmeg flower, a little pepper and saffron
3. Cook all together for 2 hours
4. Make a yellow thin roux from butter or good beef drippings
5. Thicken the soup with the roux and simmer for another quarter hour
6. At the end, add a couple of spoonfuls of good cream
7. Carefully remove the onion, tripe greens and bay leaves before serving over toasted bread
