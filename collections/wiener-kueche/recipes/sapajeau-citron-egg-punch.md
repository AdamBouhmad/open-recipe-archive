## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- sugar
- 6 whole eggs
- 0.5 pound pounded sugar
- 1 bottle Rhenish wine or good Austrian wine

## Directions

1. Rub the zest from 3 lemons on sugar
2. Place the zest, lemon juice, 6 whole eggs and 0.5 pound pounded sugar in a casserole
3. Gradually stir in 1 bottle of Rhenish wine or good Austrian wine
4. Beat with a whisk over the fire until boiling hot and risen
5. Fill into chocolate cups
6. Serve
