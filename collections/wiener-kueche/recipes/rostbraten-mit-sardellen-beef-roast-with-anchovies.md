## Ingredients

- beef roast pieces
- finely chopped anchovy fillets
- green parsley
- flour

## Directions

1. Pound the roast very thin
2. Spread finely chopped anchovy fillets with green parsley on each piece
3. Fold each piece in half
4. Pound lightly with the back of a knife
5. Dip in flour and quickly fry on both sides
