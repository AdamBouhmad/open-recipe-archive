## Ingredients

- cream
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a beautiful thick milk pudding from cream
2. Let it boil well
3. Spread on a sheet and let cool
4. Cut into strips
5. Turn in eggs and bread crumbs
6. Fry in lard
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
