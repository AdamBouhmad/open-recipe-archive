## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Onions
- Mushrooms
- Spices (optional)
- Broth
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice or wine

## Directions

1. Place the veal sweetbreads in lukewarm water and keep at moderate heat on the stove for approximately half an hour to maintain this temperature.
2. Remove them from the water, release from the membrane, and boil the sweetbreads in salt water.
3. When tender, remove and place in cold water, then lay on a board, cover with another board, and weight it down.
4. In the meantime, place some butter in a casserole with a little onion, mushroom, and if desired a little spice, roast these.
5. Pour in good broth and let the meat from the sweetbreads that has been loosened braise covered until tender.
6. Strain the sauce and cut the meat and sweetbreads into desired pieces.
7. Make a roux from flour and butter in the proportion of 2 tablespoons each, pour it with the strained sauce in which the meat was cooked, and dilute as needed with broth.
8. Mix 2 egg yolks and the juice of one-fifth of a lemon or some wine, combine with the sauce, and stir together over heat until the eggs are incorporated.
9. Strain through a sieve and serve with the sweetbreads and meat on a plate.
