## Ingredients

- 8 pieces of rolls (32 Loffel), finely diced
- 16 Loffel fat (butter or pork lard)
- 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- 1 1/2 liter flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Dice 8 pieces of rolls (32 Loffel) finely and roast in 16 Loffel fat (butter or pork lard) with 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley; the rolls must brown quickly
2. Mix 1 1/2 liter flour and 1 teaspoon salt together in a deep bowl
3. Stir the flour with 1/2 liter cold water to a thin dough
4. Add the roasted rolls
