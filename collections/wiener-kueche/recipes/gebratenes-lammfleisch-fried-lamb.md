## Ingredients

- Lamb pieces, 2 fingers wide and 1 finger long
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard, very hot
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- Salad or fine vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut the front part of the lamb, namely the breast and shoulder, into pieces 2 fingers wide and 1 finger long
2. Salt the pieces and let them rest for 1/4 hour
3. Dip them in flour, then in beaten eggs
4. Roll them in breadcrumbs
5. Fry them on both sides in very hot butter or lard
6. Remove them with a wooden spoon and let them rest briefly on blotting paper
7. Arrange them neatly on a platter, cover with fried green parsley, and serve with salad or fine vegetables
