## Ingredients

- One well-nourished, fat goose
- Salt
- Wheat bran
- Twine

## Directions

1. After slaughter, pluck the goose very carefully so as not to damage the skin
2. Singe it at a straw fire
3. Wash well with water and bran
4. Cut wings and neck close to the body
5. Rub with salt and lay in a barrel until after three days the salt has penetrated
6. Roll the salted geese in wheat bran
7. Hang by the legs with twine in smoke for eight days
8. Store like any other smoked meat
9. Can be eaten raw or cooked
