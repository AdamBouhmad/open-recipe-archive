## Ingredients

- 16 eggs
- 32 half eggshells wine
- quarter pound sugar
- lemon
- whole cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 16 eggs into a new pot
2. Pour in 32 half eggshells of wine
3. Mix in quarter pound sugar rubbed with a lemon and some whole cinnamon
4. Let come to a gentle boil over low fire with vigorous beating
5. The cream must become quite thick and foamy
6. Arrange on a plate or in serving shells
7. Pass around with the shells
