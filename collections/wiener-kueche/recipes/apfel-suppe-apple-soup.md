## Ingredients

- 15 peeled Reinette or similar apples
- 1/4 liter Rhine wine or white wine
- 32 deca sugar
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 kilo crushed sugar
- 24 deca cleaned currants
- 24 deca raisins
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Cut one-third of 15 peeled apples into slices, add 1/4 liter Rhine wine or white wine, 32 deca sugar, the juice of 1 lemon and let simmer.
2. Cook the remaining, similarly thinly cut apples in 1/2 liter water and the juice of 1 lemon, strain through a sieve and stir this together with 1/2 kilo crushed sugar, the juice of 1 lemon and 1 1/2 liters good wine.
3. Clean and search through 24 deca currants and an equal amount of raisins, let them simmer slightly in 1/2 liter water, strain through a sieve and pour this with the apple slices into the soup bowl, then place on ice.
4. Serve this soup in the same manner as the Strawberry Soup.
