## Ingredients

- Selected ripe raspberries
- Wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Fill a bottle one-third full with selected ripe raspberries
2. Pour genuine wine vinegar over them, but the bottle should not be completely full
3. Cork it well and proceed as with Sour Cherry Vinegar (No. 1896)
