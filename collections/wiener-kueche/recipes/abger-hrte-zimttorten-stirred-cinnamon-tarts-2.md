## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound butter
- egg yolks
- half pound ground almonds
- half pound sifted sugar
- two loth ground cinnamon
- three-quarters pound flour
- tart sheet
- preserved morello cherries
- currants

## Directions

1. Stir three-quarters pound butter until very foamy
2. Add egg yolks gradually
3. Add half pound ground almonds, half pound sifted sugar, and two loth ground cinnamon
4. Stir everything together for one hour
5. Mix in three-quarters pound flour
6. Brush a tart sheet and rim with the batter to the thickness of half a knife-back
7. Fill with preserved morello cherries, currants, etc.
8. Make a lattice from the remaining dough over the top
9. Bake slowly
10. Pour icing over it
