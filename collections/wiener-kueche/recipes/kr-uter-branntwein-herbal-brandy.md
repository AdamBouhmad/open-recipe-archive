## Ingredients

- several bunches of finely cut milfoil
- 3 bunches wormwood
- 1 bunch mugwort
- 1 bunch melissa
- some pieces of orange blossoms and orange peel
- 1 clove
- 7 tea cups grape berries
- 2 tea cups finely cut licorice root
- 5 pieces crushed juniper berries
- 5 grains white pepper
- 1 teaspoon mace and equally much nutmeg
- 1 liter corn brandy
- 16 tea cups sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- optional: 1 teaspoon anise, some orange blossoms or 1 small piece vanilla

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients listed in a bottle
2. After sealing the bottle, let it stand 3 to 4 weeks in the sun and shake it frequently
3. After this time, strain the liquid into a second bottle and dilute with sugar water if desired
4. To make the sugar water: boil 16 tea cups sugar in 1/2 liter water for 1/4 hour, to which you can add 1 teaspoon anise, some orange blossoms or 1 small piece vanilla if desired
5. Cool and strain, then mix with the strained brandy
6. Pour good brandy again over the ingredients remaining in the bottle
