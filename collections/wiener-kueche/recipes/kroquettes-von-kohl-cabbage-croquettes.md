## Ingredients

- 3 small firm heads of cabbage
- 2 water-soaked rolls, well pressed
- some fat
- ½ finely chopped onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- 2-3 tablespoons flour
- breadcrumbs for breading
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Clean, divide, and wash the cabbage
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook for 10 minutes
3. Drain very well and chop
4. Mix with 2 water-soaked, well-pressed rolls
5. In a saucepan, heat some fat and briefly fry the finely chopped onion and parsley
6. Add the cabbage, salt, pepper, and 2-3 tablespoons flour
7. Stir and let cool
8. If the mixture is too soft, add more flour or breadcrumbs
9. Form into croquettes, bread them, and fry in hot lard
10. Drain and serve with head lettuce salad
