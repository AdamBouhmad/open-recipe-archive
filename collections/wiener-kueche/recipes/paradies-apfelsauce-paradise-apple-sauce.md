## Ingredients

- 6 to 7 paradise apples, cleaned and washed
- one spoonful flour
- butter, warmed
- broth if needed
- one piece sugar

## Directions

1. Braise 6 to 7 cleaned and washed paradise apples, cut into several pieces, in a casserole until very soft
2. Let one spoonful of flour in warmed butter run slightly brown
3. Dissolve the browned flour with the braised apples that have been pressed through a sieve
4. If too thick, add broth
5. Add one piece of sugar
