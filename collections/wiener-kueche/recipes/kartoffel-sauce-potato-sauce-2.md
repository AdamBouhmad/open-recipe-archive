## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- thinly sliced onions
- diced root vegetables
- some parsley and spices
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons vinegar
- approximately 1/2 liter broth
- several spoonfuls sour cream
- salt
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Put butter in a saucepan with thinly sliced onions and diced root vegetables, some parsley and spices, and let it roast
2. Dust with 8 tablespoons flour
3. Add 6 tablespoons vinegar, approximately 1/2 liter broth, several spoonfuls sour cream, some salt, and lemon peel
4. Let it boil very thoroughly
5. Pass the sauce through a sieve over raw, peeled, diced potatoes
6. Let it continue cooking covered until the potatoes are completely tender
