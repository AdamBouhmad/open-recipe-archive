## Ingredients

- eggs
- bread roll
- milk
- butter
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs
- chives

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard and shell them
2. Cut them in half lengthwise
3. Remove yolks and chop small with a piece of bread roll soaked in milk
4. Drive off butter, stir in the bread
5. Add nutmeg, pepper, salt, and green parsley
6. Fill the egg halves so a mound forms on top
7. Arrange in a row in a dish rubbed with lard and sour cream
8. Pour butter over them
9. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
10. Pour sour cream over so it does not cover the eggs
11. Add green parsley and chives
12. Bake with heat from below and above
