## Ingredients

- Black wild boar, cleaned
- Bacon
- Truffles
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Parsley
- Dry herbs
- Butter
- Sauce pikante

## Directions

1. Cut beautiful fillets from cleaned black game as desired, lard them with bacon and truffles, and place them in a marinade of vinegar, wine, salt, pepper, many onions and parsley, along with dry herbs
2. Add a piece of butter and let it come to a boil slightly in a casserole over gentle charcoal fire so that the fillets take on the taste of the marinade
3. Stick them on the spit, bind them with paper, and roast them slowly, basting them with butter and marinade
4. Make a sauce pikante from the marinade, arrange the fillets nicely on the plate, glaze them, and serve the sauce over them
