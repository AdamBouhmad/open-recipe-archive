## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 liter boiling custard or milk
- 8 ounces butter
- 12 ounces brown sugar
- 1 knife point salt
- butter pastry baskets
- 3 egg whites
- 12 ounces finely sifted flour
- optional: 1 piece grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Make fine dumplings from 4 egg yolks
2. Cook them in 1 liter of boiling custard or milk with constant stirring
3. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes
4. Add 8 ounces butter, 12 ounces brown sugar, and 1 knife point of salt
5. Stir well
6. Fill 1 tablespoon of the mixture into baked butter pastry baskets and arrange in a bowl
7. Alternatively, if horns (pastries) have been made, fill them with some dumplings and place in the croustades
8. Beat the snow from 3 egg whites and mix with 12 ounces finely sifted flour and optionally 1 piece of grated chocolate
9. Cover the whole with this mixture, dust with fine flour, and bake in the open oven for a few minutes
