## Ingredients

- 1 braised veal knuckle
- onion purée or other purée as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal knuckle as in the previous recipe, then cut it into desired pieces
2. Arrange these pieces in a circle on a platter and place the chosen purée in the center
