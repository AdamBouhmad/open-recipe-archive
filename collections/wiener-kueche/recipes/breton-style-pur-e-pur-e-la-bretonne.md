## Ingredients

- 1 liter dried, softly cooked peas
- butter sauce
- salt
- pepper
- garlic (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix 1 liter of softly cooked dried peas with some butter sauce
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. If desired, pass a small piece of crushed garlic through the sieve with the peas
4. Sour the peas to taste as desired
