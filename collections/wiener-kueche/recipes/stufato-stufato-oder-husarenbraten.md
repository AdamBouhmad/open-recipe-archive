## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg black beef or oxtail
- 6 dkg root vegetables
- 6 dkg bacon
- 2 tomatoes
- ½ l red wine
- salt
- pepper
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion ring
- 1 bay leaf
- thyme

## Directions

1. Beat the meat, wash it, salt and pepper it, and lard it.
2. In 6 dkg hot fat, roast the cleaned, finely cut root vegetables, 1 onion ring, 1 bay leaf, and some thyme until dark brown.
3. Add the meat, pour in a little water and the wine, cover and braise for 2-2¼ hours, pouring in liquid occasionally.
4. During braising, add tomatoes or tomato paste.
