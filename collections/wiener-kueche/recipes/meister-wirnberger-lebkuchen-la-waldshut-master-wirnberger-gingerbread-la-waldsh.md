## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 56 deka finely pounded sugar
- 9 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 8 deka finely cut citron
- 8 deka finely cut orange peel
- 8 deka finely cut lemon peel
- 2 deka cinnamon
- 3 pounded spice cloves
- 1 pinch nutmeg
- 48 deka fine dry flour
- 48 deka blanched almonds, finely cut with a razor and roasted golden in the oven

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Add 56 deka finely pounded sugar and mix very well together
3. Stir in stiffly beaten egg white from 9 eggs for 3/4 hour
4. Add 8 deka finely cut citron, 8 deka finely cut orange peel, 8 deka finely cut lemon peel, 2 deka cinnamon, 3 pounded spice cloves, 1 pinch nutmeg, and 48 deka fine dry flour, stir well together
5. Finally mix in 48 deka blanched almonds finely cut with a razor and roasted golden in the oven
6. Cut round slices from wafers of desired size
7. Spread 1 to 2 fingers high with the above mass, but not to the very edge as it easily adheres to the sheet during baking
8. Place a blanched almond or a piece of finely cut citron in the center of each slice
9. Put on a sheet and bake at moderate heat in the oven until completely dry
