## Ingredients

- calf liver
- bacon pieces
- sliced bacon
- whole yellow turnips
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- broth
- vegetables
- rice

## Directions

1. Skin a calf liver and lard it with pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of bacon.
2. In a casserole put sliced bacon, lay the liver with the larded side up, add whole, cleaned yellow turnips, the necessary salt and, if desired, pepper, pour some white wine and broth over it and let it braise covered for about 1 hour.
3. The liver is then removed, cut into desired pieces and the strained juices served with vegetables or rice alongside the liver.
