## Ingredients

- 2 bottles Pontac
- 1 seidel water
- distilled substance and juice from 2 lemons
- 1 pound sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- 3 loth bishop's essence

## Directions

1. Place 2 bottles Pontac, 1 seidel water, the distilled substance and juice from 2 lemons, 1 pound sugar and a little grated nutmeg in a terrine on coals
2. Heat until a white foam appears
3. Add 3 loth bishop's essence
4. Let it simmer a little longer
5. Serve in ordinary punch glasses
