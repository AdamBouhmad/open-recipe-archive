## Ingredients

- Beef designated for roasting, from the rib piece
- Garlic
- Minced bacon fat
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut as much beef designated for roasting from the rib piece as needed
2. Salt it, knock it, season with garlic and minced bacon fat
3. Place it on the grill and roast it over glowing coals
