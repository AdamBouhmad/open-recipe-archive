## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- vinegar
- water
- onions
- cloves
- garlic
- bay leaves
- lemon peel
- juniper berries
- pepper
- bacon
- cream

## Directions

1. Wash the haunch well, salt it, and let it stand in salt for a while
2. In a small pot, combine half vinegar and half water, two onions studded with cloves, one clove of garlic, two bay leaves, lemon peel, some juniper berries, and some whole pepper
3. Let the brine simmer and scald the haunch with it
4. Let it lie in the brine for two or three days, scalding it with boiling brine twice daily
5. When ready to use, remove the skin, lard it well with bacon, and roast it slowly on a spit
6. During roasting, baste it frequently with brine and cream mixed together
7. When well roasted, place it on a dish, garnish it like a hare, and serve with an anchovy or caper sauce made from the brine
