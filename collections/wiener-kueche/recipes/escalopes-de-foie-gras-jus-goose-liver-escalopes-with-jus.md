## Ingredients

- goose liver
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- jus sauce
- lemon juice
- small roasted onions
- roasted bread pieces

## Directions

1. Cut the goose liver into elongated strips, half a finger thick and 3 fingers wide.
2. Brush a shallow casserole well with butter and lay out the liver pieces separately.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and cover with a sheet of paper brushed with butter.
4. 10 minutes before serving, place the casserole on the heat and move it gently for several minutes.
5. Turn the liver pieces one by one and continue moving them over the heat for the same length of time.
6. Arrange these pieces in a circle on a platter.
7. To the butter in the casserole, add some jus sauce with a little lemon juice and pour this over the goose liver escalopes.
8. In the center, small roasted onions can be added and the platter garnished with roasted bread pieces.
