## Ingredients

- 4 yolks of butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 pinch of nutmeg
- 10 dried figs, soaked in water, well pressed out, minced
- 8 tablespoons shelled almonds, finely blanched with some milk
- 8 tablespoons shelled almonds, finely cut into sticks
- 4 tablespoons finely flaked or oblong cut citron
- 4 tablespoons small raisins (currants)
- 4 tablespoons raisins
- 1 strong pinch of fresh parsley
- 4 tablespoons sugar (to taste)

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, gradually add the whole eggs and egg yolk.
2. Mix in the finely minced figs, the blanched almonds, the almond sticks, citron, raisins, currants, parsley and sugar to taste.
3. Mix everything well together and fill the chickens with this mixture.
4. Prepare further as described for roasted chickens (No. 818).
