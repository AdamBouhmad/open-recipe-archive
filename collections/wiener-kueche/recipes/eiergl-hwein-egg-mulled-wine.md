## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 28 deciliters vanilla sugar
- juice of 1 orange
- 1 liter white or red wine

## Directions

1. In a whisk bowl, add 8 egg yolks, 28 deciliters vanilla sugar, the juice of 1 orange and 1 liter white or red wine
2. Mix well, place the whisk bowl on the fire where the mixture is beaten with the whisk until it is foamy
3. Serve warm in glasses
