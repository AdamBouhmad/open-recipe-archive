## Ingredients

- lamb head and feet
- salt water
- fat
- root vegetables
- onion
- broth
- heart and tongue
- liver

## Directions

1. Clean the lamb head and feet as specified for calf's head, cook them gently in salt water and clean again.
2. In a casserole, add fat, root vegetables and onion, place the head and feet, let them brown uncovered and pour the necessary broth over.
3. After some time, about 1/2 hour, add the heart and tongue, and 10 minutes before the meat is completely tender, add the liver.
4. When everything is sufficiently tender, remove it, cut it into desired pieces and arrange on a platter.
5. Make a flavorful, piquant sauce with the broth, pour it over the arranged meat and border the platter with cut-out butter pastry.
