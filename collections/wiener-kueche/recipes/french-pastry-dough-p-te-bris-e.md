## Ingredients

- Six pounds flour
- 3 pounds butter
- 2 ounces salt
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Place the flour on a table and make a depression in it
2. Into this depression put the butter, salt, egg yolks from the eggs, and one glass of water, more or less according to the proportion of other ingredients
3. Work the butter with the water, eggs, and salt
4. Ensure the butter is very soft, gradually mix in the flour, and gather everything together well
5. Once this dough is well compressed, knead it with your fingers until it has thoroughly combined; if it is too dry, moisten it somewhat
6. Do not knead this dough more than twice, as it could become grainy, especially in summer, and therefore not hold together properly
