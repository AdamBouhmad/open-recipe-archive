## Ingredients

- 1 measure Pontac or Ofner wine
- 0.5 loth cinnamon
- 0.5 quintal vanilla
- 12 loth sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- cold Pontac

## Directions

1. Heat 1 measure Pontac or Ofner wine, 0.5 loth cinnamon, 0.5 quintal vanilla and 12 loth sugar on the fire until boiling
2. Beat 8 egg yolks with some cold Pontac
3. As soon as the wine boils, pour the egg mixture into it
4. Hold over the fire for a few minutes while beating constantly until it rises
5. Serve in cups
