## Ingredients

- Half a pound flour
- 6 loths pounded sugar
- White of ten eggs
- A little wine

## Directions

1. Place flour and pounded sugar into an earthenware dish
2. Gradually stir in the white of ten eggs and a little wine, taking care not to stir too vigorously so it does not foam
3. Let this mass run through a funnel with a narrow opening into hot lard, forming round puffs
4. When they are done, lay them over a rolling pin so they become curved
5. Sprinkle them with sugar and cinnamon
