## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons hot butter
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut onion
- Calf's brain

## Directions

1. Roast 1/2 teaspoon of finely cut onion in 8 tablespoons of hot butter
2. When the onion is brown, add the peeled brain
3. Let it completely contract and serve quickly at table
