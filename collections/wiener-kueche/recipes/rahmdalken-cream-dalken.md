## Ingredients

- cream (Rahm)
- 10 dkg flour
- 5 dkg sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip the cream well with 10 dkg flour, 5 dkg sugar, 3 egg yolks and salt
2. Fold in 3 beaten egg whites
3. Heat fat in a dalken pan, pour in 1 tablespoon of dough into each indentation
4. Bake light brown on both sides
5. Remove dalken, add more fat and dough and continue until all mixture is used
6. Spread jam between two dalken, press together, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm
