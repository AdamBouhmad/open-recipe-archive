## Ingredients

- four drams of cooked rice
- warm water or meat broth
- lemon zest
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place four drams of previously cooked rice in a small bowl
2. Rub it with a spoon
3. Cook it in warm water or meat broth with some lemon zest and a little grated nutmeg
