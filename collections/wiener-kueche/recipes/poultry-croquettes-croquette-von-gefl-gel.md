## Ingredients

- cold roasted poultry meat
- béchamel sauce
- white bread, grated
- eggs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from cold roasted poultry and cut it finely
2. Make a béchamel and mix it with the cut meat
3. Spread it on a flat casserole lid the thickness of a thumb
4. When cold, cut it out with a small patty form
5. Roll in grated white bread
6. Pull through beaten eggs
7. A second time coat thickly with grated bread
8. Shortly before serving, fry quickly in butter or lard
9. Serve immediately
