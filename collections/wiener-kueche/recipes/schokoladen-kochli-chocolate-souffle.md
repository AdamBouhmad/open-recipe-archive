## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter
- 16 Deka sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 Deka crusts removed, bread softened in milk and well pressed out
- 8 egg whites
- 16 Deka grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat butter until fluffy
2. Stir in sugar, egg yolks for ¼ hour
3. Add bread with crusts removed, softened in milk, and well pressed out
4. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 16 Deka grated chocolate
5. Fill a suitable butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold with this mixture
6. Cook covered for ½ to ¾ hour in steam or bake in the oven
