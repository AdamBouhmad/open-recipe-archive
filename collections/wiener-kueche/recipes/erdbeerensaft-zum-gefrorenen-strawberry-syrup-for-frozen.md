## Ingredients

- Freshly picked wild strawberries
- 1 kilo strained strawberries
- 1 kilo finely pounded sugar
- Almond oil (optional)

## Directions

1. Freshly picked wild strawberries are passed through a fine sieve, then weighed
2. Then in an earthen or porcelain bowl 1 kilo strained strawberries are stirred with 1 kilo finely pounded sugar for 3 hours
3. Then filled into bottles, topped according to desire with some almond oil
4. Fitted with a suitable stopper and sealed
5. The bottles are then kept at a cool, breezy place, where however they must not lie, but always stand
6. This juice is used not only for frozen desserts, but also for creams, whipped cream and sauces
