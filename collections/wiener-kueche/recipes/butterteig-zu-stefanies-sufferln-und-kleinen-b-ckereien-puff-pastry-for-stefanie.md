## Ingredients

- 24 Deca beaten butter
- 8 Deca finely crushed sugar
- 1 knife-point finely cut lemon peel
- 3 egg yolks
- 32 Deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 24 Deca beaten butter gradually with 8 Deca finely crushed sugar, 1 knife-point of finely cut lemon peel and 3 egg yolks until well mixed.
2. Mix thoroughly with 32 Deca flour.
3. Smooth the dough on a flour-dusted board and let it rest covered at a cold place.
4. Once the dough feels firm, roll it out on a flour-dusted board knife-back thick, divide it into 2 equal parts.
5. Roll one part onto a rolling pin and unroll it onto a buttered baking sheet.
6. Brush the dough with beaten egg, leaving a finger-width border free on all 4 sides, which brush with whipped egg white.
7. Roll the second part of the dough onto the rolling pin and place it over the brushed dough so that the edges lie evenly on one another, pressing them together.
8. Brush the dough with whipped egg and bake in the oven at medium heat until golden.
9. Remove the baking sheet and once the dough has completely cooled, glaze it with glaze.
10. Once the glaze is completely dry, cut the dough into finger-width strips.
