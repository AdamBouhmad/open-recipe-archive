## Ingredients

- 1 large cabbage head
- 0 tablespoons fat
- 10 tablespoons hash
- 8 tablespoons very finely cut smoked ham
- 4 tablespoons soaked rolls without crust, well pressed
- 2 small pieces potatoes, cut into cubes
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- 2 whole eggs
- salt and spices
- 1 knife-point paprika, if available
- 3 small pieces finely cut cooked mushrooms
- salt water or fatty broth
- 3/4 hour
- twine
- 1 1/2 liters cream

## Directions

1. Dip 1 large cabbage head in boiling water
2. Remove the 3 outer coarse leaves by letting the stem remain at the bottom
3. Take out the inner leaves (petals), cut finely, roast slightly in approximately 0 tablespoons fat with a little broth and stew gently
4. Mix together 10 tablespoons hash, 8 tablespoons very finely cut smoked ham, 4 tablespoons soaked rolls without crust, well pressed, 2 small pieces potatoes cut into cubes, 1/4 liter milk cream, 2 whole eggs, some salt and spices, and if available 1 knife-point paprika, 3 small pieces finely cut cooked mushrooms
5. Place this mixture in the middle of the cabbage, overlap the leaves, bind with twine
6. Place in a casserole, pour salt water or fatty broth over it and cook covered for 3/4 hour
7. Then strain the liquid and pour 1 1/2 liters cream over it, then let it stew for about 1/2 hour
8. Before serving, loosen the twine from the head, place it on a platter, salt and season according to taste the juice that has formed with paprika, and pour the juice over the cabbage head
