## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons fine flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons wine
- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Put 5 tablespoons fine flour and 3 tablespoons sugar into a saucepan
2. Add gradually 5 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons wine
3. Stir this very finely and gently fold in the stiff beaten egg whites from 4 eggs
4. Drop this batter through a funnel or pastry bag into very hot fat
5. When the strands are baked, remove them with a pastry spoon, bend them over the back of an oblong rounded form, and arrange them sprinkled with sugar
6. Serve with any desired chaudeau sauce
