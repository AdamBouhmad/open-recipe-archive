## Ingredients

- Mutton cutlets
- Butter, fat, or oil
- Salt and pepper
- Paper for papillotes
- Braising sauce or mustard sauce or vegetables of choice

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets neatly and beat them slightly.
2. Dip in butter, fat, or oil, salt briefly before use.
3. Grill on both sides on a grill grate over strong heat.
4. Cover the bones with papillotes (paper cuffs).
5. Arrange the cutlets neatly on a platter.
6. Place a thick braising or mustard sauce or vegetables of choice in the center.
7. These cutlets can also be given as a garnish with mushroom, truffle, asparagus, or calf's liver preparations.
8. Alternatively, they can be braised in a light (à la minute) pan with little fat.
