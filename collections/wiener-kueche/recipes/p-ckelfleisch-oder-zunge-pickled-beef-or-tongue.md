## Ingredients

- A lean piece of beef or a tongue
- Ordinary salt
- Salt petre
- Coriander
- Bay leaves
- Pepper
- Juniper berries
- Rosemary
- Grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Take half ordinary salt and half salt petre and rub the beef or tongue designated for this well with it and place it in a screw-top vessel
2. Several pieces can however be placed together better
3. On each piece sprinkle some ordinary and salt petre, coriander, bay leaves, pepper and juniper berries, as well as some rosemary and a little grated horseradish
4. Screw it down well or weigh it down so that the brine covers it
5. Large pieces must always lie for 14 days
6. The tongues are cooked soft for use, and before serving are wrapped in a roll or scattered with various green herbs, served cold
