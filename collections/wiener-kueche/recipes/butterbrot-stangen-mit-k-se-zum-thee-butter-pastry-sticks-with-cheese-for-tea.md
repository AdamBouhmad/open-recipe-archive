## Ingredients

- Butter pastry dough of finest kind, unsweetened
- Eggs, beaten
- Grated Parmesan or Emmental cheese

## Directions

1. Roll the butter pastry dough to the thickness of a knife back and cut into strips 2 fingers wide and 2 fingers long.
2. Brush lightly with beaten eggs, but not the edges.
3. Place grated Parmesan or Emmental cheese, one small-finger high, in the middle.
4. Fold the dough over on itself, press together, cut with a knife or needle, pierce the dough with a knife, brush with beaten eggs.
5. Bake in the oven and remove carefully with a knife.
6. Serve immediately. The sticks should be enjoyed fresh.
