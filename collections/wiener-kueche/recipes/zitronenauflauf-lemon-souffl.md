## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- juice and zest of ½ lemon
- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. 30 minutes before serving, beat the 4 egg yolks with 7 dkg sugar until very foamy.
2. Stir in the juice and zest of ½ lemon and the 4 beaten egg whites.
3. Bake the same way as the chocolate soufflé.
4. This mixture is sufficient for 4 persons. For 6 persons, one must take 2 more eggs and correspondingly more of the other ingredients.
