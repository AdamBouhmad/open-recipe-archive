## Ingredients

- crepes
- cream filling
- 0.5 liter milk
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 6 hectograms sugar
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the crepes as indicated in 'Baked Crepes' (No. 1451), coat with any thick but smooth cream, roll together and cut into half-finger-long pieces
2. Place these in a short casserole lined thickly with butter and dusted with flour
3. Beat 0.5 liter milk with 3 whole eggs, 3 egg yolks and 6 hectograms sugar together, pour over the crepes
4. Let them cook covered for 3/4 to 1 hour in the broth or bake in the oven
