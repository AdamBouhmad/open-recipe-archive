## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt water
- lemon juice
- beer or wine batter
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Peel and remove the leaves from the artichokes, cook them soft in salted water with lemon juice
2. Dip in beer or wine batter, fry in hot lard and serve heaped on a platter
