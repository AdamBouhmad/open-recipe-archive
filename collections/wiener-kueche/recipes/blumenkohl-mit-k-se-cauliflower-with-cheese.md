## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- Parmesan cheese
- butter sauce
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. After the cauliflower is cleaned and blanched in water, lay it on cloth to dry
2. Dress it on a dish and sprinkle Parmesan cheese over it
3. Make a butter sauce and also take grated cheese with it
4. Pour this sauce over the cauliflower
5. Sprinkle again with cheese
6. Then set it in an oven until it becomes beautifully golden and crispy
7. Remove the butter with blotting paper when serving and arrange the cauliflower hot
