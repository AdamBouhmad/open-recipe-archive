## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the skin as carefully as possible from the oranges until the finest part, then cut them into round slices or divide them into sections and lay them in a casserole with clarified sugar for a quarter hour.
2. You must first have rubbed this sugar on the oranges.
3. When this has boiled a couple of times and the syrup is somewhat thick, arrange them for the table.
