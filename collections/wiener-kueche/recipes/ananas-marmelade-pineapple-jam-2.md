## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo butter
- 1/4 liter cold water
- 1 kilo peeled pineapple, cut in slices
- 1/2 kilo finely pounded sugar
- 1 1/4 liters water

## Directions

1. Put into a stone mortar 1/2 kilo butter, 1/4 liter cold water and 1 kilo peeled, sliced pineapple
2. This is pounded very finely into a dough
3. Then with 1/2 kilo finely pounded sugar and 1 1/4 liters water in a saucepan placed on the fire and cooked under constant stirring for approximately 10 minutes
4. Then quickly passed through a sieve and cooked again for 10 minutes
5. Then fill this jam hot into jars
6. One must be very careful in this preparation, so that the pineapple does not lose its yellow color through too long cooking
