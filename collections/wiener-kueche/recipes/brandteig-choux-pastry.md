## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk
- 5 Deca butter
- 22 Deca flour
- 5 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter of milk and 5 Deca of butter in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
2. Stir in 22 Deca of flour and stir this dough over the fire until it is dry and separates from the spoon and saucepan.
3. Transfer the dough to a deep bowl and let it cool with occasional stirring.
4. Once cooled, stir in 5 whole eggs, which causes the dough to stretch and take on a yellow color.
5. Neither sugar nor salt should be added during the preparation of this dough.
6. Use for various purposes.
