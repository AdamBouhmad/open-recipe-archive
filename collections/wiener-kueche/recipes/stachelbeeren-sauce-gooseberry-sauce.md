## Ingredients

- 6 deca butter
- equal amount flour
- 3/4 liter ripe gooseberries, mashed
- necessary broth
- some white wine

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter and flour
2. Add the mashed ripe gooseberries
3. Pour in the necessary broth and some white wine
4. Season to taste and let it cook well
5. This sauce can be served strained or unstrained
