## Ingredients

- Veal breast and rib bones
- Lard pieces
- Salt
- Spices
- Soup aspic glaze

## Directions

1. After the breast is removed from the piece and the rib bones are hacked to 3 finger lengths, the veal back remains from the second to seventh rib
2. Hack away the backbone bones with the ribs, remove all skin from the thick meat, rub with lard and spices
3. Lard it with pencil-thick pieces of lard, half-finger length long
4. Quickly roast it on a spit
5. Coat the meat with dissolved aspic glaze, remove from the spit, cut into pieces, arrange on a platter and serve with any desired brown sauce, fine vegetables, or salad
