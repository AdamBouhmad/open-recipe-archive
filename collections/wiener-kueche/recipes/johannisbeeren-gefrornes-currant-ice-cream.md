## Ingredients

- 2 and a half pounds red currants (Ribisel)
- 1 pound ripe cherries
- 1 small glass wine
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Select 2 and a half pounds red currants and strip them from the stems
2. Take 1 pound ripe cherries, remove the stems and pits
3. Press these fruits through a hair sieve into a bowl
4. Press through with a small glass of wine to extract the strength of the fruits
5. Stir in three quarter pound clarified sugar, cool everything well, and then process as usual
