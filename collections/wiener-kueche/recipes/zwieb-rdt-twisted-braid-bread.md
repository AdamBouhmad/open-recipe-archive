## Ingredients

- 12 Deca butter
- 8 Deca finely sifted sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 24 Deca flour
- unsalted bacon fat for coating

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deca butter until creamy and add 8 Deca finely sifted sugar and 4 egg yolks one by one.
2. Stir in 3 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk along with 24 Deca flour and mix well.
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon.
4. Place on a flour-dusted board, divide into five parts, and form two of these parts into finger-length, finger-thick rolls.
5. Place on a buttered baking sheet, cover, and let rise once more in a warm place.
6. Bake on the sheet in the oven at fairly strong heat until golden.
7. Remove from the sheet with a knife and cool on a board or sieve.
8. The next day, cut each roll lengthwise into two equal parts, place cut-side up on a dry sheet, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with chopped pistachios mixed with coarsely cut almonds, and bake until light brown at low heat.
