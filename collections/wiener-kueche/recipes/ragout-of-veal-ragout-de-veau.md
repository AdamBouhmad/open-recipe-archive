## Ingredients

- 1 calf's spinal cord
- 12 ounces butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- 1 knife-tip of nutmeg
- 1 knife-tip of white pepper
- 16 ounces white bread crumbs, moistened with milk
- flour for lining the mold

## Directions

1. Blanch the calf's spinal cord
2. Beat 12 ounces butter until fluffy
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
4. Stir this into the prepared spinal cord
5. Stir in 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley, 1 knife-tip of nutmeg and 1 knife-tip of white pepper
6. Mix in 16 ounces white bread crumbs, moistened with milk, to form a dough
7. Cook in a buttered mold lined with flour in a steamer, or bake in a shallow casserole
8. Cut into desired pieces and add to the soup
