## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beautiful strawberries
- 1 seidel cream
- one quarter pound sugar
- one piece cinnamon
- 6 ground chicken gizzard membranes

## Directions

1. Wash 2 pounds beautiful strawberries and press them through a hair sieve as finely as possible
2. Cook 1 seidel cream with one quarter pound sugar and one piece cinnamon
3. When the cream is lukewarm, add 6 ground chicken gizzard membranes and strain several times through a hair sieve
4. Place on some warm ash, cover with a lid, and lay only a few coals around it
5. Let the cream stand a quarter hour so it becomes like a jelly
6. Set the plate in the cellar, though it is always better if this is done in a cream cup
