## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 8 decagrams peeled almonds
- 3 egg yolks, finely ground
- 14 decagrams sifted sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 10 decagrams butter
- 3 tablespoons strong black coffee
- 16 decagrams butter
- coarse sugar
- finely sliced almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold in 8 decagrams of peeled almonds ground finely with 3 egg yolks, along with 14 decagrams of sifted sugar, mixing well.
2. Line a baking sheet with paper and divide the mixture into three equal parts, spreading each finger-height thick with a knife on the paper, forming three round plates of equal size.
3. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and bake in the oven at moderate heat for 1 1/4 hours.
4. Meanwhile, stir together 3 egg yolks, 10 decagrams butter, and 3 tablespoons strong black coffee in a small pot over low heat until the mixture becomes thick, but do not allow it to boil.
5. Beat 16 decagrams butter in a deep bowl until fluffy, then stir in the cooled coffee mixture and continue stirring until completely combined.
6. Once the baked plates have cooled completely and are removed from the paper, spread the filling between the layers, stack them together, and spread the remaining mixture on the top plate.
7. Sprinkle with coarse sugar and finely sliced almonds, or decorate as desired with a paper piping bag filled with the cream.
