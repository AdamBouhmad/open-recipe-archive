## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons hot butter or lard
- 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar
- 1 head peeled onion, finely sliced
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 liter soup
- Some vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. In 8 tablespoons hot butter or lard, add 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar and let it become dark brown
2. Brown 1 head peeled onion, finely sliced, with roasting
3. Stir 8 tablespoons flour to it, let it roast a bit more
4. Pour it with 3/4 liter soup and some vinegar
5. Salt it and simmer, let it boil up and pass through a sieve
6. The sauce must be pleasantly sweet-sour
