## Ingredients

- 3 large, beautiful, straight green cucumbers
- veal forcemeat or fish forcemeat
- bacon leaves
- boiling salt water

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut them in half lengthwise, hollow them out and fill firmly with veal forcemeat or fish forcemeat.
2. Wrap them in thin bacon leaves.
3. Place in a shallow casserole so that the filled end lies against the side of the casserole.
4. Add enough boiling salt water to cover the cucumbers.
5. Braise covered until soft.
6. Carefully remove them, cut into desired pieces and use for dressing beef or other meat dishes.
