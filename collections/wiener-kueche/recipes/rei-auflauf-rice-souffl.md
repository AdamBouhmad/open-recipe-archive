## Ingredients

- One pound rice
- Water
- One measure milk
- One Seidel cream
- One piece cinnamon
- Half pound butter
- One-quarter pound sugar
- Zest of one lemon
- One-quarter pound large raisins
- Equal amount small raisins
- 16 egg yolks
- Egg whites from 12 eggs
- Butter for greasing
- Finely grated roll crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well, boil it in water with some salt, then cool it in cold water.
2. Cook one measure milk and one Seidel cream with a piece of cinnamon, mix the rice into it, and let it swell on weak fire.
3. When the rice is soft, remove it, add half pound butter, and set it to cool.
4. When cooled, stir in one-quarter pound sugar with grated lemon zest, one-quarter pound large raisins, an equal amount small raisins, and 16 egg yolks.
5. Finally add the snow from 12 eggs.
6. Grease the mold or ring well with butter and sprinkle it with finely grated roll crumbs.
