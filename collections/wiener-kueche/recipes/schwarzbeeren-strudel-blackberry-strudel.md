## Ingredients

- Strudel dough
- black currants, picked
- raisins
- currants
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Fill with black currants picked from the berries instead of apples
2. Preparation is otherwise the same as for Apple Strudel (No. 1238)
