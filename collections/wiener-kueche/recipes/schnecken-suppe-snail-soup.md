## Ingredients

- snails
- salt
- water
- hard-boiled eggs
- onions
- green parsley
- bread soaked in milk
- flour
- pea broth
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the snails in salted water until the lids loosen
2. Remove them from their shells with a fork
3. Clean them by removing the tail, peeling off the brown skin, cutting off the front point and removing the stinger
4. Lay them in a bowl, rub with salt to remove the slime, and rinse with fresh water
5. Chop them finely together with some hard-boiled eggs, onions, green parsley and bread soaked in milk
6. Make a roux from one spoonful of flour in a pot
7. Add the chopped mixture
8. Pour in not-too-thick pea broth
9. Season with pepper and salt
