## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter clear, hot vinegar
- heavy cream
- salt to taste
- white pepper to taste
- nutmeg to taste
- lemon juice to taste
- tarragon vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Pour 1/4 liter of clear, hot vinegar into a sauce pot and whip it with a whisk on ice until it forms stiff foam
2. When this is the case, gradually pour in 1/4 liter heavy cream while constantly stirring with the whisk
3. Flavor to taste with lemon juice and tarragon vinegar
4. Add horseradish, white pepper, and nutmeg as needed
5. Transfer to a clean dish and keep on ice until serving
6. Serve with fish, lobster, and langoustine
