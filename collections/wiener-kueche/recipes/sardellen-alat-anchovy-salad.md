## Ingredients

- Anchovies
- Wine
- Lemon
- Flowers
- Citrus fruits (lemons and oranges)
- Orange blossoms
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper
- Preserved orange blossoms

## Directions

1. First soak the anchovies, after removing the bones, in wine for one hour
2. Place a small celery in the middle of a serving dish with an artfully cut lemon on top
3. Decorate it with flowers, encircle the lemon with anchovies winding around it, and arrange the remaining anchovies in a circle around it, with the tails lying on the edge of the dish
4. Line the dish edge with lemons or orange blossoms
5. Mix together vinegar, oil and some pepper and pour over the anchovies
6. Squeeze lemon juice over them, arrange thinly sliced lemon peel wound around the dish
7. Mix them alternately with sliced oranges and lemons, arrange them at the edge of the dish
8. Sprinkle the anchovies with flowerwork and preserved orange blossoms
