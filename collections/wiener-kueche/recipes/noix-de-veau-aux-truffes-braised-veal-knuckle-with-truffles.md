## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo truffles
- butter
- brown gravy
- juice of 1 lemon
- several spoonfuls Madeira wine
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Peel the truffles and cut them into knife-blade-thick slices
2. Braise these slices in butter for a few minutes
3. Add brown gravy, juice of 1 lemon, and several spoonfuls of Madeira wine, season to taste, stir well
4. Add this mixture to the veal knuckle as described in recipe 585
