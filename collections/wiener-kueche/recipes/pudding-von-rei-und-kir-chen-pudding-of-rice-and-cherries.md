## Ingredients

- 3 Pfund rice
- 2 Pfund sour cherries
- 1/2 Pfund butter
- 10 eggs
- 7 Pfund sugar
- 4 Pfund flour
- zest of one lemon
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash the rice several times and let it swell in milk, then put it in a colander to drain
2. Pit the cherries with a feather quill, briefly cook them with sugar, and when cooled, add them to the mass
3. Set aside the whites of 5 eggs; you can also add some bread crumbs if desired
4. Otherwise treat this mass as usual
5. Cook in water for 12 hours
6. When serving, pour wine foam sauce over it
