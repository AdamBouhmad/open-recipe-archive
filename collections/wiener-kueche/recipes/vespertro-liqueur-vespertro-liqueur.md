## Ingredients

- 1 Maß good brandy
- 2 drams angelica roots
- 1 ounce coriander
- anise and fennel, as much as can be held between four fingers
- yellow peel from 2 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Crush all roots and seeds roughly together
2. Place in brandy
3. Carefully cut the yellow peel from 2 lemons so no white part is included
4. Add the lemon peel and juice to the brandy
5. Add 1.5 pounds sugar
6. Place everything in a preserving glass
7. Let stand for 6 days, stirring twice daily
8. On the seventh day, filter through filter paper with a funnel
9. Fill into bottles, cork well, and store in a cool place
10. The older it becomes, the better it is
