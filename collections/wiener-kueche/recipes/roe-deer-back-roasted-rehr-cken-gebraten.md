## Ingredients

- venison back
- vinegar
- onion
- brine/marinade
- bacon
- paper
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the back like the haunch, namely marinated in vinegar and onion, and let it lie in brine for one or two days
2. Remove the skin cleanly, lard it with bacon, cover it with paper, roast it slowly on a spit, and baste it a few times with brine and cream mixed together
3. When well roasted, place it on a dish and garnish it like the venison haunch
