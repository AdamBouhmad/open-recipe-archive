## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter (or proportionally more as needed)
- flour
- 1/2 pound lean ham cut into cubes
- onions sliced
- parsley roots
- good brown stock

## Directions

1. Melt 1/2 pound of butter (or proportionally more depending on desired quantity), adding enough flour so it becomes neither stiff nor too thick, but can be stirred comfortably
2. Stir this continuously on moderately strong heat until it takes on a light brown color
3. Add 1/2 pound of lean ham cut into cubes, some onion slices, and parsley roots
4. Braise or brown these in it for a few more minutes
5. Then pour in as much good brown stock as desired for the coulis
6. Let it cook very slowly from the side
7. During cooking, remove all rising fat and foam with a spoon
8. Once the coulis has cooked long enough to become properly thick, pass it through cheesecloth and use it
