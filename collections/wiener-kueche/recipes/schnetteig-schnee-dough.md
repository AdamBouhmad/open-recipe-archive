## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter fine flour
- 1/4 liter white wine
- 1 tablespoon oil
- Salt to taste
- Egg whites from 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter fine flour into a deep bowl.
2. Pour 1/4 liter white wine gradually while stirring constantly, along with 1 tablespoon of oil which has been previously heated until boiling on the plate.
3. Stir in salt with a knife point until smooth.
4. Lightly fold in the whipped egg white from 4 eggs.
5. Use the dough for various purposes.
