## Ingredients

- Ham
- Shallots
- Crayfish butter
- Coulis
- Thick sour cream
- Lemon juice
- Ordinary butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Sauté ham and shallots in crayfish butter.
2. Fill with coulis and stir in thick sour cream.
3. Once everything is cooked, strain through a fine cloth.
4. Press lemon juice into the sauce.
5. This sauce can also be made with ordinary butter.
