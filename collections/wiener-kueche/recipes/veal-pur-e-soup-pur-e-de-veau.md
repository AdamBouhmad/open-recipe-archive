## Ingredients

- 1 mushroom
- a little parsley
- 1 shallot
- 6 ounces butter
- approximately 1 liter finely minced veal meat remains
- 8 ounces rice
- 2.5 liters beef soup
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Roast 1 mushroom, a little parsley and 1 shallot in 6 ounces butter
2. Add approximately 1 liter of finely minced veal meat remains and 8 ounces rice
3. Pour in 2.5 liters of beef soup and let it cook until the rice is completely soft
4. Pass through a fine sieve
5. Boil briefly, add salt and spices if desired
6. Serve with braised bread pieces
