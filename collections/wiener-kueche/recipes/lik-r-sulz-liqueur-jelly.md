## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar, clarified
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- 1/2 liter liqueur of choice, instead of wine, lemon and orange juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
2. Instead of wine, lemon and orange juice, use 1/2 liter of liqueur of choice
