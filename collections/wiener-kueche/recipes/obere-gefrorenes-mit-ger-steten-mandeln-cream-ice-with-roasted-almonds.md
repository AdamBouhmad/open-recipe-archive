## Ingredients

- 16 deca peeled almonds
- 8 deca sugar
- 1/8 liter cream
- 1 liter good cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Toast 16 deca peeled almonds with 8 deca sugar until yellow
2. Pound them very finely with 1/8 liter cream and heat this with 1 liter good cream until boiling
3. Let cool covered and press through cloth
4. Add this liquid to 8 egg yolks and 16 deca sugar in a saucepan and proceed further as with Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720)
