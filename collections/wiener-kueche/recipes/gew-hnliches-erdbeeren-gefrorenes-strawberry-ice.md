## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liter fresh strawberries
- 28 deca fine sugar
- 1/4 liter cold water
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Pass 1 1/4 liter fresh strawberries by means of a wooden spoon through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl
2. Stir 28 deca fine sugar with 1/4 liter cold water, mix this with the juice of 2 lemons into the strawberries
3. Pass the whole again through a fine sieve and stir well together
4. To determine if this mixture contains the necessary sugar content for freezing, use the so-called sugar gauge, by which one can determine exactly how many degrees of sugar the mixture contains
5. Weigh the prepared strawberry juice with this sugar gauge by dipping it like a thermometer into the liquid where the gauge should show 22 degrees
6. If the juice does not reach this degree, add the necessary sugar stirred in some water, while in the opposite case
