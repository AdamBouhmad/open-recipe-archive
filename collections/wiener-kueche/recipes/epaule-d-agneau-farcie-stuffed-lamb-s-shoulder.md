## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- veal forcemeat
- twine
- salt water
- bacon lardons, pencil-thick and half-finger long

## Directions

1. When the shoulder bone has been removed and the resulting cavity filled with veal forcemeat, tie the shoulder with twine (i.e., sew it together), place in cold water, set to the fire and let it come to a boil.
2. Remove the meat, place it again in cold water, dry with a cloth, salt it, lard it with pencil-thick, half-finger long pieces of bacon and further treat as the glazed lamb leg.
