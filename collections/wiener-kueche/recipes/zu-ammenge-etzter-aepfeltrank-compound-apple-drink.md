## Ingredients

- twenty Loth of peeled apples (Maschanzger or Renetten variety)
- one Seidel of water
- one Quentchen of crushed nutmeg
- two Loth of grated bread
- four Loth of Rhine wine
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take twenty Loth of peeled apples (Maschanzger or Renetten variety)
2. Cook them in one Seidel of water in a covered pot for one hour
3. Press them through a cloth
4. Add one Quentchen of crushed nutmeg
5. Add two Loth of grated bread
6. Add four Loth of Rhine wine
7. Add as much sugar as needed for pleasant taste
