## Ingredients

- Half a pound of butter
- Seven pounds of sugar (one pound added later)
- One seidel of milk
- Three pounds of flour
- 16 egg yolks
- Salt
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat half a pound of butter, seven pounds of sugar, and one seidel of milk in a casserole
2. Stir in three pounds of flour until the dough is stiff
3. Let it cool
4. Stir in one pound more of sugar, 16 egg yolks, and some salt
5. Continue stirring for a while
6. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in so nothing is visible
7. Bind the pudding in a napkin that has been soaked in water for an hour
8. Cook in water salted with meat broth for the appropriate time
9. For the sauce: Set one pound of washed sago with finely cut lemon zest on the heat
10. When it begins to boil and becomes somewhat clear, pour in one seidel of red wine
11. Add as much sugar as needed, lemon slices, several spoonfuls of currant jelly, and some whole cinnamon
12. Let the broth cook until the sago is fully swollen
13. Pour this sauce over the pudding when serving
