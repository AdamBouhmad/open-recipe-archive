## Ingredients

- Pig's head, cleaned
- Onion, cut into pieces
- Root vegetables
- Parsnip, small amount
- Vinegar
- Water
- Salt
- Seasonings
- Brains

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from a cleaned pig's head into pieces
2. Boil it with a piece of cut onion, some root vegetables and a little parsnip in vinegar and water so that the liquid covers the meat (3/4 liter water and 1/2 liter vinegar)
3. Add salt and seasonings when the meat is sufficiently tender
4. Remove the meat with a wooden spoon and arrange it on a platter in a circle
5. Place grated brains in the middle
6. Serve with vinegar and oil
