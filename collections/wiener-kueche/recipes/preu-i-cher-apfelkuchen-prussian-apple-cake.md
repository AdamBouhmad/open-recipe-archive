## Ingredients

- 0.25 kilogram Masanzker apples
- 1 lemon
- 14 decagrams flour
- 11 decagrams butter
- 4 decagrams swelled, grated almonds
- 5 decagrams sugar
- 0.5 raw egg yolk and 1 hard-boiled egg yolk
- Finely chopped lemon peel
- 1 spoonful lemon juice
- Apples for filling
- Sugar cubes
- Lemon peel
- Water
- Raisins
- Egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from 14 decagrams flour, 11 decagrams butter, 4 decagrams swelled grated almonds, 5 decagrams sugar, 0.5 raw and 1 hard-boiled egg yolk, finely chopped lemon peel, and 1 spoonful lemon juice (see doughs)
2. After resting, roll out thinly and cut into a rectangle
3. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and bake in the oven until light brown
4. Spread the filling on top
5. Form thin rolls from the dough scraps, lay them in a lattice pattern on the filling
6. Brush with beaten egg and bake the cake at higher heat until done
7. Cut, sprinkle with sugar, and cool before serving
8. Make the filling the same way as for the Crumb Dough Apple Strudel
