## Ingredients

- 24 tablespoons rice
- 1 liter milk
- 16 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons hot water
- juice of 1 orange
- juice of 1 lemon
- 3 egg whites
- 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 24 tablespoons rice into 1 liter of boiling milk and let it slowly steam until soft and the milk is absorbed
2. Meanwhile, brown 16 tablespoons sugar in 2 tablespoons butter until golden
3. Add 3 tablespoons hot water, the juice of 1 orange and 1 lemon
4. Bring once to a boil
5. Pour this into the cooked rice and mix well
6. Let it steam for a few minutes
7. Set the rice aside and stir in 3 tablespoons rum after it has cooled completely
8. Butter a serving dish
9. Layer the rice with hot marmalade, applesauce or compote
10. Repeat until the dish is full
11. Cover with stiff egg white snow mixed with 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar
12. Dust with sugar and bake in an open oven for about 10 to 15 minutes
13. Remove the dish and serve immediately
