## Ingredients

- eel
- salt water
- aspic
- butter
- mayonnaise
- cold mustard sauce

## Directions

1. The eel is prepared like the white-prepared eel, then let it cool in its broth, arrange it neatly on a platter and garnish it nicely with chopped and broken aspic, colored butter and the like. The mayonnaise is served in a sauce boat and the fish is somewhat brushed with it. Some cold mustard sauce can also be served with it. You can also keep the eel whole and prepare and arrange it as indicated in the braised eel recipe.
