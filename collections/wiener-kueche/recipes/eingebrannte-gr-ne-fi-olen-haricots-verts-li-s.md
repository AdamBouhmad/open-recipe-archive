## Ingredients

- green kidney beans
- salt water
- 12 ounces butter
- 12 ounces flour
- green parsley
- onion
- garlic (optional)
- soup stock
- vinegar
- sorrel (optional)
- 1/2 liter cream

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from the green kidney beans, break them in two and cook in salt water
2. Make a roux from 12 ounces of butter and in it roast some finely chopped parsley, onion, and if desired, a little garlic
3. Add 12 ounces of flour and roast
4. Stir in soup stock and some vinegar and let the roux cook well
5. Add the cooked kidney beans with some finely chopped sorrel
6. Pour in 1/2 liter of cream, stir well together and let it cook
7. Garlic and onion can be omitted
