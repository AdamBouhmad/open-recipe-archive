## Ingredients

- 5 decagrams bacon
- 1 large head onion
- 40 decagrams roast beef
- Paprika
- Salt
- Stock

## Directions

1. 5 decagrams of bacon and 1 large head onion are chopped very finely together with the chopping knife, placed in a casserole and golden roasted.
2. Meanwhile, 40 decagrams of roast beef is beaten thin, cut into finger-long pieces of equal width.
3. These pieces are added at the same time with 2 decagrams of paprika and some salt to the golden roasted onion and braised gently for 1 hour covered.
4. Then remove the fat, pour 1/2 liter of stock to it and let it boil well.
