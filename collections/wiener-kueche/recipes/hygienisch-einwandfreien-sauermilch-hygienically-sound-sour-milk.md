## Ingredients

- 1 liter good fresh milk
- Contents of one bottle of 'Vindobona' pure cultures for 'Sauermilch'

## Directions

1. Boil the fresh milk as usual and allow to cool to 30°C
2. Pour the contents of the 'Vindobona' pure cultures bottle for 'Sauermilch' into the milk at this temperature, stir well, and fill into bottles or pots
3. Let the vessels stand quietly near the stove (on the plate warmer) for 8 to 10 hours
4. After this time, the milk should already be thick and should then immediately be placed in cold water or an icebox
5. For further preparation, take one tablespoon of the finished sour milk per liter of fresh milk and proceed as initially described
