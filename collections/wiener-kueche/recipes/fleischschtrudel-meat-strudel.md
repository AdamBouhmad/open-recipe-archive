## Ingredients

- 15 dkg flour
- 1 egg
- salt
- ½ dl lukewarm water
- 35 dkg cooked beef or roast leftovers or ½ cooked pork spleen
- 3 dkg hot fat
- minced onion
- parsley
- beef broth or water
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a strudel dough from 15 dkg flour, 1 egg, salt, and ½ dl lukewarm water
2. Let rest under a warm casserole for 15 minutes
3. Stretch the dough not too thin and brush with egg white
4. Prepare the filling: finely chop 35 dkg cooked beef or roast leftovers or ½ cooked pork spleen
5. Heat 3 dkg fat in a casserole, sauté minced onion and parsley until golden
6. Add the meat and moisten with a little broth or water
7. Season with salt and pepper and keep warm
8. Distribute the filling on the stretched dough, trim the edges, and roll up the strudel
9. Using a floured spoon handle, measure off equal-sized pieces, cut them through, place on a floured board
10. Lay them in a large casserole in boiling beef broth for 8 minutes
