## Ingredients

- Boretsch (cleaned, salted, and pressed)
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and finely chop boretsch well.
2. Salt it and press out the liquid thoroughly.
3. Mix with vinegar, oil, and pepper.
4. Fresh cucumbers, peeled and finely chopped, can be prepared the same way.
