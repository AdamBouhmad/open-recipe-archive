## Ingredients

- 50 roasted chestnuts
- 25 roasted chestnuts (to be processed)
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- some cream
- 6 egg yolks
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Shell 50 roasted chestnuts, pound and pass 25 of them through a sieve
2. Add 12 tablespoons butter, 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar, 1 pinch salt, and some cream to the passed chestnuts
3. Stir over heat until it becomes a dough
4. Set aside and after it has cooled somewhat, gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
5. After completely cooling, place the mass on a flour-dusted board and divide into portions
6. Arrange them evenly separated, place a halved chestnut in the middle of each, and cover evenly with the dough
7. Dip in beaten egg, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry in very hot lard
8. Remove with a slotted spoon when light golden, place briefly on blotting paper, and arrange in a bowl covered with a napkin
