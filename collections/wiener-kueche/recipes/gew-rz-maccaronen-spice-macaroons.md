## Ingredients

- Sweet macaroon dough
- A spoonful of finely pounded cinnamon
- 6 to 8 finely pounded cloves
- A spoonful of finely chopped candied orange peel
- A spoonful of finely chopped citron
- Zest of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. To the sweet macaroon dough add a spoonful of finely pounded cinnamon, 6 to 8 finely pounded cloves, a spoonful of finely chopped candied orange peel, a spoonful of finely chopped citron, and the zest of 2 lemons
2. Stir all these ingredients thoroughly with the almond dough
3. Form into oval or round shapes
