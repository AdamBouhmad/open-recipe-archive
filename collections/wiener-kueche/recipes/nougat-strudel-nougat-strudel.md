## Ingredients

- Strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
- nougat

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from 1/2 liter flour and let it rest for 3-4 hours
2. Stretch it thin as indicated for Lung Strudel (No. 75)
3. Brush with nougat
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. Roll dough lightly together
6. Treat further as for Ham Strudel (No. 1229)
