## Ingredients

- 60-70 dkg beef (Rindszapfen)
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 finely chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- broth or water
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 2 bunches carrots
- 2 kohlrabi
- ½ kg round potatoes
- 3 mushrooms
- finely chopped lemon zest
- marjoram
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and cut into large cubes
2. In 6 dkg hot fat, sauté 1 finely chopped onion until lightly browned
3. Add the meat and 1 tablespoon vinegar
4. Pour in broth or water and braise covered for 2-2½ hours
5. Add finely chopped lemon zest, capers, marjoram, carrots cooked in salted water and cut into noodle shapes, kohlrabi cut into cubes, and potatoes, plus raw chopped mushrooms
6. Let everything braise together a while longer
7. Serve the meat on a round dish
