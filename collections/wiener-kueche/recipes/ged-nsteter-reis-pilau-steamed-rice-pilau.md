## Ingredients

- 16 deka rice, washed and blanched
- cold water
- 8 deka butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 small Spanish onion
- 5 bundles fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Place 16 deka rice in a casserole
2. Pour in enough cold water to cover it by a finger's height
3. Add 8 deka butter, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 small Spanish onion, and 5 bundles of fresh parsley
4. Stir well together and let the rice steam covered for 10-15 minutes
5. The liquid will evaporate and each grain of rice will remain separate
6. Remove the onion and parsley and use the rice as a garnish for various meat dishes
7. If a crust is desired on the rice, add 2 deka butter after the rice has steamed, and let it roast uncovered for a few minutes
