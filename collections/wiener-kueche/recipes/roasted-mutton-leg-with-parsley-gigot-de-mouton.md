## Ingredients

- 1 leg of mutton
- Salt and pepper
- Lard
- Fine table oil

## Directions

1. Beat the leg of mutton with the flat side of a meat cleaver and trim.
2. Rub the meat with salt and pepper.
3. Brush with fine table oil.
4. Cover with paper and let rest for 1 hour.
5. Place fat in a pan and lay the leg on it.
6. Roast in a hot oven at high heat, basting frequently with the drippings.
7. Remove the leg, cut into pieces of any size, arrange on a platter.
8. Serve with various vegetables.
