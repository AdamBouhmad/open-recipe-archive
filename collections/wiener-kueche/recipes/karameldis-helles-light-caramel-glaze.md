## Ingredients

- a proportionate quantity of butter
- sugar and water (as for 'Clear Sugar' No. 1542)

## Directions

1. Cook a proportionate quantity of butter until it reaches the thread stage ('clear sugar' No. 1542).
2. When a cake has been covered with any hot caramel and somewhat over-dried in the oven, pour the sugar over it and set the cake cold.
3. For a cake or a cake of about 30 drams, 24 drams of sugar are cooked with 1/4 liter of water to the thread stage.
