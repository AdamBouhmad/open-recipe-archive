## Ingredients

- rolls of any size
- roasted brain or marrow, or carp milt with herring milt prepared in butter and parsley, or any other desired filling
- cold milk
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Trim the rolls and halve them
2. Cut each half deeply enough to be able to insert desired filling
3. Dip filled rolls in cold milk, allowing them to absorb some
4. Dip in beaten eggs
5. Sprinkle generously with bread crumbs
6. Fry well in hot fat
7. Serve as a vegetable side dish
