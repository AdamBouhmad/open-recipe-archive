## Ingredients

- 1 braised veal knuckle
- macaroni with Parmesan sauce or browned noodles

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal knuckle as in recipe 585, but place macaroni with Parmesan sauce or browned noodles in the center of the platter instead of onion purée
