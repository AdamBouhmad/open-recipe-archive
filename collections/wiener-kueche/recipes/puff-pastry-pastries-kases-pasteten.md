## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Eggs
- Filling (beliebigen Fasch)

## Directions

1. Roll out puff dough knife-back-thick and cut regular squares of desired size with a knife
2. Place on a water-moistened baking sheet and brush with beaten eggs, leaving a knife-back-width border at the edge
3. Place desired filling in the middle of each square
4. Cover with equally large squares arranged in a diamond shape
5. Press the filling down slightly and brush again with beaten eggs, leaving a finger-width border
6. Bake in the oven at fairly strong heat until golden yellow
