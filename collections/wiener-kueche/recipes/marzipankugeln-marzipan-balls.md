## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 21 dkg sugar
- 16 dkg soaked grated almonds
- currant jam

## Directions

1. Stir 2 egg whites with 21 dkg sugar
2. Mix in 16 dkg soaked grated almonds
3. It must be a stiff mass from which you form equal-sized balls
4. Press in the top and fill with currant jam
5. Glaze them either white or red
