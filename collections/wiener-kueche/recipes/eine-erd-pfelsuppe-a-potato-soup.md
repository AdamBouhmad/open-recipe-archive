## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 medium potatoes, peeled
- much fat
- handful of onions
- parsley
- 2 spoons of fine flour
- beef broth or strong broth
- baked bread rolls cut into strips

## Directions

1. Slice 10 or 12 medium peeled potatoes into fine slices.
2. Put a handful of onions, parsley, and the potatoes into a casserole with much fat.
3. Let them braise together well, dust with 2 spoons of fine flour, and after it has stood for a while, fill it with good beef broth or better strong broth.
4. Add some baked bread roll strips and let them cook well together.
5. Before serving, pass it through a sieve and serve over baked diced rolls.
