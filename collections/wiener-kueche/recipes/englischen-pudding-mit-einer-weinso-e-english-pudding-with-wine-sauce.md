## Ingredients

- 2 rolls (Mundsemel)
- cream or milk for soaking
- 1/2 pound butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 2 pounds bone marrow, diced
- 1/2 pound crushed sugar
- 2 pounds finely cut almonds
- 2 pounds beautiful raisins
- zest of 1 lemon
- egg whites (from the eggs used)

## Directions

1. Remove the crusts from 2 rolls, cut them into pieces, and soak them in cream or milk.
2. Beat 1/2 pound butter with 12 egg yolks into a foam, then add 2 pounds bone marrow diced into cubes, 1/2 pound crushed sugar, 2 pounds finely cut almonds, the well-squeezed bread, and 2 pounds beautiful raisins gradually.
3. Add the rubbed zest sugar from a couple of lemons, then slowly fold in the egg white beaten to snow.
4. Proceed with this mass as described above, but it must cook for two hours.
