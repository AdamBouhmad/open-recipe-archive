## Ingredients

- 6 decagrams fresh picked violet flowers
- 40 decagrams boiling, clarified sugar (see recipe 1542)
- 1 teaspoon cochineal dye or 1 drop alkermes juice
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Clear juice of 2 lemons
- Cherry water or Champagne, as much as needed
- Total liquid of 1 liter

## Directions

1. Place 6 decagrams fresh picked violet flowers in a porcelain bowl
2. Pour 40 decagrams boiling, clarified sugar over it
3. Add 1 teaspoon cochineal dye or 1 drop alkermes juice
4. Leave covered overnight
5. The next day, heat it again, strain through cloth
6. Pour hot to 7 decagrams of hot dissolved isinglass
7. Strain this jelly mass through fine, thin linen cloth into a deep bowl
8. Add the clear juice of 2 lemons and as much cherry water or Champagne (preferred) so that the whole makes 1 liter of liquid
9. Stir somewhat and proceed as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
