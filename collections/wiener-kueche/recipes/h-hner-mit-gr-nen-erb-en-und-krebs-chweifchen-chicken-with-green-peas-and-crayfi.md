## Ingredients

- Small chickens
- Butter
- Butter sauce
- Green peas
- Green parsley
- Crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Blanch small chickens and cut them into four parts.
2. Make a butter sauce, add the chickens to it.
3. Sweat a pint of green peas with butter and green parsley until soft.
4. Add them to the chickens, also add shelled crayfish tails, and let it cook for another quarter hour.
5. When serving, place the chickens in the middle of the dish, arrange the peas like a ring around the edge, garnish with crayfish tails so that the peas always remain visible two fingers wide.
