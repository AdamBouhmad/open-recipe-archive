## Ingredients

- 30 dkg flour
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 5 dkg hot butter or fat

## Directions

1. Make a dough from flour, eggs, and salt (see noodle dough)
2. Cut into wide noodles
3. Cook in plenty of boiling salted water for 5 minutes
4. Drain and cool with cold water
5. Place in a casserole with 5 dkg hot butter or fat
6. Season with salt and warm in the oven
