## Ingredients

- Fresh veal leg
- Bacon, thumb-thick pieces
- Rosemary
- Bay leaves
- Wine
- Water
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Nutmeg
- Cloves
- Basil
- Clay

## Directions

1. Skin a beautiful fresh veal leg
2. Lard it with bacon pieces the thickness of a small finger, using a particularly long larding needle to ensure the bacon is threaded through completely
3. Hack off the bone completely
4. Place it in a vessel lined with rosemary and bay leaves
5. Pour two-thirds wine and one-third water over it
6. Season with pepper, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves
7. Add more rosemary, basil, and bay leaves
8. Cover the vessel tightly and seal it with clay
