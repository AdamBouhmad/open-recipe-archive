## Ingredients

- Eel, tuna, Rhine salmon, sea pike, gudgeon, herring, smoked salmon, sardines, Russian marinated sardines
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Shallots
- Star anise
- Caviar
- Large piece of butter
- Cleaned, split sardines
- Smoked fish or anchovies
- Capers
- Olives
- Lemon slices
- Orange pieces
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cut the skinned, marinated or boiled fish into neat pieces and pour vinegar and oil over them
2. If desired, crush shallots and star anise finely, pour vinegar over them, let stand for 1/4 hour and then strain over the fish
3. Serve the salad in a round bowl with the fish arranged elevated if possible
4. From a large piece of butter, scrape a leaf using a tablespoon or blunt knife, holding the knife so the butter pushes against the thumb to form a ribbed leaf of any desired size
5. Arrange the butter leaves to form a dome or shell shape, place it elevated in the center of the fish and fill it shortly before serving with caviar
6. Garnish the bowl with cleaned, split, washed sardines with bones removed, arrange them in a spiral shape and place some smoked fish or capers in the center
7. Decorate the dish with olives that have had the pit carefully removed and replaced with a small piece of smoked salmon or anchovy
8. Also garnish with small butter flowers shaped and filled with caviar or olives
9. Add colored butter, capers, lemon slices, orange pieces and similar items
10. Serve vinegar and oil on the side
11. Serve as an appetizer with supper, tea or breakfast
12. Serve with Sherry wine
