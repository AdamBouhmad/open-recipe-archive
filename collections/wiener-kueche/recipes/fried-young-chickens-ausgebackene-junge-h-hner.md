## Ingredients

- young chickens
- water
- parsley
- shallots
- onions
- basil
- cloves
- lemon peel
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bouillon
- eggs
- white bread, grated
- flour
- lard (schmalz)

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the chickens
2. Cut them properly and lay in lukewarm water to become white
3. After an hour, spread them on a clean cloth and let them dry
4. Make a marinade of fine parsley, shallots, onions, basil, cloves, lemon peel, some vinegar, salt, pepper and bouillon
5. Set the cut chickens in the marinade on hot ashes so they can take on a good flavor
6. Finally remove them from the marinade to cool
7. Dip in beaten eggs
8. Coat with grated bread and flour
9. Fry somewhat quickly in lard until golden
