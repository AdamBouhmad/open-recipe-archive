## Ingredients

- half pound marzipan dough
- 4 to 5 egg whites
- one quarter pound farina sugar
- half pound sweet almonds, cut into fine elongated pieces
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pound marzipan dough in 4 to 5 egg whites.
2. Add one quarter pound farina sugar, half a pound sweet almonds which must be cut into fine elongated pieces beforehand, and some ground cinnamon.
3. Stir everything well together.
