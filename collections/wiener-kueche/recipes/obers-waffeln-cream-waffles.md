## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter whipped cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 6 deca flour
- 4 deca orange sugar
- 1 knife-tip salt
- 4 egg whites
- 10 deca melted but cooled butter

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 liter whipped cream into a snow beaker which is set in crushed ice and whip it with the snow whisk into a thick snow, let it stand covered for a while and pour off the buttermilk that will have settled in this time. Meanwhile stir 5 egg yolks and 6 deca flour with some whipped cream smooth and give this with 4 deca orange sugar and 1 knife-tip salt to the whipped snow, fold lightly and fold in the snow from 4 egg whites lightly. Pour 10 deca melted but cooled butter on top and fold in lightly.
2. Baking and further treatment is as described for 'Waffles from Batter' (No. 1648).
