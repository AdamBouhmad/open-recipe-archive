## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Vermicelli
- Salt water

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickens as described for 'Chickens Braised in Their Own Juice' (No. 822).
2. Cook the vermicelli in salt water, then drain.
3. Mix the cooked vermicelli well with the juice from the chickens and serve with the chickens arranged in a circular pattern on a platter.
