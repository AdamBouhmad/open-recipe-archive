## Ingredients

- strudel dough made from: 20 dkg flour, 1 egg, salt, 1 dl lukewarm water
- filling made from: 9 dkg fat, 3 egg yolks, salt, more than 1/2 l cream, 11 dkg semolina, 3 egg whites
- flour
- fat for roasting
- Zwetſchkenröſter (plum sauce)

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from flour, egg, salt and water (see Teige section).
2. Let it rest, then stretch thin.
3. Cut off the edges and brush the dough with egg white.
4. Prepare the filling: beat egg whites, then beat in fat, egg yolks, salt, cream and semolina.
5. Distribute the filling on the dough and roll into a strudel on the table.
6. Using a floured wooden spoon handle, press through to mark equal-sized pieces and cut with a knife.
7. Place on a floured board.
8. Cook the pieces in boiling salted water for 5-8 minutes in a large pot.
9. Lift onto a round platter, pour over with breadcrumbs roasted in fat and serve with Zwetſchkenröſter as a sweet dish or without it as a side to Beuſchel.
