## Ingredients

- 15 dkg butter
- 15 dkg grated chocolate
- 6 egg yolks
- 15 dkg sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 13½ dkg potato flour
- marmalade
- chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Beat the butter for 15 minutes
2. Stir in the grated chocolate
3. Gradually mix in the egg yolks with the sugar
4. Beat the entire mixture for 45 minutes total
5. Gently fold in the egg whites with the potato flour
6. Fill the batter into the cake form and bake very slowly over weak heat
7. Bake the day before serving
8. The next day, cut the top level, turn it over, brush with marmalade and coat with chocolate glaze
