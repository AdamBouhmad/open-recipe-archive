## Ingredients

- Sprouts
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 8 drams butter
- 8 drams flour
- Broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut away as much of the white stem as needed so the sprout does not fall apart; boil it in salt water and place it in cold water.
2. Make a butter sauce from 8 drams butter and 8 drams flour; pour it with broth over the drained sprouts; add the necessary salt and let it boil a bit longer.
