## Ingredients

- 16 spoonfuls rolled barley (graupen)
- 0.75 liter white broth
- clear white or brown broth

## Directions

1. Place 16 spoonfuls rolled barley in a casserole
2. Pour 0.75 liter white broth over it and let it cook slowly until soft, covered
3. Remove the barley with a sachet spoon or press through a sieve
4. Add to clear white or brown broth in the soup pot
