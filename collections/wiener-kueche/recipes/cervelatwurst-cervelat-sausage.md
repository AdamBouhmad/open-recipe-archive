## Ingredients

- lean meat from a pork shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- basil
- bacon, finely diced
- white beef intestines

## Directions

1. Cut the lean meat from a pork shoulder
2. Remove all sinews
3. Chop it first, then pound it finely
4. Add salt, pepper, spices, and basil
5. Finely dice the bacon and work it well into the meat
6. Stuff it firmly into white beef intestines
7. Cure it properly
