## Ingredients

- Green peas
- Butter
- Green parsley
- Veal, finely diced and slightly overbrowned
- Nutmeg
- Ground pepper
- Cream
- Artichokes
- Salt water
- Butter sauce
- Fried goose liver

## Directions

1. Braise green peas with butter and green parsley
2. Add to a light butter sauce with finely diced, slightly overbrowned veal, a little nutmeg, some ground pepper, and a spoonful of cream
3. Boil artichokes in salt water and carefully remove the insides
4. Fill the artichokes with the pea mixture
5. Place the filled artichokes in the remaining butter sauce and let them cook well together
6. Garnish with fried goose liver
