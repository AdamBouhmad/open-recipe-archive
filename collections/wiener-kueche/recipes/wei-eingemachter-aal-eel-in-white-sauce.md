## Ingredients

- eel
- salt water

## Directions

1. After preparing the eel, cut it into 2-finger wide pieces and poach gently in salt water. Then lay the eel on a napkin so that all fat drains off, arrange it then in a platter and serve with any desired sauce: sugar, mustard, Hollandaise sauce or the like.
