## Ingredients

- Kronawetter birds
- Bacon
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bind these birds, like larks, with bacon, stick them on small spits, and roast them
2. Alternatively, roast them without bacon and sprinkle them with breadcrumbs
