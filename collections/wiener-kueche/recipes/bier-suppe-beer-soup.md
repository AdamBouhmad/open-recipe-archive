## Ingredients

- white beer, 3 seidels
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- sugar
- egg yolks, 8
- cream, half a seidel
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Simmer 3 seidels of white beer with some cinnamon, lemon peel and sugar until sweet enough
2. Beat 8 egg yolks in a pot
3. Gradually add half a seidel of hot cream
4. Pour in the hot beer while stirring vigorously
5. Serve over toasted bread slices
