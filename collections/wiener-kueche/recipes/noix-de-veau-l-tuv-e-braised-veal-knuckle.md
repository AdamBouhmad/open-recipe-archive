## Ingredients

- 1 veal knuckle
- salt
- lard in thin strips
- shallots, sliced thin
- 4 bay leaves
- pepper
- butter mixed with cream for basting
- flour
- cream
- butter
- lemon peel, finely cut

## Directions

1. Salt the unsalted veal knuckle and let it marinate for 2-3 days, pouring off and reboiling the brine twice daily, always pouring it back cold over the knuckle, which should be turned daily
2. After this time, place the knuckle on a spit and roast it frequently, basting with a sauce mixed with cream
3. In a saucepan on the stove, place some of the brine and add the drippings from the roasting knuckle, letting it come to a boil
4. Gradually whisk flour with a few spoonfuls of cream into the sauce until it is smooth
5. Add butter and finely cut lemon peel to the boiling sauce and let it come to a boil
6. Cut the knuckle into desired pieces, arrange them on a platter so that the knuckle retains its original form, and serve with the sauce
7. Alternatively, the knuckle can be roasted in the oven, which is preferable
