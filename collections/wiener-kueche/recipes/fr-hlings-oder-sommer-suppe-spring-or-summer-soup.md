## Ingredients

- young yellow turnips and young white turnips in equal quantity
- 3 liters broth
- 1 handful green beans, cut into pieces
- equal amount of green peas
- thin asparagus
- 2 pieces beautiful cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cut young turnips into desired pieces or cut out with a cutter, wash thoroughly.
2. Pour 3 liters of broth over them and cook until fairly tender.
3. Add 1 handful of green beans cut into pieces, an equal amount of green peas and thin asparagus.
4. Half an hour later, cut 2 pieces of beautiful cauliflower into small florets and add.
5. Let this soup cook until everything is tender and, after it has been properly seasoned, serve it over roasted bread rolls.
