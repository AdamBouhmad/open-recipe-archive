## Ingredients

- 1 pound grated cooked potatoes
- 1/2 pound bacon cut in cubes
- 1/2 pound white bread
- some finely chopped onions
- 6 eggs
- 3 good spoonfuls cream
- salt
- nutmeg
- some spoonfuls fine flour
- meat broth
- salt water
- grated bread rolls toasted golden in butter
- brown butter
- smoked pork meat
- dried fruit

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, peel them, and grate on a grater
2. For 1 pound grated potatoes, roast 1/2 pound bacon cut into cubes, the same amount of white bread, along with some finely chopped onions
3. Stir the potatoes with 6 eggs and 3 good spoonfuls cream with salt and nutmeg until smooth
4. Add the roasted bread and bacon, and some spoonfuls of fine flour
5. Form round dumplings from this and cook them in meat broth and salt water
6. Do not arrange these until just before they are to be served, then serve with grated bread rolls toasted golden in butter and brown butter
7. Commonly smoked pork meat and dried fruit are also served with them
