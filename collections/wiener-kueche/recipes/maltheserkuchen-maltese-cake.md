## Ingredients

- half pound fine sweet almonds
- egg white
- half pound ground sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- half hour of stirring
- zest of one lemon
- 4 loth melted butter
- half pound fine flour
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Pound half pound fine sweet almonds with some egg white until tender
2. Stir for half an hour with half pound ground sugar and 16 egg yolks
3. Add lemon zest and 4 loth melted butter
4. Finally stir in half pound fine flour and the egg white of 8 eggs whipped to peaks slowly
5. Bake in ordinary small forms for half an hour slowly
