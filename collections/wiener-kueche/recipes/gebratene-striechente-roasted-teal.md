## Ingredients

- teal duck
- salt and spices
- fat or butter

## Directions

1. After the ducks are plucked, hold them over a flame or spirits lamp to singe them, then wash them, but do not scrub - only wipe inside and out clean with a cloth.
2. Season and truss them.
3. Either spit-roast them for 15-20 minutes over strong heat, or place them in the oven where they are basted with melted fat for 3/4 to 1 hour until beautifully golden brown with crispy skin, basting frequently.
4. Remove the trussing strings, carve the ducks and arrange them garnished with orange and lemon slices.
5. Orange sauce or any desired compote may also be served with them.
