## Ingredients

- one pike of several pounds
- salt water
- 50 cooked crayfish
- crayfish butter
- 1 Pfund grated bread crumbs
- 1 Maß milk
- 1/2 Maß cream
- salt
- nutmeg
- finely chopped anchovies
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites
- good butter dough

## Directions

1. Cook a pike of several pounds in salted water
2. Remove the bones from the flesh and shred it finely
3. Add finely cut meat from 50 cooked and shelled crayfish
4. Make crayfish butter, reserving as much as needed for the sauce after it cools
5. Toast 1 pound grated bread crumbs in the remaining crayfish butter
6. Fill it with 1 Maß milk and 1/2 Maß cream
7. Let it cook to a moderately stiff consistency
8. Season this batter, once cooled in a bowl, with salt and nutmeg
9. After a quarter hour, stir in finely chopped anchovies, 12 egg yolks, finely chopped pike and crayfish flesh, and lastly the whites of 12 eggs beaten to snow
10. Line a mold or casserole with good butter dough, fill in the mass, and bake for 1.5 hours
11. Serve with crayfish sauce, using the remaining crayfish butter
