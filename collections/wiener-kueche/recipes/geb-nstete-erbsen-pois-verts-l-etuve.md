## Ingredients

- 1 liter fine green peas
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 8 ounces butter
- water
- butter sauce
- salt
- green parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Place 1 liter of rinsed fine green peas in a casserole
2. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar and 8 ounces of butter
3. Add enough water to just cover the peas
4. Cover and braise until the peas are tender
5. Add some butter sauce, salt, and if desired, a pinch of finely chopped green parsley
6. Mix well and serve
