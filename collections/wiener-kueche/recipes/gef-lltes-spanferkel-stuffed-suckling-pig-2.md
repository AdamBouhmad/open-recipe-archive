## Ingredients

- cleaned and prepared suckling pig
- forcemeat

## Directions

1. From a cleaned and prepared suckling pig as described in the previous recipes, loosen the spine, joints, shoulders, and the legs from the feet to the claws
2. Fill the empty space created with forcemeat and sew the opening closed
3. Line a roasting pan with thinly sliced speck, place the suckling pig with the belly side down on it
4. Roast in the oven under frequent basting with speck for 1-1 1/2 hours
5. After this time, remove it, carve it, and serve with red cabbage, green salad, or the like
