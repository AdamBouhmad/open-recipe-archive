## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar, clarified
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- 1/4 liter Champagne, instead of wine, orange and lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
2. Use Champagne instead of wine, orange and lemon juice
3. Do not add orange peel to the sugar
