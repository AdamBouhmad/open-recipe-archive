## Ingredients

- garden strawberries, fresh
- thinly refined sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the strawberries in fresh water and dry them well on a sieve
2. Roll them in very thinly refined sugar
3. Use the sugar instead of egg white because of their great moisture content
4. Proceed as described above
