## Ingredients

- Beef from the front quarters of the ox, or tenderloin
- 16 decagrams diced bacon
- 1 large head onion, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
- Meat stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika

## Directions

1. For goulash, choose a well-aged piece of beef from the front quarters of the ox. Tenderloin can also be used if it is not too expensive.
2. After cleaning the meat thoroughly, cut it into pieces of desired size, cube-shaped.
3. Place 16 decagrams of cube-cut bacon in a casserole and let it roast until it looks glassy.
4. Add 1 large head onion, finely chopped, and let it roast a little.
5. Add the meat and 1 teaspoon of caraway seeds, let it cook covered until the resulting liquid has evaporated.
6. Add enough stock so that it covers the meat.
7. Only when the meat is tender, season it with salt and the point of a knife full of paprika (or as desired with paprika alone) and let it boil a little.
8. The sauce should not be too thin when running from the spoon.
9. Before serving, the fat should be removed carefully.
