## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- necessary flour
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 10 Deca butter
- 1 pinch of salt
- 8 Deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 11 egg whites (snow)

## Directions

1. When a grated barley has been prepared from 1 egg, 1 egg yolk and the necessary flour, stir this into 1/2 liter boiling milk, cook until thick, stir with 10 Deca butter and 1 pinch of salt and let cool
2. Meanwhile stir 8 Deca vanilla sugar with 6 egg yolks for 1/2 hour, add the cooled barley, mix well and finally fold in lightly the snow from 11 egg whites
3. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour and cook covered for 3/4 hours in steam or bake in the oven
4. When serving, sprinkle the soufflé with finely pounded sugar and serve with dissolved jam or cream
