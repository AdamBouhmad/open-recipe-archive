## Ingredients

- Sauce (sorrel sauce, tarragon sauce, caper sauce, lentil or any puree)
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Put some desired sauce into a shallow casserole, such as sorrel sauce, tarragon sauce, caper sauce, lentil or any puree.
2. Once it boils, crack the eggs into it and let them cook until the egg white is set.
3. Remove the eggs with 2 metal spoons and place in a bowl containing the same sauce in which they were cooked.
