## Ingredients

- 2 drams onion
- 1 piece celery
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsley leaf
- 16 drams butter
- approximately 2 liters cold water
- 1 heaping liter peeled potatoes, sliced
- 1/4 liter cream
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Finely slice the onion, celery, carrot, and parsley
2. Toast these vegetables in butter
3. Add approximately 2 liters of cold water
4. Add sliced peeled potatoes and simmer until completely soft
5. Pass the entire mixture through a sieve
6. Add 1/4 liter of cream, nutmeg, and salt
7. Bring to a boil once more
8. If the potato soup is too thick, it can be thinned with broth, milk, or water
