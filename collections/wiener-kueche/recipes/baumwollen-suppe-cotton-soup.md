## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons fine flour
- hot milk
- 5 to 6 eggs
- pea broth

## Directions

1. Stir 5 tablespoons of fine flour with hot milk to form a batter like a drip soup
2. Beat 5 to 6 eggs into it and stir well
3. Let it drop into boiling pea broth and stir constantly until it becomes thick
4. Season to taste when serving
