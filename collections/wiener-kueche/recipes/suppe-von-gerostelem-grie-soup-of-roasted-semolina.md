## Ingredients

- 8 spoonfuls butter
- 16 spoonfuls semolina
- approximately 2 liters boiling beef broth
- 3 spoonfuls sour cream
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Roast 16 spoonfuls semolina in 8 spoonfuls butter until yellow
2. Pour approximately 2 liters boiling beef broth over it
3. Let cook for 1/2 hour, skimming frequently
4. Beat 3 spoonfuls sour cream with 3 egg yolks
5. Add to the soup pot, stir slowly, salt, and serve at table
