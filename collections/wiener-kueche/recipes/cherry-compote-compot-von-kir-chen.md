## Ingredients

- 1 pound sour cherries
- 0.5 pounds sugar
- water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut the stems of the cherries halfway off.
2. Pour cold water over them.
3. Dissolve the sugar on charcoal fire with a little water, then cook it down almost to syrup.
4. The cherries must first be drained well of water, then throw them into the syrup.
5. Bring to a strong boil several times.
6. Skim off the foam with a spoon.
7. Allow to cool and serve in a compote dish.
