## Ingredients

- grated poppy seeds
- milk
- 2 spoons sugar
- 4 dkg butter
- 3 dkg raisins
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook the grated poppy seeds with the milk, 2 spoons sugar, 4 dkg butter, 3 dkg raisins, and lemon peel for 30 minutes with frequent stirring until thick.
2. Use the filling after it has cooled.
