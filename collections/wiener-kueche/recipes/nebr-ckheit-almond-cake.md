## Ingredients

- 16 ounces butter
- 24 ounces finely ground sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 ounces blanched and ground almonds
- 12 ounces grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon peel
- 6 egg whites
- 8 ounces breadcrumbs or dark breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 16 ounces butter until fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 24 ounces finely ground sugar and 8 egg yolks for 1 hour
3. Mix in 12 ounces blanched and ground almonds, 12 ounces grated chocolate, and 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon peel
4. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 6 egg whites with 8 ounces breadcrumbs or dark breadcrumbs
5. Fill into a buttered and floured Nebrücken mold and bake in a moderate oven for 3/4 hour
6. Turn out the baked cake onto a sieve and after complete cooling, cover with chocolate glaze
7. When the glaze is completely dry, pierce the cake lengthwise into 3 strips with neatly cut pistachios or blanched almonds cut the same way, and blanched, peeled and similarly cut pistachios, using a pastry needle to insert them so that two-thirds protrude
