## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 quarter-pounds sugar
- 5 loth potatoes
- zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat 1/4 pound butter in a bowl until fluffy
2. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks, well beaten
3. Stir in 4 quarter-pounds sugar, 5 loth potatoes, and zest of 1/2 lemon
4. Pour into a buttered casserole
5. Bake slowly
