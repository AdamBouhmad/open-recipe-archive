## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- approximately 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1/4 liter broth
- some vinegar
- finely cut watercress
- 1/4 liter wine
- salt

## Directions

1. Put butter in a saucepan with approximately 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
2. When these are brown, add 1 teaspoon flour and let this also become brown
3. Pour in with 1/4 liter broth and some vinegar
4. Add finely cut watercress, 1/4 liter wine, and salt
5. Let it boil thoroughly
