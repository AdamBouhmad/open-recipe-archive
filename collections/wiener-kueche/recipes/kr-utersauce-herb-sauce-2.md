## Ingredients

- Chives
- Savory
- Parsley
- Basil
- Onion stems
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Sugar (to taste, optional)

## Directions

1. Finely chop with a chopping knife: chives, savory, parsley, basil, and onion stems.
2. Mix well with vinegar and oil.
3. Add sugar to taste if desired.
4. Serve with cold fish or roasts.
