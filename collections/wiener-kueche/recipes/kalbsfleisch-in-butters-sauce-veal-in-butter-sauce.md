## Ingredients

- Butter
- Leaf-cut neck meat
- Mushrooms
- Spices
- Veal meat from the shoulder
- Broth
- Nutmeg
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Put some butter in a casserole, add leaf-cut neck meat, mushrooms and spices if desired
2. When sufficiently roasted, add the veal meat from the shoulder and braise covered for approximately 1/4 hour
3. After this time, pour in the necessary broth and continue braising until the meat is tender
4. Remove the meat, strain the broth through a sieve, make a butter sauce with nutmeg and lemon peel
5. Remove the bones and cut the meat into pieces, arrange with the sauce
