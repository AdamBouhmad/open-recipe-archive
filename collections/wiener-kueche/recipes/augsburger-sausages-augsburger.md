## Ingredients

- 4-6 Augsburger sausages
- flour
- hot lard
- spinach or lettuce salad

## Directions

1. Halve the sausages lengthwise and remove the skin
2. Make diagonal cuts on top
3. Roll the sausages in flour on one side
4. Place in hot lard with the floured side down
5. Fry brown on both sides
6. Serve with spinach or lettuce salad
