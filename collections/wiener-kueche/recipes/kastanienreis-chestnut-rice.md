## Ingredients

- 1 kg chestnuts
- sugar to taste
- milk
- ½ l whipped cream

## Directions

1. Wash chestnuts, make slit on round side, cook in plenty of water for 1½ hours
2. Drain and cool slightly
3. Shell and pass through sieve
4. Stir with sugar to taste and some milk to make thick paste
5. Press mixture through inverted grater into center of glass bowl
6. Arrange sweetened whipped cream around the edges, or arrange whipped cream in center and chestnut rice around edges
