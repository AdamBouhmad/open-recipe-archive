## Ingredients

- 4 large or 8 small cabbages
- Cold water
- Salt water
- 16 ounces hot fat
- Finely cut onion
- Garlic, if desired
- 16 ounces flour
- Salt and spices

## Directions

1. Cut off the stems of 4 large or 8 small cabbages
2. Remove the leaves and cut them apart along the main vein and place in cold water
3. After washing well, place the leaves in boiling salt water and cook them soft
4. Add some finely cut onion and, if desired, a small piece of crushed garlic to 16 ounces hot fat and let roast somewhat
5. Add 16 ounces flour and continue cooking under
