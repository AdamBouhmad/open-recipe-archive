## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 16 decagrams lemon sugar
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- equal amount finely cut orange peel
- 1 crushed clove
- 8 egg whites
- 16 decagrams black bread crumbs
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold
- 0.5 liter red wine
- 32 decagrams sugar
- 1 clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 16 decagrams lemon sugar for 3/4 hour
2. Add 1 pinch cinnamon, finely cut orange peel, and 1 crushed clove, mix well
3. Lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 16 decagrams black bread crumbs
4. Butter a mold, dust with flour, fill with this mixture
5. Bake in the oven at moderate heat for 1/2 hour
6. Meanwhile, heat 0.5 liter red wine with 32 decagrams sugar, 1 clove, and cinnamon
7. Turn the pudding into a dish and pour the hot wine sauce over it spoonwise
8. Serve only after the liquid has been completely absorbed
9. Cored and peeled small Mcintosh apples can be filled and placed in the pudding mixture before baking if desired
