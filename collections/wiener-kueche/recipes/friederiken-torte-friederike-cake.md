## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 12 dkg sugar
- 12 dkg swollen grated almonds
- grated nuts
- 3 dkg raw grated almonds
- 7 dkg sugar
- lemon zest
- jam

## Directions

1. Make crumb dough on board from butter, sugar, and swollen grated almonds
2. Let rest 15 minutes
3. Divide into 4 parts and roll each round like cake form base
4. Place each dough layer on unbuttered baking sheet
5. Bake at higher heat
6. Coat two layers with nut filling, third layer with jam
7. Assemble together and place fourth layer on top
8. Press down
9. Let cake stand 1-2 days
10. Sprinkle with sugar or glaze white
11. For nut filling: Cook grated nuts, raw grated almonds, sugar, and lemon zest with 1 dl water for several minutes, then cool
