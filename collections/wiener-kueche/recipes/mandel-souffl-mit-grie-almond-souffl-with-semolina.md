## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter semolina
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 8 Deka butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 Deka powdered sugar
- 10 Deka blanched, finely ground almonds
- 5 egg whites
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 liter semolina into 1/2 liter boiling milk and let it thicken.
2. Add 8 Deka butter, mix well and set aside to cool.
3. Beat 5 egg yolks with 10 Deka powdered sugar for 1/2 hour.
4. Mix well with 10 Deka blanched, finely ground almonds and the cooled semolina mass.
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 5 egg whites.
6. Fill a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour and cover and bake for 3/4 hour in steam or in the oven.
