## Ingredients

- 1 portion common soup herbs
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters meat broth
- salt as needed
- 8 tablespoons heavy cream or sour cream
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. After the herbs are well washed and finely cut, braise them in 8 tablespoons of butter.
2. Add 3 tablespoons flour and let braise a bit longer.
3. Pour in 2 liters of meat broth, cook through and strain.
4. Add necessary salt.
5. Before serving, bind with 8 tablespoons of heavy cream or sour cream and 3 egg yolks.
