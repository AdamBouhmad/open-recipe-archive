## Ingredients

- Half a calf's head with the ear
- Salt water
- Fricassee sauce

## Directions

1. Cook half a calf's head with the ear completely tender in salt water
2. Strain it, cut off the ear, divide the meat into desired pieces
3. Arrange the pieces on a platter
4. Place the ear in the middle, cutting it several times at the upper edge to fan it out
5. Serve with fricassee sauce
