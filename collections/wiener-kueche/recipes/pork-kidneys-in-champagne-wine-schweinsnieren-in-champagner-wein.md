## Ingredients

- Pork kidneys
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Butter
- Finely chopped shallots
- Parsley
- Flour
- One glass champagne wine
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys thinly and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg.
2. Cook them in butter with finely chopped shallots and parsley for a while over high heat until the kidneys begin to stiffen.
3. Dust them with a little flour and pour in a glass of champagne wine.
4. Turn them frequently so they do not stick, but they must not boil.
5. Arrange croutons on a plate and serve the kidneys hot.
