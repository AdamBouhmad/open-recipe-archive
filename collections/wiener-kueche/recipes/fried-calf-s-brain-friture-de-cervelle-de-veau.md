## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Flour
- Beaten eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and peel the brain and cook in salt water
2. When completely cooled, cut in half and cut each half lengthwise
3. Dip in flour, then in beaten eggs, and coat in breadcrumbs (bread)
4. Shortly before serving, fry in very hot fat
