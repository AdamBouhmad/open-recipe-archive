## Ingredients

- Mutton tails
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Good coulis or espagnole
- Bouillon or stock
- Blanched calf sweetbreads
- Fine liver dumplings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices

## Directions

1. Cook several mutton tails in the braise only halfway so they do not fall apart in the ragout
2. Remove them and cut them in half
3. In a casserole, sweat mushrooms in butter
4. Add good coulis or espagnole
5. Pour in bouillon or stock as needed so it can cook completely
6. Place blanched calf sweetbreads, the mutton tails, and fine liver dumplings into the sauce
7. Season the ragout well
8. Finish on gentle charcoal fire
9. The ragout should be neither too thick nor too thin
10. It can be served in soup pots, in Bernese casseroles, or in a pastry
