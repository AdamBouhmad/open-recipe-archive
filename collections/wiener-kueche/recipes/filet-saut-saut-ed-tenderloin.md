## Ingredients

- Skinned tenderloin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Hot fat
- Meat gravy

## Directions

1. Cut finger-thick slices from a skinned tenderloin.
2. Lightly flatten these with the flat of a knife, salt and pepper them.
3. Dip both sides in flour and quickly fry in hot fat in a pan.
4. Arrange on a platter and serve with meat gravy.
