## Ingredients

- Egg whites (6)
- Sugar, finely crushed (2 tablespoons)
- Flour (2 tablespoons)
- Egg yolks (6)
- Butter (hot)
- Marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff
2. Lightly mix with finely crushed sugar, flour and egg yolks
3. Heat a soufflé pan and coat with 1 tablespoon hot butter
4. Pour in half the mixture and bake at fairly high heat in the oven
5. When done, slide the soufflé into a dish using a wooden spoon, brush with dissolved marmalade and place in a warm spot
6. Coat the pan again with 1 tablespoon hot butter and bake the second half of the mixture
7. When done, slide onto the marmalade-coated first soufflé so that one covers the other exactly
8. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
9. Alternatively, both soufflés can be baked on the stove: when the bottom of the first is golden, cover with a second hot buttered pan so the soufflé is in contact with both pans without pressure, turn it so the uncooked side now faces down and bake until golden
