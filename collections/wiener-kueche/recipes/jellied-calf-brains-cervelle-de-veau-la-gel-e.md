## Ingredients

- One cleaned calf brain, freed of skin, cooked in salted water
- Aspic
- Fine green parsley
- Chervil
- Sauce thickened with aspic
- Truffles

## Directions

1. After the brain is cooked, placed on cloth and dried, put it in a saucepan
2. Cover with sauce bound with aspic, mixed with finely cut green parsley and chervil
3. Let it come to a boil once and set aside to cool
4. Pour a finger's depth of melted aspic into a mold set in ice
5. Once set, arrange the brain neatly on it
6. Cover again with melted aspic, let it set, add another layer of brain with truffles
7. Continue until the mold is full
8. After standing covered in ice for 1 hour, remove, rinse, dip briefly in boiling water
9. Dry with a cloth, turn out onto a platter, remove the mold
10. Garnish the turned-out dish with chopped aspic
