## Ingredients

- 12 deca leafy-cut fat (beef marrow, lard, veal kidney fat or marrow)
- 12 deca beef liver
- 1 piece veal bone
- 1/3 kilo beef broth meat
- 1/2 kilo chopped beef bones
- 1 piece yellow turnip
- 1 piece parsley
- 1 piece onion
- 1 table piece (approximately 2 kilo) beef
- approximately 4 liters cold water
- 1/4 liter cold water
- poultry scraps (if available)
- spice clove, 3 grains pepper, 3 grains allspice (optional)

## Directions

1. Place leafy-cut fat in a casserolle, either beef marrow, lard, veal kidney fat or marrow
2. Add 12 deca beef liver, 1 piece veal bone, 1/3 kilo beef broth meat, 1/2 kilo chopped beef bones, 1 piece yellow turnip, 1 piece parsley, and 1 piece onion, all leafy-cut
3. Cover with a lid, place on fire and let it brown well (brown roast)
4. Meanwhile, take a table piece weighing approximately 2 kilo, pound it, wash it out, bind it with twine, place it on fire with approximately 4 liters cold water and bring to a boil
5. Remove the foam that forms cleanly
6. When the roasted mixture with meat and liver has evaporated well and has taken on a brown color, pour 1/4 liter cold water over it and let it evaporate
7. After 10 minutes, add a ladle full from the boiling broth and let it cook 10 to 15 minutes
8. Then add the roasted mixture from the casserolle into the meat pot
9. If you have poultry scraps, add them to the pot as well, and then let it cook covered for 3 hours slowly
10. A quarter hour before using the broth, move the pot aside so it no longer boils
11. Then remove the fat cleanly, strain the broth through fine, clean cloth and use as desired
12. The meat remains covered in a warm place, after which the twine is removed and the meat is served at table as needed
13. If you wish to add spices, add a spice clove, 3 grains pepper and 3 grains allspice with the root vegetables
