## Ingredients

- butter dough, two knife-backs thick
- egg white
- butter sauce
- asparagus, boiled
- turtle, boiled and divided into pieces
- mushrooms
- crayfish tails
- liver, baked

## Directions

1. Make a bowl pastry from butter dough two knife-backs thick
2. Bake brushed with egg white
3. Make a small butter sauce and braise boiled asparagus, cleaned turtle cooked in salted water and divided into pieces, mushrooms, shelled crayfish tails, and baked liver in it
4. Once the pastry is baked, carefully remove the lid
5. Fill the mixed dish neatly inside
6. Put the lid back on and serve quickly
7. The pastry must be placed on a dish before it is filled
