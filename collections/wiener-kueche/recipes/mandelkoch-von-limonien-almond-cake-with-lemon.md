## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound ground almonds
- 3/4 pound sugar
- 1 large lemon

## Directions

1. In a bowl, combine 3/4 pound ground almonds and 3/4 pound sugar
2. Grate 1 large lemon on a grater and mix in well
