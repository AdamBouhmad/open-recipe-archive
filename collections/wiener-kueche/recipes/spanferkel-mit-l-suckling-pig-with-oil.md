## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Tarragon (finely chopped)
- Aspic (chopped)
- Mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the suckling pig until tender as with boiled meat
2. When it is in the broth, arrange it on a platter
3. Pour vinegar and oil over it
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped tarragon
5. Garnish with chopped aspic
6. Serve with mustard sauce
