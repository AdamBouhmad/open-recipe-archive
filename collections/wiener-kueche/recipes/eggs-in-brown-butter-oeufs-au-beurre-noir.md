## Ingredients

- 16 ounces butter
- 12 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- 8 ounces butter
- 3 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Shake 16 ounces butter in a pan over high heat until it turns dark brown.
2. Break in 12 eggs one after another, season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Heat over the fire until the eggs are almost completely set.
4. Arrange the eggs in a bowl using two serving spoons.
5. In a separate pan, brown 8 ounces butter and add 3 tablespoons vinegar, let it come to a boil once, and pour over the arranged eggs.
