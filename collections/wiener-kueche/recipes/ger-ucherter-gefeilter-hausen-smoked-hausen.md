## Ingredients

- smoked hausen
- water
- salt
- caraway seed
- vegetables or purée or boiled potatoes with butter

## Directions

1. Cook the smoked hausen in water with some salt and caraway seed, covered, until soft (approximately 1/2 to 1/3 hour)
2. Remove from water and cut into pieces
3. Arrange in a serving dish and serve with vegetables or purée or boiled potatoes with butter
