## Ingredients

- 1 head of young cabbage
- dark and light yellow turnips
- celery
- parsley
- vinegar
- water
- suckling pig

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage, turnips, celery, and parsley into thin noodle-like strips
2. Place in a casserole, pour in one part vinegar and one part water, and bring to a boil
3. Cut the suckling pig into desired pieces and add to the casserole
4. Cook until the meat is tender
5. Remove the meat with a slotted spoon and arrange in a round shape on a plate
6. Garnish with the noodle-like vegetables and place grated horseradish in the center of the plate
