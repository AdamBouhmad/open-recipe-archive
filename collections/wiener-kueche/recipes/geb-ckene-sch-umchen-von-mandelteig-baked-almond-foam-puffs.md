## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams blanched almonds mixed with 1 egg white, finely ground
- 1/4 liter butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 6 decagrams spice sugar
- 8 decagrams flour
- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Combine ground almonds and egg white mixture in a deep bowl
2. Stir well with 1/4 liter butter, 5 egg yolks, spice sugar, and flour
3. Lightly fold in the beaten egg whites
4. Bake puffs as indicated in the recipe for 'Baked Puffs'
5. Fill with chantilly cream or any desired cream
6. If serving cold, fill with whipped cream, strawberries, or other desired filling
