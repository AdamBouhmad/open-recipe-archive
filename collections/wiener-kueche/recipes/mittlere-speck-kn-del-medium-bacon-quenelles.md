## Ingredients

- 32 Loffel bacon, finely diced
- 32 Loffel rolls, finely diced, mixed with 1/8 liter milk
- 1/8 liter milk
- 3 whole eggs
- salt
- knife point of finely cut parsley
- 1 liter flour

## Directions

1. Dice bacon finely and roast in a saucepan over heat until the bacon appears glassy
2. Pour over 32 Loffel finely diced rolls that have been mixed with 1/8 liter milk and stir well
3. Beat 1/8 liter milk with 3 whole eggs, pour over the rolls, add salt, a knife point of finely cut parsley and 1 liter flour, stir well
4. Let stand covered for 1/2 hour
5. Dip a spoon in hot water and fill with as much dough as you want to make one knödel and round it with a cooking spoon, or dip hands in water and form the knödel this way
6. Put the knödel into boiling salted water
