## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Salt water
- Butter or oil
- Fine vegetables or purées

## Directions

1. Clean the lobster carefully in cold water with a knife
2. Place it in a suitable pot and add enough boiling salt water so that it comes 3 finger-widths above the lobster
3. Cover it. Cooking time depends on size: smaller ones 3/4 hour, larger ones 1 hour
4. When serving warm, remove the claws and tail, and place the front part on a dish covered with a napkin
5. Cut the tail lengthwise through, cut the shell through from the claws and remove the meat carefully, cut it neatly and place it back on the shell
6. Do the same with the small claws
7. Reassemble the lobster on the dish and lightly pour the cooking liquid over it
8. Serve with a warm sauce: sauce tartare, sauce à la hollandaise, or another fine wine sauce
9. Everything must be done very quickly and carefully so it arrives at the table warm
