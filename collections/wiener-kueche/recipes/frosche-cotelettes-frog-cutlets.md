## Ingredients

- Frog legs
- Salt
- Pepper (optional)
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten and salted
- Breadcrumbs
- Hot lard or butter
- Puree or vegetables

## Directions

1. Skin the frog legs and remove the lower legs and cross bone
2. Since one frog leg has little meat, remove the meat from one leg and place it on another leg, ensuring the bone protrudes
3. Tap the meat slightly and form it to resemble a cutlet
4. Salt the cutlets and optionally sprinkle with pepper
5. Dredge in flour
6. Dip in beaten, salted eggs
7. Roll in breadcrumbs
8. Fry in hot lard until golden
9. Remove with a slotted spoon
10. Arrange round-shaped on a serving plate
11. Place any desired puree or vegetables in the center
