## Ingredients

- Six to eight calf's feet
- Salt water
- Rendered beef kidney fat, finely cut
- Salt
- 8 eggs
- Ground mace
- A little pepper
- Butter
- Chopped parsley
- Finely cut onions
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil six to eight calf's feet until soft in salted water
2. Cut the meat from the bones
3. Mix it with rendered beef kidney fat cut into small pieces, salt, 8 eggs, ground mace, a little pepper, chopped parsley, finely cut onions, and breadcrumbs
4. Stir everything together well
5. Cook in a napkin brushed with butter for 3 hours
6. When serving, pour over a parsley sauce made from beef broth, butter, and breadcrumbs
