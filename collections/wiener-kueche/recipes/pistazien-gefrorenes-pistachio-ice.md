## Ingredients

- Vanilla Ice prepared
- 10 pieces pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720) and when it is nearly frozen, mix in 10 pieces pistachios that have been blanched, peeled and cut very finely
