## Ingredients

- 20 pieces cleaned snails
- salted water
- 2 pieces mushrooms
- 5 sprigs fresh parsley
- 1 half-finger-length piece lemon peel
- 4 pieces peeled capers
- 8 drams butter
- 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter cream
- milk cream
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Place 20 cleaned snails in boiling salted water and cook for approximately 20 minutes
2. Remove snails and extract them from their shells using a dressing needle
3. Remove the head, intestines, and small stone from inside the shell
4. Chop the remaining snail meat into pieces
5. Finely chop 2 pieces mushrooms, 5 sprigs parsley, 1 half-finger-length piece lemon peel, and 4 pieces peeled capers together
6. Roast this mixture in 8 drams butter
7. Add 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs and the chopped snails to the roasted mixture
8. Once completely cooled, mix with 3 egg yolks and 1/2 liter cream and set in a cool place
9. Clean the removed shells with warm water and salt, place on a sieve, and once completely dry, fill with the prepared cold mixture
10. Place the filled shells in a casserole, cover with milk cream, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, drizzle with melted butter
11. Bake for approximately 1/2 hour
12. Remove from oven, arrange on a napkin-covered dish, and serve with vinegar and oil
