## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 15 dkg cooked, passed, and cooled potatoes
- 4-6 dkg flour
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, stir in the egg, salt, cooled potatoes, and flour
2. Form small dumplings with a spoon
3. Drop into hot lard and fry until light brown
4. Drain and serve warm separately
5. The same mixture can be formed into larger dumplings (Knöderl) and prepared the same way
