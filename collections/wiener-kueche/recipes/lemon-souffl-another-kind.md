## Ingredients

- 10 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter cold water
- 14 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 lemon
- 7 egg yolks
- 7 egg whites
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Put flour in a saucepan and gradually pour in 0.5 liter of cold water, stirring over the fire until it forms a thick dough that separates from the spoon
2. Transfer to a deep bowl and let cool
3. Meanwhile, beat 14 tablespoons lemon juice (from 1 lemon) with 7 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
4. Gradually mix this into the cooled mixture
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 7 egg whites
6. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
7. Cover and cook in a water bath or bake slowly in the oven at low heat for 1.5 hours, during which time it should rise to twice its height
8. Turn out onto a serving dish, dust with finely ground sugar, and serve immediately
9. If desired, before baking or cooking, insert baked pralines into the mixture
