## Ingredients

- nail mushrooms (Nägerlschwämme)
- pea broth
- butter
- green parsley
- flour
- pepper
- nutmeg flower
- cream
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Wash nail mushrooms in warm water several times
2. Let them cook in pea broth
3. Pour the broth into another pot
4. Braise the mushrooms in some butter and green parsley
5. Dust with one spoonful of flour
6. Pour the above pea broth over
7. Salt and season with ground pepper and nutmeg flower
8. Add one spoonful of good cream
9. Serve over toasted bread slices
