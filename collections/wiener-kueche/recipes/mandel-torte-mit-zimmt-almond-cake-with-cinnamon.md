## Ingredients

- 12 Deka fine ground and sieved almonds
- 1½ liter cream
- 8 egg whites
- Marzipan apples with kernels removed and filled with marmalade of choice
- Butter for the pan
- Flour for dusting
- Powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Beat butter until fluffy, gradually stir in fine ground and sieved almonds and 1½ liter cream
2. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
3. Remove the kernels from marzipan apples and fill the resulting empty space with marmalade of choice
4. Lay them in a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold at finger-width distance
5. Pour the above-mentioned mixture over them and bake in the oven at low heat for 1 hour
6. Remove the cake mold, remove the rim, and arrange the baked item on a cake plate in a bowl, serving it sprinkled with fine powdered sugar
7. Can also be baked in a glass roll, then pushed out with a pastry spoon and served that way
