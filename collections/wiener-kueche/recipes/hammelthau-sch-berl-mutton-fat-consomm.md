## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter simmering milk
- 25 Deca mutton fat
- 10 Deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- Salt
- Snow from 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook 25 Deca mutton fat in 1/2 liter simmering milk and let it cook through with occasional stirring
2. Beat 10 Deca butter until fluffy, beat in 8 egg yolks
3. Add the prepared mutton fat and some salt, stir everything well together and lightly mix in the snow from 8 eggs
4. Handle further as with Griesschöberl
