## Ingredients

- 6 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 30 dkg flour
- 1/4 kg cooked, passed, warm potatoes
- yeast dough
- milk
- hot lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cream 6 dkg butter and stir in 3 egg yolks and salt
2. Add 30 dkg flour, 1/4 kg cooked and passed warm potatoes, risen yeast dough, and a few spoonfuls of lukewarm milk
3. Mix and knead the dough smooth on a floured board
4. Form thick noodles and place to rise near the stove
5. Fry in hot lard
6. Drain and serve with sugar
