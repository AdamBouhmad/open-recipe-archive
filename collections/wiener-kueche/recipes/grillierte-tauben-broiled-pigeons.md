## Ingredients

- Pigeons, well cleaned
- Butter or oil
- Egg
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeons lengthwise down the middle.
2. Loosen the bones and beat the meat with the flat side of a meat cleaver.
3. Salt and pepper the meat, dip in melted butter, and coat with bread crumbs.
4. Place buttered or oiled writing paper on a grill, put the pigeons on it, and broil 15-20 minutes over moderate heat on both sides until golden.
5. Alternatively, place them in a shallow pan with some fat and cook quickly over heat.
