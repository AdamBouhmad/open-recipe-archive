## Ingredients

- 10 ounces vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites, beaten to snow
- 16 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Beat 10 ounces vanilla sugar with 8 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Gently fold in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 16 ounces flour
3. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour and bake at low heat in the oven
4. When completely cooled, cut into knife-back-thick slices, slightly smaller in circumference than the casserole intended for cooking
5. Line the casserole with butter and flour, lay a biscuit slice on the bottom, cover finger-high with raw snow made from 6 eggs
6. Cover with another biscuit slice and continue this way until all is used
7. Finally cover with a biscuit slice
8. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour
9. Turn out onto a plate and serve with fruit juice, chocolate, or cream of choice
