## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets (carbonades)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Fat

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as for the natural cutlets, with the difference that the salted cutlets are lightly dipped in flour, then dipped in beaten eggs, sprinkled with bread crumbs and fried in a shallow casserole in very hot lard.
2. With a pastry server, they are turned over once the bottom side is brown and also fried brown on the second side.
