## Ingredients

- wine
- 2-3 tablespoons sugar
- 1-2 eggs
- 16-18 dkg flour
- apples
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Beat together the wine, 2-3 tablespoons sugar, 1-2 eggs, and 16-18 dkg flour until smooth.
2. Peel the apples, core them using an apple corer without separating them, and cut into slices.
3. Dip the apple slices in the batter.
4. Fry them in plenty of hot lard on both sides until light brown.
5. Drain and sprinkle with sugar.
6. Serve warm.
