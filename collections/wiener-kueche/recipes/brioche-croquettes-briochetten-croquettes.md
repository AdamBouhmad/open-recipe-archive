## Ingredients

- 3 eggs (for brioche dough)
- jam of choice
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare a brioche dough from 3 eggs
2. Bake it in very hot lard as described in the brioche recipe
3. Cut each brioche into hand-width and finger-length pieces
4. Spread with jam of choice and roll together
5. Dip in flour, dip in beaten egg, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry in very hot lard
6. Continue treatment as for Chestnut-Croquettes (No. 1143)
