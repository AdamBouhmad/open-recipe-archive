## Ingredients

- Skinned hare
- 32 deca butter
- 3 bay leaves
- Salt and pepper
- 0.5 liter red wine
- 0.75 liter soup
- 0.25 liter cream
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 4 deca sugar
- Hare blood (if available)

## Directions

1. Cut the skinned hare into 2 finger-width pieces
2. Put these in a casserole with 32 deca butter, 3 bay leaves, some salt and pepper, 0.5 liter red wine and 0.75 liter soup
3. Let this braise covered for approximately 1 hour
4. Remove the hare pieces
5. Stir 0.25 liter cream with 2 tablespoons flour, pour this into the sauce and season with 4 deca sugar, and if available, with hare blood
6. If the sauce is too thick, pour in some spoonfuls of red wine, let it come to a boil, then strain and bring to a boil again
7. Skim off the fat and pour the sauce over the hare pieces arranged neatly on a platter
8. Serve with polenta, noodles of choice, small fried onions or similar
