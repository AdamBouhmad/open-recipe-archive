## Ingredients

- cleaned snails
- pea broth
- ginger
- nutmeg flower
- pepper
- maurach fish
- crayfish tails and claws
- artichoke bottoms
- fish pieces
- fish livers
- carp roe
- small bread dumplings
- asparagus
- young lettuce
- cauliflower
- crayfish broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Simmer cleaned snails with pea broth, ginger, nutmeg flower and pepper in a small pot
2. Simultaneously simmer maurach fish and strain the broth from both
3. Mix the maurach with the snails
4. Shell the tails and claws from cooked crayfish and add them
5. Add some beautiful sliced artichoke bottoms
6. Can also add half-overcooked filleted fish pieces, sliced overbrowned fish livers, carp roe, small bread dumplings, asparagus, young lettuce and cauliflower as available in season, all half-overcooked
7. Pour crayfish broth over everything in a wide pan
8. Add a good piece of butter
9. Season with nutmeg flower, pepper and ginger
10. Let it simmer until it becomes thick, for which purpose the broth is poured off several times and added back
11. When serving, sprinkle with nutmeg flower
