## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 3 dl milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 piece vanilla
- 14 dkg sugar

## Directions

1. In a snow kettle, whisk 3 dl milk with 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1 piece vanilla and 14 dkg sugar over gentle heat until slightly thickened.
2. Strain the cream, cool it, fill it into a snow kettle or freezing container and freeze it.
3. If making the ice in a snow kettle, place it in a tub with well-salted ice and let the mass become stiff under continuous turning of the snow kettle.
4. When frozen material begins to form on the wall of the kettle, it must be loosened with a cooking spoon or spatula.
5. If making the ice in a machine, the container must first be inserted, then surrounded with salted ice, after which the mass is filled in and frozen under continuous turning.
