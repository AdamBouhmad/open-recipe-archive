## Ingredients

- 14 Loffel butter
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- 18 Loffel semolina
- 9 Loffel semolina

## Directions

1. Beat 14 Loffel butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons water, 2 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, salt and 18 Loffel semolina
3. Let stand covered for 1/2 hour
4. Stir in 9 Loffel semolina and let stand covered for 2 hours
5. Form small knödel and cook 1/4 hour in salted water
6. Cool with cold water, let stand covered for 5 minutes, then remove with a skimming spoon and put in the soup
