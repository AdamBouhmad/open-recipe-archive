## Ingredients

- veal leftovers or 30 dkg freshly braised veal shoulder
- 3 passed anchovies
- salt
- bread rolls, buttered

## Directions

1. Grind veal leftovers or veal shoulder twice and pass through a sieve
2. Mix with 3 passed anchovies and salt
3. Spread on buttered bread rolls
4. Serve
