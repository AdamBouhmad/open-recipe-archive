## Ingredients

- One pound early cherries
- One half pound sugar
- Egg white

## Directions

1. From one pound of such cherries, half cut off the stems, then clarify half a pound sugar with egg white and skim it, lay the cherries in it, and bring them to a boil a couple of times.
2. Arrange them together with the syrup on a plate.
