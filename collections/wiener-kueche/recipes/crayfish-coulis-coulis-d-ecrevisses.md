## Ingredients

- 25 medium-sized crayfish
- salt water
- meat broth as needed

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish in salt water
2. Remove and set aside the claws and tails in the soup pot
3. Crush the crayfish shells with meat broth
4. Then strain through a sieve
5. Continue as with the previous soup
