## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 deca powdered sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 4 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/2 liter lukewarm milk
- 60 deca flour
- 1/4 liter poppy seeds ground very fine
- 1/8 liter boiling milk
- 1/2 to 9 or 12 deca sugar or syrup as needed
- 1/2 pinch finely cut lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cream butter with egg yolks, powdered sugar, and salt
2. Add fresh yeast dissolved in milk and flour, mix well
3. Beat until dough separates from spoon
4. Cover and let rise one-third higher in warm place
5. Meanwhile prepare poppy filling: pour 1/8 liter boiling milk over finely ground poppy seeds, stir in sugar or syrup and lemon peel, cook well and set aside to cool
6. Roll out dough to knife-back thickness on flour-dusted board
7. Cut into desired-size squares
8. Brush each corner with beaten egg and top with 1 teaspoon poppy filling
9. Roll up from the brushed corner to enclose filling, twisting into peaked shape
10. Place on butter-brushed baking sheet 3 fingers apart
11. Cover and let rise 1/4 to 1/2 hour in warm place
12. Brush with beaten egg
13. Bake slowly in oven
14. Arrange on serving dish and sprinkle with pounded sugar
