## Ingredients

- Roe deer leg, skinned and spitted
- Fat
- Marinade (as described in No. 703)
- Onion
- Root vegetables
- Spices
- Juniper berries
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Butter
- Paper for wrapping
- Cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade as specified in No. 703
2. Put some fat in a casserole, place the skinned and spitted leg on it, put it in the oven and baste it frequently with several spoonfuls of marinade during roasting
3. After approximately 1½ hours it will be tender
4. 1 hour before serving, baste it with ½ liter of cream and let it finish roasting
5. Cut the meat into pieces as desired, strain and pass the sauce and pour it over or serve in a sauceboat
6. Alternatively, place the leg directly in the casserole prepared with fat, onion, root vegetables, spices and juniper berries, and after it has gone down, baste it with equal parts wine, vinegar and water
7. Let it braise covered with a sheet of paper brushed with butter for approximately 1⅛ hours in the oven
8. Baste it with cream ⅓ hour before it is done and let it finish roasting uncovered
9. Transfer it with the fat skimmed from the sauce to another casserole and set it in a warm place
10. Strain the sauce and pour it over the cut meat or serve in a sauceboat
