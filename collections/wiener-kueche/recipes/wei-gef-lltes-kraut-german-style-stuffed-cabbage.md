## Ingredients

- cabbage leaves
- boiling water
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 30 tablespoons finely cut bacon
- 1/6 teaspoon finely cut parsley
- 1 knife-point pepper
- salt, if necessary
- 1 1/4 liters milk cream
- 4 tablespoons soaked rolls without crust, well pressed
- 1/4 liter broth
- milk cream
- 1/4 liter butter sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the cabbage, dip them in boiling water, lay them on a cloth so each leaf lies separately
2. Make a filling: 12 tablespoons butter are beaten fluffy, 2 whole eggs, 30 tablespoons finely cut bacon, 1/6 teaspoon finely cut parsley, 1 knife-point pepper, if necessary some salt, 1 1/4 liters milk cream, 4 tablespoons soaked rolls without crust, well pressed, all mixed together
3. Spread this on the leaves, roll them together, place in a casserole lined with butter, pour 1/4 liter broth and some milk cream over them and stew for approximately 3/4 hours
4. After this time, pour 1/4 liter butter sauce over the steamed dish and cook once more
5. Then remove the fat cleanly, lift out the cabbage with a serving spoon, place on a platter and serve the sauce with it
