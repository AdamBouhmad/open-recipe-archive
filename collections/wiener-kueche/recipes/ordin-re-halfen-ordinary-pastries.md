## Ingredients

- 1 liter flour
- 3 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter milk
- 4 deca melted butter or lard
- 1/4 liter milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter flour into a deep bowl, make a well in the center and pour in the yeast dissolved in milk, stir a little together and let stand covered in a warm place
2. Beat together 4 deca melted butter or lard, 1/4 liter milk, 2 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, 1 tablespoon sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt, pour into the flour, stir well and beat the dough until it separates from the spoon, then let rise in a warm place
3. Further treatment is as described in the preceding numbers
