## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Sugar, finely ground
- Egg batter
- Fat, very hot

## Directions

1. Cut apricots in half and remove the pits
2. Cut the pits finely
3. Place apricots in a deep bowl, sprinkle with finely ground sugar, and let rest for about 1 hour
4. Take apricots with a fork, dip in egg batter, and fry in very hot fat until light brown
5. Remove with a slotted spoon, place briefly on blotting paper, arrange on a platter, and serve hot with sugar sprinkled on top
