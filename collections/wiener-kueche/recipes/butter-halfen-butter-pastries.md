## Ingredients

- 1 liter flour
- 3 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 4 deca melted beef tallow
- 7 1/4 liter milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 8 deca lard
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter flour into a deep bowl, make a well in the center, pour in the yeast mixture dissolved in lukewarm milk, stir a little together and let it stand covered in a warm place
2. Meanwhile, beat together 4 deca melted beef tallow, 7 1/4 liter milk, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 6 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
3. Pour this mixture into the flour, stir well and beat the dough for a long time until it separates from the spoon, then let it stand covered in a warm place until it has risen to double height
4. When this has happened, put 8 deca lard in a casserole, let it become hot and put a few drops into each indentation of a pastry pan, then put 1 tablespoon of the dough into each
5. When the bottom side is brown, pour some hot lard or clarified butter from the side into each pastry, turn the halfen using a pastry needle or fork and bake the other side brown as well
6. Place the halfen on paper, lay 2 together and fill between them with whatever filling desired, arrange on a plate and serve sprinkled with sugar
