## Ingredients

- 1 well-aged beef round steak
- Bacon strips (cut into finger-length pieces)
- Salt and pepper
- Onion
- Green parsley
- Capers
- Onion, root vegetables, cabbage, and whole spices (for the braising bed)
- Wine and broth (as needed)

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the beef round steak
2. Pound it with the flat side of the meat mallet so it becomes somewhat wider
3. Insert finger-length pieces of bacon that have been rolled in salt and pepper into the meat
4. Sprinkle the meat with pepper
5. Finely chop bacon, onion, green parsley, and capers; spread this mixture on the inner side of the meat
6. Roll the meat with the thicker side inward toward the thinner part, and wrap it tightly with kitchen twine
7. Line a casserole with onion, root vegetables, cabbage, and whole spices
8. Place the meat on top so that the open side faces upward
9. Brown the meat, then pour in enough wine and broth to cover the meat
10. Braise for 4-5 hours
11. When the roast is tender, remove it from the casserole
