## Ingredients

- Eggs, as many as needed
- Fine flour
- Green parsley
- Salt
- Ginger
- Nutmeg flower (Muscatblüthe)
- Butter

## Directions

1. Stir eggs, add fine flour, green parsley, salt, ginger, and nutmeg flower, and mix everything well together.
2. Tie it in a serviette and simmer it like a pudding in water.
3. When it has cooked long enough, slice it, bring the slices into a casserole with hot butter, bake them in it, and serve dry.
