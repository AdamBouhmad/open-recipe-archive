## Ingredients

- Cleaned partridges
- Large lentils
- Capon fat or fat from another roast
- Onions
- Bacon
- Lemon peel
- Lemon juice
- Wine

## Directions

1. Cut the heads off the cleaned partridges, bend the wings around, salt and truss them with thin skewers
2. Cook beautiful large lentils until soft, keeping them whole and removing the husks completely
3. After draining the water, stew the lentils in a pan with some capon fat or fat from another roast and finely cut onions
4. The partridges can be roasted or stewed with bacon, lemon peel, a little lemon juice, onions and some wine
5. Pour the small sauce from the partridges onto the lentils and let them stew together for a short while
6. When serving, place the partridges in the middle of the dish and arrange the lentils over them
