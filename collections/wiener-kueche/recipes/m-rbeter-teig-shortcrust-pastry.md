## Ingredients

- 56 Deca fine flour
- 42 Deca cold, firm butter
- 3/4 teaspoon of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter of cream or milk cream
- 1/4 liter of white wine

## Directions

1. Spread 56 Deca fine flour on a board.
2. Cut 42 Deca cold, firm butter into small pieces with a knife, add 3/4 teaspoon of salt and rub the butter together with the flour using both hands.
3. Make a depression in the middle, add 4 egg yolks, 1/4 liter of cream or milk cream and 1/4 liter of white wine.
4. Mix with a knife and work with both hands as quickly as possible into a smooth dough until bubbles are visible.
5. Place on a flour-dusted tray, cover with cloth and let rest at a cold place for about 1 hour.
6. Further treatment of rolling out and folding is evident from the description of 'Butterteig feinster Art' (No. 1045).
7. Use for pastries, croustades and the like.
