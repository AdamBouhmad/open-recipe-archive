## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 10 hectograms decrustred semolina bread soaked in milk
- 6 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 6 tablespoons flour
- plums

## Directions

1. Beat 6 hectograms butter until fluffy
2. Gradually add 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
3. Mix with 10 hectograms decrustred semolina bread soaked and squeezed dry, 6 tablespoons bread crumbs, and 6 tablespoons flour
4. Let rest for 1/4 hour
5. Place dough on a flour-dusted board, press out with a noodle roller to the thickness of a finger, and cut pieces the size of plums
6. Place 1 plum in the center of each piece
7. Distribute the dough evenly over it and place the noodles in boiling salted water
8. Cook covered for 15 minutes, stirring the bottom occasionally with a wooden spoon so the noodles do not stick
9. After 15 minutes, remove with a slotted spoon
