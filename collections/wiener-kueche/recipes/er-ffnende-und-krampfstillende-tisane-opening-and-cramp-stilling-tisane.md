## Ingredients

- one handful of pure barley
- one half Loth each of Scorzonera and chicory root
- three Maß of water

## Directions

1. Take one handful of pure barley, Scorzonera, and chicory root, one half Loth each
2. Cook these together in three Maß of water until two Maß remain
3. Strain through a sieve
