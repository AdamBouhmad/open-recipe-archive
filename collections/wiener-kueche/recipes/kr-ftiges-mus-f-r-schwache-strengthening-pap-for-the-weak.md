## Ingredients

- thin white bread slices
- rose water or cinnamon water
- sugar
- almond milk

## Directions

1. Cut thin white bread slices
2. Toast them until yellow
3. Pour rose water or cinnamon water over them so they become soft
4. Sprinkle sugar on top
5. Rub everything together with almond milk to make a pap
6. It will dissolve on the tongue
