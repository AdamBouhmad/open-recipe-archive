## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast with bones removed
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the veal breast by pushing back the meat, turning the bone, and pulling it out
2. Salt and pepper the breast
3. Roll it together and bind it with twine
4. Treat it like the naturally roasted veal breast
