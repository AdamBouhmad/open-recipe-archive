## Ingredients

- several beautiful apples
- sugar
- lemon peel
- water
- Mundgemmel (a type of roll)
- melted butter
- apricot marmalade
- optional: raisins, orange peel, citron peel, cinnamon, or crushed vanilla

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into pieces, and cook with sugar, lemon peel, and a little water into a marmalade.
2. Allow the marmalade to cool.
3. Slice a Mundgemmel into thin slices and dip each piece into melted butter.
4. Lay them symmetrically one over the other in a pan, spreading the apple marmalade over them but leaving a hole in the middle.
5. Fill the hole in the middle with apricot marmalade.
6. Cover the top completely with bread slices dipped in butter.
7. Place in a well-heated oven and bake for three-quarters of an hour.
8. If desired, add large and small raisins, orange peel or citron peel, cinnamon, or some crushed vanilla.
