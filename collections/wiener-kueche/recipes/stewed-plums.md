## Ingredients

- fresh pitted plums
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place fresh pitted plums in a casserole with some water
2. Add sugar and cinnamon to taste
3. Braise covered until soft
4. Add several spoonfuls of butter sauce
5. Let it come to the boil
6. If desired, add some white or red wine or crushed spices
