## Ingredients

- 21 dkg sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 14 dkg raw grated almonds
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 25 dkg cooked, passed chestnuts (40 dkg raw)
- 6 dkg vanilla sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoons water
- 8 dkg grated chocolate
- 8 dkg chopped butter
- ¼ l schlagobers (whipped cream)
- angelica or candied fruits

## Directions

1. Beat sugar and egg yolks for 30 minutes
2. Mix in raw grated almonds
3. Fold in stiff egg whites together with cooked, passed chestnuts
4. Fill into form
5. Bake at moderate heat
6. Cut in half, coat with filling, reassemble
7. Glaze brown or coat with sweetened schlagobers, decorate with angelica or candied fruits
8. For filling: Beat 6 dkg vanilla sugar with 2 egg yolks and 2 spoons water over steam until thick, cool, fold in 8 dkg grated chocolate and 8 dkg chopped butter, and lightly mix in ¼ l schlagobers
