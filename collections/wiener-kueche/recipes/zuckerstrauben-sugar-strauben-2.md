## Ingredients

- six tablespoons fine flour
- six egg yolks
- one seidel cream
- four tablespoons ground sugar
- hot lard
- sugar and cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Make a batter from six tablespoons fine flour, six egg yolks, one seidel cream, and four tablespoons ground sugar
2. Stir everything well
3. Drop it through the strauben spoon into hot lard
4. Fry the strauben until beautifully golden yellow
5. Dust them with sugar and cinnamon and serve
