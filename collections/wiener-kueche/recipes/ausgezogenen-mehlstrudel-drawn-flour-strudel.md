## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound flour
- 2 eggs and the white of 1 egg
- a nut-sized piece of melted butter
- salt
- lukewarm water
- melted butter for brushing
- sour cream
- fine bread crumbs
- 1 measure simmering cream

## Directions

1. Mix flour, eggs, melted butter, salt, and a little lukewarm water to make a dough as firm as butter dough
2. Knead the dough under the hands until very fine
3. Wrap in cloth to keep from drying and keep slightly warm
4. After half an hour, spread a clean cloth over a pillow or table
5. Roll out the dough slightly and have two people gently stretch it from both sides
6. Place on the cloth and stretch it all around as finely as possible without tearing
7. Let it dry slightly when thin enough
8. Brush with melted butter, then with sour cream
9. Sprinkle fine bread crumbs over it
10. Roll loosely together and place in a buttered pan shaped like a spiral
11. Pour about 1 measure of simmering cream around it
12. Bake in an oven or with heat above and below until lightly baked
13. Serve in the pan or removed onto a platter
