## Ingredients

- Beef feet, boiled until very soft
- Butter
- 6 eggs
- Breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- Chopped veal
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Chopped chervil
- Onions

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the bones of the boiled beef feet and chop it very finely
2. Beat butter until fluffy
3. Add 6 eggs gradually
4. Add some breadcrumbs soaked in milk and chopped veal
5. Add the chopped beef feet
6. Season with salt and nutmeg
7. Stir everything together well and proceed as with other puddings
8. For the sauce: make it from chopped chervil and onions sweated in butter, cooking it with a little flour scattered over it in meat broth
