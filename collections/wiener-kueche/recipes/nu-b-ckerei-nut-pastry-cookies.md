## Ingredients

- 17 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg grated nuts

## Directions

1. On the board, make a dough from the flour, butter, sugar and grated nuts
2. Roll out and cut into desired shapes
3. Place on an unbuttered baking sheet
4. Bake at moderate oven heat
5. Sprinkle the pastry with sugar
