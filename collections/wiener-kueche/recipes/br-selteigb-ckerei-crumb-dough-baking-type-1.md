## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 10 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- pinch of cinnamon
- lemon zest
- egg white
- coarse sugar or chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Crumble 14 dkg flour with 10 dkg butter on the board
2. Mix with 7 dkg sugar, 2 egg yolks, a pinch of cinnamon, and lemon zest
3. Let the dough become stiff in a cool place
4. Roll out thin and cut into stars and rings
5. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and bake light brown
6. Treat like the pretzels: brush with egg white and dip in coarse sugar or chopped almonds
