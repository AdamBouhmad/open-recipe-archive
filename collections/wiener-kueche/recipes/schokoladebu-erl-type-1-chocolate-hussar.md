## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 10 dkg sugar
- 10 dkg raw grated almonds
- 4 dkg grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Blend 2 egg whites with 10 dkg sugar, 10 dkg raw grated almonds, and 4 dkg grated chocolate in a wide mixing basin
2. Using two small spoons, form little mounds on a buttered baking sheet
3. Bake in a moderately hot oven until brown
