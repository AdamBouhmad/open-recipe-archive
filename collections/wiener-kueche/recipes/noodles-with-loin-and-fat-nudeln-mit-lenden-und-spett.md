## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of loin, prepared as faded noodles (No. 1201)
- 32 tablespoons of strained mushrooms
- 8 tablespoons of finely diced, golden-roasted bacon

## Directions

1. Prepare faded noodles from 1/2 pound of loin
2. Add 32 tablespoons of strained mushrooms and mix well
3. Arrange heaped on a platter
4. Pour 8 tablespoons of finely diced, golden-roasted bacon over the noodles
5. Serve hot
