## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Shallots
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- A few cloves of garlic
- Some finely chopped ham or lean bacon
- Bay leaves
- Onion slices
- Butter
- Beef broth
- Egg yolks
- Blanched pistachios

## Directions

1. Braise parsley, shallots, mushrooms, truffles, garlic, ham or bacon, bay leaves, and onion slices in butter.
2. Pour in beef broth in proportion.
3. Let cook well.
4. Pass through a fine sieve.
5. Thicken the sauce with egg yolks.
6. Add blanched pistachios.
7. Serve over meat.
