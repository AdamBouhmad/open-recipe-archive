## Ingredients

- Half-medlars
- 1/2 liter medlar juice
- 42 grams sugar
- Spices (cloves and cinnamon bound in a cloth)

## Directions

1. Remove the medlars from their stems, crush them with the cores, and let them stand in an earthenware or porcelain bowl in a cool place for 2 days
2. Strain the juice through a felt sack and pour what has run out into bottles, but do not fill these completely
3. Add the crushed sugar, namely 42 grams sugar per 1/2 liter
4. Add spices (cloves and cinnamon) bound in a cloth
5. When this medlar juice has stood in the sun for 2-3 weeks, seal the bottle and store it in a cool place
6. This juice is used for glazes, creams, as an addition to sauces and various dishes
7. One can also prepare a very good liqueur from this juice by adding a mixture of brandy or fruit brandy after 5-10 weeks
