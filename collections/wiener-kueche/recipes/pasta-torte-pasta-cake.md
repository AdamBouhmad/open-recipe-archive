## Ingredients

- 5½ dkg flour
- 2½ dkg butter
- 2 dkg sugar
- ½ egg yolk
- jam
- 5 egg whites
- 21 dkg vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from 5½ dkg flour, 2½ dkg butter, 2 dkg sugar, and ½ egg yolk
2. After resting, roll out round (to the size of the torte form bottom) and bake on an ungreased sheet at higher heat
3. Spread with jam
4. Mix stiff egg whites from 5 eggs with 21 dkg vanilla sugar
5. Pile the meringue smoothly and high onto the torte, sprinkle with sugar, optionally pipe rosettes on top, and place in the open oven for a few minutes until the meringue is baked golden
