## Ingredients

- Kohlrabi, neatly cut
- Yellow turnips
- Cabbage
- Artichokes
- Skirret
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Goose fat
- Thin bacon slices
- Boiled pickled tongues
- Ham, nicely cut

## Directions

1. Braise neatly cut kohlrabi, yellow turnips, cabbage, artichokes, and skirret each separately in a little butter until soft
2. Dust each with a little flour, add a small amount of broth, and shake well so they do not fall apart
3. Line the bottom of a mold brushed with goose fat with thin bacon slices
4. Layer the cabbage, then a layer of boiled pickled tongues cut into thin slices
5. Add another layer of braised kohlrabi, then another layer of tongue, then layers of yellow turnips, tongue, artichokes, again tongue, and finally skirret
6. Place the mold in a steam bath for a quarter hour
7. Turn it out onto a platter, line the edge with nicely cut ham
8. Remove the bacon from the top and serve the dish quickly to the table
