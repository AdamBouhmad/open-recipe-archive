## Ingredients

- 14 decagrams whipped butter
- 14 decagrams finely pounded sugar
- 6 hard-boiled, passed egg yolks
- 12 decagrams peeled, finely pounded almonds
- cinnamon
- finely cut lemon peel
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold
- bitter almond cream

## Directions

1. Mix 14 decagrams whipped butter with 14 decagrams finely pounded sugar well
2. Add 6 hard-boiled, passed egg yolks
3. Add 12 decagrams peeled, finely pounded almonds, cinnamon, and finely cut lemon peel
4. Mix well
5. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
6. Fill a mold buttered and dusted with flour
7. Cook covered in steam for 1/2 hour
8. Serve with bitter almond cream
