## Ingredients

- Morels
- Asparagus
- Butter
- Fresh parsley
- Cream
- Asparagus water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the morels; wash the heads, cut them into strips, and braise them in butter with fresh parsley.
2. Prepare a butter sauce with cream and some asparagus water and cook it well.
3. Pour this sauce over the boiled asparagus prepared in the usual way and serve with the morels.
