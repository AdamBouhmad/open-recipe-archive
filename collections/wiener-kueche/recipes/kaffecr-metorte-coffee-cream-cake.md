## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 15 dkg sugar
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 spoons very strong black coffee
- 25-30 small biscuits
- 1 dl schlagobers (whipped cream)
- sugar
- swollen chopped pistachios

## Directions

1. Beat butter, gradually add sugar, whole eggs, and very strong black coffee
2. Coat bottom of cake form with this cream
3. Lay a layer of small biscuits closely together with no gaps
4. Cover with cream, then biscuits again, continuing until all used
5. Coat with cream at the end, place paper on top
6. Set pot or pan with cold water on top to weigh down mass
7. Let stand one day
8. Remove paper, loosen edges, turn out of form
9. Cover with sweetened schlagobers
10. Sprinkle with swollen chopped pistachios, or make rosettes or rings with star piping tube if more schlagobers available
