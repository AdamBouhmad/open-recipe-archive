## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 4 ounces butter
- 1 whole egg
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 liter flour on a board, add 1/4 teaspoon salt and 4 ounces butter, rub fine with both hands
2. Make a hollow in the center, add 1 whole egg and mix by adding necessary lukewarm water with a knife, mixing well together
3. Work into a smooth dough with both hands as specified in Lungen-Strudel (Recipe 75)
