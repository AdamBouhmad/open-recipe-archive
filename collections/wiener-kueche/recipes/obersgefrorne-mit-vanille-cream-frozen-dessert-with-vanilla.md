## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 20 deca of finely sifted sugar
- 1 half-finger-long piece vanilla
- 1 liter unsweetened, good cream

## Directions

1. Place egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla in a snow-beater (whisk bowl)
2. Gradually add 1 liter unsweetened good cream
3. Beat with the whisk over weak fire until the mixture begins to thicken (becomes thick), but it must not boil
4. Remove from fire and continue beating until lukewarm
5. Pass through a fine hair sieve into an earthenware or porcelain bowl
6. Fill the freezing box with this mixture and proceed as explained above
