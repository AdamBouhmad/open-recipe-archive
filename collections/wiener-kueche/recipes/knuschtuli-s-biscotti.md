## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 14 ounces finely sifted sugar
- 14 ounces flour

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites until stiff
2. Lightly mix in 14 ounces finely sifted sugar and 14 ounces flour
3. Fill this mass into a pastry bag and pipe finger-long biscotti or desired figures onto a baking sheet covered with writing paper
4. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar and bake in low heat in the oven
