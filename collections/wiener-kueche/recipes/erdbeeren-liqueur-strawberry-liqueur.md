## Ingredients

- 1 measure strawberries
- half measure good cherry spirit or plum brandy
- 4 loth linden blossom water
- 4 loth rose water
- 4 loth sloe blossom water
- 4 loth hawthorn water
- 4 loth black cherry water
- 8 loth cinnamon water
- 1 loth cinnamon
- 2 quentchen cloves
- 14 pounds Canary sugar

## Directions

1. Take 1 measure strawberries and half measure cherry spirit or plum brandy
2. Pour both into a glass flask
3. Place in a cool location for 8 days
4. Shake well once daily
5. Press through a cloth
6. To 1 measure of pressed juice, add 4 loth each of linden blossom water, rose water, sloe blossom water, and hawthorn water
7. Add 4 loth black cherry water and 8 loth cinnamon water
8. Add 1 loth cinnamon and 2 quentchen cloves
9. Add 14 pounds Canary sugar
10. Place the glass in the sun for 8 days
11. Shake well daily
12. Filter the prepared liqueur and fill into bottles
