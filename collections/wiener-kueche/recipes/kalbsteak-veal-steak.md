## Ingredients

- Veal loin, 2 finger-thick slices
- Flour
- Fat

## Directions

1. Cut 2 finger-thick slices from a veal loin
2. Pound the slices
3. Salt them
4. Shape them round
5. Dip one side in flour
6. Place floured side down in a pan with hot fat
7. Brown on both sides
