## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water
- 12 deca sugar
- 12 deca (3 tablets) chocolate
- 1/2 liter cold cream
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 liter water with 12 deca sugar
2. Meanwhile, melt 12 deca (3 tablets) chocolate in the oven on a baking sheet
3. Put the chocolate in a saucepan
4. Gradually pour the boiling sugared water onto it
5. Stir until completely smooth and fully cooked
6. Put 1/2 liter cold cream and 4 egg yolks in a snow kettle and mix
7. While on the heat, slowly pour in the chocolate and beat with a whisk until the mass begins to thicken, but do not boil
8. Use immediately
9. If desired, strain the mass through a sieve
