## Ingredients

- 30 dkg beef sirloin
- 30 dkg pork shoulder, boned
- 2 rolls
- 4 dkg bacon
- 1/2 cream
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove bones, skin, and fat from the meat, wipe dry, and cut into cubes
2. Pass through a meat grinder twice with 1½ rolls (soaked in water and squeezed), 1/4 onion, and parsley
3. Place in a bowl and mix with 1 egg, salt, pepper, and bacon cut into small cubes
4. Place on a board sprinkled with breadcrumbs and shape into 2 rolls
5. Place in a pan, top with fat pieces, add a little water
6. Roast in the oven for 1 hour, basting frequently with its own juices and hot water
7. After 30 minutes, gradually baste with cream
8. Transfer to a platter, slice, and serve with the pan drippings
