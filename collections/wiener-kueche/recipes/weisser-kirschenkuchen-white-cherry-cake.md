## Ingredients

- 28 deca butter
- 24 deca caster sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 30 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 28 deca butter with 24 deca caster sugar and 6 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 6 egg whites with 30 deca flour
3. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour, 2 to 3 fingers high
4. Further treatment is as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
