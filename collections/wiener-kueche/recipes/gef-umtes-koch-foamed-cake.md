## Ingredients

- 3 loth sifted sugar
- 4 loth fruit sauce
- 4 eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. In a bowl, combine 3 loth sifted sugar and 4 loth fruit sauce
2. Beat the egg whites from 4 eggs to stiff peaks
3. Stir for a quarter hour
4. Butter a cake sheet
5. Spread the mixture on it and bake slowly in a cake pan
6. Sprinkle with sugar when done
