## Ingredients

- oysters with their sauce
- pastry cases
- feather for decoration
- Madeira or Tokay wine

## Directions

1. Place the required number of oysters with their sauce on gentle heat for a quarter hour
2. During this time, form the pastry cases and place one oyster in the middle of each
3. Draw a feather crosswise over the oyster to make it look like a rose
4. Bake the pastries quickly on a baking sheet in the oven
5. Drizzle a little Madeira or Tokay wine in the middle
6. Serve immediately warm
