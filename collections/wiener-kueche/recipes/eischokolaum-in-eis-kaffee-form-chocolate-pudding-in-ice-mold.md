## Ingredients

- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 deca flour
- 6 egg whites
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Make a biscuit mass from 16 deca vanilla sugar, 6 egg yolks, 12 deca flour and the snow from 6 egg whites
2. Spread this on two baking sheets knife-edge thick
3. Dust one of them heavily with grated chocolate
4. When both are baked, remove from the sheet and let stand in a cool place for several hours to soften and be cut better
5. Cut a star shape from the brown biscuit mass and use it to cover the bottom of the corresponding smooth mold with the brown side
6. Cut pieces from the other biscuit to fill the empty spaces in the star, ensuring the yellow side faces the mold so it contrasts with the black star
7. Cut strips of desired width and cut equal-sized triangles from these, the smaller the more elegant
8. Line the walls of the mold with these so that the edges of a yellow triangle always connect to those of a black one
9. Place the lined casserole in ice and fill with whipped bitter almond cream or any cream with isinglass or gelatin
10. Let stand 1 hour covered in ice
11. Press slightly on the walls with fingers and turn out
12. If necessary, carefully loosen the biscuit mass from the walls with a thin knife and tap the mold lightly when turning out
13. Instead of baked biscuit mass, biscotti can also be used
