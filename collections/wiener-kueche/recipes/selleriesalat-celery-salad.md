## Ingredients

- celery, sliced
- rapunzel
- lactuke or other greens
- wine vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Slice the celery into pieces
2. It can be eaten mixed with rapunzel, lactuke, and other greens, or alone raw, blanched with boiling water, or also cooked
3. For an excellent flavorful preparation: stew the slices in good wine vinegar, well covered
4. When soft enough, remove from heat and let cool in its gelatinous sauce
5. Add the necessary oil without pouring additional fresh vinegar over it
