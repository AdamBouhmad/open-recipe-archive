## Ingredients

- Several finger-thick slices of salmon fillet
- Truffle pieces, pencil-thick and half-finger long
- Flounder, cut similarly
- 1 bay leaf
- Onion, thinly sliced
- Fresh parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Butter dough
- Fish farce
- Bacon slices

## Directions

1. Cut several finger-thick slices from the salmon fillet.
2. Lard these with pencil-thick and half-finger-long pieces of truffle and similarly cut flounder.
3. Place them in a deep bowl, cover with 1 bay leaf, thinly sliced onion, some fresh parsley.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, spray with lemon juice, and let stand covered for several hours, turning occasionally.
5. Prepare the butter dough and form the pâté as described in the 'Goose Liver Pâté' recipe (No. 1057).
6. Once the dough of the prepared pâté is lined inside with thin slices of bacon, spread the bottom 2 fingers high with fish farce, lay the salmon slices upon it, spread again 2 fingers high with farce, and continue until the pâté is completely filled.
7. Proceed as described in the 'Goose Liver Pâté' recipe.
8. This pâté, which is served on fast days, can be enjoyed both warm and cold; in the latter case, however, only after 2 days.
