## Ingredients

- one good piece baked pike
- one roll
- milk
- butter
- three whole eggs
- four egg yolks
- lemon peel
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the flesh from a good piece of baked pike away from the bones, but first remove the brown skin
2. Cut the flesh finely with a knife
3. Cook the crumb of one roll in milk like a panada and let it cool
4. Meanwhile beat a piece of butter fluffy
5. Beat in three whole eggs and four egg yolks
6. Add the panada, the cut pike, some lemon peel and sugar to taste
7. Coat a dish and mold with butter, pour the pudding in
8. Coat it with foamed egg white, sprinkle it with sugar, and bake it beautifully in a cake pan
