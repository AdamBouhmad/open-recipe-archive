## Ingredients

- 1/4 l lentils
- cold water, hand-high amount
- 12 dkg bacon
- salt

## Directions

1. Sort and wash the lentils.
2. Cover them with cold water, hand-high amount.
3. Cover and braise for about 1 hour, braising more than boiling.
4. Do not stir much so the lentils do not fall apart.
5. Strain them and arrange on a round platter.
6. Salt them.
7. Cut the bacon into small cubes, roast it until light yellow, strain it and pour over the lentils.
8. Place the cracklings in the middle.
