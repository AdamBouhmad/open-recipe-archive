## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams butter
- 4 decagrams finely ground sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 3 decagrams pressed yeast dissolved in 1/2 liter lukewarm milk
- 56 decagrams flour
- melted butter for brushing
- 1 teaspoon of filling such as poppy seed, nut filling, compote or marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, gradually stir in the sugar, salt, egg yolks, and whole egg
2. Mix in the pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk and the flour, stirring well
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
4. Cover with a cloth and let it rise in a warm place
5. When risen, place the dough on a flour-dusted board, roll it out to the thickness of a small finger, and cut into strips 3 fingers wide and 1 finger long
6. Fill each strip with 1 teaspoon of desired filling such as poppy seed, nut filling, compote or marmalade
7. Roll up the filling in the dough and lay the rolls in a butter-greased casserole so that the ends of the rolled dough face upward
8. Brush with melted butter at the points where the rolls touch each other
9. Cover with a cloth and let rise in a warm place until the dough has risen again
10. Brush with melted butter and bake slowly for 3/4 to 1 hour
11. Remove the casserole, turn out the rolls, separate them from each other, arrange on a plate, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
