## Ingredients

- 8 oranges
- 4 lemons
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 seidel Madeira wine, Tokay, or sweet sparkling wine
- 1 small glass water

## Directions

1. Rub 8 oranges and 4 lemons on a pound of sugar
2. Put the rubbed portion into a soup pot along with the juice from the oranges and lemons
3. Add a seidel of Madeira wine, Tokay or sweet sparkling wine, and a small glass of water
4. Clarify the remaining sugar as usual
5. Add to the mass
6. Pass through a fine sieve into the freezing container and process
