## Ingredients

- half-ripe quince
- quince skin
- soft center pulp

## Directions

1. Take a half-ripe quince, remove the skin and the soft part from the middle
2. Cut the firm part into neat pieces
3. Treat further as the peaches (Nr. 1850)
