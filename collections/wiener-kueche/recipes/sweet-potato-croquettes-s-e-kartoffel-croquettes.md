## Ingredients

- 1 kilo potatoes
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream or milk
- flour for coating
- eggs for dipping
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Bake 1 kilo potatoes in the oven or in hot ashes, peel them
2. Pass through a ricer into a saucepan
3. Mix with 12 tablespoons butter, 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar, and some salt
4. Stir in 6 egg yolks one after another
5. Place the saucepan on the heat, pour in 1/4 liter of cream or milk, and bring to a boil while stirring constantly
6. Pour into a bowl and cool
7. After completely cooling, prepare as Rice-Croquettes (No. 1136)
