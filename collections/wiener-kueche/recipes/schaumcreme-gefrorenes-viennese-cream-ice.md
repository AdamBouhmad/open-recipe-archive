## Ingredients

- Vanilla Ice prepared
- 1/4 liter beaten cream
- small pieces of crushed Schaumertolk

## Directions

1. Make the mixture as Vanilla Ice
2. When frozen, mix 1/4 liter beaten cream with small pieces of crushed Schaumertolk that has been prepared in the previously described way from 1/4 liter cream
3. When serving, decorate the ice, which can also be served in glasses, with chopped pieces of Schaumertolk as desired
