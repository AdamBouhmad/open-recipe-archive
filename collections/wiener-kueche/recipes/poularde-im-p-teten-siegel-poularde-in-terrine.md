## Ingredients

- 1 poularde
- Bacon slices
- Farce
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. The poularde can be filled into a pâté terrine instead of butter dough.
2. Such a terrine is lined with slices of bacon, spread 2 fingers high with farce, and otherwise treated as described in the 'Poularde Pâté' recipe (No. 1060).
