## Ingredients

- Small cucumbers, picked in dry weather
- Salt
- Cucumber herb
- Birch leaves
- Linden leaves
- 5 sweet mustard seeds
- 5 bitter mustard seeds
- Vinegar (prepared 2 days earlier from 2 parts vinegar with 1 part water, plus 5 shallots, 5 Carambola, and 5 grains each of mixed spices)

## Directions

1. Prepare the vinegar 2 days in advance by boiling 2 parts vinegar with 1 part water
2. In 5 liters of prepared vinegar, add 5 shallots, 5 Carambola, and 5 grains each of various spices, boil for half an hour removing foam, and let cool in an earthenware dish
3. Pick small cucumbers in dry weather, lay them on cloth, sprinkle with salt, and rub with the cloth until they shine, taking no more than 1 hour
4. Place them in an earthenware dish, mix them together, let them sit for quarter hour, and drain on a sieve
5. Line the bottom of prepared jars with some cucumber herb, birch leaves, and linden leaves
6. Add a layer of cucumbers, then herbs and leaves again, continuing until the jar is full
7. On top place 5 sweet and 5 bitter mustard seeds
8. Pour the prepared cold vinegar over them, leaving a space 3 fingers wide at the top
9. Bind with covers and store in a cool, airy place
10. After 3 to 4 weeks the cucumbers can be enjoyed
11. If the vinegar becomes cloudy, strain it, mix fresh vinegar with water, boil and skim it, let it cool completely, and pour over the cucumbers, binding the jars as before
