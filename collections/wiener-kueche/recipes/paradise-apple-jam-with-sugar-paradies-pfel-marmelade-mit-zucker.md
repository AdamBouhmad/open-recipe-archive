## Ingredients

- Paradise apples (1 kilo)
- Sugar (1/2 kilo sugar per 1 kilo of paradise apples)

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as Paradise Apple Jam (No. 1891) cooked in a steam bath
2. After pressing the apples through a sieve, cook them with sugar (1/2 kilo sugar per 1 kilo of paradise apples) with continuous, vigorous stirring for 1/2 hour
3. Fill this marmalade hot into glasses and store it like Apricot Marmalade (No. 1870)
4. When cooking, be careful so that the beautiful red color is not lost
