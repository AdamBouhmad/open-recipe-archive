## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 3 loths bitter almonds
- 1 glass of water
- 1 pound fine sugar
- 1 seidel water

## Directions

1. Carefully clean 1 pound sweet and 3 loths bitter almonds
2. Wash them
3. Pound them very finely with a drinking glass full of water so they do not become oily
4. Cook 1 pound fine sugar with 1 seidel water, stirring frequently, until when you draw a little from the spoon with your second finger, or pull it apart with the help of your thumb, it draws a small thread that breaks immediately and leaves a drop on your thumb
5. Add the pounded almonds gradually to the gentle fire and stir
6. Press through a linen cloth while still warm
7. Fill this syrup into bottles, where it keeps very well for a long time
