## Ingredients

- 3 roasts à 25 dkg
- 6 tablespoons root vegetables
- 2 anchovies
- 1 pickled gherkin
- 4 tablespoons capers
- 1 ring onion
- 1 bay leaf
- thyme
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon zest
- fat for roasting

## Directions

1. Wipe the roasts, remove fat and bones, pound them, salt and pepper them, brush with the farce, roll and tie them
2. In a casserole, roast the root vegetables (cleaned, washed and cut into strips), 1 ring onion, 1 bay leaf, thyme, 5 peppercorns and spice berries in hot fat until dark
3. Add the meat, pour in some water and vinegar, cover and braise for 1-1½ hours, frequently adding hot water or broth
4. Remove the meat, untie it, dust the sauce, pass it through a sieve, return to the casserole, bring to a boil again and serve either separately or with the meat
5. For the farce: bone the anchovies and finely chop them with the gherkin, parsley, capers, ½ onion, 1 bay leaf and lemon zest
