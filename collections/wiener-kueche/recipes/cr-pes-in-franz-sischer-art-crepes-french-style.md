## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter flour
- 3 whole eggs
- 0.5 liter cold milk
- 1 pinch salt
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Place 0.5 liter flour in a deep bowl and gradually stir in 3 whole eggs, 0.5 liter cold milk and 1 pinch salt so that no lumps form but the batter is smooth and fluid
2. Pour 2 tablespoons of this batter on a hot, fat-coated frying pan so that the pan is evenly coated with it
3. When the lower side has a yellow color, carefully turn the crepe over with a spatula or a large fine knife so that it is not damaged, pour a little hot fat from the side if necessary and bake the second side also yellow
4. After placing the baked crepe in a bowl kept warm and continuing the baking in this manner until the batter is finished, coat with apple sauce or any marmalade or almond filling
