## Ingredients

- Calf brain, freed of skin, cooked in salted water and vinegar
- Shell dishes
- Mayonnaise
- Aspic, chopped

## Directions

1. After the brain is completely cooled, cut into pieces of choice
2. Fill the centers of the shells somewhat heaping with the pieces
3. Garnish with chopped aspic
4. Place shells on ice 1.5 hours before serving
5. Cover the brain with one teaspoon of mayonnaise
