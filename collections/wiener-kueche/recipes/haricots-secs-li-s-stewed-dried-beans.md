## Ingredients

- Finely cut bacon
- Garlic, if desired
- 8 ounces fat
- Required soup
- Vinegar
- Herbs and spices as needed
- Salt
- 1/2 liter soft-cooked beans

## Directions

1. Let finely cut bacon and, if desired, some garlic roast in a deep saucepan with 8 ounces fat
2. Pour soup and some vinegar over it
3. Add herbs, salt, and necessary spices and bring to a boil
4. Remove the herbs
5. Add 1/2 liter soft-cooked beans, stir well together, and let them cook
