## Ingredients

- medlars (mispeln)
- pineapples
- strawberries with stems
- mulberries
- blackberries
- cloudberries
- service berries (dirnelbeeren, kirschen or cornel cherries)
- gooseberries
- sloes
- lingonberries
- 80 decilitres sugar
- sugar water

## Directions

1. All these fruits are treated in the same way
2. Always choose a beautiful, large variety and place them, but dry and with completely undamaged skin, in the preserving jars
3. Add so much sugar water of 80 decilitres (as with the Muscatel pears Nr. 1842) that it covers the fruit
4. Proceed as with the Muscatel pears and let them cook 3-4 hours in steam
5. Before placing medlars in the jars, cut off the protruding sepals with a knife
6. Gooseberries are left whole and, if desired, the seeds are removed
