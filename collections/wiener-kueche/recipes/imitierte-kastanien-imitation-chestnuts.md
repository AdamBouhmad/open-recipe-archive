## Ingredients

- 16 deca blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 16 deca fine sifted sugar
- Small pieces of pineapple, citron, nuts, chocolate, or pitted cherries
- Hot chocolate glaze
- Egg white
- Fine pounded sugar
- Spun sugar
- Small paper cases

## Directions

1. Pound 16 deca blanched, finely pounded almonds with 16 deca fine sifted sugar until a dough forms
2. Form into nut-sized knobs, placing a small piece of pineapple, citron, nut, chocolate, or pitted cherry in the center of each
3. Place on paper sprinkled with fine pounded sugar
4. Hold each knob with a decoration needle and dip into hot chocolate glaze only as far as a small spot the size of half a cross remains dry
5. Place on a sugar-sprinkled paper and dry
6. Alternative preparation: Dip the knob in egg white, roll in fine pounded sugar, and hold over a brightly burning spirit flame for several minutes to achieve the appearance of a roasted chestnut
7. Third method: Dip in spun sugar and place in small paper cases
