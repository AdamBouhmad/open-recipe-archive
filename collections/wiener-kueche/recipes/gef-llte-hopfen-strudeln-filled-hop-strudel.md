## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter
- 5 whole eggs
- 48 Deka mashed hops
- 1 1/8 liter milk cream
- salt
- finely chopped green parsley
- 1/2 liter flour for dough
- 4 Deka white bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Whip 12 Deka butter until fluffy
2. Beat in 5 whole eggs one by one
3. Add 48 Deka mashed hops, milk cream, salt, and parsley
4. Mix well
5. Spread on strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
6. Sprinkle 4 Deka bread crumbs over it
7. Roll lightly and treat as lung strudel
