## Ingredients

- 4 Deka butter
- 8 Deka apricot marmalade
- 8 Deka fine ground powdered sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 7 egg whites
- Butter for the mold
- Powdered sugar for dusting
- Biscuit

## Directions

1. Beat butter with apricot marmalade until fluffy
2. Add fine ground sugar and 7 egg yolks one after another and stir for ¾ hour
3. Lightly mix in the stiff snow from 7 egg whites
4. Line a butter-brushed, powdered sugar-dusted mold or bowl densely with biscuit
5. Fill with the above-mentioned mixture
6. Bake for 1 hour in the oven, whereby the souffle rises to twice its height
