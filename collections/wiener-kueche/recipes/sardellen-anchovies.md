## Ingredients

- Salted anchovies
- Water
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Gently wash the salted anchovies in lukewarm water
2. Remove the substances found on the back
3. Separate the anchovies and remove the bones
4. Coil them around the finger
5. Place on a designated plate or serving bowl
6. Pour vinegar and oil over them
7. Serve to table
