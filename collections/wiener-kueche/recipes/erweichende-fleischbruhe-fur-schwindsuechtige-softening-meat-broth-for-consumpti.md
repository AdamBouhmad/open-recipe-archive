## Ingredients

- Two pounds pounded veal meat
- Four loths pure barley
- One and a half measures water
- Eight loths salsify root
- Half pound common lettuce
- Eight loths lamb's lettuce

## Directions

1. Cook the pounded veal meat and barley together in a well-sealed vessel with one and a half measures water for three hours
2. At the end, add eight loths salsify root, half pound common lettuce, and eight loths lamb's lettuce
3. Let it simmer for another quarter hour
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Drink one teacup full every two hours
6. If the broth is too nourishing and too thick, dilute with warm water as desired
