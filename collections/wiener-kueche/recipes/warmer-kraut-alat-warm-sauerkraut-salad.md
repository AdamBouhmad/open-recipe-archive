## Ingredients

- 1 large head of cabbage
- salt
- caraway seeds
- water
- 10 dkg bacon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and cores from the cabbage, wash it, and cut finely into noodle-like strips
2. Sprinkle with salt and caraway seeds in a colander, pour boiling water over it, let it stand covered for 15 minutes, drain the water, and repeat the process
3. Cut the bacon into small cubes, roast until light yellow in a saucepan, and strain
4. When the fat has cooled, add vinegar, bring to a boil, and pour over the drained cabbage
5. Arrange the salad on a round platter and place the warmed bacon pieces in the center
