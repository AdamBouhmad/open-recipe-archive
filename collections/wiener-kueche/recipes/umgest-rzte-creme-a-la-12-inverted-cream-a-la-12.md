## Ingredients

- Half a Maß good cream
- A piece of vanilla
- One quarter pound sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- Butter
- Lemon
- 8 yolks
- Almonds
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take half a Maß good cream, a piece of vanilla, and one quarter pound sugar, set the cream on coal fire, let it boil a few times, and then cool
2. Beat 12 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs well in a casserole, stir in the cold cream, pass everything a couple of times through a fine sieve
3. Brush the mold very lightly with butter and pour the cream into it
4. Set a casserole with water on coal fire, when it is hot place the cups or molds into it, and take care that they do not begin to boil; cover with a lid with light coals
5. As soon as the cream begins to set, turn this onto a plate
6. Continue stirring the remaining cream on the coal fire until it adheres to the cooking spoon
7. Set aside a little, this can also be poured over and served
