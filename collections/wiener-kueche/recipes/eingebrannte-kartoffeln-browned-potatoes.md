## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 4 dkg fat
- 1 onion
- broth or water
- 1 spoonful vinegar
- salt
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Wash, cook, and peel the potatoes, quarter them
2. Make an onion roux with 4 dkg fat and pour it up with broth or water
3. Add the potatoes, 1 spoonful vinegar, salt and some marjoram
4. Cook well for 15 minutes
