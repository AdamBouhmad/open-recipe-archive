## Ingredients

- Redcurrants
- Redcurrant juice of the specified quantity

## Directions

1. Boil it for 1/4 hour with continuous rapid stirring
2. Strain it through flannel and fill it into bottles only when it has cooled
3. Do not use iron or enameled vessels during preparation of the redcurrant juice, as the juice would become blue or black from it; instead of flannel, one can also use a finer linen cloth
