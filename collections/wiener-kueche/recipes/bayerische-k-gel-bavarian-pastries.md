## Ingredients

- 2 dl lukewarm milk
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 8 dkg melted butter
- salt
- 4 dkg sugar
- 1½ dkg yeast
- 30 dkg warmed flour
- hot fat for frying
- powdered sugar
- jam

## Directions

1. Mix 2 dl lukewarm milk with 1 egg, 1 egg yolk, 8 dkg melted butter, salt, 4 dkg sugar, and the risen starter from 1½ dkg yeast
2. Sprinkle this mixture into 30 dkg warmed flour in a mixing bowl
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon and let it rise near the stove
4. After approximately 1 hour, roll it out to finger thickness on a floured board
5. Cut out with a large doughnut cutter and let rise covered for another 30 minutes
6. Make a small indentation in the center of each pastry
7. Place them indentation-side down into hot fat and fry until light brown on both sides
8. Drain, sprinkle with powdered sugar, fill the indentation with jam, and serve warm
