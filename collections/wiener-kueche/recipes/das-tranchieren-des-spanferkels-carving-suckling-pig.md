## Ingredients

- Suckling pig head
- Suckling pig body

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and divide it lengthwise down the middle
2. Cut each half apart again so that 4 pieces result from the head
3. Arrange these on a platter so that the division of the head is not noticeable
4. Without damaging the skin, separate the shoulders from the back, likewise the legs
5. Cut off the belly pieces, divide them into 4 pieces and place them against the head lengthwise
6. Cut the back into strips three fingers wide and lay these on the belly pieces
7. On both sides of the back, place the shoulders cut into desired pieces toward the head
8. Finally, place the two legs cut into pieces together so that they appear as one whole
