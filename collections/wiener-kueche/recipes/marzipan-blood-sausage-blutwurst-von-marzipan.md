## Ingredients

- 1 pound red marzipan
- half pound white marzipan dough
- clean, fat-free dried pork intestines
- almond oil

## Directions

1. To 1 pound red marzipan, take half pound white marzipan dough
2. Cut everything into small irregular pieces and mix together
3. Take clean, transparent, fat-free dried pork intestines
4. Stuff the mixed marzipan pieces tightly in and press well together
5. Moisten your fingers with almond oil during this work, which makes the sausage glossy
