## Ingredients

- ¼ l water
- salt
- caraway seeds
- ¼-½ l cream
- flour
- bread rolls for croutons

## Directions

1. Bring ¼ l water to a boil with salt and caraway seeds
2. Whisk cream with a few tablespoons of flour until smooth
3. Stir the cream mixture into the boiling water
4. Let the soup simmer for 20 minutes
5. Serve with bread croutons
