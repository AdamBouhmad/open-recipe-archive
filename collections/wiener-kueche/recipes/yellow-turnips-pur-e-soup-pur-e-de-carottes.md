## Ingredients

- 16 pieces yellow turnips, cleaned and washed
- 2 Spanish onions
- 1 large parsley root
- 30 ounces butter
- 3 liters soup
- sugar

## Directions

1. Slice 2 Spanish onions and 1 large parsley root thinly
2. Place in 30 ounces butter with 16 pieces of cleaned and washed yellow turnips, cover and braise very slowly until soft
3. Gradually pour in 3 liters of soup and let it cook for 1/2 hour
4. Pass through a fine sieve, place on the fire and slowly bring to a boil so the rising fat and scum can be cleanly removed
5. If the turnips are not sweet enough, adjust with sugar
