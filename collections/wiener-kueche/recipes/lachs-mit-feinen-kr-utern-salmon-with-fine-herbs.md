## Ingredients

- About three pounds of fatty smoked salmon
- Green farce
- Chopped anchovies
- Small cucumbers
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- Half a bottle of white wine
- Bay leaf
- Fine herbs
- Half a pound of butter
- One spoonful of flour
- Peel and juice of a lemon or orange

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon lengthwise into pieces about three fingers wide and one finger long.
2. Make some incisions in each piece and spread green farce well into them.
3. For the farce, mix chopped anchovies, small cucumbers and mushrooms, and always add some truffles.
4. Layer the salmon tightly in a cassole, pour half a bottle of white wine over it, add a bay leaf and fine herbs.
5. Cook the salmon a quarter hour this way, knead one spoonful of flour into a quarter pound of butter, and add it to the sauce in small pieces.
6. Take the peel of a lemon or orange and its juice as well, let everything cook together, and serve.
