## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 21 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 spoon rum
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 21 dkg potato flour
- marmalade
- powdered sugar
- white icing

## Directions

1. Beat 21 dkg butter for 20 minutes.
2. Gradually stir in 21 dkg sugar and 6 egg yolks and beat again for 20 minutes.
3. Mix in 1 spoon rum and 6 stiff egg whites together with 21 dkg potato flour.
4. Fill the mass into the form and bake very slowly.
5. Fill with marmalade, dust with powdered sugar or brush the top with marmalade and white ice.
