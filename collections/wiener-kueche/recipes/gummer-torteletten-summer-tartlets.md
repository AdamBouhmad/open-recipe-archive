## Ingredients

- 14 deka butter
- 16 deka rye flour
- 14 deka almonds with shell, finely pounded and passed through a sieve
- 14 deka flavored sugar
- 1 knife point nutmeg
- 1 whole egg
- sugar syrup
- 8 deka blanched, cut into sticks almonds
- equal amount pistachios
- finely cut citron
- 1 drop almond extract

## Directions

1. Rub 14 deka butter finely with 16 deka rye flour, add 14 deka finely pounded and sieved almonds with shell, 14 deka flavored sugar, 1 knife point nutmeg, and 1 whole egg
2. Work together into a dough and let it rest covered in a very cold place for about 1.5 hours
3. Roll out the dough knife-back thick on a floured board and cut out round shapes with a tartlet cutter
4. Place on a butter-brushed baking sheet, brush with beaten egg white, and bake quickly in the oven
5. Remove from the sheet with a knife and cool on a board or sieve
6. Meanwhile, cook 14 deka sugar with the necessary water until it spins threads
7. Mix this syrup with 8 deka blanched almonds cut into sticks, equal pistachios, finely cut citron, and 1 drop almond extract
8. Top each tartlet with 1 teaspoon of this mixture and let dry
