## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Salt
- Beer vinegar
- Flour
- Butter
- Sugar
- Pepper
- Nutmeg flower
- Basil
- Pork cutlets or other roasted meat

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut them into slices, and pickle them with salt for a couple of hours; then strain off the much extracted water, and put them with good beer vinegar over the fire.
2. When they are almost cooked enough, roast a couple of spoonfuls of flour in butter in a casserole, stir it smooth with the vinegar from the cooked cucumbers, break the sourness with sugar, season with pepper, nutmeg flower and basil, add the cucumbers, and let them braise.
3. On meat days you can serve them with pork cutlets or other roasted meat; on fast days with omelets and other egg dishes.
