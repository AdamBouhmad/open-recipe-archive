## Ingredients

- 18 dkg flour
- 18 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 1 spoon vinegar
- some water
- egg white for brushing
- lingonberries for filling

## Directions

1. Make a butter dough from the flour, butter, egg, salt, vinegar and water (see doughs section), roll out not too thin
2. Cut out 2 and 2 leaves
3. Place one leaf on an ungreased baking sheet and brush the edge with egg white
4. Cut out the second leaf with a smaller cutter and place the ring on the first leaf
5. Just before serving, brush the pastries on top with beaten egg, bake until light brown over very high heat
6. Press in the middle and fill with lingonberries
