## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- flour
- tomatoes
- broth
- onion
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and cut into usual pieces
2. Braise with butter
3. Dust with a little flour
4. Add strained tomatoes that have been previously well cooked with broth and a whole onion
5. Let the chickens cook until soft enough
6. At the end add one spoonful of good cream
7. Arrange nicely on a dish and pour the sauce over
