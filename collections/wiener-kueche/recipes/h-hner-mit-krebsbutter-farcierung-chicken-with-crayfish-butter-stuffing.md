## Ingredients

- 2 chicken livers, roasted beforehand in butter
- 10 pieces shelled crayfish tails and claws, finely cut
- 12 tablespoons crayfish butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 pieces mushrooms or morels, braised in butter
- 8 tablespoons dried figs, soaked in water, well pressed out
- Salt
- Spices

## Directions

1. Finely cut the roasted chicken livers with the shelled crayfish tails and claws.
2. Beat the crayfish butter until fluffy and gradually add the whole eggs and egg yolk.
3. Mix in the cut livers and crayfish, the braised mushrooms or morels, and the soaked figs, season to taste with salt and spices.
4. Mix everything well together, fill the chickens with this mixture, and prepare further as described for filled roasted chickens.
