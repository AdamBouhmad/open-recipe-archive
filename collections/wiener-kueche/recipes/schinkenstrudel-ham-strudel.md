## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- butter
- lean ham
- 2 whole eggs
- half seidel milk cream (Milchrahm)

## Directions

1. Make an ordinary strudel dough with some butter added to make it tender
2. Let it rest a while, covered with a warm cloth, after it is well worked
3. Meanwhile, cut a piece of lean ham into small pieces
4. Draw out the dough finely, brush it with melted butter
5. Beat 2 whole eggs in half seidel milk cream well together, pour this over the dough
6. Spread it evenly on all sides, sprinkle the ham on it
7. Roll the strudel loosely together, place it in a butter-brushed ring mold, brush it with melted butter, and bake it beautifully
