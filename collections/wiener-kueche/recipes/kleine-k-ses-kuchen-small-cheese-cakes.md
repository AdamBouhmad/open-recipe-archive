## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk
- 8 deka butter
- 16 deka flour
- 3 whole eggs
- 12 deka grated Parmesan cheese
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter milk and 8 deka butter in a saucepan and bring to a boil together
2. Mix with 16 deka flour
3. Stir over heat until the dough releases from the spoon
4. Transfer to a deep bowl and stir until cold
5. Gradually mix in 3 whole eggs, 12 deka grated Parmesan cheese, and some salt
6. Place walnut-sized pieces on a butter-greased sheet at finger-width distance
7. Brush with beaten eggs
8. Bake at moderate heat in the oven
9. Serve sprinkled with grated Parmesan cheese with tea or as an appetizer
