## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of sugar
- 9 measures of water
- 12 measures of brandy
- 1 pound of crushed bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Dissolve 4 pounds of sugar in 9 measures of water
2. Add 12 measures of brandy and 1 pound of crushed bitter almonds
3. Let the mixture stand for some time in the sun
4. Filter it
