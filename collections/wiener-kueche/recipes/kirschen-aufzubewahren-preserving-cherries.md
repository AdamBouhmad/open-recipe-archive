## Ingredients

- black, sour cherries
- bottles with large openings
- cork stoppers
- resin

## Directions

1. Pick black, sour cherries when fully ripe on clear sunny days, not shortly after rain
2. Pick with gloved hands
3. Cut the stems very close to the cherries with scissors
4. Place them carefully into very well-dried bottles with large openings
5. Cork them well and seal with resin so no air can enter
6. Set in a cellar; they can be kept fresh until after Christmas, especially if put up late
7. Alternatively, after placing cherries in bottles and stopping them with a well-fitting lid bound tightly with a bladder, hang them in a well, where they will also keep very well
