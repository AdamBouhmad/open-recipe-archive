## Ingredients

- veal breast
- 8 pieces smallest mushroom variety
- 8 pieces ham comb
- veal sweetbreads cut into neat pieces
- truffle pieces, formed
- beef glace or brown stock (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as described for braised veal breast.
2. Add the mushroom pieces, ham comb pieces, and prepared sweetbreads to the thickened sauce.
3. Stir well, bring to a boil once, and serve with the arranged veal breast.
4. If desired, give the sauce a brown color by adding dissolved beef glace or brown stock.
5. Formed truffle pieces may also be added as desired.
