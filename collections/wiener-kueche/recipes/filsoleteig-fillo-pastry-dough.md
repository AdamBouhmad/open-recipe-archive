## Ingredients

- 56 Deca fine flour
- 28 Deca cold, firm butter
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/8 liter milk cream
- 3 tablespoons white wine or juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Spread 56 Deca fine flour on a board.
2. Cut 28 Deca cold, firm butter into small pieces with a knife and rub them finely with the flour.
3. Make a depression in the middle into which add 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 3 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, 1/8 liter of milk cream and 3 tablespoons of white wine or the juice of 1 lemon.
4. Mix together with a knife, work the mass into a smooth dough, place on a flour-dusted tray, cover with cloth and let rest at a cold place for 1 hour.
5. After this time, roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board, fold together and repeat as indicated for 'Butterteig feinster Art' (No. 1045).
