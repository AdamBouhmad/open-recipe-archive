## Ingredients

- 50 deca flour
- 28 deca butter
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- finely cut lemon peel as desired
- raisins
- currants
- butter for greasing
- flour for lining the mold

## Directions

1. Place 50 deca flour and 28 deca butter cut into small pieces on a board and rub together finely
2. Place this in a deep bowl with 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 6 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, 3 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk and finely cut lemon peel as desired
3. Mix well together and beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
4. Mix in raisins and currants
5. Proceed as described for the Ordinary Gugelhupf
