## Ingredients

- fresh cream as needed
- one spoonful of double-distilled orange blossom water
- finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Place fresh cream in an earthenware pot
2. Add one spoonful of double-distilled orange blossom water and finely ground sugar
3. Whip it to foam
4. Using a skimming spoon, place the foam on a hair sieve set over a soup pot to catch dripping cream
5. Pour the dripped cream back into the pot
6. Repeat until it becomes entirely foam, stirring together each time fresh cream is added
7. Arrange pyramidally on the dish
8. Set it in a cool place to keep it fresh until serving
9. Garnish the dish with meringues
