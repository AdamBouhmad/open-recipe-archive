## Ingredients

- Ripe melons
- Powdered sugar (1/2 kilo sugar per 1 kilo melon)

## Directions

1. From ripe melons remove the watery parts and the skin, cut the rest into pieces
2. Put these with the powdered sugar in a cassole, where they are allowed to stew gently covered
3. Then quickly passed through a sieve and filled into preserve jars
4. These are bound when the jam has set and cooked in steam for 3/4 hours like the nutmeg pears
