## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds good oats
- 1 good handful chicory roots cut small
- 12 measures water
- 1 loth purified saltpeter
- 12 loth sugar or pure honey

## Directions

1. Take 1.5 pounds of good oats and clean them well
2. Wash several times with spring water in a sieve
3. Take 1 good handful of chicory roots cut small
4. Place the roots and oats in a clean pot
5. Pour 12 measures of water over
6. Cook until reduced to half
7. Strain the remaining 6 measures through a clean cloth
8. Add 1 loth purified saltpeter and 12 loth sugar
9. Allow to boil together over heat
10. Let cool in the same covered vessel for 24 hours in the cellar until completely cold
11. Clarify from the thick matter that has settled on the bottom into clean earthen vessels
12. Store for drinking
