## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter boiling milk
- 2 deca orange blossoms
- 1 liter boiling milk
- finely cut bread slices
- 1/4 liter cold milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/3 liter boiling milk
- 8 deca sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 liter with orange blossoms

## Directions

1. Into 1/4 boiling milk put 2 deca orange blossoms and let stand covered and warm for about 1 hour
2. Pour 1 liter boiling milk over finely cut bread slices in the soup pot and cover
3. Beat 8 egg yolks in 1/4 liter cold milk; in 1/3 liter boiling milk put 8 deca sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, then mix in the 1 liter milk with the 8 egg yolks
4. Add the 1/4 liter with orange blossoms, stir everything together well and pour into the milk with bread slices. This yields 2 liters of soup.
