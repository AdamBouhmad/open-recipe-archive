## Ingredients

- Muscatel apples
- sugar
- 2 tablespoons marmalade
- black or wheat bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Peeled and sliced Muscatel apples are put into a saucepan with the necessary sugar and covered, stewed until soft, then passed through a sieve into another saucepan, stirred well with 2 tablespoons marmalade, brought to a boil and then set aside. Now cut from black or wheat bread a slice as thick as a finger's width, which is adapted to the size of the short saucepan to be used; furthermore 2 fingers wide and 2 fingers long strips are cut, which are dipped with the previously cut bread slice in hot butter. Then cover the bottom of a buttered short saucepan with the bread slice, line the side wall with the 2 finger wide bread strips so that one lies close beside the other. Then put the apple mass indicated above on top, cover it with a bread slice and then bake in the oven at low heat for 1 hour. Turn the mold onto a dish, sprinkle the baked food with finely pounded sugar, or cover it with dissolved marmalade, or decorate it as desired with white apple puree. The preparation of this dish requires great care, as it can easily fall apart through a small oversight when turning out.
