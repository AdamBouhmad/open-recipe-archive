## Ingredients

- ¼ l Milk
- 35 dkg Semolina
- 1 Egg
- 5 dkg Raisins
- Salt
- 6 dkg Butter or fat
- Sugar
- Compote or plum roast

## Directions

1. Let semolina soak in milk for 1 hour in a pot
2. Mix in egg, raisins, and salt
3. Heat butter or fat in a flat pan
4. Pour in mixture and place in oven for 45 minutes
5. Pierce with a wooden spoon and let cook, stirring frequently
6. Should be grainy yet soft
7. Arrange on a round platter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve with compote or plum roast
8. Alternative method: bring milk to boil, add semolina by the spoonful, add salt, sugar, and butter or fat, pour into flat pan and proceed as above
