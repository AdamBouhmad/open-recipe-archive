## Ingredients

- 16 Deka roasted Mocha coffee
- 1/4 liter boiling cream
- 24 Deka fine sugar
- 4 Deka isinglass with 1/2 liter water, reduced to 1/2 liter
- 1/2 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. Roast 16 Deka Mocha coffee until golden brown, add it to 1/4 liter boiling cream, cover and let it cool, then strain it through cloth and add 24 Deka fine sugar
2. If you have previously prepared 4 Deka isinglass with 1/2 liter water and reduced it to 1/2 liter, mix this with the strained mixture, set it on ice and stir this mass until it begins to thicken, then mix in 1/2 liter whipped cream, fill a mold and freeze it in ice
