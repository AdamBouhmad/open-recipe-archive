## Ingredients

- Black game, cleaned
- Bacon
- Truffles
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Parsley
- Dried herbs
- Butter
- Paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Cut beautiful fillets from cleaned black game as desired
2. Spike the fillets with bacon and truffles
3. Place them in a marinade made of vinegar, wine, salt, pepper, many onions, parsley, and dried herbs
4. Add a piece of butter and let it simmer gently in a casserole on low charcoal fire until the fillets have absorbed the flavor of the marinade
5. Thread the fillets onto a spit, wrap them in paper, and roast them slowly
6. Baste them with butter and marinade
7. Make a sauce piquante from the marinade
8. Arrange the fillets beautifully on a plate, glaze them, and pour the sauce over top
