## Ingredients

- Thrushes
- Binding thread
- Bacon strips
- Thin spit
- Large spit
- Bacon
- Bread slices
- Semolina crumbs
- Mushroom or onion puree
- Bread slices for garnish

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe, clean and draw the thrushes
2. Dress with binding thread and layer with inserted bacon strips, bind and thread on thin spit, then bind to large spit
3. Roast quickly over strong fire for 6-8 minutes
4. Alternatively, line a casserole with bacon and bread slices, lay the drawn thrushes on top and roast at strong heat for 15 minutes
5. Garnish with bread slices and bacon
6. Serve with puree, especially mushroom or onion puree, with bread slices interspersed
7. Prepare the sauce of thrushes as partridge sauce, Nr. 753
