## Ingredients

- Young chickens cut into pieces like for a fricassee
- Melted butter
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bunch of fine herbs
- Nutmeg
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Puff pastry
- Veal farce
- Wine
- Bacon slices
- Egg yolk
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place young chickens cut into pieces like for a fricassee on a clean cloth and well-washed
2. Braise them with a large piece of melted butter, onions, salt, pepper, a bunch of fine herbs, and nutmeg in a casserole
3. Dust 2 spoonfuls of flour into it
4. Shake it well together
5. Pour in about as much bouillon as needed
6. Let this sauce cook down until it becomes thick
7. Set it aside with the chickens to cool
8. Arrange the chickens with balls formed from veal farce on the puff pastry base laid on a dish, quite neatly
9. Sprinkle the chickens with some wine
10. Lastly lay bacon slices on top
11. Make a lid from pastry over it
12. Brush the pastry before baking
13. Once it is already yellowish, cut out the lid
14. Bind the remaining sauce with egg yolk
15. Add lemon juice
16. Finally pour it hot over the middle and pass it through a sieve
