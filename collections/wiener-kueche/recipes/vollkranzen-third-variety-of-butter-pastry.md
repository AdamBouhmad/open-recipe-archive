## Ingredients

- Butter pastry of finest type, made from 28 deca flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Whipped cream or cold cream for filling

## Directions

1. Roll the dough strongly knife-back-thick on a flour-dusted board
2. Cut into strips 2 fingers wide and 2 fingers long
3. Spirally wind each strip individually on 3-finger-thick round wooden sticks or round lead forms
4. Brush with beaten eggs
5. Place 3 fingers apart on a water-dampened baking sheet and bake slowly at gentle heat in the oven
6. Once done, carefully remove the sticks and fill the Moulades after complete cooling, by means of a paper piping bag or a coffee spoon, with whipped cream or cold cream
