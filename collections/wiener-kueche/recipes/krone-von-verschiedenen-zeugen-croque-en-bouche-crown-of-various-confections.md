## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram sweet chestnuts
- cold water

## Directions

1. Make slits (chamfer) in 1 kilogram sweet chestnuts
2. Place in a casserole in enough cold water to cover them
3. Cook about 1/2 hour until the interior of the chestnuts is soft and mealy
4. After the water is drained, remove with a spoon
