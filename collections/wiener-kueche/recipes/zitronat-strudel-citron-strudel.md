## Ingredients

- 34 deca fine citron peel, cut into thin strips
- 8 deca pistachios
- 28 deca blanched almonds, cut into thin sticks
- 32 deca finely ground sugar
- finely cut lemon peel
- juice of 3 lemons
- Strudel dough
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Place the citron peel, pistachios, blanched almonds cut into thin sticks, finely ground sugar and finely cut lemon peel in a deep bowl
2. Mix well with the juice of 3 lemons and let stand for some time
3. Brush the stretched strudel dough with melted butter
4. Sprinkle with the mixture in the bowl
5. Roll lightly together
6. Treat further as for Ham Strudel (No. 1229)
