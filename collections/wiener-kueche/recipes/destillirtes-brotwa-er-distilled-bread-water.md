## Ingredients

- 2 pounds homemade bread
- 6 to 7 measures good spring water
- 2 pieces lemon peel about two fingers long

## Directions

1. Take 2 pounds of homemade bread and wrap it in cloth
2. Place it in a distilling flask
3. Pour 6 to 7 measures of good spring water over it
4. Add about 2 pieces of lemon peel about two fingers long
5. Distill it
