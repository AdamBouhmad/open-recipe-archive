## Ingredients

- 20 dkg potatoes
- 4 dkg butter
- salt
- 1 egg
- flour
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Make a potato dough from the potatoes, butter, salt, egg and flour (see doughs section)
2. Roll out thin
3. Cut out with a small round cutter
4. Bake in hot lard, drain
5. Do not fry many at once
