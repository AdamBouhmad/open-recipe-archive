## Ingredients

- flour
- pressed yeast
- milk
- melted beef tallow
- salt
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- fresh finely cut and salted cabbage
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough in the same way as for Böhmische Halfen (Nr. 1273), only without sugar
2. When finished baking, lay 2 together and fill instead of with powdered sugar with the following cabbage filling
3. Braise the fresh finely cut and salted cabbage in fat, season to taste with sugar and pepper and baste frequently with cream while braising
4. When the cabbage is completely tender, use it warm for filling
