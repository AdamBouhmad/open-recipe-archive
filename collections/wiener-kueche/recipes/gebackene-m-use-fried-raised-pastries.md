## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 5 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- 1½ dkg yeast
- a few tablespoons lukewarm milk
- hot lard for frying
- sugar for sprinkling or grated chocolate for coating

## Directions

1. Warm 25 dkg flour with salt and 2 tablespoons sugar
2. Rub in 5 dkg butter
3. Add 1 egg, the activated yeast, and a few tablespoons of lukewarm milk
4. Beat the dough until it leaves the spoon
5. Cover and place near the stove to rise
6. When nearly doubled in volume, use a spoon dipped in hot lard to drop dough into plenty of hot lard
7. Fry until light brown on both sides
8. Drain and serve either sprinkled with sugar or rolled in grated chocolate
