## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- Butter
- Roux
- Cream
- Egg yolks
- Cold milk
- Parmesan cheese
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower by cooking it soft in salt water as in the previous recipe
2. Make a light roux, thin it with cream, let it reduce somewhat on the fire, and stir in egg yolks mixed with a little cold milk
3. Butter a porcelain dish and place it on a trivet
4. Arrange the cauliflower heaped on the dish, pour the prepared sauce over it, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, pour melted butter over it spoonful by spoonful, and bake in the oven until golden
