## Ingredients

- 1 kilo cooked stockfish
- Browned onion
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- 2 cloves crushed garlic
- 6 tablespoons oil

## Directions

1. Remove the cooked stockfish from the bones and place the meat, still warm from the broth, in a casserole.
2. Add browned onion, finely chopped green parsley and 2 cloves of crushed garlic.
3. Pour 6 tablespoons of oil over it and shake the casserole over the fire until the fish inside is very hot.
