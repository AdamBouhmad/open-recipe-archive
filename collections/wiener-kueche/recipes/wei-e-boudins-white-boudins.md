## Ingredients

- 2 pounds pork fat, finely worked in a mortar or grindstone
- onions, softened in butter
- white bread, soaked in milk and squeezed out
- poultry meat, finely chopped and ground in a grindstone, equal amount to the fat
- three-quarter measure sweet cream
- six to seven egg yolks
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Soften the onions in butter as described above
2. Finely work the 2 pounds pork fat in a mortar or grindstone
3. Mix the worked fat with the milk-soaked and squeezed white bread, and combine with the softened onions
4. Take as much onion as fat was used
5. Stir in the equal amount of finely chopped and ground poultry meat and three-quarter measure sweet cream
6. Mix in six to seven egg yolks, salt, and spices until well combined
7. Fill into thoroughly cleaned and washed pork intestines, tying both ends securely
8. Ensure no air remains in the intestines, otherwise the boudins will burst
9. Cook in water and milk using the aforementioned method
10. After cooling, place them on paper brushed with butter or fine oil
11. Roast them very slowly on a grill
