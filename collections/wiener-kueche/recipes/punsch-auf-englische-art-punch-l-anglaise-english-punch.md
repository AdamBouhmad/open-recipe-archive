## Ingredients

- Half a lemon
- Three-quarter pound sugar
- 5 lemons
- 3 seidels boiling water
- 1 seidel rum
- 1 seidel genuine fruit brandy

## Directions

1. Peel half a lemon as finely as possible.
2. Cut the peel into small pieces and put it in a soup pot with three-quarter pound sugar.
3. Press the juice of 5 lemons into the pot.
4. Pour in first 3 seidels of boiling water, then 1 seidel of rum and the same amount of genuine fruit brandy.
5. Set the pot for several minutes on hot ashes or over a boiling water kettle.
6. Serve as usual.
