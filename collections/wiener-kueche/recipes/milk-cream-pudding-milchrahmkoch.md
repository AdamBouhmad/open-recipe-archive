## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound fresh good butter
- five whole eggs
- five egg yolks
- one roll soaked in milk
- sugar
- one seidel sweet milk cream
- lemon peel, finely cut

## Directions

1. In a mixing bowl, beat one-quarter pound fresh good butter until fluffy
2. Beat in five whole eggs and five egg yolks
3. Crumble a roll that has been soaked in and pressed out of milk under it
4. Sweeten it
5. Coat a dish with butter
6. Pour in one seidel sweet milk cream and finely cut lemon peel
7. Arrange everything in, apply heat top and bottom, and bake it beautifully in a cake pan
