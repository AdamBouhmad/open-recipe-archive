## Ingredients

- 16 deca powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 25 deca baked, grated graham bread
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- jam of choice
- glaze

## Directions

1. Stir 16 deca powdered sugar and 8 egg yolks together for 1/2 hour
2. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten snow (egg whites) from 8 egg whites together with 25 deca baked, grated graham bread
3. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Bake for 1 hour in a not too hot oven
5. Divide the baked and completely cooled cake into two layers
6. Spread one layer with jam of choice
7. Top with the second layer
8. Cover the assembled cake with glaze of choice, which is necessary as the cake would otherwise be too dry
