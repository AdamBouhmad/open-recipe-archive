## Ingredients

- 16 Deca butter, beaten
- 16 Deca powdered sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 teaspoon rum
- 16 Deca flour
- 1 egg white, beaten
- finely sifted sugar
- finely chopped citron
- raisins
- currants
- blanched almonds, cut into sticks
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Mix 16 Deca beaten butter gradually with 16 Deca powdered sugar, 4 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
2. Stir for 1 hour and then mix well with 1 teaspoon rum and 16 Deca flour
3. Spread knife-back width thick on a butter-brushed baking sheet
4. Cover with the beaten egg white from 1 egg
5. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar
6. Distribute finely chopped citron, raisins, currants, blanched almonds cut into sticks and pistachios over the top
