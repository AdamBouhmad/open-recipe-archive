## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 1 liter Gries (semolina)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 24 deka lard

## Directions

1. Stir 1 liter milk slowly into 1 liter Gries
2. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt
3. Cover and let stand for 2 hours
4. Heat 24 deka lard in a casserole until hot
5. Pour the Gries mixture into it
6. Treat further as described for the Gries Schmarn mit Eier
