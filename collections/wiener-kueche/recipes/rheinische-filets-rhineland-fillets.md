## Ingredients

- 3 cutlets à 25 dkg
- 1 onion
- 2 knife points paprika
- broth or water
- salt
- pepper
- fat for searing

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the meat, wipe, pound, salt and pepper it, lard it, quickly sear on both sides in hot fat
2. In the remaining fat, roast 1 finely chopped onion and 2 knife points of paprika, add the meat, pour in broth or water and braise covered under frequent basting for 2 hours
3. Dust the sauce very lightly and pour unpassed over the meat
