## Ingredients

- approximately 1 handful each of spinach and sorrel leaves
- 1 bunch green parsley and chervil
- some chives and tarragon
- salt water
- butter sauce
- some lemon juice
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- several spoonfuls wine

## Directions

1. Put spinach, sorrel leaves, green parsley, chervil, chives, and tarragon in boiling salted water without covering it so it retains its color
2. Put it in cold water and let this drain
3. Press the cooked greens strongly and cut finely
4. Put it in a butter sauce with some lemon juice
5. Pour in 1 1/2 liters broth and several spoonfuls wine
6. Let it boil thoroughly and pass it
