## Ingredients

- 14 ounces butter, beaten
- 24 ounces powdered sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 28 ounces almonds, finely ground with shell and then passed through sieve
- cinnamon, nutmeg, fine-cut lemon peel, spice mixture to taste
- 6 egg whites, beaten to snow
- 4 tablets grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat 14 ounces butter with 24 ounces powdered sugar and 10 egg yolks, adding them one by one
2. Add 28 ounces finely ground almonds, then add cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon peel, and spices
3. Mix well and gently fold in the snow from 6 egg whites simultaneously with 4 tablets grated chocolate
4. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
5. Cover and steam for 1 hour
6. Serve with chocolate or cream of choice
