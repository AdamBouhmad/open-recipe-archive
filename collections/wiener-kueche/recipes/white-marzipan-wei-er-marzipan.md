## Ingredients

- marzipan dough
- sugar

## Directions

1. Work the marzipan dough well with sugar
2. Stretch it to the thickness of a knife back
3. Cut various figures from it with the pastry wheel: hearts, stars, rosettes, etc.
4. Surround these figures with a finger-width marzipan border and pinch it with a crimping iron so it looks curly
5. Let the marzipan dry well for 8 days
6. Then bake in a weakly heated oven until light yellow
