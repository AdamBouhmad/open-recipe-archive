## Ingredients

- crayfish pudding mass
- green peas, cooked
- calf's liver, cooked and cut into pieces
- salt

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as in the description of the 'Crayfish Pudding' (No. 1383), only here the sugar is replaced with cooked green peas and cooked calf's liver cut into desired pieces and the necessary salt
2. This pudding is garnished with small ragout and served immediately
