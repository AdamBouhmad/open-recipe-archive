## Ingredients

- 20 dkg fine sugar
- 4 spoons rum
- 1/2 egg white
- 1-2 spoons hot water
- a few drops Breton

## Directions

1. Stir 20 dkg fine sugar, 4 spoons rum, 1/2 egg white, 1-2 spoons hot water and a few drops of Breton until well combined
