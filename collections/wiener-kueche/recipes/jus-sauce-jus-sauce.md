## Ingredients

- 8 ounces marrow
- 1 onion studded with spices
- 1/2 bay leaf
- savory and saffron
- some veal meat
- a little ham
- roe and liver of a hen if available
- 1/2 liter broth
- good wine
- beaten egg white

## Directions

1. Put 8 ounces marrow, a spice-studded onion, 1/2 bay leaf, savory, saffron, some veal meat, and a little ham into a saucepan, along with roe and liver of a hen if available
2. Let it roast; when light brown, pour in 1/2 liter broth and some good wine
3. Let it simmer slowly for 1 hour
4. When the sauce is very clear, add some beaten egg white
5. Let it simmer slowly for another 1/2 hour
6. Pass the whole thing through a sieve
7. Serve with various dishes
