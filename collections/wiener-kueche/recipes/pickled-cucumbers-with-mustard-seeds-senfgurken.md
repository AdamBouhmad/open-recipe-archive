## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- bitter mustard seeds
- sweet mustard seeds
- vinegar (see No. 1901)

## Directions

1. Remove the cores carefully and cover the cucumbers in an earthenware vessel overnight.
2. The next day, lay them on a hair sieve or cloth so they rest evenly and water can drain well.
3. Pack them very tightly in half-liter jars or other suitable containers, placing the cucumbers upright lengthwise.
4. Add 1 teaspoon of bitter mustard seeds and 1 teaspoon of sweet mustard seeds to each jar.
5. Fill with vinegar (see No. 1901), seal with beeswax, and let them steam for half an hour.
6. These cucumbers are superior to so-called salt cucumbers both in flavor and digestibility.
7. Peeled and pierced cucumbers can be treated the same way, but larger jars must be used accordingly and they must steam a quarter hour longer.
