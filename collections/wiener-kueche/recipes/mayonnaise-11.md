## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1½ dl oil
- ½ lemon
- mustard
- salt
- white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. In a porcelain bowl, beat 3 egg yolks well with a whisk.
2. Stir in the oil thread by thread until the mayonnaise is thick.
3. Mix in the lemon juice thread by thread.
4. Add mustard, salt, and optional white pepper.
5. Keep the mayonnaise on ice until use.
6. To fix curdled mayonnaise: beat 1 egg yolk until foamy in another bowl, then whisk in the curdled mayonnaise thread by thread with constant stirring.
