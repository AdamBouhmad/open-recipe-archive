## Ingredients

- grated nuts
- milk
- 3 dkg sugar
- lemon zest
- grated chocolate
- 3 spoons honey

## Directions

1. Cook the grated nuts with milk, 3 dkg sugar, lemon zest, grated chocolate and 3 spoons honey for 15-20 minutes, stirring frequently
2. Cool and serve
