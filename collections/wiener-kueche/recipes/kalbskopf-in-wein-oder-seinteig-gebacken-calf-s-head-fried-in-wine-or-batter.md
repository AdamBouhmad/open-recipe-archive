## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Salt water
- Wine batter or egg batter
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Preparation is the same as for fried calf's head, with the difference that instead of flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs, the tender-cooked pieces are dipped in wine batter or egg batter
2. Fry in hot fat
