## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls washed spinach
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound the washed spinach very finely in a mortar
2. Strain the pounded mixture
3. Pour the strained juice into a small saucepan and leave it on the fire until it begins to reduce and becomes thick
4. Pour the whole mixture onto a sieve so the liquid drains off quickly
5. The remaining thick mass serves as the coloring agent for butter, Mayonnaise, soup, sauces, vegetables, and the like
