## Ingredients

- 5 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 5 dkg sugar
- 1 dkg breadcrumbs
- 1 clove
- 1 nutmeg seed
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 4 egg whites
- 8 dkg raw grated almonds
- red wine
- 3 cloves
- 1 piece cinnamon bark
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 5 dkg butter until creamy
2. Stir in 4 egg yolks, 5 dkg sugar, 1 dkg breadcrumbs, 1 crushed clove, 1 nutmeg seed, finely chopped lemon zest, and 5 dkg grated chocolate
3. Fold in 4 egg whites and 8 dkg raw grated almonds
4. Pour into a pudding mold and cook in a water bath for 1 hour
5. Turn out onto a platter, pour mulled wine over it, and serve warm
6. For the mulled wine: boil red wine with 3 cloves, 1 piece of cinnamon bark, and sugar to taste, then strain
