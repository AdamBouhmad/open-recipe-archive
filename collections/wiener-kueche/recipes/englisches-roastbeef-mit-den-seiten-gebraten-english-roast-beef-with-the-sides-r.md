## Ingredients

- A well-aged piece of beef from the rib, 3 kilos, with the backbone removed but ribs left on
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tendons and top fat (3 finger widths from the thicker side) removed

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as described in the previous recipe, removing the backbone but keeping the ribs, and removing the tendons and top fat (3 finger widths from the thicker side only, not from the thinner side).
2. Pound the meat, salt it, sprinkle with some pepper, and proceed as described in the previous recipe.
