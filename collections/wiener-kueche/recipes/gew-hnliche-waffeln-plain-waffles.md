## Ingredients

- 16 deca finely pounded sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 16 deca flour
- unsalted bacon

## Directions

1. Put 16 deca finely pounded sugar into a deep bowl, stir 8 egg yolks one after another into it and, after it has been stirred for 1 1/2 hours, fold in the snow from 8 egg whites together with 16 deca flour lightly.
2. Coat a fork with unsalted bacon and brush both parts of the hot waffle iron with it, fill it with 1 spoon of the above batter, close it well and bake the waffle as described for 'Waffles from Batter' (No. 1648).
