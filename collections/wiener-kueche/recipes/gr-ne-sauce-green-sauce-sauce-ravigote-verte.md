## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons finely cut sorrel
- equal amount green parsley
- tarragon
- sour sorrel
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter broth
- some cream

## Directions

1. Pound finely the sorrel, green parsley, tarragon, and sour sorrel with 6 tablespoons butter in a mortar
2. Add 6 tablespoons flour and mix well
3. Put this in a saucepan and let it boil somewhat
4. Pour in 1/2 liter broth and some cream
5. Let it boil again
