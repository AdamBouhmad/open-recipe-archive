## Ingredients

- 120-140 dkg pork loin or young pork rib
- caraway seeds
- salt
- fat
- water or soup

## Directions

1. Wash the meat
2. Rub both sides with caraway seeds and salt
3. Place in a roasting pan with the good side down
4. Add a piece of fat and some water on top
5. Roast in the oven for 2-2½ hours, basting frequently with hot water or soup
6. Turn when brown
7. Remove, slice, then chop through
8. Serve on a long platter with the degreased pan juices
