## Ingredients

- Vienna Torte dough (Recipe 1502)
- Butter
- Flour
- Icing
- Spanish almond paste

## Directions

1. Fill buttered and floured torte form or flat saucepan with dough
2. Bake at moderate heat until pale golden
3. Turn baked cake onto board
4. Cut out decorative figures with desired cutters
5. Cover with icing
6. When dry, decorate as desired with Spanish almond paste
