## Ingredients

- Beautiful yellow endive
- Salt water
- Butter
- Milk cream, 4 to 5 spoons
- Fine bread crumbs
- Veal carbonades

## Directions

1. Remove the green leaves from beautiful yellow endive, wash it (divided into 4 parts), rinsing several times
2. Cook it in salt water so it does not become too soft, then drain
3. Coat a dish with butter, spread 4 to 5 spoons of cream over it
4. Arrange a layer of salad neatly over it, then more cream and butter, then salad again, etc., until full
5. Top with cream and several butter pieces, sprinkle with fine bread crumbs
6. Apply heat above and below and let it cook well
7. Garnish with veal carbonades
