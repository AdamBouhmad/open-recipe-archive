## Ingredients

- capon
- butter, two lots
- breadcrumbs, one handful
- oysters, twenty
- salt
- nutmeg
- more butter and flour for the sauce
- good broth
- lemon juice
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Warm a piece of butter and add breadcrumbs and twenty shelled oysters, a little salt and nutmeg, stirring well together
2. Fill the capon with this mixture and roast it nicely in its juices
3. Make a golden roux from butter and flour
4. Add a spoonful of good broth, the roasting sauce from the capon, twenty oysters, and lemon juice, and let all cook together
5. When serving, mix the sauce with egg yolks
