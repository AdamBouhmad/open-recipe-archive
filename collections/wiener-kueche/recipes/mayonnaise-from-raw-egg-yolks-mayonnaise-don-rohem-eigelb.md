## Ingredients

- 6 raw egg yolks
- 1/4 liter oil in ice
- juice of 1 lemon or 2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt to taste
- 2 knife tips of pepper

## Directions

1. Put 6 raw egg yolks into a deep bowl and stir them on ice
2. Stir in the oil drop by drop gradually along with the lemon juice or vinegar
3. When vinegar and oil are well mixed, add the necessary salt and 2 knife tips of pepper
4. Keep this mayonnaise covered on ice until use
5. Serve with boiled cold chicken, similarly boiled ham, and with fatty fish dishes
