## Ingredients

- same dough as for Schneebällen
- marmalade
- egg wash

## Directions

1. Prepare the same dough as for Schneebällen, roll it out knife-back thick, cut or stamp out square pieces
2. Put some marmalade on the middle of each piece, coat the edges with beaten eggs, and fold the pieces over so that they form a triangle
3. Press the edges together lightly, but in the middle the triangles must be very thin
4. Bake them under frequent shaking of the saucepan in very hot fat and sprinkle with sugar immediately
