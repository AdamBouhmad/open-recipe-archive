## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- cream
- cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped anchovies
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as potatoes with ham, but instead of ham use 2 tablespoons finely chopped anchovies and 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
