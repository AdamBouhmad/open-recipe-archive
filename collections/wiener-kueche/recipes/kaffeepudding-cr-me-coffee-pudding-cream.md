## Ingredients

- ½ liter milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 dag sugar
- 4 spoons strong black coffee
- ¼ liter whipped cream (Schlagobers)

## Directions

1. Beat the milk, egg yolks, sugar, and coffee over a water bath until thick
2. Cool and fold in the whipped cream
