## Ingredients

- good sauerkraut
- butter or roasting fat
- flour
- meat broth
- onion
- pheasant

## Directions

1. Take good sauerkraut, press out the water, and braise it well in butter or roasting fat.
2. Dust it lightly with flour and add meat broth and finely cut onion, and let it cook well together.
3. Roast one or more pheasants completely in their juices.
4. Add them to the sauerkraut with the roasting fat, let it come to a good boil, plate it, and serve together with the sauerkraut.
