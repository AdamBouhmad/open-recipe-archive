## Ingredients

- One measure of wine
- Almost the same amount of water
- Six loth of freshly baked rye bread

## Directions

1. Mix one measure of wine and almost the same amount of water
2. Throw six loth of freshly baked rye bread into it
3. In summer, this vinegar placed in the sun can be good for use in three days
4. In winter, at oven warmth, it can be good for use in eight days
