## Ingredients

- 16 Deka blanched almonds
- 1 egg white for grinding almonds
- 32 Deka beaten butter
- 24 Deka caster sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 32 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Grind blanched almonds very finely with egg white
2. Mix well with beaten butter
3. Add caster sugar and egg yolks one by one
4. Stir together for 1/2 hour
5. Mix in flour
6. Fill into a butter-greased and flour-dusted torte form
7. Bake for 3/4 to 1 hour
