## Ingredients

- 24 pieces Maschanzger apples
- Water
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar
- Juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Peel 24 pieces of Maschanzger apples, cut them up and remove the seeds
2. Pound them fine in a mortar and let them cook with some water on a charcoal fire
3. Press through a hair sieve and mix the puree with three quarter pound clarified sugar
4. When the mixture is well cooled, press in the juice of 2 lemons, mix well together, and place in the freezing pan
