## Ingredients

- puff pastry, half finger-thick
- egg yolk
- chicken breasts, diced
- béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Roll out puff pastry half a finger thick and cut pastries round
2. Place on a baking sheet not too close together
3. Brush with egg yolk and press a circle in the middle with a cutter
4. Bake nicely
5. Once baked, remove the soft dough from the middle
6. Fill with diced chicken breasts in well-prepared béchamel
7. Serve hot
