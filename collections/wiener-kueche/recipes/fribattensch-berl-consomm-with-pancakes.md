## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- Flour
- 1/2 liter simmering milk
- 10 Deca butter
- 5 egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Make baked fribatts from 4 eggs, cut them into small pieces and cook them in 1/2 liter simmering milk and let them cook through
2. Beat 10 Deca butter until fluffy and beat in 5 egg yolks
3. Add some salt, the prepared fribatts, and if desired some nutmeg and stir everything well together
