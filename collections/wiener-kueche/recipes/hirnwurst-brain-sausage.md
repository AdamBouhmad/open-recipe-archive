## Ingredients

- a couple beef brains
- a couple calf brains
- a quarter Pfund marrow
- two small trimmed rolls
- good milk
- salt
- pepper
- finely cut lemon peel
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- casings

## Directions

1. Wash and strip the membrane from a couple beef and a couple calf brains, and chop them along with a quarter pound of marrow very finely
2. Dip two small trimmed rolls in good milk, squeeze them out, and mix them in
3. Season with salt and pepper as needed and add finely cut lemon peel
4. Beat two whole eggs along with two egg yolks and stir them in well
5. Fill the mixture into casings and tie well
6. Blanch and over-cook them, then roast them on the grate
