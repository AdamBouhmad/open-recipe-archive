## Ingredients

- apples
- marmalade or preserved fruit as desired
- butter dough
- eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the core casing of the apples by pressing a small corer into the side of the stem and inserting a narrow knife into the corer so that it reaches the core casing, twist slightly and pull it out with the casing
2. The apples must not be pierced through, but a knife-back-width thick bottom must remain
3. Peel the apples and fill the empty space created by the coring with desired marmalade or preserved fruit
4. Cut knife-back-width rolled out butter dough into squares that must correspond to the size of the apples
5. Brush all edges of each square with beaten egg, place an apple in the middle of each square with the open side facing up
6. Bend up the 4 corners and fasten them together above the middle of the apple
7. Place the apples in nightgown on a sheet dampened with water and brush the dough with beaten egg so that a knife-back-width border remains dry on each side
8. Place them in the hot oven and bake for 1.5 to 2.5 hours
9. After this time, remove them from the sheet with a very thin knife, arrange them on a plate and sprinkle them heavily with sugar
