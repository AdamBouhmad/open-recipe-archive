## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter cold cream coffee
- 12 deca sugar
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put 3/4 liter cold cream coffee, 12 deca sugar, and 4 egg yolks in a snow kettle
2. Beat over heat with a whisk until the mass begins to thicken
