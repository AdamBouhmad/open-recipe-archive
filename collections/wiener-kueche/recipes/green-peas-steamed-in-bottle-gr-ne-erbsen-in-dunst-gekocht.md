## Ingredients

- Fresh green peas
- salted water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Place fresh green peas in boiling salted water, cook until half-tender, and place in cold water.
2. Fill Champagne bottles or other suitable steam jars halfway with the peas.
3. Pour very lightly salted, steamed, and cooled water over the peas, leaving three fingers' breadth of space at the top.
4. Stopper well and seal with tinfoil and corks.
5. Steam for 1 hour, then store in a cool, airy place (see Preserves Recipe No. 1842).
6. Note: the water poured over the peas in the bottle should be only lightly salted.
7. When using the peas, drain the water, warm them in salted water, and prepare as desired.
8. Small green string beans and asparagus can be preserved the same way, but not in Champagne bottles.
