## Ingredients

- Breast and all good meat from a capon braised in its own juices
- Two bread rolls (Semmeln), trimmed and soaked in milk
- Finely cut lemon zest
- Three to four egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg flower

## Directions

1. Take the breast and all good meat from a capon that has been braised in its own juices and cut very fine with a knife
2. Cook two trimmed bread rolls soaked in milk on the fire while constantly stirring until soft
3. After fine cooking, add the finely cut capon meat, finely cut lemon zest, three to four egg yolks, necessary salt, and nutmeg flower
4. Stir everything well together
5. Fill into intestines of desired size
6. Blanch them in water
7. Then roast them on the grill
