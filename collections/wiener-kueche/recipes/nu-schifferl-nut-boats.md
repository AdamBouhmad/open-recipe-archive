## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 5 dkg sugar
- 14 dkg raw grated almonds
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Make a dough on the board from the flour, butter, sugar, raw grated almonds and 1 egg
2. After resting, form a roll
3. Cut equal-sized pieces from it
4. Press a piece of dough into a mold to form it
5. Fill the mold
6. Cover it with a sheet of dough cut to the shape
7. Place on a baking sheet
8. Bake the mass until light brown
9. After cooling, turn out the mold
10. Decorate the boat with ½ nut
11. Sprinkle with sugar
