## Ingredients

- raw breast of a large hen or poularde
- 8 deca bread rolls, softened in milk
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- some salt
- 8 deca butter

## Directions

1. Remove the raw breast from a large hen or poularde and remove the skin
2. Stir 8 deca bread rolls softened in milk over the fire until they resemble a dough and let cool
3. Pound this mass together with the chicken breast, 1 whole egg, 2 egg yolks, some salt, and 8 deca butter and pass through a sieve
4. Form into quenelles, dumplings, or snail-shaped mounds
5. Using two coffee spoons dipped in hot water, scoop the quenelles into a buttered shallow casserole, not too close together
6. Pour boiling water over them so it covers them
7. Cover and let simmer, then remove with a slotted spoon and serve with brown soup
