## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet, select almonds
- 1 small glass of water
- 4 measures fresh water
- fine pounded sugar to taste
- a little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Wash 1 pound of sweet, selected almonds three times in fresh water
2. Rub them with your hands
3. Pound them in a mortar or grinder, adding a small glass of water, very finely
4. Transfer to another vessel
5. Gradually stir in 4 measures of fresh water
6. Press well through a napkin
7. Sweeten with finely pounded sugar as desired
8. Add a little orange blossom water at the end, which gives this beverage an extremely pleasant taste
