## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 20 deca finely pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 16 deca bread crumbs
- 25 deca currants

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy
2. Gradually add 20 deca finely pounded sugar and 6 egg yolks, stir for 1/2 hour
3. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites at the same time as 16 deca bread crumbs
4. Fill into a form brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. 25 deca picked currants are placed in the dough 4 berries at a time, sinking them into the dough
6. Bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour in the oven
7. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and serve only the following day
