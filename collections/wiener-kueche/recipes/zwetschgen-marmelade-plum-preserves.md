## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram powdered sugar
- 2 kilograms very ripe fresh plums

## Directions

1. Put 1 kilogram of powdered sugar in a saucepan.
2. Quickly peel 2 kilograms of very ripe fresh plums, remove the kernels, and immediately place each plum into the sugar.
3. Place over heat and cook while stirring constantly and rapidly into a thick preserves, being careful that the plums do not lose their beautiful color, which resembles that of apricot preserves.
4. After cooking for 10 to 15 minutes, fill hot into jars like the Apricot Preserves.
5. If desired, you can add 1 teaspoon of vanilla, cinnamon, or clove oil, or a mixture of these, while cooking the preserves.
