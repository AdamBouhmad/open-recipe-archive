## Ingredients

- dressed chickens
- fat pieces
- cold water
- hot water or broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the dressed chickens breast-side down in a pan and cover the top with fat pieces.
2. Pour some cold water beside them.
3. Roast first slowly, then at higher heat for ¾-1 hour, basting frequently with hot water or broth.
4. After 30 minutes, turn the chickens.
5. At the end, let a piece of butter brown in the pan juices.
6. Carve the chickens by first cutting off the wings and legs, then cutting the breast and back into 2-3 pieces.
7. On a warmed platter, place the back pieces first, then the breast pieces on top, and arrange the wings and legs on both sides so the chicken returns to its original shape.
8. Attach paper ruffles to the legs and decorate the chicken with curled parsley.
9. Pour the pan juices alongside the chickens.
