## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar in small pieces
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 oranges
- 1/4 liter rum
- 1/8 liter barley grains
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Prepare like the Gesundheits-Punch mit Reis
2. Instead of rice, boil 1/8 liter rinsed barley grains in 1 liter water for 1/2 hour
3. Strain and add to the punch components
