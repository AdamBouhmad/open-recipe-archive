## Ingredients

- Himmelbrandt tea
- Boiling water
- Sugar
- Milk

## Directions

1. Pour boiling hot water into a pot to warm it, pour this out after a few minutes, and add 1 tablespoon full of Himmelbrandt tea (for 1 cup).
2. Pour it over with a few spoonfuls of boiling hot water and after a few minutes add the remaining necessary water.
3. Strain this tea through a very fine sieve and drink it like coffee with sugar and milk.
