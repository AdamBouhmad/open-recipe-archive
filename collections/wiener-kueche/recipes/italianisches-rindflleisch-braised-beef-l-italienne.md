## Ingredients

- A beef rump of 3 kilos
- Pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of lard dipped in spices
- Lard
- Onion
- Root vegetables
- Spices
- Soup
- Cream
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Beat and fold the beef and insert pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of lard that have been dipped in spices
2. In a casserole, place lard, onion, root vegetables and spices, lay the meat on top, pour soup over it and braise for about 3 hours
3. Transfer the meat to another casserole, strain the defatted sauce over it, pour cream over it, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and braise in the oven until it develops a brown crust
4. Serve with macaroni or other thinned flour preparations or pickled cucumbers (salt or mustard cucumbers) or olive sauce
