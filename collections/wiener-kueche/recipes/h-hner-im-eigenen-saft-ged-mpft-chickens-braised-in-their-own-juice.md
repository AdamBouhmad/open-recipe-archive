## Ingredients

- 2 plucked, salted chickens
- 4 tablespoons butter wrapped with 5 sprigs of parsley
- Bacon slices
- Soup
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. After inserting 4 tablespoons of butter wrapped with parsley sprigs into each chicken, dress them and sprinkle with lemon juice.
2. Line a casserole with bacon slices, place the chickens in it, cover with bacon slices and let them braise covered slowly.
3. Once the chickens' own juice has evaporated, pour some soup over them and braise until they are soft.
4. Remove the chickens, cut the twine, pull it out, cut the chickens into desired pieces, arrange them in a circular pattern in a bowl, pour the defatted cooking juice over them, and place ragout, braised mushrooms, truffles, or braised rice in the center of the bowl.
