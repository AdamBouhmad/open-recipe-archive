## Ingredients

- 5 sprigs tarragon leaves
- 5 sprigs green parsley
- some sorrel
- burnet
- 5 sprigs chervil
- 5 juniper berries
- 4 teaspoons butter
- 1 tablespoon oil
- mayonnaise made from raw egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon French mustard optionally

## Directions

1. Finely cut 5 sprigs of tarragon leaves, 5 sprigs of green parsley, some sorrel, burnet, and 5 sprigs of chervil
2. Pound them finely in a mortar with 5 juniper berries, 4 teaspoons of butter, and 1 tablespoon of oil and pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
3. Mix in the mayonnaise made from raw egg yolks (No. 209) and then serve
4. If desired, 1 tablespoon of French mustard can also be stirred in
