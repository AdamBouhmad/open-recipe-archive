## Ingredients

- Hare
- Butter
- Marinade and cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the head and front legs from the hare, or leave them and position them together, either one over the other or crossed
2. After the hare is skinned and prepared, spit it and roast it like venison, either plain or with marinade and cream
