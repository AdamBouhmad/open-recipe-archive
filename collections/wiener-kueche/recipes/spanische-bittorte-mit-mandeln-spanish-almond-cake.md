## Ingredients

- 48 deca peeled, dried, and finely cut almonds
- 8 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 32 deca vanilla sugar
- powdered sugar
- writing paper
- unsalted bacon

## Directions

1. Prepare as with the preceding recipe, with the difference that 48 deca peeled, well-dried, and thereafter finely cut almonds are mixed into the snow mixture.
2. The mixture is then piped onto the paper strips with the aid of a small spoon.
