## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo pork meat from head, shoulder, or neck
- 1/2 kilo pork liver
- 1/4 kilo bacon
- Meat seasoning
- Pepper
- Salt
- 1 onion, finely minced and roasted
- 6 tablespoons raw, well-rinsed rice, or 3/4 liter barley cooked in water, well drained and cooled
- Cleaned and washed intestines
- Twine

## Directions

1. Boil the meat, liver, and bacon together, then finely chop on a board.
2. Add meat seasoning, pepper, salt, the roasted minced onion, and either the raw rice or the cooked barley.
3. Mix everything together thoroughly.
4. Fill the cleaned and washed intestines with the mixture, tie with twine, and bind at intervals.
5. Cook in boiling salted water for 1/2 hour.
6. If the sausages are to be stored, lay them separately in a cool place.
7. If they are to be fried, place them in hot fat and fry brown on both sides.
