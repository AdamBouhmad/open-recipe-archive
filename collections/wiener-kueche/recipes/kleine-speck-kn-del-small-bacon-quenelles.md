## Ingredients

- 14 Loffel fresh, finely diced bacon
- 24 Loffel grated, finely diced rolls mixed with milk
- 6 Loffel butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- 6 Loffel flour

## Directions

1. Put the fresh, finely diced bacon in a saucepan and heat it until the bacon is glossy
2. Mix the grated, finely diced rolls with milk, then add the bacon over it and mix well
3. Beat 6 Loffel butter until fluffy, stir in 3 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
4. Mix in salt, 6 Loffel flour with the bacon and rolls
5. Let stand covered for 1/2 hour
6. Form knödel of desired size and cook as with the previous knödel
