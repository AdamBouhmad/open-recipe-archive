## Ingredients

- 1 liter boiling water
- 1/2 liter buckwheat flour

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter of boiling water in a fairly large pot
2. Add 1/2 liter buckwheat flour
3. Cook covered for 15 minutes
4. Turn the flour mass with a wooden spoon so the bottom comes to the top
5. Cook covered again for 15 minutes
6. Continue with the treatment described in the recipe for 'Sterz von Haidenmehl'
