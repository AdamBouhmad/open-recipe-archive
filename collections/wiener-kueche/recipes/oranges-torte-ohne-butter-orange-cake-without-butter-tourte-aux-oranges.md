## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams orange sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 30 decagrams peeled almonds
- juice of one lemon
- 6 egg whites
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir 24 decagrams orange sugar with 12 egg yolks for 1 1/4 hours, then add 30 decagrams peeled almonds, finely ground with lemon juice.
2. Stir for another 3/4 hour, then lightly fold in the beaten snow of 6 egg whites.
3. Fill a buttered cake form dusted with flour with this mixture and bake at low heat in the oven for 1 hour, then handle as described for the previous orange cake.
