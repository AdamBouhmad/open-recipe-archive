## Ingredients

- 12 cleaned, singed quails (not gutted)
- Hen breast
- Bacon (6 teaspoons)
- Semolina bread (6 teaspoons, soaked in water and well pressed out)
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- Salt and spices
- Bacon slices
- Butter
- Root vegetables
- Celery
- Cleaned, thinly cut mushrooms
- Soup
- White wine
- Madeira wine
- Truffles (optional)

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the breast and leg bones from 12 quails without gutting them
2. Cut the hen breast with bacon, place in a mortar and pound with soaked pressed bread, 1 whole egg, 2 egg yolks, salt and spices until fine
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Fill the quails with this stuffing and sew closed
5. Wrap with bacon slices
6. Place in a casserole with butter, root vegetables, celery, and mushrooms
7. Cover and roast together
8. Pour some soup and white wine over it
9. Let simmer gently covered until tender
10. Remove the quails and cut off the string
11. Arrange the quails in a circle on a plate
12. Pour prepared salmis sauce over them
13. Place artichoke hearts or truffles in the center of the plate
