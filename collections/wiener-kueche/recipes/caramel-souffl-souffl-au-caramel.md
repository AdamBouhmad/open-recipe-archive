## Ingredients

- 12 drams sugar
- cold water
- 12 drams flour
- 1/2 liter cold cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 16 drams butter
- 16 drams sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Dip 12 drams sugar in water, place in a casserole, and heat without stirring until the sugar is brown.
2. Add 6 tablespoons cold water and simmer until the sugar is completely dissolved and liquid.
3. Set aside and let cool.
4. Pour 1/2 liter cold cream over 12 drams flour and stir.
5. Add 5 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, 16 drams butter, and the cooled, dissolved caramel sugar.
6. Stir over heat until the mixture has thickened considerably.
7. Set aside and let cool.
8. Beat 16 drams sugar with 8 egg yolks for 1/4 hour.
9. Slowly mix this into the thickened, cold mixture.
10. Lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites.
11. Fill a buttered, flour-dusted melon mold.
12. Cover and steam or bake for 3/4 hour in a water bath.
