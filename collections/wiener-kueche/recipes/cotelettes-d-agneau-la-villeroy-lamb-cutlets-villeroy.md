## Ingredients

- Lamb cutlets
- Salt
- Butter, hot
- Fricassee sauce
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard, very hot
- Papillotes for the bones
- Fried potato straws
- Fine vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb cutlets so that the bone is free from meat
2. Tap them with the back of a knife and salt them
3. Put hot butter into a flat casserole, place the cutlets on it, let them rest for one minute, turn them over, and let them rest again until they have a white color
4. Remove them and place on a cloth
5. When the cutlets are completely cool, dip them in fricassee sauce
6. Place them next to each other on a platter and put it on ice until the cutlets are stiff
7. Dip them in beaten eggs, roll them in breadcrumbs, and fry them in very hot fat
8. Arrange them in a circle on a platter, cover the bones with papillotes, arrange fried potato straws around the edge of the platter, and serve with fine vegetables
