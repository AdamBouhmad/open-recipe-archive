## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Champagne
- 6 lemons
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar
- 1 measuring cup water
- Egg whites beaten to snow

## Directions

1. Take 1 bottle Champagne, 6 lemons, three quarter pound clarified sugar, 1 measuring cup water, and egg whites beaten to snow
2. Prepare as for Maraschino ice cream
3. Can also be made in this way from Muscat, Rivalta, Marsala, Madeira and generally from all white liqueur wines
