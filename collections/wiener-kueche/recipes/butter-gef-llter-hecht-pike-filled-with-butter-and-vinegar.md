## Ingredients

- water
- vinegar (or tarragon vinegar)
- onion piece
- parsley root, cut in leaflets
- 5 sprigs thyme
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon caraway (optional if using tarragon vinegar)
- pike

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, put 1 part water and 1 part vinegar (tarragon vinegar if desired), 1 piece onion, some parsley root, all cut in leaflets, 5 sprigs thyme, 1 tablespoon salt, and optionally 1/4 teaspoon caraway (no caraway if using tarragon vinegar)
2. Let this simmer for 1/2 hour
3. Meanwhile, prepare the pike in the fish pan as for boiled pike (No. 894), which can also be cut into 2-finger-wide pieces
4. Pour the simmering broth over it, which should cover the pike and have an agreeably sour taste
5. The pike is otherwise treated as with boiled pike
