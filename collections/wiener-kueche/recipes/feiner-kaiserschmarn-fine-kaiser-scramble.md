## Ingredients

- 14 deka butter
- 12 deka vanilla sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deka flour
- 1/4 liter milk
- 6 egg whites (snow)
- 6 deka raisins
- 6 deka lingonberries
- 6 deka hot butter

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deka butter until fluffy
2. Mix in 12 deka vanilla sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 6 egg yolks one after another
3. Add 16 deka flour and 1/4 liter milk, mixing slowly but simultaneously, always some flour and some milk
4. Gently fold in the snow from 6 egg whites with 6 deka raisins and 6 deka lingonberries
5. Pour this dough into a casserole containing 6 deka hot butter
6. Place in the oven and bake until golden
7. When finished, break up the dough with a small pastry knife
8. Arrange in a bowl, sprinkle with finely ground sugar, and serve
