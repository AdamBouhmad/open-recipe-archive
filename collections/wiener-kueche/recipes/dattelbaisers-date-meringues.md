## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 1 egg white
- 14 dkg chopped nuts
- 14 dkg dates, cut into strips

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 1 egg white until stiff
2. Mix in chopped nuts and dates
3. Using two spoons, form small mounds on buttered sheet
4. Dry rather than bake in the oven
