## Ingredients

- 24 deca chocolate
- 2 liters boiling milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons cold milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- baked bread or bread slices

## Directions

1. Lay 24 deca chocolate on a baking sheet and let soften in the oven, put it in a casserole and gradually pour 2 liters boiling milk into it while stirring constantly
2. Must be very careful so it does not become crumbly. The chocolate can also be grated into the milk
3. The cooked chocolate is then beaten with 6 egg yolks, 3 tablespoons cold milk, sugar as needed and, if desired, some cinnamon
4. Serve with baked bread or bread slices
