## Ingredients

- head of carp or other fish
- flour
- bread crumbs
- 8 deca bread sliced thin
- 1 piece onion
- whole eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the head of carp or other fish into pieces, bread with flour and bread crumbs, fry with 8 deca thinly sliced bread, 1 piece onion and whole eggs in lard
