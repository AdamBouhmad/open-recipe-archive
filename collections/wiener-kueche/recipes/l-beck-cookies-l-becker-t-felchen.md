## Ingredients

- 8 deca beaten butter
- 8 deca muscovado sugar
- 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
- 12 deca flour
- 2 pinches cinnamon
- some nutmeg
- 1 pounded clove

## Directions

1. Mix beaten butter with muscovado sugar, egg yolks, and whole egg gradually
2. Add flour mixed with cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove, mixing well
3. Place covered at a very cool location until dough becomes stiff
4. Roll out dough on flour-dusted board to the thickness of a knife back
5. Cut out in hard or any desired forms and treat as described for the Almond Cakes (No. 1611)
