## Ingredients

- large yellow turnips
- kohlrabi
- salsify (Eckäpfel)
- butter
- finely cut parsley
- flour
- broth
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare large yellow turnips, kohlrabi, and salsify
2. Using a hollow iron, cut small balls from each vegetable
3. Place butter in a casserole and let it warm
4. Add the finely cut green parsley and the vegetables
5. Let them steam a little, but do not let them brown
6. Dust a little flour over them
7. Let them steam a while longer
8. Pour in some broth, but not much, so no sauce is visible
9. Add some sugar
10. Let it steam until the vegetables are tender enough
11. Serve with veal carbonades
