## Ingredients

- One well-washed calf lung
- One spoonful cleaned rice
- Red currants
- Black currants
- Raisins
- Dates
- Figs
- Two loths of each
- Two reinettes apples

## Directions

1. Cook all ingredients together in a sufficient quantity of water for several hours
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Drink one to two cups full every two hours
