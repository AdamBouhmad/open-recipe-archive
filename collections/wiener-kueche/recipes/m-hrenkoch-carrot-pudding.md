## Ingredients

- yellow carrots, clean washed and scraped
- 5 eggs
- butter
- 11 eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- softened bread roll (Semmel), soaked and squeezed out
- three-quarters pound grated sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the clean washed and scraped yellow carrots finely on a grater without first soaking them in water
2. In a casserole, stir them completely dry, then beat in 5 eggs and continue stirring until completely dry throughout
3. Stir them smoothly in a mixing bowl (Weidling), add some butter that has been melted over the fire, as well as 11 eggs, and stir well together
4. Beat in 10 egg yolks, the soaked and squeezed out bread roll, and three-quarters pound grated sugar
5. Stir everything together diligently for a quarter hour
6. Pour the batter into the prepared bowl and ring mold, place it in the oven or torte pan, and let it bake slowly
7. Serve it warm or cold at table, although it is better eaten warm
