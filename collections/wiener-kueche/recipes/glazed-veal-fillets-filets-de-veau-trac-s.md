## Ingredients

- Veal forcemeat
- Flour
- Fat
- Bouillon glace

## Directions

1. When fillets have been cut out from the forcemeat prepared as described above, they are dipped in flour on both sides and fried in a casserole in finger-thick, very hot fat.
2. They are then arranged on a serving dish and the fillets are covered with dissolved bouillon glace.
