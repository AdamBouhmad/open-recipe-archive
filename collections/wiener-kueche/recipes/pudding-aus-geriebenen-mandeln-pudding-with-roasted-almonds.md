## Ingredients

- 12 ounces finely ground sugar
- 16 ounces blanched almonds
- 16 ounces sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 clove
- 8 egg whites, beaten to snow

## Directions

1. In a dry casserole, put 12 ounces finely ground sugar and let it brown
2. Add 16 ounces blanched almonds, roast them in the oven until golden, let cool completely, and grind finely
3. Beat 16 ounces sugar with 8 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
4. Add the ground almonds and stir with lemon juice and 1 clove for 1/2 hour
5. Gently fold in the snow from 8 egg whites
6. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
7. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour
8. Turn out onto a plate and serve with orange cream
