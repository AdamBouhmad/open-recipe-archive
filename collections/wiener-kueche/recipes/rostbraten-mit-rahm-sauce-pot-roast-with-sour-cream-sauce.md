## Ingredients

- 6 Rostbraten (pot roasts)
- Fett (fat)
- 1/2 Liter sauren Rahm (1/2 liter sour cream)
- 1.5 Liter braune Suppe (1.5 liters brown broth)
- Knoblauch (garlic)
- 1 Lorbeerblatt (bay leaf)

## Directions

1. Pound the pot roasts, sear them and quickly brown in fat.
2. Transfer them without the fat to another casserole.
3. Pour 1/2 liter sour cream and 1.5 liters brown broth over them.
4. Season with some garlic and one bay leaf.
5. Braise covered over high heat until tender.
6. The sauce should be thick and light brown in color.
7. Pour the sauce over the pot roasts when serving.
