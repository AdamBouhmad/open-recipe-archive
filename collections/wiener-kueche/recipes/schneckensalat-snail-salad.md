## Ingredients

- Snails
- Water
- Anchovies
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Onions
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the snails soft in water
2. Clean them nicely and cut them into small oblong pieces
3. Also cut anchovies, hard-boiled eggs and onions into small pieces
4. Mix everything together
5. Dress with vinegar and oil, salt and pepper and serve at table
