## Ingredients

- 14 Deka blanched, rinsed, and finely ground rice
- 1⅔ liter boiling milk
- 14 Deka butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 14 Deka fine ground powdered sugar
- 14 Deka blanched, finely ground almonds
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook blanched, rinsed, finely ground rice in 1⅔ liter boiling milk
2. Stir well with 14 Deka butter and set aside
3. Beat 8 egg yolks and 14 Deka fine ground sugar for ⅛ hour
4. Add blanched, finely ground almonds with the expressed almond juice and mix well
5. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
6. Fill into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold
7. Bake for ¾ hour in the oven
