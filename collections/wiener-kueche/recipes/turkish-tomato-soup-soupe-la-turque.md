## Ingredients

- 8 large tomatoes
- 3 sprigs parsley
- One small bunch white onion
- 1.5 liters white broth or water
- 8 Deca butter
- 8 Deca flour
- Salt and pepper
- Optional: 0.25 liters white wine or 0.125 liters Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Cut each tomato in half and place in a casserole
2. Add the parsley sprigs and white onion bunch
3. Simmer with 1.5 liters white broth or water for 0.5 hour slowly
4. In another pan, blend 8 Deca butter and 8 Deca flour together
5. Pour the above soup into this mixture while stirring constantly
6. Simmer for another 1 hour
7. Add salt and pepper as needed
8. Pass through a sieve
9. Optional: add white wine or Madeira
10. Serve with braised meat and green peas, or other desired garnishes
11. If fresh tomatoes are unavailable, use 0.25 liters tomato marmalade and 3.5 liters broth, preparing as above
