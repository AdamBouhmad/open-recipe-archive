## Ingredients

- approximately 1 liter of mushrooms
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons rolls softened in cold water
- butter sauce
- 1/4 liter boiling cream
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from approximately 1 liter of mushrooms
2. Wash the mushroom caps
3. Cut the mushroom caps and stems very finely along with 8 tablespoons of rolls that have been softened in cold water and well pressed out
4. Braise this in 4 tablespoons butter
5. Gradually add butter sauce with 1/4 liter of boiling cream, the necessary salt, and some nutmeg
6. Stir smooth and cook covered
7. When well cooked, pass through a fine strainer
8. Stir in some butter
9. Thin with cream if necessary
10. Stir together well and serve
