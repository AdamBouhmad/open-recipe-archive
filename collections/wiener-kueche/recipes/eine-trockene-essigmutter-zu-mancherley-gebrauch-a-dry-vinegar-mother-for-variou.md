## Ingredients

- wine stone (tartar)
- good wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the wine stone until the water runs clear
2. Dry and powder it
3. Evaporate good wine vinegar over the powder by pouring it on and evaporating it 7 to 8 times repeatedly
4. Store the resulting highly sour powder in glass containers for travel
5. To use: dissolve a few ounces of the powder in a beer glass with wine or water, and it will become sour like vinegar
6. To restore vinegar that is spoiling: add the wine stone powder 4 to 5 times to it
