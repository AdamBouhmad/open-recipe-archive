## Ingredients

- 1¼-½ dkg yeast
- ½ liter milk
- ½ kg plum butter (Powidl)
- 30 dkg flour
- 5 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 whole egg
- salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- lukewarm milk
- flour for dusting
- breadcrumbs roasted in fat

## Directions

1. Warm 30 dkg flour in a mixing bowl and crumble in 5 dkg butter
2. Add 1 egg yolk, 1 whole egg, salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, the risen starter (Dampfl), and a few tablespoons lukewarm milk
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon and let it rise near the stove
4. When it has nearly doubled in size, use a floured spoon to place small pieces of dough on a floured board
5. Pull them apart with floured fingers, fill with plum butter, press the edges together, and shape into dumplings
6. Cook in a saucepan with plenty of boiling salted water for 8-10 minutes
7. Transfer to a round dish, pour breadcrumbs roasted in fat over them, and serve
8. Alternative toppings: grated poppy seeds or gingerbread with hot fat poured over
