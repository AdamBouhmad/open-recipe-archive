## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 10 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- jam

## Directions

1. Make a dough of 14 dkg flour, 10 dkg butter, 7 dkg sugar, and 2 egg yolks on the board
2. If soft, let rest in a cool place until stiff
3. Roll out and cut into rounds
4. Place on an ungreased baking sheet
5. Bake in a sharp fire until light brown
6. Cool, brush with jam, and sandwich two together
7. Sprinkle with sugar or ice
