## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- butter
- beef broth
- wine
- ginger
- pepper
- cloves
- flour
- salt
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Chop the meat into small pieces, wash it clean, let it sweat in a casserole with butter, and cover it so it becomes nicely yellow.
2. Meanwhile, make a sauce from beef broth, wine, ginger, pepper and cloves, let it cook, add the meat to it, pour some of the butter over it, and bind it with flour.
3. Then let it cook sufficiently, season it with salt, and garnish when serving with finely cut lemon peel.
