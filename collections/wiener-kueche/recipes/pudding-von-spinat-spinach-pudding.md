## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram spinach
- 6 decagrams butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- 1 bread roll soaked in milk
- 12 decagrams cooked spinach
- 3 spoonfuls cream
- 4 egg whites
- 8 decagrams fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Create a base by beating 6 decagrams butter until creamy
2. Stir in 3 egg yolks, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. Add 1 bread roll soaked in milk, squeezed dry and passed through a sieve
4. Add 12 decagrams cooked, passed and drained spinach and 3 spoonfuls cream
5. Fold in 4 stiffly beaten egg whites together with 8 decagrams fine breadcrumbs
6. Fill the mixture into a buttered mold scattered with breadcrumbs
7. Steam for 45 minutes until set
8. Turn out the pudding and pour browned breadcrumbs fried in butter over it
9. Serve with hot butter on the side
