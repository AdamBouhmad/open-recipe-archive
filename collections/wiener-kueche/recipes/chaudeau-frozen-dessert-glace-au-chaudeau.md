## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter wine
- 12 egg yolks
- 24 deca grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a chaudeau from 1/2 liter wine, 12 egg yolks, and 24 deca grams granulated sugar
2. Place it in cold water and whisk or beat it until it becomes thick
3. Fill the freezing container with it and proceed as with the Vanilla Cream Frozen Dessert (Recipe 1720)
