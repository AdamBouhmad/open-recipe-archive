## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter flour
- a standard butter dough (No. 1052)
- 1 liter milk or cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter cold milk or cream
- 12 tablespoons powdered sugar
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Prepare a standard butter dough from 1/4 liter flour
2. Fill this dough into a pastry bag with a tube opening (not star-shaped)
3. 3/4 hour before serving, bring 1 liter milk or cream to a boil in a casserole
4. Apply gentle pressure to the pastry bag so the dough comes out of the tube, cutting it into pieces of desired size with a knife into the boiling milk
5. Let the dumplings boil covered, shaking the casserole frequently
6. Meanwhile, beat 4 egg yolks with 3/4 liter cold milk or cream and 12 tablespoons powdered sugar
7. Pour this mixture into the casserole with the dumplings, stir together, and place in the oven for several minutes
8. When the milk has cooked down sufficiently, arrange the dumplings on a platter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
