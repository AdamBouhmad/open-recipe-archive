## Ingredients

- cream mass from previous recipe
- wafer cookies
- water
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying
- spice sugar

## Directions

1. Cut pieces from the steamed cream mass
2. Wrap in moistened wafer cookies
3. Dip in beaten eggs
4. Sprinkle generously with bread crumbs
5. Fry immediately in very hot fat
6. Serve sprinkled with spice sugar
