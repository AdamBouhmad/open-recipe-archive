## Ingredients

- Head lettuce or Bologna lettuce
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. If using head or Bologna lettuce, cut out the finest and cleanest yellow part from each head
2. If using heads or hearts, make four parts from each one
3. Wash them clean and press the water out thoroughly
4. Dress the salad with oil, vinegar, and salt
5. Serve at table
