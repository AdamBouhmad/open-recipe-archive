## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt water
- lemon juice
- cold water
- broth
- butter sauce
- peas
- ham slices or cutlets

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the artichokes and remove the three lowest leaves
2. Cut off the tip with a strong scissors 2 fingers width
3. Place the artichokes in boiling salted water with lemon juice and leave until the leaves can be easily pulled from the crown
4. Transfer to cold water, hollow out the hairy middle with a teaspoon, place in a shallow casserole and cover with boiling well-strained broth
5. Before serving, fill the hot artichokes with hot braised peas, heap on a platter, pour butter sauce over them and garnish with various cutlets or ham slices fried in butter
6. Note: if too little lemon juice is added to the salt water, the artichokes will lose color
