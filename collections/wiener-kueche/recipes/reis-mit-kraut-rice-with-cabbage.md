## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cabbage, finely diced
- 12 deka lard, diced
- 16 deka rice, steamed (Pilau)

## Directions

1. Dice 1 small head of cabbage finely
2. Brown it in 12 deka diced hot lard
3. Prepare 16 deka steamed rice (Pilau)
4. Mix well with the steamed cabbage
5. Arrange in a bowl and serve with bratwurst or roasted liver at table
