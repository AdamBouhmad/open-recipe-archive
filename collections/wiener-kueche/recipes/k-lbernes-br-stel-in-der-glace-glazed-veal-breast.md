## Ingredients

- Beautiful veal breast
- Bacon, finely sliced
- Ham pieces
- Shallots or chives
- Fresh parsley
- Brown broth
- Ground veal meat
- Bacon
- Bread crumbs
- Milk
- Egg yolks
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Remove the ribs from the veal breast and trim the shield
2. Blanch it by placing in boiling water for a few moments, then cool in cold water
3. Lard it in five or six rows with finely sliced bacon, trimming the bacon points so it resembles a hedgehog
4. Place in a casserole with ham pieces, shallots or chives, and a small bunch of fresh parsley
5. Fill the casserole with brown broth, cover, and braise until tender
6. Remove the breast and strain the sauce through a sieve
7. Return the sauce to the casserole and simmer until it becomes a glaze with little sauce remaining
8. Return the breast to the glaze and coat it thoroughly on all sides
9. Place in the center of the serving dish and pour the sauce over it
10. For the garnish: make a farce from half a pound of veal, eight loth of bacon, a bread crumb soaked in milk, all finely chopped and pounded in a mortar
11. Add two or three egg yolks to form a dough, then small-cut shallots and finely chopped parsley
12. Form into a thick sausage on a floured board, cook in beef broth
13. When cooked, slice knife-thick pieces and arrange around the edge of the dish to form a crown
