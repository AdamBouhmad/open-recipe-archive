## Ingredients

- puffballs
- butter
- green parsley
- cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break off the stems and clean the puffballs inside
2. Peel them and place briefly in cold water, then drain
3. Dip them in butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and place in a well-buttered casserole
4. Braise them covered
5. Meanwhile, cut the removed stems with green parsley very finely and braise them in butter
6. Add cream, some salt and pepper and let it boil
7. Arrange the puffballs on a plate and pour this sauce over them
8. Puffballs can also be prepared like cèpes
