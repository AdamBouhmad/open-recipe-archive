## Ingredients

- 12 deca marrow
- 2 deca butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 16 deca rind-removed, finely diced rolls
- milk
- salt
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Let 12 deca marrow become soft
2. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
3. Add 2 deca butter and beat until fluffy
4. Stir in 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg
5. Add 16 deca rind-removed rolls finely diced and soaked with milk
6. Add some salt
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 3 egg whites
8. Treatment same as for potato dumplings
