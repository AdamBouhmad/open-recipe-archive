## Ingredients

- butter dough, slightly thinner than a small finger
- egg white
- butter dough for border
- small crisps of butter dough
- butter sauce
- veal, diced
- veal sweetbreads
- mushrooms
- butter
- parsley
- asparagus or cauliflower
- crayfish
- broth
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Using a warm knife, cut a piece of butter dough from a dish following its shape
2. Brush the edge with egg white and apply a two-finger-wide band of butter dough neatly around the edge
3. From the remaining dough, cut out small crispels the size of a twenty-kreuzer piece
4. Arrange them around the wreath leaving a two-finger-wide space between them
5. Bake nicely
6. Prepare the ragout by making a butter sauce
7. Add diced veal cooked in salted water and some veal sweetbreads
8. Braise 15 mushrooms with butter and parsley
9. Cut asparagus or cauliflower, cooked in salted water, into oblong pieces
10. Mix with 20 to 30 crayfish, bring to a boil
11. Stir in 2 spoonfuls of broth mixed with an egg yolk
12. Add a little more broth
13. Once the butter dough is nicely golden, pile the ragout high in the opening
14. For fast days, fish such as sturgeon, pike, or salmon can be used instead of veal
15. On meat days, calf's head can also be used, cut into large dice after boiling and removing the bones
