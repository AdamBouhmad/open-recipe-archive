## Ingredients

- 1 large ham
- 5 juniper berries, crushed
- 1 bay leaf
- sprigs of thyme
- water
- fingerthick black bread dough

## Directions

1. Place the ham in a casserole with 5 crushed juniper berries, 1 bay leaf, and some sprigs of thyme
2. Fill with cold water and cook the ham slowly for about 1 hour
3. Remove the ham, dry it with a cloth, and wrap it the next day in fingerthick black bread dough
4. Make several cuts so the dough does not burst during baking
5. Bake for 3 hours in the oven or baking chamber
