## Ingredients

- diced bacon
- finely cut onions
- 2 cleaned and prepared chickens
- 1/2 liter soup
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- salt
- 1 strong tablespoon paprika

## Directions

1. Put diced bacon and finely cut onions in a casserole
2. Cut 2 cleaned chickens each into 4 pieces, hack at the joints, twist wings back toward the neck, slightly cut the leg bone, and tuck the legs inward
3. Place the dressed chickens in the casserole with the root vegetables and braise covered
4. After approximately 1/2 hour, pour 1/2 liter soup and 1/2 liter sour cream over the chickens
5. Season with salt and 1 strong tablespoon paprika and braise covered until tender
6. Remove the chickens, arrange on a platter and pour the defatted sauce over them
7. The root vegetables may be omitted if desired
