## Ingredients

- 6 tablets chocolate
- 0.5 liters water
- 12 Deca sugar
- 2 Deca fine gelatin
- 0.5 liters water
- 0.5 liters heavy cream

## Directions

1. Melt 6 tablets chocolate in a warm place, then transfer to a saucepan and pour in 0.5 liters water in which 12 Deca sugar has been dissolved.
2. Stir well until the chocolate is smooth and shiny.
3. Add 2 Deca fine gelatin, which has been dissolved in 0.5 liters water.
4. Fold in 0.5 liters whipped heavy cream.
5. Proceed as directed for Rice with Vanilla Cream (No. 1689).
