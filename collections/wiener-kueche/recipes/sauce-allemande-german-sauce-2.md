## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Mushrooms
- Shallots
- Finely chopped anchovies
- Salt
- Pepper
- Good bouillon
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour together well.
2. Add mushrooms, shallots, finely chopped anchovies, salt, and pepper.
3. Pass it over heat.
4. Let it cook a little with good bouillon.
5. When serving, stir in some egg yolks.
6. Press in lemon juice.
7. Pass the sauce through a fine sieve.
