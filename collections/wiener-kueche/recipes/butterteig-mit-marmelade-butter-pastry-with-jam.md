## Ingredients

- Butter pastry dough made from 7-8 parts flour
- Jam of choice
- Eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Cut the knife-back-thick butter pastry dough into 2 parts
2. Roll one part onto the rolling pin and then unroll it immediately on a water-dampened baking sheet
3. Spread with jam of choice, but only so far that a 2-finger-wide border remains free on all four sides
4. Brush the border with beaten eggs
5. Roll the second part of the dough onto the rolling pin and roll it over the jam so that the edges lie exactly on top of each other and press them together lightly
6. Make several cuts in the top pastry sheet with a knife
7. Brush the dough with beaten eggs
8. Place in the oven and bake at strong heat for 1.5 to 3/4 hours
9. Once the butter pastry is completely baked, brush it very thinly with the mass it was filled with and dry it in the somewhat overfilled oven for a few minutes
10. Remove the sheet from the oven and cover the butter pastry after it has cooled completely with spun sugar or glaze of choice
11. Once this is dry, cut the butter pastry into pieces of choice
