## Ingredients

- peas
- whole onions
- celery
- green parsley
- leek
- parsnip
- butter
- tench or carp
- clear pea soup
- Parmesan cheese
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Cook peas in water very slowly so the bouillon remains clear
2. Cook with whole onions, celery, green parsley, leek and parsnip
3. Let the thick parts settle and strain through a fine sieve
4. In a casserole, line the bottom with thinly sliced onions
5. Add a piece of butter, finely cut parsnip, parsley and other root vegetables, celery and gundel herb
6. Let everything simmer gently, then place tench or carp in it and braise together slowly
7. Finally cook it with clear pea soup and a piece of Parmesan cheese, let it rest briefly and strain through a fine sieve
8. Serve over toasted bread slices
