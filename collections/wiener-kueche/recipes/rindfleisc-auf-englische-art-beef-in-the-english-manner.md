## Ingredients

- a beautiful, well-salted piece of meat larded through and through with bacon (hip-shank)
- belly herb (Kuttelkraut)
- marjoram
- lemon peel, finely cut
- juniper berries, finely ground
- white wine, one part
- vinegar, one part
- water, one part
- onion
- garlic
- celery
- yellow turnips
- ham meat
- flour, a couple spoonfuls
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful, well-salted piece of meat larded through and through with bacon (hip-shank).
2. Mix belly herb, marjoram, finely cut lemon peel, and finely ground juniper berries together.
3. Rub the meat well with this mixture, place it in an earthenware dish, cover well, and let it stand overnight.
4. The next day, put into a casserole one part white wine, one part vinegar, and one part water; also add onion, garlic, celery, and yellow turnips.
5. Put the meat in as well and let it stew covered with a piece of ham meat until beautifully tender.
6. Shake it several times so it does not burn.
7. When tender enough, remove the meat and let the sauce reduce a little.
8. Dust a couple spoonfuls of flour into it, and when this has reduced for a while, add some beef broth.
9. Let it come to a boil again, put the meat back in, and before serving, place it on a plate and strain the small sauce over it.
