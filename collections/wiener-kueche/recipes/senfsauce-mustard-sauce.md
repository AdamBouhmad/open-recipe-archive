## Ingredients

- Anchovies
- 4 hard egg yolks
- Onions
- Parsley
- Mustard
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Sugar or salt

## Directions

1. Finely chop anchovies, hard egg yolks, onions, and parsley.
2. Mix everything on a plate or in a shallow bowl with mustard, vinegar, oil, and a little finely ground sugar (or salt instead of sugar).
3. Usually served with cold fish or roasts.
