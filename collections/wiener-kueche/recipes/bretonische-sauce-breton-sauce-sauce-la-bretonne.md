## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter white beans
- water
- some vinegar
- salt
- 1/2 liter broth
- optional: 1 knife point crushed garlic
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from 4 tablespoons butter and equal amount flour
2. Cook 1/4 liter white beans in water until tender, drain the water, and pass the beans through a sieve into the roux
3. Cook with some vinegar, salt, and 1/2 liter broth and serve
4. If desired, add 1 knife point of crushed garlic and white pepper
