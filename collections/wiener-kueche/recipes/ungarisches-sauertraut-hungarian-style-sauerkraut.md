## Ingredients

- 1 cellar full of sauerkraut
- 1 piece smoked pork meat
- 10 tablespoons fat
- 1 small head of onion, cut
- 10 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter sour broth and sauerkraut broth
- 1 to 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1/4 liter cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1 cellar full of sauerkraut together with 1 piece of smoked pork meat gently
2. Make a light brown roux from 10 tablespoons fat, 1 finely cut small head of onion and 10 tablespoons flour, stirring carefully so no lumps form
3. Pour it with 1/4 liter sour broth and sauerkraut broth
4. Place the meat on a platter, pour some sauerkraut broth over it, cover and set aside
5. Stir the strained sauerkraut into the roux, add 1 to 2 teaspoons paprika and 1/4 liter cream, stir well together
6. If the sauerkraut is too thick, add some sauerkraut broth and let it cook well
7. At serving time, slice the meat into desired pieces and serve it with the sauerkraut in a bowl
