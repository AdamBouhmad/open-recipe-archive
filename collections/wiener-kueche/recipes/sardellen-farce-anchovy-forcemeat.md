## Ingredients

- 32 drachm salted anchovies, cleaned and boned
- 16 drachm butter
- bread dough as specified for Capon Forcemeat
- salt and pepper as needed
- nutmeg
- 1 bay leaf
- chervil
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned anchovies into small dice
2. Place in a casserole containing 16 drachm butter and stew for a short time
3. Set aside to cool
4. Meanwhile, prepare a bread dough of the same consistency as for Capon Forcemeat
5. Once completely cooled, place the dough with the anchovies and butter in a mortar
6. Pound finely together with salt, pepper, nutmeg, 1 bay leaf, chervil, 3 egg yolks, and 1 whole egg
7. Pass through a sieve
8. Use as forcemeat for patties or loaf molds
