## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- 8 decagrams butter
- 8 decagrams flour
- ½ liter soup stock
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped green parsley
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare chickens as in the recipe for 'Chickens in Their Own Juice'
2. Roast equal amounts of butter and flour
3. Add ½ liter soup stock and cook through
4. Strain this sauce
5. Stir in 1 tablespoon finely chopped green parsley and juice of 1 lemon
