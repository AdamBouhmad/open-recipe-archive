## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- one-half pound apple pulp (after pushing through)
- one and one-half quarter pounds butter
- one-quarter pound blanched and finely ground almonds
- finely chopped lemon zest
- ten egg yolks
- the beaten whites of five eggs
- one-quarter pound sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Take peeled apples and stew them, then push them through a sieve
2. For every half pound of pushed-through apples, beat one and one-half quarter pounds butter until very fluffy in a bowl
3. Stir in one-quarter pound blanched and finely ground almonds, finely chopped lemon zest, then ten egg yolks, each stirred well
4. Stir in the pushed-through apples
5. Take the beaten whites of five eggs and one-quarter pound sifted sugar, and stir these two ingredients in gradually
6. Spread a sheet pan and rim with butter
7. Bake it slowly for one hour in a cake pan
