## Ingredients

- young and fat guinea fowl, slaughtered some days before
- salt
- bacon sheets for binding
- Sauce Robert

## Directions

1. Clean it and eviscerate it properly
2. Dry it off, flame it, cut off the wings, and prepare it for roasting
3. Season it with salt
4. Bind it with bacon sheets and place it on the spit
5. Roast it slowly so it gradually takes on color
6. When done and arranged on the platter, serve with Sauce Robert
