## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt water
- soup
- grated Parmesan cheese
- brown or any preferred soup

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into half-finger-length pieces
2. Add them to boiling salt water and cook covered until soft
3. Remove them with a wooden spoon, add them to brown or preferred soup in the soup pot
4. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese
