## Ingredients

- shad
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Dip the fish pieces in flour, then in beaten and salted eggs, then in breadcrumbs and bake them like calf cutlets. Serve fried fish pieces with potatoes; with breaded pieces serve any desired salad; with both you can serve lemon slices.
