## Ingredients

- 3 veal tails
- Salt water
- Butter
- White sauce
- Egg yolks
- Green parsley
- Gum arabic marmalade (optional)
- Wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the veal tails into finger-length pieces and boil for approximately 15 minutes in salt water.
2. Remove, dry with a cloth, and braise covered in 15 ounces of butter for approximately one quarter hour.
3. Moisten with as much white sauce as to cover the pieces one finger-height.
4. When the sauce is well cooked, strain it through a sieve, thicken with 4 egg yolks beaten separately, combine with some finely cut green parsley, and pour over the pieces arranged on a plate.
5. Alternatively, add 3 tablespoons of gum arabic marmalade to the cooked sauce, wine as needed, and let come to a boil again.
