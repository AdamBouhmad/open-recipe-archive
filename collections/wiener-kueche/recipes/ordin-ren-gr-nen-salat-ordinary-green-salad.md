## Ingredients

- head or Bolognese lettuce
- oil
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. If it is head or Bolognese lettuce, cut out the finest and cleanest yellow parts from each
2. If they are heads or hearts, make only four parts from each
3. Wash them clean and press the water out thoroughly
4. Prepare the salad with oil, vinegar, and salt
5. Serve it to the table
