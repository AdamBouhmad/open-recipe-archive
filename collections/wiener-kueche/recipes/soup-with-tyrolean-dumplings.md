## Ingredients

- 50–70 dkg beef shoulder and tail
- 6–8 rolls
- 1/2 milk
- 12 dkg cured meat

## Directions

1. Cut the rolls into cubes and place in a bowl
2. In 5 dkg hot fat, let a small piece of finely chopped onion and parsley brown, pour over the rolls, and let stand for a while
3. Beat the milk with 1 egg and salt, pour over, mix without stirring much
4. Mix in 2–3 spoons of flour, form dumplings, press flat on a floured board
5. Place chopped ham in the center, close the dough over it, and form dumplings
6. Place in boiling strained beef broth for 8 minutes and cook slowly
7. Alternatively, mix the chopped ham into the dough and proceed as above
