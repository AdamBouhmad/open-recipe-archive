## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg veal loin
- 7 dkg bacon
- ½ liter cream
- 5 dkg hot fat
- 1 finely chopped onion
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it, and lard with bacon strips.
2. In a pot, heat 5 dkg fat and sauté finely chopped onion.
3. Add the meat, pour in a little broth, and simmer for 1¼ hours, adding broth as needed.
4. Strain the sauce, possibly pass through a sieve, pour cream over it, simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Serve on a round dish with dumplings or noodles.
