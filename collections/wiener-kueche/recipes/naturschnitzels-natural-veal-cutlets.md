## Ingredients

- Veal loin, finger-thick pieces
- Flour
- Fat
- Brown soup
- Butter
- Soup glace (optional)
- Lemon
- Salad or potato pancake

## Directions

1. Cut finger-thick pieces from veal loin in a thin, layered manner
2. Pound with a moistened wooden mallet
3. Salt them, and pepper if desired
4. Dip both sides in flour
5. Place in a shallow pan with finger-high, very hot fat
6. When the bottom is light brown, turn the cutlets with a fork and brown the second side
7. Place finished cutlets in a casserole containing brown soup and a small piece of butter
8. Keep covered until all cutlets are browned
9. Arrange neatly on a serving dish
10. Remove fat from the pan, bring the remaining sauce to a boil with some brown soup
11. Pour this sauce over the cutlets
12. If dissolved soup glace is available, it may be spread over the cutlets
13. Serve with thinly sliced lemon pieces and salad or potato pancake
