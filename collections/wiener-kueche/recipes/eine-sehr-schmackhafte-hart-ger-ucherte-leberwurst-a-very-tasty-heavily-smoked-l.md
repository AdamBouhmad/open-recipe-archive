## Ingredients

- Fresh calf liver, one third
- Pork liver, one third
- Grated white bread soaked in meat broth, one third
- Salt and spices to taste
- Fine herbs
- Fresh bacon cut into fine dice, about half the amount of the mass

## Directions

1. Clean and finely chop the livers
2. Mix all ingredients well together
3. Season and spice well with spices and fine herbs
4. Add about half the amount of fresh bacon cut into fine dice
5. Mix everything well together
6. Fill this mass into well-cleaned intestines
7. Bind them well
8. Lay them in hot water to set
9. Remove them and let them drain
10. Hang them in smoke until they become quite hard
