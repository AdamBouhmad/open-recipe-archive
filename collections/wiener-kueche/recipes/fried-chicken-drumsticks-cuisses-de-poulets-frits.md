## Ingredients

- Chicken drumsticks
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the drumsticks as for the stuffed chicken drumsticks and braise them until tender.
2. Set them aside with their liquid and after they have cooled completely, remove them, lay them on cloth and let them dry.
3. Dip them in flour, dunk them in beaten eggs, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them on both sides in a casserole with finger-high very hot fat until golden.
4. Arrange them neatly in a serving dish, sprinkle them with fried green parsley and line the edge of the dish with lemon wedges.
5. They can also be served with fine green peas, puree, vegetables etc.
