## Ingredients

- 1 kilo veal from the knuckle
- salt
- twine
- 1 piece yellow onion, thinly sliced
- 4 bay leaves
- pepper
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/4 kilo olive oil or fine table oil
- 4 pieces finely cut anchovies

## Directions

1. Remove the sinews from the veal, salt it, and tie it with twine
2. In a casserole, place thinly sliced onion, 4 bay leaves, and pepper, lay the meat on top, add 1/2 liter water, and braise gently covered for 1/4 hour
3. Remove the meat, pour 1/4 kilo olive oil into a second casserole, and when very hot, add 4 finely cut anchovies
4. Lay the veal on top, pour the sauce over it, and braise gently covered for 3/4 hour with frequent basting, keeping the meat white without browning
5. The sauce should reduce until only the oil remains
6. Remove the twine, cut the meat into desired pieces, arrange in a platter with oil poured over, and serve with risotto or macaroni
7. Beef can also be prepared this way
