## Ingredients

- 1 leg of mutton
- Salt and pepper
- Lard
- Fine table oil
- Soup stock
- Parades sauce
- Raw potatoes, diced

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous roasted leg, but use soup stock instead of wine and vinegar for deglazing.
2. Make a standard Parades sauce and stir it with the leg's drippings.
3. When the leg is almost completely tender, add diced raw potatoes next to the leg in the pan.
4. Let them braise until soft.
5. Arrange the meat with the potatoes on a platter.
6. The sauce can also be served in a sauceboat.
