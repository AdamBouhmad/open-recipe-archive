## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 10 decagrams white butter
- white pepper
- 1 strong tablespoon French mustard
- spinach thick juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Press the hard-boiled egg yolks into a deep bowl
2. Beat them fluffy with 10 decagrams white butter
3. Season with salt and some white pepper
4. Gradually stir in 1 strong tablespoon of French mustard, adding it bit by bit
5. Some spinach thick juice may also be mixed in
