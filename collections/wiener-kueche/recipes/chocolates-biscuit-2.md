## Ingredients

- 50 ounces whole sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 6 egg whites
- 25 ounces chocolate
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 liter water onto 50 ounces whole sugar and cook until it reaches the thread stage
2. Beat stiff snow from 6 egg whites and pour the cooked sugar into it, stirring until cold, but the mass should not become too cold
3. Soften 25 ounces chocolate in the oven, place in a bowl, and beat fluffy with 4 egg yolks
4. Mix this with the prepared mass and pipe onto a buttered sheet as desired as biscotti, wreaths, etc.
5. Place the sheet on a board, put in a full oven, and leave in until a crust forms
6. Now bake the biscotti with the board in the hot oven until completely dry, then carefully remove with a thin knife while still warm
7. A board must be placed under the sheet or the biscotti will bake too dark and crack as a result
