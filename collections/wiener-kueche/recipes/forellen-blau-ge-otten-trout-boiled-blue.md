## Ingredients

- trout
- broth for cooking
- hot wine vinegar
- hot butter for serving
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Do not scale the trout; cut open toward the head, gut, and wash well
2. In the meantime, simmer the broth in a pan for 30 minutes
3. Place trout on a dish and pour hot wine vinegar over to make them blue
4. Carefully slide them into the broth so they are completely covered
5. Pull the pan back and let the fish poach for 10-12 minutes
6. Lift with a slotted spoon onto a long serving dish, back side up
7. For warm serving: drizzle with hot butter and serve boiled potatoes and hot butter separately
8. For cold serving: let cool in the broth, lift onto the dish, garnish with parsley, and serve vinegar and oil separately
