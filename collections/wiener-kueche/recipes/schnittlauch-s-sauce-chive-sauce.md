## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- Equal amount of flour
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped chives
- 1/4 liter soup
- Equal amount of sour cream

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from 8 tablespoons butter and equal amount of flour
2. Add 3 tablespoons finely chopped chives, let them roast a bit
3. Pour this with 1/4 liter soup and equal amount of sour cream and let it simmer well
