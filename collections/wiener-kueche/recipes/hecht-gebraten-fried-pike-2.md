## Ingredients

- 1 small pike
- 3 anchovies
- ½ l cream
- butter
- onion rings
- soup
- crescent potatoes
- parsley
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, cut it open, clean it, and wash it well. Salt it.
2. Let it sit for 1 hour, then dry it with a cloth.
3. Make incisions on top or on the side, insert pieces of anchovy or finely cut mushrooms.
4. Spread the bottom of a roasting pan with butter, lay onion rings on it, and place the pike with the back up into it.
5. Baste it frequently with hot butter, soup, and later with cream for 25-30 minutes without too high heat.
6. Lift it with a fish spatula onto a dish.
7. Arrange crescent potatoes around it that have been tossed in butter and parsley.
8. Decorate it with parsley and lemon slices.
9. Strain the sauce and serve separately. Pike is prepared the same way, requiring slightly longer roasting time.
