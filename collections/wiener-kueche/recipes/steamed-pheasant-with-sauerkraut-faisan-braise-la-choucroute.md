## Ingredients

- Pheasant, dressed and trussed
- Bacon lardons
- Ham slices
- Onion, sliced in leaves
- 1 bay leaf
- Bacon slices
- 1/2 liter white wine
- Stock
- Sauerkraut, steamed

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with bacon slices, ham slices, thinly sliced onion, and 1 bay leaf
2. Place the dressed and trussed pheasant wrapped with bacon slices into the casserole and braise covered
3. After approximately 1.5 hours, pour in 1/2 liter white wine and some stock and continue braising covered until the meat is tender
4. Remove the pheasant carefully after it has cooled somewhat in its own juices
5. Remove the bacon, cut the trussing string, and carve the pheasant
6. Reassemble on a platter and serve with defatted juices and steamed sauerkraut
