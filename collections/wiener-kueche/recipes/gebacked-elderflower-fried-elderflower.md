## Ingredients

- Fully bloomed elderflower
- Cold water
- Egg batter, egg batter, or thin pancake batter
- Fat, very hot
- Sugar

## Directions

1. To clean, dip the fully bloomed elderflower several times in cold water and place on a cloth to drain
2. Dip the flowers up to the stem in egg batter, egg batter, or thin pancake batter
3. Fry in very hot fat until light brown
4. Remove with a slotted spoon, place briefly on blotting paper, arrange on a platter, and sprinkle with sugar as desired
