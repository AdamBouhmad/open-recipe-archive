## Ingredients

- very ripe elderberries picked in dry weather
- finely ground sugar
- 2 tablespoons sugar per jar

## Directions

1. Pick very ripe elderberries in dry weather from their stems
2. Fill into preserving jars
3. Add 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar to each jar
4. Bind and cook as the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842) in steam
