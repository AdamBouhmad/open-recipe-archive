## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley, finely chopped
- Pea broth
- Egg yolks
- Endive salad
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Bay leaves
- Onions, finely cut
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cut the sea bream lengthwise in half, wash them well, then brown them in a casserole, add salt, pepper, and finely chopped fresh parsley, let them simmer in pea broth until they become thick, then stir in a couple of egg yolks.
2. Meanwhile cook well-cleaned endive salad in pea broth seasoned with cloves, nutmeg, a couple of bay leaves, green parsley, salt, and finely cut onions.
3. When it is tender enough, remove it and let it drain.
4. Beat five or six eggs, dip the salad well into them, sprinkle it with breadcrumbs, fry it in hot, only lightly salted lard, and garnish the ragout with it.
