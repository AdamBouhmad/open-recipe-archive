## Ingredients

- 70-80 dag boned veal shoulder or leg
- 6 dag root vegetables
- 1 pickled cucumber
- 5 dag bacon
- 5 dag tongue
- ½ liter cream (Rahm)
- 5 dag hot fat
- soup or water

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the meat, then lard it with cucumber, tongue, and bacon; roll and bind if necessary
2. In a roasting pan, brown the prepared, sliced root vegetables and a sliced onion in hot fat
3. Add the meat and pour in soup or water
4. Roast in the oven for 1½-2 hours, basting frequently with its own juices and adding liquid as needed
5. Remove and keep warm
6. Dust the pan juices with flour, strain, mix with cream, bring to a boil again, and serve separately
7. Slice the meat, arrange on a long platter, and garnish with pommes duchesses
