## Ingredients

- 1 large light turnip and 1 dark yellow turnip
- 1 red turnip
- 4 peeled potatoes
- 2 heads of cabbage
- 1 head of onion (optional)
- Leftover roasted meat such as goose, duck, chicken, pork or roasted goose liver
- Butter or goose fat
- Raw turnips
- Salt, cayenne pepper or paprika as needed
- 1 liter broth
- Rice
- Optionally strawberry jam or various roasted sausages

## Directions

1. Peel and cut turnips, potatoes and cabbage thinly; also add onion if desired.
2. Cut leftover roasted meat into desired pieces.
3. Line a casserole with butter or goose fat, cover the bottom with raw turnips, lay a portion of the above vegetable mixture on top.
4. Sprinkle with necessary salt, cayenne pepper or paprika, then add a layer of cut, roasted meat and continue in this manner until the casserole is filled.
5. Rice should be added last.
6. Pour 1 liter of broth over all, close the casserole well and braise the contents for 1 hour.
7. If the rice is not yet soft enough, pour in additional broth as needed.
8. Turn everything out onto a platter and serve with clear broth.
9. If desired, strawberry jam or various roasted sausages can be added to the casserole.
