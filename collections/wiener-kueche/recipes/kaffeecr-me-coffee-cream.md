## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter very strong black coffee
- 16 dkg cube sugar
- 1 dkg gelatin
- 1/2 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. In a small pot, dissolve 16 dkg cube sugar in the coffee, bring to a boil, immediately remove from heat and simmer slowly for several minutes
2. Allow to cool, dissolve 1 dkg gelatin, and whip the cream
3. Mix the cooled coffee mixture with the whipped cream and slowly pour the melted gelatin through a tea sieve while stirring continuously
4. Fill the mixture into a pudding mold rinsed with water
5. Bury in ice for 2-3 hours, then loosen, hold briefly in hot water, turn out, and optionally decorate with whipped cream
