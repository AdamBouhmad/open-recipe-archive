## Ingredients

- Horseradish
- Breadcrumbs
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Sugar or salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the horseradish.
2. Grate it with a little breadcrumbs.
3. Mix with vinegar, oil, and sugar, or alternatively with salt and pepper if desired.
