## Ingredients

- Rostbraten, finger-thick slices (pot roasts)
- Zwiebel, geschnitten (onions, cut)
- Grüne Petersilie (green parsley)
- 1 Lorbeerblatt (bay leaf)
- Öl, Butter oder Fett (oil, butter or fat)
- Salz und Gewürz (salt and spices)
- Wein oder Essig (wine or vinegar)
- Suppe (broth)
- Mehl (flour)

## Directions

1. Pound the finger-thick sliced pot roasts, trim them neatly, dust with salt and spices, and brown in oil, butter or fat.
2. On a platter, arrange cut onions, green parsley and one bay leaf.
3. Place the pot roasts on top and let them lie covered for several hours.
4. Then quickly brown them on a flat pan or roasting pan.
5. Take the vegetables that remained on the platter, dust with flour, and brown them in the pan with the remaining fat.
6. Let them cook for a few minutes with several spoonfuls of wine or vinegar and some broth.
7. Serve these pot roasts with their own sauce or with brown piquant sauce.
