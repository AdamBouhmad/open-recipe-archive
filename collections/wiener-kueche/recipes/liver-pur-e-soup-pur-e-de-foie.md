## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo calf's liver
- flour
- fat
- 1 piece thinly sliced onion
- 1 piece mushroom
- 3 pieces finely sliced bread
- 2 whole eggs
- approximately 3 liters beef soup
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice 1/2 kilo of calf's liver thinly, coat in flour and fry in hot fat until brown
2. Remove with a basting spoon to a dish covered with blotting paper
3. In the hot fat, fry 1 piece thinly sliced onion, 1 piece mushroom, 3 pieces finely sliced bread and 2 whole eggs until brown
4. Add the fried mixture to the liver in a mortar and pound it fine
5. Place this pounded mixture in a casserole and pour approximately 3 liters of beef soup over it
6. Cook slowly for 1 hour, stirring frequently
7. Pass through a fine sieve, return to the casserole and bring to a boil again
8. Remove the fat that forms
9. In a pot, whisk 3 egg yolks with 1/4 liter cream, then slowly add the boiling liver soup, season with salt and pour into a soup pot over bread cubes cut into pieces and fried in butter
