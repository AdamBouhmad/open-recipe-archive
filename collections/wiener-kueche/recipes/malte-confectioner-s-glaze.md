## Ingredients

- 32 drams of finely sifted sugar
- 1/4 liter of clarified, cold sugar
- 1 tablespoon of Maraschino

## Directions

1. Put 32 drams of finely sifted sugar into a deep bowl.
2. Stir in gradually 1/4 liter of clarified, cold sugar, then 1 tablespoon of Maraschino.
3. Stir this finely for about 1 hour, by which a thickly flowing mass should form (the glaze must not be so thick that it drops heavily from the spoon).
4. Spread the glaze on a cake knife-back thick; if necessary, dry it a few minutes in the lukewarm oven and then set it cold.
5. When coating the cake, use a knife that is always dipped in very hot water, but allow it to drip off.
6. This glaze must be of shining, matte-white color and fine taste.
7. One can prepare various glazes in this way by adding any liqueur or flavored sugar.
