## Ingredients

- Veal loin, finger-thick slices
- Filling as for Stuffed Cutlets
- Onion
- Carrot
- Mushrooms
- White wine or vinegar
- Soup
- Cream
- Capers

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Stuffed Cutlets
2. After the defatted sauce is strained, add 1/2 liter of cream and 2 tablespoons of capers
3. Bring to a boil and pour over the arranged cutlets
