## Ingredients

- 210 grams flour
- 140 grams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1-2 tablespoons milk
- 70 grams sugar
- 1 egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from flour, butter, egg yolks, milk, and sugar
2. Let rest, then divide into 2 equal parts
3. Roll out both parts equally
4. Place one part on an unbutterd baking sheet
5. Spread the filling on top
6. Place second part on top and press edges together
7. Brush with beaten egg
8. Bake at high heat until light brown
9. Cut into pieces, sprinkle with sugar, and serve cooled
