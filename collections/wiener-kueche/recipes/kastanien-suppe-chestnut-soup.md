## Ingredients

- 1 kilo chestnuts
- boiling water
- cold water
- 1 small piece raw ham
- 1 small onion with 1 clove
- 1 small piece bacon
- 8 deca butter
- 1 liter beef broth
- 1 liter good beef broth
- salt
- roasted bread or bread slices

## Directions

1. Shell the chestnuts, put them in boiling water and let lie until the thin shell can easily be removed with a cloth
2. Put them in cold water and remove all impurities
3. Put the chestnuts in a casserole with 1 small piece raw ham, 1 small onion spiked with 1 clove, 1 small piece bacon, 8 deca butter and 1 liter beef broth and let braise covered until tender
4. Remove onion and ham, stir the chestnuts well, pour on 1 liter good beef broth and pass through a sieve
5. Before serving the soup must be stirred boiling hot, salt it and pour over roasted bread or bread slices
