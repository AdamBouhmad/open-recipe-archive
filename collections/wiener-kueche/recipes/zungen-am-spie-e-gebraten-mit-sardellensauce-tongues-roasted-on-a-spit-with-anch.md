## Ingredients

- fresh large tongue
- bacon
- sardelles
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Cook a fresh large tongue until tender and remove the skin; it should not be taken from the pot before being put on the spit, and must be served from the spit.
2. When the skin is removed, lard it beautifully and neatly with two parts bacon and one part sardelles.
