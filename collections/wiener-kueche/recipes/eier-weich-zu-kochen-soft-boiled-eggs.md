## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Take some softened salt, rub the eggs with it and wash them in cold water.
2. Place them with a spoon into boiling water, cover and cook for approximately 5 minutes.
3. Remove them and place in a napkin laid on a plate to serve.
4. Note that freshly laid eggs or eggs with thin shells require less time for soft-boiling than older eggs or those with thick shells.
5. If you use an egg cooker at table, place the eggs directly in cold water and remove after cooking 2-3 minutes.
