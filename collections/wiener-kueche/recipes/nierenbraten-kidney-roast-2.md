## Ingredients

- 120-140 dkg kidney roast
- 5 dkg hot fat
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and salt it
2. Place skin-side down in 5 dkg hot fat and brown
3. Add a little water and roast in the oven at moderate heat for 1½-2 hours, basting frequently with its own juices and adding water as needed
4. Turn when the top is brown
5. Bring the sauce to a boil with a piece of butter
6. Slice the meat, chop through, and place on a long platter
7. Top with kidney sliced separately into slices and serve with the sauce
