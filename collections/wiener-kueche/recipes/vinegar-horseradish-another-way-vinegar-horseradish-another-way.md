## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 teaspoon grated horseradish
- salt
- pepper
- raspberry vinegar

## Directions

1. Pass 6 hard-boiled eggs through a sieve
2. Stir in 2 tablespoons mustard and the same amount of oil, then add 1 teaspoon of grated horseradish, some salt and pepper and stir well with the necessary raspberry vinegar
