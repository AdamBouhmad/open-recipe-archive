## Ingredients

- 32 teaspoons butter
- 32 teaspoons finely pounded sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 tablespoons almond flour
- 32 teaspoons flour

## Directions

1. Mix 32 teaspoons butter with 32 teaspoons finely pounded sugar, add 8 egg yolks one after the other, and after each yolk stir in 1 tablespoon almond flour and the remaining flour, of which 32 teaspoons total by weight.
2. Fill this mass into a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour and bake approximately 1 hour in the oven.
