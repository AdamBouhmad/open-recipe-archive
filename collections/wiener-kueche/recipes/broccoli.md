## Ingredients

- broccoli
- salt water
- braising liquid
- coulis
- fine oil

## Directions

1. Clean the broccoli carefully to the tip
2. Cook it in salt water
3. Pour off the water
4. Cook it briefly with good braising liquid and some coulis
5. For variation you can serve it with fine oil to table
