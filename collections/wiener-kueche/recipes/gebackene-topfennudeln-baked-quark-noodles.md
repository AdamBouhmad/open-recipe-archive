## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 3 handfuls quark (curd), well pressed
- 4 Loth (about 2 oz) lard
- 1 Seidel (about 1/2 liter) milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls yeast
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound flour in a bowl with 3 handfuls of well-pressed quark, 4 Loth lard, and 1 Seidel milk
2. Let everything warm up and bubble well in a pot
3. Add 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, and 3 spoonfuls of yeast
4. Pour this in gradually, beat the dough well, and salt it lightly
5. Form oblong noodles from the dough
6. Make a cut in the middle of each noodle and let them rise well
7. Bake them very carefully in lard
