## Ingredients

- calf's feet stock
- half a bottle of Rhine or French wine
- 12 oranges
- 3 lemons
- half a pound of sugar
- whole cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare the calf's feet stock with 12 pieces as in Rhine wine jelly
2. Rub the zest of the lemons into sugar, without adding other sugar
3. Rub the zest of the oranges into sugar along with half a pound of sugar
4. Add the sugar with lemon zest, 3 parts Rhine wine, and the cinnamon to the stock
5. Place the yellow zest of oranges and their juice on a plate with the remaining wine
6. Pass this through a jelly bag onto the dish on which the jelly is to be arranged
7. Clarify the calf's stock with egg white as in Rhine wine jelly
