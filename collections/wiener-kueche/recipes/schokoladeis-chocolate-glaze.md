## Ingredients

- 28 drams of finely sifted sugar
- 4 tablets of chocolate
- 1/2 liter of boiling water

## Directions

1. Put 28 drams of finely sifted sugar into a deep bowl.
2. Warm 4 tablets of chocolate on a sheet in the oven until the chocolate is so soft that the finger can break through it.
3. Put it in a saucepan, pour 1/2 liter of boiling water over it and stir on the stove until the chocolate is completely stirred and smooth.
4. Add it hot to the sugar and stir this together for about 1/2 hour and then use it.
5. It must flow thickly.
6. If the mass is too thick, add some warm water; if it is too thin, add some sugar; in both cases it must still be stirred for 1/2 hour.
