## Ingredients

- approximately 2 measures or 4 pounds of ripe wild strawberries
- 1 half measure or 1 pound of raspberries
- juice of one lemon
- 2 measures of water

## Directions

1. Place the strawberries and raspberries in an earthenware vessel
2. Crush them
3. Add the juice of one lemon
4. Pour 2 measures of water over them
5. Let it stand for half an hour
6. Press it through a napkin
7. Sweeten to taste
8. Pass it through a hair sieve
