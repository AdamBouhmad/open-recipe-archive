## Ingredients

- Rostbraten, finger-thick slices (pot roasts)
- Salz und Pfeffer (salt and pepper)
- Zerlassene Butter oder Fett (melted butter or fat)
- Fein geschnittener Knoblauch (finely cut garlic)
- Butter oder Fett (butter or fat)
- Salz und Pfeffer (salt and pepper)
- Essig (vinegar)
- Suppe (broth)

## Directions

1. Pound the finger-thick sliced pot roasts well, dust with salt and pepper, and brown in melted butter or fat.
2. Brown them quickly, either on a roasting pan or in a very hot pan with fat.
3. Arrange on a platter and coat with sauce.
4. For the sauce: quickly roast finely cut garlic in butter or fat over high heat, add some salt and pepper, deglaze with a few spoonfuls of vinegar and some broth, and pour this sauce over the pot roasts.
