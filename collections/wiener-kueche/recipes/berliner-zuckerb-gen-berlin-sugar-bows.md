## Ingredients

- One pound sugar
- One pound fine flour
- 6 whole eggs
- Aniseed
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Work together the sugar, flour, and eggs into a mass like aniseed cake
2. Add aniseed and orange blossom water
3. Spread this mass on butter paper to the thickness of a good knife-back
4. Bake slowly for a quarter hour
5. Cut into strips three finger-widths wide
6. While still warm, bend over a round piece of wood the thickness of a wax candle
