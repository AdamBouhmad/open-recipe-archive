## Ingredients

- 2 large heads cabbage
- 8 dag bacon
- hash
- rice (optional)
- breadcrumbs
- soup broth

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage, separate leaf by leaf, wash it, place the beautiful leaves in boiling salt water for 10 minutes, then drain and dry well.
2. Lay the leaves flat on a board, brush with hash, roll them, place in an omelet pan, pour in a little broth.
3. Cut the bacon into slices, make some slits in them, cover the cabbage with it, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, place in the oven to gratinée for 10 minutes.
4. Serve as a side dish to roast beef or beefsteak or as an evening dish.
5. Fresh steamed rice or leftover rice can be mixed into the hash.
6. Leftover cabbage can be used for soup or bound.
