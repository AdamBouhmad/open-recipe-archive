## Ingredients

- young or old kohlrabi
- salt
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi, cut them first into fine leaf-like slices, then into noodle-like strips.
2. Place in cold salted water and cook covered for 1 hour (young ones need only 20 minutes), then drain.
3. Place on a serving dish and pour over breadcrumbs toasted in butter.
4. They require a lot of butter.
5. They can also be served as a salad with vinegar and oil.
