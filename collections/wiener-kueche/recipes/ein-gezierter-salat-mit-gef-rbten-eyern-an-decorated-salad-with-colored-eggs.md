## Ingredients

- Salad (fresh or head lettuce)
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Red beet vinegar
- Blue cabbage
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose salad as desired, fresh or head lettuce, and clean it finely
2. Hard-boil as many eggs as needed
3. Chop the egg whites separately into fine pieces
4. Make three kinds of colors: red from red beet vinegar, blue by cooking a head of blue cabbage in vinegar then straining and cooling it, and green by cooking the same blue cabbage in water instead of vinegar, then straining and cooling
5. Divide the egg white into four parts
6. Place one quarter in each of the three colored liquids and let them steep for a couple of hours until colored
7. Leave the remaining quarter part white
8. Chop the egg yolks separately
9. Spread the chopped yolks over the salad and then sprinkle the four colored egg white pieces in a pleasing and neat arrangement
10. Dress the salad only when it is to be used
