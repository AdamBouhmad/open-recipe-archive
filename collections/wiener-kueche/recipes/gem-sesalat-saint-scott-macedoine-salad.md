## Ingredients

- 2 large cooked potatoes, thinly sliced
- 4 pieces pickled gherkins, thinly sliced
- 1 red beet, roasted in the oven, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 artichoke heart, soaked in salt water, cut into small pieces
- 20 cooked green beans, broken in the middle
- 10 asparagus spears, soaked in salt water, cut into finger-length pieces
- Some cooked celery leaves, thinly sliced
- Some thinly sliced yellow beets
- Vinegar
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon French mustard
- Salt and pepper
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 8 tablespoons oil
- 4 tablespoons tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut all vegetables as described and place in a deep bowl
2. Pour some vinegar over and let stand covered for 10 minutes
3. In the meantime, press 4 hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve into a deep bowl
4. Add 1 tablespoon French mustard, some salt and pepper
5. Stir with a raw egg yolk, then gradually add 8 tablespoons oil and 4 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
6. Mix everything well with the vegetables
7. The salad should be thick and well bound
8. Serve with various roasted meats or roasted fish
