## Ingredients

- half milk and half water
- whites of 15 eggs

## Directions

1. Place a casserole with half milk and half water on fire
2. Beat the whites of 15 eggs to stiff snow
3. As soon as it boils, insert a spoon of snow at a time, let boil once, lay on a sieve or napkin to drain, and continue until all the snow is used
