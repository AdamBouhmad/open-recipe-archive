## Ingredients

- cooked or braised venison
- onions
- butter
- vinegar
- Espagnole sauce
- salt
- pepper
- bread croutons or poached eggs

## Directions

1. Cut away the black skin from the cooked or braised venison
2. Divide the meat into fillets
3. Cut a dozen onions in the middle and finely break them into layers
4. Sauté in butter until nicely browned
5. Pour vinegar and a couple of serving spoonfuls of Espagnole sauce into it
6. Add salt and pepper and let it cook briefly
7. Remove the fat from the top
8. Lay the fillets in the sauce and keep very hot until serving (do not boil)
9. When serving, garnish with croutons or poached eggs
