## Ingredients

- 1 liter flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 whole eggs
- milk or cream as needed
- salted water
- 16 hectograms hot butter or fat

## Directions

1. In a deep bowl, combine 1 liter flour, 1 tablespoon salt, and 2 whole eggs
2. Stir with as much milk or cream as needed to make a smooth, pourable dough
3. Beat the dough until it falls from the spoon
4. Place dough on a board and strip pieces of desired size into boiling salted water using a knife kept in hot water
5. Cover and poach for 10-15 minutes
6. Pour into a colander, rinse with cold water, place in a pot containing 16 hectograms hot butter or fat, stir well, and use as a garnish when hot
7. Alternatively, pour 4 whole eggs beaten with 1/8 liter sour cream over the noodles in butter and braise covered for 10-15 minutes with frequent stirring
8. Heap onto a plate and serve as a flour dish with salad if desired
