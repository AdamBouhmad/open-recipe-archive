## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- salt water
- finely chopped onion
- parsley
- butter
- 1 pound cooked ham
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 Seidel (about 1.5 liters) sour cream
- nutmeg (very little)
- butter for the form
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Make a regular noodle dough from 4 eggs, roll it out, and cut it into square patches
2. Boil them in salt water and strain through a colander
3. Braise finely chopped onion and parsley in butter
4. Cut 1 pound of cooked ham very finely
5. In a bowl, stir together 6 egg yolks with 3 Seidel of sour cream and nutmeg (but very little)
6. Add the braised mixture and ham to this
7. Carefully stir the patches into the mixture
8. Coat a form well with butter, dust it with grated bread
9. Fill the noodles into it and let it bake for about an hour
