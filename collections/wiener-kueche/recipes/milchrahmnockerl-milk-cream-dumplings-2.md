## Ingredients

- 1 Seidel (about 1/2 liter) cream
- 3 Loth (about 1.5 oz) melted butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls flour
- 1 inch high cream for cooking
- sugar (optional)
- vanilla (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat the cream, melted butter, and egg yolks well together
2. Stir 4 spoonfuls of flour into the mixture
3. When the mixture is light and fluffy, take 1 inch high cream in a pan or cooking vessel
4. Once the cream simmers, place the dumplings into it and provide heat from below and above
5. If desired for better flavor, add sugar and vanilla
