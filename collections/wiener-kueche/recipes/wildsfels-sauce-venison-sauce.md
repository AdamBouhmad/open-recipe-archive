## Ingredients

- 6 deca butter
- equal amount flour
- 3/4 liter ripe gooseberries, mashed
- red wine
- necessary broth

## Directions

1. Prepare like the gooseberry sauce, with the difference that red wine is used instead of white wine for the venison sauce
