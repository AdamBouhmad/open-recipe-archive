## Ingredients

- 1/2 ham
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 8 egg whites
- 1/2 liter flour
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Finely chop 1/2 ham with a knife
2. Gradually beat in 8 egg yolks and 1/4 liter sour cream
3. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites
4. Prepare strudel dough from 1/2 liter flour as for baking and let rest 1/2 hour
5. Cover a table with a clean cloth, sprinkle with flour, roll and pull the dough as much as possible
6. Brush the well-pulled strudel dough with melted butter using a brush
7. Distribute the ham filling evenly over it
8. Turn up the dough handbreadth, add the rest of the filling on top, then roll up the dough over the cloth (Lungen-Strudel Recipe 75)
9. Place the strudel in a saucepan lined with butter in a spiral shape
10. Brush with melted butter and bake in a very hot oven for 1/2 to 3/4 hour
11. Cut into desired pieces, arrange on a plate with a slotted spoon and serve
