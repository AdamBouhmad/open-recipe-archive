## Ingredients

- blonde and light yellow hens
- 1 head onion
- 3 slices root vegetables
- 1 piece parsley root
- 1 piece leek
- 3 sprigs thyme
- 5 grains each of mixed spices
- 1 clove
- 2 cleaned and dressed chickens
- ¼ liter wine
- ¼ liter water
- ⅛ liter vinegar
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons butter
- sour cream
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Very finely dice onion, root vegetables, parsley root, and leek and place in a casserole
2. Bind together thyme, mixed spices, and clove in a small bag and add to the vegetables
3. Place 2 cleaned and dressed chickens on top
4. Pour ¼ liter wine, ¼ liter water, and ⅛ liter vinegar over
5. Cover and poach chickens until tender
6. Remove chickens and cut each into 4 pieces
7. Arrange in a serving dish, pour some sauce over, and garnish with strained root vegetables and grated horseradish
8. Whisk 2 egg yolks with 3 tablespoons butter and sour cream
9. Gradually stir in the cooking liquid as needed
10. Serve this sauce with the chickens in a gravy boat
