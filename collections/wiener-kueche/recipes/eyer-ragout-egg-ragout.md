## Ingredients

- Eggs, as many as needed
- Sweet or sour cream (Schmette)
- Nutmeg, grated
- Pepper
- Salt
- Green parsley, chopped
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil as many eggs as needed until completely hard, cut them in half lengthwise, and place them in a saucepan.
2. Pour sweet or sour cream over them, add grated nutmeg, pepper, salt, chopped green parsley, and a piece of butter.
3. Let this cook with the eggs until a thick broth forms.
4. Arrange neatly on a dish and garnish the edge with hard-boiled whole eggs.
