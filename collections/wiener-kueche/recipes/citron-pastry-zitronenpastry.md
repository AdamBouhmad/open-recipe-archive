## Ingredients

- Dough (prepared as for almond tarts, Recipe 1611)
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Roll out dough to the thickness of a knife back
2. Cut out round discs using a cutter
3. Place half the discs on a baking sheet lined with butter at finger-width distance apart
4. Brush with beaten eggs
5. From remaining dough rounds, cut out the center with a small cutter to create a ring
6. Place rings on the egg-brushed discs so the edges meet evenly
7. Brush the rings with beaten eggs
8. Coat with hail sugar
9. Bake at low heat in the oven until golden
