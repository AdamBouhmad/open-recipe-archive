## Ingredients

- half a measure simple bread tisane
- citron juice
- 3 loth cinnamon water
- 1 loth Rhine wine
- 1 quarter measure crushed sugar
- yolk of one egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Take half a measure of simple bread tisane
2. Add citron juice, 3 loth cinnamon water, 1 loth Rhine wine, and crushed sugar to taste until pleasantly sweet
3. Mix all together
4. If desired and there is no high fever, you may add the yolk of one egg
