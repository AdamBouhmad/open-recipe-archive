## Ingredients

- a pork lung or pluck
- calf pluck and mesentery (for several sausages)
- fatty pork or fat
- water
- four rolls
- three to four onions
- a piece of garlic

## Directions

1. Take a pork lung or pluck (as much as needed; for several sausages one can also add calf pluck and mesentery), some fatty pork or fat
2. Over-cook this in water beforehand, but not until soft, and then carefully remove all glands and cartilage
3. Cut everything into small pieces and chop it together more finely or more coarsely as desired
4. For two plucks and a couple pounds of meat, cut four rolls into pieces and add them to the broth in which the meat for it was boiled
5. After they become soft, cut them into it, add three to four finely cut onions along with a piece of finely cut garlic
