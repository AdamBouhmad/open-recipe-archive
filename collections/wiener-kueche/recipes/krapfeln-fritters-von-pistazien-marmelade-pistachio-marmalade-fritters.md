## Ingredients

- 16 Deca finely ground sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Stir together 16 Deca finely ground sugar, juice of 1 lemon, and 2 stiffly beaten egg whites for 3-4 hours.
2. Take 1 coffee spoon of this mixture and pipe raised mounds onto a baking sheet coated with gauze using the handle of a second coffee spoon.
3. Sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and dry in a moderately warm oven, but they should only dry on the outside while remaining soft inside.
4. When properly dried, remove from the baking sheet, pair them up two by two, and join them with marmalade.
