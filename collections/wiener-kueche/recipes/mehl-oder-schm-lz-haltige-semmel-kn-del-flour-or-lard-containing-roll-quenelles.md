## Ingredients

- 14 Loffel butter, or 12 Loffel lard or skimmed fat
- 24 Loffel finely diced rolls, mixed with 1/8 liter milk
- 1/8 liter milk
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- 20 Loffel flour

## Directions

1. Heat 14 Loffel butter, or 12 Loffel lard or skimmed fat and pour over 24 Loffel finely diced rolls mixed with 1/8 liter milk
2. Beat 1/8 liter milk with 1 egg yolk and 2 whole eggs and pour over it as well
3. Add salt and bind with 20 Loffel flour
4. Form knödel of desired size
