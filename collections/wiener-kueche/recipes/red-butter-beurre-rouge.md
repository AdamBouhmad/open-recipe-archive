## Ingredients

- 5 decagrams ordinary crayfish butter
- 5 decagrams ordinary butter
- 5 decagrams anchovy butter
- 3 hard-boiled, strained egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 tablespoon strong vinegar
- salt
- cochineal or currant juice for coloring

## Directions

1. Beat the crayfish butter and ordinary butter fluffy with the anchovy butter
2. Add the strained hard-boiled egg yolks and beat fluffy
3. Gradually stir in 2 tablespoons of oil
4. At the same time as the oil, stir in 1 tablespoon strong vinegar
5. Salt the butter and color it slightly red with cochineal or currant juice
