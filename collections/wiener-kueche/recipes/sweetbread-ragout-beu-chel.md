## Ingredients

- ½ calf or 1 pork sweetbread, approximately ½-¾ kg
- 6 dkg root vegetables
- ⅛ liter cream
- 1 scallion
- salt and vinegar
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 5 dkg fat for roux
- lemon juice
- semolina strudel or dumplings

## Directions

1. Wash the sweetbread twice with plenty of water
2. Cook with cleaned, washed, thinly sliced root vegetables, 1 scallion, several tablespoons salt and vinegar, 5 peppercorns and spice berries, 2 bay leaves for 1-1¼ hours
3. Lift out, cool and cut finely into noodle-like pieces
4. Make a light brown roux with 5 dkg fat
5. Pour sweetbread cooking water over it, add the sweetbread
6. Season with lemon juice, cook well together
7. Add the cream and bring to a boil again
8. Serve with semolina strudel or dumplings
