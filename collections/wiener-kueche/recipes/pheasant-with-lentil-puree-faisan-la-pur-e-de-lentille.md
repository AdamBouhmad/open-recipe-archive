## Ingredients

- Pheasant, dressed and trussed
- Bacon slices
- 1.5 liters cooked and passed lentils
- Stock and white wine as for steamed pheasant

## Directions

1. Prepare as for steamed pheasant
2. Deglaze and defat the pheasant's juices
3. Mix the defatted juices with 1.5 liters of cooked and passed lentils thoroughly
4. Carve the pheasant, arrange on a platter, and garnish with the lentil puree
