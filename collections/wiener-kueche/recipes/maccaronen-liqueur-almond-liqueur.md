## Ingredients

- 1 half pound of selected good bitter almonds
- 9 measures of brandy
- 5 pounds of sugar
- 1 measure of water
- 2 measures of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Pound 1 half pound of selected good bitter almonds in a stone mortar
2. Pour 9 measures of brandy into a jug over the almonds and stir diligently
3. After 14 days, draw the mixture through a water bath over gentle and even fire
4. When 5 measures have been obtained, mix in the sugar water
5. Prepare sugar water by dissolving 5 pounds of sugar in 1 measure of water and 2 measures of orange blossom water
6. If the liqueur is too spirituous, add more sugar water
7. Filter the whole
