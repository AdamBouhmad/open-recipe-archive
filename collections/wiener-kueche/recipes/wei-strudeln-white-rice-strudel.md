## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 20 Deka cooked rice
- 10 Deka butter
- 4 whole eggs
- salt
- green parsley
- 1/3 liter flour for dough

## Directions

1. Cook 20 Deka rice in 1/2 liter milk until soft
2. Place in a bowl and let cool
3. Whip 10 Deka butter until fluffy
4. Beat in 4 whole eggs one by one
5. Add cooled rice, salt, and green parsley
6. Mix well
7. Spread on strudel dough made from 1/3 liter flour
8. Treat as lung strudel
