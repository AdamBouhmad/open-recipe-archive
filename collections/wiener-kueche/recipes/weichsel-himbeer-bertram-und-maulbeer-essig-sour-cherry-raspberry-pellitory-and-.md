## Ingredients

- sour cherries, raspberries, pellitory, and mulberries as needed
- good wine vinegar to fill the bottle
- pellitory leaves, 1 strong handful per measure

## Directions

1. Put as much of these ingredients as needed into a bottle
2. Fill the bottle with good wine vinegar
3. Let it stand in the sun for 8 to 14 days
4. After this time, strain the vinegar through filter paper
5. Stopper the bottles well
6. Let it stand in the cellar, where it keeps well
7. Note: of pellitory leaves take only 1 strong handful per measure; of the other ingredients, too much will not hurt
