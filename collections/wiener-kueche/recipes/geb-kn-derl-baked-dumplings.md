## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter or fat
- 1 egg
- salt
- 2 bread rolls (Semmeln)
- finely chopped parsley
- 8 dkg breadcrumbs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cream together 4 dkg butter or fat
2. Mix in 1 egg, salt, 2 bread rolls (trimmed, soaked in water, and well drained), finely chopped parsley, and 8 dkg breadcrumbs
3. Form into dumplings
4. Fry in hot lard, drain, and serve warm
