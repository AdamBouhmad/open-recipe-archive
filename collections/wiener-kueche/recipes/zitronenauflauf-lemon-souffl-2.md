## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dkg sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. 30 minutes before serving, beat 4 egg yolks with 7 dkg sugar until very foamy
2. Stir in the juice and zest of 1/2 lemon and 4 stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Bake the same way as the chocolate soufflé
4. This amount serves 4 persons; for 6 persons add 2 more eggs and proportionally more of the other ingredients
