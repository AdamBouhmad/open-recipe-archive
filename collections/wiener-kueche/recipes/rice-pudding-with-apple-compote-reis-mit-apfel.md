## Ingredients

- Boskop apples
- 16 tablespoons washed rice
- 0.5 liter milk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Core the Boskop apples and cut them into slices
2. Fry the slices on both sides until golden in very hot butter
3. Place in a serving dish
4. Stir 16 tablespoons of washed rice into 0.5 liter of boiling milk under constant stirring and cook until thick
5. Mix with 8 tablespoons butter and 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar
6. Set aside
7. When the rice has cooled slightly, arrange it in a mound on a serving dish
8. Make a depression in the center, place the fried apples in it
9. Cover the whole with Spanish meringue, dust with finely ground sugar
10. Bake in an open oven for 10 to 15 minutes
