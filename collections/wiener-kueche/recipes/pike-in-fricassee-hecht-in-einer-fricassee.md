## Ingredients

- pike
- gallbladder from liver
- salt
- wine vinegar
- water
- onions
- bay leaves
- fricassee broth
- clear pea broth or white fish consommé

## Directions

1. Scale and clean a pike thoroughly
2. Carefully remove the gallbladder from the liver (only the liver and gallbladder from pike are usable, not the rest of the entrails)
3. Salt it and let it rest in salt for a while
4. Cook it in half vinegar and half water with some onions and bay leaves
5. Meanwhile prepare a fricassee broth using clear pea broth or, better if available, a white fish consommé instead of meat broth
6. Place the fish on the dish and the fricassee over it
