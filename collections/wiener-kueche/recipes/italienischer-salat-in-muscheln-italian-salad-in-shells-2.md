## Ingredients

- ½ kg Kipfler potatoes
- 1 apple
- 10 h root vegetables
- 1 mild herring
- 2 anchovies
- 10 h corn salad
- 1 pickled cucumber
- 1 red beet
- 2 h capers

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the root vegetables, cut them into small cubes, put in cold salt water, cook for 30-45 minutes, strain.
2. Cut the cooked cold potatoes into cubes, as well as the apple and cucumber.
3. Debone and clean the herring, wash it.
4. Cut one half into larger pieces, the other half and the deboned anchovy into small pieces.
