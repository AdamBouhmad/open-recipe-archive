## Ingredients

- 14 deca shelled hazelnut kernels
- 6 tablespoons cream
- 1/2 liter cream
- 10 deca sugar
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grind 14 deca shelled hazelnut kernels with 6 tablespoons cream
2. Cook in 1/2 liter cream with frequent stirring
3. Press through a cloth
4. Beat with 10 deca sugar and 4 egg yolks over heat with a whisk
5. Continue beating until the mass begins to bind and thicken
6. Use immediately
