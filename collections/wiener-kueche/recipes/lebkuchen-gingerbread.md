## Ingredients

- ½ kg honey
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 ground clove
- 6 dkg sugar
- ½ kg flour
- 1 whole egg
- 4 dkg butter
- Not quite 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 6 dkg grated hazelnuts for filling
- Lemon zest
- 3 dkg finely chopped citron
- 2 dkg chopped orange peel
- 1 egg for brushing
- Almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Heat honey with spices until boiling
2. Mix on the board with flour and remaining ingredients
3. Work the dough well
4. Cover and let rest one day
5. Roll out and cut various shapes
6. Add filling to the center
7. Cover with dough sheet
8. Press edges firmly
9. Brush with egg
10. Top with half an almond
11. Place on unbuttered sheet
12. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
13. Let rest 4-5 days before serving
14. For filling: mix grated hazelnuts, lemon zest, chopped citron, and chopped orange peel with 1 egg and sugar to taste
