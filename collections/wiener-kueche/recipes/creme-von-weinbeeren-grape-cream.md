## Ingredients

- 2 measures juice from ripe grapes
- quarter pound sugar
- lemon
- 12 egg yolks
- 2 strong glasses Malaga or Tokayer wine
- ripe grapes

## Directions

1. Press 2 measures juice from ripe grapes
2. Let this reduce to half a measure
3. Add quarter pound sugar and let cool
4. Put 12 egg yolks in a pot
5. Beat with 2 strong glasses Malaga or Tokayer wine
6. Pour in the reduced juice
7. Let come to a boil once over charcoal fire with constant stirring
8. Line the plate with ripe grapes
9. Pour the cream cold over it
