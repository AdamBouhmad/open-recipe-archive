## Ingredients

- Rostbraten (pot roasts), somewhat thicker slices
- Speckschnitte (bacon strips)
- Salz, Gewürz, fein gefrorener Petersilie und Schalotten (salt, spices, finely frozen parsley and shallots)
- Schinken und Speckplatten (ham and bacon sheets)
- Zwiebel (onion)
- Gelbe Rübe (yellow turnip)
- 1 Lorbeerblatt (bay leaf)
- Wein (wine)
- Optional: Glace (glaze)
- Kartoffelpuree oder gefülltes Kraut oder Linsen Puree oder Knobeln (potato puree or stuffed cabbage or lentil puree or garlic)

## Directions

1. Cut the pot roasts somewhat thicker and lard them with bacon strips that have been rolled in salt, spices, finely frozen parsley and shallots.
2. Line a casserole with ham and bacon sheets.
3. Place the pot roasts on top and add some onion, yellow turnip and one bay leaf.
4. Braise covered for a short time.
5. Then add some wine and let the meat braise gently until tender.
6. Before serving, if you have it, glaze the pot roasts.
7. Strain the broth into a casserole, reduce it somewhat, degrease it and serve in a sauce boat or pour over the pot roasts.
8. Serve with pressed horseradish or stuffed cabbage, or lentil puree, or garlic and the like.
