## Ingredients

- 16 dkg butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 16 dkg sugar
- 16 dkg flour
- 1 kg cherries or plums
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Cream together 16 dkg butter
2. Gradually stir in 3 whole eggs and 16 dkg sugar
3. Stir in 16 dkg flour by the spoonful
4. Spread finger-thick on a buttered, floured baking sheet
5. Cover with cherries or pitted, halved plums with round side down
6. Bake at higher heat until light brown
7. Cut, sprinkle with sugar
8. Serve cooled
