## Ingredients

- quince pieces
- one-half pound quince pulp
- same amount sugar
- Seville orange peel, finely cut
- lemon peel, finely cut
- ten fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Cook some pieces of quince, scrape them finely, and beat them through a hair sieve
2. From the quince use one-half pound, put the same amount of sugar and finely cut Seville orange and lemon peel in it
3. Beat foam from ten fresh eggs onto a sieve and add it piece by piece
4. It must be stirred together for a while
5. Then put it on a sheet lined with wafers and arrange it beautifully high and round
6. Cut a cross in the top and bake it in the same way in a cool oven
