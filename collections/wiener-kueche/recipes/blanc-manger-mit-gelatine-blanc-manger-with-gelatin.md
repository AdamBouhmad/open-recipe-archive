## Ingredients

- 2 deca gelatin
- 1 liter liquid
- 1/4 liter cold water
- 3/8 liter heavy cream
- 8 deca sugar
- 1/8 liter gelatin
- 3/8 liter black hot coffee
- 16 deca sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 deca gelatin with 1/4 liter cold water on the hot plate with constant stirring
2. Mix 3/8 liter of the dissolved hot gelatin with 3/8 liter heavy cream mixed with 8 deca sugar and 1/8 liter gelatin to 3/8 liter black hot coffee with 16 deca sugar
3. Proceed further as described above
