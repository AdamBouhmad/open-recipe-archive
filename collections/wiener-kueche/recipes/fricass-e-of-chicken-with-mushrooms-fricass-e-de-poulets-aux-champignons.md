## Ingredients

- finely picked and dressed chickens
- fat
- onion, thinly sliced
- root vegetables, thinly sliced
- mushrooms, thinly sliced
- whole spices
- soup stock

## Directions

1. Place finely picked and dressed chickens in a casserole containing fat, thinly sliced onion, root vegetables, mushrooms, and whole spices
2. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, pour in enough soup stock to cover the chickens
3. Braise uncovered until tender
4. Remove chickens and strain the sauce through a sieve
5. Prepare a butter sauce with this sauce
6. Cut chickens into attractive pieces
7. Arrange on a serving dish and pour the sauce over
