## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 ounces butter
- 24 ounces granulated sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 50 ounces cooked, passed, pitted potatoes
- 8 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy
2. Add the granulated sugar and egg yolks one after another
3. Mix in the cooked, passed, and pitted potatoes
4. Gently fold in the whipped egg white snow
5. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
6. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour or bake in the oven
7. Turn out onto a serving dish and sprinkle with finely powdered sugar
