## Ingredients

- Pork leg, skinned
- Salt
- Caraway
- Garlic, crushed (optional)
- Fat
- Fat, melted

## Directions

1. Rub the leg of a skinned pig with salt, caraway and if desired, crushed garlic
2. Put some fat into a roasting pan, place the leg on it, pour melted fat over the meat
3. Roast at moderate heat, basting frequently with the pan juices, for 1 1/2 to 2 hours in the oven
4. After this time, remove the meat, cut it into pieces, place these on a platter and serve the sauce in a sauceboat
5. The sauce can be soured and the fat removed as desired
