## Ingredients

- 19 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 7 dkg sugar
- 10 dkg grated chocolate
- 1/2 milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 7 dkg grated chocolate

## Directions

1. On the board, make a dough from flour, butter, egg yolk, sugar, and grated chocolate
2. Roll out the dough and cut rounds
3. Bake on a lightly buttered sheet at moderate heat
4. For the filling: Over steam, beat 1/2 milk, 2 egg yolks, 1 teaspoon flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 7 dkg grated chocolate until thick
5. Fill the baked tartlets
6. Ice with white or red icing or sprinkle with sugar if desired
