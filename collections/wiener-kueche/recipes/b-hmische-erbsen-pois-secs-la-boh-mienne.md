## Ingredients

- 1 liter dried peas
- 2 liters cold water
- salt
- roasted onions
- bread crumbs
- boiled meat, bacon or similar

## Directions

1. Place 1 liter of dried peas in 2 liters of cold water and cook slowly for two hours so they become evenly tender
2. While cooking, carefully skim off any foam that rises to the surface
3. Drain the water and salt the peas
4. Place on a platter and pour roasted onions and equal amount of bread crumbs over them
5. Serve with boiled meat, bacon or similar
6. If desired, smoked pork (Kaiser-fleisch) can be cooked until tender with the peas
