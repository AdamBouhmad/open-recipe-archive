## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams rose petals, freed of stamens and green parts
- 40 decagrams boiling, clarified sugar
- 7 decagrams isinglass, hot and dissolved
- Clear juice of 2 lemons
- Cherry water or additional liquid as needed

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as Violet Sulz (see recipe 1677)
2. Use 16 decagrams rose petals instead of violet flowers, freed of their stamens and all green parts
