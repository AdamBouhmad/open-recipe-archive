## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- flour as needed
- 1 liter milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 liter sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix 3 whole eggs with enough flour to make a thin, fluid dough
2. Slowly pour this dough into 1 liter of boiling milk under constant stirring and cook thick
3. Meanwhile, beat 2 egg yolks with 4 tablespoons sugar and 0.5 liter of sour milk
4. Pour this into the cooked dough, stir well
5. Bring once to a boil
6. Arrange in a serving dish and dust with desired preserves
