## Ingredients

- hartshorn
- 1 measure of water
- 1 loth of tournesol
- finely ground egg white and sugar
- half a bottle of the best rose water
- juice of 6 lemons
- half a bottle of good Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Cook hartshorn with 1 measure of water as mentioned for strawberry jelly
2. Add 1 loth of tournesol
3. Let it come to a boil once with the beaten egg white and sugar, from which it obtains a beautiful red color
4. Remove it from the fire
5. Add half a bottle of the best rose water, the juice of 6 lemons, and half a bottle of good Rhine wine
