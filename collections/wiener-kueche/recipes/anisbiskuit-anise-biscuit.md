## Ingredients

- 4 eggs' weight sugar
- 5 fresh eggs
- juice and zest of ½ lemon
- 4 eggs' weight flour
- 5 dkg melted butter
- anise seeds

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs' weight of sugar with 5 fresh eggs in a snow basin with a whisk for 20 minutes until thick
2. Add juice and zest of ½ lemon
3. Fold in 4 eggs' weight of flour, 5 dkg melted (not hot) butter, and some anise seeds using a wooden spoon
4. Fill into a buttered, floured biscuit mold
5. Bake slowly
6. The next day, after turning out, cut into thin slices
7. Optionally place slices on a baking sheet and bake on both sides in an open oven to keep longer
