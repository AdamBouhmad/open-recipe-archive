## Ingredients

- 1 whole duck liver
- 16 deca of bacon
- 16 deca of pork or veal meat
- 6 deca of bread rolls softened in milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 8 deca of diced second half of duck liver
- 8 deca of truffles
- 8 deca of bacon

## Directions

1. Cut the duck liver, bacon, and pork or veal meat very finely
2. Place in a mortar and pound very finely with the bread rolls softened in milk and well pressed out, egg yolks, and whole egg
3. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
4. Cut the remaining duck liver, truffles, and bacon into fine dice
5. Add this to the passed meat, season, add spices, and mix well together
6. Fill the loosened skin of the poularde with this mixture and treat further as indicated in the preceding recipes
