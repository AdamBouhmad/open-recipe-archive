## Ingredients

- 12 beautiful quince pieces
- Water
- 1 pound strained quince puree
- Three quarter pound clarified sugar
- 2 glasses Malaga wine or Tokayer
- Zest of one lemon and juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Cook 12 beautiful quince pieces in water until soft
2. Let them drain and press through
3. For 1 pound strained quince, take three quarter pound clarified sugar, 2 glasses Malaga wine or Tokayer, the zest of one and the juice of two lemons
4. Press through once more and make the ice
