## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- flour
- onion
- broth
- white vinegar
- cream
- capers
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Dress the chickens nicely and blanch them
2. Warm a piece of butter, add two spoonfuls of flour, let it brown slightly
3. Add a whole onion and fill with broth
4. Let the sauce reduce and strain it through a sieve
5. Place the chickens in the sauce
6. Add one spoonful of good white vinegar, three spoonfuls of cream, a handful of capers, and finely cut lemon peel
7. Let it simmer until the chickens are completely cooked
8. Arrange the chickens on a dish and pour the sauce over
