## Ingredients

- Strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
- fresh cherries, pitted
- stems removed

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Apple Strudel (No. 1238)
2. Fill instead with apples with raw cherries, freed from stems
3. Treat further as indicated
