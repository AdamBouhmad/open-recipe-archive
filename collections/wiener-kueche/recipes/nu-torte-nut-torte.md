## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 10 dkg grated nuts
- 6 egg whites
- 6 dkg flour
- 6 dkg melted butter
- filling made from 15 dkg grated nuts, 2 spoons sugar, some spoons milk, some rum
- white glaze
- candied angelica stems, candied cherries, halved nuts for decoration

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar with egg yolks for 30 minutes
2. Mix in the grated nuts
3. Gently fold in the egg whites together with the flour
4. Stir in the melted but not hot butter
5. Fill the batter into a buttered and floured cake form and bake slowly over moderate heat
6. Bake the day before serving
7. The next day, turn it over and cut in half
8. Spread the filling on one layer, place the smooth layer on top, spread filling on the edge and coat the torte with white glaze
9. When dry, decorate with angelica stems, candied cherries and halved nuts
10. For the filling: mix grated nuts with sugar, some spoons of milk and some rum into a thick paste (whipped cream can be used instead of milk)
