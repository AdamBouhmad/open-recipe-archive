## Ingredients

- small rolls
- stewed apples
- sweetened red wine
- flour
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying
- any desired spice sugar or cream filling options: chocolate, vanilla, hazelnut cream

## Directions

1. Trim the rolls
2. Cut off a lid at the side
3. Remove the crumb from inside
4. Fill with stewed apples
5. Close with the cut-off lid
6. Dip in sweetened red wine
7. Dust with flour
8. Dip in beaten eggs
9. Sprinkle generously with bread crumbs
10. Fry in very hot fat
11. Arrange heaped in a dish and sprinkle with spice sugar
12. Alternatively fill with chocolate, vanilla, or hazelnut cream, close with lids, dip in sweetened milk, and proceed as above
13. If filled with chocolate cream, sprinkle with grated chocolate mixed with sugar when serving
