## Ingredients

- mass as for No. 1495
- whipped vanilla cream
- Spanish wind mass for decoration

## Directions

1. Prepare the same mass as described for the Walnut Cake (No. 1495), fill it into a ring form brushed with butter and dusted with flour and bake at low heat for 3/4 to 1 hour in the oven.
2. Turn the cake onto a sieve and allow to cool.
3. Arrange the cooled cake on a platter, fill the middle empty space with whipped vanilla cream and finish with the following decoration.
