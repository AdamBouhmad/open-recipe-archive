## Ingredients

- goose liver
- chopped onions
- butter
- bread, soaked in milk and squeezed out
- truffles, finely chopped
- fresh fat
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- herb seasonings (thyme, basil, marjoram)
- egg yolks
- one cup cream
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Cook the goose liver and grate it on a grater
2. Soften chopped onions in butter
3. Add milk-soaked and squeezed bread to the onions
4. Finely grind small chopped truffles with fresh fat in a grindstone and add to the mixture
5. Add salt, pepper, spices, and finely pounded sausage herbs (thyme, basil, marjoram) in moderate amounts
6. Beat several egg yolks as for a farce and add them
7. Pour in one cup of cream and stir everything together well
8. Fill into thoroughly cleaned pork intestines
9. Cook the sausages in milk
10. Place in paper casings brushed with butter
11. Roast on a grill and serve
