## Ingredients

- Two measures fresh water
- Two pounds lean beef
- One pound mutton or mutton feet
- One calf bone
- One old pigeon
- Two yellow turnips
- Two parsnip roots
- Two parsley roots
- Two celery stalks
- Leek
- One beautiful whole onion
- Several whole cloves
- A couple cloves garlic
- One bay leaf
- Salt

## Directions

1. Set a pot with two measures fresh water, two pounds lean beef, one pound mutton or mutton feet, a calf bone, and an old pigeon over the fire
2. When it has formed scum, add two yellow turnips, two parsnip roots, two parsley roots, two celery stalks, leek, and one beautiful whole onion
3. Let it simmer over low heat
4. Insert several whole cloves
5. Add a couple cloves garlic and one bay leaf
6. Cover the pot with a lid that fits well and seal it with plain dough
7. Let the soup simmer for three hours
8. Strain off the broth
9. Give it twice a day to drink for moistening
10. Salt as desired
