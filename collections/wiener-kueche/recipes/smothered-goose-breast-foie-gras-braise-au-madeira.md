## Ingredients

- 1 beautiful, large goose liver
- bacon slices
- cold milk
- salt
- pepper
- truffles, cut into thin slices
- 1/8 liter Madeira wine or white wine
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the bile from the goose liver and discard any green spots
2. Wash the goose liver and soak it in cold milk for 1/4 hour so that the milk covers it
3. Remove the liver, place it on cloth, dry it, and dust with salt
4. Using a knife, make 3-4 cuts across the upper side of the liver, about thumb-width apart, and insert thinly sliced truffles into these cuts
5. Line a casserole with bacon slices, place the liver on top, and cover with one sliced bacon slice
6. Place this casserole in a second casserole containing about 3 fingers depth of slowly simmering water
7. Let it stand covered for approximately 1.5 hours, adding 1/8 liter of Madeira or white wine after 1/3 hour and finely sliced truffles as desired
8. Remove the goose liver to a serving dish and garnish with truffle ragout, salmis, puff pastry, oyster dumplings, steamed rice, macaroni or similar
9. Serve the cooking liquid or a strong brown Madeira-wine sauce on the side
10. Alternatively, the liver can be steamed covered in the oven, but then add 3/4 liter of Madeira or white wine at the beginning and pay special attention during steaming as the liver burns easily at too high heat
