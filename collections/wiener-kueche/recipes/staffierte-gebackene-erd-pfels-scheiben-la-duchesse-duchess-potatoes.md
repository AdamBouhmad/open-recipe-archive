## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams fine butter
- 3 egg yolks
- approximately 1/2 kilogram mashed potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix 8 decagrams fine butter with 3 egg yolks thoroughly
2. Stir in approximately 1/2 kilogram mashed potatoes, along with salt and nutmeg to make a dough-like mass
3. Roll the mixture finger-thick on a board dusted with flour
4. Cut out shapes with a cutter dipped in flour
5. Place in very hot lard and let brown on both sides to a light golden color
6. The lard should be only finger-high in the pan so the potatoes do not fall apart
