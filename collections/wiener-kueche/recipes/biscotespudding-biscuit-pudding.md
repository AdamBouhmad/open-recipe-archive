## Ingredients

- 16 ounces hard biscuits or biscuit rusks
- dissolved marmalade for spreading
- 3/5 liter cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 ounces powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Use 16 ounces hard biscuits, laying 2 and 2 together with the lower sides facing up
2. Spread dissolved marmalade between them
3. Beat 3/5 liter cream, 6 egg yolks and 12 ounces powdered sugar until frothy
4. Soak the biscuits in this mixture
5. Lay them side by side in a buttered mold lined with flour
6. Pour the remaining liquid over
7. Cover immediately and steam for 3/4 hour
