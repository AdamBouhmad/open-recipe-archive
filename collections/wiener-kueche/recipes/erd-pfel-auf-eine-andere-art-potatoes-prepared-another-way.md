## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Boiling water
- Salt
- Butter or beef fat

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and cut them into pieces the size of a thumb.
2. Wash them thoroughly clean.
3. Pour boiling water over them in a suitable pot; salt them properly and add a piece of butter or beef fat, then let them cook slowly until soft.
4. Before they reach the proper point of doneness, melt a piece of butter or fat until the foam begins to fall away.
5. Quickly strain the potatoes, pour them into a bowl or casserole, and finish them with this butter.
