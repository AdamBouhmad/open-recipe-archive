## Ingredients

- 21 dkg butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 21 dkg sugar
- 21 dkg sifted hard curd cheese
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 21 dkg swelled grated almonds
- 7 dkg breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cream together 21 dkg butter
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks and 21 dkg sugar
3. Mix in 21 dkg sifted hard curd cheese, then fold in 6 stiff egg whites together with 21 dkg swelled grated almonds and 7 dkg breadcrumbs
4. Fill the batter into a buttered, floured torte form and bake at higher heat until light brown
