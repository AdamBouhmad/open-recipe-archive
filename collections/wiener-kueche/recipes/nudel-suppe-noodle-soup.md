## Ingredients

- flour to accommodate 2 eggs
- 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a well in flour that holds 2 eggs
2. Break eggs into well, add salt
3. Work dough with both hands or a wide knife until smooth
4. Cut into pieces and form into small strips
5. Let rest and dry covered
6. Roll very finely like paper
7. When dried, cut into 2-finger-wide strips, stack, and cut into fine noodles
8. Drop into boiling soup and cook 8-10 minutes
9. Serve soup
10. For noodles used as garnish rather than in soup, roll dough only knife-back thick and cut so noodles are round
