## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls
- 1 liter boiling milk
- juice of 1 orange
- 10 decagrams whipped butter
- 16 decagrams orange sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 14 decagrams peeled, finely pounded almonds
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Cook 8 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls in 1 liter boiling milk until well softened
2. Mix with the juice of 1 orange
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Beat 10 decagrams whipped butter with 16 decagrams orange sugar and 6 egg yolks gradually
5. Add 14 decagrams peeled, finely pounded almonds with the passed bread rolls
6. Mix well
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
8. Fill a buttered mold dusted with flour
9. Bake covered in steam for 1 to 1.5 hours
10. Serve with any desired sauce
