## Ingredients

- 16 Deka cherry sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 18 Deka white bread crumbs
- Butter for the mold
- Flour for dusting
- Stewed cherries

## Directions

1. Stir cherry sugar and 8 egg yolks together
2. Lightly mix this with the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 18 Deka white bread crumbs
3. Fill into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted cake mold
4. Top with stewed cherries, which then slowly sink in
5. Bake for ¾ hour in the oven
