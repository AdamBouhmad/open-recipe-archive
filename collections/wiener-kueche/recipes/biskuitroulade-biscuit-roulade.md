## Ingredients

- 7½ dkg sugar
- 4 whole eggs
- 7½ dkg flour
- 7½ dkg melted butter
- jam

## Directions

1. Beat 7½ dkg sugar with 4 whole eggs in a snow basin on the stovetop until thick
2. Mix in 7½ dkg flour
3. Quickly fold in 7½ dkg melted butter
4. Spread finger-thick onto a buttered, floured baking sheet
5. Bake in a moderately hot oven until light brown
6. Immediately brush with jam and roll up quickly, as the dough breaks when cooled
7. Return to the oven briefly so the roulade browns on top
