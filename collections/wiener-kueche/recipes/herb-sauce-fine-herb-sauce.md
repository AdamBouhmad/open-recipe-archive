## Ingredients

- about 1 handful spinach
- about the same amount of common soup herbs
- 4 pieces capers
- some tarragon
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons oil
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from about 1 handful of spinach and about the same amount of common soup herbs, wash the former, boil it in salt water until soft, strain the water and place in cold water and press out well
2. Chop it very finely with 4 pieces of capers, some tarragon and 1 tablespoon capers, pound the chopped mixture together with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks and pass through a sieve
3. Mix with 4 tablespoons oil, some vinegar and water and stir sugar and pepper to taste underneath
