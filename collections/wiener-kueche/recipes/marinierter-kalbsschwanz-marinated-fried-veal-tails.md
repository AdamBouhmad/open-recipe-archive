## Ingredients

- 3 veal tails
- salt water
- lemon juice
- green parsley
- thinly sliced Spanish onion
- 1 bay leaf
- salt
- spices
- egg batter or wine batter
- very hot fat

## Directions

1. After cutting 3 veal tails into 2-finger-wide pieces and cooking them until soft in salt water, remove them once they are completely cooked.
2. Place them in a bowl with lemon juice, green parsley, thinly sliced Spanish onion, 1 bay leaf, the necessary salt and spices and let stand 1 hour.
3. After this time, dip the pieces one after another in egg batter or wine batter and fry them in fingerless, very hot fat.
