## Ingredients

- 4-6 fresh eggs
- 2 rolls
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 4 dkg butter
- chopped parsley
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- 4-6 dkg breadcrumbs
- hot lard for frying
- spinach or cooked lettuce for serving

## Directions

1. Heat 4 dkg butter in a saucepan
2. Fry chopped parsley in it
3. Add milk-soaked, well-pressed rolls
4. Add 2 hard-boiled egg yolks and salt
5. Let cool
6. Fold in 2 stiff egg whites and 4-6 dkg breadcrumbs
7. Form into egg or croquette shapes on a floured board
8. Bread and fry in hot lard
9. Serve with spinach or cooked lettuce
