## Ingredients

- Woodcocks
- Wine
- Meat broth
- Butter
- Flour
- Woodcock liver, chopped
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the woodcocks, after they have been well cleaned and steamed in half wine and half meat broth, into two parts.
2. Make butter hot, toast flour in it until beautifully brown, place the cut woodcocks in it, stir the chopped liver and what else is reckoned to woodcock ragout into the roux, pour over the wine and meat broth in which the woodcocks were cooked, season with cloves, pepper, nutmeg and salt, add some mushrooms, and let it come to a boil.
