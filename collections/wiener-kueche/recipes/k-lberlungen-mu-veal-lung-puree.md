## Ingredients

- One veal lung
- Sugar candy equal in weight to the lung

## Directions

1. Place the veal lung and sugar candy together in a clean, well-sealed pot
2. Set this pot in a kettle full of water
3. Let it cook for three to four hours until it becomes like a juice or puree
4. Take three spoonfuls each morning and evening
