## Ingredients

- the young of a hare
- some blood
- 6 dkg root vegetables
- cold water
- salt
- vinegar
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 1 onion
- 2 bay leaves
- thyme
- 6 dkg fat
- 2 pieces sugar
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Wash the hare or, if freshly killed, place in cold water with salt, vinegar, cleaned root vegetables cut into thin slices, peppercorns, spice berries, onion, bay leaves, and thyme.
2. Cook for 1½ hours.
3. In hot fat, brown 2 pieces of sugar, make a dark roux with flour, and pour the strained broth over it.
4. Piece the hare, add with the blood to the sauce, and simmer until cooked.
5. Serve with bread, semolina, or noodle dumplings.
