## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 14 Deca vanilla sugar
- signoli or blanched, finely cut almonds, or similarly prepared pistachios
- finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites until stiff.
2. Lightly mix in 14 Deca vanilla sugar.
3. Using a pastry bag, syringe, or coffee spoon, pipe sharp-pointed mounds onto a baking sheet coated with unsalted bacon fat.
4. Top the points with signoli or blanched, finely cut almonds, or similar prepared pistachios.
5. Sprinkle the whole with finely sifted sugar and let dry slowly in a moderately warm oven.
