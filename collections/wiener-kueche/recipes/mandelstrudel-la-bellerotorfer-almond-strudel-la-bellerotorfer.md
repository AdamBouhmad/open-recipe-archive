## Ingredients

- 28 decagrams flour
- 16 decagrams vanilla or orange sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 decagrams peeled, finely ground almonds with 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry from 28 decagrams flour as described in the puff pastry instructions (No. 1045) and let it rest in a cool place.
2. Meanwhile, place 16 decagrams of vanilla or orange sugar in a deep bowl, add 8 egg yolks one after another, stirring well, then mix with 16 decagrams of peeled almonds very finely ground with 3 egg whites.
