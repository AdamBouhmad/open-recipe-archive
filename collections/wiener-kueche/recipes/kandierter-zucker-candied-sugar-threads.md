## Ingredients

- sugar, cooked to the sixth stage
- 1/2 teaspoon finely ground platinum or cream of tartar
- almond oil
- alum or cream of tartar for cooking

## Directions

1. Cook sugar until it reaches the sixth stage (hard crack stage), at which point add 1/2 teaspoon finely ground platinum or cream of tartar
2. Place a few drops of this sugar onto a form brushed with almond oil
3. Draw a thread from this sugar, winding it around the form like a glaze
4. When the sugar begins to set on a spoon, warm it again
5. Alum or cream of tartar should be cooked with the sugar from the beginning so that the thread for spinning lasts longer
