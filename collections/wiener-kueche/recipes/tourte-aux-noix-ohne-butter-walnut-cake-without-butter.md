## Ingredients

- 28 teaspoons egg whites
- 12 egg yolks
- 8-10 teaspoons pounded and passed hazelnuts
- 16 teaspoons pounded walnuts
- 1 pinch grated coffee
- 1 tablet grated chocolate
- 8 egg whites
- powdered sugar and pounded nuts or Spanish wind mass for filling and decoration

## Directions

1. Beat 28 teaspoons of egg whites with 12 egg yolks for 1 2 hours, add 8-10 teaspoons pounded and passed hazelnuts, 16 teaspoons pounded walnuts, 1 pinch grated coffee and 1 tablet grated chocolate, stir well together and lastly lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites.
2. Divide this mass into two equal parts, which are filled into cake forms brushed with butter and dusted with flour and baked for 3/4 to 1 hour in a well-heated oven, then cool on a sieve.
3. When this is done, trim the edges from both cakes that were on the upper side during baking, brush with desired filling, place one on the other and cover with desired glaze or decorate with Spanish wind mass, in which case allow it to dry a few minutes in the open oven.
4. As desired, instead of filling with pastry cream, the stiffly beaten snow of 2 egg whites may be lightly mixed with 8 teaspoons sugar, 1 tablet grated chocolate and 1 tablespoon pounded nuts and used to fill the cake.
