## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- 3 tablespoons butter
- Salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- Lemon juice (optional)
- 1 tablespoon white breadcrumbs
- 1 spoon soup stock

## Directions

1. Soak the calf's brain in cold water for several minutes, then peel it
2. Place 3 tablespoons butter in a casserole and heat it
3. Add the peeled brain and sprinkle with necessary salt on top
4. Knead 3 tablespoons butter with 1/2 teaspoon finely cut green parsley and, if desired, some lemon juice, and place on the brain
5. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon white breadcrumbs
6. Place in the oven and cook 10-15 minutes at high heat; the brain should completely contract during this time
7. Using a serving spoon, place the brain on a dish and pour the sauce, which has been mixed with 1 spoon of soup stock, from the side
8. Garnish with lemon slices
