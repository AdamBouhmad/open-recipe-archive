## Ingredients

- arbitrary quantity of cold cracklings (rendered lard)
- 1 pinch of salt
- paprika (optional)
- 1 whole egg or 1 egg yolk
- wine, rum or schnapps
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Place an arbitrary quantity of cold cracklings on a board and cut them very finely with a knife
2. Add 1 pinch of salt and if desired 1 pinch of paprika
3. Beat in 1 whole egg or 1 egg yolk, wine, rum or schnapps
4. Work it with the necessary flour into a fairly stiff dough and let it rest for 1/2 hour
5. Roll out the dough 1 or 2 fingers thick and cut out with a round cutter of arbitrary size
6. Place these gatscherfln on a baking sheet covered with buttered paper or brushed with butter
7. Cut in several times on top with a knife, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with coarse salt, and bake at stone-hot temperature
