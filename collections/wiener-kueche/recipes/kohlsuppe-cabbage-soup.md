## Ingredients

- 2 smaller heads cabbage
- 1 bread roll
- 6 dkg butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove bad leaves and stalks from the cabbage and wash well.
2. Place in a pot with boiling salt water, cook uncovered for 20 minutes.
3. Strain and pass through a sieve.
4. Make a roux with 6 dkg butter, pour the cabbage water over it, add the passed cabbage, cook for 15 minutes, optionally thicken, and serve with bread rolls.
