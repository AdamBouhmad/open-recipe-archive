## Ingredients

- half a measure cream
- quarter pound sugar
- lemon
- 4 bitter macaroons
- 4 biscuits
- orange blossom
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place half a measure cream with quarter pound sugar rubbed with a lemon on charcoal fire and cook
2. Pound 4 bitter macaroons and 4 biscuits very fine
3. Add some orange blossom
4. Beat 8 egg yolks well
5. Stir in the rest
6. Pass the cream through a hair sieve into bowls
7. Cook in a double boiler or water bath
