## Ingredients

- butter dough
- bratwurst, roasted or cold
- eggs

## Directions

1. Strongly knife-back-thick rolled-out butter dough is cut into strips 2 fingers wide and once again as long, and these are then brushed with beaten eggs so far that at the edge where it was cut, a knife-back-width margin remains dry.
2. From roasted or cold bratwurst, the skin is removed, the filling is laid lengthwise in the middle of the egg-brushed strips at equal distance from the edges, the dough is now folded over a small-finger's breadth, and a cut edge must come to lie on top.
3. The roulades are then brushed with beaten eggs, at the edge however where the dough overlaps, a knife-back-width margin is left dry, and the roulades are placed on a water-moistened baking sheet across its width so that the upper-lying margin with the cut side is turned toward the back wall of the oven.
4. The roulade pâtés are then placed in the oven and baked yellow at fairly strong heat.
