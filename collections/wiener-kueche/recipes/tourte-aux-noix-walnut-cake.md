## Ingredients

- 24 teaspoons finely pounded walnuts
- 24 teaspoons finely pounded sugar
- 24 teaspoons peeled and passed almonds pounded with shell
- 1 pinch grated coffee
- 4 teaspoons white bread crumbs
- 1 1/6 liter boiling water
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 whole eggs
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix together 24 teaspoons finely pounded walnuts, 24 teaspoons finely pounded sugar, 24 teaspoons peeled and passed almonds pounded with shell and 1 pinch grated coffee.
2. Take 6 teaspoons of this mass, place it with 4 teaspoons white bread crumbs in a casserole, pour with 1 1/6 liter boiling water and let it boil for several minutes with constant stirring, then set aside and let cool.
3. In the meantime, mix the remaining mass, which is placed in a deep pan, gradually with 6 egg yolks and 4 whole eggs, and lastly lightly fold in the snow of 6 egg whites.
4. Divide this mass into two equal parts, which are filled one by one into cake forms brushed with butter and dusted with flour and baked at low heat for 1 2 hours in the oven.
5. After this time, place the cakes on a sieve and allow to cool, then spread one cake with the aforementioned cooked but cold filling, cover with the other cake, and frost or sprinkle with sugar as desired.
6. This baked cake mass may be cut out with any desired forms or cut into pieces the width and length of a finger.
