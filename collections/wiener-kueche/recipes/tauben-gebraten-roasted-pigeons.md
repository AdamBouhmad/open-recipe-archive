## Ingredients

- young fleshy pigeons
- butter
- semmel (bread roll), one and a half
- milk
- parsley, finely cut
- saffron
- salt
- two eggs and one yolk

## Directions

1. Blow the pigeons up with a feather quill, pluck and clean them well, salt them, and let them lie for half an hour
2. For the filling for two pigeons: beat a piece of butter until fluffy
3. Rub the crust from one and a half semmels, soak the crumb in milk, press it out well, add finely cut green parsley, a little saffron and salt, stir well together
4. Beat in two eggs and one yolk, stir for a while
5. Fill the pigeons with this mixture through a funnel, tie them up, thread them on a thin spit
6. Roast them for half an hour, frequently basting with butter during roasting
