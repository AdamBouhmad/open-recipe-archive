## Ingredients

- tongue
- salt water
- bacon sheets
- one large onion
- one yellow carrot
- whole allspice
- veal bones
- flour
- red wine
- broth
- 3 to 4 loth of gooseberries
- 3 loth of blanched and julienned almonds

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in salt water until tender, remove the skin, and place it in a casserole; add bacon sheets, a large onion, a yellow carrot cut into slices, some whole allspice and some veal bones; let all of this braise on a moderate fire until it becomes nicely brown.
2. Dust a good spoonful of flour over it, let it brown, fill it halfway with red wine and halfway with broth, and let it cook well; strain the sauce through a sieve into another casserole, add 3 to 4 loth of cleaned gooseberries and 3 loth of blanched and julienned almonds, place the tongue in it, and let it come to a good boil.
3. Before serving, lard the tongue throughout with julienned almonds, arrange the sauce on the dish, and place the tongue on top.
