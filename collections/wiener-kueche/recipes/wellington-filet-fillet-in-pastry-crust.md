## Ingredients

- 1 to 1.20 kg tenderloin
- 10 dkg bacon
- 3 mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- hot fat
- 15 dkg flour
- 15 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- water
- fines herbes
- egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare and roast the meat as for English Fillet, but it must be finished 1 hour before serving and allowed to cool completely.
2. Make a butter dough from 15 dkg flour, 15 dkg butter, 1 egg, salt, 2 tablespoons vinegar, and water.
3. Roll out the dough into a rectangle, not too thin.
4. Place the cooled meat in the center and brush with fines herbes.
5. Brush the dough with egg white and wrap it around the meat from all sides.
6. Place seam side down on a baking sheet and brush the top with beaten egg.
7. Cut decorative shapes from remaining thin dough, brush with egg, and arrange on top of the meat.
8. Bake at high heat until light brown.
9. Slice thinly and arrange on a long platter.
10. Serve with mushroom sauce on the side.
