## Ingredients

- Turtle
- Salt water
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Lemon peel
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the turtles very soft in salt water, open the shells, remove the turtles, divide them into four parts, and carefully preserve the juice found in the shell.
2. Carefully separate the gall from the liver so it does not burst.
3. Lay some pieces of butter in the turtle shell, add the juice found in it, then lay the cleaned turtle pieces back in, add some bread crumbs, some pieces of butter, chopped lemon peel, and the juice of a lemon.
4. Place them in a pan, apply heat from below and above, let them boil, and they are done.
