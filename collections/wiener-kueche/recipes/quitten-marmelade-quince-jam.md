## Ingredients

- Quince apples or pears, split
- Quince, peeled and rubbed with lemon juice
- Cold water then boiling lemon water
- 1 kilo quince, 1/2 kilo sugar and 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Quince apples or pears are cut in splits, the core removed, quinces peeled, each piece rubbed with lemon juice
2. Then thrown into cold, then into boiling lemon water and therein stewed fairly soft
3. Then put the quinces in boiling quince water (1 kilo quinces, 1/2 kilo sugar and 1/2 liter water)
4. When they are then quite soft cooked, they are quickly passed through a sieve, allowed to come to boil again and filled, still hot, into jars
5. Should the quinces have lost their color, add a few drops of cochineal at the last cooking
6. One can also let some pieces of orange blossoms steep with it
