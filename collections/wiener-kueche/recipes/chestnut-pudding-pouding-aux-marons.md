## Ingredients

- 32 decagrams chestnuts
- 6 decagrams butter
- 3 decagrams flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 8 decagrams finely pounded sugar
- 6 decagrams blanched raisins
- 4 decagrams crushed almond paste
- 12 decagrams finely diced marrow
- 10 pieces lengthwise cut dates
- 5 dissolved nuts
- 4 tablespoons rum
- 4 tablespoons cream
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Roast chestnuts, peel, divide in half
2. Pound 1 half with 6 decagrams butter, pass through a sieve
3. Mix with 3 decagrams flour, 3 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, 8 decagrams finely pounded sugar, 6 decagrams blanched raisins, 4 decagrams crushed almond paste, 12 decagrams finely diced marrow, 10 lengthwise cut dates (pits removed), 5 dissolved nuts, 4 tablespoons rum, and 4 tablespoons cream
4. Mix well
5. Cut the remaining chestnut half into pieces and fold in
6. Fill a buttered mold dusted with flour
7. Bake covered in steam for 1 hour
