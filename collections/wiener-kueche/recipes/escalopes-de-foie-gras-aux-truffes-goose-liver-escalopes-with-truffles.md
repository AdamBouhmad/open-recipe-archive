## Ingredients

- goose liver
- butter
- truffles
- morel mushrooms
- blanched mushrooms
- asparagus tips
- Madeira sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare these escalopes as in the previous recipe.
2. Sprinkle the liver pieces in the casserole with 32 decagrams of peeled, thinly sliced truffles.
3. Let these roast and serve alongside.
4. Small morel mushrooms, blanched mushrooms, and asparagus tips can be arranged decoratively alongside and Madeira sauce added.
5. These escalopes can also be arranged in a baked vol-au-vent or richly garnished with puff pastry.
