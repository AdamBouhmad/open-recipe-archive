## Ingredients

- 1 poularde (prepared as in Poularde in White Mussel Sauce)
- 12 oysters, shelled
- 8 decagrams butter
- 4 tablespoons wine vinegar crumbs
- 1/2 liter milk cream
- juice of 1 lemon
- oyster liquor
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Place shelled oysters in a casserole containing 8 decagrams of butter
2. Let them braise covered briefly
3. Add 4 tablespoons of wine vinegar crumbs
4. Once these have begun to heat, add 1/2 liter of milk cream, the juice of one lemon, and the oyster liquor
5. Season to taste and let simmer well
6. If the sauce is too thick, thin it with the liquid in which the poularde was braised
7. Cut the gently braised poularde into pieces of choice
8. Pour the oysters with their sauce over the poularde
9. For a brown oyster sauce, add some dissolved meat glaze to the strained sauce and let it simmer well
