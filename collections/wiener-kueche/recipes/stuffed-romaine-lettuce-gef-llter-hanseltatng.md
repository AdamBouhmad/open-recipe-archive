## Ingredients

- Romaine lettuce heads
- Ground meat of choice
- Crayfish
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Onions
- Herbs
- Fresh parsley
- Seasoning
- Salt
- Twine
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Leaf through selected beautiful heads after they have been well washed, and fill them with a forcemeat made of ground meat of choice, crayfish, bread crumbs, butter, onions, herbs, fresh parsley, seasoning and salt.
2. Bind them with twine and place them with a piece of butter in a casserole, into which you also pour meat broth, and cook the lettuce in it.
3. Finally make a sauce from meat broth, crayfish, crayfish butter, marrow, onions and herbs, cook the lettuce in it thoroughly and serve it to table.
