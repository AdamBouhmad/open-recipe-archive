## Ingredients

- 50-60 dkg calf's liver
- salt
- bread crumbs
- hot lard
- cooked lettuce, spinach, or potato salad

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the liver, wash, and cut into slices.
2. Salt just before frying.
3. Coat in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in hot lard.
5. Serve with cooked lettuce, spinach, or potato salad.
