## Ingredients

- 15 dkg butter
- 15 dkg sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 15 dkg softened chocolate
- 5 egg whites
- 15 dkg raw grated almonds
- 6 dkg rum-moistened breadcrumbs
- jam
- brown glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 15 dkg butter and gradually add 15 dkg sugar, 5 egg yolks, and 15 dkg softened chocolate
2. Fold in 5 stiff egg whites together with 15 dkg raw grated almonds and 6 dkg rum-moistened breadcrumbs
3. Fill the mixture into the form
4. Bake at higher heat
5. Fill with jam and ice brown on top
