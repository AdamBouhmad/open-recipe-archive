## Ingredients

- Veal
- Warm water
- Salt
- Unripe gooseberries
- A couple of lightly baked bread slices
- Meat broth
- A little vinegar
- Ginger
- Pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Butter
- Cream (Schmette)

## Directions

1. Set the meat with warm water over the fire, salt it, skim it clean, and let it simmer gently until tender.
2. Meanwhile, put unripe gooseberries with a couple of lightly baked bread slices in a small pot, pour meat broth and a little vinegar over them, and let them simmer until quite soft.
3. Press them through a strainer, pour meat broth over them again, season with ginger, pepper, salt and grated nutmeg.
4. Add more gooseberries and several spoonfuls of cream, and let them simmer until soft.
5. Add butter and serve over the meat.
