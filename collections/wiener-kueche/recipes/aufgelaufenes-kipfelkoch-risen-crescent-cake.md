## Ingredients

- 4 kipfel or horns or other tender baked goods
- milk
- 4 loth butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- small raisins, cleaned and washed
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut 4 kipfel or horns or other tender baked goods into cubes and soak in milk
2. Beat 4 loth butter
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks
4. Mix this with well-cleaned and washed small raisins, sugar and cinnamon
5. Butter a pan well
6. Pour everything in and bake with heat from above and below
