## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- milk
- 20 eggs (only half the whites)
- half pound sugar
- 3 loth cinnamon, finely pounded

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the rice with milk
2. Stir it with 20 eggs (using only half the whites) in a mixing bowl (Weidling) without butter
3. Mix this with half pound sugar and 3 loth finely pounded cinnamon
4. Stir everything together thoroughly
5. Place the rice on a tin plate, pour the stirred mixture into it, and bake it slowly
