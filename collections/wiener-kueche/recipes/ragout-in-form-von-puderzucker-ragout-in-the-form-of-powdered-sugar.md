## Ingredients

- Ragout mixture (from Ragout Croquettes recipe)
- Flour for dusting
- Water-softened wafers
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Take the mixture used for Ragout Croquettes and place it on a flour-dusted board
2. Cut it into pieces 1 finger long and 2 fingers thick
3. Give these pieces a sugar hat shape by rolling them
4. Wrap them in water-softened wafers, dip them in beaten, whipped eggs, roll them in breadcrumbs and fry them golden in very hot lard
5. Remove with a slotted spoon, place them on blotting paper, arrange them on a napkin in a bowl and serve as garnish at table
