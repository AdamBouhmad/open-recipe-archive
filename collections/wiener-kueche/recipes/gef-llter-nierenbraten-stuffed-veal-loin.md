## Ingredients

- 16 deca lard
- 4 deca rolls softened in cold water and well wrung out
- 3 pieces cleaned and washed mushrooms
- 1 small piece onion
- 6 bunches fresh parsley
- 5 pieces cleaned crayfish
- 1 small veal loin with the sinews removed
- Twine
- Wood shavings
- Hot fat
- Broth
- Aspic glaze

## Directions

1. Cut the lard, rolls, mushrooms, onion, parsley, and crayfish very finely with a knife
2. After the veal loin is sufficiently trimmed, brush the inside with the finely chopped filling
3. Roll it together, turning the thick part inward and rolling the meat toward the thin part
4. Bind it with twine and place it on wood shavings in a roasting pan, positioning it so that the end of the backbone faces upward
5. Cover the meat with some warm fat and after 1/2 hour pour some broth alongside the roast
6. After the meat has roasted for 1.5 hours with frequent turning and basting, remove it
7. Loosen the twine, cut the meat into pieces, coat them with dissolved aspic glaze, and arrange on a platter
