## Ingredients

- Peel of one orange
- Juice of 4 oranges
- 8 Loth sugar
- Half a Seidel water
- 5 eggs (whites separated)
- 5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the peel from one orange very thinly and place it in a casserole
2. Press the juice of 4 oranges into the casserole
3. Mix in 8 Loth sugar and half a Seidel water
4. Beat the whites of 5 eggs into stiff snow and stir slowly into the casserole
5. Set on a moderate coal fire and stir the mixture until it thickens
6. Pass everything through a hair sieve and let it cool
7. Beat 5 egg yolks and pour them with the above cream into a casserole
8. Set on a small coal fire and stir until it almost begins to boil
9. Let it cool, fill cream cups with it, and serve
