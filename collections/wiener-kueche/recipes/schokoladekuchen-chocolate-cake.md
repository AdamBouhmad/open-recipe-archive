## Ingredients

- 12 dkg butter
- 12 dkg sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 12 dkg softened or grated chocolate
- 4 egg whites
- 12 dkg flour
- butter and flour for the pan
- jam or sweetened whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cream together 12 dkg butter
2. Gradually mix in 12 dkg sugar with 4 egg yolks
3. Mix in 12 dkg softened or grated chocolate
4. Fold in 4 stiffly beaten egg whites together with 12 dkg flour
5. Spread finger-thick on a buttered and floured baking sheet
6. Bake at lower heat
7. While still warm, cut into rectangles
8. Cool completely, then sandwich 2 and 2 together with jam or sweetened whipped cream
