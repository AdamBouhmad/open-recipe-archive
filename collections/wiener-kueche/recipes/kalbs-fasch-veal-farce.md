## Ingredients

- 1 kilo veal from the knuckle
- 16 deca bread rolls softened in milk
- 12 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- some salt

## Directions

1. Pound 1 kilo veal from the knuckle finely in a mortar
2. Add 16 deca bread rolls softened in milk and well pressed out, 12 deca butter, 4 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, and some salt
3. Pound everything together well, pass through a sieve, stir well together, and use as needed
4. Can be used to form roulades, quenelles, dumplings, and cutlets for ragout
