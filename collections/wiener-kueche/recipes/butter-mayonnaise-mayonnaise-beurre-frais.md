## Ingredients

- 6 teaspoons butter
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 1/16 to 1 1/4 liters fine oil
- juice of 2 oranges or 2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt to taste
- 1 knife tip white pepper
- mustard or herbs optionally

## Directions

1. Beat 6 teaspoons of butter in a deep bowl until very fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 4 raw egg yolks, beat both together for 10 minutes and then place on ice
3. Stir in 1/16 to 1 1/4 liters of fine oil drop by drop along with the juice of 2 oranges or 2 tablespoons of vinegar
4. Stir in the necessary salt together with 1 knife tip white pepper
5. Keep uncovered on ice until use
6. Before use, mustard or herbs can be stirred in as mentioned for the previously described mayonnaise
