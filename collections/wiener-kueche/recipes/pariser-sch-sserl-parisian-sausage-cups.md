## Ingredients

- 25-35 dkg Parisian sausage
- 30 h spinach
- hot fat
- 1 fried egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Carefully slice the sausage so the skin is not torn, otherwise no cup will form.
2. Quickly sear the slices on both sides in hot fat, remove them.
3. Fill with prepared spinach, optionally top with 1 fried egg.
