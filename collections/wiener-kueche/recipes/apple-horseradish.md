## Ingredients

- 2 apples
- 6 tablespoons horseradish
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the apples
2. Grate the horseradish as well
3. Mix with salt, a little sugar, vinegar, and cold water
4. Let stand covered in a porcelain bowl until serving
