## Ingredients

- one fine Ortscherzel (beef cut)
- ham, cut lengthwise and in thickness of a small finger
- bacon, cut lengthwise and in thickness of a small finger
- some green parsley, finely chopped
- some shallots, finely chopped
- some mushrooms, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut some green parsley, shallots, and some mushrooms finely together, add some salt and pepper, and mix everything well together
2. Wrap the cut pieces of ham and bacon in this mixture
3. Lard the prepared meat with these pieces and place it in a casserole so that the piece of meat lies comfortably in it
4. Place leafwise into the casserole first
