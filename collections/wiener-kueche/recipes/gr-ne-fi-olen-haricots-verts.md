## Ingredients

- green kidney beans
- salt water
- green parsley
- butter sauce
- salt
- white pepper
- lemon juice (optional)
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Break off both ends of the green kidney beans and remove the strings from the sides
2. Cut lengthwise or diagonally as desired, or break into pieces
3. Cook in salt water and drain well
4. Place in cold water and let stand
5. In a butter sauce, roast some finely chopped green parsley
6. Add the cooked green kidney beans
7. Season with salt and white pepper and bring to a boil
8. Can also be soured with lemon juice and sweetened with 1 spoonful of sugar if desired
