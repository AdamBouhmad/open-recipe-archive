## Ingredients

- 4 pounds veal from the leg, skinned and chopped
- 2 pounds bacon, chopped
- bread soaked in milk, rubbed and squeezed out
- finely chopped onions
- shallots
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- 6 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Chop 4 pounds veal from the leg and 2 pounds bacon very finely
2. Add bread that has been soaked in milk, rubbed, and squeezed out
3. Sweat finely chopped onions and shallots in butter
4. Add salt, pepper, spices, and fine herbs
5. Pound everything together with 6 whole eggs in a mortar
6. Ensure the filling is well seasoned, as cold foods require more seasoning
7. This filling can be used for all kinds of cold pastries
