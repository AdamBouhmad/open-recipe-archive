## Ingredients

- 1 rinsed herring (Pomeranian)
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 peeled Manzanilla apples with seeds removed
- 4 ounces grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out
- some shallots
- 5 juniper berries
- 1 pinch pepper
- 2 tablespoons oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Skin one rinsed herring and remove the bones
2. Place 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, 2 peeled Manzanilla apples with seeds removed, 4 ounces of grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out, some shallots, and 5 juniper berries in a mortar
3. Pound everything together with the herring and the milt finely and pass through a sieve
4. Stir the sieved mixture with 1 pinch of pepper, 2 tablespoons oil, and the necessary vinegar and serve this sauce with cold roasted meat or any roasts
