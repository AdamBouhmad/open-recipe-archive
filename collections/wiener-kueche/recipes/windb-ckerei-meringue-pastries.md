## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 5 dkg fine vanilla sugar
- 10 dkg sugar
- butter for sheet
- optional: a few drops of food coloring, coffee, or finely sifted chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg whites very stiff
2. Mix in 5 dkg fine vanilla sugar
3. Beat both together for 10 minutes
4. Stir in 10 dkg sugar
5. Fill the mixture into a piping bag with a star tip
6. Pipe rings and small puffs onto a buttered baking sheet
7. Sprinkle heavily with sugar
8. Place in the oven to dry
9. Only glowing coals should be in the oven
10. For colored pastries, add a few drops of food coloring, coffee, or finely sifted chocolate to the sugared meringue
