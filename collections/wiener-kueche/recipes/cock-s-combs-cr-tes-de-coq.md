## Ingredients

- chicken combs
- water
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Take chicken combs that have been trimmed slightly at the tips and place them in lukewarm water so they become white
2. Once this is achieved, place them again in lukewarm water, remove them and lay them on a cloth
3. Salt them well and rub with the cloth until the fine skin comes off
4. Place them in cold water and keep them at a warm place until the water becomes lukewarm
5. Repeat this until the combs are completely white
6. Place 1 3/4 liters of water, 4 ounces of butter and the juice of 1 lemon in a casserole, add the chicken combs and bring to a boil only once
7. Use them for various ragouts
