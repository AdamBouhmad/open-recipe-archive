## Ingredients

- veal leg
- slices of ham and bacon
- thinly sliced onion
- 1 bay leaf
- white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. After the veal leg has been trimmed as previously indicated, wiped with a cloth and blanched, pound it, salt it and, if desired, season it with white pepper.
2. Place several slices of ham and bacon and thinly sliced onion along with 1 bay leaf in a casserole, place the leg on top and braise it well covered in the oven at moderate heat, turning it frequently, until soft.
3. This must, however, be done at moderate heat so that the liquid released does not burn.
4. When the leg is soft enough, arrange it on a platter and serve the defatted sauce with it; if too little sauce is present, it is increased with some soup.
