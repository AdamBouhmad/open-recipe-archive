## Ingredients

- water
- butter
- lemon juice
- small firm mushrooms
- salt

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 liter of boiling water, 4 ounces of butter and the juice of 1 lemon in a casserole and bring to a boil
2. Cut away the earthy stem from 10 small firm mushrooms and carefully separate the stem from the cap
3. Peel the caps smooth and finely, peel the stems, place them in cold water and then with the stems and a pinch of salt in the broth described above
4. Bring to a boil once and set aside covered for use
5. Use the peels for soup or for a sauce
