## Ingredients

- 32 deka rum sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 32 deka almonds with skin, finely chopped and sifted
- 8 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 8 deka bread crumbs
- 6 deka flour
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put 32 deka rum sugar and 8 egg yolks in a deep bowl
2. Beat together for 1/2 hour
3. Mix in 32 deka almonds with skin, finely chopped and sifted
4. Lightly fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from 8 eggs simultaneously with 8 deka bread crumbs, 6 deka flour, 1 tablespoon cinnamon, and nutmeg
5. Fill a butter-greased and flour-dusted aniseed bread mold with this mass
6. Bake slowly in the oven at low heat
7. When done, remove and let cool completely
8. Cut into thick knife-blade-wide slices
9. Cover with desired glaze and let dry
