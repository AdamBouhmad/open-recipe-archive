## Ingredients

- 3 Pfund cornmeal
- 10 Pfund boiling water
- 4 Pfund syrup or sugar
- linen bag

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, stir 3 pounds cornmeal with 10 pounds boiling water
2. Mix in 4 pounds syrup or sugar at the same time
3. Pour the entire mixture into a linen bag, tie it so there is enough room for it to swell
4. Cook it in a pot with boiling water for 6 hours
5. Replace the evaporated water constantly with boiling water
6. When removed, the pudding weighs 10 pounds
7. It must not fall apart
8. The linen bag or napkin should be dipped in hot water beforehand
9. The cooking must never stop
