## Ingredients

- 10 ounces flour
- 1.5 liters milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 ounces granulated sugar
- 8 egg whites, whipped

## Directions

1. Gradually whisk flour into cold milk
2. Add egg yolks and granulated sugar one by one
3. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites
4. Fill a butter-greased, flour-lined mold
5. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour or bake in the oven
