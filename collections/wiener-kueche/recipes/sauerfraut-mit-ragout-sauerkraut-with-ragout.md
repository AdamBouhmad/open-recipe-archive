## Ingredients

- 1 heaping plate of sauerkraut
- approximately 1 liter fatty broth
- approximately 10 tablespoons fat
- 1 kilo prepared fish or pork
- 2 crushed shallots or 1 piece garlic
- 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- 8 tablespoons fat
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter white wine
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter cream
- salt
- a little lemon juice if necessary
- bread crumbs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 1/6 liters sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1 heaping plate of sauerkraut with approximately 1 liter fatty broth and approximately 10 tablespoons fat gently in a covered pot
2. Remove the bones from 1 kilo prepared fish or pork, break into pieces
3. Let 2 crushed shallots or 1 piece garlic with 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley roast in 8 tablespoons fat, add 2 tablespoons flour to it, pour it with 1/4 liter white wine and let it cook well
4. In a pot, put 4 egg yolks and 1/4 liter cream, bubble together and pour the cooked sauce to the cream
5. Add the fish or meat and the sauce together in a casserole with some salt and, if necessary, a little lemon juice, stir well together and let it come to a boil
6. If there is still liquid present, it must be strained from the sauerkraut
7. Place a portion of the sauerkraut in a deep bowl, make a depression and place the fish ragout in it, fold the sauerkraut lightly over it and sprinkle with bread crumbs, pour 2 egg yolks mixed with 1 1/6 liters sour cream over it and place in the open oven for 1/3 to 3/4 hours
8. Instead of fish, one can use roasted pork, cooked ham, or roasted meat
9. The procedure remains the same
10. If desired, sprinkle with finely cut chives when serving
