## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Pig liver
- Blanched bacon
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Capers
- Anchovy, soaked
- Garlic, 2 cloves
- Fine herbs
- Provençal oil

## Directions

1. Scald the suckling pig well and clean it
2. Finely chop the liver and cook it in a casserole with blanched bacon, truffles, mushrooms, capers, soaked anchovy, garlic cloves, and fine herbs
3. Season the mixture
4. Stuff the cavity of the suckling pig with this filling
5. Bind it closed
6. Roast it on a spit
7. Baste it with Provençal oil during roasting
