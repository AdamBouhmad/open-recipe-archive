## Ingredients

- 0.5 loth vanilla
- 1.5 measures sweet cream
- 18 egg yolks
- 0.75 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the vanilla into small pieces
2. Cook the sweet cream with the vanilla
3. Beat the cream with 18 egg yolks
4. Clarify three-quarters pound of sugar and mix it into the cream
5. Beat everything well so it binds together
6. Pass through a fine sieve
7. Stir until cooled so no skin forms
8. Pour into the freezing container
