## Ingredients

- 12 Deca butter
- A knife-point of green parsley
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 liter semolina
- 1 liter flour
- 16 Deca cooked, passed potato
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deca butter until fluffy
2. Mix in a knife-point of green parsley, 6 egg yolks, 1 liter semolina and the same amount of flour
3. Add 16 Deca cooked, passed potato and some nutmeg
4. Stir everything well together and lightly mix in the snow from 6 eggs
5. Bake as with Griesschöberl
