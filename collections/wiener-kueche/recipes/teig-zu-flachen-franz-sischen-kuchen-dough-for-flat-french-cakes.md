## Ingredients

- 56 Deka flour
- 32 Deka butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 16 Deka finely sifted sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest
- 5 egg yolks
- approximately 1/8 liter cream

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board
2. Cut in 32 Deka butter and rub fine with hands
3. Make a well in the center
4. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt, 16 Deka finely sifted sugar, 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest, 5 egg yolks and approximately 1/8 liter cream into the well
5. Mix well with a knife and work into a fairly firm, smooth dough like strudel dough
6. Place on a flour-dusted board, cover with a cloth and let rest one hour in a cool place
7. After this time, roll out strongly knife-back thick on a flour-dusted board and use as desired
