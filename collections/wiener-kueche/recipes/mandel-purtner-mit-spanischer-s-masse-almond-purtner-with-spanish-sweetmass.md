## Ingredients

- mass as prepared for Mandelbäckerei (recipe 1617)
- wafer strips

## Directions

1. Prepare the same mass as in Mandelbäckerei (recipe 1617)
2. Spread knife-back-thick on wafer strips that are 2 fingers wide and finger-long
3. Bake on a baking sheet at low heat in the oven until pale yellow
4. Remove and after completely cooling, decorate with Spanish sweetmass as desired
5. Sprinkle with sugar and dry in the oven
