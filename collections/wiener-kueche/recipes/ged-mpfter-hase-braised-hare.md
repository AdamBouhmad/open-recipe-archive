## Ingredients

- Hare
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ground bay leaves
- Thyme
- 1 onion
- Vegetables, finely cut
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 2 pieces clove spice
- Spices, 5 grains of each type
- 6 juniper berries
- Garlic (as desired)
- 0.375 liter white wine
- 0.375 liter soup

## Directions

1. Skin and prepare the hare
2. Thread it with bacon seasoned in salt, pepper, ground bay leaves and thyme
3. Wrap the hare in bacon slices and tie it with twine
4. Put 1 onion, some finely cut vegetables, 1 bay leaf, 2 sprigs thyme, 2 clove spices, 5 grains of each spice, 6 juniper berries, and garlic if desired in a long casserole
5. Lay the hare on top
6. Pour approximately 0.375 liter white wine and the same amount of soup over it
7. Let this braise covered slowly for approximately 1.5 hours
8. Remove the hare and serve in pieces on a platter
9. Remove fat from the braise, reduce it slightly and serve as sauce
10. Alternatively, this dish can be served cold; let the hare cool in the braise and stand until it sets to jelly
11. When serving, remove twine and bacon, proceed as with venison back, and garnish neatly with aspic
