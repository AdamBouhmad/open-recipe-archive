## Ingredients

- noodles prepared as in 'Noodles with Poppy' (No. 1204)
- finely ground nuts
- sugar or breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare noodles as described in 'Noodles with Poppy' (No. 1204)
2. Instead of honey and poppy, add finely ground nuts mixed with sugar or breadcrumbs
