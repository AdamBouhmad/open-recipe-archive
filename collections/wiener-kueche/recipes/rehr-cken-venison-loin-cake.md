## Ingredients

- 10 dkg sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 dkg raw grated almonds
- 5 dkg breadcrumbs moistened with rum
- 3 stiff egg whites
- 7 dkg grated chocolate
- jam
- swelled almonds or pine nuts
- for glaze: 25 dkg cube sugar
- water
- 12 dkg chocolate
- butter the size of a hazelnut

## Directions

1. Beat 10 dkg sugar with 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks for 20 minutes
2. Stir in 10 dkg raw grated almonds and 5 dkg breadcrumbs moistened with rum
3. Fold in 3 stiff egg whites together with 7 dkg grated chocolate
4. Fill the batter into a buttered, floured large Rehrücken form and bake slowly
5. Turn out and cut apart the next day
6. Spread jam on the inside and outside, reassemble, frost with brown glaze, and stud with swelled almonds cut into sticks or pine nuts
7. For the glaze: combine 25 dkg cube sugar with corresponding water, 12 dkg chocolate, and butter the size of a hazelnut
