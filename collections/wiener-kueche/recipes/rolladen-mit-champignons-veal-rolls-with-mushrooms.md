## Ingredients

- veal schnitzel from the leg
- leftover roast
- piece of bacon
- marrow
- cooked ham
- green parsley
- chives
- truffles
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls of cream
- bacon strips
- butter
- mushrooms
- flour
- half coffee cup of white wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut fine schnitzels from a veal leg, pound them well; then take some leftover roast, a small piece of bacon, some marrow, a piece of cooked ham, green parsley, chives, some truffles, chop all this finely together
2. Mix salt, pepper, 2 egg yolks, and 2 spoonfuls of cream well together, spread this on the mentioned schnitzels, and roll them together
3. In a casserole, lay some bacon strips and place the rolls on them, cover with bacon strips, put the lid on the casserole, apply heat from below and above, and let them stew on a gentle charcoal fire for two hours
4. Strain off the fat, cut the rolls lengthwise, lay them on the dish so that the stuffing is visible
5. Take small, clean-washed mushrooms, let a piece of butter become hot, add green parsley, chives and the mushrooms, and let them stew until the sauce is completely reduced
6. Dust some flour on it, and let it stew down a little more; finally add half a coffee cup of white wine, the same amount of broth and 2 spoonfuls of cream, and let it cook until only very little sauce remains
7. Strain the fat from the sauce well, and pour it over the rolls; with the mushrooms, form a ring around the edge of the dish, and arrange the rolls in the middle of the dish
