## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams butter
- 4 decagrams flour
- 1/2 liter cream
- cooked potatoes
- 6 tablespoons finely chopped ham
- 5 hard-boiled eggs, diced (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together
2. Pour on cream with constant stirring
3. Add one ladle of cooked, diced potatoes and 6 tablespoons finely chopped ham
4. Stir well together, bring to a boil and serve
5. If desired, also serve with 5 hard-boiled, diced eggs
