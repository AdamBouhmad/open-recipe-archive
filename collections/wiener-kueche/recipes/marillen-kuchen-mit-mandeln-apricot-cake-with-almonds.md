## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 14 deca caster sugar
- 16 deca peeled almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 12 deca semmel crumbs
- halved apricots

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter with 14 deca caster sugar, 16 deca peeled almonds finely ground with 2 egg whites, and 8 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 6 egg whites with 12 deca semmel crumbs
3. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
4. Arrange halved apricots on top
5. Bake for approximately 1 hour in an adequately hot oven
6. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and serve the following day sprinkled with sugar
