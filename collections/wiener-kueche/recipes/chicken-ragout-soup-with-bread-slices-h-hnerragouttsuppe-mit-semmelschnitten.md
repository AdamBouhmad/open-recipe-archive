## Ingredients

- ½ head cauliflower
- 2 mushrooms
- 1 bread roll
- 2-3 chickens
- chicken liver
- salt
- soup greens (optional)
- 8 dkg butter
- parsley
- bread slices or bread dumplings

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the young chicken, and place it in a pot with cold salted water and optional soup greens, except for the liver.
2. Cook for 1-1½ hours depending on the age of the chicken.
3. Add the liver later in the cooking process.
4. Remove the chicken and cut it into pieces.
5. Make a light roux with 8 dkg butter and parsley, and pour it into the strained chicken broth.
6. Add the chicken pieces, cauliflower, and mushrooms (which have been cleaned, washed, and separately cooked in salted water for 20 minutes).
7. Cook everything together well.
8. Serve the soup with bread slices or bread dumplings.
