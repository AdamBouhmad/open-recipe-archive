## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- water
- 5 dkg sugar
- 8 dkg butter
- 1 liter milk
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from the flour, eggs, salt and water
2. Cut into wide noodles
3. Add the noodles to 1 liter boiling milk in a pot together with 5 dkg sugar and 8 dkg butter
4. Cook until the milk has thickened
5. Beat ½ liter milk with 2 egg yolks and 5 dkg sugar
6. Pour this mixture over the noodles
7. Braise in the oven for 30-45 minutes
8. Transfer to a round serving dish, dust with sugar, and serve warm
