## Ingredients

- mass from plum croquettes recipe
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- grated chocolate mixed with sugar
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Form small dumplings from the plum croquette mass
2. Dip in beaten eggs
3. Roll in bread crumbs
4. Fry in very hot fat until golden brown
5. Remove with a baking spoon
6. Roll in grated chocolate mixed with sugar
7. Arrange on dish and serve
