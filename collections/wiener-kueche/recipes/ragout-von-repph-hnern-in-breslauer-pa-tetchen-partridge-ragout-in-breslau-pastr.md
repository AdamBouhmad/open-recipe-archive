## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Bread crumb paste
- Egg
- Two partridges
- Ham, diced
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Coriander
- Cloves
- Anise
- Peppercorns
- Wine
- Meat broth
- Sugar

## Directions

1. For the pastries, make a good puff pastry and roll it out.
2. Grease small pastry forms two fingers high with butter and sprinkle them with breadcrumbs; line them with this dough and place a piece of bread crumb paste cut into squares in the middle.
3. The lid is rolled out somewhat thicker than the bottom, and after being brushed with egg, is placed over the pastries, the excess cleanly cut off so the dough can rise high, and bake the pastries quickly.
4. For the ragout, roast two partridges, remove the breast meat and cut into small pieces, but pound the rest.
5. Set this on with some butter, a piece of ham cut into cubes, finely chopped shallots, coriander, some whole cloves, anise and peppercorns.
6. Sweat for a quarter hour, pour in half a pint of good wine, and let everything cook through well.
7. Then add some meat broth and let it cook again.
8. Strain the sauce through a hair sieve, place the finely cut partridges in it, and fill the pastries piping hot: namely remove the lids, remove the bread crumb paste, fill with as much filling as needed, then cover them again and sprinkle with sugar.
