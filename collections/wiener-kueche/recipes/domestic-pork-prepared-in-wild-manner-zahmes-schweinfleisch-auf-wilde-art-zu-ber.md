## Ingredients

- Pork from the leg, filets, and cutlets
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Cloves
- Juniper
- Bay leaves
- Basil
- Thyme
- Good wine vinegar
- Sprigs of spearmint
- Green walnut shells

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into proper pieces and place it in a marinade consisting of salt, coarse pepper, cloves, juniper, bay leaves, basil, thyme, and good wine vinegar.
2. Add several sprigs of spearmint and green walnut shells.
3. Leave it to marinate for ten or twelve days in a cold place.
4. The pork will then take on the taste and color of wild boar meat.
5. For best results, choose a not too large and not too fatty young pig. To verify its age, make a small cut in the rind; if it tears easily, it is correct.
