## Ingredients

- 4 large cabbages cut into quarters, or 10 small whole cabbages
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 12 ounces hot fat
- 8 ounces sugar
- 8 ounces finely cut bacon
- 1 onion, finely cut
- 1/2 liter soup
- 1/4 liter butter sauce
- Salt and spices

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage in salt water, remove with a skimming spoon, and place in cold water
2. Heat 12 ounces fat, add 8 ounces sugar and let it brown
3. Add 8 ounces finely cut bacon and 1 finely cut onion
4. When this has roasted somewhat, pour 1/2 liter soup over it
5. Add the well-pressed cabbage and braise covered
6. Pour 1/4 liter butter sauce over it, spice it, and let it cook a bit longer
7. Remove the fat carefully and serve the cabbage with its broth on a platter, garnished as desired
