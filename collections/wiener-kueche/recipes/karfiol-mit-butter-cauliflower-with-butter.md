## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and lower stalk from the cauliflower, wash it, place in cold salt water, cook covered 20-30 minutes, drain it.
2. Carefully place on a dish with the beautiful side facing up, pour over with breadcrumbs toasted in hot butter until still liquid.
