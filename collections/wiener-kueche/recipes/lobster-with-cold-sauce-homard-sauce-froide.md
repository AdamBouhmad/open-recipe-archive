## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Eggs of the lobster (if available)
- Small meat pieces
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 raw egg yolks
- Shallots (as desired)
- Rocambole (as desired)
- 4 tablespoons French mustard
- 6 tablespoons fine oil
- Tarragon vinegar or lemon juice
- Finely cut parsley
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster as described above
2. After complete cooling, remove from the cooking water
3. Cut lengthwise through the lobster
4. From the claws, cut away the shell finger-wide and carefully remove all meat
5. If lobster eggs are present, combine them with the small meat pieces, 4 hard-boiled and 2 raw egg yolks, shallots, rocambole and 4 tablespoons French mustard as desired
6. Pass through a sieve and mix with 6 tablespoons fine oil, some tarragon vinegar or lemon juice, finely cut parsley, pepper and necessary salt into a thick sauce
7. Arrange the lobster on a dish or in the lobster shell and small claws
8. Pour some sauce over it and serve the remainder in a sauce boat
