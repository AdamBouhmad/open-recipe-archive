## Ingredients

- 1 heaping teaspoon Holland's tea
- 0.5 liter heavy cream, boiling
- 16 hectograms flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 14 hectograms butter
- 16 hectograms sugar
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Place 1 heaping teaspoon Holland's tea in a casserole, pour 0.5 liter boiling heavy cream over it and let stand covered until cold
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Place 16 hectograms flour in a casserole, gradually stir smooth with the strained tea
4. Add 4 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, 14 hectograms butter and 16 hectograms sugar and stir over the fire until the mixture separates from the spoon
5. Set aside, and after completely cooling, add 6 egg yolks one by one, stirring
6. Finally fold in the snow from 6 egg whites lightly
7. Fill this mixture into a form buttered and floured, and bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour
