## Ingredients

- large snails
- salted water
- salt
- fresh parsley
- anchovies, washed and cleaned
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- butter
- fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook large snails, remove them from their shells, salt them, and let them lie in salted water for a while.
2. Wash the shells clean and clean the snails of their slime, and cut off the tails.
3. Take fresh parsley, some washed and cleaned anchovies, some lemon peel, and some of the snails, chop everything fine together.
4. Beat a piece of butter until light, add the chopped mixture, some lemon juice and a small handful of fine bread crumbs, and stir everything well.
5. Fill part of this mixture into a snail shell, add half a snail, then fill again with the mixture until the shell is full.
6. Place them on a metal dish with heat above and below, and they are ready.
