## Ingredients

- 28 deca softened butter
- 12 deca lemon sugar
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 10 hard-boiled egg yolks, passed through a sieve
- 16 deca blanched almonds, pounded very finely with 1 egg white
- 30 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 28 deca butter until creamy
2. Gradually mix in 12 deca lemon sugar, juice of 1 lemon, and 10 hard-boiled egg yolks passed through a sieve
3. Mix in 16 deca blanched almonds that have been pounded very finely with 1 egg white
4. Mix in 30 deca flour and work into a smooth dough
5. Cover and let rest in a cold place
6. Once stiff, roll out the dough finger-thick on a flour-dusted board
7. Cut out round discs with a puff cutter and place on a butter-brushed baking sheet
8. Brush with beaten eggs and bake until pale yellow in a moderate oven
9. Once cool, remove from the sheet with a thin knife, place on a sieve or board, sprinkle with finely pounded sugar, and let dry in an oven
