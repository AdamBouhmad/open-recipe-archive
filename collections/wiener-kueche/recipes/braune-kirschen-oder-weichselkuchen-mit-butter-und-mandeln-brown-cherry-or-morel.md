## Ingredients

- 24 deca butter
- 30 deca vanilla sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 32 deca almonds, finely ground and sifted
- 12 egg whites
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- 12 deca black flour

## Directions

1. Beat 24 deca butter until fluffy in a deep bowl
2. Gradually add 30 deca vanilla sugar and 16 egg yolks, stirring for 2 1/4 hours
3. Add 32 deca almonds, finely ground with the shell and sifted
4. Gently mix in 12 egg whites simultaneously with 2 tablets grated chocolate and 12 deca black flour
5. Further treatment is as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
