## Ingredients

- 1 loth peeled oats
- half a measure water
- 2 loth citric acid and mulberry juice prepared with sugar
- 2 quentchen cinnamon water

## Directions

1. Take 1 loth of peeled oats
2. Cook in half a measure of water for half an hour
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Add 2 loth each of citric acid and mulberry juice prepared with sugar, and 2 quentchen cinnamon water
