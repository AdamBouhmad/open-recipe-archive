## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine sugar
- 2 pounds lemon juice
- One and a half bottles rum or arrack

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 pound fine sugar and add 2 pounds lemon juice as well as one and a half bottles rum or arrack
2. Place juice and sugar on gentle charcoal fire and cook not too long with constant stirring
3. Cool it down, pour the rum in slowly, stir it well, and fill it into bottles
