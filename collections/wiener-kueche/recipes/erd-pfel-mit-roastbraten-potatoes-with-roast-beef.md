## Ingredients

- 2 pounds roast beef, bones removed, beaten and salted
- Fat
- 4 handfuls onion, finely cut
- Milk cream
- Butter or beef lard
- Boiled potatoes, cut lengthwise
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Let the 2 pounds of roast beef stand for 1/4 hour
2. Braise in a pan with a little fat and 4 handfuls of finely cut onion until soft, turning the meat frequently
3. Cut the meat into equal oblong pieces
4. Return to the pan with 1/4 measures of good milk cream and braise a little longer
5. Coat a platter with butter or beef lard and spread milk cream on it
6. Layer boiled, lengthwise-cut potatoes, salt them
7. Add a layer of braised roast beef with braised onions, and continue layering until all is layered
8. Top with milk cream and a few pieces of butter
9. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, apply heat from above and below, and let it bake a nice golden color
