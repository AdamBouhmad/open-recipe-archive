## Ingredients

- mutton neck, breast, and shoulders
- bacon
- onions, leafy-cut
- flour
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- turnips, white

## Directions

1. Cut the neck, breast, and shoulders into pieces two fingers wide and let them brown in a casserole with bacon and finely cut onions over fairly strong heat.
2. Remove the browned meat pieces and place them in another casserole.
3. In the fat remaining in the original casserole, stir in several spoonfuls of flour, let it brown, and then deglaze with some broth.
4. Season with 1 bay leaf, salt, white pepper and some thyme, strain this, and pour over the meat pieces.
5. Place neatly cut white turnips at the side of the casserole and let everything braise together covered until tender.
6. Alternatively, braise the meat alone with the strained sauce and garnish with white turnips braised in butter.
