## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 turtles, head, feet and tail
- finely chopped green parsley
- butter
- flour
- pea broth
- pepper
- nutmeg flower
- cream
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut off the head, feet and tail from 3 or 4 turtles, scald and remove them, cut into fine pieces
2. Braise them with finely chopped green parsley and a small piece of butter in a pot
3. Dust with a little flour and pour pea broth over
4. At the end add a little pepper, nutmeg flower and 2 spoonfuls of cream
5. Let it simmer well
6. Serve over toasted bread
