## Ingredients

- Eggs, any number
- Butter or cream (Schmette)
- Nutmeg flower (Muscatblüthe)
- Butter for cooking
- Chives (Schnittling), finely cut

## Directions

1. Break any number of eggs into a pot and stir them with sufficient butter and a little nutmeg flower.
2. Let the butter melt in a pan, pour the eggs into it, stir them frequently, and do not let them become hard or grainy.
3. When serving, sprinkle with finely cut chives.
