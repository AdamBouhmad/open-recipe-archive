## Ingredients

- 8 deca butter
- 8 deca not completely white braised ham
- 16 deca finely cut and salted ham
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/8 liter cream
- Salt to taste
- 1 knife point of pepper

## Directions

1. Beat 8 deca butter until fluffy.
2. Add 8 deca not completely white braised ham, 16 deca finely cut and salted ham, 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk, 1/8 liter cream, necessary salt, and 1 knife point of pepper.
3. Stir everything well together.
4. Fill the cleaned, prepared, and salted turkey with this mixture.
5. Follow the same further preparation as described for 'gefüllter Indianer' (stuffed turkey).
