## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter cold milk
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 16 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- jam

## Directions

1. Slowly pour 0.5 liter of cold milk into 16 tablespoons of flour and stir over the fire until it forms a thick dough that separates from the spoon
2. Transfer to a deep bowl and stir in 12 tablespoons of butter and let cool
3. Beat 16 tablespoons of vanilla sugar with 8 egg yolks one after another
4. Mix this well with the cooled mixture
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites
6. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
7. Cover and cook 3/4 hours in a water bath or bake in the oven
8. Turn out onto a serving dish and serve with jam at table
