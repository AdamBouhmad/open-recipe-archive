## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound deer antler gelatin
- 1 Maß water
- 3 half-measures ripe wild strawberries
- a little water
- 8 egg whites
- 1/2 pound sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1/2 bottle sweet wine

## Directions

1. Cook 3/4 pound deer antler gelatin with 1 Maß water in the known manner, so that it becomes even thicker than for rose jelly
2. Cook 3 half-measures of ripe wild strawberries with a little water over gentle heat, then pass them through a hair sieve without pressing them through
3. Pour this juice to the strained deer antler gelatin
4. Beat 8 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold them in
5. Bring the mixture to a boil over heat and set it aside
6. Add 1/2 pound sugar, the juice of 2 lemons, and 1/2 bottle sweet wine
7. Pass through a cloth as usual
