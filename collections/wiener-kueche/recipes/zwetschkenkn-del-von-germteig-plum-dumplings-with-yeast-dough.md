## Ingredients

- 6 ounces butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 4 ounces fine sugar
- 5 grams salt
- 1/2 liter flour
- 3 ounces yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter cold milk
- fresh plums

## Directions

1. Beat 6 ounces of butter until light
2. Gradually beat in 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 4 ounces fine sugar, and 5 grams salt
3. Stir in 1/2 liter flour and 3 ounces yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter cold milk until the dough releases from the spoon
4. Cover and let rest in a warm place until it rises by one-third
5. With an egg spoon, place portions on a flour-dusted board, separate with floured fingers
6. Top with wiped raw plums, distribute dough evenly over them
7. Place dumplings on a flour-dusted board, cover with cloth, let rise in a warm place until somewhat larger
8. Proceed as in Zwetschkenknödel von Strudelteig (Recipe 1223)
9. Any ripe fruit such as small apricots or greengage plums can be used instead of plums
