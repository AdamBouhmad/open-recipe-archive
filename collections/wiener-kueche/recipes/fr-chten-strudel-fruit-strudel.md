## Ingredients

- 12 deca beaten butter
- 20 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- 6 egg whites
- Strudel dough made from 1/4 liter flour
- blanched almonds, cut into thin sticks
- peeled oranges, cut into cubes
- fine citron peel, cut into thin strips
- figs, cut into fine pieces
- dates

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter with 20 deca vanilla sugar
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix with 1/4 liter milk cream
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Prepare strudel dough from 1/4 liter flour, stretched thin
6. Spread with this mixture
7. Sprinkle with blanched almonds cut into thin sticks, peeled oranges cut into cubes, fine citron peel, figs and dates cut into fine pieces
8. Roll lightly together
9. Treat as for Ham Strudel (No. 1229)
