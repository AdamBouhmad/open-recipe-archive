## Ingredients

- 38 dkg flour
- salt
- 3 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter lukewarm milk
- 1 1/2 dkg yeast
- jam
- lard

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a bowl, add a pinch of salt and 3 dkg sugar
2. In a small pot, melt the butter, beat it well with 4 egg yolks and 1/2 liter lukewarm milk (everything should be only lukewarm), add to the flour, stir in the yeast
3. Beat the dough very smooth for about 15 minutes, cover and place near the stove to rise
4. After it has risen (about 1 1/2-2 hours), roll it thin on a well-floured board and cut out with a fritter cutter
5. Place hazelnut-sized jam in the middle, lay a second sheet of dough on top with the upper side down, press the edges with floured fingers, cut out the fritters again and place them on a floured board without touching the edges
6. Cover with a napkin and let them rise near the stove
7. When they have risen on one side, turn them over and let them rise again
8. Place them with the upper side down in a baking pan with plenty of hot lard, being careful not to touch the edges when placing them in
9. Cover the pan and shake it gently
10. When the fritters are golden brown, turn them and finish baking uncovered
11. Let them drain on paper and serve dusted with sugar, completely cooled
