## Ingredients

- 2 heads sauerkraut
- 12 ounces hot fat
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- pepper
- paprika
- 1/2 liter flour
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- optionally: 1/4 kilo braised or stewed pork, or the same amount of pickled ham, or 1/4 kilo braised sausage

## Directions

1. Remove the ribs from 2 heads of sauerkraut, cut them roughly, then chop very fine with a knife
2. In a saucepan with 12 ounces hot fat, place the pressed-out sauerkraut so all liquid runs out
3. Add 1 teaspoon caraway and let cook covered, stirring often, until soft
4. Season with pepper and paprika to taste, set aside and cool
5. Prepare finely pulled strudel dough from 1/2 liter flour, brush with melted butter
6. Top with the cooled braised sauerkraut, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, then treat as in Ham Strudel
7. The sauerkraut filling distributed on the dough can optionally be sprinkled with 1/4 kilo braised or stewed pork, or the same amount of pickled ham, each very finely chopped, or 1/4 kilo braised sausage cut into small pieces
