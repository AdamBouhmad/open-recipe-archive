## Ingredients

- Egg yolks (8)
- Vanilla sugar (14 drams)
- Flour (2 tablespoons)
- Cream (1/4 liter)
- Blanched almonds, finely crushed (16 drams)
- Almond flour
- Egg whites (4)
- Marmalade
- Pitted apples (8-10)
- Butter

## Directions

1. Put egg yolks, vanilla sugar, flour and cream in a casserole and stir over heat until thick
2. Set aside and let cool
3. When cooled, add blanched, finely crushed almonds and egg yolks one after another
4. Finally fold in the snow of egg whites
5. Hollow out the core of 8-10 apples, fill with marmalade
6. Place the apples at finger-width distance in a butter-coated dish
7. Pour the almond mixture over them
8. Bake at moderate heat for 3/4 to 1 hour in the oven
