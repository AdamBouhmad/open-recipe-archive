## Ingredients

- 1 capon, prepared as described previously
- 1 tablespoon capers, cleaned and finely diced
- finely diced gherkins
- imitated woodcock sauce (see no. 838)
- lemon slices
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Add 1 tablespoon of cleaned and finely diced capers and finely diced gherkins to the sauce, bring it once to a boil and add it to the capon in a sauce boat
2. The capon, prepared as described in the previous number, is arranged on a platter cut into pieces and reassembled
3. Garnish with imitated woodcock sauce, lemon slices and brown sauce
