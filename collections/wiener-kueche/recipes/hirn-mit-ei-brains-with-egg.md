## Ingredients

- 1 calf's or pork brain
- 5 dkg hot butter
- finely chopped parsley
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the brain and wash well.
2. In a small pot, heat 5 dkg butter and sauté finely chopped parsley.
3. Add the brain, salt, and white pepper, roast for a few minutes.
4. Break an egg into the pot and roast for a few more minutes.
