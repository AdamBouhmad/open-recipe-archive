## Ingredients

- 8 deca rind-removed rolls
- milk
- 34 deca raw veal meat
- 6 deca butter
- 2 whole eggs
- white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/4 liter milk

## Directions

1. Cut 8 deca rolls finely into leaflets, soak with milk and stir over heat until it looks like dough
2. Place on a plate and let cool
3. Meanwhile cut 34 deca raw veal meat very finely
4. Put the meat with the prepared rolls in a mortar and pound very finely with 6 deca butter and 2 whole eggs
5. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
6. Add a pinch of white pepper, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk, and 1/4 liter milk
7. Mix well together
8. Fill into a mold greased with butter and lined with flour
9. Cook in steam, turn out, cut into pieces, and place in the soup
10. For ragout, place in a round rice mold
