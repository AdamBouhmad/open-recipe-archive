## Ingredients

- 4 calf feet and 2 pork feet
- Wine vinegar
- Water
- 1 onion
- Spice cloves
- 1 yellow turnip
- Celery
- 5 pieces each of spices
- 5 sprigs of parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 shallot
- Salt
- White wine
- Egg yolk
- Snow water

## Directions

1. Chop the calf feet and pork feet and place them in a braising pan
2. Add wine vinegar and water and cook together well
3. Remove the fat and scum carefully during cooking
4. Add 1 piece of onion pierced with 1 spice clove, 1 yellow turnip, 1 small piece of celery, 5 pieces each of spices, 5 sprigs of parsley, 1 bay leaf, 1 piece of shallot, and some salt
5. Let everything cook together for one hour
6. Add the suckling pig cut into desired pieces and cover with white wine
7. Cook until the meat is sufficiently tender
8. Remove the meat with a slotted spoon, lay it on a platter and cover with a cloth soaked in white wine
9. Pour the broth through a sieve and then through a cloth into an earthen vessel and remove the fat carefully
10. In a braising pan, place the egg yolk from 3 eggs and whisk it slightly with a whisk
11. Add the suckling pig broth and whisk over the fire until it begins to boil
12. Pull the braising pan back, cover it with a fitting lid, place it on the same coals
13. The protein on top will shrink together so that the liquid obtains a clear color
14. Pour the broth through a cloth as with aspic and set aside in an earthen vessel
15. Place a mold in ice, pour in strongly finger-deep of the prepared broth and let it cool
16. Lay the suckling pig pieces with the skin side down on the cooled gelatin so that they join together to form the whole suckling pig
17. Cover with enough broth to fill the mold
18. When the liquid is completely cooled, remove the mold, wipe it off, dip it for a moment in hot water, dry it with a cloth, turn it out onto a fitting platter, carefully remove the mold and garnish with the above chopped aspic
