## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- salt
- pepper, as desired
- butter for roasting
- warm butter for basting
- meat drippings

## Directions

1. After quickly rinsing the veal breast and drying it with a cloth, rub it with salt and pepper as desired
2. Place it in a roasting pan in which some butter is present
3. Cover the meat with warm butter and roast it in the oven for approximately 4½ hours, basting frequently with the drippings that form
4. When the meat is half roasted, loosen the bones and continue roasting until it has a brown color
5. Remove the meat from the pan, cut it from the thick side lengthwise into pieces of desired size, usually as thick as a knife back
6. Arrange the pieces on a serving dish and pour the defatted drippings alongside
