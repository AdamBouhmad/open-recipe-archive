## Ingredients

- 40 deca flour
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 knife point of salt
- 2 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/16 liter cold cream
- 1 egg and 3 egg yolks in 1/16 liter cream
- 4 deca melted butter

## Directions

1. Put 40 deca flour, 1 teaspoon sugar and 1 knife point of salt in a deep bowl
2. Dissolve 2 deca pressed yeast in 1/16 liter cold cream and beat 1 egg and 3 egg yolks in 1/16 liter cream, pour into the flour, stir together into a dough, pour in 4 deca melted butter, stir well and beat until the dough separates from the spoon
3. Cover with a cloth and let rest covered in a warm place
4. When the dough has risen, put it on a floured board, roll out to the thickness of a little finger and cut out round cakes using a pastry cutter
