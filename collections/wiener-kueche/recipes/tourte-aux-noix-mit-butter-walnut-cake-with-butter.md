## Ingredients

- 28 teaspoons butter
- 28 teaspoons finely pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 24 teaspoons walnuts pounded very finely with 1 egg white
- 28 teaspoons flour

## Directions

1. Beat 28 teaspoons butter with 28 teaspoons finely pounded sugar and 6 egg yolks for 1 4 hours, add 24 teaspoons walnuts pounded very finely with 1 egg white, stir again for 3/4 hour and finally mix in 28 teaspoons flour.
2. Fill into a cake form brushed with butter and dusted with flour and bake for 1 hour in a well-heated oven.
3. After this time, remove the form's edge, place the cake on a board and cover with desired glaze once completely cooled.
