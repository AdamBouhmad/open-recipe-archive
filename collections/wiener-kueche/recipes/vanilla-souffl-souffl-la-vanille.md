## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter milk
- 12 tablespoons sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- 9 egg whites
- butter
- flour
- strawberry jam or other jam

## Directions

1. Slowly stir 0.5 liter of milk into 16 tablespoons of flour and stir over the fire until it forms a thick dough that separates from the spoon
2. Transfer to a deep bowl and let cool
3. Meanwhile, beat 12 tablespoons of sugar with 9 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
4. Slowly mix this into the cooled mixture
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 9 egg whites
6. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
7. Cover and cook 3/4 hours in a water bath or bake in the oven at moderate heat
8. Turn out onto a serving dish and pour dissolved strawberry jam or other jam over it
