## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter, beaten
- 16 Deka fine ground powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 Deka almonds, finely ground with the skin
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter with fine ground powdered sugar
2. Add egg yolks one after another
3. Mix fine ground almonds with cinnamon and add to the mixture
4. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
5. Fill this mixture into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold
6. Let it cook covered for ½ hour in steam or bake in the oven
