## Ingredients

- Beautiful raspberries
- Liquid red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Take beautiful raspberries, remove the stems from them, arrange them on a compote dish, and cover them completely with liquid red currant jelly, which you pour on carefully.
2. Then serve them.
