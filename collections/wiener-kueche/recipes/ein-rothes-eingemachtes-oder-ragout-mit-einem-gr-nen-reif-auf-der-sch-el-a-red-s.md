## Ingredients

- plenty of spinach juice, clarified
- two small rolls, peeled
- milk
- about one pound fine veal forcemeat
- a couple of eggs
- a handful of finely cut green parsley
- butter
- stewed chicken
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Take plenty of clarified spinach juice and soak two small peeled rolls in milk
2. When soft, take about one pound fine veal forcemeat and stir in the rolls with a couple of eggs
3. Add a handful of finely cut green parsley
4. Stir in the made spinach juice until the forcemeat is beautifully green
5. Form a ring from this mixture on a buttered platter, as before
6. In the middle of the ring place a stewed or braised chicken in tomato sauce
7. Leave this for half an hour in a warm oven
8. You can place a buttered sheet of paper on the ring and stew so the color does not fade too much from the heat
9. Remove the paper and serve the ragout
