## Ingredients

- 30 teaspoons butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 12 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- ground nutmeg
- ground cloves
- 40 teaspoons flour
- eggs
- marmalade
- glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 30 teaspoons of butter until fluffy, add the juice of 1/2 lemon with 12 teaspoons powdered sugar, stir well together and mix with 1 pinch cinnamon, equal amount of grated nutmeg, crushed cloves and 40 teaspoons flour, which is stirred in spoonful by spoonful.
2. Work the dough on a flour-dusted board into a round loaf, roll it out and cut into three equal round slices.
3. Place these on a sheet lined with buttered paper, brush with beaten eggs and bake at moderate heat in the oven.
4. Remove from paper, place on a sieve and allow to cool completely, then layer all three slices together with marmalade spread between them.
5. Cover the whole with glaze.
