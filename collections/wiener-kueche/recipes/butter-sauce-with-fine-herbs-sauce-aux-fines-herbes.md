## Ingredients

- Butter-Sauce
- green parsley
- savory
- tarragon
- sorrel
- salt water
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare like the previous recipe, but when serving, finely chop together a handful of green parsley, savory, tarragon, and sorrel (which have been previously cooked in salt water)
2. Stir into the sauce with a knife point of white pepper
3. This sauce is served with fish
