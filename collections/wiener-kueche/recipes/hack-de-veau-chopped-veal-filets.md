## Ingredients

- 6 Deca butter, beaten
- 2 whole eggs
- 8 Deca bread softened in water, well pressed out
- 10 Deca finely cut cooked or braised meat leftovers
- some roasted, green parsley
- a little finely cut lemon zest, if desired
- salt
- pepper
- flour for dusting
- beaten, stale eggs for coating
- breadcrumbs for rolling
- butter for frying, very hot

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and stir in 2 whole eggs, the softened and pressed bread, the finely cut meat leftovers, roasted green parsley, and lemon zest if desired
2. Season well with salt and pepper and mix everything thoroughly
3. Form filets of any desired size, or patties thick as a knife back
4. Dust these on both sides with flour
5. Brush them with beaten, stale eggs
6. Roll them in breadcrumbs
7. Fry them in a flat baking pan in very hot butter on both sides until golden yellow
8. Place them on a platter and arrange them in a serving dish
