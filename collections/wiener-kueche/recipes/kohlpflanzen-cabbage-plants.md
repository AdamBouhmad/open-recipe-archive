## Ingredients

- 24 h cabbage plants
- 5 dkg butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the plants.
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook uncovered for 25-30 minutes.
3. Drain and chop finely.
4. Make a parsley butter sauce with 5 dkg butter, add the vegetable water or broth, add the cabbage plants, season to taste with salt and pepper, and cook down well.
