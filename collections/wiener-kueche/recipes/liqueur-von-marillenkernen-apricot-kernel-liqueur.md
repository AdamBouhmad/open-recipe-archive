## Ingredients

- 1 pound healthy apricot kernels, some of which must be bitter
- 1 Maß brandy
- half Maß water
- some cinnamon
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Crush apricot kernels slightly in a mortar, but do not pound them so they do not become oily
2. Place in a large preserving glass
3. Add 1 Maß brandy, half Maß water, some cinnamon, and 1.5 pounds sugar
4. Let stand for 9 days, stirring twice daily
5. On the tenth day, filter through filter paper
6. Fill into bottles, cork well, and let stand for some months
7. The later it is used, the better it becomes
