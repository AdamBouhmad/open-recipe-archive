## Ingredients

- 5 Pfund lean beef
- 3 Pfund bacon
- 3 Pfund pork
- 10 to 11 Loth salt
- 3 Loth cardamom
- 4 Loth ground pepper
- wide casings

## Directions

1. Remove all skin and connective tissue from the 5 pounds lean beef, 3 pounds bacon, and 3 pounds pork
2. Chop the meat very finely, the bacon somewhat more coarsely
3. Mix well together and let stand unsalted for 24 hours
4. Mix 10 to 11 Loth salt, 3 Loth cardamom, and 4 Loth ground pepper well into the chopped meat
5. Fill the mixture firmly into wide casings and tie well
6. Pierce the casings all around with needles
7. Hang in an air chimney or on an air loft, which should usually be done in a winter month
8. After hanging for 14 to 15 days, they can be stored for further use in a cool place
