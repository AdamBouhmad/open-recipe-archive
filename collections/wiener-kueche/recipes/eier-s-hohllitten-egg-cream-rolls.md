## Ingredients

- 12 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 liter cream
- 2 whole eggs
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- white wax
- muslin paper

## Directions

1. Gradually stir together 12 tablespoons flour, 1/4 liter cream, 2 whole eggs, and 8 tablespoons sugar.
2. Once well beaten, brush a metal iron with white wax, wipe it with muslin paper, and lightly brush with wax again.
3. Spread the mixture onto the iron to the thickness of a knife-back.
4. Once baked golden, while still on the stove, cut the mixture into rectangular pieces and roll them around a rounded stick or wooden spoon handle.
