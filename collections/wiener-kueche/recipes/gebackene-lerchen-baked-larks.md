## Ingredients

- larks
- fresh bacon
- breadcrumbs
- flour
- ginger
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. After the larks have been properly cleaned and gutted, skewer them on a small iron spit
2. Between every two larks, place a small piece of fresh bacon
3. Sprinkle them with breadcrumbs, flour, ginger, pepper and salt, which must all be well mixed together
4. Drizzle them with butter so they remain juicy
5. Before serving, toast breadcrumbs in butter until golden
6. Sprinkle the arranged larks with these crumbs
