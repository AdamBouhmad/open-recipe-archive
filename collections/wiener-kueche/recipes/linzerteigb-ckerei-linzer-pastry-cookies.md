## Ingredients

- 18 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 7 dkg raw grated almonds
- 10½ dkg sugar

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from the flour, butter, raw grated almonds and sugar
2. Let it rest
3. Roll it out
4. Cut it into desired shapes
5. Place the pastry on an unbuttered baking sheet
6. Bake until light brown
