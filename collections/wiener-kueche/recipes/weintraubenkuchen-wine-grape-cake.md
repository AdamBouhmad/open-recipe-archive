## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 16 dkg butter
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 dkg sugar
- salt
- 3 egg whites
- 16 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg grated raw almonds
- 1 kg black grapes

## Directions

1. Prepare a crumbly dough (see Doughs) from the flour, butter, milk, egg yolks, sugar, and salt
2. After resting, roll out the dough to sheet size and place on an unbuttered baking sheet
3. Bake at higher heat until light brown
4. Whip 3 egg whites into stiff peaks and stir in 16 dkg sugar, 7 dkg grated raw almonds, and the grapes which have been washed and removed from their stems
5. Spread the mixture on the baked cake and allow it to bake in the oven until light brown
6. Cut the cake and serve cooled
