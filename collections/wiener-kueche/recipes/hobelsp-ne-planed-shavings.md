## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 4 egg yolks
- 12 deca sugar
- 8 deca flour
- wax

## Directions

1. Mix the well beaten snow from 4 egg whites well with 4 egg yolks, 12 deca sugar and 8 deca flour. A baking sheet coated with wax and hollowed out is thinly coated with this mass and baked in a fairly hot oven until golden yellow, then you immediately cut it with a long fine knife into finger-wide strips, but twist these over wooden spoon handles and only remove them after a few moments, which gives them the form and appearance of wood shavings.
2. The rolling up of the strips over the wooden spoon handle must happen as quickly as possible, as the dough will otherwise become too stiff and not roll up. If desired, you can place 1/2 spoon full of the above batter at 2 hand-widths distance on a baking sheet coated with wax, which you stretch thin lengthwise with a knife or spoon and bake in the oven until the dough releases from the sheet, whereupon you quickly roll one strip after another over a wooden spoon handle and remove after a few moments.
