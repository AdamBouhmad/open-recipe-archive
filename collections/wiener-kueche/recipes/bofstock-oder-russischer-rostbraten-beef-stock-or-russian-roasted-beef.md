## Ingredients

- Lung or loin roast
- Salt
- Melted butter
- Horseradish, grated

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from a lung or loin roast and cut schnitzel from it as thick as a finger crosswise
2. Salt them and dip them in melted butter
3. Roast them on both sides on the grill over strong heat until they are roasted through but still very juicy
4. Place on the serving dish and sprinkle grated horseradish on top
