## Ingredients

- Butter (1/8 liter melted)
- Bread crumbs, black (1 heaped tablespoon, grated)
- Périgueux (16 drams)
- Cloves (1 crushed)
- Cinnamon (1/4 teaspoon)
- Lemon peel (1/4 teaspoon, finely grated)
- Roasted bread mixture
- Peeled roasted chestnuts
- Apple sauce
- Currants
- Raisins

## Directions

1. In a casserole containing melted butter, add a heaped tablespoon of grated black bread, 16 drams of Périgueux, 1 crushed clove, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon and equally finely grated lemon peel
2. Mix well together and let it brown, then set aside to cool
3. When cooled, line a butter-coated casserole, lined with pastry, with a layer of the roasted bread mixture
4. On this place peeled roasted chestnuts, sprinkle again with bread and cover with apple sauce mixed with currants and raisins
5. Give another layer of roasted bread and continue until the casserole is filled
6. The last layer should be roasted bread, which is then pressed down firmly with a spoon
7. Bake for 1/2 hour in the oven
