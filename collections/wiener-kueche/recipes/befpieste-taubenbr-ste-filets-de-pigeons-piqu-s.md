## Ingredients

- Pigeon breasts
- Root vegetables
- Speck (bacon)
- Whole spices
- Broth
- Red wine
- Butter
- Glace

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts as described for pheasant breasts, peel away the fine membranes, and lard the breasts.
2. Place the remaining meat of the pigeons with root vegetables, speck, and whole spices in a casserole, roast slightly, then add broth and red wine, and simmer well.
3. Pour half of this simmered and strained sauce into a shallow casserole.
4. Place the breasts with the larded side up on the sauce, cover with writing paper brushed with butter, and braise in the oven for approximately 1/2 hour, basting frequently.
5. After this time, remove the paper and roast the breasts until the speck becomes golden in color.
6. Brush the breasts with dissolved glace, arrange on a platter, and pour the remaining half of the strained sauce over them.
