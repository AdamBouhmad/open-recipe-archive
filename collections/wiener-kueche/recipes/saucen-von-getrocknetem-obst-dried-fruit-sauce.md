## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter dried fruit
- 6 deca finely cut black bread
- 1/2 liter broth
- 1/4 liter red or white wine
- nutmeg as desired

## Directions

1. Crush 1/4 liter dried fruit
2. Put the crushed fruit in a casserole
3. Add 6 deca finely cut black bread
4. Pour 1/2 liter broth and 1/4 liter red or white wine
5. Season to taste
6. If desired, add 1 pinch of nutmeg
7. Let it cook well, stir and strain it
8. Serve this sauce with roasted game or similar meat dishes
