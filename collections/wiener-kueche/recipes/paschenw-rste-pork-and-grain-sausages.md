## Ingredients

- 16 deca black fat, diced
- 2 pieces onion, very finely minced
- 32 deca bacon, boiled in salted water for 15 minutes, drained, dried, and finely diced
- 1/2 liter groats (or barley or millet cooked in water or broth)
- Salt
- 1 knife-tip of pepper
- 1 crushed bay leaf
- Garlic, minced, optional
- 1/2 liter pork blood
- Cleaned intestines, well soaked

## Directions

1. Roast the diced black fat and add the finely minced onion.
2. Add the prepared bacon pieces.
3. Mix in the cooled groats (or barley or millet), salt, pepper, crushed bay leaf, and optional garlic.
4. Place the mixture in a deep bowl and add the pork blood when it is slightly overflowing.
5. Fill the cleaned, well-soaked intestines with this mixture, but not too tightly as they may burst during cooking.
6. Tie the intestines with twine every four fingers wide.
7. Boil them at gentle motion in salted water for approximately 10 minutes.
8. After cooling slightly, remove from the broth and store in a cool place.
9. Shortly before use, fry them brown in fat.
