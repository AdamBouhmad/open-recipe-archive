## Ingredients

- 30 deca finely diced lard
- 32 deca finely diced rolls
- 1 liter flour
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 liter milk
- Optional: finely diced, fried boiled meat or braised vegetables such as sauerkraut or cabbage

## Directions

1. Roast 30 deca of finely diced lard until it looks glassy.
2. Add 32 deca of finely diced rolls, stir both together over the fire until the fat has been absorbed into the rolls.
3. Place them in a deep bowl and let them cool.
4. Half an hour before cooking, add 1 liter of flour to the rolls.
5. Beat 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk with 1/2 liter of milk well together, pour it over the rolls, stir everything well together, and let it rest covered until cooking time so that the roasted rolls soften.
6. Form dumplings of any size, place them in simmering salted water, and let them cook covered for 1.5 hours.
7. If desired, a spoonful of finely diced, fried boiled meat or similarly braised vegetables such as sauerkraut or cabbage can be placed in the center of each dumpling.
