## Ingredients

- yellow peel of 1 orange
- 1 liter boiling hot cream
- 24 deca sugar
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Carefully scrape off the yellow of an orange peel, ensuring no white peel remains
2. Place it in 1 liter boiling hot cream and let cool uncovered
3. Stir 24 deca sugar with 8 egg yolks well together
4. Gradually add the cooled cream
5. Place on fire and treat further as Vanille-Gefrornees (No. 1720)
