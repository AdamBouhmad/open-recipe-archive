## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams butter
- 8 decagrams flour
- finely chopped onion
- bay leaves
- lemon peel
- soup
- 1/2 liter cream
- salt
- spices
- cooked potatoes, leaf-like sliced
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. In 8 decagrams butter roast an equal amount of flour
2. Add finely chopped onion, bay leaves and lemon peel
3. Stir well together
4. Pour soup over it
5. Once this has cooked with 1/2 liter cream, salt and spices, add approximately one ladle of cooked, leaf-like sliced potatoes with finely chopped fresh parsley
6. Stir well together and let simmer once more
7. If too thick, thin with soup
