## Ingredients

- Sprouts
- Salt water
- Hot butter
- Toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the sprouts as above in salt water; place them in 8 drams hot butter, stir them well together, and use as a garnish.
2. May also be topped with toasted bread crumbs.
