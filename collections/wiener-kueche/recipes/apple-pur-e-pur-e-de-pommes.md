## Ingredients

- approximately 8 Muskät apples
- 12 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons white wine or juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel about 8 Muskät apples
2. Cut into pieces
3. Place in a casserole with 12 tablespoons sugar and 4 tablespoons white wine or lemon juice
4. Braise until soft
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Arrange heaped on a platter
7. Sprinkle with fine sugar and, if desired, immediately burn the sugar with a glowing iron
