## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe apricots
- crushed sugar
- fruit brandy (Franzbrantwein)

## Directions

1. Select beautiful ripe apricots, peel off the skin, and cut into two pieces.
2. Marinate with crushed sugar and fruit brandy.
