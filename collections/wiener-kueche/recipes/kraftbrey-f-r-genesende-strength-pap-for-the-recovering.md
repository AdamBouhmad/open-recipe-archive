## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- almond milk

## Directions

1. Take the yolks from eggs, being careful that no white remains
2. Stir the yolks often and strongly
3. Add sugar and cinnamon
4. Pour in almond milk
5. Cook it into a thin pap
