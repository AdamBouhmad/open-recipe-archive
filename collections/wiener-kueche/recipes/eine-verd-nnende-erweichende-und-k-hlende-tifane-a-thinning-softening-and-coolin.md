## Ingredients

- 2 loth crushed white poppy seed
- 4 quentchen oats
- 12 quentchen crushed raw kitchen peas
- 7 quentchen each borage and marshmallow leaves
- 4 loth scorzonera root
- 1.5 measures water
- 2 loth each currants and elderberry pulp

## Directions

1. Take 2 loth crushed white poppy seed, 4 quentchen oats, 12 quentchen crushed raw kitchen peas, 7 quentchen each of borage and marshmallow leaves, and 4 loth scorzonera root
2. Cook together for one hour in 1.5 measures of water
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Add 2 loth each of currants and elderberry pulp
