## Ingredients

- Half pound of rice
- Milk or good broth
- Butter, size of one egg
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- Tails and claws from 30 crayfish
- 2 or 3 soft-cooked stomachs of chickens or geese
- Some cooked calf's head, cut lengthwise
- Salt
- Whites from 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook half pound of rice in milk or good broth
2. Let it cool
3. Beat a piece of butter the size of an egg until fluffy
4. Add the rice to it
5. Gradually stir in 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
6. Add the extracted tails and claws from 30 crayfish
7. Add 2 or 3 soft-cooked stomachs of chickens or geese
8. Add some cooked calf's head cut lengthwise
9. Season with salt
10. Finally stir in the snow from the whites of 3 eggs
11. Line the bottom of a casserole brushed with butter entirely with white paper
12. Brush the paper with butter
13. Artfully arrange crayfish tails and asparagus boiled in salted water on it
