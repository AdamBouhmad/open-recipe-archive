## Ingredients

- 16 Deka blanched almonds
- 2 egg whites for grinding almonds
- 10 hard-boiled and passed egg yolks
- 32 Deka butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 32 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Grind blanched almonds very finely with egg whites
2. Finely grind with hard-boiled and passed egg yolks
3. Place on a board and work with lemon juice and flour into a dough
4. Cover and let rest for 1/2 hour in a cold place
5. Roll out dough to finger thickness on a flour-dusted board
6. Proceed as in the 'sugar dough torte' (no. 1479)
