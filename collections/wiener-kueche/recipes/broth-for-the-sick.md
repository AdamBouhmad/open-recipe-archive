## Ingredients

- 1 clean plucked old hen
- 3 cleaned calf's feet
- 1 liter white wine
- 1½ liters water

## Directions

1. Cook the hen and calf's feet together with the white wine and water until reduced to 1½ liters
2. Strain it, remove the fat completely, lightly whip the meat, add the liquid back
3. When clear, season with salt
4. If not acidic enough, add lemon or vinegar
5. If brown broth is desired, add 1 spoonful of caramelized sugar and place in the coolness
