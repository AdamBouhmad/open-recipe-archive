## Ingredients

- Fat
- Root vegetables, finely cut
- Onion
- 1 bay leaf
- Whole spices
- Vinegar
- Water
- Carp, filleted and cut into pieces
- Butter
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put fat, finely cut root vegetables, onion, 1 bay leaf and whole spices into a casserole.
2. Pour vinegar and water over and cook well.
3. When the root vegetables are soft, add the filleted carp cut into pieces and let it cook for a few minutes.
4. Remove the carp, pull off the skin, arrange in a bowl and garnish with the finely cut root vegetables.
5. Make a butter sauce from the broth and thicken it with a few egg yolks.
6. Serve the sauce in a sauceboat with the carp.
