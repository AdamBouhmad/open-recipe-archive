## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 16 deca caster sugar
- 8 deca peeled almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 10 deca semmel crumbs
- grapes

## Directions

1. In a deep bowl, beat 14 deca butter until fluffy
2. Add 16 deca caster sugar, 8 deca peeled almonds finely ground with 2 egg whites, and 8 egg yolks
3. Stir together for 1/4 hour
4. Lightly mix in the stiff peaks of 6 egg whites with 10 deca semmel crumbs
5. Fill into a baking pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
6. Allow plucked grapes, 3 and 3 one upon the other, to sink into the dough
7. Further treatment is as with the Brown Cherry or Morello Cherry Cake (No. 1460)
