## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fine flour
- 12 eggs
- 3/4 pound butter
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 3/8 pound ground sugar
- 3 spoonfuls good beer yeast
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- salt
- good cherry preserves
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- wine
- egg yolk
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Keep 2 pounds fine flour warm in a bowl and 12 eggs equally warm in lukewarm water
2. Melt 3/4 pound butter in 1 cup sweet cream over a fire, but the milk must not boil
3. Beat all the eggs well and froth them, then stir in 3/8 pound ground sugar, 3 spoonfuls good beer yeast along with the eggs into the flour
4. Beat this until it becomes a dough that loosens from the pan, add cinnamon and lemon zest as desired, and also a bit of salt
5. Lay the dough on a floured baking table, work it together a bit, and then roll it out the thickness of a knife back
6. Prepare good cherry preserves, which must be cooked not too thick with sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest, and wine
7. Lay 2 spoonfuls of preserves in heaps on the dough, brush the dough around each heap of preserves with egg yolk
8. Beat a piece of dough the same size as the bottom over it, press down firmly around the preserves, and cut out these pancakes with a pastry wheel
9. Place them in a moderately warm location so they begin to rise
10. Then fry them slowly in lard, shaking the pan frequently during baking
11. When they are nice and golden, lay them on blotting paper and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
