## Ingredients

- turbot
- lemon juice
- salt
- water
- milk
- lemon slices
- onions
- bay leaves
- hollandaise sauce or clear butter

## Directions

1. Scale the turbot cleanly
2. Gut it on the side
3. Clean it white with lemon juice and salt
4. Make a crosscut on its back
5. In a copper round dish put water, milk, some salt, lemon slices, onions, and some bay leaves
6. Let this boil
7. Remove from fire and place the fish in it
8. Set it on a gentle coal fire and let it poach slowly until cooked through
9. Serve with hollandaise sauce or clear butter in a sauceboat
