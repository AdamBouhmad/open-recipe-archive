## Ingredients

- flour to accommodate 2 eggs
- 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Using same dough as for noodles, cut dried, finely rolled dough into 2-finger-wide strips
2. Stack strips and cut small squares (Fleckerl)
3. See noodle soup for further preparation
4. Alternatively, rolled dough can be cut like ribbons with a crinkle wheel and called Lasagne Soup, or cut into Schifferln with a crinkle wheel or knife
