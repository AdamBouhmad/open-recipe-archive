## Ingredients

- 12 drams butter
- 12 drams flour
- 12 drams vanilla sugar
- 1/2 liter hot milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites, beaten stiff
- butter for the mold
- flour for dusting
- puff pastry
- marmalade
- pitted and peeled firm apples

## Directions

1. Place butter, flour, and vanilla sugar in a casserole, stir finely, and pour in 1/2 liter boiling milk while stirring constantly.
2. Stir over heat until the dough is dry and releases from the spoon.
3. Pour the dough into a deep bowl and let cool completely.
4. Gradually mix in the 8 egg yolks, then lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites.
5. Line a buttered mold, dusted with flour, with butter puff pastry.
6. Fill with half the dough mixture.
7. Fill peeled apples (freed from the core house) with marmalade and place in the filled mold.
8. Add the second half of the dough mixture and cover with a butter pastry sheet, pressing the edges together.
9. Bake in the oven at first at strong heat, later at reduced heat, for a total of 1 1/3 hours.
10. Turn the mold into a dish, dust the soufflé with sugar, and serve.
