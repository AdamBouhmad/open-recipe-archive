## Ingredients

- barley
- meat broth
- egg yolks
- nutmeg flower
- chives
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cook selected and well-washed, blanched barley in meat broth in a pot
2. Stir often during cooking to prevent scorching, and do not place too close to the fire
3. When serving, whisk 3 to 4 egg yolks into the well-strained soup set aside for serving
4. Pour it over the barley, stir it, sprinkle with some nutmeg flower, and garnish the soup with some finely chopped chives
5. Serve halved lemons on a separate plate for expressing juice into the soup
