## Ingredients

- Macheanz apples
- preserves or pistachio cream
- wine dough
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and hollow out the center.
2. Fill with preserves or pistachio cream.
3. Wrap in wine dough.
4. Bake very slowly.
5. Glaze with sugar.
