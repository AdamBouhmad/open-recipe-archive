## Ingredients

- 1 well-hung and cleaned pheasant
- 8 deca bacon, finely chopped
- 1 goose liver
- 12 deca truffles, diced
- Second half of goose liver, diced
- Stock
- Red wine
- Soup
- Root vegetables
- Juniper berries
- 6 deca black bread, sliced in leaves
- Bacon slices
- Fat

## Directions

1. Cut the skin on the pheasant's back and carefully remove the skeleton without damaging the skin
2. Remove the meat from the skeleton and mince it with 8 deca bacon and 1 goose liver
3. Fry in a mortar and pass through a sieve
4. Mix the passed mixture with 12 deca diced truffles and diced second half of goose liver
5. Spread the pheasant skin on a board and fill with this forcemeat
6. Sew together in the natural pheasant shape, tuck the feet into the cavity, wrap with bacon slices
7. Place in a pan and roast in the oven at moderate heat, basting frequently with stock and fat
8. The removed bones are finely pounded and placed in a casserole with fat, root vegetables, juniper berries, and 6 deca sliced black bread
9. Toast slightly, add 1/4 liter red wine and equal amount of soup, reduce well, pass, and strain
10. Serve with the arranged pheasant
