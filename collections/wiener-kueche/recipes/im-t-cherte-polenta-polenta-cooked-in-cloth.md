## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams flour
- 1/2 liter cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Put 16 decagrams flour in a deep bowl
2. Gradually add 1/2 liter cream and stir well
3. Add 4 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs and salt, and stir
4. Butter a white, dense cloth (serviette) and sprinkle it with flour, dust the cloth somewhat
5. Hang it in a deep bowl and put the dough mixture on it
6. Tie the cloth tightly above the dough
7. Put it in boiling water and cook for 3/4 hour
8. Remove the polenta from the cloth and arrange on a dish
9. Pour hot butter over it
10. Serve with beef, roast or game
11. Can be served whole or in pieces
