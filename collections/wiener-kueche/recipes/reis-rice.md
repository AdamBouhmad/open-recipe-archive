## Ingredients

- 1 bowl rice
- 1½ bowls water
- 5 dkg butter or fat
- 1 onion, studded with cloves (optional)
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the selected rice in a casserole with cold water (1 bowl rice to 1½ bowls water), 5 dkg butter or fat, optionally an onion studded with cloves, and salt.
2. After it has boiled for a few minutes, pull it back from the heat, cover it and let it steam for 30–40 minutes.
3. Do not stir the rice before the water has evaporated, or it will become mushy.
4. If after this time it is not yet soft, pour in a little hot water and let it finish steaming.
5. If serving in a mold, rinse the mold first with hot water, press the rice in and turn it out.
6. Garnish the center with curled parsley.
