## Ingredients

- 4 anchovies
- 6 olives
- 1 tablespoon capers
- Butter
- Soup

## Directions

1. The preparation is the same as the anchovy sauce, except only 4 anchovies, 6 olives and 1 tablespoon capers are used
2. If desired, before serving two anchovies cut into cubes, 1 tablespoon whole capers and some tablespoons of cream can be added to the finished passed sauce
3. This sauce is served with roast meat but can also be added to veal schnitzel or in general to meat or game
