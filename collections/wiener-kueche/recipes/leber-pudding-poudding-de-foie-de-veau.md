## Ingredients

- 60 deca calf's liver
- 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- 1 small piece onion
- pinch of marjoram
- butter
- 16 deca rind-removed, finely cut rolls
- milk
- 10 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- spices (optional)
- 1 1/4 liter white breadcrumbs
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scrape and finely cut 60 deca calf's liver
2. Meanwhile roast 1 teaspoon finely cut green parsley, 1 small piece onion, and a pinch of marjoram in some butter
3. Pound 16 deca rind-removed finely cut rolls soaked with milk with the liver and the roasted mixture and pass through a sieve
4. Beat 10 deca butter and stir in the passed liver, 4 egg yolks, some salt, and spices if desired
5. Lightly fold in 1 1/4 liter white breadcrumbs mixed with the snow from 4 egg whites
6. Fill into a mold greased with butter and lined with flour
7. Cook in steam
