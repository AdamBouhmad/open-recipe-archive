## Ingredients

- Veal loin, finger-thick slices
- Bacon
- Fat
- Sour cream
- Soup
- Lemon juice
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cut finger-thick slices from a veal loin
2. Pound them lightly
3. Salt them
4. Lard one side with fine bacon
5. Dip in hot fat and place larded side up in a casserole
6. Roast in the oven for several minutes until bacon is slightly golden
7. Pour off the fat and baste the meat with sour cream
8. Continue roasting with frequent basting
9. Remove meat to a serving dish
10. Add some soup to the defatted, strained sauce and add lemon juice
11. Cut the lemon peel into fine strips and add to the sauce
12. Stir well, strain, and serve with the cutlets
