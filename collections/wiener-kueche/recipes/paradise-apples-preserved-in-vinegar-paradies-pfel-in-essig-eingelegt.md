## Ingredients

- Fresh, ripe paradise apples
- Wine vinegar (cooled)
- Rendered tallow (finger-thick layer)

## Directions

1. Place fresh, ripe paradise apples in a storage glass
2. Pour cooled wine vinegar over them and over this a finger-thick layer of rendered tallow
3. Bind the glass with a bladder
4. Alternatively, one can also mix vinegar and water in equal parts, boil them and pour this cooled mixture over the paradise apples
5. These can also be preserved instead of in glasses in earthenware or wooden vessels, which should however be narrower at the top so that less tallow is needed for pouring over, which must be melted again and poured over each time paradise apples are taken from the vessel
6. The vessels must moreover either be covered with a fitting lid or sealed airtight with paper or bladders
