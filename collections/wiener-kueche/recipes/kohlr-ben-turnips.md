## Ingredients

- 4-6 turnips
- salt
- 5 dkg fat
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the turnips and cut them into leaf-like or dice-like pieces.
2. Place them in cold salted water and cook for 30 minutes.
3. Make a light roux from 5 dkg fat, pour it up with the turnip water or broth, add the turnips, salt to taste, and cook for 20 minutes.
