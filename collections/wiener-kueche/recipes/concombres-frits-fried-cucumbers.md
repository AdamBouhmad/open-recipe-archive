## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- flour
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. After peeling the cucumbers, cut them lengthwise into four equal pieces, then into finger-length and finger-thick pieces or any desired pieces.
2. Salt them and let stand 10 minutes.
3. Place on a cloth, dry them off, dust lightly with flour and fry light golden in hot butter or fat.
4. Lay on tissue paper to remove excess fat.
5. Can also be fried in oil.
6. Serve quite hot as garnish with pot roasts, beef or similar.
7. The cucumbers can also be dipped in beer or wine batter before frying and served with meat dishes or fried fish.
8. The same method can be used for small gourds, but these must be served immediately after frying.
