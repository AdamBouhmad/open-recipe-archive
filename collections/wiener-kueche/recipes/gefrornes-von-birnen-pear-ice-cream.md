## Ingredients

- About 30 pieces ripe Bergamotte or Butter pears
- Water
- Juice of 3 lemons
- 1 pound sugar, clarified

## Directions

1. Peel about 30 pieces of ripe Bergamotte or Butter pears
2. Cut into 4 pieces and remove the cores
3. Cook with a little water into a marmalade
4. Press through a hair sieve into a terrine
5. Add the juice of 3 lemons
6. Clarify 1 pound sugar and when cooled mix with the pear mixture
7. Place in the freezing pan
