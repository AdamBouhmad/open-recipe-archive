## Ingredients

- 15 decagrams cooked beef
- 2 bread rolls soaked in milk
- 7 decagrams butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Create a base by beating 7 decagrams butter until creamy
2. Stir in 3 egg yolks
3. Add the cooled minced meat (hash)
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add chopped parsley and well-drained bread rolls
6. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Fill the mixture into a buttered, floured pudding mold
8. Steam for 45 minutes until set
9. Turn out the pudding and serve warm with Hollandaise as an appetizer, or without sauce as a soup accompaniment
