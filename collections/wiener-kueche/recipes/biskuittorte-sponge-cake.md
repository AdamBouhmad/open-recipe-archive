## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 12½ dkg sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 12½ dkg flour

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 12½ dkg sugar for 20 minutes
2. Fold in 6 stiff egg whites together with 12½ dkg flour
3. Fill the mixture into the form
4. Bake slowly
5. The cake can be cut through 1-2 times and filled in various ways
6. Fill with jam, ice white or sprinkle lightly with sugar
