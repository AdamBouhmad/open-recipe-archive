## Ingredients

- 1 pound bratwurst
- Whole eggs (one per person)
- Rye bread, very thinly sliced and crisped
- Fresh parsley, finely cut
- Chives, finely cut
- Fat, properly salted broth

## Directions

1. Slice rye bread very thin and dry it until crisp. Place in soup pot and sprinkle finely cut parsley and chives over it. Cut bratwurst into thumb-sized pieces and fry well. Add bratwurst pieces and whole eggs to the soup pot. Pour hot fat and properly salted broth over everything, cover and serve. When the soup is properly simmering, the eggs will reach proper firmness in a few moments.
