## Ingredients

- Rissole dough with farce (see Dir. 1123)
- Desired cooked fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare them like rissoles with farce (see Dir. 1123), with the difference that instead of farce, use any desired cooked fruit
2. After the rissoles have been baked, dust them with sugar and arrange them in a bowl, serve as a flour dish
3. The fruit rissoles can also be filled with whipped egg whites, 3 tablespoons, honey and breadcrumbs, 6 tablespoons of each
