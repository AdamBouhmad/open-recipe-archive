## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Vinegar
- Chicken blood
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon
- Onion
- Cloves
- Whole pepper
- Allspice
- Flour
- Broth
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. When the chickens are slaughtered, catch the blood in a pot with some vinegar, stir it with the vinegar so it does not coagulate.
2. When the chickens are cleaned, salt them lightly and dress them nicely, arrange some bacon leaves in a casserole, cut an onion in half in the middle, lard each part with three or four cloves, add some whole pepper and a few grains of allspice, add the chickens, and let them stew without browning.
3. Remove them, dust two spoonfuls of flour on them, let it become brownish, fill with broth, add some lemon juice, and let it cook well together.
4. Strain it through a sieve, add the chickens, and let them cook until tender.
5. Then mix the blood well with the sauce and let it cook together for only a few minutes.
