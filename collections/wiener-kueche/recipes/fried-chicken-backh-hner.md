## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned and washed chicken
- salt
- breadcrumbs for breading
- hot lard
- lemon slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Quarter the cleaned and washed chicken and salt it.
2. Let it sit for one hour.
3. Dry it between cloth, bread it, and fry it very slowly in hot lard.
4. Drain and arrange on a platter with lemon slices and parsley decoration.
5. The neck with head can remain on one quarter and be fried together.
6. Stomach and liver can also be breaded and fried last.
