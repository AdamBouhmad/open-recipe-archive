## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- cold water
- salt water
- 12 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- 16 deca breadcrumbs
- milk
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put 1 calf brain in cold water
2. Clean and remove skin
3. Cook in salt water and pass through a sieve
4. Beat 12 deca butter until fluffy
5. Stir in the brain, 4 egg yolks, some salt, nutmeg, and 16 deca breadcrumbs soaked in milk
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
7. Fill into mold and bake or cook in steam
