## Ingredients

- 3 pounds white cheese
- 1 pound almonds, of which one-quarter pound should be bitter
- Half pound sugar with 2 lemons zest
- Some pounded cinnamon
- 1 pound butter
- 21 eggs

## Directions

1. Pound the peeled almonds with 4 egg whites very finely, then similarly pound the cheese and butter as finely as possible.
2. Beat all this in a bowl with 18 egg yolks until very foamy.
3. Gradually mix in the almonds, sugar and cinnamon, and when all is done, slowly fold in the snow from the remaining eggs.
4. Coat a form or rimmed dish with butter, fill the mass into it, but only half full, and bake the soufflé in an hour and a half, not too hot.
