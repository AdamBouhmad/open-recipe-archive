## Ingredients

- crayfish, approximately 25 pieces
- milk
- semmels (rolls)
- 6 eggs
- butter
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Break open approximately 25 live crayfish
2. Chop the tails very finely
3. Soak semmels in milk
4. Mix the chopped tails with the milk-soaked semmels and 6 eggs
5. Cut off the heads from the shells
6. Remove the bitter parts
7. Pound the shells with a good piece of butter in a mortar until fine
8. Soften them covered for a while
