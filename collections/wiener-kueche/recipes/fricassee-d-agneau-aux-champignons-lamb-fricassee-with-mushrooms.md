## Ingredients

- Lamb meat, cut into pieces
- Lukewarm water
- Butter
- Mushrooms, cleaned and finely sliced
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- Flour
- Broth as needed

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb meat into pieces and place in lukewarm water
2. Let it rest for 1/2 hour so it becomes white in color
3. Put butter into a casserole and braise some cleaned, finely sliced mushrooms in it
4. Add finely chopped green parsley
5. Dust with flour and roast it slightly
6. Pour the necessary broth over it
7. Add the dried meat from the water, stir well together, cover and braise gently
8. During braising, stir the meat carefully and frequently
9. When braised, place the meat on a platter, remove fat from the sauce and pour it over the meat
