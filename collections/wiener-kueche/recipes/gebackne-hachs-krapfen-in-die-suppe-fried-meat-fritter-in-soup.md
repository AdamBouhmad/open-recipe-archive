## Ingredients

- scraps of butter or short crust dough
- flour
- leftover meat
- 1 egg
- lard
- soup

## Directions

1. Mix flour with scraps of butter or short crust dough
2. Knead it out to the thickness of a knife back
3. Make a good hash from leftover meat
4. Beat an egg into it
5. Spread it one knife-back thick on the dough
6. Fold the dough double
7. Cut small fritters with a spoon
8. Fry them in lard
9. Serve with soup
