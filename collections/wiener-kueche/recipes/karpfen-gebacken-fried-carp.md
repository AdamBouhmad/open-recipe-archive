## Ingredients

- 1 carp, scaled and gutted
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs for breading
- Lard or fat for frying
- Lemon slices
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean, gut, and wash the carp
2. Cut the fish in half and cut three-finger-width pieces through it
3. Hack the pieces
4. Salt each piece well and let rest for 1 hour
5. Wipe off with a cloth
6. Bread the pieces thoroughly
7. Fry very slowly in plenty of hot lard or fat
8. Drain on blotting paper
9. Arrange on a long platter
10. Top with lemon slices and parsley garnish
11. Serve
12. If cooking the heads as well, fry them whole at the very end very slowly
