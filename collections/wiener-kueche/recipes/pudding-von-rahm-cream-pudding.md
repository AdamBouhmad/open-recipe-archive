## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter cream
- 4 decagrams flour
- salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. In a small pot, beat the cream with 4 decagrams flour, a pinch of salt, and 3 egg yolks until smooth
2. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Prepare and garnish the same way as the Cheese Pudding
