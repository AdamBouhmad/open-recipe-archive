## Ingredients

- ½ liter flour
- 32 deca rice
- 1 liter milk
- 12 deca butter
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 16 deca raisins

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from ½ liter flour and let it rest
2. Slowly cook 32 deca selected rice in 1 liter boiling milk
3. Pour the cooked rice into a deep bowl and stir cold with 12 deca butter, 16 deca vanilla sugar, and ½ teaspoon salt
4. Gradually stir 8 egg yolks one after another into the rice
5. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 8 egg whites last
6. Stretch the strudel dough finely, brush with melted butter, distribute the rice mixture on top
7. Sprinkle with 16 deca selected raisins and proceed as described in the Ham Strudel recipe
