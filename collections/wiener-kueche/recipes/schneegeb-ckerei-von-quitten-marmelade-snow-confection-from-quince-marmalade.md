## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 16 Deca finely sifted sugar
- same amount of quince marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat 2 egg whites until stiff.
2. Mix thoroughly with 16 Deca finely sifted sugar and an equal amount of quince marmalade.
3. Drop large spoonfuls onto a baking sheet lined with wafers.
4. Sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and let dry in an airy location.
