## Ingredients

- 1 pound early cherries
- 0.5 pound sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Remove the stems halfway from the cherries
2. Clarify and skim half a pound of sugar with egg white
3. Place the cherries in the clarified sugar
4. Boil the mixture a couple of times
5. Serve in a bowl with the syrup
