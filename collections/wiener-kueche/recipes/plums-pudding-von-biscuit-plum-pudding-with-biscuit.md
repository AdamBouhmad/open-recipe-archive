## Ingredients

- 14 deca flour for biscuit
- various fruits, citron, almonds, pistachios, dates as desired
- 1/2 liter milk or cream
- 3 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 8 deca finely pounded fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare and bake a biscuit from 14 deca flour
2. After completely cooling, cut round or into cubes
3. Place in a buttered and floured mold whose bottom has been lined with various fruits, citron, almonds, pistachios, dates in desired pattern, or alternately light and dark biscuit pieces
4. Cover with remaining biscuit mixed with desired fruits
5. Beat together 1/2 liter milk or cream with 3 whole eggs, 5 egg yolks, and 8 deca finely pounded fruit
6. Pour evenly over the biscuit
7. Steam covered for 1 hour
