## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter fine flour
- 1/4 liter white wine
- 1 tablespoon oil
- Salt
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter of fine flour into a deep bowl, pour 1/4 liter of white wine gradually while stirring constantly along with 1 tablespoon of oil which has been previously made hot and boiling on the plate, stir with a knife-point of salt into a smooth dough.
2. Lightly fold in the whipped egg white from 4 eggs and use the dough for various purposes.
