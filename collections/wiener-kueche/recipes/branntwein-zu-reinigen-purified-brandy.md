## Ingredients

- 14 Maß corn brandy with various spices
- 14 Maß milk
- 5 Pfund fresh beef cleaned of all fat

## Directions

1. Add the milk and beef to the brandy
2. Distill the mixture
3. At the beginning, check the receiving vessel and remove the forerun which has a fusel taste
4. All subsequently distilled brandy is pure, pleasant and spicy
5. Mix equal parts sugar water with it
