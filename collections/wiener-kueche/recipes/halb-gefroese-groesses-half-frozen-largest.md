## Ingredients

- Half-frozen mixture
- Butter sauce
- 4 egg yolks
- Flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Poach the half-frozen mixture until tender and cut into small cubes
2. Make a butter sauce and beat 4 egg yolks into it
3. Add the frozen mixture, let it cool, place it on a flour-dusted board
4. Form small sausages or dumplings from it and proceed as with the Ragout Croquettes
