## Ingredients

- 32 Deka butter
- 32 Deka dark flour
- 32 Deka blanched and passed almonds
- 24 Deka finely crushed sugar
- 1 knife tip lemon zest
- 1 knife tip spice
- 1 knife tip nutmeg
- 1 knife tip cinnamon
- 4 egg yolks
- jam for filling

## Directions

1. Rub butter and flour together finely on a board
2. Add blanched and passed almonds, sugar, lemon zest, spice, nutmeg, and cinnamon
3. Make a well and add egg yolks
4. Quickly work into a dough and cover
5. Let rest for 1/2 hour in a cool place
6. Roll out dough to finger thickness on a flour-dusted board
7. Press out with a torte form and place in a butter-greased and flour-dusted torte form
8. Brush with beaten egg
9. Make thin strips from remaining dough and arrange in a lattice pattern on top
10. Brush with beaten egg and edge with a finger-thick dough border
11. Brush the border with beaten egg
12. Bake for 1/2 to 3/4 hours
13. Remove and when completely cool, fill the lattice with jam and sprinkle sugar on the edges
