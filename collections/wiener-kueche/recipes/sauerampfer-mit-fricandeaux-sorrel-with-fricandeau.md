## Ingredients

- sorrel
- butter or fat
- good meat soup
- salt
- veal shank
- fine bacon
- glace

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the sorrel thoroughly
2. Cut it finely with a chopping knife
3. Set butter or fat on heat, braise it like spinach
4. Add good meat soup and salt
5. Cut the fricandeau from a veal shank into two or three pieces: one from the nut, one from the kaiserbein, and one from the top shell
6. The fricandeau should be about one hand's breadth wide and somewhat elongated
7. Remove the skin and all nerves properly
8. Blanch briefly beforehand so they are easier to lard
9. Lard them on both sides with fine bacon
10. Lay these fricandeau in a braise
11. Let them cook until tender and soft
12. Before serving, turn them in glace so they become brown and get a beautiful shine
13. Lay them properly on the arranged sorrel
