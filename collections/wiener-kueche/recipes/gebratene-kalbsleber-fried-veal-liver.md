## Ingredients

- calf liver
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- fat
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into finger-long and knife-back-thick slices, as with roasted calf liver, lightly salt them, dredge on both sides in flour, dip in beaten salted eggs, coat in breadcrumbs and fry in finger-deep, very hot fat.
2. Arranged on a platter, serve it with fine vegetables at table.
