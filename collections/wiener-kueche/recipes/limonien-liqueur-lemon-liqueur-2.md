## Ingredients

- yellow peel from 6 lemons
- 5 seidels brandy
- 1 and one-half pounds sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Cut off the yellow peel from 6 lemons
2. Clean away the white part completely
3. Cut the yellow peel finely
4. Put it into a bottle or jug containing 5 seidels brandy
5. Simmer 1 and one-half pounds sugar in 1 glass water until well skimmed
6. Pour the sugar syrup under the brandy
7. Let stand for three weeks
8. Shake well and let stand for another twelve days
