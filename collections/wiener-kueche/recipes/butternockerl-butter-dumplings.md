## Ingredients

- 5 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 2 egg whites
- 6 dkg flour

## Directions

1. To the mixture of 5 dkg butter, add 2 egg yolks and salt.
2. Fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 2 egg whites together with 6 dkg flour.
3. Let it stand for a while.
4. With a spoon dipped in hot broth, form small dumplings, place them in the strained boiling beef broth, and let them cook slowly for 5 minutes.
5. If the dough is too loose, add more flour.
