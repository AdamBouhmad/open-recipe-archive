## Ingredients

- 16 deca chocolate
- 1/2 liter boiling water

## Directions

1. It is prepared in the same way as the 'Creme im Dunst mit Vanille' (No. 1426). One softens 16 deca chocolate on a baking sheet in a warm oven, then puts it into a saucepan, stirs it thoroughly smooth with 1/2 liter boiling water over the fire and mixes it into the already prepared cream without vanilla.
