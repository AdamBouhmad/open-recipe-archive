## Ingredients

- Dough from any yeast dough recipe
- Flour for dusting
- Lukewarm fat for coating fingers
- Hot fat for deep frying
- Sugar for garnish

## Directions

1. Take equal-sized pieces of dough with a spoon and place them on a flour-dusted board.
2. Flatten them lightly and evenly with floured hands and let them rise uncovered in a warm place until they increase in size.
3. Once risen, stretch the dough gently in the center with fingertips dipped in lukewarm fat, creating a hole about finger-thickness.
4. Hold each piece in the middle with three fingertips, allowing the edges of the dough to rest over your hand, and carefully place the dough in hot fat with fingertips directed downward.
5. Fry until golden on both sides.
6. Remove with a slotted spoon and place briefly on paper.
7. Arrange on a dish and dust with sugar before serving.
