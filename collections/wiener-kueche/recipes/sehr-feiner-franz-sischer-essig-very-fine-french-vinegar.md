## Ingredients

- Strongly frozen wine vinegar
- Leaves of dragon root
- Many rocambole
- Laurel leaves
- Basil
- Very little peppermint leaves
- Some shallots with the rocambole
- Leek
- Angelica roots
- Some calamus roots
- Some anchovies

## Directions

1. Observe the proportions well according to preference, but do not add too much
2. Chop everything coarsely
3. Digest it in a wide-mouthed, capacious bottle closed with a bladder, so that it can be shaken from time to time
4. Make a hole in the bladder by means of a needle prick
5. Digestion in the sun is better than at a warm oven
6. If you love such vinegar or need it often, it is good to prepare a quantity of it at once
7. When it is filled into bottles and well stoppered and placed in the right location, it will keep longer than one lives
