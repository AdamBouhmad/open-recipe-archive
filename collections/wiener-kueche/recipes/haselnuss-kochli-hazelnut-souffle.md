## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter
- 16 Deka sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 30 Deka fine ground, sieved hazelnut kernels
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 16 Deka sugar and 8 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix this with 30 Deka fine ground, sieved hazelnut kernels
4. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
5. Put this mixture into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold
6. Cook ½ to ¾ hour either in steam or bake in the oven
7. Sprinkle with fine ground sugar immediately and serve
