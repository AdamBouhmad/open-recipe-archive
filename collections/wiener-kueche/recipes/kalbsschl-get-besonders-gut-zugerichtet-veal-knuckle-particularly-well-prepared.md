## Ingredients

- one fresh veal knuckle
- lard the thickness of a small finger
- rosemary
- bay leaves
- two parts wine
- one part water
- pepper
- ginger
- mace
- cloves
- basil

## Directions

1. Skin a beautiful fresh veal knuckle
2. Lard it with lard the thickness of a small finger, using a particularly long larding needle so the lard can be drawn through completely
3. Remove the bone from the knuckle entirely
4. Place it in a pot lined with rosemary and bay leaves
5. Pour two parts wine and one part water over it
6. Season with pepper, ginger, mace, and cloves
7. Add some more rosemary, basil, and bay leaves
8. Cover the pot tightly and seal it with clay so the spirit cannot escape
9. Set it on coals and let it simmer slowly
10. When the knuckle is sufficiently braised or stewed, carefully remove the clay and lid from the pot
11. Lift out the knuckle, place it in a bowl, and strain the broth over it through a sieve
12. During stewing, move the pot back and forth occasionally so the knuckle does not stick to the bottom
