## Ingredients

- millet porridge cooked in milk
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Place the millet porridge, which has been cooked well in milk, into a casserole dish brushed with butter
2. Top with 4 tablespoons butter and bake for 1/2 hour in the oven
3. Serve the dish in the casserole or turned out onto a platter
