## Ingredients

- Fresh sour cherries
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Use fresh sour cherries, which after being removed from their stems are crushed and put into a bottle, filling only the third part
2. Add some cinnamon and a few cloves
3. Fill the bottle completely with genuine wine vinegar, cork it well and place it in the sun
4. When the cherries have settled to the bottom after some time, strain the vinegar through a felt sack, fill it into bottles, cork and seal these, and store them in a cool place
