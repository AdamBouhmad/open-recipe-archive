## Ingredients

- several small fattened goose livers
- half a pound of truffles, cut into slices
- onions, parsley and shallots, sautéed in butter and then finely cut
- bacon, scraped with a knife, half as much as goose liver
- soaked and squeezed out bread roll
- 5 or 6 whole eggs
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the goose livers finely with a chopping knife
2. Sauté the truffles in butter
3. Finely cut the sautéed onions, parsley and shallots
4. Prepare bacon scraped with a knife, half the amount of goose liver
5. Soak and squeeze out bread roll
6. Pound all ingredients finely in a mortar with pepper, salt and nutmeg
7. Use this forcemeat for both cold and warm pastries, as well as for stuffing roasted poultry on the spit and goose livers studded with truffles
