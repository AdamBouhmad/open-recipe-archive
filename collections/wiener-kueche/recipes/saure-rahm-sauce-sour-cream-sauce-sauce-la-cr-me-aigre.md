## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons fine flour
- 1/2 liter cream
- 10 tablespoons butter
- 1 knife point salt
- some pepper
- nutmeg
- 8 tablespoons butter (additional)
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Put 6 tablespoons fine flour in a saucepan and gradually stir in 1/2 liter cream
2. Add 10 tablespoons butter, 1 knife point salt, some pepper, and nutmeg
3. Mix everything well together and stir it on the fire until the sauce forms
4. Remove it from the fire and add 8 tablespoons butter in small pieces and the juice of 1 lemon
5. Stir it on the hot plate until the sauce has a foamy appearance, being careful not to let it boil
6. Serve it hot with boiled chicken and root vegetables
