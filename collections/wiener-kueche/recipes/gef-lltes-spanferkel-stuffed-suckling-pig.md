## Ingredients

- Suckling pig liver
- Bacon, soaked
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Sage
- Veal meat from the loin
- Veal kidney and its fat
- Nutmeg
- Cloves
- Onions
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make a forcemeat from the pig's liver, soaked bacon, mushrooms, salt, pepper, fine herbs, and sage
2. Let it sweat in a casserole
3. Fill the suckling pig with this forcemeat from the inside
4. Sew it closed so the filling does not escape
5. Roast as usual
6. Alternatively: remove the skin from the raw suckling pig as one would strip a hare skin
7. Cut out the lean meat and chop it together with veal from the loin, veal kidney and its fat, salt, pepper, nutmeg, cloves, onions, thyme, and parsley
8. Fill this mixture into the skin, keeping the feet up to the joints
9. Sew it closed and bake it in a pie pan or oven
10. Remove the twine when serving
