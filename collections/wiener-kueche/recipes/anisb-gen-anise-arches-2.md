## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 eggs' weight of sugar
- 2 eggs' weight of flour
- anise seeds

## Directions

1. Beat 3 whole eggs with 3 eggs' weight of sugar for 15 minutes
2. Mix in 2 eggs' weight of flour
3. Using two spoons, make small discs on buttered, floured baking sheet
4. Sprinkle with anise seeds
5. Bake in moderate heat until light brown
6. Carefully lift with a knife and immediately twist over a wooden spoon handle
