## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 spoon tarragon vinegar
- 1 spoon lemon juice
- 1 spoon water
- 10 dag clarified butter
- salt
- mustard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a warm mayonnaise (see Appetizers: Eggs with Mayonnaise) from 4 egg yolks, 1 spoon tarragon vinegar, 1 spoon lemon juice, and 1 spoon water.
2. Pull it from the heat slightly and stir in 10 dag clarified but only lukewarm butter thread by thread.
3. Season with salt, mustard, and vinegar and serve separately to the cooked cauliflower.
4. The sauce must be thick-liquid.
5. It can also be served with asparagus or cooked warm fish.
6. If the hollandaise cannot be served immediately, place in a water bath so it does not break.
7. If it breaks, add a little cold water and stir until smooth.
