## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter finely ground poppy seeds
- 1-2 Deka vanilla sugar
- 1/8 liter boiling milk
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest
- 1 blanched almond

## Directions

1. Place ground poppy seeds and vanilla sugar in a saucepan
2. Pour 1/8 liter boiling milk over them
3. Stir over heat until the mixture thickens
4. Mix in 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest to taste and 1 blanched almond
