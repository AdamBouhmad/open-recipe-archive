## Ingredients

- ½ l Milk
- 2 Eggs
- Salt
- 20 dkg Flour
- 8 dkg Butter or fat

## Directions

1. Mix milk with eggs, salt, and flour until smooth
2. Heat butter or fat in a flat pan until hot
3. Pour in flour mixture and let cook on the stove for 30 minutes, stirring frequently
4. The schmarren should be soft yet grainy
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve on a round platter with compote
