## Ingredients

- Cleaned and chopped corncrakes
- Fish farce
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. The cleaned and chopped corncrakes are braised and, once completely cold, are filled into the pâté with fish farce as described in the 'Poularde Pâté' recipe (No. 1060).
