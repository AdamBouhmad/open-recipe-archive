## Ingredients

- 2 calf thymus glands or 1 half calf thymus gland
- Salt water
- 16 dessert spoons butter
- Equal amount of flour
- Finely chopped parsley
- Onion (optional)
- 1/2 liter soup stock
- 1/4 liter vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 liter sour cream (optional)
- 1 pinch lemon peel
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- The prepared veal thymus gland

## Directions

1. Boil the calf thymus glands in salt water until soft.
2. Remove from water and cut into almond-like pieces once cooled.
3. In a pot, toast the flour in butter, add finely chopped parsley and onion if desired, and brown.
4. Deglaze with 1/2 liter soup stock and 1/4 liter vinegar.
5. Stir well together, salt and pepper to taste.
6. Add 1/4 liter sour cream if desired and 1 pinch of lemon peel.
7. Add the juice of 1/2 lemon with the prepared veal thymus gland pieces.
8. Let cook together well.
9. The sauce should be pleasantly sour and not too thick.
10. Optionally, a small head of cauliflower can be braised with it, but removed before serving.
11. The veal thymus gland can also be served with fried eggs if desired.
