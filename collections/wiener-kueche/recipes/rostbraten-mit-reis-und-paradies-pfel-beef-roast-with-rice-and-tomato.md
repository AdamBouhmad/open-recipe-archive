## Ingredients

- beef roast pieces
- 1/8 liter rice
- about 3 tablespoons tomato marmalade

## Directions

1. Prepare as described above
2. Instead of potatoes, add 1/8 liter rice and about 3 tablespoons of tomato marmalade to the liquid of the half-tender roast
3. Braise until tender
4. Alternatively, use 3 pieces of raw tomato, torn apart with seeds removed, and braise without straining when serving
5. Rice can also be braised without tomatoes
6. Serve salt gherkins or mustard gherkins with this roast
