## Ingredients

- 1.30-1.40 kilograms beef tenderloin
- flour
- salt
- hot fat
- butter
- parsley
- Maitre d'hôtel butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat like the venison beefsteaks and cut into two-finger-wide pieces.
2. Lightly pound with the back of a knife and push back into round shape.
3. Just before cooking, salt on both sides.
4. Coat one side with flour.
5. Place flour-side down in a flat pan with hot fat.
6. Brown and serve as the venison beefsteaks.
