## Ingredients

- rissole dough (No. 1049)
- 1 teaspoon any farce or forcemeat
- eggs for coating
- breadcrumbs
- very hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare rissole dough and roll to the thickness of a knife-back
2. Place 1 teaspoon of farce in rows 3 fingers apart along the length
3. Make a 3-finger-wide strip of empty dough between each row of filling
4. Brush dough around the farce mounds with beaten eggs
5. Fold dough over the farce, press down lightly
6. Cut out semicircles between each farce mound with a crescent-shaped cutter
7. Dip all cut-out rissoles in beaten eggs, coat in breadcrumbs
8. Fry in very hot fat on both sides
9. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on blotting paper
10. Arrange on a platter lined with a serviette and serve as an appetizer
