## Ingredients

- 1 liter red wine
- 1/4 kilogram sugar
- orange and lemon peel
- cinnamon pieces
- spices

## Directions

1. In 1 liter red wine add 1/4 kilogram sugar, some orange and lemon peel, pieces of cinnamon and some spices
2. Let it become very hot but not boil
3. Strain through a fine sieve
4. This mulled wine is always served warm in small glasses
