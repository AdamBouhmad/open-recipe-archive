## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg Beef (shoulder cut)
- 1 pair Sausages
- 6 dkg Hot fat
- 1 Onion (finely chopped)
- Bread (cut into strips)
- Beef broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. 20 minutes before serving, roast finely chopped onion and small pieces of noodle-cut bread in hot fat in a casserole
2. Pour strained beef broth over the mixture
3. Add sausages (washed and cut into strips)
4. Cook together for a few minutes
5. Optionally, stir in 1 egg, but the soup must not boil after this
