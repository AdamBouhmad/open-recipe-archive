## Ingredients

- 14 dkg flour
- 21 dkg butter, diced
- 14 dkg sugar
- 14 dkg raw grated, sieved almonds
- egg white
- pistachio nuts

## Directions

1. Make a dough of 14 dkg flour, 21 dkg diced butter, 14 dkg sugar, and 14 dkg raw grated, sieved almonds on the board
2. Roll out thinly in portions as the dough breaks easily; if needed, knead in 1 small piece of butter
3. Cut into ellipse shapes and halve lengthwise to resemble bread slices
4. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and bake light brown
5. Cool and brush with vanilla butter, then place on baking sheet again and put in an open oven for a few minutes to dry
6. Brush the top with egg white and sprinkle with softened, chopped pistachios
