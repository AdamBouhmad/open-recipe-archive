## Ingredients

- young cabbage
- salt water
- veal cutlet
- bacon
- bread roll
- milk
- cooked cabbage
- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful cabbage that is still young and not large
2. Clean and wash it thoroughly
3. Cook the head whole in salt water
4. Strain off the water and lay it on a sieve so the water drains completely
5. Make the filling as follows: cut a veal cutlet, some bacon, and a bread roll soaked in milk
6. Press it out well
7. Take some cooked cabbage and cut everything very finely together
8. Beat a piece of butter, add the cut filling into it
9. Beat in two whole eggs and two yolks
10. Add a little salt and ground pepper
11. Take the cabbage on a board
12. Carefully loosen the leaves and place the filling on them
13. Cover it again with leaves
14. Form a cabbage head from it and wrap it in flour
15. Put some bacon leaves in a cooking pot, some ham slices, lay the cabbage on top so it does not stick
