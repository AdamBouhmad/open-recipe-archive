## Ingredients

- 1 young goose
- 1 roll
- 8 dkg butter
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the young goose, and cook in salted water for 1.5-2 hours, except for the liver
2. Prepare a parsley sauce with 8 dkg butter and pour it over the strained goose broth, season with salt
3. Cut the young goose into small pieces (except head and feet), add to the soup, simmer for 25 minutes, and serve with bread slices
4. If the liver is not large enough to be roasted separately, add it after 1 hour and let it cook with the rest
