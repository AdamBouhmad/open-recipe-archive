## Ingredients

- 1 liter old milk
- 2 grams powdered rennet

## Directions

1. To obtain sweet whey, put 1 liter of old milk in a coffee pot and add 2 grams of powdered rennet (obtainable at the apothecary) to it.
2. Stir it well together and let it become hot to the boiling point, whereby the rennet curdles.
3. Strain this through a cloth and obtain the pure sweet whey.
