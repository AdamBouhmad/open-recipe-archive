## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter
- 4 eggs
- 25 Deka semolina total, including 4 tablespoons already stirred in
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Deka butter until creamy
2. Gradually add 4 eggs, adding 1 tablespoon semolina after each egg
3. Mix in 2 tablespoons milk
4. Let the dough rest covered for 2 hours
5. Proceed as with the flour Nockerl
