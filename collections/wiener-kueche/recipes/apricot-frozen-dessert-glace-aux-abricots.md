## Ingredients

- 15 to 20 pieces ripe apricots
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 28 deca grams sugar
- 1/4 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Pass 15 to 20 pieces of ripe apricots through a fine sieve into a deep bowl
2. Add the juice of 2 lemons and 28 deca grams sugar dissolved in 1/4 liter cold water to this mass
3. Crush the stones of the apricots somewhat and stir them into the mixture
4. Let this sit covered for 1 hour, then weigh it to 22 degrees, strain it through a fine sieve, and proceed further as with the 'Ordinary Strawberry Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1746)
