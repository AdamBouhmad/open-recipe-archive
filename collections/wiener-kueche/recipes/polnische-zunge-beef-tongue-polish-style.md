## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- 3/8 liter stock
- 1/4 liter soup
- sour cream
- capers
- 2 tablespoons currants
- 2 tablespoons small raisins
- 2 tablespoons finely sliced almonds or 2 tablespoons pignoli nuts cut into 2 pieces
- lemon peel
- diced lemon
- 1 tablespoon smoked, cooked, diced tongue
- 1 knife-tip juniper berries if desired

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in the usual way
2. Prepare the caper sauce as above, but pour 3/8 liter stock and 1/4 liter soup over the browned meat
3. After adding cream and capers, add 2 tablespoons currants, 2 tablespoons small raisins, 2 tablespoons finely sliced almonds or pignoli nuts, lemon peel, diced lemon, 1 tablespoon smoked cooked diced tongue, and if desired, a knife-tip of crushed juniper berries
4. Heat through and pour over the sliced tongue
