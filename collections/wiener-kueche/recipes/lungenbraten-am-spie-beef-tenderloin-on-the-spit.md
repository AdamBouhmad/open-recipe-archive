## Ingredients

- well-aged beef tenderloin
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- paper
- glace
- small roasted potatoes
- potato purée
- macaroni
- brown piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Take a well-aged beef tenderloin, skin it as with a naturally roasted tenderloin, spit it and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Insert a small spit lengthwise through the middle and bind it to a large spit with this
3. Place 4 sheets of paper brushed with butter on the tenderloin so that the buttered side lies against the meat, then bind with string
4. Roast slowly for 1 hour
5. 1/2 hour before the meat is completely tender, remove the paper and make a stronger flame so the tenderloin gets a nice color
6. Before removing from the spit, dust again with salt
7. Arrange on a platter, brush with dissolved glace and garnish with small roasted potatoes, potato purée, macaroni and various other items
8. Serve a brown piquant sauce in a sauce boat
