## Ingredients

- 32 Deka beaten butter
- 10 Deka caster sugar
- 4 hard-boiled and passed egg yolks
- 48 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter with caster sugar and hard-boiled and passed egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Mix this well with flour
3. Shape the dough into a loaf on a flour-dusted board and cover it
4. Let it rest for 1/2 hour in a cold place
5. Roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board and proceed further as indicated in the recipe for 'brown pâte brisée torte' (no. 1482)
