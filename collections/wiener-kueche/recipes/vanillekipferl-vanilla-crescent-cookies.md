## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 16 dkg butter
- 10 dkg raw grated almonds
- 5 dkg sugar
- vanilla sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Make a dough on the board from flour, butter, raw grated almonds, and sugar
2. Form it into a roll
3. Cut off equal-sized pieces
4. Form each into a crescent
5. Place on an unbuttered baking sheet
6. Bake at higher heat until light brown
7. While still warm, roll in vanilla sugar
