## Ingredients

- butter dough
- half pound almonds
- half pound sugar
- zest of an orange or lemon, finely cut
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 loth citron (Citronat), finely cut
- egg white

## Directions

1. Make a butter dough, roll it out more in length than width, like a noodle dough
2. Blanch and pound half pound almonds finely, then equally much sugar finely pounded, cut the zest of an orange or lemon finely
3. Put all this in a mixing bowl, add 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks, stir for half an hour so it becomes quite fluffy
4. Spread it on the butter dough, spread it with a knife, sprinkle it with 2 loth finely cut citron
5. Roll the strudel loosely together, place it on a butter-brushed baking sheet, brush it with egg white, and bake it with sustained heat
