## Ingredients

- Dough as prepared for flat cakes
- Fresh cherries or morello cherries
- Butter for the baking sheet
- Egg for brushing
- Finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough for flat cakes
2. Roll out strongly knife-back thick on a flour-dusted board
3. Cut so long and wide that it fits the size of the baking sheet on which the cake will be baked
4. Roll up loosely on the rolling pin (strudel roller) and unroll directly onto a butter-greased baking sheet
5. Cover densely with fresh cherries or morello cherries, leaving a 2-finger-wide empty border on all 4 sides
6. Fold up the border and brush with beaten egg
7. Bake in the oven for 3/4 to 1 hour at fairly strong heat
8. Remove from oven, sprinkle with finely sifted sugar, cut into desired pieces, arrange in a bowl and serve warm or cold as desired
