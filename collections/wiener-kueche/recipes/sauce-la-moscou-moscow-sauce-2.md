## Ingredients

- Ham
- Shallots
- Crayfish butter
- Coulis
- Thick sour cream
- Lemon juice
- Ordinary butter (alternative)

## Directions

1. Sweat ham and shallots in crayfish butter.
2. Fill with coulis.
3. Stir in thick sour cream.
4. Once everything is cooked, strain through cheesecloth.
5. Add lemon juice.
6. Can also be made with ordinary butter instead of crayfish butter.
