## Ingredients

- 56 deca flour
- 32 deca butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 16 deca fine sifted sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board
2. Cut 32 deca butter into it
3. Rub finely with both hands
4. Make a well in the middle
5. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt, 16 deca finely sifted sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped ingredients into the well
