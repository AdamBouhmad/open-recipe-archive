## Ingredients

- 5 dkg butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- finely chopped ham from 7 dkg roll ham
- 2 stiffly beaten egg whites
- 5 dkg fine breadcrumbs
- 2 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add egg yolks, salt, chopped parsley, pepper and finely chopped ham
2. Fold in egg whites, breadcrumbs and flour
3. Let stand for a while, form small dumplings and place in boiling beef broth for 5 minutes
