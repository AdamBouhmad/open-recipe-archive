## Ingredients

- 24 deca blanched almonds
- water
- 16 deca sugar
- 1 liter milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. After 24 deca blanched almonds have been pounded very finely with some water, boil them with 16 deca sugar in 1 liter milk for 1/2 hour. Then strain the mixture through a sieve, mix in 8 egg yolks and 1 whole egg and proceed further as indicated.
