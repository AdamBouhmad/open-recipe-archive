## Ingredients

- 1 kilo sea tongues
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh green parsley, finely cut
- Onion, finely minced
- Flour
- Paradise sauce

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the sea tongues according to the method for gudgeons
2. Cut into equal-sized pieces and brush with oil
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, finely cut green parsley and finely minced onion
4. Let rest for 1 hour
5. Dry with a cloth, dip in flour and fry golden on both sides
6. Arrange in a bowl and serve with Paradise sauce
