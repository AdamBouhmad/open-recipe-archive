## Ingredients

- Pickled herrings, one pair
- Butter
- Eggs, as many as needed
- Water, one tablespoon
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the pickled herrings clean, remove the skin, bone them, and chop finely.
2. Melt butter in a pan, break in as many eggs as needed.
3. Add one tablespoon of water, salt, and pepper.
4. Stir in the chopped pickled herring and make a scrambled egg dish, which should be somewhat moist.
