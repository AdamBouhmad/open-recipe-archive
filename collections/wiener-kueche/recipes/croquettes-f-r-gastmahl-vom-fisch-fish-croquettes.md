## Ingredients

- 32 tablespoons of any braised fish flesh
- 1/8 liter boiled peas
- 10 pieces softened crayfish tails
- 3 hard-boiled eggs, cut with a knife
- 5 pieces cleaned, finely diced pickled cucumbers
- 4 tablespoons soaked, squeezed out breadcrumbs without crust
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- Milk liver herbs if available
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Shred the fish flesh and place in a deep bowl
2. Add boiled peas, softened crayfish tails, hard-boiled eggs cut with a knife, finely diced pickled cucumbers, soaked and pressed breadcrumbs, egg yolks, whole egg, herbs if available, salt and pepper
3. Mix everything together well and proceed as with the Ragout Croquettes
