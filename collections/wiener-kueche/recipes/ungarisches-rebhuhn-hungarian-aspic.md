## Ingredients

- 2 veal feet
- 2 pork feet
- 15 dkg lean pork meat
- 6 l prepared and washed root vegetables, cut into leaves
- 1 scallion
- ¼ l vinegar
- 2 spoons salt
- 1½ l water

## Directions

1. Boil all the prepared and washed ingredients in a pot like aspic for 2-3 hours.
2. When the meat is tender, remove it (leaving the rest to continue cooking), cut it into small pieces and put it in a bowl or mold.
3. Strain the finished cooking broth over it, place the bowl in a cold place until the aspic is set.
4. Unmold it and serve with vinegar, oil and also with onion rings.
