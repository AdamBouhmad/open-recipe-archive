## Ingredients

- veal breast
- lard, diced
- root vegetables, thinly sliced
- 2 mushroom pieces, diced
- 1 onion piece
- white broth
- bread or roll slices
- milk cream
- paprika

## Directions

1. Place diced lard and thinly sliced root vegetables, mushroom pieces, and onion piece in a casserole.
2. Place the veal breast on top and braise covered.
3. When the liquid has evaporated, remove the bones from the meat.
4. Pour white broth over, add bread or roll slices, and braise until the meat is tender.
5. Add milk cream, let it cook well through.
6. Remove the meat, cut into neat pieces, and arrange heaped on a platter.
7. Season the defatted sauce with paprika, bring to a boil once, and pour through a sieve over the meat.
8. The sauce should be yellowish in color.
9. Serve with noodles, braised peas, or potatoes as desired.
