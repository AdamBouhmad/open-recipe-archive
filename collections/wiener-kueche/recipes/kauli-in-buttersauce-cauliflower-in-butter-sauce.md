## Ingredients

- Cauliflower, cleaned and cut into quarters if roses are large
- Salt water
- Flour
- Butter
- Good broth
- Nutmeg, finely ground
- Milk cream
- Fried chicken or frog cutlets

## Directions

1. Cook cleaned cauliflower in salt water
2. Make a roux with 2 to 3 spoonfuls of flour in warmed butter
3. Fill with good broth and add a little finely ground nutmeg and 2 to 3 spoonfuls of milk cream
4. Arrange the cauliflower with shortened stems in beautiful order in the shape of a rose on the platter, raised high
5. Pour the well-cooked sauce over it and let it come to a boil
6. Ensure the roses show beautifully when serving
7. Garnish with fried chicken or frog cutlets
