## Ingredients

- ½ l milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- flour as needed
- 20-25 dkg cooked beef hash, or 2 small pork kidneys, or 3 large mushrooms
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Make pancakes from the milk, 1 egg, salt, and flour (see Menu 16)
2. Distribute the hot prepared hash on the pancakes, roll them, place on a dish, garnish with parsley, and serve
3. Alternatively, fill them with kidneys or mushrooms
