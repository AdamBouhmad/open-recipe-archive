## Ingredients

- one fresh liver
- 1/2 Pfund smoked bacon
- lemon peel
- shallots
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 Pfund grated bread crumbs soaked in milk
- 10 whole eggs
- thyme or basil
- fresh butter
- caul

## Directions

1. Scrape the raw liver so that all veins and membranes separate
2. Put it in a dish
3. Stir in 1/2 pound smoked bacon, finely cut, some finely cut lemon peel and shallots, salt and nutmeg
4. Stir in 1 Pfund grated bread crumbs soaked in milk
5. Gradually add 10 whole eggs, and if desired, some thyme or basil
6. Fill the mass into a casserole thickly coated with fresh butter
7. Cover it with caul soaked an hour beforehand
8. Bake it slowly in the oven
