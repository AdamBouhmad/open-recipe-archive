## Ingredients

- Vegetables and broth (prepared as for French root vegetable soup)
- bouched chicken liver per person
- braised chicken meat pieces

## Directions

1. Prepare the soup somewhat stronger than French root vegetable soup, adding one bouched chicken liver per person.
2. Add braised chicken meat pieces to the soup pot.
