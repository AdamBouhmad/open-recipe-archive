## Ingredients

- 10 medium potatoes
- 1 whole egg
- flour as needed
- 1 teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes until soft, peel them, and pass through a sieve
2. After cooling completely, place on a board
3. Work into a smooth but not too soft dough with 1 whole egg, necessary flour, and 1 teaspoon of salt
4. Roll the dough on a floured board into thumb-thick ropes
5. Cut into finger-width pieces and form each into small finger-thick noodles with pointed or blunt ends
6. Add to boiling salted water and cook covered for 10 to 15 minutes
7. Drain in a colander and rinse with cold water
8. Toast 8 tablespoons of breadcrumbs or semolina in 16 tablespoons of hot butter
9. Add the drained potato noodles, season to taste, stir well
10. When hot, arrange in a platter using a spoon and serve
