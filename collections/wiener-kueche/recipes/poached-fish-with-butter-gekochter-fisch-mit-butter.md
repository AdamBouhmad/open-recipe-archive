## Ingredients

- ¼ liter vinegar
- 8 tablespoons salt
- root vegetables
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- thyme
- fish
- hot butter
- chopped parsley
- potatoes
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. In the fish pan, cook ¼ liter vinegar, 8 tablespoons salt, root vegetables, 1 onion, 5 peppercorns, 2 bay leaves and thyme for 30 minutes
2. Lower the fish into the pan, pull the pan back (the fish must not boil as it will fall apart)
3. Let the fish steep without boiling for 8-10 minutes
4. Lift it out carefully, tie it up, place quickly on the fish platter
5. Drip with hot butter, sprinkle with chopped parsley, garnish with potatoes
6. Serve with hot butter or Hollandaise sauce separately
7. Slice the vegetables and lay them on the rim of the platter
