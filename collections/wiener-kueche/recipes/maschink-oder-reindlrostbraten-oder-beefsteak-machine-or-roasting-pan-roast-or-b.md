## Ingredients

- 1 roast or 1 beefsteak of 25 dkg
- 2 anchovies or 4 mushrooms
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- kipfel potatoes, raw sliced
- butter

## Directions

1. Trim the meat of skin, fat and bone, wipe it off, tap lightly with the back of the knife, salt, pepper it.
2. In a pan on the stove, quickly sear both sides in some fat (5 minutes).
3. Put some fat in the roasting pan, lay in the roast or beefsteak, then layer raw sliced kipfel potatoes, salt, pieces of anchovy or sliced mushrooms, butter pieces, then more potatoes and ingredients, continuing until the pan is full.
4. Cover and place in the oven for 45 minutes.
5. Serve the meat in the roasting pan. Instead of anchovies, sliced kohlrabi or yellow turnips can be used.
