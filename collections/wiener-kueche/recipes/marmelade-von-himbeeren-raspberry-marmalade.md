## Ingredients

- 8 pounds raspberries
- 4 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Pass 8 pounds of well-selected raspberries through a fine sieve
2. Place the sieved berries in a casserole over a charcoal fire
3. Stir constantly until reduced to half
4. Clarify and cook 4 pounds of sugar as described above
5. Add the berry mass to the sugar
6. Proceed as for cherry marmalade
