## Ingredients

- Lobster or crayfish
- One shallot
- Fine herbs
- Meat of one sardine
- Strong vinegar, several spoonfuls
- A little oil or butter
- Lemon juice
- White meat broth

## Directions

1. Boil the lobster or crayfish until soft
2. Remove the head innards and pound them with a shallot, fine herbs, the meat of a sardine
3. Stir over gentle heat with several spoonfuls of strong vinegar and a little oil or butter, almost to boiling
4. Dilute it with some lemon juice and white meat broth and pass it through a clean sieve
5. Meanwhile, cut the meat from the tails and claws of the lobster or crayfish into dice
6. Place this into the strained sauce, add salt and a little spice, and let it become hot together
7. Then fill it into the patties
