## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Eggs
- Cold pâté (walnut-sized pieces)
- Beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Make puff dough and let it rest sufficiently
2. Roll out strongly knife-back-thick on a flour-dusted board
3. Cut out round discs using a pastry cutter
4. Place half the discs at finger-width distance on a water-moistened baking sheet
5. Brush with beaten eggs, leaving a finger-width border all around
6. Place a walnut-sized piece of cold pâté in the center of each disc
7. Cover each with the remaining round discs from the board, with the sides that were on the board facing upward rather than on the pâté
8. Press down lightly and brush with beaten eggs, leaving a strong knife-back-width border all around
9. Bake in the oven at fairly strong heat until golden yellow
10. Remove with a knife from the sheet and arrange in a bowl lined with a napkin
