## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons peeled, finely shredded rolls
- 1/2 liter white stock
- 2 tablespoons grated horseradish
- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut nutmeg or garlic
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. 8 tablespoons peeled, finely shredded rolls are poured with 1/2 liter white stock and cooked with 2 tablespoons grated horseradish, 1/2 teaspoon finely cut nutmeg or garlic, a little salt, and nutmeg
2. 2 egg yolks are whisked with 2 tablespoons cream and mixed well with the bread sauce
3. Eggs and cream can also be omitted
