## Ingredients

- Turkey wings, plucked and cleaned
- Fat
- Soup
- Salt
- Spices
- Sauce
- Breadcrumbs
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Place the plucked and cleaned wings in a casserole containing some fat and braise covered for approximately 1/2 hour.
2. Pour some soup over them, salt and season them, and braise covered until they are soft.
3. Set them aside and let them cool in the broth.
4. When cooled, remove the wings and place on a cloth.
5. After completely cooling, cut the wings into desired pieces, brush with bound sauce, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, dip in beaten eggs, coat again in breadcrumbs, and bake in a casserole in finger-high, very hot fat.
