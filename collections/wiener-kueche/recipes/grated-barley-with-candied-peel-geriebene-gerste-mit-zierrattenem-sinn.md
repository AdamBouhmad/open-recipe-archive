## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- flour as needed
- 0.25 liter milk
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons lemon or orange sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 3 egg whites
- 12 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough from 1 egg and enough flour as it will hold
2. Grate this dough on a grater
3. Cook the grated barley in 0.25 liter of boiling milk under constant stirring until well cooked
4. Add 8 tablespoons butter and 8 tablespoons lemon or orange sugar
5. Stir well together and let it cook covered until thickly done
6. Set aside and when it has cooled slightly, stir in 4 egg yolks
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 4 egg whites
8. Pour this mixture into a buttered dish and bake for 0.5 hour in the oven
9. After this time, cover the top crust with egg white snow mixed with 12 tablespoons sugar
10. Dust this with finely ground sugar and bake in an open oven for about 10 minutes
11. Remove the dish and serve on a second serving dish
