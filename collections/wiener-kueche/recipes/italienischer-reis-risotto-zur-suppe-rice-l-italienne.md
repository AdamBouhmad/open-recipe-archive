## Ingredients

- 24 deca butter
- 1 piece finely chopped onion
- 3 pieces cleaned, washed, dried mushrooms, finely chopped
- 60 deca rice
- 1/8 liter white beef broth
- approximately 1.5 liters beef broth
- salt
- 8 deca grated Parmesan cheese
- fried sausages or other garnish

## Directions

1. Heat 24 deca butter until very hot in a casserole
2. Lightly roast 1 piece of finely chopped onion
3. Add 3 pieces of cleaned, washed, dried, finely chopped mushrooms to the onion and let them roast lightly without browning
4. Add 60 deca rinsed rice to the roasted ingredients and let it roast for approximately 4 minutes with frequent stirring
5. Add 1/8 liter white beef broth and let it simmer covered for 15 minutes
6. If the broth evaporates before the rice is done, add approximately 1.5 liters more broth and some salt and let it simmer for another 10 minutes
7. Add 8 deca grated Parmesan cheese to the rice and stir well
8. On a platter, place a portion of the risotto using a wooden spoon and arrange pieces of fried sausages or other garnish around it
