## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams flour
- 18 dekagrams butter
- salt
- 2 spoons wine
- some cold water
- 1 deciliter whipped cream

## Directions

1. Make butter dough from the ingredients.
2. Roll the finished dough long and thin.
3. Cut long two-finger-wide strips from it.
4. Loosely wrap them around the foam roll cylinder so that the dough edges overlap approximately half a centimeter.
5. Compress the dough slightly at the bottom.
6. Place the cylinders seam-side down on a buttered sheet.
7. Bake at very high heat until light brown.
8. After cooling, fill them with whipped cream using a pastry bag.
9. Sprinkle with sugar and serve.
10. The cylinders must be completely cooled before new strips are wrapped.
