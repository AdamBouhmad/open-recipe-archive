## Ingredients

- 1 small bread roll
- 1 seidel cream
- 1/2 quarter-pound butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Pour 1 seidel cream over 1 small bread roll
2. Let it boil and stir over heat until fine
3. Let cool
4. Beat 1/2 quarter-pound butter until high and fluffy
5. Add 2 whole eggs and 10 egg yolks, one after another
6. Add sugar to taste and lemon zest
7. Make a ring mold of dough
8. Bake until done
