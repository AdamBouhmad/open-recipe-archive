## Ingredients

- 10 teaspoons cleaned, quickly washed anchovies
- 30 teaspoons butter

## Directions

1. Finely pound 10 teaspoons of cleaned, quickly washed anchovies with 30 teaspoons of butter in a mortar
2. Pass through a sieve and arrange neatly on a plate
