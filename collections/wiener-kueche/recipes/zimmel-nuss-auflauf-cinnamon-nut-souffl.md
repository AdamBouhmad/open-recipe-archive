## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 20 Deca cinnamon-nut powder
- 12 Deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 Deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg whites (snow)

## Directions

1. Cook 20 Deca cinnamon-nut powder with constant stirring into 1/2 liter boiling milk and let cook, then add 12 Deca butter, stir well together and set aside
2. Meanwhile stir 6 egg yolks and 12 Deca vanilla sugar for 1/2 hour, add the cooled mass, stir well together and finally fold in lightly the snow from 6 egg whites
3. Fill half of this mass into a buttered form dusted with flour
