## Ingredients

- ½ kg kipfel potatoes
- prepared root vegetables (yellow turnip, parsley root, celery)
- 1 apple
- 1 cucumber
- 1 red beet
- 2 fresh hard-boiled eggs
- ½ l cooked peas
- mustard
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- mayonnaise
- salad greens (optional)
- 10 h bird's lettuce

## Directions

1. Wash, cook, drain, and peel ½ kg potatoes, cut into small dice
2. Cut prepared root vegetables into small dice, cook in salted water for 20 minutes, and drain
3. Peel and dice the apple, cut the cucumber
4. Mix potatoes, root vegetables, apple, cucumber, mayonnaise, peas, mustard, salt, oil, and vinegar together
5. Pile in a cone shape in the middle of a glass bowl
6. Slice or cut 2 hard-boiled eggs into 8 pieces
7. Wash and cook the red beet for 2 hours, peel, cut into thin slices or julienne strips
8. Wash salad greens and dress with salt, vinegar, and oil
9. Decorate the cone with hard eggs, red beet, and salad greens
