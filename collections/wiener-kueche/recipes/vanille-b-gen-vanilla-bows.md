## Ingredients

- One quarter pound sugar
- Vanilla pounded with sugar
- Snow from 2 egg whites
- A little lemon juice
- 2 loths flour

## Directions

1. Place the sugar, pounded vanilla, and snow from 2 egg whites into a pot
2. Add a little lemon juice
3. Stir until it becomes thick
4. Mix in 2 loths of flour
5. Spread it on broad wafers
6. Bake until beautiful
