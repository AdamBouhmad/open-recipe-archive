## Ingredients

- Apples
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat, very hot
- Sugar, finely ground
- Optional: Rum

## Directions

1. Using a fine round corer, remove the seed cavity and peel the apples
2. Cut them into small finger-thick slices
3. Sprinkle with sugar, moistened with a little rum if desired, and let rest for some time
4. Take the apple slices with a fork, dip in egg batter, and fry on both sides in very hot fat until golden brown, shaking the pan frequently
5. Remove with a slotted spoon, place briefly on blotting paper, arrange on a platter, and serve hot with finely ground sugar sprinkled on top
