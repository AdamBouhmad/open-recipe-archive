## Ingredients

- 1 liter boiling milk
- 25 Deca semolina
- Salt
- 12 Deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- Snow from 8 eggs
- Flour

## Directions

1. Stir 25 Deca semolina into 1 liter boiling milk, let the semolina cook completely, add some salt and pour into a deep bowl
2. Beat 12 Deca butter until fluffy, beat in 8 egg yolks
3. Add the prepared semolina, stir everything well together
4. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 eggs
5. Pour into a shallow casserole dish greased with butter and dusted with flour and let it bake for 3-4 hours
6. Cut the schöberl into pieces of any size or cut out with a cutter
7. A few minutes before serving, add the cut pieces with some soup to the soup pot so the schöberl can warm up, then add the necessary soup and serve
