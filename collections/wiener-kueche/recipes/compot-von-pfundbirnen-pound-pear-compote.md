## Ingredients

- Pound pears
- Water
- Lemon slices
- Sugar
- Wine or water

## Directions

1. Peel these pears, cut them in half in the middle, remove the cores and any stones.
2. Cook them in a casserole with a couple of lemon slices in water until half soft, then lay them for a quarter hour in cold water.
3. Afterwards cook them very soft with sugar and wine or water until the sauce shows itself nice and jellied.
4. Arrange them on a plate and use them.
