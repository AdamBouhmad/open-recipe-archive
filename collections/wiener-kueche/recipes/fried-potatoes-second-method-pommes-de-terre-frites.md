## Ingredients

- Raw potatoes
- Cold water
- Fat
- Salt

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes, wash them in cold water, and cut them into slices 2 knife-back-widths thick.
2. Fill deep pans (as shown in Plate II with wire sieves inside) three-quarters full with fat and place on the fire. Put the cut potatoes into one pan in hot fat, stirring constantly until they have a fine crust but still feel soft in the middle.
3. Lift them out with the sieve, salt them, and shake them into the fat in another pan which has meanwhile become boiling hot, let the potatoes bake golden yellow with constant stirring, then transfer them to blotting paper or clean cloth to remove excess fat, and arrange them immediately on a platter.
4. They must be served immediately.
