## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- jam
- Spanish windmill mass
- sugar
- optional: coffee essence, strawberry essence, or other essence

## Directions

1. Prepare a butter or beaten egg cake dough, roll out finger-thick, cut out round, fill into a buttered and floured cake form, and bake slowly in the oven
2. Remove the hoop from the form. After the cake has cooled, spread with jam, cover 3 fingers high with Spanish windmill mass
3. Decorate as desired using a paper cone with Spanish windmill mass, sprinkle with sugar, and let dry in an open oven
4. Instead of a cake made from butter dough or sugar dough, one can also use a Sachertorte, which however should only be finger-high
5. Optionally, add 1 teaspoon coffee essence, 1 teaspoon strawberry essence, or other essence to the Spanish windmill mass
