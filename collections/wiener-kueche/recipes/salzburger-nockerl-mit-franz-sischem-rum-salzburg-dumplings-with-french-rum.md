## Ingredients

- Salzburg dumplings (No. 1328) finished cooking in milk
- 4 egg whites
- 16 tablespoons finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Arrange the cooked Salzburg dumplings on a platter
2. Evenly distribute the beaten egg whites from 4 eggs mixed with 16 tablespoons finely sifted sugar over the top
3. Sprinkle once more with finely sifted sugar
4. Bake in the oven for approximately 10-15 minutes with the oven open and serve immediately
