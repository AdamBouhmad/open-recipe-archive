## Ingredients

- veal cutlet from the leg
- small bacon
- beef broth
- pepper
- mace
- whole onions
- garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Cut thick slices from a veal cutlet, about three fingers wide, tap them slightly with a hammer, and lard them as one would a partridge with small bacon.
2. Place them in a pan, let them brown, pour beef broth over them, and add pepper, mace and whole onions.
3. When it has reduced somewhat, add a couple of cloves of minced garlic and let it simmer in the sauce.
