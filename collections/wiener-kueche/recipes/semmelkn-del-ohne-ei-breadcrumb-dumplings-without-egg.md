## Ingredients

- 8 pieces of rolls (32 deca)
- 16 deca lard or pork fat
- 1 teaspoon of finely chopped parsley
- 1 liter flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 liter cold water

## Directions

1. Cut the rolls into cubes and roast them in the lard or pork fat with a teaspoon of finely chopped parsley until they turn golden brown quickly.
2. In a deep bowl, combine 1 liter of flour and 1 teaspoon of salt.
3. Stir the flour with 1/2 liter of cold water into a thin dough.
4. Add the roasted rolls and stir everything together well.
5. Instead of water, milk can also be used for these dumplings.
6. Follow all further steps as directed in the recipe for ordinary bread dumplings.
