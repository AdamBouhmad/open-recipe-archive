## Ingredients

- One Seidel good cream
- A piece of sugar
- Some whole vanilla
- 6 egg yolks
- Egg white
- One spoonful flour
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook one Seidel good cream with a piece of sugar and some whole vanilla
2. Let it cool, beat 6 egg yolks and the egg white each separately very well
3. Stir one spoonful of flour into this, mix in the rest, set on a coal fire, and stir until sufficiently thick
4. Pour it into a mold, sprinkle it strongly with sugar, and brown it
