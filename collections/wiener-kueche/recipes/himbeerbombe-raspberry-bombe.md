## Ingredients

- fresh raspberries
- whipped cream
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Pass the raspberries through a sieve.
2. Mix with firmly whipped cream and sugar to taste.
3. Follow the further procedure as in Feiertagsmenu 1: Kaffeebombe.
