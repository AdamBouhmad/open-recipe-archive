## Ingredients

- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. In autumn when parsley is still good and vigorous, pick as much as you will need throughout the winter, clean it well, wash it thoroughly, and chop it very finely.
2. Take a good portion of butter and let it become hot, add the chopped parsley, let it fry a little, and then let it stand for a few minutes, whereupon remove it with a spoon.
3. Be sure to drain off the juice that has settled at the bottom, otherwise it will spoil; then place it in another vessel, let it melt again, and let it cool, and preserve it in this manner.
4. Prepared in this way it will keep for a long time and can be used very well.
