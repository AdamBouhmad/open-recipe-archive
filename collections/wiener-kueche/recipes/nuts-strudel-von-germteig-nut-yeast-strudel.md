## Ingredients

- yeast dough as per recipe for Mocha Strudel 1256
- 8 deca nuts
- 8 deca roasted and ground hazelnuts
- 16 deca finely sifted sugar
- 1 pinch grated coffee

## Directions

1. Prepare yeast dough as specified for Mocha Strudel (No. 1256)
2. Prepare the nut filling: pound nuts very finely and mix with roasted ground hazelnuts, finely sifted sugar, and a pinch of grated coffee
3. Apply the nut filling to the strudel dough as specified for Poppy Seed Strudel
