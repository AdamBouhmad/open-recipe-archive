## Ingredients

- 3–4 white turnips
- salt
- water
- 4 dkg fat
- onion
- beef broth or water
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips and cut them into small cubes
2. Cook in salted water for 30–40 minutes
3. Make a light onion roux with 4 dkg fat, deglaze with beef broth or water
4. Add the drained turnips
5. Season with salt, sugar, and vinegar to taste
6. Cook for 30 minutes more
