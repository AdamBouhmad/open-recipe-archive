## Ingredients

- rice croquette mass from recipe 1136
- 3 tablespoons strong black coffee

## Directions

1. Prepare the mixture exactly as for the Rice-Croquettes (No. 1136)
2. When the rice is almost soft, add 3 tablespoons of strong black coffee and let it cook together with the mixture
