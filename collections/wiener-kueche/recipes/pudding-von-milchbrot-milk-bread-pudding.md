## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound milk bread
- 16 egg yolks
- 4 loths fresh butter
- 1/2 pound crushed sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- egg whites (from the eggs used)

## Directions

1. Rub off the crust from half a pound of milk bread, soak it, and squeeze it out, place it in a bowl, and beat in 16 egg yolks one after another, stirring for a good hour.
2. Then stir it for a while longer with 4 loths fresh butter, 1/2 pound crushed sugar, and the rubbed zest of a lemon.
3. Mix in the snow beaten from the above egg whites, treat it like any pudding, except that it must cook for 1.5 hours in water, and pour a wine sauce over it when serving.
