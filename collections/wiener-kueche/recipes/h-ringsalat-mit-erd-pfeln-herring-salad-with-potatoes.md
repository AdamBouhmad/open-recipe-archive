## Ingredients

- Herring
- Potatoes
- Onions
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean a herring, remove the bones and cut the remaining part into small dice
2. Mix it with cleaned and cut potatoes and onions
3. Place on a serving dish
4. Dress with vinegar, oil and sugar
