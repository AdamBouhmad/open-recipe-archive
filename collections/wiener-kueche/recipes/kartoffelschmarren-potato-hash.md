## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- 8 dkg fat
- 1 chopped onion

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes, place them in cold salted water, cook for 20-30 minutes, drain them, peel them, and cut them into leaf-like slices.
2. In a casserole, roast 1 chopped onion in 8 dkg hot fat (the onion can also be omitted).
3. Add the potatoes, salt them, and roast them with frequent stirring until they have a brown crust.
