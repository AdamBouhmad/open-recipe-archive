## Ingredients

- Fine wheat flour
- Milk
- Good butter
- Egg yolks
- A little sugar
- Egg whites, beaten to foam
- Cinnamon, vanilla, lemon peel, or orange peel (any one used sparingly)

## Directions

1. Stir fine wheat flour into milk over strong heat until a thick paste forms
2. Let this paste cool completely
3. Half an hour before serving, take it out again
4. Stir good butter, some egg yolks, and a little sugar cold together with the milk paste
5. When everything is well combined, fold in the foam from beaten egg whites
6. Season as desired with cinnamon, vanilla, lemon peel, or orange peel, each used individually and sparingly
