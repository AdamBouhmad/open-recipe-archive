## Ingredients

- 2 small heads cauliflower
- 5 dag ham
- 2 dag Parmesan cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the cooked cauliflower on a plate with parchment paper, add salt if needed, sprinkle one head with chopped ham and the other with grated cheese, serve hot butter separately.
2. You can also sprinkle the cauliflower with chopped parsley.
