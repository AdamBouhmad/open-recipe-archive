## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Calf's sweetbread
- Prepared mushrooms
- Cock's combs
- Cock's kidneys
- Truffles
- Calf's head (white and soft-boiled, cut into neat pieces)
- Sauce (bound)

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken the same way as for chicken with butter sauce or stewed chicken.
2. When it is tender-braised, cut it into any desired pieces and arrange these running round in a bowl or pastry nest.
3. Meanwhile, braise a calf's sweetbread, cut it with the meat into any desired pieces; prepare 10 pieces of prepared mushrooms, the same number of cock's combs, cock's kidneys, truffles, and a calf's head white and soft-boiled in salt water, cut into neat pieces.
4. Arrange all this beautifully in the middle of the chicken arranged running round in the bowl and pour over with some bound sauce; serve the remaining sauce in a sauceboat.
5. It must be served very hot.
6. The calf's sweetbread, which has been rinsed several times in lukewarm water, can if desired be braised at the same time as the chicken.
