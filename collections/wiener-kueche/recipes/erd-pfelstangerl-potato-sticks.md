## Ingredients

- 15 dkg flour
- 15 dkg butter
- 21 dkg cooked, peeled, passed, cool potatoes
- egg yolk for brushing
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. On the board make a smooth dough from 15 dkg flour, 15 dkg butter, and 21 dkg cooked, peeled, passed, cool potatoes
2. Fold and knead the dough
3. Roll it out and cut into strips with a pastry wheel
4. Lay them on an unbuttered baking sheet
5. Brush with egg yolk
6. Sprinkle with caraway seeds
7. Bake until light brown
8. Serve immediately
