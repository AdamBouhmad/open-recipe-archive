## Ingredients

- Lamb cutlets, trimmed
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard, very hot
- Piquant sauce or fine vegetables

## Directions

1. Tap the trimmed cutlets with the back of a knife and salt them
2. Dip them in flour, then in beaten eggs
3. Roll them in breadcrumbs
4. Fry them in very hot butter or lard
5. Serve with piquant sauce or fine vegetables
