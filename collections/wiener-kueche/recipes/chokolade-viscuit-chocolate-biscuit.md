## Ingredients

- Twelve egg yolks
- Half a pound sugar
- 6 Loth finely sieved chocolate
- 12 Loth fine flour
- Whites of 12 eggs
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Stir twelve egg yolks, half a pound sugar, and 6 Loth finely sieved chocolate for a quarter hour as usual
2. Then gradually add 12 Loth fine flour, beat the whites of 12 eggs to snow, and fold in slowly
3. Finally fill this mixture into capsules, sprinkle them with sugar, and bake slowly
