## Ingredients

- 16 Deka finely ground sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- 8 Deka mashed potatoes with skin, strained
- 4 Deka finely chopped citron
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped orange zest
- cinnamon
- 1 crushed spice clove
- 48 Deka roasted, strained potatoes
- 9 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together 16 Deka finely ground sugar with 9 egg yolks
2. Add 8 Deka mashed potatoes with skin that have been strained, 4 Deka finely chopped citron, 1 teaspoon finely chopped orange zest, cinnamon, 1 crushed spice clove and 48 Deka roasted, strained potatoes
3. Stir for 1.5 minutes
4. Lightly fold in the whites of 9 eggs
5. Pour this mixture into a cake pan coated with butter and lined with white breadcrumbs
6. Bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour in the oven
