## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- small spits
- twine
- speck or pork fat
- beer

## Directions

1. Prepare the suckling pig as in the previous recipe with the difference that instead of roasting it in the oven, place it on a spit
2. Secure it with small spits to the large spit as well as possible
3. In the absence of small spits, tie the snout and tail with twine so the suckling pig does not rub itself raw from turning
4. Roast it under frequent basting with speck at moderate heat for 1-1 1/2 hours
5. Wipe away any juice emerging from inside the pig with a clean cloth; if neglected, blisters form from which burn spots result, giving the roast a less favorable appearance
6. Once it has a beautiful yellow and crispy crust, remove it from the spit and treat it further as described in the previous recipe
