## Ingredients

- gurnard fish
- milk water
- salt
- oil
- parsley, finely chopped
- anchovies, passed (optional)

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, remove the guts, wash them, place in milk water for 30 minutes
2. Make an incision on the side, salt them, let them rest for 30 minutes and wipe them off
3. Heat several spoonfuls of oil in a pan, place the fish in it, fry them on both sides for 10-15 minutes and transfer them to a platter
4. In the oil, let finely chopped parsley run through, add optionally 1 passed anchovy and pour it over the fish
