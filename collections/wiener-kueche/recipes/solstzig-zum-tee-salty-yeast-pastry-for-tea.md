## Ingredients

- 16 Deca flour
- 4 Deca butter
- 1 heaping tablespoon salt
- Cold milk
- Beaten egg
- Salt
- Caraway seeds
- Anise

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board and make a well in the center
2. Add butter, salt, and enough cold milk to form a smooth, fine dough
3. Shape into a thick disc, cover, and let rest for 1 hour
4. Cut off a 3-finger-wide piece and roll out knife-back-thick on a floured board
5. Cut out crescent shapes and place on a buttered baking sheet at finger-width intervals
6. Cut another piece from the dough, roll out knife-back-thick, cut out leaf shapes, and place on the sheet
7. Continue until all dough is used, forming sticks, pretzels, and similar shapes as desired
8. Brush everything with beaten egg, sprinkle with very finely crushed salt mixed with caraway seeds and anise
9. Bake in a moderately hot oven with careful attention to prevent burning
