## Ingredients

- dried plums
- water
- butter sauce
- red wine
- sugar
- spices
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Place dried, washed plums in a casserole
2. Pour enough water over them so it covers them and let them cook soft
3. Make a butter sauce
4. Add the broth in which the plums were cooked and some red wine
5. Add the softened plums with as much sugar as desired, spices, and finely cut lemon peel
6. Let simmer
