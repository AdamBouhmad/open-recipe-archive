## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo sweet almonds
- 1 tea cup bitter almonds
- boiling water
- cold water
- sugar, as desired
- 2 spoonfuls orange flower water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over 1/2 kilo sweet almonds and 1 tea cup bitter almonds, shell them, wash several times and pound finely with some cold water
2. Place them in a porcelain bowl, pour 1 liter cold water over them and let this stand for about 1/2 hour
3. Press the almonds through a strong clean cloth, rinse them again with some water and press through once more
4. To this almond milk add sugar as desired and 2 spoonfuls of orange flower water
5. Preserve in bottles
6. When serving the almond milk warm, hold the bottle or glass in warm water
