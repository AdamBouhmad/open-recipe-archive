## Ingredients

- Veal round
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon
- Meat mallet
- Bouillon
- Bouillon glace

## Directions

1. Cut strips from a veal round that are finger-long and two fingers wide.
2. After beating these with a meat mallet, salt them and lard with bacon, place them larded side up in a shallow braising casserole in very hot fat and let them braise in the oven with frequent basting with a little bouillon and their own juices.
3. When the bacon has a light yellow color, place the pan on the stove, spread the strips with dissolved bouillon glace, arrange them on a serving dish and serve with the de-fatted juices.
