## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- milk
- chicken or capon broth
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut salsify roots into slices like radishes
2. Simmer them in milk or, better yet, in strong chicken or capon broth until very soft
3. Press through a fine cloth
4. Add a little milk or chicken broth to the pressed mixture along with some egg yolks, cinnamon, and sugar
5. The result should be a thin purée
6. More broth can be added to make a soup instead
7. The spices may be omitted depending on circumstances
