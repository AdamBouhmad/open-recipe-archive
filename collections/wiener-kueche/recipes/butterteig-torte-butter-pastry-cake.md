## Ingredients

- Butter pastry of finest type, made from 56 deca flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Cooked poppy seeds
- Cooked apple filling
- Compote
- Jam of choice
- Cream of choice
- Chestnut purée
- Apple compote with wine and raisins
- Roasted chestnuts dusted with sugar

## Directions

1. After resting for 1 hour, roll out the dough knife-back-thick on a flour-dusted board
2. Cut out about 5 round plates using a cake form and place them 3 fingers apart on a water-dampened baking sheet
3. Make several cuts in each round plate with a knife
4. Brush with beaten eggs
5. On one of the plates, lay finger-wide strips of butter pastry cutout or scraped in a lattice pattern, 2 fingers from the edge, with a finger-wide border remaining free all around
6. Lay a border of the same width on this border, brush with beaten eggs, leaving knife-back-wide borders dry
7. Place all plates in the oven and bake at fairly strong heat
8. Remove the plate from the oven and detach it carefully from the sheet with a thin knife
9. Brush 4 of the baked plates with cooked poppy seeds, cooked apple filling, compote, jam, cream of choice, chestnut purée, or apple compote with wine and raisins
10. Layer them so that a brushed side is covered by a dry side and place last the plate that was covered with the lattice, whose small diamond shapes are filled with stewed cherries or currants, jam of choice, or with the mass used to brush the plates, and dusted with finely pounded sugar
11. If filled with chestnut purée, cover the diamond shapes with roasted chestnuts dusted with sugar and serve the cake warm or cold as desired
