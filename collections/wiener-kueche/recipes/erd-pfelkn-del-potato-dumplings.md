## Ingredients

- good portion of starchy potatoes
- half as much butter as potatoes
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- finely chopped parsley
- 3 egg whites
- bouillon
- sauce bechamel
- clarified butter
- toasted white bread
- good meat broth

## Directions

1. Wash a good portion of starchy potatoes, cook them, and pass them through a wire sieve while still warm after peeling
2. Put them in a grindstone and add half as much butter as potatoes, 6 egg yolks gradually worked in, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and finely chopped parsley
3. Work the whites of 3 eggs well underneath
4. Form dumplings from this, cook them in bouillon, and serve them with béchamel sauce, clarified butter, toasted white bread, and good meat broth
