## Ingredients

- 17 dkg sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 17 dkg flour
- 14 dkg grated hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Beat 17 dkg sugar with 3 whole eggs with the whisk in the mixing bowl until thick.
2. With a wooden spoon, well mix in 17 dkg flour and 14 dkg grated hazelnuts.
3. The mass is thick; fill it into the buttered, floured biscuit form and bake slowly.
4. The next day, cut into thin slices.
5. Serve unbaked or baked, rolled in vanilla sugar, and served with tea.
