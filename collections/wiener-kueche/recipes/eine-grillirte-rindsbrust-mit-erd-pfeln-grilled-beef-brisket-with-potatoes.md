## Ingredients

- A fatty piece of beef (brisket) of desired size
- Butter
- Salt
- Bread crumbs
- Potatoes, peeled and round
- Fat from the braising liquid

## Directions

1. Cook a fatty piece of beef (brisket) of desired size soft in a good braise
2. Coat it well with butter, salt it, and sprinkle with bread crumbs
3. Roast it on both sides on the grill
4. In the braise of this brisket cook uniformly round, peeled potatoes until soft
5. Add the fat found in the braise, arrange on the serving dish with the brisket on top, and serve
