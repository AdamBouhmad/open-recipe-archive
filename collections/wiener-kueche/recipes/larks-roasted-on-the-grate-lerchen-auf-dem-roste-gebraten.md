## Ingredients

- larks
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck the larks and remove the heads
2. Carefully remove the small entrails so that the fat is not pulled out with them
3. Place them in water for a quarter hour
4. Salt them, roast them on a lark spit over the grate, basting frequently with butter
5. Sprinkle them with breadcrumbs
