## Ingredients

- 30 live, washed crayfish
- approximately 1 liter white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place 30 live, washed crayfish in a casserole dish
2. Heat approximately 1 liter of white wine that has been well seasoned and somewhat peppered
3. Pour the boiling wine over the crayfish
4. Cook covered for approximately 10 minutes until they turn red
5. Serve as previously described
