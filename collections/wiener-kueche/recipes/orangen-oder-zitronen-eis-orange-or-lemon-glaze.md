## Ingredients

- 28 drams of finely sifted orange sugar
- juice of one whole orange

## Directions

1. Put 28 drams of finely sifted orange sugar and the juice of a whole orange into a deep bowl.
2. Stir this for 1/2 to 1 hour into a thick mass, to which, if it is too thick, 1 tablespoon of hot sugar can be added.
3. Pour it into a sugar pan or, in the absence of one, into a snowflake or saucepan, put it on the fire and let it stand there with constant stirring until the mass becomes liquid again.
4. Now coat the cake immediately with it; if necessary, dry it a few minutes in the lukewarm oven and set it cold.
5. Strawberry glaze is prepared the same way.
