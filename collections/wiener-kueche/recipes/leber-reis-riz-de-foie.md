## Ingredients

- liver mixture as in the preceding recipe
- boiling salted water
- hot broth

## Directions

1. Place a colander over a saucepan containing boiling salted water
2. Press the liver mixture through with a cooking spoon into the boiling soup or salted water
3. Remove the liver rice that forms with a skimming spoon and place in a soup pot containing 4 fingers-width of hot soup
4. Continue until all the dough is used
5. A colander can be used instead of the sieve to press through the liver dough
6. Pour the required hot soup over the liver rice and serve
7. Add 2 tablespoons flour at the same time as the Nockerl
