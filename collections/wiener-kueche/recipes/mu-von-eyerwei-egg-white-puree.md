## Ingredients

- White of five eggs
- Sugar
- One seidel of milk
- Orange water
- Grated lemon
- Sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Take the white of five eggs and whip it with sugar
2. Add one seidel of milk
3. Let it warm together with constant whisking
4. Place it in a bowl
5. Pour some orange water into it
6. Sprinkle grated lemon and sugar over it
