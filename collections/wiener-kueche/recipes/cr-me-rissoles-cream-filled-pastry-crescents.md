## Ingredients

- rissole dough
- any cream filling
- eggs for coating
- pearl sugar
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare rissole dough as in the recipe for Rissoles mit Farce
2. Fill with any desired cream
3. Cut out into semicircles
4. Place on a board moistened with water
5. Coat with beaten eggs, sprinkle with pearl sugar and finely chopped almonds
6. Bake in the oven at medium heat until golden
