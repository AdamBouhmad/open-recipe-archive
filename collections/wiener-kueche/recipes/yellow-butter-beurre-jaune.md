## Ingredients

- butter
- saffron

## Directions

1. Rub some saffron threads and stir them in about 2 tablespoons of cold water
2. Let them stand in a warm place until they dissolve well
3. Press them through a cloth and obtain the mixed color
4. Proceed as indicated for green butter
