## Ingredients

- veal
- bacon
- onion
- roasted Indian fowl or capon
- bread roll
- milk
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- caul
- brown broth
- lemon peel
- cream
- capers
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let half a pound of veal braise with some bacon and onion.
2. Chop together the braised veal with bacon, onion, roasted fowl or capon, a bread roll soaked in milk, some parsley, all finely.
3. Add salt and ground pepper, stir in one whole egg and two yolks.
4. Brush a casserole with butter, sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs, lay in the caul and forcemeat.
5. Cover again with caul, put the lid on, apply heat above and below, and bake it nicely.
6. Make a small roux, fill with brown broth, add some lemon peel, and let cook well.
7. Finally add two spoonfuls of cream, small capers, and lemon juice, and let come to a boil.
8. Turn the forcemeat onto a dish, pour sauce over it, and garnish with small cut-out lemon peel. When the forcemeat is baked, it goes into the sauce and is simmered a quarter hour.
