## Ingredients

- A good piece of beef from the rib (rib piece), aged 6 to 8 days and at least 3 kilos
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon slices
- Potatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the sinews from the beef, pound it, and wipe it dry with a cloth without washing it. Do not damage the fat on top; remove the tendons from the thick upper side.
2. Rub the meat thoroughly with pepper and salt and let it rest for 1 hour.
3. Line a flat pan with bacon slices, place the meat on it, and roast it at the highest possible heat for 1 hour.
4. After this time, remove it from the oven where it should have formed a crust. Cut it into fairly thin slices and serve garnished with potatoes.
5. The roast should show a beautiful rose color inside when cut. Note that the meat must be basted very frequently with its own juices during roasting.
6. For a very thick piece or meat from a strong animal, roast for about 1.5 hours; for a thinner piece or meat from a young animal, roast for only 0.75 to 1 hour.
7. Serve cold and it is excellent, particularly suitable for tea.
