## Ingredients

- 15 dkg butter
- 15 dkg sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 15 dkg softened chocolate
- 5 stiff egg whites
- 15 dkg raw grated almonds
- 6 dkg rum-moistened breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat together butter and sugar gradually, then egg yolks and softened chocolate
2. Fold in stiff egg whites together with raw grated almonds and rum-moistened breadcrumbs
3. Fill mixture into cake form
4. Bake at higher heat
5. Fill with jam when cooled
6. Glaze brown on top
