## Ingredients

- 1 herring
- Butter
- Oil
- Paper

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the herring as described in previous instructions
2. Wrap it in paper brushed with butter and oil
3. Grill on each side for approximately 4-5 minutes
4. When cooked through, unwrap from the paper and arrange in a serving bowl
5. Serve with warm mustard sauce, crayfish sauce, or sauerkraut
