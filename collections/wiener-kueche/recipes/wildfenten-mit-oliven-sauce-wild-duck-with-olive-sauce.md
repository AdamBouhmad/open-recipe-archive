## Ingredients

- wild duck, prepared as for common wild duck
- Madeira wine (4/5 liter)
- olives (12 pieces), pitted

## Directions

1. Prepare the duck as for common wild duck.
2. The only difference is that to the prepared butter sauce, which is poured over the broth, add 4/5 liter of Madeira wine, bring to a boil and about 1 minute before serving add 12 pitted olives to the sauce.
3. Pour this over the carved duck arranged on the platter and garnish with some additional sliced olives.
