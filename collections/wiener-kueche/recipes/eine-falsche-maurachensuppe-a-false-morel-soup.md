## Ingredients

- raw chicken livers
- chicken gizzards
- parsley
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- salt
- nutmeg blossom
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Chop some raw chicken livers and gizzards together finely with parsley.
2. Mix them with a couple of eggs, add bread crumbs, some salt, and nutmeg blossom.
3. Form small morals from this mixture.
4. Dip each one in beaten eggs and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
5. Fry them in not too hot lard until the end.
