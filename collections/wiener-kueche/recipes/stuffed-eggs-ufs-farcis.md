## Ingredients

- 12 hard-boiled eggs
- 8 ounces breadcrumbs, deveined and softened in milk
- 6 peeled capers
- egg yolks from the hard-boiled eggs
- 8 ounces butter, beaten fluffy
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon milk cream
- salt
- pepper
- 1 strong pinch finely chopped green parsley
- bread crumbs
- milk cream for pouring

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise and carefully remove the yolks.
2. Press the deveined breadcrumbs softened in milk through a sieve into a deep dish.
3. Gradually stir 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks into 8 ounces of fluffy beaten butter, then mix well with the sieved breadcrumb mixture, the boiled egg yolks, 1 tablespoon milk cream, salt, pepper, and 1 pinch of finely chopped green parsley.
4. Heap this filling generously on each egg half so it regains the form of a whole egg.
5. Place filled eggs in a casserole, pour milk cream over them, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and bake for 1/4 hour in the oven.
6. Serve the eggs with the milk cream in a dish, or remove them from the casserole if desired, arrange in a dish, and garnish with finely diced capers in a lattice pattern, serving the cream sauce separately.
