## Ingredients

- small trout
- salt
- fine table oil or olive oil

## Directions

1. Scale the small trout, wash inside and outside thoroughly
2. Make three diagonal slits on each side
3. Rub with salt
4. Place in a bowl
5. Pour fine table oil (or olive oil if available) over them
6. Let stand for 1/3 hour
7. Pour the oil from the bowl onto a wide pan
8. Add more oil if needed
9. Fry the trout on both sides in the hot oil
10. Arrange on a platter
11. Garnish with green parsley and lemon slices
12. Serve with mustard, beaten mustard sauce, and cooked potatoes
