## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- 4 dkg hot butter
- chopped parsley
- some soup or water
- salt
- lemon juice
- several spoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms, chop them finely.
2. In hot butter, let chopped parsley come to a boil, add the mushrooms.
3. Pour in some soup or water, add salt, lemon juice, and several spoonfuls of cream.
4. Let simmer for 8-10 minutes. There should not be much liquid.
5. Spread on the Fridatten, roll them, place in a buttered dish.
6. Pour milk mixed with 1 egg yolk over them.
7. Place in the oven for 15-20 minutes to bake.
8. Serve in the dish.
