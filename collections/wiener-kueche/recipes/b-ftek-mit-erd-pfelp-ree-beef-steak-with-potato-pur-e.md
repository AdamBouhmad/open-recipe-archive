## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- Flour
- Good meat broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook and peel the potatoes.
2. Once they are cold, grate them on a grater.
3. In a casserole, warm a piece of butter.
4. Add one cooking spoon of flour, which should take on some color but not brown.
5. Add the grated potatoes and pour good meat broth over them in proportion.
6. Stir it well until it has the consistency of butter.
7. Salt it and let it cook well.
