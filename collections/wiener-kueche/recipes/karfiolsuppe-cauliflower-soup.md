## Ingredients

- 1 large head of cauliflower
- salt
- water
- 6 dkg butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove leaves from cauliflower, divide, and wash
2. Place in cold salted water
3. Cook for 20 minutes and pass through a sieve
4. Make a parsley roux with 6 dkg butter
5. Pour the cauliflower water over it
6. Add the strained cauliflower mixture
7. Salt and cook for 15 minutes
8. Serve with bread slices
