## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 8 dkg sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 8 dkg flour
- 10 dkg chocolate
- 20 dkg cube sugar
- Water as needed
- Sweetened whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks with 8 dkg sugar for 15 minutes
2. Gently fold in 4 beaten egg whites with 8 dkg flour
3. Fill buttered, floured Indian puff molds
4. Bake first a few minutes in open oven, then in closed oven until light brown
5. Turn out and cool
6. Hollow out from the flat side
7. Place with rounded side up for glazing
8. Coat with chocolate icing made from 10 dkg chocolate and 20 dkg cube sugar with water
9. Place sweetened whipped cream between 2 puffs
