## Ingredients

- rissole dough
- brain
- green parsley
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Prepare rissole dough as in the recipe for Rissoles mit Farce
2. Clean and skin the brain, then braise it in butter with finely chopped green parsley
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Bind with egg yolks mixed with a little milk
5. Use this brain mixture as filling following the method for Rissoles mit Farce
