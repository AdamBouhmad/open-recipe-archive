## Ingredients

- 8 deca flour
- 3 whole eggs
- 2 liters beef broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 8 deca flour in a pot, add 3 whole eggs and stir into a smooth thick dough
2. Let it run into 2 liters of boiling beef broth through a small funnel
3. If the dough is too thick, add 1 or more eggs; if too thin, add more flour
4. When the surface of the soup is covered with the piped dough, remove it with a wooden spoon and add it to the soup pot containing some soup
5. Continue this process until all the dough is used
6. Pour any remaining soup needed into the soup pot
