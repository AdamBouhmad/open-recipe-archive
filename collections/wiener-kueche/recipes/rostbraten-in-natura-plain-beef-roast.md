## Ingredients

- 6 pieces flattened beef roast, not too heavily salted, optionally with pepper
- 1 piece onion, leafy sliced
- bunches of savory herb
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
- vinegar and water or broth

## Directions

1. Place the flattened beef roast pieces in a casserole
2. Add 1 piece of leafy sliced onion
3. Add bunches of savory herb
4. Add 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
5. Add enough vinegar and water or broth so the liquid rises above the meat
6. Braise until tender
7. Serve with potatoes
