## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms, cleaned, washed and cut into pieces
- butter
- finely chopped onions
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- strong meat broth or pea soup
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Lay the cleaned, washed, and cut mushrooms in fresh water so they remain white
2. In a casserole on a charcoal fire, briefly stew finely chopped onions and parsley in butter
3. Add pepper, salt, and some nutmeg flower
4. Fill with strong meat broth or pea soup and let the mushrooms cook thoroughly
5. Before serving, thicken the mushrooms and add toasted bread slices to the soup
