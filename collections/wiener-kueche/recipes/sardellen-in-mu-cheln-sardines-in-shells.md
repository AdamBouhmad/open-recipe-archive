## Ingredients

- 3 sardines
- 5 decagrams Parmesan cheese
- 6 decagrams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- 2 egg whites
- 3 decagrams grated cheese
- breadcrumbs
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Create a base by beating 6 decagrams butter until creamy
2. Stir in 2 egg yolks, the boned and passed sardines, a pinch of salt and pepper, and 3 decagrams grated cheese
3. Fold in 2 stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Fill the mixture into buttered shells (not too generously, as the mixture rises)
5. Scatter cheese and breadcrumbs on top
6. Drizzle with hot butter
7. Place the shells on a baking sheet and gratinée in the oven for 8-10 minutes
8. Serve on a plate with a folded napkin
9. Do not open the oven during baking, as the mixture will collapse
