## Ingredients

- dried beans
- water
- fat or butter
- onion
- parsley
- meat broth
- egg yolks
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the beans in fresh water until very soft.
2. Strain off the water.
3. Let fat or butter run in a pan with finely cut onion and parsley.
4. Add the beans with a little good meat broth and let them boil up a little before serving.
5. Take several egg yolks mixed with a little wine vinegar, fricassee the beans with this mixture, and serve.
