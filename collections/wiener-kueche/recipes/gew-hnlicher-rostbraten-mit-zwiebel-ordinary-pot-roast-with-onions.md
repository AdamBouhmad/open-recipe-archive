## Ingredients

- Rostbraten (pot roasts)
- Zwiebel (onions), finely cut
- Heißes Fett (hot fat)
- Suppe (broth)
- Optional: Mehl (flour)

## Directions

1. Stuff the pot roasts and sear them in hot fat.
2. Sprinkle finely cut onions over them.
3. Turn the meat as soon as it begins to turn yellow.
4. Once the pot roasts are browned on both sides, remove them from the pan.
5. Add some broth to the fat in the pan, let it come to a boil briefly, and pour this sauce over the pot roasts.
6. For juicier results, dust the pot roasts with a little flour before adding them to the hot fat.
