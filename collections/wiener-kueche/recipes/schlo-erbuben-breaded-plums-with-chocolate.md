## Ingredients

- milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 10 dkg flour
- 1 tablespoon rum
- plums
- water
- ½ swelled almond per plum
- hot lard
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat together the milk, 1 egg, salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, 10 dkg flour, and 1 tablespoon rum until smooth.
2. Wash the plums and place them in boiling water.
3. Cook for 8 minutes, then drain and let cool.
4. Remove the pits from the plums and insert ½ swelled almond in place of each pit.
5. Dip the plums in the batter.
6. Fry in plenty of hot lard until light brown.
7. Drain and while still hot, roll them in grated chocolate.
8. Serve warm.
