## Ingredients

- 1/16 liter millet
- 1/4 liter soup
- Salt
- Pepper
- 8 tablespoons finely diced meat
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the millet porridge as follows: for 2 chickens, measure 1/16 liter of millet into a casserole, add 1/4 liter of soup, season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook slowly until the millet forms a thick porridge.
2. Add 8 tablespoons of finely diced meat, mix well and set aside.
3. When the mixture has cooled, beat in 2 egg yolks, mix well and fill the chickens with this mixture.
4. Prepare the chickens as described for the recipe reference given.
