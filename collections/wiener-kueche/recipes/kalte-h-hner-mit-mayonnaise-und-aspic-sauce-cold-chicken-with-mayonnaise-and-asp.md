## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Herbs or mayonnaise of choice
- Cold aspic sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as for 'In eigenem Safte gedämpfte' (reference 822)
2. Once the chicken is tender, set aside in its broth
3. After completely cooling, remove and cut into desired pieces
4. Coat each piece with herb mayonnaise or cold aspic sauce
5. Arrange raised in the center of a platter
6. Cover with mayonnaise
7. Border the platter with neatly cut or shaped aspic
