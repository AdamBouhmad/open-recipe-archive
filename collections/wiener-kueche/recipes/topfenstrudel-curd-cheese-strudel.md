## Ingredients

- 20 dkg Flour
- 1 Egg
- Salt
- Butter (walnut-sized)
- Almost 1 dl lukewarm water
- 6 dkg Butter (for filling)
- 3 Egg yolks
- 8 dkg Sugar
- 40 dkg sieved curd cheese
- ¼ l Cream
- Lemon zest (finely chopped)
- 3 Egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from flour, egg, salt, butter, and lukewarm water
2. After resting, stretch thinly on floured cloth
3. Prepare filling by creaming butter, add egg yolks and sugar, mix in sieved curd cheese, cream, and lemon zest, fold in beaten egg whites
4. Spread filling on dough, roll up, and place in buttered baking pan
5. Bake until light brown, basting frequently with hot milk
6. Cut into pieces, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm
