## Ingredients

- About 12 peeled apples
- Water and necessary sugar
- 8 Deka butter, beaten
- 16 Deka lemon sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 8 Deka white bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut peeled apples into thin slices
2. Stew covered with some water and necessary sugar until soft
3. Place on a sieve, let juice drain, and pass through a sieve into a deep bowl
4. Mix 8 Deka beaten butter with 16 Deka lemon sugar
5. Add 8 egg yolks one after another and stir for ¼ hour
6. Add the cold apples and lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 8 Deka white bread crumbs
7. Fill this mixture into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted cake mold
8. Bake for ¾ hour in the oven
9. Remove the rim and arrange the souffle on a cake plate in a bowl, sprinkling with sugar and serving
