## Ingredients

- 1 goose liver
- 4 loth butter or beef lard
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 bread rolls
- milk
- green parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper, ground
- fine bread crumbs
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scrape the skin from a goose liver and chop it finely
2. Beat 4 loth butter or beef lard until fluffy
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks, one after another
4. Scrape the crust from 2 bread rolls, soak them in milk
5. Add the soaked bread with finely chopped green parsley, salt, ground pepper, and fine bread crumbs
6. Stir everything well together
7. Fold in the snow from 2 egg whites
8. Coat a casserole with butter
9. Sprinkle with fine bread crumbs
10. Fill in the mixture
11. Bake in the oven or on coals
12. Cut into pieces
13. Pour pressed liver soup over it and serve
