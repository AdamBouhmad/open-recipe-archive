## Ingredients

- Beef marrow (6 drams)
- Cold cream (1/2 liter)
- Egg yolks (14)
- Vanilla sugar (16 drams)
- Marmalade
- Sugar (16 drams finely crushed)
- Egg whites (4)

## Directions

1. Stir beef marrow slowly with cold cream, then add egg yolks and vanilla sugar
2. Fill into a butter-coated, flour-lined mold and cover
3. Poach for 3/4 hour
4. When done, turn out onto a dish and cover with dissolved marmalade
5. Mix finely crushed sugar with the snow of egg whites
6. Fill into paper cornettos and pipe decoratively onto the marmalade
7. Sprinkle with sugar and dry in an open oven for 1/2 hour
