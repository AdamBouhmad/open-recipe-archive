## Ingredients

- 2 Maß brandy
- 1 Maß sweet cream
- 1 Loth cochineal
- 1 Loth vanilla, finely cut
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Place in a bottle at a warm stove for 14 days
3. Filter through filter paper
4. The liqueur is ready
