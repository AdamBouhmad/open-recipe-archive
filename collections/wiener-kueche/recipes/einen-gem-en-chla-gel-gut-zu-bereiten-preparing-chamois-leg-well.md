## Ingredients

- chamois leg
- salt
- bacon for larding
- half a seidel of Tokayer wine
- half a seidel of red Ofner wine
- vinegar
- cloves
- various herbs
- Spanish onion
- cream
- flour
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Wash the leg well, skin it, salt it, and let it lie in salt for an hour
2. Lard it
3. Prepare the marinade by simmering together the Tokayer wine, red Ofner wine, vinegar, cloves, and various herbs
4. Pour the hot marinade over the leg and let it lie well covered for several days, adding a Spanish onion to the marinade
5. To use, roast the leg
6. Add a little cream and flour to the marinade, let it simmer a little, and strain it over the leg
