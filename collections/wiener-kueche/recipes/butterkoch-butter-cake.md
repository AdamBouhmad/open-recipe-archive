## Ingredients

- 20 loth butter
- 16 eggs
- 12 loth sugar
- zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat 20 loth butter until smooth
2. Beat in 16 eggs, one after another
3. Add 12 loth sugar and zest of 1 lemon
4. Seal the dough well with a ring mold
5. Bake slowly until done
