## Ingredients

- Young green beans, trimmed and cut lengthwise
- Simmering water
- Fat
- Green parsley, finely cut
- Garlic, finely cut
- Good broth
- Milk cream
- Fried veal cutlets

## Directions

1. Blanch young green beans, trimmed and cut lengthwise, in simmering water and let stand for a while
2. Heat 2 spoonfuls of fat
3. Add a good amount of green parsley and a little finely cut garlic
4. Add the beans after they have been well drained
5. Braise them with frequent stirring
6. Pour good broth with 5 to 6 spoonfuls of milk cream over them
7. Garnish finally with fried veal cutlets
