## Ingredients

- half pound finely grated chocolate of the best kind
- 1 seidel cream
- 20 egg yolks
- some lukewarm milk

## Directions

1. Cook half pound finely grated chocolate of the best kind with 1 seidel cream
2. Beat 20 egg yolks in a pot and stir them clear with some lukewarm milk
3. Pour the boiling chocolate to the eggs while constantly whisking
4. It becomes a thick foam, which you pour onto a plate and serve cold
