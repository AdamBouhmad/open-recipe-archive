## Ingredients

- rabbit fillets
- rabbit meat from the shanks
- white bread
- milk
- good butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- fine dried herbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut some nice fillets from the rabbit, scrape the meat from the shanks, separate skin and sinews
2. Pound finely on a grindstone and then pass through a wire sieve
3. Soak white bread in milk, press it out very strongly, put it with good butter and the fillets in the grindstone
4. Mix in salt, nutmeg, pepper, and fine dried herbs
5. Beat in eggs one at a time, until 5 are added, and pound together a long time until everything is well mixed
6. Form the dumplings on paper brushed with butter and cook them in bouillon or salted water
7. If the mixture is too stiff, beat in another egg, work it well through, and use as desired
8. Make a piquant sauce from the bones
9. Note that when making these dumplings, equal parts butter, rabbit meat, and pressed white bread should be taken
