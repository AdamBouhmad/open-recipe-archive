## Ingredients

- 8 deca of bacon or butter
- onion
- root vegetables
- whole spices
- poularde
- broth
- butter
- flour
- 4 cleaned, finely sliced crayfish tails
- mussels
- white wine

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with bacon or butter, onion, root vegetables, and whole spices
2. Place a cleaned, trussed, seasoned, and larded poularde on top and braise covered
3. When the resulting juice has evaporated, pour in enough broth so the liquid covers the poularde and braise covered until tender
4. Remove the poularde, place it in a casserole, and cover well
5. Remove the fat from the braising liquid, place in a casserole and toast an equal amount of flour, pour the defatted broth over it, stir together, and cook well
6. Remove the fat cleanly, pass the sauce through a sieve, and add 4 cleaned, finely sliced crayfish tails
7. Wash the mussels in white wine, place them in 9 deca of melted butter, braise them briefly, then pour in enough white wine to cover them and cook covered until done
