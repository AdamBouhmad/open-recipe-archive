## Ingredients

- ¼ liter wine
- 8 Deka sugar
- 1 clove
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 12 Deka bread crusts removed, cut into thin slices

## Directions

1. Put wine with sugar, clove, and cinnamon in a saucepan
2. Allow to simmer well and pour through a sieve over bread with crusts removed, cut into thin slices
3. Stir on the fire until this mass has cooked together, then set aside and let cool
