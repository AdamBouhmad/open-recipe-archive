## Ingredients

- 24 deca cooked meat (leftovers)
- some roasted parsley
- 1 mushroom
- 1 bread roll softened in milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- some salt
- some nutmeg
- 1 knife point white pepper
- 1/2 liter cold soup

## Directions

1. Pound the cooked meat very finely together with roasted parsley, mushroom, and the bread roll softened in milk, and pass through a sieve
2. Add 3 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, some salt, some nutmeg, 1 knife point white pepper, and 1/2 liter cold soup
3. Mix this mixture together well
4. Fill into a form greased with butter and dusted with flour
5. Cook in a water bath for 1 hour
