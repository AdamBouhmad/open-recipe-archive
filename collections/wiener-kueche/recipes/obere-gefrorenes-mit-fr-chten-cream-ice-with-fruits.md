## Ingredients

- prepared as Erdbeeren-Gefrorenes mit Eiern und Obere
- fresh raspberries
- currants
- pitted and sieved cherries
- maraschino cherries
- peaches
- other fruits
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 deca more sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as Cream Ice with Strawberries (Recipe 1732) using fresh raspberries, currants, pitted and sieved cherries, maraschino cherries, peaches and all other fruits along with the juice of 2 lemons, whereby one can add 2 deca more sugar
