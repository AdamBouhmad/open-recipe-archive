## Ingredients

- Three pounds live crayfish
- Two measures water
- Eight loths oat roots
- Four loths salsify roots
- Two loths ox tongue leaves
- Three loths borage

## Directions

1. Cook the live crayfish for one hour in two measures water
2. Remove the crayfish, pound them together with their shells
3. Cook them again in the previous water for four hours, adding water from time to time as it evaporates
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Add eight loths oat roots, four loths salsify roots, two loths ox tongue leaves, and three loths borage
6. Let everything simmer a couple of times and strain through a sieve again
7. Take one teacup full every two hours
