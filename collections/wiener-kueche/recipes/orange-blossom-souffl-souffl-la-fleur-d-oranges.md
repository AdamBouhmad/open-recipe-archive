## Ingredients

- 16 drams flour
- 1/2 liter cold cream or milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 16 drams butter
- 16 drams sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 drams finely chopped and candied orange blossoms
- 6 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Place flour in a casserole, stir with 1/2 liter cold cream until smooth.
2. Add 3 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, 16 drams butter, and 16 drams sugar.
3. Stir over heat until the mixture has thickened considerably.
4. Set aside and let cool completely.
5. Gradually mix in 6 egg yolks and 6 drams finely chopped and candied orange blossoms.
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites.
7. Fill a buttered mold, dusted with flour, or pour into a buttered dish.
8. Bake at low heat for 3/4 hour in the oven.
