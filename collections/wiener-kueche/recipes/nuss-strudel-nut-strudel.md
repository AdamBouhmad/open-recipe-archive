## Ingredients

- Strudel dough
- melted butter
- finely ground nuts
- bread crumbs soaked in milk
- finely ground sugar
- 2 egg yolks, stirred with a little cold cream
- honey

## Directions

1. Brush stretched strudel dough with melted butter
2. Sprinkle with finely ground nuts
3. Sprinkle with bread crumbs soaked in milk and finely ground sugar
4. Sprinkle spoonwise with 2 egg yolks stirred with a little cold cream and a little honey
5. Roll dough together
6. Treat further as for Ham Strudel (No. 1229)
