## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deca finely cut roasted meat
- parsley
- salt
- 6 deca breadcrumbs
- milk
- peas or asparagus peas formed in salted water
- crayfish tails
- 1/16 liter old fat
- 1/2 liter cream
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter until fluffy
2. Beat in 6 egg yolks
3. Add 16 deca finely cut roasted meat, some parsley, salt, and 6 deca breadcrumbs that were moistened with milk
4. Add peas or asparagus peas formed in salted water and crayfish tails
5. Add 1/16 liter old fat and 1/2 liter cream
6. Mix everything well together
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
8. Bake or cook in steam
9. Can also use roasted or cooked fish meat
