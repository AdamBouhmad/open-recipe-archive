## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 20 deca finely sifted sugar
- 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 20 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with sugar for half an hour
2. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites together with flour
3. Spread finger-high on baking sheet lined with butter
4. Bake at moderate heat in oven
5. When done, cut into desired pieces or cut out shapes while still warm
