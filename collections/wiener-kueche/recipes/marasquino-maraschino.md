## Ingredients

- 100 pounds of ripe cherries
- 5 pounds of cherry leaves
- 1 pound of sugar per measure of spirits
- 1 pound of water

## Directions

1. Crush 100 pounds of ripe cherries together with 5 pounds of cherry leaves
2. Bring to wine fermentation with yeast and then distill
3. Draw the obtained liquid again through a water bath
4. Add 1 pound of sugar dissolved in 1 pound of water per measure of spirits
5. Alternatively, first draw off the spirits, then draw over the fragrant water to prepare the sugar solution, and add 8 ounces of this solution per measure of liqueur
6. Age to improve quality
