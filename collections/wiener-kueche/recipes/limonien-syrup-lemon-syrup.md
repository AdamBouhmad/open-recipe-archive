## Ingredients

- 18 beautiful juicy lemons
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1/2 measure water

## Directions

1. Take 18 beautiful juicy lemons
2. Rub the zest from 6 of them onto sugar
3. Press the juice over the rubbed zest
4. Cook 2 pounds sugar with 1/2 measure water to make a syrup
5. Stir the rubbed zest and lemon juice into the syrup while still warm
6. Pass both through a fine sieve
7. When cooled, fill into bottles
8. To use, pour a small portion into sufficient water and stir
