## Ingredients

- 40 dkg leftover cooked pike or pike-perch
- 6 dkg hot fat
- onion, finely chopped
- parsley, finely chopped
- flour
- soup
- 1 egg yolk
- breadcrumbs for breading
- hot lard for frying
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Bone the leftover fish and press it well.
2. In hot fat, let finely chopped onion and parsley start to color; add a little flour, pour in a small amount of soup, let it reduce, add the fish, and mix with an egg yolk if desired.
3. The mixture must be firm.
4. Divide into small pieces and form croquettes.
5. Bread them, fry in hot lard until golden, and drain.
6. Serve with or without salad.
7. Arrange the croquettes on a flat round platter with a cake paper and place curled parsley in the center.
