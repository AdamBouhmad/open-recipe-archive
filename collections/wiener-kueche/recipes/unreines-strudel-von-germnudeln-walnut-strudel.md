## Ingredients

- 8 Deka shelled walnuts
- 8 Deka blanched, roasted hazelnuts
- 16 Deka finely sifted sugar
- 1 pinch grated coffee
- Dough as per Mohn-Strudel Nr. 1256(b)

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as specified in Mohn-Strudel Nr. 1256(b)
2. Very finely chop 8 Deka of walnuts and mix well with 8 Deka of blanched, roasted hazelnuts and 16 Deka of finely sifted sugar and 1 pinch of grated coffee
3. Apply this filling to the strudel dough in the same manner as the poppy seed filling
4. Roll the dough completely together, place it on a buttered baking sheet and allow it to rise covered in a warm place
5. Once risen, brush it thoroughly with beaten eggs and bake in the oven
