## Ingredients

- fresh cucumbers, peeled and cut to thickness of knife back
- butter
- flour
- broth
- lemon peel, cut in strips
- pepper
- juice from half a lemon
- 2 to 3 spoonfuls milk cream
- beef soup (alternate method)
- onion, finely cut
- clarified butter
- lemon peel
- lemon juice or white vinegar

## Directions

1. Braise in a casserole the peeled and knife-thick sliced fresh cucumbers with butter and a little flour
2. After braising, add as much broth as required, strips of lemon peel, pepper, and the juice from half a lemon
3. Finally add 2 to 3 spoonfuls of milk cream
4. Alternatively: simmer the cucumbers in beef soup, let finely cut onion run golden in clarified butter, add the cucumbers with the soup, some lemon peel, lemon juice or white vinegar, and 2 to 3 spoonfuls of milk cream at the end
5. Take care that the cucumbers cook as little as possible
