## Ingredients

- 14 deca chocolate
- 14 deca butter, beaten
- 16 deca sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 deca almonds, finely ground with skin and passed
- 3 egg whites
- 8 deca black flour
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- chocolate or other glaze

## Directions

1. Place 14 deca chocolate on a baking sheet and soften in the oven
2. Mix smoothly with 14 deca beaten butter
3. Add 16 deca sugar and stir with 3 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
4. Add 8 deca finely ground and passed almonds and stir for another 1/4 hour
5. Fold in the snow (stiffly beaten egg whites) from 3 egg whites together with 8 deca black flour
6. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour to a height of 2 fingers
7. Bake for 3/4 hour at low heat
8. Turn out onto a sieve after this time
9. Once completely cooled, cover with warm or cold chocolate or other glaze
