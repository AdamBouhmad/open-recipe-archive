## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter
- 1 tablespoon finely ground sugar
- 1 knife point salt
- 9 egg yolks, one after another
- 2 Deka yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk
- 24 Deka flour
- 1/4 liter cream
- 4 Deka finely ground sugar
- 4 Deka melted butter
- Crayfish butter or ordinary butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Beat 16 Deka butter until fluffy, gradually add 1 tablespoon finely ground sugar, 1 knife point salt and 9 egg yolks one after another
2. Mix this well with 2 Deka yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk and 24 Deka flour and beat the dough until it detaches from the spoon
3. Let it rise covered in a warm place until it has doubled in height
4. Place the dough on a flour-dusted cloth, roll it out strong finger-thickness and cut out round plates with a crumpet cutter
5. In a casserole brushed with crayfish butter or ordinary butter, place 1/4 liter cream, 4 Deka finely ground sugar and 4 Deka melted butter and, when this has become lukewarm, lay the round cut-out dough plates next to each other in it
6. Place the casserole in a warm place
7. When the dough has risen once more to double height, brush it with melted crayfish butter and bake it for 1/2 to 3/4 hour in the oven
8. When the dumplings are finished baking, turn out the casserole, separate the dumplings from one another, arrange them in a bowl and serve at table with custard or desired cream
