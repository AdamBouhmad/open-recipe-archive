## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk for Brandteig (recipe 1052)
- 14 ounces butter, beaten
- 24 ounces powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites, beaten to snow

## Directions

1. Prepare Brandteig from 1/2 liter milk, fill into a pastry bag
2. Press the dough through continuous pressure, cut into desired pieces, and fry in hot fat until golden with frequent shaking of the pan
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on blotting paper
4. Beat 14 ounces butter with 24 ounces powdered sugar
5. Add 8 egg yolks one by one and stir
6. Gently fold in the snow from 6 egg whites simultaneously with the fried Brandteig
7. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
8. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour
