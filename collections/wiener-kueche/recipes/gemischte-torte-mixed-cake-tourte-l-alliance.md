## Ingredients

- various cake batters
- lingonberry jam or cream
- glaze
- fruits

## Directions

1. Bake finger-height plates from various cake batters and let them cool completely.
2. Spread each cooled plate with desired lingonberry jam or cream, then stack them together.
3. Cut the edges evenly all around with a fine, sharp knife.
4. Cover the entire cake with a glaze of choice and, once dry, decorate with fruits as desired.
