## Ingredients

- 12 deca sugar
- 5 tablespoons cold water
- 4 liters egg white, firmly whipped
- 1 liter firm, very finely worked Orange-Gefrorenes
- 1 3/4 liters Champagne wine
- 6 tablespoons Arrack or Rum
- Juice of preserved pineapple, equal amount
- 1 tablespoon Allspice juice or 1 teaspoon Cochineal coloring

## Directions

1. Dissolve 12 deca sugar in 5 tablespoons cold water.
2. Mix this with the firm whipped egg white from 4 liters and blend into 1 liter firm, very finely worked Orange-Gefrorenes.
3. Stir in 1 3/4 liters Champagne wine, 6 tablespoons Arrack or Rum, an equal amount of juice from preserved pineapple, and 1 tablespoon Allspice juice or 1 teaspoon Cochineal coloring.
4. Serve this punch in glasses. The color should not be held too dark, but only appear very pale rose.
