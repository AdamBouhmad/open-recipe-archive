## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Bread crumb
- Milk
- Asparagus
- Crayfish
- Green peas
- Crayfish butter
- Fresh parsley
- Egg
- Egg yolk
- Beef broth
- Mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the veal breast clean and prepare it for filling
2. Soak a bread crumb in milk
3. Boil the asparagus and crayfish, remove them, and dice them finely along with the asparagus
4. Make a crayfish butter from the shells
5. Press out the bread crumb, dice it finely, and braise it slightly with finely chopped fresh parsley
6. Add the asparagus with the crayfish tails and some green peas, braise again briefly
7. Remove from heat, season and salt it, and let it cool slightly
8. Beat in one whole egg and one egg yolk, mix well together
9. Fill the breast with this mixture
10. Place in a cooking vessel on skewers, pour beef broth over it, and braise until tender
11. Before serving, prepare a mushroom sauce and pour it over the breast
