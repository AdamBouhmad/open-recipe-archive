## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- Peeled and finely ground almonds
- Previously mentioned vegetables
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites and mentioned vegetables together
2. Add finely ground and strained peeled almonds with the previously mentioned vegetables
3. Mix well and finally lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
4. Fill into a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. Cook covered for 3/4 hour in a water bath
6. Turn out onto a platter and serve with whipped cream or crème
