## Ingredients

- 1 herring
- Water
- Milk
- Herring roe
- Pickled apples
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Place 1 herring on a board moistened with water and lightly tap with the handle of a knife.
2. Using a knife, lightly cut the skin from tail to head, continuing this at the side of the head and pulling the skin down from the head to the tail.
3. When this is done on both sides, remove the head and tail, loosen the spine, remove the bones and soak the herring 1 hour in soaked milk, then dry on a cloth.
4. Cut both halves of the herring into three pieces diagonally and arrange them in a bowl so that the two pieces lie beside each other.
5. Similarly, cut the herring roe and pickled apples into neat pieces and garnish the herring with these.
6. Place the head and tail in their proper positions and sprinkle the whole thing with capers, serve with vinegar and oil.
7. A little green parsley can be placed in the herring's mouth.
