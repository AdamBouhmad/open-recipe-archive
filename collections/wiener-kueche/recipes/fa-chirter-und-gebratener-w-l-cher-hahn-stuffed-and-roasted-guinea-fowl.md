## Ingredients

- guinea fowl
- chicken livers, whole or leafed
- truffles, boiled
- small whole onions, several dozen
- butter or chopped bacon
- salt
- pepper
- basil
- fond (schü)

## Directions

1. Prepare the guinea fowl in the known manner for roasting
2. Blanch the onions in broth until half cooked
3. Hack together the chicken livers, truffles, onions, salt, pepper, and basil and pass through butter or chopped bacon
4. Stir everything well together with a little fond
5. Fill the guinea fowl with this mixture so nothing falls out
6. Roast it slowly and nicely on the spit
7. Serve with clear fond underneath
