## Ingredients

- 8 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 1/4 kg curd
- 3 egg whites
- 12-14 dkg breadcrumbs
- salt water
- butter for breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cream 8 dkg butter and stir in 3 egg yolks and salt
2. Add the strained curd
3. Fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites together with 12-14 dkg breadcrumbs
4. Let stand for a while
5. If the curd is stiff, add a few spoonfuls of cream
6. Form dumplings
7. Place in a casserole in boiling salted water
8. Cook slowly for 5-8 minutes
9. Lift onto a round platter
10. Pour over with breadcrumbs roasted in butter
