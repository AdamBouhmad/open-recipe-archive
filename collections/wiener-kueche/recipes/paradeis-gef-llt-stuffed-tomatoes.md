## Ingredients

- 1-2 per person: beautiful, firm, equally large, round tomatoes
- hashé from 20-25 dkg beef scraps
- grated Parmesan
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the tomatoes, remove the stems, cut off the tops, and carefully hollow them out.
2. Fill with hashé, place in an omelet pan, sprinkle with grated Parmesan and breadcrumbs, dot with butter.
3. Bake for 10-15 minutes in the oven.
4. Alternatively, fill with braised rice instead of hashé.
5. The hollowed-out portions and tops can be added to soup to clarify it, or made into a sauce.
