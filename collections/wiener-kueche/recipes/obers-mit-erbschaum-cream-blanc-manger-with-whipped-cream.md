## Ingredients

- 30 Deka almonds, blanched
- 1 liter milk
- 10 Deka fine sifted vanilla sugar
- 4 Deka dissolved, still warm isinglass (prepared with 1/2 liter cold water and reduced to 1/8 liter)
- 1/2 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. After blanching 30 Deka almonds, pound them very fine, wetting them with some milk, and cook them with constant stirring in 1 liter milk, let this cool and press the liquid through cloth into a saucepan, to which add 10 Deka fine sifted vanilla sugar, heat this warm and add 4 Deka dissolved, still warm isinglass (which has been prepared with 1/2 liter cold water and reduced to 1/8 liter), then put it in an earthenware bowl and stir until it begins to thicken, after which mix in 1/2 liter whipped cream, place this mass in a mold set in ice, cool and unmold
