## Ingredients

- Turtle
- Salt water
- Butter
- Onion
- Cloves
- Meat broth or cooking water
- Lemon peel
- Whole pepper
- Lemon juice
- Turtle blood

## Directions

1. Having let the turtle bleed out and preserved the blood in a bowl, place it in salt water over heat and boil until the shell can be opened.
2. Remove the shells, take out the turtle, peel off its outer skin, carefully remove the interior, and divide it into pieces of your choice.
3. Make a small brown roux with butter, add half an onion studded with cloves, fill it with meat broth or the water in which the turtle was cooked, add some lemon peel, whole pepper, and the juice of half a lemon.
4. Lay the turtle pieces in it, bring to a boil, add the turtle blood last, and let them cook a while longer, then they are done.
