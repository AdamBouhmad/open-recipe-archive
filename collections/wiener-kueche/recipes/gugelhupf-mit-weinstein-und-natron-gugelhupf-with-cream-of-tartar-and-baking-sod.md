## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 10 deca sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 liters cold milk
- 28 deca flour
- 13 grams cream of tartar
- 5 grams baking soda
- egg white from 4 eggs
- butter for greasing
- flour for lining the mold

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy
2. Add 10 deca sugar and 4 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix with 1 1/2 liters cold milk and 28 deca flour
4. Mix in 13 grams cream of tartar and 5 grams baking soda
5. Lightly fold in stiffly beaten egg white from 4 eggs
6. Fill a butter-coated, flour-lined mold and bake at moderate heat in the oven for 1 hour
