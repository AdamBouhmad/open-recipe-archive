## Ingredients

- good white wine, half a measure
- water, 1 coffee cup full
- sugar
- whole cinnamon
- lemon peel
- cloves
- egg yolks, 4
- sweet cream, 2 spoonfuls
- toasted bread slices
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cook half a measure of good white wine with one coffee cup of water, a piece of sugar, some whole cinnamon, lemon peel and some cloves for half an hour
2. Remove the spices
3. Beat 4 egg yolks in a pot with 2 spoonfuls of sweet cream
4. Pour the hot wine onto the egg mixture while stirring vigorously
5. Pour into the soup pot
6. Top with neatly cut toasted bread slices dusted thickly with fine sugar and glazed with a hot shovel
7. Serve quickly to the table so the bread does not soften
