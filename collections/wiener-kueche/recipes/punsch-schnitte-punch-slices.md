## Ingredients

- 16 Deca finely sifted sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 10 Deca flour
- parchment paper
- previously prepared baked biscuit
- fine citron, chopped
- raisins
- currants
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Stir 16 Deca finely sifted sugar with 6 egg yolks for 1 1/2 hours
2. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites from 6 eggs with 10 Deca flour
3. Spread this mixture knife-back width thick on a parchment-lined, filled baking sheet and bake pale yellow in the oven
4. Cut a previously prepared, cooled biscuit into finger-thick cubes and place on the pale yellow baked biscuit
5. Sprinkle with finely chopped citron mixed with raisins and currants
6. Fold the longer side of the dough 2 fingers wide, also cover with biscuit
7. Roll the dough up completely, set aside, weigh down with a light board
8. After about 1 hour remove the board, sprinkle the dough with sugar and cut into pieces of any desired size
