## Ingredients

- one gutted but unscaled tench
- a handful of peas
- whole spices
- onion
- parsley
- a piece of celery
- some lard

## Directions

1. Put the tench with a handful of peas, whole spices, onion, parsley, a piece of celery and some lard in a pot
2. Pour water over it and let it cook for two hours
3. Strain it through a sieve and obtain a broth into which you can cook a flour dish before serving
