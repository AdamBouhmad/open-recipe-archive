## Ingredients

- 24 deca butter cut into pieces
- 32 deca warm flour
- 32 deca sugar
- 32 deca almonds finely pounded and passed through a sieve with their skin
- 1 table spoon grated chocolate
- some cinnamon and nutmeg
- 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix butter with warm flour on a board, rubbing finely with hands
2. Add sugar, almonds, chocolate, cinnamon, and nutmeg, mixing well
3. Make a hollow in the center and add 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks
4. Mix well with a fork and work into a dough, letting it rest covered in a cool place
5. After approximately half an hour, roll out the dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a knife back
6. Cut out in cake form and place at finger-width distance on a butter-greased baking sheet
7. Brush the cakes with beaten eggs, cover with halved blanched almonds, and bake at moderate heat in the oven
8. Remove from baking sheet with a thin, long knife and let cool on a sieve or board
