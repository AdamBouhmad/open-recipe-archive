## Ingredients

- small chickens
- butter
- green peas, one Seidel
- green parsley
- crayfish tails, shelled

## Directions

1. Blanch small chickens and cut them into four pieces
2. Make a butter sauce and lay the chickens in it
3. Braise one Seidel of green peas with butter and green parsley until soft
4. Add the peas to the chickens along with the shelled crayfish tails
5. Let it cook for another quarter hour
6. To serve, place the chickens in the center of the dish, arrange the peas like a ring around the edge, and garnish with crayfish tails such that the peas remain two fingers' width visible
