## Ingredients

- peaches of good quality
- apricots of good quality
- lemon juice
- cold lemon water
- boiling lemon water
- 80 decilitres sugar
- 1 litre water

## Directions

1. Choose good quality fruits that are somewhat hard and picked in dry weather
2. Cut peaches and apricots in half, remove the stones, carefully peel the fruits
3. Rub with lemon juice
4. Place first in cold, then in boiling lemon water
5. Cook peaches until fairly soft, leave apricots in only a moment
6. Pass three times through fresh cold water
7. Treat further as the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842), using 80 decilitres sugar in 1 litre water instead of 60
