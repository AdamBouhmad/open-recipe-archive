## Ingredients

- 1 scaled carp
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Butter dough
- Cold fish farce

## Directions

1. Open, clean, and wash a scaled carp.
2. Sprinkle with salt and braise in the oven with occasional basting.
3. Meanwhile, line a fish mold brushed with butter and lined with flour with finger-thick rolled-out butter dough.
4. Spread 1 finger high with cold fish farce.
5. Place the roasted but cold carp upon it, spread with some farce again, and fill up.
6. Cover with a finger-thick butter dough plate fitted to the size of the fish mold, whose edge is pressed together with that of the side wall.
7. Score the dough plate several times with a knife and brush with beaten eggs.
