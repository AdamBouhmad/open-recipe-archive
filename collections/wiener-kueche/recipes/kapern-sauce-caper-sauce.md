## Ingredients

- a little onion, finely diced
- a few spoonfuls flour browned in butter or other fat
- one quarter-litre milk
- 4 loth capers
- broth if needed
- lemon juice
- pepper
- lemon peel (optional)

## Directions

1. Let finely diced onion run golden in butter or other fat with a few spoonfuls of browned flour
2. Add one quarter-litre milk and 4 loth capers
3. If too thick, pour in more broth
4. If the sauce is to be served over a roasted hare or calf's leg, add the pan drippings from the roast and let it boil together thoroughly
5. Season with lemon juice, pepper, and lemon peel as desired
6. Pour the sauce over the roast when serving
