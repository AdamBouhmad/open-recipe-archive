## Ingredients

- Muskät apples
- fine powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wipe the Muskät apples with a cloth
2. Insert a small corer at the side of the stem
3. Twist slightly and draw out the core housing without piercing the apples, so that about two knife-backs of the bottom remain
4. Fill the hollow space created with finely powdered sugar
5. Place the apples in a flat, dry pan
6. Roast in the oven without any additional liquid
7. When the apples feel soft, arrange them heaped on a platter
8. Pour the thick juice created by roasting over them
9. Serve with various dishes or also for tea
10. In this way not only Muskät apples, but any variety of apple can be roasted
11. Particularly recommended for invalids
