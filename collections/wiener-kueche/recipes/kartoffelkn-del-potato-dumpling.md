## Ingredients

- 1 kg potatoes
- 2 sliced bread rolls
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon semolina
- Flour
- Bread cubes roasted in fat

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in their skins the day before
2. Strain and refrigerate
3. Before use, peel and pass through a ricer
4. Mix with bread cubes that have been roasted in hot fat and cut into cubes
5. Add salt, 1 tablespoon semolina, and flour stirred in by the spoonful
6. Add flour until dumplings show no cracks
7. Form dumplings
8. Place in a casserole in much boiling salt water
9. Cook slowly for 5-8 minutes
10. Serve like Semmelknödel
