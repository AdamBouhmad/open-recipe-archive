## Ingredients

- 14 dkg sugar
- 1 egg white
- 14 dkg chopped nuts
- 14 dkg dates, cut into noodle-like strips

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dkg sugar with 1 egg white in a wide mixing basin
2. Mix in 14 dkg chopped nuts and 14 dkg dates cut into noodle-like strips
