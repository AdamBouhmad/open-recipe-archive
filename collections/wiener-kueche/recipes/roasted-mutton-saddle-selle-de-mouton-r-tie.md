## Ingredients

- 1 mutton saddle
- Salt and pepper
- Bacon pieces, pencil-thick and finger-long

## Directions

1. Remove the fine membranes and hack the ribs lengthwise several times.
2. Rub with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Lard with pencil-thick and finger-long pieces of bacon.
4. Prepare further as the roasted mutton leg.
