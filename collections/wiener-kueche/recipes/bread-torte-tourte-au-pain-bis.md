## Ingredients

- 30 deca powdered sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 30 deca almonds, finely ground with skin and passed
- 3 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 12 deca grated house bread
- 6 tablespoons rum
- 1 knife point finely cut lemon zest
- 8 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon black flour
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan

## Directions

1. Stir 30 deca powdered sugar with 16 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Add 30 deca finely ground and passed almonds, 3 tablespoons grated chocolate, 12 deca grated house bread moistened with 6 tablespoons rum, and 1 knife point finely cut lemon zest
3. Stir for 1/2 hour
4. Gently fold in the snow (stiffly beaten egg whites) from 8 egg whites with 1 tablespoon black flour
5. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
6. Bake for approximately 1 hour in the oven at low heat
