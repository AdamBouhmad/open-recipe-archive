## Ingredients

- Ripe elderberries
- Powdered sugar (1/2 kilo sugar per 1/4 liter juice)
- Juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Remove the elderberries from their stems, crush them, and strain the juice through a felt sack
2. Add powdered sugar, namely 1/2 kilo sugar per 1/4 liter of juice
3. Dissolve the sugar and boil it with the juice of 2 lemons
4. When the liquid begins to become thick and has cooled, fill it into glasses or small jars, cork them, bind with bladders, and store in a cool place
