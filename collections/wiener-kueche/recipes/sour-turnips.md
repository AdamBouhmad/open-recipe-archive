## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg turnips
- caraway seeds
- salt
- cold water
- 4 dkg fat
- onion

## Directions

1. Lightly wash the turnips
2. Place them in a pot with caraway seeds, salt, and enough cold water to cover
3. Cook for 1½ hours
4. Add a light onion roux made with 4 dkg fat
5. Cook for 30 minutes more
