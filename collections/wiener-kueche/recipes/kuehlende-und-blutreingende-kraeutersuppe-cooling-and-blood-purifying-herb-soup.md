## Ingredients

- Young leaves and stems of lettuce
- Young leaves and stems of endive
- Young leaves and stems of purslane
- Young leaves and stems of pig's ear
- Twelve loths of each
- Six loths sorrel
- Butter
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Thin meat broth

## Directions

1. Wash the young leaves and stems of lettuce, endive, purslane, and pig's ear in warm water
2. Gently press out the water
3. Cook gently with thin meat broth
4. Add butter, salt, and some nutmeg
