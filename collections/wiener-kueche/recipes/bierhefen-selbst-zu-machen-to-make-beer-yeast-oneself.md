## Ingredients

- 6 Maß water
- 2 handfuls barley or wheat malt
- one spoonful wine stone salt
- one knife point of wine stone

## Directions

1. Take 6 Maß water and 2 handfuls of barley or wheat malt
2. Let it cook down over slow fire to 2 Maß
3. Cool it until it is lukewarm
4. Add one spoonful of wine stone salt and one knife point of wine stone
5. You have usable yeast, which you only need to clean by passing it through a sieve
