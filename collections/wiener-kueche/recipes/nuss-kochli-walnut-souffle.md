## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter, beaten
- 16 Deka sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 15 Deka fine ground common walnuts
- Equal amount of fine ground and sieved hazelnuts
- 1 knife-tip grated nutmeg
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter gradually with sugar and egg yolks
2. Mix in fine ground common walnuts and fine ground and sieved hazelnuts with grated nutmeg
3. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 egg whites
4. Fill this mixture into a butter-brushed, flour-dusted mold
5. Let it cook covered for ¾ to 1 hour in steam or bake in the oven
6. Turn out onto a plate and serve with sauce of choice
7. Can serve, for example, water-chocolate sauce in one saucière and whipped cream in another, serving the souffle with both
