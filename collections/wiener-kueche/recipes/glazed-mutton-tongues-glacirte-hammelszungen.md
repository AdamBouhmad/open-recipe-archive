## Ingredients

- Mutton tongues
- Bacon, finely cut
- Ham
- Veal meat
- Bunch of herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat glaze
- Sauce of choice

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the tongues
2. Lard them with finely cut bacon
3. Cook them tender in a braise with ham, veal meat cuts, and a bunch of herbs
4. Season well
5. Arrange the cooked tongues
6. Pass some of the braise into another casserole
7. Add a piece of glaze to it
8. Place the tongues in the casserole
9. Leave the casserole on hot ashes until they glaze themselves beautifully golden
10. When serving, give any desired sauce with them
