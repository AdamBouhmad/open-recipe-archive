## Ingredients

- Veal marrow
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Lemon juice
- Lard
- White pepper
- Green parsley
- Bread or egg batter
- Hot fat

## Directions

1. Since a veal loin does not provide much marrow and it does not keep well, it is necessary to order it at the right time
2. Carefully remove the marrow from the outer skin
3. Poach it for 1 minute in boiling salt water
4. Remove it and shock with cold water
5. Lay on a cloth and cut into finger-length pieces
6. Place in a bowl, season with lemon juice, necessary lard, white pepper and green parsley
7. Cover with butter-brushed paper and stir occasionally
8. Shortly before serving, dip each piece in bread or egg batter and fry in very hot fat
9. It is usually arranged mixed with fried brains
