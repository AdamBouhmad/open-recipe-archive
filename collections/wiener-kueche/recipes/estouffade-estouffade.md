## Ingredients

- A beef rump of 3 kilos
- Salt and spices
- 12 ounces ham
- 12 ounces tongue
- 12 ounces lard
- Bacon sheets for lining
- Finely cut onion pieces
- Root vegetables
- 1 bay leaf
- Mixed spices
- Beef marrow bones
- 5 crushed juniper berries
- 3 pieces dried figs
- 1 piece black bread
- 1 liter white or red wine
- Soup as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the beef well and rub with salt and spices
2. Cut 12 ounces each of ham, tongue and lard into equal pieces and insert alternately into the meat
3. Line the bottom of a casserole with bacon sheets, finely cut onion pieces, root vegetables, 1 bay leaf, some spices, beef marrow bones, 5 crushed juniper berries, 3 pieces dried figs and 1 piece black bread
4. Place the meat on top and let it braise covered for a short time, then add 1 liter white or red wine and braise covered for 3-4 hours
5. If the liquid evaporates too much, add some soup
6. Serve with macaroni, rice, potatoes or finely cut turnips
