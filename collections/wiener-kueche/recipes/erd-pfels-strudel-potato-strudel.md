## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 32 deca mashed potatoes (cooked and passed through a sieve)
- ⅓ teaspoon salt
- ¼ liter milk cream
- 6 egg whites
- 16 deca raisins
- breadcrumbs
- flour for dough

## Directions

1. Mix 16 deca butter with 16 deca vanilla sugar
2. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
3. Blend in 32 deca mashed potatoes, ⅓ teaspoon salt, and ¼ liter milk cream, one after another, mixing well
4. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 6 egg whites last
5. Spread this filling on the finely stretched strudel dough
6. Sprinkle with 16 deca raisins and some breadcrumbs
7. Roll the dough together lightly and treat as described in the Sweet Semolina Strudel recipe
