## Ingredients

- 1 orange
- ½ ounces sugar
- 1 lemon
- some cold water
- 12 ounces grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- some vinegar
- 4 tablespoons oil
- 2 tablespoons grated horseradish
- salt

## Directions

1. Grate ½ ounce sugar from an orange and place this sugar together with the expressed juice of one orange, 1 lemon, and some cold water in a bowl and let it stand covered
2. Meanwhile, pass 12 ounces of grated rolls soaked in water and well pressed out together with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve
3. Mix the dissolved orange horseradish with the passed mixture and some vinegar, 4 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons grated horseradish and some salt, stirring well
4. If it should be too thick, it can be thinned with some water
5. The taste should be pleasantly sweet and yet somewhat sour
