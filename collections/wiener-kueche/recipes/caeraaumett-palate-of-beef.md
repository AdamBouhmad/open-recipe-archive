## Ingredients

- Beef palate
- salt
- water
- soup
- lemon sauce, béchamel sauce, butter sauce, or green parsley sauce
- Sauce Piquante with capers or anchovies
- boiled rice, Riz-à-Bisi, puff pastry cut out with cookie cutters, or similar garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the beef palate in strongly salted water until the fine skin can be loosened.
2. Cut it lengthwise in half, remove the spongy part filled with coagulated blood, and cut into 2 finger-widths wide oval pieces.
3. Cook the pieces completely tender in the soup.
4. Serve the hot palate pieces covered with any desired sauce: lemon sauce, béchamel sauce, butter sauce with finely cut green parsley, Sauce Piquante, or with piquant caper or anchovy sauce.
5. Garnish with boiled rice, Riz-à-Bisi, puff pastry cut out with cookie cutters, or similar.
