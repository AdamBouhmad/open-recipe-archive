## Ingredients

- 1 prepared herring
- Peeled Muscovy apples
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as above and cut peeled Muscovy apples into cubes.
2. Mix well together with lemon juice or vinegar and oil and arrange in a bowl.
