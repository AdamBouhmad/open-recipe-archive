## Ingredients

- beautiful large redcurrants
- red and white redcurrants (optional)

## Directions

1. Pluck beautiful large redcurrants from their stems
2. Fill preserving jars with them
3. Treat further as the medlars (Nr. 1845)
4. One can mix red and white redcurrants
