## Ingredients

- beautiful yellow and ripe quinces
- sugar (1 pound sugar per pound of quince pulp)
- juice of 2 lemons
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces, cut them into four parts, and remove the cores.
2. Place in a pan with plenty of water on charcoal fire.
3. Cook until completely soft.
4. Pour off and press the pulp through a sieve.
5. Weigh the sugar: one pound of sugar per pound of quince pulp.
6. Clarify and cook all the sugar.
7. Stir the quince pulp into the sugar.
8. Add the juice of 2 lemons while cooking.
9. Keep on charcoal fire until thick, stirring to prevent burning.
10. Allow to cool before storing in jars or containers.
