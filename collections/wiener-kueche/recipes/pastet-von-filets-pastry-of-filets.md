## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- Good veal farce
- Beautiful larded filets, well marinated
- Good braising liquid
- Bacon slices
- Egg yolk
- Sauce haché

## Directions

1. Cover a tin dish with pastry rolled to half a finger's thickness and cut round all around
2. Pipe a good veal farce somewhat thick
3. Then place the filets, which have been cut into pieces and braised tender in a good braising liquid, on top of it
4. Lay bacon slices on top
5. Brush the inner edge with egg yolk
6. Make a lid from pastry over it
7. Brush the pastry
8. Place on a sheet and bake quickly
9. Cut out the lid
10. Remove the bacon slices
11. Pour a sauce haché into it
