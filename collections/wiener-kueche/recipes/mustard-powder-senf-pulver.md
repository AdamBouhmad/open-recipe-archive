## Ingredients

- 2 pounds mustard powder
- 0.5 pound ground sugar, or more
- 12 loths cinnamon, ground
- 12 loths ginger, ground
- 12 loths allspice, ground
- 1 quenchen fennel seeds
- Very strong vinegar, fortified by frost
- 1 loth marjoram per measure of vinegar
- 2 loths tarragon herb, dried and chopped
- 1 loth thyme, dried and chopped
- 1 loth sage, dried and chopped
- 2 loths rocambole onions

## Directions

1. To the very strong vinegar fortified by frost, add 1 loth marjoram, 2 loths tarragon herb, thyme and sage (1 loth each), all dried and well chopped, then add 2 loths rocambole onions
2. Let this infuse in the sun for 5 to 6 days, then strain through
3. Take 2 pounds of the mustard powder, 0.5 pound ground sugar or more, cinnamon, ginger, and allspice ground (12 loths of each), and 1 quenchen fennel seeds
4. When well mixed, combine with as much vinegar as necessary to form a paste
5. Store in a suitable place, well sealed
