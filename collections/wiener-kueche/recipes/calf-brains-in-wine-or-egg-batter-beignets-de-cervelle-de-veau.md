## Ingredients

- Calf brain cooked in salted water
- Wine or egg batter

## Directions

1. Once the cooked brain is completely cooled, cut into pieces of choice
2. Dip in wine or egg batter
3. Fry in very hot fat
4. Serve with fine vegetables or fresh parsley
