## Ingredients

- 12 dkg butter
- 12 dkg sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 12 dkg softened or grated chocolate
- 4 egg whites
- 12 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Cream together the butter with sugar and egg yolks gradually
2. Mix in the softened or grated chocolate
3. Fold in the flour together with the beaten egg whites
4. Spread the mixture finger-thick on a buttered, floured baking sheet
5. Bake at a low heat
6. While still warm, cut into rectangles
7. Let cool, then sandwich pairs together with jam or sweetened whipped cream
8. Dust with sugar or decorate with whipped cream
