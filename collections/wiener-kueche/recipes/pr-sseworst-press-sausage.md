## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo cooked ham or tongue
- 1 kilo cooked pork
- pig's ears
- 1 teaspoon green parsley
- marjoram
- onion
- 8 decagrams bacon, diced
- 8 decagrams fresh bacon, diced
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 liter pig's blood
- pig's stomach

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked ham or tongue, cooked pork, and pig's ears into small cubes and place in a deep bowl
2. Roast the green parsley, marjoram, and onion together in 8 decagrams of diced bacon
3. Add the rendered fat without the onion and parsley to the cut meat along with 8 decagrams of diced fresh bacon, the required salt and pepper, and 1/2 liter of pig's blood, and mix well
4. Fill cleaned pig's stomach with the mixture and tie off both ends
5. Cook covered in salted water for 2 full hours
6. Place on a board, cover with another board, and weight it down
7. Once completely cooled, slice thinly, arrange in a bowl, and serve with vinegar and oil
