## Ingredients

- 1 large liter of sauerkraut
- 2 liters cold water
- 10 deciliters hot fat
- 1 head onion finely chopped
- 10 deciliters flour
- 1 ladleful white beef broth

## Directions

1. Place 1 large liter of sauerkraut in cold water and cook very soft uncovered
2. In 10 deciliters hot fat, let 1 finely chopped head of onion brown slightly, but it must not become dark
3. Add approximately 10 deciliters flour and brown it with frequent stirring until it has a yellow color
4. Pour the roux with 1 ladleful of white beef broth over the sauerkraut with constant stirring
5. Pass the sauerkraut through a sieve and add to the roux as much as is necessary
