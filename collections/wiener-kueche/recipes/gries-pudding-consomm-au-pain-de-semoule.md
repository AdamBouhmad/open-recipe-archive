## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 20 deca semolina
- salt
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 5 egg yolks, 20 deca semolina, and some salt
3. Lightly fold in the snow from 5 egg whites
4. Fill into a mold greased with butter and lined with flour
5. Cook in steam for 3/4 hour
6. Turn the pudding out onto a platter
7. Let cool, cut into desired pieces
8. Pour boiling soup over and let stand covered for 10 minutes
