## Ingredients

- 8 deka blanched almonds
- 1 egg white
- 8 deka butter
- 16 deka sugar of any kind
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 32 deka flour

## Directions

1. Pound 8 deka blanched almonds with 1 egg white into a very fine paste
2. Whip 8 deka butter until fluffy, stir in the ground almonds
3. After these add 16 deka sugar of any kind, 6 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons sour cream, and 32 deka flour
4. Let this dough stand for a while in a cool place after it is well mixed together, then put it on a board dusted with flour, roll it out knife-back thick, and cut from it finger-long and 3-finger-wide strips
5. Then tie a piece of twine or string approximately 1.5 meters long, quite thick, tightly at the beginning of the mold and pull the twine through it
