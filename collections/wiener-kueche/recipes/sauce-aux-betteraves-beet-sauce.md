## Ingredients

- 1 piece cooked or roasted red beet
- 8 tablespoons butter
- Stock
- Red wine
- Nutmeg
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel 1 piece of cooked or roasted red beet and cut it very finely with a vegetable knife
2. Make a butter sauce from 8 tablespoons butter as previously described
3. Stir in the finely cut beets, necessary stock, some red wine, and nutmeg
4. Let it cook well with a little sugar, strain it through a sieve, and bring to boil once more
