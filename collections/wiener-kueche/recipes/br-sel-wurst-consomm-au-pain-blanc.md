## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 18 deca white breadcrumbs
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks and some salt
3. Lightly fold in 18 deca white breadcrumbs with the snow from 5 egg whites
4. Treatment same as for potato dumplings
