## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled, strained egg yolks
- 3 raw egg yolks
- 1/2 to 1 1/4 liters oil
- juice of 2 lemons or 1 tablespoon vinegar, or some drops of dissolved citric acid or 4 tablespoons very strong tarragon vinegar
- salt to taste
- 2 knife tips pepper

## Directions

1. Put 6 hard-boiled, strained egg yolks and 3 raw egg yolks in a deep bowl and stir them for 15 minutes on ice
2. Into this, stir in 1/2 to 1 1/4 liters of oil gradually, drop by drop
3. Add the lemon juice or vinegar drop by drop
4. Finally stir in some salt with 2 knife tips of pepper
5. Serve this sauce with stiffened, cold meat or cold vegetables
