## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams finely ground sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 8 decagrams melted butter
- 8 decagrams flour
- 4 egg whites
- butter
- flour
- cream or marmalade

## Directions

1. Stir 8 decagrams finely ground sugar with 4 egg yolks for 1 1/2 hours.
2. Mix in 8 decagrams melted butter with 8 decagrams flour gradually, adding 1 tablespoon flour and 1 tablespoon butter alternately, then lightly fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 4 egg whites.
3. Fill a buttered cake form dusted with flour with this mixture and bake at moderate heat in the oven for 1 1/2 hours, then place on a sieve.
4. Once completely cooled, cut away the crust on the side that was on top during baking, spread with desired cream or marmalade, and place the mixed cake No. 1 (cut as described) on top.
5. If the edges do not fit exactly, trim them evenly with a sharp, thin knife and cover the entire cake with glaze.
