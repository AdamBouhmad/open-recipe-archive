## Ingredients

- 2 measures of the best and purest vinegar
- rue, 1 handful
- sage, 1 handful
- mint, 1 handful
- wormwood, 1 handful
- lavender, 1 handful
- 1.5 loth of camphor

## Directions

1. Set rue, sage, mint, wormwood, and lavender (1 handful each) into 2 measures of the best and purest vinegar
2. Let the mixture stand for digestion at a warm place for 4 days, preferably in the sun
3. Let it steep and strain it, then press it out
4. Pour it into bottles
5. Add 1.5 loth of camphor to it
6. Use by rinsing the mouth with it and drinking a small sip, particularly when visiting the sick
7. Wash face and hands with it for additional protection
