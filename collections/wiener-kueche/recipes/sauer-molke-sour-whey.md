## Ingredients

- 1 liter cold milk
- Juice from 0.5 lemon or 1 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the juice from 0.5 lemon or 1 tablespoon of vinegar into 1 liter of cold milk.
2. Let it become hot to the boiling point and proceed further as described above.
