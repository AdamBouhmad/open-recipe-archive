## Ingredients

- veal leg
- bone marrow
- white wine
- spices
- cleaned mutton intestines

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the leg and scrape from the nerves
2. Make it fine first with a knife, then grate on a grater
3. Mix the finely diced marrow underneath
4. Season well and moisten with some white wine
5. Fill this mass into cleaned mutton intestines
6. Divide into lengths as desired
7. Fry slowly on a roasting rack
