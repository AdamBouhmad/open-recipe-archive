## Ingredients

- 28 Deka beaten butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 whole eggs
- 24 Deka sugar
- 1 knife tip finely chopped lemon zest
- 28 Deka flour
- jam for assembly
- white glaze

## Directions

1. Add egg yolks, whole eggs, sugar, and lemon zest gradually to beaten butter
2. Stir for 1/2 hour
3. Mix in flour well
4. Cut round or square sheets from paper and brush with butter
5. Spread the mixture finger-high on them
6. Place them next to each other on a baking sheet
7. Bake at moderate heat in the oven
8. Remove from oven and once completely cooled, remove from paper
9. Brush the side that was not covered with paper with dissolved jam
10. Stack the sheets so that a spread side is covered with a dry side
11. Do not spread the top sheet
12. Cut the edges evenly with a fine, sharp knife
13. Cover with white glaze and let dry
