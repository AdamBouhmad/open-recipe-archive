## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Pepper (optional)
- Paper
- Aspic glaze

## Directions

1. Soak veal sweetbreads in lukewarm water for one hour, then boil for 10 minutes in salt water.
2. Clean with cold water and cook completely tender in less broth.
3. When removed and cooled, cut diagonally following the shape of the board into slices the thickness of a knife back.
4. Lay them side by side in a casserole heavily lined with butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs and, if desired, some pepper, cover with paper brushed with butter, and set aside in a cool place.
5. About one quarter hour before serving, fry the sweetbreads at medium heat for 2-3 minutes.
6. Turn the slices over and fry equally on the second side.
7. To the sauce from which the fat has been removed earlier, pour in dissolved aspic glaze and let it come to a boil once.
8. Arrange the sliced sweetbreads alternately with bread slices roasted in butter on a plate and pour the sauce over.
