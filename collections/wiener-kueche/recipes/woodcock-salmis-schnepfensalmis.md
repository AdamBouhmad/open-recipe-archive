## Ingredients

- Woodcock innards
- Liver
- Piece of poultry, chicken, or veal liver, finely cut
- Mushroom piece
- Small piece of soaked black bread, well pressed out
- Sprigs of parsley
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 2 crushed juniper berries
- Thick semolina slices

## Directions

1. Remove innards from woodcock and set aside the liver
2. Cut the liver and other liver pieces very finely together with mushroom, black bread, and parsley
3. Roast this mixture in butter
4. Add the finely cut liver with necessary salt, pepper, and crushed juniper berries
5. Roast briefly
6. Pass through a sieve and stir well with an egg yolk
7. Brush thick semolina slices with this mixture
8. Place on a sheet in the oven and let dry slightly
9. Garnish the woodcock with these semolina slices
