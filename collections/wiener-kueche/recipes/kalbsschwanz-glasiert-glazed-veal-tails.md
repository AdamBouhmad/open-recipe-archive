## Ingredients

- veal tails
- salt water
- cold water
- thinly sliced bacon
- white soup
- aspic glaze
- rice, garnished

## Directions

1. Cut the veal tails into finger-length pieces and after some rinsing, cook them 15 minutes in salted water.
2. Transfer them from there to cold water.
3. After they have completely cooled, remove them and place them in a casserole lined with thinly sliced bacon and cover the meat with equally sliced bacon.
4. Pour white soup over them and let them cook until soft.
5. Then set it aside, arrange the bacon slices after some cooling in a shallow casserole, place the tail pieces one after another on top, coat them with dissolved aspic glaze, place the casserole briefly in the oven, arrange the pieces on a platter and serve with garnished rice.
