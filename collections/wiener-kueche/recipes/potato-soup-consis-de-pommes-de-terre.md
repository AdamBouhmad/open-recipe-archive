## Ingredients

- 16 drams butter or fat
- 1 teaspoon diced onion
- green parsley
- flour as needed
- 2 liters cold water
- 1 liter diced, washed, raw potatoes
- 1 pinch grated marjoram
- salt
- toasted semolina bread

## Directions

1. Toast the onion and green parsley in butter
2. Add flour as needed and toast until slightly yellow
3. Gradually add 2 liters of cold water while stirring to prevent lumps
4. Bring to a boil
5. Add diced raw potatoes, marjoram, and salt
6. Simmer until potatoes are completely soft
7. Serve with toasted semolina bread
