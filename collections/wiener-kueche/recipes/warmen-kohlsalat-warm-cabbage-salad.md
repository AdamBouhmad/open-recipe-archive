## Ingredients

- head cabbage
- salt
- butter
- vinegar
- wheat flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the head cabbage very finely or shave it finely
2. Sprinkle it with salt
3. Set a pan over charcoal fire and let butter melt in it
4. Sweat the cabbage in the butter
5. After a quarter hour, pour in the vinegar
6. Finally, to have a thickish broth, add some stirred wheat flour and pepper
