## Ingredients

- Dough as prepared in Nr. 1258
- Lard for brushing
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described in Nr. 1258
2. After the dough has risen covered in a warm place, use a spoon to place pieces of desired size in a casserole brushed with lard
3. Lay them closely next to each other, brush them meanwhile with melted lard
4. Cover them with a cloth and let them stand in a warm place until they have risen
5. Brush them with melted lard, pour over them 1/2 liter boiling milk mixed with 2 tablespoons sugar and bake for 3/4 to 1 hour in a fairly hot oven
6. After this time, remove the casserole from the oven, turn it out, dust the dumplings with sugar and serve at table
