## Ingredients

- 7 pounds peaches of the best variety and sufficient ripeness
- 4 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the kernels from the peaches.
2. Press the peaches through a sieve so that only the skins remain.
3. Clarify 4 pounds of sugar according to the method for marmalade.
4. Stir the peach pulp into the clarified sugar.
5. Fill into jars or containers.
