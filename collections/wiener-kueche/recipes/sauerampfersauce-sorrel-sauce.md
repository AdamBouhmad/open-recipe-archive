## Ingredients

- 12 h sorrel
- 4 dkg fat
- 5-6 tablespoons cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and finely chop the sorrel
2. Cook in salted water for 10 minutes
3. Make a light roux from the fat, add the sorrel
4. Add the cream, cook together for 20 minutes
