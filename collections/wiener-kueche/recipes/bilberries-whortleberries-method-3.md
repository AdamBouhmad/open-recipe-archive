## Ingredients

- 1 kilo fine large bilberries
- 1 kilo fine sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Place the bilberries in a glass or earthen or porcelain container
2. Add the fine sifted sugar
3. Cover with paper, pricking several holes in it with a needle
4. Leave the bilberries to sit without cooking in a cool, airy place
5. From time to time stir the bilberries so the sugar mixes together
