## Ingredients

- 4 Pfund almonds
- several eggs
- 13 Vierding sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 whole eggs
- 1 Pfund melted butter
- capon meat
- 6 peeled Borstorfer apples
- 1/2 Pfund candied citron
- 1 Pfund bread crumbs
- 2 Esslöffel flour
- nutmeg flower
- 1 Pfund rice
- 2 Seidel milk

## Directions

1. Pound 4 pounds almonds, which have been crushed fine with several eggs, with 13 Vierding sugar, 6 egg yolks, and 6 whole eggs for a quarter hour
2. Gradually add 1 pound melted, then mostly cooled butter
3. Mix in the capon meat, 6 peeled Borstorfer apples, 1/2 pound candied citron, finely cut, 1 pound bread crumbs, 2 spoonfuls flour, some nutmeg flower
4. Finally add 1 pound rice that has been soaked in 2 Seidel milk, drained cleanly, and cooled
5. Bind and cook as with the others
6. Serve with wine foam
