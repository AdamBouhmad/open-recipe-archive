## Ingredients

- Rostbraten (pot roasts)
- Zwiebel (onions)
- Fein geschnittener Knoblauch oder Rocambole (finely cut garlic or rocambole)
- Fett (fat)
- Suppe (broth)

## Directions

1. Prepare as you would ordinarily prepare pot roast with onions, but instead of onions use finely cut garlic or rocambole.
2. Add the garlic at the same time you pour the fat with broth over the meat.
