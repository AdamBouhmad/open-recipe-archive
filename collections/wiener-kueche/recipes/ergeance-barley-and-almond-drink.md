## Ingredients

- Barley water
- 12 deka peeled almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare barley water, pound some spoonfuls of it with 12 deka peeled almonds.
2. Pour the remaining barley water over them and let them rest quietly.
3. Pour the almonds through a strong clean cloth, overflow them again with some barley water, squeeze them again, and add finely pounded sugar to taste.
