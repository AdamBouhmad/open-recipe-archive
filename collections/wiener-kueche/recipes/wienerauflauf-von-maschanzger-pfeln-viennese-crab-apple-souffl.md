## Ingredients

- 24 beautiful crab apples
- Three-quarters pound hard roasted zwieback
- 3 measures milk
- Lemon juice
- Three-quarters pound sweet finely pounded almonds
- Half pound clear sugar
- Lemon zest
- Half pound butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 whole eggs
- Egg whites from 8 eggs
- Butter puff pastry

## Directions

1. Peel 24 beautiful crab apples and grate them on a grater.
2. Pound three-quarters pound hard roasted zwieback finely and sift it.
3. Make cheese from 3 measures milk with a little lemon juice, press it dry, and mix with three-quarters pound sweet finely pounded almonds, half pound clear sugar and grated lemon zest along with the zwieback flour and grated apples.
4. Beat half pound butter with 12 egg yolks and 6 whole eggs to foam, fold in both masses gradually, and stir for 3 quarters of an hour so that the mass becomes very light.
5. Finally add the snow from 8 eggs and bake the soufflé in a form lined with butter puff pastry.
