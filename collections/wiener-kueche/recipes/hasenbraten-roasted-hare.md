## Ingredients

- 1 small hare back and leg
- 12 dkg bacon
- 1 lemon
- 4 tablespoons capers
- 1 dkg cream
- salt
- pepper
- fat or butter
- vinegar
- water
- lingonberries

## Directions

1. Skin and wipe the hare
2. Salt and pepper it
3. Lard it and place in a pan
4. Sprinkle with onion rings and lemon peel
5. Baste with hot fat or butter
6. Add a little vinegar
7. Roast for about 1 to 1½ hours, basting frequently with its own juices and adding hot water
8. Before it is done, gradually baste with the cream so the sauce has a nice brown color
9. Remove the hare and press the juices, strain them, and let boil once more
10. Cut the back into pieces, arrange on a warm platter, and place the leg in its former shape at the bottom
11. Top the hare with thin lemon slices, alternating with capers and lingonberries
12. Garnish one side with vol au vent and the other with rice
13. Decorate with curled parsley
14. Serve the sauce separately
