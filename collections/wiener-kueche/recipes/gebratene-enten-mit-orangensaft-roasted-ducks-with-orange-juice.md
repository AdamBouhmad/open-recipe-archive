## Ingredients

- Two ducks
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onions
- Shallots
- Basil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Orange slices
- Orange juice
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Clean and dress the two ducks properly for roasting
2. Take a piece of butter, roll it repeatedly in finely chopped parsley, onions, shallots, basil, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
3. Stuff each duck with such a piece of butter with herbs in the belly and put them on the spit
4. When they are nearly done, dress them on a plate, make several cuts lengthwise, sprinkle salt and pepper on them, cover them with orange slices
5. Skim off the fat dripped from the ducks and give it under the ducks
6. Serve them quite hot, squeeze orange juice on them, and garnish the plate with croutons
