## Ingredients

- raspberries
- juice of one lemon
- 2 measures of water per 1 measure of raspberry juice
- finely ground sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Crush the raspberries in an earthenware vessel
2. Press them through a napkin
3. To 1 measure of juice, add 2 measures of water and the juice of one lemon
4. Add finely ground sugar to taste
5. Pass it through a hair sieve or napkin
