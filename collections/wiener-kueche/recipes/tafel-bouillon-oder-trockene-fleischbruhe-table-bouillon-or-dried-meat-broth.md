## Ingredients

- about 12 pounds of juicy and strong beef
- marrow from some bones
- flesh from a couple of old hens together with their bones
- one calf's foot
- some mace
- some ginger and pepper

## Directions

1. Let all of this cook for 12 hours in an earthenware pot over charcoal fire, covered, stirring occasionally and skimming
2. Strain it through a hair sieve, skim off the fat and let it cool
3. Let the gelée slowly thicken over gentle charcoal fire, then allow it to solidify in a tin dish, cut it when cooled into pieces three fingers long and wide
4. Dry it in an earthenware dish in a baking oven when the bread has been removed, and keep it dry
5. For unexpected visits, on travels, etc., make a meat soup by cooking chervil, spinach, etc. in water with salt and now dissolving one lot of these tablets over charcoal fire in two soup plates of broth
6. Add some seasoning if desired
