## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters water
- 12 dekagrams chocolate, broken up
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- 2 spoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Cook the water with the chocolate, sugar and potato flour under frequent boiling until thickened and cool it.
