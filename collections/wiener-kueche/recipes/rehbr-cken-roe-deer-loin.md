## Ingredients

- Roe deer loin
- Butter
- Large flat knife
- Sauce (piquant or other)
- Lingonberries or rowan berries sauce (optional)
- Buttermilk pastry for garnish (optional)

## Directions

1. In the raw state, trim the loin neatly, and with a kitchen mallet lightly strike the bones on the sides, but they must not be broken through so that the loin lies evenly lengthwise on the platter
2. The skinning, spitting and further treatment is the same as for the deer loin and roe deer leg
3. When serving, place the roe deer loin on a board and remove the meat from the bone using a large flat knife
4. Place the backbone on a platter, cut the meat into slices two fingers wide, lay them close together on the bone so that the loin looks completely whole, pour juice over it and garnish with buttermilk pastry
5. You can also serve lingonberries or rowan berries sauce with it
