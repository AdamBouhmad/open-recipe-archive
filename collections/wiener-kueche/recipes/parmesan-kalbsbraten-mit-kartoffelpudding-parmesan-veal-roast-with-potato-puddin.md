## Ingredients

- 80-100 dkg veal shoulder or leg
- 6 dkg bacon
- 4 dkg Parmesan
- ½ liter cream
- 5 dkg hot fat
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, season with salt, lard it, place in a roasting pan with 5 dkg hot fat
2. Pour in water and roast in the oven for 1½ hours, basting and adding liquid frequently
3. Mix the meat juices with grated Parmesan and cream
4. Pour over the meat and serve on a long platter
