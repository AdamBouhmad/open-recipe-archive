## Ingredients

- 80 dkg pollack fish
- 4 anchovies
- 1/4 liter cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as oyster fish, but baste it with cream while frying
2. Transfer it to a platter and pour hot anchovy butter over it
