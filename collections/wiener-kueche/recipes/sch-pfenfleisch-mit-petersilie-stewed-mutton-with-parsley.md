## Ingredients

- 2 kilos mutton meat
- 3/4 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- 1 head of onion (optional)
- Finely cut green parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton meat into pieces of any size.
2. Place in a casserole and pour over 3/4 liter water and 1/2 tablespoon salt.
3. Add 1 teaspoon caraway and optionally 1 head of onion.
4. Cover and cook gently for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
5. Arrange on a platter, sprinkle with finely cut green parsley.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes as a snack, instead of roast meat, or at tea.
