## Ingredients

- Some onion
- Green parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 large peeled carrot, cut into cubes
- Some soup
- 6 tablespoons butter
- Equal amount of flour
- 1-2 teaspoons finely chopped dill herb
- 1 1/4 liters cream
- Some salt

## Directions

1. Let some onion and green parsley roast in 2 tablespoons butter
2. Braise 1 large peeled carrot, cut into cubes, with some soup until the gherkin pieces are soft
3. Make a roux from 6 tablespoons butter and equal amount of flour
4. Braise the gherkins with 1-2 teaspoons finely chopped dill herb, 1 1/4 liters cream and some salt
5. Stir together well and, if desired, pass through a sieve
