## Ingredients

- Asparagus heads
- Endive or escarole lettuce
- Char fish
- Butter
- Mace
- Parmesan lettuce head
- Baked bread roll

## Directions

1. Cut asparagus heads to the length of one segment and clean endive or escarole lettuce. Blanch both together, strain and place in fresh water. Chop one lettuce head with several well-washed char underneath. Strain asparagus well on sieve. Put butter in a casserole, place the ingredients in it and steam slightly, season with mace and set aside. When the jus has reduced, strain it and let it simmer well with the remaining minced lettuce. A quarter hour before serving, add baked bread roll and salt. The celery soup, minced cabbage and other herb soups are made the same way.
