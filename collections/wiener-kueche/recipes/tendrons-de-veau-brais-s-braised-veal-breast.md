## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- boiling salted water
- white butter sauce as needed

## Directions

1. Cook the veal breast in boiling salted water until it has a white color
2. To maintain this color, remove the veal breast and place it in cold water for a few minutes
3. Cut the meat into pieces of desired size
4. Place it in a baking pan and cover it with as much white butter sauce as needed
