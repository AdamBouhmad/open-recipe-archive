## Ingredients

- Veal cutlet of any size
- Bacon
- Fat
- Cream

## Directions

1. Salt a veal cutlet on both sides
2. Lard it with pencil-thick, half-finger-long bacon
3. Place in a casserole with fat
4. Brown on both sides
5. Pour off the fat
6. Baste the cutlet with cream
7. Let it braise in the oven until it has a light yellow color on both sides
8. Capers may be added if desired
