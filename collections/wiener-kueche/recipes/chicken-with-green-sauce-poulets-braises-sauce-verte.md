## Ingredients

- chickens
- sauce ingredients from chicken with lemon sauce recipe
- 1 tablespoon spinach puree
- juice of 1½ lemons

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Chicken with Lemon Sauce
2. In the strained butter sauce, stir in 1 tablespoon spinach puree and juice of 1½ lemons
