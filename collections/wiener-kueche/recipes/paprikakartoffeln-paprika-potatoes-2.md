## Ingredients

- 5 dkg fat
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 knife points paprika
- cold water
- raw potatoes, peeled and quartered
- salt
- cream
- flour

## Directions

1. In a casserole, let 1 finely chopped onion turn golden in 5 dkg fat
2. Add 3 knife points paprika
3. Quench with a ladle of cold water
4. Add raw peeled, quartered potatoes, salt and let them stew covered for 30-35 minutes, adding water occasionally
5. At the end stir in a few spoonfuls of cream and cook for another 5 minutes
6. If the cream is too thin, it must be thickened with 1 spoonful of flour
