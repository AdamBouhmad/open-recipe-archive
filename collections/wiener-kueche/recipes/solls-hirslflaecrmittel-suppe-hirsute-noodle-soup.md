## Ingredients

- 4 whole eggs
- approximately 12 deca white flour
- 1 tablespoon full of sweet milk
- 2 liters beef broth

## Directions

1. Mix 4 whole eggs with approximately 12 deca white flour and 1 tablespoon of sweet milk, stirring well so that the dough is liquid
2. In 2 liters of boiling beef broth, pour the dough through a foam ladle or funnel and simmer for 10-15 minutes
