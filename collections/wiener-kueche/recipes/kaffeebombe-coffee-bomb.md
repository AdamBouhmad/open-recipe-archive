## Ingredients

- 1 dl very strong black coffee
- 10 dkg powdered sugar
- 1/2 liter firmly whipped cream
- salt
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Mix the coffee very well with 10 dkg powdered sugar and fold in the firmly whipped cream
2. Fill the mixture into a bomb mold rinsed with water, cover with a white sheet of paper, put on the lid, and seal the edge well with butter or fat so that no salt water can penetrate
3. Completely bury the mold in finely chopped ice mixed with 1/4 kg rock salt and let it stand for at least 4-5 hours
4. Remove the fat, hold the mold in hot water, and turn out like a cream
