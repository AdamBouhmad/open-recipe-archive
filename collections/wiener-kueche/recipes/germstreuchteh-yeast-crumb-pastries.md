## Ingredients

- 14 Decaliter butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 Decaliter fresh yeast dissolved in 1/2 liter milk
- 42 Decaliter flour

## Directions

1. Beat 14 Decaliter butter until fluffy.
2. Add 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 5 egg yolks one after another, stirring them in.
3. Mix this with 3 Decaliter fresh yeast dissolved in 1/2 liter milk and 42 Decaliter flour until well combined.
