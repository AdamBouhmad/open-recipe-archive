## Ingredients

- ¼ liter water
- 7 dag butter
- salt
- 11 dag flour
- 3-4 small eggs
- 1 spoon sugar
- whipped sweetened cream (Schlagobers)
- chocolate sauce

## Directions

1. Make a choux pastry (Brandteig) from the water, butter, salt, flour, eggs, and sugar
2. Using 2 small spoons, form oblong puffs on a buttered baking sheet
3. Brush with egg white or beaten egg
4. Bake until light brown over brisk heat and let cool
5. Slice them open on the side, fill with sweetened whipped cream
6. Place on a glass dish and pour chocolate sauce over them
