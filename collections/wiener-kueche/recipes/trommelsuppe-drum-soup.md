## Ingredients

- dough from the Bohemian Maultaschen recipe
- 1/4 liter poppy seed filling or nut and almond filling
- 2 deca finely pounded nuts
- 20 pieces shelled nuts pounded with sugar
- 20 pieces pounded, sieved hazelnuts or almonds
- 1 tablespoon breadcrumbs
- 1/8 liter boiling milk or cream
- optionally 1 pinch grated coffee or finely cut lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as for Bohemian Maultaschen
2. After dough has risen one-third higher, roll out knife-back thickness on flour-dusted board
3. Cut out round discs with dough cutter
4. Cover one disc with poppy seed filling and brush edges with beaten egg
5. Cover with a second dough disc and top with filling
6. Continue layering 6 dough discs total, each covered with different filling (vanilla, apricots or jam)
7. Place on butter-brushed baking sheet using a knife
8. Sprinkle with grated nuts
9. Brush with melted butter or cream
10. Bake slowly in moderate heat oven
11. Serve warm
12. Alternatively, can be baked on a butter-brushed tart pan to form a large cake-shaped pastry
