## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter Champagne wine
- 8 teaspoons sugar
- 4 teaspoons beaten butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- finely crushed sugar for the pan
- baked biscuit

## Directions

1. In a glazed pot, add 5 egg yolks, which are whisked on the hot plate with 1/2 liter Champagne wine mixed with 8 teaspoons of sugar until it begins to thicken and rises in height, then set aside and whisk until completely cooled
2. To this, beat 4 teaspoons of beaten butter with 6 egg yolks and finally lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
3. A desired butter-greased, finely crushed sugar-dusted form is lined with baked biscuit, half of the chaudeau is poured in, which is again covered with biscuit and the second half of the chaudeau is poured over
4. Let it cook covered for 1/2 hour in a water bath, after which time the soufflé is turned out onto a plate and baked in the oven for a few minutes; dust it with finely crushed sugar and serve immediately
5. This soufflé must be made very carefully
