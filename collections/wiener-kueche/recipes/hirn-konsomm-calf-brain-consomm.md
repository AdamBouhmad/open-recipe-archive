## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- cold water
- salt water
- a second calf brain
- approximately 8 deca butter
- parsley
- 3 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- some salt
- 1 knife point white pepper
- 1/2 liter white beef soup

## Directions

1. Place 1 calf brain in cold water and remove the skin carefully
2. Place it again in cold water, then in boiling salted water
3. Cook until the brain is properly contracted
4. Place on a sieve and cover with cloth
5. Take a second skinned brain and roast parsley in approximately 8 deca butter and braise both slightly
6. Mix this with 3 whole eggs, 4 egg yolks, some salt, 1 knife point white pepper, and 1/2 liter white beef soup, stirring well together
7. Pour this mixture into a form greased with butter and dusted with flour
8. Cut the brain cooked in salt water into cubes
9. Distribute the cubes in the form under the liquid mixture
10. Cook the whole thing in a water bath for 1 hour
