## Ingredients

- 50-70 dkg black scherzl (beef)
- 1 egg
- 1 spoon flour
- 1 pinch salt

## Directions

1. In a small pot, stir together 1 egg, 1 spoon flour, and 1 pinch salt very smoothly for 15 minutes.
2. Let this mixture drip from high above into the strained boiling beef broth and cook for 5 minutes.
