## Ingredients

- 1 completely prepared herring
- Mashhausen apples
- A small apple corer
- Herring milk
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring according to recipe 974 and cut into desired pieces
2. Arrange the pieces round-shaped in a serving bowl
3. To remove the core housing of a Mashhausen apple, press a small corer into the side of the stem, turn it slightly and pull it out with the core housing
4. Do not pierce through the apple completely so that a bottom remains
5. Hollow out the apple further until it is only half a finger thick in the opening, then peel it
6. Mix the strained herring milk with a few drops of oil, vinegar and some pepper
7. Fill the hollowed-out apple top with the herring milk mixture
8. Place it in the center of the arranged herring
