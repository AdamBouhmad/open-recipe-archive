## Ingredients

- Muscatel apples
- sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- butter
- finely pounded sugar
- baked biscuit
- dissolved marmalade

## Directions

1. From Muscatel apples the core is removed, the tips are cut, then cut in half, put into boiling sugar water into which the juice of 1 lemon has been dripped, covered and stewed until soft, then removed with a spoon and placed on a sieve. Then a short saucepan brushed with butter and dusted with finely pounded sugar is lined with baked biscuit, covering the bottom with a suitably sized slice and the side wall of the saucepan with 2 finger wide and 2 finger long strips, of which the shiny side comes outward. The bottom is now lined with the soft-stewed apples, covered with baked biscuit which is brushed with melted marmalade, then covered with apples and so continues until the saucepan is full. It is now placed in the oven and the apple charlotte is baked at low heat for 3/4 hour.
