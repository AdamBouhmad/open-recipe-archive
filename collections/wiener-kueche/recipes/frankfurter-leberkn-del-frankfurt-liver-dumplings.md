## Ingredients

- 1 calf's liver
- onions
- parsley
- marjoram
- 1 pound bread in 1 measure milk
- nutmeg
- salt
- 0.5 pounds bacon, diced and roasted
- 5 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- fine flour

## Directions

1. Clean the calf's liver and scrape out all the veins
2. Chop finely with some onions, parsley, and marjoram
3. Soak 1 pound bread in 1 measure milk
4. Combine the chopped liver with the soaked bread, nutmeg, salt, and roasted diced bacon
5. Stir in 5 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks with enough fine flour to make the mixture stiff enough to form dumplings
6. Cook the dumplings in salted water until cooked through
7. Better if cooked in bouillon instead
