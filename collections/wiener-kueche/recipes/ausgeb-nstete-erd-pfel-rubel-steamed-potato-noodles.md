## Ingredients

- potato noodles prepared as for Abgeschmalzene Erdäpfel-Rubel (No. 1209)
- 1 liter boiling milk
- 3/4 melted butter
- finely sifted sugar
- cinnamon (optional)
- 4 tablespoons maraschino (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare potato noodles and place them in 1 liter boiling milk instead of salt water
2. Cover and let cook until the milk is absorbed sufficiently
3. Place the noodles in a casserole with melted butter and steam in the oven until dry
4. Arrange heaped on a platter with a spoon, sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and optionally cinnamon, and serve
5. Before serving, optionally pour 4 tablespoons maraschino over them
