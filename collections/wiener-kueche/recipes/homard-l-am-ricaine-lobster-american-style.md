## Ingredients

- 6 deca fine oil or 6 deca butter
- 1 onion head
- 3 small carrots
- 1 liter white wine
- 1/2 liter bouillon
- Cayenne pepper
- Salt and pepper
- 1 living, cleaned lobster or langoustine
- 3 spoonfuls tomato purée
- 2 spoonfuls cognac
- 3 spoonfuls Madeira wine
- 1 spoonful (optional) meat glaze

## Directions

1. Put oil or butter in a shallow pot with finely cut onion head and carrots, let roast pale yellow
2. Pour with 1 liter white wine and 1/2 liter bouillon
3. Add a knife point of cayenne pepper and let reduce by half
4. Meanwhile, cut a living, cleaned lobster or langoustine into finger-wide pieces
5. Sprinkle with necessary salt and pepper
6. Half an hour before serving, add the cut lobster to the pot with the braised ingredients
7. Let it braise covered until the shell turns red
8. Add 3 spoonfuls tomato purée, 2 spoonfuls cognac, 3 spoonfuls Madeira wine and if desired 1 spoonful meat glaze
9. Let everything come to a boil once more covered
10. Arrange the lobster pieces on a deep dish and pour the sauce over them
11. Alternatively, remove the shells and pass the sauce through a sieve
