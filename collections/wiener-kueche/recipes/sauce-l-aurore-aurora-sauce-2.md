## Ingredients

- White coulis
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- 4 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Place some white coulis in a casserole.
2. Add the juice of 1 lemon, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Let it cook and skim well.
4. Strain the yolks from 4 hard-boiled, peeled eggs through a wire sieve so they look like fine noodles.
5. Add to the sauce when serving.
6. These egg yolks must not be stirred further or allowed to cook again.
