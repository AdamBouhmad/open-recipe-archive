## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls
- 1.5 ounces milk
- 8 decagrams caramelized sugar
- 10 decagrams whipped butter
- 16 decagrams finely sifted sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the mold
- finely sifted sugar for the mold

## Directions

1. Soften 16 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls in 1.5 ounces boiling milk
2. Transfer to a saucepan containing 8 decagrams caramelized sugar, mix well
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Beat 10 decagrams whipped butter gradually with 16 decagrams finely sifted sugar and 6 egg yolks
5. Add the passed bread rolls, mix well
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
7. Butter a mold, dust with finely sifted sugar, fill with this mixture
8. Bake covered in steam for 1.5 hours
