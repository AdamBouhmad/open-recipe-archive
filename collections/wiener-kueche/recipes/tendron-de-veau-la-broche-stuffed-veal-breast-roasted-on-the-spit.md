## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- 6 Deca beaten butter
- 2 whole eggs
- ½ teaspoon finely cut green parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 8 Deca bread, rind removed, softened in milk, well pressed out
- paper brushed with butter
- twine

## Directions

1. From the lower broad side up toward the neck, open the meat with a knife from the ribs, insert the hand, and separate it as widely as possible, being careful not to create another opening
2. Beat the butter and stir in 2 whole eggs, ½ teaspoon finely cut green parsley, salt, and season with nutmeg
3. Add 8 Deca softened and well-pressed bread and stir well together
4. Fill the veal breast with this mixture by spreading it between flesh and ribs and distributing it evenly, closing the opening while pressing the hand on the upper skin and pushing the filling in
5. Sew the opening shut with a trussing needle using twine
6. Press the breast flat, salt it on both sides, bind it with 2 paper towels brushed with butter, and roast it on the spit for approximately 1 hour over strong fire
7. After this time, remove the paper and continue roasting until it has a brown color
8. Remove the breast from the spit, cut it into pieces of desired size, and serve it with the defatted drippings and any desired salad or vegetables
