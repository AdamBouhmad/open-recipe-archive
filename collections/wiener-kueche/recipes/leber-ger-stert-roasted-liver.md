## Ingredients

- 50 dkg calf's liver
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion
- salt
- pepper
- water or broth
- vinegar
- fried potato pancakes or rice

## Directions

1. In hot fat, lightly sauté a whole onion and push aside.
2. Add the cleaned, washed liver and roast on both sides over high heat for 10-15 minutes.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Pour in a little water or broth with a few drops of vinegar.
5. Serve immediately with fried potato pancakes or rice.
