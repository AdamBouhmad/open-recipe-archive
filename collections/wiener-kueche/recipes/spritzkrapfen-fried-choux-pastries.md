## Ingredients

- choux pastry made from: 1/2 l milk, 5 dkg butter, salt, 15 dkg flour, 2-3 small eggs
- hot lard for frying
- sugar
- Chaudeau or raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Prepare choux pastry from milk, butter, salt, flour and eggs (see Teige section).
2. The dough must be quite stiff.
3. Fill the pastry bag with the dough and pipe various shapes like sticks, rings, and spirals into hot lard.
4. Fry until light brown.
5. Drain and sprinkle with sugar.
6. Make a test puff first - if the dough is too loose, add more flour and stir well.
7. Serve warm with Chaudeau or raspberry juice.
