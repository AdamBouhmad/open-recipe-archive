## Ingredients

- three small meaty chickens
- lard
- butter
- flour
- brown broth
- half pomerance
- half lemon
- sugar
- three or four cloves
- whole sweet pomerance

## Directions

1. Clean, dress, and lard the chickens with finely cut lard
2. Roast them, but not too much, just until they get a nice color
3. In a casserole or pan, make a nice golden-brown roux with butter and flour
4. Fill with brown broth
5. Add the peel and juice from half a pomerance and half a lemon
6. Add a piece of sugar and three or four cloves
7. After the sauce has simmered a while, strain it through a sieve
8. Place the chickens in it and let them boil a bit longer
9. Before serving, take a whole sweet pomerance, remove the peel, cut into twelve pieces
10. Garnish the chickens with the peel pieces and pour the sauce (after carefully removing the fat) over
