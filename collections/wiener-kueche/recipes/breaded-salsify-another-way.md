## Ingredients

- Softly cooked salsify roots
- Cloth
- Wine vinegar
- Hot lard
- Flour
- Bread crumbs
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Dry the softly cooked roots with a cloth, dip them in wine vinegar and bake them in hot lard, or alternatively, dry the softly cooked roots with a cloth, dip them in flour, then in beaten eggs, coat with bread crumbs, and bake them in hot lard shortly before serving.
