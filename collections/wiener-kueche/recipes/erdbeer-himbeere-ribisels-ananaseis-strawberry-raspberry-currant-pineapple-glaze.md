## Ingredients

- finely sifted, unscented sugar
- about 6 tablespoons of strained juice from fresh strawberries, raspberries, currants or pineapples

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as the Orange Glaze (No. 1560), only that finely sifted, unscented sugar is used instead of sugar and about 6 tablespoons of strained juice of fresh strawberries, raspberries, currants or pineapples is used instead of orange juice.
