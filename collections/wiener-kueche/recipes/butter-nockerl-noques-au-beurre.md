## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter
- 6 tablespoons cold uncooked milk
- 1 tablespoon milk after each tablespoon
- 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
- approximately 1.5 liters flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until creamy
2. Stir in 6 tablespoons cold milk, then after each tablespoon add 1 tablespoon milk
3. Gradually add 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
4. Bind with approximately 1.5 liters flour
5. Using a spoon that is dipped in boiling water each time, drop pieces of dough into boiling soup or salted water
6. Alternatively, the egg whites can be beaten to stiff peaks and folded in
