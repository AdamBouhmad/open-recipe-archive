## Ingredients

- Stockfish
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Toasted breadcrumbs
- Brown onions

## Directions

1. Soak the stockfish thoroughly in cold water
2. Place on heat and cook until soft as an egg
3. Remove the skin and lay on a napkin
4. Arrange on a platter
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Pour over clear butter and toasted breadcrumbs as well as brown onions
7. Keep warm until serving
8. Alternatively, after boiling and salting, it can be breaded in eggs, flour and breadcrumbs and fried, being only lightly golden and not heavily fried
