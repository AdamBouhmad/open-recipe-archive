## Ingredients

- 4-5 bread rolls (Schneidſemmeln)
- 1 pork or calf brain (Schweins- oder Kalbshirn)
- 1/4 liter milk

## Directions

1. Cut the bread rolls into slices, keeping each pair connected together
2. Spread the inner side with roasted brain (see Evening Dishes: Brain with Egg)
3. Dip the slices in salted milk
4. Bread them (paniér)
5. Fry them in hot lard until golden
