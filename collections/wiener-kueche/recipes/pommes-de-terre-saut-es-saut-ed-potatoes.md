## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- butter
- salt
- parsley
- breadcrumbs (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes and cut them into thin slices
2. Salt them and place on a hot flat pan in very hot butter
3. When one side is brown, sprinkle with finely chopped fresh parsley, turn them over and let the other side brown
4. Alternatively, before roasting, dust with breadcrumbs, roast with them, and call them 'turned potatoes'
