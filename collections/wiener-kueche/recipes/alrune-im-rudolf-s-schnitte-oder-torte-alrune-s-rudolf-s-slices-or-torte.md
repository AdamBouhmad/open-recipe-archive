## Ingredients

- 32 Deca butter
- 16 egg yolks
- 32 Deca blanched almonds, very finely chopped with 2 egg whites
- 8 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 12 Deca flour
- marmalade as needed
- orange glaze
- candied cherries
- angelica, finely chopped
- green almonds
- black currants

## Directions

1. Beat 32 Deca butter and 16 egg yolks for 1 hour
2. Add 32 Deca blanched almonds very finely chopped with 2 egg whites, mix well
3. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites from 8 eggs with 12 Deca flour at the same time
4. For a torte: place in a torte mold 2 finger widths high and bake carefully in the oven for 3/4 hour
5. For slices: spread the mixture knife-back width thick and bake until light yellow
6. Spread half immediately with marmalade, place the other half on top, press slightly, cover with orange glaze knife-back width thick and immediately cut out half-moon shapes with a round pastry cutter
7. Place a candied cherry on the center of each slice and decorate with finely chopped angelica, green almonds, black currants or as desired
8. The slices can also be glazed with any desired color so they appear shaded
