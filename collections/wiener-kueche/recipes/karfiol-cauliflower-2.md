## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower
- salted water
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and divide the cauliflower into florets.
2. Place in cold salted water and cook for 20-30 minutes.
3. Drain and arrange.
4. Pour over with bread crumbs roasted in butter.
