## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 liter white beef stock
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- 1 cup grated horseradish

## Directions

1. A butter sauce of 8 tablespoons butter and the same amount of flour is poured with 3/4 liter white beef stock
2. Add some nutmeg and salt and let boil
3. Add 1 cup grated horseradish
4. The horseradish must not boil
