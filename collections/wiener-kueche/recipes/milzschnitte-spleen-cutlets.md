## Ingredients

- 3 dag butter
- ¾ egg
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped parsley
- 5 dag beef spleen, washed and scraped
- 2 slices of bread (Schneidssemmeln)

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and beat in the egg, salt, pepper, parsley, and the washed, scraped, and passed spleen, and a few breadcrumbs if needed
2. Cut 2 slices of bread first in half, then into leaf-like strips
3. Spread the spleen mixture on one side, then fry in plenty of hot lard on both sides
4. Drain and serve warm
