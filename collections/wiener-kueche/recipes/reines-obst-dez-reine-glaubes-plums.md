## Ingredients

- beautiful Reine-Claudes plums that are ripe but not soft
- stems
- 80 decilitres sugar
- 1 litre water

## Directions

1. After weighing beautiful Reine-Claudes that are ripe but not soft, cut the stems short
2. Pierce each piece five times with a skewer snappily at the stone, the needle must go through to the opposite side
3. Place immediately in jars
4. Treat further as the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842), only using 80 decilitres sugar in 1 litre water instead of 60
