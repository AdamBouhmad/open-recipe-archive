## Ingredients

- 0.5-1 liter washed rice, roasted barley, oat gruel, or sago
- 0.5 liter water or unsalted broth
- Salt
- Red wine or lemon juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 0.5-1 liter of washed rice, roasted barley, oat gruel, or sago in 0.5 liter more water or unsalted broth.
2. If made with broth, add some salt after straining.
3. If made with water, mix it with some red wine or lemon juice and sugar.
