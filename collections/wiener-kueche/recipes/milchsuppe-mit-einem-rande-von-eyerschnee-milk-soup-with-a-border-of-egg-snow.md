## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- a pair of spoonfuls of fine flour
- crushed sugar
- cinnamon water
- hot sweet milk
- thin small sugar rolls

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites on a bowl with a wide rim to snow
2. Place this on the rim and let the foam dry facing the heat
3. Rub sugar on the outer side and hold a glowing shovel over it so the whole rim becomes brown
4. Beat several egg yolks with a pair of spoonfuls of fine flour, some crushed sugar and cinnamon water
5. Gradually pour in hot sweet milk while beating it to foam
6. Finally add very thin and small sugar rolls to the plated soup
