## Ingredients

- kidney beans
- 8 ounces butter
- 1 teaspoon green parsley
- 1 lemon
- salt
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the kidney beans as for salad
2. Place the cooked kidney beans in a casserole dish
3. Mash 8 ounces of butter with 1 teaspoon of green parsley and the juice of 1 lemon
4. Mix with the kidney beans, some salt, and a pinch of ground pepper
5. Let stand covered on the hot plate for a few minutes
6. Stir well together and when the beans are hot throughout, serve
