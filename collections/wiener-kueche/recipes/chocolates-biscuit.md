## Ingredients

- 12 ounces butter
- 15 ounces sugar
- 4 tablets chocolate
- 8 ounces blanched and ground almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 6 ounces flour
- blanched finely sliced almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Place 12 ounces butter, 15 ounces sugar, and 4 tablets chocolate in a pan and stir over heat until soft and smooth
2. Add 8 ounces blanched and ground almonds and mix well
3. Empty this mass into a deep bowl and stir until cold
4. Gradually add 4 egg yolks and gently fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 4 egg whites with 6 ounces flour
5. Spread this mass finger-thick on a buttered sheet and brush with beaten eggs
6. Sprinkle with blanched, finely sliced almonds and bake in moderate heat for approximately 3/4 hour
7. Immediately afterwards, pipe decoratively into desired forms or cut into 2-finger wide and finger-long pieces and serve cold
