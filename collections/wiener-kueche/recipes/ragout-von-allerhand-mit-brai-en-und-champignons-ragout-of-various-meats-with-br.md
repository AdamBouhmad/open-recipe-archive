## Ingredients

- Palate
- Cow udder
- Calf ears
- Pork ears
- Feet (of various meats)
- Calf braises
- Salt water
- Mushrooms
- Crayfish claws and tails
- Oysters
- Flour
- Beef broth
- Nutmeg
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take palate, cow udder, calf ears, pork ears, and feet of each as much as needed, and simmer them with a pair of calf braises in salt water until tender.
2. The braises are larded lightly, the other parts are cleaned well; then cut them lengthwise, but not too small.
3. Cut some mushrooms into leaves.
4. You may also add crayfish claws and tails, as well as oysters.
5. Make the flour brown in a casserole, let the broth run light brown into it, then pour in beef broth, salt it, and add the finely cut pieces, season with nutmeg and lemon juice, and let it come to a boil once.
6. When serving, place the braises on top.
