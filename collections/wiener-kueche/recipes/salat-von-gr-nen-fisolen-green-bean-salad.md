## Ingredients

- Young green beans
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh green parsley, finely chopped
- Oil

## Directions

1. Trim the ends of the green beans and cut them into thin sticks
2. Cook in salt water
3. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to cold water
4. Drain well on a sieve
5. Place in a salad bowl with vinegar, some salt, a pinch of pepper, finely chopped green parsley and oil
6. Mix everything well together
7. Serve with various meat dishes
8. The beans can be broken into pieces or left whole, and parsley can be added or omitted as desired
