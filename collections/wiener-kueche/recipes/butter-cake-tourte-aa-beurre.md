## Ingredients

- 28 deca butter
- 28 deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- 28 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 28 deca butter until fluffy, mix in 28 deca vanilla sugar
2. Gradually beat in 8 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
3. Lightly mix in the snow of 8 egg whites with 28 deca flour
4. Divide this mass into 2 parts, fill into buttered and floured cake forms and bake at low heat in the oven
5. Remove the hoops from the forms, place the cakes on a board or sieve, and after complete cooling, spread with dissolved jam
6. Stack them so that the sides that were on the baking sheet during baking have one side down and one up
7. Place this cake on a sheet of writing paper, pour hot jam over it, and after it is completely dry, cover with white glaze
