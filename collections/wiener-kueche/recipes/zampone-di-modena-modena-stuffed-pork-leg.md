## Ingredients

- Skin from the foreleg or front foot of a pig
- Meat and cartilage from the bones
- Finely chopped tender meat
- Kidney fat
- Salt and spices to taste

## Directions

1. Draw the skin from the foreleg or front foot of the pig without tearing or otherwise damaging it
2. Loosen the meat and cartilage from the bones
3. Coarsely chop the cartilaginous parts and mix with finely chopped tender meat and some kidney fat
4. Season and salt sufficiently according to taste
5. Stuff the mixture into the inside fatty skin of the foreleg, but not too tightly
6. Bind the skin at both ends as strongly as possible
7. Place it in salt for as long as the skin requires to become durable
8. Hang it in smoke, and later until use in a dry place
9. Before cooking, soak and rinse it
10. Simmer it for only about half as long as a ham
11. Serve warm or cooled, as an appetizer or as a side to vegetables
