## Ingredients

- 1 kilo ripe, soft rose hips
- 80 grams sugar
- 3/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Take approximately 1 kilo quite ripe, soft rose hips, slit them lengthwise, remove the stars and the hairy material and put them in a warm place to become softer
2. After some time put them in a saucepan with 80 grams sugar and 3/4 liter water, place on the stove and let cook under frequent stirring
3. When the rose hips have been passed through a sieve, let them come to boil again and fill them, still hot, into jars
4. Then put them in a breezy place and after 5 days bind with writing paper, which is perforated with a needle
