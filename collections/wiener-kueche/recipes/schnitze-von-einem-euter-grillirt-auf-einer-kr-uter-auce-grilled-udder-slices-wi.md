## Ingredients

- one whole udder
- salt
- melted butter
- bread crumbs
- herb sauce

## Directions

1. Cook one whole udder until very soft
2. Slice it into pieces the thickness of a knife back
3. Sprinkle the slices with salt
4. Turn each slice in melted butter and bread crumbs
5. Grill or fry them on both sides on the grate
6. Meanwhile prepare a herb sauce, arrange it on the platter, place the udder on top, and serve
