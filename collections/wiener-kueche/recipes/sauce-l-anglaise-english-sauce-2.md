## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound ham
- Onions
- Roots
- Herbs
- Several cloves
- Butter
- Flour
- 0.5 measure bouillon
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 8 chopped anchovies
- Estragon (tarragon)
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sweat 0.5 pound ham, onions, roots, herbs, and several cloves in butter.
2. Stir in some flour.
3. Fill the casserole with 0.5 measure of bouillon.
4. Pound the yolks from 4 hard-boiled eggs and add them with 8 chopped anchovies to the sauce.
5. Cook together.
6. Strain through cheesecloth.
7. Stir in coarsely chopped boiled tarragon and lemon juice.
