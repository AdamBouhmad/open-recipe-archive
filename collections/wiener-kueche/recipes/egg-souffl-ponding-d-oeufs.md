## Ingredients

- 8 ounces flour
- 1/3 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1/2 liter milk
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites, beaten
- 8 ounces hot butter
- Clarified butter
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Place 8 ounces flour in a deep bowl with 1/3 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon white pepper.
2. Gradually pour in 1/2 liter milk while stirring constantly.
3. Add 12 egg yolks one after another while stirring.
4. Gently fold in the beaten snow from 12 egg whites.
5. Pour the mixture into an egg pan containing 8 ounces hot butter.
6. Bake in the oven at moderate heat for approximately 1/8 hour until the tops begin to dry.
7. When they begin to dry, brush with clarified butter. When they turn golden, remove from the oven and serve in the egg pan at table, or divide into pieces with a serving spoon, arrange in a bowl, and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
8. This dish must be prepared in time before serving, as it must be brought to table immediately after baking and cannot wait.
