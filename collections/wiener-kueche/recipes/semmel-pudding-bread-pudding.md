## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk or cream
- 16 deca finely sliced rolls
- 14 deca butter, beaten
- 14 deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 8 deca plump raisins
- 8 deca currants
- 8 deca finely diced citron
- 8 deca blanched, cut into sticks almonds

## Directions

1. Pour 1/4 liter milk or cream into a saucepan
2. Add 16 deca finely sliced rolls and stir over heat until completely softened and very thick
3. Set aside and let cool
4. Beat 14 deca butter with 14 deca vanilla sugar and 8 egg yolks gradually until well mixed
5. Add the cooled rolls, mix well
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites together with 8 deca plump raisins, 8 deca currants, 8 deca finely diced citron, and 8 deca blanched almonds cut into sticks
7. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
8. Steam covered for 1/2 hour
9. Serve with desired cream
