## Ingredients

- 1 heaped bowl of peeled, diced cucumbers
- 8 decagrams fat
- 6 decagrams flour
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- vinegar
- cold cream
- dill

## Directions

1. A quarter hour before serving, salt the peeled, diced cucumbers and let them stand.
2. Meanwhile make a roux from 8 decagrams fat and 6 decagrams flour.
3. Add the cucumbers and let them roast somewhat.
4. Pour with 1 1/2 liters broth and a little vinegar, stir well and let boil.
5. When serving, arrange on a platter, pour 1 tablespoon cold cream over and dust with finely cut dill.
