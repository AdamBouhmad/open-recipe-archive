## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls
- 0.25 liter boiling milk
- 12 decagrams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 14 decagrams granulated sugar
- 8 egg whites
- 8 decagrams finely pounded almonds
- 18 decagrams finely minced dates
- some dissolved almond kernels
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Soften 12 decagrams trimmed, finely sliced bread rolls in 0.25 liter boiling milk
2. Stir over heat until well softened
3. Mix in 12 decagrams butter and set aside
4. Beat 8 egg yolks with 14 decagrams granulated sugar for 1/2 hour
5. Add the bread roll mixture and beat well
6. Lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites simultaneously with 8 decagrams finely pounded almonds, 18 decagrams finely minced dates (pits removed), and some dissolved almond kernels
7. Fill a buttered mold dusted with flour
8. Bake covered in steam for 0.75 to 1 hour
