## Ingredients

- 20 tea cups (about 4 tablets) chocolate
- 1.5 liters milk

## Directions

1. Place 20 tea cups chocolate in one saucepan and 1.5 liters milk in a second saucepan, place both on the stove
2. When the chocolate has become soft, pour it with some boiling milk and stir until it is completely smooth and shiny
3. Gradually add the remaining boiling milk while stirring constantly, let the chocolate boil well, bubble up and boil once more, then serve
4. For water chocolate: prepare the same way, using water instead of milk
5. For egg butter chocolate: for 1/2 liter use about 3 egg butters, which you sprinkle with some cold milk and then strain into the chocolate
6. If desired, add 8 tea cups sugar at the beginning and serve with powdered sugar and whipped cream
