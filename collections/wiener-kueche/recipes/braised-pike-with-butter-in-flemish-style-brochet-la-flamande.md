## Ingredients

- 1 pike (approximately 1 kilo)
- salt
- pepper
- 5 bunches parsley
- parsley
- chervil herb
- chervil
- 2 pieces mushroom
- 32 deca butter
- potatoes in salt water

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, open it, wash it, cut it into 2-finger-wide pieces, and place in a casserole
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Cut very finely: 5 bunches parsley, equal amount chervil herb and chervil, and 2 pieces mushroom
4. Let roast in 32 deca butter and pour over the fish pieces
5. Let braise covered for approximately 1/4 hour, then arrange nicely on a platter and pour the juices over
6. Serve with potatoes cooked in salt water
