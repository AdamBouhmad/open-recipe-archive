## Ingredients

- butter
- mushrooms, coarsely chopped
- salt
- chopped fresh parsley
- pounded pepper
- a handful of fine bread crumbs
- several spoonfuls of cream
- ten to twelve eggs
- butter for the omelet pan

## Directions

1. Let a piece of butter become warm, add coarsely chopped mushrooms, salt them, and add some chopped fresh parsley and pounded pepper.
2. Let everything stew until the sauce has reduced.
3. Add a handful of fine bread crumbs and several spoonfuls of cream, and let it stew again.
4. Beat ten to twelve eggs in a pot, salt and stir them.
5. In an omelet pan, make a little butter hot, add a small portion of the eggs, and let them spread out in the pan to form the shape of an omelet.
6. Turn it to the other side, turn it out quickly, and continue until all are cooked.
7. Fill them with the mushroom mixture, roll them together, butter a dish, place several spoonfuls of cream on it, lay the rolls in, and they are ready after a brief boil.
