## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- salt water
- mayonnaise
- aspic
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a pike as you would boiled pike and let it cool in the stock.
2. Remove it, pull off the skin, remove the bones, break the pike into pieces as desired.
3. Arrange the pieces on a platter, pour mayonnaise over them, and garnish the platter beautifully with aspic and hard-boiled eggs.
