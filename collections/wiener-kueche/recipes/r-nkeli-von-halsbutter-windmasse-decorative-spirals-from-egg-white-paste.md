## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 20 Deca vanilla sugar
- finely ground sugar mixed with finely cut almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the stiffly beaten egg whites thoroughly with 20 Deca vanilla sugar.
2. Fill this mixture into a pastry bag and pipe wreaths, pretzels, puffed shapes, or desired figures onto a baking sheet coated with wax or unsalted bacon fat.
3. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar mixed with finely cut almonds and dry in a moderately warm oven.
