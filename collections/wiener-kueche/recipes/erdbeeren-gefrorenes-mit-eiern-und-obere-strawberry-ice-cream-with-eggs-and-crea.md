## Ingredients

- 20 deca sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter good, unboiled cream

## Directions

1. Stir 20 deca sugar with 8 egg yolks in a saucepan well
2. Gradually add 1 liter good, unboiled cream and heat over the fire, beating until it begins to thicken, without boiling
3. After the mixture has been passed through a fine hair sieve, let it cool while continuously beating with the snow whisk and treat it further as described in Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720)
4. When it is frozen solid and worked well, mix 1/2 liter sieved strawberries with it
