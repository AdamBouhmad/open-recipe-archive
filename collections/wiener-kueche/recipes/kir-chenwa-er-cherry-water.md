## Ingredients

- ripe cherries
- a few cloves
- cinnamon
- 2 measures of water per 1 measure of cherry juice

## Directions

1. Remove the cherries from their stems
2. Pound them with the pits, a few cloves, and cinnamon in a mortar
3. Let them stand for several hours in an earthenware vessel
4. Press them through linen cloth
5. To 1 measure of juice, add 2 measures of water
6. Sweeten to taste
7. Pass it through a napkin
