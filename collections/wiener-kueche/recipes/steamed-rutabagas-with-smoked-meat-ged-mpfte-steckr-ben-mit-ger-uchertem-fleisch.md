## Ingredients

- Rutabagas
- Melted beef fat or butter
- Anis
- Sugar
- Butter
- Meat broth
- Flour
- Smoked meat

## Directions

1. Place the scraped and lengthwise-cut rutabagas in a pan with melted beef fat or butter, and roast them until they turn brownish.
2. Then place a layer of them in an earthenware vessel, sprinkle anis and sugar over it, lay butter pieces over it, then rutabagas again, and so on until the rutabagas are arranged alternately in the vessel.
3. Pour meat broth over it, let the rutabagas simmer slowly, and finally sprinkle a little flour over it to thicken the broth.
4. Then arrange and garnish it with pieces of smoked meat.
