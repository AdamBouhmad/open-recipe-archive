## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- salt
- pepper
- veal forcemeat, approximately ½ kilo
- pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of boiled ham
- cleaned sardelles
- cooked tongue
- truffles, finely sliced, alternatively
- butter for roasting
- warm butter for basting

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the breast by carefully pushing back the meat, turning the bone, and pulling it out
2. Carefully remove the cartilage from the breast
3. Beat the breast with the flat side of a meat cleaver
4. Salt and pepper it
5. Brush it finger-thick with veal forcemeat (approximately ½ kilo)
6. Lay pencil-thick, finger-long pieces of boiled ham, cleaned sardelles, and cooked tongue on this, or use finely sliced truffles instead of the sardelles
7. Roll it together: turn the thick part inward and roll the meat toward the thin part
8. Bind it tightly with twine and place it in a roasting pan so that the end where the meat closes lies on top
9. Cover the meat with warm butter and roast it for 1½ hours, basting frequently with the drippings that form
10. Remove the meat, loosen the twine, cut it into pieces of desired size, arrange these on a serving dish, and pour the defatted drippings alongside
