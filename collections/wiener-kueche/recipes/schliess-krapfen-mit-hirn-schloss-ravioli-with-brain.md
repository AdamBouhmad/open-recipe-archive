## Ingredients

- 1 skinned calf brain
- 4 deca butter
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- 4 deca trimmed, finely chopped, in milk soaked and well wrung out bread rolls
- 1 whole egg
- 1 pinch of nutmeg
- same amount of white pepper
- dough and other ingredients as in Schliess Krapfen recipe

## Directions

1. Roast the skinned calf brain in 4 deca butter with 1 teaspoon of finely chopped green parsley
2. Add to a mixing bowl 4 deca trimmed bread rolls that have been finely chopped, soaked in milk, and well wrung out, then add 1 whole egg, 1 pinch of nutmeg, and the same amount of white pepper
3. Mix everything well together
4. Continue as with the Schliess Krapfen
