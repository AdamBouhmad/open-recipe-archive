## Ingredients

- 1 piece onion
- crushed garlic if desired
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- vinegar as needed
- fried cold carp pieces
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut 1 piece onion very finely and add some finely crushed garlic if desired
2. Mix this with 1 tablespoon vinegar and pour with the necessary vinegar over fried cold carp pieces
3. Let it stand for one hour
4. About 5 minutes before serving, pour oil and some more vinegar over it
