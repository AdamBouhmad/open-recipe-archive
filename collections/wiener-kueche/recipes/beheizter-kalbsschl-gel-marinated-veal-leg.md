## Ingredients

- veal leg
- ground spices
- pencil-thick, half-finger-long bacon
- vinegar
- water
- wine
- thinly sliced vegetables (carrots, celery)
- 3 bay leaves
- cabbage
- lemon peel
- salt
- cream
- aspic

## Directions

1. After trimming the leg and removing the skin, pound it with a meat mallet, rub it with ground spices, lard it with pencil-thick, half-finger-long bacon and let it rest 1 hour.
2. Meanwhile make a brine of vinegar, water and some wine, add thinly sliced vegetables, 3 bay leaves, cabbage and lemon peel and let it cook well.
3. Pour this prepared brine over the unsalted leg and let it marinate 2–3 days, during which time drain the brine twice a day, bring it to a boil and pour it cold again over the leg, which should be turned daily.
4. Before pouring over it the last time, salt both the brine and the leg and let it stand another night.
5. After this time, place it on the spit and roast it, frequently basting with a brine mixed with cream.
6. To some brine which is standing in a casserole at the stove, add more ingredients and serve with the meat.
