## Ingredients

- Sour cherries
- Three quarter pound sugar per pound of reduced juice

## Directions

1. Pound a quantity of sour cherries and press the juice through a clean napkin
2. Cook it for one hour
3. For every pound of reduced juice take three quarter pound sugar and let both cook until it becomes thick
4. To ensure the juice is reduced enough, pour a few drops on a plate; if it jells immediately, it is done
5. When the juice is cooled, fill it into preserve jars, tie with pig bladders, and keep in a dry place
