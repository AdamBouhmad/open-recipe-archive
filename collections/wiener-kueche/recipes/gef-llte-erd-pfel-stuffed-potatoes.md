## Ingredients

- large potatoes, not mealy
- butter
- eggs
- lemon peel
- nutmeg flower
- salt
- cream
- breadcrumbs
- optional: meat forcemeat on meat days

## Directions

1. Take large but not mealy potatoes, scrape them clean, rinse them, cut off a piece from the top, and hollow them out.
2. Beat together a piece of butter with a couple of eggs, lemon peel, nutmeg flower, salt, a little cream, and the scooped-out potato flesh.
3. Add as many breadcrumbs as needed to make the forcemeat somewhat thick.
4. Fill the hollowed potatoes with this mixture, place the cut-off pieces on top, put them with butter in a baking dish, cover the top with butter slices, braise them over heat, pour cream over them, and let them finish cooking.
5. On meat days, fill them with a forcemeat of choice.
