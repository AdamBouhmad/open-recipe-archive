## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 1 piece finely cut melon (preserved or fresh)
- 1/4 liter broth
- some wine or the juice in which the melon was preserved
- some vinegar
- some sugar

## Directions

1. Make 6 tablespoons butter hot, stir in equal amount flour, and add 1 piece finely cut melon and roast it somewhat
2. Gradually pour in 1/4 liter broth while stirring constantly, letting it boil with some wine or the juice in which the melon was preserved, or also a little vinegar, then with some sugar
3. Pass it and use it as a sauce for roasted meat
4. Small pieces of cut melon can be added to it
