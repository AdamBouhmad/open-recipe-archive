## Ingredients

- Roastbraten
- Salt
- Hot fat
- Onions, finely cut
- Broth

## Directions

1. Pound the roastbraten, salt them, and fry them quickly in hot fat.
2. Sprinkle finely cut onions over them and turn the meat as soon as it begins to turn yellow.
3. Once the roastbraten are fried on both sides, remove them and pour some broth over the fat in the pan, let it simmer a little, and pour this sauce over the roastbraten.
