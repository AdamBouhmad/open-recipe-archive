## Ingredients

- 8 deka worked butter
- 6 deka sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 16 deka flour
- beaten egg white for brushing
- crystal sugar mixed with anise

## Directions

1. Stir 8 deka worked butter gradually with 6 deka sugar, 3 egg yolks, and 16 deka flour well together, let this mass rest covered in a cold place for some time
2. After about half an hour, form arbitrarily large pretzels from this dough mass, place them on a butter-brushed baking sheet with the help of a knife
3. Brush with beaten egg white, sprinkle with crystal sugar mixed with anise, and bake at low heat in the oven until golden yellow
