## Ingredients

- Whole veal head with skin
- Veal brains
- Farce (fine ground raw meat)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch the whole veal head with its skin
2. Cook it in a braised preparation
3. Remove and lay out; cut the skin lengthwise and remove the brains
4. Prepare a well-seasoned farce and mix in the brains
5. Stuff the veal head and ears with this farce
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Coat in melted butter, beaten eggs, and fine bread crumbs
8. Bake in a hot oven until golden yellow
9. Serve with a piquant sauce
