## Ingredients

- Pig's ears
- Braise
- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Lard
- Piquant sauce
- Sauce Robert

## Directions

1. Clean the ears well and cook them soft in a braise.
2. When cold, turn them in butter.
3. Coat with bread crumbs and sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
4. Fry quickly in lard until they have a yellowish color.
5. Grill them as you would ox palates.
6. Serve with piquant sauce and Sauce Robert underneath.
