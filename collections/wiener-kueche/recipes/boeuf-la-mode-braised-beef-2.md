## Ingredients

- 1 piece beef chuck, approximately 4 kilos
- pepper
- bacon
- marrow
- cooked tongue
- sliced onion
- root vegetables
- 1/2 liter wine
- 3/4 liter vinegar
- water or broth
- black bread

## Directions

1. Pound and salt the beef chuck weighing about 4 kilos
2. Sprinkle with pepper
3. Using a small knife, make diagonal slits in the meat and insert finger-length pieces of bacon, marrow, and cooked tongue while drawing the knife out
4. Let it rest covered in a cool place until the next day
5. Add to the casserole: sliced onion, root vegetables, 1/2 liter wine, 3/4 liter vinegar, and enough water or broth so the liquid rises above the meat
6. Braise covered for 4-5 hours until tender
7. Add a piece of black bread and continue braising covered until done
8. Place the meat on a serving dish
9. Defatten the sauce and strain it over the roast
10. For a rosy appearance resembling corned beef, rub with saltpeter mixed with 1 teaspoon of sugar 48 hours in advance
11. Let rest covered and weighted
12. After this time, rinse it off, place in a casserole lined with bacon fat or marrow
13. Pour wine and rich broth over it so the liquid rises above the meat
14. Braise covered with bunches of savory but without root vegetables
