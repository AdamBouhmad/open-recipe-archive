## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg potatoes
- 5 dkg butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the potatoes
2. Place in cold salt water
3. Cook for 15-20 minutes
4. Strain
5. Place in a casserole with 5 dkg hot butter and chopped parsley
6. Toss together
