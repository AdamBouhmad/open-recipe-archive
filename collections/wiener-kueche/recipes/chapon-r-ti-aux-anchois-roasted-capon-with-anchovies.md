## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned capon
- salt
- cleaned, finely cut anchovies
- butter
- watercress
- lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Salt the cleaned capon 1 hour before roasting
2. Rub the capon with cleaned, finely cut anchovies
3. Truss it and roast it on a spit for 1 and 1/2 hours, basting frequently with butter
4. After this time, remove the capon, cut it into pieces of choice, arrange these in a platter
5. Pour the capon's own, defatted juices over it and garnish the meat with watercress and lemon wedges
6. Alternatively, the trussed capon can be placed in a pan containing butter and roasted in the oven with frequent basting with the resulting juices
7. The capon may be larded before roasting with finely cut bacon and anchovies if desired
