## Ingredients

- 3 measures good Rhine or Franconian wine
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Lemon peels
- 1/4 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Take 3 measures of good Rhine or Franconian wine, never Champagne, which is not suitable for this.
2. Put it in a soup pot with some cinnamon, cloves and lemon peels.
3. Sweeten it with 1/4 pound sugar.
4. Make it boiling hot on hot ashes and ignite it.
