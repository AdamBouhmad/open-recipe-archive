## Ingredients

- Beautiful dates
- 10 dkg sugar
- 1 egg white
- A few drops of rum or lemon juice
- Softened chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Cut dates open on one side
2. Remove the pits
3. Mix sugar with egg white, a few drops of rum or lemon juice, and softened chopped almonds
4. Form the mixture into shapes matching the pit shape
5. Fill the dates with this mixture
