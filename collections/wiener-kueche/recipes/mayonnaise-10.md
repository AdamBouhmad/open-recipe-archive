## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon water
- oil
- salt
- mustard

## Directions

1. In a mixing bowl over a hot stovetop, beat the 4 egg yolks with 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and 1 tablespoon water with a whisk until thick
2. Cool
3. Mix in oil, salt, and mustard
