## Ingredients

- 1 aged pheasant
- Hare farce (cold pâté farce)
- Truffles
- Farce

## Directions

1. Prepare as described in the 'Poularde Pâté' recipe (No. 1060), with the difference that hare farce is used instead of veal farce, and 1 aged pheasant, filled with truffles and farce as the poularde, is used instead.
