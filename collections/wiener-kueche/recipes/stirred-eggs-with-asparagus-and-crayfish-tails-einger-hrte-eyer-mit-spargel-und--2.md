## Ingredients

- asparagus boiled in salt water, cut into small pieces
- crayfish tails from 20 crayfish, halved

## Directions

1. Take a bundle of asparagus boiled in salt water and cut into small pieces
2. Remove the tails from 20 crayfish and halve each one
