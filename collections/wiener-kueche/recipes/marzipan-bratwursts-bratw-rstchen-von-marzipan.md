## Ingredients

- quarter pound chocolate
- 1 pound marzipan dough
- 1 loth finely ground cinnamon
- 6 to 8 powdered cloves or some vanilla
- coarsely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Set quarter pound chocolate on a baking sheet over charcoal fire to soften and melt
2. Throw it into an earthen vessel to dissolve it with a little water
3. Add 1 pound marzipan dough, 1 loth finely ground cinnamon, 6 to 8 powdered cloves or some vanilla
4. Color the mass red
5. Knead this mixture well through and form sausages from it
6. Scatter coarsely chopped almonds on the table where you roll them
7. Let the finished sausages lie on paper for several days to dry
8. Then bake at moderate heat
