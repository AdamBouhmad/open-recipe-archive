## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Vinegar solution
- Cold water
- Thread
- Salt water
- Juice of one lemon
- Butter sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. When the roots have been scrubbed as described above and placed whole in the vinegar solution, remove them, rinse them quickly in cold water, tie them together on both sides with thread (but not too tightly), and let them cook in salt water with the juice of a lemon.
2. When they are soft, remove them with a wooden spoon, place them on a platter, untie the threads, pour over them a butter sauce seasoned with some lemon juice, and serve without further additions.
