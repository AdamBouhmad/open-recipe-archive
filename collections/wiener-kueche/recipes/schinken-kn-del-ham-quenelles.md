## Ingredients

- 20 Loffel fresh butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- 32 Loffel very finely cut lean ham
- 1/2 liter roll crumbs

## Directions

1. Stir 20 Loffel fresh butter until fluffy
2. Gradually add 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
3. Add salt and nutmeg
4. Finally add 32 Loffel very finely cut lean ham and 1/2 liter roll crumbs and stir well together
5. Make a small knödel as a test
6. If it falls apart during cooking, add more crumbs
7. Shortly before serving, cook them 10 minutes slowly and covered in salted water and lift them out with a skimming spoon into the soup
