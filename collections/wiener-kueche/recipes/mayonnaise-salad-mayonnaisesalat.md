## Ingredients

- kipfel potatoes
- mayonnaise
- vinegar
- salt
- bird's lettuce or head lettuce

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes and place them in cold water.
2. Cook for 25 minutes, drain, cover with cloth and let sit for ½ hour.
3. Peel and cut into thin slices.
4. Mix with mayonnaise, some vinegar, and salt.
5. Serve cooled.
6. Garnish with bird's lettuce or finely cut head lettuce.
