## Ingredients

- 40 dkg flour
- a pinch of salt
- 2 spoonfuls sugar
- ½ kg cooked, passed, still warm potato
- 1 egg
- yeast leaven from 2 dkg yeast
- some spoonfuls milk
- 10 dkg butter, cut into pieces
- 5 dkg currants
- egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a mixing bowl, add a pinch of salt, 2 spoonfuls sugar, ½ kg cooked, passed, still warm potato, 1 egg, the yeast leaven from 2 dkg yeast, and some spoonfuls milk
2. Beat the dough until it releases from the spoon
3. On the board, knead it with 10 dkg cut butter until the butter is completely mixed in
4. Knead in 5 dkg currants
5. Divide the dough into 2 parts
6. Form each part into loaves and place on a greased baking sheet near the oven to rise
7. Brush with beaten egg yolk
8. Bake in a moderately hot oven until brown
9. The dough must be firm
