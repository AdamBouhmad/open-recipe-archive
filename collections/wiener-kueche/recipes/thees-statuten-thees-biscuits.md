## Ingredients

- 16 deka beaten butter
- 16 deka (umid) sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 teaspoon rum
- 16 deka flour
- 1 egg white, beaten
- fine sifted sugar
- finely chopped citron
- raisins
- blanched, finely cut almonds
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deka beaten butter with 16 deka sugar, 4 egg yolks, and 1 whole egg gradually until well combined
2. Stir for 1 hour, then mix in 1 teaspoon rum and 16 deka flour well together
3. Spread this mixture knife-blade-thick on a butter-greased baking sheet
4. Cover with beaten egg white from 1 egg
5. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar
6. Scatter finely chopped citron, raisins, blanched finely cut almonds, and pistachios over it
7. Bake in a hot oven, then cut diagonally into 2 finger-wide and finger-long pieces
8. Serve with tea
