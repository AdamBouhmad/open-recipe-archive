## Ingredients

- 3 anchovies
- nutmeg-sized butter
- soup
- 4 dkg fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Bone the anchovies
2. Knead together with nutmeg-sized butter on a board
3. Cook with some soup in a small pot for 5 minutes
4. Pass through a sieve
5. Add to a roux made from 4 dkg fat
6. Salt and cook for 15 minutes
