## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 small bread roll, sliced leaf-wise and cooked in milk
- 1.5 quarter-pounds almonds, softened and finely ground
- 6 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- zest of 1/2 lemon, finely cut
- 1/4 pound quinces, softened, pitted, cooked, and pressed through a sieve
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 1/4 pound butter until fluffy
2. Add the soft interior of 1 small bread roll sliced leaf-wise and cooked in milk
3. When cooled and well beaten, add 1.5 quarter-pounds softened and finely ground almonds
4. Add 6 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks, each well beaten
5. Add finely cut zest of 1/2 lemon, 1/4 pound quinces softened, pitted, cooked, and pressed through a sieve
6. Add sugar to taste and a little cinnamon
7. Stir everything for another quarter hour
8. Butter a metal dish and ring mold
9. Fill the mixture in and bake slowly in a cake pan
10. Sprinkle with sugar afterwards
