## Ingredients

- Beans, lentils, green peas (about 1/6 pound of each)
- 1 cooked red beet, diced
- 1 dark yellow beet, diced
- 4 pieces peeled asparagus tips, diced
- 4 pieces peeled pickled gherkins, diced
- 2 large cooked potatoes, diced
- White from 4 hard-boiled eggs, diced
- 4 pieces peeled capers
- 1 peeled herring, rinsed in milk
- Herring roe
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 1 eel and some truffles, optional
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks, pressed
- 2 raw egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons French mustard
- About 8 tablespoons oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Several tablespoons strong vinegar
- Salt
- 1 heaping pinch of white pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the beans, lentils and peas
2. Dice all the vegetables and fish as described and place in a deep bowl
3. Press 4 hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve
4. Add 2 raw egg yolks and 2 tablespoons French mustard
5. Gradually stir in about 8 tablespoons oil and the juice of 1 lemon
6. Add several tablespoons strong vinegar, some salt and 1 heaping pinch of white pepper
7. Mix everything well with the various vegetables
8. The salad should be thick and have a pleasant, sweet-sour taste
9. Arrange in a bowl and garnish carefully with various marinated fish varieties
10. This salad can also serve as a garnish for various cold dishes, namely fish, chicken with mayonnaise and aspic, and generally all meat dishes served with mayonnaise
