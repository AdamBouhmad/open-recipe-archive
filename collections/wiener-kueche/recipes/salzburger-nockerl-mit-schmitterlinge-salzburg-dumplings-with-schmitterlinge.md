## Ingredients

- Salzburg dumplings cooked in milk
- Schmitterlinge (No. 1345)

## Directions

1. When the Salzburg dumplings are finished cooking in milk, line the bottom of a platter with Schmitterlinge
2. Add a portion of the dumplings, layer with more Schmitterlinge, and continue alternating until all dumplings are used
3. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and serve
