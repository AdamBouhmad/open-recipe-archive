## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites, firmly beaten
- 24 decagrams powdered sugar
- 28 decagrams peeled almonds
- 1 egg white
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- grated chocolate (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the beaten egg whites and powdered sugar over the fire until the mass is hot
2. Add peeled almonds that have been very finely pounded with 1 egg white or pounded with the shell and passed through a sieve
3. Continue stirring over the fire until hot and thick throughout
4. Remove from fire and stir in a pinch of nutmeg and cinnamon until cool
5. Pipe the mass into desired figures on a baking sheet coated with wax using a piping bag or paper cone
6. Bake at low heat in the oven
7. If desired, add 1 tablet of grated chocolate to the mass before piping
