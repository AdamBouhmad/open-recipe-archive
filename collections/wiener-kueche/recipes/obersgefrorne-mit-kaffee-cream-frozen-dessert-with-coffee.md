## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter very strong black coffee without addition
- 3/4 liter unsweetened cream
- 20 deca sugar
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Combine very strong black coffee, unsweetened cream, sugar, and egg yolks in a snow-beater
2. Treat further as indicated for Gefrorne mit Vanille (No. 1720)
