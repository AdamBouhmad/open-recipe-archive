## Ingredients

- 60 Deka flour
- 3 Deka yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk
- 8 Deka melted fat
- 1/8 liter milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 heaping tablespoon sugar
- Filling of choice
- Fat for brushing and cooking

## Directions

1. Place 60 Deka flour in a deep bowl, make a well in the middle, pour in 3 Deka yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk into this well
2. Stir it together somewhat, dust it a little with flour and let it stand covered in a warm place
3. Beat together 8 Deka melted fat, 1/8 liter milk, 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1 heaping tablespoon sugar and pour this into the so-called sponge after it has risen
4. Stir well together and beat the dough until it detaches from the spoon
5. Let it stand covered in a warm place
6. When the dough has risen somewhat, use a slotted spoon dipped in hot fat to scoop out pieces of desired size, place them in a fat-brushed casserole and brush them on the sides with melted fat
7. Cover them with a cloth and let them rise somewhat in a warm place
8. Brush them once more with melted fat and bake them for 3/4 to 1 hour in the oven
