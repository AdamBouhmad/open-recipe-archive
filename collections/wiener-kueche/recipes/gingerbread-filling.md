## Ingredients

- 6 dkg grated hazelnuts
- lemon zest
- 3 dkg finely chopped citron
- 2 dkg chopped Araneini
- 1 egg
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix the grated hazelnuts, lemon zest, finely chopped citron and chopped Araneini together
2. Stir in 1 egg and sugar to taste
