## Ingredients

- 4 loth of dried orange peel
- 2 measures of once-rectified spirits
- 14 pounds of sugar
- 2 pounds of boiling water

## Directions

1. Soak orange peel in water and remove the inner spongy or marrow-like skin, leaving only the upper yellow part
2. Cut the yellow peel into fine pieces and dry it
3. Dissolve 14 pounds of sugar in 2 pounds of boiling water
4. Take 4 loth of the dried peel, 2 measures of once-rectified spirits, and the dissolved sugar
5. Let everything stand for some days in gentle heat
6. Filter into bottles
