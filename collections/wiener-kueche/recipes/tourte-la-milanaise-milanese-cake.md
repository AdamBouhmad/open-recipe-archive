## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons orange blossoms
- 6 tablespoons cold water
- 30 teaspoons peeled almonds
- 28 teaspoons butter
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest
- pinch of cinnamon
- egg yolks
- 30 teaspoons flour

## Directions

1. Soak the orange blossoms in cold water for approximately 2 hours, then pour through a sieve and pound very finely with the peeled almonds.
2. Mix the butter with the lemon zest, the aforementioned almonds, a pinch of cinnamon and egg yolks, adding one after the other.
3. Stir for 1 to 2 hours and gradually mix in 30 teaspoons of flour.
4. Continue as described for the Viennese Cake (No. 1486).
