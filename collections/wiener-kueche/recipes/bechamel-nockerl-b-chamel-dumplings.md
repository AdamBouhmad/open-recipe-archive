## Ingredients

- Béchamel sauce
- butter
- sweet cream
- sugar
- ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Make Béchamel sauce and work it together
2. Set butter and sweet cream a quarter inch deep with sugar and ground vanilla on heat
3. When everything simmers, drop in the dumplings
4. Cook as for butter dumplings
5. Serve immediately
