## Ingredients

- 60-80 dkg veal fricandeau or boned veal shoulder
- 5 dkg bacon
- 6 dkg root vegetables
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/4 liter wine
- 6 dkg hot fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it, lard it, and optionally roll and tie it
2. In a roasting pan, lightly brown the cleaned, washed, and thinly sliced root vegetables and one onion ring in 6 dkg hot fat
3. Add the meat and braise it in the oven for 1.5-2 hours, basting frequently with its own juices and adding more liquid as needed
4. Remove the meat, dust the pan juices, lightly strain them, add cream and wine, return to the pan and simmer with the meat for another 10 minutes
5. Slice the meat thinly, arrange on an oblong platter in its original shape, and serve with the sauce
