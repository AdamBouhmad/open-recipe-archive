## Ingredients

- 60-80 dekagrams veal leg
- flour
- salt
- fat

## Directions

1. Remove skin and bones from the meat and cut into not-too-thin cutlets.
2. Lightly pound the cutlets.
3. 15 minutes before serving, salt them.
4. Coat one side with flour.
5. Place flour-side down into a flat pan with hot fat.
6. Brown quickly on both sides and remove to a plate to keep warm.
7. Once all cutlets are done, drain the fat.
8. Add a piece of butter to the pan and brown it.
9. Deglaze with a little broth or water and bring to a boil.
10. Arrange the cutlets on a long platter and pour the strained sauce over them.
