## Ingredients

- Pheasant, plucked and singed
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. After plucking and singeing the pheasant, cut the skin back at the neck, remove the crop and esophagus with a cloth, and lift away the skin over the breast
2. Make an incision at the top just below the wings
3. Carefully detach the breast on each side from the breastbone and skeleton
4. Carefully remove the strong sinew connecting the two parts without damaging the underbreasts
5. Smooth the underbreasts with a knife and place on a plate
6. From the large breasts, remove the fine skin attached with a knife and smooth these as well
7. Salt both large and underbreasts, place side by side in a casserole, cover with a paper sheet buttered and shaped to fit the casserole, and set aside
8. A few minutes before cooking, place the casserole over the fire and sauté the breasts on both sides over moderate heat for a few minutes
