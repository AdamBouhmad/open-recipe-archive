## Ingredients

- 10 decagrams butter
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley (if available)
- some chervil
- a little salt
- white pepper
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Pound the butter finely in a mortar with the chopped parsley, chervil, salt, white pepper, and lemon juice until well mixed and crushed
2. Serve with beefsteaks or cutlets
