## Ingredients

- Reed hen
- Vinegar
- Water
- Kuttelkraut
- Bay leaves
- Lemon peel
- Onion
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Pea broth
- Wine

## Directions

1. Skin the reed hens, cut them into several pieces, wash them well.
2. Make a marinade: take half vinegar and half water, Kuttelkraut, bay leaves, lemon peel, onion, and salt.
3. When this marinade simmers, lay the reed hens in it and let them braise gently over heat until soft.
4. Make a brown sauce: take butter in a pan, make a small roux, and fill it half with the marinade in which the reed hens were cooked, and half with pea broth.
5. Let it come to a boil, then thin it with wine, lay the reed hens in it, and let them braise a little more.
6. Arrange the reed hens on a platter, strain the sauce through a sieve, and pour the brown sauce over them.
