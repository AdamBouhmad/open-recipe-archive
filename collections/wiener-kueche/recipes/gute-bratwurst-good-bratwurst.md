## Ingredients

- Meat from the leg of a young well-fattened pig, cleaned of skin and all gristle
- Two pounds lard for every four to five pound leg
- Zest of one lemon
- Salt and pepper
- Half a measure of water
- Saitlinge (striped sheep intestines)

## Directions

1. Finely chop the cleaned leg meat
2. Finely chop the lard and lemon zest
3. Salt and pepper well
4. While constantly mixing, pour half a measure of water into the mixture
5. Fill one finger thick into Saitlinge intestines
6. Use as desired
