## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram sugar in pieces
- thin peel of 1 orange (without white pith)
- juice of 3 oranges
- 1/4 liter Rhine wine
- 1/4 liter very cold champagne
- 1/8 liter juice of concentrated berries (optional)

## Directions

1. In a soup pot, add 1/4 kilogram sugar cut into pieces and the thin peel of 1 orange with no white pith
2. Add the juice of 3 oranges and 1/4 liter Rhine wine
3. Mix well, fill into bottles and set on ice
4. Shortly before serving, strain this liquid through a cloth and add 1/4 liter very cold champagne
5. The flavor can be enhanced with 1/8 liter juice of concentrated berries
