## Ingredients

- One goose
- Bacon
- Small whole onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Seasonings
- Bouillon
- White wine
- Small English potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the goose as is proper, dry it well, and place it in an oblong roasting pan with bacon, whole small onions, salt, pepper, and seasonings
2. Pour in some bouillon and white wine, and roast it in the oven at moderate heat
3. Baste it often from time to time and turn it
4. When it is roasted through, skim off the fat, dress the goose on the plate, garnish it all around with the onions, and give the fond, reduced briefly and strained, over it
5. Alternatively, small English potatoes can be roasted with the goose, glazed, and used to garnish it
6. The geese may also, for variety, be filled with cooked quince or apple marmalade, with raisins, chestnuts, etc., when they are roasted
