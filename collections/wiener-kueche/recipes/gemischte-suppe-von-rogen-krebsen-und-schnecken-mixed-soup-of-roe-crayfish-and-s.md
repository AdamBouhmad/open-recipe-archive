## Ingredients

- Cooked carp roe
- Cooked snails, finely cut
- Crayfish tails, removed from shells
- Crayfish claws
- Good pea broth
- Butter
- Spices
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place cooked carp roe in a bowl
2. Add cooked, finely cut snails, removed crayfish tails and claws
3. Pour good pea broth over everything
4. Add butter and spices
5. Salt as needed and let it come to a boil once
