## Ingredients

- Clear orange or lemon jelly mass
- Rice with cream, or vanilla, or any cream, or striped blanc-manger

## Directions

1. Make a clear orange or lemon jelly
2. Pour into a high, flat form set in ice, enough jelly mass so the bottom is covered 2 fingers thick and let it freeze
3. Beforehand, freeze rice with cream, or vanilla, or any cream, or striped blanc-manger in a round form, which is approximately 3 fingers smaller in diameter than the high form chosen for this jelly
4. Place this cream carefully on the 2 fingers thick clear jelly poured in, but so that the upper side lies face down on the jelly, with a 2 finger-wide empty space remaining at the walls
5. Fill the middle empty space with jelly and then fill the form with the remaining clear jelly mass
6. Let freeze and proceed further as for Orange Sulz (see recipe 1660)
