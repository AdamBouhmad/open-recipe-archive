## Ingredients

- 28 deca vanilla sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 22 deca grated chocolate
- 16 deca grated house bread
- 8 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan
- glaze

## Directions

1. Stir 28 deca vanilla sugar with 16 egg yolks for 1/3 hour
2. Add 22 deca grated chocolate and 16 deca grated house bread, mix well
3. Gently fold in the snow (stiffly beaten egg whites) from 8 egg whites
4. Fill a cake pan brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. Bake for 1 hour at low heat
6. Once completely cooled, cover with glaze of choice
