## Ingredients

- 32 teaspoons butter
- 16 teaspoons peeled almonds
- 1 egg white
- juice of 1 lemon
- 16 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks, passed
- 36 teaspoons flour

## Directions

1. Beat 32 teaspoons of butter with 16 teaspoons of peeled almonds pounded very finely with 1 egg white, the juice of 1 lemon and 16 teaspoons powdered sugar, mix thoroughly.
2. Mix this with 6 hard-boiled, passed egg yolks and 36 teaspoons flour.
3. Treat the dough as described for the 'Sugar Dough Cake in French Style' (No. 1481).
