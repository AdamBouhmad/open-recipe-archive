## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 8 deca finely pounded sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter flour
- 1/2 liter milk
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deca butter until fluffy
2. Gradually add 8 deca finely pounded sugar, 2 teaspoons salt, and 6 egg yolks
3. Slowly add 1/2 liter flour and 1/2 liter milk, always adding a little milk first
4. When the dough is smooth and free of lumps, lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Further treatment, namely baking, is specified in the 'Germ-Pastries' (recipe 1273)
6. These pastries can be served with grated Parmesan cheese if desired, in which case use 8 deca grated Parmesan instead of sugar in the dough
7. Before serving, sprinkle the pastries with grated Parmesan and serve with cream sauce as specified in 'Pastries in Cream Sauce' (recipe 1276)
8. These pastries can be prepared with stewed and sugar or also with Spanish wine as in 'Strawberries with Spanish Wine' (recipe 1453)
