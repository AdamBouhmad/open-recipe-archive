## Ingredients

- peeled and tender cooked beef tongue
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 1 strong tablespoon parsley
- 5 sprigs tarragon
- equal amount chervil
- some sorrel
- juice of 1 lemon
- glaze in 1/8 liter soup
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled and tender cooked tongue into finger-thick slices
2. In a shallow saucepan, put 8 tablespoons butter with 1 tablespoon parsley, 5 sprigs tarragon, equal amount chervil, and some sorrel, all finely cut, and stir in the melted butter
3. Lay the cooked tongue slices in beside each other and cover with a butter-brushed piece of paper
4. 10 minutes before serving, place the saucepan on the fire, remove the paper, let the tongue slices brown for 2 minutes on each side
5. Arrange them nicely in the center of a deep round bowl
6. To the herbs remaining in the saucepan, add juice of 1 lemon and some glaze dissolved in 1/8 liter soup, bring to a boil and pour this sauce over the tongue
7. Border the bowl with finely plucked parsley sprigs
