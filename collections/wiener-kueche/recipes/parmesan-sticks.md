## Ingredients

- 6 dkg flour
- 4 dkg butter
- 4 dkg grated Parmesan cheese
- a knife tip of paprika
- a pinch of salt
- 1 spoonful cold water
- beaten egg for brushing
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. On the board rub 6 dkg flour with 4 dkg butter
2. Knead with 4 dkg grated Parmesan cheese, a knife tip of paprika, a pinch of salt and 1 spoonful cold water into dough
3. Roll out thin
4. Cut into very narrow, finger-long strips with a pastry wheel
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with caraway seeds
7. Bake on lightly buttered sheet in moderate heat until very light brown
8. The thinner and narrower, the better they are
9. They must only be light brown, otherwise they taste bitter
