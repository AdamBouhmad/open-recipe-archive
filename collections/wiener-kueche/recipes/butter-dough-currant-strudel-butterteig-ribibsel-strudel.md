## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 21 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- water
- egg white for brushing
- preserved currants

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the apple strudel, using preserved currants as filling instead of apples
