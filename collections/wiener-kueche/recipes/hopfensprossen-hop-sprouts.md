## Ingredients

- hop sprouts
- water
- butter
- flour
- lean ham
- onions
- bouillon
- sugar
- egg yolk
- lemon juice
- cotelettes

## Directions

1. Clean the hop sprouts and cook them almost tender in water
2. Sweat a piece of butter and flour
3. Add a slice of lean ham and a couple of onions
4. Salt and fill with bouillon
5. When the hop sprouts that are already somewhat cooked have been washed properly white, put them in the sauce
6. Also add a small piece of sugar
7. Let them cook gently
8. At serving time, bind the sauce with egg yolk and some lemon juice
9. Remove the onions and the ham
10. Garnish them with cotelettes
