## Ingredients

- carp
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon zest
- mace
- cloves, all finely pounded
- 5 eggs
- mutton intestines
- brown butter

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the carp, wash it, cut out the white flesh
2. Remove all bones from the flesh
3. Add butter, salt, pepper, parsley, lemon zest, mace, and cloves, all finely pounded
4. Mix in a binding made from 5 eggs with the flesh
5. Braise the mixture in a casserole
6. Fill into mutton intestines
7. Roast on a grill
8. Serve with brown butter
