## Ingredients

- capon
- calf sweetbread, blanched
- fresh oysters
- mushrooms
- anchovies
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon peel
- thyme
- egg yolk
- bacon sheets
- clear fond or pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Flame, eviscerate, and wash the capon, dry it, and carefully slip your hand between the skin and breast so the latter does not tear
2. Finely hack together a blanched calf sweetbread, several fresh oysters, mushrooms, anchovies, pepper, nutmeg, lemon peel, and thyme, mixed with an egg yolk
3. Stuff the capon between the loosened skin with this mixture
4. Fill the body with oysters and prepare it for roasting so the stuffing and oysters do not fall out
5. Cover it with bacon sheets and roast it slowly on the spit
6. When done, remove it, lay lemon on it, and arrange with clear fond or pepper sauce
