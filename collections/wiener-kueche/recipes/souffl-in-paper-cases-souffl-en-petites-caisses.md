## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 24 drams vanilla sugar
- 9 egg whites, beaten stiff
- 2 drams flour
- thick whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 24 drams vanilla sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Lightly fold in the snow from 9 egg whites mixed with 2 drams flour.
3. Lightly blend this with a few spoonfuls of thick whipped cream.
4. Fill paper cups halfway with the mixture.
5. Place on a paper-covered baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 1/4 hour.
