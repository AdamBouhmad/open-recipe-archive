## Ingredients

- 15 dekagrams flour
- 15 dekagrams butter
- 6 dekagrams swollen grated almonds
- 4 dekagrams sugar
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Make a crumb dough from 15 dekagrams flour, 15 dekagrams butter, 6 dekagrams swollen grated almonds, 4 dekagrams sugar and 1 egg
2. After resting, roll out the dough on a floured board not too thin, cut to the size of the torte base (medium form)
3. Place the sheet in the unbuttressed torte form
4. Form a finger-thick roll from half of the remaining dough, press it flat slightly in the form at the edge
5. Bake at high heat until light brown, let cool and then spread the filling on it
6. Form thin rolls from the remaining dough, place them in a grid pattern over the filling
7. Brush with egg, bake at higher heat until light brown
8. Remove, cool, sprinkle with sugar and serve
