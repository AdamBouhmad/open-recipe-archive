## Ingredients

- green walnuts
- lemon juice
- cold lemon water
- boiling lemon water
- 80 decilitres sugar
- cold sugar water
- vanilla (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove green walnuts at the beginning of June
2. Let them lie for several days
3. Remove the green skin down to the white, cut the walnut neatly
4. Rub with lemon juice
5. Throw into cold, then into boiling lemon water and cook fairly soft
6. Pass three times into cold water and fill the jars with them
7. Pour so much cold sugar (of 80 decilitres) into these that the walnuts are completely covered
8. Bind with papers and cook further as the Muscatel pears (Nr. 1842) for 3-4 hours in steam
9. One can cook a small piece of vanilla in the sugar
