## Ingredients

- ripe and cleaned rosehips (Heehepetſchen)
- sugar (half pound per 7 loth of pulp)

## Directions

1. Press the rosehips through a fine sieve.
2. Weigh the pulp and measure half a pound of sugar for every 7 loth of pulp.
3. Clarify and cook the sugar.
4. Stir the pulp well into the sugar.
5. Once the marmalade reaches proper thickness, place in a cool place.
6. Fill into containers or jars.
