## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- grated Parmesan
- 3 dkg breadcrumbs
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Cream together 4 dkg butter, 1 egg, salt, grated Parmesan, and 3 dkg breadcrumbs
2. Press the mixture through a fine grater into boiling beef broth
3. Let cook for 5 minutes
