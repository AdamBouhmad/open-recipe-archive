## Ingredients

- 3 cleaned, washed cabbage heads
- salt
- 16 Deka butter
- pepper
- marjoram
- 6 whole eggs
- 1/8 liter milk cream
- 1/2 liter flour for dough
- 6 Deka bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil cabbage heads in salt water, drain and chop very finely
2. Heat 16 Deka butter and braise cabbage with salt, pepper, and marjoram
3. Put into a sieve to drain
4. Beat in 6 whole eggs and add 1/8 liter milk cream
5. Spread on strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
6. Sprinkle 6 Deka bread crumbs over it
7. Roll together and treat as previous strudels
