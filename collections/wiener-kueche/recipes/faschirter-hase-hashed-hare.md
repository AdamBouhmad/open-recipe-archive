## Ingredients

- cold hare
- venison
- onion
- roasted goose liver
- bread roll
- beef broth
- egg yolks
- cream
- pepper
- goose fat
- breadcrumbs
- hare or venison shank
- brown roux
- broth
- vinegar
- lemon peel
- capers
- cream

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cold hare and a piece of venison, some onion, half of a roasted goose liver, a bread roll soaked in beef broth.
2. Chop this all finely together, add two egg yolks, two spoonfuls of cream, and ground pepper, and stir all well.
3. Brush a mold with goose fat, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, add part of the forcemeat, then thin slices of hare or venison shank, layer them in the middle of the mold, add the remaining forcemeat.
4. Apply heat above and below and bake nicely.
5. Make a somewhat brown roux, fill with broth, add some vinegar, lemon peel, capers, and finally a couple spoonfuls of cream, let come to a boil.
6. Turn the hare onto a dish and pour the sauce over it.
