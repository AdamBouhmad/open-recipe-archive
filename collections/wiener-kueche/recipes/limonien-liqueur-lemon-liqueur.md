## Ingredients

- yellow peel of 6 lemons
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 glass water
- 5 Seidel brandy

## Directions

1. Cut the yellow peel from the lemons, remove the white part cleanly, then cut the yellow peel finely and place in a bottle or jug
2. Add 5 Seidel brandy to the bottle
3. Boil 1.5 pounds sugar in 1 glass water until well skimmed
4. Pour the sugar mixture into the brandy
5. Let stand for three weeks
6. Shake well and let stand for another twelve days
7. It becomes better the older it gets
