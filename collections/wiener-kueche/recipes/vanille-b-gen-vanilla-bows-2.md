## Ingredients

- One quarter pound sugar
- Vanilla pounded with sugar
- Snow from 2 egg whites
- A little lemon juice
- 2 Loth flour
- Oblaten

## Directions

1. Put one quarter pound sugar, some vanilla pounded with sugar, and the snow from 2 egg whites in a pot
2. Add a little lemon juice and stir until it becomes thick
3. Mix in 2 Loth flour, spread it on wide oblaten, and bake them nicely
