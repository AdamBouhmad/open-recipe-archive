## Ingredients

- 10 deca beaten butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- some salt
- 20 deca white bread crumbs
- 4 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until creamy. Stir in the egg yolks, whole egg, and salt.
2. Add the white bread crumbs that have been soaked in 4 tablespoons milk and mix well together.
3. Let stand covered for 1/4 hour.
4. Form small knödel from the mixture.
5. Cook them covered for approximately 10 minutes in boiling salted water or in the soup.
6. They can be added to soup or to preserved dishes.
