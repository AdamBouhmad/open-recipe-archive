## Ingredients

- 1 plate of mushrooms, approximately 1 kilo (Pilsling, Bratling, yellow mushrooms, morels or champignons)
- 12 Deka butter or lard
- chopped green parsley
- 8 Deka bread crumbs
- 1/2 liter flour for dough
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- 4 whole eggs
- white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and wash mushrooms, dry with cloth, chop finely
2. Braise in 12 Deka butter or lard with parsley
3. When half braised, add 8 Deka bread crumbs and braise together
4. When done, spread filling on strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
5. Whip 1/4 liter milk cream with 4 whole eggs and pour over in spoonfuls
6. Roll dough lightly and treat as lung strudel
7. Add white pepper if desired
