## Ingredients

- 8 ounces butter
- 3 veal tails
- Water
- Rice
- Parsley
- Mushroom
- Onion
- Clove

## Directions

1. Put 8 ounces of butter with 3 veal tails cut into finger-length pieces in half a liter of water and let simmer.
2. When they begin to become tender, add one quarter liter of rice, some sprigs of bound parsley, one piece of cleaned mushroom, and a small piece of onion skewered with a clove.
3. Braise until the rice is tender.
4. Before serving, remove the onion and parsley and serve the rice with the pieces on a plate.
