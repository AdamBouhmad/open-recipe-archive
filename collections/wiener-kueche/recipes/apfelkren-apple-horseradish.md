## Ingredients

- Apples
- Horseradish
- Salt
- Sugar
- Vinegar
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the apples
2. Grate the horseradish in the same manner
3. Mix together with salt, sugar, vinegar, and cold water
4. Cover in a porcelain bowl and let stand until serving
