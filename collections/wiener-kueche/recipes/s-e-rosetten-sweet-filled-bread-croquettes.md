## Ingredients

- rolls of any size
- jam of choice
- cold milk or sweetened white wine or red wine
- flour
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat for frying
- vanilla sugar or sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Trim the rolls and halve them
2. Cut each half deeply and fill with jam
3. Soak in cold milk, sweetened white wine, or red wine
4. Dust with flour
5. Dip in beaten eggs
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. Fry in hot fat
8. Sprinkle with vanilla sugar or sugar and cinnamon when serving
