## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- 21 dkg sugar
- 21 dkg grated nuts or raw grated almonds
- 21 dkg finely cut dates
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat sugar into stiff egg whites
2. Fold in grated nuts or raw grated almonds, finely cut dates, and lemon juice
3. Fill mixture into form
4. Bake at lower heat
5. When cooled, turn out of form
6. Cover with chocolate glaze
