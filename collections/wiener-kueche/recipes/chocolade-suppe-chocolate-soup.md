## Ingredients

- chocolate, 4 small tablets
- milk, 3 seitels
- sugar
- butter
- egg yolks, 3
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Grate 4 small tablets of chocolate into 3 seitels of milk
2. Cook with a little sugar and a small piece of butter, stirring vigorously
3. Pour the soup over 3 egg yolks in a pot
4. Serve over toasted bread slices cut into small pieces
