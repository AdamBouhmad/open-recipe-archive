## Ingredients

- 1 calf's lung with heart
- butter
- green parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper, ground
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- strudel dough
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook a calf's lung with heart until soft
2. Remove the esophagus and chop the rest finely
3. Warm a piece of butter
4. Add finely chopped green parsley and let it warm through
5. Add the chopped lung with salt and ground pepper
6. Let it brown a little
7. Beat 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk over it
8. Stir it well together
9. Let it cool
10. Make ordinary strudel dough and stretch it thin
11. Fill the lung mixture into it, leaving an empty space of about one and a half spans in the middle
12. Cut the empty space in the middle
13. Roll the dough from both sides together so the empty space wraps around the filled part several times
14. Press with a flour-dusted cooking spoon handle one small finger length through
15. Separate these parts with a knife
16. Place them in a light brown soup and serve
