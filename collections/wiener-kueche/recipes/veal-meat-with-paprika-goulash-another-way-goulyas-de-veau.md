## Ingredients

- 16 ounces lard, diced
- root vegetables, diced
- onion
- mushrooms, thinly sliced
- veal breast, cut into neat pieces
- broth
- milk cream
- paprika
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place diced lard, diced root vegetables, onion, and thinly sliced mushrooms in a casserole.
2. When these have roasted together slightly, add the veal breast pieces.
3. When they have roasted a little, pour broth over and braise the meat until tender.
4. Add milk cream, salt, and season with paprika to taste.
5. Let it cook together slightly and arrange in a platter.
6. Alternatively, one can use 1 kilogram of meat from the shoulder, neck, or leg.
