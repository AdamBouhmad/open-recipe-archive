## Ingredients

- 2 and a half pounds strawberries
- 5 loth red currants (Ribisel)
- 1 pound sugar, clarified

## Directions

1. Press 2 and a half pounds strawberries and 5 loth red currants through a hair sieve
2. Mix in 1 pound clarified sugar
3. After cooling, stir until it is time to pour everything into the freezing pan
