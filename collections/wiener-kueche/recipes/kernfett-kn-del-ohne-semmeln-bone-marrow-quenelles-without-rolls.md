## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter flour
- 1 1/4 liter sour cream
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- 22 Loffel bone marrow, freed from all skin, finely diced, soaked and squeezed out in cold water

## Directions

1. Mix into 3/4 liter flour 1 1/4 liter sour cream, 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk and salt into a dough
2. Add 22 Loffel bone marrow that has been freed from skin, finely diced, soaked and squeezed out in cold water
3. Stir well together and let stand covered for 1/2 hour
4. Put knödel of desired size into boiling beef broth and cook covered for 20 minutes
5. Serve either with soup or drizzled with butter and roll crumbs and serve with roasts, lung roasts, roast beef, etc.
