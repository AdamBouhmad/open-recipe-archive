## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar in small pieces
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 4 oranges
- 1/4 liter rum
- 12 deciliters rice
- 1 liter water
- 4 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. In a porcelain bowl, add sugar broken into small pieces with the juice of 1 lemon and the juice of 4 oranges along with 1/4 liter rum
2. Set this covered in a warm place
3. In the meantime, boil 12 deciliters rice in 1 liter water for approximately 3/4 hour
4. Add the boiling rice to the dissolved sugar instead of tea
5. If desired, whip 4 egg yolks and add them
