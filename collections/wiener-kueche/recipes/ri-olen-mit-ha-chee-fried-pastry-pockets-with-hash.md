## Ingredients

- 25 decagrams cooked beef
- leftover game or roasted meat

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as Riſolen mit Hirn (Fried Pastry Pockets with Brain), using hash made from 25 decagrams cooked beef, game, or leftover roasted meat as filling
2. Can be served as a side dish to meat with sauce or with spinach like the other Riſolen
