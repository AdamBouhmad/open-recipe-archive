## Ingredients

- a piece of pike
- salted water
- crayfish tails
- mushrooms
- asparagus, boiled in salted water
- butter
- flour
- cream
- salted simmering water

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of pike in salted water, remove the bones, and shred it into pieces.
2. Cut crayfish tails, some mushrooms, and a little asparagus that has been boiled in salted water into small pieces.
3. Stew these together in butter and dust a little flour on top.
4. Place several spoonfuls of cream and the ragout on a dish.
5. Poach the eggs in salted simmering water only until they set together, but not until they are hard.
6. Remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and lay them on the ragout.
7. Let the ragout with the cream come to a simmer and serve on this dish.
