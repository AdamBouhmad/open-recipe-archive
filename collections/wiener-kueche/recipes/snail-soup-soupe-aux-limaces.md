## Ingredients

- 25 cooked snails
- butter
- parsley
- semolina breadcrumbs
- 2 liters pea water or water
- cream
- spices (optional)
- toasted semolina slices

## Directions

1. Finely chop the cooked snails
2. Toast them in butter with parsley and semolina breadcrumbs
3. Add 2 liters of pea water or water and cream
4. Simmer
5. Add spices if desired
6. Pour over toasted semolina slices
