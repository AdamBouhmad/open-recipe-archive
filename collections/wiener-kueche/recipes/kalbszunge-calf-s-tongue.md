## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Lukewarm water
- Salt water
- Piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the calf's tongue in lukewarm water to rinse it
2. Cook it in salt water until the outer skin can be removed
3. When this has happened, divide it lengthwise, cut into desired pieces
4. Serve with a piquant sauce, or fry it like calf's head
