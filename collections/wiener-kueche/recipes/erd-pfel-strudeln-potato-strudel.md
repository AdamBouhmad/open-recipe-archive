## Ingredients

- 12 Deka butter
- 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
- 32 Deka hot mashed potatoes, preferably fried
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- salt
- 1/8 liter milk cream
- 2 eggs for snow
- 1/8 liter flour for dough
- 2 tablespoons semolina

## Directions

1. Whip 12 Deka butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
3. Add 32 Deka hot mashed potatoes, parsley, salt, and 1/8 liter milk cream
4. Fold in snow from 2 eggs
5. Spread on stretched strudel dough made from 1/8 liter flour
6. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons semolina over it
7. Treat as previous strudels
