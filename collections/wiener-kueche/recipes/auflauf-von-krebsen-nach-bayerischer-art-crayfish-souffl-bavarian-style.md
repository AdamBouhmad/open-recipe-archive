## Ingredients

- 20 beautiful crayfish
- 2 Seidel milk
- 16 egg yolks
- Three-quarters pound crayfish butter
- One-quarter pound pistachios
- One-quarter pound sugar
- One-quarter pound grated rolls
- Wine vinegar

## Directions

1. From 15 crayfish make one-quarter pound crayfish butter.
2. From the remaining half of the crayfish, remove the bitter part and pound them alive, then stir under 2 Seidel milk and strain through a hair sieve, ensuring no shell passes through.
3. Heat this strained milk with a little wine vinegar over the fire, stirring constantly, until it curdles into cheese, then pour it onto a hair sieve.
4. When the sour water has drained off, pound the cheese finely, beat it to foam with the crayfish butter, and gradually add 16 egg yolks.
5. Mix in the remaining ingredients and finally fold in the snow from the eggs.
6. Bake this mass in a rimmed form for one hour.
