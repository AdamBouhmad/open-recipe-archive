## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon fennel or caraway seeds
- 0.5 liter boiling water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1 teaspoon of fennel or caraway seeds in a coffee pot.
2. Pour 0.5 liter of boiling water over it and let it boil for a quarter hour.
3. Strain it and serve cold or warm. In the latter case, sugar can be added.
