## Ingredients

- Large, dry, black cherries
- Sugar water (made from sugar syrup with 60 decigrams sugar per liter, as described in recipe 1841)
- Morello cherries
- Sugar water (made with 80 decigrams sugar per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the cherries
2. Place them in preserving jars
3. Pour 2 to 3 fingers' height of sugar water into the jars
4. Proceed as with the Meusel-Cateller pears (recipe 1842)
5. For morello cherries, cut the stems slightly, leave them attached, and use sugar water made with 80 decigrams sugar per liter of water, treating them the same way
