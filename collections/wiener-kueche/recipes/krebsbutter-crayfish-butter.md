## Ingredients

- tails from 24 cooked crayfish
- shells and claws from the crayfish
- 1/4 pound butter
- cold water

## Directions

1. Remove the tails from 24 cooked crayfish
2. Remove the gall and belly contents
3. Pound together only the shells and claws with 1/4 pound butter until small
4. Fry this mixture in a pan on the coals, sprinkling with a little cold water, until well cooked out
5. Strain the butter through a cloth dipped in cold water and press it well
