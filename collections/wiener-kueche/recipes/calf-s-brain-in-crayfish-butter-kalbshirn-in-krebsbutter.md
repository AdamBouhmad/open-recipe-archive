## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Braise
- Bacon slices
- Good white sauce
- Crayfish butter
- Croutons
- Crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Wash the brains well and cook them in a good braise, covered with bacon slices.
2. Prepare a good white sauce and enrich it with crayfish butter, seasoning well.
3. Remove the brains from the braise and arrange them on a plate.
4. Garnish with croutons and crayfish tails.
5. Serve sauce over them.
