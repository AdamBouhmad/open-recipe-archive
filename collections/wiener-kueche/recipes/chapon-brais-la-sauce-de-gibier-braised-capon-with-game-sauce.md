## Ingredients

- 1 braised capon with game marinade
- bacon slices
- finely sliced black bread

## Directions

1. The capon, prepared as in the previous recipe with game marinade, is placed in a casserole with the marinade, the capon is covered with bacon slices and braised with 9 decagrams of finely sliced black bread until tender
