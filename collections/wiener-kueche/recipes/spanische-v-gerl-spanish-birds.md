## Ingredients

- 40-50 dkg beef roulade slices
- 2 anchovies
- farce
- 5 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- rice, noodles or potato pancakes

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat, trim the skin, tap lightly, salt, pepper, coat with farce, roll up and tie.
2. In a roasting pan, in 5 dkg hot fat, let 1 finely chopped onion turn golden, add the meat.
3. Pour in some water or broth, cover and braise 1 1/2 hours, adding water frequently.
4. Remove the meat, untie it, strain the sauce, optionally pass it through a sieve, serve with the meat.
5. Side dishes: rice, noodles, potato pancakes.
