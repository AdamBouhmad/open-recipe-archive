## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram almonds, of which 20 pieces are bitter
- cold water
- 1 casserole
- 1 liter cold water
- 40 deca finely sifted caster sugar
- 8 deca dissolved warm isinglass
- 2 deca gelatin
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Soak 1 kilogram almonds (including 20 bitter pieces) in cold water for 24 hours
2. Remove the shells
3. Pound the almonds very finely with some water
4. Place in 1 casserole with 1 liter cold water on the stove
5. Stir until it comes to a boil, but do not let it boil
6. Press through a cloth into a casserole
7. Add 40 deca finely sifted caster sugar
8. Let warm and stir in 8 deca dissolved warm isinglass well
9. Fill this mass into a desired mold that was buried in ice 1 hour earlier
10. Proceed as with Rice with Vanilla Cream (No. 1689)
11. Alternatively use 2 deca gelatin dissolved in 1/2 liter water and mix hot with 3/4 liter strained almond milk
