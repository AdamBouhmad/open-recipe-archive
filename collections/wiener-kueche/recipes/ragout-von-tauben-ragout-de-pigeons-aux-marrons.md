## Ingredients

- 4 plucked, scalded, washed, and salted pigeons
- 16 deca of liver forcemeat (No. 1032)
- 10 deca of butter
- Flour
- White broth
- 16 deca of smoked sausage
- 30 pieces of roasted, peeled chestnuts
- 4 deca of sugar
- 20 small onions
- 10 pieces of boiled, dressed mushrooms

## Directions

1. Fill the pigeons with liver forcemeat, dress them, and place in a casserole containing 10 deca of butter.
2. Let them braise covered slowly for approximately 1 1/2 hours.
3. Remove the pigeons and place in another casserole.
4. Dust the butter remaining in the first casserole with flour, roast it, add white broth, stir well, and simmer.
5. Pour the sauce through a sieve over the pigeons and braise covered until they are tender.
6. 1/4 hour before they are completely tender, add 16 deca of smoked sausage to the pigeons.
7. Meanwhile, keep 30 roasted, peeled chestnuts warm with 4 deca of sugar in the same amount of butter.
8. Cut the pigeons into 4 pieces, arrange on a platter, garnish with chestnuts, 20 small onions braised in butter until white and tender, and 10 boiled, dressed mushrooms, and serve with the sauce.
