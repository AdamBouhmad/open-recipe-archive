## Ingredients

- 10-12 medium tomatoes
- 4 tablespoons finely chopped veal or chicken meat
- 4 decagrams bread softened in milk, well wrung out
- finely chopped fresh parsley
- finely chopped onion
- pepper
- 4 decagrams cooked rice
- 1 egg
- salt
- 8 decagrams butter, whipped until fluffy
- cream
- breadcrumbs
- lard
- grated Parmesan cheese
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. From 10-12 medium tomatoes carefully pull out the stems and empty the juice and seeds through the opening
2. Pass some of the juice through a sieve
3. Mix 4 tablespoons finely chopped veal or chicken meat, 4 decagrams bread softened in milk and well wrung out, finely chopped fresh parsley and onion, a knife point of pepper, 4 decagrams cooked rice, 1 whole egg, salt and the juice from the tomatoes with 8 decagrams butter whipped until fluffy
4. Fill the hollowed out tomatoes with this mixture
5. Place them close together in a baking dish lined with lard and sprinkled with breadcrumbs
6. Pour cream over them
7. Cover and braise in the oven for approximately 1 hour
8. A few minutes before removing from the oven, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
9. Arrange piled on a platter and serve with well-cooked butter sauce
10. The Parmesan cheese can also be omitted
