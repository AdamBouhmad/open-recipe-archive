## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter semolina
- 1 liter boiling milk
- 14 Deca butter
- 1 pinch of salt
- 14 Deca lemon sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites (snow)
- chocolate (optional)

## Directions

1. Slowly dissolve 1/4 liter semolina in 1 liter boiling milk and cook until thick, then add 14 Deca butter and 1 pinch of salt, stir well together and let cool
2. Meanwhile stir 14 Deca lemon sugar with 10 egg yolks for 1/2 hour, add the cooled semolina mass, stir together and finally fold in lightly the snow from 10 egg whites
3. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mass and cook covered for 3/4 hours in steam or bake in the oven
4. Sprinkle the soufflé with sugar and serve with dissolved jam or any cream
5. If desired, 2 tablets of grated chocolate can be stirred into one half of the semolina mass before filling the form, then pour it into the other half in the form, but wafer sheets must be laid between
