## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 16 dkg sugar
- rum or punch to taste
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks with 16 dkg sugar until very foamy, mix in rum or punch to taste, and add whipped cream.
