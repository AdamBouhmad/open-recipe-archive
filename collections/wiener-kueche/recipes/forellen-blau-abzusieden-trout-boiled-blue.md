## Ingredients

- Trout
- wine vinegar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Trout must not be removed from water until needed for cooking
2. Strike the trout on the head, quickly slit the belly, remove the insides, wash it clean so the blood comes out
3. Immediately spray good wine vinegar on it
4. Set water with salt on the fire, and when it boils, add the fish with the vinegar and let it cook
5. When serving, add fresh wine vinegar again over it, cover the trout, and serve it cold or warm as desired
6. If served cold, it must remain in the broth
7. Prepared this way it will remain beautifully blue
