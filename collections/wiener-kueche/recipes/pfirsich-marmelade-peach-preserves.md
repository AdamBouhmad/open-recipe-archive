## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- powdered sugar equal in weight to the peaches without kernels
- currant preserves

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the Plum Preserves (recipe 1875).
2. Choose beautiful ripe garden peaches and must work very quickly, always immediately and well coating the peaches in sugar after peeling.
3. A few minutes before removing this preserves from the heat, stir in some spoonfuls of currant preserves, which gives the peaches a hint of red color.
