## Ingredients

- 12 dkg flour
- 3 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Knead the flour, sugar, butter and egg yolk on the board to a dough
2. After resting, roll out
3. Cut into rounds
4. Place on an unbuttered baking sheet
5. Bake at higher heat until light brown
6. Join 2 pieces together with filling
7. Top with ½ nut or sprinkle with sugar unglazed
