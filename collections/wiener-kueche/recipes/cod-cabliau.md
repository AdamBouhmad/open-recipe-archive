## Ingredients

- cod
- fresh water
- salt
- water
- milk
- lemon slices
- onions
- bay leaves
- potatoes
- clear butter or hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Scale the fish
2. Gut it
3. Soak it in fresh water for some time
4. Tie it with twine
5. Cook it slowly with the same ingredients as the turbot
6. Serve with boiled and peeled potatoes and melted clear butter or hollandaise sauce
