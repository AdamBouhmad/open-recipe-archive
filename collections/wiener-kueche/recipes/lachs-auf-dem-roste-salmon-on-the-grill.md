## Ingredients

- A piece of salmon of three or four pounds
- Vinegar
- Finely chopped herbs
- Fine oil

## Directions

1. Scale the salmon, remove the central bone, marinate it in vinegar with plenty of finely chopped herbs for about an hour, then dry it off.
2. Prick it with a knife, brush it with fine oil, and slowly roast it on the grill.
3. You must turn it and brush it while roasting.
4. After the salmon has roasted for half an hour this way, arrange it and serve with a hollandaise sauce poured over it.
