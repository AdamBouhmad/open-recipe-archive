## Ingredients

- A lean piece of beef from the top round
- Sugar
- Fat
- Onion, cut
- Glace or beef broth
- Raspberry or sour cherry juice

## Directions

1. Take a lean piece of beef from the top round, cook it properly soft
2. In a casserole, brown a piece of sugar with fat, add a handful of cut onion which must become quite soft in the sugar
3. Place the beef in it, which uncovered must become beautifully brown on all sides
4. Then add a little glace or beef broth and let it reduce properly
5. Before serving, add a couple of spoonfuls of raspberry or sour cherry juice, let this reduce very quickly while it must be turned often, and ensure that it gets a beautiful glace on both sides
