## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon finely cut parsley
- 1 piece finely cut mushroom
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 8 hard-boiled, finely cut eggs
- 3 tablespoons cream
- Breadcrumbs in equal amount to cream
- Flour for dusting
- Eggs for dipping
- Breadcrumbs for rolling
- Hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Roast the parsley and mushroom lightly in butter
2. When cooked, mix in the 2 whole eggs, add the hard-boiled eggs, cream and breadcrumbs
3. Mix well, place on a flour-dusted board and form eggs, dumplings or small sausages from this mixture
4. Dust with flour, dip in beaten eggs, roll in breadcrumbs and fry in very hot lard
5. Serve as garnish with vegetables or sauces, or as a dish by itself
