## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams powdered vanilla sugar
- 16 decagrams beaten butter

## Directions

1. Stir the powdered vanilla sugar into the beaten butter
2. Let it stand on ice for 1 hour
3. If the butter is stiff, place it in a decorative form on a plate
4. Garnish with butter bread cut into strips or serve with whipped cream or strawberries
