## Ingredients

- ½ kilo bratwurst
- 4 pieces of mushrooms, finely sliced
- finely cut green parsley
- butter
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- pepper
- veal breast

## Directions

1. Remove the filling from ½ kilo of bratwurst
2. Roast 4 pieces of finely sliced mushrooms with finely cut green parsley in butter
3. Mix this roasted mixture with 2 whole eggs
4. Stir well together, add salt and pepper as desired
5. Spread this mixture on the spread out, boiled veal breast
6. Roll it together, bind it tightly with twine, and roast it like the previously described veal breast, or braise it with soup and wine
7. When serving, garnish it with any desired puree or braised mushrooms or braised cucumbers and pour the defatted drippings over the meat
