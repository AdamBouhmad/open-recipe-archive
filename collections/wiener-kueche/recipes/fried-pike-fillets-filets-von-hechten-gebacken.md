## Ingredients

- cleaned and washed pike
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs
- flour
- white bread, grated
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from a cleaned and washed pike as if making pike forcemeat
2. Cut into pieces approximately two fingers thick
3. Lay in salt and vinegar for half an hour
4. Dry well
5. Dip several times in beaten eggs
6. Coat with a mixture of flour and grated white bread
7. Fry quickly in butter or lard until golden
