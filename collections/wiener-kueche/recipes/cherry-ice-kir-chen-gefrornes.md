## Ingredients

- 6 pounds sour cherries
- 1 pound sugar
- 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Wash 6 pounds of sour cherries with stems removed
2. Pound the cherries with their pits together
3. Pass through a fine sieve
4. Clarify 1 pound of sugar
5. Mix the strained cherries and the juice of 4 lemons with the clarified sugar
6. Work it very carefully as it freezes with difficulty because the cherries have more watery parts than other fruit
