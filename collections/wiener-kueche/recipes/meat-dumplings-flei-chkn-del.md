## Ingredients

- hash of 25-30 dkg cooked beef
- 6 dkg roll ham
- 8 dkg ham fat
- 4 dkg bacon
- some bread crumbs
- 30 dkg salted flour
- 4-5 scoop-fuls boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over 30 dkg salted flour in a pot, stir smooth and cover
2. When cooled, form equal-sized dumplings from the dough on a floured board, flatten slightly
3. Place a meatball inside, close the dough over it
