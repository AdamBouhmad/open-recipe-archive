## Ingredients

- half finger-thick dough
- good veal forcemeat
- larded and marinated filets
- braising liquid
- bacon slices
- egg yolk
- sauce haché

## Directions

1. Cover a baking dish with dough rolled half a finger thick and cut round
2. Pipe a thick layer of good veal forcemeat onto the dough
3. Lay the filets, which have been larded, well marinated, cut into pieces, and braised until tender, on top
4. Place bacon slices on the filets
5. Brush the inner edge of the dough with egg yolk
6. Cover with a dough lid, brush the pastry, place on a baking sheet, and bake quickly
7. Cut out the lid, remove the bacon leaves, and pour sauce haché inside
