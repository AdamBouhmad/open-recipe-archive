## Ingredients

- 12 hard-boiled eggs
- 6 ounces crayfish butter
- 8 ounces breadcrumbs, deveined and softened in milk
- egg yolks from the hard-boiled eggs
- 2 raw yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 spoon of cream
- salt
- pepper
- crayfish ragout
- lemon sauce or other sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off the white of 12 hard-boiled eggs at the top down to the yolk and carefully remove them.
2. In a mortar, finely pound 6 ounces crayfish butter, 8 ounces deveined breadcrumbs softened in milk, the removed egg yolks, 2 raw yolks, 1 whole egg, 1 spoon of cream, salt, and pepper; pass through a sieve.
3. Fill a desired form brushed with crayfish butter and dusted with flour with half the prepared mass, insert the eggs with their opened side facing up, fill them with crayfish ragout, and cover everything with the second half of the mass.
4. Cover this filled form, place in a hot water bath for 3/4 hour, then turn the eggs into a dish and serve with lemon sauce or other desired sauce.
