## Ingredients

- 5 dkg melted butter
- 2 dl lukewarm milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1-2 spoons sugar
- salt
- 30 dkg warmed flour
- 1½ dkg yeast starter (Dampfl risen)

## Directions

1. Whip together 5 dkg melted butter with 2 dl lukewarm milk, 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1-2 spoons sugar and salt
2. Add to 30 dkg warmed flour in a tall pot
3. Mix in the risen yeast starter
4. Whip the dough well and set near the oven to rise
5. When doubled in height, stir it and proceed as with the cream dalken
6. If the dough is too loose, add more flour
