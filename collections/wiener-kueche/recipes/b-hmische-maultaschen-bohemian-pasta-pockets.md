## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 4 deca finely pounded sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 deca flour
- filling with stewed fruit and vanilla as desired

## Directions

1. Stir together 16 deca butter with 4 deca finely pounded sugar, 1 pinch of salt, and 5 egg yolks
2. Add 3 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter lukewarm milk and 50 deca flour, mix well
3. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
4. Follow the same treatment as for the previous Maultaschen, but fill the indentations generously with stewed fruit and vanilla
