## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- finely cut onions
- root vegetables
- 2 plucked guinea fowls
- water as needed for soup
- 12 deca butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put 12 deca butter, finely cut onions and root vegetables into a casserole, lay 2 plucked guinea fowls on top and braise covered
2. When the root vegetables are brown, pour in as much water as needed for soup and let the fowls cook covered until tender
3. Meanwhile make a brown roux from 12 deca butter, pour this in with the strained broth in which the fowls were cooked, stir together and let cook well
4. Cut the fowls into neat pieces and serve in the soup with Faschiertl, roasted croutons or similar
