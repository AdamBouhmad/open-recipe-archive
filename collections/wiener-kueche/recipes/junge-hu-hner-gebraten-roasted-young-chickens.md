## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- hot butter for basting
- breadcrumbs (optional)
- bacon for larding (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean them well and press in the breastbone, then remove it
2. Salt them and bend the feet in
3. Roast them over a fresh fire on the spit
4. Frequently baste them with hot butter
5. If desired, dust them with breadcrumbs and let them roast a little more
6. Serve quickly
7. They can also be larded with finely cut bacon
