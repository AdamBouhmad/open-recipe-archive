## Ingredients

- 80 drachm well-skinned veal fat
- 40 drachm skinned calf meat, finely chopped (usually from the round)
- 6 whole eggs
- brandied dough made from 1/2 milk
- 1 bay leaf
- salt as needed
- spices

## Directions

1. Combine the veal fat with the finely chopped calf meat
2. Add 6 whole eggs, brandied dough made from 1/2 milk, 1 bay leaf, salt as needed, and spices
3. Pound everything together finely in a mortar
4. Use in the same manner as capon forcemeat or add to soup as consommé
