## Ingredients

- 4 measures wine spirit
- essential lemon oil (1 quentchen)
- essential bergamot oil (1 quentchen)
- 4 ounces double orange blossom water
- sugar water (made from 8 measures water and 4 pounds sugar)

## Directions

1. Pour 4 measures wine spirit into a pitcher
2. Add 1 quentchen essential lemon oil and 1 quentchen essential bergamot oil
3. Add 4 ounces double orange blossom water
4. Prepare sugar water by dissolving 4 pounds sugar in 8 measures water
5. Add the sugar water to the mixture
6. Shake everything together well
7. Let it stand for 6 to 8 days with frequent stirring
8. Filter the liquid
