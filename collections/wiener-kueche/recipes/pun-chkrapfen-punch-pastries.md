## Ingredients

- 15 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 spoon fine breadcrumbs
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 15 dkg soaked grated almonds
- 7 dkg grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat 15 dkg sugar with 6 egg yolks for 30 minutes
2. Stir in 1 spoon fine breadcrumbs
3. Fold in 6 stiff egg whites with 15 dkg soaked grated almonds
4. Spread half the mass onto a buttered, floured baking sheet, not too thin
5. Bake until light brown
6. Immediately cut with a small pastry cutter
7. Mix 7 dkg grated chocolate into the other half and proceed the same way
8. Spread a dark layer quite thickly with filling
9. Place a light layer on top
10. Put the pastry in the cutter
11. Press down evenly with a matching weight so the edge becomes nice and smooth
12. Ice them with red glaze
