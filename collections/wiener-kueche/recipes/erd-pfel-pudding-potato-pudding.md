## Ingredients

- 14 teaspoons beaten butter
- 12 teaspoons lemon sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 24 teaspoons cooked, passed, but cold potatoes
- 12 teaspoons peeled almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the pan
- flour for the pan

## Directions

1. When 14 teaspoons of beaten butter are mixed little by little with 12 teaspoons of lemon sugar and 8 egg yolks, add 24 teaspoons of cooked, passed, but cold potatoes with 12 teaspoons of peeled almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites, mix well together and finally lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
2. Fill this mass into a butter-greased, flour-dusted form and cook covered in a water bath for 1/2 hour, then turn into a bowl and serve with cream
