## Ingredients

- Chervil
- Bertram herb
- Ground ivy
- Sorrel
- Spinach
- Yarrow
- Cold salt water
- 6 dkg butter
- Beef broth
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the herbs
2. Place in a pot with cold salt water
3. Cook for 20 minutes
4. Strain and chop finely
5. Make a roux with 6 dkg butter
6. Pour beef broth over the roux
7. Add the herbs
8. Bind if desired
9. Serve with bread slices
