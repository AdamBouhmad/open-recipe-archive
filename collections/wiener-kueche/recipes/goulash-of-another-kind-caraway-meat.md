## Ingredients

- Cube-cut beef
- Vinegar
- Water
- 1 whole onion
- Caraway seeds
- Paprika
- Salt and spices
- Sour cream
- Flour

## Directions

1. Place the cube-cut meat in a casserole with enough vinegar and water to cover it.
2. Season with 1 whole onion studded with a spice clove, 1/2 teaspoon caraway and paprika.
3. Cook gently until tender, then salt.
4. If desired, mix 8 tablespoons of sour cream with 2 tablespoons of flour, add it and let it boil.
5. Remove the onion before serving.
