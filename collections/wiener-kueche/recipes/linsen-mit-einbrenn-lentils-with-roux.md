## Ingredients

- ½ kg lentils
- salt
- 5 dkg fat
- water or broth
- vinegar
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Sort and wash the lentils
2. Soak overnight
3. In the morning, drain the soaking water and add fresh cold water to cover
4. After bringing to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 1½ hours or more
5. Prepare a dark yellow roux from 5 dkg fat
6. Add the cooked lentils to the roux
7. Season with vinegar, salt, and sugar if desired
8. Add water or broth and simmer for 20 minutes
