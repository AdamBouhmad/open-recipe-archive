## Ingredients

- cress
- sorrel
- watercress
- tarragon
- borage
- pimpernel
- fennel herb
- lemon balm
- sugar beets
- cowslips
- yellow violets
- oil
- rose vinegar

## Directions

1. Select herbs which one likes most, such as cress, sorrel, watercress, tarragon, borage, pimpernel, fennel herb, lemon balm, sugar beets, cowslips, yellow violets, etc., using some or all of them
2. Wash them thoroughly
3. Prepare with oil and vinegar like other salad
4. Use rose vinegar instead of ordinary wine vinegar for better flavor
5. One can also sweeten the salad a little, and if desired, surround it with another salad
