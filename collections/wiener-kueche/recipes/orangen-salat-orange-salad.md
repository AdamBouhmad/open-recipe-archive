## Ingredients

- Any desired number of oranges
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and cut them into slices the thickness of a knife back, then cut these slices into two parts
2. Sprinkle them very generously with sugar and let them stand covered for 10 minutes so the sugar can be absorbed
3. Arrange them very decoratively on a compote dish, laying the larger and smaller pieces in a crown shape
4. A few stewed sour cherries can be placed in the center
5. Pour the orange juice over it by the spoonful
6. The preparation of this dish should be done shortly before serving, as the oranges lose their good flavor and become bitter with longer standing
