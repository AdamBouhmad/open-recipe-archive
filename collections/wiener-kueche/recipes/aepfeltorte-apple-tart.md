## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Peel and cut apples into pieces
2. Cook them with sugar, lemon peel, and some cinnamon into a jam that is neither thin nor watery
3. Let cool
4. Make a tart from puff pastry
5. Fill with the apple mass
6. Spread it finger-thick
7. Make a thin slitted lid over the top
8. Bake the tart slowly
9. Glaze it
