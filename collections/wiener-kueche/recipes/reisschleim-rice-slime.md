## Ingredients

- Four loths rice
- Two pounds spring water

## Directions

1. Grind four loths rice to powder in a marble mortar
2. Let it cook in two pounds spring water until it becomes a clear broth
3. Press through cloth while very hot and squeeze out strongly
4. The mixture has the consistency of jelly
5. Mix one or several spoonfuls with warm water or meat broth
