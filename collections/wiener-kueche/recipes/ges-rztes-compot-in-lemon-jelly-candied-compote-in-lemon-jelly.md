## Ingredients

- Lemon jelly
- Various fruits such as redcurrants, pitted cherries, Angelica pieces, pineapple pieces, nuts, and other desired compote

## Directions

1. Prepare a lemon jelly and pour it into a mold set in ice to a depth of 2 fingers
2. Once this is set, decoratively arrange with a pastry needle redcurrants, hollowed-out pieces of pineapple, nuts, Angelica or similar, but so that the walls of the mold remain untouched
3. Carefully pour 2 fingers high of jelly mixture along the wall
4. Let it freeze again and then in the same way arrange various desired compote
5. Pour jelly over it and continue this way until the mold is full
6. Of course, jelly mixture must be used at the end
7. Cover and handle further as with orange jelly (No. 1060)
8. Small fruits such as currants, pitted cherries and similar can be used raw or preserved
9. Other fruit must however be steamed or cooked in steam
