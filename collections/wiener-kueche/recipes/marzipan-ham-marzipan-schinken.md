## Ingredients

- 8 pounds marzipan dough
- finely ground sugar
- 1 loth finely ground cinnamon
- 1 loth finely ground cloves
- quarter pound chocolate
- water
- optional: vanilla or chocolate
- optional: crumbled macaroons

## Directions

1. Take 8 pounds marzipan dough and work it with finely ground sugar
2. Divide the mass into two unequal parts of 5 and 3 pounds
3. Color the larger portion red and season it with 1 loth cinnamon and 1 loth cloves, both finely ground
4. Form the known ham shape
5. With a knife make cuts in the middle of the red dough, which represents the lean meat
6. Fill them with white dough, which represents the fat
7. Stretch the rest of the remaining white marzipan dough with a rolling pin and lay it as an upper fat layer, 2 to 3 fingers high, done skillfully and naturally
8. To represent the rind of the ham, cook quarter pound chocolate in water quite thick
9. Brush the surface of the ham several times with it
10. The chocolate must be applied warm
11. If you want to make this confection more delicious, take some vanilla or chocolate and sprinkle the rind with crumbled macaroons, as cooks cover cooked ham with a bread crust
