## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound butter
- crumb of one small roll
- milk
- lung
- ten whole eggs
- salt
- one half-seidel good cream
- lemon peel, finely cut
- fresh parsley, finely cut

## Directions

1. Beat one-quarter pound butter fluffy
2. Soak the crumb of a small roll in milk
3. Cook the lung very soft and cut it very finely
4. Put it with the soaked roll in the beaten butter
5. Stir well
6. Take ten whole eggs, each well beaten, and some salt
7. Stir in one-half seidel good cream, a little finely cut lemon peel, and finely cut fresh parsley
8. When everything is mixed, stir for a quarter hour
9. Coat a metal dish and mold with butter, put the pudding in, and bake it slowly
