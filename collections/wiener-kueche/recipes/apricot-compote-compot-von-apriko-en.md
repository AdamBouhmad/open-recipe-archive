## Ingredients

- apricots (early ones without removing kernels or skin; later ones with kernels and skin removed)
- water
- sugar (clarified)
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Leave the kernels in early apricots and do not peel them; for later ones, remove the kernels and skin once they are larger.
2. Blanch the apricots lightly in water.
3. Once they become soft, place them on a cloth to drain and cool.
4. Place them in clarified sugar.
5. Bring to the boil several times and skim off the foam.
6. Keep the syrup short cooked.
7. Arrange the apricots on a compote dish and pour the syrup over them.
