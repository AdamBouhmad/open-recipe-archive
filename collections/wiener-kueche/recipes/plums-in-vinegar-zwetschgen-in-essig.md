## Ingredients

- 4 kilos beautiful fresh plums with bloom still on them
- 3/4 liter vinegar
- 1 kilo sugar
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Cloves
- Allspice

## Directions

1. Trim the stems slightly on each plum and pierce each plum five times near the pit
2. Place in an earthen vessel
3. Meanwhile, cook 3/4 liter vinegar with 1 kilo sugar, add cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice tied in a cloth, and let it boil for 1.5 hours
4. Pour over the plums and let them stand overnight
5. On the next day, place them with their juice on the stove, let them become hot and then cool again
6. On the third day, place the plums in preservation jars and pour the juice over them
