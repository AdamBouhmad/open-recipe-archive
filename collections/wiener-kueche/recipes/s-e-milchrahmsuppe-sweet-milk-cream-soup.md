## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- a few drops of cold water
- 2 measures of sweet cream heated with sugar

## Directions

1. Put 6 egg yolks with a few drops of cold water in a pot
2. Pour 2 measures of sweet cream heated with sugar into it while constantly stirring
3. Serve over toasted bread slices
4. If desired, you can also cook some peach leaves or bitter almonds with it for flavor
