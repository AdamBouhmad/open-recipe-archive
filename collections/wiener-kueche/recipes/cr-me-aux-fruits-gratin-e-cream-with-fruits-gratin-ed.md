## Ingredients

- Flour (4 drams)
- Sugar (14 drams)
- Milk (1/4 liter cold)
- Egg yolks (8)
- Egg whites (8)
- Peaches, apricots or similar fruits (halved, pitted)

## Directions

1. Put flour and sugar in a casserole, pour in cold milk slowly and stir over heat until thick
2. Empty into a deep dish and stir until cold
3. Add egg yolks one after another
4. Finally fold in the snow of egg whites
5. Line the bottom of a butter-coated dish so that the fruit halves lie with their skin side down
6. Pour the cream mixture over, sprinkle heavily with finely crushed sugar
7. Bake for 1/4 hour in an open oven
