## Ingredients

- Carp roe
- Parsley
- Celery
- Salt
- Water
- Flour, roasted dark red
- Carp spleen, crushed
- The black matter between spleen and roe
- Marjoram
- Garlic
- Carp blood
- Rye bread, cut lengthwise and roasted

## Directions

1. Cook the roe of a carp with parsley and celery in salted water until well cooked
2. Make a roux with dark red roasted flour
3. Add the crushed spleen and black matter with some marjoram and garlic
4. Finally pour in the carp blood and let the soup come to a boil once more
5. Serve over lengthwise cut, roasted rye bread
