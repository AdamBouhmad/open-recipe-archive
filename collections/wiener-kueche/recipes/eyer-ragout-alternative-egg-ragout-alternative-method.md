## Ingredients

- Eggs, as many as needed
- Butter
- Nutmeg, grated
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallots, chopped
- Thyme, finely grated
- Pea broth (Erbsenbrühe)
- Shallots
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast eggs in hot ashes until completely hard, shell and slice them.
2. Let them fry in butter and add grated nutmeg, salt, pepper, chopped shallots, and finely grated thyme.
3. When the eggs have taken on a brownish color, pour some pea broth over them and salt slightly.
4. Rub a plate with shallots, arrange the ragout on it, and squeeze some lemon juice over it.
