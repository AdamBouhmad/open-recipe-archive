## Ingredients

- 80 dkg Fisch (fish)
- 6 Champignons (mushrooms)
- Butter
- ½ Ringelzwiebel (scallion)
- Sardellen (anchovies)
- Petersilie (parsley)
- 5 dkg Butter (butter)
- Salt
- Salzlkartoffeln (boiled potatoes) for serving

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the fish, clean it, wash it and place it in milk water, then remove and salt well.
2. Make small incisions either on top or on the side and insert either small pieces of mushrooms or anchovies into them.
3. Heat a piece of butter in a roasting pan, add the fish and half a scallion and roast it in the oven for 30-45 minutes, basting frequently with its own juice and adding more liquid as needed.
4. Finely chop the mushrooms and soften them with a small piece of finely chopped onion and parsley in 5 dkg butter.
5. Lift the fish onto a platter, coat with the fines herbes, strain the sauce and serve it separately.
6. Serve boiled potatoes as a side dish.
