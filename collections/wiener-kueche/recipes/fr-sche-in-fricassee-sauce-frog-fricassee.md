## Ingredients

- Approximately 20 frogs
- Salt water
- Butter
- Flour
- Water from cooking the frogs
- Egg yolks
- Soup

## Directions

1. Cook the frogs in salted water until tender
2. Meanwhile, roast flour in hot butter
3. Pour the water in which the frogs were cooked onto this, stirring and cooking well
4. Mix egg yolks with a ladle of cold soup and stir into the sauce
5. Arrange the cooked frog legs in a serving bowl
6. Pour the sauce over them and serve
