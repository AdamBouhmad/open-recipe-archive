## Ingredients

- butter, about the weight of two eggs
- three whole eggs
- one small roll soaked in milk
- one-half Seville orange peel
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat butter, about the weight of two eggs, and three whole eggs well together
2. Add one small roll, finely cut and soaked in milk
3. Rub the peel of one-half Seville orange with sugar
4. Press out the juice and add it
5. Sweeten everything to taste
6. Coat a casserole with butter and sprinkle it with breadcrumbs
7. Pour the pudding in, sprinkle the top again with breadcrumbs, and let it bake
