## Ingredients

- fresh asparagus
- lemon water
- salt
- salt water
- tin foil
- bladders

## Directions

1. Lightly scrape fresh asparagus, break off the woody parts, and rinse briefly in cold water
2. Place in boiling lemon water with a little salt and cook for 3 minutes
3. Transfer to cold water and then place in glasses so that the asparagus heads point upward
4. Pour over with light, cooked, and cooled salt water so that the asparagus is covered 2 fingers high
5. Tie the glasses, which must remain 1 finger width empty at the top, with tin foil and bladders
6. Process in brine for 1/2 hour
7. All types of freshly picked vegetables can be preserved this way
8. When using, strain off the water, place in hot water, and prepare like fresh vegetables with butter or in sauces
