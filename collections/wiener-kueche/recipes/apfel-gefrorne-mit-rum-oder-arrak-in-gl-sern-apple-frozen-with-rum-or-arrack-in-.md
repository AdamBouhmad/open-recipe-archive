## Ingredients

- Apple-Frozen, prepared and finely worked
- 1/4 liter Rum or Arrack
- Stiffly beaten snow from 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. After an Apple-Frozen has been prepared and finely worked, fold it well with 1/4 liter Rum or Arrack and the stiffly beaten snow from 3 egg whites.
2. Work it well and serve in glasses.
