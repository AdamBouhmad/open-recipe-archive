## Ingredients

- 1 large pot of sauerkraut
- about 2 liters cold water
- 10 tablespoons fat
- 1 head of onion, finely cut
- about 10 tablespoons flour
- 1 ladle full of white beef broth
- vinegar or white wine and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cover 1 large pot of sauerkraut with approximately 2 liters of cold water, place on heat and cook very gently
2. In 10 tablespoons of hot fat, roast 1 finely cut head of onion slightly, but it must not turn brown
3. Add approximately 10 tablespoons flour to it, roast under frequent stirring until it has a yellow color
4. Pour the roux onto the broth with 1 ladle full of white beef broth under constant stirring
5. Strain the sauerkraut and add it to the roux, adding as much of the sauerkraut water as needed, stir together and cook well
6. If it should be too little sour, add some vinegar or white wine and salt
7. The sauerkraut must have a pleasantly mild flavor
