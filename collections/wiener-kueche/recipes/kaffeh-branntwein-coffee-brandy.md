## Ingredients

- 4 pounds sugar
- 1 pound roasted and ground coffee beans

## Directions

1. Take 4 pounds sugar and stir in 1 pound roasted and ground coffee beans
2. Let them ferment together
3. Distill the mixture
4. Pour the spirit over another pound of coffee beans
5. Let it macerate with the beans for several days in a well-sealed bottle
