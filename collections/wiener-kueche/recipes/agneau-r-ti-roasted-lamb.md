## Ingredients

- lamb leg or piece
- pepper
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Select a suitable leg or piece, bone it and let it rest for 1 hour.
2. After this time, sprinkle the meat with some pepper and place it on wood pieces or cooking spoons in a roasting pan so that the skin faces up.
3. Baste with hot fat and roast in the oven at strong heat for about 3/4 to 1 hour.
4. When the meat has a golden crust on top, cut it into desired pieces, reassemble beautifully on a deep platter and serve with salad, vegetables or orange slices.
