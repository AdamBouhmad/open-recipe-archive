## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Poultry forcemeat
- Bacon strips
- Spices
- Fine herbs
- Glass of wine
- Anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and gut a pair of nice ducks
2. Break out the bones
3. Season well
4. Beat them quite flat
5. Turn them several times in well-prepared poultry forcemeat
6. Roll them up and carefully bind them with bacon strips
7. Cook them in a braise with some spices, fine herbs, and a glass of wine, short and tender
8. Drain the fat
9. When serving, give anchovy sauce underneath
