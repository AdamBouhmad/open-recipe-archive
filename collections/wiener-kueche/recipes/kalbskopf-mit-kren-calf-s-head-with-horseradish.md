## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Vinegar
- Water
- Salt
- Whole peppercorns
- Sprigs of thyme
- Root vegetables, finely cut

## Directions

1. In equal parts vinegar and water, add some salt, whole peppercorns, some sprigs of thyme, and finely cut root vegetables
2. Cook a prepared calf's head in this liquid
3. When the head is completely tender, remove it and cut into desired pieces
4. Arrange the pieces on a platter, garnish with the root vegetables and grated horseradish
5. Serve with vinegar and oil
