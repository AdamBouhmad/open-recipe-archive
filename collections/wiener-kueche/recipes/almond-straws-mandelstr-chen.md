## Ingredients

- 16 deca finely pounded, passed almonds
- 16 deca sugar
- 1 pinch of finely cut lemon zest
- cinnamon and cloves to equal measure
- 1 table spoon grated chocolate
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix almonds, sugar, lemon zest, cinnamon, cloves, and chocolate together well
2. Lightly fold in the snow from 2 egg whites
3. Roll out the mixture on a sugar-dusted board to the thickness of a small finger
4. Cut out with various cutters in lovely forms, layer 2 by 2 with a little boiled jam in the middle
5. Place on a baking sheet lined with wax or unlarded lard using a knife
6. Brush with beaten eggs, sprinkle with finely pounded sugar, and dry in the preheated oven
