## Ingredients

- 16 Deca flour
- 1/4 liter cold milk
- 16 Deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 Deca powdered sugar
- 8 egg whites (snow)
- Muscateller apples
- jam for filling

## Directions

1. Put 16 Deca flour into a saucepan, pour 1/4 liter cold milk to it while stirring constantly and stir it over the fire until the dough loosens from the spoon, then add 16 Deca butter, stir well and set aside
2. Meanwhile stir 8 egg yolks with 16 Deca powdered sugar for 1/4 hour, add the cooled flour mass, mix well and finally fold in lightly the snow from 8 egg whites
3. Peel Muscateller apples after the core has been removed, fill the resulting empty space with any jam, place them in a buttered form dusted with flour at finger-breadth distance, cover with the flour mass described above and bake for 1/4 hours in the oven
4. Remove the form, take away the rim and arrange the soufflé on the cake plate in a bowl and sprinkle with finely pounded sugar
5. Instead of apples any fruits can be inserted
