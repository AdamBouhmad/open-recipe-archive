## Ingredients

- A tenderloin roast, freed of bones and sinews, salted and peppered
- Fat for coating
- Large raw potatoes
- Butter or fat for roasting
- Brown sauce
- Capers or olives
- Truffles in Madeira wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Coat a tenderloin roast freed of bones and sinews with fat and braise it quickly in a very hot oven for 15 minutes.
2. Cut it into slices as thick as a finger, and cut equally sized pieces from very large raw peeled potatoes.
3. Roast the potato pieces in butter or fat.
4. Coat a shallow casserole with butter, lay a piece of tenderloin on it, then a piece of potato, then another piece of tenderloin, arranging them so that half of each piece covers half of the piece beneath it, continuing until all is arranged.
5. Pour brown sauce over it so it covers everything, and place it in the oven for a quarter hour before serving.
6. Braise it and arrange it on a round dish in a crown shape, and pour the sauce over it.
7. If desired, sprinkle with whole capers or olives cut into pieces and freed from pits.
8. The whole dish can also be poured over with truffles cut into leaves and cooked in Madeira wine at the time of serving.
