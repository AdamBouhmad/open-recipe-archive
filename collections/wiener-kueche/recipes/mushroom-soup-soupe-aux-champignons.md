## Ingredients

- 16 Deca butter or fat
- Flour as needed
- Finely chopped onion
- 2 liters water
- Salt and spices
- 1 plate (about 0.5 liters) finely sliced mushrooms
- 6 Deca butter
- Green parsley
- 0.5 liters milk
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from 16 Deca butter or fat and flour, with finely chopped onion
2. Pour in 2 liters water and cook well
3. Add salt and spices as desired
4. Wash the mushrooms well
5. Sauté them in 6 Deca butter with some green parsley and stew with 0.5 liters milk and vinegar as needed
6. Pour this into the cooked and strained soup
7. Simmer for about 1.5 hours
8. Remove the fat carefully and serve with boiled bread and the mushrooms
9. If mushrooms are not desired in the soup, strain before serving
10. Best mushrooms for soup are champignons, morels, or cultivated varieties
