## Ingredients

- Rhine wine jelly base
- a couple of good cups of genuine Maraschino

## Directions

1. Make it exactly like Rhine wine jelly
2. When it is ready enough to run through the jelly bag, add a couple of good cups of genuine Maraschino
3. Proceed in the manner described above
