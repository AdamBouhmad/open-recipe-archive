## Ingredients

- breadcrumb pieces
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- chopped lemon zest
- fresh egg yolks
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak breadcrumb pieces in milk for about 2 hours until very soft
2. Pass them through a fine sieve
3. Add a little salt, sugar, and chopped lemon zest
4. Beat in as many fresh egg yolks as needed
5. Mix with the breadcrumbs
6. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
7. Coat a casserole with butter
8. Place the mixture inside
9. Bake in the oven or under a cake cover with heat from below and above
10. Turn out onto a serving dish
11. Sprinkle with sugar
12. Glaze with a hot shovel
