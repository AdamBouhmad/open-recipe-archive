## Ingredients

- 21-27 pieces of cherries
- 1 piece clove
- 1 small piece cinnamon
- 1 liter good brandy (corn, wheat or fruit brandy)

## Directions

1. Place cherries, 1 piece clove and 1 small piece cinnamon in a bottle
2. Pour 1 liter good brandy over them
3. Seal the bottle well and proceed as with the Herbal Brandy
