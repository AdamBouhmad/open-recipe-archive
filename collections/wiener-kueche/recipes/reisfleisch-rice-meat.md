## Ingredients

- 50-60 dkg calf's shank or shoulder
- 6 dkg bacon
- 30 dkg rice
- 3 dkg Parmesan
- 6 dkg hot butter or fat
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 knife-tips paprika
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. In 6 dkg hot butter or fat, let the diced bacon, then 1 finely chopped onion turn light brown.
2. Add 2 knife-tips of paprika, the lightly washed, diced, salted meat, pour in a little water and braise 1/2 to 1 hour, adding water and stirring frequently.
3. Add the separately braised rice, pour in a little broth or water, braise another 15 minutes.
4. Mix in the grated cheese and serve on a round or square dish.
