## Ingredients

- one glass Provençal oil
- 3 to 4 slices ham, diced
- several onions, diced
- mushrooms, diced
- garlic cloves, diced
- shallots, diced
- bay leaves
- basil
- thyme
- fine spices
- beef broth
- several spoonfuls capers
- lemon peel
- anchovies
- one glass wine

## Directions

1. On coal fire, braise in one glass of Provençal oil the diced ham, onions, mushrooms, garlic cloves, and shallots with bay leaves, basil, thyme, and fine spices until golden-yellow
2. Pour some beef broth over it
3. Cook it through with several spoonfuls of capers, lemon peel, and anchovies
4. Skim off the foam
5. Add one glass of wine
6. Strain through a hair sieve
