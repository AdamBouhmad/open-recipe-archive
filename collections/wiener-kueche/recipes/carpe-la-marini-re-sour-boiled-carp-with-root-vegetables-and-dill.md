## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter water
- 0.5 liter vinegar
- 1 onion, finely diced
- root vegetables, finely diced
- 1 bay leaf
- some thyme
- pepper
- salt as needed
- one carp
- dill, grated
- mustard or mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a broth of 3/4 liter water and 0.5 liter vinegar with 1 finely diced onion and finely diced root vegetables, 1 bay leaf, some thyme, pepper and necessary salt
2. Let this cook for 1/4 hour
3. While this is cooking, scale the fish, open it, clean it, split it lengthwise and cut it into pieces 2 fingers wide, which are sprinkled with salt
4. Split the head lengthwise in 2 parts and add it to the softened root vegetables
5. After 1/4 hour add the fish pieces and let them come to a boil once, then set them aside covered in the pot
6. Shortly before serving, place the fish on the dish, which can be garnished with the cooked root vegetables and grated dill, and add some of the broth
7. Serve mustard or mustard sauce with it
