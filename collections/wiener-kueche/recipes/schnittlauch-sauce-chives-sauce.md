## Ingredients

- 12 deca bread crusts, soaked in water and pressed out
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- vinegar
- water
- 6 tablespoons oil
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely cut chives
- 1 coffee spoon sugar

## Directions

1. Pound bread soaked in water and pressed out with hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Stir with vinegar and water
4. Mix with 6 tablespoons oil, pepper, 2 tablespoons finely cut chives, and 1 coffee spoon sugar
5. Mix everything well together
