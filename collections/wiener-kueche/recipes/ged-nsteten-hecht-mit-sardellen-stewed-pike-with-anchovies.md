## Ingredients

- Pike
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onions
- shallots
- lemon peel
- anchovies
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as with the previous recipe, then place it whole or cut into pieces in a pot with salt and pepper
2. Add a piece of butter, finely cut onions, shallots, and lemon peel, and braise it well
3. Before it is completely cooked through, add some finely cut anchovies with parsley and a little lemon juice
4. Arrange the pike with this sauce on a serving dish
5. Alternatively, it can be cut into pieces, fried with butter, anchovies, and lemon peel
