## Ingredients

- Onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Glace
- Egg yolk
- Good vinegar or lemon juice
- Spinach juice (a few drops)

## Directions

1. Sweat onion slices cut into pieces with a piece of butter and a couple spoonfuls of flour on low coals.
2. Fill the casserole with good bouillon and add a piece of glace.
3. Cook the whole thing briefly and skim well.
4. Skim off the fat.
5. Finish the sauce with egg yolk, some good vinegar or lemon juice, and a few drops of spinach juice.
