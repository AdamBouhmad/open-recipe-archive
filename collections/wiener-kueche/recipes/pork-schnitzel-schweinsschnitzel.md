## Ingredients

- 40-50 dkg pork leg
- salt
- flour
- egg
- lard for frying
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices and pound
2. Season with salt just before cooking
3. Coat with flour and beaten egg
4. Fry in hot lard until golden brown
5. Drain on paper
6. Arrange on a long platter, garnish with lemon slices, and serve
