## Ingredients

- 6 pieces beef roast
- 1 small bottle Malaga wine or 1/2 liter white wine
- broth
- brown butter sauce

## Directions

1. Pour 1 small bottle of Malaga wine or 1/2 liter white wine and some broth over 6 roast pieces
2. Place a fitting lid on top so the alcohol does not escape
3. Braise until tender
4. Remove the roast, trim neatly, and glaze if desired
5. Reduce the defatted cooking liquid slightly
6. Mix with a few spoonfuls of brown butter sauce
