## Ingredients

- ½ liter milk
- 3 dl milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 piece vanilla
- 14 dkg sugar

## Directions

1. In the mixing bowl, beat 3 dl milk with 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, 1 piece vanilla, and 14 dkg sugar over gentle heat until weakly thick.
2. Strain the cream through a sieve, cool it down, fill it into a mixing bowl or freezing container, and freeze it.
3. If the ice is made in the mixing bowl, place it in a container with well-salted ice and let the mass stiffen under continuous turning of the mixing bowl.
