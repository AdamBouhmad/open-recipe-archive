## Ingredients

- 8 ounces butter
- 8 ounces flour
- 1/4 liter white wine
- equal amount of white broth

## Directions

1. Toast equal amounts of flour in 8 ounces butter
2. Pour this with 1/4 liter white wine and equal amount of white broth
3. Stir well together and let it cook well
