## Ingredients

- Bacon or other fat (finely sliced)
- Onion (1 piece)
- Parsley
- Thyme (5 bunches)
- Juniper berries (5 pieces)
- Bacon slices
- Wine
- Soup broth
- Black bread (8 ounces, thinly sliced)
- Olives (12 pieces, peeled and pitted)
- Cream

## Directions

1. Put finely sliced bacon or other fat into a casserole, also 1 piece of onion, some parsley, thyme thinly sliced, 5 bunches of thyme, 5 pieces of juniper berries, then lay the dressed and salted poultry on it, cover it with bacon slices, and let it fry a little covered.
2. Pour over with as much wine and broth that the liquid covers it, add 8 ounces of thinly sliced black bread, and let the poultry braise until tender; then remove it, defat and strain the liquid, and add 12 pieces of peeled and pitted olives and some cream, adjust the taste if necessary with lemon juice, and serve this sauce with the poultry.
3. All kinds of poultry, especially older poultry, can be prepared in this manner.
