## Ingredients

- Beautiful firm cabbage
- Salt water
- Yellow roux
- Finely cut onion
- Pepper
- Broth
- Rice
- Goose fat
- White paper
- Ham, finely cut into triangular pieces
- 5 spoons of cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 Seidel of good broth
- Smoked beef

## Directions

1. Wash the cabbage well and cook in salt water
2. Make a yellow roux, let some finely cut onion brown slightly in the roux, add the cabbage and some pepper, pour some broth over it
3. Cook until the sauce is completely reduced
4. Stew the rice separately
5. Coat a pan with goose fat, cut white paper to fit the bottom and coat with the same fat
6. Line the bottom with triangular thin-cut ham pieces arranged so nothing of the bottom shows
7. Add a layer of rice, then a layer of cabbage (stems must be removed), alternating until the pan is full
8. Pour 5 spoons of cream, 6 egg yolks and 1/2 Seidel of good broth (all well mixed) over
9. Place in steam and cook for a good hour
10. Carefully turn onto a serving dish
11. Remove the paper and garnish with beautifully cut smoked beef
