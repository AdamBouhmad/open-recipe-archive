## Ingredients

- 1 carp, scaled, gutted and cleaned
- 1 small fresh eel
- 8 decilitres butter
- 1 large Spanish onion
- 1 piece yellow turnip
- parsley and celery, all finely chopped
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 sprigs thyme
- 5 grains of each type of spice
- 1 spice clove
- some nutmeg
- 1 litre red wine
- 1/2 litre broth
- 16 decilitres butter
- 12 decilitres flour
- small roasted onions
- stewed mushrooms
- crayfish cooked in wine
- toasted bread pieces

## Directions

1. Scale, gut and clean 1 carp, then cut it and 1 small fresh eel into 2-finger wide pieces
2. In a casserole, place 8 decilitres butter with 1 large Spanish onion, 1 piece yellow turnip, parsley and celery, all finely chopped, 1 bay leaf, 4 sprigs thyme, 5 grains of each type of spice, 1 spice clove, and some nutmeg
3. Layer the carp and eel pieces and small pieces of calf's brain on top
4. Pour 1 litre red wine and 1/2 litre broth over
5. Mix 16 decilitres butter with 12 decilitres flour and place on top
6. Simmer covered for approximately 3/4 hour, then set aside for 1/4 hour
7. Arrange the fish pieces on a platter, garnish with small roasted onions, stewed mushrooms, crayfish cooked in wine, and toasted bread pieces
8. Pour the sauce, which must be thick and defatted, over the top
