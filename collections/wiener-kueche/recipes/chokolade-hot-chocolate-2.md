## Ingredients

- 2 loth chocolate per cup
- water or milk

## Directions

1. Allow 2 loth chocolate per chocolate cup, cut or grated on a grater
2. Place in a pot and let come to a boil
3. Remove from the fire and let stand a little
4. Place cups in hot water
5. Stir the chocolate with a stirrer or whisk into foam
6. Use a small foam spoon to fill the cups with foam until they are completely heaped full
7. Optionally, lightly bake the foam with a glowing shovel held half an inch above the cups to prevent it from settling too quickly
8. Serve mostly with rusks or finely toasted roll
9. Milk can be used instead of water with the same procedure
10. If making chocolate in Rhenish wine or Champagne, ensure the Champagne does not burn, do not spare the sugar, and allow for 1 egg yolk per cup to whip the chocolate with
