## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt water
- lemon juice
- cold mustard sauce
- parsley or watercress for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the artichokes as previously described, cook them in salted water with lemon juice and let them cool in their broth
2. Before serving, arrange them so they stand easily and securely and the whole looks fan-like
3. Place cold mustard sauce in the center and decorate the platter with peeled gherkins, capers, marinated fish, olives, and if available, small fatty pieces
4. Sprinkle the artichokes with cold mustard sauce and cover with parsley or watercress
5. Serve as an appetizer or cold dish
