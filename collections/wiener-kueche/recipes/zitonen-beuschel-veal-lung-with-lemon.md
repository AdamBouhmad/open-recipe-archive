## Ingredients

- 2 lamb or 1 veal lung
- Salt water
- 16 deca butter
- Flour
- Parsley
- Onion
- 1/2 liter broth
- 1/4 liter vinegar

## Directions

1. Poach 2 lamb lungs or 1 veal lung in salt water until tender
2. Remove and when cooled, cut into noodle-like pieces
3. Roast equal amounts of flour in butter, add finely cut parsley and onion if desired
4. When brown, pour in 1/2 liter broth and 1/4 liter vinegar and stir well together
