## Ingredients

- 6 large apples
- cold water
- lemon juice or vinegar
- dissolved alum (optional)
- 1 liter cold water
- 6 ounces sugar
- juice from ½ lemon
- 8 ounces grated rolls soaked in cold water and well pressed out
- salt
- 2 tablespoons grated horseradish
- 1 tablespoon oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut 6 large apples into quarters, peel them and place in 1 liter of cold water which has been soured with lemon juice or vinegar (or alternatively a pinch of dissolved alum can be added)
2. This souring keeps the apples white
3. Meanwhile, place 1 liter of cold water, 6 ounces sugar, and the juice of ½ lemon in a casserole
4. As soon as this sugar water boils, add the apples, cover them and let them stew until soft
5. Place the apples on a sieve and pass 8 ounces of grated rolls soaked in cold water and well pressed out into a deep bowl
6. When the apples are completely softened, add some salt, 2 tablespoons grated horseradish, 1 tablespoon oil, and vinegar as needed, and stir into a thick sauce
7. If it should be not sweet enough, add the necessary sugar
