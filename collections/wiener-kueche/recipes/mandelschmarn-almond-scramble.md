## Ingredients

- 14 deka butter
- 12 deka finely ground sugar
- 6 deka peeled almonds with 1 egg yolk, finely ground
- 7 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter milk
- 14 deka flour
- 6 egg whites (snow)
- 10 deka very hot butter

## Directions

1. Beat 14 deka butter until fluffy
2. Mix in 12 deka finely ground sugar, 6 deka ground almonds, and 7 egg yolks, one after another
3. Slowly mix in 1/2 liter milk and 14 deka flour alternately, always some milk and some flour
4. Gently fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Pour this dough into a casserole containing 10 deka very hot butter
6. Treat further as described for the kleine Kaiserschmarn
