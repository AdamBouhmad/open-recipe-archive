## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter flour
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- water
- 8 decagrams hot butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 liter flour on a board, make a hollow in the middle
2. Put 1 teaspoon salt, 2 whole eggs and if necessary some cold water in it
3. Work this into a smooth dough as described in 'Feudelsuppe'
4. On a board dusted with flour, roll the small dough pieces knife-back thick
5. When they are dried, cut them into 3-finger-wide strips
6. When these are evenly piled on top of each other, cut them into knife-back-wide noodles
7. Put the noodles in boiling salt water and cook covered until they come to the surface
8. Pour them into a colander, rinse with cold water
9. When the water has drained well, put them in 8 decagrams very hot butter
10. Salt to taste and leave in, stirring often, until the noodles are hot
11. Arrange raised on a dish and serve
12. These noodles can also be served with grated Parmesan cheese if desired
13. The noodles can also be put directly from boiling water into the hot fat
