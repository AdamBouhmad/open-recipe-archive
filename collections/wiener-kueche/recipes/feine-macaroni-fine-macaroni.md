## Ingredients

- 16 deca macaroni
- salt water
- 2 liters beef broth
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. First, cook 16 deca macaroni for a few minutes in salt water
2. Drain them, then add them to 2 liters of boiling beef broth
3. Let them simmer for approximately 15 minutes so they swell
4. When serving the soup, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
