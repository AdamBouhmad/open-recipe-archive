## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons French mustard
- 1 tablespoon rose hip marmalade
- sugar
- pepper as desired
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 3 tablespoons French mustard in a sauce boat
2. Add 1 tablespoon rose hip marmalade
3. Mix well together
4. Add necessary sugar
5. Add pepper and salt as desired
