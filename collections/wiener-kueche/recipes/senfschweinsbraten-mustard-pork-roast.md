## Ingredients

- 60-80 dekagrams pork shoulder
- 5 dekagrams bacon
- 1/2 liter cream
- 4 dekagrams fat
- 1 onion
- salt
- paprika
- parsley
- 2 tablespoons French mustard
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt it, lard it with bacon rolled in paprika and finely chopped parsley
2. In a roasting pan, brown a finely chopped onion in 4 dekagrams hot fat until light golden
3. Add the meat, pour in broth or water and roast for 1½-2 hours under frequent basting with its own juice and additional liquid
4. Remove the meat, defat the juice, dust it, strain it, mix with 1/2 liter cream and 2 tablespoons French mustard, bring to a boil and serve with the meat
