## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pfund rendered kidney fat
- 1 Pfund raisins
- 4 Pfund sugar
- 3 round bread rolls
- milk

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 pound rendered kidney fat until foamy
2. Stir in 1 pound raisins, 4 pounds sugar, and 3 round bread rolls from which the crust has been removed, cut into pieces, soaked in milk, and squeezed out
3. This pudding can either be cooked tied in a napkin or baked slowly in a butter-greased rimmed baking dish
