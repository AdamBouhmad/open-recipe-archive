## Ingredients

- 12 to 15 pieces beautiful peaches
- 28 deca grams sugar
- 1/8 liter cold water
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Almond essence or Maraschino liqueur

## Directions

1. Remove the stones from the peaches and pass them through a fine sieve into a deep bowl
2. Add 28 deca grams sugar dissolved in 1/8 liter cold water and the juice of 2 lemons to this mass
3. Stir in some almond essence or Maraschino liqueur so the liquid obtains a pale pink color
4. Pass the mass again through a sieve, weigh it to 22 degrees, and proceed further as with the 'Ordinary Strawberry Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1746)
