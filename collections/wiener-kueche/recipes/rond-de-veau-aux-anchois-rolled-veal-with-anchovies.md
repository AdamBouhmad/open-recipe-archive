## Ingredients

- 2.5 kilo veal from the knuckle
- cleaned anchovies, half-finger-length pieces
- twine
- yellow turnips, thinly sliced
- parsley
- leek
- lard, cut into cubes
- 1/2 liter wine
- 1/8 liter vinegar
- 10 deca cleaned anchovies
- 8 deca capers
- parsley
- 1 clove garlic, crushed, if desired
- vinegar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 10 deca hot oil

## Directions

1. Pound the veal thin as paper after removing all sinews and skin, thread it with cleaned, half-finger-length pieces of anchovies, roll it up, and bind it with twine
2. Line a casserole with thinly sliced turnips, parsley, leek, cubed lard, and the trimmings from the anchovies wrapped in cheesecloth
3. Lay the bound veal on top, add 1/2 liter wine and 1/8 liter vinegar, and braise gently covered until tender
4. Set aside and let cool in the cooking liquid
5. Cut into thin slices and arrange on a platter
6. For the sauce: finely cut 10 deca cleaned anchovies with 8 deca capers and parsley, add crushed garlic if desired, vinegar, and juice of 2 lemons to a casserole containing 10 deca hot oil, bring to a boil once, and pour over the meat
7. Serve warm or cold with macaroni, cold aspic, and lemon wedges
