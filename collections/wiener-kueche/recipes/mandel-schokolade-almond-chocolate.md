## Ingredients

- 16 drams brown sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16 drams blanched almonds (finely ground with 1 egg white)
- 4 egg whites
- 6 drams flour
- butter for greasing the baking sheet
- warm candied peel for filling
- powdered sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat 16 drams brown sugar and 8 egg yolks together for 1/8 hour
2. Add 16 drams blanched almonds (finely ground with 1 egg white) and beat well
3. Lightly fold in the stiffly beaten snow of 4 egg whites with 6 drams flour
4. Spread the mixture thickly on a baking sheet greased with butter
5. Bake at moderate heat in the oven until pale yellow
6. Remove the dough from the sheet with a very thin long knife
7. Brush with warm candied peel, fold the dough 2 fingers wide on the longer side, brush again with candied peel, and roll up completely
8. Dry for a few more minutes in the oven
9. Remove and dust with sugar after cooling completely, then cut into pieces of desired size
10. Alternatively, tortes can be made: spread the dough the thickness of a knife back on the torte sheet, bake until light yellow, and continue as desired
