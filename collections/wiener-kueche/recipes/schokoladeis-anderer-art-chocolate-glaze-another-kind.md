## Ingredients

- 24 drams of fine chocolate
- 1/4 liter of water
- 24 drams of sugar
- 1/8 liter of water

## Directions

1. Put 24 drams of fine chocolate in a saucepan, pour 1/4 liter of water into it and let it dissolve on the fire, stir the mass finely and then add 24 drams of sugar and 1/8 liter of water and mix this well.
2. Let this mass boil until a thin thread pulls when you dampen thumb and forefinger with it.
3. Take it from the fire and stir it in the same saucepan for about 1/2 hour, then pour the glaze over the cake and let it dry.
4. This glaze should be brown, shiny and mirror-smooth.
5. If double-roasted chocolate is used, the glaze will be black.
6. You can also add 3 drops of lemon juice, after which the glaze must not boil any longer.
