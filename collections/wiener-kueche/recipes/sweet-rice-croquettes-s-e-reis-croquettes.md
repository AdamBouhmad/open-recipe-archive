## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk or cream
- 32 tablespoons rice
- 16 tablespoons sugar
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 16 tablespoons blanched almonds
- some milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- flour for coating
- breadcrumbs for coating
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Pour 1 liter milk or cream into a saucepan and add 32 tablespoons riced rice, letting it cook covered for a long time
2. During cooking, add 16 tablespoons sugar, 12 tablespoons butter, and 1 pinch of salt
3. Meanwhile, grind 16 tablespoons of blanched almonds with some milk into a dough and add to the rice mass, stir well and bring to a boil once
4. Pour into a deep bowl and cover with buttered paper
5. After completely cooling, stir in 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
6. Place the rice mass on a flour-dusted board and form thick sticks of equal size
7. Dip in beaten egg and roll in breadcrumbs
8. Fry in very hot lard with frequent shaking of the pan until evenly golden
9. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on blotting paper, then arrange in a bowl covered with a napkin
