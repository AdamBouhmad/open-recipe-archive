## Ingredients

- 1/3 liter of flour
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter of cream
- 30 tablespoons of cooked, lean, finely sliced ham
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 1/3 liter of flour
2. Cut the thinly rolled sheets into arbitrarily large squares
3. Add to boiling salted water and cook covered until they rise to the surface of the water
4. Drain in a colander and rinse with cold water and let stand
5. Place 8 egg yolks in a deep bowl
6. Add 1/2 liter of cream gradually
7. Add 30 tablespoons of cooked, lean, finely sliced ham
8. Add the drained noodles and 1/2 teaspoon of salt one after another
9. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 8 eggs
10. Fill a buttered, floured mold with this mixture
