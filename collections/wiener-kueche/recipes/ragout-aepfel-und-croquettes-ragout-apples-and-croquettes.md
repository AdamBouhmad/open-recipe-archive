## Ingredients

- Ragout mixture (from Ragout Croquettes recipe)
- Flour for dusting
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Use the same preparation as for Ragout Croquettes, with the difference that instead of sugar hats, form apples, pears or croquettes from the mixture in any desired size
2. Continue as with the Ragout Croquettes
