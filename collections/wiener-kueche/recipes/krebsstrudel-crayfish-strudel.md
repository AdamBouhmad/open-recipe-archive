## Ingredients

- dough as in previous recipe
- 0.5 pound crayfish butter
- crayfish mousseline made from 6 eggs
- 20 small crayfish tails
- 0.25 pound butter
- 0.25 pound crayfish butter
- 1 bread roll soaked in milk
- 2 eggs and 2 yolks
- sugar
- sweet simmering cream and sugar for cooking

## Directions

1. Make dough as in previous recipe and stretch it out similarly
2. Make crayfish butter and a crayfish mousseline from 6 eggs
3. Chop 20 small crayfish tails very fine
4. Work 0.25 pound butter with the mousseline and chopped tails, adding sugar
5. Add to the crayfish butter a bread roll soaked in milk, 2 eggs and 2 yolks, and work together
6. Spread this filling thinly on the strudel
7. Roll together as before and place in a pan brushed with crayfish butter
8. Pour sweet simmering cream and sugar around it
9. Apply heat above and below
10. Serve when beautifully browned
