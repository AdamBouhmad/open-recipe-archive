## Ingredients

- 1 roasting chicken
- 2 large mushrooms
- 1 small head cauliflower
- fat
- 5 dkg butter
- parsley
- flour
- soup
- salt
- optionally: peas, asparagus heads, sweetbreads, cock combs, goose liver, tongue

## Directions

1. Place the chicken prepared for roasting in a pan with some fat and roast it in the oven on both sides for 30 minutes.
2. Divide it into 8 pieces, optionally remove the skin.
3. Cook the stomach and liver separately for 1 hour.
4. Cook mushrooms and cauliflower beforehand for 20 minutes.
5. Make a butter mixture of 5 dkg butter, parsley, and flour.
6. Dilute with the soup in which the stomach was cooked or with the cauliflower soup.
7. Add the chicken and salt.
8. Cook well for 20 minutes.
9. Cut the stomach and liver into cubes, add cut mushrooms and cauliflower, but do not stir too much anymore.
10. Serve on a round dish. Optional garnishes: cooked young green peas, asparagus heads cooked separately, blanched cut sweetbreads, cooked cock combs, fried goose liver, and finely cut tongue can be mixed in.
