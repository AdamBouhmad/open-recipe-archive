## Ingredients

- 3 measures sweet cream
- strong black coffee
- sugar
- 9 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 3 measures sweet cream well
2. Pour in strong black coffee, colored as if one wanted to drink it
3. Sweeten sufficiently with sugar
4. Beat 9 egg yolks in a clean pot, beat well
5. Pour in the mixed coffee gradually and beat vigorously
6. Pass through a sieve
7. Treat as the pistachio cream above
