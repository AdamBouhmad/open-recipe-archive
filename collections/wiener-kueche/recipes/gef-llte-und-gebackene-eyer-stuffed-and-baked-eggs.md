## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- breadcrumbs
- almonds
- raisins
- sugar
- cinnamon
- fresh eggs
- lard
- wine
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise
2. Mash the yolks in a bowl
3. Mix in finely grated breadcrumbs, thinly sliced almonds, small raisins, sugar, and cinnamon
4. Bind with a couple of fresh eggs
5. Fill the egg white halves with the mixture so it is heaped up
6. Fry them in lard
7. Heat wine in a small pan with sugar and a little fine seasoning
8. Arrange the eggs on a serving dish
9. Pour the wine sauce over them
