## Ingredients

- cock's combs
- warm water
- bouillon
- bacon slice
- lemon slice
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- fricassee or Sauce Espagnole
- white bread croutons

## Directions

1. Clean the cock's combs in lukewarm water to make them white
2. Remove the skin
3. Lay them in warm water for a while
4. Cook them in bouillon with a bacon slice, a lemon slice, salt, pepper, and a piece of butter the size of a walnut that has been rolled in flour
5. When they are soft, serve these cock's combs in fricassee or Sauce Espagnole
6. Garnish with croutons of white bread fried in butter cut in the style of cock's combs
