## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 16 deka vanilla sugar
- paper cups or piping bags

## Directions

1. After stirring 6 egg yolks with 16 deka vanilla sugar for 1 hour, fill this mass into paper cups which are placed on a baking sheet in a cool oven and let the sugar mass dry
2. Instead of in paper cups, one can also fill this mass into piping bags, which are then placed in bran or sand on a baking sheet and similarly dried in the cool oven
3. This dried sugar mass can be served in the cups or bags or one can dissolve it from the paper and serve it mixed with other baked goods
