## Ingredients

- 21 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- egg white for brushing
- beaten egg
- cooked fruit such as apples, pears, peaches, plums, or cherries

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from 21 dkg flour, 14 dkg butter, and 7 dkg sugar.
2. After resting, roll it out thinly.
3. Cut out rounds with a large cutter and brush the edges with egg white.
4. Shape thin rolls from the dough scraps and place them on the edges of the rounds.
5. Brush with beaten egg and place on an unbuttoned baking sheet.
6. Bake at high heat until light brown.
7. Once cool, top with various stewed or freshly cooked fruits.
8. These tartlets are better made a day ahead.
