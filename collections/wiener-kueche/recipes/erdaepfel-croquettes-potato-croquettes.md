## Ingredients

- 12 tablespoons butter
- 1.5 liters riced potatoes
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy and mix well with the riced potatoes
2. Gradually add 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
3. Season with salt, nutmeg and finely cut lemon zest
4. Place this dough on a flour-dusted board and form small sausages 1 finger long and 2 fingers thick
5. Fry these in very hot lard
6. These croquettes are served as garnish for roasted beef or boiled beef
7. Dumplings can also be formed from this mixture
