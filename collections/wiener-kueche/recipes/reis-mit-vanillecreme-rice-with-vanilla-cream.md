## Ingredients

- 16 Deca washed rice
- 7.5 liters milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 0.5 liters milk
- 24 Deca finely pounded vanilla sugar
- 2 Deca gelatin
- 0.25 liters cold water
- 1.5 liters heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook 16 Deca washed rice in 7.5 liters milk until very thick and set aside covered.
2. Beat 8 egg yolks, 0.5 liters milk, and 24 Deca finely pounded vanilla sugar together in a saucepan over heat, stirring until it begins to thicken, but do not allow to boil.
3. Press this mixture through a sieve into the rice and stir well together so that each grain of rice is separate, then place the rice on the stove.
4. Dissolve 2 Deca gelatin with 0.25 liters cold water in a saucepan over heat, stirring until completely dissolved and steaming hot, but do not allow to boil.
5. Add the dissolved gelatin together with the rice in a saucepan and stir both over low heat until well mixed, but do not allow to boil.
6. Transfer this mass to a deep bowl and stir until it cools.
7. Once cooled, whip 1.5 liters heavy cream to stiff peaks.
8. When the prepared cream feels lukewarm, fold the whipped cream in as quickly as possible until completely mixed.
9. Immediately transfer into a mold brushed with fine oil, which has been buried in ice 1 hour earlier.
10. Cover the mold with a plate and place a cloth over it.
11. After approximately 2 hours the cream will be sufficiently set.
12. Remove the mold from the ice, wipe it, press the cream away from the sides of the mold with your fingers, and turn the cream out onto a flat plate.
13. If it does not turn out easily, dip the mold briefly in hot water, wipe it dry, and turn out.
14. If the cream does not look quite beautiful with visible oil, quickly wipe it with a clean cloth.
15. Garnish with meringues, Spanish canes, small pastries, or fruit cooked in sugar.
