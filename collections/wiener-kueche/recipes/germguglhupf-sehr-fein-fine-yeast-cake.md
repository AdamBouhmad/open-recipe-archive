## Ingredients

- 14 dkg butter
- 8 dkg sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- lemon zest
- 20 dkg warmed flour
- a pinch of salt
- 3 spoonfuls lukewarm milk or cream
- yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast
- 4 dkg currants

## Directions

1. Beat the butter for 15 minutes
2. Gradually mix in 8 dkg sugar, 3 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk and lemon zest
3. Add 20 dkg warmed flour and a pinch of salt, 3 spoonfuls lukewarm milk or cream, and yeast leaven from 1½ dkg yeast
4. Mix the dough well
5. Beat it for several more minutes
6. Fold in 4 dkg currants
7. Fill the dough into the mold
8. Let it rise covered
9. Bake it in weaker heat until light brown
