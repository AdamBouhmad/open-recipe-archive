## Ingredients

- 1/2 maß apricot sauce
- 3/4 pound almonds, not too finely ground
- 6 whole eggs
- 14 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- zest of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. In a bowl, combine 1/2 maß apricot sauce and 3/4 pound not too finely ground almonds
2. Stir for a quarter hour
3. Beat 6 whole eggs and 14 egg yolks gradually into the mixture
4. Stir well
5. Add sugar to taste and the zest of 2 lemons
6. Stir it on one side slowly and beautifully
7. Fill into a well-sealed ring mold, as it tends to flow out
8. Bake slowly and cool for one hour
