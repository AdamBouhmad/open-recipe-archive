## Ingredients

- 10 Deca butter
- Finely chopped onion
- Flour
- 1.5 liters water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. In 10 Deca butter, add 1 teaspoon of finely chopped onion
2. Let it roast somewhat
3. Add flour as needed, about 3-4 tablespoons, and roast together
4. Pour in 1.5 liters water and as much vinegar as required
5. Cook well
6. Pass through a fine sieve
7. Add salt and nutmeg
8. Turnips, noodles or similar can be cooked in the soup
