## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter or lard
- Equal amount of flour
- 4 tablespoons finely chopped dill herb
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- Necessary soup
- 1/4 liter sour cream

## Directions

1. In 8 tablespoons butter or lard add equal amount of flour
2. Let it roast with 4 tablespoons finely chopped dill herb and 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
3. Pour with the necessary soup and 1/4 liter sour cream, simmer and let cook well
