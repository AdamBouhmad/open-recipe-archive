## Ingredients

- 60-80 dkg veal leg
- salt
- flour
- egg
- lard for frying
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Pound the veal slices and season with salt
2. Dredge first in flour, then in beaten egg
3. Fry in hot lard
4. Serve the same way as pork schnitzel, garnished with lemon slices
