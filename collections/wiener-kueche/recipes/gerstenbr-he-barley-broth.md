## Ingredients

- 40 deka barley grains
- 1 liter cold water
- Sugar
- Wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the barley grains somewhat, then put them in 1 liter of cold water.
2. Let them boil until they burst.
3. Let them stand for some time until the thick part has settled to the bottom.
4. Strain it through a fine sieve or a cloth and add sugar, wine, and lemon juice as desired.
