## Ingredients

- 1 bread roll made from bread dough
- 8 dkg ham, not too finely chopped
- 4 dkg tongue, not too finely chopped
- 3 anchovies, not too finely chopped
- 1 pickled cucumber, not too finely chopped
- 2 hard eggs, not too finely chopped
- 1 hard egg yolk, not too finely chopped
- 2 spoonfuls capers, not too finely chopped
- 10 dkg butter
- salt
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut off one end of the bread roll
2. Completely hollow out the roll with a long knife
3. Chop ham, tongue, anchovies, pickled cucumber, hard eggs, hard egg yolk, and capers not too finely
4. Mix with 10 dkg butter
5. Add salt and mustard
6. Fill the bread roll with this mixture
7. Press together
8. Wrap in parchment paper and a cloth
9. Let it lie in a cool place for 1-2 days
10. Cut into thin slices
11. Arrange slices in a wreath shape on a glass platter
