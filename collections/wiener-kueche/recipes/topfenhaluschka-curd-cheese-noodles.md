## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- water
- 20 dkg curd cheese
- 12-15 dkg bacon

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough and cut into larger fleckerln
2. Cook in boiling salted water for 5 minutes, drain and shock
3. Cut the bacon into small dice and roast until light yellow
4. Strain the fat into a large casserole
5. Add the fleckerln, salt them and warm in the oven
6. Arrange on a round platter, sprinkle with strained curd cheese and top with bacon cracklings
