## Ingredients

- 28 deca vanilla sugar
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Lightly mix 28 deca vanilla sugar with the stiff snow of 8 egg whites
2. Fill into a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour
3. Cover with parchment paper if desired
4. Cook covered for 1/2 to 3/4 hour in a water bath
5. Turn out onto a platter
6. Pour desired dissolved and cooled marmalade over it
7. Serve immediately
