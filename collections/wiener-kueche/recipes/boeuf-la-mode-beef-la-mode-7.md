## Ingredients

- 70-80 dkg black chuck or oxtail
- 5 dkg bacon
- 1 pickled cucumber
- 1 parsley root
- 1 yellow turnip
- 6 h root vegetables
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion
- bay leaf
- thyme
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- broth or water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, salt and pepper it
2. Cut yellow turnip, parsley root, cucumber, and bacon into finger-thick pieces
3. Make holes in the meat and insert the cut vegetables and bacon
4. In a casserole, roast the finely cut root vegetables, onion, bay leaf, thyme, peppercorns and spice berries in 6 dkg hot fat until dark
5. Add the meat, pour broth or water over it
6. Braise covered for 2 to 2½ hours, basting frequently and turning occasionally
7. Remove the meat and cut into thin pieces
8. Strain the sauce, season with vinegar
9. Bring the meat and sauce to a boil once more and serve on a long platter
