## Ingredients

- 1-2 goose livers
- hot fat
- swollen almonds, cut into sticks
- cloves
- optionally: onion rings
- rice as side dish

## Directions

1. The liver should be nice and rosy. If not completely fresh, wash it and place in cold milk for 2 hours, then wipe dry, otherwise just wipe with melted fat.
2. You can lard it with swollen almonds cut into sticks and cloves.
3. Place it with the rounded side down in a casserole with some hot fat.
4. Let it braise covered for 20-30 minutes, turn it and let it brown on this side as well.
5. Transfer to a dish and salt it.
6. Pour the remaining fat over it.
7. Optionally, let an onion ring turn golden in the fat beforehand.
8. Serve with rice as a side dish. You can also garnish it beautifully: fill a rice mold rinsed with hot water with 30 dkg braised rice, turn it out onto a dish, place the liver in the middle on top, pour the sauce over it, and arrange cooked cauliflower or cabbage sprouts around it.
