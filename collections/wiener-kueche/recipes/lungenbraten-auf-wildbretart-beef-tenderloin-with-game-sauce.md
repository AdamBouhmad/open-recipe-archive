## Ingredients

- large beef tenderloin
- 3 tablespoons table oil
- fat and skin
- 2 small onions
- 1 small piece yellow turnip and celery
- 15 dried plums
- 16 deca black bread
- 10 juniper berries
- spice grains
- 1 clove
- 5 sprigs thyme
- 2 pieces crushed rocamboles
- 20 fresh or dried rosehips or 2 tablespoons rosehip sauce
- 1 liter red wine
- 1/2 liter broth
- cream
- 1/4 liter cream
- 4 deca sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- capers
- puff pastry
- purée
- macaroni
- beef marrow dumplings
- parsley
- watercress

## Directions

1. Skin a removed, cut, somewhat peppered and spitted large beef tenderloin
2. Place on a platter and cover with 3 tablespoons table oil
3. Meanwhile, line a casserole with the removed fat and skin, 2 small finely cut onions, 1 small piece yellow turnip and celery, 15 dried plums without pits, 16 deca finely cut black bread, 10 crushed juniper berries, 5 grains of each type of spice, 1 clove, 5 sprigs thyme, 2 pieces crushed rocamboles and 20 pieces fresh or dried rosehips or 2 tablespoons rosehip sauce
4. Place the spitted and oiled tenderloin on top, braise covered for 1/2 hour, then add 1 liter red wine and 1/2 liter broth and braise covered slowly for 1.5 hours
5. Remove the fat, place it in another casserole and lay the tenderloin covered with cream on top to roast uncovered in the oven for 15-30 minutes so it gets a nice color
6. To the sauce with the root vegetables add 1/4 liter cream and 4 deca sugar, let it boil, strain and let it boil again
7. If the sauce is too thin, mix the cream with 1 tablespoon of flour and add it, then strain and boil
8. Garnish the tenderloin on the platter with game pâté or variously cut puff pastry, fine purée, macaroni, beef marrow dumplings, or give a mixed garnish
9. Decorate the ends of the platter with sprigs of green parsley or watercress
10. Mix the sauce, which must be piquant and thick, with 1 tablespoon capers and serve in a sauce boat after removing the fat
11. This tenderloin serves 10 to 12 persons
