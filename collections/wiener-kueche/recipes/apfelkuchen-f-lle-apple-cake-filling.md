## Ingredients

- ¼ kg apples
- 6-8 pieces cube sugar
- small amount of water
- 50 grams raisins

## Directions

1. Peel and core the apples, cut into thin slices
2. Stew gently with cube sugar and a small amount of water until soft
3. Mix in raisins
4. Use filling only when cooled and without liquid
