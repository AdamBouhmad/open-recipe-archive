## Ingredients

- cacao beans and shells
- 1/2 liter water
- 3 spoonfuls cacao
- cold water
- milk and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Roast cacao beans and shells, pound and mix them
2. Place 1/2 liter water on the fire, when it boils add 3 spoonfuls cacao and stir until it boils again
3. After 10 to 15 minutes pour in some cold water and let the cacao rest quietly to the side until it is clear
4. Serve with milk and sugar
