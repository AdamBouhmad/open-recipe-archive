## Ingredients

- approximately 2 measures or 4 pounds ripe wild strawberries
- 1/2 measure or 1 pound raspberries
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 measures water
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take approximately 2 measures or 4 pounds ripe wild strawberries and 1/2 measure or 1 pound raspberries
2. Place them in an earthenware vessel
3. Crush them
4. Add the juice of 1 lemon
5. Pour 2 measures water over it
6. Let it stand for half an hour
7. Press it through a napkin
8. Sweeten to taste
9. Pass it through a hair sieve
