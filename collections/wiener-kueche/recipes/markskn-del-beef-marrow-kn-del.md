## Ingredients

- 14 deca finely cut marrow
- 3 grated bread rolls
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- some salt
- 20 deca flour
- approximately 1/8 liter milk

## Directions

1. Place the finely cut marrow in a deep bowl and chill it.
2. After 10 minutes, beat it until fluffy.
3. Stir in 3 bread rolls that have been grated and cut into fine dice and soaked in 1/8 liter milk, 2 whole eggs, 2 egg yolks, and some salt.
4. Mix in approximately 20 deca flour.
5. Form knödel of any desired size and cook them in salted water.
6. If desired, a knife point of nutmeg can also be added.
