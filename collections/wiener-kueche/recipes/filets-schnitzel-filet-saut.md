## Ingredients

- Finger-thick slices from a well-aged beef roast or tenderloin, 3 fingers wide and 1 finger long
- Salt and pepper
- Hot lard for frying
- Parsley or tarragon butter
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Cut finger-thick slices from a well-aged beef roast or tenderloin that are 3 fingers wide and 1 finger long
2. Cut them neatly, beat, salt and pepper them
3. Fry quickly in hot lard
4. When serving, place a piece of parsley or tarragon butter and a lemon slice on each slice
