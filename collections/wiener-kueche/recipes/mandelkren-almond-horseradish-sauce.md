## Ingredients

- 1 quarter-litre good milk cream
- one small spoonful flour
- one ladle broth
- one handful pounded and swollen almonds
- one piece sugar
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 quarter-litre of good milk cream with one small spoonful of flour
2. Add one ladle broth, one handful of pounded and swollen almonds with one piece of sugar
3. Finally add some grated horseradish
4. Stir well again and bring to the fire a couple of times until it comes to a boil, then the sauce is ready
