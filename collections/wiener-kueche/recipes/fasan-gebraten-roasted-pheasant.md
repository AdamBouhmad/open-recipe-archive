## Ingredients

- one pheasant
- salt
- lard for larding, finely chopped
- butter for basting
- lemon peel for garnish
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cleanly pluck the pheasant, remove the entrails, wash it thoroughly, salt it well
2. Lace it elegantly with finely chopped lard and baste it frequently with butter
3. When it is roasted through, garnish it with lemon peel, baste it with lemon juice and serve it to the table
