## Ingredients

- 1 measure boiled potatoes
- 1/2 pound melted butter, warmed
- 6 eggs
- 1 pound pitted raisins
- 1 glass brandy
- butter for the cloth

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes, crush them, and press them warm through a fine sieve.
2. Stir them together with 1/2 pound melted and slightly warm butter until quite thick, fold in 6 eggs, and add 1 pound pitted raisins and 1 glass brandy.
3. Bind this mass loosely in a butter-coated cloth, cook it for 1.5 hours in a large pot of simmering water, and pour a wine foam sauce over it when serving.
