## Ingredients

- Firmly frozen, well-worked Pineapple-Frozen
- 1/2 liter Champagne wine

## Directions

1. To a firmly frozen, well-worked Pineapple-Frozen, stir in 1/2 liter Champagne wine shortly before serving.
2. Serve it in glasses.
