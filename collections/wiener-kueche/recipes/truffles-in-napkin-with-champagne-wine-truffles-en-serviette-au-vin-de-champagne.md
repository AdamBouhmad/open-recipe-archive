## Ingredients

- large truffles
- Champagne or ordinary wine
- good broth
- soup glaze

## Directions

1. Clean large truffles as described above and place them unpeeled in a casserole
2. Add Champagne or ordinary wine with good broth, using 2 parts wine and only 1 part broth, but add only enough liquid so that it barely covers the truffles
3. Let them cook covered for approximately 1/4 hour
4. Remove them with a serving spoon and serve them still hot in a napkin-lined bowl or in a dish with a lid, keeping their black skin intact
5. To the remaining cooking liquid, add some soup glaze and serve this in a sauce boat with the truffles
6. Serve as an intermediate course
