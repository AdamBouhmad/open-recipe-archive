## Ingredients

- 4 dkg butter
- 1 dkg flour
- 1/2 milk
- salt
- sugar
- 3 dkg soaked, chopped almonds
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Heat 4 dkg butter in a pot
2. Stir in 1 dkg flour and deglaze with milk
3. Season with salt and sugar
4. Add 3 dkg softened, chopped almonds and cook thoroughly
5. Just before serving, add the cleaned grated horseradish
