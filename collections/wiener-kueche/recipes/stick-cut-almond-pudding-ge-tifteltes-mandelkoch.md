## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound sugar
- fourteen loths ground almonds
- eight whole eggs
- lemon peel
- fourteen loths stick-cut almonds
- butter

## Directions

1. Take three-quarters pound sugar, cook it nicely thick, and let it cool
2. Take fourteen loths ground almonds into the sugar and stir well
3. Beat in eight whole eggs, one after the other
4. Add lemon peel and finally fourteen loths stick-cut almonds
5. Stir everything well together
6. Coat a mold with butter and bake it slowly
