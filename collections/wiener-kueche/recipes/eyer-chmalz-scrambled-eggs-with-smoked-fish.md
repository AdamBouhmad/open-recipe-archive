## Ingredients

- Smoked fish, already cooked, cut into small thin slices
- Butter
- Eggs, any number
- Salt
- Spices

## Directions

1. Cut cooked smoked fish of any kind into small thin slices and add adequate butter.
2. Let them heat in a pan.
3. Break any number of eggs into a pot and beat them well.
4. Salt them adequately and add some spices.
5. Pour them over the fish slices, stir everything together well, and do not let the eggs clump together.
