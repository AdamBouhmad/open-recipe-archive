## Ingredients

- 25 dkg flour
- Salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 7 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 whole egg
- 1.5 dkg yeast
- 1 dl lukewarm milk
- Plums
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Warm 25 dkg flour with salt and 2 tablespoons sugar in a bowl
2. Rub in 7 dkg butter
3. Add 1 egg yolk, 1 whole egg, and the risen leaven from 1.5 dkg yeast
4. Add nearly 1 dl lukewarm milk
5. Beat the dough until it leaves the spoon and comes away from the sides
6. Place near the stove to rise
7. When sufficiently risen, place on a well-greased baking sheet
8. Stretch out with floured fingers
9. Cover with plums as with the previous cake
10. Bake at lower heat
11. Divide into equal pieces
12. Sprinkle with sugar
13. Serve cooled
