## Ingredients

- quarter pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- half seidel sweet milk cream
- 2 spoonfuls good yeast (Germ)
- 2 spoonfuls pounded sugar
- salt
- flour
- cut almonds
- citron (Citronat)
- currants (Zibeben)
- wine berries (Weinberl)

## Directions

1. Beat quarter pound butter quite fluffy
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks
3. Add half seidel sweet milk cream, 2 spoonfuls good yeast, 2 spoonfuls pounded sugar, and some salt
4. Add enough flour so the dough would be good for making a pastry, roll it out finger-thick
5. Fill it with cut almonds, citron, currants (with the kernels removed), and wine berries
6. Roll it together, place it on a butter-brushed baking sheet, let it rise in a warm place, and bake it beautifully
