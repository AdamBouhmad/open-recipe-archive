## Ingredients

- Rye bread, very thinly sliced
- Fat, properly salted broth
- Fresh parsley, finely cut
- Chives, finely cut

## Directions

1. Slice rye bread very thin and dry it until crisp. Place in soup pot, sprinkle finely cut parsley and chives over it, pour hot fat and properly salted broth over it, cover and serve.
