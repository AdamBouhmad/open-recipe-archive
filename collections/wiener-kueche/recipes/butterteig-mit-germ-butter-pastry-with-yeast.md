## Ingredients

- 60 deca flour
- 50 deca cold firm butter, cut into small pieces
- 8 deca sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter cold cream

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board
2. Cut cold firm butter into small pieces and rub finely with both hands with the flour
3. Make a well in the middle
4. Add 8 deca sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 8 egg yolks, and 4 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter cold cream into the well
5. Mix with a knife and work with the greatest speed with both hands into a smooth dough
6. Place on a flour-dusted sheet, cover with a cloth, and let rest in a cold place
7. After 2 1/4 hours, roll out strongly knife-back-thick on a flour-dusted board and can now be used for various purposes
