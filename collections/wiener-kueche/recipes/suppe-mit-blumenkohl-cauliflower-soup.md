## Ingredients

- Several well-cleaned cauliflower florets
- Good bouillon
- 6 lothes cauliflower
- 2 pounds butter
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Toasted bread roll slices, fine dumplings, or similar

## Directions

1. Blanch well-cleaned cauliflower florets in hot water and then simmer in good bouillon until completely soft. For 6 lothes of cauliflower, take 2 pounds butter and let it sweat in the butter after removing from bouillon. Place in soup pot (terrine). Draw off broth with several egg yolks, season with salt and nutmeg, and pour over cauliflower. May add toasted bread roll slices, fine dumplings, or similar. The soup can be further improved by adding minced chicken or veal breast, crayfish tails and similar, with some crayfish butter added.
