## Ingredients

- 60 dkg lean and fatty pork
- 1 kg sauerkraut
- salt
- caraway
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the sauerkraut, wash lightly, place in a pot with salt, caraway, vinegar, and water.
2. Simmer for 45 minutes.
3. Add the lightly washed, diced pork and simmer for another 45 minutes.
