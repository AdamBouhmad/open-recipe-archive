## Ingredients

- 1 kilo boned, lean beef
- 1 kilo veal meat
- 1 old, cleaned chicken cut into 4 pieces
- 1 old, roasted hen or old rooster
- 4 liters cold water
- 1 piece parsley
- 1 piece yellow turnip
- 1 piece celery
- 1 small piece onion with 1 spice clove inserted (optional)
- salt (to taste)

## Directions

1. Chop 1 kilo boned, lean beef and 1 kilo veal meat
2. Add 1 old, cleaned chicken cut into 4 pieces and 1 old, roasted hen or old rooster
3. Place in a casserolle and fill with 4 liters cold water
4. Cover and place on fire, letting it cook slowly for 1 hour
5. Then add 1 piece parsley, yellow turnip, celery and if desired, 1 small piece onion with a spice clove inserted
6. Let it cook slowly for 2-3 more hours, so that the total cooking time is 3-4 hours
7. Set it aside, remove the fat that has formed cleanly, and strain it through a fine sieve into an earthen vessel
8. Add salt when the broth is being used
9. If necessary, strain the broth through fine cloth
