## Ingredients

- Half a pound of lean veal
- Liver, heart, blood and meat from one turtle of ordinary size
- Half a measure of water

## Directions

1. Cook the veal, turtle liver, heart, blood and meat with the water for several hours until it becomes thick
2. Strain it through and let it cool
3. The patient takes two to three loth of this in four to six cups of thin broth
