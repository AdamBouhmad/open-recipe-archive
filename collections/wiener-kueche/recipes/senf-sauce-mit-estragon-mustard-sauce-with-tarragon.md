## Ingredients

- 8 pieces cleaned anchovies
- 1 handful tarragon
- 1 handful sorrel
- 1 handful chives
- 3 pieces peeled shallots
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons French mustard
- 4 tablespoons oil
- pinch of pepper

## Directions

1. Cut finely together 8 pieces cleaned anchovies, 1 handful tarragon, sorrel, chives, and 3 pieces peeled shallots
2. Pound together in a mortar with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
3. Pass into a bowl with 4 tablespoons French mustard
4. Stir in well with 4 tablespoons oil and a pinch of pepper
