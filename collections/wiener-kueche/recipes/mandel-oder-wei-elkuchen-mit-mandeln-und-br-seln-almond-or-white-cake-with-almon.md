## Ingredients

- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 16 deca blanched almonds
- 2 egg whites
- 8 egg whites
- 8 deca bread crumbs
- white wafer
- black cherries or morello cherries

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca vanilla sugar with 10 egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Add 16 deca blanched, very finely pounded almonds with 2 egg whites
3. Stir for 1.5 hours and lightly fold in the snow from 8 egg whites at the same time as 8 deca bread crumbs
4. Fill this mixture into a form brushed with butter and dusted with flour, 2 fingers high
5. Cover the mixture with a white wafer and place black cherries or morello cherries on top, which are covered with the remaining mixture
6. Bake for approximately 1 hour in the heated oven
7. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and serve only the following day
