## Ingredients

- hard-boiled, cooled eggs
- capers
- crayfish tail meat
- fish
- capers
- cold, good butter
- salt
- pepper
- cold mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled cooled eggs lengthwise in half and remove the yolks without damaging the whites.
2. Finely mince the yolks with capers (1 caper per yolk), crayfish tail meat, some arbitrary fish, and capers, then add about 1 tablespoon cold good butter, some salt and pepper to taste, and mix with enough cold mustard sauce to form a thick ragout.
3. Heap this filling into the whites of the halved eggs and arrange them on a dish, raised on a platter, which can also be garnished with aspic and olives, and sprinkle some capers over them.
4. Serve cold mustard sauce or cold Fricassee sauce separately in a sauceboat.
