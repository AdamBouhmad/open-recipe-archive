## Ingredients

- Butter sauce
- Milk cream
- Lemon juice
- Capers
- Nutmeg
- Sliced ham (optional)
- Leftover roasted veal

## Directions

1. To a butter sauce add milk cream, lemon juice, capers, nutmeg, and finely sliced ham if desired.
2. Let simmer together well.
3. Meanwhile, slice the leftover roasted veal into pieces of any size.
4. Add the pieces to the sauce and let boil.
