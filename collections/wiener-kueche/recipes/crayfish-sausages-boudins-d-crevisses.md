## Ingredients

- 30 smallest crayfish
- salt water
- braised or cooked trout
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 goose liver cooked in salt water
- crayfish shells
- 12 deciliters butter
- 24 deciliters trimmed, in milk-soaked, well-pressed panettone
- crayfish butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- sausage casings

## Directions

1. Boil 30 of the smallest crayfish in salt water
2. Shell them completely, reserve the cooked flesh
3. Finely pound the crayfish shells
4. Toast them in 12 deciliters butter for 10-15 minutes
5. Press through a cloth into cold water to extract the crayfish butter
6. Finely dice the cooked trout, chicken breast, and goose liver
7. Mix with the reserved crayfish flesh
8. Add the crayfish butter, 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, the panettone, pepper, nutmeg, and salt to taste
9. Mix everything well
10. Thoroughly clean the sausage casings in fresh water and soak for 7.5 hours
11. Fill the casings with the prepared mixture using a syringe
12. Tie into finger-length pieces with twine
13. Slowly fry in butter on both sides
14. Serve with fine vegetables or purées
