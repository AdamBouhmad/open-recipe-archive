## Ingredients

- milk
- 20 dkg semolina
- 10 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls sugar
- finely chopped lemon zest
- 4 egg whites
- butter and sugar for the mold

## Directions

1. Bring milk to a boil in a pot
2. Slowly stir in 20 dkg semolina, stirring constantly until thick, and cool
3. Mix with creamed butter, egg yolks, sugar, and lemon zest
4. Lightly fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Fill into a well-buttered and sugared soufflé dish
6. Bake light brown over moderate heat
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve in the dish
