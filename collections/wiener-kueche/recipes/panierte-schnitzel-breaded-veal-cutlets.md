## Ingredients

- Veal loin, finger-thick slices
- Flour
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Natural Cutlets, but dip the flour-coated cutlets into beaten eggs
2. Coat with bread crumbs (paneer)
3. Fry in hot lard
