## Ingredients

- 7 dkg flour
- 7 dkg butter
- 7 dkg raw grated almonds
- 5 dkg grated chocolate
- 2 egg yolks
- Jam for filling
- 1 egg white
- Pearl sugar

## Directions

1. On the board, make a dough from flour, butter, raw grated almonds, grated chocolate, and egg yolks
2. After resting, roll out and cut shapes as desired
3. Place on unbuttered sheet and bake
4. Alternative: cut rings, brush with jam, sandwich together, brush with egg white, and roll in pearl sugar
5. Can be used as Christmas pastries
