## Ingredients

- 8 deca butter
- 24 deca sugar
- 24 deca flour
- 1/4 liter boiling water
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place butter, sugar, and flour in saucepan
2. Pour boiling water over and stir constantly
3. Boil until dough releases from spoon
4. Set aside and let cool completely
5. Gradually stir in egg yolks one by one
6. Place dough on flour-dusted board
7. Form into desired shapes such as breads, rods, rings
8. Place on butter-lined baking sheet
9. Brush with water
10. Sprinkle with hail sugar
11. Bake at low heat in oven until pale golden
