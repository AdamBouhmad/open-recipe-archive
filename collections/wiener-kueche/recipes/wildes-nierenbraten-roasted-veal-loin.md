## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Salt
- Fat
- Broth

## Directions

1. The loin from the hipbone to the second rib is called the veal loin
2. Hack the rib bones in the middle; either hack the bone or remove it, though removing it causes loss of juices during roasting
3. When the bones are hacked, wipe the meat with a cloth and salt it, then let it rest
4. Place the salted veal loin in a casserole containing fat, cover with hot fat, and roast in the oven with frequent basting
5. For a larger piece, roasting time is approximately 2 hours; for a smaller one, 1-1.5 hours
6. When the meat has a light brown crust, remove it and cut into pieces
7. Remove the fat from the juices, pour a few spoonfuls of broth into the drippings, reduce slightly and serve with the meat
8. The marrow can be removed, trimmed of fat, cut into pieces and added to the roast, or carve the roast with the marrow inside
9. Note that the juices should not be poured over the roast but served separately
