## Ingredients

- 16 ounces butter
- 2 pieces mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 1 piece onion
- Several sprigs parsley
- 1 rabbit, skinned and cleaned
- Soup stock
- 8 ounces flour
- Egg yolks (optional)
- Cold milk (optional)
- Cream (optional)
- Vinegar, lemon juice, or white wine (optional)
- Madeira wine and truffles (optional, for brown preparation)

## Directions

1. Put 16 ounces butter in a casserole with mushrooms, onion, and parsley sprigs
2. Cut the cleaned rabbit into 2-finger-wide pieces and add to the casserole
3. Cover and brown the rabbit well
4. Pour some soup stock over it and simmer until tender
5. Remove the cooked meat and place in a casserole, pouring some of the cooking liquid over it and keeping it warm in a warm place
6. Meanwhile, brown 8 ounces flour in the same amount of butter, pour the cooking liquid over it, stir well, and let it simmer
7. Pour this brown butter sauce over the arranged meat on a platter
8. Optionally, whisk 3 egg yolks with a few spoonfuls of cold milk and mix well with the butter sauce
9. Or add some cream and let it simmer well
10. The butter sauce can also be seasoned with some vinegar, lemon juice, or white wine
11. For brown preparation, keep the butter sauce darker and enhance the flavor with Madeira wine and truffles
12. All game given with piquant sauce can also be garnished with small roasted onions and butter paste
