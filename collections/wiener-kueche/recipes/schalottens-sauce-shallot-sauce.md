## Ingredients

- 3 shallots cut into 4 pieces
- 1 bay leaf
- About 6 tablespoons vinegar
- 8 tablespoons butter or lard
- Equal amount of flour
- 1/3 liter soup
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 3 shallots cut into 4 pieces with 1 bay leaf in about 6 tablespoons vinegar
2. Make a dark roux from 8 tablespoons butter or lard and equal amount of flour
3. Add the cooked shallots with the vinegar to the roux
4. Pour 1/3 liter soup over it
5. Salt and sugar it to taste, let it simmer well and pass through a sieve
