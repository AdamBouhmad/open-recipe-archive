## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 0.25 liter milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 coffee spoon salt
- 0.5 liter flour
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Beat 5 whole eggs with 0.25 liter milk, 1 tablespoon sugar and 1 coffee spoon salt together and gradually pour onto 0.5 liter flour while constantly stirring
2. When the batter is smooth, pour 1 tablespoon hot fat on a hot frying pan and evenly pour over it 3 tablespoons of the above-mentioned batter
3. When the lower side is baked brown, turn the batter over with a spatula and bake the second side brown as well
4. Lift the pancake with a spatula into a bowl, keep it warm and continue baking in this manner until the batter is finished
5. Coat the baked pancakes with preserved fruit as desired, roll together, cut into pieces as desired and arrange on a bowl
6. The above-mentioned batter can also be prepared as desired without the addition of sugar, and in this case coat the baked pieces with grated Parmesan cheese, egg dishes and the like instead of preserved fruit
