## Ingredients

- 80 dkg to 1 kg shoulder roast or rib
- 1/2 kg sauerkraut
- vinegar
- caraway
- salt
- water
- 6 dkg fat

## Directions

1. Wash the meat
2. Place in cold water without salt
3. Cook for 1½ to 2 hours
4. Remove and cut, then chop through
5. Serve on a long platter
6. Wash the sauerkraut and cut 2-3 times
7. Cook with vinegar, caraway, salt, and enough water to cover for 1½ to 2 hours
8. Add a light onion roux made from 6 dkg fat
9. Season with salt and sour to taste
10. Cook together for 20 minutes
11. Optionally add roasting juices
