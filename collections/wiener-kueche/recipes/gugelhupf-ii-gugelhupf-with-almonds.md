## Ingredients

- 24 deca butter
- 3 tablespoons finely ground sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 12 egg yolks
- 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 3 tablespoons milk
- egg white from 6 eggs
- 28 deca flour
- slivered almonds
- butter for greasing
- flour for lining the mold
- powdered sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat 24 deca butter until fluffy
2. Add 3 tablespoons finely ground sugar, 1 pinch of salt and 12 egg yolks one after another
3. Mix in 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 3 tablespoons milk
4. Lightly fold in egg white from 6 eggs simultaneously with 28 deca flour
5. Warm a gugelhupf mold, coat with butter, line with flour and arrange sliced almonds around the sides
6. Fill with dough, cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled in height
7. Place in a fairly hot oven and bake slowly for 3/4 to 1 hour
8. Turn out onto a sieve and sprinkle with powdered sugar after completely cooling
