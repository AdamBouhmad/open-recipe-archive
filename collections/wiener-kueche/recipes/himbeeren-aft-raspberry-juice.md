## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare like cherry juice, with the note that if after some time the juice becomes thin, place it in a casserole and cook again until it becomes thick
2. Cut round white papers and dip the inner side in brandy and finely pounded sugar
3. When the juice is filled into glasses, place such a paper on the juice and tie the glass with a bladder
