## Ingredients

- 4 washed, unsalted, sour apples
- 0.75 liter water
- Sugar
- Wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the apples into 4 pieces and remove the core.
2. Put them in a coffee pot and boil them with 0.75 liter of water until quite soft.
3. Strain them through a sieve or cloth and add sugar to taste, or also wine or lemon juice.
