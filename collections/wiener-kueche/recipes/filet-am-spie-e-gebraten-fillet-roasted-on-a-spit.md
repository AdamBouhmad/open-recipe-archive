## Ingredients

- a beautiful fillet well covered with fat
- pepper
- thyme
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful fillet well covered with fat
2. Trim the fat immediately
3. Rub the fillet well with pepper and thyme
4. Insert a long skewer through it
5. Bend the fillet at both ends and tie the whole thing to a larger spit
6. Roast it slowly
7. Baste it with butter and lemon juice
8. When it is nicely in its juices, pour off the fat
9. Place the fillet on a platter
10. Serve a piquant sauce or tomato sauce alongside in a sauce boat
