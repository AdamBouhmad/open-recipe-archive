## Ingredients

- Salzburg dumplings (No. 1328)
- 4 egg whites
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. After the Salzburg dumplings have been prepared in milk and placed in the oven, lightly fold in the beaten egg whites from 4 eggs
2. Arrange the dumplings on a platter and sprinkle with sugar
3. Bake for 10 minutes in the open oven
