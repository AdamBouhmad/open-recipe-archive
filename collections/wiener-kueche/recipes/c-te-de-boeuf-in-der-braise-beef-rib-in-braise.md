## Ingredients

- a piece from the side rib
- coarse bacon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- ground cloves
- bacon leaves
- whole onions
- carrots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Flatten the rib piece and trim it
2. Lard it with coarse bacon
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and finely ground cloves
4. Lay bacon leaves in an oblong casserole, then the rib, which you cover again with bacon
5. Add many whole onions, carrots, a bay leaf, thyme and parsley
6. Let everything brown quickly over charcoal fire
7. Finally fill it with bouillon or water
8. Let it cook through; it must be very tender, but the sauce should be reduced quickly so it glazes itself
9. First skim off the fat
10. Place the rib on a platter, pour some bouillon into the sauce, and serve it underneath
11. Glaze it with evenly cut carrots and parsnips, glazed onions in butter, roasted potatoes, minced cabbage or white cabbage, which must all be cooked until tender, nicely arranged, and glazed
