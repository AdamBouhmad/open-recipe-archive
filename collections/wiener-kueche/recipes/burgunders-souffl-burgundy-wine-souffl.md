## Ingredients

- 28 Deka trimmed, thinly sliced rolls
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold
- dried currants
- raisins
- sliced almonds
- finely cut citron peel
- 1/2 liter red wine
- 24 Grams sugar
- cinnamon to taste
- nutmeg
- 1 piece lemon peel
- 7 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Bake the 28 Deka of trimmed, thinly sliced rolls in the oven.
2. Lay them two by two on top of each other, brushing between with any desired preserved filling.
3. Line the bottom of a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour with these rolls.
4. Sprinkle these with dried currants, raisins, finely cut almonds, and finely cut citron peel.
5. Cover again with a layer of the rolls mentioned above and continue in this manner until all rolls are used.
6. Heat 1/2 liter red wine with 24 Grams sugar, cinnamon to taste, nutmeg, and 1 piece lemon peel, bringing it to a boil once, then set it aside.
7. After it has cooled completely, beat it with 7 egg yolks and pour it over the rolls in the mold.
8. Once it has absorbed somewhat, bake for 1 1/2 hours in the oven.
