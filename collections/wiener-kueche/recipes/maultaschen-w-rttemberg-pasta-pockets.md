## Ingredients

- 16 deca butter
- 4 deca finely pounded sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 6 tablespoons lukewarm milk
- 54 deca flour
- filling as desired

## Directions

1. Cream together 16 deca butter with 4 deca finely pounded sugar and 1 pinch of salt
2. Stir in 4 egg yolks one after another
3. Add 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 6 tablespoons lukewarm milk and 54 deca flour, mix well
4. Beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
5. Cover and let rise in a warm place until it has risen three finger-widths higher
6. Dust a board with flour and roll out the dough 2 knife-back thicknesses thick
7. Cut out round discs with a dough cutter and place 2 finger-widths apart on a butter-brushed baking sheet
8. Push the center of each round disc apart with fingers so a small-finger-width rim forms around the edge
9. Fill the depression with desired filling
10. Cover and let rise in a warm place until somewhat higher
11. Brush the rims with beaten egg
12. Cover the filling with Spanish windforce mixture knife-back thickness thick
13. Sprinkle with finely cut almonds mixed with white hail sugar
14. Bake slowly in the oven at moderate heat
