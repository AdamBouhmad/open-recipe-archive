## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- salt water
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil a pike in salt water.
2. Remove all bones and mix the shredded pike in butter sauce to which lemon juice has been added.
3. Arrange heaped on a platter and garnish with decorative butter paste cut out as desired, or place in a butter paste mold or pastry form.
