## Ingredients

- 2 pieces of 25-30 dkg
- 1 large onion
- 6 dkg hot fat
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- potato pancakes

## Directions

1. Trim the rostbraten of bones, skin and fat (these can also remain), wipe off, tap lightly.
2. Just before roasting, salt and pepper, dip one side in flour, place this side in a shallow pan with about 6 dkg hot fat.
3. At the same time add 1 large onion to roast with it, turn the rostbraten after a few minutes and roast both sides over high heat on the stove for a total of 12 minutes.
4. Serve the rostbraten with the onion on a long platter. Side dish: potato pancakes.
