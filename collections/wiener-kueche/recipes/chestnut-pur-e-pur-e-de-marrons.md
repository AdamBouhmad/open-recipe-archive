## Ingredients

- cooked chestnuts
- melted butter
- cream
- sugar
- nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel any desired quantity of cooked chestnuts
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Place in melted butter
4. Add cream
5. Stir in some sugar and, if desired, a little nutmeg
6. Simmer briefly
7. Keep the chestnut purée thick enough that it can be served heaped
