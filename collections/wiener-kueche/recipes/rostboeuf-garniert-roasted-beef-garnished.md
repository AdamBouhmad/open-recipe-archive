## Ingredients

- 1.20 kg deboned beef sirloin
- 15 dg fat
- 4 dkg butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Remove bones and fat from the meat, season with salt and pepper, and lightly bind if desired.
2. Heat 15 dg fat in a low pan until very hot, add the meat, sear on all sides over strong heat, place in a hot oven, and continue roasting for 25-30 minutes under frequent basting with its own juices.
3. Place on a plate and keep warm in the oven.
4. Make the sauce by pouring off the fat, browning 4 dkg butter in the pan, adding broth or water, bring to a boil, and strain.
5. Slice the meat very thinly, arrange on a long platter, garnish alternating around with 1 spoon of vegetables and between them 1 small scoop of rice, and decorate with parsley.
