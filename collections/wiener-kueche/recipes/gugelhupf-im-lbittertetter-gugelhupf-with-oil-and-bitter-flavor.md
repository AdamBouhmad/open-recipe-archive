## Ingredients

- 28 deca butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 4 deca finely ground sugar
- 5 grams salt
- 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter cold milk
- 56 deca flour
- egg white from 2 eggs
- 14 deca raisins
- butter for greasing the mold
- flour for lining the mold

## Directions

1. Beat 28 deca butter until fluffy
2. Add 10 egg yolks one after another
3. Add 4 deca finely ground sugar and 5 grams salt
4. 10 minutes before use, dissolve 2 deca fresh yeast in 1/4 liter cold milk and stir at a warm location until the yeast is completely dissolved and lukewarm
5. Add this yeast mixture simultaneously with 56 deca flour to the butter mixture and beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
6. If the dough is too stiff, add 3 tablespoons milk
7. Lightly fold in egg white from 2 eggs and 14 deca raisins
8. Coat a gugelhupf mold with butter, line with flour and fill with dough
9. Place 2 tablespoons on the mold and cover with a cloth to prevent air from entering
10. Let the dough rise near the hearth until it has doubled in height
11. Place in a moderately hot oven and bake for 1 hour
12. Turn out onto a sieve and sprinkle with sugar when cold
