## Ingredients

- 1 liter fine green peas
- salt water
- 12 ounces butter
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped green parsley
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 3 tablespoons white soup

## Directions

1. Cook 1 liter of fine green peas in salt water until tender
2. Drain the liquid and place the peas in a casserole dish
3. Mix 12 ounces of butter with 1 tablespoon of finely chopped green parsley and 1 tablespoon of cream, beating well together
4. Add this mixture to the cooked peas
5. Stir in 3 tablespoons of white soup
6. Cover and let stand on the hot plate until ready to serve
