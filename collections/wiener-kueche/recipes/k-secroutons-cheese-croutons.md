## Ingredients

- 1¼ bread rolls
- 6 dkg butter
- 5 dkg Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Slice the bread rolls first in half, then into leaves.
2. Melt 6 dkg butter in a small bowl, dip the bread slices in it, turn them on both sides in grated Parmesan.
3. Place them in an omelet pan, bake them at moderate heat in the oven until light brown and serve separately.
