## Ingredients

- 1 liter root vegetables
- 10 dkg liver and spleen
- 5 dkg fat
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns and spice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- thyme
- a piece of black bread crust
- vinegar
- water or beef broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and slice the root vegetables
2. In a pot, roast the vegetables with 5 dkg fat, 1 onion, scraped liver and spleen, 5 peppercorns and spice berries, 1 bay leaf, thyme, and a piece of black bread crust until dark brown
3. Sprinkle with vinegar and pour in water
4. Braise for 1¼ hours, stirring frequently and adding more liquid as needed
5. Press everything through a sieve
6. Add water or leftover beef broth, season to taste with vinegar and salt, and cook well
7. Alternative: instead of liver, use leftover game meat and bone remnants, then it is called wild puree soup
