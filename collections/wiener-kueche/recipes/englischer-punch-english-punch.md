## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 liter coffee
- thin peel of 2 sweet oranges and 2 lemons (without white pith)
- 1/4 liter rum
- 1/4 liter Rhine wine
- 1/4 liter Burgundy wine
- juice of 4 oranges and 4 lemons

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, add sugar, coffee, and the thin peels of 2 sweet oranges and 2 lemons, without any white pith remaining
2. Let it boil for approximately 2 minutes
3. Add 1/4 liter rum, 1/4 liter Rhine wine, and 1/4 liter Burgundy wine
4. Stir in the juice of 4 oranges and 4 lemons
5. Let it stand covered in a warm place
6. Serve hot in a soup pot or bowl
