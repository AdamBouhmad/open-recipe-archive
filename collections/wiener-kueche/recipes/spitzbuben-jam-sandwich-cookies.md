## Ingredients

- 17 dkg flour
- 14 dkg butter
- 7 dkg sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from flour, butter, sugar, and egg yolks
2. Let it rest
3. Roll it out and cut out rounds
4. Cut out eyes from half of the rounds with a small round cutter
5. Place all rounds on an unbuttered baking sheet
6. Bake at higher heat until light brown
7. Spread jam on a whole round, place a round with eyes on top, and sprinkle with sugar
