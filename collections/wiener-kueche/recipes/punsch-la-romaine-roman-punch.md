## Ingredients

- 10 biscuits
- 4 egg yolks
- 16 dkg sugar
- rum or punch to taste
- 1/2 whipped cream
- marmalade
- whipped cream for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks with 16 dkg sugar until very foamy
2. Mix in rum or punch to taste and whipped cream
3. In a glass bowl, layer biscuits that have been spread with marmalade on the bottom
4. Pour the prepared mixture on top
5. Place the bowl on ice and let stand for 2–3 hours
6. Optionally garnish the top with 1/2 whipped cream before serving
7. Alternatively, fill glasses without biscuits, bury in ice, and top with whipped cream before serving
