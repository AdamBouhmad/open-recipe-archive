## Ingredients

- Wild boar head
- Good truffle forcemeat
- Wild boar meat
- String
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Veal feet
- Bacon
- Onions
- Dry herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Seasonings
- Gelée

## Directions

1. Clean the wild boar head, remove the bones completely, line it with good truffle forcemeat, and fill it with wild boar meat
2. Put the head together, sew it with string as if it were whole, and cook it in a strong braise of wine, vinegar, veal feet, bacon, onions, and dry herbs until it is tender
3. Do not forget salt, pepper, and seasonings
4. The next day it can be served sliced with gelée
