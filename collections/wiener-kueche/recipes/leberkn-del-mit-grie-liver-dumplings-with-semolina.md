## Ingredients

- 12 deca butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 64 deca strained calf's liver
- 24 deca rolls, rind removed and diced, soaked in 1/8 liter milk
- 26 deca semolina

## Directions

1. Beat 12 deca of butter until fluffy.
2. Gradually stir in 5 egg yolks and 3 whole eggs.
3. Add 64 deca of strained calf's liver, 24 deca of finely diced rolls (rind removed and softened in 1/8 liter milk), and 26 deca of semolina.
4. Let this rest for 1 hour.
5. Form dumplings from the dough and place them in simmering salted water.
6. Cook covered for approximately 15-20 minutes, depending on the size of the dumplings.
7. They will become considerably larger during cooking.
8. Remove them with a skimmer, place on a plate, sprinkle with breadcrumbs roasted in butter or, if desired, with grated horseradish, and drizzle with melted butter.
9. If they are for soup, add nothing to them.
