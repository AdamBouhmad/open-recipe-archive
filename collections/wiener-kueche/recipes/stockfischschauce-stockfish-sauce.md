## Ingredients

- Butter
- Fresh mushrooms
- Fine onions
- Garlic (optional)
- Finely chopped parsley
- Flour
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Sauté fresh mushrooms with fine onions in butter, and if desired, with a small piece of garlic until soft.
2. Mix in some finely chopped parsley.
3. Stir in approximately one spoonful of flour.
4. Fill the casserole with bouillon.
5. Let it reduce to half.
6. Before serving over cooked stockfish, pass this sauce through a sieve.
