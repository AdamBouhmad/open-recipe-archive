## Ingredients

- partridges
- bacon

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges well, wash them, and salt them
2. Cut very thin slices of bacon about the size of a hand
3. Bend the wings and feet of the partridges, dress them well, wrap them in these bacon slices, and roast them on a spit
4. During roasting, baste them frequently with butter
5. Alternatively, instead of wrapping them in bacon slices, one can lard them with finely cut bacon and then roast them
