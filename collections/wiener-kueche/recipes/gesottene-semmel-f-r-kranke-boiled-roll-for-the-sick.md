## Ingredients

- round pieces of a roll
- milk
- egg yolk
- butter
- plum or damson broth

## Directions

1. Take round pieces of a roll and cut away the rind
2. Soak the roll pieces in milk for several hours
3. Gently press them through a cloth
4. Roll the roll pieces in egg yolk several times
5. Simmer them in butter
6. Pour plum or damson broth over them
