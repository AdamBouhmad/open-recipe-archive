## Ingredients

- Suckling pig legs
- Fine veal forcemeat
- Chopped truffles
- Herbs
- Bacon
- Braise
- Sour jelly

## Directions

1. Remove all the bones from the legs and stuff them with fine veal forcemeat, chopped truffles, and herbs, well seasoned.
2. Shape them nicely and cook thoroughly in a good braise.
3. Cover with bacon.
4. Allow to cool in its cooking liquid.
5. Serve with sour jelly.
