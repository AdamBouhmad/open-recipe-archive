## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Salt
- Green parsley
- Caraway
- Rice
- Butter
- Thirty crayfish tails
- Pepper
- One egg yolk
- Three spoonfuls of cream
- White wine
- Cut green parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish with salt, green parsley, and some caraway, clean them, and take them apart.
2. Braise some rice with butter and finely chopped green parsley, then coarsely chop thirty crayfish tails together, add some salt and pepper, and let it cool.
3. Mix it well with one egg yolk and three spoonfuls of cream, fill the crayfish shells with it, lay the lower part back on top so it looks as if the crayfish were whole, press the shell firmly together.
4. Lay them on a platter, add some butter on top, also some chopped green parsley, and six spoonfuls of cream, some spoonfuls of white wine, caraway, and the crayfish.
5. Apply heat from below and above, and let them boil well, then they are done.
