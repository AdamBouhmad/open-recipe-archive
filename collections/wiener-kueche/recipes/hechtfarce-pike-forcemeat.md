## Ingredients

- Meat from a 3-pound pike
- Small onion, chopped and sweated in butter
- 3 bread rolls (with crusts removed, rest cut, soaked in milk and squeezed out, or cooked into a paste in milk)
- 1/4 pound marrow or butter
- 3 whole eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Carefully cut the pike meat and remove all bones
2. Chop very finely and rub tender in a mortar
3. Rub the meat with the onion, bread, marrow or butter, eggs, salt, pepper and nutmeg for a long time until very fine
4. Press the forcemeat through a brass sieve
5. Form small dumplings and use as garnishes and in stuffed dishes
