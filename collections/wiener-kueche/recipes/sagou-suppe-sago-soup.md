## Ingredients

- 8 spoonfuls fine brown sago
- 2 liters boiling water
- approximately 2 liters white or brown beef broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls cream
- lemon wedges

## Directions

1. Cook 8 spoonfuls fine brown sago in 2 liters boiling water for 1/2 hour until the grains are transparent
2. Pour the cooked sago through a sieve and rinse with cold water
3. Once the water has drained, add the sago to approximately 2 liters white or brown beef broth and let it cook for 1 hour
4. Put it in the soup pot and serve by beating 2 egg yolks and 2 spoonfuls cream with the sago soup and pour it in
5. Serve with lemon wedges
6. Alternatively, 8 spoonfuls unwashed sago can be added directly to boiling soup, cooked for 1 hour and served this way
7. Note that the fat must be completely removed from all these soups
