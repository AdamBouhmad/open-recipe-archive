## Ingredients

- pike
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- egg yolks
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Scale, gut and clean the pike thoroughly
2. Cut pieces of desired size
3. Place these pieces in a reine or casserole
4. Add a piece of butter and the juice of a lemon
5. Salt it, cover, and let it cook until tender
6. When the fish is cooked, stir together six or eight egg yolks with a few drops of fresh water and the juice of a lemon
7. Add this to the pike and stir a couple of times until it becomes quite thick
8. Arrange the pike with the sauce on a dish and let it cool before serving at table
