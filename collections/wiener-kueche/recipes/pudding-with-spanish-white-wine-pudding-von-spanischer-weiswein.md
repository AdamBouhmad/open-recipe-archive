## Ingredients

- 28 decagrams granulated sugar
- 8 egg whites
- butter for the mold
- flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Lightly mix 28 decagrams granulated sugar with the stiff snow from 8 egg whites
2. Fill a buttered mold dusted with flour shaped like a melon
3. Cover with parchment paper if desired
4. Bake in steam for 0.5 to 0.75 hour
