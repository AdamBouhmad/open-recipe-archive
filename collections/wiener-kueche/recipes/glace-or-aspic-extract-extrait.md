## Ingredients

- 5 kg beef and the same amount of veal meat
- 2 old hens
- 2 old partridges or 2 old pigeons
- 5 liters boiling water

## Directions

1. Place beef, veal, hens, and partridges or pigeons in a pot with 5 liters boiling water
2. Cover well and simmer slowly for 6 hours like a stockpot
3. Strain through hair sieve into a casserole, remove fat completely
4. Reduce on strong fire to 2 liters, place in earthenware vessel and let stand overnight
5. Remove the fat that has formed
6. Place the liquid in a casserole on open fire, stirring constantly until it runs thick from the spoon
7. Pour into oiled forms, a plate, or casserole and place in an airy cool location
8. In several days, remove the set glace, cut into desired pieces, and place on a wooden platter in an airy location to dry completely
9. Wipe the glace from time to time with a clean cloth; if mold appears, wash with cold water and dry with clean cloth
10. Note: no salt, root vegetables, or spices may be used in this broth, as it would not keep
11. To use: dissolve 2 decagrams glace in ½ liter cold water with salt to taste, bring slowly to boil for good meat soup
12. Can also be used for sauces
