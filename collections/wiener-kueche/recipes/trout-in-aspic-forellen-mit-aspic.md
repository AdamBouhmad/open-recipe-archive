## Ingredients

- small trout
- aspic, very clear

## Directions

1. Choose a small variety of trout that has been boiled blue and cooled in its broth
2. Chill an oblong smooth mold in ice ½ hour before use
3. Pour 2 fingers high of very clear, cooled aspic into the mold
4. When set, arrange the trout so they rest with their backs on the aspic
5. Position them so the head of one trout lies next to the tail of another
6. Ensure they do not touch the edge of the mold and appear to swim freely
7. Use chopped aspic to secure them in place
8. Add some green parsley between the trout and in their mouths
9. Carefully pour 2 more fingers high of aspic over them
10. Break up the remaining trout into unequal pieces
11. Lay these skin-side down on the set aspic
12. Fill the mold completely with cooled liquid aspic
13. Use a tall mold, about twice hand-breadth high
14. Once the aspic is completely set and covered, remove from ice
15. Plunge briefly into hot water
16. Wipe dry and quickly turn out onto a flat platter or porcelain torte plate
17. Decorate with very finely chopped yellow and red aspic and some green parsley
18. Scatter capers here and there over it
19. Serve with vinegar and oil
