## Ingredients

- 2.5-3 kg goose
- apples or rolls for stuffing
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the goose, season well inside and out with salt
2. Optionally fill with apples or rolls, place in a roasting pan, add some water beside it, and roast in the oven for 2-3 hours depending on size and age
3. Baste frequently with its own juices and turn once
4. If too much fat accumulates, skim it off before it browns
5. Carve the goose, arrange on a round platter in its original shape, and serve with the sauce which should be a nice brown color
6. Sauerkraut salad or compote can be served alongside
