## Ingredients

- beef for soup
- 8 h vegetables
- 1 onion
- 5 peppercorns and spice kernels
- 1 bay leaf
- some thyme
- 5 dkg hot fat
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare beef as usual for soup.
2. One hour before serving, clean, wash and slice the vegetables into leaves.
3. Roast the vegetables, onion, peppercorns, spice kernels, bay leaf and thyme in hot fat until dark.
4. Remove the beef from the soup and place it in the vegetables.
5. Pour on a little water, cover and braise, pouring in more liquid occasionally.
6. The sauce should be nicely brown.
7. Remove the beef, dust the sauce with flour, strain it, season with vinegar and cook together with the beef again.
8. Slice the beef and serve with the sauce on a long platter.
