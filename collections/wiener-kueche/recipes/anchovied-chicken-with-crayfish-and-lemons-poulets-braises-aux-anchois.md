## Ingredients

- 1 head of onion
- green parsley
- 6 pieces of anchovy fillets
- 12 decagrams of fat
- 3 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 chickens, cut into pieces
- 4¾ liters of beef stock

## Directions

1. Finely chop together 1 head of onion, green parsley, and 6 pieces of anchovy fillets
2. Place in a casserole containing 12 decagrams of fat
3. Toast with 3 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
4. Add 2 chickens cut into pieces and juice of 1 lemon
5. Cover and braise for some time
6. Pour 4¾ liters of beef stock over
7. Cover and braise until tender
8. Arrange chickens in a serving dish and pour the defatted sauce over
