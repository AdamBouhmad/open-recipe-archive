## Ingredients

- Calf brain prepared as for brain with Périgueux sauce
- Shell dishes
- Breadcrumbs
- Parmesan cheese, grated, or fresh finely cut herbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the brain as described in recipe 546
2. Fill the shells heaping with the mixture
3. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
4. Place shells on a baking sheet and bake for 3/4 hour
5. Remove and if desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese or fresh finely cut herbs
6. Arrange on a platter lined with a napkin
7. Serve as a first course, intermediate course, or with tea
