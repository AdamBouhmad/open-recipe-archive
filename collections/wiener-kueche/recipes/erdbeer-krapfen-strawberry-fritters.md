## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram fresh strawberries
- 24 tablespoons finely powdered sugar
- Wafers moistened with water
- Wine batter
- Lard for frying
- Powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 kilogram fresh strawberries well with 24 tablespoons finely powdered sugar.
2. Fill 1 teaspoon full of this mixture into wafers moistened with water, dip in wine batter and fry quickly until golden yellow in very hot lard.
3. Remove with a skimming spoon, place on paper towels for a few moments, arrange heaped in a dish, sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and serve immediately.
