## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh milk
- Contents of one bottle of 'Vindobona' yogurt pure cultures

## Directions

1. Boil the fresh milk well and cool to 50°C
2. Pour the contents of the 'Vindobona' yogurt pure culture bottle into the milk, stir well, and fill into bottles or pots
3. Prepare a larger vessel with 55-degree water
4. Place the filled bottles into this vessel with water and cover the vessel
5. Position this vessel at a location on the stove that maintains an average temperature of approximately 40°C, best achieved by placing the larger vessel on a second smaller vessel filled with water on the warm stove
6. Let the bottles rest quietly in the warm water for approximately 3 to 3¼ hours
7. Carefully remove the bottles from the water and place them on a table without shaking them during transport, and let them cool slowly
8. Once the milk begins to thicken, bring it to a cool place to become completely thick
9. For further preparation of true yogurt, use one tablespoon of finished yogurt per liter of milk, treated as previously described
