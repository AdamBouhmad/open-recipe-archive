## Ingredients

- 2 loths fine tea
- 1 measure water
- 2 pounds sugar
- 12 lemons
- 6 bottles good Medoc

## Directions

1. Cook 2 loths of fine tea in 1 measure of water.
2. Pour through a napkin onto 2 pounds sugar.
3. Press the juice of 12 lemons into the mixture.
4. Add 6 bottles of good Medoc.
5. Make it boiling hot on a charcoal fire.
6. Serve in glasses.
7. This beverage must absolutely not boil, as it will otherwise lose its pleasant strength.
