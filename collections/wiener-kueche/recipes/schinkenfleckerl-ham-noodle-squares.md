## Ingredients

- 30 dkg flour
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 8 dkg butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 dkg cream
- ham, chopped

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 30 dkg flour, 3 eggs, and salt
2. Cut into small squares
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook for 5 minutes
4. Drain and pour cold water over them
5. To the creamed 8 dkg butter, stir in 3 egg yolks and salt
6. Add 1 dkg cream, the chopped ham, the squares, and 3 egg whites beaten stiff
7. Fill the mixture into a well-buttered baking pan
8. Bake until light brown over higher heat
9. Remove with a wooden spoon and serve on a round platter
