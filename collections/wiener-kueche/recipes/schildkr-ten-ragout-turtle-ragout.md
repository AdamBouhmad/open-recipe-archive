## Ingredients

- turtle, prepared as previously described, cut into small pieces
- crayfish claws
- 1 boiled calf's sweetbread
- green peas
- turtle eggs, removed from shell
- butter
- butter sauce
- 3 egg yolks
- lemon juice
- truffles, sliced in leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare the turtle as previously described
2. Cut the turtle into small pieces
3. Add crayfish claws, 1 boiled calf's sweetbread, green peas, and the removed turtle eggs; mix everything well together
4. Place in the center of a serving dish and garnish with croquettes, marrow dumplings, etc.
5. Make a butter sauce from the turtle broth
6. Bind the sauce with 3 egg yolks beaten with some lemon juice
7. Mix the sauce with sliced truffels as desired
8. Pour part of this sauce over the ragout and serve the remaining sauce in a sauce boat
