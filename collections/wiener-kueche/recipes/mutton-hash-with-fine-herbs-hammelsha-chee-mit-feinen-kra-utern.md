## Ingredients

- Roasted mutton leg
- Butter
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Mushrooms, finely chopped
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Bouillon
- Croutons
- Poached or fried eggs

## Directions

1. Cut out the meat from a roasted mutton leg
2. Remove the skin and any sinews
3. Mince the meat finely with a chopping knife
4. Place a piece of butter in a casserole
5. Lightly pass a portion of finely chopped shallots in it
6. Add finely chopped mushrooms and parsley
7. Season them properly
8. After they have sweated for several minutes, add some flour and bouillon
9. Let it cook completely
10. Finally stir in the chopped meat and mix it well while warm, but it must not boil
11. When dressed, garnish it with croutons and place poached or fried eggs on top
