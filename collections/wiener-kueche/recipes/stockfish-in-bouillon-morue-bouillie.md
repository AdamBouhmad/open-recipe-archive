## Ingredients

- 1 prepared stockfish
- Salted water

## Directions

1. After the skin is removed and the fish has soaked one hour in fresh water, place it in enough boiling salted water so that the water covers it completely.
2. Bring the stockfish to a boil once covered; then place it on the side of the stove and let it stand covered in its broth for 1.5 hours.
3. After this time, pour off the water, lay the stockfish on a cloth so the water drips off.
4. If desired, either serve the fish in one piece or break it into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon, place these in a bowl and cover with anchovy butter or breadcrumbs.
5. Finely sliced onion, if desired, is browned until golden in hot butter and then poured over the arranged dish.
