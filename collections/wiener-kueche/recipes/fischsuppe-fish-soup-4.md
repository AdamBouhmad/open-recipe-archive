## Ingredients

- pieces of carp or pike
- bread slices baked in two eggs
- bouillon or pea broth
- salt
- the usual root vegetables
- croutons (baked bread)
- some nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fill a casserole with pieces of carp or pike with bread slices baked in two eggs, fill with bouillon or pea broth
2. Salt and cook it with the addition of the usual root vegetables until tender
3. After straining through cheesecloth, add croutons and some nutmeg before serving
