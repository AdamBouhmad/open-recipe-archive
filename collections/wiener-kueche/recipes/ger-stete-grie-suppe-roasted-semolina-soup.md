## Ingredients

- 8 dkg fat
- 8 dkg semolina
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. In a casserole, let 8 dkg semolina turn light brown in 8 dkg fat
2. Pour over with cold water
3. Salt to taste and let the soup cook well
