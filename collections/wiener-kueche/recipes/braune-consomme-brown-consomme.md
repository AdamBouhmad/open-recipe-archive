## Ingredients

- fat (beef marrow, lard, beef fat or veal fat)
- 1 large Spanish onion cut into finger-thick slices
- 1 kilo lean beef
- 1/2 kilo veal meat
- 12 deca lean ham, leafy-cut
- 1 old, roasted hen and 1 old rooster or old dove
- light beef broth (if available) or 4 liters cold water (if not)
- 1 small piece yellow turnip
- 1 piece parsley
- 1 piece celery

## Directions

1. Line a casserolle with fat, which can be marrow, lard, beef fat or veal fat, and 1 large Spanish onion cut into finger-thick slices
2. Add 1 kilo lean beef, 1/2 kilo veal meat, and 12 deca lean ham, leafy-cut
3. Cover the casserolle with a lid, place on fire and let it brown very well (roast), but it must not burn
4. Pour 1 ladle full of cold water over it, let it evaporate well and add a second ladle full
5. Add 1 old, roasted hen and 1 old rooster or old dove
6. Pour light beef broth (if available) approximately 4 liters over it, or if not available, pour the same amount of cold water
7. When the rising foam has been removed, add 1 small piece yellow turnip, parsley and celery
8. Further treatment is as in recipe 3
