## Ingredients

- 1 veal breast
- salted water
- white butter sauce
- fresh parsley sprigs
- cleaned mushrooms

## Directions

1. Boil the veal breast in simmering salted water until it turns white.
2. Remove the veal breast and place it in cold water for a few minutes to maintain its color.
3. Cut the meat into pieces of your choice and place in a casserole.
4. Pour white butter sauce over it, enough so that the sauce reaches a finger's height above the meat.
5. Braise completely with parsley sprigs and cleaned mushrooms, removing the fat that rises during braising.
6. Pass the sauce into another casserole, thicken it with beaten egg yolks, and pour over the meat arranged on a platter.
