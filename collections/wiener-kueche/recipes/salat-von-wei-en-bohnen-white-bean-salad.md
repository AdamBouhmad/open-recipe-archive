## Ingredients

- White beans (haricots), cooked in salt water
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper
- Onion or garlic, optional
- Horseradish, grated

## Directions

1. Cook white beans in salt water
2. Season with vinegar, oil and some pepper
3. If desired, add some onion or garlic
4. Before serving, sprinkle the bean salad with some grated fresh horseradish
5. For 3/4 pound of bean salad, use 4 tablespoons of fine oil
