## Ingredients

- 16 Hektogram coffee
- 1.2 liters heavy cream, boiling
- 16 Hektogram flour
- 16 Hektogram milk
- Spanish almond paste
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Immediately after roasting, pour boiling heavy cream over 16 hectograms of coffee and let stand covered
2. After completely cooling, strain through a sieve
3. Slowly pour the mixture into 16 hectograms of flour while constantly stirring
4. Treat further as indicated in the previous 'Coffee Souffle' recipe
5. After baking and turning out into a bowl, immediately coat with Spanish almond paste as desired, dust with fine sugar
6. Dry in the open oven for 10-15 minutes and serve immediately to table
