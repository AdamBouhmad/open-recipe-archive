## Ingredients

- fat
- onion, thinly sliced
- root vegetables
- mushrooms
- whole spices
- 2 cleaned and dressed chickens
- butter
- paper
- 1.5 liters of soup stock

## Directions

1. Line a casserole with fat, thinly sliced onion, root vegetables, mushrooms, and whole spices
2. Place 2 cleaned and dressed chickens on top
3. Cover with buttered paper and braise
4. After about 15 minutes, remove the paper
5. Pour 1.5 liters of soup stock over
6. Cover and braise until chickens are tender
7. Remove chickens, cut into pieces, and arrange in the center of a serving dish
8. Mix some of the strained sauce with cooked rice and use to garnish the chickens
9. Serve remaining sauce in a gravy boat
