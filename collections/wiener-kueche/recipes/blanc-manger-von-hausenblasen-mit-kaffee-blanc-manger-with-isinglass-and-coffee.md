## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cold, good cream with 8 Deka fine sifted sugar
- 1/2 liter clear, strained coffee with 20 Deka sugar
- 8 Deka dissolved, still warm isinglass (prepared with 1 liter cold water and reduced to 1/4 liter)

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 liter cold, good cream with 8 Deka fine sifted sugar into one saucepan
2. Put 1/2 liter clear, strained coffee with 20 Deka sugar into a second saucepan
3. Of 8 Deka dissolved, still warm isinglass, which has been prepared with 1 liter cold water and reduced to 1/4 liter, stir 2 parts to the warm coffee and 1 part into the warm cream
4. After setting a mold in ice 1 hour earlier, fill the prepared mass with alternating colors, 2 fingers high, starting with the black coffee mass
5. When filled, let the mass set well, which takes about 1/2 hour
6. Then turn it out, but without putting the mold in warm water beforehand; instead press somewhat on the mass with your fingers, which will loosen it more easily
