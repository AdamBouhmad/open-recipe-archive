## Ingredients

- hop stems
- salt water
- 6 tablespoons hot butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- some finely cut green parsley
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- several spoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. In early spring when hops are not yet leafed, take the stems, cook them in salt water, drain them, press them out, and cut them finely
2. Put 6 tablespoons flour in 6 tablespoons hot butter, add some finely cut green parsley, let it roast somewhat
3. Pour it with 1 1/2 liters broth and several spoonfuls cream
4. Add the cooked and finely cut hops and let this boil thoroughly
