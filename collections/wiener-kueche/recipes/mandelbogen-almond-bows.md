## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 20 decagrams fine sifted sugar
- 8 decagrams finely cut almonds
- 8 decagrams almonds cut with a sabre knife
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites in a bowl to stiff snow
2. Add 20 decagrams fine sifted sugar and stir together over the fire until hot, being very careful that the sugar does not burn
3. Add finely cut and sabre-knife-cut almonds and lemon juice
4. Stir with a pastry spoon over the fire until hot and thick throughout, being careful the mass does not burn
5. Spread this thick almond mass knife-back-thick on wafer strips that are 2 fingers wide and finger-long
6. Place on a baking sheet that has been warmed in the oven
7. Sprinkle with fine pounded sugar and dry in the oven
