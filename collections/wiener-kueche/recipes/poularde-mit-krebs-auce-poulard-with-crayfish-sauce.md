## Ingredients

- poulard
- crayfish coulis with shelled crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Clean the poulard well and prepare it for roasting
2. Once nicely roasted in its juices, arrange it on the platter and serve with a good crayfish coulis with shelled crayfish tails underneath
