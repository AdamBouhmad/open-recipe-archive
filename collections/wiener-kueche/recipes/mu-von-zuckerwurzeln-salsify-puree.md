## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Milk or strong chicken or capon broth
- Egg yolks
- Cinnamon
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Slice the salsify roots in the manner of radishes into slices
2. Simmer them in milk, or preferably in a strong chicken or capon broth until very soft
3. Press it through a linen cloth
4. Add to the pressed mixture a little milk or chicken broth, several egg yolks, cinnamon and sugar to make a thin puree
5. Alternatively, if more broth is used, a soup can be made from it
6. According to circumstances, the spices may also be omitted
