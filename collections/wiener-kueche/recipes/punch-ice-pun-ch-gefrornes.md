## Ingredients

- cream
- egg yolk
- clarified sugar
- 1 seidel good arrack or rum

## Directions

1. Take the same mass as for other ices, but clarify somewhat more sugar
2. When sufficiently frozen in the freezing container, work in a seidel of good arrack or rum gradually with the stirring spatula before serving
