## Ingredients

- Approximately 1 kilo fresh, still hard rose hips
- 1.5 liter water
- 80 Deca sugar

## Directions

1. Split the rose hips open and remove the down and seeds with a small spoon
2. Place the rose hips in a warm location for several days so they become soft
3. In a tin-lined pot, put 1.5 liters water and 80 Deca sugar, let it boil for 15 minutes
4. Add the rose hips and let them simmer together for several minutes, stirring frequently
5. Remove the rose hips from the liquid and cook the liquid until somewhat thicker
6. Fill the rose hips into jars and pour the sugar over them so that it completely covers them
7. After complete cooling, bind the jars with bladders and proceed further like the Muscatel pears (No. 1842)
