## Ingredients

- dough and plums as for Zwetschkenknödel von Germteig
- fat for frying
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as in Zwetschkenknödel von Germteig (Recipe 1225), with the difference that instead of cooking them in water, fry them in a saucepan in very hot fat
2. Remove with a slotted spoon, arrange on a plate and sprinkle with desired spice sugar
