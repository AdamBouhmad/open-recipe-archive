## Ingredients

- 32 deca finely ground blanched almonds with skin
- 32 deca finely ground sugar
- 8 deca black molasses
- some vinegar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- juice of one lemon
- some finely chopped lemon peel
- 1 wafer sheet

## Directions

1. On a kneading board mix 32 deca finely ground blanched almonds with skin, 32 deca finely ground sugar, 8 deca black molasses, and some vinegar together well
2. Make a depression in the middle, add 3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, lemon juice with some finely chopped lemon peel
3. Work everything into a dough and let rest in a cool place for 1/2 hour
4. Roll out the dough finger-thick on a floured board and cut out a round disc, which fits into the prepared buttered and floured cake form with a wafer sheet
5. Spread the dough in the form with egg wash
6. Cut the remaining dough into small, neat shapes, place them on the egg wash in any arrangement, and brush with beaten egg
7. Bake in the oven at low heat for 1/2 to 3/4 hours
8. Place on a sieve and let cool. Use this cake after 1 to 2 days as it becomes firmer with time
