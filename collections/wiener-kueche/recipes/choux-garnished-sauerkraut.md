## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Cloth

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as green cabbage (No. 329), except omit the butter sauce
2. Place the cabbage 2 fingers high on a cloth
3. Fold the cloth over it and press it with the flat hand, moving up and down repeatedly, until it has the appearance of a thumb-thick slice of wax
4. Carefully unfold the cloth
5. Cut the cabbage into finger-length pieces and use to garnish the meat
6. Some soft-cooked yellow turnips can be cut the same way and placed between the cabbage
