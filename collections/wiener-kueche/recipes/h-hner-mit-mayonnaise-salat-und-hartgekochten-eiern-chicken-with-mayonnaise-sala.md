## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Chervil leaves
- Lettuce heads
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Mayonnaise
- Aspic
- Salt
- Spices

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken as for 'Im eigenem Safte gedämpfte' (reference 822)
2. After steaming and cooling in its broth, remove and portion: cut both legs from the backbone, remove breasts from both sides without breaking the ribs, separate the spine from the thigh piece, cut remaining meat into pieces
3. Place meat in a deep bowl, dress with vinegar and one spoon of oil, sprinkle with finely cut chervil leaves, cover and let sit for several hours
4. Clean lettuce heads by removing large green outer leaves, cutting larger yellow leaves leaving only the heart, cutting hearts into sections and washing in spoonfuls with water
5. Remove lettuce, press out water well, place in a bowl, dress with vinegar and oil to taste, salt and season, mix well
6. Line the bottom of a deep platter with cut lettuce
7. Arrange meat pieces on top in layers, brushing each layer with mayonnaise, arranging breasts on top
8. Cover entire dish with mayonnaise
9. Cut hard-boiled eggs each into 4 pieces, trim one side and arrange alternately with lettuce hearts around the edge
10. Garnish the whole dish with neatly cut and chopped aspic and serve mayonnaise in a sauce boat
