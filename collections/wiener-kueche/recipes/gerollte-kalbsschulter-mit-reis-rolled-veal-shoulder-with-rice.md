## Ingredients

- 60-80 dkg boned veal shoulder
- salt
- butter or fat pieces
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and salt it
2. Tie it up
3. Place skin-side down in a pan
4. Top with butter or fat pieces
5. Add some water beside it
6. Roast in the oven for 1½ hours, basting and replenishing hot water frequently
7. Remove, untie, slice, and serve with the pan juices
