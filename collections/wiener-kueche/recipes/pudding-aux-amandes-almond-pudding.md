## Ingredients

- 12 ounces butter, beaten
- 16 ounces sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 18 ounces blanched almonds, finely ground with 2 egg whites
- 6 egg whites, beaten to snow

## Directions

1. Beat 12 ounces butter with 16 ounces sugar and 8 egg yolks for 1 hour
2. Add 18 ounces blanched almonds finely ground with 2 egg whites, mix well
3. Gently fold in the beaten snow from 6 egg whites
4. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
5. Cover and steam for 1 hour
6. Serve with fruit juice of choice
