## Ingredients

- 10 small kohlrabi
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Farce (forcemeat), approximately 1/2 pound for this quantity
- Sugar
- Soup
- Butter
- Butter sauce

## Directions

1. Remove and discard the green part, peel the kohlrabi carefully so no unevenness results
2. From the root end, cut a lid the thickness of a knife back, and hollow out the kohlrabi
3. Cook the kohlrabi and their lids in salt water until tender
4. Remove carefully and place in cold water, then on a sieve
5. When dry, fill the kohlrabi with farce
6. Place the lids on top and arrange them in a casserole smeared with butter so the lids rest on the bottom of the casserole
7. Add some sugar and pour soup over them
8. Cover the kohlrabi with a sheet of paper smeared with butter so the edges rest on the paper inside the casserole
9. Cook for 3/4 hour
10. Arrange them cone-shaped on a serving dish with the green side up and pour the sauce, which has been mixed with butter sauce, over them
