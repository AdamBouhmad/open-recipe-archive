## Ingredients

- 7 dkg butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 14 dkg sugar
- 14 dkg softened chocolate
- 6 egg whites
- 8 dkg flour
- 7 dkg sugar for filling
- 10 dkg grated chocolate for filling
- 1 tablespoon water for filling
- 3 egg yolks for filling
- ½ l whipped cream for filling
- optional: jam and glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dkg butter and gradually add 6 egg yolks, 14 dkg sugar, and 14 dkg softened chocolate
2. Soften the chocolate on a small pan briefly in the oven
3. Fold in 6 stiff egg whites together with 8 dkg flour
4. Fill the mixture into the form and bake at weaker heat
5. For the filling: beat 7 dkg sugar, 10 dkg grated chocolate, 1 tablespoon water, and 3 egg yolks in a double boiler until thick
6. Cool and fold in ½ l whipped cream
7. Fill the cake inside and brush outside, or brush inside and on top with jam and glaze white or brown
