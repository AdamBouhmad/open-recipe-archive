## Ingredients

- ham, cut small and well soaked
- broth
- white wine
- green parsley
- chives

## Directions

1. Place small pieces of ham that have been well soaked in a pan or casserole, pour a measure of broth and a glass of good white wine over it, then add some finely chopped green parsley and chives
2. Let all this boil until it becomes soft and only very little sauce remains; note that the ham must be well soaked of salt
