## Ingredients

- 1/8 liter cold water
- 4 Deca butter
- 16 Deca flour

## Directions

1. Place water and butter in a saucepan and bring to a boil
2. While stirring constantly, add flour gradually and stir over the fire until the dough is dry and pulls away from the spoon and pan
3. Spread the dough on a flour-dusted board and let cool covered
4. Roll the dough knife-back-thick, cut out with desired cutters, and place on a buttered baking sheet
5. Make light cuts with a knife, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with finely crushed salt
6. Bake until golden in the oven
7. Remove from sheet with a knife and serve with tea
