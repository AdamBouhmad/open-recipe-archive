## Ingredients

- 16 Deka butter, beaten
- 8 whole eggs
- 20 Deka peeled, diced, milk-soaked breadcrumbs, well pressed
- 8 Deka diced marrow
- 1/2 liter cooked green peas
- 1 cooked, small, diced yellow turnip
- 1 cooked, diced red turnip
- 1 hard-boiled egg, diced
- 16 Deka finely chopped, roasted meat
- 8 pieces cooked and halved chicken combs
- 1 1/8 liter broth
- some salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Add the eggs gradually to the beaten butter
2. Add the breadcrumbs, marrow, peas, yellow and red turnips, hard-boiled egg, and meat
3. Add chicken combs if available
4. Add broth, salt and pepper
5. Mix everything well together
6. Cook in a napkin in salted water like the preceding Grünbund
7. On fast days, use naturally roasted fish meat from pike, shad, turbot, carp or coalfish instead of meat; cooked stockfish flesh can also be used
8. On fast days, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
9. On meat days, garnish with small cut vegetables, roasted goose liver, and small bread pieces roasted in butter
10. Place a cooked chicken comb, roasted goose liver, or sausage piece between two toasted bread crusts on top in the middle
11. Serve quite hot with the soup
