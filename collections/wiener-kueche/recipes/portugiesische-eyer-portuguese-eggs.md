## Ingredients

- Hard-boiled egg yolks, as many as needed
- Wine
- Sugar
- Preserved lemon peel
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Pistachios

## Directions

1. Grind hard-boiled egg yolks, add wine, sugar, and preserved lemon peel.
2. Pound everything well together in a mortar.
3. Let this mixture simmer in a buttered dish over charcoal fire until it becomes a thick but not hard paste.
4. Sprinkle with nutmeg and garnish with preserved lemon peel and pistachios.
