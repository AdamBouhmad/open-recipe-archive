## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- 16 ounces butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1/4 liter boiling water
- 8 ounces crumbled butter
- lemon juice
- spices

## Directions

1. Rub together 1 heaping tablespoon flour with 16 ounces butter, salt, and nutmeg in a saucepan using a wooden spoon
2. Pour in 1/4 liter boiling water and stir quickly over heat with a wooden spoon
3. At the moment it binds, it forms a sauce, thickens, and must be removed from heat immediately
4. If it becomes too thick, add several spoonfuls of warm water
5. Then add 8 ounces crumbled butter and stir on the hot plate with a wooden spoon until the fresh butter has melted
6. It should spin from the spoon and become fluffy and light from the constant stirring
7. For asparagus, cauliflower, artichokes, and salsify, use the sauce as it is; for fish dishes, enhance the flavor with lemon juice, salt, and spices
8. If desired, you can stir in 3 egg yolks with some lemon juice into the sauce
9. Instead of lemon juice, vinegar can also be used
