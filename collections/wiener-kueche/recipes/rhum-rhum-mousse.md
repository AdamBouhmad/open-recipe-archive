## Ingredients

- 24 deca orange or 24 deca citron sugar
- juice of 4 oranges and 4 lemons
- 12 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter white wine
- 3/6 liter rum
- 4 eggs (for snow)
- 4 deca isinglass
- 1/2 liter cold water
- 1/8 liter reduction
- 1 1/2 deca gelatin
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Put 24 deca orange or citron sugar in a snow bucket with the juice of 4 oranges and 4 lemons, 12 egg yolks, 3/4 liter white wine and 3/6 liter rum
2. Beat over the fire to a thick foam
3. Remove from fire and fold in the very stiff snow from 4 eggs
4. Mix in 4 deca isinglass (prepared with 1/2 liter cold water and reduced to 1/8 liter) or add 1 1/2 deca gelatin dissolved in 1 liter water hot and whisk with the whisk until cold
5. Fill the mass into a desired mold and proceed as with Whipped Bitter Almond Cream (No. 1713)
