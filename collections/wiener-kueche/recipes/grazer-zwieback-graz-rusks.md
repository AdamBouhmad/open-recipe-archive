## Ingredients

- 5 stiff egg whites
- 13 dkg vanilla sugar
- 11 dkg flour
- 5 dkg melted, hot butter

## Directions

1. Stir 13 dkg vanilla sugar and 11 dkg flour with a wooden spoon into 5 stiff egg whites.
2. Mix in 5 dkg melted, hot butter.
3. Fill the mass into the buttered, floured biscuit form and bake slowly.
4. The next day, cut the rusks into thin slices and place on a baking rack or sheet in the open oven to dry.
5. The slices must be dried slowly and become beautifully white and fine, feeling hard on both sides.
6. Immediately roll them on a flat plate in very finely sieved vanilla sugar.
7. If well dried, they keep for a very long time.
8. The mass in the form should only be two fingers high, otherwise it will not bake through and is difficult to dry properly.
