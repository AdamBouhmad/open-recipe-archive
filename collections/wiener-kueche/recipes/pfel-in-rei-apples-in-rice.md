## Ingredients

- Several apples
- Sugar
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Rice
- Milk
- Preserves
- Candied citron or orange peel
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel some apples, leave them whole, and hollow out only the core.
2. Cook a thin syrup with lemon juice, sugar and water, and then place the apples in it.
3. When you see that they become soft, remove them and lay them on a hair sieve to drain.
4. Make a marmalade especially from some apples.
5. Butter a dish and cover the bottom with rice cooked in milk, as for rice Chaudeaux.
6. Arrange the apples on top, fill them in the middle where the core was with preserves, and cover them completely with rice.
7. Finely cut candied citron or orange peel, together with fine powdered sugar, are still sprinkled on top.
8. Let these apples in rice take on a beautiful glazed color in the oven.
