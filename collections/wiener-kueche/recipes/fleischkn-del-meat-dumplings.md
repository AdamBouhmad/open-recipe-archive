## Ingredients

- 25-30 dkg cooked beef
- 6 dkg smoked ham
- 8 dkg ham bacon
- 4 dkg bacon
- some bread crumbs
- 30 dkg salted flour
- 4-5 ladles boiling water
- 1 chopped onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- fat for roasting
- bread crumbs for serving
- sauerkraut or lettuce salad

## Directions

1. In a bowl, pour 4-5 ladles of boiling water over 30 dkg salted flour, stir smooth, and cover.
2. When cooled, form equal-sized dumplings from the dough, flatten on a floured board.
3. Place a meat ball inside each dumpling and seal the dough over it.
4. Cook the dumplings in a pot of boiling salted water for 8-10 minutes.
5. Lift onto a dish and pour over bread crumbs roasted in fat.
6. Serve with sauerkraut or lettuce salad.
7. For the filling: Heat 4 dkg fat, sauté 1 finely chopped onion and parsley, add finely chopped beef, ham, bacon, and bread crumbs with salt, pepper, and broth, simmer a few minutes, and form into equal-sized balls.
