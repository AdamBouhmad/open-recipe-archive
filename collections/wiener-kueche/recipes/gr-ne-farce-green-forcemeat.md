## Ingredients

- Several handfuls of young spinach, washed
- Water

## Directions

1. Wash the spinach and pound finely in a mortar
2. Press out the juice through a cloth into a saucepan
3. Place the expressed matter in boiling water until it coagulates like curds
4. Press through a hair sieve and let the water drain off completely
5. Mix this forcemeat into poultry forcemeats
