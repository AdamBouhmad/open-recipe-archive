## Ingredients

- 4 large green cabbages cut into quarters, or 10 small whole cabbages
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 8 ounces butter or lard
- Finely cut green parsley
- 1 liter soup
- Butter sauce
- Salt and spices

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage in salt water without covering so it does not lose its color
2. Put in cold water
3. Roast finely cut green parsley in 8 ounces butter or lard
4. Pour 1 liter soup over it
5. Add the well-pressed cabbage
6. Pour butter sauce over it, salt and spice it, and let it cook a bit longer
