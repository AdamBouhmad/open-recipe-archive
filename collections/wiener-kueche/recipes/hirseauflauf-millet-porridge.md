## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 1/3 liter hulled millet
- 12 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- a pinch of salt
- finely sifted sugar
- grated pepper cake (Lebkuchen)

## Directions

1. In 1 liter of boiling milk, stir in 1/3 liter of hulled millet while stirring constantly and let it cook well
2. When the mixture begins to thicken, add 12 tablespoons butter, 8 tablespoons sugar, and a pinch of salt and stir well
3. When the millet porridge is cooked soft and thick, arrange it in a bowl and sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and grated pepper cake, then serve
