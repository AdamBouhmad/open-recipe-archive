## Ingredients

- 56 Deca cold and firm butter
- One third of 56 Deca finest flour
- 3/4 of 56 Deca flour
- 1/8 teaspoon of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 quart of cream or sour cream
- 3 tablespoons rum or wine

## Directions

1. Beat 56 Deca cold and firm butter and one third of 56 Deca finest flour together lightly as indicated for 'Butterteig feinster Art' No. 1045.
2. Place on a flour-dusted board, cover with cloth and set aside.
3. On a board place the remaining 3/4 of 56 Deca flour, make a depression in the middle, add 1/8 teaspoon salt, 4 egg yolks, 1/4 quart of cream or sour cream, and 3 tablespoons rum or wine, and work into a smooth dough as indicated for 'Butterteig feinster Art'.
4. Place on a flour-dusted tray, cover with cloth and let rest at a cold place.
5. After 1/4 hour, place the dough on the flour-dusted board and roll out to the size of the butter.
6. Place the prepared cold butter on the rolled-out dough and proceed as indicated for 'Butterteig feinster Art' (No. 1045).
7. Use for pastries, croustades, biscuits, strudels, etc.
