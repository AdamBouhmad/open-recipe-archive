## Ingredients

- 40 deca flour
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/16 liter warm cream
- 1 egg and 3 egg yolks beaten in 1/16 liter cream
- 4 deca melted butter
- flour for dusting
- butter for greasing the pan
- butter in small pea-sized pieces
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Place 40 deca flour, 1 teaspoon sugar and 1 tablespoon salt in a deep bowl
2. Dissolve 2 deca fresh yeast in 1/16 liter warm cream
3. Beat 1 egg and 3 egg yolks in 1/16 liter cream and add to the yeast mixture
4. Pour this into the flour mixture and stir together into a dough
5. Add 4 deca melted butter, stir well and beat the dough until it separates from the spoon
6. Cover with a cloth and let it rest in a warm place
7. When the dough has risen, place it on a flour-dusted board and roll out to the thickness of a small finger
8. Cut out round flat pieces using a doughnut cutter and place on a buttered baking sheet
9. Make 3 indentations in each piece with a spoon handle
10. Place pea-sized pieces of butter in each indentation and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
11. Bake in a fairly hot oven until golden and serve warm
