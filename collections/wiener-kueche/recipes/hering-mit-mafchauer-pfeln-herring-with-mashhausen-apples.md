## Ingredients

- 1 completely prepared herring
- Mashhausen apples, peeled
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring according to previous instructions and peel the Mashhausen apples
2. Cut both the herring and apples into cubes
3. Mix well together with lemon juice or vinegar and oil
4. Arrange in a serving bowl
