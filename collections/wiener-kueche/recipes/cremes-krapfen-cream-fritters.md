## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter cream
- 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 6 egg yolks
- Butter for the mold
- Flour for the mold
- Flour for dredging
- Eggs for coating
- Breadcrumbs for rolling
- Lard for frying
- Powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter cream in a deep dish and stir it well with 8 tablespoons vanilla sugar, 1 tablespoon flour and 6 egg yolks.
2. Fill it into a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour and let it steam covered for 1/2 hour.
3. After this time, remove the mold, cover it with a dish, turn it over and carefully remove it.
4. After completely cooling, cut out fritters of any desired size with a round cutter, or cut strips into square pieces.
5. Dip these in beaten eggs, roll in breadcrumbs and fry them on both sides until golden yellow in very hot lard, shaking the pan frequently.
6. Remove them with a skimming spoon, arrange them mixed with other similar dishes in a dish, sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and serve hot.
