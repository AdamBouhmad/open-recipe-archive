## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons finely cut capers
- 1 crushed shallot or garlic
- Stock
- Vinegar or wine
- Hay
- 1/2 bay leaf
- 1 bunch thyme

## Directions

1. Place 8 tablespoons butter in a cassette dish and let it become hot, add 8 tablespoons flour
2. Into this roux, add 6 tablespoons finely cut capers, 1 crushed shallot or garlic, and let these roast slightly
3. Pour in with a little stock and the necessary vinegar or wine, stirring constantly, and bring to boil
4. Skim off the fat cleanly
5. Let hay, 1/2 bay leaf, and 1 bunch of thyme cook slowly with occasional stirring for about 1/2 hour
6. Strain the sauce through a sieve, let it become hot once more, and serve
