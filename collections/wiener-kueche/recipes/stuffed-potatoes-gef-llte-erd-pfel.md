## Ingredients

- Large potatoes (not floury)
- Butter
- Eggs
- Lemon zest
- Nutmeg flower
- Salt
- Cream (Schmette)
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take large but not floury potatoes, scrape them clean, rinse them out, cut off a top slice, and hollow them out.
2. Beat a piece of butter with a couple of eggs, lemon zest, nutmeg flower, salt, a little cream, and the hollowed-out parts, and add as many bread crumbs as are necessary to make the forcemeat somewhat thick; then fill the hollowed-out potatoes [remainder of recipe cut off]
