## Ingredients

- 3 measures good Rhenish or Frankish wine
- cinnamon
- cloves
- lemon peel
- 0.5 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Place 3 measures of good Rhenish or Frankish wine with some cinnamon, cloves and lemon peel in a soup pot
2. Sweeten with 0.5 pound sugar
3. Heat on hot ashes until boiling hot
4. Ignite with burning paper
5. Let it burn until it extinguishes itself
6. Strain through a napkin
7. Serve warm
