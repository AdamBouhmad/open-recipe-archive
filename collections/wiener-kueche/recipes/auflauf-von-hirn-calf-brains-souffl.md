## Ingredients

- One whole calf brain
- Lukewarm water
- One-quarter pound butter
- Equal amount finely pounded almonds
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- Interior of one roll
- One Seidel cream
- One-quarter pound sugar
- One lemon, zest grated

## Directions

1. Place the whole calf brain in lukewarm water and clean the red veins away carefully.
2. Blanch it slightly, let it dry on a hair sieve, and cut it finely with a knife.
3. Beat one-quarter pound butter to foam and add an equal amount finely pounded almonds, 5 whole eggs, and 5 egg yolks.
4. Take the interior of one roll, soak it in one Seidel cream, and beat it together with the brain, one-quarter pound sugar, and grated lemon zest for a quarter hour vigorously.
5. Coat a rimmed dish with butter, fill the mass into it, and bake it slowly and carefully.
