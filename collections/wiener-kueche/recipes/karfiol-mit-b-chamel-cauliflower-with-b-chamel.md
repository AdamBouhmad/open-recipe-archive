## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower
- 2 dag Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 liter milk
- 4 dag butter
- flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower head until soft, drain it, place in a buttered dish and brush with cooled béchamel, sprinkle with grated cheese or breadcrumbs, gratinée in the oven 15-20 minutes.
2. Serve in the dish.
3. For the béchamel: heat 4 dag butter in a smaller casserole, add flour to make a light roux, pour in the milk, salt it, let it cook down to a thick consistency for a few minutes and cool completely.
4. Stir in 3 egg yolks, then fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites, spread this mixture over the cauliflower.
