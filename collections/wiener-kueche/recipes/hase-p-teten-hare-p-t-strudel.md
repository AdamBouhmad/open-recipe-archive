## Ingredients

- Butter
- 1 onion
- Root vegetables
- 1 kilo chopped pork meat
- Young hare liver
- ¼ liter red wine
- Spices
- Thyme
- 5 crushed juniper berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 32 deca flour
- Butter dough
- Egg

## Directions

1. Put butter, 1 onion, and root vegetables, all thinly sliced, into a casserole.
2. Add 1 kilo chopped pork meat and braise covered.
3. Add the young hare liver, ¼ liter red wine, spices, thyme, 5 crushed juniper berries, and 1 bay leaf, and braise covered until soft.
4. When done, remove all meat from the bones, strain the liquid, and finely pound all remaining ingredients in a mortar, then pass through a sieve. It should be as thick as a purée.
5. Make butter dough from 32 deca flour of the finest kind and roll it out strong, knife-back thickness, in strips 2 hand-widths wide and ½ meter long.
6. Spread the meat mixture about 1 finger high and 3 fingers wide in the middle of the strips.
7. Fold the dough from one side over the meat, brush the top with egg, then fold from the second side over it, so that it overlaps only a finger width, and brush with egg as well, but leave the edge of this dough side 1 finger wide unbrashed (like 'Butter Dough Strudel').
8. Score the dough several times with a knife and place the filled butter dough on a baking sheet wetted with cold water.
9. Bake at fairly strong heat.
10. Cut into desired pieces and serve cold or warm as an appetizer for supper or tea.
