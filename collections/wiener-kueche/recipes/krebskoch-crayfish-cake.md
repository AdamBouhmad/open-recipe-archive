## Ingredients

- the crust from a small roll
- one measure cream or milk
- half a pound crayfish butter
- seven pounds finely ground almonds
- six egg yolks
- twenty-five crayfish tails, finely chopped then ground
- one lemon, zest grated on sugar
- sugar to taste
- dough for the rim

## Directions

1. Cut the crust from a small roll and simmer it in one measure cream or milk like a child's pudding
2. Allow it to cool
3. Prepare half a pound crayfish butter and allow it to cool
4. Take seven pounds finely ground almonds, six egg yolks, and twenty-five crayfish tails that have been finely chopped and then ground
5. Put all of this into the pudding
6. Stir it well for one and a half hours
7. Grate one lemon on sugar and add the grated zest
8. Sweeten it to taste with sugar
9. Make a rim of dough on a sheet pan and bake it
10. Spread the sheet and rim with crayfish butter
11. Pour the stirred mixture into it
12. Bake it in a heated oven for three-quarters of an hour and serve it sweetened
