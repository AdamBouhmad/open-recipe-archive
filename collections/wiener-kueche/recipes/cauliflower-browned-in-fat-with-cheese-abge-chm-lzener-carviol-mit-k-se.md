## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Grated Parmesan or Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Split the cauliflower into several pieces, peel off all the hard rind, cut the pieces lengthwise to be fairly equal, and throw them into cold water to remove the raw taste.
2. Simmer it in salted water until tender, arrange it in a dish brushed with butter, and press it down with a clean cloth so it becomes fairly level on top.
3. Sprinkle it with melted butter and spread it fairly thickly with grated Parmesan or Swiss cheese.
4. Bake it in a hot oven until the cheese turns a golden-yellow crust.
