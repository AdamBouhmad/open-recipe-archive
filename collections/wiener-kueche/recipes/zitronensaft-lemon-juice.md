## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Lemon juice
- Citric acid dissolved in water

## Directions

1. To preserve the natural color of peeled apples, pears and other peeled fruit that one wishes to cook in steam, use lemons, lemon juice, or dissolved citric acid with which the fruits are rubbed as explained below, and then thrown into cold water mixed with lemon juice
2. A more economical alternative to this is dissolved citric acid
