## Ingredients

- Eel
- Warm butter
- Salt
- Maitre d'hôtel butter

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the eel and cut into desired pieces
2. Brush with warm butter on both sides and sprinkle with salt
3. Fry golden on both sides on the grill
4. Arrange in a bowl and serve with Maitre d'hôtel butter
5. Alternatively, the eel can be fried in the same manner on a flat pan
