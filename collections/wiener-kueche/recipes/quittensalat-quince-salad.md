## Ingredients

- Beautiful large quinces
- Water
- Good wine
- Sugar
- Lemon peel
- Whole cinnamon
- Cloves
- Almonds
- Citron
- Spanish sour cherries

## Directions

1. Take beautiful large quinces, peel them and cut them in half
2. Remove the seeds
3. Cook them in water until they become soft to the touch
4. Pour off the water and add good wine and sugar
5. Let them cook together until soft
6. Arrange them on a serving dish so they remain beautifully white
7. Add to the sauce lemon peel, whole cinnamon and cloves
8. Let them reduce to a nice thick consistency, strain them and pour the sauce over the quinces
9. Cover them with oblong-cut almonds, citron and Spanish sour cherries
