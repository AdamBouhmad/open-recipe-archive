## Ingredients

- roach (Maurachen)
- butter
- finely chopped green parsley
- flour
- pea broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg flower
- cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roach until completely clean
2. Braise finely cut roach with butter and finely chopped green parsley
3. Dust with one spoonful of flour and braise again
4. Add as much pea broth as needed
5. Salt and pepper it
6. Let it simmer well with a little nutmeg flower
7. Before serving, you can add one spoonful of cream and serve over toasted bread slices
