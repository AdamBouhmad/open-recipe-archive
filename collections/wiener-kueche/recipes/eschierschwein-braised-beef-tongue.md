## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- salt water
- bacon
- yellow turnips
- parsley root
- celery
- bay leaf
- mugwort
- 1 liter white wine
- 1/4 liter soup
- yellow turnips or other vegetables for garnish
- rice, lentil purée, or puff pastry cut out with cookie cutters

## Directions

1. Cut the beef tongue into half-finger-length pieces and cook in salt water for 1/2 hour.
2. Meanwhile, line a casserole with some bacon, 1 sliced yellow turnip, 1 parsley root, 1 piece of celery, all cut in leaf-like pieces, 1 bay leaf, and some bunches of mugwort.
3. Place the pre-boiled tongue pieces on top, cover, and let brown.
4. Add 1 liter of white wine and 1/4 liter of soup and let braise covered for 3 hours slowly.
5. Arrange the tongue pieces on a serving dish, garnish with the yellow turnips that braised with it or with other desired vegetables, or serve with rice, lentil purée, or puff pastry cut out with cookie cutters.
6. Skim fat from the braising liquid, pass through a sieve, and serve in a sauce boat.
