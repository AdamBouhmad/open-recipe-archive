## Ingredients

- 12½ dkg raw grated almonds
- 17 dkg sugar
- 3-4 egg whites, added gradually

## Directions

1. Stir the raw grated almonds and sugar with 3-4 egg whites, added gradually, to a stiff mass
2. Spread it thinly on wafers
3. Cut strips from it
4. Sprinkle with soaked chopped almonds
5. Place on an unbuttered baking sheet
6. Bake at low heat
7. Immediately place over a pastry roller while hot
