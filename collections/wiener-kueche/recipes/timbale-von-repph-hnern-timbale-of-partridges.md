## Ingredients

- 4 prepared partridges
- Butter
- Salt
- Fine herbs
- Pepper
- Spices
- 1 glass of wine
- Shortcrust dough
- Bacon sheet
- Eggs
- Spanish or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts and legs from the prepared partridges
2. Brown them in butter with salt, fine herbs, pepper, and spices
3. Add a glass of wine and cook everything until tender, then let cool
4. Roll out shortcrust dough to finger thickness
5. Line a buttered casserole symmetrically with the dough, pressing firmly so it does not fold
6. Cover the bottom and sides with the forcemeat
7. Arrange the partridges in the casserole
8. Cover them with the remaining forcemeat and the herbs in which they were cooked
9. Top with a thin bacon sheet
10. Make a proper lid from the remaining dough, brush the protruding pastry edge with eggs, place the lid on top and seal well
11. Make a small hole in the center of the lid
12. Bake slowly for approximately 2 hours
13. Turn out onto a plate, carefully remove the lid so it does not break, and make small openings in the forcemeat with a knife
14. When serving, gradually pour Spanish or piquant sauce into the timbale
