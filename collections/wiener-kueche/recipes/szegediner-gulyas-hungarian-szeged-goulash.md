## Ingredients

- 30 dkg Schweinsschulter (pork shoulder)
- 25 dkg jungschweinernes Bauchfleisch (young pork belly)
- ½ kg Sauerkraut (sauerkraut)
- ¼ l Rahm (sour cream)
- 7 dkg Speck (bacon)
- Zwiebel (onion)
- 4 dkg Fett (fat)
- Paprika (paprika)
- Kümmel (caraway)
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, cut into cubes and salt it.
2. Cut the bacon into small cubes and lightly brown it with finely chopped onion in 4 dkg hot fat.
3. Add the meat, then add the lightly washed and cut sauerkraut, paprika, caraway and some water.
4. Let it braise covered for 1-1½ hours.
5. Add the sour cream and braise for another 20 minutes.
6. Serve the meat on a round platter.
