## Ingredients

- 2 dl unsweetened strawberry pulp
- 2 lemons
- 2 dl spun sugar (approximately 18 dkg cube sugar cooked with 1 dl water for 15 minutes)
- juice of 1½ lemons
- 2 dl cold water

## Directions

1. Mix the strawberry pulp with 2 dl spun sugar, the juice of 1½ lemons and 2 dl cold water, and fill into a tall, narrow container.
2. Insert the sugar scale and it should read 18 degrees.
3. If it sinks too deep, add more sugar; if it doesn't sink deep enough, add cold water until the scale shows the correct degree.
4. The solution must not have fewer than 18 degrees, but also not more, as the mass will otherwise not freeze.
5. Strain through a hair sieve and freeze (see Vanilla Ice Cream).
6. Alternatively, 25 dkg wild strawberries can be sieved, measured and prepared in the same way.
