## Ingredients

- 1/8 liter peas
- 1/8 liter beans
- 1/8 liter lentils
- 3 tablespoons groats
- 2 tablespoons rice
- some diced root vegetables
- some diced potatoes
- some salt
- some spices
- 8 Deciliter diced bacon
- either 4 kilos smoked meat (so-called Kaiser-fleisch), or 2 doves, or 1/2 kilo calf meat, or 1 young chicken
- 3 liters water
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Put peas, beans, lentils, groats, rice, diced root vegetables, diced potatoes, salt, spices, and diced bacon in a pot
2. Cut meat into arbitrary pieces and add to the above
3. Cover with 3 liters water and cook slowly
4. When it is well cooked such that the meat is tender and the soup is thick, add toasted bread and serve
