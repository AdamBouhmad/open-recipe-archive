## Ingredients

- About 8 tablespoons fat
- Some finely sliced onion
- Equal amount of root vegetables finely sliced
- Some herbs and spices
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons vinegar
- About 1/2 liter soup
- Some tablespoons sour cream
- Some salt
- Some lemon zest

## Directions

1. In a saucepan add about 8 tablespoons fat, some finely sliced onion, equal amount of finely sliced root vegetables, some herbs and spices
2. Let it roast and dust with 8 tablespoons flour
3. Add 6 tablespoons vinegar, about 1/2 liter soup, some tablespoons sour cream, some salt and some lemon zest
4. Let it cook very well
