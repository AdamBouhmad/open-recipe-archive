## Ingredients

- Turbot
- Salt water
- Sauce Hollandaise

## Directions

1. The turbot, the largest of the flatfish, has a very appetizing, though somewhat firm flesh
2. The flesh on the underside, which is covered with white skin, is finer than that on the upper side, from which the scales must be removed and the entrails near the head removed
3. Boil the turbot in salt water 10 minutes longer than freshwater fish
4. Serve with Sauce Hollandaise
