## Ingredients

- prepared as Maraschino Ice
- any desired liqueur
- cochineal coloring
- 1/2 liter beaten cream

## Directions

1. Prepare as Ice with Maraschino (Recipe 1743)
2. Use any desired liqueur instead and after the ice is stirred, it can be colored pink with some cochineal coloring
3. When this liqueur ice is frozen solid and worked well, it can also be mixed with 1/2 liter beaten cream and served in glasses
