## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- pepper
- lemons
- pork rind
- fat
- sour cream
- capers
- mustard

## Directions

1. Season the pork loin with salt and pepper, lay peeled lemon slices cut the thickness of a knife-back on it, spread the thin rind of pork over it
2. Place in a pan containing fat and roast in the oven
3. Shortly before serving, pour some sour cream mixed with a tablespoon of capers over it and let it roast in well
4. Cut the meat lengthwise into desired pieces, arrange on a platter, pour the defatted juices over it and serve with braised rice, turnips, various vegetables or salad
5. Mustard is served with this meat
