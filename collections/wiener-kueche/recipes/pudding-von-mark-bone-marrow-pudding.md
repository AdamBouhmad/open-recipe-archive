## Ingredients

- 1/2 measure warm cream
- 1 roll (Mundsemel)
- 1 pound finely cut pure bone marrow
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons brandy
- butter for the cloth

## Directions

1. Soak a rubbed roll, cut into pieces, in half a measure of warm cream.
2. Stir it together with 1 pound finely cut pure bone marrow, 1/2 pound sugar, 4 eggs, and 2 tablespoons brandy until quite thick.
3. Butter a cloth and proceed as with the others.
