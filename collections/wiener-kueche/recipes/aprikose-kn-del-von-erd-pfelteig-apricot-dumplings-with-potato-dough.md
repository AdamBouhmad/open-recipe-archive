## Ingredients

- potato dough as for Abgeschmalzene Erdäpfelnudeln, Recipe 1209
- apricots

## Directions

1. Prepare potato dough as specified in Abgeschmalzene Erdäpfelnudeln (Recipe 1209)
2. Roll it out on a flour-dusted board very thin
3. Proceed as specified in Zwetschkenknödel von Strudelteig (Recipe 1223)
