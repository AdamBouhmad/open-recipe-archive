## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned, trimmed, beaten, trussed capon
- game marinade
- butter
- capers
- cleaned, finely diced anchovies
- cream
- meat glaze
- watercress
- lemon wedges
- simulated snipe liver

## Directions

1. A cleaned, trimmed, beaten, trussed capon is larded, placed in a suitable vessel, covered with game marinade and, if time permits, left to soak for 1 day in the marinade
2. 8 hours is also sufficient
3. After this time, remove the capon and roast it by frequent basting with butter for 1 to 1 and 1/2 hours on a spit
4. Or place the capon in a pan containing butter and roast it under frequent basting with the resulting juices in the oven
5. If desired, a butter sauce may be made with some marinade and simmered well
6. After this, add some cream, and once this has thinned, add 1 tablespoon of capers and cleaned, finely diced anchovies
7. Let the sauce simmer once and serve it in a sauceboat with the capon
8. The capon, which has been cut into pieces of choice and reassembled on a platter, is garnished with simulated snipe liver, lemon wedges and watercress
