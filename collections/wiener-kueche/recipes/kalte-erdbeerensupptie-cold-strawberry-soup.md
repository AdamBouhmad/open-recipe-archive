## Ingredients

- 2 liters ripe strawberries
- 1 kilo crushed sugar
- 2 liters white wine (preferably Rhine wine)
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Lay 2 liters ripe strawberries on a cloth and select with a needle approximately one-third of the most beautiful berries, which are then placed in a vessel and set on ice.
2. Press the remaining strawberries through a sieve into an earthenware bowl.
3. Stir the sieved strawberries with approximately 1 kilo crushed sugar until well combined.
4. Pour 2 liters white wine (preferably Rhine wine) with the juice of 1 lemon into the mixture.
5. Strain the whole once more through a sieve into a soup pot already buried in ice.
6. Cover the pot and let it sit cold for approximately 3 to 4 hours.
7. Add the whole strawberries, stir everything together lightly, and serve in a deep glass or porcelain bowl at table in soup bowls or in glasses suitable for this purpose.
