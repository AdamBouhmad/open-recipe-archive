## Ingredients

- 1 liter fine green peas
- Salt water
- 12 drams butter
- 1 tablespoon finely cut green parsley
- 1 spoonful cream
- 3 spoonfuls white broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. After 1 liter of fine green peas have been boiled tender in salt water, drain the liquid and place the peas in a casserole; add 12 drams butter worked well with 1 tablespoon finely cut green parsley and 1 spoonful cream; stir in 3 spoonfuls white broth and mix well; let stand covered on the warm stove until needed.
