## Ingredients

- veal leg
- sweet, rinsed milk (optional)
- cold water
- salt
- truffles (optional)
- bacon
- small sausage, smoked and cooked
- fat

## Directions

1. After the veal leg has been trimmed as indicated for the spit-roasted version, if it is not white enough, soak it 1 hour in sweet, rinsed milk.
2. Remove it from there, wash it in cold water, salt it well and lard the leg, if desired, with truffles, bacon, a small sausage (smoked, cooked)—namely: prick the meat with a small knife and as you withdraw the knife, insert pencil-thick, half-finger-long, salted bacon inside.
3. In this way the leg can be larded more or less heavily.
4. Place the leg in a pan to which fat has been added, pour some hot fat over it and roast it in the oven at moderate heat for 2–3 hours depending on the size of the leg, frequently basting with the sauce that forms.
