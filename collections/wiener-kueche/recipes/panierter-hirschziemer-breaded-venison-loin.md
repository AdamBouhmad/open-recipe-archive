## Ingredients

- venison loin
- twine
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- whole pepper
- fine herbs
- bay leaves
- rosemary
- cloves
- onions, sliced
- grated rye bread crumbs
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- cloves
- chopped almonds
- grated lemon peel
- egg, beaten
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Wash the loin clean and tie it with twine so it does not fall apart
2. Place it in a large pot with water and vinegar over heat
3. Season with salt, whole pepper, fine herbs, bay leaves, rosemary, cloves, and large sliced onions
4. Let it cook through completely
5. Mix grated rye bread crumbs with sugar, ground cinnamon, cloves, chopped almonds, and grated lemon peel
6. Remove the loin from the pot onto a baking pan and loosen the twine
7. Brush the meat with beaten egg and spread the grated and seasoned bread mixture over it
8. Pour some butter or fat over it, place it in the oven, and let it become golden
9. Once browned, serve it warm. As a variation, one can also brush the loin on the surface with a sugar glaze and let it dry in the oven
