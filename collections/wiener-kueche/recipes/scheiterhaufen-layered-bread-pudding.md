## Ingredients

- 1/3 liter flour
- salt
- boiling salted water
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- 30 decagrams cooked, lean, finely sliced ham
- grated cheese
- 7.5 teaspoons salt
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 1/3 liter flour
2. Cut the finely rolled cheese into pieces of desired size
3. Put the pieces into boiling salted water
4. Cook covered until they float on the surface of the water
5. Pour into a colander, rinse with cold water, and let stand
6. In a deep bowl, beat 8 egg yolks with 1/2 liter sour cream, 30 decagrams cooked, lean, finely sliced ham, grated cheese, and 7.5 teaspoons salt one after another
7. Gently fold in the snow of 8 beaten egg whites
8. Fill a buttered, flour-dusted mold with this mixture
9. Bake in the oven according to the height of the mixture; for example, 1/2 hour if the mixture is half-high, 1 hour if it is higher
