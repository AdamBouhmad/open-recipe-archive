## Ingredients

- 6 Maß water
- 2 handfuls barley or wheat malt
- 1 coffeespoon cream of tartar salt
- 1 knife point cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Take 6 measures of water and 2 handfuls of barley or wheat malt
2. Cook slowly over gentle heat until reduced to 2 measures
3. Cool until lukewarm
4. Add 1 coffeespoon of cream of tartar salt and 1 knife point of cream of tartar
5. You will have usable yeast which need only be cleaned through a sieve
