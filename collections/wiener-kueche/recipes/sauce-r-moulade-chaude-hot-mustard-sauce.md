## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons French mustard
- 1/4 liter cold white beef stock
- 1 strong pinch of salt
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon rosehip marmalade (optional)

## Directions

1. Into a deep cassette dish or snowball whisk, place 8 tablespoons butter, 6 egg yolks, 6 tablespoons French mustard, 1/4 liter cold white beef stock, and 1 strong pinch of salt
2. Whisk over the fire with the whisk and bring to the proper heat, taking special care that it does not boil
3. Slowly drizzle in the juice of 1/2 lemon
4. This sauce is served with all kinds of meat dishes, for roasting, game birds and the like
5. When desired, add 1 tablespoon rosehip marmalade
