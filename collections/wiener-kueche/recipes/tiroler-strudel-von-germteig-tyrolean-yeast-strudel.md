## Ingredients

- 14 deca butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 2 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter milk
- 30 deca flour
- almond filling as specified in Almond Strudel of Butter Dough (No. 1090)
- raisins
- currants
- finely chopped citron

## Directions

1. Beat butter fluffy, gradually add sugar, salt, and egg yolks
2. Mix in the yeast dissolved in milk and flour, mix well
3. Beat the dough until it lifts from the spoon
4. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled in height
5. Roll out dough on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a small finger
6. Brush with almond filling, sprinkle with raisins, currants, and citron
7. Roll up the dough and place on a butter-greased baking sheet
8. Cover and let rise in a warm place for 1/2 hour
9. Brush with melted butter and bake at moderate heat for 3/4 hour
10. Serve warm or cold at table with cream or custard milk
