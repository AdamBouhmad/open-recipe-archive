## Ingredients

- 18 lemons
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 seidel water

## Directions

1. Rub half of 18 lemons on a pound of sugar
2. Scrape the yellow from the sugar into a terrine
3. Press the juice from the 18 lemons into it
4. Clarify the remaining sugar with a seidel of water
5. Add the cooled sugar to the mass
6. Pass everything through a fine sieve
7. Pour into the freezing container and prepare
