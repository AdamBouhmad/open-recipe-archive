## Ingredients

- Calf brain, freed of skin, cooked in salted water and vinegar
- Mayonnaise
- Aspic, chopped

## Directions

1. After the brain is completely cooled, cut into pieces of choice
2. Pile on the edge of a platter, garnish with chopped aspic
3. Place on ice for a while
4. Before serving at table, cover the brain with several spoonfuls of desired mayonnaise
5. Serve mayonnaise additionally in a saucepan
