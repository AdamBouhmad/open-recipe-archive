## Ingredients

- whole udder
- salt
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook a whole udder very tender, then cut it into slices the thickness of a knife back, sprinkle them with salt, turn each slice in melted butter and in breadcrumbs, grill or fry them on both sides on the grate.
2. Meanwhile, an herb sauce is prepared.
