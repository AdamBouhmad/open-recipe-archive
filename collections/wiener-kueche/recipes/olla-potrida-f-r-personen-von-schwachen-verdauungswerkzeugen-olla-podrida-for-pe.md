## Ingredients

- 1 Loth ham
- 2 pounds beef
- 1 pound veal
- 1 young chicken
- 1 young pigeon
- Roots, herbs, and vegetables

## Directions

1. Put the ham, beef, veal, young chicken, and young pigeon into a pot over the fire
2. Let the meat sufficiently foam and cook
3. Add as many roots, herbs, and vegetables as the market offers
4. Let it cook thoroughly
5. Arrange the vegetable soup in the middle of the table and the four types of meat with their accompaniments of anchovies, fresh butter, radish, cucumber salad, etc. around it
