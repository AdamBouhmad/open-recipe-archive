## Ingredients

- salmon
- truffles
- goose liver
- rooster combs
- calf's sweetbreads
- mushrooms
- onions
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- grated bacon
- egg yolk
- bacon
- ham
- wine
- strong braise
- piquant sauce
- fried bread slices
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the salmon thoroughly in the same manner as above
2. Stuff the belly with a good salpicon or ragout made from truffles, goose liver, rooster combs, calf's sweetbreads, and mushrooms all cut into dice, along with onions, parsley, salt, pepper, fine herbs, grated bacon and some egg yolk - this mixture must be well prepared beforehand
3. Once the skin is removed, lard the salmon with truffles, bacon and ham
4. Cook it slowly in a strong braise with wine
5. Skim off the fat
6. Dress and glaze the salmon
7. Serve a piquant sauce underneath
8. Garnish with fried bread slices, rooster combs, crayfish tails, truffles and other garnishes
