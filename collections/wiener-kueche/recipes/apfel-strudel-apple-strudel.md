## Ingredients

- Strudel dough made from 1/2 liter flour
- crème made from 12 deca butter and 16 deca vanilla sugar, beaten cold
- 6 egg yolks, stirred in gradually
- 6 egg whites, snow, lightly mixed in
- peeled apples, cut into thin slices
- selected raisins
- currants
- finely ground sugar
- cinnamon, as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from 1/2 liter flour and let it rest
2. Beat 12 deca butter with 16 deca vanilla sugar until cold
3. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
4. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Stretch the dough until thin
6. Spread with the crème mixture
7. Layer with peeled apples cut into thin slices
8. Sprinkle with selected raisins, currants, and finely ground sugar, and cinnamon as desired
9. Treat further as for Ham Strudel (No. 1229)
10. Cut into desired pieces and arrange in a bowl
11. Serve dusted with sugar
