## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- Corresponding amount of flour
- 1-2 liters boiling milk
- 8 Deca butter
- 8 egg yolks
- Salt
- Snow from 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a grated barley bread from 2 eggs and a corresponding amount of flour, add it to 1-2 liters boiling milk and let it cook through
2. Beat 8 Deca butter until fluffy, add 8 egg yolks and some salt and stir in the prepared barley
3. Lightly mix in the snow from 8 eggs
4. Further handling is as with Griesschöberl, except the barley schöberl comes to the table together with the soup
