## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- white or red wine
- white or blue grapes
- cinnamon as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare like the quince sauce, pouring white wine with white grapes and red wine with blue grapes
2. If desired, add 1 strong pinch of cinnamon
