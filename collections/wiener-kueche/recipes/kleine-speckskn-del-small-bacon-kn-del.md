## Ingredients

- 14 deca fresh bacon
- 24 deca bread crumbs
- milk
- 6 deca butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- some salt
- 6 deca flour

## Directions

1. Cut the fresh bacon into fine dice and place in a saucepan.
2. Heat it on the fire until the bacon is glossy.
3. Add the bread crumbs that have been cut into dice and soaked in milk.
4. Mix well together.
5. Beat 6 deca butter until creamy.
6. Stir in 3 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, and salt.
7. Add 6 deca flour and the bacon-bread mixture.
