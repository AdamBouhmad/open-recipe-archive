## Ingredients

- 1 pike of 1-1¼ kg
- much water
- 10 spoonfuls salt
- 1/4 l vinegar
- 2 lemons for garnish
- 4 h capers
- 1 red beet
- butter
- lemon juice
- caviar
- sauce tartare
- parsley
- hard eggs

## Directions

1. In a fish pan, bring much water with 10 spoonfuls of salt and 1/4 l vinegar to a boil.
2. Clean and wash the pike, tie it to the insert, lower it into the fish pan, then immediately pull it back out.
3. Let it simmer for 10-12 minutes depending on the size of the fish.
4. Let it cool first in the broth, then remove it.
5. Once completely cooled and untied from the insert, place it on the fish dish.
6. Spray it on the sides with butter that has been beaten beforehand, drizzle it with lemon juice.
7. Decorate it with capers, top with lemon slices that have been topped with caviar.
8. Serve it with tartare sauce.
9. Garnish it with parsley, hard eggs, and red beet slices.
