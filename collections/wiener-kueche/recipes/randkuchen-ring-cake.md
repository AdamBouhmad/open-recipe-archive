## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound butter
- 12 egg yolks
- zest of one lemon
- 1 pound fine flour
- half pound sugar
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir three-quarters pound butter with 12 egg yolks and lemon zest to foam
2. Mix 1 pound fine flour with half pound sugar together
3. Fold in the peaks of 6 eggs
4. Draw everything into the mass
5. Bake slowly in a ring form
