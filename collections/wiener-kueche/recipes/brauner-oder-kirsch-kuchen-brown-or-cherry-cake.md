## Ingredients

- 30 deca finely pounded sugar
- 14 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg
- 32 deca almonds with skin
- 14 egg whites
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- 12 deca flour
- cherries or morello cherries

## Directions

1. Stir together 30 deca finely pounded sugar, 14 egg yolks, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, and 1 nutmeg for 3/4 hour
2. Add 32 deca almonds with skin, finely pounded and prepared
3. Lightly fold in the snow from 14 egg whites at the same time as 2 tablets grated chocolate and 12 deca flour
4. Fill this mixture into a form brushed with butter and dusted with flour
5. If desired, cherries or morello cherries are allowed to sink into the dough
6. Bake for approximately 1 hour at low heat in the oven
7. Turn the cake out onto a sieve and serve only the following day
