## Ingredients

- barley
- broth
- roasted bread pieces
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare barley the same way as rice, but barley must cook 1 hour longer than rice
2. When serving, add roasted bread pieces to the barley cream soup
3. Serve with 1 lemon cut into 8 pieces
4. This soup is recommended for sick people
