## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Anchovy butter
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Aspic
- Butter
- Caviar
- 8 olives
- 1 teaspoon capers
- Smoked salmon
- Mayonnaise or mustard (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the brain in salt water, then cut into pieces when completely cooled
2. Arrange the cut brain alternately around a dish with anchovy butter and hard-boiled eggs
3. Line the edge of the dish with chopped aspic
4. In the center place a basket formed from ordinary butter, which is filled with caviar
5. Cover the whole with 8 olives from which the pits have been removed
6. Sprinkle with about 1 teaspoon capers and add some pieces of smoked salmon cut into small pieces
7. Alternative arrangement: use brain, head lettuce, hard-boiled eggs and anchovy butter instead of the butter basket
