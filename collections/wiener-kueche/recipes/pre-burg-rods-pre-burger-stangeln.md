## Ingredients

- 8 deca butter
- 8 deca flour
- 8 deca almonds finely pounded with their skin and passed through a sieve
- 8 deca vanilla sugar
- 1 pinch nutmeg
- finely cut lemon zest to equal measure
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place butter and flour on a board and rub finely together with hands
2. Add almonds, vanilla sugar, nutmeg, and lemon zest
3. Work quickly with 2 egg yolks into a smooth dough and let become stiff covered in a very cool place
4. Roll out on a flour-dusted board to the thickness of a small finger
5. Cut into thumb-width and finger-length rods and place at finger-width distance on a butter-greased baking sheet
6. Brush with beaten eggs and bake pale yellow at low heat in the oven
