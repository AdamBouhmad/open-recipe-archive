## Ingredients

- 1 measure of stock (Schü)
- 1/2 pound lean ham cut into cubes
- 2 to 3 spoonfuls of flour
- good stock

## Directions

1. Let 1 measure of stock reduce over moderately strong heat with 1/2 pound of lean ham cut into cubes until it becomes like a thick syrup or resembles dissolved glue
2. Stir in 2 to 3 spoonfuls of flour
3. Add again as much good stock so that a quarter of it must reduce to become properly thick or creamy
4. Stir this broth on the fire until it boils
5. Let it simmer on slow heat from the side
6. Remove all foam and fat coming to the surface with a spoon
7. Let it simmer until it becomes properly thick or creamy
8. Strain through cheesecloth but do not press through, because it would otherwise not remain clear, especially if vegetables were cooked in it
