## Ingredients

- 40 decagrams sugar
- 1 liter water
- 1/4 kilo fresh strawberries
- cochineal coloring or alkermes juice
- 1 lemon peel
- 7 decagrams isinglass dissolved and heated
- 2 lemons
- white wine
- optional: 1/8 kilo white and red currants instead of strawberries

## Directions

1. Clarify 40 decagrams sugar in 1 liter water (No. 1542).
2. Crush 1/4 kilo fresh strawberries and pour one-third of the hot, clarified sugar over them, cover well and let stand overnight.
3. The next day, dissolve cochineal coloring or a few drops of alkermes juice in the remaining clarified sugar while hot, add lemon peel, cover and pour hot over the strawberries.
4. Stir and strain through cloth, then add hot to 7 decagrams of dissolved, boiling isinglass (Orange Jelly, No. 1660).
5. Strain the jelly mass through fine linen, add juice from 2 lemons and enough white wine to make the total mass 1 liter.
6. Proceed as with Orange Jelly.
7. Alternatively, use 1/8 kilo strawberries mixed with 1/8 kilo white and red currants instead of 1/4 kilo strawberries, omitting cochineal coloring and lemon juice.
8. Note: juice and sugar together should only total 1/2 liter and isinglass should be 1/2 liter, totaling 1 liter together.
