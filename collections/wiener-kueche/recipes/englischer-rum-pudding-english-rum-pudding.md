## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo suet, skinned
- 25 deca flour
- 1/4 kilo large raisins
- 1/4 kilo small raisins
- 1/4 kilo currants
- 26 deca finely chopped citron
- 15 deca sugar
- 6 whole eggs
- 6 tablespoons rum or cognac or desired liqueur

## Directions

1. Finely chop 1/2 kilo skinned suet and 25 deca flour together
2. Beat this mixture for 1/3 hour until light
3. Add 1/4 kilo large raisins, 1/4 kilo small raisins, 1/4 kilo currants, 26 deca very finely chopped citron, 15 deca sugar, 6 whole eggs, and 6 tablespoons rum or cognac or desired liqueur
4. Mix everything well together
5. Set the dough in a cool place to become slightly firmer
6. Bind into a buttered napkin
7. Cook for 1 hour
8. Serve with Chaudeau
9. Can also be cut cold and keeps for several weeks
