## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 liter flour, 6 eggs, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon sugar, and some salt on a board
2. Make a kind of strudel dough from these, form small balls from it, which are rolled out knife-back thick
3. Make 5 cuts in these pieces at little-finger-width distance with a knife or needle
4. Grasp the dough by lightly pressing it together with the fingertips, put it in the mold already heated in very hot fat, cover it with the lid, and bake the snowballs in very hot fat under frequent shaking
5. When they are baked light brown, let them roll out of the mold, sprinkle them immediately with sugar, put the next already prepared snowball immediately back in the same mold, and continue until all the dough is used
