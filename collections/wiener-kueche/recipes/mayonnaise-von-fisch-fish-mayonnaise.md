## Ingredients

- cooked fish scraps of pike or shad, or a small such fish about 1/2 kg, best is the tail piece of a larger shad
- 20 dkg lettuce salad
- 2 anchovies
- some capers
- aspic if desired
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, wash it, and place in boiling water in a pan with some salt and 1/2 liter vinegar.
2. Remove the pan from heat and let the fish poach for 8-10 minutes.
3. Remove the fish, cool it, remove the bones, and press the fish between your hands (treat fish scraps the same way).
4. Form into a cone, place in the center of a glass bowl, coat with thick mayonnaise.
5. Decorate the cone with capers and rolled anchovies.
6. Surround with prepared lettuce salad, hard egg slices, and optionally red or white aspic.
7. Alternatively, the fish can be mixed with mayonnaise or mixed with tartar sauce, filled into shells, coated with mayonnaise, and decorated with capers.
