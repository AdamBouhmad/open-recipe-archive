## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1/4 pound good anchovies, cleaned and washed

## Directions

1. Pound together 1 pound butter with 1/4 pound cleaned and washed good anchovies
2. Press the pounded butter through a fine hair sieve
3. Store for later use in sauces and forcemeats
