## Ingredients

- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Beef broth
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. To remove garlic's unpleasant taste, first simmer it in vinegar.
2. Roast the finely chopped garlic in butter until golden yellow.
3. Mix in breadcrumbs that have been roasted in butter.
4. Add as much beef broth and some vinegar as needed.
5. Let it simmer slowly, seasoned to taste.
6. Before serving, add a small piece of butter.
