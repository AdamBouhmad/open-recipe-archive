## Ingredients

- 48 Deka beaten butter
- 16 Deka caster sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 60 Deka flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter with caster sugar and egg yolks for 1/4 hour
2. Add flour and mix
3. Shape the dough into a loaf on a flour-dusted board and cover it
4. Let it rest in a cool place for 1/2 hour
5. Roll out the dough to finger thickness
6. Cut out 3 round discs and place them in torte forms brushed with butter and lined with flour
7. Bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour
8. Once removed and completely cooled, stack them
9. Brush between layers with jam and cover the whole with a glaze
