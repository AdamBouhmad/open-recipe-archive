## Ingredients

- 1 strong tablespoon green parsley
- 8 pieces washed mushrooms
- Butter
- Some white soup
- Light butter sauce
- Some white wine
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut 1 strong tablespoon green parsley and 8 pieces washed mushrooms finely and braise in butter
2. Pour with some white soup, simmer and set aside
3. Add the braised mushrooms to a light butter sauce with some white wine and the juice of one lemon
4. Let it boil once more and pass through a sieve
5. This mushroom sauce must be of light color and pleasant acidity
