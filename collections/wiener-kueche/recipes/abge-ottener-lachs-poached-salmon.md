## Ingredients

- Salmon piece, three or four pounds, preferably from the middle
- Water
- Half a measure of white wine
- Wine vinegar
- Onions
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- Root vegetables

## Directions

1. Place the salmon piece in a casserole with water, half a measure of white wine and wine vinegar so it is covered
2. Add onions, cloves, pepper, bay leaves, a little fine herbs, salt and root vegetables
3. Cook the piece for three-quarters of an hour
4. Arrange on a platter
5. Strain the sauce through a fine sieve and serve with it
