## Ingredients

- three good handfuls each of rue and sage
- half a seidel of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Chop the rue and sage small
2. Place them with the sweet cream in a stone pot
3. Let it stand for twenty-four hours
4. Churn the butter
5. Strain the butter through a loose linen cloth, leaving the herbs behind
6. Have the patient eat herb bread with this butter as often as hunger strikes
7. Do not allow the patient to eat other foods until the butter is consumed
8. The patient may drink usual beverages as normal
