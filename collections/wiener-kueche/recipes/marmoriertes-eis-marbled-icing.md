## Ingredients

- white icing without lemon juice
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare white icing without lemon juice
2. Once it is sufficiently beaten, add 1/4 deka of grated chocolate in the last moment, which, however, should be mixed only very lightly with the icing
