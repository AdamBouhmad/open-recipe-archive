## Ingredients

- 1 kg Fisch (fish)
- 10 l Vögerlsalat (corn salad)
- 1 rote Rübe (beet)
- 1 Gurke (cucumber)
- 1 Zitrone (lemon)
- 2 l Kapern (capers)
- 15 dkg Butter (butter)
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, gut it, wash it and place in milk water.
2. Bind it at the head and tail tightly and loosely in the middle, otherwise it will remain raw in the middle.
3. Bring much water with 10 spoons of salt and ¼ l vinegar to a boil in a fish pan and poach the fish for 10-15 minutes depending on size.
4. Remove from heat and let it cool first in the broth, then remove and let it cool completely.
5. Unbind the fish, place it on the platter and spray with 15 dkg well-whipped butter.
6. Arrange the corn salad seasoned with salt, vinegar and oil around it.
7. Rim the platter alternately with cooked beets sliced into leaves, cucumber and halved lemon slices and decorate the whole with capers or caviar.
