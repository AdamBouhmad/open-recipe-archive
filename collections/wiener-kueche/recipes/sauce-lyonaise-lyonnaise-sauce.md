## Ingredients

- Fifteen onions, sliced
- Half a pound of butter
- Flour
- Broth (Schü)
- A little sugar
- Some wine
- Nutmeg
- Wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Sauté the sliced onions in butter until they are brown and completely soft.
2. Add flour.
3. Fill the casserole with broth.
4. Add a little sugar, some wine, nutmeg, and wine vinegar.
5. Stir everything together well.
6. Cook until thick.
7. Finally, strain the sauce through a fine cloth.
