## Ingredients

- several pieces of mushrooms
- water
- fresh butter
- salt
- white pepper
- coulis
- bouillon
- small bread rolls
- thick sweet cream
- eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and wash several pieces of mushrooms
2. Cook them in water for a few minutes
3. Lay them in cloth to dry
4. Pass them in fresh butter with salt, white pepper
5. Add some coulis and bouillon
6. When they are cooked, rub the rind from small bread rolls
7. Hollow them out inside
8. Pour some thick sweet cream into a ragout
9. Fill the rolls with it
10. Serve very hot. If you want the ragout with mushrooms to be white, bind them with eggs, add some nutmeg, and stir in sweet cream
11. Then you can use them as entremet or ragouts as needed. If you have no small mushrooms, take large ones and cut them into fine pieces, which must be washed in water a couple of times
