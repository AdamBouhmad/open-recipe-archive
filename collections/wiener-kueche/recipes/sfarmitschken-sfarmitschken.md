## Ingredients

- 14 deca finely pounded sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 10 deca flour
- wax
- 1/2 tablespoon batter
- 1/4 liter whipped cream
- 16 deca sugar
- 1 coffee cup fresh strawberries or 12 deca vanilla sugar, or any marmalade, or 2 bars grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Stir 14 deca finely pounded sugar and 3 whole eggs for 1 hour and after this time mix well with 10 deca flour.
2. On a baking sheet coated with wax and completely cooled, place 1/2 tablespoon of the above batter at 2 hand-widths distance apart, which you stretch with the spoon back-of-knife thickness lengthwise. Place the sheet in the oven and let it bake at moderate heat until the batter releases from the sheet; then remove the baking sheet from the oven, place it on the warm stove where you hold one end of the baked batter strip at the pointed end of the Sfarmitsche form, turn the batter obliquely over the form so that it forms a cone and then place it on a strainer.
3. Repeat this procedure with a second Sfarmitsche form, which is then also placed on the sheet, whereupon you remove the first twisted Sfarmitsche from the form, place it on a sieve and let it cool. Continue in this way until the batter is used up.
4. Then beat 1/4 liter whipped cream to thick foam, mix lightly with 16 deca sugar and 1 coffee cup fresh strawberries or 12 deca vanilla sugar, or any marmalade, or also with 2 bars grated chocolate and fill the cold Sfarmitschen with this. The Sfarmitschen should be served at table as soon as they are filled as they become soft after 10 minutes.
