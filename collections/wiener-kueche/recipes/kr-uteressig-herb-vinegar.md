## Ingredients

- good vinegar
- basil
- fennel
- tarragon
- melissa
- thyme
- chervil and dill
- celery
- parsley root
- pimpinella
- lavender blossoms
- rosemary blossoms
- orange and lemon peels
- foreign spices as desired

## Directions

1. Take basil, fennel, tarragon, melissa, thyme, chervil and dill, celery, parsley root, pimpinella and other desired herbs
2. Add some lavender blossoms, rosemary blossoms, some orange and lemon peels, and foreign spices as desired
3. Proceed as with French vinegar
