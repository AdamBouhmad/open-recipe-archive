## Ingredients

- dried sour cherries
- wine
- butter
- eggs
- egg white
- breadcrumbs or crushed biscuits
- sifted sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel, finely cut

## Directions

1. Wash dried sour cherries clean and cook them in wine
2. Press them through a sieve and let them cool
3. Beat butter fluffy
4. Stir in eggs and egg white beaten to foam
5. Take breadcrumbs or finely crushed biscuits, sifted sugar, and cinnamon to taste
6. Add the cooled cherries and finely cut lemon peel
7. Bake it slowly
