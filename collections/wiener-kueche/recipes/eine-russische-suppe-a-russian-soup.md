## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- cabbage, 6 to 7 pieces each cleaned
- 4 pounds beef from the breast
- 1 old hen
- 5 measures water
- cabbage heads, finely chopped
- butter
- flour
- 2 pounds raw lean ham, diced
- salt
- pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Split white cabbage and cabbage, 6 to 7 clean pieces of each
2. Set 4 pounds beef from the breast and an old hen in 5 measures of water over the fire
3. Let it simmer with cabbage for a couple of hours
4. Braise some finely chopped cabbage heads in a piece of butter with a couple of spoonfuls of fine flour
5. Fill the soup with broth from the meat
6. Add the meat, finely chopped, along with the disjointed hen and the cooked cabbage
7. Cut 2 pounds of raw lean ham into small pieces and add to it
8. Let everything cook briefly together
9. Salt and pepper it
10. Before serving, a few spoonfuls of sour cream can be added
11. Serve with meat and ham at table
