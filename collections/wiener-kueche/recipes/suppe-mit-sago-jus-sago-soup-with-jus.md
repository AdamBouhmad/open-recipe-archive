## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sago
- Fresh water
- Good jus (Schü)
- Parsnip
- Yellow from lettuce head
- Chervil
- Sorrel, finely chopped
- Toasted bread roll slices

## Directions

1. Wash sago twice with fresh water. Blanch for 4 hours. Lay on hair sieve and cool with fresh water. In a casserole, fill with sago and good jus, adding parsnip, yellow from lettuce head, chervil, and finely chopped sorrel. After cooking for half an hour, serve with toasted bread roll slices.
