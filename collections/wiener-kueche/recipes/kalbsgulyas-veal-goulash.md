## Ingredients

- 60-70 dkg veal loin, rib, or shoulder
- 6 dkg hot fat or 6 dkg diced bacon
- 1 finely chopped onion
- 3 pinches paprika
- water
- ½ liter cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and cut into cubes, salt lightly.
2. In a pot, heat 6 dkg fat or fry diced bacon, sauté finely chopped onion.
3. Add 3 pinches of paprika, then the meat.
4. Pour in a little water, cover, and simmer for 1 hour with occasional stirring and adding water as needed.
5. Strain the sauce, pour cream over it, and simmer for 10 more minutes.
6. Serve with noodles, dumplings, or rice.
