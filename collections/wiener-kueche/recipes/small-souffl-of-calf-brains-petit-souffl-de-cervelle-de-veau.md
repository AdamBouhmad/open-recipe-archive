## Ingredients

- Two calf brains, freed of skin and cooked in salted water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 1/8 liter milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 12 tablespoons finely diced ham
- Salt and spices as needed
- 5 egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks
- Puff pastry for lining the mold

## Directions

1. Roast the two prepared brains in 1.5 tablespoons butter for about 3/4 hour
2. Place 6 tablespoons butter, 6 tablespoons flour, and 1/8 liter milk in a saucepan and cook over steady heat, stirring constantly until the mixture loosens from the spoon
3. Pour into a deep bowl and when completely cooled, add 5 egg yolks
4. Mix together with the roasted brains, 12 tablespoons finely diced ham, necessary salt, and spices if desired
5. Lightly fold in the beaten snow of 5 egg whites
6. Line a large, flour-dusted mold (or small shell molds) with puff pastry drawn with a knife back
7. Fill with the mixture leaving a finger's breadth of empty space
8. Brush the edge of the dough with beaten eggs, fold lightly over and bake slowly
9. Serve instead of roasted meat or as an entrée
