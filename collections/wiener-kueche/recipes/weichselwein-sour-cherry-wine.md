## Ingredients

- 10 Maß (or one quarter Eimer) wine
- one Büttl of sour cherries
- 2 Büttl of small black cherries with their pits
- one and a half or 2 pounds sugar
- 1 loth cinnamon
- 1 loth cloves
- 1 loth nutmeg blossom
- one stone

## Directions

1. Crush the sour cherries and black cherries in a mortar
2. With a second mortar, add one and a half or 2 pounds sugar
3. Place all spices in a sachet and fasten a stone at the end of it so the spices sink to the bottom
4. Make the crushed cherries and cherries into a juice and press them well
5. Pour the juice into the small barrel, add the wine on top
6. Stir it and let it stand for eight days
7. When taking from it, you can always pour fresh wine in for a while
