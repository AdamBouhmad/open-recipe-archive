## Ingredients

- Mirabelle plums
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Over-stewed in a cassole covered
2. After approximately 10 minutes quickly passed through a sieve
3. Formed again for the same length of time and filled hot into jars like apricot jam
4. The mirabelle plums must not be cooked too long, as they otherwise lose their green color
