## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 kilo dried plums, half-cooked and finely cut
- 16 tablespoons blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- 8 tablespoons breadcrumbs, decrumbed, soaked in water, and well squeezed out
- some finely cut lemon zest

## Directions

1. Beat 8 tablespoons butter until fluffy
2. Gradually stir in 4 egg yolks
3. Mix in 1/4 kilo of dried, half-cooked, finely cut plums, 16 tablespoons of blanched, finely pounded almonds, 8 tablespoons sugar, 8 tablespoons of decrumbed breadcrumbs soaked in water and well squeezed out, and some finely cut lemon zest
4. Mix all ingredients well together
