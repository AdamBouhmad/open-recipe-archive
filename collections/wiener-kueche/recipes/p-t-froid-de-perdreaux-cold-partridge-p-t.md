## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Partridge farce
- Butter dough
- Bacon slices
- Truffles

## Directions

1. Prepare and treat the pâté the same way as described in the 'Poularde Pâté' recipe (No. 1060), with the exception that partridge farce is used instead of veal farce, and partridges are used instead of the poularde.
