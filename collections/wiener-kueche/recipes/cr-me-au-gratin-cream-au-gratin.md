## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- half a measure sweet cream
- sugar
- orange blossom
- a couple tablespoons fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks into a casserole
2. Stir in gradually half a measure sweet cream
3. Add sugar and orange blossom
4. Carefully stir in a couple tablespoons fine flour and beat well so it does not become lumpy
5. Place a dish with some cream on hot ashes
6. When it begins to cook, pour the entire mass in
7. Stir for some time
8. Let set over gentle charcoal fire so the cream gets a nice crust on the bottom of the plate
9. Finally burn it with a shovel and serve
