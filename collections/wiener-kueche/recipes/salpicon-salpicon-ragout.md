## Ingredients

- ox palate
- rooster combs
- beef sweetbreads
- crayfish tails
- truffles
- mushrooms
- cucumbers or small capers
- butter
- one tablespoon flour
- coulis or white sauce
- smoked tongue
- egg yolk
- lemon juice
- fine brown breadcrumbs
- crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Cook the ox palate, rooster combs and beef sweetbreads until tender in a good braise
2. Let cool
3. Cut into small dice, as well as the crayfish tails, truffles, mushrooms, and cucumbers or small capers
4. Sauté in butter
5. Dust with one tablespoon of flour
6. Add coulis or white sauce and let simmer briefly
7. Add some cooked smoked tongue
8. Season well
9. Bind with egg yolk and some lemon juice
10. Can also use leftover poultry
11. Fill into cream cups
12. Sprinkle fine brown breadcrumbs on top
13. Place a little crayfish butter on top
14. Allow to brown in a lukewarm oven
15. This ragout can be used for various dishes
