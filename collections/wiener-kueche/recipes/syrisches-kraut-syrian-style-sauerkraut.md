## Ingredients

- sauerkraut
- boiling salt water
- diced bacon
- finely cut onion
- caraway seeds
- flour
- beef broth
- vinegar
- milk cream

## Directions

1. Cut the sauerkraut as for sauerkraut salad, place in a deep bowl, pour boiling salt water over it and let it stand for a while
2. In a casserole, place diced bacon and finely cut onion, add the expressed sauerkraut, salt it, add a little caraway seed, stew it for some time
3. Dust it with the necessary flour, pour some beef broth, a little vinegar and milk cream to it, season it, let it stew for some time more and serve on a platter
