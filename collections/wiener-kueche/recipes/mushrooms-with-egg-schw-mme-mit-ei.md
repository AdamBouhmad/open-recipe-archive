## Ingredients

- 6-8 medium porcini mushrooms or champignons
- ⅛ liter cream
- 6-8 dkg hot butter
- finely chopped onion
- finely chopped parsley
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms, slice thinly or chop
2. In a saucepan, let finely chopped onion and parsley run in 6-8 dkg hot butter
3. Add the mushrooms, let them stew covered for 30-40 minutes, adding cream as needed
4. Beat 1 egg and add it, let it steam through
5. Mushrooms can also be served with fried eggs
6. Mushrooms should not be reheated
