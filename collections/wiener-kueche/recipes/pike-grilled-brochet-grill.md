## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter
- green mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Clean, open, and wash a pike, cut it lengthwise along the back, and remove the back and rib bones
2. Cut into 2-finger-wide and 2-finger-long pieces, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and let sit for some time
3. Dip in flour and breadcrumbs, dry quickly, dip in clarified butter, and sprinkle well with breadcrumbs
4. Grill on both sides over medium-strong fire
5. Serve green mustard sauce with it
6. Alternative: instead of on the grill, fish pieces may also be quickly fried in finger-high, very hot fat in a flat casserole
