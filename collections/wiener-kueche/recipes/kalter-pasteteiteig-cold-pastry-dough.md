## Ingredients

- 4 pounds fine flour
- 6 loth butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 or 2 seidels boiling water

## Directions

1. Under 4 pounds of fine flour, work in 6 loth butter
2. Make a deep hole in the middle
3. Sift the flour
4. Beat 3 eggs into the hole
5. Mix these with the remaining ingredients
6. Pour in 1 or 2 seidels of boiling water as needed
7. Work everything as quickly as possible into a firm dough
8. Form the dough into a round ball
9. Wrap it in a napkin, as it is too hot to work with at this stage
