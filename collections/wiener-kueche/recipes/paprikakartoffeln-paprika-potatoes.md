## Ingredients

- ¼ kg potatoes
- 5 dkg fat
- 1 finely minced onion
- 3 knife points paprika
- cold water
- salt
- ¼ l cream

## Directions

1. Heat 5 dkg fat in a casserole and sauté 1 finely minced onion until golden
2. Add 3 knife points of paprika
3. Quench with a ladleful of cold water
4. Add raw peeled and quartered potatoes and salt
5. Cover and cook for 30-35 minutes, adding water as needed
