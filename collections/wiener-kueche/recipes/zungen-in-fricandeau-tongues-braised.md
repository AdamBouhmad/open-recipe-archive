## Ingredients

- tongues
- parsley
- chives
- yellow turnips
- salt
- bacon
- onion
- ham
- brown broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the tongues in a pot with a bundle of parsley, chives, some yellow turnips, and salt, let cook until the skin can be removed.
2. Then lard them with finely cut bacon in three or four rows, one after another.
3. Arrange bacon, onion, yellow turnips, and ham all cut in thin slices in a casserole, place the tongues in it.
4. Fill with brown broth, cover the casserole, and let the tongues braise on gentle heat until tender enough.
5. Then remove them, strain the sauce, add two spoonfuls of good vinegar, and let the sauce cook until it becomes glace.
6. Then put the tongues back in, turn them on all sides until done. You can also lay a wreath of small braised onions around the edge of the dish.
