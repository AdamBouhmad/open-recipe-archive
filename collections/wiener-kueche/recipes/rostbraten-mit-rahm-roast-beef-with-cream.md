## Ingredients

- 2-3 pieces of 25-30 dkg
- 1/2 l cream
- 6 dkg hot fat
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- paprika
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- 2 chopped anchovies (optional)

## Directions

1. Trim the meat of bones, fat and skin (these can also remain), wipe off, salt, pepper.
2. In a roasting pan, in 6 dkg hot fat, brown 1 finely chopped onion, add some paprika, lay in the meat.
3. Pour in water or broth and braise 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
4. Strain the sauce, add the cream and simmer a few minutes. Optional: 2 chopped anchovies can be braised with it.
