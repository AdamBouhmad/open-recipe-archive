## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- brine/marinade
- bacon
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the wild ducks well and place them in brine for several days
2. Salt them and lard the breast neatly in four or six rows with finely cut bacon
3. Roast them on a spit, basting frequently with cream and brine
4. Serve with the strained sauce poured over them
