## Ingredients

- 6 lemons
- white wine
- same quantity of lemon peel as orange peel in the orange jelly
- refined sugar
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as Orange Jelly (No. 1660), using juice from 6 lemons and only white wine.
2. Cook the same amount of lemon peel in the sugar as would be used for orange jelly.
