## Ingredients

- 16 tablespoons butter
- 16 tablespoons sugar
- 16 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 liters hot milk
- 9 egg yolks
- 9 egg whites
- 8 tablespoons finely ground almonds or hazelnuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Place butter, sugar, and flour in a saucepan and finely mash with a spoon
2. Pour in 1.5 liters of hot milk and stir quickly over the fire until the dough separates from the pan
3. Transfer to a deep bowl and let cool completely
4. Stir in 9 egg yolks one after another
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 9 egg whites, and if desired, 8 tablespoons of finely ground almonds or hazelnuts
6. Fill a buttered form dusted with flour with this mixture
7. Cover and cook 3/4 to 1 hour in a water bath or bake slowly in the oven at low heat
8. If desired, add 5 tablespoons of strong black coffee with the milk
