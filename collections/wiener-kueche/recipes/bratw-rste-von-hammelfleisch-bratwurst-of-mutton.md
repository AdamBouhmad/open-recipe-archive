## Ingredients

- mutton leg with fine meat
- four pounds lean mutton
- one pound fresh bacon
- ground pepper
- half a Seidel of white wine
- lemon zest
- sausage herbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Scrape the fine meat without nerves and fat from a mutton leg
2. Chop it very finely with a knife
3. Take under four pounds lean mutton one pound fresh bacon and chop it into small cubes
4. Add some ground pepper, half a Seidel of white wine, lemon zest, and fine sausage herbs
5. Work the entire mass together
6. Stuff firmly into narrow pork intestines
7. If serving with vegetables, brush with butter and fry gently on a roasting rack
