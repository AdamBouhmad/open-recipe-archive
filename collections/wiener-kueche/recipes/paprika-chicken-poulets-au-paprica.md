## Ingredients

- 8 decagrams hot fat or lard
- 2 fallen and dressed chickens
- onion, finely diced
- flour

## Directions

1. Place 8 decagrams hot fat or lard in a casserole
2. Add finely diced onion as desired and place 2 fallen and dressed chickens in it
3. Cover and braise
4. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, dust the chickens with some flour
5. Pour over ⅛ liter of the cooking liquid
