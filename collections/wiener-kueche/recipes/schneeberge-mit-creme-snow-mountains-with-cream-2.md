## Ingredients

- 15 egg whites
- half milk and half water
- 2 loth finely ground bitter almonds
- 1 seidel cream
- some orange blossom water
- one quarter pound sugar
- egg yolks
- sliced almonds
- colored sugar

## Directions

1. Set a casserole with half milk and half water on the fire
2. Beat the whites of 15 eggs into stiff snow
3. Once it boils, spoon in one serving spoon full of snow at a time, let it boil once, lay it on a sieve or napkin to cool, and continue until all the snow is used
4. Stir the egg yolks with 2 loth finely ground bitter almonds, 1 seidel cream, some orange blossom water, and one quarter pound sugar
5. Layer the cooked snow on a serving dish, arrange cream over it, then snow again, then cream again, until the snow is piled up like a mountain
6. Cut some almonds lengthwise and stick them in the snow, arrange the cream completely over it, and sprinkle with colored sugar
7. Serve the snow mountains cold
