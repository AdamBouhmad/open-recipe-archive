## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage
- salt
- caraway seeds
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the cabbage from bad leaves and stems, wash it, cut it into fine noodle-like pieces
2. Put it into boiling salted water and cook for 10-15 minutes
3. Drain it and place in a bowl
4. While still warm, mix with some salt, caraway seeds, vinegar and oil to taste
5. Cool completely and serve on a round dish
6. The salad must stand for at least 1 hour, as otherwise the cabbage will be tough
