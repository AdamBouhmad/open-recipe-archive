## Ingredients

- two whole juicy lemons
- one Seidel of barley water
- four Loth of mulberry juice prepared with sugar
- sixteen Loth of Rhine wine
- four Loth of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Take two whole juicy lemons and cut them into pieces
2. Separate and discard the white spongy pith that sits between the flesh and the outermost yellow peel
3. Discard the pith and seeds
4. Crush the flesh
5. Pour one Seidel of barley water over it
6. Add the cut yellow peels
7. Add four Loth of mulberry juice prepared with sugar
8. Add sixteen Loth of Rhine wine
9. Add four Loth of toasted bread
10. Store and keep for drinking
