## Ingredients

- 28 Deka flour
- Butter for dough
- Almond filling as specified for almond strudel
- Butter for forms
- Flour for forms
- Egg for brushing
- Finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a fine puff dough from 28 Deka flour (No. 1045) and let rest 3/4 to 1 hour
2. Roll out knife-back thick on a flour-dusted board and cut into strips approximately four fingers wide
3. Line each butter-greased and flour-dusted almond mold with a piece of dough
4. Fill with 1 tablespoon almond filling as specified for almond strudel
5. Fold the 4 corners lightly over the filling in the middle
6. Fasten one corner to another with beaten egg and brush the remaining dough with it, but leave a knife-back-width border dry along the edges
7. Place the almond molds on a baking sheet in the hot oven for 1/2 to 3/4 hours
8. Remove the sheet from the oven, lift the almonds from the forms with a fork and arrange elevated in a bowl
9. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and serve
