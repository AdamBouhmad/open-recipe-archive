## Ingredients

- Veal
- 1/2 liter washed rice
- Broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal as described for Veal Goulash
2. When the meat is half-tender from braising, add 1/2 liter of washed rice
3. Pour enough broth over it so that the broth covers the rice
4. Allow it to finish braising
