## Ingredients

- 2 pounds butter
- 2 pounds fine flour
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 30 eggs
- half a lot cinnamon and mace
- zest of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar for half an hour
2. Melt the butter and, when it is no longer hot, add it to the eggs and sugar
3. Stir until the mixture is quite foamy
4. Stir in the spices
5. Gradually add the flour
6. Slowly fold in the egg white (snow) from the aforementioned eggs
