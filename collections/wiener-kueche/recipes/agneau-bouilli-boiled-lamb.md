## Ingredients

- yellow turnips
- carrot piece
- parsley piece
- celery piece
- salt water
- vinegar
- thyme sprigs
- spices (cloves, bay, etc.)
- lamb meat, cut into pieces
- green parsley, finely cut
- chervil
- tarragon
- butter
- sour cream
- broth

## Directions

1. In a casserole, add yellow turnips, a piece of carrot, a piece of parsley, a small piece of celery, all very finely cut like noodles, then pour in 1 liter of boiling salt water and 1/4 liter vinegar.
2. Let it cook with 5 sprigs of thyme and 5 grains each of the spices (which are tied together in an organza cloth).
3. When the root vegetables are almost tender, add the lamb meat cut into desired pieces and braise covered until tender.
4. Remove the meat with a slotted spoon, arrange it piled on a platter, sprinkle with finely cut green parsley, chervil and tarragon and garnish with the strained root vegetables.
5. Blend several ounces of butter with some sour cream, pour in 1-1/2 liters of hot broth (from the root vegetables) and serve in a sauceboat with the meat.
