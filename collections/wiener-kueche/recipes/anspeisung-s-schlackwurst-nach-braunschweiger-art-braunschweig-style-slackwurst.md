## Ingredients

- Two parts lean meat
- One part fat
- Ham
- Loin bacon
- Good meat without sinews
- Salt
- Black pepper, several handfuls

## Directions

1. Cut all meat into small pieces
2. Remove every sinew, even the smallest, with a knife
3. Chop very finely
4. Sprinkle several handfuls of salt and about one handful of ordinary black pepper over it
5. Chop again diligently and continuously until no peppercorn remains whole
6. Continue chopping until all pepper is completely minced
