## Ingredients

- one Loth each of parsley root, burdock root, and asparagus root
- one Seidel of water
- one handful each of chicory leaves and chervil herb
- two Quentchen of polychrest salt
- two Loth of orange syrup

## Directions

1. Take the root of parsley, burdock, and asparagus, one Loth each
2. Scrape and cut them into pieces
3. Let them cook in one Seidel of water for a good hour
4. Finally add chicory leaves and chervil herb, one handful each
5. Let it boil several more times
6. Strain it through a sieve
7. Dissolve two Quentchen of polychrest salt in it
8. Add two Loth of orange syrup
9. Drink half a beer glass full every two hours, or one whole glass every two hours
