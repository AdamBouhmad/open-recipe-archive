## Ingredients

- 12 bunches of Bertram
- 1 bunch of Pimpinella
- 1 bunch of Verbena
- 1 bunch of Lemon balm
- 1 small bunch of Chamomile
- 1 bunch of Parsley
- 1 small bunch of Elderflowers
- 5 shallots
- 5 Carambola
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 Nutmeg flowers
- 5 juniper berries, crushed
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 teaspoon sweet mustard seeds
- 1 teaspoon bitter mustard seeds
- 3 cloves
- 5 grains of Allspice
- 5 peppercorns
- 1 knife-point of Alum
- 5 liters of good wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in a bottle
2. Pour 5 liters of good wine vinegar over everything
3. Cork and bind the bottle, which should not be completely full
4. Set it in the sun for several weeks until the herbs settle to the bottom
5. Strain the vinegar through 2 linen cloths so it becomes completely clear
6. Fill it into bottles, cork and seal them well, and store in a cool place
