## Ingredients

- 8 Deka flour
- 6 Deka hot butter
- 1/4 liter milk
- 6 Deka cold butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 Deka sugar
- 8 egg whites
- butter for coating the mold
- flour for dusting the mold
- baked biscuit
- liqueur or dissolved marmalade

## Directions

1. Roast 8 Deka flour in 6 Deka hot butter for a short time.
2. Pour in 1/4 liter milk and stir over heat until the dough releases from the pan.
3. Pour it into a deep bowl and stir cold with 6 Deka cold butter.
4. Beat 8 egg yolks and 12 Deka sugar for 1/2 hour, then gradually add to the cooled dough mass.
5. Lightly fold in the snow of 8 egg whites.
6. Fill half of this mass into a mold brushed with butter and dusted with flour.
7. Arrange baked biscuits that have been soaked in liqueur or dissolved marmalade on top.
8. Distribute the second half of the mass evenly over it and bake covered for 3/4 hour in steam or in the oven at medium heat.
9. Turn the cake out into a bowl, pour dissolved marmalade over if desired, and serve immediately.
