## Ingredients

- Two handfuls each of spoonwort, watercress, figwort, and celery
- One quench bitter orange peel
- One loth wild turnip seed
- Two sliced calf hearts
- Tails and claws of one dozen crayfish
- Water

## Directions

1. Cook all ingredients together in a sufficient quantity of water
2. Use in two portions
3. This broth disperses phlegm, drives out urine, and purifies the blood
