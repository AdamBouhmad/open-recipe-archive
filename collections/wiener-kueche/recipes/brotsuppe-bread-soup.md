## Ingredients

- Grated bread rind
- Water
- Wine
- Sugar
- Caraway
- Four loths rusks
- One and a half pounds water
- Two to three spoonfuls wine
- Half loth sugar
- One egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cook the grated bread rind in two parts water and one part wine
2. Add some sugar and caraway
3. Alternatively, cook four loths rusks in one and a half pounds water until sufficiently softened
4. Add two to three spoonfuls wine and half loth sugar beaten with an egg yolk
