## Ingredients

- scheiden (shad)
- butter
- salt water

## Directions

1. If you have a small shad, treat it like pike or perch. To poach it, cut it into thumb-thick pieces of arbitrary size. Fold these pieces, lay them on the bottom of a fish pan and with this into the pot; or, in the absence of that, put the fish pieces in a pan and pour boiling salt water over them, place a buttered piece of paper and a lid on top and let them simmer on the stove for approximately 1 hour, during which time the broth must always remain simmering hot, but must not boil. Arrange the fish pieces on a platter and serve with any desired sauce, such as: Dutch-style whipped mustard sauce, sugar sauce with lemon juice.
