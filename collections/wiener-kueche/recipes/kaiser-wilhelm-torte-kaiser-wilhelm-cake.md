## Ingredients

- 11 dkg butter
- 15 dkg sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 11 dkg softened chocolate
- 6 stiff egg whites
- 9 dkg flour

## Directions

1. Beat butter and gradually add sugar, egg yolks, and softened chocolate
2. Fold in stiff egg whites together with flour
3. Fill mixture into form
