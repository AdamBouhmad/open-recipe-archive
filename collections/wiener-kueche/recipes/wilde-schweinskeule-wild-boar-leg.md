## Ingredients

- Wild boar leg
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Dried herbs, finely crushed
- Onions, sliced
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Wine
- Water
- Yellow turnips
- Cloves
- Breadcrumbs, finely grated
- Glaze

## Directions

1. Singe the wild boar leg well, wash it, and brush it clean
2. Dry it thoroughly, remove the bones, and lard it with bacon
3. Rub it with salt, pepper, spices, and finely crushed dried herbs
4. Bind it together
5. Marinate it for several days with sliced onions, bay leaves, thyme, salt, parsley, and pepper
6. Place it in a stone vessel
7. Fill completely with two measures of wine and water
8. Add yellow turnips, cloves, whole onions, and the above marinade
9. Cover carefully and cook for six to seven hours until tender
10. Skim off the fat and let the leg rest in the braise for some time
11. Brush with glaze and sprinkle with finely grated breadcrumbs
12. Place briefly in the oven and serve in a beautiful presentation
