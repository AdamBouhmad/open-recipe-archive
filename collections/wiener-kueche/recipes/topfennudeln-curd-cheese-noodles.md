## Ingredients

- curd cheese, pressed
- 1 egg
- salt
- 4 dkg butter
- 8 dkg flour
- 12 dkg hot butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mix the pressed curd cheese with 1 egg, salt, 4 dkg butter, and 8 dkg flour on a board to form a dough
2. Roll into a log and cut into equal-sized pieces
3. Form thick noodles
4. Place in a casserole with boiling salted water
5. Cook very slowly for 3-4 minutes
6. Toast breadcrumbs in 12 dkg hot butter until light brown
7. Drain the noodles and toss in the browned breadcrumbs
8. Serve on a round platter
