## Ingredients

- Fat mutton leg
- Coarse bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Dried herbs, finely ground
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Potatoes, peeled
- Bacon strips
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Beat a fat mutton leg properly
2. Loosen the end bones and trim the longer parts
3. Cut coarse bacon
4. Turn it repeatedly in salt, pepper, spices, and finely ground dried herbs, and pierce it with this mixture
5. Place it in a long casserole with some yellow carrots and whole onions, cloves, a bay leaf, a bunch of parsley, some peeled potatoes, salt, pepper, and bacon strips
6. Set the casserole on charcoal fire with all its contents
7. Pour in a couple of spoonfuls of bouillon
8. Cover it then
9. In this way the leg must cook for nearly two hours and be turned often
10. When serving, dress it on the platter with the vegetables around it
11. Skim off the fat
12. Reduce the sauce briefly and pour it over the mutton leg
