## Ingredients

- 4 dkg Butter
- 1 dkg Flour
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- 3 dkg softened chopped almonds
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a casserole
2. Stir in flour
3. Pour in milk and bring to a boil
4. Season with salt and sugar
5. Add softened chopped almonds and cook well
6. Just before serving, add cleaned grated horseradish
