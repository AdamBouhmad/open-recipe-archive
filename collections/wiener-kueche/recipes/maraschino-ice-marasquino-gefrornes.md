## Ingredients

- cream
- egg yolk
- clarified sugar
- maraschino

## Directions

1. Take cream, egg yolk and clarified sugar in the same measure and treat as the previous recipes
2. Pour the maraschino in last, as much as you believe will make it strong enough
3. Mix well with the stirring spatula
