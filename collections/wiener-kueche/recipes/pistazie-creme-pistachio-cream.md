## Ingredients

- half a measure cream
- good piece of sugar
- quarter pound pistachios
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take half a measure cream, add good piece of sugar, and cook on fire
2. Shell a quarter pound pistachios and pound them fine
3. Beat 6 egg yolks in a pot
4. Slowly stir in pistachios and cream so everything combines well
5. Pass the cream through a hair sieve
6. Fill into bowls, not too full
7. Place bowls in a casserole with simmering water, watching carefully so water does not enter the bowls
8. Place some charcoal fire on the lid
9. When done, serve cold
