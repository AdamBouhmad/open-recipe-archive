## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs
- Bread
- Cooked veal
- Parsley
- Lemon peel
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Stir into 6 decagrams of softened butter 2 whole eggs, 8 decagrams of bread softened in water and well pressed, 10 decagrams of finely cut, roasted or braised meat leftovers, some roasted green parsley and, if desired, a little finely cut lemon peel.
2. When this mixture is well stirred together with the necessary salt and seasoning, fillets of any desired size are formed.
