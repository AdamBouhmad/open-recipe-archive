## Ingredients

- 14 dkg vanilla sugar
- 1-2 egg whites
- 14 dkg pine nuts

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dkg vanilla sugar with 1-2 egg whites
2. Fold in 14 dkg pine nuts
3. Make small mounds with 2 spoons on a buttered baking sheet
4. Bake at low heat
