## Ingredients

- 14 Deca butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 Deca fresh yeast dissolved in 1/2 liter milk
- 42 Deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy, then stir in the sugar, salt, and egg yolks one after another
2. Mix in the yeast dissolved in milk and flour, combining well
3. Beat the dough until it pulls away from the spoon
4. Cover and let rest in a warm place for 1/2 hour
5. Divide the dough into 2 equal parts on a flour-dusted board and roll each out to fit the size of the baking pan
6. Line a buttered and floured baking pan with one sheet of dough
7. Spread with marmalade, leaving a finger-width border, and brush the border with beaten egg
8. Place the second dough sheet on top and press the edges together
9. Cover and let rest in a warm place for 1/4 hour
10. Brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with coarse sugar mixed with finely chopped almonds
11. Bake slowly for 3/4 to 1 hour
