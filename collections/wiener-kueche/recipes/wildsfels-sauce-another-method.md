## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter water
- 1/4 liter red wine
- 6 deca hot butter
- equal amount flour
- 1/2 liter crushed juniper berries
- 1 nutmeg
- 3 pieces crushed juniper berries
- 1 tablespoon finely cut lemon peel
- lemon juice as desired

## Directions

1. Heat water and red wine together
2. Make a roux from hot butter and flour, let it roast a little
3. Pour the cooked wine on it
4. Let it cook for 1/2 hour
5. Add the crushed juniper berries, nutmeg, and crushed juniper berries
6. Season to taste
7. Add finely cut lemon peel
8. Let it cook once more
9. Add lemon juice as desired
10. This sauce can be strained or served with game
