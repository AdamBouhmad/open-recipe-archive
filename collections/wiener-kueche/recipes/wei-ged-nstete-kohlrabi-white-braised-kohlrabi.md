## Ingredients

- Young kohlrabi, peeled and cut into knife-back-thick slices
- Green parsley, finely cut
- Skimmed fat
- Broth
- 2 or 3 cleaned chickens, cut into pieces
- Flour
- Nutmeg
- Milk cream

## Directions

1. Cut round discs from knife-back-thick slices of young peeled kohlrabi
2. Braise them with finely cut green parsley and skimmed fat, adding broth occasionally so they become soft and remain white
3. Braise 2 or 3 cleaned, cut-up chickens in skimmed fat
4. Dust with flour and when they have browned somewhat, add enough broth to achieve the proper consistency
5. Cook with a little nutmeg until the chickens are soft
6. Add the kohlrabi with a spoonful of good milk cream
7. When all is softly braised, arrange the kohlrabi in the center of the platter and the chickens neatly around the edge
