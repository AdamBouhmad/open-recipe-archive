## Ingredients

- 8 ounces butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 ounces flour
- less than 1/8 liter cold milk
- 32 ounces flour
- 1 pinch salt
- cloth for covering
- 32 ounces finely chopped ham
- 3 tablespoons cream
- 2 egg yolks
- finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- flour for dusting
- eggs for coating
- very hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Beat 8 ounces butter until fluffy in a bowl
2. Gradually stir in 4 egg yolks
3. Dissolve 2 ounces flour in less than 1/8 liter of cold milk
4. Add this mixture along with 32 ounces flour and 1 pinch of salt to the butter
5. Stir well and beat the dough until it comes away from the spoon
6. Cover the bowl with a cloth and let rest beside the stove in a warm place until the dough doubles in height
7. Meanwhile prepare the ham filling: mix 32 ounces finely chopped ham with 3 tablespoons cream, 2 egg yolks, finely chopped parsley, salt, and pepper
8. Roll the dough on a floured board to the thickness of two knife-backs
9. Proceed as described in the recipe for Rissoles mit Farce, using the ham mixture as filling
10. Place on a floured board, cover with a cloth, and let rest in a warm place
11. When risen, fry in very hot fat on both sides until golden
