## Ingredients

- 5 sprigs fresh parsley
- 5 sprigs chervil
- 3 mushrooms
- 3 shallots
- 3 rocamboles, all very finely chopped
- 1.5 deciliters butter
- 1/4 liter vinegar
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- salt
- 1 liter cream
- 24 live, washed crayfish

## Directions

1. Finely chop the parsley, chervil, mushrooms, shallots, and rocamboles
2. Roast these ingredients briefly in 1.5 deciliters of butter
3. Add 1/4 liter vinegar, 1 teaspoon caraway, salt, and 1 liter cream
4. Allow this to reduce well
5. Place 24 live, washed crayfish in a casserole
6. Pour the prepared sauce through a sieve over the crayfish
7. Cook covered well for 1.5 hours
8. Arrange them in a deep dish and cover with a napkin
9. Serve the sauce in a sauce boat
