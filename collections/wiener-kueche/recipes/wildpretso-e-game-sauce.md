## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- Sugar
- Shallots
- 4 hard egg yolks
- Onions
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 20 crushed juniper berries
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Grate the zest of 2 lemons onto a piece of sugar.
2. Grind it finely and mix with finely chopped shallots, 4 hard egg yolks, and onions.
3. Add the juice of 2 lemons, 20 crushed juniper berries, vinegar and oil until it reaches sauce consistency.
4. Commonly served with dark game.
