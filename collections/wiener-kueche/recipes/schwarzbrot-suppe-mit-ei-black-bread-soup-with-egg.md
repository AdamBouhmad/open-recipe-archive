## Ingredients

- a piece of black house bread
- approximately 2 liters beef broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice black house bread thinly and roast on a baking sheet in the oven
2. Place the roasted bread in a casserole
3. Add approximately 2 liters beef broth and let cook slowly for approximately 1/4 hour
4. Pour into the soup pot and mix
