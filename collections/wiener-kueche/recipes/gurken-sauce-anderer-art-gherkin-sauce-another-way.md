## Ingredients

- Some onion
- Green parsley
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- Some soup
- Some vinegar
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 1 large peeled gherkin
- Salt water
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped dill herb

## Directions

1. Let some onion and green parsley roast in 8 tablespoons butter
2. Add 8 tablespoons flour, pour it with the necessary soup, some vinegar and 1/4 liter sour cream
3. Let it cook, allow it to simmer, stir well together and pass through a sieve
4. Cut 1 large peeled gherkin into cubes, blanch in salt water, strain and dry well
5. Add to the passed sauce and boil briefly
6. Before serving, sprinkle the sauce with 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped dill herb
7. If using pickled sour gherkins instead of fresh ones, cut them finely into thin slices and add to the passed sauce without letting them simmer in it beforehand
