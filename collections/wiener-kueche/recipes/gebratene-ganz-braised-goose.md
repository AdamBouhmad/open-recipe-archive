## Ingredients

- goose
- salt and pepper
- bacon slices
- raw ham (12 ounces)
- onion and root vegetables
- bay leaf
- spices
- broth (1 liter)
- white wine (1 liter)

## Directions

1. Clean the goose very thoroughly, open it, remove the insides and wash it.
2. Cut off the neck at the breast bone and the wings at the second joint, push the meat from the thighs of the third joint to the body, and remove these thighs as well as the feet below the knee.
3. Tuck the wings under the breast and truss the goose.
4. Sprinkle it with salt and pepper.
5. Put bacon slices into a casserole along with 12 ounces of raw ham, then onion and root vegetables, all finely sliced, 1 bay leaf and whole spices.
6. Place the goose on top and braise covered.
7. After 1/2 hour, pour 1 liter of broth and 1 liter of white wine over it and let the goose braise covered until completely tender - about 1 hour for a young goose, 2 hours for an old one.
8. Remove it then, degrease and pass the broth.
