## Ingredients

- 1 rose (sweetbread)
- salt
- breadcrumbs for breading
- hot lard for frying
- spinach
- cooked lettuce

## Directions

1. Wash the sweetbread and blanch it (cook 10 minutes in salted water).
2. Cool and cut into thin slices.
3. Salt the slices, bread them, and fry them in hot lard until golden.
4. Drain well.
5. Serve with spinach and cooked lettuce.
