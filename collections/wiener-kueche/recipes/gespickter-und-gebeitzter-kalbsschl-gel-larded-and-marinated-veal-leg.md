## Ingredients

- Small veal leg
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Brined bread
- Wine vinegar
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Skin the leg and lard it with bacon
2. Place it in a marinade for 24 hours
3. Remove and rub with salt
4. Roast it beautifully and juicily on a spit
5. Soak a slice of house bread in a small pot
6. Pour good wine vinegar over it
7. Season with pepper, cloves, and nutmeg
8. Let it simmer
9. When serving, garnish with thin and neatly cut lemon slices
10. Strain the broth into the dish
