## Ingredients

- 1 bitter orange
- 16 deciliters sugar
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Mark the peel of a bitter orange with a knife cut and roast the orange over coals
2. In the meantime, boil 16 deciliters sugar in 1/4 liter water somewhat thick
3. Add the orange and let it cool covered
4. Press it through a cloth to the prepared drink
5. This beverage can be served warm or cold
