## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 liter milk
- 10 deca butter
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg (optional)
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make beaten fritters from 4 eggs
2. Cut these into noodle-like pieces
3. Cook in 1/2 liter boiling milk until done
4. Meanwhile, beat 10 deca butter until fluffy
5. Beat in 5 egg yolks
6. Add some salt, the fritters, nutmeg if desired, and mix everything well
7. Lightly fold in the snow from 5 egg whites
8. Cook in steam or bake and treat like Gries-Schwerl
