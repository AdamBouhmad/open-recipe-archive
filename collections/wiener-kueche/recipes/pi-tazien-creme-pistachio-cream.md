## Ingredients

- Half a measure cream
- A good piece of sugar
- A quarter pound pistachios
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take half a measure cream, throw a good piece of sugar in it and cook it down on the fire
2. Then peel a quarter pound pistachios and pound them clean
3. Beat 6 egg yolks in a pot and stir long
