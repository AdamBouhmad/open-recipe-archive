## Ingredients

- pork leg
- root vegetables
- salt
- fat
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. After a pork leg has been salted and left in root vegetables for 3 days as described in the spit-roasted version, place it with the root vegetables and some fat in a pan
2. Pour a little vinegar over it and roast for approximately 1½ to 2 hours
3. During this time, frequently baste the meat with the juices that form and some broth
4. When the meat has a beautiful golden-brown color, remove it, cut into pieces, arrange on a platter and serve with desired purée or salad
