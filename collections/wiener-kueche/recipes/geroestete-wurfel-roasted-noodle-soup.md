## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 liter flour
- 10 deca butter
- 2 liters beef broth

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from the eggs and flour
2. Rub this dough on a grater
3. Roast the noodles in 10 deca butter until they take on a yellowish color
4. Otherwise proceed as indicated in the previous soup recipe
5. If it is to be used for seasoning, pour the roasted noodles with 1/4 liter boiling soup and let it evaporate
