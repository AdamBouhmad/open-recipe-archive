## Ingredients

- 16 spoonfuls pearled barley
- 1 liter boiling broth
- salt
- 2 egg yolks (optional)
- lemon juice or sour cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash 16 spoonfuls pearled barley in cold water
2. Stir into 1 liter boiling broth and cook slowly until soft
3. If the broth becomes too thick, thin with hot clear broth as needed
4. If desired, beat 2 egg yolks with lemon juice or sour cream and stir into the cooked barley soup
5. Serve at table
