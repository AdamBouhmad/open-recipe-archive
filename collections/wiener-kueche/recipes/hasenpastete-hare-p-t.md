## Ingredients

- 32 decagrams finely cut hare meat
- 32 decagrams pork meat
- 16 decagrams finely cut bacon
- 1 piece scraped marrow
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 8 decagrams trimmed, water-softened, well-pressed rolls
- salt to taste
- 1 knife tip of pepper
- 5 juniper berries
- 1 spice clove
- 1 sprig thyme
- 4 decagrams bacon cut into small cubes
- 4 decagrams cooked, smoked tongue, cut the same way
- 1 small raw goose liver, cut into small cubes
- 5 pieces peeled gherkins, cut into small cubes as desired
- diced truffles if available
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound all ingredients (first batch) very finely in a mortar
2. Pass through a sieve into a deep bowl and stir well together
3. Mix in the cubed bacon, tongue, goose liver, gherkins, and truffles with the pâté
4. Fill a long mold brushed with butter and lined with flour
5. Strike the mold several times on a flat hand so the pâté compacts firmly
6. Cut parchment paper to fit the mold size and make three cuts with a knife
7. Brush with butter, place on the pâté, and cook covered in steam for 1½ hours
8. After this time, turn out onto a board and let stand covered with the mold for ½ hour
9. Serve warm with a mushroom sauce, or on the next day brush with dissolved aspic, slice into knife-back thickness, arrange on a dish, and garnish with chopped and cut-out aspic
