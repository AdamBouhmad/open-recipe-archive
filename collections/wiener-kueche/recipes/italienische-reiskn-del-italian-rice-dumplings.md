## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds rice
- good bouillon
- 0.5 pounds beef marrow
- 0.5 pounds breadcrumbs
- butter for roasting
- 8 whole eggs
- 12 loth grated Parmesan cheese
- nutmeg
- salt
- butter for assembling
- celery and parsley roots (optional)

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice in water
2. Cook the rice until soft and brief in good bouillon with the beef marrow
3. Toast the breadcrumbs from grated rolls in butter
4. Stir the toasted crumbs, 8 whole eggs, 12 loth grated Parmesan cheese, and nutmeg into the rice
5. Test the mixture for sufficient salt
6. Butter a napkin and divide the mixture into three parts
7. Bind each dumpling separately with string
8. Cook for one hour in good bouillon to which celery, parsley roots, and similar may be added as desired
9. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and brown butter
