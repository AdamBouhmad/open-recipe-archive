## Ingredients

- 3/16 liter of cold water
- 3 1/6 liter of milk
- 21 drams of butter
- 1 knife point of salt
- 30 drams of flour
- 8 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Put 3/16 liter of cold water, 3 1/6 liter of milk, 21 drams of butter and 1 knife point of salt in a saucepan, and when this boils, gradually stir in 30 drams of flour while stirring constantly, then stir it on the stove until the dough is dry and separates from the saucepan.
2. The dough is now poured into a deep bowl and after it has cooled completely, 8 whole eggs are gradually stirred in.
3. Using a spoon, remove pieces of dough of any size, which are piped at a distance of 3 fingers on a baking sheet brushed with butter and sprinkled with flour, brushed with beaten egg and baked golden in the fairly hot oven.
4. When this is done, take them from the baking sheet with a very thin knife and cut them open on the side to the middle.
