## Ingredients

- 14 ounces butter, beaten
- 16 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons genuine maraschino
- 16 ounces almonds, finely ground with shell and then passed through sieve
- 6 egg whites, beaten to snow
- 1/2 liter milk, prepared bruised chestnuts

## Directions

1. Beat 14 ounces butter with 16 ounces sugar, 10 egg yolks and 3 tablespoons genuine maraschino for 1 hour and 15 minutes
2. Add 16 ounces finely ground almonds, mix well
3. Stir again for 1 hour and 15 minutes
4. Gently fold in the snow from 6 egg whites simultaneously with crushed chestnuts prepared from 1/2 liter milk
5. Fill a buttered mold lined with flour
6. Cover and steam for 3/4 hour
