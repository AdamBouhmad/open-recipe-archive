## Ingredients

- 1 measure water
- 3 beautiful juicy lemons
- sugar rubbed with the zest of one lemon
- 1/4 pound sugar
- optional: 1 seidel wine

## Directions

1. Take 1 measure water
2. Use 3 beautiful juicy lemons, rub the yellow zest from one of them onto sugar
3. Press the juice from all 3 lemons onto it
4. Stir well together
5. Sweeten with 1/4 pound sugar
6. Pass through a napkin
7. If desired, add 1 seidel wine, which greatly enhances the taste of the lemonade
