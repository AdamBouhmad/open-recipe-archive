## Ingredients

- ingredients for Anchovy Forcemeat
- crayfish tails and claws instead of anchovies
- crayfish butter instead of regular butter

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as Anchovy Forcemeat, substituting crayfish tails and claws for anchovies and using crayfish butter instead of regular butter
