## Ingredients

- 30 pieces of Maasanian or Tafeliner apples
- 2 liters water
- Sugar equal in weight to the juice
- A few drops of elderberry juice (optional, for coloring)

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the apples in half
2. Boil them quickly in a casserole with 2 liters of water until soft
3. Strain through a cloth
4. Add to this juice an equal amount of sugar as the weight of the juice and boil it again quickly until it becomes thick, removing the foam as it forms
5. Fill the jelly into glasses, bind these and boil for 3/4 hour in a steam bath
6. If desired, the jelly can be colored red with a few drops of elderberry juice before filling into glasses
