## Ingredients

- 22 deca flour
- 12 deca vanilla sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1/8 liter cream
- wax
- blotting paper

## Directions

1. Put 22 deca flour and 12 deca vanilla sugar into a deep bowl, gradually add 4 egg yolks, 1 whole egg and 1/8 liter cream and stir this very well until it flows smoothly from the spoon.
2. A flat wooden Hohihuppen form is heated over charcoal fire; when it is very hot, coat it with white wax, wipe it with blotting paper, lightly coat it again with wax and pour 1 spoon full of the prepared mass onto the hot iron, close it tightly and place it over the fire; only gradually is it then pressed together so that the dough does not fall apart through too rapid heating of the iron.
3. After about 1 minute, turn the iron over the coals and after an equally long time remove it, open it, whereupon the Hohihuppe should have a golden brown color. Then quickly remove it from the iron and roll it still warm over the Hohihuppen wood.
