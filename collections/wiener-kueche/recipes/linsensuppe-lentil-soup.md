## Ingredients

- brown broth from meat or fish
- a baked pike, tench or carp head
- baked bread slices
- crushed pepper
- butter
- flour
- lentils
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a brown broth from meat or fish and a baked pike, tench or carp head
2. Pound it very finely with baked bread slices
3. Add some crushed pepper
4. Make a brown roux with butter and flour
5. Add the cleaned, picked, cooked and salted lentils and the pounded ingredients with the broth
6. Let it cook well, sour it a bit, force it through a fine sieve
7. Salt and season it, then serve over baked pike liver and toasted bread slices
