## Ingredients

- a beautiful beef brisket
- ham, cut into strips the length and thickness of a small finger
- bacon, cut into strips the length and thickness of a small finger
- fresh parsley, finely chopped
- shallots, finely chopped
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- bacon, sliced in leaves
- onion
- yellow turnips
- white wine, one small drinking glass
- good broth, one small drinking glass

## Directions

1. Take a beautiful beef brisket and lard it thoroughly with strips of ham and bacon, both cut to the length and thickness of a small finger.
2. Finely chop some fresh parsley, shallots, and mushrooms together, add salt and pepper, and mix well.
3. Wrap the chopped ham and bacon strips in this mixture.
4. Lard the prepared meat with these wrapped pieces and place it in a casserole so the meat lies comfortably.
5. In the casserole, first lay sliced bacon, onion, and yellow turnips in leaves.
6. Place the meat on top, cover well, and let it stew slowly over gentle heat for four hours, turning it often so it does not burn.
7. Pour in one small drinking glass of white wine and an equal amount of good broth.
8. Continue stewing until the meat is tender enough, then remove the fat.
9. It is ready to serve. This Boeuf à la Mode can also be served cold.
