## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters green fresh peas
- salt water
- 1 tablespoon poulette sauce
- 2 ounces fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- roasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Cook 1.5 liters of green fresh peas in salt water
2. Press through a sieve
3. Pass through a fine strainer
4. Place in a casserole with 1 tablespoon of poulette sauce
5. Add 2 ounces fresh butter
6. Stir together well
7. Add salt and pepper to taste as needed
8. Arrange the peas heaped on a platter with roasted bread slices and various meat preparations
