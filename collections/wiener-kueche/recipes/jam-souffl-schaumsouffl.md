## Ingredients

- 16 ounces sugar
- 16 ounces marmalade of choice
- 2 whole eggs
- 5 egg whites, whipped
- blanched almonds
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put sugar and marmalade in a deep dish
2. Beat with 2 eggs for 1/2 hour
3. Gently fold in the stiffly whipped egg whites
4. Mound on a butter-greased dish
5. Arrange with sliced blanched almonds as desired
6. Sprinkle with finely powdered sugar
7. Bake in the open oven for 1 1/2 hours
