## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 16 ounces vanilla sugar
- 8 egg whites
- 14 ounces flour
- finely ground sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. After stirring 8 egg yolks with 16 ounces vanilla sugar for 3/4 hour, gently fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 8 egg whites with 14 ounces flour
2. Using a pastry bag or syringe, pipe this mass onto a sheet covered with unglazed paper in biscuit form, sprinkle with finely ground sugar, and bake in low heat in the oven until golden
3. Remove with a thin knife from the sheet and place on a sieve
4. Instead of small biscotti, one can form arbitrarily large pieces from this mass
