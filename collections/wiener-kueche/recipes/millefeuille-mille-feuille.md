## Ingredients

- Puff dough
- Butter
- Flour
- Eggs
- Filling (beliebigem Fasch or Pâté)
- Pastry decorations from puff dough

## Directions

1. Roll out chilled puff dough knife-back-thick and cut approximately 10 discs using a cake pan
2. Cut a large square from the rolled puff dough and lightly fold the corners together in the middle
3. Place in a buttered and flour-dusted cake pan with the four corners spread apart and draped over the sides
4. Brush the bottom finger-high with filling or pâté and cover with a puff dough disc
5. Continue alternating layers until the form is full
6. When the final dough disc is added, make several cuts with a knife and fold the four corners over each other
7. Brush with beaten eggs and add puff pastry decorations brushed with beaten eggs
8. Place the cake pan on a sand-strewn baking sheet and bake at moderate heat for 1 to 1.5 hours
9. Turn the pastry into a bowl and serve at the table
10. This dish can also be made with strudel dough instead of puff dough
