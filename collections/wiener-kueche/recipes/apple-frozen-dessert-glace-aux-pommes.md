## Ingredients

- About 10 pieces of Kaiserapfel, Pastanäpfel, Tyrolean, or Rosmarin apples
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 16 deca grams sugar
- 1/2 liter cold water
- 16 deca grams sugar
- 1/2 liter cold water
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel about 10 apples, cut them into wedges, and quickly rub them with lemon juice
2. Pound them very finely with 16 deca grams sugar in a stone mortar, adding 1/2 liter cold water gradually
3. Place this mass in a porcelain bowl
4. Dissolve 16 deca grams sugar in 1/2 liter cold water, mix this with the juice of 1 lemon into the apple mass, and let it sit covered for 1 hour
5. Pass it through a coarse sieve, weigh it to 22 degrees, and proceed as with the 'Ordinary Strawberry Frozen Dessert' (Recipe 1746)
