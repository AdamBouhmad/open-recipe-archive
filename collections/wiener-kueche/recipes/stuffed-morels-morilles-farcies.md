## Ingredients

- butter
- breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- green parsley
- egg yolks
- cream
- morels
- cold water
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix 8 ounces of butter with 4 ounces of breadcrumbs soaked in milk, some finely chopped green parsley, 3 egg yolks and 1 tablespoon of cream
2. Remove the morels from their stems and place them in cold water
3. After 1/4 hour, remove them and place them on a strainer
4. When the water has drained, fill them with the above mixture
5. Butter a deep casserole, pour some cream into it, place the filled morels in it, pour some more cream over them, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and braise uncovered
