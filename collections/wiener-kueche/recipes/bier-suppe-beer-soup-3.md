## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons salted beef broth
- 1 liter beer
- 12 ounces sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir together 6 egg yolks and 6 tablespoons salted beef broth
2. Bring 1 liter beer, 12 ounces sugar, and cinnamon to a good boil
3. Sprinkle the mixture into the boiling beer
