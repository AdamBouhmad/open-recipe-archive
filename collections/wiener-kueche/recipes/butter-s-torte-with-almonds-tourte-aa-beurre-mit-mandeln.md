## Ingredients

- 32 deca sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour per egg
- 32 deca melted butter
- 32 deca finely ground and sifted almonds
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 32 deca sugar with 10 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. After each egg, mix in 1 tablespoon of flour
3. Add 32 deca melted butter and 32 deca finely ground and sifted almonds gradually, stirring well after each spoonful of almonds and adding 1 tablespoon butter
4. Lightly mix in the stiffly beaten snow of 10 egg whites
5. Divide this mass into three equal parts, fill into buttered and floured cake forms and bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour
6. Treat as the previously described cake
