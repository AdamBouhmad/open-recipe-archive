## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 dkg sugar
- 2 dkg flour
- Butter
- Jam

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites until stiff peaks form
2. Stir in 4 egg yolks, 2 dkg sugar, and 2 dkg flour
3. Pour into a large, well-buttered omelet pan
4. Bake at moderate heat for 8-10 minutes until light brown
5. Loosen and quickly tip onto a plate
6. Spread with jam
7. Fold together
8. Sprinkle with sugar
9. Serve quickly
