## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- milk
- sugar
- finely cut orange peel
- salt
- fresh butter
- a couple of sweet macaroons
- 4 egg yolks
- eggs for coating
- fine bread crumbs
- lard for frying
- sugar for finishing
- ground cinnamon for finishing

## Directions

1. Wash 1/4 pound rice a couple of times in warm water, then in good milk and sugar with finely cut orange peel, some salt and fresh butter, not too soft
2. Pound a couple of sweet macaroons and stir them into the cooked rice
3. Remove from heat and slowly stir in 4 egg yolks
4. Using a tablespoon, form small balls, shape them nicely round, and coat them first in beaten eggs, then in fine bread crumbs
5. A few minutes before serving, fry them until golden, sprinkle with sugar and finely ground cinnamon while still hot, and serve hot
