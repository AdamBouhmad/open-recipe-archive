## Ingredients

- 16 deca beaten butter
- 16 deca finely ground sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 16 deca finely ground blanched almonds with skin
- 16 deca grated chocolate
- 1 knife tip finely chopped lemon and orange peel
- equal amount cinnamon
- 1 ground clove
- 4 egg whites
- 8 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat 16 deca of beaten butter gradually with 16 deca of finely ground sugar and 4 egg yolks for 1/2 hour
2. Add 16 deca of finely ground blanched almonds with skin, 16 deca of grated chocolate, 1 knife tip of finely chopped lemon and orange peel, equal amount of cinnamon, and 1 ground clove, stir well
3. Lightly mix in the snow of 4 egg whites with 8 deca of flour
4. Divide this mass into 3 parts, fill each into buttered and floured cake forms and bake 1/2 to 3/4 hours in a moderately hot oven
5. Place the layers on a sieve and after complete cooling, stack them together with jam or cream spread between them and cover with white glaze
