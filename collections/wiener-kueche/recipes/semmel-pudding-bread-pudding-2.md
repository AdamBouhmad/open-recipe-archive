## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk or cream
- 16 teaspoons trimmed, finely sliced rolls

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, add 3/4 liter of milk or cream, 16 teaspoons of trimmed, finely sliced rolls and stir after they have softened completely
