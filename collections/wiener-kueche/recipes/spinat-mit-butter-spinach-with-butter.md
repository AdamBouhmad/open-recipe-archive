## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- cream

## Directions

1. When the spinach is carefully sorted and washed, blanch it in water uncovered, otherwise it becomes yellow
2. Then pour off with cold water and press out well
3. Pass it in good butter with salt and some nutmeg
4. Finally pour in a cup of good cream
5. Arrange it very hot. Spinach can also be used for various dishes, for egg dishes, rissoles, tarts and more
