## Ingredients

- turnips
- 5 dkg hot butter
- 5-6 pieces of sugar cubes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Peel, wash, and slice the turnips thinly or into cubes.
2. In a saucepan, brown 5-6 sugar cubes in 5 dkg hot butter, add the turnips, season with salt, add a very small amount of water, cover, and stew for 1 hour under occasional shaking of the pan, lightly dust with flour at the end if desired.
