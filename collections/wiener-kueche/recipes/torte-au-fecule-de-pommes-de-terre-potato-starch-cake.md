## Ingredients

- 24 deca lemon sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- juice of 1 whole lemon
- 12 egg whites
- 12 deca potato starch
- 12 deca finest almond flour
- butter
- 8-10 deca small raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat 24 deca lemon sugar and 12 egg yolks with the juice of 1 whole lemon for 1 hour.
2. Lightly fold in the snow (beaten egg whites) from 12 egg whites alternately with 12 deca potato starch and 12 deca finest almond flour.
3. Fill a buttered cake form lined with arrowroot with this mixture.
4. Bake at low heat for approximately 1 hour.
5. Alternatively, omit the almond flour and use only 24 deca potato starch; in this case, beat the juice of 2 lemons with the eggs, lightly fold in half the flour, and then lightly fold in the beaten egg whites simultaneously with the remaining half of the flour by spoonfuls.
6. Small raisins (8-10 deca) can be mixed into the dough if baked in a Gugelhupf form.
7. This cake can also be covered with chocolate glaze.
