## Ingredients

- 1 piece onion, very finely cut
- garlic, finely crushed (optional)
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- vinegar as needed
- oil

## Directions

1. Mix the finely cut onion with crushed garlic if desired
2. Stir this mixture with 1 tablespoon vinegar
3. Pour the vinegar over cold carp pieces
4. Let stand for 1 hour
5. Before serving, pour oil and more vinegar over the fish
