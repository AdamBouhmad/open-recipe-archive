## Ingredients

- 16 decagrams butter or lard
- 1 piece finely cut onion
- very little finely sliced root vegetables
- 12 decagrams flour
- 0.25 liter vinegar
- 0.5 liter water
- 3 sprigs thyme
- 2 crushed juniper berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 grains each of spice
- 5 sprigs parsley tied together
- 1 small piece of lemon peel
- approximately 1 kilo fallen fish, cut into 2 finger wide pieces

## Directions

1. In 16 decagrams butter or lard place 1 piece finely cut onion and very little finely sliced root vegetables, let this braise covered somewhat, add 12 decagrams flour and let it brown
2. Then add 0.25 liter vinegar, 0.5 liter water (in total as much liquid as sauce is needed), 3 sprigs thyme, 2 crushed juniper berries, 1 bay leaf, 5 grains each of spice, 5 sprigs parsley tied together and 1 small piece of lemon peel
3. Let this cook for 0.5 hour
4. In a pot place approximately 1 kilo fish cut into 2 finger wide pieces
