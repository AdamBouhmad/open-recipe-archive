## Ingredients

- 12 whole eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk or cream
- Salt
- Pepper
- 12 deca grams very hot lard or butter

## Directions

1. Whisk 12 whole eggs with 4 tablespoons milk or cream, the necessary salt and some pepper.
2. Pour into a pan or egg dish casserole in which 12 deca grams very hot lard or butter is contained and treat further as for Scrambled Eggs with Bacon.
