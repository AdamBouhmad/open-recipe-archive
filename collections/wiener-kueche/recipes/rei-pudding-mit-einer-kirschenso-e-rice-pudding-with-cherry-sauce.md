## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice
- 1/2 portion cream
- 1 measure milk
- whole cinnamon
- 1/2 pound butter
- 16 egg yolks
- 2 pounds sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1/2 pound small raisins
- 8 eggs (for snow)
- butter for the cloth

## Directions

1. Select and rinse the rice, then scald it with boiling water.
2. Fill it with boiling milk and cook slowly until thickened, allowing it to swell well while remaining granular.
3. Cook the prepared rice with half a portion of cream, one measure of milk, and some whole cinnamon, then let it cool.
4. Beat 1/2 pound butter with 16 egg yolks into a foam, stir in 2 pounds sugar with the zest of 1 lemon rubbed on it, then 1/2 pound small raisins, and carefully fold in the rice.
5. Slowly draw in the snow from 8 eggs.
6. Butter a clean cloth the size of a plate, lay it in a bowl, pour the mixture into it, and bind loosely so the mass can swell.
7. Hang the pudding in a pot of simmering water and let it cook continuously for 1.5 hours.
8. Serve with cherry sauce poured over the pudding.
