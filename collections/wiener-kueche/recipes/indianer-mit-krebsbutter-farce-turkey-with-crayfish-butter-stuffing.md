## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- Crayfish butter stuffing (double the amount used for chicken)

## Directions

1. The treatment is the same as specified under recipe 821 ('Hühner mit Krebsbutter Farce'), with the difference that for a turkey you must make double the amount of stuffing.
