## Ingredients

- 1 yellow turnip
- 1 parsley root
- 1 piece of celery
- 1 small cabbage
- 1 kohlrabi
- 6 dekagrams Parmesan
- 1/2 kilogram macaroni
- 8 dekagrams butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and cut the root vegetables, kohlrabi and cabbage into fine noodle-like pieces
2. Put them in cold salted water, except the cabbage, and cook for 1¼ hours
3. After about 20 minutes, add the cabbage and cook together
4. Make a roux with 8 dekagrams butter, pour the vegetable water on it, cook well, salt
5. Add the vegetables and cook together
6. Add 4 dekagrams macaroni cooked in salted water, cut small, and serve the soup with grated Parmesan cheese
