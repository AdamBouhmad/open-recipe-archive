## Ingredients

- 14 dkg raw grated almonds or hazelnuts
- 7 dkg sugar
- 1-2 egg whites
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dkg raw grated almonds or hazelnuts with 7 dkg sugar and 1-2 egg whites on a board sprinkled with sugar
2. Knead to a dough
3. Form into a roll
4. Cut pieces from it
5. Form each piece into a crescent
6. Roll the crescents in pine nuts, pressing them in slightly so they stick
7. Place the crescents on a buttered baking sheet
8. Bake at low heat until light brown
