## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- Powdered sugar
- Wafers moistened with water
- Wine batter
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Preparation is the same as for Strawberry Fritters, using raspberries instead of strawberries.
2. Fresh cherries and other berries can be used the same way as strawberries and raspberries, only the stems must be removed.
