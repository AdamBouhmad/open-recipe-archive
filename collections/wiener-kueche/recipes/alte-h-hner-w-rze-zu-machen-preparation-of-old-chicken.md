## Ingredients

- Old chicken
- Cold water
- Salt

## Directions

1. From a slaughtered old chicken, immediately remove the entrails and place the chicken in a deep bowl.
2. Pour cold water over it and let it lie with the feathers at a cool place for about three days.
3. Change the water fresh every day.
4. After this time, remove it, pluck the feathers, clean, dress, and salt the chicken, and let it lie for some hours.
5. Roast or braise it like a poularde and serve it with a fine sauce or with cabbage.
