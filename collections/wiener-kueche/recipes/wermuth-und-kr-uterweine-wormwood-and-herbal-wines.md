## Ingredients

- 3 handfuls common wormwood
- 3 handfuls Roman wormwood
- 3 handfuls Kardobenedikten (blessed thistle) herb
- 2 handfuls liverwort
- 2 handfuls rosemary
- 2 handfuls lemon balm
- 2 handfuls hyssop
- must (newly pressed wine)

## Directions

1. Cut the herbs finely
2. Fill them into a small sachet
3. Hang the sachet in the small barrel with the must for 12 to 14 days
4. After this time, the wine is already drinkable
