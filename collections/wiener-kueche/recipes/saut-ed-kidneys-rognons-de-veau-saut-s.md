## Ingredients

- Fat
- Onion, cut into small pieces
- Kidneys, skinned and finely sliced
- Flour
- Salt and pepper
- Soup or white wine
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Put fat and finely cut onion in a saucepan and roast
2. Cut skinned kidneys into fine slices and add to the roasted onion
3. Dust with a little flour, salt and pepper
4. Stir well together, roast for 5 to 10 minutes
5. Pour in some soup or white wine
6. Add a pinch of marjoram, stir everything well together
7. Serve in a platter
