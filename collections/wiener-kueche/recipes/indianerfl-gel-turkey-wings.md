## Ingredients

- Turkey wings
- Ground turkey meat
- Broth
- Household broth or preserves

## Directions

1. After plucking and cleaning the wings, they can be cooked with the ground turkey meat in the broth and then, cut into neat pieces, served in the broth.
2. Alternatively, the softly cooked, neatly cut wings can be served in household broth or preserves.
