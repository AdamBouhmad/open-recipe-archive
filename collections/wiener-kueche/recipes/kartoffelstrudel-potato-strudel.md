## Ingredients

- 20 dkg Flour
- 1 Egg
- Salt
- Butter (walnut-sized)
- Almost 1 dl lukewarm water
- 5 dkg Raisins
- 10 dkg Butter (for filling)
- 8 dkg Sugar
- 3 Egg yolks
- ½ Lemon (juice and zest)
- 6 dkg softened grated almonds
- 26 dkg cooked mashed cooled potatoes
- 2 dl Cream
- 3 Egg whites

## Directions

1. Make strudel dough from flour, egg, salt, butter, and lukewarm water
2. After resting, stretch thinly on floured cloth
3. Dot with butter and trim edges
4. Spread filling on dough and sprinkle raisins on top
5. Roll up strudel and place in buttered baking pan
6. Bake on higher heat until light brown
7. Baste frequently with hot milk during baking
8. Cut into pieces, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm
9. For the filling: cream together butter, sugar, and egg yolks, add lemon juice and zest, grated almonds, mashed potatoes, and cream, fold in beaten egg whites (whites should be beaten while dough is resting)
