## Ingredients

- 4-6 paprika peppers
- 25 dkg pork shoulder
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 cup cream
- 12 dkg cooked rice
- 4 dkg fat
- 1 onion
- 1 egg
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut off the tops of the peppers at the stem and hollow them out.
2. For the filling: finely chop the meat. In a pan, heat 4 dkg fat and sauté finely chopped onion and parsley, add the meat and brown for 15 minutes.
3. Add 12 dkg cooked rice, the insides of the tomato, salt and pepper, and braise for 30 minutes.
4. Mix with 1 egg and a few spoonfuls of cream.
5. Fill the peppers with this mixture, place them in a pan with a little fat, pour cream over them, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and braise for 30 minutes.
6. Place on a serving dish, skim the fat from the cooking liquid, bring the remainder to a boil with broth, and pour over the peppers.
