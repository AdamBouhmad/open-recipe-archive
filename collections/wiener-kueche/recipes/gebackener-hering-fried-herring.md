## Ingredients

- 1 herring
- Milk
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter or lard, very hot
- Green parsley
- Lemon wedges
- Vegetables (optional)

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove head and tail, and dissolve the bones
2. Soak the herring in milk for some time
3. Dredge in flour
4. Dip in beaten eggs
5. Roll in breadcrumbs
6. Fry on both sides in very hot lard until golden
7. Arrange in a serving bowl, sprinkle with green parsley
8. Garnish with lemon wedges or serve with vegetables
9. Alternatively, the herring can be fried whole after being skinned, but must be scored on both sides
