## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- 1-2 liters stock
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 1/4 liter milk cream
- Several tablespoons grated horseradish
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. To 8 tablespoons butter, let 8 tablespoons flour roast equally, pour it with about 1-2 liters stock
2. Add some salt, nutmeg, about 1/4 liter milk cream, and several tablespoons grated horseradish and let boil
3. Before serving, whisk 1 egg yolk with 2 tablespoons cream and stir it into the boiled milk-horseradish sauce
