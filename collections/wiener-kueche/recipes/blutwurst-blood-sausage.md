## Ingredients

- A couple dozen onions
- Butter
- Two pounds diced pork fat
- Salt, pepper, spices
- Finely chopped parsley
- Young onions
- Two measures blood
- One measure sweet cream
- Cleaned and washed pork intestines

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cut into small cubes
2. Soften gently in butter
3. Add two pounds pork fat cut the same way to the onions
4. Add salt, pepper, spices, finely chopped parsley and young onion
5. Stir together one measure sweet cream with two measures blood in a pot
6. Pour this into the casserole with the other ingredients and stir thoroughly so the pork fat distributes well and does not become lumpy
7. The mass should be neither too thick nor too thin and must have a particularly good taste
8. Fill into cleaned and well-washed pork intestines using a sausage press
9. Tie both ends of each intestine securely
10. Throw into a kettle of very hot water that must not boil
11. Once the sausages start to become somewhat stiff, prick with a needle
12. As soon as no more blood runs out, remove from water, let cool, and grill afterwards
