## Ingredients

- carp
- pork blood
- onions
- shallots
- butter
- basil
- thyme
- marjoram
- milk
- semmels (rolls)
- salt
- spices
- melted butter
- fatty pork intestines

## Directions

1. Prepare as with pike sausages, but use pork blood instead of eggs and milk
