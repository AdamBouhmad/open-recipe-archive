## Ingredients

- 4 large paradise apples
- thin slices of lemon
- 16 deka steamed rice (Pilau)

## Directions

1. Break apart 4 large paradise apples
2. Steam them with a few thin slices of lemon
3. Press them through a sieve into 16 deka steamed rice
4. Stir well together
5. Arrange in a bowl and serve at table
