## Ingredients

- a nice fillet
- fine bacon
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- finely ground cloves
- thyme
- basil
- fine oil
- butter
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and fat from a nice fillet and trim it well
2. Lard one side symmetrically with fine bacon
3. Cut the other side lightly across the grain and fill it with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, finely ground cloves, thyme and basil, moderately
4. Pour fine oil over it and let it marinate for a while
5. Roast it on a spit
6. Baste it with butter and lemon juice
7. Before it comes off the spit, sprinkle the side of the fillet that is not larded with breadcrumbs and finely chopped parsley
8. It must be in its juices when served
9. Serve an Italian sauce, piquant sauce, or pepper sauce with it
