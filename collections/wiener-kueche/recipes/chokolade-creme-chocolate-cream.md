## Ingredients

- Half a pound fine chocolate
- One Seidel boiling milk
- 5 chicken stomachs
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Stir half a pound fine chocolate in one Seidel boiling milk until it is only lukewarm
2. Finely pound 5 chicken stomachs and add them
3. Sweeten the mixture sufficiently
4. Strain it through a hair sieve, set on warm ash, cover with a lid, and place some coals on top
5. Place the cream in the cellar to set
