## Ingredients

- 10 dkg ham
- ⅛ liter cream
- 40 dkg raw peeled potatoes
- 8 dkg butter
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- 25-30 dkg flour
- flour for dusting
- egg white for brushing
- hot lard for frying
- mushroom sauce, spinach or lettuce salad

## Directions

1. Cook the 40 dkg raw peeled potatoes, drain and pass through a sieve, let cool
2. Beat 8 dkg butter, mix with 1 egg yolk, salt, the potatoes and 25-30 dkg flour
3. Work on a floured board, roll out thinly
4. Cut out with a cutter, put filling in the middle
5. Brush the edges with egg white, lay a second sheet of dough on top, press the edges together
6. Cut out again with a smaller cutter
7. Fry in hot lard
8. Serve warm with mushroom sauce, spinach or lettuce salad
