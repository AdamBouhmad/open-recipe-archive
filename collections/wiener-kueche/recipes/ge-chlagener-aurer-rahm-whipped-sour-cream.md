## Ingredients

- 3 sidels of thick sour cream, not bitter-tasting
- 8 loths of finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Take 3 sidels of thick sour cream that does not taste bitter
2. Add 8 loths of finely ground sugar
3. Whip it into thick foam, taking about a quarter hour
4. Do not whip the thick cream too long as it easily becomes buttery
5. Serve it like the preceding whipped cream
