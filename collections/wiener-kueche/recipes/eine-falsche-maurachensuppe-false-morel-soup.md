## Ingredients

- raw chicken livers
- chicken stomachs
- parsley
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- nutmeg flower

## Directions

1. Chop the raw chicken livers and stomachs finely together with parsley
2. Mix with a couple of eggs, add breadcrumbs, some salt and nutmeg flower
3. Form small morels from this mixture
4. Dip each in beaten eggs and coat with breadcrumbs
5. Fry in not too sweet lard until golden
6. Place the fried morels in soup made from ordinary broth or consommé
7. Let it simmer once more and serve
