## Ingredients

- Beef
- Kernel fat or bacon
- Onion
- Root vegetables
- Caraway seeds
- Salt
- Spices
- Paprika
- Vinegar
- Water
- Flour
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Skin a piece of beef and cut it into pieces of desired size, cube-shaped.
2. Kernel fat or bacon and onion, both finely cut, are roasted somewhat with cube-cut root vegetables.
3. Add the meat, also a little caraway, salt, spices, paprika, then add enough vinegar and water so that the liquid comes over it.
4. Let it braise until the meat is half tender, then stir 4 decagrams of flour with 1/2 liter of sour cream and pour it to the goulash, letting it braise covered under frequent stirring until tender.
5. If 4 decagrams of gingerbread is braised with it, the flour can be omitted.
