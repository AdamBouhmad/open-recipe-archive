## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/3 liter flour

## Directions

1. Make firm dough from 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk, and 1/3 liter flour
2. Work and knead dough
3. Cut very finely with a sharp knife
4. Rub several times with flat hands
5. Push through a sieve
