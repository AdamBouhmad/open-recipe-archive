## Ingredients

- 2 smaller cabbage heads
- 25 dag smoked meat
- 1/2 liter cream
- 10 dag Parmesan cheese
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks and bad leaves from the cabbage, wash it, place in boiling salt water for 5 minutes, drain, lay the leaves one by one on a board, fill and roll into small sausage shapes.
2. For the filling: mix 2 eggs with half the cream, salt, pepper, and the chopped ham.
3. Butter and flour the pudding mold, add 2 eggs...
