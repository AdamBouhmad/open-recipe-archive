## Ingredients

- cleaned and prepared chickens
- finely cut sardelles (anchovies)
- lard
- onions
- root vegetables
- whole spices
- soup

## Directions

1. After cleaning and preparing chickens, spit them with finely cut sardelles instead of bacon
2. In a casserole, place lard, onions, root vegetables and whole spices
3. Lay the chickens on top and braise covered
4. When the liquid that forms has evaporated, pour some soup over them
5. Braise the chickens until tender
6. Remove them, cut into desired pieces, arrange on a platter
7. Serve with sardelle sauce
