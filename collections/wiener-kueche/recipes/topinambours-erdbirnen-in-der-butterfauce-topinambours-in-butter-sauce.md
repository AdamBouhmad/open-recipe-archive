## Ingredients

- topinambours (Jerusalem artichokes)
- butter
- optional: veal or mutton

## Directions

1. Boil the topinambours or Jerusalem artichokes properly and peel them.
2. Cut the large ones into several pieces and leave the small ones whole.
3. Make a butter sauce and boil them in it.
4. If desired, they can be used as a side dish and boiled together with veal or mutton.
