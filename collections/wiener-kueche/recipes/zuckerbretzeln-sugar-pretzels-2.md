## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- Half a pound sugar
- One and a half pounds flour
- 9 eggs
- 1 Quäntchen cinnamon
- Zest of one lemon
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Stir the butter to a foam, gradually beat in the eggs, add the spices, and finally the flour
2. Make small pretzels from it, brush them with egg white, and bake not too hot
