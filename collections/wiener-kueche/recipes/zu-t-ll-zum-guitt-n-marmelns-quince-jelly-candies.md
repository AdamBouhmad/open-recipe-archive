## Ingredients

- 8 Deca finely sifted sugar
- 16 Deca quince marmalade
- 1 tablespoon very finely chopped citron
- some nutmeg
- more sugar as needed

## Directions

1. Place 8 Deca of finely sifted sugar and 16 Deca of quince marmalade in a bowl and stir together over heat until the mixture separates from the bowl.
2. Pour onto a board sprinkled with finely sifted sugar and work together with 1 tablespoon very finely chopped citron, some nutmeg, and additional sugar to form a dough.
3. Roll out to finger-thickness and cut into desired pieces.
4. Lay these pieces on white paper and let them dry in an airy location.
