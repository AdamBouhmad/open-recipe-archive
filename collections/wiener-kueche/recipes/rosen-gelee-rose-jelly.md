## Ingredients

- Hirschhorn (deer antler gelatin)
- 1 Maß water
- 1 Loth Tournesol (litmus)
- egg white, beaten
- sugar
- 1/2 bottle rose water
- juice of 6 lemons
- 1/2 bottle good Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Cook the deer antler gelatin with 1 Maß water as described for strawberry jelly
2. Add the Tournesol to the cooked gelatin
3. Allow it to come to a boil once with the beaten egg white and sugar, which will give it a beautiful red color
4. Remove from heat and add 1/2 bottle of the best rose water, along with the juice of 6 lemons and 1/2 bottle good Rhine wine
5. Proceed as described in the general method
