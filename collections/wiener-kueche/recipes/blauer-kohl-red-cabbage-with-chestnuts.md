## Ingredients

- 10 pieces of red cabbage
- salt water
- cold water
- 10 deciliters butter
- 10 deciliters flour
- 1/2 liter broth
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 12 deciliters sugar
- 40 pieces roasted and peeled chestnuts

## Directions

1. Strip the leaves from the cabbage stems, wash them, cook in salted water and then place in cold water
2. Make a roux from 10 deciliters butter and 10 deciliters flour
3. Add the expressed finely chopped cabbage, pour in 1/2 liter broth, add the necessary salt and 1 tablespoon sugar and let it cook well
4. If the cabbage is too thick, thin it with a little broth
5. For the chestnuts: let 12 deciliters sugar brown, add it with some water, add 40 roasted peeled chestnuts as desired, and let them braise covered
6. When serving, place the cabbage on a platter and arrange the braised chestnuts on top
7. Garnish as desired with calf's meat, finely minced meat, etc.
