## Ingredients

- 10 dkg flour
- 10 dkg butter
- 10 dkg sugar
- 10 dkg raw grated almonds
- 3 hard-boiled sifted egg yolks
- 1 ground clove
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- finely chopped lemon zest
- apple jam

## Directions

1. Rub together 10 dkg flour and 10 dkg butter on a board
2. Knead with 10 dkg sugar, 10 dkg raw grated almonds, 3 hard-boiled sifted egg yolks, 1 ground clove, 1 pinch cinnamon, and finely chopped lemon zest into a dough
3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes and divide into three parts
4. Roll out one part to the size of the torte form bottom and place in the form
5. Form a roll from the second part, place in the form, and press the dough against the wall
6. Spread the bottom with apple jam
7. Form several thin rolls from the third part, arrange them in a lattice pattern on top, and press at the edges
8. Bake at higher heat
9. You may fill additional jam into the gaps and cut the torte evenly
