## Ingredients

- 30 dkg diaphragm meat or beef broth
- 3 bread rolls

## Directions

1. Rinse the bread rolls, soak them in broth, beat them well, pour in the remaining strained broth.
2. Cook for a few minutes and optionally thicken with liaison.
