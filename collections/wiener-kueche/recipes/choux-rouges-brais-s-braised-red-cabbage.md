## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams hot fat
- 6 decagrams crushed sugar
- 1 head of cabbage, finely cut
- 1 onion
- broth
- vinegar
- salt
- 3 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. In 12 decagrams hot fat, let 6 decagrams crushed sugar brown.
2. Let 1 finely cut head of onion roast lightly, then add the cabbage as described above and let braise together somewhat.
3. Add enough broth and vinegar so that it covers the cabbage.
4. When the liquid has completely evaporated and the cabbage is rather soft, dust with 3 tablespoons flour, stir well and let evaporate somewhat more.
5. Pour with approximately 1/2 liter broth, stir well and let boil.
6. Add the necessary salt and, if not sour enough, some vinegar.
7. The cabbage should be pleasantly sweet-sour.
8. You can also add butter sauce instead of the flour dust. The cabbage must be agreeably sweet-sour.
