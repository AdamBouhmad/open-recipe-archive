## Ingredients

- About 10 beautiful Manzanita apples
- Lemon juice
- Cold water
- 32 deka sugar
- 1/4 liter water
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 deka isinglass
- 7/8 liter cold water
- 1/4 liter water for reduction
- Marmalade
- Angelica cut into neat pieces
- Green almonds cut into neat pieces

## Directions

1. Peel about 10 beautiful apples, hollow out 5 pieces and halve the others
2. Rub all with lemon and place in cold water with lemon juice to keep them white
3. Boil 32 deka sugar, 1/4 liter water, and the juice of 1/2 lemon, remove the rising foam
4. Cook the apples in this clarified sugar until somewhat tender
5. Place them in cold water with some lemon juice
6. Prepare 2 deka isinglass by stirring it with 7/8 liter cold water and reducing to 1/4 liter
7. Pour this while still warm into the sugar, mix both together and let it cool
8. Pour into a mold that has been previously placed in ice, filling finger-high with the sugar mixture
9. Let this freeze, then arrange the apples very decoratively, finger-width apart, filling the hollowed ones with marmalade
10. Also use whole redcurrants and neatly cut pieces of Angelica or green almonds
11. Pour the remaining sugar mixture over, let it set solid, dip the mold briefly in hot water, turn the apples out onto a dish and serve
