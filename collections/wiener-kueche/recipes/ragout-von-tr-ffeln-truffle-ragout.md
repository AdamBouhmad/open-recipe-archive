## Ingredients

- truffles
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- white coulis
- lemon juice
- croutons (roasted bread slices)

## Directions

1. Wash the truffles, peel them, and cut them into thin slices
2. Set a large piece of butter on fire in a casserole
3. Pass the truffles for a while
4. Add salt and pepper
5. Stir in a white coulis
6. When serving, skim off the fat and press lemon juice into it
7. Garnish them with croutons (roasted bread slices)
