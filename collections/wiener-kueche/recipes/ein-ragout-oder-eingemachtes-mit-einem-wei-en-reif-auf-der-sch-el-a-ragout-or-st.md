## Ingredients

- one quarter pound rice
- good meat broth
- three egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter
- stewed veal or chicken
- crayfish sauce
- crayfish butter
- flour
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Cook rice in good meat broth until very thick
2. Stir in egg yolks while the rice is still warm
3. Add salt and nutmeg
4. Butter a flat platter and form a round rice ring on it
5. In the middle of the ring place stewed veal or chicken in crayfish sauce
6. Make a butter sauce from crayfish butter and flour
7. Add the meat or chicken with crayfish tails to the sauce
8. Put the stew with sauce in the ring
9. Place it in a hot oven or tube for a quarter hour and serve
