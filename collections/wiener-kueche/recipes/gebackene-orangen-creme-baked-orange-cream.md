## Ingredients

- half a measure good cream
- 12 egg yolks
- sugar
- a couple of oranges
- lemons
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a cream from half a measure good cream with 12 egg yolks, sufficiently sweetened, rubbing with a couple of oranges and lemons
2. Prepare it in the previous manner, place in simmering water, and cook until done
3. When the cream is quite cold, cut into pieces
4. Turn in flour and breadcrumbs
5. Fry quickly in lard
6. Better taste if turned in wine batter and fried in lard
7. For the batter: take 2 handfuls flour, a couple tablespoons fine oil, sugar rubbed with a lemon, mix with white wine to a fluid batter
8. Beat the whites of 6 eggs to snow, stir in, and fry slowly in lard
