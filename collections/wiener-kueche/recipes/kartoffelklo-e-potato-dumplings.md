## Ingredients

- 1 Vierding (about 4 oz) butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls sweet cream
- 4 Loth (about 2 oz) sugar
- orange blossom water
- 3/4 pound grated potatoes
- lard for frying
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. Beat butter with 3 whole eggs and 5 egg yolks into a foam
2. Add 3 spoonfuls of sweet cream and 4 Loth sugar, and some orange blossom water
3. Slowly stir in 3/4 pound of grated potatoes until the mass is stiff enough to form dumplings
4. Fry them until golden in lard
5. Serve with a cream sauce
