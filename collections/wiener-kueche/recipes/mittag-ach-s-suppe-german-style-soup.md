## Ingredients

- 14 Deciliter hot butter
- approximately 14 Deciliter flour
- 2 liters cold beef broth
- salt
- parsley, mushrooms, or morels for flavoring
- optional: calf's head, small pieces, egg noodles, green peas, toasted rolls, or a mixture

## Directions

1. Add approximately 14 Deciliter flour to 14 Deciliter hot butter
2. Let it roast
3. Pour it over with 2 liters cold beef broth and stir well
4. Add salt and let it come to a boil
5. Flavor with parsley, mushrooms, or morels as desired
6. Add optional ingredients such as calf's head, small pieces, egg noodles, green peas, toasted rolls, or a mixture
