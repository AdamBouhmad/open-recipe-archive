## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Fricassee sauce

## Directions

1. Let the brain soak in cold water for several minutes, then remove the skin
2. Place the brain in boiling salt water and cook until it completely contracts
3. Remove with a serving spoon and arrange on a dish with fricassee sauce
