## Ingredients

- 6 deca roasted orange blossoms
- cream
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as Orange Gefrornees (No. 1728), with the difference that instead of orange peel, throw 6 deca roasted orange blossoms into the hot cream
