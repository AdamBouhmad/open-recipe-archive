## Ingredients

- 24 deca rice
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 12 deca butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg whites
- raisins, currants, pistachios, almonds, pignoli, and dates as desired

## Directions

1. Stir 24 deca rice into 1/2 liter boiling milk and cook thoroughly
2. Add 12 deca butter, mix well and let cool
3. Beat 6 egg yolks with 12 deca vanilla sugar for 1/2 hour
4. Add the cooled rice, mix well
5. Lightly fold in the snow from 6 egg whites
6. If desired, add raisins, currants, pistachios, finely cut almonds, pignoli, and dates with kernels removed
7. Fill a buttered and floured mold with this mixture
8. Steam covered for 3/4 hour
