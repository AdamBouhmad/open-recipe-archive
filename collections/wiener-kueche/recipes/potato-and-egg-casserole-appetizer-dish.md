## Ingredients

- 12 boiled potatoes of smaller variety
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- salt
- butter
- flour
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- capers, peeled and finely diced
- 1/2 liter milk cream
- 3 whole eggs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the boiled potatoes and cut them into thin slices, mixing them well with 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley and some salt.
2. Butter the bottom of a casserole dish (which can also be brought to table) and dust it with flour.
3. Layer the cut potatoes, then sprinkle with hard-boiled eggs cut into slices and peeled capers finely diced.
4. Beat 1/2 liter of milk cream with 3 whole eggs and pour some spoonfuls over the eggs and capers.
5. Add another layer of potatoes, sprinkle with eggs and capers, and continue until the casserole is full.
6. If potatoes are the last layer, pour the remaining cream over them, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and bake in the oven for 3/4 to 1 hour, covered at first so the crumbs do not burn.
7. When the egg mixture shows no more liquid, remove from oven and serve at table in the casserole, or turn out into a dish earlier.
8. Alternatively, the whole dish can be made in a buttered bowl and baked in the oven, or lined in a buttered torte form with knife-thick layer of butter dough, filled with potatoes as described above, and baked in the oven. Before serving, remove the rim so the egg dish comes to table in the crust.
