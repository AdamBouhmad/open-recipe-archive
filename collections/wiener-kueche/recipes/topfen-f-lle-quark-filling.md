## Ingredients

- 20 Deka strained quark
- sugar to taste
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest
- finely chopped and blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Pass 20 Deka of quark through a sieve
2. Sweeten to taste
3. Mix in 1 teaspoon finely chopped lemon zest and finely chopped, blanched almonds
