## Ingredients

- 16 ounces flour
- 12 ounces butter
- 1.5 pinches salt
- 1 whole egg
- 3 tablespoons sour cream or sweet custard
- 3 tablespoons wine
- flour for dusting
- butter for greasing the molds
- 5 fresh prunes for filling
- optional: other fresh fruit

## Directions

1. Place 16 ounces flour on a board and cut in 12 ounces butter
2. Rub flour and butter together with hands
3. Make a well in the center and add 1.5 pinches salt, 1 whole egg, 3 tablespoons sour cream or sweet custard, and 3 tablespoons wine
4. Mix well with a knife and work everything into a smooth dough
5. Place on a floured board, cover with a cloth, and let rest in a cool place for 1 hour
6. Roll dough to the thickness of a knife-back
7. Cut into squares to fit the tartlet molds
8. Line the buttered molds dusted with flour
9. Line the sides and bottom with 5 fresh prunes cut in half
10. Cut away overhanging dough
11. Place on a floured board, cover with a cloth, and let rise in a warm place
12. Bake at medium heat for 1.5 hours
13. Carefully remove from the molds with a spatula, arrange elevated on a platter, and dust with sugar
14. Alternative fruits may be used instead of prunes
