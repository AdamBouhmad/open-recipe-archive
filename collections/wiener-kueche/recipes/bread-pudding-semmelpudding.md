## Ingredients

- 10 dkg butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 8 dkg sugar
- 3 dkg citron (cut into thin strips)
- 5 dkg swelled almonds (cut into thin sticks)
- 3 dkg raisins
- 2 dkg currants
- 4 egg whites
- 3 bread rolls (rinded, cut into cubes, and moistened with milk)

## Directions

1. Beat 10 dkg butter until light, then gradually add 4 egg yolks mixed with 8 dkg sugar.
2. Mix in 3 dkg finely cut citron, 5 dkg swelled almonds cut into sticks, 3 dkg raisins, and 2 dkg currants.
3. Fold in 4 egg whites together with 3 bread rolls that have been rinded, cut into cubes, and moistened with milk.
4. Fill the mixture into a pudding mold and let it set in steam for ½ hours.
5. Loosen, turn out, and serve warm with chaudeau or vanilla cream.
