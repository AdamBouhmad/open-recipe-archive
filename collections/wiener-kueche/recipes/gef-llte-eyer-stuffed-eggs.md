## Ingredients

- Eggs, as many as needed
- Butter
- Bread (Semmel), soaked in milk
- Sour cream (Schmette)
- Pepper
- Salt
- Green parsley
- Bread crumbs (Semmelbrösel)
- Chives (Schnittling)

## Directions

1. Boil eggs until hard, shell them, and cut them in half lengthwise.
2. Remove the yolks, chop them with a piece of bread soaked in milk, then mix with butter.
3. Add sour cream, pepper, salt, and green parsley to the yolk mixture.
4. Fill the egg white halves with this mixture, mounding it on top.
5. Arrange the filled egg halves in a row in a dish brushed with lard and sour cream.
6. Pour melted butter over them, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and add sour cream without covering the eggs.
7. Garnish with green parsley and chives.
8. Bake with heat from above and below in the oven.
