## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 4 lemons
- 12 lemons
- 3 measures boiling water
- Half a loth tea
- 1 bottle good arrack

## Directions

1. Rub the yellow of 4 lemons onto the 2 pounds of sugar.
2. Put the sugar in a soup pot.
3. Place the rubbed-off zest in a fine hair sieve or a colander lined with a napkin.
4. Press the juice of 12 lemons through the sieve.
5. Pour 3 measures of boiling water in which half a loth of tea has been steeped, along with 1 bottle of good arrack, over the mixture.
6. Cover the soup pot and set it on gentle charcoal fire.
7. When a white foam appears at the edge, remove it.
