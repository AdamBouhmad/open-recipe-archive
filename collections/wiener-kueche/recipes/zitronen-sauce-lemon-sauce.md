## Ingredients

- 1 peeled lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- some finely cut lemon peel
- some sugar
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the flesh of 1 peeled lemon and cut it into dice
2. Add it with the juice of 1 lemon and some finely cut lemon peel, some sugar, salt, and nutmeg to a butter sauce
3. Boil thoroughly and pass it
