## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- Raspberries
- Currants
- 1 kilo such juice with 1 1/4 kilo powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Strawberries, raspberries, currants are quickly passed through a sieve and to 1 kilo such juice 1 1/4 kilo powdered sugar is given
2. This is immediately placed on the fire and cooked very quickly under constant stirring for approximately 10-15 minutes
3. Then, still hot like the apricot jam, filled into jars
