## Ingredients

- ⅓ kg round potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 3 anchovies
- ⅛ liter cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook, peel and cool the potatoes, slice them
2. Cut the hard-boiled eggs and cleaned anchovies into pieces
3. Layer alternately potatoes, eggs and anchovies in a buttered porcelain saucepan until full
4. Drip hot butter on top
5. Beat the cream with 2 eggs and pour over
6. Bake for 20 minutes
7. Serve in the same saucepan
8. Can also be served as a fasting dish
