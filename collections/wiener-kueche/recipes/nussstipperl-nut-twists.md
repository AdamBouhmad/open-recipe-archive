## Ingredients

- 20 pieces shelled nuts
- sugar
- 20 pieces pounded hazelnuts or almonds
- 1 tablespoon breadcrumbs
- 1/8 liter boiling milk or cream
- grated coffee or finely cut lemon peel as desired

## Directions

1. Prepare as Mohnstipperl but use nut filling instead of poppy seed
2. Pound shelled nuts with sugar
3. Mix with pounded hazelnuts or almonds and breadcrumbs
4. Pour 1/8 liter boiling milk or cream over mixture
5. Sweeten to taste, mix well and set aside cold
6. Optionally add grated coffee or finely cut lemon peel
