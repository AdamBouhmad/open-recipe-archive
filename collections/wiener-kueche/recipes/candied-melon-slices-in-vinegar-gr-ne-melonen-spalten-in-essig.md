## Ingredients

- 1 melon
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/4 liter vinegar
- 60 Deca sugar
- 3 pieces cloves
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- 1 small piece vanilla

## Directions

1. Remove skin and seeds from the melon, cut the remainder into arbitrary pieces
2. Cook the pieces in boiling lemon water and then dry on cloth
3. Meanwhile, prepare 1/2 liter water, 1/4 liter vinegar, 60 Deca sugar, 3 pieces cloves, some cinnamon, nutmeg, and 1 small piece of vanilla, and let it boil
4. Add the melon pieces and let them cook for 5-8 minutes
5. Let them cool covered in the liquid, then fill the melon pieces into jars
6. Pour the juice over them so they are covered, bind them and cook like the Muscatel pears (No. 1842) for 3/4 hours in steam
