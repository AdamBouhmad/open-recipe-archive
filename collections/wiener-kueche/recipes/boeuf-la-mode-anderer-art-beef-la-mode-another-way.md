## Ingredients

- beef brisket, approximately 4 kilos
- bacon
- tongue
- anchovy fillets
- bacon fat
- onion
- root vegetables
- spices
- 1 bay leaf
- broth

## Directions

1. Pound and salt a beef brisket weighing about 4 kilos
2. Using a small knife, make diagonal slits and insert half finger-length pieces of bacon, then tongue and anchovy fillets alternating with these
3. Line the bottom of a casserole with bacon fat, onion, root vegetables, spices, and 1 bay leaf
4. Place the meat on top
5. Braise covered for 1 hour, adding broth occasionally
6. Continue braising as the liquid reduces
