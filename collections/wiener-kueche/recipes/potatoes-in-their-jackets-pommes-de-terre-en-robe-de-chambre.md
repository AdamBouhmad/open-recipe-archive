## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Cold water
- Caraway seeds
- Salt
- Fresh butter
- Anchovy butter or goose fat

## Directions

1. Put the thoroughly washed potatoes in a pot, pour on only enough cold water to cover them, add some caraway seeds and salt, and cook them covered gently, which takes about 3/4 hour from when they begin to boil.
2. Then place them on a neatly folded napkin and serve with fresh butter. The potatoes can also be roasted under glowing ashes in pans or in the oven. If putting them in the oven, wash them first and then rub them with salt and caraway seeds.
3. One must always observe the moment when they become soft, as they lose much in flavor through too long cooking. Serve fresh butter, anchovy butter, or goose fat with them.
