## Ingredients

- Small birds
- Salt
- Lard or butter
- Onion
- Butter
- Paper
- Breadcrumbs
- Onion, roasted
- Purée

## Directions

1. After they are plucked, flamed and cleaned, they are dried with a cloth and salted. Put finely sliced lard or butter in a shallow casserole, cut a piece of onion very finely and add it, then place the birds breast-side down, cover them with buttered paper and stew for 1/2 hour. 20 minutes before serving, place them on strong heat and let them brown quickly, turn the birds around, dust the breasts with breadcrumbs, baste them with their own juices and arrange them raised on a dish with golden roasted onion. Serve with any purée, especially potato purée or also sauerkraut and similar.
