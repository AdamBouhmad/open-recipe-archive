## Ingredients

- Hop salad
- Salt water
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the hop salad thoroughly and rinse
2. Boil it in salted water
3. Place it in a pot with a piece of butter, salt, pepper and a little vinegar
4. Cook it until tender
5. Before serving, fricassee it with a couple of egg yolks
