## Ingredients

- 7 dkg flour
- 7 dkg sugar
- 7 dkg grated hazelnuts
- 7 dkg fine breadcrumbs
- 14 dkg diced butter

## Directions

1. Quickly make a dough from the flour, sugar, grated hazelnuts, fine breadcrumbs and diced butter
2. Let it rest
3. Roll it out in portions
4. Cut with a round cutter
5. Place the tartlets on an unbuttered baking sheet
6. Bake at higher heat until light brown
7. If the dough has not rested well, the tartlets will usually come out of shape; then cut them again with a smaller cutter while still hot
8. Brush them on the flat side with jam
9. Press them together
10. Glaze them white, red or brown
11. Decorate them with candied fruit
