## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters flour
- 1/2 liter milk
- 8 Deca melted butter
- Necessary spice
- Egg whites from 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Put 1 1/2 liters flour into a deep bowl.
2. Pour 1/2 liter of milk gradually while stirring constantly.
3. Mix with 8 Deca melted butter and necessary spice.
4. Lightly fold in the whipped egg white from 4 eggs.
