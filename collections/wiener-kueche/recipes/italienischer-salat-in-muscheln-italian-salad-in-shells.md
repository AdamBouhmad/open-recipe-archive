## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg Kipfler potatoes
- 1 apple
- 10 dag root vegetables
- 1 milk herring
- 2 anchovies
- 10 dag lamb's lettuce
- 1 pickled gherkin
- 1 red beet
- 2 dag capers
- 3 egg yolks for mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the root vegetables, cut into small dice, place in cold salt water and cook 30-45 minutes, then drain.
2. Cut the cooked cold potatoes into dice, as well as the apple and gherkin.
3. Clean the herring, remove the bones and innards, wash it. Cut one half into larger pieces and the other half and the cleaned anchovy into small dice.
4. Mix everything with a cold mayonnaise made from 3 egg yolks (see Mayonnaise Salad: Menu 14), fill into shells, decorate each one with prepared lamb's lettuce or head lettuce, red beets, hard-boiled egg slices, capers, and anchovy strips.
5. Serve the shells on a cup or glass plate with a folded napkin.
6. Alternatively, season the diced mixture without mayonnaise with vinegar, oil, and mustard, fill into shells, spread warm or cold stirred mayonnaise over it and decorate as above.
7. The herring roe can be mixed in or used as decoration.
