## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- flour
- butter and sweet milk or cream
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Make dough with butter, eggs, and flour by working together
2. Set a flat pan or casserole with butter and sweet milk or cream on the heat
3. When this simmers, drop the dumplings in with a spoon
4. Apply heat above and below
5. Let the milk reduce until they become beautifully brown
6. Remove and place on a platter to serve
7. Sugar may be added to the milk if desired
