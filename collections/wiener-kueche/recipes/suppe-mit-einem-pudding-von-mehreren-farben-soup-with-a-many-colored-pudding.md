## Ingredients

- 1 bread roll, crust removed
- milk
- 1/4 pound butter or good beef lard
- 6 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- marrow, finely diced
- crab butter
- spinach juice, clarified
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak a bread roll with crust removed in milk
2. Beat 1/4 pound butter or good beef lard until fluffy
3. Add the well-squeezed bread roll
4. Beat in 6 eggs and 2 egg yolks, one after another
5. Add finely diced marrow
6. Make two types of mixture: one red from crab butter and one green from clarified spinach juice
7. Cut each type into large cubes and add to the mixture
8. Stir gently in so as not to break apart the cubes
9. Dust with as much flour as can be held between five fingers
10. Coat a cloth with butter
11. Place the mixture on it
12. Tie tightly with string so it cannot rise
13. Place in a pot of simmering, slightly salted water
14. Let it cook for a whole hour
15. Remove and untie the cloth
16. Place the pudding in a serving dish
17. Pour meat broth or consommé over it
18. Serve at table
