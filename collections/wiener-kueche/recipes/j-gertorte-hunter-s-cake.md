## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 10 dkg butter
- 5 dkg sugar
- 3 dkg grated hazelnuts
- jam
- schlagobers (whipped cream)

## Directions

1. Make sponge cake from egg yolks
2. Cut in half
3. Fill with jam or following filling
4. Coat top and sides with ½ schlagobers and sprinkle with chopped pistachios
5. For filling: Beat 10 dkg butter, mix with 5 dkg sugar and 3 dkg grated hazelnuts
