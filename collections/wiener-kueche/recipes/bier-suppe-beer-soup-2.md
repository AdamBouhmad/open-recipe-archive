## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons cold egg
- 1 liter beer
- 12 deca sugar
- some cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir 6 egg yolks and 6 tablespoons cold egg together well
2. Let 1 liter beer, 12 deca sugar and some cinnamon cook well and beat it into the egg yolks
