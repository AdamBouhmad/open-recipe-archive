## Ingredients

- Eel
- Flour
- Beaten eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the eel and clean it thoroughly
2. Cut lengthwise in 3 places and salt it
3. Dip in flour, dunk in beaten eggs, and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
4. Fry golden on both sides
5. Arrange in a bowl and serve at table
