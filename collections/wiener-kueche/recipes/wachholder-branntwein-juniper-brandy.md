## Ingredients

- Juniper berries
- Purified brandy in proportion to the berries

## Directions

1. Crush a desired quantity of juniper berries without crushing the inner kernels, which give a harsh and unpleasant taste
2. Pour purified brandy over them in proportion
3. Allow it to stand and digest in the sun or at the oven
