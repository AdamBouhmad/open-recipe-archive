## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Oil
- Mixed spices
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the artichokes clean and cut off the tips
2. Cook them until soft
3. Remove the rough and fibrous parts
4. Arrange them on an oiled grate, drizzled with fine oil and sprinkled with mixed spices
5. Roast them slowly
6. Place them on a serving dish
7. Dress with vinegar and oil and serve cold at table
