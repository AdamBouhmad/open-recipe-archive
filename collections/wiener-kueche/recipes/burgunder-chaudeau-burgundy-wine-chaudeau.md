## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter Burgundy wine
- 24 deca sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- Small piece of cinnamon
- 1 clove

## Directions

1. Bring 1/2 liter Burgundy wine to a boil with 24 deca sugar, 12 egg yolks, and if desired, a small piece of cinnamon and 1 clove
2. Treat further as described in the previous chaudeau recipe
