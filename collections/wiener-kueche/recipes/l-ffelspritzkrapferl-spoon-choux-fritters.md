## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter milk
- Lard for frying
- Powdered sugar for serving
- Warm marmalade for serving

## Directions

1. Prepare a choux pastry from 1/4 liter milk, place it on a board moistened with water and using an inverted spoon handle dipped in hot lard, drop pieces of any desired size into the hot lard, which must be stirred.
2. The pieces fall to the bottom at first but rise again after a few moments.
3. When the surface of the lard is fairly covered, stop adding the dough pieces as they will puff up twice their size during frying.
4. Move the pan gently during frying and turn the fritters with a skimming spoon so that they get an even color.
5. When they have a brown, crispy crust, remove them with a skimming spoon, place on paper towels, arrange in a dish, sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and serve with warm marmalade.
