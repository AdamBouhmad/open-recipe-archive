## Ingredients

- approximately 20 sprigs fresh woodruff
- 30 deciliters sugar in pieces
- 1 peeled orange cut in half
- 1 liter good white wine

## Directions

1. In a porcelain dish, add approximately 20 sprigs freshly picked woodruff, 30 deciliters sugar broken into pieces, 1 peeled orange cut in halves and 1 liter good white wine
2. Let it stand covered 1/2 hour
3. Strain and serve immediately
4. Shortly before serving, 1/4 liter ice-cold water can be added
