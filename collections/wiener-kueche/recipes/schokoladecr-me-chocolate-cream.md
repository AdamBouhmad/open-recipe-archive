## Ingredients

- 14 dkg cube sugar
- 1/4 liter water
- 12 dkg chocolate
- 1 dkg white gelatin
- 1/2 liter whipped cream

## Directions

1. In a small pot, spin 14 dkg cube sugar with 1/4 liter water
2. Soften 12 dkg chocolate in a double boiler and gradually mix with the spun sugar, stirring until smooth, then set aside to cool
3. Dissolve the gelatin and whip the cream
4. Fold the chocolate mixture into the cream, then slowly pour the melted gelatin through a tea sieve while stirring continuously
5. Fill the mixture into a pudding mold rinsed with water
6. Bury in ice for 2-3 hours until firm, then loosen, hold briefly in hot water, turn out onto a glass dish, and optionally decorate with 1/2 liter whipped cream
7. In winter, it is sufficient to place the cream in cold water
