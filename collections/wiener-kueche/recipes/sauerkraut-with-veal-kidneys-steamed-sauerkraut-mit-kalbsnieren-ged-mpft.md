## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Roasted veal kidneys
- Butter
- Sour cream (Schmette)

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauerkraut as you ordinarily serve it at table.
2. Meanwhile, chop the roasted veal kidneys finely, brush a dish with butter, line it thinly with the chopped kidneys, place a layer of sauerkraut over it, then kidneys again, and continue alternating; however, the last layer must be sauerkraut.
3. Pour sour cream over this, cover the dish, set it over a gentle charcoal fire, and let the sauerkraut warm for a few minutes, after which it can be served to table.
