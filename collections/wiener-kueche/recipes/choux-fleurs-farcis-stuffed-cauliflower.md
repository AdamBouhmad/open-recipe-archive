## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- Butter
- Veal forcemeat
- Breadcrumbs and butter, or butter sauce, or any desired piquant sauce

## Directions

1. After peeling and soft-cooking the cauliflower in salt water, divide it into small rosettes and place on a sieve
2. Butter a charlotte mold well and place the dried cauliflower rosettes so they lie on the bottom and side walls of the mold
3. Fill the empty space in the center of the mold with veal forcemeat
4. Cover the mold and cook in steam for 1 hour
5. Turn the mold out onto a platter, pour roasted breadcrumbs in butter, or butter sauce, or any desired piquant sauce over the cauliflower
