## Ingredients

- 30 deca beaten butter
- 6 deca vanilla sugar
- 1 knife point salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- 2 deca pressed yeast dissolved in 1/8 liter lukewarm milk
- 48 deca flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter gradually with vanilla sugar, salt, egg yolks, and whole eggs
2. Add the yeast dissolved in milk and flour, mix well
3. Beat the dough until it lifts from the spoon
4. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled
5. Place dough on a flour-dusted board, roll out to the thickness of a small finger
6. Cut out with an oblong pastry cutter
7. Place half of the obtained pieces on a butter-greased baking sheet at a distance of 2 finger widths apart
8. Brush with melted butter, sprinkle with vanilla or almond sugar, cover each piece with a lid
9. Cover with cloth and place in a warm spot
10. Once the dough has risen somewhat, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with white pearl sugar, and bake slowly in the oven
11. When the dough is pale yellow, loosen from the baking sheet with a very thin knife and arrange on a platter
