## Ingredients

- Fresh oysters
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Open the oysters and remove the top shell
2. Rinse the shells clean so the water runs out
3. Place them on the grate with the bottom shell down
4. Season with pepper and lemon juice
5. Add a little sugar
6. Roast them and bring quickly to the table
