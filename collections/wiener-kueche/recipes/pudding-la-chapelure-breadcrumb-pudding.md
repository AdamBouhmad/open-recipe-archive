## Ingredients

- 2 pounds milk bread crusts or fine roll crusts
- 1 pound butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 measure cream
- 10 eggs
- 1/2 pound crushed sugar
- 15 loths sweet almonds
- 1 loth bitter almonds
- zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Dry 2 pounds of milk bread or fine roll crusts in an oven, crush them finely, and sieve them through a hair sieve.
2. Beat 1 pound butter and 8 eggs into a foam.
3. Add to this pudding: 1 measure cream and 10 eggs; 1/2 pound crushed sugar, 15 loths sweet almonds combined with 1 loth bitter almonds, and the zest of 1 lemon.
4. Crush the bitter almonds with a couple of eggs, alternately adding a handful of fine bread and 1 spoon cream to the butter beaten to a foam.
5. Stir the sugar together with the sweet almonds and gradually add a spoonful at a time, add the remaining ingredients similarly gradually, and let it cook for 2 hours.
6. Serve with a wine foam when serving.
