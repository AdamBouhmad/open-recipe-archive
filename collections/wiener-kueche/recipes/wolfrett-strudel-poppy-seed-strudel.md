## Ingredients

- ½ liter flour
- 12 deca butter
- 16 deca vanilla sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 48 deca passed poppy seeds
- ⅓ teaspoon salt
- ¼ liter milk cream
- 6 egg whites
- raisins
- Canary milk (as described in Cream Strudel recipe)

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough from ½ liter flour and let it rest for ¾ hour
2. Mix 12 deca butter with 16 deca vanilla sugar
3. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks
4. Blend in 48 deca passed poppy seeds, ⅓ teaspoon salt, and ¼ liter milk cream gradually
5. Lightly fold in the snow (whipped egg whites) from 6 egg whites last
6. Stretch the rested strudel dough finely as described in the Young Strudel recipe
7. Spread with the poppy seed filling
8. Sprinkle with raisins
9. Roll up lightly and treat as described in the Baked Ham Strudel recipe
10. Cut into desired pieces, arrange on a serving dish, and serve with Canary milk as described in the Cream Strudel recipe
