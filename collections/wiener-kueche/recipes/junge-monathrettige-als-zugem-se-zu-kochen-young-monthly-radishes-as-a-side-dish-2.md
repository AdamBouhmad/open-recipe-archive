## Ingredients

- small, young monthly radishes
- sugar
- fat or lard
- meat or brown soup broth
- flour

## Directions

1. Take small, uniform young radishes, scrape and wash them clean.
2. Put a piece of sugar with fat or lard on heat and let it become chestnut brown.
3. Add the radishes, which must have been blanched beforehand, let them braise in it until soft, dust with a little flour, add some meat or brown soup broth, let it cook down completely, and serve to the table.
