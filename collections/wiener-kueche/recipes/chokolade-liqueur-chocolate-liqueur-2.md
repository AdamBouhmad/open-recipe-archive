## Ingredients

- 1 pound cacao beans
- 1 loth cinnamon
- 1 quentchen vanilla (optional)
- 2 measures strong brandy
- half pound roasted cacao beans
- cinnamon (same amount as before)
- vanilla (same amount as before)
- quarter quentchen cloves
- 2 measures brandy (for second infusion)
- 2 pounds sugar
- 2 measures water

## Directions

1. Crush 1 pound cacao beans and 1 loth cinnamon, and if desired, add 1 quentchen vanilla
2. Pour 2 measures strong brandy over the mixture
3. Let it stand for several days, shaking it meanwhile
4. Distill off 1 measure through distillation
5. Take half pound roasted cacao beans and the same amount of cinnamon and vanilla as before, plus quarter quentchen cloves
6. Combine with 2 measures brandy and add to the remaining distilled spirit
7. Let it stand for 8 to 10 days in gentle warmth
8. Filter it and mix with the distilled spirit
9. Dissolve 2 pounds sugar in 2 measures water and add to the mixture
10. After the mixture has stood for several weeks, filter again
