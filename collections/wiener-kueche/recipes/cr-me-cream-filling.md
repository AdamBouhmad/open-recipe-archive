## Ingredients

- milk
- 2-3 tablespoons flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 piece vanilla
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. In a snow basin, mix some milk with flour until smooth.
2. Add the remaining milk, egg yolks, vanilla and sugar to taste.
3. Beat thick over steam and cool before using.
4. If the cream is not thick enough, mix some milk and flour in a small pot and pour into the cream.
5. You can also fold in 1-2 egg whites or 1/2 l whipped cream into the cold cream.
