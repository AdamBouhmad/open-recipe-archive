## Ingredients

- Rice prepared as for ordinary rice soufflé
- Fresh butter
- Thin roll slices
- One measure Burgundy wine
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Several onions

## Directions

1. Coat the form with fresh butter and line it neatly with thin roll slices which are pressed firmly.
2. Place the rice prepared as for ordinary rice soufflé into it, cover the top again with thin roll slices which are lightly brushed with butter, and bake for 2 hours.
3. Cook one measure Burgundy with sugar, cinnamon, lemon peel and several onions, well covered, until reduced to half.
4. Strain through a hair sieve and serve in a sauce boat with the soufflé.
