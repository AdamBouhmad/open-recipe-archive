## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Speck (bacon)
- Semmel (bread roll) or liver forcemeat
- Hot fat
- Salad

## Directions

1. Make an incision in the skin between the breast and thighs down to the thigh bones and turn the wings inward against the back so that the skin holds them in place.
2. Place the pigeons in a pan, pour hot fat over them, and roast in the oven for approximately 1/4 hour, basting frequently.
3. Remove the pigeons from the oven, cut the string, remove the stuffing, and cut each pigeon lengthwise down the middle.
4. Arrange on a platter and serve with salad of your choice.
5. The pigeons can be filled with liver forcemeat instead of bread forcemeat.
