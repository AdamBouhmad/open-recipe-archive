## Ingredients

- 15 dkg flour
- 10 dkg butter
- a pinch of salt
- 1-2 spoonfuls cream
- egg yolk for brushing
- coarse salt or caraway seeds

## Directions

1. On the board rub 15 dkg flour with 10 dkg butter
2. Knead with a pinch of salt and 1-2 spoonfuls cream into dough
3. Form into finger-thick long rolls
4. Place on buttered, floured baking sheet
5. Brush with egg yolk
6. Sprinkle with coarse salt or caraway seeds
7. Bake until light brown using sharper heat
