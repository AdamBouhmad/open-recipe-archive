## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons butter
- 8 tablespoons flour
- Stock
- Wine vinegar
- 1/4 liter cream
- 6 tablespoons whole capers

## Directions

1. Make a brown butter sauce from 8 tablespoons butter
2. Pour it with some stock and wine vinegar and let cook
3. Add 1/4 liter cream and strain the sauce over 6 tablespoons whole capers
4. Let boil once more
