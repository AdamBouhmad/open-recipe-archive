## Ingredients

- 60 spoonfuls of cleaned rice
- 1/8 liter white beef broth
- 8 spoonfuls grated Parmesan cheese
- fried marrow bones or beef liver, or other fried or steamed meat
- chicken combs or poultry scraps
- steamed truffles (optional)
- strong brown broth
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Roast the cleaned rice for about 4 minutes under frequent stirring without letting it brown
2. Add 1/8 liter white beef broth and let it steam covered for 15 minutes
3. If the broth evaporates, add about 1.5 liters of broth and a little butter, and let it steam for another 10 minutes
4. Add 8 spoonfuls of grated Parmesan cheese and stir well
5. Using a sachet spoon, place a portion of risotto on a serving dish and cover it with pieces of fried marrow bones, beef liver, or other fried or steamed meat, chicken combs, or poultry scraps, and steamed truffles if desired
6. Cover this with the remaining risotto, then another layer of meat, then risotto again
7. Press everything together with 2 sachet spoons so it forms a ball on the dish
8. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired
9. Serve with strong brown broth and grated Parmesan cheese
