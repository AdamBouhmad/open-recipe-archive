## Ingredients

- fat
- onion
- root vegetables
- whole spices
- 2 cleaned, plucked, and dressed chickens
- wine
- soup stock
- bacon strips or buttered paper
- tarragon leaves
- 3 egg yolks
- cold wine

## Directions

1. Place fat, onion, root vegetables, and whole spices in a casserole and roast briefly
2. Add 2 cleaned, plucked, and dressed chickens
3. Pour wine and soup stock over
4. Cover with bacon strips or buttered paper
5. Braise slowly until tender
6. Remove chickens, cut each into 4 pieces, but reassemble them
7. Arrange in a serving dish, scatter finely chopped tarragon leaves over, and pour the strained sauce over
8. Optionally, whisk 3 egg yolks with a little cold wine and blend well with the strained sauce, pour some over the chickens and serve the rest in a gravy boat
