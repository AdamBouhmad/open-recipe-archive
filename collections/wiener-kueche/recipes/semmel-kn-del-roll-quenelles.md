## Ingredients

- 12 Loffel butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 20 Loffel finely diced rolls mixed with butter milk
- roasted parsley in butter
- salt
- 3 tablespoons flour
- approximately 3 tablespoons roll crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 12 Loffel butter until fluffy
2. Stir in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
3. Mix 20 Loffel finely diced rolls with butter milk, add roasted parsley in butter
4. Add salt, 3 tablespoons flour and approximately 3 tablespoons roll crumbs and stir well together
5. Form knödel of desired size
