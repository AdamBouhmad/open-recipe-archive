## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 deca vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 liter cream, 6 egg yolks, and 12 deca vanilla sugar in a snow kettle
2. Beat over heat with a whisk until the mass begins to thicken
