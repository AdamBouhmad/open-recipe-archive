## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 16 Deca finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites until stiff.
2. Lightly fold in 16 Deca finely sifted sugar.
3. Fill this mixture into a pastry bag and pipe equal-sized mounds onto a baking sheet coated with unsalted bacon fat.
4. Sprinkle with finely sifted sugar and dry in a cool, open oven.
5. Alternatively, the mixture can be dropped with a spoon to create rose-shaped mounds.
