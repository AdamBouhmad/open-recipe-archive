## Ingredients

- 16 deca roasted or boiled peeled chestnuts
- 5 pieces peeled, crushed bitter almonds
- 1 liter boiling cream
- 40 deca fine sieved sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter beaten cream

## Directions

1. Crush 16 deca roasted or boiled peeled chestnuts with a wooden spatula on a board
2. Add with 5 pieces peeled, crushed bitter almonds to 1 liter boiling cream, simmer for a few minutes and cool
3. In a saucepan beat 40 deca fine sieved sugar with 8 egg yolks
4. Gradually add the cream with the chestnuts, place the saucepan over moderate heat and beat the mixture with the snow whisk until it begins to thicken, but it must not boil
5. Pass through a sieve and beat until stiff
6. Fill the freezing can and proceed as with Vanilla Ice (Recipe 1720), finally work 3/4 liter beaten cream into it
