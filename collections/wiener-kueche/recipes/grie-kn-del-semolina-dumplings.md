## Ingredients

- 25 dkg semolina
- salt
- 5 dkg hot fat
- 1/2 l boiling water
- fat for roasting breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Salt the semolina and pour hot fat over it in a bowl.
2. Stir it, then pour boiling water over it.
3. Let it stand covered for 1 hour.
4. Form dumplings and place them in a pot of boiling salted water.
5. Let them cook slowly for 5-8 minutes.
6. Lift onto a round platter, pour over with breadcrumbs roasted in fat and serve as the Grießſtrudel.
