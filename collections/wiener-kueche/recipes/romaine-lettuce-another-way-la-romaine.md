## Ingredients

- Beautiful, large Romaine lettuce
- Salt water
- Sour salad dressing
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Divide beautiful, large Romaine lettuce into 4 parts, wash it, boil it in salt water, remove it, place on a sieve, and when the water has drained, place it on a plate and sprinkle with several spoonfuls of sour salad dressing mixed with its juice and toasted bread crumbs in butter.
