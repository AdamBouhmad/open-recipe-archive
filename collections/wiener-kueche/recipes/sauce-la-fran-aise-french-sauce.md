## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons fat
- 1/2 liter cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 tablespoons tarragon butter
- 1 piece crushed garlic
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. In a cassette dish, place 4 tablespoons fat and 1/2 liter cream and stir together into a smooth batter
2. Add 3 egg yolks, 8 tablespoons tarragon butter, 1 piece crushed garlic, and the juice of 1 lemon
3. Season and whisk over the fire with the whisk for as long as it takes to combine well (but it must not boil)
4. If the sauce is too thick, thin it with white stock and then serve with poultry, fish, or game
