## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo black ripe cherries, stems removed
- 1/4 kilo red currants
- 40 decagrams refined sugar
- 1 lemon peel
- 7 decagrams isinglass
- 1/2 liter of the strained juice

## Directions

1. Remove stems from 1/2 kilo ripe black cherries and 1/4 kilo red currants, crush them all, cover in an earthen vessel and let stand overnight.
2. Press the juice through cloth.
3. Add 40 decagrams of refined sugar heated with lemon peel cooked in it to 7 decagrams of hot dissolved isinglass.
4. Cook this together for 10 minutes, strain through fine, filtered linen cloth, add 1/2 liter of the pressed juice and proceed as with Orange Jelly (No. 1660).
