## Ingredients

- nine decagrams butter
- salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 decagrams 5 grams sugar
- lemon zest from one lemon
- 3 tablespoons diluted yeast
- flour
- milk
- chopped almonds
- raisins
- grated roll
- sugar and vanilla for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Rub nine decagrams of butter
2. Season with salt
3. Add 4 egg yolks, 4 decagrams 5 grams sugar, chopped lemon zest from one lemon, and 3 tablespoons diluted yeast
4. Add enough flour and milk to make a stiff dough
5. Let rise for half an hour
6. Mix in chopped almonds and raisins
7. Oil the babovka, sprinkle with grated roll, line with chopped almonds, add dough around
8. Oil it
9. When it has risen for a quarter hour, bake in oven
10. Turn out and sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
