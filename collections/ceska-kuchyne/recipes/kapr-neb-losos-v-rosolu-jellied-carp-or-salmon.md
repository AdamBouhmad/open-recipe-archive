## Ingredients

- 2 cups vinegar
- 2 cups white wine
- Water
- 1 large onion sliced into rounds
- 1 clove garlic
- 20 grains pepper
- 20 grains allspice
- Ginger
- 6 cloves
- 1½ sprigs thyme
- 1½ bay leaves
- Lemon peel
- Gelatine
- Salt
- 4 pounds fish
- 2 whole eggs with shells
- White muslin (for salmon)
- Lemon slices
- Capers
- Mayonnaise (for salmon)

## Directions

1. In a large stone pot, combine vinegar, white wine and enough water to make it pleasantly sour
2. Season appropriately with salt
3. Add sliced onion, garlic, pepper, allspice, ginger, cloves, thyme, bay leaves, lemon peel, and a little gelatine
4. Cover and simmer gently for half an hour, then strain into another pot
5. When it returns to a boil, add the cut fish (about 4 pounds) and cook 15-20 minutes
6. For salmon, wrap each piece in white muslin to keep the nice pink color
7. Remove fish and arrange neatly on a deep platter
8. Break 2 whole eggs with shells into the sauce, simmer a little and strain through a thick cloth or flannel bag into a clean pot
9. Pour this jelly over the prepared fish and let cool in a cold place
10. When serving, dip the mold in hot water and turn out onto a flat platter
11. Garnish around the fish with lemon slices, capers, etc.
12. Serve mayonnaise with salmon
