## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- water
- bread
- butter
- cooked rice
- salt
- paprika
- nutmeg
- thyme
- basil
- milk
- breadcrumbs or bread

## Directions

1. Rinse the crabs with fresh water and let them boil in boiling salted water for about fifteen minutes
2. Arrange them on a platter and serve with bread and butter
3. Alternatively, cut the boiled crabs, mix with half of cooked rice, salt, add a little paprika, nutmeg, thyme, basil, moisten with milk, fill the shells with this mixture
4. Sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs or bread, place a piece of butter on each shell, arrange on a baking sheet and let bake in the oven
