## Ingredients

- meat scraps
- onion
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- broth
- 2 egg yolks
- toasted bread roll

## Directions

1. Chop the meat scraps with onion and parsley
2. Braise in butter and flour
3. Add broth
4. Cook quickly
5. Mix with 2 egg yolks
6. Pour over toasted bread roll
