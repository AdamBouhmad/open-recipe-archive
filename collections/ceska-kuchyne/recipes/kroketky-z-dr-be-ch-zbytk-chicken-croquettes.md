## Ingredients

- 2 cups meat from poultry leftovers, finely chopped
- 1 spoon butter
- 2 spoons flour
- 2 cups hot milk
- salt
- white ground pepper
- a little paprika
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 spoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped onion
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot fat (half butter and half lard)

## Directions

1. Chop finely two cups of meat from poultry leftovers
2. Rub one spoon of butter with two spoons of flour, mix it into two cups of hot milk and cook with constant stirring until it thickens into a thick sauce, being careful it does not burn
3. Salt the chopped meat, pepper it with white ground pepper and a little paprika, add a little grated nutmeg, one spoon of finely chopped parsley and one teaspoon of finely chopped onion
4. Mix everything well into the meat, add the sauce to the meat and mix well again and let cool
5. When cooled, form small patties smaller than the palm of the hand, dip them in egg, coat in bread crumbs and fry in hot fat—best is half butter and half lard
