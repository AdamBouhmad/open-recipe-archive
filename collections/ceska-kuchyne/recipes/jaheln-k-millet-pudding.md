## Ingredients

- Millet
- Milk
- Sugar
- Lemon rind, finely chopped
- Eggs
- Butter
- Sour cream
- Apples (optional)
- Dried prunes, cooked and pitted (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook millet in milk according to the previous method
2. Sweeten to taste and season with finely chopped lemon rind
3. Place in a buttered mold or form
4. Pour over the top a mixture of beaten egg with a piece of butter and some sour cream
5. Bake in an oven for three quarters of an hour
6. You may also add chopped apples or cooked and pitted dried plums to the millet
