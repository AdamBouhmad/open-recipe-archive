## Ingredients

- 3 liters water
- Salt
- Vegetables
- A little flower (kvetu)
- Pepper
- Half a liter of barley groats
- A piece of butter
- Fresh pork cracklings (škvarky)

## Directions

1. Salt 3 liters of water, add vegetables, a little flower, and pepper
2. When cooked, select and rinse half a liter of groats
3. Add a piece of butter and cook
4. Strain
5. If fresh pork cracklings are available, add a handful
6. Pour the soup over the groats, cook briefly and serve
