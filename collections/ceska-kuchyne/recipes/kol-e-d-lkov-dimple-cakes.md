## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram and 12 decagrams (1 pound and 6 lots) flour
- 15 decagrams (10 lots) butter
- 14 decagrams (about 9 lots) sugar
- 6 hard grated yolks
- raw eggs as needed for dough consistency
- raisin for filling
- chopped almonds for topping
- egg for brushing
- sugar and vanilla for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Sift flour on board, add butter, sugar, grated hard yolks, and raw eggs until dough does not stick to hands
2. Take a piece, form into ball, place on sheet
3. Make small indentation and place raisin in it
4. Brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with chopped almonds
5. Bake slowly in oven and sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
