## Ingredients

- a dozen smaller ripe tomatoes or a 3-pound can of preserved tomatoes
- a piece of onion
- 1 quart of water
- a knife point of baking soda
- 2 cups of milk
- a small teaspoon of flour
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- a small teaspoon of sugar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a dozen smaller ripe tomatoes or a 3-pound can of preserved tomatoes, add a piece of onion and cook in a quart of water.
2. Let it cook for about half an hour and then strain through a sieve into another pot.
3. Add a knife point of baking soda, pour in 2 cups of milk in which a small teaspoon of flour has been dissolved, and cook again.
4. When it comes to a boil, add a tablespoon of butter, a small teaspoon of sugar, a little pepper and salt.
5. If it is too thick, thin it with milk or water.
