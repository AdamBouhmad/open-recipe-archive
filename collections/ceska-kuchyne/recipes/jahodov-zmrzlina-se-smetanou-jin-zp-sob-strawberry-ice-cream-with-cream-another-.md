## Ingredients

- One and one-half liters ripe strawberries
- More than one liter good cream
- Fine crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Rub one and one-half liters ripe strawberries through sieve
2. Add more than one liter good cream to the juice
3. Sweeten with fine crushed sugar
4. Transform into ice cream by the already described method
