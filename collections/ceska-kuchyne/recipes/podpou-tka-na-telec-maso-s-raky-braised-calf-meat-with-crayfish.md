## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms calf brisket
- 125 grams butter
- broth
- spices
- root vegetables
- crayfish
- salt
- 120 grams butter for crayfish butter
- bread
- cauliflower
- asparagus
- flour
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the brisket into pieces, blanch and bake in 125 grams of hot butter, then simmer slowly with broth, spices and root vegetables until tender
2. Boil crayfish in salted water, remove the meat, crush the shells with 120 grams butter to make crayfish butter
3. Fill cleaned crayfish shells with chopped meat or bread, cook in crayfish broth
4. Add cauliflower and asparagus cooked in salted water
5. Make a roux, simmer with broth, color with crayfish butter, add nutmeg and pour over the meat, vegetables and bread, garnish with stuffed crayfish shells
