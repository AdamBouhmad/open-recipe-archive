## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram of fine dried strawberries
- 375 grams of sugar
- water

## Directions

1. For 1/2 kilogram of dried strawberries, take 375 grams of sugar, boil it with water, place the strawberries in it, let them come to a boil once, remove them, boil the sugar until it pulls, place the strawberries back in it for 5 minutes, then carefully place in jars
2. Boil the sugar until completely thick and pour over the strawberries
3. When cooled, seal and store in a cool place
4. Alternatively, for 1/2 kilogram of strawberries, boil 375 grams of sugar until it pulls
5. When the sugar begins to turn red, shake the container, add the strawberries and let stand until the sugar begins to boil, shake the container again
6. Pour the liquid onto a plate, cover with a sieve and let cool
7. The next day, pour into a jar, seal, wrap in hay and place in water where it boils slowly for three-quarters of an hour
8. Then let cool and store the next day
