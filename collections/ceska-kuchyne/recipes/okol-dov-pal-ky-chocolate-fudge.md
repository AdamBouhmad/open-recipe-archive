## Ingredients

- 2 cups fine sugar
- 1/2 cup cream (or 1/2 cup milk with 1 tablespoon butter)
- 2 ounces chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- peanuts or coconut (optional)

## Directions

1. Put 2 cups fine sugar in a bowl
2. Add 1/2 cup cream, or if not available, 1/2 cup milk with 1 tablespoon butter
3. Add 2 ounces chocolate
4. Cook on high heat for about 10 minutes
5. When cooked, add 1 teaspoon vanilla and you can mix in shelled peanuts or coconut
6. When nicely thickened, pour onto a plate and let it harden for about an hour
7. Then cut into squares
