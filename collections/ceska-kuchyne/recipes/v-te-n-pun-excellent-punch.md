## Ingredients

- 2 liters water
- 1 kilogram sugar
- juice of 3 lemons
- 1 liter good rum
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 2 liters of water with 1 kilogram of sugar several times
2. Skim off the foam
3. Add the juice of 3 lemons
4. Add 1 liter of good rum and a little sugar
5. Mix everything together
6. Let it rest and serve at table
