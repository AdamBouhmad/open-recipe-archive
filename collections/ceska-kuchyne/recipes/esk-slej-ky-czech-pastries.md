## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter sifted flour
- 125 grams hard butter, torn into pieces
- 3/8 liter sour cream
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- 30 grams yeast
- 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Spread 3/4 liter sifted flour on a board, mix with 125 grams hard butter torn into pieces, 3/8 liter sour cream, 2 egg yolks, salt, 30 grams yeast and 2 tablespoons finely ground sugar
2. Mix thoroughly, work the dough and roll into long pastries
3. Brush with beaten egg and bake slowly on a greased sheet
