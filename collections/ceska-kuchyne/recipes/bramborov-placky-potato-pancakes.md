## Ingredients

- potatoes
- eggs
- flour
- salt
- lard
- fruit preserves

## Directions

1. Cook well-washed potatoes as many as will fit on a plate, peel them and let them cool completely
2. Grate them on a grater, add two whole eggs, two cupfuls of white flour, salt and knead well
3. Make pancakes about as thick as a straw, bake them on a pan greased with lard on both sides until golden
4. Brush them with hot lard, spread with fruit preserves and serve
