## Ingredients

- bread roll, cut into cubes
- milk
- egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- lemon peel rubbed
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 9 dekagrams raisins (6 lots)
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak bread roll cut into cubes in milk
2. Mix well with egg yolks, 2 tablespoons flour, salt, lemon peel, 3 tablespoons sugar, raisins and snow from 6 eggs
3. Make rolls from this mixture
