## Ingredients

- rutabaga
- weak meat broth
- lard
- sugar
- salt
- ground ginger
- pork meat

## Directions

1. Cut rutabaga into cubes and cook about an hour in weak meat broth
2. Place a heaping spoonful of lard and a teaspoon of sugar on a pan
3. Once the sugar dissolves, add the drained rutabaga, salt it, and braise until tender
4. Make a brown roux, mix with the drained broth, pour over the braised rutabaga, and season with ground ginger
5. Cook a piece of pork meat, cut into neat pieces, arrange rutabaga on a serving dish and top with the pork
