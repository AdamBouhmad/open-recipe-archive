## Ingredients

- two small cups of good lentils
- soft water
- salt
- lard
- onion, grated
- thyme
- vinegar
- water
- eggs fried in butter or sausages

## Directions

1. Sort and wash two small cups of good lentils, add soft water and let them cook for three-quarters of an hour, then drain the water and pour them into another pot and salt them.
2. Put a piece of lard on the stove and stew the grated onion in it until it froths.
3. Add it to the poured-out lentils, sprinkle a little thyme into it, make a brown roux with vinegar and water and pour it onto the lentils and let them cook until soft.
4. Then pour onto a platter and garnish with fried eggs or sausages.
