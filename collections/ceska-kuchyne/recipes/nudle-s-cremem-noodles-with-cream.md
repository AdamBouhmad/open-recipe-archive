## Ingredients

- ordinary noodles
- one liter cream
- 14 decagrams sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons flour
- vanilla
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make ordinary noodles, strain them, and pour cold water over them so they don't stick together
2. Mix one liter of cream, 14 decagrams of sugar, 10 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons of flour, and a little ground vanilla over heat until it thickens
3. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs
4. Pour noodles into it, mix with cream, bake until golden, and sprinkle with sugar
