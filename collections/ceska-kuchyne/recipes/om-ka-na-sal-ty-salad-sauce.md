## Ingredients

- hard-boiled egg yolks, grated and rubbed
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- mustard to taste

## Directions

1. Combine grated hard-boiled egg yolks with vinegar, oil, and salt
2. Add mustard to taste
3. Use to dress watercress or head lettuce
