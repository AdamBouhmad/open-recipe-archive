## Ingredients

- 1 ripe pineapple
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 1 bottle Burgundy wine
- 1 bottle champagne wine

## Directions

1. Cut a ripe pineapple into small slices
2. Place with 0.5 kilogram of sugar on a plate
3. Pour 1 bottle of Burgundy wine over it
4. Cover with cloth and let sit peacefully for at least an hour
5. Add 1 bottle of champagne wine
6. Stir constantly
7. Pour into long, narrow glasses
