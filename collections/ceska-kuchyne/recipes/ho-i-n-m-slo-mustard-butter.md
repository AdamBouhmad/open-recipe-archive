## Ingredients

- Half pound fresh butter
- Three hard-boiled egg yolks
- Three tablespoons ground mustard
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Beat half pound of fresh butter with three hard-boiled egg yolks and three tablespoons of ground mustard, salt it, add a little pepper and place in a cool place.
2. Instead of mustard, two tablespoons of paprika and juice from one lemon may be added.
