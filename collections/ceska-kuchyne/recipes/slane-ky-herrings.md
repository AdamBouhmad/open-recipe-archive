## Ingredients

- Herrings
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Red beets
- Hard-boiled egg whites
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Soak herrings for two days in cold water or milk
2. Split them and remove the backbone
3. Cut halves into pieces one inch long
4. Arrange two halves on an oblong flat dish so they appear uncut
5. Place the split head at one end and tail at the other
6. Drizzle with a little oil and vinegar
7. In the middle between the two herring halves, place a small heap of chopped hard-boiled egg yolk
8. Beside the yolk, between the herring halves, place a heap of chopped red beet
9. Add a heap of chopped hard-boiled egg white
10. Finally add a heap of chopped parsley
