## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- onion
- garlic
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes and slice into thin slices
2. Stew for quarter hour in butter with salt, pepper, chopped parsley and onion
3. Rub a plate with garlic
4. Place potatoes on it
5. Pour over a sauce made from 4 egg yolks and 3 tablespoons vinegar
