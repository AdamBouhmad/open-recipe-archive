## Ingredients

- venison from hindquarters
- butter
- egg yolks
- houska (bread roll)
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove membranes from venison from the hindquarters and cut into cutlets
2. Pound the cutlets
3. Salt them
4. Dip in butter mixed with 2 beaten egg yolks
5. Coat with grated houska
6. Fry on butter
7. Usually served with green beans or green peas
