## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked or roasted beef or veal meat, leftover from previous day
- butter
- piece of onion
- 1 quart good beef soup
- 1 to 1.5 bread rolls or 2 slices of white bread or 3 crackers
- 2 to 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. For 6 people, finely chop 1 cup of beef or veal meat, whether leftover roasted or cooked
2. Sauté it for about 15 minutes in butter into which you have sliced a piece of onion
3. Pound it in a mortar into a paste
4. In 1 quart of good beef soup, soften 1 to 1.5 bread rolls, or 2 slices of white bread, or 3 crackers
5. Add to this 2 to 3 hard-boiled, sifted egg yolks and the pounded meat
6. Mix everything well and pass through a sieve
7. Dilute as needed with beef soup and pour into a soup bowl with croutons made from toasted bread cut into cubes, or with a rolled accompaniment according to recipe 9
