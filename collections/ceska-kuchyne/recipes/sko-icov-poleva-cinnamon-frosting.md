## Ingredients

- white frosting base
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon extract or 1 teaspoon finely ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare frosting as previously mentioned
2. Fold in 1 teaspoon of cinnamon extract, or 1 teaspoon of finely ground cinnamon
