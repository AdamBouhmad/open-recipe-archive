## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Salt
- Caraway
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Brush the crayfish, rinse in several waters.
2. When removing the intestine, be careful not to let them fall apart.
3. Salt the water, add caraway and green parsley, and when boiling, throw in the crayfish for 5 minutes.
4. Remove and place on a platter.
