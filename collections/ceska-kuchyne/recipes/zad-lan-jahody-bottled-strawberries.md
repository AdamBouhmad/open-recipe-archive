## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar (equal part to strawberries)

## Directions

1. Boil an equal part of sugar with an equal part of strawberries
2. When at a full boil, add the cleaned strawberries
3. Boil everything several more times
4. Remove foam and put the preserves into containers
5. When cooled, tie with a bladder
