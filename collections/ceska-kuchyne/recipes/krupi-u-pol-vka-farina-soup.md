## Ingredients

- a handful of caraway seeds
- a little fresh parsley
- celery
- salt
- boiling water
- 0.5 cup of farina
- 1 to 2 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- a little nutmeg
- a little ginger
- sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Put a handful of caraway, a little fresh parsley and celery in a pot, salt it, pour boiling water over it and let it simmer briefly, then strain it.
2. When it begins to boil again, add half a cup of farina.
3. Stir often to avoid lumps; cook for a quarter hour.
4. Then beat 1 to 2 whole eggs, add a tablespoon of butter, a little nutmeg, a little ginger, and if desired some sweet sour cream, mix well and stir into the soup.
