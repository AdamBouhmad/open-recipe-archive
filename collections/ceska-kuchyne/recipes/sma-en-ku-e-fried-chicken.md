## Ingredients

- young chickens, one to one and a half pounds each
- salt
- flour
- egg
- fresh parsley
- breadcrumbs or bread
- crackers
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Use young chickens weighing about one to one and a half pounds each
2. Cut into quarters or joint pieces
3. Salt them
4. Coat in flour, dip in beaten egg mixed with finely chopped fresh parsley
5. Coat with breadcrumbs or bread, or if desired, crushed crackers
6. Fry until golden in hot butter or lard
7. For larger chickens, coat only in flour since larger chickens do not fry through well with breadcrumb coating
