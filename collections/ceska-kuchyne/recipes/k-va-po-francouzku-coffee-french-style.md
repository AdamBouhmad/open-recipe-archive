## Ingredients

- 60 grams of roasted coffee beans
- sugar
- milk
- 1/4 liter of boiling water

## Directions

1. For four larger cups, grind or crush sixty grams of roasted coffee beans.
2. Steep the coffee in a coffee machine with approximately 1/4 liter of boiling water.
3. Cover well so that no steam escapes, and let it stand on moderately warm stove for about two hours, without boiling again.
4. Remove from heat, let it settle, and serve with sugar and milk.
