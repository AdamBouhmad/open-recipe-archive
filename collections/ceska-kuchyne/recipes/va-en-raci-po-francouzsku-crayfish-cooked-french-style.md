## Ingredients

- cleaned crayfish
- water or wine with vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bay leaf
- thyme
- clove
- onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Remove the intestine from the tail of cleaned crayfish
2. Place crayfish in a container filled with water mixed with vinegar or wine
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Add butter, bay leaf, thyme, clove, and sliced onion
5. Cook vigorously for about 20 minutes
6. Remove from heat and serve the crayfish either warm or chilled
