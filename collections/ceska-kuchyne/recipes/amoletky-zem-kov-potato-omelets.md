## Ingredients

- quarter kilogram (half pound) cooked potatoes
- same amount white flour
- several eggs
- egg white
- salt
- butter
- crackling

## Directions

1. Mix cold grated cooked potatoes with equal amount of white flour, several eggs, egg white, salt and butter, working well.
2. Cut off pieces of dough, roll out like noodles, sprinkle with cracklings, roll up, place side by side on a pan, brush with lard and bake.
