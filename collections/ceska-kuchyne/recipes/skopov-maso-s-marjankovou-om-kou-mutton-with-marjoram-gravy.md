## Ingredients

- 3 pounds young mutton from the shoulder
- butter
- flour
- broth from cooking the meat
- garlic, rubbed with salt
- fresh parsley, finely chopped
- marjoram
- cloves
- small boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook three pounds of young mutton from the shoulder and cut it into neat pieces.
2. Make a light roux from one tablespoon of butter and one and a half tablespoons of flour, thin it with the broth in which the meat was cooked, and place the meat in it.
3. For each pound of meat, add a piece of garlic rubbed with salt, a little finely chopped fresh parsley, a little marjoram, and a pinch of cloves.
4. Peel small boiled potatoes, add them, let it simmer a little, then arrange the meat on a dish, surround with potatoes, and pour the gravy over it.
5. Alternatively, you can scrape raw potatoes, cut them into pieces, and when the meat is half-cooked, add them to the broth and cook them together with the meat.
