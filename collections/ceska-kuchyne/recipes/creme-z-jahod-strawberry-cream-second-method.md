## Ingredients

- strawberries
- 375 grams (25 lots) sugar
- red wine
- spoon of fine flour
- snow from 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Press strawberries through a sieve
2. Boil 375 grams sugar with red wine
3. Boil everything with a spoon of fine flour
4. When it has cooled a little, add snow from 6 egg yolks
