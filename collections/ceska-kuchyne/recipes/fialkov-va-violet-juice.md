## Ingredients

- violet petals
- boiling water
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the colored petals of violets carefully so that not even a small green part is mixed in, clean them and place in a stone pot.
2. Pour with boiling water (use a proportionate amount: if many petals, more water is needed, but neither too much nor too little).
3. Cover the container completely and place on warm stove for at least one day, then press out in fine cloth.
4. Mix the strained juice with sugar, add lemon juice and heat everything together slowly over a gentle fire, but it must not boil.
5. As soon as a white skin appears on top, remove the container from the fire, collect the skin and foam and pour the juice into heated bottles, and only when everything has cooled, wrap with bladders and store in dry, cool places.
6. This juice must not come into any metal containers as it would completely lose its delicate blue color.
