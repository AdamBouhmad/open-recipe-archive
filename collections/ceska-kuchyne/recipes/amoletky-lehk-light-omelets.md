## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons crushed sugar with vanilla or grated lemon peel
- flour (same amount as yolks)
- butter
- preserved jam
- cinnamon
- sugar
- cheese curds

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 6 tablespoons of sugar crushed with vanilla or grated lemon peel until foamy.
2. Add flour in amounts equal to the number of yolks, stirring for a quarter hour.
3. Grease an omelet pan with butter and pour a thin batter to the thickness of two fingers, baking over a gentle fire.
4. Turn out onto a plate and spread with preserved jam, roll up, and sprinkle with sugar and vanilla.
5. Alternatively, smaller omelets can be made and instead of jam, filled with sugar and cinnamon or cheese curds with raisins.
