## Ingredients

- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- nutmeg
- green parsley
- salt
- one grated French roll
- good sweet cream
- butter
- beef broth
- white beurre manié

## Directions

1. Break two whole eggs and two egg yolks into a cup, beat them
2. Add a little nutmeg, a little green parsley, a little salt and one grated French roll
3. Then add a little good sweet cream and mix everything together well
4. Then grease a small baking dish with butter, dust with grated French roll
5. Pour the mixture into it and let the colombine bake
6. When it cools, cut it into pieces, pour beef broth over it and let it cook a little
7. Then make a white beurre manié for the broth and serve it at table
