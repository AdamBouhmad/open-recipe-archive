## Ingredients

- Large, green, firm cucumbers without seeds
- Water
- Salt
- Dill, sliced
- Cherry leaves
- Horseradish, sliced
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Stone container

## Directions

1. Rinse the cucumbers and salt them for 12 hours, then dry them
2. Layer cucumbers with sliced dill, cherry leaves, sliced horseradish, salt and pepper in a stone pot
3. Pour vinegar to cover the cucumbers completely
4. After 12-14 days, pour off the vinegar, boil it, let it cool, and pour it back over the cucumbers
5. Cover with slate, weight with a stone, tie up the pot, and place in a cold location
6. For small cucumbers, clean with a brush, soak in salted water for 4 hours, dry, and layer similarly
7. After 3-4 days, pour off and boil the vinegar; repeat this process two more times
8. Some add paprika pods, fennel and other spices to the cucumbers
