## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salted water
- crayfish intestines
- beef bones, half kilo
- vegetables
- salt
- 1 liter and 4 deciliters water
- pale roux
- crayfish butter
- pepper
- cauliflower
- ginger
- roll cut into noodles

## Directions

1. Clean crayfish with a brush, remove intestines and throw into boiling salted water for 6 minutes
2. Remove meat from claws and tails, crush everything else in a mortar
3. Crush half kilo of beef bones, add all vegetables, salt and cook in 1 liter and 4 deciliters of water
4. When you have a good strong soup, strain it and add pale roux
5. Press crayfish butter through a sieve into the soup, add meat from claws and tails with pepper, cauliflower and ginger
6. Fry roll cut into noodles and pour soup over it
