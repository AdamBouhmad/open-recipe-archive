## Ingredients

- stewed raspberries
- sugar
- 12 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix stewed raspberries with ground sugar with snow from 12 egg whites so the mixture becomes fluffy
2. Grease the form, put the mixture into it, sprinkle with sugar, place it in hot water for a quarter hour and serve on the table with wine sauce
