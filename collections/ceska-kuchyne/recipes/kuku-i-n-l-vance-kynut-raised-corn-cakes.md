## Ingredients

- one cup corn flour
- hot water
- two small cups milk
- two tablespoons leaven from dry yeast, or half a piece of soaked pressed yeast
- one teaspoon yellow sugar
- two beaten eggs
- salt
- half teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. In the evening, pour one cup of corn flour with hot water, but not into a thin batter, but only so that it is just soaked with hot water
2. When it cools, add two small cups of milk and two tablespoons of leaven from dry yeast, or half a piece of soaked pressed yeast, one teaspoon of yellow sugar and let it rise until morning
3. In the morning, add two beaten eggs, salt, dissolve half a teaspoon of baking soda in hot water, mix and bake
