## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) semolina
- 4 1/4 liters milk
- 12 decagrams (6 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- 45 grams (3 lots) ground almonds
- salt
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook 24 decagrams semolina in 4 1/4 liters milk and turn out to cool
2. Beat 12 decagrams butter until fluffy, add 8 egg yolks, 9 decagrams sugar, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, 45 grams ground almonds and salt
3. Mix semolina with snow from 8 egg whites into greased mold and bake for 1½ hours
