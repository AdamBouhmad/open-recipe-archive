## Ingredients

- three rolls
- eggs
- sour cream
- grated bread
- chopped almonds
- four egg whites
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut three rolls into slices, dip in eggs beaten with sour cream, roll in grated bread, chopped almonds, and four beaten egg whites.
2. Bake on both sides in butter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm.
