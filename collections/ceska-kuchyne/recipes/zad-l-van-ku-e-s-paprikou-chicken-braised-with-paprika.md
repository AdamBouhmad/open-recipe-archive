## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- vegetables
- paprika
- salt

## Directions

1. Divide the chicken into halves or quarters, braise it with vegetables.
2. When it is tender, add to it the following sauce: Strain the pale broth that formed during braising, let it boil, strain it, place the meat in it, and boil a knife-point of paprika in the sauce.
