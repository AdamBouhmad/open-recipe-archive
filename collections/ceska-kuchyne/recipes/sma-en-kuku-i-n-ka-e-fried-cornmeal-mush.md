## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1 quart cornmeal
- cold water
- salt
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs or flour
- hot lard
- syrup

## Directions

1. Bring two quarts of water to a boil in a large pot or earthen bowl
2. Mix one quart of cornmeal in cold water to make a thin batter
3. Slowly pour this batter into the boiling water while stirring constantly
4. Salt it and let it cook loosely for two hours, stirring frequently to prevent burning
5. When the mush is cooked, pour it onto a shallow dish no thicker than two inches
6. The next day or when cooled, cut it into slices
7. Dip them in beaten egg and coat in bread crumbs or flour
8. Fry in hot lard
9. Serve with syrup
