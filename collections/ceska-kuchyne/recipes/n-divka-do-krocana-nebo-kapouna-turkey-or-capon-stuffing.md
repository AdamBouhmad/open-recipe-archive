## Ingredients

- 6 bread rolls
- milk
- 2 braised onions
- 1 kilogram chopped pork
- 0.5 kilogram veal
- 6 to 8 sardines
- lemon zest
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- truffles
- red wine

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from bread rolls and soak in milk
2. Squeeze out and mix on a baking sheet until they no longer stick
3. When cooled, mix with 2 braised chopped onions
4. Add 1 kilogram chopped pork and 0.5 kilogram veal
5. Add 6 to 8 sardines, grated lemon zest, pepper, pounded cloves, and nutmeg
6. Mix with 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
7. Pound everything in a mortar and press through a sieve
8. Add truffles cooked in red wine and cut into slices
9. Pour in a little red wine
10. For turkey or capon, use less meat
11. Alternatively, cook heart, stomach, and lungs of turkey with 0.5 kilogram pork or veal, add butter, 3 eggs, 6 grated bread rolls, fried mushrooms, garlic, salt, and nutmeg
12. Or chop 0.5 kilogram pork, 0.5 kilogram veal, 24 dekagrams bacon, 12 dekagrams sardines, 2 fried onions, several mushrooms, 2 bread rolls, 3 eggs, salt, nutmeg, grated Parmesan cheese, and several spoonfuls of wine, mix and press through a sieve
13. Finally add several cooked chopped chestnuts
