## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- flour
- salt
- butter
- water
- two egg whites
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots, cut lengthwise and cook as in the previous recipe
2. When cooked, dry with a cloth and coat each piece in batter prepared as follows: Take six spoons of flour, a little salt, one spoon of hot butter and water as needed to make the batter just thick enough for coating, but not too thin
3. Add two beaten egg whites to this
4. Dip the pieces in the batter, fry on both sides and serve with fried parsley
