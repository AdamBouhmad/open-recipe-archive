## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) rice
- cold water
- warm water
- milk
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 4 or 6 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams sugar
- vanilla
- 4 or 6 egg whites
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pick over 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) of rice, pour cold water on it, which is poured off again
2. Soak in warm water for a quarter hour, pour off the water
3. Place the rice on the stove with milk to make soft porridge
4. When the porridge is thick, let it cool
5. Rub 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter with 4 or 6 egg yolks, add 6 dekagrams of sugar, a piece of vanilla and snow from 4 or 6 egg whites, mix everything and put into a form greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs, then bake it in the oven
