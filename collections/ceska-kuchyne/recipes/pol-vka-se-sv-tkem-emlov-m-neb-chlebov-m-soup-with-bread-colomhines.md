## Ingredients

- bread roll or stale white bread
- sour cream
- two whole eggs
- one egg yolk
- ground cloves
- salt
- butter
- grated bread roll, bread or crackers
- good beef broth

## Directions

1. Cut bread roll or stale white bread into small cubes, pour a little sour cream over it to soften it.
2. Beat two whole eggs and one egg yolk in a cup, add a pinch of ground cloves, add the soaked bread, salt and mix everything well.
3. Butter a small pot, sprinkle with grated bread roll, bread or crackers, pour the mixture in and let it bake well until golden; then take it out on a board, let it cool and cut into small cubes.
4. Make a little white roux, strain good beef broth through it, let it simmer a little, and serve it this way.
5. Serve the colomhines separately on a small plate.
