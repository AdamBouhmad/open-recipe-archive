## Ingredients

- bread roll
- parsley
- milk
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Grate the bread roll
2. Finely chop the parsley
3. Fry the grated bread roll with the chopped parsley
4. Mix with milk and eggs
5. Season with salt
