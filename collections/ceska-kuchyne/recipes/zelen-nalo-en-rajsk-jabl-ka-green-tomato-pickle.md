## Ingredients

- 1 gallon tomatoes sliced into circles
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 quart sugar
- 1/4 cupful mustard seed
- 2 teaspoons whole allspice
- 1 teaspoon ground pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 12 small onions, sliced

## Directions

1. Cook all ingredients together for 10 minutes
2. Tomatoes must be completely green
3. Place in earthenware jars
4. Seal well so that air cannot reach them
