## Ingredients

- older partridges
- salt
- bacon slices
- onion
- bay leaf
- pepper
- juniper berries
- broth
- wine
- calf's feet
- cloves
- lemon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Salt the older partridges, wrap with bacon slices, and braise with onion, bay leaf, several peppercorns and juniper berries, several spoonfuls of broth, and a glass of wine.
2. For each partridge, take one calf's foot; cut them up and cook with onion, salt, pepper, several cloves, and lemon sliced into strips in water.
3. Strain; mix the juice from the partridges with the strained liquid, remove the fat, and acidify with vinegar.
4. Place the partridges in a pot and pour the warm juice from the feet over them.
5. Finally pour a little fat over to form a crust.
6. Thus preserved, the partridges will keep for a long time.
