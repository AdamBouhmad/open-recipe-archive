## Ingredients

- very ripe plums picked by hand
- crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- good wine vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take very ripe plums that were picked by hand, cut off the ends of the stems, place plums in a container
2. Sprinkle with crushed cinnamon and cloves
3. Pour good wine vinegar mixed with sugar, cook together and skim off the foam
4. When the vinegar has cooled, pour it over the plums and seal the container tightly
5. After eight days, pour off the vinegar again, cook it anew, skim off the foam, and when cooled, pour it over the plums and seal the container tightly
