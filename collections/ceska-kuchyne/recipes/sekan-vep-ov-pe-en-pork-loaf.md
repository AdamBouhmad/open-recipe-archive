## Ingredients

- three pounds pork
- 4 slices white bread soaked in water or milk, or 6 crackers
- half a grated onion
- a piece of finely chopped lemon peel
- three eggs
- a bit of white wine
- salt
- ground pepper
- allspice
- grated bread for topping

## Directions

1. Chop three pounds of pork
2. Add 4 slices of white bread soaked in water or milk, or 6 crackers
3. Add half a grated onion
4. Add a piece of finely chopped lemon peel
5. Add three eggs and a bit of white wine
6. Salt it
7. Season with ground pepper and allspice
8. Mix well with your hands
9. Form into an oblong loaf
10. Grease a baking pan with butter or lard
11. Place the meatloaf in it
12. Sprinkle with grated bread
13. Bake until nicely browned
14. Baste frequently during baking with melted lard or butter
15. Remove to a platter
16. Slice into equal pieces
17. Pour over either the pan drippings or a lemon sauce
