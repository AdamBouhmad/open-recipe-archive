## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of nice rice
- two or three coffee cups of hot water
- a piece of butter
- salt
- two cleaned spring chickens
- beef broth
- a small piece of nutmeg
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Pick over one coffee cup of nice rice and clean it thoroughly, put it in a pot and pour two or three coffee cups of hot water over it, add a piece of butter and salt and let it cook until the rice softens.
2. Take two cleaned spring chickens and let them cook; when they are cooked tender, remove them and let them cool, then cut them neatly and place them in a soup bowl.
3. Strain the broth in which the chickens were cooked through a fine sieve, skim all the fat from it, grate a small piece of nutmeg and add a little fresh parsley and pour it over the previously cooked rice; then put this in the soup bowl with the prepared chickens and bring to the table.
