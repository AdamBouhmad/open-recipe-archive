## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) peeled beaten almonds
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- lemon juice
- 1/4 kilogram (1/4 pound) cooked grated potatoes
- salt
- 9 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar with 9 egg yolks
2. Add 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of peeled beaten almonds, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, lemon juice, 1/4 kilogram (1/4 pound) of cooked grated potatoes, and salt
3. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 9 egg whites
4. Grease the mold and bake the mixture slowly for one hour
