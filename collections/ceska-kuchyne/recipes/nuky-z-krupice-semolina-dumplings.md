## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters (2 žejdlíků) of milk
- a piece of butter
- salt
- sugar
- semolina (amount as needed to make thick porridge)
- 7 1/2 deciliters (5 žejdlíků) of milk
- crushed nutmeg
- sugar
- 45 grams (5 lots) of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Put milk, butter, salt and sugar on the stove
2. When it begins to boil, mix in semolina to make a thick porridge
3. Cook and let cool
4. Mix second portion of milk with crushed nutmeg, sugar, butter and salt
5. When milk boils, dip spoon in milk and cut dumplings
6. Let them heat through and serve warm in milk
