## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter pearl barley
- hot water
- 1 liter milk
- butter
- 4 eggs
- sweet cream
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald the pearl barley in hot water to remove its distinctive taste.
2. Boil in 1 liter of milk with butter.
3. Pour onto a plate to cool.
4. Add 4 eggs, sweet cream, salt, and flour to form a thick dough.
5. Form dumplings and coat with flour.
6. Boil in salted water.
7. Serve with butter or preserve.
