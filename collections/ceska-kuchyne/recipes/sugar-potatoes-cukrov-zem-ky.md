## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram grated almonds
- 1/4 kilo sugar
- egg yolks as needed for stiff dough

## Directions

1. Mix grated almonds, sugar, and egg yolks to form stiff dough
2. Form into balls
3. Prick to resemble actual potatoes
4. Roll in ground coffee
5. Dry on baking sheet
