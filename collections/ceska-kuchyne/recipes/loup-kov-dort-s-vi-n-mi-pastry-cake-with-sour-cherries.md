## Ingredients

- layered pastry dough
- egg yolk
- pitted sour cherries or sweet cherries
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roll layered dough into thin sheets
2. Cut into any desired size and place on a sheet brushed with egg yolk
3. Cut small noodles from the rolled dough and border the cake with them
4. Stuff pitted cherries between the openings thus formed
5. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon
6. When baked, cut into oblong pieces
7. During baking, brush the noodles that form the strips on the cake with beaten egg yolk
