## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 24 dekagrams powdered sugar
- lemon oil
- 1/5 kilogram grated cooked potatoes
- 8 egg whites
- 1 liter cream
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 1/8 liter preserved raspberries
- 15 grams (1 lot) dissolved gelatin
- fresh raspberries

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel the almonds, dry them, crush them, and mix them with 8 egg yolks, 24 dekagrams of powdered sugar, lemon oil and 1/5 kilogram of grated cooked potatoes
2. Beat for 1.5 hours, slowly fold in snow from 8 egg whites
3. Bake two cakes of finger thickness
4. When cooled, place one cake in a soufflé mold, brush with raspberry juice and a mixture of 1 liter cream, 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar, 1/8 liter preserved raspberries and 15 grams (1 lot) dissolved gelatin
5. Place half the mixture on the bottom cake, brush the top with the other half and cover with the bottom cake
6. Place in a cold spot, decorate with fresh raspberries and a lattice of cream
