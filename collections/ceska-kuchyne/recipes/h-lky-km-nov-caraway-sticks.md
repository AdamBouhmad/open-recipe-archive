## Ingredients

- 250 grams heated and sifted flour
- 125 grams washed butter
- 16 grams yeast (dissolved in warm milk)
- salt
- 2 to 3 tablespoons finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Mix flour with butter, yeast dissolved in warm milk, salt, and sugar
2. Work everything thoroughly
3. Let rise
4. Roll into thin sticks about twelve centimeters long
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with caraway
7. Let rise again
8. Bake at moderate heat until light brown
