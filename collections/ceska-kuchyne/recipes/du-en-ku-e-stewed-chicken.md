## Ingredients

- cleaned and gutted chicken
- salt
- butter
- clove
- onion
- parsley
- carrot
- peppercorns
- bay leaf
- bacon
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the cleaned and gutted chicken, salt it, and lard it.
2. Place a piece of butter on the breast and put it in a pot just large enough to fit it.
3. Add to it sliced onion, parsley, and carrot, several peppercorns, a bay leaf, and a piece of bacon.
4. Pour beef broth or water over it so that the chicken is covered.
5. Cover first with clean white paper and then with a well-fitting lid.
6. Let the chicken stew until tender, turning it once.
7. Remove the chicken, cut it into pieces, quickly boil the liquid and reduce it on high heat until thick.
8. Skim the fat from it and pour it over the chicken.
9. Young chicken will stew in one and a half hours, older chicken in two hours.
