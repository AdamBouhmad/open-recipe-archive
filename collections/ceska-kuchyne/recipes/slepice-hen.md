## Ingredients

- 1 hen
- parsley
- cabbage
- celery
- salt
- 1-2 eggs
- cloves
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. For the hen to be tasty, it must be salted and marinated for at least 2 days before preparation; then cut it into at least 4 parts; add parsley, cabbage and celery to it; cook until completely tender.
2. Meanwhile, make thin noodles from one or two eggs, strain the broth when cooking, put the noodles in the broth, add cloves and cauliflower.
3. Young hen may also be roasted, but must be marinated in salt longer for that purpose.
