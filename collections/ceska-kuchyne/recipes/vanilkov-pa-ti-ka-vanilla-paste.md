## Ingredients

- vanilla
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Dry vanilla, finely powder it and cook in a little water
2. Strain through clean cloth
3. Add plenty of sugar and cook together until sugar forms a grain
4. Proceed as with the previous paste
