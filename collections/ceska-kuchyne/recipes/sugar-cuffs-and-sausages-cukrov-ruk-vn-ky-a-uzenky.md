## Ingredients

- 6 chocolate tablets
- 15 grams cinnamon
- 15 grams allspice
- 15 grams cloves
- ground sugar grated with lemon zest equal in weight to the chocolate
- flour as needed
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix chocolate, spices, and sugar together
2. Add flour as needed
3. Mix with 3 eggs to make a well-worked, stiff dough
4. For cuffs: shape into oblong pieces thickness of ordinary sausages, press indentation in center with fingers, form knots at both ends like sausages, place two halves parallel next to each other
5. For sausages: form short thick pieces, pierce hole through center from both sides to make small cuffs
6. Brush with beaten egg
7. Dry on waxed sheet
8. When done, may be decorated with small ribbons glued with icing on both sides of cuffs
9. These candies keep a long time
