## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons grated bread or white flour
- 3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablets grated chocolate
- lemon zest
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Gradually dissolve 3 tablespoons grated bread or white flour in 3 cups milk
2. Add 2 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons sugar
3. Keep stirring and let it cook, then allow to cool
4. Add 4 grated tablets of chocolate and a little lemon zest
5. Add 6 egg yolks and beat well
6. Fold in stiff peaks made from 6 egg whites
7. Put into a buttered mold lined with white flour
8. Bake slowly, then turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar
