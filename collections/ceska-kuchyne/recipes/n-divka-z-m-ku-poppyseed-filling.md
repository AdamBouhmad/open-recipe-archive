## Ingredients

- poppyseeds
- cream
- honey or sugar to taste
- ground clove
- lemon zest
- vanilla
- finely ground almonds

## Directions

1. Pound or grind poppyseeds finely, place in a pot, pour cream over, and let cook until it becomes a thick paste, stirring frequently so the poppyseeds do not scorch.
2. Add honey or sugar to taste.
3. You can also add ground clove, lemon zest, vanilla, or finely ground almonds.
