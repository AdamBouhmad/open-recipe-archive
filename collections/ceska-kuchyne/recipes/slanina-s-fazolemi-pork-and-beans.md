## Ingredients

- Two cups of dried beans
- Cold water
- Kitchen soda
- Salt
- Half a can of tomatoes or three to four fresh tomatoes
- One onion
- One tablespoon of butter
- About one pound of salted pork
- One tablespoon of syrup
- One tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. In the evening, soak two cups of beans in cold water
2. In the morning, put them on the stove with that water, add half a teaspoon of kitchen soda and a little salt and let cook for about half an hour
3. In a pan, put half a can of canned tomatoes or three to four fresh tomatoes, add one chopped onion, one tablespoon of butter and let braise for about half an hour
4. Then wash well about one pound of salted pork, add to the beans and strain the tomato sauce into them
5. Add one tablespoon of syrup and one tablespoon of sugar, salt to taste, cover well and bake slowly for two to three hours
