## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 0.25 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup coconut

## Directions

1. Beat the 2 eggs well (reserve 1 egg white for frosting).
2. Add 1 cup sugar and 0.25 cup butter and cream together thoroughly.
3. Add 1 cup sweet milk, 2 cups flour, and 2 teaspoons baking powder and mix well until smooth.
4. Add 1 cup coconut and mix well.
5. Pour into a large mold and bake.
6. Beat the reserved egg white and spread on top, then sprinkle with coconut.
