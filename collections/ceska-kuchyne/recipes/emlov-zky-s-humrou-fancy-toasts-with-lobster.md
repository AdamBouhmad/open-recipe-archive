## Ingredients

- Lobster meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Round fried bread slices
- Crab butter
- Egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut lobster meat into small dice
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Dress with lemon juice
4. Place on round fried bread slices with a border of crab butter
5. Lobster can also be cut into thin slices and garnished with chopped yolks, whites, and parsley
