## Ingredients

- one onion, chopped
- two tablespoons butter
- eight tomatoes
- three tablespoons flour
- two cups beef broth
- several tablespoons vinegar or one glass wine
- half a lemon rind, finely chopped
- piece of sugar
- salt
- one and a half pounds veal from the leg, in neat pieces

## Directions

1. Place chopped onion, two tablespoons butter, and eight tomatoes in a pan and let stew
2. Sprinkle with three tablespoons of flour and mix well
3. Add two cups of beef broth and several tablespoons of vinegar or one glass of wine if desired
4. Add half a finely chopped lemon rind and a piece of sugar, season with salt
5. Let simmer for half an hour and strain
6. Cut one and a half pounds of veal from the leg into neat pieces and add to the strained sauce
7. Let simmer for about three-quarters of an hour until the meat is tender
8. Remove meat to a platter and pour the gravy over it
