## Ingredients

- sweet pears, plums or cherries
- sugar, amount equal to half the weight of fruit
- honey or caramelized sugar
- optional: almonds for plums

## Directions

1. Arrange fruit on a board covered with grape leaves
2. Dry in the sun, turning frequently
3. When fruit is sufficiently dried, dip several times in thick boiled sugar
4. Dry again after each dipping
5. Repeat dipping and drying until all sugar is used
6. If fruit has no stem, insert a stick
7. Before serving, dip in honey or caramelized sugar
8. For plums, optionally place an almond inside instead of the pit
