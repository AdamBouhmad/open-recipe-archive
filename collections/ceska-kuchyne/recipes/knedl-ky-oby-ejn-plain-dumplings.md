## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram flour (type 00)
- salt
- 2 bread rolls
- 1 egg
- spring water

## Directions

1. Place 1 kilogram of flour on a baking sheet and salt it
2. Cut 2 bread rolls into cubes and fry them
3. Mix 1 egg with as much spring water as is needed for the flour
4. Mix the egg and water with the flour to form a stiff dough that does not stick to the hand
5. Add the fried bread cubes
6. Form dumplings
7. Boil in salted water
