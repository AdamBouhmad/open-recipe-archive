## Ingredients

- Half a kilo of nut kernels
- Boiling water
- Six egg whites
- More than a liter of sour cream
- More than a kilo of finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Scald half a kilo of nut kernels in boiling water
2. Shell them and clean them
3. Grind with sour cream
4. Beat six egg whites
5. Cover with more than a liter of sour cream
6. Mix with the nuts and add more than a kilo of finely crushed sugar
7. Beat this mixture constantly
8. Heat several times and allow to cool
9. Strain and prepare as other ice cream
