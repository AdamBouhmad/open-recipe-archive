## Ingredients

- 8 to 10 mealy potatoes
- 8 beaten egg yolks
- lemon zest
- 240 grams sugar rubbed with lemon

## Directions

1. Bake 8 to 10 mealy potatoes, scrape out the inside with a spoon, press through a sieve
2. Mix in 8 beaten egg yolks, lemon zest, and 240 grams sugar rubbed with lemon
3. Stir for half an hour, then add snow from egg whites
4. Fill several boxes made of stiff paper halfway and bake for 20 to 25 minutes
