## Ingredients

- brewed tea
- four deciliters cream
- seven deka sugar
- six egg yolks
- rum
- orange sugar

## Directions

1. Mix brewed tea with four deciliters of cream
2. Add seven deka sugar and six egg yolks
3. When it has been whipped enough and cooled, add rum and orange sugar
