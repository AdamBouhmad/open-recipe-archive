## Ingredients

- seven decagrams sugar
- water
- three and one-half deciliters cream
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dissolve seven decagrams of sugar in water and boil until brown
2. Mix with three and one-half deciliters of cream into which six egg yolks have been beaten
