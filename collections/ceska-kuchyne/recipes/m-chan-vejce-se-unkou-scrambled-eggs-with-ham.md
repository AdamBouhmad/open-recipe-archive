## Ingredients

- cooked ham (finely chopped)
- butter or lard
- chives (finely chopped) or green parsley
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Crush the cooked and finely chopped ham in butter or lard with a little finely chopped chives or green parsley
2. Season with pepper
3. Mix it into the scrambled eggs prepared according to the previous recipe
4. Alternatively: add finely chopped cooked ham directly to the beaten eggs and stir everything together in a pan with butter
