## Ingredients

- 10.5 deciliters (3 tablespoons) flour
- 42 dekagrams (28 lots) butter
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) almonds
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- 8 cooked egg yolks
- 2 raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, almonds, sugar, 8 cooked egg yolks, and 2 raw egg yolks into a dough
2. Make 2 cakes from the dough
3. Coat one with jam, cover with the other
4. Bake in a form as a cake and glaze
