## Ingredients

- 1-2 liters raspberry juice
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) ground sugar
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- several tablespoons ground Buchara
- 8 egg whites for snow

## Directions

1. Boil 1-2 liters raspberry juice with 18 dekagrams ground sugar
2. While it cools, beat 12 dekagrams butter with 8 egg yolks, 12 dekagrams sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice, several tablespoons ground Buchara and stiff snow from 8 egg whites
3. Put everything in a mold and boil for half an hour in water
