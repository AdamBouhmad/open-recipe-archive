## Ingredients

- salted water
- celery
- carrot
- parsley
- onion
- green parsley
- cauliflower
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. In salted water put to cook a piece of celery, carrot, parsley and onion, mainly however green parsley
2. When the vegetables are cooked, add a head of cauliflower and let it cook until soft
3. Meanwhile make a light roux from butter, strain the soup on it, let it cook until it thickens
4. Gently place cauliflower heads into it and serve at table
