## Ingredients

- one pot of nice rice
- warm water
- a piece of butter
- salt
- two cleaned spring chickens

## Directions

1. Pick over one pot of nice rice and rinse it clean, put in a pot and pour two or three pots of hot water over it, add a piece of butter and salt and let it cook until the rice is soft.
