## Ingredients

- berries
- wine
- water
- sugar
- onion
- thyme

## Directions

1. Stew the berries until almost all water evaporates
2. Strain, removing seeds and skins
3. Add wine, water, a piece of sugar, a piece of chopped onion, and thyme
4. When cooked, strain again
5. Add more sugar if not sweet enough
