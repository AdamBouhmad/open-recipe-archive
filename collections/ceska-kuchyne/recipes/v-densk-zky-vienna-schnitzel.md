## Ingredients

- veal cutlets from the leg
- salt
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut neat cutlets from the veal leg
2. Beat each one nicely with a wooden mallet
3. Salt it
4. Coat in flour
5. Dip in beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
7. Fry in hot clarified butter or lard until golden
8. Arrange on a plate and surround with sliced lemon
9. Salted cutlets may also be coated only in flour and fried in hot butter or lard
10. You may also use half butter and half lard
