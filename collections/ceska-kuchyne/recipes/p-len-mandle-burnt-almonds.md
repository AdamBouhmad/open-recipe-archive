## Ingredients

- beautiful selected almonds
- sugar
- water
- ground cinnamon
- vanilla or other fine spices (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and rub the beautiful selected almonds
2. Put sugar in a pot or pan as desired
3. Add a little water and mix
4. Add the almonds and heat everything well so the sugar dissolves quickly
5. Continuously stir and cook and turn so long until the almonds begin to crack
6. Remove the almonds, coat them in crushed cinnamon
7. Mix again in a pan with sugar and heat strongly until everything is completely dried
8. When the sugar has completely adhered to the almonds, cover the pan with clean cloth and let stand for some time, by which a special shine is achieved
9. Instead of cinnamon, vanilla or other fine and desired spices can be added to the almonds
