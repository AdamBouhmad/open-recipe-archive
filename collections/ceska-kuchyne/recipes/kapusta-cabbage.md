## Ingredients

- cabbage
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- pale roux
- smoked meat

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and slice the cabbage into cubes, pour water over it and scald it.
2. Rub two cloves of garlic with salt, add a little pepper, a piece of fresh butter, and braise until the cabbage is soft.
3. Strain very little roux through a sieve, chop a piece of smoked meat and add to it, just enough so the cabbage gets the taste of smoked meat, which is placed in the middle of the platter and cabbage around it.
