## Ingredients

- yeast
- lukewarm milk or water
- 1/2 liter flour
- cooked grated potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- salt
- 1-2 spoonfuls sugar
- butter for spreading
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk or water
2. Mix with flour and let rise in warm place
3. Add cooked grated potatoes (while warm), eggs, butter, salt, and sugar
4. Roll out dough to finger thickness
5. Place on floured baking sheet and let rise
6. Pierce with fork
7. Brush with butter
8. Sprinkle thickly with sugar and cinnamon
9. Drizzle with butter
10. Bake for half hour
11. Serve warm with coffee
