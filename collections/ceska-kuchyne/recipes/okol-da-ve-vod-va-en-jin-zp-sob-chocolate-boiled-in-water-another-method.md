## Ingredients

- 4-5 grams fine chocolate per cup
- boiling water
- two egg yolks
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take 4-5 grams of fine chocolate per cup and break into pieces
2. Add to boiling water and stir constantly until completely boiled
3. Remove from heat and let it foam up again
4. Stir in two beaten egg yolks
5. Pour into cups and top with whipped cream foam
