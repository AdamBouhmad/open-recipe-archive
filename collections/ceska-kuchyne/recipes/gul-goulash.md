## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fatty meat from ribs
- butter
- chopped onion
- caraway seed
- flour
- fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes, salt it, and braise with chopped onion in butter
2. Add caraway seed tied in a piece of cloth
3. After half an hour, dust with flour, stir the sauce, and let it simmer briefly
4. Remove the caraway seed before serving
5. Arrange fried potatoes around the meat on a plate
