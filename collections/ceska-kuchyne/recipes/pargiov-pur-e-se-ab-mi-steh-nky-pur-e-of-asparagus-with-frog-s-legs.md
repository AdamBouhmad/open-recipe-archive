## Ingredients

- asparagus heads
- asparagus lower parts
- salt
- water
- butter
- asparagus heads
- flower (seasoning)
- bread cubes, fried
- frog's legs
- butter
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus heads, slice the lower parts and cook in salted water until soft
2. Press through a sieve and brown with light butter
3. Add the asparagus heads and let cook for about a quarter hour
4. Season with salt and a little flower (seasoning)
5. Pour into a dish with fried bread cubes and frog's legs that have been stewed in butter with chopped parsley
