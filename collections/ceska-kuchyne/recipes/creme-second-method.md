## Ingredients

- 2-3 spoons flour
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 liter cream
- sugar
- chopped lemon zest
- vanilla
- almonds

## Directions

1. Mix flour with 8 egg yolks, 1/2 liter of cream and boil until a thick porridge forms
2. Add sugar, chopped lemon zest, vanilla, almonds, etc.
