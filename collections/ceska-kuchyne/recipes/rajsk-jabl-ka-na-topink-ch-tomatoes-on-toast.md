## Ingredients

- 4 medium tomatoes
- 2 small onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 small cup sweet milk or sour cream
- Butter
- Toast

## Directions

1. Scald, peel and cook together four medium tomatoes and two small finely chopped onions for three quarters of an hour.
2. Then drain all the water, salt and pepper to taste, add a small cup of sweet milk or sour cream, add a piece of butter and mix well.
3. In the meantime prepare several slices of toast from white bread and pour the tomatoes over them.
