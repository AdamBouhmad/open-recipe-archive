## Ingredients

- calf brains
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Place the brains in lukewarm water for half an hour
2. Remove all membranes and place in cold water
3. Shortly before serving, remove from water and place on clean cloth
4. Cut each brain in half
5. Salt and pepper the pieces
6. Dust with flour
7. Dip in beaten egg
8. Coat with breadcrumbs
9. Fry in clarified butter until golden
10. Serve with lemon cut into quarters
