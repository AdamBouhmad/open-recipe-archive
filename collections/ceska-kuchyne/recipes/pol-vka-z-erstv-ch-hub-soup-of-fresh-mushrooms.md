## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- green parsley
- salt
- water
- bread or bread cubes

## Directions

1. Slice several nice fresh mushrooms, clean them well
2. Put them on a shallow pan with a piece of butter, add a little chopped green parsley, a little salt and let stew under a lid
3. When the water begins to be lost from them, put them in a pot, pour good browned flour water on them as much as you need soup and let cook a lot more
4. Then pour the soup into a bowl over fried bread or bread cubes
