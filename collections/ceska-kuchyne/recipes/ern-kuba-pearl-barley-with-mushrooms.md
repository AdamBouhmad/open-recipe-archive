## Ingredients

- 2 small cups large pearl barley
- 1 small cup fresh mushrooms or 1/2 small cup dried mushrooms
- green parsley
- butter or lard
- raw lard cut into fine dice or grated butter
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- garlic

## Directions

1. Cook two small cups of large pearl barley according to the previous method
2. Meanwhile, let one small cup of fresh sliced mushrooms or half a small cup of dried mushrooms stew in butter or lard with green parsley
3. Mix the mushrooms with the barley
4. Add a piece of raw lard cut into small dice or grated butter
5. Season with a little pepper, marjoram and garlic rubbed with salt
6. Stir well and bake in the oven for half an hour
7. Instead of pearl barley, you can also use torn grits
8. Dried mushrooms are more substantial and tastier when soaked in cold water for several hours before use; pour only enough water over them to cover them
