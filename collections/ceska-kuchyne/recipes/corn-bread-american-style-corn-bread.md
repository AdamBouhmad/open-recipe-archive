## Ingredients

- 2 small cups sour milk
- 2 egg yolks
- salt to taste
- 1/2 tablespoon lard
- 2 small cups cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put sour milk in a bowl, add egg yolks, salt, and lard and mix well
2. Add cornmeal and baking soda dissolved in hot water and mix
3. Beat the mixture well with a wooden spoon
4. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold in gently
5. Grease a baking pan with lard, pour the batter into it and bake until nicely browned
6. Serve with syrup or butter
