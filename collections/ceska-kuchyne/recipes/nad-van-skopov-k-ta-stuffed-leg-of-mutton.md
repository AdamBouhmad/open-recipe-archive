## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- shallots
- anchovies
- white bread
- sour cream
- eggs
- salt
- lard
- onions
- carrots
- water
- butter
- sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the leg and cut out additional meat to create a proper opening
2. Melt lard and fry finely chopped shallots and anchovies
3. Add the cut-out meat, four tablespoons grated white bread, four tablespoons sour cream, three whole eggs, and salt
4. Mix well
5. Stuff the leg with this mixture
6. Sew it shut carefully, ensuring it keeps its original shape
7. Place two sliced onions and two carrots on a baking pan
8. Place the leg on them
9. Pour a little water and melted butter under it
10. Roast in the oven, basting with sour cream during roasting
11. When cooked, strain the sauce and remove all fat from it
12. Remove the thread from the leg before serving
13. Pour sauce over it
