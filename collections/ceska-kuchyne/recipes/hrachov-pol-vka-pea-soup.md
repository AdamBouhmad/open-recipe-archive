## Ingredients

- green or dried peas
- soft water
- vegetable roots, sliced
- thyme
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- browned meat broth
- 1 tablespoon spinach
- salt
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Soak green or dried peas, boil them the next day in soft water
2. Add sliced vegetable roots, thyme, 45 grams (3 lots) of butter
3. Press through a sieve, add soup, browned meat broth, 1 tablespoon of spinach
4. Salt and pour over fried roll
