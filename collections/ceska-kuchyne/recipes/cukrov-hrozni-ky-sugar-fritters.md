## Ingredients

- ¼ kilogram fine flour
- a little sugar
- egg whites from 8 eggs
- a little wine

## Directions

1. Mix the fine flour with a little sugar
2. Add egg whites from 8 eggs and a little wine
3. Mix only slightly so it does not foam
4. Press dough through a sieve or funnel into hot butter to form small fritters
5. Fry until golden red
6. Roll around a round stick
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
