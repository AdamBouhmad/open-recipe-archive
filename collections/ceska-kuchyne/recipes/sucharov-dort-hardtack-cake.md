## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 375 grams fine sifted sugar
- 200 grams peeled and grated almonds
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 grams finely crushed cinnamon
- 1 small teaspoon clove powder
- stiff snow from 15 egg whites
- 250 grams ground hardtacks

## Directions

1. Mix 15 egg yolks with 375 grams fine sifted sugar, 200 grams peeled and grated almonds, grated lemon zest, lemon juice, 4 grams finely crushed cinnamon, 1 small teaspoon clove powder
2. Work everything together for about half an hour
3. Add the stiff snow from 15 egg whites and 250 grams ground hardtacks
4. Pour into buttered cake molds and bake slowly
