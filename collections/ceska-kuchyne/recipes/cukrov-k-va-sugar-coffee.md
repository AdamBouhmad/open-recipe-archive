## Ingredients

- strong coffee
- tragacanth flour
- finely ground coffee
- sugar

## Directions

1. Moisten tragacanth flour in strong coffee
2. Press it through clean linen cloth
3. Add finely ground coffee with sugar
4. Make a dough from everything
5. Shape small coffee beans from the dough
6. When sufficiently hardened and dried, sugar as for almonds
