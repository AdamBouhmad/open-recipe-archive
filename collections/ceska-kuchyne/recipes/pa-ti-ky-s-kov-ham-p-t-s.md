## Ingredients

- Ham leftovers or freshly baked and cooled ham
- Meat
- Ribs, crushed in mortar
- Butter
- Truffles, finely chopped
- Coulis
- Good wine
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from leftover or fresh ham, chop finely.
2. Stew the crushed ribs in butter and finally press through fine sieve with the meat.
3. Mix everything again with finely chopped truffles, coulis, and good wine, and stew.
4. Place pâtés in butter dough and serve hot.
