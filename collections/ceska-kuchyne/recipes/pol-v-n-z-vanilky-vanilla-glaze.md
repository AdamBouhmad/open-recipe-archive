## Ingredients

- half vanilla pod
- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) finely crushed sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Crush half vanilla pod
2. Mix with 14 dekagrams (8 lots) finely crushed sugar
3. Add egg white and mix until white and thick
4. Dry on baked goods in oven
