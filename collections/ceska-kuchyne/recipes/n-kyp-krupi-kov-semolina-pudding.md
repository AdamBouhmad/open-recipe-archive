## Ingredients

- two cups milk
- four tablespoons semolina
- two tablespoons fresh butter
- three egg yolks
- two tablespoons sugar
- three egg whites
- salt
- sliced apples
- sugar and cinnamon for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Bring two cups milk to a boil and sprinkle four tablespoons semolina into it, stirring constantly for about two minutes.
2. Salt and set aside to cool.
3. Beat two tablespoons fresh butter until foamy, then rub it together with three egg yolks.
4. Add the cooled semolina mixture to this and mix in two tablespoons sugar and stiff snow made from three egg whites.
5. Grease a pudding mold with butter, line the bottom with sliced apples, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, and spread a layer of semolina mixture over it.
6. Add another layer of apples and cover with semolina mixture again.
7. Bake for half an hour.
