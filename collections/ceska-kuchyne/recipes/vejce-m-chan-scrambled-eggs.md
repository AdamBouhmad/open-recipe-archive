## Ingredients

- several eggs
- salt
- fresh butter
- chives (optional)
- pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs into a bowl and add salt.
2. Melt a piece of fresh butter, pour in the eggs, place over heat and stir constantly until they reach the desired thickness.
3. Finely chopped chives may be added directly to the pan or served on a separate plate.
4. Pickled cucumbers are usually served with them.
