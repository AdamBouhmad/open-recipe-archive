## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- salt
- bacon slices
- 1/2 onion, sliced into rounds
- green parsley, chopped
- lemon zest, chopped
- butter, melted
- wine
- beef broth
- mushrooms, 1 small plate
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 1 small white onion, finely chopped
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut veal into wide pieces, pound them gently to flatten and salt both sides
2. Place several slices of good bacon on a roasting pan
3. Lay the veal cutlets on the bacon
4. Slice half an onion into rounds and place on the cutlets
5. Sprinkle with chopped green parsley and chopped lemon zest
6. Pour over some melted butter, cover and let stew gently over heat
7. Frequently add a little wine and beef broth while cooking
8. Clean fresh mushrooms well, trim the stems, rinse in clean water and slice thinly
9. In a small roasting pan, melt 3 tablespoons of butter and add the mushrooms
10. Pour the juice of 1 lemon over them, add 1 small finely chopped white onion, salt and let stew for a while
11. When ready to serve, pour the stewed mushrooms onto a platter and top with the stewed cutlets and their juices
