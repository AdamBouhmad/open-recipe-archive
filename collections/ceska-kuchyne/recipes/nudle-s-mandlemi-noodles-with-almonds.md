## Ingredients

- noodles
- fresh butter
- chopped zest from half a lemon
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of powdered sugar
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of chopped almonds
- cinnamon (optional)
- butter for pan
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make and cook noodles
2. Let cool and drain
3. Rub fresh butter, add lemon zest and 6 egg yolks
4. Add sugar and chopped almonds
5. Optional: add cinnamon
6. Mix with noodles
7. Place on baking pan greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs
8. Bake
