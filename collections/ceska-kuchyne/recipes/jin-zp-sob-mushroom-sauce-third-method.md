## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- mushrooms
- lemon juice
- flour
- bouillon
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Melt 125 grams of butter until brown
2. Braise several mushrooms in it
3. Remove them and sprinkle with lemon juice
4. Make a roux from butter and flour
5. Pour in bouillon
6. Season with pepper and spices
7. Add mushrooms
8. Simmer all together
