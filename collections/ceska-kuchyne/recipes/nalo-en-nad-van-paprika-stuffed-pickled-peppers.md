## Ingredients

- large bell pepper pods
- salt
- salted water
- onion
- red cabbage
- tomatoes
- carrots
- corn kernels
- small cucumbers
- horseradish
- whole mustard
- celery seed
- Spanish pepper (curry powder)
- ground cloves
- cinnamon
- allspice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Select large pepper pods, cut each one on the side and carefully remove the seeds
2. Pour salted water over them, let them lie for six days and renew the salted water several times
3. Finely chop onion, red cabbage, tomatoes, carrots, corn kernels from the cob and small cucumbers, grate a piece of horseradish and mix well
4. Add whole mustard, celery seed and a little Spanish pepper (curry powder)
5. Remove the pepper pods from the water, let them drain, dust them inside with ground cloves, cinnamon and allspice, fill them with the filling so they regain their original shape and sew them carefully
6. Arrange them in a stone crock, pour over them all the spices boiled and cooled vinegar, cover the crock well and set in a cold place
7. The proportion of individual components of the filling should be adjusted according to individual taste
