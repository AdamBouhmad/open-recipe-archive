## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- finely crushed cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare as described for white sugared almonds
2. When almonds are completely coated with sugar, add finely crushed cinnamon to the warm sugar
3. Stir all together again
4. Place almonds on sieves to dry completely
