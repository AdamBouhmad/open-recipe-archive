## Ingredients

- Water
- A piece of butter
- A little salt
- A little flower (kvetu)
- White roux (bílou jíškou)
- Sweet or sour cream
- Sorrel leaves
- Purslane (opence/oponky)
- Daisy leaves
- Strawberry leaves
- Young nettle leaves
- Dandelion leaves
- Salted water
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter, a little salt, and a little flower into water
2. When cooked, brown with white roux and strain
3. Add either sweet or sour cream, mix and cook for a while
4. Cook the tender leaves of sorrel, purslane, daisy, strawberries, young nettles, and dandelion in salted water
5. Drain the water, chop the leaves finely and add to the soup in which 2 egg yolks have been mixed
