## Ingredients

- ingredients as for lemon soufflé
- finely crushed cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare this soufflé like lemon soufflé, but add finely crushed cinnamon to the dough
2. In this way one can also prepare vanilla, clove, and other soufflés
