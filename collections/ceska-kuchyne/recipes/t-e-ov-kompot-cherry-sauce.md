## Ingredients

- Cherries (one pound)
- Sugar (one quarter pound)
- Water (one small cup)
- A piece of whole cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Red wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook one quarter pound sugar with one small cup of water, a piece of whole cinnamon and lemon peel
2. Add cherries with stems removed and washed in cold water
3. Let them cook on high heat and then cool
4. Remove the cherries with a slotted spoon to a dish
5. Let the juice cook a little longer and pour it over the cherries
6. You can also add a little red wine to the sugar
7. Prepare compote from sour cherries similarly
