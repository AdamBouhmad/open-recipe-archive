## Ingredients

- one and one-half liters strawberries
- half liter sweet cream
- 24 grams crushed sugar
- lemon zest from half a lemon
- half a small teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rub one and one-half liters of strawberries with half a liter of sweet cream
2. Strain
3. Mix 24 grams of crushed sugar with zest from half a lemon rubbed with sugar and half a small teaspoon of cinnamon
4. Pour into glasses
