## Ingredients

- young kohlrabi
- veal
- cooked smoked pork
- kohlrabi pulp
- eggs
- white bread or crackers
- ground spice
- fresh butter
- salt
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Take small kohlrabi of equal size, peel them, cut off the tops, carefully hollow them out and place in cold water
2. Remove to a clean cloth, place at an angle to let water drain, and stuff with veal forcemeat
3. Finely chop raw veal, cooked smoked pork, and the hollowed kohlrabi pulp
4. Mix in three eggs, three tablespoons of grated white bread or crushed crackers, ground spice, a piece of fresh butter, and salt
5. Mix well and fill the kohlrabi
6. Cover each with its cut-off top, grease a pan with butter, place stuffed kohlrabi in it, add beef broth to about half the height of the kohlrabi, and braise until tender
7. Arrange on a shallow dish and pour a little brown sauce over
