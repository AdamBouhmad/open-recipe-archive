## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls of sugar
- rind from one lemon, cut up
- a piece of cinnamon
- 1 pint of white wine

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks, 4 spoonfuls of sugar, and cut-up rind from one lemon together well in a large pot
2. Add a piece of cinnamon
3. Pour 1 pint of white wine into it
4. Place over moderate heat and whisk with a wire whisk until it froths
5. Do not let it boil. When it thickens, pour into a dish and serve immediately at the table
