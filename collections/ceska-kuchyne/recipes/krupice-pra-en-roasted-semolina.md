## Ingredients

- semolina
- water
- salt
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Place semolina on a baking sheet and stir constantly on the stove until it turns reddish
2. Pour as much water as is used for semolina porridge from milk
3. Salt it and stir until it is sufficiently thick
4. On the plate pour melted butter with onion over it
