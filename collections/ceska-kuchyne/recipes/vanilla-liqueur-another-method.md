## Ingredients

- Vanilla
- 3 liters good spirits (kořalka)
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Crush or finely chop the vanilla and place it in a bottle with about 3 liters of good spirits
2. Seal the bottle well and leave it in a warm place for at least two weeks
3. Shake and stir daily
4. Add sugar to taste and let it sit for several more days
5. Strain and it is ready to drink
