## Ingredients

- Nice tomatoes
- Fresh butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar
- 1 cup sour cream or milk
- 1 spoonful flour

## Directions

1. Dip nice tomatoes into boiling water for two minutes, peel off the skins, cut them in half, place on a pan with a piece of fresh butter, salt, pepper, sprinkle with sugar and let stew for about a quarter hour.
2. Take one cup of sour cream or milk if sour cream is not available, mix in one spoonful of flour, pour into the tomatoes and when it begins to boil, remove from heat and serve with beefsteak or roast.
