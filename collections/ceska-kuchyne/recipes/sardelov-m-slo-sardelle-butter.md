## Ingredients

- Two ounces sardelles
- Half pound fresh butter
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Clean two ounces of sardelles, remove the bones and crush the sardelles with a pinch of paprika in a mortar into a paste; then work it on a clean board with half pound of fresh butter until smooth, like butter again, put it on a dish and place in a cool place.
