## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of eggs (8 or 9)
- one pound of sifted sugar
- one teaspoon of almond extract
- two spoonfuls of rum or cognac
- one pound of fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until it becomes foamy
2. add the eggs and beat
3. add the sifted sugar
4. add the almond extract and rum or cognac
5. stir for half an hour
6. finally fold in the fine flour
7. grease the pan with butter and fill it three-quarters full with batter
8. let the cake bake slowly and when cooled, coat with white or red icing, or sprinkle with sugar
