## Ingredients

- wine, vinegar or spirits barrel
- clean water
- fruit scraps and peelings
- rotten fruit

## Directions

1. Fill wine, vinegar or spirits barrels half-full with clean water and place the barrel in a warm location (in summer on the attic or storage room, in winter in a warm room or kitchen).
2. Throw all kinds of skins, scraps, and even rotten fruit into the barrel.
3. Place a perforated lid on top so that air has access to the interior.
4. In a short time excellent vinegar develops, which is then strained and poured into containers.
