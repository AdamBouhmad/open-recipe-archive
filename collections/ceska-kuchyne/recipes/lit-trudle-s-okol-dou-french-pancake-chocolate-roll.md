## Ingredients

- quarter pound chocolate
- 6 tablespoons white flour
- sugar
- 1 quart milk
- butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate quarter pound chocolate on a grater
2. Place in a small pan
3. Mix 6 tablespoons white flour and the same amount of sugar
4. Pour 1 quart milk into it
5. Mix well and cook on mild heat into a thick porridge
6. You must stir constantly while cooking
7. Pour the porridge into a platter
8. Let it cool
9. Add 2 tablespoons fresh butter
10. Eight egg yolks
11. Rub well
12. Then slowly mix in stiff snow from eight egg whites
13. Coat the pancakes prepared as before with this filling
14. Roll them up
15. Place one next to the other in a pan greased with butter
16. Sprinkle with ground sugar
17. Pour a little milk
18. Let bake slowly in the oven
