## Ingredients

- one small cup syrup
- one small cup sugar
- half small cup butter
- half small cup strong black coffee
- one teaspoon soda
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Combine one small cup syrup, one small cup sugar, half small cup butter, half small cup strong black coffee
2. Add one teaspoon soda and two egg yolks from two eggs
3. Mix together
