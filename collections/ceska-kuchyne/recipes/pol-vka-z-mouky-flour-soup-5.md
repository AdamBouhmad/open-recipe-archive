## Ingredients

- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 litre boiling water
- salt
- flower
- lemon peel, chopped
- 2 egg yolks
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Melt 6 decagrams (4 lots) of butter and mix with 3 tablespoons of flour until the roux begins to rise
2. Slowly pour in 1 litre of boiling water and boil
3. Mix well, salt it, add flower, chopped lemon peel, two egg yolks and chopped parsley
