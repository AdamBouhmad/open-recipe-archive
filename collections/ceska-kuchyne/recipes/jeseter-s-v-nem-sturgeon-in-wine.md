## Ingredients

- Sturgeon, a four-pound piece from the middle if possible
- Carrot, sliced into rounds
- Onion
- Parsley, a bunch
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves, two pieces
- Garlic, two cloves, minced
- Red wine
- Weak beef broth
- Butter and flour roux
- Anchovies, two, mashed
- Lemon, one, juice of
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Place a four-pound piece of sturgeon in a deep pot with carrot sliced into rounds, onion, a bunch of parsley, salt, pepper, two pieces of cloves and two minced garlic cloves
2. Pour equal parts red wine and weak beef broth over it
3. Cover and let simmer slowly for one hour
4. Remove the fish to a dish and place in a warm spot
5. Strain the sauce
6. Make a brown butter and flour roux and fry it
7. Let it simmer briefly
8. Strain again
9. Mix in a piece of butter, juice from one lemon and a pinch of paprika
10. Pour over the fish
