## Ingredients

- beef kidney
- cold water
- 2-3 tablespoons vinegar
- a piece of lemon peel
- bay leaf
- 6 grains of allspice
- 4 cloves
- onion, sliced
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- one cup good, thick sour cream
- one tablespoon flour
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Let the kidney soak for an hour, then cut it into slices.
2. Place in a pot and pour cold water over it and bring to a boil.
3. Skim off the abundant foam that rises; then salt it.
4. Add 2-3 tablespoons of vinegar, a piece of lemon peel, a bay leaf, 6 grains of allspice, 4 cloves, sliced onion, and one tablespoon of butter.
5. Cover and let it cook.
6. When the kidneys are tender, take a bowl of good, thick sour cream, add one tablespoon of flour, mix it well, pour it into the kidneys, and let it simmer a little longer.
7. When removing from heat, add one more egg yolk and mix well.
