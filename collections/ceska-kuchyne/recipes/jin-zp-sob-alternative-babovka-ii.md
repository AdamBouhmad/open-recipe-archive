## Ingredients

- 500 grams flour
- 60 grams lukewarm milk
- yeast
- four eggs
- 125 grams dissolved butter
- two tablespoons crushed sugar
- small teaspoon salt
- 250 ml lukewarm milk
- 125 grams raisins

## Directions

1. Sift 500 grams of flour into a bowl
2. Make a depression in it
3. Pour 60 grams of lukewarm milk with dissolved yeast into the depression
4. Beat in four eggs, 125 grams dissolved butter, two tablespoons crushed sugar, small teaspoon salt, and 250 ml lukewarm milk
5. Mix in 125 grams of raisins
6. Work everything thoroughly
7. Place in mold
8. Let rise
9. Bake slowly at moderate heat
