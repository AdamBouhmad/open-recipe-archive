## Ingredients

- pickled green beans
- pickled asparagus
- cauliflower
- field salad
- watercress
- young dandelion centers
- wine vinegar
- good oil
- hard-boiled eggs
- bacon

## Directions

1. Combine pickled green beans, pickled asparagus, cauliflower, field salad, watercress, and young dandelion centers
2. Salt and mix well
3. Add wine vinegar and good oil
4. Arrange hard-boiled eggs sliced around the salad
5. Garnish with sliced and rinsed bacon
