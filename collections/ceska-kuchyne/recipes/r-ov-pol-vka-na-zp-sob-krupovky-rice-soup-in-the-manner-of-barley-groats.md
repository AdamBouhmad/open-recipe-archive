## Ingredients

- rice
- cold water
- soup
- butter
- salt
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in cold water and boil it halfway
2. Drain the water and finish cooking in soup with butter
3. Press the rice through a sieve and add soup
4. Season with salt, boil with constant stirring, add two egg yolks and thicken as desired
