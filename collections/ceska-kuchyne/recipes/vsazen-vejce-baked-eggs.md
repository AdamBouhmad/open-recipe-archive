## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Place butter on a muffin pan; when hot, beat one egg into each indentation.
2. Salt them and leave until the yolk sets; do not let them overcook as they will not be palatable.
3. Usually served on lentils or with beefsteaks.
