## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- butter
- flour
- beef or chicken broth
- onion
- carrot
- leek
- celery
- parsley roots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- wine vinegar
- sour cream
- bread rolls grated
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare these kidneys in exactly the same way as calf's liver with sour cream (recipe 212)
2. Slice the kidneys into thin slices first
