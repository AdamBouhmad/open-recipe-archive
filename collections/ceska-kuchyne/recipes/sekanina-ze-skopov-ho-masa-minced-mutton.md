## Ingredients

- leftover roasted mutton leg
- onions
- flour
- beef broth
- meat juice
- red wine
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove skin and fat from leftover roasted mutton leg, cut into strips and set aside.
2. Braise 2 onions with a spoonful of flour.
3. Add half a liter of good broth, a little meat juice and a glass of red wine.
4. Cook the sauce, remove fat, strain, pepper and salt it.
5. Add the meat, heat it in the sauce and can be served with mashed potatoes.
