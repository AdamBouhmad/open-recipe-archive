## Ingredients

- half kilogram flour
- 3 grated bread rolls
- 3 tablespoons heated butter
- 15 grams pounded sugar
- milk or cream
- plums
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix half kilogram flour with 3 grated bread rolls.
2. Pour in 3 tablespoons of heated butter.
3. Add 15 grams of pounded sugar and mix everything.
4. Salt and make a stiff dough with milk or cream.
5. Make dumplings as ordinary plum dumplings, or roll the dough into 2 layers.
6. Remove pits from plums and place them by halves on one layer.
7. Cover with the second layer.
8. Grease a baking sheet, carefully place the entire dough on it and bake.
