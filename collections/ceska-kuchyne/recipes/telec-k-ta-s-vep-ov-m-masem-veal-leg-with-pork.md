## Ingredients

- Veal leg or kidney
- Pork meat with caraway, one and a half kilograms
- Pork feet

## Directions

1. When roasting veal leg or kidney, it acquires a special flavor when one and a half kilograms of caraway-spiced pork meat or pork feet are added to it and roasted together with the veal.
2. Strain the sauce so the caraway remains on the strainer.
3. Slice each meat separately.
