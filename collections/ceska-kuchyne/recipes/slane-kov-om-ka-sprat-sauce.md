## Ingredients

- chopped onion
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- finely chopped cleaned sprat
- wine or vinegar
- capers
- pepper
- garlic
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion in butter with a spoonful of flour until golden
2. Cook with beef broth
3. Mix with finely chopped cleaned sprat, wine or vinegar
4. Add capers, pepper, and garlic if desired
5. Add blanched egg yolks before serving
