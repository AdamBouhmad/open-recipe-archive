## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- milk
- sufficient chocolate
- enough sugar

## Directions

1. Place egg yolks in a bowl and beat
2. Add milk, chocolate, and sugar to taste
3. Mix everything and boil
4. Remove from heat and beat constantly
5. Pour into a chocolate pot
6. The chocolate is very tasty and foams well with constant beating
