## Ingredients

- large oysters
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread, sliced and buttered
- milk

## Directions

1. Let nice large oysters drain on a sieve
2. Dip them one by one into heated butter so they do not stick to the wire tongs for broiling
3. Broil them quickly over glowing coals
4. When they are nicely brown on both sides, pepper and salt them
5. Arrange them on hot buttered toast that has been moistened slightly with milk
6. Serve very hot as they are only good this way
