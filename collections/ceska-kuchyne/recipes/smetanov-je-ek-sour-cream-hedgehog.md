## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 20 dekagrams sugar
- 12 tablespoons good sour cream
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- salt
- half kilogram grated roll
- 12 egg whites for snow
- cleaned raisins

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg yolks with 20 dekagrams sugar, 12 tablespoons good sour cream and lemon zest, cinnamon, salt, half kilogram grated roll
2. Add snow from 12 egg whites and cleaned raisins
3. Boil the hedgehog for 3/4 hour in boiling water
