## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 24 decagrams (16 lots) sugar
- small spoonful of crushed cinnamon
- 24 decagrams grated potatoes
- snow from egg whites
- breadcrumbs for dusting the form
- fat for greasing the form

## Directions

1. Mix 10 egg yolks with 24 decagrams (16 lots) sugar, a small spoonful of crushed cinnamon, 24 decagrams grated potatoes, and snow from egg whites.
2. Bake in a greased form dusted with grated breadcrumbs.
