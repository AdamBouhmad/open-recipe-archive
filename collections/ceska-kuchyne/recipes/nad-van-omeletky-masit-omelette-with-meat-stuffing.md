## Ingredients

- cooked ham, or braised sweetbreads, kidneys, brains, goose liver, mushrooms, oysters, crayfish or fish meat
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare omelettes according to the previous recipe, but instead of bacon fill them with either cooked ham, braised sweetbreads, kidneys, brains, goose liver, mushrooms, oysters, crayfish or fish meat
2. The meat must be finely chopped and braised in butter
3. When the omelette begins to set, spread the braised filling over it, then fold the omelette
