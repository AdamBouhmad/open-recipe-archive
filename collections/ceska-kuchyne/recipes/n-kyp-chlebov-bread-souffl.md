## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) homemade bread, grated
- 1/4 kilogram (1/4 pound) ground sugar
- 7.5 grams (1/2 lot) new spice
- 7.5 grams cinnamon
- 7.5 grams clove
- 10 egg yolks
- wine as much as the bread absorbs
- egg white snow from 5 egg whites
- butter
- hard bread, grated
- sour cherries (optional)
- Château sauce

## Directions

1. Grate 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) homemade bread
2. Add 1/4 kilogram (1/4 pound) ground sugar, 7.5 grams (1/2 lot) new spice, 7.5 grams cinnamon, 7.5 grams clove; mix everything on a board
3. Beat 10 egg yolks with a whisk for a quarter hour
4. Slowly sprinkle the grated bread with spices into the beaten egg yolks
5. Add as much wine as the bread absorbs
6. Beat everything again for a quarter hour
7. Add snow made from 5 egg whites
8. Butter the mold and sprinkle with grated hard bread
9. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake
10. If sour cherries are ripe, the dough can be poured, then sprinkled with cherries until the mold is almost full
11. Château sauce suits this soufflé
