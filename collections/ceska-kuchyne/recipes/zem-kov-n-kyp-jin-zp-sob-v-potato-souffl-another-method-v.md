## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- flour
- sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- 60 grams (4 lots) butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Let cooked potatoes cool, peel, and grate them
2. Mix equal parts flour and sugar so the total weight equals half the weight of the potatoes
3. Mix everything together and add a little cinnamon
4. Beat 60 grams (4 lots) of butter with 10 egg yolks
5. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 10 egg whites
