## Ingredients

- well-soaked cod
- celery
- parsley
- lemon zest
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- marjoram flowers
- half an onion
- water
- light roux (pale jíška)
- 3½ deciliters (žejdlík) good sweet cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse well-soaked cod and place on a baking dish with celery, parsley, lemon zest, butter, marjoram flowers, and half an onion
2. Pour water to about 2 finger-widths height
3. Braise until cod is soft
4. Arrange cod on a platter
5. Add light roux, cream, and lemon juice to the braising liquid, simmer, then strain over the cod
