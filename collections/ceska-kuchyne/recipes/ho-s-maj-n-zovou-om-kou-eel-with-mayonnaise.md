## Ingredients

- eel
- mayonnaise sauce
- aspic, broken up

## Directions

1. Prepare the eel as you would prepare marinated carp
2. Arrange the cooled eel in a ring on a platter
3. Place the mayonnaise sauce in the center
4. Create a border around it from the broken up aspic
