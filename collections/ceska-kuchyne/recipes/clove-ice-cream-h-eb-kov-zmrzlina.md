## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- One liter of sour cream
- Quarter kilo of sugar
- Several drops of clove oil

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs into a bowl
2. Pour in one liter of sour cream
3. Add a quarter kilo of sugar
4. While constantly stirring, heat several times and allow to cool each time
5. Add several drops of clove oil
6. Transform everything into ice cream
