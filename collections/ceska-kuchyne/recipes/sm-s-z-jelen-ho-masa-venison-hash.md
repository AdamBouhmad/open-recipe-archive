## Ingredients

- venison meat
- hot water
- cold water
- bacon slices
- bay leaves
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- vinegar
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Bone the venison meat, rinse in hot water, then place in cold water.
2. Put on a baking pan with bacon slices, bay leaves, onion, salt and pepper.
3. Braise until the onion turns golden.
4. Remove the meat and make a roux with bacon using 2 tablespoons of flour.
5. Add 3/4 liter of broth and vinegar.
6. After half an hour, strain the sauce and braise the meat a little longer.
7. Add either mashed potatoes or roasted potatoes.
