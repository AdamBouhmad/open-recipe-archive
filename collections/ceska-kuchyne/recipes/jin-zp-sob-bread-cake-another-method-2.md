## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams almonds with skins, crushed
- 24 dekagrams sugar
- 2 grated chocolate tablets
- 6 dekagrams bread dried dark and crushed to flour-like consistency
- lemon zest
- grated nutmeg
- 7.5 grams cloves (0.5 lot)
- 7.5 grams cinnamon
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 egg
- snow from 12 egg whites
- ice for decoration

## Directions

1. Mix 24 dekagrams crushed almonds with skins with 24 dekagrams sugar, 2 grated chocolate tablets, 6 dekagrams bread dried dark and crushed to flour-like consistency, lemon zest, grated nutmeg, 7.5 grams cloves, 7.5 grams cinnamon, 12 egg yolks, 1 egg, and snow from 12 egg whites
2. Beat for an hour
3. Bake the cake in a buttered, flour-dusted mold and decorate with ice
