## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- two peeled sour apples
- one onion
- a piece of cooked ham
- a piece of cold veal roast
- cooked smoked tongue
- a clean piece of herring, with bones removed
- hard-boiled eggs
- cleaned sardines
- beetroot
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes while warm and slice into rounds
2. Cut two peeled sour apples into small cubes
3. Cut one onion, a piece of cooked ham, a piece of cold veal roast, cooked smoked tongue, and a clean piece of herring (with bones removed) into small cubes
4. Separate the egg whites and yolks from hard-boiled eggs and chop each separately
5. Cut cleaned sardines lengthwise
6. On a platter, layer potatoes, sprinkle with diced apples, onion, herring, meat and capers and repeat until the platter is full to the top
7. Arrange sardines on top in a star pattern
8. Fill the spaces between the sardines alternately with chopped egg yolk and egg white
9. Around the edge of the platter, arrange alternating identical rounds cut from potatoes and beetroot
10. Finally, pour the salad with oil mixed with vinegar
