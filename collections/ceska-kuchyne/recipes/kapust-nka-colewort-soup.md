## Ingredients

- two cabbages
- two cauliflower florets
- beef broth
- handful of mushrooms
- butter
- chopped green parsley
- pepper
- liver and heart from three chickens
- bread roll
- milk
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook two cabbages and two cauliflower florets in beef broth, then carefully remove them, cut off the cauliflower florets and cut the cabbage into thin strips
2. Chop a handful of mushrooms, clean them well and add to them a piece of butter, a little chopped green parsley, a little pepper and let them stew
3. Chop the liver and heart from three chickens cooked in beef broth
4. Slice the bread roll, soak in milk, dip in flour and then in egg
5. Mix the liver, heart and mushrooms, place some of this mixture on one bread slice, cover with another slice, coat in grated bread crumbs and fry until golden
6. Put in a bowl with the sliced cabbage and cauliflower and pour good beef broth over it
