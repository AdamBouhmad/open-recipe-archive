## Ingredients

- beef from ribs
- salt
- pepper
- onion (chopped)
- pepper (about 4 grains)

## Directions

1. Pound the meat, salt it, place on baking sheet
2. Add chopped onion and about 4 grains of pepper
3. Braise until tender
4. Serve on a plate with the juices that remain from braising
