## Ingredients

- Sweet cherries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Add cleaned cherries that are scored on both sides to boiled sugar.
2. Let them cook slowly with the sugar.
3. Spread them on a dish, cover with paper and let sit undisturbed.
4. On the second day, drain the sugar, boil it to make it thicker, add the cherries back to it, and cook everything slowly together, repeating this for two more days.
5. Always skim off the foam; when the preserve cools, place in containers, bind them, and store in a cold place.
