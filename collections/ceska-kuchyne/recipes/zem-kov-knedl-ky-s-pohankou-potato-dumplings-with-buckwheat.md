## Ingredients

- 1 plate cooked potatoes
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) bacon cut into cubes
- 2 eggs
- salt
- buckwheat flour

## Directions

1. Grate cooked potatoes
2. Add bacon cubes, eggs, and salt
3. Add buckwheat flour until firm dough forms
4. Make small dumplings
5. Cook in salted water until they float to surface
