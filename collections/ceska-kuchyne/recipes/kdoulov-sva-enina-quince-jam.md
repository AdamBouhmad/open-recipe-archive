## Ingredients

- ripe quinces, sliced and peeled
- water
- sugar (three-quarters cup per cup of pulp)
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cook sliced and peeled ripe quinces in water until soft, then strain and press through a sieve.
2. Add three-quarters cup of sugar per cup of pulp and juice from one lemon.
3. Let everything cook down to a thick consistency, pour the jam into jars while hot, and seal only after it has cooled completely.
