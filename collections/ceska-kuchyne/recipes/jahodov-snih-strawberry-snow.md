## Ingredients

- two liters strawberries
- 24 decagrams crushed sugar
- crushed snow made from two and one-half liters cream

## Directions

1. Press two liters of strawberries through a sieve
2. Mix with 24 decagrams of crushed sugar and crushed snow made from two and one-half liters of cream
3. Arrange in snow-like mounds
