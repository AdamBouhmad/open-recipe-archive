## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- one teaspoon of chopped chives
- about four spoons of farina
- beef broth
- parsley
- saffron

## Directions

1. Rub one spoon of butter until it froths, mix in one whole egg and one egg yolk, one teaspoon of chopped chives and then add about four spoons of farina.
2. Let the dough stand for 10 minutes, then make small dumplings the size of small walnuts, cook them in beef broth covered for 10 minutes, add parsley, chives and saffron as usual.
