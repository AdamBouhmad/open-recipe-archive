## Ingredients

- two young chickens
- beef broth
- herring
- butter
- flowers or herbs
- lemon rind
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Pluck two young chickens and cook them halfway in beef broth
2. Remove them and let them cool
3. Meanwhile, wash and cut the herring into elongated strips, like bacon
4. Thread the herring through the chickens on the breasts
5. Cut the chickens in half and place them on a platter
6. Pour over them the broth in which they were cooked
7. Add a piece of butter, some flowers or herbs, lemon rind, lemon juice, and remaining herring pieces
8. Cut the herring roe into cubes and add it as well
9. Brown this with a little flour and let the chickens simmer until tender
10. Remove them to a platter and strain the sauce through a sieve over them
