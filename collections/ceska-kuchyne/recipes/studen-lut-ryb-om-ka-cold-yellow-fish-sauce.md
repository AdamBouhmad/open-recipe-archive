## Ingredients

- piece of carp or pike
- salt
- celery
- carrot
- flower
- ginger
- saffron
- pale roux
- wine
- four grated hard egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean a piece of carp or pike and salt it
2. Place it in a dish with celery and carrot, flower, ginger and saffron
3. Braise it for a while, adding water so it does not burn
4. Add pale roux
5. Add a little wine and four grated hard egg yolks
6. Place on a cold place
7. This sauce is usually added to pike or trout
