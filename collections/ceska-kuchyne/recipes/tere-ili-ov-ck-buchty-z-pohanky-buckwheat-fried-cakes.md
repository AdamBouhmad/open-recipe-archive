## Ingredients

- buckwheat
- water or milk
- butter
- salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook buckwheat with constant stirring in water or milk with butter and salt
2. Let the porridge cool, add 4 egg yolks and snow from 4 egg whites
3. Mix everything, grease a baking pan and bake the štere in an oven
