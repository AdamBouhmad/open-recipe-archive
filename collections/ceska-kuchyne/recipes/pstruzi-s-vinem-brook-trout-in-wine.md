## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- shallots or onion
- burgundy wine
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Clean the trout, gut them, salt them and let them lie for an hour.
2. Line the bottom of a pan with sliced shallots or onion, place the prepared trout on top, pour a pint of burgundy wine over them, add several bay leaves to them and let them boil for a quarter hour.
3. Then remove them onto a dish, pour the sauce over them and bring them quickly to table.
