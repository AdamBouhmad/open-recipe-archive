## Ingredients

- Hare hindquarters
- Bacon
- Onions
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Lemon peel
- Blanquette sauce
- Wine vinegar
- White flour roux
- Beef broth
- Sugar
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove all skin from the hindquarters, wash and salt them
2. Lard with bacon from both sides
3. Chop 2 onions and add pepper, thyme, bay leaf, a piece of lemon peel, and a piece of blanquette sauce
4. Moisten with 3 spoonfuls of good vinegar and braise until tender
5. Cut the hare into pieces and place on a platter
6. To the juices left in the pan, add a white roux, a little vinegar and beef broth
7. Simmer, and if the sauce is sour enough, add a piece of sugar
8. Pour the sauce over the hare and add three-quarters of a deciliter of good thick sour cream
9. Leave in a warm place for a while and continually baste with the sauce
10. Alternatively, roast the hare in the marinade liquid until almost cooked out, add the juice of one lemon and strain the remaining sauce without roux over it
