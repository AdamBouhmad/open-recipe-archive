## Ingredients

- sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- beef broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Slice the sirloin into pieces the thickness of a finger
2. Salt and pepper them
3. Quickly roast on butter
4. Place on another baking sheet, baste with broth
5. Add juice from one lemon, braise briefly
6. Serve with buttered potatoes
