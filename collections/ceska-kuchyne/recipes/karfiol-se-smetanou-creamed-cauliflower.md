## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- egg yolks
- flour
- sweet cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean cauliflower, keep longer stems, rinse in cold water and cook in salted water, not too soft
2. Drain on a sieve
3. Mix 8 egg yolks with 2 tablespoons flour smoothly in two cups of cream and blend with the yolks
4. Place the bowl in another bowl with hot water and whisk with a whisk until everything thickens
5. Then pour through a sieve and whisk until cooled
6. Arrange cauliflower neatly on a dish well-buttered and pour the cooled cream over it
7. Place the dish in the oven on a trivet and bake
8. Brush with butter once more during baking
9. Bake for half an hour total
