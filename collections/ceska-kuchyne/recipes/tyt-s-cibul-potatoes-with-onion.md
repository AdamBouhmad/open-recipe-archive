## Ingredients

- small potatoes
- small onions
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim small even potatoes and wash them
2. Prepare small onions, about one quarter the amount of potatoes
3. Layer potatoes and onions with butter
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Pour a little water with vinegar over them
6. Stew for three quarters of an hour
7. Serve with roasted meat of all kinds
