## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams barley
- 1 and 1/2 liters water
- butter
- wine
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the barley with water and butter
2. Add wine
3. Add lemon juice, cinnamon, and sugar
4. Boil everything for a quarter hour
5. Strain the broth
6. Add 2 egg yolks
