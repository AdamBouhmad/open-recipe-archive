## Ingredients

- 2 pounds tender meat, cut into small pieces
- 1 or 2 onions, sliced
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces and place in a pan.
2. Season with pepper and salt.
3. Slice the onions and add to the meat.
4. Cover with water, cover with a lid, and let simmer slowly until the meat is tender.
5. Mix 2 tablespoons butter with 2 tablespoons flour and add to the meat.
6. Let boil for a moment until the gravy is smooth.
