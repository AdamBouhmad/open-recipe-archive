## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 deciliters of smetana (sour cream)
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of powdered sugar
- a stick of vanilla
- 3 spoonfuls of flour
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix smetana with powdered sugar
2. Add vanilla stick
3. When smetana boils, add flour while stirring constantly
4. Mix in egg yolks
5. Stir until thickened
6. Chill
7. Shape dumplings with a spoon and add to cream
