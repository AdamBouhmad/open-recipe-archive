## Ingredients

- small white onions
- butter
- good milk
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel small white onions, cut into small pieces, braise in butter until soft and completely pale
2. Place on a sieve to drain completely
3. Pour good milk into a pot, bring to boil, salt and pepper it, add the onions and cook everything together
4. After cooking, strain, beat in a few egg yolks and the soup is ready to serve
