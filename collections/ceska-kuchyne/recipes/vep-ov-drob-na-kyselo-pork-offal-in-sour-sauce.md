## Ingredients

- pork offal (liver, lungs, heart, kidneys)
- salt
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- new spices
- pepper
- lemon zest
- vinegar
- water
- flour
- burnt sugar or gingerbread
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the offal, salt it, place on a baking pan, add sliced onion, thyme, bay leaf, new spices, pepper, and a piece of lemon zest.
2. Cook in vinegar diluted with water.
3. When the offal is cooked, remove it and leave it either whole or cut into pieces.
4. Add flour to the sauce and cook either with burnt sugar or gingerbread, strain over the offal, and if not sweet enough, add sugar.
