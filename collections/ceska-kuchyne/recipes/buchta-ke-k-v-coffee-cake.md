## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one cupful of grated butter
- eight tablespoons of fine sugar
- four egg yolks
- a little grated lemon zest
- a little pounded flower
- a little salt
- a piece of pressed yeast
- lukewarm milk or cream
- sugar mixed with cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Put into two pounds of flour one cupful of grated butter, eight tablespoons of fine sugar, four egg yolks, a little grated lemon zest, a little pounded flower, a little salt and mix well.
2. Then add a piece of pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk or cream and the snow from four egg whites, make a medium stiff dough, beat for half an hour and then let it rise.
3. Grease a pan with butter, sprinkle with grated bread, put into it the dough rolled out to the thickness of a finger, spread butter over the surface, let it rise and then bake slowly in the oven.
4. Sprinkle then with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
