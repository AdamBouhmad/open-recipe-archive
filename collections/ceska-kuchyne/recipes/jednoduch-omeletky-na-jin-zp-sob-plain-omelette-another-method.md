## Ingredients

- Two eggs
- One teaspoon of good cold sour cream or one spoonful of milk
- Salt
- Ground white pepper
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. For one person, beat two eggs with one teaspoon of good cold sour cream or one spoonful of milk
2. Beat with a pinch of salt and ground white pepper
3. Heat a piece of butter or lard in a frying pan
4. Pour the beaten eggs into it and tilt the pan from one side and then the other so it spreads evenly
5. When the omelette is slightly set on top, fold it in half or roll it up
6. Turn onto a plate and serve immediately
7. Before folding you may sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice, and sprinkle with sugar on the plate as well
8. A good omelette should be soft inside and golden on the outside
9. The sour cream or milk may be omitted if desired
