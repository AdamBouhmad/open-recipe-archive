## Ingredients

- 24 grams butter
- flour
- water
- lemon juice
- capers

## Directions

1. Mix 24 grams of butter with a spoonful of flour and 1/4 liter of water
2. Place bowl in hot water
3. Beat the mixture until it foams
4. Add lemon juice and capers
5. Can be served with fish, chicken and veal
