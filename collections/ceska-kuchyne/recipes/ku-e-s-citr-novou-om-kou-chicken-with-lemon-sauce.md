## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- flour
- lemon
- sugar
- white wine

## Directions

1. Stew a nice chicken according to the previous method, remove it to a platter, cover it and place it in a warm place.
2. Make a white roux from a spoonful of butter and a spoonful of flour, thin it with the strained broth in which the chicken stewed, add juice from half a lemon, a little finely grated lemon zest and a small piece of sugar, if desired add a few tablespoons of white wine, cook it into a smooth sauce, and pour it over the cut up chicken.
