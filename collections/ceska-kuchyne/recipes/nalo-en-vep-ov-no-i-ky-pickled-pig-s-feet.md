## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- onion
- spices

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the pig's feet well
2. Place them in a pot
3. Pour enough water over them so they are completely covered
4. Let them cook
5. After about one hour of cooking, salt them and let them continue cooking until tender
6. Pig's feet require four to five hours to cook completely
7. Arrange them in a clay pot
8. Pour boiling vinegar over them, simmered with onion and all spices
9. Let stand for two days
