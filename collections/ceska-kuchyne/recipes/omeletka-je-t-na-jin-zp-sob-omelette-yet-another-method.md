## Ingredients

- Several whole eggs
- Flour
- Salt
- Milk
- Clarified butter
- Powdered sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat several whole eggs
2. Add as many spoonfuls of flour as there are eggs
3. Salt lightly and thin with a little milk so it is like thick batter
4. Beat well
5. Heat a little clarified butter in a small pan and pour in some of the batter
6. Let it turn golden on the bottom then turn it over
7. Cover the pan and cook on the other side
8. Remove the omelette from the pan
9. Pour a little more melted butter in the pan and repeat until all batter is used
10. Sprinkle the omelettes with powdered sugar and ground cinnamon
11. Fold them up, sprinkle with sugar again and arrange on a plate
