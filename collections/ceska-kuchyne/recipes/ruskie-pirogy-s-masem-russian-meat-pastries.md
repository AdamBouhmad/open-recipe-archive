## Ingredients

- onion
- minced meat from leftover roast
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- dough
- egg
- grated bread roll
- butter

## Directions

1. Brown the onion
2. Add minced meat from leftover roast to the onion
3. Pour in broth, season with pepper and salt
4. Allow to cool
5. Roll out dough as for noodles
6. Sprinkle the minced meat mixture on the dough
7. Cut out round pieces of dough
8. Shape them into four-sided pieces
9. Brush with egg
10. Roll in grated bread roll
11. Fry in butter
