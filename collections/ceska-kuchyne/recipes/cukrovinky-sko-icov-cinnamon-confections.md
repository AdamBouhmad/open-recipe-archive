## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 1/2 kilogram unpeeled almonds, ground or very finely chopped
- 32 grams cinnamon ground to fine powder

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites into stiff snow
2. Add 1/2 kilogram of sugar, 1/2 kilogram of unpeeled ground or very finely chopped almonds, and 32 grams of cinnamon ground to fine powder
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Form into dough, roll out, and cut various confections in different shapes
5. Place on a baking sheet and bake slowly at gentle heat
