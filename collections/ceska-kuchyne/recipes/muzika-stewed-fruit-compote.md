## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams (16 lots) plums
- 28 dekagrams dried apples
- 28 dekagrams dried pears
- a little sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums, dried apples and pears
2. Place on a stove with enough water so the mixture is just submerged
3. Add a little sugar
