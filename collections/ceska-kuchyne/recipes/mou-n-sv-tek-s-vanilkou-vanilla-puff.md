## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 cups milk
- salt
- flour
- 5 egg whites
- butter
- vanilla
- sugar
- 2 cups sour cream

## Directions

1. Put 5 egg yolks, 2 teaspoons sugar, 2 cups milk into a bowl, season lightly, add enough flour to make it thick like batter and mix well
2. Gently fold in snow made from 5 egg whites
3. Spread a piece of butter in a pan, pour the mixture into it and bake in the oven
4. Boil a piece of vanilla with a piece of sugar in 2 cups sour cream and place the cut puff into this sauce
5. Alternatively: whisk 5 egg yolks and 1 teaspoon flour in this sour cream, stir on the stove until it thickens and serve with the puff separately in a bowl
