## Ingredients

- mutton leg
- garlic
- salt
- potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Remove bones, fat and all membranes from the mutton leg.
2. Beat, wash and salt it.
3. Pierce it and fill the holes with garlic.
4. Place on a baking sheet, cover and add water.
5. Braise slowly in the oven, turning until both sides are evenly cooked.
6. Cut into neat pieces and arrange with potatoes.
