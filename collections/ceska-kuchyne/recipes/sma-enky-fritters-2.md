## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one cup milk
- half a teaspoon of salt
- flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- lard

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs, or beat the whites and yolks separately.
2. Add to them one cup of milk, half a teaspoon of salt, and enough flour to make a thin batter, thinner than for pancakes, so that it flows from a spoon.
3. Beat until smooth.
4. Prepare a deep pan and heat good lard in it.
5. Add two teaspoons of baking powder to the batter and mix well.
6. Drop spoonfuls of this batter into the hot lard.
7. When browned on one side, turn over, then remove with a skimmer and place hot on the table.
8. Do not pierce them with a fork, as the steam would escape and they would become heavier.
