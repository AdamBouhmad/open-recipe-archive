## Ingredients

- 500 grams (1 pound) grated cooked potatoes
- 500 grams flour
- 15 grams (1 lot) fennel
- 120 grams (8 lots) sugar
- salt
- 4 grated hard egg yolks
- raw eggs (quantity needed to make stiff dough)

## Directions

1. Mix 500 grams grated cooked potatoes with equal amount of flour
2. Add 15 grams fennel, 120 grams sugar, a pinch of salt, 4 grated hard egg yolks, and enough raw eggs to make stiff dough
3. Form cookies and bake on a waxed sheet or on wafers
