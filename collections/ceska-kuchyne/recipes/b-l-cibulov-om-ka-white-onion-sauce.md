## Ingredients

- several white onions, cut into thin slices
- butter
- white light roux
- meat broth
- sweet cream
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Cut the white onions into thin slices
2. Fry them in butter until soft, without letting them turn yellow
3. Prepare a white light roux, which is cooked with meat broth and sweet cream
4. Add the prepared onions to the mixture
5. Season with salt, pepper and spices
6. Mix everything again and let it simmer
7. Strain the cooked sauce
