## Ingredients

- 4 liters of fresh strawberries
- 1 kilogram of boiled thick sugar

## Directions

1. Pour 4 liters of fresh strawberries into a glazed pot.
2. Pour 1 kilogram of boiled thick sugar over them.
3. Tie the pot and place in a cool place for 3 days.
4. Pour everything onto spread cloth and let the juice drip freely onto a dish.
5. Fill small bottles with the juice, seal well, place briefly in hot water in which they are allowed to cool, then seal well and store in a cool place.
