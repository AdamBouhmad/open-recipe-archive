## Ingredients

- live and well-cleaned shrimps
- butter, hot
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place live, well-cleaned shrimps briefly in boiling water and they are done
2. Alternatively, place in hot butter, keep for a moment, shake gently several times
3. Arrange on a dish and pour butter over them
4. Canned or pickled shrimps should be soaked briefly in fresh water before use
5. Both types can be prepared with butter as described above
