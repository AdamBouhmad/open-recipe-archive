## Ingredients

- nice squabs
- salted water
- butter
- onion
- carrot
- parsley
- salt
- flour
- good beef broth
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon juice
- green asparagus heads

## Directions

1. Gut and rinse the squabs well and let them boil about three minutes in salted water
2. Let them cool and dry well
3. Put a piece of butter on a roasting pan with one onion, carrot, and a piece of parsley
4. Place the squabs in it, salt it and let it braise for several minutes
5. Dust a little flour on it, mix it with good beef broth and let it boil for half an hour, skimming the fat from it
6. When the squabs are tender, remove them to another roasting pan and put them on a warm place so they do not cool
7. Let the sauce reduce well now, thicken it with four yolks
8. Add a spoonful of sugar, a little lemon juice, salt it properly and strain it through a sieve over two small bowls of green asparagus heads which you have previously cooked in salted water
9. Place the squabs on a dish and pour the prepared sauce over them
