## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- beans (half the volume of potatoes)
- soft water
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- fried onion

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut them into pieces, and boil them.
2. Cook the beans in soft water until tender.
3. Mash the beans in their cooking water.
4. Press through a sieve.
5. Mix with the potatoes.
6. Add beef broth, salt, and pepper.
7. Before serving, sprinkle with fried onion.
8. Peas may be used instead of beans.
