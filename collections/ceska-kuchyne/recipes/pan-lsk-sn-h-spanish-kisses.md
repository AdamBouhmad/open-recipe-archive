## Ingredients

- five egg whites
- one pound powdered sugar
- zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Whip five egg whites into stiff snow
2. Slowly mix in one pound powdered sugar
3. Add zest scraped from one lemon
4. Using a small spoon, form oblong mounds on a baking sheet thinly coated with wax
5. Bake in the oven until cooled, nice and crispy, and pale yellow
6. Remove mounds from sheet and arrange on a serving dish
