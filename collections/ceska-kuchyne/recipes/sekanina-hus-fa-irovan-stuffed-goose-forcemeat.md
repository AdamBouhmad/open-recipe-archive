## Ingredients

- leftover goose meat
- bread soaked in milk
- hard-boiled eggs
- flour
- pepper
- garlic
- marjoram
- butter or goose fat
- eggs

## Directions

1. Remove any bones from the leftover goose and chop very finely
2. Add finely chopped bread soaked in milk (according to the amount of leftover meat), finely chopped hard-boiled eggs, flour, pepper, garlic, marjoram, and either butter or goose fat
3. Add raw eggs as needed; mix everything well
4. Place on a baking pan greased with butter or fat and bake
