## Ingredients

- caraway seeds
- salted water
- flour for brown roux
- toasted bread cubes or croutons
- noodles, or drops of batter

## Directions

1. Let a lot of caraway cook in salted water.
2. Meanwhile, make a brown roux, strain the water from the pot into it and let it cook well.
3. Pour into a bowl with toasted bread cubes, or you can add noodles, or drops of batter to it.
