## Ingredients

- bass
- salt
- fresh butter
- lemon juice
- toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the bass, scale it, salt it and let it lie for about an hour; if too large, cut it into pieces.
2. Let plenty of salted water boil on a pan, place the bass in it and let it boil for a quarter hour.
3. Meanwhile mix a good piece of fresh butter with lemon juice and let it heat.
4. When the bass is arranged on a dish and all the water that dripped from it has been removed from the dish, pour this butter over it and sprinkle with toasted bread crumbs.
