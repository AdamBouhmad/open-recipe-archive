## Ingredients

- 25 decagrams (17 lots) almonds
- 12 hard yolks, sifted
- 25 decagrams sugar
- snow from 6 whites

## Directions

1. Blanch and grate almonds
2. Mix with hard yolks passed through sieve, sugar, and snow from whites
3. Bake mixture on floured sheet
4. Brush with preserved fruit
