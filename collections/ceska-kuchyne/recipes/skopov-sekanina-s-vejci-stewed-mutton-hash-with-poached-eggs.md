## Ingredients

- leftover roasted mutton leg with bone
- brown gravy
- beef broth
- salt
- lemon juice
- butter
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. From nice leftover roasted mutton leg, chop the meat with the bone finely and place on a dish.
2. Add to the dish as much brown gravy as needed (similar to what is prepared for calf sweetbreads), then add half as much beef broth as you added gravy.
3. Mix well, season with salt, squeeze lemon juice into it, strain the gravy through a sieve onto the hash, mix well, place pieces of butter on top, pour a little brown broth over it, and set it in a warm place.
4. Just before serving, bring it to a hot fire, stir until the hash is boiling, arrange it on a dish, surround it with poached eggs.
5. Prepare the poached eggs as follows: into boiling water mixed with vinegar, quickly drop fresh eggs, and as soon as they rise to the surface, remove them with a skimmer, sprinkle them with a little pepper and salt, and arrange them around the meat.
