## Ingredients

- coffee cup of large raisins
- water
- vinegar
- sugar
- butter
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take a coffee cup of large raisins from which you have removed the seeds
2. Place in a glazed pot
3. Add a coffee cup of water, 4 tablespoons of vinegar, half a coffee cup of sugar and not quite a tablespoon of butter
4. Lemon peel is also very good
5. Let all this simmer for about 15 minutes
6. Then add a little more...
