## Ingredients

- large, ripe medlars
- soft water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select large, ripe medlars, cut them in half, remove the seeds
2. Blanch in soft water, and when cooled, place on a dish
3. Pour over with cooled dissolved sugar, in which they are allowed to rest quietly until the next day
4. After two days, always pour off the sugar, cook it more concentrated, and when cooled, pour it over the medlars
5. On the last day, cook together, skim off the foam and place the preserve in containers
