## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram calf's liver
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 roll, grated
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled and salted
- marjoram
- dill
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grate the calf's liver and remove the sinews
2. Mix with eggs, egg yolks, grated roll, salted garlic cloves, marjoram, dill, and pepper, and blend everything together
3. Drop the dumplings into boiling beef soup using a coffee spoon
4. Cook until done
5. Add a piece of chives
6. Serve at table
