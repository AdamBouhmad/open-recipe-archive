## Ingredients

- bean pods
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the bean pods into fine noodles
2. Scald with boiling water
3. Allow to drain
4. Dry
5. Salt
6. Pour with cold well water
7. Every fourteen days, clean, turn them over, and add more liquid if necessary
