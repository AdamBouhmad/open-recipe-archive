## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup fine sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 coffee spoon cinnamon
- 1/2 finely chopped lemon zest
- 1/4 pound flour
- almonds cut into noodles
- fine sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 cup fine sugar, 1 whole egg, and 1 egg yolk in a bowl and stir for half an hour
2. Add 1 coffee spoon cinnamon, 1/2 finely chopped lemon zest, and 1/4 pound flour
3. Mix well
4. Coat a sheet with wax, drop small mounds of dough onto it
5. Cover with almonds cut into noodles
6. Sprinkle with fine sugar and bake slowly
