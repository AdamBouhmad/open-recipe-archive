## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, prepared for braising
- pepper
- a piece of celery root
- a piece of parsley root
- a piece of carrot
- new spices
- thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- lemon zest
- 2 juniper berries
- 2 tablespoons good wine vinegar
- 15 gr. (1 lot) butter
- white sugar
- burned sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as for braising in general, add pepper, a piece of celery root, parsley root, a piece of carrot, new spices, thyme, 2 bay leaves, lemon zest, 2 juniper berries, 2 tablespoons good wine vinegar and 15 gr. (1 lot) butter.
2. Braise everything under a lid.
3. When the chicken is tender, place it on a platter and strain the pale broth through a sieve into the sauce.
4. Boil with a piece of white and burned sugar and pour over the sliced chicken.
