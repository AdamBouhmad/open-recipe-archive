## Ingredients

- calf thymus
- salt
- orange
- egg
- grated bread roll
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the thymus and remove all skin, cut into pieces like breaded cutlets
2. Dry, salt, sprinkle with orange zest, dip in beaten egg and coat with grated bread roll
3. Fry in butter until golden
