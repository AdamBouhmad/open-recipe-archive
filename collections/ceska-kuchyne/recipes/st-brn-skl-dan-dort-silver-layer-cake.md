## Ingredients

- less than 1 cup butter
- 1.5 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make dough as described previously.
2. Mix in stiff snow from 8 egg whites.
3. Bake into 4 cakes.
4. When cooled, spread white frosting between them.
5. Cover the top of the cake with white frosting as well.
6. Alternatively, you may use a few drops of almond extract instead of vanilla.
