## Ingredients

- clarified butter
- fish
- bread roll
- egg yolks
- white flour
- lean broth or parsley water
- salt
- dill

## Directions

1. Fry three or four pieces of fish in clarified butter with half a bread roll sliced into strips
2. Cut up the fish, remove the bones and pound them together with the fried bread roll and three hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar
3. Put this into a pot, make a light roux from butter and white flour, dissolve it in lean broth or parsley water, pour it into the pot with the pounded fish
4. Pour in enough broth, salt it, season with dill, let it simmer for a quarter hour and strain through a fine sieve over the fried sliced bread roll
