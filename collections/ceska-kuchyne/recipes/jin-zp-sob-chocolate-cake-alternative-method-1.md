## Ingredients

- 375 grams butter
- 200 grams sugar
- 14 egg yolks
- 200 grams unpeeled almonds
- 200 grams grated chocolate
- 90 grams fine flour
- stiff egg white snow from 14 whites

## Directions

1. Beat 375 grams butter until it foams
2. Slowly add 200 grams sugar
3. Add 14 egg yolks, 200 grams unpeeled almonds, 200 grams grated chocolate, 90 grams fine flour, and stiff egg white snow from 14 whites
4. Mix everything together perfectly
5. Pour into baking forms rubbed with butter
6. Bake at moderate heat in one hour
7. While still warm, coat with glaze
8. Dry again in a heated oven
