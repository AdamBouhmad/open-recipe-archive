## Ingredients

- barley
- hot water
- milk
- salt
- 6 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon zest
- 6 egg whites
- butter
- bread crumbs
- cinnamon
- parsley
- pork cracklings

## Directions

1. Pick through barley, wash and scald with hot water, drain water
2. Place barley on a baking sheet, pour sufficient milk over it, salt and make a thick porridge
3. When cooked, let cool, mix in 6 egg yolks, sugar as needed, a little chopped lemon zest and stiff snow from 6 egg whites
4. Mix everything together, grease baking sheet with butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs, place barley porridge in sheet and bake
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Green parsley and pork cracklings can be added and mixed into the barley porridge
