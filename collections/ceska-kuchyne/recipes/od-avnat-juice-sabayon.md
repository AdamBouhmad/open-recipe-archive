## Ingredients

- three deciliters strawberry or raspberry juice or similar
- lemon juice
- 15 decagrams sugar
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add a little lemon juice to three deciliters of strawberry, raspberry, or similar juice
2. Add 15 decagrams of sugar and six egg yolks
3. Beat thoroughly and heat until it rises from the pot
