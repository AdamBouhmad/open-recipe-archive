## Ingredients

- large, red, ripe raspberries
- sugar (equal amount to raspberries)

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, large, red raspberries before they become overripe
2. Boil an equal amount of sugar to the sixth degree
3. When boiling, add the raspberries and cook several times together
4. Skim the foam from the surface and pour all onto a dish to cool
5. The next day, strain everything through a fine sieve
6. Pour off the sugar and place raspberries in jars
7. Add a little currant juice to the sugar, boil until thickened, and when warm pour over the raspberries
8. Only after cooling, seal and store in a dry, cool place
