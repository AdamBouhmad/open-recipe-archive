## Ingredients

- butter
- sour cream (one cup)
- eggs (six whole)
- butter (finely chopped)

## Directions

1. Grease a baking dish with butter
2. Pour one cup of sour cream into it
3. Break six whole eggs into it
4. Sprinkle with a little finely chopped butter on top
5. Bake in the oven until they set somewhat
6. Serve immediately at the table
