## Ingredients

- lamb heart and lungs
- onion
- carrot
- sprigs of thyme
- fresh parsley
- bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- vinegar
- oil
- butter
- flour
- broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Clean the lamb heart and lungs, cut into pieces
2. Place in an earthenware pot with sliced onion and carrot, sprigs of thyme and fresh parsley, several bay leaves, a little salt, pepper, cloves, vinegar and oil
3. Let stand for twelve hours, turning frequently
4. Melt a piece of butter in a pan, add the pluck, let stew for a while
5. Dust with flour
6. Add equal parts broth and wine
7. Let cook until tender
8. Remove the pluck onto a plate and pour the strained sauce over it
9. Can also be prepared like veal
