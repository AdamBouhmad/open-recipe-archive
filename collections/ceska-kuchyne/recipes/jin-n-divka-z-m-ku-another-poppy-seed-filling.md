## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 small cup cooked ground poppy seed, or poppy seed cooked in cream and well rubbed
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 3 or 4 tablespoons cream
- 1/2 small cup seedless raisins
- cinnamon, finely ground, to the point of a knife
- vanilla, to the point of a knife
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub 6 tablespoons of sugar with three egg yolks in a bowl
2. Add to it a small cup of either cooked ground poppy seed, or poppy seed cooked in cream and well rubbed
3. Add 3 tablespoons melted butter, 3 or 4 tablespoons cream, and 1/2 small cup seedless raisins
4. Add cinnamon finely ground and vanilla to the point of a knife
5. Mix well
6. Fold in stiff snow (beaten egg whites) made from 3 egg whites
