## Ingredients

- Pork intestines
- Pork head
- Pork brain
- Belly meat
- Neck meat
- Lungs
- Heart
- Liver
- Pepper
- Marjoram
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Bread or breadcrumbs soaked in water
- Pork blood
- Millet groats
- Butter
- Onion

## Directions

1. Rinse pork intestines many times in cold water, turn inside out, and scrape well inside with a blunt knife
2. Salt each piece well, turn again, and rinse with water
3. For immediate use, keep in cold water; for later use, tie the end, inflate, and dry
4. If making liver sausages at home, cut the head in half, remove the brain, and cook the head with pieces of belly, neck, lungs, heart, and a piece of liver
5. When cooked, remove all bones and finely chop the meat
6. Mix with pepper, marjoram, cloves, grated ginger, and chopped garlic
7. Add some bread or breadcrumbs soaked in water for each portion of chopped meat
8. Thin with meat broth if needed, mix and stuff into intestines
9. Drop into boiling broth and cook carefully so they do not burst, occasionally adding cold water
10. Remove with a skimmer and arrange side by side
11. For another version, prepare intestines with a mixture of breadcrumbs, blood, and millet groats, seasoned with fried onion instead of the spices used for liver sausages
