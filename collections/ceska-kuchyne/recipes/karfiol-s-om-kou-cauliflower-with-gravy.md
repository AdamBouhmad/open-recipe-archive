## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- butter
- beef broth
- lemon juice
- parsley
- nutmeg
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook cleaned cauliflower in salted water and when done, remove with a skimming spoon to a dish
2. Pour butter sauce over it mixed with juice of one lemon and a little cauliflower water
3. To prepare the sauce: make a good butter roux, mix with beef broth in the desired amount, add a little parsley and nutmeg, let the sauce simmer and pour over prepared cauliflower
4. Alternatively: cook cauliflower in salted water and when almost done, drain the water and pour a little good milk over it to reach about the same level as the cauliflower, add butter and pepper, and finish cooking
5. Before removing from heat, thicken with a little flour roux mixed in milk
