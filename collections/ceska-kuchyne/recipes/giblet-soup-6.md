## Ingredients

- goose giblets (excluding liver)
- water
- vegetables of all kinds
- salt
- dill
- butter (if goose was lean)
- rice or thin noodles

## Directions

1. Clean goose giblets except liver
2. Chop into small pieces
3. Rinse and place in water (for giblets from one goose use 1.5 liters)
4. Add vegetables of all kinds, salt, and dill
5. If the goose was lean, add a piece of butter and simmer briefly
6. Meanwhile, braise rice in butter and pour the giblet soup over it, or make thin noodles
7. In the latter case, strain the soup and add thickening
8. If there are mushrooms or cauliflower, add them with parsley
