## Ingredients

- pigeons, cleaned
- pigeon breasts and thighs
- remaining pigeon meat with bones
- pigeon livers
- piece of smoked bacon
- egg yolk
- bread
- butter
- spice for game sauce

## Directions

1. From cleaned pigeons, cut out the breasts and thighs; scrape the remaining meat from the bones
2. Finely chop the remaining meat with bones, livers, and a piece of smoked bacon
3. Crush this mixture in a mortar with a raw egg yolk and bread, then press through a sieve
4. Spread the mixture on the thighs; lard the breasts with bacon, halve them, remove the bones, and cut off the wing tips
5. Place everything in butter on a baking pan, cover with buttered paper and a lid, braise it
6. Finally place into a sauce prepared as for game
