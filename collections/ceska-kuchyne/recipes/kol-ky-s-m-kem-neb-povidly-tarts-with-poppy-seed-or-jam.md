## Ingredients

- dough (prepared according to previous recipe)
- egg
- jam or poppy seed filling
- blanched almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare dough according to the previous recipe
2. Form into tarts
3. Brush the edge of each tart with beaten egg
4. Fill with prepared jam or poppy seed filling
5. Place one whole almond in the center of each tart and arrange sliced almonds around it in a star pattern
6. Bake in the oven
7. Turn out onto a work surface or rolling pin
8. Dust with sugar
9. Arrange cooled tarts on a shallow plate
