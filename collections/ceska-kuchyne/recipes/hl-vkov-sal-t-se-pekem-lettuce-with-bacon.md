## Ingredients

- lettuce
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- sugar
- bacon

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lettuce, squeeze it out, arrange on a dish and salt it
2. Mix water with vinegar according to taste and add a little sugar, pour this over the lettuce and mix
3. Meanwhile, render bacon cut into small cubes, when rendered pour it over the lettuce and serve immediately at table
