## Ingredients

- Cabbage head
- Butter
- Sugar
- Beef broth
- Caraway
- Pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop or shred the cabbage head and place on a baking sheet
2. Mix with butter, sugar, beef broth, caraway, pepper and vinegar, stirring so that everything becomes equally soft
