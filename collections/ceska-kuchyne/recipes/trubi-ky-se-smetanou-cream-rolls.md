## Ingredients

- butter dough
- egg for brushing
- whipped snow with sugar or whipped cream with vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough to the thickness of straw
2. Cut into long strips
3. Place on a tube mold, put on baking sheet
4. Brush surface with beaten egg and bake quickly in a hot oven
5. When cooled, fill with either whipped snow mixed with sugar or whipped cream with vanilla sugar
