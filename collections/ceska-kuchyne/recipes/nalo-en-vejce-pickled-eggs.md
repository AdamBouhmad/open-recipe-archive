## Ingredients

- Two dozen eggs
- Two cups of vinegar
- Six to eight peppercorns
- A piece of ginger
- About four cloves
- A slice of garlic

## Directions

1. Boil two dozen eggs until hard and peel them carefully without breaking them
2. Place them in a stone jar
3. Take two cups of vinegar and add six to eight peppercorns, a piece of ginger, about four cloves, and a slice of garlic
4. Boil this mixture for about five minutes
5. Pour the boiling mixture over the eggs
6. Store in a cool place
7. Use after two weeks
