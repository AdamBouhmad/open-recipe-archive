## Ingredients

- butter
- grated white flour
- sliced apples
- powdered sugar
- chopped almonds
- 2.5 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- lemon or vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Butter a mold and line it with grated white flour
2. Layer it with sliced apples
3. Sprinkle them with powdered sugar and finely chopped almonds
4. Continue layering apples and sprinkling until the mold is half full
5. Measure 2.5 cups flour and 1 cup sugar
6. Beat 4 eggs on a dish and beat them with the sugar for half an hour
7. Gently mix in the flour
8. Add lemon or vanilla flavoring
9. Pour over the apples and bake slowly for one hour
