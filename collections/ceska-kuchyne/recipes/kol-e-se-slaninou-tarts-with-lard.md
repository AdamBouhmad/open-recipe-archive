## Ingredients

- butter dough
- egg
- salt
- caraway seeds
- lard cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Spread butter dough about half centimeter thick
2. Brush with egg
3. Sprinkle with salt and caraway seeds
4. Border with lard cut into cubes
5. Bake until golden
