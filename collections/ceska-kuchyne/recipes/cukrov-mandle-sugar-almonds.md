## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (quarter pound) almonds
- equal amount flour
- equal amount sugar
- a little cinnamon
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the almonds
2. Mix flour, sugar, and cinnamon with 4 egg yolks to make a thin batter
3. Coat each almond individually in the batter
4. Dust with flour
5. Dry one beside another on a baking sheet in an oven
