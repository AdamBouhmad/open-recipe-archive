## Ingredients

- thirteen dekagrams crushed sugar
- six raw egg yolks
- clove
- cinnamon
- sliced lemon
- flour
- sugar dissolved in water
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Mix thirteen dekagrams crushed sugar with six raw egg yolks for half an hour, add clove, cinnamon, sliced lemon, and enough flour to make a thick dough.
2. Shape into a round loaf, brush with sugar dissolved in water, sprinkle with grated chocolate.
3. When baked, slice the loaf, brush with lemon icing, and dry in the oven.
