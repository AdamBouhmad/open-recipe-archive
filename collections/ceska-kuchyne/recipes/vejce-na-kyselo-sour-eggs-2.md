## Ingredients

- Fresh butter
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Good milk
- Pinch of flower (herb)
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of fresh butter in a saucepan
2. Let a large spoonful of flour brown on it
3. Add several spoonfuls of vinegar and mix
4. Gradually pour in good milk while constantly stirring until a thickened sauce forms
5. When the sauce is cooking, add a pinch of herb
6. Quickly drop eggs one by one into the sauce
7. Let them set in the sauce and serve immediately
