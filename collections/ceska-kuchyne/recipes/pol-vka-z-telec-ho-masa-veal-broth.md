## Ingredients

- veal from the neck
- 35 grams butter
- parsnip root
- parsley
- carrot
- cauliflower
- salt
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place veal in a roasting pan or low pot
2. Add butter, parsnip root, parsley, carrot, and a little cauliflower
3. Season with salt
4. Braise until tender
5. Strain the broth
6. Beat the egg yolks and add to the broth (1 egg yolk per cup of broth)
