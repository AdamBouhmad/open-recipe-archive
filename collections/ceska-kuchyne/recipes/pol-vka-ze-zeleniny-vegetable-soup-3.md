## Ingredients

- 3 carrots
- 4 small onions
- 4 small potatoes
- 2 cups chopped cabbage
- 1 large tomato
- A handful of young green peas
- Water
- 2 cups milk
- 1 spoon butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- A little flour
- A pinch of baking soda

## Directions

1. Chop all vegetables finely on a wooden board
2. Place in a pot, add just enough water to cover
3. Stew until vegetables are soft
4. Add boiling water and cook for 15 minutes
5. Add 2 cups milk and 1 spoon of butter
6. Add 2 beaten eggs
7. Mix in a little flour and a pinch of baking soda
8. Allow to simmer for a few more minutes
