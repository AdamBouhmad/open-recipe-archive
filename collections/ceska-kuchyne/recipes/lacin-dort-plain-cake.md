## Ingredients

- one full spoonful of butter
- one coffee cup of sugar
- two whole eggs
- two and a half coffee cups of flour
- one coffee cup of milk
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- vanilla or lemon to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the butter well with the sugar
2. add the two eggs and mix well
3. add the flour, milk, baking powder, and vanilla or lemon to taste
4. bake in a pan or on parchment plates
5. make a layered cake and fill with some fruit syrup
