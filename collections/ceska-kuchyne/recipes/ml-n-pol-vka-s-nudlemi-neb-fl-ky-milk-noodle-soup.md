## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of milk
- noodles or noodle strips made from 1 egg
- a piece of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Let 2 quarts of milk cook, but be careful that it does not burn.
2. As it cooks, add noodles or noodle strips made from one egg, add a piece of butter and salt, and let it simmer for a minute.
