## Ingredients

- preserved cherries
- crushed sugar
- gum dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Take preserved cherries, squeeze them in your hands and shape nicely round
2. Place them in crushed sugar in which they become well coated
3. Place on a sieve so they do not touch each other
4. Let them dry
5. Dampen the dried cherries with dissolved gum
6. Dust again with finely crushed sugar
7. Let dry on warm stoves
8. After a few hours when completely dry, repeat everything described here again, which can be repeated several times depending on how heavily sugared the cherries should be
