## Ingredients

- kilogram peeled potatoes
- 6 dekagrams butter
- 4 tablespoons hot milk
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook kilogram peeled potatoes and press through a ricer
2. Mix in 6 dekagrams butter, 4 tablespoons hot milk, pepper and salt
3. Press everything through a sieve while moving it so the individual pieces look like groats
4. Add the hot potato groats to fried dishes
