## Ingredients

- medium-sized boiled potatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- sour cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel medium-sized boiled warm potatoes and slice them into not too thin slices
2. Place on a dish and mix in finely chopped onion, salt and pepper then pour the following sauce over: Boil 3 spoonfuls of vinegar, a spoonful of water and a spoonful of sugar
3. Take 2 cups of good thick sour cream, beat three egg yolks into it and pour into the boiling vinegar
4. When it begins to rise, remove from heat and when cooled, pour this over the salad
