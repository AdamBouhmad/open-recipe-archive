## Ingredients

- two-day-old white or rye bread
- meat, tongue, or cheese

## Directions

1. A sandwich consists of two thin slices of bread with a thin slice of meat, tongue, cheese, etc. between them.
2. It is best to use two-day-old bread without holes, either white or rye bread, cut into equal slices about a quarter inch thick.
3. The meat must be tender, juicy, well-roasted or cooked, and cut into thin, equal slices.
4. The edges of the sandwiches should be even and the contents should not protrude.
5. If the sandwiches become dry before serving, dampen a cloth in cold water, wring it out well, cover the sandwiches with it, or wrap each sandwich in thin waxed paper.
6. Mustard is served with sandwiches.
