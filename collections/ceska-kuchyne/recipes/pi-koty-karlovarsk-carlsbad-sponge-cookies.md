## Ingredients

- 20 eggs
- 750 grams beaten sugar
- flour (amount not specified)
- lemon zest, finely grated

## Directions

1. Break 20 eggs into a bowl
2. Beat as if making snow from them
3. Add 750 grams beaten sugar and mix
4. Heat several times and let cool again, beating constantly throughout
5. Slowly add flour and finely chopped lemon zest
6. Proceed as with other cookies
