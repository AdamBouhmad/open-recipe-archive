## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth
- Finely sliced champignons (mushrooms)
- Lemon sauce
- 4 egg yolks
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter and flour and add to meat broth
2. Cook finely sliced mushrooms in butter, broth, and lemon sauce
3. Pour everything into the meat broth into which 4 egg yolks have been beaten and mixed
4. Serve with bread cubes cut and fried
