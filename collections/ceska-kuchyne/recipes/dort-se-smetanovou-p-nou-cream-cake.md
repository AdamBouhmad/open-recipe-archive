## Ingredients

- shortcrust pastry
- beaten egg
- butter
- strawberries or other fruit
- sugar
- cream foam
- cream (alternative filling)

## Directions

1. Make shortcrust pastry as in the previous recipe
2. Roll it out to the thickness of a finger
3. Place it on clean paper
4. Cut a round cake using an inverted plate
5. Brush the edges with beaten egg
6. Roll out the remaining dough to two fingers wide and make a border around the round pastry
7. Press the lower edges together
8. Pierce the pastry several times with a small knife to prevent it from puffing up
9. Place on a pan with the paper and bake until golden
10. Let cool completely in a cool place
11. Shortly before serving, fill the center with strawberries or other fruit
12. Sprinkle with sugar and cover with cream foam
13. Alternatively, fill with cream alone and place briefly in the oven to set the cream
