## Ingredients

- Smelts
- butter
- parsley butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the smelts as in the previous method
2. Dry them with a cloth
3. Salt and pepper them
4. Brush with softened butter
5. Broil either on a double grill or on a baking sheet
6. Arrange on a dish, pour melted parsley butter over them, and surround with sliced lemon
