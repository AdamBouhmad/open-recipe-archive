## Ingredients

- truffles
- strong broth or wine

## Directions

1. Clean and peel truffles
2. Slice into thin slices
3. Braise in strong broth or wine until soft
4. Meanwhile prepare a tasty wine sauce
5. Pour the wine sauce over the truffles
