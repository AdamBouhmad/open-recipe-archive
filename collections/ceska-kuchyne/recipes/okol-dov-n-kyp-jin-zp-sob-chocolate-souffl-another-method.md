## Ingredients

- half a liter of milk
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) chocolate
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 19 dekagrams (almost 13 lots) sugar
- 2 tablespoons grated bread roll
- a little ground vanilla
- 8 egg whites
- sponge cake (pishkotem) for coating

## Directions

1. Cook 24 dekagrams (16 lots) of chocolate in half a liter of milk, stirring continuously, and let it cool
2. Beat 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of butter until it froths
3. Add 19 dekagrams (almost 13 lots) of sugar, the cooled chocolate, 2 tablespoons of grated bread roll, and a little ground vanilla
4. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 8 egg whites
5. Bake in a mold coated with sponge cake and sprinkle the baked soufflé with sugar
