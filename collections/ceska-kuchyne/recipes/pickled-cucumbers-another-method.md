## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Cold water
- Good vinegar
- Water
- Cherry or grape leaves
- Dill
- Ginger
- Allspice
- Horseradish, sliced into cakes
- Stone for pressing
- Wood or clay container

## Directions

1. Trim the cucumbers at both ends and soak in cold water for two hours
2. Meanwhile, boil good vinegar with water
3. In a container (preferably wooden or clay), place cherry or grape leaves and dill at the bottom
4. Add a layer of cucumbers, salt them, sprinkle with ginger, allspice, sliced horseradish cakes, and add leaves again
5. When the container is full, pour the boiled warm vinegar mixed with water over the cucumbers
6. Weight down the cucumbers with a board and stone
7. The pickled cucumbers can be removed on the third day
8. If desired, add a few pieces of paprika
