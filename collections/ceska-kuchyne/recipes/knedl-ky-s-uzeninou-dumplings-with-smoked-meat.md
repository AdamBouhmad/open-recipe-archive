## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked smoked meat, minced fine
- 3 slices bread cut into cubes and fried in butter
- 3 slices bread soaked in milk
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 cups good milk
- salt
- flour (as much as needed)

## Directions

1. Chop fine 1 pound of cooked smoked meat
2. Add to it 3 slices of bread cut into cubes and fried in butter and 3 slices of bread soaked in milk
3. Beat 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into it
4. Add 2 cups of good milk, salt and mix well
5. Then add as much flour as needed to make thick dough, which you knead with a spoon
6. Make dumplings about the size of an apple and put them in boiling water; then stir them loosely at the bottom of the pot so they rise to the surface and let boil for half an hour
7. Take them out onto a plate, tear them in two with two forks, sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread crust and pour fresh melted butter over them
