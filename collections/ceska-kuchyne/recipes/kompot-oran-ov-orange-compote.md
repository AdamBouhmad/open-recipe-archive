## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and finely trim the peel
2. Separate the orange segments from one another
3. Arrange on a plate
4. When ready to serve, sprinkle with crushed sugar or pour over a thickly boiled sugar syrup and allow to cool
