## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rub almonds thoroughly with cloth and split
2. Place in a container on hot stove
3. Pour with boiled sugar to which lemon juice has been added
4. Dry on hot stove until whitened
