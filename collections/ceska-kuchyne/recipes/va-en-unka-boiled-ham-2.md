## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Place a well-soaked and washed ham in a large pot, add enough water to cover it and let it cook until tender, which depending on its size may take 2 to 3½ hours.
2. If it is very salty, drain the water when it is half-cooked and pour fresh boiling water over it.
3. Turn it several times during cooking and add fresh boiling water.
4. Remove the skin while hot, sprinkle it with marjoram and ground pepper, cover it again with skin and let it cool.
