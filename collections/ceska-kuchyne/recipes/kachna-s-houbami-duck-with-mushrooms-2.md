## Ingredients

- cleaned and plucked duck
- rendered lard or bacon
- sliced onion
- peppercorns
- strong broth
- two or three dozen small peeled mushroom heads or other fresh mushrooms
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned and plucked duck in a pot on rendered lard or bacon
2. Add sliced onion and a few peppercorns and let it braise for a while
3. Pour several spoonfuls of strong broth over the duck
4. Cover the pot with a lid and let it braise again, turning the duck at least once
5. When the duck is more than halfway done, add two or three dozen small peeled mushroom heads or other fresh mushrooms
6. Let everything braise until tender
7. Remove the duck to a dish and surround it with mushroom heads
8. Strain the sauce, mix it with a little tomato sauce and pour it over the duck
