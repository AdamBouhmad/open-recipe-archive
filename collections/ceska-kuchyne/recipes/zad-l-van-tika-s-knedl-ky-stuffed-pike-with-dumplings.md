## Ingredients

- pike
- onion
- asparagus
- broth
- butter
- pike meat
- pike innards
- soaked bread
- crayfish
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Clean the pike and cut into slices
2. Braise with onion, asparagus, broth and butter
3. Chop some pike meat and mix with butter, pike innards, and soaked bread
4. Mix thoroughly until a thick paste forms
5. Pound the mixture in a mortar and form dumplings
6. Fry half the dumplings and boil the other half in the sauce
7. Place dumplings in the center of a dish with pike around them
8. Decorate with crayfish and place a cauliflower rose in the center
9. Note: Chicken can be prepared the same way and the dumplings can be made from veal
