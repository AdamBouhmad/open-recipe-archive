## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- flour
- strong bouillon
- onion
- thyme
- parsley
- lemon rind
- varied spices
- egg yolks
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Kill the squabs one to two days before preparation, pluck, clean, wash and quarter them
2. With the hearts, gizzards and livers, braise slowly in boiling butter for about ten minutes
3. Salt the pigeons, dust with flour and pour strong bouillon (clear good broth) over them, to which onion, thyme, parsley, lemon rind and varied spices are added
4. Simmer and braise everything slowly
5. Remove the pigeons, strain the sauce, beat in several egg yolks, a little white wine, simmer all, add lemon juice
