## Ingredients

- Egg, 1 whole
- Water, 1 koflíčku
- Flour, sufficient amount
- Salt
- Cottage cheese, about 3/4 pound
- Sugar to taste
- Eggs, 2
- Raisins, small, a little
- Bitter almonds, a few, grated
- Breadcrumb crust or white bread crust
- Butter, hot

## Directions

1. Make a nice thick dough from 1 egg, 1 koflíčku water, sufficient flour, and salt
2. Let it rest for a while
3. Meanwhile, prepare the filling: sweeten about 3/4 pound cottage cheese well
4. Add 2 eggs, a little small raisins, and a few grated bitter almonds
5. Mix well
6. Roll out the dough thin as for noodles
7. Spread the cheese filling evenly over it
8. Roll it up like a roller and cut into pieces about four fingers wide, pressing well at both ends with a spoon handle
9. Drop into plenty of boiling salted water
10. When cooked, sprinkle with breadcrumb crust or white bread crust and pour hot butter over them
