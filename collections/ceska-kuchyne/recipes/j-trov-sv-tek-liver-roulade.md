## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram liver
- 3 eggs
- 2 cloves garlic
- a little grated marjoram
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated bread roll
- butter
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Grate the liver and add 3 eggs, 2 cloves garlic, a little grated marjoram, salt, nutmeg and grated bread roll until a batter forms
2. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with grated bread roll, place the mixture in it and bake
3. Cut into cubes, place in a soup bowl and pour over with beef broth
