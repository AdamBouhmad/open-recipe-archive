## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters water
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- Zest and juice of 3 lemons
- 2 bottles white wine
- 0.75 liters arrack
- 0.5 liters tea

## Directions

1. Boil 0.5 kilogram of sugar with lemon zest and juice in 0.5 liters of water until thickened
2. When cooled, add 2 bottles of white wine and 0.75 liters of arrack
3. Add 0.5 liters of tea
4. Heat before serving
