## Ingredients

- Cow udder
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Bread roll
- Butter
- Salads or sardine or mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the udder and cook it, allow to cool
2. Slice into rounds, salt and pepper
3. Dip in beaten egg on all sides
4. Coat in grated bread roll and fry in butter
5. Serve with various salads or sardine or mustard sauce
