## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 1/4 kilogram flour
- 1/4 kilogram potatoes
- 1/4 kilogram sugar
- 6 hard, cooled egg yolks
- lemon zest from one lemon
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 1.5 deciliters (1/2 žejdlík) wine
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Cook, cool and grate potatoes
2. Mix 1/4 kilogram flour, 1/4 kilogram grated potatoes, 1/4 kilogram sugar
3. Grate 6 hard cooled egg yolks and mix in lemon zest
4. In a separate bowl, beat 9 dekagrams butter
5. Add 6 whole eggs and 1.5 deciliters wine
6. Mix everything together
7. Place on a greased baking pan and bake slowly
