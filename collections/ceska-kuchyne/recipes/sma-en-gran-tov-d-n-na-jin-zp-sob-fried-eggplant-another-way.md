## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cook the eggplant until soft, cut it open and scrape out the flesh from inside.
2. Salt and pepper it; take some of it on a spoon, coat with beaten egg and roll in breadcrumbs and fry in hot lard.
