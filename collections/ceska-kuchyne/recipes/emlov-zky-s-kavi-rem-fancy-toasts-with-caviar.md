## Ingredients

- Round fried bread slices
- Fresh butter
- Caviar
- Lemon juice
- Oil
- Onion

## Directions

1. Take round fried bread slices
2. Make a border of fresh butter
3. Spread the middle with caviar mixed with lemon juice and a few drops of oil
4. Sprinkle the caviar with finely chopped onion
