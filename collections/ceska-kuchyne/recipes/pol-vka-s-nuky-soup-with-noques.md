## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- four egg yolks
- nutmeg
- salt
- four egg whites
- eight tablespoons of flour

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of butter and mix four egg yolks into it one by one
2. Season with nutmeg and salt
3. Make stiff snow from four egg whites and add to the yolks with eight tablespoons of flour, mixing so that always a tablespoon of flour and a piece of snow are well blended in, alternating
4. Then let the dough rest for a quarter hour
5. Then cook one noques in the broth as a test, and if it is too soft, add a little flour
6. Let them cook for ten minutes and then you must serve the broth immediately so the noques don't lose their best flavor
