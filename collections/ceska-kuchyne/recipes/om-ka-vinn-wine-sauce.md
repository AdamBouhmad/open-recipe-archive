## Ingredients

- half liter white wine
- quarter liter vinegar and water
- sugar
- eighth liter good sour cream
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix half liter white wine with quarter liter vinegar and water
2. Cook for fifteen minutes
3. Add sugar and one eighth liter of good sour cream
4. Mix three egg yolks and cook for a short time
