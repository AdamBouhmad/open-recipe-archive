## Ingredients

- small potatoes
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Cook smaller potatoes until soft and let them cool, or use cold leftover potatoes
2. Peel and cut them into slices
3. Place a spoon of good lard in a skillet and let it heat
4. When quite hot, add the potatoes
5. Salt and pepper them and toss them with a knife until they turn reddish
6. Place on a plate and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley
