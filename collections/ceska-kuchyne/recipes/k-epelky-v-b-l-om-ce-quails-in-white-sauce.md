## Ingredients

- quails
- onion
- carrot pieces
- butter (15 grams per quail)
- broth
- salt
- lemon (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare quails as for roasting; cut them into pieces or leave whole.
2. Place quails in a pot with chopped onion, carrot pieces, and butter (15 grams per quail).
3. Add a little broth and salt.
4. Braise until they turn red.
5. Make a pale roux and strain it into the sauce from which the quails were removed.
6. Boil briefly, add the quails back to the sauce.
7. Add a piece of butter and optionally squeeze lemon juice into the sauce.
