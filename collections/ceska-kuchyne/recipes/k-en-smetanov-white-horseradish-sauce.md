## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- white flour
- blanched and well-pounded almonds
- powdered sugar
- lukewarm cream
- horseradish
- salt

## Directions

1. Rub a piece of fresh butter with a little white flour in a small pot
2. Add several peeled and well-pounded almonds and half a teaspoon of powdered sugar
3. Pour over it somewhat more than 1 cupful of lukewarm cream
4. Mix it well, place it on the fire and let it cook a little while stirring slowly constantly
5. When it has thickened sufficiently, add grated horseradish and a small pinch of salt
6. Mix well and serve
