## Ingredients

- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter
- flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 1/2 deciliters (1 žejdlík) of good smetana (sour cream)
- more flour as needed
- 7 1/2 deciliters (5 žejdlíků) of milk
- flour
- a piece of fresh butter
- a piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix butter with flour and 4 egg yolks
2. Rub together
3. Add smetana and enough flour to make a stiff dough that does not stick to the rolling pin
4. Put milk, flour, butter and sugar in a baking pan
5. When milk boils, dip a spoon in milk, take dough with it and throw into milk
6. Serve the cooked dumplings in milk
