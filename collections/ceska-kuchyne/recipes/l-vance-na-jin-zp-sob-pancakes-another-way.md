## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- two small cups sweet milk
- one teaspoon melted butter
- one cup flour
- one heaping teaspoon baking powder
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Put six egg yolks in a bowl and mix two small cups of sweet milk with them
2. Add one teaspoon of melted butter
3. Mix one heaping teaspoon of baking powder into one cup of flour, sift into the milk and mix nicely
4. Beat the six egg whites into snow and gradually mix into the batter
5. Quickly bake small pancakes on a lard-greased pan
