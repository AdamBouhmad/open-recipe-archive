## Ingredients

- two handfuls of clean picked parsley
- three or five egg yolks
- lukewarm wine
- cloves
- ginger
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pound two handfuls of clean picked parsley in a mortar
2. Beat in three or five egg yolks and mix well together
3. Pour lukewarm wine over this, but not too much so it is not too thin
4. Strain through cloth
5. Season with cloves, ginger, pepper and nutmeg
6. Allow to simmer slowly, stirring constantly so the sauce becomes a nice color
