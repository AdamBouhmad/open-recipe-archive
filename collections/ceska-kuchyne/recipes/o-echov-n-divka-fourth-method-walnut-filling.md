## Ingredients

- approximately 14 dek sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 25 dek finely shredded peeled walnuts
- vanilla
- lemon zest
- 4 tablespoons rum
- approximately 5 dek raisins

## Directions

1. Boil sugar in water until completely thick
2. Add finely shredded peeled walnuts
3. Add crushed vanilla
4. Add finely shredded or chopped lemon zest
5. Add rum and raisins
