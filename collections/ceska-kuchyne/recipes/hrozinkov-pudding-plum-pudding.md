## Ingredients

- one cup finely chopped suet from kidneys
- one cup sugar
- one cup raisins without seeds
- one cup washed dried currants
- one cup candied citron
- one cup flour
- half pound grated white bread
- four eggs
- four tablespoons brandy
- half teaspoon allspice
- half teaspoon cloves
- one cup milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix one cup finely chopped suet from kidneys, one cup sugar, one cup raisins without seeds, one cup washed dried currants, one cup candied citron, one cup flour, and half pound grated white bread together.
2. Add four eggs, four tablespoons brandy, half teaspoon allspice, and half teaspoon cloves.
3. Add one cup milk with a little salt mixed in.
4. Place everything in a cloth sprinkled with flour and tie it loosely.
5. Boil it; it will rise during cooking.
6. Let it cook for one and a half to two hours.
