## Ingredients

- 195 grams (13 lots) butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 195 grams wheat flour
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) potato flour
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) raisins
- zest of lemon, grated
- snow from 6 egg whites
- glaze

## Directions

1. Whip the butter until foamy.
2. Gradually mix in the egg yolks, wheat flour, potato flour, raisins, grated lemon zest, and snow from 6 egg whites.
3. Either pour the mixture into paper molds or spread it thinly on a greased baking sheet.
4. Bake slowly until done.
5. When cooled, cut into oblong slices and coat with glaze.
