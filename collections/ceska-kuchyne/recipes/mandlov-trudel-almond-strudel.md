## Ingredients

- half kilogram fine flour
- 1 egg
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) butter
- pinch of salt
- half cup lukewarm water
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) peeled ground almonds
- 4 egg whites
- lemon zest
- sweet sour cream
- egg white snow

## Directions

1. Make dough on a floured board from flour, 1 egg, 3 dekagrams butter, salt and half cup lukewarm water
2. Work the dough until bubbles form
3. Cover with a warm bowl and let rest for an hour
4. Spread cloth on table, flour it, lightly roll out dough, then stretch by hand on all sides as thinly as possible without tearing
5. Prepare filling: grate 6 dekagrams butter; gradually add 9 dekagrams sugar, 9 dekagrams peeled ground almonds, 4 egg whites, chopped lemon zest, several spoonfuls sweet sour cream and snow from egg whites
6. Spread filling on dough, slowly roll up strudel and bake on greased pan
7. Brush surface with sour cream and pour rest into pan
8. Bake slowly with gentle heat, baste with milk or sour cream during baking to prevent burning
9. Serve sprinkled with sugar
