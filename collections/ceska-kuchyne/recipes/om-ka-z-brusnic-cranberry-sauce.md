## Ingredients

- canned cranberries
- water
- wine
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook canned cranberries in water with wine, sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest, and nutmeg
2. Strain and thicken with flour
