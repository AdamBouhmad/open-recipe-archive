## Ingredients

- 1.5 quarts of good milk
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- salt
- 3 egg yolks
- a little cold milk
- a piece of crushed sugar
- lemon zest
- bread crust
- finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1.5 quarts of good milk in a pot and let it cook, add a tablespoon of sugar and salt it lightly.
2. In another pot, beat 3 egg yolks with a little cold milk, add a piece of crushed sugar mixed with lemon zest.
3. Pour this into a soup tureen and add the cooked milk to it, stirring as you pour.
4. Cut bread crust into cubes, place on a baking sheet, sprinkle generously with finely crushed sugar, put in the oven and let it bake.
5. Put the bread cubes on a plate and serve separately with the soup.
