## Ingredients

- beef broth
- raw ham
- spices
- wine
- butter
- lemon juice
- mushrooms
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Simmer broth made from beef, raw ham and spices
2. Mix with a glass of wine
3. Add butter and lemon juice
4. Add mushrooms cooked until soft
5. Simmer all together
6. Strain
7. Add three to four egg yolks
