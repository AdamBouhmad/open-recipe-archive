## Ingredients

- well-developed but unripe walnuts
- fresh water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Select well-developed but unripe walnuts
2. Wash them and cut off the tips from top and bottom
3. Pierce them three to four times completely through
4. Place in fresh water and soak for fourteen days, changing the water daily
5. Then squeeze them by hand until soft
6. Place softened walnuts in fresh water and let sit quietly
7. On the second day, pour off the water, pour purified, very thin sugar over them and let sit quietly overnight
8. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, let it cool, and pour back over the walnuts
9. Repeat this for fourteen days until the sugar completely crystallizes
10. Add ground cinnamon and cloves
11. Layer in jars, bind well, and store in a dry, cool place
