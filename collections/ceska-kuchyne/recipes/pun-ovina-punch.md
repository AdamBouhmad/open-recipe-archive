## Ingredients

- 200 grams sugar
- Zest of 3 lemons
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 0.75 liters boiling water
- 0.5 liters good rum

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar with zest from 3 lemons
2. Crush the sugar and squeeze juice from 2 lemons into it
3. Pour in 0.75 liters of boiling water
4. Mix everything together thoroughly until the sugar dissolves completely
5. Add 0.5 liters of good rum
6. The punch is ready to serve
