## Ingredients

- venison
- vegetables
- spices
- brown roux
- burnt sugar
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Add all vegetables and spices to the venison as for braised venison with white sauce
2. When cooked, add brown roux and a piece of burnt sugar
3. Slice the venison into strips, place in the sauce
4. Arrange potatoes around it
