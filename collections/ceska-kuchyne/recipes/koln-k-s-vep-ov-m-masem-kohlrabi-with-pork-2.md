## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Kohlrabi
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar
- Flour
- Water
- Pork broth

## Directions

1. Rinse the pork meat and cook like beef with all vegetables, salt it and cook until done.
2. Meanwhile, peel the kohlrabi cleanly, cut into cubes or thin slices, cover with water and cook.
3. As soon as it begins to boil, drain the water to remove bitterness.
4. Pour pork broth over it, salt, and if it should be sweet, add sugar; otherwise pepper.
5. When softened, mix with pale flour, cook a bit, add the pork meat cut into neat pieces, and cook for a while.
6. If it should be dark, caramelize sugar.
7. In the same way, vodnice and brukve (kohlrabi) are prepared with pork.
8. If not all the broth is used for kohlrabi, it can be eaten as usual with a fried roll cut into noodles.
