## Ingredients

- two pounds sweet almonds
- six bitter almonds
- one glass milk
- one quart milk
- two teaspoons starch
- one teaspoon sugar
- a pinch of salt
- two tablespoons butter
- one glass cold milk
- almonds cut into thin strips

## Directions

1. Scald and peel two pounds of sweet almonds
2. place them in a mortar with six bitter almonds and one glass of milk and crush well
3. slowly add one quart of milk while stirring constantly
4. place this paste in a cloth and press out all the milk thoroughly
5. put the almond milk in a pot and add another quart of milk
6. heat well but do not let it boil
7. in a small pot, beat together two teaspoons of starch, one teaspoon of sugar, a pinch of salt, two tablespoons of butter and one glass of cold milk
8. mix this well with the almond milk
9. pour into a bowl over almonds cut into thin strips
