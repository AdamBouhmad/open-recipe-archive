## Ingredients

- Mixed offal pieces
- Salt
- Brown roux
- Pepper
- Marjoram
- Garlic
- Green parsley
- Water

## Directions

1. Mix together pieces of various offal, clean them well
2. Cook in salted water for three-quarters of an hour
3. Drain the water, add fresh water, salt and cook until tender
4. Drain the broth and add plenty of brown roux, pepper, marjoram and garlic
5. Cook everything together and strain
6. Place the tripe in cold water to cool, then slice into strips
7. Sprinkle with chopped green parsley
8. Place in the prepared sauce and cook for another quarter hour
