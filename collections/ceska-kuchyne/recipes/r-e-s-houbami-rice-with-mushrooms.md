## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- pepper
- beef broth
- eggs
- cheese

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, slice them, salt and braise until all the liquid is reabsorbed, then braise briefly with butter, chopped parsley and pepper
2. Meanwhile cook rice in beef broth
3. Put a layer of rice in a greased mold, a layer of mushrooms, until the mold is full
4. Brush the top rice layer with egg, butter it, sprinkle with cheese and bake for 3/4 hour
5. Usually served with roasted beef
