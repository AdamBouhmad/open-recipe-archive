## Ingredients

- Sweet apple compote
- One egg white
- One spoonful of sugar
- Half an ounce of peeled almonds
- Lemon zest
- Butter

## Directions

1. Bake two omelettes by the previous method
2. Grease the pan with butter again
3. Place one omelette in it
4. Cover it with a layer of sweet apple compote about the thickness of a finger
5. Cover with the second omelette
6. Beat the white of one egg until stiff
7. Mix it with one spoonful of sugar and half an ounce of peeled almonds finely chopped and a piece of finely chopped lemon zest
8. Pour this onto the top omelette
9. Bake in the oven until golden
