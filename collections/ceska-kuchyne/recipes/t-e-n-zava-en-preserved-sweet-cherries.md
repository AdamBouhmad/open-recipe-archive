## Ingredients

- Beautiful, large sweet cherries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, large sweet cherries and remove the stems.
2. Remove the pits carefully with a pointed straw.
3. Boil the sugar until it reaches the sixth degree.
4. Add the cherries to it.
5. Proceed with everything else as has been described for other preserved cherries.
