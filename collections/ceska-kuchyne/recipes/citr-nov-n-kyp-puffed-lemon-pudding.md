## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 quart milk
- 2 cups grated bread
- 1 lemon
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream together 1 tablespoon butter and 1 cup sugar
2. Add 4 egg yolks, 1 quart milk, and 2 cups grated bread
3. Grate the zest from 1 lemon and add it with the lemon juice
4. Mix well, butter a mold, put in the mixture, and bake for half an hour
5. Meanwhile whip 4 egg whites, add 1/2 cup sugar and juice from 1 lemon
6. When the pudding is baked, spread this meringue on top and put in the oven briefly to brown
