## Ingredients

- one pound beef from the round, scraped or ground
- salt
- pepper
- onion, very finely chopped
- vinegar
- oil
- alternatively: sardines
- alternatively: capers
- alternatively: hard-boiled eggs, sliced

## Directions

1. Scrape with a sharp metal spoon or grind in a meat grinder one pound of beef from the round into a paste
2. Make four mounds of it on a plate
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and very finely chopped onion
4. Pour vinegar and oil over it
5. Let it stand at least half an hour so that the flavor of the ingredients is absorbed into the meat
6. You can also use sardines, capers and hard-boiled eggs, sliced
