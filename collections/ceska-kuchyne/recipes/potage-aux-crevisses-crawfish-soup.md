## Ingredients

- two dozen small crawfish
- salted water
- two quarts lean broth or parsley water
- quarter pound crawfish butter
- half of the crawfish butter
- a little white flour
- salt
- a pinch of thyme
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Throw two dozen small crawfish into boiling salted water and let them boil until they turn red
2. Peel off the tails and claws
3. Make a quarter pound of crawfish butter from the shells
4. Put the shells into a pot, add two quarts of lean broth or parsley water, let it boil for about ten minutes and strain
5. Make a pale roux from half of the crawfish butter and a little white flour, brown the crawfish soup with it and let it boil for a quarter hour
6. Salt the soup sufficiently, season with a pinch of thyme, add the remaining crawfish butter and pour into a soup dish over cut crawfish tails, claws and fried bread
