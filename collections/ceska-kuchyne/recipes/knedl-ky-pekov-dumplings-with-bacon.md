## Ingredients

- 4 slices white bread
- ½ pound smoked bacon
- 2 cups milk
- 3 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- flour (as much as needed)
- grated bread roll or bread crust
- fresh butter
- 1 pound smoked meat
- braised cabbage

## Directions

1. Cut 4 slices of white bread and ½ pound smoked bacon into small cubes
2. Put on a saucepan and let fry until golden
3. Then put it in a bowl, pour 2 cups of milk over it
4. Beat 3 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks into it, salt and mix
5. Now add as much flour as needed to make thick dough, which you knead with a spoon
6. Make dumplings about the size of an apple and boil either in salted boiling water or in broth in which you have boiled 1 pound of smoked meat
7. Take the whole dumplings out onto a plate, cut them in half, sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread crust, pour melted fresh butter over them and serve with the smoked meat cut into neat pieces and braised cabbage
