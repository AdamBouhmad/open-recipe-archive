## Ingredients

- Several beautiful fatty livers
- Truffle
- Good oil
- Finely chopped truffles
- Mushrooms
- Lemon juice
- Fine spices
- Chicken breasts
- Onion
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Chives
- Bacon
- Carrot
- Two whole eggs
- Two egg yolks
- Salt
- Parmesan cheese
- Goose liver
- Bacon

## Directions

1. Lard beautiful fatty livers well with truffle
2. Oil them with good oil, finely chopped truffles, mushrooms, lemon juice and fine spices
3. Place over mild heat
4. Chop raw chicken breasts with onion, truffles, mushrooms, chives, bacon, carrot, two whole eggs and two egg yolks
5. Salt everything
6. Cover the pastry bottom with this mixture, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
7. Place prepared goose liver on top as previously described
8. Line everything with bacon, cover the pastry and arrange everything as with the first pastry
