## Ingredients

- quarter liter of beef broth
- vinegar
- salt
- roux or three tablespoons of flour
- dill, finely chopped
- quarter liter of good sour cream
- two raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Simmer a quarter liter of beef broth with vinegar and salt
2. Add roux or flour, stirring so it is evenly mixed
3. Strain everything
4. Finely chop the dill, add it to a quarter liter of good sour cream in which two raw egg yolks have been mixed
5. Pour to the sauce
