## Ingredients

- butter
- woodcocks
- salt
- juniper berries
- hot water

## Directions

1. Melt butter browned on a baking pan.
2. Place cleaned uncut woodcocks tightly next to each other.
3. Salt them, roast first on the breasts, then on the back slowly.
4. Finally pour two to three tablespoons of hot water over them.
5. Sprinkle with crushed juniper berries and braise slowly again.
6. Woodcocks are roasted in moderate heat for at most half an hour.
7. Serve the woodcocks with strained sauce.
