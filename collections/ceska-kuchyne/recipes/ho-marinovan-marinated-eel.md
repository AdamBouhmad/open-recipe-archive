## Ingredients

- eel
- butter
- vinegar
- water
- onion
- pepper
- spices
- rosemary
- thyme
- bay leaf
- capers
- paprika

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the eel and cut into equal pieces
2. Rinse and dry
3. Line a baking dish with small sticks, butter and bake until golden
4. Allow to cool
5. In a pot, combine vinegar mixed with water enough to cover the eel
6. Layer at the bottom: sliced onion, whole pepper and coarsely crushed pepper, new spices, rosemary, thyme, bay leaf, capers, and paprika
7. Place the eel on top and continue layering spices until the pot is full
8. Cover with a lid and weigh down with something heavy
9. Store in a cool place
10. Before serving, remove all spices from the eel
