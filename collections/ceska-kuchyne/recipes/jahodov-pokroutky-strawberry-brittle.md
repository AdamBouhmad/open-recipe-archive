## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries
- sugar
- linen cloth

## Directions

1. Place ripe strawberries in a closed pot on warm stove to slowly bake and stew
2. Press them out with linen cloth
3. Pour the juice onto pounded sugar, mix with it in a mash
4. Mix everything and warm on warm stove without boiling
5. When everything thickens, remove from heat, let cool and pour onto paper
6. When completely hardened, carefully peel off and cut into pieces
