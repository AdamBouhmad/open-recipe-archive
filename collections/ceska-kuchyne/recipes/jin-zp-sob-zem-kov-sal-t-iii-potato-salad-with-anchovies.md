## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- salt
- onion
- bacon
- good oil
- wine vinegar
- canned sardines

## Directions

1. Peel, slice, and salt the cooked potatoes
2. Finely chop half an onion
3. Remove all bones from bacon and chop finely
4. Add good oil and wine vinegar to the mixture
5. Mix everything well
6. Arrange on a platter
7. Decorate with sardines arranged in a cross pattern
