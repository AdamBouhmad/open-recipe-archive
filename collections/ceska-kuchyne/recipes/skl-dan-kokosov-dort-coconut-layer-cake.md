## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- flour
- 3 egg whites
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- shredded coconut

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar and 0.5 cup butter well
2. Add egg yolks from 3 eggs
3. Add 1 cup sweet milk
4. Add 2 teaspoons baking powder and enough flour to make batter slightly thicker than pancake batter
5. Divide into three plates and bake
6. Beat 3 egg whites into stiff peaks
7. Add about 1 cup powdered sugar
8. When cake is baked and slightly cooled, stack individual layers together
9. Spread some of the beaten egg whites on each layer and sprinkle with shredded coconut, including on top
