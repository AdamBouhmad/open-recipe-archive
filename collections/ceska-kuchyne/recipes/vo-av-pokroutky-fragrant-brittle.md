## Ingredients

- fine pounded sugar
- reseda, jasmine, musk or other pleasant-smelling essence

## Directions

1. Add a few drops of reseda, jasmine, musk or other pleasant-smelling essence to fine pounded sugar
2. Prepare everything together as with other brittle pieces and arrange as usual
