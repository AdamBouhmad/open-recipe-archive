## Ingredients

- calf lungs
- broth
- 2 onions
- butter
- parsley
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak and cook calf lungs in broth, cut into pieces
2. Fry 2 onions in butter with parsley, add the lungs, good broth and salt
3. Simmer for a quarter hour, then add a mixture with lemon juice
