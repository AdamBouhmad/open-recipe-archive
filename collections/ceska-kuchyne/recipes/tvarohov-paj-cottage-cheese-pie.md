## Ingredients

- pie plate dough
- one coffee cup of fresh good cottage cheese
- a piece of butter about the size of a walnut
- half a coffee cup of small raisins
- two eggs
- one coffee cup of sweet milk
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one teaspoon of lemon extract

## Directions

1. Cover a pie plate with good crisp dough
2. Take one coffee cup of fresh good cottage cheese and crumble it onto the dough
3. Take a piece of butter about the size of a walnut and scatter it over the cheese
4. Take half a coffee cup of small raisins that are well picked and washed and sprinkle over the cheese
5. Make custard: beat two eggs, add one coffee cup of sweet milk, two tablespoons of sugar, and one teaspoon of lemon extract
6. Mix everything well and pour over the cheese and bake
7. Raisins can be omitted and it is also good
