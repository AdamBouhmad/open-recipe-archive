## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 10 dek sugar
- 14 dek finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks and 1 egg white until frothy
2. Add sugar
3. Add finely chopped almonds
