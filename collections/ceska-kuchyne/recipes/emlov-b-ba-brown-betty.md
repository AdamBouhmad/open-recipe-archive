## Ingredients

- six hard rolls or slices of white bread
- three eggs
- one quart of milk
- salt
- apples
- raisins
- powdered sugar
- powdered cinnamon
- sour cream
- two yolks
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Slice six hard rolls or slices of white bread thinly.
2. Beat three eggs in one quart of milk, salt it and pour over the sliced bread.
3. Grease a pan with butter, place a layer of soaked bread in it, cover with a layer of peeled and thinly sliced apples, sprinkle with raisins, powdered sugar and powdered cinnamon, and now continue alternating these two types of layers for as long as everything is used.
4. Beat a little sour cream with two yolks, pour over the bread pudding, place pieces of fresh butter on it and bake in the oven.
