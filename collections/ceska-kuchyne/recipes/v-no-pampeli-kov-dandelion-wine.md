## Ingredients

- dandelion flowers
- water
- white sugar
- yellow sugar
- oranges
- lemons

## Directions

1. Chop a full basket of dandelion flowers
2. Place in a pot and pour five gallons of cold water over them
3. Boil for one hour
4. Let stand for 12 hours or overnight
5. Strain
6. Add seven pounds of white sugar and five pounds of yellow sugar to the liquid
7. Add the finely chopped flesh from half a dozen oranges and half a dozen lemons from which you have removed all the rind and white pith
8. Mix well and when the sugar has dissolved, pour into jugs to ferment
9. When fermented, cork and store
