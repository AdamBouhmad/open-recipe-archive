## Ingredients

- onion
- flour
- duck sauce or gravy from previous day
- broth or water
- vinegar
- red wine
- sour cucumber
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- new spice
- duck leftovers

## Directions

1. Simmer chopped onion, add a little flour, reserved duck sauce or gravy from the previous day, broth or water, several tablespoons vinegar, a glass of red wine, sour cucumber cut into slices, bay leaf, several peppercorns and new spice. Cook everything briefly, then reheat the duck leftovers in the sauce.
