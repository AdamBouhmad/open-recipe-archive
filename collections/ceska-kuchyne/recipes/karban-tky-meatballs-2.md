## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram ground or chopped pork
- 1/4 kilogram ground beef
- salt
- spices
- pepper
- 2 cloves garlic
- onion
- lemon zest
- 60 grams rendered pork fat
- grated bread roll
- 2 cheese eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix meat with salt, spices, pepper, minced garlic, finely chopped onion, lemon zest, rendered fat, grated bread, and eggs
2. Form small balls from the mixture, flatten, and fry in lard
3. Usually serve with potato salad
