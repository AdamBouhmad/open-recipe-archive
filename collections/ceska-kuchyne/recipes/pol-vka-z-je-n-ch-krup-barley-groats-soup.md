## Ingredients

- 1 kilo beef
- all vegetables
- salt
- 0.25 liter barley groats
- 0.25 liter good sweet sour cream
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the beef with all vegetables and salt
2. Cook the barley groats in the soup, adding them by the spoonful until completely soft
3. Strain the soup that remained on the meat and pour over the groats
4. Boil for a quarter hour, strain again, remove the groats and add 2 spoonfuls of good sweet sour cream
5. Mix 4 egg yolks and combine with the soup
