## Ingredients

- Fresh fish fillets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Flour or thin batter
- Lard for frying
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Cut fillets from any fresh fish
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Dip in thin batter made from eggs
4. Fry in hot lard
5. Arrange on a platter and garnish with fried parsley and lemon slices
