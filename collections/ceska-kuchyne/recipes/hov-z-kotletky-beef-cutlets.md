## Ingredients

- beef from ribs
- 375 grams beef kidney fat (to 1.5 kilograms meat)
- 2 soaked rolls
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- rib bone
- butter
- grated breadcrumbs
- onion
- beef broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut meat from ribs and mince
2. Mix with kidney fat, 2 soaked rolls, 2 eggs, salt and pepper
3. Form cutlets from the mixture with a piece of rib bone inserted
4. Dip in butter and roll in grated breadcrumbs
5. Brown chopped onion on butter
6. Add broth and place cutlets in it, cover well and braise
7. When done, simmer remaining juices with lemon and add sauce to cutlets
