## Ingredients

- anchovies
- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- butter
- flour
- finely chopped lemon zest
- onion
- strong meat broth
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the anchovies, chop into small pieces
2. Place on a baking sheet with one or two egg yolks, a little butter, a spoonful of flour, finely chopped lemon zest, and onion
3. Cook with constant stirring and adding strong meat broth and good white wine, along with lemon juice
4. When everything has cooked sufficiently, strain and use as a sauce
