## Ingredients

- prepared chicken
- butter
- parsley root
- celery
- carrot
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut prepared chicken in half or in quarters and place on a baking dish or in a pot
2. Add a piece of good butter, parsley root, celery and carrot
3. Braise or cook until tender
4. Strain the broth from the chicken onto another baking dish
5. Make a light roux, add pounded flower, strain the roux through a sieve into the broth as needed
6. Add more butter if needed
7. Chop fresh parsley and mix it together with the chicken
8. Arrange the chicken on a dish like a mound and cover with sauce
