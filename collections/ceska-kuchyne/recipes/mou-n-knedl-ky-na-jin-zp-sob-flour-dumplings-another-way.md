## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon fresh or clarified butter
- 3 eggs
- white flour (1 heaping tablespoon per egg)
- 6 tablespoons milk or sour cream
- salt
- ½ bread roll (žemličku)

## Directions

1. Beat about ½ tablespoon of fresh or clarified butter until foamy
2. Add 3 eggs to it, and after each egg add a heaping tablespoon of white flour and mix well
3. Then add 6 tablespoons of milk or sour cream, whether sour, and after each tablespoon of milk mix in a tablespoon of flour and salt it
4. Cut ½ bread roll into small cubes, fry it until golden and let it cool
5. Once the dough is well mixed, mix the cooled bread cubes into it
6. If the dough is too thin, add another tablespoon of flour
7. Boil them in salted boiling water
