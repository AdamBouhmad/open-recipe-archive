## Ingredients

- cleaned wild goose
- bacon
- ham
- 125 grams butter
- salt
- spices
- vegetables (vegetable pieces)
- 6-8 juniper berries
- 1/4 liter water
- 1/4 liter vinegar

## Directions

1. Wrap the cleaned goose in bacon.
2. Place on a baking sheet with thin-cut ham and bacon.
3. Add butter, salt, spices, vegetable pieces, and juniper berries.
4. Add 1/4 liter water and 1/4 liter vinegar.
5. Cover the baking sheet tightly and place on hot coals.
6. Baste the goose frequently with boiling water during braising.
7. The goose will become tender in 2-3.5 hours.
8. Remove the bacon and cut the goose into pieces.
9. Strain the sauce, thicken with flour, and simmer.
10. Serve with soft-cooked rice on the side.
