## Ingredients

- goose liver
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- onion
- goose fat or butter
- sauce

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the goose liver from the bile, salt and pepper it (those who wish may insert a clove into each half for flavor). Finely chop half an onion and simmer it in goose fat or butter. Then add the liver, cover and turn occasionally so it cooks evenly. When tender, pour strained sauce over it. If the livers are large, they can be cut into pieces and surrounded with potatoes.
