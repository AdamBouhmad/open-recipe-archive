## Ingredients

- sugar (half cup)
- water (two cups)
- lemon juice (from one lemon)
- twelve sour apples, halved, peeled, and cored

## Directions

1. Put half cup of sugar, two cups of water and juice from one lemon in a pot.
2. Add twelve halved, peeled, and cored sour apples, bring to fire and let cook slowly until soft, but do not let them fall apart.
3. Remove from heat, let cool, take the apples out on a dish, let the sauce cook down until thick and press through a sieve onto the apples.
4. You can also add a piece of whole cinnamon to the sugar and use equal parts water and white wine if desired.
5. You can also arrange preserved cherries or raisins in the middle of the apples on the dish, then pour the sauce over them.
