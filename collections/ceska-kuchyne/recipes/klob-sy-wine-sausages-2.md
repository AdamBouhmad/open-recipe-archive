## Ingredients

- pork meat from the neck
- white bread soaked in sour cream
- white pepper
- allspice
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- salt
- wine
- sheep intestines

## Directions

1. Take a piece of pork meat from the neck and chop finely
2. For every three parts of meat, add one part white bread soaked in sour cream
3. Season with ground white pepper, allspice, and finely chopped lemon zest; salt properly
4. Add a little wine and mix thoroughly and knead for at least ten minutes
5. Fill into properly cleaned sheep intestines
6. Alternatively: take two parts pork and one part veal, chop, moisten with sour cream, season, and proceed as above
7. Roll the finished sausages into a coil and pierce across with two thin skewers to hold them together neatly
