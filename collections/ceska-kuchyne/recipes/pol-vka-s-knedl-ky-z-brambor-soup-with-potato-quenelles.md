## Ingredients

- four large floury potatoes
- two spoons of butter
- fresh green parsley
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- salt
- grated bread or crackers
- lard or butter
- beef or potato broth

## Directions

1. Cook four large floury potatoes, peel them and grate them on a grater.
2. Rub two spoons of butter until it froths, add finely chopped fresh green parsley, one whole egg, three egg yolks and a little salt. Mix everything together well with the potatoes.
3. Then make dumplings the size of a walnut, coat them in grated bread or crackers, fry them nicely to a golden color in lard or butter, place in a soup bowl and pour beef or potato broth over them (according to recipe number 37).
