## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- one egg yolk
- two whole eggs
- half a small pot of farina
- salt

## Directions

1. Cream two tablespoons of butter until foamy
2. Add one egg yolk and two whole eggs to it
3. Add half a small pot of farina, salt slightly, and mix well
4. Let it stand for one hour
5. With a coffee spoon, shape small dumplings and drop them into boiling salted water
6. Let them cook for half an hour
7. Remove them with a skimming spoon, arrange on a dish
8. Sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread crust and pour melted fresh butter over them
9. You can serve them with braised pigeons, chickens, or braised veal
