## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pick through and wash the rice
2. Pour hot water over it
3. Boil in water for 2 hours
4. Strain the liquid
5. Season with salt
6. Sweeten with sugar
7. Add lemon juice for sourness
