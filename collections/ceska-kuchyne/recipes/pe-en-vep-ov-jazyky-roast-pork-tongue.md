## Ingredients

- pork tongues
- lard
- salt
- white wine
- vinegar
- sugar
- lemon peel
- cloves
- allspice

## Directions

1. Blanch the pork tongues
2. Remove the outer coarse skin from them
3. Lard them with bacon on both sides
4. Salt them
5. Roll them slightly and pass wooden skewers through both ends
6. Brush them with melted butter and roast until golden on butter
7. Meanwhile make a sauce from equal parts wine and vinegar
8. Add a piece of sugar, a piece of lemon peel, several cloves and a bit of allspice to the sauce
9. Let it simmer
10. Thicken lightly with flour and pour the sauce over the tongues
