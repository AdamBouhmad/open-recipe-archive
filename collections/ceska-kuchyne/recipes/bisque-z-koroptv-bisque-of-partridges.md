## Ingredients

- two roasted partridges
- two dozen large chestnuts
- one quart beef broth for chestnuts
- one quart beef broth for bread
- one pound bread
- two glasses port wine
- two ounces butter

## Directions

1. Roast two partridges and remove all meat from them
2. Roast and peel two dozen large chestnuts and let them cook in one quart of beef broth
3. In another quart of broth, let one pound of bread cook
4. Finely chop the partridge meat and pound it in a mortar, add the chestnuts and pound again
5. Finally add the bread and pound for several more minutes, press through a fine sieve
6. Pour in the broth and place in a pot with two glasses of port wine
7. Mix it well over the fire, but it must not boil anymore
8. Season to taste, add two ounces of butter, mix well and pour into a serving dish with fried bread cubes
