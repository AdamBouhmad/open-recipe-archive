## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 pike
- butter
- 1 3/4 deciliter (half a spoon) good wine
- 3 3/4 deciliter (1 spoon) good pea soup
- peppercorns
- piece of ginger
- flower
- pale roux
- roe from one small fish
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the pike thoroughly and salt it
2. If there are 2 smaller pike, roll them in an arch
3. Place a piece of butter underneath
4. Pour 1 3/4 deciliter of good wine and 3 3/4 deciliter of good pea soup
5. Add several peppercorns, a piece of ginger, and a little flower
6. Cook the pike until soft, carefully turn to the other side
7. Place the pike on a platter
8. Add pale roux to the soup and mix in ground roe from one small fish if desired
9. Boil briefly and strain over the fish
