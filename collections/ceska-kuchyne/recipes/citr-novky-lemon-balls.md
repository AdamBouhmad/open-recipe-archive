## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram butter
- ¼ kilogram crushed sugar
- grated zest from several lemons
- ½ kilogram flour
- several eggs

## Directions

1. Work the butter, crushed sugar, lemon zest, flour, and eggs into dough
2. Press through a fine funnel to form small balls
3. Brush with beaten egg white
4. Sprinkle with sugar
5. Bake on buttered paper on a baking sheet over moderate heat
