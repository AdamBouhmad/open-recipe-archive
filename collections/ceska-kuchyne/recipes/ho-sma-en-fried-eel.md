## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Kill the eel, eviscerate, make an incision around the head and remove the skin
2. Cut into equal pieces
3. Rinse and dry with a clean cloth
4. Salt, roll in flour, dip in egg, coat with breadcrumbs
5. Fry in hot clarified butter until golden
6. Serve with cabbage, head lettuce, cucumber salad, or horseradish
