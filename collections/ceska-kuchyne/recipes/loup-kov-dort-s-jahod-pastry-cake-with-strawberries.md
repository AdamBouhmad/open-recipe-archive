## Ingredients

- layered pastry dough
- finely ground rusk
- cooked strawberries or plum preserves

## Directions

1. Make a layered pastry cake
2. Sprinkle with finely ground rusk
3. Coat the top with cooked strawberries or plum preserves
4. Decorate with pastry strips
5. Bake slowly
