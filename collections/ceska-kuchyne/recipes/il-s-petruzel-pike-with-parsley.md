## Ingredients

- pike, cleaned and cut into pieces
- butter
- onion, sliced
- parsley roots
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- meat broth
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Coat the cleaned pike pieces with butter
2. Place in a casserole lined with sliced onion and parsley roots
3. Season with salt, pepper, and spices
4. Pour meat broth over it
5. Cook until tender
6. Arrange the cooked pike on a platter and pour over the thickened sauce from the casserole
7. Serve with roasted, mashed, or sliced potatoes around it
