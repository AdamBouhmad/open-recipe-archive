## Ingredients

- Cooked ham
- Butter
- Six eggs
- Green parsley or chives (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop cooked ham into small pieces
2. Place in a pan with hot butter
3. When slightly browned, break several eggs into it
4. Mix well together
5. Optionally mix in some chopped green parsley or chives
