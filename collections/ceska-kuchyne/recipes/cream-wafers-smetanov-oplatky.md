## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- pinch of cinnamon
- 1/2 liter sweet cream

## Directions

1. Rub together egg, flour, sugar, cinnamon, and sweet cream
2. Let rest
3. Oil wafer iron, fill with batter
4. Bake thin wafers golden brown on both sides
5. When removed from iron, wrap around rounded wooden stick
