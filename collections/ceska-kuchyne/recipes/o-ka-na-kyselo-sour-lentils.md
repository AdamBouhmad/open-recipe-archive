## Ingredients

- lentils
- soft water
- butter
- flour
- vinegar
- fried onion
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pick through lentils, wash and cook in soft water
2. Make a roux on butter
3. Add to lentils as needed
4. Pour in a little vinegar and cook
5. On the plate, pour fried onion over it
6. Hard-boiled eggs can be added to this lentil dish
