## Ingredients

- half a kilogram of fresh strawberries
- 1 liter of cold water
- juice from 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Crush half a kilogram of fresh strawberries.
2. Add 1 liter of cold water and juice from 3 lemons.
3. Let stand for one hour, press through a sieve, strain and sweeten as needed.
