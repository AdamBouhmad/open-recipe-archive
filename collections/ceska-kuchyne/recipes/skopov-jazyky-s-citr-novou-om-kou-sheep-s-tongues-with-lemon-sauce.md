## Ingredients

- mutton tongues
- salted water
- celery
- parsley
- carrot
- onion
- butter
- flour
- sugar
- lemon
- wine
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook several mutton tongues until tender in salted water with a piece of celery, parsley, carrot, and half an onion.
2. Peel the tongues, cut each one lengthwise into two halves, and place them on a serving dish.
3. In a small pot, put one tablespoon of butter, one tablespoon of flour, a piece of sugar on which you have grated the zest of one lemon, and add the juice of that same lemon, mix well.
4. Pour in a skimmer-full of boiling broth from the tongues mixed with a glass of wine, mix well, and pour over the tongues and let it simmer.
5. You can also thicken the sauce with two egg yolks.
