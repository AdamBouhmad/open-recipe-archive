## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of lean pork meat
- 2 tablespoons of lard
- 1 large onion
- paprika (a knife tip)
- raw rice (1 coffeepot)
- meat gravy from the cooked meat
- cabbage or vine leaves
- half-boiling water or broth

## Directions

1. Cut the two pounds of lean pork meat into small pieces and place 2 tablespoons of clean lard in a pot
2. Chop one large onion and add it to the lard along with a knife tip of paprika
3. When the onion begins to yellow, add the meat and salt it; stir and cover with a lid
4. Let it braise over gentle heat until half-cooked without adding any water, as the meat will give off enough juice on its own
5. When the meat is half-tender, remove it to a board and chop it finely
6. Place the chopped meat in a bowl and add one coffeepot of raw rice as well as the gravy from the meat; mix everything together well
7. Lay this mixture on cabbage or vine leaves and roll it up like an envelope
8. Layer these rolls in a pot and pour half-boiling water or broth over them
9. Let it braise for one hour
