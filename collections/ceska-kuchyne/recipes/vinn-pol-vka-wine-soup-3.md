## Ingredients

- 70 grams butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- juice of 1 whole lemon
- peel of half a lemon
- 100-125 grams sugar
- piece of cinnamon
- 1 bottle white wine
- 4 egg yolks
- fried roll cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Brown 2 tablespoons flour in 70 grams of butter until yellow
2. Add 1/2 liter of boiling water
3. Add lemon juice and peel from half a lemon, 100-125 grams sugar, a piece of cinnamon, and a bottle of white wine
4. Simmer for about ten minutes while stirring constantly
5. Whisk 4 egg yolks and add to the soup
6. Once it has simmered again, pour over fried roll cut into cubes in a bowl
