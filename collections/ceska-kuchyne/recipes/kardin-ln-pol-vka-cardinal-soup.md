## Ingredients

- crayfish butter
- 2-3 tablespoons roux
- strong meat soup
- fine groats or rice
- egg yolks
- cooked crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Make a roux from crayfish butter with 2-3 tablespoons roux
2. Cook it with strong meat soup, add fine groats or rice
3. When serving, beat in a few egg yolks and add cooked crayfish tails
