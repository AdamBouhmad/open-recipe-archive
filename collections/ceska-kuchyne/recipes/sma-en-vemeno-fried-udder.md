## Ingredients

- udder
- salt water
- 2 eggs
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the udder in salted water until tender, which usually takes 4 hours. When tender, remove and cut into thin slices.
2. Beat two eggs, season lightly with salt and pepper. Coat the slices in flour, dip in egg and then into grated breadcrumbs and fry until golden in melted butter. The same method can be used for tongue or tripe.
