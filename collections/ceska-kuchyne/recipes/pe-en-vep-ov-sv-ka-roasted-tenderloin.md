## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloins
- Salt
- Caraway seeds
- Flour
- Lard
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean the pork tenderloins
2. Arrange lengthwise on a roasting pan
3. Sprinkle with salt, caraway seeds and a little flour
4. Place a piece of lard on top
5. Pour a little water
6. Roast in oven, basting frequently with pan juices until sufficiently cooked
