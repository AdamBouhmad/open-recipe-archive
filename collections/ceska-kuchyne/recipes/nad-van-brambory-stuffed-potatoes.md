## Ingredients

- medium-sized potatoes
- cooked or roasted beef and pork (usually leftover)
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- beef broth
- lard

## Directions

1. Choose medium-sized potatoes, peel them, trim them and wash them well
2. Using a spoon, carve a small cavity in each potato so they look like little cups
3. Take cooked or roasted beef and pork, usually leftover, chop it finely and add an appropriate amount of onion, also finely chopped
4. Season well with pepper and salt; if needed, add some beef broth, and the stuffing is ready
5. Take a baking pan, put a little lard on it, place the hollowed potatoes with the stuffing at hand, and using a spoon, fill and arrange them on the pan one next to the other so the stuffing does not spill
6. Cover with a lid and put in the oven to bake
7. When the potatoes are soft, remove the lid and let them brown, then serve hot
