## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter dried blueberries
- 1/4 liter water
- 195 grams sugar (13 lots)
- 3/4 liter red wine
- lemon peel
- potato flour

## Directions

1. Boil the dried blueberries in water
2. Add sugar
3. Pour in red wine
4. Mix in lemon peel
5. Strain the sauce
6. Mix with a small teaspoon of potato flour
