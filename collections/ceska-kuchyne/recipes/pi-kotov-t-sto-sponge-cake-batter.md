## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) beaten sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- lemon
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) flour
- 7 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat together 18 dekagrams beaten sugar, 7 egg yolks, lemon, 9 dekagrams flour, and stiff snow from 7 egg whites
2. Spread on waxed paper with a knife
3. Let it set in the oven
4. Cut into rectangular pieces
5. Spread one half with preserves, cover with the other half, and glaze with icing
