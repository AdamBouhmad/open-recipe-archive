## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- one whole egg
- two egg yolks
- two ounces fine sugar
- half teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub two tablespoons butter until it froths
2. Add one whole egg, two egg yolks, two ounces fine sugar and half teaspoon vanilla
