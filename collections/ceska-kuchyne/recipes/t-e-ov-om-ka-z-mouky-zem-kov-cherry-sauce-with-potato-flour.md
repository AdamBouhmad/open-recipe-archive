## Ingredients

- half kilogram ripe cherries
- water
- whole cinnamon stick
- lemon zest
- cherry pits
- red wine
- sugar
- potato flour mixed in wine

## Directions

1. Stew the ripe cherries in a little water with whole cinnamon and lemon zest
2. Strain everything
3. Crush the cherry pits, boil them in water and strain into the cherry juice
4. Thin the cherry juice with red wine, sweeten with sugar and mix with potato flour dissolved in wine
5. Boil until the sauce thickens properly
