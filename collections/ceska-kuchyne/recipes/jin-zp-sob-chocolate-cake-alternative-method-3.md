## Ingredients

- half kilogram sugar
- 14 egg yolks
- 375 grams ground almonds
- 200 grams grated chocolate
- egg white snow from 11 whites

## Directions

1. Take half kilogram sugar and beat with 14 egg yolks
2. Add 375 grams ground almonds and 200 grams grated chocolate
3. Add egg white snow from 11 whites
4. Mix everything together
5. Pour into a baking form greased with butter
6. Bake
