## Ingredients

- frog legs
- salt
- vinegar
- good broth
- white wine
- sardine butter
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Rub the frog legs with salt and vinegar, wash in water.
2. Braise them in good broth, then remove and keep covered.
3. Pour white wine into the sauce, add sardine butter and lemon juice.
4. Mix in 2 egg yolks to finish the sauce.
