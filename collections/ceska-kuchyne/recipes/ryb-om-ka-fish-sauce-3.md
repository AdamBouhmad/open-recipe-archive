## Ingredients

- butter
- carp or pike, cleaned, cut into rounds
- carrots and celery, sliced into rounds
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- ginger
- saffron
- water

## Directions

1. Place a piece of butter on a pan
2. Add a piece of cleaned carp or pike and carrots and celery sliced into rounds
3. Season with salt, pepper, thyme, ginger and saffron
4. Let it simmer, pouring in water occasionally so it doesn't burn and there is enough broth
5. Then strain it
