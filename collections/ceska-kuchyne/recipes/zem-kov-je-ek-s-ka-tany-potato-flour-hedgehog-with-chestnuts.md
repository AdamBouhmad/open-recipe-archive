## Ingredients

- kilogram of chestnuts
- milk
- vanilla, chopped
- 75 grams (5 lots) potato flour
- three-quarters liter cream
- 15 decagrams (10 lots) crushed butter
- 10 egg yolks
- snow from egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel the kilogram of chestnuts and scald them in milk with chopped vanilla, cook until tender, and press through a sieve.
2. Cook 75 grams (5 lots) potato flour with three-quarters liter cream to make a paste.
3. Add to the chestnuts along with 15 decagrams (10 lots) crushed butter, 10 egg yolks, and snow from egg whites.
4. Put into a form and place in boiling water.
