## Ingredients

- fresh pork blood
- fatty pork meat from under the neck
- internal pork fat
- milk-soaked white bread
- salt
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- ground pepper
- allspice
- ginger
- clove
- melted lard
- fried onion
- barley groats (boiled in water or milk)
- intestines

## Directions

1. Catch and stir the fresh pork blood when slaughtering
2. Take a piece of fatty meat from under the neck and a piece of internal fat, boil them both, and cut into small cubes; divide into two portions
3. Into one portion, add milk-soaked and squeezed white bread and half the blood; add salt, finely chopped lemon zest, ground pepper, allspice, ginger, clove, and a little melted lard and fried onion pieces if desired
4. Mix this well and fill into thick, well-cleaned intestines; pin shut and boil
5. Mix the second half of the cut meat and fat with small barley groats (boiled in water or milk), add the remaining blood, season, add fat, fill and cook as above
6. Serve fried or roasted potatoes with fried onion and cabbage alongside both types
