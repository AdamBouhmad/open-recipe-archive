## Ingredients

- sour cream
- good milk
- eighth of a kilogram fine flour
- a little milk
- ground sweet almonds
- fresh butter
- cinnamon
- sugar
- egg yolks
- fresh cherries

## Directions

1. Boil sour cream with good milk
2. Mix an eighth of a kilogram fine flour with a little milk
3. When the cream begins to boil, add the flour mixture to it, make a dough and pour into a form
4. Mix ground sweet almonds, fresh butter, cinnamon, and plenty of sugar, beat continuously while adding several egg yolks
5. Spread this mixture on the cake about a finger's thickness thick, arrange pitted cherries in it
6. Bake well
