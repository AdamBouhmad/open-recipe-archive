## Ingredients

- Goose giblets
- Cauliflower
- Beef broth
- Fresh butter
- Flour for roux
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the goose giblets by the previous method.
2. Clean a nice cauliflower and cook it either in beef broth or in the broth from the giblets until tender, being careful not to let it fall apart.
3. Remove it and place it in cold water so it remains nicely white.
4. Make a light roux from fresh butter, strain the giblet broth and thicken it with the roux, season lightly with ground nutmeg, add the cooked giblets and cauliflower and let simmer a little longer.
5. Arrange the cauliflower neatly in the center of a serving dish, surround it with the cooked giblets, pour sauce over and bring to the table.
