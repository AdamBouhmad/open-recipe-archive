## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Beef meat
- All spices
- Intestines

## Directions

1. Chop a portion of pork and a portion of beef meat
2. Mix in all spices
3. Stuff into intestines
4. For home smoking: place sticks in a barrel, pile up oak shavings and twigs and light them so they only smolder, cover the barrel and let smoke for half an hour
