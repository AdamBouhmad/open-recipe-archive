## Ingredients

- Half a liter and a third of ripe strawberries
- More than a liter of good sour cream
- Finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Pass the strawberries through a sieve
2. Add to the juice more than a liter of good sour cream
3. Sweeten everything with finely crushed sugar
4. Transform into ice cream using the method already described
