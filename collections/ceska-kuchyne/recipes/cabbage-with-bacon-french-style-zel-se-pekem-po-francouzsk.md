## Ingredients

- Small firm heads of white cabbage
- Bacon cut into thin slices
- Good meat broth
- Parsley
- Chives
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the core from small firm heads of white cabbage and peel off the outer leaves
2. Cut the heads into quarters and blanch in boiling water
3. Drain and place on a baking sheet lined with bacon sliced thinly
4. Pour good meat broth over it, interlace with parsley, chives, pepper and nutmeg, and cook slowly without salt
5. Drain and serve with various meat dishes
