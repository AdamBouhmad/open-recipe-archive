## Ingredients

- eggs
- sardines
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs into a bowl and mix well.
2. Clean the sardines, remove all bones, and cut them into strips or mash them with butter.
3. Place on a pan, stir over heat until reaching the desired thickness.
4. Pepper is usually added.
