## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt
- celery
- carrot
- parsley
- 1 onion
- rice or groats (1/4 pound for 6-8 people)
- butter
- pepper
- ginger
- fresh or dried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Peel and wash as many potatoes as needed for the soup
2. Pour on water so they are covered twice over
3. Add salt, a piece of celery, carrot, parsley, and 1 onion
4. Let cook; when potatoes are half cooked, add a little cleaned and rinsed rice or groats (1/4 pound for 6-8 people)
5. Let finish cooking
6. Make a roux with butter, season with pepper and ginger
7. Serve on the table
8. If desired, you may also let a little fresh or dried mushrooms cook in the soup
