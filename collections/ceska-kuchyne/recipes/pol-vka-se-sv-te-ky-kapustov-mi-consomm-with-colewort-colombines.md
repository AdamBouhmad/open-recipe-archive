## Ingredients

- one large cabbage
- butter or sliced beef fat
- pepper
- three whole eggs
- half a bread roll, grated and soaked in milk and wrung out
- salt
- bread for the small molds

## Directions

1. Cook one large cabbage with the beef meat, then chop it and let it stew a little with a piece of butter or sliced beef fat
2. Season it lightly with pepper and let it cool on a plate
3. Beat three whole eggs into it, add half a grated bread roll soaked in milk and wrung out, add a pinch of salt and mix everything well
4. Place a poulette pan with indentations on the stove, grease the indentations with butter, place a spoonful of the mixture in each, bake on both sides and continue until all the colombines are done
5. Cut each one into four pieces, place them in a bowl and pour either consommé or another clear soup over them
