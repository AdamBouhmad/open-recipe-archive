## Ingredients

- brewed tea
- four deciliters cream
- seven decagrams sugar
- six egg yolks
- rum
- orange sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the brewed tea with four deciliters of cream
2. Add seven decagrams of sugar and six egg yolks
3. Beat well and let cool
4. Add rum and orange sugar
