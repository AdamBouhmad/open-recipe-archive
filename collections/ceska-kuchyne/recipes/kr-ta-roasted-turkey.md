## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- edible chestnuts
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- houska (bread roll)
- parsley
- chives

## Directions

1. Cut off the turkey's head and hold it by the legs so all blood drains out
2. Pluck, scald, and clean the turkey
3. Salt the turkey
4. Prepare stuffing: cook edible chestnuts in an oven, peel them, and stuff the turkey with them, or alternatively mix together 3 decigrams (2 lots) of butter, 8 grated edible chestnuts, a piece of crushed sugar, and 2 eggs
5. If the mixture is too thin, add more chestnuts or 2 grated houska rolls, 3 decigrams (2 lots) of butter, a little parsley and a little chives, and mix together
6. Fill the turkey with the stuffing and sew it shut
7. Spread butter on the turkey, roast until golden brown, and carve
