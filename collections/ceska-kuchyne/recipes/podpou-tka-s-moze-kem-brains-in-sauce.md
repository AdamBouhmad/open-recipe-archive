## Ingredients

- 4 to 6 calf brains
- cold water
- salted water
- flour
- grated nutmeg
- butter
- onion
- salt
- good broth
- lemon juice
- egg yolks
- parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse calf brains in cold water, remove membranes
2. Cook in boiling salted water, cool in cold water, dry, dust with flour and grated nutmeg
3. Place in butter with sliced onion, season with salt and pour over with good broth and lemon juice
4. Make a sauce by stirring in several egg yolks and chopped parsley, pour over the brains
