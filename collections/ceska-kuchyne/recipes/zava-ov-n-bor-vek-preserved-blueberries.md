## Ingredients

- 375 grams of sugar
- water
- 1/2 kilogram of sorted blueberries
- rum

## Directions

1. Boil 375 grams of sugar with water until bubbles begin to appear
2. Add 1/2 kilogram of sorted blueberries and boil quickly without allowing them to break apart
3. The next day, pour off the juice and boil it until thick, so that it drips slowly from a spoon
4. Put the blueberries into jars, pour the boiled sugar over them
5. Cover with paper soaked in rum and store sealed
