## Ingredients

- Fresh cucumbers
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. The main thing is that the cucumbers be fresh; it is best when picked the same day
2. When trimming the cucumbers, it is best to check that they are not bitter
3. The salad should be served immediately on the table; it is not good, as is commonly thought, to let the salad wilt and then press it out, as this makes it difficult to digest and it loses its refreshing taste
4. Finely grate the cucumbers on a grater, pour oil and vinegar over them, sprinkle with pepper, and serve immediately on the table
