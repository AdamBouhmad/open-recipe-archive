## Ingredients

- half liter beef broth
- three tablespoons flour
- piece of sugar
- finely chopped almonds
- horseradish
- good sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cook half liter beef broth with three tablespoons flour
2. Mix and strain
3. Add a piece of sugar
4. Add a small amount of finely chopped almonds
5. Add a piece of grated horseradish and a little good sweet cream
6. Cook and serve with beef or dumplings
