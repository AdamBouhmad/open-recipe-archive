## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of water
- one large tablespoon of corn starch
- cold water
- one tablespoon of butter
- three yolks
- zest of one lemon
- juice of half a lemon
- half a coffee cup of sugar
- three egg whites
- one tablespoon of sugar or juice from the other half lemon

## Directions

1. Bring one coffee cup of water to a boil
2. Mix one large tablespoon of corn starch in cold water and add it to the hot water
3. Let it boil for about a minute
4. Remove from heat and add one tablespoon of butter and let it cool
5. Add three yolks, grated zest from one lemon, juice from half a lemon, and half a coffee cup of sugar
6. Meanwhile, bake the bottom crust of the pie and when baked, pour the prepared mixture onto it
7. Beat the three egg whites to stiff snow, add one tablespoon of sugar or juice from the other half of the lemon, mix well and spread over the pie
8. Place in the oven to brown
