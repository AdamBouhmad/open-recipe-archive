## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- water
- sugar (half cup per cup of pulp)
- lemon (one per gallon), sliced
- whole ginger root

## Directions

1. Dip ripe tomatoes into hot water and peel off the skins.
2. Put them in a pot, set on fire, crush and let cook (without adding any water) for about three-quarters of an hour.
3. Add half cup of sugar per cup of pulp, one lemon per gallon sliced into rounds, whole ginger root, and cook about an hour.
