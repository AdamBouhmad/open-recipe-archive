## Ingredients

- half pint of dried peas
- beef broth
- yellow butter roux
- marjoram
- green parsley
- bread, cooked peas, cooked rice, or noodles cooked in beef broth

## Directions

1. Cook half a pint of dried peas until soft, drain the water, mix with a little beef broth and press through a sieve or strainer into a pot, gradually adding beef broth
2. Thicken with yellow butter roux, season with pepper, let simmer a while and pour into a bowl over fried bread, cooked peas, cooked rice, or noodles cooked in beef broth
3. If desired, add a little marjoram and chopped green parsley
