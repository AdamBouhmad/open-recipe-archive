## Ingredients

- three and one-half deciliters of beef broth
- four tablespoons of wine vinegar
- ground pepper
- four tablespoons of Provençal oil
- soft roe
- four grated egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix three and one-half deciliters of beef broth with four tablespoons of wine vinegar
2. Add a little ground pepper and four tablespoons of Provençal oil
3. Clean the soft roe of all membranes
4. Clean it
5. Rub it with oil and four grated egg yolks
6. Mix everything together
7. When it has cooled, pour it over the remaining fish
