## Ingredients

- oysters
- grated bread
- butter
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove oysters from their shells
2. Fry in grated bread with butter and chopped parsley
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Mix well and use for stuffing oysters, capons and hens
