## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup milk
- parsley, chopped
- lemon zest, chopped
- a pinch of ground clove
- pepper
- grated bread roll or white bread
- veal brisket
- water

## Directions

1. Ask the butcher to make a pocket in the brisket for stuffing, or use a knife to carefully cut a deep pocket between the ribs and skin
2. Beat 2 tablespoons of butter and mix in 2 whole eggs, salt, the finely chopped small onion, 1/2 cup milk, chopped parsley, chopped lemon zest, a pinch of ground clove and pepper
3. Add grated bread roll or white bread until the mixture is thinner than dumpling dough
4. Mix well and fill the brisket pocket with this mixture, then sew shut with white thread
5. Salt both sides of the brisket
6. Place on a baking pan with ribs on top, add water, place pieces of fresh butter on top, and roast at frequent intervals until beautifully browned
