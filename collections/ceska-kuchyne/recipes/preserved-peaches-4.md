## Ingredients

- 8 pounds firm ripe peaches that cling to the pit
- 4 pounds sugar
- 2 ounces whole cinnamon
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 cloves per peach

## Directions

1. Clean the peaches well with cloth
2. Combine sugar, cinnamon, and vinegar and bring to a boil, cooking for five minutes
3. Insert two cloves into each peach
4. Cook the peaches in the liquid until soft enough to pierce with a fork
5. Remove peaches and place in jars
6. Boil the liquid down to half its volume
7. Pour over the peaches
8. Follow general preservation rules for canning
