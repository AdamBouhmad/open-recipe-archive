## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon saltpeter
- 1 gallon water
- salt

## Directions

1. Place a tablespoon of saltpeter into a gallon of water and bring to a boil for about a quarter hour, seasoning it well with salt. Let it cool completely.
2. Pour this brine over the meat. The meat will keep throughout the whole summer and remain as if fresh. Pork can also be preserved this way, but requires more salt.
