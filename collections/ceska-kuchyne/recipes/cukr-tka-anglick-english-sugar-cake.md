## Ingredients

- 200 grams creamed butter
- Twelve egg yolks
- 70 grams finely minced lemon peel
- Candied orange peel
- 160 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Mix 200 grams creamed butter with twelve egg yolks added slowly
2. Add 70 grams finely minced lemon peel, candied orange peel and 160 grams fine flour
3. Mix everything into dough and work it well
4. Roll out and cut with various desired small forms
5. Place on a flour-dusted baking sheet
6. Bake slowly until golden yellow
