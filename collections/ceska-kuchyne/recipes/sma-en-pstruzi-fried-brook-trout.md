## Ingredients

- Small brook trout
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs, beaten
- Bread crumbs, grated
- Clarified butter
- Lemon, cut into quarters

## Directions

1. Scale the trout
2. Clean and gut them
3. Make a lengthwise cut on each side of each trout
4. Salt them and let them lie covered for half an hour
5. Dry them with a cloth
6. Coat in flour
7. Dip in beaten eggs
8. Coat generously with grated bread crumbs
9. Fry in hot clarified butter
10. Arrange on a dish
11. Surround with lemon quarters
12. Serve hot to the table
