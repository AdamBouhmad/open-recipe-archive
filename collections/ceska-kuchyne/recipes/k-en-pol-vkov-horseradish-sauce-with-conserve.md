## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- white flour
- lukewarm beef broth
- grated horseradish
- salt
- clove

## Directions

1. Put a piece of fresh butter in a pot with a little white flour and rub well
2. Pour lukewarm beef broth over it and stir slowly until it boils
3. Add grated horseradish to it, salt and season with clove
