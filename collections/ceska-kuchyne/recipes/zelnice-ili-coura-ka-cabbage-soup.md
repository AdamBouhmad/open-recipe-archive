## Ingredients

- 1 quart cabbage water
- 1 pint sour cabbage
- 1 quart water
- 6 egg yolks or 3 eggs
- A piece of butter
- 1 cup good sour cream
- Optional: 1 spoon white flour for thicker soup

## Directions

1. Put cabbage water, sour cabbage, and water in a pot and cook
2. Beat 6 egg yolks or 3 eggs with a piece of butter and 1 cup sour cream in a small bowl
3. Optionally, add 1 spoon of white flour to the cream and eggs for a thicker soup
4. Temper the soup by slowly adding it to the boiling liquid, stirring constantly
5. Pour into a serving dish
