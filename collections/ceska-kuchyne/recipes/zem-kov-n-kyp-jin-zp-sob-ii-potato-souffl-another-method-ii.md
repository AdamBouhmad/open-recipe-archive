## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) sugar
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) almonds
- salt
- half a kilogram cooked grated potatoes
- 12 egg whites
- cinnamon and sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of butter with 12 egg yolks and 18 dekagrams (12 lots) of sugar until foamy
2. Add 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of almonds, salt, and half a kilogram of cooked grated potatoes
3. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 12 egg whites
4. Put into a greased mold and bake either in the oven or cook in hot water
5. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and serve as an accompaniment
