## Ingredients

- 8 raw egg yolks
- egg white snow from 8 egg whites
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar
- zest and juice from 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat 8 raw egg yolks and snow made from 8 egg whites with 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar, zest, and juice from 1 lemon for a quarter hour
2. Quickly bake the soufflé in a mold
