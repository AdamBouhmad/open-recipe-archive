## Ingredients

- Hard heads of red cabbage
- Salted boiling water
- Meat broth
- Bacon
- Good vinegar
- Butter mixed with flour
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut hard heads of red cabbage in half, remove the core and cut the cabbage into quarters
2. Cook once in salted boiling water and remove to a strainer
3. Pour meat broth over it on a baking sheet, add bacon and good vinegar, as well as butter mixed with flour, pepper and salt
4. Cook slowly and serve with partridges or cured meats
5. Remove the bacon from the cabbage before serving
