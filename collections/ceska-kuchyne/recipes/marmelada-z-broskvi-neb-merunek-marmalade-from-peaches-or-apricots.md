## Ingredients

- Well-ripened peaches
- Sugar - equal measure to the pulp

## Directions

1. Select well-ripened peaches
2. Halve them
3. Remove the pits
4. Press peaches through a fine sieve
5. Take sugar in the same measure as pulp, cook it to the sixth degree first
6. Cook together with peach pulp with constant stirring
7. Skim foam from the top
8. Pour marmalade into containers
9. When cooled, cover with paper and bladder
