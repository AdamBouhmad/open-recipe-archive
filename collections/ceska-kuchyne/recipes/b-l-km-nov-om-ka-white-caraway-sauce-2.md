## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- butter
- quarter liter clear meat broth
- 1 tablespoon caraway seeds, crushed
- salt
- 2 blanched egg yolks
- butter
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from flour and butter
2. Cook with quarter liter clear meat broth
3. Add a spoonful of crushed caraway seeds
4. Season with salt
5. Mix with two blanched egg yolks and butter
6. Add lemon juice or good vinegar to taste
7. This white caraway sauce is suitable both for savory meats and for fish
