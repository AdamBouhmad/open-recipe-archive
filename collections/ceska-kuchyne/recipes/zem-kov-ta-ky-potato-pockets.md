## Ingredients

- cooled floury potatoes
- 1/4 liter milk
- 6 eggs
- salt
- flour
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Grate the cooled floury potatoes
2. Mix with 1/4 liter milk, 6 eggs, salt and flour to form a dough that can be rolled out
3. Brush with butter and sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs and roll up
4. When cooled, cut into pieces
5. Cook in salted water and pour melted butter over them
6. Serve with fruit or stewed meat
