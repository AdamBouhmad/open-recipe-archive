## Ingredients

- Veal brain
- Butter
- Onion
- Salt
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. Remove membranes and blood from the brain and wash it; chop onion and soften with butter, salt the brain, add flour, place on the onion, mix well, beat an egg into it just before serving.
