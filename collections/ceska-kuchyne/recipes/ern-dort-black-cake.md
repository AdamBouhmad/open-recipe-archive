## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups sugar
- 1.5 cups butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 cup black coffee
- flour
- 0.5 cup coffee teaspoon cinnamon
- 0.25 teaspoon cloves
- 0.25 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 5 egg whites
- 1.5 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup water
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and butter well.
2. Add 5 yolks and 1 cup black coffee and mix again.
3. Add enough flour to make the dough slightly thicker than pancake batter.
4. Add 0.5 tablespoon cinnamon, 0.25 teaspoon cloves, 0.25 teaspoon nutmeg, and 2 teaspoons baking powder and mix again.
5. Beat 5 egg whites and fold into the dough.
6. Grease and flour the plates and pour the dough into them.
7. Bake in a moderately heated oven.
8. When baked, make frosting by taking 1.5 cups sugar and about 0.5 cup water, bringing to a boil and cooking until it becomes a thick syrup.
9. While the syrup cooks, beat 2 egg whites.
10. When the syrup is ready, slowly pour it into the egg whites while beating rapidly.
11. As soon as it begins to thicken considerably, begin assembling the cake by layering one cake on a plate, spreading frosting on it, and repeating until finished.
