## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) of fine groats
- water
- butter
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- 2.5 liters of hot milk
- a little sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the fine groats in water with butter, add lemon peel with cinnamon, 2.5 liters of hot milk, a little sugar and salt, then boil everything for a quarter hour
