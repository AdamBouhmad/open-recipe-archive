## Ingredients

- tripe
- water
- salt
- beef soup
- flower
- pepper
- ginger
- parsley, finely chopped
- white roux for thickening

## Directions

1. Boil the tripe thoroughly in several changes of water and let it cook for about half an hour
2. Remove it onto a board, scrape each piece with a knife or old sharp spoon, trim all the edges, rinse the tripe thoroughly again, place it in another pot, pour clear water over it, salt it and let it cook until tender
3. When sufficiently tender, cut it into noodles and place it in beef soup thickened with white roux, season with ground flower, pepper and ginger, add some finely chopped parsley and serve
4. If the tripe is still tough, let it cook in the unthickened soup long enough until it softens, then thicken the soup
