## Ingredients

- pike
- salted water
- wine
- butter
- anchovy fillets
- parsley
- bread or roll
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scrape, clean and gut the pike, cut into pieces.
2. Bring two parts salted water and one part wine to boil, place the pike in it and let cook for half an hour.
3. Meanwhile melt a quarter pound of butter, add three ounces of cleaned and finely chopped anchovies, a teaspoon of chopped green parsley and let fry for five minutes.
4. Remove the pike onto a dish, sprinkle with grated bread.
5. Pour over it the prepared butter, into which you must first strain the juice from one lemon.
