## Ingredients

- two cups milk
- about one cup good butter
- stale bread

## Directions

1. Bring two cups of milk to a boil; have about one cup of good butter heated.
2. Cut the bread into thin slices.
3. As the milk boils, add one slice at a time.
4. When sufficiently softened, place on a plate.
5. Pour over with a spoon of the heated butter.
6. Mix together ground poppy seed with a bit of sugar and sprinkle over the slices.
7. Do not add the bread to the milk all at once; add only three or four slices at a time.
