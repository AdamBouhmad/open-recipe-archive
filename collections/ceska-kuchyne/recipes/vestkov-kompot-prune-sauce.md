## Ingredients

- Ripe prunes
- Sugar
- A piece of cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Red wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut ripe prunes in half and remove the pits
2. Cook them with sugar, a piece of cinnamon and lemon peel cut into thin strips
3. When they soften, remove them to a dish and pour thickly boiled juice over them
4. If you wish them to be particularly delicate, peel them and add a little red wine
