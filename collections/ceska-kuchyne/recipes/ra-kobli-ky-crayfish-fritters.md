## Ingredients

- piece of crayfish butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) crayfish tails and claws
- 6 eggs
- 2 soaked rolls
- a little flour
- salt
- butter dough
- sugar or fried green parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cream together a piece of crayfish butter with chopped crayfish tails and claws
2. Beat well with 6 eggs
3. Add 2 soaked rolls, a little flour, and salt
4. Spread butter dough on a baking sheet
5. Place spoonfuls of the mixture on the dough as small mounds
6. Wrap each individually and fry
7. Sprinkle with sugar or fried green parsley
8. Typically served at larger meals after beef
