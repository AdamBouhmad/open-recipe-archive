## Ingredients

- ripe but firm melons
- soft water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select ripe but still firm melons
2. Cut into oblong, thin pieces
3. Clean and peel them
4. Place in soft water and set on warm stove
5. As soon as they have cooked slightly, remove and place in thinly cooked sugar
6. If not all pieces are completely soft, keep covered so they finish cooking in the steam
7. Cook the melons with the sugar several times, skim off foam, and cover the vessel with paper
8. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, add the melons, cook together
9. Repeat this for at least two more days, each time cooking the drained sugar thicker and thicker, always skimming foam
10. Once completely candied and cooled, place in jars and bind
