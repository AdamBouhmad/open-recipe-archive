## Ingredients

- onion
- beef broth
- whole pepper
- spices
- bay leaf
- thyme
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar
- half a lemon
- roux
- sour cream (if for game)

## Directions

1. Cut the onion and place it in beef broth with whole pepper, spices, bay leaf, thyme and salt
2. Simmer for a while
3. Caramelize a piece of sugar and add it with vinegar to the sauce
4. If not sour enough, add juice from half a lemon
5. Add roux and strain
6. If it is intended for game, add sour cream and it can also be eaten cold
