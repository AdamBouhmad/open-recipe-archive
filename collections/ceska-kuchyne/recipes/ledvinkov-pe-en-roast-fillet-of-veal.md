## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 pound piece of veal with kidney
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water
- grated bread roll or bread

## Directions

1. Take a 3 to 4 pound piece of veal with kidney, clean it thoroughly, salt it
2. Pepper both sides, place on a baking pan and top with butter
3. Add water and place in the oven
4. When browned on the top side, turn it over and baste with butter 4 to 5 times during roasting
5. Half an hour before serving, sprinkle the surface with grated bread roll or bread
6. If the juices have dried out, add more hot water and baste the top with pan juices before serving to help the bread crust adhere
7. When the bread coating is browned, place on a platter, pour pan juices over it and serve
