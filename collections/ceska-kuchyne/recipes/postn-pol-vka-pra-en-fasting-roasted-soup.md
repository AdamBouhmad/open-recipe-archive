## Ingredients

- 2 litres water
- 2 pinches of caraway seeds
- a piece of butter
- salt
- meat broth, well browned
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Put 2 pinches of caraway seeds, a piece of butter and a little salt into 2 litres of water
2. After boiling for a quarter hour, add well-browned meat broth
3. Boil a little longer and pour over fried bread
