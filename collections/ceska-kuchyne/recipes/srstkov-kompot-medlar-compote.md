## Ingredients

- nice developed medlars that are not yet ripe
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take nice developed but not yet ripe medlars
2. Clean them and remove the seeds
3. Boil the medlars once in soft water
4. Leave them covered to cool
5. When completely cooled, boil them again once but so they do not fall apart
6. Remove them and place in fresh water, and when cooled, let them dry on sieves
7. Place the medlars in well-boiled sugar
8. Boil together, skim the foam, cover the vessel and let it rest peacefully until the next day
9. The next day boil the medlars with the sugar several times, skim the foam
10. When cooled, place on a dish, pour over and sprinkle with sugar
