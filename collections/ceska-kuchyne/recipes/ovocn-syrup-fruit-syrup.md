## Ingredients

- juice of strawberries, raspberries, currants, and other fruit
- one and a half times as much sugar as juice

## Directions

1. For each part of juice, add one and a half times as much sugar
2. Boil everything together several times
3. Skim the foam
4. Let cool
5. Pour the finished syrup into bottles
