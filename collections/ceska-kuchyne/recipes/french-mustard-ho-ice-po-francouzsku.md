## Ingredients

- brown mustard (fifteen decagrams)
- white mustard (fifteen decagrams)
- sugar (thirty decagrams, cooked brown)
- sardines (fifteen decagrams, boned)
- onion (two, finely chopped)
- good wine vinegar (about three deciliters)
- boiled seasoned must

## Directions

1. Thoroughly crush together fifteen decagrams of brown mustard and fifteen decagrams of white mustard
2. Add about thirty decagrams of sugar cooked brown
3. Add fifteen decagrams of boned sardines
4. Add two finely chopped onions
5. Mix everything with about three deciliters of good wine vinegar and boiled seasoned must
6. Strain everything into glass vessels
