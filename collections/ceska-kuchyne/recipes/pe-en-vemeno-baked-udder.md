## Ingredients

- Udder
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- Vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean the udder and cook it
2. Peel it and slice into pieces
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Bake on a baking sheet in butter until browned
5. Serve with vegetables
