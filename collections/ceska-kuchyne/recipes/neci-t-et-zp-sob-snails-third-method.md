## Ingredients

- snails in shells
- salted water
- beef broth or salted water
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Wash the snails in their shells and cook in salted water.
2. When the shells begin to open, remove the snails and cut off the tails.
3. If they are tough, cook them either in beef broth or in salted water.
4. When completely soft, serve on a plate with horseradish.
