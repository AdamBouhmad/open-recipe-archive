## Ingredients

- veal roast scraps
- butter
- parsley
- flour
- broth
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop the veal roast scraps
2. Fry in butter with parsley
3. Add 2 tablespoons flour and heat over fire
4. Add sufficient broth
5. Beat 2 egg yolks and add to the soup
