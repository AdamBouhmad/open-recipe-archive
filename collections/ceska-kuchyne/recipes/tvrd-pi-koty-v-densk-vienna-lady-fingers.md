## Ingredients

- one bowl fine granulated sugar
- five egg yolks
- one egg white
- four egg whites
- one spoon cold sugar cooked to thick with water
- two bowls sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put one bowl of fine granulated sugar in a bowl
2. Add five egg yolks and beat so long until quite foamy
3. Gradually fold in one egg white
4. Beat the other four egg whites into meringue and fold in
5. Then fold in one spoon of cold sugar cooked to thick with water
6. Finally fold in two bowls of sifted flour
7. Put this mixture into molds or make on paper into finger-length pieces
8. Sprinkle with sugar powder and bake in a not too hot oven
