## Ingredients

- 8 deca bread crumbs
- 2 egg yolks
- crayfish butter
- salt
- parsley
- crayfish meat
- egg white
- asparagus
- beans

## Directions

1. Mix 8 deca bread crumbs with 2 egg yolks
2. Add crayfish butter and season with salt
3. Add chopped parsley and finely chopped crayfish meat
4. Fold in beaten egg white
5. Add cooked asparagus cut into tiny pieces and beans
6. Use for stuffing chickens and similar roasts
