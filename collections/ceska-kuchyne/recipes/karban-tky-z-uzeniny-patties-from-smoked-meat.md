## Ingredients

- Half kilogram of cooked marbled smoked meat
- Half kilogram of cooked mashed potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons of flour
- Pepper
- Flowers
- Rusks, crushed
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop half kilogram of cooked marbled smoked meat.
2. Mix with half kilogram of cooked mashed potatoes, 3 eggs, 2 tablespoons of flour, pepper and flowers.
3. Form patties from the mixture, sprinkle with crushed rusks and fry in butter.
4. Usually they are served with vegetables.
