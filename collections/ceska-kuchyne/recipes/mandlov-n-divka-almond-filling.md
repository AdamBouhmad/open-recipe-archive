## Ingredients

- 10 dek finely crushed sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 10 dek almonds
- raisins
- citron peel
- snow from 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Thoroughly mix the finely crushed sugar with egg yolks and almonds
2. Add raisins and citron peel
3. Fold in the snow from beaten egg whites
