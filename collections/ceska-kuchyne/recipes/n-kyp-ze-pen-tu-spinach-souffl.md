## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 raw egg yolks
- a little flour
- marjoram
- 2 cloves garlic, rubbed with salt
- half a chopped onion
- 4 grated hard egg yolks
- grated fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Wash spinach, salt it, and cook in a pot until soft
2. Strain the water, rinse spinach with cold water, and press through a sieve
3. Beat 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter and add 3 whole eggs and 3 raw egg yolks
4. Add a little flour, salt, marjoram, 2 cloves of garlic rubbed with salt, half a chopped onion, and 4 grated hard egg yolks
5. Mix everything with grated fried bread roll into the spinach and stir well
6. Put into a greased mold coated with grated bread roll
7. Bake for half an hour
