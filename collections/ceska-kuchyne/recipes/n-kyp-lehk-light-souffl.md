## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- lemon peel
- stiff snow from 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 6 tablespoons sugar.
2. Add a piece of lemon peel and stiff snow from 6 egg whites.
3. Pour into a round bowl.
4. Bake in the oven, timing so the soufflé is ready when it is to be served at table, otherwise it will fall.
5. Place the bowl on a plate, wrap with a cloth, and bring to the table.
6. This is a very light and delicious dish, especially suitable for recovering persons.
7. Lemon juice can be added if desired.
