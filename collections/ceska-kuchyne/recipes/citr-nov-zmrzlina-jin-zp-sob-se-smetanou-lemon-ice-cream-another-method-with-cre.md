## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- More than one liter cream
- Three-eighths kilogram crushed sugar
- Grated peel of two lemons

## Directions

1. Break four eggs
2. Add more than one liter cream, about three-eighths kilogram crushed sugar and grated peel of two lemons
3. Whip, cook and proceed as described for simple ice cream
