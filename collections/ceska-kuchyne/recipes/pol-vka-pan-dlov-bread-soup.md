## Ingredients

- six white rolls
- milk
- 8 egg yolks
- herbs
- salt
- beef soup

## Directions

1. Soak the white rolls in milk and press them well
2. Mix the egg yolks with the rolls thoroughly, add a little herbs
3. Add salt and pour 4 glasses of beef soup
4. Boil for about a quarter hour, pour into cups and serve
5. If it is still too thin, add another egg yolk
