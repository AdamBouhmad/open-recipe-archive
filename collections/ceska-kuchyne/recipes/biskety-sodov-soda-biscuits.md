## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 tablespoon lard
- salt to taste
- 1 1/2 small cups sour milk or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add lard and salt and mix well
2. Moisten 1 teaspoon baking soda with vinegar, add to the sour milk or buttermilk and mix with the prepared flour
3. Dust the rolling pin with flour, turn the dough onto it and roll smooth, but not too stiff
4. Grease a baking sheet, cut rounds with a cutter, place on the sheet and bake in a hot oven
