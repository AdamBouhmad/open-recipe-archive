## Ingredients

- chicken
- 1/8 kilogram rice
- water
- butter
- salt
- parsley root
- carrot
- celery
- cabbage
- flower
- flour

## Directions

1. For one chicken calculate 1/8 kilogram rice
2. Rinse rice, add water, butter and salt, braise until soft
3. Cut chicken into 4 pieces and braise in pot on butter with parsley root, carrot, celery, cabbage and flower
4. When chicken is tender, remove it and strain the broth
5. Make light roux, strain into broth, boil and add chicken
6. After cooking together a while longer, place chicken on dish and arrange rice in a wreath around it
