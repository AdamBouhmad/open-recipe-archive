## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- salt
- poppy seeds, cheese, gingerbread, or breadcrumbs for topping

## Directions

1. Place flour on board, make well in center
2. Add eggs and salt
3. Work into stiff dough that does not stick to rolling pin
4. Cut off piece, roll out, let dough dry
5. Cut into short, wide noodles
6. Throw into boiling salted water
7. Cook a short time and drain
8. Immediately butter noodles so they do not stick together
9. Sprinkle with poppy seeds, cheese, gingerbread or breadcrumbs
