## Ingredients

- pork spleen and ears, cut into cubes
- rendered lard with cracklings
- raw pork blood
- pepper
- ginger
- clove
- allspice
- onion, fried in lard
- pork stomach

## Directions

1. Cut pork spleen and ears into cubes
2. Add rendered lard with cracklings
3. Pour in raw pork blood
4. Season with pepper, ginger, clove, and allspice
5. Add onion fried in lard
6. Mix everything well
7. Fill the cleaned pork stomach with this mixture
8. Cook it in spleen soup for half an hour
9. It is done when pierced with a thin stick and no blood runs out
10. While hot, place it in a press and compress it further as it cools
11. For an especially good version, add cooked beef or pork tongue cut into larger pieces
12. Cut it cold into slices
13. Arrange on a plate and serve
14. You may also sprinkle it with chopped onion and pour vinegar over it, or serve only with vinegar and pepper
