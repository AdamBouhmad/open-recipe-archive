## Ingredients

- water
- flower (seasoning)
- cabbage
- celery
- butter
- butter (light)
- mushrooms
- noodles or flakes

## Directions

1. To two quarts of water add a little flower, a piece of cabbage, a piece of celery and a piece of butter
2. Brown with a bit of light butter if you have it, add a little mushrooms
3. Let simmer, strain and cook noodles in it
4. Let simmer a while longer, then pour the soup into a dish and serve
5. To make noodles: beat two whole eggs into half a pound of flour and make a stiff dough
6. Roll out on a board until nearly transparent and cut into noodles or flakes
7. Spread the cut noodles on the board so they do not stick together
8. Instead of noodles and flakes you can also cook grated breadcrumbs or pucks
9. For breadcrumbs make a somewhat stiffer dough, which you grate on a grater and cook in the soup
10. Pucks require moderately stiff dough, which you roll into long, finger-thick rolls by hand and then cut into small pieces (pucks)
