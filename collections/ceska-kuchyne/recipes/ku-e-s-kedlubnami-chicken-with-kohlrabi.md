## Ingredients

- some fat
- rooster
- kohlrabi
- salt
- pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon powder, sweet
- a little salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Put some fat in a pot; clean the rooster, cut it into pieces and place in the pot
2. Clean the kohlrabi, rinse them, cut into pieces and place on top of the rooster
3. Salt and pepper everything, cover and let it braise over gentle heat
4. When you see that everything will be ready in about half an hour, make a batter from two eggs, one cup milk, one teaspoon sweet powder, a little salt and flour for dumplings
5. Divide the batter into four parts with a spoon and place on the surface, letting everything braise
6. When the dumplings are half-cooked, turn them over with two forks to the other side
7. When done, remove them and add a little water, mix it with a little flour in the pot and thicken the sauce, then remove the rooster separately and the sauce and kohlrabi also separately
