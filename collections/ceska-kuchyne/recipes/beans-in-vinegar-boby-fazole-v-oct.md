## Ingredients

- young green bean pods
- potash (a knife tip per liter of water)
- onion
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- vinegar (boiled, diluted with water, cooled)
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut off young green bean pods
2. Wash and cook in strong salted water to which potash is added (a knife tip per liter) so the pods retain their greenness
3. Allow the cooked pods to cool
4. Drain the vinegar from them
5. On the second day add onion, bay leaf, and peppercorns among the beans
6. Layer the beans with these additions into jars
7. Pour over with cooled vinegar prepared as described above and boiled
8. Cover the top with fat
9. Tie up so air cannot enter inside
