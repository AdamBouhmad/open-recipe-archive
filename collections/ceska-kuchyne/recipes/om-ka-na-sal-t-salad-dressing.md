## Ingredients

- three egg yolks
- one teaspoon of mustard
- two teaspoons of salt
- one half spoon of paprika
- two spoons of sugar
- two spoons of heated butter or good olive oil
- half a cup of sour cream
- one half cup of hot vinegar
- three egg whites, half-beaten

## Directions

1. Take three egg yolks, one teaspoon of mustard, two teaspoons of salt, one half spoon of paprika, two spoons of sugar, two spoons of heated butter or good olive oil, half a cup of sour cream, one half cup of hot vinegar and three egg whites that have been half-beaten
2. Mix everything together in order as written
3. Cook over low heat in a double boiler, similar to how oat meal is cooked, until it thickens like sauce
4. Pour into a sauce dish and allow to cool
5. This preparation will keep up to 14 days in a cool place and is excellent on head lettuce, potatoes, cooked beans in pods, cauliflower, etc.
