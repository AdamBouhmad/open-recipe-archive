## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- pepper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Clean young chickens well, remove the insides, split them in half along the back
2. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper
3. Place them in wire tongs (broiler)
4. Broil over hot coals, first the inside and then the outside until nicely browned
5. Place on a plate in the oven so they do not cool
6. When all are broiled, cover with fresh butter and serve
