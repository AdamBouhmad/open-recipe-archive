## Ingredients

- String beans
- Salt
- Baking soda
- 1 spoonful butter
- 1 small teaspoon sugar
- 1 large spoonful flour
- 1 spoonful vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the string beans for cooking according to the previous method, salt and cook them for an hour in salted water to which you have added a piece of baking soda the size of a pea.
2. When cooked, make a roux from one spoonful of butter, a small teaspoon of sugar and a large spoonful of flour and when it browns, add it to the beans, pour in one spoonful of vinegar and serve.
