## Ingredients

- Grape berries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the grape berries and press them, squeeze out the insides of the berries from the skins into a bowl and put the skins in another bowl.
2. Put the insides of the berries with the seeds to cook just as they are, without adding water, as they have enough juice.
3. Cook about half an hour, until you see that the seeds have separated from the berry, then strain through a sieve, or squeeze in a thin cloth until dry and put it back to cook along with those skins.
4. Add to each cup of cooked grapes half a cup of sugar, or more to taste, cook one and one-half hours and then put into jars.
