## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- three whole eggs
- three egg yolks
- one pound of good curd cheese
- salt
- grated bread roll
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream a piece of fresh butter, beat three whole eggs and three egg yolks into it
2. Add one pound of good curd cheese, salt, and mix well
3. Add enough grated bread roll and a little flour so that dumplings can be made from it, and mix thoroughly again
4. Shape round dumplings with your hands
5. Boil them in salted boiling water and let them cook for a quarter hour
6. Remove them with a strainer and arrange halved on a dish
7. Sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread crust and generously pour melted fresh butter over them
