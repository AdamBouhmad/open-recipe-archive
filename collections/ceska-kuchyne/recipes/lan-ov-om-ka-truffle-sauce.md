## Ingredients

- truffles
- onion
- parsley
- fresh butter
- wine
- good gravy
- strong meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean and peel truffles
2. Slice them into thin rounds
3. Add finely chopped onion and chopped parsley
4. Add fresh butter and a little wine
5. Braise until truffles are completely soft
6. Add a little good gravy and strong meat broth
7. Boil thoroughly once more
8. Strain and serve
