## Ingredients

- veal lungs
- butter
- parsley
- 2 onions
- good broth
- several egg yolks
- lemon juice
- salt
- fried bread or biscuit for serving

## Directions

1. Soak veal lungs and boil them in broth
2. Cut lungs into cubes
3. Fry 2 onions in butter with parsley
4. Add lungs and good broth, salt
5. Boil for a quarter hour
6. Mix in several egg yolks pounded with lemon juice
7. Serve surrounded by fried bread or biscuit
