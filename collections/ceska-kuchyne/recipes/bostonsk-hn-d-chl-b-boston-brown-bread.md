## Ingredients

- two cups cornmeal
- one cup rye flour
- one half cup syrup
- one heaping teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water
- three cups sour milk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put two cups of cornmeal and one cup of rye flour in a bowl
2. Add one half cup of syrup
3. Dissolve one heaping teaspoon of baking soda in hot water and mix it into three cups of sour milk
4. Add this mixture to the flour and season with salt, mixing well
5. Pour into a greased deep mold, place in a steamer and steam for 4 hours
6. Then place in an oven for 20 minutes to dry it slightly
7. Remove and turn out onto a plate
