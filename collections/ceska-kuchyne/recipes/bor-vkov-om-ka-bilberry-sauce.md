## Ingredients

- 1 liter bilberries
- 90 grams (6 lots) sugar
- a little cinnamon
- lemon zest
- potato flour mixed in wine

## Directions

1. Sort through the bilberries, wash them, and mash them in a glazed baking dish with a mashing tool
2. Add sugar, a little cinnamon and lemon zest
3. Strain everything, add potato flour mixed in wine
4. Boil once more
