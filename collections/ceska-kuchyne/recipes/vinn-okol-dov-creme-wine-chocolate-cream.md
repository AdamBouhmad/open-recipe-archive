## Ingredients

- one liter red wine
- 250 grams chocolate
- 200 grams sugar
- 30 grams dissolved fish bladder
- egg white from four eggs

## Directions

1. Boil one liter of red wine with 250 grams of chocolate and 200 grams of sugar
2. Mix in 30 grams of dissolved fish bladder
3. Sieve the crushed chocolate
4. Add snow from four egg whites
5. Pour into molds and place on ice
