## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) almonds
- 20 dekagrams (14 lots) sugar
- egg whites, as needed for stiff dough
- chopped almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Rub the almonds with cloth and beat with egg white
2. Mix in the sugar and enough egg whites to form stiff dough
3. Roll out the dough and sprinkle with chopped almonds
4. Roll pieces of dough into crescent shapes
5. Bake until golden yellow
