## Ingredients

- pike, cleaned and skinned
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lemon juice
- mayonnaise sauce
- crayfish tails
- capers
- sardines
- aspic (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, skin it, and remove all bones
2. Cut into small pieces and salt them
3. Braise in butter and remove to a sieve to drain and cool
4. Season with salt and pepper, and coat with lemon juice
5. Place the pike pieces on a platter in mayonnaise sauce
6. Garnish with crayfish tails, capers, sardines, or aspic
