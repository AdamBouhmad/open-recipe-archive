## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 liters of ordinary wine
- 20-26 dekagrams of sugar
- A little ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs and 4 egg yolks until frothy.
2. Add to them 2 liters of ordinary wine and 20-26 dekagrams of sugar, a little ground cinnamon, and heat everything with constant stirring on warm stoves until it rises.
