## Ingredients

- largest unripe walnuts
- fresh water
- soft water
- alum
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select the largest unripe walnuts
2. Peel the green shell down to the white interior
3. Place in fresh water
4. Boil soft water with a small amount of alum and when boiling, add the walnuts
5. Constantly press them so they soften
6. After a short time, remove the walnuts and place in fresh water to which lemon juice has been added
7. Let everything cool
8. Remove the walnuts, place in a vessel, pour over with dissolved sugar, and let sit at least a day
9. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, skim foam, let cool and immediately pour off the sugar again
10. Repeat this same process for two more days
11. On the third day, cook the sugar to complete thickness together with the walnuts, repeating several times on the last day
12. When everything has cooled, place in jars
