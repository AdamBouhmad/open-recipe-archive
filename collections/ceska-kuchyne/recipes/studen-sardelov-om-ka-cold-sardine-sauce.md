## Ingredients

- four sardines
- six hard-boiled egg yolks
- chopped fresh parsley
- wine vinegar
- fine olive oil or fresh melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean four sardines and remove all bones from them
2. Mash them in a cup with six hard-boiled egg yolks and chopped fresh parsley
3. Mix this with wine vinegar and fine olive oil or fresh melted butter
4. Pour into a serving dish
