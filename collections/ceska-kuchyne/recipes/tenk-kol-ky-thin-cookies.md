## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram butter
- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix butter, flour, sugar, eggs, and sour cream to form dough
2. Knead the dough well and roll it out
3. Cut cookies with a small form or glass
4. Bake on waxed sheet and sprinkle with sugar
