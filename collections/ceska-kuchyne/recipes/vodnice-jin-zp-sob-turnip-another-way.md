## Ingredients

- mutton or pork meat
- water
- vegetables
- turnip
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- pale roux

## Directions

1. Clean mutton or pork meat and cook in just enough water as is needed; add all vegetables to the sauce.
2. Meanwhile, peel the turnip, slice into leaves, let it come to a boil, strain the water; salt and pepper the turnip.
3. Add only a small piece of sugar so it is not bitter.
4. Cook for a while, then add pale roux and meat, and cook everything for a while before serving.
