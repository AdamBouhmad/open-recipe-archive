## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites
- 6-8 chocolate tablets
- 4-6 tablespoons ground sugar
- 120 grams (8 lots) walnuts or hazelnuts, grated

## Directions

1. Make stiff snow from 8 egg whites
2. Grate 6-8 chocolate tablets and add to whites
3. Add 4-6 tablespoons ground sugar
4. Add 120 grams grated walnuts or hazelnuts
5. Place spoonfuls on waxed sheet
6. Sprinkle with grated chocolate
7. Dry in oven
8. Serve with tea or other candies
