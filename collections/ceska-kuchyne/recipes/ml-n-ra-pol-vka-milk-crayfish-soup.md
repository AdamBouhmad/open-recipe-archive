## Ingredients

- 15-20 small crayfish
- salted water
- roll toasted in oven
- 125 grams butter
- 2-2.5 liters fresh milk
- salt
- sugar
- roll cut into cubes and fried

## Directions

1. Select 15-20 small crayfish, cook in salted water, shell the meat from tails, remove intestines and gallbladder
2. Crush the rest completely in a mortar with toasted roll and 125 grams of butter
3. Place this mixture on a baking sheet, cook with 2-2.5 liters of fresh milk, salt and sweeten
4. Strain everything
5. Add roll cut into cubes and fried to the soup
