## Ingredients

- citron peel
- wine
- almonds

## Directions

1. Cut citron peel into long thin slices
2. Boil in wine
3. Add some cut almonds
4. Strain everything
5. Make cake from the skins
6. Lay the prepared citron peel on top
7. Decorate with pastry strips laid crosswise
8. Bake slowly
