## Ingredients

- 375 grams butter
- four eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- 250 grams grated roll
- half liter sour cream
- a little salt
- 375 grams sugar
- zest of six lemons
- lemon juice
- grated nutmeg
- 50 grams finely chopped citron peel
- 10 egg whites for snow

## Directions

1. Beat 375 grams butter until foamy
2. Gradually add four eggs, ten egg yolks, 250 grams grated roll, half liter sour cream, a little salt, 375 grams sugar thoroughly rubbed with zest of six lemons, lemon juice, grated nutmeg, 50 grams finely chopped citron peel and snow from ten egg whites
3. Mix everything thoroughly, put in a well-greased mold and bake for two hours
