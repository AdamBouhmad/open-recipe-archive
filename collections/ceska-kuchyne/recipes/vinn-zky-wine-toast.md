## Ingredients

- one roll or white bread, grated
- a handful of peeled and chopped almonds
- lemon zest
- orange zest
- a little cinnamon
- two egg yolks
- two egg whites, beaten to snow
- unsalted butter or good lard for frying
- sugar
- white or red wine

## Directions

1. Grate one roll or white bread.
2. Mix together a handful of peeled and chopped almonds, lemon zest, orange zest, a little cinnamon, two egg yolks, and the beaten egg whites from two eggs, adding the egg white snow last.
3. Spread this mixture on 15-16 slices of white bread.
4. Fry in unsalted butter or good lard with the spread side down.
5. Turn and fry the other side very lightly so it does not burn.
6. Dust with sugar as desired and pour over with white or red wine.
