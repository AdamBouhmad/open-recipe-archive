## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and carefully remove all green leaves so the florets do not break
2. Wash it and place in salted water with a piece of butter
3. Cook until tender
4. Meanwhile, grate the breadcrust and fry it in butter
5. Carefully place the cooked cauliflower on a dish
6. Sprinkle with the fried breadcrumbs and pour hot melted butter over it
