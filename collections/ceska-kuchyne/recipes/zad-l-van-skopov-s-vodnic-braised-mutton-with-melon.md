## Ingredients

- melon
- water
- mutton meat
- mutton broth
- piece of sugar
- sugar caramelized to pink

## Directions

1. Peel melon and cut into thin slices
2. Place on a baking pan, pour water over it, and let it come to a boil
3. Drain water
4. At the same time, prepare mutton meat and pour broth over the melon
5. Add a piece of sugar and braise until tender, adding more broth as needed
6. Press pale gravy through a strainer and add a piece of sugar caramelized to pink
7. Cut meat into pieces, add to the braised melon and simmer briefly
