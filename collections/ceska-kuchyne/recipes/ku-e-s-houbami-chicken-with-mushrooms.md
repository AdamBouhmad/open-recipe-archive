## Ingredients

- well-cleaned mushroom caps
- piece of fresh butter
- a little green parsley
- cleaned chicken, cut into pieces
- salt
- beef broth
- flour
- sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Slice the well-cleaned mushroom caps and let them braise briefly in a piece of fresh butter and a little green parsley
2. Place cleaned chicken cut into pieces on top of them, salt them and let braise under a cover, being careful not to burn
3. When softened and the liquid begins to evaporate, dust lightly with flour, add a little beef broth and let it come to a boil
4. Strain and serve
5. To the sauce you can also add a little cooked sweet sour cream
