## Ingredients

- goose giblets (excluding liver)
- water (1.5 liters per giblets from one goose)
- vegetables of all kinds
- salt
- herbs
- butter (if goose is lean)
- rye
- thin noodles
- mushrooms or cauliflower (optional)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the goose giblets, chop them into small pieces, rinse them and place in water
2. Add all kinds of vegetables, salt and herbs
3. If the goose was lean, add a piece of butter and let it simmer for a while
4. Meanwhile, simmer rye in butter and pour the giblet soup over it; or make thin noodles
5. In the latter case, strain the soup, add flour and if there are mushrooms or cauliflower, add them with parsley
