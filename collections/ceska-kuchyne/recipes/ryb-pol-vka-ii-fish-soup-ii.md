## Ingredients

- half kilogram of fish
- salted water
- 2 hard egg yolks
- 2 rolls soaked in milk and wrung out
- roux
- soup from peas and vegetables
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Cook half kilogram of fish in salted water and remove bones
2. Pound 2 hard egg yolks and 2 rolls soaked in milk and wrung out in a mortar
3. Mix this mixture with roux and soup from peas and vegetables, cook, strain
4. Pour over fried roll
