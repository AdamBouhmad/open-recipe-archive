## Ingredients

- 76 dekagrams peeled dry almonds
- 59 dekagrams sugar mixed with vanilla or lemon
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Grind 76 dekagrams of peeled dry almonds like flour
2. Add 59 dekagrams of sugar mixed with vanilla or lemon
3. Mix well
4. Place a spoonful of the mixture in a mortar, add some egg white, and grind like dough
5. Continue until 4 egg whites are used
6. Mix everything well
7. Put the mixture in a pastry bag and pipe directly onto waxed paper in crescent shapes
8. Bake slowly
