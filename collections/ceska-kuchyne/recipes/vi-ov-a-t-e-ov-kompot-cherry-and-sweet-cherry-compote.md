## Ingredients

- cherries and sweet cherries
- sugar
- currant juice

## Directions

1. Either pit the cherries and sweet cherries, or place them whole in sugar boiled to the sixth degree
2. Boil several times with this
3. Remove the cherries and sweet cherries carefully with a spoon and place on a sieve
4. Boil part of the syrup with a little currant juice into a puree and arrange it so that when cold it can be poured over the cherries
