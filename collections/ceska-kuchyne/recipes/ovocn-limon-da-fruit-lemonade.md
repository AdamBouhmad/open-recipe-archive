## Ingredients

- fresh cold water
- raspberry, strawberry, cherry, currant or other fruit juice
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. To a quart of fresh cold water, add one cup of raspberry, strawberry, cherry, currant or other fruit juice
2. Add strained juice from two lemons
3. Sweeten adequately with pounded sugar and mix well
