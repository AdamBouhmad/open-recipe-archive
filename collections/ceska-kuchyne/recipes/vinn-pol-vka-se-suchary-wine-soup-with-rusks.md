## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- about 8 rusks pounded to powder
- 1/2 bottle wine
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil water with cinnamon, finely cut lemon peel, and about 8 rusks pounded to powder for at least a quarter hour
2. Strain through fine sieve
3. Add half a bottle of wine
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Bring to a boil again
6. Finally add several whisked egg yolks
