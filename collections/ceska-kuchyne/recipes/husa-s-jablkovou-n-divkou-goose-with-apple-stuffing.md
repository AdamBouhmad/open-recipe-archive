## Ingredients

- One goose, prepared for roasting
- Two cups of stewed, softened and mixed apples
- One cup of grated bread
- A little sage leaves, roasted and sifted
- One onion, finely chopped
- A little red pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the goose for roasting as previously described.
2. Mix together the stewed apples, grated bread, sage leaves, finely chopped onion, and red pepper.
3. Stuff the goose with this mixture.
4. Sew it closed and roast by the previous method.
