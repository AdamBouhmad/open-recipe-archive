## Ingredients

- small cucumbers
- water
- wine vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- alum powder
- cinnamon bark
- whole cloves

## Directions

1. Take small cucumbers, the smaller the better.
2. Pour boiling water over them and let them sit for 4 hours.
3. Boil wine vinegar with sugar and spices: for one gallon of vinegar add one cup of sugar, one cup of salt, one teaspoon of alum powder (which helps preserve color and firmness), one ounce of broken cinnamon bark, and one spoon of whole cloves.
4. Let everything boil for about 15 minutes.
5. Remove the cucumbers from the water and pack them into jars.
6. Pour the hot vinegar and spices over them and screw the jars shut while hot.
