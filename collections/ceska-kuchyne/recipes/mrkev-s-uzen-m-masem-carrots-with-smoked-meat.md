## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Water or broth
- Smoked meat scraps
- Flour roux
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Scrape, rinse and cut carrots into cubes.
2. Cook in water or broth, then add chopped pieces of smoked meat.
3. Mix in a flour roux, pepper, vinegar and salt; cook everything well together.
4. Serve on a dish with cooked potatoes.
