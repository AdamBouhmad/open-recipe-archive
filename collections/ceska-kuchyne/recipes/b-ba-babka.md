## Ingredients

- fourteen hard rolls
- two handfuls almonds
- raisins
- six dekagrams crushed sugar
- cinnamon
- six larger apples or pears
- butter
- three and three-quarters deciliters good sweet sour cream
- three eggs
- six dekagrams butter

## Directions

1. Cut fourteen hard rolls in half, then into small flat cakes.
2. Scald two handfuls almonds, peel, and chop coarsely; wash raisins in cold water, let dry, and pick over.
3. Mix six dekagrams crushed sugar with cinnamon; peel and slice six larger apples or pears and mix everything together.
4. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated bread.
5. Place mixture in mold, pour over three and three-quarters deciliters good sweet sour cream mixed with three eggs and six dekagrams butter.
6. Top with pieces of butter and bake in the oven.
7. If fresh fruit is unavailable, cottage cheese or dried plums may be used instead of apples or pears.
