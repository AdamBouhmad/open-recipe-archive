## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter good sour cream
- 5 eggs
- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) cooked grated potatoes
- 4-5 spoonfuls flour
- 3 bread rolls cut into cubes and fried
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix sour cream with eggs
2. Add cooked grated potatoes, flour, fried bread roll cubes, salt, and nutmeg
3. Work dough until it does not stick to roller
4. Make dumplings by spoon
5. Cook in salted water
