## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- hot water
- boiling milk
- five eggs
- sugar
- lemon juice or lemon rind
- butter
- white bread or hardtack

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in hot water
2. Pour boiling milk over it
3. Let it cook slowly on a low heat until it becomes a thin porridge, about half-cooked
4. When it cools, beat five eggs and separate the yolks from the whites
5. Beat the yolks separately with sugar
6. Mix with the porridge
7. Add lemon juice or just the rind
8. Finally beat the egg whites separately into snow and gently fold into the porridge
9. Pour into a mold which has been previously buttered and sprinkled with white bread or hardtack
10. Place the mold in another dish with boiling water, cover it, and let it cook slowly on low heat for three-quarters of an hour
11. Then put it in the oven for a quarter hour so the top becomes golden
12. Bring every pudding immediately from the oven to table, as when it sits for a long time it becomes watery
