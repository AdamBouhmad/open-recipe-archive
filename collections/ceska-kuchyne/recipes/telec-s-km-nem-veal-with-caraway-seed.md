## Ingredients

- two pounds veal
- two heaping tablespoons butter
- salt
- caraway seed
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of veal into pieces and place in a pan
2. Add two heaping tablespoons of butter, a little salt, a little caraway seed
3. Add a little water and let it stew quickly
4. When it becomes tender, dust with flour
5. Add a little boiling water and let it come to a boil
6. Place on a platter and pour the gravy over it
