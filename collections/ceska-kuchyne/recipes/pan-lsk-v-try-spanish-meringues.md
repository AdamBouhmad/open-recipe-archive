## Ingredients

- 10 egg whites
- sugar (1 tablespoon per egg white)
- 24 dekagrams almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Count 1 tablespoon of pounded sugar per egg white
3. Blanch and chop 24 dekagrams almonds into noodles, let them dry, and add to the meringue
4. Mix well together
5. Form mounds on a waxed or paper-covered baking sheet
6. Dry for 3 hours in a warm oven
