## Ingredients

- 250 grams sugar
- 250 grams unpeeled almonds
- 12 crushed and well-beaten egg yolks
- 24 grams ground cinnamon
- lemon zest shavings
- candied citron zest finely cut
- egg white snow from 6 to 8 whites

## Directions

1. Take 250 grams sugar and 250 grams unpeeled almonds
2. Beat 12 egg yolks well and add to the almonds
3. Add 24 grams ground cinnamon, lemon zest shavings, and finely cut candied citron zest to everything
4. Add stiff egg white snow from 6 to 8 whites
5. Pour into a baking form
6. Bake slowly
7. Cover the top with sugar glaze
