## Ingredients

- dough for noodles
- spinach
- clove of garlic
- marjoram
- flowers
- butter
- onion
- grated bread crumbs
- egg white

## Directions

1. Make dough like for noodles and roll out
2. Cut square pieces with a cutter
3. Clean and sort spinach, salt only the leaves and scald
4. Drain with cold water
5. Chop or press through sieve
6. Add minced garlic clove, marjoram, and flowers
7. Fry butter with onion until half done
8. Add spoon of grated bread crumbs
9. Mix in spinach and cool
10. Place on dough squares
11. Brush edges with egg white
12. Cover with another square
13. Boil in salted water
14. Drain
15. Sprinkle with grated bread and pour melted butter over
16. Instead of spinach, brain, chopped meat, etc. may be used
