## Ingredients

- one quart cornmeal
- two cups sifted rye flour
- one cup black syrup (New Orleans molasses)
- one small teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water
- sour milk as needed
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put one quart of cornmeal and two cups of sifted rye flour in a bowl and add one cup of black syrup
2. Add one small teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in hot water and enough sour milk so that the dough is somewhat thicker than for pancakes
3. Season with salt and mix everything well
4. Pour it into a baking pan and bake at a moderate temperature for four hours
