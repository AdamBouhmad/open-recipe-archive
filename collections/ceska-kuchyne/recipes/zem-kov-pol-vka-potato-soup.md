## Ingredients

- several potatoes
- onion
- celery
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Wash and slice potatoes, boil them with onion and celery in water
2. Mash, strain the soup, add butter, salt, pepper and chopped parsley
