## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 small spoon mustard
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- 4 tablespoons oil
- 3 tablespoons wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the potatoes
2. While still warm, beat the hard-boiled eggs with a small spoon of mustard, salt, pepper, finely chopped onion, 4 tablespoons of oil, and 3 tablespoons of wine vinegar
3. Continue mixing until a sauce forms
4. Pour the sauce over 75 decagrams (40 lots) of potatoes
