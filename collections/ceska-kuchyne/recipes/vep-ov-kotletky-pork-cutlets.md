## Ingredients

- Pork cutlets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- White bread or crackers
- Butter or lard
- Onion
- Sage
- Parsley butter
- Potato croquettes

## Directions

1. Neatly trim and pound the pork cutlets
2. Salt, pepper, dip in beaten eggs, coat in grated white bread or crumbled crackers and fry in hot butter or lard until golden
3. You can mix a little finely chopped onion and sage into the bread
4. Arrange on a hot platter, top with pieces of parsley butter and surround with potato croquettes
5. You can also serve with the same sauces as veal cutlets or garnish with various vegetables
