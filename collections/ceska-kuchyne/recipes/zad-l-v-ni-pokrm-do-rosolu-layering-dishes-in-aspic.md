## Ingredients

- aspic
- various vegetables and meats
- red beets
- eggs
- cucumbers
- beans
- parsley
- cooked tongue
- decorative leaves and flowers

## Directions

1. Layer various dishes into molds with aspic in stages
2. First layer of aspic poured into molds must be completely set before adding food items for aspic
3. Place food items on the set first layer
4. Slowly pour new layer of melted aspic on top
5. When this layer sets, repeat the process as with the first layer
6. In the first layer place various items such as red beets, eggs, cucumbers, beans, cooked parsley, and tongue arranged in a pleasing pattern
7. Use various leaves and flowers of camellias and similar for the first layer of aspic for decoration
