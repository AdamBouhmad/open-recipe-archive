## Ingredients

- Bread
- Butter
- 3 cloves garlic
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut bread into small slices
2. Fry in butter until golden
3. Crush 3 cloves garlic with butter and salt
4. Boil briefly with water
5. Pour over the fried bread
6. Alternatively, onion may be used instead of garlic
