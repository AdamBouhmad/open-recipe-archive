## Ingredients

- game haunch or shoulder
- salt
- wooden mallet
- 8 juniper berries
- thyme
- bay leaf
- ginger
- lemon zest
- whole pepper
- new spices
- 3 onions
- celery
- parsley root
- carrots
- vinegar
- water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Carefully scrape the haunch or shoulder with a sharp knife and marinate as follows: salt it, pound well with a wooden mallet.
2. Add 8 juniper berries, thyme, bay leaf, ginger, lemon zest, whole pepper, new spices, and 3 onions cut into slices, celery, parsley root and carrots, add vinegar and water as needed.
3. Turn the haunch occasionally.
4. When ready to roast, pour off half the liquid and roast in the other half until almost all the juices have cooked away.
5. Remove the outer skin from the roast, slice neatly, strain the remaining sauce and garnish with lemon.
