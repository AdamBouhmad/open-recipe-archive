## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Salt
- Melted lard
- Onions, cut into pieces
- Truffles
- Mushrooms
- Bay leaf
- Ham, finely sliced
- Vegetables
- Spices
- White wine
- Coulis
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcock but do not cook it, salt it
2. Place it on a roasting pan in melted lard
3. Lay several pieces of sliced onion, truffles and mushrooms under it, along with bay leaf, finely sliced ham, vegetables and spices
4. Braise the woodcock in this mixture for about ten minutes
5. Pour good white wine and coulis over it, season with pepper and braise completely
6. Remove the fat, strain the sauce and adjust the taste with lemon juice
