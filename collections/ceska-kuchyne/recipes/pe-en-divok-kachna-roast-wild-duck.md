## Ingredients

- 1 part vinegar
- 2 parts water
- several grains of allspice
- 1 onion
- a piece of carrot
- a piece of lemon peel
- salt
- wild duck
- lard, thinly sliced
- butter
- sour cream
- marinade

## Directions

1. Boil together in a pot one part vinegar, two parts water, several grains of allspice, one onion, a piece of carrot, a piece of lemon peel and some salt
2. Let this cool, then pour it over well-cleaned wild duck placed in a deep clay vessel and let it lie there for several days
3. When ready to prepare, dry the duck, salt it, lard it on the breast with four to six rows of thinly sliced lard, place it on a baking pan, put pieces of butter on it and let it roast in the oven until tender
4. During roasting, baste it frequently with sour cream and the marinade in which it was marinated
5. When roasted, arrange it on a platter and pour the baked sauce over it
