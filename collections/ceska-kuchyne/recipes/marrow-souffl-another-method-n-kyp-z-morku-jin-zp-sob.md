## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) white marrow
- 8 egg yolks
- flower water
- 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) white flour
- salt
- 8 egg whites
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Remove bones and membranes from 1/8 kilogram white marrow
2. Beat for 1/2 hour
3. Add 8 egg yolks, flower water, 1/8 kilogram white flour and salt
4. Add stiff snow made from 8 egg whites
5. Mix everything and either wrap in cloth or place in a greased closed mold
6. Place the mold in boiling water and cook until a stick comes out clean
