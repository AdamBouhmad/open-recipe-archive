## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- beef
- 45 grams fish bone
- salt
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Remove bones from the fish, chop finely, and place in water with beef and at least 45 grams fish bone
2. Add salt and vegetables as in other broths
3. Cook and strain through a sieve so it strains slowly
4. Place on heat and cook
5. Chop fish innards and add to broth, or alternatively leave uncooked like ordinary beef broth and add toasted bread roll
