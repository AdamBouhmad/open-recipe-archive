## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- wine

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 liter cream with 2 teaspoons sugar
2. Finish with 2 egg yolks and wine
3. Mix everything
4. Strain
