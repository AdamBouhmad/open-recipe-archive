## Ingredients

- veal leg
- 4 sardeles
- 2 cloves garlic
- salt
- sardele butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from a fine veal leg and tenderize it well by pounding
2. Clean and cut 4 sardeles and 2 cloves of garlic into thin strips
3. Using a thin sharp knife, make holes in the leg and insert a piece of sardele and garlic in each hole
4. Cover the leg with a clean cloth and let it rest for half an hour
5. Prepare and roast according to the previous method, using sardele butter instead of ordinary butter if desired
6. Half an hour before serving, baste with half a cup of good sour cream, let it roast a bit longer
7. Place whole or sliced on a platter, pour a little sauce over it and serve the remaining sauce in a gravy boat
