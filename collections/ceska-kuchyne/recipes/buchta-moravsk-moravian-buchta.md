## Ingredients

- 15 egg whites
- 500 grams beaten sugar
- 24 egg yolks
- lemon zest from one lemon
- 250 grams grated almonds
- 500 grams sifted flour

## Directions

1. Make stiff snow from 15 egg whites
2. Mix in 500 grams of beaten sugar
3. Add 24 egg yolks
4. Add chopped zest from one lemon
5. Beat everything for half an hour
6. Mix in 250 grams of grated almonds
7. Mix everything and slowly add 500 grams of sifted flour
8. Bake
