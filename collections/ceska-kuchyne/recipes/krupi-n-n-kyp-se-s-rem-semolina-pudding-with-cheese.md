## Ingredients

- semolina porridge
- broth
- sour cream
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg whites
- breadcrumbs
- ham or other meat

## Directions

1. Cook semolina porridge with broth, sour cream and butter
2. Mix the cooled porridge with egg yolks, salt, nutmeg and snow, put into a greased form sprinkled with breadcrumbs and place it in hot water
3. Turn out the pudding onto the table, usually served with ham or other meat
