## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) butter
- Flour (added by the spoonful)
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) sweet sour cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) ground sugar
- 5 tablespoons yeast (leavening)
- Lemon zest, finely chopped
- Salt
- A little flower essence

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, add flour by the spoonful
2. In sweet sour cream, beat 8 egg yolks with 12 decagrams ground sugar
3. Add this mixture bit by bit to the butter, finally add 5 tablespoons yeast (leavening)
4. Beat the dough until bubbles appear
5. Add finely chopped lemon zest, salt and a little flower essence
6. When risen, make large thin cakes, brush with something and bake
