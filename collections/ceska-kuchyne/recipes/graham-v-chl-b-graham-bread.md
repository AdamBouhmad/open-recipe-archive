## Ingredients

- two cups sweet milk
- two-thirds cup black syrup (molasses)
- salt to taste
- one teaspoon baking soda dissolved in water
- one quart graham flour

## Directions

1. Put two cups of sweet milk in a bowl and add two-thirds cup of black syrup and a little salt
2. Add one teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in water and mix well
3. Add one quart of flour, beat well with a spoon or beater
4. Pour into a greased baking pan and bake at a moderate temperature for approximately three-quarters of an hour
