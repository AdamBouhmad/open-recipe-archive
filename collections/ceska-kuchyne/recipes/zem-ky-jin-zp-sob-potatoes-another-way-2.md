## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt water
- celery
- leek
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cut potatoes into pieces
2. Cook in salted water with celery and leek
3. Before placing on a dish, brush with butter
4. Sprinkle with chopped parsley
