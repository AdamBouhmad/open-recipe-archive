## Ingredients

- very ripe, large quinces
- soft water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select very ripe, large quinces, cut them into long, narrow pieces, remove the seeds, peel the quinces
2. Cook loosely in soft water, remove softened pieces, let the remaining pieces cook further until soft, then remove and place on a sieve to cool and dry
3. Arrange on a dish, pour over with dissolved sugar
4. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it more concentrated than the day before, add the quinces, cook slowly with it
5. Repeat this for three more days, and when the quinces are completely candied and the sugar turns to pearls, place in containers, and when dry, wrap them
6.
