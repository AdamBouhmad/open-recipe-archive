## Ingredients

- bean pods
- salt (half kilogram per ten kilograms of pods)

## Directions

1. Cut the bean pods into noodles
2. Mix with salt
3. Layer the cut pod noodles into stone vessels or barrels
4. Weight down the top with a board and stone
5. Arrange everything as explained in the pickle cabbage method
