## Ingredients

- fully developed but not quite ripe apricots
- sugar
- apple puree

## Directions

1. Take fully developed but not quite ripe apricots
2. Halve and peel them
3. Boil slowly in dissolved sugar
4. When cooled, place on sieves
5. Boil the sugar with a little apple puree to the pearl stage
6. Place the apricots into it
7. Arrange on a dish, pour over with sugar and sprinkle with finely ground sugar
