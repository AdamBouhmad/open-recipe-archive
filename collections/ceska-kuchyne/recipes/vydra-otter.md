## Ingredients

- otter
- 2 liters vinegar
- several onions
- dough
- marjoram
- sage
- half a lemon, sliced into leaves
- several cloves
- pepper
- butter
- lemon leaves
- bay leaf
- spices
- salt
- mushrooms, grated

## Directions

1. Cut off the head, feet, and tail; soak the meat in water for half an hour
2. Bake on a baking sheet and baste with marinade made from 2 liters of vinegar, several onions, dough, marjoram, sage, half a lemon sliced into leaves, several cloves, and pepper
3. After the meat has been marinated for 2 days, brown onions in butter
4. Add several lemon leaves, bay leaf, pepper, spices, salt, and several spoonfuls of vinegar
5. Baste the roast with this mixture
6. When it begins to turn red, add 1 1/4 liters of wine and as much vinegar
7. Add roux and grated mushrooms
8. Strain and add lemon juice with potatoes
