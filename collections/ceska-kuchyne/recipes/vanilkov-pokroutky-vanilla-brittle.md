## Ingredients

- vanilla
- soft boiling water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pound vanilla finely and sieve it
2. Place it in a closed pot
3. Add enough hot soft water according to the amount of vanilla
4. Seal the pot tightly and let it sit overnight
5. In the morning strain the contents and press out the vanilla completely
6. Boil the juice with sugar until thickened
7. Pour onto paper, spread it out and when hardened and dried, carefully peel off and cut into pieces of any size
