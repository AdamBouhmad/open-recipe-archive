## Ingredients

- fresh butter or bacon sliced into thin pieces
- thyme
- parsley
- whole onion
- peppercorns
- ginger
- cloves
- salt
- wine vinegar
- fresh butter
- flour
- sugar
- beef broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Put a piece of fresh butter or thinly sliced bacon on a roasting pan with a little thyme and parsley, a whole onion, a few peppercorns, a piece of ginger and four cloves
2. Place the squabs on it, salt them and pour two spoonfuls of wine vinegar over them
3. Cover the pan well and let the squabs braise slowly until tender
4. Remove them to another pan, cut them in half and put them on a warm place
5. Meanwhile, put a piece of fresh butter on a small pan to heat
6. Add two scant spoonfuls of flour and a spoonful of sugar and make a brown roux
7. Mix it with the sauce from the squabs
8. Add beef broth and let it boil
9. Strain it through a sieve over the cut squabs
10. Let them heat for about a minute longer on the fire but it must not boil anymore
11. Bring it then immediately to the table
12. You can also add lemon cut into rounds to the sauce
