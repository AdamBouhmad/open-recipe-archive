## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- salt
- bacon
- 8 onions
- pepper
- new spices
- lemon zest
- capers
- beef broth
- bread, grated
- butter or beef fat
- cranberries

## Directions

1. Clean the venison haunch and salt it.
2. Cut bacon into long strips and lard the meat by threading the bacon through the entire piece.
3. Place on a baking pan with finely chopped onions, pepper, new spices, lemon zest cut into strips, a few capers, and cover with beef broth.
4. Baste frequently until the roast is completely tender.
5. Fry half a grated bread roll in butter or beef fat and sprinkle over the roast.
6. Braise briefly and pour the pan juices over.
7. Garnish with cranberries. This method can also be used for venison and roe deer.
