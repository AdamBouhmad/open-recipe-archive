## Ingredients

- Bread rolls or white bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut bread rolls or white bread into quarter-inch thick slices of the same size
2. Cut pieces from them either oblong, half a quarter inch long and two inches wide, or round about three inches in diameter
3. Fry them in butter until golden
4. When cooled, pipe a piece of crab or other butter around the edge with a piping cone or funnel
5. Fill the middle and arrange on a plate covered with a napkin, one beside the other
