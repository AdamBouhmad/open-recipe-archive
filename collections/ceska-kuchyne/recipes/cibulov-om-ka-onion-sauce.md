## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- onion
- good hearty broth
- caraway

## Directions

1. Roast about two spoonfuls of flour in butter
2. Add onion cut into fine pieces
3. Let all brown together with constant stirring until red
4. Add good hearty broth
5. Boil all together with constant stirring
6. Caraway can be added if desired
7. Strain cooked sauce through a sieve
