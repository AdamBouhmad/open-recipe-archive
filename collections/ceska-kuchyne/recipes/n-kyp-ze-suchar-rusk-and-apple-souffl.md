## Ingredients

- about ten juicy apples
- sugar
- cinnamon, ground
- lemon zest, grated
- butter
- rusks
- cream
- 1 to 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Peel about ten juicy apples, cut them into fine slices
2. Sprinkle with sugar, ground cinnamon, and grated lemon zest
3. Put half of the prepared apples in a mold buttered and oiled
4. Cover with a layer of rusks arranged so tightly together that no apple is visible
5. Arrange the second half of the prepared apples on the rusks and cover with rusks again
6. Put sufficient butter between the rusks and apples
7. Pour cream sweetened with sugar into which 2 or 3 beaten eggs have been mixed over everything
8. Bake at moderate heat slowly for about three quarters of an hour until done and serve to the table
