## Ingredients

- whipped cream made from rice, semolina, sago or similar
- milk
- egg yolk
- sweet spiced cream

## Directions

1. Mix whipped cream with milk into a porridge
2. Add egg yolk and sweet spiced cream
3. Mix slowly and ensure neither ingredient is too hot or too cold
4. Optionally add fish bladder to help the cream thicken
