## Ingredients

- four cups flour
- two teaspoons baking powder
- salt
- half a cup butter, finely crumbled
- milk

## Directions

1. Put four cups flour in a bowl
2. Mix in two teaspoons baking powder and a little salt
3. Mix well
4. Add half a cup finely crumbled butter and mix until all flour is well blended with butter
5. Add milk to achieve the desired consistency
