## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 6 tablespoons soup
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Mix well 6 egg yolks, 2 egg whites, and 6 tablespoons of soup.
2. Strain and pour into greased small molds.
3. Place in the oven until the mixture sets.
4. Turn out onto a plate and serve with soup.
