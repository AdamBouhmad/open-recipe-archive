## Ingredients

- 10 deca grated hard bread roll
- broth
- 7 deca marrow
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- 2 eggs
- 23 deca finely chopped ham

## Directions

1. Soak grated bread roll in broth and squeeze out
2. Add marrow browned with onion and parsley
3. Add pepper, 2 eggs, and finely chopped ham
4. Mix everything together well
5. Use as a very tasty stuffing
