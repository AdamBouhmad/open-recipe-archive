## Ingredients

- most beautiful gooseberry clusters
- egg white
- powdered sugar
- thick cooked sugar

## Directions

1. Select the most beautiful gooseberry clusters and dry them.
2. Place them on a small baking pan and mix with beaten egg white, then coat them several times in powdered sugar — then place in thick cooked sugar, put them on a sieve on warm stove to dry well.
