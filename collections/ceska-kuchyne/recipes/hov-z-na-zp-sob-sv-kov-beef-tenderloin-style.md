## Ingredients

- 4 pounds beef without bone
- 1 pound bacon
- onion
- allspice berries
- ginger
- pepper
- bay leaves
- flour
- water
- lemon juice
- lemon zest
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean 4 pounds of beef thoroughly, trimming fat and connective tissue
2. Cut 1 pound of bacon into noodle-like strips and pierce the meat with them
3. Place one larger onion, a few allspice berries, a piece of ginger, pepper, and two bay leaves in a baking dish, then place the meat in it
4. Salt the meat, dust with flour, and pour over with two spoonfuls of water
5. Place in the oven, basting it frequently
6. When the meat is roasted, place on a serving dish and slice into cutlets
7. Add a little lemon juice and finely chopped lemon zest to the gravy, and sweeten it slightly to make it somewhat sweet
8. Pour this over the cutlets or serve the gravy separately
