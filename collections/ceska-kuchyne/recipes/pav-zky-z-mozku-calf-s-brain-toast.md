## Ingredients

- calf brain
- salt
- pepper
- chives
- parsley
- lemon peel
- bread or rolls
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the brain thoroughly and poach it
2. Chop the brain very finely
3. Salt and season with ground pepper
4. Add finely chopped chives, finely chopped parsley, and a bit of lemon peel
5. Mix everything together
6. Cut thin slices from hardened bread or rolls
7. Spread generously with the brain mixture
8. Cover with another slice of bread or roll
9. Dip in beaten eggs
10. Coat in breadcrumbs
11. Fry in clarified butter
