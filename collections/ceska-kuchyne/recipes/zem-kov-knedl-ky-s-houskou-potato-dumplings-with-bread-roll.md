## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) cooked grated potatoes
- 3-4 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls flour
- 1 spoonful salt
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- nutmeg
- snow from egg whites
- fried bread roll cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Mix cooked grated potatoes with egg yolks, flour, salt, butter, nutmeg, and snow from egg whites
2. Make small dumplings
3. Place fried bread roll cubes in center of each
4. Cook in salted water
