## Ingredients

- 1 houska or hard white bread
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- lemon zest
- 1-2 tablespoons grated almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 1 cup hot wine with sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the houska or hard white bread finely
2. Cream together 2 tablespoons butter and add 4 egg yolks one by one
3. Add 1 tablespoon sugar for each yolk
4. Add lemon zest and 1-2 tablespoons grated almonds
5. Mix in the grated bread, using about 4 tablespoons
6. Whip the 4 egg whites into stiff peaks, fold in three times using only a spoon, do not beat
7. Pour into a baking dish and bake
8. When baked, pour over 1 cup hot wine sweetened with sugar
