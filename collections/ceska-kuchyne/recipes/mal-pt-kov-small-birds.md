## Ingredients

- small birds, plucked
- butter
- onion
- bread crumbs, toasted and grated

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads of plucked small birds, clean them and salt them
2. Put a piece of butter on a small baking pan, add a piece of onion and place the birds on it breast-side down
3. Let the birds roast for about a quarter hour over brisk heat and when they begin to brown, sprinkle them with toasted grated bread crumbs
4. When the butter begins to sizzle, add several spoonfuls of broth and bring quickly to the table
