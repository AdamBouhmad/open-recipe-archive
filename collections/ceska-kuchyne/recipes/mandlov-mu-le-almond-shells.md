## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams almonds
- 18 dekagrams sugar, finely pounded
- lemon peel, finely chopped
- clove
- cinnamon
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Blanch and dry the almonds, then chop finely
2. Place sugar on a baking sheet with finely chopped lemon peel, clove, and cinnamon
3. When the sugar is brown, add the almonds
4. Grease small molds with butter and bake the mixture in them
