## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- three eggs
- two small pots of fine farina
- half a pound of finely minced smoked meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Cream two tablespoons of butter with three eggs
2. Add two small pots of fine farina and half a pound of finely minced smoked meat
3. Salt slightly and mix well
4. With a spoon, take out pieces and drop into boiling salted water
5. Remove the dumplings with a skimming spoon, place on a dish
6. Sprinkle with bread roll or bread fried in butter and pour melted fresh butter over them
