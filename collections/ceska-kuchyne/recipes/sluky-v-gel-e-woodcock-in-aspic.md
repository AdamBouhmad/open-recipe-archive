## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Bones from woodcock
- Butter
- Fine minced game or chicken
- Lard
- Truffles, chopped
- Smoked tongue
- Aspic

## Directions

1. Pluck and cook the woodcock, remove the bones
2. Pound the bones in a mortar, braise them in butter, press through a fine sieve
3. Mix with fine minced game or chicken and use for stuffing the woodcock
4. Sew up the stuffed woodcock, wrap it in lard, roast it buttered
5. When roasted, place it on ice, cover it with chopped truffles and smoked tongue
6. Pour aspic over everything and let it set
7. Arrange decoratively for serving
