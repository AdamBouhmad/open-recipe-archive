## Ingredients

- Green bean pods
- Butter
- Pepper
- Wine vinegar
- Salt
- Beef broth
- Roux
- Sugar
- White sugar
- Sour cream or lemon juice
- Boiled pork or veal cutlets

## Directions

1. Remove the tough strings from the green beans, slice diagonally into thin strips or into cubes, or leave whole
2. Place on a baking pan with fresh butter, pepper, wine vinegar, salt, and beef broth as needed, and stew until tender
3. If needed, moisten with good beef broth
4. When tender, add roux and a piece of white sugar and simmer
5. May also add good sour cream or juice from half a lemon
6. May be served with boiled pork or veal cutlets
