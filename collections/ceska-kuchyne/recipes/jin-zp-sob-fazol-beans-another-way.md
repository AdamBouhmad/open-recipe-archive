## Ingredients

- beans
- soft water
- bacon
- sour cucumbers

## Directions

1. Pick through beans, wash and cook half-done in soft water
2. Dice bacon and add to beans
3. When soft, drain the water
4. Turn out onto a plate and surround with sour cucumbers
