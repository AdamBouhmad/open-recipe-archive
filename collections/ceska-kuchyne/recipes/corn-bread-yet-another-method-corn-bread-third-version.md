## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cooled lard
- salt to taste
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 small cup sour milk or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 small cup milk

## Directions

1. Beat the egg and mix in the cooled lard, salt and sugar, beating until smooth
2. Add the sour milk or buttermilk and mix well
3. Dissolve the baking soda in 1/4 small cup milk and mix thoroughly into the batter
4. Pour onto a heated baking pan in a well-heated oven and bake
5. The bread prepared this way is very tender and flavorful, especially with butter or fresh cracklings
