## Ingredients

- three quarters cup warm water
- one tablespoon butter
- one whole egg
- one cup flour
- salt (as needed)
- apples, sliced
- ground cinnamon
- almonds, finely chopped
- raisins without seeds (sultanas)
- fresh butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take three quarters cup of warm water, add one tablespoon butter and one whole egg
2. Put one cup flour on a pastry board, make a well in it, pour the water into it and work into a nice loose dough
3. Knead well, adding flour as needed, beat thoroughly with a rolling pin and place in a clean cloth
4. Let rest under a warm bowl for about fifteen minutes
5. Meanwhile, prepare sliced apples, ground cinnamon, finely chopped almonds, raisins without seeds (sultanas), butter and sugar
6. Spread the cloth on the table, roll out a little dough and then stretch it
7. When the dough is stretched thin enough, lay apples on it, sprinkle with almonds, cinnamon and sugar
8. Take fresh butter and cut thin slices from it, lay them thickly on the apples
9. Roll up the strudel and bake
