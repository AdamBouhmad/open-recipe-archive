## Ingredients

- brown coulis or brown roux
- strong meat broth
- caraway, cleaned and crushed
- salt

## Directions

1. Use brown coulis or brown roux and boil it with strong meat broth
2. Add the cleaned and crushed caraway
3. Season with salt
4. Boil several times
5. Serve with beef or mutton
