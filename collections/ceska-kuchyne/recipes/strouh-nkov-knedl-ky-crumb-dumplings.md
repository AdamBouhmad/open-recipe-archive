## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated hard rolls, bread rolls or bread
- ½ cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 egg
- ½ cup milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Take 1 cup of grated hard rolls, bread rolls or bread and ½ cup flour
2. Mix in 1 teaspoon baking powder evenly
3. Beat 1 egg and add ½ cup milk to it
4. Pour it to the flour and add a little salt
5. Mix well, put the dough on a rolling pin, divide into 3 parts, round each one in flour, put to boil in salted water, cover and let boil ten minutes
