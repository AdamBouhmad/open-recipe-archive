## Ingredients

- Ripe elderberries
- Butter - a piece
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Wine (optional)
- Sweet cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Strip ripe elderberries
2. Sprinkle with water
3. Stew on a baking pan with butter, sugar, and cinnamon
4. Those who wish may add a little wine
5. If to be stored, cover well
6. If to be eaten soon, add a little sweet cream
