## Ingredients

- milk
- water
- butter
- oyster juice
- salt
- pepper
- oysters
- crackers

## Directions

1. Let a quart of milk, half a cup of water, half a pound of butter and the juice from one pint of oysters boil, salt and pepper to taste
2. Add the oysters and a little finely rolled crackers to thicken and let it simmer several more minutes
3. With this soup serve small crackers on a separate dish (Serves 6 people)
