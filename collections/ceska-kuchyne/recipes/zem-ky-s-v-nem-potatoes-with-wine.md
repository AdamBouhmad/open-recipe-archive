## Ingredients

- tablespoon chopped parsley
- chives
- tablespoon flour
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- good broth
- 1/4 liter wine
- cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Fry the tablespoon chopped parsley, some chives, tablespoon flour, pepper and salt in butter
2. Cook in good broth and 1/4 liter wine
3. Add several cooked potatoes sliced into thin slices
4. Stew them in the sauce for a while
