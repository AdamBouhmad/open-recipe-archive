## Ingredients

- goose
- salt
- caraway seeds
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the goose, remove the innards, wash it thoroughly, remove all membranes, salt it, season with caraway
2. Truss it, place on a baking pan, add water, and roast slowly in the oven
3. If it is fat, prick it while basting under the wings and on the back so all the fat renders out
4. Then carve: wings, neck, breasts and middle section into 4 pieces each, the rump (called the bishop) remains whole
5. Divide the legs into 2 pieces each and arrange everything on a platter
6. Cabbage is usually served with goose
