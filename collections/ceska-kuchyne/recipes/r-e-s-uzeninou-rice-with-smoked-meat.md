## Ingredients

- rice
- butter
- salt
- Parmesan cheese
- smoked meat, chopped
- eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook the rice, butter it, salt it, mix with Parmesan cheese
2. Put the rice in a greased dish, sprinkle with chopped smoked meat, pour with eggs beaten in milk
3. Sprinkle rice on top, brush with egg on the outside, sprinkle with cheese and bake
