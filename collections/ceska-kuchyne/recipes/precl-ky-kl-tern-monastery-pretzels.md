## Ingredients

- 250 grams fine flour
- 50 grams butter
- 70 grams sugar
- egg
- yolk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, egg, yolk, and salt
2. Work into a firm dough
3. Let rest for at least half an hour
4. Roll out
5. Shape into pretzels
6. Brush with beaten yolk
7. Bake on buttered baking sheet
