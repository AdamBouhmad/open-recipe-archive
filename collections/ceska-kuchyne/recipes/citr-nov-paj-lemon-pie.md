## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of milk
- one teaspoon of starch
- milk
- two eggs
- four tablespoons of sugar
- zest of one lemon
- juice of one lemon
- dough
- butter

## Directions

1. Let one coffee cup of milk heat in a pot
2. Mix one teaspoon of starch with a little milk and add it to the hot milk
3. Let it heat through and cool
4. Add two beaten eggs with four tablespoons of sugar, grated lemon zest, and juice from one lemon
5. Mix well
6. Fill two dough-lined forms with this mixture and bake in a moderate oven for half an hour
7. Optional: add one teaspoon of butter and use only yolks; in that case make stiff snow from egg whites and sugar, spread it on the baked pies and let it set in a hotter oven
8. This amount is enough for two pies
