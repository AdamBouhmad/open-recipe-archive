## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- bread dough
- salt

## Directions

1. If the ham is quite salty as is customary here, soak it overnight in a large dish so it is completely submerged in water. If the ham is only slightly salted as it is usually salted in the old country, then you can do without soaking.
2. Wash the ham nicely, trim the skin and lard and wrap about a finger's thickness in bread dough, but so that no hole remains.
3. Pierce the dough on top several times so it does not crack, then place the ham thus prepared on a shallow roasting pan or roaster and let it bake slowly in the oven for four or five hours; then remove the bread crust from the ham and place it on a platter.
