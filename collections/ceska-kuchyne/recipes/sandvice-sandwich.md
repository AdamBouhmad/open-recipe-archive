## Ingredients

- white bread, preferably two days old
- butter
- meat, cheese, or other filling

## Directions

1. Cut white bread into equal slices about one-quarter inch thick
2. Remove crusts so that nice pieces remain, about four inches square
3. Slice meat thinly and equally, ensuring it is prime, tender, and juicy, well-roasted or boiled
4. Ensure sandwich edges are straight and filling does not protrude
5. Arrange finished sandwiches on a plate covered with a napkin
6. Garnish with fresh parsley or watercress
7. Serve with a small dish of mustard so each person may help themselves as desired
8. If sandwiches dry out before use, moisten a napkin in cold water, wring it out, and cover the sandwiches
9. Alternatively, each sandwich may be wrapped in thin waxed paper
