## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of fresh butter
- two eggs
- one small pot of milk
- one tablespoon of sugar
- salt
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- two small pots of flour
- beautiful ripe prunes

## Directions

1. Cream one tablespoon of fresh butter, beat two eggs into it, and beat well
2. Add one small pot of milk, one tablespoon of sugar, and salt
3. Mix one teaspoon of baking powder into two small pots of flour and add to the rest
4. Add flour as needed to work soft dough, about as stiff as for biscuits
5. With a spoon, scoop out pieces of dough, shape in your hands, place on a board, flatten slightly, and wrap a beautiful large and ripe prune in each piece
6. Boil in boiling salted water and cook for about 15 minutes
7. Do not overcook or they will fall apart
8. Dumplings with apples can be made the same way instead of prunes
9. Large apples are cut into quarters and small apples into halves; the core must of course be removed
