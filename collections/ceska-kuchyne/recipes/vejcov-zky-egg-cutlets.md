## Ingredients

- ¼ kilo fine flour
- 16 eggs
- sweet sour cream as needed
- hot butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix ¼ kilo fine flour with sixteen eggs and as much sweet sour cream as needed to make a thick mixture
2. Pour into a cake mold and bake
3. When cooled, cut into long narrow pieces
4. Fry them in hot butter and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
