## Ingredients

- venison thigh
- cooled cooked marinade
- salt
- clarified butter
- pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Slice nice pieces from venison thigh
2. Let them lie in cooled cooked marinade for about two hours
3. Salt them and quickly roast on hot clarified butter
4. Serve with pepper sauce or another piquant sauce in a separate dish
