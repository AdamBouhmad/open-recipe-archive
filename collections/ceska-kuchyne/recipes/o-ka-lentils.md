## Ingredients

- lentils
- soft water
- salt
- butter or fried onions
- eggs, cooked in butter

## Directions

1. Pick through lentils and cook in soft water
2. When half-cooked, salt them
3. Add water if needed and finish cooking
4. Drain the water and turn lentils onto a plate
5. Pour melted butter or fried onion over them
6. Add eggs that have been cooked in butter
