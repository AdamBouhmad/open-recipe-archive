## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cream or milk
- a piece of cinnamon
- sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon flour
- cold cream
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the cream or milk with a piece of cinnamon
2. Add a little sugar and let it stand
3. In another vessel, mix half a teaspoon of flour with cold cream into which 4 egg yolks have been beaten
4. When the cinnamon mixture has cooled completely, pour into this sauce
5. Mix everything again
6. Slowly thicken over gentle heat
7. Strain; it is excellent for flour dishes
