## Ingredients

- one and a half pots of peas
- one quart of prepared stock (recipe 38)
- two cloves of garlic, grated
- marjoram leaves
- flower
- ginger
- butter roux
- fried bread roll cut thin or pearl barley cooked soft in salted water

## Directions

1. Cook one and a half pots of peas until soft
2. Mix well
3. Pour one quart of prepared stock (recipe 38) over it
4. Add two grated cloves of garlic
5. Let it cook a little longer
6. Press it through a sieve
7. Add a bit of marjoram leaves
8. Season with a bit of flower and ginger
9. Thicken with a light butter roux
10. Let it cook a little longer
11. Pour over fried bread roll cut into pieces or you can add beautiful pearl barley cooked soft in salted water to it
