## Ingredients

- Thick noodles
- Milk
- Salt
- Crushed nutmeg
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make thick noodles according to the previous method
2. Boil them in boiling milk
3. Add salt and season with a little crushed nutmeg
4. Add a piece of sugar and let cook for a while
5. Pour the noodles with milk onto a dish and serve at the table
