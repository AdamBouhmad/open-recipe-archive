## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- salt
- warm salted water
- good butter
- white flour
- strong beef or chicken broth

## Directions

1. Place sweetbreads in lukewarm salted water and let soak for an hour
2. Put them on the fire with the water and boil for about two minutes
3. Transfer them to cold water and let cool
4. Carefully peel them and thread them with salt pork
5. Make a thin sauce on the stove from good butter and white flour
6. Dissolve it with strong beef or chicken broth
