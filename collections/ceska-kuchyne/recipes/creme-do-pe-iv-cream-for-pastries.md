## Ingredients

- three deciliters cream
- five egg yolks
- small spoon of flour
- ten deka sugar with good flavor
- 1.5 deka butter
- about five deka of chopped and roasted almonds

## Directions

1. Mix three deciliters of cream, five egg yolks, small spoon of flour, ten deka sugar with good flavor and 1.5 deka butter perfectly
2. At the end add about five deka of chopped and roasted almonds
