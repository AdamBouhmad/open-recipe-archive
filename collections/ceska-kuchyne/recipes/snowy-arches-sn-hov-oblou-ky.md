## Ingredients

- approximately 1/2 kilo ground sugar
- 7 whole eggs
- grated lemon zest
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Mix ground sugar with whole eggs
2. Add grated lemon zest and flour to form a thick dough
3. Spread onto a buttered baking sheet
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped almonds
5. Bake slowly
6. Cut into strips and curl around rounded wooden forms while hot
