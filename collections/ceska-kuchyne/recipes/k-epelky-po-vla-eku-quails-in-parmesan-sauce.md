## Ingredients

- 125 grams rice flour
- 1/2 liter strong broth
- 70 grams fresh butter
- pepper
- salt
- 2-3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- roasted quails

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil and add rice flour.
2. Add fresh butter, a little pepper, and salt.
3. Gradually bring to a thick paste, stirring constantly.
4. Add 2-3 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese while cooking.
5. Arrange the roasted quails on a platter.
6. Pour the remaining sauce over the quails.
