## Ingredients

- water
- butter and sugar in equal parts
- grated lemon zest
- ground cinnamon
- flour, as needed
- 6 eggs
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a small amount of water, equal parts butter and sugar, grated lemon zest, and ground cinnamon on a hot baking pan
2. Gradually add flour until stiff dough forms
3. Remove the pan and allow dough to cool
4. When cooled, add 6 eggs and 6 egg yolks
5. Heat butter until very hot
6. Dip a spoon in the hot butter, scoop dough the size of a walnut, drop into butter
7. Fry quickly with constant stirring
8. When cooked, remove and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
