## Ingredients

- cleaned capon
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- 2 halved onions
- 4 peppercorns
- new spice
- juice of 2 lemons or good wine vinegar
- 2 calf feet

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned capon on a baking dish with carrot, parsley, celery, 2 halved onions, 4 peppercorns, new spice and juice of 2 lemons or good wine vinegar
2. Simmer until the capon is tender
3. Allow to cool, and instead cook 2 calf feet in the broth
4. Cut the carrot, celery and parsley in which the capon was cooked into stars
5. Place in a mold, decorate with green parsley, arrange the capon on top
6. Strain the broth over it and place on ice
