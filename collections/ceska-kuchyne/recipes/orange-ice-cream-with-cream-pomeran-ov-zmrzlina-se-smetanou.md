## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- More than a liter of sour cream
- Quarter kilo of sugar
- Zest of two oranges

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs
2. Beat them well
3. Pour in more than a liter of sour cream
4. Add a quarter kilo of sugar
5. Grate zest from two oranges
6. Bring everything to a boil
7. Prepare as other ice creams
