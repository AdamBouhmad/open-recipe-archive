## Ingredients

- onion
- sugar
- butter
- wine
- spices
- bay leaf
- black gingerbread or dark beets
- three tomatoes
- roux
- wine

## Directions

1. Chop the whole onion and caramelize it with sugar and butter
2. Mix in one-third liter of wine
3. Add all spices, bay leaf and black gingerbread or dark beets
4. Add three tomatoes and roux
5. Add a little wine
6. Simmer
7. Strain and serve to the table
