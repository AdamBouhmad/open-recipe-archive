## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of finely chopped bacon
- two onions, sliced into rounds
- beef chuck, cleaned and rinsed
- one cup hot water
- salt
- pepper
- one garlic clove
- one tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Put a quarter pound of finely chopped bacon and two onions sliced into rounds in a pot.
2. When it begins to turn yellow, add a piece of cleaned and rinsed beef chuck and let it brown on both sides on high heat.
3. Add one cup of hot water, salt and pepper it, mash one garlic clove with salt and add to the pot.
4. Cover well and let simmer on moderate heat.
5. Watch that it does not burn and occasionally baste with hot water as needed, but not much at once, always about two spoonfuls.
6. Meat from a young animal or aged meat will be stewed in two hours.
7. When it is tender, remove the meat and to the remaining sauce mix in one spoon of flour dissolved in a little water, let it boil and pour over the meat.
