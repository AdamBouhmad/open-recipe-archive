## Ingredients

- 6 tablets of chocolate
- 3 tablets worth of sugar
- 3 tablets worth of raw grated almonds
- flour equal in weight to chocolate
- 3 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- wafer rounds
- 1 blanched almond per piece

## Directions

1. Grate 6 tablets of chocolate
2. Mix it with sugar equal to 3 tablets in weight, the same amount of raw grated almonds, and flour equal in weight to the chocolate
3. Mix everything well, then beat in 3 eggs and 1 egg yolk
4. Work this into a batter like for dripping, so it is not too stiff
5. Place round pieces cut from wafers on waxed baking sheets
6. Pour batter onto each piece, place a blanched, peeled almond in the middle
7. Dry the brittle in the oven
