## Ingredients

- two spoons of beef marrow
- two whole eggs
- salt
- flowers
- a piece of grated lemon peel
- grated white bread or crackers

## Directions

1. Take two spoons of beef marrow and beat it so long until it whitens, add two eggs one after the other, add a little salt and flowers, a piece of grated lemon peel, mix everything well; then add as much grated white bread or crackers as necessary to make the dough properly firm for dumplings.
2. As a test, cook one dumpling and if it is not sufficiently firm, add a little more bread. Then form dumplings from it.
3. About a quarter hour before serving the soup, cook the dumplings in ordinary beef broth, cover the pot and let it cook slowly for about ten minutes.
