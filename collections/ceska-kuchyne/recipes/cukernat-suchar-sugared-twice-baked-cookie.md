## Ingredients

- 200 grams very finely beaten sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 125 grams melted butter
- 500 milliliters lukewarm milk
- 750 grams fine flour
- 50 grams dissolved yeast
- lemon zest, finely grated
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat 200 grams very finely beaten sugar with 4 egg yolks until foamy
2. Slowly add 125 grams melted butter and 500 milliliters lukewarm milk
3. Add 750 grams fine flour and 50 grams dissolved yeast
4. Season with salt and finely grated lemon zest
5. Work the mixture thoroughly into dough until bubbles burst
6. Cover and let rise in a warm place
7. After rising, knead thoroughly and form into small loaves
8. Place on a sheet and let rise again
9. Brush with butter or beaten egg yolk
10. Bake gently until light brown
11. When cool, cut into thin slices
