## Ingredients

- pike
- beef broth
- wine
- salt
- vegetables
- pale roux
- fresh butter
- saffron
- garlic
- marjoram
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean the pike thoroughly to remove all blood
2. Set aside one portion, place the rest on a baking dish
3. Pour beef broth mixed with wine over it, add salt and all vegetables
4. Cook until the pike becomes tender
5. Arrange on the final serving dish
6. To the cooking liquid, add pale roux, a piece of fresh butter, saffron, and let cook
7. Meanwhile, remove all bones from the remaining pike meat, chop finely, and add a bit of grated garlic, marjoram, grated bread crumbs, a piece of butter, pepper and one egg
8. Mix everything well
9. Form dumplings in hands moistened with cold water
10. Place the dumplings on a baking dish in the strained sauce and add to the pike on the serving dish
