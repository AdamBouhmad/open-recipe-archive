## Ingredients

- a small cup of rice
- one quart of milk
- hard wheat bread
- butter
- five eggs
- raisins
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a small cup of rice in one quart of milk
2. When cooked, let it cool
3. Toast finely grated hard wheat bread in butter until golden
4. Beat five eggs
5. Butter a mold for a bábovka cake with cold butter
6. Sprinkle the bread around the inside so the rice does not stick
7. Put a two-finger-thick layer of rice around the bottom
8. Add a layer of beaten eggs
9. Then add a layer of raisins, cinnamon, and sugar
10. Grease with butter and start layering rice again
11. Repeat this three times
12. Bake slowly in the oven
