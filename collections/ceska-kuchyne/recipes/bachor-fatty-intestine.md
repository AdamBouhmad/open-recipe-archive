## Ingredients

- veal meat
- rendered lard
- raw pork blood
- pepper
- ground ginger
- new spice
- flower
- onion
- pork stomach
- veal broth

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into cubes
2. Add rendered lard and raw pork blood to the meat
3. Season with pepper, ground ginger, new spice, and flower
4. Add whole onion browned in pork lard
5. Mix everything together in a bowl
6. Clean the pork stomach well
7. Stuff the stomach with the mixture
8. Cook in veal broth for half an hour
9. Pierce with a thin stick - if no blood runs out, it is done
10. While still warm, put it in a press and it will compress more as it cools
11. Serve plain or with oil and vinegar
