## Ingredients

- eight tablespoons of butter
- eight eggs
- three tablespoons of fine sugar
- a small cupful of raw cream
- a little pounded flower
- a little salt
- four small cupfuls of dry sifted flour
- one and a half tablespoons of peeled finely cut almonds
- one and a half ounces of citron also finely cut
- a piece of pressed yeast
- five egg whites
- pounded sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat eight tablespoons of butter until foamy, add to it eight eggs, one by one, three tablespoons of fine sugar, a small cupful of raw cream, a little pounded flower, a little salt; mix well.
2. Then add to it four small cupfuls of dry sifted flour, one and a half tablespoons of peeled finely cut almonds, one and a half ounces of citron also finely cut, one piece of pressed yeast, beat for half an hour, then add to it the stiff snow from five egg whites.
3. Grease a mold with butter, fill it halfway with dough and let it rise in a warm place; when the mold is almost full, put it in the oven and let the cake bake until golden, then turn it out carefully and sprinkle with pounded sugar.
