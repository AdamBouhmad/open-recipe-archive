## Ingredients

- three and one-half deciliters cream
- vanilla
- seven decagrams sugar
- six to eight egg yolks or one tablespoon flour and five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil three and one-half deciliters of cream with vanilla
2. Let cool and strain
3. Add seven decagrams of sugar and six to eight egg yolks or one tablespoon flour and five egg yolks
4. Beat on warm stove
