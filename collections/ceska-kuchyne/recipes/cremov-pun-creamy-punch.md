## Ingredients

- 4 liters water
- 2 kilograms sugar
- juice of 8 lemons
- 4 liters arak
- vanilla
- 3 pints milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 kilograms sugar in 4 liters of water
2. Add the juice of 8 lemons
3. Add 4 liters of arak and a little vanilla
4. Mix everything together and heat on warm stove
5. When the liquid begins to thicken, slowly pour in 3 pints of milk while constantly stirring
6. Remove the pot from the stove, cover with a cloth and let it stand peacefully for 2 hours
7. Strain the mixture, pour into bottles and cork them
8. Serve cold only
