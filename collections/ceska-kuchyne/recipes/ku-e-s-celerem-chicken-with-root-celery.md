## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- beef broth or butter
- 2 heads of root celery
- piece of fresh butter
- several egg yolks
- flower (seasoning)
- white roux
- green celery greens

## Directions

1. Cook two chickens either in beef broth or braise them in butter
2. Let two heads of celery cook and when softened, slice one head into rounds and add to the chickens
3. The second head of celery, cooked until soft, rub through with a piece of fresh butter and several egg yolks
4. Season with flower, brown with white roux, strain through a sieve onto the chickens and let heat through
5. Place the chickens on a platter, pour the sauce over, surround with celery rounds and garnish with green celery greens
