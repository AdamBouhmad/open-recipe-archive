## Ingredients

- half kilogram fine flour
- half kilogram butter
- one eighth finely beaten sugar
- grated lemon zest
- several eggs

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough from all ingredients
2. Knead the dough thoroughly
3. Roll out and cut as desired
4. Layer the butter dough in thin sheets
5. Fill with various preserves as desired
6. Brush with egg yolk
7. Bake quickly in a well-heated oven
8. Always prepare the dough in a cold room; if to be added to game, omit the sugar
