## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre cold milk
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) semolina
- 1-2 litres boiling milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 9 decagrams (6 lots) of semolina in 1/2 litre of cold milk
2. Pour in 1-2 litres of boiling milk
3. Salt it, sweeten with sugar, add cinnamon and stir for 10 minutes
