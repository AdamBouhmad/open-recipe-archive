## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal
- a piece of fatted lard
- lemon rind
- 1 roll or slice of white bread soaked in milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- salt
- ground pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated roll

## Directions

1. Take 1 pound of veal, a piece of fatted lard, lemon rind, 1 roll or slice of white bread soaked in milk and squeezed out, and chop everything together
2. Beat 2 tablespoons of butter, mix into it 4 whole eggs
3. Add the chopped mixture to this, pour in 2 tablespoons sour cream, season with salt and ground pepper, add 2 tablespoons grated roll and mix well
4. Grease a pan with butter, fill with the mixture, place in the oven and let it bake
5. Cut the baked puff into pieces and serve
