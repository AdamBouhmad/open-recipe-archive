## Ingredients

- 500 grams (1 pound) cooked grated potatoes
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- thick sour cream
- sugar
- butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Mix cooked grated potatoes with eggs, egg yolk, flour, and salt
2. Form small mounds and roll into pieces the thickness of a finger
3. Brush with thick sour cream and sprinkle with sugar
4. Cut into wide strips and fold
5. Press edges so cream doesn't leak out
6. Place in hot butter until golden on both sides
7. Brush with sour cream mixed with beaten egg and sugar
8. Bake until a crust forms
9. Serve warm on a platter
