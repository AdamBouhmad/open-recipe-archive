## Ingredients

- 2 cups sweet sour cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Place sour cream in a bowl
2. Mix in egg yolks
3. Add butter and salt
4. Mix baking powder into flour
5. Add flour to make dough thicker than for pancakes
6. Bake according to the method for regular muffins
