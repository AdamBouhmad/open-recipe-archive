## Ingredients

- medlars
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the medlars and rub off the hairs
2. Cut into thin pieces and cook slowly, but do not let them break apart
3. Drain the water, cover the vessel so the medlars cook in steam
4. When hot, press the juice from them
5. Add crushed sugar equal to the amount of juice
6. Cook together with constant stirring until completely thickened
7. Constantly skim off the foam and when thickened, pour into containers
