## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Sweet beer
- Salt
- Caraway
- Parsley
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Place cleaned crayfish in a vessel filled with sweet beer.
2. The beer must be salted with the addition of caraway, parsley, and pepper.
3. The crayfish cook quickly in beer.
