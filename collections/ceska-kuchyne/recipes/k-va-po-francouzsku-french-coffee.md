## Ingredients

- ground coffee
- boiling water

## Directions

1. This coffee is prepared in a special pot equipped with a cloth filter made of firm thin linen, which should be washed and dried after each use
2. Place ground coffee in the filter, pour the required amount of boiling water over it, let it pass through and pour into cups
3. The filtered coffee can also be left for a while on the edge of the stove, but must not be boiled again
4. To achieve excellent black coffee, pour the filtered liquid once more over the ground coffee in the filter and let it pass through again
5. For this method of preparation, coffee should be ground very finely
