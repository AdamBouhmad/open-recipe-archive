## Ingredients

- 36 dekagrams (24 lots) sugar
- 13 egg yolks
- 13 egg whites
- 36 dekagrams flour
- zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Add 13 egg yolks one by one to 36 dekagrams sugar
2. Beat everything
3. Mix in the snow from 13 egg whites
4. Mix in 36 dekagrams flour and lemon zest
5. When everything is beaten, bake in a buttered mold dusted with flour
