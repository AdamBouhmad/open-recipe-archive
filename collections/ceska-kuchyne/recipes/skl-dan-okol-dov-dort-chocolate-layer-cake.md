## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- two-thirds cup water
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 3 cups flour
- vanilla
- 0.5 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup syrup
- 0.5 cup milk or cream
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 5 tablespoons grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Mix 1.5 cups sugar well with 0.5 cup butter
2. Beat egg whites and yolks separately
3. Add yolks to sugar and butter
4. Add two-thirds cup water and 2 teaspoons baking powder mixed into 3 cups sifted flour
5. Finally add egg whites and a little vanilla
6. Bake in thin layers on metal plates
7. For the chocolate coating: boil 0.5 cup sugar, 0.5 cup syrup, 0.5 cup milk or cream, and 1 teaspoon butter for five minutes
8. Add 5 tablespoons grated chocolate
9. Cook until thick and when cooled add vanilla
10. Spread the chocolate coating between layers and on top
