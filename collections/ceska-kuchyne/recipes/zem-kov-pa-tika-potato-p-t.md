## Ingredients

- ham
- bacon
- sour cream
- pepper
- chopped onion
- cooked potatoes
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut equal amounts of ham and bacon into small cubes
2. Mix with sour cream, pepper and chopped onion
3. Into a buttered mold layer alternately sliced cooked potatoes then the mixture
4. Make the last layer of ham and bacon
5. Bake for one hour
