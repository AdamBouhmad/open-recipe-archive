## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 1/2 kilogram pounded and sifted fine sugar
- juice and grated peel of 1 lemon
- 1/2 kilogram pounded almonds
- 1 tablespoon finely pounded cinnamon
- butter or pure wax
- sugar water or egg yolks for glazing

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Fold in 1/2 kilogram pounded and sifted fine sugar
3. Add lemon juice and grated peel from 1 lemon
4. Add 1/2 kilogram pounded almonds and 1 tablespoon finely pounded cinnamon
5. Mix thoroughly and work well
6. Roll out on a board and cut into long thin pieces
7. Curve into arches as desired
8. Brush with melted butter or pure wax
9. Bake on a sheet slowly to golden over gentle heat
10. While baking, brush with sugar water or egg yolks for a nice sheen
