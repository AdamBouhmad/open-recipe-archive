## Ingredients

- 250 grams butter
- 4 to 5 tablespoons flour
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- vanilla
- salt
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Melt 250 grams of butter and mix with 4 to 5 tablespoons of flour, brown it, add milk and stir until a thick porridge forms
2. Sprinkle the porridge with sugar, cinnamon and vanilla, salt it and cook until completely thickened
3. Add 6 egg yolks to it, mix everything and cook again for about a quarter hour
4. Remove from heat and place on a plate
