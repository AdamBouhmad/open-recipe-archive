## Ingredients

- dough (recipe 33, very stiff)
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Make dough according to recipe 33, but make it very stiff
2. Grate the dough on a grater
3. Let the grated dough dry a bit
4. If desired, sift it through a fine sieve
5. Pour into beef broth
