## Ingredients

- Ten to twelve deca of roasted coffee
- Boiling sour cream
- Six egg whites
- Sour cream
- Quarter kilo of sugar

## Directions

1. Scald the roasted coffee with boiling sour cream
2. Boil for several hours
3. Strain through a cloth
4. Beat the six egg whites perfectly
5. Pour in the sour cream gradually
6. Add a quarter kilo of sugar
7. Continue beating
8. Bring to a boil
9. Let it cool
10. Repeat this process several times
11. Then prepare ice cream from it
