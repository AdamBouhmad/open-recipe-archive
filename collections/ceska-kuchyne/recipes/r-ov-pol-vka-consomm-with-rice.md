## Ingredients

- six ounces rice
- lukewarm water
- two quarts clear beef soup
- salt
- flower (seasoning)
- piece of fresh butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Pick over the rice and rinse it several times in lukewarm water.
2. Put in a pot, pour two quarts of clear beef soup over it, salt, add a little flower and a piece of fresh butter.
3. Let simmer slowly for half an hour until rice softens, taking care that the rice does not burn and does not become mushy when stirring.
4. If desired, serve with a plate of grated Parmesan cheese on the side.
