## Ingredients

- millet porridge
- butter
- jam

## Directions

1. Cook the millet as before
2. Spread two fingers' worth of porridge on a greased baking sheet, spread with jam, and continue this way until the sheet is almost full
3. Bake the pudding for 3/4 hour
