## Ingredients

- 3 medium potatoes
- 1 slice white bread
- 3 eggs
- beef broth, 1 quart

## Directions

1. Boil three potatoes of medium size, peel, and let cool
2. Grate the potatoes
3. Cut a slice of white bread and fry until golden
4. Boil three eggs hard, peel, and remove yolks
5. Add the yolks to the fried bread and mash everything together finely
6. Put into a pot, pour 1 quart of beef broth over it and let simmer half an hour
7. Thin with beef broth, strain, and pour into a soup bowl
