## Ingredients

- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- several tablespoons flour
- 1 litre boiling water
- salt
- bread or roll, diced and fried
- nutmeg or fried onions in butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Heat 6 decagrams (4 lots) of butter until brown
2. Roast several tablespoons of flour in it
3. Pour in 1 litre of boiling water while constantly stirring
4. Salt it, boil, pour over fried bread or roll cut into cubes
5. May be seasoned with nutmeg or topped with fried onions in butter
