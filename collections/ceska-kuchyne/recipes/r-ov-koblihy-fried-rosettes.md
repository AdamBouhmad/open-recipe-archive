## Ingredients

- dough as for Boží Milosti
- egg white
- clarified butter or lard
- sugar
- jam or cream

## Directions

1. Make and roll out dough as for Boží Milosti
2. Cut round, scalloped-edged flowers with a small cutter to make petal-like pieces
3. Brush the center of each petal with egg white
4. Stack three petals on top of each other and press down in the center with a finger
5. Fry in heated clarified butter or lard, using enough fat so the rosettes float
6. Place jam or cream in the center, sprinkle generously with sugar, and arrange on a platter
