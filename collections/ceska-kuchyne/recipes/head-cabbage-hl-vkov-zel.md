## Ingredients

- Cabbage of any color
- Wine vinegar
- Pepper
- Bacon
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage, remove the cores and finely chop the leaves
2. Place on a baking sheet with wine vinegar, pepper, bacon and a piece of white sugar
3. Cook everything together
4. Add the cabbage, mix, press down on a plate and pepper the surface to taste
