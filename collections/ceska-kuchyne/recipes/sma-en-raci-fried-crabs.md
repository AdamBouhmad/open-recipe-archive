## Ingredients

- crabs
- flour
- salt
- green parsley, finely chopped
- butter, clarified
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs in the usual way
2. Remove the meat from the shells
3. Dust with flour mixed with salt and finely chopped green parsley
4. Fry in clarified butter over high heat until golden
5. Arrange on a dish
6. Garnish with fried parsley
