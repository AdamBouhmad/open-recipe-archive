## Ingredients

- Fully ripe strawberries
- Lemon juice
- Sugar
- Wine or water

## Directions

1. Press fully ripe strawberries through fine sieve
2. Add lemon juice to the juice
3. Sweeten generously
4. Dilute with wine or water
5. Freeze with regular treatment
