## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- roll (žemličky) or bread soaked and squeezed in milk
- fresh butter
- eggs
- lemon rind
- nutmeg flower
- white ginger

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape the pike, remove the entrails and cut it into pieces
2. From several pieces remove the bones and chop the meat finely
3. Cook the remaining pieces in salted water, cover and set aside
4. Pound the chopped meat in a mortar with two hard-boiled egg yolks and half a roll or slice of bread soaked in milk and squeezed
5. Rub a piece of fresh butter, add the mixture to it, stir in two eggs, add finely chopped lemon rind, salt, season with a little nutmeg flower and mix well
6. If too thin, add more grated roll, shape into small dumplings and cook them in the broth in which the pike was cooked
7. Brown with white butter roux, place the cooked pike into it, season with nutmeg flower and white ginger, let it boil for a minute
8. Arrange the pike in the middle of a dish, surround with dumplings, pour sauce over and serve
