## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 500 grams sugar, crushed
- 16 grams crushed cinnamon
- salt
- 500 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs with crushed sugar for at least half an hour
2. Add crushed cinnamon and a little salt
3. Add fine flour and work into dough
4. Form small cookies on a buttered baking sheet
5. Bake slowly until golden
