## Ingredients

- violet petals
- lemon juice
- apple pulp
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine, clean, colored violet petals
2. Moisten with a few drops of lemon juice
3. Press the juice through a fine sieve
4. Add fine, sweetened apple pulp
5. Add powdered sugar and mix thoroughly
6. Cook once strongly with constant stirring
