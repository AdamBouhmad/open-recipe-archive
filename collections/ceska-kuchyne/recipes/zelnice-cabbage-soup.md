## Ingredients

- cabbage water
- sour cabbage
- water
- egg yolks
- eggs
- butter
- cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Put into a pot a quart of cabbage water, a pint of sour cabbage and a quart of water and let cook
2. Whisk in a small pot six egg yolks or 3 eggs with a piece of butter and a glass of good cream
3. Properly whisk this into the soup, pour into a bowl and serve
4. Those who like thick soup can thicken the cream and egg yolks with a spoonful of nice white flour
