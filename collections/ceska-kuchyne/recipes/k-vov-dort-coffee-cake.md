## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram almonds with skins, crushed
- 14 eggs
- 14 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) not too roasted ground coffee

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 kilogram crushed almonds with skins, 14 eggs, 14 egg yolks, and 12 dekagrams ground coffee
2. Butter the mold and bake the cake
