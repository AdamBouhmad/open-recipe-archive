## Ingredients

- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 liter boiling water
- salt
- flower (or herb)
- lemon peel chopped
- 2 egg yolks
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Melt 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter and mix with 3 tablespoons flour until the roux begins to rise
2. Slowly pour in 1 liter of boiling water and simmer
3. Mix well
4. Season with salt
5. Add flower, chopped lemon peel, 2 egg yolks, and chopped parsley
