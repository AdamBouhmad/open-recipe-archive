## Ingredients

- 1 litre salted water
- 4 rolls
- a piece of butter
- a little flower
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil 4 rolls in 1 litre of salted water, mash them with a whisk
2. Add a piece of butter and a little flower to them
3. After boiling for a while, add 4 beaten eggs
4. If the soup is too thick, add a little water
