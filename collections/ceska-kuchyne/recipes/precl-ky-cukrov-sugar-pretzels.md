## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- 125 grams fresh butter, torn into pieces
- 100 grams crushed sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 egg yolks
- sour cream as needed
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, egg, egg yolks, and salt
2. Add sour cream as needed and work into light dough
3. Roll out completely
4. Form into small braided strips
5. Make small pretzels from these strips
6. Place on flour-dusted baking sheet
7. Brush with beaten egg whites
8. Sprinkle with coarsely crushed sugar
9. Bake slowly until golden brown
