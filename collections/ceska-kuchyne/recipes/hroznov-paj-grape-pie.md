## Ingredients

- pie crust dough
- egg
- sugar
- grape seeds

## Directions

1. Line a pan with dough
2. Beat one egg
3. Add half a coffee cup of sugar to it
4. Add a large coffee cup of grape seeds
5. Spread this mixture over the pan
6. Dust with a tablespoon of flour
7. Dot with butter and add a top crust
