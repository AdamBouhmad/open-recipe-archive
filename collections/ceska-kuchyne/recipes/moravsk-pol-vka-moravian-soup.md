## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 turnip (kelerub)
- 2 parsley roots
- 1 leek
- 1 celery root
- butter
- beef broth
- 1 sliced roll

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots, turnip, parsley roots, leek, and celery root into cubes
2. Braise the vegetables in butter for a quarter hour
3. Add the braised vegetables to beef broth and simmer for half an hour
4. Pour the soup over sliced roll in a bowl
