## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of fresh mushrooms
- fresh butter
- chopped green parsley
- 20-30 young asparagus heads
- half pint of young green peas
- piece of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- two quarts of beef broth
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean a sufficient quantity of fresh mushrooms, chop them finely and let stew them on good fresh butter with chopped green parsley
2. In the same way let stew 20-30 young asparagus heads and half a pint of young green peas
3. When everything is sufficiently soft, put it in a soup bowl
4. Make a pale roux from a piece of butter and two tablespoons of flour, thicken two quarts of beef broth with it, let boil, bind with four egg yolks and pour into the bowl over the mushrooms, asparagus and peas
