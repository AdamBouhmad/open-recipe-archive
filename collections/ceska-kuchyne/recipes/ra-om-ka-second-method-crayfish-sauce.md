## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 125 grams crayfish butter
- half liter good meat broth
- lemon juice
- anchovy butter
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry flour in crayfish butter
2. Cook with half liter good meat broth, stirring constantly
3. Make more pleasant with lemon juice
4. Before serving, add anchovy butter and two egg yolks
