## Ingredients

- truffles
- onion
- parsley
- mushrooms
- bacon
- salt
- pastry spice
- broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Wash the truffles, peel them, and chop them
2. Chop with onion, parsley, and mushrooms
3. Mix in bacon and salt
4. Add pastry spice
5. Bind with good broth and wine
6. Braise until all liquid evaporates
7. Cool before using for pheasants, capons, and similar birds
