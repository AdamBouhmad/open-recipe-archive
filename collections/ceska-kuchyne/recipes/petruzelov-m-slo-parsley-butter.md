## Ingredients

- Half pound fresh butter
- Two tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- Juice from one lemon
- Pinch of pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. To half pound of fresh butter take two tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, juice from one lemon, a pinch of pepper and a little salt, mix everything well and store the butter in a cool place.
2. This butter is added to various roasts instead of sauce; small pieces of butter are spread on the surface of the roast and allowed to melt from the heat.
