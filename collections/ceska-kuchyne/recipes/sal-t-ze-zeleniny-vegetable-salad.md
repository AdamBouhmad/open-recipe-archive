## Ingredients

- carrots
- asparagus
- small celery
- green beans
- rutabaga
- cauliflower
- salted water
- hard-boiled eggs, cut into quarters
- sauce made from grated egg yolks, oil, mustard, vinegar, pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Clean carrots, asparagus, small celery, green beans, and rutabaga
2. Cut asparagus and beans into pieces
3. Divide cauliflower
4. Cut rutabaga and carrots into slices
5. Cook everything in salted water
6. Drain the water
7. Arrange cauliflower in the middle of a dish and the remaining vegetables around it
8. Pour sauce made from grated egg yolks, oil, mustard, vinegar, pepper and salt
9. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
