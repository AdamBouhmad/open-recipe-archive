## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 small cups fine sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 egg whites
- vanilla or lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Cook 1 1/2 small cups of fine sugar with 2 tablespoons of water until it threads
2. Meanwhile beat the whites of 2 eggs and fold the hot sugar into them, stirring until it cools
3. Then add vanilla or lemon juice to taste
