## Ingredients

- 1 bottle white wine
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Lemon zest
- 8-10 eggs
- 30 dekagrams (20 lots) sugar
- Warm arrack

## Directions

1. Mix 1 bottle of white wine, lemon juice, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, 8-10 eggs, and 30 dekagrams of sugar
2. Place the bowl in hot water
3. Beat the mixture until it froths
4. Finally add warm arrack
