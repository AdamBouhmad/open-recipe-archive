## Ingredients

- pork meat from the kidney or neck with bone
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pound the meat with a wooden mallet, salt it, coat in flour, then in egg, and then in breadcrumbs.
2. Fry until golden brown.
