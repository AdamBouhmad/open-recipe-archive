## Ingredients

- peeled and finely chopped apples
- hard bread, grated
- cinnamon
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds, chopped
- six eggs
- two cups of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Steam peeled and finely chopped apples
2. On a buttered baking pan, place a layer of grated hard bread and a layer of apples
3. Add a little cinnamon, sugar, raisins, and chopped almonds
4. Continue alternating bread and apples until the pan is full
5. Beat six eggs (set aside three whites) with two cups of sweet cream
6. Pour this mixture over the layers
7. Bake in a moderate oven for one hour
8. While baking, beat the three reserved whites into snow
9. When baked, pour this snow over it and put back in the oven for another minute
