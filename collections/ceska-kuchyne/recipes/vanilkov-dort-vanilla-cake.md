## Ingredients

- half kilogram butter
- 16 to 18 egg yolks
- half kilogram ground sugar
- ground vanilla
- half kilogram fine flour
- salt
- firm snow from egg whites

## Directions

1. Take half kilogram butter and beat it thoroughly
2. Add 16 to 18 egg yolks, half kilogram ground sugar, and ground vanilla
3. Add half kilogram fine flour and salt
4. Mix with firm snow from egg whites
5. Bake in cake forms
6. Fill with vanilla cream and coat with vanilla glaze
