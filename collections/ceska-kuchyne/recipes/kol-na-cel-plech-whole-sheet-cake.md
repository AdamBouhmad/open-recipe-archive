## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) butter
- 4 eggs
- 3 decagrams yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter warm milk
- 75 grams (5 lots) sugar
- Flour (as much as needed to make airy dough)
- Sugar, cinnamon and chopped almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Beat 24 decagrams butter until foamy with 4 eggs, 3 decagrams yeast dissolved in 1/4 liter warm milk, 75 grams sugar and enough flour to make airy dough
2. Spread the dough to the thickness of a finger across the entire baking sheet
3. Let it rise for a while in a warm place
4. Brush with egg, sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon and chopped almonds and bake slowly
