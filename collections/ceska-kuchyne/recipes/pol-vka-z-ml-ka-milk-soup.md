## Ingredients

- milk
- salt
- noodles, flakes, breadcrumbs, or dropped dough
- bread cubes, fried
- rye bread, thinly sliced
- semolina
- torn semolina
- eggs

## Directions

1. Let the milk boil, salt it and cook into it either noodles, flakes, breadcrumbs or dropped dough
2. The boiled milk can also be poured into a dish with fried bread cubes or rye bread cut into thin slices
3. You can also cook semolina, torn semolina in the milk or poach as many eggs in it as there are people at the table
