## Ingredients

- one kilogram potatoes
- hot beef broth
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil, peel, and slice one kilogram of potatoes
2. Place the potatoes on a platter and pour hot beef broth over them
3. Drain the broth
4. Add oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, a little sugar, and finely chopped onion
5. Mix everything well and serve
