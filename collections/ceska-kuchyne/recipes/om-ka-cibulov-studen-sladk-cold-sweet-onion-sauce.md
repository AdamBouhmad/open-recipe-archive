## Ingredients

- white fresh onion
- sugar
- butter
- pepper
- beef broth
- white wine
- white flour
- sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Finely slice white fresh onion
2. Add sugar, butter and pepper
3. Braise until red
4. Pour in beef broth and half as much white wine
5. Sprinkle two spoonfuls of white flour
6. Strain
7. Add four spoonfuls of sweet sour cream
8. Mix well on a plate and serve with ice
9. Excellent with cold game
