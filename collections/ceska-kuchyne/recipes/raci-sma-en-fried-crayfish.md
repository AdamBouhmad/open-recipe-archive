## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salted water
- flour
- salt
- parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- cleaned crayfish shells

## Directions

1. Cook crayfish in salted water
2. Carefully remove the meat from the shells
3. Mix flour with salt and finely chopped parsley
4. Coat the crayfish meat in this mixture
5. Fry in butter
6. Place the meat back into the cleaned shells
7. Serve on a plate garnished tastefully with fried parsley
