## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter fresh rose hips
- 3/4 liter water
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- 1/4 liter white wine
- 9 dekagrams sugar
- potato flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Clean 1/2 liter of fresh rose hips
2. Cook in 3/4 liter of water with cinnamon and lemon peel
3. Strain
4. Add 1/4 liter of white wine
5. Add 9 dekagrams of sugar
6. Add a small spoonful of potato flour and a little cold water
