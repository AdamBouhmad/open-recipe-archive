## Ingredients

- Cleaned ham
- Warm water
- Fresh cold water
- Chopped onion
- Bay leaf
- Fine spices
- Crushed pepper
- Clove
- Nutmeg flower
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Beef meat from the haunch
- Tendons
- Fat from kidneys
- Cleaned sardelles
- Capers
- Grated rye bread
- Lemon zest, finely chopped
- Several egg yolks
- Chopped truffles
- Mushrooms
- Brown butter roux
- Good broth

## Directions

1. Clean the ham and place in warm water, cook slowly until soft
2. Remove bones and place ham in fresh cold water for several days
3. Remove ham from water and place in a pot
4. Sprinkle with chopped onion, bay leaf, fine spices, crushed pepper, clove and nutmeg flower
5. Pour wine and vinegar over until ham is fully covered
6. Cover and let rest for several days
7. Seal the pot completely so no steam can escape
8. Cook the ham
9. Take beef from the haunch, remove tendons and chop with onion, kidney fat, cleaned sardelles and capers
10. Add grated rye bread, finely chopped lemon zest, several egg yolks, chopped truffles and mushrooms, crushed pepper and nutmeg flower
11. Prepare pastry as previously described
12. Place chopped meat on the bottom, then the ham on top and arrange everything as with other pastries
13. Prepare sauce from brown butter roux, add good broth
14. When cooked, strain through a fine sieve
15. Add finely chopped sardelles, capers, lemons and capers to the strained sauce
16. Cook again, strain and pour over the pastry
