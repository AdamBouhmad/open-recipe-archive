## Ingredients

- 200 grams flour
- 100 grams butter, torn into pieces
- 70 grams ground peeled almonds
- grated zest and juice of half a lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Combine flour, butter pieces, ground almonds, lemon zest, lemon juice, egg yolks, and salt
2. Work into fine, crumbly dough
3. Work dough well and spread it out thinly
4. Cut cookies as desired
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar and chopped almonds
7. Make a small indentation in the center with a finger
8. Bake in oven until reddish, slowly
9. After baking, place fine preserves or other jam in the center of each cookie
