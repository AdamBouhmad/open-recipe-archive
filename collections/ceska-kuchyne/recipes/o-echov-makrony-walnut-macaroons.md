## Ingredients

- 500 grams walnuts
- 500 grams sugar
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Select walnuts, crack them, remove the kernels, scald and peel
2. Crush 500 grams walnuts and mix with egg whites and more than 500 grams beaten sugar
3. Mix everything into stiff dough, place on wafers on paper and bake slowly
