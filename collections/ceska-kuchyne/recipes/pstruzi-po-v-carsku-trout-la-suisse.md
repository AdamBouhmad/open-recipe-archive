## Ingredients

- 1 liter wine
- 1 liter broth
- salt
- rye bread
- onion
- parsley
- 2 slices lemon
- pepper
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix wine with broth and salt
2. Add fried rye bread, onion, parsley, two slices of lemon, and a little pepper
3. Boil everything together thoroughly and strain
4. Place trout in this sauce and braise
5. Strain the sauce, brown with flour, and serve with the trout
