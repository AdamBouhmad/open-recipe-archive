## Ingredients

- Elderflower blossoms with stems
- Egg
- Salt or sugar
- Bread crumbs mixed with flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the elderflower blossoms with stems and clean them thoroughly to remove any insects.
2. Beat an egg with either salt or sugar.
3. Dip the flower in the beaten egg, then roll in a mixture of bread crumbs and flour.
4. Fry in hot butter until pale pink in color.
5. Arrange on a platter with stems pointing upward, sprinkle with sugar, and serve hot.
