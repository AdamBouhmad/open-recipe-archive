## Ingredients

- small calf's head
- two tablespoons of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- two quarts of broth from the head
- fresh or preserved mushrooms, cut
- cloves
- allspice
- paprika
- a bunch of green parsley
- two hard-boiled eggs
- half a lemon, cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Cook a small calf's head
2. When cooked, remove the head and take off the meat, cut it and strain the broth from it and strain it
3. Make a roux from two tablespoons of butter and one tablespoon of flour and when it browns, pour two quarts of the broth from the head into it
4. Cook for five minutes and then add the meat cut earlier and cut fresh or preserved mushrooms
5. Add a pinch of cloves and allspice, a pinch of paprika and a bunch of green parsley
6. Cook for another five minutes
7. Then add two hard-boiled eggs cut up and half a lemon cut into pieces
