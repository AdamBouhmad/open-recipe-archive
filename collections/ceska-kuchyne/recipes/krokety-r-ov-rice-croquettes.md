## Ingredients

- rice
- 0.5 liter broth
- butter
- salt
- onion
- 2 cloves
- 3 egg yolks
- Parmesan cheese
- nutmeg
- minced ham or any meat
- egg for coating
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Braise the rice in 0.5 liter of broth
2. Grease it and season with salt
3. Mix with onion and 2 cloves
4. Remove the onion
5. Add 3 egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, and nutmeg
6. Let cool
7. Divide into equal portions and flatten
8. Sprinkle with minced ham or any meat
9. Roll up
10. Brush with egg
11. Roll in bread crumbs
12. Bake
