## Ingredients

- 1 liter beer
- sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 6 egg yolks
- a little smetana (sour cream)

## Directions

1. Pour sugar into a liter of beer and bring to boil
2. When boiling, add 1 tablespoon flour, 6 egg yolks, and a little smetana
