## Ingredients

- cleaned and peeled truffles
- olive oil
- parsley, chopped
- thyme
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Slice the cleaned and peeled truffles into thin slices
2. Pour olive oil over them
3. Layer with chopped parsley, thyme, pepper, and salt
4. Braise slowly on warm stove
5. Add lemon juice
6. Serve to table
