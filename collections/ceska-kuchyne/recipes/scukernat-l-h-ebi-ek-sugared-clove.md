## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram cloves
- 1 kilogram sugar
- finest potato flour

## Directions

1. Crush quarter kilogram of cloves with kilogram of sugar
2. Sift and mix with finest potato flour
3. Mix together with dissolved sugar to form a dough
4. Shape the dough to resemble cloves
5. Place on warm stoves to dry and harden
