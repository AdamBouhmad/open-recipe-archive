## Ingredients

- One duck
- Three onions
- One teaspoon sage leaves
- Two tablespoons grated bread
- One egg
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Duck is roasted in exactly the same way as goose.
2. It can also be stuffed as follows: finely chop three onions, roast and sift one teaspoon of sage leaves, add two tablespoons of grated bread, one egg, a piece of butter, salt and pepper, mix well, stuff the duck and sew it closed.
3. Roast as you would a goose.
