## Ingredients

- Meissen apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 3½ deciliters (spoonful) sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and place on a baking sheet with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon zest
2. Stew until soft
3. Press through a sieve into a pot
4. Add 3½ deciliters of sour cream
5. Add more sugar if not sweet enough
6. Mix well and place in cold location
7. For those who do not like sweet sauces, Austrian wine can be used instead of sour cream, and either sauce can be used with cold game
