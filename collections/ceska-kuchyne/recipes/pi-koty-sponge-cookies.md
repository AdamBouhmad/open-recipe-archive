## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- egg yolks
- 36 dekagrams (24 lots) beaten sugar
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) flour

## Directions

1. Beat stiff snow from 12 egg whites
2. Add egg yolks and 36 dekagrams beaten sugar
3. Mix on a warm surface until it warms
4. Remove from heat but continue stirring until it cools again
5. Add 18 dekagrams flour and mix
6. Form cookies, sprinkle with sugar, and bake on a paper-covered sheet
