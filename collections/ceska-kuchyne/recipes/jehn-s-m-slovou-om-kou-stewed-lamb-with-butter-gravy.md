## Ingredients

- butter (3 spoonfuls)
- onion
- carrot
- lamb brisket
- salt
- lemon peel
- butter (3 spoonfuls)
- flour (2 spoonfuls)
- hot water or beef broth
- pepper
- cloves (2)
- nutmeg
- green parsley
- white wine (1 glass)
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place three spoonfuls of butter, diced onion and carrot on a braising pan
2. Put neatly cut lamb brisket on top
3. Salt it, add a piece of diced lemon peel and braise it halfway
4. On another braising pan, heat three spoonfuls of butter, add two spoonfuls of flour and make a light roux
5. Slowly add, while constantly stirring, enough hot water or beef broth to make a thickened sauce
6. Add several peppercorns, two cloves, a little nutmeg and a piece of chopped green parsley
7. Let it cook for a while
8. Pour over the lamb, add a glass of white wine and the juice of one lemon
9. Let the meat cook tender in this
10. Remove the meat to a hot platter
11. Remove the fat from the sauce, salt it, strain through a sieve onto the meat and serve
