## Ingredients

- 150 grams sugar
- 150 grams finest flour
- finely crushed vanilla
- 5 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 fresh raw egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Take 150 grams sugar, 150 grams finest flour, finely crushed vanilla, five hard-boiled egg yolks sieved, four fresh raw egg yolks, and salt
2. Mix everything into dough
3. Work the dough well
4. Spread into small baking forms
5. Bake until golden yellow
6. If desired as a filled cake, cut the baked cake, fill the inside with juice or fine preserves, and coat the whole cake with lemon glaze
