## Ingredients

- toast
- one quart of milk
- flour
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Dry the toast as described previously.
2. Then boil one quart of milk, mix one spoonful of flour with a little cold milk, add it to the boiling milk and also add one spoonful of butter and a little salt.
3. Let it boil through; then place the dried toast on a deep plate and pour this milk over it and serve immediately at the table, before it softens.
