## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram currants
- water
- 1 1/2 liters juice
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) sugar
- cinnamon
- 1/4 liter potato starch flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 kilogram of currants in water
2. Strain
3. Cook 1 1/2 liters of juice with 24 dekagrams (16 lots) of sugar and cinnamon
4. Mix 1/4 liter of potato starch flour in cold water
5. Add to the juice
6. Stir the mixture over heat until a thick porridge forms
7. Serve warm at table
