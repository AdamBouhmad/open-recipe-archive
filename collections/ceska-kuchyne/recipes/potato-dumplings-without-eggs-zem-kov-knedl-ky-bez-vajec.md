## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- nutmeg
- sugar
- fried bread, cut into pieces
- flour

## Directions

1. Grate potatoes and mix with butter and salt
2. Add lemon peel rubbed with sugar, nutmeg, sugar, fried bread pieces, and flour
3. Form small dumplings
4. Cook in salted water
5. Serve with braised fruit or sauce
