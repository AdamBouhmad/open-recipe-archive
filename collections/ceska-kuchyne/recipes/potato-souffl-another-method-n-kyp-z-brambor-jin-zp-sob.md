## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- flour
- sugar
- cinnamon
- 60 grams (4 lots) butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- butter for greasing
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Allow cooked potatoes to cool, peel and grate them
2. Mix equal parts flour and sugar so the mixture weighs half the weight of the potatoes
3. Mix everything and add a little cinnamon
4. Beat 60 grams of butter with 10 egg yolks
5. Add stiff snow made from 10 egg whites, folding in carefully
6. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs
7. Bake the soufflé
