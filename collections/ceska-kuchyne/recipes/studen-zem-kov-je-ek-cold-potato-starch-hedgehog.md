## Ingredients

- one liter milk
- cinnamon
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) lemon zest
- 12 dekagrams ground almonds
- 12 dekagrams potato starch mixed in milk
- salt
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Cook one liter milk with cinnamon, mix with 12 dekagrams lemon zest and 12 dekagrams ground almonds, 12 dekagrams potato starch mixed in milk, salt and 45 grams butter
2. Cook slowly, pour into a mold moistened with water and place in a cold location to cool
3. Turn out onto a platter and decorate with preserved fruit
