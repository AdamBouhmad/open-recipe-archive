## Ingredients

- sugar
- fine flawless fragrant clove ground to powder

## Directions

1. Prepare these brittle pieces the same as cinnamon brittle, but use only fine flawless fragrant clove ground to powder instead of cinnamon, added to the sugar for strong cooking
