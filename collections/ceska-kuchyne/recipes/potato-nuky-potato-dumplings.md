## Ingredients

- Butter, grated
- Egg yolks
- Whole eggs
- Flour
- Cooked potatoes, grated
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Mix 6 decagrams of grated butter with two egg yolks, 2 eggs, a tablespoon of flour, 8 tablespoons of cooked grated potatoes, salt, nutmeg and chopped parsley
2. Shape into nuky with a spoon, cook in salted water, and add to broth
