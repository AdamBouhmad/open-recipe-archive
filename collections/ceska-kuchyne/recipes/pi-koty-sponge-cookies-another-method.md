## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- 12 egg yolks
- 250 grams finely beaten sugar
- 250 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Make snow from 12 egg whites
2. Add 12 egg yolks, 250 grams finely beaten sugar, and beat everything well
3. Place on a moderately heated surface and beat constantly
4. Let cool and heat again, repeating this several times with constant beating
5. Mix in about 250 grams fine flour
6. Spread the dough on paper as desired, sprinkle with sugar, and bake at high heat
7. Do not open the oven before the cookies are baked, or they will fall and shrink
8. Cut the baked cookies carefully
9. Cinnamon, vanilla, cloves, chocolate, crushed almonds, or nuts can be added for flavoring
