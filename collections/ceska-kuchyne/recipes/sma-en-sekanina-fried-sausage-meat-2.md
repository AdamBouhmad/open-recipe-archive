## Ingredients

- One pound ground pork
- Three eggs
- Pepper
- Onion
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Place one pound ground pork in a deep bowl
2. Add three whole eggs
3. Add ground pepper, finely chopped onion, salt
4. Add four slices of grated white bread
5. Mix well
6. Shape into small patties
7. Fry quickly in lard
