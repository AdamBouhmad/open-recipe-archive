## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram pounded sugar
- 1/2 kilogram fine flour
- grated lemon and orange peel
- several eggs

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 kilogram pounded sugar with 1/2 kilogram fine flour
2. Add grated lemon and orange peel
3. Mix in several eggs to form a stiff dough
4. Place on greased paper
