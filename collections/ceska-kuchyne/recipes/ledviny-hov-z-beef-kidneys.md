## Ingredients

- Beef kidney
- Water
- Onion
- 20 grams butter
- Caraway
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the kidney into pieces and rinse it
2. Place in a pot without water and braise until the liquid from the kidney evaporates, then drain
3. Rinse the kidney again
4. Chop the onion and add 20 grams of butter, caraway and pepper
5. Braise until the kidney is tender
