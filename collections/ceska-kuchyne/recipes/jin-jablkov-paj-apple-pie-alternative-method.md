## Ingredients

- peeled tart apples
- cinnamon
- sugar
- sultanas
- wine
- lemon peel
- pie crust dough

## Directions

1. Slice the peeled tart apples into thin slices
2. Sprinkle them with cinnamon and sugar
3. Place a layer of apples in a pan lined with dough
4. Sprinkle with sultanas stewed with wine, sugar and finely chopped lemon peel
5. Then place another layer of apples
6. Cover the pie with dough and proceed as in the previous recipe
