## Ingredients

- frog legs, cleaned and rinsed
- onion, browned
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place cleaned and rinsed frog legs on a baking sheet with browned onion.
2. Salt and pepper them.
3. Turn them so they are braised equally on both sides.
4. Serve on a plate.
