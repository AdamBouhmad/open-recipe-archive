## Ingredients

- two tablespoons fresh butter
- two small cups farina
- hot well water
- salt

## Directions

1. Put about two tablespoons of fresh butter on a pan to heat, pour two small cups of farina into it and let it roast until golden, you must constantly stir so it does not burn and no lumps form
2. Then pour in enough hot well water until you have a thick mush, stirring constantly, salt it and let it boil for a while, then pour hot melted butter over the mush
