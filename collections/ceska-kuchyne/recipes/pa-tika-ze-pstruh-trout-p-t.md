## Ingredients

- Trout
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Anchovies
- Capers
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour
- Wine
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Lemon slices
- Salt
- Butter dough

## Directions

1. Clean the trout and cut them up, pour boiling vinegar over.
2. Stew onion, anchovies, capers, and parsley in butter, brown with flour, and add to the sauce.
3. Pour vinegar and wine with onion, cloves, bay leaf, and lemon slices over the trout, season with salt, and simmer until soft.
4. Place the pâté on a baking sheet lined with butter, line with butter dough, and bake well.
