## Ingredients

- pork meat from the thigh and head
- pork ears
- pork snout
- pork tongue
- salt
- ground pepper
- clove
- lemon, sliced
- prepared mustard
- oil and vinegar
- ground clove or dried sage (optional)

## Directions

1. Take pork meat from the thigh and head (trim away the fatty jaw), ears, snout, and tongue (it may also be beef)
2. Boil everything until tender
3. Remove bones and cartilage
4. Skin the tongue
5. Cut everything into neat, oblong pieces
6. Arrange in a round, flat mold
7. On the bottom, lay a layer of thigh meat
8. Sprinkle with a little salt, ground pepper, and clove
9. Then add a layer of ears, salt and season again
10. Then a layer of tongue and continue this way until the mold is full
11. Place a lid on the mold
12. Weight it down well so everything is compressed properly
13. Let it stand overnight in the cold
14. Turn out the cheese and cut into slices
15. Serve either with sliced lemon, prepared mustard, or with oil and vinegar
16. The meat must be placed in the mold quickly and while hot, otherwise proper cohesion will not be achieved and the cheese will crumble when cut
17. Before turning out the cheese, loosen it around the edges with a knife so it does not remain stuck inside
18. Instead of clove, you may also use ground clove or ground dried sage as you prefer
19. The mold may also be lined on the bottom and sides with fat-free boiled pork rind, which is also placed on top before the cheese is weighted down
