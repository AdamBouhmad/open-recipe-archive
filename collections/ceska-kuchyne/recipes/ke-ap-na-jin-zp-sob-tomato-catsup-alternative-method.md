## Ingredients

- 1/2 bushel tomatoes
- onions
- green peppers
- vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- mixed spices
- nutmeg
- cloves

## Directions

1. Take half a bushel of tomatoes, scald and peel them, place in a glazed pot and let them cook well.
2. Then strain through a flour sieve.
3. Return to the pot, add 6 medium-sized onions and 3 green peppers and let them simmer down to a thick consistency.
4. Remove the seeds from the peppers before adding them to cook.
5. Then strain again through a sieve.
6. Add 2 cups of vinegar, half a cup of sugar, 2 teaspoons of salt, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, 1 teaspoon of mixed spices, 1 teaspoon of grated nutmeg and half a teaspoon of cloves.
7. Let simmer for another 10 minutes.
8. Pour into bottles, cork and seal with wax.
