## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs
- 5 tablespoons diluted yeast
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- salt
- piece of lemon peel
- 4 dekagrams 5 grams (3 lots) sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix together milk, egg yolks, eggs, diluted yeast, butter, salt, lemon peel, and sugar well.
2. Add enough flour to make a batter like pancake batter and allow to ferment.
3. Place a piece of butter in each indentation of a special lívance pan, then drop a spoonful of batter and fry until golden on both sides.
4. Brush cooked lívance with thinned plum jam or sprinkle with gingerbread, cheese curds or poppy seeds; they may also be sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon.
