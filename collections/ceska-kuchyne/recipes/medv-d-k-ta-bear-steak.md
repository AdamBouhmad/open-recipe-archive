## Ingredients

- young bear meat from the thigh
- salt
- melted butter
- grated horseradish
- sardine sauce, mustard sauce, or other sharp sauce

## Directions

1. Meat from young bears can be eaten and tastes almost like beef
2. Cut nice steaks from the thigh, salt them, brush with melted butter
3. Roast either on a grill or in a roasting pan
4. Surround them with grated horseradish and serve with sardine sauce, mustard sauce or another sharp sauce
