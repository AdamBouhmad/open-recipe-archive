## Ingredients

- leftover cooked or roasted pike
- sour cabbage
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove bones from leftover pike and cut into pieces
2. Arrange in layers with sour cabbage in a buttered mold lined with breadcrumbs
3. Top with breadcrumbs
4. Bake for half an hour until browned
