## Ingredients

- water
- lemons
- sugar

## Directions

1. To a quart of water, squeeze the juice from three lemons and add six tablespoons of sugar
2. Let it stand covered for a while, then stir and pour into glasses
3. If the water is not cold enough, it can be cooled with ice
4. For the sick, add more water
5. For hot lemonade, use hot water instead of cold
6. Orange lemonade (orangeade) is prepared the same way
