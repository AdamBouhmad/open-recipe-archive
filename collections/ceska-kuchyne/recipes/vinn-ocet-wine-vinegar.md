## Ingredients

- wine remnants from barrels
- good vinegar
- heat at 17-20° R.

## Directions

1. Drain the wine remnants from the barrels so they transform into vinegar
2. Add good vinegar to them and place everything in a warm or sunny location where air and heat have access
3. Heat to 17-20° R.
4. Decant the prepared vinegar into vessels, which must always be topped up so the vinegar does not spoil and become ruined
