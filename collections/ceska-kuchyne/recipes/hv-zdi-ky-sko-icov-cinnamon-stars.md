## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 625 grams finely ground sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 16 grams ground cinnamon
- grated lemon zest
- 1/2 kilogram finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the 8 eggs into stiff snow
2. Add the 625 grams of finely ground sugar and lemon juice, mix thoroughly
3. Add at least 16 grams of ground cinnamon
4. Set aside about 6 tablespoons of this mixture
5. To the remaining mixture add grated lemon zest and 1/2 kilogram of finely chopped almonds, mix and work well
6. Roll out and cut into star shapes with molds
7. Brush the stars with the reserved mixture
8. Bake slowly at gentle heat
