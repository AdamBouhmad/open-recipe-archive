## Ingredients

- firm part of watermelon
- vinegar
- 4 pounds watermelon flesh
- 3 pounds sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- several pieces of cinnamon
- about 8 cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the firm part of the watermelon into small pieces, removing the seeds
2. Place in a stone bowl
3. Pour over enough vinegar to make it slightly sour, not too sour
4. Let stand overnight
5. The next morning bring to a boil
6. Add sugar, lemon, cinnamon, and cloves
7. Simmer gently until it takes on a reddish color and becomes as thick as syrup, stirring frequently to prevent burning
8. When cooled, place in stone jars, seal well, and store in a cool place where it will keep for a long time
