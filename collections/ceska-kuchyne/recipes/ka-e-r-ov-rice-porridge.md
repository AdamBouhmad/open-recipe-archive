## Ingredients

- 17.5 deciliters milk (5 tablespoons)
- 5.25 deciliters rinsed rice (1.25 tablespoons)
- salt
- fresh butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Add 5.25 deciliters of rinsed rice to 17.5 deciliters of milk, add a little salt, a piece of fresh butter, a piece of sugar, and stir over the stove until it thickens
2. Place on a plate and sprinkle with sugar or honey
