## Ingredients

- rice
- finely chopped smoked meat or tongue
- strained peas
- eggs
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Braise the rice
2. Mix with finely chopped smoked meat or tongue and strained peas
3. Bind with eggs and mushrooms
4. Use for stuffing young pigs, etc.
