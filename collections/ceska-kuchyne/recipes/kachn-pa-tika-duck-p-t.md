## Ingredients

- Plucked and parboiled duck
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Cloves
- Pâté dough

## Directions

1. Cut the duck along the backbone.
2. Remove the bones, cut the breasts, lard them.
3. Season thoroughly inside and out with salt and pepper, nutmeg, and cloves.
4. Place in pâté dough form and bake.
5. This pâté is very tasty when served cold.
