## Ingredients

- partridges
- raw egg
- two eggs soaked in milk
- squeezed roll
- flour
- ginger
- pepper
- quarter onion
- lard
- 1/8 kilo (1/8 pound) lard

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges and carefully remove the skin, keeping only the wing tips and lower part of the legs with skin.
2. Chop the meat and pound it in a mortar, do the same with the bones.
3. Add to the mortar four tablespoons of chopped meat, one raw egg and two eggs soaked in squeezed milk, a little flour, ginger, pepper, a quarter chopped onion and a piece of lard.
4. When pounded, push the mixture through a fine sieve.
5. Add chopped lard cubes to the pressed mixture and mix everything together.
6. Stuff the skin with the mixture and sew it closed.
7. Boil for half an hour.
8. Slowly press until all juice flows out.
9. Let cool, then cut into thin slices.
