## Ingredients

- cleaned rabbit
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- eggs
- grated bread roll
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Divide the well-cleaned rabbit into equal pieces, wash them and dry them well with a cloth.
2. Salt and pepper them, dust with flour, dip in beaten eggs, coat in grated bread roll and fry them in hot butter or lard until golden.
3. They go very well as a side dish with vegetables or potato mash.
