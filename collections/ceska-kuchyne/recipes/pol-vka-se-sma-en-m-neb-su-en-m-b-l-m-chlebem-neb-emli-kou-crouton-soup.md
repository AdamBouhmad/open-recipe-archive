## Ingredients

- white bread or bread roll
- one spoon of butter

## Directions

1. Cut white bread or bread roll into small squares, put one spoon of butter on a pan and when the butter melts, put the cut bread in it and stir until it is fried golden.
2. If the broth is strong and fatty, the cut bread can simply be dried in the oven.
3. Serve the clear broth and serve the fried or dried bread separately on a small plate so each person can serve themselves as desired.
