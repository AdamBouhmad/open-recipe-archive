## Ingredients

- canned sardines
- green parsley
- capers
- good Provençal oil

## Directions

1. Open the can with the special tool designed for this purpose
2. Remove sardines from the can just before serving
3. Arrange sardines tastefully on a dish, garnish with green parsley and capers, pour over with good Provençal oil
