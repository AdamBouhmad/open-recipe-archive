## Ingredients

- celery, cooked in salted water until soft
- red beets
- corn salad with indented leaves
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook celery and red beets in salted water until soft
2. Cut into thin slices
3. Mix with corn salad with indented leaves
4. Season with salt
5. Dress with vinegar and oil
6. Add pepper
