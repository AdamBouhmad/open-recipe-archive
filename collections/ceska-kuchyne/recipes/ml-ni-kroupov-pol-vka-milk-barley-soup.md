## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams fine barley
- water
- butter
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- 2 and 1/2 liters hot milk
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the barley in water with butter
2. Add lemon peel and cinnamon
3. Add 2 and 1/2 liters hot milk
4. Add a little sugar and salt
5. Boil everything for a quarter hour
