## Ingredients

- asparagus heads
- asparagus stems
- salted water
- butter
- flour
- parsnip flower
- bread cubes
- frog legs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Reserve the asparagus heads, remove the lower part of the stems and cook in salted water until soft
2. Then press through a sieve and fry with a light roux
3. Add the asparagus heads, let cook about a quarter hour
4. Salt, season with a little parsnip flower and pour into a bowl over fried bread cubes and frog legs, stewed on butter with chopped parsley
