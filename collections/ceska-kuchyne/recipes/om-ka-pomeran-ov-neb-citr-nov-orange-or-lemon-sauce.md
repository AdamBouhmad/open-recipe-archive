## Ingredients

- rind from half an orange (or lemon)
- 2 cupfuls of boiling cream
- 4 spoonfuls of sugar
- 1 spoonful of flour
- a little cold milk
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut off the yellow rind from half an orange with a sharp knife so that no white rind remains on it, put into a pot
2. Pour 2 cupfuls of boiling cream over it, add 4 spoonfuls of sugar, cover the pot and let it cool
3. Mix 1 spoonful of flour with a little cold milk and 4 egg yolks
4. Add to this the cooled cream with the orange
5. Place over heat and stir until it begins to boil
6. Quickly remove the sauce from heat, strain through a sieve and serve immediately at the table
7. For lemon sauce, use lemon rind instead of orange rind. Otherwise the recipe is the same.
