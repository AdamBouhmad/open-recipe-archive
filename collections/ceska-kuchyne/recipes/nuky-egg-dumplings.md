## Ingredients

- Butter, softened and heated
- Egg yolks
- Whole eggs
- Flour

## Directions

1. To 15 decagrams of heated butter, add 6 egg yolks, one after another; after each yolk add 4 whole eggs, and after each egg add a tablespoon of flour
