## Ingredients

- half kilogram flour
- 5 eggs
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 6 dekagrams sugar
- 12 dekagrams picked currants (8 lots)
- salt
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Form a dough from flour, eggs, butter, sugar, currants, salt and cinnamon
2. When well worked, let it rise
3. Grease a pan and bake the rolls on both sides
4. Serve with fruit sauce
