## Ingredients

- Half kilogram smoked meat, bones removed
- 8 eggs
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- 3 eggs
- 150 milliliters good milk or cream
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Finely chop the smoked meat after removing bones
2. Beat 8 eggs and mix with flour to make a dough like noodles
3. Salt and allow to dry
4. Cut into square pieces
5. Boil in salted water, drain, and rinse with cold water so they do not stick
6. Place on a baking pan with the chopped meat
7. Add 3 beaten eggs, 150 milliliters milk or cream, and a little pepper
8. Mix everything together
9. Grease a baking form and sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs
10. Pour the mixture into the form
11. Dot with butter on top
12. Bake in the oven until golden
