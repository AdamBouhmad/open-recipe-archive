## Ingredients

- young venison
- butter
- celery
- carrot
- parsley
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves
- wine
- vinegar
- sugar
- flour
- lemon
- capers

## Directions

1. Place young venison in one piece on a baking sheet with butter, celery, carrot, parsley, onion, thyme, bay leaf, and cloves
2. Pour wine over it and braise until tender
3. Remove the venison
4. To the sauce in which the venison was braised, add vinegar and sugar
5. Boil for a while
6. Add a thickened butter sauce, a piece of burnt sugar, a long-cut lemon, and capers
7. Finally place the sliced venison in the sauce
