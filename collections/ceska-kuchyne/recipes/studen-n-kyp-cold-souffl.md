## Ingredients

- 1.5 deciliters cream
- 6 raw egg yolks
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream
- 35 grams (2 lots) fresh gelatin
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Heat 1.5 deciliters of cream on the stove with 6 raw egg yolks, stirring until completely thick
2. Set aside to cool
3. Add 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) of cream in which 35 grams (2 lots) of fresh gelatin has been mixed
4. Mix everything together and pour into a mold in which biscuits have been placed on the bottom
5. Place on ice
