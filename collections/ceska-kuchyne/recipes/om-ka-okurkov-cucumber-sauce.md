## Ingredients

- quarter liter of beef broth
- pepper
- pale roux
- vinegar
- pickled cucumber

## Directions

1. Mix a quarter liter of beef broth with pepper, pale roux and vinegar
2. Simmer everything
3. Cut the pickled cucumber either thinly into slices or into cubes
4. Simmer for a while longer
