## Ingredients

- dough as for Czech tarts
- filling of choice
- egg, beaten
- butter
- optional: currants for cheese filling, almonds for jam and poppy seed filling, cherries or sour cherries

## Directions

1. Make dough as for Czech tarts
2. Form into a full sheet of tarts one finger thick with a raised edge
3. Prick with a fork so bubbles do not form
4. Brush edges with beaten egg
5. Brush the middle with butter and fill with filling
6. Curd cheese is usually decorated with currants, jam and poppy seed with chopped almonds; the cake can also be filled in stripes or in a circle with several different fillings of cherries or sour cherries
7. Bake in a well-heated oven and slide from the pan onto paper when done
