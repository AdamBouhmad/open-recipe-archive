## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water or wine
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Cloves
- Onion

## Directions

1. Remove the intestine from the cleaned crayfish.
2. Place crayfish in a vessel filled with water and vinegar or wine, season with salt and pepper, add butter, bay leaf, thyme, cloves, and chopped onion.
3. Simmer vigorously for about 20 minutes, remove from heat, and serve the crayfish warm or cold.
