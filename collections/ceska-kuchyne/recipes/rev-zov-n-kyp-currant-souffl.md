## Ingredients

- half liter milk
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 1 liter redcurrant juice
- sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- lemon peel
- snow from 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Dissolve 9 dekagrams sugar and 4 tablespoons flour in half liter milk.
2. Add 12 dekagrams butter and cook until a thick porridge forms.
3. Let cool.
4. Cook 1 liter redcurrant juice with sugar.
5. Pour several tablespoons of the redcurrant juice into a greased mold.
6. Mix the cooled porridge with 6 egg yolks, salt, lemon peel, and snow from 6 eggs.
7. Pour over the redcurrant juice in the mold.
8. Bake for one hour.
