## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- bacon sliced thin and peppered
- fresh lard
- onion
- carrot
- leek
- celery
- parsley roots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- wine vinegar
- sour cream
- bread rolls grated
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose nice calf's liver, remove all skin and thread it with bacon sliced thin and peppered
2. Grease a small pan with fresh lard
3. Put on it sliced onion, sliced carrot, leek, celery, parsley roots, bay leaf and a little thyme
4. Place the liver on top and let it brown slightly in the oven
5. Then add half a wine glass of good vinegar and a cup of sour cream
6. Sprinkle with two spoonfuls of grated bread roll and let the liver stew slowly in its juices, basting it constantly with this liquid
7. When bringing the liver to the table, salt it and place on a dish
8. Remove all fat from the sauce, salt it and strain it through a sieve over the liver
