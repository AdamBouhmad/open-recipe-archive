## Ingredients

- canned salmon
- lemon slices
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Remove the lid from the can and carefully place the salmon on a dish so that the largest pieces remain intact and remove the bones.
2. Pay attention as the salmon in the can is cooked and therefore fragile.
3. Arrange on the dish with lemon slices and serve vinegar and spices alongside.
