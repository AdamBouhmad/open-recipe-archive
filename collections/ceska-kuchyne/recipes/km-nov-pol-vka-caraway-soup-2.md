## Ingredients

- About 3 tablespoons flour
- Butter
- 2-3 liters boiling water
- Various vegetables (kořínky)
- 1 tablespoon caraway
- Salt
- Bread or fried bread

## Directions

1. Fry about 3 tablespoons of flour in butter
2. Cook the roux in boiling water (2-3 liters) with various vegetables
3. Add 1 tablespoon of caraway, salt everything and pour into a bowl where bread or fried bread cut into pieces is located
4. Weak broth can be used instead of water for cooking and preparing the soup
