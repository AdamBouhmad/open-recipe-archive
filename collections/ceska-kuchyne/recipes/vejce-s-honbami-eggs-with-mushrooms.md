## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Pepper
- Caraway seeds
- Salt
- Eggs
- Milk
- Flour
- Bread crumbs
- Pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Clean mushrooms, slice them thinly, and rinse.
2. Place on a baking sheet with butter, pepper, caraway seeds, and salt.
3. Stew until tender.
4. Use about half as many eggs as mushrooms by weight.
5. Add eggs to the mushrooms and mix over heat.
6. When thickened, place on a plate and surround with pickled cucumbers.
7. Alternatively, slice bread thinly, soak in milk, coat in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs, fry in butter, and arrange around the eggs.
8. All edible mushrooms can be used, but the best are: porcini, champignons, or morels.
9. For morels, drain the liquid that releases during cooking before adding butter.
10. If there are plenty of mushrooms, use only the caps; stems may be used but must be carefully inspected for worms.
