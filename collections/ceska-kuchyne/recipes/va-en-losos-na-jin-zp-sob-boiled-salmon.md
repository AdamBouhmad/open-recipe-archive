## Ingredients

- salmon
- hot water
- sardine or fish sauce
- lemon

## Directions

1. When choosing salmon for cooking, be careful that it is not too dry
2. Wrap the salmon in white thin cloth (cheesecloth), pour hot water over it and let it simmer gently
3. When cooked, remove the cloth, place the salmon on a hot dish and serve with sardine or fish sauce and sliced lemon
