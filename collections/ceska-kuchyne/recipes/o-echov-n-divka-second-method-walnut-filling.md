## Ingredients

- 14 dek sugar
- 1 deciliter water
- 20 dek finely crushed peeled walnuts
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- orange zest
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar with water
2. Add finely crushed peeled walnuts, cinnamon, and finely chopped lemon and orange zest
3. Add some vanilla for pleasant taste
