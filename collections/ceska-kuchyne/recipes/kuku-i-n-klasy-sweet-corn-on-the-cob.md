## Ingredients

- young corn cobs (sweet corn variety)
- salt
- sugar
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the corn cobs and remove the silks, but keep the inner leaves
2. Boil them in hot salted water to which you can also add a little sugar
3. Cook for 20 minutes, then remove the cobs, remove the inner leaves and serve
4. Serve with fresh butter and salt
