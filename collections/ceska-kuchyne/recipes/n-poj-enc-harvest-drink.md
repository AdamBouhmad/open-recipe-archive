## Ingredients

- cold water
- sifted ginger
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. To a quart of cold water, add one tablespoon of sifted ginger, three tablespoons of sugar and one cup of vinegar
2. This drink is very pleasant and healthy, especially for those who work in the fields in summer
