## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- kohlrabi leaves
- salt
- pork meat
- vegetables
- pepper
- pale roux

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi and keep the fine middle leaves, cook in salted water.
2. Slice the kohlrabi into cubes or sticks, place on a baking dish, salt and pepper.
3. Take a nice piece of pork meat, salt it, add to it all the vegetables and cook.
4. When the broth is sufficiently strong, pour it over the kohlrabi, which likewise cooks.
5. When soft, add the chopped green leaves, a little pale roux, and cook everything; finally add the cooked pork meat.
