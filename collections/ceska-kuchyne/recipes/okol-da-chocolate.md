## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 deciliters cream
- 5 tablets grated chocolate
- three to four egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the grated chocolate in a saucepan with the cream
2. Mix on the stove until completely thick
3. If desired, mix in three to four egg yolks
