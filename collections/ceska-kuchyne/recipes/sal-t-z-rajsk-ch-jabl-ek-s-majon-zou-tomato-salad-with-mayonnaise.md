## Ingredients

- twelve tomatoes
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- one raw egg yolk
- one small teaspoon of salt
- half a teaspoon of paprika
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one tablespoon of butter
- two tablespoons of mustard
- one small cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice twelve tomatoes into rounds and put on a platter
2. Mash four hard-boiled egg yolks, add one raw egg yolk, one small teaspoon of salt, half a teaspoon of paprika, one tablespoon of sugar, one tablespoon of butter, two tablespoons of mustard and one small cup of vinegar
3. Mix everything well, pour over the tomatoes and keep on ice until ready to use so they are very cold
