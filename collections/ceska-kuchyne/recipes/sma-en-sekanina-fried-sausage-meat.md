## Ingredients

- one pound ground pork
- 3 eggs
- ground pepper
- finely chopped onion
- salt
- 4 slices grated white bread

## Directions

1. Put one pound of ground pork in a deep bowl
2. Beat in 3 whole eggs
3. Add a bit of ground pepper
4. Add a bit of finely chopped onion
5. Salt it
6. Add 4 slices of grated white bread
7. Mix it well
8. Form the mixture into small patties
9. Fry them quickly in lard
