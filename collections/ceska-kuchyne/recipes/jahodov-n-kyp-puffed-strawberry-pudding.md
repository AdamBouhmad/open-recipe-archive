## Ingredients

- 1 quart fresh strawberries
- 1 cup sugar
- water
- 10 egg whites
- powdered sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Mash the strawberries and strain them through a sieve
2. Dissolve less than 1 cup sugar in a little water on a saucepan, do not brown it
3. Pour onto a plate and add the strained strawberries
4. Mix well and then gradually fold in stiff peaks made from 10 egg whites until it becomes very thick and a spoon can stand in it
5. Butter a metal dish and fill it with the mixture, mounding it nicely
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake slowly in the oven
7. When the pudding begins to crack on top from inside, it is done
8. If baking in a ceramic dish, place it on a baking sheet heavily sprinkled with salt
9. The same method is used for raspberry and other fruit puddings
