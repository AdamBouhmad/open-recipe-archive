## Ingredients

- half liter groats
- butter
- salt
- three-tenths deciliter (one tablespoon) dried mushrooms
- pepper
- allspice
- ginger
- clove
- two cloves garlic, grated
- pork lard

## Directions

1. Cook groats in water with a piece of butter until soft
2. Rinse dried mushrooms and cook in salted water
3. Strain mushrooms and chop finely
4. Mix groats with mushrooms
5. Add pepper, allspice, ginger, clove, and grated garlic
6. Grease a baking dish with pork lard
7. Bake groats with mushrooms in it
