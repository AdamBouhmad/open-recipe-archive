## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- flour
- flower
- salt
- butter
- strained broth

## Directions

1. Make dough from 4 eggs, flour, flower and salt (flour enough to make a thin batter)
2. Heat butter in a pan and strain thin dough through a strainer into it
3. Carefully remove individual drops from the butter, place in soup bowl with chopped gills and livers
4. Pour broth over them
