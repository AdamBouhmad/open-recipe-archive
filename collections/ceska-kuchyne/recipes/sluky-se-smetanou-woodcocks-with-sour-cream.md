## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- salt
- vinegar
- onion
- juniper berries
- pepper
- bay leaf
- butter
- sour cream
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcocks and cook them.
2. Salt and place in vinegar with onion, juniper berries, pepper and bay leaf.
3. When marinated, roast in butter and braise completely in sour cream mixed with vinegar.
