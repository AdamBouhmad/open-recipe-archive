## Ingredients

- piece of butter
- two onions
- one celery root
- two parsley roots
- two carrots
- one cabbage
- one pound beef
- one pound veal
- various leftover poultry
- three quarts beef soup

## Directions

1. Put butter on a pan, add chopped onion, celery, parsley roots, carrots, and cabbage.
2. Cut the beef and veal into pieces, place with various poultry leftovers on the vegetables and let braise until brown, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
3. Transfer to a pot, pour three quarts of beef soup over it and let cook for about an hour.
4. Strain the soup through a damp cloth or fine sieve and pour onto fried bread cubes or various garnishes.
5. This soup must be very clear, and if it becomes cloudy during cooking, it must be clarified before serving.
6. Serves 6 to 8 persons.
