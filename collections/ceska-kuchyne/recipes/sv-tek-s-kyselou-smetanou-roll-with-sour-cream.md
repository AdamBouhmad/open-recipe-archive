## Ingredients

- half liter sour cream
- 75 grams sugar (5 lots)
- 15 egg yolks
- flour mixed with sour cream
- salt
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat sour cream with sugar and 15 egg yolks until foamy
2. Mix with flour that was beaten with sour cream, salt and snow from 10 egg whites
3. Bake the roll, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and serve with sauce
