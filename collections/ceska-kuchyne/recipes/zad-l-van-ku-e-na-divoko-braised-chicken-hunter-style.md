## Ingredients

- chicken
- pepper
- celery root
- parsley root
- carrot
- new spices
- thyme
- bay leaves
- lemon peel
- juniper berries
- good wine vinegar
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken as for braising in general
2. Add pepper, piece of celery root, parsley root, piece of carrot, new spices, thyme, 2 bay leaves, lemon peel, 2 juniper berries, 2 spoonfuls of good wine vinegar and 15 grams butter
3. Braise everything covered
4. Place tender chicken on dish
5. Make light roux, strain through sieve into sauce
