## Ingredients

- fresh morels
- butter
- onion
- fresh parsley
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter roux
- beef broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the morels several times in lukewarm water to remove any sand
2. Slice them into rounds and place on a saucepan with a piece of melted butter, sliced onion, and a piece of chopped fresh parsley
3. Salt and season with nutmeg
4. Let them stew until tender
5. Make a small butter roux and thin it with beef broth
6. Add this to the morels and let them simmer a bit longer
7. Finally season with lemon juice and serve hot
