## Ingredients

- egg whites from 4 eggs
- approximately two bowls of powdered sugar
- vanilla or lemon juice to taste
- butter
- stiff thick paper (preferably writing paper)

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites from 4 eggs into meringue
2. Add approximately two bowls of powdered sugar and vanilla or lemon juice to taste
3. Beat for a quarter to half hour until stiff
4. Butter stiff thick paper (preferably writing paper) and place on a baking sheet or wooden board
5. Drop heaps the size of approximately half an egg about an inch apart
6. Bake in the oven with the door open
7. Be careful that they do not turn red
8. When baked to golden color, remove and after about five minutes lift from the paper with a knife, pressing the side that was on the paper together
