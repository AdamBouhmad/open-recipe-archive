## Ingredients

- ripe medlars
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from the ripe medlars.
2. Place the medlars on a dish and rub and stir them several times daily until they are completely soft.
3. Press the very soft medlars through a sieve.
4. Add an equal amount of crushed sugar to the pressed pulp.
5. Mix everything together and cook together, then pour into containers.
