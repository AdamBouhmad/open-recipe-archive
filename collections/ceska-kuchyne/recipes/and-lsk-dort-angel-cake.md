## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of flour
- one teaspoon of cream of tartar
- one coffee cup of fine granulated sugar
- eleven egg whites, as fresh and cold as possible
- half teaspoon of vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Mix the cream of tartar with the flour and sift five times
2. sift the sugar five times
3. place the egg whites in a deep bowl and beat with a wire whisk until they stop flowing and stand up when the whisk is lifted, but do not overbeat
4. gradually sprinkle the sifted sugar into the whites while mixing loosely
5. similarly fold in the flour
6. add the vanilla extract, mix, and pour into a clean, completely dry ungreased pan
7. bake in a moderately warm oven for three-quarters of an hour, but do not open the oven during the first quarter hour as the cake will easily collapse
8. invert the pan with the cake onto a plate and let it cool completely in the pan
9. when cooled, remove from the pan without damage
10. coat with sugar icing made by mixing one spoonful of milk with powdered sugar in a cup until it is a thick paste, rubbing briefly, then adding a few drops of vanilla extract and adding more sugar if needed
