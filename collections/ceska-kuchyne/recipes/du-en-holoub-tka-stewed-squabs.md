## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- onion, sliced into rounds
- bay leaves
- thyme
- juniper berries
- peppercorns
- allspice berries
- ginger
- wine vinegar
- flour
- browned sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs and cut them into quarters
2. Put a piece of butter in a pot and layer the sliced onion
3. Add bay leaves, thyme, several juniper berries, six peppercorns, six allspice berries and a piece of ginger
4. Place the cut squabs on top
5. Pour a little wine vinegar over it
6. Cover with a lid and let it braise
7. Then remove the squabs to another pot and strain the sauce over them
8. Mix about three spoonfuls of flour with a piece of butter by hand and form into several small balls which you place here and there in the sauce
9. Let it simmer slowly with frequent stirring until thick
10. Add a piece of browned sugar to the sauce
