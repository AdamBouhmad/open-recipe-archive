## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 1/4 pound powdered sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped almonds (optional)
- fine sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Make snow from 2 egg whites
2. Gradually add 1/4 pound powdered sugar
3. Add juice from 1 lemon
4. Stir for half an hour
5. Then add zest from 1 lemon finely chopped and if desired 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
6. Mix in
7. Make small mounds on a wax-coated sheet
8. Sprinkle with fine sugar and bake quickly
9. If dough is too thin, add sugar and stir for a while longer
10. Instead of almonds you can add grated coconut; in that case omit the lemon juice and add a little vanilla extract
