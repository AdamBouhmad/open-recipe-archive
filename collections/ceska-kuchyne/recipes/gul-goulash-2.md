## Ingredients

- front or back beef or pork meat
- 30 grams butter or rendered beef fat
- 1 large onion or half of a second small one
- pepper
- flour
- water or beef broth
- fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut meat into pieces
2. Heat butter or fat with chopped onion until softened
3. Add chopped meat, season with pepper, and braise covered until the onion turns red and meat softens
4. If meat becomes dry, add water or beef broth
5. Transfer meat carefully to a separate plate
6. Add a tablespoon of flour to the sauce, stir, braise briefly on the stove, and strain over the meat
7. Usually fried potatoes are added
