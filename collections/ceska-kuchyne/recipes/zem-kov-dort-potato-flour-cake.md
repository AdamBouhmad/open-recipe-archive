## Ingredients

- half a kilogram (pound) butter
- half a kilogram sugar
- 14 egg yolks
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sweet almonds
- several crushed bitter almonds
- grated lemon zest
- half a kilogram potato flour
- stiff snow from 14 egg whites
- pickled cherries
- glaze
- pickled fruit for decoration

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until foamy for 3/4 hour.
2. Add half a kilogram sugar, 14 egg yolks, 12 decagrams (8 lots) sweet almonds and several crushed bitter almonds, and grated lemon zest.
3. Continue mixing.
4. Add half a kilogram potato flour and stiff snow from 14 egg whites.
5. Bake two thin cakes from the mixture.
6. On one cake, place pickled cherries.
7. Cover with the second cake.
8. Glaze and decorate with pickled fruit.
