## Ingredients

- several mushrooms
- green parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- flour
- strong meat broth
- pepper
- good cream
- flour dumplings or croutons

## Directions

1. Clean and wash several mushrooms and slice them thinly
2. Add some finely chopped green parsley and braise in butter
3. Dust with a little flour and let braise a bit more
4. Pour in enough strong meat broth to make a thin sauce
5. Add pepper and several tablespoons of good cream
6. Let simmer and pour into a soup bowl over sliced flour dumplings or croutons fried until golden
