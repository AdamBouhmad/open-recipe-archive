## Ingredients

- 125 grams cinnamon
- 1 liter pure water
- 750 grams sugar
- 3-4.5 liters good, fine spirits (kořalka)

## Directions

1. Crush 125 grams of cinnamon coarsely and boil it in 1 liter of pure water for about half an hour
2. Add 750 grams of sugar and let it settle
3. Decant the clear liquid and when it has cooled completely, add 3-4.5 liters of good, fine spirits
4. Seal everything well and place on warm stove for several days where excessive heat will not harm it
5. Strain everything into a bottle
