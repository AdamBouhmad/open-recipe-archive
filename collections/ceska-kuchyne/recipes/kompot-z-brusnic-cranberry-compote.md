## Ingredients

- cranberries, cleaned
- sugar
- lemon peel
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned cranberries with sugar, lemon peel, and cinnamon for a quarter hour
2. Place on a plate
3. Remove the cinnamon and lemon peel
4. The cranberries can be added to baking
