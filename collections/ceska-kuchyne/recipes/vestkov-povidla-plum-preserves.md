## Ingredients

- Fresh or dried plums
- Sugar
- Cinnamon (optional)
- Lemon zest (optional)

## Directions

1. Carefully select and clean the plums in water
2. Cook them thoroughly
3. Remove the pits
4. Chop them completely and finely
5. Add broth from the cooked plums as needed
6. Many housewives add ground cinnamon, finely chopped lemon zest, and other optional additions for pleasant flavor
7. Store in containers
