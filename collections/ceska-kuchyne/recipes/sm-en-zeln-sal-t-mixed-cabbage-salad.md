## Ingredients

- white cabbage head
- red cabbage head
- salted boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Select a nice head of white and a nice head of red cabbage
2. Remove leaves and ribs and cut leaves into very fine thin strips
3. Cook each type of cabbage separately in boiling salted water for about five minutes
4. Drain the water and let the salad cool
5. Season each type separately with salt and pepper
6. Dress with vinegar and oil
7. Arrange neatly side by side on a dish
