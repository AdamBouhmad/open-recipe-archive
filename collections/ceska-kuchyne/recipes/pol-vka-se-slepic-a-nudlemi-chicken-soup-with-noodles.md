## Ingredients

- one older hen
- two quarts cold water
- salt
- celery
- cabbage
- parsley root
- a small handful of peas
- fine noodles

## Directions

1. Pluck an older hen and place it in a pot
2. Pour two quarts of cold water over it and salt it
3. Let it cook, skim it carefully
4. Add some vegetables: celery, cabbage, parsley root and a small handful of peas
5. Let it cook for about three hours
6. When the hen is tender, remove the pot from heat
7. Skim off the fat
8. Remove the hen, cut it into neat pieces and place in a bowl
9. Strain the broth from the hen
10. Add and pour fine noodles into it
11. Let it cook for about five minutes
12. Pour over the cut hen in a soup bowl
