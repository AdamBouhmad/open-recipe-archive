## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- salted water
- beef broth
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut them into pieces, and boil them in salted water.
2. Grate and mash the potatoes.
3. Thin the mixture with beef broth.
4. Press through a sieve.
5. Season with butter, pepper, and salt.
6. Stir over heat for a short time.
