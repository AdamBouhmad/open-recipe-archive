## Ingredients

- several pieces of various fish
- celery
- parsley
- leek or white onion
- several pieces of bread
- egg yolks from hard-boiled eggs
- clear fish soup
- four whole eggs and two egg yolks
- flowers (spice)
- crayfish butter (if available)

## Directions

1. Put several pieces of various fish in a pan
2. add celery, parsley, leek or white onion and let it stew
3. then fry several pieces of bread
4. crush it in a mortar with hard-boiled egg yolks
5. put this with the stewed mixture in a pot
6. pour fish bouillon on it and let it boil
7. meanwhile beat four whole eggs and two egg yolks into a small cup of cold clear fish soup
8. add a little flowers to it
9. beat well, cover with clean cloth, tie the cup with string
10. hang it in boiling water and let it boil for one hour
11. then take it out with a small spoon into a bowl like small dumplings
12. strain the soup, beat it with several egg yolks
13. add flowers to it, and if you have it, a little crayfish butter
14. pour it into a bowl over the dumplings
