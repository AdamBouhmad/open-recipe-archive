## Ingredients

- cooked spinach
- bacon
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Let the cooked spinach cool so the water is released, place on a strainer
2. Cut bacon into cubes and fry, make a roux with flour, and while constantly stirring stew and mix with the spinach
