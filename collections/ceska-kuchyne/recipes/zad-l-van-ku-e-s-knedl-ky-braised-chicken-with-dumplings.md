## Ingredients

- prepared chicken
- 30 grams butter
- flower
- egg yolk
- whole egg
- salt
- grated bread roll
- cold water
- beef broth
- flour
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken as braised chicken with rice, make similar sauce
2. Beat 30 grams butter with a little flower, one egg yolk, one whole egg and a little salt
3. Mix in enough grated bread roll to make dumplings
4. Dip fingers in cold water, take a piece of mixture, round it and place in prepared beef broth
5. Add dumplings to chicken with sauce
6. Alternatively, beat 30 grams butter with 2 egg yolks, a little flower and salt until foamy
7. Beat 2 egg whites into stiff peaks and mix in flour to make dumplings with coffee spoon
8. Cook in beef broth and arrange around chicken on dish
9. The sauce for dumplings is very tasty when lemon juice is added
