## Ingredients

- one cup clear warm water
- a piece of butter about half an egg in size
- one whole egg
- about one quarter cup sugar
- a pinch of salt
- about one quart flour
- good apples
- butter
- bread, grated and fried
- one half pound raisins
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- melted butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pour one cup of clear warm water into a cup and dissolve in it a piece of butter about half an egg in size
2. Add one whole egg, about one quarter cup sugar, and a pinch of salt
3. Put about one quart of flour on a pastry board, make a large well in it, pour the water mixture into it, and work into a stiff dough like for buns
4. Knead well, beat with a rolling pin, cover with a bowl and let rest for one hour
5. Meanwhile, peel good apples and cut into thin slices
6. Fry grated bread in butter and pick through and clean one half pound of raisins
7. Spread a cloth, dust lightly with flour, and stretch the dough very thin on it
8. Lay apples in a line next to the cloth edge, sprinkle with the fried bread and raisins, then with sugar, dust with ground cinnamon and sprinkle with melted butter
9. Gradually lift one side of the cloth, rolling the strudel into a roll
10. Grease a baking pan well with butter, roll the strudel into it in a circle, brush the surface with butter and let it bake slowly for one hour
11. While baking, brush with butter; when half-baked, pour over a cup of milk and finish baking
12. When done, turn out onto a long platter (platter), cut into slices and sprinkle generously with powdered sugar
