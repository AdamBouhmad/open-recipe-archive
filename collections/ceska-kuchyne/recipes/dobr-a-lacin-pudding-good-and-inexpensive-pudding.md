## Ingredients

- half cup syrup
- half cup hot water
- one teaspoon baking soda
- half cup large pitted and finely chopped raisins
- one beaten egg
- one and a half cups flour
- one cup sugar
- half cup water
- half cup butter
- one large tablespoon rum essence

## Directions

1. Mix half cup syrup, half cup hot water with one teaspoon baking soda added, half cup large pitted and finely chopped raisins, one beaten egg, and one and a half cups flour.
2. Dip a small pudding bag in hot water and sprinkle it with flour.
3. Pour the mixture into it and tie it loosely so that the mixture has enough room to rise when cooking.
4. Boil the bag in boiling water for one hour.
5. For the sauce, cook one cup sugar, half cup water, half cup butter, and one large tablespoon rum essence together for five minutes.
