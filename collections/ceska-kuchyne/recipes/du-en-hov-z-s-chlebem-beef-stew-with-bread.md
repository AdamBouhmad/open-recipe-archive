## Ingredients

- a piece of nice meat from the rump
- bacon
- onion, chopped and fried in butter
- several cloves
- rye bread, cut into cubes
- broth
- white wine (optional)
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of nice meat from the rump, singe it, salt it, and pound it.
2. It is good to thread it with bacon and place it in a pot on chopped onion fried in butter.
3. Add several cloves and let it braise.
4. Cut rye bread into cubes and add an appropriate amount to the meat.
5. Let it braise covered, frequently baste it with broth, and according to preference you can also add a small glass of white wine.
6. Before finishing, remove the meat to a platter, cut it into slices, and pour the strained sauce over it.
