## Ingredients

- one quart sauerkraut
- one pound pork
- salt
- pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Take one quart of sauerkraut and drain all the water from it
2. Place in a glazed pot or clay dish (not iron or tin as it will not taste good)
3. Add a bit of salt
4. Grind one pound of pork finely on a meat grinder
5. Mix the meat into the sauerkraut and season with pepper
6. Stir well
7. Cover with a lid and place in an oven to cook for about one hour
8. Remove and pour a cupful of sour cream over it, but do not stir
9. Return to the oven for another quarter hour
10. When the top is nicely reddened it is done
