## Ingredients

- Hare
- Onion
- 6-7 peppers
- New spice berries
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Lemon peel
- Wine vinegar
- Water or broth
- Flour
- Black gingerbread
- White sugar
- Burnt sugar
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Hang the hare by its hind legs
2. Make an incision around the knees and skin the hare up to the head, removing ears and eyes
3. Remove the eyes completely, cut the upper and lower lips
4. Remove the gallbladder carefully
5. Cut off the skin and small ribs from the loins on both sides so the back with spine remains
6. Set aside the liver, lungs and heart
7. Remove the lower jaw, clean it well, remove the gullet, sever the feet and shoulder blades separately, wash everything in several waters to completely remove blood
8. Put the entire front with offal in a pot with a liter of water or broth
9. Add chopped onion, 6-7 peppers, several grains of new spice, thyme, bay leaf, lemon peel, and 4 spoonfuls of good wine vinegar
10. Simmer until the meat is tender
11. Place the meat on a platter and prepare the sauce
12. Mix a roux with a piece of black gingerbread, white sugar and burnt sugar, simmer and strain
13. If not sour enough, add vinegar
14. Place the hare in this sauce, simmer briefly, then pour onto a platter and arrange nicely
15. Usually peeled potatoes are added
16. The whole hare or rabbit may be prepared this way
