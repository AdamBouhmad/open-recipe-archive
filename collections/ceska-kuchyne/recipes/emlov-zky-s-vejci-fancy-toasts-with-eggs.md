## Ingredients

- Oblong fried bread slices
- Green butter
- Sardines
- Hard-boiled egg yolk
- Hard-boiled egg white

## Directions

1. Around oblong fried bread slices, make a border of green butter
2. Divide the middle with sardines placed across into several compartments
3. Fill these compartments alternately with hard-boiled chopped yolks and whites
