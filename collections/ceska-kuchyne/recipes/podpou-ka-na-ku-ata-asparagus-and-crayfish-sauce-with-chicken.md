## Ingredients

- cleaned chicken
- butter
- salt
- bundle of asparagus
- salted water
- crayfish
- chicken broth or broth from chicken bones
- 60 grams butter
- flour
- nutmeg flower
- sweet cream
- parsley
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Clean chicken and cut into quarters, salt them
2. Brown chicken on both sides in butter
3. Cook a bundle of asparagus in salted water until soft
4. Cook crayfish in salted water and remove meat from tails and claws
5. To half a liter of crayfish broth add 60 grams of butter mixed with flour, add powdered nutmeg flower and a little sweet cream
6. Boil everything well and add finely chopped parsley
7. If using chicken bone broth instead, thicken it slightly with flour, mix with several spoonfuls of cream, crayfish butter and eggs
8. When serving, arrange crayfish meat in the center, surround with asparagus with heads pointing inward, arrange browned chicken around these
9. Cover crayfish and asparagus with sauce, moisten chicken with its own sauce, garnish the edge with fried bread roll
