## Ingredients

- 1 kg honey
- flour
- 2 tablespoons liquor
- 1 dekagram potash (potassium carbonate) dissolved in a teaspoon of water
- a little allspice
- orange peel, finely chopped
- 5 grams cardamom

## Directions

1. Heat the honey and mix in flour with the boiling honey, pouring in only 1/4 of the honey at once, adding the remaining 1/4 as the dough forms
2. Add 2 tablespoons liquor, 1 dekagram potash dissolved in a teaspoon of water, and spices: a little allspice, finely chopped orange peel, and 5 grams cardamom to the dough
3. Knead the dough thoroughly on a work surface until it is pliable
4. Form pieces of dough the size of walnuts into small cookies
5. Place them a thumb's width apart on a greased baking sheet
6. Bake in an oven until golden
7. Store in a dry place in a container tied with parchment
