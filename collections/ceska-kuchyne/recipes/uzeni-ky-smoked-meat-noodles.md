## Ingredients

- flour
- 5 eggs
- salt
- smoked meat broth

## Directions

1. Salt flour and add eggs
2. Make dough like for noodles
3. When rolled-out dough is dry, cut into square pieces
4. Boil in salted water
5. Drain with cold water so they do not stick together
6. Alternatively, pieces of dough may be boiled in the broth in which smoked meat was cooked
7. Finely chop the meat
8. Mix with dough
9. Add 4 eggs beaten with milk and butter
10. Add piece of sugar or pepper
11. Salt
