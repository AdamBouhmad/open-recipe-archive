## Ingredients

- fresh asparagus
- salted water
- fresh water
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean fresh asparagus well and wash it
2. Remove tough lower stalks completely
3. Cut into two-centimeter pieces
4. Cook in salted water until tender
5. Cool in fresh water
6. Place on a strainer to drain
7. When cooled, prepare with oil, pepper, salt, and vinegar
