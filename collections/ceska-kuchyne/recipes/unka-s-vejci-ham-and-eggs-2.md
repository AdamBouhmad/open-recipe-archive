## Ingredients

- Cooked ham
- Butter
- Eggs
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Slice cooked ham into thin slices about the size of a palm
2. Heat a piece of butter in a pan
3. Fry the ham on both sides
4. Arrange the ham on a heated dish
5. Place one fried egg on each slice of ham
6. Serve with sliced and fried potatoes
