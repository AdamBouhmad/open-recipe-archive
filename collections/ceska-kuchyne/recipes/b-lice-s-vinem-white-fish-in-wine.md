## Ingredients

- white fish
- salt
- stuffing
- butter
- flour
- red wine
- paprika
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the white fish, salt it and fill it with either ordinary stuffing or carp stuffing, place on a baking pan greased with butter, sprinkle with butter mixed with flour, pour with half a pint of red wine and let it bake for an hour with frequent basting.
2. Then remove the fish onto a dish, strain the sauce, add a little more red wine, a teaspoon of browned flour, a pinch of paprika, mix well, pour over the fish and garnish with sliced lemon.
