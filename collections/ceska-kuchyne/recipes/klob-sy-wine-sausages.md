## Ingredients

- pork meat from the neck
- white bread soaked in sour cream
- ground white pepper
- clove
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- salt
- wine
- sheep intestines, cleaned

## Directions

1. Take a piece of short pork meat from the neck and chop it finely
2. For every three parts of meat, add one part of white bread soaked in sour cream
3. Season with ground white pepper, clove, and finely chopped lemon zest
4. Salt to taste
5. Add a little wine
6. Mix and work the mixture thoroughly for at least ten minutes
7. Fill the cleaned sheep intestines with the mixture
8. Alternatively, take two parts pork and one part veal, chop, and moisten with sour cream, then season and proceed as above
9. Shape the finished sausages into a coil
10. Pierce crosswise with two thin skewers to hold them together nicely
11. Store in a cool place until needed
12. Serve roasted with braised or fried cabbage, or only with mustard
13. Good dumplings or mashed potatoes with fried onions also go very well with them
