## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit, salted for 2-3 days
- beef broth
- fresh butter
- salt
- parsley root
- celery
- lemon peel
- flower (cloves)
- flour for roux
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Salt the rabbit for 2-3 days, otherwise it will be tough.
2. Wash the salted rabbit, cut it into pieces, and place on a baking dish.
3. Pour beef broth over it and add a piece of fresh butter, salt, parsley root, celery, lemon peel, and cloves.
4. Braise until completely tender.
5. Carefully remove the rabbit pieces.
6. Make a pale roux from butter and flour and add to the remaining broth to achieve appropriate thickness.
7. Strain the sauce over the rabbit and add the juice of one lemon.
