## Ingredients

- fresh radishes
- cold water
- ice
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Select nice fresh radishes - not hollow ones - place them in cold water for half an hour and then dry them well
2. Cut off the roots and leave only two or three of the smallest leaves from the tops
3. Place on a separate dish with several pieces of ice
4. Put fresh butter on the table so each person can serve themselves as they wish
