## Ingredients

- 4-pound cabbage head
- salt
- butter (about the size of a chicken egg)
- flour
- water (about half a cup)
- sugar (1 tablespoon)
- black pepper (1 tablespoon)
- dough (same as for kolaches)

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage well
2. Chop it finely on a vegetable grater
3. Salt lightly and squeeze out the water
4. Place on a baking pan in the oven to braise until soft
5. Make a roux with butter and flour
6. Add about half a cup of water to the sauce
7. Add the cabbage to the sauce
8. Add sugar and black pepper, and more salt if needed
9. Prepare dough as for kolaches
10. When risen, roll out and wrap the cabbage filling
11. Bake well, testing with a fork after about half an hour—if the fork comes out clean of dough, it is done
