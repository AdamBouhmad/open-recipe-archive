## Ingredients

- 75 grams butter
- 3 eggs
- 25 dekagrams sugar
- 25 dekagrams pounded almonds
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- 25 dekagrams flour

## Directions

1. Beat 75 grams butter
2. Add 3 eggs and 25 dekagrams sugar
3. Add 25 dekagrams pounded almonds and sugar-rubbed lemon peel
4. Mix for 1/4 hour
5. Add 25 dekagrams flour
6. Form small balls and bake slowly
