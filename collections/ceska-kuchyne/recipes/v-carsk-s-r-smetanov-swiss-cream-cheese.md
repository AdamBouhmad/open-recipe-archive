## Ingredients

- freshly milked milk
- rennet
- oleander or saffron (optional)
- salt

## Directions

1. Swiss cream cheese is prepared from freshly milked milk.
2. The milk is heated to +30° R., set aside, curdled, and colored with oleander or saffron; unccolored cheese has a buttery color.
3. The cheese is slowly broken and crumbled, the whey is poured off, and the cheese is placed into molds, into which it is firmly pressed.
4. In a few hours it is turned out, left to dry, salted, turned, and dried with cloth, which is repeated for about fourteen days, and then placed into boxes on edges so it becomes sweaty.
5. After sweating, a fatty layer appears, which is scraped away with knives and brushes, which is repeated for several more weeks; the cheese ripens more quickly if it is brushed with warm whey, then dried and stored in boxes.
