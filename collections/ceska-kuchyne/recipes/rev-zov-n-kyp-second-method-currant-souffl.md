## Ingredients

- 1 liter redcurrant berries
- sugar
- 1 liter sour sour cream
- 9 eggs
- 4-5 tablespoons grated roll
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter redcurrant berries with sugar into a mold.
2. Dissolve 1 liter sour sour cream with 9 eggs, 4-5 tablespoons grated roll, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, and 12 dekagrams crushed sugar.
3. Add the currant mixture.
4. Bake the soufflé for half an hour.
5. When removing from the oven, prick with a fork to prevent it from collapsing.
