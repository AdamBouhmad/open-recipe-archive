## Ingredients

- leftover roasted goose
- vinegar
- water or beef broth
- lemon peel
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaves
- pepper
- spice
- salt
- pale roux
- burnt sugar
- Austrian wine
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted goose into neat pieces
2. On a baking pan, put part vinegar and part water or beef broth
3. Add a piece of sliced lemon peel, a whole onion, thyme, several bay leaves, a little pepper, spice, and salt
4. Boil everything together; then press pale roux into it, add a piece of burnt sugar and a little Austrian wine
5. Boil again; place the sliced goose on another baking pan and strain the sauce over it
6. Let simmer for half an hour; finally arrange on a platter surrounded by fatty potatoes
