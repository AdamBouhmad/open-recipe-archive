## Ingredients

- two quarts parsley water or lean broth
- meat from two lobsters
- liquid from four dozen small cooked mussels
- three tablespoons pale butter roux
- a dozen small onions
- half a pint of sherry
- a pinch of ground paprika
- four ounces crawfish butter

## Directions

1. Put the meat from two lobsters into two quarts of parsley water or lean broth, add the liquid from four dozen small cooked mussels
2. Brown it with three tablespoons of pale butter roux, add a dozen small onions, half a pint of sherry, and a pinch of ground paprika
3. Let it boil for half an hour
4. Remove the lobster meat from the soup, cut it into cubes and place in a dish with the cooked mussels and onions
5. Skim the foam from the soup, add four ounces of crawfish butter, mix well and strain into the dish over the cut lobster
