## Ingredients

- handful of caraway seed
- green parsley
- celery
- salt
- boiling water
- farina
- 3 or 4 egg yolks or 2 whole eggs
- butter
- flowers
- ginger
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put into a pot a handful of caraway seed, a little green parsley and celery, add salt and pour boiling water on it
2. Let it boil a little
3. Strain it, and when it begins to boil again, add farina, stirring often so no lumps form
4. Then add 3 or 4 egg yolks or 2 whole eggs, depending on the amount of soup
5. Add a piece of butter, a little flowers, a little ginger, a little sweet cream
6. Mix it well and thicken the soup with it
7. This soup may be served in cups in place of barley soup, only it must be thin; not much farina must be cooked into it
