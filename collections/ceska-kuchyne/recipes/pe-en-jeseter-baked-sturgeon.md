## Ingredients

- Fresh sturgeon
- Salt
- Butter, melted
- Bread crumbs, grated
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Skin the sturgeon
2. Wash the meat from fresh sturgeon and salt it
3. Place it in a baking pan in melted butter
4. Coat the surface generously with butter
5. Sprinkle with grated bread crumbs
6. Bake in the oven
7. Place on a dish
8. Pour hot butter mixed with lemon juice over it
9. Serve to the table
