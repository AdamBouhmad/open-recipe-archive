## Ingredients

- a little scalded millet porridge
- 2-3 litres milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil a little scalded millet porridge in 2-3 litres of milk for half an hour
2. Salt it, add sugar and cinnamon
