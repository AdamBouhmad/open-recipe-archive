## Ingredients

- 3-4 dekagrams (2-3 lots) coffee
- finely crushed, sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Brew 3-4 dekagrams (2-3 lots) coffee in a cup
2. Let cool
3. Mix with finely crushed, sifted sugar until very thick
4. Heat and apply to baked goods
