## Ingredients

- half pound (1 cup) fine sugar
- zest of orange or lemon, grated
- six eggs and six yolks
- 2 small cups sifted flour
- fresh butter for greasing the form
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Put into a bowl half pound fine sugar, grated orange or lemon zest, six eggs and six yolks, mix until well frothed
2. Add 2 small cups sifted flour and mix well again
3. Grease form with fresh butter
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped almonds
5. Put the prepared batter into it and bake for three-quarters of an hour
