## Ingredients

- pike head and tail
- water
- vinegar
- onion
- cloves
- salt
- bay leaves
- lemon
- pepper
- allspice
- egg whites
- wine
- gelatin
- oil
- capers
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and tail of the pike and let them sit salted for an hour
2. After an hour, dry and boil with water, vinegar, onion, cloves, salt, two bay leaves, sliced lemon, pepper and allspice
3. Skim foam as it rises; after a quarter hour remove the fish
4. Strain the sauce, boil, mix in 3 egg whites, a quarter liter of wine and a bit of gelatin
5. Oil a mold and decorate the bottom with sliced lemon, capers, and parsley leaves
6. Pour in aspic and let cool
7. Then place alternating layers of pike pieces and aspic until the mold is full
