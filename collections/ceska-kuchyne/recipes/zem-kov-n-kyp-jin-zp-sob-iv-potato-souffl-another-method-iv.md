## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- water
- white paper
- 15 egg yolks
- half a kilogram (one pound) beaten sugar
- a piece of vanilla or lemon zest
- 15 egg whites
- potato starch flour

## Directions

1. Peel and grate raw potatoes into water
2. Keep changing the water until it runs clear, leaving behind white flour
3. Spread this flour on white paper placed on a board and dry it on the stove or in the sun
4. Beat 15 egg yolks, half a kilogram (one pound) of beaten sugar, and a piece of vanilla or lemon zest for 1/4 hour
5. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 15 egg whites
6. Gradually add the dried potato flour, about one tablespoon at a time (half a kilogram or one pound total)
7. Bake in a greased mold coated with grated bread roll
