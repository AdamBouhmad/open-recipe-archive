## Ingredients

- About 5 eggs
- A piece of new butter
- A piece of chives (pažitky)
- 2 liters water
- All kinds of vegetables
- A piece of butter or a piece of paprika-flavored bacon
- Roux (jemnou jíškou)
- Bread (housky)
- Milk
- Bread crumbs
- More chives or parsley

## Directions

1. Mix about 5 eggs, a piece of new butter, and a piece of chives
2. Stir this over the fire until a thick porridge forms
3. Put all kinds of vegetables into 2 liters of water, add a piece of butter or paprika-flavored bacon and brown with fine roux
4. When everything has cooked, strain
5. Cut bread into rounds, soak in milk, dip in egg and bread crumbs, and fry
6. Spread one side with the prepared porridge, cover with another round
7. Pour soup over it, to which chopped chives or parsley has been added
