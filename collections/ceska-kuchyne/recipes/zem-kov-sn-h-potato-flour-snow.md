## Ingredients

- white floury potatoes
- salted water

## Directions

1. Wash and peel white floury potatoes.
2. Cook slowly in salted water.
3. Drain the water and leave the potatoes in the oven until they fall apart.
4. Press through a fine sieve into a lukewarm bowl.
5. Leave fluffy and add to baked goods or sauces.
