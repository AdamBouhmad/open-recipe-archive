## Ingredients

- soft clams in shells
- salt
- pepper
- parsley stems, finely chopped, half a teaspoon
- bacon, six tiny cubes
- fresh butter, thin layer

## Directions

1. Place one large or two smaller clams on each shell
2. Season lightly with salt and pepper
3. Add half a teaspoon of finely chopped parsley stems
4. Add six tiny cubes of bacon
5. Cover with a thin layer of fresh butter
6. Bake in the oven until golden
7. Arrange neatly on a dish on folded cloth
8. Garnish with green parsley or watercress
