## Ingredients

- raisins
- sultanas
- citron peel
- sugar
- rum

## Directions

1. Rinse the raisins and sultanas
2. Chop into small pieces
3. Add finely chopped citron peel and sugar
4. Moisten with rum
5. Heat on a warm griddle
