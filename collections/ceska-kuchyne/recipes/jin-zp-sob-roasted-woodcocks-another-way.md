## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Some do not cook the woodcocks for roasting, others do cook them, leaving only the heart and liver inside the woodcocks.
2. Wrap the salted woodcocks in lard.
3. Roast them in abundant butter to which crushed juniper berries have been added.
