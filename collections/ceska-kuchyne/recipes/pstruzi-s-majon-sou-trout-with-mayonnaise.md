## Ingredients

- large trout
- mayonnaise
- egg
- anchovies
- aspic
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean and cut larger trout into pieces
2. Cook in court-bouillon
3. Remove from skin, remove bones
4. Arrange in mayonnaise on a platter, neatly arranged with the sauce
5. Garnish variously with eggs, anchovies, aspic, or capers
