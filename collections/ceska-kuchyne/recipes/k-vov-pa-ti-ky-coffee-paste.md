## Ingredients

- coffee
- egg white
- sugar

## Directions

1. Brew strong coffee and strain it
2. Add egg white and strain everything
3. Add plenty of cooked sugar and boil again until no bubbles form
4. When everything is thick, pour onto paper or slate and let dry
5. The next day, dampen the paper underneath, carefully remove the pastes
6. Place between fine paper in boxes and store in a dry, cold room
