## Ingredients

- Quail
- Onion, chopped
- Carrot root
- Butter (15 grams per quail)
- Flour
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the quail for roasting
2. Cut them into pieces or leave them whole and place in a pot with chopped onion, carrot root, butter and a little flour and salt
3. Braise until they turn reddish
4. Make a light roux, strain it into the sauce from which the quail have been removed
5. Simmer briefly, return the quail to the sauce
6. Add a piece of butter and adjust seasoning as desired
