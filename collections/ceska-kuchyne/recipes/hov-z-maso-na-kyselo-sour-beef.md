## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram beef brisket
- vegetables as for beef broth
- pepper
- vinegar
- piece of burnt sugar
- roux
- lemon juice
- thyme
- bay leaf
- bezinky or black gingerbread

## Directions

1. Boil beef with all vegetables used for beef broth, pepper, and vinegar
2. Cut cooked meat into neat pieces and strain the broth
3. Add burnt sugar, roux as much as needed, lemon juice, thyme, and bay leaf to the broth
4. If darker color is needed, add bezinky or black gingerbread
5. Strain over meat laid on a platter
