## Ingredients

- 1 small cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- two eggs
- 1 small cup sour milk with half teaspoon baking soda dissolved in water, or 1 small cup sweet milk with 1 teaspoon baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Put into a deep bowl 1 small cup sugar, 1 tablespoon butter and two eggs and rub well until frothed
2. Add 1 small cup sour milk into which you have poured half teaspoon baking soda dissolved in water, or add 1 small cup sweet milk and add 1 teaspoon baking powder to the flour
3. Add enough flour to make the dough about as stiff as for biscuits
4. Roll out to half a finger thickness and cut out rings, either with a special cutter which cuts out both the doughnut and the hole in the middle, or cut out the doughnut with a glass and cut out the middle with a large thimble
5. Put into prepared hot lard and fry on both sides
