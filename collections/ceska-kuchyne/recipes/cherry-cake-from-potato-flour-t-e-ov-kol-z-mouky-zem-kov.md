## Ingredients

- cherries, pitted
- breadcrumbs or ladyfinger crumbs
- 6 egg yolks
- 120 grams (8 lots) sugar
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 6 egg whites
- 90 grams (6 lots) potato flour

## Directions

1. Place pitted cherries on a buttered baking sheet sprinkled with bread crumbs or ladyfinger crumbs
2. Rub egg yolks with sugar
3. Add lemon peel rubbed with sugar and lemon juice
4. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Mix in potato flour
6. Spread over cherries
7. Bake for one hour
