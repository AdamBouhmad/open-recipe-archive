## Ingredients

- Carp
- New butter
- Salt
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the carp, place sticks crosswise on a pan or baking sheet so it doesn't fall to the bottom.
2. Put a piece of new butter in a cup, coat the carp with it, salt it, add lemon cut into quarters.
3. This method prepares the fish most quickly.
4. Can be baked either on stove or in oven, but if baking in oven, take care that it is not undercooked inside.
