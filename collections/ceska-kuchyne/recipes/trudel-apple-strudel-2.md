## Ingredients

- flour
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons warm butter
- 1.75 deciliters (1/4 žejdlík) water
- apples
- 2 rolls
- almonds
- raisins
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar mixed with 7.5 grams (1/2 lot) cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board with a well in the center
2. Mix in a bowl 1 egg, 3 tablespoons warm butter, 1.75 deciliters water and pour into the flour
3. Work the dough for 1/2 hour without adding more flour, so it doesn't stick to the board
4. Cover with a warm pan and let rest for 1 hour
5. Meanwhile, core and slice apples into thin pieces
6. Grate 2 rolls and fry them in butter
7. Scald almonds, peel and chop them
8. Rinse and pick over raisins
9. Dissolve 12 dekagrams sugar mixed with cinnamon and a piece of butter
10. Spread a clean white cloth on the table, sprinkle with flour, place dough in the center
11. Carefully stretch the dough so it doesn't tear
12. Trim the edges, sprinkle dough with butter, then with fried roll crumbs, prepared raisins, almonds, apples and the cinnamon sugar mixture
13. Lift one edge of the cloth, roll up the strudel, carefully place in a greased pan
14. Brush with butter, bake in the oven
15. Slice into pieces and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar
16. Instead of apples, quark cheese may be used
