## Ingredients

- sour cream
- salted and diluted beer
- parsley
- cumin
- butter

## Directions

1. Pour sour cream over live crayfish, which eat it gladly and thus lose their watery taste
2. Cook the crayfish in salted and water-diluted beer, to which parsley and cumin have been added
3. After cooking, pour hot melted butter over the crayfish from time to time before serving at table
