## Ingredients

- cabbage
- garlic
- caraway seeds
- pepper
- beef broth
- rose hip puree

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage, separate each leaf, cut out the stem, pour hot water over it to scald it, drain the water.
2. Rub 3 cloves of garlic, add a little caraway seeds and pepper, pour good beef broth; braise until the cabbage is soft, and add rose hip puree.
