## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or neck
- butter
- parsley
- herbs
- cauliflower
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean lamb shoulder or neck pieces and braise with butter, parsley and herbs.
2. When soft, strain pale roux over it.
3. Cook cleaned cauliflower in separate salted water.
4. Arrange one floret beside another around the meat.
