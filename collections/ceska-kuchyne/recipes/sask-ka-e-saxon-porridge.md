## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter fresh, cold milk
- 6 tablespoons fine flour
- 250 grams butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 100 grams finely ground sugar
- lemon zest from 1 lemon
- salt
- 12 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- wine or fruit sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Mix 6 tablespoons of fine flour into 1/2 liter of cold milk, stirring well so no lumps remain
2. Place on the stove, stir constantly until it boils
3. Pour onto a plate and let cool
4. Meanwhile, cream 250 grams of butter until fluffy, beat in twelve egg yolks, 100 grams of finely ground sugar, grated zest from one lemon, salt everything
5. Pour into the lukewarm porridge, add the stiffly beaten snow from twelve egg whites
6. Grease a mold with butter, fill it, and bake for about an hour with gentle heat
7. Serve the baked porridge with wine or fruit sauce
