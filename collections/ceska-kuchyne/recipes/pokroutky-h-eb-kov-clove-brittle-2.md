## Ingredients

- Dark clove leaves
- Clove spice
- Boiling water
- Clove essence
- Fine refined sugar
- Paper

## Directions

1. Take dark clove leaves and place them with clove spice in a sealed container
2. Pour in boiling water until the leaves are completely submerged
3. Seal the container well and leave it on warm stove for several hours
4. Completely press out all the contents
5. Add clove essence to the liquid
6. Cook this liquid with fine refined sugar until it becomes completely thick and firm
7. Pour it onto paper and when cooled, peel it off and cut into small pieces
