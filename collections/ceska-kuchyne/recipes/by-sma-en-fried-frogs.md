## Ingredients

- frog legs
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- grated roll
- butter
- parsley, green and fried

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from frog legs, wash and dry them.
2. Salt them, roll in flour, egg and grated roll.
3. Fry on butter.
4. Place on a serving dish and garnish with green fried parsley.
