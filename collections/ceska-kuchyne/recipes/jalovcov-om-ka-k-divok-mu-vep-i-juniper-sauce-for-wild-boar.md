## Ingredients

- one hundred twenty grams of butter browned to redness
- rye bread, grated
- salt
- crushed juniper berries
- pepper
- sugar
- lemon peel, grated
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Take one hundred twenty grams of butter browned to redness
2. Add grated rye bread and salt
3. Add some crushed juniper berries, pepper, sugar with grated lemon peel, and vinegar
4. Boil everything with broth
