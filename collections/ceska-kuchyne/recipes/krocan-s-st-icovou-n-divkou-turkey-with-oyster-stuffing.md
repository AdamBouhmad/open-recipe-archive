## Ingredients

- turkey
- bread
- eggs
- butter
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- small onion
- two dozen fresh large oysters

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey and stuffing according to the previous method, but instead of sage add a small onion browned in butter
2. Mix two dozen beautiful fresh large oysters into the stuffing
3. Fill and roast according to the previous method
