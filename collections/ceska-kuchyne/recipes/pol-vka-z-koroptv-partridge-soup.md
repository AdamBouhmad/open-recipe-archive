## Ingredients

- tougher partridges
- partridge breast
- partridge meat
- partridge bones
- toasted bread roll

## Directions

1. Roast the tougher partridges
2. Separate the breasts; chop and pound the remaining meat in a mortar
3. Cook the bones, strain, and cook again with toasted bread roll and pounded partridge meat
4. Strain the broth over separated breasts and toasted bread roll
