## Ingredients

- several cooked potatoes
- several egg yolks
- cheese, grated
- salt
- pepper
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate cooked potatoes
2. Mix with egg yolks, grated cheese, salt, and pepper
3. Pour into buttered form
4. Bake for about quarter hour
5. Cover with stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Bake until surface browns
