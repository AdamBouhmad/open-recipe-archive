## Ingredients

- crayfish
- onion
- parsley root
- celery
- leek
- carrot
- cabbage
- potato
- 35 grams butter
- salt
- crayfish tails and claws
- 25 grams butter
- pale gravy
- bread roll
- chives or fried parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the crayfish, remove head and feet, and cut into small pieces; cook salted
2. Chop onion, parsley root, celery, leek, carrot, cabbage, and raw potato
3. Mix with 35 grams butter and braise; add to the crayfish when cooking
4. When crayfish is tender, strain the broth
5. Boil the tails and claws; shell them and add to the strained broth whole or chopped
6. Pound the shells in a mortar, push through a fine sieve, mix with 25 grams butter, and add to the broth with pale gravy
7. Cut bread into noodles and pour the broth over it; garnish with chopped chives or fried parsley
