## Ingredients

- Two pounds of cooked tripe
- Two cups of water
- Half cup of vinegar
- One onion
- A few grains of new spice
- Four cloves
- A clove of garlic
- Salt
- Two tablespoons of butter
- One tablespoon of sugar
- One heaping tablespoon of flour
- Pepper
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked tripe into pieces and place in a pot
2. Pour over it two cups of water and half cup of vinegar
3. Add one onion finely chopped, a few grains of new spice, four cloves, and a clove of garlic rubbed with salt
4. Let simmer
5. Make a roux from two tablespoons of butter; when it melts, add one tablespoon of sugar and one heaping tablespoon of flour and let it brown well
6. Pour the roux into the tripe, add a pinch of pepper and a pinch of marjoram
7. Let it simmer together and pour onto a platter with the gravy
