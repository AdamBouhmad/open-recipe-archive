## Ingredients

- turtle
- ham slices
- butter
- rice
- flour
- onion
- celery stalks
- green parsley
- bay leaf
- thyme
- sage
- cloves
- allspice
- paprika
- water

## Directions

1. The turtle must be killed and hung overnight to bleed out
2. To make the turtle extend its head, place heated iron on its back
3. When it extends its head, cut off both the head and extremities
4. Hang the turtle and scald the legs so the black skin can be removed
5. Place in a cold location
6. Separate the shell pieces carefully so the gall bladder does not spill
7. Put the eggs, legs and meat into cold water
8. Cook everything in twice the amount of water as you want soup to be and cook for two hours, skimming carefully
9. Add several slices of ham about a quarter pound of butter and cook further for two hours
10. Add one wine glass of rice, one wine glass of flour mixed with a generous tablespoon of butter, one finely chopped onion, two celery stalks, green parsley, bay leaf, thyme and sage, half a teaspoon of cloves and allspice and a pinch of paprika
11. Cook for another hour
