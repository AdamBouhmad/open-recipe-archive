## Ingredients

- Mature green cucumbers
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sieve
- Onion
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Grape leaves
- Cherry leaves
- Dill greens
- Mustard seeds

## Directions

1. Peel the mature green cucumbers and salt them heavily
2. Pour the strongly boiled hot vinegar over the cucumbers, straining through a sieve, and allow both cucumbers and vinegar to cool on the sieve
3. Layer the cold cucumbers in glass jars, interspersing with onion, garlic, pepper, grape leaves, cherry leaves and dill greens, with mustard seeds in small bags on top
4. Cover everything with cold vinegar and seal the jars
