## Ingredients

- very cold raw cream
- sugar
- spices or liqueur

## Directions

1. Take very cold raw cream and whip it until completely fluffy
2. Sweeten and flavor with spices or a pleasant liqueur
3. Optionally add a small amount of fish bladder to thicken
4. Mix with chopped almonds, ground chocolate, etc.
5. Use excellently for filling various pastries
