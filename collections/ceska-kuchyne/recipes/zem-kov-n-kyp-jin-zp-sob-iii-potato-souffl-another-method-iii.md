## Ingredients

- 195 grams (13 lots) butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 39 dekagrams (26 lots) cooked grated potatoes
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) candied lemon peel, chopped
- 6 dekagrams raisins
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- 12 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 195 grams (13 lots) of butter and mix with 12 egg yolks and 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar
2. Add 39 dekagrams (26 lots) of cooked grated potatoes, 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of chopped candied lemon peel, 6 dekagrams of raisins, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
3. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 12 egg whites
4. Bake in a greased mold coated with grated bread roll
