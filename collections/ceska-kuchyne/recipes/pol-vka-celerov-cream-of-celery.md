## Ingredients

- several celery stalks
- salted water
- four ounces of butter
- pepper
- four ounces of pale roux
- two quarts of beef broth
- four egg yolks
- a spoonful of sour cream
- two ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare several celery stalks, let them boil five minutes in salted water, immerse in cold water and when cooled remove and let drain
2. Chop them finely, put in a saucepan with four ounces of butter, salt, pepper and let stew until soft
3. Press through a strainer, thicken with four ounces of pale roux, add two quarts of beef broth, mix well, let boil fifteen minutes and then press through a fine sieve
4. Put back in a pot on the stove and stir frequently, it must be very hot but must no longer boil
5. Before the soup is served, thicken with a liaison of four egg yolks, a spoonful of sour cream and two ounces of butter and pour into a bowl over sliced bread fried pale in butter
