## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- salt
- white flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs or crushed crackers
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's feet until tender
2. Dry them
3. Cut into four parts
4. Remove the larger bones
5. Salt the pieces
6. Coat in white flour
7. Dip in beaten egg
8. Coat in breadcrumbs or crushed crackers
9. Fry in clarified butter until golden
