## Ingredients

- trout
- vinegar
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- white wine
- spices for carp

## Directions

1. Pour boiled salted vinegar over sliced trout so they turn a nice blue and then arrange them in a kettle, add to them all the spices as for carp, pour over them one part fresh water, one part vinegar and one part white wine, only enough so that the trout are covered, salt them and let them boil for a quarter hour.
2. Then remove the fish onto a dish, strain the sauce over them and let them cool.
