## Ingredients

- Beautiful beef tongue
- Salted water
- Butter pastry dough
- Butter
- Wine
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in salted water until done, skin it
2. When cooled, cut into small slices
3. Prepare pastry from butter dough
4. Place butter and similar items on the bottom as mentioned for pork tongue pastry
5. Prepare sauce from wine, butter, sugar and cinnamon
6. Cook the sauce, strain it
7. When the pastry is baked, pour the sauce over it
