## Ingredients

- young partridges
- melted butter
- grated bread roll
- egg, beaten
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the young partridges and halve them.
2. Dip in melted butter, roll in grated bread roll.
3. Dip in beaten egg and roll in bread roll again.
4. Quickly fry in hot butter until golden.
5. Serve with fried parsley as garnish or with vegetables on the table.
