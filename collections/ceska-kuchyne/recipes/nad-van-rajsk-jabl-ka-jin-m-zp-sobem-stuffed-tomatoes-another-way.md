## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Meat or poultry leftovers
- Onion
- Grated bread
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare the tomatoes in the previous manner, chop finely any leftover meat or poultry, chop onion very finely and fry until golden, add the chopped meat to it, add two spoonfuls of grated bread, salt and pepper and let it stew for about a minute.
2. Then add an egg yolk, mix well, fill the tomatoes with this and place them in a deep dish, sprinkle with grated bread, place pieces of butter and let bake.
