## Ingredients

- marinated wild pigeon
- red roux
- marinade from the pigeon
- burnt sugar
- caramelized potato

## Directions

1. Remove the marinated pigeon from its marinade.
2. Make a red roux and add it to the marinade sauce.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Add a piece of burnt sugar.
5. Strain everything.
6. Cut the pigeon into quarters and place in the sauce.
7. Let simmer on the coals briefly.
8. Serve surrounded by caramelized potatoes.
