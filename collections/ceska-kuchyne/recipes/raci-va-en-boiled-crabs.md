## Ingredients

- crabs, cleaned
- caraway seeds
- pepper, ground
- salt
- water
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the crabs thoroughly
2. Place in a pot and add caraway seeds and a little ground pepper
3. Pour boiling salted water over them, enough to cover
4. Cover tightly with a lid
5. Let boil for about a quarter hour
6. Drain the water
7. Arrange the crabs neatly on a dish
8. Garnish with green parsley
9. Serve
