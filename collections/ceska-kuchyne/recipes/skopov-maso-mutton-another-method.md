## Ingredients

- mutton breast
- vegetables
- salt
- raw potatoes
- pepper
- garlic
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook mutton breast with all vegetables.
2. When half-cooked, add raw potatoes cut into slices and continue cooking until everything is tender.
3. Cut the meat into neat pieces and make a sauce.
4. Strain the broth in which the potatoes were cooked, add pepper, crushed garlic, and a little marjoram.
5. Mix well and simmer for about a quarter hour.
6. Strain the potatoes and sauce onto the meat.
