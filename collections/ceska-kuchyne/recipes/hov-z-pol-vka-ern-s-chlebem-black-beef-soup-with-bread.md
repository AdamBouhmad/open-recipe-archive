## Ingredients

- 1 kilo of meat without fat
- vegetables
- beef fat scraps
- a piece of beef heart
- various vegetables
- sugar
- goose fat
- bread
- 3 whole eggs or 4 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the meat with vegetables; stew the beef fat scraps, a piece of beef heart and various vegetables, moisten with soup
2. When stewed, brown a piece of sugar and strain everything into the soup
3. Fry bread cut into cubes in goose fat, beat 3 whole eggs or cut 4 hard-boiled eggs into pieces and add to the soup bowl
