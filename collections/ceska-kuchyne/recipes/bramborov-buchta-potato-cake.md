## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- eight yolks
- grated lemon zest
- salt
- 1 small cup sugar
- three grated cooked potatoes of medium size
- stiff snow from eight whites

## Directions

1. Beat 4 tablespoons butter until frothed
2. Add to it eight yolks, grated lemon zest, a little salt, 1 small cup sugar, three grated cooked potatoes of medium size and stiff snow from eight whites
3. Bake it at moderate heat for about an hour
