## Ingredients

- carrot
- parsnip
- celery
- leek
- fresh butter
- green parsley
- peppercorns
- salt
- water
- perch

## Directions

1. Cut a well-cleaned carrot, parsnip, celery and leek into small strips, place in a pot with a piece of fresh butter, add green parsley, several peppercorns, salt it and let it fry for a while.
2. Then pour a quart of water over it and let it cook for a while.
3. Meanwhile scale and gut the perch, salt them and place in a kettle and pour the cooked strained vegetables over them, cover well and let them stew until tender.
4. Place them in a deep dish and pour the sauce with vegetables over them.
