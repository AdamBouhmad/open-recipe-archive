## Ingredients

- half kilogram potatoes
- half kilogram truffles
- wine
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- wine vinegar
- cooked carrots
- beetroot

## Directions

1. Cook and slice half a kilogram of potatoes
2. Cook half a kilogram of truffles in wine and slice them
3. Mix with oil, salt, pepper, and wine vinegar
4. Arrange on a platter
5. Decorate with cooked carrots and beetroot cut into shapes
