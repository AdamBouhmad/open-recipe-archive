## Ingredients

- veal leg cutlets
- bacon
- onion
- green parsley
- lemon zest
- butter
- wine
- beef broth
- fresh mushrooms
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut pieces from the veal leg, one finger thick and three fingers wide
2. Beat them slightly in width
3. Salt both sides properly
4. Place several slices of good bacon on a pan
5. Lay the veal cutlets on them
6. Cut half an onion into slices and place around the cutlets
7. Sprinkle with green parsley and some chopped lemon zest
8. Pour a little melted butter over it
9. Cover and let braise until tender, occasionally adding a little wine and beef broth
10. Clean beautiful fresh mushrooms, take a small plateful
11. Cut the roots slightly, rinse them in clean water and cut into thin slices
12. Place three spoonfuls of butter on a pan, add the mushrooms to it
13. Strain the juice of one lemon over them
