## Ingredients

- fine white sugar
- water
- peppermint oil

## Directions

1. Pound fine white sugar
2. Dissolve it in water and cook it, stirring constantly, until it thickens completely
3. Add a few drops of peppermint oil
4. Mix everything again and let it thicken further
5. Pour onto paper and when hardened, carefully peel off and cut into small pieces
