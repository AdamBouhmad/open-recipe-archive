## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 2 small cups semolina
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- hot melted butter

## Directions

1. Pour a quart of milk into a pot and bring it to a boil
2. Gradually sprinkle in two small cups of fine semolina while stirring constantly
3. Move the pot to the side where it won't boil vigorously and let it cook for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring frequently
4. Salt it, stir, and turn out onto a dish
5. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon and dress with hot melted butter
