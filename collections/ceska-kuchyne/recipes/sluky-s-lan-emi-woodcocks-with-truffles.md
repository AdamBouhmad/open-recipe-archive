## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- truffles
- salt
- butter
- brown coulisse
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcocks, remove innards, clean and cut them.
2. Salt and stuff with finely chopped truffles.
3. Place on a baking pan in boiling butter and braise about a quarter hour.
4. Pour a glass of butter over and roast again for about a quarter hour, turning on all sides.
5. Finally add about a quarter liter of brown coulisse.
6. Simmer the sauce, make it more flavorful with lemon juice, strain and pour over the woodcocks.
