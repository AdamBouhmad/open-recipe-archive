## Ingredients

- chopped onion
- flour
- reserved duck stock from the previous day
- broth or water
- several tablespoons of vinegar
- glass of red wine
- sour cucumber, sliced into strips
- bay leaf
- several peppercorns
- new spice
- duck leftovers

## Directions

1. Sauté the chopped onion until translucent, add a little flour
2. Add the reserved duck stock from the previous day, broth or water, several tablespoons of vinegar, a glass of red wine
3. Add the sour cucumber sliced into strips, bay leaf, several peppercorns, and new spice
4. Boil everything for a while
5. Warm the duck leftovers in the sauce
