## Ingredients

- twenty well-cleaned frog legs, chopped fine
- one small onion, finely chopped
- a piece of green parsley
- a little crushed thyme
- a piece of butter
- salt
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- two quarts lean broth or parsley water
- a little pale butter roux
- crushed thyme
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Chop twenty well-cleaned frog legs finely, place them in a pan, add a small finely chopped onion, a piece of green parsley, a little crushed thyme and a piece of butter, salt it and let it stew for about a quarter hour
2. Pound it then in a mortar with four hard-boiled egg yolks, put it in a pot, add two quarts of lean broth or parsley water and let it boil for a quarter hour
3. Strain it, brown it with a little pale butter roux, let it boil a little longer, add a little more crushed thyme and pour into a dish over fried bread. You can also add fish dumplings to it
