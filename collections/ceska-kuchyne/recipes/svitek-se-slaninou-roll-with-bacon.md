## Ingredients

- regular roll dough
- chopped onion or chives
- bacon, cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Add chopped onion or chives to the regular roll dough
2. Heat bacon cut into cubes on a pan
3. Pour a tablespoon of dough on it and repeat until all dough is used
4. In this case, do not add sugar to the dough
