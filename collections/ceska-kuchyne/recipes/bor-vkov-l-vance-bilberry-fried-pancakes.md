## Ingredients

- half liter milk
- 6 eggs
- salt
- flour
- half liter bilberries (blueberries)
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix 6 eggs into half a liter of milk with a little salt and enough flour to make a thick batter.
2. Pour half the batter onto a greased pan, and when it begins to firm, sprinkle half a liter of cleaned bilberries, which should be sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon and covered with the remaining batter.
3. Fry on both sides and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
