## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 deciliters (žejdlík) poppy seeds
- 7 deciliters (2 žejdlíky) milk
- sugar equal to the milk amount
- 2 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Pass the poppy seeds through a sieve, crush them, and place on the stove
2. Add milk and sugar in the proportions stated
3. Stir for a long time until the filling is sufficiently thick
4. Add egg yolks if desired
