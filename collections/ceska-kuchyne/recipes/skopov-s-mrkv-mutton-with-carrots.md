## Ingredients

- salted mutton ribs with all vegetables
- 6 to 8 carrots
- butter
- pepper
- sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook salted mutton ribs with all vegetables until tender.
2. Meanwhile, peel 6 to 8 carrots and cut them into strips.
3. Place them on a shallow pan with a piece of butter, pepper, add a spoonful of sugar and let them braise with frequent basting with the broth in which the meat is cooking until tender.
4. Then make a light roux from a spoonful of butter and a heaping spoonful of flour to thicken sufficiently.
5. Add the cooked ribs cut into neat pieces and a piece of caramelized sugar.
6. Mix everything well and let it simmer once more.
7. Mutton with cabbage is prepared the same way, with cabbage cut into quarters; one can also use half cabbage and half carrots.
