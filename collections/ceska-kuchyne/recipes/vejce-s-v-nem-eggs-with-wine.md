## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with wine and sugar, beating continuously until the wine begins to rise.
2. Alternatively, boil half a liter of wine with half the amount of water and 6 decagrams of sugar.
3. Whisk 2 egg yolks in water and slowly add the wine while continuously stirring.
4. Pour the wine into glasses and serve as a pleasant beverage.
