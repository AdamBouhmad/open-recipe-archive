## Ingredients

- frog legs
- butter
- onion, chopped
- salt
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the frog legs, place on a baking sheet, salt them, add butter and chopped onion.
2. Braise the frogs.
3. Place the frogs on a serving dish.
4. Squeeze the juice from one lemon into the sauce.
5. Add the frogs to the sauce and serve shortly after.
