## Ingredients

- half a liter of beef broth
- vinegar
- roux made from butter
- dill, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Simmer half a liter of beef broth with vinegar
2. Add roux made from butter
3. Strain
4. Add finely chopped dill and simmer for a while
