## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) rice
- warm water
- milk
- 8-10 egg yolks
- butter
- lemon zest
- sugar
- salt
- 8-10 egg whites
- breadcrumbs
- jam

## Directions

1. Rinse 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) of rice, soak it in warm water, make soft porridge with milk, add 8-10 egg yolks rubbed with butter, a piece of lemon zest, sugar, snow from 8-10 egg whites, a little salt and mix everything
2. Pour into a greased form sprinkled with breadcrumbs, spread a layer of rice porridge, on it place several spoonfuls of jam as garnish, then another layer of rice porridge
3. Continue this way until the form is two fingers full; at the top there should be a layer of rice, place pieces of butter on it and bake
4. When served on the table, you can also separately add the jam that is in the rice
5. Rice pudding can be prepared in exactly the same way as millet pudding
