## Ingredients

- about 1 kilo of beef
- potatoes
- 3 cloves of garlic
- pepper
- marjoram
- fresh mushrooms
- pale roux

## Directions

1. Cook about 1 kilo of beef, strain the soup; peel potatoes, cut them into pieces and cook in water until half done, drain the water
2. Place the potatoes in beef broth; add 3 cloves of ground garlic, a little pepper, marjoram and several finely chopped mushrooms
3. Make a pale roux, mix it in and let the soup boil for a while longer
