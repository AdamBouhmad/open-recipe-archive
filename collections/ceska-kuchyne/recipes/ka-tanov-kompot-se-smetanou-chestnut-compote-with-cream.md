## Ingredients

- baked chestnuts
- egg yolks
- sweet cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the baked chestnuts and cut into small cakes
2. Mix egg yolks with sweet cream and slowly pour into sugar cooked to the eighth degree
3. Allow to cook together with constant stirring
4. Strain onto beautifully arranged cooked chestnuts on a dish
