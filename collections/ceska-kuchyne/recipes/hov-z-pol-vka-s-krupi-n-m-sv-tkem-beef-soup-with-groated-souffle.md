## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- fresh butter
- milk
- herbs
- flour grits
- salt
- 4 egg whites
- roll crumbs

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks, butter, milk, herbs, flour grits and salt
2. Make stiff snow from egg whites and mix in
3. Spread the mixture on a greased and bread-crumbed baking sheet, bake until golden
4. Cut into cubes and add to beef soup before serving
