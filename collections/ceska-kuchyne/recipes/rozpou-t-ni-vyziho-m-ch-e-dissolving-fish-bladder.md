## Ingredients

- good fish bladder
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Good fish bladder, made from bladder, must be clean, smooth, white and transparent
2. Put the fish bladder in clean paper and crush with a wooden mallet as much as needed for aspic
3. For two dekagrams of crushed fish bladder, pour about two deciliters of fresh water
4. Let it sit several hours on warm stove
5. Place the container in boiling water and slowly bring to a boil until the fish bladder is completely dissolved
6. Skim foam carefully during cooking, stir constantly
7. Strain through cloth
8. The liquid is fine, transparent and whitish
