## Ingredients

- several potatoes
- salt
- 90 grams (6 lots) butter
- lemon peel, grated
- 120 grams (8 lots) sugar
- cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- bread roll, grated

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in salted water
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Mix over heat with butter, lemon peel, sugar, and cream
4. Remove from heat
5. Add egg yolks
6. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Pour into buttered form sprinkled with grated bread
8. Bake for half an hour, preferably in hot ashes surrounded by coals
