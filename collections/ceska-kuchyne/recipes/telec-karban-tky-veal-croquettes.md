## Ingredients

- approximately 2 pounds veal meat from the leg
- 2 heaping tablespoons butter, or 2 tablespoons marrow
- 2 eggs
- 2 rolls, or 4 slices bread
- rind of half a lemon, grated
- salt
- pepper
- pinch of flowers (spice)
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the veal meat finely
2. Rub two heaping tablespoons of butter or two tablespoons of marrow and beat in two eggs, then rub again
3. Add to the meat
4. Grate two rolls or 4 slices of bread and the rind of half a lemon
5. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of flowers
6. Form the meat into small patties the size of a palm
7. Sprinkle with grated bread
8. Fry in butter until golden
9. Serve with lemon cut into quarters
