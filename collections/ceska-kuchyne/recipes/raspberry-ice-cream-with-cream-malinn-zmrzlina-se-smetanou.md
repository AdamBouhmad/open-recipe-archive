## Ingredients

- Two eggs
- One and a half liters of sour cream
- Two and a half liters of raspberry juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs
2. Beat them well
3. Pour in one and a half liters of sour cream
4. Bring to a boil while constantly stirring
5. When the juice has cooled, add it to the remaining two and a half liters of raspberry juice
6. Sweeten generously
7. Transform into ice cream
