## Ingredients

- several potatoes
- onion
- chopped parsley
- butter
- bay leaf
- hot water or broth
- half a liter of boiling milk
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Slowly cook several potatoes, drain the water and stew them; meanwhile, stew onion and chopped parsley in butter
2. Press the potatoes through a sieve and add them to the onions, mix well, add one bay leaf and pour in the necessary amount of hot water or broth to make a thin porridge
3. Add half a liter of boiling milk, season with pepper, salt and nutmeg; let the soup boil for a while and pour over fried bread roll
