## Ingredients

- vanilla bean
- water
- half liter white wine
- potato flour
- sugar
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Finely chop the vanilla and dissolve it in a little water
2. Let it stand for at least an hour on a warm place, well covered
3. Add half a liter of white wine
4. Add potato flour and sugar to taste
5. Add four beaten egg yolks, mix everything well
6. Heat slowly and preserve it, stirring constantly
