## Ingredients

- 250 grams fine sifted flour
- 125 grams finely ground sugar
- 2 to 3 eggs
- salt
- 8 grams finely ground cinnamon
- 50 grams melted butter
- 1/2 liter lukewarm cream

## Directions

1. Mix 250 grams of fine sifted flour with 125 grams of finely ground sugar
2. Beat in 2 to 3 eggs, season with salt
3. Add 8 grams of finely ground cinnamon and 50 grams of melted butter
4. Knead with 1/2 liter of lukewarm cream into a firm dough
5. Butter the metal molds (tube pans), fill with dough
6. Bake slowly on both sides until golden
7. Remove quickly and wind onto round wooden forms and let cool
