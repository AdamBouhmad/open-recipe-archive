## Ingredients

- Well-ripened, sweet apples
- Sugar - 250g per kilogram of apples
- Cinnamon - a little

## Directions

1. Slice well-ripened, sweet apples into quarters
2. Peel and remove seeds
3. Place on a baking pan
4. Add sugar and a little cinnamon
5. Stew until completely soft
6. Press through a sieve
7. If not sweet enough, add more sugar and stew until thickened
8. Place in containers
9. Cover with bladder or parchment paper so air cannot enter
