## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water
- 250 grams chocolate
- 100 grams sugar
- some vanilla
- 1 bottle wine
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil water with chocolate, sugar, and vanilla
2. Let foam and simmer for at least fifteen minutes
3. Pour in a bottle of wine and boil again
4. Before serving, stir in four beaten egg yolks
