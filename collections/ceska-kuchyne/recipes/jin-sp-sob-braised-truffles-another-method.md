## Ingredients

- truffles, washed
- bacon
- bay leaf
- parsley
- thyme
- pepper
- clove
- beef broth with wine or wine alone

## Directions

1. Clean truffles with a brush and peel
2. Place in a baking dish with bacon, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, pepper, and clove on the bottom
3. Pour either strong beef broth with wine or wine alone over them
4. Cover with a lid
5. Braise for about fifteen minutes
6. Strain everything
7. Remove truffles to a plate
8. Pour over sauce that has been boiled again and is free of fat
