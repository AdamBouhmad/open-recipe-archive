## Ingredients

- small white onions
- salt
- water
- sweet milk
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Peel small white onions and salt them slightly.
2. Boil them for ten minutes in equal parts water and sweet milk.
3. Remove and let them drain.
4. Pack them into jars and pour spiced hot vinegar over them, but do not add any new spices that would darken them.
5. Screw shut while still hot.
