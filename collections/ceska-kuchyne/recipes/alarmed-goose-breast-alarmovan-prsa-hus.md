## Ingredients

- goose breasts from two or three geese
- salt
- cloves
- peppercorns
- new spice
- vinegar
- bacon
- potatoes
- sweet or sour cream

## Directions

1. From two or three geese, remove the breasts, pull off the skin, salt them and place in a pot with several cloves, peppercorns, new spice and pour over with vinegar. After the breasts have been marinating for several days, clean them, lard them with bacon, place on a baking sheet with the larded side up and roast until red. Slice the breasts diagonally with a sharp knife into strips, reform them in their original shape, add fried potatoes. Pour sweet or sour cream into the sauce and serve separately in a dish.
