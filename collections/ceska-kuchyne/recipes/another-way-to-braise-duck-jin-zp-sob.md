## Ingredients

- duck
- water
- beef broth
- white wine
- bay leaves
- cloves
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- onion
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise the prepared duck with water and broth, a glass of white wine, 2 bay leaves, cloves, salt and parsley. To the sauce add onion fried in butter and dusted with flour, and lemon juice, then pour over the duck.
