## Ingredients

- cranberries
- sugar (1 kilogram per 8 liters of cranberries)
- cinnamon
- apples (20 of better quality)

## Directions

1. Sort and clean the cranberries, blanch them, drain the water
2. For 8 liters of cranberries, add 1 kilogram of sugar and several pieces of cinnamon
3. Boil, remove the foam, and when individual berries begin to burst, remove them and cover with half the juice
4. Prepare 20 better quality apples
