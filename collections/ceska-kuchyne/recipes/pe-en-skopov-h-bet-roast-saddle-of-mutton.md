## Ingredients

- a nice saddle from a young mutton
- sour cream or vinegar
- lard for larding
- salt
- pepper
- water
- butter
- paper for covering

## Directions

1. Let a nice saddle from a young mutton lie for three days in sour cream or vinegar and turn it frequently.
2. Remove all the skin and fat from it.
3. Lard it well with lard.
4. Place it on a baking pan, salt it, pour some water and the liquid in which it was marinated over it.
5. Add plenty of butter and cover with greased paper so that it does not brown too quickly.
6. Let it roast for three hours with frequent basting with the roasted juices.
7. Then place it on a platter and serve with dumplings or boiled potatoes.
