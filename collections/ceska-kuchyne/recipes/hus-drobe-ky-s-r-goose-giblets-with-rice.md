## Ingredients

- Goose giblets
- Celery, small piece
- Parsley root
- Salt
- Three tablespoons fresh butter
- One cup sifted and rinsed rice
- Beef broth
- Flour for roux
- Nutmeg
- Smoked meat, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Boil the goose giblets.
2. When they float, add a piece of celery and parsley root, salt them, and cook until tender.
3. On a stove put three tablespoons of fresh butter and one cup of sifted rice that has been rinsed several times in hot water.
4. Pour a few tablespoons of beef broth over it, salt lightly, and let the rice stew slowly on low heat until tender.
5. Watch carefully so it does not stick to the bottom and add more beef broth as needed.
6. Make a light roux and thin it with a little beef broth, season with nutmeg, salt, and let it cook into a smooth sauce.
7. Finally arrange the cooked goose giblets on a serving dish, pour the sauce over them, make a wreath of rice around them, and place diagonal strips of finely chopped smoked meat on top.
8. Instead of roux, you can also use drippings from the roast or fry a little caraway seed in butter or goose fat, to which add a little chopped fresh parsley.
9. Do not forget to add a little nutmeg and a small amount of saffron to the rice.
10. The giblets include wings, legs, neck, liver, and stomach. Well-cleaned intestines, when turned inside out, can also be used and serve as a good delicacy.
