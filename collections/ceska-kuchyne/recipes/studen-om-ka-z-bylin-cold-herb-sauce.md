## Ingredients

- 4 cold egg yolks
- fine oil
- salt
- pepper
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 4 finely chopped onions
- chives
- parsley
- garlic

## Directions

1. Beat cold egg yolks with fine oil
2. Add salt, pepper, vinegar, finely chopped onions
3. Add chives, parsley, and garlic
4. Mix everything well and serve with fish, cold roasts, etc.
