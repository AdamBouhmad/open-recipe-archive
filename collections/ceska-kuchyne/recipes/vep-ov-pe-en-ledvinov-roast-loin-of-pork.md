## Ingredients

- A nice piece of loin near the kidney
- Salt
- Caraway seeds
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean a nice piece of loin, salt it, sprinkle with a little caraway, place on a baking sheet skin side down, pour a little water over it and bake on that side until the skin is tender
2. Then turn the meat, cut the skin into squares with a sharp knife, sprinkle lightly with salt and bake at high heat until nicely golden
3. Pork leg can be cooked the same way
4. If the meat is very fatty, remove the skin and all the lard
5. Bread dumplings with cabbage are best served with this roast
