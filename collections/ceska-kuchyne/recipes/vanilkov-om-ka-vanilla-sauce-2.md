## Ingredients

- about 15 dekagrams sugar
- 3 egg whites
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar to the fourth degree
2. While constantly stirring, pour gradually into stiff snow from 3 eggs, to which vanilla has been beaten and added
