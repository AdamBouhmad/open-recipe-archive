## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk (for starter)
- 3 tablespoons flour (for starter)
- compressed yeast or dried yeast
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- salt
- flour (enough to make stiff dough like bread)

## Directions

1. In the morning make a starter from ½ cup milk, 3 tablespoons flour and a piece of compressed yeast, or from dried yeast overnight
2. When the starter has risen, beat 1 tablespoon butter and 2 eggs on a plate
3. Add the risen starter and 2 cups milk to it, salt and add enough flour to make dough as stiff as for bread and mix well in the bowl
4. Roll out on a rolling pin until the dough is nice and smooth and put it back in the bowl to rise
5. When it has risen so it is twice as much as before, put on rolling pin and cut dumplings the size of an apple, round them in your hands and let them rise again on the rolling pin
6. When they have risen again, put them in boiling water, cover well and let boil for half an hour
7. Take them out, tear each one with a fork and pour melted butter over them
