## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of apricot or peach preserve
- 1 cupful of white wine
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- rind from half a lemon

## Directions

1. Put 1 cupful of apricot or peach preserve into a small pot
2. Slowly pour 1 cupful of white wine over it
3. Add a piece of whole cinnamon and rind from half a lemon
4. Place over heat and stir constantly
5. When it begins to boil, remove from heat
6. Strain through a fine sieve, sweeten to taste, cover with a lid and let stand in a warm place
