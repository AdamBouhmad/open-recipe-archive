## Ingredients

- handful of morels
- parsley
- pale roux
- beef broth
- six raw egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash a handful of morels and cut them
2. Place on a pan and braise with chopped parsley
3. Mix with pale roux and press through a sieve into beef broth
4. Cook for a short time
5. Mix in six raw egg yolks and stir the sauce until it thickens
6. Let it cool
