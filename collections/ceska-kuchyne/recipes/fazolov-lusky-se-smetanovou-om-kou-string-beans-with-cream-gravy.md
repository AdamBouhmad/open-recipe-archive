## Ingredients

- String beans
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 spoonful flour
- 2 spoonfuls fresh dill

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in the previous manner and when cooked, add to them one cup of sour cream into which you have mixed one spoonful of flour, add about two spoonfuls of finely chopped dill, let it simmer and serve.
