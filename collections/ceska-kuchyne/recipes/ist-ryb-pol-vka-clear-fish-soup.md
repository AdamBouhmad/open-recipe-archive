## Ingredients

- two ounces butter
- onion
- parsley
- carrot
- a piece of celery
- two quarts water
- salt
- a handful of dried mushrooms
- about two pounds of fish (heads and various scraps)
- two egg whites (for clarifying if needed)

## Directions

1. Let onion, parsley, carrot and a piece of celery stew in two ounces of butter
2. add two quarts of water, salt and a handful of dried mushrooms
3. let it boil until soft
4. then add about two pounds of fish, namely heads and all kinds of scraps from them
5. let it boil for one hour and strain the soup through a cloth
6. if it is cloudy, clarify it with snow from two egg whites
