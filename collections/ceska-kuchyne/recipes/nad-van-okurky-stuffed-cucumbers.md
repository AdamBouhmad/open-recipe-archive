## Ingredients

- medium-sized cucumbers
- veal
- pork
- rendered lard
- raw eggs (2-3)
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- flower (garnish)
- nutmeg
- bread (1-2 pieces soaked in milk)
- beef broth
- good wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut cucumbers lengthwise and hollow them out
2. Finely mince equal quantities of veal and pork, add a piece of rendered lard, 2-3 raw eggs, a little salt, pepper, ginger, flower, a little nutmeg for flavor, and 1-2 pieces of bread soaked in milk and squeezed out, mix everything together
3. Fill the cucumber halves with this mixture and close them
4. Place on a baking sheet, pour beef broth and good wine vinegar over them, and either bake in the oven or cook in the oven
5. Serve the prepared cucumbers after the soup course
