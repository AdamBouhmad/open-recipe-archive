## Ingredients

- Liver, half kilogram
- Three eggs
- Two garlic cloves
- Marjoram
- Salt
- Flour
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Grate half kilogram of liver, add 3 eggs, 2 garlic cloves, a little grated marjoram, salt, flour and bread crumbs until a batter like pancake batter forms.
2. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs, place the mixture in it and bake; cut into cubes, place in a soup bowl and pour beef broth over it.
