## Ingredients

- lentils
- marjoram
- garlic
- 2 litres water
- meat broth
- fried roll or bread

## Directions

1. Boil lentils until completely soft
2. Add marjoram and garlic to them
3. Pour in 2 litres of water, add meat broth
4. Boil for a quarter hour, strain the soup and pour over fried roll or bread
