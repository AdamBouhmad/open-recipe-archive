## Ingredients

- firm pears
- 1 quart of white sugar
- about 2 tablespoons of water

## Directions

1. For preserving, take firmer pears that will not fall apart.
2. Peel them, cut in half or quarters depending on size, cut out the cores and place the pears in cold water so they do not brown.
3. Put 1 quart of white sugar and about 2 tablespoons of water into a glazed pot and when it boils, add 2 quarts of pears to the syrup and let it boil slowly for several hours.
4. When the syrup has thickened, place into jars according to the instructions in the general rules.
