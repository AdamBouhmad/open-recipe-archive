## Ingredients

- onion
- milk
- 1 cupful grated white bread
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the onion finely and cook it in milk
2. Take a cupful of grated white bread and when the onion is cooked, strain it onto the bread and let it stand for half an hour
3. Put into a pot, add two tablespoons of butter, pepper and salt
4. Let it boil and thin as desired
