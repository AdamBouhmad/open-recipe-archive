## Ingredients

- two pounds veal leg
- bacon slices
- bacon and ham, chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- parsley
- celery
- beef broth
- lemon, cut into quarters

## Directions

1. Cut slices from two pounds of nice raw veal leg, pound them and remove the skin
2. Lard them generously with bacon
3. Place chopped bacon and ham in a pan, add finely chopped onion, parsley and celery
4. Add the slices and let stew, gradually adding beef broth in small amounts
5. When the slices are sufficiently tender, remove to a platter
6. Pour the strained liquid over them and garnish with lemon cut into quarters
7. May also be garnished around the platter with spinach, lettuce, or dumplings cut into slices for the meat
