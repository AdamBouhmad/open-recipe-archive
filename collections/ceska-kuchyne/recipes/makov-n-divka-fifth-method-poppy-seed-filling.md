## Ingredients

- 14 dek sugar
- 1 deciliter water
- crushed or ground poppy seeds
- vanilla
- cinnamon
- cloves
- lemon zest
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in water
2. Add crushed or ground poppy seeds until it thickens completely
3. Season with vanilla, cinnamon, and cloves
4. Add chopped lemon zest
5. Add abundant sour cream
6. Mix until a porridge forms
