## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of sugar
- half coffee cup of butter
- half coffee cup of milk
- one and a half coffee cups of flour
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- snow from four egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and butter well until it is like whipped cream
2. add the milk and flour with baking powder
3. finally add the snow from four egg whites
4. mix well and place in a pan to bake in the oven
