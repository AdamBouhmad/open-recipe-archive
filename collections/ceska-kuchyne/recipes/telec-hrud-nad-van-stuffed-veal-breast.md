## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- 45 grams fresh butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- Spices
- Salt
- 2 bread rolls
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut open the veal breast, break the ribs
2. Rinse and salt it
3. Mix 45 grams of fresh butter, 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk, a little spice, salt and 2 grated bread rolls, thin with milk
4. Mix everything together and place in the cut breast
5. Sew it shut, grease and bake
