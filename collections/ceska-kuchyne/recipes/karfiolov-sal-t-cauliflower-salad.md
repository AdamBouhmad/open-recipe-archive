## Ingredients

- cauliflower, divided into small florets
- salted water
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Divide cauliflower into small florets
2. Cook in salted water until tender
3. Drain well
4. Dress with oil, vinegar, pepper and salt
