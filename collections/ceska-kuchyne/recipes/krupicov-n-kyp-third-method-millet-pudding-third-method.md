## Ingredients

- millet porridge
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) ground almonds
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- lemon zest
- salt
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the millet porridge with 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of ground almonds, 12 dekagrams of butter, 10 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, snow from 10 egg whites, lemon zest, and salt
2. Mix everything well and bake in a greased form
