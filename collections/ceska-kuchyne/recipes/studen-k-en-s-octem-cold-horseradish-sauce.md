## Ingredients

- horseradish
- grated roll
- grated apple
- sugar
- salt
- beef broth
- white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Grate a little horseradish and put in a bowl
2. Add a teaspoon of grated roll and a grated small apple
3. Add a small piece of sugar and salt appropriately
4. Pour a teaspoon of beef broth and pour good white vinegar over it
