## Ingredients

- currants
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the currants and crush them
2. Pour a little water over them and cook slowly, stirring constantly
3. Strain through a sieve to draw off the juice better
4. Pour cooked sugar into the juice and cook both until well thickened
5. Remove from heat, let the liquid cool, and if any scum forms on top, remove it slowly
6. Pour into containers when cooled, seal with paper and isinglass, and store in a cold, dry place
