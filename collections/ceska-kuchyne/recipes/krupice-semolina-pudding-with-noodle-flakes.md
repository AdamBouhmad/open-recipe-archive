## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) semolina
- butter
- 1.5 liters hot milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg dough (for noodles)
- stewed fruit

## Directions

1. Toast the semolina in butter
2. Add 1.5 liters of hot milk and salt
3. Bake for 0.5 hour in the oven
4. Roll out dough as for noodles and let it dry
5. Cut into flakes and boil in salted water
6. Drain the water from the flakes
7. Add the flakes to the semolina with sugar and cinnamon
8. Place in a buttered form, bake, and serve with stewed fruit
