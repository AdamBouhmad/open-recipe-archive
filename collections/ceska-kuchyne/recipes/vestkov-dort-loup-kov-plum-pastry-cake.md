## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- sugar
- cinnamon
- pastry strips

## Directions

1. Clean ripe plums, split them, and remove the pits
2. Arrange the plums on pastry cake
3. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon
4. Decorate with pastry strips
5. Bake well until golden
6. During baking, brush the cake with beaten egg yolk
