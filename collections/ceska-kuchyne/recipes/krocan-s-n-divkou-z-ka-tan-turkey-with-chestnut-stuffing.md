## Ingredients

- two pounds of fine chestnuts
- water
- four spoonfuls of butter
- two spoonfuls of ground sugar
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Peel two pounds of fine chestnuts
2. Place them in boiling water and leave them there until you can remove the thin skin with a clean cloth
3. Wash them and let them drain
4. Put four spoonfuls of butter on a platter, add two spoonfuls of ground sugar
5. Place near the fire and stir until the sugar turns slightly brown
6. Pour a little beef broth or water over it
7. Put the chestnuts in it and let them stew, but not completely tender, and especially make sure the chestnuts remain whole
8. Remove them and let them cool a little
9. Wash the well-cleaned and eviscerated turkey
10. When cooking be careful not to tear the cavity
11. Now fill the cavity with the chestnuts, sew or skewer it shut
12. Salt the turkey and roast according to the previous method
