## Ingredients

- lamb or kid meat
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Take neck or kidney part from lamb, from kid any part.
2. Clean carefully, remove the skin, cut into neat pieces.
3. Wash in clean water, dry with a clean cloth.
4. Salt, pepper and dip in egg diluted with milk (or milk in place of one egg), roll in grated bread and fry in butter.
