## Ingredients

- 7 dek butter
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- 14 dek curd cheese
- raisins
- sultanas

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Rub with egg yolks and sugar
3. Add curd cheese
4. Mix with raisins and sultanas
