## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- orange peel
- sugar
- water
- raisins
- figs
- other additions

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into quarters
2. Cut out the seeds
3. Braise on a baking sheet
4. Add finely chopped orange peel, sugar, water, raisins and figs or other additions as desired
5. Boil everything together until the apples are completely soft
6. Arrange nicely on a plate and sprinkle with sugar
