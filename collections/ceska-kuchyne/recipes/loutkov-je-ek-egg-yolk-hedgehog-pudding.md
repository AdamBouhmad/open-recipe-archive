## Ingredients

- 16 hard egg yolks
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) butter
- 24 dekagrams almonds
- 24 dekagrams sugar
- zest from 2 lemons
- 4 whole eggs
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Beat 16 hard egg yolks with 24 dekagrams butter, 24 dekagrams almonds, 24 dekagrams sugar and zest from 2 lemons for half an hour
2. Add 4 whole eggs and 8 egg yolks
3. Steam in boiling water or bake in a form
