## Ingredients

- four brains
- 8 egg yolks
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- flower water
- salt
- onion
- 2 grated rolls
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Remove membranes and blood from four brains
2. Beat together with the brains: 8 egg yolks, 9 dekagrams butter, a little flower water, salt, chopped onion, and 2 grated rolls
3. Make stiff snow from 8 egg whites and fold in
4. Cook either just in steam or bake in the oven
