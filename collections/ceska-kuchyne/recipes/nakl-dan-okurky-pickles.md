## Ingredients

- cucumbers of any size
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick cucumbers of any desired size and place in an earthenware jar
2. Boil water with plenty of salt
3. Pour boiling water over the cucumbers so they are completely submerged
4. If cucumbers rise, a plate weighted with a stone can be placed over them
5. Additional spices may be added to cucumbers prepared this way
