## Ingredients

- Two large lemons
- Four cups water
- Six egg yolks
- Three cups sugar
- Half cup butter
- Five tablespoons cornstarch
- Cold water
- Six egg whites

## Directions

1. Custards must be cooked in a double boiler.
2. Cut up two large lemons, pour four cups of water over them and let them cook down.
3. Meanwhile, beat six egg yolks with three cups of sugar and half cup butter, strain the water from the cooked lemons into this mixture and return to heat.
4. Add five tablespoons of cornstarch mixed in cold water and let cook about one more minute.
5. Remove from heat and gently fold in the snow (beaten whites) from six egg whites.
6. Let cool and serve in small bowls as a dessert.
