## Ingredients

- twelve nice apples
- butter
- grated bread or rolls
- powdered sugar
- raisins
- white wine

## Directions

1. Peel twelve nice apples and slice them thinly.
2. Grease a pan with butter and sprinkle with grated bread.
3. Put a layer of sliced apples about a finger thick, sprinkle with powdered sugar and cleaned and washed raisins, then cover with a layer of grated bread and continue alternating these layers until everything is used; the last layer must be of grated bread or rolls, which then place pieces of fresh butter on; put it in the oven and bake.
4. Meanwhile pour in little by little one cup of ordinary white wine and when it is baked, turn it onto a plate, sprinkle with sugar and bring to the table.
