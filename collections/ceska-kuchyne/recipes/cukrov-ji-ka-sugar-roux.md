## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons flour
- small piece of butter
- 2 ounces fine sugar

## Directions

1. Fry 4 tablespoons of flour with a small piece of butter on a small pot until light yellow
2. Then sprinkle 2 ounces of fine sugar
3. You can use this to sprinkle small cakes instead of chopped almonds
4. Or crush 3 tablespoons of butter on a roller and mix with 1 small cup of flour and 3 tablespoons of fine sugar, mix thoroughly and sprinkle on large cakes for coffee
