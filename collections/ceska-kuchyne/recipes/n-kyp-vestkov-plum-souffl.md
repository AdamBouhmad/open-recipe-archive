## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- wine
- sugar
- butter
- 5 egg yolks
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- 3 egg whites
- 2 spoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel the ripe plums, halve them, remove the pits and stew them in wine with sugar until almost soft
2. Beat butter, add 5 egg yolks, sugar, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, a little cinnamon and snow from 3 egg whites
3. Add the plums with 2 spoons of crushed rusks to the mixture
4. Bake in a greased mold
