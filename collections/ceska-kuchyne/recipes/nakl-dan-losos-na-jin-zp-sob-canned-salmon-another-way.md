## Ingredients

- canned salmon
- several eggs
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Remove the salmon from the can onto a dish in the manner mentioned above.
2. Hard boil several eggs, peel them and cut them lengthwise in half.
3. Arrange the salmon and pour mayonnaise over it.
