## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- milk
- noodles
- 75 grams (5 lots) butter
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- lemon zest
- 5 egg whites
- sugar for topping
- sauce or preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from 4 eggs, work it well, grate it on a grater
2. Place the noodles on the stove, and when they boil, add the grated noodles to them and let cool
3. Rub 75 grams (5 lots) of butter, 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of sugar and 5 egg yolks with lemon zest, make stiff snow from 5 egg whites, slowly add it to everything, grease the form with butter, put the mixture into it and bake slowly in the oven
4. Turn out the pudding and sprinkle with sugar, add sauce, crème or preserved fruit to it
