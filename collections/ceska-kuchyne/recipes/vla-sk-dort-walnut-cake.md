## Ingredients

- half kilogram finely crushed sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- finely sifted flour
- stiff snow (beaten egg whites)
- butter for greasing cake molds
- finely grated bread crumbs for dusting molds

## Directions

1. Take half kilogram of finely crushed sugar and beat egg yolks into it
2. Mix everything thoroughly
3. Gradually add sifted fine flour and stiff snow
4. When everything is worked together, pour into cake molds greased with butter and dusted with finely grated bread crumbs
5. Bake slowly
6. The cakes may also be filled with preserves, layered on top of each other, and coated with various glazes
