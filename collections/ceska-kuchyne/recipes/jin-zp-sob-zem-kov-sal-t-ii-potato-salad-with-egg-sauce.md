## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- salt
- three egg yolks
- Provençal oil
- chives
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked peeled potatoes and salt them
2. Rub three hard egg yolks with Provençal oil
3. Add finely chopped chives and wine vinegar to the egg yolk mixture
4. Add potatoes to the sauce
5. Mix everything well
6. Arrange on a platter and garnish with fish
