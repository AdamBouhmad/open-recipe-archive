## Ingredients

- Shad
- Butter
- Parsley root, a piece
- Parsley, a bunch of green
- Onion, sliced
- Allspice, a whole piece
- Salt
- Pepper
- Red wine

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the shad
2. Place a piece of butter, a piece of parsley root, a bunch of green parsley, and sliced onion on a pot
3. Place the fish on top
4. Salt and pepper it
5. Pour red wine over it so it is covered
6. Cover and let simmer slowly for three-quarters of an hour
7. Remove the fish to a dish
8. Surround it with sliced onion and keep in a warm place
9. Strain the sauce
10. Make a brown butter and flour roux and fry it
11. Let it simmer briefly
12. Strain again
13. Mix in a piece of butter, juice from one lemon and a pinch of paprika
14. Pour over the fish and serve to the table
