## Ingredients

- beef
- 4 sardines
- 45 grams butter
- beef broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare meat as in the previous recipe
2. Remove bones from sardines and mix with 45 grams butter
3. Place on a pan, arrange cutlets on top
4. Pour beef broth over and braise
5. Usually add lemon cut into quarters
