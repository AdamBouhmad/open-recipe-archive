## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- peeled and pitted apples
- kidney fat
- small raisins
- sugar
- bread crumbs soaked in milk
- wine
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop six hard-boiled peeled eggs with peeled and pitted apples
2. Add necessary kidney fat and mix well
3. Add small raisins, sugar, bread crumbs soaked in milk and squeezed, wine, salt, crushed cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg
4. Bake this together
5. Fill small pastries with this filling
