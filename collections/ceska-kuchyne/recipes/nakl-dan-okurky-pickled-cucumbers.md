## Ingredients

- cucumbers of any size
- water
- salt
- dill fronds or dill seed (optional)
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick cucumbers of any desired size and place them in an earthenware pot.
2. Boil water and add plenty of salt to it.
3. Pour the hot water over the cucumbers so they are completely submerged.
4. If the cucumbers rise to the surface, place a plate on top and weigh it down with a stone.
5. Add dill fronds or dill seed if desired for flavor.
6. Let the cucumbers soak in the salt water for 3-4 days so they absorb the brine well and develop proper flavor.
7. Remove the cucumbers and pack them into bottles.
8. Pour boiled vinegar over them.
9. Screw the bottles shut as you would for preserving.
10. These cucumbers will keep until the new harvest.
