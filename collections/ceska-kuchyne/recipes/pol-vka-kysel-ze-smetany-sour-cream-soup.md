## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre good sour cream
- 1/2 litre whey
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a little flower
- salt
- fried eggs or 4 beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 litre of good sour cream and 1/2 litre of whey on the stove
2. Add 2 tablespoons of flour, a little flower and salt
3. Stir until completely cooked, then add either fried eggs or 4 beaten egg yolks
