## Ingredients

- half cup tapioca
- two cups water
- one cup boiling water
- two tablespoons sugar
- butter
- apples or pineapple
- sour cream and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Soak half a cup of tapioca overnight in two cups of water.
2. In the morning, add one cup of boiling water and let it cook for about an hour until it is cooked like cooked starch.
3. Add two tablespoons sugar.
4. Grease a pudding mold with butter, spread a layer of tapioca through the mold, place thin-sliced apples or pineapple on top, add another layer of tapioca, and continue until everything is used.
5. Bake for half an hour.
6. This pudding tastes best served cold with sour cream and sugar.
