## Ingredients

- venison liver
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- flour
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash venison liver well, remove the skin and cut into thin slices
2. Place a piece of butter on a pan, add finely chopped onion and a little pepper, and arrange the cut liver slices on top
3. Let them brown, then sprinkle with a little flour, add some vinegar, salt and let cook a little longer
