## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- salt
- 6 hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped
- 2 spoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- fish roe, if available
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and wrap it in clean cloth
2. Place in a pot and pour boiling water over it, salt with a spoonful of salt and cook for half to three-quarters of an hour
3. Take 6 hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped, 2 spoonfuls of butter, 1 teaspoon pepper, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon mustard and fish roe if available and a little milk for the sauce
4. Cook this mixture together and pour it over the fish
