## Ingredients

- 9 decagrams rendered butter
- 9 decagrams fresh butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons yeast
- 24 decagrams flour
- 5 tablespoons good sour cream
- a little vanilla
- salt
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Rub 9 decagrams of rendered butter until foamy
2. Add 9 decagrams of fresh butter
3. Add 10 egg yolks, each well mixed
4. Add 3 tablespoons yeast, 24 decagrams flour, 5 tablespoons good sour cream, a little vanilla and salt
5. Bake the babovka
6. Sprinkle with sugar
