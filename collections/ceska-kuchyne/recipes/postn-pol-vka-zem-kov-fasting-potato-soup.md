## Ingredients

- 3 litres water
- potatoes, sliced
- parsley
- pepper
- flower
- new spices
- garlic
- salt
- a small piece of onion
- a little mushroom
- caraway seeds
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Slice potatoes into 3 litres of water, add parsley, pepper, flower, new spices, garlic, salt, a small piece of onion, a little mushroom and caraway seeds
2. Make a meat broth, add it and bring to a boil
