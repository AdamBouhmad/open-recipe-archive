## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram cooked cold potatoes
- semolina or flour (as needed)
- salt
- 3 eggs
- 2 bread rolls
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Grate 1 kilogram of cooked cold potatoes
2. Mix with semolina or flour as needed
3. Season with salt
4. Beat 3 eggs and mix in
5. Knead until the dough does not stick to the hand
6. Cut 2 bread rolls into cubes and fry in fresh butter
7. Add to the dough
8. Knead thoroughly
9. Form dumplings the size of a medium apple
10. Boil in salted water
11. When cooked, cut in half, sprinkle with fried bread crumbs, and drizzle with melted butter
12. Serve hot
