## Ingredients

- dough
- one egg
- half a coffee cup of sugar
- one large coffee cup of grape seeds
- flour
- butter scraps

## Directions

1. Line a form with dough
2. Beat one egg
3. Add half a coffee cup of sugar and one large coffee cup of grape seeds to it
4. Spread this mixture in the form
5. Dust with a spoon of flour
6. Place butter scraps and add top crust
