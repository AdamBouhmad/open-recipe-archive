## Ingredients

- beef kidney
- cold water
- butter
- cumin
- pepper
- one onion, finely chopped
- garlic clove with butter, ground together
- flour
- several tablespoons good beef broth or calf roast gravy
- salt

## Directions

1. Place beef kidney in cold water and leave it for about an hour to soak thoroughly.
2. Cut it into thin slices.
3. Put a piece of butter, a little cumin and pepper on a board, with one finely chopped onion, or a clove of garlic with butter ground together.
4. Now add the sliced kidney and let it braise.
5. Then salt it, dust with a tablespoon of flour, mix it in, add several tablespoons of good beef broth or calf roast gravy, and let it braise until the kidneys are sufficiently tender.
