## Ingredients

- 2 cups beef soup
- 0.5 cup groats
- 3 tablespoons fresh butter
- 3 whole eggs
- salt
- bread crumbs for coating

## Directions

1. In a pot, bring 2 cups of beef soup to a boil and stir in 0.5 cup of groats until it becomes a thick paste
2. Remove from heat and let cool
3. Cream 3 tablespoons of fresh butter until foamy and mix in 3 whole eggs alternately, always one egg at a time and one-third of the paste, and season with salt
4. Grease small molds with butter, sprinkle them generously with grated bread crumbs, fill them halfway with this mixture and bake slowly
5. When the croquettes are baked, turn them out and place in a soup bowl
6. Pour hot beef soup over them
7. The croquettes may also be cooked in a steamer
