## Ingredients

- 180 grams fresh, melted butter
- 8-10 cleaned, deboned, finely chopped anchovies

## Directions

1. Take 180 grams of fresh, melted butter
2. Add 8-10 cleaned, deboned, finely chopped anchovies to it
3. Stew them in the butter until they turn yellow and firm, stirring constantly
4. Use hot instead of sauce for fish
