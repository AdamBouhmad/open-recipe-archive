## Ingredients

- half a liter of white coulis
- 60 grams of sardine butter
- two egg yolks
- white wine

## Directions

1. Simmer half a liter of white coulis with 60 grams of sardine butter
2. Beat in two egg yolks that have been bloomed in white wine
