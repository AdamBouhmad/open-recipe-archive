## Ingredients

- goose giblets
- carrots
- celery
- parsley
- cabbage
- cauliflower
- salt
- rice
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Prepare the giblets as when preparing them with noodles
2. When they have cooked for about half an hour, rinse the rice and place on a separate baking pan
3. Pour about two spoonfuls of broth from the giblets gradually until the rice is completely soft
4. When both are soft, add a little cauliflower and mix the giblets with the rice
5. The only difference is that no roux is added in this case
