## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- water
- salt
- bacon
- butter
- white flour
- beef or chicken broth
- wine
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Place sweetbreads in lukewarm salted water and let soak for one hour
2. Put them in water on the fire and let boil for about two minutes
3. Transfer to cold water and let cool
4. Carefully peel them and thread through with bacon
5. Make a thin roux on the stove from good butter and white flour
6. Mix it with strong beef or chicken broth and cook to a smooth sauce
7. Add a glass of wine
8. Place the sweetbreads in this and braise them
9. Add sliced mushrooms that have been braised in wine
10. Let it reduce slightly
11. Place sweetbreads on a dish, pour sauce over them and surround with mushrooms
