## Ingredients

- lemon
- 1 kilogram crushed sugar
- juice of 3 lemons
- 1.5 liters boiling water
- rum
- good spirits

## Directions

1. Peel a lemon finely halfway
2. Chop the peel and place it on a baking sheet with 1 kilogram of crushed sugar
3. Extract the juice from 3 lemons and pour it with 1.5 liters of boiling water over the sugar and peel
4. Add rum and good spirits to everything
5. Boil everything together
