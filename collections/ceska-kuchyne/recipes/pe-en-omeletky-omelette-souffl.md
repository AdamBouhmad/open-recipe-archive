## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- grated lemon zest
- 4 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Put 4 egg yolks in a bowl, add 4 tablespoons of sugar and a bit of grated lemon zest, and beat well for a quarter hour
2. Gently fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from 4 eggs
3. Pour into a deep dish or pan greased with butter
4. Bake over moderate heat for at most ten minutes
5. Serve immediately to the table
