## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder and neck pieces
- fresh butter
- water
- salt
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- herbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean shoulder and neck pieces.
2. Place new butter with 2 spoonfuls of water and salt, piece of carrot, parsley, celery and herbs on a baking sheet.
3. Braise until everything softens.
4. Press pale roux through it until it reaches the proper thickness.
5. Squeeze juice from the whole lemon into it.
