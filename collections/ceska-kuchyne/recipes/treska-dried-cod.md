## Ingredients

- dried cod
- salt
- flour
- egg
- bread, grated
- butter
- cabbage or mustard or horseradish

## Directions

1. Soak the dried cod in fresh water for several days, changing the water daily
2. Cut into equal pieces, wipe, salt, coat with flour, dip in beaten egg, and coat with grated bread
3. Fry in butter
4. Serve with cabbage, mustard, or horseradish
