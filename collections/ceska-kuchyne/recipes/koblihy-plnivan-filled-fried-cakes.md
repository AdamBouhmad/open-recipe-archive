## Ingredients

- three ounces butter
- two whole eggs
- half ounce pressed yeast
- three tablespoons sifted sugar
- small piece lemon zest
- salt
- one pound warmed and sifted flour
- 1 tablespoon rum or wine
- 1 small cup milk or sour cream
- strawberry, raspberry or currant preserves, or poppy seed, plum or apricot filling
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Rub three ounces butter on a bowl until frothed
2. Mix into it two whole eggs dissolved with half ounce pressed yeast
3. Add three tablespoons sifted sugar, a small piece lemon zest, a little salt, one pound warmed and sifted flour, 1 tablespoon rum or wine (which is added so that they do not absorb too much fat), 1 small cup milk or sour cream
4. Beat the dough for a quarter hour constantly with a whisk in one direction
5. Dust it with flour and let it rise in warmth
6. Then take the dough out onto a floured board, roll it to little finger thickness
7. Cut out round circles from half with a cutter or glass, and mark circles on the other half but do not cut them out
8. Into the middle of each marked circle put a heap of strawberry, raspberry or currant preserves, or poppy seed, plum or apricot filling
9. Cover them with the cut out pieces, then cut out slightly smaller round cakes with a glass or cutter
10. Lay them on a warmed board covered with a floured cloth and let them rise
11. Then put them in rendered fat
