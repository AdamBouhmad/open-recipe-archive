## Ingredients

- juice
- finely chopped peel of two lemons
- pinch of cinnamon
- one liter of sugar syrup at 18°
- one bottle of malaga
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Place on a dish juice and finely chopped peel from two lemons, a pinch of cinnamon, and one liter of sugar syrup at 18°
2. Let everything stand for three to four hours in a room, then strain the liquid and let it freeze
3. Before pouring, add one bottle of malaga and four egg whites to the liquid, work everything for at least ten minutes quite rapidly so the egg whites do not freeze but turn into snow, then pour immediately into glasses
4. Instead of malaga, any other good and preferred wine can be used
