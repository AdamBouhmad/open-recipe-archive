## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes clean, place them in a pot, pour cold water over them, salt them slightly and cook until soft
2. Pour off all the water, cover the pot firmly with a clean cloth and let them stand in steam for a while
3. Place them unpeeled on the table and serve with fresh butter and salt on the side
