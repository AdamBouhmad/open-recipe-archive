## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- three beaten eggs
- one tablespoon melted butter
- one teaspoon salt
- one teaspoon baking powder mixed with one tablespoon flour (if milk is sweet), or one teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water (if milk is sour)
- two cups slices or remnants of dry white bread

## Directions

1. Add three beaten eggs to one quart of milk, one tablespoon of melted butter and one teaspoon of salt
2. If the milk is sweet, mix one teaspoon of baking powder mixed with one tablespoon of flour; if the milk is sour, add one teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in hot water
3. Then grate or roll slices or remnants of dry white bread so you have two cups, mix it with the milk, make a batter and bake pancakes
