## Ingredients

- pumpkin flesh
- water
- one quart of pumpkin puree
- four whole eggs
- two coffee cups of sugar
- one tablespoon of syrup
- one teaspoon of grated ginger
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- half a teaspoon of nutmeg
- half a coffee cup of melted butter
- one and a half quarts of milk
- dough

## Directions

1. Chop pumpkin flesh into small cubes
2. Place in a pot with a little water and cook over high heat until soft
3. Drain the water and push the flesh through a sieve
4. Put one quart of this puree in a bowl
5. Add four whole eggs, two coffee cups of sugar, one tablespoon of syrup, one teaspoon of grated ginger, one teaspoon of ground cinnamon, half a teaspoon of nutmeg, half a coffee cup of melted butter, and one and a half quarts of milk
6. Mix everything together well
7. Fill dough-lined forms for two pies and bake the pie for three-quarters of an hour
