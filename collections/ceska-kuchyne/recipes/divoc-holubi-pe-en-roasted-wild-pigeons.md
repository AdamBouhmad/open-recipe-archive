## Ingredients

- young wild pigeons
- bacon
- grape leaves

## Directions

1. Young wild pigeons (note: old pigeons have tough meat and must be marinated for several days beforehand) should be wrapped in bacon and grape leaves.
2. Roast slowly until brown on a baking sheet.
3. Baste frequently during roasting and pour the baked sauce over them.
