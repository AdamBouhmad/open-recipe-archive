## Ingredients

- Smelts
- Milk
- Flour
- Clarified butter
- Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs, grated
- Lemon, sliced into rounds

## Directions

1. Clean as many smelts as needed
2. Remove the fins
3. Wash the fish thoroughly
4. Dry with a cloth
5. Dip in milk
6. Coat in flour
7. Fry in hot clarified butter
8. Alternatively, dust with flour, dip in eggs beaten with a little salt and pepper, coat in grated bread crumbs, fry and serve garnished with lemon slices
