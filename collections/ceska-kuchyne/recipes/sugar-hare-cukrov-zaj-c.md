## Ingredients

- 10 chocolate tablets
- ground sugar equal in weight to chocolate
- flour as needed
- 15 grams cinnamon
- 15 grams allspice
- 15 grams cloves
- clove flower
- ginger
- grated lemon zest from one lemon
- 120 grams grated peeled almonds
- eggs

## Directions

1. Grate 10 chocolate tablets
2. Add sugar equal to weight of chocolate, flour as needed
3. Add spices: cinnamon, allspice, cloves, clove flower, ginger
4. Add grated lemon zest
5. Add 120 grams grated almonds
6. Add eggs as much as dough will absorb and knead well
7. Roll out dough
8. Line baking sheet with wafer
9. Shape into rabbit hindquarters
10. Brush with beaten egg
11. Pierce all over with sliced, peeled almonds
12. Bake
13. Cut into portions like ordinary rabbit
