## Ingredients

- stale white bread
- three eggs
- half a cup of milk
- salt
- four tablespoons lard

## Directions

1. Cut stale white bread into slices.
2. Beat three eggs, add half a cup of milk and salt.
3. Put four tablespoons of lard in an iron pan and when it is heated, dip the bread in the egg mixture and fry nicely to a golden color on both sides.
4. Serve hot with syrup or honey.
