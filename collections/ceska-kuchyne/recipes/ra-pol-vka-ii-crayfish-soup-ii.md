## Ingredients

- 25-30 crayfish
- 250 grams butter
- finely chopped onion
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 liters soup
- egg yolks
- crayfish meat
- fried roll cut into cubes
- green peas braised in butter
- asparagus
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cook 25-30 crayfish, shell the meat, crush the rest of the body with 250 grams of butter
2. Add finely chopped onion, 3 tablespoons flour, fry while stirring constantly
3. Pour with 3 liters of soup, cook together for an hour, strain and beat in egg yolks
4. Add crayfish meat cut into cubes, fried roll cut into cubes, peas, asparagus and cauliflower to the soup
