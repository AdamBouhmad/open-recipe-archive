## Ingredients

- developed but not fully ripe melons
- soft water
- alum
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut developed but not fully ripe melons into oblong pieces
2. Clean and soak in soft water in which a small amount of alum has been dissolved
3. Cook the melon slices until soft, then place in fresh cold water
4. Remove to a dish, layer them and pour filtered sugar over them
5. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it stronger, and preserve it with the melons
6. On the third day, cook the sugar thicker again, add the melons and cook together
7. Repeat this until the fifth day in the same manner
8. Skim off the foam that forms, place completely candied melons in clean jars, cover with sugar syrup and bind
