## Ingredients

- clams, 50
- clam liquid
- water, two cups
- salt
- pepper
- butter, two tablespoons fresh
- milk, two cups
- crackers, several, finely crushed

## Directions

1. Cook the liquid from 50 clams
2. Meanwhile, chop the clams finely
3. Remove foam from the boiling liquid and add two cups of water
4. When it returns to boiling, add the chopped clams
5. Season with pepper
6. Let simmer very slowly for 5 minutes
7. Add two tablespoons fresh butter
8. Finally add two cups of milk and let heat through, but do not boil
9. Remove from heat, add several finely crushed crackers
10. Serve hot
