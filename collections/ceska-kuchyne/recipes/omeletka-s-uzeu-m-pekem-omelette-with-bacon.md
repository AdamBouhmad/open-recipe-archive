## Ingredients

- bacon, cut into small cubes
- water
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into small cubes and parboil it first in boiling water
2. Then fry it in a pan and drain the fat
3. Prepare a simple omelette and when it is slightly dry on top, place the fried bacon in the middle
4. Fold the omelette in half and ensure the edges are well sealed
5. Turn it out onto a plate and garnish with green parsley
