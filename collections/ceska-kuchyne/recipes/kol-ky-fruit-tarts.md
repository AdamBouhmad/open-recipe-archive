## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 4-6 egg yolks
- 1 piece fresh pressed yeast
- 4 egg whites
- 1 quart sweet sour cream
- sugar
- lemon zest
- salt
- 1 pound fine sifted flour
- filling (jam, cheese curd, or other filling)

## Directions

1. Cream butter
2. Add egg yolks one by one
3. Add broken piece of pressed yeast
4. Beat egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
5. Add sour cream
6. Add sugar
7. Add lemon zest and salt
8. Add flour and mix until dough leaves the spoon
9. Let dough rise
10. Scoop dough with a spoon and form small tarts
11. Top with jam, cheese curd, or other filling
12. Let rise again
13. Bake
