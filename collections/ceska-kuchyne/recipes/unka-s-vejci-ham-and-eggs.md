## Ingredients

- boiled ham
- butter
- eggs
- potatoes

## Directions

1. From nice boiled ham, cut thin slices about the size of a palm, warm a piece of butter in a pan, let the ham brown on both sides, then arrange it on a heated platter and place one fried egg on each piece.
2. Serve with sliced and fried potatoes.
