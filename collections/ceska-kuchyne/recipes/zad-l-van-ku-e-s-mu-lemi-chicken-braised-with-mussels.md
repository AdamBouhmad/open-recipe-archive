## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 3 1/2 deciliters (jezdlík) Austrian wine mixed with water
- pepper
- new spices
- cloves
- 1 onion, sliced
- mussels
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and divide the chicken, braise it with 3 1/2 deciliters (jezdlík) of Austrian wine mixed with water, pepper, new spices, cloves and sliced onion; braise everything.
2. When the chicken is tender, place it separately on a platter.
3. Strain the pale broth into the sauce, which is then boiled with the chicken.
4. Meanwhile, clean the mussels, wash them whole with their shells in wine, and boil them in wine until they open by themselves.
5. Pour the wine in which the mussels were cooked into the sauce, arrange the chicken on a platter, mussels around it and pour the sauce over everything.
6. If the sauce is pale, burn a piece of sugar.
