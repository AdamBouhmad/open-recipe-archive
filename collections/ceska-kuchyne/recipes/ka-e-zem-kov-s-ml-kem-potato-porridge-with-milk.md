## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- milk
- 15 dekagrams butter (10 lots)
- salt
- 5-6 eggs
- cheese, grated
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut raw potatoes, scald them and immediately drain the water, in its place add milk so that the potatoes are covered, finish cooking in it, press through a sieve, let cool
2. Mix in 15 dekagrams of butter, salt, 5-6 eggs and place the mixture in a greased mold
3. Brush with snow, sprinkle with grated cheese and pieces of butter, and bake for half an hour in the oven
