## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- fresh butter
- juice of one lemon
- small onion, finely chopped
- salt
- beef broth
- white wine (optional)
- egg yolk and cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel fresh mushrooms, trim the stems a little at the bottom, wash them in fresh water, slice them into strips, put them on a shallow pan on a piece of fresh butter, add lemon juice, finely chopped small onion, a little salt and several tablespoons of good beef broth and let them stew for a quarter hour.
2. Then pour them onto a platter and serve immediately.
3. You can also add a little white wine and mix an egg yolk blended with cream.
