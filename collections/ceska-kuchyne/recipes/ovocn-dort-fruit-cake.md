## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of sugar
- one coffee cup of butter
- seven egg yolks
- half coffee cup of black syrup (molasses)
- half teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in warm water
- seven egg whites
- one quart of sifted flour
- one pound of small currants
- one pound of large raisins with seeds removed
- half pound of citron chopped finely
- approximately one-quarter coffee cup of flour
- one pound of sliced almonds
- one teaspoon of grated nutmeg
- one teaspoon of cinnamon
- half teaspoon of cloves
- half teaspoon of mixed spices

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar with the butter
2. add seven egg yolks
3. add the molasses mixed with the baking soda dissolved in warm water
4. beat the seven egg whites and add them
5. fold in the sifted flour
6. clean the small currants and large raisins and chop the citron finely
7. mix all together and dust with about one-quarter coffee cup of flour and mix again so the flour coats everything (this is done so the raisins do not sink to the bottom)
8. add to the batter along with the sliced almonds
9. season with nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves, and mixed spices
10. grease the pan with butter and line with paper, grease the paper as well
11. pour the batter into the pan so it is about three-quarters full
12. bake in a moderately warm oven for two hours
13. if it bakes too quickly, cover with paper
14. when baked, turn out and coat with white icing
