## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram (1/2 pound) of curd cheese
- flour or grated breadcrumbs (as much as curd will take)
- yeast (as needed)
- eggs (as needed)
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix curd cheese with flour or grated breadcrumbs
2. Add yeast, eggs as needed, salt and flour
3. Work into a stiff dough like ordinary noodles
4. Let rolled-out dough dry somewhat
5. Cut into short, wide noodles (slejžky)
6. Grease baking pan
7. Bake noodles on both sides
