## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one small cup sugar
- one and one-half small cups flour
- two teaspoons baking powder
- four teaspoons hot water
- salt
- jelly

## Directions

1. Place three eggs in a bowl
2. Add one small cup sugar and mix well
3. Add one and one-half small cups flour mixed with two teaspoons baking powder
4. Add four teaspoons hot water while continuing to mix
5. Pour onto a pan and bake, but not too thickly—about the thickness of a finger, otherwise it will crack when rolled
6. Prepare a towel, dampen it with warm water, and spread it on a table
7. Turn out the baked pastry onto the towel
8. Spread with jelly and roll using the towel
9. Leave covered for about ten minutes
10. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve
