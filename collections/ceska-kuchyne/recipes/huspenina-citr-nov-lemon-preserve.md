## Ingredients

- sugar
- lemon zest
- isinglass
- water

## Directions

1. Rub lemon zest onto sugar
2. Scrape this sugar from the other and press the juice from it
3. Cook finely torn isinglass with water until at least half has boiled away
4. Pour everything into the lemon juice, sweeten again, cook and strain into glass or earthenware containers
5. Place on ice to cool
6. Various aromatic substances (vanilla, etc.) may be added to give it a special flavor
