## Ingredients

- onion, finely chopped
- beef fat, butter or lard
- beef broth
- roux
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the whole onion very finely
2. Fry it on a piece of beef fat, butter or lard until golden
3. Pour a little beef broth over it
4. Add a little roux and mix so the sauce is sufficiently thick
5. Finally strain through a sieve
