## Ingredients

- Violet juice
- Lemon juice
- Plenty of sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Take violet juice described elsewhere
2. Add lemon juice
3. Add plenty of sugar
4. Dilute slightly with water
5. Proceed to make ice cream
