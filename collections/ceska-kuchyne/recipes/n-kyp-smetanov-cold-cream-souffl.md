## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters (3 tablespoons) very good cream
- 0.5 kilogram (1 pound) sugar
- lemon peel
- colorants: alkermes juice, violet juice, saffron
- cherry and violet leaves

## Directions

1. Mix 1.5 liters (3 tablespoons) of very good cream with 0.5 kilogram (1 pound) of sugar rubbed with lemon peel that has been grated
2. Place in a stone bowl and put on ice
3. Beat with a whisk as if making snow until completely thick
4. Decorate the tin molds in which the mixture will remain with various ornaments made with sugar colorants: red with alkermes juice, violet with juice from soaked, squeezed and sugared violets, yellow with saffron
5. Draw a pattern on the bottom of the mold with a stick or brush
6. Leave the mixture in the molds until the next day, then turn out and decorate with so-called sugar aspic or with green cherry, violet and other leaves
