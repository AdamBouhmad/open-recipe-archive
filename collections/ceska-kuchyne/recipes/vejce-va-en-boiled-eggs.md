## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. For soft-boiled eggs where the white is partially cooked but not hard and the yolk is completely liquid, put clean washed eggs into boiling water, remove the pot from the heat so the water does not boil further, cover well and leave for five minutes
2. The best and safest method to achieve the desired degree of cooking is that the eggs are completely heated through and the white is nicely gelatinous and not hardened
3. Must be carefully watched and not left in water longer than five minutes
4. Another good method is to put eggs in cold water, bring to the fire and let the water come to a boil
5. As soon as the water begins to boil, the eggs are just right and the cook cannot be mistaken in this case
6. The most common method is to put eggs in boiling water and let them boil for three minutes
7. In this time they are soft-boiled
8. If we let them boil for five minutes, the white is cooked and the yolk is semi-liquid, whereas in 8 to 10 minutes they are hard-boiled
