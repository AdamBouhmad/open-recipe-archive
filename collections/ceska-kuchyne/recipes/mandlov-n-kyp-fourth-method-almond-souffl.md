## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 5 cooked egg yolks
- 24 dekagrams butter
- 9 raw egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- snow from 9 egg whites
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Grease a mold and sprinkle with grated roll.
2. Beat together 24 dekagrams almonds, 5 cooked egg yolks, 24 dekagrams butter, 9 raw egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, snow from 9 egg whites with sugar and lemon peel added.
3. Bake the soufflé in the mold, or place the mold in boiling water. In the latter case, pour sauce made from wine over the soufflé.
