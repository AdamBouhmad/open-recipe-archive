## Ingredients

- wine
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- large sultana raisins
- fried grated bread

## Directions

1. Add a little water to wine as needed
2. Add sugar, cinnamon, and finely chopped lemon peel
3. Simmer together
4. Add cleaned and picked large sultana raisins and fried grated bread
5. Allow everything to simmer together
6. Serve at table
