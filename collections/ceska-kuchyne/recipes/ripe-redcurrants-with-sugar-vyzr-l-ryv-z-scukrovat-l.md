## Ingredients

- best ripe redcurrant bunches
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select the best ripe redcurrant bunches (about three or four), tie them together by the stems, and place in stone containers.
2. Cook as much sugar as needed for the amount of redcurrants being prepared.
3. Pour the boiling sugar over the redcurrants.
4. Cover the container with paper and let sit undisturbed.
5. On the second day, carefully drain all the syrup, cook it thick, add the redcurrant berries, and cook everything together.
6. Then place in containers or jars and seal tightly with a bladder.
