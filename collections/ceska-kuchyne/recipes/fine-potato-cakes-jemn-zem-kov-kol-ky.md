## Ingredients

- 240 grams (16 lots) butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 195 grams (13 lots) sugar
- lemon peel, grated
- 15 grams (1 lot) bitter almonds
- 240 grams wheat flour
- 275 grams (18 lots) potato flour
- 120 grams (8 lots) raisins
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter
2. Gradually add egg yolks
3. Add sugar, grated lemon peel, pounded bitter almonds, wheat flour, potato flour, and raisins
4. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Oil small forms
6. Fill halfway
7. Bake slowly for half an hour
