## Ingredients

- parsley
- chives
- spinach
- marjoram
- butter
- tablespoon flour
- nutmeg
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Chop parsley, chives, spinach, marjoram and similar herbs
2. Fry with a tablespoon of flour in butter, season with nutmeg
3. Add 6 eggs and bake the roll
