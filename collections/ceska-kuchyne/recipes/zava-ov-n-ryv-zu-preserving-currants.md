## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms currant berries
- ½ liter wine vinegar
- ½ kilogram sugar
- 15 grams (1 lot) cinnamon
- 8 cloves

## Directions

1. Wash the currant berries and allow to dry on a sieve
2. Boil wine vinegar with sugar, cinnamon, and cloves until thickened
3. Cook the currants in this mixture and pour into a stone jug
4. The next day, pour off the syrup, boil it again, and pour back over the currants
5. Repeat this for two days
6. Cover the jug well, tie it securely, and store in a cool place
