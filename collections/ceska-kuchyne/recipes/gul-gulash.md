## Ingredients

- two pounds meat from the shoulder, cut into small cubes
- two tablespoons butter
- one large onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves
- 8 grains of allspice
- a slice of lemon
- bay leaf
- two tablespoons vinegar
- one tablespoon sugar
- salt
- one tablespoon flour
- water
- 2-3 tablespoons good tomato ketchup
- a pinch of paprika
- fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of meat from the shoulder into small cubes.
2. Put two tablespoons of butter in a pot and one large finely chopped onion; when it begins to turn yellow, add the meat, salt it.
3. Add 4 cloves, 8 grains of allspice, a slice of lemon, a bay leaf, two tablespoons of vinegar, and one tablespoon of sugar.
4. Cover and let it braise.
5. When the meat is tender, dust it with one tablespoon of flour; if it has dried out too much, add water, 2-3 tablespoons of good tomato ketchup, and a pinch of paprika.
6. Mix, let it simmer, and then place on a platter.
7. Serve with fried potatoes.
