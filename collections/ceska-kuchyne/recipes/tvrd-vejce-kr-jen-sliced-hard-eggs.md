## Ingredients

- Butter
- Bread crumbs
- Parsley, chopped
- Hard eggs, sliced
- Sour cream, quarter liter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry grated bread crumbs in butter and add chopped parsley.
2. Cook in quarter liter of sour cream until a sauce forms.
3. Season with salt and mix with the sliced eggs.
