## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two eggs
- sour cream or good milk
- salt
- beautiful prunes
- fried bread roll, curd cheese, gingerbread, or ground poppy seed
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with two eggs and as much sour cream or good milk as needed for thick dough
2. Salt and knead well
3. Roll out on a board, cut into pieces about the size of a walnut, and wrap a beautiful prune in each piece
4. Boil the dumplings in boiling salted water, carefully lift them from the bottom of the pot with a spoon, and when they float to the top and cook a little longer, remove them with a strainer to a dish
5. Sprinkle them with either fried bread roll, curd cheese, gingerbread, or ground poppy seed, and pour melted butter over them
6. You can also add a piece of butter to the dough
7. The pits can be removed from the prunes beforehand; if the prunes are sour, you can put a piece of sugar in place of the pit before wrapping
