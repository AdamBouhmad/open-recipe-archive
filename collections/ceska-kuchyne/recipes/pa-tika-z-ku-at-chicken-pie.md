## Ingredients

- four to five pound chicken
- one spoonful of butter
- piece of chopped onion
- two bowls of hot water
- half pound of ham, cut into small pieces
- potatoes
- puff pastry
- salt
- pepper
- one egg white
- fresh parsley
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken into pieces at the joints
2. Put one spoonful of butter in a pot, and when the onion begins to turn yellow, put the chicken in the pot
3. Pour two bowls of hot water over it and let it stew
4. If desired, you can add half a pound of ham cut into small pieces
5. Trim and cut potatoes into squares, boil them for a quarter hour and drain them
6. When the chicken is stewed, cut the meat from the larger bones and remove them
7. Line a shallow two-quart dish with puff pastry
8. Lay a layer of the cut potatoes at the bottom, then a layer of meat
9. Place on top the pieces of pastry cut from the edges of the dish that protruded over the rim
10. Add a spoonful of chopped fresh parsley, salt, pepper, and a few small pieces of butter the size of a walnut
11. Cover with the top puff pastry, which you have slit in the middle
12. Press down and trim the edges
13. Brush the top with beaten egg white and bake in a hot oven for half an hour until the crust is fully baked
14. Meanwhile prepare the following sauce: Put a small spoonful of butter in a pot and when it melts, mix in two level spoonfuls of flour, stir but do not let it brown
15. Pour one bowl of the broth in which the chicken was cooked and one bowl of milk, stirring constantly until it begins to boil
16. Remove from heat, salt and pepper it, add the beaten yolk of one egg and one spoonful of finely chopped parsley
17. Pour this into the pie when it is baked, using a funnel through the hole made in the top pastry, or serve the sauce in a bowl so each person can help themselves
18. Serve hot at the table
