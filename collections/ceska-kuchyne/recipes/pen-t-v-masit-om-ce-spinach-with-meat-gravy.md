## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- White flour
- Good beef broth
- Ground pepper
- Flowers

## Directions

1. Sort and clean spinach thoroughly in several waters
2. Blanch in boiling salted water
3. Drain, rinse with cold water and press out well
4. Chop finely
5. Make a thin roux from butter and white flour
6. Add chopped spinach and stew gently
7. Gradually add good beef broth
8. Season with ground flower and salt
9. Cook until spinach is very thick
10. Serve with veal cutlets, fried chicken, or fried veal udder if desired
