## Ingredients

- corned beef
- cold water
- hot water

## Directions

1. Clean the meat well; if it is too salty, place it in cold water, which when it comes to a boil, after about 10 minutes, drain and replace with fresh boiling water.
2. If it is not too salty, you can place it in hot water.
3. Skim off the foam diligently and boil about half an hour for each pound of meat, so 4 pounds for about two hours.
4. If it is to be served cold, it is better not to remove it immediately after cooking, but rather set it aside and leave it in the water in which it was boiled until it has almost cooled.
5. Then place the meat in a shallow dish, lay a board over it, and weigh it down with bricks.
6. The compressed meat cuts better.
