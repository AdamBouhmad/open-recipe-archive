## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 15 dekagrams sugar
- 3 dekagrams ground almonds
- lemon zest
- small teaspoon cinnamon
- 9 dekagrams grated breadcrumbs
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate cooked potatoes
2. To half kilogram potatoes add 12 dekagrams butter, 9 egg yolks, 15 dekagrams sugar, 3 dekagrams ground almonds, grated lemon zest, a small teaspoon cinnamon, 9 dekagrams grated breadcrumbs and beaten egg whites
3. Place in a buttered mold
4. Either bake for one hour or cook for 2 hours in hot water
