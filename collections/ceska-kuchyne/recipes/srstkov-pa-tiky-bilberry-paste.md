## Ingredients

- bilberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook cleaned bilberries until soft
2. Pour onto a sieve so the water drains
3. Press through the sieve
4. For one part of pressed bilberry mass, add one and a half parts sugar
5. Cook together with constant stirring until very thick
6. Pour onto slate tablets and allow to dry completely
