## Ingredients

- three coffee cups of cooked apples strained through a sieve
- one coffee cup of sugar
- six well-beaten eggs
- one quart of milk
- a little grated nutmeg
- dough

## Directions

1. Take three coffee cups of cooked apples strained through a sieve, one coffee cup of sugar, six well-beaten eggs, one quart of milk, and a little grated nutmeg
2. Mix everything together and pour onto prepared dough
3. When baked, spread with snow from egg white and place in the oven to dry
