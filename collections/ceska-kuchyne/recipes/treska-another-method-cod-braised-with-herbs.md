## Ingredients

- well-soaked cod
- butter
- water
- parsley
- pepper
- bay leaf
- light roux (pale jíška)
- lemon

## Directions

1. Place well-soaked cod on a baking dish with butter, water, chopped parsley, pepper, and bay leaf
2. Braise until cod is soft
3. Arrange cod on a platter
4. Make a light roux, strain over the cod, add a piece of butter and simmer briefly
5. Serve with sauce, usually with lemon cut into quarters
