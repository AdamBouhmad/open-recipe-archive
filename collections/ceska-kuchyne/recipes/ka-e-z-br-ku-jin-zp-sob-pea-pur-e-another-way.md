## Ingredients

- peas
- salt water
- butter or bacon
- butter or cracklings
- smoked meat or sausages

## Directions

1. Cook peas in salted water and press through a sieve so that only the indigestible skins remain on the sieve
2. Put the pressed porridge in a baking dish with melted butter or sliced bacon, stir while cooking, pour with butter or cracklings
3. Smoked meat or sausages can be added to it
4. If hard water must be used or if the peas do not want to cook, add a little soda, which softens any water
