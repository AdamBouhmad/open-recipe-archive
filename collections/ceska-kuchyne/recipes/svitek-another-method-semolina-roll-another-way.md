## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams butter (8 lots)
- 4 eggs
- 24 dekagrams semolina (16 lots)
- 4 tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Beat butter with 4 eggs, semolina and 4 tablespoons of sour cream
2. Place the mixture in a greased pan and bake in moderate heat
3. Cut the baked roll into cubes and cook in salted water or broth
