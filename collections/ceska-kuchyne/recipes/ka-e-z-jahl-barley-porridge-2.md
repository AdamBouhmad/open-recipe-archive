## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter barley
- 1 liter milk
- 10 egg yolks
- salt
- 9 dekagrams sugar (6 lots)
- lemon zest, rubbed with sugar
- 6 dekagrams ground almonds
- 10 egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Scald 1/4 liter of barley
2. Cook in 1 liter of milk and press through a sieve
3. When cooled, add 10 egg yolks, salt, 9 dekagrams of sugar, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, 6 dekagrams of ground almonds, and stiffly beaten snow from 10 egg whites
4. Bake in a greased mold
