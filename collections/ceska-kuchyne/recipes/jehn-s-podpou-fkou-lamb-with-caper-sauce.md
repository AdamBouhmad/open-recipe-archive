## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bouillon
- onion
- herbs
- bay leaf
- clove
- capers
- lemon slices
- mushrooms
- sardines
- white wine
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut lamb meat into pieces, wash, salt and pepper it.
2. Brown in butter and pour with bouillon.
3. Add onion, herbs, bay leaf and clove, simmer until meat is completely tender.
4. Strain the sauce, strain it, add capers, lemon slices, sautéed mushrooms cut into small pieces and sardines.
5. Pour everything with white wine, brown with flour and pour over the meat.
