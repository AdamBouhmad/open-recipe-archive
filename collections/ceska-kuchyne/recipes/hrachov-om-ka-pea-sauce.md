## Ingredients

- peas
- broth
- butter
- salt
- onion
- thyme

## Directions

1. Cook the peas and drain the water
2. Add broth
3. Add butter, salt, onion, and thyme
4. Simmer the peas slowly for half an hour
5. Strain the sauce and serve with smoked meat
