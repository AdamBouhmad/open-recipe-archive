## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- fresh sage
- fresh butter
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the pike and rinse it in several waters
2. Cook in salted water
3. When cooked, place on a platter
4. Finely chop fresh sage and sprinkle over the fish
5. Pour heated fresh butter over it
