## Ingredients

- a piece of fresh butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 3.5 deciliters sweet cream
- flour
- 2 bread rolls cut into cubes
- smoked meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Cream a piece of fresh butter finely.
2. Add 3 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs, salt.
3. Add 3.5 deciliters of sweet cream and enough flour so the beaten dough does not stick to the hand.
4. Mix in 2 bread rolls cut into cubes.
5. Form dumplings and place a piece of chopped smoked meat in the center of each.
6. Grease and sprinkle with grated bread roll.
