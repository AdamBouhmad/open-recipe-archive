## Ingredients

- eight tablespoons of butter
- twelve whole eggs
- three cupfuls of sifted flour
- four tablespoons of sugar
- half a teaspoon of vanilla extract
- a tiny pinch of salt
- a piece of pressed or dry dissolved yeast
- five egg whites

## Directions

1. Put eight tablespoons of butter on a board, let it warm and beat it for half an hour, then add to it twelve whole eggs and three cupfuls of sifted flour, namely always one egg and one tablespoon of flour, until you use everything up.
2. Then add four tablespoons of sugar, half a teaspoon of vanilla extract, a tiny pinch of salt, a piece of pressed or dry dissolved yeast, beat for a quarter of an hour, then add the stiff snow from five egg whites.
3. Grease a mold with butter, fill it halfway with the prepared dough, set it to warmth and let it rise; when the mold is almost full, put it in the oven and let it bake slowly until golden, which may take about forty minutes, then turn it out carefully and sprinkle generously with sugar.
