## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- 75 grams (5 lots) fresh butter
- bundle of parsley
- various vegetable roots
- vinegar
- cleaned mushrooms, sliced into leaves
- fish sauce
- water in which peas were cooked
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the pike into pieces and salt it
2. After half an hour, dry it and place on a baking sheet with fresh butter, bundle of parsley, various vegetable roots, and vinegar
3. Cook for a quarter hour in water in which peas were cooked
4. After a quarter hour, carefully remove the skin
5. Stew several cleaned mushrooms sliced into leaves and cook them with fish sauce
6. Pour the fish sauce over the pike
