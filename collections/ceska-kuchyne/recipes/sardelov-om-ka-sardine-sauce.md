## Ingredients

- two sardines
- two tablespoons of capers
- two hard egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- good broth
- 60 grams of sardine butter

## Directions

1. Finely chop two sardines, two tablespoons of capers and two hard egg yolks
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Pour good broth over
4. Simmer with 60 grams of sardine butter
5. This sauce is suitable for veal, mutton, poultry and fish
