## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram rye flour
- 1 kilogram honey
- 2 tablespoons alcohol
- 1 dekagram soda dissolved in a spoonful of water
- a little new spice
- orange zest cut finely
- 5 grams cardamom

## Directions

1. Roast 1 kilogram rye flour until golden yellow
2. Sieve the roasted flour through a thick sieve
3. Heat 1 kilogram honey and scald the flour with the boiling honey, pouring only 1/4 of the honey at first, adding the rest when the dough is formed
4. Add to the dough 2 tablespoons alcohol, 1 dekagram soda dissolved in a spoonful of water, and spices: a little new spice, finely chopped orange zest, and 5 grams cardamom
5. Knead the dough thoroughly on a board until it is pliable
6. Take pieces of dough the size of a walnut and form them into small cakes
7. Place them a finger's width apart on a greased baking sheet and bake in an oven until golden
