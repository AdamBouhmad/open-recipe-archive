## Ingredients

- sheatfish
- salt
- water
- coriander
- onion
- bay leaf
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Cook the sheatfish in salted water in which coriander, onion, bay leaf, and vegetables have been added
2. Some prefer to cook all these ingredients first, strain the mixture, and when cooled, pour it over the raw fish, in which it is then cooked
3. Whole cooked fish is placed on a platter surrounded by various dishes (potatoes, vegetables, etc.) and decorated variously
