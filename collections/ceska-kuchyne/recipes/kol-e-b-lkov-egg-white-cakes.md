## Ingredients

- 28 deciliters (8 tablespoons) flour
- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) fresh butter
- 6 tablespoons yeast (leavening)
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) thick sour cream
- 3 tablespoons ground sugar
- 12 egg whites beaten stiff
- Salt
- Jam or preserves for filling
- Chopped almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Crumble 1 kilogram fresh butter into 28 deciliters flour, mix in 6 tablespoons yeast, 7 deciliters thick sour cream, 3 tablespoons ground sugar and stiff egg white foam from 12 egg whites
2. Work the dough so that it stretches
3. When risen for a while, salt it, make small hats, fill with jam, brush with egg white foam and sprinkle with chopped almonds
