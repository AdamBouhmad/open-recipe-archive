## Ingredients

- 40-50 small crayfish
- salted water
- crayfish intestines
- 180 grams fresh butter
- 2 hard rolls
- 2 hard sardines
- 3-4 liters excellent meat soup
- 6 finely chopped mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- 2 cloves
- crayfish tails
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Remove intestines from 40-50 small crayfish, cook in salted water, remove meat from tails
2. Crush bodies and shells with 180 grams of fresh butter, 2 hard rolls and 2 hard sardines
3. Place on a baking sheet, pour with 3-4 liters of excellent meat soup to which 6 finely chopped mushrooms have been added
4. Salt and pepper, add chopped onion and 2 cloves, cook the soup for 2 hours
5. Strain, add crayfish tails to it and pour over fried roll
