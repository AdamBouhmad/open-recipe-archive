## Ingredients

- bass
- salt
- butter
- oyster sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the bass well, gut it, cut it in half along the back, skin it, remove the bones and cut the meat into small thin pieces, salt it and let it stew slowly in hot butter.
2. Then drain the butter, arrange the fish nicely on a dish and pour oyster sauce over it.
