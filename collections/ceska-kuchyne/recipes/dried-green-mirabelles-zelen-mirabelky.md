## Ingredients

- Developed, unripe mirabelles with soft pits
- Soft water
- Alum (small amount)
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pierce the mirabelles several times
2. Place them in soft water containing a little alum
3. Set the container on warm heat
4. Remove from heat as soon as they begin to boil, keeping covered
5. Transfer to cold water
6. Proceed as instructed for plums
