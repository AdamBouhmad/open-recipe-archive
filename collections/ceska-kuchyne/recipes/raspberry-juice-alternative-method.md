## Ingredients

- fresh raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place fresh raspberries in a pot with warm water on the stove.
2. Crush the raspberries while cooking and strain through clean cloth.
3. For half a kilogram of juice, add 375 grams (25 lots) of sugar, cook together and set aside so it continues to cook until the foam stops rising.
4. Let cool, pour into jars, seal well, place in boiling water for 5 minutes and store in a cool place.
