## Ingredients

- onion, cut into slices
- slices of smoked meat
- peppercorns
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- saffron
- wine
- sardine butter
- small piece of lemon peel
- juice from half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the onion into slices and braise it for a while with slices of smoked meat and a few peppercorns in butter
2. Sprinkle with flour and pour beef broth over it
3. Add a pinch of saffron
4. Strain
5. Add wine
6. Mix in sardine butter
7. Add a small piece of lemon peel and finally juice from half a lemon
