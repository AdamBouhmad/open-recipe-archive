## Ingredients

- asparagus
- parsley water
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut asparagus into pieces and let cook in parsley water until soft
2. Then take out the asparagus and put into a bowl
3. Fry the soup with a light butter roux, let it thicken and pour into the bowl over the cooked asparagus
