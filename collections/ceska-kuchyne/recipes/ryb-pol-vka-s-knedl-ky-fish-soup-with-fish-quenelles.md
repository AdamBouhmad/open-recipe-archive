## Ingredients

- two pieces of fish
- stock (recipe 39)
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- two tablespoons of butter
- one whole egg and one yolk
- a bit of flower
- a bit of white grated ginger
- a piece of lemon peel, finely chopped
- salt
- bread roll or white bread, grated

## Directions

1. Prepare the broth according to recipe 39
2. From two pieces of fish, trim the skin and remove all bones
3. Chop the fish meat finely and pound it with three hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar into a paste
4. Cream two tablespoons of butter, beat in one whole egg and one yolk
5. Add a bit of flower, a bit of grated white ginger, a piece of lemon peel finely chopped, and salt
6. Add the pounded fish meat and mix well
7. Add enough grated bread roll or white bread so that quenelles can be made from it
8. Pour them into the above-mentioned broth, but you must not thicken this broth until the quenelles are cooked
