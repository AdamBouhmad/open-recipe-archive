## Ingredients

- onion
- parsley
- butter
- 3 or 4 eggs
- one-eighth liter flour
- salt
- cooked potatoes, grated

## Directions

1. Fry onion and parsley in butter, beat with 3 or 4 eggs and one-eighth liter flour
2. Salt it, mix with suitable amount of cooked grated potatoes and bake in butter in a baking pan
