## Ingredients

- ripe currants
- sugar
- wine
- liquor

## Directions

1. On a dry, sunny day, pick the ripe currants, place in a wooden container and crush and mash them completely in it
2. Let stand to sour
3. Strain the soured juice and press it
4. Add sugar to taste, depending on how sweet you want the wine
5. Mix everything, add a little wine and good liquor, and let stand for at least six weeks again
6. As soon as the juice clarifies, pour into bottles, or if it is cloudy, transfer to other barrels, and only when it is completely settled, pour into bottles
