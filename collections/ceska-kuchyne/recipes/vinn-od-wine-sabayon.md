## Ingredients

- three and one-half deciliters good wine
- 14 decagrams sugar
- seven to eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add 14 decagrams of dissolved sugar to three and one-half deciliters of good wine and boil together
2. Let cool
3. Add seven to eight egg yolks
4. Mix together constantly until foamy
