## Ingredients

- Lungs
- Butter
- Onion
- Vinegar
- Wine, one and a half deciliters
- Spices
- Sauce from broth
- Lemon juice
- Roasted pastries
- Roasted larded liver

## Directions

1. Cut the lungs into noodles; on a baking pan brown the chopped onion with butter, add the lungs, pour a little vinegar and one and a half deciliters of wine and season with various spices; braise until all the wine evaporates, then pour in sauce from broth, add lemon juice, place roasted pastries around and roasted larded liver on top.
