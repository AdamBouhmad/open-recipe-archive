## Ingredients

- lamb hindquarters
- garlic
- salt
- fresh butter
- water
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Remove membranes and fat from lamb hindquarters.
2. Cut the legs at the joints and skewer them together.
3. Wash it clean, split the hindquarters and remove the intestines found there.
4. Rub garlic with salt and grease with this and fresh butter.
5. Add water and roast until golden.
6. Can be sprinkled with grated bread before it is completely roasted to brown it.
7. Those who do not like garlic may omit it.
