## Ingredients

- milk or water
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- buckwheat

## Directions

1. Place milk or water either on the stove or in the oven, add a piece of butter, sugar (this can also be omitted), and salt
2. Pour buckwheat into it and stir until it thickens
3. Finally add a little milk and let it bake in the oven for a while until cooked
4. Divide into portions or brush the whole thing with butter and sprinkle with sugar
