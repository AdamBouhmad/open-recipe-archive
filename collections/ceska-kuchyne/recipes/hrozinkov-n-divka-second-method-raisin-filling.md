## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters wine
- 10 dek sugar
- approximately 14 dek finely chopped almonds
- raisins
- sultanas
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Boil the wine with sugar
2. Add finely chopped almonds, raisins, and sultanas in the quantities stated
3. Add finely chopped lemon zest
4. When cooled, use for filling pastries
