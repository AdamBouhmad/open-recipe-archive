## Ingredients

- mushrooms sliced into strips
- butter
- lemon juice
- brown roux
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Braise mushrooms sliced into strips in butter
2. Add lemon juice and brown roux
3. Season with pepper
4. Add broth and cook
