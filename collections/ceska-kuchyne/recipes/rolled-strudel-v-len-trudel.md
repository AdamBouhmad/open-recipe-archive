## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 eggs
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- salt
- quark cheese
- egg or cream
- sugar
- raisins
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Make a well in flour on a board
2. Place in the well 2 eggs, 12 dekagrams butter and salt
3. Work the dough so it doesn't stick to the board
4. Let rest in a cool place
5. Meanwhile, thin the quark cheese with egg or cream
6. Add sugar and raisins
7. Roll out the dough thinly
8. Sprinkle with melted butter
9. Coat with prepared quark cheese
10. Carefully roll up or fold into quarters
11. Place on a greased pan, brush with butter on top and bake slowly
12. Serve sprinkled with sugar, either sliced or whole
