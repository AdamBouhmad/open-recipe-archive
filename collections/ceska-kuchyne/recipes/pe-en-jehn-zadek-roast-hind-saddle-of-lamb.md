## Ingredients

- lamb hind saddle
- butter or lard
- salt
- bread crumbs
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb hind saddle well and make small incisions on the underside of the legs so they bend and fit in the roasting pan
2. Salt it and place it meat-side down in the roasting pan on a piece of butter or lard
3. It is best to use a roasting pan with a grate at the bottom, or place two clean unstained wooden spoons on the bottom
4. Roast it with frequent basting and buttering
5. During roasting, add a little broth or hot water
6. Turn it over, sprinkle with bread crumbs, surround with pieces of fresh butter and roast until golden
7. Roast for one and a half to two hours depending on size
8. Cut into neat pieces, arrange on a platter and pour the strained meat juice over it
9. Lamb saddle of kid can be roasted the same way
