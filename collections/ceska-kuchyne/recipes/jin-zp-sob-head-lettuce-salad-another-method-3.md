## Ingredients

- head lettuce
- wine vinegar
- Provençal oil
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Break off the outer leaves of the head if young
2. Divide into quarters and rinse in several waters
3. Arrange on a plate
4. Season with salt
5. Pour wine vinegar and Provençal oil over it
6. Divide hard-boiled eggs in halves or quarters and arrange around the edge of the plate
