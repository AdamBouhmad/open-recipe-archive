## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 1/4 kilogram (1/2 pound) ground sugar
- a piece of vanilla or lemon zest
- 15 egg whites
- dried potato flour
- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) potato flour
- butter for greasing
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 15 egg yolks with 1/4 kilogram of ground sugar and a piece of vanilla or lemon zest for 1/4 hour
2. Add stiff snow (beaten egg whites) made from 15 egg whites
3. Sift dried potato flour and add it by the spoonful, 1/2 kilogram total
4. Mix the mixture and bake in a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with grated breadcrumbs
