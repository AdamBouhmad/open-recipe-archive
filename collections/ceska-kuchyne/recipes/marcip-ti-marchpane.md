## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1/2 pound ground almonds
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 egg whites
- egg white for brushing
- whole almonds
- white or red glaze for decoration

## Directions

1. Cook 1 cup sugar with 2 tablespoons water until thick
2. Add 1/2 pound ground almonds and mix well
3. Cook, then squeeze juice from 1 lemon into the mixture
4. When slightly cooled, add snow from 2 egg whites
5. Brush wafers with egg white and spread the paste on them
6. Border with whole almonds
7. Bake over moderate heat on a buttered sheet
8. When done, decorate with white or red glaze
