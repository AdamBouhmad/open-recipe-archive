## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) ground almonds
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- grated lemon zest
- 6 egg yolks
- snow from 4 egg whites
- butter dough
- egg for brushing
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Make the filling by mixing the ground almonds, sugar, grated lemon zest, egg yolks, and egg white snow, stirring for a quarter hour
2. Roll out the butter dough and cut into square pieces
3. Place the prepared filling on each piece
4. Moisten the edges with water, fold and press together
5. Brush the pastry pockets with egg
6. Bake on a floured baking sheet
