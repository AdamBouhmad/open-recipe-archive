## Ingredients

- half a pound of pork
- half a pound of veal
- a piece of beef fat
- two beaten eggs
- half a roll soaked in water and squeezed out
- two whole eggs
- salt
- ground cloves
- clean cloth
- cold water
- mushroom broth with mushrooms, champignons, or porcini

## Directions

1. Chop half a pound of pork, half a pound of veal, and a piece of beef fat together
2. Add two beaten eggs and half a roll soaked in water and squeezed out, chop everything together finely
3. Place on a plate, add two whole eggs, salt it, season with a bit of ground cloves, and mix and beat everything well
4. Soak a clean cloth in cold water, squeeze it out, place the mixture in it, tie it loosely, and cook it in broth for one hour
5. Remove it to a board, cut it into small four-sided pieces
6. Pour mushroom broth with mushrooms, champignons, or porcini over the pieces in a serving bowl
