## Ingredients

- Omental meat
- Rendered lard
- Raw pork blood
- Pepper
- Ground ginger
- Allspice
- Cloves
- Onion
- Pork lard
- Pork stomach

## Directions

1. Cut omental meat into cubes
2. Add rendered lard
3. Pour in raw pork blood
4. Season with pepper, ground ginger, allspice, cloves, and whole onion fried in pork lard
5. Mix everything together in a bowl
6. Stuff into pork stomach
