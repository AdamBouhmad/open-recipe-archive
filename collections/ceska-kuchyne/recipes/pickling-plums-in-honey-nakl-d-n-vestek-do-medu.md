## Ingredients

- plums
- ground cinnamon
- cloves
- honey

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the plums and place them in a clean barrel.
2. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon and cloves.
3. Pour honey over them and seal the barrel well.
4. Roll the barrel and then store it in the cellar.
5. Shake the barrel every day.
6. Let them be preserved for several months.
