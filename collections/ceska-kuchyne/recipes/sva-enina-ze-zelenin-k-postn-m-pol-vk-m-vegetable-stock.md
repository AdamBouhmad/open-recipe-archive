## Ingredients

- two carrots
- three onions
- leek
- head of celery
- all cut into slices
- piece of fresh butter
- half pot of peas
- piece of green parsley
- turnip
- fresh water

## Directions

1. Place two carrots, three onions, leek and a head of celery, all cut into slices, in a deep pot
2. Add a piece of fresh butter and let it fry until golden
3. Add half a pot of peas, a piece of green parsley and turnip
4. Pour fresh water over it and let it cook slowly for several hours, carefully skimming the foam
5. Remove from heat and let it rest quietly for half an hour
6. Slowly strain through a thick cloth into an earthenware pot without clouding the vegetable
7. Store in a cold place
8. This stock is suitable for every fast soup and for fast sauces
