## Ingredients

- Ripe, juicy apples
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take ripe, juicy apples, peel and cut them into quarters, placing them in water as you cut so they do not blacken in the air.
2. When all are cut, cook them in water until soft, then strain through thick cloth.
3. Then cook the juice with sugar, as described in the preceding recipe.
