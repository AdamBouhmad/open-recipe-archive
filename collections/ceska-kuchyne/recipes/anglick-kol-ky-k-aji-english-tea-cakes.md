## Ingredients

- 200 grams grated butter
- one kilogram fine flour
- salt
- two beaten eggs
- lukewarm milk
- 36 grams dissolved yeast

## Directions

1. Take 200 grams of grated butter
2. Mix with one kilogram of fine flour
3. Season with salt
4. Beat in two beaten eggs, lukewarm milk, and 36 grams of dissolved yeast
5. Work the dough thoroughly
6. Let rise
7. Roll out
8. Cut small cakes with an inverted glass from the dough
9. Bake until reddish
10. Brush with butter
