## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup of rice
- hot water
- 1 cup of hot water
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- a pinch of nutmeg
- 2 quarts of milk
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Wash half a cup of rice several times in hot water.
2. Place it in a pot and pour 1 cup of hot water over it, add a tablespoon of butter and a pinch of nutmeg.
3. Cover and let it cook.
4. When the water is almost evaporated, pour 2 quarts of milk over the rice, salt it, and when it comes to a boil, beat an egg yolk in it.
