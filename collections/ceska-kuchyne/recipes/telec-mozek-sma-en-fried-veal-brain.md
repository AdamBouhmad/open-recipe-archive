## Ingredients

- Veal brain
- Flour
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Soup broth

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes and blood from the brain, wash, dry, salt, coat in flour, egg and bread crumbs, fry, place in a soup bowl and pour soup over it.
