## Ingredients

- mutton kidneys
- cold water
- butter
- shallots
- mushrooms
- green parsley
- hot water
- brown broth
- flour
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the kidneys clean, let them drain, cut them in half lengthwise, and slice into thin strips.
2. Melt a piece of fresh butter in a pan, add four finely chopped shallots, several mushrooms, and green parsley, all finely chopped, then the sliced kidneys and let them stew until tender.
3. Remove the kidneys from the pan, add several tablespoons of hot water or, if available, brown broth and a little flour to the juices in the pan, and mix well.
4. Return the kidneys to the pan, season with salt, squeeze lemon juice into it, and let the kidneys stew a little longer, then serve them hot on a dish.
