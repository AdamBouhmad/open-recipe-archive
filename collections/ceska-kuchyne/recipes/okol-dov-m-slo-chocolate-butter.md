## Ingredients

- Half pound freshly beaten butter
- Three hard-boiled egg yolks
- Two small tablets grated chocolate
- Sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat half pound of freshly beaten butter with three hard-boiled egg yolks and two small tablets of grated chocolate; if you wish, you may also add a little sugar and a little ground vanilla.
2. This butter is served either with freshly baked rolls or spread on wafers.
