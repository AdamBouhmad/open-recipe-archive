## Ingredients

- herring fillets
- milk
- cooked potatoes
- cold veal roast
- pepper
- pickled cucumbers
- red beets, cooked
- cooked ham
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks, ground
- 1/8 liter of oil
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- vinegar
- salt
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Soak herring in milk for a day
2. Remove skin and bones and cut the herring
3. Add cooked and cut potatoes, cold veal roast, pepper, pickled cucumbers, cooked red beets, cooked ham, hard-boiled eggs and several tablespoons of capers
4. Mix everything together
5. Pour sauce made from 4 ground hard-boiled egg yolks, 1/8 liter of oil, 2 tablespoons mustard, vinegar, pepper, salt and beef broth
6. Decorate with small cucumbers, capers, hard-boiled eggs and similar
