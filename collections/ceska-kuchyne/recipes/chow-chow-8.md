## Ingredients

- half peck of green tomatoes
- one large head of cabbage
- dozen red bell pepper pods
- dozen green bell pepper pods
- dozen onions
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop separately: half a peck of green tomatoes, one large head of cabbage, a dozen red and a dozen green bell pepper pods, and a dozen onions.
2. Mix everything together thoroughly and salt lightly.
3. Put into a cloth bag and hang overnight to drain.
4. In the morning, squeeze dry with your hands.
5. Place in a deep bowl and pour cold vinegar over it.
6. Let it sit for six hours and squeeze dry again.
