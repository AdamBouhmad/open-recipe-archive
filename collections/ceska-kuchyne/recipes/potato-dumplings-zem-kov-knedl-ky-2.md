## Ingredients

- 250 milliliters milk
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- 60 grams (4 lots) potato flour mixed in cold milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat milk and cook butter in it
2. Add potato flour mixed with cold milk
3. Stir constantly over heat until dough no longer sticks to the pan
4. Cool completely
5. Add eggs, flour, and salt
6. Form small dumplings
7. Cook in salted water
8. Serve in soup
