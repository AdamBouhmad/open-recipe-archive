## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound calf's or beef liver
- 2 tablespoons carrot fat or butter
- marjoram
- lemon peel, finely chopped
- garlic, minced and mixed with salt
- clove
- pepper
- 2 whole eggs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop or mince the liver and remove all veins and sinews
2. Mix well with the fat or butter
3. Add marjoram, lemon peel, garlic, clove, and pepper
4. Beat in two whole eggs
5. Salt appropriately and mix in enough grated bread so the mixture is neither too thin nor too thick
6. Mix everything well together and form into quenelles
7. Cook in 2 quarts of boiling beef broth
8. Let simmer about ten minutes and serve in a bowl on chopped fresh parsley. Goose liver may be used instead of calf's or beef liver.
