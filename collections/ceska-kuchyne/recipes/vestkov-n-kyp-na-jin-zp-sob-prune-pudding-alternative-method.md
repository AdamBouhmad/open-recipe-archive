## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup sugar
- grated almonds
- lemon zest
- butter
- 1/2 pound dried prunes
- 2 grated houskas
- 6 egg whites
- rum

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 1/2 cup sugar until it froths
2. Add several grated almonds, chopped lemon zest, and a piece of butter
3. Cook 1/2 pound dried prunes, strain the liquid onto 2 grated houskas
4. Chop the prunes and add them with the houska to the beaten yolks
5. Finally fold in stiff peaks made from 6 egg whites
6. Pour the batter into a well-buttered mold
7. Bake for one hour in a water bath by setting the mold in a deep dish of hot water, cover well, and place in the oven
8. When the pudding is baked, turn it out, sprinkle with sugar, and sprinkle with rum
