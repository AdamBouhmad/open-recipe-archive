## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- salt
- 1 full cup wheat semolina
- butter
- sour sauce or sweet milk

## Directions

1. Take 6 eggs, whip well, salt slightly and sprinkle in 1 full cup wheat semolina, pour into a greased baking pan and bake in a hot oven
2. Sometimes serve the puff with sour sauce, sometimes preserve it in sweet milk
