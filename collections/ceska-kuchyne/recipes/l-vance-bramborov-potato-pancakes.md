## Ingredients

- six large raw potatoes
- six tablespoons sweet cream
- two eggs
- four tablespoons flour
- a little salt
- butter for frying
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Take six large raw potatoes and grate them, then press through a clean cloth
2. Add six tablespoons of sweet cream, two eggs, four tablespoons of flour and a little salt, and mix it all together
3. Then put one tablespoon of butter on a heated pan and put the pancakes on the pan with a spoon, turn them and fry them until red, then put on a plate and sprinkle with sugar
