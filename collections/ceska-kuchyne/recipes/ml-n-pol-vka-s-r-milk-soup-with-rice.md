## Ingredients

- rice
- hot water
- cold water
- milk
- sugar
- vanilla
- salt
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- milk
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Rinse a quarter pound of nice rice several times in hot water, then pour cold water on it and let it cool
2. Drain the water, put the rice in a deep pot, pour two quarts of good boiling milk on it
3. Add a quarter pound of sugar and if you wish you can also crush a piece of vanilla with it, salt lightly and let the rice cook until soft
4. Beat in a cup four or five egg yolks or two or three whole eggs with a little milk and a piece of fresh butter and pour into the cooked soup, which you must stir diligently
5. Pour into a dish and serve
6. For 6 people
