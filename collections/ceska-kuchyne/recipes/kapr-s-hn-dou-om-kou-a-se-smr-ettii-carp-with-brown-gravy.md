## Ingredients

- carp
- butter
- salt
- ground pepper
- ground cloves
- onion
- thyme
- white flour
- broth
- wine vinegar
- anchovies
- morel mushrooms

## Directions

1. Grease a pot well with butter, arrange the cleaned and cut into pieces carp in it, sprinkle with salt, ground pepper and two ground cloves.
2. Add a large onion finely chopped, a piece of thyme and let it stew.
3. Place a piece of butter in the pot and make a brown roux with some white flour.
4. Pour in a pint of broth or some meat soup and a little wine vinegar, mix well, let it cook and strain through a sieve.
5. Pour this sauce over the carp, add three cleaned and finely chopped anchovies, about twenty well-cleaned whole morel mushrooms and let it simmer a little.
6. Arrange the fish on a dish, surround it with morels and serve the sauce in a small bowl.
7. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a little soup or hot water.
