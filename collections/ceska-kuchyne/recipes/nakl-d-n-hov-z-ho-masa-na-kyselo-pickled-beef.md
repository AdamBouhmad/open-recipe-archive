## Ingredients

- beef
- vinegar
- water
- ground spices

## Directions

1. Parboil the meat so no blood remains, then remove the bones. Boil vinegar with water in equal parts, adding ground spices as used for gravy.
2. Let the meat cool so it does not steam, then the next day cut it into small pieces, about 2 to 3 pounds each. Place in a container and pour the cooled brine over it. Such meat will keep for a whole year.
