## Ingredients

- lamb brisket
- butter or lard (3-4 spoonfuls)
- parsley
- celery
- salt
- beef broth or hot water
- sardeles (5 nice ones)
- butter
- beef broth
- wine
- pickled salmon (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut lamb brisket into neat pieces
2. Place on a braising pan with butter or lard, chopped parsley and a piece of celery
3. Salt and let braise, occasionally adding a little beef broth or hot water
4. Wash five nice sardeles, remove the bones and mix the sardeles with butter
5. Make a light roux from 1 spoonful of butter, add the sardeles, pour in half beef broth and half wine and let it cook
6. You can also add a piece of pickled salmon
7. Then add the braised lamb and the strained juice from it
8. Let it cook a little more
9. Remove the meat to a platter and pour the sauce over it
10. Wine may be omitted
