## Ingredients

- Small tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Eggs
- Butter
- Flour
- Crackers
- Green parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel small tomatoes as noted in the previous recipe, cut them into thicker cakes, salt, pepper, season with lemon juice, dip them in thin batter made from eggs, butter and flour and fry in hot butter.
2. Or dip the prepared cakes in beaten eggs, coat in rolled crackers and fry in hot fat; you can also mix a little chopped fresh parsley into the eggs.
3. They are served either alone or as a garnish to various roasts.
