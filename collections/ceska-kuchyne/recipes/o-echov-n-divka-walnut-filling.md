## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters boiled sugar or good syrup or pleasant honey
- finely chopped peeled walnuts
- cinnamon
- allspice
- vanilla
- cloves
- orange zest
- lemon zest
- a tablespoon of good rum and wine

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar, syrup, or honey with constant stirring
2. Add finely chopped peeled walnuts until everything thickens
3. Season with cinnamon, allspice, vanilla, cloves, orange zest, and lemon zest
4. Add a tablespoon of good rum and wine
