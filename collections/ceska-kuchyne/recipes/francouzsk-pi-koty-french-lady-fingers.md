## Ingredients

- one bowl fine granulated sugar
- six egg yolks
- water
- five egg whites
- two bowls sifted flour

## Directions

1. Beat one bowl of fine granulated sugar with six egg yolks and a little water over heat until hot and thickened
2. Remove from heat and beat until completely cooled
3. Beat five egg whites into stiff meringue
4. Slowly fold in the meringue and on the end fold in two bowls of sifted flour
5. Put into molds or make on paper into finger-length pieces
6. Sprinkle with fine sugar and bake in a hot oven until done
