## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) sugar
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) ground almonds
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- 195 grams (13 lots) semolina
- snow from 12 egg whites
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg yolks with 24 dekagrams sugar
2. Add 9 dekagrams ground almonds and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
3. Add 195 grams semolina
4. Beat everything for half an hour
5. Slowly add snow from 12 egg whites
6. Pour into a greased form sprinkled with grated roll
7. Bake for one hour
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
9. Glaze by passing a hot spatula over it several times
