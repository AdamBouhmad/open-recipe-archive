## Ingredients

- half a cup butter
- three egg yolks
- one tablespoon ground sugar with vanilla
- one cup cream or sweet milk
- salt
- two cups flour
- a piece of pressed yeast or dry yeast soaked and drained
- curd cheese or poppy seed filling
- egg yolk for brushing
- sugar mixed with vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream half a cup of butter with three egg yolks
2. Add one tablespoon ground sugar mixed with vanilla, one cup cream or sweet milk, and salt
3. Mix in two cups flour and a piece of pressed yeast or dry yeast that has been soaked and drained, mixing well
4. Butter a baking sheet and spread half the dough on it
5. Brush with curd cheese or poppy seed filling
6. Cover with the remaining half of the dough and press the edges together with your fingers
7. Let rise for a longer time
8. Brush the cake with egg yolk and bake in the oven until golden
9. When cool, cut into squares and sprinkle generously with sugar mixed with vanilla
