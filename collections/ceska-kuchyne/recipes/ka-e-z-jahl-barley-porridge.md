## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter barley
- 1 liter milk
- 10 egg yolks
- salt
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) sugar
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Scald 1/4 liter barley
2. Cook in 1 liter milk and press through a sieve
3. When cooled, add 10 egg yolks, salt, 9 decagrams sugar, lemon zest rubbed with sugar
