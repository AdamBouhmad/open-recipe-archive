## Ingredients

- Four cups milk
- One small cup grated sweet chocolate
- Six egg yolks
- One cup sugar
- One tablespoon butter
- Cornstarch flour
- Six egg whites

## Directions

1. Heat four cups of milk and add to it one small cup of grated sweet chocolate.
2. Beat six egg yolks with one cup of sugar and one tablespoon of butter, pour the milk with chocolate into this, add cornstarch flour as in the preceding recipe and cook.
3. Finally when you remove it from heat, fold in the snow from six egg whites.
