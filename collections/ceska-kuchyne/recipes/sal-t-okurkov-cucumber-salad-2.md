## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- thick sour cream
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel nice cucumbers and either slice them thinly or grate them on a grater, salt them, pour vinegar and good thick sour cream over them, mix well and sprinkle with ground pepper
