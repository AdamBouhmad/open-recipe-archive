## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram peeled and dried almonds
- egg whites
- lemon zest
- orange zest
- finely crushed vanilla
- cinnamon
- clove
- finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Finely grate 1/2 kilogram of peeled and dried almonds with egg whites
2. Make snow from egg whites, to which lemon and orange zest are added
3. Add finely crushed vanilla, cinnamon, clove, and finely crushed sugar to the soufflé
4. Mix everything well and prepare as described for other soufflés
