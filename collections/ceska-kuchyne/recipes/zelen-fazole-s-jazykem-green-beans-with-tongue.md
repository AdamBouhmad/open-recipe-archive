## Ingredients

- Green bean pods
- Salt
- Butter
- Parsley
- Butter sauce
- Vinegar
- Spices
- Egg yolks
- Cream
- Smoked tongue

## Directions

1. Remove the tough parts from the beans and slice diagonally
2. Cook in salted water until tender, then strain onto a baking pan
3. Simmer with butter, chopped parsley, butter sauce, vinegar, pepper and spices
4. Before serving, pour over egg yolks mixed with cream and surround with smoked tongue
