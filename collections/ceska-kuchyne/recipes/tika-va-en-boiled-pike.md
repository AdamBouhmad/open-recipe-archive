## Ingredients

- pike
- salted water
- onion
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Scrape and clean the pike, removing innards
2. Bend it into an arch and tie it
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook for a quarter to half hour until done
4. Meanwhile, chop half an onion and fry it to golden color in butter
5. Carefully place the pike on a dish
6. Sprinkle with toasted bread crumbs and the fried onion
