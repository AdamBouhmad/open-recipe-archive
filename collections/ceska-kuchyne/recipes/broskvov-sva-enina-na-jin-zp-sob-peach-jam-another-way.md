## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- white sugar (equal amount to the peaches by volume)

## Directions

1. Take nice ripe peaches, wash them well and rub them so that all the fuzz comes off the skin.
2. Slice them into quarters or smaller pieces.
3. Take an equal amount of fine white sugar as peaches and cook both together for 5 or 6 hours with constant stirring.
4. Do not allow it to burn under any circumstances.
5. When it becomes syrupy, pour into jars.
