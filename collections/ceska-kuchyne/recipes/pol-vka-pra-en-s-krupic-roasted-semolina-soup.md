## Ingredients

- 2 litres water
- a little caraway seeds
- a piece of butter
- salt
- children's semolina

## Directions

1. Put a little caraway seeds, a piece of butter and a little salt into 2 litres of water
2. When it boils, strain the soup
3. Meanwhile, roast children's semolina until brown
