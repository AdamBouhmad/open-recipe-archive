## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams butter
- 24 decagrams beaten almonds
- 6 whole eggs
- 7 egg yolks
- snow from 7 egg whites
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- clove
- grated roll
- sugar
- raw cherries

## Directions

1. Mix 24 decagrams of butter, 24 decagrams of beaten almonds, 6 whole eggs, 7 egg yolks, snow from 7 egg whites, cinnamon, lemon zest, clove, grated roll, and sugar to form a paste
2. Fill the mold alternately with the mixture and raw cherries
3. When baked, sprinkle with sugar
