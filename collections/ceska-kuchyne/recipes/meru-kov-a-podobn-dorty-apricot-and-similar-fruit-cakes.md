## Ingredients

- well-cleaned and halved fruit
- sugar
- cinnamon
- pastry strips

## Directions

1. Prepare apricot, peach, and other fruit cakes as you would prepare plum cake
2. Use well-cleaned and halved fruit, either peeled or unpeeled
