## Ingredients

- Well-ripened medlars
- Sugar - crushed, equal measure to the pulp

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from well-ripened medlars
2. Place medlars on a dish
3. Rub and stir them several times daily so they become completely soft
4. Press very soft medlars through a sieve
5. Add crushed sugar equal to the pressed pulp
6. Mix everything together
7. Cook together
8. Store in containers
