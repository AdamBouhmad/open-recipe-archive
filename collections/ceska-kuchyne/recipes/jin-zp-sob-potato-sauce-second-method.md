## Ingredients

- potatoes
- beef broth
- marjoram
- flowers
- garlic
- fresh mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cook several scraped potatoes in beef broth
2. Strain the broth
3. Press potatoes through a sieve into it
4. Add marjoram
5. Add a little flowers and rubbed garlic strands
6. If using fresh mushrooms add a boletus for flavor
