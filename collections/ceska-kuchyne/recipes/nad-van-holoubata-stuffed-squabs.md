## Ingredients

- cleaned, plucked, and rinsed squabs
- butter
- eggs and egg yolks
- milk
- salt
- lemon zest
- chopped fresh parsley
- grated bread roll

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads of well-cleaned, plucked and rinsed squabs
2. Carefully remove the giblets and nicely lift the skin on the breasts for the stuffing
3. Rub butter, beat one egg and one yolk into it
4. Add two spoonfuls of milk, a pinch of salt, a little finely chopped lemon zest, a little chopped fresh parsley, and four spoonfuls of grated bread roll
5. Mix everything well
6. Stuff the squabs with this mixture
7. Tie the giblets shut with thread
8. Tie the legs to the tail ends
9. Place the squabs on a baking sheet on a piece of butter
10. Add a little water and roast them with frequent basting with the roasted juices until nicely done
11. Cut them into quarters, arrange nicely on a dish and pour the roasted juices over them
