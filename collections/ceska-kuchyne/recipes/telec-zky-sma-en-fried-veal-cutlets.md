## Ingredients

- Veal leg meat
- Flour
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Onion
- Butter
- Cauliflower, optional
- Anchovies
- Lemon, optional

## Directions

1. Cut meat from the veal leg to a thickness of two fingers, pound it, clean it, salt it, coat in flour, egg and bread crumbs, then fry until golden.
2. Alternatively, chop onion, let it soften in butter and place the cutlets in it for about two minutes. To both preparations, cooked cauliflower may be added; or prepare anchovy butter, coat the cutlets with it and let them brown, or add only lemon.
