## Ingredients

- two cups lukewarm water
- two cups sweet milk
- one ounce pressed yeast, or one piece of dry yeast
- buckwheat flour
- one teaspoon salt
- two tablespoons syrup
- half teaspoon baking soda dissolved in lukewarm water

## Directions

1. In the evening, take two cups of lukewarm water and the same amount of sweet milk, dissolve one ounce of pressed yeast in it, or soak one piece of dry yeast and gradually add enough buckwheat flour to make a thin batter, cover and let it stand overnight
2. In the morning, add one teaspoon of salt, two tablespoons of syrup and half a teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in lukewarm water, mix well and bake the pancakes on a hot fire
3. If you do not have milk, you can use water, but you can then add two eggs per quart of water, which you beat and add before baking with salt and soda, so that the milk is replaced
4. Syrup is added so the pancakes get a better color
5. From prepared buckwheat flour, pancakes are baked in the same way as from wheat flour
