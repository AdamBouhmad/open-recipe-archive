## Ingredients

- Cauliflower florets
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Pale roux
- Spices
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower florets and remove the green leaves
2. Cook in salted water to which fresh butter has been added
3. When tender, place on a dish
4. Make a pale butter sauce, strain it through the water in which the cauliflower was cooked, add spices, squeeze juice from one lemon and simmer until thickened
5. Place the cauliflower in the sauce
