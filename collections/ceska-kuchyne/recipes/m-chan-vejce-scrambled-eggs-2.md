## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt
- milk or sour cream (optional, one spoon per two eggs)
- chives (chopped)
- pepper (ground)

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a pan
2. Pour in the required number of beaten eggs
3. Season lightly with salt
4. Slowly stir the eggs so they do not stick to the bottom
5. When they thicken slightly, turn them out onto a plate
6. Sprinkle with chopped chives
7. Optional: add a little milk or sour cream, chopped chives or parsley, or ground pepper to the beaten eggs
