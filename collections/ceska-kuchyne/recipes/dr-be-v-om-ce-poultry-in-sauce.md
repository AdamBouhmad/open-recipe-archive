## Ingredients

- Poultry leftovers
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Pepper
- Allspice
- Salt
- Egg yolk
- Burnt sugar
- White wine
- Roasted or fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook together with onion, several bay leaves, a little pepper, allspice and salt.
2. When cooked, mix in a pale egg yolk, a piece of caramelized sugar and a little white wine.
3. Let it simmer further and strain over neatly cut poultry leftovers arranged on a baking pan.
4. Let the leftovers heat through in the sauce, arrange them on a platter, pour the sauce over them and garnish with roasted or fried potatoes.
