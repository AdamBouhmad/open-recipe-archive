## Ingredients

- good ripe apples
- wine
- sugar
- grated orange zest
- small raisins
- layered pastry dough

## Directions

1. Peel ripe apples, quarter them, and remove the cores
2. Cook them in a baking dish with a little wine and sugar with grated orange zest
3. Mix the cooked apples with small raisins
4. Spread on a layered pastry cake and bake together
