## Ingredients

- ripe raspberries
- very little water
- a little currant juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select the ripest raspberries and crush them by hand
2. Add very little water and mix thoroughly with a whisk
3. Cook the mixture
4. Pour the cooked raspberries through a sieve, press the juice through well and strain it
5. Add a little currant juice to it
6. Cook a sufficient quantity of sugar to the sixth degree
7. Pour the juice into the sugar, cook together until completely thickened, constantly skimming off the foam
8. Pour into containers, and when cooled, seal and store in a cool room
