## Ingredients

- sirloin
- bacon
- onion (sliced)
- pepper (6 grains)
- lard or butter
- well water
- breadcrumbs (grated)
- lemon (sliced into pieces)

## Directions

1. Clean the sirloin, remove all membranes and bones, pound it
2. Salt it and lard with bacon like hare
3. Place sliced onion, 6 grains of pepper, a piece of lard, bacon or butter on baking sheet
4. Baste with well water, place roast in it, cover
5. Slowly braise in oven until tender on all sides
6. Cut into equal portions and place on platter
7. Strain sauce from baking sheet over it, sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs
8. Border with lemon sliced into pieces before serving
