## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (half pound) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 whole eggs
- 8 egg whites for snow
- 8 rolls cut into cubes and sprinkled with sour cream
- a little lemon zest
- almonds cut into noodles
- sufficient amount of sugar

## Directions

1. Almonds cut into noodles are mixed with a sufficient amount of sugar
2. Bake either in a mold, or tie in a clean cloth and hang over steam above boiling water
3. Pour the hedgehog with a sauce
