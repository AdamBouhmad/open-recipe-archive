## Ingredients

- whole cocoa beans
- water or milk
- cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Roast whole cocoa beans like coffee with their shells
2. Pound them finely
3. Boil in water or milk
4. Mix with cream and sweeten like coffee
5. Use one teaspoon of cocoa per cup, though this can be adjusted to taste
