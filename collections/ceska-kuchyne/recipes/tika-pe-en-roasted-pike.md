## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- sardines, sliced into pieces
- sour cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Clean the pike and rinse it in several waters
2. Salt it and roll in an arch
3. Place on small sticks arranged crosswise on a baking sheet
4. Pierce it with sardines sliced into pieces
5. Spread with sour cream, place in the oven, and baste with sour cream until baked
6. Make eggs dressed in sour cream, place them around the fish, and serve warm
