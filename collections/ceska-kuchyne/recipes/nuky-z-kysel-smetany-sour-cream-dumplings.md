## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/4 liter of sour cream
- nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- flour (as needed)

## Directions

1. Mix eggs with sour cream, nutmeg, butter and flour to make a dropping batter
2. Shape dumplings with a spoon
3. Cook in soup or water
4. Serve in soup or with cooked fruit
