## Ingredients

- Ordinary dough for cakes
- Raisins
- Chopped almonds
- Sour cream with beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Make cakes from ordinary dough the thickness of a finger, form a sufficiently high edge, sprinkle with raisins and chopped almonds, pour sour cream in which eggs have been beaten
