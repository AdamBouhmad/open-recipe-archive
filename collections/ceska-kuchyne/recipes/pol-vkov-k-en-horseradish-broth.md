## Ingredients

- one good horseradish of medium size
- flour
- salt
- flower
- two lots butter
- piece of sugar
- half liter good beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean a good horseradish of medium size
2. Grate it
3. Place on a baking pan
4. Dust with flour
5. Add salt and flower
6. Add two lots of butter and a piece of sugar
7. Pour over half liter of good beef broth
8. Stir constantly while cooking
