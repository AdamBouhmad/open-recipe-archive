## Ingredients

- goose
- water
- salt
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Clean and eviscerate the goose thoroughly
2. Wash it in cold water, salt the surface and inside
3. Sprinkle the inside with a little caraway seeds
4. Place it on a roasting pan, pour a little water underneath
5. Put it in the oven and roast
