## Ingredients

- partridges
- lard
- sour cabbage
- onion
- broth
- wine
- partridge juices

## Directions

1. Braise the tougher partridges halfway in lard.
2. Meanwhile, braise the sour cabbage with fried onion, broth and wine.
3. Place the partridges into the cabbage and braise completely.
4. Pour the partridge juices over the cabbage and brown.
5. Surround with cut partridge pieces.
