## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar
- lakmus

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces, press out the juice while hot and cook with plenty of sugar until thickened, then pour into bottles while still hot.
2. If a little lakmus is added during the preservation of the juice, it will be colored nicely red.
