## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- veal
- ham
- pork
- butter
- bacon
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean the lettuce leaves, blanch them, and place in cold water, then arrange on a platter
2. Finely chop veal and spread on the lettuce leaves
3. Sprinkle with chopped ham
4. Fry with bacon and onion in butter
5. Before serving, slice into rounds
