## Ingredients

- two handfuls of freshly picked violets
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the calyxes from two handfuls of freshly picked violets, pour boiled and cooled vinegar over them and let stand for 24 hours, then strain the vinegar, pour into small bottles and serve with fish prepared in court-bouillon or with salad.
2. Alternatively, the violets can be exposed to the sun with fresh vinegar for 14 days.
