## Ingredients

- one pound veal meat from the ribs
- one quarter pound beef marrow
- one shallot or small onion
- fresh parsley
- lemon zest, finely chopped or grated
- salt
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter or lard
- rib bones
- lemon juice
- fried potatoes or vegetables

## Directions

1. From one pound of veal meat from the ribs, remove the bones
2. Remove sinews and tendons and chop finely with one quarter pound of beef marrow, one shallot or small onion, some fresh parsley, and finely chopped or grated lemon zest
3. Salt it
4. Mix well
5. Form into cutlets three fingers wide and as long as a finger
6. Dip in beaten eggs
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
8. Insert a well-scraped rib bone into each one
9. Fry in hot clarified butter or lard
10. Arrange on a plate
11. Drizzle with lemon juice
12. Surround with fried potatoes
13. You may also surround with vegetables
