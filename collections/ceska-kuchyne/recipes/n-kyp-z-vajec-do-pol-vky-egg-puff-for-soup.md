## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- Sour cream or milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Salt
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Mix 2 eggs with sour cream or milk, 2 tablespoons of flour, salt, and chopped parsley.
2. Slowly pour the mixture into the soup and cook.
3. Use 2 eggs per liter of soup.
