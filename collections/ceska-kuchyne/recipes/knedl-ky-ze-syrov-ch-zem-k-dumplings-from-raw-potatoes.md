## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- cold water
- hot rice porridge or semolina porridge or barley porridge
- milk
- eggs
- 2-3 fried bread rolls cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Grate potatoes
2. Drain water several times
3. Let stand in cold water overnight
4. Squeeze out water
5. Mix with hot rice, semolina, or barley porridge made in milk
6. Add beaten eggs and fried bread roll cubes
7. Make dumplings
8. Cook in salted water until they float to surface
9. Serve with meat
