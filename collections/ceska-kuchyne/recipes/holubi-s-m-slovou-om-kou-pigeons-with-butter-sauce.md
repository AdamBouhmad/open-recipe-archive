## Ingredients

- pigeon
- vegetables as for chicken
- butter
- dried mushrooms or rusk or lemon juice
- small dumplings, nuky, or cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeon into quarters
2. Add the same vegetables as for chicken and butter, braise until tender
3. Allow to cool, strain the pale roux through a sieve into the broth
4. Chop some dried mushrooms or add rusk or lemon juice, or make small dumplings, nuky, or add cauliflower
5. Simmer all together with the pigeon
