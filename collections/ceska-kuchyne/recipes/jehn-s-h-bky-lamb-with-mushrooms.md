## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or neck
- vegetables
- mushrooms
- butter
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Braise lamb shoulder or neck pieces with vegetables.
2. Press pale roux through a sieve into it.
3. Clean mushrooms, cut them, braise them in butter.
4. Chop green parsley and add with mushrooms to the meat.
