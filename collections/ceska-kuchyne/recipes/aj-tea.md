## Ingredients

- fresh water
- good tea

## Directions

1. Boil fresh water in a large pot
2. For every three cups of water, put one teaspoon of good tea into a teapot
3. Pour the boiling water over the tea
4. Leave it on a hot place where it will not boil for about 5 minutes
5. The longer it stands, the more bitter the tea becomes, as tannins from the tea leaves are extracted
