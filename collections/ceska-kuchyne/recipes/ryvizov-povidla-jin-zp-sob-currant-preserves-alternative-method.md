## Ingredients

- Fully ripe currants
- Sugar - 500g per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Select fully ripe currants
2. Mash in a container
3. Press through a sieve
4. Place juice on a baking pan
5. Add sugar according to taste and need
6. Cook slowly
7. Constantly skim the foam while cooking
8. Cook until thickened, taking care not to scorch
9. Store in stone containers covered with cloth and bladder
