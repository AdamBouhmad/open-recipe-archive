## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salted water
- eggs
- anchovies, cleaned and boneless
- capers
- smoked salmon, sliced thinly
- green parsley
- piquant, salad, or mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cook crayfish in salted water
2. Remove meat from tails and claws
3. Arrange tastefully on a plate
4. Surround with quarters of hard-boiled eggs
5. Around those arrange crayfish tails garnished with cleaned and boneless anchovies, capers, and thinly sliced smoked salmon, and green parsley
6. Pour piquant, salad, or mayonnaise sauce over everything
