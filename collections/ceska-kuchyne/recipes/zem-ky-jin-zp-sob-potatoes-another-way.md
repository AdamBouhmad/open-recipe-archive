## Ingredients

- chopped onion
- butter
- flour
- broth or water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- sour pickled cucumber

## Directions

1. Stew the chopped onion in butter
2. Add flour, pour in broth or water and vinegar
3. Add salt and pepper
4. Cook everything together
5. In this sauce stew potatoes either raw or cooked
6. May also add sliced sour pickled cucumber
7. Serves as a side dish for sausages and liver sausages
