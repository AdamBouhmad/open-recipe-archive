## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) cooked potatoes
- 24 decagrams butter
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar
- salt
- 12 decagrams raisins
- 6 whole eggs
- several spoonfuls of cognac

## Directions

1. Rub cooked potatoes with butter, sugar, and salt
2. Add raisins, beaten whole eggs, and cognac
3. Place in a greased mold
4. Bake or cook
