## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- two tablespoons of water
- one and a half tablespoons of corn starch flour
- salt
- one egg white
- one quart of milk for the sauce
- one tablespoon of cold milk
- one egg yolk
- three tablespoons of sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Bring one cup of milk to a boil
2. Mix two tablespoons of water with one and a half tablespoons of corn starch flour
3. Add a little salt and mix well
4. Pour into the boiling milk
5. Stir well and when it thickens, remove from heat
6. While the milk is cooking, beat one egg white into stiff snow
7. When the pudding is removed from heat, fold in the snow
8. Pour into small cups to cool
9. For the sauce: bring one quart of milk to a boil
10. In a cup, mix one tablespoon of cold milk, one egg yolk, and three tablespoons of sugar
11. Pour this into the boiling milk and stir so it does not scorch
12. It will thicken immediately; remove from heat
13. Add a few drops of vanilla
14. Chill the sauce
15. When serving, turn the pudding out of the cups onto plates and pour the sauce over it
