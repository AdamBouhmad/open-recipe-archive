## Ingredients

- 20 eggs
- sufficient finely crushed sugar
- grated lemon zest
- finest flour
- butter for greasing mold
- paper for the mold edge

## Directions

1. Take 20 eggs, mix well and add sufficient finely crushed sugar
2. Heat the mixture slightly, let it cool, and repeat this process several times
3. Mix the grated lemon zest with the finest flour and add to everything
4. Mix everything again
5. Fill a buttered cake mold with the prepared dough
6. Since this cake tends to overflow from the mold and rises greatly, place paper very high on the rim of the mold to prevent the dough from overflowing
