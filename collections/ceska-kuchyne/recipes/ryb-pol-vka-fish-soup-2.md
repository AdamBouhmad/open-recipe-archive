## Ingredients

- entrails of two fish, one male and one female
- stock (recipe 38)
- piece of green parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- white butter roux
- a bit of crushed flower
- white grated ginger
- fried bread roll or white bread cut into cubes

## Directions

1. From the entrails of two fish, one male and one female, carefully remove the intestines and gall bladder
2. Place the entrails in a pot and pour stock (recipe 38) over them
3. Add a piece of finely chopped green parsley and salt
4. Let it cook for an hour
5. Thicken it with a white butter roux
6. Add a bit of crushed flower and white grated ginger
7. Let it cook a little longer
8. Pour the broth into a bowl over fried bread roll or white bread cut into cubes
