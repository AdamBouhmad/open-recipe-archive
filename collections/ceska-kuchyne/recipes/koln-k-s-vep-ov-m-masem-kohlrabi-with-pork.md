## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- pork meat
- vegetables
- pork lard, diced
- sugar
- pepper
- rose hip puree

## Directions

1. Clean the kohlrabi, slice it into discs or dice it, pour boiling water over it and scald it.
2. Cook the pork meat separately with vegetables.
3. Strain the broth over the kohlrabi, to which diced raw pork lard and sugar may be added, or these may be omitted and pepper added instead.
4. Cook everything until the rose hip puree is strained through a sieve; add the pork meat and serve hot.
