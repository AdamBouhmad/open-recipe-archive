## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound large raisins
- white wine
- lemon zest
- sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash 1/4 pound of fine large raisins in cold water
2. Dry them well
3. Remove all the seeds from them
4. Chop them finely
5. Put them in a small pot with several teaspoons of white wine
6. Add 1/2 finely chopped lemon zest, a little pounded sugar, cloves and cinnamon
7. Let them cook a little and then cool
8. You can use this filling instead of preserved currants, in cakes or tarts
