## Ingredients

- goose
- salt
- vegetables (carrots, celery, parsley, cabbage, cauliflower)
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Clean the goose and cut it up
2. Place in a pot, salt it, add all vegetables and cook until soft
3. Strain the broth; arrange the goose on a platter and serve with horseradish
