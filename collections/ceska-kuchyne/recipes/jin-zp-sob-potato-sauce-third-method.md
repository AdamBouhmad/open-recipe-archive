## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- small onion
- flour
- butter
- beef broth
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Slice boiled potatoes
2. To fried onion on butter with flour pour in beef broth
3. Add pepper, salt and chopped parsley
4. When sauce is cooked add potatoes and simmer for a while
