## Ingredients

- lamb front quarter
- salt
- water
- three tablespoons butter
- flour
- lamb broth
- small onion
- fresh parsley
- lemon zest
- wine
- egg yolks or two whole eggs
- cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb front quarter well and boil it in salted water for a quarter hour
2. Transfer to cold water, let it cool, and cut into neat pieces
3. Make a thin roux from three tablespoons butter and the same amount of flour
4. Mix it with strained lamb broth
5. Add a small grated onion, a little chopped fresh parsley, a piece of finely cut lemon zest, and a glass of wine
6. Let it simmer to a smooth sauce
7. Add the cut lamb pieces and stew until tender
8. Remove the meat with a fork onto a plate
9. Beat four egg yolks or two whole eggs with a little cream in a cup
10. Mix this into the boiling sauce
11. Add a little lemon juice and pour the sauce over the meat
