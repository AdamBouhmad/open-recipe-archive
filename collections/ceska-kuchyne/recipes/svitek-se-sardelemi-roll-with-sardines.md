## Ingredients

- half liter milk
- quarter liter flour
- 8 eggs
- 12 dekagrams sardines, deboned (8 lots)
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Mix flour into half liter of milk, add 8 eggs, chopped sardines, 2 tablespoons chopped parsley and bake
2. This roll is ready very quickly
