## Ingredients

- pickled cucumber
- beef broth
- white wine vinegar
- green dill
- brown roux

## Directions

1. Slice the pickled cucumber into thin pieces
2. Put it in a small pot and pour a little beef broth and some white wine vinegar over it
3. Add a piece of finely chopped green dill and let everything boil
4. Brown the sauce with a brown roux, mix it well and let it cook
