## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- 14 decagrams (1/4 pound) sardels
- 14 decagrams butter
- Lemon juice
- Lemon wedges
- Mustard
- Sticks for baking sheet

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish and leave it whole, wipe with cloth, salt it.
2. Clean the sardels, remove bones and rub with 14 decagrams butter.
3. Coat the fish inside and out with the sardel mixture.
4. Place on sticks in a baking sheet placed crosswise so the fish doesn't fall to the bottom.
5. Squeeze juice from one lemon into the baking sheet.
6. Bake the fish well, place on a platter, strain the juice over it.
7. Can be garnished with lemon cut into quarters or serve mustard on a separate plate.
8. Carp, pike and catfish can be prepared this way.
