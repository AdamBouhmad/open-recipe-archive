## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 15 dekagrams flour (10 lots)
- 2-3 eggs
- 9 dekagrams flour (6 lots)
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Boil 3/4 liter of milk, mix in 15 dekagrams of flour so that a thick porridge forms that does not stick to the pot, and let it cool
2. Then mix in 2-3 eggs, 9 dekagrams of flour, salt it
3. Dip a spoon in boiling water and cut dumplings of any size with it, which are cooked in salted water for a quarter hour
