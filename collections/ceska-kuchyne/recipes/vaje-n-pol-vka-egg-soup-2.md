## Ingredients

- eggs
- 35 grams (2 lots) butter
- 3 spoonfuls grated roll
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat together eggs, butter, grated roll, salt and nutmeg
2. Slowly pour the mixture into the soup and boil it
