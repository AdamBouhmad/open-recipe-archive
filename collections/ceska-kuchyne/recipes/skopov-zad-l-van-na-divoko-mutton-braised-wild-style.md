## Ingredients

- mutton
- wine vinegar
- water
- thyme
- bay leaf
- pepper
- allspice
- onion
- lemon zest
- elder preserves or dark gingerbread
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash mutton, place on a baking pan
2. Add wine vinegar, water, thyme, bay leaf, pepper, allspice, onion, and lemon zest
3. Braise until meat is tender
4. Cut meat into pieces and make a pink gravy
5. Use either elder preserves or dark gingerbread and cook with the gravy
6. If bitter, add a piece of sugar
7. Strain sauce over meat and serve with potatoes around it
