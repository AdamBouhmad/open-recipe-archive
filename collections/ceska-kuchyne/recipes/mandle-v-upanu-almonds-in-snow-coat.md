## Ingredients

- egg whites
- crushed sifted sugar
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Whip egg whites into stiff snow
2. Add sufficient amount of crushed sifted sugar
3. Scald blanched almonds, dip in the snow
4. Place on paper and dry in oven
