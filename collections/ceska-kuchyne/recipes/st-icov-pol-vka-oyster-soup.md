## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 1 quart of oysters
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- salt
- pepper
- small crackers

## Directions

1. Let a quart of milk cook and be careful that it does not burn.
2. When the milk begins to boil, add a quart of oysters, a tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper.
3. Remove from heat before the milk comes to a boil again, as soon as the oyster edges begin to curl.
4. Serve with small crackers.
