## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- fresh butter
- yeast
- sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 yolks
- salt
- sweet sour cream
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare dough from flour, butter, yeast, sugar, egg, yolks, salt, and sour cream
2. Let dough rise for a while
3. Roll out and form into rings
4. Let rise again on stove
5. Brush with yolk, sour cream, and butter, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Bake until golden
