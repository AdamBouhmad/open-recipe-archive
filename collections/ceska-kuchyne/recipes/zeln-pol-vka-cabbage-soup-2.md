## Ingredients

- fresh head of cabbage
- salt
- a little cauliflower
- 15 grams (1 lot) sugar
- lard, butter, or cracklings

## Directions

1. Cut a fresh head of cabbage, salt it, add a little cauliflower and 15 grams (1 lot) of sugar
2. Cook it either with lard, butter, or cracklings until the cabbage is fully cooked
3. Thicken with roux, then either strain the soup and add peeled potatoes, or leave it unstrained and add sliced bread
