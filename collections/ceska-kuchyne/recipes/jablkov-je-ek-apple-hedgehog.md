## Ingredients

- good apples
- almonds
- 8 egg whites
- 8 tablespoons ground sugar
- juice of one lemon
- sugar
- vanilla, cinnamon, or two crushed cloves mixed with sugar

## Directions

1. Peel, wash the apples, dry with a clean cloth and place on a deep plate
2. Blanch almonds, peel, cut into noodles, dry in a warm place and stick into the apples
3. Make stiff snow from 8 egg whites, mix with 8 tablespoons ground sugar and juice of one lemon; mix everything well and pour over apples
4. Bake slowly in an oven and sprinkle with sugar mixed with vanilla, cinnamon, or two crushed cloves
5. Serve on the plate on which it was baked
