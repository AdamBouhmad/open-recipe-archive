## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 6 dekagrams sugar
- lemon peel
- 5 egg yolks
- potato or wheat flour
- salt
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 liter of milk with 6 dekagrams of sugar that has been rubbed on lemon peel
2. Add 5 egg yolks
3. Add a spoonful of potato or wheat flour
4. Add salt and several spoonfuls of cold milk
