## Ingredients

- cabbage head
- hot water
- onion
- good vinegar
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the cabbage head into small strips, pour hot water over them which you then squeeze out and drain, and if desired add a little onion cut into strips
2. Cook good vinegar on a pan, add a piece of butter, salt and put the blanched cabbage in it, let it braise under a lid on high heat for about ten minutes, stirring frequently
3. Remove the braised cabbage to a dish, pepper it and let it cool
