## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- butter
- salt
- sweet or sour cream

## Directions

1. Slice cooked peeled potatoes
2. Brush with butter in a baking dish
3. Season with salt and bake for a while
4. When they begin to brown, pour sweet or sour cream over them
5. Stew for quarter hour
