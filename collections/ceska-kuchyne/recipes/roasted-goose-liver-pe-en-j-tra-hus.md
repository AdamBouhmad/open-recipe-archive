## Ingredients

- large goose liver
- milk
- salt
- butter
- truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Place large goose liver in milk and let it rest. Cut into round slices, salt them, place on a baking sheet lined with butter in which they roast for several minutes, turning frequently. Arrange attractively on a plate and pour truffle sauce over them.
