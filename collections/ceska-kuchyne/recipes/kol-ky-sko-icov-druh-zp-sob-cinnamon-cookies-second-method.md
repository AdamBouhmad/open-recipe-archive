## Ingredients

- 500 grams fine, sifted, slightly warmed flour
- 500 milliliters lukewarm sour cream
- 50-60 grams dissolved yeast
- 2 eggs and 2 egg yolks
- 200 grams fresh butter
- salt
- sugar to taste
- 2 fresh eggs
- 16 grams finely crushed cinnamon
- 180-200 grams crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Work flour, sour cream, yeast, eggs, egg yolks, butter, salt, and sugar into dough
2. Let dough rise
3. Roll out risen dough and cut out cookies
4. Let them rise again
5. Bake on a buttered sheet slowly until golden
6. Meanwhile, beat 2 fresh eggs with 16 grams finely crushed cinnamon mixed with 180-200 grams crushed sugar
7. Brush the hot freshly baked cookies with this mixture
8. Return to oven to dry
