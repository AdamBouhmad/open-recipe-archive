## Ingredients

- two spoons of butter
- one egg
- two egg yolks
- flour
- snow from two egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream two spoons of butter, beat in one egg and two egg yolks, mix well together.
2. Add flour until it is as thick as batter, add the snow from two egg whites.
3. Butter a shallow pot, pour the batter into it and bake until golden, then turn it out onto a board and let it cool.
4. Cut into small cubes (or cut out with a small mold), place in a soup bowl and pour good beef broth over it.
