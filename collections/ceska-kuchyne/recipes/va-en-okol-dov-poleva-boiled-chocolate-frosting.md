## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablet bitter chocolate
- 1/2 small cup milk or cream
- 1/2 small cup sugar

## Directions

1. Grate 1/2 tablet of bitter chocolate
2. Cook it with 1/2 small cup of milk or cream and 1/2 small cup of sugar
3. Cook until it thickens into a porridge
