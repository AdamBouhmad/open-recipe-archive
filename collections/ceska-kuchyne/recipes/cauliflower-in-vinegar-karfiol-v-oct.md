## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and divide into small florets
2. Remove the skin from the stalks
3. Cook the cauliflower in salted water
4. Remove and place on a sieve to dry
5. Place on a dish
6. Pour with boiling vinegar
7. On the second day, boil the strained and water-diluted vinegar again and pour over the cauliflower
8. Place the cauliflower into jars
9. Cover with boiled vinegar
10. Tie well so air cannot enter inside
