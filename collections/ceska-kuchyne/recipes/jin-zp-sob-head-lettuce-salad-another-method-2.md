## Ingredients

- head lettuce
- wine vinegar
- salt
- bacon
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Remove outer leaves from head
2. Cut lettuce into quarters and salt
3. Pour wine vinegar over it
4. Cut and melt bacon
5. Pour bacon over the lettuce
6. Top with sliced hard-boiled eggs
