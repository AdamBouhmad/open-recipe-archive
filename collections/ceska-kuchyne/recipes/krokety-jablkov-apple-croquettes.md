## Ingredients

- good apples
- white wine
- grated lemon zest
- chopped almonds
- vanilla (crushed)
- egg
- dry bread crumbs
- butter for frying
- sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a compote from good apples, white wine, and grated lemon zest
2. Strain it
3. Mix in chopped almonds and a little crushed vanilla
4. Spread the mixture on small omelets
5. Roll them, fold the ends, brush with egg
6. Roll in crushed bread crumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Serve with sauce
