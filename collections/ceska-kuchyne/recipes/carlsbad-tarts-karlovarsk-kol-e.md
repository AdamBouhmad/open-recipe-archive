## Ingredients

- six ounces fresh butter
- eight egg yolks
- two cups flour
- four heaping tablespoons powdered sugar
- fine zest from half a lemon
- one cup milk
- a piece of yeast mixed earlier with a little milk and a piece of sugar and allowed to rise
- salt
- egg white, stiffly beaten
- powdered sugar for sprinkling
- preserved cherries or sour cherries

## Directions

1. Cream six ounces of fresh butter with eight egg yolks until foamy
2. Add two cups flour, four heaping tablespoons powdered sugar, fine lemon zest from half a lemon, one cup milk, and a piece of yeast that has been mixed earlier with a little milk and a piece of sugar and allowed to rise
3. Add a little salt and let rise in a warm place
4. Line a baking sheet with clean paper and brush with melted butter
5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto it in mounds about the size of a walnut
6. Spread them with a knife to about a finger's thickness
7. Brush generously with stiffly beaten egg white from a height
8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
9. Place a preserved cherry or sour cherry in the center of each
10. Bake in the oven
11. Note: only sprinkle with sugar as many tarts as will fit in the oven at once, otherwise the snow will become watery
