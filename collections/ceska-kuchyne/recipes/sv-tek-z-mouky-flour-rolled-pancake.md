## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 3 decagrams butter
- flower
- flour
- salt
- 5 egg whites
- butter
- grated bread roll
- cream sauce or horseradish with sour cream

## Directions

1. Cream 6 egg yolks with 3 decagrams of butter, flower and salt.
2. Add as many tablespoons of flour as there are egg yolks.
3. Make stiff snow from 5 egg whites and mix everything.
4. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated bread roll.
5. Pour the batter into it so it is only two fingers from full.
6. Bake it, carefully remove it and serve with prepared cream sauce or sour cream horseradish.
