## Ingredients

- goose giblets, especially the stomach
- carrots
- celery
- parsley
- cabbage
- cauliflower
- salt
- one or two eggs
- white roux
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the giblets, especially the stomach, cut them up, and place in water
2. Add carrots, celery, parsley, cabbage, cauliflower, and salt; cook until completely soft
3. Allow to cool
4. Make thin noodles from one or two eggs
5. Strain the broth in which the giblets were cooked onto a separate baking pan
6. Add a piece of white roux, strain it, and cook the noodles in the boiling broth
7. For a sour taste, add lemon juice
8. Peel the stomach and cut into small pieces, then add with the remaining giblets to the sauce
