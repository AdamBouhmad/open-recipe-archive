## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Chopped or finely sliced ham
- Sliced onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sour cream
- Fresh parsley or chives
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with some chopped or finely sliced ham
2. Add some sliced onion that has been cooked in fat
3. Add salt, pepper and sour cream if desired
4. Add some chopped fresh parsley or chives
5. Cook the omelette in butter or lard in the usual way
6. Serve alone or surround with spinach or other vegetables
