## Ingredients

- completely ripe strawberries
- whipped cream foam
- finely ground sugar with vanilla and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Select completely ripe strawberries and press them through a fine sieve so that only the juice passes through and the skins are removed
2. Add stiff whipped cream foam, finely ground sugar with vanilla and cinnamon
3. Mix everything perfectly and pour as a pleasant cooling drink into glasses
