## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- ground pepper
- sage leaves
- butter
- breadcrumb rind, grated
- lemon, cut into quarters

## Directions

1. Skin the eel, clean it, dry it and cut it into pieces the length of a finger
2. Sprinkle it with salt and ground pepper and let it rest for an hour
3. Skewer three or four pieces at a time on thin wooden skewers
4. Place a sage leaf next to each piece of eel
5. Brush the cleaned grill with butter, place the prepared eel on it and broil it on both sides until brown
6. Sprinkle it with grated breadcrumb rind
7. Remove from skewers, arrange it on a platter
8. Surround it with lemon cut into quarters
9. Serve hot
