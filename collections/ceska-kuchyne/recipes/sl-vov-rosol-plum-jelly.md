## Ingredients

- Plums, wild or cultivated
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Shell the plums, wild or cultivated, put them in a pot, add a little water and cook until soft.
2. Then strain the juice and cook it with sugar, as with grape jelly.
3. If they are sweet, they do not require as much sugar.
