## Ingredients

- two calf brains
- one medium onion
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- parsley
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Place two brains in lukewarm water for half an hour
2. Remove all membranes from the brains and boil them in cold water
3. On a small pan, steam finely chopped medium onion with one tablespoon butter
4. Salt the brains and season with a pinch of parsley
5. Place the brains on the onion, mix well
6. Before serving, mix four beaten eggs into the mixture
