## Ingredients

- three and one-half deciliters of strawberries
- sugar
- one and one-half deciliters of beef broth
- one and three-quarters deciliters of wine
- pale roux
- burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Strain three and one-half deciliters of strawberries through a sieve
2. Boil the juice with a piece of sugar
3. Add one and one-half deciliters of beef broth and one and three-quarters deciliters of wine
4. Strain a pale roux through a sieve into the mixture
5. Add a piece of burnt sugar
