## Ingredients

- wild boar breast
- vinegar
- butter
- sauce from the marinade
- meat broth
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Marinate the breast in vinegar for one to two days.
2. Roast in butter.
3. During roasting, baste with the marinade and good meat broth.
4. The breast can also be prepared with sour cream like hare.
