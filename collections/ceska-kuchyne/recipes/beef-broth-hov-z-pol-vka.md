## Ingredients

- Fresh, not overly fatty beef meat (1/2 kilogram per liter)
- About half the amount in veal meat
- Several chopped veal and beef bones
- Salt
- Vegetable roots

## Directions

1. Do not wash the meat, only wipe it clean with a cloth
2. Place the meat and bones in cold water
3. Bring to a gentle boil over mild heat
4. Remove the foam, as the broth will not be clear otherwise
5. Once the foam has been removed, salt the broth
6. Add vegetable roots
7. Simmer gently for several hours until the broth is strong
8. Skim off the fat
9. Strain the broth through a sieve
