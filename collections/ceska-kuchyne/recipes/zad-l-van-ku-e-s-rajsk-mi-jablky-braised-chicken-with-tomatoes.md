## Ingredients

- chicken
- vegetables
- Austrian wine
- vinegar
- pepper
- new spices
- cloves
- sugar
- tomatoes or tomato preserve
- butter
- flour
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Divide chicken into quarters and braise with vegetables
2. Strain broth and add one deciliter Austrian wine, 3 spoonfuls vinegar, several peppercorns, new spices, 3 cloves, piece of sugar and 3 tomatoes or a piece of tomato preserve
3. Boil well together
4. Add light butter roux with piece of burnt sugar
5. Strain sauce over chicken
6. On dish make a wreath of fried potatoes around chicken and strain sauce over it
