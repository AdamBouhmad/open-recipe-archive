## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the cherries, remove the pits, crush them and let them lie for three to four days in an open place, then press out the juice and pour it into strong, clean bottles, cork and tie them.
2. Wrap the bottles in hay, place them in a container filled with water and put on warm stove so that not only the water but also the juice in the bottle is preserved, then allow to cool gradually, clean the bottles and store in a dry, cool room.
3. This juice can also be used to make ice cream, to which sugar is added as desired.
