## Ingredients

- 4 to 6 slices of white bread
- salted water
- 3 eggs
- sweet sour cream
- a piece of fresh butter
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut 4 to 6 slices of white bread and let them cook in salted water for about a quarter hour.
2. Beat 3 eggs in sweet sour cream and stir this mixture into the boiling soup with a piece of fresh butter.
3. Season the soup with a little nutmeg and serve it in a soup tureen.
