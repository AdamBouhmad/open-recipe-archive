## Ingredients

- ripe currants
- crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the ripest currants and press to extract the juice
2. Add crushed sugar equal to the amount of juice
3. Cook in several strong boils for at least three hours without stopping until completely thickened
4. Fill into small glasses and store on ice
