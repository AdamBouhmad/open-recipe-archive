## Ingredients

- five quarts peeled ripe tomatoes
- six quarts trimmed and cored apples
- sugar (6 pounds)
- ground cloves (two tablespoons)
- ground cinnamon (three tablespoons)

## Directions

1. Cook five quarts of peeled ripe tomatoes and six quarts of trimmed and cored apples separately into a thick pulp.
2. Mix them together and add 6 pounds of sugar, two tablespoons of ground cloves and three tablespoons of ground cinnamon.
3. Cook for about another hour.
