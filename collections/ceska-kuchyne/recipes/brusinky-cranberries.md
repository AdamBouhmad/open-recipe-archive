## Ingredients

- cranberries (large and small)
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select the best and largest cranberries and place in a pot, cover with a lid
2. Place sorted smaller cranberries in another container
3. Bake the cranberries but they must not be too soft - both large and small must be equally soft, then press out the juice
4. For the juice, add half sugar, boil everything together until thick, constantly removing the foam as it rises
5. Pour the boiled juice over the berries, mix, and when everything has cooled, seal in the containers into which it was poured
