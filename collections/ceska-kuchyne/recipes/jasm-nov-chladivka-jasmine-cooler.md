## Ingredients

- sugar syrup
- jasmine water

## Directions

1. Prepare jasmine cooler exactly as rose cooler, only instead of rose water add jasmine water to it
2. To obtain jasmine water: pour hot sugary syrup over jasmine flowers and let them steep in a covered container for several hours, then strain
