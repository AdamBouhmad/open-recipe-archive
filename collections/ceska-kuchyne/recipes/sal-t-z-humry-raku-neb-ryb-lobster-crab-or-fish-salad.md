## Ingredients

- meat from cooked fresh lobster or canned lobster
- mayonnaise
- lobster roe (if available)

## Directions

1. Tear the meat from cooked fresh or canned lobster into pieces with a fork
2. Prepare salad the same way as chicken salad
3. If the lobster had roe, you can use it to garnish the top of the salad
4. The same preparation is used for salad made from cooked fish, crayfish and crabs
5. Another fish salad can be made from shredded fish meat mixed with chopped sardines and capers and layered with sliced potatoes and beetroot; it can be dressed with either mayonnaise, remoulade, or simply oil and vinegar and garnished in the usual way
