## Ingredients

- half pound almonds
- egg white
- one pound powdered sugar
- one tablespoon vanilla extract
- vanilla sugar for glaze

## Directions

1. Prepare in exactly the same way as cinnamon stars
2. Instead of cinnamon, add one tablespoon vanilla extract
3. Brush baked stars with vanilla sugar
