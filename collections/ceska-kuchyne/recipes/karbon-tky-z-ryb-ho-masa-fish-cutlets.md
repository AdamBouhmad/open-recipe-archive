## Ingredients

- fish meat scraps
- onion
- parsley
- thyme
- butter
- various spices
- water
- wine
- vinegar
- white sugar
- burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Remove bones from the fish meat scraps and place on a baking sheet with onion, parsley, thyme, butter, various spices and a spoonful of water.
2. Braise until brown.
3. Pour a little wine and vinegar over it and make a liaison.
4. Add a piece of white sugar and a piece of burnt sugar.
