## Ingredients

- Plucked woodcock
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Juniper berries
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- Butter
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcock and cook it, then salt it
2. Place it in vinegar with onion, juniper berries, pepper and bay leaf
3. When it has lain in all of this, roast it in butter
4. Braise it completely in sour cream mixed with vinegar
