## Ingredients

- two quarts of dry flour
- one cupful of melted cooled butter
- one small cupful of fine sugar
- four egg yolks or two whole eggs
- a little pounded flower
- a piece of finely cut lemon zest
- a little salt
- a piece of pressed yeast
- lukewarm cream or milk
- curd cheese, poppy seed, fruit preserves, sugar with cinnamon, fruit or jam for filling

## Directions

1. Sift two quarts of dry flour onto a board, make a hollow in the middle, pour into it one cupful of melted cooled butter, add one small cupful of fine sugar, four egg yolks or two whole eggs, a little pounded flower, a piece of finely cut lemon zest, a little salt, a piece of pressed yeast dissolved with as much lukewarm cream or milk to make a loose dough.
2. Work it well until the dough no longer sticks to the spoon, let it rise in a warm place, then cut small pieces from it, roll them out, fill with either curd cheese, poppy seed, fruit preserves, sugar with cinnamon, fruit or jam, wrap them, place one next to the other in a buttered pan, let them rise a little more and then bake in the oven.
