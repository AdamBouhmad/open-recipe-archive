## Ingredients

- 2 or more beef ribs
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- fresh parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Take two or more beef ribs, cut off the backbone, separate the individual ribs and cut them into pieces about six fingers long, being careful not to let the meat slip off. Lard the meat on the ribs slightly, salt, pepper, and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley.
2. Heat a piece of butter in a skillet, place the prepared ribs in it and let them brown slightly on both sides. Cover the skillet, place in the oven and let the ribs finish cooking. When done, remove to a platter, pour over the strained gravy from which all fat has been completely removed, and arrange fried potatoes around it.
