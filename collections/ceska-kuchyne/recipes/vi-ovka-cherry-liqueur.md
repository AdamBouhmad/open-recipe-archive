## Ingredients

- Ripe, cleaned cherries
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Allspice
- Lemon peel
- Orange peel
- Sugar
- Good spirits (kořalka)

## Directions

1. Remove pits from ripe, cleaned cherries and place them in large bottles
2. On each layer of cherries, add various spices: cloves, pepper, cinnamon, allspice, lemon peel, and orange peel, with plenty of sugar
3. Pour good spirits over everything
4. Cover the bottle opening with clean cloth and leave in the sun for some time
5. Strain the cherry liqueur into special bottles and seal firmly to prevent evaporation
