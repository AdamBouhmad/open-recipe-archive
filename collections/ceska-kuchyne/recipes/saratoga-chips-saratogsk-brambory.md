## Ingredients

- raw peeled potatoes
- salt water
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut peeled raw potatoes into slices with a kitchen mandoline, or if you do not have one, carefully with a knife so that all slices are as equal and thin as possible
2. Place them in salt water for half an hour
3. Remove them and dry them well with a cloth; the better you dry them, the sooner they will be fried and the lighter they will be
4. Heat plenty of lard in a pot as for frying doughnuts and when it is quite hot, fry the dried potatoes until golden, which takes only a few moments
5. Remove the fried potatoes with a skimmer and place in a dish; when all are fried, if they are not salty enough, salt them
