## Ingredients

- tragacanth flour
- crushed sugar
- crushed cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix tragacanth flour with crushed sugar and a bit of crushed cinnamon
2. Work into a dough
3. Shape small thin sticks from the dough
4. Let dry completely
5. Place in a pot or small pan and sugar as described for almonds
6. Sugared cinnamon can also be colored in various ways
