## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- onion
- lemon peel
- capers
- broth or water
- aspic
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean a marinated or properly aged venison haunch, lard it with bacon, salt and pepper it
2. Place on a baking sheet with thyme, bay leaf, 4-5 sliced onions, lemon peel, and capers
3. Add 3 tablespoons of broth or water
4. Turn it so it browns evenly
5. Slice it and arrange on a serving dish
6. Surround with aspic prepared as for boar, garnish with lemon slices
7. Sauce can be served separately
