## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo peeled almonds
- egg white
- 1/4 kilo ground sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Grate and rub 1/2 kilo peeled almonds with egg white
2. Add 1/4 kilo ground sugar with vanilla
3. Mix with egg white snow
4. Spread onto wafers
5. Bake on rounded wood forms
