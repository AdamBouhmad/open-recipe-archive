## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cut off the upper green part of nicely cleaned asparagus and cook it in salted water
2. Drain the water, let the asparagus drain, arrange it on a plate, pour with oil and vinegar and sprinkle with a little pepper
3. You can make a border of cauliflower cooked in salted water, broken into small florets and moistened either in oil and vinegar or in remoulade
