## Ingredients

- large liver
- truffles
- bacon
- goose liver puree
- woodcock or wild duck puree
- game meat
- meat aspic

## Directions

1. Lard large liver with truffles, braise in pieces of bacon until tender, let cool and cut into pieces. Place in a small mold lined with truffles in layers, each layer covered with meat aspic. Or the liver can be braised until tender, mashed and mixed with puree of woodcock or wild duck and finely chopped roasted game meat, lined with truffles in molds with meat aspic layered on top, and chilled on ice.
