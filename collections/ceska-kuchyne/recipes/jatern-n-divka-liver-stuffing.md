## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- parsley
- mushrooms
- bread roll
- milk
- eggs or egg yolks
- cooked goose or turkey livers

## Directions

1. Braise finely chopped onion in butter
2. Add chopped parsley and mushrooms
3. Moisten bread roll in milk, squeeze out, and crumble
4. Mix everything with eggs or egg yolks to keep moist
5. Chop cooked goose or turkey livers finely
6. Mix with the stuffing
