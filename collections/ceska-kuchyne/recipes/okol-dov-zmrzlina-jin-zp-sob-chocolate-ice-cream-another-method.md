## Ingredients

- One liter cream
- Vanilla
- 250 grams sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 250 grams fine chocolate

## Directions

1. Cut vanilla into very small pieces into one liter cream
2. Cook cream with 250 grams sugar
3. Strain it
4. Add 12 egg yolks and 250 grams fine chocolate
5. Whip everything over moderate heat
6. Place in ice until ice cream forms from it
