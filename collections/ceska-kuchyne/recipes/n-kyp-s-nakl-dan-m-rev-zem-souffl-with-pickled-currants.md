## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 3 egg yolks
- roll soaked in milk
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- 6-8 tablespoons preserved currants
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- snow from 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream 12 dekagrams butter.
2. Add 3 egg yolks, a roll soaked in milk and wrung out, 9 dekagrams sugar, cinnamon, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, and 6-8 tablespoons preserved currants.
3. Finally add 1/4 liter sour cream and snow from 5 egg whites.
4. Bake for half an hour.
