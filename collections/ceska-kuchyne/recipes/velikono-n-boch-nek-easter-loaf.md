## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- eggs
- milk
- yeast
- sugar
- salt
- lemon zest
- ground cloves or anise

## Directions

1. Make the dough in the same way as for Christmas twist
2. Make two larger or three smaller loaves
3. Brush them with beaten egg
4. Make a cross on each surface with a knife
5. Let them rise and then bake slowly in the oven
