## Ingredients

- green, small, unripe lemons
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut green, small, unripe lemons into small strips.
2. Place them in water into which a bit of lemon juice has been dripped.
3. Work them in your hands until they soften, remove them and place them in a dish.
4. Pour cleaned cold sugar over them.
5. On the second day, pour off the sugar, add a bit more cleaned sugar to it, boil them together again, carefully skim the foam from the top, add the cleaned lemon peel to it and boil everything again strongly, leaving it to rest in the room until the second day.
6. On the third day, boil everything again more strongly; on the fourth day, boil until the sugar pearls, but also put the lemon peel into it several times and remove it again.
7. On the fifth day, cook the sugar completely, add the lemon peel to it and boil it together with it, then transfer everything together into containers, seal and place in a cool room.
