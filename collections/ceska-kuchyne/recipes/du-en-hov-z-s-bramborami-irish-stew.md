## Ingredients

- three pounds meat from the rump, cut into cubes
- approximately six potatoes, peeled and cut into thick slices
- two onions, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- small tablespoons of butter
- water
- half a small cup of water

## Directions

1. Take three pounds of meat from the rump and cut it into not-too-large cubes.
2. Peel about six potatoes and cut them into thick slices, and chop two onions finely.
3. In a deep pot or casserole, place a thin layer of meat and a layer of potatoes, sprinkle with onion, salt and pepper, and add about one small tablespoon of butter.
4. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
5. The last layer on top must be potatoes.
6. Cover with butter, pour about half a small cup of water over it, cover well, and let it braise in the oven for two hours.
