## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Lemon zest
- White wine
- Juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice, parboil it in water, salt it and butter it
2. Add lemon zest
3. Make a roux, add white wine, water, and juice of half a lemon
4. Cook everything, sweeten and add the rice
