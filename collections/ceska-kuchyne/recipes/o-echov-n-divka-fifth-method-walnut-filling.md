## Ingredients

- 10 dek butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 10 dek sugar
- vanilla flavoring
- 2 dek finely chopped citron peel
- 10 dek finely shredded or chopped walnuts
- raisins
- several tablespoons of good wine

## Directions

1. Rub butter with egg yolks and sugar
2. Add vanilla flavoring
3. Add finely chopped citron peel, walnuts, and raisins
4. Mix with several tablespoons of good wine
