## Ingredients

- millet
- water
- salt
- butter
- onion
- milk
- grated gingerbread
- egg
- milk
- white flour

## Directions

1. Sort the millet and rinse it three times in boiling water
2. For two small cups of millet, use 1.5 quarts of water
3. Salt and let it cook with frequent stirring for 1.5 to 2 hours
4. Turn out onto a dish and dress with butter in which sliced onion has been fried
5. Alternatively, the sorted and rinsed millet can be cooked in milk, salted, sprinkled with grated gingerbread and dressed with melted butter
6. For a more delicate version, beat one egg in a small cup with a little milk and a tablespoon of white flour, pour this over the millet, stir well and let it cook for about another minute
