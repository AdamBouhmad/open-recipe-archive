## Ingredients

- turnips
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- pepper
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cook turnips in water; when it begins to boil, drain and replace with fresh hot water, as turnips can sometimes be bitter
2. Add a little salt and a teaspoon of sugar, cover and cook until tender
3. Drain all water and mash the turnips with a wooden spoon
4. Add a little more salt if needed, add a spoonful of good butter, a bit of pepper, and two or three tablespoons of good thick sweet cream
5. Let it rest a while and serve hot
