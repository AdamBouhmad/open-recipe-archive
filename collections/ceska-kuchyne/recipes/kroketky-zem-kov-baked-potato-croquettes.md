## Ingredients

- 75 grams (5 lots) butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 small tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 6 tablespoons grated cooked potatoes
- egg for coating
- grated bread
- butter for baking

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Slowly add 2 eggs
3. Mix in 1 small tablespoon of flour, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Add 6 tablespoons of grated cooked potatoes
5. Form into small croquettes
6. Brush with egg
7. Coat in grated bread
8. Bake in butter
