## Ingredients

- An eighth of a kilogram (quarter pound) rice
- Soft water
- A piece of new butter
- A little flower (kvetu)
- Pepper
- Salt
- 2 liters water
- Mushrooms
- A piece of butter
- A little cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Soak rice in soft water for about a quarter hour (in which case the rice will cook in half an hour)
2. Drain the water and pour in fresh water
3. Add a piece of new butter, a little flower, pepper, and salt
4. Braise the rice so it doesn't burn, pouring water over it
5. When soft, pour 2 liters of water over it
6. Slice mushrooms and add a piece of butter, a little cream mixed with 2 egg yolks
