## Ingredients

- best and largest anchovies
- capers
- lemon
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Select the best and largest anchovies, wash, clean, remove bones, cut lengthwise
2. Arrange in a star shape on a plate
3. Garnish with capers and lemon sliced into rounds
4. Pour vinegar and oil over according to taste
