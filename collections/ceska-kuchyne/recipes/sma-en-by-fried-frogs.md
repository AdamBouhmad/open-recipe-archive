## Ingredients

- frog hind legs
- oil
- lemon juice
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the frog legs, put them in a deeper bowl, salt them, pour a little oil and lemon juice over them, sprinkle with chopped onion and green parsley and let them marinate for about an hour
2. Remove them onto a clean cloth, dust with flour, dip in beaten egg and coat in grated breadcrumbs
3. Fry them in hot melted butter briefly before serving, arrange on a platter and garnish with green parsley
