## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- salt
- milk
- 1 pound cottage cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- raisins
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix 8 tablespoons flour in a little milk
2. Add to it five whole eggs
3. 1 tablespoon sugar
4. Grated zest of one lemon
5. Salt slightly
6. Mix well
7. Add enough milk to make a batter like for pancakes
8. Coat a hot omelet pan or sheet with clarified butter
9. Pour the batter thinly
10. Bake on both sides and place on a clean cloth
11. Rub 1 pound cottage cheese with 1 tablespoon butter on a plate
12. Add to it five egg yolks
13. 6 tablespoons sugar
14. Grated zest of half a lemon
15. A handful of picked and rinsed raisins
16. Mix well
17. Coat the pancakes with this
18. Roll them up
19. Place them in a pan greased with butter
20. Sprinkle with sugar
21. Pour a little boiled milk
22. Let bake slowly in an oven at mild heat
23. Then remove piece by piece with a metal spatula and arrange on a platter
24. Sprinkle generously with sugar and serve
