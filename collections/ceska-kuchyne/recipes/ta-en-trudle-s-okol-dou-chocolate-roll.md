## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- six tablespoons sugar
- one quarter pound pounded almonds
- two tablespoons grated chocolate
- three egg whites
- dough (as for apple strudel)
- flour
- butter
- good cream
- sugar
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat six egg yolks with six tablespoons sugar, add to it one quarter pound pounded almonds and two tablespoons grated chocolate and mix well
2. Slowly fold in snow from three egg whites
3. Make dough as for apple strudel, stretch it thin on a cloth dusted with flour
4. Brush the stretched dough with butter and spread the filling about as thick as a stalk, lift one side of the cloth and slowly roll up the strudel
5. Grease a baking dish with butter, place the strudel in it, pour a little good cream over it, sprinkle with sugar and powdered grated chocolate
6. Bake properly, turn out onto a dish, cut into pieces and bring to the table
