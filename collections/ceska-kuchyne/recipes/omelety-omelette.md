## Ingredients

- Five or six eggs
- Half a small teaspoon of cornstarch
- Half a teaspoon of flour
- Two spoonfuls of good milk
- Sweet sour cream or additional milk

## Directions

1. Beat five or six eggs well
2. Mix smooth half a small teaspoon of cornstarch and half a teaspoon of flour in two spoonfuls of good milk
3. Add more milk to make nearly one cup
4. Pour this milk mixture into the beaten eggs and mix well
5. Salt lightly
6. Pour onto a heated and greased baking dish
7. Bake in the oven for fifteen minutes until it is high and nicely red on top
