## Ingredients

- sirloin
- pepper (several grains)
- new spices
- bay leaf
- thyme
- 2 onions (chopped)
- celery
- carrot
- parsley
- lemon peel
- water (0.5 liter)
- vinegar (0.5 liter)
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove sinews and fat from sirloin, lard with bacon or leave unlarded
2. Place several grains of pepper, new spices, bay leaf, thyme, 2 chopped onions, celery, carrot, parsley, and a piece of lemon peel on baking sheet
3. Baste with water and vinegar, equal amounts of each (0.5 liter each)
4. Place roast in this and let lie for at least 12 hours
5. Place in oven and braise slowly until nearly all sauce is absorbed into the roast
6. Cut roast into pieces, squeeze juice from one lemon into remaining sauce
7. Add a piece of caramelized sugar
8. Create a wreath of pastry around the roast and strain sauce over it
