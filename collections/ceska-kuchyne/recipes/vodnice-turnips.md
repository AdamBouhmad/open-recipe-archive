## Ingredients

- turnips
- fresh butter
- sugar
- mutton broth
- flour
- white grated ginger
- new spices

## Directions

1. Slice peeled turnips into rounds and wash them in cold water
2. Put a heaping spoonful of fresh butter and a teaspoon of sugar in a pot and let it brown slightly
3. Now put the turnips in it and let them braise, basting with mutton broth and stirring frequently and carefully
4. Make a brown roux, pour in mutton broth, mix it well, pour it over the turnips
5. Season with white grated ginger and a little new spices and let it braise until tender
