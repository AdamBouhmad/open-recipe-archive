## Ingredients

- Fresh mushrooms
- Fresh butter
- Caraway seeds
- Fresh parsley
- Salt
- Sour cream
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and slice fresh mushrooms
2. Put them in a saucepan, add a piece of fresh butter and a little caraway seed
3. Let them stew gently
4. Add finely chopped fresh parsley, salt and continue stewing until the liquid evaporates
5. Add another piece of butter
6. Beat several eggs lightly in a little sour cream
7. Pour into the mushrooms and stir as for ordinary scrambled eggs
