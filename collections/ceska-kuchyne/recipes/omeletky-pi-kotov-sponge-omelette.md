## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 6 egg whites
- fresh butter
- fresh cooked fruit preserves (raspberry or strawberry)
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 6 tablespoons of sugar
2. Make stiff snow from 6 egg whites and gently fold into the yolks
3. Melt a small spoonful of fresh butter in a shallow pan and pour one third of the mixture onto it
4. Bake it slightly from below on a hot griddle, then very carefully turn it out onto a plate
5. Do the same with the other two thirds of the mixture
6. Grease the pan in which you baked the omelettes with butter again, place one omelette in it, spread it with fresh cooked raspberry or strawberry preserves, cover with the second omelette and spread with preserves again, cover with the third omelette
7. Sprinkle the top omelette with sugar and pour lemon juice over it
8. Place in a not too hot oven and let bake very gently for about a quarter hour
9. Serve quickly to the table
