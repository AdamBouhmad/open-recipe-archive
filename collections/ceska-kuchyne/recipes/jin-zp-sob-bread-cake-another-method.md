## Ingredients

- roasted bread
- 0.5 kilogram finely crushed sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- about 0.5 kilogram almonds
- several eggs
- cinnamon
- vanilla
- cloves
- finely chopped lemon and orange zest
- 16 egg whites
- butter for greasing molds
- finely grated hardtack for dusting molds

## Directions

1. Finely crush the roasted bread, set aside one-eighth of a kilogram separately
2. Mix 0.5 kilogram finely crushed sugar with 16 egg yolks
3. Set the egg whites aside separately
4. Peel about 0.5 kilogram almonds, dry them, finely crush in a mortar and mix with the bread and sugar described above
5. Add several eggs as needed, a little cinnamon, vanilla, cloves, finely chopped lemon and orange zest, etc.
6. Prepare snow from 16 egg whites and mix with the remaining dough
7. Spread into molds buttered and lightly dusted with finely grated hardtack and bake carefully and slowly
