## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- fresh chopped parsley
- grated bread
- milk
- 1 tablespoon fresh butter
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- bread crumbs for coating

## Directions

1. Boil the calf brain in salted water, clean it thoroughly and place it in a pot
2. Add 2 tablespoons of butter with chopped fresh parsley and lightly fry the brain
3. Soak grated bread in milk and when it is well soaked, mash it and add it to the pot
4. Pour 1 cup of milk over it, place on heat and while constantly stirring, let it cook slightly, then remove and let cool
5. Cream 1 tablespoon of fresh butter until foamy, add the cooled cooked bread mixture, a little salt, 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks and beat for about 30 minutes
6. Then fold in the beaten whites from 2 eggs and mix gently
7. Grease small molds with butter, fill them with this batter and bake
