## Ingredients

- one quart wine
- a piece of sugar
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- a piece of lemon peel
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- one small cup cream
- a small teaspoon flour
- a little cold wine
- a pinch of salt
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Put one quart of wine in a pot with a piece of sugar, a piece of whole cinnamon and a piece of lemon peel
2. let everything boil together, remove scum and strain
3. in another pot beat six egg yolks or three whole eggs with one small cup of cream, a small teaspoon of flour, a little cold wine and a pinch of salt
4. pour boiling wine over this
5. mix well and let it stand on warm heat but do not let it boil
6. serve either in cups or pour over fried bread cubes
7. if you want a weaker soup, you can boil it with one quart of wine and one pint or even one quart of water
