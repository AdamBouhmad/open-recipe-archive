## Ingredients

- 60 grams butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 kilogram flour
- 1.5 bread rolls cut into cubes and fried
- salt

## Directions

1. Cream 60 grams of butter.
2. Add 3 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs, salt.
3. Gradually add flour to the butter and eggs and beat the dough so it does not stick to the hand.
4. When the dough is well beaten, add the bread rolls.
5. Wrap everything in a cloth and place in boiling water.
6. To test if the dumpling is sufficiently cooked, pierce it with a stick; if the dough does not stick to it, it is done.
7. When sufficiently cooked, cut with thread into pieces, sprinkle with bread roll and grease.
