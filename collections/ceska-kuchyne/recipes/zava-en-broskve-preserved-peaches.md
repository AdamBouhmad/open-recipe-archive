## Ingredients

- peaches
- 1 pound of white sugar
- 1.5 small pots of hot water

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, halve them, remove the pits and place the peaches in cold water.
2. Put 1 pound of white sugar into a pot, pour 1.5 small pots of hot water on it and let it boil into a syrup.
3. Fill jars with peaches and place them into a pot with water as stated in the general rules.
4. On each layer of peaches place a layer of sugar and when the jar is full, pour the hot sugar syrup over them, screw the lids on lightly and boil for 10 minutes.
