## Ingredients

- Ripe pineapple
- Lemon juice
- Water
- Dissolved sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the ripe pineapple into pieces
2. Grate on a grater
3. Squeeze lemon juice onto it
4. Dilute with water
5. Sweeten with dissolved sugar
6. Proceed as with other ice creams
