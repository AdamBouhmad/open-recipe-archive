## Ingredients

- 1 liter cherry juice
- 2 liters water
- 357 grams (25 lots) sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 2 tablespoons potato starch flour
- fried bread (smaženou housku) or macaroni

## Directions

1. Mix 1 liter of cherry juice with 2 liters of water, 357 grams (25 lots) of sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest, and 2 tablespoons of potato starch flour
2. Bring to a boil
3. Remove foam
4. Strain over fried bread or macaroni
