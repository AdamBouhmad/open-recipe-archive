## Ingredients

- black mustard (ten decagrams)
- white mustard (twenty decagrams)
- hot wine must

## Directions

1. Grind the mustard
2. Mix about ten decagrams of black and twenty decagrams of white mustard until a thick paste forms
3. Dilute the mustard with hot wine must
