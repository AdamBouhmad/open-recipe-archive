## Ingredients

- handful of fresh parsley
- spinach
- onion
- roux
- broth
- lemon juice
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Pound parsley, spinach, and onion in a mortar
2. Press through a cloth
3. Add roux to broth with salt and juice from half a lemon
4. Simmer for a quarter hour
5. Add herb juice and one egg yolk
