## Ingredients

- beautiful, completely healthy grapes, preferably of firm grain
- vinegar
- pepper
- allspice
- cinnamon
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, completely healthy grapes, preferably of firm grain, and place them in a stone jar
2. Boil vinegar with whole pepper, allspice, and cinnamon, and sugar to taste
3. After simmering gently for half an hour, remove from heat
4. Pour over the grapes
5. When cooled, tie up the jar and store in a cool place
6. The grapes must be completely submerged and lightly weighted down with something such as a small plate and a stone
