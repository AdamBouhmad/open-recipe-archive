## Ingredients

- Fresh head of cabbage
- Salt
- A little flower (kvetu)
- 15 grams (1 lot) sugar
- Lard, butter, or pork cracklings (škvarky)
- Roux (jíškou)
- Potatoes or bread

## Directions

1. Cut a fresh head of cabbage into pieces, salt it
2. Add a little flower and 15 grams of sugar
3. Cook with lard, butter, or pork cracklings until the cabbage falls apart
4. Brown with roux
5. Either strain the soup and add peeled potatoes, or don't strain and add cut bread
