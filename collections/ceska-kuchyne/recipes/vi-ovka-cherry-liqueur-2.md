## Ingredients

- ripe sour cherries without pits
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- allspice
- cherry pits
- wine alcohol or good rye liquor

## Directions

1. Fill a large glass bottle halfway with ripe sour cherries without pits
2. Add sugar according to desired sweetness—generally half a pound of fine sugar per two pounds of cherries
3. Add broken cinnamon, some cloves, half an ounce of allspice and some kernels from the cherry pits
4. Fill the bottle with wine alcohol or good rye liquor
5. Seal the bottle well and place where it gets the most sun
6. Stir it frequently so everything mixes
7. It will be ready in three weeks
