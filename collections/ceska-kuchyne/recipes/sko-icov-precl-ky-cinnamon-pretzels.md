## Ingredients

- 125 grams well-creamed butter
- 1 egg
- 250 grams sugar
- 4 grams finely ground cinnamon
- 375 grams sifted fine flour
- 1 gram ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Take 125 grams of well-creamed butter
2. Gradually beat in 1 egg, 250 grams of sugar, 4 grams of finely ground cinnamon, 375 grams of sifted fine flour, and 1 gram of ground vanilla
3. Knead into perfect dough
4. Roll out and form into pretzel shapes
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Bake on a baking sheet slowly at gentle heat
