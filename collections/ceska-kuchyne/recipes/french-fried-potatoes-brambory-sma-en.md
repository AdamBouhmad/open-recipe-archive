## Ingredients

- medium-sized raw peeled potatoes
- salt water
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut medium-sized peeled raw potatoes lengthwise into eight pieces
2. Let them lie in salt water for one hour
3. Peel the potatoes as thinly as possible; the potato under the skin is the best part
4. Heat plenty of lard in a pot as for frying doughnuts
5. Remove the potatoes from the salt water and dry them well with a cloth
6. Place them in the hot lard so they lie side by side
7. When they begin to turn red, turn them over
8. When you determine with a fork that they are soft inside, remove them with a skimmer
9. Let them drain for a minute over the pot and place on absorbent paper or a sieve to drain further
10. Place them in a heated dish and bring to the table
