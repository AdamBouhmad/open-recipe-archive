## Ingredients

- rye flour
- water
- salt
- butter
- onion
- 2 to 3 eggs
- 3 to 4 tablespoons of sour cream

## Directions

1. Have a special stone pot for fermenting ready.
2. In the evening, put 4 to 5 tablespoons or more of nice rye flour in it, as much as you wish to have plenty of soup, make a starter and cover the pot with a clean cloth and let it stand near the fire until morning so the starter ferments well.
3. In the morning, let water boil, season it well with salt, and when the water boils, with one hand pour the starter and with the other stir with a wooden spoon until it boils about three times.
4. It should not be thin, but also not thick, about like a thin sauce.
5. Then set it aside, put a nice piece of butter in a pan, and when it begins to sizzle, add finely chopped onion, and when it begins to redden, beat 2 to 3 eggs and throw into the onion, and when these begin to fry, mix lightly with the onion and add everything to the bread sponge soup along with 3 to 4 tablespoons of sour cream.
