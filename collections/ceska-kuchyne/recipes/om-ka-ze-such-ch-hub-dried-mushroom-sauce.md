## Ingredients

- dried mushrooms
- fresh water
- meat broth
- butter roux
- clove

## Directions

1. Rinse dried mushrooms nicely in fresh water
2. Put them in a pot and pour meat broth over them and cook until soft
3. Strain the broth and chop the mushrooms finely
4. Pour the broth they were cooked in back over them
5. Brown with butter roux and add a pinch of clove
6. Let it cook a little longer
7. If you want the sauce sour, add a little white wine vinegar
