## Ingredients

- beef meat, cut into pieces
- mutton meat, cut into pieces
- pork meat, cut into pieces
- bacon
- onion, chopped
- cumin
- pepper
- flour
- beef broth or water
- salt
- paprika
- several grains of allspice
- wine (optional)
- boiled or fried potatoes
- potatoes, peeled and chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut beef, mutton, and pork meat into pieces.
2. Brown the chopped onion in bacon fat, add a little cumin and pepper.
3. Add the beef meat and let it braise while stirring frequently.
4. After a while, add the mutton meat and finally the pork.
5. Dust with flour, add beef broth or water, salt, paprika, several grains of allspice, and a little wine if desired.
6. Let it braise until everything is sufficiently tender.
7. Place the gulash in a deep platter and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
8. Alternatively, peeled and chopped potatoes can be cooked with the meat.
9. For six people, 2-3 pounds of all meats combined is sufficient.
