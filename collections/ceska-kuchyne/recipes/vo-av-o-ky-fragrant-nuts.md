## Ingredients

- nuts
- sugar
- jasmine extract
- reseda extract or similar

## Directions

1. When sugaring the nuts, add jasmine extract, reseda, and similar fragrant liqueurs to the sugar, which will give the nuts a very pleasant and sweet fragrance
