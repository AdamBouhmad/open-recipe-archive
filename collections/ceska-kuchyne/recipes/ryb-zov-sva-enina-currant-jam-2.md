## Ingredients

- currants
- sugar (three-quarters cup sugar per cup of currants)

## Directions

1. Remove currant berries from their stems, wash them, put them in a pot, crush them and let them cook.
2. When the mixture has come to a boil several times, add sugar—three-quarters cup of sugar per cup of currants.
3. Crush the berries with a wooden spoon or masher and cook over moderate heat for half an hour.
