## Ingredients

- quarter kilogram cooked grated potatoes
- melted butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 60 grams (4 lots) peeled finely ground almonds
- lemon zest grated with sugar
- sweet sour cream
- 4 egg whites
- flour
- quarter liter milk
- 10 peeled almonds
- sugar
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Place cooked grated potatoes on a plate
2. Mix melted butter, 4 egg yolks, 60 grams peeled finely ground almonds, lemon zest grated with sugar and sweet sour cream
3. Fold in snow from 4 egg whites
4. Place mixture in a greased form and cook for half an hour in boiling water
5. After half an hour, turn out and pour the following sauce around it
6. Mix 1 spoonful flour with quarter liter milk
7. Grind 10 peeled almonds and strain with milk through cloth
8. Add sugar and lemon zest
9. Heat over fire, stirring until it thickens like barley pudding
