## Ingredients

- carp innards
- parsley cut into thin strips
- carrot
- celery
- a small piece of ginger
- water
- salt
- white roux
- flowers (spice)
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Take the innards from carp, carefully remove the bile so it does not break, also remove the fatty intestine
2. clean the innards thoroughly and keep aside until needed
3. put parsley cut into thin strips, carrot, celery and a small piece of ginger in a pot
4. pour water on it, salt it and let it boil
5. when the vegetables are sufficiently cooked, strain
6. thicken the soup with white roux
7. add the carp innards to it
8. season with a little flowers and let it simmer nicely
9. pour the soup into a bowl over finely chopped and fried bread cubes, or you can also use bread cut into cubes and fried
