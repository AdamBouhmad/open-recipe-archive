## Ingredients

- tongue
- one to two eggs
- water
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- salt
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue until tender
2. Peel the tongue
3. Cut into finger-thick cutlets
4. Beat one to two eggs with a little water
5. Add a pinch of nutmeg, several drops of lemon juice, and salt
6. Coat the cutlets in bread on both sides
7. Dip into the egg mixture
8. Fry in butter on both sides
