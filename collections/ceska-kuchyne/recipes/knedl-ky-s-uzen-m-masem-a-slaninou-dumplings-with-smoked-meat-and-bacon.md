## Ingredients

- 60 grams (4 lots) of butter
- Salt
- 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
- Flour (1 kilogram - 2 pounds)
- Milk
- 2 rolls
- One quarter kilogram (one quarter pound) of smoked meat
- 60 grams (4 lots) of bacon
- Water

## Directions

1. Cream 60 grams of butter with a mixer; salt it, add 2 whole eggs and an egg yolk, and beat again.
2. Mix with flour (1 kilogram), pour in milk and work the dough so it is as stiff as for buns.
3. Cut 2 rolls into cubes and fry in butter.
4. Similarly cut one quarter kilogram of smoked meat and 60 grams of bacon into cubes; mix everything with the dough and salt.
5. The water in which the dumplings are to be cooked must already be boiling and salted.
6. Wet a large spoon in this water, take dough with it and cook the dumplings for a quarter hour.
7. When cooked, halve them, grease and sprinkle with grated roll.
8. Usually cabbage is added to them.
