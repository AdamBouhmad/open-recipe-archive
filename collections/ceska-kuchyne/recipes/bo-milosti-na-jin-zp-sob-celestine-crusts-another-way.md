## Ingredients

- one coffeepot flour
- one coffeepot sweet cream
- two egg yolks
- one whole egg
- salt
- one teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. On a board, combine flour, sweet cream, egg yolks, whole egg, salt, and sugar
2. Mix together and knead the dough like noodle dough
3. Roll out and cut into pieces, then prick with a fork
4. Fry on hot lard and sprinkle with sugar
