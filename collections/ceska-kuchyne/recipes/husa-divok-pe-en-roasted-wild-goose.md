## Ingredients

- wild goose
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. A killed wild goose should not hang for long if it is to become a tasty dish.
2. Only young geese can be roasted; older geese can only be marinated or braised.
3. Remove the head, neck, wings, and legs from the plucked and cleaned wild goose.
4. Prepare it like a domestic goose on a baking sheet.
5. Blanch it in boiling water, dry it inside and out, season with salt and pepper.
6. Roast thoroughly, basting frequently or coating with butter.
