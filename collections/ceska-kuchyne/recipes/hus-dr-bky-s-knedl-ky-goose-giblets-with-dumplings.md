## Ingredients

- goose giblets
- carrots
- celery
- parsley
- cabbage
- cauliflower
- salt
- pale butter roux
- goose livers
- butter
- flour
- garlic
- marjoram
- egg
- bread

## Directions

1. Prepare the giblets as when preparing them with noodles
2. Press pale butter roux through a sieve into the broth in which the giblets were cooked and bring to a boil
3. Meanwhile, make dumplings either from goose livers (cleaned and chopped, removing membranes, with butter, flour, garlic, marjoram, and egg added, all mixed and formed into small dumplings) or from bread (mixing butter, flour, and egg, adding milk if needed and mixing with grated bread)
4. Cook the dumplings in the broth, then add the giblets
5. Instead of noodles, rice, or dumplings, cauliflower or asparagus (previously cooked in salted water) may be used
6. Duck giblets may be prepared the same way as goose giblets
