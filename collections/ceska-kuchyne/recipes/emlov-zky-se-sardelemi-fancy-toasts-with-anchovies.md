## Ingredients

- Round fried bread slices
- Fresh butter
- Anchovies
- Hard-boiled egg yolk
- Hard-boiled egg white
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Around round fried bread slices, make a border of fresh butter
2. In the middle, arrange anchovies in a rose shape
3. Garnish between the anchovies with hard-boiled rubbed egg yolk, hard-boiled chopped egg white, and chopped green parsley
