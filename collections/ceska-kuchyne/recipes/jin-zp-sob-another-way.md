## Ingredients

- Coffee beans
- chicory (7-8 grams chicory per 150 grams coffee)
- water (three liters)
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Before cooking, grind the coffee and add crushed chicory.
2. Heat everything on the stove with constant stirring.
3. Slowly strain (filter) the boiling water through a coffee machine.
4. Let it stand on warm stove for at least ten minutes, then serve with milk and sugar.
