## Ingredients

- game meat
- salt water
- celery
- parsley root
- carrots
- 2 onions
- water
- wine
- vinegar
- egg yolk
- rose hip preserves
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the game and rinse it thoroughly in several waters.
2. Add celery, parsley root, carrots, and 2 onions cut into slices or strips.
3. Add 2 parts water with 1 part wine and vinegar.
4. Cook until the game is tender.
5. Add an egg yolk to the sauce and rose hip preserves.
6. If it should be darker, burn a piece of sugar, cook, sweeten if needed, and strain over the sliced game.
