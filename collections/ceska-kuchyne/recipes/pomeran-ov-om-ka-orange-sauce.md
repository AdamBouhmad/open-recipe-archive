## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- 1 lemon
- 10 dekagrams sugar-water with orange flavor

## Directions

1. Squeeze the oranges and lemon
2. Pour in the sugared water with orange flavor
3. Boil everything
4. Use for pouring over flour dishes
