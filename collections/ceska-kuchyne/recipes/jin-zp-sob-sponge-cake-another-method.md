## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- egg yolks
- 36 dekagrams (24 lots) sugar
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) flour

## Directions

1. Make snow from 12 egg whites
2. Add the egg yolks and 36 dekagrams sugar
3. Beat for half an hour
4. Mix with 18 dekagrams flour
5. Bake in a buttered mold and decorate as desired
