## Ingredients

- Dough (as prepared in previous recipe)
- Stewed plums (povidla), almonds or similar filling
- Egg for brushing
- Pernik (gingerbread), curd cheese or poppy seed filling

## Directions

1. Prepare dough the same way as in the previous recipe
2. Roll out or stretch the dough evenly everywhere
3. Cut out small cakes with a form
4. In the center, place either thinned stewed plums, almonds, etc., and brush the edges with beaten egg
5. Alternatively, press a piece of dough into a square shape, place stewed plums, gingerbread, curd cheese or poppy seed in the center, fold the corners over, brush with egg and sprinkle with chopped almonds
