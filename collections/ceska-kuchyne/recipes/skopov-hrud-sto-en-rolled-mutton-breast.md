## Ingredients

- mutton breast
- salt
- onion chopped
- sardines cut up
- twine
- broth
- spices
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Remove bones from breast and salt it
2. Sprinkle with chopped onion and cut-up sardines
3. Roll and tie with twine
4. Pour in enough broth to cover
5. Add spices and vegetables to cover
6. Braise slowly
7. Remove fat from sauce and pour over roast
