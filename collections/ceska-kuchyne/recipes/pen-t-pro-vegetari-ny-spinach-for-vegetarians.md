## Ingredients

- spinach
- hot water
- butter
- onion
- salt
- bread crust

## Directions

1. Cook nice selected and cleaned spinach in hot water until tender, drain the water, chop the spinach
2. Melt butter until brown and fry, add onion and slowly stew the spinach with it, salt it and before serving sprinkle with fried grated bread crust
