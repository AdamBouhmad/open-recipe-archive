## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams blanched hazelnuts
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar
- zest from half lemon
- two yolks
- snow from 4 whites
- butter dough
- egg white for brushing

## Directions

1. Grind hazelnuts with sour cream, mix with sugar, lemon zest, yolks, and snow from whites
2. Spread mixture on butter cake dough
3. Make lattice from dough over it
4. Brush with white, sprinkle with sugar
5. Bake in oven
