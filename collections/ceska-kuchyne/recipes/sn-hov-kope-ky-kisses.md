## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- approximately 2 cupfuls of powdered sugar
- vanilla or lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites from 4 eggs into peaks
2. Add about 2 cupfuls of powdered sugar and vanilla or lemon juice to taste
3. Beat for a quarter to half hour until it stiffens
