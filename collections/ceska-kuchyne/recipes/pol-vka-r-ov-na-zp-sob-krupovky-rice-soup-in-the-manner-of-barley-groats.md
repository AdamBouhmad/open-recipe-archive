## Ingredients

- 0.25 kilo rice
- 0.5 liter good beef soup
- 7 grams (5 lots) butter
- herbs
- salt
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice and let it soak for 1/8 hour
2. Pour 0.5 liter of good beef soup over it, add butter, herbs and salt
3. Boil with constant stirring until the rice softens and becomes porridge
4. Let it cool, add 8 egg yolks and mix well
5. Pour in beef soup, boil for a quarter hour, strain and pour into cups
6. Best served in the evening
