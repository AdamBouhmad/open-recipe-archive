## Ingredients

- Ripe apricots of medium size
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Plunge ripe apricots of medium size into hot water until soft.
2. Remove them and place on a sieve to dry.
3. Peel them and remove the pits.
4. Boil a kilogram of sugar, let the apricots sit in it for a while, remove from heat, cover the baking pan with paper and cloth, and leave the apricots in the sugar for 24 hours.
5. On the second day, place them on a sieve to let the sugar drain off, press them, sprinkle with ground sugar, and dry in a warm oven.
