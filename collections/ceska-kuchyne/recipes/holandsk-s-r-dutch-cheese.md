## Ingredients

- warm freshly milked milk
- rennet
- warm water
- salt

## Directions

1. Warm, freshly milked milk is strained, rennet is added to it, it is curdled, and the cheese is then broken and cut abundantly, but always allowed to settle again, the whey is poured off.
2. When all the whey is poured off, warm water is poured under the cheese; the more water is added, the more crumbly and drier the cheese becomes, which dries quickly and can be kept longer.
3. Cheese that was little watered is more delicious but does not last as long. The water is poured off in about ten minutes, the cheese is removed, broken, worked through, and allowed to settle, then placed into molds, salted, flipped, and placed to ripen.
