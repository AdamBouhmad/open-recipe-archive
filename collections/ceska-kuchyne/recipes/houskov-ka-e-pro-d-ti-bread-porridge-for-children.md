## Ingredients

- hard white rolls
- boiling water
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Finely grate hard white rolls
2. Mix with a little boiling water
3. Add a small amount of butter and a pinch of sugar
4. With constant stirring and gentle heating, make a soft porridge
