## Ingredients

- well-cooked ham
- bread slices
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove all fat from well-cooked ham
2. Slice ham thinly and equally
3. Cut these slices into smaller pieces so that three or four pieces fit in one sandwich
4. Butter prepared bread slices
5. Arrange ham pieces on one slice
6. Cover with another slice
7. Press together gently and serve
8. Alternatively, if only one large slice of meat is placed in the sandwich, use smaller pieces as described above to prevent the meat from falling out when bitten
9. Ham may also be finely chopped
