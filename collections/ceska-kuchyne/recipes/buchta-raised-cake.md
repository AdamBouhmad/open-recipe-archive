## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of butter
- five egg yolks
- six tablespoons of sugar
- lemon zest
- a tiny pinch of pounded flower
- a little salt
- four cupfuls of flour
- a piece of pressed yeast
- four egg whites
- raw cream

## Directions

1. Beat four tablespoons of butter until foamy, add to it five egg yolks, one by one, six tablespoons of sugar, lemon zest, a tiny pinch of pounded flower, a little salt and mix well.
2. Then add four cupfuls of flour, a piece of pressed yeast, the snow from four egg whites and as much raw cream as needed to make a nice stiff dough, beat for half an hour.
3. Grease a large mold with butter, sprinkle with grated bread, put the dough into it, spread butter over the top and let it bake in the oven for three quarters of an hour slowly until golden.
4. Turn out the cake and sprinkle with sugar.
