## Ingredients

- pie crust dough
- peeled tart apples
- sugar
- cinnamon or nutmeg
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Line a pie pan thinly with rolled out pie dough
2. Fill with peeled tart apples sliced into thin slices
3. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, or nutmeg
4. Dot with several small pieces of butter
5. Moisten the edges with water
6. Cover the pie again with dough
7. Press the edges together firmly
8. Cut off excess dough
9. Make several slits on top
10. Bake in a moderate oven for three quarters of an hour
11. Never use sweet apples for pies
