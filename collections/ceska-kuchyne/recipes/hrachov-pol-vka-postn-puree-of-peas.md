## Ingredients

- peas
- parsley water
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- parsnip flower
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook peas until soft
2. Mix the peas
3. Pour parsley water on them and let simmer a bit more
4. Strain the soup
5. Fry it with a light browned roux, in which you can let a finely chopped onion become pale
6. You can also add about two cloves of well ground garlic, as well as a little parsnip flower and if you wish, also a little marjoram, which however can be omitted
7. Pour the soup over fried bread cubes
