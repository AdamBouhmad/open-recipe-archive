## Ingredients

- six ounces rice
- boiling water
- good beef soup
- piece of fresh butter
- four egg yolks
- boiling beef soup

## Directions

1. Cook six ounces of picked and thoroughly rinsed rice in good beef soup with boiling water.
2. Rub it with a piece of fresh butter and four egg yolks.
3. Pour boiling beef soup over it, mix well and pour into a dish.
4. You may add chopped gizzards and livers from poultry.
