## Ingredients

- 120 grams almonds
- 60 grams crushed sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 egg
- 4 teaspoons rose water
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make dough from almonds, crushed sugar, egg yolks, egg, rose water, and cinnamon
2. Roll out dough and cut into strips
3. Form wreaths from the strips
4. Fry in butter
5. Sprinkle with sugar
