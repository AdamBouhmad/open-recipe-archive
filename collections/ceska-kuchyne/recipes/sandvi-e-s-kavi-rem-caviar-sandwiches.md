## Ingredients

- caviar
- onion, finely chopped
- butter
- lemon juice
- rye bread

## Directions

1. For each sandwich use one tablespoon caviar
2. Add half a teaspoon finely chopped onion
3. Add a piece of butter the size of a walnut
4. Add juice from half a lemon
5. Mix well together
6. Spread on prepared rye bread slices
7. Press together gently
8. Cut sandwich crosswise into two triangular halves and serve
