## Ingredients

- salmon
- fresh butter
- shallots
- capers
- sardines
- parsley root
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salt
- beef broth
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a nice piece of salmon into pieces, place several tablespoons of fresh heated butter in a pan
2. Add several finely chopped shallots, capers, four cleaned and finely chopped sardines, a piece of parsley root, lemon juice, season with ground pepper and salt
3. Place the cut salmon in this and let it lie for twenty-four hours, turning it several times
4. Half an hour before serving, remove the salmon from the sauce and place on a well-buttered baking sheet
5. Add pieces of butter, place in the oven and bake at high heat for twenty minutes, basting frequently with the marinade sauce
6. Make a light butter roux, pour in a little beef broth and the remaining sauce in which the salmon was marinated, let it simmer a while
7. Arrange the baked salmon on a dish and surround with fried parsley, pour the sauce into the pan with the rest, let it boil for a minute and strain through a sieve into a small bowl
