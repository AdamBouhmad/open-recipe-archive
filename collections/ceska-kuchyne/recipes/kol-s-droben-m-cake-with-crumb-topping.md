## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 6 egg yolks
- Salt
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) ground sugar
- 4 tablespoons diluted yeast
- Flour (as much as needed so dough is not too thick)
- 1/3 kilogram (1/4 pound) butter for crumb topping
- Flour for crumb topping
- Egg for brushing
- Sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Beat butter until foamy, add 6 egg yolks while beating, add a pinch of salt, 6 decagrams ground sugar, 4 tablespoons diluted yeast and enough flour so the dough is not too thick
2. Let it rise for a while, then spread out in a flat cake
3. Mix 1/3 kilogram butter with flour to make a crumb topping like breadcrumbs
4. Brush the cake around with beaten egg, sprinkle with crumb topping, bake, cut into pieces and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
