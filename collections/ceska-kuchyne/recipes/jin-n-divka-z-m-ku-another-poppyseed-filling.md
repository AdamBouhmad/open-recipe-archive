## Ingredients

- six tablespoons sugar
- three egg yolks
- one small cup poppyseeds, cooked and finely ground or cooked in cream and well rubbed
- three tablespoons melted butter
- three or four tablespoons cream
- half a small cup seedless raisins
- cinnamon, finely ground
- vanilla
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub six tablespoons of sugar with three egg yolks, add one small cup of poppyseeds (either cooked and finely ground, or cooked in cream and well rubbed), three tablespoons of melted butter, three or four tablespoons of cream, half a small cup of seedless raisins, a knife point of finely ground cinnamon and vanilla.
2. Mix well, then fold in stiff snow from three egg whites.
