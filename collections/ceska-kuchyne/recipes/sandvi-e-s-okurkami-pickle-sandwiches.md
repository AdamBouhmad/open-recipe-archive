## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- sour pickle
- veal roast

## Directions

1. Butter prepared bread slices
2. On one slice place thinly sliced sour cucumber
3. Then place a thin slice of roasted veal
4. Cover with another slice
5. Press together gently and serve
