## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- bay leaf
- parsley
- vegetable roots, finely chopped
- white wine
- lemon, sliced
- cloves
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scale the salmon, remove bones, wash, and cut into pieces two fingers thick
2. Soak in water for half an hour, dry, salt, and sprinkle with vinegar for half an hour
3. Boil halfway in salted water
4. Place on a baking sheet lined with bay leaf, parsley, and finely chopped vegetable roots
5. Pour white wine over it, arrange lemon slices on top
6. Season with salt and add cloves and sugar
7. Cook until the salmon is tender
8. Strain the sauce, whisk in several egg yolks, and pour over the salmon
