## Ingredients

- trout
- crayfish tails
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- lemon zest
- onion
- clove

## Directions

1. Braise trout until not completely soft
2. When cooled, insert crayfish tails into the meat
3. Make a special sauce from 70 grams of melted butter, 1 spoon flour browned, bouillon, lemon zest, onion, and clove
4. Simmer together
5. Strain and pour over the trout
