## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pass the ripe plums through a sieve
2. Pour hot water over them
3. Peel them
4. Arrange them one next to another on an earthenware baking dish
5. Sprinkle with sugar
6. Pour cold water over them
7. Cover and cook slowly
