## Ingredients

- large trout
- carp meat
- mushrooms
- truffles
- parsley
- eggs
- onion
- white bread
- milk
- pepper
- nutmeg
- thyme
- white wine
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and eviscerate larger trout and rinse
2. Make stuffing from carp meat, mushrooms, truffles, parsley, eggs, onion, white bread soaked in milk, pepper, nutmeg, and thyme
3. Season with salt and cook with white wine
4. Cool the mixture and drain
5. Fill the trout with the stuffing
6. Dip in beaten egg, coat with grated breadcrumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Serve with tomato sauce
