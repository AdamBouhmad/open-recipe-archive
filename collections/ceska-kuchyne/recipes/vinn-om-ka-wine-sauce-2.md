## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls parsley
- 3 egg yolks
- wine
- ginger
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flower

## Directions

1. Chop two handfuls of parsley very finely, place in a bowl, add three egg yolks and mix well.
2. Pour warm wine over it, as much as you want sauce to be of appropriate thickness.
3. Season with ginger, pepper, nutmeg and flower.
4. Simmer slowly and stir constantly for about five minutes.
