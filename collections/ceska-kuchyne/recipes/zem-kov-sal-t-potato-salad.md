## Ingredients

- cold cooked potatoes
- salt
- onion
- wine vinegar
- fine oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the cold cooked potatoes into thin slices and salt them
2. Finely chop raw onion and add to potatoes
3. Add wine vinegar, fine oil, and a little pepper
4. Mix well
