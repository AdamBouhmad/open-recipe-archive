## Ingredients

- noodles
- crayfish
- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make and cook noodles in salted water
2. Cook crayfish and peel them
3. Carefully remove meat from tails and body
4. Crush shells in mortar, press through sieve and mix with butter to make crayfish butter
5. Beat eggs with butter, salt and pepper
6. Add to noodles
7. Put mixture in greased baking pan sprinkled with breadcrumbs in layers: noodles, crayfish tails, noodles (last layer)
8. Bake
