## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- flour
- bacon, cut into cubes
- onion, chopped
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and grate them.
2. Squeeze out the water and press through a sieve.
3. Stir with butter and a few spoonfuls of flour over heat.
4. Add the chopped bacon, onion, pepper, and salt.
5. Stir the mash over heat for half an hour.
