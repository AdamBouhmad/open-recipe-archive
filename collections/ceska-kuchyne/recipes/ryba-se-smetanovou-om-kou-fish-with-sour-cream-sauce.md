## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Sticks for baking sheet
- Good sour cream
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as usual for cooking and salt it.
2. Place sticks crosswise on a baking sheet, lay the fish on them so it doesn't touch the bottom.
3. Pour good sour cream over it until the fish is baked.
4. Carefully place on a platter, pour sauce over it and garnish with lemon.
5. This method can be used for carp, catfish, perch and pike.
