## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 6 hard yolks, grated
- 1/2 kilogram sugar rubbed with lemon
- 1/2 kilogram sugar crumbled
- 3-4 eggs
- preserved fruit for filling

## Directions

1. Sift flour on a board, grate in hard yolks
2. Add grated sugar rubbed with lemon and crumbled sugar
3. Mix in eggs and knead dough thoroughly in a cool place
4. Add flour if too thin
5. Roll out dough, set aside a piece for border
6. Bake on ungreased sheet in oven quickly
7. When baked and cooled, brush with fine preserved fruit
8. Or make latticed decoration from remaining dough and bake
