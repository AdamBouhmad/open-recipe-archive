## Ingredients

- turkey, not older than one year
- pepper
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. The best turkey is not older than one year
2. After slaughtering, pluck the turkey well, singe the down, clean it, wash it, salt it, and let it lie in a cold place for at least two to three days in winter and at least twenty-four hours in summer before roasting
3. Place it on a roasting pan, dust it with pepper inside and on the surface
4. Insert and place pieces of butter on top—altogether about one bowl of butter
5. Pour one bowl of water underneath
6. Bake it
7. Every fifteen minutes, baste it with the rendered juices at a steady but not strong heat
8. Roasting takes one to one and a half hours, depending on the size of the turkey
9. When roasted, cut it into neat pieces, place on a platter and pour the juices over it
10. Serve with cranberry marmalade
