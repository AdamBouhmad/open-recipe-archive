## Ingredients

- Codfish
- butter
- bread crumbs
- sardines
- lemon
- nutmeg
- cream

## Directions

1. Cook the codfish until tender, drain and cool
2. Carefully remove the bones and flake the meat
3. Generously butter a baking dish or mold and dust with grated bread crumbs
4. Place a layer of codfish at the bottom, then a layer of grated bread crumbs mixed with chopped sardines, well rubbed with lemon zest and nutmeg
5. Continue alternating layers until the dish is full
6. Top with a layer of bread crumbs, pour a little cream over it, place pieces of butter on top and bake in the oven
7. Either turn out onto a serving plate or serve in the baking dish
