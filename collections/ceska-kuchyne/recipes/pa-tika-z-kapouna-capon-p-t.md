## Ingredients

- Butter dough
- Capon
- Flour
- Butter
- Lard, finely chopped
- Onion, finely chopped
- Nutmeg flower, crushed
- Lemon zest, finely chopped
- Wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare a pâté from butter dough and bake it.
2. Prepare the capon as for ragout.
3. Brown some flour with butter, add the finely chopped lard and mix everything together.
4. Place pieces of capon on top, cover with finely chopped onion, crushed nutmeg flower, and finely chopped lemon zest.
5. Pour wine over, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer.
6. Pour lemon juice over the finished pâté.
