## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo peeled almonds
- egg white
- 1/2 kilo sugar
- 1/4 kilo raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Rub 1/2 kilo peeled almonds very finely with egg white
2. Boil 1/2 kilo sugar to the sixth degree
3. Add finely rubbed almonds to syrup
4. Push raspberries through fine sieve
5. Add 1/4 kilo strained juice to mixture
6. Stir constantly and heat slowly until it thickens
7. When cooled, place in molds as desired
8. Heat again until thickened
