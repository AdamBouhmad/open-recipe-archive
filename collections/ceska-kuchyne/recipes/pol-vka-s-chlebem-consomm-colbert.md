## Ingredients

- hard bread roll, sliced thin
- clarified butter
- onion, sliced into noodles
- green chives, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- beef soup
- flowers
- ginger
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Slice a hard bread roll thinly and fry it in clarified butter until brown
2. Similarly fry onion sliced into noodles until nicely brown—it must not burn
3. Place both in a soup bowl with chopped green chives and parsley, pour good beef soup over it, season with flowers and ginger
4. Carefully place as many poached eggs as there are people at table into the soup
5. Alternatively the eggs may be served on a separate plate; then neither onion nor chives go into the bowl, but the eggs are placed on the plate, sprinkled with chopped chives and surrounded with a wreath of fried onion
