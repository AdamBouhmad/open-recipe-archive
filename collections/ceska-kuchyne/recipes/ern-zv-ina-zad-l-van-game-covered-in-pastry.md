## Ingredients

- game meat
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- thyme
- bay leaf
- pepper
- lemon zest
- new spices
- clove
- 2 garlic cloves
- fresh or dried cherries
- butter dough

## Directions

1. Cut the meat, rinse it, and place on a baking pan with wine, vinegar and water.
2. Add thyme, bay leaf, pepper, lemon zest, new spices, clove, 2 garlic cloves, and several fresh or dried cherries.
3. Braise until almost all liquid has evaporated.
4. Carefully remove the cherries and place the game on a serving dish.
5. Strain the remaining pan juices over it and add the cherries back.
6. Cover everything with butter dough.
