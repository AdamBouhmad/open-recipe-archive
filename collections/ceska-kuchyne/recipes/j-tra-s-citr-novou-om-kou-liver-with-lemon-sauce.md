## Ingredients

- Liver
- Salt
- Butter
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the liver and slice it into thin pieces
2. Salt it, place on a baking sheet with butter, season with pepper and braise
3. When tender, remove and strain the sauce
4. Add the juice of one lemon to the sauce, return the liver to it and cook briefly
