## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- onion
- clove
- carrot
- beef broth
- salt
- flour
- parsley
- egg yolks
- sour cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken well and place it in cold water.
2. Place a piece of butter, an onion stuck with a clove, and a piece of carrot in a pot, pour beef broth over it, and salt it.
3. Remove the chicken from cold water and arrange it nicely in the pot, or cut it into pieces and arrange it in the pot, and let it stew, but not until very tender, turning it frequently so it remains nicely white.
4. Place a piece of butter in a pot, a piece of sliced onion, a little chopped fresh parsley, add two tablespoons of flour and make a pale roux, pour a little beef broth over it and mix well.
5. Strain the broth in which you stewed the chicken, skim the fat from it, and pour it into the diluted roux.
6. Place the stewed chicken in this sauce and let it cook a bit.
7. Before serving, beat two egg yolks with a little sour cream, mix it into the sauce, place the chicken on a platter, strain the sauce over it, and serve.
8. If desired, you can squeeze lemon juice into it.
