## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar (at least one and a half parts per one part of fruit pulp)

## Directions

1. Cut the fruit into pieces
2. Cook until completely soft
3. Place in fresh water
4. When cooled, strain so water drains better
5. Grate and press through a fine sieve
6. For one part of fruit pulp obtained this way, add at least one and a half parts of crushed sugar
7. Mix everything and cook together
8. When all watery substances have boiled off, remove
9. Place on slate tablets
10. Shape into long, round cakes
11. Allow to dry
12. Place between paper in clean vessels
