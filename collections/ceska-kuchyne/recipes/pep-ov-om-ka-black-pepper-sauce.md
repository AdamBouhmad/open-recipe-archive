## Ingredients

- two spoons of butter
- one sliced onion
- one spoon of whole pepper
- one teaspoon of ground pepper
- several grains of new spice
- a piece of ginger
- a slice of garlic
- two cloves
- two bay leaves
- a piece of thyme
- about two spoons of wine vinegar
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Make a brown roux from two spoons of butter
2. Add to it one sliced onion, one spoon of whole pepper, one teaspoon of ground pepper, several grains of new spice, a piece of ginger, a slice of garlic, two cloves, two bay leaves and a piece of thyme
3. Add about two spoons of wine vinegar
4. Pour in as much beef broth as you need sauce
5. When everything has cooked together for a little while, strain the sauce into a dish
6. If the sauce is too thick, add more broth; if not sour enough, add a little vinegar
