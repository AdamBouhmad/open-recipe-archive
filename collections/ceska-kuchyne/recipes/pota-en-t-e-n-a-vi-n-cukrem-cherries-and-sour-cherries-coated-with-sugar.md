## Ingredients

- cherries or sour cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Trim the stems halfway.
2. Place the cherries on a small baking pan and pour thickened, strained sugar over them, in which they soak well.
3. Remove them and coat them several times in powdered, fine sugar, then place them on a sieve to dry.
