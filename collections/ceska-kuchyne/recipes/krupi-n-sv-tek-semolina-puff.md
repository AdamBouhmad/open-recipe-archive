## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar
- semolina
- milk

## Directions

1. Take 2 eggs, whip the whites so that stiff snow forms; then add 2 yolks, a pinch of salt, nutmeg and sugar to taste
2. Then lightly sprinkle semolina into it so that not too thick batter forms
3. It must not be thin, about like for pancakes
4. Grease a pie plate with butter and pour the puff into it and bake in the oven
5. It must bake quickly to golden color
6. When the puff is baked, cut it into cubes
7. In the meantime have milk on the stove, and as soon as it begins to boil, place the puff in it and let it come to a boil several times
8. Then remove each piece onto a designated plate, pour over it the milk in which the puff was boiled and serve
