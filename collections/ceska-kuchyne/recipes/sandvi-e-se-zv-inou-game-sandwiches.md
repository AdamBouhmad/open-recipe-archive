## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- French mustard
- hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cold game meat
- tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Butter one bread slice and spread the other thinly with French mustard
2. Slice hard-boiled eggs into rounds, remove yolks and mash them with oil, vinegar, salt and pepper
3. Place four egg white rounds on the buttered bread
4. Chop half a pound of cold game meat finely
5. Season with salt and pepper, add mashed yolks and a little tarragon vinegar and mix well
6. Fill the egg white rounds with this mixture
7. Cover with the other bread slice, press gently and serve
8. Poultry and other fine meats may be used instead of game
