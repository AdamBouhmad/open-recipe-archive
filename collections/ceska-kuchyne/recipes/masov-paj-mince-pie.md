## Ingredients

- 3 pounds beef from the neck
- 2 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds small raisins, cleaned and well washed
- 1/2 pound citron peel
- 3 nutmegs
- 1 1/2 pounds suet from the kidneys, finely chopped
- 8 large apples, peeled and finely chopped
- 1 pint brandy
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 pint apple must
- pastry for bottom and top crusts

## Directions

1. Take 3 pounds beef from the neck, cook well, salt, and chop finely
2. Add 2 pounds raisins, 2 pounds small raisins that have been cleaned and well washed, 1/2 pound citron peel, 3 nutmegs, 1 1/2 pounds suet from the kidneys finely chopped, 8 large peeled apples finely chopped, 1 pint brandy, 1 cup sugar, and 1 pint apple must
3. Mix everything together well and heat thoroughly on the stove until very hot, but do not let it boil
4. When it has cooled slightly, spread into forms, cover with top crust, and bake
5. This pie is always eaten warm
