## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- lemon juice
- mushroom stems, finely chopped
- fresh parsley
- meat
- butter
- breadcrumbs, grated and fried
- salt
- flower (cloves?)
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel the mushrooms, remove the stems, scrape out the insides and put them in water strained through lemon juice.
2. Finely chop the stems with a piece of fresh parsley, add a little meat and let it stew in butter; then add a tablespoon of fried grated breadcrumbs, a little salt and a small amount of flower, two egg yolks, mix it into a fairly thick stuffing, fill the hollowed mushrooms to the top, put them on a shallow, butter-coated baking pan and let them brown nicely in the oven.
3. You can serve them either alone or use them to garnish various roasts.
