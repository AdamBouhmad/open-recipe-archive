## Ingredients

- several floury cooked potatoes
- 0.5 liter milk
- salt
- sugar
- vanilla (crushed)
- several egg yolks
- egg for coating
- bread crumbs
- butter for frying
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge from several floury cooked potatoes, 0.5 liter milk, salt, sugar, crushed vanilla, and several egg yolks
2. Let it cool
3. Form small croquettes from the porridge
4. Brush with egg
5. Coat with bread crumbs
6. Fry in butter
7. Serve with wine sauce
