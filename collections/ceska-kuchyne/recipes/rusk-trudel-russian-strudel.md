## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) flour
- 4 eggs
- sour cream
- 28 dekagrams (16 lots) butter
- filling

## Directions

1. Mix flour, 4 eggs and sour cream to make a dough thinner than for noodles
2. Roll out and thicken with knife blade using 28 dekagrams butter
3. Fold 4-5 times
4. Add any desired filling
