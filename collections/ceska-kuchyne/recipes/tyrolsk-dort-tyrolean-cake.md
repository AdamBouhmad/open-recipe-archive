## Ingredients

- 375 grams well-beaten butter
- 375 grams finely crushed sugar
- finely grated lemon zest
- 375 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Mix butter with 375 grams finely crushed sugar
2. Add finely grated lemon zest and 375 grams fine flour
3. Work everything into dough and shape into cakes
4. Bake slowly on a cake sheet until golden
5. Turn cakes out onto a sieve when baked golden yellow, and when cool
6. Coat with optional preserves, layering them together
7. Brush everything with beaten egg
8. Bake again and coat with glaze
