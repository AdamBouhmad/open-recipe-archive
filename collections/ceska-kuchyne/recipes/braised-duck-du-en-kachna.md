## Ingredients

- duck
- butter
- beef broth
- wine
- onion
- bay leaf
- celery
- cloves
- pepper
- sardines

## Directions

1. Braise the duck in butter on both sides with beef broth, a glass of wine, onion, bay leaf, cut onion, celery, several cloves, pepper and two sardines. Baste frequently. Remove fat from the sauce, strain and add brown roux.
