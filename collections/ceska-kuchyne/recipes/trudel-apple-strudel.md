## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- water
- butter
- curd cheese
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- lemon zest, grated with half its sugar
- 9 dekagrams sugar
- 15 grams (1 lot) ground almonds
- cinnamon
- raisins
- sour cream or milk

## Directions

1. Mix 24 dekagrams flour with 2 eggs, 1 egg yolk, salt and water
2. Work the dough and cover with a warm baking pan, let it rest for a while
3. Roll out the dough, then stretch it as thinly as possible on a floured cloth
4. Brush the stretched dough with butter
5. Prepare a filling from curd cheese, 2 eggs, 2 egg whites, 9 dekagrams butter, lemon zest grated with half its sugar, 9 dekagrams sugar, 15 grams ground almonds, cinnamon and raisins
6. Spread the filling on the dough, roll it up
7. Place in a greased baking pan and pour sour cream or milk over it
8. Bake until golden
