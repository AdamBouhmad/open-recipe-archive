## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 liter water
- 1 liter wine
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- Zest of several lemons
- 0.5 liters good rum
- Juice of 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Crush 6 whole eggs and mix with 6 egg yolks using a whisk
2. Slowly add 1 liter of water and 1 liter of wine
3. Add 0.5 kilogram of sugar that has been rubbed with lemon zest
4. Continuously beat the mixture and heat slowly
5. Remove from stove
6. Slowly add 0.5 liters of good rum and juice from 3 lemons
