## Ingredients

- 250 grams finely ground almonds
- 250 grams butter
- 125 grams sugar
- ground anise
- grated lemon zest
- 4 egg yolks
- good wine
- 250 grams flour
- egg yolk for brushing
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the finely ground almonds
2. Mix with butter, sugar, ground anise, grated lemon zest, egg yolks, good wine, and flour
3. Work into dough
4. Knead well and work thoroughly
5. Cut into triangular pieces
6. Brush with beaten egg yolk
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
8. Bake slowly
