## Ingredients

- cleaned and gutted chicken
- fresh butter
- lemon juice
- onions
- carrot
- leek
- parsnip
- beef broth
- sugar
- white flour
- ginger

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the chicken, wash it, salt it inside, and place a piece of fresh butter in it.
2. Lightly rub the breast with lemon juice and butter, then place it in a pot and cover well.
3. In another pot, arrange thinly sliced onions, two small carrots, some leek and parsnip all cut into slices.
4. Add a piece of butter and let it stew for a while, then pour in a quart of good beef broth and let it cook for half an hour.
5. Half an hour before serving the chicken, strain this sauce over it and let it stew in a covered pot, but make sure the chicken remains completely white and the meat stays juicy.
6. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it drain.
7. Arrange it on a platter and pour good brown gravy over it, prepared as follows: Place a piece of fresh butter in a pot, add a small piece of sugar, and let it heat, then add a little white flour and make a brown thin roux, thin it with good beef broth to make a thickened sauce, add a little ground ginger, and mix well.
