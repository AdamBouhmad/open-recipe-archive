## Ingredients

- 1 small cupful of sugar
- sweet almonds
- 4 egg whites
- ½ teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Take 1 small cupful of sugar
2. Blanch sweet almonds and cut into strips so there is ½ cupful
3. Beat 4 egg whites into stiff peaks
4. Add 1 large spoonful of sugar and beat the peaks longer until thickened by the sugar so it can be cut
5. Mix the remaining sugar with the almonds and sprinkle into the peaks
6. Also add ½ teaspoonful of vanilla and mix slowly
7. Before making the wreaths, wax a baking sheet
8. Place small heaps on it and spread them with a spoon in the middle so they form round wreaths
9. Bake until pale with moderate heat
10. They are very good, beautiful and keep in dry conditions for up to a month
11. They should be made in the cold
