## Ingredients

- turnip
- good beef broth
- floury potatoes
- hot water
- parsley
- cooked meat
- butter

## Directions

1. Scald sliced turnip
2. Cook in good beef broth
3. Add potatoes cut into quarters which have been scalded with hot water
4. Cook until completely soft, adding broth as needed
5. Add chopped parsley
6. Serve usually with cooked meat and cutlets
7. Alternatively, small cooked potatoes may be added to turnip which was cooked with butter in broth
