## Ingredients

- 10.5 deciliters (3 tablespoons) good milk
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 24 decagrams (16 lots) semolina and sugar
- 12 eggs mixed in milk
- Salt
- Chopped lemon zest
- Gingerbread and butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Cook a thick porridge from 10.5 deciliters good milk, 12 decagrams butter, 24 decagrams semolina and sugar
2. When cooled, mix with 12 eggs beaten in milk, add salt and chopped lemon zest
3. Bake small cakes on a baking sheet smeared with wax, some of which brush with gingerbread mixed with butter
4. Spread a layer across the entire bottom of a bowl, arrange a smaller layer with gingerbread on top and continue until all are stacked
5. These cakes can serve as a flour dish
