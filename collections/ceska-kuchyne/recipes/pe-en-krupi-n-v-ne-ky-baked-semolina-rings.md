## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams fine semolina
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 75 grams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- salt
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook semolina in milk
2. Let cool
3. Mix in butter and egg yolks
4. Add salt
5. Roll in grated bread crumbs
6. Shape into rings
7. Fry on both sides in butter
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
