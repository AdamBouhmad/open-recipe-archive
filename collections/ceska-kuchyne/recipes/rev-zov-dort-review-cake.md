## Ingredients

- 4 cooked egg yolks
- 1 raw egg
- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) ground almonds
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 28 dekagrams (16 lots) flour
- piece of lemon zest

## Directions

1. Mix 4 cooked egg yolks, 1 raw egg, 14 dekagrams ground almonds, 14 dekagrams sugar, 12 dekagrams butter, 28 dekagrams flour, and lemon zest
2. Make a cake two fingers thick from the dough
3. Coat with jam, make a lattice from the dough, place on the jam
4. Bake the cake
