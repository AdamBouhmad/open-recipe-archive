## Ingredients

- two cups corn flour
- hot water
- small teaspoon butter
- small teaspoon sugar
- small teaspoon salt
- five tablespoons sweet milk
- three broken egg yolks
- one teaspoon baking powder mixed with one tablespoon wheat flour
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. Put two cups of corn flour in a pot, pour with hot water, let it stand half an hour and when it has cooled, mix in a small teaspoon of butter, a small teaspoon of sugar and a small teaspoon of salt, five tablespoons of sweet milk and three broken egg yolks
2. Then mix one teaspoon of baking powder in one tablespoon of wheat flour, add it to the batter and mix well
3. At the end, mix in snow from three egg whites and bake
