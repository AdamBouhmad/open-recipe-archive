## Ingredients

- wild duck
- vinegar
- water
- pepper
- new spice
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- salt
- 2 onions
- roux
- burnt sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the wild duck except for the head, remove the innards, and lard with bacon if desired.
2. Place on a baking sheet and baste with vinegar mixed with water.
3. Add pepper, new spice, thyme, bay leaf, lemon peel, and salt.
4. Add 2 sliced onions and let it rest for 24 hours.
5. Place everything in the oven and roast.
6. Once roasted, add roux and a piece of burnt sugar, then strain.
7. Chop the duck and serve with the sauce.
8. Alternatively, add grated bread to the sauce instead of roux, strain the sauce around the chopped duck, and garnish with lemon.
9. If the sauce is not sour enough, add juice from one lemon.
