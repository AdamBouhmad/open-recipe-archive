## Ingredients

- grapes, green and undeveloped
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the grapes as described in the previous section
2. On the second day, pour off the sugar and cook it to the sixth degree
3. Add the grape berries to it, cook everything together
4. Skim off the foam and select the grapes to dry
5. Dust them finely with pounded sugar, coating them thoroughly
6. Dry again and turn frequently
7. Once completely dried, store between fine paper in boxes
