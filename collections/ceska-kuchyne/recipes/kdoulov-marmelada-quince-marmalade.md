## Ingredients

- Quinces
- Sugar - equal measure to the pulp

## Directions

1. Clean and rub quinces
2. Cook until soft
3. Press the pulp through a fine sieve
4. Add sugar in the same measure as the pulp
5. Cook sugar to the sixth degree
6. Add quince pulp to it
7. Boil several times
8. Carefully skim the foam
9. Store the preserve in containers
