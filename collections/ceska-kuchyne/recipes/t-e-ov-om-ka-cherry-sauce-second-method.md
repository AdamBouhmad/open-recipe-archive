## Ingredients

- cherries
- water
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- sugar
- potato flour mixed in wine

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries together with the pits
2. Boil in water with cinnamon, lemon zest and sugar
3. Strain
4. When the sauce begins to boil again, add a small spoon of potato flour that has been mixed in wine
