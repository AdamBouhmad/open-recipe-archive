## Ingredients

- Fresh herring
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Oil
- Sour cream sauce

## Directions

1. Scale, clean and wash fresh herring
2. Make lengthwise cuts on both sides of each
3. Place them in a deep dish
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. Season with lemon juice
6. Pour oil over them
7. Let them lie for one hour, turning occasionally
8. Broil them on a grill
9. Arrange on a dish
10. Serve with sour cream sauce
11. Alternatively, arrange hot broiled herring on a heated dish and place pieces of parsley butter on top
12. Fresh herring may also be prepared like perch
