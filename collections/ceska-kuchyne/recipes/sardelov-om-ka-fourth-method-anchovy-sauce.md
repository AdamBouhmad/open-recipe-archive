## Ingredients

- butter
- egg yolks
- wheat flour
- finely chopped lemon zest
- finely cleaned anchovies
- good meat broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Beat butter until creamed
2. Add egg yolks and a little wheat flour
3. Add finely chopped lemon zest
4. Add finely cleaned anchovies
5. Mix everything with good meat broth to which some white wine has been added
6. Cook everything and use as a sauce
