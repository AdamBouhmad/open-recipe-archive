## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- ham
- lard
- butter
- salt
- onion
- juniper berries
- pepper
- brown coulisse
- good wine
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Place perfectly plucked and cooked woodcocks on a baking pan.
2. Wrap with ham and lard, place tightly next to each other.
3. Pour butter over, salt, wrap with lard and braise with onion, juniper berries and pepper.
4. Add a quarter liter of brown coulisse, good wine and a little lemon zest.
5. Braise the woodcocks in this sauce until tender.
6. Strain the sauce and pour over the woodcocks when serving.
