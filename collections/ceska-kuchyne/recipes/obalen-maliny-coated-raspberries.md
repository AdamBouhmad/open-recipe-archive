## Ingredients

- fully ripe, selected raspberries
- egg white
- powdered sugar
- cooked sugar

## Directions

1. Fully ripe, selected raspberries must be dried a bit.
2. Place them on a small baking pan and pour beaten egg white over them, in which they coat completely.
3. Then roll them several times in powdered sugar, dip in cooked sugar and coat again several times in sugar powder until finally they are placed on sieves and left to dry.
