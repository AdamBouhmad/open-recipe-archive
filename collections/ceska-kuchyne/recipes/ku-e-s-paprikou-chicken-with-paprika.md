## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- onion
- chicken
- salt
- flour
- beef broth
- sour cream
- paprika

## Directions

1. Place two spoonfuls of fresh butter in a pan, one thinly sliced onion, place two quartered chickens on it, salt it lightly, cover and let it stew for a while.
2. Then remove it to another pan, add a full spoonful of flour to the broth, mix it, pour a little beef broth and a few spoonfuls of sour cream and let it cook a bit more.
3. Strain this over the chicken, add about half a teaspoon of paprika and let it cook in the sauce.
