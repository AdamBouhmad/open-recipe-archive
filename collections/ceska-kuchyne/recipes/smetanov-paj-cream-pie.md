## Ingredients

- pie plate dough
- two and a half coffee cups of sweet cream
- three tablespoons of sugar
- half a coffee cup of sweet cream
- two tablespoons of corn starch
- two egg whites
- two tablespoons of sugar
- coconut

## Directions

1. Line a pie plate with pie dough and bake
2. Before it is baked, take two and a half coffee cups of sweet cream and three tablespoons of sugar and bring to a boil
3. Meanwhile, take half a coffee cup of sweet cream and two tablespoons of corn starch, make a paste and add it to the boiling cream
4. When thickened, pour onto the baked pie
5. This is for two pies
6. Flour can be used instead of starch, but starch is better
7. On top, put well-beaten snow with sugar and sprinkle with coconut and place in the oven briefly to brown
8. Each pie requires snow from two egg whites and two tablespoons of sugar
