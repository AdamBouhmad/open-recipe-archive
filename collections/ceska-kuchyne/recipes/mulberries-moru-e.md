## Ingredients

- sugar, one and a half kilos
- beautiful, selected, ripe mulberries

## Directions

1. Cook one and a half kilos of sugar until it reaches the sixth degree of thickness.
2. Add beautiful, selected, ripe mulberries to it and cook everything again.
3. Pour onto a dish, cover with paper, and let it sit undisturbed.
4. On the second day, drain off the sugar and cook it thicker.
5. Add the mulberries to it, skim off the foam, and place the preserve in containers.
