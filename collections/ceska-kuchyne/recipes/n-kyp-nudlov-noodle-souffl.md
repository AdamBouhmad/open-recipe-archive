## Ingredients

- thick, short noodles
- milk
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon zest, chopped
- 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) sweet cream
- butter for greasing the mold
- grated bread roll for coating
- cinnamon and sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Make thick, short noodles and cook them in milk, then let them cool
2. Beat 3 egg yolks with sugar, chopped lemon zest, and mix in 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) of sweet cream
3. Grease a mold and coat it with grated bread roll
4. Mix the cream mixture with the noodles and bake in the prepared mold
5. Turn out onto a serving dish and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar
