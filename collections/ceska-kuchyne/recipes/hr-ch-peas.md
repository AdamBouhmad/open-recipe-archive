## Ingredients

- peas
- soft cold water
- salt
- soda (if hard water is used)
- goose or pork lard, or crackling
- boiled smoked meat
- pickled cucumbers or raw cabbage

## Directions

1. Sort the peas the evening before, wash them thoroughly in cold water and let them soak in it overnight.
2. For cooking, take an elongated (tall) but not large pot.
3. Peas nearly double in volume when cooked, so if you want to cook about two small cups of peas, take a quart pot.
4. Drain the water in which the peas soaked and put the peas in the pot and add soft cold water until the pot is full.
5. If you do not have soft water, add a pinch of soda to it.
6. Cover and as the water boils away, replenish with hot water and when it has boiled away, salt it.
7. Then drain the water, pour the peas onto a platter, coat with goose or pork lard or crackling, garnish with boiled smoked meat and serve with pickled cucumbers or raw cabbage.
