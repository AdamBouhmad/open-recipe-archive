## Ingredients

- cold meat leftovers
- 6 large raw potatoes
- 2 to 3 eggs
- onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the cold meat, peel about six large raw potatoes and grate them. Add 2 to 3 eggs, chop some onion, add 2 tablespoons of flour and season to taste with salt and pepper. Mix well and bake in a well-buttered pan.
