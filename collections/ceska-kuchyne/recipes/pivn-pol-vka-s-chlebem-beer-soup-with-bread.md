## Ingredients

- one plate of sliced bread
- two quarts beer or one quart beer and one quart water
- a pinch of caraway
- salt
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- a piece of butter
- two ounces sugar
- one or two small cups cream
- lemon juice or vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Put about one plate of sliced bread in a pot
2. add two quarts of beer or one quart beer and one quart water
3. add a pinch of caraway and salt
4. let it boil until the bread dissolves
5. in another pot beat six egg yolks or three whole eggs
6. add a piece of butter, two ounces sugar and one or two small cups of cream
7. beat well and pour into the soup
8. let it simmer for a while longer while stirring constantly
9. pour into a bowl
10. if you want it sour, you can add lemon juice or several tablespoons of vinegar
11. this soup can also be made with water instead of beer, omitting the sugar, and is then called beggar's soup (serves 12 people)
