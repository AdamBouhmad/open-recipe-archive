## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1/2 cup sweet cream
- 1 teaspoon butter
- grated coconut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook 2 cups white sugar with 1/2 cup sweet cream and 1 teaspoon butter
2. Cook for 15 minutes
3. Add grated coconut as desired
4. Mix in 1 teaspoon vanilla
5. Pour onto a plate
6. When cooled, cut into squares
