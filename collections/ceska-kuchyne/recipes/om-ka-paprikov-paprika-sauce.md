## Ingredients

- piece of butter
- sliced bacon
- onion
- herb roots and vegetables
- several mushrooms
- two pods of paprika
- several grains of spice
- half liter broth
- lemon juice
- pale roux
- several tablespoons cream
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of butter, sliced bacon, onion, herb roots, vegetables, several mushrooms, two pods of paprika and several grains of spice
2. Pour over half liter broth
3. Add lemon juice and cook for half an hour
4. Strain the broth and remove fat
5. Add pale roux, several tablespoons cream and two egg yolks
