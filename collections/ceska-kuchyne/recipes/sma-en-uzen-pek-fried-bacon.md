## Ingredients

- Smoked bacon
- Lard
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Slice smoked bacon into very thin slices
2. Remove the skin
3. Place slices on a hot griddle
4. When browned on one side, turn and fry until crispy
5. Remove to a plate
6. Fry eggs in the rendered lard and serve with the bacon
