## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram peeled almonds
- egg white
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 1/4 kilogram raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds with egg white very finely
2. Cook the sugar until it reaches the sixth degree, then add the finely ground almonds to it
3. Press raspberries through a fine sieve and add 1/4 kilogram of the juice to the prepared mixture
4. Stir constantly and heat slowly until it thickens
5. When cooled, place in molds as desired and bake again
