## Ingredients

- canned salmon
- eggs
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Remove the salmon from the can onto a dish in the manner described above
2. Cook several eggs hard, peel and cut lengthwise into halves
3. Arrange the salmon and pour mayonnaise over it
