## Ingredients

- half a kilo of peeled and dried almonds
- egg whites
- lemon peel
- orange peel
- vanilla, finely ground
- cinnamon
- clove
- finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Grate half a kilo of peeled and dried almonds finely with egg whites.
2. Make snow from the egg whites, to which lemon and orange peel are added.
3. Add finely ground vanilla, cinnamon, clove, and finely ground sugar to the mixture.
4. Mix everything thoroughly and prepare as described for other soufflés.
