## Ingredients

- several egg yolks
- cold milk or soup
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat several egg yolks in cold milk or soup, salt it, add nutmeg
2. Place in a covered pot in hot water, then set the pot with water aside for three-quarters of an hour so the water maintains a slow boil until the jelly forms
3. Cut the jelly with a spoon and add to the soup
