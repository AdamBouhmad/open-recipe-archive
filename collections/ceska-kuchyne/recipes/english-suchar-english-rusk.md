## Ingredients

- 250 milliliters warm milk
- 70 grams butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon good yeast
- 500 grams fine sifted flour
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Add 70 grams butter to 250 milliliters warm milk and stir until dissolved
2. Add two beaten eggs and 1 tablespoon good yeast
3. Mix everything, then add 500 grams fine sifted flour and sugar to taste
4. Mix everything again and let it rise for at least one hour
5. Thoroughly knead everything, shape into small loaves, place on a greased baking sheet and bake until red
6. Once cooled, cut the suchar into thin slices, which are then baked again on both sides on a greased baking sheet
