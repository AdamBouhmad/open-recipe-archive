## Ingredients

- wine sabayon
- good rum or liqueur
- or maraschino liqueur, orange juice, sugar, and eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare ordinary wine sabayon and add several tablespoons of good rum or liqueur
2. Alternatively, use half maraschino liqueur and half orange juice with enough sugar and eight egg yolks
