## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- fresh parsley or dried parsley
- onion
- meat broth
- vinegar
- lemon peel
- ground pepper
- thyme
- cooked potatoes
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Place a spoon of butter in a pot and add a little flour to make a thin roux
2. Add a bit of green parsley in summer or dried parsley in winter
3. When the roux takes on a light golden color, add finely diced onion and let it froth
4. Pour in meat broth, a little vinegar, a piece of lemon peel, a little ground pepper and a piece of thyme
5. Let it simmer until the sauce is neither too thick nor too thin
6. Cut several cooked potatoes into it and add about a cup of sour cream, but then it must not boil
