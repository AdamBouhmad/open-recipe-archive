## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) ground almonds
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- 18 dekagrams butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- piece of lemon zest
- 7 egg whites
- salt
- grated roll
- sauce

## Directions

1. Mix 18 dekagrams ground almonds, 18 dekagrams sugar, 18 dekagrams butter, 9 egg yolks, 3 whole eggs, piece of lemon zest, snow from 7 egg whites and salt well
2. Grease a form and sprinkle with grated roll
3. Bake pudding in it and pour sauce over it
