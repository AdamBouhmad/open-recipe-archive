## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, 6
- Sugar, 4 tablespoons
- White breadcrumbs, grated, sufficient amount
- Salt
- Butter, melted and clarified
- Sour cream, 2 koflíky
- Sugar, 2 tablespoons
- Lemon rind, grated from 1 lemon
- Egg yolks, 5

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 4 tablespoons sugar until foamy
2. Add enough grated white breadcrumbs so it becomes thick like ordinary quennelles
3. Add salt and mix well
4. Shape small pieces with a coffee spoon and fry in hot clarified butter until golden
5. Boil 2 koflíky good sour cream
6. Add 2 tablespoons sugar and grated lemon rind from 1 lemon
7. Mix well and add the cooked quennelles to the cream
