## Ingredients

- several lamb kidneys
- butter or lard
- salt
- pepper
- shallot or small onion
- mushrooms
- fresh parsley
- lemon juice
- fresh butter
- potato croquettes

## Directions

1. Peel several lamb kidneys, split them in half lengthwise, clean them well
2. Brown them in hot butter or lard
3. Salt and pepper them
4. Add chopped shallot or small onion that has been browned in butter
5. Add a little chopped mushrooms that have been stewed in butter
6. Add a little chopped fresh parsley and finish with lemon juice
7. Mix in a piece of fresh butter
8. Arrange the kidneys on a plate, pour the sauce over them, and surround with potato croquettes
