## Ingredients

- poultry giblets ragout
- cooked peas
- mushrooms
- egg yolks
- bread roll
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare ragout from poultry giblets - combs, stomachs, and livers
2. Mix strained cooked peas into the ragout
3. Add mushrooms and several egg yolks
4. Mix in grated bread roll browned in butter
5. Bind with sour cream and work together well
