## Ingredients

- Veal lungs
- Salt water
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- New spices
- Ginger
- Pale sauce
- Lemon juice
- Burnt sugar, optional

## Directions

1. Clean the lungs and boil in salted water with vinegar, pepper, new spices and ginger until softened; then cut into strips, strain the liquid in which they were boiled, make pale sauce and prepare the sauce, add the lungs, let them simmer a while and season with lemon juice; if it is to be darker, burn a piece of sugar.
