## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 kilogram sugar
- cleaned and dried anise
- fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and add 1/4 kilogram sugar to them
2. Mix everything with fine flour
3. Knead the dough
4. Fill small molds with the dough
5. Bake slowly
