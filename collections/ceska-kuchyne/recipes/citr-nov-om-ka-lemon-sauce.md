## Ingredients

- nine dekagrams (six lots) of butter
- one tablespoon flour
- broth
- lemon cut into slices
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Heat nine dekagrams of butter
2. Add one tablespoon of flour and brown to a rosy color
3. Add broth
4. Add lemon cut into slices and nutmeg
5. Boil for a short time and strain
6. Serve with veal, chicken, and fish
