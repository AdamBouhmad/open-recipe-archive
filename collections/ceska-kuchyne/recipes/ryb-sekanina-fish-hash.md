## Ingredients

- pike or carp
- 5 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 bread rolls soaked in milk
- sage
- marjoram
- flower
- 4 cloves garlic
- half a small onion browned in butter
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) butter
- piece of lemon peel
- 4 raw eggs
- pepper
- vinegar
- olive oil
- optional mussels
- wine

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the fish, remove skin and bones, and chop finely.
2. Add the hard-boiled eggs and bread rolls soaked in milk.
3. Add a piece of sage and marjoram rubbed in the hand, a bit of flower, 4 cloves of garlic, and half a small onion browned in butter on 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of butter.
4. Add a piece of finely chopped lemon peel and 4 raw eggs.
5. Mix everything well on a dish.
6. Grease a small baking pan with butter, sprinkle with grated bread and bake in the oven.
7. Cut the mixture into equal pieces, add pepper, vinegar and olive oil.
8. If there are mussels, add them cooked in wine.
