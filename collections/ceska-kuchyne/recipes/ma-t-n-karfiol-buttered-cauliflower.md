## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- butter
- bread crust
- hot melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, carefully remove all greens so the florets do not break, rinse it, place it in salted water with a piece of butter, and cook until tender
2. Meanwhile, grate the bread crust and fry it in butter until golden
3. Carefully place the cooked cauliflower on a dish, sprinkle with the fried bread and pour hot melted butter over it
