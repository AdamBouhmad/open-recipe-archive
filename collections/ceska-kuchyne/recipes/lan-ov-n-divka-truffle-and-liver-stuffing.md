## Ingredients

- peeled truffles
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- cooked poultry livers

## Directions

1. Cut peeled truffles into fine pieces
2. Add finely chopped bacon
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Mix in finely beaten cooked poultry livers
