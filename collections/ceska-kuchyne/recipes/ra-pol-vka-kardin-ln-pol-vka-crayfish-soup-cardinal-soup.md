## Ingredients

- 30 crayfish
- salted water
- 200 grams butter
- leek
- parsley
- celery
- flour
- soup
- crayfish meat
- crayfish tails
- asparagus
- cauliflower
- fried roll cut into cubes

## Directions

1. For 3 liters of crayfish soup, cook 30 crayfish in salted water
2. Shell the cooked meat from tails and claws, remove intestines and gallbladder, crush everything else in a mortar with 200 grams of butter
3. Add finely chopped leek, parsley and celery, fry everything in butter, brown with flour
4. Slowly cook with soup for about an hour, strain, beat in a few egg yolks
5. Add shelled crayfish meat, tails, asparagus, cauliflower, or fried roll cut into cubes
