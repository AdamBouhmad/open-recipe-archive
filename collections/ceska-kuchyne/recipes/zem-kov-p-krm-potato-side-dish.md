## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- beef broth
- parsley
- fried onion

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes into quarters
2. Wash them several times
3. Pour hot water over them and let them stand covered
4. Drain the water
5. Place the potatoes in a baking dish and cook in beef broth without letting them fall apart
6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and fried onion
7. Serve usually with beef or veal
