## Ingredients

- 2-3 tomatoes
- sour bread or white bread, grated and soaked in sour cream, or softened cracker
- beef broth
- flower (seasoning)
- small lemon zest
- piece of sugar
- cooked or braised chicken

## Directions

1. Cook 2-3 tomatoes in wine or water
2. Add grated bread or white bread soaked in sour cream, or softened cracker
3. Pour in a little beef broth, season with flower and small lemon zest
4. Add a piece of sugar, mix well and strain over cooked or butter-braised chicken
