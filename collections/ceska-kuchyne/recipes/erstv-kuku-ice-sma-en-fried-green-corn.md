## Ingredients

- corn kernels from cobs
- butter
- lard
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Shell or scrape corn kernels from the cobs
2. Heat one spoon of butter and one spoon of lard
3. When hot, add the corn, salt and pepper
4. Cover and fry for a quarter hour, stirring frequently
