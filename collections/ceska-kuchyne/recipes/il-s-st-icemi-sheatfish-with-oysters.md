## Ingredients

- sheatfish
- butter
- oyster sauce or oyster ragout
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the sheatfish thoroughly and gut it
2. Rinse and cut in half along the back
3. Remove skin and bones from both halves
4. Slice the meat into small thin pieces and salt
5. Slowly stew in melted butter
6. Drain the butter, arrange the sheatfish pieces neatly on a platter, and pour oyster sauce over them or garnish with oyster ragout in the center
