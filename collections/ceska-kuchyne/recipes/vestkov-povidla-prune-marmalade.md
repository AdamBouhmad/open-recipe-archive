## Ingredients

- dried prunes
- piece of fresh butter
- finely chopped lemon zest
- ground clove
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook dried prunes, chop them finely or pass through a machine, or use prune butter.
2. Place in a saucepan and add a piece of fresh butter, some finely chopped lemon zest, some ground clove, and enough sugar to make it sufficiently sweet.
3. Let simmer briefly, then cool.
4. If too thick, stir in some warm broth, or if you don't have that, some water.
