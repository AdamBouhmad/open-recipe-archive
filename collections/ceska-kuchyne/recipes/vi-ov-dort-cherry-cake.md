## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) scalded almonds
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 eggs
- snow from 4 egg whites
- lemon zest
- several grated rolls
- picked pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Mix 12 dekagrams scalded almonds, 12 dekagrams sugar, 4 egg yolks, 3 eggs, snow from 4 egg whites, lemon zest, and several grated rolls into a fairly stiff dough
2. Place half in a form and bake for about a quarter hour
3. Spread picked pitted cherries on top
4. Cover with the remaining dough
5. Bake and glaze the cake
