## Ingredients

- Cooked cooled potatoes
- Red beets
- Celery, or various salads (chicken, lobster, etc.)
- Thick mayonnaise
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut cooked cooled potatoes into thin slices
2. Cut rounds from these slices with a mold about one and a half inches in diameter
3. Cut rounds of the same size from red beets
4. Arrange potatoes and beets alternately around the edge of a flat dish so individual rounds slightly overlap
5. Fill the middle of the dish with either diced celery, celery stalks, or various salads
6. Cover the middle with thick mayonnaise
7. Garnish with chopped parsley
