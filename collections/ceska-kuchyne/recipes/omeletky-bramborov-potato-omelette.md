## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 larger potatoes, previously boiled, peeled, cooled and grated
- 2 tablespoons peeled grated almonds
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- salt
- 12 egg whites
- sugar for garnish

## Directions

1. Cream 4 tablespoons of butter, add to it two larger grated potatoes that have been previously boiled, peeled and cooled, 2 tablespoons of peeled grated almonds, 4 tablespoons of sugar, twelve egg yolks, a small amount of salt, and mix everything well
2. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites from twelve eggs
3. Bake three equally large omelettes in fresh butter on both sides until golden
4. Sprinkle them generously with sugar, stack them one on top of the other on a plate and serve warm to the table
