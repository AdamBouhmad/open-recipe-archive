## Ingredients

- one kilogram pork meat
- one kilogram veal
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- new spices
- thyme
- bay leaf
- onion
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook the pork and veal in water with vinegar, pepper, new spices, thyme, bay leaf, onion, carrot, parsley, celery, and lemon zest.
2. Finely chop the cooked meat.
3. Strain the liquid through cloth; place the meat in a mold and add just enough of the liquid to cover it.
4. Season with pepper and place on ice.
5. If it does not unmold easily, wrap the form in a thin cloth and it will slip out easily.
