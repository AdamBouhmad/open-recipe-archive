## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of fresh butter
- three whole eggs
- six small pots of flour
- salt
- three tablespoons of yeast
- good milk or sour cream
- pitted prunes

## Directions

1. Cream one tablespoon of fresh butter
2. Beat three whole eggs into it and mix well
3. Add six small pots of flour, salt, and add three tablespoons of yeast, mix
4. Add as much good milk or sour cream as needed for dough thicker than for ordinary dumplings
5. Work well with a spoon and let rise
6. When risen, that is, when it has doubled in volume, remove the dough onto a board sprinkled with flour
7. Cut it into pieces about the size of a walnut, wrap a pitted prune in each piece
8. Place the dumplings on a board, let them rise a bit more, and cook as mentioned above
