## Ingredients

- half a pound of calf's or beef liver
- two ounces of beef marrow fat or lard
- marjoram
- lemon peel, finely chopped
- two cloves of garlic rubbed with salt
- flower
- pepper
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- salt
- bread roll, grated
- beef soup
- green parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Chop or grind half a pound of calf's or beef liver, remove all the sinews and membranes and rub it well with two ounces of beef marrow fat or lard
2. Add to it a little marjoram, a little finely chopped lemon peel, two cloves of garlic rubbed with salt, a pinch of flower, a pinch of pepper, beat two whole eggs and two yolks into it
3. Salt it properly and mix in grated bread roll until the mixture is neither too thin nor too thick
4. Mix everything well together and cook it into two quarts of boiling beef soup using a coffee spoon
5. Let it cook for about ten minutes and pour the soup into a bowl over chopped green parsley
