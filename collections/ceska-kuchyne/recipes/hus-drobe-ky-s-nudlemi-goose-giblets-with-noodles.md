## Ingredients

- Goose giblets
- Eggs
- Flour for noodles

## Directions

1. Cook the goose giblets as previously described.
2. Make fine noodles using two eggs.
3. When the giblets are sufficiently tender, strain the broth and let it settle, then pour it into a pot and let it come to a boil.
4. When it is boiling nicely, cook the noodles in it.
5. Have the neatly cut giblets prepared in a warm place.
6. Place them on a serving dish and arrange the cooked noodles on top.
7. Cooked giblets can also be surrounded with macaroni cooked in salted water, dressed with fat and sprinkled with cheese.
