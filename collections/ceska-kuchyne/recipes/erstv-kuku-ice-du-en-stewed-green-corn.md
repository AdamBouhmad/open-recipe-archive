## Ingredients

- six corn cobs
- milk
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Shell or scrape the corn kernels from six cobs and place in a bowl
2. Pour about one cup of milk over them and cook
3. Cook for about 10 minutes
4. Mix one spoon of butter with one spoon of flour, blend well and add to the corn
5. Salt, pepper and add a pinch of sugar
6. When it comes to a boil, serve
