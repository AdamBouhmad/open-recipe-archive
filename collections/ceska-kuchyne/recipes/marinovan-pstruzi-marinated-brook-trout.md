## Ingredients

- Small fried brook trout
- Onion, sliced
- Peppercorns, several grains
- Bay leaf
- Rosemary, a piece
- Lemon peel, peeled
- Good wine vinegar, enough to cover the trout
- Good olive oil, several spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Arrange small fried trout in a deep dish and let them cool completely
2. In a pot, arrange sliced onion, several peppercorns, a bay leaf, a piece of rosemary, and peeled lemon peel
3. Add good wine vinegar in enough quantity to completely cover the trout
4. Let this boil for one hour
5. When completely cooled, mix in several spoonfuls of good olive oil
6. Pour this over the trout
7. Let them lie in a cool place for several hours
8. Then arrange them on a dish
9. Pour the marinade over them and serve to the table
