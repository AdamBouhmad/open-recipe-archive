## Ingredients

- several beautiful white sweetbreads
- salt
- butter
- onion, grated
- fresh parsley, chopped
- lemon rind, chopped finely
- butter and white flour for pale sauce
- beef broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take several beautiful white sweetbreads and boil them for about five minutes in salted water
2. Place them in cold water and let them cool
3. Cut them into quarters
4. Place them in a pot with a piece of butter
5. Add grated onion, a little chopped fresh parsley, and a piece of finely chopped lemon rind
6. Season with salt and stew for several minutes
7. Meanwhile, make a pale butter sauce in another pot, dissolve it with beef broth and cook to a smooth sauce
8. Strain the sauce with a little lemon juice
9. Pour it over the sweetbreads
10. Let simmer for about 10 minutes
11. Place on a dish and serve
