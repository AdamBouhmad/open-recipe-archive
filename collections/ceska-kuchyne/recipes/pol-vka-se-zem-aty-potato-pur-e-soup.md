## Ingredients

- several boiled potatoes
- beef broth
- pepper
- a little grated garlic
- marjoram
- a thin beurre manié (butter and flour roux)
- chopped parsley
- boiled potatoes cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Peel and grate several boiled potatoes
2. Put them into strained beef broth
3. Season with pepper, add a little grated garlic and marjoram
4. Let it cook for a while
5. Press it through a sieve
6. Make a thin beurre manié and let it cook
7. Add a little chopped parsley
8. Pour onto a dish with boiled potatoes cut into cubes
9. Alternatively: scrape and cut into pieces as many raw potatoes as needed for the number of people
10. Clean them, place on the stove and cook them halfway
11. Drain the water, pour beef broth over them
12. If you have fresh mushrooms (either porcini or champignons), add them and let finish cooking
13. Season, add a little chopped parsley or chives
14. Pour into a dish and serve at table
15. You can also cook a little rice or torn groats in the soup
