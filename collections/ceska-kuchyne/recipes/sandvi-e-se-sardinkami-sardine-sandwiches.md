## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- French mustard
- sardines
- lettuce leaves
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Butter one bread slice and spread the other very thinly with French mustard
2. Halve two sardines and remove the backbone
3. Arrange sardine halves on the buttered bread
4. Sprinkle with a little lemon juice
5. Place a crisp lettuce leaf on top
6. Cover with the other slice
7. Press together gently and serve
