## Ingredients

- ripe peaches, pitted
- sugar (half cup per cup of peaches)

## Directions

1. Press well-ripened, pitted peaches through a sieve.
2. Add half cup of sugar per cup, let it cook until thickened, stirring constantly and skimming off foam.
3. Remove from heat, let cool halfway, pour into jars, and seal well only after completely cooled.
