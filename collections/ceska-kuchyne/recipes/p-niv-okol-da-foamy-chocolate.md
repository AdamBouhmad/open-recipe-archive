## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- 1 liter milk or water
- 250 grams chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs thoroughly
2. Add 1 liter of milk or water
3. Add 250 grams of chocolate
4. Place on warm stove and beat the chocolate constantly until serving time
