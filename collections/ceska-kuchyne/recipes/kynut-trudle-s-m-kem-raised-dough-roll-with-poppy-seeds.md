## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 small pot good sour cream
- flour
- salt
- piece of soaked pressed yeast
- 2 small pots ground poppy seeds cooked in milk
- half a small pot ground sugar
- zest of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Rub 4 tablespoons butter
2. Beat in six whole eggs
3. Mix well
4. Add 1 tablespoon sugar
5. Add 1 small pot of good sour cream
6. Add enough flour to make a dough like for small pastries
7. Salt slightly
8. Add a piece of soaked pressed yeast
9. Beat well and let it rise
10. Dust the rolling pin with flour
11. Place the risen dough on it and roll it with a rolling pin to about the thickness of a stalk
12. Mix 2 small pots of ground poppy seeds cooked in milk
13. Half a small pot of ground sugar
14. Very finely chopped zest of half a lemon
15. Spread the rolled dough generously with this
16. Roll up into a strudel
17. Grease a pan with butter and let the strudel bake
18. Remove to a platter and sprinkle with ground sugar
