## Ingredients

- chives
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- flower
- cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Let chopped chives stew in butter.
2. Add broth, salt, pepper, season with ground flower.
3. Brown with a light roux, let it simmer a little, and pour into a container.
4. You may also finish it with cream and egg yolks.
