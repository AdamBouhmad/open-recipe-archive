## Ingredients

- Veal feet
- Salt water
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Pepper
- New spices
- Ginger
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Lemon peel
- Cooked vegetables
- Crushed pepper
- Vinegar
- Olive oil

## Directions

1. Remove all bones from the feet, clean and boil in salted water to which vinegar, onion, pepper, new spices, ginger, thyme, bay leaves and lemon peel are added.
2. Remove the feet carefully and place in a dish, which can be decorated with various cooked vegetables; strain the liquid in which the feet were boiled over them and place them in cold storage.
3. Serve crushed pepper, vinegar and olive oil on the side.
