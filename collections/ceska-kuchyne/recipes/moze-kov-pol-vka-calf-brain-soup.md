## Ingredients

- veal brain
- egg
- bread roll
- butter
- cauliflower
- salt
- toasted bread roll

## Directions

1. Clean the veal brain and remove membranes
2. Cut into pieces and roll in egg and breadcrumbs
3. Fry in butter with chopped bread roll
4. Pound in a mortar
5. Place with broth on a roasting pan and cook
6. Strain and add a little cauliflower, butter, and salt
7. Pour over toasted bread roll
