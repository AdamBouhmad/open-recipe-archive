## Ingredients

- baked cast strudel
- raisins
- almonds cut lengthwise
- 2 whole eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- 7 deciliters (2 žejdlíky) sweet cream
- sugar rubbed with lemon

## Directions

1. Slice baked cast strudel into noodle-like pieces
2. Place in a mold alternately with raisins and lengthwise-cut almonds
3. Beat together 2 whole eggs, 10 egg yolks, 7 deciliters sweet cream and sugar rubbed with lemon
4. Pour this mixture over the almonds
5. Bake the soufflé
