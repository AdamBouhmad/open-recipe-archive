## Ingredients

- 10 grated baked potatoes
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 12 decagrams ground sugar
- 6 decagrams ground almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- finely grated lemon zest
- 1/4 liter sweet sour cream
- snow from 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Pass baked potatoes through a sieve
2. Mix with butter, ground sugar, ground almonds, egg yolks, grated lemon zest, sour cream, and snow from egg whites
3. Grease a mold and sprinkle with grated bread roll
4. Place mixture in mold
5. Set in boiling water for one hour
6. Serve with lemon sauce
