## Ingredients

- raspberries
- good wine vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour two parts of good wine vinegar over three parts of raspberries, cover everything with paper and let it stand for at least two days, then press and strain.
2. Add an arbitrary amount of sugar to the vinegar, boil together, remove the foam, and when everything has cooled, strain and pour into containers.
3. Vinegar prepared this way is suitable not only for preparing dishes, but can also be diluted with a good amount of water, sweetened and used as a cooling beverage.
