## Ingredients

- 4 deka tea
- 1 liter water
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 4-5 bitter oranges
- 0.5 bottle arak
- 1 bottle good rum

## Directions

1. Brew 4 deka of tea in 1 liter of water
2. Strain onto 0.5 kilogram of sugar that has been grated with 4-5 bitter oranges
3. Pour 0.5 bottle of arak and 1 bottle of good rum over the mixture
