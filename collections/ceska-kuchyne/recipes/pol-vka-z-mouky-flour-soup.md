## Ingredients

- 2 heaping tablespoons flour
- cold water
- 1 liter boiling water
- salt
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Whisk 2 heaping tablespoons flour in a little cold water
2. While stirring, slowly pour in 1 liter of boiling water
3. Season with salt
4. Add 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter
5. Simmer for a while
6. Add 2 egg yolks
