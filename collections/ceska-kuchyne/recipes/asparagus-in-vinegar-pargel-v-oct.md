## Ingredients

- medium-sized asparagus
- salt
- good vinegar (not too sharp)

## Directions

1. Clean medium-sized asparagus
2. Cut into two-centimeter long pieces
3. Wash
4. Cook in salted water
5. Lay on a sieve to drain
6. Place into jars or stone vessels
7. Pour with good vinegar that is not too sharp
8. After eight days, strain off the vinegar
9. Boil it again
10. Strain it, and when cooled, pour it back over the asparagus
11. Seal the vessel completely and place in a cool location
