## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 24 dekagrams sugar
- 10 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- cinnamon
- finely chopped lemon zest
- clove
- brown grated roll

## Directions

1. Grind 24 dekagrams almonds with 24 dekagrams sugar and sieve
2. Add 10 eggs and 4 egg yolks
3. Add snow from 4 egg whites
4. Add cinnamon, finely chopped lemon zest and clove
5. Mix well
6. Bake in a form sprinkled with brown grated roll, or steam in boiling water
7. If steamed, make a wine sauce to serve
