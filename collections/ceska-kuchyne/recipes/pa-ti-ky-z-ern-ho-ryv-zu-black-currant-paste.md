## Ingredients

- black currants
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove berries from their stems
2. Place in containers, cover with bladder and put in boiling water
3. Cook for 20 to 30 minutes
4. Press through a sieve
5. Mix the pressed mass with juice and sugar
6. Slowly bring to a boil several times with constant stirring
7. When completely thick, spread onto slate tablets
8. Peel and stack together
