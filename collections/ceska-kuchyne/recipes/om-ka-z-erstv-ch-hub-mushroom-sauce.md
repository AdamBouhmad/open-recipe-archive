## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- beef broth
- pale butter roux or sour cream and flour
- clove

## Directions

1. Clean and chop fresh mushrooms and cook them for about a quarter hour in beef broth
2. Brown the sauce with pale butter roux or thicken it with sour cream and flour
3. Season with a little clove
4. Let it cook and pour into a bowl with the mushrooms
