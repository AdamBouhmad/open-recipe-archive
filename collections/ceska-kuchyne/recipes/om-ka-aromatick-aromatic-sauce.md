## Ingredients

- several bread rolls soaked in vinegar
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- a little thyme
- several bay leaves
- one teaspoon capers
- two sardines
- a piece of onion
- oil
- wine vinegar
- cold beef broth

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar several bread rolls soaked in vinegar with three hard-boiled egg yolks, a little thyme, several bay leaves, one teaspoon capers, two sardines and a piece of onion
2. Add a little oil and wine vinegar
3. Strain it and thin with cold beef broth as needed
4. This sauce is suitable for beef and cold veal roast
