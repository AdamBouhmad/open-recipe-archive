## Ingredients

- small trout
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley
- vegetables
- lettuce
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean, gut, wash, and salt the small trout and let rest at least half an hour in a cold place
2. Dry in clean cloth
3. Coat in flour and beaten eggs, then sprinkle several times with finely grated bread crumbs
4. Fry carefully in hot butter
5. Garnish with green parsley, fine vegetables, lettuce, or lemon cut into quarters
