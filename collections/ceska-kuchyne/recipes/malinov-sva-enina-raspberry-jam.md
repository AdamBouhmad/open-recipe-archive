## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar (half cup sugar per cup of raspberries)

## Directions

1. Carefully pick over the raspberries and pick them clean, put them in a pot, set on fire and crush them in the pot.
2. When the mixture has come to a boil several times, add sugar—half cup of sugar per cup of raspberries.
3. Cook for half to three-quarters of an hour.
