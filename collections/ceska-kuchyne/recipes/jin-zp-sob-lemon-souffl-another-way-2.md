## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- fine crushed sugar
- grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Whip 4 egg whites perfectly
2. Mix in sufficient fine crushed sugar and grated lemon zest
3. Work everything well, put in a mold, cover with paper, and bake slowly
