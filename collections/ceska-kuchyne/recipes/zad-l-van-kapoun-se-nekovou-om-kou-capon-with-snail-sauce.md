## Ingredients

- capon
- 5 sardels
- 30 gr. butter
- 2 cloves garlic
- clove flower
- pepper
- marjoram petals
- new spice
- half a rusk

## Directions

1. Cook the capon as for wine sauce
2. Strain the broth
3. For the sauce, take 5 sardels, clean them well and remove all bones
4. Add 30 gr. butter to them, mix well
5. Add 2 cloves grated garlic, clove flower, pepper, marjoram petals, new spice and half a grated rusk
6. Place the capon with all the mixture into part of the broth
7. If the sauce is too thick, add more broth and simmer for a while
