## Ingredients

- 1 pint white wine
- 1 pint water
- A piece of sugar
- A piece of cinnamon stick
- A piece of lemon peel
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 spoons good sour cream
- 1 small spoon flour
- A little cold wine
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Boil white wine with water
2. Add a piece of sugar, cinnamon stick, and lemon peel
3. Boil together, then strain
4. Beat 4 egg yolks in a bowl
5. Mix with 2 spoons sour cream, 1 small spoon flour, a little cold wine, and a pinch of salt
6. Slowly pour the boiling wine over the mixture, stirring constantly
7. Keep over heat for a short time but do not let it boil
8. Serve in coffee cups
