## Ingredients

- veal leg
- bacon, cut into thin strips
- spices
- onion, finely chopped
- green parsley
- salt
- onion, sliced into rounds
- carrot
- lemon
- orange peel, chopped
- white wine, 1 bottle
- brandy, a little
- beef broth, a few tablespoons
- sugar, 1 teaspoon
- spices

## Directions

1. Lard the leg with thin strips of bacon that have been coated in spices, finely chopped onion and green parsley
2. Salt the leg and place in an appropriate pot
3. Add sliced onion, carrot, lemon, chopped orange peel
4. Pour in white wine and a little brandy
5. Add a few tablespoons of beef broth and 1 teaspoon of sugar
6. Season with various spices
7. Cover the pot tightly with a lid and stew over moderate heat until tender
8. Remove the leg to a platter, strain the sauce over it and serve
