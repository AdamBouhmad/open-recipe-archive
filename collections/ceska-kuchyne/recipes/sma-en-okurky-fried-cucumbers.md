## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- flour
- melted fat

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers, slice them into thicker slices, remove the cores with a special tool, salt them
2. Let them stand for a quarter hour, then drain, dry, coat in flour and fry in hot melted fat
