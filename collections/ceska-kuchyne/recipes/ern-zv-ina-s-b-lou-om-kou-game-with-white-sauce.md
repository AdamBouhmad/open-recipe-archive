## Ingredients

- game meat
- all spices
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon zest
- vinegar
- water
- egg yolk
- sour cream
- sugar
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Take a piece of game that can be well cut.
2. Add all spices, thyme, bay leaf, lemon zest and vinegar with water.
3. Marinate and clean the game, then braise until completely tender.
4. Slice the game, add an egg yolk to the sauce, 3.75 deciliters of sour cream and a piece of sugar.
5. Simmer for a quarter hour, arrange the game on a dish, strain the sauce over it and garnish with buttered potatoes.
