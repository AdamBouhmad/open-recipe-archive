## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Grated vanilla chocolate

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar
2. Add grated vanilla chocolate to it
3. Mix slowly and continue cooking with constant stirring until no bubbles appear
4. Pour the thickened liquid onto paper
5. When it hardens, cut it into pieces
6. To remove the paper, first dampen the bottom with a damp sponge
