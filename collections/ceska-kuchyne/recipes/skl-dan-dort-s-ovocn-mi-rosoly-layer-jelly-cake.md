## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 0.5 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 egg whites
- raspberry or currant or other fruit jelly
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 cup butter in a deep bowl and beat well
2. Gradually add 1 cup sugar in small pieces and beat well with the butter
3. Add 3 egg yolks one by one, mixing well each time
4. Sift 2 cups flour with 2 teaspoons baking powder
5. Pour 0.5 cup milk into batter and add half the sifted flour
6. Add another 0.5 cup milk and remaining flour, beat well
7. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla extract and gently fold in stiff peaks from 3 egg whites
8. Divide among three greased plates and bake
9. When cooled, spread each layer with raspberry, currant or other fruit jelly and stack together
10. Leave top layer unspread with only powdered sugar or cover with sugar glaze
