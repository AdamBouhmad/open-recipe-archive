## Ingredients

- Smoked meat
- Horseradish
- Rye bread
- Dumplings with cabbage or cooked peas with pickled cucumbers (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil smoked meat in several changes of water until tender
2. Slice into neat pieces
3. Arrange on a heated dish
4. Serve with grated horseradish and rye bread, or with dumplings and cabbage, or cooked peas with pickled cucumbers
