## Ingredients

- quail
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- dry buttered toast

## Directions

1. Clean quail nicely and split them along the back
2. Place them skin side down on well-buttered wire tongs (broiler) and roast slowly until nicely browned
3. Salt and pepper them and brush generously with butter
4. Serve on dry buttered toast
