## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter medium barley
- butter
- beef broth
- 3 egg yolks
- toasted bread roll

## Directions

1. Cook the barley in water with butter
2. Add beef broth
3. Strain the broth
4. Add 3 egg yolks
5. Pour over toasted bread roll
