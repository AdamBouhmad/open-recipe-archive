## Ingredients

- tripe, well cleaned and rinsed
- cold water
- salt
- beef broth
- green parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- pepper
- garlic
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Put well-cleaned and rinsed tripe in a pot, pour cold water over and simmer half an hour
2. Remove, clean again, rinse in cold water, put in another pot, pour fresh cold water over, salt and simmer until tender
3. Remove, slice into strips and put in a pot
4. Pour beef broth over, add finely chopped green parsley, fry in a small amount of butter, season with ground pepper, add a clove of garlic and a pinch of marjoram
5. Let simmer a while longer and pour into a soup bowl
