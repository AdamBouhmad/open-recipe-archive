## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters milk or cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- piece of sugar
- flour
- lemon zest
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix milk or cream, egg yolks, whole eggs, sugar, and lemon zest
2. Add salt
3. Add enough flour to make thick batter
4. Butter baking sheet generously and spread batter not too thick
5. Bake
6. Break up with two forks
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon or sugar with vanilla
8. Usually served with preserves
