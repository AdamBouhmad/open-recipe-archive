## Ingredients

- two small pots of fine farina
- two whole eggs
- fresh butter
- one teaspoon of ground sugar
- salt
- one small pot of sour cream
- five egg yolks
- one tablespoon of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Cook two small pots of fine farina into thick porridge
2. Remove from heat and let cool
3. Beat two whole eggs into it
4. Add a piece of fresh butter, one teaspoon of ground sugar, a little salt, and mix well
5. With a coffee spoon, take pieces and drop into boiling salted water
6. When cooked, let them drain on a clean cloth
7. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with grated bread roll crust and arrange the dumplings in it
8. Beat one small pot of sour cream with five egg yolks, one tablespoon of ground sugar, and mix well
9. Pour over the dumplings and let cook in steam for one hour
10. Place in a hot oven for a short while, then remove
11. Sprinkle with ground sugar and serve
