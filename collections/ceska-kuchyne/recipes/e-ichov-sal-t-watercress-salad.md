## Ingredients

- watercress
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- sugar
- endive or cabbage
- sour apples

## Directions

1. Clean the selected watercress well and prepare it with vinegar and oil, a little salt and sugar
2. Put it on a plate either alone or border it with endive, cabbage, or peeled apples sliced into rounds
