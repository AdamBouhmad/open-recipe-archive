## Ingredients

- two pounds beef meat
- water
- two tablespoons vinegar
- a piece of lemon peel
- half an onion, finely chopped
- 5-6 grains of allspice
- 4-5 cloves
- bay leaf
- salt
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon sugar
- one heaping tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Cook about two pounds of beef meat with all the vegetables, or use leftover meat.
2. Cut it into neat pieces and pour water over it until covered.
3. Add two tablespoons of vinegar, a piece of lemon peel, half a finely chopped onion, 5-6 grains of allspice, 4-5 cloves, a bay leaf, and salt.
4. Cover and let it simmer for a while.
5. Prepare a roux from two tablespoons of butter, add one tablespoon of sugar and when the sugar browns, add one heaping tablespoon of flour and let it roast until brown.
6. Mix the roux into the meat and serve.
7. Whoever wants it more sour can add more vinegar to taste, and whoever wants it sweeter can add sugar.
