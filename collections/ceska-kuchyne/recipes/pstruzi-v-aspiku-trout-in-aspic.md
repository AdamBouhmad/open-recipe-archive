## Ingredients

- small trout
- vinegar
- water
- vinegar for cooking
- bay leaf
- pepper
- onion
- lemon
- salt
- lemons for decoration
- truffles
- crayfish tails
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- aspic

## Directions

1. Clean, gut, and wash the trout carefully
2. Pour boiling vinegar over them, cover with paper, and let rest until they turn completely blue
3. Prepare only as much water as needed to cover the trout, add vinegar, bay leaf, pepper, onion, lemon, and salt, then cook
4. Add the trout to this liquid but do not boil; after a quarter hour remove them and let cool
5. Decorate molds with lemons, truffles, crayfish tails, hard-boiled eggs, and capers
6. Pour aspic over the decorations, and when set, arrange the trout sliced lengthwise and sparsely throughout the mold
7. Pour aspic over again and layer in stages, repeating until the mold is full
8. Place the full mold on ice to set the aspic
9. Unmold and arrange the trout in various ways, tastefully decorated, and serve
