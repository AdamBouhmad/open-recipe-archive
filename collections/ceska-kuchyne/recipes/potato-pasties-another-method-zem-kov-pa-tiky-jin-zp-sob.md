## Ingredients

- several nice potatoes
- parsley
- onion, chopped
- butter
- salt herring, soaked and deboned
- yeast dough
- cream
- egg
- sardelova sauce

## Directions

1. Slice potatoes and braise with parsley and chopped onion in butter
2. Chop the salt herring into small pieces
3. Line a form with yeast dough
4. Layer chopped herring, cream-covered potatoes, and salt herring
5. Cover with dough
6. Bake for one hour
7. Remove skin with a knife
8. Serve with sardelova sauce
