## Ingredients

- wine vinegar
- two onions
- nutmeg
- lemon
- bay leaf
- thyme
- pepper
- cloves
- garlic

## Directions

1. Prepare spiced vinegar from wine vinegar, to which are added two onions, nutmeg, lemon, bay leaf, thyme, pepper, cloves, garlic and similar ingredients.
2. Depending on what taste the vinegar is to have, the housewife adjusts the mixture of spices.
3. Let the vinegar remain with the spices in the sun or on warm stoves for at least three months, then strain and use as desired for preparing dishes.
