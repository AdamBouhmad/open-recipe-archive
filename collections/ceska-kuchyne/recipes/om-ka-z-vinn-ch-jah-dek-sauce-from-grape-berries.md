## Ingredients

- berries from sweet grapes
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- orange peel
- clove
- sugar
- toast from rolls
- boiling liquid

## Directions

1. Remove the berries from the grapes
2. Stew for about half an hour with cinnamon, lemon peel, orange peel, and clove
3. Add sugar and toast from rolls
4. Pour with the boiling liquid
5. Strain through a sieve
