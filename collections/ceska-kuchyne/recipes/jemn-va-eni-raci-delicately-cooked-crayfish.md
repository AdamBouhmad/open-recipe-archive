## Ingredients

- Onion
- Caraway
- Pepper
- Spice
- Water
- Wine
- Salt
- Melted butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut onion into small pieces, and simmer with caraway, pepper, and other spices in equal parts water and wine.
2. Strain the boiling salted liquid over the cleaned crayfish, cover the vessel tightly, and simmer vigorously for ten to twelve minutes.
3. Strain everything, pour melted butter over the crayfish, garnish with green parsley, arrange neatly on a platter, and serve.
