## Ingredients

- young, tender watercress leaves
- salt
- good oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim young, tender leaves
2. Rinse
3. Season with salt
4. Pour good oil and vinegar over them or prepare with salad sauce
5. Serve to table
