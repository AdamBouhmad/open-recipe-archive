## Ingredients

- mutton breast, neck, or ribs
- salt
- parsley root
- celery
- carrots
- 3 peppercorns
- sardines
- 15 grams (1 lot) butter
- 3 cloves garlic
- marjoram flowers
- half a grated bread roll
- lemon cut into slices or quarters

## Directions

1. Place mutton on a baking pan, salt it
2. Add parsley root, celery, carrots, and peppercorns
3. Braise until meat is tender
4. Strain broth over meat on another pan
5. Clean 4-5 sardines and remove bones
6. Rub sardines with butter, add 3 cloves garlic, marjoram flowers, and half grated bread roll
7. Add this mixture to the meat and braise briefly
8. Arrange on a platter and surround with lemon slices or quarters
