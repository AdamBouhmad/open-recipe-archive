## Ingredients

- about half kilogram good butter
- about 16 egg yolks
- lemon zest finely shaved
- finely ground cinnamon
- clove
- half kilogram ground sugar
- half kilogram finest flour

## Directions

1. Heat the baking pan
2. Spread about half kilogram good butter on it
3. Beat 16 egg yolks into the butter
4. Beat and mix everything together for at least half an hour
5. Add finely shaved lemon zest, finely ground cinnamon, clove, half kilogram ground sugar, and half kilogram finest flour
6. Mix and beat everything together
7. Make stiff snow from the remaining egg whites and mix into the described dough
8. Pour the prepared dough into greased baking forms
9. Bake slowly and gently
10. The cakes must be carefully removed from the forms while still hot, but never immediately after they come from the oven, as they are prone to collapse and fall
