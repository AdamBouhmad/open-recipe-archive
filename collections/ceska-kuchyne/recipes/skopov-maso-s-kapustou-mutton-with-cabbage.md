## Ingredients

- mutton meat
- vegetables
- cabbage
- cabbage broth
- 3 cloves garlic
- pepper
- marjoram
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Wash mutton and boil it with vegetables
2. Remove outer leaves of cabbage, clean and cut into quarters or remove ribs from each leaf separately
3. Boil prepared cabbage leaves, drain water
4. Pour mutton broth over it, add 3 cloves minced garlic, pepper, and marjoram
5. Braise until tender and thicken with brown gravy
6. If cabbage was cut into quarters, arrange carefully on plate so it does not break, and strain gravy into the cooking liquid
7. Warm meat in this, arrange neatly in the center of platter, and cabbage around it
