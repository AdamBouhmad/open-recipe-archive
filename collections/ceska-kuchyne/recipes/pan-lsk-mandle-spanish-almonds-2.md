## Ingredients

- almonds prepared white and sugared as previously described
- carmine dissolved in gum water
- dissolved sugar

## Directions

1. Coat the almonds with constant turning on warm stoves
2. Color them with carmine dissolved in gum water (both substances obtainable from a materials supplier)
3. Once the layer has hardened and dried, pour dissolved sugar over again
4. Turn the almonds in it until they reach the size of pigeon eggs
