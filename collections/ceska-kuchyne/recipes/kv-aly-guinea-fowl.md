## Ingredients

- Guinea fowl
- Juniper berries
- Onion
- Lard
- Pepper
- Beef stock

## Directions

1. Prepare guinea fowl for roasting, save the stomach, liver and lungs
2. Crush about 6 juniper berries and rub the guinea fowl with them
3. Chop the onion and fry it in lard
4. When the onion is golden, place the guinea fowl on it
5. Add several peppers, pour in beef stock and roast well on both sides
6. Cook the saved organs at the same time with the guinea fowl
