## Ingredients

- fresh ripe tomatoes
- onions
- salt
- brown sugar
- mustard powder
- black pepper
- cinnamon
- cloves
- red paprika
- nutmeg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place fresh ripe tomatoes in a large clean pot, crush with a potato masher, and for each gallon of this pulp add three finely chopped or grated onions, 4 tablespoons of salt, 5 brown sugar.
2. Mix everything and let it cook completely.
3. Press through a sieve, pressing until only seeds and skins remain in the sieve.
4. Place the pressed pulp back on the stove and add the following spices: 4 tablespoons of mustard powder, 8 tablespoons of black pepper, 2 tablespoons of cinnamon, 2 small teaspoons of cloves, 1 teaspoon of red paprika, 1 grated nutmeg, 3.5 cups of vinegar.
5. Simmer slowly but not vigorously until it is thicker than syrup, stirring so it does not burn.
6. Fill bottles while hot, seal and wax them.
