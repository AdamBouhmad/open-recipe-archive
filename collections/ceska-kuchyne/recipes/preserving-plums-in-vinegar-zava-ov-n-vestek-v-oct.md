## Ingredients

- plums
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- cloves, several
- good wine vinegar, two cups

## Directions

1. Pit the plums and place them in a pot.
2. Cover with sugar and add ground cinnamon and several cloves.
3. Pour in two cups of good wine vinegar.
4. Place the pot in cold water so that the water reaches halfway up the sides of the pot.
5. Tie the pot with paper and boil the water for a full hour.
6. Then tie the pot with a bladder and let it cool in the water.
7. For the first 14 days, shake the pot occasionally so the plums sink to the bottom.
8. It is best to seal the pot with some fat so that air cannot enter.
