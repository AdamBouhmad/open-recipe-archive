## Ingredients

- 8 to 10 hard eggs
- 45 grams fresh butter
- 30 grams white flour
- 3.5 deciliters good sweet cream
- Butter for greasing
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel 8 to 10 hard eggs, cut in half, and remove the yolks.
2. Cut the whites into thin strips.
3. Rub 45 grams fresh butter and add the removed yolks and 30 grams white flour, grinding them together.
4. Pour in 3.5 deciliters good sweet cream.
5. Pour onto a baking sheet and stir over heat until thickened.
6. Let cool.
7. Add the sliced whites to the cooled mixture.
8. Place everything on a greased baking sheet sprinkled with bread crumbs and bake in the oven.
