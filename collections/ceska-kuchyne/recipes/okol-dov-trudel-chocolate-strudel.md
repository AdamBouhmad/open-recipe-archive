## Ingredients

- dough for ordinary strudel
- sugar
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) ground almonds
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) grated chocolate
- 6 egg yolks
- egg white snow
- sour cream
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Make dough for ordinary strudel and work it well
2. Let rest under a lukewarm bowl
3. Stretch the dough
4. Sprinkle the buttered dough with sugar, 9 dekagrams ground almonds, 18 dekagrams grated chocolate
5. Add 6 grated egg yolks and snow from egg whites
6. Roll up strudel
7. Pour sour cream over it and sprinkle with grated chocolate
