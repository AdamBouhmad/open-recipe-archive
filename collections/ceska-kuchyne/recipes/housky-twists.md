## Ingredients

- dough for rolls
- 1 egg
- poppy seeds, caraway seeds and salt

## Directions

1. You can make twists from the same dough as rolls.
2. Divide the risen dough into small pieces, roll them into longer pieces and always braid 3 pieces together into one twist.
3. Let them rise, then brush them with beaten egg, sprinkle thickly with unground poppy seeds, and let them bake.
4. You can also sprinkle them with salt and caraway seeds or poppy seeds mixed with salt.
