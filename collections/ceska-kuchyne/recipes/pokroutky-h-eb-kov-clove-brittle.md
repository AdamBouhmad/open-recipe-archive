## Ingredients

- dark clove leaves
- clove spice
- boiling water
- clove essence
- refined fine sugar

## Directions

1. Take dark clove leaves and place them with clove spice in a closed pot
2. Pour enough boiling water to completely submerge the leaves
3. Seal the pot well and let it sit on warm stove for several hours
4. Press out the entire contents completely
5. Add clove essence
6. Boil this liquid with refined fine sugar until completely and firmly thickened
7. Pour it onto paper and when cooled, peel it off and cut into small brittle pieces
