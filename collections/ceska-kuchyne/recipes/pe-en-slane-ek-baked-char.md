## Ingredients

- char
- water
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Place char in water, remove skin, tear open lengthwise, remove bones, slice diagonally, dry
2. Coat with flour, dip in egg and grated bread, and bake in butter
