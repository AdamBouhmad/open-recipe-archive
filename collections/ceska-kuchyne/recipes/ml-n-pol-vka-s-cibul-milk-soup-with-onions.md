## Ingredients

- small white onions
- butter
- good milk
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- dried bread roll, sliced

## Directions

1. Peel small white onions, cut them into small pieces and stew them in butter until soft and completely white
2. Then place them on a sieve and let them drain
3. Boil as much good milk as you want soup, salt it, pepper it, add the onion and let everything cook together
4. After cooking strain the soup, add several beaten egg yolks or whole eggs, mix well and pour into a dish with dried sliced bread roll
