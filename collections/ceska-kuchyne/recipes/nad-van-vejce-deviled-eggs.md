## Ingredients

- eggs (about one dozen)
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- butter (heated)
- vinegar (two spoons)

## Directions

1. Boil about one dozen eggs hard
2. Let them cool in cold water
3. Peel them and cut in half so that the halves of whites remain whole
4. Remove yolks and mix finely
5. To the mixed yolks add a little mustard, pepper, salt, heated butter, two spoons of vinegar and mix well
6. Put the mixture back into the whites, taking care to make them look nice
7. Put about as much filling as would fit into two whites and pour more vinegar into that
8. Mix and pour over the eggs
9. These eggs are good for outings
