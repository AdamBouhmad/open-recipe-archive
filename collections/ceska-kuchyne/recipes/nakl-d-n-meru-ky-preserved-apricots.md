## Ingredients

- Half-ripe apricots
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel half-ripe apricots.
2. Boil the sugar until it thickens completely.
3. Place the apricots in jars, pour sugar over them, seal well, and place in a pot with hay.
4. Boil for a while, then place in a cold location.
