## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- salt
- lard for larding
- pepper
- bay leaf
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the pheasant, wash thoroughly, and salt.
2. Lard with bacon, especially on the breast from both sides.
3. Truss like ordinary poultry and place on a baking dish.
4. Put several peppercorns, a bay leaf, a piece of lemon peel, and a piece of chopped bacon underneath.
5. Roast until done, cut into pieces, and arrange neatly on a platter.
