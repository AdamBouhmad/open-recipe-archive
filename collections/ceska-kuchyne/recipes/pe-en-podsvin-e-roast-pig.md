## Ingredients

- Young pig
- Salt
- Pepper
- Caraway seeds
- Bread
- Butter or lard
- Beer or cider
- Lemons
- Mustard or cabbage

## Directions

1. Clean the pig thoroughly
2. Rub inside with ground pepper and salt
3. Sprinkle with caraway seeds
4. Insert a slice of bread
5. Sew closed
6. Tie the legs and cover the ears with greased paper cones
7. Place on a shallow roasting pan over thin sticks that are not sooty
8. Position pig leg-side up so it does not touch the pan
9. Pour several spoonfuls of beer or cider over it
10. Place in oven and brush frequently with fresh butter
11. When the skin is crispy and reddened, turn and continue roasting, brushing with butter
12. When nearly done, brush several times with beer to make the skin crispier
13. When done, make an incision around the neck to allow steam to escape and prevent the skin from becoming soft
14. Place on a dish and surround with lemon slices
15. Place a lemon slice in the mouth
16. Serve with either cabbage or mustard on the side
