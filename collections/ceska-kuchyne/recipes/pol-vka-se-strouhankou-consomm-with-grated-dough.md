## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- good broth
- chives

## Directions

1. Make dough as for noodles but make it as stiff as possible
2. When it is well kneaded, grate it on a grater
3. While grating, frequently spread out the grated dough so it dries
4. Cook as much grated dough in good broth as needed so it is neither too thin nor too thick
5. Let it cook nicely
6. Pour the broth into a dish and add finely chopped chives
