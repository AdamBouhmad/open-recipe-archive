## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- almonds

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the ripe plums
2. Insert a whole almond in place of each pit
3. Dry them in an oven or drying room
4. To use them, soak in milk
5. Roll them in flour mixed with sugar
6. Fry them in butter
