## Ingredients

- a piece of fresh butter
- meat broth
- a little flower
- salt
- 1 egg
- parsley or parsnip, chopped

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of fresh butter, meat broth, a little flower and salt in 2 litres of water
2. Make a thick grating from one egg
3. Strain the soup and add the grating
4. Add chopped parsley or parsnip
