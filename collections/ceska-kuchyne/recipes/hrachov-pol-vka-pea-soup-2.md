## Ingredients

- Peas
- 3 cloves garlic
- Pepper
- Marjoram
- Roux (bledá jíška)
- Fried bread, bread, barley groats, or rice

## Directions

1. Cook the peas until soft
2. Add 3 cloves of garlic, pepper, marjoram, and roux
3. Strain the soup over fried bread, bread, barley groats, or rice
