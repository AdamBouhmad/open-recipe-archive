## Ingredients

- tragacanth flour
- water
- sugar
- finely crushed clove

## Directions

1. Prepare dough from tragacanth flour moistened with water
2. Next day press, dry, and grate it
3. Add sugar and finely crushed clove
4. Make a dough from everything
5. Shape a clove from the dough
6. Let dry on warm stoves
7. When sufficiently hardened, sugar as described for almonds
