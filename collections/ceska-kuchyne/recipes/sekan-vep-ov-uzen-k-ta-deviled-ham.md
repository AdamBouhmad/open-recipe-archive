## Ingredients

- smoked ham hock with skin
- water
- flour
- paprika or pepper
- allspice
- salt

## Directions

1. In the evening, place the ham hock with skin in a container
2. Pour enough water over it so it is completely submerged and hold it down with a stone so it stays submerged
3. In the morning, rinse it well in about two changes of water
4. Mix flour with water and knead into a large dough patty
5. Spread it over the entire ham hock, pressing it well everywhere
6. Place on a baking pan and bake at moderate heat for 3-4 hours depending on the size of the ham hock
7. When done, remove the dough and skin
8. When cooled, cut the meat into small pieces
9. Put through a meat grinder and grind finely
10. Season to taste with paprika or pepper, allspice, and salt if not salty enough
11. Mix everything well with the meat
12. Put into glass jars, seal well and store in a cool place
13. It will keep as long as it is not eaten
