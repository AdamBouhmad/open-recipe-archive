## Ingredients

- Hard boiled eggs
- Cooked cooled potatoes
- Butter
- Chives
- Pepper
- Four egg yolks or two whole eggs
- Sour cream or sweet milk
- Grated bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Slice several hard boiled eggs and an equal amount of cooked cooled potatoes into thin slices
2. Grease a baking dish with butter
3. Put a layer of potatoes on the bottom
4. Sprinkle with chopped chives and pepper
5. Alternate layers of eggs and potatoes until the dish is full, ending with a layer of potatoes
6. Beat four egg yolks or two whole eggs with a glass of good sour cream or sweet milk
7. Pour this mixture over the contents of the baking dish
8. You may also sprinkle with grated bread and dot with small pieces of butter
9. Bake in the oven
