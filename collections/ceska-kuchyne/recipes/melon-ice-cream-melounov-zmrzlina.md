## Ingredients

- Ripest melons
- Lemon juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the ripest melons
2. Remove fibers and seeds
3. Peel everything
4. Cut into pieces
5. Press through a sieve
6. Add lemon juice to the obtained juice
7. Sweeten generously
8. Prepare as other ice cream
