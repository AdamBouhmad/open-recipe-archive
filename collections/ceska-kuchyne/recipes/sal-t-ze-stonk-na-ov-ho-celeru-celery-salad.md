## Ingredients

- leaf celery stalks
- salt
- pepper
- mustard powder
- oil
- vinegar
- mayonnaise or remoulade
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Remove the upper hard and stringy stalks, rinse the inner white and tender stalks well, dip them briefly in cold water, then slice them into pieces about one and a half inches long and cut these again into thin noodles
2. Rinse them once more, then dry, salt, season with pepper and a little mustard powder, pour with oil and vinegar, shake well and put on a platter lined with lettuce leaves
3. Instead of oil and vinegar, the salad can first be mixed with a little mayonnaise, arranged in a pyramid on a platter lined with lettuce leaves and poured over with remaining mayonnaise or remoulade
