## Ingredients

- venison liver
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- egg
- houska (bread roll)

## Directions

1. Remove membranes from venison liver and slice
2. Dust with flour, pepper, and salt
3. Fry on butter
4. Alternatively, pepper, dip in egg, and coat with grated houska
