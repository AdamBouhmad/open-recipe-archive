## Ingredients

- dough
- butter
- potato slices
- mutton cutlets
- good beef broth

## Directions

1. Make a border around a deep dish from dough
2. Brush the dish with butter
3. Into it place slices of potatoes alternating with mutton cutlets so that one tenth of the mixture is meat
4. Pour good beef broth over the mixture
5. Cover with dough and bake for 2 hours
6. Serve hot at the table
