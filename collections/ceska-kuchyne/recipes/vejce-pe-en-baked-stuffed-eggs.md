## Ingredients

- About 8 eggs
- 30 grams fresh butter
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Mushrooms (porcini, champignons or morels), finely chopped and fried in butter
- Crayfish tails or 3 grated potatoes (if mushrooms are unavailable)
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Hard boil about 8 eggs, plunge into cold water, cool, peel, and cut in half.
2. Carefully remove the yolks and rub them with 30 grams fresh butter, salt, nutmeg, finely chopped parsley, and finely chopped mushrooms that have been fried in butter.
3. If mushrooms are not available, crayfish tails or 3 grated potatoes may be added to the mixture.
4. Mix everything well and fill the egg whites with the mixture.
5. Place the halves on a greased baking sheet with the filling side up and bake, or join two halves together, soak in milk, coat in bread crumbs, and fry.
6. If asparagus or cauliflower is available, prepare it in butter, arrange on a plate, and surround with the eggs.
