## Ingredients

- fish scraps, heads
- onion
- turnip
- lemon peel
- bay leaves
- thyme
- vinegar
- various spices
- white wine
- water
- fish bladder
- large fish stock
- saffron
- stewed roots

## Directions

1. Cook fish scraps, heads, onion, turnip, lemon peel, bay leaves, thyme, vinegar, various spices, white wine and water together, with the solid parts submerged
2. Add dissolved fish bladder and stock from a large fish
3. Color the aspic with saffron and a solution from stewed roots
4. Strain everything and let it rest
5. For clear fish aspic, strain through heated cloth repeatedly until completely translucent and clean
