## Ingredients

- several handfuls of violet petals
- boiling water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take several handfuls of violet petals and clean them
2. Place them in a porcelain or stone pot
3. Pour boiling water over them, seal the pot tightly and let it sit on warm stove overnight
4. In the morning strain and press out the liquid
5. Boil the juice with sugar and prepare everything in the already known manner
