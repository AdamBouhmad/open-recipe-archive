## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- salt
- flour
- butter
- poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Boil about a quart of peeled potatoes, adding about half a spoonful of salt
2. When they are almost done, sprinkle an incomplete cupful of white flour on them and let them boil for about five minutes more
3. The potatoes and flour will finish cooking at the same time
4. Drain the water, salt the cooked potatoes to taste, add a piece of butter and mash everything well with a stick so that no piece of potato or lump of flour remains
5. Cut the mush with a spoon into pieces, place on a plate and sprinkle with poppy seeds and hot melted butter
