## Ingredients

- Eel
- mayonnaise sauce
- aspic

## Directions

1. Prepare the eel as directed for marinated carp
2. Arrange the cold eel in a ring shape on a dish
3. Place the mayonnaise sauce in the center and make a border of broken aspic around it
