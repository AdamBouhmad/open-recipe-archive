## Ingredients

- several celery heads
- salt
- watercress
- wine vinegar
- olive oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape several celery heads, cut off the top with the greens and put in cold water
2. Cook the heads in salted water, then remove and put in cold water
3. Scrape them clean with a knife and slice thinly
4. Stand the tops that you cut off in the center of a salad bowl and arrange the sliced celery around it
5. Make a border of well-cleaned and washed watercress, sprinkle with salt, pour with wine vinegar and good olive oil, and sprinkle with ground pepper
