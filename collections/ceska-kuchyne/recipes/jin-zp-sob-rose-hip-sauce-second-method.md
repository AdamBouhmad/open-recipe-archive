## Ingredients

- onion
- pepper
- allspice
- three cloves
- wine vinegar
- roux with butter
- white wine
- burnt sugar
- rose hip preserves

## Directions

1. Slice onion
2. Add pepper, allspice and three cloves
3. Add wine vinegar
4. Simmer all
5. Mix in roux made with butter
6. Strain
7. Add four spoonfuls of wine
8. Add a piece of burnt sugar and two spoonfuls of rose hip preserves
