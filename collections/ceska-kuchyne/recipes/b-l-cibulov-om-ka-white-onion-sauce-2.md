## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the onion into fine pieces
2. Stew it in butter until soft and white
3. Add a tablespoon of flour and mix well
4. Pour beef broth over it and let it cook for about half an hour
5. If you want the sauce sour, add a little vinegar before cooking
