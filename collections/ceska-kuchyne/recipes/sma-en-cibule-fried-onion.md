## Ingredients

- bacon
- four medium onions
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into cubes and render it
2. When the bacon is rendered, remove the cracklings and into the hot fat cut four medium onions very thinly
3. Cover, let stew for 15 minutes and stir frequently
4. Season with pepper and salt as needed
