## Ingredients

- young cleaned rabbit
- salt
- piece of sliced bacon
- two sliced onions
- lemon zest
- sprig of thyme
- bay leaf
- several peppercorns
- three tablespoons of wine vinegar
- one tablespoon of butter
- one and a half tablespoons of flour
- two small cups of thick good sour cream
- vinegar
- beef broth or hot water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned young rabbit into equal pieces, wash it, salt it and place on a pan with a piece of sliced bacon.
2. Add two sliced onions, a piece of lemon zest, a sprig of thyme, a bay leaf, several peppercorns.
3. Pour over three tablespoons of wine vinegar and let it braise until tender.
4. Remove the meat to a dish and strain the sauce.
5. On the pan put one tablespoon of butter and one and a half tablespoons of flour, and when it dissolves, add two small cups of thick good sour cream and the sauce in which the rabbit was cooked.
6. If needed, add a little more vinegar and beef broth or hot water, let it simmer a little longer, and if the sauce is too sour, add a piece of sugar.
7. Pour the sauce over the rabbit and serve.
