## Ingredients

- eggs
- smetana (sour cream)
- salt
- pepper
- chopped fresh parsley, chives, or chervil

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sour cream, salt and pepper
2. Add to them either chopped fresh parsley, chives, or chervil
3. Prepare the omelettes in the usual way
4. You can also add a small amount of the herbs to the eggs
