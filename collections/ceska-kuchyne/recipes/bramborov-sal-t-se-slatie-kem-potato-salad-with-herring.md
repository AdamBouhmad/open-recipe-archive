## Ingredients

- two milk herrings
- salt
- boiled potatoes
- sour apples
- onion
- pepper
- herring roe

## Directions

1. Rinse two milk herrings well, skin them, halve them, remove the bones and cut the herrings into pieces
2. Mix potatoes sliced into rounds with sour apples and onion cut into cubes on a dish, salt and pepper
3. Arrange herring pieces on the surface
4. Mix the herring roe with vinegar and pour this over the salad
