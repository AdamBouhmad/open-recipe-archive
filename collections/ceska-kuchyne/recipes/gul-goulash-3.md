## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram beef from ribs
- onion (chopped)
- butter
- caraway seed
- flour
- potatoes (fried)

## Directions

1. Cut meat into cubes, salt it
2. Braise with chopped onion on butter
3. Add caraway seed tied in a piece of cloth
4. After half an hour, dust with flour and mix sauce
5. Simmer briefly
6. Before serving, remove caraway and border with fried potatoes
