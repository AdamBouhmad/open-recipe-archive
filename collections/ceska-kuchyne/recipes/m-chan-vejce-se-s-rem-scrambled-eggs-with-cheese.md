## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- Swiss cheese (cut into cubes)
- butter
- grated cheese (for sprinkling)

## Directions

1. Beat several eggs in a small bowl with a pinch of salt, pepper, and a piece of Swiss cheese cut into cubes
2. Pour into a pan with melted butter
3. Stir until it thickens
4. Turn quickly onto a plate
5. Sprinkle with grated cheese
6. Optional additions: cooked asparagus tips, stewed mushrooms, lightly fried onion, or smoked salmon
