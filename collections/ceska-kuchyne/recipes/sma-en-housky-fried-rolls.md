## Ingredients

- eight rolls
- hard egg yolks
- preserved fruit
- sliced almonds
- lemon
- lemon juice
- sugar
- three and one-half deciliters red wine
- cherry juice
- cinnamon
- cloves
- lemon zest
- one large spoon cooked raisin

## Directions

1. Grate eight rolls; from other rolls cut out a piece, hollow out the center, dry, pound in mortar, and sift.
2. To this add four hard egg yolks, one heaping spoon of preserved fruit, two spoons chopped almonds, lemon and lemon juice, and sugar as needed.
3. Fill the hollowed rolls with this stuffing, cover with a lid dipped in egg white, and place on a baking sheet.
4. Meanwhile boil three and one-half deciliters red wine with a little cherry juice, a piece of cinnamon, several cloves, a piece of lemon zest, one large spoon cooked raisin, and a piece of sugar; strain over the rolls.
5. Bake the rolls for a while, then arrange on a serving dish, place a piece of preserved fruit on each roll and sprinkle with sugar.
