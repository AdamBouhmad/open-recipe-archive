## Ingredients

- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 4-5 egg yolks
- lemon or orange peel
- salt
- sugar (equal weight to yolks)
- 4 tablespoons yeast
- flour
- milk
- plum jam
- cheese curds
- poppy seed
- raisins
- almonds
- roasted semolina
- gingerbread

## Directions

1. Cream 6 dekagrams of butter with 4-5 egg yolks, lemon or orange peel, salt, sugar equal to the weight of yolks, 4 tablespoons yeast, flour and milk to form a dough that doesn't stick to the hands.
2. Allow to ferment.
3. Stretch the dough to the thickness of a pinky finger, spread with plum jam, cheese curds or poppy seed, and sprinkle with raisins, almonds, roasted semolina and sugar or gingerbread.
4. Make a rim around the dough, brush with egg, and bake.
5. When cooled, cut into squares or triangles.
