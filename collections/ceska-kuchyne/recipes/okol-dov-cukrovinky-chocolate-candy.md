## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup syrup
- 1 cup grated chocolate
- 1.5 cups butter
- 1.5 cups water
- 1/2 cup sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup syrup, 1 cup grated chocolate, 1.5 cups butter, 1.5 cups water, and 1/2 cup sweet cream in a pan
2. Cook on high heat long enough until it draws like threads
3. Pour onto a buttered oblong plate and let cool
4. Before it completely hardens, mark with a knife into squares
