## Ingredients

- several carrots
- salt
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) grated carrot
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams beaten sugar
- 2 grated bread rolls
- a little new spice
- nutmeg
- a little fennel if desired
- 6 egg whites
- tallow for greasing
- grated bread roll for coating

## Directions

1. Scrape, wash, and grate several carrots, salt lightly
2. Add 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of grated carrot, 6 egg yolks, 6 dekagrams of beaten sugar, 2 grated bread rolls, a little new spice, nutmeg, and optionally a little fennel
3. Place the mixture in a bowl that sits in hot water, renewing the water as it cools
4. Stir the mixture for half an hour
5. Grease the mold with tallow and coat with grated bread roll
6. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake in the oven
7. Sprinkle with sugar when serving; since it usually turns out poorly, wrap the mold with a cloth and serve in the mold
