## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- butter
- barley
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- apples

## Directions

1. Mix butter and barley in 1 liter of milk, several egg yolks, sugar, lemon peel rubbed with sugar and snow from the egg whites
2. Grease a mold, place sliced apples without seeds on the bottom
3. Add the barley mixture to the mold, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
4. Bake slowly for one hour
