## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- salt water
- beef broth
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut peeled potatoes, boil in salted water, grate and mash them, thin with beef broth, press through a sieve, butter them, pepper and salt
2. Stir them over the fire for a while longer
