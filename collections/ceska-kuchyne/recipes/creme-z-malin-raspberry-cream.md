## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 195 grams (13 lots) sugar
- 45 grams (3 lots) flour
- 1/2 liter raspberry juice
- lemon zest rubbed on sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 10 egg yolks with 195 grams sugar and 45 grams flour
2. Mix with 1/2 liter of raspberry juice and lemon zest rubbed on sugar
3. Mix and beat with snow until it cools
