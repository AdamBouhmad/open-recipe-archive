## Ingredients

- pork meat
- vegetables
- kohlrabi (kedlubny)
- water
- pork broth
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Parboil the pork and cook with all vegetables as you would beef.
2. Meanwhile, peel the kohlrabi (kedlubny) cleanly, cut into cubes or thin slices, pour water over them and place by the fire.
3. When it begins to boil, drain the water, pour pork broth over it, salt to taste, and add sugar if it should be sweet, otherwise pepper.
4. When softened, fry it with a light roux, let it simmer a little, add the pork meat cut into neat pieces, let it simmer a bit longer, pour onto a platter and serve.
5. If you want it dark, you can add a piece of caramelized sugar.
6. In the same way, pork with carrots, celery, beets, or cabbage is also prepared.
