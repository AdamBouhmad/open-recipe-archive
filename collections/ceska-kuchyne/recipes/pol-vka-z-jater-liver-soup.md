## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram veal liver
- flour
- butter
- two hard bread rolls
- beef broth or vegetable water
- pepper
- cauliflower
- garlic
- ginger
- noodles

## Directions

1. Cut the liver, roll in flour, and fry in butter
2. Dry two bread rolls in the oven and pound in a mortar
3. Add either to beef broth or salted vegetable water
4. Add pepper, cauliflower, 2 minced garlic cloves, and a little ground ginger
5. Cook and cut the bread into noodles
6. Serve in the soup bowl
