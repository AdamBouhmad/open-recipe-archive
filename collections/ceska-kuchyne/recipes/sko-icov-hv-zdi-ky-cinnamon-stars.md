## Ingredients

- half pound almonds
- egg white
- one pound powdered sugar
- half ounce ground cinnamon
- sugar glaze
- cinnamon for glaze

## Directions

1. Grind half pound of almonds finely with egg white
2. Knead well with one pound powdered sugar and half ounce ground cinnamon into a thick dough
3. Roll out the dough on paper to the thickness of a straw
4. Cut out stars with a metal star-shaped mold
5. Place them on a baking sheet lined with paper
6. Bake slowly
7. When removed from the oven, brush with sugar glaze mixed with a little cinnamon
8. When the stars cool, remove them from the paper
