## Ingredients

- half a pound blanched and peeled almonds
- four egg whites
- two small cups fine sugar
- zest of one lemon
- one more egg white

## Directions

1. Dry half a pound of blanched and peeled almonds in an oven, then pound them in a mortar with four egg whites.
2. Place in a bowl and add two small cups of fine sugar, grated zest of one lemon, and one more egg white.
3. Mix well and beat thoroughly.
4. Using a pastry bag or simply a teaspoon, form mounds about the size of a quarter on paper placed on a baking sheet, spacing them equally.
5. Bake at gentle heat until pale yellow.
6. The macaroons must rise during baking, the surface must crack, the inside must be white, and the bottom pale brown.
7. Instead of almonds, you can also use hazelnut kernels.
