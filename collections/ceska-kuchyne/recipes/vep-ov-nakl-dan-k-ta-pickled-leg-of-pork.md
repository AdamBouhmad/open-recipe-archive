## Ingredients

- pork leg
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- onion
- bay leaves
- thyme
- ginger
- peppercorns
- cloves
- allspice
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove skin and lard from the pork leg, wash it cleanly, salt it, and place it in a deep dish.
2. Have two cups of vinegar, the same amount of water, one chopped onion, several bay leaves, some thyme, a piece of ginger, several grains of pepper, cloves, and allspice simmer in a pot, and when cooled, pour over the leg.
3. Let it lie this way for three days, turning it twice daily; then place it on a roasting pan, strain a little of the liquid over it, and let it roast, basting it frequently with gravy.
4. When nearly done, mix a tablespoon of flour with a little sour cream in a cup, add it to the leg, let the sauce thicken a little, then remove the leg to a platter and pour the sauce over it.
