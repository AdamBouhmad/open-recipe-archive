## Ingredients

- onion, cut into cubes
- beef broth
- pepper
- salt
- roux

## Directions

1. Cut the onion into cubes
2. Pour beef broth over it
3. Add pepper and salt
4. When it has cooked somewhat, add roux
5. Strain through a sieve
