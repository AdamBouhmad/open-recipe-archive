## Ingredients

- 3.5 deciliters good children's semolina
- hot milk
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 6 egg yolks
- ground sugar
- finely chopped lemon zest
- vanilla or cinnamon
- 6 egg whites
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook 3.5 deciliters of good children's semolina in hot milk to make a thick porridge and let it cool
2. On a separate platter, rub 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter, 6 egg yolks, a piece of ground sugar, a small piece of finely chopped lemon zest, a piece of ground vanilla or cinnamon, and make snow from 6 egg whites
3. Add the porridge, mix everything well, grease the form with butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and bake the mixture in the oven
