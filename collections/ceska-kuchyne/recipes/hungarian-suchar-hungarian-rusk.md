## Ingredients

- 500 grams flour
- 32 grams dissolved yeast
- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams sugar
- 4 eggs
- salt
- warm milk

## Directions

1. For 500 grams flour, take 32 grams dissolved yeast, 125 grams butter, 125 grams sugar, 4 eggs, salt, and mix with warm milk into dough
2. Let the dough rise, then roll it out into thin narrow slices
3. Let the slices rise again on a baking sheet, then bake slowly
4. On the second day, cut them and brush with egg, then sprinkle with sugar and chopped almonds
