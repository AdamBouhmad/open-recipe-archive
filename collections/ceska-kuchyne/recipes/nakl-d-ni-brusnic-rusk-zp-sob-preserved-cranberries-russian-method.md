## Ingredients

- ripe cranberries
- apples or pears
- hard water

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse and dry ripe cranberries and layer them alternately with wrapped apples or pears in stone containers
2. Pour hard water over them, press down with a heavy object and place in a cool location
3. Cranberries prepared this way will last the whole winter; when needed, they are prepared with sugar
