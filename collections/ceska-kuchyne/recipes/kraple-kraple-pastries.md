## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- one pound hard butter
- four egg yolks
- one whole egg
- salt
- one coffeepot sweet milk
- jam
- sugar

## Directions

1. Divide one pound of flour into two equal halves
2. Mix one pound of butter into one half of the flour
3. Beat the egg yolks and whole egg separately on a plate, then add to the other half of the flour along with salt and one coffeepot of sweet milk
4. Mix together and add just enough flour to make the dough rollable
5. Roll out the yellow (egg) dough slightly and place the white (butter) dough on top, rolling both to the same thickness
6. Fold together into a rectangle
7. Spread a clean cloth on the work surface, dust with flour, and roll out the entire dough as thin as possible
8. Fold like a handkerchief and place on a plate
9. Let rest overnight
10. Early the next morning, roll out again to about noodle thickness
11. Cut into strips about a hand's width long and across
12. Cut the rectangles into squares and fold each into a cross pattern with ends meeting at the center, creating a four-pointed shape
13. Place on a buttered baking sheet and bake in an oven
14. When baked, spread jam on top, place on a platter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
