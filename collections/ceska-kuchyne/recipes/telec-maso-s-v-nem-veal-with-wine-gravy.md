## Ingredients

- veal breast, cut into pieces
- beef broth
- salt
- one glass white wine
- several cloves
- ground pepper
- two or three lemon cakes
- butter
- flour
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut veal breast into pieces, wash them clean and let simmer a little in beef broth, salt it
2. When the meat foams, add one glass of white wine
3. Add several cloves and a pinch of ground pepper
4. Add two or three lemon cakes from which you have removed the rind
5. Let simmer for a good half hour
6. Strain the liquid into a pan and make a pale roux, add the cooked veal to this gravy
7. Let simmer a little longer, then whisk in two egg yolks
8. Pour into a deep platter and serve
