## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- bacon
- onion
- white pepper
- diced bacon
- beef broth or water
- 3½ deciliters (žejdlík) sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the liver, remove all membranes, lard with bacon
2. Season with pepper, add sliced onion, white pepper and a piece of chopped bacon
3. Pour over with beef broth or water and braise until tender
4. Strain the sauce, add 3½ deciliters of sour cream, place the liver in it and simmer for a while
