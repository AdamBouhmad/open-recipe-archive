## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 0.25 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 1.5 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
- 0.5 teaspoon vanilla
- chocolate for frosting
- egg whites
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup sugar and 0.25 cup butter well
2. Add 2 eggs and mix well
3. Add 0.5 cup milk and 3 tablespoons grated chocolate and mix again
4. Take 1.5 cups flour, add 1 teaspoon cream of tartar, mix well and add to chocolate batter
5. Add 0.5 teaspoon dissolved baking soda and 0.5 teaspoon vanilla and mix all together
6. Alternatively use 3 teaspoons baking powder instead of cream of tartar and soda
7. Bake in thin layers on metal plates
8. For frosting: melt chocolate and mix into egg whites
9. Add sugar and continue mixing until thick enough
10. Spread frosting between layers and on top, making three layers
