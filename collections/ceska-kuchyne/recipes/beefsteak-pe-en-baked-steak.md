## Ingredients

- 1 steak slice, pounded or chopped well
- Butter (if the meat is not sufficiently fat or marbled)
- Salt and pepper
- Grated white bread
- A little water

## Directions

1. Place the well-pounded or chopped steak slice on a griddle.
2. If not fatty enough or well marbled, place a piece of butter on it.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Sprinkle with grated white bread.
5. Add a little water so it does not stick to the griddle.
6. Bake about twenty minutes, or longer if you prefer it well done.
