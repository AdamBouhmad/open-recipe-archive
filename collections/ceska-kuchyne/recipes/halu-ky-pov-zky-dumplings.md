## Ingredients

- hard rolls
- milk
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- grated roll
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls into 4 or 5 pieces, place one next to the other and pour milk over them
2. When the milk is absorbed, take the rolls before they become soaked, salt on both sides, coat in flour, dip in egg and roll in grated roll
3. Bake on butter until golden
4. Serves as a fast food dish with carrots, lentils, etc.
