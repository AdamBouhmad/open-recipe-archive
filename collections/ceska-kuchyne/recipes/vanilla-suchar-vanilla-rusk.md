## Ingredients

- 500 grams sugar
- egg yolks from 18 eggs
- egg white from 6 eggs
- a little vanilla
- 750 grams flour

## Directions

1. Mix 500 grams sugar with the egg yolk from 18 eggs, egg white from 6 eggs, a little vanilla, and 750 grams flour
2. Spread the dough thinly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
3. Brush with a beaten egg yolk and bake quickly until golden
4. Once cooled, cut into pieces and sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
