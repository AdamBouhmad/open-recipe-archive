## Ingredients

- Peeled apples, cut into small pieces
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook peeled apples cut into small pieces in water until soft
2. Press through a sieve
3. Mix with sugar
4. Boil strongly several more times
5. Store in containers
