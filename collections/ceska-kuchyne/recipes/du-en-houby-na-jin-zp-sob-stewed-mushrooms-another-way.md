## Ingredients

- well-cleaned mushrooms
- butter
- fresh parsley, chopped
- caraway seeds
- salt
- white wine (optional)
- butter roux
- vinegar (optional)
- sour cream (optional)
- breadcrumbs
- cheese

## Directions

1. Slice well-cleaned mushrooms, wash them in fresh water, put them on a shallow pan on a piece of butter, add a little chopped fresh parsley, a little caraway seeds and salt and let them stew for about three-quarters of an hour with frequent stirring.
2. You can also add a little white wine and then thicken with a thin pale butter roux.
3. Or add a little wine vinegar to the stewed mushrooms, add sour cream and let it cook a little longer.
4. Stewed mushrooms thickened with cream can also be put on a platter in which you want to serve it, sprinkle them with grated breadcrumbs and cheese, top with small pieces of butter and let them brown in the oven.
