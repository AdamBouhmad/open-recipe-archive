## Ingredients

- older partridges
- lard
- bacon
- onion
- salt
- vegetable roots
- 2 heads cabbage
- smoked sausages

## Directions

1. Clean the older partridges; lard the breasts and thighs with lard and ham.
2. Braise with lard, onion, salt, and vegetable roots.
3. Cut two heads of cabbage into quarters, wash, cool in water, and wring out.
4. Braise the cabbage with smoked meat and lard in broth.
5. When the partridges are half-braised, add them to the cabbage and pour with the juices.
6. When done, arrange the partridges surrounded by cabbage, pour with the juices.
7. Fried sausages may be placed on top of the cabbage.
