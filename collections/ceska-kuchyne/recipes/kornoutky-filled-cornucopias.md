## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two tablespoons sugar
- two heaped tablespoons flour
- one tablespoon sweet milk
- butter for greasing
- three egg whites
- sugar for meringue

## Directions

1. Take two eggs, two tablespoons sugar, two heaped tablespoons flour and one tablespoon sweet milk
2. Whip until bubbles form
3. Grease a baking sheet well with butter, put on stove to heat
4. Take a teaspoon of dough and drip onto sheet so they do not touch each other
5. Bake only five minutes, just until freshly baked and not dried
6. Remove, shape like cornucopias
7. Whip three egg whites until stiff snow, add sugar and cook over heat (this is better) and fill cornucopias
