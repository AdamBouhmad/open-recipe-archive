## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup water
- 2 eggs
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Take 0.5 cup butter, 2 cups sugar, 1 teaspoon lemon extract, 3 whole eggs, 1 cup milk, 3 cups flour and 3 teaspoons baking powder and make batter
2. Bake into 4 layers
3. Put 1 cup sugar, 0.5 cup water, 2 beaten eggs, grated zest of 1 lemon, juice of 2 lemons, 1 teaspoon butter and 2 tablespoons flour in a pot
4. Set pot in a pan of boiling water and stir over heat until thick
5. When cooled, spread between layers and cover cake with lemon glaze
6. Orange cake is prepared the same way
