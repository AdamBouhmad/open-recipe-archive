## Ingredients

- 1 liter boiling milk
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) chocolate
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- toasted bread (usmaženou housku)

## Directions

1. In 1 liter of boiling milk, dissolve 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of chocolate
2. Add 2 egg yolks
3. Salt to taste
4. Pour over toasted bread
