## Ingredients

- butter
- flour (one large spoon)
- vinegar (several spoons)
- milk (good quality)
- salt (pinch of flowers)
- eggs

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of fresh butter in a pan
2. Let one large spoon of flour brown on it
3. Add several spoons of vinegar
4. Mix and gradually pour in good milk while constantly stirring until a thickened sauce forms
5. When the sauce is cooking, add a pinch of salt flowers
6. Quickly drop eggs one by one into the sauce
7. Let them set in the sauce and serve immediately
