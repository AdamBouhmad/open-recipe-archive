## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram flour
- ½ kilogram sugar
- butter dough
- egg for brushing
- chopped almonds
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Make butter dough from the flour and sugar and roll it out
2. Cut circles with a glass
3. Cut the center with a smaller glass or mold to form rings
4. Place the rings on a moist baking sheet
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with chopped almonds and sugar
7. Bake until golden
