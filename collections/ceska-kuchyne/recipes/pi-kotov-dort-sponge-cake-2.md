## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- half pound sifted sugar
- eight egg whites
- two small cups flour
- butter
- grated and sifted roll

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks in a deep bowl
2. Add half pound sifted sugar and beat for half an hour
3. Add stiff snow made from eight egg whites and mix for another half hour
4. Then add two small cups flour and mix a little longer
5. Butter a form and dust it with grated and sifted roll
6. Pour the batter into the form so it remains two fingers from the top
7. Bake very slowly in the oven
8. Test doneness by piercing with a wire—if it comes out dry, the cake is done
9. Turn out the cooled cake carefully
10. Cover with glaze and decorate with preserved fruit
