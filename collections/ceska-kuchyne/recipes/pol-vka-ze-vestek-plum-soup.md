## Ingredients

- Fresh plums
- 2 fried bread slices (rozstrouhané housky)
- Half a liter milk
- A little flower (kvetu)
- 1 crushed clove
- Salt
- Bread
- Sugar (if plums are sour)

## Directions

1. Cook fresh plums with pits until they fall apart
2. Strain through a sieve and remove the pits
3. Add 2 grated bread slices, half a liter of milk (if the plums are sour, a piece of sugar), a little flower, 1 crushed clove, and salt
4. Cut bread into cubes and pour the soup over it
