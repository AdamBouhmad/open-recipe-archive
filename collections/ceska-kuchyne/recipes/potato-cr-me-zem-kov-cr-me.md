## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon potato flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 75 grams (5 lots) sugar
- 1 cup cold milk
- 500 milliliters boiling milk
- 15 grams (1 lot) sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- lemon peel, grated

## Directions

1. Rub potato flour with egg yolks
2. Add sugar and cold milk
3. Mix in boiling milk
4. Add blanched and pounded sweet and bitter almonds and grated lemon peel
5. Stir constantly over heat
6. Pour onto a platter
7. Cool on ice or in a cellar
