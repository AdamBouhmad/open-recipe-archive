## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram cooked peeled potatoes
- fine semolina
- salt
- goose or pork lard

## Directions

1. Grate cooked potatoes
2. Salt
3. Mix with fine semolina to make thick lumps
4. Spread on baking sheet greased with lard
5. Sprinkle with same lard on top
6. Mix and bake
