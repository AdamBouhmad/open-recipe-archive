## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar, one and a half small cups per half gallon of peaches
- clove
- lemon zest
- nutmeg flower

## Directions

1. Pick peaches, peel the skin finely, and remove the pits, then place in a glazed pot that can be filled with them.
2. Cover so steam cannot escape and place in a hot oven, letting them stew until soft.
3. Mash well with a wooden spoon.
4. For every half gallon of peaches, add one and a half small cups of sugar.
5. Mix well, cover with a lid, and return to the oven, stewing and stirring occasionally, keeping them there until they turn a nice brown color.
6. Place in preserving bottles or stone jars, cover well, and set in a cool place.
7. Peaches stewed this way do not scorch easily and have a pleasant taste.
8. When you want to use them, add a little clove, lemon zest, and nutmeg flower and mix well.
