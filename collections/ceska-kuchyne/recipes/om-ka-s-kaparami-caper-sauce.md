## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- brown broth
- lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon capers

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a pan and fry a little flour until it turns yellowish
2. Pour brown broth over it and cook well
3. Add a little lemon juice and a tablespoon of capers
4. Remove from heat and do not let it boil anymore
