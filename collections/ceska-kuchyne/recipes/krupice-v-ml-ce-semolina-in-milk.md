## Ingredients

- 2 liters milk
- salt
- piece of fresh butter
- sugar
- semolina
- cinnamon or honey or syrup

## Directions

1. Salt 2 liters milk slightly and add a piece of fresh butter and sugar
2. When boiling, stir it and with the other hand sprinkle semolina, not too much
3. Stir constantly until it thickens appropriately
4. On the plate sprinkle either cinnamon and sugar, or make indentations with a spoon and pour butter, or omit butter and coat with honey or syrup
