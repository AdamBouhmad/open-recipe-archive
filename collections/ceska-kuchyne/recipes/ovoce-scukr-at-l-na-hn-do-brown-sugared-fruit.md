## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- sugar
- lemon juice
- preserved nuts, cherries, sour cherries, apples, pears or other fruit

## Directions

1. Roast chestnuts, peel them, and pierce them on thin pointed sticks on both sides
2. Chestnuts can be pierced on these sticks in any quantity desired
3. Besides chestnuts, preserved nuts from sugar can be cleaned, cut lengthwise and placed on sticks either in pieces or whole
4. The same applies to preserved sour cherries, cherries, apples, pears and other fruit
5. Boil the finest sugar until it thickens completely, then add a few drops of lemon juice
6. Dip the pierced fruit into this prepared liquid, immediately withdraw it and turn it around in the juice
7. Repeat this as long as the sugar thickens completely and hardens and dries on the fruit
8. When this is done, place or better lay the sticks with fruit in a place where they can dry comfortably without touching each other
9. Such sugared fruit can be used for decoration and assembly of various columns, rocks, etc.
