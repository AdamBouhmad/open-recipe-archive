## Ingredients

- butter
- sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon extract
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- milk
- flour
- baking powder
- figs, halved

## Directions

1. Bake 4 cakes as for silver layer cake, but omit the extract.
2. For yellow dough: use 0.5 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon cinnamon extract, 7 yolks, 1 whole egg, 0.5 cup milk, 0.5 cup flour, and 0.5 teaspoon baking powder.
3. Pour part of this dough into 2 plates, cover with halved figs, dust lightly with flour, cover with remaining dough, and bake.
4. Make 2 cakes by placing 1 yellow cake between 2 white cakes, with cinnamon frosting between them.
5. Cover the cakes with cinnamon frosting.
6. Instead of figs, you may use other fresh or preserved fruits, such as strawberries, raspberries, pineapples, peeled and quartered oranges, and so forth.
