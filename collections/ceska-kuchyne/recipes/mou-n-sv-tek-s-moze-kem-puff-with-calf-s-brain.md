## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons fresh butter
- 3 full tablespoons flour
- 2 cups boiling milk
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 egg whites
- butter for greasing the pan
- 1/2 calf's brain
- salt water
- small piece of butter
- parsley
- onion, finely chopped
- ground pepper
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Melt 3 tablespoons fresh butter, add 3 full tablespoons flour, let it foam
2. Gradually pour in 2 cups boiling milk while constantly stirring
3. Let it cook until it becomes a fine paste, then let it cool
4. Add 6 egg yolks and mix well, season with salt
5. Gently fold in snow made from 3 egg whites
6. Grease a shallow pan with butter and bake 2/3 of the prepared paste until golden
7. Meanwhile, cook 1/2 calf's brain in salted water, remove veins, cut into small slices
8. Place the slices in a pan, add a small piece of butter, chopped parsley, finely chopped onion, season with ground pepper and seasonings, let it stew briefly
9. Grease a pan with butter, cut the baked puff into 3 parts, place one layer in the pan, cover with a portion of brain mixture, place the second part of puff, cover again with brain, place the third part of puff, cover again with brain, top with the fourth part of raw paste
10. Place the pan in a larger pan with boiling water and let it steam for three-quarters of an hour
11. Turn out onto a platter and serve quickly
