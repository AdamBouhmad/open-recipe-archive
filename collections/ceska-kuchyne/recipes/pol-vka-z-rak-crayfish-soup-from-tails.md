## Ingredients

- crayfish tails
- butter
- 2 hard egg yolks
- dried roll
- 14 deciliters soup
- cauliflower
- 20 crayfish

## Directions

1. Cut crayfish tails lengthwise into noodles, braise shells in butter for a while
2. Pound them in a mortar with 2 hard egg yolks and dried roll
3. Pour 14 deciliters of soup on them, boil, strain, add cut tails and a little cauliflower
4. Use about 20 crayfish per 14 deciliters of soup
