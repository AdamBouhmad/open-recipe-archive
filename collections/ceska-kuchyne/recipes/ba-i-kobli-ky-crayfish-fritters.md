## Ingredients

- a piece of crayfish butter
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) of crayfish tails
- 6 eggs
- 2 soaked rolls
- a little flour
- salt
- butter dough

## Directions

1. Rub together a piece of crayfish butter with 12 decagrams (8 lots) of chopped and beaten crayfish tails.
2. Mix well with 6 eggs.
3. Add 2 soaked rolls, a little flour and salt.
4. Grease the butter dough with egg, place spoonfuls of the mixture on the dough and wrap each one individually.
5. Fry and sprinkle with sugar or with fried green parsley.
6. Typically served at larger lunches after beef.
