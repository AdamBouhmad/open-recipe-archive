## Ingredients

- several floury potatoes
- 0.5 liter milk
- salt
- sugar
- vanilla
- 3 egg yolks
- egg for coating
- bread crumbs
- butter for frying
- sauce

## Directions

1. Cook several floury potatoes
2. Press through in 0.5 liter of milk with salt, sugar, vanilla, and 3 egg yolks
3. When the porridge cools, form small croquettes with a spoon
4. Coat with dough
5. Brush with egg
6. Coat with bread crumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Usually served with sauces
