## Ingredients

- beef
- weak vinegar
- bacon
- juniper
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Pound the meat
2. Marinate in weak vinegar
3. Lard with bacon
4. Roast with juniper on butter
5. Brush with sour cream and baste with sour cream
