## Ingredients

- Water
- All kinds of vegetables
- Roux (bledá jíška)
- Fresh mushrooms (hříbky)
- A piece of butter
- Caraway
- 4 eggs
- A little green parsley
- Bread

## Directions

1. Add all kinds of vegetables to water and let it cook for a while
2. Add roux and strain the soup
3. Slice fresh mushrooms thinly, add a piece of butter
4. Braise under a lid with caraway
5. Add 4 eggs and a little green parsley
6. Put everything into the strained soup
7. If too thin, bread can be added
