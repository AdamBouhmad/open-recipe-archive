## Ingredients

- 15 decagrams (10 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 20 cooked grated potatoes
- snow from 8 egg whites
- finely chopped meat or any roast sliced thin

## Directions

1. Rub butter until foamy
2. Mix in egg yolks and cooked grated potatoes
3. Add snow from egg whites
4. Mix half the batter with finely chopped meat or thinly sliced roast
5. Place in a greased mold
6. Cover with remaining batter
7. Bake
