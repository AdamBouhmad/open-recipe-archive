## Ingredients

- Cooked and cooled codfish
- parsley
- red pepper
- lemon
- cream or milk
- butter
- flour
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- lard
- green parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the cooked and cooled codfish and chop the meat finely
2. Add a spoonful of finely chopped parsley, a little red pepper and the juice of one lemon and mix well
3. Heat a cup of cream or milk
4. Mix a spoonful of butter with two spoonfuls of flour and add to the boiling cream, let it boil for three minutes
5. Mix this sauce into the codfish and mix well, spread on a dish to cool
6. Once cooled, form into patties, dip in beaten egg, coat in grated white bread or crushed crackers and fry in hot lard
7. Garnish with green parsley
