## Ingredients

- ten juniper berries
- a piece of sugar
- lemon peel, finely chopped
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- half an onion, chopped
- two tablespoons good olive oil or fresh heated butter
- ten tablespoons wine vinegar
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar ten juniper berries with a piece of sugar and finely chopped lemon peel
2. Add three hard-boiled egg yolks and half a chopped onion and pound together thoroughly
3. Add two tablespoons of good olive oil or fresh heated butter, ten tablespoons of wine vinegar and the juice of two lemons
4. Mix well and then strain it
5. If the sauce is too thick, thin it further with vinegar as needed
