## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound calf's liver
- 3 or 4 tablespoons beef marrow
- 3 egg yolks
- 10 tablespoons breadcrumbs or white bread soaked in good milk
- grated lemon rind
- ground pepper
- salt
- 3 egg whites
- butter for greasing
- grated roll for coating

## Directions

1. Rinse 1/2 pound of fine calf's liver, grate it on a grater and press it through a sieve
2. Heat 3 or 4 tablespoons of beef marrow, strain it and heat until it foams
3. Mix in 3 egg yolks one by one
4. Add 10 tablespoons of breadcrumbs or white bread soaked in good milk and well squeezed out
5. Add the grated liver and beat well
6. Mix in finely chopped lemon rind, ground pepper, and salt
7. Gently fold in stiff snow made from 3 egg whites
8. Grease a form with butter, coat with grated roll, fill with the mixture and bake in a not too hot oven for one hour
