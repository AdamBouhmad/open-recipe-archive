## Ingredients

- Salami, cervelat, or mortadella
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel salami, cervelat, or mortadella
2. Slice thinly
3. Arrange in the middle of a dish
4. Garnish around with a wreath of chopped parsley
5. Can also be drizzled with vinegar and sprinkled with finely chopped onion
