## Ingredients

- goose giblets (wings, stomach, liver, heart, neck)
- fresh water, 2 quarts
- salt
- celery, 1/2
- cabbage, small piece
- carrot
- green parsley
- peas, handful
- pepper
- egg noodles, from 1 egg

## Directions

1. Clean the goose giblets and divide them into neat pieces
2. Put in a pot, pour 2 quarts of fresh water over, salt and let simmer
3. Skim foam and when it stops foaming, add vegetables: half celery, piece of cabbage and carrot, some green parsley and a handful of peas
4. When giblets are tender, remove them and slice the stomach into pieces
5. Strain the broth, season with pepper, cook noodles made from one egg in it and pour over the giblets in a bowl. The liver may also be fried instead of boiled, then sliced and added to the other giblets before pouring the broth over them, making them tastier.
