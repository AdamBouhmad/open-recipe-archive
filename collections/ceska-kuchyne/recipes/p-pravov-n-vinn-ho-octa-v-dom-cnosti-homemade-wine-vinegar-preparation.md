## Ingredients

- ½ liter of good wine vinegar
- piece of fermented leaven, salted and peppered
- ½ liter of weak wine (added every other day)
- alternatively: fallen fruit, water, 1 kilogram of sugar
- alternatively: evaporated vinegar and ½ liter of good vinegar or 1 liter of alcohol

## Directions

1. Take a stone vessel and pour half a liter of good wine vinegar into it
2. Wrap a piece of salted and peppered fermented leaven in clean cloth and suspend it in the vessel
3. Every other day, add ½ liter of weak wine until the vessel is full
4. Remove the leaven and after 8-10 days the vinegar is ready
5. After consumption, continue adding weak wine to maintain the vinegar
6. Alternatively, to prepare vinegar from fallen fruit: crush the fruit, place it in a barrel, pour almost as much water as there is fruit pulp, add 1 kilogram of sugar, allow it to ferment, strain after 6 days, pour into a barrel of evaporated vinegar, add ½ liter of good vinegar or 1 liter of alcohol if stronger vinegar is desired, and after 6-8 weeks it becomes very palatable but cannot be kept long
