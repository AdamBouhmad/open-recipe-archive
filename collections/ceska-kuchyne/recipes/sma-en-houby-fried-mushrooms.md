## Ingredients

- larger mushroom caps
- salt
- pepper
- egg, beaten
- breadcrumbs, grated
- melted butter
- fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
- caraway seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the caps of larger mushrooms, slice them into thicker pieces, salt, pepper them, dip them in beaten egg, coat them in grated breadcrumbs and fry them to a golden color in melted butter.
2. You can also mix a little chopped fresh parsley or a little picked and crushed caraway seeds into the eggs.
