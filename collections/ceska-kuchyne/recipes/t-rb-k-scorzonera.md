## Ingredients

- Fresh butter
- Scorzonera
- Salt
- 2 cups good sour cream
- 1/2 teaspoon ground sugar
- Ground flowers

## Directions

1. Slice the scorzonera, squeeze water from them, then cook in salted water so that you can easily mash them like spinach between your fingers.
2. Drain the water from the scorzonera, let it cool and chop it very finely.
3. Melt a piece of fresh butter on the stove, add the chopped scorzonera and let everything stew until it thickens.
4. Add two cups of good sour cream, salt, add 1/2 teaspoon ground sugar and a little ground flowers.
5. Let it stew for about another quarter hour, then mix in another spoonful of fresh butter and remove to a dish.
6. You can garnish it with various fried foods.
