## Ingredients

- mutton breast
- vegetables
- salt
- handful of marjoram
- pepper
- 4 cloves garlic
- mutton fat
- brown gravy
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash mutton breast and boil with all vegetables and salt
2. When cooked, cut into neat pieces and place on platter
3. Rub a handful of marjoram and add to the broth with pepper and 4 cloves garlic
4. Boil and add enough mutton fat to brown gravy to thicken it
5. Boil, strain through a sieve, add mutton meat
6. Leave briefly on a baking pan and pour over boiled potatoes
