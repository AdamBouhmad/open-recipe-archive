## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- salt
- veal meat (1 pound)
- loin fat (1 pound)
- nutmeg
- small onions
- butter
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare cutlets from lamb ribs and trim them
2. Salt on both sides and cook in a small pan in fresh butter for two minutes
3. Remove and let cool
4. Clean veal meat and remove all glandular meat, cut into pieces
5. Mince with loin fat
6. Place in a bowl, salt it, add a little ground nutmeg and a spoonful of small onions chopped and fried in butter
7. Mix into a paste and add three eggs
8. Mix well and spread this mixture on both sides of the cooled cutlets about the thickness of a straw
9. Dip in beaten egg
10. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and set aside
11. Shortly before serving, place in hot clarified butter and fry for eight minutes until golden
12. Surround with young braised peas
