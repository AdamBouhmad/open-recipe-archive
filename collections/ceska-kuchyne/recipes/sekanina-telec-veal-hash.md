## Ingredients

- leftover cooked veal, about 2 cups chopped finely
- piece of fresh butter
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 1 teaspoon chopped onion
- fresh parsley, chopped finely
- good beef broth
- juice from roasted meat
- lemon rind, chopped finely
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the leftover cooked veal very finely into about two cups
2. Melt a piece of fresh butter on the stove
3. Add one teaspoon of flour and one teaspoon of chopped onion and brown it until the onion turns slightly yellow
4. Add a little finely chopped fresh parsley
5. Dissolve with good beef broth
6. Add a little juice from the roast and let simmer for about ten minutes
7. Add the chopped meat, season lightly with salt
8. Sprinkle with very finely chopped lemon rind
9. Cover the pot and let it stand on the fire for about ten minutes, but do not boil
10. Pour lemon juice over it and serve
