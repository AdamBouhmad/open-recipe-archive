## Ingredients

- clam liquid
- clams
- butter
- onion, finely chopped, one
- salt
- pepper, ground
- wine, one glass
- brandy, one tablespoon
- egg yolks
- cream
- butter, fresh, a piece
- bread crumb slices in shape of small hearts

## Directions

1. Boil the clam liquid and let clams thicken slightly in it
2. Remove clams and let drain
3. Fry the finely chopped onion in butter until white
4. Add the clams to the onion
5. Add a pinch of salt and ground pepper
6. Add one glass of wine and one tablespoon of brandy
7. Let everything heat well
8. Thicken with egg yolks mixed with cream and a piece of fresh butter
9. Keep stirring but do not boil
10. Pour onto dish
11. Garnish with bread crumb slices shaped like small hearts
