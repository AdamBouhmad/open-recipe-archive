## Ingredients

- water (one pint)
- vinegar (two spoons)
- water (one spoon)
- eggs

## Directions

1. Pour one pint of boiling water into a deep pan
2. Add two spoons of vinegar and one spoon of water
3. Heat on the stove
4. Drop several eggs into the water carefully so they remain whole
5. Let them cook for five minutes
6. Remove with a slotted spoon onto a plate
7. Pour melted butter over them
8. Or remove and place on toast
