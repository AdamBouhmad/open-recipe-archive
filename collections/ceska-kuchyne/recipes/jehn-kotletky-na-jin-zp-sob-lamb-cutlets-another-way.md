## Ingredients

- lamb meat from ribs
- butter
- salt
- parsley butter or garlic

## Directions

1. Prepare cutlets from lamb meat from ribs in the manner of mutton cutlets
2. If they are very small, cut them diagonally
3. Dip in melted butter
4. Cook quickly on both sides in a pan or on a grill
5. Then sprinkle with salt
6. Arrange on a heated platter and garnish with green parsley
7. You can also intersperse with pieces of parsley butter or prepare a gravy from the cooked juices, to which a little grated garlic may be added
