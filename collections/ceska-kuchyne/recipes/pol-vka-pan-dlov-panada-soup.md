## Ingredients

- water
- bread rolls, finely broken
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- butter
- cream
- salt

## Directions

1. To two quarts of boiling water add half a pound of finely broken bread rolls and let it cook for about three quarters of an hour with frequent stirring
2. Beat in a cup four egg yolks or two whole eggs with two tablespoons of butter and a spoonful of cream and pour into the cooked soup, which you must stir diligently
3. Season with salt as desired, pour into a dish and serve
4. Instead of water you can also use milk, which will also make a very tasty soup
5. For 8 people
