## Ingredients

- four bread rolls, crusts removed
- milk
- half a cup of sugar
- quarter pound of ground blanched almonds
- a piece of lemon rind, finely chopped
- eight egg yolks
- a little salt
- eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from four bread rolls
2. Soak in milk and mix to form a thick paste
3. Add half a cup of sugar
4. Add quarter pound of ground blanched almonds
5. Add finely chopped lemon rind
6. Add eight egg yolks
7. Add a little salt
8. Mix well
9. Gradually fold in the stiff snow made from eight egg whites
10. Pour into a buttered and sprinkled mold
11. Bake in the oven for half an hour
12. If you use potato porridge instead of bread rolls, you will have potato pudding
