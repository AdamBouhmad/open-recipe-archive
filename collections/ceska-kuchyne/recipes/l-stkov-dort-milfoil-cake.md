## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- beaten egg
- preserved fruit preserves
- white glaze
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Roll out puff pastry to the thickness of a finger
2. Cut five or six round layers using an inverted plate, each one slightly smaller than the last
3. Place them on papers
4. Brush with beaten egg and bake
5. When cooled, spread all layers except the smallest with preserves
6. Stack them on top of each other, starting with the largest on the bottom
7. Place the smallest layer on top
8. Cover the cake with white glaze
9. Sprinkle with chopped almonds
