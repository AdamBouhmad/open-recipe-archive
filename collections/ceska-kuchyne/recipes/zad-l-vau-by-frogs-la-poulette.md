## Ingredients

- frog hind legs
- fresh water
- salt
- butter
- lemon juice
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks
- parsley

## Directions

1. Only the hind legs of frogs marked with a green stripe on the back are suitable for consumption
2. Frog legs are cut off, stripped and soaked in fresh water for twenty-four hours
3. Cleaned legs are blanched several times in hot salted water, then placed in a saucepan with a piece of fresh butter, season with lemon juice and let simmer for a while
4. Then thicken them with a pale roux, let them stew until tender and before serving bind with two or three egg yolks thinned with a little broth, add a tablespoon of chopped green parsley, pour into a platter and serve
