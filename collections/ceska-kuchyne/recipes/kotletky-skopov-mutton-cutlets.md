## Ingredients

- mutton ribs
- pepper
- salt
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Cut cutlets from nice ribs.
2. Beat them well, pepper and salt them.
3. Place in fresh butter on a pan or baking sheet.
4. Fry one to two minutes on one side, then on the other.
5. Drain the butter, pour strong beef broth over the meat.
6. Let it simmer, arrange nicely on a plate and pour over with baked sauce.
7. Mustard can be conveniently used with the cutlets.
