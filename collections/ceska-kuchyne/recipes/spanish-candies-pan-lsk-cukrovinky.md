## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram almonds
- 8-10 egg whites
- 8-10 tablespoons grated sugar
- coarsely ground sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Peel almonds and cut into thin noodles
2. Leave in warm place to dry
3. Make stiff snow from 8-10 egg whites
4. Add 8-10 tablespoons of grated sugar mixed with almonds
5. Wax the baking sheet
6. Place small spoonfuls on sheet
7. Sprinkle with coarsely ground sugar
8. Dry in oven
