## Ingredients

- 2 quarts cherries
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- zest of lemon
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. From 2 quarts of cherries remove the pits
2. Place cherries in a small pan
3. Add four tablespoons sugar
4. A piece of lemon zest chopped fine
5. Let it cook until the cherries are sufficiently thick
6. Place them on the pancakes prepared as before
7. Roll them up
8. Place in a small pan greased with butter
9. Sprinkle with sugar
10. Pour a little milk
11. Bake in the oven until golden
12. Then remove with a metal spatula and arrange on a platter
13. Sprinkle generously with sugar and serve warm
