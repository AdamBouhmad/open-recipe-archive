## Ingredients

- whey
- caraway
- fresh butter
- egg yolks
- cold water
- salt
- bread, thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Take two quarts of whey, add a little caraway and let it boil
2. Meanwhile mix in a cup a piece of fresh butter with two egg yolks and a bit of cold water
3. When the caraway has boiled well in the whey, pour a little boiling whey onto the mixed egg yolks, stir well so it does not curdle, then mix it with the remaining whey
4. Season with salt as desired and pour into a dish with bread cut into thin slices
5. In city households whey can be prepared as follows: put about two quarts of raw milk on the stove, add a few drops of lemon juice to it so it curdles, then strain through a sieve
6. Set the curd aside and cook the soup from the whey as described above
7. This soup is very tasty and is especially recommended for the sick with chest ailments
