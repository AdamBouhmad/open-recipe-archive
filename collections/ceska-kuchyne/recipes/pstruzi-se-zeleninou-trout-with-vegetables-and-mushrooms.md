## Ingredients

- large trout
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- coulis
- lemon juice
- anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Eviscerate larger trout, rinse, cut into pieces, skin, and remove bones
2. Salt and braise in butter with finely chopped onion, parsley, and mushrooms on both sides, being careful not to brown the delicate vegetables
3. Drain the butter, season, add a little coulis
4. Finish with lemon juice and anchovy butter
5. Cook gently and arrange neatly on a platter
