## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- 18 dekagrams cooked grated potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- 3 egg whites
- 3 dekagrams butter

## Directions

1. Beat 9 dekagrams butter until foamy
2. Mix in 3 egg yolks, 6 tablespoons sour cream, 18 dekagrams cooked grated potatoes, salt and nutmeg
3. Fold in the beaten egg whites from 3 eggs
4. Bake the dough in a shallow pan with 3 dekagrams butter
5. Cut into cubes and place in broth
