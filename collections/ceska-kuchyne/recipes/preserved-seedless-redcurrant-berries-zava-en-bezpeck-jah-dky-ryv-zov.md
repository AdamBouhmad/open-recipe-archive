## Ingredients

- the most beautiful and largest redcurrant berries
- sugar, thickly cooked

## Directions

1. Pick the most beautiful and largest berries from the bunches.
2. Carefully remove the small seeds from them using a pointed stick.
3. Pour the prepared berries into thickly cooked sugar.
4. Cook everything slowly and place in small containers, preferably jars.
5. When cooled, seal with paper and a bladder.
