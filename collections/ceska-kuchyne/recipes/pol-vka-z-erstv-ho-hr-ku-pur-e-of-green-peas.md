## Ingredients

- about half a pint or more of fresh green peas
- three ounces of good fresh butter
- cold water
- chopped green parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two quarts of clear beef broth

## Directions

1. Shell about half a pint or more of fresh green peas, drain and put in a pot with three ounces of good fresh butter and a little cold water and stir by hand for a long time until the butter coats the peas
2. Drain the water clearly, add a spoon of chopped green parsley with a little salt and pepper and let stew under a lid until soft
3. Press through a fine sieve, dilute with two quarts of clear beef broth, let simmer a while and carefully skim off foam and fat
4. Pour into a bowl over fried bread rolls
