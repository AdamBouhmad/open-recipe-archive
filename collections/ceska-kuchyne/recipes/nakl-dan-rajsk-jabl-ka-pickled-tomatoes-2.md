## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe but not too soft tomatoes
- wine vinegar
- peppercorns
- allspice
- cloves
- salt
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Wash the tomatoes in cold water and let them dry on clean cloth
2. Arrange them in jars or stone jars
3. Meanwhile boil wine vinegar with a few peppercorns, allspice, cloves, and a little salt
4. When completely cooled, pour over the tomatoes
5. The vinegar must completely cover the tomatoes and the surface is sealed with melted butter or lard
6. Seal the containers tightly or tie them up and store in a cool place
7. Alternatively, you may use equal parts vinegar and water, but in that case use more salt and proceed otherwise as before
