## Ingredients

- grape berries
- sugar (half cup per cup of cooked grape pulp)

## Directions

1. Remove grape berries from the stems, wash and squeeze the insides of the berries from their skins into a bowl, putting the skins in another bowl.
2. Cook the grape insides with pits as they are, without adding water, since they yield enough juice.
3. Cook about half an hour until you see the pits have separated from the fruit, then strain through a sieve or squeeze in thin cloth to dryness and put it back to cook with the skins.
4. Add half cup of sugar per cup of cooked grapes, or more according to taste, cook one and a half hours and pour into jars.
