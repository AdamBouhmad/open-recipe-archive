## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 liters flour
- 6 eggs in 1/4 liter sour cream
- 45 grams yeast (dissolved in milk)
- 24 decagrams butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour, eggs in sour cream, yeast dissolved in milk, butter, and salt
2. Work the dough well
3. Let the dough rise for a while
4. Roll out and cut into square pieces
5. Roll the squares into rolls
6. Let rise on a baking sheet
7. Bake
