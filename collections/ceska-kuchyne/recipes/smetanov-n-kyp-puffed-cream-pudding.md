## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 pound blanched ground almonds
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 cup thick sour cream
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cream together 2 tablespoons butter
2. Add 1/4 pound ground blanched almonds and 1/2 cup sugar
3. Add 8 egg yolks, 1 cup thick sour cream, and lemon zest cut into pieces
4. Beat for at least 15 minutes
5. Fold in stiff peaks made from 8 egg whites
6. Put into a mold and bake in the oven
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
