## Ingredients

- young leek
- onion
- salt water
- raisins
- beef broth
- pale anchovy
- vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- beef

## Directions

1. Clean young leek, cut off the leaves
2. Clean and halve the onions
3. Cook in salted water for quarter hour
4. Drain on a sieve so water runs off
5. Cook with raisins in beef broth
6. Add pale anchovy, a little vinegar, salt and sugar
7. Add the sauce to beef and potatoes
