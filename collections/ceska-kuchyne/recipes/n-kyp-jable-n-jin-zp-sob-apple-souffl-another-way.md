## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- water
- wine
- sugar
- cinnamon
- vanilla
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) crushed almonds
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the apples, cook in water with wine until a mash forms, press through a sieve
2. Stew with sugar, cinnamon and vanilla; when cool, add 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter, 3 egg yolks, 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of crushed almonds and snow from 3 egg whites
3. Bake in a greased mold
