## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram grated potatoes
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) bacon cut into cubes
- 1 grated bread roll
- 1 bread roll cut into cubes
- 1 spoonful salt
- 4-5 eggs
- bread roll grated and fried in butter

## Directions

1. Fry grated potatoes in bacon cubes
2. Add grated bread roll and bread roll cubes
3. Fry a while
4. Cool
5. Add salt and beaten eggs
6. Make dumplings
7. Dust with flour
8. Cook in salted water
9. Add grated bread roll fried in butter
10. Serve with sauces or roasts
