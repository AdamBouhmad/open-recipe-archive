## Ingredients

- Green grapes
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Preserve and prepare the grapes in the same way as described in the previous section
2. On the second day, pour off the sugar and cook to the sixth degree
3. Add the grape berries, cook everything together, skim the foam
4. Remove the grapes to dry, then sprinkle with finely ground sugar, coat thoroughly, dry again and turn frequently
5. Once completely dried, store between fine paper in boxes
