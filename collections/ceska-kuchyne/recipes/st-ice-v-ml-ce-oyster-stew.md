## Ingredients

- one quart can of oysters
- one quart milk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of milk and watch that it does not scorch
2. Take a spoonful of butter and a small spoonful of flour, rub them well together and mix into the boiling milk
3. When the boil is complete, quickly pour the oysters into the milk with a little of their juice
4. As soon as they begin to rise, remove from heat
5. Salt and pepper
6. Serve at table
