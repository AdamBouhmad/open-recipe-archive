## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 14 dek sugar
- 9 dek almonds
- 5 dek grated bread moistened with wine
- snow from 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix and rub the egg yolks with sugar
2. Add almonds
3. Add grated bread moistened with wine
4. Fold in snow from beaten egg whites
