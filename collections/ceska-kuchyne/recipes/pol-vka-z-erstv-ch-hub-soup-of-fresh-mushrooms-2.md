## Ingredients

- several nice fresh mushrooms (porcini, button mushrooms, or other varieties)
- butter
- a little chopped fresh parsley
- salt
- good beef stock
- fried bread or roll cubes

## Directions

1. Cut several nice fresh mushrooms, clean them well, place them in a pan with a piece of butter, add a little chopped fresh parsley and salt, and let them stew covered.
2. When they begin to lose their water, transfer them to a pot, pour over them as much good beef stock as you need soup, and let it cook well.
3. Then pour the soup into a bowl over fried bread or roll cubes.
