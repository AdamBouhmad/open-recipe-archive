## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- butter
- sugar
- broth
- salt
- brown roux
- ground ginger
- kohlrabi greens

## Directions

1. Peel several kohlrabi and cut into thin rounds
2. Place butter and sugar on a pan, add cut kohlrabi, add a little broth, salt, and braise until soft
3. Brown with a light brown roux and add ground ginger
4. Select younger leaves from the kohlrabi greens, rinse in clean water and cook until tender, then drain
5. Chop the greens finely, place on pan with butter, add broth and brown with the same roux
6. Arrange on a serving dish in the center of the kohlrabi and border with the chopped greens
