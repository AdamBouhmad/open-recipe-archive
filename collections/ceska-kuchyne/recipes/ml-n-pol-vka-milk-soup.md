## Ingredients

- 2 litres milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- salt
- several tablespoons roasted flour
- several egg yolks
- rolls, fried

## Directions

1. Boil 2 litres of milk with sugar, cinnamon and salt
2. Add several tablespoons of roasted flour, boil for a while
3. Mix several egg yolks and pour over fried rolls
