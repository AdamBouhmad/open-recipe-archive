## Ingredients

- water
- cornmeal
- salt
- bacon bits
- grated cheese or firm curd cheese

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil in a clean iron pot
2. Sprinkle in cornmeal with one hand while stirring
3. Salt to taste
4. Sprinkle generously with bacon bits and stir
5. Cover the pot well and bake in an oven or stove for 1.5 hours
6. When done, turn out the hot mixture onto a shallow dish
7. Cut into slices with a strong thread
8. Grease as desired and sprinkle with grated cheese or firm curd cheese
9. When turned out, it looks like a loaf of bread
