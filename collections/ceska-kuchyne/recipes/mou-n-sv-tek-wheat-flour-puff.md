## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 small cups sifted good flour
- 3 tablespoons cream
- salt
- milk
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub 4 tablespoons butter until it foams, add to it 6 whole eggs, 4 tablespoons sugar, 2 small cups sifted good flour, 3 tablespoons cream, salt slightly and mix well
2. Grease a metal baking pan with butter, pour the batter about halfway up the pan and let it bake slowly in the oven
3. Then remove the puff from the oven, pierce it several times with a small knife all the way to the bottom, pour cold milk over it
4. After a while, when the milk has soaked into the puff, add as much milk as before, but you must sweeten the milk and add a little vanilla
5. Then when the puff is sufficiently soaked with milk, remove it from the heat, drain the remaining milk from it, turn it out onto a dish
