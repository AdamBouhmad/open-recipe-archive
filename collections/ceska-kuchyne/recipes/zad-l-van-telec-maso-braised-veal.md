## Ingredients

- Veal neck
- Butter
- Vegetables
- Salt
- Spices
- White roux
- Lemon juice or mushrooms or parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat and cook it, add a good piece of butter, various vegetables but not too much, and salt
2. When the meat is tender, cut into pieces
3. Strain the broth and make a white roux in butter, strain into the broth
4. Mix in a little ground spice and cook
5. Add lemon juice for sour sauce, or add fresh or dried finely chopped mushrooms and chopped green parsley, or use mushrooms that can be cauliflower cooked in beef broth, button mushrooms or sliced morels
