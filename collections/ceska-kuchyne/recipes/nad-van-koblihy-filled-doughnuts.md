## Ingredients

- white flour
- 2 eggs
- sour cream
- salt
- jam
- raisins
- chopped almonds
- curd cheese
- sugar
- egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix white flour with 2 eggs, sour cream and salt
2. Work the dough, beat with a roller and let rest for an hour under a warm bowl
3. Roll out, pull out as for strudel, cut into wide strips
4. Spread jam on the strips, sprinkle with raisins, chopped almonds or curd cheese mixed with sugar and egg yolks
5. Stack the strips one on top of another, cut out doughnuts and fry in butter until pale
