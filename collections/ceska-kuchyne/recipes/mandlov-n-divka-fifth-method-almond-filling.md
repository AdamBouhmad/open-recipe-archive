## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 14 dek sugar
- 4 dek citron peel
- 14 dek finely grated or chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs and 4 egg yolks
2. Add sugar until all is frothy
3. Add the peeled lemon zest
4. Add 4 dek citron peel and finely grated almonds
