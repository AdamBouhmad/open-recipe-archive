## Ingredients

- large, ripe plums
- sweet almonds, peeled
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons rum
- 2 egg whites
- hot butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the large ripe plums
2. Remove the pits with a wooden stick and replace each with a peeled sweet almond
3. Mix flour, sugar, rum, and egg whites to make a batter
4. Dip each plum one by one in the batter
5. Fry in hot butter
6. Place on blotting paper
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
8. Serve
