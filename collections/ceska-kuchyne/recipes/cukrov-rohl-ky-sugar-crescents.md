## Ingredients

- two egg whites
- six ounces sugar with vanilla
- lemon zest
- ground almonds with skins

## Directions

1. Make stiff snow from two egg whites
2. Slowly mix in six ounces of sugar beaten with vanilla and a little fine lemon zest
3. Using a teaspoon, place pieces of snow into ground almonds with skins
4. Arrange on a baking sheet coated with wax and bake slowly
5. Remove from sheet while hot
