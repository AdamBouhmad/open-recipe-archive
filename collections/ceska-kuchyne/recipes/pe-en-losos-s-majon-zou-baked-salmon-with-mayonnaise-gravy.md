## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- fresh butter
- lemons
- lemon rind
- capers
- new spice
- pepper
- cloves
- parsley
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. From a nice piece of salmon cut small pieces about two fingers wide
2. Salt them and bake in fresh butter
3. Place on a dish, pour fresh heated butter over them
4. Squeeze juice from several lemons over them, sprinkle with finely chopped lemon rind and capers
5. Crush a few grains of new spice, pepper and four cloves, sprinkle this over the salmon and let it lie for several hours
6. Remove the salmon, arrange on a dish, pour mayonnaise sauce in the middle and make a border of parsley
