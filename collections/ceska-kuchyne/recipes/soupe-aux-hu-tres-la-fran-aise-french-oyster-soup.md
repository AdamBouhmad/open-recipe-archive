## Ingredients

- white part of a small leek, finely chopped
- one small onion, finely chopped
- two ounces butter
- two ounces grated bread
- about sixty medium oysters with their liquid
- two quarts parsley water or lean broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- fresh parsley, chopped
- four egg yolks or two whole eggs
- one small cup cream
- a piece of butter

## Directions

1. Chop the white part of the leek finely and slowly brown it with the finely chopped onion in two ounces of butter
2. Add two ounces of grated bread to it, let it brown a little with the onion and add about sixty medium oysters with their liquid and two quarts of parsley water or lean broth
3. Let it boil over high heat and stir constantly so the oysters do not stick to the bottom
4. Skim off the foam, salt and pepper the soup, season with nutmeg
5. Before serving, mix in a little chopped fresh parsley and thicken it with four egg yolks or two whole eggs beaten with a small cup of cream and a piece of butter
