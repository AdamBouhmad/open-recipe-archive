## Ingredients

- 7 dek butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dek sugar
- 4 dek chopped sultanas
- almonds
- approximately 28 dek shredded curd cheese

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with egg yolks and sugar
2. Add chopped sultanas and almonds
3. Mix with shredded curd cheese
