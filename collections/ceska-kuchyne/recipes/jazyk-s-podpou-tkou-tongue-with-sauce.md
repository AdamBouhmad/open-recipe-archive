## Ingredients

- Fresh beef or veal tongues
- Salt
- Various spices
- Flour
- Butter
- Tongue cooking broth
- Lemon slices
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Wine

## Directions

1. Rinse fresh beef or veal tongues, clean them, salt them and cook them with various spices until tender, skimming foam from the surface as they cook
2. Remove the skin from the cooked tongues and slice them into thin rounds
3. Make a light roux from flour and butter, add only the broth from the cooked tongues
4. Add lemon slices, white pepper, nutmeg, and a little wine to the sauce
5. Place the sliced tongues in the sauce and cook everything together for about a quarter hour
6. Strain the sauce and beat in egg yolks, then pour over the tongues
