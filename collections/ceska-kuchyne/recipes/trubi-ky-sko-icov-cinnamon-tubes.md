## Ingredients

- 250 grams butter
- 7 egg yolks
- 1/2 kilogram ground sugar
- 6 grams cinnamon
- grated lemon zest
- 1/2 kilogram fine sifted flour

## Directions

1. Spread out 250 grams of butter
2. Gradually beat in 7 egg yolks, 1/2 kilogram of ground sugar, 6 grams of cinnamon, a little grated lemon zest, and 1/2 kilogram of fine sifted flour
3. Mix and knead thoroughly
4. Let the dough rest for about an hour
5. Fill buttered metal tubes with the dough
6. Bake slowly until golden
7. Remove and roll onto wooden sticks while still warm
