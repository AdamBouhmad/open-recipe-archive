## Ingredients

- 15-20 tender asparagus stalks
- two quarts of beef broth
- a spoonful of chopped green parsley
- fresh butter
- flour
- pepper
- saffron
- lamb or mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take 15-20 tender asparagus stalks, clean the upper part from the head downward, shell and cut into pieces the size of peas, then cook them in a pot in two quarts of beef broth
2. Meanwhile let a spoonful of chopped green parsley cook down on fresh butter and add enough flour to thicken the broth
3. When the flour begins to turn yellow, strain the asparagus broth through it, mix well, season with pepper and saffron, pour back over the asparagus and let simmer thoroughly
4. Serve with lamb cut into pieces and cooked with beef or several mushrooms stewed in butter
