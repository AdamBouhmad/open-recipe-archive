## Ingredients

- a piece of cold capon or veal roast
- grated roll
- finely chopped green parsley
- cloves
- butter
- beef broth
- three egg yolks
- dough for noodles
- egg white
- fresh mushrooms
- fresh butter
- green parsley
- cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop a piece of cold capon or veal roast finely
2. Add grated roll, finely chopped green parsley, a bit of cloves, and stew everything on butter for a bit
3. Add about one or two spoonfuls of beef broth and three egg yolks, stew for a bit longer, then let it cool
4. Make dough as for noodles, roll it out, and cut it into small pieces for ravioles
5. Brush the edges with egg white, place a small heap of the filling on each piece, press the edges together, and seal the ravioles
6. Cook them in two quarts of good broth
7. If fresh mushrooms are available, chop them finely and fry them on a pan with a piece of fresh butter, add green parsley, a bit of cloves, and a bit of pepper, then add them to the broth
