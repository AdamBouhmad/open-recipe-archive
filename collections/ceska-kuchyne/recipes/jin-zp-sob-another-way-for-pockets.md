## Ingredients

- dough for noodles
- prune butter
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg white
- crumb or cheese filling

## Directions

1. Make dough like for noodles and roll out well without letting it dry
2. Cut square pieces with a cutter
3. Mix prune butter with water, sugar, and cinnamon
4. Place on dough squares
5. Cover with another square
6. Press edges with cutter brushed with egg white
7. Boil in salted water
8. Drain
9. Butter and sprinkle with crumb or cheese
