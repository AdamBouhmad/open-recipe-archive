## Ingredients

- butter
- 2 eggs
- a little flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 6 heaping tablespoons grated cooked floury potatoes
- egg for coating
- bread crumbs
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Cream the butter until it foams
2. Add 2 eggs
3. Mix in a little flour, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Add 6 heaping tablespoons of grated cooked floury potatoes
5. Form into elongated croquettes
6. Brush with egg
7. Coat with bread crumbs
8. Fry in butter
9. Serves as a garnish for meat
