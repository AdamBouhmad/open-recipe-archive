## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- One liter sweet cream
- More than half kilogram fine crushed sugar
- Finely crushed vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs, whip them until foamy
2. Slowly add one liter sweet cream while adding more than half kilogram fine crushed sugar and finely crushed vanilla
3. Cook the mixture with constant stirring and whipping
4. Remove from heat and continue whipping until completely cooled
5. Strain through fine sieve
6. Place in ice cream machine and make as described for regular ice cream
