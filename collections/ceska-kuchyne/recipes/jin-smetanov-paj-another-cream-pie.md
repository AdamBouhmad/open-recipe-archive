## Ingredients

- 1 pastry crust
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a bottom crust
2. Take 1 cup sweet cream and 1 cup sugar and mix well together
3. Take 3 egg whites, whip them well into stiff snow, and pour into the cream, mixing slightly
4. Spread onto the crust and bake
5. Pierce the crust with a fork so that small cracks do not form
6. Bake to golden color
7. No snow topping is necessary as it is rich enough on its own
