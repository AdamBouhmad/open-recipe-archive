## Ingredients

- one quart of shelled green peas
- half a small cup of water
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of sugar
- half a tablespoon of good butter
- half a tablespoon of flour
- a small cup of cream or good milk
- a small amount of pepper

## Directions

1. Take a quart of shelled green peas and add half a small cup of water, a pinch of salt and a pinch of sugar and let it cook until soft, which takes about half an hour if it is true sweet and young peas.
2. If the water boils away, add a little, but not much at once.
3. When soft, rub half a tablespoon of good butter with half a tablespoon of flour and mix it into the boiling peas.
4. When it thickens, add a small cup of cream or good milk, season lightly with pepper and remove from heat.
5. It is especially good served with stewed chicken.
