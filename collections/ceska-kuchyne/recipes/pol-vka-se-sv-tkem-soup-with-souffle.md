## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- 70 grams (4 lots) butter
- 6 egg yolks
- herbs
- ginger
- 2 spoonfuls flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites for half an hour, add butter, egg yolks, herbs, ginger and flour
2. After beating for a quarter hour, place in a greased mold and steam for a quarter hour
3. Remove from mold, let cool and cut into cubes, add to soup
