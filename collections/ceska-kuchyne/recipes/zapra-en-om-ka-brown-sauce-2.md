## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons dry flour
- 2 cups beef broth
- bunch of vegetables
- peppercorns
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Make a smooth brown roux from two tablespoons of butter and the same amount of dry flour.
2. Add two cups of beef broth and mix well.
3. Let it simmer well and strain into a bowl.
4. This sauce is served on its own or serves as a base for other sauces.
5. A bunch of vegetables, several peppercorns and about two cloves may also be simmered in it.
