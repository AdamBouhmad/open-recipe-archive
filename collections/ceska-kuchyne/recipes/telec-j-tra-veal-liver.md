## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Bacon
- Onion
- Butter or bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Wash the liver, remove the membrane; salt it, pierce with bacon, chop onion and braise with butter or bacon for a while; then place the liver on a baking pan with the onion, add pepper and 4 cloves, braise until softened, strain the sauce over it, and the liver may be sliced or left whole.
