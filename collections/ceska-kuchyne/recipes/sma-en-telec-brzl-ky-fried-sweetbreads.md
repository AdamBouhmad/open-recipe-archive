## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- salt
- warm water
- 2 eggs
- piece of heated butter
- grated roll or bread
- fresh butter for frying
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the veal sweetbreads well and place them in lukewarm water for a quarter hour to blanch them
2. Put them in boiling salted water and boil for five minutes to firm them up
3. Place them again in cold water and let them cool
4. Split each one lengthwise in half
5. Season both sides with salt
6. Beat two eggs with a piece of heated butter
7. Dip the sweetbreads in this mixture and sprinkle both sides with grated roll or bread
8. Melt fresh butter on the stove and fry the sweetbreads on it until brown, not too quickly
9. Arrange them on a dish and sprinkle with lemon juice
10. Serve; you can add carrots, spinach, or other vegetables
