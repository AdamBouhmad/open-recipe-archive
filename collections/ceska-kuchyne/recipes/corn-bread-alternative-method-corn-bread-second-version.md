## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons melted butter or lard
- 1/4 small cup wheat flour
- 1 1/2 small cups cornmeal
- 1 small cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon baking soda beaten well until foamy

## Directions

1. Mix egg, sugar, salt and flour
2. Add the sour milk with baking soda beaten in
3. Mix well
4. Pour into a baking pan and bake in a hot oven for at least half an hour
