## Ingredients

- Birch sap
- 200 grams of sugar per liter
- 3-4 full spoons of yeast (per 24 liters of sap)
- 4 liters of good wine (per 24 liters of sap)
- 1/8 of a wheel of sliced lemons without seeds (per 24 liters of sap)

## Directions

1. Boil birch sap for half an hour, add 200 grams of sugar per liter, strain and let cool.
2. Pour into a barrel, then to every 24 liters of sap add 3-4 full spoons of yeast, 4 liters of good wine, and 1/8 of a wheel of sliced lemons without seeds.
3. Let the sap ferment and seal the barrel tightly.
4. After four weeks transfer to bottles, cork them well and seal, and let rest peacefully for at least two to three months before drinking, which must then happen fairly quickly as it does not keep long.
5. The more sugar and wine are added, the longer the wine keeps.
