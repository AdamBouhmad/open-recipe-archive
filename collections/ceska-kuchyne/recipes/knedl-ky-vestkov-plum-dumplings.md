## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram sifted flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 15 grams yeast diluted with milk, sugar and flour
- milk
- plums
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Heat a piece of butter.
2. Put the sifted flour on a baking sheet and salt it.
3. Add the heated butter, 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs.
4. Mix in 15 grams of yeast diluted with milk, sugar and flour.
5. Add milk as needed so the dough does not stick to the hand.
6. Work the dough thoroughly.
7. Cut the dough into pieces and wrap a plum in each piece.
8. Roll into balls properly.
9. After a quarter hour of rising, boil in salted water.
10. Grease with butter and sprinkle with either cheese or gingerbread.
