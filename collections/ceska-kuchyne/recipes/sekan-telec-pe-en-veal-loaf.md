## Ingredients

- 3 pounds veal meat
- ½ pound salt pork, or ¼ pound butter
- 3 eggs
- 2 slices bread, rolled out
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Chop the veal meat and salt pork finely by hand or with a grinder
2. Add three eggs, two slices of rolled bread, two tablespoons of sour cream, one tablespoon of butter
3. Season with salt and pepper and mix well
4. Form into an oblong loaf
5. Grease a baking pan with butter and place the loaf in it
6. Place a piece of butter on top
7. Pour approximately two tablespoons of water over it
8. Bake for about two hours, basting frequently with the pan juices
9. When cooled after baking, slice into neat pieces
