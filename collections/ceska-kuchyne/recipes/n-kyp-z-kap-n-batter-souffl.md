## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon water
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 whole eggs
- egg white snow from 8 egg whites
- lemon zest
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make thick batter from 6 eggs, 1 tablespoon water, and 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream
2. Add 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter and beat so long that all the butter is incorporated into the batter, then let it cool
3. Beat again with 8 egg yolks, 4 whole eggs, snow made from 8 egg whites, lemon zest, and sufficient sugar
4. Put a layer of batter in a buttered mold, alternating with stewed cherries, until the mold is almost full
5. Bake and sprinkle with sugar
