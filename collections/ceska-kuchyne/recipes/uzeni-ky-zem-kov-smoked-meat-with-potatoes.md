## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Half kilogram cooked smoked meat
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 4-5 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes, peel, and cut into slices when cooled
2. Chop the cooked smoked meat
3. Grease a baking pan with butter
4. Place the mixture of smoked meat and potatoes on the pan
5. Salt and pepper
6. Beat 4-5 eggs and pour over the mixture
7. Crumble butter on top
8. Bake in the oven
9. Serve on the table in the baking pan wrapped with a cloth
