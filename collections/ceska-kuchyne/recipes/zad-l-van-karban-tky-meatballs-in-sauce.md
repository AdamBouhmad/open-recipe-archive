## Ingredients

- calf meat
- a piece of goose liver
- a piece of marrow
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- butter sauce
- burnt sugar
- good broth
- bay leaf
- a little wine
- butter dough hearts

## Directions

1. Finely chop the calf meat, a piece of goose liver and marrow, season with pepper and salt
2. Form meatballs and bake them in butter
3. Prepare the sauce: add burnt sugar, good broth, bay leaf and a little wine to butter sauce
4. Boil the sauce, pour it over the meatballs, arrange butter dough hearts around them
