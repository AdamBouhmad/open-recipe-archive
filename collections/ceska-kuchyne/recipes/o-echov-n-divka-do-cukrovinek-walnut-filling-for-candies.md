## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 1/8 liter water
- 20 dekagrams (13 lots) walnut kernels, beaten with cream
- a little cinnamon or vanilla
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) candied lemon peel

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar in the water
2. Add the walnuts beaten with cream, a little cinnamon or vanilla, and the candied lemon peel
3. Mix everything well
4. Use the mixture as a filling for various candies
