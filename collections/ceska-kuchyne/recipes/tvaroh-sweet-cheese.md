## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter
- one pound fresh sweet tvaroh
- four egg yolks
- six tablespoons sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- pinch of salt
- half a small cup currants or almonds cut into strips

## Directions

1. Rub one tablespoon of butter in a bowl, mix in one pound of fresh sweet tvaroh, then stir in four egg yolks, six tablespoons of sugar, grated zest of half a lemon, a pinch of salt, and half a small cup of picked currants or almonds cut into strips.
