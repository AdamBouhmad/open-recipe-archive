## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- rendered bacon
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Peel boiled potatoes and let them cool
2. Slice them into slices, add finely chopped onion, salt and pepper, mix well
3. Pour rendered bacon over them and then vinegar mixed with half water into which you have mixed a little sugar to give the salad a better taste
4. Mix with a knife and arrange hard-boiled eggs cut into slices on top
