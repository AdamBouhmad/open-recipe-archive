## Ingredients

- quarter pound sago
- two quarts clear strong beef soup
- four egg yolks
- lukewarm soup

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago, pour two quarts of clear strong beef soup over it and let cook until soft.
2. In a small cup, mix four egg yolks with a little lukewarm soup, add to the rest of the boiling soup and serve.
3. Soup with groats is prepared similarly.
