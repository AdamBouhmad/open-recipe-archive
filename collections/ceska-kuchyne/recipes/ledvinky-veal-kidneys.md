## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- flour
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Remove the fat from the veal kidneys, cut them into slices, and put them in hot butter.
2. Salt, pepper, and add chopped parsley.
3. Stir over high heat, dust with flour, add good beef broth, and stir until the sauce thickens.
