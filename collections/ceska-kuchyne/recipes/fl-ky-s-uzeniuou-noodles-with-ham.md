## Ingredients

- Smoked pork meat, well-marbled
- Dough for noodles (flour, eggs, salt)
- Broth from cooking the meat
- Lard or butter for greasing
- Milk or sour cream, 2 koflíky
- Egg yolks, 4
- Butter, new, in pieces
- Breadcrumb crust

## Directions

1. Cook well-marbled smoked pork meat until tender, then chop finely
2. Make dough as for noodles and cut into flíčky (thin noodles)
3. Cook the flíčky in the strained broth in which the meat was cooked
4. Drain them well
5. Grease a mold or shallow pan with lard or butter
6. Layer alternately flíčky and chopped smoked meat, ending with flíčky on top
7. Beat together 4 egg yolks with 2 koflíky milk or sour cream
8. Pour this mixture over the flíčky
9. Top with pieces of new butter
10. Bake slowly in the oven until golden
11. Sprinkle the breadcrumb crust on top and serve at the table
