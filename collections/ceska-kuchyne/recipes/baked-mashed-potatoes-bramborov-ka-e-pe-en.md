## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- butter
- grated roll

## Directions

1. Prepare the mash according to the previous method
2. Butter a mold and place the mash in it
3. Place pieces of fresh butter on top
4. Sprinkle with grated roll and bake in the oven until golden
