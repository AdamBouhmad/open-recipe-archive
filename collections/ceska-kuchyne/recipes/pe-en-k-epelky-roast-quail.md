## Ingredients

- quail
- salt
- bacon
- grape leaves
- butter
- beef broth
- string
- optional filling
- optional: onion, parsley, mushrooms, red wine

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean quail, sprinkle with a little salt
2. Wrap them in bacon and if available, grape leaves, and tie with string
3. Arrange them on a roasting pan, place pieces of butter, baste with beef broth
4. Roast in the oven until golden
5. Untie the strings, remove the leaves from the quail, arrange nicely on a platter and pour the strained juices over them
6. You can also fill them with a fine stuffing
7. You can also place sliced onion, parsley and mushrooms on the pan under the quail and when they are nearly done, pour in a glass of red wine
