## Ingredients

- pike
- onion
- butter
- bread
- milk
- egg yolks
- anchovy butter
- salt
- marjoram
- bread crumbs
- cheese
- lard
- capers or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. From cleaned pike, cut the sides away from the ribs so the head and remaining skeleton remain joined by a strip of meat on the back
2. Chop the cut meat finely with two onions fried in butter, two bread rolls soaked in milk, three egg yolks, anchovy butter, salt and marjoram
3. Spread this mixture back on the cleaned ribs so the pike regains its original shape
4. Place in a greased pan
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and cheese
6. Brush with fat and cover with lardoons
7. Bake until done
8. Serve with caper or anchovy sauce
