## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- butter
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower and carefully separate the florets, inspect well to remove any insects, rinse in several waters
2. Pour clean cold water over it, salt and cook
3. When completely tender, drain the water and add a piece of butter, sweeten to taste and add half a cup of good sweet cream, or retain a little of the cooking water
4. Make a thin roux from a spoonful of butter, brown it and sweeten to taste
5. The version with cream is more delicious
6. A few florets of cauliflower can always be added to broth to make it very tasty
