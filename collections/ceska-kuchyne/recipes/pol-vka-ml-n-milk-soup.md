## Ingredients

- 1 litre milk
- 2 handfuls flour
- sugar (optional)
- lemon juice (optional)
- roll, fried and diced
- butter

## Directions

1. Place 1 litre of milk on the stove
2. When it begins to boil, mix in 2 handfuls of flour
3. Add sugar if you prefer it sweet, or lemon juice if you prefer it sour
4. Fry a roll in butter, dice it and add to the soup
