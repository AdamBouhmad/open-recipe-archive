## Ingredients

- Ham
- Cold water
- Vegetables

## Directions

1. Rinse the ham, scrub well and soak in cold water for several hours to easily remove all impurities.
2. Bring the thus prepared ham to boiling in cold water slowly, and when softened - remove from heat and let cool in its own cooked broth either completely or partially, depending on whether it is to be served hot or cold.
3. The skin is removed and the hot ham is placed on a dish - surrounded by various vegetables (spinach, lentil or pea purée, potatoes, etc.), or the cold ham is decorated in various ways and garnished with aspic.
