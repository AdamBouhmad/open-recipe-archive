## Ingredients

- pork loin meat from the ribs
- salt
- butter
- flour
- pork broth
- vinegar
- lemon zest
- allspice
- cloves
- blanched almonds
- raisins without pits
- syrup
- gingerbread

## Directions

1. Salt and let a nice piece of pork loin from the ribs cook.
2. Make a light brown roux (from 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon flour for 2 pounds), thin it with broth from the meat, add a little vinegar, a piece of fine lemon zest, a little allspice, three crushed cloves, blanched almonds, raisins without pits, a little syrup, and half a cup of grated gingerbread and let it simmer.
3. Place the meat, cooked until tender and cut into larger pieces, into this sauce, let it simmer for another minute, and serve.
