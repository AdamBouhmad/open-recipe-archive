## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried mushrooms
- salt water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 2 rolls or 2 slices of bread soaked in milk
- lemon rind
- caraway seeds
- seasonings
- salt
- butter for greasing
- grated roll crust

## Directions

1. Soak 2 cups of dried mushrooms in cold water and cook them in salted water until soft
2. Strain the water, finely chop the mushrooms
3. Beat 2 tablespoons of butter on a platter, crack 4 whole eggs into it and mix
4. Add the chopped mushrooms, 2 rolls or 2 slices of bread soaked in milk and squeezed out, finely chopped lemon rind, a pinch of caraway seeds, season lightly, and salt and mix well
5. Grease a small pan with butter, coat with grated roll crust, pour the mixture into it and bake in the oven
6. Turn out onto a platter and serve whole, or cut it and use it to border spinach, lentils, or other vegetables
