## Ingredients

- 20-30 crayfish
- salted water
- 150 grams butter
- flour
- 2.5-3 liters clear light meat soup or fish, pea or vegetable soup for fasting
- fried roll, sliced and lightly pounded
- salt
- fried roll cut into cubes
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Cook 20-30 crayfish in salted water, shell the tails, crush the rest after removing intestines and gallbladder in a mortar
2. With 150 grams of butter on a low flame, brown with a spoonful of flour
3. Add 2.5-3 liters of clear light meat soup or fish, pea or vegetable soup, cook everything together with fried sliced and lightly pounded roll, salt and strain
4. Add to the soup fried roll cut into cubes with crayfish tails
