## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- More than a liter of sour cream
- About three-quarters of a kilo of crushed sugar
- Zest of two lemons

## Directions

1. Beat the four eggs
2. Add more than a liter of sour cream
3. Add about three-quarters of a kilo of crushed sugar
4. Add grated zest from two lemons
5. Beat everything, bring to a boil
6. Proceed as described for simple ice cream
