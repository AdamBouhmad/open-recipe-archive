## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- 2 bay leaves
- Sour cream
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the carp, cut into pieces and sprinkle with salt and ground pepper
2. Butter a baking pan and place 2 bay leaves in it
3. Arrange the cut carp in the pan
4. Pour a little good sour cream over it
5. Add the juice of 1 lemon
6. Bake in the oven until golden, basting frequently with sour cream during cooking
7. Carefully remove from the pan and arrange on a platter
8. Strain the sauce and serve in a small bowl
