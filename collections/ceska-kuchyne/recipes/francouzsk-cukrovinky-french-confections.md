## Ingredients

- 375 grams finely pressed and sifted sugar
- 15 egg yolks
- water from orange blossoms
- 50 grams finely chopped candied orange peel
- 230 grams fine flour
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 375 grams of finely pressed and sifted sugar
2. Add 15 egg yolks and stir for at least an hour, always in one direction
3. Add water from orange blossoms
4. Add 50 grams of finely chopped candied orange peel and 230 grams of fine flour
5. Add stiff snow from egg whites
6. Mix and work thoroughly
7. Place in small molds or on a buttered baking sheet
8. Bake slowly at moderate temperature until golden
