## Ingredients

- head lettuce
- salt
- wine vinegar
- sour cream
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Remove outer leaves from lettuce and cut
2. Salt and pour boiling water over it
3. Mix one part vinegar with one part good sour cream
4. Pour this mixture over the lettuce
5. Top with hard-boiled eggs, cut in quarters or halves
