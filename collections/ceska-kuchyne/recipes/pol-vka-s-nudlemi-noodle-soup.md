## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- two whole eggs
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of flour on a board, make a well in it
2. Break two whole eggs into the well and mix with a knife into stiff dough
3. Then knead it with your hands, constantly dusting with flour
4. Finally roll it out thin with a rolling pin
5. Spread it on a cloth and let the dough dry, but not too much
6. Dust the board with flour and cut the dough in half with a sharp knife
7. Dust it lightly with flour, fold it again and then cut into strips three fingers wide
8. Stack these strips on top of each other and cut into thin noodles
9. Spread the cut noodles on the board so they don't stick together
10. Cook a proportionate amount of noodles in good beef broth
11. Stir them and let them pass through several boilings so they don't overcook
12. The noodle dough can also be cut into small pieces or various shapes can be cut out with cutters
