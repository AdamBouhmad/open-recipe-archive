## Ingredients

- Hard rye bread
- Water
- Caraway
- Salt
- Butter
- Several egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut hard rye bread into cubes
2. Cook in water with caraway until it falls apart
3. Salt, strain, add butter and cook again
4. Beat in several egg yolks
