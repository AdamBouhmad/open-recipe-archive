## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) cherries
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- lemon zest
- 4 tablespoons ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 5 and 1/4 deciliters (1 and 1/2 tablespoons) sweet sour cream
- flour
- 6 egg whites
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put cherries in cold water
2. Beat or melt 6 dekagrams butter, salt it, add chopped lemon zest, 4 tablespoons ground sugar and beat together in a bowl
3. Add 6 egg yolks and 5 and 1/4 deciliters sweet sour cream
4. Add enough flour to make a thick batter and stiff snow from 6 egg whites
5. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated roll
6. Lay cherries in the mold, pour in the batter and bake
7. Turn out and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar
