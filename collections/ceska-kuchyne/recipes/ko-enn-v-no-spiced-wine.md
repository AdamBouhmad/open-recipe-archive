## Ingredients

- 1 liter of good red wine
- 20 dekagrams (13 lots) of sugar
- A piece of cinnamon
- Cloves
- Lemon zest rubbed with sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1 liter of good red wine with 20 dekagrams of sugar, a piece of cinnamon, cloves, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar, strain and pour into glasses.
