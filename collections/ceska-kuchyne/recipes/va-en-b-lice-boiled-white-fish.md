## Ingredients

- white fish
- salt
- lemon juice
- water
- onion
- green parsley
- parsley butter

## Directions

1. Clean the white fish and let it boil in salted water seasoned with lemon juice with sliced onion and a bunch of green parsley.
2. Place the boiled fish on a hot dish, pour melted parsley butter over it and arrange with sliced lemon and green parsley.
