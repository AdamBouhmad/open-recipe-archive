## Ingredients

- Peas from young pea pods
- Butter
- Sugar
- Salt
- Beef broth or water
- Pale roux
- Burnt sugar (if needed)
- Any fried fat

## Directions

1. Remove the peas from the young pea pods and wash them
2. Place on a baking pan with a piece of butter, sugar and salt, and stew until tender
3. If needed, moisten with beef broth or water
4. When tender, mix with pale roux
5. If it should not be pale, add a piece of burnt sugar
6. Pour and place any fried fat on top
