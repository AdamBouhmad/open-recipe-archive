## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Bacon
- White pepper
- Onion
- Beef broth or water
- Cream, three and a half deciliters
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the liver, remove all membranes, pierce with bacon, season with pepper, chop onion, add a piece of chopped bacon, add beef broth or water and braise until softened.
2. Strain the sauce, add three and a half deciliters of cream, place the liver in it and simmer a while.
