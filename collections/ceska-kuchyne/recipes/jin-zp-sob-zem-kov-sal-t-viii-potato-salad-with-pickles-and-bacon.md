## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- sour cucumber
- bacon

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked potatoes and mix with oil, salt, pepper, chopped onion, finely sliced sour cucumber, and chopped bacon
2. Alternatively, first pour beef broth over the potatoes, then add the oil and other ingredients
