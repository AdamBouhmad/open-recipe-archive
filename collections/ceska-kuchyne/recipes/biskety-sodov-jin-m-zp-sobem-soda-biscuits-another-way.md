## Ingredients

- 4 cups sifted flour
- 1 tablespoon hard lard
- a little salt
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon hot water

## Directions

1. Put 4 cups of sifted flour into a bowl, add 1 tablespoon of hard lard and a little salt and mix everything well with your hands with the flour.
2. Take 2 cups of sour milk; in a small cup dissolve 1 tablespoon of baking soda in 1 tablespoon of hot water, pour it into the sour milk and mix well.
3. Pour the milk into the flour while constantly stirring and work the dough well.
4. If necessary, add flour so that the dough is nicely firm.
5. Roll out the dough, cut round biscuits with a cutter, place them on a baking sheet greased with lard and put into a hot oven.
6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes and serve hot.
