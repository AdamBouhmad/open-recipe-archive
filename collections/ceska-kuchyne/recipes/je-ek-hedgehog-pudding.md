## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) butter
- 20 egg yolks
- sugar
- 24 dekagrams almonds
- 8 grated rolls with crusts removed
- 20 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 24 dekagrams butter until it foams
2. Add 20 egg yolks one by one
3. Add sufficient sugar
4. Add 24 dekagrams almonds and 8 grated rolls with crusts removed
5. Finally fold in snow from 20 egg whites
6. Steam in boiling water before serving
