## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) sugar
- 4 eggs
- 24 dekagrams unpeeled almonds cut into thin strips
- 12 dekagrams candied lemon zest
- 3 dekagrams cinnamon
- 8 grams (½ lot) cloves
- ½ kilogram flour

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar with the eggs
2. Add the almonds cut into thin strips
3. Mix in the candied lemon zest, cinnamon, and cloves
4. Add the flour
5. Form the dough into oblong loaves
6. Bake on a floured baking sheet
7. While still warm, slice into thin slices
8. Dry both sides to golden color
