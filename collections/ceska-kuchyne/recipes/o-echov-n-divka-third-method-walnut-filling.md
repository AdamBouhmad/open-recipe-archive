## Ingredients

- 40 dek finely chopped walnuts
- boiling sour cream
- 2 deciliters honey
- 2 tablespoons rum
- vanilla
- fine spices

## Directions

1. Scald the finely chopped walnuts with boiling sour cream
2. Add honey
3. Add rum, vanilla, and fine spices
