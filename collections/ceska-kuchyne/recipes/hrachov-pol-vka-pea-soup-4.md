## Ingredients

- half pint of peas
- good beef broth
- a little chopped marjoram
- a little green parsley
- two grated raw potatoes
- pale roux

## Directions

1. Soak half a pint of peas overnight in cold water and the next day cook until soft
2. Mash the peas and strain into a pot, pour good beef broth over it, add a little chopped marjoram, a little green parsley, two grated raw potatoes and let cook
3. Meanwhile make a pale roux, thicken the broth with it and pour into a bowl over fried and sliced bread rolls
