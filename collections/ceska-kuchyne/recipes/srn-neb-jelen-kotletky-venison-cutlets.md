## Ingredients

- venison cutlets
- salt
- melted butter
- wine
- pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare venison cutlets in the manner of mutton cutlets, salt them
2. Dip in melted butter and roast either on a grill or in a roasting pan until nicely browned
3. Dilute the roasting juices with a little wine, add pepper sauce, boil until thick and dip the cutlets in it
4. You can also let them lie in marinade for several hours before roasting
