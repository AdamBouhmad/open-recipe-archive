## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- beaten egg
- cooked fish
- soup
- butter
- lemon juice
- alternatively: fish or crayfish
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut puff pastry into small cakes, brush the edges with beaten egg
2. Fill with minced cooked fish that has been prepared with soup, butter, and lemon juice
3. Alternatively: Clean and cook fish or crayfish, finely chop and stew on a baking sheet
4. Fill the pastries with this filling and cover with a lid
