## Ingredients

- raw chestnuts
- flour
- ground sugar
- 8-10 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel raw chestnuts and grate them on a grater, using as many as desired for the soufflé
2. Mix equal parts flour and ground sugar so that the flour and sugar together weigh half what the grated chestnuts weigh
3. Add 8-10 egg yolks and mix everything well
4. Make stiff snow from the reserved egg whites and fold slowly into the mixture
5. Place in a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with breadcrumbs
6. Bake in the oven
