## Ingredients

- 37.5 dekagrams (25 lots) flour
- 20 dekagrams sugar
- 5 eggs
- 4 grams crushed cinnamon
- 70 grams finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Mix 37.5 dekagrams flour, 20 dekagrams sugar, 5 eggs, and 4 grams crushed cinnamon to form dough
2. Cut pieces the thickness and length of a finger
3. Roll in 70 grams finely chopped almonds
4. Bake on a sheet at moderate heat
