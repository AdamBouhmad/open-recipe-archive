## Ingredients

- Cleaned ham
- Black bread, grated
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned ham until tender in its own broth
2. Allow it to cool in its own cooking liquid
3. Remove the skin
4. Sprinkle the ham with grated black bread, sugar, cinnamon, and cloves
5. Grease with lard
6. Bake in the oven
