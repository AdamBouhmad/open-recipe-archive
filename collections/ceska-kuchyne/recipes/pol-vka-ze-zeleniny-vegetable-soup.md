## Ingredients

- onions
- parsley roots
- carrot roots
- celery
- butter
- ginger
- water
- salt
- pea water
- green parsley
- chervil
- green peas

## Directions

1. Cut into slices two large onions, three parsley roots, three carrot roots, one large or two small celeries
2. Put on a saucepan, add a piece of butter, two pieces of whole ginger, one or two spoonfuls of water, a little salt and let stew until brown
3. Then pour pea water on it, in which green parsley was cooked, and if it is spring, also chervil, and let it cook at least half an hour
4. Then strain the soup through a sieve
5. Meanwhile cut into strips a piece of onion, carrot and celery, let it with a handful of green peas brown on butter
6. Put it into the soup, let simmer a bit more, add a little chopped green parsley and pour into a bowl
