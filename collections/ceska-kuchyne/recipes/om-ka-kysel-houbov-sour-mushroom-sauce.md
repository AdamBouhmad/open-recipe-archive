## Ingredients

- half liter beef broth
- vinegar
- roux
- pepper
- dried mushrooms
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cook half liter beef broth with vinegar, roux and pepper
2. Wash dried mushrooms and cook in salted water
3. When cooked, add to the sauce and cook a short time longer
