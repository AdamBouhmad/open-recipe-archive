## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams sugar
- vanilla
- lemon peel, grated from 1/2 lemon
- 18 dekagrams flour
- fennel
- eggs

## Directions

1. Beat 9 dekagrams sugar with vanilla
2. Add the grated peel from 1/2 lemon on a grater
3. Add 18 dekagrams flour and fennel
4. Add enough eggs to form a batter-like dough
5. Grease a baking sheet and pour oblong strips onto it
6. While hot, wrap around a roller and dry in the oven until crisp
