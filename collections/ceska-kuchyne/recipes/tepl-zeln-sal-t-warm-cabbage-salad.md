## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- salt
- hot water
- butter or bacon, cut into small pieces
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Select white cabbage
2. Remove the core from cleaned and peeled heads
3. Remove thick ribs from leaves
4. Cut prepared leaves very finely
5. Either pour boiling water over or salt and let sit for two to three hours
6. Pour warm bacon or butter cut into small pieces over the salad on a dish
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Drizzle with vinegar
