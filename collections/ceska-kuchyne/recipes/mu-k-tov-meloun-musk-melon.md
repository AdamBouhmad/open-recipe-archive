## Ingredients

- Melons
- Sugar or salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chill melons on ice or in a cool place before serving to make them very cold
2. Clean and cut into halves or quarters
3. Arrange on grape leaves or other large leaves
4. Serve sprinkled with sugar or salted and peppered
