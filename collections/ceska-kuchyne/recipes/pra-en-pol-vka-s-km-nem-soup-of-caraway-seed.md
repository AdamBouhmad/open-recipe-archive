## Ingredients

- plenty of caraway seed
- salt
- water
- flour for roux

## Directions

1. Let plenty of caraway seed boil in salted water
2. Meanwhile make a brown roux
3. Strain the water from the pot into the roux and let boil well
4. Pour into a tureen on fried bread cubes, or you can add groats, noodles, or dumplings to it
