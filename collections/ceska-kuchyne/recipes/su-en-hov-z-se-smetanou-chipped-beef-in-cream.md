## Ingredients

- dried beef
- butter
- flour
- cream or milk
- 1 egg
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the dried beef into thin slices with a sharp knife. Place on a skillet with a piece of butter and stir over heat for a moment.
2. Dust with flour, then add enough good cream or milk to make a thickened gravy. Let it cook together and at the end stir in one beaten egg. Season with pepper and serve in a covered dish.
