## Ingredients

- pike
- vinegar
- water
- celery
- carrot
- onion
- parsley
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- pepper
- allspice
- egg with shell
- carp scales (if available)
- saffron (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the pike thoroughly and rinse in several waters
2. Cut into pieces and salt
3. Pour hot vinegar diluted with water over so the pike is submerged
4. Place underneath star-cut celery, carrot, 3 onions, parsley, thyme, bay leaf, a piece of lemon peel, several whole peppercorns and allspice
5. Let it cook together
6. Carefully place on a deep dish
7. Arrange several whole pieces of vegetables around it
8. Leave the rest on the baking dish and add a finely crushed whole egg with shell; if carp scales are available, cook for a quarter hour
9. If it should be golden-yellow, add saffron
10. Tie a cloth to the legs of an overturned chair, pour the liquid onto it, and when it has dripped through into the dish, pour it over the pike and place on ice
11. When serving, wrap the dish in a cloth soaked in hot water so it unmolds more easily
12. Serve with oil, vinegar and pepper on the side
