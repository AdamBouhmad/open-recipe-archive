## Ingredients

- chicken
- cauliflower
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare chicken as with mushrooms
2. Cook cauliflower in broth from chicken
3. Prepare sauce as with mushrooms
4. Carefully arrange cauliflower around chicken and pour sauce over it
5. May be sprinkled with chopped parsley
6. Chicken with asparagus is prepared the same way, asparagus having been cooked in salted water
