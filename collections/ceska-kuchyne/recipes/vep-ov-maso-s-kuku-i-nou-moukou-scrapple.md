## Ingredients

- pork meat (head, liver, heart, and other offal)
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- marjoram
- cornmeal
- water

## Directions

1. Any pork meat can be prepared this way, even leftovers from roasted meat in small quantities, but it is best made from pork head, liver, heart, and other offal that you do not wish to use otherwise.
2. Put all of this in a pot with salt, pepper, garlic, and marjoram to cook until tender, then remove the meat and strain the water and pour it back into the pot.
3. Slowly stir in enough cornmeal to make a nice porridge and let it cook for half an hour.
4. In the meantime, chop the meat finely and mix it in; there should be approximately equal quantities of both.
5. Pour while hot into molds and let cool.
6. When you wish to use it, cut it into slices and fry on both sides and serve warm.
