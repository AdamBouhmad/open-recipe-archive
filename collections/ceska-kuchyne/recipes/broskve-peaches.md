## Ingredients

- hard but ripe peaches
- 4 pounds of sugar for 8 pounds of fruit
- 2 ounces of whole cinnamon
- 1 quart of vinegar
- cloves

## Directions

1. For this purpose firm, although ripe peaches that adhere to the pit are best suited.
2. Wipe them well with cloth until clean.
3. For 8 pounds of fruit take 4 pounds of sugar, 2 ounces of whole cinnamon and 1 quart of vinegar and let boil for five minutes.
4. Insert two cloves into each peach and boil them in this liquid and when they are soft enough to be easily pierced with a fork, remove them, place into jars, boil the liquid down to half and pour over them, then boil further according to the general rules of preserving.
