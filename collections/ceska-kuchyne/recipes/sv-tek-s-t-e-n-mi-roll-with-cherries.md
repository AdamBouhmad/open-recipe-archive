## Ingredients

- ripe cherries
- rum
- quarter liter milk
- 10 tablespoons flour
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams sugar (4 lots)
- 8 egg whites
- salt

## Directions

1. Pit ripe cherries, pour rum over them and let stand a while
2. Prepare dough from quarter liter milk, 10 tablespoons flour, 8 egg yolks, sugar, snow from 8 egg whites and salt
3. Mix everything well and pour dough onto a greased pan
4. After a short time, add cherries to the soft dough, pour dough over them and bake the roll
