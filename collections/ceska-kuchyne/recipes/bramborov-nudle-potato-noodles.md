## Ingredients

- potatoes
- flour
- eggs
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook as many potatoes as will fit on a plate, peel them and while hot mash them on a board with a rolling pin
2. Salt them, add two large spoonfuls of flour and three whole eggs, knead into a dough, then roll out
3. Cut from this noodles about a finger's thickness
4. Grease a shallow pan or baking dish with clarified butter, arrange the noodles in it and bake on both sides until golden
5. These noodles can also be cooked in salted water, sprinkled with grated cheese, and greased with hot lard or butter
