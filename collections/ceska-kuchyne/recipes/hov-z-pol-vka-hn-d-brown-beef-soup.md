## Ingredients

- quarter kilo of beef or veal liver
- 70 grams (4 lots) of finely chopped beef fat
- quarter of a celery root, split
- 1 whole onion, chopped
- leek
- quarter of a carrot
- a piece of green parsley
- a little white flour
- beef broth
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the liver; add the chopped beef fat to it, quarter of a split celery root, whole onion chopped, leek, quarter of a carrot, a piece of green parsley and a little white flour
2. After stewing for about 5 minutes, strain into beef broth
3. Slice bread thinly, fry it in butter, place it in a soup bowl, and pour the soup over it
