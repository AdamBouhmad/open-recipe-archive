## Ingredients

- salmon
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- celery, sliced
- parsley
- carrot
- lemon peel
- pepper
- ginger
- allspice
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley, green

## Directions

1. Scale the salmon
2. Boil vinegar with water and pour over the salted salmon to turn it evenly blue
3. Place sliced celery, parsley, carrot, lemon peel, pepper, ginger, allspice, thyme, and bay leaf on a special baking sheet
4. Place the salmon on this bed, pour vinegar over it
5. Cook evenly on all sides
6. Transfer to a platter, surround with green parsley, the vegetables that were cooked with it, and lemon
7. Serve cold
