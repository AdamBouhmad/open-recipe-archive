## Ingredients

- 125 grams grated white bread
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ½ liter milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon crushed caraway
- egg white snow

## Directions

1. Take 125 grams grated white bread, 1 tablespoon flour, half a liter of milk, three egg yolks, mix and salt everything
2. Add 1 tablespoon crushed caraway and snow from egg whites
3. Fry the cakes made from all these ingredients on both sides in lard
4. Serve with smoked meat, ham, sausages or salad
