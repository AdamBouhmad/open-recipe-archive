## Ingredients

- white beans
- soft water
- onion
- wine vinegar
- salt and pepper
- anchovies or hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook beans like peas in soft water
2. Drain the water
3. Finely dice onion as needed and add to beans and mix
4. Pour in wine vinegar, pepper and add either anchovies or hard-boiled eggs
5. Usually served cold
