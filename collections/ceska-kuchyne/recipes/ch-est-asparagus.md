## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Water
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the asparagus as far as it is tender
2. Scrape the skin from the top downward
3. Tie several pieces together with string
4. Salt the water and when it begins to boil, place the asparagus in it and cook until tender
5. Carefully remove from the water and untie the string
6. Sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs and pour over melted hot butter
