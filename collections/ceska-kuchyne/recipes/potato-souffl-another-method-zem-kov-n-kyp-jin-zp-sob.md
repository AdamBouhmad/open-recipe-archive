## Ingredients

- baked peeled potatoes
- 240 grams (16 lots) base amount
- 60 grams (4 lots) sugar
- salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 30 grams (2 lots) blanched pounded almonds
- lemon peel, grated
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mash baked peeled potatoes
2. Add sugar, salt, egg yolks, almonds, and lemon peel
3. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Pour into form
5. Bake for half an hour
