## Ingredients

- Veal shoulder or breast
- Salt
- Caraway
- Water
- 30 grams butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the veal, salt it and season with caraway
2. Place on a baking sheet, add water, 30 grams of butter and braise quickly
3. When tender, place on a platter and pour the strained sauce over it
