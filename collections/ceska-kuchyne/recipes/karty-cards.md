## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 ounces fine sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 ounces flour
- a little ground cinnamon
- cloves
- flower
- a little finely grated lemon zest
- egg white
- split almonds
- fine sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Cream 2 tablespoons butter until frothy
2. Add 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks
3. Add 2 ounces fine sugar, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, 2 ounces flour, a little ground cinnamon, cloves, flower, and a little finely grated lemon zest
4. Mix everything well, work into dough on a board
5. Roll out to the thickness of a straw and cut into square cards
6. Brush with egg white, border with split almonds, sprinkle with fine sugar
7. Bake on a buttered sheet until pale yellow
8. Alternatively, you can bake them first, then coat with a glaze and then border with almonds
