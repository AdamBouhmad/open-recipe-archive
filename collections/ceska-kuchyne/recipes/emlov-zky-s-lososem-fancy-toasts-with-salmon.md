## Ingredients

- Oblong fried bread slices
- Anchovy butter
- Salmon slices
- Parsley
- Hard-boiled egg white
- Hard-boiled egg yolk

## Directions

1. Take oblong fried bread slices
2. Make a border of anchovy butter around the edge
3. Place several narrow thin salmon slices across
4. Garnish with chopped parsley, chopped egg white, and chopped egg yolk
