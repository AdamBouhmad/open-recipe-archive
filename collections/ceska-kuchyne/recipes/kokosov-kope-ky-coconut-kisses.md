## Ingredients

- one pound dried coconut
- one pound white sugar
- 3 egg whites (unbeaten)
- 10 tablespoons water
- flour
- egg white

## Directions

1. Mix dried coconut, white sugar, 3 unbeaten egg whites, and 10 tablespoons water thoroughly together
2. Cook in a hot pan in a pot with constant stirring for 15 minutes
3. Must stir constantly so it does not burn
4. Prepare a baking sheet, lightly brush with fat and sprinkle evenly with flour
5. Beat one egg white into stiff meringue
6. Quickly mix the cooked coconut with the meringue
7. Using two small spoons, form small heaps one next to the other, making two mounds from one full spoon
8. When the sheet is full, place in a not very hot oven
9. When they begin to turn yellow, open the oven and let them dry in the open oven for about 15 minutes
10. When removed from the oven, do not take from the sheet immediately, but wait until they cool, then they come away easily
