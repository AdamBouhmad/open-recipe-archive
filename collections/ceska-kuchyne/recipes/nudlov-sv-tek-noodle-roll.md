## Ingredients

- fine noodles
- half liter milk
- 7 eggs
- 6 dekagrams sugar (4 lots)
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar

## Directions

1. Make fine noodles, cook them, then place on a sieve to dry
2. Mix half liter milk with 7 eggs, sugar, lemon peel rubbed with sugar and noodles
3. Bake the roll from this mixture and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
