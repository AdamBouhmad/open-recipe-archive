## Ingredients

- two boiled cold potatoes
- wine vinegar
- water
- good beef broth
- butter or olive oil
- fish roe from herring

## Directions

1. Grate two boiled cold potatoes
2. Pour in wine vinegar
3. Add 1/8 liter water and the same amount of good beef broth
4. Mix well
5. Add two spoonfuls of melted fresh butter or two spoonfuls of olive oil
6. Remove the fish roe from herring of all membranes
7. Rub through
8. Add to sauce and mix well
9. Sauce is served with leftovers of roasted meat
