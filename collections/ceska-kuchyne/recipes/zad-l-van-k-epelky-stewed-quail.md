## Ingredients

- quail
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- water
- wine
- bay leaf
- onion

## Directions

1. Season quail with salt and pepper, lightly roast them in butter
2. Dust with a little flour, pour equal parts water and wine over them
3. Add one bay leaf, half an onion, salt and let braise, turning them several times
4. When the quail are tender, remove them to a plate and keep in a warm place
5. Let the sauce boil down well, then place the quail on a platter and strain the sauce over them
6. You can also prepare grouse and other game birds this way
