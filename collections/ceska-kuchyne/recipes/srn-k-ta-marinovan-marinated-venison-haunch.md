## Ingredients

- onion
- carrot
- parsley roots
- celery
- leek
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- cloves
- peppercorns
- vinegar
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Slice 2 onions, 2 carrots, several parsley roots, half celery, and leek
2. Add bay leaf, lemon peel, several cloves, and peppercorns and cook in a liter of vinegar
3. Let cool completely and pour over the prepared haunch
4. Place in a cool location
5. After 4-5 days of marinating, roast on butter on both sides
6. Pour liquid and sour cream over it and serve with strained sauce
7. Lamb and beef haunches can be prepared the same way
