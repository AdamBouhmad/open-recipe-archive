## Ingredients

- firm lettuce heads
- broth
- carrots
- rutabaga
- asparagus
- green beans
- lard slices
- sausages or minced meat

## Directions

1. Clean firm lettuce heads and stew in broth without breaking them apart
2. Stew carrots, rutabaga, asparagus and green beans, cut into equal pieces
3. In a metal mold, layer lard slices, then lettuce, with cut vegetables on the sides
4. On the bottom layer of lettuce place sausages or minced meat, then another layer of lettuce
5. Cover the mold, place in hot water, turn out after an hour and remove the lard
