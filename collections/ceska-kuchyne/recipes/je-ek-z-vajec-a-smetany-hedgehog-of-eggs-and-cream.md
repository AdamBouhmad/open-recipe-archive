## Ingredients

- Half liter milk or cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 12 decagrams ground almonds
- Vanilla
- Salt
- 5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook half a liter of milk or cream with 3 tablespoons sugar, 12 decagrams of ground almonds, vanilla, and salt for fifteen minutes.
2. Strain and mix in 5 whisked egg yolks.
3. Place the container in boiling water and stir continuously.
