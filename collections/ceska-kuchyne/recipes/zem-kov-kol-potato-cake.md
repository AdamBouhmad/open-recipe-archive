## Ingredients

- cold potatoes
- 2 2/3 liters grated potatoes
- 1 liter heated flour
- 315 grams (32 lots) butter
- 5 eggs
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) diluted yeast
- a little sour cream
- salt
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate cooled potatoes
2. Work dough the day before
3. Let rise overnight in kitchen
4. Next day roll out into 2-3 thin pieces
5. Place on floured lukewarm baking sheets
6. Brush well with butter
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
8. Bake at mild heat for quarter hour
