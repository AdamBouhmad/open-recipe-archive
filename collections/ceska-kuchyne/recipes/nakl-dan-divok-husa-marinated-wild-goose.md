## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- 1/2 lemon
- parsley
- thyme
- 4-5 cloves
- 8 peppercorns
- 3 juniper berries
- 1 liter good vinegar
- wild goose, interlarded with bacon

## Directions

1. Slice the onions, carrots, and lemon into pieces.
2. Boil them with parsley, thyme, cloves, peppercorns, and juniper berries in 1 liter of good vinegar.
3. Allow the mixture to cool completely.
4. Place the heavily bacon-interlarded goose in the cooled marinade.
5. Cover well and let it marinate for 3-4 days.
6. Roast the marinated goose in abundant butter for 3-4 hours, or braise it in butter with added onion, spices, lemon, vegetables, and abundant bacon.
7. Remove the fat from the sauce and take out the goose.
8. To the strained sauce, add lemon juice and sour cream.
9. Boil again, thicken with flour, add red wine, and pour over the goose when it is soft-cooked or braised.
