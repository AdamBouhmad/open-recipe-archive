## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, chopped and cleaned
- mushrooms
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Braise the chopped, cleaned chicken with vegetables or cook it.
2. Scrape the mushrooms, slice them thinly, and braise them salted in butter.
3. Strain the pale broth from the chicken, add the chicken to it, bring to a boil, and finally add the mushrooms braised in butter.
4. Other mushrooms may be used instead of the ones specified.
