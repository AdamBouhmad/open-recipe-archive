## Ingredients

- three-quarters kilo of ripe strawberries
- one quarter reau of water
- three-quarters liter of sugar syrup
- juice of two lemons
- champagne or burgundy wine

## Directions

1. Press three-quarters kilo of ripe strawberries through a fine sieve together with one quarter reau of water
2. Add three-quarters liter of sugar syrup to the obtained juice, add juice from two lemons, and strain everything again
3. Bring the juice to 18°, then place it in an ice cream maker to freeze
4. Before serving, pour champagne or burgundy wine into the frozen liquid, mix everything thoroughly, and serve in glasses
