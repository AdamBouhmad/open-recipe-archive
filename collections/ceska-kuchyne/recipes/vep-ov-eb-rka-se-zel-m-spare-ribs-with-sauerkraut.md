## Ingredients

- two pounds spare ribs
- sauerkraut
- onion
- flour
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of spare ribs and cut them so that there are always 2-3 ribs together.
2. Parboil and salt them and let them cook.
3. When they have cooked for half an hour, add a quart of pickled sauerkraut and half an onion chopped, cover and let cook until both meat and sauerkraut are tender.
4. Mix a tablespoon of flour in a little water and thicken the sauerkraut with it.
