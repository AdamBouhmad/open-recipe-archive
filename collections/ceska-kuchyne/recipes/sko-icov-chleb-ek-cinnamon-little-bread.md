## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 16-20 grams ground and sifted cinnamon
- 1/2 kilogram unpeeled, ground almonds
- 1/2 kilogram flour
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 kilogram of sugar with 8 egg yolks and beat
2. Add 16-20 grams of ground and sifted cinnamon, 1/2 kilogram of unpeeled ground almonds, and 1/2 kilogram of flour
3. Knead into dough, roll out, and form into thin sticks
4. Cut into pieces about ten centimeters long and about five centimeters wide
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Bake slowly on a baking sheet in a gently heated oven
