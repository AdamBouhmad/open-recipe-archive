## Ingredients

- large oysters
- heated butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread, buttered and slightly moistened with milk

## Directions

1. Let the oysters drain on a sieve
2. Dip them one by one into heated butter so they don't stick to the wire tongs for broiling
3. Broil them quickly over glowing coals
4. When nicely browned on both sides, pepper and salt them, arrange with butter
5. Place on hot buttered bread slightly moistened with milk
6. Serve very hot
