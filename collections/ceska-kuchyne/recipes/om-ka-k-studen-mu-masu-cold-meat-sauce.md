## Ingredients

- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- half a coffee cup of fine olive oil
- three cleaned sardines, finely chopped
- one spoon of finely chopped parsley
- two spoons of fine mustard
- one spoon of ground sugar
- a knife point of ground white pepper
- one coffee cup of wine vinegar
- a little water
- a knife point of salt

## Directions

1. Push four hard-boiled egg yolks through a wire sieve
2. Add to this half a coffee cup of fine olive oil and stir until a fine paste is formed
3. Add three cleaned, finely chopped sardines, one spoon of finely chopped parsley, two spoons of fine mustard, one spoon of ground sugar, a knife point of ground white pepper, one coffee cup of wine vinegar, a little water and a knife point of salt
4. Mix well
5. Serve with ham, smoked tongue, cold roast or fish
6. This sauce will keep in the cold for eight days or longer
