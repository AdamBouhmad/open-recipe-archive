## Ingredients

- roasted or boiled cold chicken
- white celery stalks
- mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Slice the meat from roasted or boiled cold chicken into small pieces, mix it with the same amount of white celery stalks cut into noodles and three tablespoons of mayonnaise
2. Arrange it in a pyramid on a platter lined with lettuce leaves, pour mayonnaise over it, garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters, capers or olives
3. Instead of celery you can also use other vegetables cut into noodles
