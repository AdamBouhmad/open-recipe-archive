## Ingredients

- pitted cherries
- 6 egg yolks
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar
- zest of lemon, rubbed with sugar
- juice of one lemon
- snow from 6 egg whites
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) potato flour
- breadcrumbs or rusks for dusting the pan
- fat for greasing the pan

## Directions

1. Place pitted cherries on a greased baking pan dusted with either grated breadcrumbs or rusks.
2. Beat 6 egg yolks with 12 decagrams (8 lots) sugar until combined.
3. Add the lemon zest that has been rubbed with sugar, lemon juice, snow from 6 egg whites, and 9 decagrams (6 lots) potato flour.
4. Spread everything over the cherries.
5. Bake the cake for one hour.
