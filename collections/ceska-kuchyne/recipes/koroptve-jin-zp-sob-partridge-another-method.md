## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- lard or bacon
- butter

## Directions

1. Wipe the partridges inside with a cloth without washing; salt them.
2. Lard or wrap with bacon.
3. Place in butter and fry first the breast, then the back, continuously basting with butter.
4. When fried, cook the juices with water or broth and add to the partridges.
