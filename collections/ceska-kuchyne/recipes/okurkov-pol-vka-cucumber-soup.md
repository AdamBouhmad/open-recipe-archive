## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- good beef broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- 2 egg yolks
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the cucumbers
2. Braise in butter
3. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons flour
4. Pour in good beef broth
5. Simmer for 2 hours
6. Strain
7. Season with salt and nutmeg
8. Add 2 egg yolks
9. Pour over fried roll
