## Ingredients

- several bitter almonds
- 1.5 litres hot milk or cream
- sugar
- 1/4 litre potato flour, mixed in water

## Directions

1. Parboil several bitter almonds, peel and grind them
2. Pour 1.5 litres of hot milk or cream over them and strain
3. Sweeten it, add 1/4 litre of potato flour mixed in water
4. Boil, let cool and serve with sugar
