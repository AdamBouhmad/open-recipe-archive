## Ingredients

- Red beets
- Horseradish
- Anise or fennel
- Salt
- Cloves (optional)
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and cook red beets until tender
2. Peel and cut into rounds
3. Grate horseradish and cut into rounds
4. Layer in a jar: beet round, several horseradish rounds, sprinkle with anise or fennel and salt
5. Continue layering until all ingredients are used
6. Pour good wine vinegar over
7. Let stand for twenty-four hours
8. Can substitute cloves for anise or fennel
9. Serve on a separate dish with a little of the liquid drizzled over
