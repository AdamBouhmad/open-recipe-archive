## Ingredients

- set aspic in mold
- warm water

## Directions

1. Turn the mold with set aspic onto a plate
2. Surround the mold with a cloth dipped in warm water
3. In a few moments slowly pull the mold upward and the aspic will remain on the plate in its original shape
4. If any liquid melts during unmolding, remove it with a straw and discard
5. Molds can be greased with almond oil before pouring aspic, but this gives the aspic an unpleasant taste
