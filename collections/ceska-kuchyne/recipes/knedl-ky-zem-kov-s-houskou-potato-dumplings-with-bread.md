## Ingredients

- cooked cold potatoes
- flour or semolina
- several eggs
- salt
- 2 or 3 bread rolls
- butter
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Grate the cold cooked potatoes
2. Mix with flour or semolina as desired
3. Add several eggs
4. Mix everything and knead into a stiff dough
5. Cut 2 or 3 bread rolls into cubes and fry in butter
6. Mix into the dough
7. Form dumplings of medium size
8. Boil in salted water
9. Cut in half, grease, and coat with grated bread crumbs
10. Must be eaten hot
