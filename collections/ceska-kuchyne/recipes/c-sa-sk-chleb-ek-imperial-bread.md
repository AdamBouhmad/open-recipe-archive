## Ingredients

- 30 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup sultanas
- 1/4 pound almonds, cut into noodles
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoon candied citron, chopped
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 30 egg yolks with 1/2 cup sugar for three-quarters of an hour
2. Add 1 cup flour, 1/2 cup sultanas, 1/4 pound almonds cut into noodles, 1/4 pound grated chocolate, and 1 teaspoon chopped candied citron
3. Mix everything well together
4. Slowly fold in stiff snow from 10 egg whites, mixing for a while
5. Pour into a buttered mold
6. Bake over moderate heat
7. When cooled, slice
