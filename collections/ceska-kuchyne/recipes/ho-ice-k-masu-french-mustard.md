## Ingredients

- onion
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- salt
- sugar
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. Slice onion into a large cup, pour vinegar over it to cover and let stand for two or three days.
2. Pour the vinegar into a bowl and add a teaspoon of ground pepper, a teaspoon of salt, a tablespoon of yellow sugar and mustard powder as needed to make a thick paste.
3. First mix the mustard powder with a little of the vinegar to avoid lumps.
4. Place on the stove and stir constantly, and when it begins to boil, remove from heat.
5. Serve cold.
