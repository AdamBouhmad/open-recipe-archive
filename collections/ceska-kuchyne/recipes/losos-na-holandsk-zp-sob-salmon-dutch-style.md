## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- butter
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- egg yolks, raw
- parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Wash the salmon and pour boiling water over it, leaving for half an hour
2. Scrape off scales, wash, and salt
3. Place on a baking sheet, add a piece of fresh butter and sour cream
4. Place in the oven and baste continuously with sour cream
5. When soft, transfer to a platter
6. Add juice from one lemon to the sauce and whisk in four raw egg yolks, then strain over the salmon
7. Sprinkle finely chopped fresh parsley on top
8. This method may also be used for catfish
