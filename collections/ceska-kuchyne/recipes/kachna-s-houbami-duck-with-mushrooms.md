## Ingredients

- One cleaned and gutted duck
- Rendered bacon or lard
- Onion, sliced
- Peppercorns
- Strong broth

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned and gutted duck in a roasting pan on rendered bacon or lard.
2. Add sliced onion and several peppercorns and let it braise a little.
3. Baste the duck with a few tablespoons of strong broth, cover the pan with a lid and continue braising.
