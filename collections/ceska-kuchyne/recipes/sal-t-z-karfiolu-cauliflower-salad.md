## Ingredients

- several nice white cauliflower florets
- salt
- fresh butter
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- four tablespoons of olive oil
- five tablespoons of wine vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean several nice white cauliflower florets and cook them in salted water with a piece of fresh butter until tender, being careful to keep the florets whole
2. When the cauliflower cools, place it on a clean cloth and let it drain
3. Either break it into small pieces or arrange the whole head neatly in a pyramid on a salad platter
4. Press four hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve, mix them well with four tablespoons of olive oil, add five tablespoons of wine vinegar, salt and a little pepper, mix well and pour over the prepared cauliflower
