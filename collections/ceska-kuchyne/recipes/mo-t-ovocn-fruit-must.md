## Ingredients

- lesser quality fruit, unripe or rotted fruit
- water

## Directions

1. Crush the fruit
2. Leave it in a container for several days
3. Strain and press the fruit
4. Dilute the juice with water
5. Pour into wine barrels
6. When fermentation is complete, transfer to other containers
7. Care for it like wine
8. The must gains a pleasant taste with various spice and wine additions
