## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls beef broth
- butter roux
- 1/2 celery root

## Directions

1. Brown 2 cupfuls of beef broth with roux, cook well and strain
2. Grate half a celery root and add the grated amount to the sauce
3. Let it cook a while
