## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1 celery root sliced into rounds
- 2 parsley roots
- handful of peas
- green parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Put into 2 quarts of water 1 celery root sliced into rounds, 2 parsley roots, a handful of peas, and green parsley
2. Salt it and let it simmer slowly for about 2 hours, carefully skimming the foam
3. Remove from heat and let stand quietly for about half an hour
4. Slowly strain through a thick cloth into an earthenware pot without clouding it
5. Set in a cool place
6. Where fish bouillon cannot be prepared, this stock works very well for preparing fasting soups and sauces
