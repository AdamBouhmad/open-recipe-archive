## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one small head of cabbage
- leaf celery stalks
- twelve eggs
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one tablespoon of mustard
- one small cup of wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop finely the meat from one chicken, also a small head of cabbage and the same amount of leaf celery stalks, each separately
2. Cook twelve eggs hard, chop the whites finely and rub the yolks with two tablespoons of butter, two tablespoons of sugar and one tablespoon of mustard
3. Add to this one small cup of wine vinegar, mix well, then mix all the chopped ingredients and pour the sauce over it
