## Ingredients

- piece of beef ham or various forcemeat
- bacon, chopped
- onion or chives, finely chopped
- mushrooms, stewed and finely chopped
- pepper
- two whole eggs
- beef broth
- veal leg
- salt
- thread
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Finely chop a piece of beef ham or various forcemeat, add chopped bacon, finely chopped onion or chives
2. Let this mixture fry on a pan for a while; you may also add stewed and finely chopped mushrooms
3. Season with pepper, add two whole eggs, mix well and thin with beef broth as needed
4. Cut thin slices from veal leg, flatten with the back of a knife, season with pepper and salt
5. Spread the forcemeat on the slices, roll them up and tie with clean thread
6. Place in a pan, add finely chopped bacon and onion, cover with a lid and let stew slowly for about an hour, adding beef broth by spoonfuls
7. Make a gravy from the liquid and leftover forcemeat
8. Remove the thread from the rolls, arrange on a platter, strain the gravy over them and garnish with lemon slices
9. Wine may also be added to the gravy if desired
