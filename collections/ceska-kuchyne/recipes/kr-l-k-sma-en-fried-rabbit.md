## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- flour
- egg
- grated bread roll

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces, wash, and dry.
2. Coat in flour, dip in egg, and roll in grated bread roll.
3. Fry until golden brown.
4. Serve alone as a tasty dish or add to spinach or potato mash.
