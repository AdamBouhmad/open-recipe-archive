## Ingredients

- fine dough
- layer of cooked raspberries or ground rusk with fresh raspberries
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) finely ground almonds
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- snow from 4 egg whites
- 15 grams (1 lot) flour

## Directions

1. Spread a finger's thickness of fine dough on the bottom of a form
2. Coat with a layer of cooked raspberries or sprinkle with ground rusk and fresh raspberries
3. Mix 18 dekagrams finely ground almonds, 18 dekagrams sugar, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, snow from 4 egg whites, and 15 grams flour
4. Pour the mixture over the raspberries
5. Bake at low heat for three-quarters of an hour
