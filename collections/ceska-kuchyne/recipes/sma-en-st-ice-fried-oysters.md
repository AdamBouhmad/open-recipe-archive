## Ingredients

- two dozen oysters, as large as possible
- salt
- pepper
- two whole eggs and one yolk
- flour
- finely crushed crackers or cornmeal
- half lard and half butter

## Directions

1. Dry the oysters with a cloth
2. Salt and pepper them
3. Beat two whole eggs and one yolk
4. Coat the oysters in flour, then dip them in eggs and coat them in finely crushed crackers or cornmeal
5. Put half lard and half butter in a pan and when it is hot, quickly fry the oysters in it
