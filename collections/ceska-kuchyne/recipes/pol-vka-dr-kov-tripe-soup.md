## Ingredients

- about 3 kilos tripe
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- 4 cloves garlic
- parsley
- butter (for roux)
- beef soup (for second version)

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe thoroughly
2. Boil for an hour, rinse again after the water is drained, and cook until completely soft
3. Strain the soup and add marjoram, 4 cloves of garlic, pepper, salt and a handful of finely chopped parsley
4. Add a butter roux to it
5. Strain everything, cut the tripe into strips, boil for a bit longer and season
6. Alternative: drain the second water and use good beef soup instead
