## Ingredients

- tender young spinach leaves
- salt
- water
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Select tender young spinach leaves, rinse several times, cook in salted water, place on a strainer to cool so the water drains, squeeze out, chop
2. On a small pan, stew for a few minutes with salt, butter and pepper
3. Serve on a dish, decorated as desired with other dishes
