## Ingredients

- sorted cherries
- good vinegar
- sugar (1/2 kilogram)
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Pour good vinegar over the sorted cherries so all are submerged
2. After two days, pour off the vinegar and boil it with 1/2 kilogram sugar, cinnamon, and several cloves
3. Strain it hot over the cherries
4. After three days, boil again, pour over the cherries
5. When it cools, tie the containers
