## Ingredients

- Ripe cherries with pits
- Currant juice, one-fourth part

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from ripe cherries, crush them with the pits, let them stand overnight.
2. Then press them, add one-fourth part currant juice, strain once more.
3. Then cook as with grape jelly.
