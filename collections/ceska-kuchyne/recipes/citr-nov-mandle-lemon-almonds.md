## Ingredients

- blanched almonds
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and dry blanched almonds
2. Sprinkle with sugar and pour melted sugar as described for white sugared almonds
3. On hot stove in a container, continuously stir
4. Finally, add lemon juice to the boiled sugar and proceed as with other kinds of sugared almonds
