## Ingredients

- One whole beef tongue
- Sliced bacon
- A few pieces of veal
- Two carrots
- Four onions
- Thyme
- Clove
- Beef broth
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the whole beef tongue for about half an hour, or a whole hour if it is large
2. Let it cool, peel off the skin, and trim it neatly
3. Line the bottom of a pot with sliced bacon and a few pieces of veal
4. Add two carrots and four onions, both sliced into rounds, a little thyme and clove
5. Place the prepared tongue on top
6. Pour over enough beef broth until it is completely covered
7. Braise it until the tongue is completely tender, which takes three or four hours
8. From time to time add a little beef broth and squeeze in the juice of one lemon
9. When the tongue is properly tender, remove it from the pot and arrange on a platter
10. Slice it lengthwise from the back end to the front, or to the tip, but so that it remains slightly connected at the front end, and when laid out on the platter, it resembles a heart
11. Set it in a warm place
12. Strain the gravy through a sieve, pour a little over the tongue and serve the rest in a small bowl
