## Ingredients

- 1 liter blueberries
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- a little cinnamon
- lemon peel
- potato flour
- wine

## Directions

1. Sift and wash the blueberries
2. Mash them in an enameled dish with a pestle
3. Add sugar, a little cinnamon, and lemon peel
4. Strain everything
5. Add potato flour mixed in wine
6. Boil once more
