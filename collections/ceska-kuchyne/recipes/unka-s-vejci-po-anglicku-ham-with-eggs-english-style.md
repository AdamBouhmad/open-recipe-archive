## Ingredients

- Ham
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Instead of bacon, one can also use ham cut into fine slices or cubes; everything else is arranged as in the previous recipe, or eggs are prepared scrambled in butter, and these are then mixed with the ham.
