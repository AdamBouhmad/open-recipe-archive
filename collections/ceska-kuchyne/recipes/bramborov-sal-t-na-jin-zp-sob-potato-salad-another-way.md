## Ingredients

- boiled peeled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- onion, finely chopped
- parsley, chopped
- oil or rendered butter
- vinegar
- sour apples, peeled

## Directions

1. Let boiled peeled potatoes cool, slice them thinly, put on a dish, salt and pepper
2. Sprinkle with finely chopped onion and a bit of chopped parsley if desired
3. Pour oil or rendered butter and vinegar over them and mix gently so the potatoes do not break
4. You may also add peeled sour apples cut into slices
