## Ingredients

- blanched and peeled almonds
- hard-boiled eggs
- finely ground sugar
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- lemon zest
- citron shavings cut into fine pieces

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel almonds and grate them with several hard-boiled eggs
2. Beat the sugar finely and mix with egg yolks from several eggs, keeping the whites separate
3. Beat the sugar and yolks together thoroughly
4. Add some citron zest shavings and finely cut almond pieces
5. Beat everything together for at least half an hour
6. If the paste seems thick, beat in a few more eggs
7. Make snow from the egg whites and mix into the cake dough
8. Pour into a baking form greased with butter and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs
9. Bake slowly and very carefully, as the cake will fall with even a small shock
