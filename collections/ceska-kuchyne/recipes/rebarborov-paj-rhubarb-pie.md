## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- sugar
- flour
- pie crust dough

## Directions

1. Rinse and peel the rhubarb stalks
2. Cut into pieces one inch long
3. Sweeten generously with sugar
4. Fill a pie pan lined with dough with the rhubarb
5. Dust with flour
6. Cover with top dough
7. Make several slits in it
8. Bake the pie in the oven
9. Before putting rhubarb in the pie, you must first dust the bottom crust lightly with flour
