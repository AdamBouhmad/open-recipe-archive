## Ingredients

- partridges
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) raw veal
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) fat smoked meat
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- parsley
- bay leaf
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- broth
- lemon
- vegetable bundle
- sardine butter
- lemon juice
- white wine

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges in the ordinary way.
2. Cut the raw veal and fat smoked meat into cubes.
3. Add onion, carrot, celery, and parsley cut into pieces, bay leaf, and several peppercorns.
4. Braise everything in butter.
5. Dust with 2 spoonfuls of flour and brown.
6. Pour in broth, cook with lemon and a bundle of vegetables.
7. Strain the sauce, mix with the juice from the partridges, add sardine butter, lemon juice, and white wine.
8. Add the roasted partridges to this sauce.
