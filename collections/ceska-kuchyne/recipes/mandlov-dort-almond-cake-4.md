## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound sweet almonds
- one ounce bitter almonds
- three egg yolks
- three-quarters pound powdered sugar
- ten egg yolks
- a little flour
- one dried roll (žemlička)
- ten egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Scald and peel three-quarters pound sweet almonds and one ounce bitter almonds
2. Pound them in a mortar with three egg yolks
3. Place the pounded mixture in a deep bowl
4. Add three-quarters pound powdered sugar, ten egg yolks, and a little flour
5. Mix for one hour
6. Pound one dried roll in a mortar and add it to the mixture
7. Finally add stiff snow made from ten egg whites
8. Fill a form buttered and dusted with grated roll
9. Bake in an oven for one hour
10. Test the oven temperature by placing a piece of paper in it—if it browns, the oven is hot enough for baking the cake
11. When cooled, spread with white glaze applied thickly
12. Let it dry in lukewarm water
