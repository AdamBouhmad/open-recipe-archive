## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- water
- 1-2 liters raspberries

## Directions

1. Place 12 dekagrams sugar in water and boil until it thickens
2. Add 1-2 liters of raspberries
3. After a short time remove them and pour over with the boiled syrup
