## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 cupful of peanuts, finely cut
- ½ cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Take 1 cupful of butter, 1 cupful of sugar and 1 cupful of peanuts finely cut (they may also be crushed with a roller if you don't want to chop them), ½ cupful of milk, 2 eggs and 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
2. Mix in only as much flour as is needed so the cookies can be rolled
