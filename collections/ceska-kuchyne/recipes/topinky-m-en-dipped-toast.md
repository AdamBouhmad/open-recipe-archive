## Ingredients

- bread
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon or orange or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Slice bread into slices, which I lightly dip on one side in milk, then place one next to the other on a baking sheet.
2. Mix sugar with cinnamon or grated orange or lemon peel and sprinkle the bread with this.
3. In a moderately heated oven, dry it and you have good rusks for coffee.
