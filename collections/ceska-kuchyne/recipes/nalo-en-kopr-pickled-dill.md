## Ingredients

- young dill leaves
- wine vinegar
- peppercorns
- cloves

## Directions

1. Place young washed dill leaves into a clean pot.
2. Add enough wine vinegar to completely cover them.
3. Add several peppercorns and cloves.
4. Let it boil for several minutes.
5. Remove from heat and let it cool.
6. Pour into jars, seal well, and store in a dry, cool place.
7. The dill must always be completely covered with vinegar; if it dries out, add some boiled vinegar.
