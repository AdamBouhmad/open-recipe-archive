## Ingredients

- quart of cranberries
- water
- pinch of baking soda
- sugar (two cups)

## Directions

1. Sort through a quart of cranberries and pick them clean.
2. Put them in a granitware bowl, pour cold water over them until covered, and let them cook.
3. Add a pinch of baking soda and let cook until they burst.
4. Then add two cups of sugar and let cook for an hour.
