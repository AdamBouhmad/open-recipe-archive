## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- lemon rind
- a pinch of ground cloves
- 3 spoonfuls of milk
- flour as needed
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Boil 6 eggs hard for about 20 minutes and cool in cold water
2. Rub 1 cupful of butter with 1 cupful of sugar, 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
3. Add lemon rind and a pinch of ground cloves
4. Remove only the yolks from the cooled eggs and crush them with the rest
5. Add 3 spoonfuls of milk and enough flour so the dough rolls well
6. Roll out to the thickness of a straw and cut out circles
7. Brush with egg yolk, sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and bake in a moderate oven
