## Ingredients

- two dozen large snails
- salted water
- three ounces butter
- three tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- one tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- flour
- about four tablespoons sour cream
- two quarts lean broth or parsley water
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Let two dozen large snails boil for about half an hour in salted water, then remove them from their shells with a picker, break them in half, discard the tails and chop the front parts finely
2. Place three ounces of butter in a pan, add three tablespoons of chopped mushrooms and one tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley, add the chopped snails and let them fry for several minutes
3. Brown with a little flour, add about four tablespoons of sour cream, mix well, add two quarts of lean broth or parsley water and let it boil for about half an hour, then pour into a dish over fried bread
