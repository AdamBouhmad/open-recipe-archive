## Ingredients

- 90 grams hot butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 onion
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- finely chopped crayfish meat
- fish milt (soft roe)
- egg
- breadcrumbs, grated
- green parsley

## Directions

1. In hot butter, brown 2 tablespoons flour and 1 onion
2. Add 1/4 liter sour cream and cook the sauce until it thickens completely
3. Strain the sauce
4. Add finely chopped crayfish meat and fish milt to it
5. Place on ice and when completely thickened, turn out onto a rolling surface
6. Roll into small cones or cylinders
7. Dip in egg
8. Coat in grated breadcrumbs and fry
9. Garnish with green parsley and serve after soup at table
