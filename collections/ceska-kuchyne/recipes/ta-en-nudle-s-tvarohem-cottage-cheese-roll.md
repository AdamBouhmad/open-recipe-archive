## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 pound cottage cheese
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- sugar
- vanilla
- raisins
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Make dough as for apple strudel
2. Rub together 4 tablespoons butter with 1 pound cottage cheese
3. Add to it four egg yolks and one whole egg and enough sugar with vanilla so that the cottage cheese is sufficiently sweet
4. Mix everything well
5. Roll out the dough for strudel
6. Spread it with beaten egg and coat with the prepared filling
7. Sprinkle with raisins and finely chopped almonds
8. Roll up the strudel
9. Place in a pan greased with butter
10. Coat the surface with butter and let it bake
