## Ingredients

- Cleaned bluefish
- Vinegar
- Salted water
- Spices
- Vegetables
- Onion
- Bay leaf
- New spices
- Oil

## Directions

1. Clean the bluefish and remove guts.
2. Pour vinegar over them and cover with paper.
3. Let sit for half an hour.
4. In a large baking sheet, put spices, vegetables, onion, bay leaf and new spices in salted water.
5. Add the bluefish with vinegar.
6. Boil quickly and serve with vinegar and oil.
