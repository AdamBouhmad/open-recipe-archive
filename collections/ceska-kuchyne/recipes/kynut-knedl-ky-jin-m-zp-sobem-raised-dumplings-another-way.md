## Ingredients

- ¾ pound flour
- ½ pound cottage cheese
- 3 eggs
- yeast
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Take ¾ pound flour, ½ pound cottage cheese, 3 eggs, a piece of yeast, milk as needed and salt, work everything together and let it rise
2. Make small round dumplings from it, let them rise again in the cold
3. Fry them in melted butter
4. Eat them hot immediately
5. Each person can sprinkle them with what they like
