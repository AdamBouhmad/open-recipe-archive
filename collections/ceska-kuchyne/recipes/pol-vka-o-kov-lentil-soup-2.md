## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Marjoram
- Garlic
- 2 liters water
- Roux (jíška)
- Fried bread or bread

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils until completely soft
2. Add marjoram and garlic to them
3. Pour in 2 liters of water and add roux
4. Cook for a quarter hour
5. Strain the soup and pour over fried bread or bread
