## Ingredients

- 1 liter strawberries
- wine
- sugar
- lemon peel, grated
- lemon juice
- a little white flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Press the strawberries through a sieve
2. Dilute with wine, sugar, grated lemon peel, and lemon juice
3. Boil a little white flour with cream
4. Pour this into the sauce
