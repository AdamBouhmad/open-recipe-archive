## Ingredients

- 12-15 crayfish
- 125 grams crayfish butter
- 1 teaspoon fine flour
- 1/2 liter cream or warm milk
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish
2. From the shells prepare crayfish butter
3. Mix 125 grams of this butter with 1 teaspoon fine flour
4. Boil with 1/2 liter cream or warm milk
5. Season with salt and sugar
6. Beat in 2 egg yolks
