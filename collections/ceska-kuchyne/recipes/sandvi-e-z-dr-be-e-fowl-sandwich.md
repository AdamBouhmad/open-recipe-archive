## Ingredients

- roasted chicken, roasted squab, or roasted turkey
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove skin and fat from roasted poultry
2. Slice meat very thinly
3. Sprinkle with oil and vinegar
4. Prepare as other meat sandwiches
