## Ingredients

- butter
- onions, sprinkled with flour
- broth
- vinegar
- sugar
- white wine

## Directions

1. In butter, partially braise the onions sprinkled with flour
2. Add broth, vinegar, sugar and a little white wine
3. Mix everything carefully and simmer
4. When fully cooked, strain through a sieve
