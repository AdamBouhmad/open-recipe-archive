## Ingredients

- Fresh cabbage
- Caraway
- Salt
- Vinegar mixed with water
- Onion
- Bacon
- Pork or goose fat
- Flour
- Beef broth or water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Chop fresh cabbage very finely, place in a pot and add caraway, salt and vinegar mixed with water
2. When softened, set aside
3. Chop onion finely, add chopped bacon and pork or goose fat, and braise so it does not brown
4. Add flour according to the amount of cabbage, fry until it turns red, then add the cabbage
5. Pour in beef broth or water and simmer briefly
6. If darker cabbage is desired, burn a piece of sugar
