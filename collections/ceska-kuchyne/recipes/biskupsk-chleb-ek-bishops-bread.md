## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup whole unpeeled almonds
- 1 cup sultana raisins
- butter
- flour for dusting the pan

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs with 1 cup sugar using a wire whisk for half an hour until very thick
2. Mix in 1 cup flour into which you have mixed 1 teaspoon baking powder
3. Add 1 cup whole unpeeled almonds
4. Finally add 1 cup sultana raisins coated in flour
5. Mix everything well
6. Butter an oblong mold and dust with flour
7. Pour batter into the mold and bake slowly for at least half an hour
8. Let cool completely
9. When completely cold, slice into pieces
