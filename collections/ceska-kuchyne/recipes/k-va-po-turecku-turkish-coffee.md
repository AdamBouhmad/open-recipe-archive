## Ingredients

- black coffee, finely ground
- coffee beans
- sugar
- orange extract

## Directions

1. Place as much regular black coffee as you need in a pot with a handle, for each cup add a teaspoon of finely ground coffee sifted through a fine strainer and a piece of sugar, boil it for two minutes and pour boiling and unfiltered into cups
2. Serve pounded sugar so each person can sweeten to taste
3. You can also add a few drops of orange extract to it
