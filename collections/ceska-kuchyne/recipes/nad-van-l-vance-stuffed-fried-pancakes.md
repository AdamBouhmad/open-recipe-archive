## Ingredients

- leftover roast meat
- beef marrow
- onion
- grated roll
- salt
- butter
- broth
- egg

## Directions

1. Make a filling by chopping together leftover roast, beef marrow, onion, and grated roll with salt, cooking briefly with butter and broth over heat.
2. Allow filling to cool.
3. Spread cooled filling on prepared lívance, roll them up, cut into pieces, coat with egg, roll in grated roll, and fry in butter.
4. Serve with vegetables.
