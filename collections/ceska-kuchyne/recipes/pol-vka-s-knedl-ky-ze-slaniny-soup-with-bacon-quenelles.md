## Ingredients

- half a pound of nice fatty smoked meat
- one grated roll
- sweet cream
- two egg yolks
- two whole eggs
- salt
- chives
- a full spoon of nice flour
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Cook half a pound of nice fatty smoked meat, let it cool and then cut it into cubes
2. Cut one grated roll into cubes
3. Soak half of the roll cubes in sweet cream and fry the other half until golden
4. Let half of the meat cubes melt, then cool, crush them, add two egg yolks and two whole eggs
5. Add the roll cubes soaked in cream to the mixture, salt it lightly, add some chopped chives, then add a full spoon of nice flour and also the fried roll cubes
6. Test if the mixture is thick enough by putting a piece of dough from a spoon into the broth - if it is too thin, add more flour and cut-up smoked meat
7. Mix everything well and drop quenelles into the broth using a small spoon
