## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Eggs
- Bread crumbs or crushed crackers
- Lard
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the carp, remove the entrails, scale it and cut into pieces
2. Quickly rinse in cold water and dry with cloth
3. Salt and sprinkle with ground pepper
4. Cover with cloth and let sit for half an hour
5. Coat in flour, dip in beaten eggs, coat in bread crumbs or crushed crackers
6. Fry in hot fat made of half lard and half butter until golden
