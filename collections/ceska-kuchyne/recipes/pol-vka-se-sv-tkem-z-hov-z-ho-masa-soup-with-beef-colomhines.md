## Ingredients

- cooked beef, preferably leftover
- salt
- a piece of onion
- parsley
- a piece of lard
- a piece of butter
- one egg
- some grated bread roll soaked in milk or white bread

## Directions

1. Chop cooked beef finely, add salt, a piece of onion and parsley and chop them finely as well.
2. Put a piece of lard on a pan, place all the chopped meat in it and let it stew.
3. Meanwhile, take a piece of butter and cream it well, add one whole egg, some grated bread roll soaked in milk or white bread, add the stewed cooled meat, salt and mix everything well.
4. Butter a small pot, put the chopped mixture in it and bake it.
5. Then cut as desired, place in a bowl and pour good beef broth over it.
