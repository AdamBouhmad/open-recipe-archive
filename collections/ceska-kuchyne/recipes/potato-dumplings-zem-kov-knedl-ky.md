## Ingredients

- cooled potatoes, grated
- several rolls, cut into cubes and fried
- rolls soaked in milk
- onion
- parsley
- chives
- butter
- bacon
- flour
- several eggs
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix grated cold potatoes with fried roll cubes
2. Soak part of the rolls in milk
3. Braise the milk-soaked rolls with onion, parsley, and chives in butter with bacon
4. Mix potatoes with flour, eggs, nutmeg, and salt
5. Add the braised mixture to the potatoes
6. Form dumplings
7. Cook in salted water
