## Ingredients

- five to six nice not too ripe bunches of blue or green grapes
- 200 grams sugar
- one quarter to one half liter water

## Directions

1. Remove the berries from five to six nice not too ripe bunches of blue or green grapes
2. Dissolve 200 grams sugar in one quarter to one half liter water and boil with it but the berries must not burst
3. Remove the berries carefully with a spoon
4. Continue boiling the sugar syrup until it thickens completely
5. Pour over the grape berries and let cool together with them
