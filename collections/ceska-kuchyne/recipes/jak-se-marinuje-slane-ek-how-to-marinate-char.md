## Ingredients

- milt char
- wine vinegar
- onion
- capers
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Peel the milt char, remove the milt and mash in 2 or 3 tablespoons of wine vinegar
2. Slice onion into thin rounds, add a few capers, peppers, and spices
3. Place the char in this mixture for about an hour
