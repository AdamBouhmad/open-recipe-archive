## Ingredients

- Veal feet
- Flour
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Split the feet and remove the larger bones, then clean and wash them, place in a pot, salt and boil until softened, place in cold water, dry, and remove remaining bones; salt, coat in flour, egg and bread crumbs and fry.
