## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound shelled hazel-nut kernels
- half pound sifted sugar
- eight egg yolks
- four egg whites
- two tablespoons flour
- butter
- grated roll
- lemon or orange glaze
- preserved nuts or fruit for decoration

## Directions

1. Grate one-quarter pound of cleaned hazel-nut kernels
2. Place them on a dish with half pound sifted sugar
3. Add eight egg yolks and mix for at least half an hour until it froths and thickens
4. Gradually add stiff snow made from four egg whites
5. Add two tablespoons flour
6. Butter a form and dust it with grated and sifted roll
7. Pour the batter into the form and let the cake bake slowly
8. When cooled, turn it out and let it cool completely
9. Cover the cake with lemon or orange glaze
10. Let it dry in a lukewarm oven
11. Decorate with preserved nuts or fruit
12. The same method can be used for walnut cake
