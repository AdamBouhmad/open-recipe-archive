## Ingredients

- 12 liters of beautiful strawberries
- 12 liters of cold water
- 4 kilograms of sugar
- 8 liters of apple must
- thin lemon peel
- 3 decagrams of ground wine stone
- 2 liters of lukewarm water with 1.5 kilograms of dissolved sugar
- 1 liter of strong liquor

## Directions

1. Crush 12 liters of strawberries
2. Pour 12 liters of cold water over them
3. Let stand for 24 hours
4. Strain and add 4 kilograms of sugar, 8 liters of apple must, thin lemon peel, and 3 decagrams of ground wine stone
5. Pour everything into a barrel, filling it over halfway
6. Close with a cork with a small hole and let stand for 4 weeks in a lukewarm place so the wine ferments completely
7. After this time, add 2 liters of lukewarm water in which 1.5 kilograms of sugar has been dissolved and close the barrel again
8. After about 8 weeks, pour off the clear wine, add 1 liter of strong liquor, and pour back into a cleaned barrel
9. Let lie in the cellar for two months, then pour into a smaller barrel so it is full
10. After 3-4 years, draw into bottles
