## Ingredients

- 1.5 quarts good milk
- 1 spoon sugar
- Salt
- 3 egg yolks
- Cold milk

## Directions

1. Heat 1.5 quarts good milk
2. Add 1 spoon sugar and a little salt
3. In a separate bowl, beat 3 egg yolks with a little cold milk
