## Ingredients

- roux (zápražka)
- 0.5 liter sour cream
- pepper
- salt
- good broth (several tablespoons)
- 2 egg yolks
- hard boiled eggs (cut into cubes)
- cooked veal sweetbread
- minced garlic
- several tablespoons aspic
- butter for frying
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the roux with 0.5 liter of sour cream
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Add several tablespoons of good broth
4. Add 2 egg yolks and strain
5. Mix in cubed hard boiled eggs, cooked veal sweetbread, minced garlic, and several tablespoons of aspic
6. Place on ice to cool
7. When cooled, cut into elongated pieces
8. Shape into small croquettes
9. Fry in butter
10. Garnish with fried parsley
