## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- Four spoonfuls of cold water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley or chives
- Butter

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs with a fork on a deep plate
2. Add four spoonfuls of cold water (one spoonful per egg)
3. Salt and pepper to taste
4. Add finely chopped fresh parsley or chives if desired
5. Put a small teaspoon of butter in a saucepan and let it melt
6. When hot but not browning, pour the beaten eggs into the saucepan
7. Cook carefully, lifting the edge with a knife from time to time so the liquid reaches the bottom and the omelette cooks through
8. When sufficiently set, fold and turn onto a heated plate
