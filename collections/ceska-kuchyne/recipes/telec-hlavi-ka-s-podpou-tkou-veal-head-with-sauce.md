## Ingredients

- Veal head
- Salt
- Water
- Wine
- Vinegar
- White sugar
- Burnt sugar
- Pepper
- Onion
- Roux
- 30 grams butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the veal head and cook in salted water until tender
2. Carefully cut into equal pieces
3. Make sauce from wine, vinegar, white sugar, burnt sugar, pepper and onion
4. Cook everything in the broth from the head, add roux and 30 grams of butter
5. Cook and strain the sauce over the head
