## Ingredients

- 195 grams (13 lots) sugar
- zest from 2 lemons
- 195 grams butter
- 12 egg yolks
- juice from 2 lemons
- 150 grams (10 lots) potato flour
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 2 lemons with 195 grams (13 lots) sugar; crush the sugar
2. Beat 195 grams butter until it becomes fluffy
3. Add 12 egg yolks and juice from 2 lemons
4. Add 150 grams (10 lots) potato flour
5. Put the mixture in a buttered mold
6. Serve with wine sauce
