## Ingredients

- half kilogram peeled ground almonds
- 4 eggs
- half kilogram sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar

## Directions

1. Mix half kilogram peeled ground almonds with 4 eggs, half kilogram sugar, 16 egg yolks, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
2. Beat for half an hour
3. Slowly add snow from egg whites
4. Pour the mixture into a greased form
5. Bake at low heat for a quarter hour
