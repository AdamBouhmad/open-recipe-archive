## Ingredients

- four tablespoons of butter
- seven egg yolks
- two tablespoons of sugar
- a little pounded flower
- a little salt
- two cupfuls of good lukewarm raw cream
- white flour
- a piece of pressed yeast
- almonds
- sour cherries preserved in sugar

## Directions

1. Beat four tablespoons of butter until foamy, add seven egg yolks one by one, two tablespoons of sugar, a little pounded flower and salt, two cupfuls of good lukewarm raw cream, mix well.
2. Then add as much fine white flour as needed to make a nice stiff dough, add a piece of pressed yeast and beat so long until the dough no longer sticks to the bowl or the beater.
3. Grease a mold with butter, line it with almonds cut into thin strips, sprinkle with flour and put into it the larger half of the dough, then put a layer of sour cherries preserved in sugar, place the second half of the dough on top and let it rise in a lukewarm place.
