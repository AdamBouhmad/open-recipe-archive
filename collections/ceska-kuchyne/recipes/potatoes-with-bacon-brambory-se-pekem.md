## Ingredients

- fresh bacon
- one small onion
- cooked peeled potatoes
- salt
- white ground pepper

## Directions

1. Dice the fresh bacon and one small onion finely and fry them
2. Add cooked, peeled potatoes cut into slices
3. Salt and season with a little white ground pepper
4. Fry the potatoes until golden brown
