## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- eggs
- water
- smoked meat
- milk
- butter
- sugar or pepper

## Directions

1. Salt the flour, add eggs and make dough like for noodles
2. When the rolled-out dough has dried, cut it into square pieces
3. When salted water is boiling, cook the cut pieces in it, then rinse with cold water so they do not stick together
4. Alternatively, the cut dough pieces can be cooked in the broth in which smoked meat was boiled
5. Finely chop the smoked meat and mix with the dough, add 4 eggs mixed with milk and butter, and either a piece of sugar or pepper, salt it, crumble 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter into it, mix well, spread on a greased and breadcrumb-sprinkled baking sheet, on which it is placed on the table or turned out
