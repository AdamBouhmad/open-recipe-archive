## Ingredients

- lamb neck, shoulder and breast
- salt
- ground pepper
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- clarified butter
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb neck, shoulder and breast into equally large neat pieces
2. Salt them and sprinkle lightly with ground pepper
3. Sprinkle lightly with flour
4. Dip in beaten egg
5. Roll in bread crumbs
6. Fry in clarified butter until golden
7. Sprinkle lightly with ground salt
8. Arrange on a platter and surround with parsley sprigs
