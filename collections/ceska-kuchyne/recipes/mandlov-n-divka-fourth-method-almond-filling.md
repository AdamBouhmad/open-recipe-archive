## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 28 dek sugar
- 28 dek finely chopped almonds
- 3 dek grated bread moistened with wine

## Directions

1. Take 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks
2. Mix with sugar and finely chopped almonds
3. Add grated bread moistened with wine
4. Mix into a pleasant filling
