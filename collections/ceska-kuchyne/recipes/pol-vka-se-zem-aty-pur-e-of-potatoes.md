## Ingredients

- several cooked potatoes
- beef broth
- pepper
- grated garlic
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Peel and grate several cooked potatoes, put them in strained beef broth, season with pepper, add a little grated garlic and marjoram and let simmer a while
