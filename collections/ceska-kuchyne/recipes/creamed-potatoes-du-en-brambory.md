## Ingredients

- butter
- small onion
- flour
- milk
- cooked potatoes
- caraway
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place a heaping spoon of butter in a skillet and dice a small onion very finely into it
2. Add half a spoon of flour and let it just barely brown, not to dark color
3. Pour in a cup of milk and when it begins to rise, add a full plate of cooked potatoes cut into slices
4. Add a little caraway, salt and pepper, cover and move to the back of the stove
5. When all the milk is absorbed by the potatoes, they are ready
