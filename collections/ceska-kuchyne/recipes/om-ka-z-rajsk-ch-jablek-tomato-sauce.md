## Ingredients

- quarter liter of beef broth
- three fresh or pickled tomatoes or tomato preserve
- two finely chopped onions
- eight peppercorns
- four grains of allspice
- two cloves
- roux
- sugar
- wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Place three fresh, pickled, or preserved tomatoes in a quarter liter of beef broth
2. Add two finely chopped onions
3. Add eight peppercorns, four grains of allspice, two cloves
4. Add roux and a piece of sugar
5. A little wine can also be mixed in
