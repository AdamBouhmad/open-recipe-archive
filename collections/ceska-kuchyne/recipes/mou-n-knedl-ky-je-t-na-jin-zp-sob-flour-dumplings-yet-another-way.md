## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon lard or butter
- 2 whole eggs
- ½ pint sweet sour cream or good milk
- 1 pound flour
- 2 bread rolls cut and fried, or 4 slices white bread

## Directions

1. Beat a heaping tablespoon of lard or butter on a plate
2. Add 2 whole eggs and mix well
3. Add ½ pint of sweet sour cream or good milk
4. Mix in 1 pound of flour and work it well with a spoon so that the dough does not stick to the hand
5. Add 2 fried bread rolls or 4 slices of white bread, again mix well
6. Shape the dumplings, put them in boiling water and let them boil for half an hour
7. They go very well with cooked dishes and can also be served with cabbage or various sauces
