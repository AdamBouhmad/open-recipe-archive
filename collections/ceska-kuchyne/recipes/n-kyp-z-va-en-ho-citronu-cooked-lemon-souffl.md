## Ingredients

- large lemon
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil a large lemon for one hour, drain the water and boil again in fresh water for one hour
2. Divide the lemon into quarters, remove the seeds and crush it in a mortar
3. Mix the crushed lemon with 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar and 8 egg yolks, beat for a quarter hour
4. Fold in snow from 8 egg whites
5. Pour the mixture into a greased mold sprinkled with bread crumbs
6. Cook in steam, sprinkle with sugar before serving
