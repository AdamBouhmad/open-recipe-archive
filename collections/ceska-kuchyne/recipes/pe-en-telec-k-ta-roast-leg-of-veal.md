## Ingredients

- veal leg, white and fine
- salt
- pepper
- 4 cloves
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1 small bay leaf
- water
- 1/2 cup butter
- grated bread roll or bread

## Directions

1. Clean a fine white veal leg thoroughly, dry it with a cloth
2. Salt and pepper both sides
3. Place on a baking pan, insert 4 cloves into the leg, add chopped onion and a small bay leaf
4. Add a little water and 1/2 cup butter
5. Place in the oven and roast for two hours
6. Turn it over, sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread, top with butter
7. Roast at frequent intervals, basting with pan juices until golden brown
8. Place on a platter and pour strained sauce over it
