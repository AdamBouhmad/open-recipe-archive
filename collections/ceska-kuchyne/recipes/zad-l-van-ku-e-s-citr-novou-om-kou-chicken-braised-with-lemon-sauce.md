## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- butter
- 1 lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and braise or cook it in butter.
2. Make a pale broth, strain it through a sieve into the broth that was strained from the chicken.
3. Squeeze the juice from one lemon into this and boil for a while longer.
