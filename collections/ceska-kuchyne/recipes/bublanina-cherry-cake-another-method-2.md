## Ingredients

- half kilogram (1 pound) hard homemade bread
- 1 kilogram (2 pounds) cherries
- 8 egg yolks
- 75 grams (5 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- spices
- flower
- clove
- 3 and 1/2 deciliters (1 tablespoon) wine
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate the hard bread
2. Put cherries in cold water
3. Beat 8 egg yolks with 75 grams sugar
4. Mix the yolk mixture in a bowl with bread, lemon zest, spices, flower, clove, wine, and snow from 8 egg whites
5. Grease a mold and sprinkle with grated bread
6. Pour in the mixture with cherries and bake
