## Ingredients

- butter dough
- half liter picked strawberries
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 6 beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cover a sheet form with butter dough
2. Place on the dough a mixture of half liter picked strawberries, 4 tablespoons sugar, and 6 beaten eggs
3. If fresh strawberries are not available, pickled ones may be used in winter
