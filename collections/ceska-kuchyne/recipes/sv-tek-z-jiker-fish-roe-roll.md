## Ingredients

- Fish roe, finely chopped
- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Egg white foam (from several whites)
- Marjoram
- Garlic
- Grated bread roll
- Greased baking form
- Red bread roll

## Directions

1. Mix finely chopped fish roe with butter, egg yolks, foam from several egg whites, marjoram, garlic and grated bread roll.
2. Fill the greased baking form sprinkled with red bread roll with the mixture.
3. Bake the roll.
4. Slice and add to soup.
