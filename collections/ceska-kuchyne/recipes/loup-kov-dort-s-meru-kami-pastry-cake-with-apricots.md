## Ingredients

- layered pastry dough
- apricots
- sugar
- white paper
- apricot kernels

## Directions

1. Prepare a layered pastry cake
2. Put apricots into boiling water, remove the pits, and peel them
3. Place the peeled apricots on the cake
4. Sprinkle generously with finely ground sugar
5. Cover with white paper as large as the cake so the apricots do not burn
6. Place the cake in the oven and bake carefully
7. Peel the kernels, split them, and when the cake is baked, place them on top
