## Ingredients

- three spoons of butter
- two spoons of flour
- salt
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- one pint of boiling water
- two egg yolks
- one quarter pound of butter
- juice of one lemon
- two finely chopped hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Mix three spoons of butter with two spoons of flour
2. Season with salt, ground pepper and nutmeg
3. Add one pint of boiling water and beat well with a whisk to make it smooth
4. Allow to simmer a little
5. Remove from heat and stir in two egg yolks, one quarter pound of butter and juice of one lemon
6. Strain through a fine sieve or cloth
7. Finally, add two finely chopped hard-boiled eggs
