## Ingredients

- 10 dek sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 14 dek finely ground or crushed poppy seeds
- 5 dek melted butter
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 7 dek raisins
- a pinch of cinnamon
- vanilla
- snow from 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with egg yolks
2. Add the finely ground poppy seeds, melted butter, and sour cream
3. Add raisins, cinnamon, and vanilla
4. Fold in the snow from beaten egg whites
