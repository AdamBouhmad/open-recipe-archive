## Ingredients

- Very ripe cherries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean well-ripened cherries
2. Remove pits and stems
3. Place in a pot
4. Stir constantly and cook until completely soft
5. Press through a fine sieve
6. Cook sugar to the sixth degree
7. Pour juice into it
8. Cook everything together
9. Store in containers
