## Ingredients

- twenty five dekagrams (17 lots) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sweet almonds, finely crushed
- 8 bitter almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- lemon peel
- 3 rolls soaked in milk
- stiff snow from 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 25 dekagrams butter, then slowly add 12 dekagrams well-crushed sweet and 8 bitter almonds.
2. Add 8 egg yolks, 12 dekagrams sugar, lemon peel, 3 rolls soaked in milk, and stiff snow from 8 egg whites.
3. Grease a mold with butter and bake the soufflé.
4. Serve warm at table.
