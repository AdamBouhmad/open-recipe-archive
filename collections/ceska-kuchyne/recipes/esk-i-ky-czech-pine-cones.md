## Ingredients

- half liter fine flour
- 16 grams yeast
- a little milk
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Make soft yeast from half liter fine flour, 16 grams yeast, a little milk, salt and 1 tablespoon sugar
2. Work thoroughly until bubbles appear
3. Form into thin, long sticks, let rise on a greased sheet
4. Brush with beaten egg, bake slowly until dark golden, brushing with water glaze during baking
