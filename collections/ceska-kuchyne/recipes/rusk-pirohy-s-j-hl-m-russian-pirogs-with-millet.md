## Ingredients

- millet
- milk
- butter
- yeast
- 6 egg yolks
- flour
- 6 egg whites
- salt
- minced meat
- grated roll

## Directions

1. Scald millet, boil in milk and press through a sieve
2. Add butter, a little yeast and let stand in a warm place to rise
3. Add 6 egg yolks, a little flour, snow from 6 egg whites and salt
4. Work well and let rise again
5. Roll out thick dough, cut round pieces, sprinkle with minced meat, fold, brush with egg, coat in grated roll and fry in butter
