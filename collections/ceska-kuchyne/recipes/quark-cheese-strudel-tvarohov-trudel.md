## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) flour
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- water
- quark cheese
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- lemon zest rubbed with half the sugar
- 9 dekagrams sugar
- 15 grams (1 lot) ground almonds
- cinnamon
- raisins
- sweet cream or milk

## Directions

1. Mix 24 dekagrams flour with 2 whole eggs, 1 egg yolk, salt and water
2. Work the dough thoroughly, cover with a warm pan and let rest briefly
3. Roll out, then stretch as thinly as possible on a floured cloth
4. Brush the stretched dough with butter
5. Prepare filling by mixing quark cheese, 2 eggs, 2 egg whites, 9 dekagrams butter, lemon zest rubbed with half the sugar, 9 dekagrams sugar, 15 grams ground almonds, cinnamon and raisins
6. Spread the filling on the dough
7. Roll up, place in a greased mold and pour sweet cream or milk over it
