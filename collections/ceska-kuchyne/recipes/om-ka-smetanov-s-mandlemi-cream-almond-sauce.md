## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of blanched sweet almonds
- 4 or 5 bitter almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 spoonful of flour
- sugar to taste
- a piece of vanilla or lemon rind
- ½ cupfuls of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pound 2 ounces of blanched and peeled sweet almonds and about 4 or 5 bitter almonds in a mortar
2. Mix 4 egg yolks with 1 spoonful of flour
3. Sweeten this mixture to taste with sugar
4. Add a piece of vanilla or lemon rind to it
5. Mix in the pounded almonds and ½ cupfuls of sweet cream
6. Place over heat and stir constantly until it thickens, but do not let it boil
7. Remove the vanilla or lemon rind, pour the sauce into a dish and serve at the table
