## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- 75 grams (5 lots) of potato flour
- 1/4 liter of hot smetana (sour cream)
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- egg whites from 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Rub butter with potato flour and hot smetana until it does not stick to the baking pan
2. Pour onto baking pan
3. Add beaten egg yolks and salt
4. Fold in snow from 4 eggs
5. Shape small dumplings
6. Cook in salted water
7. Serve in soup
