## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram butter
- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) flour
- 1/4 kilogram sugar
- Juice from 1/2 orange
- 3 decagrams (2 lots) chopped candied orange peel
- 4 grated hard egg yolks
- Raw eggs (as much as needed to make dough like for sweet rolls)
- Jam or preserves for coating
- Chopped almonds, specially roasted, for topping

## Directions

1. Place butter on a baking sheet with flour, make a pink roux and let it cool
2. On a work surface, add to it 1/4 kilogram sugar, juice from 1/2 orange, 3 decagrams chopped candied orange peel, 4 grated hard egg yolks and as many raw eggs as needed to make a dough like for sweet rolls
3. Roll out the dough on a baking sheet, make an edge from dough and bake
4. Brush with jam and sprinkle with chopped, specially roasted almonds
