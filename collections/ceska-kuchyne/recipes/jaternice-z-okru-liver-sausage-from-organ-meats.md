## Ingredients

- calf organ meats
- salted water
- 3 onions
- butter
- parsley
- 5 eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- sour cream
- grated bread roll
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated lemon zest
- sausage casings

## Directions

1. Clean calf organ meats, blanch in salted water, cook, dry and finely chop
2. Mix with 3 onions fried in butter, parsley, 5 whole eggs, 5 egg yolks beaten with sour cream, grated bread roll, pepper, salt, nutmeg and grated lemon zest
3. Fill clean sausage casings with the mixture
4. Simmer for half an hour, then fry in butter
