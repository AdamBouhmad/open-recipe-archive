## Ingredients

- one tablespoon potato starch
- water
- white wine
- juice of three lemons
- zest of two lemons, grated or thinly sliced and finely chopped
- 250 grams sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 30 grams gelatin, melted

## Directions

1. Dissolve one tablespoon potato starch in water, add white wine, juice of three lemons, grated or thinly sliced and finely chopped zest of two lemons, 250 grams sugar mixed with 10 egg yolks
2. Stir constantly and cook, quickly remove from heat
3. Mix with 30 grams melted gelatin, pour into a mold greased with almond oil and put on ice to set
