## Ingredients

- Ripest peaches
- Kernels from the pits
- Water
- Wine

## Directions

1. Select the ripest peaches
2. Peel them and split them in half
3. Press through a sieve
4. Grind the kernels from the pits with water and wine
5. Press and strain
6. Add this milk to the peach juice
7. Sweeten everything
8. Transform into ice cream
