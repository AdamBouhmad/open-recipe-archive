## Ingredients

- partridges
- onion, sliced
- juniper berries
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges down to the head; eyes may be removed or left in.
2. Cut off the lower jaw, remove the crop, scrape the beak and legs, cut off the spurs.
3. Clean, wash, cut open the gizzard and clean it out, and salt.
4. Truss like chickens and lard the breast in two rows.
5. Place on a baking dish with sliced onion according to the number of partridges.
6. Add several juniper berries and peppercorns, pour with water, cover with a lid, and braise until tender.
7. Cut into neat pieces, strain the sauce over them, and serve usually with cabbage.
