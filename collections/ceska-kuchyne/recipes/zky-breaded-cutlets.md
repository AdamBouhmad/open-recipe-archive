## Ingredients

- beef
- onion
- butter
- garlic
- lemon zest
- salt
- pepper
- 4 tablespoons beef broth
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil beef and chop finely
2. Fry half an onion in butter and add it
3. Add minced garlic, lemon zest, salt, pepper, and beef broth
4. Simmer for a quarter hour
5. Strain through a sieve so all liquid drains and meat cools
6. Flatten pieces like eggs, coat with egg and bread crumbs
7. Fry
