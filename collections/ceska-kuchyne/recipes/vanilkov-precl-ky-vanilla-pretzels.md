## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 250 grams sugar
- vanilla
- 375 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar
2. Add crushed vanilla
3. Gradually add flour and mix until well-worked dough forms
4. Shape into small pretzels
5. Brush with beaten egg yolks
6. Place on buttered baking sheet
7. Bake slowly
8. Sprinkle with sugar
9. Serve
