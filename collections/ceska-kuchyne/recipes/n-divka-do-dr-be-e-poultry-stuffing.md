## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams mashed veal livers
- 2 sardines
- onion
- juniper berries
- pepper
- thyme
- blood
- wine

## Directions

1. Mash veal livers finely
2. Add sardines, onion, juniper berries, pepper, and thyme
3. Pound everything finely and mix
4. Add blood mixed with wine
5. Braise on warm stove
6. When fully prepared, use as stuffing for poultry
