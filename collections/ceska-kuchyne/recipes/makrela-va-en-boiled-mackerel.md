## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- salt
- water
- butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the mackerel as directed previously
2. Drain the water, pour hot water over it and let it soak on a hot spot for about five minutes
3. Drain the water again and place the fish on a heated oval dish
4. Place pieces of fresh butter around it and surround with small peeled potatoes boiled in salted water
