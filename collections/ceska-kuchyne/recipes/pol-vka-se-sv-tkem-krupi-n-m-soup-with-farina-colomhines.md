## Ingredients

- two eggs
- a pinch of cloves
- salt
- ground pepper
- half a pot of wheat farina
- three spoons of lard, or half lard and half butter

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs for five minutes.
2. Add a pinch of cloves, salt and then ground pepper and half a pot of wheat farina and mix lightly.
3. Heat three spoons of lard, or half lard and half butter in a deep pot, and when the fat is very hot, pour the batter into it and let it fry.
4. When it is browned on the bottom, turn it like a pancake and when well cooked, take it out of the pot, cut into small cubes, place in boiling beef broth and let it cook for about five minutes.
