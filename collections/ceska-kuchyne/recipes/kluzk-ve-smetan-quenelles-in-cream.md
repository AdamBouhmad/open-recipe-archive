## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- white bread, grated
- salt
- 2 cups good cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- grated zest of one lemon
- five egg yolks
- a little cold cream
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Beat together six egg yolks with 4 tablespoons sugar until foamy
2. Add enough grated white bread so that the mixture is as thick as ordinary quenelles, salt it, and stir well
3. Cut out small pieces with a coffee spoon and fry them golden in hot clarified butter
4. Boil 2 cups of good cream, add 2 tablespoons sugar and grated lemon zest
5. Beat five egg yolks with a little cold cream and pour into the boiling cream, stirring well
6. Arrange the quenelles in a deep porcelain dish, pour the prepared cream over them
7. Place on a baking sheet that you have sprinkled well with salt and put into a hot oven
8. When it is very hot, remove from the oven (do not let it bake) and bring to the table
