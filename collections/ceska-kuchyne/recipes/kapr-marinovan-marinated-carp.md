## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- butter
- onion
- rosemary
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaves
- allspice
- ginger
- capers
- lemon peel
- celery (optional)
- parsley (optional)
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the carp and salt it
2. Place on a baking pan, supporting with sticks so it does not touch the bottom
3. Brush with butter in the oven until cooked through
4. Slice onion into thin pieces and mix with rosemary, several peppercorns, a sprig of thyme, 3 bay leaves, allspice, a piece of whole ginger, a handful of nice capers, lemon peel cut into small sticks, and optionally celery and parsley
5. Mix everything together
6. Cut the fish into pieces and layer them one on top of another, sprinkling with the mixture between layers, or arrange them side by side
7. Cover the dish well with a tight-fitting lid or plate, ensuring the lid is not made of tin, copper, or other metal
8. Place in cold storage
9. The marinated fish will keep for two months in winter
10. When serving, add vinegar and oil
