## Ingredients

- dough as for previous coconut cake
- red sugar
- coconut

## Directions

1. When the dough is ready as for the previous cake, take 5 tablespoons of it and place in a separate bowl.
2. Mix in red sugar to taste until the dough is evenly red.
3. Add 0.25 cup coconut and mix well.
4. Pour the white dough into a large lacy mold, then place the red dough by spoonfuls on top, about an inch apart.
5. The red dough will sink to the bottom but will leave stripes behind that give the cake a beautiful appearance when cut.
6. When the cake is done, spread frosting made from egg whites on the surface and sprinkle with coconut.
