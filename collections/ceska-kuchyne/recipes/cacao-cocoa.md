## Ingredients

- whole cocoa beans
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Instead of ground cocoa, buy whole cocoa beans
2. Roast them like coffee beans
3. Remove the shell
4. Grind in a mill or crush in a mortar
5. Boil in milk or water
