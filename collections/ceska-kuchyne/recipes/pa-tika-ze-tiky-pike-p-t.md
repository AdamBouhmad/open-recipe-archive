## Ingredients

- Pike
- Pike liver
- Fish meat
- Melted butter
- Eggs
- Grated bread roll
- Vinegar
- Lemon slices
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Capers
- Lemon
- Crayfish tails
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Butter dough
- Egg yolk
- Strong broth
- Caper sauce with lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, tear it apart, gut it, and cut into pieces.
2. Remove all bones from the meat, place meat in vinegar, and interlace with lemon slices.
3. Prepare forcemeat from pike liver, fish meat, melted butter, several eggs, and grated bread roll, season with salt and nutmeg.
4. Spread the forcemeat on a platter, place the pike brushed with butter on top.
5. Top with chopped mushrooms, capers, lemon, and crayfish tails, season with salt.
6. Add another layer of forcemeat, then fish, and so on until the dish is full.
7. The last layer is made of forcemeat, covered with strong broth, covered with leaf pastry and butter dough, brushed with egg, and baked at strong heat for about 1.5 hours.
8. Serve with caper sauce with lemon juice.
