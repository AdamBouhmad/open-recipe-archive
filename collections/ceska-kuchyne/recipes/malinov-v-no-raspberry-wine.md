## Ingredients

- ripe raspberries
- finely ground sugar
- raspberry juice or ordinary white wine

## Directions

1. Crush the ripe raspberries
2. Press them and strain the juice through thick cloth
3. To each liter of juice, add finely ground sugar to taste
4. Mix everything and let stand peacefully for three days covered
5. Pour off the settled, clarified juice
6. Add to it as much raspberry juice, either the same amount or twice as much ordinary white wine
7. Transfer to bottles
8. After eight days it is drinkable
