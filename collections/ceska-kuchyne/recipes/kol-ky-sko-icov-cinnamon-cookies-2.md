## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- 1/2 kilogram ground sugar
- 16 grams ground cinnamon
- 1/2 kilogram fine flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs with ground sugar for at least half an hour
2. Add ground cinnamon, salt lightly, and flour
3. Form small cookies on buttered sheet
4. Bake slowly until golden
