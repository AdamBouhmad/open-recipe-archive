## Ingredients

- about one kilogram peeled and cooked chestnuts
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Salt the chestnuts and stuff them into the stomach of the turkey
2. When the turkey is roasted, remove the chestnuts
3. Brown sugar in butter until golden, sprinkle with flour and add broth
4. Stew the chestnuts in this mixture until all liquid is absorbed
5. Use the chestnuts to garnish the turkey
