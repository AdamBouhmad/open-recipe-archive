## Ingredients

- seven decagrams chocolate
- four deciliters cream
- seven decagrams sugar
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil seven decagrams of chocolate with four deciliters of cream
2. Let cool
3. Mix with seven decagrams of sugar and six egg yolks
