## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters fresh juniper berries
- Fine, pure spirits (kořalka)
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Take 0.5 liters of good, fresh juniper berries and crush them
2. Pour fine, pure spirits over them
3. Seal completely and leave in the sun for at least fourteen days
4. Strain and pour, then add sugar dissolved in water
5. Seal well and leave in the sun for several more days
6. Can then be transferred to special bottles
