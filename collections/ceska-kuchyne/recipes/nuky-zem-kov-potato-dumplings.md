## Ingredients

- 75 grams (5 lots) of butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon of flour
- 8 tablespoons of cooled, mashed potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Rub butter, add whole eggs and egg yolks, flour and mashed potatoes
2. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg and parsley
3. Shape dumplings with a spoon
4. Cook in salted water
5. Serve in soup
