## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 deca butter
- 4 egg yolks
- bread roll
- sour cream
- raisins
- sliced almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 3 to 4 deca of butter with 4 egg yolks
2. Add grated bread roll moistened and squeezed out in sour cream
3. Mix with raisins, sliced almonds, and sugar
4. Use for stuffing turkeys or bake separately and serve with the roast
