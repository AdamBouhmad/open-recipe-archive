## Ingredients

- one chicken cut into pieces
- a piece of veal bone
- three quarts water
- a bundle of fresh parsley
- one bay leaf
- a stroke of garlic
- a pinch of ground pepper
- cloves
- mace
- salt
- one quarter pound carrots
- one quarter pound turnip
- one quarter pound onion
- one ounce celery root
- two apples
- two ounces ham
- three ounces butter
- three tablespoons flour
- three teaspoons Indian curry
- a piece of sugar
- cooked rice

## Directions

1. Place cut chicken and a piece of veal bone in a pot with three quarts of water and let cook
2. Skim the foam and add a bundle of fresh parsley, one bay leaf, a stroke of garlic, a pinch of ground pepper, cloves and mace
3. Salt and let cook until the chicken is tender, then remove it and cut into pieces
4. Remove all fat from the broth and strain through a sieve
5. Chop one quarter pound carrots, one quarter pound turnip, one quarter pound onion, one ounce celery root, two apples and two ounces of ham, each separately
6. Place three ounces of butter on a pan and let the chopped onion brown on it, then add carrots, turnip, ham, celery, apple and chopped chicken meat and let braise
7. Then add three tablespoons of flour, three teaspoons of Indian curry, a piece of sugar, pour in the broth and mix well
8. Let cook a while, then remove the meat, strain the broth through a sieve, return the strained broth with the meat to the pot and stir over the fire until it begins to boil
9. Place in a soup bowl and serve at table with cooked rice in a separate dish
