## Ingredients

- 1/2 to 1 liter buttermilk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- milk
- water
- salt
- sugar
- grated bread

## Directions

1. To 1/2 to 1 liter of buttermilk, add 1 tablespoon flour that has been mixed with milk beforehand
2. Simmer everything while stirring constantly
3. Add water to desired consistency
4. Season with salt and sugar
5. Add grated bread and mix
6. Serve at table
