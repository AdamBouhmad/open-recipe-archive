## Ingredients

- butter
- cake dough
- preserved fruit (apricots, peaches, berries, and similar)
- almonds
- sugar
- egg whites (at least 6)

## Directions

1. Butter a cake mold
2. Line the bottom with thinly made cake dough
3. Top with preserved fruit (apricots, peaches, berries, and similar)
4. Sprinkle with almonds, sugar, and stiff snow beaten from at least 6 eggs
5. Bake
