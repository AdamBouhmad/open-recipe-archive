## Ingredients

- pike or carp
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) fresh butter
- flower
- salt
- 2 whole eggs
- bread roll soaked in milk
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) water
- lemon juice
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) butter
- parsley root
- whole celery
- light roux

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, remove skin and all bones, and chop the meat finely.
2. Mix 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of fresh butter with a bit of flower and salt.
3. Add 2 whole eggs and a bread roll soaked in milk.
4. Mix in the chopped meat and form small dumplings with a spoon.
5. The dumplings can be served in a meat broth or as a standalone dish.
6. For the standalone dish: mix 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) of water, lemon juice from one lemon, 3 dekagrams (2 lots) of butter, a bit of crushed flower, a piece of parsley root and whole celery.
7. Salt it and boil.
8. Add light roux, strain, and when boiling, add the previously prepared dumplings.
9. To this sauce, cooked fish prepared in white water can be added.
