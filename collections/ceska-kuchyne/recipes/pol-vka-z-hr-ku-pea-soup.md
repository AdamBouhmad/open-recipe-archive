## Ingredients

- peas
- sugar
- butter
- beef soup
- flour (for roux)
- burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the peas and stew them with sugar in butter
2. Make a light roux and add it to the beef soup, strain
3. Add the peas and a piece of burnt sugar
