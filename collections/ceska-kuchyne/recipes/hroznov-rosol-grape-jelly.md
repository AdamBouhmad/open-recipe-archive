## Ingredients

- unripened grape berries
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the grains from unripened grapes and put in a pot or kettle
2. For every 8 pounds of grapes (a small basket of grapes holds 8 pounds, a large one 20 pounds) add 1 small cup of water and let it cook about 1/2 hour until the grains are completely cooked, stirring occasionally so the skins do not stick to the bottom
3. When cooked, set aside and let cool
4. Then strain through thin cloth (cheese cloth)
5. If you want clear jelly, let it only drip and do not squeeze much
6. Measure the juice and pour it back into the pot and for however many small cups of juice, prepare that many small cups of fine sugar
7. Put the sugar in the oven to warm, but do not let it burn and when the juice has cooked 1/2 hour, add the sugar to it, let it cook another 10 minutes, during which collect the foam, then set aside and when it cools, put in jars
8. Jelly from wild grape is also very delicious
