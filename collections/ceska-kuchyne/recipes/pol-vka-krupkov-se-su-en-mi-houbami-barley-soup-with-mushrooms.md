## Ingredients

- 1 small cup good torn barley grains
- 1 spoon new butter
- Salted water
- 3 handfuls dried mushrooms
- Green parsley
- Broth (recipe no. 38)
- Water from cooked mushrooms
- Light butter roux
- Salt
- A little pepper
- Ginger

## Directions

1. Cook 1 small cup of torn barley grains with 1 spoon new butter in salted water until soft
2. Cook 3 handfuls of dried mushrooms in a separate pot
3. Drain water from mushrooms and chop them finely with fresh parsley
4. Pour broth (recipe no. 38) and mushroom water over the chopped mushrooms
5. Add the cooked barley grains
6. Thicken with a light butter roux
7. Season with salt
8. Add a little pepper and ginger
9. Let simmer
