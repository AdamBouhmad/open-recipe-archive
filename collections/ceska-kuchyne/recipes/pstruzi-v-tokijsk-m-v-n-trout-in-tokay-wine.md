## Ingredients

- cleaned trout
- salt
- Tokay wine
- bay leaf
- rosemary
- saffron
- flour
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make incisions on both sides of the cleaned trout
2. Salt and pour Tokay wine over
3. Layer with bay leaf, rosemary, and a little saffron
4. Cook until tender
5. Strain the sauce, mix with flour and butter, and adjust with lemon juice
6. Pour over the fish and serve
