## Ingredients

- two fresh brains
- warm water
- hot water
- butter
- salt
- eggs
- hard rolls
- milk

## Directions

1. Soak two fresh brains in lukewarm water, remove all membranes, cook in hot water, and strain through a sieve so water drains.
2. Melt butter in a baking pan, place the brains in it, salt, and fry with two eggs over moderate heat until a thick paste forms.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
4. Remove crusts from hard rolls, cut into small flat cakes, dip in milk, spread with the brain mixture, place two cakes together, dip in egg, roll in bread, and fry in butter.
5. May be served after beef broth.
