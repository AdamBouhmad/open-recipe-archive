## Ingredients

- Two liters fresh, well-ripened strawberries
- 375 grams dissolved sugar
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Press two liters fresh, well-ripened strawberries through sieve
2. Add 375 grams dissolved sugar to the juice
3. Add juice of one lemon
4. Place in ice cream machine, or if not available, in a dried metal container placed in ice
5. Whip the liquid with a whisk until it thickens
6. Place on table in small glass bowls
