## Ingredients

- a piece of lard
- one onion, sliced
- bay leaf
- a piece of lemon peel
- clove
- two pounds of beef
- flour
- beef broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Put a piece of lard, one sliced onion, bay leaf, a piece of lemon peel and clove on a pot.
2. Salt and clean two pounds of beef, put it in the pot and let it stew.
3. Sprinkle it then with a little flour and let it stew some more.
4. Then add a little beef broth and a little wine and let it braise until the meat shrinks, then put it on a plate and sprinkle with lemon peel.
