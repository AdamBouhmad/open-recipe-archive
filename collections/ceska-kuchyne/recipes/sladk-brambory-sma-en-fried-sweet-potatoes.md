## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- lard or butter
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash sweet potatoes well and cook them
2. When they are soft, remove them, peel and split lengthwise into halves
3. Put about two spoonfuls of lard or butter in a pan, salt the potatoes, coat in flour and fry on both sides in hot fat until reddened
4. Note: sweet potatoes that are merely boiled tend to be too watery
