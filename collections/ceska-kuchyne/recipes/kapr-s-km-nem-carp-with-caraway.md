## Ingredients

- Cleaned carp
- Caraway
- Salt
- New butter
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. The cleaned carp is seasoned with caraway or caraway is cooked and it is poured with just caraway water, salted, placed on a pan.
2. Add a piece of new butter, cover with a lid, braise until soft.
3. Carefully place on a platter with a spoon and add the sauce to it.
4. Usually grated horseradish is added to it.
