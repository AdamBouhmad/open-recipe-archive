## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) pearl barley
- one liter milk
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) ground almonds
- 4 tablespoons grated roll
- salt
- 8 egg whites for snow
- grated rusks

## Directions

1. Blanch 24 dekagrams pearl barley several times in hot water, cook in one liter milk, pass through a sieve and let cool
2. Beat 12 dekagrams butter with cinnamon, lemon zest, 6 dekagrams ground almonds, 4 tablespoons grated roll and salt
3. Add the porridge with snow from 8 egg whites and everything into a greased mold sprinkled with grated rusks and cook for 1 1/2 hours
