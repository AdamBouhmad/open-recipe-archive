## Ingredients

- dough prepared by the previous method
- apples, peaches, plums, strawberries, cherries or other fruit
- sugar

## Directions

1. From dough prepared by the previous method, you can also bake large fruit cakes either in baking dishes or on a baking sheet
2. Roll out the risen dough to about half a finger's thickness
3. Line a baking pan or baking dish with it and cover with quartered apples, halved peaches or plums, strawberries, cherries or other fruit
4. Sprinkle with sugar as needed, and if you use apples, mix the sugar with cinnamon
5. Let rise and bake
