## Ingredients

- young carrots
- peas
- butter
- meat broth
- thickening agent (jíška)
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Clean young carrots and slice into rounds
2. Place carrots and peas on a baking sheet
3. Add butter and pour meat broth in which thickening agent has been mixed
4. Add chopped parsley and braise for a while
5. If carrots are older, cook them alone for an hour first, then add to peas
