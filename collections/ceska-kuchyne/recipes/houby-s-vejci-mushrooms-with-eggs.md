## Ingredients

- cleaned and sliced mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- caraway seeds
- pepper
- fresh parsley, chopped
- eggs

## Directions

1. Let cleaned and sliced mushrooms stew in butter with salt and caraway seeds; when the water has evaporated from them, add butter, a pinch of pepper and a little chopped fresh parsley and finally mix it with several eggs.
2. You can also put scrambled eggs in the middle of a platter and surround them with stewed mushrooms.
3. You can also put the eggs in a shallow pan at the same time with the mushrooms and seasonings; this is a fresher method and many prefer it better.
