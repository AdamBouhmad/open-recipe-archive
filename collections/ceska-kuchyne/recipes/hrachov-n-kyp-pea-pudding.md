## Ingredients

- 1 liter peas
- butter
- 10 egg yolks
- lemon zest
- sugar
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook 1 liter of peas, pour off the water, press through a sieve and let the porridge cool
2. Meanwhile, rub butter and mix it with 10 egg yolks, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar, salt and nutmeg, then put it with the pea porridge into a greased form and bake for an hour at moderate heat
