## Ingredients

- A full basket of dandelion leaves
- 5 gallons of cold water
- 7 pounds of white sugar
- 5 pounds of yellow sugar
- meat from half a dozen oranges, finely chopped, with all peel removed (both outer and white)
- meat from half a dozen lemons, finely chopped, with all peel removed (both outer and white)

## Directions

1. Fill a basket with dandelion leaves, place in a pot or kettle, pour 5 gallons of cold water over them, and boil for one hour.
2. Let stand for 12 hours or overnight, then strain.
3. Add to the liquid 7 pounds of white sugar and 5 pounds of yellow sugar, the finely chopped meat from half a dozen oranges and half a dozen lemons, from which you have removed all the peel, both outer and white.
4. Mix everything well and when the sugar has dissolved, pour into jugs to ferment.
5. When fermented, stopper and store.
