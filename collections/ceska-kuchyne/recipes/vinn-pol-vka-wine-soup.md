## Ingredients

- 1 liter wine
- 3/8 liter water
- lemon juice
- 100 grams sugar
- a little ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour mixed in cold water

## Directions

1. Mix wine, water, lemon juice, sugar, and cinnamon together
2. Bring to a good boil
3. While stirring constantly, slowly add 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour mixed in cold water
4. Simmer until done
5. Pour the soup over fried roll in a bowl and serve at table
