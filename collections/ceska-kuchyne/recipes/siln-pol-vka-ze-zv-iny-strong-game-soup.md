## Ingredients

- old hare
- wild rabbit
- old partridges
- veal
- salt
- onion
- clove
- carrot
- celery
- leek
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cook the hare, rabbit, partridges, and veal together slowly for three to four hours
2. Add salt, onion, clove, carrot, celery, leek, and bay leaf to the soup
3. Strain the soup
4. Remove the fat from it
5. Serve at table
