## Ingredients

- 2 liters milk
- piece of fresh butter
- piece of sugar
- salt to taste
- flour, as much as needed to make a thick porridge

## Directions

1. Mix milk, butter, sugar and salt
2. Add flour while stirring to make a thick porridge
3. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
