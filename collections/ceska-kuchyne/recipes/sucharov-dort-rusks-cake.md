## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 375 grams fine sifted sugar
- 200 grams peeled and grated almonds
- 8 grated lemon zests
- juice of one lemon
- 4 grams finely ground cinnamon
- 1 small teaspoon ground clove powder
- firm snow from approximately 15 egg whites
- 250 grams ground rusks

## Directions

1. Mix 15 egg yolks with 375 grams fine sifted sugar, 200 grams peeled and grated almonds, 8 grated lemon zests, and juice of one lemon
2. Add 4 grams finely ground cinnamon and 1 small teaspoon ground clove powder
3. Work everything together for about half an hour
4. Slowly add firm snow from approximately 15 egg whites and 250 grams ground rusks
5. Pour into greased cake forms and bake slowly
