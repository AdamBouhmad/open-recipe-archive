## Ingredients

- 2 heaping tablespoons flour
- 1 litre boiling water
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- salt
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the flour in a little cold water
2. Pour in 1 litre of boiling water while stirring
3. Salt it, add 6 decagrams (4 lots) of butter
4. Boil for a while and add 2 egg yolks
