## Ingredients

- 1 kilo beef
- salt
- piece of liver
- bones
- water (2.5 liters)
- parsley
- onion
- celery
- noodles

## Directions

1. Rinse the beef thoroughly, season with salt, add a piece of liver and some bones, cook in water
2. When it begins to foam, skim the foam
3. After skimming, add parsley, onion, celery to make the soup slightly sweet (a whole potato can be added if desired)
4. When the water has cooked down by half, strain it and add noodles
