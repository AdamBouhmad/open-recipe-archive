## Ingredients

- Veal leg meat
- Flour
- Onion
- Salt
- Water or beef broth
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Cut meat from the leg to a thickness of two fingers, pound it, clean it, salt it, coat in flour and place on a baking pan in which onion has been half-fried.
2. Braise on both sides for 10 minutes each. If there is little sauce, add water or beef broth.
3. Strain the sauce over the cutlets and add lemon.
