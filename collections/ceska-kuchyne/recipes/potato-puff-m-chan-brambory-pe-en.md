## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups cooked and mashed potatoes
- one egg yolk
- one spoon butter
- 0.5 cup milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take 1.5 cups of cooked and mashed potatoes, preferably leftover
2. Mix in the yolk from one egg, a spoon of butter and half a cup of milk
3. Salt and pepper and finally whip the white from one egg and gently fold it in
4. Butter a small dish, place the mixture in it and bake
