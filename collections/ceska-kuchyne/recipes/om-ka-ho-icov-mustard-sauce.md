## Ingredients

- beef broth
- butter roux
- lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1 egg yolk
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Brown beef broth with butter roux
2. Add lemon juice and a tablespoon of good mustard
3. Mix one egg yolk in a little sweet cream and add it to the sauce
