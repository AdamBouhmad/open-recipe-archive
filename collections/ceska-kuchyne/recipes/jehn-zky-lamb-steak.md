## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- salt
- clarified butter or lard
- parsley butter or fried onion

## Directions

1. Cut neat steaks from the lamb leg
2. Salt them and quickly fry in hot clarified butter or lard
3. You can also grill them
4. Arrange on a heated platter, place pieces of parsley butter on top or sprinkle with chopped fried onion
5. Leg meat can also be cut crosswise in the manner of beefsteak and prepared the same way
