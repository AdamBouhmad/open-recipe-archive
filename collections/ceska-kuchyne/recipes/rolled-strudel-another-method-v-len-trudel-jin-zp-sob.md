## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 large tablespoon lukewarm water
- salt
- jam or raisins, quark cheese, apples, or other filling
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Crumble 9 dekagrams butter into flour on a board
2. Add 2 eggs
3. Add 1 large tablespoon lukewarm water
4. Salt to taste
5. Work the dough almost like noodle dough
6. Let rest briefly
7. Repeat the folding process several times
8. Roll out like thick noodles
9. Coat with jam or sprinkle with raisins, quark cheese, apples or other filling
10. Roll up and bake on a greased pan
