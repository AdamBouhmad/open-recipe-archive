## Ingredients

- 195 grams (13 lots) butter
- 3-4 eggs
- 240 grams (16 lots) bread soaked in milk
- half kilogram finely minced veal
- 195 grams bacon
- salt
- ground nutmeg
- several tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Rub butter with 3-4 eggs
2. Mix in bread soaked in milk, minced veal, bacon, salt, and ground nutmeg
3. Add several tablespoons of sour cream
