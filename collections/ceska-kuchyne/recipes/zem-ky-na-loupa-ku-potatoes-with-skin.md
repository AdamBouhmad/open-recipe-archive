## Ingredients

- Good potatoes
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes thoroughly, preferably with a brush, each one separately.
2. Keep in cold water, then cook without completely covering with water.
3. Drain the water and leave in the oven until completely dry and split open.
4. Alternatively, cook potatoes halfway through, quickly drain the water, cover the pot with a folded cloth, and leave in the oven until done.
