## Ingredients

- 500 grams cooked potatoes
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 15 decagrams (10 lots) ground sugar
- 3 decagrams (2 lots) ground almonds
- grated lemon zest
- 1 small spoon cinnamon
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) grated bread roll
- snow from egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked potatoes
2. Mix with butter, egg yolks, ground sugar, ground almonds, grated lemon zest, cinnamon, grated bread roll, and snow from egg whites
3. Place in a greased mold
4. Bake for one hour or boil in hot water for 2 hours
