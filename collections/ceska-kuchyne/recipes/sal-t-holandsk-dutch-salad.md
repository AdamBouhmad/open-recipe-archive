## Ingredients

- potatoes
- celery
- small onions
- cabbage
- cleaned and rinsed herring, cut into small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook each ingredient separately: potatoes, celery, small onions, and cabbage
2. Cut potatoes, celery and onions into slices
3. Mix with cabbage cut into small pieces, cleaned and rinsed herring
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Dress with vinegar and oil
