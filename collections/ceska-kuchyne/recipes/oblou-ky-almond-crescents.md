## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons fresh butter
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- a pinch of salt
- zest of 1/2 lemon, finely grated
- a pinch of flower
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 glass rum
- 1 whole egg (if needed)
- 1/4 pound almonds, finely chopped and mixed with crushed sugar
- 1/4 pound crushed sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Crumble 4 tablespoons fresh butter on a board with 2 cups flour
2. Add 1/2 cup sugar, a pinch of salt, finely grated zest from 1/2 lemon, and a pinch of flower
3. Make a well in the center
4. Mix in 4 egg yolks and 1 glass rum; if dough is too stiff, add 1 whole egg
5. Roll out thin, fold like puff pastry, roll out again and fold again
6. When rolled out for the third time, cut into strips two fingers wide and six fingers long
7. Brush with beaten snow from the 4 egg yolks
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped almonds mixed with crushed sugar
9. Place on crescent-shaped molds and bake until golden
10. Equal quantities of almonds and sugar for sprinkling should be 1/4 pound each
11. You can use good milk instead of rum
