## Ingredients

- salmon, pike, zander or perch
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- bay leaf
- optional spices
- light roux
- fish broth
- capers
- sardine butter
- lemon juice
- egg yolks
- potato flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Kill the fish, clean, and cut into pieces.
2. Salt the pieces and let sit for several hours.
3. Dry the pieces and place in water with salt, pepper, onion, bay leaves and optional spices.
4. Boil together, continuously skimming foam.
5. When the fish is cooked, remove it, peel off the skin, remove bones, arrange nicely on a dish and pour white sauce over it.
6. For the white sauce: make it from light roux cooked with fish broth, capers and sardine butter, with lemon juice added.
7. Alternatively, make the sauce from several egg yolks mixed with potato flour and good vinegar, mixed with boiling fish broth.
