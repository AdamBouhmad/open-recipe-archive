## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- butter
- semolina

## Directions

1. Salt the water and add a piece of butter.
2. When the water begins to boil, add enough semolina to thicken sufficiently.
3. Heat either onion with butter or butter alone.
4. Dip a spoon in the butter, then use it to take dumplings.
5. Sprinkle on a plate with either gingerbread or sugar and cinnamon.
