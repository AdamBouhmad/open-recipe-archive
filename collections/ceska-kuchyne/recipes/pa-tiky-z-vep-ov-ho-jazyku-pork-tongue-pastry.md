## Ingredients

- Pork tongue
- Salted water
- Finely chopped almonds
- Raisins
- Chopped bacon
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Lemon zest
- Butter pastry dough

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in salted water until soft
2. Remove the skin and chop the meat finely
3. Mix finely chopped almonds, raisins, chopped bacon, sugar, cinnamon, pepper and lemon zest
4. Salt the mixture
5. Fill into butter pastry dough
6. Add a pleasant sauce as described for other pastries
