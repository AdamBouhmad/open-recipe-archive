## Ingredients

- one cup of well-rinsed rice
- water
- one heaping tablespoon of butter
- salt
- one cup of sweet milk
- a little nutmeg, grated
- two tablespoons of sugar
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Bring water to boil and add one cup of well-rinsed rice
2. Cook for 10 minutes
3. Drain the water
4. Let stand uncovered on the stove for a while to evaporate the moisture
5. Add one heaping tablespoon of butter
6. Mix in with a fork
7. Add a little salt
8. Add one cup of sweet milk
9. Let simmer slowly for half an hour until the rice is soft
10. Turn the rice out onto a dish
11. Grate a little nutmeg over it
12. Add two tablespoons of sugar (more may be used according to taste)
13. Add the yolks of four eggs, beaten
14. Finally add the stiffly beaten snow made from four egg whites
15. Pour into a well-buttered mold
16. Bake in a very hot oven
17. When baked, sprinkle with sugar and bring to table
