## Ingredients

- firm white bacon
- vinegar
- head lettuce
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut firm white bacon into small cubes
2. Fry until crispy
3. Add a little vinegar
4. While everything is warm—neither too hot nor too cold—pour over a well-prepared head lettuce salad
5. Mix and serve while the bacon is still warm
