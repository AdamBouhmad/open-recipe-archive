## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- butter
- bay leaf
- rosemary
- pepper grains
- lemon
- cloves
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and wash the trout, salt well, let rest an hour, then dry
2. Brush with butter and bake
3. When cooled, place in a jar with bay leaf, rosemary, peppercorns, sliced lemon, and a few cloves
4. Pour cooked vinegar over them, seal with oil on top so no air can reach it, and cover the jar well
