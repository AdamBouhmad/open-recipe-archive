## Ingredients

- half kilogram butter
- eight whole eggs
- eight egg yolks
- 125 grams peeled and crushed or chopped almonds
- half kilogram sugar
- twelve hard-boiled eggs, pressed through a sieve
- finely grated lemon zest
- 4 grams crushed nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat half kilogram of butter until creamy
2. Gradually add eight whole eggs, eight egg yolks, 125 grams peeled and crushed or chopped almonds, half kilogram sugar, twelve hard-boiled eggs pressed through a sieve, finely grated lemon zest, and 4 grams crushed nutmeg
