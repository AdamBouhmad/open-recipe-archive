## Ingredients

- ripe washed cranberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook well-ripened, washed cranberries over a gentle fire, or place them in a wider container with boiling water until the juice bursts out, then strain.
2. Add sugar (for 3 liters, half a kilogram), cook and remove the foam; then fill bottles and seal well. The juice can later be used for sauces and similar purposes.
