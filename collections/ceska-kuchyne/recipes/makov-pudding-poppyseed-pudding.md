## Ingredients

- four tablespoons poppyseeds
- two tablespoons sugar
- two tablespoons ground almonds
- finely chopped zest of one lemon
- seven crushed cloves
- five egg yolks
- five egg whites
- butter
- grated rolls
- chocolate sauce

## Directions

1. Grind or pound four tablespoons of poppyseeds finely, place in a bowl, and add two tablespoons sugar, two tablespoons ground almonds, finely chopped zest of one lemon, and seven crushed cloves.
2. Stir for a quarter hour, then add snow made from five egg whites.
3. Place the mixture in a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with grated rolls.
4. Let it steam cook for one hour.
5. Then remove it onto a dish and pour chocolate sauce over it.
