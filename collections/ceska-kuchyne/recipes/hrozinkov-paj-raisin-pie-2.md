## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of large raisins
- one coffee cup of water
- four tablespoons of vinegar
- half a coffee cup of sugar
- less than one tablespoon of butter
- lemon zest
- flour
- one egg white
- sugar for sweetening

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from raisins and place in a glazed pot
2. Add one coffee cup of water, four tablespoons of vinegar, half a coffee cup of sugar, and less than one tablespoon of butter
3. Lemon zest is also very good
4. Let this simmer for about 15 minutes
5. Add a little flour so everything is about as thick as a preparation for lemon pie
6. Prepare dough on a baking sheet and bake it halfway first
7. Spread the raisin mixture on the prepared pie crust and bake in the oven
8. When the crust is golden, cover it with beaten and properly sweetened egg white and place in the oven to dry
