## Ingredients

- eight tablespoons cream
- two pieces pressed yeast
- one teaspoon sugar
- four tablespoons flour for starter
- one cup fresh butter
- two tablespoons sugar
- zest grated from half a lemon
- ground flower
- four eggs
- four egg yolks
- two cups sifted flour
- jam for filling

## Directions

1. Heat eight tablespoons of cream; when lukewarm, add two pieces of pressed yeast and one teaspoon sugar and mix well
2. Add four tablespoons flour to make a thin starter dough
3. Put one cup fresh butter in a deep bowl, add two tablespoons sugar, grated zest from half a lemon, and a pinch of ground flower
4. Cream well until foamy
5. Add four eggs one by one, mixing well after each
6. Add four egg yolks the same way, one by one
7. Add the risen starter dough and add a good two cups of sifted flour
8. If there is too much flour, the tarts will be tough; if too little, they will be loose
9. Beat the dough well and let rise, but not in too warm a place
10. When risen, make small thin tarts and place on a buttered baking sheet
11. Brush with beaten egg, place jam on top, let rise and bake
12. Sprinkle with sugar when baked
