## Ingredients

- ripe raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Press ripe raspberries through a sieve
2. Cook the mass with sugar to the sixth degree
3. Add what remained on the sieve during the pressing of the mass
4. Cook again with constant stirring until completely thick
5. Pour onto slate tablets to cool and dry
6. When dry, peel off the thin crust, cut and arrange as desired
