## Ingredients

- 500 grams cooked beef
- brown broth
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- sardines
- egg (beaten)
- breadcrumbs (grated)
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Finely mince the cooked beef
2. Mix with brown broth into which 3 egg yolks have been beaten
3. Season with salt and pepper, add a little chopped parsley and sardines
4. Mix thoroughly and allow to cool
5. Form into cone shapes from the cooled mixture
6. Dip in beaten egg, roll in grated breadcrumbs and fry well
