## Ingredients

- 2 quarts fresh cherries
- 1 pound almonds
- half a small pot of sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- hard roll
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Make dough as for apple strudel
2. Pit 2 quarts of fine cherries
3. Place them in a small pan and let them cook a little
4. Strain the juice and let them cool
5. Roughly pound 1 pound of almonds
6. Add half a small pot of sugar and grated zest of half a lemon
7. Grate hard roll and mix everything together
8. Spread a cloth and sprinkle with a little flour
9. Roll out the dough thinly
10. Brush with melted butter
11. Place the cherries on it
12. Sprinkle with the nut mixture
13. Lift the cloth and roll up the strudel
14. Place in a pan or baking dish greased with butter
15. Brush the strudel on top with butter
16. Let it bake for a good half hour
17. Remove to a platter and sprinkle with sugar
18. You can also use fresh whole cherries
