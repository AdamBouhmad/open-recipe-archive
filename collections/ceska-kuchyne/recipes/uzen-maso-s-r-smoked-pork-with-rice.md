## Ingredients

- One pound cooked smoked meat
- Half pound washed rice
- Butter
- Beef broth
- Fresh or dried mushrooms
- Flour

## Directions

1. Chop one pound cooked smoked meat into small pieces
2. Cook half pound washed rice in broth strained from the meat
3. Grease a mold with butter
4. Layer rice, then smoked meat, then rice again, alternating
5. Place rice on top, add pieces of butter
6. Pour with a spoon of beef broth
7. Bake in an oven for half an hour
8. If fresh mushrooms are available, stew them in butter, add to the mixture and sprinkle with flour; if using dried mushrooms, boil them in beef broth, chop and add to the smoked meat
