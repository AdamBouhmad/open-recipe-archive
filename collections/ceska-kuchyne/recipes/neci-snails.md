## Ingredients

- snails with shells
- beef broth or salted water
- 1/8 kilogram of butter
- 1/8 kilogram of sardines
- garlic, salt
- marjoram leaves
- grated roll
- broth from cooking the snails

## Directions

1. Wash the snails with their shells and cook in beef broth or salted water.
2. When the shells open, remove the snails with a fork or knife and cut off the tails.
3. Finely chop the snails.
4. Add 1/8 kilogram of butter and the same amount of sardines which have been boned.
5. Grind the sardines finely with garlic that has been rubbed with salt, marjoram leaves and grated roll.
6. Combine with some of the broth in which the snails were cooked.
7. Pour the mixture onto a baking sheet, bake in an oven and serve on a plate.
8. If the shells are kept and cleaned, they can later be filled with just the filling without snails.
