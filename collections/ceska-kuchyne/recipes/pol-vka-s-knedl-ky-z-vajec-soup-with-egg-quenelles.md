## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- equal amount of cream or milk
- 1 tablespoon fresh butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put the egg whites in a cup and pour over them as much cream or milk as there is egg white
2. Put a tablespoon of good fresh butter on a saucepan and when hot, pour the egg whites with cream into it and scramble while stirring constantly
3. Remove from heat and when cooled, add three egg yolks, a little salt, nutmeg, and pepper
4. Mix well and spoon small quenelles into boiling strained broth with a small spoon
5. Let simmer two or three minutes, pour into a soup bowl and serve
