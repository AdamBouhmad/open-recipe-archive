## Ingredients

- Flour, 4 koflíky
- Eggs, 4 whole
- Salt
- Cheese, grated or breadcrumb crust
- Butter, hot

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board and make a well in the center
2. Add 4 whole eggs and mix in the flour with a knife
3. Then knead well by hand
4. Divide the kneaded dough into several pieces
5. Roll each piece thin with a roller, but not too thin
6. Place the rolled pieces on a clean cloth and let dry a little
7. Cut into thick noodles
8. Boil in salted boiling water, let cook for a while
9. Drain the water, place noodles on a dish
10. Sprinkle with grated cheese or breadcrumb crust from white bread
11. Pour hot melted butter over them
