## Ingredients

- 250 grams rendered pork lard
- 4 onions, finely chopped
- 250 grams internal pork fat, diced
- 250 grams breadcrumbs, diced
- Cream
- 1 liter pork blood
- Half liter cream
- Marjoram
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Bay leaf
- Nutmeg
- Intestines

## Directions

1. Heat the rendered pork lard and fry the finely chopped onions in it
2. Add the diced internal fat and the breadcrumbs softened with cream
3. Mix everything with 1 liter pork blood and half liter cream
4. Season with marjoram, pepper, cloves, ginger, bay leaf, and nutmeg
5. Mix well and stuff into intestines
6. Boil for quarter hour
7. Bake the intestines
8. Serve with grated radish
