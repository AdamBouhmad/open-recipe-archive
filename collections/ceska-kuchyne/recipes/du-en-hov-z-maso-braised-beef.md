## Ingredients

- beef
- beef fat
- onion
- vegetables
- salt
- pepper (3 grains)
- beef broth
- 3 ladles flour
- eggs
- cabbage
- kohlrabi
- pickled cucumbers
- other vegetables as desired

## Directions

1. Trim all fat from the meat and cut into pieces
2. Cook the meat and cut the fat into cubes
3. Add onion and all chopped vegetables, salt with 3 grains of pepper and braise until brown
4. To prevent burning, baste with beef broth
5. Mix in 3 ladles of flour, pour in more broth and simmer
6. Cut the cooked beef into portions and place on a baking sheet
7. Strain the sauce through a sieve, braise for a quarter hour
8. Place on a platter, pour sauce over it
9. Create a wreath around the meat made from eggs, vegetable marrow, cabbage, kohlrabi, pickled cucumbers, water vegetables or any other vegetables as desired
