## Ingredients

- bread rolls
- sugar
- salt
- milk
- egg yolks
- whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cut two bread rolls into thin slices, sprinkle with sugar, let them dry brown in the oven and put them in a dish with a bit of salt
2. Boil two quarts of good milk, add four beaten egg yolks or two whole eggs, mix well (but it must not boil anymore) and pour into the dish with the dried bread roll
