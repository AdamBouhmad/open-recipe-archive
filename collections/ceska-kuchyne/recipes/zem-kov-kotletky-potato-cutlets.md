## Ingredients

- good floury potatoes
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) grated cooked potato
- 75 grams (5 lots) creamed butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg for coating
- grated bread crumbs
- butter for frying
- stewed meat and various sauces

## Directions

1. Cook and grate good floury potatoes
2. Mix 24 dekagrams of the grated potato with 75 grams of creamed butter, 3 egg yolks, salt, and nutmeg
3. Form the mixture into cutlets the thickness of a finger
4. Brush with egg
5. Coat with grated bread crumbs
6. Fry in butter on both sides
7. Serve with stewed meat and various sauces
