## Ingredients

- two small cups white sugar
- half a small cup sweet cream
- one teaspoon butter
- shredded coconut to taste
- one teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Bring two small cups of white sugar to a boil with half a small cup of sweet cream and one teaspoon of butter.
2. Let boil for 15 minutes, then stir in shredded coconut to taste.
3. Mix in one teaspoon of vanilla.
4. Pour onto a plate and when cooled, cut into squares.
