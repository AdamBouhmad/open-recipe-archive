## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- fish broth
- two tablespoons mustard
- one tablespoon Provençal oil
- chopped parsley
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Rub butter with flour and fish broth
2. Add two tablespoons of mustard, one tablespoon of Provençal oil, and chopped parsley
3. Add juice from one lemon
4. Simmer over moderate heat
5. If needed, thin with fish broth
