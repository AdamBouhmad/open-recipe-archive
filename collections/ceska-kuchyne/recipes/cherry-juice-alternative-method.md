## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. From cherries previously crushed and stored in a container for several days, press out all the juice.
2. Pour into bottles, add plenty of sugar, crushed cinnamon and cloves.
3. Seal the bottle and shake the juice vigorously so the sugar dissolves more easily.
4. Let the bottles with juice stand quietly for at least eight days, then strain the juice through thick, clean cloth, pour again into clean bottles which are firmly sealed and wrapped, then store in a cool place.
