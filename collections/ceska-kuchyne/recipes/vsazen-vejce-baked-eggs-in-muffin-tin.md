## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place butter in a muffin tin and heat it.
2. When hot, break an egg into each cup.
3. Salt the eggs.
4. Leave them in the oven until the yolks are set.
5. Do not allow them to overcook, as they will not be tasty.
6. Usually served on lentils or with beefsteaks.
