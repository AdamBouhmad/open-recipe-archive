## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- oil
- vinegar
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- sardines
- olives

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the cooked potatoes
2. Mix with oil, vinegar, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper
3. Slice the sardines and add them along with olives that were pickled in vinegar
4. Alternatively, mix potatoes with parsley, chopped onion, sliced hard-boiled eggs, and sardines
