## Ingredients

- Beautiful green peaches
- Sugar
- Soft water

## Directions

1. Select beautiful green peaches, prick them in several places with a needle, place them in boiling water, leave them for a while, remove them, clean them and remove the fuzz.
2. Then place them in soft water in which they cook until soft, remove them, place in cold water to cool.
3. When dried, pour thin dissolved sugar over them.
4. For three days, drain the sugar and each time boil it thicker and thicker, also cooking it with the peaches, until on the sixth day they are completely candied and dry, then place them in containers.
