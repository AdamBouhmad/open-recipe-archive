## Ingredients

- lemon peel
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- flour
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 liter of sour cream over lemon peel
2. Cover and set aside
3. Mix a spoonful of flour with four egg yolks and cold sour cream
4. Add prepared sour cream with lemon peel
5. Sweeten with 9 dekagrams of sugar
6. Stir sauce over fire until it begins to boil
7. Strain
