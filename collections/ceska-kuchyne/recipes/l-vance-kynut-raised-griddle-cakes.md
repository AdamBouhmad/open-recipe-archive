## Ingredients

- two cups good milk
- three whole eggs
- one teaspoon ground sugar
- a piece of melted butter
- two or three tablespoons of leaven from dry yeast, or half a piece of pressed yeast
- salt
- flour
- melted butter for serving
- plum jam or cottage cheese
- ground cinnamon and sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three whole eggs into two cups of milk, add one teaspoon of ground sugar and a piece of melted butter
2. Add two or three tablespoons of leaven from dry yeast, or half a piece of pressed yeast, salt and mix well
3. Then add enough flour to make a thick batter and let it rise
4. Grease the griddle with melted butter, take batter with a scoop, pour the pancakes on it and bake on both sides until golden
5. Spread each pancake with melted butter, brush with plum jam, sprinkle with grated cottage cheese or with ground and sugar-mixed cinnamon and butter, and sprinkle with melted butter
6. You can also sprinkle them with ground cinnamon and sugar
