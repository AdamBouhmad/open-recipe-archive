## Ingredients

- turtle
- butter
- onion
- flour
- beef broth
- wine
- pepper
- lemon juice
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- truffles
- small mushroom heads

## Directions

1. Butcher the turtle as indicated in the recipe for turtle soup
2. Remove the upper shell, take out the meat, remove the bile and entrails, and cut the meat into pieces
3. Let finely grated onion foam in butter
4. Add a tablespoon of flour to it, make a pale roux, pour a little beef broth over it, add a glass of wine, a little ground pepper, juice of two lemons, a piece of green parsley and juice of one lemon and let it simmer into a smooth sauce
5. Place the cut turtle meat in this sauce and stew it, then remove it to a platter, pour the strained sauce over it and garnish with fried breadcrumbs cut into noodles
6. You can also let truffles soaked in wine and small mushroom heads simmer in the sauce, with which the turtle is then garnished
