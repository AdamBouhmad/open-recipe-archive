## Ingredients

- Farina, 2 koflíky
- Lard, 2 tablespoons
- Salt
- Water, boiling
- Eggs, 2 whole
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Place 2 koflíky farina in a bowl
2. Heat 2 tablespoons lard in a pan and pour it over the farina
3. Add salt and pour enough boiling water so that when mixed well it becomes like soft dough
4. Let cool
5. Add 2 whole eggs and mix well
6. Dip a spoon in water and shape large quennelles from the dough
7. Drop into boiling salted water
8. When cooked, drain them well
9. Fry them in butter until brown
10. Serve hot at the table
