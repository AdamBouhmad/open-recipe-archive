## Ingredients

- cleaned, ripe redcurrant berries
- sugar, cooked to thickness

## Directions

1. Place cleaned ripe redcurrant berries into boiled sugar when it is at its peak boiling.
2. Skim the foam, pour everything into a pitcher, cover with paper, and let sit undisturbed overnight.
3. On the second day, drain the syrup and boil until thickened.
4. Add the berries to it, stir until the liquid becomes completely thick.
5. Then place in containers; when cooled, seal with a bladder.
