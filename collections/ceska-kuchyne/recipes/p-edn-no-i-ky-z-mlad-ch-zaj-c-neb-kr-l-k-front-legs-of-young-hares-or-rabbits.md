## Ingredients

- Hare or rabbit shoulders
- Salt
- Bacon
- Onion, finely chopped
- Whole peppers
- Chopped bacon
- Bread crust
- Bread

## Directions

1. Wash the hare or rabbit shoulders, make an incision above the elbow and skewer them
2. Salt them, lard with bacon and place on a baking pan with finely chopped onion, several whole peppers, and chopped bacon
3. Moisten, braise for a while and then bake in the oven until almost pink
4. Fry grated bread crust, let it steam for a while and partly sprinkle the meat with it
5. Pour with strained sauce and partly garnish with bread around it
