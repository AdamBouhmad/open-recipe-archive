## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- ground pepper
- butter
- bay leaves
- sour cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the carp and cut into pieces, sprinkle with salt and ground pepper.
2. Grease a baking pan with butter and place two bay leaves in it.
3. Arrange the cut carp in the pan.
4. Pour a little good sour cream over it, add juice from one lemon.
5. Bake in the oven until golden, frequently basting with sour cream.
6. Carefully remove from the pan onto a dish, strain the sauce and serve in a small bowl.
