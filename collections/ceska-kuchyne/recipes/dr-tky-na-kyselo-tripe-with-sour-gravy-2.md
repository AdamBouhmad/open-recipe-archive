## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cooked tripe
- 2 cups water
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1 onion
- few grains of allspice
- 4 cloves
- garlic
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Cut the 2 pounds of cooked tender tripe, place in a pot and pour 2 cups of water and 1/2 cup vinegar over it. Add one finely chopped onion, a few grains of allspice, 4 cloves and a clove of garlic crushed with salt. Let simmer.
2. Make a roux from 2 tablespoons of butter; when melted, add 1 tablespoon of flour.
