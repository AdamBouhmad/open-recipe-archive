## Ingredients

- two pairs of livers and breasts from chickens
- half and one ounce of beef fat
- ten cleaned shelled crawfish tails
- two beaten eggs
- half a grated roll soaked in cream and squeezed out
- crawfish shells
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- salt
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Chop all the chicken livers, breasts, and crawfish tails finely
2. Add two beaten eggs, half a grated roll soaked in cream and squeezed out, and mix everything well together
3. Make crawfish butter from the crawfish shells, place a spoonful on a plate, add the chopped mixture
4. Add one whole egg and one egg yolk to this, add a bit of salt and beat it well
5. Mix in the other half of the grated roll
6. Make quenelles and cook them in two quarts of beef broth
7. In a small cup, beat six egg yolks, add them to the boiling broth
8. Finish the soup with crawfish butter
