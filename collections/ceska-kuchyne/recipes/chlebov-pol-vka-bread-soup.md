## Ingredients

- 1 slice black bread
- 1 slice white bread
- water
- lemon peel
- sugar
- raisins
- salt
- 1 egg yolk
- wine

## Directions

1. Cut a slice of black bread and a slice of white bread into a pot filled with water
2. Simmer completely and press through a sieve
3. Add lemon peel, sugar, and raisins
4. Season with salt
5. Simmer for a quarter hour
6. Add egg yolk whisked with wine
