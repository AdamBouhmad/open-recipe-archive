## Ingredients

- dough (as for apple strudel)
- one cup nice cleaned rice
- one quart milk
- two tablespoons butter
- salt
- five egg yolks
- sugar
- vanilla
- three egg whites
- melted butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make dough as for the apple strudel
2. Cook one cup of nice cleaned rice in one quart of milk until soft and let it cool
3. Beat two tablespoons of butter, add to the cooled rice, salt it, add five egg yolks, a little sugar with vanilla and mix well
4. Slowly fold in stiff snow from three egg whites
5. Stretch the dough on a cloth, spread with the rice filling, lift one side of the cloth and roll up the strudel
6. Grease a baking dish with butter, place the strudel in it, brush the surface with melted butter and bake
7. Turn out onto a dish, cut and sprinkle with sugar
