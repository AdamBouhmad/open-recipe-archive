## Ingredients

- 2 liters water
- A little caraway
- A piece of butter
- A little salt
- Children's semolina
- A little chopped fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Put a little caraway, a piece of butter, and a little salt into two liters of water
2. After boiling, strain the soup
3. Meanwhile, roast the children's semolina until brown
4. When the soup is boiling, add the semolina and a little chopped green parsley
