## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 4 tablespoons farina
- salt
- 2 tablespoons fresh butter

## Directions

1. Heat 2 cups milk and when simmering, add 4 tablespoons farina
2. Let cook for about 2 minutes while stirring constantly
3. Season with salt, remove from heat, and let cool
4. Add 2 tablespoons fresh butter and beat well
