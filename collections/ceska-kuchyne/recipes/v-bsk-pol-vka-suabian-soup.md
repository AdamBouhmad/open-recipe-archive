## Ingredients

- good homemade bread, sliced thin
- beef soup
- salt
- pepper
- roasted sausages
- chives, chopped finely

## Directions

1. Slice good homemade bread thinly and dry the slices either on a clean sheet or in a pan until yellow or light brown
2. About half an hour before serving, place them in a pot, pour clear beef soup over them, salt it, pepper it lightly and let it cook for a while
3. Meanwhile cut roasted sausages into pieces, place them in a bowl, pour the soup over them and add a little finely chopped chives
