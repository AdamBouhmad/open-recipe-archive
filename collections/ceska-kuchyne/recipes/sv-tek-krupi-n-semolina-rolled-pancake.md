## Ingredients

- 75 grams butter
- salt
- flower
- 6 egg yolks
- semolina
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream 75 grams of butter with salt, flower and 6 egg yolks.
2. Add as much semolina as it will take.
3. Make stiff snow from 6 egg whites and fold it in.
4. Grease a baking sheet with butter, pour the batter onto it and bake.
5. Remove the baked rolled pancake and let it cool.
