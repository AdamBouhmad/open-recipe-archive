## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of fresh butter
- two or three eggs
- several spoonfuls of sour cream
- quarter pound of peeled and sliced almonds
- a little ground flowers or herbs
- small piece of lemon rind, finely chopped
- salt
- sugar
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Beat two spoonfuls of fresh butter, crack two or three eggs into it
2. Pour in several spoonfuls of sour cream, a quarter pound of peeled and sliced almonds, a little ground flowers or herbs, a small piece of finely chopped lemon rind
3. Salt it, and if you want the stuffing slightly sweetened, add one spoonful of sugar
4. Add enough grated bread to make a sufficiently thick stuffing
5. Fill the cavity of a well-cleaned and eviscerated turkey with this, tie it up, salt it
6. Place the turkey on a roasting pan, pour several spoonfuls of broth underneath
7. Place pieces of butter on it and let it slowly roast on both sides until golden
8. However, you must frequently baste it with fresh butter
9. Then cut it, arrange neatly on a platter and pour over it the juices in which the turkey was roasted
