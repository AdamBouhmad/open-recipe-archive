## Ingredients

- Hind leg or shoulder of game
- Salt
- 8 juniper berries
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Ginger
- Lemon peel
- Whole pepper
- New spice
- 3 onions
- Celery
- Parsley root
- Carrot
- Wine vinegar
- Water
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Scrape the hind leg or shoulder clean with a sharp knife
2. Salt it and beat well with a wooden mallet
3. Add 8 juniper berries, thyme, bay leaf, ginger, lemon peel, whole pepper, new spice, and 3 onions sliced into rounds, celery, parsley root and carrot as needed, and vinegar mixed with water
4. Turn the leg from time to time
5. When ready to roast, pour off half the liquid and roast in the remaining liquid until almost all the juices are cooked out
6. Remove the upper skin from the roast, slice neatly, strain the remaining sauce and garnish with lemon; something sour should generally accompany this roast
7. Note: For the game to be completely clean, it must first be singed and scraped well with a sharp knife
