## Ingredients

- potatoes in their skins
- well-soaked herring
- pickled cucumbers
- several tablespoons of capers
- finely chopped onion
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in their skins
2. Peel and cut into slices
3. Soak herring well, remove bones and skin, and cut into slices
4. Cut pickled cucumbers into slices
5. Mix potatoes with several tablespoons of capers, finely chopped onion, oil, salt, pepper and vinegar
