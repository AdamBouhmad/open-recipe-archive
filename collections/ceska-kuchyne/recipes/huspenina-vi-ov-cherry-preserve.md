## Ingredients

- cherries
- bitter almonds
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the pits from the cherries
2. Crush the cherries with several bitter almonds in a mortar
3. Let the mixture rest for four to five hours in a stone pot
4. Press through a clean cloth to extract the juice
5. Sweeten the juice well and place on ice to cool
6. Clove or other flavorings may be added to this preserve if desired
