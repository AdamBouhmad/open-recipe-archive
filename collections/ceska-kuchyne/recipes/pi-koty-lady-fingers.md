## Ingredients

- half pound fine granulated sugar
- eight egg yolks
- eight egg whites
- two bowls sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put half pound of fine granulated sugar in a bowl
2. Add eight egg yolks and beat until foamy
3. Fold in stiff meringue from eight egg whites and two bowls of sifted flour
4. Pour batter into small molds dusted with flour, or if you do not have these, pour onto paper into finger-length pieces
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake in a moderately warm oven
