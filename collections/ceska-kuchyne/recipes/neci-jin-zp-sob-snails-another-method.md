## Ingredients

- snails with shells
- beef broth or salted water
- 1/4 kilogram of butter
- more than 1/2 kilogram of sardines
- garlic in salt
- marjoram leaves
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- grated roll
- broth from cooking the snails

## Directions

1. Wash the snails with their shells and cook in beef broth or salted water.
2. When the shells open, remove the snails with a fork or knife and cut off the tails.
3. Clean the shell interiors well with salt, cook out, place opening side down to drain, and dry with a clean cloth.
4. For 24 snails, use 1/4 kilogram of butter and slightly more than 1/2 kilogram of sardines.
5. Remove all bones from the sardines.
6. First grind the sardines alone, then together with the butter.
7. Add either 6 smaller or 4 larger pieces of garlic rubbed in salt, a pinch of well-ground marjoram leaves, a little ground pepper, nutmeg and enough grated roll so the filling does not run, and pour in a little of the broth in which the snails were cooked.
8. Into each shell place first a piece of filling, then a snail, then filling again; finally add grated roll and press down with a knife.
9. Place the shells opening side up next to each other on a baking sheet, pour broth over them and bake slowly in an oven.
10. Arrange neatly on a serving dish and cover with a cloth.
