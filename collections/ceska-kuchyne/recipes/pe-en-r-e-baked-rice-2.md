## Ingredients

- 1 small cup rice
- 3 pints milk
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- salt
- sweet cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse a small cup of rice, put it in a deep dish and pour three pints of milk over it
2. Add four tablespoons of sugar and a little salt
3. Bake for three hours at moderate temperature
4. As the milk bakes down, replenish it with boiling milk kept ready on the stove
5. Serve with sweet cream and sugar
