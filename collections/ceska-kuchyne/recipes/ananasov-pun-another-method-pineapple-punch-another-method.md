## Ingredients

- 1-2 ripe pineapples
- Sugar
- 1 kilogram sugar
- Wine (white)
- Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Cut 1-2 completely ripe pineapples into slices
2. Heavily sugar them and place on a plate
3. Let them rest peacefully for 24 hours
4. Dissolve 1 kilogram of sugar in wine and pour over
5. Add several bottles of white wine
6. When ready to serve, add 1 bottle of champagne wine
7. Mix everything and serve on the table
