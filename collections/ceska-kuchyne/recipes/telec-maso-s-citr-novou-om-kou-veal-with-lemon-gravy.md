## Ingredients

- twenty-five veal shoulder slices, about a finger thick
- beef broth
- one tablespoon fresh butter
- two tablespoons flour
- juice of half a lemon
- lemon rind, finely chopped
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut veal shoulder into slices about a finger thick and place in a pan
2. Pour beef broth over them and let simmer for a good half hour
3. Heat one tablespoon of fresh butter in a pan, add two tablespoons of flour and make a pale roux
4. Thin the roux with the beef broth in which you cooked the slices
5. Place the cooked slices in this gravy
6. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon into it, add finely chopped lemon rind and a little nutmeg
7. Let the slices cook until tender and serve
