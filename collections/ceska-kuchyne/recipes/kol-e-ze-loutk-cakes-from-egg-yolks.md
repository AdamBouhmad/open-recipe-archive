## Ingredients

- 7 hard egg yolks
- butter
- 5 fresh egg yolks
- sugar
- flour
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Crush seven hard egg yolks, rub butter with them
2. Add 5 fresh egg yolks, the same amount of sugar and flour, finely chopped lemon peel
3. Form small cakes from the mixture
4. Bake on a floured, bread-crumb-covered baking sheet
5. Sprinkle the baked cakes with sugar
