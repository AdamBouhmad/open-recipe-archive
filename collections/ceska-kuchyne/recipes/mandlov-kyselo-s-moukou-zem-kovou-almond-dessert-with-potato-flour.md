## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 1.5 litres water
- 24 decagrams (16 lots) sugar
- 1/4 litre potato flour
- fruit juice

## Directions

1. Parboil sweet and bitter almonds, grind them
2. Mix in 1.5 litres of water and strain
3. Mix some almond milk with the ground almonds and strain again
4. Boil with 24 decagrams (16 lots) of sugar and 1/4 litre of potato flour mixed in water until a thick paste forms
5. When cool, serve with fruit juice
