## Ingredients

- 2 raw egg yolks
- oil
- 5-6 tablespoons wine vinegar
- meat jelly
- beef broth
- pepper
- 5-6 tablespoons sour cream
- potatoes
- hot broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat together the 2 raw egg yolks, oil which is poured in slowly, 5-6 tablespoons wine vinegar, meat jelly, beef broth, pepper and 5-6 tablespoons sour cream to make a sauce
2. The sauce may be chilled on ice
3. Boil the potatoes, slice them into thin slices and pour hot broth over them
4. Pour half the sauce over the potatoes
5. Arrange them with butter and add the remaining sauce
