## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons sweet baking powder
- 2 cups sugar
- jelly for filling

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites into stiff snow.
2. Mix in 2 cups flour and 2 teaspoons sweet baking powder.
3. In another bowl, beat the yolks well with 2 cups sugar.
4. Combine both mixtures together and mix well.
5. Divide among six metal plates and bake.
6. When cooled, layer with jelly. The most delicious jelly is lemon jelly, made as follows: Take 3 eggs, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup butter, 3 lemons (zest and juice), and cook while stirring constantly until it thickens.
