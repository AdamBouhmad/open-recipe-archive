## Ingredients

- leftover plain dumplings
- butter
- 3 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut leftover plain dumplings into slices
2. Place a piece of butter underneath
3. On a baking sheet, stir constantly and bake well
4. When baked, beat about 3 eggs
5. Stir for a while with the eggs
6. Can be served as a standalone dish or added to beef
