## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 3 small cups sugar
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of 3 eggs into nice stiff snow
2. Slowly add 3 small cups of sugar while stirring constantly
3. Stir a little more
4. Then fold in grated chocolate to taste
