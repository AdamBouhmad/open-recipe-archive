## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram white flour
- 120 grams ground sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 egg yolks
- thick sweet cream as needed

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, butter, egg yolks, and enough sweet cream to form batter like cake batter
2. Oil wafer iron
3. Make wafers and bake on both sides until golden
4. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon
5. Place second wafer on top in form, press down, and hold briefly over heat
6. Store in dry place for long preservation
