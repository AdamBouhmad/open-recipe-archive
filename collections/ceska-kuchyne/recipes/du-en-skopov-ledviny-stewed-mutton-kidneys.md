## Ingredients

- mutton kidneys
- salted water
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion
- hot water
- gravy from roasted meat
- red wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Select nice mutton kidneys and cook them for an hour in salted water, then remove the skin and cut them in half lengthwise, then into thin strips.
2. Mix several tablespoons of flour with a little salt and ground pepper and coat the cooked cut kidneys in it.
3. Melt a piece of fresh butter in a pan, add a little finely chopped onion, place the kidneys coated in flour into it, and let them stew for ten minutes, stirring several times.
4. Squeeze the juice of one lemon into it, add hot water or, if available, a little gravy from roasted meat and one bowl of red wine, and let it simmer a few more minutes until thickened, but it must not boil.
