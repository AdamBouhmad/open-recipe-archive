## Ingredients

- trout
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- oyster sauce or caper sauce or anchovy sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the trout and remove the bones
2. Cut into pieces, dip in beaten egg, coat with grated breadcrumbs
3. Fry in butter
4. Serve with oyster, caper, anchovy, or tomato sauces
