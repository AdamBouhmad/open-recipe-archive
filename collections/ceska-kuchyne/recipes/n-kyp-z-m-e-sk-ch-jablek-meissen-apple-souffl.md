## Ingredients

- Meissen apples
- 8 egg whites
- sugar
- vanilla
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Peel the Meissen apples, remove the seeds and stems, arrange them on a platter in the shape of a pine cone
2. Make stiff snow from 8 egg whites, beat sugar with vanilla; for each egg yolk calculate one spoon of sugar, which is slowly folded into the snow
3. Brush the apples thickly with the snow, place them on a plate in the oven and bake
4. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with vanilla or sugar mixed with cinnamon
