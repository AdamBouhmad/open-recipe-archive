## Ingredients

- several oranges
- sugar
- orange zest
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Grate oranges onto sugar
2. Finely chop orange zest
3. Add orange juice
4. Make an icing with ground sugar
5. Spread onto wafers
6. Bake slowly as with other wafer cookies
