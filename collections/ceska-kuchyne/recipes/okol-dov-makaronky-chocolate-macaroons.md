## Ingredients

- macaroon mixture as in previous recipe
- three ounces melted fine chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare the macaroon mixture as in the previous recipe and stir in three ounces of melted fine chocolate.
2. Alternatively, you can stir in one ounce of finely pounded cinnamon instead of chocolate.
3. Instead of lemon zest, you can pound the sugar with a piece of vanilla for the macaroons.
