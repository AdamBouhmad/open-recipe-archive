## Ingredients

- lamb brisket
- stuffing (same as for veal brisket)
- salt
- white ground pepper
- onion
- carrot
- fresh melted butter or lard
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb brisket well and dry it
2. Carefully trim the breast and fill with the same stuffing as used for veal brisket
3. Sew it closed
4. Salt on both sides and sprinkle with white ground pepper
5. Place diced onion and carrot in the roasting pan and put the brisket on top
6. Pour fresh melted butter or lard over it
7. Add a little beef broth or water
8. Roast for about one hour with frequent basting with the meat juice
9. Remove the brisket to a platter
10. Let the gravy simmer with a little beef broth, remove all fat and pour it over the roasted meat
