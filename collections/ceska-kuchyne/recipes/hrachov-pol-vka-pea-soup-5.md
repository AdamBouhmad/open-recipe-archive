## Ingredients

- one pot of peas (for six persons)
- cold water
- beef broth
- a bit of chopped marjoram
- a bit of fresh parsley
- two raw potatoes, grated
- light roux
- fried white bread, cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Take one pot of peas for six persons
2. Let it soak overnight in cold water
3. The next day cook it until soft
4. Mash it and strain it into a pot
5. Pour good beef broth over it
6. Add a bit of chopped marjoram, a bit of green parsley, two grated raw potatoes
7. Let it cook
8. Meanwhile make a light roux, darken the broth with it and pour it into a bowl over fried white bread cut into pieces
