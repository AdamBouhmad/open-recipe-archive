## Ingredients

- live carp
- vinegar
- water
- onion
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- bay leaf
- thyme
- allspice
- pepper
- ginger
- fish bladder (optional)

## Directions

1. Kill and clean the carp without scraping scales, removing all blood
2. Cut into neat pieces and arrange on a baking dish
3. Salt the pieces
4. Heat vinegar mixed with water until boiling and pour over the carp
5. In a separate baking dish, prepare vinegar and water with sliced onion, carrot cut into slices, parsley and celery, bay leaf and thyme, allspice, pepper, and a piece of whole ginger
6. Arrange the fish neatly in this mixture
7. Cover and decorate around with the vegetables that were cooked together
8. Optionally add a piece of fish bladder to help it set
9. Strain the mixture over the fish and place on ice to chill
