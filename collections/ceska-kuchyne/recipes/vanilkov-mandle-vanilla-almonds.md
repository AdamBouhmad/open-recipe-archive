## Ingredients

- fine large almonds
- refined sugar
- vanilla, finely crushed
- water

## Directions

1. Clean fine large almonds
2. Boil in soft water
3. Remove the skin and dry on a sieve until completely dry
4. Dissolve pure refined sugar in water
5. As described for white sugared almonds, pour warm sugar onto the almonds and stir
6. When partially coated with sugar, add a little finely crushed vanilla mixed with boiled sugar
7. Continue until almonds are completely coated with sugar
