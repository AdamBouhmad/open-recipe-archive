## Ingredients

- Pork offal (liver, heart, lungs, kidneys)
- Salt
- Onion
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Allspice
- Pepper
- Lemon zest
- Vinegar
- Water
- Flour
- Sugar or gingerbread
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Rinse the offal that was removed before roasting, salt it, and place on a baking sheet.
2. Chop onion and add thyme, bay leaf, allspice, pepper, and a piece of lemon zest.
3. Cook in vinegar diluted with water.
4. When the offal is cooked, remove it and either leave it whole or cut it into pieces.
5. Add flour to the sauce and cook either with caramelized sugar or gingerbread.
6. Strain the sauce over the offal, and if not sweet enough, add sugar.
7. Potatoes may be added to the sauce.
8. The liver can be used to make liver dumplings: grate the liver, add half a grated roll, 1 egg, 15 grams (1 lot) of butter, salt, a pinch of flowers, a little pepper, and marjoram; form small dumplings with a spoon and cook in the soup; if too thick, add more roll.
