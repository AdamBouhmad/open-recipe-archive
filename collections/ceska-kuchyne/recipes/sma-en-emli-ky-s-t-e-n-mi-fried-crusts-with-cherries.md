## Ingredients

- small rolls or banduska
- cherries or sour cherries
- sugar
- thin lemon peel
- red wine

## Directions

1. From small rolls or banduska cut off the top, remove the interior and fry them in heated rendered butter
2. From cherries or sour cherries remove the pits and stew them with sugar and fine lemon peel
3. Fill the rolls with this stewed filling, cover them with the cut-off pieces, arrange them in a dish, pour red wine over them and let them bake in the oven
4. Then dust them with sugar and serve
5. Instead of cherries you can also use other fruit, or you can fill the rolls with various marmalade
