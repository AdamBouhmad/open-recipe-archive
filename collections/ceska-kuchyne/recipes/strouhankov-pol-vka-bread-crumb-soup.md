## Ingredients

- 2-3 eggs
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Make firm dough from eggs as for noodles and let it dry
2. Grate the dried dough and let the bread crumbs dry
3. Add the bread crumbs to the soup or fry them in butter first
