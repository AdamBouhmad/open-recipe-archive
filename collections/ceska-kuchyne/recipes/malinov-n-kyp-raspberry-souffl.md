## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 24 dekagrams crushed sugar
- lemon oil
- 3/4 kilogram grated cooked potatoes
- snow from 8 egg whites
- 1 liter sour cream foam
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 1/2 liter preserved raspberries
- 15 grams (1 lot) dissolved gelatin
- fresh raspberries

## Directions

1. Scald 24 dekagrams almonds, peel them, dry them, and crush them.
2. Beat with 8 egg yolks, 24 dekagrams crushed sugar, and lemon oil for 1 1/2 hours.
3. Add 3/4 kilogram grated cooked potatoes.
4. Slowly fold in snow from 8 egg whites.
5. Bake two cakes the thickness of a finger from the mixture.
6. When cooled, place one cake in a soufflé mold.
7. Brush with raspberry sauce made from 1 liter sour cream foam, 9 dekagrams sugar, 1/2 liter preserved raspberries, and 15 grams dissolved gelatin.
8. Put half the sauce on the bottom cake, brush the top cake with the other half, and place on top.
9. Place on a cold place and decorate with fresh raspberries.
