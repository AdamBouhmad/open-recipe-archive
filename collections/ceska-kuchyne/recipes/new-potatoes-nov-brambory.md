## Ingredients

- young potatoes
- fresh water
- salt
- caraway
- fresh parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Scrape young potatoes and wash them well in cold water
2. Place them in a pot and pour fresh water over them
3. Add salt, caraway, and a piece of green parsley
4. When cooked, strain the water from them
5. Remove the parsley and turn the potatoes out onto a dish
6. Leave them uncovered for about five minutes in the back of the stove
7. Then dress them with fresh hot butter
