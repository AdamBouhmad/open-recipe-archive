## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt water
- 4 calf feet
- vegetables
- onion
- pepper
- spices
- thyme
- bay leaf
- vinegar
- salt
- calf bladder
- parsley
- carrot
- celeriac

## Directions

1. Boil tongue in salt water, peel, cut into slices, and let cool
2. Boil calf feet with all vegetables, especially chopped onion, pepper, spices, thyme, bay leaf, and vinegar, salt to taste
3. If thicker aspic is desired, add calf bladder
4. Strain
5. Line a mold with parsley, carrot cut into stars, parsley, and celeriac
6. Place tongue on this, add more vegetables
7. Pour a little aspic carefully; when set, gradually add more aspic
8. Finally set in ice
