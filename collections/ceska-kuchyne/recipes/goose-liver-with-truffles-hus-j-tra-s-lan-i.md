## Ingredients

- large goose liver
- truffles
- salt
- bacon
- beef broth
- wine
- butter
- butter paste

## Directions

1. Split large goose liver in half, wash and dry it. Pierce with truffles, salt, wrap in bacon, pour broth and a glass of wine over it and braise for an hour in butter. Meanwhile braise the truffles in broth, wine and butter, surround with liver and cover with butter paste.
