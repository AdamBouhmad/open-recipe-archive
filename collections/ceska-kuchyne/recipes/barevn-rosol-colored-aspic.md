## Ingredients

- aspic
- spices
- roasted meat
- liver
- burned sugar
- beet juice

## Directions

1. For light aspic, do not add spices or sugar
2. For dark aspic, cook spices, roasted meat and liver with more or less burned sugar into the liquid
3. For red aspic, add beet juice to pale aspic or dark aspic as needed for color
