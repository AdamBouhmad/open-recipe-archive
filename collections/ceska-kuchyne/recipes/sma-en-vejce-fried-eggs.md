## Ingredients

- three tablespoons lard
- one tablespoon good butter
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a clean and smooth saucepan and melt three tablespoons of lard with one tablespoon of good butter
2. When the fat sizzles, break one egg after another into it
3. If you are not sure that all eggs are fresh, do well to break each one into a cup and only then put it in the saucepan
4. This will protect you from spoiled eggs getting in with the others
5. As the eggs fry, continuously pour the sizzling fat over the surface so that the upper part of the egg is also fried, without loss of neatness, as happens when the egg is turned
6. When the eggs are fried to your liking, remove them with a spoon onto a plate
7. Usually they are made with fried ham, which is fried at the same time in thin slices, which are arranged with the eggs and thus served
