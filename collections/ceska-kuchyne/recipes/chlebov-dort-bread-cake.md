## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- finely chopped orange and lemon zest
- abundant eggs
- roasted bread, finely grated

## Directions

1. Crush the almonds with their skins
2. Add abundant sugar, finely chopped orange and lemon zest, and abundant eggs
3. Mix for at least an hour with finely grated and roasted (to red) bread and beat together
4. Bake in molds in the usual way
