## Ingredients

- potatoes
- flour
- butter or lard
- salt
- poppy seeds
- sugar
- cheese
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape nice potatoes, cut them into larger pieces, place in cold water, wash them well and cook
2. When they are soft, drain half the water, using an inverted masher poke dense holes from top to bottom
3. Pour two cupfuls of white flour into these holes; poke a bit more with the masher and let it cook, but be careful it does not burn
4. After cooking for about a quarter hour, salt it and mix well with the masher so there are no lumps and add a spoonful of butter
5. Dip a spoon in hot melted butter or lard, take the mush piece by piece and place it on a plate
6. Sprinkle with poppy seeds crushed with sugar or grated cheese and grease with hot melted butter or lard
7. If you want the mush finer, drain all the water as soon as the potatoes are soft, replace it with milk, but be careful it does not burn
