## Ingredients

- 18 decagrams (12 lots) butter
- 18 decagrams sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- Some sour cream
- Lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- Chopped almonds
- Sugar and vanilla for topping

## Directions

1. Mix together 18 decagrams butter, 18 decagrams sugar, 3 egg yolks, some sour cream, lemon zest rubbed with sugar and 3 whole eggs
2. Spread the dough evenly on a waxed baking sheet
3. Sprinkle with chopped almonds, sugar and vanilla
4. When dried in the oven for a while, cut into long pieces and bake
