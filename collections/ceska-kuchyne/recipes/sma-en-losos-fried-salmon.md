## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- butter
- grated roll or crackers
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut salmon into slices two inches wide, rinse, salt and sprinkle with pepper
2. Let stand covered for about an hour
3. Dip in melted butter mixed with egg yolks, coat in grated roll or crackers
4. Place on a buttered baking sheet, pour melted butter over them and bake in the oven until golden
5. Arrange on a heated dish with the fallen breadcrumbs and either pour lemon juice over or surround with lemon cut into quarters so that each person can squeeze juice as they like
