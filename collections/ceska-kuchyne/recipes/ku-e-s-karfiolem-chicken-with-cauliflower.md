## Ingredients

- 2 cleaned chickens
- 1 heaping spoon butter
- 1.5 spoons flour
- beef broth or salted water
- clean white cauliflower
- ground flower (seasoning)

## Directions

1. Cook two cleaned chickens well
2. On a griddle, let 1 heaping spoon of butter melt, add 1.5 spoons of flour, make a pale roux and brown the broth in which the chickens were cooked
3. Meanwhile, let clean white cauliflower cook in beef broth or salted water
4. Cut the chickens into neat pieces and place on the griddle
5. Pour the prepared sauce over them, season with ground flower and let simmer briefly
6. Place the chickens with sauce on a platter and surround them with cauliflower
7. Asparagus chickens are prepared the same way
