## Ingredients

- mutton brisket
- salt water
- beef broth
- large cup of rice
- broth from the brisket
- water
- onion
- two cloves
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean a nice mutton brisket well, cut off a small lower bone, let it boil for a quarter hour in salt water, then cool.
2. Now trim the brisket neatly and let it cook slowly in beef broth until tender.
3. Meanwhile, pick over a large cup of nice rice, place it on a shallow pan, pour two cups of broth from the brisket and two cups of water over it, add an onion into which you have pressed two cloves, a piece of butter, salt it.
4. Cover the pan and let the rice braise slowly until it is tender and all the liquid is absorbed into it.
5. Now place the rice on an oblong platter, lay the brisket cut into neat pieces on it, and pour a little of the broth in which you cooked the brisket over it.
