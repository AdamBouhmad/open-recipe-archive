## Ingredients

- carrots, grated
- three and one-half deciliters of beef broth
- sugar
- saffron
- salt
- roux
- parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Grate the whole carrot and squeeze the juice from it
2. Mix the juice with three and one-half deciliters of beef broth
3. Add a piece of sugar, a little saffron and salt
4. Mix in roux
5. Simmer and strain
6. Mix in finely chopped green parsley
