## Ingredients

- pie crust dough
- peaches
- flour
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Line a pan with dough
2. Peel the peaches and halve them
3. Remove the pits and arrange the peaches in the pan to fill it
4. Dust with two tablespoons of flour
5. Sprinkle with a coffee cup of sugar, or more if the peaches are not sweet
6. Pour a coffee cup of thick sweet cream over and bake
