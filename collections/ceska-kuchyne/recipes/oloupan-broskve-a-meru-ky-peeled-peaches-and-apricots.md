## Ingredients

- Nearly ripe peaches or apricots
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Select nearly ripe peaches or apricots, halve them and peel them.
2. Boil them with a little water and cover them to let sit for a while until the water becomes only lukewarm.
3. Remove them and place in fresh water.
4. After about a quarter hour, remove them, place on a sieve to dry, spread on a dish, pour boiled sugar over them, cover with paper and let sit quietly.
5. On the second day, drain the sugar, boil it thick, add the peaches to it, boil everything gently.
6. On the third day, drain the sugar again and boil it until the sixth degree, add the peaches to it and boil everything again; skim off the foam that appears, and when everything cools, place in containers.
