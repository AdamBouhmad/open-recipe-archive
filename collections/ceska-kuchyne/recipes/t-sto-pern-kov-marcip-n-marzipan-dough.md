## Ingredients

- sweet almonds
- bitter almonds
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel sweet almonds, adding some bitter almonds
2. Grind or pound them finely
3. Add an equal amount of sugar that has been rubbed with lemon peel
4. Roast everything on moderately heated baking sheets until it separates from the pan
5. Remove and tear into pieces and let cool
6. Knead together again, shape into desired forms, and bake slowly
