## Ingredients

- Young fresh spinach leaves
- Salt
- Water
- Shallots or young onion greens
- Beef broth
- Pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 hard biscuits
- 0.5 cup thick sour cream

## Directions

1. Pick and clean beautiful young leaves
2. Wash in several waters
3. Place in a pot and pour boiling water over, cover and heat about five minutes
4. Squeeze out moisture, place on a board and add some shallots or young onion greens
5. Chop together finely
6. Cook in a clean pot with about 1 cup beef broth, or water if necessary, covered tightly until completely thickened
7. Season with pepper and salt
8. For each cup of chopped spinach, use 1 rounded tablespoon butter, 2 softened hard biscuits and 0.5 cup thick sour cream mixed in at the end
