## Ingredients

- Peas
- Butter
- Sugar
- Pale roux
- Sweet cream

## Directions

1. Stew the peas in butter with sugar
2. Add pale roux so it becomes quite thick
3. Pour in one and a quarter decilitres of sweet cream and simmer briefly
