## Ingredients

- Venison
- Onion
- Bacon
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Butter dough
- Butter
- Egg yolk
- Flour for roux
- Good meat broth
- Wine vinegar
- Sugar
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Simmer the venison until tender, remove bones, add onion, bacon, cloves, and pepper.
2. Either pound the venison well or cut into pieces.
3. Spread butter on the bottom of pâté dough, add meat and forcemeat.
4. Brush edges with beaten egg, cover with pâté dough, and bake at strong heat with sauce.
5. For the sauce: make a brown roux with butter, add good meat broth and a little wine vinegar, add crushed sugar, chopped onion, crushed pepper and cloves, simmer and strain.
6. Add lemon slices, chopped mushrooms, capers, and a piece of butter, simmer everything together once more, and pour over the pâté through a sieve.
