## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 125 grams crayfish butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- half liter meat broth

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks
2. Mix in a spoonful of flour
3. Add crayfish butter, salt, and nutmeg
4. Add half liter meat broth
5. Mix everything well and cook slowly with constant stirring
6. Pour over prepared vegetables
