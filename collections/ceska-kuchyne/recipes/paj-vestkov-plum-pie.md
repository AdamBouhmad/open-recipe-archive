## Ingredients

- plums
- dough
- two eggs
- three tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Take picked and softly cooked plums and mix them well
2. Line a pie plate with dough
3. Spread with these plum preserves, about half the plate full, and bake
4. When baked, spread with snow from two eggs sweetened with three tablespoons of sugar
5. Return to the oven and leave there until the snow browns
