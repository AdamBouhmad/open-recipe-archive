## Ingredients

- rennet apples
- water
- sugar
- apple puree

## Directions

1. Peel the rennet apples and cut into quarters
2. Remove the seeds
3. Boil with water and sugar until completely soft and yellow
4. Place on a plate, level with a knife and sprinkle with sugar
5. Alternatively: boil the rennet apples separately and sugar separately
6. Let the cooked rennet apples cool on sieves
7. Arrange on dishes and pour over with sugar boiled to the sixth degree, or surround with apple puree
