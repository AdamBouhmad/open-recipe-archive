## Ingredients

- truffles, cleaned and rinsed
- salt
- pepper
- good vinegar or lemon juice
- good Provençal oil

## Directions

1. Peel cleaned and rinsed truffles
2. Cut into very fine slices
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Dress with good vinegar or lemon juice
5. Drizzle with good Provençal oil
6. Mix everything thoroughly again and arrange nicely on a dish
