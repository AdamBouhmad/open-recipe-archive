## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- white flour
- beef broth
- 2 garlic cloves
- butter
- marjoram (optional)

## Directions

1. Make a roux from fresh butter and a little white flour
2. Pour beef broth over it, salt and let it cook
3. Take about two garlic cloves, rub them well with a piece of good butter
4. When ready to serve, put the garlic butter into the sauce and mix well
5. If desired, a little pounded marjoram can be added
