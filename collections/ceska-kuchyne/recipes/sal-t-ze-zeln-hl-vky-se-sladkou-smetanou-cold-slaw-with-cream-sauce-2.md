## Ingredients

- fresh crisp cabbage head
- salt
- onion
- good butter
- vinegar
- sweet cream
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice a nice fresh crisp cabbage head, put the cabbage in a wooden dish, sprinkle with salt, add half an onion finely chopped and pound it with a wooden mallet for 5 minutes
2. Then put it into a dish and pour the following sauce over: In a small pan melt two spoonfuls of good butter
3. When it reddens but before it begins to burn, add a spoonful of vinegar and when it begins to boil, pour a cup of sweet cream into it with constant stirring so it does not curdle and add a pinch of pepper
4. Pour this sauce over the salad
