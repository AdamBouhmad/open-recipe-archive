## Ingredients

- ripe lemons
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut ripe lemons into pieces and squeeze out the juice.
2. Place them in boiling water and cook until soft.
3. When cooled, remove them and prepare them in the same manner as described for candied lemon peel.
