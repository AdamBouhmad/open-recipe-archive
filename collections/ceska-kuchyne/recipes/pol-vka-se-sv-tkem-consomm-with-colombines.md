## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- four egg yolks
- a quarter pound of fine flour
- salt
- four egg whites
- good boiling broth
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Whip a piece of butter, add four egg yolks and a quarter pound of fine flour to it
2. Beat it well, salt it and gradually mix in the snow made from four egg whites
3. Then grease a baking dish with butter and pour the dough onto the heated butter
4. Put it quickly in the oven and let it bake until golden
5. Turn the colombine out onto a board, let it cool, cut it into squares or oblong pieces, put in a dish and pour good boiling broth over it
6. Alternatively, you can beat four egg yolks with a little warm broth, add a little chopped green parsley, mix it into strained boiling broth and pour it into the dish over the colombine
