## Ingredients

- tomatoes of medium size
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash, dry, and salt the tomatoes
2. Let stand for about 4 hours
3. Boil good vinegar and when cooled, pour over the tomatoes and let stand until the next day
4. On the second day, drain the vinegar, boil it again, and when cooled pour over the tomatoes
5. On the third day, boil fresh vinegar
6. Place tomatoes in jars and pour the fresh boiled and cooled vinegar over them until the jars are about two inches from full
7. Stand the jars close together in water so they do not bump each other during cooking, and let them boil in steam for about five minutes
8. Remove jars to table, attach rubber seals, tighten lids well, and return to the same water and let cool completely
9. Stored this way, pickled tomatoes will keep for over two years and remain fresh even if air reaches them
