## Ingredients

- beef or meat broth
- potatoes
- cabbage
- carrots
- celery
- leek
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes with cabbage, carrot, celery and leek in beef or general meat broth; strain the soup and add 2 egg yolks
