## Ingredients

- 60 grams sugar
- 1/2 liter good sweet cream
- two spoonfuls flour
- vanilla
- lemon peel
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 60 grams of sugar, 1/2 liter of good sweet cream, two spoonfuls of flour, a piece of vanilla and a piece of uncut lemon peel until thickened
2. Mix in three egg yolks
3. Can be served cold or warm
4. Usually served with buns
