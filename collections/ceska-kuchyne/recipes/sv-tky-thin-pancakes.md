## Ingredients

- half liter fresh milk
- 6 to 8 eggs
- quarter liter flour
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon rum
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a thin batter from half liter of fresh milk, 6 to 8 eggs, quarter liter of flour, salt and 1 tablespoon sugar; lemon zest and 1 tablespoon of rum may also be added.
2. Mix the egg yolks in milk and fold in the egg white snow from the whites.
3. Heat 3 decagrams of butter on a pan, and when hot, pour 1 tablespoon of batter into it.
4. Shake the pan occasionally and check with a knife if the pancake sticks to the pan.
5. When the pancake is done on the bottom side, turn it onto a greased pan.
6. Sprinkle the finished pancake with sugar and cinnamon, place on a platter and put in the oven to keep warm; pile the rest one on top of the other.
7. Serve with compote, salad and sauces.
