## Ingredients

- quince pulp
- sugar (equal amount to the quince pulp)
- crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- lemon or orange peel (cut into small pieces)

## Directions

1. Rub the hairs from the quince with cloth, cut off the ends, cook in soft water, place on a sieve to dry
2. Press the pulp, collect the flesh and place on a sheet or glass
3. Take as much crushed sugar as there is quince flesh—cook everything together with constant stirring vigorously
4. For better flavor, add crushed cinnamon and cloves, also lemon or orange peel cut into small pieces, mix everything thoroughly
5. Spread on sheets and let dry in a cool place, and when completely dry, place the quince bread between papers in boxes
