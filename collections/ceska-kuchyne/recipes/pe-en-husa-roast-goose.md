## Ingredients

- One cleaned and gutted goose
- Salt
- Water
- Caraway seeds, sifted

## Directions

1. Wash the well-cleaned and gutted goose in cold water.
2. Salt it on the surface and inside.
3. Sprinkle the inside with a little sifted caraway seeds.
4. Place it on a baking pan and add a little water.
5. Put it in the oven and let it roast slowly.
6. Turn it and frequently baste it with the roasted drippings.
7. When nearly done, drain off the fat and finish roasting the goose on high heat until brown.
8. If during roasting you prick the skin on the back and under the wings with a fork, the fat will cook out better and the skin will become crisp and more appetizing.
9. Serve with dumplings with cabbage, stewed cabbage, or various prepared potatoes.
