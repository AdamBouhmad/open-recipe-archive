## Ingredients

- leek
- onion
- butter
- bread roll
- oysters
- parsley water or lean broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Chop the white part of a small leek finely and let it slowly fry with one finely sliced small onion in two ounces of butter
2. Add two ounces of grated bread roll to it, let it fry briefly with the onion and add about sixty medium oysters with their juice and two quarts of parsley water or lean broth
3. Let it boil over high heat and stir constantly so the oysters do not stick to the bottom
4. Skim the foam, salt and pepper the broth, season with nutmeg and before serving, stir in a little chopped green parsley
