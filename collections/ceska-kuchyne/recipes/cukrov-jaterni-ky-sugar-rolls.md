## Ingredients

- several apples
- one tablespoon lard
- sugar to taste
- lemon zest
- citron zest
- a little pepper
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Peel several apples, cut into small slices or grate
2. Put in a saucepan with one tablespoon lard and when hot, add the apples
3. Add sugar to taste and cook over frequent stirring into a porridge
4. Add some lemon zest, citron zest and a little pepper
5. Add as many finely chopped almonds as needed to make it nicely thick
6. When cooled slightly, place on a surface dusted with sugar
7. Roll out small rolls and form into crescents so they look like real liver sausages
