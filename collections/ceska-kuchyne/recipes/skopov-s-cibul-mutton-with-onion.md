## Ingredients

- mutton
- onion leaves
- crushed caraway seeds
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Boil mutton
2. Remove foam
3. Add several onion leaves and crushed caraway seeds
4. Boil together
5. Add fried bread to the sauce
