## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef
- vegetables
- water
- vinegar
- lemon zest
- onion
- allspice berries
- cloves
- bay leaf
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook about 2 pounds of beef with all the vegetables, or use leftover meat
2. Slice it into neat pieces and pour water over them to cover
3. Add 2 spoonfuls of vinegar, a piece of lemon zest, half a finely chopped onion, 5-6 allspice berries, 4-5 cloves, a bay leaf, and salt
4. Cover and let simmer for a while
