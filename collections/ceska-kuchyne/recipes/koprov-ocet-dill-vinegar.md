## Ingredients

- dill tops, while green
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut off the dill tops while they are green, wash them, place in a large jar, pour over with good vinegar, seal and set in the sun.
2. After some time the dill is placed among cucumbers, which are then poured over with the vinegar.
3. The vinegar can be preserved and used for preparing sauces.
