## Ingredients

- Ripe cherries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the ripe cherries, remove the stems, crush the cherries and press them through a fine sieve.
2. Add sugar to the juice, mix everything together and pour the wine into a barrel or bottles (if there is little of it) so that it can ferment.
3. Cork the fermented wine, let it rest peacefully for three months, then siphon it into bottles.
