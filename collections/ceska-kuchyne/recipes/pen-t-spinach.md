## Ingredients

- spinach leaves
- salt
- butter
- onion
- flour or bread crust
- beef broth
- garlic (2 cloves)
- flower (garnish)
- raw egg yolks (2)
- smoked tongue, poached eggs, or other fat preparations (optional)

## Directions

1. Use only spinach leaves, discard the stems, rinse and cook in salted water until tender, strain and chop finely or press through a coarse sieve
2. On a small pan, place half or a whole chopped onion with butter according to the amount of spinach
3. When heated, add flour or grated bread crust until pink, then add the spinach and mix
4. Add good beef broth and let it cook
5. During cooking add minced 2 cloves of garlic, flower garnish and 2 raw egg yolks and mix
6. Can be served with smoked tongue, poached eggs or any other fat preparation
