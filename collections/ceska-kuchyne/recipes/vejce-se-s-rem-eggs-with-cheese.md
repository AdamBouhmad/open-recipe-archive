## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- grated cheese (one third the weight of the eggs in their shells)
- butter (one sixth the weight of the eggs)
- milk (1 spoonful per egg)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. For one person, weigh 2 eggs in their shells.
2. Add one third their weight in grated cheese and one sixth in butter.
3. Add 1 spoonful of milk per egg, some salt and plenty of pepper.
4. Stir until the egg white sets.
5. Serve on a warm plate.
