## Ingredients

- eel
- anchovies
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the eel
2. Depending on size, rub with anchovies, then either roll the eel or cut into pieces
3. Mix anchovies with butter and coat the eel
4. Place in oven
5. When baking begins, baste frequently with good sour cream until completely soft
6. Pour remaining sour cream over the anchovies when the eel is plated
7. Strain sauce over the eel
