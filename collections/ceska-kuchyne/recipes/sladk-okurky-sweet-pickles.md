## Ingredients

- Sweet gherkins
- Onion
- Paprika peppers
- Shallots
- Tarragon leaves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Arrange sweet gherkins cut into rounds on a dish
2. Intersperse with sliced onion and paprika peppers cut into rounds
3. Surround with several shallots
4. Sprinkle with chopped tarragon leaves
5. Pour vinegar over all
