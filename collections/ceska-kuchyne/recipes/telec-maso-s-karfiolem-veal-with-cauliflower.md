## Ingredients

- two pounds veal leg or shoulder
- beef broth
- fresh butter
- flour
- one large cauliflower head or two small ones
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut slices from two pounds of veal leg or shoulder and let simmer for about half an hour
2. Clean one large or two small cauliflower heads and cook them in beef broth until tender
3. Make a pale roux from fresh butter and flour, thin it with the beef broth in which you cooked the cauliflower
4. Season with salt and let the gravy simmer a little, season with nutmeg
5. Add the veal slices to the gravy
6. Cut the cooked cauliflower into quarters and add to the slices in the gravy
7. Let simmer a little longer and serve
