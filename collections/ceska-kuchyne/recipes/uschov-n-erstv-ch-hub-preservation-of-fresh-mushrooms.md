## Ingredients

- fresh young mushrooms
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean fresh, young mushrooms, leave small ones whole, cut larger ones into thick slices
2. Boil in salted water, remove with a skimmer and put into tin boxes, shake well
3. Pour salted water over the mushrooms until all are submerged, do not fill the boxes completely
4. Close well and seal with pitch or wax
5. Let cool, later they can be fried in butter like fresh ones
