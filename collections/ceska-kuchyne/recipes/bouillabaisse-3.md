## Ingredients

- pike
- eel
- perch
- live sea crayfish
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- bay leaf
- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- lemon
- tomatoes
- saffron
- thyme
- white wine
- water
- parsley
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Take one pike, one eel, two perch and one live sea crayfish
2. Clean the fish well, split them, remove the bones and cut the meat into pieces about half an inch thick
3. Remove the meat from the tail, body and claws of the crayfish and also cut into slices
4. Put two ounces of butter, finely sliced onion, two cloves of grated garlic and a bay leaf into a large pan and let it fry briefly
5. Add the cut fish, crayfish and a dozen large raw oysters, salt, pepper, add lemon cut into slices, three peeled and sliced tomatoes, half a teaspoon of ground saffron, a little thyme, a pint of white wine and enough water to cover the fish
6. Let it cook for about half an hour, then add a tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley and let it cook another minute
7. Remove the fish, crayfish and oysters and put them into a soup bowl
8. Strain the broth through a very fine sieve, let it come to a boil once more, pour it over the fish and serve
9. On a separate plate serve fried bread roll cut into cubes (Serves 8-10 people)
