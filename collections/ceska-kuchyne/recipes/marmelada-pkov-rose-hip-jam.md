## Ingredients

- ripe rose hips
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select very ripe rose hips and remove the seeds.
2. Place the rose hips on a baking sheet and mix them several times daily.
3. When soft, press through a sieve.
4. Add an equal amount of dissolved sugar to the pulp, mix and cook several times, then pour into containers and seal.
