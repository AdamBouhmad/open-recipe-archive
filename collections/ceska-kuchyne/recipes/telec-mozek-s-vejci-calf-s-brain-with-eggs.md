## Ingredients

- 2 calf's brains
- water
- onion
- butter
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Place 2 brains in lukewarm water for half an hour
2. Remove all marrow and clean them in cold water
3. Let a finely chopped onion of medium size with 1½ spoonfuls of butter disperse on the stove
4. Salt the brains, season with a little thyme, place on the onion, mix well
5. Before serving mix 4 eggs into it
