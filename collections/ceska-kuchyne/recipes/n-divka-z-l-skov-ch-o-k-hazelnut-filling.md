## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- piece of vanilla
- 1/2 small cup hazelnuts, coarsely ground
- a little white wine

## Directions

1. Rub 3 tablespoons of butter with 2 egg yolks in a bowl
2. Fold in 6 tablespoons of sugar pounded with a piece of vanilla
3. Add 1/2 small cup of coarsely pounded hazelnuts and a little white wine
