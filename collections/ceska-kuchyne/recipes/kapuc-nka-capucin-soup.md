## Ingredients

- one quart white wine
- a piece of sugar
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- lemon peel
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- one tablespoon flour
- one small cup water
- one tablespoon butter
- half pound black bread, grated
- sugar
- spices

## Directions

1. Boil one quart of white wine with a piece of sugar, a piece of whole cinnamon and lemon peel
2. remove from heat, remove scum and strain
3. in another pot beat well six egg yolks or three whole eggs with one tablespoon of flour and one small cup of water
4. pour into boiling wine
5. heat one tablespoon of butter until red
6. fry half pound of grated black bread in it
7. add a little sugar and spices to it
8. let it cool and turn out into a soup bowl
9. heat the wine soup mentioned above and pour it into the bowl over the prepared bread
10. if you want a weaker soup, use half wine and half water
