## Ingredients

- sixty dekagrams (four lots) of butter
- flour
- fish broth
- several tablespoons vinegar
- herbs
- salt
- two or three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix sixty dekagrams of butter with flour
2. Add a little fish broth and several tablespoons of vinegar
3. Add herbs and salt
4. Boil with two or three egg yolks
