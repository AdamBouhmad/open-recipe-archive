## Ingredients

- 4-6 deka ground good cocoa
- cold cream
- 3 deciliters cream
- sugar
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix 4-6 deka of ground good cocoa with cold cream
2. Add 3 deciliters of cream, a little sugar and six egg yolks
