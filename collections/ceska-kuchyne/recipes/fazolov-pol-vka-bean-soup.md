## Ingredients

- small white pearl beans
- light butter roux
- beef broth or water with vegetables
- onion
- carrot
- parsley
- parsnip
- garlic
- marjoram
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook small white pearl beans until soft
2. Press them through a sieve
3. Make a light butter roux and pour beef broth or water in which you cooked vegetables (onion, carrot, parsley and parsnip) over it
4. Strain the vegetables and use only the water to thin the bean paste according to preference
5. Salt to taste and season with spices—a bit of garlic mixed with salt and a bit of marjoram
