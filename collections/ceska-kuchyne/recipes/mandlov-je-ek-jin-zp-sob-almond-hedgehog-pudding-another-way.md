## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams ground almonds
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 1 lemon
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams butter with 4 egg yolks
2. Add 12 dekagrams ground almonds
3. Add 9 dekagrams sugar and lemon
4. Add 6 hard-boiled egg yolks and snow from 4 egg whites
5. Steam in a cloth
