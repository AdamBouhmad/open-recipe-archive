## Ingredients

- large tasty apples
- fine plum puree (apricot or peach)
- dissolved sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the large tasty apples
2. Carefully cut out the cores
3. Fill the center of the apples with fine apricot or peach puree
4. Place side by side on a baking sheet
5. Pour dissolved sugar over them
6. Bake gently in an oven until soft
