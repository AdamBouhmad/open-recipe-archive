## Ingredients

- leftover roasted mutton
- butter or lard
- parsley, finely chopped
- shallots, finely chopped
- mushrooms
- flour
- wine
- beef broth or hot water
- salt
- pepper
- meat extract
- breadcrumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted mutton into neat pieces.
2. In two tablespoons of butter or lard, stew one finely chopped parsley root, the same amount of finely chopped shallots, and four tablespoons of mushrooms.
3. Dust with a little flour, add wine and twice as much beef broth or hot water, and cook slowly for half an hour, season with salt and pepper.
4. Place the cut meat into the gravy and ensure it is very hot, but do not let it boil.
5. Add one tablespoon of flour, one bowl of hot water, and if available, one teaspoon of meat extract.
6. Arrange everything on a dish and add three tablespoons of grated breadcrumbs.
