## Ingredients

- floury potatoes
- 10 egg yolks
- 24 decagrams (16 lots) sugar
- lemon zest and juice
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) crushed almonds
- half a kilogram (pound) butter
- glaze
- sugar for dusting the form

## Directions

1. The day before use, boil floury potatoes, peel them, and when cooled, grate them and spread on a rolling pin or shallow grater.
2. Beat 10 egg yolks with 24 decagrams (16 lots) sugar, lemon zest and juice, and 12 decagrams (8 lots) crushed almonds.
3. After half an hour, add half a kilogram (pound) butter.
4. Add the grated potatoes and snow from egg whites.
5. Put the mixture into a greased form dusted with sugar.
6. Bake slowly for one hour.
7. When baked, glaze the cake with any glaze.
