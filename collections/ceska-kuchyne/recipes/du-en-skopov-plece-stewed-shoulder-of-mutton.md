## Ingredients

- nice mutton shoulder
- salt
- string for tying
- cup of fat broth
- onion
- carrot
- bay leaf
- small piece of garlic
- peppercorns
- two cloves
- piece of parsley
- beef broth
- Liebig's meat extract

## Directions

1. Clean a nice mutton shoulder well and wash it clean, remove bones, salt the inside, then roll it and tie it with string.
2. Place the roll into a deep pan, pour a cup of fat broth over it, add onion, carrot, bay leaf, a small piece of garlic, several peppercorns, two cloves, a piece of parsley and let it braise slowly to a golden color.
3. Then pour another cup of beef broth and let it braise until tender.
4. Then remove the roll, remove the string, add a spoon of Liebig's meat extract to the gravy, let it simmer, then skim the fat and strain the gravy through a sieve onto the roll; serve the remaining gravy in a gravy boat.
