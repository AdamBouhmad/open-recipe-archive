## Ingredients

- one eighth kilogram bacon
- 2 bread rolls cut into cubes
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into cubes and fry, or leave it raw.
2. Cut 2 bread rolls the same way and add to the bacon.
3. Mix 2 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs and milk as needed in a bowl, salt and mix everything well.
4. Drop dumplings into boiling salted water with a skimmer.
5. Serve these dumplings with cabbage and smoked meat.
