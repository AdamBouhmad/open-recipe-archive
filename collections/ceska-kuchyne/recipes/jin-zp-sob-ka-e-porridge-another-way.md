## Ingredients

- several spoonfuls flour
- water
- 1 liter water
- 75 grams (5 lots) butter
- salt
- butter for frying
- fried onion

## Directions

1. Mix several spoonfuls of flour well in water
2. Boil 1 liter water with 75 grams butter and salt
3. Add the mixed flour and stir until a thick porridge forms
4. Before serving, pour brown fried butter and fried onion over it
