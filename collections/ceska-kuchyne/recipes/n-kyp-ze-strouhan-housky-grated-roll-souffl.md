## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) butter
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) ground almonds
- 4 eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- egg white snow from 8 egg whites
- sugar
- lemon zest
- grated roll, sprinkled with cream
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream with vanilla and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix 24 decagrams (16 lots) butter, 6 decagrams (4 lots) ground almonds, 4 eggs, 8 egg yolks, snow made from 8 egg whites, sugar, and lemon zest
2. After each egg, add a handful of grated roll sprinkled with cream
3. Bake in a buttered mold
4. When the soufflé is done, pour 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream with vanilla and cinnamon over it
