## Ingredients

- sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- lard or butter
- onion
- 12 dekagrams sardines
- 12 dekagrams butter
- 2 cauliflower roses
- salt water

## Directions

1. Cut sirloin into thin slices, flatten with a mallet, salt, pepper, and dust with flour
2. Fry half an onion in lard or butter and brown the schnitzel well on both sides
3. Mix sardines with butter, coat schnitzel with this mixture and braise for a quarter hour
4. Boil two cauliflower roses in salt water, place one in the center
5. Arrange schnitzel around it and cover with the other rose
6. Pour sauce over schnitzel and top cauliflower with butter
