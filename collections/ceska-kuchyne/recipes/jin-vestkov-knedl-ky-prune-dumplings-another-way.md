## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- three tablespoons of curd cheese
- three whole eggs
- a little salt
- one pound of flour
- milk or sour cream
- beautiful prunes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cream three tablespoons of butter with three tablespoons of curd cheese in a bowl
2. Add three whole eggs, a little salt, one pound of flour, and as much milk or sour cream as needed for dough thicker than for small cakes
3. Work it well with a spoon, remove the dough onto a board sprinkled with flour
4. Sprinkle your fingers with flour, pinch off a piece of dough, wrap a beautiful prune in it, and continue until all dumplings are ready
5. Cook and serve as mentioned in the previous recipe
6. If the prunes are not sweet enough, serve fine sugar in a sugar bowl and a coffee spoon at the table so each person can sweeten the dumplings as they like
7. Before wrapping the prunes in dough, you can also remove the pits and put a piece of sugar in the prunes instead
