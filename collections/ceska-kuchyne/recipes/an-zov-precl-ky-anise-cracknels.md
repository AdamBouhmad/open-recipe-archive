## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two small cups flour
- two whole eggs
- half small cup powdered sugar
- two teaspoons ground anise

## Directions

1. Crumble two tablespoons butter with two small cups flour
2. Beat in two whole eggs
3. Add half small cup powdered sugar and two teaspoons ground anise
4. Mix everything well and work into smooth dough
5. Form into small pretzel shapes
6. Place on waxed sheet, brush with beaten egg and bake until golden
