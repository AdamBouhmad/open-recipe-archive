## Ingredients

- Medium-sized, firm, green cucumbers
- Stone pots
- Good vinegar
- Yellow mustard seed
- Onion
- Garlic
- Horseradish, cut up
- Cherry and grape leaves
- Dill greens
- White mustard seed

## Directions

1. Select medium-sized, firm, green cucumbers, clean and rinse them, and place in stone pots, pour salted boiled vinegar over them
2. To three liters of good vinegar add twenty decagrams of yellow mustard seed, fifty decagrams of onion, five decagrams of garlic, cut up horseradish, cherry and grape leaves, and dill greens
3. On top, put white mustard seed in linen bags so the cucumbers are completely well covered, then seal the pots completely
4. After fourteen days, pour off the vinegar from the cucumbers, boil it again, and if needed add more; let it cool and pour it cold over the cucumbers
