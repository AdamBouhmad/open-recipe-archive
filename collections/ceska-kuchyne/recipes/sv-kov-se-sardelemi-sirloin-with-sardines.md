## Ingredients

- sirloin
- salt
- 30 grams butter (2 lots)
- water
- 5 sardines
- 60 grams butter (4 lots)
- marjoram
- pepper
- ginger (grated)
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from sirloin, salt it well, pound it
2. Baste with water on baking sheet, add 30 grams butter
3. Braise until half-cooked
4. Clean sardines, scrape and wipe the meat
5. Mix sardine meat with 60 grams butter, add a little marjoram, pepper and finely grated ginger
6. Spread this mixture on the roast
7. Baste with beef broth or water and braise until tender
8. Cut into slices, place on platter, pour sauce over it and border with anything desired
