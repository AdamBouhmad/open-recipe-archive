## Ingredients

- butter
- piece of cleaned carp or pike
- carrots, sliced into rounds
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- saffron
- water
- flour for roux
- wine
- four hard-boiled egg yolks, grated

## Directions

1. Place butter in a pan
2. Add piece of cleaned carp or pike, carrot slices, and celery
3. Season with salt, pepper, cloves, ginger, and saffron
4. Allow to braise; periodically add water so it does not burn and to ensure sufficient liquid
5. Strain the liquid
6. Make a light butter roux and fry the strained liquid in it
7. Add a little wine and four grated hard-boiled egg yolks
8. Mix well and allow to cool
9. This sauce is typically served with pike or trout
