## Ingredients

- several same-sized potatoes
- 2-3 cooked potatoes
- chopped onion
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- bacon cut into cubes
- 1 egg
- chopped parsley
- chives
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and split potatoes lengthwise
2. Scoop out centers
3. Mash cooked potatoes with chopped onion, butter, bacon cubes, egg, chopped parsley, chives, pepper, and salt
4. Pound everything in a mortar
5. Grease split potatoes with butter
6. Fill with stuffing
7. Close potatoes together
8. Place in greased mold and bake
