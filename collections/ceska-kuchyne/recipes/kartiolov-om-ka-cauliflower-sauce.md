## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- flour
- nutmeg
- water
- egg yolks
- crayfish butter
- cauliflower
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Brown 125 grams of butter
2. Add two spoonfuls of flour and a little grated nutmeg
3. Pour in 1/4 liter of water in which cauliflower has been cooked
4. Let all the liquid simmer with frequent stirring
5. Add two to three egg yolks to the sauce
6. Season with crayfish butter
7. Pour over cauliflower cooked until soft in water
8. Alternatively add a little sour cream to the egg yolks, blend together and omit crayfish butter
