## Ingredients

- quarter pound of sago
- two quarts of clear strong beef broth
- four egg yolks
- a little warm broth

## Directions

1. Rinse a quarter pound of sago and pour two quarts of clear strong beef broth over it and let cook until soft
2. In a small cup, beat together four egg yolks with a little warm broth, mix this into the remaining boiling broth and serve
