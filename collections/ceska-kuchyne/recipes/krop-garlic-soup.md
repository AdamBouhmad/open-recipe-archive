## Ingredients

- about 6 garlic cloves
- butter
- salt
- bread
- water

## Directions

1. Rub about 6 garlic cloves with butter and salt
2. Slice bread into small pieces and fry on butter
3. Boil the rubbed garlic with water for a while
4. Pour over the fried bread and serve
