## Ingredients

- 2-3 egg whites
- finely crushed sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Beat 2-3 egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Spread on baked goods
3. Sprinkle with finely crushed sugar
4. Sprinkle with cold water
5. Bake in oven until golden
