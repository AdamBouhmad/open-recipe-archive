## Ingredients

- 250 grams finely beaten, sifted sugar
- 8 eggs
- lemon zest, grated
- 200 grams finely beaten almonds

## Directions

1. Together and with constant stirring, roast 250 grams finely beaten sifted sugar, 8 eggs, grated lemon zest, and 200 grams finely beaten almonds on a warm surface for about half an hour
2. Knead everything and place into buttered molds
3. Bake slowly with gentle heat until golden
4. The next day, cut the cookie into thin slices
5. Carefully toast on both sides until brown
