## Ingredients

- Fish
- Water
- Salt
- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon mustard
- Fish roe (if available)
- A little milk

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and tie it in clean cloth
2. Place in a pot and pour boiling water over it, salt with a tablespoon of salt
3. Cook for half to three-quarters of an hour
4. Finely chop the 6 hard-boiled eggs
5. Mix together the eggs, butter, pepper, salt, mustard, fish roe if available, and a little milk
6. Cook this mixture and pour it over the fish after removing it from the cloth and arranging it on a platter
