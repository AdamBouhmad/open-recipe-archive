## Ingredients

- six yolks
- two teaspoons of sugar
- four full tablespoons of flour
- salt
- one cup of good milk
- egg whites
- two tablespoons of butter

## Directions

1. Put six yolks, two teaspoons of sugar, four full tablespoons of flour, a little salt into a cup.
2. Add one cup of good milk and mix well.
3. Beat the egg whites into snow and fold gently into the batter.
4. Heat two tablespoons of butter on a shallow pan, pour the batter into it and fry until it gets a nice crispy bottom, then carefully turn it, mash with a spoon, sprinkle with sugar and put on the table.
