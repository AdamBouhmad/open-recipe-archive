## Ingredients

- 25 decagrams butter
- 10 whole eggs
- 6 handfuls grated bread roll
- 12 cooked eggs halved and cut into cubes
- parsley
- chives
- a little flower
- cream
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix 25 decagrams of butter, 10 whole eggs, 6 handfuls of grated bread roll, 12 cooked halved eggs cut into cubes, a little parsley, chives, a little flower, cream, pepper and salt.
2. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated bread roll.
3. Bake the rolled pancake.
