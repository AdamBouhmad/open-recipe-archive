## Ingredients

- Thin slices of rye bread
- Sardines
- Lemon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of rye bread into heart-shaped pieces the size of a palm
2. Place two sardines on each piece
3. Sprinkle with their oil
4. Place a piece of lemon on top
5. Garnish on the sides with a piece of parsley
6. Present one at a time on small plates
7. Sardines must be kept on ice and removed from storage only before preparation
