## Ingredients

- One older goose
- Sliced bacon
- Veal meat, sliced
- Carrot, sliced into rounds
- Parsley
- Celery
- Salt
- Bay leaves
- Peppercorns
- Beef broth
- White wine (optional)

## Directions

1. If you have an older goose that is not suitable for roasting, you can stew it as follows:
2. Put sliced bacon and sliced veal meat on the bottom of a pot.
3. Add carrot sliced into rounds, parsley, and celery.
4. Place the gutted and salted goose on top.
5. Add several bay leaves and several peppercorns.
6. Cover with beef broth and add a little white wine if desired.
7. Cover with a lid and let the goose stew until tender.
8. Remove the goose to a serving dish and arrange celery salad around it.
