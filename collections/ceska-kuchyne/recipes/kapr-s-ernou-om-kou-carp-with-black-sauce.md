## Ingredients

- Carp
- Good wine vinegar
- Sweet beer
- Good wine vinegar
- Carrot
- Onion
- Celery
- Parsley root
- Handful of nutmeats
- Handful of raisins
- Salt
- Grated gingerbread
- Piece of white sugar
- Piece of burned sugar

## Directions

1. Tear open the carp, remove entrails, carefully remove gall, discard intestines, save roe or soft roe.
2. Rinse the carp inside with good wine vinegar, save the blood in a separate cup, save all blood during preparation.
3. Remove scales from the carp, place on a baking sheet and pour sweet beer over it until it floats.
4. Add a little good wine vinegar, add carrot cut into slices, onion, a whole small or half large celery, parsley root, a handful of nutmeats and a handful of raisins.
5. Salt and cook well for a quarter hour, flame should only act on one side.
6. Carefully place the fish on a platter.
7. Add grated gingerbread, a piece of white sugar and a piece of burned sugar to the mixture in which the fish was cooked—pour in the blood.
8. Cook everything for a while, then remove parsley, carrot and celery, pour the remaining mixture over the carp.
