## Ingredients

- turkey
- three bowls of grated white bread
- two eggs
- butter
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- sage

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey for roasting as described in the previous recipe
2. For the stuffing: Take three bowls of grated white bread, place in a bowl
3. Break two eggs into it, salt and pepper it
4. Add a piece of butter and enough milk to make the stuffing as thick as ordinary dumpling dough
5. Mix one spoonful of crushed and sifted sage into the stuffing
6. Fill the turkey with the stuffing, sew it up and bake according to the previous method
