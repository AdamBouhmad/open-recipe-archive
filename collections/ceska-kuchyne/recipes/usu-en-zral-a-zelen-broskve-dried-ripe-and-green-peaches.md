## Ingredients

- Peaches preserved without apple pulp
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Place preserved peaches on a dish and dry them.
2. Place them on a fine sieve so the sugar drains.
3. Sprinkle them finely with ground sugar.
4. When the peaches are coated on all sides, dust them off and pack between paper into boxes.
