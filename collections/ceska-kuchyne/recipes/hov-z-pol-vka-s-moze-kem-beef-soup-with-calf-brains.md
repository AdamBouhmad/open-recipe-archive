## Ingredients

- calf brains
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- marjoram
- flour
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- brown, black or white beef broth

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from the calf brains; beat 3 egg yolks with the brains, season with salt and add marjoram
2. Roll small pieces of brains in flour, then in egg and grated bread crumbs
3. Fry to a golden color, arrange in a soup bowl and pour with brown, black or white beef broth
