## Ingredients

- Young turnips (brukve/kelrubny)
- Carrots
- Good broth
- Green beans
- Asparagus
- Cauliflower
- Fried bread
- Salt

## Directions

1. Slice young turnips and carrots and cook in good broth
2. Add diagonally sliced green beans, sliced asparagus, and cauliflower
3. Cook everything in the broth
4. Add fried bread and salt and pour into a bowl
