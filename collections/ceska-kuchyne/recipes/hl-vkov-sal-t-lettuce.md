## Ingredients

- lettuce heads
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- good table oil

## Directions

1. Remove the outer, unsightly leaves from lettuce heads, clean them well, rinse, cut into quarters and salt
2. Arrange on a dish with the hearts in the middle
3. Pour over with vinegar mixed with water and a little good table oil if desired
