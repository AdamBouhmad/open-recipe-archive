## Ingredients

- fine flour
- lukewarm water
- yeast
- dissolved butter
- crushed sugar
- finely chopped lemon zest
- several eggs
- chopped almonds
- raisins

## Directions

1. Prepare a thick dough from fine flour, lukewarm water, and yeast
2. Let it rise
3. Add dissolved butter, crushed sugar, finely chopped lemon zest, several eggs, chopped almonds, and raisins
4. Work everything with necessary flour into a stiff dough
5. Place in a greased mold
6. Brush with beaten egg
7. Bake slowly
