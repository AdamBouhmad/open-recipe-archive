## Ingredients

- thick short noodles
- milk
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon peel
- 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) sweet cream
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make thick short noodles, cook them in milk and let cool
2. Beat 3 egg yolks with sugar and chopped lemon peel, fold in 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) of sweet cream
3. Mix the mixture with the noodles and bake in a mold that has been greased and sprinkled with bread crumbs
4. Turn out onto a platter
