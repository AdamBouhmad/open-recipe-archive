## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt
- chives

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a saucepan, pour the required number of beaten eggs into it, salt them a little and slowly stir them so they do not stick to the bottom
2. When they thicken a little, remove them to a plate and sprinkle with chopped chives
