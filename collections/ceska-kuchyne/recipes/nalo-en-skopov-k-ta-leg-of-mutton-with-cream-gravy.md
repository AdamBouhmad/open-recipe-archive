## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- onion
- cloves
- allspice
- pepper
- bay leaf
- ginger
- garlic
- bacon
- lemon peel
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and salt the leg of mutton
2. Boil one and a half cups vinegar with two cups water
3. Add one large sliced onion, six cloves, ten grains of allspice, ten peppercorns, bay leaf, a piece of ginger, and two garlic cloves
4. Cover and boil for fifteen minutes
5. Remove from heat and let cool
6. Place the salted leg in a deep container
7. When the liquid cools, pour it over the leg
8. Keep in cold place for five to six days in winter, two to three days in summer, turning once or twice during this time
9. When ready to roast, place on a board and lard with bacon
10. Place on baking pan
11. Pour about half the marinade and one cup water under it
12. Add a piece of lemon peel and a piece of butter on top
13. Roast until half done
14. Pour five to six tablespoons of good thick sour cream over it
15. Turn once during roasting and baste as needed
16. When done, transfer to a dish
