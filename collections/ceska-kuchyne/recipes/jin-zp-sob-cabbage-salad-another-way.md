## Ingredients

- cabbage, finely chopped
- finely chopped onion
- bacon or salt pork, fried
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage finely as mentioned in the previous recipe
2. Mix with finely chopped onion and fried bacon or salt pork while still hot
3. Drizzle with vinegar and mix well
4. Let steam for at least a quarter hour
5. Season with salt and pepper
