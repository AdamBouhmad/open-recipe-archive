## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams fresh hazelnuts
- 9 dekagrams pounded sugar
- 7-8 egg whites
- sugar
- pounded cinnamon
- powdered sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel fresh hazelnuts, chop coarsely, and toast with 9 dekagrams pounded sugar
2. Beat 7-8 egg whites to stiff peaks
3. Mix with sugar and pounded cinnamon and the prepared nuts
4. Form small mounds with a spoon on sugar-sprinkled paper
5. Bake in a warm oven
