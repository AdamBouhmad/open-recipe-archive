## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup small raisins
- pinch of cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 pastry crust on a plate

## Directions

1. Take 1 cup sour cream, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup small raisins, a pinch of cinnamon, clove, nutmeg, and 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2. Mix everything well together
3. Pour onto prepared crust on a plate and bake
4. Make the pie in a single crust
5. It is good but lasts only one day
