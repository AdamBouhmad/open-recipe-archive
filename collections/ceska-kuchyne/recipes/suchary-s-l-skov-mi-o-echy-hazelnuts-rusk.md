## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- sugar
- 240 grams peeled hazelnuts
- 4 eggs
- lemon zest
- egg white from 6 eggs
- 75 grams flour

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with sugar, crush 240 grams peeled hazelnuts with 4 eggs until it rises
2. Add lemon zest rubbed with sugar, stiff snow from 6 egg whites, and 75 grams flour
3. Pour everything into a greased mold and bake the rusks over moderate heat
