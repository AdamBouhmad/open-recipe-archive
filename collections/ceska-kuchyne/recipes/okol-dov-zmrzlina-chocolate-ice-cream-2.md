## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- One liter cream
- Quarter kilogram sugar
- One-eighth grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs and whip until foamy
2. Add one liter cream, quarter kilogram sugar and one-eighth grated chocolate
3. Mix and whip until it sets
4. Let cool
5. Proceed exactly as described for cream ice cream
