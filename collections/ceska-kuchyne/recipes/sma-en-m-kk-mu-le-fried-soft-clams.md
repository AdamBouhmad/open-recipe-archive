## Ingredients

- large soft clams
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg, beaten
- bread crumbs, finely grated
- lard, heated
- lemon
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Remove clams from shells
2. Remove the hard muscles from the clams
3. Let drain on cloth
4. Salt and pepper the clams
5. Dust with flour
6. Dip in beaten egg
7. Roll in finely grated bread crumbs
8. Fry in heated lard until golden
9. Arrange on dish
10. Garnish with sliced lemon and fried parsley
