## Ingredients

- fresh peas
- soft boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour fresh peas into soft boiling water
2. Once it comes to a boil, strain through a sieve
3. Spread on cloth and let dry
4. Place on firm paper with turned-up edges on a baking sheet in the oven
5. Let dry slowly and store in jars
6. When ready to cook, soak the evening before in soft, cold water
7. Cook the next day like fresh peas
