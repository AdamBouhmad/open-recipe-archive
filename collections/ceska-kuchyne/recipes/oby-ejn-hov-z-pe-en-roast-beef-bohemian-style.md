## Ingredients

- beef chuck
- salt
- ginger
- garlic cloves
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaves
- carrot
- water
- potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and salt a piece of beef chuck thoroughly
2. Pierce the entire piece with ginger and several cloves of garlic
3. Place on a roasting pan or baking sheet, add several smaller onions cut in half with a clove pierced through each, several bay leaves, and one small cut carrot
4. Pour in some water, using more than for other roasts
5. Roast for three to four hours, frequently turning and basting with the pan juices
6. If the gravy dries out, add more water or unsalted broth
7. When the meat is tender, place on a serving dish surrounded by boiled and peeled potatoes
8. Strain the gravy over the meat and serve
