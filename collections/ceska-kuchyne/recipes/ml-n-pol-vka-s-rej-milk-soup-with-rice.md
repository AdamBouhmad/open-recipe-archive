## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams (8 lots) rice
- 2 litres boiling milk
- sugar
- cinnamon stick
- lemon peel
- several egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pick through and parboil 12 decagrams (8 lots) of rice and set in cold water
2. Boil it and let the water drain off, put it into 2 litres of boiling milk
3. Add sugar, unbroken cinnamon stick and lemon peel, boil and add several egg yolks
