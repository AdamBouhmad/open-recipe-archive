## Ingredients

- cleaned chicken killed the day before
- butter
- salt
- broth from chicken head, neck, wings and innards
- bread cut into cubes
- pepper
- lemon juice
- egg yolks
- mushrooms
- asparagus
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Clean chicken, cut into quarters and salt them, let rest at least an hour
2. Make strong broth from chicken head, neck, wings and innards
3. Dry chicken quarters and braise on both sides in butter
4. Add prepared broth from other parts of poultry and braise chicken slowly in steam
5. At the same time cook bread cut into cubes together with chicken and remove to dish with chicken
6. Strain broth and thicken it, season with pepper and lemon juice, add several egg yolks
7. Pour sauce over chicken and bread cubes
8. Add browned mushrooms, asparagus, cauliflower and similar tender vegetables to sauce
