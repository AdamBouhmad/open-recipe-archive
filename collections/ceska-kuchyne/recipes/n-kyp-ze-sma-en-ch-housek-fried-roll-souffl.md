## Ingredients

- 5 rolls
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream
- 4 eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- egg white snow from 8 egg whites
- chopped almonds
- raisins
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) hot wine

## Directions

1. Fry 5 rolls until brown and grate them
2. Fry in 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter, add 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream to make a thick porridge
3. When cooled, add 4 eggs, 8 egg yolks, snow made from 8 egg whites, chopped almonds, raisins, sugar, cinnamon, and clove
4. Mix everything and bake
5. Pour 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) hot wine over it and let it stay in the oven a while until the wine soaks in
