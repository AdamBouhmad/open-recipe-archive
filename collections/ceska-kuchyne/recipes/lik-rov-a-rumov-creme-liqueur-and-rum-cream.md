## Ingredients

- four deciliters cream
- six egg yolks
- ten deka sugar
- three spoons of liqueur
- two spoons of rum or arrack

## Directions

1. Take four deciliters of cream, six egg yolks, ten deka sugar and mix everything
2. Add three spoons of liqueur and two spoons of rum or arrack
