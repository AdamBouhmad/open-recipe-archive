## Ingredients

- young mutton ribs
- salt
- pepper
- butter or lard
- breadcrumbs
- sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare cutlets from young ribs with bone attached as described previously
2. Salt the cutlets
3. Dust lightly with pepper
4. Dip in melted butter or lard
5. Coat in breadcrumbs
6. Fry in hot lard until golden
7. Arrange on a heated dish
8. Pour sauce over them or serve sauce separately on the side
