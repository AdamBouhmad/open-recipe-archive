## Ingredients

- ¼ kilogram melted butter
- ¼ kilogram finely crushed sugar
- several bitter almonds beaten with egg white
- 4 eggs
- approximately ½ kilogram fine flour

## Directions

1. Mix the melted butter, crushed sugar, almonds beaten with egg white, and eggs
2. Beat until foamed
3. Gradually add the fine flour
4. Form into cakes
5. Bake gently
