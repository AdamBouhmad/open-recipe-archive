## Ingredients

- hard rolls
- boiling water
- butter
- gingerbread

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls into several pieces or just split them, place on a plate, pour boiling water over them
2. After two minutes, drain the water, butter the rolls and sprinkle with gingerbread
