## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt water
- 12 dekagrams bacon
- flour
- 1/4 liter hot water
- vinegar
- pepper
- potatoes
- sausages

## Directions

1. Clean cabbage and cut finely
2. Pour several times with boiling water
3. Place on a sieve to drain
4. Meanwhile cut 12 dekagrams bacon into small pieces and fry
5. Remove the bacon
6. To the fat add 2 tablespoons flour
7. Pour 1/4 liter hot water and several tablespoons vinegar
8. Season with pepper and salt
9. Cook briefly and set aside so the sauce stays lukewarm
10. Place sliced potatoes in a baking dish with a little salted water
11. When they begin to cook, add the cabbage
12. Season with salt
13. Pour the sauce over it
14. Cover well and stew until potatoes are soft
15. Then mix the potatoes with cabbage
16. Add sausages and cabbage
17. Serve at the table
