## Ingredients

- red radish
- salt
- flour
- egg
- bread crust
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel the red radish, remove the outer leaves leaving only the inner ones, salt it, coat with flour, dip in beaten egg, roll in grated bread crust, drop into hot butter and fry until golden
2. Serve around beef roast
