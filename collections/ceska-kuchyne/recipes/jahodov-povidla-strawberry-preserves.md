## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Press strawberries through a fine sieve
2. Sweeten strongly and mix with sugar
3. Boil several times
4. Skim the foam
5. Pour into containers when cooled
6. Tie up the bladder
