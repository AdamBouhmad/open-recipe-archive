## Ingredients

- 150 grams (10 lots) butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 500 milliliters good sweet cream
- 120 grams (8 lots) curd cheese
- salt
- several large cooked grated potatoes

## Directions

1. Beat butter with eggs and egg yolks
2. Mix in cream and curd cheese
3. Add salt and grated cooked potatoes
4. Form dumplings
5. Cook in salted water
6. Garnish with grated roll crumbs
