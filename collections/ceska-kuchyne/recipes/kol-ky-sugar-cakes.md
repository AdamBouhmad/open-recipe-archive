## Ingredients

- ¼ kilogram butter
- 4 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- ⅛ kilogram sugar
- ¼ kilogram fine flour
- grated lemon zest
- chopped almonds for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the butter with eggs and egg yolks until foamed
2. Add the sugar, fine flour, and grated lemon zest
3. Mix well
4. Spread the dough on a buttered baking sheet as small cakes
5. Sprinkle with chopped almonds
6. Bake slowly
