## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 30 grams yeast (dissolved in 1/4 liter warm water)
- 125 grams butter
- 70 grams sugar
- salt
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift flour
2. Add yeast dissolved in warm water
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Let rise
5. Add butter, sugar, and salt
6. Beat in eggs
7. Work everything well
8. Let rise
9. Shape into oblong or round rolls
10. Let rise again
11. Brush with beaten egg
12. Bake slowly on buttered baking sheet
