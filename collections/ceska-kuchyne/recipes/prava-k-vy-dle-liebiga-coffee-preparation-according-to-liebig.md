## Ingredients

- Coffee beans
- sugar (15 grams per 500 grams coffee)
- water (approximately 15 grams coffee per two cups water)

## Directions

1. Slowly roast the coffee beans only to a light brown color, to preserve the aroma and caffeine.
2. When nearly roasted, add fifteen grams of sugar per half kilogram of coffee.
3. Mix thoroughly until the coffee beans are properly coated with the dissolved sugar, which prevents the escape of fine aromatic particles.
4. Spread the roasted beans on a pan to cool.
5. Close them in tightly sealed containers and store in dry places.
6. When brewing coffee, grind or crush three-quarters of the amount you wish to brew, place in water, and steep for ten minutes.
7. Then add the remaining quarter of ground coffee and immediately remove from heat.
8. Cover tightly and let it stand for at least five minutes.
9. By this method, the best parts of the coffee are extracted, and the final addition of fresh grounds imparts a pleasant flavor to the beverage.
