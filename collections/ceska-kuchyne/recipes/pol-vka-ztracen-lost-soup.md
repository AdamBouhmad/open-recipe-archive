## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Caraway
- Pepper
- A clove of garlic
- Salt
- Butter or goose lard
- Fried bread
- Pork meat from the flank

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash peeled potatoes and cook with caraway, pepper, a clove of garlic rubbed with salt, and butter or goose lard
2. Carefully strain the soup so potatoes don't get into it
3. Pour over fried bread
4. A piece of pork meat from the flank can also be cooked in the strained soup, which gives the soup a particularly good flavor
