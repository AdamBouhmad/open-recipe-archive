## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- white flour
- beef broth
- white wine vinegar
- dill leaves
- sour cream or sweet cream

## Directions

1. Make a white thin roux from fresh butter and a little white flour
2. Mix it with as much beef broth as you need sauce
3. Add a little white wine vinegar and let it boil
4. When ready to serve, add finely chopped dill leaves and several tablespoons of good sour cream to the sauce
5. If you do not have sour cream, you can use half a cupful of sweet cream, but you must add a little vinegar
