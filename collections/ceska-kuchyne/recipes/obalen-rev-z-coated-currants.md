## Ingredients

- beautiful currant clusters
- egg white
- crushed almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select the most beautiful currant clusters and dry them
2. Place them on a baking sheet and mix with beaten egg white
3. Coat them multiple times in crushed almonds
4. Then coat them multiple times in thick, well-cooked sugar
5. Place on a sieve on warm stove to dry thoroughly
