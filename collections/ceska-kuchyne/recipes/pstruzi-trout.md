## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- wine vinegar
- water
- thyme
- bay leaf
- onion
- whole pepper
- new spices
- ginger
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Scale the trout, eviscerate, and leave whole
2. Salt them
3. Boil good wine vinegar mixed with water
4. Pour the boiling vinegar mixture over the trout
5. Add thyme, bay leaf, onion cut into thin strips, whole pepper, new spices, a piece of ginger, and lemon zest cut into thin strips
6. Simmer for a quarter hour
7. Place on a platter and add all the ingredients that were cooked with them
