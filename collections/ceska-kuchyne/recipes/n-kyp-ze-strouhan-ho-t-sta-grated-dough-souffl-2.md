## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 eggs
- milk
- piece of vanilla
- sugar as needed
- 45 grams (3 lots) almonds
- 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) sweet cream
- 4 or 5 egg yolks
- hard roll, grated

## Directions

1. Put some flour on a board, make a hollow in the middle, crack 2 eggs into it
2. Mix with a knife and work well to form stiff dough for grating
3. Grate on a grater
4. Meanwhile boil milk with a piece of vanilla and sugar as needed to make it very sweet
5. Cook the grated dough in this milk until a thick porridge forms; let it cool
6. Blanch 45 grams (3 lots) almonds, peel them, and chop not too finely
7. Add 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) sweet cream and 4 or 5 egg yolks; mix everything
8. Put in a buttered mold sprinkled with grated hard roll
9. Bake in the oven
