## Ingredients

- figs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the figs as described in the previous section
2. Finally cook the sugar to the sixth degree
3. Remove the figs, dry them, dust with sugar and coat them thoroughly
4. Allow to dry
5. Store dried figs in boxes between papers
