## Ingredients

- 2 cups lukewarm milk
- 2 egg yolks
- ½ cup butter
- 1 piece of compressed or dried yeast
- salt
- flour to make a dough like for dumplings

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups of lukewarm milk, put into it 2 egg yolks and ½ cup of butter, 1 piece of compressed or dried yeast, salt and add flour so that the dough is about like for dumplings.
2. Let it rise, then dust the work surface with flour, turn out the dough and work it so that it is stiffer than for cake.
3. When you have worked the dough, roll it out to about finger thickness, cut into square pieces, cut off one corner from each and roll them so that the cut side is rolled to the inside; place on a baking sheet greased with lard, bend them, let them rise, brush with beaten egg and let them bake.
4. You can also sprinkle them with poppy seeds or caraway seeds and salt.
