## Ingredients

- layered pastry dough
- peeled apples cut into small pieces
- sugar
- small raisins
- finely chopped almonds
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Line the pastry cake with peeled apples cut into small pieces
2. Sprinkle with sugar, small raisins, finely chopped almonds, and ground cinnamon
3. During baking, brush the cake with beaten egg yolk
