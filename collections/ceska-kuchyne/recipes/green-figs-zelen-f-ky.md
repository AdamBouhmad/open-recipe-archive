## Ingredients

- beautiful, large, green, not fully ripe figs
- pure soft water
- salt
- spinach
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, large, green, not fully ripe figs
2. Pierce several times with a fine knife
3. Place in clean soft water and cook slowly
4. Remove and place again in fresh water
5. On the second day, place again in fresh water on warm stove, salt them, add a little spinach and cook slowly until the figs are completely soft
6. Remove and place in fresh water and when completely cooled, place on sieves to dry quickly
7. Dried figs place on a dish, pour over with thinly dissolved sugar
8. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, skim foam, add the figs and cook with the sugar several times
9. Pour again onto a dish, cover with paper and let sit quietly
10. For two more days, always cook the drained sugar thicker and thicker and always add the figs to cook together with it several times until finally the sugar is cooked in pearls, add the figs, cook several times again, skim foam and place cooled in jars
