## Ingredients

- good or bad fruit, green or rotten fruit
- warm water (not boiling)

## Directions

1. Crush the fruit intended for vinegar production in large vessels
2. Pour warm—but not boiling—water over it; do not use too much water as the vinegar would be very weak
3. Allow the fruit pulp made in this manner to rest in special vessels for 2-3 weeks, stirring thoroughly several times daily so the layers are completely worked through
