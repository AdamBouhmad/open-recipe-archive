## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg white
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- salt
- lukewarm water
- butter
- egg yolk
- sour cream
- sugar
- egg white snow
- raisins
- half a liter of boiling milk or sour cream
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix flour, 2 eggs, 1 egg white, 45 grams butter, salt and lukewarm water to form a firm dough
2. Work the dough until bubbles form in it
3. Let rest for half an hour
4. Prepare filling from grated butter, egg yolk, sour cream, salt, sugar and snow from egg whites
5. Stretch dough on a cloth, stretch as thinly as possible
6. Spread with filling; sprinkle with raisins
7. Roll up and place in a greased baking pan
8. Pour half a liter of boiling milk or sour cream on the pan
9. Brush strudel with sour cream
10. Serve with sweet milk or sour cream sauce
