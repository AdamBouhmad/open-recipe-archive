## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- 1.5 litres water
- salt
- bread, sliced

## Directions

1. Finely chop onion and fry it with butter until pink
2. Boil about 1.5 litres of water, salt it and pour it into a bowl with sliced bread mixed with the fried onion
