## Ingredients

- 20 eggs
- half kilogram sugar
- grated chocolate
- finest flour

## Directions

1. Prepare dough from 20 eggs and half kilogram sugar, beaten well together
2. Add grated chocolate and a little finest flour
3. It is possible to prepare the chocolate cake in the same way as the almond cake, and to add almonds to it (but less than in an almond cake)
