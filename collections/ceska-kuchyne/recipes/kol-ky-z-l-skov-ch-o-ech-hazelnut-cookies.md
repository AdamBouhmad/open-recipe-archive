## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams (8 lots) blanched hazelnuts
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) almonds
- 135 grams (9 lots) sugar
- firm snow from two whites
- several tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Grind hazelnuts with almonds, mix with sugar and snow from whites
2. Add flour to form stiff dough
3. Roll out and cut cookies with small form
4. Bake on greased sheet
