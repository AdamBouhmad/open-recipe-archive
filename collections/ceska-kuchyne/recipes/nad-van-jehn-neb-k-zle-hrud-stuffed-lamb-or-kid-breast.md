## Ingredients

- lamb or kid breast
- bread
- butter
- eggs
- egg yolk
- salt
- herbs (parsley or chives optional)
- milk
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the breast, salt it.
2. If breast is small, take one loaf, otherwise 2 loaves of bread, grate them, press through a sieve, dry on the stove and crush.
3. Beat 30 grams of butter, 2 eggs, 1 yolk, a little salt, a little herbs.
4. Add grated bread to everything.
5. If the stuffing is too thick, add milk or an egg; the stuffing should be the consistency of porridge.
6. Fill the cleaned breast with the stuffing using a syringe or spoon and sew it shut.
7. Add 2 large spoonfuls of water and 30 grams of butter to the breast and bake until reddened.
8. Cut into neat pieces and serve.
