## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram butter
- 5 1/2 kilogram flour
- several eggs
- piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Work the butter in water to remove buttermilk
2. Mix with flour, eggs, and sugar until the dough does not stick to the rolling pin
3. Let rest in a cold place for about half an hour
4. Shape into croissants (loupáčky)
5. Brush with beaten egg, milk, or butter
6. Sprinkle with sugar, poppy seeds, or chopped almonds
7. Bake slowly until golden
