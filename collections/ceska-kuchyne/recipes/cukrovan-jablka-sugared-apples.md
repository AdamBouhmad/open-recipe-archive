## Ingredients

- good apples
- sugar
- flour
- egg whites
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the good apples.
2. Cook one quarter pound in water until it stretches.
3. Place the apples in melted sugar and leave them for half an hour.
4. Remove them and add the remaining sugar to flour and coat the apples arranged on a baking sheet with it.
5. Finally, dust with snow from 3 egg whites, bake for a bit, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and serve on the baking sheet on which they were baked.
