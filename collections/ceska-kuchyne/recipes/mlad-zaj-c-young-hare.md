## Ingredients

- Young hare legs, split head and sides
- Bacon, cut into pieces
- Butter
- Flour
- Red wine
- Broth
- Lemon peel
- Bundle of vegetables
- Juniper berries
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Hare blood

## Directions

1. Fry bacon pieces cut into cubes in butter
2. Brown the hare pieces on both sides in the fat without bacon
3. Dust with flour and cover with bacon again
4. Pour in red wine and a little broth
5. Add lemon peel, a bundle of vegetables, juniper berries, salt, pepper and spices
6. Cover the pan and braise the meat
7. After half an hour, add onion fried in butter and sugar, and several mushrooms
8. When the meat is tender, remove it and add hare blood to the sauce and simmer briefly
9. Similarly rabbit and roe deer may be prepared
