## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams blanched, pounded almonds
- 20 dekagrams sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- flour
- butter
- sliced almonds
- lemon glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams blanched, pounded almonds
2. Beat 20 dekagrams sugar with 12 egg yolks
3. Beat 6 egg yolks to stiff peaks with 1 tablespoon flour
4. Make oblong indentations from stiff paper, grease with butter, sprinkle with sliced almonds
5. Fill with the mixture and dry
6. When removed, coat with lemon glaze
