## Ingredients

- wild duck
- salt
- bacon
- wine vinegar
- water
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pluck the wild duck except for the head.
2. Clean it and prepare for roasting; optionally lard with bacon.
3. Place on a baking sheet and baste with wine vinegar mixed with water.
4. Add pepper and other spices as desired.
