## Ingredients

- Cabbage head
- Beef broth or water
- Onion
- Sugar
- Raw bacon or butter
- Salt
- Roux made from flour mixed with butter
- Rose-hip paste
- Burnt sugar
- Pork or mutton meat

## Directions

1. Break off the upper leaves from the head, cut the head into equal pieces and remove the core
2. Rinse the cabbage, clean it and pour boiling water over it, letting it stand for ten minutes
3. Place on a baking sheet, add beef broth or water, add finely chopped onion, a good piece of sugar, bacon cut into cubes or butter, and salt
4. Braise until completely soft
5. Press rose-hip paste through it and mix in a piece of burnt sugar
6. Place pork or mutton meat on a dish and surround with cabbage
