## Ingredients

- leftover veal roast
- butter
- parsley
- 2 tablespoons flour
- broth
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop the veal roast leftovers
2. Sauté in butter with parsley
3. Add 2 tablespoons flour
4. Hold over heat
5. Add sufficient amount of broth
6. Thicken with 2 egg yolks
