## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- fresh parsley (in summer) or dried parsley (in winter)
- onion, finely chopped
- beef broth
- vinegar
- lemon zest
- ground pepper
- caraway
- cooked potatoes
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Put a spoonful of butter in a pot and add a little flour to make a thin roux
2. Add a bit of fresh parsley in summer or dried parsley in winter
3. When the roux turns light golden in color, add finely chopped onion and let it foam
4. Pour in beef broth, a little vinegar, a piece of lemon zest, a little ground pepper and a piece of caraway
5. When the gravy is cooked to be neither too thick nor too thin, cut several cooked potatoes into it and add about a cupful of sour cream, but then it must not boil
