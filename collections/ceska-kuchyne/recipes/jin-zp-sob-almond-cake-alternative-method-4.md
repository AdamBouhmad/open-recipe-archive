## Ingredients

- half kilogram peeled almonds
- half kilogram finely ground sugar
- lemon zest shavings
- 16 to 20 eggs
- butter
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the almonds, dry them, crush them, and sift through a fine sieve
2. Weigh half kilogram of almonds and mix with half kilogram of finely ground sugar
3. Add lemon zest shavings and mix together
4. Beat everything thoroughly
5. Slowly beat 16 to 20 eggs into the dough
6. Beat again for about an hour
7. Pour into baking forms greased with butter and dusted with fine breadcrumbs
8. Bake slowly and carefully
