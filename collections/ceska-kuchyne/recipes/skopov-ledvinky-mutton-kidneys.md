## Ingredients

- mutton kidneys
- cold water
- onion
- butter
- caraway seeds
- breadcrumbs or flour
- pepper
- salt
- fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Slice the mutton kidneys lengthwise into thin strips and rinse in cold water.
2. Chop the onion finely and fry it in a piece of butter until golden.
3. Add the kidneys to it, sprinkle with a little caraway seed, and let them stew until tender.
4. Then season with salt and pepper, add either a little grated breadcrumbs or dust with flour, mix, and let stew a little longer.
5. Remove them onto a dish and surround with fried potatoes.
