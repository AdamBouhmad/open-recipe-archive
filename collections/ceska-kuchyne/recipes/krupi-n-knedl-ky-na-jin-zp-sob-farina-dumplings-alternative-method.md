## Ingredients

- two small pots of farina
- hot milk
- three eggs
- a little milk
- three slices of bread cut into small cubes and fried
- salt
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put two small pots of farina in a bowl
2. Pour it with one small pot of hot milk and stir until cooled
3. Add three eggs beaten with a little milk
4. Add three slices of bread cut into small cubes and fried
5. Salt and mix everything well
6. Wet your hands in cold water and shape round dumplings from the dough
7. Boil them in boiling water for half an hour
8. Remove them, tear apart with a fork, arrange on a dish
9. Sprinkle with grated bread roll or bread crust and pour melted fresh butter over them
10. You can also dress them with fried and chopped onion, or finely chopped and fried bacon
11. Instead of milk, you can scald the farina with hot water in which you have dissolved a piece of butter
