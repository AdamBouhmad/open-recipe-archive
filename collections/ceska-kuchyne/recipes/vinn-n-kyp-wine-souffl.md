## Ingredients

- 4 rolls
- red wine
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- sugar
- 4 tablespoons arak

## Directions

1. Cut 4 rolls into cubes and soak in red wine.
2. Beat 12 dekagrams butter and mix with 8 egg yolks, sugar, 4 tablespoons arak, and the soaked rolls.
3. Grease a mold and bake the soufflé in it over moderate heat.
