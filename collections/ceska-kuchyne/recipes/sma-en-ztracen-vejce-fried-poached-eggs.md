## Ingredients

- eggs
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs or crushed crackers
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook eggs using the previous poaching method
2. Let them cool
3. Coat with beaten egg
4. Sprinkle with grated bread or crushed crackers
5. Fry quickly in heated butter
