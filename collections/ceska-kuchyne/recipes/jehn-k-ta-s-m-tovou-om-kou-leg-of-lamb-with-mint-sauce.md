## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- salt
- butter or lard
- mint leaves (4 spoonfuls finely chopped)
- sugar (1 spoonful)
- paprika (pinch)
- wine vinegar (4-5 spoonfuls)
- broth or hot water

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb leg, scrape it, pound it and salt it
2. Place in a roasting pan greased with butter or lard
3. Dot with small pieces of butter
4. Roast with frequent basting with its own juices, adding a little broth or hot water during cooking
5. Meanwhile, mix four spoonfuls of finely chopped mint leaves with a spoonful of sugar and a pinch of paprika and mix well, pouring in a spoonful at a time of wine vinegar, four or five altogether
6. Place the roasted leg on a platter
7. Pour a little of the cooked and defatted meat juice over it
8. Serve the mint sauce separately in a bowl
