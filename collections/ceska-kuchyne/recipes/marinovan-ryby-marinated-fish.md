## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- Provençal oil
- parsley roots
- carrot
- celery
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves
- spices

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and coat with flour.
2. Roast in Provençal oil until golden.
3. Cook the parsley roots, carrot, celery, onion, thyme, bay leaf, cloves and all spices together.
4. Pour the mixture over the fish and let cool.
