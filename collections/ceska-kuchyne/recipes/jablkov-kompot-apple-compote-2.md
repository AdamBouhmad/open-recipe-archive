## Ingredients

- nice apples
- sugar
- water
- orange peel
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Select nice apples and peel them
2. Remove the cores and seeds
3. Place on a baking sheet
4. Add a little sugar, water, orange peel and cinnamon
5. Cover with a lid and stew
6. Transfer the stewed apples to a dish
7. Sprinkle with crushed sugar
