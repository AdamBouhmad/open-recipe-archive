## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon white flour
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 cups good broth or hot water

## Directions

1. Cook asparagus as in the previous recipe and arrange on a platter
2. For the gravy: cream together 2 tablespoons butter and beat in 4 egg yolks
3. Add 1 tablespoon white flour
4. Squeeze in juice from 1 lemon and salt to taste
5. Mix until it froths
6. Add 2 cups good broth or hot water and place over heat
7. Stir constantly until it thickens
8. Pour the gravy over the asparagus
