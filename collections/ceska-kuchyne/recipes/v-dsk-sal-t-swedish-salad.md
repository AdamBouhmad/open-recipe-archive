## Ingredients

- marinated bacon
- roasted beef
- cooked potatoes
- red beetroot
- apples
- sardines
- capers
- pickled cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs
- onion
- mugwort

## Directions

1. Cut marinated bacon into small cubes
2. Mix with roasted beef cut into small pieces
3. Add cooked potatoes, red beetroot, diced apples
4. Wash and clean sardines and add them
5. Add a little capers, pickled and peppered cucumbers
6. Add hard-boiled eggs cut into pieces
7. Add a little chopped onion and Siberian mugwort
8. Mix everything together
