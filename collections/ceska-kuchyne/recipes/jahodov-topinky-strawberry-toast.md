## Ingredients

- hard rolls
- sweetened wine
- sweetened strawberries
- hot butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls into slices, dip in sweetened wine, spread with sweetened strawberries, fry in hot butter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm.
