## Ingredients

- 20-25 quails
- 250 grams foie gras, cleaned, peeled and sliced thin
- fresh butter
- 2-3 onions
- 125 grams smoked ham, cut into cubes
- pepper
- parsley
- 1/2 to 3/4 liter red wine
- quail bones and carcasses
- strong beef and poultry broth

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts from the ribs of well-cleaned quails.
2. Heat fresh butter in a casserole and add sliced foie gras.
3. Add the peeled onions, smoked ham cut into cubes, pepper, parsley, and red wine.
4. Braise on hot coals.
5. Crush the quail carcasses finely, braise in fresh butter, and cook in very strong beef and poultry broth.
6. Strain everything and keep warm.
7. Arrange the quail portions on a platter, pour the sauce over them, and decorate variously for serving.
