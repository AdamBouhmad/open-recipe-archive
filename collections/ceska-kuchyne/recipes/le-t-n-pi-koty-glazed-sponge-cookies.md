## Ingredients

- 16 eggs
- beaten sugar
- 1500 grams finest flour
- glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 16 eggs in a bowl
2. Add beaten sugar
3. Heat everything several times and let cool again, stirring constantly throughout
4. Slowly mix in 1500 grams finest flour until dough forms
5. Place cookies on paper or wafers
6. Brush with glaze while baking, which gives them special shine and firmness
