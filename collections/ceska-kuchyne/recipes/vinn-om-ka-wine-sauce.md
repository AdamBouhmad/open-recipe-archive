## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon flour
- 2 dekagrams butter
- 10 dekagrams sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- a little salt
- 1 glass good wine, preferably Madeira

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, butter, sugar, egg yolks, and salt
2. Pour in a glass of good wine, preferably Madeira
3. Stir thoroughly and constantly on warm stove until it boils and cooks
4. Stir for a while longer then remove from heat
