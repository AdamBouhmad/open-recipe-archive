## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- sour cream
- cheese
- several eggs

## Directions

1. Slice raw potatoes thin
2. Mix on baking sheet with butter, salt, and sour cream in which cheese and several eggs have been beaten
3. Bake crisps on both sides until potatoes are completely soft
4. Turn out onto plate
5. Serve hot
