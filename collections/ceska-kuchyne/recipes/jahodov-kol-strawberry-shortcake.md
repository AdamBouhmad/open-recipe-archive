## Ingredients

- four cupfuls of flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- a little salt
- half a cupful of butter
- milk
- two quarts of strawberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put four cupfuls of flour into a bowl, add to it two teaspoons of baking powder, a little salt and mix well.
2. Add half a cupful of butter, finely crumbled, and three, until all the flour is well mixed with the butter.
3. Then add as much milk as needed to make a not too stiff dough, similar to biscuit dough.
4. Dust the rolling pin with flour, turn the dough onto it, roll it out a little more, divide into two halves, grease the baking pan with butter, roll out one half of the dough about half a finger thick and line the pan with it, spread with butter and place the second half of the dough, also rolled out, on top and put into a nice hot oven to bake.
5. Let it bake well, which takes about half an hour, then turn out onto a board, split and let cool.
6. In the meantime, mash two quarts of strawberries with sugar.
7. When the cake has cooled, place one half on a plate, the broken (inner) side facing up, spread a nice thick layer of prepared strawberries over it, place the second half of the cake on top, again with the inner side facing up and spread a thick layer of strawberries over the top.
8. Cut then into square pieces, place each one in a deep plate, pour over good thick whipped cream and bring to the table.
