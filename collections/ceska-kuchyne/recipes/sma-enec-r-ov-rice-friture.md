## Ingredients

- one cup of nice rice
- one quart of good milk
- two tablespoons of sugar
- salt
- four eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Rinse about one cup of nice rice several times in hot water, cook it in one quart of good milk, add two tablespoons of sugar, salt it a little and cook until soft.
2. Remove the porridge onto a plate, let it cool and mix it with four eggs and a piece of butter.
3. Heat four tablespoons of butter in a pan, put the mixed porridge into it and bake like the previous friture.
