## Ingredients

- two pounds veal
- butter
- one large onion, finely chopped
- two tablespoons vinegar
- one lemon cake
- one teaspoon flour
- one pint sour cream
- pepper
- salt
- fried potatoes for serving

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of veal into square pieces about two fingers thick and salt them
2. Place a piece of butter in a pan, add one large finely chopped onion, and put the meat in to roast until red
3. Add two tablespoons of vinegar and a lemon cake to the meat
4. Mix one teaspoon of flour with one pint of sour cream and add to the meat
5. Season with a little pepper and let it simmer for a while longer
6. Remove the meat to a platter, strain the gravy over it, and surround with fried potatoes
