## Ingredients

- 1 cup cleaned rice
- beef broth or hot water
- 3 egg yolks or 2 whole eggs
- piece of fresh butter
- beef broth
- flower (seasoning)
- 1 teaspoon green parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Rinse the cleaned rice first in cold water and then well in boiling water
2. Pour beef broth or hot water over it and let it cook until soft, being careful that it remains whole
3. In a small pot, beat together three egg yolks or two whole eggs with a piece of fresh butter and some beef broth
4. Season with flower, pour this over the rice and add the finely chopped green parsley, stirring gently
5. Place the cooked chicken on a platter, cover it with rice and serve
