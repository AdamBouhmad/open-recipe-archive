## Ingredients

- juice from two dozen large oysters
- oysters
- salt
- butter
- lemon juice
- flour
- sour cream
- egg yolks or whole egg
- parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Let the juice from two dozen large oysters come to a boil
2. Place the oysters in it, let them set slightly, then remove them
3. Salt the juice, add a spoonful of butter, lemon juice, a spoonful of flour mixed with a cup of sour cream and two beaten egg yolks or one whole egg
4. Keep stirring constantly over the fire and let it heat well, but it must not boil
5. Place the oysters on a hot platter
6. Pour the sauce over them
7. Sprinkle with a spoonful of finely chopped parsley
