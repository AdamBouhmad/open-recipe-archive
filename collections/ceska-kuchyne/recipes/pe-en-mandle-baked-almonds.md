## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (quarter pound) almonds, grated
- equal amount sugar
- equal amount flour
- 4 egg yolks
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Mix grated almonds, sugar, and flour
2. Add 4 egg yolks and make a stiff dough
3. Press almonds through a small mold
4. Either brush with butter or bake on a baking sheet in an oven
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Store in a dry place
