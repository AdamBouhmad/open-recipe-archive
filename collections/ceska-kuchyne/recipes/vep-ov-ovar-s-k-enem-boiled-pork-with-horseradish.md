## Ingredients

- piece of pork head
- piece of meat from the neck
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of the head and a piece of meat from the neck
2. Slice the meat into neat pieces
3. Serve hot on a preheated platter
4. Serve with grated horseradish
