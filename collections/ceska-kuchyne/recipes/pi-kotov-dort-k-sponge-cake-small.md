## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- fine lemon zest
- 0.5 kilogram finest potato flour
- egg white snow from remaining egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 0.5 kilogram sugar and add 20 egg yolks one by one
2. Mix and beat everything together
3. Add fine lemon zest
4. Prepare stiff snow from the remaining egg whites and add to the above dough
5. Add 0.5 kilogram finest potato flour
6. Spread the dough in buttered molds and bake slowly and carefully
