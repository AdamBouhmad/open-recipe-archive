## Ingredients

- Two woodcock
- Thirty grams butter
- One hundred grams lard, cut into cubes
- Three hundred grams mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
- Flour
- Half liter white wine
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the woodcock but do not cook them
2. Melt thirty grams butter with one hundred grams diced lard
3. Add three hundred grams cleaned mushrooms cut into slices
4. Place the woodcock in this to braise for at least ten minutes
5. Sprinkle with flour and pour in the fat
6. Pour half a liter of boiling white wine over, season with pepper and salt
7. Mix everything well, simmer and serve garnished with chopped parsley
