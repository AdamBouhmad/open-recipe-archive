## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams barley
- water
- butter
- 2 liters boiling water
- salt
- green parsley
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the barley in water with butter
2. Add 2 liters boiling water
3. Season with salt
4. Add green parsley and 2 egg yolks
