## Ingredients

- mutton meat, preferably from ribs
- vegetables
- two pounds of meat yields one cup of rice
- rice
- hot water
- butter
- broth from cooking the meat
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut mutton meat, preferably from ribs, into larger pieces and cook it with vegetables as for beef until tender.
2. For two pounds of meat, take one cup of rice, pick it over several times in hot water, pour broth in which you cooked the meat over it, and cook the rice until tender.
3. Then make a light butter roux, thicken the remaining broth with it, place the cooked meat on a shallow pan, pour the thickened broth over it, add the cooked rice to it, season with spices and let it simmer a little longer.
4. Then place the rice on a platter and top it with the cooked meat.
