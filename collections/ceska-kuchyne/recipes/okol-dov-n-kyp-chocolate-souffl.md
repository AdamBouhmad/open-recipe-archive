## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) sweet cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- juice from half a lemon
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams ground almonds
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) grated chocolate
- lemon zest from half a lemon
- 5 egg whites
- butter and flour for the mold

## Directions

1. Mix 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) of sweet cream with 2 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, and juice from half a lemon
2. Cook this mixture until it curdles, then strain off the water
3. Beat 18 dekagrams (12 lots) of butter with 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar and 10 egg yolks
4. Add 12 dekagrams of ground almonds and 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of grated chocolate
5. Add lemon zest from half a lemon
6. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 5 egg whites
7. Grease the mold with butter and dust with flour
8. Bake the soufflé, then sprinkle with sugar
