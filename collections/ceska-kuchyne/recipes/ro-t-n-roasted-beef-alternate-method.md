## Ingredients

- beef from ribs (250 grams per serving)
- salt
- pepper
- beef fat (15 grams/1 lot)
- beef broth (2 tablespoons)

## Directions

1. Pound the meat, salt and pepper it
2. Place on 15 grams of fat and braise under a lid until reddened
3. Pour 2 tablespoons of good beef broth over it
4. Braise for a quarter hour
