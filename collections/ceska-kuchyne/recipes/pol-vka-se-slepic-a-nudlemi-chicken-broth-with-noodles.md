## Ingredients

- one old hen
- two quarts of cold water
- salt
- some vegetables: celery, cabbage, parsley root
- a small handful of peas
- a little nutmeg
- fine noodles

## Directions

1. Clean an old hen and put it in a pot
2. Pour two quarts of cold water over it, salt it and let it cook
3. Clean it well, then add some vegetables such as celery, cabbage, parsley root and a small handful of peas
4. Let it cook for about three hours
5. When the hen is tender, remove the pot from the heat
6. Skim the fat, remove the hen, cut it into neat pieces and place in a bowl
7. Strain the broth, add a little nutmeg and cook fine noodles in it
8. Let it cook about 5 minutes and then pour into a soup tureen over the cut hen
