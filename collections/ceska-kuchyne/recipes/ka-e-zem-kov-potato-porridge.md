## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram potatoes
- 6 dekagrams butter (4 lots)
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- fried onion
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook 1 kilogram of potatoes, peel them, mash them and mix with 6 dekagrams of butter, 2 tablespoons of sour cream, an egg yolk, salt, fried onion and pepper
2. Put it with the dish in the oven, make criss-cross marks with a fork on the surface, and bake for a while
