## Ingredients

- beef broth
- potatoes
- all spices
- fresh mushrooms
- fried bread roll or hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Strain the beef broth, peel potatoes and cook them until half done in the broth
2. Add all spices and cook until the potatoes are completely soft
3. Strain the soup through a sieve and add fresh mushrooms and pour over fried bread roll or hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
