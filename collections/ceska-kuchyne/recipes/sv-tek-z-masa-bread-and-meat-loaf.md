## Ingredients

- bread rolls
- milk
- 3 eggs
- salt
- calf kidneys
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice bread rolls and soak in a mixture of milk, 3 eggs and salt
2. Mix in finely chopped calf kidneys
3. Bake in butter, stirring frequently, tear into larger pieces with a fork and serve with salad
