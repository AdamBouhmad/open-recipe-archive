## Ingredients

- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of grated butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon of flour
- 8 tablespoons of cooked, grated potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Mix the butter with egg yolks, eggs, flour, cooked grated potatoes, salt, nutmeg and parsley
2. Shape dumplings with a spoon
3. Cook in salted water
4. Serve in soup
