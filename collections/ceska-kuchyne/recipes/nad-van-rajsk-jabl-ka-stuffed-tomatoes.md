## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- White grated and fried bread
- Fresh parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the top like a lid and set it aside.
2. Remove the inside of the tomatoes with a small spoon using the indentations and place on a plate.
3. To this add white grated and fried bread, finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper, mix and fill the tomatoes with this and finally cover each with the lid again and place on a buttered and breadcrumb-coated baking pan, top with several pieces of butter and put in a not too hot oven.
4. Cover the top with ordinary thick paper, because they like to burn.
5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
