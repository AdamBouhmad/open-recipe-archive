## Ingredients

- small white onions
- butter
- good milk
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- bread, cut into cubes and toasted

## Directions

1. Peel small white onions and cut into small pieces
2. Stew in butter until soft and completely white
3. Place on a sieve so they drain completely
4. Take as much good milk as needed for the soup
5. Bring milk to a boil, salt and pepper it
6. Add the onions and bring everything to a boil together
7. After boiling, strain through a sieve
8. Beat a few egg yolks into it
9. Pour the soup prepared this way onto a plate into which bread cut into cubes and toasted has been placed
