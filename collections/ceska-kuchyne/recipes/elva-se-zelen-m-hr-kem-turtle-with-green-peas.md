## Ingredients

- small river turtles
- hot iron
- boiling water
- salt
- flour
- meat broth
- peas

## Directions

1. Kill small river turtles by placing hot iron on their rear end
2. Withdraw the turtles, throw them into boiling water
3. Wash and cook in salted water until they completely peel
4. Cut the meat into quarters, place on a baking pan
5. Prepare the peas in meat broth with browned flour
6. Add the peas to the turtle
