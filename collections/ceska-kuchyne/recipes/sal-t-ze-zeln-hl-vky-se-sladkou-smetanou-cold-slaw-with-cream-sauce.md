## Ingredients

- one fresh crisp head of cabbage
- salt
- half an onion
- two tablespoons of good butter
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one small cup of sweet cream
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice a nice fresh crisp cabbage head and put the cabbage in a wooden bowl
2. Sprinkle with salt, add half a finely chopped onion and pound it with a wooden pestle for 5 minutes
3. Put it in a bowl and pour the following sauce over it: Heat two tablespoons of good butter in a small pot
4. When it turns red, but before it begins to burn, add one tablespoon of vinegar
5. When it begins to boil, pour in one small cup of sweet cream with constant stirring so it does not curdle
6. Add a pinch of pepper
7. Pour this sauce over the salad
