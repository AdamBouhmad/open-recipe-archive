## Ingredients

- 1 liter ripe raspberries
- sugar
- 1 liter sour cream
- vanilla
- ripe raspberries for garnish

## Directions

1. Press 1 liter of ripe raspberries through a sieve
2. Mix with sugar
3. Beat 1 liter of sour cream with crushed vanilla on a cold place
4. Carefully add the raspberry juice
5. Garnish on a dish with ripe raspberries
