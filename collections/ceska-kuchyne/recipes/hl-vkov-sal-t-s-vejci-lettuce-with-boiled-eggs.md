## Ingredients

- nice white or red-speckled lettuce heads
- eggs
- good fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. From nice white or red-speckled lettuce heads, remove the outer leaves, cut the head into quarters
2. Rinse several times in clean water and let drain
3. Rub two hard-boiled egg yolks with three spoonfuls of good fresh heated and carefully strained butter, salt it and add a little ground pepper and several spoonfuls of wine vinegar, mix well
4. Pour this onto the prepared lettuce, mix it well, arrange on a salad dish and top with hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
