## Ingredients

- Butter or fresh lard
- One tablespoon of refined sugar
- One small glass of wine
- A little vinegar
- Meat broth
- Twelve peppercorns
- New spice
- Clove
- Small bay leaf
- A piece of lemon peel
- Salt
- A handful of large picked raisins
- Two ounces of peeled almonds

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter or fresh lard, add one tablespoon of refined sugar until it browns well
2. Add to this one small glass of wine, a little vinegar and as much meat broth as needed to make a thick gravy
3. Add twelve peppercorns, new spice, clove, a small bay leaf, and a piece of chopped lemon peel
4. Salt and let simmer for a quarter hour, then strain
5. Add a handful of large picked raisins and two ounces of peeled almonds sliced into strips
6. If the gravy is too thick, add a little meat broth, salt to taste and let simmer a little longer
7. Peel the cooked beef tongue, slice it into thick rounds, place in a deep platter and pour the prepared gravy over it
