## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of rice
- two spoons of flour
- fresh water
- good beef broth
- half an old hen (if possible)
- salt
- a piece of fresh butter
- four egg yolks
- three spoons of sour cream

## Directions

1. Pick over one coffee cup of rice and clean it several times in boiling water.
2. Dissolve two spoons of flour in fresh water, add this to the rice, then pour good beef broth over it, in which (if possible) half an old hen has been cooked.
3. Now place the pot on the fire and stir diligently until the broth comes to a boil. Then season with salt and add a piece of fresh butter, and let it cook slowly for about an hour, adding a little beef broth meanwhile.
4. Then strain the broth through a fine sieve and place it back on the fire until it comes to a boil again; before bringing to the table, beat four egg yolks in three spoons of sour cream and mix them in.
