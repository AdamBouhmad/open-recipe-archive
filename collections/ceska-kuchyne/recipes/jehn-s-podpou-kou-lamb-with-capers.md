## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- butter
- bouillon
- onion
- aromatic vegetables
- bay leaf
- clove
- capers
- lemon slices
- mushrooms
- anchovies
- white wine
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the lamb meat into pieces, wash, salt and pepper it.
2. Brown it in butter and add bouillon.
3. Add onion, aromatic vegetables, bay leaf, and clove, and cook together until the meat is completely tender.
4. Strain the sauce and add capers, lemon slices, finely chopped sautéed mushrooms, and anchovies.
5. Pour white wine over everything, thicken with flour, and pour the sauce over the meat.
