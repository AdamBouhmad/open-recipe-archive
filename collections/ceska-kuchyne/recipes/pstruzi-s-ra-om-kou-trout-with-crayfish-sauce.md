## Ingredients

- cleaned trout
- salt
- crayfish butter
- crayfish sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and cut trout into pieces
2. Salt and arrange in a small baking dish greased with crayfish butter
3. Braise until completely soft
4. Pour crayfish sauce over and serve
