## Ingredients

- brown roux
- salt
- pepper
- flower
- chopped parsley
- chopped chives
- half liter cream
- cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Add salt, pepper, flower, chopped parsley and chopped chives to brown roux
2. Mix with half liter cream
3. Cook the sauce
4. Add a plate of sliced cooked potatoes
5. Cook and serve
