## Ingredients

- Liver
- Salt
- Lard
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter
- 3.5 deciliters sweet cream

## Directions

1. Remove all skin from the liver and salt it
2. Pierce with lard and season with pepper
3. Coat in flour and brown on both sides
4. Place in sweet cream and cook for a quarter hour so the sauce takes on flavor from the liver
