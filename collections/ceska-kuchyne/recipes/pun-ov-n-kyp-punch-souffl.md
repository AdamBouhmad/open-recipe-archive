## Ingredients

- half a liter of cream
- 95 grams (6 lots) butter
- 180 grams (12 lots) sugar
- 180 grams flour
- 10 egg yolks
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- juice from 2 lemons
- half a liter of rum
- a little salt
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook half a liter of cream, 95 grams (6 lots) of butter, and 180 grams (12 lots) of sugar together
2. Sprinkle in 180 grams of flour and stir over gentle heat until a paste forms
3. Let cool
4. Mix in 10 egg yolks, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, juice from 2 lemons, half a liter of rum, and a little salt
5. Fold in stiff egg white snow
6. Bake for one hour
