## Ingredients

- onion
- goose fat or butter
- goose blood
- caraway
- salt
- breadcrumb
- milk

## Directions

1. Finely chop half an onion, add goose fat or butter and simmer. To the goose blood add caraway, salt, grated breadcrumb and a little milk. Mix well, pour onto the onion and fry for ten minutes with continuous stirring. Place on a plate, surround with sliced breadcrumb rolls.
