## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Water
- Breadcrumbs
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Clean the asparagus and trim the lower woody white ends so all spears are the same length
2. Wash thoroughly, arrange heads together and tie loosely
3. Cook in salted water, being careful not to overcook so the heads do not fall apart
4. Carefully drain the water and arrange on a platter in a circle with heads together
5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and pour hot melted fresh butter over top
