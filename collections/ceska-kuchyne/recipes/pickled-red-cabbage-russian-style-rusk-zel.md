## Ingredients

- Well-aged sour cabbage
- Mustard
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Raisins

## Directions

1. Press out the well-aged, sour cabbage and place on a dish
2. Pour mustard, oil, vinegar and sugar over it
3. Mix everything together
4. Add raisins
