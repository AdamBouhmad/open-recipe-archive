## Ingredients

- milk
- pressed yeast
- sugar
- flour
- butter
- 2 egg yolks
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Warm milk until lukewarm
2. Add yeast and sugar and mix well with milk
3. Add flour and beat well
4. Let rise in a warm place
5. Cream butter
6. Add egg yolks one by one
7. Add lemon zest and cinnamon
8. When starter rises, add to butter mixture
9. Salt and mix well
10. Add lukewarm milk and sifted flour
11. Add more flour if needed to reach proper consistency
12. Beat dough well
13. Dust with flour and let rise
14. When risen, dust surface with flour
15. Scoop pieces of dough with a spoon, shape into balls
16. Flatten slightly on work surface with fingers
17. Place on buttered baking pan
18. Brush with egg yolk mixed with milk
19. Top with cheese curd or jam
20. Let rise again
21. Bake until golden
22. Turn out onto plate and dust with sugar
