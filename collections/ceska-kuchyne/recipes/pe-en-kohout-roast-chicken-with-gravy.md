## Ingredients

- rooster
- egg
- breadcrumb
- fat
- caraway
- onion
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Clean the rooster well, cut into pieces
2. Coat in egg and then in breadcrumbs
3. Place on a pan, add a little fat, sprinkle with caraway and onion, cover
4. Bake gently until cooked
5. Remove the rooster
6. To the fat in the pan, add flour as desired for thickness
7. Brown until golden
8. Add milk to the pan as desired for sauce consistency
9. Serve the sauce with the rooster
