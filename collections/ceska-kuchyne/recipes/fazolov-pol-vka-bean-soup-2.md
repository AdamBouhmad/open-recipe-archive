## Ingredients

- Small white pearl beans
- Beef soup broth or water in which vegetables were cooked
- Onion
- Carrot
- Parsley
- Parsnip
- Butter
- Salt
- Garlic
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook small white pearl beans until soft
2. Press the cooked beans through a sieve
3. Make a light butter roux
4. Pour beef soup broth or water in which vegetables were cooked into the roux
5. Cook onion, carrot, parsley and parsnip in the broth, then strain and use only the liquid
6. Thin the bean paste with the vegetable water according to taste
7. Season with salt
8. Add a small piece of garlic crushed with salt and a little marjoram
