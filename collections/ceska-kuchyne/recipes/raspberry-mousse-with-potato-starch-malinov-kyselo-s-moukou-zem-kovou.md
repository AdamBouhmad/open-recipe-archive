## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram raspberries, currants, blueberries and similar berries
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 1 liter juice
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) sugar
- 1/4 liter potato starch flour dissolved in water or wine
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pick through 1/2 kilogram of raspberries, currants, blueberries and similar
2. Cook with cinnamon and lemon zest
3. Strain through cloth
4. Cook 1 liter of juice with 24 dekagrams (16 lots) of sugar and 1/4 liter of potato starch flour mixed in water or wine
5. Stir constantly until a thick porridge forms
6. Pour into a moistened mold to cool
7. Turn out onto a plate
8. Serve at table with sugar and sweet cream
