## Ingredients

- one pound can of salmon
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- one teaspoon sugar
- one teaspoon salt
- one teaspoon mustard powder
- one tablespoon overheated fresh butter
- two tablespoons vinegar
- equal amount of as white lettuce head as possible, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Take one pound can of salmon and remove the bones
2. Chop the meat finely
3. Chop equal amount of as white lettuce head as possible
4. Rub four hard-boiled egg yolks together
5. Add one teaspoon sugar, one teaspoon salt, one teaspoon mustard powder, one tablespoon overheated fresh butter and two tablespoons vinegar
6. Mix well and pour this mixture over the salmon meat
