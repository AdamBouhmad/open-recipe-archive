## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 3 teaspoons milk
- 3 teaspoons wine
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg until as thick as batter consistency
2. Add 3 teaspoons milk, 3 teaspoons wine, and a little sugar
3. When the dough is thick enough, dip a mold in hot butter, then in the dough, and fry quickly
4. Remove the basket from the mold and fill with château cream and serve
