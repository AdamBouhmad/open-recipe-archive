## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- salt
- water
- butter
- sweet cream
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the mackerel overnight in three quarts of water if preparing for breakfast
2. The next morning, drain the water and pour boiling water over the fish, let it sit for one or two minutes without boiling
3. Drain again and place the fish on a well-buttered baking sheet or pot, skin side up
4. Pour half a cup of sweet cream over it and season with pepper to taste
5. Place in the oven and let it brown
6. If preparing for lunch, soak the mackerel in the morning and let it soak for 6 hours
