## Ingredients

- red beets
- potatoes
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean several red beets and cook them, place in cold water, and when cooled, peel and slice into discs.
2. Slice an equal quantity of cooked, cooled potatoes likewise into discs.
3. In a pot, place a layer of potatoes, then a layer of beets, until the container is full.
4. Pour good wine vinegar over and let stand for a while; this mixture is served with beef.
