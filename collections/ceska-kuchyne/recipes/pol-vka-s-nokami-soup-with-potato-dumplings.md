## Ingredients

- tablespoon of chopped parsley
- butter
- flour
- 2 liters of beef broth
- 10-12 cooked potatoes
- 2 egg yolks
- 8 cooked potatoes
- chopped veal
- butter
- eggs
- several tablespoons of cream
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated cheese
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Stew chopped parsley in butter with flour; add to 2 liters of beef broth into which 10-12 cooked potatoes and 2 egg yolks have been pressed through a sieve
2. Make potato dumplings from 8 cooked grated potatoes, chopped veal, butter, eggs, several tablespoons of cream, salt, nutmeg and grated cheese, sprinkled with grated bread
