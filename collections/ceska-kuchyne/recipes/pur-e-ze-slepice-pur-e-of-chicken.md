## Ingredients

- meat from roasted or boiled chicken
- butter
- small onion
- beef broth
- roll
- hard-boiled eggs
- grated roll or pastry

## Directions

1. Chop the meat from roasted or boiled chicken finely
2. Stew it about a quarter hour on butter with a finely chopped small onion
3. Pound it in a mortar into a paste
4. Depending on the amount of meat, let one or half a roll cook soft in one quart of beef broth
5. Add four to six hard-boiled crushed egg yolks and the pounded meat
6. Mix everything well and press through a sieve
7. Thin with beef broth as needed
8. Pour it into a soup bowl on fried grated roll cubes or on pastry
9. Other meats can also be used for this soup
10. Alternative method: Cook an old hen in beef broth until soft, cut out the breasts and chop them finely
11. Pound the rest of the hen in a mortar, put it in a pot, add chopped parsley, celery and carrot, pour in beef broth and let it cook for one hour
12. Then pour it onto the roll or pastry and the chopped breasts
