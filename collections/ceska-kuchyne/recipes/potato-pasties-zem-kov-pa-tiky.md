## Ingredients

- 12-15 mealy potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- onion, chopped
- butter
- 6 egg yolks
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes and pass through a sieve
2. Fry with salt, nutmeg, and chopped onion in butter
3. When the mixture no longer sticks to the pan, transfer to a platter
4. Add egg yolks
5. Roll dough thickly on a board
6. Cut out circles with a form or small glass
7. Brush both sides with beaten egg
8. Sprinkle with grated roll crumbs
9. Mark a circle on the pasties with a smaller glass
10. Bake on butter until golden on both sides
11. Cut out the marked circle, deepen the hole with a spoon
12. Fill with any meat mixture
13. Serve as a main dish or with beef soup
