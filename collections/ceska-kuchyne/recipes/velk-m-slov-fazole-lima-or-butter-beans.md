## Ingredients

- 1 pint large butter beans
- Salt water
- 1 small cup sour cream or milk
- 1 spoonful butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Shell one pint of large butter beans from their pods and cook in salted water until soft.
2. Then drain them, pour a small cup of sour cream or milk over them, add one spoonful of butter, salt and pepper, let it simmer a bit more and serve.
