## Ingredients

- half kilogram flour
- sugar
- 30 grams (2 lots) yeast
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- salt
- warm milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, butter, salt and as much warm milk as needed for a thick batter.
2. Allow to ferment briefly.
3. Pour onto a greased omelet pan and fry on both sides.
4. They may be rolled up and baked in the oven, or left unrolled, spread with fat and stacked on a plate.
