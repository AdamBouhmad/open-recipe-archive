## Ingredients

- half kilogram almonds
- sour cream
- half kilogram sugar
- 8 eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- juice of one lemon
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) potato flour
- egg white snow from 8 whites

## Directions

1. Pound half kilogram almonds together with sour cream
2. Add half kilogram sugar, 8 eggs, 8 egg yolks, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, and lemon juice
3. Beat everything together for a whole hour
4. Finally add 12 dekagrams potato flour and egg white snow from 8 whites
5. Pour into a baking form greased with butter and dusted with fine breadcrumbs
6. Bake for over an hour
