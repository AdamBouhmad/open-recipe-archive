## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) flour
- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- snow from egg whites
- lemon peel
- 3 1/2 deciliters sour cream
- 3 3/4 deciliters milk

## Directions

1. Beat 18 dekagrams flour, 24 dekagrams sugar, 2 whole eggs, 10 egg yolks, snow from egg whites, and lemon peel together.
2. Grease a mold, sprinkle with flour, and bake the soufflé.
3. Mix 3 1/2 deciliters sour cream and 3 3/4 deciliters milk.
4. Pour this mixture over the soufflé and sprinkle with sugar.
