## Ingredients

- best wheat flour
- milk
- yeast
- salt
- eggs (to taste)
- sugar (to taste)
- vanilla (to taste)

## Directions

1. Make a loaf from best wheat flour, milk, yeast, salt, and add eggs, sugar, and vanilla to taste
2. Bake well until done
3. Let cool and slice
4. Place the sliced pieces on both sides on a buttered sheet
5. Toast until golden, then sprinkle with sugar
