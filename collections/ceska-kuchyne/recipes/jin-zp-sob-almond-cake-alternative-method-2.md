## Ingredients

- 26 dekagrams (16 lots) ground almonds
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 2 eggs
- 16 egg yolks
- 227 grams (13 lots) flour
- egg white snow from 10 whites
- piece of lemon zest

## Directions

1. Mix 26 dekagrams ground almonds, 28 dekagrams sugar, 2 eggs, 16 egg yolks, 227 grams flour, egg white snow from 10 whites, and a piece of lemon zest
2. Pour into a greased and flour-dusted baking form
3. Bake
