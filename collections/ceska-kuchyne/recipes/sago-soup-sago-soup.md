## Ingredients

- sago
- ordinary beef soup
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse sago several times in lukewarm water and finally in hot water
2. Place it in a pot and pour ordinary beef soup over it
3. Cook until soft, which takes only a few minutes
4. Salt it, flavor with a little pepper and serve
5. Calculate one spoonful of raw sago per person
