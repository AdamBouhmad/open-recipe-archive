## Ingredients

- Codfish
- water
- milk
- butter
- flour
- eggs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak a pound of codfish in a shallow gallon-sized dish of water for 3 to 4 hours or overnight if preparing for breakfast
2. Once soaked, transfer to another vessel, pour fresh cold water over it and bring to boil
3. When the fish boils, drain the water and let it cool
4. Remove the skin and bones carefully and flake the meat
5. Place the fish in another vessel, pour milk over it until covered, and bring to boil
6. Mix a heaping spoonful of butter with a heaping spoonful of flour and add this to the boiling milk while stirring constantly
7. Beat one or two eggs and add to the sauce, mix well and season with pepper to taste
8. If the fish was too much soaked, salt to taste
