## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- half a pound (1.5 cups) of farina
- three tablespoons of butter
- six egg yolks
- six tablespoons of fine sugar
- rind of half a lemon, grated
- salt
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the milk and farina together into a thick porridge
2. Cream three tablespoons of butter until frothy
3. Gradually mix in six egg yolks
4. Add the cooked porridge
5. Add six tablespoons of fine sugar
6. Add the grated lemon rind
7. Season with a little salt
8. Gently fold in the stiff snow made from six egg whites
9. Pour into a buttered mold
10. Dust with sugar
11. Bake in a well-heated oven for three-quarters of an hour
12. Instead of farina, you may also use breadcrumb porridge, semolina porridge, or noodles cooked in milk
