## Ingredients

- dough prepared by method previously mentioned
- filling of choice, prepared
- butter for greasing
- egg, beaten
- sliced almonds or butter mixed with sugar and flour

## Directions

1. Prepare dough by the method previously mentioned, work it until it no longer sticks to the spoon, let it rise, then roll it out as thin as possible on a flour-dusted surface
2. Cut small square pieces from the dough
3. Place a piece of prepared filling in the center of each piece
4. Fold opposite corners of the dough together on both sides
5. Place the tarts on a buttered baking sheet
6. Brush them with beaten egg
7. Sprinkle either with chopped almonds or with butter mixed with sugar and flour
8. Let them rise a little more
9. Bake in a well-heated oven and sprinkle with sugar after baking
