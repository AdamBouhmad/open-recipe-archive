## Ingredients

- half a liter of peas
- soft water
- 2 liters of beef broth
- pepper
- marjoram
- garlic ground with salt
- pale butter roux
- fried bread roll or bread
- or groats or rice cooked in butter

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in soft water until they fall apart into a porridge
2. Pour 2 liters of beef broth over them, add a little pepper, marjoram, garlic ground with salt and a little pale butter roux
3. Strain everything, let it boil for a while and pour it over fried bread roll or bread; alternatively, groats or rice cooked in butter can be used instead of bread
