## Ingredients

- one bottle burgundy wine
- two ounces white sago, well rinsed and scalded with hot water
- half pound sugar
- a piece of cinnamon
- a small piece of well-cut orange peel

## Directions

1. Put one bottle of burgundy wine in a pot
2. add two ounces of well-rinsed and scalded white sago, half pound of sugar, a piece of cinnamon and a small piece of well-cut orange peel
3. let it boil until the sago softens sufficiently
4. if the soup becomes too thick, add more hot wine
5. pour the soup into a bowl and serve
