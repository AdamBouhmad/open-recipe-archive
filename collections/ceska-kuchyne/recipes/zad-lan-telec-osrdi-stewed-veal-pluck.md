## Ingredients

- veal lungs and heart
- butter
- flour
- onion
- parsley
- beef broth
- lemon zest
- sugar
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean veal lungs with heart and boil until tender
2. Let cool and slice into strips
3. Meanwhile melt 2 spoonfuls of butter on the stove
4. Add a heaping spoonful of flour and roast it slowly on mild heat with finely chopped onion until the onion turns yellow
5. Then add finely chopped parsley and mix it with good beef broth
6. Add the sliced pluck, finely chopped lemon zest, a spoonful of sugar browned until dark, and vinegar
7. Let it simmer for another half hour
8. When bringing the pluck to the table, salt it and squeeze the juice from half a lemon over it
