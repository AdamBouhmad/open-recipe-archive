## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- hot water
- sugar
- cold water
- blanched almonds
- lemon peel
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place ripe plums on a sieve and pour hot water over them
2. Peel them and place on a clay baking sheet one next to another
3. Sprinkle with sugar and pour cold water over
4. Cover and braise slowly
5. Alternatively: pit the parboiled and peeled plums and insert half a blanched almond in place of the pit
6. Place on a covered baking sheet, sprinkle with sugar, add lemon peel and whole cinnamon
