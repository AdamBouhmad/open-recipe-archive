## Ingredients

- 2 liters strawberries
- 24 dekagrams (18 lots) crushed sugar
- beaten snow from 2.5 liters cream

## Directions

1. Press the strawberries through a sieve
2. Mix in the crushed sugar and beaten snow from the cream
3. Arrange in snow-like mounds
