## Ingredients

- Sprouted potatoes
- Salt
- Parsley
- Water

## Directions

1. Partially peel the potatoes in small strips.
2. Place in salted water with parsley.
3. Cook slowly, but do not fully cook through.
4. Drain and allow to finish cooking in steam.
