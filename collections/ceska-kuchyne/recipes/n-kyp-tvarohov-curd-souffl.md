## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 12 dekagrams peeled sweet almonds
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- a little salt
- 165 grams (11 lots) curd
- snow from 8 egg whites
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams butter.
2. Add 8 egg yolks, 9 dekagrams sugar, 12 dekagrams peeled sweet almonds, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, a little salt, and 165 grams curd.
3. Fold in snow from 8 egg whites.
4. Bake in a greased mold over moderate heat.
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
