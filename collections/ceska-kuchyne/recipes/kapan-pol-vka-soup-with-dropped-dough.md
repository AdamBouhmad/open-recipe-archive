## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- salt
- water
- eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Let a piece of fresh butter heat up and pour as much boiling salted water on it as you need soup
2. Put two eggs in a cup and only as much flour as to make a thin dripping dough
3. Mix well and drip slowly into the boiling soup, which you must constantly stir so that large lumps do not form
4. If the dripping is too thin it will thicken by adding flour, if too thick it will thin by adding a few drops of water
5. You can also prepare this soup in another way by browning the salted water with butter roux and a piece of onion, finely chopped and browned until golden
