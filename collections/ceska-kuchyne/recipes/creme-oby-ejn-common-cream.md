## Ingredients

- several spoons of flour
- 10-12 egg yolks
- 20-30 grams sugar
- cream
- appropriate and optional spices
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix several spoons of flour with 10-12 egg yolks
2. Add 20-30 grams sugar, mix with cream and add suitable and optional spices
3. Cook in steam in a vessel surrounded by water until it thickens
4. When everything cools, add stiff snow from egg whites
