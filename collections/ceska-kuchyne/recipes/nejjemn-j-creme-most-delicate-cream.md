## Ingredients

- chicken gizzards
- best cream
- vanilla, coffee, tea, chocolate or similar flavorings
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean chicken gizzards thoroughly, rinse them, and dry them slowly
2. When ready to use, crush them coarsely
3. Heat the best cream with various suitable additions such as vanilla, coffee, tea, or chocolate, sweetened well and not too hot
4. Pour over the crushed gizzards, which gradually dissolve
5. Proceed as with other cooked creams
6. Serve in the same containers in which it was made
