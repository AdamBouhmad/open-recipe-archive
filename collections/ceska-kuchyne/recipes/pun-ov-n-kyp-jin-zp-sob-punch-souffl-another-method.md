## Ingredients

- half a bottle of wine
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) beaten sugar
- 16 peeled Meissen apples
- 1 bottle of punch
- finely beaten sugar

## Directions

1. Cook half a bottle of wine with 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of beaten sugar and 16 peeled Meissen apples on a baking sheet with constant stirring
2. When completely softened, pour in 1 bottle of punch and sprinkle with finely beaten sugar
3. Pass a red-hot spatula several times over the sugar to form a glaze
4. Serve the soufflé while it is still warm
