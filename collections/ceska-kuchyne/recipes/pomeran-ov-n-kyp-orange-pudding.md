## Ingredients

- 4 large oranges
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 quart milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel and separate 4 large oranges, place on a pudding dish, sprinkle with 1 cup sugar, and let sit
2. Heat 1 quart milk and when simmering, mix 2 tablespoons cornstarch in cold milk and stir into the hot milk
3. Add 1/2 cup sugar, 3 egg yolks, mix well, and when thickened, remove from heat and let cool
4. When cooled, pour over the oranges
5. Whip 3 egg whites, add 1 tablespoon sugar, spread on top, and bake to brown
