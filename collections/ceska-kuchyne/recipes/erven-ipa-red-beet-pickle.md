## Ingredients

- red beets
- horseradish
- vinegar
- fennel

## Directions

1. Clean the red beets, rinse them and place whole in a pot; it is better to leave them whole than to cut them in half lengthwise, and cook until soft.
2. Place in cold water, let cool, and peel; slice into discs.
3. Scrape a nice piece of good horseradish and slice it likewise into discs.
4. If the beets are to be eaten immediately, boil the wine vinegar; if to be stored longer, do not boil it.
5. In a container, layer beets, sprinkle with fennel, then add a layer of horseradish.
6. When the container is full, pour vinegar over and seal.
7. Usually served with beef.
