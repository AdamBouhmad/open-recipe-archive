## Ingredients

- meat broth
- salt
- a handful of caraway seeds
- bread or roll, fried

## Directions

1. Make a well-browned meat broth
2. Add salt and a handful of caraway seeds to water
3. When the soup boils, add the broth so it becomes quite thick
4. Fry bread or roll and strain the soup over it
