## Ingredients

- 1 onion, chopped
- pepper berries
- whole allspice
- cloves
- water
- vinegar
- veal fillet with kidney
- salt
- bacon or lard
- vegetables (carrot, parsnip, celery, onion)
- pan juices
- 3 egg yolks
- beef broth
- sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Put a chopped onion, pepper berries, whole allspice and cloves in a pot
2. Add water and vinegar, bring to a boil and let simmer for half an hour, then cool
3. Place cleaned veal fillet in a roasting pan, pour the cooled pickling liquid over it, and let it rest in a cool place for 3 to 4 days, turning it daily
4. Remove the fillet and lard it with thin strips of bacon or lard, salt it
5. Place on a baking pan with chopped vegetables underneath
6. Add half a cup of the pickling liquid and roast until cooked through
7. Remove from oven, slice into cutlets and place on a heated platter
8. Beat 3 egg yolks with a little broth in a small cup
9. Pour this into the simmering strained sauce, stirring over heat until the sauce begins to rise, then pour over the meat
10. For basting you may use sour cream instead of wine, and serve with potato croquettes or sliced and fried dumplings
