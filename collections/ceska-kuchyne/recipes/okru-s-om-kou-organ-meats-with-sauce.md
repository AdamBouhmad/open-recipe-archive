## Ingredients

- organ meats
- salted water
- root vegetables
- flour
- butter
- parsley
- good broth
- wine
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- sour cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook cleaned organ meats in salted water with root vegetables
2. Make a roux with flour and butter, add fried parsley, good broth and wine
3. Cut the organ meats, add lemon juice, nutmeg and egg yolks beaten with sour cream
