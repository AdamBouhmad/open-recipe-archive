## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon peel
- juice
- half lemon
- quarter liter sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut cooked potatoes into slices
2. Braise on a pan with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, lemon peel, juice and half a lemon
3. Pour over quarter liter of sour cream
4. After a short time serve the sauce in a warm dish
