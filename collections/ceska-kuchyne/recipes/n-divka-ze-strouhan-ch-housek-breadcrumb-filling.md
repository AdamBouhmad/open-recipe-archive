## Ingredients

- 2 rolls (housky)
- good butter
- blanched almonds, chopped
- raisins
- sugar
- vanilla or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate 2 rolls
2. Fry the grated breadcrumbs in good butter and let them cool
3. Mix with chopped blanched almonds, raisins, and sugar either pounded with vanilla or mixed with cinnamon
