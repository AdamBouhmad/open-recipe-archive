## Ingredients

- one pound macaroni
- two tablespoons fresh butter
- salt
- Swiss cheese, grated
- new butter
- ham, sliced thin (optional)
- cream
- four egg yolks
- cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Break the pound of macaroni into pieces about two inches long
2. Boil in salted water, add two tablespoons fresh butter, salt lightly, and cook about half an hour
3. Drain the macaroni, grease a dish with butter, place a layer of cooked macaroni in it
4. Sprinkle with grated Swiss cheese and place pieces of fresh butter on top
5. Continue layering macaroni, cheese, and butter until all is used, ending with a layer of macaroni topped with pieces of butter
6. Let stand in a hot oven for about three quarters of an hour without baking
7. Remove, sprinkle generously with grated cheese and bring to the table
8. Optional: add thinly sliced ham to the layers
9. For baked macaroni: prepare as above in a baking dish, beat four egg yolks with cream in a bowl, pour over the macaroni, bake until golden in the oven, and sprinkle with cheese
