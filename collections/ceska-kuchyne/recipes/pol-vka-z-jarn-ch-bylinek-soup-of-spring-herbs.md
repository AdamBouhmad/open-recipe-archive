## Ingredients

- sorrel
- yarrow
- strawberry leaves
- violet leaves
- caraway leaves
- primrose
- chicory
- chervil
- green parsley
- young green onions
- watercress
- mache
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- cold cream
- bread cubes

## Directions

1. In spring, various herbs are gathered from which a very tasty and healthy soup can be made
2. Take sorrel, yarrow, strawberry leaves, violet leaves, caraway leaves, primrose, chicory, chervil, green parsley, young green onions, watercress, mache, clean everything well and rinse in several waters
3. Then on a board chop it finely and let cook in salted water until soft
4. Then in a small pot mix a piece of butter, three spoonfuls of flour, three egg yolks and three spoonfuls of cold cream
5. Pour it into the soup, whisk well — but it must not cook any longer — and pour into a bowl on fried bread cubes
