## Ingredients

- beef meat
- beef fat
- onion
- assorted chopped vegetables
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- beef soup

## Directions

1. Trim all fat from the meat before cooking it for soup
2. Cook the meat
3. Cut the fat into cubes
4. Add onion and all chopped vegetables
5. Salt and add three grains of pepper
6. Braise until brown, being careful not to scorch it by regularly basting with beef soup
7. Mix in 3 spoonfuls of flour
8. Pour more soup over it and simmer
