## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram ripe cherries
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) sugar dissolved in water
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pour the dissolved sugar (or a mixture of sugar with lemon juice, cinnamon, and lemon peel) over the ripe cherries
2. Shake the mixture several times
3. Cook for half an hour
