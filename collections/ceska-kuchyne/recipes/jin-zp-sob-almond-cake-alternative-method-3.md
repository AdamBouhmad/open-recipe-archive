## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) ground almonds
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 7 dekagrams (4 lots) flour
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 egg
- egg white snow from 5 whites

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dekagrams ground almonds, 14 dekagrams sugar, 7 dekagrams flour, 8 egg yolks, 1 egg, and egg white snow from 5 whites
2. Grease a baking form with butter and dust with flour
3. Pour in the mixture
4. Bake
