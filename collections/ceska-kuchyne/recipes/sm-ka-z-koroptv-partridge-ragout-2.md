## Ingredients

- partridges
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) raw veal
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) smoked meat
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- parsley cut into small pieces
- bay leaf
- several peppercorns
- butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- broth
- lemon
- bunch of herbs
- sardine butter
- lemon juice
- white wine
- rice

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges in the usual manner.
2. Cut the raw veal and smoked meat into cubes.
3. Braise everything together with onion, carrot, celery, parsley, bay leaf and peppercorns in butter.
4. Sprinkle flour over and brown it.
5. Add broth and simmer with lemon and a bunch of herbs.
6. Strain the sauce.
7. Mix the sauce with partridge juices and add sardine butter, lemon juice and white wine.
8. Place the partridges in the hot sauce and surround with rice.
