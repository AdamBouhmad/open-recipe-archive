## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1½ cupfuls of sour cream
- 1 teaspoonful of baking soda
- vanilla to taste
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cupful of butter and 2 cupfuls of sugar well
2. Add 3 eggs and mix well again
3. Add 1½ cupfuls of sour cream, 1 teaspoonful of baking soda, and vanilla to taste
4. Mix in flour until the dough is thick
5. Roll out thin, cut with a form, sprinkle with sugar and bake
