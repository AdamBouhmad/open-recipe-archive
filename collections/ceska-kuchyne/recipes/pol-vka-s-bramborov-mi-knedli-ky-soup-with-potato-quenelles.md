## Ingredients

- three ounces of butter
- five egg yolks
- one whole egg
- six tablespoons of grated potatoes
- a heaping spoon of nice flour
- a bit of chopped green parsley
- salt
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Beat three ounces of butter on a plate, add five egg yolks and one whole egg
2. Add six tablespoons of grated potatoes, a heaping spoon of nice flour, a bit of chopped green parsley, and salt
3. Mix and beat everything well together
4. Drop small spoonfuls of the mixture into boiling strained broth as quenelles
5. Let them cook for several minutes then pour into a serving bowl
6. If desired, brown the soup slightly with white roux
