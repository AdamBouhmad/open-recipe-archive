## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Salt
- Fresh butter
- Water
- Bread crumbs
- Caraway seeds
- Sage sprigs
- Anchovies
- Vinegar
- Water
- Onion
- New spices
- Pepper
- Lemon peel
- Carrot
- Parsley root
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Austrian wine
- Burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from the veal leg; bones may be removed, meat is cleaned, preferably in soft water.
2. If from an older calf, pound it, salt it, coat with fresh butter, add water and roast in the oven on a baking pan until golden.
3. Alternatively, after partial roasting, coat with butter and sprinkle with bread crumbs, then finish roasting.
4. Alternatively, coat with butter and sprinkle with caraway seeds, or coat with butter and place four sage sprigs underneath.
5. Alternatively, blend anchovies with butter, coat the leg with this and roast.
6. Alternatively, salt the leg and place on a baking pan with vinegar mixed with water, onion, new spices, pepper, piece of lemon peel, carrot, parsley root, larger onion, thyme, bay leaf and butter. Let it rest for 12 hours, turning occasionally so it marinates evenly on both sides. Roast the next day. To the sauce left after roasting, add three-quarters deciliter of Austrian wine, a piece of burnt sugar and boil. Strain the sauce over the leg, which has been sliced, and surround it with either butter dough or potatoes.
7. Alternatively, take the leg, pierce it with bacon, salt it, place bacon underneath on a baking pan and roast until golden.
