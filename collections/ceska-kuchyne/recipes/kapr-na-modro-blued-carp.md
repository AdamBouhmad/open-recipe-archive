## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Vinegar (wine)
- Celery
- Parsley
- Onion
- Garlic cloves
- Whole pepper
- Allspice
- 5 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- Ginger
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Clean the carp without scaling, remove entrails and cut into pieces
2. Arrange on a platter
3. Heat 1 pint wine vinegar with a little salt
4. Pour the hot vinegar over the carp, which will turn a nice blue color
5. Drain the vinegar into a pot and add more vinegar
6. Add celery, parsley, onion (all cut into slices), 3-4 garlic cloves, whole pepper, allspice, 5 cloves, 1 bay leaf, a piece of ginger, and lemon peel cut into thin strips
7. Salt and simmer for half an hour
8. Arrange the carp in the pot
9. Add enough additional vinegar to completely cover the fish
10. Cover tightly with a plate and lid so steam cannot escape
11. Cook gently for half an hour, being careful the fish does not burn
12. Carefully remove the fish and arrange on a platter
13. Strain the sauce through a sieve over the fish
14. Sprinkle with lemon peel cut into thin strips
15. Cover with celery leaves
16. Cover well and set in a cold place
17. When serving, provide a bowl with vinegar, oil and pepper
