## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 10 dek sugar
- lemon or vanilla flavoring
- 10 dek almonds
- snow from 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks with sugar
2. Add lemon or vanilla flavoring
3. Add almonds
4. Fold in snow from beaten egg whites
