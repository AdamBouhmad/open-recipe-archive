## Ingredients

- Tastiest and ripest pears
- Wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the tastiest and ripest pears until soft
2. Peel and cut them
3. Press through a sieve
4. Dilute the obtained juice with wine
5. Sweeten generously
6. Proceed as with other ice creams
