## Ingredients

- beautiful goose liver
- aspic
- lemon slices
- truffles
- smoked tongue

## Directions

1. Braise the beautiful goose liver in a covered baking sheet. When cooled, cut into finger-thickness slices. Grease a mold, place on ice or in cold water, pour a little aspic. When set, line the bottom with lemon slices, truffles or smoked tongue, sprinkle with aspic until everything sets. Place the cut goose liver on top, fill the mold with aspic and after setting, turn out onto a plate and decorate.
