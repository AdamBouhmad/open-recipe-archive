## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams semolina (12 lots)
- three-quarters liter hot milk
- 12 dekagrams butter (8 lots)
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix semolina into three-quarters liter hot milk and cook
2. When cooled, add butter and 6 eggs
3. Place on a greased baking pan, cover and when fried on one side, turn it
4. Break into pieces and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
