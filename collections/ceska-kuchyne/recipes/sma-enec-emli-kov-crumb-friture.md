## Ingredients

- slices from white bread or rolls
- three whole eggs
- two cups of milk
- salt
- one and a half tablespoons of butter
- powdered sugar
- powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slice a piece of white bread or rolls into thin slices.
2. Beat three whole eggs in two cups of milk, add a little salt and pour this over the sliced bread and let it stand for a while until it softens.
3. Heat one and a half tablespoons of butter on a pan, put the soaked bread into it and fry until it has a nice golden crispy bottom, mash it with a spoon and fry again.
4. When ready to serve, sprinkle generously with powdered sugar and powdered cinnamon.
