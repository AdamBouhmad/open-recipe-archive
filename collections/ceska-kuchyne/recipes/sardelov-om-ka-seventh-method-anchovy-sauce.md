## Ingredients

- cleaned, washed, and deboned anchovies
- peeled onion
- brown roux
- strong meat broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Finely chop the anchovies with the peeled onion
2. Cook with brown roux for several minutes, stirring constantly
3. Cook until almost all liquid has evaporated
4. Add strong meat broth
5. Add lemon juice to taste if desired
