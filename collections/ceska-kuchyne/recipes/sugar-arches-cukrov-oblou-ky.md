## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilo ground sugar
- 1/2 kilo fine flour
- grated lemon zest
- grated orange zest

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together to form a stiff dough
2. Spread thickly on oiled paper
3. Bake slowly until golden
4. Cut into strips while hot and curl around rounded wooden forms
