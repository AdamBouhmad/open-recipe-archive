## Ingredients

- 1 bottle white wine
- 3/8 liter sugar water
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- fine potato flour
- 4 egg yolks
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Boil white wine with 3/8 liter of sugar water to which a little cinnamon, lemon peel, and fine potato flour have been added
2. Simmer everything together in the soup
3. Whisk 4 egg yolks and add to the soup
4. Once it has simmered again, pour over fried roll in a bowl
