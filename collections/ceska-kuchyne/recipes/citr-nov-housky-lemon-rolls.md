## Ingredients

- three egg yolks
- one hundred ninety-five grams sugar
- lemon zest from two lemons
- two egg whites
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Rub three egg yolks with one hundred ninety-five grams sugar; add lemon zest rubbed with sugar from two lemons and snow made from two egg whites.
2. Place small pieces on a greased or waxed baking sheet, flatten, sprinkle with chopped almonds, and bake.
