## Ingredients

- large sheatfish
- thin sticks
- onion, split
- bay leaves
- clove
- pepper
- other spices
- parsley roots
- butter
- salt
- green parsley
- crayfish tails
- buttered potatoes

## Directions

1. Tear the sheatfish, clean it, rinse, tie with threads, salt it, and place on a baking sheet with thin sticks arranged like a grill
2. Arrange split onion underneath, bay leaves, clove, pepper, and other spices, along with cleaned and chopped parsley roots and plenty of butter
3. Quickly stew the sheatfish; when soft, remove it, untie it, and arrange on a platter
4. Place green parsley in the mouth, surround with parsley and crayfish tails, buttered potatoes, etc.
5. Pour butter over everything and serve
