## Ingredients

- Millet
- Sweet milk, 1 quart
- Eggs, 4 whole
- Salt
- Smoked pork meat, thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Rinse millet in clean lukewarm water
2. Cook in sweet milk until soft and thickened into a porridge
3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly
4. Beat 4 whole eggs and mix into the porridge
5. Add a little salt
6. Slice smoked pork meat into thin slices and place on a baking pan
7. Pour the porridge mixture over the meat slices
8. Bake until the top is golden yellow
9. This prepared millet pudding is very tasty; if you do not have smoked meat, you can omit it
