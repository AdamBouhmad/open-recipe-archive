## Ingredients

- Two cups cooked ham or marbled smoked meat
- Four cups cooked potatoes
- Two tablespoons flour
- Three eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour or breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop two cups cooked ham or marbled smoked meat
2. Add four cups cooked potatoes pressed through a sieve or strainer
3. Add two tablespoons flour, three eggs, salt, pepper and flour to taste
4. Mix well together
5. Shape into croquettes
6. Coat in breadcrumbs
7. Fry in butter or lard until golden
8. Serve with vegetables, especially salad with sour cream
