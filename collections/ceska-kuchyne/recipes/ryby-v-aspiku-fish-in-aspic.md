## Ingredients

- clear gelée made from fish broth
- truffles
- crayfish tails
- hard eggs
- capers
- parsley leaves
- trout or other fish
- dark aspic

## Directions

1. Pour clear gelée into a round mold.
2. Line it with carved truffles, crayfish tails, sliced hard eggs, capers and parsley leaves.
3. Cover again with gelée and set on ice to harden.
4. Cook trout or other fish "au bleu" and remove from bones.
5. Place the fish in the center on the hardened gelée in the mold.
6. Pour more gelée over it, arrange a new layer of fish, cover again with gelée and set on ice to harden.
7. Turn out the hardened fish with aspic onto a dish and decorate with dark red aspic.
