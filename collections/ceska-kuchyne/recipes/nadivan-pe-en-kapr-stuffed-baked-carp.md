## Ingredients

- carp
- carp roe
- water
- lemon juice
- butter
- bread or roll soaked in milk
- eggs
- boiled roe
- egg yolks
- ground flower
- ground pepper
- salt
- anchovy fillets
- mushrooms or truffles

## Directions

1. Clean the carp well, remove gall and intestines from the roe.
2. Place roe in a pot, pour well water and lemon juice on it, let it simmer for about five minutes, then remove.
3. Beat four tablespoons of butter, add one roll soaked and squeezed out in milk or two slices of bread, two beaten eggs, chopped boiled roe, six egg yolks, a bit of ground flower and some ground pepper, salt everything and mix well.
4. Fill the cleaned and salted carp with this mixture and sew it shut.
5. Grease a baking pan well with butter, place onion sliced into rounds, lay the salted carp on it, place pieces of fresh butter on it.
6. Bake and toward the end sprinkle with cleaned and finely chopped anchovies and bake until golden.
7. Frequently baste the carp with its baked juices during baking.
8. If desired, you can use anchovy butter instead of ordinary butter in the stuffing, and cook finely chopped mushrooms or truffles in butter instead.
