## Ingredients

- half kilogram peeled ground almonds
- 4 eggs
- half kilogram sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- egg white snow

## Directions

1. Mix half kilogram of peeled ground almonds with 4 eggs
2. Mix half kilogram sugar, 16 egg yolks, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
3. Beat this mixture for half an hour
4. Slowly add the egg white snow
5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking form
6. Bake at moderate heat for a quarter hour
