## Ingredients

- seven deka peeled or sugared almonds
- 3.5 deciliters boiling cream
- seven deka sugar
- seven egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take seven deka of peeled or sugared almonds and add to them 3.5 deciliters of boiling cream
2. Strain everything, sweeten with seven deka sugar and mix with seven egg yolks
