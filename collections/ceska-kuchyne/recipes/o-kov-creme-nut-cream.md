## Ingredients

- 3.5 deciliters boiling cream
- peeled, crushed or sugared nuts
- five egg yolks
- seven deka sugar

## Directions

1. Add peeled, crushed or sugared nuts to 3.5 deciliters of boiling cream
2. Strain everything and mix with five egg yolks and seven deka sugar
