## Ingredients

- 5 to 6 eggs
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 3.5 deciliters good thick sour cream
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Hard boil 5 or 6 eggs.
2. When cooled, peel and slice into halves, quarters, or cubes.
3. Whisk 4 raw egg yolks into 3.5 deciliters of good thick sour cream.
4. Grease a baking sheet with butter.
5. Place the sliced hard eggs on it and salt.
6. Pour the whisked yolks in cream over the eggs.
7. Place in the oven and let thicken slowly.
8. Turn out onto a plate and serve with pepper sprinkled over.
9. If the cream is not thick enough, flour may be added.
