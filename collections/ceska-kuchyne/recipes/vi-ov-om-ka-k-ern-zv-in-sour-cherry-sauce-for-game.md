## Ingredients

- sour cherries
- cherry pits
- crushed cherry pits
- bread crust
- cinnamon
- clove
- all kinds of spices
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Sort the sour cherries and crush the pits in a mortar
2. Place the crushed pits with the pitted cherries on a baking pan
3. Add bread crust, cinnamon, clove, and all kinds of spices
4. Boil until completely broken down
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Add wine and a sufficient amount of sugar
7. Boil until the sauce thickens properly
8. Serve in a special dish with roasted game
