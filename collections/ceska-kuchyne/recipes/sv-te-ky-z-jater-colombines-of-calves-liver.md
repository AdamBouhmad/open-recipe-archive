## Ingredients

- half a calf's liver
- milk
- 2 ounces of beef fat
- 4 whole eggs
- 1 grated roll soaked in milk

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from half a calf's liver and let it soak in milk for about two hours, then rinse it and scrape it on a board, removing the blood vessels and sinews; chop the liver with two ounces of beef fat, add four whole eggs to it, mix well, add one grated roll soaked in milk
