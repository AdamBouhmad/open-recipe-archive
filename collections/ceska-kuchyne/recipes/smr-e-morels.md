## Ingredients

- fresh morels
- warm water
- melted butter
- onion, sliced
- fresh parsley, chopped
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter roux

## Directions

1. Only fresh morels are suitable for preparation; dried ones are indigestible and are added to dishes only for flavor.
2. Wash them several times in warm water so that no sand remains in them, slice them into round pieces, put them on a shallow pan on a piece of melted butter, add sliced onion and a piece of chopped fresh parsley, salt them, season with nutmeg and let them stew until soft.
3. Make a little butter roux.
