## Ingredients

- 15 grams (1 lot) pressed yeast
- Sugar
- Flour
- 45 grams (3 lots) butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter warmed flour
- Salt
- 3 egg whites beaten stiff
- Butter for the filling
- Egg yolks for the filling
- Sugar for the filling
- Curd cheese for the filling
- Several tablespoons raisins
- 3 egg whites beaten stiff for the filling
- Sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Mix 15 grams pressed yeast with a little sugar and flour and let rise in a warm place
2. Beat 45 grams butter with 3 egg yolks, mix in 3/4 liter warmed flour, a little salt and yeast, work the dough, finally add stiff egg white foam from 3 egg whites
3. Cover the dough
4. When risen, roll out, cut out cakes, press down in the middle with your hand and brush with a mixture of beaten butter, egg yolks, sugar, curd cheese, several tablespoons raisins and stiff egg white foam from 3 egg whites
5. Sprinkle with sugar and when risen for a while, bake over gentle heat
