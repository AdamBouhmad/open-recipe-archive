## Ingredients

- half peck of green tomatoes
- one large head of cabbage
- a dozen red bell pepper pods
- a dozen green bell pepper pods
- a dozen onions
- salt
- whole mustard
- ground mustard
- three tablespoons celery seed
- one tablespoon ground cloves
- three tablespoons allspice
- one quart grated horseradish
- vinegar
- one pound sugar

## Directions

1. Chop the green tomatoes, cabbage head, red pepper pods, green pepper pods, and onions separately, then mix them well together
2. Add a little salt, place in a cloth bag and let it drain overnight
3. In the morning, squeeze the mixture dry by hand
4. Place in a deep bowl, pour cold vinegar over it, let it stand for six hours and squeeze again
5. Add whole mustard, ground mustard, three tablespoons celery seed, one tablespoon ground cloves, three tablespoons allspice, and one quart grated horseradish, mix well
6. Place in glass jars or stone crocks, pour boiling vinegar sweetened with one pound sugar over it so the mixture is completely covered
7. When cooled, seal the containers well and store
8. The end of September or beginning of October is the most suitable time to make this mixture
