## Ingredients

- mussels
- water
- wine
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Wash the mussels with water.
2. Cook the closed mussels in wine with parsley and onion until they open.
3. Remove one half of the shell, keep the other half full and add to the sauce.
4. Use to garnish many dishes.
5. From large types of mussels, remove the cartilage after cooking.
