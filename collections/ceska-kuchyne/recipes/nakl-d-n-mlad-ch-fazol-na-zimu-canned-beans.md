## Ingredients

- young bean pods
- salt
- water
- hay
- soft water

## Directions

1. Select only very young bean pods.
2. Blanch them once in salted water by bringing to a boil.
3. Quickly dry them on a sieve and store in boxes. Or, prepare them this way for better flavor: Fill bottles with beans and screw them loosely shut.
4. Place the bottles in a basin on the bottom of which you have placed some hay.
5. Fill the basin with soft water and bring it to a boil.
6. Remove the basin from the heat, let the bottles cool completely, then screw them tightly shut.
7. Store them in a dry, cool cellar.
