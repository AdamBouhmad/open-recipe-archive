## Ingredients

- 1 coffee spoonful of flour
- a little cream
- 5 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- a little vanilla extract
- 1 pint of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put 1 coffee spoonful of flour and a little cream into a pot, mix well
2. Add 5 egg yolks, mix everything well again
3. Sweeten to taste, add a little vanilla extract and 1 pint of sweet cream
4. Place over heat and stir slowly until it thickens, but it must not boil
