## Ingredients

- Young tender spinach leaves
- Salt
- Water
- Beef broth
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Small teaspoon flour
- 2 whole eggs
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pick young tender leaves, best when about six are unfolded
2. Clean and wash the leaves thoroughly, then cook in salted boiling water for about ten minutes
3. Drain in a colander
4. Place on a wooden cutting board and chop finely with a knife
5. Return to a bowl and add 1.5 cups beef broth, or water if broth is unavailable
6. Season with salt and stew for about 20 minutes
7. Make a pale roux from 1 tablespoon butter and a small teaspoon of flour
8. Add to spinach
9. Break 2 whole eggs into the mixture
10. Season with pepper and mix well
11. Cook a bit longer
12. If desired, add a pinch of nutmeg
