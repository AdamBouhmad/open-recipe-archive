## Ingredients

- Young peas
- Butter
- Sugar
- Salt
- Rice
- Beef broth
- Smoked meat
- Lemon sauce and boiled chicken (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the young peas, place on a baking pan, salt, add a piece of butter and a piece of sugar, and stew until tender
2. Prepare the rice: wash it, place on another pan with butter and salt, and stew in beef broth until tender
3. Chop the smoked meat finely and mix with the peas into the rice
4. Serve as a dish by itself or add to boiled chicken in lemon sauce
