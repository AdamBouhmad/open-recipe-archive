## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Slice onion and brown in butter with a spoonful of flour
2. Pour in 1/2 liter of broth
3. Add salt, pepper and two to three spoonfuls of vinegar
4. Strain over sliced boiled potatoes
