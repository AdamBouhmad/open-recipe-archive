## Ingredients

- half a liter of small mushrooms
- salt
- water
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- pepper
- partridges
- lard

## Directions

1. Rub the mushrooms with salt, rinse in water and dry.
2. Dissolve the butter and add the mushrooms, salt and pepper.
3. Let cool.
4. Stuff the partridges with the mushroom mixture.
5. Wrap in lard, tie and roast or fry.
