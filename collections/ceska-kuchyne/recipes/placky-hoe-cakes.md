## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 2 eggs
- 6 tablespoons pork or goose lard
- salt
- ginger
- milk

## Directions

1. Place flour on a work surface and make a well in the center
2. Add eggs to the well
3. Add lard and salt
4. Season with ginger
5. Mix with a knife using several tablespoons of milk
6. Work into completely smooth dough with hands
7. Form into thick cakes about the size of a walnut
8. Score with a knife in a cross pattern
9. Arrange on a baking pan
10. Bake slowly in the oven
