## Ingredients

- Pork from shoulder with some fat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sage
- Ginger

## Directions

1. Take pork from shoulder with some marbling and chop finely, preferably with a meat grinder
2. For five pounds of meat add two heaping tablespoons salt, one level small teaspoon pepper, one level tablespoon sage, half small teaspoon ginger
3. Mix thoroughly and store in a cool place where it will keep for days
4. Fry as croquettes or thin with warm water and stuff into sheep casings to make pork sausages
