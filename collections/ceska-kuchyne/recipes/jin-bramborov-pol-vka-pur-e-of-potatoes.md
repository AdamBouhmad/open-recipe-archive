## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- carrot
- celery
- cabbage
- leek
- boiling water
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook peeled potatoes and strain the water from them
2. Mix them with a piece of butter, add salt, pepper, and marjoram, and pour the strained water back on them
3. Meanwhile braise carrot, celery, cabbage, and leek on butter
4. Add a little boiling water and strain the juice from that into the potato soup
5. Let cook for another half hour and pour into a tureen on fried bread slices
