## Ingredients

- 30 grams alcohol
- 30 drops fennel oil
- 1 drop cinnamon oil
- 4 liters good spirits (kořalka)
- 750 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the alcohol with the fennel oil and cinnamon oil
2. Mix with 4 liters of good spirits
3. Sweeten with 750 grams of sugar
4. Strain and pour into bottles
5. Seal bottles well to prevent evaporation
