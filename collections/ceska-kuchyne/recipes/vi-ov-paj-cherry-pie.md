## Ingredients

- pitted cherries
- sugar
- brandy (optional)
- flour
- pie crust dough

## Directions

1. Pit the cherries
2. Sprinkle with sugar
3. Sprinkle with brandy if desired
4. Dust with flour
5. Prepare a pie in the same manner as apple pie
6. Pies made from strawberries, raspberries, blueberries and grapes are prepared the same way
7. The amount of sugar should be adjusted according to the sweetness of the fruit
