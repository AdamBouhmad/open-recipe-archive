## Ingredients

- black grouse
- red wine
- vinegar
- lemon juice
- bread crust
- 3 small onions
- juniper berries
- bay leaves
- thyme
- broth

## Directions

1. Marinate the older black grouse and let it lie for several days.
2. Place on a baking pan and pour red wine, vinegar and lemon juice over it.
3. Add bread crust, three small onions, a tablespoon of juniper berries, several bay leaves and several sprigs of thyme.
4. Cover the pan well and braise the grouse for three hours.
5. When tender, pour over it the juice that was simmered with broth.
