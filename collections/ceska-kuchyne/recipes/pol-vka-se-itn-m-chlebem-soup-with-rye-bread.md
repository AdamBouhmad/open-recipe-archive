## Ingredients

- rye bread
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- half a chopped fried onion
- a little caraway
- cloves
- green parsley
- chives
- saffron
- two whole eggs
- two or three spoons of good sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut rye bread into thin slices and let them dry in a hot oven on a sheet until brown.
2. Put the bread in a pot, pour broth over it, salt, pepper, add half a chopped fried onion, a little caraway and cloves.
3. Let the broth cook for several minutes and when cooked, add chopped green parsley, chives and saffron, pour it into a soup bowl.
4. Beat two whole eggs with two or three spoons of good sweet sour cream and add it to the broth.
