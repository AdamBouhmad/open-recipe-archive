## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) ground sifted sugar
- Zest from 1 lemon
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- 15 decagrams (10 lots) butter
- Eggs (as much as needed to make dough like for sweet rolls)
- Jam or preserves for coating
- Sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Put flour on a baking sheet and stir it until it turns red, then let it cool
2. When cooled, place on a work surface and mix with 12 decagrams ground sifted sugar, zest from 1 lemon and lemon juice
3. Add a little salt, 15 decagrams butter and as many eggs as needed to make a dough like for sweet rolls
4. Make the cake either round or spread across the entire baking sheet, make an edge from dough
5. When baked, brush with jam, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and cut into pieces
