## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Juice of 2 sweet oranges
- 3 bottles red wine
- 1 bottle rum
- 1 kilogram sugar

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks, lemon juice, red wine, and sugar
2. Heat and beat until foaming
3. Remove from heat
4. Slowly add rum
5. Serve on the table
