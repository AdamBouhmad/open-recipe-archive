## Ingredients

- 18 decagrams (12 lots) almonds in shells, crushed
- 18 decagrams sugar
- 2 eggs
- 11 egg yolks
- egg white snow from 11 egg whites
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- cinnamon
- clove
- a handful of flour
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 18 decagrams (12 lots) crushed almonds in shells, 18 decagrams sugar, 2 eggs, 11 egg yolks, snow made from 11 egg whites, 2 tablets grated chocolate, cinnamon, clove, a handful of flour, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
2. Put everything in a buttered mold and bake the soufflé
