## Ingredients

- Leftover meat
- Three eggs
- Pepper
- Mixed spices
- Ginger
- Caraway seeds
- Lemon peel
- Salt
- Lard
- Potatoes
- Milk
- Egg yolks
- Lard

## Directions

1. Chop all leftover meat finely
2. If you have about three cups of meat, add three eggs, pinch of pepper, mixed spices, ginger, a little caraway seed, chopped lemon peel and salt to taste
3. Mix well and shape into small round pieces
4. Fry in lard until reddish
5. Serve with potato mash made as follows: peel potatoes, salt and boil; drain well and add sweet milk, two egg yolks and a tablespoon of lard; mash until thick; arrange on a dish and pour over with browned butter
