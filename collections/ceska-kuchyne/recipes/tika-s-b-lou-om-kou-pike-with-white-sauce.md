## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) butter
- small onion
- ground ginger
- flower
- nutmeg
- soup in which pike was cooked
- pale roux
- white sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse and salt the pike
2. Place butter on a pan, add onion, and heat until it becomes white
3. Add a little ground ginger, a little flower, and a small piece of nutmeg
4. Place the pre-cooked pike in salted water into this mixture and cover with a lid
5. Stew for a quarter hour; if the onion begins to burn, add soup in which the pike was cooked
6. Carefully place on a platter and add 7 deciliters (2 spoons) of soup
7. Make a pale roux and add it to the onion
8. Cook with a piece of white sugar, strain into a sauce bowl, and serve hot
9. The fish is served cold; mustard may also be added in a separate dish
