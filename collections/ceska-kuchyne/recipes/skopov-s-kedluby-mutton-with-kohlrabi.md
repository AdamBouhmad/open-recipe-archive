## Ingredients

- mutton ribs
- small kohlrabi
- butter
- pepper
- sugar
- young tender leaves from kohlrabi root
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Cook mutton ribs; meanwhile peel small kohlrabi and cut into noodles.
2. Place on a shallow pan with a small piece of butter, pepper, add a piece of sugar, also young tender leaves from the kohlrabi root finely chopped and let braise until tender.
3. When both the meat and kohlrabi are tender, make a light roux, dilute it with broth in which the meat was cooking and pour into the kohlrabi.
4. Add a pinch of marjoram and mix and add the already cooked meat and serve.
