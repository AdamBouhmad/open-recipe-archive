## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- sugar
- sweet orange juice

## Directions

1. Bake or stew the chestnuts until tender
2. Peel and mash into small cakes
3. Place on a baking dish
4. Pour over with boiled sugar
5. Warm again in a warm oven without boiling
6. Remove to a dish and pour over with boiled sugar to which sweet orange juice has been added
