## Ingredients

- Two veal brains
- Salt water
- Pale sauce
- Cream
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Three eggs
- Butter
- Truffle sauce, caper sauce or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the brains in salted water.
2. To pale sauce add a little cream to make a paste, let it cool, mix with chopped brains, fried onion, salt, pepper and 3 eggs, then form patties and fry in butter, top with an egg and serve with truffle, caper or anchovy sauce.
