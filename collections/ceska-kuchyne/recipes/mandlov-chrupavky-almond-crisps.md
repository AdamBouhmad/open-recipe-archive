## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams blanched, pounded almonds
- 20 dekagrams sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- flour
- butter
- sliced almonds
- lemon glaze

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams blanched, pounded almonds with 20 dekagrams sugar and 12 egg yolks
2. Beat 6 egg whites to stiff peaks and add 1 tablespoon flour
3. Make indentations from stiff paper, grease with butter, sprinkle with sliced almonds
4. Fill with the mixture and dry
5. When removed, coat with lemon glaze
