## Ingredients

- beef meat
- salt
- pepper
- allspice flowers
- allspice

## Directions

1. Boil beef for 5-6 hours.
2. Remove bones, fat from the meat and sinews.
3. Tear the meat into strands and cut the sinews.
4. When the water in which it was boiled has cooled, skim off the fat and add the same amount of it to the meat so that it becomes like a paste.
5. Salt it, pepper it, add allspice flowers and allspice, and boil for 15 minutes with constant stirring.
6. Pour into a container and let it cool; when it becomes aspic-like, cut it into slices.
