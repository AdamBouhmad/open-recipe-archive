## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram strawberries
- 1/2 liter red wine
- lemon peel
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- crushed rusk or 1 small teaspoon potato flour

## Directions

1. Press the strawberries through a sieve
2. Pour half a liter of red wine over them
3. Add lemon peel, sugar, and crushed rusk or potato flour
4. Boil for a quarter hour
5. Strain
