## Ingredients

- Cleaned small ham
- Red wine
- Onion
- Bay leaf
- Pepper
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Remove bones from the cleaned small ham
2. Cook in water for about two hours
3. Remove to a baking pan
4. Pour red wine over the ham
5. Add sliced onion, bay leaf, pepper, and cloves underneath
6. When ready to serve, trim the fatty layer from the ham
7. Strain the sauce and pour over the ham on a serving dish
