## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook cleaned crayfish in water to which vinegar, butter, salt, and pepper have been added.
2. Serve on a platter so that the claws stick out, placing fresh green parsley between the claws.
3. Since crayfish stain napkins and such stains do not come out easily, use special napkins or paper napkins.
