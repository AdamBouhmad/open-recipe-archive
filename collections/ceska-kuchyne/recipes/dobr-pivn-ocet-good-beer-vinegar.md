## Ingredients

- beer dregs
- clean oak shavings

## Directions

1. Place beer dregs into a barrel or other vessels in which clean oak shavings are found
2. Cover the vessel at the top with a lid having many holes (perforated)
3. In a short time the beer will become sour
4. The vinegar is very palatable and healthy; pour it into bottles and it will keep for a long time
