## Ingredients

- 2 small cups milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- dried yeast cake or ordinary piece of softened compressed yeast or 1/2 small cup liquid yeast
- flour as needed
- 1/2 small cup fresh butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 egg whites
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Heat the milk in the evening, add sugar and yeast and mix in enough flour to make a batter as thin as for pancakes
2. Dust with flour and let stand in a warm place overnight
3. In the morning add fresh butter, egg yolk and beaten egg white snow, salt and add enough flour to make a stiff dough that does not stick to hands or rolling pin
4. Let rise
5. When risen, roll out to a thickness of three quarters of an inch and cut rounds with a round cutter
6. Brush with egg wash, fold one half over, place on a baking sheet greased with lard or butter and let rise
7. When risen, bake in a moderately hot oven for about 25 minutes
