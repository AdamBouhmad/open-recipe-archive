## Ingredients

- three dozen pods of red pepper
- two heads of cabbage
- one horseradish root
- one cup of mustard seed
- a little cloves
- a little sugar
- two quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in two quarts of vinegar for a quarter hour
2. Allow the cooked mixture to stand for three hours
3. Strain and pour into bottles
