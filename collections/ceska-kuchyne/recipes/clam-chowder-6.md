## Ingredients

- about 1 quart hard clams in shells
- 2 dozen soft clams
- 1 quart peeled potatoes
- 1/2 pound hardtack
- 2 large onions
- 2 ounces salt pork
- 6 peeled tomatoes
- 1 quart water
- 3 pints beef or chicken broth
- ground pepper
- thyme
- sage
- flowers
- 4 ounces butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Open and remove hard clams from shells, reserving the juice and chopping the clams finely
2. Cut 2 dozen soft clams, from which you have previously removed the hard parts
3. Cut 1 quart of peeled potatoes into small cubes
4. Soak 1/2 pound of crumbled hardtack in cold water
5. Chop 2 large onions into fine cubes and fry them with 2 ounces of salt pork in a pot until golden
6. Add potatoes, 6 peeled and sliced tomatoes, 1 quart water, and 3 pints beef or chicken broth
7. Season with ground pepper, thyme, sage, and flowers
8. Let cook for 15 minutes
9. Add the chopped clams with their juice and the soaked hardtack
10. Let simmer for another half hour, carefully skimming the foam
11. Finally add 4 ounces butter and chopped fresh parsley, stir carefully and serve
12. This quantity is sufficient for about 8 people
