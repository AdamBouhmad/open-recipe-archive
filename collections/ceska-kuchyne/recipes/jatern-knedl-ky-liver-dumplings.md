## Ingredients

- 4 spoonfuls of butter
- 6 whole eggs
- 3 cloves garlic
- butter
- lemon zest
- nutmeg
- white pepper
- marjoram leaves
- salt
- 2 pounds fresh calf's liver
- bread rolls, bread or crackers, grated

## Directions

1. Cream 4 spoonfuls of butter until fluffy
2. Beat in 6 whole eggs
3. Add 3 cloves of garlic rubbed with butter, finely chopped lemon zest from half a lemon, a little nutmeg, a little ground white pepper and a teaspoonful of picked marjoram leaves
4. Salt it and mix well
5. Grate 2 pounds of fresh calf's liver on a grater and add to the rest
6. Then add enough grated bread roll, bread or crackers to make a thick dough
7. Mix well and let rest for half an hour
8. Then form round dumplings about the size of an apple
9. Put them in boiling salted water and let simmer for half an hour
10. Remove them, cut in half, sprinkle with grated bread roll or crackers and pour hot melted butter over them
