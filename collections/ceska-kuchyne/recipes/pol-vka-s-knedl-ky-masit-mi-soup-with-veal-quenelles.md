## Ingredients

- half a pound of cooked veal, preferably leftover
- three slices of white bread
- milk
- two spoons of butter
- two whole eggs
- one egg yolk
- salt
- grated bread or crackers
- lard
- strong hot beef broth

## Directions

1. Chop half a pound of cooked veal into small pieces, add three slices of white bread soaked in milk, put everything in a mortar and mash into a paste, or rub in a bowl.
2. Then rub two spoons of butter until it froths, beat in two whole eggs and one egg yolk, add the mashed mixture, salt it and mix well; if it is still too thin, add a little grated bread, make small dumplings from it, coat them in grated bread or crackers and fry them nicely to a golden color in lard.
3. Place them in a soup bowl and pour boiling strong beef broth over them.
