## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar
- vanilla, cinnamon, or lemon zest
- 1 or 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Slice apples into small pieces
2. Put them in a pot, add a little water and a piece of sugar
3. Add either vanilla, cinnamon, or lemon zest
4. Let the apples stew until soft
5. Press them through a strainer
6. Fold in 1 or 2 egg whites beaten to stiff snow, so the filling does not run
7. You may also add raisins or chopped almonds as an addition
