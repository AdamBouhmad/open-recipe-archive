## Ingredients

- Small medlars
- Soft water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the medlars gently and remove the stems
2. Pierce the pears several times
3. Cook in soft water until they can be easily crushed with fingers
4. Transfer to cold water
5. When cooled, place on a plate and cover with thinly dissolved sugar
6. Cover with paper and let sit quietly
7. Each day pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, add the medlars and bring to a boil, continuing until the fifth day when everything is candied
8. On the fifth day, remove the medlars to containers, place between papers and store in a cool place
