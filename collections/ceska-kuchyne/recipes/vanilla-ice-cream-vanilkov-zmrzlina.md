## Ingredients

- One-eighth kilo of sugar
- Finely crushed vanilla
- One liter of sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Finely crush one-eighth kilo of sugar
2. Add finely crushed vanilla
3. Pour in one liter of sweet sour cream
4. Beat the sour cream until foamy
5. Collect the foam and strain through a sieve
6. Continue beating the remaining sour cream until all becomes foamy and transforms into snow
7. Place the snow in a container with ice
8. It will be frozen within an hour
9. Unmold the ice cream by first placing it in warm water and then immediately turning the mold onto a plate
