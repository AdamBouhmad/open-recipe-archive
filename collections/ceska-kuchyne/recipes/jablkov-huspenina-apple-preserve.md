## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar
- juice

## Directions

1. Quarter the apples, peel them, remove the cores
2. Cook strongly with a little water until completely broken down
3. Press through a fine sieve
4. Add juice and sugar
5. Cook again with constant stirring until completely thickened
6. Pour into containers, store in a cool room and seal well
