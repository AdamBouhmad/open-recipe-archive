## Ingredients

- raspberries, not overripe
- ½ kilogram sugar
- ¼ liter water

## Directions

1. Pick raspberries during dry weather immediately after picking
2. Boil sugar in water, add raspberries
3. Cook slowly over gentle heat
4. Cover with a sieve on a plate and let stand until the next day
5. Carefully place raspberries in jars
6. Strain the boiled sugar through a sieve
7. Boil until completely thickened and pour the cooled syrup over the raspberries
8. Place the sealed jars in a cool place
