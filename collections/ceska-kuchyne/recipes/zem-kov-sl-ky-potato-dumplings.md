## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- 1 liter hot milk
- 33 decagrams (22 lots) grated bread roll
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) butter
- 2 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate cooked potatoes
2. Mix with hot milk to make thin porridge
3. Add grated bread roll, butter, eggs, salt, and nutmeg, adding more milk to make firm dough
4. Drop small dumplings by spoon into salted water
5. Pour melted butter over them
