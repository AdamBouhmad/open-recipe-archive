## Ingredients

- pike, cleaned
- salt
- water
- onion
- bay leaf
- spices
- horseradish, grated
- butter, browned
- potatoes, boiled or fried

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned pike into smaller pieces
2. Boil in salted water to which onion, bay leaf, and other spices have been added
3. Remove from heat and arrange on a platter
4. Sprinkle generously with grated horseradish
5. Pour browned butter over it
6. Serve with boiled or fried potatoes around it
