## Ingredients

- Dough for thick noodles
- Salt
- Water
- Spinach prepared as in recipe 499
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make dough for thick noodles and cut into flíčky (flat noodle pieces)
2. Cook in salted water and drain
3. On a platter, alternate layers of noodles and spinach prepared according to recipe 499
4. Repeat until all ingredients are used, ending with a spinach layer
5. Sprinkle generously with breadcrumbs and pour melted hot butter over top
