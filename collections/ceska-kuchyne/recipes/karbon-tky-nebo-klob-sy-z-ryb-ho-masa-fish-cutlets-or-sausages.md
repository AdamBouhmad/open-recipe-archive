## Ingredients

- fish meat scraps
- garlic clove
- marjoram
- green parsley
- flower
- salt
- 4 eggs
- 3 soaked bread rolls
- parsley root piece
- bread crumbs
- optional lemon

## Directions

1. Remove all small bones from the fish meat scraps and chop finely.
2. Add a clove of garlic, a bit of marjoram, green parsley, flower, salt, 4 eggs and 3 soaked bread rolls.
3. Form cutlets from this mixture, inserting a piece of parsley root instead of a bone.
4. Coat in egg and grated bread.
5. Serve with sliced lemon or any vegetable.
6. The mixture can also be made into small sausages.
