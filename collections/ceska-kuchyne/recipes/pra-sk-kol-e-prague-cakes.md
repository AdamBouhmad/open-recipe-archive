## Ingredients

- 375 grams (25 lots) flour
- 4 egg yolks mixed in sour cream and salted
- 20 decagrams (almost 13 lots) butter
- 4 egg whites
- Ground sugar
- Vanilla
- Almond slivers for topping

## Directions

1. Place flour on a work surface, make a depression in the middle, pour in 4 egg yolks mixed with sour cream and salted
2. Knead the dough with 20 decagrams butter
3. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a knife, cut out round cakes, place on a floured baking sheet
4. Make snow from 4 egg whites, mix in ground sugar and vanilla
5. Brush the cakes with this mixture, sprinkle with almond slivers and bake at moderate heat
