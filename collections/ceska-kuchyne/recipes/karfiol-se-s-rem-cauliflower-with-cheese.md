## Ingredients

- Cauliflower florets
- Parmesan cheese
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook whole cauliflower florets until tender
2. Place on a dish
3. Sprinkle with finely grated Parmesan cheese
4. Pour melted, reddish-brown butter over the top
