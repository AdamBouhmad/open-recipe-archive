## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- lettuce hearts
- chopped onion
- a little vinegar
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut cold roast beef into small pieces
2. Add lettuce hearts and chopped onion
3. Season with a little vinegar, mustard, pepper and salt
4. Top with hard-boiled eggs
