## Ingredients

- smoked bacon or ham, half pound, cut into small cubes
- potatoes, three, cut into small cubes
- onion, finely chopped
- dill
- marjoram
- green parsley
- salt
- pepper
- veal, one pound, cut into half-inch cubes
- tomatoes, stewed or pickled, two cups
- clams, 50, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Place the cubed bacon or ham on the bottom of a deep pot
2. Add the cubed potatoes
3. Sprinkle with finely chopped onion
4. Season with dill, marjoram, green parsley, pepper and salt
5. Add the veal cubes
6. Add two cups of stewed or pickled tomatoes
7. Finally add a layer of finely chopped clams
8. Pour boiling water over all, just barely covering
9. Cover well and let simmer slowly for half an hour without stirring
10. Add two cups of milk and six crushed crackers
11. Mix well, boil for 10 minutes more and serve hot
12. The tomatoes may be omitted if desired
