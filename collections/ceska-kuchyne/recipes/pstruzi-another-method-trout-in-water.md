## Ingredients

- trout
- water
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean the trout, eviscerate, and place in boiling water
2. After 10 minutes, place on a platter
3. Add oil, vinegar, pepper, and a small whole onion
