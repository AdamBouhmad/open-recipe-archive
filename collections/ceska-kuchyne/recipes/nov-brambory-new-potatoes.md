## Ingredients

- young potatoes
- water
- salt
- caraway seeds
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the young potatoes, wash them well in cold water, place them in a pot and pour fresh water over them
2. Salt them, add caraway seeds and a piece of green parsley
3. When they are cooked, drain the water from them, remove the parsley and turn the potatoes out onto a plate
4. Let them stand uncovered for about five minutes in steam on the stove
