## Ingredients

- 1 spoonful of white flour
- a little white wine
- 4 egg yolks
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- rind from half a lemon, finely cut
- 2 cupfuls of white wine
- ½ cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Put a spoonful of white flour into a pot, add a little white wine and mix well
2. Add 4 egg yolks and mix again
3. Add a piece of whole cinnamon and finely cut rind from half a lemon
4. Pour 2 cupfuls of white wine over it, add ½ cupful of sugar
5. Place over heat and stir slowly until it thickens
6. When the sauce begins to boil, remove from heat
7. Strain through a sieve and serve immediately at the table
