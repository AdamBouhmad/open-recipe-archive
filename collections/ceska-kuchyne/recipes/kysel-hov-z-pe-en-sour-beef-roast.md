## Ingredients

- beef (choice cut)
- salt
- butter
- hot water
- onion
- vinegar
- mushrooms
- lemon (sliced)

## Directions

1. Pound the meat, salt it
2. Brown on both sides on butter
3. Add a little hot water and onion
4. When the roast is done, add vinegar, mushrooms, and a little chopped onion
5. Add lemon sliced into strips
6. Simmer and add to the roast
