## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 cup water
- cinnamon
- flour

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound sorted sweet almonds in a new pot and let them sit on heat for a while until hardened
2. In another pot cook 1 pound sugar with 1 cup water until it draws in threads
3. Throw the almonds into it and stir until everything is coated in sugar
4. Then crush sugar with cinnamon, mix in a bit of nice flour, and put on a plate
5. Put the almonds into it, mix them so they coat nicely and separate from each other
6. Let them dry on a sieve under a cloth
