## Ingredients

- Most ripe raspberries
- Sugar - finely crushed, equal measure to juice

## Directions

1. Press the ripest raspberries through a sieve
2. Add finely crushed sugar equal to the amount of juice
3. Mix everything together
4. Cook with constant stirring, continuously skimming the rising foam
5. Allow cooked juice to cool
6. Let stand undisturbed until a crust forms on top
7. Carefully remove this crust
8. Pour marmalade into containers
