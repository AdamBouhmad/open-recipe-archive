## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon flour
- chopped dill
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful sweet cold cream
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix vinegar, flour, and chopped dill together
2. Add one egg and mix again
3. Pour one cupful of sweet cold cream into it
4. Cook everything on high heat with constant stirring
5. When cooked, season with salt and add a little sugar to taste
6. If desired, the sugar can be omitted
