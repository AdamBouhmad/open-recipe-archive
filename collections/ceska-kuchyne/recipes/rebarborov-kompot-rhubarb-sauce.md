## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash nice rhubarb stalks clean and chop into small pieces.
2. Put them in a dish, sprinkle generously with sugar, cover with a plate, and bake in an oven.
3. Bake about 25 minutes and add grated nutmeg.
4. If needed, add more sugar to taste.
