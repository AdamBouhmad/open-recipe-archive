## Ingredients

- dried mushrooms
- pale roux
- two tablespoons beef broth
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scald dried mushrooms and drain the water
2. Finely chop the mushrooms
3. Add pale roux and two tablespoons beef broth
4. Cook the sauce
5. Add chopped parsley if desired
