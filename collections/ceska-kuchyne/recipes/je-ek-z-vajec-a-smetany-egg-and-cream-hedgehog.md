## Ingredients

- ½ liter milk or sour cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) ground almonds
- vanilla
- salt
- 5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil half a liter of milk or sour cream with 3 tablespoons sugar, 12 decagrams ground almonds, vanilla and salt for a quarter hour
2. Strain it, mix in 5 beaten egg yolks
3. Place the container in boiling water and stir until it thickens
4. Pour the mixture into a greased mold, cover with buttered paper and let stand for one hour in boiling water
