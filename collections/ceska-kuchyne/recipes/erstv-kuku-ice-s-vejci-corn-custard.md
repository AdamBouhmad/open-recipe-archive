## Ingredients

- six corn cobs
- milk
- two eggs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Shell or scrape kernels from six corn cobs
2. Add one cup of milk and two beaten eggs
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Place in a bowl and bake in the oven for half an hour
