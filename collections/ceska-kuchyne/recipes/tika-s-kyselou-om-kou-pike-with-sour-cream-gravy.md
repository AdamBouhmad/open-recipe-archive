## Ingredients

- pike
- sardines
- butter
- white flour
- meat broth
- sour cream
- nutmeg flower
- white ginger
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the pike in the same manner as blued pike
2. Remove the pike from the broth and arrange on a dish
3. Clean three or four sardines, rub them with a piece of butter
4. Add a tablespoon of white flour and mix well
5. Add about a small cup of meat broth and a small cup of thick sour cream, a little ground nutmeg flower and grated white ginger
6. Mix well and let simmer
7. Add a few capers, pour over the pike and serve
