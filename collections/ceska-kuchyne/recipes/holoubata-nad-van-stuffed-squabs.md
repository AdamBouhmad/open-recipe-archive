## Ingredients

- squab
- 15 gr. butter
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- clove flower
- salt
- rusk
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the squab, draw it carefully so it does not tear, cut off the head and legs, place in cold water
2. For the stuffing: mix 15 gr. butter, one whole egg, one egg yolk, clove flower and salt
3. Grate rusk and add as much as needed
4. Fill the crop with stuffing and tie with white thread
5. Place on a baking dish, add water, roast first on the back then on the breast
