## Ingredients

- half a coffee cup of small pearl barley
- one coffee cup of hot water
- one spoon of butter
- salt
- fresh parsley
- chives
- hot beef broth

## Directions

1. Take half a coffee cup of small pearl barley, clean it well in twice-heated water, pour one coffee cup of hot water over it, add one spoon of butter and a little salt and cook for 1½ hours; be careful that it does not burn, and as needed, as it cooks down, add more hot water.
2. When cooked, put it in a soup bowl, add a little fresh parsley and chives and pour hot beef broth over it.
