## Ingredients

- Ice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Line the container in which ice cream is to be made in a smaller container with ice broken into pieces and salted to produce greater cold
2. Chill the ice cream container for at least ten minutes by rapidly rotating it until completely cooled
3. Clean it, fill with ice cream mixture and rotate and whip it rapidly until it begins to cool and harden
4. Open the lid which must be firmly attached, scrape the sides of the container and in the sealed container the ice cream is again whipped and mixed much more rapidly than before
5. After a while stop again, scrape everything and repeat several times until the ice cream is completely ready
6. Place the ice cream in decorative containers and molds
7. Remove ice cream from molds by placing it in warm water, warming it and turning the ice cream out onto a plate
8. Paint the outside of the ice cream with fruit juice from the fruit which the mold should resemble, so that it also has that taste
