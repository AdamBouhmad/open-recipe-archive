## Ingredients

- sweet sour cream
- butter
- cumin
- salt
- white wine
- cleaned crayfish

## Directions

1. In sweet sour cream, add a little butter, cumin, salt and white wine
2. Add cleaned crayfish
3. Cook for about a quarter hour
4. Remove and serve with strained sauce at table
