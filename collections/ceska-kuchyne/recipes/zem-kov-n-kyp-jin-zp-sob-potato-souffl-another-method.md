## Ingredients

- 15 dekagrams (10 lots) butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/4 kilogram cooked grated potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- sour cream
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) grated cheese
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 15 dekagrams (10 lots) of butter until foamy
2. Add 8 egg yolks, 1/4 kilogram of cooked grated potatoes, salt, nutmeg, sour cream, and 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of grated cheese
3. Fold in stiff egg white snow from 8 egg whites
4. Put into a mold and cook in hot water
5. Usually served as an accompaniment to game
