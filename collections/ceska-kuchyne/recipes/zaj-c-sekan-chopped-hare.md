## Ingredients

- Whole hare
- Bacon
- Bread
- Milk
- Pepper
- Flower spice
- Ginger
- Onion
- Eggs
- Lemon peel
- Capers
- Lard or bacon
- Broth

## Directions

1. Clean the whole hare, remove all skin and trim all meat from the bones
2. Chop the bones and pound in a mortar
3. Strain the bone stock through a sieve into a container
4. Chop the meat finely and pour the bone stock onto it
5. Chop 3/8 kilo (1/4 pound) of bacon
6. Soak 2 bread rolls in milk, squeeze them out
7. Add crushed pepper, a little crushed flower spice, a little ginger, finely chopped onion, 2 raw and 2 hard chopped eggs, a piece of finely chopped lemon peel, and several chopped capers
8. Mix everything well
9. Grease the pan with lard or bacon, arrange the meat like a hare hindquarter, lard the top with bacon, moisten with broth and roast in the oven until golden
10. Serve whole or sliced into strips
