## Ingredients

- half kilo of freshly roasted coffee
- three liters of boiling water
- one kilo of finely crushed sugar
- cognac

## Directions

1. Grind half kilo of freshly roasted coffee, place it in a machine, and pour over it three liters of boiling water
2. Add one kilo of finely crushed sugar to the prepared coffee, seal everything tightly, place in hot water in a warm place, and stir constantly so the sugar dissolves quickly
3. Strain the finished coffee through cloth, let it cool, pour it into an ice cream maker, and let it freeze similarly
4. Add cognac to it and serve in glasses on the table
