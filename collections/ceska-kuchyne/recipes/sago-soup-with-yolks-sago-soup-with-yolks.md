## Ingredients

- sago
- strong beef soup
- 4 egg yolks
- soup broth for tempering

## Directions

1. Rinse sago as in the previous recipe and place it in a pot
2. Pour strong beef soup over it and let it cook until the sago softens
3. Before removing from heat, mix 4 egg yolks tempered in lukewarm soup into the pot for 6 people and immediately remove the soup from heat
4. If the yolks cook, they will curdle
