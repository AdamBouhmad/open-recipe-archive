## Ingredients

- butter
- 5 yolks
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) ground sugar
- a little ground vanilla
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- flour as needed
- egg for brushing
- preserved fruit for filling

## Directions

1. Rub the butter, add yolks, sugar, vanilla, and lemon zest
2. Add flour as needed
3. Form dough into walnut-sized balls, flatten slightly
4. Make a small indentation in the center and fill with preserved fruit
5. Brush with egg and bake on a greased sheet
