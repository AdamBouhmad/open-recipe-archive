## Ingredients

- half kilogram ripe cherries
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar dissolved in water
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pour 9 dekagrams sugar dissolved in water, or mixed with lemon juice, cinnamon and lemon peel, over half a kilogram of ripe cherries
2. Shake several times and boil for half an hour
