## Ingredients

- about three soda crackers or about three rolls
- one coffee cup of milk
- one lemon
- one teaspoon of butter
- one egg
- one yolk
- sugar to taste
- dough for pie plate

## Directions

1. Pour one coffee cup of milk over about three soda crackers or rolls and let them soak
2. Meanwhile, grate one lemon
3. Take one teaspoon of butter, one egg and one yolk, and sweeten to taste
4. Mix everything together
5. Pour this into a dough-lined pie plate and bake
6. When baked, beat the egg white from one egg and spread it on the surface
7. Place in the oven until the surface browns, then remove and serve
