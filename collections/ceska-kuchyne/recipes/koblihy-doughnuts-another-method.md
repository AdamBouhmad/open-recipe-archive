## Ingredients

- half kilogram grated, boiled potatoes
- 75 grams (5 lots) beaten butter
- 45 grams sugar
- flower
- salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 eggs
- flour
- jam
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix grated boiled potatoes with 75 grams beaten butter to which 45 grams sugar, a little flower and salt have been added
2. Mix in 3 egg yolks and 3 eggs
3. Add enough flour to make a dough
4. Roll out and cut circles with a form as for ordinary doughnuts
5. Spread jam on the bottom part, press on the second half, trim with a form and fry in butter
6. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
