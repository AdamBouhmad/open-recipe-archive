## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams rice (16 lots)
- cold water
- 1 liter milk
- 9 dekagrams butter (6 lots)
- sugar, rubbed with lemon
- salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 egg yolks
- egg whites, stiffly beaten
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Soak 24 dekagrams of rice in cold water for a quarter hour, stirring often, then drain
2. When the rice has dried, braise it for a quarter hour in 1 liter of milk, add 9 dekagrams of butter, sugar rubbed with lemon, salt and cook for a quarter hour
3. Mix in 4 egg yolks and turn out onto a plate
4. Add 2 more egg yolks and stiffly beaten snow from all the egg whites, put in a greased mold and bake for an hour with gentle heat
5. Serve the baked rice with wine sauce
