## Ingredients

- beautiful cleaned chicken
- salt
- lemon juice
- butter
- green parsley
- beef broth
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 small glass good olive oil or heated fresh butter
- juice of 1 lemon
- salt
- pepper
- lettuce hearts
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Salt the cleaned chicken, lard it, rub the breast with lemon juice so it whitens nicely and let it braise in a griddle with butter, green parsley and a little beef broth
2. Cut it into neat pieces and let cool
3. Beat six egg yolks, add gradually a small glass of good olive oil or heated fresh butter, stirring constantly until it is smooth
4. Then add the juice of one lemon, salt and pepper it lightly
5. Dip the pieces of chicken in this sauce, arrange them on a platter, pour the remaining sauce over them and surround them with lettuce hearts and hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
