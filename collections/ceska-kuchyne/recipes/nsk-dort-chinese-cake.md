## Ingredients

- 500 grams butter
- 8 whole eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- 125 grams peeled and crushed or chopped almonds
- 500 grams sugar
- 12 hard-boiled eggs, pressed through a sieve
- lemon zest, finely grated
- 4 grams crushed cinnamon
- 250 grams finest flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat 500 grams butter until foamy
2. Slowly beat in 8 whole eggs and 8 egg yolks
3. Add 125 grams peeled and crushed or chopped almonds, 500 grams sugar, and 12 hard-boiled eggs pressed through a sieve
4. Add finely grated lemon zest and 4 grams crushed cinnamon
5. Mix in 250 grams finest flour
6. Season lightly with salt and mix into dough
7. Divide into 4-5 equal-sized cake portions
8. Place on buttered paper on a baking sheet
9. Bake slowly until completely done
10. Turn out onto a sieve to cool
11. When cooled, brush with preserves, stack together, decorate variously, and coat with glaze
