## Ingredients

- domestic yeast, thinned as described in article 757
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. In the morning, make a thin starter (kvásek) using the thinned domestic yeast and let it rise in a warm place
2. It should be risen in about one hour
3. Add as much flour as needed so that the dough is moderately firm
4. Handle the dough as described in the preceding article
