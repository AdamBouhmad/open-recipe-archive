## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound chicken breast
- one-quarter pound cold cooked beef tongue
- vinegar
- celery salt
- paprika
- mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop chicken breast and cold cooked beef tongue into pieces
2. Pound together in a mortar
3. Moisten with several drops of vinegar
4. Add celery salt, paprika and four or five tablespoons of mayonnaise
5. Mix well together
6. Place in cold bowl and keep on ice until ready to use
7. Spread bread slices with this mixture
8. Place a crisp lettuce leaf moistened in tarragon vinegar on one piece
9. Cover with another buttered bread slice
10. Press together gently and serve
11. Ham combined with veal roast also makes a very good salad sandwich
