## Ingredients

- onion
- cabbage
- pepper
- salt
- beef broth
- fish
- butter
- mushrooms
- dough
- bread crust

## Directions

1. Fry onion, add cabbage, season with pepper and salt, stew for a while, can be moistened with beef broth occasionally
2. Cut fish into slices, remove bones and skin, fry in butter and add to the cabbage with fried chopped mushrooms
3. Wrap in dough, roll in bread crust and fry in butter
