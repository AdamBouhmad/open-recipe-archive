## Ingredients

- Six bitter oranges
- Sugar
- Juice of several lemons
- Water

## Directions

1. Rub six bitter oranges by their skin against sugar
2. Grate the skin
3. Press juice from the oranges and several lemons
4. Strain the juice
5. Dilute slightly with water
6. Mix and sweeten heavily
7. Proceed as with simple ice cream
