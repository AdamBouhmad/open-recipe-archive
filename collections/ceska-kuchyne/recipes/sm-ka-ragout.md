## Ingredients

- veal roast sliced thin
- mushrooms pickled and chopped
- sour cucumbers cut up
- capers
- lemon sliced thin
- butter
- flour
- sardines
- lemon zest
- onion
- broth
- wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Grease a deep bowl and line it with thin slices of veal roast
2. Sprinkle with pickled mushrooms, chopped sour cucumbers, and capers
3. Layer thin slices of lemon and pieces of butter
4. Place the bowl over a pot of boiling water in the oven
5. Fry 2 tablespoons flour in butter, add several chopped sardines, lemon zest, and onion
6. Boil everything with broth, add some wine and lemon juice
7. Cook the sauce, pour it over the meat
8. Simmer briefly and serve
