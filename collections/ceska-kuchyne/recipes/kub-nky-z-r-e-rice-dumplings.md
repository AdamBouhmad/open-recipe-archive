## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams washed rice
- water or milk
- 4 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated bread roll
- salted water
- good broth

## Directions

1. Boil the washed rice in water or milk.
2. Mix with 4 eggs, salt, and nutmeg.
3. When cooled, form small dumplings rolled in grated bread roll.
4. Boil in salted water.
5. Serve in good broth.
