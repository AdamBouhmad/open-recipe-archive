## Ingredients

- approximately 4 veal sweetbreads
- fresh parsley, chopped
- 6 shallots
- several mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- brown gravy or beef broth
- hot water
- lemon juice
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- parsley root
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ wine glass vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch approximately four veal sweetbreads and boil in salted water
2. Drain and cool in cold water
3. Cut into thin slices
4. Chop finely fresh parsley, six shallots, and several mushrooms
5. Stew this mixture in a pot with butter for a quarter hour
6. Heat a piece of butter on the stove and arrange the cut sweetbreads beside each other
7. Season with salt and fry slightly on both sides
8. Add the chopped mixture
9. Pour approximately 2 small cups of brown gravy, beef broth, or hot water over it
10. Let simmer for a quarter hour
11. Remove all fat from the gravy, squeeze lemon juice into it
12. Season with a little more salt if needed and serve hot
13. For the brown gravy: Cut one onion, one carrot, and parsley root into pieces; heat three tablespoons of butter in a pan over high heat until brown; add the vegetables; add half a wine glass of vinegar and a little crushed pepper; season with salt and let cook; strain the gravy through a sieve
