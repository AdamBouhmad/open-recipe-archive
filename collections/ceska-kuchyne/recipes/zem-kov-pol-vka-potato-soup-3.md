## Ingredients

- cold potatoes
- butter
- chopped parsley
- onion
- tablespoon of flour
- broth or water
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Peel cold potatoes, grate them and cook with butter, chopped parsley, onion, a tablespoon of flour, broth or water, salt and pepper; beat eggs in it and pour over fried bread roll
