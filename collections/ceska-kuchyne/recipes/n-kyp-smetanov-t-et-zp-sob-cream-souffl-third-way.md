## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) blanched crushed almonds
- 8 eggs
- 3 spoons flour
- 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) thick sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of blanched crushed almonds with 8 eggs, adding one at a time, 3 spoons of flour and 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) of thick sour cream
2. Bake in a greased mold
