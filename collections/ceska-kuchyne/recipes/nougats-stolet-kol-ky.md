## Ingredients

- one pound fresh butter
- one pound fine sugar
- grated zest from one lemon
- fifteen egg yolks
- one-quarter pound peeled grated almonds
- a little ground flower
- salt
- one pound white dry flour
- preserved sour cherries
- egg yolks for brushing
- finely chopped almonds
- ground sugar

## Directions

1. Beat one pound fresh butter until foamy
2. Mix in one pound fine sugar, grated lemon zest, and fifteen egg yolks one by one
3. Add one-quarter pound peeled grated almonds, a little ground flower, and a pinch of salt
4. Mix well and add one pound white dry flour
5. Stir for half an hour
6. Drop by spoonfuls in mounds the size of a walnut on a clean buttered baking sheet, spacing them well apart
7. Press one preserved sour cherry into the top of each mound
8. Brush the mounds with beaten egg yolks
9. Sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and ground sugar
10. Bake in the oven over moderate heat
11. Carefully slide onto a work surface or table when done
