## Ingredients

- 3 liters of ripe, sour cherries
- 1.5 liters of wine vinegar
- sugar equal in weight to the vinegar

## Directions

1. Crush 3 liters of ripe, sour cherries in a mortar and pour 1.5 liters of wine vinegar over them, let it stand in a cool place for 8 days, stirring daily, then push through cloth.
2. Add sugar equal in weight to the vinegar, boil the vinegar, removing the foam as it forms, and when it cools, pour into bottles and seal.
3. When mixed with water, it can be used for preparing lemonade.
