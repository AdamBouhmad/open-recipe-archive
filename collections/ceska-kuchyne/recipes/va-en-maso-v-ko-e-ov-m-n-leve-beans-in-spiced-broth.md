## Ingredients

- beans
- water
- baking soda
- salt
- tomatoes
- onion
- butter
- salt pork
- tomato sauce
- syrup
- sugar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place the beans with water on the stove, add half a teaspoon of baking soda and a little salt and let them cook for about half an hour.
2. In a pan, put half a three-pound can of tomatoes, or 3-4 fresh ones, add one chopped onion, a tablespoon of butter, and let them stew for about half an hour.
3. Then wash well about a pound of pickled salt pork, add it to the beans and strain the tomato sauce into them.
4. Add a tablespoon of syrup and a tablespoon of sugar, salt to taste, cover well and bake slowly for two to three hours.
