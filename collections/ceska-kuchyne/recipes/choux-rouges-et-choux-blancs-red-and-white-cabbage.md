## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- white cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut red and white cabbage, arrange on a dish alternately either in stripes, circles or star shapes, salt and pepper it and dress with vinegar and oil. Various other arrangements can create very nice effects
