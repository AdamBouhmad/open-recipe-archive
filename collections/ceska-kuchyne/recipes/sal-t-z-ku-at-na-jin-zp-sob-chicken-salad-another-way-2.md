## Ingredients

- meat from roasted or boiled cold chicken
- equal amount of white celery stalks cut into thin strips
- three tablespoons mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
- capers
- olives
- alternatively: sour pickles cut into thin strips
- alternatively: tender lettuce leaves torn into pieces
- alternatively: vinegar mixed with oil, salt and pepper instead of mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from roasted or boiled cold chicken into small pieces
2. Mix with equal amount of white celery stalks cut into thin strips and three tablespoons mayonnaise
3. Arrange on a platter lined with lettuce leaves
4. Pour mayonnaise over top
5. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters, or with capers and olives
6. Alternatively, use sour pickles cut into thin strips or tender lettuce leaves instead of celery
7. Alternatively, use vinegar mixed with oil with a bit of salt and pepper instead of mayonnaise
8. The same method can be used for salad made from sweetbread, goose liver and other meats, and many leftovers can be used this way quite appropriately and deliciously
