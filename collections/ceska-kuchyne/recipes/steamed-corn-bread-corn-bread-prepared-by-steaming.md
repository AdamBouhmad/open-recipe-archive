## Ingredients

- 3 small cups cornmeal
- 1 small cup boiling water
- 1 small cup wheat flour
- 1 small cup milk
- 1 small cup dark molasses
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- salt as needed

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the cornmeal and mix well
2. When cooled, add wheat flour, milk, molasses, baking powder and salt, mixing everything well
3. Place in a deep bowl and steam in a well-closed pot for three hours
4. Serve while hot, sliced thin and with butter
