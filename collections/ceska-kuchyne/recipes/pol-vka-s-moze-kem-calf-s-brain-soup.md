## Ingredients

- one pound of veal meat
- veal necks
- poultry pieces
- two onions
- several sprigs of parsley, celery, and carrot
- two cabbage leaves
- fresh butter
- beef broth
- white roux
- veal or lamb brain
- flour
- egg
- grated roll
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Cut one pound of veal meat, necks, and various poultry pieces into chunks
2. Cut two onions and several sprigs of parsley, celery, and carrot
3. Stew everything with a piece of fresh butter, being careful that it does not brown
4. Pour beef broth over it and let it cook for one hour
5. Strain about two quarts of the broth and brown it with white roux
6. Clean the brain, boil it in cold water, dry it, dredge it in flour, dip it in beaten egg, coat it in grated roll, and fry it in hot butter
7. Pour the broth over the fried brain
8. Alternatively, if there is no time for frying, cook the cleaned brain in the strained broth and serve on fried grated roll cut into cubes
