## Ingredients

- large tablespoon potato flour
- half liter sour cream
- 12 dekagrams almonds (8 lots)
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams sugar (4 lots)
- 24 dekagrams cooked grated potatoes (16 lots)
- 12 egg whites
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat potato flour with half liter sour cream
2. Pound almonds, add 12 egg yolks, sugar, grated cooked potatoes, snow from 12 egg whites and salt
3. Bake the roll and serve with cherry or raspberry sauce
