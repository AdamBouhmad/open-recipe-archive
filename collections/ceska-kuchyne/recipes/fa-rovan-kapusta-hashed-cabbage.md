## Ingredients

- young leaves from ordinary or curly cabbage
- salt water
- fresh butter
- onion, finely chopped
- flour
- beef broth or roasting liquid
- ground sugar
- pepper
- ginger, grated

## Directions

1. Pluck young leaves from ordinary or curly cabbage, cook them in salted water until soft enough to mash with fingers
2. Soak in cold water, press out, and chop very finely
3. Place a piece of fresh butter on a pan, add finely chopped onion and a spoonful of flour, let it braise until brown
4. Then add a little beef broth or roasting liquid, add a little ground sugar, pepper and grated ginger, salt and let it cook a bit longer
