## Ingredients

- 8 hard eggs
- Equal weight of cooked, peeled, cooled potatoes
- Butter for greasing
- Chives, chopped
- Pepper
- 4 egg yolks
- 3.5 deciliters good sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel 8 hard eggs and slice them.
2. Slice the same weight of cooked, peeled, cooled potatoes.
3. Grease a baking sheet and layer potatoes.
4. Sprinkle with chopped chives and pepper.
5. Add a layer of eggs.
6. Continue alternating layers until the sheet is full, with the top layer being potatoes.
7. Whisk 4 egg yolks into 3.5 deciliters good sour cream and pour over.
8. Bake in the oven.
