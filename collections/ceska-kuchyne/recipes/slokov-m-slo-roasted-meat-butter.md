## Ingredients

- meat from a roasted, fatty, juicy sole, cooled
- approximately 250 grams of butter
- lemon juice
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. From a roasted, fatty, juicy sole that has cooled, cut off the meat
2. Pound it together with approximately 250 grams of butter in a mortar
3. Push through a fine sieve
4. Salt it, sprinkle with lemon juice and pepper
5. Let it cool
