## Ingredients

- nice ripe pears
- lemon juice
- sugar
- apple puree
- sugared syrup

## Directions

1. Peel the nice ripe pears and cut off the stems
2. Cut large pears into pieces, leaving small ones whole
3. Boil the pears in soft water to which a little lemon juice has been added
4. Place in boiled sugar and boil again together
5. Place nicely on dishes
6. Surround with apple puree and pour sugared syrup over
