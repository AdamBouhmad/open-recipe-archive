## Ingredients

- smoked meat
- butter
- flour
- broth
- peas
- onion
- parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut smoked meat into cubes
2. Fry briefly in butter
3. Remove the meat
4. To the remaining fat add flour and broth
5. When this has simmered briefly, add the peas with the smoked meat
6. Add onion, parsley, and a little pepper
7. Let simmer gently for a while
