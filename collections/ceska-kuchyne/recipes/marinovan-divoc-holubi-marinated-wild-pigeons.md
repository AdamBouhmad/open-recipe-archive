## Ingredients

- young wild pigeons
- vinegar
- spices
- onion
- butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Young wild pigeons should be larded at least one day before roasting; older pigeons must be tenderized and marinated for at least three days beforehand.
2. Salt the pigeons and marinate them in vinegar with appropriate spices and onion.
3. Roast them in butter.
4. Serve in tomato sauce.
