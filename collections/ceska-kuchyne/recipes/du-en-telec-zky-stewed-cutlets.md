## Ingredients

- veal leg cutlets
- salt
- bacon
- fresh butter
- carrot
- onion
- beef broth
- beef broth
- small onions
- water
- fresh butter
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut neat pieces from the veal leg
2. Salt them and lard with bacon
3. Place a piece of fresh butter on a pan, a piece of carrot and one onion both cut into slices
4. Pour beef broth over this
5. Add the prepared veal cutlets and let them braise on both sides on high heat until browned
6. Frequently baste the cutlets with the sauce they are braising in; however, if the sauce dries too much, you must add beef broth
7. On another pan, place a piece of fresh butter and about three handfuls of small onions
8. Pour a few spoonfuls of water over them and let them braise until tender
9. On another pan, heat a spoonful of butter, add a spoonful of flour, mix it with a small cup of sour cream and let it simmer
10. Pour this sauce onto a plate
11. Arrange the braised cutlets in it and surround with the braised onions
