## Ingredients

- Pork chops
- Salt
- Caraway seeds
- Hot water
- Flour

## Directions

1. Clean the chops and arrange them in a pan one beside the other
2. Salt and season with caraway seeds and pour over three or four tablespoons of hot water
3. Cover and let braise
4. When the water evaporates, the lard usually renders from them, but if not, add a piece of lard
5. When they turn red on one side, turn them so they also turn red on the other side
6. When done, remove them to a platter, pour hot water into the rendered fat and add about half a tablespoon of flour to make a gravy, salt if needed and pour over the chops
7. They can also be baked on a baking sheet in an oven
