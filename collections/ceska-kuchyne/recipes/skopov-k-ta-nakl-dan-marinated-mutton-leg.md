## Ingredients

- mutton leg
- salt
- pepper (coarsely crushed)
- juniper berries
- new spices
- thyme
- bay leaves
- onion
- good vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove membranes and fat from the mutton leg, salt it.
2. Mix roughly crushed pepper, juniper and new spices, sprinkle on the leg.
3. Add a piece of thyme, about 4 bay leaves and sliced onion.
4. Boil one liter of good vinegar with 1 liter and 4 deciliters of water.
5. Pour this over the leg, place in a wide container, weigh down with a stone to keep it well compressed; keep in winter up to 14 days.
6. When ready to prepare, wash it and place on a baking sheet.
7. Pour part of the pickling liquid over it, dilute the remainder with water and vinegar until about half submerged.
8. Add several peppercorns, thyme and 2 whole onions, bake well on both sides.
9. Make either white or brown sauce as for game.
10. Cut the roast and arrange with neat pieces of puff pastry.
