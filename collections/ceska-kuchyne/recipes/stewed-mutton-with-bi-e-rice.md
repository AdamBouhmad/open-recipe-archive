## Ingredients

- mutton, preferably from ribs
- vegetables (as for beef)
- rice
- hot water
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into larger pieces, preferably from the ribs, and cook it with vegetables as you would beef until tender.
2. For every two pounds of meat, take one bowl of rice, rinse it several times in hot water, pour the broth in which you cooked the meat over it, and cook the rice until tender.
3. Make a pale butter roux and use it to thicken the remaining broth.
4. Place the cooked meat on a serving dish, pour the thickened broth over it, add the cooked rice, season with cloves, and let it simmer a little longer.
5. Place the rice on the dish and arrange the cooked meat on top.
