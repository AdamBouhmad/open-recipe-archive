## Ingredients

- two small cups hot water
- one small cup butter
- two small cups flour
- six whole eggs
- flour for cream filling
- milk
- one egg (for filling)
- one small cup sweet milk
- one small cup sugar
- one teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Bring two small cups hot water and one small cup butter to a boil together
2. While boiling, stir in two small cups flour
3. Remove from heat and let cool
4. When cooled, add six whole eggs without beating them first
5. Mix for five minutes
6. Using a large spoon, drop portions onto well-buttered pans so they do not touch each other
7. Bake in a hot oven for 25 minutes without opening the door unless absolutely necessary
8. For the cream filling: mix three large tablespoons flour in a little milk until smooth
9. Add one small cup sweet milk, one egg, one small cup sugar, and one teaspoon vanilla
10. Mix well and cook
11. Cook the cream best over water so it does not scorch
12. When the puffs are cold, cut them open at the point and fill with the cream using a spoon
13. Can be served as a dessert at table or instead of pudding
