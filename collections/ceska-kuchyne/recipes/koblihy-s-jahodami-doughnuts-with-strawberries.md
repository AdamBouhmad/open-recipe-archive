## Ingredients

- 15 dekagrams (10 lots) flour
- salt
- 3 dekagrams (2 lots) sugar
- 3 dekagrams fresh butter
- 2 egg yolks
- quarter liter warm milk
- egg
- sugared strawberries
- butter
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Prepare dough from 15 dekagrams flour, salt, 3 dekagrams sugar, 3 dekagrams fresh butter, 2 egg yolks and quarter liter warm milk
2. Roll out, brush with beaten egg, cut into wide strips
3. Lay heaps of sugared strawberries on the strips, cut out doughnuts and fry in butter
4. Sprinkle with sugar; can be eaten hot or cold
