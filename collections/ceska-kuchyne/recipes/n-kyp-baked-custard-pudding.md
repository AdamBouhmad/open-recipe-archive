## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter milk
- 3-4 spoonfuls flour
- 5 dekagrams butter
- 75 grams (5 lots) sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- lemon zest
- salt
- saffron
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix 3-4 spoonfuls of flour and 5 dekagrams of butter into half a liter of milk
2. Mix the resulting dough on a warm place so long until it does not stick to the bowl, then place it on a platter
3. When it cools, add 75 grams (5 lots) of sugar, 8 egg yolks, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, a little salt, saffron and stiff snow from 8 egg whites
4. Pour the mixture into a greased form and bake at even heat for half an hour
