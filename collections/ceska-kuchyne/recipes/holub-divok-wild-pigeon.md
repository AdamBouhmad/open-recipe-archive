## Ingredients

- wild pigeon
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- pepper
- allspice berries
- vinegar
- wine
- water
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the pigeon.
2. Prepare a marinade with chopped onion, thyme, bay leaf, lemon peel, pepper, and allspice berries.
3. Mix equal parts vinegar, wine, and water for the marinade.
4. Marinate the pigeon until the next day.
5. Wrap the pigeon in bacon strips.
6. Roast until it turns red.
7. Either strain the sauce and serve the chopped pigeon on a plate with sauce poured over it, with pastries or lemon cakes around it, or prepare the sauce as described in the following recipe.
