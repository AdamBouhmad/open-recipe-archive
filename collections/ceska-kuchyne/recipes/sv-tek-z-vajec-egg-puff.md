## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 roll soaked in milk
- butter
- flowers
- several tablespoons sour cream
- salt
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook 10 eggs hard, shell and chop them finely
2. Beat 3 tablespoons of butter until it foams, add 5 egg yolks to it and mix well
3. Add 1 roll soaked in milk and beaten with a piece of butter, a little flowers, several tablespoons sour cream, and salt
4. Add the chopped eggs and mix well
5. Gently fold in stiff snow made from 3 egg whites
6. Grease a form with butter, pour the mixture into it and bake in the oven, turn out onto a platter and serve
