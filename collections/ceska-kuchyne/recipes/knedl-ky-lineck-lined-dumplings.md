## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram sifted flour
- 2 to 3 whole eggs
- a piece of butter
- milk
- 2 bread rolls cut into cubes and fried
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 1 kilogram of sifted flour on a platter and salt it.
2. Mix 2 to 3 whole eggs in a separate bowl, add a piece of butter and as much milk as the flour will take.
3. Mix everything with the flour and knead well.
4. Mix in 2 bread rolls cut into cubes and fried, and beat the dough well.
5. There must be boiling water in the pot; dip the skimmer always in hot water and cut a piece of dough with it, from which a dumpling is formed.
6. Cut cooked dumplings into quarters, grease with hot butter and sprinkle with grated bread roll.
7. Serve with tomato sauce, horseradish with cream, or cabbage.
