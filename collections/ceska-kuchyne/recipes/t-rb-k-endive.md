## Ingredients

- 8-10 pieces beautiful yellow endive
- Salt
- Water
- Fresh butter
- 2 cups sour cream
- 0.5 teaspoon ground sugar
- Ground flowers
- Various fried vegetables for garnish

## Directions

1. Choose 8-10 pieces of beautiful yellow endive with unblemished inner leaves not nibbled by snails
2. Remove outer green leaves
3. Wash yellow leaves and cut into pieces, pressing out water
4. Cook in salted water until tender like spinach that can be easily crushed between fingers
5. Drain and let cool
6. Chop finely
7. Melt fresh butter on the stove, add chopped endive and stew until thickened
8. Add 2 cups sour cream, salt, 0.5 teaspoon ground sugar and ground flowers
9. Simmer about 15 minutes
10. Stir in 1 more tablespoon fresh butter
11. Place on a platter and garnish with various fried vegetables
12. This is a delicate and rare dish that also makes an excellent salad
