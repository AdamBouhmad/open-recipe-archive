## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram crushed sugar
- 16 well-beaten egg yolks
- grated lemon zest
- stiff snow from 16 egg whites
- 375 grams fine flour
- optional: vanilla, anise, or similar spices

## Directions

1. Take the crushed sugar and beat in the well-beaten egg yolks
2. Add the grated lemon zest and stiff snow
3. Add the fine flour and mix everything together
4. Using a pastry syringe or funnel, press small loaves onto paper
5. Bake slowly until golden
6. Optional: add vanilla, anise, or similar spices for additional flavor
