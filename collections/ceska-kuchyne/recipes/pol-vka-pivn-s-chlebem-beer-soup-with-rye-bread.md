## Ingredients

- Rye bread
- Water
- Beer
- Salt
- Caraway seeds
- Garlic
- Butter
- 1 cup good sour cream
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut rye bread into a pot, about enough to fill a deep plate
2. Pour half water and half beer (1 quart each)
3. Season with salt and add a little caraway seed
4. Add garlic crushed with a piece of butter
5. Simmer for half an hour
6. Beat 4 egg yolks in 1 cup sour cream
7. Add this mixture to the boiling soup, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
8. Pour into a serving dish
