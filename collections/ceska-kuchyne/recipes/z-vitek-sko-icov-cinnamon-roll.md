## Ingredients

- good dough for strudel
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- sliced almonds
- raisins
- lemon zest
- roasted grated roll

## Directions

1. Prepare good strudel dough and stretch it well until thin
2. Brush with butter
3. Brush with beaten egg yolks
4. Sprinkle with sugar, finely ground cinnamon, sliced almonds and raisins mixed with grated lemon zest
5. Sprinkle with roasted grated roll on butter
6. Roll up and coil, bake slowly in oven
7. During baking, brush often with egg yolks, butter or sour cream, then sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
