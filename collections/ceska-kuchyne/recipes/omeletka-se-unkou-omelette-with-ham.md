## Ingredients

- eggs
- chopped or finely diced ham
- chopped and fried onion
- salt
- pepper
- smetana (sour cream), optional
- chopped fresh parsley or chives
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with a bit of chopped or finely diced ham, a bit of chopped and fried onion, salt, pepper, and if desired sour cream and a bit of chopped fresh parsley or chives
2. Cook the omelette in butter or lard in the usual way
3. Serve it alone, or arrange it around spinach or other vegetables
