## Ingredients

- 500 grams beaten sugar
- lemon peel, grated
- 8 eggs
- 500 grams (1 pound) potato flour
- 500 grams butter

## Directions

1. Beat sugar with grated lemon peel for half an hour
2. Add eggs
3. Gradually add potato flour and butter, one tablespoon at a time
4. Pour into round form
5. Bake for one hour
6. Glaze when done
