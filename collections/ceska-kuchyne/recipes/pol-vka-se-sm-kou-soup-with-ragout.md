## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- vegetables
- marjoram
- salt
- good butter
- fresh mushrooms
- fresh veal meat

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with vegetables, marjoram and salt until tender
2. Strain the soup and make a fine white roux from good butter
3. Fry fresh mushrooms in butter and place them in the soup with chopped chicken and finely chopped veal meat
