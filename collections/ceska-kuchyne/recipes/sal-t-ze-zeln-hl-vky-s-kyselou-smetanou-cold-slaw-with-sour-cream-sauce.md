## Ingredients

- one nice firm cabbage head
- one tablespoon of butter
- three tablespoons of boiling water
- one egg
- half a tablespoon of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- half a small cup of sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut a nice firm head into fine noodles and put on a grater with one tablespoon of butter
2. When it dissolves, add the cabbage to it, add three tablespoons of boiling water, cover and let it sit for a quarter hour at the edge of the stove in warmth, but do not let it boil
3. Then beat one egg and mix it into the cabbage, add half a tablespoon of vinegar, salt and pepper
4. Finally mix in half a small cup of sour cream
5. Let it cool and serve cold on the table
