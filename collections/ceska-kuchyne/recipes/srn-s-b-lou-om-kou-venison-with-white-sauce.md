## Ingredients

- venison
- butter or beef lard
- lemon peel
- vegetables
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the venison and place on a baking sheet with 3 decigrams (2 lots) of butter or beef lard, lemon peel, and all vegetables
2. Braise, then remove and clean
3. Make small dumplings from butter and flour and let them cook well in the sauce
4. Squeeze lemon juice into the sauce, boil, and pour over the meat
