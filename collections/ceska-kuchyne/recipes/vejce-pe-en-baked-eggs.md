## Ingredients

- butter (two spoons fresh)
- egg yolks (five)
- flour (one small spoon white)
- salt
- vinegar (small amount)
- sour cream (two cups thick)
- pepper (ground white, small amount)
- hard-boiled eggs (ten, cut into small cubes)

## Directions

1. Cream two spoons of fresh butter until foamy
2. Add five egg yolks and one small spoon of white flour
3. Salt and mix well
4. Add a small amount of vinegar and two cups of thick sour cream
5. Add a little ground white pepper
6. Mix thoroughly
7. Slowly fold in ten hard-boiled eggs cut into small cubes
8. Grease a baking dish with butter
9. Pour the mixture into it
10. Pour melted butter over the top
11. Bake in the oven until done
