## Ingredients

- killed turtle
- salt
- water
- flour
- wine
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the killed turtle thoroughly
2. Boil in salted water for fifteen minutes
3. Peel the turtle, removing the skin
4. Eviscerate it
5. Cut the meat into pieces
6. Coat in flour and wine
7. Fry in butter
