## Ingredients

- butter
- carrot
- onion
- parsley
- several pieces of fish
- allspice
- ginger
- dill
- clove
- lean broth or parsley water
- salt
- sugar
- fish fillet
- milk
- bread roll
- egg yolks
- majoram
- garlic

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pan and add carrot, onion, parsley, several pieces of fish, allspice, ginger, dill, and clove, all crushed together, and let it braise until brown, being careful not to scorch it
2. Pour lean broth or parsley water over it, salt it and let it simmer for about half an hour
3. Strain the broth, roast a piece of sugar until brown, add it to the broth, and if it is bitter, add a piece of white sugar, some crushed dill and pour it into a bowl over the quenelles
4. For the quenelles: chop fish fillet finely, soak a bread roll in milk and let it cook with a piece of butter on coals into a paste
5. When cooled, add four egg yolks, the chopped fish and a little salt, and if desired, a piece of garlic and some majoram
6. Mix well or pound in a mortar and form small quenelles with two spoons
7. Grease a bowl with butter, place the quenelles on it, cover them with paper and let them cook in steam over boiling water
8. Remove them and pour the broth over them
