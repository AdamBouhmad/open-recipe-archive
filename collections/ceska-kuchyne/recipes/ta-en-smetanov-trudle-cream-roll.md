## Ingredients

- dough (as for apple strudel)
- three tablespoons butter
- four egg yolks
- one cup good sweet cream
- four egg whites
- flour
- raisins, picked through
- almonds, chopped
- sugar
- butter
- two cups cream
- six egg yolks
- a little wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make dough as for apple strudel and cover with a warm baking dish
2. Beat three tablespoons of butter with four egg yolks, add one cup of good sweet cream, beat snow from four egg whites and fold into the cream
3. Dust a cloth with flour, stretch the dough thin, spread with the cream and egg mixture, sprinkle generously with picked-through raisins, chopped almonds and pounded sugar
4. Lift one side of the cloth and roll up the strudel
5. Grease a baking dish with butter, place the strudel in it and let bake slowly
6. Boil two cups of cream, beat six egg yolks with a little wine and pounded sugar, add to the boiling cream and stir until it thickens
7. Bring to the table with the strudel
