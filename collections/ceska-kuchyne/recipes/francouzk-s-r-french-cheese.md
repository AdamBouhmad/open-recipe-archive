## Ingredients

- warm freshly milked milk (+25-27° R.)
- rennet
- salt

## Directions

1. From warmly milked milk (+25-27° R., if it does not have such temperature it must be heated) cheese is made with the help of rennet.
2. After two hours the cheese is removed and immediately placed into molds. Care is taken so that the whey can drain comfortably.
3. The cheese remains in the molds for a whole day, is flipped, salted on top, which is repeated for several days, and when the cheese is completely cold, it is removed from the molds and placed on straw mats on shelves.
4. The cheese is flipped daily. Depending on whether only warm or also collected milk is given, the cheese becomes either fattier (richer) or more crumbly (drier).
