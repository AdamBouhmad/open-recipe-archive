## Ingredients

- 120 grams (8 lots) sugar
- lemon
- lemon juice
- water
- piece of butter
- spoon of flour
- salt
- half liter wine

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar on the lemon, squeeze out the lemon juice onto it
2. Boil in water with a piece of butter, a spoon of flour and salt
3. Add half a liter of wine and boil
