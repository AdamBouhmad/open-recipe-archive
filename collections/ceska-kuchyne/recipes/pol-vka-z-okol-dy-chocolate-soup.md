## Ingredients

- two quarts good milk
- half pound grated chocolate
- quarter pound sugar
- a piece of vanilla
- a few grains of salt
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- candied and dried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of good milk in a pot
2. add half pound grated chocolate, quarter pound sugar, a piece of vanilla and a few grains of salt
3. let it boil for half an hour, occasionally removing any scum that forms
4. beat six egg yolks or three whole eggs with a little milk
5. mix this into the remaining liquid
6. pour over candied bread cubes that have been dried in the oven
