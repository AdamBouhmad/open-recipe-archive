## Ingredients

- Cleaned cabbage heads
- Finely chopped onion
- Parsley
- Celery
- Butter
- Good meat broth
- Flour
- Vinegar
- Cloves
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Finely shred or chop the cleaned cabbage heads
2. Mix the cabbage with finely chopped onion, parsley and celery that have been previously sautéed in butter
3. Braise everything in good meat broth
4. When the cabbage has softened considerably, brown it lightly with flour, add vinegar, cloves and a bit of sugar
5. Mix thoroughly and cook again
