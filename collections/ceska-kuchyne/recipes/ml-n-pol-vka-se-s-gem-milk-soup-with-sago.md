## Ingredients

- sago
- milk
- sugar
- lemon peel
- salt
- egg yolks
- whole eggs
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a quarter pound of nice white sago, pour two quarts of good milk on it
2. Add a piece of sugar over which you have grated lemon peel, salt lightly and let the sago cook until soft
3. Beat in a cup either six egg yolks or three whole eggs with a little milk and a piece of butter, pour into the soup, stir and serve
4. For 6 people
