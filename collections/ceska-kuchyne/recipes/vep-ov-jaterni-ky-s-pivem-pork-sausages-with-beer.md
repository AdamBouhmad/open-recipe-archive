## Ingredients

- 1 pound of fatty pork meat
- salt
- ground pepper to taste
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop one pound of fatty pork meat, salt it and season to taste with ground pepper, then form it into small rolls in the manner of sausages, coat them in breadcrumbs and place on a buttered baking pan.
2. When they begin to turn red on top, baste them with small amounts of beer while turning them; then arrange them on a platter, pour the pan drippings over them and serve.
