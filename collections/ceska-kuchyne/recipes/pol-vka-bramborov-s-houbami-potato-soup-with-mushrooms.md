## Ingredients

- 4 large potatoes
- 2 quarts cold water
- Salt
- 1/2 celery root
- A handful of fresh or dried mushrooms
- 2 spoons butter
- 1 spoon flour
- Garlic
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter 4 large potatoes
2. Add 2 quarts cold water and bring to boil
3. Season with salt and add 1/2 celery root and a handful of mushrooms
4. When potatoes are cooked, remove the celery
5. Make a roux from 2 spoons butter and 1 spoon flour and mix into the soup
6. Crush garlic with salt and add to the soup
7. Add a pinch of marjoram
