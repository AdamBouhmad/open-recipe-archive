## Ingredients

- red beets
- horseradish
- anise or fennel seeds
- salt
- wine vinegar
- cloves

## Directions

1. Clean a nice red beet well, cook until tender, peel and slice into rounds
2. Scrape horseradish and cut it into rounds
3. Put into a jar alternating layers of beet, border it with several rounds of horseradish, sprinkle with a little anise or fennel and a little salt; continue until everything is packed
4. Pour good wine vinegar over it and let it stand for twenty-four hours
5. Instead of anise or fennel you can also interlace it with several cloves
6. Serve on a separate plate and pour a little of the liquid over it
