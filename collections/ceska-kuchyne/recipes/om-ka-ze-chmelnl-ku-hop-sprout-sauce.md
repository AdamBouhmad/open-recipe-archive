## Ingredients

- fresh hop sprouts
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- beef broth
- roux

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the fresh hop sprouts
2. Pour vinegar and water over them
3. Braise them, drain
4. Chop them
5. Make a brown roux
6. Drain beef broth and mix it with the water in which the hop sprouts were cooked
7. Caramelize a piece of sugar and add the chopped hop sprouts to it
