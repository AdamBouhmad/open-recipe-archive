## Ingredients

- 3 sprat heads
- half liter beef broth
- pale roux
- sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Put sprat heads in a pot with half liter of beef broth
2. Let cook until soft
3. Add pale roux and strain
4. Alternatively, sour cream and flour can be used instead of roux, then simmer
