## Ingredients

- butter
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- eggs
- sardines
- parsley or chives
- grated bread roll
- butter

## Directions

1. Oil a regular dish and place it on the stove.
2. Pour 1/4 liter of sour cream into it.
3. Carefully beat in the eggs.
4. Sprinkle with chopped sardines, chopped parsley or chives, grated bread roll and small pieces of butter.
5. Place the dish in the oven so the eggs bake.
