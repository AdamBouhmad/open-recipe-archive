## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- egg white
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar in egg white with lemon juice
2. Mix well for about quarter hour until completely white and thick
3. Apply evenly to baked goods and dry
