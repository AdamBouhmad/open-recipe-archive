## Ingredients

- barley
- water
- salt
- gingerbread
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar
- honey

## Directions

1. Pick through and rinse the barley, pour hot water over it to scald it, salt it and cook until tender
2. Drain it, then spread it on a platter, sprinkle with gingerbread and pour with butter
3. This way barley is also prepared in milk, except that it is sprinkled with cinnamon and sugar or brushed with honey
