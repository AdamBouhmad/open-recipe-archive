## Ingredients

- three pounds of venison
- water
- vinegar
- ordinary wine
- salt
- whole spices
- juniper
- bay leaves
- onion
- clarified butter
- flour (2 spoons)
- grated black bread
- lemon zest
- brown gingerbread or ginger

## Directions

1. Clean three pounds of venison well and place in a pot
2. Pour water, vinegar and ordinary wine over it, salt it
3. Add whole spices, some juniper, several bay leaves, and onion
4. Let the venison cook until almost tender
5. Heat clarified butter in a pan, add two spoons of flour and a small handful of grated black bread
6. Let it brown in the butter, then mix it with some of the sauce in which the venison is cooking
7. Pour this onto a platter and strain the sauce over the meat
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped lemon zest
9. If desired, you can add a piece of brown gingerbread or ginger to the sauce at the end
