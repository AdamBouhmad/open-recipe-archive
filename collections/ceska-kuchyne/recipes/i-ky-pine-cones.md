## Ingredients

- 36 dekagrams (24 lots) butter
- 2 and 1 deciliters (6 tablespoons) flour
- 2 egg yolks
- sweet sour cream
- several tablespoons yeast (baking soda)
- lemon
- 1 tablespoon ground sugar
- flower
- salt
- wine
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) chopped almonds
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat 36 dekagrams butter, add 2 and 1 deciliters flour, 2 egg yolks, sweet sour cream, several tablespoons yeast, lemon, 1 tablespoon ground sugar and flower
2. Let stiff dough rise a while, then salt it, add wine and 12 dekagrams chopped almonds
3. Form into pine cones and sprinkle with sugar
