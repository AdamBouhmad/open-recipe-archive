## Ingredients

- half liter beef broth
- five cloves of garlic
- salt
- pinch of marjoram
- pepper
- roux

## Directions

1. Add five cloves of garlic rubbed in salt to half liter beef broth
2. Add a pinch of marjoram that has been rubbed in the hand and a little pepper
3. Cook with roux and strain
