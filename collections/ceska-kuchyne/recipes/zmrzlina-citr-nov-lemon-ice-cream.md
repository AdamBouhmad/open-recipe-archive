## Ingredients

- Half kilogram sugar
- Three lemons
- Three-quarters liter water
- Glass of white wine
- Juice of six to eight lemons

## Directions

1. Grate three lemons for half a kilogram of sugar
2. Dissolve sugar in three-quarters liter of water
3. Add a glass of white wine and juice from six to eight lemons
4. Proceed with everything else as with other kinds of ice cream
