## Ingredients

- round steak or flank steak
- salt
- pepper
- white bread or bread rolls
- milk
- eggs
- onion
- butter
- green parsley
- allspice
- breadcrumbs or bread roll crumbs
- water

## Directions

1. Take a round steak or flank steak and pound it well, especially if it is round steak; salt and pepper it lightly
2. Make a filling: take three slices of white bread or two bread rolls, soak in milk, and when soft, press out and add two eggs, one small finely chopped onion browned in butter until golden, a little chopped green parsley, a piece of butter, a pinch of allspice, salt, and pepper; mix well
3. Spread this filling over the entire steak, roll it up, tie with strong thread, and place in an oblong baking dish
4. Pour in a cup of warm water, place pieces of butter on top, and let roast slowly for two hours, basting with the rendered juice every 10 minutes
5. Half an hour before serving, sprinkle with grated bread or bread roll crumbs
6. When roasted, slice into cutlets and serve
