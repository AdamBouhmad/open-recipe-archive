## Ingredients

- one cup of cleaned and well-rinsed rice
- milk or cream
- two tablespoons of butter
- eight egg yolks
- half a cup of sugar
- a little vanilla
- a little grated lemon rind
- a pinch of salt
- eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk or cream into a thick porridge
2. Let it cool
3. Add two tablespoons of butter
4. Add eight egg yolks
5. Add half a cup of sugar
6. Add a little vanilla
7. Add a little grated lemon rind
8. Add a pinch of salt
9. Stir for fifteen minutes
10. Gradually fold in the stiff snow made from eight egg whites
11. Pour immediately into a mold
12. Dust with sugar
13. Bake in the oven for three-quarters of an hour
14. Dust with sugar again and bring to table
15. Sago pudding is prepared in the same way
