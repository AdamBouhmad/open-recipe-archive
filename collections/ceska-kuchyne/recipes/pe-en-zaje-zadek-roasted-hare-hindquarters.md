## Ingredients

- Hare hindquarters
- Bacon
- Onion
- 5-6 whole peppers
- Bacon
- Bread
- Milk

## Directions

1. Lard the hindquarters with bacon
2. Slice onion, add 5-6 whole peppers and roast on a pan with sliced bacon
3. Soak bread in milk, squeeze it out, add to the sauce when the hare is placed on a platter, and simmer with it
4. Arrange the remaining bacon around the hare and pour with sauce
