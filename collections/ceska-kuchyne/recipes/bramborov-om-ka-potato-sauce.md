## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- 1/2 onion
- 3 large boiled potatoes
- beef broth
- wine vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Stew half a chopped onion in a piece of butter or lard.
2. Add three large boiled and grated potatoes.
3. Add a little beef broth and a little wine vinegar, salt.
4. Let it simmer for half an hour, then strain the sauce.
