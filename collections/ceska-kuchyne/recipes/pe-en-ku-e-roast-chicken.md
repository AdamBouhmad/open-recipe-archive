## Ingredients

- cleaned chickens
- salt
- pepper
- fresh parsley
- butter
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash well-cleaned chickens, salt inside and on the surface
2. Truss them, place several fresh parsley leaves and a piece of butter inside
3. Dust the inside lightly with ground pepper
4. Put them on a baking pan with a piece of fresh butter and a little beef broth or water
5. Roast them on both sides until golden, frequently brushing with butter
6. Baste every 10 minutes with the pan juices
7. Place them whole or cut into quarters on a plate
8. Pour the sauce over them and serve
