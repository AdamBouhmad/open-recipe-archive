## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 1 pint of oysters
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 0.5 tablespoon of flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Let a quart of milk cook in a double boiler.
2. Meanwhile, finely chop a pint of oysters in a wooden bowl.
3. Take a tablespoon of butter and half a tablespoon of flour, mix them well and stir into the boiling milk, then add the oysters.
4. Let it simmer, pour into a bowl, pepper and salt.
5. If they are allowed to cook longer, they will become tough.
