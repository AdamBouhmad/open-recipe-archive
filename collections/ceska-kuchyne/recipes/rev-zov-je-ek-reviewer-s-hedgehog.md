## Ingredients

- half kilogram sliced rolls
- 1/4 liter milk
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 15 dekagrams (10 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- 10 egg whites for snow
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut sliced rolls into thin slices, soak in 1/4 liter milk and stir over heat with 12 dekagrams butter until a thick porridge forms
2. When cooled, add beaten 10 egg yolks, 15 dekagrams sugar and lemon zest
3. When snow from 10 egg whites is mixed in, put everything in a mold in layers: a layer of sugared Revíz, then the mixture, then another such layer
4. Boil the hedgehog for 2 hours in hot water
