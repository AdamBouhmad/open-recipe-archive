## Ingredients

- 15 dekagrams Swiss cheese, grated
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 liter cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix the grated cheese with 3 eggs and 1/2 liter of cream.
2. Place the mixture on heat with butter.
3. Stir constantly until it begins to boil and rise.
4. Serve on a heated plate.
