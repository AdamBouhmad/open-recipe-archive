## Ingredients

- cleaned suckling pig
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon zest
- new spices
- onion
- veal trotters

## Directions

1. Salt the cleaned pig.
2. Pour onto a baking pan enough water mixed with 3½ deciliters (half a tablespoon) of good vinegar so that the pig, when placed on the pan, will be submerged.
3. Add pepper, thyme, bay leaf, a piece of lemon zest, new spices, and plenty of onion to the water.
4. To rosolate better, add veal trotters.
5. Boil everything.
6. Place the pig on a platter and pour part of the hot broth over it; pour the rest onto a baking pan.
7. When cooled, carefully roast the pig in that broth on both sides.
8. When cooked, it can either be chopped or placed whole in a mold decorated with various vegetables.
9. Slowly strain the aspic through cloth onto the pig and place on ice to cool; then unmold onto a platter and add pepper and vinegar.
10. If it does not unmold well, wrap the form in a warm cloth.
