## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram lean beef
- 1 kilogram veal
- 140 grams (8 lots) lean ham
- 3 liters water
- old hen or 3-4 old pigeons or partridge
- carrot, sliced
- bundle of vegetables
- celery

## Directions

1. Slice 1 kilogram of lean beef and 1 kilogram of veal
2. Add 140 grams of lean ham
3. Cook everything slowly in soup, then add 3 liters of water and old hen, old pigeons, or partridge
4. Cook for 6-7 hours over gentle heat with sliced carrot, bundle of vegetables, and celery
5. Remove fat
6. Strain
7. Can be kept for a long time
