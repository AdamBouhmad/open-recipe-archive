## Ingredients

- half kilogram pure butter
- four whole eggs
- eight egg yolks
- 375 grams sugar
- finely grated lemon zest
- 250 grams potato flour
- 250 grams fine wheat flour
- stiff snow made from eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat half kilogram of pure butter until creamy
2. Gradually add four whole eggs, eight egg yolks, 375 grams sugar and finely grated lemon zest
3. Mix to one side for at least three quarters of an hour
4. Mix with 250 grams potato flour and 250 grams fine wheat flour
5. Finally add stiff snow made from eight egg whites
6. Bake the dough slowly in the form of cakes
7. Turn these onto a sieve, and when cool
8. Layer them on top of each other
9. Between the cake layers spread optional preserves and cover everything with glaze
