## Ingredients

- Six hard-boiled and peeled eggs
- Peeled and cored apples
- Necessary fat from kidneys
- Small raisins
- Sugar
- Bread roll soaked and squeezed in milk
- Wine
- Salt
- Crushed clove
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop the six hard-boiled peeled eggs with the peeled and cored apples
2. Add the necessary fat from kidneys and mix well
3. Add small raisins, sugar, bread roll soaked and squeezed in milk, wine, salt, crushed clove, cinnamon and nutmeg
4. Mix everything together
5. Fill the filling into small pastries
6. Bake
