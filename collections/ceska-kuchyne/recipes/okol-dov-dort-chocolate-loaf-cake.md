## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of butter
- two coffee cups of sugar
- half coffee cup of sweet milk
- six egg whites
- three coffee cups of flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- one teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a dough from the ingredients
2. divide it in half
3. grate one quarter tablet of chocolate into the second half of the dough
4. alternately place one spoonful of white dough and one spoonful of dark dough into the pan, continuing until the pan is full
5. bake until done
6. coat the surface with chocolate icing when baked
