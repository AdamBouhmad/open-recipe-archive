## Ingredients

- beef broth
- roux
- pepper
- flower
- young onion greens, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Add roux, pepper and flower to beef broth
2. Simmer
3. Strain
4. Finely chop the green parts from young onions
5. Add and simmer
