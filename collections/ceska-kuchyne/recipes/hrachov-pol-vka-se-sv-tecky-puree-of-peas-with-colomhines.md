## Ingredients

- parsley
- celery
- salt
- water
- peas
- butter
- egg yolks
- eggs
- marjoram
- milk
- bread
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of parsley and one celery with greens into pieces and let cook in salted water
2. Mix the soft cooked peas, strain the salted water on it, mix it and let simmer a bit more
3. Strain the soup, add a little parsnip flower and fry it a bit with a light browned roux
4. For the colomhines: Rub half an ounce plus a half of fresh butter, add 4 spoonfuls of mixed and sieved peas to it, salt it, press two egg yolks and two whole eggs into it, mix it well
5. Add a little marjoram and half a bread soaked in good milk and then squeezed out, two or three spoonfuls of sweet cream
6. Mix everything well and bake small colomhines from it on a dimpled baking sheet
7. Then cut each into four pieces, place them in a soup bowl
8. When they cool, pour boiling soup on them and serve at table
