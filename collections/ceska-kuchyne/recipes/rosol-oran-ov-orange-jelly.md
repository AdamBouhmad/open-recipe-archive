## Ingredients

- ripe juicy apples
- water
- sugar
- zest of two oranges
- juice of two oranges

## Directions

1. Peel ripe juicy apples, cut into thin slices
2. Pour a little water over them and cook
3. During cooking, mash the apples so all the juice flows out easily
4. When well cooked, press through a sieve and pour the juice into a separate container
5. Cook the juice with sugar that has been rubbed with the zest of two oranges until thickened
6. Add the juice of two oranges and cook again until completely thickened
7. Pour the thickened juice into containers and seal tightly
