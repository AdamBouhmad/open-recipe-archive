## Ingredients

- chicken meat
- celery stalks
- tarragon leaves
- mayonnaise
- salt
- pepper
- small bread rolls

## Directions

1. Chop chicken meat into small cubes
2. Cut celery stalks into small strips, using as much as there is meat
3. Add tarragon leaves and enough mayonnaise to bind everything together
4. Season with salt and pepper and mix well
5. Take small bread rolls, cut off the top
6. Carefully scoop out the interior
7. Fill rolls with the mixture
8. Cover with the cut-off pieces and arrange on a napkin-covered plate
9. Veal roast, crab, pickled salmon and similar may be used instead of chicken
