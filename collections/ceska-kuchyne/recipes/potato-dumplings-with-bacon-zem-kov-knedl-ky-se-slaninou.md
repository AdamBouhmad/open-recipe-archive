## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram grated potatoes
- 90 grams (6 lots) bacon, cut into cubes
- 1 grated roll
- 1 roll, cut into cubes
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 4-5 eggs

## Directions

1. Fry the grated potatoes in the diced bacon
2. When fried for a while, let it cool
3. Add salt, eggs, and form dumplings
4. Flour the dumplings
5. Cook in salted water until they float to the surface
6. Serve with sauces or roasted bread crumbs browned in butter
