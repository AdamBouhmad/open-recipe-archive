## Ingredients

- 2 litres water
- a little flower
- a piece of cabbage
- a piece of celery
- a piece of butter
- a piece of pale meat broth
- mushrooms (if available)

## Directions

1. Put flower, cabbage, celery, butter, pale meat broth, and mushrooms if available into 2 litres of water
2. Bring to a boil and strain
