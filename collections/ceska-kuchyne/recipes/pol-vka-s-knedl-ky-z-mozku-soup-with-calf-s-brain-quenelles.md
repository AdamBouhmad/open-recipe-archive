## Ingredients

- calf or pork brain
- salted water
- parsley
- salt
- ground pepper
- butter
- white bread
- milk
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- saffron
- lívětem

## Directions

1. Clean calf or pork brain thoroughly and cook it in salted water, remove the skin and chop it finely with parsley, add salt and a little ground pepper, then lightly fry everything in butter.
2. Put it in a mortar, add a slice of white bread soaked in milk and mash together finely, or rub well in a bowl with a spoon.
3. Further rub one spoon of butter, mix in the mashed brain, add one whole egg and one egg yolk, add a little grated bread, mix everything well and then make small dumplings from it, but cook one first as a test.
4. If it falls apart, add a little more grated bread. Then cook them in good broth, remove the cooked dumplings to a soup bowl and pour the broth over them. Season with saffron and lívětem.
