## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- about 1.5 celery roots
- 2 parsley roots
- 2 Spanish onions
- 1 carrot
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 clean pike
- head of pike
- head of bream or carp
- 3 handfuls peas
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 4 bay leaves
- 5 cloves
- piece of ginger
- white pepper
- salt
- saffron
- flowers

## Directions

1. Grease an appropriate pot with fresh butter
2. Put in the pot 1.5 celery roots, 2 parsley roots, 2 Spanish onions, 1 carrot, and 2 garlic cloves, all cut up
3. Add 2 cleaned, gutted pike, the head of pike, and the head of bream or carp
4. Add 3 handfuls of peas
5. Let simmer slowly until the juices turn golden
6. Pour in 2 quarts of boiling water
7. Add 4 bay leaves, 5 cloves, a piece of ginger, several grains of white pepper, enough salt, a little saffron, and flowers
8. Cover the pot well and let boil for about 2 hours
9. Remove from heat and let stand quietly for about half an hour
10. Slowly strain through a cloth into an earthenware pot without clouding it
11. If using the bouillon for sauces and other fasting soups, keep it in a cool place, otherwise serve it hot in cups
