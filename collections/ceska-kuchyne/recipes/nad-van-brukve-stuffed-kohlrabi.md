## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- veal
- lard
- egg yolks
- butter
- soaked bread roll
- rose hip puree
- cauliflower rosettes
- sausage
- beef ribs

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi and cook until half done; when cooled, hollow them out and fill with the following forcemeat: chop veal with lard, place on a baking dish with two egg yolks, butter, soaked bread roll, let it soften on the stove.
2. When cooled, press everything through a sieve, add a raw egg yolk.
3. Fill the kohlrabi with this forcemeat, place in sauce and let steam until soft, then place on a platter hollow side up, which is covered with a cauliflower rosette, and surround alternately with a slice of sausage and beef ribs.
