## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- Meissen apples
- ham
- smoked tongue
- bacon
- wine vinegar
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Allow cooked peeled potatoes to cool
2. Peel several Meissen apples and chop finely
3. Cut pieces of ham, smoked tongue, and bacon (with bones removed) into small pieces
4. Slice the potatoes into rounds and spread on a platter
5. Sprinkle with apples and onion
6. Layer with pieces of ham, tongue, and bacon
7. Add another layer of potato rounds
8. Sprinkle with apples and onion again
9. Place cleaned and halved sardines on top
10. Mix wine vinegar with salt and Provençal oil and pour over everything
