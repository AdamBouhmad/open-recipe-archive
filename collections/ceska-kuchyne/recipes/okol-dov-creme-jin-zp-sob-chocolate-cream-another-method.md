## Ingredients

- seven deciliters cream
- 28 decagrams chocolate
- 14 decagrams sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 2 eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil seven deciliters of cream with 28 decagrams of chocolate and 14 decagrams of sugar
2. Let cool
3. Mix in 12 egg yolks, 2 eggs, and a little vanilla
4. Pour cream into small pots and place in a water bath in the oven
5. Bake for half an hour
6. Spread a cloth on a plate, place the pots on it, and serve at table
