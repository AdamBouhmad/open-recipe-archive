## Ingredients

- 2 small cups pearl barley
- 1.5 quarts water
- salt
- a large pinch of caraway seeds
- melted butter or lard
- smoked meat or sausage

## Directions

1. Rinse two small cups of sorted barley
2. Pour 1.5 quarts of water over them, salt
3. Add a large pinch of sorted caraway seeds and let them cook with frequent stirring until tender and thick
4. Turn out onto a dish and dress with melted butter or lard
5. Surround with smoked sausage or smoked meat cut into pieces
