## Ingredients

- Quail
- Rice
- Butter
- Parsley
- Onion
- Flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the quail for roasting or braising
2. Rinse the rice in several waters and drain
3. Add a piece of butter and flour to the rice and braise until tender
4. Meanwhile, braise the quail in butter with parsley and onion
5. Strain the liquid from the quail into the rice
6. Place the quail in the rice and let them rest together briefly on the stove
