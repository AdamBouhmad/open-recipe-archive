## Ingredients

- freshly milked milk
- rennet
- salt

## Directions

1. Freshly milked milk maintained at +18 to +20° R. temperature is curdled.
2. The cheese is broken, the whey is poured off; it is heated and poured over the cheese.
3. The cheese is removed, the whey poured off, placed in cloth, pressed. Once pressed, the cheese is left to cool to +12° R., then it is salted, pressed again, and flipped several times.
4. Cheese placed on shelves is turned and flipped daily; in six weeks it develops a firm rind.
