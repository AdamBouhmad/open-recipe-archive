## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sweet milk or cream
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix butter with sugar
2. Add eggs and mix well
3. Add flour mixed with baking powder
4. Mix everything
5. Roll out thin and place on a clean baking sheet or baking pan
6. Bake in a properly heated oven
7. Vanilla may be added if available
