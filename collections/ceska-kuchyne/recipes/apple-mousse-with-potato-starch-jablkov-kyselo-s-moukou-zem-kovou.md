## Ingredients

- several apples
- 1 1/2 liters water
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 1/2 liter potato starch flour mixed in cold water
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Cut several apples into pieces and remove the seeds
2. Cook in 1 1/2 liters of water with cinnamon and lemon zest until completely soft
3. Strain
4. Cook 1 liter of the liquid with 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar
5. Mix in 1/2 liter of potato starch flour that has been mixed in cold water
6. When everything has simmered, turn out onto a plate
7. Let cool in the cellar
8. Serve at table with sugar and sweet cream
