## Ingredients

- Fried bread rounds
- Tongue
- Aspic
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare round fried bread slices about half a third inch in diameter
2. Cover with thin tongue slices of the same size and shape
3. Place a small ball of chopped tongue in the middle
4. Coat with aspic
5. Sprinkle with chopped green parsley
6. Similar preparation can be made with ham
7. Other meat and poultry can also be used
