## Ingredients

- ripe currants
- crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the ripest currants, crush them and press to extract the juice
2. Add crushed sugar equal to the amount of juice
3. Mix both together until completely thickened
4. Pour into containers and seal
5. White currants make white preserve, while red currants make red preserve
