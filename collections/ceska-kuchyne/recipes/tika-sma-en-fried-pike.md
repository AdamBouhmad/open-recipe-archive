## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- pepper
- sardine butter
- flour
- egg
- roll grated or crackers
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Clean the pike well, cut into pieces
2. Salt, sprinkle with ground pepper and let lie covered for an hour
3. Dry the pike with a clean cloth, spread lightly with sardine butter
4. Coat in flour, dip in beaten egg, coat in grated roll or crackers
5. Fry in clarified butter
