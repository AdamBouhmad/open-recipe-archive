## Ingredients

- mutton loin or back meat
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut thick chops from the loin or back
2. Pound them with a mallet
3. Trim neatly with a knife
4. Salt the chops
5. Brush with melted butter
6. Roast on a grill or baking pan on both sides quickly
7. Cook for ten to twelve minutes until done
