## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- fresh butter
- parsley
- vegetable roots
- vinegar
- water
- pea cooking water
- mushrooms
- fish sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the pike into pieces and salt
2. After half an hour, dry
3. Place on a baking dish with 75 grams of fresh butter, a bundle of parsley, various vegetable roots, vinegar
4. Cook for a quarter hour in water in which peas were cooked
5. After a quarter hour, carefully remove the skin
6. Braise several mushrooms cleaned and sliced
7. Cook with fish sauce and pour over the pike
