## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fresh strawberries
- rum
- 0.5 kilogram crushed sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 3-4 liters hot water

## Directions

1. Crush the fresh strawberries with a spoon and place them in a pot
2. Pour rum over them and let them stand for 2-3 days, stirring them once daily
3. Strain off the rum and press the strawberries through a sieve
4. Add 0.5 kilogram crushed sugar to the liquid
5. Add the juice of 2 lemons
6. Pour the previously strained rum and 3-4 liters of hot water over the mixture
7. Cover and let it cool completely before serving cold
