## Ingredients

- squabs
- 3 dekagrams butter
- clove flower
- parsley root
- piece of celery
- piece of cabbage
- rice
- smoked meat

## Directions

1. Pluck the squabs, singe them, draw them, place on a baking dish with 3 dekagrams butter, clove flower, half parsley root, piece of celery and piece of cabbage
2. Braise on butter until the squabs are tender, being careful not to damage them
3. Remove the squabs from the broth
4. Pour this broth into rinsed rice, braise it
5. Place the pigeons in it for a moment, or place each separately
6. In that case, the rice may be sprinkled with finely chopped smoked meat
