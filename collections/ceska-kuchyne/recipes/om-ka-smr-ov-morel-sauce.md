## Ingredients

- fresh morels
- butter
- pepper
- parsley
- salt
- beef broth
- roux

## Directions

1. Slice fresh morels and braise until water evaporates
2. Add a piece of butter, pepper, green parsley and salt
3. Braise with the morels
4. Cook beef broth with roux and add to the morels
