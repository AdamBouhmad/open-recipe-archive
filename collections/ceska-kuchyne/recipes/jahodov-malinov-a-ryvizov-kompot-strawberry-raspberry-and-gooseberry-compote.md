## Ingredients

- strawberries or raspberries or gooseberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take nice strawberries, raspberries or gooseberries
2. Wash them clean and place on a sieve to dry
3. Place the aforementioned fruit in sugar boiled to the sixth degree
4. Boil everything slowly but it must not fall apart
5. Remove and place on a dish and sprinkle with ground sugar
