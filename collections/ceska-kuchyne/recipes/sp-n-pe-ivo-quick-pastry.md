## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- 21 dekagrams white flour
- 8 egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mix 24 dekagrams sugar with 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks
2. Add sugar-rubbed lemon peel
3. Mix with 21 dekagrams white flour and the stiffly beaten 8 egg whites
4. Fill greased molds, sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Bake on a baking sheet
