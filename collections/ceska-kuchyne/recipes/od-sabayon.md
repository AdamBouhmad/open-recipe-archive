## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- sweetened wine or fruit juice

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with sweetened wine or fruit juice
2. Pour into a pot and heat on warm stove while constantly whisking with a whisk
3. When the liquid begins to rise, remove the pot from heat, continuing to whisk until it is completely foamy and serve at table
4. To serve cold, place the pot in cold water while continuing to whisk
