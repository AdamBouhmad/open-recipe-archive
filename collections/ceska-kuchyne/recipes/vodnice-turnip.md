## Ingredients

- turnip
- butter
- sugar
- caramelized sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the turnip, slice into discs, place in a pot, pour water over it, let it come to a boil, strain the water.
2. Place the turnip on a baking dish with fresh butter, salt it, add a good piece of sugar, and braise until soft; pay attention that the discs do not break.
3. Strain pale roux through a sieve into it and cook.
4. Thus prepared turnip is usually served around beef.
5. If the turnip is to be darker, add a piece of caramelized sugar.
