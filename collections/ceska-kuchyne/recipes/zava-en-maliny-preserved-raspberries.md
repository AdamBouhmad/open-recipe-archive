## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil as much sugar to the sixth degree as the amount of raspberries to be preserved
2. Add the raspberries to the sugar
3. Turn more frequently but do not stir
4. Boil until the sugar is completely thickened
5. Place in jars
