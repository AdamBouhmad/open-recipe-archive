## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar
- batter
- apricots
- peaches
- fresh plums
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel 2 or more oranges, divide them into quarters, remove the pits, dust the orange pieces with sugar, dip in batter and fry
2. Banana fritters are prepared the same way; sliced bananas are first dusted with sugar
3. Apricots and peaches are first pitted, dusted with sugar and allowed to stand for a while; then they are coated in batter and fried to golden color
4. Fresh plums are first stewed with sugar and cinnamon, then allowed to cool, candied almonds are stuffed in place of the pits, each plum is pierced on a thin skewer, dipped in batter or stroganoff and fried
5. All fritters must be served hot and generously dusted with powdered sugar
