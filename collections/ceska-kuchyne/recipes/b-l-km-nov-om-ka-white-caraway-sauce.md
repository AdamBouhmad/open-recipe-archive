## Ingredients

- two tablespoons flour
- butter
- a quarter liter of clear meat broth
- one tablespoon of crushed caraway
- salt
- two egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a light roux from two tablespoons of flour and butter
2. Boil it with a quarter liter of clear meat broth
3. Add one tablespoon of crushed caraway
4. Season with salt
5. Mix in two beaten egg yolks and butter
