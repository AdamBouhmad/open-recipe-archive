## Ingredients

- water melon
- 4 small pots of sugar
- 1 small pot of water
- 1 large lemon or 2 small lemons
- 2 handfuls of raisins

## Directions

1. When we eat the red flesh from melons, trim the green rind, cut the white flesh into cubes not too large and not too small; then take 4 small pots of sugar and 1 small pot of water, make a syrup from this and add the melon to boil in it.
2. Boil it for a full 4 hours, then add one large lemon cut into slices or two small ones, 2 handfuls of raisins and let it boil 1.5 hours longer.
3. When the melon is boiled, it should not be white but transparently yellow.
