## Ingredients

- two and one-half deciliters cooled tea
- juice from one lemon
- juice from two oranges
- sugar
- rum
- eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add juice from one lemon and two oranges to two and one-half deciliters of cooled tea
2. Add sugar and rum
3. Add eight egg yolks
