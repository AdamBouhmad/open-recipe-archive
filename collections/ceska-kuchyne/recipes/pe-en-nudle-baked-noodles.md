## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 1/2 deciliters (1 žejdlík) of smetana (sour cream)
- butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of ground almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- a little lemon zest
- butter dough for base and top
- almonds
- sugar mixed with cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make noodles from 3 yolks and 2 whole eggs
2. Cook in salted water and drain
3. Place on baking pan
4. Pour sour cream over noodles, add butter, sugar and ground almonds
5. Bake until sour cream is absorbed
6. Let cool
7. Mix 6 egg yolks with lemon zest and pour over noodles
8. Cover baking pan bottom with butter dough
9. Place almonds on dough, then noodles, then cover with dough
10. Bake until noodles are done
11. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon
