## Ingredients

- one quart beer
- two ounces sugar
- a pinch of salt
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- one small cup cream
- one tablespoon flour
- a piece of butter
- a pinch of flowers (spice)
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Boil one quart of beer with two ounces of sugar and a pinch of salt
2. in a pot beat six egg yolks or three whole eggs
3. add to them one small cup of cream, one tablespoon of flour, a piece of butter and a pinch of flowers
4. beat well together
5. pour into boiling beer and beat well once more over heat
6. pour the soup into a bowl over fried bread cubes, or over bread cut into cubes, or serve plain in cups
7. for ordinary household use you can use half beer and half water
