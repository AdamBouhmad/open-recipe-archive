## Ingredients

- sifted sugar
- juice from half a lemon
- half tablespoon of rum
- several drops of water

## Directions

1. Mix sifted sugar with juice from half a lemon
2. Add half tablespoon of rum and several drops of water
3. Apply lukewarm to baked goods
