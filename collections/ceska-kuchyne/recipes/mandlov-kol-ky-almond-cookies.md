## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram melted butter
- 1/4 kilogram finely ground sugar
- several bitter almonds with egg white, ground together
- four eggs
- approximately 1/2 kilogram fine flour

## Directions

1. Mix the butter, sugar, almonds, and eggs until foaming
2. Slowly add the flour
3. Shape the batter into cookies
4. Bake gently
