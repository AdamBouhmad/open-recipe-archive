## Ingredients

- cherries not preserved in sugar
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- potato starch flour dissolved in wine
- fried bread (smaženou housku)

## Directions

1. Crush cherries that were not preserved in sugar
2. Thin with water
3. Boil several times
4. Strain
5. Add sugar, cinnamon, and potato starch flour mixed in wine
6. Pour over fried bread
