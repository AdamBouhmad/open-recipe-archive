## Ingredients

- one quart dry flour
- three-quarters pound fresh butter
- six egg yolks
- half a cup cold water
- one tablespoon fine sugar
- salt
- fine filling
- fine paper or parchment
- optional: apples, peaches, plums, strawberries, cherries or other suitable fruit
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Sift one quart of dry flour onto a work surface
2. Crumble three-quarters pound of fresh butter into it finely
3. Mix it, gather into a mound, make a well in the center
4. Put six egg yolks, half a cup of cold water, one tablespoon of fine sugar, and a pinch of salt into the well
5. Mix with a knife, then knead with hands
6. Roll out the dough and fold it, roll it out again and fold again
7. Dust a clean cloth with flour, wrap the dough in it, and let it rest for half an hour in a cold place or on ice
8. Roll out the dough again and cut into square or round pieces
9. Fill with fine filling and form tarts according to the previous recipe
10. Place on a baking sheet lined with buttered paper
11. Alternatively, line a baking dish with dough rolled to about half a finger thickness and place quartered apples, halved fresh peaches, plums, strawberries, cherries or other suitable fruit on top
12. Sprinkle with sugar to taste and bake until golden
