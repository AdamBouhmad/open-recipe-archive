## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- two bowls powdered sugar
- zest of one lemon
- four egg yolks
- two bowls flour
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites into stiff meringue
2. Gradually fold in two bowls of powdered sugar and grated zest of one lemon
3. Then fold in four egg yolks and two bowls of flour
4. You may use vanilla extract instead of lemon
5. When well mixed, make on paper into finger-length pieces
6. Sprinkle with sugar powder and bake quickly in a warm oven
7. When they begin to turn yellow, cover with paper
8. When baked, the top should be pale yellow and the bottom pale brown
9. When baked, brush the paper underneath with a damp sponge; after several minutes remove the lady fingers from the paper, place on a large sieve, put in a warm place and let the lady fingers dry
