## Ingredients

- beef soup
- carrots
- leeks
- whole cabbage
- parsley roots
- celery
- 4 large potatoes
- 0.25 liter water from red beets
- 0.5 liter finely chopped red beets
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- flour grits

## Directions

1. Strain beef soup in which much carrot, leek, whole cabbage, parsley roots, celery and potatoes were boiled
2. Set the vegetables aside, chop the cabbage finely and cut the remaining vegetables into strips, add back to soup
3. Add water from red beets and finely chopped red beets, season with salt and pepper
4. If not sour enough, add vinegar
5. When the soup boils, add a little flour grits, boil a bit more and serve
