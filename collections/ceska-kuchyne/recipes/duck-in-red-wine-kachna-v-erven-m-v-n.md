## Ingredients

- roasted duck
- hot water
- onion
- lemon zest
- spice
- salt
- brown roux
- red wine
- broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over the roasted duck, add onion, lemon zest, spice and salt, braise briefly. To the sauce add brown roux, a quarter liter of red wine, several tablespoons of broth and juice from half a lemon. When the duck is tender, pour the sauce over it.
