## Ingredients

- 2 liters water
- A handful of caraway
- A piece of butter or lard
- Mushrooms
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Salt 2 liters of water, add a handful of caraway and cook
2. Strain, add a piece of butter or lard and mushrooms
3. Cook again and pour over fried bread
