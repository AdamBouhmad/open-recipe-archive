## Ingredients

- four pork legs
- two veal legs
- one kilogram pork meat
- veal meat
- skin from ham and pork meat
- six liters water
- lard
- onion
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- bay leaf
- pepper
- allspice
- vinegar
- burned sugar

## Directions

1. Take four pork legs, two veal legs, one kilogram pork meat, veal meat, and skin from ham and pork meat, cleaned and chopped, and cover with six liters of water and cook
2. Fry onion, carrot, parsley and celery (cut into pieces) in lard
3. Put into pot, add bay leaf, pepper and allspice and cook several hours on gentle heat
4. When the pork skin is completely soft, salt the aspic, add good vinegar and wine, and add burned sugar for color
5. Cook all for about half an hour, set aside, strain and let the aspic set in a cool place or with ice
