## Ingredients

- two venison shoulders
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- lard
- carrot
- cloves
- bay leaf
- vinegar
- meat broth
- additional meat broth for sauce

## Directions

1. Wash two venison shoulders well and remove the bone from the joint
2. Sprinkle them inside with finely chopped onion, parsley, pepper and salt
3. Roll each one up so the filling goes inside, tie well with string, and lard the outer part with bacon, then salt
4. Place carrot and onion sliced into rounds on a pan with several peppercorns, two cloves, a bay leaf and good lard
5. Place the prepared shoulders on this, pour a little vinegar and meat broth over them
6. Let them braise slowly until browned
7. When the venison is tender enough, remove it and place on a platter
8. Remove the string, then add several more spoons of meat broth to the sauce
9. Let it boil a little longer, then strain it through a sieve, skim off the fat and pour it over the braised shoulders
10. You can also serve pepper sauce on the side as for venison saddle
