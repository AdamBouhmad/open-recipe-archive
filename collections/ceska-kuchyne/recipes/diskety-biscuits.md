## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 2 full tablespoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon lard or butter
- salt to taste
- 1 small cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add baking powder, lard or butter and salt and mix well
2. Add sweet milk, 1 tablespoon at a time and mix well
3. Dust the rolling pin with flour, turn the dough onto it and add just enough flour so you can roll it to about one inch thickness
4. Cut rounds with a cutter, place on a baking sheet and bake immediately in a hot oven for 20 minutes
