## Ingredients

- peeled and washed potatoes
- butter
- lard
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut peeled and washed potatoes into slices
2. Let them lie in cold water for half an hour, then dry them well with a cloth
3. Place half butter and half lard in a skillet
4. Salt and pepper the potatoes and place them in the skillet and cover tightly
5. Occasionally toss them with a knife and shake the pan until all the slices are nicely browned
