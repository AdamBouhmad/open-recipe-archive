## Ingredients

- strawberries with stems
- sugar (equal weight to strawberries)
- rum

## Directions

1. During dry weather, gather strawberries with stems and lay them on a shallow dish so they don't touch each other and sprinkle with sugar
2. Press through a sieve 1 liter of ripe strawberries, add as much sugar as the strawberries weigh, and boil over mild heat
3. Strain through muslin, boil the juice again and remove foam
4. When the juice cools, place the sugared strawberries in it
5. Bring to heat but do not allow to boil and let cool
6. Repeat this about four times
7. Carefully remove strawberries and place in jars
8. Boil the juice once more, let cool and pour over the strawberries
