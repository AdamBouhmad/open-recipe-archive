## Ingredients

- sliced mushrooms
- butter
- lemon juice
- brown roux
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Stew the sliced mushrooms in butter on thin slices
2. Add lemon juice
3. Add brown roux
4. Season with pepper
5. Pour in broth
6. Simmer until cooked
