## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- salt
- lard
- peppercorns
- juniper berries
- lemon zest
- onion
- pepper
- flour
- nutmeg
- bread soaked and fried
- marjoram
- garlic

## Directions

1. Clean the woodcocks and remove innards; save the liver, stomach and heart.
2. Salt the woodcocks and lard them like partridges.
3. Add several peppercorns, juniper berries, a piece of lemon zest and chopped onion.
4. Braise the woodcocks in this.
5. Finely chop the saved innards and add pepper, flour, nutmeg, a piece of fried onion in butter, fried grated bread, some marjoram and a stroke of garlic.
6. Mix everything with the innards.
7. Grease a small pan, spread the mixture on it and fry it.
8. Straighten the woodcocks and arrange the fried mixture around them, or place the mixture in the middle and the woodcocks around it.
