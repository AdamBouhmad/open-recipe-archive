## Ingredients

- cleaned trout
- salt
- onion
- pepper
- parsley
- red wine

## Directions

1. Cut cleaned trout into pieces and salt
2. Arrange on a base of onion, pepper, and parsley
3. Cover with red wine
4. Simmer for a quarter hour
5. Remove the trout
6. Strain the sauce, brown with flour, and pour over the fish
