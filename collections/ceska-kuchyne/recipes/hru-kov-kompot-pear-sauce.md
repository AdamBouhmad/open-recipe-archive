## Ingredients

- Pears
- Water
- Sugar
- A piece of whole cinnamon
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cut pears in half, peel them, remove the seeds
2. Cook them like apples in water with sugar, a piece of whole cinnamon and lemon peel
3. Arrange them on a dish and pour thickly boiled juice over them
4. Alternatively, you can use equal parts water and red wine
5. You can also cook the pears whole if you wish
