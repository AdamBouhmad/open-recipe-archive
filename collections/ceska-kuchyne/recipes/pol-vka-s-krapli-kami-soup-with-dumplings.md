## Ingredients

- leftovers of poultry or veal
- 2 tablespoons of soup
- a piece of small onion
- fresh butter
- dough
- beef broth

## Directions

1. If there are leftovers of poultry or veal meat, chop them finely and place on a baking sheet, add 2 tablespoons of soup, a piece of small onion, fresh butter
2. While this is stewing, roll out dough like for noodles and cut into squares; place a bit of the mixture on each square, fold the corners and cook in beef broth
3. When the soup is boiling, pour it over the prepared dumplings
