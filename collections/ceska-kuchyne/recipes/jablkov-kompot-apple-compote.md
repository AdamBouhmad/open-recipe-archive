## Ingredients

- nice apples
- a little sugar
- water
- orange peel
- cinnamon
- apple puree

## Directions

1. Select nice apples and peel them
2. Cut out the cores and seeds
3. Place on a baking sheet
4. Add a little sugar, water, orange peel and cinnamon
5. Cover with a lid and braise
6. Place the braised apples on a bed of flour
7. Sprinkle with ground sugar
8. If desired, decorate around the edges with apple puree
