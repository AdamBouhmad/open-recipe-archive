## Ingredients

- Fully ripe currants
- Sugar as needed

## Directions

1. Select fully ripe currants
2. Remove all seeds using a pointed tool like a feather
3. Place currants on a baking pan
4. Add sugar as needed
5. Cook until thickened
6. Proceed as with other preserves
