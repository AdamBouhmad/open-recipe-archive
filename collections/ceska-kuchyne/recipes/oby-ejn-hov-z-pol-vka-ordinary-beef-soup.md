## Ingredients

- two pounds beef
- three to four quarts water
- one tablespoon salt
- small parsley root
- small carrot
- piece of celery
- one onion
- piece of liver

## Directions

1. Clean everything well. Put bones in cold water, place over fire, and when water is hot, add the meat and piece of liver.
2. Let simmer slowly, skim the foam, salt the soup.
3. When meat is tender, strain the soup through fine sieve into a pot, in which you will thicken it.
4. The soup may also be thickened when the meat is half cooked by adding cleaned groats, torn groats, wheat groats, rice or sago, as preferred, and let them cook in the soup with the meat.
5. For 6 to 8 persons, one quarter pound of any thickening agent suffices, and half an hour is enough to cook it, except groats, which are cooked in fifteen minutes.
6. The weight of meat and quantity of water, vegetables and thickening agent should be adjusted according to the number of persons.
