## Ingredients

- meat remnants
- roux
- broth
- onion
- bay leaf
- spices
- bunch of vegetables
- vinegar
- lemon juice
- sour cucumbers or mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare sauce from roux with broth, onion, bay leaf, spices and a bunch of vegetables
2. Strain the sauce
3. Add vinegar and lemon juice
4. Add sliced sour cucumbers or mushrooms
5. Add cut meat and heat
