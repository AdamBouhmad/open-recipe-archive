## Ingredients

- 4-5 liters of cherries
- 1/2 liter of vinegar
- 1/2 kilogram of pounded sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Pour 1/2 liter of vinegar over the cherries and let stand for two days
2. On the third day, boil the poured-off vinegar with 1/2 kilogram of pounded sugar
3. Sprinkle the cherries with 1/2 kilogram sugar and let stand 24 hours in the cold
4. Then boil gently in the vinegar, remove with a spoon
5. Add vanilla to the juice, boil it, pour over the cherries and seal the jars well
