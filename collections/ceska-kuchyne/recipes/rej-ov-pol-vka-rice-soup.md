## Ingredients

- half a pot of rice
- warm water
- salt
- cloves
- fresh butter
- green parsley
- saffron
- beef broth
- sour cream
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Pick over half a pot of rice, rinse it several times in lukewarm water, put in a pot, pour a pot of hot water over it, salt, add cloves, a piece of fresh butter, let it cook slowly for half an hour until the rice is soft.
2. If the water cooks out before the rice is soft, add a little more water.
3. Be careful that the rice does not burn and does not break apart when stirring.
4. When the rice is cooked, put it in a soup bowl, add a little chopped green parsley, saffron powder and pour hot strained beef broth over it.
5. Alternatively, beat one egg yolk in good sour cream (about as much as fits in a coffee pot), pour it into the cooked broth, quickly beat this, pour into a soup bowl and serve immediately.
