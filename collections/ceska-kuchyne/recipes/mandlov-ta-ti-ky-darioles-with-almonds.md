## Ingredients

- half pound butter for dough
- half small cup sugar
- lemon zest from one lemon
- four egg yolks
- three ounces peeled grated almonds
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Make butter dough from half pound butter
2. Roll out to the thickness of straw and cut into twelve square pieces
3. Put half small cup sugar in a bowl, add lemon zest from one lemon
4. Add four egg yolks and three ounces peeled grated almonds
5. Mix well and finally fold in stiff snow from four egg whites
6. Put a teaspoon of mixture into each square
7. Brush corners with egg, fold two opposite corners over each other
8. Place pouches on a butter-greased sheet and bake in the oven
9. While hot, dust generously with powdered sugar
