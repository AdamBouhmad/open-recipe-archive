## Ingredients

- 1 quart lukewarm water
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- salt to taste
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Pour the lukewarm water with baking soda mixed in to the starter
2. Salt the mixture
3. Add flour as needed and work the dough thoroughly with a wooden spoon so it does not stick to the bowl or hands
4. Dust with flour again, cover with a warm cloth and let rise for about half an hour
5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface, shape into a loaf, place on a baking sheet and let rise again
6. Place in a well-heated oven and bake for at least two hours
