## Ingredients

- 1 roasted veal kidney
- 5 whole eggs
- salt
- 3 heaping tablespoons of breadcrumbs
- a little finely chopped lemon zest
- a little chopped fresh parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the roasted veal kidney finely, place it in a bowl, add five whole eggs to it, salt it, add three heaping tablespoons of breadcrumbs, a little finely chopped lemon zest, a little chopped fresh parsley and mix well.
2. Butter the indentations on a muffin pan, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, put a spoonful of the mixture into each indentation and bake until golden.
