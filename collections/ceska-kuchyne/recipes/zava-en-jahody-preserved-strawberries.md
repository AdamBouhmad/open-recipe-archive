## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 quarts of strawberries
- 2 pounds of white sugar
- half a small pot of water

## Directions

1. Pick beautiful strawberries, strain through a sieve and let drain well.
2. Put 2 pounds of white sugar and half a small pot of water into a glazed pot and let it boil for about 10 minutes.
3. Then add the strawberries, let them boil for about 25 minutes and skim the foam well, but do not stir much so you do not crush them.
4. Remove the strawberries with a skimming spoon and place into jars carefully so they remain as whole as possible.
5. The juice with sugar in which they were boiled is then cooked further until it becomes thick and pour over the strawberries so the jars are nicely full.
6. Then cook further as stated in the general rules.
