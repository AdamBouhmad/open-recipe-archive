## Ingredients

- mutton off the bone
- carrots
- parsley root
- cabbage
- 15 grams (1 lot) good butter
- asparagus

## Directions

1. Braise mutton with carrots, parsley root, cabbage, and butter until meat is tender
2. Strain pale gravy over the meat
3. Cut asparagus at the point where it becomes woody
4. Braise asparagus briefly with meat until tender
5. Arrange asparagus around the meat or in the center on a platter
