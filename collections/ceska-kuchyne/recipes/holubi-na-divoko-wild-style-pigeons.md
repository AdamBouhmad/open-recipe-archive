## Ingredients

- young pigeons, cleaned
- bacon
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Wrap cleaned young pigeons in bacon.
2. Place on a baking sheet and roast until brown.
3. Remove and take off the bacon.
4. Pour sweet cream over the roasted pigeons.
5. Roast everything again.
