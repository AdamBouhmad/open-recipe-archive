## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 whole eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 2 cups flour on a board, make a well in it, beat two whole eggs into it, divide with a knife, then work with hands until the dough is quite stiff
2. Divide the dough into three equal parts, dust the board with flour and roll each piece with a roller until very thin
3. Place the rolled dough on a white cloth and let it dry a little
4. Cut each rolled piece in half, place one on top of the other, cut into strips about two fingers wide, arrange several strips together one on top of the other and cut into thin noodles which you can cook in any meat broth. From the same dough you can instead of noodles cut fine small flakes.
