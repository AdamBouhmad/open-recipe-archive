## Ingredients

- 2 pounds powdered sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 2 pounds fine sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon strong lemon extract
- 24 fresh egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream together the powdered sugar and butter until light.
2. Add 1 cup sweet cream, 2 pounds sifted flour, 1 teaspoon strong lemon extract, and beat well.
3. Beat very stiff snow from 24 fresh egg whites and gradually fold into the dough.
4. Line a deep mold with well-buttered paper.
5. Fill it three-quarters full with dough and bake at moderate and steady heat for about an hour.
6. Test with a wire or straw to see if it is baked.
7. When done, turn out onto a large plate.
8. When cooled, place on a cake stand, spread white frosting over the entire surface, and decorate with candy, figures, or sugar flowers.
