## Ingredients

- semolina
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- butter for frying
- sugar with ground vanilla for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Boil semolina in milk with butter and sugar
2. Spread as thick as a finger on a sheet
3. Cut out small doughnuts, heat the sheet so they come off easily
4. Fry in butter and sprinkle with sugar mixed with ground vanilla
