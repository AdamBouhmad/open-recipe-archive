## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) chocolate
- 10 egg yolks
- several tablespoons of beaten sugar
- 1/4 liter potato starch flour
- 1/2 liter cold milk
- several tablespoons hot milk
- 1 1/2 liters boiling milk
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Grate 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of chocolate
2. Mix with 10 egg yolks and several tablespoons of beaten sugar
3. Gradually add 1/4 liter of potato starch flour
4. Add 1/2 liter of cold milk
5. Add several tablespoons of hot milk and stir
6. Mix over heat in 1 1/2 liters of boiling milk until a thick porridge forms
7. Let cool in a deep dish
8. Serve at table with whipped cream
