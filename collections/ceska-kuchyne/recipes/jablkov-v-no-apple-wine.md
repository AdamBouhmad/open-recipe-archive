## Ingredients

- Fine, ripe, juicy apples
- Several baked apples in their skins

## Directions

1. Peel the fine, ripe, juicy apples, grate them, press them through fine cloth into a container.
2. To the pressed apple juice add several baked apples in their skins and let everything ferment.
3. Skim off the scum that appears on the surface after several days.
4. Siphon the must into bottles, cork it well, and store in a cool place.
