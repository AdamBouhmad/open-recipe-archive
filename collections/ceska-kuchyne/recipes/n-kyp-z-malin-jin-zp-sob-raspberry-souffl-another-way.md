## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters ripe raspberries
- 12 egg whites
- 375 grams (25 lots) powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Press the ripe raspberries through a sieve
2. Fold in snow from 12 egg whites and 375 grams (25 lots) of powdered sugar
3. Fill a mold with the mixture, sprinkle with sugar
4. Bake in moderate heat for half an hour
