## Ingredients

- beef broth
- carrot
- asparagus
- celery
- beans
- green cabbage
- green parsley
- bread dumplings
- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- butter
- gravy
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook various vegetables in beef broth such as carrot, asparagus, celery, beans, and green cabbage until all is tender
2. Add green parsley and bread dumplings cooked in salted water with cauliflower
3. Alternatively, cook the vegetables in salted water with butter, gravy, and several egg yolks
4. Pour over toasted bread roll
