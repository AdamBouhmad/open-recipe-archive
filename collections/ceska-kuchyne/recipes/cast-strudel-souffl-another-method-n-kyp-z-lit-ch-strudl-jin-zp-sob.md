## Ingredients

- cast strudel
- butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 3.5 deciliters (1 žejdlík) sweet cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Slice cast strudel into noodle-like pieces
2. Fry in butter and place in a mold greased with butter
3. Beat together on the stove 12 egg yolks, 3.5 deciliters sweet cream and sufficient sugar, stirring until it thickens completely
4. Pour this mixture over the prepared noodles
5. Bake the soufflé
