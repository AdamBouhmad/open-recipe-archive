## Ingredients

- finely beaten sugar
- egg whites
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sift finely beaten sugar through a sieve
2. Place egg whites (more or less to taste) on a deep plate
3. Mix with sugar until it turns into a paste
4. Add a little lemon juice
5. Stir and beat continuously; the longer this is done, the better the glaze
6. Spread the prepared glaze quickly onto the cake
7. Place the cake in a warm oven
8. When the glaze is completely dry, then decorate
