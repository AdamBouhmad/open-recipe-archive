## Ingredients

- watercress
- salt
- wine vinegar
- Provençal oil

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse watercress
2. Place on a plate
3. Season with salt
4. Pour wine vinegar with Provençal oil over it
5. Can also be prepared like head lettuce as described previously
