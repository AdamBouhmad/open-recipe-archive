## Ingredients

- Buckwheat
- water or milk
- butter
- salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook buckwheat in water or milk with butter and salt, stirring constantly.
2. Allow the porridge to cool.
3. Mix in 4 egg yolks and fold in snow made from 4 egg whites, mixing everything together.
4. Grease a baking pan and bake the mixture in the oven.
