## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- piece of veal
- beef soup
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- white turnip
- salt
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Pound the chicken and piece of veal
2. Cook in beef soup with carrot, parsley, celery, white turnip, and salt
3. Strain
4. When ready to serve, add several pieces of cabbage
