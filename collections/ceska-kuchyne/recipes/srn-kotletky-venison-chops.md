## Ingredients

- aged venison
- bacon
- salt
- butter
- tomato or mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Cut chops from aged venison
2. Pound them
3. Lard with bacon
4. Salt and fry on both sides in butter
5. Pour off the butter and serve with tomato or mushroom sauce
