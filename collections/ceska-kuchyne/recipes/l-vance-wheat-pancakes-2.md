## Ingredients

- two cups sour milk
- two egg yolks
- salt
- about one cup sifted flour
- two egg whites
- small level teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in a little hot water

## Directions

1. Take two cups of sour milk and add two egg yolks and mix well
2. Add a little salt and about one cup of sifted flour and mix well again
3. If the batter is too thin, thinner than for a batter, add more flour and beat well to a smooth batter
4. Beat the two egg whites into stiff snow and add it to the batter with a small level teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in a little hot water, and mix gently
5. Heat a griddle, grease it with lard and bake the pancakes, taking a spoon of batter for each pancake and when cooked on one side, turn it
6. Serve with syrup or honey
