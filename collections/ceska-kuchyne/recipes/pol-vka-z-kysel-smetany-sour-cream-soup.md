## Ingredients

- 1 litre sour cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 15 grams (1 lot) butter
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks in 1 litre of sour cream
2. Add 15 grams (1 lot) of butter
3. Boil over moderate heat and pour over fried roll
