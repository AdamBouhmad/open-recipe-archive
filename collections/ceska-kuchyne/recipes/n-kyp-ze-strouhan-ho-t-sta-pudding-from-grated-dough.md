## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- flour
- 7 deciliters (2 quarts) sour cream
- 4 eggs
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs with flour, grate it and cook with 7 deciliters (2 quarts) of sour cream
2. When it cools, add 4 eggs and 8 egg yolks
