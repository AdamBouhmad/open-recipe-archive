## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- raw eggs (two)
- lemon zest (small piece)
- green parsley (chopped)
- salt
- milk
- bread crumbs (grated)
- sardeles (optional)
- pike or crayfish meat (optional)
- goose liver (optional)
- sweetbreads (optional)
- mushrooms (optional)
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled eggs lengthwise into halves
2. Remove yolks and crush in a bowl with a piece of butter
3. Add two raw eggs
4. Add a small piece of lemon zest
5. Add a little chopped green parsley
6. Add salt and milk and enough grated bread crumbs to make a thick filling
7. Optional: add crushed sardeles, chopped pike or crayfish meat, stewed goose liver, sweetbreads, or mushrooms
8. Mix well
9. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
10. Put the eggs back together
11. Place on a greased baking sheet
12. Mix the remaining filling with a little sour cream
13. Pour under the eggs
14. Bake for half an hour
15. Serve alone on a plate or surrounded with various vegetables
