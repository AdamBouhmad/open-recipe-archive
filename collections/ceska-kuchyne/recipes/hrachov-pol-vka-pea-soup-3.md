## Ingredients

- peas
- soft water
- seasoning
- vegetables
- salt
- broth
- fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Pick over the peas, wash them, soak overnight and the next day place in cold soft water, add seasoning, chopped vegetables and salt, later add as much broth as needed
2. When the peas are soft, strain the soup and pour over fried bread roll
