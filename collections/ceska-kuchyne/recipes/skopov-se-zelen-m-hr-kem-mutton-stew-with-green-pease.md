## Ingredients

- two pounds of mutton from the shoulder
- flour
- lard
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- water
- green peas

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of mutton from the shoulder into large cubes.
2. Sprinkle with flour and brown them to a golden color in half lard and half butter.
3. Salt and pepper and pour water into the pan so that the meat is covered.
4. Cover and let it braise until tender.
5. When the meat is tender, remove it and skim all the fat from the gravy.
6. Before serving at the table, pour the gravy into the green peas, which you have previously cooked, and mix the prepared meat into it.
