## Ingredients

- large anchovies
- flour
- wine
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Select large anchovies, wash clean, cut off tails and fins, tear in half, remove bones, dry in clean cloth
2. Sprinkle with flour, dip in batter made from flour and wine, fry in hot butter
3. Serve garnished with green parsley after soup
4. Alternative: Anchovies may also be dipped in beaten egg and coated in grated bread, then fried the same way as other fish
