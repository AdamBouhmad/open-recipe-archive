## Ingredients

- pears, peeled and quartered
- sugar, two pounds for every ten pounds of pears

## Directions

1. In the evening, peel pears and cut into quarters, sprinkle with sugar—two pounds of sugar for every ten pounds of pears.
2. Let sit overnight.
3. The next day, cook for three hours in the juice released by the pears.
4. When cooled, place in stone jars, cover well with strong paper or clean cloth, and store in a cool place.
