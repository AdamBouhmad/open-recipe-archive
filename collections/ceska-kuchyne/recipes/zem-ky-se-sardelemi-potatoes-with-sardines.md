## Ingredients

- potatoes
- olive oil
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- sardines

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes and slice into thin slices
2. On a baking dish pour olive oil over them
3. Add chopped parsley, chives, a piece of garlic, pepper, salt and a little vinegar
4. While frying, shake them several times
5. Finally add several chopped sardines
