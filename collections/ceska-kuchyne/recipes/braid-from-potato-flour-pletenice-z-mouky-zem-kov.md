## Ingredients

- 500 milliliters milk
- 180 grams (12 lots) butter
- 250 milliliters potato flour
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Cook butter in milk
2. Add potato flour while stirring constantly over heat
3. Remove from heat and stir until completely cool
4. Mix in egg yolks, salt, and sugar
5. Roll dough into thin strips
6. Cross them over each other
7. Press in the middle
8. Brush with egg white
9. Bake on a baking sheet
10. Sprinkle with sugar
