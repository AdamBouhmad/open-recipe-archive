## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- sugar
- cinnamon
- rum or wine
- half liter milk
- 2 eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 6 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Cut ripe plums in half, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
2. Pour rum or wine over them, cover and let stand a while
3. Prepare dough from half liter milk, 2 eggs, 6 egg yolks, salt, 6 tablespoons flour and snow from egg whites
4. When it begins to harden from the bottom, place plums on the roll and pour dough over them
5. When baked, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
