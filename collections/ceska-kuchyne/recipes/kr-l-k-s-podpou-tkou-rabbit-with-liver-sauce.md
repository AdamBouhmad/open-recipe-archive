## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned rabbit, head, neck, and offal reserved
- butter
- finely chopped onion
- broth or boiling water
- parsley
- celery
- lemon juice
- liver (from reserved offal)

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned rabbit into pieces, reserving the head, neck, and offal.
2. Wash and dry the meat, salt it, dust with flour.
3. Fry in hot butter with finely chopped onion.
4. After a short time, add either broth or boiling water, parsley, and celery.
5. Add a little lemon juice and braise the meat in this sauce.
6. Strain the sauce and add the liver to it, season it, and pour over the rabbit meat.
7. This method can also be used for beef.
