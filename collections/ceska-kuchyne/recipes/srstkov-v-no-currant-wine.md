## Ingredients

- half-ripe currants
- abundant sugar

## Directions

1. Only comb the half-ripe currants when dry
2. Select the most beautiful, crush and press them
3. Mix the juice with abundant sugar and pour into a barrel filled completely
4. Let the wine rest peacefully for four to six weeks
5. Then draw into other containers in which it remains for four to five months
6. Then draw the wine into bottles
