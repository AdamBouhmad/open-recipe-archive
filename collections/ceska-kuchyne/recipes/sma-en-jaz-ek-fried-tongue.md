## Ingredients

- Pork tongue
- Salt
- Bay leaf
- Vegetables
- Spices
- White wine
- Vinegar
- Egg
- Bread roll, grated
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook pork tongue in salted water to which various spices such as bay leaf, vegetables, spices, as well as white wine and a little good vinegar have been added.
2. Remove the skin from the cooked tongues, dip the tongues in beaten egg, coat in grated bread roll and fry in butter.
3. The fried tongue is served to the table garnished as desired with vegetables or other dishes.
