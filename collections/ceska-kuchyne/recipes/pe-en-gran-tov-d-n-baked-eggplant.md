## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Crackers
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the eggplant until soft; when cooked, cut it in half, scrape out the flesh, mix it well, and for each cup of flesh add one spoonful of rolled crackers and one spoonful of butter, salt and pepper, mix everything well and put in a bowl.
2. Beat eggs, spread half of it over the mixture and sprinkle with rolled crackers, then coat with the other half of the egg and sprinkle with crackers again and let it bake until golden.
