## Ingredients

- two pounds veal
- two onions, finely chopped
- three tablespoons butter
- salt
- beef broth
- flour
- paprika
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash two pounds of veal and cut into neat pieces
2. Place two finely chopped onions and three tablespoons of butter in a pan and let it foam
3. Add the cut veal, salt it, cover and let it stew until tender
4. Add several tablespoons of beef broth to the meat
5. Dust with a little flour and sprinkle with paprika
6. Let it stew a while longer, then add a small cup of sour cream
7. Let it come to a boil, place on a platter, strain the gravy over it and serve
