## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs well
2. Add 1 cup sweet milk and 1 tablespoon salt to it
3. Mix 1 teaspoon baking powder into 3 cups flour and add to the milk
4. Work this well on a plate
5. Shape five dumplings, roll them on a rolling pin to round them, and put them in water to boil
