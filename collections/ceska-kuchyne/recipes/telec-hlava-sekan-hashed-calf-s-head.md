## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head, cleaned
- veal meat, chopped
- piece of veal fat from kidney
- piece of onion, chopped
- lemon rind, chopped
- 1 roll soaked in milk and squeezed, or 2 slices white bread with crust removed
- 3 eggs mixed with butter
- 4 anchovies, cleaned and rubbed with butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- salt
- fresh butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned calf's head in salted water until tender
2. Remove the bones and cut the head into square pieces
3. Chop together a piece of veal meat and a piece of veal fat from the kidney with a piece of chopped onion and chopped lemon rind
4. Add one roll soaked in milk and squeezed, or two slices of white bread with crust removed
5. Chop everything together, remove sinews and rub the mixture in a bowl
6. Add three eggs mixed with butter, four anchovies cleaned and rubbed with butter, six egg yolks, and six tablespoons of sour cream
7. Season with salt and mix well
8. Grease a baking dish with fresh butter and spread half of the mixture on it
9. Arrange the cut calf's head on top
10. Pour lemon juice over it
11. Add the remaining half of the mixture on top
12. Pour melted fresh butter over it
13. Sprinkle with grated bread crumbs and finely chopped anchovies
14. Bake slowly in the oven and serve in the same dish
