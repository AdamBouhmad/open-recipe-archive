## Ingredients

- Smelts
- milk
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the smelts by removing the fins and thoroughly washing them
2. Dry them with a cloth
3. Dip in milk and coat in flour
4. Fry on hot butter
5. Alternatively, dust with flour, dip in beaten eggs mixed with salt and pepper, coat in grated bread crumbs, fry, and serve garnished with sliced lemon on patties
