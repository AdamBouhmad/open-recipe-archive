## Ingredients

- two cups sweet milk
- about one cup self-raising flour

## Directions

1. Take two cups of sweet milk and mix about one cup of flour into it to make a completely thin batter
2. If you do not have milk on hand, add two cups of cold water into which you mix a beaten egg
3. No other preparation or salt is needed
4. Bake as usual
