## Ingredients

- two cabbages
- 2 heads cauliflower
- beef soup
- handful mushrooms
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- liver and heart of 3 chickens
- roll
- milk
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbages and cauliflower in beef soup
2. When cooked, carefully remove them, cut off the cauliflower florets and chop the cabbage into strips
3. Slice the mushrooms, rinse them, add butter, fresh chopped parsley, pepper and stew
4. Chop the chicken livers and hearts that were boiled in beef soup
5. Slice the roll into patties, dip in milk, then flour, then egg
6. Mix the livers, hearts and mushrooms, place on a roll patty, top with another slice
7. Roll in grated roll and fry until golden
8. Mix with cauliflower and cabbage, place in a soup bowl and pour with good beef soup
