## Ingredients

- pale roux
- broth
- lemon peel
- two anchovies, cleaned
- white wine
- one tablespoon mustard
- a small teaspoon of crushed sugar
- pepper
- four beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a pale roux and add broth
2. Add lemon peel and two cleaned anchovies
3. Boil for a short time and strain
4. When cooled, add one glass of wine, one tablespoon of mustard, a small teaspoon of crushed sugar, and pepper
5. Add four beaten egg yolks
6. Place in boiling water and beat with a whisk until it froths
