## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Salt
- Caraway
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish thoroughly in fresh water.
2. Bring salted water to a boil, throw in the crayfish and cook quickly without much trouble in about a quarter hour.
3. Add caraway, salt, finely chopped parsley, and butter to the water.
4. Stir the crayfish frequently and let them remain in the pot a while after cooking.
5. Such prepared crayfish are very tasty.
