## Ingredients

- fresh calf's liver
- butter
- onion
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean fresh calf's liver and remove all skin, then slice it into thin slices
2. Melt a piece of butter on the stove
3. Add a spoonful of finely chopped onion and braise for about a minute
4. Then place the sliced liver in it, add a little ground pepper and braise the liver, constantly turning it with a spatula, until it is no longer bloody but also not too dry
5. Before serving, salt it
6. You can also sprinkle it with a little finely chopped lemon zest
