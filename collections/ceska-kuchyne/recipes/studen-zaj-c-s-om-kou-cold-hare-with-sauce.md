## Ingredients

- 2-3 whole onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Capers
- Cold hare, chopped

## Directions

1. Fry 2-3 whole onions in butter until browned
2. Remove the onions and make a roux with the butter in which they were fried and flour
3. Cook the roux with broth
4. Add lemon juice, salt, pepper and a spoonful of capers
5. Place the chopped cold hare in the sauce and heat it in the sauce
