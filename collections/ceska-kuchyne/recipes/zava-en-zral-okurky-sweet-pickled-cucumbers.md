## Ingredients

- well-ripened (yellow) cucumbers
- salt
- water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1 pint hot water
- half a quart of sharp vinegar
- 2 heaping small pots of white sugar
- 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon of cloves
- 1 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1 teaspoon of nutmeg or nutmeg seed
- 1 handful of raisins

## Directions

1. Take well-ripened (yellow) cucumbers, peel them, remove the seeds and also the soft parts where the seeds were, cut into neat pieces and place in salted water.
2. Then scald them with boiling water and let stand until morning, then remove them and measure out 1 quart of vinegar.
3. Into a pot put 1 pint of hot water, half a quart of sharp vinegar, 2 heaping small pots of white sugar, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, 1 teaspoon of cloves, 1 teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of nutmeg or nutmeg seed, 1 handful of raisins, then add the cucumbers to this and boil until they can be easily pierced with a fork; then pour the half quart of vinegar into it, after which immediately place them into jars.
