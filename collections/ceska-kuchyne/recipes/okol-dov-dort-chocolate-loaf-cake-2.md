## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup sweet milk
- 6 egg whites
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 0.25 square chocolate

## Directions

1. Make a dough with 1 cup butter, 2 cups sugar, 0.5 cup sweet milk, 6 egg whites, 3 cups flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
2. Divide the dough in half.
3. To the second half, grate 0.25 square of chocolate.
4. Alternate in the mold: 1 tablespoon white dough, then 1 tablespoon dark dough, continuing until the mold is full.
