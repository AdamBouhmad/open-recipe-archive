## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- woodcock livers
- woodcock hearts
- chicken livers
- butter
- bread
- egg yolks
- salt
- juniper berries

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcocks and cook them; boil the livers, hearts and several chicken livers.
2. Chop finely and mix with butter, grated bread and egg yolks.
3. Salt and add a few crushed juniper berries.
4. Stuff the woodcocks with this filling and sew them closed.
5. Slowly roast on both sides in butter.
