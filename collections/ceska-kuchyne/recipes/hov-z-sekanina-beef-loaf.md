## Ingredients

- 3 pounds ground beef
- 1/4 pound finely chopped lard
- 3 heaping tablespoons grated breadcrumbs
- 3 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- sage

## Directions

1. Take 3 pounds of ground beef, 1/4 pound finely chopped lard, three heaping tablespoons of grated breadcrumbs and three beaten eggs. Mix everything together well, salt, season with pepper and sage.
2. Place on a buttered baking sheet, sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs or bread, let bake for an hour and a half, basting frequently with melted butter. Can be served hot or cold.
