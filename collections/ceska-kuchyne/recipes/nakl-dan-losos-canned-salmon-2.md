## Ingredients

- canned salmon
- lemon
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Remove the lid from the can and carefully take the salmon out onto a dish so that the largest pieces remain as intact as possible and remove the bones
2. Be careful as the salmon in the can is cooked and therefore fragile
3. Arrange on a dish surrounded by lemon slices and serve with vinegar and spices
