## Ingredients

- fish meat scraps
- 2 bread rolls
- milk
- 4 eggs
- flower
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the fish meat scraps finely.
2. Soak 2 bread rolls in milk.
3. Mix on a baking sheet with 4 eggs until a thick paste forms.
4. When cooled, grind the meat with the bread rolls, flower and salt in a mortar.
5. Press through a sieve and fry the dumplings or small patties in butter.
