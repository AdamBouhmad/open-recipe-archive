## Ingredients

- one coffee cup of pearl barley
- a piece of fresh butter
- two quarts of hot beef broth
- six egg yolks
- a little lukewarm broth

## Directions

1. Cook one coffee cup of pearl barley. When it is soft, put it in a clean pot; add a piece of fresh butter and mix it well.
2. Then pour two quarts of hot beef broth over it, mix again, strain through a sieve and season with flowers.
3. In another pot, beat six egg yolks with a little lukewarm broth, pour this into the boiling broth, remove from heat and beat again and serve in coffee cups.
