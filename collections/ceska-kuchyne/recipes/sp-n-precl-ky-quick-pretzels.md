## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram butter
- 8 egg yolks
- salt
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- flour
- beaten egg for brushing
- sugar and vanilla for topping

## Directions

1. Cream 1/8 kilogram of butter with 8 egg yolks until fluffy
2. Season lightly with salt
3. Add 12 dekagrams of sugar and enough flour to form a firm dough
4. Fold six times in a cool place and rest 15 minutes, then fold again
5. Break off a piece each time, roll into a long shape, and make small pretzels
6. Place on a baking sheet, brush with beaten egg
7. When baked, sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
8. Store in a dry place
