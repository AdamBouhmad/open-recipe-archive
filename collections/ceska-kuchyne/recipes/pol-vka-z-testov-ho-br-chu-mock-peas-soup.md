## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons milk
- salt
- pepper
- lard or clarified butter

## Directions

1. Put two whole eggs into a pint pot, add two tablespoons flour, two tablespoons milk, salt lightly, add pepper and beat well until the dough is smooth
2. Heat lard or clarified butter in a pan and pour the dough through an inverted grater into it
3. When fried golden, remove with a spoon, put in a soup bowl and pour good meat broth over it
