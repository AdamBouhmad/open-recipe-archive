## Ingredients

- old hen
- beef broth
- parsley
- celery
- carrot

## Directions

1. Cook an old hen in beef broth until soft
2. Cut out the breasts and chop them finely
3. Pound the rest of the hen in a mortar
4. Put it in a pot and add chopped parsley, celery, and carrot
5. Pour in beef broth and let it cook for one hour
6. Pour it onto roll or pastry and the chopped breasts
