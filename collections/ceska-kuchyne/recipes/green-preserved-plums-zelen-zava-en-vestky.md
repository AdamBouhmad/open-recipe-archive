## Ingredients

- Beautiful, large green plums with soft pits
- Soft water
- Alum (small amount)
- Fresh water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, large green plums where the pit has not yet hardened
2. Pierce several times with a narrow knife
3. Cook in soft water to which a little alum has been added, until soft
4. Transfer to fresh water and let sit for two days
5. Pour off the water every six hours and replace with fresh water to remove all sourness from the plums
6. Place in a container and cover with strained sugar
7. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it again, skim the foam, and when cooled, pour over the preserves; repeat this for two days
8. On the fourth day, add more sugar and cook to the second degree, add the plums and preserve with it
9. On the fifth day, add even more sugar, cook to the sixth degree, cook the plums with it, skim the foam, and place the preserves in containers
