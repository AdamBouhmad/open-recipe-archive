## Ingredients

- capon
- 1 liter and 4 deciliters water
- 6 dekagrams butter
- parsley root
- piece of celery
- carrot
- salt
- clove flower
- shells and claws of cooked crayfish
- oysters or mussels cooked in wine, snails, or stuffed frogs

## Directions

1. Clean the capon and divide into 4 pieces
2. Place on a baking dish with 1 liter and 4 deciliters water, 6 dekagrams butter, parsley root, piece of celery, carrot, salt and clove flower
3. Braise until the capon is completely tender, then divide into smaller pieces
4. Make a pale butter roux, strain it into the sauce
5. Crush the shells and claws of cooked crayfish and strain through a sieve into the sauce
6. Simmer the sauce, place the capon in it for a moment, then arrange on a platter
7. Pour the sauce over it and arrange crayfish tails around it
8. Instead of crayfish tails, oysters, mussels cooked in wine, snails or stuffed frogs may be used
