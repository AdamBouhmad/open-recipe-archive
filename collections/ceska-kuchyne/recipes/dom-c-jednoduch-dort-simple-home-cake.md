## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram finest flour
- sufficient raw egg yolks
- abundant finely crushed and sifted sugar
- beaten egg white snow
- finely crushed cinnamon
- vanilla
- cloves
- finely chopped lemon and orange zest

## Directions

1. Vigorously mix and rub together the flour, egg yolks, and sifted sugar
2. Mix in the beaten egg white snow
3. Mix in the finely crushed cinnamon, vanilla, cloves, and finely chopped lemon and orange zest
4. Bake the dough slowly and carefully in buttered molds
