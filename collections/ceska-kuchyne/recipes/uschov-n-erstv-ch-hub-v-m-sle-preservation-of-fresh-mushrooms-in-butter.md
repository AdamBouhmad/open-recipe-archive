## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean mushrooms, let them dry on a sieve
2. Stew on hot butter until the absorbed butter flows out clear again
3. Salt well, shake, pour hot into stone jars
4. The next day pour melted butter over them and seal with cloth
