## Ingredients

- milk or buttermilk
- caraway
- dye

## Directions

1. Take milk collected or buttermilk; heat it to +30-40° R. and let it curdle.
2. Place the cheese in clean cloth, press it, mix with caraway, dye it, and place into molds, then either dry it completely or pickle it.
3. Slightly dried cheese is placed in vessels, covered with clean linen cloth soaked in beer.
4. Completely dry cheese is suitable for grating, pickled cheese then for eating.
