## Ingredients

- 14 decilitres (4 tablespoons) white wine
- sugar
- zest of six oranges
- a little milk
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) of isinglass, dissolved in water and strained

## Directions

1. Mix white wine with sugar rubbed with orange zest from six oranges and a little milk
2. Place in a warm location without cooking
3. Strain the mixture through a cloth
4. Mix with isinglass that has been dissolved in water and strained
5. Place on ice to set
