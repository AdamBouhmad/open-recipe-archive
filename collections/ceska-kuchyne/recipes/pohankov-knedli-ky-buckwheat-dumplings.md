## Ingredients

- half liter buckwheat flour
- one eighth liter boiling water
- bacon cut into cubes
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour half liter of buckwheat flour with one eighth liter of boiling water and mix well.
2. Fry diced bacon with the flour.
3. Form small dumplings from the dough and boil in salted water.
