## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 1/4 liter water
- 1/2 kilogram grated almonds

## Directions

1. Boil half kilogram of sugar in quarter liter of water
2. Add half kilogram of grated almonds
3. Stir over heat until they begin to crack
4. Remove from heat and stir until dry
5. Stir again over heat until they shine
6. Lay one beside another on a baking sheet to cool completely
