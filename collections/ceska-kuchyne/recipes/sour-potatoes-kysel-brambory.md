## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- onion
- beef broth
- vinegar
- lemon peel
- pepper
- salt
- cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Place a heaping spoonful of butter in a pot and add two spoons of flour and let it froth
2. Add a spoon of finely diced onion and make a brown roux
3. Pour in good beef broth to make a thick sauce
4. Add a little vinegar, a piece of finely diced lemon peel, a little ground pepper and salt and let it simmer
5. Peel cooked potatoes, cut them into slices and place in the pot
6. Pour the sauce over them and let them simmer a little longer
