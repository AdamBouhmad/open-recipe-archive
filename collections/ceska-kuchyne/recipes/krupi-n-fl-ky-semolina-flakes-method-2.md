## Ingredients

- semolina
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Fry semolina in butter
2. Salt it
3. Bake 1/2 hour in oven
4. Cut dough into flakes like noodles
5. Cook in salted water and drain
6. Add flakes to semolina with sugar and cinnamon
7. Bake
