## Ingredients

- calf meat
- boiling water
- butter
- flour
- onion
- celery
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- asparagus
- cauliflower
- bread
- butter
- flour for roux
- 60 grams butter
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the washed meat into small pieces, blanch in boiling water, drain and dry
2. Place in hot butter, sprinkle with flour and brown on both sides
3. Pour over with enough strong broth to cover the meat
4. Add onion, celery, parsley, salt, pepper and bay leaf, cover the pot and braise for about two hours
5. Cook asparagus cut into pieces and cauliflower in salted water, as well as bread and meat pieces
6. Make a roux from flour and 60 grams butter, simmer with strained broth from the meat, add lemon juice, nutmeg and several egg yolks
7. Cook the asparagus, cauliflower, bread and meat together with this sauce, season and serve
