## Ingredients

- 12 fresh egg yolks
- 1.5 liters wine
- 190 grams (16 lots) sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat 12 fresh egg yolks, 1.5 liters wine, 190 grams sugar and cinnamon over fire
2. Serve in glasses on the table
