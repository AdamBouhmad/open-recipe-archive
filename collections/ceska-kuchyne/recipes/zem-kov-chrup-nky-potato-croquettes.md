## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- salt
- seasoning
- melted butter
- bread crumbs (grated)
- butter for frying
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mash the cooked peeled potatoes
2. Press through a sieve
3. Mix with salt, seasoning, and melted butter
4. When cooled, form into small elongated croquettes
5. Coat with grated bread crumbs
6. Fry in butter
7. Arrange with parsley and serve
