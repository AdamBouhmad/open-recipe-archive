## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) almonds
- 3 whole eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- a plate of grated cooked potatoes
- flower water

## Directions

1. Beat together 24 dekagrams butter, 12 dekagrams almonds, 3 whole eggs, 10 egg yolks, 12 dekagrams sugar and a plate of grated cooked potatoes
2. Add flower water
3. Bake the soufflé in a mold
