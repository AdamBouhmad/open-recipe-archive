## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams flour
- 12 decagrams butter
- 700 ml good milk
- 4 whole eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- snow from 8 egg whites
- salt
- sugar
- lemon zest
- stewed cherries

## Directions

1. Let 24 decagrams of flour and 12 decagrams of butter foam on a baking sheet
2. Pour in 700 ml of good milk and stir until a thick paste forms
3. Let it cool
4. Rub it together with 4 whole eggs, 8 egg yolks, snow from 8 egg whites, salt, sugar, and lemon zest
5. Place half the paste in a mold
6. Place stewed cherries in the middle
7. Finally add the remaining paste
8. When baked, sprinkle with sugar
