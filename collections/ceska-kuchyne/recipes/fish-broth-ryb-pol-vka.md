## Ingredients

- Several small fish
- Butter
- Spices
- Vegetables
- Egg yolks
- Fried bread croutons
- Cooked, roasted or stewed fish

## Directions

1. If using fish for the table, place in cold water to make better broth (though the fish will taste less good)
2. Better to use several smaller fish so as not to waste a large fish
3. Diligently skim off the foam
4. After the fish has simmered for a quarter hour, strain the broth
5. Boil briefly with sardine butter, spices, and vegetables
6. Add several egg yolks and fried bread croutons
7. For richer broth, press cooked, roasted or stewed fish through a sieve into the broth
