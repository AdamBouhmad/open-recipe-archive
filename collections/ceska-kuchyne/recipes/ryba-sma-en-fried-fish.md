## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Flour
- Egg
- Grated bread roll
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, rinse it, dry it with a clean cloth.
2. Salt it, coat with flour, dip in egg, coat with grated bread roll.
3. Slowly fry it golden brown in butter.
4. All types of fish can be prepared this way. Small fish are fried whole, larger ones are cut into equal pieces.
