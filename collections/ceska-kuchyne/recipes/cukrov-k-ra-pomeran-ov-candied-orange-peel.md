## Ingredients

- sugar
- orange peel, cleaned and preserved

## Directions

1. Cook out the cleaned, melted sugar.
2. Add the preserved cleaned orange peel to it.
3. Stir everything frequently and boil again until everything cooks down well and thickens.
4. Remove the orange peels, place them on a sieve and let them dry.
