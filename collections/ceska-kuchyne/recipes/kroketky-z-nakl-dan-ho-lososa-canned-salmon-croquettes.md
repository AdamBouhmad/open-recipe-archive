## Ingredients

- canned salmon
- two eggs
- four tablespoons grated bread
- pepper
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the salmon from the can onto a dish, remove the small bones, beat in two eggs and four tablespoons of grated bread, pepper and mix well.
2. Place a piece of lard and a piece of butter on a pan and when hot, take spoonfuls of pieces the size of an oyster and fry on both sides until golden.
