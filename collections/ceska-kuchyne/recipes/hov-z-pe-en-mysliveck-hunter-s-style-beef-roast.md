## Ingredients

- aged beef from ribs
- eggs (mixed)
- choice cured meats (sliced)
- chives
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- beef broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove bones and skin from aged rib meat and pound
2. Mix together eggs, sliced choice cured meats, chives, pepper and salt
3. Spread mixture on the meat and roll it up
4. Tie with string
5. Roast on butter in the oven
6. Simmer in beef broth and lemon juice until cooked
