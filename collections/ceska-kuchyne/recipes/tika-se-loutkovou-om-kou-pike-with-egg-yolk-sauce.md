## Ingredients

- medium pike
- water
- vinegar
- vegetable roots
- salt
- onion
- bay leaves
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks
- mushrooms
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, roll it and cook gently in water with vinegar, vegetable roots, salt, onion, bay leaves and pepper
2. Place on a dish and cover
3. Prepare the sauce from 75 grams of hot butter, a spoonful of flour, broth mixed with 8 egg yolks
4. Add braised mushrooms and juice from half a lemon
5. Bring to a boil and pour over the pike
