## Ingredients

- cleaned crayfish
- salted water
- good oil

## Directions

1. Place cleaned crayfish in boiling, well-salted water
2. Cook for ten to twelve minutes
3. Remove from water
4. Coat with good oil
5. Arrange tastefully on a plate and serve
