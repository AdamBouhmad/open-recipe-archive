## Ingredients

- Apples, peeled and sliced
- Water - a small amount
- Sugar - finely crushed
- Sugar - equal measure to pulp

## Directions

1. Peel and slice apples into rounds
2. Cook in a small amount of water
3. Press through a fine sieve
4. Add finely crushed sugar
5. Cook strongly while constantly stirring
6. Allow to cool
7. Store in clean stone containers
8. Cover well with bladder
9. Keep in a cold, clean place
