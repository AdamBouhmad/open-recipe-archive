## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter milk
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- millet
- 10 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon zest
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil 3/4 liter of milk with 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of butter
2. When it cools, add millet, stir it until it does not stick to the baking sheet, and pour it onto a platter so the porridge cools
3. Rub butter with 10 egg yolks and sugar with grated lemon zest, with snow from egg whites bake the pudding for 3/4 hour
