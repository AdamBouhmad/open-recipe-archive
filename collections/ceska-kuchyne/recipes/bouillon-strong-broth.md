## Ingredients

- three pounds beef
- one pound veal
- half old hen or old pigeon
- one celery root
- two parsley roots
- one carrot
- one leek
- one onion
- slice of garlic
- half cabbage
- piece of ginger
- one bay leaf
- four quarts cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean everything well.
2. Put beef and veal in a pot with four quarts cold water, let simmer slowly and skim the foam.
3. When the soup is skimmed of foam, salt it, add vegetables and spices and let cook for two hours.
4. Then remove the veal, add the hen or pigeon, and let cook for another three hours.
5. Carefully skim the fat and finally strain the soup through a damp cloth or fine sieve.
6. Serves 12 persons.
