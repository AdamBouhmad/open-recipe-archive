## Ingredients

- selected truffles
- butter
- onion, finely chopped
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- madeira
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and peel the selected truffles
2. Slice into thin, small rounds
3. Place in a baking dish with butter
4. Add finely chopped onion, parsley, and pepper
5. Season with salt
6. Braise gently over moderate heat for about ten minutes
7. Add a little madeira
8. Add lemon juice
9. Season with salt and pepper
10. Serve hot with the sauce
11. Sprinkle with fried, finely broken bread roll
