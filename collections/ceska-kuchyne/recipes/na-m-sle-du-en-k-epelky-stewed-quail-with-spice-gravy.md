## Ingredients

- cleaned and prepared quail
- salt
- bacon sliced into strips
- butter
- small onion
- small carrot
- bay leaf
- cloves
- peppercorns
- meat juices
- white wine
- string

## Directions

1. Salt the cleaned quail, wrap them with bacon strips and tie with string
2. Butter a pan, arrange the quail in it
3. Add one small onion sliced into rounds, a small carrot, one bay leaf, two cloves, several peppercorns, some meat juices and a small glass of white wine
4. Cover well and let braise gently for a quarter hour over moderate heat
5. Remove the quail, press the sauce through a sieve, coat the quail with it
6. Arrange them in a circle either on braised green peas, braised cabbage or regular cabbage
