## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- leek
- butter
- white flour
- broth
- vinegar
- wine
- pepper
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, remove entrails, scrape it well, clean and salt it inside, cover with cloth and let it lie for two hours.
2. Wipe it with cloth and make several cuts across both sides.
3. Place one large onion, one carrot, half celery, one leek all sliced thinly into a pot, add a piece of butter and let it fry to golden, then add two tablespoons of white flour, let it stew for another minute.
4. Pour a pint of broth, a coffee cup of vinegar, a glass of wine on it, add several grains of pepper, one bay leaf and let it cook well for half an hour.
5. Grease a baking pan well with butter, place a piece of cut celery and parsley in it, lay the pike on it and bake in the oven, frequently brushing with butter.
6. When the pike is baked, carefully remove it, cut into neat pieces and arrange on a dish.
7. Push the prepared sauce through a sieve into the baking pan, let it cook for another minute in the oven and then strain through a sieve over the cut pike.
