## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- new spice
- onion
- pepper
- lemon peel
- celery
- parsley
- carrot
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pale roux
- sour cream
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse a marinated venison shoulder, add new spice, finely chopped onion, pepper, lemon peel, celery, parsley, carrot, equal parts vinegar and water, and salt
2. Roast in an oven until the meat becomes completely tender
3. Transfer the meat to another dish
4. Add pale roux to the sauce to thicken it well, add 3.5 deciliters (1 ladle) of sour cream and a piece of white sugar
5. During roasting, baste the meat and turn it
6. After a quarter hour, slice it and pour the sauce over it
