## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram beef
- kidney fat
- fried onion
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 kilogram cooked cold potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop beef with kidney fat, fried onion, and cooked potatoes
2. Mix with eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Form meatballs, coat with breadcrumbs
4. Fry in butter
