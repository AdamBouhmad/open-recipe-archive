## Ingredients

- veal mesentery
- butter or lard
- bread rolls or bread
- eggs
- green parsley or chives
- leek
- thyme
- lemon zest
- pepper
- caul fat

## Directions

1. Boil cleaned mesentery and chop it finely
2. To the chopped meat add a piece of butter or lard, 2 grated bread rolls or 4 slices of bread, 3 eggs, a little chopped green parsley or chives, or just a piece of leek, a little thyme, a piece of finely chopped lemon zest and a pinch of pepper
3. Mix everything well
4. Grease a casserole with butter or lard
5. Sprinkle with grated bread roll
6. Put the mixture in it
7. Cover with caul fat which you keep whole for this purpose and let it bake
