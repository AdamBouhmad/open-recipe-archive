## Ingredients

- not quite one cup of wine vinegar
- one bay leaf
- a sprig of thyme
- three shallots or one small onion
- a pinch of salt and pepper
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Boil not quite one cup of wine vinegar with one bay leaf, a sprig of thyme, three shallots or one small onion, a pinch of salt and pepper down to half
2. Pour beef broth over this
3. Allow to simmer about half an hour
4. Strain it
5. Make a roux, sweeten with a piece of sugar
6. Allow to simmer again and pour into a sauce boat
