## Ingredients

- Cooked cauliflower
- Flour or beaten egg
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter
- Lard

## Directions

1. Prepare cooked cauliflower by slicing it into cutlets
2. Coat in flour or beaten egg
3. Coat in breadcrumbs
4. Fry in equal parts butter and lard until golden brown
