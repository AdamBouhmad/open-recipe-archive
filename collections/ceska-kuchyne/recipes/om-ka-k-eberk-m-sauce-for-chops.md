## Ingredients

- onion
- garlic
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- rose roux
- broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop onion, garlic, bay leaf, and marjoram
2. Stew briefly in rose roux
3. Cook with broth and season with lemon juice
4. This sauce is excellent with veal chops
