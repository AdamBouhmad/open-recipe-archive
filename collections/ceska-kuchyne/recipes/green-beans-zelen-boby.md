## Ingredients

- green young beans
- alum (a small amount added to soft water)
- sugar (cooked)
- water

## Directions

1. Take green young beans
2. Clean and cut into halves (if thick) or into pieces
3. Wash in soft water with a small amount of alum added
4. Crush with fingers until completely softened
5. Remove and place in fresh water
6. Place in a dish
7. Pour with diluted cooked sugar
8. For three days, always strain off the sugar, boil it, and pour it back over the beans when cooled
9. On the fourth day, boil the sugar to the second stage and cook the beans in it
10. On the fifth day, strain the sugar again and boil it to the fourth stage, add the beans to it and boil together
11. Skim the foam
12. When the preserve has cooled, place in containers
