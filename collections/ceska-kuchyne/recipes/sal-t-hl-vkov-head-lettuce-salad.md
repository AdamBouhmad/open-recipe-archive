## Ingredients

- head lettuce
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- Provençal oil
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove outer leaves and cut into quarters
2. Rinse thoroughly
3. Rub three hard-boiled egg yolks
4. Pour Provençal oil and wine vinegar onto the yolks and mix well
5. Add the lettuce and mix
6. Arrange on a plate
7. Surround with sliced hard-boiled eggs
