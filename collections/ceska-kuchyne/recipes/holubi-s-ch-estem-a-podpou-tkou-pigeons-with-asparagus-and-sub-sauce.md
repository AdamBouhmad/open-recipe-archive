## Ingredients

- pigeons
- butter
- raw ham
- onion
- asparagus
- good broth or cooking water
- salt
- asparagus tips
- roux
- nutmeg
- egg yolks
- bread
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Quarter the pigeons and fry in butter
2. In a small baking dish place raw ham cut into cubes, onion and thick hard ends of asparagus, braise everything for a few minutes in butter
3. Pour good broth or cooking water over, salt and simmer covered slowly for an hour with the pigeons
4. Remove the pigeons, keep warm over hot water
5. Cook the asparagus cut into pieces in the broth
6. Thicken the sauce with roux, season with nutmeg, beat in two egg yolks and pour over the pigeons, add cooked asparagus, bread cut into cubes and fried
7. In addition to asparagus, cauliflower may also be used
