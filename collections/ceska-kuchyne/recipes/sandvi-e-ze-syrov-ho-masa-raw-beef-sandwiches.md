## Ingredients

- prime tender beef meat
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Finely chop a piece of prime tender beef meat with onion
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Spread on one bread slice
4. Cover with another bread slice
5. Press together slightly and serve
6. Onion may be omitted if desired
7. Butter and mustard are not used with these sandwiches
