## Ingredients

- fresh butter or lard
- flour
- garlic cloves
- butter
- marjoram leaves
- beef or mutton broth
- cooked potatoes, sliced
- salt
- white grated ginger
- spices

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of fresh butter or lard in a pot, add two spoonfuls of flour
2. Add a clove of garlic with a piece of butter mashed together and make a brown roux
3. Add a little picked marjoram leaves
4. Pour in beef or mutton broth (the latter gives this gravy a special flavor)
5. Mix well so that a thickened gravy forms
6. Add cooked sliced potatoes to it, salt it, season with a little white grated ginger and spice
7. Let it simmer a while longer
