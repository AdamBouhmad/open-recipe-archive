## Ingredients

- 3.5 deciliters good sour cream
- 30 grams fresh butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs
- Ham, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Crumble 30 grams fresh butter into 3.5 deciliters sour cream seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Pour onto a baking sheet.
3. When the butter is melted, crack enough eggs onto it to cover the entire surface.
4. Sprinkle the entire surface with finely chopped ham.
5. Bake in the oven until the eggs are cooked.
6. Cut around the edges with a knife and carefully transfer to a plate so the top eggs remain undamaged.
