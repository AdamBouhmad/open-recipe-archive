## Ingredients

- 3 liters good spirits (kořalka)
- 180 grams crushed caraway seeds
- 12 grams star anise

## Directions

1. Mix 3 liters of good spirits with 180 grams of crushed caraway seeds and 12 grams of star anise
2. Pour into a bottle and tie with a bladder
3. Place the bottle in a pot filled with water
4. Heat the water until it boils, then remove and let cool completely
5. Sweeten the liqueur with sugar, strain, and pour into clean bottles
6. Seal bottles firmly
