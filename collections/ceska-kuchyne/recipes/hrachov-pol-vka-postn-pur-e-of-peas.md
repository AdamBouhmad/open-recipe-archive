## Ingredients

- peas
- parsley water
- butter
- egg yolk
- small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- flowers
- marjoram
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook peas until soft and mix them
2. Pour parsley water on them and let cook a little longer
3. Strain the soup and make a roux with butter to which you have added a finely chopped small onion
4. You may also add about 2 garlic cloves well rubbed, as well as a little flowers and, if desired, a little marjoram, which however may be omitted
5. Pour the soup over fried bread
