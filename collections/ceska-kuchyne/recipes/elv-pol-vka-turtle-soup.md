## Ingredients

- turtle
- butter
- onion
- carrot
- parsley roots
- meat broth
- wine
- pepper
- eggs or bread

## Directions

1. Clean the killed turtle in lukewarm water
2. Simmer slowly for at least three hours
3. Strain and salt
4. Melt butter in a baking pan and sauté onion, carrot, and parsley roots
5. Place the turtle on top of everything
6. Pour meat broth over it
7. Braise, repeatedly pouring broth over it and allowing it to simmer further
8. Cut the cooked turtle into pieces
9. Salt it, pour wine over it, and pepper it
10. Add eggs or bread to the soup thus prepared
