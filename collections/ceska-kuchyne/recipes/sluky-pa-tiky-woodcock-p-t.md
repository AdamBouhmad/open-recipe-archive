## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Butter
- Veal meat
- Ham
- Bread roll
- Eggs
- Sour cream
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Pâté mold
- Mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck and cook the woodcock, remove neck and breast
2. Roast legs, head and wings in butter and pound them as finely as possible
3. Braise the woodcock in butter for a quarter hour until completely soft
4. Prepare a pâté mold, grease it with butter and line it with fine minced veal, ham, bread roll and eggs
5. Pour good sour cream over this, salt, pepper and spice it, and brown in butter
6. Place the braised woodcock tightly on this base, coat with butter
7. Work the pounded meat in with butter, cover the top with butter dough
8. Bake together for at least an hour and a half
9. Serve with mushroom sauce
