## Ingredients

- coffee beans, coarsely ground
- water
- egg white
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. For five cups of coffee, usually take three heaping tablespoons of coarsely ground coffee
2. Pour it into boiling water in a pot, let it come to a boil several times, remove from heat and let it settle
3. To clarify, pour a little cold water into it after boiling
4. Alternatively, mix a piece of egg white with a little water into the ground coffee, or add it to the water right after pouring in the ground coffee
5. When it settles, pour it into a coffee pot
6. Many also pour it from the pot in which it was boiled, of course through a strainer
7. It is not good to keep the sediment in the pot and reheat for more than a day or two if you want good coffee
8. The best coffee is achieved by steeping in the French way, but it requires more coffee
9. At ordinary occasions, milk is usually used in coffee, but if we want truly good coffee, we must use cream
10. In Bohemia milk or cream is boiled, here raw is used
11. Sweeten the coffee according to taste
