## Ingredients

- half liter milk
- 195 grams (13 lots) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- vanilla sliced
- potato starch
- sour cream
- salt
- 10 egg yolks
- egg whites for snow
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Cook half liter milk with 195 grams butter, 12 dekagrams sugar and sliced vanilla
2. Add potato starch equal in amount to the butter, beaten with sour cream and cook until a thick porridge forms
3. While porridge cools, remove vanilla and stir the porridge again for a while, salt it, add 10 egg yolks and snow from egg whites
4. Place the mixture in a mold in hot water and boil for 1 1/2 hours
5. Serve with wine sauce
