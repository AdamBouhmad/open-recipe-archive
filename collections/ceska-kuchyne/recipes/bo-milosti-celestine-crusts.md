## Ingredients

- three eggs
- three teaspoons sugar
- one tablespoon butter
- one quarter coffeepot sweet milk
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat together the eggs, sugar, butter, and sweet milk, seasoning with salt
2. Add flour to make a dough not as stiff as noodle dough
3. Knead well, roll out thin
4. Cut into four-sided pieces and prick with a fork
5. Fry in lard until pink-golden in color
