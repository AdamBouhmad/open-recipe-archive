## Ingredients

- Dough (as for ordinary cakes)
- Butter
- Semolina mixed with butter and chilled for several hours
- Sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Roll out dough to make a cake, make an edge, prick it, let it rise for a while in a warm place
2. Brush with butter and with semolina that has been mixed with butter and stood in the cold for several hours
3. Sprinkle thickly with sugar and cinnamon and bake
