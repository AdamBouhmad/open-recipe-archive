## Ingredients

- beautiful small pear-shaped unripe yellow tomatoes
- sugar
- lemon rind
- ginger

## Directions

1. In the evening, pour boiling water over and peel beautiful small pear-shaped unripe yellow tomatoes, prick each one with a needle so it does not crack during boiling and add as much sugar as the tomatoes weigh.
2. In the morning pour off all the juice from them, boil until it becomes thick like a syrup.
3. Then add the tomatoes to boil in it and add a piece of lemon rind and whole ginger and let boil until the tomatoes look transparent, then place into jars and screw shut while hot.
