## Ingredients

- dried mushrooms
- water
- grains
- butter
- green parsley
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Let two handfuls of dried mushrooms soak in water
2. Meanwhile sort and clean a quarter pint of torn grains, then let them cook with a piece of butter and the proper amount of water
3. Then pour off the black water from the mushrooms, clean them once more
4. Then pour a little clean water on them, add a little chopped green parsley, salt them and let them also cook
5. Then pour the water from them onto the grains, chop the mushrooms finely, add them also to the grains and add enough water so that the soup is neither thin nor too thick
6. Fry it a bit with white thin roux and let it cook a bit more
