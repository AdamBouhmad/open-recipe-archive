## Ingredients

- well-cleaned squabs
- butter
- salt
- flour
- beef broth
- young green peas
- sugar
- clove

## Directions

1. Cut the well-cleaned squabs into quarters
2. Place them in a pot on a piece of butter, salt them and let them braise for half an hour
3. Dust them with two spoonfuls of flour and mix with them
4. After a while, pour two bowls of beef broth and make a smooth sauce
5. Then remove the squabs and strain the sauce
6. Add a quart of young green peas to it, a piece of sugar and a little clove
7. Let it braise for a while, then put the squabs back in it and let everything braise until tender
8. Arrange the squabs in the middle of a dish and attractively surround them with peas
