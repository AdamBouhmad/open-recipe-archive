## Ingredients

- 1 piece of fresh yeast (about 2 ounces)
- 1 cup milk
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- salt
- flour to make a firm dough like for a roll

## Directions

1. Let a piece of fresh yeast soak in 1 cup of milk and make a thin batter and let it rise.
2. When the batter is risen, beat 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks and add to the batter.
3. Then rub ½ cup of butter with 2 tablespoons of sugar, add to the batter, mix everything well with a fork and add a little salt.
4. Add enough flour to make a firm dough like for a roll, roll it out, put it in a warm place to rise, then roll it again and make rolls this way: Roll out the dough to finger thickness, cut it into small pieces with a small glass or round cutter, roll each piece with a rolling pin into a long shape about 6 or 8 inches long and then roll it up.
5. Hold one end, twist the other end and roll it with your hand, then press the end down with tongs.
6. When placing on a baking sheet, bend each into a roll shape, grease with warmed butter and let rise well.
7. When well risen, brush the top with beaten egg and put into a nicely warm oven to bake nicely.
