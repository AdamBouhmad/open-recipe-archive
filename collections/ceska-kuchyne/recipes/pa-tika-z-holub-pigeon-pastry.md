## Ingredients

- Pastry dough
- Squabs
- Good broth
- Butter
- Spices
- Onion
- Lemon
- Squab gizzards and lungs
- Cooked milk
- Mushrooms
- Crayfish
- Salted water
- Good meat broth
- Champagne wine
- Butter
- Capers
- Egg yolks
- Flour

## Directions

1. Prepare pastry from the previously described dough
2. The day before preparation, select squabs, pluck them, clean them and cut into quarters, wash them
3. Place with some good broth, butter, spices on onion and lemon on a baking sheet and let braise
4. Let the braised squabs cool
5. Chop the uncooked gizzards and lungs, mix with cooked milk and chopped mushrooms
6. Cook crayfish in salted water, shell them and chop the meat
7. Place this mixture on the bottom of the pastry and layer everything together, cover with good meat broth
8. Cover the top with a dough lid and bake for at least an hour over mild heat
9. Pour a sauce made from pigeon broth, champagne wine, a little butter and capers mixed with egg yolks, cooked and sprinkled with flour over the baked pastry
