## Ingredients

- peeled peaches
- sugar
- rum (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and pit from peaches
2. Cut into quarters or smaller pieces
3. Sprinkle with sugar
4. Sprinkle with rum if desired
5. Make a pie in the same manner as apple pie
6. Plum pie and apricot pie are prepared the same way, except that peeled plums and apricots are only halved and pitted
