## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- one cup of cream
- six teaspoons of fine flour
- two quarts of boiling beef broth

## Directions

1. Beat six egg yolks in a cup with one cup of cream and six teaspoons of fine flour
2. Pour this with constant stirring into two quarts of boiling beef broth
3. You can season it as you like
