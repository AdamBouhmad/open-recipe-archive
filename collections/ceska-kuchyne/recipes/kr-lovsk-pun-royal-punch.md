## Ingredients

- 0.75 kilograms sugar
- Tea water
- Juice of 6 lemons
- Burgundy wine
- Arrack
- Good Rhine wine
- 1 bottle champagne wine

## Directions

1. Chop 0.75 kilograms of sugar into pieces
2. Dissolve in tea water
3. Strain everything with cloth
4. Add juice from 6 lemons and mix
5. Add some Burgundy wine, arrack, good Rhine wine, and 1 bottle of champagne wine
6. Mix everything and serve cold on the table
