## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- white flour
- boiling beef broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Rub a piece of fresh butter well with a little white flour
2. Pour boiling beef broth over it and mix well
3. Add two egg yolks
4. Squeeze the juice from one lemon into it and add finely chopped lemon zest
5. Stir until it thickens, but it must not boil
