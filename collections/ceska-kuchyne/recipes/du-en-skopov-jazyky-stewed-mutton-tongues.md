## Ingredients

- mutton tongues
- lukewarm water
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let the mutton tongues lie in lukewarm water for an hour, then cook them for a quarter hour, and let them cool in cold water.
2. With a sharp knife, remove the skin, cut each tongue in half lengthwise, and season with salt.
3. Put a piece of butter in a pan and line the bottom with about an inch of thinly sliced onion, arrange the halved tongues on it, sprinkle them with ground pepper, place pieces of butter on top, cover the pan well, and let the tongues stew slowly until tender.
4. You must ensure that the onion does not burn; the onion must be as golden and completely tender as the tongues.
5. When serving, arrange the tongues on a dish with the onion in an attractive pyramid and squeeze lemon juice over them.
