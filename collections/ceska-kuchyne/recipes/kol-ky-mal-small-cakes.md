## Ingredients

- dough (as in preceding recipe)
- plum jam
- almonds
- egg
- gingerbread
- cheese curds
- poppy seed

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in the preceding recipe.
2. Roll out evenly and cut cakes with a form.
3. Fill the center with either thinned plum jam and almonds, or roll out a piece of dough into four sides, fill the center with plum jam, gingerbread, cheese curds or poppy seed, fold the corners, brush with egg and sprinkle with chopped almonds.
