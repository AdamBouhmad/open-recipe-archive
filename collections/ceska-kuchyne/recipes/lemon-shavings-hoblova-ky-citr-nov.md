## Ingredients

- finely ground sugar
- lemon juice
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a thick icing from finely ground sugar, lemon juice, and egg whites
2. Spread onto wafers cut into oblong strips
3. Bake slowly
4. Cool on a sieve
