## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) sugar
- cinnamon
- 12 eggs mixed with flour and butter

## Directions

1. Boil 1 liter of milk with 6 decagrams sugar and cinnamon
2. When it begins to boil, mix in 12 eggs beaten with flour and butter
3. Stir the mixture until it thickens
4. Drop small spoonfuls onto a dish and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
