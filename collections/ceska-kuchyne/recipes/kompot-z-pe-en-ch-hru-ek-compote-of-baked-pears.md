## Ingredients

- nice and tasty pears
- cinnamon
- sugar
- sugared syrup

## Directions

1. Carefully bake nice and tasty pears on a baking sheet
2. Remove the skin from them
3. Boil in dissolved sugar, cooking with it several times
4. Sugar and cinnamon may be added
5. Place the cooked pears on a dish and pour sugared syrup over
