## Ingredients

- cleaned carrots
- salted water
- wine vinegar
- oil
- cooked prunes

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned carrots in salted water and let them cool
2. Slice the carrots into rounds
3. Pour wine vinegar and oil over them
4. Arrange cooked prunes around the salad
