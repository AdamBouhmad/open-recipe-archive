## Ingredients

- aspic
- egg white snow
- cold water

## Directions

1. From settled aspic, remove the fat accumulated on top and the sediment from the bottom
2. Select the clean aspic, put in a pot, heat to melt, add snow from egg whites
3. Whisk everything until it begins to set on one side, stir the aspic and clean it
4. Add a little cold water, set aside and let settle
5. Strain the aspic through heated cloth several times until completely clean
6. Place the cleaned aspic in a cool place or with ice until it sets solid enough to cut
7. The cleaned aspic is transparent, can be put in glasses and stored for long as a garnish for dishes
