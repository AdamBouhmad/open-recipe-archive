## Ingredients

- pork leg
- rice
- onion
- butter
- salt
- beef broth

## Directions

1. From pork leg, cut across about as thick as a finger, slices, beat them well and salt them.
2. Shred several onions and let them foam in butter, then spread them on the prepared slices.
3. Place on each a full tablespoon of well-rinsed rice, roll them up, wrap each with string, arrange them in a buttered roasting pan and let them brown somewhat on high heat.
4. Then add beef broth to them so that the rice can soften and let them then stew until tender.
