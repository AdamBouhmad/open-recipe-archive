## Ingredients

- One liter strawberries
- Sugar
- One liter sweet cream

## Directions

1. Press one liter strawberries through sieve
2. Add sugar and one liter sweet cream
3. Place in ice cream machine
4. Or crush 375 grams sugar and 16 egg yolks until foamy with one liter sweet cream over moderate heat
5. Strain it
6. Place in ice cream machine with one liter strained strawberries
