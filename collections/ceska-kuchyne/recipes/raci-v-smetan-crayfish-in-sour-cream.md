## Ingredients

- sour cream
- parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- cumin
- cleaned crayfish
- white wine or vinegar
- good meat broth

## Directions

1. Add finely chopped parsley, browned in butter, to sour cream
2. Season with salt and cumin
3. Add cleaned crayfish
4. When the sour cream comes to a boil, add either white wine or vinegar
5. After cooking, pour good meat broth over the crayfish and serve
