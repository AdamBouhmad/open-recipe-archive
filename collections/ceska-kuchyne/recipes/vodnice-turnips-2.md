## Ingredients

- peeled turnips
- fresh butter
- sugar
- mutton broth
- white grated ginger
- allspice

## Directions

1. Slice peeled turnips into rounds and rinse in cold water
2. Place a heaping spoonful of fresh butter and a teaspoon of sugar on a pan and let it brown to golden
3. Add the turnips and braise them, basting with mutton broth and stirring frequently and carefully
4. Make a brown roux, mix it with mutton broth, pour it over the turnips, season with white grated ginger and a bit of allspice, and braise until tender
