## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- two cups fine farina
- salt
- sugar and cinnamon
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Pour one quart of milk into a pot and when it begins to boil, gradually sprinkle two cups of fine farina into it with constant stirring
2. Move it to the side where it will not boil vigorously and let it cook 10 to 15 minutes, stirring frequently
3. Then salt it, mix and pour onto a platter, sprinkle with sugar mixed with ground cinnamon and grease with melted butter
