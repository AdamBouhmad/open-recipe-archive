## Ingredients

- truffles, carefully cleaned and peeled
- Madeira wine
- salt
- pepper
- good oil
- parsley, finely chopped
- garlic
- Siberian wormwood
- mayonnaise sauce
- mustard

## Directions

1. Peel truffles carefully and place on a shallow baking sheet
2. Season with salt and drizzle with a little Madeira wine
3. Braise gently for about three to four minutes
4. Cut into very fine thin slices
5. Season slices with salt and pepper
6. Drizzle with good oil
7. Let rest in a cool place for at least ten minutes
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley, garlic and Siberian wormwood
9. Mix in mayonnaise sauce and add mustard
