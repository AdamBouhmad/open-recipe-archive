## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) milk
- piece of sugar
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- piece of chopped lemon peel
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix milk, sugar, butter, whole eggs, egg yolks, salt, lemon peel and enough flour to make a thick batter.
2. Grease an omelet pan with butter, pour in the batter, quickly fry on both sides and place on a plate.
3. When all omelets are ready, spread with plum jam or sprinkle with chopped cooked meat, fried roll, raisins, almonds, sliced apples or cheese curds.
4. Place on a sheet pan, pour with milk and beaten eggs, and dry in the oven.
