## Ingredients

- hard rolls, buns, or white bread
- hot milk
- fresh butter
- poppy seed
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls, buns, or white bread into cubes.
2. Place in a clay dish and pour over with hot milk; one cup of milk is sufficient for half a gallon of bread.
3. Cover with a metal plate and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour.
4. Remove, shake it together, turn out onto another dish, pour over with heated fresh butter, and sprinkle with poppy seed mixed with sugar.
