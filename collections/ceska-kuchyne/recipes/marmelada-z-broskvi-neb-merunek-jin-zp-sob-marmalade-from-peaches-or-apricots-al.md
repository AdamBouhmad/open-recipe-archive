## Ingredients

- Well-ripened peaches
- Sugar - crushed, equal measure to the pulp

## Directions

1. Select well-ripened peaches
2. Remove pits from them
3. Press the pulp through a fine sieve
4. Add crushed sugar in the same measure as the pulp
5. Mix together
6. Boil several times
7. Store in containers
