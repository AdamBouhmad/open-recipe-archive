## Ingredients

- ripe currant berries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Press ripe currant berries through a fine sieve.
2. Add sufficient crushed sugar to the strained juice.
3. Cook with it until thickened, place in small containers, and when cooled, wrap with paper and bladder.
