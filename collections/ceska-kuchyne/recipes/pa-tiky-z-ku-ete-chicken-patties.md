## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken, young hen or capon
- 1/8 kilo (1/4 pound) smoked meat
- half that amount of lard
- new spices
- a small onion, finely chopped
- a pinch of marjoram
- cloves
- 2 cloves of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- cooking twine
- salted water
- aspic or horseradish

## Directions

1. Skin a large chicken, young hen or capon; cut along the neck and pull out the meat, handling the wings the same way, then sew the skin on the wings and legs.
2. Finely chop the meat, and for one chicken take 1/8 kilo (1/4 pound) smoked meat, half that amount of lard; cut both into small cubes.
3. Salt and pepper it, add new spices, finely chopped small onion, a pinch of marjoram, cloves, two cloves of garlic; mix everything together.
4. Mix well with the raw meat, smoked meat and lard, fill the skin with this mixture through the neck, sew it closed, wrap it in cooking twine and boil for half an hour in salted water.
5. Press it, let it cool, remove the twine, slice the cooled mixture into thin pieces and serve aspic or horseradish around it.
