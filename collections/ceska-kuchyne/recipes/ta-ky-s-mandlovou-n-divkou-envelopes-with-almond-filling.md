## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams ground almonds
- 12 dekagrams sugar
- grated lemon zest
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites beaten stiff
- butter dough
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare filling by mixing 12 dekagrams of ground almonds, 12 dekagrams of sugar, grated lemon zest, 6 egg yolks, and stiff snow from 4 egg whites
2. Mix everything for a quarter hour
3. Roll out butter dough and cut into square pieces
4. Place the prepared filling on them
5. Fold, moisten edges with water, seal, and press
6. Brush the envelopes with egg
7. Bake on a floured baking sheet
