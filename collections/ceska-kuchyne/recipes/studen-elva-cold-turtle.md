## Ingredients

- killed turtle
- water
- salt
- red wine
- broth
- bay leaves
- onion
- pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Soak the killed turtle in water for one hour
2. Boil in salted water for approximately fifteen minutes
3. Peel and eviscerate
4. Season with salt
5. Place on a baking pan
6. Pour over with red wine and broth
7. Add bay leaves, onion, pepper and cloves
8. Braise slowly until the meat is completely tender
9. Allow to cool with the broth
10. Slice into portions
