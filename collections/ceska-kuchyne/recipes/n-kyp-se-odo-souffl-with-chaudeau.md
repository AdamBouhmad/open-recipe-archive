## Ingredients

- 25 dekagrams (1/8 pound) almonds
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) powdered sifted sugar
- lemon peel
- 6 egg whites
- crushed biscuit
- 0.5 liter wine
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grate 25 dekagrams (1/8 pound) of almonds, crush the remains in a mortar
2. Add to 12 egg yolks 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of powdered sifted sugar rubbed with lemon peel and beat until completely thick
3. Fold in the almonds with snow from 6 egg whites, beat everything and pour into a greased mold sprinkled with crushed biscuit
4. Bake the soufflé
5. Meanwhile, pour 0.5 liter of wine into a pot, place on the stove, add 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar and 6 egg yolks, stir until thick
6. Turn out the soufflé and pour the chaudeau over it
