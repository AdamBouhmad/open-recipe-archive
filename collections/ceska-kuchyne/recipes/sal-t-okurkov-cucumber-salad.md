## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers and slice or cut thinly with a knife
2. Place slices on a plate and cover with the plate
3. Drain the water
4. Salt the cucumbers
5. Mix well with forks
6. Pour vinegar and good oil over
7. Season with pepper
