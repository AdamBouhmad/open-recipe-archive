## Ingredients

- bacon cut into thin strips
- ground clove
- pepper
- salt
- beef meat, trimmed
- lard
- parsley
- onion
- carrot
- several bay leaves
- wine
- braised carrot or cabbage
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into thin strips and roll them in ground clove, pepper, and salt.
2. Thread them properly through trimmed meat.
3. Place it in a pot with heated lard, parsley, onion, and carrot.
4. Add several bay leaves and place it on the fire.
5. When the meat begins to brown, turn it, add a little ground pepper, clove, and allspice.
6. Pour in a little wine, cover it, and let it braise on low heat for about three hours.
7. Then remove the meat to a platter, pour its own juice over it, and serve garnished with braised carrot or cabbage, or decorated with lemon slices.
