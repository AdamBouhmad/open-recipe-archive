## Ingredients

- Quail
- Onion, sliced
- Pepper
- Allspice berries
- Juniper berries
- Water or stock
- Bread roll, fried and grated
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pluck the quail up to the head, being careful not to tear them
2. Slice the onion and add several peppers, several grains of allspice and several juniper berries
3. Pour in water or stock and braise until completely tender
4. Add some fried grated bread roll to the sauce to make it thicker
5. Arrange the quail on a dish and pour the strained sauce over them
