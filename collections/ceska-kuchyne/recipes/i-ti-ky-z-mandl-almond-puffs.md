## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams almonds with skin
- breadcrumb crust, grated
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 dekagrams butter
- 75 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Pound almonds with skin and sift
2. Mix with grated breadcrumb crust, 2 egg yolks, 2 whole eggs, 3 dekagrams butter, and 75 grams sugar
3. When everything is beaten, form puffs
4. Fry them and drizzle with punch on a plate
5. Before serving at the table, pour liquor over them and set on fire
