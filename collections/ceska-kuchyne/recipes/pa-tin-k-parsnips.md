## Ingredients

- parsnips
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fresh parsley
- milk
- egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Scrape parsnips, wash, cut into rounds or sticks and boil in boiling water until tender
2. Place on a saucepan with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, add some chopped fresh parsley and let stew with frequent stirring for a while
3. Place on a hot plate, pour over with milk mixed with a beaten egg or a spoonful of flour and a piece of butter
4. You can also cut it lengthwise and fry it in bacon
5. Cooked parsnips can also be pressed through a sieve, seasoned to taste with salt and pepper, mixed with a piece of butter and a little sour cream and reheated well
