## Ingredients

- half pound pearl barley
- cold water
- two ounces fresh butter
- two quarts good beef soup
- flower (seasoning)
- six egg yolks
- lukewarm soup or cooked cooled cream

## Directions

1. Clean the barley well and put in a pot with cold water to cook, being careful it does not burn.
2. When tender, transfer to another pot with two ounces fresh butter, rub well, pour two quarts of good beef soup over it, mix well.
3. Press through a strainer or sieve and season with flower.
4. In another pot, mix six egg yolks with a cup of lukewarm soup or cooked cooled cream and thicken the soup with this mixture.
5. Serve in cups at dinners or evening entertainments with a quarter slice of lemon to each portion.
6. If to be served at lunch, at least one third of the barley may be left in it.
7. Serves 12 persons.
