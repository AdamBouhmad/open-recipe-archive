## Ingredients

- salmon
- vinegar
- onion
- bay leaf
- salt
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- fresh butter
- parsley
- shallots
- mustard
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse a nice piece of salmon in cold water, place in a pan
2. Pour a little vinegar over it, add onion cut into slices, bay leaf, salt and pour water until the salmon is completely covered
3. Cover and cook until tender
4. Press four hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve, add six tablespoons of fresh heated clean butter and stir until a fine sauce forms
5. Add finely chopped parsley and several finely chopped shallots, a tablespoon of mustard, salt and season with a little ground pepper
6. Add vinegar until the sauce is thick, mix well, pour into a small bowl
7. Serve with the cooked and buttered salmon
