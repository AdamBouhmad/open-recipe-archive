## Ingredients

- zest of 2 lemons
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 320 grams fine potato flour
- juice of 2 lemons
- snow from 16 egg whites
- butter for greasing molds
- fine grated hardtack for dusting molds
- glaze

## Directions

1. Grate the lemon zest over the sugar
2. Crush the sugar by half, sift it, and beat and mix with the 16 egg yolks for about three-quarters of an hour in one direction
3. Add the 320 grams of fine potato flour, the juice of 2 lemons, and the snow from the 16 egg whites
4. Pour the prepared dough into a mold well buttered and lightly dusted with fine grated hardtack
5. Bake slowly for about an hour
6. Brush with glaze and let dry again in the oven
