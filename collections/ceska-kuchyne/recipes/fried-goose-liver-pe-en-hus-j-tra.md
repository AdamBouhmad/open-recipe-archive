## Ingredients

- large goose liver
- flour
- butter
- lemon juice
- sauce
- breadcrumb

## Directions

1. Cut large goose liver into pieces, roll in flour and butter, fry until golden, sprinkle with lemon juice and serve with sauce. Alternatively, after cutting, salt and pepper the liver, roll in grated breadcrumb and fry in butter. Boil the butter with the juices and serve with the liver on a plate.
