## Ingredients

- Young pork ribs
- Salt
- White ground pepper
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Four onions
- Flour
- Hot water
- Meat extract
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare cutlets from young pork ribs
2. Salt both sides and lightly dust with white ground pepper
3. Dip in melted butter, coat in grated breadcrumbs, and about a quarter hour before serving, bake on a grill or in a pan
4. Meanwhile, slice four onions into thin rounds, put a piece of fresh butter on a small pan, place the sliced onion in it and fry until golden
5. Add one tablespoon of flour, then pour in one cup of hot water and add one and a half teaspoons of meat extract, salt, add a few grains of pepper and a piece of sugar
6. Let simmer until the onion is completely soft, then press this mixture through a fine sieve, put it hot on a platter and arrange the cutlets around it in a wreath
