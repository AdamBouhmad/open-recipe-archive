## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- egg yolks
- green parsley (chopped)
- sardeles (cut into pieces)
- sour cream
- bread crumbs
- salt
- egg (beaten)
- flour-mixed bread crumbs
- butter (for frying)
- flour (pale, thin sauce)
- pea water or broth
- sardeles (cut into pieces)
- green parsley or chives
- lemon juice
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard and cut a piece from one side of each so the egg can stand on a plate
2. Cut a larger piece from the other end to form a lid
3. Remove yolks and crush in a mortar
4. Cream a piece of butter and add the crushed yolks
5. Add chopped green parsley, sardeles cut into pieces, several spoons of sour cream, a little grated bread crumbs, salt and mix well
6. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
7. Press the lid on
8. Dip in beaten egg
9. Coat in bread crumbs mixed with flour
10. Fry in heated butter and arrange on a hot plate
11. Make a pale thin sauce, dilute with a little pea water or broth
12. Add sardeles cut into pieces and a little green parsley or chives to the sauce
13. Season with lemon juice
14. Add several spoons of sour cream
15. Cook everything together
16. Pour the sauce over the fried eggs and serve
