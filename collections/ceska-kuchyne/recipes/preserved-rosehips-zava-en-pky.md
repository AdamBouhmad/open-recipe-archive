## Ingredients

- beautiful, large, fully ripe rosehips
- sugar, cold and thin at first, then progressively thicker

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, large, completely ripe rosehips.
2. Cut them lengthwise and clean them completely.
3. Cook them in fresh river water until soft.
4. Place in cold water to cool completely.
5. Arrange on a baking sheet and pour over them a cold, thin sugar syrup.
6. On the second and third days, drain off the sugar each day and cook it progressively thicker and thicker, then pour the cooled sugar back over the rosehips.
7. Continue this process until the fourth day, then pour hot sugar over the rosehips and cook together several times.
8. Skim off the foam and place the preserve in containers.
