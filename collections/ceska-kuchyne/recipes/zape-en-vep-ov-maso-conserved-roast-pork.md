## Ingredients

- slaughtered pork
- salt
- caraway

## Directions

1. When the meat from the slaughtered pig has cooled, slice it into neat pieces about half an inch thick
2. Remove all fat and bones
3. Salt the meat and let it lie in salt for at least one hour
4. Take a baking pan and lay the slices side by side on it
5. Sprinkle with caraway
6. Bake without adding any water until done
7. Remove the slices and lay them on a platter again side by side
8. When everything is done, let the meat cool
9. Take a large clay pot and pack the meat tightly into it
10. Pour lard over it so that the lard reaches two inches above the meat
11. When removing it, take care not to damage the pieces that are to remain in the pot; therefore it is best to remove more meat at once and pour the remainder well with lard so that air cannot reach it
