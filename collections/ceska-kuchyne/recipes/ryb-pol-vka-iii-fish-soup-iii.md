## Ingredients

- fish meat
- flour
- butter
- soup
- parsley
- fried roll

## Directions

1. Remove bones from the meat, coat in flour and fry in butter
2. Add soup to this, let it braise for a while, strain it
3. Mash the fish meat, add parsley and pour over fried roll
