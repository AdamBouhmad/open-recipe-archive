## Ingredients

- veal sweetbread
- veal brain
- lukewarm water
- cold water
- mushrooms
- broth
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Wash the veal sweetbread and brain in lukewarm water
2. Cool in cold water
3. Braise with several mushrooms in broth
4. Add egg yolks to the juices and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
5. Place on ice to set
6. Form into small croquettes
7. Fry in butter
