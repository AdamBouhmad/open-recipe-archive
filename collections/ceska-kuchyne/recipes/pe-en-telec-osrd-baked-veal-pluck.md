## Ingredients

- veal pluck (heart and lungs)
- butter or lard
- eggs
- lemon zest
- marjoram
- pepper
- garlic
- bread or bread rolls
- milk

## Directions

1. Boil pluck in salted water until tender
2. Slice it, remove all sinews and marrow and chop finely
3. Cream 3 spoonfuls of butter or lard on a plate
4. Add 3 eggs, finely chopped lemon zest, a little chopped and rubbed marjoram, a pinch of pepper, a slice of garlic, the chopped pluck, two slices of bread or a bread roll soaked and squeezed out in milk, half a grated bread roll and mix everything well
5. Grease a mold with butter
6. Sprinkle with grated bread roll
7. Put the mixture in it and let bake in the oven for an hour
8. Turn it out onto a plate and serve with a sardine or caper sauce in a separate bowl
