## Ingredients

- chocolate, grated
- finely crushed moist tragacanth flour
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Grate chocolate and mix with finely crushed moistened tragacanth flour into a dough
2. Add fine sugar
3. Shape into any desired forms and molds
4. When completely dry, sugar as described for almonds
