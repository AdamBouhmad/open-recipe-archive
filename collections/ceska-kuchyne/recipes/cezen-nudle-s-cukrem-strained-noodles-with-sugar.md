## Ingredients

- several eggs
- milk
- sugar
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- sugar mixed with cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare dough from several eggs for noodles and cut into short, fairly wide pieces
2. Put sugar and milk in pot and cook
3. When boiling, add noodles
4. Serve either in milk or grease baking pan with butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and bake
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon when serving
