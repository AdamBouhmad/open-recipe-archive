## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- water
- sugar (half cup or more per cup of pulp)
- crushed cinnamon
- crushed cloves

## Directions

1. Wash nice ripe plums, remove the pits if they come away easily, and cook with a little water.
2. If the pits do not come away easily, cook with the pits in.
3. When soft, press through a sieve and put back in the pot.
4. After cooking for a while, add sugar—half cup or more per cup of pulp—along with a little crushed cinnamon and cloves.
5. Cook with frequent or constant stirring until the desired thickness is reached.
