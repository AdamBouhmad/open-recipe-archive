## Ingredients

- red beets with greens
- water
- dill
- sour cream (1 liter)
- kvass (0.5 liter)
- hard-boiled eggs
- crayfish tails
- cucumbers
- lemon slices
- pepper
- salt
- ice cream

## Directions

1. Cook red beets with their greens in water, strain, chop the vegetables and mix with dill, pour one liter of sour cream and half liter of kvass
2. When serving, decorate with hard-boiled eggs, crayfish tails, cucumbers cut into cubes, lemon slices, season with pepper and salt, and surround with ice cream
