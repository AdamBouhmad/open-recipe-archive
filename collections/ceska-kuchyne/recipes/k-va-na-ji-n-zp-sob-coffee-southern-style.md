## Ingredients

- Coffee beans
- sugar

## Directions

1. In southern regions where much coffee is drunk, never roast the coffee before the moment of preparation.
2. Roast the beans in a mortar, crush them, and cook them with sugar.
3. Do not wait for the coffee to settle; instead, pour it into small cups and drink it with its thick contents.
