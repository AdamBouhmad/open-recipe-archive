## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries gathered in dry weather
- pounded sugar
- good rum

## Directions

1. Place ripe strawberries gathered in dry weather with pounded sugar in jars
2. Pour enough good rum over them to cover the strawberries
3. Close and seal the jars
