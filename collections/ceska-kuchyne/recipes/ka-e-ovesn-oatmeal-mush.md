## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk, or half water and half milk
- 1 teaspoon butter
- salt
- oatmeal
- sweet cream or milk

## Directions

1. Take a quart of milk or half water and half milk, add a teaspoon of butter, salt it and bring to a boil
2. When it boils, sprinkle in the oatmeal
3. If cooking in a double boiler, it requires no stirring
4. If cooking on direct heat, stir frequently
5. Cook for half an hour or longer, either in a double boiler or on gentle heat
6. Serve with sweet cream or milk
