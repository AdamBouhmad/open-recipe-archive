## Ingredients

- partridges
- lard for larding
- thick bacon slices
- butter
- small celeries
- broth

## Directions

1. Lard the partridges with lard and brown on both sides in butter with slices of thick bacon.
2. Clean the small celeries, cut into 4-6 pieces, and add to the partridges.
3. Pour with broth and braise everything together until tender.
