## Ingredients

- lobster
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Tie the lobster's claws
2. Plunge into boiling salted water
3. If about a pound, boil for about a quarter hour; if larger, boil a bit longer
4. Do not overcook or the meat will toughen
5. After cooking, split lengthwise
6. Serve either warm or cold with mayonnaise or with vinegar and spices
