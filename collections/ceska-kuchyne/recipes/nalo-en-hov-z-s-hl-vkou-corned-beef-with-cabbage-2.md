## Ingredients

- 3 pounds corned beef
- 1 head of cabbage
- 4 to 5 potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Rinse a piece of corned beef, about three pounds, in hot water. Place it in a pot, pour water over it until submerged, bring to a boil and skim off the foam. Cover the pot well and let it simmer over gentle heat.
2. After cooking for about 1.5 hours, prepare the cabbage: Quarter the head of cabbage, remove the core, place in cold water and let it sit for about a quarter hour. Drain well on a sieve and place into the pot with the meat, along with 4 to 5 potatoes.
3. After a quarter hour, add 4 to 5 peeled larger potatoes and let cook until the potatoes are tender. Remove the meat onto a platter and arrange the cooked vegetables around it.
