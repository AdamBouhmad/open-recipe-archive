## Ingredients

- one pound cottage cheese
- four whole eggs
- two ounces butter
- one good cup semolina
- salt
- grated rolls

## Directions

1. Beat one pound of cottage cheese well, add four whole eggs, two ounces of butter, one good cup of semolina, and salt very lightly.
2. Stir this for half an hour.
3. Grease a clean cloth with butter and sprinkle it with grated rolls.
4. Place the prepared cottage cheese into it, tie it loosely, place it in boiling water and let it cook for one hour.
5. Then remove the pudding onto a dish, sprinkle it with grated roll crust, and pour melted fresh butter over it.
