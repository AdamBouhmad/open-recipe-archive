## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- salt
- lard
- onion
- truffles
- mushrooms
- bay leaf
- ham
- herbs
- spices
- white wine
- coulisse
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcocks but do not cook them.
2. Salt and place on a baking pan in melted lard.
3. Lay underneath several pieces of chopped onion, truffles and mushrooms, as well as bay leaf, finely chopped ham, herbs and spices.
4. Braise the woodcocks in this mixture for about ten minutes.
5. Pour good white wine and coulisse over, add pepper and braise everything completely.
6. Skim off the fat, strain the sauce and finish with lemon juice.
