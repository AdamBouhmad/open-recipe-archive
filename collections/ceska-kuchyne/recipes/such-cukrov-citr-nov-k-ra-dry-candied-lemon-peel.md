## Ingredients

- lemon peel, peeled
- sugar
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Dry the peeled lemon slices.
2. The cleaned sugar, to which powdered sugar has been added, should be well boiled.
3. Add the lemon peel to it, boil with it several times, stirring constantly.
4. When the lemon peel is well sugared, let everything cool, remove the peel, place it on a sieve and dry it well on warm stove.
5. Then it is packed into boxes, interspersed with fine paper and stored in a dry place.
