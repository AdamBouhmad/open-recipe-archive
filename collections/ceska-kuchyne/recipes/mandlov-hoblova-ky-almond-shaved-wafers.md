## Ingredients

- 240 grams sweet almonds
- several bitter almonds
- juice of 2 lemons
- 240 grams sugar
- wafers

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel sweet almonds, adding some bitter almonds
2. Grate or pound them
3. Mix with lemon juice and 240 grams sugar to form a thick paste
4. Spread on wafers and let rest overnight
5. Cut into strips as thick as a finger
6. Bake in a warm oven, then bend on round wood
