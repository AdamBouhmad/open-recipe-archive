## Ingredients

- fresh cherries
- ½ kilogram sugar per 2 decagrams cherries

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from fresh cherries
2. Pound the sugar and alternate layers of cherries and sugar
3. Seal well
4. Place in boiling water for 8 minutes
5. Dry the jars or boxes and store
