## Ingredients

- crayfish butter
- flour
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- asparagus
- roll
- other garnishes from other crayfish soups

## Directions

1. Make a roux from crayfish butter and several tablespoons of flour
2. Cook it in boiling milk, salt and sweeten
3. When serving, top with crayfish butter
4. Add the same garnishes as in other crayfish soups (asparagus, roll, etc.)
