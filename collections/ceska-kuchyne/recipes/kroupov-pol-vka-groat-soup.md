## Ingredients

- quarter liter of medium groats
- water
- butter
- beef broth
- 3 egg yolks
- fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Cook the groats in water with butter, pour in beef broth, strain, add 3 egg yolks and pour over fried bread roll
