## Ingredients

- morels, washed
- butter
- salt
- lemon juice
- lemons, sliced

## Directions

1. Place washed morels whole in a baking dish with butter and salt
2. Braise until soft
3. Drizzle with lemon juice
4. Arrange with sliced lemons around them
