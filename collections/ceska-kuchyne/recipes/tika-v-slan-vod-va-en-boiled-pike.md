## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- water
- butter
- potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the pike well, remove entrails and fins, coil it into a ring shape, place the tail in the mouth, bind the mouth firmly with thread.
2. Place the pike in a pot, pour enough water to cover it completely, bring to fire and let it cook until tender, then place it to the side on a warm spot.
3. When ready to serve, carefully remove it with a skimmer onto a dish, arrange around it small round potatoes boiled in salted water.
4. Pour generously with hot fresh butter in which you have fried finely chopped green parsley.
5. Be especially careful that the pike does not overcook.
