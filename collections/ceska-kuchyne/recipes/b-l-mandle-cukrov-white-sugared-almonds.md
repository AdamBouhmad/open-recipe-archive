## Ingredients

- fine large almonds
- fine refined sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine large almonds and rub with cloth to remove all dust
2. Place in a pot or saucepan
3. Boil fine refined sugar
4. Continuously stir almonds in the container so they heat evenly over the heat
5. Add crushed sugar and continue stirring until each almond is coated
6. When dry, add new sugar and repeat several times until a complete sugar layer forms around the almonds
7. Finally, pour melted warm sugar to give the almonds shine
8. Remove almonds from container several times and place on a sieve to collect debris and fine sugar
9. Crush and melt this sugar again, then pour warm back onto the almonds
