## Ingredients

- mushrooms (porcini, truffles, or champignons)
- butter
- salt
- good broth
- 10 eggs
- good sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, stew them briefly in butter with salt
2. Then stew them in good broth until completely soft
3. Prepare dough from 10 eggs, good sour cream, salt and flour
4. Bake thin rolls and always put prepared mushrooms between two rolls
