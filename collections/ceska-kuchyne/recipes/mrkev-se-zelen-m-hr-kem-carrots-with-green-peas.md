## Ingredients

- small red carrots
- cold water
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- flour
- beef broth
- sour cream
- dill
- green peas
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the small red carrots cleanly, cut into thin sticks and rinse in cold water
2. Place on a small saucepan with a piece of fresh butter and a piece of sugar, having salted them first
3. Stew until tender, adding broth or water as needed to prevent burning
4. Dust with a little flour, add a little beef broth and a little sour cream, season with dill and let stew a bit longer
5. On another small saucepan place a piece of butter and a quart of fine green peas, add a piece of sugar and let stew until soft
6. Add a piece of finely chopped fresh parsley, dust with flour and salt lightly
7. Add a little beef broth and let stew a bit longer
8. Place the peas in the center of a dish and arrange the carrots around them in a wreath
