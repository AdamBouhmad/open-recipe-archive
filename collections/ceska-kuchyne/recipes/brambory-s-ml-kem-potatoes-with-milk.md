## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- lard
- fresh milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice raw potatoes into the thinnest slices possible
2. Put them in a pot and add about a spoonful of lard, stirring all the while
3. Then add about a cupful of fresh milk, and eat it hot
4. This is a tasty side dish
