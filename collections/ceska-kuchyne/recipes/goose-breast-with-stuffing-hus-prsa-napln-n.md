## Ingredients

- goose breast with bone
- breadcrumb
- goose fat
- onion
- clove
- new spice
- pepper
- marjoram
- garlic
- eggs
- beef broth
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the breast from the bone and remove the skin. Finely chop the meat and add 1½ grated breadcrumbs, onion fried in goose fat, 4 tablespoons goose fat, a little clove, new spice, pepper, crushed marjoram and crushed garlic. Mix everything well on a board and add two eggs. Place the mixture on the breast bone, put on a baking sheet, pour beef broth over it and roast the breasts. Either serve them whole or cut into pieces. Cabbage is usually added as a side.
