## Ingredients

- four to six decagrams ground cocoa
- cold cream
- three deciliters cream
- sugar
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the ground cocoa with cold cream
2. Add three deciliters of cream
3. Add a little sugar and six egg yolks
4. Mix well
