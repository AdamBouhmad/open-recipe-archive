## Ingredients

- mutton ribs
- water
- all vegetables
- salt
- carrots
- pepper
- piece of sugar
- pale gravy

## Directions

1. Place mutton ribs on a baking pan and pour water over them
2. Add all vegetables and salt lightly
3. Boil until meat is tender
4. Meanwhile wash and cut carrots into thin noodles
5. Season with pepper and add a piece of sugar
6. Braise carrots, continually basting with broth until completely tender
7. Make pale gravy and press enough of it through to thicken properly
8. Cut meat into neat pieces, add to carrots, caramelize a piece of sugar to give it a pink color
