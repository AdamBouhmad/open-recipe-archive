## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams butter (6 lots)
- 7 egg yolks
- half liter milk
- 30 dekagrams cooked potato flour (20 lots)
- salt
- 7 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter until it foams
2. Add 7 egg yolks, half liter milk, cooled potato flour, salt and snow from 7 egg whites
3. Serve the potato roll with compote or salad
