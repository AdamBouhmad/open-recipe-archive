## Ingredients

- diver
- bacon
- pepper
- allspice
- 2 onions
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the diver and cut off the head and legs.
2. Lard the cleaned bird with bacon, or wrap it in bacon strips.
3. Add a few peppercorns, allspice, and 2 finely chopped onions.
4. Season with salt and roast until golden, turning it so it browns evenly on both sides.
5. Chop and pour sauce over it, then sprinkle with fried breadcrumbs or make a wreath of fried breadcrumbs.
6. Alternatively, make a dark sauce as for game and place the diver in it.
