## Ingredients

- cooked peas
- onion, sliced and browned to golden
- beef broth
- garlic, minced
- smoked meat, roasted beef, fried brain, fried tongue, or sausages

## Directions

1. To the cooked peas add a little diced onion browned to golden, mix it well and press it through a sieve into a pot.
2. Add a little beef broth, add minced garlic to it and let it cook a little longer.
3. Pour it onto a platter, coat with fat and cover with smoked meat, roasted beef fried in butter, fried brain, fried tongue, or sausages.
