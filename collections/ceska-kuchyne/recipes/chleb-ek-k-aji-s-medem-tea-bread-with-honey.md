## Ingredients

- 250 grams butter
- 1 kilogram fine flour
- 4 eggs
- 125 grams sugar
- 125 grams washed and sorted sultanas and raisins
- 50 grams yeast dissolved in lukewarm water
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until it foams
2. Gradually add the fine flour, eggs, sugar, sultanas and raisins, yeast
3. Season with salt and work into dough
4. Allow to rise
5. Form into small round loaves
6. When risen again, brush with egg beaten with honey
7. Bake slowly in a moderately heated oven
