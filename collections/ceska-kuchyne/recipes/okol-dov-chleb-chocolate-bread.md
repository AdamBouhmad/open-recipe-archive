## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 200 grams crushed sugar
- 60 grams grated chocolate
- 375 grams ground almonds
- wafer sheets

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs into stiff snow
2. Add the crushed sugar, grated chocolate, and ground almonds
3. Mix everything together
4. Spread thinly on wafer sheets
5. Bake at moderate temperature
6. Cut the baked bread into long strips
