## Ingredients

- marinated herring, cut into small cubes
- roasted beef, cut into small pieces
- cooked potatoes
- red beets, cut into cubes
- apples, cut into cubes
- cleaned and rinsed sardines
- a little capers
- pickled and peppered cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs, cut into pieces
- a little finely chopped onion
- Siberian wormwood
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut the marinated herring into small cubes
2. Mix with roasted beef cut into small pieces
3. Add cooked potatoes, red beets cut into cubes, apples cut into cubes, cleaned and rinsed sardines, a little capers, pickled and peppered cucumbers, and hard-boiled eggs cut into pieces
4. Mix in a little finely chopped onion and Siberian wormwood
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Drizzle with vinegar and oil and mix well
