## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- At least one liter sweet cream
- About three-quarters kilogram sugar
- Three to four deca good tea

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs
2. Take at least one liter sweet cream, about three-quarters kilogram sugar and three to four deca good tea
3. Cook everything with constant whipping
4. Let cool
5. Strain
6. Process into ice cream
