## Ingredients

- veal, pork, or lamb kidneys
- butter
- red wine
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the kidneys, salt them, and braise in butter with red wine until tender.
2. Let them cool, cut not too thinly, dip in egg, coat in breadcrumbs, and fry on both sides until brown.
3. Serve with lemon cut into quarters and can be served right after the soup course.
