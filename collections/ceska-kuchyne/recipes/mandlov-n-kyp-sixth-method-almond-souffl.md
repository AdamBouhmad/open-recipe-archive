## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) flour
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) butter
- 16 dekagrams sugar
- 12 dekagrams crushed sweet almonds
- several bitter almonds
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 more egg yolks
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- stiff snow from 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve 12 dekagrams flour in 1/4 liter sour cream.
2. Add 9 dekagrams butter, 16 dekagrams sugar, 12 dekagrams crushed sweet almonds, several bitter almonds, and 5 egg yolks.
3. Heat over low heat, stirring, until the mixture does not stick to the pan.
4. Let cool.
5. Add another 5 egg yolks, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, and stiff snow from 10 egg whites.
6. Bake in a greased mold.
