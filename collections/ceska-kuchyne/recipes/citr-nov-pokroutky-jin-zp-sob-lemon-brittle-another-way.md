## Ingredients

- sugar
- lemon zest from 3-4 lemons per kilo of sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape the zest from several lemons (about 3-4 lemons per kilo of sugar)
2. Boil everything until thickened and prepare as the previous brittle pieces
