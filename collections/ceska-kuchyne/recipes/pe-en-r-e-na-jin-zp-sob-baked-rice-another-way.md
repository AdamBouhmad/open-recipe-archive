## Ingredients

- 1/2 small cup rice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons fine sugar
- salt
- 1 quart milk
- cinnamon and sugar

## Directions

1. Sort and rinse half a small cup of rice well
2. Put it in a form or baking dish
3. Add three tablespoons of butter, two tablespoons of fine sugar, a little salt and a quart of milk
4. Put it in the oven and let it bake until all the milk is baked out
5. Then sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and serve
