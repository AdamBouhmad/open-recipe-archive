## Ingredients

- 1.5 quarts water
- 1 cup sour cabbage
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 spoons white flour
- 5 or 6 eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Boil water with sour cabbage for 15 minutes
2. Mix sour cream with 2 spoons white flour and beat well
3. Pour the mixture into the soup and stir
4. Season with salt
5. Break eggs one at a time and drop them into the soup
6. Let simmer for 3 minutes and the soup is ready
