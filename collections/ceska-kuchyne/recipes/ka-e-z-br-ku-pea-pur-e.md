## Ingredients

- peas, somewhat firm
- butter
- broth
- salt
- sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Put somewhat firm peas in a pot with butter, cover and braise for a while, shaking often
2. After a quarter hour, add broth, press through a sieve
3. Thin the porridge with broth, salt it, add a piece of sugar and a little flour
