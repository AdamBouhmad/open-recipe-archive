## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- chopped chives
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Slice potatoes and bake in butter
2. Mix several eggs with salt, pepper, chopped chives and several tablespoons sour cream
3. Pour over the potatoes so the eggs look scrambled
