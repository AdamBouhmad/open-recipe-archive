## Ingredients

- good cream
- vanilla, orange, tea, roasted coffee or similar
- four whole eggs
- eight egg yolks
- 20 to 28 decagrams sugar
- salt
- flavored cream

## Directions

1. Steep vanilla, orange, tea, roasted coffee and similar items in good cream, then strain
2. Let cool
3. Add four whole eggs and eight egg yolks
4. Add 20 to 28 decagrams of sugar and salt
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Pour the prepared liquid into containers placed in a larger pan of water and steam
7. Top with whipped flavored cream
