## Ingredients

- 250 grams fresh, heated butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 liter sour cream
- 375-400 grams fine flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter until foamy
2. Beat in eggs and sour cream
3. Work in flour thoroughly
4. Let rise
5. Roll out dough and shape into thin long pieces
6. Brush with beaten egg
7. Salt
8. Place on buttered baking sheet
9. Bake until golden and crispy
