## Ingredients

- 1 kilo of meat
- vegetables
- 3 egg yolks
- pieces of butter
- 3 tablespoons of flour
- paper piping cone
- butter for frying
- chives
- good beef broth

## Directions

1. Cook the meat with vegetables; from 3 egg yolks, pieces of butter and 3 tablespoons of flour, make thin dough
2. Using a paper piping cone, squeeze small pieces like peas into hot butter and fry to golden color
3. Place in a soup bowl, sprinkle with chives and pour good beef broth over them
