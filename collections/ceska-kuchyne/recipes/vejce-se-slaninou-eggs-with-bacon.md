## Ingredients

- bacon, cut into cubes
- eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into cubes and fry on a baking sheet until pink.
2. Add beaten, mixed, salted, and peppered eggs.
3. Stir constantly until sufficiently thick.
