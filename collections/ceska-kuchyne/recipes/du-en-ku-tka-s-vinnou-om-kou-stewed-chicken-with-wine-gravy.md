## Ingredients

- chicken
- ham
- fresh butter
- flour
- onion
- clove
- lemon
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- beef broth
- good wine

## Directions

1. Gut and clean the chicken, then cut it into neat pieces and place in cold water for a while.
2. Place a piece of sliced ham and a piece of fresh butter in a pot, dust it with flour, add an onion stuck with a clove, lemon cut into slices, a piece of parsley, carrot, and celery; tie this vegetables together with string.
3. Place the chicken in this and let it stew, then pour in good beef broth and let it cook over moderate heat.
4. When ready to serve, pour in a glass of good wine, squeeze in lemon juice, let it cook a bit more, remove the chicken to a platter, strain the sauce over it through a sieve, and serve.
