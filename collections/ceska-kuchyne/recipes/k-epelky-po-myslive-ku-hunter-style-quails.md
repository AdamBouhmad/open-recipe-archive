## Ingredients

- quails
- salt
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- mushrooms
- red wine

## Directions

1. Prepare quails as for roasting and season with salt.
2. Place on a baking sheet with butter, onion, parsley, and mushrooms underneath.
3. Baste regularly and turn constantly during slow roasting.
4. When nearly done, pour red wine over them.
5. Continue roasting in this wine for a while longer.
