## Ingredients

- kid meat from loin, neck, breast, liver and kidneys
- butter
- onion
- spices
- flour
- meat broth
- sour cream
- paprika

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces, salt it, and braise in butter with onion and spices until completely tender.
2. Dust with flour and add meat broth and good sour cream.
3. Sprinkle with paprika and continue braising, stirring frequently, until tender.
4. Carefully remove the cooked meat from the fat, which is allowed to settle, and serve with just the thick sauce poured over.
