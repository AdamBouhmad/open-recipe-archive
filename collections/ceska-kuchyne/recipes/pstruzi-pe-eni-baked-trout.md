## Ingredients

- cleaned trout
- salt
- white pepper
- lemon juice
- olive oil
- breadcrumbs
- anchovy sauce or crayfish sauce or caper sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and eviscerate trout, rinse, dry, cut into pieces
2. Salt and season with white pepper
3. Marinate in lemon juice for half an hour
4. Dip in good olive oil, coat with grated breadcrumbs
5. Bake until brown
6. Serve with anchovy, crayfish, or caper sauce
