## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Broth
- Egg yolks
- Smoked tongue or veal roast

## Directions

1. Cut the asparagus into pieces and cook in salted water or broth
2. Before serving, remove and mix with egg yolks until evenly thickened
3. Surround with either smoked tongue or veal roast
