## Ingredients

- Beer
- Sugar
- Milk
- Salt
- 1, 2 or 3 eggs
- Water

## Directions

1. Boil beer and add 1 spoon sugar
2. Boil milk separately (use less milk than beer) and salt according to milk's needs
3. Slowly pour the milk into the beer, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
4. Beat 1, 2 or 3 eggs (depending on soup quantity) in about 2 spoons water and beat well
5. Pour the egg mixture into the hot soup while stirring, being careful not to let it curdle
6. Add sugar according to soup quantity and desired sweetness
