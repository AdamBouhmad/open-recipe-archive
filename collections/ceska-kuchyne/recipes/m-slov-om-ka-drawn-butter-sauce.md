## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cupfuls water
- parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix four tablespoons of butter smoothly with two tablespoons of flour on a deep plate
2. Put two cupfuls of water in a pot and when it boils, add the butter and flour mixture with constant stirring
3. When it begins to boil again, add chopped parsley, pepper and salt and remove from heat
