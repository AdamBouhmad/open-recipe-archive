## Ingredients

- 1 full cup butter
- sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- flour
- baking powder
- currant or wine preserves
- yellow frosting

## Directions

1. Make the cake the same way as the silver layer cake, using 1 full cup butter instead of less.
2. Use 7 egg yolks and 1 whole egg instead of egg whites.
3. Use 1 teaspoon lemon extract instead of vanilla.
4. Spread frosting mixed with currant or wine preserves between the cakes.
5. Cover the cake with yellow frosting.
