## Ingredients

- one spoon of flour
- a little water
- six egg yolks or three whole eggs
- one quarter pound of fresh butter
- several grains of pepper
- sufficient amount of pure fish broth or parsley water
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Mix in a cup one spoon of flour with a little water
2. Beat into it six egg yolks or three whole eggs
3. Add one quarter pound of fresh butter, several grains of pepper and sufficient amount of pure fish broth or parsley water
4. Stir constantly over the fire until it begins to rise
5. Add juice of half a lemon and strain the sauce
6. This sauce is served with fish and must be prepared just before use; if it must stand longer, it is placed in a pot in hot water
