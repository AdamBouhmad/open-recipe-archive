## Ingredients

- tender green pods
- salt (half the weight of the pods)

## Directions

1. Trim both ends of the tender green pods
2. Mix with salt
3. Place into jars for preservation
4. Tie them up and let them rest for several days
5. After a few days when they have settled, refill the jars from other jars with pickled pods, repeating this from time to time
6. Before use, rinse the pods in clean water
7. Drizzle with vinegar
8. Add oil and pepper
