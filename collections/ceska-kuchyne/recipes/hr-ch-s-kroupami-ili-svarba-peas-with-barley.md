## Ingredients

- peas
- barley
- salt
- caraway seeds
- lard or melted butter
- smoked meat or fried blood sausage

## Directions

1. Cook the peas according to the previous method and meanwhile also cook the barley with a little salt and roasted caraway seeds until soft and thick.
2. Then drain the peas and alternately layer them with the barley in another pot, let it cook a little longer, shake it in the pot, pour onto a platter and coat with hot lard or melted butter.
3. Sprinkle with crackling if desired and garnish with either smoked meat or fried blood sausage.
4. Alternatively, put the cooked peas alternately with the barley into the platter in which you want to serve it, put peas on top and coat and garnish as described above.
