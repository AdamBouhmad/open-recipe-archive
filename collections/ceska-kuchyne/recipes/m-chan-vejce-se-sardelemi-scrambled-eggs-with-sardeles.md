## Ingredients

- eggs
- sour cream (two spoons)
- flour (one coffee spoon)
- salt
- pepper
- sardeles or small fish (cleaned and cut into thin long pieces)

## Directions

1. Beat several eggs with two spoons of sour cream
2. Add one coffee spoon of flour
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Add cleaned sardeles or small fish cut into thin long pieces
5. Stir everything together in a pan with a piece of heated butter
6. Alternatively: finely chop the sardeles and cook them in butter with chopped green parsley, then mix into the scrambled eggs
