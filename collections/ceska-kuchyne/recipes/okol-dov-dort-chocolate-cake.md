## Ingredients

- 36 dekagrams (24 lots) peeled ground almonds
- half kilogram sugar
- 15 egg yolks
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) grated chocolate
- a little vanilla
- lemon zest from one lemon
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- wafer
- sugar glaze

## Directions

1. Mix 36 dekagrams peeled ground almonds, half kilogram sugar, 15 egg yolks, 18 dekagrams grated chocolate, a little vanilla, and lemon zest from one lemon
2. If too thin, add a handful of grated breadcrumbs
3. Beat for an hour
4. Grease a baking form with butter and line with wafer
5. Bake the cake
6. Decorate with sugar glaze
