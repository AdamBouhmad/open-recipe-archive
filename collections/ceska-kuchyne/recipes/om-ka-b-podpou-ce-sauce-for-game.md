## Ingredients

- pale roux
- good broth
- lemon peel
- white wine
- several chopped anchovies
- juice of half a lemon
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a pale roux and boil with good broth
2. Add lemon peel, white wine, and several chopped anchovies
3. Add juice from half a lemon
4. Mix in egg yolks
5. Serve typically with boiled pigeons or old hens
