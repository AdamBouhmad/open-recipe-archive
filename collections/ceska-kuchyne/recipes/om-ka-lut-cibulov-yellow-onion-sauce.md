## Ingredients

- 1/2 onion
- beef broth
- 2 slices white bread
- butter
- saffron
- cumin

## Directions

1. Chop half an onion finely, place in a pot, pour beef broth over it and let it simmer briefly.
2. In another pot, place two slices of white bread soaked in water and squeezed dry, a piece of fresh butter, a little dried saffron ground to powder, mix well.
3. Pour the broth with onion over this mixture, mix well and let it simmer a little longer.
4. Finally brown the sauce with a roux and let it simmer a little longer.
5. If desired, you may also add a little ground caraway.
