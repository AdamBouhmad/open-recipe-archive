## Ingredients

- venison saddle
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- peppercorns
- spices
- cloves
- juniper berries
- sprigs of thyme
- bay leaves
- onion
- lemon zest from half a lemon
- sugar (1 spoon)
- lemon juice
- vinegar (half a small cup)
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Wash the venison saddle well, salt it and place in a large pot
2. Pour wine and vinegar over it with enough water to make it pleasantly sour
3. Add several peppercorns, some spices, several cloves, juniper berries, sprigs of thyme, two or three bay leaves, and one onion
4. Let the saddle cook until it becomes tender
5. Remove it from the sauce and add horseradish sauce to it
6. Prepare the horseradish sauce as follows: grate the zest from half a lemon into a small bowl, add one spoon of sugar, squeeze lemon juice into it, add half a small cup of vinegar and enough grated horseradish to make it thick
