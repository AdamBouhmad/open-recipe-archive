## Ingredients

- large, well-developed, still green grapes
- sugar
- soft water

## Directions

1. Select large, undeveloped, still green grapes
2. Remove the seeds and cook the grapes in soft water several times
3. Cover the vessel and when the berries are soft enough to crush with fingers, remove to a sieve to dry
4. For equal weight of grape berries, take sugar and cook to the sixth degree
5. Add the wine berries to it and cook everything together
6. Skim off foam, pour the preserve onto a dish and cover with paper
7. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, add the berries, cook together several times, skim foam, and pour into jars after cooling
8. Alternatively, on the second day place berries in jars and pour the thickened, well-cooked sugar over them once cooled
