## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram flour
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- sugar
- 3 dekagrams yeast
- milk
- sauerkraut

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, rub the butter, add eggs, 2 egg yolks, some ground sugar so the dough is not bitter
2. Add 3 dekagrams of yeast to the dough and milk as needed
3. When the dough has risen halfway, cut off a piece of dough, roll it out, make a small triangle, place a piece of ordinary sauerkraut in the middle, fold the triangle over it
4. Place the buns on a greased baking sheet in the oven until they turn red, or drop them into boiling water, cook them and butter them
