## Ingredients

- Eight egg yolks
- Half a lemon
- One teaspoon of sugar
- Eight egg whites
- Butter
- Powdered sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks well in a deep bowl
2. Grate the zest of half a lemon on a grater and add it to the yolks with one teaspoon of sugar
3. Salt lightly
4. Beat eight egg whites until stiff
5. Mix with the yolks
6. Heat a little butter in a frying pan
7. Pour in a portion of the mixture and cook until golden
8. Continue this way until all the mixture is used
9. Sprinkle the omelettes with powdered sugar and ground cinnamon
10. Arrange on a plate and serve warm
