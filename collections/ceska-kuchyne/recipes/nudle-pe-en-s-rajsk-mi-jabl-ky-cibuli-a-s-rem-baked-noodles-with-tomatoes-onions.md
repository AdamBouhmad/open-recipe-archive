## Ingredients

- Thick noodles
- Onions, plenty
- Butter or lard for frying
- Tomatoes
- Salt
- Garlic (optional)
- Cheese, grated or thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Make thick noodles and cook them
2. Meanwhile, fry plenty of onions in butter or lard
3. Add the drained noodles to the fried onions
4. Season with salt
5. Add tomatoes (and a little garlic if desired)
6. Mix everything well
7. Place in a clean dish
8. Top with cheese, either grated or sliced thin
9. Cover and bake in the oven for about three quarters of an hour
10. About ten minutes before it is done, uncover it in the oven so the top becomes slightly reddish
11. Serve hot at the table
