## Ingredients

- young venison
- bacon
- egg yolks
- butter
- houska (bread roll)
- cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of young venison with bacon
2. On a baking sheet, beat together 6 egg yolks, a piece of butter, and 2 soaked houska rolls until a thick paste forms
3. Let it cool
4. In a mortar, pound everything with 4 raw egg yolks, cloves, and pepper
5. Press through a sieve onto a floured board
6. Roll it into an elongated patty
