## Ingredients

- goose livers
- chopped truffles
- butter
- roux (zápražka)
- gravy from the livers
- 4 egg yolks
- juice of half a lemon
- chopped livers
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Braise goose livers with chopped truffles in butter
2. When cooled, cut into cubes
3. Mix the roux with the gravy from the livers, 4 egg yolks
4. Cook this mixture
5. Add lemon juice and the chopped livers
6. Place on ice to set
7. Cut and form into small croquettes
8. Brush with egg
9. Roll in bread crumbs
10. Fry in butter
