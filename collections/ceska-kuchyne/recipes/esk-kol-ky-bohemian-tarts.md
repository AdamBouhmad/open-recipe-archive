## Ingredients

- dough (prepared as previously described)
- prepared filling
- egg
- sliced almonds or butter mixed with sugar and flour

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as previously described
2. Work until dough no longer sticks to the spoon
3. Let rise
4. Roll out thinly on a floured surface
5. Cut into small squares
6. Place a piece of prepared filling in the center of each square
7. Fold opposite corners together toward the center
8. Arrange on a buttered baking pan
9. Brush with beaten egg
10. Sprinkle with sliced almonds or with butter mixed with sugar and flour
11. Let rise briefly
12. Bake in a well-heated oven
13. Dust with sugar when done
