## Ingredients

- truffles, washed and peeled
- wine

## Directions

1. Place washed and peeled truffles in a Papillon pot with wine
2. Cook and braise together
3. Remove cooked truffles and place in buttered paper
4. When completely softened, remove the paper
5. Arrange attractively on a plate or dish for serving
