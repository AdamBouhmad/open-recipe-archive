## Ingredients

- hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as mayonnaise, except use hard-boiled egg yolks instead of raw ones
2. The hard-boiled yolks must first be rubbed with salt, pepper and a little oil into a fine paste
