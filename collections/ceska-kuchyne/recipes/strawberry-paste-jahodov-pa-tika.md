## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar (at least half again as much as the fruit pulp)

## Directions

1. Press strawberries through a fine sieve
2. To one part of strawberry pulp add at least a half part of crushed sugar
3. Cook everything together several times
4. Place in vessels
5. When cooled, tie up
6. One can also add abundant sweetened apple pulp to the strawberry pulp and proceed as already described
