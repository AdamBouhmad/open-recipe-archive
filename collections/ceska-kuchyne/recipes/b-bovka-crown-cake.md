## Ingredients

- one yeast cake (magic yeast)
- half pound fresh butter
- half pound good lard
- half pound sugar
- 6 yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- two small cups milk
- flour
- quarter pound or more raisins
- salt

## Directions

1. The evening before, make a starter from one yeast cake, which should be nicely soured by morning
2. Next day take half pound fresh butter, half pound good lard, half pound sugar, mix everything well together
3. Beat in 6 yolks one at a time with constant stirring and 2 whole eggs
4. Add two small cups milk
5. Pour in the yeast starter, mix well and add flour until it is like thick porridge
6. Pepper quarter pound or more raisins, mix them into the batter and let it rise
7. Grease the form and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
8. Put the batter into it, let it rise again and bake
9. When baked, sprinkle it well with ground sugar
10. Do not forget salt, as that is understood by itself
