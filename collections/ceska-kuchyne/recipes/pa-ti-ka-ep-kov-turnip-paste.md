## Ingredients

- turnips
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Press sieved turnip mass through a sieve
2. Mix with finely powdered sugar
3. Cook until thick
4. Spread thinly onto slate tablets in a warm place
5. When dry, peel and cut, then layer on top of each other
