## Ingredients

- two chickens
- beef broth or fresh butter
- water
- new butter
- eggs
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- bread or crackers

## Directions

1. Boil two chickens in beef broth or stew on a pan in fresh butter with water added.
2. Meanwhile, prepare the dumplings as follows: Beat a spoonful of new butter in a deep plate until foamy, beat in two eggs, mix in a teaspoon of chopped fresh parsley, a little salt and pepper, and mix in enough grated bread or crumbled crackers so that dumplings the size of hazelnuts can be made from it, and cook them separately in salted water.
3. When the chickens are tender enough, remove them, strain the broth or liquid from them into a pot, and if there is not enough broth, add more, thicken it with a white roux, pour it back into the pan, place the chickens in it and let it cook.
4. Place the cooked dumplings on a platter with the chickens and pour the sauce over it.
