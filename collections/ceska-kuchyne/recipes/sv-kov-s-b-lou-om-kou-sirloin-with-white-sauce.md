## Ingredients

- sirloin
- flour (2 tablespoons)
- sour cream (3.5 deciliters)
- cranberries or preserves
- other vegetables as desired

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the sirloin
2. After it has lain for 12 hours, roast it
3. Cut into pieces
4. Add 2 tablespoons flour to the sauce and braise for a quarter hour
5. Strain, add 3.5 deciliters of good sour cream
6. Stir for 5 minutes and pour over the roast
7. Arrange cranberries or preserves and other vegetables around the roast
