## Ingredients

- bilberries
- sugar, 1 kilogram per 2 liters of bilberries
- cinnamon, a few pieces
- apples, 20 of good quality
- or sweet pears instead of apples

## Directions

1. Sort through and clean the bilberries, scald them, and drain the water.
2. Add 1 kilogram of sugar and a few pieces of cinnamon per 2 liters of bilberries.
3. Boil the mixture, skim off the foam, and when the individual berries begin to burst, remove from heat and cover with half the syrup.
4. Cut 20 apples of good quality into halves, remove the seeds, place in the remaining half of syrup, sprinkle with sugar, and cook until dissolved.
5. Combine the bilberries with the apples, mix well, place in glazed containers, and store sealed.
6. Instead of apples, sweet pears may be used.
