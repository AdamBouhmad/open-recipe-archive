## Ingredients

- Pork tenderloins
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs
- Sage powder
- Lard

## Directions

1. Boil pork tenderloins thoroughly
2. Remove sinews and veins
3. Cut into 4 pieces by slicing lengthwise and then crosswise
4. Pound well with a wooden mallet until nicely flattened
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Dust with flour
7. Dip in beaten egg
8. Coat in breadcrumbs mixed with sage powder
9. Fry in hot lard
