## Ingredients

- approximately two tablespoons flour
- butter
- onions, cut into small pieces
- good meaty broth
- caraway seeds (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the flour in butter
2. Add the finely chopped onions and fry everything together until reddish, stirring constantly
3. Add good meaty broth and simmer everything together, stirring constantly
4. Optionally add caraway seeds to the sauce
5. Strain the cooked sauce through a sieve
