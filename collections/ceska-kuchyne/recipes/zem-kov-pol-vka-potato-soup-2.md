## Ingredients

- peeled potatoes
- water
- butter
- salt
- onion
- vegetable roots
- 2 egg yolks
- fried bread roll

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes, cut into pieces and cook, drain the water; mash the potatoes, add butter, salt and pour water in which onion and vegetable roots were cooked
2. Boil the soup for a quarter hour, add 2 egg yolks and pour over fried sliced bread roll
