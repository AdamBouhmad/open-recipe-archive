## Ingredients

- young fresh mushrooms
- cold water
- vinegar
- onion
- peppercorns
- spices
- salt
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the stem off young fresh mushrooms and place them in cold water.
2. Pour vinegar into a pot and add one onion sliced thin, several peppercorns, and spices.
3. Salt it and bring to a boil.
4. Add the mushrooms and let them boil for five minutes.
5. Remove the mushrooms and let them drain.
6. Pack them into bottles and pour cooled wine vinegar over them in which you have boiled several peppercorns and several cloves.
7. Screw tightly and store in a cool, dry place.
