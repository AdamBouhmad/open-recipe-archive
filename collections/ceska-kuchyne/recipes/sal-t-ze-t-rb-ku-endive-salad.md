## Ingredients

- endive
- powdered sugar
- vinegar
- olive oil
- salt

## Directions

1. In autumn endive is most beautiful; remove all the green leaves from it, rinse the yellow ones well in cold water and let them drain well
2. Put it on a platter, sprinkle with powdered sugar, mix several tablespoons of wine vinegar with olive oil and a little salt
3. Pour this over the endive leaves and mix well, then arrange on a platter
