## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef from the hindquarters
- one beef kidney fat
- one onion, grated
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- half lard
- half butter

## Directions

1. Chop two pounds of beef from the hindquarters finely, preferably with a meat grinder. In cities you can get it chopped from the butcher, but usually those are various scraps, whereas when you chop it yourself, you make ground meat from whichever part you wish.
2. You can add a piece of beef kidney fat and one grated onion.
3. Salt and pepper it and knead well with your hands, then make small patties about the size of a finger and coat in flour.
4. Put half lard and half butter in a pan and when it begins to brown, place the patties in the fat and let them fry nicely on both sides until golden brown.
