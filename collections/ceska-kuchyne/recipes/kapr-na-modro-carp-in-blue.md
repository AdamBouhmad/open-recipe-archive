## Ingredients

- Carp
- Good wine vinegar
- Water
- Onion
- Carrot
- Parsley
- Celery
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- New spices
- Pepper
- Piece of ginger
- Salt

## Directions

1. Tear open the carp and don't scrape it (usually a fresh, scaleless carp is taken), remove all blood, cut into neat pieces, arrange on a baking sheet, salt it.
2. Cook good wine vinegar with water, and when the mixture is boiling, pour it over the fish.
3. In the meantime, put vinegar with water on a baking sheet, cut onion into slices, add a nice carrot also cut into slices, parsley and celery, bay leaf and thyme, new spices, pepper, a piece of whole ginger.
4. Neatly arrange the fish in this, cook it, carefully place on a platter, arrange decoration around it from the vegetables which are cooked together.
