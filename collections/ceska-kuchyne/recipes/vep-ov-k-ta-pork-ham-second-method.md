## Ingredients

- pork ham
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- parsley root
- bay leaf
- thyme
- pepper
- new spices
- lemon zest
- capers

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from the ham.
2. Slice the onion, carrot, celery, add parsley root, bay leaf, thyme, pepper, new spices, lemon zest, and capers.
3. Pour everything onto a baking pan and let the ham lie in it for a full day.
4. On the second day, roast it.
5. When roasted, slice it and make a brown sauce; place it in the sauce in which the ham was roasted, and braise for another 10 minutes.
