## Ingredients

- 2 quarts oysters
- white pepper
- nutmeg
- 2 pieces whole flowers
- bay leaf
- paprika
- 2 ounces butter
- 1 quart beef broth
- 3 pints cooked milk
- 1 pint raw cream
- 1/4 pound butter in small pieces
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Put 2 quarts of oysters into a pot with a pinch of white pepper, a pinch of nutmeg, 2 pieces of whole flowers, a bay leaf, a pinch of paprika, 2 ounces butter, and 1 quart beef broth
2. Let boil for 10 minutes, then strain through a sieve
3. Make a light butter roux and strain the broth into it
4. Add 3 pints of cooked milk
5. Chop the oysters finely and add to the soup
6. While stirring constantly, let boil for another 10 minutes
7. Push through a fine sieve
8. Finally stir in 1 pint of raw cream and 1/4 pound butter in small pieces
9. Pour into a tureen and serve with fried bread cubes on a separate plate
