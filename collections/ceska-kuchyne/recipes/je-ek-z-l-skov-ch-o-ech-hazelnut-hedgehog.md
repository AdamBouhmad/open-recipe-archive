## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons rice
- 3/4 liter water
- half kilogram hazelnuts with shells
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites for snow
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch 2 tablespoons rice and cook in 3/4 liter water, pass through a sieve
2. Weigh half kilogram hazelnuts with shells, crush, roast until the shell comes off
3. Crush the nuts, mix with rice, 12 dekagrams sugar, lemon zest, 4 egg yolks and snow from 4 egg whites
4. Grease a mold with butter dough and put the mixture in the mold
