## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- half pound fresh butter
- one-quarter pound sifted sugar
- one egg
- pinch of salt
- half small cup cold water
- preserved fruit
- prepared batter (sponge cake, almond, or chocolate)

## Directions

1. Put one pound flour on a board
2. Crumble half pound fresh butter into it
3. Add one-quarter pound sifted sugar, one egg, a pinch of salt, and half small cup cold water
4. Mix well and knead thoroughly
5. Place the dough under two plates and let it rest in a cool place for at least one hour
6. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a finger
7. Line a buttered form with it
8. Bake until half done
9. Arrange some preserved fruit around the edges
10. Fill the form to two fingers from the top with prepared batter (sponge cake, almond, or chocolate)
11. Bake slowly for one hour
12. Turn out the cake carefully
13. Sprinkle with sugar
