## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram rice
- 14 dekagrams sugar (8 lots)
- 4 egg yolks, beaten
- 7 deciliters sour cream or good milk (2 tablespoons)
- salt
- grated roll crumbs

## Directions

1. Rinse 1/2 kilogram of rice in cold water, add 14 dekagrams of sugar, 4 beaten egg yolks, 7 deciliters of sour cream or good milk and a little salt
2. Mix well, pour onto a buttered, grated roll crumb-sprinkled baking sheet and bake until golden
3. Turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
