## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams (8 lots) flour
- cold milk
- 1 liter milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with cold milk
2. Boil 1 liter milk with salt
3. Pour the thin prepared batter into it and cook for a quarter hour while constantly stirring
4. Sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon and pour butter over it
