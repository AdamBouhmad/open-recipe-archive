## Ingredients

- tea
- sugar
- cream or rum or lemon juice
- egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare tea according to the previous method
2. Strain it and sweeten
3. Add either cream, rum or lemon juice
4. Beat several egg yolks with a little water
5. Pour them into the tea while constantly stirring
6. Heat over fire until it froths, but do not let it boil
7. Pour immediately into cups
