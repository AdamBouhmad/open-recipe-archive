## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt water
- wine vinegar
- lemon zest
- parsley root
- onion
- carrot
- celeriac
- thyme
- bay leaf
- pepper
- almonds or nuts
- brown roux
- burnt sugar
- white sugar
- raisins

## Directions

1. Clean and wash tongue, cook in salt water for half an hour
2. Change the water, add salt if needed, vinegar, lemon zest, parsley root, onion, carrot, celeriac, thyme, bay leaf, pepper, and almonds or nuts
3. Cook until tender, strain
4. Remove tongue, peel while warm, cut into slices
5. Mix brown roux with strained broth, add burnt and white sugar
6. Simmer and strain
7. Add sliced tongue with almonds or nuts and raisins to sauce
