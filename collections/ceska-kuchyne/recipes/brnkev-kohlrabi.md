## Ingredients

- kohlrabi leaves
- salt
- kohlrabi bulbs
- butter or beef fat
- sugar
- flower
- beef broth
- caramelized sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the finest, middle leaves in salted water.
2. Peel the kohlrabi bulbs, slice into discs, place on a baking dish with fresh butter or beef fat, a piece of sugar, flower and salt, and braise.
3. When browned, add beef broth and braise until completely soft.
4. Add pale roux with a piece of caramelized sugar, mix in the chopped cooked green leaves, and usually serve around beef.
5. If to be a standalone dish, serve with fried brain, fried calves' feet, cutlets, or any fried foods.
