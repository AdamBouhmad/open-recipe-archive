## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- six egg yolks
- sugar
- salt
- 500 grams fine flour
- 40 grams yeast
- finely chopped lemon zest
- raisins
- peeled and chopped almonds
- snow from six egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub 125 grams of butter until foamy
2. Mix with six egg yolks, sugar, and salt
3. Mix in 500 grams of fine flour and 40 grams of yeast
4. Slowly add finely chopped lemon zest, raisins, peeled and chopped almonds, and snow from six egg whites
5. Work the dough thoroughly
6. Let rise in babovka molds
7. Bake at moderate heat slowly for about three-quarters of an hour
