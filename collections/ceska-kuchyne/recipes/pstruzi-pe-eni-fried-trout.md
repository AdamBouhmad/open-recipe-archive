## Ingredients

- cleaned and gutted trout
- salt
- white pepper
- lemon juice
- good olive oil
- grated bread
- sardine sauce, crayfish sauce, or caper sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the trout, wash and dry them, cut into pieces
2. Salt and sprinkle with white pepper, then place in lemon juice
3. After half an hour, dip in good olive oil, coat in grated bread, and bake until brown
4. Serve with sardine sauce, crayfish sauce, or caper sauce
