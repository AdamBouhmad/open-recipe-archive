## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons fresh butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 quart fresh cottage cheese, well pressed
- salt
- as much groats (krupička) as possible

## Directions

1. Take 2 tablespoons of fresh butter and beat it well on a plate
2. Add 3 eggs to it and mix well
3. Then add about 1 quart of well-pressed fresh cottage cheese, salt it appropriately and mix well with the eggs and butter so that no lumps remain
4. Then add as much groats as possible so that you can make little dumplings from it
5. Make the little dumplings medium-sized so that they cook through sooner
6. Before putting them to boil, coat each one in flour and then put them in salted water to boil
7. The water must not stop boiling, otherwise they would become soggy
8. When the little dumplings start to 'dance' on the surface, take one out and with two forks tear it apart
9. If there is no raw dough in the middle, take the little dumplings out onto a plate and pour melted butter over them
