## Ingredients

- game haunch or shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon zest
- onion
- veal feet
- egg shells
- parsley
- lemon slices
- meat aspic

## Directions

1. Remove sinews from a game haunch or shoulder, salt and pepper it.
2. Add 1 part good vinegar and 2 parts water, thyme, bay leaf, a piece of lemon zest and plenty of onion.
3. Braise until the meat is completely tender.
4. Slice the meat and let it cool.
5. Add 2 or 4 veal feet to the sauce and cook until tender.
6. When almost cooked, add 2 or 3 eggshells to help it set better.
7. Tie a cloth to the legs of an inverted chair, place a dish underneath, and pour the liquid onto the cloth so it drips freely.
8. Arrange the roast on a dish decorated with green parsley and lemon slices.
9. Pour a quarter of the liquid over the roast and let the rest cool.
10. Cut the resulting aspic into cubes and arrange around the roast; part of the aspic can also be colored red.
