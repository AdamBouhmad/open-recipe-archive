## Ingredients

- morning warm milk
- cream
- rennet
- salt
- beer, vinegar, and saffron (optional for color)

## Directions

1. Take morning warm milk, add cream to it, heat it, and curdle with rennet.
2. The cheese is broken, placed in clean cloth into a mold.
3. Cheeses in cloth are placed in layers into the mold, covered, frequently turned, salted; after four days they are turned out, wrapped in clean linen cloth, and placed in a cellar to ripen.
4. As needed the cheese is flipped, salted, and if it is to have a nice color, it is brushed with beer, vinegar, and dissolved saffron.
