## Ingredients

- eight tablespoons of fresh butter
- six tablespoons of fine sugar
- lemon zest
- twelve egg yolks
- twelve tablespoons of white flour
- a little salt
- a piece of pressed yeast
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat eight tablespoons of fresh butter for half an hour, add to it six tablespoons of fine sugar, grated lemon zest, twelve egg yolks and twelve tablespoons of white flour, namely add always one egg yolk and one tablespoon of flour, beat well each time and continue until you use everything up.
2. Then add a little salt and a piece of pressed yeast, work the dough well, then gradually add the stiff snow from three egg whites.
3. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with grated bread, put the prepared dough into it, let it rise and then bake in the oven for an hour slowly.
