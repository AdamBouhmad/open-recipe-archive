## Ingredients

- one quart of warmed flour
- half a coffeepot of rubbed butter
- four egg yolks
- a bit of salt
- finely grated lemon peel
- ground cloves
- a piece of pressed yeast dissolved in lukewarm cream
- two tablespoons of sultanas

## Directions

1. Put the warmed flour on a board, add the rubbed butter, egg yolks, salt, lemon peel, and cloves
2. Mix in a piece of pressed yeast that has been dissolved in enough lukewarm cream to make a thick dough
3. Knead thoroughly until the dough no longer sticks to the board or spoon
4. Add the sultanas, dust with flour, and let rise in a warm place
5. Turn the dough onto a flour-dusted board, cut into pieces, and shape into small elongated forms (šišky)
6. Let them rise again
7. Fry in heated clarified butter or lard, using enough fat so the šišky do not touch the bottom
8. When golden brown on both sides, remove from the pan and coat in powdered sugar
