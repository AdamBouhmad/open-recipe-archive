## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 pound good cottage cheese
- salt
- grated bread roll
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat a piece of fresh butter, beat 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into it
2. Add 1 pound of good cottage cheese, salt and mix well
3. Then add as much grated bread roll and a little flour so that dumplings can be made from it, mix well again
4. Make round dumplings with your hands, boil them in salted boiling water
