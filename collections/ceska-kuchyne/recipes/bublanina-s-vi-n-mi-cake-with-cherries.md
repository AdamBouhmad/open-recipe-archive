## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons fresh butter
- 4 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar with a piece of crushed vanilla
- 2 cups white flour
- 1 cup good cream
- salt
- large cherries

## Directions

1. Rub 2 tablespoons fresh butter until it foams
2. Add to it 4 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs, 4 tablespoons sugar with a piece of crushed vanilla and mix everything well
3. Then mix in 2 cups white flour, 1 cup good cream, salt slightly, and beat well
4. Grease a baking dish with butter, put half the batter into it and on it a layer of beautiful large cherries
5. Sprinkle them with sugar and on top add again the second half of the batter
6. Put it now into a hot oven and let it bake slowly for about an hour
