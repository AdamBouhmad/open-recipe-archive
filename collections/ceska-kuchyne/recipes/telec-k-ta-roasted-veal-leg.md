## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Salt
- Fresh butter
- Water
- Bread roll
- Caraway or sage

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from the veal leg, bones may be removed, rinse it thoroughly, preferably in soft water
2. If from an older calf, pound it, salt it, grease with fresh butter, add water and roast in the oven on a baking sheet until golden
3. Or bake the nearly cooked leg, grease with butter and sprinkle with grated bread roll, then finish baking
4. Or grease with butter and sprinkle with caraway, or grease with butter and lay four sprigs of sage underneath
