## Ingredients

- semolina
- milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon zest
- almonds
- salt
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook semolina in milk and let cool
2. To beaten butter add egg yolks, sugar, lemon zest, chopped almonds, several spoonfuls of salt, semolina and snow from 8 egg whites
3. Put everything in a greased mold and bake for a quarter hour
