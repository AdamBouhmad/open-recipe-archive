## Ingredients

- goose breasts with breastbone
- bread, grated
- onion
- goose fat
- flour
- spice
- pepper
- marjoram
- garlic
- eggs
- beef broth
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the breasts with the breastbone, remove the meat from the bone and chop finely
2. Add grated bread, onion browned in goose fat, goose fat, flour, spice, pepper, marjoram, and garlic
3. Mix everything well on a platter and add two eggs
4. Place the mixture on the breastbone, put this on a baking pan, add beef broth and bake the breasts
5. Either serve whole or cut into pieces
6. Cabbage is usually served with them
