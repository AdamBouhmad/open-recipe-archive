## Ingredients

- mushrooms, cut into slices
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- sardines
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut mushrooms into slices
2. Braise in oil
3. When cooled, drizzle with vinegar
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Surround with sardines and capers
