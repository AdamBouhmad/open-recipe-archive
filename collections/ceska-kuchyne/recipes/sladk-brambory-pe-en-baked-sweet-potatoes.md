## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well, put them in the oven to bake and leave them in the oven according to size—about half an hour for small ones if the oven is nice and hot, and up to an hour for large ones
2. When they are soft enough to be mashed in the hand, they are baked
3. Serve with butter, salt and pepper
