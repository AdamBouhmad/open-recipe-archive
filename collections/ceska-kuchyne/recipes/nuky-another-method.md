## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Broth, defatted
- Sour cream
- Flowers
- Salt

## Directions

1. Dilute 15 eggs in 3¾ deciliters of broth from which all fat has been removed, add a little sour cream, flowers and salt
2. Mix everything well and place the bowl in boiling water, then add the nuky to the broth
