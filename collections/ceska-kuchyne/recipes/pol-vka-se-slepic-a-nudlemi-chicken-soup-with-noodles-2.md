## Ingredients

- 1 older chicken
- 2 quarts cold water
- salt
- celery
- cabbage
- parsley root
- peas, small handful

## Directions

1. Clean an older chicken, put in a pot, pour 2 quarts cold water over, salt and let simmer, skim cleanly, then add some vegetables such as celery, cabbage, parsley root and a small handful of peas
