## Ingredients

- White beans
- Salted water
- A piece of garlic
- A little marjoram
- Salt
- A little pepper
- A piece of celery
- Parsley
- Pink roux (růžovou jíškou)
- Fried bread or bread

## Directions

1. Cook white beans in salted water until completely soft
2. Add a piece of garlic, a little marjoram, salt, a little pepper, a piece of celery, and parsley
3. When everything has cooked, brown with pink roux
4. Strain and pour over fried bread or bread
