## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound of sweet almonds
- one ounce of bitter almonds
- three egg yolks
- three-quarters pound of powdered sugar
- ten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald and peel three-quarters pound of sweet almonds and one ounce of bitter almonds
2. pound them in a mortar with three egg yolks
3. place in a deep bowl
4. add three-quarters pound of powdered sugar and ten egg yolks
