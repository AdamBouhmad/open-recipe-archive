## Ingredients

- pork trotters
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut the trotters, wash them, dry with a cloth, salt them, roll in flour, then in egg, and then in breadcrumbs.
2. Fry in butter or lard.
