## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon fine flour
- 100 grams crayfish butter
- quarter liter good meat broth
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- crayfish tails
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry the flour in crayfish butter
2. Cook with quarter liter good meat broth
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Add lemon juice
5. Mix in finely chopped crayfish tails
6. Strain the sauce and add two egg yolks
