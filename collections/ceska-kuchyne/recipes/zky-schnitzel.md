## Ingredients

- meat from leg
- salt
- pepper
- bacon
- 30 grams butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut meat into slices two fingers thick
2. Remove all sinews and fat, beat with a mallet
3. Salt and pepper, lard with bacon
4. Place on a baking dish with butter or lard
5. Braise slowly
