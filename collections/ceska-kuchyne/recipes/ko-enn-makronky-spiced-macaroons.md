## Ingredients

- sweet almonds to taste
- sugar
- vanilla
- cinnamon
- clove
- orange zest

## Directions

1. Crush sweet almonds to taste and strain through a fine sieve
2. To egg whites add sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and clove, all finely ground, and add chopped orange zest
3. Mix everything into stiff dough, spread on paper or wafers and bake gently
4. The baked macaroons can be brushed with glaze and finished in a warm oven
