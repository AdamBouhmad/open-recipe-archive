## Ingredients

- 1 liter good cream
- 8 egg yolks
- flour
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- vanilla
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 1 liter of good cream until it foams
2. Fold in 8 egg yolks, flour, 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of sugar beaten with vanilla and stiff snow from 8 egg whites
3. Pour the mixture into a greased mold
4. Bake for three quarters of an hour
