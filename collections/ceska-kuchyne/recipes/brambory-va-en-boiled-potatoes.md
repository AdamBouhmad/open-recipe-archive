## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and soak them in cold water for an hour
2. Then boil them in boiling water and cook slowly until soft, as tested with a fork
3. Drain the water carefully
4. Remove the lid from the pot, salt the potatoes and shake them so that cold air envelops them from all sides
5. Cover the pot with a lid and let it stand in a cooler place on the stove for about 5 minutes and they are ready to serve
