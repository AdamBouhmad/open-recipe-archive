## Ingredients

- six large potatoes
- approximately 1½ quarts lukewarm water
- flour, sifted, as needed
- one cup of thinned yeast

## Directions

1. Peel and cook six large potatoes
2. When cooked, drain the water and mash the potatoes into a pulp
3. Pour approximately 1½ quarts of lukewarm water over them and strain through a fine sieve
4. Mix in sifted flour until you have a thin dough and add one cup of thinned yeast
5. Let it stand and when risen, add more sifted flour to the starter and let it rise again
6. Then add more flour to make the dough firm and let it rise a third time
7. When risen, roll out small loaves, place on baking sheets, let them rise again, and when risen, bake
