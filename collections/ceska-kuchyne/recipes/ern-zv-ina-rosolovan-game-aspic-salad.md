## Ingredients

- game meat
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- thyme
- bay leaf
- lemon zest
- onion
- veal feet
- egg shells

## Directions

1. Prepare the game the same way as with aspic, only cut the meat into cubes or pieces, mix well and let cool.
