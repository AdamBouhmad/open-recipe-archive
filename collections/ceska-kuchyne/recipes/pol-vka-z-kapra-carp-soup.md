## Ingredients

- carp
- celery
- parsley
- leek
- water
- salt
- garlic
- majoram
- lemon peel
- clove
- ginger
- pepper
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Skin several pieces of carp and remove the bones, set the meat aside
2. Put the trimmings into a pot with half a celery, one parsley, a leek, pour water over it, salt it and let it cook
3. Meanwhile, chop the meat finely, add about four cloves of grated garlic, a little majoram, a little lemon peel, a little crushed clove, a little ginger and a little pepper
4. Put this into a clean pot, strain the broth in which the trimmings were cooked and let it simmer briefly
5. Thicken it with a brown roux and let it simmer a little more
6. Pour the broth over fried bread roll and serve it
