## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram melted butter
- 1/4 kilogram finely ground sugar
- three eggs and three yolks
- finely ground almonds
- 1/4 kilogram fine flour
- a little pleasant liqueur

## Directions

1. Rub the butter, add the finely ground sugar
2. Add three eggs and three yolks, mix until foaming
3. Add finely ground almonds, flour, and a little liqueur
4. Proceed as with previous cookies
