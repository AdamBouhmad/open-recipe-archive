## Ingredients

- white fish
- salt
- grated white bread
- milk
- butter
- walnut
- pepper
- corn flour
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the white fish well, wipe it with a cloth and salt it inside.
2. Make a stuffing from grated white bread soaked in milk, add a piece of butter the size of a walnut, salt and pepper it.
3. Stuff the white fish and sew it shut.
4. Sprinkle with corn flour, place on a baking pan, pour with water and bake.
5. During baking baste with the juice from the pan.
6. Bake for about an hour. Lake trout or pike are baked similarly.
