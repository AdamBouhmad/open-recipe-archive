## Ingredients

- Woodcock (2-3 per person)
- Butter
- Juniper berries, crushed
- Red wine
- Stock
- Bread, fried
- Puff pastry
- Truffles
- Goose liver

## Directions

1. Pluck the woodcock and roast them in butter
2. Add crushed juniper berries and red wine
3. Let the roasted woodcock cool, then cut off the breasts whole
4. Pound everything else except the head and stomach in a mortar
5. Pour in stock and some red wine, press through a sieve
6. Cook down into a thick sauce and place the reserved breasts in it to heat through
7. Arrange the salmi artfully on a dish and garnish with fried bread or puff pastry around the edges
8. Also garnish with truffles or braised goose liver
