## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams medium groats
- water
- butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- grated bread roll
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the groats in water with a piece of butter.
2. Let cool.
3. Add butter, 4 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks, grated bread roll, salt, and nutmeg.
4. Form dumplings.
5. Boil in broth.
