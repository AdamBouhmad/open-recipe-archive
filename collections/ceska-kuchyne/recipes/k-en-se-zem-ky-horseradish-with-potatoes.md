## Ingredients

- eight to ten starchy potatoes
- same amount of grated horseradish
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil eight to ten starchy potatoes
2. Peel and let cool
3. Press through a sieve
4. Mix with an equal amount of grated horseradish
5. Add oil and vinegar
6. Serve with beef
