## Ingredients

- 1 liter beer
- 80 grams sugar
- lemon peel finely chopped
- piece of butter
- salt
- caraway
- ginger
- grated bread
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add 80 grams sugar, finely chopped lemon peel, a piece of butter, salt, caraway, ginger, and grated bread to a liter of beer
2. Simmer until everything dissolves
3. Strain
4. Add 2 egg yolks
