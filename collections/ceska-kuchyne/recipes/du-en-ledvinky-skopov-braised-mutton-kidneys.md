## Ingredients

- mutton kidneys
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- glass of wine

## Directions

1. Soak kidneys in water and remove fat
2. Lard them, season with pepper and salt
3. Braise in butter
4. Cook sauce with lemon zest, lemon juice, and a glass of wine
5. Add kidneys to the sauce
