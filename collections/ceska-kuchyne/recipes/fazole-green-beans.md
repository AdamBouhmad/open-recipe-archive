## Ingredients

- Bean kernels
- Fresh butter
- Sugar
- Flour
- Sweet sour cream
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. While the bean kernels are so soft that they can be mashed between the fingers, shell them from the pods.
2. On the stove melt a piece of fresh butter with a little sugar, add the beans and let them stew covered.
3. After about twenty minutes, make a thin roux with flour, pour in sweet sour cream (hot rather than cold) and beef broth and let them stew a little more until the liquid is absorbed.
4. The beans should not be watery.
5. For two cups of beans, three spoonfuls of sour cream and one spoonful of broth are sufficient.
