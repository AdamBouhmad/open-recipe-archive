## Ingredients

- cleaned and drawn chicken
- salt
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- carrot
- pepper
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Clean and draw the chicken well, wash, salt, and truss it
2. Place a piece of butter on the breast
3. Place it in a pan just large enough to hold it
4. Add onion cut into slices, parsley, carrot, several peppercorns, and a bay leaf
