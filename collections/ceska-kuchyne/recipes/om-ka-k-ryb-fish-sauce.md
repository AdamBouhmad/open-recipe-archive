## Ingredients

- one spoonful of chopped parsley
- egg yolks from three eggs
- red pepper
- half a teaspoon of salt
- one spoon of vinegar
- half a cup of fresh heated butter
- one spoon of finely chopped small pickles

## Directions

1. Place one spoonful of chopped parsley in a bowl and pound it into a paste
2. Add egg yolks from three eggs and mix well
3. Season with red pepper
4. Add half a teaspoon of salt
5. Thin with one spoon of vinegar
6. Gradually mix in half a cup of fresh heated butter
7. Add one spoon of finely chopped small pickles
