## Ingredients

- 8-10 egg whites
- juice from one lemon or orange
- 8-10 tablespoons ground sugar

## Directions

1. Make stiff snow from 8-10 egg whites
2. Beat in lemon or orange juice
3. Add 8-10 tablespoons ground sugar equal to number of whites used
4. Mix well
5. Clean and wax baking sheet
6. Place small pieces on sheet
7. Bake
