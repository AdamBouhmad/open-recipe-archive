## Ingredients

- Hare front quarter meat
- 2 pounds of fatter pork meat
- pork fat or 6 ounces of scraped bacon
- bread roll soaked in wine and well squeezed
- half a grated bread roll
- one or two eggs
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cut the raw hare front quarter meat with 2 pounds of fatter pork meat.
2. If the pork meat is not fatty, add 6 ounces of scraped bacon, then one bread roll soaked in wine and well squeezed and half a grated bread roll, one or two eggs, necessary salt, pepper and a little finely chopped lemon zest.
3. Mix everything well together and form into a small hare shape.
4. Lard it with bacon, place on a baking sheet lined with butter and onion cut into slices.
5. Bake with frequent basting.
6. Alternatively, the minced meat can be covered with sour cream, in which case the lemon zest must be omitted.
