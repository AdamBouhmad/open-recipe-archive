## Ingredients

- bass
- salt
- butter
- onion
- bay leaves
- rosemary
- cloves
- celery flower
- peppercorns
- pea water
- wine
- vinegar
- bread crumbs
- flour
- lemon juice
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Quarter the bass, gut and salt it, place butter, onion, bay leaves, a piece of rosemary, three cloves, a piece of celery flower and several peppercorns on a pan, place the bass on it, add a little pea water, a little wine and vinegar and let it stew well covered.
2. Meanwhile place a piece of butter on a small pan, a tablespoon of grated bread, a little flour, let it foam and pour into the sauce in which the bass was stewing, and if it were too little sour, season it with a little lemon juice.
3. Then place the fish on a dish, pour the sauce under it, arrange with lemon slices and bring to table.
