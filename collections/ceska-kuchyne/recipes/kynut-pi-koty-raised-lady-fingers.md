## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- four egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 bowls flour
- one bowl good fresh sour cream
- one small ball of yeast mixed with milk
- two egg whites
- flour
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Rub 4 tablespoons of butter with four egg yolks until foamy
2. Add 2 tablespoons of sugar, 4 bowls of flour, one bowl of good fresh sour cream, and one small ball of yeast mixed with milk
3. Mix everything well together
4. Finally fold in stiff meringue from two egg whites and let the dough rise
5. Put on a surface dusted with flour, roll out with a rolling pin and cut out lady fingers with a mold
6. Place on a baking sheet rubbed with butter
7. Let rise
8. Brush with beaten eggs
9. Sprinkle with finely pounded sugar and bake in the oven
10. Remove and arrange on a dish when cooled
