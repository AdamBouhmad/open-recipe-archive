## Ingredients

- Three-quarters liter milk
- Quarter liter cream
- Six ounces grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Place three-quarters liter milk, quarter liter cream and six ounces grated chocolate in a pan
2. Heat and constantly stir
3. Let it cool
4. Proceed as with other ice creams
