## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Anchovies, one-quarter kilogram
- Butter
- Salt
- Lemon, optional

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from the veal leg and cut into pieces.
2. Remove bones from the anchovies, blend with butter and strain over the meat placed on a baking pan.
3. Braise the meat, basting occasionally so it does not dry out, braise for at most one and a half hours and serve either in the baking pan or on a dish lined with bread; if desired, add lemon cut into quarters.
