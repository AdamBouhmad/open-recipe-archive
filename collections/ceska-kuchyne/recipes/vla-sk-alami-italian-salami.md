## Ingredients

- 5 kilograms fine pork meat
- 2.5 kilograms juicy beef meat
- 2 kilograms bacon, diced
- Good red wine
- 200 grams salt
- 50-60 grams finely ground pepper
- Garlic
- Beef intestines

## Directions

1. Finely chop 5 kilograms pork and 2.5 kilograms beef meat completely
2. Place in cloth, tie firmly, and hang for 24 hours to allow the juices to drain, preventing early spoilage
3. Mix the chopped meat with 2 kilograms bacon cut into small cubes
4. Moisten with good red wine (2-3 small glasses)
5. Salt with about 200 grams salt and pepper with 50-60 grams finely ground pepper
6. Moisten again with a little (one small glass) red wine mixed with garlic pressed through a sieve
7. Mix thoroughly and work together well
8. Clean beef intestines completely and soak in wine with various spices for 24 hours
9. Stuff with the prepared meat mixture
10. Smoke and store in a cool place
