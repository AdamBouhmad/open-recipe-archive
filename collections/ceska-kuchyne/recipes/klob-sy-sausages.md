## Ingredients

- Raw pork meat
- Wine
- Cream
- Cloves
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- Sheep intestines
- Flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Remove all bones from raw pork meat; if you have a meat grinder, grind it; otherwise chop very finely
2. Mix with a little wine, cream, cloves, salt, and finely chopped breadcrumbs soaked in milk
3. Mix everything together and stuff into thin sheep intestines
4. Twist each piece in a cross, pierce with a skewer, dust lightly with flour, and fry in lard
