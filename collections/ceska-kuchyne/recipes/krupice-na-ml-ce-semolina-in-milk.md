## Ingredients

- 2 liters milk
- salt
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) almonds
- large raisins
- sugar
- butter
- semolina
- pernik (spiced cake)

## Directions

1. Strain 2 liters milk, salt and cook on a baking sheet
2. Scald almonds, peel and coarsely chop; add equal amount of large raisins with sugar and butter to milk
3. Cook everything together
4. When milk comes to a boil, sprinkle appropriate amount of semolina
5. On the plate pour hot butter over it and sprinkle with pernik
6. Fine semolina, so-called children's semolina, is best for all semolina porridges; coarser is less tasty and requires more milk
