## Ingredients

- three rolls
- boiling milk
- three tablespoons melted fresh butter
- finely chopped lemon zest
- three tablespoons fine sugar
- two tablespoons ground almonds
- six egg yolks
- one pound cherries
- wine
- sugar
- six egg whites
- butter
- grated rolls
- cherry sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the crumb of three rolls in boiling milk, place in a mortar with three tablespoons melted fresh butter and stir until it becomes smooth dough.
2. Set aside, add a bit of finely chopped lemon zest, three tablespoons fine sugar, and let it cool.
3. Then add two tablespoons ground almonds, six egg yolks, and mix well together.
4. Take one pound of cherries, remove the pits, place the cherries in a mortar with a little wine and sugar, let them cook briefly, and place on a sieve so the juice drains, as otherwise the pudding would be heavy.
5. Add the cherries to the remaining mixture, mix carefully, and fold in snow made from six egg whites.
6. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with grated rolls, and let the pudding steam cook for three-quarters of an hour.
7. Remove onto a dish and pour cherry sauce over it.
