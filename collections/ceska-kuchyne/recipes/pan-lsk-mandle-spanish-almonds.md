## Ingredients

- white sugared almonds
- sugar
- carmine dye dissolved in gum water

## Directions

1. Take almonds prepared white and sugared as previously described
2. On hot stove with constant turning, coat with carmine dye dissolved in gum water
3. When the layer has hardened and dried, pour boiled sugar again
4. Turn almonds in it until they reach the size of pigeon eggs
