## Ingredients

- wild boar meat
- salt
- bacon
- ham slices
- onion
- aromatic vegetables
- bay leaf
- water
- wine
- vinegar
- caper or cucumber sauce

## Directions

1. Salt any piece of wild boar meat.
2. Place on a baking pan and wrap with bacon and ham slices.
3. Lay down with onion and other aromatic vegetables, and bay leaf.
4. Cover with bacon, add water, wine and vinegar.
5. Braise slowly and serve with caper or cucumber sauce.
