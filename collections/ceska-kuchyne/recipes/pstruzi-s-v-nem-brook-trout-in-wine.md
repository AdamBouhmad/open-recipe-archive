## Ingredients

- Brook trout
- Shallots or onion, sliced
- Burgundy wine, one pint
- Bay leaves, several

## Directions

1. Clean, scale, salt the trout and let them lie for one hour
2. Line the bottom of a pot with sliced shallots or onion
3. Place the prepared trout on top
4. Pour one pint of Burgundy wine over them
5. Add several bay leaves
6. Let them cook for a quarter hour
7. Remove the trout to a dish
8. Pour the sauce over them and serve quickly to the table
