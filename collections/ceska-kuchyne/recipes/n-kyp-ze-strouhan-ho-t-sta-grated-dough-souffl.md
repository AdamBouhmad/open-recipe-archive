## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
- flour
- 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream
- 4 eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- egg white snow from egg whites
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter
- sugar
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar

## Directions

1. Make stiff dough from 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs with flour
2. Grate the dough
3. Boil the grated dough with 7 deciliters (2 tablespoons) cream; when it cools, add 4 eggs, 8 egg yolks, snow made from egg whites, 12 decagrams (8 lots) butter, sugar, and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
4. Mix everything well and bake in a buttered mold
5. This soufflé can be filled with stewed cherries
