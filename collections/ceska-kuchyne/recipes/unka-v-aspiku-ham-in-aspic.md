## Ingredients

- Small ham
- Meat broth or aspic
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Capers
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook the small ham and allow to cool
2. Remove the skin from the ham
3. Grease a mold with oil and place in ice
4. Pour liquid meat broth or aspic on the bottom of the mold and allow to set
5. Arrange sliced ham tastefully in the mold
6. Layer sliced hard-boiled eggs on top of the ham
7. Sprinkle everything with capers
8. Pour aspic over and allow to set completely in ice
9. To empty the mold, place it briefly in lukewarm water and turn the contents out onto a plate
