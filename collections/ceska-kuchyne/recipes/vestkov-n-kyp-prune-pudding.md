## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons melted butter or lard
- salt
- 1 cup flour
- 2 small teaspoons baking powder
- stewed prunes
- cinnamon
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs, add 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 cup milk, and 3 tablespoons melted butter or lard
2. Season with a pinch of salt and mix in 1 cup flour with 2 small teaspoons baking powder
3. Butter a metal dish and pour in half the batter
4. Layer with stewed prunes from which the pits have been removed, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
5. Pour the remaining batter over the top so the prunes are completely covered
6. Bake for about half an hour and serve with whipped cream
7. During peach season, fresh peaches may be used instead, making it even more delicious
