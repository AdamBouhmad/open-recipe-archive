## Ingredients

- one quarter pound grated bitter chocolate
- four tablespoons butter
- one pound yellow sugar
- half a small cup syrup
- half a small cup cream
- one teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Place one quarter pound grated bitter chocolate in a bowl and add four tablespoons of butter, one pound yellow sugar, half a small cup syrup, half a small cup cream, and one teaspoon vanilla extract.
2. Stir over gentle heat until everything is perfectly mixed.
3. Continue boiling until it cracks when a drop is placed in very cold or iced water.
4. Butter a flat-edged plate, pour the mixture onto it to make a layer about half an inch thick, let cool.
5. When almost cold, cut into squares.
