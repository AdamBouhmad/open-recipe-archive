## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) sugar
- lemon peel
- vanilla
- 10 spoons sour cream
- 4 spoons flour
- salt
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of sugar, lemon peel rubbed with sugar and crushed vanilla until it foams
2. Gradually add 10 spoons of sour cream, add 4 spoons of flour and a little salt
3. Fold in snow from 6 egg whites
4. Bake the mixture in a greased mold
