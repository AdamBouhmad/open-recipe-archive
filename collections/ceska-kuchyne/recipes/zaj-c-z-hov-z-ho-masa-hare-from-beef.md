## Ingredients

- beef
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare meat as in recipe 344 for meatballs
2. Shape the mixture to resemble a hare's hindquarters
3. Coat with bread crumbs and cold lard
4. Bake in the oven, then cut into pieces
