## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- celery
- good beef broth
- pepper
- cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as with paprika, only instead of all the vegetables, add celery cut into small slices to it and cook everything together.
2. Also cook a whole celery in good beef broth.
3. Place the chicken on a platter.
4. Push the celery with which the chicken was cooked onto the chicken.
5. Let the second celery cool, grate it on a grater and add it with pepper and cloves to the chicken.
