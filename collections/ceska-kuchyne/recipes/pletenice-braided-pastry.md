## Ingredients

- 2 kilograms flour
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 liter warm milk
- prepared yeast
- butter

## Directions

1. Make dough from flour, eggs, warm milk, and yeast
2. Let rise for three quarters of an hour
3. Divide in half and roll out
4. Spread thinly sliced butter on one portion and cover with the other
5. Fold in three and roll out three times
6. Divide in two portions
7. Roll out lengthwise
8. Braid together
9. Press ends together
10. Brush with egg
11. Bake at moderate heat
