## Ingredients

- milk
- butter
- semolina
- salt
- sugar
- lemon zest
- 6 egg whites for snow

## Directions

1. Cook semolina in milk with butter until a thick porridge forms
2. Spread out and let cool
3. Add salt, sugar, lemon zest and snow from 6 egg whites
4. Put in a greased mold, place in hot water and after two hours serve with a rolled sauce
