## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram sugar
- Zest of several lemons
- Juice of 8-10 lemons
- 3 bottles good wine
- 1 bottle arrack
- 1.5 bottles rum

## Directions

1. Grate lemon zest over 1 kilogram of sugar
2. Chop the sugar and place on a plate
3. Pour juice from 8-10 lemons over it
4. Add 3 bottles of good wine, 1 bottle of arrack, and 1.5 bottles of rum
5. Mix everything together and let it rest for several hours
6. Serve on the table
