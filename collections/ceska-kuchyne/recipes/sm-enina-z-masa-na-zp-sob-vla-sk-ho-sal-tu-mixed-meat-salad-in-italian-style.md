## Ingredients

- leftover veal, venison, and beef (roasted and boiled)
- sardines
- capers
- sour cucumbers
- salt
- oil
- wine vinegar
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs
- mushrooms in vinegar
- smoked tongue or sardines

## Directions

1. Remove bones and membranes from the leftover meat and cut into pieces
2. Add soaked sardines without bones, several tablespoons of capers, and sour cucumbers cut into cubes
3. Mix everything well
4. Season with salt, oil, wine vinegar, and pepper
5. Arrange on a platter and garnish with hard-boiled eggs, mushrooms in vinegar, and sliced smoked tongue or sardines
