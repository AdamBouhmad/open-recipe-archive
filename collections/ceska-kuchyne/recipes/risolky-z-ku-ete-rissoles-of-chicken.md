## Ingredients

- leftover meat from roasted or cooked chicken
- 4 spoons chopped braised mushrooms (per 2 cups meat)
- 4 spoons brown sauce (per 2 cups meat)
- 4 egg yolks (per 2 cups meat)
- salt
- pepper
- flower (seasoning)
- a little chopped green parsley
- lemon juice
- nice noodle dough
- egg white
- beaten eggs
- fat for frying
- fried green parsley

## Directions

1. Chop finely the leftover meat from roasted or cooked chicken
2. To two cups of meat add 4 spoons of chopped braised mushrooms, 4 spoons of brown sauce, 4 egg yolks, a little salt, pepper and flower
3. Heat this well with constant stirring, add a little chopped green parsley, finish with lemon juice, remove to a plate and let cool
4. From nice noodle dough, cut round leaves with a small glass or mold
5. Place a little of the mixture on each, brush the edges with egg white, cover with another leaf and press the edges
6. Place the rissoles on a floured board; then dip them in beaten eggs, fry in hot fat, arrange on a platter, garnish with fried green parsley and serve a suitable sauce separately
7. Rissoles from other meat, poultry and game are prepared the same way, and various leftovers can be used very advantageously in this manner
