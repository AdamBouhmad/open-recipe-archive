## Ingredients

- one liter of sugar syrup
- two ounces of rose water
- several small glasses of alcohol

## Directions

1. Take one liter of sugar syrup, add two ounces of rose water, and let everything freeze
2. Pour several small glasses of alcohol into it, mix everything thoroughly, and serve in glasses
