## Ingredients

- sardines
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Clean the sardines and mash them with butter.
2. Place a little butter with sardines in each indentation of a muffin pan.
3. When melted, place one egg in each indentation and leave until the yolk sets.
4. Alternatively, place butter in the indentations, add eggs, and place sardine strips on top.
5. Sour cucumbers are usually served with these eggs.
