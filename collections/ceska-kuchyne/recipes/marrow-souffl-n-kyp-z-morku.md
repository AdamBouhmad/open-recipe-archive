## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram (1/4 pound) marrow
- 6 egg yolks
- flour equal to the weight of the yolks
- flower water
- salt
- 6 egg whites
- butter or lard for greasing

## Directions

1. Remove all bones and membranes from 1/8 kilogram marrow
2. Beat it until it turns white
3. Add 6 egg yolks, flour equal to the weight of the yolks, flower water and salt
4. Make stiff snow from 6 egg whites and fold in slowly
5. Place in a mold greased with butter or lard
6. Bake in the oven
