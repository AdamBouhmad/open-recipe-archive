## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters strawberries
- 0.5 liters sweet cream
- 24 grams (18 lots) crushed sugar
- zest of half a lemon, rubbed with sugar
- 0.5 small teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rub the strawberries with the sweet cream
2. Strain the mixture
3. Mix in the crushed sugar
4. Add the lemon zest rubbed with sugar and cinnamon
5. Pour into glasses
