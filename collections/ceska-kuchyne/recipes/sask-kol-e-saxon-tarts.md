## Ingredients

- 500 grams fine flour
- 100 grams fresh butter
- six egg yolks
- 90 grams sugar
- lukewarm sour cream
- 40 grams dissolved yeast

## Directions

1. From fine flour, fresh butter, egg yolks, sugar, lukewarm sour cream, and dissolved yeast, prepare dough and season with salt
2. Work the dough well and roll it out
3. Cut into thin sticks
4. Let rise on a greased baking sheet
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Bake until brown
7. While baking, brush the tarts on the stove with dissolved sugar and cinnamon
