## Ingredients

- Green nearly developed peaches with pits still not hardened
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Select green nearly developed peaches whose pits have not yet hardened and place in boiling water.
2. Remove them quickly, wipe with cloth, rinse, and prick them.
3. On the second day, place them in dissolved sugar, boil them with it, repeating this for several days, so that the syrup is boiled first and when it is boiling, the peaches are added to it.
4. When the peaches are properly candied, remove from heat and place in special containers; when cooled, bind them and place in a dry, cold location.
