## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- at least 6 beaten egg yolks
- 70 grams peeled ground almonds
- 250 grams sugar
- thin lemon zest finely cut
- a little ground cinnamon
- stiff egg white snow
- 200 grams fine flour
- butter for greasing
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 125 grams butter
2. Add at least 6 beaten egg yolks
3. Add 70 grams peeled ground almonds, 250 grams sugar, finely cut thin lemon zest, a little ground cinnamon, stiff egg white snow, and 200 grams fine flour
4. Beat and work everything together thoroughly
5. Pour into baking forms greased with butter and dusted with fine breadcrumbs
6. Bake slowly
7. Sprinkle with sugar and finely cut almonds
