## Ingredients

- 4 teaspoons flour
- 1 egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 4 teaspoons flour in a cup, beat an egg into it, salt and beat well until like thin porridge
2. Slowly pour this into boiling beef broth, stirring with a fork as it flows in and simmer about five minutes
