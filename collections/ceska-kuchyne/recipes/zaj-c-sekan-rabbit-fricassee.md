## Ingredients

- Meat from rabbit front
- Two pounds of fatter pork meat
- Lard, scraped, if the pork is not fatty
- One roll soaked in wine and well squeezed
- Half a grated roll
- One or two eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Finely chopped lemon zest
- Lard for larding
- Butter
- Onion sliced into rounds

## Directions

1. Mince the meat from the rabbit front raw with 2 pounds of fatter pork meat.
2. If the pork is not fatty, add scraped lard, then one roll soaked in wine and well squeezed, and half a grated roll, one or two eggs, necessary salt, pepper and a little finely chopped lemon zest.
3. Mix everything well, form a small rabbit from it, lard it with bacon, place on a baking pan, dot with butter and onion sliced into rounds, and roast while basting frequently.
4. The mixture may also be covered with sour cream, though in this case the lemon zest must be omitted.
