## Ingredients

- lentils
- parsley water
- butter
- marjoram
- clove
- bread
- garlic

## Directions

1. Cook lentils nicely until soft
2. Mix them
3. Pour parsley water on them and let simmer a bit
4. Strain the soup, fry it with brown roux, add a little marjoram, a little clove and pour over fried bread cubes
5. Whoever likes it can boil one or two cloves of garlic with the lentils before straining
6. It is also possible to cook grains into the strained soup and let cook until they soften
7. Lentils can also be soaked overnight in cold water and then placed in hot water for cooking
