## Ingredients

- developed but not fully ripe peaches
- water
- sugar
- apple puree

## Directions

1. Boil developed but not fully ripe peaches until soft in water
2. Remove and cool in cold water
3. Place on sieves to dry
4. Peel and place in boiled sugar and preserve together with it
5. Boil a little juice with apple puree
6. Arrange in a puree and pour over the preserved peaches
