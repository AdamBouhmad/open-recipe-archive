## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram beef topside
- ½ kilogram calf meat
- ½ kilogram ham
- 3 eggs
- butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the beef, calf meat and ham, mix with 3 eggs, butter and 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Place in greased molds and bake slowly, serve with various vegetables
