## Ingredients

- ten hard yolks
- 25 decagrams (17 lots) sugar
- 25 decagrams butter
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- 2 raw eggs
- 12 decagrams (8 lots) flour

## Directions

1. Pass hard yolks through sieve, add sugar
2. Add butter, lemon zest, raw eggs, and flour
3. Beat for a whole hour
4. Place on sheet with greased paper
5. Bake at gentle heat
6. Decorate with preserved fruit
