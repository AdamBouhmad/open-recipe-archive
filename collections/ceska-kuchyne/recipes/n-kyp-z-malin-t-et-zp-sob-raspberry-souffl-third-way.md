## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- 9 dekagrams (6 lots) peeled crushed almonds
- 9 egg whites
- 6 dekagrams (5 lots) flour
- raspberries, sugared

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with 9 egg yolks and 9 dekagrams (6 lots) of peeled crushed almonds, snow from 9 egg whites, until it foams, and 6 dekagrams (5 lots) of flour
2. Pour half the mixture into a greased mold, add a layer of sugared raspberries, then the rest of the mixture
3. Bake for 2/3 hour
