## Ingredients

- veal meat from the ribs
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- clarified butter or lard
- tomatoes (optional, for sauce)

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal meat from the ribs
2. Remove all sinews and tendons and cut so that each piece has one rib bone attached
3. Clean the bone at the end for the length of one inch with a knife
4. Beat the cutlets well
5. Salt and pepper them
6. Dust with flour
7. Dip in beaten egg
8. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
9. Fry in hot clarified butter until golden
10. You may serve them like cutlets, or you may serve with a sauce made from tomatoes in a separate dish
