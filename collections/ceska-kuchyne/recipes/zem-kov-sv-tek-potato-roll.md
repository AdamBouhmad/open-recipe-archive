## Ingredients

- three-quarters liter milk
- 6 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- heaping tablespoon wheat flour
- tablespoon potato flour

## Directions

1. Mix 6 eggs and 2 egg yolks into three-quarters liter milk, add salt, wheat flour and potato flour
2. Mix everything well and bake the roll
