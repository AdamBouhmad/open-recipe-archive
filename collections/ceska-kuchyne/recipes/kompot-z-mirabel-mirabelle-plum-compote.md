## Ingredients

- fully developed but not quite ripe mirabelle plums
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the fully developed but not quite ripe mirabelle plums
2. Prick in several places with a needle
3. Heat slowly and as soon as they begin to boil, remove them covered so they finish in steam
4. Remove and place in cold water
5. The next day when they have dried, bring them to a boil several times with boiled sugar
6. Arrange nicely on a dish and pour over with sugar boiled to the pearl stage
