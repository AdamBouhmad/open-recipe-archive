## Ingredients

- Dough (as for ordinary cakes)
- Strawberries
- Sugar
- Egg whites (alternative)

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as for ordinary cakes, roll out the dough, place on a baking sheet, make an edge and bake until pale
2. Cover the surface with strawberries that have either been poured with cooked sugar and stood for some time, or mixed with egg white foam beaten with sugar
3. Spread the strawberries across the cake and sprinkle with sugar, then bake the cake completely
4. Alternatively, instead of egg white, sour cream can be beaten and strained through a sieve onto the strawberries
5. Small cakes can be made the same way as large cakes
