## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters poppy seeds
- warm sour cream
- sugar or 2 deciliters honey
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Thoroughly crush the poppy seeds or grind in a good coffee mill
2. Mix with warm sour cream into a thick dough
3. Sweeten with sugar or honey
4. Season with cinnamon, lemon zest, and vanilla
