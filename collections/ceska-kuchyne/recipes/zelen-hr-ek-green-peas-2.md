## Ingredients

- 1 quart shelled green peas
- 1/2 cup water
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take one quart of shelled green peas and add 1/2 cup water, a little salt and a little sugar and let it cook until soft, which takes about half an hour if it is true sweet and young peas.
2. If the water boils away, add a little more, but not much at once.
3. When soft, mix in 1/2 spoonful of good butter with 1/2 spoonful of flour and blend into the peas.
