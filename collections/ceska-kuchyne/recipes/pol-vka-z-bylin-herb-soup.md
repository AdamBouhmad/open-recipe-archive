## Ingredients

- handful of chervil
- two handfuls young spinach
- four lettuce heads
- bundle of parsley
- butter
- beef broth
- vegetable roots
- toasted bread roll

## Directions

1. Sort and coarsely chop the chervil, spinach, lettuce, and parsley
2. Braise in butter
3. Add to beef broth and cook with vegetable roots
4. Remove the fat
5. Pour over toasted bread roll
