## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour
- milk
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- bitter macaroons, crushed
- lemon zest
- 7 egg yolks
- wine or vanilla sauce
- lemon foam

## Directions

1. Make fine noodles from 2 eggs and necessary flour
2. Cook in milk, butter, salt and sugar them
3. Mix in crushed bitter macaroons and lemon zest
4. Add 7 egg yolks, mix thoroughly and let cool
5. Form into balls, fry them and pour with wine or vanilla sauce or lemon foam
6. Serve to table
