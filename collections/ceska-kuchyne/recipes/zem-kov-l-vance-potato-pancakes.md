## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- butter
- several eggs
- several spoonfuls flour
- a little sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. Mash cooked potatoes
2. Add butter, eggs, flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and milk
3. Mix well to make batter
4. Fry pancakes on both sides in butter
5. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
6. Serve with sauce
