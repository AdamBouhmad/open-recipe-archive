## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter cream
- 16 grams ground cinnamon
- lemon zest finely cut
- 125 grams butter
- 375 grams sifted flour
- 90 grams ground sugar
- salt
- good yeast

## Directions

1. Heat the cream with 16 grams of ground cinnamon and finely cut lemon zest
2. While warm, strain onto 125 grams of butter and stir until completely dissolved
3. Add 375 grams of sifted flour, 90 grams of ground sugar, a little salt
4. Mix with cream and dissolved good yeast
5. Cover the dough with a warm cloth and let it rise near the stove
6. Turn out the risen dough, roll out, and make thin sticks from it
7. Brush with beaten egg yolk
8. Bake slowly in a gently heated oven
