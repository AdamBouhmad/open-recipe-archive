## Ingredients

- game pieces
- thyme
- bay leaf
- new spices
- pepper
- onion
- white wine
- beef broth or water
- vinegar
- 4 juniper berries
- salt
- 2 pieces dark gingerbread
- 2 pieces white gingerbread
- egg yolk
- burnt sugar
- dumplings with ingredients: 3 decagrams fresh butter, salt, pounded clove, 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1.5 deciliters sour cream
- flour
- bread

## Directions

1. Rinse game pieces and cook with thyme, bay leaf, new spices, pepper, onion, white wine, beef broth or water, vinegar, 4 juniper berries and salt.
2. When the game is tender, place on a dish.
3. Add 2 pieces dark and 2 pieces white gingerbread, an egg yolk and a piece of burnt sugar to the sauce.
4. Simmer and then press through onto the game.
5. For the dumplings: beat 3 decagrams fresh butter until fluffy, add a little salt, a little pounded clove, 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks.
6. Mix 1.5 deciliters sour cream with flour, alternating spoonfuls of flour and then sour cream into the butter.
7. Fry bread cut into cubes, make dough from everything and add enough flour so it does not stick to the hands.
8. Dip a spoon in boiling salted water, take a spoonful of dough with the spoon and drop into water.
9. After a quarter hour, test one dumpling to see if it is cooked.
10. Cut into quarters and arrange around the game.
