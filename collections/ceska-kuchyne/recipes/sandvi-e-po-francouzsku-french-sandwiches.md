## Ingredients

- prime ham, finely chopped
- fresh butter
- tarragon vinegar
- ground white pepper

## Directions

1. Finely chop sufficient amount of prime ham
2. Add a piece of fresh butter and several drops of tarragon vinegar
3. Season with ground white pepper and mix well
4. Spread this mixture on prepared bread slices
5. Cover with another slice
6. Press together gently
7. Cut finished sandwiches crosswise into two triangular halves and serve
