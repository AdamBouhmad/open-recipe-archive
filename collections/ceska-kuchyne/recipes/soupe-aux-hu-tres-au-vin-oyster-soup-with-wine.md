## Ingredients

- three dozen oysters with their liquid
- one glass white wine
- two quarts water, parsley water, or lean broth
- four egg yolks
- one small cup cream
- a piece of butter

## Directions

1. Let three dozen oysters with their liquid and a glass of white wine boil until they become firm
2. Remove them and place on a folded napkin to dry
3. Strain the liquid back into the pot
4. Add either two quarts of water, parsley water, or lean broth, let it boil for a while and strain again
5. Beat four egg yolks with a small cup of cream and a piece of butter, thicken the soup well with this mixture and pour into a dish over the cooked oysters
