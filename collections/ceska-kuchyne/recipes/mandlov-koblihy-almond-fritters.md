## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram almonds
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- chocolate
- 9 egg whites or 10 egg whites to form stiff meringue

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds
2. Add 1/2 kilogram sugar and chocolate
3. Fold in egg whites beaten to stiff peaks to form a moderately thick dough, adding more egg whites and sugar if needed to thin
4. Form fritters from the dough
5. Bake on a baking sheet lined with paper
6. Leave on the paper until cooled
