## Ingredients

- mutton breast
- vegetables
- salt
- marjoram
- pepper
- four cloves of garlic
- mutton fat
- flour
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the mutton breast and cook it with all vegetables and salt.
2. When cooked, cut it into neat pieces and set aside on a plate.
3. Crush a handful of marjoram and add it to the broth with pepper and four cloves of garlic, bring to a boil.
4. Add enough mutton fat to flour prepared in a roux so that it thickens.
5. Boil and strain through a sieve, add the mutton meat, leave for a while longer on the baking sheet.
6. Pour over boiled potatoes.
