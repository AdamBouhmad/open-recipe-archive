## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- salt
- Meissen apples
- red wine vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel, slice, and lightly salt the cooked potatoes
2. Peel and slice several Meissen apples into thin slices
3. Add the apple slices to the potatoes
4. Pour red wine vinegar mixed with crushed sugar over everything
5. Mix well and arrange on a platter
