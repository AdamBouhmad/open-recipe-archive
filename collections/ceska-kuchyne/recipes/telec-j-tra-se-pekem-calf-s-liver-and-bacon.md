## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 thin slices of bacon
- calf's liver
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Fry 6 to 8 thin slices of bacon
2. When done, remove to a plate and keep warm
3. In the rendered bacon fat, fry liver sliced into cutlets, which you have salted, peppered, dusted with flour and sprinkled with caraway seeds
4. When fried brown, remove it and arrange with the bacon
5. Serve hot on the table
