## Ingredients

- several beautiful apples
- brandy
- powdered sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon melted butter
- pinch of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Peel several beautiful apples, slice them into slices of the thickness of a knife back, remove the cores
2. Put the apples in a bowl, sprinkle them with a bit of brandy, dust them with powdered sugar and let them stand for half an hour
3. Pour 2 eggs into a cup, add 1 teaspoon melted butter, a pinch of salt and just enough flour to make a thin batter like for pancakes and mix well
4. Dip the apples piece by piece in this batter, fry them to golden color in heated rendered butter, arrange them on a dish and dust generously with sugar
5. You can also add a little sugar to the batter and use a few tablespoons of milk instead of butter
