## Ingredients

- cooked peeled potatoes
- vinegar
- salt
- onion
- apples
- pepper
- sugar
- oil

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked peeled potatoes directly into vinegar
2. Add salt, chopped onion, diced apples, pepper, sugar, and oil
3. Mix everything well
4. Can also be served warm by slicing potatoes into hot vinegar and mixing with the other ingredients
