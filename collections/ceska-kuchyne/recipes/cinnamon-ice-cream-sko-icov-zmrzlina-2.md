## Ingredients

- Two deca of crushed cinnamon
- Quarter kilo of crushed sugar
- One liter of sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Put two deca of crushed cinnamon and a quarter kilo of crushed sugar into one liter of sweet sour cream
2. Beat everything into foam
3. Transform into ice cream
