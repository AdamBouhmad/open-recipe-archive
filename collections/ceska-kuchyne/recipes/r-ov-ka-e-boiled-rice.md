## Ingredients

- 1 small cup rice
- cold water
- 1 quart milk
- salt
- cinnamon
- sugar
- fresh melted butter
- dried prunes, cooked and pitted

## Directions

1. Sort one small cup of rice and rinse it twice in cold water
2. Pour a quart of milk over it and let it cook with frequent stirring
3. When cooked, salt it, turn out onto a dish
4. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon and sugar and pour hot fresh melted butter over it
5. In some places, cooked and pitted dried plums are also added to rice mush
