## Ingredients

- 1 quart beer
- 1 quart water
- About 2 spoons sugar
- 1 small spoon butter
- A little nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cup sour cream or milk

## Directions

1. Boil beer with water
2. Once boiling begins, add about 2 spoons sugar, 1 small spoon butter, and grated nutmeg
3. Beat 3 egg yolks with 1 cup sour cream or milk
4. Slowly pour the egg mixture into the boiling soup, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
5. Once the soup begins to rise, remove from heat
