## Ingredients

- peeled turnips
- sugar
- butter or lard
- onion
- caraway
- salt
- flour
- wine vinegar
- roasted sausage or young pork

## Directions

1. Peel turnips, cut into slices and then into strips
2. Pour boiling water over them, cover and let sit briefly
3. In a pan, brown a teaspoon of sugar in butter or lard until golden
4. Add drained turnips, finely diced onion, caraway, and salt
5. Braise, then dust with flour and braise a bit longer
6. Pour wine vinegar over it and let it rest in warmth briefly
7. Transfer to a serving dish and serve with roasted sausage or young pork
