## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- beef broth
- morels
- cauliflower
- pepper
- marjoram
- pale gravy
- green parsley
- salt
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut potatoes into small pieces and fry with butter until half done
2. Add potatoes and morels that have been cut and braised in butter with cauliflower, pepper, and marjoram to the beef broth
3. Simmer briefly
4. Add pale gravy and chopped green parsley
5. Season with salt
6. If desired to make the soup sour, add egg yolks beaten in sour cream
