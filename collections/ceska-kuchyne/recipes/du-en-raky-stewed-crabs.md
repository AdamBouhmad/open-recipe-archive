## Ingredients

- crab meat from tails and large claws
- bread, grated, about a quarter of the meat's weight
- parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- crab shells
- butter, in small pieces
- lemon juice, one
- bread crumbs, layer
- butter, additional pieces

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the meat from the tails and large claws of the crabs
2. Chop finely and mix with about a quarter of grated bread and some finely chopped parsley
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Fill the crab shells with this mixture
5. Arrange on a baking pan
6. Dot with small pieces of butter
7. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over all
8. Sprinkle thickly with bread crumbs
9. Top with additional pieces of butter
10. Cover and let braise in the oven for about twenty minutes
