## Ingredients

- half kilogram (pound) of cleaned strawberries
- a piece of apricot preserve
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
- snow from 24 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix strawberries with a piece of apricot preserve and 12 dekagrams sugar
2. Stir everything for a whole hour
3. Gradually mix in snow from 24 egg whites while it cools
4. Put everything in a greased mold and bake slowly
