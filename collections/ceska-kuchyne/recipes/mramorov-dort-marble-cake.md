## Ingredients

- For white dough: egg whites from seven eggs, three coffee cups of sugar, one coffee cup of butter, one coffee cup of sour milk, small teaspoon of baking soda, four coffee cups of flour
- For dark dough: seven egg yolks, three coffee cups of brown sugar, one coffee cup of butter, one coffee cup of sour milk, four coffee cups of flour, small teaspoon of baking soda, one large spoonful of mixed spices, cinnamon and cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare both doughs separately
2. layer them in a greased pan by alternating one spoonful of dark dough with one spoonful of light dough until the pan is full
3. bake for one hour and a quarter
