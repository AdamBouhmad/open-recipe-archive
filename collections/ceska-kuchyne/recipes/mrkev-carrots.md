## Ingredients

- carrots
- butter
- fresh parsley
- sugar
- salt
- beef broth
- flour
- paprika

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the carrots and cut them into small sticks
2. Place on a saucepan with a piece of foamed butter, add some chopped fresh parsley, a piece of sugar, and a little salt
3. Let them stew in their own juice for a while, stirring frequently to prevent burning
4. Add a little beef broth
5. When soft, dust with flour and season with a pinch of paprika
