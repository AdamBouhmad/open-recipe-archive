## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram cleaned and prepared currants
- ½ kilogram sugar
- ¼ liter water

## Directions

1. Boil sugar in water until it threads, then add the currants
2. Cook until the berries do not burst, stirring gently
3. Remove with a spoon
4. Boil the syrup once more
5. Pour over the currants
6. Place in jars and seal well
