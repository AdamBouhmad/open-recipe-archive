## Ingredients

- strong coffee
- fine pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Brew strong coffee and strain it through fine cloth
2. Add it to fine pounded sugar
3. Boil it strongly together until everything thickens
4. Everything else is as with all other brittle pieces
