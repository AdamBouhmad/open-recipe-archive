## Ingredients

- 375 grams good butter
- about ten eggs
- 375 grams finely ground sugar
- 100 grams ground caraway
- 1/2 kilogram fine sifted flour

## Directions

1. Beat butter until foaming, slowly add eggs
2. Add finely ground sugar
3. Add ground caraway and flour
4. Knead thoroughly
5. Pour into greased torte form lined with grated bread
6. Bake quickly
