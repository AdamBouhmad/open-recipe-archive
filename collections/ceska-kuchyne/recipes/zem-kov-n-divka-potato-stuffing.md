## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- roasted sausage stuffing

## Directions

1. Peel raw potatoes, cut into cubes, wash and dry them
2. Braise with butter, parsley and onion
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Mix with roasted sausage stuffing
5. Cool completely before using for stuffing
