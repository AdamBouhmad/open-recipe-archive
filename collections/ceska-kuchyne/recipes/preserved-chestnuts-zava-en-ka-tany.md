## Ingredients

- beautiful chestnuts
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the most beautiful chestnuts, throw them into boiling water and let them cook strongly, then remove
2. In another container in boiling water finish cooking them completely
3. Completely cooked chestnuts are cleaned, peeled again, placed in lukewarm water with lemon juice
4. When cooled, rinse in water, dry and place on a dish, pour over with dissolved sugar
5. Cook together several times and let cool
6. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it more concentrated up to the sixth degree, add the chestnuts, and when they have been cooked several times, place them in containers
