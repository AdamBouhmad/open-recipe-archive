## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- half a pint of farina
- salt
- snow from four egg whites
- butter
- bread roll, grated

## Directions

1. Mix four egg yolks with half a pint of farina, salt it and mix in the snow from four egg whites
2. Grease a pan with butter, sprinkle with grated bread roll, pour the batter into it and bake in a hot oven until golden
3. Turn out onto a board, let cool, cut into square or oblong pieces and cook them in boiling good beef soup
4. This colombine gains substance only through cooking
