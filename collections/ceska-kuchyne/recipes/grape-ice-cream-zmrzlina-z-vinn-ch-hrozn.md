## Ingredients

- Fully developed but not completely ripe grapes
- Water
- One to two spoonfuls of dissolved and pressed gum tragacanth
- Sugar
- Elder flowers

## Directions

1. Pick berries from fully developed but not completely ripe grapes
2. Press them
3. Dilute slightly with water
4. Add one to two spoonfuls of dissolved and pressed gum tragacanth
5. Sweeten everything
6. Scald elder flowers and add the obtained juice to the ice cream, giving it a special nutmeg flavor
7. Proceed as with other ice creams
