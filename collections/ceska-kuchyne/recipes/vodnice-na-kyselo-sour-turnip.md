## Ingredients

- turnip
- caraway seeds
- salt
- wine vinegar
- lard or pork fat
- onion
- flour
- beef broth or water

## Directions

1. Peel the turnip and grate on a cabbage grater, place in a container with caraway seeds, salt and water mixed with wine vinegar; cook until soft.
2. When cooked, place separately on a small baking dish diced lard or pork fat with finely chopped onion and fry; add flour as needed and fry.
3. Add the greens, mix; if thick, pour beef broth or water, and cook for a while.
4. When there is no head cabbage, it is good with pork, partridges or pheasants.
