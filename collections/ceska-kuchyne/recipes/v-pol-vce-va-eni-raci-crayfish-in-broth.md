## Ingredients

- Crayfish
- Water
- Salt
- Caraway
- Parsley
- Strong broth
- Grated bread roll
- Butter
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Throw cleaned crayfish into boiling salted water to which caraway and parsley have been added.
2. Simmer for ten to twelve minutes depending on size, then remove meat from tails and claws.
3. Cover with strong broth and mix with grated bread roll, crayfish butter, and lemon slices.
4. Simmer everything once more gently and slowly, then serve immediately.
