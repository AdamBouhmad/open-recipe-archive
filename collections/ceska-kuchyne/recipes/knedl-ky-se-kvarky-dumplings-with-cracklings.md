## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 bread rolls
- 1.5 deciliters cracklings
- 3 eggs
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and put it on a baking sheet.
2. Cut 2 bread rolls and fry them.
3. Add 1.5 deciliters of cracklings with the bread roll to the flour with 3 eggs.
4. Beat the dough.
5. Drop dumplings into a pot with a spoon.
6. Grease the cooked dumplings and serve with smoked meat on a platter.
