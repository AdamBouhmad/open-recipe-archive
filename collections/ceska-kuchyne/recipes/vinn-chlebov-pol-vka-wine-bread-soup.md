## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- hard bread grated
- lemon peel
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- 1/4 liter white wine
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Boil grated hard bread in a liter of water with lemon peel
2. Season with salt and sugar, add cinnamon
3. Blend well
4. Strain and simmer again
5. Add 1/4 liter of white wine and 1 egg yolk
