## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- butter
- sage
- rolls, cut into slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Skin the eel and clean it
2. Cut it into pieces, salt it and let it rest covered for an hour
3. Put a piece of butter in a pan and place it near the fire
4. When the butter is hot, add a little sage, cover it and let it rest in a warm place for a quarter hour
5. Dry the salted eel with a cloth
6. Strain the prepared butter through a sieve into a shallow iron pan
7. Place the eel in it along with two rolls cut into slices and let both brown on both sides
8. Remove it, arrange the eel in the center of a platter
9. Surround it with the browned rolls
10. Sprinkle with lemon juice
11. Serve hot
