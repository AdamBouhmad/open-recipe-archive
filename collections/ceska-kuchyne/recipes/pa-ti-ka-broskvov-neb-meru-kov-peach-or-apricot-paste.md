## Ingredients

- ripe peaches or apricots
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Press sieved fruit mass into a container on the stove so all moisture evaporates
2. Slowly add finely powdered sugar with constant stirring
3. Cook together until completely thick
4. Pour onto slate tablets to cool
