## Ingredients

- beef tenderloin
- salt
- bacon strips
- onion
- whole ginger
- peppercorns
- allspice berries
- thyme
- bay leaf
- wine vinegar
- water
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. If you have purchased the beef, remove the skin, salt it, and pierce densely with bacon cut into small strips
2. Place on a roasting pan one onion cut into slices, a piece of whole ginger, several peppercorns and allspice berries, some thyme, and one bay leaf
3. Pour over 2 spoonfuls of wine vinegar and the same amount of water, and let it simmer for half an hour
4. Then pour the boiling vinegar mixture over the prepared meat and let it marinate for about two days, turning it every day
5. Place the meat on a baking sheet, put a piece of butter under it, roast slowly, basting with sour cream, and roast until brown
