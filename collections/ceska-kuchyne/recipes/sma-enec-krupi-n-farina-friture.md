## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two tablespoons of sugar
- a piece of fresh butter
- salt
- one cup of nice semolina
- six whole eggs

## Directions

1. In one quart of milk boil two tablespoons of sugar, add a piece of fresh butter, salt it a little and cook one cup of nice semolina into it, stir frequently and let it become very thick.
2. Remove the porridge onto a plate and when it is half cooled, mix into it six whole eggs and a piece of fresh butter.
3. Heat three tablespoons of butter on a pan, put the porridge into it, cover the pan with a lid and bake until it gets a nice crispy bottom, then turn it and bake well, mash with a spoon, smooth onto a plate, sprinkle with sugar and bring to the table.
