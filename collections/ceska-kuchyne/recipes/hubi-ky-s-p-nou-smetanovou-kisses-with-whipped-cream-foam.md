## Ingredients

- two pounds good sweet cream
- 25 decagrams sugar
- vanilla
- three tablespoons flour
- six raw egg yolks
- sugar candies or biscuits

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of good sweet cream on the stove
2. Add 25 decagrams of sugar and a piece of vanilla
3. Stir until it thickens
4. Add three tablespoons of flour and six raw egg yolks
5. Mix again and strain into a mold where the foam will remain
6. Place on ice
7. When the foam hardens, add sugar kisses or insert biscuits on the sides
