## Ingredients

- apples
- batter

## Directions

1. Peel apples, remove the core, and slice them into not too thin slices so they do not fall apart.
2. Make batter as described in the previous recipe.
3. Dip the apple slices in it and fry on both sides.
4. In the same way, prepare fritters from dried plums, which you have previously cooked and removed the pits from.
