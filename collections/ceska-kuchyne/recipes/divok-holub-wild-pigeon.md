## Ingredients

- young wild pigeon
- salt
- wine
- vinegar
- onion, sliced
- bay leaves
- piece of lemon peel
- lard, sliced into strips
- butter
- sour cream
- marinade
- pale butter roux
- sour marinade

## Directions

1. Clean young wild pigeons, pluck them, salt them, place in a clay vessel
2. Pour a little wine and vinegar over them, add sliced onion, several bay leaves, a piece of lemon peel and let them lie for at least two days
3. Remove them, dry them, lard them on the breast with lard cut into strips, place on a baking pan, put pieces of butter on them and roast them with frequent basting with sour cream and marinade
4. Make a pale butter roux, mix it with a little of the sour marinade, add the baked broth from the pigeons, a little sour cream and let it cook
5. Place the pigeons on a platter and pour the sauce over them
6. Otherwise wild pigeons and squabs can be prepared just like domestic ones
