## Ingredients

- half liter of soft clean water
- crushed almond kernels
- three-quarters liter of sugar syrup
- cherry liqueur

## Directions

1. Let crushed almond kernels soak in half liter of soft clean water in a bottle in a warm place for four hours
2. Strain the liquid, mix it with three-quarters liter of sugar syrup, and heat everything to 17°
3. Let it cool and freeze
4. Add cherry liqueur, mix everything, pour into glasses, and serve
