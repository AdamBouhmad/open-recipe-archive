## Ingredients

- walnuts with soft shells
- cold water

## Directions

1. In July, when the shell is still soft, pick the walnuts
2. Pierce them with a needle or sharp stick
3. Soak in cold water for 12-14 days, renewing the water daily
4. Then cook slowly
