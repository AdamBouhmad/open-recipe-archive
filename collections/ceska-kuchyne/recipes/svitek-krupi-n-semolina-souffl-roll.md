## Ingredients

- 75 grams butter (5 lots)
- salt
- flower
- 6 egg yolks
- semolina, as much as it can take
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat butter with salt, flower and 6 egg yolks
2. Add as much semolina as it can take
3. Make a stiff snow from 6 egg whites and mix in
4. Grease a baking pan with butter, pour the batter in and bake
5. Turn out the baked roll and let it cool
6. Meanwhile, boil milk with sugar and butter
7. Cut the roll into large cubes and pour the milk over it
