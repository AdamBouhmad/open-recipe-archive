## Ingredients

- mussels
- water
- salt
- onion
- egg yolks
- flour
- pepper
- butter
- lemon juice or lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and soak the mussels, place in a pot with cold water, salt, and several onions.
2. Boil over high heat.
3. When the shells open, remove the mussels and let the water drain.
4. Clean the animals and remove from the shells.
5. Coat them in a batter made from egg yolks, flour, salt and pepper.
6. Fry on butter, add lemon juice or fresh lemon slices.
7. If the onion that was added turns black, it is best not to eat the mussels, as it may indicate the presence of something harmful.
