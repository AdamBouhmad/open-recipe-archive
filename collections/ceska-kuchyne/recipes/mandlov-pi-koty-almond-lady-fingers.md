## Ingredients

- quarter pound almonds
- two egg whites
- 10 tablespoons fine sugar
- fourteen egg yolks
- seven egg whites
- two tablespoons white flour
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel quarter pound almonds
2. Pound them finely in a mortar with two egg whites
3. Put on a bowl and add 10 tablespoons of fine sugar
4. Mix well and add fourteen egg yolks
5. Beat this for half an hour
6. Beat seven egg whites into stiff meringue
7. Slowly fold the meringue with two tablespoons of white flour into the remaining mixture
8. Butter small oblong grooved tin molds, dust with flour, and fill with the mixture, but not completely
9. Let bake slowly in the oven
10. Sprinkle with sugar, let cool completely and serve
