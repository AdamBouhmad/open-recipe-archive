## Ingredients

- beef cutlets from the loin or tenderloin
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- vinegar
- water
- bay leaves
- sour cream
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut nice thick cutlets from the loin or tenderloin, pound them well, pierce with bacon, and dust with salt and pepper
2. Place cut bacon and onion in a saucepan, lay the cutlets on top, cover with a lid, and let braise until the expressed juice is reabsorbed
3. Add a little vinegar, a little water, and one or two bay leaves, and let braise until completely tender
4. Remove the cutlets, strain the juice, mix with sour cream, return the cutlets to the saucepan, pour the gravy over them, let simmer briefly
5. Place on a serving dish, make a wreath of roasted or fried potatoes around them, and serve
