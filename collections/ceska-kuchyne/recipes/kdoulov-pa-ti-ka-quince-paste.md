## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and cook quinces
2. Peel and press through a sieve
3. Dry the quince mass
4. Take equal amounts of sugar as mass and cook to the sixth degree
5. Mix together and cook several more times
6. Spread onto slate tablets and when dry, cut into small, oblong or square pieces
