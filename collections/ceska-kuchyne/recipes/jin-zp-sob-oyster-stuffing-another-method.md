## Ingredients

- poultry livers
- calf livers
- fatback
- anchovies
- parsley
- carrot
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop and mix together poultry livers, calf livers, fatback, anchovies, parsley and carrot
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Use for stuffing whole oysters
