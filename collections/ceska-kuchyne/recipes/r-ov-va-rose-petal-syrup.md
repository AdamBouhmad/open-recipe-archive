## Ingredients

- cleaned rose petals
- sugar cooked to the sixth degree
- hot water

## Directions

1. Place cleaned rose petals into a heated bottle
2. Pour sugar cooked to the sixth degree and still very hot over them
3. Tie the bottle opening with a bladder
4. Leave the bottle on warm stove for at least a week
5. Strain the syrup and filter it
6. Store the syrup
7. Use for making confectionery, ice cream, etc.
