## Ingredients

- pike, cleaned
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- chopped meat
- sardine butter
- grated Parmesan cheese
- bacon, thinly sliced
- broth
- flour for thickening
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the pike and split it along the backbone, removing all bones
2. Wash the meat again and place it in lemon juice seasoned with salt and pepper, along with parsley and sliced onion, for several hours
3. Remove the meat, dry it with a clean cloth
4. Fill the pike with a mixture of chopped meat, sardine butter, and grated Parmesan cheese
5. Sew the pike closed
6. Place thinly sliced bacon on the bottom of a casserole and lay the pike on top
7. Pour broth and thin flour sauce over it, add vinegar, lemon juice, and salt
8. Cook thoroughly, basting frequently with the cooking liquid during preparation
9. Serve hot with oyster sauce or another piquant sauce
