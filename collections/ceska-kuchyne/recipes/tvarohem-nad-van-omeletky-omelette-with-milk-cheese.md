## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons melted butter
- 3 teaspoons sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- flour
- 5 egg whites
- milk cheese (tvaroh), apple, or grated bread filling
- milk

## Directions

1. Put 3 teaspoons of melted butter, 3 teaspoons of sugar and 5 egg yolks in a bowl, salt it, and add enough flour to make a batter like for dripping
2. Gradually fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from 5 eggs
3. Heat a piece of butter in a pan, pour some of the batter onto it and bake an omelette on both sides
4. Spread each one with filling of either milk cheese, apple, or grated bread, fold the omelettes and place them in a buttered baking dish
5. Put the dish in a hot oven and let the omelettes bake, pour a bit of milk in which you have beaten an egg yolk over them, and let everything bake for about 10 more minutes
6. Be careful that they do not dry out too much
