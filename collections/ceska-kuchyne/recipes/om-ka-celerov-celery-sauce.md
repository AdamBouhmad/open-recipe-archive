## Ingredients

- celery
- beef broth
- roux
- salt
- pepper
- flower

## Directions

1. Clean the celery and cook it in another beef broth
2. When the celery has softened, place it in the broth in which it was cooked
3. Add roux
4. Grate the celery
5. Place it in the broth that has been browned
6. Add salt, pepper and flower
