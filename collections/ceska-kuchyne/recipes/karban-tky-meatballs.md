## Ingredients

- equal parts beef, calf and pork meat
- fat or butter
- eggs
- salt
- grated bread roll
- nutmeg
- grated zest of half a lemon
- crushed rusks
- butter for frying
- water and sour cream for sauce

## Directions

1. Mix equal quantities of beef, calf and pork meat with fat or butter, eggs, salt, grated bread roll, nutmeg and grated lemon zest
2. Form meatballs, roll in crushed rusks and fry in butter, basting with butter meanwhile
3. A little water and sour cream may be added to the sauce
