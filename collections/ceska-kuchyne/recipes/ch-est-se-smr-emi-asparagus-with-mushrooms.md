## Ingredients

- Asparagus of medium size
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- White flour
- Good meat broth
- Fresh mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean asparagus and cut into pieces about one inch long, removing all woody parts
2. Cook in salted water
3. Make a white butter roux with flour
4. Add good meat broth to make a thickened gravy
5. Add freshly cleaned mushrooms cut into small pieces to the gravy and simmer briefly
6. Arrange cooked asparagus on a platter and pour mushrooms and sauce over top
