## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram fine sifted flour, slightly warmed
- 1/2 liter lukewarm sour cream
- 50-60 grams melted yeast
- two eggs and two yolks
- 200 grams fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with warm sour cream
2. Add melted yeast, eggs, yolks, and butter
