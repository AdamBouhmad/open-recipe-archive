## Ingredients

- frog hind legs
- salt
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- herbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Salt the cleaned legs and let them lie in salt for a while, then remove the meat from the bones and chop it finely
2. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan, one finely chopped onion, a tablespoon of chopped green parsley and a little herbs, put the legs into this and let them stew uncovered until the juice they release is reabsorbed
3. Then add another piece of butter, mix it with several eggs and put on a heated platter
