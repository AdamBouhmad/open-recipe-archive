## Ingredients

- Two cups of water
- Two spoonfuls of vinegar
- Salt
- Pinch of sugar
- Two or three spoonfuls of chopped fresh dill
- One cup of good thick sour cream
- One spoonful of flour
- Five or six eggs
- Boiled beef

## Directions

1. Bring two cups of water to boil with two spoonfuls of vinegar, salt, a pinch of sugar, and two or three spoonfuls of chopped fresh dill
2. Take one cup of good thick sour cream and one spoonful of flour and mix them well together
3. Pour the cream mixture into the boiling water and let it come to a boil
4. Drop five or six eggs into it and when they are set, serve with boiled beef
