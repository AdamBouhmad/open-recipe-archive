## Ingredients

- red cabbage head
- hot water or vinegar
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel a nice head of red cabbage and remove the stem
2. Cut into fine thin strips
3. Either pour boiling water or vinegar over, or boil for a few minutes
4. Drain the water
5. Dress with vinegar and oil
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. When completely cooled, arrange nicely on dishes
