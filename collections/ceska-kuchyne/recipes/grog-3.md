## Ingredients

- water
- sugar
- good rum or arack
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. To a quart of boiling water, dissolve three-quarters of a pound of sugar
2. Add a pint of good rum or arack
3. Put on fire and when hot enough, pour into glasses
4. You can add a little lemon juice if desired
5. If the grog is too strong, you can add a little hot water
