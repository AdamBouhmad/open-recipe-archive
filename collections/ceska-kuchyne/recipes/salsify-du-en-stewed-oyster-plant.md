## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- cold water
- salt
- milk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots and place in cold water to prevent them from losing color
2. When you have scraped the desired amount, slice into rounds about half an inch thick
3. Pour water over them and cook until tender
4. When cooked, strain the water and pour milk over them, salt and let cook for a while
5. Take one spoon of butter and one spoon of flour, mix well and add to the salsify
