## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- 125 grams fresh melted butter
- 70 grams finely ground sugar
- two teaspoons ground and sifted cinnamon
- good white wine

## Directions

1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, and cinnamon
2. Mix with white wine to form dough
3. Knead thoroughly, roll out
4. Shape cookies, brush with white, sprinkle with sugar
5. Bake slowly on greased sheet
