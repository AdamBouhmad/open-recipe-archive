## Ingredients

- vanilla chocolate
- water or milk and cream
- sugar
- egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. For the first method: Pour a pound of grated vanilla chocolate into a quart of boiling water
2. Let it dissolve completely with frequent stirring
3. Pour into cups and serve with lump sugar and hot whipped cream
4. For the second method: Boil half a pound of grated chocolate in a pint of milk or cream until completely dissolved
5. Add another pint of cream
6. Whip well and continue heating with constant stirring
7. Pour into cups
8. You can also beat a few egg yolks and add them
9. You can also place a teaspoon of whipped cream in each cup
