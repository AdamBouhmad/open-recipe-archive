## Ingredients

- Fish meat leftovers
- 2 bread rolls
- Milk
- 4 eggs
- Flour
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Finely chop the fish meat leftovers.
2. Soak 2 bread rolls in milk.
3. On a baking sheet, beat 4 eggs for a long time until a thick paste forms.
4. When cooled, crush the meat with the bread rolls, flour and salt in a mortar.
5. Press through a sieve.
6. Fry the dumplings or small flat cakes in butter.
