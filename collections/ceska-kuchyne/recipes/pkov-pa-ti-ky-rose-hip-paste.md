## Ingredients

- ripe rose hips
- apple mass
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut ripe rose hips in half
2. Remove the seeds and clean the hips
3. Place in a container and let soften completely, stirring several times daily
4. When completely soft, press through a fine sieve
5. Mix the mass with one third apple mass
6. For the same amount of mass, cook the same amount of sugar
7. Mix together and cook thoroughly until completely thick
8. Spread onto slate tablets and when dry, cut and peel
9. Store in containers between paper
