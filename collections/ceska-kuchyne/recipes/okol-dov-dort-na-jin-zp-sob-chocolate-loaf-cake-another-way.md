## Ingredients

- half coffee cup of sweet milk
- one beaten egg yolk
- half coffee cup of grated chocolate
- one coffee cup of sugar
- one heaping spoonful of butter
- half coffee cup of milk
- two coffee cups of sifted flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- one teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the sweet milk, beaten egg yolk, and grated chocolate
2. place on the stove and cook while stirring constantly until thickened
3. remove from heat and let cool
4. add the sugar, butter, milk, and sifted flour with baking powder
5. add the vanilla and mix well
6. pour into four metal plates and bake over moderate heat
7. when baked and cooled, place one coffee cup of fine sugar with two spoonfuls of water on the stove
8. let cook briefly until it drips from the spoon like threads
9. pour onto one beaten egg white while stirring constantly
10. coat each layer and let dry slightly, then stack them together
