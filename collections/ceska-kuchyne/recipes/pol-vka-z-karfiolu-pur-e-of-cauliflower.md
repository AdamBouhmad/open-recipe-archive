## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- four ounces of butter
- pepper
- nutmeg
- four ounces of pale butter roux
- two quarts of beef broth
- pint or more of boiling sour cream
- two ounces of butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean cauliflower, prepare it, divide into small florets and let boil ten minutes in salted water
2. Drain, put in a saucepan with four ounces of butter, salt, pepper, season with nutmeg, cover and let stew until completely soft
3. Press through a strainer, thicken with four ounces of very pale butter roux, add two quarts of beef broth, mix well, let boil ten more minutes and then press through a fine sieve
4. Put back in a pot, let come to a boil with constant stirring, add a pint or more of boiling sour cream, two ounces of butter and if desired a spoonful of sugar
5. Mix well and pour into a bowl over sliced bread fried pale in butter and small cauliflower florets that were saved for this purpose
