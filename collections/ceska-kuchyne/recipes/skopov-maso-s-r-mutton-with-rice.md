## Ingredients

- mutton
- vegetables
- rice
- broth from mutton

## Directions

1. Boil mutton with all vegetables
2. Meanwhile wash rice and pour a little mutton broth over it
3. Braise rice, gradually adding broth until rice is tender
4. Cut mutton into pieces, place on platter
5. Arrange rice on top
