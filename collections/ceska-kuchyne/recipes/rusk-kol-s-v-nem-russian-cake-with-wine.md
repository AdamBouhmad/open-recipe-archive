## Ingredients

- 1 liter sour cream
- 1/2 kilogram ground sugar
- wine
- 4 eggs
- juice and lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- 1 kilogram flour
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Make cold dough from sour cream, sugar, wine, eggs, juice, lemon zest, and flour
2. Knead slightly and let rest in cool place for 2 hours
3. Form into large square cake
4. Brush with wine, sprinkle with sugar and chopped almonds
