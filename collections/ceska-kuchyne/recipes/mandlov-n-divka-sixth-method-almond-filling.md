## Ingredients

- 10 dek finely chopped almonds
- 3 eggs
- 10 dek sugar
- 7 dek raisins
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Mix finely chopped almonds, eggs, and sugar
2. Add raisins
3. Add finely crushed cinnamon and cloves
