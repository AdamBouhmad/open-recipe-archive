## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one quart buttermilk
- small teaspoon baking soda dissolved in hot water
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs and add one quart of buttermilk to them
2. Add a small teaspoon of baking soda dissolved in hot water and a little salt
3. Add enough flour to make it thick, about like a batter
4. Fry on lard and serve with honey or syrup on the table
