## Ingredients

- fresh, closed mussels
- wine
- fresh butter
- flour (optional)
- lemon juice
- horseradish or lemon

## Directions

1. Dry fresh, still-closed mussels with a cloth, place on a baking sheet with wine and good fresh butter.
2. If desired, a little flour can be added.
3. Braise until they open.
4. Arrange on a serving dish, pour over the juice, add lemon juice, and serve either with horseradish or lemon.
