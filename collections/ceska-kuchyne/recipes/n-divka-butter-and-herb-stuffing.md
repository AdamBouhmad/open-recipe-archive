## Ingredients

- 48 grams butter
- salt
- flower
- one whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- chives or parsley
- sugar
- sweet cream or milk
- grated or milk-soaked bread roll

## Directions

1. Beat butter with salt and flower
2. Add one whole egg with 2 egg yolks
3. Add chopped chives or parsley and sugar if desired
4. Add a little sweet cream or milk
5. Add enough grated or milk-soaked bread roll to form a thick paste
6. Use for stuffing veal breast or squabs
