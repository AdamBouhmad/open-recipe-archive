## Ingredients

- 70 grams butter
- flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- cold water
- 120 grams fresh butter
- lemon juice
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt 70 grams of butter in a pan
2. Add a spoonful of flour
3. Season with salt
4. Add nutmeg and grated cinnamon
5. Moisten with a little cold water
6. Stir and let simmer
7. Add 120 grams of fresh butter
8. Add lemon juice and finely ground pepper
