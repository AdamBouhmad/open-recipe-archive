## Ingredients

- ordinary black coffee
- finely ground coffee
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put finely ground coffee sifted through a thick sieve into a pot with a handle, using one teaspoon per cup of coffee needed
2. Add a piece of sugar for each cup
3. Boil for two minutes
4. Pour boiling and unfiltered into cups
5. Serve with crushed sugar so each person can sweeten to taste
6. You can also add a few drops of orange extract
