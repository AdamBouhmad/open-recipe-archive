## Ingredients

- lemon juice
- sugar
- water
- red wine
- raspberries or strawberries or fresh pineapple (optional)
- soda water (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare lemonade using half water and half red wine instead of all water
2. Instead of water, you can use soda water
3. Before pouring the lemonade into glasses, you can add a few raspberries, strawberries, or a piece of fresh pineapple
