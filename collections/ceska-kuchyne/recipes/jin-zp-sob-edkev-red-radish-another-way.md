## Ingredients

- small red radish
- butter
- flour
- sugar
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean small red radish, remove green leaves leaving only the hearts
2. Melt fresh butter and place the radish coated in flour into it
3. After 5 minutes remove the radish onto a plate
4. Make a pale roux with flour and a piece of sugar caramelized to pink, strain this roux into beef broth, add the radish to it, cook and then place around beef roast
