## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cherries
- cherry pits
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 18 dekagrams (12 lots) sugar
- cinnamon
- tablespoon of potato starch flour
- cold water
- fried bread, crumbled rusks, or whipped cream

## Directions

1. Pit 1/2 liter of cherries
2. Crush some pits and boil the kernels with the cherries, 1 1/2 liters of water, 18 dekagrams (12 lots) of sugar, and cinnamon until the cherries are completely soft
3. Mix a tablespoon of potato starch flour in cold water
4. Cook with the cherries
5. Strain and let cool
6. Before serving the dessert at table, add either fried bread, crumbled rusks, cherries stewed in wine and sugar, or whipped cream beaten with sugar
