## Ingredients

- peeled sweet almonds
- water
- sugar
- bitter almonds (optional, not for the sick)

## Directions

1. Pound a quarter pound of peeled sweet almonds
2. While pounding, sprinkle with water to prevent them from becoming oily
3. Put them in a clean pot
4. Pour a quart of water over them and stir
5. Let stand for a while
6. Strain through a clean cloth
7. Sweeten with two tablespoons of sugar
8. If desired, a few bitter almonds can be added for better flavor, but not for the sick
