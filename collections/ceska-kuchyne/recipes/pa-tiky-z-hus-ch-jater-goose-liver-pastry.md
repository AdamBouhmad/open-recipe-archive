## Ingredients

- Goose livers
- White pepper, finely crushed
- Warm milk
- Pastry dough
- Paper
- Eggs
- Serviette
- Lemon
- Butter
- Bacon
- Onion
- Nutmeg flower
- Egg yolks
- Wine

## Directions

1. Wash the goose livers completely
2. Sprinkle with white finely crushed pepper
3. Pour warm milk over and let sit for one to two days, replacing the milk several times daily with fresh warm milk
4. Roll out cooled pastry dough thinly, cut into pieces as desired and lay on paper
5. Cut strips from another rolled dough to line the pastry walls and press firmly onto the base
6. Brush the bottom and walls with beaten eggs
7. Prepare a third piece of dough for the top cover
8. Fill the pastry with serviette so it does not collapse
9. Cover with paper and bake slowly, preferably the day before filling
10. Decorate the pastry with various ornaments added before filling and baking
11. Place sliced lemon, butter and bacon on the bottom of the dough, add onion patties
12. Pour a little wine over, place the prepared livers, sprinkle with nutmeg flower and cover with prepared dough lid
13. Bake over mild heat
14. While baking, beat several egg yolks into milk, perforate the pastry lid and pour the mixture through the hole, then seal the hole completely with the same dough so steam does not escape and the pastry does not lose flavor
