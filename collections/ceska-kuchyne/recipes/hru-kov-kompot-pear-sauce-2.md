## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- sugar
- whole cinnamon stick
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cut pears in half, peel, remove the core, and cook them like apples in water with sugar, a piece of whole cinnamon and lemon zest.
2. Arrange them on a dish and pour the thickly cooked syrup over them.
3. You can also use equal parts water and red wine, and if desired, you can cook the pears whole.
