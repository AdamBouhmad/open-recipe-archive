## Ingredients

- hard rolls
- one kilogram dried plums
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- milk
- egg
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls into small flat cakes.
2. Cook one kilogram dried plums in water to which sugar, a little cinnamon, and lemon zest have been added.
3. When soft, strain through a sieve so water drains off.
4. When cooled, chop the plums, dust with sugar, add sugar-rubbed lemon zest, and rapidly boiled fruit syrup.
5. Brush the bread cakes on one side, place two together, arrange on a shallow dish and pour milk over.
6. Before serving, dip in egg, roll in grated bread, fry on both sides in butter, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
