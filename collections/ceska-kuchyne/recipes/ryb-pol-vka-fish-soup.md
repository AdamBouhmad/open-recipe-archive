## Ingredients

- 1 liter and 4 deciliters water
- piece of celery
- parsley
- cabbage
- cauliflower, crushed
- pink roux
- salt
- piece of fresh butter
- chopped green parsley
- fish milt and roe
- roll cut into noodles

## Directions

1. Put 1 liter and 4 deciliters of water to boil
2. Add a piece of celery, parsley, cabbage, and crushed cauliflower
3. After it has boiled for a while, add pink roux and cook again
4. Strain it, salt it, add a piece of fresh butter and chopped green parsley
5. Add fish milt and roe, place roll cut into noodles in the soup bowl and pour the soup over it
6. Fried roll may be served on a separate plate
