## Ingredients

- young mutton ribs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- gravy

## Directions

1. Prepare cutlets from young ribs with the rib bone attached
2. Trim neatly
3. Clean the meat from the bone ends so cutlets can be held conveniently
4. Scrape the bone clean
5. Salt and dust with ground pepper on both sides
6. Place on hot iron pan with melted butter
7. Roast quickly on both sides over high heat
8. Cook five to ten minutes until done
9. Arrange on a heated dish
10. Pour good gravy over them
