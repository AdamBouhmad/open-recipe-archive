## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries
- sugar
- good sweet cream

## Directions

1. Press the strawberries through a thick sieve so that the juice drips into a stone dish
2. To 3 1/2 deciliters (1 serving spoon) of juice add 9 dekagrams (8 lots) sugar
3. Boil until it foams
4. Stir until it cools
5. Pour in 5 1/2 deciliters (1 1/2 serving spoons) of good sweet cream
6. Mix everything
7. Place on ice
8. Serve in small dishes with whipped cream
