## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- one cup olive oil or fresh melted butter
- two tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat two egg yolks
2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to them
3. While beating constantly, gradually add one cup of olive oil or fresh melted butter
4. When all the oil or butter is beaten with the yolks, gradually add two tablespoons of vinegar while continuing to beat constantly
5. Do not add the vinegar earlier or it will curdle
6. If the sauce curdles, beat an egg yolk with salt and pepper in a separate bowl and gradually add the curdled mayonnaise in small amounts while beating constantly, continuing until you have a nice smooth sauce without shine
7. If the sauce becomes shiny, add a few drops of vinegar
