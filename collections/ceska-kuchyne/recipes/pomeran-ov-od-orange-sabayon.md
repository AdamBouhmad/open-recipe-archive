## Ingredients

- two deciliters orange juice
- one deciliter lemon juice
- one deciliter water
- 18 decagrams sugar
- two deciliters beaten egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add one deciliter of lemon juice to two deciliters of orange juice
2. Add one deciliter of water
3. Add 18 decagrams of sugar dissolved and boiled in water
4. Add two deciliters of beaten egg yolks
