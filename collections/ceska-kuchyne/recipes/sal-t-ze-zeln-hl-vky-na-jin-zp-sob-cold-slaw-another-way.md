## Ingredients

- cabbage head
- bacon, quarter pound
- onion
- wine vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the cabbage head as described previously
2. Cut a quarter pound of nice bacon into small cubes and put on a pan, add to it one onion also cut into small cubes and let fry until golden
3. Then add a pint of wine vinegar, put the cabbage into it, salt and let it braise for ten minutes, then sprinkle the salad with ground pepper and serve warm at table
