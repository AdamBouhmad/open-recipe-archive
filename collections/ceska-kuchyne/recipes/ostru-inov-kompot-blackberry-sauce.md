## Ingredients

- Blackberries
- Water
- Sugar
- A piece of cinnamon
- Oranges
- Red wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Let rinsed and drained blackberries cook in water with sugar and a piece of cinnamon
2. Arrange them on a dish and pour thickly boiled juice over them
3. Sprinkle with sugar and line with oranges cut into thin pieces
4. Alternatively, you can use equal parts water and red wine
5. Instead of cinnamon, you can also use lemon peel
