## Ingredients

- wild boar meat
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- spices
- aromatic vegetables
- bay leaf
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Salt a piece of wild boar meat.
2. Boil it in water, vinegar, spices, aromatic vegetables and bay leaf.
3. Serve with grated horseradish.
