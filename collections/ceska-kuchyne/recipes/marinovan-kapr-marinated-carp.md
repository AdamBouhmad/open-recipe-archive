## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Wine vinegar
- Pepper
- Allspice
- Onion
- Garlic cloves
- Ginger
- Cloves
- Lemon peel
- Oil

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the carp, cut into pieces and salt
2. Roast on a grill or in a pan, basting frequently with butter
3. Place in a deep dish and cover
4. In a pot, combine wine vinegar with several grains of pepper and allspice, onion cut into slices, 2 cloves garlic, a small piece of ginger, 3 cloves, and a piece of lemon peel
5. Heat the mixture and pour over the prepared fish, cover well and set in a cold place
6. When serving, provide a bowl with oil, vinegar and pepper
