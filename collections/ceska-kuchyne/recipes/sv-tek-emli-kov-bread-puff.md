## Ingredients

- crust from 4 rolls
- 3 cups sour cream
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 egg whites
- butter for greasing the form
- grated roll crust

## Directions

1. Grate the crust from 4 rolls and soak the interior in about 3 cups sour cream
2. Beat 4 tablespoons butter with 3 egg yolks
3. Mix in the soaked rolls, season with salt
4. Gently fold in snow made from 3 egg whites and the grated roll crust
5. Grease a shallow form with butter and coat with grated roll
6. Pour the mixture into it to about the height of a finger and bake the puff
