## Ingredients

- lamb leg, shoulder or hindquarters
- salt
- bacon
- sardines
- butter
- beef broth
- capers
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes from lamb leg, shoulder or hindquarters, salt it, lard with bacon.
2. Rub sardines with butter, smear the lamb with this mixture.
3. Add beef broth, several capers with chopped lemon peel.
4. Braise under a lid on butter, adding beef broth as needed.
5. Squeeze juice from one lemon, and when meat is tender, cut into neat pieces.
