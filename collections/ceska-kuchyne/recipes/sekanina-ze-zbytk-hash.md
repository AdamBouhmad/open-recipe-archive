## Ingredients

- leftover beef, pork or veal
- potatoes
- butter
- onion
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely with a chopper in a wooden bowl. At the same time chop the potatoes separately, using about half as much potato as meat.
2. Place two tablespoons of butter and one finely chopped onion in a skillet and let fry until it begins to yellow. Add the meat and potatoes, pour in about two tablespoons of water, salt and pepper, and over brisk heat with constant stirring, cook for about 15 minutes.
