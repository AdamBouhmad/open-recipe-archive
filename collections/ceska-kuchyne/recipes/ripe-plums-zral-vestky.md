## Ingredients

- Plums
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Shorten the plum stems
2. Pierce the plums several times
3. Wash in water and place on a plate
4. Pour over with dissolved sugar and cover with paper
5. On the second day, pour off the sugar, cook it thicker, skim the foam, and when cooled, pour over the plums; repeat this until the fifth day
6. Place candied plums in containers between papers
