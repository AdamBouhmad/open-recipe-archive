## Ingredients

- sugar
- butter
- egg yolks
- sweet cream
- pineapple
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream a coffee cup of sugar with half a coffee cup of butter until fluffy
2. Add five beaten egg yolks
3. Add a coffee cup of sweet cream and one grated pineapple
4. Mix well
5. Gently fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from five egg whites
6. Fill two pie pans lined with dough with this mixture and bake
7. This pie is baked without a top crust, so before filling it you must make a thicker rim either from a strip of dough or by folding the edge of the dough
