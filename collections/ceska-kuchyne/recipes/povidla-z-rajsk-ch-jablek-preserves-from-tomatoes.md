## Ingredients

- Ripe tomatoes
- Sugar - 14 dekagrams per 30 tomatoes

## Directions

1. Mash ripe tomatoes in a container
2. Cook until soft
3. Strain
4. Add calculated sugar
5. Stir until preserves are completely thickened
6. These preserves can be stored and used for making sauce or as filling
