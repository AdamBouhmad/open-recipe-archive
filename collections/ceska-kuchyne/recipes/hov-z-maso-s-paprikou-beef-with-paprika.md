## Ingredients

- 1/4 kilogram beef
- 1/4 kilogram pork
- onion
- paprika to taste
- flour
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Cut meat into pieces
2. Chop and soften the onion
3. Add meat, season with paprika to the tip of a knife and flour as much as fits on a measuring spoon
4. Braise until meat softens
5. If there is little sauce, add beef broth as needed
