## Ingredients

- 1 small cup cornmeal
- cold water
- 2 quarts milk or half milk and half water
- salt
- sweet cream, milk, or syrup

## Directions

1. Mix one small cup of cornmeal in cold water to make a thin batter like leaven
2. If cooking in a double boiler, bring two quarts of milk or half milk and half water to a boil
3. If cooking on direct heat, use only two quarts of water as milk would easily burn
4. When the milk or water begins to boil, slowly pour in the cornmeal batter and let it cook for about two hours
5. Serve hot with sweet cream, milk, or syrup
