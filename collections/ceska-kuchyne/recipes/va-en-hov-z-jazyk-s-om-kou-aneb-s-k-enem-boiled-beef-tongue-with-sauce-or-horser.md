## Ingredients

- One beef tongue
- Capers
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned and rinsed beef tongue as you would beef, until tender
2. Remove the outer skin and slice the tongue lengthwise
3. Sprinkle with capers
4. You can serve with caper sauce, anchovy sauce, or grated horseradish
