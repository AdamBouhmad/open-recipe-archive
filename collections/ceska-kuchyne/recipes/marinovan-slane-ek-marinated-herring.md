## Ingredients

- Milk herring, three
- Milk
- Wine vinegar
- Onion, sliced into rounds
- Lemon, sliced into rounds
- Bay leaves, several
- Peppercorns, several grains
- Allspice
- Capers, a little

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from three milk herring
2. Place them in milk and let them lie overnight
3. The next day, wash them thoroughly
4. Remove the milt to a small pot
5. Split the herring in half and remove the bones
6. Roll the meat into rounds and fasten with a small skewer
7. Mix the milt thoroughly and add vinegar by the spoonful until you have enough sauce to cover the herring
8. Continue beating until it thickens
9. Line the bottom of a deep container with sliced onion
10. Place the herring on top
11. Cover with lemon slices
12. Add several bay leaves, several peppercorns, allspice and a little capers
13. Pour the sauce over it
14. Let it lie for several hours
