## Ingredients

- fresh woodcocks
- lard
- eggs
- sage
- sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Pluck the fresh woodcocks, remove innards and prepare for roasting.
2. Wrap in lard and brush with beaten egg.
3. Place sage underneath.
4. Roast with constant basting of the sauce and melted butter until completely tender.
