## Ingredients

- Eggs, 2 whole and 2 yolks
- Butter, a piece
- Sugar, 4 tablespoons
- Lemon rind, grated from half a lemon
- Cottage cheese, 1 pound good quality
- Almonds, peeled and crushed, 1 tablespoon
- Noodles, cooked from 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
- Butter for greasing
- Butter, new, in pieces
- Sugar mixed with grated almonds
- Sour cream, hot, 1/2 koflíčku
- Egg yolks, 3
- Sugar, crushed

## Directions

1. Rub 2 whole eggs and 2 yolks with a piece of butter in a bowl
2. Add 4 tablespoons sugar, grated lemon rind from half a lemon, 1 pound good cottage cheese, and 1 tablespoon of peeled and crushed almonds
3. Mix well together
4. Gently fold in cooked noodles made from 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
5. Fill a buttered mold with this mixture
6. Top with pieces of new butter
7. Sprinkle generously with sugar mixed with grated almonds
8. Bake until nicely golden
9. Beat 3 yolks with hot sour cream and a little crushed sugar
10. Pour this mixture over the noodles
