## Ingredients

- 8 peeled grated apples
- 15 grams (1 lot) butter
- 22.5 grams (1/8 lot) grated butter
- 4 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon peel
- 45 grams (3 lots) crushed biscuit
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stew the 8 peeled grated apples with 15 grams (1 lot) of butter and let cool
2. Mix with 22.5 grams (1/8 lot) of grated butter, 4 egg yolks, a piece of sugar, lemon peel, 45 grams (3 lots) of crushed biscuit and snow from 4 egg whites
3. Grease a mold with butter, sprinkle with crushed biscuit and bake the soufflé in it or cook in steam
