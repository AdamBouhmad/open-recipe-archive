## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- butter
- bread roll
- hard-boiled eggs
- pork and beef meat
- pepper
- spices
- flower
- ginger
- caraway seeds
- garlic
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi and hollow them out to create a cavity; grate or finely chop the hollowed-out insides, place in butter on a small baking dish.
2. Add grated bread roll, chopped hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped pork and beef meat, a little pepper, new spices, flower, ginger, caraway seeds, one clove of garlic, and braise until everything is soft.
3. Coat the hollowed kohlrabi with butter, stand them side by side on a baking dish hollow side up, pour beef broth over them so they do not burn, and braise until soft.
4. Then fill with the forcemeat and sprinkle with fried bread roll.
