## Ingredients

- 0.25 kilogram sweet almonds
- some bitter almonds
- 2 eggs
- 0.25 kilogram finely crushed sugar
- 28 egg yolks
- 1 kilogram fresh butter
- vanilla
- cinnamon
- nutmeg flower
- lemon zest
- 0.25 kilogram finest flour

## Directions

1. Peel the sweet almonds and some bitter almonds
2. Rub them finely with 2 eggs
3. Add the 0.25 kilogram finely crushed sugar to which the 28 egg yolks are added
4. Mix everything for at least half an hour
5. Clarify 1 kilogram of fresh butter and add it slowly to the mixture
6. Finely crush vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg flower, and lemon zest and mix thoroughly
7. Slowly add 0.25 kilogram of finest flour while constantly stirring
8. Make snow from 28 egg whites and fold into the dough
9. Pour into low molds which are usually round, buttered, and spread with very thin batter
10. Bake slowly with hot wooden coals placed around the mold both underneath and on top so that it bakes evenly on all sides
11. Stack several layers baked to golden yellow on top of each other, layering with fine jam, and bake again in the oven
12. Brush the cooled cake with glaze and decorate
