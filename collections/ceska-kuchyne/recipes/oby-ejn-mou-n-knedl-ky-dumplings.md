## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one and one half cups of milk
- one small teaspoon of butter
- salt
- flour
- two slices of bread
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs in a deep bowl; add one and one half cups of milk and one small teaspoon of butter, salt and enough flour to make a dough that can be beaten well with a spoon or beater
2. Beat for five minutes or longer until the dough is smooth and let stand half an hour
3. In the meantime, fry two slices of bread cut into small cubes in heated butter until golden, allow to cool and add to the dumpling dough
4. Dust your hands with flour, form dumplings the size of a small apple, shape with your hands, place in boiling water, cover well and allow to cook for 10 minutes
5. Remove them, split with a fork and serve with cabbage and pork
