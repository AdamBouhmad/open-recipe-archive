## Ingredients

- 5 to 6 pound veal leg
- bacon (lardons), cut into thin strips
- salt
- 2 onions, chopped
- carrot
- parsley
- celery
- butter
- water, 2 cups warm
- white sour wine, 1/2 cup
- butter, 1/2 tablespoon
- flour, 1 teaspoon
- sour cream, 1/2 cup
- lemon zest
- wine, 1/2 cup
- sour cream, additional

## Directions

1. Tenderize a 5 to 6 pound leg by pounding and trim all skin
2. Cut bacon into thin strips and lard the leg with them
3. Salt the leg
4. Chop 2 onions, carrot, parsley and celery and place on a baking pan with a piece of butter
5. Place the leg on top
6. Top with pieces of butter and add 2 cups of warm water
7. Place in the oven to roast
8. When half cooked, baste with 1/2 cup of white sour wine and finish roasting
9. In a small pot, melt 1/2 tablespoon of butter and mix in 1 teaspoon of flour
10. Mix well and add 1/2 cup of sour cream
11. The sour cream may be sweet but must be cooked and cooled
12. Turn the leg so the side that will be presented is facing up and baste with the sour cream mixture
13. Let it roast and continue basting until the sour cream is used up
14. When cooked, brush with butter and let it brown well
15. Pour a little sauce from the leg into a pot, add 1/2 cup of wine, a piece of lemon zest and bring to a boil
16. In 1/2 cup of sour cream, mix 1 teaspoon of flour, stir to avoid lumps and gradually add this to the boiling sauce, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
