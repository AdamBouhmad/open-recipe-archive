## Ingredients

- peeled boiled potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- flour
- broth with wine

## Directions

1. Slice several peeled boiled potatoes
2. Shake over fire several times with butter, salt, pepper, chopped parsley and chives
3. Dust with flour
4. Pour with broth mixed with wine
5. Simmer for quarter of an hour
