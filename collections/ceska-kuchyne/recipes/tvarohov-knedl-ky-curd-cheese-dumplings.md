## Ingredients

- half kilogram curd cheese
- 3 to 4 eggs
- grated bread roll
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix half kilogram curd cheese with 3 to 4 eggs and enough grated bread roll so the dough does not stick.
2. Knead everything, salt and make a test dumpling to see if the dough is sufficiently stiff.
3. Then boil the rest of the dumplings.
4. To determine if the dumplings are sufficiently cooked, pierce them with a stick; if the dough does not stick to it, place on a platter.
5. Pour hot butter over them and sprinkle with grated bread roll.
