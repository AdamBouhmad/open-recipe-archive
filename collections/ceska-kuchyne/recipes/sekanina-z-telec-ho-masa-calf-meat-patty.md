## Ingredients

- 195 grams (13 lots) butter
- 3-4 eggs
- 240 grams (16 lots) bread roll soaked in milk
- ½ kilogram finely chopped calf meat
- 195 grams bacon
- salt
- ground nutmeg
- several tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Cream butter with 3-4 eggs
2. Mix in bread roll soaked in milk, finely chopped calf meat, bacon, salt and ground nutmeg
3. Add several tablespoons of sour cream
