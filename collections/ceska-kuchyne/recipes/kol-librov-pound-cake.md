## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) butter
- 12 yolks
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 1/2 kilogram flour
- 24 decagrams (16 lots) raisins
- snow from 12 whites

## Directions

1. Rub butter with yolks, add sugar and flour
2. Add raisins, mix well
3. Work dough, fold in snow from whites
4. Bake in greased form
