## Ingredients

- ordinary tart dough
- raisins
- blanched almonds
- sour cream
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. From ordinary tart dough, make small tarts the thickness of a finger
2. Form a sufficiently high edge
3. Sprinkle with raisins and blanched almonds
4. Pour sour cream in which egg yolks and sugar have been beaten
5. Bake the tarts in moderate heat
