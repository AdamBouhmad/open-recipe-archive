## Ingredients

- calf or sheep larynx, lungs, heart, spleen and liver
- salt
- cloves
- onion
- pepper
- new spices
- bay leaf
- root vegetables
- thyme
- flour
- butter for roux
- egg yolks
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over the organs several times and rinse in cold water
2. Season with salt and keep in water
3. Cut into pieces and cook with salt, cloves, onion, pepper, new spices, bay leaf, root vegetables and thyme, removing scum
4. After 3 hours add a roux, strain the sauce and mix in egg yolks beaten with sour cream
