## Ingredients

- duck necks, heads, feet, gizzards, hearts and wings
- chopped pork meat
- breadcrumb
- pepper
- salt
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Clean the necks, heads, feet, gizzards, hearts and wings. Cook with dumplings made from chopped pork meat and grated breadcrumb, season with pepper and salt, add crushed marjoram, cook and pour over the giblets.
