## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 24 dekagrams butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 12 egg yolks
- snow from 10 egg whites
- several handfuls grated roll
- lemon peel rubbed with sugar
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix 24 dekagrams almonds, 24 dekagrams butter, 4 whole eggs, 12 egg yolks, snow from 10 egg whites, several handfuls grated roll, lemon peel rubbed with sugar, and a little salt together.
2. Grease a mold with butter and sprinkle with grated roll.
3. Bake the mixture in the mold and sprinkle with sugar.
