## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and rub the quinces and cook until soft.
2. Press the pulp through a fine sieve.
3. For the amount of pulp, use an equal amount of sugar, which is cooked to the sixth degree, then add the quince pulp to it.
4. Mix everything and cook several times, carefully removing the foam, and pour the mixture into containers.
