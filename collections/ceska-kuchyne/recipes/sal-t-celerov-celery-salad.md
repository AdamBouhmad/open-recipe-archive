## Ingredients

- celery
- beef broth or salted water
- wine vinegar
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the celery thoroughly, wash it, and carefully remove and clean the green center, setting it aside in cold water
2. Cook the celery in either beef broth or salted water until it softens
3. Place the cooked celery in cold water to cool
4. Slice the cooled celery into rounds, salt and pepper it
5. Pour wine vinegar and olive oil over it
6. Place the removed green heart in the center
