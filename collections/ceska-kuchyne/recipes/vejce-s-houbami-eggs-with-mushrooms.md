## Ingredients

- Fresh mushrooms
- Eggs
- Butter
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Pepper
- Salt
- Pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Slice fresh mushrooms thinly and rinse them.
2. Salt the mushrooms and place them on a baking sheet with chopped parsley, fresh butter, and pepper.
3. Fry for a while.
4. Meanwhile, on another baking sheet, place eggs with butter, chopped chives, salt, and pepper.
5. When both are ready, arrange the mushrooms in the center and around the eggs, or arrange them in a cross pattern with pickled cucumbers between the arms.
6. Use the first arrangement when mushrooms are scarce, the second when there are plenty.
