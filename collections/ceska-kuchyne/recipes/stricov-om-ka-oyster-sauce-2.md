## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- milk
- butter
- flour
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the oysters finely and simmer for five minutes in their own juice.
2. Heat a pint of milk, blend a tablespoon of butter with a tablespoon of flour and add to the milk while stirring constantly.
3. When the sauce is cooked, remove from heat, pour the oysters into it and mix in two egg yolks, salt and pepper.
