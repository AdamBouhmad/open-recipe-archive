## Ingredients

- brain
- water or milk
- chopped ham
- onion
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- chopped lemon zest
- potatoes
- beef broth
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak brain in water or milk for a time
2. Remove membranes
3. Chop and mix with chopped ham, onion, eggs, salt, pepper, and chopped lemon zest
4. Partially cook potatoes
5. Cut off tops and set aside
6. Scoop out centers
7. Fill with stuffing
8. Cover with cut-off tops
9. Stick together with egg
10. Cook in beef broth after braising in butter
