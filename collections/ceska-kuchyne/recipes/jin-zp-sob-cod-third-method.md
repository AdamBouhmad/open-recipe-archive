## Ingredients

- dried cod, well-soaked
- butter
- water
- parsley, chopped
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour for thickening
- lemon

## Directions

1. Place well-soaked cod on a baking sheet with butter, water, chopped parsley, pepper, and nutmeg
2. Braise until the cod is tender
3. Transfer to a platter
4. Thicken the remaining cooking liquid with pale roux, strain over the cod
5. Add a piece of butter and simmer briefly
6. Serve in sauce, typically with lemon cut into quarters
