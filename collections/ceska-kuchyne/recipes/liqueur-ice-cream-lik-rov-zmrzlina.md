## Ingredients

- One liter of sour cream
- Quarter kilo of crushed sugar
- Liqueur of choice

## Directions

1. Pour into one liter of sour cream a quarter kilo of crushed sugar
2. Add liqueur of choice, pouring enough to taste it in the ice cream
3. Beat everything into foam
4. Proceed as with other ice creams
