## Ingredients

- sorted black strawberries
- sugar (to the sixth degree)

## Directions

1. Put sorted black strawberries into sugar boiled to the sixth degree
2. Boil them together again, pour onto a plate
3. Cover with paper and let stand
4. The next day, pour off the sugar so it boils even more
5. Pour the strawberries back into it and boil together
6. Remove foam and put the preserves into containers
