## Ingredients

- ripe pineapple
- sugar
- 2 liters good wine
- 1 liter champagne wine

## Directions

1. Rub the ripe pineapple against sugar
2. Pour 2 liters of good wine over the sugar
3. Let it stand until the sugar is completely dissolved
4. Strain the mixture
5. Add 1 liter of champagne wine
6. Serve at table
