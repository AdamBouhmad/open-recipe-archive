## Ingredients

- four eggs
- 9 decagrams (6 lots) sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch flour
- zest from half lemon rubbed with sugar
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- salt
- sour cream
- butter pieces
- raisins

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, mix with sugar, cornstarch flour, lemon zest, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in sour cream
2. Pour onto cake with bent edge
3. Sprinkle with butter pieces and raisins
4. Bake cake
