## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons butter
- two eggs
- half small onion
- half cup milk
- white bread or breadcrumbs or crackers
- cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken for roasting and salt inside and outside
2. Beat two tablespoons butter until it foams
3. Add two eggs, half a small onion browned to yellow, half a cup of milk, salt, pepper, and a pinch of cloves
4. Mix in enough grated white bread, breadcrumbs, or crushed crackers to make a batter like for dumplings
5. Stuff the chicken with this mixture, sew it closed
6. Place on a baking pan, cover the surface with butter, add water
7. Bake, frequently basting with the pan juices during cooking
