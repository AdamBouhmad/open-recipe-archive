## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 small cup barley grits or torn grits, rice, or oat grits
- salt
- melted butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Barley grits, torn grits, rice, oat grits, etc. are best cooked in a double boiler, where the lower pot contains water and the upper pot is set into it for cooking
2. In such a pot, the food will not burn or become scorched, as easily happens when cooked directly on heat
3. For a quart of milk, take one small cup of grits or torn grits
4. Rinse them in several waters, the last time in hot water, then add to the milk
5. Cover and let cook for two hours
6. When cooked, salt it and stir, turn out onto a dish and pour hot melted butter over it
7. You can also sprinkle it with sugar and cinnamon
