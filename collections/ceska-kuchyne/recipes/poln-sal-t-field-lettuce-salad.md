## Ingredients

- field lettuce leaves
- salt
- wine vinegar
- olive oil or fresh melted butter
- celery salad or watercress

## Directions

1. Separate each leaf of field lettuce individually
2. Rinse each
3. Season with salt
4. Pour wine vinegar over
5. Add olive oil or fresh melted butter
6. Mix well
7. Arrange on a plate around the edge
8. Place celery salad or watercress in the center
9. Serve as a side dish with roasts
