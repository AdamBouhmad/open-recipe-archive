## Ingredients

- 1/2 small cup sugar
- 1/2 small cup water
- 1 small cup finely pounded walnut kernels
- a little pounded vanilla or a little finely chopped lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 small cup of sugar with 1/2 small cup of water
2. Put it in a bowl
3. Add 1 small cup of finely pounded walnut kernels, a little pounded vanilla or a little finely chopped lemon zest
4. Mix well
