## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- lemon zest
- 1/2 small cup finely pounded almonds
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put 3 egg yolks in a bowl
2. Add 6 tablespoons of sugar and grated lemon zest
3. Add 1/2 small cup of finely pounded almonds and rub well
4. Fold in stiff snow made from 3 egg whites
