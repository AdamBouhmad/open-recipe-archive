## Ingredients

- vinegar
- water or beef broth
- lemon zest
- whole onion
- thyme
- bay leaves
- pepper
- allspice
- salt
- pale roux
- browned sugar
- white wine
- leftover fowl
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Put equal parts vinegar and water or beef broth in a bowl
2. Add a piece of sliced lemon zest, a whole onion, a piece of thyme, a few bay leaves, a little pepper, allspice and salt
3. Let it boil together
4. Then mix in a pale roux, a piece of browned sugar and a little white wine
5. Let it boil a bit more and strain it over neatly cut pieces of leftover fowl arranged on a baking sheet
6. Let the leftovers heat in the sauce
7. Arrange them on a dish, pour the sauce over them and surround with roasted or fried potatoes
