## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram butter
- 0.5 kilogram finely crushed sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 0.25 kilogram almonds rubbed with egg whites
- cinnamon
- cloves
- vanilla
- finely chopped lemon zest
- 0.25 kilogram fine flour
- 6 egg whites
- egg white for brushing
- chopped almonds
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Place 0.5 kilogram butter on a heated dish
2. Rub it with 0.5 kilogram finely crushed sugar and 12 egg yolks
3. Add the 0.25 kilogram almonds rubbed with egg whites, a little cinnamon, cloves, vanilla, finely chopped lemon zest, and 0.25 kilogram fine flour
4. Mix everything
5. Make snow from 6 egg whites and add to the mixture
6. Form heaps of dough on greased papers on baking sheets
7. Flatten them, brush with beaten egg white, sprinkle with chopped almonds and sugar, and bake gently
