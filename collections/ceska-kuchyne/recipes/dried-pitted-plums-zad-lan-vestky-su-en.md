## Ingredients

- Plums
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the pits at the stem
2. Cover the plums with dissolved sugar
3. On the second day, cook together after the drained sugar has been cooked thicker
4. Pour off the sugar again and cook it, add the plums and cook together with the sugar
5. Place on a sieve to dry thoroughly, then dry slowly and completely
