## Ingredients

- front quarter of lamb
- four tablespoons fresh butter
- one onion
- one carrot
- a little leek
- celery
- flour
- beef broth
- one cup good sour cream
- paprika

## Directions

1. Take a front quarter of lamb, clean it well, and cut into neat pieces
2. Put four tablespoons fresh butter on a pan with one onion, one carrot, a little leek and celery, all cut into slices
3. Add the cut meat, salt properly, and let it stew until the meat is half tender
4. Dust with a spoonful of flour
5. Add good beef broth and one cup of good sour cream
6. Add paprika to taste and let the meat stew completely tender
7. Remove the meat pieces one by one with a fork onto a plate
8. Remove all the fat from the sauce, salt it, add more paprika if desired, and strain it over the lamb meat on the plate
9. Serve hot
10. The sauce should be thick, not thin
