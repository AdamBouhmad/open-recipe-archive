## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- rutabaga
- 2 parsley roots
- leek
- celery
- butter
- beef broth
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots, rutabaga, parsley roots, leek, and celery into cubes
2. Braise in butter for a quarter hour
3. Add the chopped vegetables to beef broth
4. Cook for half an hour
5. Pour over sliced bread roll
