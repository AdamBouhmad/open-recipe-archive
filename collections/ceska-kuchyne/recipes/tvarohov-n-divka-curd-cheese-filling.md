## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 15 dek sugar
- 30 dek shredded curd cheese
- 14 dek sweet almonds, finely grated
- 2 dek bitter almonds, finely grated
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Mix and rub the egg yolks with sugar
2. Add the shredded curd cheese
3. Add the finely grated sweet and bitter almonds to which lemon zest has been added
