## Ingredients

- 24 decagrams (16 lots) almonds
- 24 decagrams butter
- 4 whole eggs
- 12 egg yolks
- egg white snow from 10 egg whites
- several handfuls grated roll
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- a little salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 24 decagrams (16 lots) almonds, 24 decagrams butter, 4 whole eggs, 12 egg yolks, snow made from 10 egg whites, several handfuls of grated roll, lemon zest rubbed with sugar, and a little salt
2. Bake the mixture in a mold buttered and sprinkled with grated roll
3. Sprinkle with sugar
