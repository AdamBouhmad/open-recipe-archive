## Ingredients

- one head of nice white cauliflower
- strong beef broth
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of flour
- a pinch of ground cloves
- two teaspoons of sour cream
- two egg yolks
- fried bread rolls
- goose liver (if possible), fried and finely chopped

## Directions

1. Clean a head of nice white cauliflower, break it into equal pieces and cook in strong beef broth.
2. Put one spoon of butter in a pot, let it melt and add one spoon of flour; let it foam slightly and add the broth in which you cooked the cauliflower.
3. Add a pinch of ground cloves, two teaspoons of sour cream and two egg yolks, mix well and let it simmer.
4. Pour everything into a soup bowl on fried bread rolls, and if possible, add the fried and finely chopped goose liver, and place the cooked cauliflower in it.
