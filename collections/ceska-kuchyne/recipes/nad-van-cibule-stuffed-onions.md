## Ingredients

- large round onions
- salt
- water
- veal
- pork
- butter
- good meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean and peel large round onions, cook them in salted water for fifteen minutes, then place in fresh water to cool
2. Hollow out the cooled onions and stuff with chopped veal and pork
3. Place them close to each other in a pot, pour good meat broth with butter and salt added over them and stew slowly
