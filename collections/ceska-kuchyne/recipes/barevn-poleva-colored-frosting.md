## Ingredients

- white frosting base
- saffron or cochineal or spinach

## Directions

1. To make yellow frosting, prepare it as white frosting and add several drops of saffron dissolved in a little water
2. For pink frosting, use purified cochineal
3. For green frosting, color with juice from blanched and pounded spinach
