## Ingredients

- cleaned pigeons
- pigeon livers
- smoked bacon
- egg yolk
- rusk
- bacon

## Directions

1. From the cleaned pigeons, cut out the breasts and thighs
2. Scrape the remaining meat from the bones with the livers and a piece of smoked bacon, chop finely
3. Rub in a mortar with a raw egg yolk and rusk, strain through a sieve
4. Spread on the thighs, lard the breasts with bacon, split them, remove the bones, cut off the wing ends
