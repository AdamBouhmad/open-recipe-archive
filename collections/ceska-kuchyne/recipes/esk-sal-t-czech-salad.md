## Ingredients

- asparagus tips
- green vegetables
- young green beans
- carrots
- salted water
- vinegar
- oil
- lettuce hearts
- hard-boiled eggs
- crayfish tails
- egg yolks
- pepper
- salt
- fine oil
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook asparagus tips, green vegetables, sliced young green beans, and sliced carrots in salted water until tender
2. Drain the water
3. Marinate in vinegar and oil
4. Place cleaned lettuce hearts in the center of a platter
5. Arrange the marinated vegetables in colorful groups around the lettuce
6. Garnish with sliced hard-boiled eggs and crayfish tails
7. Prepare a sauce from two hard-boiled egg yolks mixed with pepper, salt, fine oil, and good vinegar
8. Pour the sauce over the salad
