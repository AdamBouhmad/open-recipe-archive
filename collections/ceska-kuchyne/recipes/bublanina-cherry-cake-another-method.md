## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams (16 lots) butter
- 24 dekagrams ground almonds
- 8 whole eggs
- 7 egg yolks
- snow from 7 egg whites
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- clove
- grated roll
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix butter, ground almonds, whole eggs, egg yolks, snow from egg whites, cinnamon, lemon zest, clove, grated roll and sugar together to form a batter
2. Fill a mold alternately with the mixture and raw cherries
3. Bake the bublanina and sprinkle with sugar
