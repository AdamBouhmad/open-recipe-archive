## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 35 grams (2 lots) butter
- herbs
- salt
- flour
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix egg yolks, butter, herbs and salt, add flour and mix
2. Make a snow from the egg whites and slowly add it
3. When the soup is boiling, dip a spoon in it, use the spoon to gather dumplings and drop them into the soup
