## Ingredients

- about 3 kilos of tripe
- marjoram
- 4 cloves of garlic
- pepper
- salt
- a handful of finely chopped parsley
- butter roux

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe thoroughly; after it has boiled for an hour, wash it again once the water has been drained, and cook until completely soft
2. Strain the soup and add marjoram, 4 cloves of garlic, pepper, salt and a handful of finely chopped parsley; make a butter roux and add it to the soup
3. Strain everything, cut the tripe into noodles, boil for a while longer and season with salt
4. Alternatively: drain the second water and use good beef broth instead
