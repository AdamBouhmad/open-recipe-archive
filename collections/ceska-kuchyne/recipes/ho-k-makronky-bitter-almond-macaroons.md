## Ingredients

- 500 grams sweet almonds
- 250 grams bitter almonds
- egg whites from 12 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald 500 grams sweet almonds and 250 grams bitter almonds, peel them, dry on a sieve, grind with egg whites from 12 eggs
2. If the dough is too stiff and thick, add a little more egg whites until a liquid dough forms
3. Spread the dough thinly on a wooden macaroon plate, spread carefully with a knife below, place on a baking sheet and bake slowly
