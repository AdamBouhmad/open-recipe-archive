## Ingredients

- 1 liter thick sour cream
- two spoons of rum, arrack, etc.
- 120 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the thick sour cream until stiff snow forms
2. Mix it with two spoons of rum, arrack etc. and 120 grams sugar
