## Ingredients

- plucked and cleaned chicken
- boiling water
- good broth
- onion
- anchovies
- salt
- white pepper
- cream
- butter
- flour
- mushrooms
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scald plucked and cleaned chicken in boiling water, remove skin and cut into quarters
2. Cook with good broth, onion, anchovies, salt and white pepper until tender
3. Strain sauce with cream and butter mixed with flour
4. Cook finely chopped mushrooms in the sauce
5. Add egg yolks and lemon juice for taste
6. Pour this sauce over prepared chicken
