## Ingredients

- 1/4 liter barley
- 1 liter milk
- salt
- 6 egg yolks
- sugar as needed
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- butter for greasing
- grated roll crumbs
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Pour the barley, spread it on a baking sheet, pour with sufficient milk, salt it and make a very thick paste
2. When cooked, let it cool, mix in 6 egg yolks, sugar as needed, a little finely chopped lemon zest and stiffly beaten snow from 6 egg whites, mix everything together
3. Grease the baking sheet with butter, sprinkle with grated roll crumbs, put the barley pudding in the baking sheet and bake
4. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
5. Finely chopped parsley and cracklings can be added to the pudding
