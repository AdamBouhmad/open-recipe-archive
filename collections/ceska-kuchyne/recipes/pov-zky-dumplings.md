## Ingredients

- hard rolls cut into cakes
- one kilogram dried plums
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- lemon zest rubbed with sugar
- eggs
- grated roll
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut hard rolls into cakes
2. Cook one kilogram dried plums in water with a piece of sugar, a little cinnamon and lemon zest
3. When soft, place on a sieve to drain
4. When cool, chop, sprinkle with sugar, add lemon zest rubbed with sugar and rapidly boiled juice
5. Brush roll cakes on one side, put two and two together, pour milk over on a shallow plate
6. Before serving, dip in egg, roll in grated roll, fry on both sides in butter
