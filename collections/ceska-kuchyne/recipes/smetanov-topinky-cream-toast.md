## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three-eighths liter sour cream
- lemon zest
- fifteen dekagrams crushed sugar
- bread slices
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with three-eighths liter sour cream, add lemon zest and fifteen dekagrams crushed sugar, and bake over heat.
2. When cooled, spread on bread slices, place in a skimmer, and fry in hot butter.
