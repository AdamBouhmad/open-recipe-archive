## Ingredients

- parsley roots
- fresh parsley
- bread crumbs
- beef broth
- cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean parsley roots, cut them into very thin slices, chop fresh parsley finely, and place both with fried grated bread crumbs in a pan.
2. Pour beef broth over it, let it simmer, season as desired, and finally mix in a little cream and a piece of butter.
