## Ingredients

- light roux
- strong meat broth
- cleaned anchovies without bones, finely chopped
- onion
- 2 egg yolks
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the light roux with strong meat broth
2. Add cleaned and finely chopped anchovies and onion
3. Simmer for one hour
4. Strain the mixture
5. Simmer again and while stirring constantly, add two egg yolks blanched in white wine
