## Ingredients

- Green peas
- Butter
- Broth
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mash the green peas in cold water with a piece of butter
2. Strain the water
3. Place the peas on a baking pan and moisten with broth
4. Stew until tender
5. Before serving, add a piece of butter mixed with flour and three egg yolks
