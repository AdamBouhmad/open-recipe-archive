## Ingredients

- 4 small cups sifted flour
- 1 tablespoon hard lard
- salt to taste
- 2 small cups sour milk
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon hot water

## Directions

1. Put the sifted flour in a bowl, add hard lard and salt and mix thoroughly with hands
2. Take 2 small cups sour milk; dissolve 1 tablespoon baking soda in 1 tablespoon hot water, pour it into the sour milk and mix with the prepared flour
