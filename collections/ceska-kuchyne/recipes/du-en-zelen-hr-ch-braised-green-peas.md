## Ingredients

- green peas
- butter
- parsley, tied together
- flour
- broth
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Braise green peas in butter with tied parsley
2. Sprinkle with flour and shake often
3. Pour in broth, add a little sugar and salt
4. Cook for a while
