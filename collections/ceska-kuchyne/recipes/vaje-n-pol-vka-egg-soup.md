## Ingredients

- flour (for roux)
- butter
- soup
- four eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- roasted roll

## Directions

1. Make a light roux and let it cool
2. Pour soup over it in which four eggs have been beaten
3. Season with salt and nutmeg, pour over a roasted roll
