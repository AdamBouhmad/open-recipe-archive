## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- parsley or garlic
- bread rolls
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat butter with two eggs
2. Mix with finely chopped parsley or garlic
3. Grate two bread rolls or soak them in milk
4. Season with salt
5. Beat finely
6. Use for stuffing veal breasts or poultry
