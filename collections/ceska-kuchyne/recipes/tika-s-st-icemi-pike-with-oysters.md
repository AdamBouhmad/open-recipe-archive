## Ingredients

- pike
- butter
- anchovies
- pike liver
- oysters or mussels
- bread or roll
- wine
- flower
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook the pike in the ordinary way au bleu (like carp).
2. Place a piece of butter in a small pot, about four anchovies, well chopped pike liver and several oysters or mussels, add grated bread or slice of white bread soaked in wine, a little flower, a little finely chopped lemon peel, mix everything well with wine.
3. Add broth in which the pike was cooked and let it simmer just a little.
4. Pour this over the fish and finish it with lemon juice, or decorate the dish with rounded lemon quarters arranged in a ring.
5. Perch, tench and other white fish may be prepared the same way.
