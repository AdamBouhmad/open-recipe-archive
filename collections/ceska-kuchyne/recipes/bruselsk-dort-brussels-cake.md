## Ingredients

- fine cake dough
- layer of almonds
- various optional preserves
- glaze

## Directions

1. Put fine dough into a cake mold
2. Sprinkle a layer of almonds on top
3. Bake slowly in the oven until it turns red
4. Turn out onto a sieve and let cool
5. Coat with various optional preserves
6. Cover the whole cake with nice glaze
7. Decorate tastefully and let dry slowly by the warm stove
