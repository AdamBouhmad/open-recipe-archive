## Ingredients

- 2 anchovies
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 2 hard egg yolks
- 6 decagrams (4 lots) butter
- good broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Finely chop the anchovies, capers, and hard egg yolks
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Add good broth and cook with the butter
