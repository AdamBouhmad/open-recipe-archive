## Ingredients

- goose meat
- goose fat
- bread soaked in milk
- lemon zest
- onion
- salt
- garlic
- clove
- pepper
- new spice
- ginger
- marjoram
- beef broth
- pork intestines

## Directions

1. Cut meat from the goose bones, remove the skin and chop it. If too fatty, reduce the fat to half a kilogram. Add 3 pieces of bread soaked in milk and squeezed, lemon zest, onion fried in goose fat, salt, crushed garlic, a little clove, pepper, new spice, ginger and crushed marjoram. Thin with beef broth, stuff into pork intestines and either boil then fry in goose fat or smoke them.
