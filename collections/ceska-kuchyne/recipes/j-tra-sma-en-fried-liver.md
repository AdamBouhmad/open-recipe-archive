## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Bread roll
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove all skin from veal liver and slice into thin pieces
2. Salt them, coat in flour, then in egg and grated bread roll
3. Fry in butter
4. Serve with vegetables or potato mash
