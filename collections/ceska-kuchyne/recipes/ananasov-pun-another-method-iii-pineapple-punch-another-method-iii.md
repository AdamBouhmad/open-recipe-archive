## Ingredients

- 1.5 kilogram sugar
- 3 liters boiling water
- 1 bottle good Rhine wine
- 1 bottle good rum
- 2 bottles champagne wine
- Juice from pressed pineapple

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1.5 kilograms of sugar in 3 liters of boiling water
2. Add 1 bottle of good Rhine wine and 1 bottle of good rum
3. Heat for an hour on the stove
4. Finally add juice from pressed pineapple
5. The container must be completely covered to prevent the aroma from escaping
