## Ingredients

- pint of oysters
- oyster liquor
- 1 cupful milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the oysters finely and cook for five minutes in their own liquor
2. Cook a cupful of milk and rub a tablespoon of butter with a tablespoon of flour
3. Add to the milk when it boils with constant stirring
4. When the sauce is cooked, remove from heat and add the oysters to it
5. Mix in two egg yolks, salt and pepper
