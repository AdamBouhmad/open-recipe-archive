## Ingredients

- Herring
- Cold milk
- Cloth
- Eggs
- Wine
- Milk
- Flour
- Salt
- Clarified butter
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Skin the herring
2. Gut it and place it in cold milk for several hours
3. Dry it with a cloth
4. Split it lengthwise and remove the bones
5. Make a batter from eggs, wine, a little milk and flour, salt it lightly, dip the herring in it and fry in hot clarified butter
6. Alternatively, dust it with flour mixed with a little ground pepper, dip in beaten egg, coat in grated bread crumbs and fry in butter
