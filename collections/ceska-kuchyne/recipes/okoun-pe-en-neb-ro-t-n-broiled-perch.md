## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- pepper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Scale the perch, clean it and wash it, salt it, sprinkle with pepper and let it lie for a quarter hour.
2. Brush it with good fresh butter and bake on a grill or in a roasting pan on both sides until yellowish.
3. Arrange the perch on a dish and sprinkle with lemon juice.
