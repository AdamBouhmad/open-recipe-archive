## Ingredients

- Veal lungs
- Salt
- Water
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- 3 egg yolks
- Lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Cook veal lungs in salted water
2. Chop them finely and add finely chopped parsley that has been braised in butter
3. Add a little flour and broth and cook until thickened
4. Allow the mixture to cool, then add 3 egg yolks
5. Grease a mold and bake the roll in it
6. Serve as a separate dish with lemon sauce or slice into soup
