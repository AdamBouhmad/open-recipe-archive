## Ingredients

- 500 grams hard homemade bread
- one kilogram cherries
- 8 egg yolks
- 75 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Grate 500 grams of hard homemade bread
2. Place one kilogram of cherries in cold water
3. Rub 8 egg yolks with 75 grams of sugar
