## Ingredients

- several clean washed potatoes
- water
- 14 dekagrams (8 lots) of bacon
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook several clean washed potatoes, drain the water, add fresh water and mash the potatoes in it, season with salt
2. Stew 14 dekagrams (8 lots) of bacon with parsley; cook half the bacon in the soup and place the other half in the bowl in which the soup is served
