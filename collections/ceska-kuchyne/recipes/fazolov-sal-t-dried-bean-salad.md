## Ingredients

- two cups dried beans
- cold water
- boiling water
- baking soda (one quarter teaspoon)
- salt
- one tablespoon butter
- one onion, finely chopped
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- pepper

## Directions

1. In the evening, soak two cups of beans in cold water
2. In the morning, drain the water and pour fresh boiling water over them
3. Add one quarter teaspoon of baking soda, salt, and let them cook until soft
4. During cooking, add boiling water as needed to keep the beans always covered with water
5. During cooking, stir in one tablespoon of butter
6. When soft, place on a strainer and let drain
7. Put the beans into a salad bowl
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped onion
9. Mix vinegar with water to taste and add a little sugar
10. While still warm, pour this mixture over the beans
11. Season with pepper and when cooled, serve on the table
12. This goes well with every roast
