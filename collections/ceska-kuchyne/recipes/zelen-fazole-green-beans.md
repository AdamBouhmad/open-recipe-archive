## Ingredients

- Young green bean pods
- Salt
- Beef broth or water
- Sugar
- Spices
- Pale roux

## Directions

1. Wash the green bean pods and remove the tough strings from both sides of the pod
2. Slice the pods diagonally into thin strips
3. Place on a baking pan, salt, moisten with beef broth or water, add a piece of sugar and a little spice, and stew
4. Strain the pale roux
5. May be served as a standalone dish or around meat
