## Ingredients

- flour (equal weight to 9 eggs)
- sugar (equal weight to 9 eggs)
- 5 egg whites
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Weigh flour and sugar equal to the weight of 9 eggs
2. Beat snow from 5 egg whites
3. Mix in egg yolks, sugar, and finally flour
4. Mix briefly
5. Press through a pastry tube onto a paper-covered sheet
6. Sprinkle with sugar and bake
