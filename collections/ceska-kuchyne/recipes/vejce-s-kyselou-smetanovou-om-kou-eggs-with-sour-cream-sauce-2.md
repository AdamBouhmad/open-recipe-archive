## Ingredients

- water (two cups)
- vinegar (two spoons)
- salt
- sugar (pinch)
- green dill (two or three spoons chopped)

## Directions

1. Bring two cups of water to boil
2. Add two spoons of vinegar
3. Add a little salt
4. Add a pinch of sugar
5. Add two or three spoons of chopped green dill
