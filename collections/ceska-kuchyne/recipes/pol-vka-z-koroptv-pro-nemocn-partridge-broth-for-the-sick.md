## Ingredients

- old partridge
- water
- cognac
- parsley
- celery
- potato
- salt
- butter
- sugar
- onion
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Prepare the partridge, setting aside the stomach, liver, and heart
2. Pour just enough water to cover the partridge and cook with cognac, parsley, celery, potato, and salt until about 2 cups of broth remain
3. Braise the reserved innards in fresh butter with sugar and onion
4. Push the innards into the broth
5. Serve plain or with bread roll to the patient
