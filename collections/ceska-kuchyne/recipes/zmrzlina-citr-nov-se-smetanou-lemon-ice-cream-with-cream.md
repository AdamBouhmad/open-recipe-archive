## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- Half liter cream
- 330 grams sugar
- One lemon

## Directions

1. Grate one lemon into 330 grams sugar
2. Place four eggs, half liter cream and the sugar on a warm stove
3. Constantly whip while heating
4. Remove from heat, let cool and constantly whip
5. Place in ice cream machines
