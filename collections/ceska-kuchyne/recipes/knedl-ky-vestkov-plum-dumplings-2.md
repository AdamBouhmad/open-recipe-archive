## Ingredients

- butter
- 1 kilogram flour
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 15 grams yeast (diluted with milk, sugar, and flour)
- milk
- prunes

## Directions

1. Heat a piece of butter
2. Place 1 kilogram flour on a baking sheet and salt it
3. Add heated butter, 2 egg yolks, and 2 whole eggs
4. Mix in 15 grams of yeast diluted with milk, sugar, and flour
5. Add as much milk as the dough can take
6. Knead thoroughly so it does not stick to the hand
7. Cut into pieces
8. Wrap a prune in each piece
9. Roll up well
