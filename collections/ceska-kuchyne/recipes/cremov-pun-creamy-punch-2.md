## Ingredients

- 4 liters water
- 2 kilograms sugar
- Juice of 8 lemons
- 4 liters arrack

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 kilograms of sugar in 4 liters of water
2. Add juice from 8 lemons and 4 liters of arrack
