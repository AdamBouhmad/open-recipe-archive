## Ingredients

- water
- 3-4 grams finely grated chocolate per cup desired

## Directions

1. Pour as many cups of water into a coffee pot as cups of chocolate you wish to make
2. Add 3-4 grams of finely grated chocolate per cup
3. Boil everything together and remove from heat to settle
4. Warm the cups in hot water beforehand
5. Once the chocolate foams up well, pour into the warmed cups
