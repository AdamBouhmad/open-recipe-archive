## Ingredients

- 12 decagrams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- Veal breast
- Bread roll

## Directions

1. Mix melted butter with 8 egg yolks
2. Cook veal breast until half done, cut into cutlets
3. Dip the cutlets in the butter and egg yolks
4. Sprinkle with grated bread roll and fry in butter
5. Serve with vegetables
