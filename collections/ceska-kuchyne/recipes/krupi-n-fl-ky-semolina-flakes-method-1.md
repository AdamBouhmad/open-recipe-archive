## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) of semolina
- butter
- 1/4 liter of hot milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Toast semolina in butter
2. Add hot milk
3. Salt it
4. Bake 1/2 hour in oven
5. Roll out dough like noodles
6. Let dry
7. Cut into flakes
8. Cook in salted water and drain
9. Add flakes to semolina with sugar and cinnamon
10. Place in greased form and bake
11. Serve with stewed fruit
