## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- 15 grams fresh butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and pour into a pan
2. Add 2 tablespoons flour and mix
3. Salt, add 15 grams fresh butter and pepper, mix well
4. Grease the omelet pan and pour the mixture into it
5. When golden on one side, turn to the other and let cook until golden
6. Fold and serve with meat filling if desired
