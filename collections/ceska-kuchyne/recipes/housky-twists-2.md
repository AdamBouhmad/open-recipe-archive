## Ingredients

- dough (same as for rohlíky)
- egg
- poppy seeds
- salt
- caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Divide leavened dough into small pieces
2. Roll them into longer pieces
3. Braid three pieces together to form one houska
4. Let them rise
5. Brush with beaten egg
6. Sprinkle thickly with unbeaten poppy seeds
7. Bake
8. Alternatively, sprinkle with salt and caraway, or poppy seeds mixed with salt
