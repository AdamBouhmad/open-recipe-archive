## Ingredients

- 17 liters (12 mázy) of cream
- 15 grams (1 lot) of finely beaten ice
- 3 liters (somewhat less than two mázy) of sour milk

## Directions

1. Add finely beaten ice to 17 liters of cream
2. Heat 3 liters of sour milk in the oven
3. When the sour milk is lukewarm, let it cool
4. In a short time it will churn
