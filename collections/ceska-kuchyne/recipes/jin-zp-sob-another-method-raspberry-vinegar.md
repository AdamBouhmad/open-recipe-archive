## Ingredients

- half a liter of ripe raspberries
- one liter of wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Crush half a liter of ripe raspberries and pour one liter of wine vinegar over them, seal the bottle and let it stand in the sun for 14 days, strain, and add to fish, salads, or use it to prepare lemonade.
