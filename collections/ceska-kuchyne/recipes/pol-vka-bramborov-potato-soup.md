## Ingredients

- Onion
- Fresh or clarified butter
- Green parsley
- Marjoram leaves
- Cooked potatoes
- Broth (recipe no. 38)
- Salt
- White butter roux
- Ginger

## Directions

1. Stew onion, green parsley, and marjoram leaves (all finely chopped) in fresh or clarified butter
2. Add cooked potatoes cut into rounds
3. Pour in the broth
4. Season with salt and cook
5. Make a white butter roux and use to thicken the soup
6. Add a little ginger
7. Mix well and simmer a little longer
8. Strain through a fine sieve and serve
