## Ingredients

- curd (tvaroh)
- alder leaves

## Directions

1. Gather alder leaves
2. Place alder leaves on the bottom of a container
3. Add a layer of curd on top
4. Continue alternating layers of alder leaves and curd until the container is full
5. Cover with alder leaves at the top
