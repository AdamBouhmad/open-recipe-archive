## Ingredients

- sage
- three and one-half deciliters of beef broth
- sauce from veal roast or roux from 30 grams of butter
- juice from half a lemon

## Directions

1. Place a piece of sage in three and one-half deciliters of beef broth
2. Add sauce from veal roast; if not available, use roux made from 30 grams of butter
3. Strain
4. Add juice from half a lemon
