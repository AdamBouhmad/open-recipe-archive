## Ingredients

- 16 whole eggs
- 1.5 deciliters (half a tablespoon) of sour cream
- Salt
- Chives
- Smoked meat, chopped
- 6 egg yolks
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix well 16 whole eggs, 1.5 deciliters of sour cream, a little salt and chives.
2. Bake omelets from this mixture and cut them into strips.
3. Mix chopped smoked meat into them, add 6 egg yolks, mix everything well, pour sour cream over and bake.
