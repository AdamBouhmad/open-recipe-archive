## Ingredients

- two whole eggs
- one teaspoon of melted butter
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Break two whole eggs into a small cup
2. Add one teaspoon of melted butter and just enough flour to make a thin batter
3. Beat it well
4. When the broth is boiling, slowly drip this mixture into the broth and stir frequently so that large lumps don't form
5. Let it cook for a few more minutes
6. If eggs are expensive, you can replace half an egg with milk
