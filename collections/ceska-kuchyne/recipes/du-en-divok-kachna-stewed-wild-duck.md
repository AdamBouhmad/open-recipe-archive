## Ingredients

- wild duck, plucked and cleaned
- cold water
- lard
- onion, sliced
- carrot
- parsley root
- celery
- bay leaf
- piece of ginger
- grains of pepper
- juniper berries
- cloves
- raw ham, thinly sliced
- salt
- water
- fresh mushrooms
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Place plucked and cleaned wild duck in cold water for half an hour, then lard it
2. Put on the bottom of a pot sliced onion, carrot, parsley root, celery, bay leaf, piece of ginger, several grains of pepper, juniper berries and cloves, and a piece of thinly sliced raw ham
3. Place the duck in this, salt it lightly, add some water and let it braise gently with frequent basting until tender; if it browns too much, cover it with paper
4. Make a sauce from fresh mushrooms, pour into it the strained broth from the duck
5. Pour the sauce over the duck and let it stand for another quarter hour in a warm place
6. The broth from the duck, browned with a roux, mixed with beef broth and cooked to a smooth sauce, is also good
