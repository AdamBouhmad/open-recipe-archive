## Ingredients

- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) coffee
- 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) cream
- 3 bread rolls, peeled and sliced
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) butter
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg whites
- a little vanilla
- bread roll crust for coating
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Cook 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of coffee in 3.5 deciliters (1 tablespoon) of cream
2. When settled, pour onto 3 peeled, sliced bread rolls and cook until it becomes a thick paste, stirring continuously
3. Add 6 dekagrams (4 lots) of butter, 7 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, and a little vanilla
4. Beat 2 egg whites into stiff snow and fold in
5. Beat all together
6. Grease a mold and coat with bread roll crust
7. Place a puff pastry lattice in the mold
8. Pour the prepared paste into the mold
9. Bake the soufflé and sprinkle with sugar
