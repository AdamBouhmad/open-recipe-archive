## Ingredients

- Ripe but not soft mirabelles
- River water
- Fresh water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Select ripe but not soft mirabelles
2. Place in river water and warm them—but do not boil
3. Transfer to fresh water
4. When cooled, place on a sieve to dry
5. Put in containers and cover with dissolved sugar
6. On the second day, pour off the sugar and cook it thicker, skim the foam, and pour hot sugar over the mirabelles
7. On the following day, cook the drained sugar further (if the sugar is insufficient to cover the mirabelles, add more dissolved and cooked sugar as needed), place the mirabelles in it, cook everything slowly, skim the foam, and cover the container
8. On the fourth day, cook the drained sugar to the sixth degree, add the mirabelles and cook with them several times, skim the foam, let everything cool, and place in containers
