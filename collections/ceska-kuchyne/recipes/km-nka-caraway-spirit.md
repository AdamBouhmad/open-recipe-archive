## Ingredients

- 1 liter rye spirits or diluted good alcohol
- 70 grams caraway seeds
- 250 grams crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Add 70 grams of caraway seeds and 250 grams of crushed sugar to 1 liter of spirits
2. Let it sit on warm stove for fourteen days, stirring frequently
3. Strain the liqueur into bottles and seal well
