## Ingredients

- 4 or 6 slices white bread
- Salted water
- 3 eggs
- Sweet sour cream
- Fresh butter
- Flower garnish

## Directions

1. Cut white bread into slices
2. Cook in salted water for about a quarter hour
3. Beat 3 eggs in sweet sour cream
4. Add this mixture to the boiling soup with a piece of fresh butter
5. Season with a little flower garnish
6. Serve in a soup dish
