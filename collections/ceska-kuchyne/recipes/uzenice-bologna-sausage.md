## Ingredients

- 2 parts beef meat
- 1 part pork meat
- salt
- pepper
- fresh intestines

## Directions

1. Take two parts beef meat and one part pork meat and chop finely or grind well through a mill
2. Season well with salt and pepper and mix thoroughly
3. Fill into fresh intestines
4. On the second day, smoke it and smoke for two days
5. When ready to use, bake for half an hour in a water bath
