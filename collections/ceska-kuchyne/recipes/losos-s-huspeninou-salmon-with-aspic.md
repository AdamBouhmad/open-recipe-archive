## Ingredients

- salt
- vinegar
- white wine
- lemon
- bay leaves
- onions
- vegetable spices
- salmon
- veal feet
- beef broth
- egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil salted water with vinegar, white wine, lemon sliced into rounds with centers removed, bay leaves, onions, and all vegetable spices
2. Add salmon pieces and boil for half an hour
3. Remove salmon from the broth
4. Add two to three cleaned veal feet and some beef broth to the cooking liquid, simmer until meat falls from the bones
5. Allow to cool, then next day remove fat and sediment
6. Add a little white wine and clarify with snow made from four eggs
7. Butter a mold and pour a finger's thickness of aspic into it, cool on ice
8. Decorate the bottom with cut lemon leaves, pour in a thin layer of warm aspic
9. Layer sliced salmon on top and alternate with aspic until the mold is nearly full
10. When the aspic is completely set, dip the mold in warm water and turn out onto a platter
11. Garnish the edge with parsley and boiled crayfish
