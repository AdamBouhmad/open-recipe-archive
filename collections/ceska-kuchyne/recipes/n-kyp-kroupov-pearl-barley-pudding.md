## Ingredients

- pearl barley
- salt
- warm milk or water
- 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) smoked meat
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 4 or 5 egg yolks
- egg whites
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Rinse the middle-sized pearl barley, salt it, pour warm milk or water on it or cook it in water until soft and let it cool
2. Finely chop 1/2 kilogram (1 pound) of smoked meat and mix it in, salt and pepper it, add nutmeg, 4 or 5 egg yolks and stiff snow from egg whites, or leave the eggs whole and do not make snow
3. Mix everything, grease the form with butter or lard and bake the mixture, usually on an oblong baking sheet so it can be cut into pieces
