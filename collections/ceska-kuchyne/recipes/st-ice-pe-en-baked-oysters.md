## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- grated breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- oyster liquid mixed with milk
- egg
- milk

## Directions

1. Grease a pan or deep dish with butter
2. Sprinkle the bottom with grated breadcrumbs
3. Place a layer of oysters, salt and pepper them
4. Place several small pieces of butter on them
5. Cover them with a layer of grated breadcrumbs
6. Moisten with oyster liquid mixed with milk
7. Continue this way until the dish is full
8. On top place a layer of breadcrumbs, dot with small pieces of butter
9. Pour over with egg beaten in milk
10. Bake in a hot oven for a quarter hour
11. If the dish is larger, it must bake a little longer
12. Serve at table in the dish in which it was prepared
