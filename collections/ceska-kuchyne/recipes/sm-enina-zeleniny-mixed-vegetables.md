## Ingredients

- asparagus heads
- young fresh peas
- young green bean pods
- cauliflower florets
- beef broth
- fresh butter
- salt
- vinegar
- wine
- pale roux

## Directions

1. Cut off asparagus heads, take several young fresh peas, several sliced young green bean pods and pieces of cauliflower florets, rinse everything, place on a baking sheet, pour good beef broth over it, add a piece of fresh butter, salt and stew
2. When the mixture is tender, add vinegar and wine, mix in pale roux, cook and place on a dish
