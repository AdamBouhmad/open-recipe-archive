## Ingredients

- noodle dough
- hard cooked eggs
- dill or parsley
- egg for brushing
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as for noodles, but do not roll too thin
2. Cut pieces with a mold or glass
3. Sprinkle with chopped hard eggs, dill or parsley
4. Fold the edges sealed with pressure until they stick
5. Brush with egg, roll in grated breadcrumbs
6. Fry in butter
