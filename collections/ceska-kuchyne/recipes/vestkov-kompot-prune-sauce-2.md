## Ingredients

- ripe plums
- sugar
- cinnamon stick
- lemon zest, cut into thin strips

## Directions

1. Halve ripe plums and remove the pits.
2. Cook them with sugar, a piece of cinnamon and lemon zest cut into thin strips, and when they become soft, take them out on a dish and pour thickly cooked syrup over them.
