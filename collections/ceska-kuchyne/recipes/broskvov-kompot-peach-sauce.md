## Ingredients

- sugar (one cup)
- water (five tablespoons)
- ripe peaches

## Directions

1. Put one cup of sugar with five tablespoons of water on a saucepan and let cook about 10 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, peel nice ripe peaches, halve them, and remove the pits.
3. Put them in the boiling syrup and let cook another 10 minutes.
