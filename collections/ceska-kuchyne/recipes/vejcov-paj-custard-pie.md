## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one quart of milk
- one teaspoon of ground vanilla
- one coffee cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of corn starch
- a pinch of salt
- dough

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs with one quart of milk
2. Add one teaspoon of ground vanilla, one coffee cup of sugar, one tablespoon of corn starch, and a pinch of salt
3. Mix everything well
4. Fill two dough-lined forms with this mixture and bake
5. Alternatively, you can use only yolks for the cream and make stiff snow from egg whites and sugar, spread it on the baked pies and let it set slightly in a hotter oven
