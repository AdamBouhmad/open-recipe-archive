## Ingredients

- three to four sweet oranges
- fine sugar
- isinglass
- water

## Directions

1. Rub three to four sweet oranges onto fine sugar
2. Crush isinglass and add with water
3. Cook everything together until half has boiled away
4. Add crushed sugar to taste, stirring constantly
5. Strain through clean thin cloth onto a dish
6. Let cool
