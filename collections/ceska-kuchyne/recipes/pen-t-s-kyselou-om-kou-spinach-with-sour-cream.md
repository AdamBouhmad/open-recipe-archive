## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt
- Water
- About 0.5 cup sour cream
- 3-4 tablespoons vinegar
- Same amount sugar
- 2 hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Clean spinach thoroughly and cook in boiling water with a pinch of salt
2. When cooked, cut finely with a knife
3. Mix together about 0.5 cup sour cream, 3-4 tablespoons vinegar and equal amount of sugar
4. Pour this mixture over the spinach
5. Garnish with 2 hard-boiled eggs cut into slices if desired
