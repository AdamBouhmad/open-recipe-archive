## Ingredients

- nice tomatoes
- one medium onion
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scald nice tomatoes, peel off the skin, slice into rounds and put on a platter
2. Chop one medium onion finely and add to them, salt, pepper and pour with vinegar mixed with water, sweetened to taste
