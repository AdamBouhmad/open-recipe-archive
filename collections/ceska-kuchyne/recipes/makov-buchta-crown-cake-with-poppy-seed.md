## Ingredients

- 1 small cup butter
- 8 yolks
- two pieces of yeast
- two tablespoons milk
- eight tablespoons sour cream
- half small cup finely chopped almonds
- zest of one lemon, chopped
- whites of eggs beaten to stiff snow
- 3 small cups flour
- 2 small cups ground poppy seed
- half small cup sugar
- salt
- butter for greasing
- almonds halved
- grated roll

## Directions

1. Put into a bowl 1 small cup butter, 8 yolks and rub well together
2. Dissolve two pieces of yeast with two tablespoons milk and eight tablespoons sour cream, put into the bowl
3. Add half small cup finely chopped almonds, zest of one lemon chopped and mix everything
4. Add stiff snow beaten from egg whites
5. Add 3 small cups flour, rub well into smooth batter
6. Add 2 small cups ground poppy seed, half small cup sugar and a little salt, mix everything well
7. Grease form with butter, line with almonds halved, sprinkle with grated roll
8. Put the batter into it, let it rise and bake
