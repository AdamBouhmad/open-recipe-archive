## Ingredients

- scraped potatoes
- hot cream or milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook scraped potatoes and strain the water from them
2. Mash the potatoes with a potato masher
3. Add a little hot cream or milk and a piece of butter
4. Salt and pepper everything and stir until the mash froths
5. Then stir it once more, place the mash on a dish and pour over it fresh butter in which you have fried a small piece of finely diced onion
6. American style is served without onion
