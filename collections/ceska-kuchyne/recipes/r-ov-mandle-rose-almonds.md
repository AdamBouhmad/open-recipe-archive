## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- rose water
- carmine dye dissolved in gum
- water

## Directions

1. Scald and blanch almonds, then dry
2. Add rose water to boiled sugar
3. When almonds are at least partially coated with sugar, add carmine dye dissolved in gum and water
4. This gives the almonds a nice red color
