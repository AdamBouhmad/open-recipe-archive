## Ingredients

- young leek
- salt
- butter
- bread crust

## Directions

1. Clean the young leek, cut off at the green leaves, tie several together, place in salted water with a piece of butter and cook until tender
2. Melt fresh butter, sprinkle the leek with grated fried bread crust and pour butter over it
