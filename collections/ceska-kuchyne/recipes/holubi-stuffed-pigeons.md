## Ingredients

- young pigeons
- gizzards and livers
- fresh parsley
- bread roll soaked in milk
- fresh butter
- ground clove
- pepper
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean young pigeons well and cut off the heads
2. Gut them and lift the skin on the breasts as far as you can
3. Chop the gizzards and livers with a little fresh parsley and bread roll soaked in milk
4. Put this in a bowl and mix it with a piece of fresh butter
5. Season with ground clove and pepper
6. Add two eggs, salt it and mix well
7. Fill the birds with this stuffing
8. Tie the neck with thread so the stuffing does not run out
9. Salt the pigeons and place them on a roasting pan
10. Put a piece of butter under them and a little broth or water
11. Let them roast, basting them frequently with butter
12. When they are tender, sprinkle them with grated bread roll crust and pour the roasted juices over them
