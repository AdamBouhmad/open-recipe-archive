## Ingredients

- Young string beans
- Salt water
- Butter
- White breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Shell young pods, cut off the tips, pull off the strings, cut each pod in half twice and cook them in salted water.
2. Then drain them, turn them out onto a dish, coat with butter and sprinkle with fried grated white bread.
