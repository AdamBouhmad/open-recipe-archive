## Ingredients

- partridges
- rice
- broth
- onion
- cloves
- partridge juices

## Directions

1. Roast the partridges.
2. Sort the rice and braise it in broth with onion and two cloves until the grains remain whole.
3. Pour the partridge juices over the rice.
4. Place the roasted partridges on the rice.
