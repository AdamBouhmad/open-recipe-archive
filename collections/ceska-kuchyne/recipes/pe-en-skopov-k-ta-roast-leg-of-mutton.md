## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- salt
- garlic
- water
- butter or lard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the leg of mutton thoroughly, remove skin and fat
2. Salt well on both sides
3. Insert garlic generously on both sides
4. Place on baking pan
5. Pour a little water under it
6. Let it roast, basting frequently with butter or lard
7. Baste at the end with the pan juices to give it good color
8. If the meat is older, pound well before salting
9. If very old, braise covered first, then roast in the oven when tender
10. Serve with boiled or roasted potatoes
11. Small potatoes may be scraped and added to the pan half an hour before serving to roast together
