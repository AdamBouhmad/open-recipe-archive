## Ingredients

- meat from sirloin roast
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Remove all membranes and sinews from meat
2. Cut into pieces about three fingers thick and flatten with a mallet
3. Salt and pepper the meat
4. Chop a little onion and fry until golden
5. Place beefsteak in it; if English style, leave for 5 minutes, if well-done, for a quarter hour
