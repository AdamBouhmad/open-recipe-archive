## Ingredients

- hard bread rolls, sliced
- milk
- salt
- egg
- smoked salmon
- grated bread
- butter
- sardine butter
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Slice hard rolls, soak in milk, salt, and drain
2. Dip in beaten egg
3. Slice the smoked salmon and place on bread slices
4. Top with another bread slice, coat with grated bread
5. Fry in butter
6. Brush with sardine butter and arrange on platter
7. Garnish with fried parsley
