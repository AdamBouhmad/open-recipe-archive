## Ingredients

- five or six medium potatoes
- one cup milk
- one tablespoon butter
- two egg yolks
- two egg whites
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel five or six medium potatoes and boil them.
2. When cooked, drain the water and push the potatoes through a sieve into a bowl.
3. Meanwhile, heat one cup of milk on the stove and add one tablespoon butter to it.
4. To the mashed potatoes add two egg yolks, salt and pepper, the hot milk with butter, and stiff snow beaten from two egg whites, and mix everything together.
5. Grease a mold with butter, pour the mixture into it, and bake in a hot oven.
6. Bake the pudding for about twenty minutes and serve immediately to the table.
7. If the pudding stands after baking, it will fall.
