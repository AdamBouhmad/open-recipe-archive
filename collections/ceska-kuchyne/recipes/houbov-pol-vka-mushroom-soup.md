## Ingredients

- Mushrooms (hříbky)
- A piece of butter
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley
- Water
- All kinds of vegetables
- Roux (bledá jíška)
- Salt
- 2 egg yolks
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Clean and slice the mushrooms and sauté them on a baking sheet with nothing else for a while
2. Add a piece of butter and pepper
3. Braise with chopped parsley
4. Cook all vegetables in water, add roux and salt
5. Add the mushrooms with 2 egg yolks
6. Pour over fried bread
