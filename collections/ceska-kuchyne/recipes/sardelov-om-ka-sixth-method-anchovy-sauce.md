## Ingredients

- large selected anchovies
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the anchovies and remove the bones
2. Pound them with fresh butter in a mortar
3. Press through a fine sieve
4. Place in a cold location
5. Cook brown coulis with strong beef broth
6. Remove fat and foam
7. Mix frequently and combine the sauce with the anchovy mixture prepared above
