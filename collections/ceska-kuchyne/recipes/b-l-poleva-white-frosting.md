## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 3/4 small cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract or juice from 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat stiff snow from 2 egg whites
2. Gradually fold in 3/4 small cup of powdered sugar
3. If desired, you can add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
4. Or add juice from 1/2 lemon, but you must add more sugar at the same time
5. Mix a little longer and spread on cooled cake
6. The frosting must be white as snow and as thick as drop cake batter
