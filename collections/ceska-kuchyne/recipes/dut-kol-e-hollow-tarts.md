## Ingredients

- 250 ml water
- 70 grams butter
- 70 grams fine flour
- six eggs
- salt
- grated lemon zest
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a quarter liter of water with 70 grams of butter
2. Add 70 grams of fine flour
3. Make a paste from everything on moderately warm stove
4. Let it cool
5. Beat six eggs into it
6. Season with salt
7. Add grated lemon zest and crushed sugar
8. When everything is well worked, remove the dough by spoon into heaps
9. Roll the tarts from these heaps
10. Place on a greased baking sheet
11. Bake until golden
