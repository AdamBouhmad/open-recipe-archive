## Ingredients

- cherries
- stems (cut in half)
- cloves (coarsely crushed)
- cinnamon (coarsely crushed)
- vinegar (previously cooked with sugar and cooled)

## Directions

1. Cut the stems in half, place cherries in jars for preserving
2. Sprinkle with coarsely crushed cloves and cinnamon
3. Pour over with cooled vinegar that was previously cooked with sugar
4. After eight days, pour off the vinegar, cook it again, skim off the foam, let the vinegar cool and pour it over the cherries again
