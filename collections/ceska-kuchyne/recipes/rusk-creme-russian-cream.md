## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- three eggs
- sugar with vanilla
- two deciliters cream
- a little rum or liqueur

## Directions

1. Mix six egg yolks and three eggs
2. Add sugar mixed with vanilla
3. Process everything until it foams
4. Add two deciliters of cream and a little rum or liqueur
