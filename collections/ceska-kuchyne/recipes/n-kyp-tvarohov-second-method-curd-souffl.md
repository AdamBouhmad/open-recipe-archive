## Ingredients

- quarter kilogram (half pound) butter
- 12 dekagrams (8 lots) almonds
- 4 whole eggs
- 10 egg yolks
- grated curd
- grated roll
- sugar
- raisins
- snow from 10 egg whites
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix quarter kilogram butter with 12 dekagrams almonds.
2. Add 4 whole eggs and 10 egg yolks one by one.
3. After each yolk, sprinkle grated curd and grated roll, sugar.
4. Add raisins, snow from 10 egg whites, and a little salt.
5. Mix everything well and put into a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with grated roll.
6. Sprinkle the baked soufflé with sugar.
