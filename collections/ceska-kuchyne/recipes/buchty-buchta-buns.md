## Ingredients

- one-eighth kilogram butter
- 6 decagrams sugar
- lemon zest
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- one kilogram flour
- 3 decagrams diluted yeast
- filling: preserves, curd, or poppy seed

## Directions

1. Rub one-eighth kilogram of butter on a baking sheet until foamy
2. Season with salt
3. Add 6 decagrams sugar, lemon zest, 4 egg yolks, and 2 whole eggs, and rub together
4. Add one kilogram of flour and mix with 3 decagrams of diluted yeast
5. Beat the dough until it no longer sticks to hands
6. Let it rise
7. Roll out and flatten pieces
8. Fill with preserves, curd, or poppy seed
9. Place on greased sheet
10. Oil them
11. Let rise for a quarter hour
12. Bake in oven
13. When baked, sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
