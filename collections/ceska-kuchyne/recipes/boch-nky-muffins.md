## Ingredients

- 1 quart buttermilk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon baking soda dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water
- salt
- flour
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Add egg yolks to buttermilk
2. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add
3. Salt the mixture
4. Add flour until dough is thicker than for pancakes
5. Fold in beaten egg white
6. Pour batter into greased molds or rings on a baking pan
7. Bake in a hot oven for 20 minutes
