## Ingredients

- semolina, not too fine
- milk
- piece of butter
- salt
- sugar
- 9 dekagrams sugar (6 lots)
- 5-6 eggs

## Directions

1. Put semolina into milk with a piece of butter
2. Stir diligently until a thick porridge forms
3. Let it cool, salt it, sweeten it and add 9 dekagrams sugar
4. Add 5-6 eggs and mix in
5. Bake the rolls in the usual way
