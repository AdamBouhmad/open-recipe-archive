## Ingredients

- kid meat
- butter
- onion
- flour
- milk
- egg yolks
- egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Carefully cut the kid meat into chops, pound them, pepper and salt.
2. Braise them in butter with finely chopped onion, dust with flour, and add milk.
3. Add two or three beaten egg yolks and continue cooking on the stove, stirring frequently, until completely thickened.
4. Coat the chops thickly with this mixture.
5. Dip them in beaten egg, coat in breadcrumbs, and fry in butter until golden brown.
