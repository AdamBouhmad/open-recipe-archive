## Ingredients

- 200 pounds meat
- 6 to 7 pounds salt
- 1 box mixed spices
- 2 cloves garlic

## Directions

1. Grind the mixed spices on a mill and add 2 cloves of garlic that have been peeled and finely crushed. Mix all of this together well.
2. Season the fresh meat with this mixture and layer it in a pot or barrel so there are no empty spaces between the pieces. Weight it down with a board or stone. This preserves the meat for a considerable time.
