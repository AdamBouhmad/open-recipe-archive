## Ingredients

- 375 grams finely crushed sifted sugar
- eight egg yolks
- four whole eggs
- finely cut lemon zest
- 375 grams fine potato flour
- 375 grams butter
- stiff snow made from eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 375 grams crushed sifted sugar and beat for at least half an hour in one direction with egg yolks and whole eggs, finely cut lemon zest, until completely thick
2. Gradually mix in 375 grams fine potato flour, 375 grams butter, and finally stiff snow made from eight egg whites
3. Make three cakes about half a centimeter thick from this dough
4. Bake slowly on a buttered sheet
5. Turn out of the pan, and when cool
6. Coat with various preserves and layer together
7. On top coat with some optional glaze
