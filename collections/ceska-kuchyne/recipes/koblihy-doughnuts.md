## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 6 dekagrams (4 lots) sugar
- lemon zest
- 2 tablespoons arrak or good rum
- 4 tablespoons diluted yeast
- flour
- warm sour cream
- butter
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 6 dekagrams sugar
2. Add chopped lemon zest, 2 tablespoons arrak or good rum, 4 tablespoons diluted yeast
3. Add enough flour (saving some for sprinkling) and warm sour cream to make a dough that does not stick to the hand when worked
4. Let rest; when risen, roll out, cut into circles for doughnuts, spread with jam, cover with another circle, trim again with a cutter and let rise a little
5. Put only as much butter on the baking sheet as absolutely necessary to give them a nice edge; as the butter is used up, add more by the piece
6. Cover the baking sheet with a lid, fry the doughnuts on both sides until golden and sprinkle with sugar while hot
