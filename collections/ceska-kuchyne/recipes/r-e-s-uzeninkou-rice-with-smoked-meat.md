## Ingredients

- One quarter kilogram of rice
- One quarter kilogram (one quarter pound) of butter
- Beef broth or water
- One quarter kilogram of smoked meat
- Pepper
- Young peas
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice, place on a baking sheet, mix with one quarter kilogram of butter.
2. Pour beef broth or water over, and braise until softened.
3. Strip all bones from one quarter kilogram of smoked meat, chop finely, carefully mix with rice and pepper.
4. If there is young peas, add it, which was braised in butter with a piece of sugar.
5. Usually this is used to surround beef meat.
