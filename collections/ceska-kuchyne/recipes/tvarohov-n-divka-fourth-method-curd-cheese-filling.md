## Ingredients

- finely shredded and rubbed curd cheese
- egg yolks
- sugar
- salt
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix and rub the finely shredded curd cheese
2. Add egg yolks and sugar
3. Salt it
4. Pour sour cream over it
5. The filling must be quite thick yet juicy
