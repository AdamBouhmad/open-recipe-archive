## Ingredients

- elderflower with stem
- eggs
- salt or sugar
- bread crust and flour (mixed)
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the elderflower with the stem, clean it well so no bugs remain in the flower
2. Place salt or sugar into beaten egg, dip the flower in it, roll in mixed bread crust and flour and place in hot butter
3. Fry until golden pink
4. Arrange on a dish with stems pointing up, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm
