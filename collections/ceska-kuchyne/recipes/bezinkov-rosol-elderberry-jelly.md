## Ingredients

- Elderberries
- Water
- Apples
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take elderberries, remove stems, wash them and put them to cook with a little water.
2. At the same time, put apples to cook.
3. When the elderberries burst and the water is darkly colored, strain the water from both the elderberries and apples.
4. To three parts apple juice add one part elderberry water.
5. Take one part (or slightly more) sugar and two parts of this mixture and cook in a double boiler.
6. When it thickens with constant stirring, store in a cool place.
