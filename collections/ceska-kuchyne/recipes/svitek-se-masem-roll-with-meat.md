## Ingredients

- leftovers of roast
- bread roll
- warm milk
- salt
- chopped lemon peel
- tablespoon flour
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the roast
2. Cut bread roll and soak in warm milk, stir to form a paste
3. Mix in the meat, salt it, add chopped lemon peel, tablespoon flour and 5 eggs
4. Mix everything well, pour onto a greased pan and bake on both sides
