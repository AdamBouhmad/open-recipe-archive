## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 1 bread roll
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoon minced parsley
- 2 pounds fish
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in well salted water
2. Soak a small piece of bread in cold water and wring dry in a clean cloth
3. Stir in salt, pepper and 1 tablespoon minced parsley
4. Clean 2 pounds fish, slice into thin pieces and lay in cold salt water
5. When potatoes are cooked, peel, mash through a sieve and season with pepper and salt
6. Butter a pudding mold in the bottom with butter and lay a layer of bread and a layer of fish alternately in it
7. Lay a neat layer of potato mixture on top and bake for 1 hour in a not too hot oven
