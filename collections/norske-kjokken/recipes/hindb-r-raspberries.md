## Ingredients

- 1 pound good raspberries
- 1 pound white sugar

## Directions

1. Pick and mix 1 pound good raspberries with 1 pound white sugar
2. The next day put the whole thing on the stove and simmer 10-12 minutes with a couple of boilings, skimming
3. Then strain through a sieve so the juice can run off and cook and skim this for 8-10 minutes
4. When removed from the stove, add the berries to blend with the juice and pour into jars
