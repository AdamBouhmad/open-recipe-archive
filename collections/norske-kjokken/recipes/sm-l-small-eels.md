## Ingredients

- Eels
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. When they are skinned and the fins are cut off, wash them very carefully and dry well.
2. Then cut them into pieces of a finger's length, and put them in a pot with just enough cold water that it stands over them, a little salt is added, as well as chopped parsley, and it cooks slowly, covered, in a good time.
3. Then add a large spoonful of butter and a bit of flour stirred in as little cold water as possible.
4. Arrange in a deep dish. The small eels are the best.
