## Ingredients

- 1 part unripe gooseberries
- 6 egg yolks
- ground cinnamon and sugar of rubber to taste
- some old white bread
- 2 small cups thereof
- 3 spoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. 1 part unripe gooseberries are peeled, shelled and fried in the oven until they become yellow, or given a boiling in water and sieved on a sieve until they are dry.
2. 6 egg yolks are stirred well with a little ground cinnamon and rubber sugar to taste.
3. Some old white bread is grated, of which 2 small cups are used, which are fried in 3 spoonfuls of butter, and when it is a bit cooled, it is stirred into the eggs together with the gooseberries, and the beaten whites.
4. It is baked in an hour's time.
