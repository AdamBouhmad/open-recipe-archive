## Ingredients

- Raspberries, currants, pears, and all kinds of fruit
- Water
- Sugar
- Corn starch

## Directions

1. In summer, cook in very little water with sugar and an imperceptible seasoning of corn starch.
2. Pears and apples are peeled and cut in pieces.
3. Cherry stones are removed.
4. For cherries, add a few whole cloves and no seasoning is used.
