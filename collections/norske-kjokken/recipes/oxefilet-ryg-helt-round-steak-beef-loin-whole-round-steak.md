## Ingredients

- Round steak
- Butter
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Bay leaf
- Water
- Flour
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the round steak into pieces as thick as a finger, beat them with a mallet, and chop them a little with a knife.
2. Place a piece of butter on the bottom of a baking dish.
3. Lay a layer of meat on it, sprinkle with a little chopped onion, salt, pepper, and spices, a couple of bay leaves, and a little butter.
4. Add another layer of meat, then the vegetables and butter, and so on.
5. Place it under gentle heat and cook until the meat begins to soften, then gradually add boiling water until it barely covers the meat.
6. When it has cooked for a couple of hours, it is tender.
7. Mix a little flour in a little bouillon or water and add it to the sauce.
8. One can also flour the meat, brown it in butter, add the same vegetables and as much boiling water as to cover the meat, and cook it for a couple of hours until the meat is tender.
9. Many like potatoes with this; these are added, peeled and cut, half an hour before the meal is done.
