## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine chocolate
- 1 pint cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate and dissolve it in 1 pint of cream
2. Place it in a high pot, set over gentle heat and cook
3. When it has cooked for 10 minutes, whisk it with an egg whisk until it froths strongly and pour it little by little into 2 egg yolks that are whisked throughout the entire work
4. Place it back in the pot and whisk continuously with the egg whisk
5. All the formed foam is transferred with a skimming spoon into pre-warmed cups, and this is continued until it stands piled up in the form of a dome over the cups
6. A tablespoon that is made glowing hot is held quite close over the foam until a kind of crust forms, after which the chocolate is served.
7. This chocolate froths even better when some sugar is stirred into the egg yolks, or also when foam birds are used, which are prepared in the following way: a sufficient number of egg whites are whisked to very stiff foam, and into it is stirred so much fine powdered sugar that the mass forms a stiff dough. This is spread on a baking sheet, and when it is about to dry, small birds are formed from it, of the size of a medium nut, which are then dried completely in the sun or by room heat. After the egg yolks in the above manner are whisked together with the chocolate, a foam bird is placed in the chocolate for each cup and whisked for a long time over the fire until all the chocolate mass is dissolved into almost a foam. One must observe the precaution that the chocolate is only warmed until nearly boiling; it must not boil at all.
