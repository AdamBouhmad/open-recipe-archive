## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet and 2 ounces bitter almonds
- 1.5 pounds dry powdered sugar
- clean peel of lemon
- 6 egg whites beaten to stiff foam

## Directions

1. 1 pound sweet and 2 ounces bitter almonds are shelled and blanched.
2. Then they are stirred with 1.5 pounds dry powdered sugar, clean peel of lemon and 6 egg whites, first beaten to stiff foam.
3. One now forms wreaths of the dough, one constantly smaller than the other, so they can be laid one on top of the other when they are baked, and form a whole cake.
4. They are baked lightly at even heat.
5. If one only bakes small wreaths, there result approximately 40 of these.
