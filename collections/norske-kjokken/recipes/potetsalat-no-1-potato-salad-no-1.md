## Ingredients

- Salad vegetables
- Butter
- Egg yolk
- Vinegar
- Cream

## Directions

1. Pick salad vegetables so no bad leaves remain; wash well and cut into 7 to 8 pieces.
2. Stir butter into an egg yolk, and add a little vinegar and cream.
3. Place the salad in this and let it rest somewhat before serving.
