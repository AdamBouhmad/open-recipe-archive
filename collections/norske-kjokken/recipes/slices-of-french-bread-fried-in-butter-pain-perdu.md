## Ingredients

- Slices of French bread
- Butter
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Dip the slices of French bread in milk and fry them in butter on both sides.
2. When fried on both sides, pour the batter over and bake in good fire, not held back by the flames, until it shines on top.
3. It does not need to be turned, and is arranged with white sugar on top.
