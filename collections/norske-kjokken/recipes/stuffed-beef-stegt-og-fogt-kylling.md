## Ingredients

- Beef
- Parsley
- Chives
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Bread
- Eggs
- Milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and herbs (parsley and chives) into fine pieces and layer them on the meat
2. Alternatively, dip the meat in butter and clarified butter that has been melted on the stove, dust with bread and roast it
3. Serve with vegetables
