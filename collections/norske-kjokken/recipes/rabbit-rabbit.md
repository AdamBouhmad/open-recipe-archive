## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Heart and liver of rabbit
- Fat bacon
- Salt

## Directions

1. It is best to have the butcher kill the rabbit, then it is easy to clean it
2. The heart and liver are cooked and chopped finely and mixed with a piece of fat bacon, which is also cut finely, then a stuffing is made like for poultry
