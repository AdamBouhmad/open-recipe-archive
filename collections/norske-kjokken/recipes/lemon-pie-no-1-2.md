## Ingredients

- pie dough
- zest of 2 lemons
- 1 grated apple
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar
- a small piece of melted butter
- 2 tablespoons corn starch
- juice of 2 lemons
- a little warm water

## Directions

1. Line the pan with ordinary pie dough.
2. Rub together the zest of 2 lemons with a grated apple; whisk 2 egg yolks with 1 cup sugar, a small piece of melted butter, 2 tablespoons corn starch and the juice of 2 lemons; stir in a little warm water, and when it is all well mixed, pour it over the dough.
3. Then handle it exactly as Egg Pie: bake, spread with butter and place in the oven again.
