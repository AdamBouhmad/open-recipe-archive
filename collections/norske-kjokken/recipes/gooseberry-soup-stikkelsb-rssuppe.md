## Ingredients

- 1 quart gooseberries
- 2 quarts water
- 3 egg yolks
- white sugar
- whole cinnamon

## Directions

1. Can be made of both unripe and ripe gooseberries. It is made partly with eggs. Place 1 quart gooseberries over heat in 2 quarts water; they do not need to be hulled.
2. Meanwhile whisk 3 egg yolks with plenty of white sugar in the tureen.
3. When the berries are cooked, strain the soup and return to heat with a little whole cinnamon and sugar, as it is uncertain if the eggs will be sufficient to complement the berries' natural flavor.
4. When it has boiled some, whisk it into the eggs and serve with bread.
