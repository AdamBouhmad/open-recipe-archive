## Ingredients

- 1 quart strawberries
- ½ pint water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Hull and cook 1 quart strawberries in ½ pint water.
2. Cook until all the juice is drawn out of the berries.
3. Strain through a fine sieve and return to the heat with sugar, according to how sour the berries are; brown sugar may be used.
4. Set aside a little of the soup when it is done, and stir into it a heaping spoonful of cornstarch.
5. When the soup is near boiling, stir this mixture in, cook up again and it is ready to serve.
6. Serve with small rusks.
7. If it seems too thick, it can be thinned.
