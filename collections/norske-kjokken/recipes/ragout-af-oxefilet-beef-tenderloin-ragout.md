## Ingredients

- 2 pounds round steak
- Butter
- Flour
- Carrot
- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water or broth
- Parsley
- Bread

## Directions

1. Cut 2 pounds of round steak into square pieces, two thumbs on each side, and fry them brown in butter.
2. Stir in 2 ounces of flour and brown it.
3. Cut a carrot into pieces about the size of a thumb.
4. Peel and mince three small onions.
5. Add a handful of mushroom slices to the meat along with a tablespoon of salt, a teaspoon of pepper, and enough water to cover the meat.
6. Skim if necessary.
7. Cook until the meat is tender.
8. Remove the meat and check if there is enough salt, adding more as needed.
9. A tablespoon of chopped parsley can be sprinkled over it, or some slices of toasted bread can be arranged around the platter when it is served.
