## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- carrots
- parsnips
- turnip
- salt
- marjoram or sage
- soup from salt meat or fish
- fresh fish

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cut them into pieces and set them on the fire with enough cold water to cover them
2. When they have cooked somewhat, add carrots and parsnips and a good turnip; remove this when it is tender and press it well
3. Cook some whole small onions with it along with salt and a little marjoram or sage
4. You can add a little less water later and eventually season the soup with soup from salt meat or fish
5. Fresh fish can also be cut into small cubes, cooked with the potatoes and served in the soup
