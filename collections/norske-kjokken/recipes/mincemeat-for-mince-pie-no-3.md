## Ingredients

- 3 pints grated and chopped apples
- 1 pint cooked, chopped beef
- 0.5 pint butter or beef fat
- 1 pint molasses
- 0.5 pint water
- 0.5 tablespoon allspice
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 0.5 tablespoon cloves
- 2.5 large tablespoons vinegar
- 0.5 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make quickly in the usual way, the form being lined with good dough; a thin layer of filling is added, dough is laid on top as a lid, holes are cut in the lid, and it is baked in a quick oven; it shall be quite light brown, but not hard.
