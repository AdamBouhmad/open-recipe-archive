## Ingredients

- pears
- apples
- sugar
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Peel and core pears and apples and cook together with sugar and a little cornstarch
2. Wash them well and soak them, with water about halfway up
3. Cook them in the same water and add sugar and a little dissolved cornstarch
