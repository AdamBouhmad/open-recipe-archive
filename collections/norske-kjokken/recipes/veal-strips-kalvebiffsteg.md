## Ingredients

- tender veal
- flesh
- brown butter
- water or meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the most tender veal into strips, 13 centimeters long, 2 fingers wide and somewhat broader than the thumb, though exact proportions are not critical
2. Beat the strips gently so they do not fall apart and season them well with flesh, leaving only one side uncovered
3. Brown butter in a shallow pan and brown the strips on all sides where they are seasoned, until the flesh is penetrated through
4. Turn the other side, which is raw, and when this is brown, add a little water or meat broth and cook on this side until thoroughly cooked
5. They are best when not overcooked but slightly juicy
6. When removed, stir a little flour into the sauce
7. You may also dip the strips in egg and pepper before seasoning, but then do not add flour to the sauce, only a piece of cold butter to finish it
