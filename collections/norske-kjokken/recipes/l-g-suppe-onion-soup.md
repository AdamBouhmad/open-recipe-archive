## Ingredients

- 1 saucepan full of peeled onions
- 6 spoonfuls of flour
- butter
- soup

## Directions

1. Brown the peeled onions in butter
2. Stir in 6 spoonfuls of flour
3. Add the soup gradually
4. Let it simmer for some time
5. Skim off the foam
6. Bring to a boil again
7. Serve with toasted bread
