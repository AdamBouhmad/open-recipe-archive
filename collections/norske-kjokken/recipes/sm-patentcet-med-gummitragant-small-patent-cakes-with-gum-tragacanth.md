## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 5 cents gum tragacanth
- soft sugar
- 2 ounces finely ground sweet almonds

## Directions

1. The evening before the cakes are to be baked, pour 1 egg white over 5 cents gum tragacanth, which stands until the next morning
2. Then stir it well and then stir with as much soft sugar as it can take and 2 ounces finely ground sweet almonds
3. Small pieces of dough are rolled with the hand against the pastry board into long strips, cut as for pralines and baked light brown on the buttered pan
