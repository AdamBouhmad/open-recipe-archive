## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 pint stock
- mustard
- 3 to 4 spoonfuls vinegar
- white wine
- pepper
- salt
- lobster or anchovy essence
- cooked parsnip
- cooked small pasta

## Directions

1. Stir 4 egg yolks in 1 pint stock and cook as for cream
2. When cooled, stir in mustard, 3 to 4 spoonfuls vinegar, white wine, a little pepper and salt, and if desired, some lobster or anchovy essence
3. In this sauce, which must be smooth, add a cooked parsnip, shelled and in small finely broken pieces, and cooked macaroni
4. Mix well together and let it stand a couple of hours before using
