## Ingredients

- Apple slices
- Sugar
- Horseradish (scraped)
- Butter (small pieces)

## Directions

1. Weigh the whole apples and calculate pound for pound of sugar with the fruit.
2. The fruit is peeled neatly, and the peelings are cut into small strips.
3. They are cooked tender, and the water is changed twice, each time with warm water.
4. The juice from the apples is poured over the sugar; this is brought to a boil, after which the strips are put in and it cooks for two minutes.
