## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1 pound coarse barley grains
- sugar
- whole cinnamon stick
- lemon zest
- salted juice

## Directions

1. Set 2 quarts of water on the fire, when it boils, stir in 1 pound coarse barley grains; when they are completely softened, strain the soup through a fairly fine sieve so that no grain follows along, and set it again over the fire with sugar to taste, a little whole cinnamon and lemon zest; when it has come to a boil, it is poured into the tureen, served with salted juice and eaten with toasted bread.
