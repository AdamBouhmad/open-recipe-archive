## Ingredients

- Red beets
- Vinegar
- Water
- Butter
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cherry juice or currant juice

## Directions

1. The outer leaves and stems are removed from red beets, which are cut into fine strips and placed on the fire with some vinegar, from which it becomes red, and a little water, and cooked with a lid over a gentle fire. If the beets dry out, more vinegar or bouillon is added to it, but not more than to make the beets moist. When tender, butter or fat from beef bouillon is added to it, and finally sugar, a little cherry or currant juice and salt. The sauce can be thickened.
