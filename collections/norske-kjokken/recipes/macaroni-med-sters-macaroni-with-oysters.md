## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Salt
- Water
- Butter
- Oysters

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in salted water and drain the water from it
2. Butter a deep pan, and layer macaroni with a layer of raw oysters and oysters
3. The top layer should be macaroni and over it lay small pieces of butter
4. Bake until it is brown
