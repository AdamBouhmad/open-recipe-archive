## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound powdered sugar
- 0.5 pound flour
- 4 eggs separated
- juice and half the zest of 1 lemon
- plenty of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bake on a greased sheet in long narrow cakes. It is advisable to test one and should it run out, beat the batter still firmer and add just a bit of flour, but the oven must be quite hot and the cakes should be golden brown. It tastes good afterward to dip them in chocolate glaze or caramel.
