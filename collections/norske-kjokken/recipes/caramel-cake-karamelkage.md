## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4.5 cups flour
- 5 eggs
- lemon zest and juice from 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat together butter and sugar as usual, then add the other ingredients. Beat the whites stiffly and fold in, as well as sifted flour which comes in by itself. Between each layer of cake place a filling made like for cocoa layer cake jelly cake, except cocoa is omitted. On top of each layer spread cold and chopped almonds, about a thumb's distance apart.
