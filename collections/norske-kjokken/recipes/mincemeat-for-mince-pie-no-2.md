## Ingredients

- 2 pounds raisins
- 1 pound currants
- 1 pound beef fat
- 2.5 pounds brown sugar
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 2 tablespoons powdered beef
- 3 lemons with peel and juice
- 1 nutmeg (grated)

## Directions

1. Wine and vinegar according to taste.
