## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
- salt
- zest and juice of 1 lemon
- butter for greasing the pan

## Directions

1. Whip the 6 egg yolks for half an hour with 6 tablespoons powdered sugar, salt, and the zest and juice of the lemon, whisking two and stirring together carefully.
2. Grease the pan with butter, pour in the batter and bake in five minutes over moderate heat until it rises; then place it in a bed of coals and bake for an hour.
3. It cannot be taken from the pan and is served with hot butter.
4. This is an exceptionally fine and delicious omelet; but one must have good patience in stirring and whisking the eggs, as the goodness of it depends on this; otherwise it becomes heavy.
