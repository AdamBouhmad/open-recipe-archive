## Ingredients

- 1 pound chocolate
- 2 quarts sweet milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Grate and cook 1 pound of chocolate in 2 quarts of sweet milk, stirring constantly until it boils.
2. After some time of boiling, pour it into the tureen where the yolks are whisked with sugar and a few drops of good vanilla extract; you must whisk into it until the soup is poured up.
