## Ingredients

- 2 pounds trout
- salt
- water
- 1 pint oysters
- 1 ounce butter
- 1 ounce flour
- 1 egg yolk
- half teaspoon salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the trout in salted water according to instructions.
2. Once the trout is done, remove the head from the fire and set it aside.
3. Meanwhile, set a pint of oysters over the fire to cook in their own sauce.
4. Once the oysters are cooked, strain the sauce and set it back over the fire to boil.
5. Melt one ounce of butter over the fire in a frying pan, stir in one ounce of flour.
6. Thicken the sauce with one beaten egg yolk, half a teaspoon of salt and a pinch of cayenne pepper.
7. Add the oysters to the sauce and serve in a sauce bowl.
