## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine butter
- 1 pound powdered sifted sugar
- 4 eggs
- 10 drops vanilla
- 1 pound moistened potato flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 pound fine butter until creamy with 1 pound powdered sifted sugar
2. First then add one by one 4 eggs, 10 drops vanilla, scraped as finely as possible, and 1 pound moistened potato flour
3. Place on the buttered pan with a spoon and bake light yellow
4. These cakes are delicious, but one must not spare effort in stirring the dough
