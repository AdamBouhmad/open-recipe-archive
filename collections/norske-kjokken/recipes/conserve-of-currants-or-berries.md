## Ingredients

- Currants or berries
- Loaf sugar
- Bitter almond or lemon zest

## Directions

1. For each pound of the largest and best berries, take 1 pound of loaf sugar, a little bitter almond or lemon zest, with which they are cooked and skimmed, until they are no longer sour, usually in a quarter.
2. They are poured off so that a cloth is spread over and the next day they are bound up. One can cook small pieces of apple together with the berries, which much improves them by removing the bitter taste.
