## Ingredients

- 6 heaping cups sugar
- 1 cup cider vinegar
- 1 cup molasses water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon saleratus dissolved in a little warm water

## Directions

1. Mix the three first parts together and cook for one hour without stirring
2. Then add butter and soda
3. Handle as the previous recipe
