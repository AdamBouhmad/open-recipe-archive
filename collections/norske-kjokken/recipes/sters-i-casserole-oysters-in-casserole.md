## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Butter a casserole dish and line the sides and all corners with bread dough
2. The oysters should be as large and fine as possible; drain all liquid from them
3. Season the oysters with pepper, salt and butter
4. Lay them on the pan with as much of their own sauce as desired, and sprinkle three hard-boiled, finely chopped egg yolks and raw bread over
5. Roll a layer toward the edge and press the edge in scallops
6. Bake with brisk heat
7. One can also add a half pint of cream with a little corn starch stirred in
