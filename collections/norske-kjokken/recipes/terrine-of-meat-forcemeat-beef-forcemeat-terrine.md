## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- butter
- greens
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Force is placed in an oval dish of either tin or lined copper, which is greased with butter.
2. In the middle a short hollow is made so that there is only about a hand's breadth of forcemeat all around on all sides.
3. This hollow is filled with finely chopped greens, which should not be too wet; force, as thin as a finger, is smeared over and sprinkled with butter; then it is placed in the oven on a trivet to cook through.
4. The oven must have heat like for sweet bread.
5. Of all kinds of greens, shelled green peas and white or green cabbage are best, because they are improved by the meat flavor and do not ruin it with their taste.
6. The peas should be cooked with a little stock or good broth.
