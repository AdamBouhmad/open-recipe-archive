## Ingredients

- About 20 pieces of parsnip
- Carrots
- A short piece of marrow bone, cut into 4 pieces
- 4 to 6 tablespoons of potato starch or wheat flour (optional)
- Salt

## Directions

1. After the soup is skimmed, add about 20 pieces of parsnip, carrots, and a short piece of marrow bone cut into 4 pieces.
2. When both parts are tender, remove them; if a smooth soup is desired, stir 4 to 6 tablespoons of unbeaten potato starch or wheat flour into the soup and let it boil until the meat is tender and the soup is smooth.
3. After this is done, set it back on the fire together with the carrots and marrow bone, which are cut into smaller pieces and get a good finishing.
4. Serve with toasted bread.
