## Ingredients

- peeled and sliced peas
- 4 quarts water
- bushel of peas of average size
- a good and large turnip
- many carrots and parsnips
- 2½ ounces flour
- 3 ounces butter
- salt
- 1 spoonful sugar
- sorrel or parsley

## Directions

1. Place peeled and sliced peas in a pot with 4 quarts water for a bushel of peas of average size
2. Once they begin to cook, add a good and large turnip and many carrots and parsnips
3. When these are tender, remove them and cut into smaller pieces
4. Press the turnip well
5. Mix approximately 2½ ounces flour and 3 ounces butter together for seasoning and combine with the soup
6. Cook together with salt, 1 spoonful sugar, sorrel or parsley
