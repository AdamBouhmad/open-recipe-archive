## Ingredients

- cooked fish
- cooked parsnip
- canned mushrooms
- stock
- vinegar
- soft roe and mustard blossom
- salt

## Directions

1. The fish can be of various kinds; when cooked, it is shelled from the skin and bones in flat pieces
2. Make a sauce from a pint of stock, which can preferably be sour, stirred with 2 spoonfuls split mushrooms together with their vinegar, a little soft roe and mustard blossom and salt
3. If the sauce is not sour enough, add a little vinegar
4. Instead of soft roe, salad oil can be used; instead of mushrooms, capers can be used
