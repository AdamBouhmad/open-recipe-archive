## Ingredients

- Round Beef
- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- browned butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. The meat is scraped from the sinews, which is done so that everything is included and only the clear sinews remain.
2. The meat is then formed with the blade of a knife into round, flat patties; salt and pepper are sprinkled on and they are fried in abundant browned butter.
3. Serve with browned butter and potatoes.
