## Ingredients

- Porterhouse or Sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion
- flour
- stock or bouillon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Have the butcher slice it into thick or thin slices, as desired; thick slices are beaten well on both sides, then sprinkled with salt and pepper, and fried in brown butter.
2. When they are sufficiently cooked, place them on a platter, a piece of butter is added to the pan, in which a tablespoonful of flour is stirred, a little chopped onion and a little ground pepper, after which it is mixed with boiling water.
3. If you have stock, a better sauce is made as follows: when the meat is taken from the pan, butter is added and stirred well with a spoon; when it is brown, chopped onion is added and cooked a little. Then good stock and ground pepper are stirred in.
