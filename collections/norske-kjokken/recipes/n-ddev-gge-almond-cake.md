## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup cold water
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 cupful of walnut kernels, chopped and fried until crispy, together with the beaten whites

## Directions

1. It is difficult to obtain good butterdough in summer, partly because one usually uses summer butter for it, from which good cannot result, partly because the butter in the heat is too soft to be handled in butterdough.
2. To remedy this, it is proper to keep a crock of fresh butter in a cool place for this use, let the unsalted butter be placed in the night over in cold water, press it out thereof with a spoon, slap it dry in a cloth and keep the dough in a cool place.
