## Ingredients

- pumpkins
- cold water
- brine
- boards
- weight

## Directions

1. Select ripe pumpkins that are not yellow. Peel, remove seeds, and cut into strips the thickness of apple peel and about a finger's length. Soak in cold water for 24 hours, then remove and place in brine for 24 hours. Then place between two boards with a light weight on top to extract the liquid, as pumpkins are more porous than cucumbers and absorb more brine. Dry well and preserve like pickled peppers.
