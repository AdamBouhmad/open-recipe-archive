## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter, melted
- 1 pound wheat flour
- Nearly 3 quarts boiling water
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and bake 1 pound wheat flour in it until it slips easily from the spoon.
2. Bit by bit pour nearly 3 quarts of boiling water onto it, so the porridge boils well between each addition and with strong stirring so it does not become lumpy.
3. It becomes very thin from this amount, but it thickens at the finishing, which must be done as soon as it is cooked; add salt.
4. Instead of baking the porridge whole, it can after it is prepared be fried on the griddle in small flat cakes.
5. These are eaten warm with sugar, butter, stewed apples or strawberries.
