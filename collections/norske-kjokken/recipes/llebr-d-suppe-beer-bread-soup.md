## Ingredients

- 1 quart water
- 1 teaspoon of butter
- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoons coarse salt
- 0.5 tablespoon pepper
- 1 quart ale

## Directions

1. Take 1 quart water, 1 teaspoon of butter, 1 pint milk, 2 tablespoons coarse salt and 0.5 tablespoon pepper; let the whole come to a quick boil and then add 1 quart ale, let it boil quickly up and then set aside from the stove
