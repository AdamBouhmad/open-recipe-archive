## Ingredients

- 10 large yellow cucumbers
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape, slice, and hollow out the cucumbers and place them in cold well water for 24 hours.
2. Remove them, dry them, and sprinkle them well with salt.
3. Lay them tightly together and let them stand for another 24 hours.
4. Remove them, place them on a sieve to drain, and dry them.
