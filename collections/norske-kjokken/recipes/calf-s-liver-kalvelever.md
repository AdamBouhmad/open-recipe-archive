## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- salt
- ground pepper
- cloves
- bouillon or egg and water
- butter
- flour
- honey
- egg
- red or white wine

## Directions

1. When the intestines are removed from the liver, place it in several changes of cold water so the blood can be drawn out, then season it with flesh
2. Brown a piece of butter and turn the liver several times in it
3. When it is light brown, add salt, ground pepper, and a couple of whole cloves
4. Add enough bouillon or egg and water so there is enough sauce
5. When the liver is tender, remove it and strain the sauce if needed, return it to the heat and thicken it with flour mixed in egg, soda, or a little cold wine
6. Pour the sauce over the liver
7. It gives good flavor to cook some whole eggs with the liver and serve them around it
8. The wine may be omitted
9. Alternatively, cut the liver into fairly thick pieces and cook on the stove in enough butter and lard or clarified butter to allow it to simmer
10. When the liver is cooked through and placed on a plate, pour off the fat from the pan, add some cold butter, and when this is brown, gradually stir in cold water or egg until the sauce is smooth, then pour over the liver
