## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 raw egg yolks
- 0.5 pound white or butter sugar
- 0.5 pound unwashed butter
- 0.5 pound flour

## Directions

1. Beat hard-boiled yolks and mix well with raw yolks, sugar and butter and flour. Mix to a stiff dough from which small pieces are taken, dipped in flour, rolled with the teeth and formed into small wreaths which are smeared with egg white on one side, strewed with white, coarse, soft sugar and baked light brown.
