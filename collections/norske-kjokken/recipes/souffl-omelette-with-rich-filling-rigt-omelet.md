## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons powdered sugar
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 6 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks for half an hour with 6 tablespoons powdered sugar, and also juice and zest of 1 lemon
2. Beat the whites stiff and stir in carefully
3. Grease the mold with butter, pour the batter in and let it stand for 5 minutes over heat to rise
4. Then put it in a hot oven and bake for 1.5 hours
5. It cannot be taken from the mold and is served with hot butter
