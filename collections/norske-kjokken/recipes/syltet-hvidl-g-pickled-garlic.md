## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- strong brine (salt water)

## Directions

1. Select fine, tender garlic cloves, not large ones. Let stand 4 days in strong brine, changing it twice. Peel the cloves before placing them in the brine. Bring the brine to a boil, add the cloves, and boil for 3 minutes. Then place them in cold water and leave for 3 hours. Pack them in crocks, layered with whole nutmeg, white peppercorns, and cloves. Fill with boiling vinegar in which 1 cup sugar per gallon of vinegar has been boiled. Bind while still warm. They can be used after a month, but are better later.
