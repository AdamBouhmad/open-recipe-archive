## Ingredients

- Pig's head
- Pig's feet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Juniper berries
- Laurel leaf
- Cloves

## Directions

1. The head is placed in cold water.
2. After soaking overnight, it is washed well with a large cloth and placed over heat in cold water that stands over it.
3. Allow nearly 20 minutes per pound and it must not cook too quickly; it is skimmed regularly.
4. The skin should not be removed before the head is cold; then it comes off easily.
5. If the head is sent whole to the table, a piece of sturdy paper can be placed around the shank to hold it when carving.
6. It should be sliced very thinly.
7. The broth can be used for various purposes and no salt is used in it.
