## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cream or milk
- Butter
- Salt
- Fine-chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Beat 8 eggs well, first alone, then with 8 small spoonfuls of cream or good milk; thereafter put it in the saucepan over the stove with an ounce of butter and a little salt. It tastes good to add a little finely-chopped parsley; there must not be any liquid and one does not stir, but scrapes continuously from the bottom with a scraper; if one stirs around, the pieces will break apart and that is not the intention. When you find them sufficiently cooked, take them off the fire. They must not be too hard. You can add some boiled asparagus tips to them. Count 3 eggs per person.
