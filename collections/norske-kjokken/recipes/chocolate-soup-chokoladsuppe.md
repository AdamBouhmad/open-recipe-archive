## Ingredients

- 1 pound chocolate
- 2 quarts milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- a few drops vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Grate and cook 1 pound chocolate in 2 quarts milk, stirring regularly until it boils.
2. After some time of boiling, pour it into a tureen where 3 egg yolks have been whisked with plenty of white sugar and a few drops of good vanilla extract.
3. Whisk until the soup is completely poured in.
4. If desired, the egg whites can be whisked, cooked, and laid on top in the tureen.
5. Serve with soft bread or small rusks, calculating 1 pint of milk per person.
