## Ingredients

- dough prepared as for pie dough but with milk instead of water
- a good peeled apple with the core removed
- for each apple: salt and sugar or sugar with lemon juice mixed together

## Directions

1. The dough is rolled out in a thin sheet
2. It is cut into squares, and on each a good peeled apple is placed with the core removed
3. The hollow where the core was is filled with either salted or sugar mixed with lemon juice, with a lump of sugar placed in the sugar
4. The dough is bent together around the apple, tied in a cloth with enough room left for the pudding to rise, and cooked for one hour
5. The water must be boiling and not off the boil
6. The cloth is opened in warm water and melted inside before using
