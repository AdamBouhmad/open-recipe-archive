## Ingredients

- 3 pounds calf's breast
- Cold water
- White pepper
- Salt
- Vegetables (carrots, turnips)
- Onion
- Cloves
- Butter
- Flour
- Nutmeg
- Egg white
- Egg yolk
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the calf's breast into small pieces and place in sufficient cold water to cover them; add half a teaspoon of white pepper, a teaspoon of salt, a good bunch of vegetables, half a scraped carrot, a peeled turnip and an onion in which 3 cloves are stuck.
2. Bring to a gentle boil and skim carefully as long as scum rises, and cook until the meat is tender; then strain it, return the soup to the fire and the meat is washed in cold water.
3. In the meantime make a white sauce by melting a little butter and stirring flour into it and gradually adding 1½ pints of the soup; add a little more pepper and salt if necessary, and half a teaspoon of grated nutmeg.
4. When the sauce has boiled up, thicken it with an egg white and two small egg yolks stirred in, add the meat and cook a couple of minutes while stirring occasionally.
5. It can be served with a tablespoon of minced parsley scattered over it after it has been placed on a warm dish.
