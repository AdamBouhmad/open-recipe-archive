## Ingredients

- Unripe crowberries
- Water
- Butter
- Cinnamon
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Take unripe berries, but do not skin them first.
2. When stems and green nobs are removed, place them on the fire with very little water and a piece of unsalted butter the size of a walnut per quart of berries.
3. They should only be boiled tender, not mashed.
4. A few sticks of whole cinnamon are boiled with the berries, and sugar is added when the compote is boiled, but it should not be very sweet.
