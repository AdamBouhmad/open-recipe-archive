## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Anchovies
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Lemon juice
- Cognac
- Beef or meat bouillon
- Beef broth
- Red wine
- Potatoes
- French bread

## Directions

1. Brown 2 ounces butter, add 1 beaten egg, then 2 anchovies scraped from bones and chopped, a little salt, pepper or parsley, juice of half a lemon, 1 teaspoon Cognac and 1 tablespoon flour
2. Add beef broth, 1 pint bouillon, strong meat broth and about 1 pint red wine (optional)
3. Simmer the whole dish for a good quarter hour
4. When serving, place browned potatoes on top or arrange slices of roasted French bread on the plate and cover with the ragout
