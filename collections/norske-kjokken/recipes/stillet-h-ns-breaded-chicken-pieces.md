## Ingredients

- Meat or chicken, preferably from the breast
- Beaten eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour for dusting
- Semolina

## Directions

1. The meat or chicken is cut into quite thin slices and dipped in beaten eggs, then in bread crumbs mixed with salt, pepper and parsley, and fried quite hot in butter.
2. Served with semolina.
