## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Broth or milk
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes raw, cut into pieces and place on heat with enough broth or milk to cover the potatoes.
2. Cook until completely softened, drain and press through a sieve.
3. Cook the mixture again with butter, salt, and if too thick, with more broth or milk.
4. Cooked potatoes can be riced and need not be pressed through a sieve.
5. Water may be used instead of broth, but then more butter must be used.
