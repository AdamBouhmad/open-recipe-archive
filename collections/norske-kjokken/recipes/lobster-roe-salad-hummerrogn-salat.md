## Ingredients

- lobster green
- lobster roe
- stock or fine salad oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- watercress
- salad leaves

## Directions

1. Rub the lobster green in a little stock or fine salad oil; add the juice that flows from the lobster when opened, which must be carefully collected, and also a little vinegar and pepper
2. Cut the roe itself into small pieces and place between small tender leaves of salad lettuce in the sauce
3. Garnish with watercress, some of the finest salad leaves, and the red roe inside, cut in figures and placed among the greens
