## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 0.5 pound white sugar
- 6 tablespoons sifted flour
- 0.5 pound grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoon baking powder dissolved in a little rosewater

## Directions

1. Beat eggs for a quarter hour with white sugar, then with sifted flour, grated chocolate and baking powder dissolved in a little rosewater. Grease the pan with tallow and must be cold again before the batter is put on in small spots; not too thick, as they will run out. Bake light brown.
