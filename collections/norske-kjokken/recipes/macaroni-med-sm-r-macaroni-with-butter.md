## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Salted water
- Butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in salted water and drain the water from them.
2. Butter a deep dish with butter, and layer the macaroni with a layer of pepper and butter.
3. The top layer should be macaroni and then small pieces of butter are laid on it.
4. Bake until it is brown.
