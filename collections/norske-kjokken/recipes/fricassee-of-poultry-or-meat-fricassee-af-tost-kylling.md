## Ingredients

- Meat
- Parsley
- Onion
- Broth
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Port wine or sherry
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into fine slices and simmer gently for a quarter hour with a handful of chopped parsley, a peeled onion cut into pieces, 1 pint broth, a bit of butter, salt and pepper
2. Quickly stir together 2 egg yolks, a glass of Port wine or Sherry, and 1 tablespoon vinegar
3. Pour this mixture in slowly to thicken the sauce
