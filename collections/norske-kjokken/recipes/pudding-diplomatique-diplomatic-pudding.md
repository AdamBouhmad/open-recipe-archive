## Ingredients

- 1 pound raisins, cleaned and pits removed
- 1 lemon, skin sliced
- 1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced
- an ordinary sponge cake, previously made
- 9 eggs beaten with a pinch of salt, 1 pound sugar, and 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. The sponge cake is cut into slices and layered with the fruit in an ordinary buttered mold
2. When everything is used, a custard is made by whisking 9 eggs with a pinch of salt, 1 pound sugar, and 1 tablespoon vanilla
3. This is poured over the pudding; a tablespoon of butter is distributed in small pieces on top
4. The roasting pan is filled with enough boiling water so that it comes two-thirds up the mold, which is then placed in it and cooked gently for one hour
5. Served usually with wine sauce
