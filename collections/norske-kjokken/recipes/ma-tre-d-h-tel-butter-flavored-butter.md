## Ingredients

- One ounce butter
- One tablespoon minced parsley
- One tablespoon lemon juice
- One small teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Blend together cold and spread thus over what you wish to use it for.
2. It is excellent for any kind of roasted fish.
