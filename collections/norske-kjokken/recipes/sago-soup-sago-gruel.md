## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds large sago grains
- 1 half lemon, zest
- 1 half cup raisins
- 3 eggs
- white sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 glass white wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains in cold water and strain in boiling water.
2. When it has cooked until the grains are swollen from the water, add the zest of half a lemon together with half a cup of raisins.
3. Stir 3 eggs into the tureen with white sugar, then with the juice of a lemon and 1 glass white wine.
4. When the grains are clear, they are cooked, and the soup is poured boiling over the eggs while stirring constantly in the tureen.
5. This is a wholesome and tasty diet and the soup is cooked in half an hour. The wine can be omitted.
