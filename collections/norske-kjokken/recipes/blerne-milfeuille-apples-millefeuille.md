## Ingredients

- Fine apples
- sugar
- water
- a piece of savory pie without juice or a piece of raspberry or cherry marmalade
- the remaining pieces of bread
- a custard made from a quart of milk
- 8 egg yolks
- a little vanilla
- a tablespoon cornstarch
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Peel fine apples, cut off a piece, hollow them out, and give them a filling of sugar and a little water, but so they do not fall apart.
2. Place them beside each other in a fairly deep dish filled with the savory pie without juice or with a piece of raspberry or cherry marmalade, and cover with the remaining pieces of bread.
3. Make a custard of a quart of milk, 8 egg yolks, a little vanilla, a tablespoon cornstarch, and sugar to taste.
4. When the custard is cold, pour it over the apples, place the dish in the oven, and bake the cake slowly.
5. When the custard begins to stiffen and has become light brown, it is done.
