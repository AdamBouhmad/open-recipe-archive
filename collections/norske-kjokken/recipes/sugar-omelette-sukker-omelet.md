## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- egg whites
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites separately; instead of salt and pepper, add 1 tablespoon powdered sugar to the yolks
2. Cook in the same manner as Simple Omelette
3. While the omelette cooks, heat the serving dish until red-hot
4. When the omelette is slid onto the plate, sprinkle powdered sugar over it and score the top with a hot iron, taking care to brown the sugar rather than burn it
5. Serve hot
