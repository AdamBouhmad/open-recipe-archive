## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 1 pint milk
- 1 good quart flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter and an equal amount fat, melted together

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs very well, yolks and whites separately
2. Then add the milk with a little salt, then the butter and finally the flour, which is stirred lightly in
3. Bake immediately in well-buttered rings that are half-filled with batter
4. The oven must be very hot and the baker must watch so that the edges brown well
