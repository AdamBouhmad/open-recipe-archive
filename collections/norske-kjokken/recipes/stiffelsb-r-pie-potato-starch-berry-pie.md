## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- 6 egg yolks
- a little finely grated vanilla
- powdered sugar to taste
- some old stale bread
- 2 small cupfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- sifted berries

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes, cut into small pieces, and fry in butter until they become yellow, or give them a coating in butter and fry on a griddle until they are dry.
2. Beat 6 egg yolks well with a little finely grated vanilla and powdered sugar according to taste.
3. Soak some old stale bread; use 2 small cupfuls of the crumbs, which are boiled in 3 tablespoons butter, and when it has cooled slightly, stir it into the eggs together with the potato starch and the sifted berries.
4. Bake it in a tart tin.
