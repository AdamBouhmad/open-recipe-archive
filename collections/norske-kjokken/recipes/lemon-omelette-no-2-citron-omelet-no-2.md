## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- egg whites
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- juice of 1 large or 2 small lemons
- zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat 5 egg yolks white with 1 cup powdered sugar
2. Then beat with juice of 1 large or 2 small lemons and zest of 1 lemon
3. Then add the beaten egg whites, which must be stirred carefully around to blend well with the batter
4. Bake for 1.5 hours
