## Ingredients

- 1 quart warm water
- Graham flour as needed
- 2 cents compressed yeast

## Directions

1. To 1 quart warm water, take as much Graham flour as is necessary to make a light dough
2. Add 2 cents compressed yeast
3. Let it rise in a warm place
