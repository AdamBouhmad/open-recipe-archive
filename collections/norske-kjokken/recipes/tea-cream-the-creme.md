## Ingredients

- Strong extract of tea
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 quart cold cream
- 5 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Make and serve it like the previous one, only using a strong extract of tea instead of coffee
2. It can be made without eggs, then taking for each portion less tea extract
