## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb butter
- 1/2 lb flour
- 1 pint milk
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Bake 1/2 lb butter and 1/2 lb flour until the spoon slides through it
2. While still over the fire, stir in 1 pint milk
3. When cooled, add 6 egg yolks and finally the beaten whites
4. Grease a frying pan with cold butter for the first layer
5. Divide the batter into 8 parts and bake as 3 thick pancakes
6. Between each layer butter compote is spread, and on the top powdered sugar is sprinkled
