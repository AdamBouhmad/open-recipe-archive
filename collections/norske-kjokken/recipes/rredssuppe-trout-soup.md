## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef
- 1 onion
- 3 carrots
- 3 quarts water
- turnip
- meatballs
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound beef very finely and cook it slowly with 1 onion and 3 carrots in 3 quarts water until the meat can be pulled apart, which takes almost 3 hours
2. For the soup not to boil away, it is necessary that it cooks gently in a pot with a tightly fitting lid
3. A pair of turnips and meatballs are added and removed when they are tender
4. Strain the soup, set it back on the fire, skim it, and when it boils, add meatballs made from ½ pound beef
5. The soup can be seasoned with 1 egg and served with toasted bread
6. This portion is for 3 people
