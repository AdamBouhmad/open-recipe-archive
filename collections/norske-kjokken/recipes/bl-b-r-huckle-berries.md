## Ingredients

- 1 pound ripe blueberries
- 1 pound powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place over fire with powdered sugar and cook for approximately one quarter hour; then strain the berries through a sieve.
2. Cook the juice for a quarter hour with cinnamon, skim and pour hot over the berries, which are covered the next day with paper dipped in vinegar and tied.
