## Ingredients

- 4 pounds Round Beef
- 6 eggs
- 5 or 6 soda crackers
- Butter
- Minced beef suet
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scrape and mince the beef finely, crumble the soda crackers finely, add a little butter, a little minced beef suet, pepper and salt.
2. Mix all of this together and form two small loaves, which are rolled in crackers and baked for one hour.
3. When they are cold, slice them.
