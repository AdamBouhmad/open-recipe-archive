## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and dry the squabs well with a clean cloth
2. They are best when fried in a pan, for which you brown sufficient butter
3. Fry them under a lid
4. When necessary, add a little water, but this must always be boiling
