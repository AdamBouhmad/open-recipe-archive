## Ingredients

- 1 pound cherries
- 1 quart water
- 1 pint water
- whole cinnamon
- cloves
- sugar
- bread rolls or baked bread

## Directions

1. Stone and cook 1 pound cherries with 1 quart water, drain, cook again with 1 pint water and strain into the first drained soup.
2. To clarify the soup, you can place a clean cloth in the sieve and pour the soup through it, after which it is returned to heat with whole cinnamon, cloves and sugar to achieve a good flavor.
3. Serve with bread rolls, which are set on as the soup boils, or with baked bread.
4. You may remove a few berries and cook them in the strained soup.
5. If you want the soup clear, a heaping spoonful of cornstarch is stirred in water or some of the soup while it is cold, mixed in when warm and stirred as the soup boils. The mixture need only boil a couple of minutes.
