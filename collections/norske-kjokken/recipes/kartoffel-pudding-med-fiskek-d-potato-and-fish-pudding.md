## Ingredients

- 2 pounds potatoes
- 2 pounds fish
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, peel and rub with a light hand so there are no lumps
2. Bake in ½ pound butter
3. When baked, stir in 15 eggs and plenty of milk, pepper and salt
4. Cook for 3 hours and serve with strong brown sauce
