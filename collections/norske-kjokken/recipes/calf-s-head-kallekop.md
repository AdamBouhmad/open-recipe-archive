## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salt
- water
- egg
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Scald, scrape, and clean the head, remove the brain, wash it well and soak it in cold water for a time so the blood can be drawn out
2. Place it in boiling salted water and cook until the bones can be easily removed, then remove with great care
3. Cut off the ears and eyes, remove the palate, cut the tongue and cook it together with the head if desired
4. Cook the head in butter or clarified butter after dipping it in beaten egg and rolling it in bread crumbs mixed with salt and pepper
5. Fry it light brown and serve with brown butter and salad or other garnish along with potatoes
