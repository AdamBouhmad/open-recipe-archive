## Ingredients

- 2 pounds calf meat
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small diced onion
- 2 large tablespoons wheat flour
- curry powder to taste
- salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. The meat is chopped into small pieces and blanched in several changes of water
2. 2 tablespoons butter is placed over the fire in a pot; when melted, not browned, a small diced onion is added and cooked for a couple of minutes
3. Then 2 large tablespoons of wheat flour is stirred in, along with curry powder to taste and salt (use curry powder sparingly, about half a teaspoon, as more can be added later)
4. It is cooked together and gradually thinned with boiling water until it becomes a thick sauce
5. The meat is added in, and it should gradually be thinned with a bit more water, which should always be boiling
6. The sauce should be fairly thick but it cooks down
7. Cook covered if possible, but skim properly
8. It is done when the meat is tender; from 2 pounds of meat it can cook for about an hour
9. If the sauce becomes lumpy, remove the meat first and strain the sauce over it
