## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Nutmeg
- Egg yolks
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Press cooked potatoes through a coarse sieve.
2. Mix with a piece of butter, salt, a very little white pepper, a little grated nutmeg and two egg yolks.
3. Spread the mixture out on a board and cut with a knife into small cakes two inches long and one inch wide.
4. Place on a buttered board, brush with egg white and bake in the oven until golden brown.
5. The oven should not be very hot.
