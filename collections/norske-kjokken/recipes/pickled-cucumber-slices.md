## Ingredients

- 2 dozen large cucumbers
- vinegar to cover
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 tablespoonful mustard seeds
- 1 ounce ginger
- 1 ounce black pepper
- 1 ounce celery seed
- 1 teaspoonful mace blossoms
- 1 ounce allspice
- 1 ounce cloves
- 1 tablespoonful turmeric
- 1 ounce grated horseradish
- 1 ounce garlic
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Slice 2 dozen large cucumbers and soak them in vinegar for 1 hour, covering them completely
2. Place them in the prepared vinegar
3. For the cold vinegar take: 1 pound sugar, 1 tablespoonful mustard seeds, 1 ounce ginger, 1 ounce black pepper, 1 ounce celery seed, 1 teaspoonful mace blossoms, 1 ounce allspice, 1 ounce cloves, 1 tablespoonful turmeric, 1 ounce grated horseradish, 1 ounce garlic, cayenne pepper
4. Add the cucumbers and simmer for 2 hours
5. It can be used as soon as it is cold
