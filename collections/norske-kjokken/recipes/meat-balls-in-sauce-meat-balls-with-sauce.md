## Ingredients

- Beef or fish stock
- water
- ground meat
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- flour
- stock
- wine
- lemon juice
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Stock and water, equal amounts of each kind, are put on the fire, in which meat or fish balls are cooked; when they are cooked, they are taken up and the stock is strained.
2. A large spoonful of butter is put on the fire, in which a little chopped onion, a little ground pepper, nutmeg, salt and a large spoonful of flour are stirred, which is mixed with the stock until it has a suitable thickness, then the balls are added and cooked a little.
3. In the same way the balls can be prepared with oysters, mussels, mushrooms, capers, celery, or whatever you want to use.
4. The onions can be omitted and you can add a little wine and lemon juice, and enrich the sauce with a couple of egg yolks.
5. If you don't have stock, you can cook the balls in water.
