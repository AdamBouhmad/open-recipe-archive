## Ingredients

- Thin beef slices
- Fat meat strips
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Butter
- Flour
- Onion
- Anchovy
- Bay leaf
- Whole ginger
- Peppercorns
- Water or broth
- Oysters or shrimp

## Directions

1. Make small incisions in several places on the piece of meat.
2. Take small fat meat strips, dip them in pepper and ginger, and insert them as deeply as possible into the incisions.
3. Close the slits with a small wooden pin or with the fat meat strips, and the meat is larded on the outside.
4. Dust the meat with flour, but not more than can be well rubbed in.
5. Brown it on all sides in butter, using no more butter than is necessary, otherwise the meat will brown unevenly later.
6. When all sides are browned, remove the meat.
7. Brown a good spoonful of flour in the butter remaining in the pan.
8. Add 1½ cups warm water gradually.
9. Return the meat to the pan and add to the sauce some small pearl onions, a couple of anchovies, a small handful of bay leaves, some pieces of whole ginger, and whole peppercorns.
10. Cook everything together under a lid until the meat is tender.
11. Strain the sauce and dilute it if too thick, then add oysters or shrimp if desired, though these are optional.
