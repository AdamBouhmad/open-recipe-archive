## Ingredients

- Fresh coconut (grated)
- 1.5 cups sour milk
- 3 egg yolks
- A little salt and sugar
- 1 tablespoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Grate fresh coconut; for 1 cup grated coconut use 1.5 cups sour milk, 3 egg yolks, a little salt and sugar, and 1 tablespoon melted butter.
2. The whole is whipped well for 5 to 6 minutes; the pan is lined as usual; it is filled and baked.
3. On top are placed the egg whites, whipped with sugar.
