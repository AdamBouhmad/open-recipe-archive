## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- Juice of 4 lemons
- 1 pint wine and white, whipped cream according to taste

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks well; add 1 tablespoon corn starch, juice of 4 lemons, 1 pint wine and white, whipped cream according to taste
2. Place this on heat under constant stirring until it begins to boil and becomes stiff
3. Then stir in 4 whites that are beaten to a foam, whereupon it is removed from heat and stirred constantly until it becomes cold
