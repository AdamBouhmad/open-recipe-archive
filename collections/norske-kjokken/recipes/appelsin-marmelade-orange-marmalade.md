## Ingredients

- Oranges (pound for pound with sugar)
- Sugar (pound for pound with oranges)
- Water
- Butter (optional, small pieces)

## Directions

1. Pound for pound, half of the oranges are peeled and the peels are cut in strips, and cooked in three changes of warm water until they are tender; after which they are set aside.
2. On the other oranges the peels are rubbed off; the inside thick white skin is removed and discarded; all oranges are cut into 4 pieces and the seeds removed; next the fruit is cut into very small pieces, and all the juice that comes from them, without pressing them, is poured over the sugar; this is kept until the sugar is melted, and if the fruit is not very juicy [continue cooking process as indicated by incomplete text].
