## Ingredients

- Calf's meat or ham and pork meat
- Butter
- Vegetables or potatoes

## Directions

1. Calf's meat is bought as ribs; the butcher cuts it into chops with a bone in each; from ham meat one must cut two bones to each piece for it to be thick enough, but only one remains adhering.
2. They are eaten with vegetables or potatoes and no other sauce than that which remains on the platter, for which reason some butter is placed there.
