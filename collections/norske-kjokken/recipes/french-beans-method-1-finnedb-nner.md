## Ingredients

- beans
- 1 ounce butter
- 1 tablespoon minced parsley
- 1 teaspoon minced onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put the beans in boiling water and cook without a lid until they are tender. Drain the water from them and place them back over the fire with 1 ounce of butter, 1 tablespoon of minced parsley, 1 teaspoon of minced onion and a little salt and pepper. Serve warm.
