## Ingredients

- 1 pound fish, cooked
- chopped parsley
- 3 tablespoons cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- a little finely chopped mace flowers
- 3 whole eggs
- a pinch of grated lemon peel
- a few tablespoons chopped anchovies

## Directions

1. The fish and parsley are mixed well with 3 whole eggs, a pinch of grated lemon peel, and a few tablespoons of chopped anchovies
2. The mixture is placed in a buttered mold and set directly in the oven
3. Served with stirred or melted butter
4. For 5 persons
