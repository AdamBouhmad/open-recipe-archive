## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 pound soft sugar
- 6 tablespoons sifted flour
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 1 tablespoon baking powder dissolved in a little rose water

## Directions

1. Beat the 4 eggs in a mixer for a quarter hour with 1/2 pound soft sugar
2. Then add 6 tablespoons sifted flour, 1/4 pound grated chocolate and 1 tablespoon baking powder dissolved in a little rose water
3. Butter the bottom of a baking pan and it must be cold again before the dough is placed on it in small strips; not too densely, as they will run out
4. In about a half hour they are usually done and are removed when they loosen from the pan
