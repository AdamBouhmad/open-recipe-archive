## Ingredients

- 1 pint sour milk
- 12 tablespoons flour
- 4 tablespoons soft rubber sugar

## Directions

1. 1 pint sour milk is whipped well and stirred with 12 tablespoons flour and 4 tablespoons soft rubber sugar
2. They are placed on the pan with a spoon in small strips and baked light brown
3. If the heat is good, full butter on the pan is needed for the first cakes
