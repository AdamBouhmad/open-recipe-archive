## Ingredients

- Round Beef
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. The meat should be scraped from the sinews, ensuring that everything is included and only the clear sinews remain, which are set aside.
2. The meat is then formed with the blade of a knife into round, flat patties.
3. Salt and pepper are sprinkled on and they are fried in abundant browned butter; it tastes good to fry some chopped onion at the same time with the meat.
4. Serve with browned butter and potatoes.
