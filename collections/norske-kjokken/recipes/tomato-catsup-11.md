## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- salt
- mustard powder
- black pepper
- allspice
- cloves
- cayenne pepper
- white wine or cider vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Choose good ripe tomatoes
2. Pour them in boiling water and rub through a coarse sieve to free them from skin and seeds
3. When cooled, add 4 tablespoonfuls salt, 3 ounces ground mustard, 2 ounces black pepper, 1 ounce allspice, 1 ounce cloves, 1 ounce cayenne pepper and 1 pint white wine or cider vinegar to every gallon
4. Boil slowly for 4 hours, pour into bottles and seal well
5. Alternatively, for 1 peck of tomatoes take 6 pounds sugar (can be soft brown sugar) and 1 gallon good cider vinegar
6. Add cloves, cinnamon and pepper to taste
7. When the vinegar with sugar boils, add the spices and tomatoes gradually, letting them boil until a wooden spoon through them leaves no trace
8. They keep well in stone jars with good paper over them
9. Or pit and slice 1 pound ripe tomatoes; add 1.5 pounds sugar, 1 ounce cinnamon and mace blossoms mixed together, 1 ounce cloves and 1 quart vinegar
10. Combine all together and boil slowly for 1 hour
