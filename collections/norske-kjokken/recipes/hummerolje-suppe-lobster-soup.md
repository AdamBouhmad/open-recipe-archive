## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 small lobsters
- 1 quart milk
- 1 pint water
- Butter
- 1 tablespoon lard
- Salt
- Red pepper

## Directions

1. Use 1 large lobster or 2 small ones; take all the meat from the shells and chop it finely; warm 1 quart milk together with 1 pint water; then add the lobster mixed with butter and 1 tablespoon lard mixed with flour, and salt and red pepper according to taste
2. Cook for 10 minutes
