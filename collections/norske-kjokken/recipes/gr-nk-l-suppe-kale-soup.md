## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 quart good milk
- 1 teaspoon of fresh crackers
- White pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Blend 1 quart boiling water in the broth with 1 quart good milk; stir in 1 teaspoon of fresh crackers mixed with a little pepper and salt according to taste
2. When it boils add 1 quart good, fresh kale; you must stir so it does not burn; finally add a piece of good butter the size of an egg, and as soon as it boils up, the soup is ready and taken from the stove
