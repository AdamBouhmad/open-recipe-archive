## Ingredients

- Beef or steak
- Flour
- Eggs
- Butter
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Bread

## Directions

1. Cut the beef or steak into thin, fine slices
2. Dip in a batter made of 1 pound flour, 3 beaten eggs, 2 ounces melted butter, 1 pint milk, a little sugar and salt
3. Roast on the stove in clarified butter
4. Serve with apple compote, starch compote, rhubarb compote or other vegetables
