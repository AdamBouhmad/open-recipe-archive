## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- 1 pound soft sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 1 ounce ground cinnamon
- one spoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat 6 whole eggs, one at a time, with 1 pound soft sugar until they are light
2. Then stir 1 pound flour, 1 ounce ground cinnamon and one spoon baking powder into it
3. They are placed with a spoon in small strips on the cold pan, which is first buttered with butter or lard; and baked at good heat
4. These cakes need strong heat to raise quickly, but must absolutely not become brown, and if the oven is therefore too slow and gives disproportionately strong top heat, then one does better not to bake them; the oven door must not be opened until the baking is done and with baking generally takes a little half hour
