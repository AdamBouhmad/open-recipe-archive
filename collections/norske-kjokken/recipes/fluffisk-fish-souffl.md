## Ingredients

- Butter
- Flour
- Fish
- Nutmeg or ginger
- Sugar
- Salt
- Egg yolk
- French bread
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a roux by melting a good piece of butter and stirring flour into it little by little until it has a suitable consistency
2. Add the fish and grated nutmeg or ginger
3. Cook it a little, then add a small piece of sugar and salt
4. Bind the fish with an egg yolk, which gives it a good flavor
5. Layer slices of French bread that have first been baked in butter on a pan, and pour the fish over
6. Alternatively, place the warm fluffisk in a deep pan, sprinkle a layer of soft bread made from a finger-spoonful over it, pour melted butter over it and set it in the oven where it should bake light brown
7. If the bread is to sink down into the fish, this should be fairly thick and is therefore better bound with two egg yolks
8. Alternatively, one can use boiled fish or cooked red fish, though the latter should be chopped fine and requires much butter
9. If one does not wish to use bread for the salted fish, small-cut cooked carrots and ginger can be added
