## Ingredients

- 1 short flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 scant cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda dissolved in warm water
- half teaspoon salt
- salt for flour
- 2 tablespoons butter in small bits
- fresh strawberries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix salt into flour, then work in butter in small bits like for pie, and blend with flour. Beat egg and soda into the milk. Mix everything together, handling as little as possible. Roll out in two layers of pie form but one inch thick. Lay one on top of the other and bake in a hot oven. While still warm but not hot, separate the layers. They will easily separate at the spot where they were joined. Spread a thick layer of fresh strawberries on the lower layer, dust with powdered sugar, and place the other layer on top. When serving, dust the top layer lightly with sugar.
