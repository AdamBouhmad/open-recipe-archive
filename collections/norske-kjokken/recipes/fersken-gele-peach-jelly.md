## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Peach kernels
- Lemon juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the fruit, cut it into pieces and remove the stones. Cut one-third of the kernels into pieces and put them in the pot with the fruit. Now set it in boiling water, according to the instructions, stirring from time to time, until the fruit is well broken into pieces. Then strain it, and for each pint of peach juice take the juice of 1 lemon. Then measure it again and give 1 pound of sugar for each pint of juice. Heat the sugar as usual, and proceed according to the instructions. This jelly gives an exquisite flavor in cakes.
