## Ingredients

- Brosme fish
- Flour
- Salt
- Parsley
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean as usual; it must be quite dry before it is rolled in flour and fried in butter, clarified or lard.
2. The lard must be boiling hot, so it fries quickly to a nice light brown color, and it must be taken up the moment it is done.
