## Ingredients

- 6 lb. lean beef
- 6 lb. round steak
- 1 lb. salt
- a small amount (in ounce) of pepper
- a small amount (in ounce) of allspice
- a small amount (in ounce) of ginger
- 3 to 4 lb. fatty beef
- pig or pork intestines

## Directions

1. Cut and finely chop the meat, or grind it using a meat grinder
2. The meat should be fine enough to form a stiff mass
3. Work the meat alone for half an hour, then work it with 1 lb. salt, a small amount of pepper, a small amount of allspice, a small amount of ginger, the ginger fairly coarse, and mix in 3 to 4 lb. fatty beef cut into small cubes
4. The mixture should be fairly hard and must be worked until it forms bubbles
5. Prepare the intestines by emptying them, cutting them to suitable lengths, and placing them in saltwater for two days
6. Turn them over and place in saltwater again for one day
7. Scrape them, rinse in mild soda water, dry them, tie one end together and blow air in from the other
8. Fill using a sausage funnel, squeezing the intestine and only filling a small piece at a time
9. Once the sausages are well stuffed, rub them with salt and let them rest for 24 hours
10. Then wipe the mixture away
