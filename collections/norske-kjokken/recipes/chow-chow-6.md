## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cabbage
- 2 pounds cauliflower
- 1 pound cucumbers
- 6 celery knobs
- 6 ounces red peppers
- 1 quart small onions
- 2 quarts green tomatoes
- 2 gallons vinegar
- 1 pound mustard
- 4 pounds mustard seeds
- 1 jar French mustard
- 1 ounce cloves
- 2 ounces turmeric

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage, cauliflower, cucumbers, celery, red peppers, onions and green tomatoes into small pieces
2. Boil each type of vegetable separately until tender, then drain
3. Put 2 gallons vinegar and the spices in a pot and bring to boil
4. Mix the vegetables and pour the hot vinegar mixture over them
