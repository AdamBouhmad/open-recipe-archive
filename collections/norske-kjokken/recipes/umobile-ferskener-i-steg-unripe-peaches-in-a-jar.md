## Ingredients

- 1 pound unripe peaches, peeled
- 1 pound white sugar
- 1 gill vinegar
- A knob of white pint water

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, divide into 4 parts and remove the stones.
2. Boil the sugar with the vinegar and a knob of white pint water.
3. When this is skimmed, add the peaches and cook for approximately 5 minutes (longer if very unripe).
4. Remove and place in a jar, boil the syrup a little longer, approximately 5 minutes, pour warm over and cover with a cloth.
5. After a few days, boil the syrup again for approximately 10 minutes, skim well, pour warm over and cover again with a cloth.
6. Finish as usual.
