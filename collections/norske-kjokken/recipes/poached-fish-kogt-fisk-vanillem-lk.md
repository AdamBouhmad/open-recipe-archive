## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- water
- cold butter
- pepper
- lemon slices
- fine bread

## Directions

1. Let the fish stand for a few hours in lukewarm water, changing the water several times.
2. Then place it loosely wrapped in cloth in cold water, set it over the fire and let it just come to a boil, then pour off all the water at once and pour boiling water over the fish.
3. Bring this just to a boil, then remove it, dry it and place it on a dish.
4. Add cold butter and pepper to it and place it in the oven for a couple of minutes.
5. Serve with lemon slices and fine bread.
