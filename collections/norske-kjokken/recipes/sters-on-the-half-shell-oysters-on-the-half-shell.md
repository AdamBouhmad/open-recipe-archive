## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Water
- Milk
- Cream
- Butter
- Salt
- White pepper
- Corn starch

## Directions

1. Best prepared in a special oyster boiler, but in its absence, place a smaller pan inside a larger one with boiling water
2. In the smaller pan put one cup warm water, one cup milk, one cup cream
3. When it boils, stir in two spoonfuls of butter, as well as a little salt and white pepper
4. Remove from heat and bind with two heaping spoonfuls of corn starch stirred in a little cold milk
5. Meanwhile, the oyster shells should be washed and buttered with butter and an intact oyster laid on each
6. Place these close together next to the edge of the frying pan; stir the cream very well, and fill each shell up, being careful not to spill anything in the pan
7. Bake 5 to 9 minutes, after the shell has become warm
8. Arrange on the shells
9. One can use oyster stock instead of water and milk instead of cream
