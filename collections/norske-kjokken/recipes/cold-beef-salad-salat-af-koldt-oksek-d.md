## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- shallot
- gherkins or anchovies

## Directions

1. Chop the beef, but not too fine and do not include any brown parts
2. Chop shallot and mix in together with finely chopped gherkins or anchovies
3. Prepare with vinegar and pepper
