## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- equal amount butter
- equal amount sugar
- equal amount flour
- vanilla or almond extract

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, and take equal amounts of butter, sugar and flour. Mix well together, and a little vanilla or almond extract can be added. Place on a greased sheet with a teaspoon. They should be light brown at the edges.
