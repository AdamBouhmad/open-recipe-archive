## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons mustard
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Grind mustard and hard-boiled egg yolks together in oil; add vinegar, pepper and a pinch of salt.
2. Prepare the salad as before.
