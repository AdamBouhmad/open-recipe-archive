## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour baked in 1/2 pound clarified butter
- 1 pint boiling water
- 10 eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. The dough is made of 1/2 pound flour which is baked well in 1/2 pound clarified butter; then 1 pint boiling water is added and when it is baked with it, it is cooled a little, stirred for half an hour with 10 eggs, one at a time and placed half in small forms, but in lack thereof with a spoon in small strips on the pan
2. From this become 25 cakes
3. They can be filled with vanilla cream or whipped cream
