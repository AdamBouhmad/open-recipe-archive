## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Butter
- Egg
- Bread crumbs or crackers
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. For this use the largest and best oysters; lay them on a clean cloth and press another cloth over them to absorb all moisture
2. In the frying pan, melt butter enough to almost cover the oysters
3. First dip them in beaten egg and then roll in soft bread or crackers
4. Fry them light brown
5. Serve dry on a hot pan
6. Lemon slices can be arranged around them
