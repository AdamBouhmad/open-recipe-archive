## Ingredients

- 2 parts cold roast or cooked salted beef (corned beef)
- 1 part mashed potatoes
- Pepper
- Salt
- Flour
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Mince the beef finely, take mashed potatoes, add a little pepper, salt, flour and melted butter.
2. Put this mixture in a roasting pan and stir it until it is heated through, but not browned.
3. The mixture can also be formed into flat cakes, dipped in beaten egg and fried in browned butter or lard.
