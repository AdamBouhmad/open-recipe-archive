## Ingredients

- Chicken's ribcage
- 1 small onion
- 1/2 carrot
- 1/2 parsnip
- 3 sprigs parsley
- 1 sprig thyme
- 3 bay leaves
- Salt
- 1/2 pint hot water
- Butter
- 1 tablespoon potato starch
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- Cloves
- 1 piece sugar

## Directions

1. Have the butcher prepare the ribcage by removing the bones and holding it flat with the help of a silver string tied around it
2. Place the roast on a roasting pan on a bed of vegetables: 1 small onion, 1/2 carrot, 1/2 parsnip, 3 sprigs parsley, 1 sprig thyme, and 3 bay leaves
3. Do not add water to the roasting pan
4. Do not salt until the surface has shrunk
5. When making the sauce, remove the vegetables and add 1/2 pint hot water to the roasting pan, boiling it quickly together with the drippings for a couple of minutes until the sauce is thick enough
6. Thicken the sauce with 1 tablespoon potato starch dissolved in 2 tablespoons cold water before stirring it into the sauce
7. Add cloves and a piece of sugar to the sauce
8. The roast becomes more tender and flavorful when basted
9. If desired, you can stud it with half the amount
10. Brown some butter in a pan and blend with flour to make a roux, then pour over the roast after it has been browned in butter and baste it often
