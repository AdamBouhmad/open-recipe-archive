## Ingredients

- 2 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup fine wheat flour
- 1 quart milk
- 2 cents worth of yeast
- 1 tablespoon butter or fat
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Let it rise overnight
2. Bake in muffin tins for two minutes in a hot oven
3. Serve warm
