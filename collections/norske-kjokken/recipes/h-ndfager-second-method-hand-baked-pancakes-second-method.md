## Ingredients

- Bread dough
- Milk
- Melted butter
- Egg
- Sugar
- Caraway seed
- Grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. A handful of bread dough is stirred, then stir in enough sour milk so the dough becomes a little thicker than it should be; add a little spoon of melted butter, 1 egg, a little handful of sugar and very little caraway seed and grated lemon peel
