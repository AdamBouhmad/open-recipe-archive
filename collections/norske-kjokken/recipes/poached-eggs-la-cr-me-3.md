## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Cream or milk
- Cornstarch
- Butter
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fill a clean saucepan nearly with boiling water, add a spoonful of vinegar, which must be quite sharp, and a small pinch of salt. Remove the water from the fire and add the broken eggs; when they have cooked three and a half minutes, remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and place on buttered toast on a warm dish. Meanwhile in another bowl half a cup of cream or milk is prepared; if using the latter, it must be thickened with a small amount of cornstarch. Bring it just to boiling point, stirring constantly, so it does not scorch, then add a spoonful of good butter, and a little pepper and salt. It boils up once and is poured over the eggs. It tastes excellent to use half cream and half bouillon.
