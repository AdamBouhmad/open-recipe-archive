## Ingredients

- Good, tender apples
- Sugar
- Wine

## Directions

1. Peel and divide into 2 or 4 pieces.
2. They get a boiling in sour wine and sugar.
3. Then cook the white sugar clear and thick and pour lukewarm over the apples; the wine is not used, but can be used for soup.
4. They should not be placed in a narrow crock the first time, but the sauce should be poured over them in a wide dish where they do not lie much on top of each other.
5. Two days later they can be placed in the crock.
6. The sauce gets a boiling and is poured cold over.
7. They cannot be kept as long as other preserved sweet dishes.
