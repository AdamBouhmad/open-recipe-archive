## Ingredients

- 4 pounds fish
- 4 quarts water
- Salt
- 1 large onion
- Carrots
- Parsley
- Butter
- 2 tablespoons lard
- Vinegar (if no stove available)

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and cook in 4 quarts water with salt, a large onion and carrots
2. When these are tender, remove them, drain and return to the soup when it is strained
3. Cook the fish completely, press it through a sieve and strain it
4. Pour a little hot water on it in the sieve to draw out the stock
5. Chop a small handful of carrots and parsley finely and mix with two tablespoons lard, stir in a scant half pint of broth, reduce somewhat with the soup which is again placed on the stove, add when it is hot, and stir until it boils; then place bread or fish roe on top
6. If you do not have a stove, you can stir a few tablespoons of vinegar into the lukewarm preparation
