## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Carrot
- Onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. When the chicken is cleaned, cut it up; remove the wings and legs first; separate the breast and back, and cut these into suitable pieces.
2. Place them over boiling water in boiling broth, which should cover them. Skim as often as foam rises, then add a tablespoon of salt, a handful of peppercorns, a carrot, an onion, a couple of parsley sprigs, and a bay leaf.
3. Simmer gently until the chicken is tender, approximately one hour, then remove it and keep it warm while the broth is strained.
4. The broth is strained and set back over the chicken to simmer; a piece of butter and an ounce of flour are stirred together over the fire; little by little, part of the broth is stirred in, adding a beaten egg and stirring until the sauce is smooth. Salt and pepper are added, and it is served on a warm dish.
