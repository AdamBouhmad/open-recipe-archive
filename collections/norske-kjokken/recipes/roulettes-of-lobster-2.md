## Ingredients

- lobster
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- boiled, finely sliced meat from lobster
- black pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- eggs
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Cook two raw egg yolks in butter
2. Bake 12 ounces flour in the butter and gradually add 1 pint bouillon
3. Cook until thick, then mix in 1 pound boiled, finely sliced lobster meat, black pepper, and salt
4. When completely cold, take small portions and roll in bread crumbs
5. Dip in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs
6. Cook in clarified fat together with a little fat for cooking
7. For this portion, which makes approximately 16 roulettes, use 5 tablespoons butter and 4 eggs
8. Can be served with sharp sauce and stewed potatoes or with Semper
