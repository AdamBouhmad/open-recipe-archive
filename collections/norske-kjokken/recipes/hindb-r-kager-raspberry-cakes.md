## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pound patent flour

## Directions

1. Whip 12 eggs and 1 pound powdered sugar well together
2. Then stir in 1 pound patent flour
3. When this dough is well stirred together, press small cakes from it with a spoon, which you place on the buttered pan, sprinkle them with sugar and bake them quite light
