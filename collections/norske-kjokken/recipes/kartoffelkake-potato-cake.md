## Ingredients

- 1 pound peeled raw potatoes
- 10 egg yolks
- 1.5 cups powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 5 cents' worth of sweet almonds
- 1 cent worth bitter almonds
- juice of 2 lemons and the clean peel of 1 lemon
- 10 beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. The potatoes are cooked and peeled the day before they shall be grated, which must be done with a light hand, but to guard against lumps one can only use what falls behind the grater.
2. To 1 pound freshly grated raw potatoes take 10 egg yolks, which are beaten well with 1.5 cups powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon baking powder, mixed well in the potatoes, for 5 cents' worth of sweet almonds and for 1 cent worth of bitter almonds, the latter ground very finely, juice of 2 and the clean peel of 1 lemon.
3. When this, together with the potatoes is stirred and beaten well in a crock, the 10 beaten whites are stirred gently therein.
4. The cake is baked straight away at even heat, especially until it has risen, then at stronger, so that it can be finished in an hour's time.
