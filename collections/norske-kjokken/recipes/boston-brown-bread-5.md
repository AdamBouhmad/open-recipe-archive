## Ingredients

- 2 cent yeast, compressed
- 6 potatoes, mashed together with 3 cups flour
- 1 quart warm water
- 2 tablespoons fat
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 quart cornmeal
- 2 quarts cornmeal
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup molasses

## Directions

1. Make a dough consisting of: 2 cent compressed yeast, 6 potatoes mashed together with 3 cups flour, 1 quart warm water, 2 tablespoons fat and 2 tablespoons brown sugar
2. Mix well and set overnight in a warm place
3. When light, sift in: 1 quart cornmeal, 2 quarts cornmeal, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon baking powder and 1/2 cup molasses
4. Mix to a very soft dough, adding more warm water gradually if needed
5. Knead well and let rise 6 to 7 hours
6. Knead again and divide into loaves, place in well greased, deep, round bread pans
7. Let rise again for about an hour in a warm place, then bake in a moderately warm oven for about 3 to 4 hours
8. If setting this in the oven early in the day when the kitchen is small, it can stay in the oven the whole night
