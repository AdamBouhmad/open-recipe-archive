## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- egg whites
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 piece butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Put the egg yolks in one bowl and the egg whites in another
2. Add 1 tablespoon salt and 1 pinch pepper, and beat for half a minute
3. Put a piece of butter the size of a walnut in a clean omelette or frying pan and place over heat to warm
4. Gently stir the beaten egg whites into the yolks, and pour into the pan
5. Run a spatula along the bottom of the pan so the omelette folds together into a ball in the middle
6. When it is cooked enough, slide it onto a warm plate held close to it
7. Serve immediately
