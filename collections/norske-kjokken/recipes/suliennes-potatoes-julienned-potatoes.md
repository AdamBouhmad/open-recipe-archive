## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- Water
- Fat

## Directions

1. Peel, pare into thin slices and soak in well salted, cold water for at least one hour.
2. Wash well in several waters and dry in a clean cloth.
3. Fry golden in hot fat, then place on gray paper to drain from fat and serve hot.
