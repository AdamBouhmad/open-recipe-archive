## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 4 ounces flour
- 1 quart milk
- 8 tablespoons sugar
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together 8 egg yolks, 4 ounces flour and 1 quart milk well with 8 tablespoons sugar, whisk the egg whites and fold them into the dough
2. The dough is held quite thin on the griddle, and when the pancake is baked on one side, it is turned together without being baked on the other, and laid straight on the table
3. Jam can be laid inside. For this 2 pounds is needed
