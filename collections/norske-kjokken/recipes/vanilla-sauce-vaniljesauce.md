## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla
- 4 egg yolks
- A little white sugar

## Directions

1. Place 1 quart cream on heat with 2 tablespoons vanilla
2. When the cream boils, remove it and stir in 4 egg yolks that have first been stirred white with a little white sugar
3. Place it back on heat and stir constantly until it is just at boiling point and has become stiff
4. Then remove it, stir constantly until it is so cold that it no longer burns the skin, and pour it into a bowl for serving
