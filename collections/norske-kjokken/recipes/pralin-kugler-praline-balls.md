## Ingredients

- same dough as Kokosmakroner
- flour
- more flour than the original dough

## Directions

1. Use the same dough as Kokosmakroner, only take a little more flour in the dough
2. Roll small balls from this, which are baked at good heat
3. You can test when they are done by breaking a ball over
