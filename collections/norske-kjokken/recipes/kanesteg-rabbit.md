## Ingredients

- Heart
- Liver
- Fat meat, finely chopped
- Butter
- Water or broth
- Flour
- Lemon
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Have the butcher skin the hare, then clean it.
2. Cook the heart and liver and chop them finely, then mix with a little finely chopped fat meat.
3. Make a forcemeat as for sausages and stuff the chopped liver and heart into it.
4. Stuff the hare with this forcemeat and sew it shut.
5. Place the hare in a pan in the usual manner with a little butter and broth, with which it is braised.
6. It usually takes about an hour to cook.
7. A few minutes before removing it from heat, dust it with flour and then lay butter on it again.
8. Add a small glass of wine to the sauce and a little lemon juice.
9. Arrange lemon slices on the platter around the hare.
10. Cut off the head before sending it to the table.
11. Serve with lingonberry preserve.
