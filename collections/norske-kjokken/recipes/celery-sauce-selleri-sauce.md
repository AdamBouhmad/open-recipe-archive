## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- beef broth
- celery

## Directions

1. Two tablespoons of flour are mixed with a tablespoon full of butter. One pint of boiling beef broth is prepared. The broth and butter are stirred into the flour. Three celery roots cut into small pieces are boiled in water, which is then strained off. After that the celery is put into the flour and it is set over the fire for five to ten minutes while constantly stirring.
