## Ingredients

- Large, green cucumbers
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into as thin slices as possible.
2. Place on a plate with a large pinch of salt between the slices and a fork placed over them.
3. Let stand for a few hours, then press firmly between the hands so no salt water follows.
4. Place in a dish and add vinegar and pepper.
5. If you wish to prepare them immediately before the meal, you can sprinkle salt on the slices, place them between two hot plates and press them firmly so the salt dissolves around them, then drain and serve.
