## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Sugar (1 pound sugar per 1 pint juice)

## Directions

1. All the inside is removed from the lemons and pressed through a sieve or wrung out in a cloth.
2. For 1 pint of juice take 1 pound of sugar, it is put all at once on the stove, cooked and skimmed well in 10 minutes, poured into bottles and corked.
