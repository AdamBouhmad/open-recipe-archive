## Ingredients

- pint of tomatoes
- large carrot
- onion
- salt pork
- bay leaf
- four cloves
- tablespoon of salt
- quart and a half of water or bouillon
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. In a small sauce pan or casserole put one pint of tomatoes, a large carrot and as much onion sliced in layers, furthermore a large piece of salt pork sliced into small pieces, a little bay leaf, four cloves, a tablespoon of salt, a quart and a half of water or bouillon, egg yolk or water. It is simmered slowly for one hour and then passed through a sieve with a spoon.
