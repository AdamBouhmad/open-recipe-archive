## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef
- 1 pint cold water

## Directions

1. Chop the beef finely
2. Place it in 1 pint of cold water in a lined pot
3. Set over heat in the same water and bring slowly to a boil
4. Simmer for 15 to 30 minutes, depending on available time
5. Strain it and season lightly with salt. Since this diet is chiefly intended for the sick, it may be that there is not time to let it stand for an hour in water, and therefore less time will do, but the longer the beef can lie in cold water beforehand, the more nourishing extract will be drawn out.
