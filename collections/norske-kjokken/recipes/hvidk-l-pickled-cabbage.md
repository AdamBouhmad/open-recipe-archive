## Ingredients

- cabbage hearts
- salt
- boiling water
- turmeric
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage hearts into 4 pieces, removing the outer green leaves. Boil in boiling water for 3 minutes. Remove, dry well, and cover with a thick layer of salt. Spread out in an oven to dry. Brush off the salt and place in cold vinegar in which enough turmeric has dissolved to color it well. Let stand for 2 weeks.
