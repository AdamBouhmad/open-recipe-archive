## Ingredients

- Parsnip (Salsify) head
- Ground veal forcemeat
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Water
- Sewing twine or cord

## Directions

1. Once the outer leaves of the parsnip head are removed, cut a slice on one side where the stem is.
2. Hollow out the head and fill with ground veal forcemeat, place the slice back on, wrap it with some of the large leaves, and bind it with fine sewing twine or a piece of soft linen.
3. Place it in boiling salted water and cook for 1 to 2 hours depending on the size of the head.
4. Serve with stirred butter with nutmeg in it.
5. The hollowed out part can be cooked with the water and used for vegetable soup or stewed.
