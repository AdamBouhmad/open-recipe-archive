## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and cut the peel into strips, and cook in three changes of water until tender; then set aside.
2. On the remaining oranges remove the peel; the inner white skin is taken off and discarded; all oranges are cut into 4 pieces and the seeds removed; then cut the fruit into very small pieces, and all the juice that comes from them, without pressing them, is poured over the sugar; this is heated until the sugar is melted, and if the fruit is not very juicy, add a very little water.
3. Cook and skim for five minutes; then add the cooked peel and cook another ten minutes and finally the diced fruit and clean peel, and the whole cooks now two minutes longer.
4. When it is cold, pour it into small glasses or pots, cover with brandy paper and seal.
