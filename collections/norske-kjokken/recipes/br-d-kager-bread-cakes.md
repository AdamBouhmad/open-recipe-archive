## Ingredients

- 5 cents toast, or as much old bread as that equals the same amount
- 1 pint milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- lemon peel
- rubber sugar
- a little salt
- a little coriander if desired

## Directions

1. For 5 cents toast, or as much old bread as that equals the same amount, it is finely chopped and mixed with 1 pint milk with which it stands for an hour
2. Then it is stirred well with 3 eggs, 3 tablespoons melted butter, lemon peel, rubber sugar, a little salt, and if desired a little coriander
3. They are fried in clarified and rather long, so they become hard outside
4. Eaten with hot sugar syrup
