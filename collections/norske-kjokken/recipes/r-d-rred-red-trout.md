## Ingredients

- Trout
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and dry very carefully.
2. Then wrap it in a cloth that fits well, with the fish, and place it in a fish kettle, covered with salted, cold water.
3. It cooks in an hour or more depending on its size.
4. Season with cream sauce like the previous one, only of course without fish sauce.
