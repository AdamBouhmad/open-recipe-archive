## Ingredients

- 12 large apples
- a little water
- 1/2 lb butter
- 1/2 lb powdered sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- egg whites
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Peel 12 large apples, cut in pieces, remove the cores, and cook to pulp with a little water underneath
2. When cooked, stir in 1/2 lb butter and 1/2 lb powdered sugar, and when cooled, stir in 4 egg yolks and finally the beaten whites
3. Line the mold or tin with breadcrumbs and bake light brown
4. Serves about 3 people, served warm with powdered sugar
