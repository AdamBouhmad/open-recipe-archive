## Ingredients

- Fresh peaches
- Pie form with good dough
- Sugar
- Peach kernels (three per pie)
- A little water
- Dough for topping

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit, remove the stones, and cut it in pieces.
2. Line a pie form with good dough, and lay the fruit in and sprinkle with sugar.
3. It tastes good to include some peach kernels, three for each pie; a little water is added, and it is baked with a layer of dough on top, like Apple pie.
4. In winter use canned peaches.
5. If desired, apples can be used instead of peaches.
