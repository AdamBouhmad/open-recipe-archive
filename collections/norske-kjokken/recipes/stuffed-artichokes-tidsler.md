## Ingredients

- artichokes
- breadcrumbs
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- parsley
- onion
- egg
- butter
- cream
- minced parsley
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Cook in ten minutes. Cut the plant open down the long sides, and pick out the kernels. Keep the opening open with the help of a piece of clean wood or other device, and place the plant in cold, salted water, while the filling is prepared as follows: Breadcrumbs, very small pieces of fat, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, parsley and a very little minced onion are mixed finely together. This is stirred with a little cream and a beaten egg. With this fill the plant, bind it with soft twine to hold it together and bake it with a little hot water in the baking pan. When they begin to fry, baste them with butter and water; test with a straw, and when completely tender place them on a platter. Three spoonfuls of cream are added to the sauce which can also be thickened with a little cornstarch. A little minced parsley is put on, it boils just up and is held over the plant.
