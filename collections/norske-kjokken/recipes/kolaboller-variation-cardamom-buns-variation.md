## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- ½ pound powdered sugar
- 12 eggs
- Little nutmeg
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 pound butter, ½ pound powdered sugar, 12 eggs, a little nutmeg, and flour to make the dough suitable consistency
2. Cut into various mold shapes with the cookie cutter and fry like the smaller buns
