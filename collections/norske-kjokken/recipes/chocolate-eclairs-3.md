## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- egg weight in sugar
- half the weight in flour
- one level tablespoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs and egg weight in sugar with half the weight in flour and one level tablespoon baking powder
2. Bake in biscuit forms; each form is buttered and one spoonful of dough is placed in each and they are baked at even, good heat
3. When they are almost cold, the round side is covered with caramel glaze
