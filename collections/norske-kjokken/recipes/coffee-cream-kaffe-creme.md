## Ingredients

- Strong extract of good coffee
- 1 quart cold cream
- 5 ounces sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 pint of coffee extract (or according to taste)

## Directions

1. Cook or draw a strong extract of good coffee
2. Beat 1 quart cold cream with 5 ounces sugar
3. Beat 10 egg yolks first alone, then with the cream
4. Add this in an enameled pot together with 1 pint of coffee extract (one can also add according to taste)
5. Stir constantly until it rises, and likewise when it is poured into a dish until it becomes cold, whereupon it is served in small cups with cakes
