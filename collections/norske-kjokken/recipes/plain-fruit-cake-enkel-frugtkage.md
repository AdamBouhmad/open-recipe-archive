## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- raisins and currants
- 1.5 teaspoons Price's Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Set 2 ounces butter near the fire until it becomes soft without becoming oily
2. Line a baking form on the inside with paper that is smeared with butter
3. Stone 1/2 pound raisins and wash and dry well 1 pound currants
4. Cut 2 ounces sugar into fine small slices
5. Stir 6 ounces sugar to foam with 2 whole eggs; then add 2 others, stir again for a couple of minutes and finally a fifth egg, after which they are stirred a little more
6. Sift 6 ounces flour into it and stir well; then add the butter and fruit, and when it is well mixed, pour it into the form and bake in a moderately warm oven for approximately half an hour
7. It can be tested in the usual manner
