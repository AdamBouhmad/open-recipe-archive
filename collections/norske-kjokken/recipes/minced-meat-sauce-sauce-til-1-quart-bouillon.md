## Ingredients

- quart of bouillon
- tablespoon of flour
- butter
- bay leaf
- caraway
- white turnips
- small meatballs
- stewed oysters
- capers
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. For one quart of bouillon take one tablespoon of good flour which is undiluted in a piece of butter. This must first be melted in an earthenware and when it begins to boil add the flour and rub it out. It must not become brown at all and it is gradually diluted with broth. After that add bay leaf, caraway, white turnips, small meatballs and whatever stewed oysters, capers or mushrooms one has. If it is for game, pheasant or roasted duck then it is also good to stir a spoon of sweet cream into the sauce when it is to be served.
