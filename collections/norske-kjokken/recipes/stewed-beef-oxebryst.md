## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef brisket
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Marjoram
- Minced onion
- Parsley
- Soda or catsup
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into pieces about one inch long and place them in a stewing pot with just enough water to cover them, and cook gently for 2 hours.
2. Set it aside until the next morning, then season it with salt, pepper, marjoram, minced onion and parsley.
3. Cook it for another half hour and add a tablespoon of soda or catsup, and a tablespoon of flour mixed with cold water.
4. Let it boil up once and serve in a dish with onion on top.
