## Ingredients

- shrimp
- butter
- flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar
- bouillon or milk

## Directions

1. Cook shrimp
2. Remove meat from shells and cut into slivers
3. Brown shells in butter, spread over a pan, and add water
4. After cooking 1 quarter hour, strain it, and the shrimp butter is set aside on the side with flour, a little nutmeg and sugar, and thinned with bouillon until it becomes a porridge
5. Stir in shrimp meat and a little salt
6. When cold, cook as directed above
7. Shells can also be cooked with milk; shrimp butter is then thinned with cream or good milk
