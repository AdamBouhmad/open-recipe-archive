## Ingredients

- 4 large carrots
- 1 celery
- 2 parsnips
- 1 small parsley root
- 4 potatoes of medium size
- 1 parsnip
- 2 ounces butter
- Salt
- a few whole peppercorns
- 2.5 quarts water

## Directions

1. Cut all the roots into strips approximately one inch long and the thickness of a finger
2. After the potatoes are peeled, drain the water from them on a kitchen towel
3. Place 2 ounces of butter on the stove; when it is melted, add the roots and cook for a quarter hour with constant stirring so it does not brown
4. Then add salt, a few whole peppercorns and 2.5 quarts water, though a quart of water can be used instead, cooking intermittently
5. The whole soup must cook for about two hours, and to prevent it from boiling over, it must cook evenly and covered
6. If you find there are too many roots, some can be held back at serving
7. Serve with toasted bread and is for 3 to 4 persons
