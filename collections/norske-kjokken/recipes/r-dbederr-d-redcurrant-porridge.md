## Ingredients

- Redcurrants
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. This is made like crowberry porridge.
2. If you want it for a roast, it is poured off carefully, and not thickened, but only boiled in very little water and sufficient sugar.
3. In all cases, the stems are picked by taking hold with a fork in the red end and thereby pulling the skin off, and cutting the stems into small pieces.
