## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 teaspoonful baking soda dissolved in warm water
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix together 2 cups sugar, 1 cup butter, 2 eggs, 2 cups sour milk, 1 teaspoonful baking soda dissolved in warm water
2. Add flour to make the dough suitable thickness, work out, cut and fry like the previous ones
