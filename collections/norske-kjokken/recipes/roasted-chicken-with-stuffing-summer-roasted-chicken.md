## Ingredients

- Young chicken
- Parsley
- Butter
- Cold butter
- Bread
- Sugar
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. There is a skin on the young chicken's sides that must be removed before cooking
2. Wash the chicken
3. Decorate it with parsley by inserting one end at the side of the other down along the backbone, taking chopped and washed parsley with a little butter and pushing it with a finger down into the heart hole so a little parsley sticks up
4. The cavity can be filled with parsley and butter or with stuffing made as follows: Soak a few slices of French bread in milk, let it stand covered for an hour and break it into pieces with a spoon
5. Stir 1 piece of cold butter until creamy, then blend in 1-2 oz of bread, a little sugar, and salt
6. When well mixed, pour it into the cavity after it is opened from one side so you can get a hand in and with this loosen the skin as high as possible on the roast
7. If there are holes in the cavity, sew them together, and when the dough is in the cavity, sew it closed
8. Dust the roast with flour and roast for one hour
9. Summer roast is handled the same as young roast, but only allow 15 minutes per pound
