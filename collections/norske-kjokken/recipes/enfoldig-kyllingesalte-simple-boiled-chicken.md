## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. This method is far preferable to any other.
2. The chicken is soaked in cold water for 12 hours; after that it is washed very well and the skin is scrubbed with a stiff brush.
3. Place it in the steamer, cover with a lid and set it over a stove with boiling water.
4. Cook approximately 22 minutes per pound.
5. The water is kept at a steady boil.
6. When the chicken is served warm, it is cut and a layer of bread crumbs is placed over it to prevent the juices from running out.
