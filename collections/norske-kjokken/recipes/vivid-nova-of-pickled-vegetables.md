## Ingredients

- meat, bones and juice from cucumbers used for pickles
- salt
- vinegar
- various herbs and spices

## Directions

1. Put all the meat, bones and juice extracted from the cucumbers used for pickles on the fire with a little salt and boil for about 1 hour, stirring constantly, so nothing sticks, which would give a burned or boiled taste; if this happens, it must be discarded
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Put the juice back on the fire with 1 gill vinegar to 1 pint juice and boil until thick, about 1 hour
4. Then cool it, and the next day pour into small bottles or glasses and tie well
5. This sauce gives a pleasant taste to all kinds of white sauces, fricassees and the like, and should be added sparingly so it boils a little with the sauce
