## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Water or light bouillon
- Minced cooked meat (veal or pork)
- Cooked ham
- Parmesan cheese
- Light bouillon
- Tomatoes

## Directions

1. Let macaroni cook in water or light bouillon until they are almost completely cooked. Then drain the water so only a little remains, with which they can move and slide. In another saucepan cook finely-cut and boned meat and ham in light bouillon until well cooked. Then remove it from the sauce, and thereafter layer everything in a deep dish, so that you first lay a layer of macaroni, and sprinkle them with grated Parmesan cheese, then a light layer of the meat, then macaroni again with cheese, and then meat and ham, and continue this way as long as you have the ingredients; but the last must be cheese. Then set the dish in a warm oven, approximately 10 minutes, until it is well warmed and has taken on a golden-brown color on top. Serve hot.
