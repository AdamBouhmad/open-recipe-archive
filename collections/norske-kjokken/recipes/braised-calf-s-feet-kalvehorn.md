## Ingredients

- Cold calf's feet
- Butter
- Catsup
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vegetable
- Prepared mustard
- Warm water
- Onion

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of cold calf's feet and place them in a tin pot set in a pot of boiling water.
2. Over the meat put a sauce made of 3 tablespoons melted butter, a tablespoon of catsup, a teaspoon of vinegar, a little salt and pepper, a tablespoon of vegetable, a teaspoon of prepared mustard and a little warm water.
3. Place an onion on top and it is ready in half an hour when you are sure that the water in the outer pot is at a gentle boil.
