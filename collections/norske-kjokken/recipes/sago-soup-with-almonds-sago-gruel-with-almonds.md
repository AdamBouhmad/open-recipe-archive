## Ingredients

- sago grains
- candied fruit
- blanched almonds
- cream
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare it like the aforementioned soup, but instead of eggs and wine, add candied fruit and, if desired, blanched almonds.
2. Fruit, whole spices, if at the beginning, so the soup can come out in its own serving dish.
