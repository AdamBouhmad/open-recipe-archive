## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- A couple tablespoons brandy
- 1 pound strawberries
- 1 pound fresh, not too ripe strawberries
- Sugar, 1 pound

## Directions

1. To make excellent preserved strawberries, cook 1 pound sugar to syrup
2. Add a couple tablespoons brandy, then 1 pound strawberries and bring to boil
3. Pour out and let sit 2-4 days
4. Then take 1 pound fresh, not too ripe strawberries
5. Pour the juice from the first batch over these and let sit a few days
6. Carefully remove the strawberries to a sieve, strain the juice so the seeds come out, and cook with 1 pound more sugar
7. This is then poured cold over the berries
8. Strawberries should be picked the same day they are preserved, otherwise they taste bitter when preserved
