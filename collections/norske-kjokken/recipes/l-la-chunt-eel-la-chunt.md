## Ingredients

- 1 eel
- salt
- water
- 1 ounce flour
- 1 quart or good veal stock
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 bunch chopped parsley
- 1 finely chopped onion
- salt
- pepper
- 0.5 pounds butter
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. After cleaning the eel, rub it with salt and place it whole in boiling water that completely covers it, and set it over the fire.
2. When it boils, place it at the back of the oven where it can simmer gently; let it cook for one hour, then remove it and pull out all the bones.
3. In a saucepan, blend one ounce of flour with a quart or good veal stock, which is added little by little.
4. Then add the juice of one lemon, a bunch of chopped parsley, one finely chopped onion and a little salt and pepper.
5. Set this over the fire and stir until it becomes a thick sauce, then add 0.5 pounds of butter.
6. Bring the whole to a boil, then strain it.
7. Pour a little of the sauce onto a deep dish, place the eel in it and pour the rest of the sauce over it.
8. Whip the whites of 6 eggs into a foam, spread it over the top and bake the whole until brown in the oven.
