## Ingredients

- 1 pint tomatoes
- 2 quarts good broth or soup stock
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- 4 ounces rice, rinsed in cold water

## Directions

1. Cook the tomatoes until soft and strain through a sieve with a pestle
2. Stir them into 2 quarts broth and soup stock previously prepared; season with 1 tablespoon salt and 1 tablespoon pepper, then add 4 ounces of rice rinsed in cold water, and cook the soup slowly for half an hour before serving
