## Ingredients

- 1 large cup apples
- 2 large tablespoons sugar
- 1 pinch each of cinnamon and cloves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Bring the apples, sugar, and spices to a boil and simmer 5 minutes
2. Pour into a stone jar and cover with cloth
3. Let stand until completely cold
4. Fill small glasses or jars three-quarters full with berries, with or without stems
5. Pour the cold vinegar over them
6. Mix very well
