## Ingredients

- Wheat bread, finely crushed
- Water
- Sugar
- A little salt
- Egg yolk (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak the finely crushed wheat bread in water until softened.
2. Stir in sugar and a little salt.
3. The soup can be enriched with an egg yolk if desired.
