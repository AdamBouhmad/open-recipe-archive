## Ingredients

- A scant pint fresh milk
- 2 small tablespoons corn starch
- 2 eggs
- A scant tablespoon sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 0.5 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Cook a scant pint of fresh milk; in a little of the cold milk mix 2 small tablespoons corn starch; whip this together with 2 eggs.
2. When the milk has cooked, stir the mixture slowly in with a scant tablespoon sugar, 0.5 cup butter and 2 tablespoons lemon juice.
3. Then make a cake as follows: 3 eggs are stirred together with 1 cup white sugar, 0.5 cup milk, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 3 tablespoons cold water; it is divided into 2 ordinary pie pans and baked in a quick oven.
4. When the cakes are ready, take them out, and between both layers the cream is filled; if desired, it can again be glazed with egg white and sugar.
5. Four egg yolks are whipped stiff; then 1 cup sugar is stirred in and then 1 cup milk, which is heated together with 1 tablespoon baking powder; when it is stirred together, the whipped egg white is added.
6. It is divided into six ordinary pie pans and baked light brown. It will be enough for 2 pies.
7. A cream is made of 1 quart milk, 1 tablespoon corn starch, dissolved in a little cold milk, 3 eggs and a little vanilla.
8. When it is cold, it is divided in two parts and spread between two cake layers.
