## Ingredients

- 1 pint sour milk or buttermilk
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 tablespoonfuls warm fat
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 pint sour milk or buttermilk, 2 beaten eggs, 1 cup sugar, 1 teaspoonful soda, 2 tablespoonfuls warm fat
2. Then add as much flour as you find suitable thickness, so it can be handled
