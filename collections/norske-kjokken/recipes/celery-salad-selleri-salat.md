## Ingredients

- equal parts cooked celeriac and celery
- egg yolks
- mustard
- salt
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the celeriac and celery into very small pieces
2. Mix the celeriac and celery well together in a deep bowl
3. Pour the sauce, which has become slightly warm, over them
4. Do not pour the sauce over until the salad is to be served
