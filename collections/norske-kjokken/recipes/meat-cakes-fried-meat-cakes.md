## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- butter
- greens
- hot water

## Directions

1. The mixture is made as firm as for meatballs, and from it small round patties are fried in brown butter.
2. They are placed on a platter with a sauce, and the spoon is dipped in hot water for each time in order for the sauce to cover them.
3. They are served with greens or with sharp sauce and potatoes.
