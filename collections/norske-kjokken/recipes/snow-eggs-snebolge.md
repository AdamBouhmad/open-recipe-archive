## Ingredients

- Egg whites
- Egg yolk
- For each 4 egg whites, a little tablespoonful of current or gooseberry jelly

## Directions

1. Only egg whites and egg yolk are used
2. For each 4 egg whites, take a little tablespoonful of currant or gooseberry jelly
3. Beat it all together for a long time, even after it has become hard foam, as it thereby becomes whiter and finer in appearance
4. From four egg whites comes a fairly large portion
5. Place it in a tall cup on a dish where there is enough cold cream so that the cup can float in it
6. Eat it with small dry cakes
