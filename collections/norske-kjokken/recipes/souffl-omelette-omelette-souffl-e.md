## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- egg whites
- powdered sugar
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. For each egg, take 1 tablespoon powdered sugar and half a tablespoon flour
2. Beat the yolks first, then with the sugar, then add flour and finally the beaten whites
3. This is not fried in fat like other omelettes, but in a frying pan covered with handkerchiefs over the entire pan, or in the oven in forms lined with stiff paper and bent to the shape of the mold
4. The forms should be about 3 fingers high; 2 fingers dough is put in
5. Grease the forms well with butter and sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and put butter in the frying pan
6. If baked in the oven and it gives too much top heat, it is best to put paper over, as they must not become too brown, which they tend to do
7. They are served cold and placed one on another with compote between
8. On top they can be topped with egg foam and compote
