## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter
- 0.5 pound water
- 1 tablespoon mace
- 0.5 teaspoon alum dissolved in warm water
- 12 teaspoons saleratus dissolved in molasses
- flour to make dough stiff

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together with flour enough to make a stiff dough, and cut into round cakes.
