## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Butter
- Flour
- Pepper
- Cucumbers or apples
- Bouillon
- Vinegar
- Soda
- Nutmeg or curry
- Salt
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Place the calf's head in sufficient boiling salted water and cook until tender, then remove it and untie the binding.
2. Make a brown sauce of browned butter, flour cooked for a time, pepper and cucumbers or apples, which are thinned with bouillon, along with a little vinegar or soda.
3. Give the head a coating in this sauce, then keep it warm on a serving platter and cover with the sauce.
4. If there is not enough head meat, some veal can be cooked with the meat and placed in the middle of the platter with the head around it.
