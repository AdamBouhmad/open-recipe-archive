## Ingredients

- Beef heart
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolk
- Onion
- Catsup
- Parsley
- Celery root
- Cayenne pepper
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Wash the heart well and cut it into quarters, about an inch long, add water so it stands over the pieces and cook for ten minutes.
2. Salt the water a little to draw out the blood and skim very carefully as the scum rises.
3. Take out the heart and strain the soup, mince the heart finely and put it back in the soup with a sliced onion, a large tablespoon of catsup, minced parsley, a little minced celery root, a little cayenne pepper and a good lump of butter.
4. Cook now until the meat is very tender, then stir a tablespoon of flour into it, dissolved in a little cold water.
5. Let it boil up once and serve straight.
