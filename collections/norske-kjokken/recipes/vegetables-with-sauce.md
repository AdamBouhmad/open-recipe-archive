## Ingredients

- Vegetables (cut into thumb-length pieces)
- Veal or game stock
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Sauce

## Directions

1. The vegetables are broken into thumb-long pieces and cooked until tender. The sauce is prepared beforehand. Half a pound of veal stock is cut in strips, placed in cold water and cooked for half an hour. Then a minced onion and a pint of tomatoes, peeled and cut in slices, are added. It cooks for an hour, then the meat is removed and the rest remains. In all, only a pint of sauce is needed for a short dish of macaroni.
