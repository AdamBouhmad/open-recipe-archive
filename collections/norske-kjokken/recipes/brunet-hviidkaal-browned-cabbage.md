## Ingredients

- Butter
- Sugar
- Cabbage
- Salt
- Egg

## Directions

1. A good spoonful of butter is browned, and sugar is stirred into it; the cabbage must be sliced very finely and laid in it over a gentle fire under a covered pot until it is tender, then salt is added.
2. It is good, if one has it, to stir a little egg into the cabbage when it is finished. Boiled cabbage is best browned this way.
