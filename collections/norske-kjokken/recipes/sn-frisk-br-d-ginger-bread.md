## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground clove
- 1 tablespoon baking soda, dissolved in boiling water
- wheat flour as needed to make suitable dough

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, warm the molasses slightly, add spices and sugar, and whisk for ten minutes
2. Then add the water, soda and flour
3. Stir very well and bake in three small loaves
4. Serve half fresh
