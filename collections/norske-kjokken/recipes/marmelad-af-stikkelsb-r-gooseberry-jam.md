## Ingredients

- Ripe gooseberries
- Sand
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the ripe gooseberries and cook them in a pot for one hour with just enough sand in the bottom of the pot so they cannot burn on the bottom. Then take 1 pound of sugar to each pound of berries, and now cook the berries again for one hour with the sugar. Stir and grind in a colander the whole time.
