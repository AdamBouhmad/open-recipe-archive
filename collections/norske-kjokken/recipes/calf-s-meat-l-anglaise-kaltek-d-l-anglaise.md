## Ingredients

- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 2 pounds calf's meat
- Water
- Flour

## Directions

1. Place a large tablespoon of butter over the fire in a pot; when it begins to brown add 2 pounds of calf's meat, which can be simple meat, shoulder or neck, and brown it.
2. Then add enough water so it stands just over the meat, skim as much as necessary; when the meat is tender stir a few tablespoons of flour in good flour paste and with this thicken the sauce.
3. It is an inexpensive and good-tasting dish.
