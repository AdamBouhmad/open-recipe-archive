## Ingredients

- 2 gallons vinegar
- 1 pint white mustard seed
- 4 ounces ginger
- 3 ounces black peppercorns
- 1 ounce allspice
- 2 ounces cloves
- 1 ounce mace
- 1 ounce nutmeg
- 2 ounces turmeric
- 1 large handful garlic
- 1 handful horseradish
- 4 pounds sugar
- 2 ounces celery seed
- 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together well and expose to the sun for 3 days.
