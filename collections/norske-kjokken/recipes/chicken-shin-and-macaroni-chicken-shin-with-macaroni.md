## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- beef or chicken stock
- chicken shin
- salt
- ground meat
- butter

## Directions

1. 1 pound of macaroni is cooked in as much beef bouillon that it barely covers them, until they are tender, but not too much cooked.
2. The chicken shin is cooked well and must not be too salty.
3. When this is cold, a mold is thickly covered with ground meat; in it are placed small slices of the lean part of the shin and the macaroni with their sauce, and then ground meat over.
4. You can either stir a little unbeaten butter into the macaroni while they are warm, or let a little of the fat remain on the shin pieces after cooking, as you prefer.
5. It must cook at least 3 hours.
