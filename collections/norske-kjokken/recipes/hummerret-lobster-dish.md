## Ingredients

- One medium lobster
- Salt
- Water
- Lobster meat
- Salad oil
- Bread
- Nutmeg
- Cayenne pepper
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Egg yolk
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Choose a medium-sized lobster, not too large.
2. Put a handful of salt in a pot of boiling water and after having tied the claws together, fasten the lobster to the water.
3. Cook for half an hour to an hour, depending on its size.
4. When it is done, take it up and lay it with the back up on a sieve to dry.
5. When it is cold, scrape the body and tail and the claws.
6. Boil the lobster and pick out the meat, being careful that the shell remains intact.
7. Rub the shell with salad oil; into as much of the red roe as you have, add a little nutmeg, cayenne pepper, lemon juice, butter and a little egg yolk.
8. If it is lobster, rub the shell with egg yolk.
9. Put the meat back into the shells, cover lightly with soft bread and butter and bake brown in the oven.
10. Arrange with parsley and lemon slices around it.
