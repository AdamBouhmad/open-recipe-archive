## Ingredients

- Good ripe berries, freshly picked
- Brandy

## Directions

1. Crush and strain the berries in a cloth into completely dry bottles
2. Pour enough brandy over so it stands a couple inches above the juice
3. Bottles should be small, as the juice does not keep once opened
4. Seal and cork
