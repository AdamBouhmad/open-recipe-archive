## Ingredients

- Water
- Milk
- Cornmeal
- Salt

## Directions

1. In the preparation of this very nourishing and economical dish, one usually lets half water and half milk boil, then stirs in as much cornmeal as it gets a proper consistency, lets it boil a short time with stirring, and adds salt at serving.
