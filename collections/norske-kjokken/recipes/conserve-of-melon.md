## Ingredients

- Melon, not fully ripe
- Strong brine

## Directions

1. A melon that is not fully ripe is peeled, hollowed out and cut into finger-length pieces, about an inch wide and half as thick, rinsed, placed in a brine that is so strong that it can just make an egg float, and stays therein for 4 days.
