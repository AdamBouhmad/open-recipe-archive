## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal breast
- 1 pound beef
- 1 large tablespoon flat bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- a little pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- half a small pinch of powdered or grated cinnamon
- brown butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Mince the veal breast and beef finely
2. Stir in one large tablespoon of flat bread crumbs, 2 eggs, a little pepper, salt, nutmeg, and half a small pinch of powdered or grated cinnamon
3. Place the veal breast in a deep roasting pan with browned butter and brown it well on both sides
4. Pour bouillon over it and cook slowly in the oven for approximately 2 hours
5. Turn it occasionally during cooking
