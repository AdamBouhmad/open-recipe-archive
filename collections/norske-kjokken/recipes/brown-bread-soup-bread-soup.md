## Ingredients

- 1 small piece of sweet bread, about 1 pound by weight
- Water
- 6 large pieces of celery
- Beef stock
- Small white carrots (cut into pieces)
- Beef balls (meatballs)

## Directions

1. Rinse the sweet bread and cook it in barely enough water together with 6 large pieces of celery until they easily fall apart.
2. Strain the whole mixture with beef stock, cook a little, rub through a colander, then through a sieve.
3. Set it back on the fire with some finely cut white carrots.
4. When it boils, place small beef balls on top.
5. In the absence of beef balls, the soup may be eaten with toasted bread.
6. Alternatively, brown a piece of butter in the pot, add 2 tablespoons full of finely cut carrots and 1 tablespoon of onion, and fry well until light brown.
7. Then add the soup gradually, simmer gently until the carrots fall apart, rub through a colander with more soup, finally through a sieve, and finish with cut white carrots and small beef balls.
