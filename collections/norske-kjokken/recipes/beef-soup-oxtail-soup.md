## Ingredients

- 2 oxtails
- Water
- Salt and pepper
- Small onions
- Celery roots (to be blanched separately)
- Parsley and celery
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Butter
- A piece of white sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the oxtails very well in cold water and let them stand for a while in fresh water.
2. Bring them to the boil slowly with even, uniform heat; skim well as they cook.
3. Remove the lid only when the soup foams.
4. Do not dilute the soup, but if necessary, add only boiling water after all has been removed.
5. Strain the soup through a fine sieve; if it is not very clear, place a cloth inside.
6. Let it cool slightly before straining; remove the fat and pour carefully back into the pot so the sediment does not come with it.
7. When the soup boils again, skim once more and remove all foam carefully.
8. The vegetables (small onions and celery roots) are best cooked separately in water or light soup so the soup remains clearer and does not lose flavor.
9. When serving, a piece of white sugar may be added for pleasant flavor.
10. This portion requires about 3 hours of cooking.
