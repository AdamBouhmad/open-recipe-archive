## Ingredients

- smoked eel
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- shallot

## Directions

1. Beat and skin the smoked eel from the bones; soak it for half an hour in cold milk, rinse in water and dry in a cloth
2. Cut into very small pieces and prepare with fine oil, vinegar, pepper and chopped shallot
