## Ingredients

- butter
- egg yolk
- flour
- egg yolk
- cream

## Directions

1. Take equal parts butter and egg yolk for brown sauce. The butter is browned in a pan and a tablespoon of flour is browned in it. This is made with a couple of tablespoons of egg yolk. When this boils, the cream is added and stirred hard until the sauce boils. If you have no egg yolk, then do not use flour either, but then the cream must be good. If it should happen that it separates, a little more cream is taken and if it becomes too thick from that, it is remedied with soda. This sauce is for veal and poultry and can also be used for beef, goose and duck. For poultry one should avoid soda.
