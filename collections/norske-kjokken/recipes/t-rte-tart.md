## Ingredients

- 10 egg yolks
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- egg whites beaten to stiff foam
- 2 ounces corn starch

## Directions

1. 10 egg yolks are stirred together with 1 pound powdered sugar.
2. The whites are beaten to stiff foam, mixed with 2 ounces corn starch and stirred carefully into the eggs, poured then into the form, which is lined with greased paper and baked a couple of times with little heat straight, later more.
