## Ingredients

- watermelon rind with skin and hard sugar
- ginger to taste
- vinegar
- turmeric
- mace blossoms
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take the thick rind, scraping off the outer green skin and the inner soft flesh
2. Cut the pieces into small strips and place in strong brine for 10 days
3. Then soak them in fresh water, changing the water daily for 10 days
4. Place them in clear water in a stewpan, heat slowly and boil for 5 minutes
5. Remove and cool in ice water; let them rest overnight
6. Next day, give them a small boiling for 5 minutes in strong brine water
7. Do not boil too hard
8. Remove directly from the brine water and place in water, where they rest for 4 hours
9. Then give another boiling for 5 minutes in fresh water and let them rest in this water overnight
10. Each day thereafter, put enough water over the pieces to cover them, and add enough sugar to make it sweet but not syrup
11. Boil the pieces in this for 10 minutes, drain the water, and spread the pieces on paper to cool
12. Meanwhile prepare a syrup, calculating the same weight of sugar as of rind pieces, ginger according to taste for 1 gallon of pickle, and 1 cup water for every 2 pounds of sugar
13. When the sugar is melted and the syrup hot but not boiling, add the pieces and boil slowly until they are clear
14. Remove and pour back on the forks, while to the syrup add 1 pint vinegar for every pound of sugar, 1 tablespoonful turmeric for 1 gallon of pickle, mace blossoms, cloves and cinnamon to taste
15. Bring to boil, add the pieces and boil for 15 minutes
16. Put in glass jars and seal well
