## Ingredients

- 1 pound clarified butter
- 1 pound powdered sugar
- 1 pound fine flour
- 10 egg yolks
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Once 1 pound clarified butter is stirred to glow, it is stirred for a quarter of an hour with 1 pound powdered sugar and thereafter with 1 pound fine flour and 10 egg yolks, one at a time, finally the beaten whites.
2. It is poured into a greased form and baked at even heat, especially in the beginning, in 1.5 hours.
