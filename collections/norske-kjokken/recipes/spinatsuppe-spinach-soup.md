## Ingredients

- 2 quarts spinach
- Salted water
- 2 quarts milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 small teaspoon salt
- A little white pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook spinach in slightly salted water; when it is completely soft so it can be drawn apart easily, drain the water through a sieve
2. Chop the spinach finely and rub through the sieve
3. Cook 2 quarts milk, stir the spinach in it, and when it is near boiling season it with 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed in cold milk; season with a little teaspoon salt, a little white pepper and nutmeg
4. It must be served as soon as it boils up
