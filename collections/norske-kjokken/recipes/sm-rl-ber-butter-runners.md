## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound dry rubber sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 pound butter and stir until it is light with 1 pound dry rubber sugar, 1 pound flour and 1 egg
2. When it is too hard to stir, knead it with the hands until it releases the fingers
3. Roll with the hands as meal into small round cakes, flatten them, place on the pan and bake quite light
