## Ingredients

- Beef
- Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Beat a piece of beef well on eight sides.
2. Place it in vinegar overnight.
3. Remove it and dry it, but do not salt it, then lard it with fat meat strips.
4. Brown butter in a pot that fits the meat nicely, then brown the meat in it.
5. Add a little water to the butter and cook it gently until tender.
6. When removed, stir a little flour into the sauce.
7. For hard and lean meat, cut it into long pieces about 2 inches thick and treat them the same way.
