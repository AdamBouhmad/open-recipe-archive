## Ingredients

- 4 ounces rice
- 4½ tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon extract
- 3 quarts milk

## Directions

1. Rinse rice well in two waters and pour into a mold that can be made of tin
2. Add 4½ tablespoons sugar and 1 tablespoon lemon extract
3. Pour 3 quarts milk over and place in a moderately warm oven to bake for 1.5 hours until it is in a creamy consistency
