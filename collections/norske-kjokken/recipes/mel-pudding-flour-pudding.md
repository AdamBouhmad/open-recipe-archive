## Ingredients

- 4 ounces flour
- 4 ounces butter
- 3 gills milk
- 8 egg yolks
- handful of sugar
- little cardamom
- one ounce salt

## Directions

1. Bake flour well with 4 ounces butter
2. Cook 3 gills milk and pour boiling into it gradually
3. When baked, take it up and let it cool
4. Stir together 8 egg yolks, handful of sugar, little cardamom and one ounce salt very well
5. Add the beaten whites gradually
6. Cook for 2 hours and serve with fruit or wine sauce
7. This cannot be baked
