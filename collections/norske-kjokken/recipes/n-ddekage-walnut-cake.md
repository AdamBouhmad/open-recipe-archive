## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup cold water
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 cupful of walnut kernels, chopped and sifted to fine, together with the beaten whites

## Directions

1. Combine ingredients as listed
2. Bake according to standard method
