## Ingredients

- 1 pint shelled green peas
- 1 pint string beans
- a couple of carrots
- a couple of parsnips
- a small cauliflower head
- salad oil
- vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- a little pepper

## Directions

1. Shell the peas. Use only the tender parts of a small bunch of asparagus. Cut the string beans into various shapes. Clean the carrots and parsnips and cut them into pieces about an inch in diameter. Cut the small cauliflower head into small florets.
2. Boil these ingredients and then place them in cold water so as not to lose their color. Boil a firm cauliflower head without breaking the florets, stirring the stem and root; when tender, peel and stir it like the carrots. Arrange the vegetables on a platter and dress with salad oil, vinegar, 1 tablespoon of salt and a little pepper.
