## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit, cut into slices and remove the core; then weigh it and take sugar and fruit in the proportion of one pound to one and a half pounds.
2. Place in preserving liquid, lay it out, and add for each pound of sugar a small cup of water.
3. When this is brought to boil, take out the fruit and spread it on dishes which are placed in the sun.
4. Cook the syrup and skim for half an hour; then add the fruit again and cook for fifteen minutes.
5. Take out the fruit, place in crocks and pour the boiling juice over; cover with a cloth until it is cold, then place brandy paper over and tie in the usual way.
