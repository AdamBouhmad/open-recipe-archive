## Ingredients

- Raw ground meat
- chicken
- sharp brown sauce

## Directions

1. Leg and wing are taken from the chicken, and from the back the lower piece is removed; the breast is divided into 4 pieces.
2. When they are well cooked and again cold, raw ground meat is placed in a baking dish, then a layer of the cooked chicken, and then again ground meat.
3. It is then fried in a roasting pan until the forcemeat is cooked; they are served with sharp brown sauce.
4. The chickens can also be filled inside with ground meat.
