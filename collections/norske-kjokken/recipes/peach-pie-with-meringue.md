## Ingredients

- Fresh peaches without stones
- Three egg whites
- Three tablespoons fine sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Line the form with fine dough; prepare peaches without stones and fill it.
2. While the pie is in the oven, whip three egg whites with three tablespoons of fine sugar until they can stand alone.
3. When the pie is just baked, take it out, and spread the egg whites on top with a little butter.
4. Set it back in the oven until the egg whites are stiff and browned.
5. They must not be brown. This is the most delicate of all pies.
