## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup molasses
- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon Price's Baking Powder
- 1 nutmeg
- a little sugar
- cinnamon
- allspice
- and 1 glass brandy

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar as usual first, then the beaten egg yolks and stir well before half of the flour is added
2. then add the raisins, the rest of the flour together with the stiffly beaten egg whites, and finally the brandy
3. This cake must bake for a couple of hours and can keep for several months
4. Bake in a hot oven
