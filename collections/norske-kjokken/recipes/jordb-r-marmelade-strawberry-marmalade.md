## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound sugar per pound fruit
- 1 pint currant juice for 4 pounds strawberries

## Directions

1. For each pound of fruit use 0.5 pound sugar
2. For 4 pounds strawberries take 1 pint currant juice and cook together with the strawberries for half an hour, stirring the whole time
3. Remove nearly all the juice from the berries, add the sugar, and let boil rapidly for 20 minutes, skimming carefully the whole time
4. Put in small glasses with brandy paper over
5. The currant juice can be omitted, but makes the taste much finer
