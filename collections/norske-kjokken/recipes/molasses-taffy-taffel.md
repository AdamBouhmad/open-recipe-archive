## Ingredients

- 1 part good molasses
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 piece butter the size of an egg
- 1 tablespoon saleratus

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the vinegar, blend with the molasses, and cook with constant stirring until it becomes hard when a spoonful is dropped in cold water
2. Then stir in the butter and saleratus, the latter dissolved in warm water
3. After this stir once more and pour into greased pans
4. When cooled so it can be handled, cut into squares or, while still soft enough, pull into sticks; for this grease your fingertips with butter
