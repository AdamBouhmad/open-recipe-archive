## Ingredients

- lobster
- fresh green salad
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- melted butter
- mustard
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the lobster finely
2. Cut three heads of fresh green salad finely; layer it in a fairly deep bowl
3. Rub out four hard-boiled egg yolks, stir in three spoonfuls melted butter, one teaspoon mustard, a little cayenne pepper and salt, two spoonfuls sugar and two cups vinegar
4. Place over heat and when warm, pour over the salad
