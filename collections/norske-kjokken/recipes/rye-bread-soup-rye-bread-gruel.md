## Ingredients

- 1 pound rye bread
- 3 quarts water
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- 1 glass white wine
- juice of two lemons
- sugar
- 1 half pint cream

## Directions

1. Cut the rye bread finely, cook it in 3 quarts of water until it is smooth, strain it through a sieve, and if this is not fine, then also through a strainer, and set it again with lemon zest and cinnamon.
2. When the soup boils, add a glass of white wine, juice of two lemons and sugar.
3. Whip half a pint of cream to foam in the tureen, and pour the soup over it while whisking with the whisk.
4. If you do not have cut bread to use, the bread can be grated, and then the soup does not need to be strained.
