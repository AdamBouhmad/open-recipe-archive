## Ingredients

- Meat or steak
- Batter made of one pound flour
- Beaten eggs
- Two ounces melted butter
- One pint milk
- A little sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. The meat or steak is cut into quite thin slices and dipped in a batter made of one pound flour, beaten eggs, two ounces melted butter, one pint milk, a little sugar and salt, and fried in butter on a pan.
