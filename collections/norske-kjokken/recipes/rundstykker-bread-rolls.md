## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- ½ pound sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 quart milk
- 2 cents yeast
- Flour
- Beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 cup butter well with ½ pound sugar; then with 4 egg yolks
2. Warm 1 quart milk, dissolve 2 cents yeast in it, mix it in and stir in as much flour as will make the dough a suitable consistency (somewhat thicker than for hand-pancakes); fold in the beaten egg whites
3. Beat it well in a bowl, which covered stands by the heat for a couple of hours, so the dough can rise
4. When the pan is hot, add a little butter in each hollow, and fill the roll a little more than halfway; when they are baked enough on one side, turn them with two spoons
