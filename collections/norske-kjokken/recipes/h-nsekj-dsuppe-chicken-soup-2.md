## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- beef or veal broth
- half meatballs
- bread or flour dumplings
- parsley
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make it like other beef soup; however, take only water for the portion of soup you desire, if the hens are young; if they are old and tough, you must take nearly double of what you wish to keep
2. Cut up the hens so that legs and wings are removed; next the thighs are separated from the breast; the breast is chopped into 2-3 pieces; the breastbone is removed, and each half is divided in two
3. Include legs, feet and inner organs
4. Use only half meatballs on chicken soup, and bread or flour dumplings, parsley and a little nutmeg
5. Serve with toasted bread
