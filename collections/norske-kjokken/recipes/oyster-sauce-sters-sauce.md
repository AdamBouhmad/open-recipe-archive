## Ingredients

- oysters
- water or broth
- butter
- flour
- oyster broth
- white wine
- lemon juice
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. The beards are removed from the oysters, boiled very briefly in a little water or broth and strained off. A tablespoon of butter is melted, the flour is stirred into it and finished with the strained broth together with white wine, a little lemon juice, salt, nutmeg and as much meat broth that it becomes the right thickness. The oysters are added at the end and should not be boiled but only heated. Very small meatballs can be added which must be boiled in the broth before it is used for the sauce. Cooked and peeled mushrooms can also be added together with the oysters. It is finished with a little egg yolk.
