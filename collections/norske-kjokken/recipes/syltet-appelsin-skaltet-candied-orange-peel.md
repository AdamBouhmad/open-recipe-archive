## Ingredients

- Apples or oranges
- Sugar (pound for pound with fruit)
- Water

## Directions

1. The fruit is weighed whole and sugar is calculated pound for pound with the fruit.
2. The fruit is peeled neatly, and the peelings are cut into small strips.
3. They are cooked tender, and the water is changed twice, each time with warm water.
4. The juice from the fruit is poured over the sugar; this is brought to a boil, after which the strips are put in and it cooks for two minutes.
5. Lemon peel can be treated the same way, but more sugar should be calculated.
