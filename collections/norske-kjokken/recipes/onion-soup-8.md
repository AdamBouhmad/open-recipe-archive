## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon of chopped onions
- Butter
- 6 tablespoons flour
- Soup (beef or other)
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Brown a tablespoon full of chopped onions in butter.
2. Stir in 6 tablespoons of flour.
3. Add the soup gradually, bit by bit, and let it simmer for one hour.
4. Strain it, skim off the fat, and finish it with another seasoning.
5. Serve with toasted bread.
