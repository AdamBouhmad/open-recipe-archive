## Ingredients

- cooked chicken celeriac and celery
- 3 or 4 egg yolks
- mustard
- salt
- cold butter
- vinegar
- salad oil
- warm stock

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked chicken celeriac and celery into small pieces
2. The whites and yolks of the eggs are pressed separately and the oil is whipped slowly into the yolks
3. Then mix all ingredients in a bowl (stock excepted) and place over heat
4. It simmers until fairly thick
5. When cooled again, the stock is whipped in and poured over the salad one hour before serving
