## Ingredients

- young peas
- water
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. They must be cooked this way: They are shelled, placed in cold water, and then in a saucepan with boiling water. When they are half done, all the water is poured off, except a little cupful; to this is added an equal amount of milk and a large spoonful of butter. It simmers until the peas are completely tender, after which they are arranged with a little salt and pepper, as desired.
