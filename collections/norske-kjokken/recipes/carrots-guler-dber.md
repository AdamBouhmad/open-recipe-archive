## Ingredients

- carrots
- butter
- flour
- milk or cream
- minced parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. If they are young, scraped, old ones are peeled thinly and sliced. They are put in boiling, salted water and cook from half an hour to an hour. Then they are cut into pieces about an inch in diameter; a large spoonful of butter is melted; into this is stirred a spoonful of flour and it is thinned with good milk or cream; into this the carrots are given a seasoning, together with a spoonful of finely minced parsley.
