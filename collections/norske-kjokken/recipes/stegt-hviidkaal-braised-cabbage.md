## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Butter
- Sugar
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. The outer and hardest leaves of white cabbage are cooked until tender in water with a little soda; this is discarded, and a little bouillon, some butter, nutmeg and a little salt are added, with which it is cooked until tender.
2. Browned potatoes can be placed on top if sauce and mushrooms are lacking.
