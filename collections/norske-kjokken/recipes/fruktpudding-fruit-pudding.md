## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- equal amount salt
- 4 ounces or 6 large tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon lemon extract
- ½ pint milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour together with baking powder and salt
2. Rub 4 ounces (or 6 tablespoons) granulated sugar and 2 tablespoons butter together well
3. When mixed but not creamed, add the flour little by little
4. Make a well in the center of the dough and add 1 egg, ½ teaspoon lemon extract, and ½ pint milk
5. Mix to a soft dough and pour into a mold that is well buttered and dusted with flour
6. Cook for approximately 1 hour
7. Test with a splinter of wood stuck down into it; if the splinter does not catch on it, it is done
8. Turn the pudding out of the mold and serve with cream sauce
