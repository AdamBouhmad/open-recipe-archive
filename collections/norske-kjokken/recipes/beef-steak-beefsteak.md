## Ingredients

- Porterhouse beef
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. The meat for beef steak should be a Porterhouse.
2. The sinew is cut from the sides; it is best to get a thick piece, which you then beat well.
3. Then place it on the grill and cook it over clear coals from hardwood.
4. When you think it is cooked enough on one side, turn it.
5. You must not let it become too dry; it should be red inside.
6. When it is done, place it on a platter, lay cold butter and salt over it, and let it rest a moment in the oven.
