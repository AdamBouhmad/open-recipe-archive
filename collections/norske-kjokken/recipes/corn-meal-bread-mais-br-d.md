## Ingredients

- 2 quarts corn meal
- 3 pints warm water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 large tablespoons homemade yeast or 2 cents compressed yeast
- wheat flour as needed
- 1 pint warm water

## Directions

1. Mix 2 quarts corn meal with 3 pints warm water
2. Add 1 tablespoon salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 2 large tablespoons homemade yeast or 2 cents compressed yeast
3. Let it rise 5 hours in a warm place
4. Add as much wheat flour as needed to make a dough that can be handled, and 1 pint warm water
5. Let it rise again for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
6. Knead it a little and divide it into bread pans
7. Let it stand a few minutes before placing in the oven
8. Bake in a hot oven for 1.5 hours
9. All bread made of corn meal should be eaten warm
