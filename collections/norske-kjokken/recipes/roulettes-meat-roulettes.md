## Ingredients

- 2 pounds calf liver
- 1 pound fresh meat from the liver
- 1 pound raw Medister sausage
- 4 anchovies
- several raw egg yolks
- black pepper
- vinegar
- salt
- a little thyme
- a little grated rye bread
- a few teaspoons of mustard
- cream
- 6 to 8 eggs
- approximately 1 pint flour
- bacon slices
- smoked bacon

## Directions

1. Scrape and grate the calf liver through a sieve
2. Mince fresh meat from the liver and mix with the liver, raw Medister sausage, and anchovies removed from bones
3. Mix well with raw egg yolks, black pepper, vinegar, salt, a little thyme, a little grated rye bread, a few teaspoons of mustard, cream, and 6 to 8 eggs
4. In a form with a firm bottom, place a layer of forcemeat, then sliced roasted meat, then another layer of forcemeat, alternating layers
5. Top layer should be forcemeat covered with pieces of fat and smoked bacon to prevent browning
6. Remove bacon layer when baked
7. Bake for 1.5 hours
8. Serve warm with sharp, brown sauce, though most commonly served cold for open-faced sandwiches without sauce
