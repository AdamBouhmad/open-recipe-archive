## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds flour
- 1/2 pound melted butter
- 1/2 pound rubber sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons baking powder dissolved in a little rose water

## Directions

1. Mix 1 1/2 pounds flour, 1/2 pound melted butter, 1/2 pound rubber sugar, 2 eggs and 2 tablespoons baking powder dissolved in a little rose water well
2. Beat and spread out and press off with a cup
3. They are covered with egg yolk, sprinkled with sugar and topped with chopped almonds
