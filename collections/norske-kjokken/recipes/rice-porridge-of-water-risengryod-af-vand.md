## Ingredients

- Water (less than for milk porridge)
- Rice grains
- Butter
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. For this, take a little less water than milk for the previous one, as grains become softer in milk than in water; you can count 2 quarts for the same amount of grains.
2. When it is cooked, stir in a spoonful of butter and while it is hot, add some cinnamon.
