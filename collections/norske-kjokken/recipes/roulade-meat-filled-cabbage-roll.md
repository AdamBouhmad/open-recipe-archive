## Ingredients

- Cabbage leaves
- ground meat
- string
- beef stock or water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Half-boil tender white cabbage leaves, and when they are cold, take ground meat and roll it in them; the thickness should be like a large meatball, and the length 3 inches.
2. They are tied together with string, cooked in beef stock or water, which is salted a little, and browned afterwards on the outside in butter.
3. The string cannot be removed until they are ready to be served.
4. They are served with browned butter.
