## Ingredients

- 1 pound finest chocolate
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 pint cream
- 1 ounce white sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Break the chocolate into small pieces and melt it in 1 tablespoon butter in a bowl over a water bath
2. Stir it into 1 pint of cream
3. Add 1 ounce white sugar and set in a high pot over the fire
4. As soon as it comes to a boil and has cooked for 5 minutes with stirring, place 2 egg yolks in a small cup of cold water and whisk them to a foam
5. Remove the chocolate and pour it little by little into the egg yolks while stirring
6. Set it again over the heat, where it is whisked with an egg whisk until it is about to boil
7. Then remove it, whisk for another 5 minutes and serve.
