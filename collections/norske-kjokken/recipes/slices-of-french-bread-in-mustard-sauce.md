## Ingredients

- Slices of French bread
- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Dip slices of French bread in milk, cover them for a short time, then turn in sugar and cinnamon and fry in butter on both sides.
2. Then cover them with a good fruit jelly and arrange.
