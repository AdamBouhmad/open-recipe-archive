## Ingredients

- Fresh, slender ginger root
- Water
- Sugar (1 pound sugar and 1 cup water per 1 pound ginger)
- Soda solution (cold soda lye)

## Directions

1. Fresh ginger that is firm and smooth is beaten first for an hour, to see if it is dried out; such pieces are removed, but the good ones are weighed and put into cold water for 24 hours.
2. Then a cold soda lye is made and when the lye is warm, so it feels smooth under the fingers, the clear part is strained off, and in it lies the ginger until it is soft to feel; then it is rinsed for 8 days, and the water must be changed every day.
3. Now weigh the sugar: 1 pound sugar and 1 cup water per 1 pound ginger.
4. The sugar and water are cooked clear and skimmed, and then the ginger is cooked in it until it is so tender that it can be pierced with a fine straw.
5. It is stored when cool, but not bound over until it is cold.
