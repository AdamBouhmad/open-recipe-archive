## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 12 tablespoons rum
- 6 small tablespoons melted butter
- Little grated lemon peel
- Ground cardamom
- 1½ pints milk or thin gruel
- Beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Whisk together 8 egg yolks well with 3 tablespoons white sugar
2. Stir in 12 tablespoons rum, 6 small tablespoons melted butter, a little grated lemon peel, ground cardamom, 1½ pints milk or thin gruel and fold in the beaten egg whites
