## Ingredients

- calf, veal, beef, pork, or mutton
- butter
- wheat flour
- salt
- water
- bundled parsley and celery leaves
- whole celery and parsley root
- green peas (optional)
- egg yolks (optional)
- fresh parsley (optional)
- oysters or mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. The meat is cut into pieces the same way as for ragout
2. A knob of butter is melted and wheat flour is stirred in to make a thick sauce, thinned with water until it is a thick consistency
3. The meat is added with salt and thinned with water until it just covers it
4. A bundle of parsley and celery leaves is added but removed before it falls apart
5. Whole celery and parsley root are added and it cooks until the meat is tender, then these are removed and cut into sticks
6. The sauce is poured over the meat, which is kept warm on the fire
7. If green peas are added, no eggs or mushrooms are used, only a little fresh parsley added just before serving
8. For oysters: the beards are removed from the oysters and cooked in a little broth which is strained and added to the fricassée; the oysters should not be cooked with it but only stirred in the sauce after it is thickened with eggs
9. For asparagus: it is scraped, cut into short pieces and cooked tender in the sauce
