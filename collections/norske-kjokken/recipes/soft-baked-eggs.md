## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter
- Bread or buttered toast

## Directions

1. In a buttered mold, which must be suitable for holding them, a few eggs are broken. A little salt is sprinkled on, and a small piece of butter is placed on each yolk. Bake in the oven until the white is set. Serve hot with butter and bread or buttered toast.
