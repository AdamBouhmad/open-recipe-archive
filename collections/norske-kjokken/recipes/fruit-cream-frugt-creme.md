## Ingredients

- 1 pint raw fruit juice, half raspberries
- Sugar
- 1 ounce gelatin
- 1 quart cream
- Beaten whites

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pint raw fruit juice, half raspberries, with sugar and 1 ounce gelatin until the gelatin is dissolved
2. Let it cool and add 1 quart cream that has been beaten to a foam
3. Continue to stir until it is cold
4. Then pour it into a mold that has first been rinsed, and from which it can be turned out again
5. It is eaten without sauce with small, dry cakes
6. It can be made in the same way from salted juice, which must then be warmed
