## Ingredients

- 6 pounds fresh beef
- 1 pound beef fat
- 5 pounds apples
- 2 pounds raisins
- 2 pounds currants
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon pickling spice
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 pounds brown sugar
- 1 pint wine
- The broth in which the beef was cooked

## Directions

1. Cook 6 pounds fresh beef tender in scanty water; when it is cold, chop it fine.
2. Chop 1 pound beef fat fine; grate 5 pounds apples and chop them; stone 2 pounds raisins, 2 pounds currants, 1 pound sugar, 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon, 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg, 1 tablespoon pickling spice, 1 tablespoon salt, 3 pounds brown sugar, 1 pint wine, and finally the broth in which the beef was cooked.
3. Mix all together and put it in a stoneware crock with a double layer of paper on top.
4. It must be left at least a week before it is used, and when the pie is prepared, a little more wine and a little more chopped apple is added, what was set aside before.
5. This sort of pie is used mainly for Christmas and other festivities, and they are therefore made in large batches; they can also keep long and be eaten both warm and cold.
