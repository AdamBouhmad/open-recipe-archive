## Ingredients

- Large yellow onions
- ground meat
- water
- salt
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- cucumber or apple
- bouillon
- stock or soy

## Directions

1. When large yellow onions are peeled, a layer of them is cut, they are hollowed out, filled with ground meat, the layers are placed on top and they are tied together in a piece of thin cloth.
2. They are placed in boiling water and cooked until they are tender, then taken up, and the binding is loosened.
3. After this a brown sauce is made from browned butter, in which onion, pepper and cucumber or apple are cooked, which is mixed with bouillon, and a little stock or soy; the onions are given a quick boil in it or kept warm on a platter and covered with the sauce.
4. If you don't have enough onions, you can cook some carrots in the water at the same time as the onions and place them in the middle of the platter with the onions around them.
