## Ingredients

- pie dough
- zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon corn starch

## Directions

1. Blend the zest and juice of one lemon with 1 cup sugar and the yolks of 3 eggs; stir well.
2. Mix 1 tablespoon corn starch in 2 cups milk; place over heat and bring just to a boil; stir into the other ingredients and pour into the pie pan, which is lined with good dough.
3. When baked, spread the whipped egg whites on top, whisked with sugar.
