## Ingredients

- a cupful of macaroni
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound sugar
- juice and grated peel of half a lemon
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into thumb-length pieces and cook tender in a quart of milk
2. While hot, stir in 2 tablespoons butter, 8 egg yolks beaten with 1/2 pound sugar, juice and grated peel of half a lemon, and lastly the beaten egg whites
3. Pour into a buttered mold about half an hour, or until nicely brown
