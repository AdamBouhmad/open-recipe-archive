## Ingredients

- 1 pint sour milk
- 1 pint sweet milk
- 6 egg yolks
- a little powdered sugar
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip together 1 pint sour milk and 1 pint sweet milk into foam
2. Then add 6 egg yolks, a little powdered sugar, and 6 tablespoons flour
3. Stir in the beaten egg whites when the batter is poured into the oven
