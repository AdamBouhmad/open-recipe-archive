## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup clarified butter
- 1 quart milk or good stock
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. When 1 cup flour is baked in nearly 1 cup clarified butter, add gradually 1 quart milk or good stock
2. Cook with vigorous stirring and whisking with a spoon
3. Let it cool slightly, then stir vigorously with 10 egg yolks and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4. Finally add the 10 beaten egg whites
5. Bake in the oven and serves 8 to 10 people
