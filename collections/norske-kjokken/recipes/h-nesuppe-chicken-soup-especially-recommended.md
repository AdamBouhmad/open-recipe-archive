## Ingredients

- 1 chicken of 2 pounds
- 2 quarts cold water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Kill and pluck the chicken, remove all fat from the interior and clean it carefully, so that you avoid breaking the gall and intestines
2. Cut up the meat and break the bones
3. Place it in a cooking pot with 2 quarts of cold water, bring slowly to a boil and skim until it is clear
4. Cover with a lid and simmer gently until the chicken has become a cohesive mass, namely from 2 to 4 hours
5. Strain it through a fine cloth until it is clear, and set it back over the heat to simmer gently until it has become a complete jelly. This is best recognized by placing a spoonful of the jelly on a saucer; if it, after being cooled, is firm, then it is ready
6. It can be seasoned lightly, depending on what the doctor allows, and kept in cups; serve cold or slightly warmed
