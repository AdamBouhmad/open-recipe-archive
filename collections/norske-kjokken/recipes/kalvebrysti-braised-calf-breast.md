## Ingredients

- 3 pounds calf breast
- vegetables saved from broth
- 1 small onion
- 2 thin slices of meat
- salt
- pepper
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. The breast is laid in a braising pan on a bed of vegetables and a small onion
2. 2 thin slices of meat are laid on top together with a little salt and pepper
3. It is baked in a fairly hot oven for approximately one hour until completely tender
4. It is served with brown sauce, which is made by rubbing the sauce in the braising pan together with the vegetables through a sieve, blended with the boiling water and seasoned to taste
