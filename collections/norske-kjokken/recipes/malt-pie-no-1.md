## Ingredients

- A scant pint fresh malt
- Corn starch
- Egg yolks
- A little vanilla and salt
- Regular dough

## Directions

1. Cook a scant pint of malt; dissolve 1 tablespoon corn starch, 3 egg yolks, a little vanilla and salt.
2. Line the pan with regular dough, the filling is added, and it is put in the oven and baked at good heat.
3. Egg whites are whipped with sugar and placed on top when it is done, after which it is put back in the oven for a moment.
