## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold milk
- 1 tablespoonful vanilla
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 10 beaten egg whites (according to instruction)
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 pint cream
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Place 1 quart cold milk over heat with 1 tablespoonful vanilla and 1 tablespoon sugar
2. Cook 10 beaten egg whites and strain through a sieve
3. Boil the milk and stir it little by little into 10 egg yolks that have been beaten white and mixed with 1 pint cream
4. Place this on heat and stir until it begins to boil, then remove it
5. Add a little salt and stir until it is cold
6. Lay the cooked whites over the cream
