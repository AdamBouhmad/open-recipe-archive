## Ingredients

- One half pound of rice flour
- 2 quarts of milk
- Finely chopped blanched almonds
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir the flour into some of the cold milk, adding it bit by bit so it does not lump.
2. Place the rest on the fire.
3. When it boils, combine the unstirred flour with a bit of the warm milk and pour it into the porridge with strong stirring back and forth.
4. Simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add finely sliced blanched almonds and salt at the end.
6. It is cooked in a good quarter hour and eaten with sugar, butter and cinnamon.
7. This porridge is particularly pleasant to eat cold; then use a little less milk and a few drops of vanilla.
8. Pour it into a mold, from which it is turned out and served with fruit juice over it.
9. As a warm porridge without butter and almonds it is good for children.
