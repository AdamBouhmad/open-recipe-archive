## Ingredients

- beef
- peas
- water
- parsley
- bay leaves
- nutmeg
- cayenne
- carrots

## Directions

1. Make ordinary beef soup from beef
2. Add as many peas as will make the soup suitable
3. The peas should be like yellow varieties, which are somewhat thin
4. When peas are cooked in strong soup, especially if it is aged, it is best to remove some soup first before the meat is completely cooked
5. Mix with water and let the peas cook tender
6. Once the meat is cooked, strain the soup and set it on the fire where butter is melted so that you can press in almost a spoonful of flour, and when this is dissolved, dilute it first with water and then with soup
7. When all the soup has come in and is cooking, add fine parsley on top with the peas and their soup, nutmeg and a little cayenne
8. If you want young carrots, they are cooked whole with the peas
9. The soup can be made thinner with peas and with less seasoning, and add meatballs and carrots
