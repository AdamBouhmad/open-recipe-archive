## Ingredients

- Walnuts
- Salt

## Directions

1. The walnuts must be picked at the right time and be fresh and firm. They are dried in a cloth, peeled and cut fine. In a crock, spread a thin layer of salt, then a layer of walnuts, as thick as three fingers, then a layer of salt, so thick that it covers the walnuts, then walnuts, and so on; the last layer must be salt.
2. It is covered with a piece of cloth, upon which a bundle is laid and upon this a somewhat heavy weight, which after some time has passed, when the walnuts have become firm, can be removed. The walnuts are tied up, placed in a dry place and keep thus until summer.
3. When they are to be used, they are put together with the salt in sufficient boiling water, and cooked until they are soft, then drained through a sieve, the water is pressed out well, and they are rinsed.
