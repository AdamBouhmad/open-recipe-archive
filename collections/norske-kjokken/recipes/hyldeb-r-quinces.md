## Ingredients

- Quinces
- For each pound of quinces: 1 pound sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Place in boiling water and cook until they begin to become soft.
2. Remove, peel, cut according to their size into 4 or 8 pieces, remove the seed cases and all the seedy matter around them, and weigh them.
3. For each pound of quinces take 1 pound sugar, which is set on the fire with 1 pint water.
