## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. The mold is greased with butter; there must be at least 3 centimeters of space in the mold before it is full, so that the pudding can rise during cooking.
2. It requires about an hour of cooking.
3. It is served either with greens and browned butter or sauce, or also white sauce with white vegetables, asparagus tips and oysters if desired.
