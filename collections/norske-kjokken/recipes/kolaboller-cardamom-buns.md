## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold malt
- A little cup sugar
- ½ cup clarified butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 8 cardamom seeds, finely crushed
- A quart tablespoonful of citron oil
- Flour
- 3 tablespoons baking powder
- Beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 quart cold malt, a little cup sugar, ½ cup clarified butter, 4 egg yolks, 8 finely crushed cardamom seeds, a quart tablespoonful of citron oil with as much flour as makes the dough a suitable thickness
2. Stir together well and bake like the previous. Cardamom buns baked in goose or duck fat are excellent
