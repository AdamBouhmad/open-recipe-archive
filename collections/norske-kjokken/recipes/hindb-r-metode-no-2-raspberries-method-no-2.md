## Ingredients

- Good raspberries
- White sugar, 2 pounds per quart of juice
- Thin cloth

## Directions

1. Set good raspberries aside for 24 hours, then strain the juice
2. Measure the juice and pour into a bag or sieve with a thin cloth laid in it, and let sit for another 24 hours
3. Then put it in the pan together with all the dregs and sediment that may have collected on top, plus 2 pounds white sugar for each quart of juice
4. Boil well but only for 5 minutes and skim well
5. The next day pour the juice into bottles and cork
