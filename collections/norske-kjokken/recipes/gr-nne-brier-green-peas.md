## Ingredients

- Peas
- Butter
- Salt
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. If the peas are cooked with steam, when they are tender, place them on a plate and stir with a good spoonful of butter and a little salt.
2. If steam is not used, they can be cooked with sugar and very little water until tender; then stir in a good spoonful of butter and arrange them nicely.
3. They can also be cooked tender in a little more water and topped with a liaison of plenty of butter and a little flour together with parsley, salt and a little sugar.
