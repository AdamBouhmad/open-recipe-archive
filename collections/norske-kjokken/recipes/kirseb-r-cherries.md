## Ingredients

- Morello or red cherries with stems
- Sweet cherries may be used
- Sugar, 1 pound to 1.5 pounds per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Remove the stones, taking care to collect all the juice
2. Weigh the fruit and take 1 pound to 1.5 pounds sugar
3. Layer gently in the pan
4. Pour the juice over and simmer until the juice begins to thicken
