## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- cold butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and dry the fish.
2. Sprinkle a little fine salt and pepper over it, place it skin-side down on a grid and roast it over clear, bright coals.
3. When it is nicely brown, turn it for a short time, then remove it and spread cold butter over it.
4. A little marrow under the fish enhances its flavor.
