## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice grains
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter
- Soft beer or malt

## Directions

1. Pick the rice grains, rinse them in cold water and hold them for two days in boiling water.
2. The most important thing is to let them soak for a short time in boiling milk; as the grains are often hard, the teacher notes that the porridge is poured off and the boiling milk extracts the hardness and gives the ready porridge a whiter appearance.
3. For 1 pound rice grains take almost 2 quarts of milk; roast the grains in the boiling milk and cook without stirring according to instructions until they are tender, usually for a little hour.
4. Add salt and eat with sugar, cinnamon and butter, as well as soft beer or malt with it.
