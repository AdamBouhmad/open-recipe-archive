## Ingredients

- Whipped cold cream
- Fine sugar or a little vanilla
- Or marmalade
- Fruit juice (when cooked with sugar and cooled before adding to the whipped cream)

## Directions

1. Whipped cold cream is sweetened with either fine sugar or a little vanilla
2. It can also be sweetened with marmalade
3. Fruit juice can also be used when it is cooked with the sugar and becomes cold again before it is added to the cream; but there should not be added more than what just gives a fine taste and a light red color
