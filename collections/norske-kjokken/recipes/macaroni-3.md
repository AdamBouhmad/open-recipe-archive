## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. It is broken into fairly long pieces and should be cooked in bouillon, which does not need to be very strong. There should not be more soup on than one can imagine suitable for it to cook into. When you take one of them up with the side of a fork and it bends easily in the middle, then they are done. Thereafter a little cold butter is stirred into them until the butter is melted. They must be arranged immediately and served with a sharp oil and melted butter.
