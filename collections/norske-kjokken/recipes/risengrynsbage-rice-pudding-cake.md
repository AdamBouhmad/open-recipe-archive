## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice grains
- 8 pints milk
- 4 ounces butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 1 pound chopped almonds and a couple of bitter ones
- clean lemon peel
- sugar and salt to taste
- beaten egg whites

## Directions

1. 1 pound rice grains is shelled and cooked to thick porridge in 8 pints milk.
2. Then it is stirred warm with 4 ounces butter, 9 egg yolks, 1 pound chopped almonds and a couple of bitter ones, clean lemon peel, sugar and salt to taste, finally the beaten whites.
3. The dough is poured into the greased and strewn form, sprinkled with sugar and baked in an hour's time.
4. Instead of almonds, vanilla, cinnamon and sugar can be added.
