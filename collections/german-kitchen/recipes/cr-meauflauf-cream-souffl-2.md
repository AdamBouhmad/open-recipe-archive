## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4 egg yolks
- half a Schoppen milk
- 4 Loth finely ground sugar
- half a lemon
- 4 more egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir 4 egg yolks into 4 tablespoons of flour in a brass pan
2. Pour in half a Schoppen of milk and cook this into a thick cream
3. Place this cream in a bowl and add 4 Loth of finely ground sugar and the zest of half a lemon, grated on a grater
4. Stir in 4 more egg yolks, one after another
5. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold slowly into the mass
6. Butter a baking sheet and spread the mixture on it
7. Let it rise beautifully in the oven
8. The soufflé is done in a quarter hour
