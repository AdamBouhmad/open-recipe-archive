## Ingredients

- 1 pound blanched almonds
- 8 egg whites
- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1 quint cardamom
- 4 loth citron
- 1 lemon zest, finely minced
- 1/2 loth ground cinnamon
- 1 pinch cloves
- 1 pound fine flour
- oblaten (wafer paper)

## Directions

1. Cut the blanched almonds as finely as possible and let them turn golden on a baking sheet in the oven
2. Whisk together 8 egg whites and 1 pound finely sifted sugar for a quarter hour
3. Mince 1 quint cardamom, 4 loth citron, and 1 lemon zest very finely
4. Add 1/2 loth ground cinnamon and 1 pinch cloves
5. Mix the almonds and 1 pound fine flour with the above ingredients
6. Make a dough and beat it flat on a pastry board with a knife
7. Form into biscuits 3 fingers wide and 1 finger long
8. Place them on oblaten and bake in the oven
9. For the glaze: cook 1/4 pound sugar and 2 tablespoons water together
10. Add the juice of 1 lemon and let it come to a boil once more
11. Brush the glaze onto the cakes with a pastry brush using the cooked mixture
12. Dry them in the oven
