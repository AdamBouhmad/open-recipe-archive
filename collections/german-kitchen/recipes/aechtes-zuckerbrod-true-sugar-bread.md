## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- zest of 1 lemon
- 3 egg whites
- 10 loth flour

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with the finely sifted sugar
2. Stir vigorously for half an hour
3. Grate the yellow zest from a lemon (the mixture must be as thick as a porridge)
4. Beat 3 egg whites into stiff snow
5. Mix 10 loth flour underneath the snow
6. Fill into a syringe with a finger-thick tube
7. Pipe long stalks, rounded at top and bottom, onto stiff paper on a black baking sheet
8. Pull through finely sifted sugar
9. Bake until yellow
