## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 1 schoppen sour cream
- 3 whole eggs
- 2 cut thymus glands
- 1 large handful morels
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 1 piece of butter
- 1 finely minced onion
- Minced lemon zest
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- Cold water
- 1 ladle spoonful meat broth
- 1/2 glass wine
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Make noodles from 2 eggs
2. Pour salt water over them
3. Draw them through cold water and let drain
4. Stir 1 schoppen sour cream, 3 whole eggs, 2 cut thymus glands, 1 large handful morels, salt and nutmeg together with the noodles
5. Bind everything in a wet cloth
6. Cook the dumpling for one hour in boiling salt water
7. For the butter sauce: let a piece of butter, 1 finely minced onion and minced lemon zest run in a small casserole with 1 cooking spoon flour until golden
8. Rinse it with cold water
9. Pour 1 ladle spoonful of meat broth, 1/2 glass wine and the juice of 1/2 lemon over it
10. Let everything cook together for a quarter hour
11. Place the pudding on a plate and serve the sauce with it
