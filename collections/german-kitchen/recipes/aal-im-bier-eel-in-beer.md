## Ingredients

- 250 g eel
- 3/8 liter white beer
- 1 spice dose
- 8 g salt
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 teaspoons flour
- 4 teaspoons potato flour
- 3 tablespoons water
- 4 onion slices

## Directions

1. Cut the eel into pieces
2. Clean each piece and wash several times
3. Pack it with spices, onion slices and salt in an earthen pot
4. Pour white beer over it and cook covered, depending on thickness, for approximately 20 minutes until soft
5. Skim it often and thicken the sauce with a small piece of butter stirred into flour
6. Arrange the eel pieces in a deep bowl
7. After tasting the sauce, quickly cook it with diluted potato flour
8. Pour it over the fish pieces and serve the remaining sauce on the side
