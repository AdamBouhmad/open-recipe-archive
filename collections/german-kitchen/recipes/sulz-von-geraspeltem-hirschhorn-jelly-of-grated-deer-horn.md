## Ingredients

- 4 loth deer horn
- 4 loth ivory
- handful of chest herbs
- watercress and chervil
- 1 1/2 maas water
- 3 loth raspberry juice
- 1 loth red cornflower juice

## Directions

1. Cut the deer horn, ivory, chest herbs, watercress, and chervil into small pieces together
2. Pour 1 1/2 maas water over them
3. Reduce by cooking until 1/2 maas of liquid remains
4. Strain through a hair sieve
5. Add 3 loth raspberry juice and 1 loth red cornflower juice
6. Give to children with fever and teething pain, one spoonful morning and evening; can also be given in tea
