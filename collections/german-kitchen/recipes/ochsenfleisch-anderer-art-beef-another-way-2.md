## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- Bacon and ham for larding
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Basil
- Celery herb
- 1 whole lemon, cut into leaves
- Bay leaves
- Provençal oil
- Water

## Directions

1. Lard a piece of beef with bacon and ham
2. Place half of the following herbs in a casserole: parsley, thyme, basil, celery herb, 1 whole lemon cut into leaves, bay leaves
3. Place the meat on top
4. Cover with the other half of the herbs
5. Pour some Provençal oil over the meat
6. Let it lie in the herbs overnight
7. The next day, pour water over it
8. Let the meat cook tender on coals
9. Arrange it
10. Pass the jus through a fine sieve over it
11. Garnish with parsley
