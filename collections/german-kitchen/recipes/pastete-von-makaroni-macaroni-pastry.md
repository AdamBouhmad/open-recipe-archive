## Ingredients

- half a pound macaroni
- butter
- 1 pound finely chopped lean ham
- 1 pint sour cream
- 3 eggs
- quarter pound grated Parmesan cheese
- butter pastry
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of macaroni with a piece of butter until very soft
2. Drain
3. Mix together 1 pound finely chopped lean ham, 1 pint sour cream, 3 eggs and quarter pound grated Parmesan cheese
4. Stir everything together with the macaroni
5. Line a mold with butter pastry
6. Fill with the mixture, make a lid
7. Brush with egg yolk, bake well in the oven and turn the pastry onto a plate
