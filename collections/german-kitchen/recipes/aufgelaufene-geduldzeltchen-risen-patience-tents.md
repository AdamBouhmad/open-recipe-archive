## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1/4 pound finest flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir sugar and egg together in a bowl for half an hour
2. Mix in flour and grated lemon zest
3. Place small heaps on a black baking sheet coated with wax or lard
4. Bake in the oven
