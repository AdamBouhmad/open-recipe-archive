## Ingredients

- 125 g rice
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 l water for blanching
- 40 g plums
- 3/8 l water
- 10 g sugar
- 5 g margarine

## Directions

1. Prepare the plums according to instructions.
2. Cook the plums slowly with 3/8 l water and 10 g sugar until soft and pour over the rice, which is prepared as water rice.
