## Ingredients

- 1 boiled lobster or sea spider
- several cleaned, boned anchovies
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls good Provence oil
- 3 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
- 2 spoonfuls good mustard
- 2 finely chopped shallot onions
- pepper
- salt
- chives, chopped

## Directions

1. After the lobster or sea spider is boiled, split it lengthwise
2. Place it in the serving dish
3. Make the sauce as follows: pound several cleaned, boned anchovies with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar into a paste, stir in 4 spoonfuls good Provence oil, 3 tablespoons tarragon vinegar, 2 spoonfuls good mustard, 2 finely chopped shallot onions, pepper, salt, and chopped chives
4. Pour this sauce over the lobster or serve it separately on the table
