## Ingredients

- one roasted chicken or veal roast
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- boiled pears
- steamed apples
- plums
- cherries
- sour cherries
- herring
- anchovies
- endive
- capers
- head lettuce
- 2 spoonfuls of tarragon vinegar
- 3 spoonfuls of Provençal oil
- salt
- pepper
- yolk of 6 hard-boiled eggs
- red beet broth
- muscat nutmeg
- one beautiful plum or preserved nut
- preserved cherries

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted chicken or veal roast into small pieces.
2. Clean and cut the herring and anchovies.
3. Combine in a bowl the roasted meat, hard-boiled eggs, boiled pears, steamed apples, plums, cherries, sour cherries, herring, anchovies, endive, capers, and head lettuce.
4. Stir together 2 spoonfuls of tarragon vinegar, 3 spoonfuls of Provençal oil, salt and pepper.
5. Take a not very deep salad bowl, arrange the salad beautifully like a pyramid.
6. Press the yolk of 6 hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve, cut the whites into desired pieces, lay them in red beet broth.
7. Stir everything together for a quarter hour, pour over the stirred mixture one tablespoon of vinegar, 3 drops of oil, a knife's point of salt, some muscat nutmeg.
8. Cover the salad completely with this and place on top a beautiful plum or preserved nut, around it the eggs which have lain in the red beet broth and preserved cherries.
