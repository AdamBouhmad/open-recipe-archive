## Ingredients

- 1 Schoppen milk
- 6 eggs
- 6 spoonfuls flour
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. To 1 Schoppen milk take 6 eggs, 1 spoonful flour for each egg.
2. Stir smooth with the milk.
3. Add sugar and cinnamon as desired.
4. Stir everything well together.
5. Coat an earthenware tile with butter.
6. Fill the batter into it.
7. Place the tile in a baking oven or on coals.
