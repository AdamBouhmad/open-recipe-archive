## Ingredients

- cabbage or savoy cabbage
- salt water
- onions
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage, cut off the ribs
2. Cook it soft in salted water
3. Strain it in a colander so the water runs off
4. When on the plate, finish it with onions and parsley which have been braised with a piece of butter
