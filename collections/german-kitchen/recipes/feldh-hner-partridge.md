## Ingredients

- partridges
- vinegar with assorted roots
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- cloves
- bacon
- lemons
- yellow turnips
- bay leaves
- liver and heart

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges and cut off the head (save it) and claws.
2. Place the birds in vinegar with assorted roots.
3. Rub with salt and pepper.
4. Stud with onions stuck with cloves and wrap in bacon and lemons.
5. Cover with a piece of paper.
6. Braise in a casserole with onions, yellow turnips, lemons, and bay leaves.
7. When serving, reattach the head (bacon and lemons may remain on the breast).
8. Remove fat from the sauce.
9. Mince the liver and heart with a little lemon but do not cook them so they do not become tough.
10. Pour the sauce onto the platter and lay the partridges on top.
