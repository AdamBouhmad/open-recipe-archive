## Ingredients

- 1/2 schoppen Rhine wine
- 1/2 schoppen black cherry water
- 3 finger-widths flour
- a little water
- 3 egg yolks
- a little pounded cinnamon
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir 3 finger-widths of flour smooth with a little water.
2. In a brass pan, cook together 1/2 schoppen Rhine wine, 1/2 schoppen black cherry water, the flour mixture, 3 egg yolks, a little pounded cinnamon, and 1/4 pound sugar rubbed with lemon zest for a short time.
3. Press through a hair sieve into any desired bowl.
4. Serve cold or warm.
