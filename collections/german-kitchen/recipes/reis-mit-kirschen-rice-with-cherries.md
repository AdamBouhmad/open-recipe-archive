## Ingredients

- 250 g sour cherries or 40 g dried cherries
- 20 g sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- 5 g salt
- 125 g rice
- 1/2 l water for blanching
- 5 g margarine
- 1 piece orange peel

## Directions

1. Wash the sour cherries, remove stems and pit them if desired. Prepare dried cherries according to instructions.
2. Stew the cherries with sugar, orange peel and 3 tablespoons water and add to the rice, which is prepared as water rice.
