## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound sifted sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 2 loth citron and orange peel
- 2 knife points cinnamon
- 1 knife point cloves
- 1/2 pound fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Stir in egg yolks, then sifted sugar, and beat together for a quarter hour
3. Add lemon zest, citron and orange peel, cinnamon, cloves, and fine flour
4. Form into balls the size of hazelnuts
5. Grease a dark baking sheet with lard
6. Bake the balls until golden in the oven
