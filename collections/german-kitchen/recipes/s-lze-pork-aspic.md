## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg pork shoulder
- 1 pork ear
- spice mixture
- 40 g onions
- 20 g salt
- 1-1/2 l water
- 1/8 l vinegar
- 65 g cucumber cubes

## Directions

1. After pork shoulder and pork ear are thoroughly washed, place them with spice mixture, onion slices, salt and 1-1/2 l water in a covered pot.
2. Skim thoroughly and let the pork shoulder simmer slowly for 1 hour and the pork ear for 2-1/2 hours until both are very soft.
3. Cut the meat into small cubes, pour the broth through a sieve, add vinegar and taste; after adding all ingredients to the broth, adjust seasoning once more.
4. Rinse the molds; fill with the mixture and after setting, turn out. You can line the bottom of the molds with cucumber, capers and lemon and let a few spoonfuls of broth set on top, then fill the aspic on top.
5. Serve with salad, vinegar and oil, oil sauce, and also fried potatoes.
