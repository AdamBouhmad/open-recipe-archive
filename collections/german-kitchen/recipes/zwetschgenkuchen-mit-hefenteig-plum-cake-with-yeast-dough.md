## Ingredients

- yeast dough (as for steam noodles)
- plums, pitted
- 3 eggs
- half a schoppen of sour cream
- one handful of ground sugar
- one handful of grated bread

## Directions

1. Make yeast dough as for steam noodles
2. Roll it out half a finger thickness
3. Place in a baking sheet
4. Arrange the pitted plums thickly on the dough
5. Stir together 3 eggs, half a schoppen of sour cream, one handful of ground sugar and one handful of grated bread
6. Pour this mixture over the plums
7. Bake the cake in the oven
