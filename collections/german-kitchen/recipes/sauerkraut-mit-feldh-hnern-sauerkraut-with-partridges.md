## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Pork fat
- Onions
- 1 glass wine
- Partridges
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemons
- Cloves
- Mace
- Bay leaves
- Yellow turnips
- Celery
- Lemon cabbage
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Cook the sauerkraut soft in a casserole with pork fat, onions and a glass of wine.
2. Dress the partridges, wrap them in bacon, add salt, pepper, lemons, cloves, mace, bay leaves, onions, yellow turnips, celery and lemon cabbage, pour a little meat broth over and braise covered in a casserole for an hour.
3. Remove the lid from the casserole, let the hens brown beautifully yellow, cut off the breast, arrange the sauerkraut, place the breasts nicely on top and pour the fat in which they were fried over the sauerkraut.
