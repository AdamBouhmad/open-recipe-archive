## Ingredients

- eel
- butter
- thick sour cream
- capers
- lemon

## Directions

1. Skin the eel and clean it out
2. Keep it whole
3. Fry it in the oven in a frying pan with a piece of butter
4. Pour thick sour cream over it
5. Add a tablespoon of capers and half a lemon
6. Finish frying the eel so that it develops a sauce from the cream and capers
