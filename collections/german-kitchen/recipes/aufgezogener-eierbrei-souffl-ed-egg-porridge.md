## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 8 egg yolks
- sugar
- lemons
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter, 8 egg yolks, sugar and lemons together
2. Beat the whites of the eggs to snow and add them, place in a suitable dish, add heat above and below and soufflé
