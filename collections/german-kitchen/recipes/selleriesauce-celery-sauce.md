## Ingredients

- 2 clean, trimmed celery roots
- a piece of butter
- a small tablespoonful of flour
- a little cold water
- one ladle of meat broth
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the celery roots as finely as noodles
2. Braise them gently in a casserole with a piece of butter, covered
3. Sprinkle a small tablespoonful of flour over them and shake
4. Pour a little cold water over them
5. Add one ladle of meat broth and some vinegar
6. Season with pepper and salt to taste
