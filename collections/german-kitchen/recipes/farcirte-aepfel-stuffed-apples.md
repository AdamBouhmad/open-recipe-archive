## Ingredients

- Borsdorf apples
- 1 schoppen wine
- 1 schoppen water
- 10 loth sugar
- yolks of 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 loth candied citron
- 1 loth blanched almonds
- 1 loth sugar
- 2 spoonfuls raspberry or currant juice

## Directions

1. Peel and halve the apples, remove the cores, and hollow them out slightly without making holes
2. Cook the apples in 1 schoppen wine, 1 schoppen water, and 10 loth sugar until soft
3. Drain on a hair sieve
4. Press the yolks of 4 hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve
5. Finely weigh 1 loth candied citron and 1 loth blanched almonds
6. Mix them with the eggs and 1 loth sugar
7. Place the apples on a plate with the openings facing up
8. Fill half of each apple with the egg mixture and cover with the other half
9. Cook 2 spoonfuls raspberry or currant juice with the syrup until it bubbles
10. Pour the syrup over the apples to give them a nice glaze
