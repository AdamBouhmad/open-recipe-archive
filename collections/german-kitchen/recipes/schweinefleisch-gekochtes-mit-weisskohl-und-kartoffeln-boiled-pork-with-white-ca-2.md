## Ingredients

- 200 g pork shoulder
- 8 g salt
- 3/4 l water
- 3/4 kg white cabbage
- water and 1 pinch salt
- 250 g peeled potatoes
- water and 1 pinch salt
- 1 spice mixture portion
- 10 g fat
- 10 g flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and pound the meat. Place 3/4 l water with 8 g salt on the stove to heat. Remove the outer leaves and thick leaf ribs from the cabbage after dividing the head into 8 or 16 pieces, then blanch in salted water and drain; boil the potatoes whole or cut into pieces until almost cooked.
2. Place the meat in the boiling salted water; after foam forms and simmers for a few minutes, skim it off, add the spice mixture, then the white cabbage and braise the dish approximately 1 hour covered. Now add the potatoes, cook the whole thing slowly for another 1/4 hour, make a roux with fat and flour, combine it with the dish, and season with pepper and salt.
