## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour
- sour cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 knife-point of salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 6 loth blanched and finely pounded almonds
- 6 to 8 bitter almonds
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound flour smooth in a bowl with sour cream.
2. Add 6 egg yolks, one after another, 1 knife-point of salt, 1 tablespoon sugar, and 6 loth blanched and finely pounded almonds, among which 6 to 8 bitter almonds must be included.
3. Stir everything together well.
4. Beat the whites of 6 eggs to a stiff snow and slowly mix under the batter.
5. Bake in a waffle iron on both sides.
