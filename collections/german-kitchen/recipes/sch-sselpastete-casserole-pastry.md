## Ingredients

- Young chickens
- Pigeons
- Birds
- Sweetbreads
- Veal
- Bratwurst
- Meat dumplings
- Butter
- 1 shallot onion
- Parsley
- Capers
- Morels
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- Pastry dough
- Flour, 3 fingers' worth
- Meat broth, 1/2 ladle full
- Lemon, half

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens and pigeons into pieces
2. Braise them in butter
3. Add 1 shallot onion and parsley, finely cut
4. Let them braise together
5. Place them in a serving dish
6. Lay the braised meat in it
7. Add capers, morels, mushrooms, and truffles
8. Cover with pastry dough
9. Place in an oven and bake the pastry
10. For the sauce: sprinkle 3 fingers' worth of flour into the casserole where the chickens were steamed
11. Stir it around
12. Pour 1/2 ladle full of meat broth to it
13. Let it boil up
14. Strain the broth through a fine sieve
15. Pour it over the pastry
16. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon over it
