## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt water
- Cold water
- Onion
- Flour
- Butter
- Meat broth
- Optional: Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and sort the spinach, wash it, cook it soft with salted water, place it in cold water for an hour, press it out firmly.
2. Mince finely with an onion, toast 2 spoonfuls of flour in a piece of butter until light yellow, braise the spinach in it, pour a spoonful of meat broth over and cook. Nutmeg can also be grated over it.
