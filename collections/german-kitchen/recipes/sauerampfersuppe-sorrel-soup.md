## Ingredients

- One handful of clean washed sorrel
- Two shallot onions
- A piece of butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Meat broth or jus
- Two egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of sweet cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Finely chop the sorrel and shallot onions together
2. Place a piece of butter in a casserole and braise everything together
3. Stir in one tablespoon of flour, pour in meat broth or jus
4. Let it come to a boil once more
5. Arrange over soup dumplings (described below)
6. In a small dish, stir two egg yolks with two tablespoons of sweet cream and add to the soup
7. Add salt and nutmeg
