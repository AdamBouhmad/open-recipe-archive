## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Salt water
- Flour
- 1 schoppen wheat beer or wine
- Lard
- Butter sauce or spinach

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and cut the salsify into finger-length pieces, and cook soft in salted water.
2. Make the following batter: stir 2 handfuls of flour smooth with 1 schoppen of wheat beer or wine, dip the salsify in this batter, fry them in a pan with hot lard until beautiful yellow, and either make a butter sauce to go with them or serve them dry with spinach.
