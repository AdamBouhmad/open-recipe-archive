## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- cleaned herrings
- 1 handful of onions
- parsley
- 1 lemon (for juice and zest)
- butter
- beef broth
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Roll out the puff pastry the thickness of a knife back
2. Line small pastry molds with it (24 pieces for 12 tarts)
3. Brush 12 pieces with water and place the others upside down on top to form lids
4. Puncture lightly with a hot knife, brush with egg yolk and bake until golden
5. Chop the cleaned herrings not too finely
6. Chop together a handful of onions and parsley along with the juice and zest of a lemon
7. Steam the mixture with butter the size of a hen's egg in a casserole for some time
8. Add the herrings, turn several times and pour in a couple of tablespoons of beef broth
9. Fill each pastry with a teaspoon of the mixture and cover with the lid
10. Serve on a plate after the soup course
