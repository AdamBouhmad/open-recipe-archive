## Ingredients

- 30 g chocolate per cup
- 1/4 liter milk per cup

## Directions

1. Break the chocolate and warm it with a few spoonfuls of water in an earthenware pot until soft
2. Then boil it with the milk
3. Alternatively, milk can be omitted entirely and water used instead (Water Chocolate)
