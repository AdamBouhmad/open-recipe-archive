## Ingredients

- Snails
- Butter
- Parsley
- Water (half a Maas)
- Chives
- 6 Eggs
- 3 spoonfuls of sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil the snails as described in recipe 419
2. Put them in a casserole and cook them for half an hour with a piece of butter, some parsley, and water
3. Chop a handful of parsley and chives if available, and braise the chopped herbs in a piece of butter
4. Take 6 eggs and 3 spoonfuls of sour cream and add them
5. Let the snails drain well, stir them together with the braised herbs over the fire
6. When the mixture is thick, serve it
