## Ingredients

- rose apples
- butter
- 4 to 6 apricots
- 4 loth sugar
- 2 knife points mace
- 6 whole eggs
- 1/2 schoppen sour cream
- 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel rose apples and cut them into small pieces, braise them soft in a small piece of butter, place them in a bowl
2. Pit 4 to 6 apricots, add 4 loth sugar, 2 knife points mace, 6 whole eggs and 1/2 schoppen sour cream
3. Grate 1/2 lemon on a grater, mix everything well together, butter a cake pan, fill the mass into it and bake the cake on charcoal fire until well done
4. Make a lemon ice on top as previously described
