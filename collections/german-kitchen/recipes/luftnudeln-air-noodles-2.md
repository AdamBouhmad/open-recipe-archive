## Ingredients

- 8 loth butter
- 8 loth water
- 12 loth sugar
- 9 loth fine flour
- 9 kreuzer ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook butter, water, and sugar together in a brass pan
2. Stir in flour and ground vanilla
