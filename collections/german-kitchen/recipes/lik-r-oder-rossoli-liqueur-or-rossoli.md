## Ingredients

- One Maas strong fruit brandy or first runoff
- One Schoppen rose water
- 12 Loth sugar
- 4 spoonfuls sweet milk
- One Loth cinnamon
- Half a Loth cloves
- Half a Loth anise oil

## Directions

1. Stir the anise oil with pounded sugar until no oil is visible
2. Pour the brandy to it
3. Mix in the remaining spices
4. Bind the mixture tightly in a wide glass
5. Leave it in the sun for 2 to 3 days
6. On the third day, strain through a felt hat
7. Pour into bottles
8. Cork the bottles and store
