## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 2 spoonfuls of good thick beer yeast
- 1/2 schoppen of lukewarm sweet cream
- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 loth of sugar
- 1 knife-tip of salt
- 1/2 quarter-pound of butter
- milk
- 1/2 glass of milk

## Directions

1. With 1 pound fine flour, 2 spoonfuls of good thick beer yeast, and 1/2 schoppen of lukewarm sweet cream make a dough and let it rise well
2. Cut in 1/4 pound butter and add 4 egg yolks; if the dough is still too stiff, add a little more sweet cream
3. Beat it for half an hour, add 1 loth of sugar and a knife-tip of salt, let it rise again
4. When half-risen, scoop out little cakes with a spoon the size of a walnut, place them on a floured sheet and let them rise completely
5. Melt 1/2 quarter-pound of butter in a frying pan, pour in milk to nail-height, place the noodles in it, cover the dish
6. Place hot ashes on the lid and coals under the dish, let the noodles boil up, pour in 1/2 glass of milk and place them on a platter with the help of a baking shovel
7. Can be served with compote or lemon sauce
