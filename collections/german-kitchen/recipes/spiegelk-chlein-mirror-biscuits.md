## Ingredients

- oblaten (wafer paper)
- jam or preserves
- 1/2 schoppen milk
- 1/2 loth butter
- 4 cooking spoonfuls flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 pinch salt
- lard for frying
- sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut oblaten into pieces 2 fingers wide and 2 fingers long
2. Spread some jam on one piece and cover with another piece to form a sandwich
3. Heat 1/2 schoppen milk and 1/2 loth butter until boiling
4. Mix 4 cooking spoonfuls flour with the milk until smooth
5. Add 3 eggs and 1 pinch salt
6. Dip the wafer sandwiches into the batter
7. Fry in hot lard
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
