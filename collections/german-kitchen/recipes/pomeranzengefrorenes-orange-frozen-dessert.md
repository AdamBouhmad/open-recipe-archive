## Ingredients

- 3 oranges
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 2 to 3 tablespoons of water
- 1 schoppen of nutmeg wine

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 3 oranges into the sugar
2. Cook 1/2 pound sugar in a brass pan with 2 to 3 tablespoons of water until it forms threads
3. Press the juice of 3 oranges and 1 schoppen of nutmeg wine through a sieve into the pan with the sugar
4. Let it come to a boil once more
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Let it become completely cold
7. Put it in the freezing tin
8. Work it according to the method given in No. 767
