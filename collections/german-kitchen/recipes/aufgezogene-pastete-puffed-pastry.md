## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Calf brain
- Chicken breasts
- Calf udders
- Beef sweetbreads
- fine white veal
- Crab udders
- 3 shallots
- zest of half a lemon
- flour
- cold water
- meat broth
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/8 pound crab butter
- 4 eggs
- half a quart sweet cream
- 1 quart sour cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 6 spoonfuls flour
- salt
- half a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a mélange ragout: this consists of veal sweetbreads, brain, chicken breasts, calf udders, beef sweetbreads, fine white veal, and crab udders
2. Keep all of these, except the crab udders, in boiling water in a large colander as long as hard eggs
3. Remove and place in cold water so it stays white
4. Weigh 3 shallots and the zest of half a lemon, braise these in a casserole, cover for a quarter hour
5. Let the meat drain and braise it in the casserole for half an hour
6. Sprinkle a small cooking spoon of flour over it, add a little cold water, turn it over, pour a soup ladle of good meat broth and the juice of 1 lemon and let it cook together for a quarter hour
7. In a small casserole, take 1/8 pound crab butter, add 4 eggs, pour in half a quart sweet cream, stir it over weak coal fire until it begins to thicken, let it rest in a colander
8. Stir 1 quart sour cream in a bowl thick, add 6 egg yolks, make stiff snow from the 6 egg whites, slowly mix 6 spoonfuls flour, salt, and half a nutmeg under the mass
9. Put the ragout without sauce in a bowl
10. Cut the crab butter as desired and place it on top of the ragout
11. Pour half of the stirred mass over it and let it puff up nicely in a baking oven
12. When nearly done, pour the other half over it and let it finish puffing in the oven
13. When ready to serve, place the puffed pastry on a plate and serve the sauce separately
