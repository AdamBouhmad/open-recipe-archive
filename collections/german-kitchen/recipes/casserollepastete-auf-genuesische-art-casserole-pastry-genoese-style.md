## Ingredients

- noodle dough made with egg yolks
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Instead of macaroni, use noodle dough made with egg yolks
2. Cut long ribbons from it, like macaroni, as wide as a small finger
3. Take sour cream and prepare the pastry in the same way as macaroni pastries
