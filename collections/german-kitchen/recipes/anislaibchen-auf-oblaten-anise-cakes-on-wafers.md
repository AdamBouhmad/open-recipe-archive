## Ingredients

- Half pound sugar
- 7 egg whites
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 6 lots fine flour
- 2 lots clean washed anise

## Directions

1. Stir half pound sugar, 7 egg whites and lemon juice into white icing
2. Add 6 lots fine flour and 2 lots clean washed anise
3. Test a small amount of the mixture in the oven to see if it no longer runs
4. Set small cakes on white wafers placed on a dark baking sheet using a coffee spoon
5. Bake slowly in the oven so they remain a nice white color and do not turn yellow
