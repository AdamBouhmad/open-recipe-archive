## Ingredients

- 1 pork leg from a young pig, 4 kg
- 10 cloves
- 60 g grated bread
- 25 g sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 10 g salt
- 30 g bread rind
- 1 spice mixture portion
- 1 small onion
- 1 pinch salt
- 1-1/2 l water

## Directions

1. From a pounded pork leg, cut off the skin together with a large part of the fat and rub the leg with salt.
2. Place the leg in the pan, gradually pour 1/2 l boiling water over it, add bread rind, spice mixture and onion and let the leg roast gently for 3 hours under frequent basting, replenishing the sauce from time to time with water and carefully skimming off the fat. 3/4 hours before serving, thickly coat the leg with a mixture of bread, sugar, cinnamon, crushed cloves and a pinch of salt, press the mixture in everywhere with a knife, drizzle it with fat and let the crust brown and harden in the oven, drizzling it with fat from time to time. Then arrange the roast and cook the sauce by scraping the pan; strain the sauce through a sieve.
