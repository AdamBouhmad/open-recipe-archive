## Ingredients

- 180 g white cheese
- 30 g flour
- 1 egg
- 30 g currants
- 1 pinch salt
- 20 g sugar
- 30 g fat

## Directions

1. Grate the cheese through a sieve and sift the flour. Prepare the currants.
2. Mix all ingredients except the fat together thoroughly. Form small cakes from the dough and fry them in the hot fat in a pan.
