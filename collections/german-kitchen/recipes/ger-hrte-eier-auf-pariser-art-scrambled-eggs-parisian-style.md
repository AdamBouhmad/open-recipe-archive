## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- good jus
- salt
- pepper
- ham
- parsley

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs into a bowl (for 6 persons use 8 eggs), salt them as desired.
2. Let half a pound of butter melt in a casserole, stir the eggs into it slowly.
3. While cooking, stir in 2 spoonfuls of good jus.
4. When the eggs are done, place them on the plate for serving, make a wreath of them, and beat the yolks of 6 eggs onto the center of the plate.
5. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper over it and place it on boiling water so the eggs set.
6. Meanwhile, cut a piece of ham into small pieces, lay these on the egg wreath and place some beautiful parsley between them.
