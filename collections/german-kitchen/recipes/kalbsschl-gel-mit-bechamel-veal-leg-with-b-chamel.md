## Ingredients

- A nice white veal quarter
- Béchamel sauce
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Salt
- Glaze or consommé

## Directions

1. Treat a nice white veal quarter as in the previous recipe, roasting it 2 hours before serving.
2. Make a béchamel and place it in a casserole.
3. Before serving, remove the roast from the spit and place it on the dish in which it is to be served so the jus remains in it.
4. Cut a large round hole in the top of the leg, but not all the way through.
5. From the cut-out hole, remove the brown rind with small-finger-thick flesh so it fits exactly over the made opening.
6. Keep the béchamel warm over coals, cut out the white flesh from the hole into filets, removing the veins, and add them to the béchamel.
7. Add a little nutmeg, pepper, salt and a little glaze or consommé and stir this in a quick oven so it heats rapidly but does not boil.
8. Fill this into the hole made in the leg and cover it with the cut-off brown rind.
9. Then glaze the leg so that the made opening is visible.
10. This entire preparation must be done very quickly.
