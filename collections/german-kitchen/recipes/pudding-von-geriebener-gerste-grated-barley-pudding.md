## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- flour
- 1 pint milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 2 loth blanched pounded almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter
- sugar
- 1/4 pound cherries
- 1 pint water
- 1 pint wine
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- cinnamon
- cloves
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough of 2 egg yolks and as much flour as these absorb, chop it very finely, cook it in 1 pint of milk and let it cool.
2. Beat together 1/4 pound butter, 4 loth sugar with lemon zest grated on it, 2 loth blanched pounded almonds, 6 egg yolks and the snow beaten from 6 egg whites.
3. Fill the mass into a pudding mold brushed with butter and sprinkled with sugar.
4. Cook the pudding one hour in boiling water.
5. For the sauce: pound 1/4 pound cherries together with the pits thoroughly, cook them half an hour in 1 pint of water, press through a sieve, add 1 pint of wine, along with 1 cooking spoon of flour, cinnamon and cloves, stir everything smooth and let it boil with a piece of sugar.
6. Pour the sauce over the arranged pudding.
