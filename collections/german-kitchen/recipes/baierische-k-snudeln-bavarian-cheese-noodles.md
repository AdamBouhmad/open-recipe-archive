## Ingredients

- flour
- 2 spoonfuls of beer yeast
- 1/2 schoppen of lukewarm milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3/4 pound cheese
- 3 egg yolks
- a large handful of chopped chives
- salt
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Put flour in a bowl and make a sponge with 2 spoonfuls of beer yeast and 1/2 schoppen of lukewarm milk
2. Let 1/4 pound butter melt in a dish and stir it lightly with 3/4 pound cheese
3. Add 3 egg yolks and work everything together with the aforementioned dough and flour
4. Add a large handful of chopped chives and salt
5. Set the dough in warmth to rise
6. Make small noodles with a spoon and bake them in hot lard
