## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 liter water
- Mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare soft-boiled eggs
2. Immediately shock the eggs with lukewarm water
3. Gently loosen and remove the shells by tapping on the table
4. Cut the eggs lengthwise on a plate with a sharp knife, or leave them whole
5. Pour cooled or warm mustard sauce over the eggs
