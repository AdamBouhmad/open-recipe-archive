## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt water
- twenty-five crayfish
- butter
- flour
- water or meat broth
- eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower as in the previous recipe
2. Cook twenty-five crayfish, remove the tails and pound the rest in a mortar very fine with butter
3. Braise it in a casserole with a piece of butter until it throws up foam, then braise it with water or meat broth
4. Remove the crayfish butter, roast it in a casserole with two spoonfuls of flour
5. Extinguish it with cold water and let it cook up with meat broth
6. Beat two eggs in a bowl, pour some of the crayfish broth over them and pour everything over the cauliflower
