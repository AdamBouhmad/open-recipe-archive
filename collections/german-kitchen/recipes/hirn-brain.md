## Ingredients

- Brain
- Butter
- Onions
- Lemon
- Flour
- Jus
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Place the necessary amount of brain in lukewarm water and remove the membrane.
2. Make a jus: melt a piece of butter in a casserole.
3. Add chopped onions, lemon and lemon pulp and braise everything for a time.
4. Sprinkle flour, as much as you can hold between 3 fingers, and stir a few times.
5. Pour in a spoonful of jus.
6. Let it cook for a quarter hour.
7. Squeeze juice from half a lemon into it.
8. Strain through a fine sieve and pour back into the casserole.
9. Season with pepper and salt to taste.
10. Now add the brain to the sauce and let it cook in the sauce for a quarter hour.
