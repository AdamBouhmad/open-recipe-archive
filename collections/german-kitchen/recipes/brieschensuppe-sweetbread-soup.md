## Ingredients

- Two sweetbreads
- Onions
- Parsley
- A piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
- Two tablespoons of flour
- Good bouillon
- Meat broth
- Two egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of sour cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take two sweetbreads, blanch slightly, chop on a chopping block
2. Also chop onions and parsley, the latter not very finely
3. Place a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg in a casserole and add the chopped ingredients
4. Braise together for half an hour
5. Stir in two tablespoons of flour, pour in good bouillon
6. Stir everything until smooth with no lumps
7. Pour in as much meat broth as needed for the soup
8. Beat two egg yolks into the soup bowl with two tablespoons of sour cream
9. Arrange the soup over it, add salt and nutmeg
