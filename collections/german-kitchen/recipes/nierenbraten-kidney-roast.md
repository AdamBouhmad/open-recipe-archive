## Ingredients

- One kidney roast
- Cloth
- Pepper
- Salt
- String
- Paper
- Onions
- Yellow turnips
- Lemon
- Fat
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. From a kidney roast, remove the lobe from the front and the streak from the back.
2. Rub clean with a cloth.
3. Season well with pepper and salt.
4. Let sit for one hour.
5. Roll it together and bind tightly with string.
6. Roast on a spit or in a roasting oven.
7. Wrap in paper.
8. Add cut onions, yellow turnips, and some lemon.
9. Add considerable fat and roast slowly.
10. When serving, remove the paper, discard the fat, and strain the sauce through a hair sieve with some meat broth.
