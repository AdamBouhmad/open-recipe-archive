## Ingredients

- 1/2 l cold whole milk
- 50 g starch flour
- 50 g sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 piece orange peel

## Directions

1. Fill a mold or some teacups with water and immediately empty them just before pouring in the flammeri.
2. In an earthenware pot, whisk the starch flour with milk, sugar, orange peel, and salt, bring to a boil on the stove while stirring, cook for 3 minutes, and after removing the peel, pour into the rinsed molds, let cool, turn out, and serve with fruit sauce.
