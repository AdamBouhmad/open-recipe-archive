## Ingredients

- 4 rolls
- milk
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- kidney fat
- salt
- nutmeg
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the crust from 4 rolls
2. Cut thin slices from the rolls
3. Cook them in milk to a thick porridge
4. Let cool
5. Cook the bread with a piece of butter, steamed onions, parsley, and some kidney fat in a casserole
6. Season with salt
7. Grate nutmeg on it
8. Add 3 eggs
9. Make long sausages from the cooked mixture
10. Dip them in eggs
11. Roast them in butter
