## Ingredients

- one quarter pound sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Pound one quarter pound sugar very finely
2. Pass it through a hair sieve
3. Beat one egg white into it and add the juice of one lemon
4. Stir for a quarter hour until it is completely white and use as desired
