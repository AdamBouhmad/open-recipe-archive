## Ingredients

- 175 g beans, lentils or peas
- 1 pinch soda
- 50 g lean bacon or pork rinds
- 2 l water
- 8 g salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 small onion
- 1/4 teaspoon marjoram

## Directions

1. Soak beans, lentils or peas the evening before according to instructions; scrape and wash the rinds or cut the bacon into small cubes; peel the onion.
2. Place the legumes in 1 l cold water with soda, heat and drain, and cook gently with 1 l water, onion cubes, rinds or bacon cubes, salt and marjoram; crush slightly with a ladle or press through a sieve as desired, then reheat, season with pepper, salt and marjoram and serve.
3. Alternative: the legumes can be cooked with 65 g salted meat instead of rinds or bacon.
