## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- salt water
- eggs
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and simmer it in salted water until tender
2. Cut into pieces of desired size
3. Dip in beaten egg, then in flour mixed with breadcrumbs
4. Fry in hot lard until golden yellow
