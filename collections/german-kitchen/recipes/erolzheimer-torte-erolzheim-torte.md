## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 1 pound blanched almonds, cut lengthwise into fine pieces
- a knife tip of fine cinnamon
- 2 lemons, grated on a grater
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 pound almonds grated on a grater
- 1/2 pound almonds cut into pieces
- wafers for lining

## Directions

1. Whip together the sugar and egg whites into a thick meringue
2. Add the cut almonds, cinnamon, grated lemons, and lemon juice
3. Mix everything together
4. Line a springform with wafers
5. Fill the mixture in
6. Bake the torte in the oven
7. Note that 1/2 pound of almonds should be grated on a grater and the other 1/2 pound cut into pieces
