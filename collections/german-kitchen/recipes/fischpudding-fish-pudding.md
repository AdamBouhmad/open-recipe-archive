## Ingredients

- 1 pound fish forcemeat
- 1 soaked and pressed roll
- 1 large finely minced onion
- 1/2 quarter pound butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 quart milk
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 1 thymus gland
- 1 onion
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1 half cooking spoon of flour
- Cold water
- 1 spoonful good meat broth
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mince the onion and steam it in 1/2 quarter pound butter
2. Mix the fish forcemeat, soaked roll, steamed onion and 3 eggs together for a quarter hour
3. Add 1/2 quart milk, salt and nutmeg and mix thoroughly
4. Bind the mass tightly in a wet cloth
5. Let it cook in boiling water for one hour
6. Clean the thymus gland and mince it finely with an onion and lemon zest
7. Steam the minced thymus with a piece of butter in a casserole
8. Sprinkle half a cooking spoon of flour over it
9. Stir smooth with cold water
10. Bring to a boil with one spoonful of good meat broth
11. Add salt and juice of half a lemon
12. Place the pudding on a plate and pour the sauce over it
