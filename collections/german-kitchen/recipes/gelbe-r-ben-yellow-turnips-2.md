## Ingredients

- yellow turnips
- butter
- onions
- small peeled potatoes
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Scrape the yellow turnips, cut them long and finely.
2. Rub them in a bowl of lukewarm water by hand until all impurities are removed.
3. In a casserole with a piece of butter and finely minced onions, steam them until tender.
4. Add small peeled potatoes and cook everything together until soft.
5. Pour some meat broth over them and serve.
