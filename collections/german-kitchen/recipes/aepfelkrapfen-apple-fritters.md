## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 8 loth melted butter
- 3 loth finely powdered sugar
- 1 knife point cinnamon
- 1 schoppen warm milk
- 5 eggs
- 1 small knife point salt
- 3 spoonfuls beer yeast
- apple purée for filling
- sugar and cinnamon for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 1 pound flour, 8 loth melted butter, 3 loth finely powdered sugar, 1 knife point cinnamon, and 1 schoppen warm milk
2. Beat in 5 eggs and scatter 1 small knife point salt
3. Work the dough on the noodle board until it throws bubbles
4. Work it a bit more with 3 spoonfuls beer yeast
5. Roll it out knife-back thick on the noodle board
6. Cut out little fritters with a mold, fill with apple purée, bake until golden, and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
