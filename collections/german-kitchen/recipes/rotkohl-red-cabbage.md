## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) red cabbage
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 50 g fat
- 1/8 l vinegar
- 30 g sugar
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 spice dose
- 1 pinch cinnamon
- 1 pinch pepper
- 10 g salt
- 2 l water

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from the cabbage and cut it in half, then into very fine strips on a cutting board.
2. Pour boiling salted water with 1/2 tablespoon of vinegar over it, drain well, and mix it in a bowl with several spoonfuls of fresh vinegar until it is nicely red.
3. Heat the fat (goose, pork fat, or lard) with the remaining vinegar, salt, and sugar in an earthenware pot.
4. Add the vinegar-refreshed cabbage, mix well once the liquid boils, and let it braise slowly covered for 2.5 hours.
5. At the very end, add a little pepper, ground spices, and cinnamon; however, this is a matter of taste.
6. Before cooking, adding spices causes a poor color.
7. Before serving, adjust the seasoning with sugar, vinegar, and salt, as vinegar evaporates more or less.
