## Ingredients

- water
- vinegar
- eggs
- salt
- sorrel
- butter
- sour cream
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Heat equal parts water and vinegar in a pan, salt the water
2. Carefully crack the eggs into it (the water should not boil hard)
3. When the egg whites are hard, remove them and place in cold water
4. For the sauce: pound 2 handfuls of washed sorrel in a mortar and press the juice firmly through a cloth
5. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole
6. Take a tablespoonful of sour cream and 1 egg yolk, stir in the sorrel juice
7. Let it warm over charcoal fire
8. Arrange the eggs on a plate and pour the sauce over them
