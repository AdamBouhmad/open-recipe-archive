## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 cabbage heads
- fresh water
- salt water
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- chervil
- tarragon
- thyme
- basil
- smoked meat
- jus
- flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- half measure of thick sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the core from 2 to 3 cabbage heads and soak them in fresh water for one hour.
2. Cook them in salt water until half tender, drain in a sieve.
3. Cut each head in two parts, bind it with string to hold it together again.
4. Place the cabbage in a casserole with a piece of butter.
5. Add minced onions, parsley, chervil, tarragon, thyme, and basil, also a piece of smoked meat.
6. Cover the casserole, turn the cabbage often, and pour a little jus over it if needed.
7. When the cabbage is almost tender, sprinkle a little flour over it, salt it, add nutmeg and pepper.
8. Half an hour before serving, pour half a measure of thick sour cream over it.
9. Remove the meat, cut it into pieces, arrange the cabbage, remove the strings, and garnish with the smoked meat.
