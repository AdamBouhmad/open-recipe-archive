## Ingredients

- 1 l lung broth
- 30 g flour
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Preparation: The broth of the lung is poured through a sieve.
2. Heat the fat in an earthenware pot, sweat the flour in it thoroughly, slowly stir in the hot broth and cook the soup open until slimy.
3. Season with salt, pepper and parsley and serve.
4. Optionally, the soup can be improved by finishing with 1 egg.
