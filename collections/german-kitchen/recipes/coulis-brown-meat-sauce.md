## Ingredients

- 4 pounds old beef or cow meat
- 2 veal knuckles
- 3 veal feet
- 1 old hen
- various roots
- onion
- nutmeg
- 6 measures of water
- 1/2 pound butter
- 2 handfuls of fine flour
- 1/2 glass of cold water

## Directions

1. Chop all the meat, bones, roots, onion, and nutmeg into small pieces
2. Cook in a covered casserole or kettle with 6 measures of water for 4 to 6 hours
3. In a separate casserole, melt 1/2 pound butter
4. Stir in 2 handfuls of fine flour and let it cook together for two minutes
5. Pour in 1/2 glass of cold water and stir the flour until smooth
6. Strain the broth through a hair sieve
7. Remove the fat from the top and pour the broth into the casserole with the flour, stirring constantly
8. Let it cook with the flour for 2 more minutes
9. Pour this coulis into an earthen dish and use it for all white sauces
