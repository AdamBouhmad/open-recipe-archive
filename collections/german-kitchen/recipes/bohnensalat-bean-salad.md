## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1-1/2 pounds) snap beans or wax beans
- 2 liters water and 10 g salt
- 10 g onions
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon broth
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1/2 teaspoon parsley or chives

## Directions

1. Prepare: Remove the beans, break them, wash them. Chop the parsley, grate the onion.
2. Cook the beans in plenty of boiling salt water; once they feel very tender, run them under cold water on a sieve. After draining well, mix them with oil, a little broth or hot water and salt, and mix with a spoon; gradually add onions, pepper and vinegar and mix carefully until all liquid is absorbed; then taste the beans carefully, sprinkle chopped parsley or chives over them and arrange the salad in a mound.
