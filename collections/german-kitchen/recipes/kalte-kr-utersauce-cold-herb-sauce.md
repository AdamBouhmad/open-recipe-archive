## Ingredients

- 1 handful of chives
- 1 handful of chervil
- equal amount of parsley
- 2 shallot onions
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the chives very finely
2. Chop together the chervil, parsley, and shallot onions very finely
3. Stir the hard-boiled egg yolks smooth with vinegar and oil
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Mix in the chopped herbs and onions
6. Place in a sauce bowl and serve with beef
