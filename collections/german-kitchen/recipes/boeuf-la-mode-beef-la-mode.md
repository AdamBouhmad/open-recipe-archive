## Ingredients

- beef from the hind quarter or a good tail piece
- vinegar
- wine
- roots of various kinds
- lemons
- bay leaves
- whole pepper
- cloves
- black bread
- meat broth or water
- lemon zest
- mace
- brown gingerbread

## Directions

1. Pound the beef until tender and place in a dish
2. Pour equal parts vinegar and wine as desired
3. Cut all sorts of roots and add lemons, bay leaves, whole pepper, and cloves
4. Cover the meat tightly and let it stand 2 to 3 days, turning it daily
5. On the day of cooking, put everything together in a casserole
6. Cut the crust from black bread and add it, pour in 1 spoonful of meat broth or water
7. Cover tightly and cook until soft in the oven or over coals
8. Remove the meat, press everything through a hair sieve, remove the fat
9. Put the sauce back in the casserole, add the meat, and let it cook together for another half hour
10. Add the zest and juice of half a lemon and a knife point of mace
11. If the sauce is not thick enough, soak a piece of brown gingerbread in a little meat broth until soft and stir it into the sauce
