## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 8 loth flour
- yolk of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Whip egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Stir in sugar, flour, and lemon yolk until very thick
3. Coat a black baking sheet with wax
4. Using a funnel dipped in water, let the mixture drip onto the sheet
5. Dry in the oven, keeping them white
