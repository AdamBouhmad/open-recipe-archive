## Ingredients

- 1/2 measure of thick cream
- 1/2 measure of raspberries
- 8 loth sugar or enough to sweeten

## Directions

1. Whip 1/2 measure of thick cream very firmly with a small whisk
2. Lay it on a sieve
3. Press 1/2 measure of raspberries through the sieve
4. Add 8 loth sugar or enough until it is sweet enough
5. Mix the whipped cream into it
6. Put it in the freezing tin without stirring it however
