## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Bay leaf
- Wine vinegar
- Bouillon
- Flour
- Half a measure of sour cream
- Capers
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Place the veal leg in a roasting pan with butter, salt, pepper, onions and bay leaf.
2. Pour a little wine vinegar over it, cook slowly, turning frequently.
3. Pour bouillon over it so it achieves a nice golden color.
4. Brown a little flour in butter and add it with half a measure of sour cream to the roast.
5. Let it cook slowly, add a little capers and the juice of a lemon.
6. Skim off the fat from the top and pour the sauce over it when the leg is on the plate.
