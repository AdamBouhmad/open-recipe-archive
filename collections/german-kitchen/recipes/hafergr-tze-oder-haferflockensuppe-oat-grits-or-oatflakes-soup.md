## Ingredients

- 50 g oat grits or 40 g oatflakes
- 1 piece of orange peel
- 10 g fat
- 10 g sugar
- 5 g salt
- 1 tablespoon currants
- 1 l water

## Directions

1. Rinse the oat grits or oatflakes three times with cold water in an earthenware pot; soak the currants according to instructions.
2. Put the rinsed oat grits with salt, orange peel and water in an earthenware pot and cook slowly until soft covered. When the oat grits are soft, press them through a fine sieve and season the soup with fat, sugar and salt, reheat once more and serve mixed with the currants.
