## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- half Maas milk
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 4 Loth sugar rubbed with 1 lemon
- 8 eggs
- 1 pound refined sugar
- half glass water
- raspberry juice
- chocolate ice (for winter)

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg whites into a snow
2. Cook half a Maas milk in a wide brass pan with 1 piece whole cinnamon and 4 Loth sugar rubbed with 1 lemon for 6 minutes
3. Slowly stir into 8 eggs in a pot
4. Let the milk with eggs come together once more in the pan
5. Strain through a hair sieve into a wide dish for the table
6. Make milk boil again
7. When it rises, place 4 quenelles made with a spoon from the egg snow into the boiling milk
8. Let them cook briefly
9. Place them on a hair sieve to drain
10. Lay them on the cold cream
11. Let 1 pound refined sugar cook with half a glass water in a brass pan until it threads
12. Using a spoon, make 2 lattices of the syrup over the quenelles
13. Color one quenelle red by pouring raspberry juice over it and another yellow
14. In winter, chocolate ice can be made over it
