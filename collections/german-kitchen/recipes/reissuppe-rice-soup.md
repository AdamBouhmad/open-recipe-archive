## Ingredients

- Rice
- Boiling hot water
- Very fatty meat broth
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Clean the rice and pour boiling hot water over it
2. Let it stand for a quarter hour, then drain and pour boiling water over it again
3. Let it stand another quarter hour
4. Place it in a casserole and pour very fatty meat broth over it
5. Slowly bring to a boil over low heat
6. When serving, Parmesan cheese can be added to the bowl or served separately
