## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound fresh butter
- 1/2 pound finely powdered sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- powdered cinnamon to taste
- 6 to 8 handfuls beautiful ripe grape berries
- 8 egg whites
- 1 spoonful flour

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound fresh butter until very foamy
2. Add 1/2 pound finely powdered sugar and 8 egg yolks one after another
3. Add powdered cinnamon to taste
4. Add 6 to 8 handfuls of beautiful ripe grape berries to the mixture
5. Beat the 8 egg whites into stiff snow
6. Mix the stiff snow and 1 spoonful flour loosely into the mixture
7. Brush a baking dish with butter, fill it with the mixture, and bake until beautifully golden
