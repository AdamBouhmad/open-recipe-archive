## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- One pound of pork or sausage meat
- Bread crumbs (for one kreuzer)
- 2 to 3 onions
- Lard
- Half a lemon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Mace
- One egg
- Carrots
- Celery

## Directions

1. Remove all bones from the veal breast, cut away the cartilage at the bottom, break through the breast, open it out so it becomes nice and wide.
2. Wrap it in cloth, pound it firmly.
3. Make the following forcemeat: place one pound of pork or sausage meat in a bowl, rub in bread crumbs (for one kreuzer) on a grater, chop 2 to 3 onions, braise them in a piece of melted lard until light golden and add them to the meat.
4. Grate the zest of half a lemon and add it with salt, pepper, cloves and mace.
5. Mix everything with one egg, spread it on the breast, roll it together, sew it closed, bind it with twine.
6. Place it in a roasting pan or casserole with a piece of fat, onions, bay leaves, carrots and celery, cover the casserole and let it cook for one hour.
7. It can be served with jus or sauce.
