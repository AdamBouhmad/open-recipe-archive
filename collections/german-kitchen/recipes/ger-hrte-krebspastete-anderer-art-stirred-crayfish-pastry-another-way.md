## Ingredients

- 30 to 40 crayfish
- salt water
- butter
- water
- 6 eggs
- sour cream
- fine flour
- salt
- egg whites
- 3 calf sweetbreads
- soft-boiled ox palate
- lemon zest
- 1 onion
- crayfish tails and claws
- flour
- cold water
- meat broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- juice of half a lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- soft-boiled morels

## Directions

1. Boil 30 to 40 crayfish in salted water until like a hard egg
2. Break out the claws and tails, remove the bile, scrape the body clean
3. Remove the shells from the tails and claws, pound them fine, braise them with a piece of butter until foaming
4. Cook with a little water, remove from fire, skim the crayfish butter into cold water
5. Lightly stir half of it in a bowl, beat in 6 eggs with 1 spoonful of sour cream per egg
6. Add 6 spoonfuls of fine flour, 2 knife-tips of salt and the snow of 4 egg whites
7. Coat a baking sheet with butter, fill the mixture and bake in the oven until golden
8. Once slightly cooled, cut off a lid, remove some of the soft interior
9. Clean 3 calf sweetbreads, cut into pieces, peel and finely chop a soft-boiled ox palate
10. Braise the sweetbreads and palate with chopped lemon zest and 1 onion in the second half of the crayfish butter
11. Add the crayfish tails and claws, 3 spoonfuls of flour, a little cold water, some meat broth, salt, nutmeg and the juice of half a lemon, also 2 egg yolks and soft-boiled morels
12. Let everything cook together, fill this ragout into the pastry, cover with the lid and serve
