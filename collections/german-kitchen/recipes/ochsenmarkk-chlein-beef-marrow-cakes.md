## Ingredients

- beef marrow
- 4 eggs
- flour
- ham
- bread
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Melt a quarter pound of beef marrow
2. Strain through a sieve into a bowl
3. Beat it very foamy
4. Add 4 eggs one after another
5. Mix in 4 spoonfuls of flour and a quarter pound of minced ham
6. Grate bread onto a board
7. Make round cakes from the mass
8. Fry them in lard until golden yellow
