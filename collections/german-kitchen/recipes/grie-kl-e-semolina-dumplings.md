## Ingredients

- 125 g semolina
- 1/4 l skim milk
- 20 g margarine
- 1 pinch salt
- 30 g sugar
- 1 egg
- water and salt for cooking the dumplings

## Directions

1. Bring milk, fat, salt, and sugar to a boil
2. Pour in the semolina while stirring constantly and cook until a shiny mass forms
3. While still hot, stir in the egg yolk
4. Beat the egg white to stiff peaks and fold into the dough
5. Using a damp spoon, drop uniform dumplings into salted boiling water just before serving
6. Cook uncovered in salted water until done
7. Remove with a skimmer, let drain, place on a warmed dish and serve with stewed fruit, fruit sauce or similar
