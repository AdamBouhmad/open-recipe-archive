## Ingredients

- 65 g lean mutton
- 20 g onions
- 15 g fat
- 15 g celery
- 125 g rice
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat and cut into uniform 2 cm pieces. Weigh the onion, clean the celery, and wash the rice thoroughly.
2. Brown the meat cubes and onion in fat until yellow, season with pepper and salt. Add celery, rice, and water and let everything cook together slowly until tender. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt before serving.
