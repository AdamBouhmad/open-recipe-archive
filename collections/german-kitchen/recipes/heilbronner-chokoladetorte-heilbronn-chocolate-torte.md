## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1/2 pound blanched, finely ground almonds
- zest of 2 lemons, grated
- juice of 2 lemons
- 12 egg yolks
- snow of 8 egg whites
- 10 loth grated chocolate
- butter and sugar for the pan

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter until light and fluffy
2. Take 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar and an equal amount of blanched, finely ground almonds
3. Add the grated lemon zest and juice from 2 lemons
4. Beat 12 egg yolks into the mixture and stir everything together for half an hour
5. Beat the snow stiffly from 8 egg whites
6. Mix 10 loth grated chocolate with the snow
7. Fold this into the mass
8. Brush a cake pan with butter and sprinkle with sugar
9. Fill the mass into it
10. Bake the torte for one hour
