## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 pound sugar
- 6 loth almonds ground with rose water
- oblaten (wafer paper)
- coarsely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk 3 egg yolks with 1 pound sugar for a quarter hour
2. Stir in 6 loth almonds ground with rose water for a quarter hour
3. Cut square pieces from oblaten
4. Spread the mixture thickly on each piece (about the thickness of a knife back)
5. Place on a black baking sheet
6. Sprinkle coarsely ground sugar on top
7. Bake
