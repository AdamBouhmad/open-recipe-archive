## Ingredients

- Dunked slices of white bread
- Weak meat broth
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Let dunked slices of white bread cook soft in a casserole with weak meat broth
2. Pass them through a hair sieve
3. Let them come together once more in the casserole and stir in an egg yolk
