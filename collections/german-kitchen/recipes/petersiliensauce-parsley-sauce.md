## Ingredients

- 1/2 l water, broth, fish water, or vegetable water
- 20 g flour
- 20 g margarine
- 10 g onions
- 1 teaspoon parsley
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Weigh the onion and parsley.
2. In the margarine, first sweat the onion until cooked but only light yellow, then add the flour. Slowly stir in the liquid, cook the whole into a bound white sauce, add the parsley, and season with pepper and salt.
3. If you use 1 teaspoon of chopped dill instead of parsley, you get dill sauce. Adding chopped chives or chives mixed with parsley gives chive sauce, and mixed herbs give herb sauce.
