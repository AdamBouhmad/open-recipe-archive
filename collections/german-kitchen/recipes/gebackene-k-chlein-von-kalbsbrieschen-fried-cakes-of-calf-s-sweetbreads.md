## Ingredients

- 2 rolls (Wecken)
- water
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 calf's sweetbreads
- 2 to 3 shallot onions, chopped
- chives or parsley
- 5 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- lard

## Directions

1. Soak 2 grated rolls in water and press out firmly
2. Stir 1/4 pound butter lightly in a bowl and add the rolls
3. Simmer 2 calf's sweetbreads in water
4. Cut them, after removing the windpipes and veins, into small pieces
5. Braise in a casserole with a piece of butter 2 to 3 chopped shallot onions, some chives or parsley, and the sweetbreads
6. Let cool, then stir in 5 eggs one after another into the butter-stirred rolls
7. Add the sweetbreads along with salt and nutmeg
8. Form round cakes from the mixture
9. Fry beautifully golden in lard
10. Can be served as a side dish
