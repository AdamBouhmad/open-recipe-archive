## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg potatoes
- 20 g flour
- 25 g lard
- 10 g onions
- 1/4 l skim milk or broth
- 5 g salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, peel them and cut into slices; mince the onions.
2. Cook the onions soft in the lard in an earthenware pot, sweat the flour in it, add milk or broth, stir frequently, season with pepper, salt and vinegar, cook slowly for 1/4 hour, add the warm potato slices in the sauce, bring to a boil and serve.
