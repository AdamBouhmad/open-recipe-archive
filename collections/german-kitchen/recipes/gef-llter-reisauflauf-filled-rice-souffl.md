## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- boiling water
- half a Maas milk
- 4 Loth butter
- 4 Loth butter
- 1 Vierling sugar
- 1 lemon
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter
- 2-3 tablespoons preserved currants

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/4 pound of rice in boiling water 2-3 times and let it drain through a hair sieve
2. Cook it with half a Maas of milk and 4 Loth of butter in a casserolle
3. Stir 4 Loth of butter in a bowl and add the rice until it cools
4. Fold in 1 Vierling of sugar grated on a lemon, finely ground, 6 egg yolks and the snow from the 6 egg whites
5. Butter a baking sheet and fill in half the mass
6. Top with 2-3 tablespoons of preserved currants and cover with the remaining half of the mass
7. Bake the soufflé in the oven
