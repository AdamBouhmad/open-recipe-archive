## Ingredients

- Napfkuchen dough without raisins
- Plums
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Spread the same dough as for Napfkuchen, but without raisins, on a clean sheet pan
2. Roll out with a rolling pin to desired thickness
3. Let rise again
4. Distribute plums, sugar, and cinnamon on top
5. Let rise for another 1/2 hour with the toppings before baking in the oven until done
