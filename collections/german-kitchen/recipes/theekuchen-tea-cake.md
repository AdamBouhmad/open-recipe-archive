## Ingredients

- 2 spoonfuls beer yeast
- milk
- flour
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 spoonfuls sour cream
- lemon zest
- salt
- 2 loth finely ground sugar
- quarter pound butter
- melted lard
- blanched almonds
- coarsely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Make a small dough from 2 spoonfuls beer yeast and milk in measured flour
2. When it has risen, add 2 whole eggs, 3 spoonfuls sour cream, some lemon zest, salt and 2 loth finely ground sugar
3. Make everything into a dough
4. Work this on the pastry board like a butter dough
5. Make it a bit firm
6. Roll it out
7. Cut a quarter pound butter into it
8. Fold it over
9. Roll it until the butter is well worked in
10. Roll it out 2 fingers thick
11. Let it rise on a butter-coated sheet
12. Brush the cake with melted lard
13. Sprinkle it with minced almonds and coarsely ground sugar
14. Then bake it
