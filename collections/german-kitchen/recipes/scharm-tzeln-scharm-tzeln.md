## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- 1 pound finely ground sugar
- firm paper

## Directions

1. Beat 5 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Whisk with 1 pound finely ground sugar for half an hour until it forms a thick icing
3. Cut round pieces from stiff paper
4. Form small capsules from the paper and stretch them over a round stick or thimble
5. Fill the capsules halfway full with the meringue mixture
6. Place on a black baking sheet
7. Put in a cooled oven and bake until completely white
