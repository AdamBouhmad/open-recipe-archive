## Ingredients

- chocolate (quarter pound)
- water (1 glass)
- milk (1 maas)
- sugar (4 loth)
- cinnamon (1 knife point)
- egg yolks (8)
- flour (half a cooking spoon)
- cold milk
- egg whites (8)
- almonds (2 loth, finely cut lengthwise)
- green granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve a quarter pound chocolate in a brass pan with one glass of water
2. Cook it with one maas milk, 4 loth sugar and a knife point of cinnamon
3. Stir 8 egg yolks smooth with half a cooking spoon of flour and cold milk
4. Stir the chocolate to it
5. Let everything draw once more in the pan together
6. Spoon it into a pot until it cools slightly
7. Pour the cream through a hair sieve onto the plate
8. Whip a stiff foam from the 8 egg whites
9. Cover the entire cream with it
10. Decorate with 2 loth almonds finely cut lengthwise
11. Sprinkle green granulated sugar over it
12. Place the cream in the roasting oven a quarter hour before serving so it becomes hot again
