## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg pears
- 1/4 liter water
- 30 g sugar
- 1 piece orange peel
- 2 g starch flour
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Peel the pears nicely, trim the stems (if the pears are large, cut them in halves, quarters or eighths, remove the hard core) and wash them. Dissolve the starch flour.
2. Let them simmer soft in an earthen pot with water, sugar and orange peel covered, add the starch flour and let the pears boil once more with it.
