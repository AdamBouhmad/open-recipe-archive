## Ingredients

- 1/2 Maas wine (red wine is best)
- 1/2 pound sugar
- whole cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Calculate 1/2 pound sugar to 1/2 Maas wine
2. Let it become very hot together in a brass pan, but it must not boil
3. Place whole cinnamon and cloves in a hair sieve
4. Pour the hot wine over them and cover immediately
