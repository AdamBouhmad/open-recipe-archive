## Ingredients

- 1/2 measure thick cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 handfuls fine flour
- 6 loth melted butter
- 1 knife-point of salt
- 4 egg whites
- sweet cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 measure of thick cream in a bowl with a small white whisk to a thick snow.
2. Whisk 4 egg yolks into it.
3. Stir in 3 handfuls fine flour, 6 loth melted butter, and 1 knife-point of salt gradually.
4. Beat the whites of 4 eggs to a stiff snow and mix with the dough.
5. If the dough is still too heavy, it can be thinned with sweet cream.
6. Bake the waffles in hot iron and sprinkle sugar on both sides.
