## Ingredients

- Borsdorf apples
- wine (1 schoppen)
- water (1 schoppen)
- sugar (quarter pound)
- milk (half a maas)
- lemon (zest and juice)
- sugar (3 loth)
- egg yolks (4)
- flour (3 finger-widths)
- cold milk
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Peel Borsdorf apples and cut in half
2. Remove the core house
3. Hollow out the apples a little
4. Cook them with one schoppen wine, one schoppen water and a quarter pound sugar until soft
5. For the cream: cook half a maas milk with the zest of a lemon and 3 loth sugar together
6. Stir 4 egg yolks and 3 finger-widths of flour smooth with cold milk
7. Add to the boiling milk
8. Let everything draw on the fire once more
9. Pour the cream through a hair sieve onto the designated plate
10. Drain the apples beforehand (the syrup in which the apples were cooked must be reduced until it forms threads)
11. Lay them on the cream with the hollow facing upward
12. Fill them with the syrup
13. Sprinkle chopped pistachios on top
