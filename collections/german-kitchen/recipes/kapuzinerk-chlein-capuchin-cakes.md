## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 3 spoonfuls beer yeast
- semi-sour cream
- 1/8 pound shelled almonds (some should be bitter)
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 pound raisins (pitted)
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a pre-dough in a bowl with 2 pounds flour, 3 spoonfuls beer yeast, and semi-sour cream
2. Let it rise
3. Pound 1/8 pound shelled almonds (including some bitter ones) very finely
4. Cut 1/4 pound butter into the dough
5. Add the almonds, 3 eggs, and 1/4 pound pitted raisins
6. Work the dough with everything together and let it rise again in warmth
7. When ripe, roll it out to knife-back thickness
8. Cut triangular pieces with the pastry wheel
9. Fry the cakes in lard
10. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
