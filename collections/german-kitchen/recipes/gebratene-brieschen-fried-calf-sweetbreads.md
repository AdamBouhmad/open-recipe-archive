## Ingredients

- calf sweetbreads
- salt water
- butter
- onion
- lemon zest
- salt

## Directions

1. Blanch the sweetbreads in salt water, clean them, and lard them
2. In a casserole, fry them in butter and sliced onion until golden yellow
3. When serving, pour the jus through a fine sieve over them, sprinkle finely chopped lemon zest over them, and salt to taste
