## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Flour
- Half a Vierling butter
- Flour
- Meat broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots clean and place them in a dish with cold water in which a handful of flour has been added.
2. After all roots are scraped, rub them clean by hand (they should only be cut to half-finger length), place them in a casserole, knead half a Vierling of butter with a handful of flour, place it on the salsify, pour a little meat broth, salt and nutmeg to taste over and cook until soft.
