## Ingredients

- Veal liver
- Onions
- Butter
- Bread for 2 kreuzer worth
- Meat broth or jus
- Egg yolks
- Sweet cream
- Toasted bread slices
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop the veal liver with onions
2. Place butter in a casserole and steam the liver in it
3. Cut bread into the mixture, steam together for half an hour
4. Pour in meat broth or jus and let it cook for one hour
5. Draw through a hair sieve, return to the casserole, and bring to a boil again
6. Beat 2 egg yolks in a soup bowl, add 2 tablespoons of sweet cream and toasted bread slices
7. Arrange the soup over this mixture
8. Season with salt and nutmeg
