## Ingredients

- 1 250 g can of pods
- 125 g shredded cooked fish (firm variety)
- 15 g margarine
- 1-1/2 milk rolls
- 20 g margarine
- 1/4 teaspoon grated onion
- 10 g margarine
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Lightly fry the fish pieces in 15 g margarine
2. Dice the peeled rolls
3. Toast the bread crumbs in 20 g margarine until crispy
4. In 10 g margarine sautéed with onion, add the drained pods and toss quickly with pepper and salt until hot
5. Mix fish, bread crumbs and pods together
6. Arrange on a hot dish
