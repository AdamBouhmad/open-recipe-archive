## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 8 loth melted butter
- 3 loth finely pounded sugar
- a knife point of cinnamon
- 1 schoppen warm milk
- 5 eggs
- a small knife point of salt
- 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast
- apple sauce (Aepfelmuß)

## Directions

1. Mix flour, melted butter, finely pounded sugar, cinnamon, and warm milk into a dough
2. Beat in 5 eggs and sprinkle salt over it
3. Work the dough on the noodle board until it throws bubbles
4. Work it a little more with 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast
5. Roll out the dough on the noodle board to the thickness of a knife back
6. Cut out small fritters with a model
7. Fill with apple sauce
8. Fry until golden
9. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
