## Ingredients

- Champignons (Mushrooms)
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms
2. Let them drain
3. Cook them for a quarter hour with a large piece of butter
4. Place them in a dish and reduce the sauce further
5. Add enough butter so that the mushrooms are completely covered with it
6. Pour the sauce over them
7. Tie the dish shut and store
