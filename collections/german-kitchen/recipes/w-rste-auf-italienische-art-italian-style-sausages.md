## Ingredients

- Pork or veal brain
- Some veal udder
- Some butter
- Egg yolks
- A little saffron

## Directions

1. Make the sausages from pork or veal brain with some veal udder and some butter added
2. The difference is that no bread is used, but only the brain itself and some egg yolks, with a little saffron added
3. Serve them warm
