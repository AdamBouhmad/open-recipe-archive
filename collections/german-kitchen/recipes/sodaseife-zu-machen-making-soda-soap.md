## Ingredients

- 1 pound pure soap
- half a meschen ox bile (which refreshes the color)
- 2 loth honey
- 3 loth sugar
- half a loth turpentine

## Directions

1. Take 1 pound pure soap, half a meschen ox bile which refreshes the color, 2 loth honey, 3 loth sugar and half a loth turpentine
2. Mix everything together and let it melt very gently in an earthenware crucible
3. When everything is mixed, pour the mass back into a crucible onto a linen cloth which has previously been dipped in hot water and then in cold water
4. On the following day you will have the most beautiful soap, which is excellent for silk washing, for ribbons and for flowers made of cotton
