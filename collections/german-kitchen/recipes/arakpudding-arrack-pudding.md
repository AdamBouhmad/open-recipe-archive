## Ingredients

- 6 spoonfuls pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 loth fine flour
- 1/2 glass arrack
- Snow from the egg whites
- Butter
- 4 good apples
- 1 piece of butter
- 1 schoppen wine
- 1 knife tip of cinnamon
- 1 piece of sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 6 spoonfuls pounded sugar and 6 egg yolks very thick like a biscuit dough
2. Add 4 loth fine flour, 1/2 glass arrack and the snow from the egg whites
3. Mix everything together
4. Coat a tin capsule with butter including the lid
5. Fill the mass into it
6. Bind the capsule firmly shut
7. Place it for a quarter hour in boiling water
8. For the sauce: peel 4 good apples
9. Remove the core
10. Steam them in a piece of butter
11. Push them through a hair sieve
12. Let them cook with 1 schoppen wine, 1 knife tip of cinnamon and 1 piece of sugar
13. Pour the sauce over the pudding
