## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- coarsely powdered fennel for a kreuzer's worth
- 1/2 pound flour

## Directions

1. Stir 1 pound sugar and 5 egg yolks together for half an hour
2. Add fennel for a kreuzer's worth, coarsely powdered, and 1/2 pound flour
3. Mix everything together
4. Make finger-long little sticks from the dough on the noodle board and bake in the oven
5. Let them cool, cut them lengthwise in two, reheat them in the oven until golden, and serve with tea and coffee
