## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen cream
- 1 schoppen milk
- orange blossom water
- 1 lemon
- 4 loth sugar
- 6 crushed macaroons
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 finger-widths flour

## Directions

1. Cook the cream, milk, orange blossom water (value of one kreuzer), water, lemon zest rubbed in sugar, 4 loth sugar, and 6 crushed macaroons together for a strong quarter hour.
2. Beat 6 egg yolks in a bowl, add 3 finger-widths of flour.
3. Slowly pour the cooked milk onto the mixture.
4. Stir everything for one minute over the fire.
5. Press through a hair sieve into the serving bowl.
6. Place the cream in boiling water, cover with a lid topped with coals and let it set.
7. Sprinkle sugar on top and brown it with a glowing spatula.
