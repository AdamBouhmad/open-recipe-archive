## Ingredients

- otter fish
- onions
- cloves
- mace
- lemon
- herbs and spices
- port wine or stone wine
- fat
- veal
- bread crust
- flour
- truffles
- citron yellow
- nutmeg
- white pepper
- kernels of cubeb peppers
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Skin the otter fish and cut into pieces
2. Soak in cold water for 5 to 6 days, changing the water daily
3. On the sixth day, remove and soak for 2 days in good wine with onions, cloves, mace, lemon, and various herbs and spices
4. Place with a bottle of Port or stone wine over the fire and let simmer for one hour
5. For the truffle sauce: line a casserole thickly with onions
6. Cook a piece of fat and 1-1/2 pounds of finely cut veal until golden
7. Add half a ladle of the stock in which the otter was cooked, bread crust, and some flour
8. Cook together and strain through a fine sieve
9. Weigh 2 to 3 truffels with their rough skin removed, with the citron yellow and juice
10. Add a pinch of nutmace, some white pepper, and 3 cubeb kernels
11. Place the otter fish in the sauce
12. Simmer for a while and add sliced mushrooms and truffles
13. Cook everything a bit longer
