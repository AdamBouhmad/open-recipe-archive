## Ingredients

- whitefish
- pepper
- salt
- lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Scale the fish and clean it out
2. Dry it with a cloth
3. Rub with pepper and salt
4. Make a cut across the back of the fish
5. Insert a lemon slice into the cut
6. Place the fish on the rack
7. Pour butter over it
8. Grill it nicely
9. When it is on the platter, pour lemon juice over it
