## Ingredients

- 140 g ground beef
- 60 g ground pork
- 1 teaspoon melted fat
- 1 egg white or egg yolk
- 1/2 roll
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 pinch salt and pepper
- 1 knife point grated onions
- 1 l water
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. Set water with salt to boil for cooking the meatballs.
2. Cut the roll into cubes, soak, and squeeze out.
