## Ingredients

- poultry
- bouillon
- béchamel

## Directions

1. Prepare the farce from poultry, as often explained
2. Make round or long dumplings from it, boil them in bouillon
3. Place them dry with béchamel in a casserole so they become quite hot and place them in the pastry
