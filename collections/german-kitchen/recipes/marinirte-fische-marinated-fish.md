## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- wine vinegar
- shallot onions
- bay leaves
- lemons
- cloves

## Directions

1. Scale and wash the fish, rub them with salt and pepper, let them sit for 1 hour
2. Place them on a grill, baste them frequently with melted butter, and brown them nicely
3. Let them cool, place them in a stone pot
4. Pour wine vinegar over them, add shallot onions, bay leaves, lemons, pepper, and cloves
5. Seal the pot
