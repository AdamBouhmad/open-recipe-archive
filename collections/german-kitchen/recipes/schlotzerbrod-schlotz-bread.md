## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- 18 Loth flour
- bacon
- anise

## Directions

1. Stir together for a quarter hour: 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar, 2 whole eggs, 3 egg yolks, and the yolk of 1 lemon (grated on a grater)
2. Mix in 18 Loth flour
3. Form finger-long small sticks on the noodle board that are somewhat thinner in the middle than at the ends
4. Brush a black sheet with bacon
5. Sprinkle it with anise
6. Place the little breads on it, not too close together
7. Bake them beautifully golden in the oven
