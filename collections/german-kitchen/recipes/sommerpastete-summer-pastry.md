## Ingredients

- Tender butter dough
- Broccoli peas, 1 pint, half-grown
- Parsley, 1 handful
- Shallot onions
- 2 to 3 cleaned chickens
- Butter
- Jus
- Flour, 3 fingers' worth
- Crushed sugar, 1 handful
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Mace
- Sour cream, 1/2 pint

## Directions

1. Roll out tender butter dough into 2 knife-back-thick cakes
2. Place the first cake in a deep cake tin
3. Lay paper (which must be made hollow and round so it can be removed after baking) on top
4. Lay an equally large cake (actually the lid) on the paper
5. Brush both cakes with water
6. Encase them with a 2-finger-wide ring of butter dough
7. Cut them smooth
8. Brush with egg
9. Poke the pastry lid with a warm knife so it gets openings while baking
10. Bake it in the oven until golden
11. For the ragout: clean 1 pint of half-grown broccoli peas
12. Steam them for a quarter hour with a handful of parsley and shallot onions
13. Lard the breast and drumsticks of 2 to 3 cleaned chickens
14. Mince the neck and everything else together
15. Steam this with the chickens in a casserole with a small piece of butter
16. Baste the chickens often with jus during steaming
17. When the chickens are tender, remove them
18. Strain the sauce through a fine sieve
19. Pour another 1/2 ladle full of meat broth to it
20. Return the chickens to the sauce
21. Let them cook a while longer so they get a nice glaze
22. Sprinkle 3 fingers' worth of flour, 1 handful of crushed sugar, salt, nutmeg, mace, and 1/2 pint of sour cream over the broccoli peas
23. Fill them into a plate
24. Lay the chickens on top
25. Cover the pastry with the baked lid
26. Serve it at table
