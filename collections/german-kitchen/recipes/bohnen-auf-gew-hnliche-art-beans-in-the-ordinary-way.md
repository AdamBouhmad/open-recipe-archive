## Ingredients

- beans
- fat
- 2 tablespoons of flour
- onions
- parsley
- bean herb
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and cook the beans in boiling water
2. When soft, drain them
3. Take a piece of fat in a pan and brown 2 tablespoons of flour in it
4. Add onions, parsley, and bean herb
5. Deglaze with half a large spoon of beef broth and bring to a boil
6. Pour this over the beans and let them cook for another half hour
