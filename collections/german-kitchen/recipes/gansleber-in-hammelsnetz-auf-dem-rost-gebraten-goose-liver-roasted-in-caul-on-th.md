## Ingredients

- goose liver
- salt
- pepper
- wheat meal
- caul

## Directions

1. Wash the goose liver well
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and grated wheat meal
3. Spread out the caul
4. Wrap the liver in the caul
5. Place on the grate
6. Put some coals underneath
7. Roast slowly until nicely browned
8. When serving, add lemon juice
