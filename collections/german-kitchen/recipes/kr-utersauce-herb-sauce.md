## Ingredients

- chives
- chervil
- basil
- tarragon
- 2 handfuls of grated black bread
- 6 to 8 crushed juniper berries
- 1 loth of sugar
- the finely minced zest of one lemon
- one spoonful of French mustard
- salt
- pepper
- 2 spoonfuls of Provençal oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince chives, chervil, basil, and tarragon together very finely
2. Mix in the grated black bread, crushed juniper berries, sugar, minced lemon zest, mustard, salt, and pepper
3. Blend everything together with 2 spoonfuls of Provençal oil and vinegar
4. Serve with fish, pickled meat, or smoked beef
