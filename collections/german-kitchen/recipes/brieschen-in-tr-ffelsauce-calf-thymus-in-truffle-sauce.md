## Ingredients

- calf thymus
- butter
- flour
- strong jus
- glass of Bordeaux or Markgräfler wine
- 2 to 3 truffles
- anchovy
- salt

## Directions

1. Blanch the thymus, remove the skin, lard it, and brown it nicely in butter in a casserole
2. In a piece of butter, let a spoonful of flour turn light yellow and deglaze with strong jus
3. Pour in a glass of Bordeaux or Markgräfler wine
4. Clean the truffles, cut off the rough parts, pound them in a mortar with a piece of butter and a clean anchovy, and add to the sauce
5. Cut the truffles themselves in a separate dish
6. Pour the sauce through a hair sieve over them
7. Add the thymus with the cooking liquid and salt to taste
8. When serving, arrange the truffles nicely on top of the thymus
