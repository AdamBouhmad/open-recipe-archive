## Ingredients

- 30 g rye flour
- 30 g fat or margarine
- 1 l skim milk
- 1 pinch salt
- 10 g sugar

## Directions

1. Sweat the flour in an earthenware pot with the fat, slowly stir in the hot milk and cook for another 20 minutes.
2. Season the soup with sugar and salt.
