## Ingredients

- 1 pound good butter
- 1 pound fine flour
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream
- 2 knife-tips salt
- 1 whole egg and half an egg
- cold water

## Directions

1. Weigh the flour and place it on the noodle board
2. Make a depression in the middle and add the sour cream, salt, eggs, and cold water
3. Work the dough to a gentle firmness and roll it out finger-thick
4. Take the butter, knead it with a little flour and place it in the dough
5. Fold the dough over, roll it out and repeat this three times
