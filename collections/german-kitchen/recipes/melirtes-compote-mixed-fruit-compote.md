## Ingredients

- Borsdorf apples
- Rose apples
- Calvil rouge apples
- Luiken apples
- Prunes
- small Muscat pears
- Quinces
- water (half a maas)
- wine (half a maas)
- large raisins (quarter pound)
- prunes (quarter pound)
- almonds (2 loth, peeled)
- raisins

## Directions

1. Peel Borsdorf apples, rose apples, Calvil rouge and Luiken apples and cut in half
2. Remove the skin from the prunes
3. Cook the small Muscat pears and quinces with half a maas water and the same amount of wine in a brass pan along with the skins and cores for a quarter hour
4. Pour into a hair sieve
5. Cook a quarter pound of large raisins a couple minutes in the syrup, together with a quarter pound of prunes
6. Arrange 2 layers of the cooked apples on a plate
7. Arrange each apple, prune, pear and dried plum next to each other
8. Make a pyramid from the fruits arranged so the pyramid looks nicely mottled
9. Cut 2 loth peeled almonds into 4 parts
10. Stick a raisin on each almond piece
11. Decorate the entire pyramid with these
12. Let the syrup reduce a bit more
13. Pour it over the pyramid
