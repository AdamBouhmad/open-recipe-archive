## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- salt
- pepper
- good rice
- bouillon
- ham
- Parmesan cheese
- bratwurst
- fat
- Provençal oil

## Directions

1. Bone out the suckling pig and rub it with salt and pepper.
2. Cook good rice in bouillon with a piece of ham; the rice must be quite hard and dry.
3. Remove from heat and when cold, rub a piece of Parmesan cheese into it.
4. Fry some bratwurst, cut them into small pieces and remove the skin.
5. Remove the ham from the rice, cut it into small pieces, and stir the rice well.
6. Fill the suckling pig with the rice mixture.
7. Place the pig in a casserole with a piece of fat and put it in the oven for one hour before serving.
8. Brush it with Provençal oil so it gets a nice color.
9. Place the roasted pig on a plate and pour a little jus over it.
