## Ingredients

- half pound flour
- 4 loth butter
- 2 egg yolks
- half glass wine
- 2 loth finely ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- one schoppen sour cream
- one schoppen sweet cream
- 3 loth ground sugar
- half a lemon zested
- quarter pound raisins and dried plums
- 4 egg whites snow
- one cooking spoon fine flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Make a dough from half pound flour, 4 loth butter, 2 egg yolks, half glass wine and 2 loth finely ground sugar on a pastry board like a butter dough
2. Roll it out
3. Fold it over itself
4. Repeat this once more
5. Lay the dough in a springform coated with butter
6. Mix together 6 egg yolks, one schoppen sour and equal amount sweet cream, 3 loth ground sugar, half a zested lemon, quarter pound raisins and dried plums, and the snow from 4 egg whites
7. Mix in one cooking spoon fine flour that has been smoothed with milk
8. Fill this onto the cake
9. Bake in the oven
