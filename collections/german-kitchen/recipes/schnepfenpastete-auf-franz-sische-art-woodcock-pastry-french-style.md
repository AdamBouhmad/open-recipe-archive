## Ingredients

- 3 cleaned woodcocks
- Butter pastry dough for a 3-inch-high pastry
- Butter
- Onions
- Juniper berries
- Bay leaves
- Lemons
- 2 shallot onions
- Parsley
- Red wine, 1 glass
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Mace
- Good jus, 2 tablespoons

## Directions

1. Make a 3-inch-high pastry from butter dough
2. Tuck the heads of 3 cleaned woodcocks onto their breasts
3. Lard the woodcocks and cut them into 4 parts
4. Cut the breast from one woodcock
5. Steam the woodcocks in a casserole with a piece of butter, onions, juniper berries, bay leaves, and lemons for half an hour
6. Press out the woodcock intestines firmly, then mince them together with the woodcock breast, 2 shallot onions, half a lemon peel, and parsley
7. Remove the steamed woodcocks from the casserole
8. Strain the sauce through a fine sieve
9. Return the sauce to the casserole with the minced woodcock mixture
10. Cook for only 1 minute more with the juice of half a lemon, 1 glass red wine, salt, pepper, cloves, mace, and 2 tablespoons good jus
11. Pour half the sauce into the pastry
12. Lay the cooked woodcocks inside
13. Pour the remaining sauce over them
14. Cover with a butter dough lid
15. Brush with egg
16. Bake the pastry in the oven
