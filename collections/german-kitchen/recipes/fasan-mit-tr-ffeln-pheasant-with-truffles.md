## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant, prepared
- butter
- 2 truffles, cleaned
- raw ham
- 1/2 schoppen meat broth
- 1/2 lemon, juice and zest

## Directions

1. In a casserole, braise the pheasant in butter until half-done.
2. Remove and cut the meat from the breast and legs into neat round pieces.
3. Slice the cleaned truffles into leaves.
4. Place butter in a casserole with the meat pieces and truffle slices.
5. Cut in some raw ham.
6. Pour 1/2 schoppen meat broth around.
7. Add the juice and zest of 1/2 lemon.
8. Cover and braise slowly for another half hour.
9. Arrange on a platter.
