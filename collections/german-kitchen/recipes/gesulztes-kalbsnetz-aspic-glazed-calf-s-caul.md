## Ingredients

- 2 pounds pork forcemeat
- 1 pound veal forcemeat
- 2 white breads, soaked in milk and squeezed dry
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- a handful of steamed onions
- 2 cleaned herring
- 1 large calf's caul or large intestine

## Directions

1. Mix the pork and veal forcemeat, white bread, salt, pepper, and mace together. Add the steamed onions and 2 cleaned herring, grinding everything together until fine and foamy.
2. Sew the mixture into a large calf's caul or large intestine. If using intestine, it must be poked (to prevent bursting).
3. Cook slowly in a pan with a handful of salt for 1 hour. Place on a platter and cover with clear aspic to set.
