## Ingredients

- about 4 pounds of finely chopped pork from the shoulder or the so-called separated piece
- 3 quarter-pounds of bacon cut into cubes
- one schoppen of water
- salt
- pepper
- ginger

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into cubes and knead it together with the finely chopped pork in a bowl by hand along with one schoppen of water
2. Cover and let it stand overnight
3. The next day, add salt, pepper, and ginger to taste
4. Work in another half schoppen of water
5. Fill the mixture into casings of your choice
6. Fry the sausages fresh or hang them to smoke
