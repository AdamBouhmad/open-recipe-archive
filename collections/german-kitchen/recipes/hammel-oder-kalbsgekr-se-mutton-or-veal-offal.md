## Ingredients

- 1 offal (stomach, net, and tripe)
- 1 spice packet
- 100 g salt
- 50 g onions
- 40 g fat or margarine
- 40 g flour
- 1 pinch saffron
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 1 pinch pepper

## Directions

1. The offal consists of stomach, net, and tripe. It should only be used from freshly slaughtered animals. Pour warm water over it repeatedly and rinse in cold water. Starting from one side, rub it vigorously with salt, let soak for 24 hours, and cut into 4 cm pieces. Slice the onions.
2. Add to the offal 1 spice packet, half the onions, salt and 1-1/2 l water and cook slowly until tender in 2-3 hours. Make a gelatinous sauce from fat with the remaining onions sweated in it, flour, and the strained broth. Color the sauce yellow with saffron or stirred egg yolk and season with chopped parsley, pepper, and salt. Drain the offal on a sieve, heat it in the sauce and serve.
