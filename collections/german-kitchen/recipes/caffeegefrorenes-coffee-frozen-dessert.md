## Ingredients

- 1/2 measure of milk or sweet cream
- 1/4 pound roasted coffee, only lightly roasted
- 4 loth sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Pound the coffee coarsely in a mortar
2. Place the coffee and sugar in the milk
3. Bring everything to a boil over the fire
4. Beat 9 egg yolks in a pot and stir smooth with cold milk
5. Slowly pour the boiling coffee milk into the eggs
6. Let it cook briefly in the pan
7. Strain through a sieve
8. Let it cool completely
9. Treat it in the freezing tin according to the method given in No. 767
