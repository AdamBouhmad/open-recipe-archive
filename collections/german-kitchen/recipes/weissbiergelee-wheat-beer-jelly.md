## Ingredients

- 9 tablets red gelatin
- 1 bottle wheat beer
- 1/2 cm cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon sugar
- 100 g sugar
- 1 tablespoon rum

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatin in 8 tablespoons wheat beer while lukewarm.
2. In the remaining wheat beer, add cinnamon, lemon sugar, sugar, rum and the gelatin poured through a sieve, stir everything thoroughly, remove the cinnamon, fill the jelly into small glasses, a porcelain or glass bowl and chill well.
