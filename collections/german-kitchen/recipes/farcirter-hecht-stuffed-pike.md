## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- 1/2 pound pork fat
- 1/4 pound bacon
- 4 anchovies
- bread soaked in milk
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- onions, finely chopped and steamed
- 2 eggs
- sliced onions
- 1 bay leaf
- lemons
- meat broth
- hot butter
- consommé

## Directions

1. Clean the pike carefully, cut out the gills, make a careful cut at the opening below, and cut the skin on the back; make a stitch at the fins but not completely through, then remove the skin
2. Lard the pike nicely with a mixture made from 1/2 pound pork fat, 1/4 pound bacon, 4 anchovies, bread that has been soaked in milk and squeezed out and finely ground, salt, nutmeg, pepper, and finely chopped steamed onions, all mixed together with 2 eggs and thoroughly pounded in a mortar
3. Fill the pike with this mixture and sew it shut
4. Place it in a roasting pan with a rack at the bottom, add sliced onions, 1 bay leaf, lemons, and a little meat broth
5. Roast in the oven until golden, frequently basting with hot butter
6. When serving, let consommé melt over it, coat the pike with it, and pour the sauce through a hair sieve over it
