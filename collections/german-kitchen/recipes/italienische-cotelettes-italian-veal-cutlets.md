## Ingredients

- beautiful white veal cutlets
- butter
- lemon juice
- 3 to 4 anchovies
- onion
- parsley
- lemon zest
- grated bread
- jus
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the sinew from the bottom of beautiful white veal cutlets, strip the bone clean, pound the meat, and tenderize it well
2. Shape it nicely, salt it lightly, and braise it in butter and lemon juice
3. Meanwhile, chop 3 to 4 anchovies finely with some onion and parsley and the zest of half a lemon
4. Toast a handful of grated bread with a piece of butter in a casserole until light golden
5. Mix the chopped ingredients into the bread
6. Spread this mixture knife-thick on the cutlets and pour jus over them
