## Ingredients

- 30 g oatflakes or 40 g common oat groats
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 slice lemon
- 5 g sugar
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Preparation: Oat groats or oatflakes are washed three times in cold water, stirring them thoroughly.
2. Add the lemon slice without pit and skin and the water to the oat groats, let them swell slowly under frequent stirring while covered, press through a hair sieve and warm them, seasoned with salt and sugar, while stirring.
3. These soups can be diluted with boiling water to make a drink, which can be made more nourishing, like the soups, by whisking with an egg yolk.
4. Optional: Hafergrützsuppe with almond flavor: Instead of lemon, add 5 blanched, finely grated sweet almonds to the oat groats, cook them slowly for 1 hour and complete the soup as above.
