## Ingredients

- one pound of fine flour
- 2 spoons of good beer yeast
- half a schoppen of lukewarm milk
- 4 loths of melted butter
- 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
- the grated yellow of one lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place one pound of fine flour in a bowl
2. Make a small dough in the center of the bowl with 2 spoons of good beer yeast and half a schoppen of lukewarm milk
3. Add 4 loths of melted butter, 2 whole eggs, 2 egg yolks, and the grated yellow of one lemon
4. Beat the dough for a quarter hour
5. Let it rise until ripe
6. On a pasta board, form 2 long sausage shapes
7. Place on a baking sheet dusted with flour
8. Set in a warm place and let rise again
9. Brush with beaten egg
10. Sprinkle with sugar
11. Bake in the oven until beautifully golden yellow
12. Do not cut until the next day
13. Can be served with tea and for dessert
