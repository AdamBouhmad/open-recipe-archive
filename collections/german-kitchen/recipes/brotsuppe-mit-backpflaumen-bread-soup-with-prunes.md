## Ingredients

- 125 g old bread
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 5 g salt
- 1 l water
- 40 g sugar
- 12 prunes
- 1/4 l water
- cinnamon and orange peel

## Directions

1. Crumble the bread and place in a pot, pour 1 l water over it and soften the bread in it. Wash the prunes and place them in a pot with 1/4 l water, where they swell covered.
2. Place the soaked bread with salt on the stove, covered; place the soaked prunes, to which cinnamon and orange peel can be added, in another pot also covered. When the bread is cooked and completely dissolved, strain the soup through a sieve, add butter, sugar, the softened prunes along with their sauce and season the soup to taste.
