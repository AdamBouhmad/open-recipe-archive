## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt (as much as needed)
- 2 handfuls of juniper berries

## Directions

1. Cut the stalk and ribs from the cabbage
2. Cut this finely in the usual way
3. Mix with salt as much as needed
4. Add 2 handfuls of juniper berries
5. Put the cabbage in a clean vessel into which cabbage leaves have been previously laid
6. Press the cabbage firmly together with a wooden pestle
7. Lay cabbage leaves on top
8. Cover with a clean cloth and the vessel's lid
9. Weight it well
10. In 14 days the cabbage is already edible
