## Ingredients

- A juicy piece of beef
- Cold water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four anchovies
- Lemon
- A large piece of fat
- Sliced onion
- Sorrel
- Butter
- Minced onion
- Parsley
- One tablespoon of flour
- Nutmeg
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Put the beef in cold water and bring to a boil; when cooked, remove to a board
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Clean four anchovies, make holes in the meat with a knife, and place one anchovy and a piece of lemon in each hole
4. Heat a large piece of fat in a casserole, add sliced onion and the meat
5. Roast until beautifully browned in the oven
6. Pour hot water over the sorrel, let it stand for fifteen minutes, and press it out
7. Mince the sorrel finely
8. Heat a piece of butter in a casserole with minced onion, parsley, and one tablespoon of flour
9. When brown, add the sorrel and turn everything over
10. Add grated nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste, and a tablespoon of meat broth
11. Let it come to a boil once more
12. Arrange on a platter and place the roasted meat on top
13. The sauce can also be served separately
