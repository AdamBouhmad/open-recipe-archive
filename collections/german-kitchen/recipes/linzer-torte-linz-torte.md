## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/2 pound pounded sugar
- 1/2 pound fine flour
- 1/2 pound unskinned, finely pounded almonds
- 4 loth grated chocolate
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 6 fresh egg yolks
- 1 loth pounded cinnamon
- 1/2 quint pounded cloves
- 1 egg white
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/4 pound sugar
- preserved currants
- firm paper
- wafers

## Directions

1. Make a dough from butter, pounded sugar, flour, unskinned finely pounded almonds, grated chocolate, hard-boiled egg yolks (pressed through a hair sieve), fresh egg yolks, pounded cinnamon, and pounded cloves
2. Roll it out finger-thick
3. Make a rim of firm paper around the torte so it does not run
4. Cut strips from the remaining dough and make a lattice over the torte
5. Place on a dark baking sheet lined with wafers and bake at medium heat for half an hour in the oven
6. Make the following frosting: from 1 egg white, the juice of 1/2 lemon, and 1/4 pound sugar
7. Spread this frosting on the lattice of the torte, dry it in the oven with the frosting
8. Before serving, place preserved currants in the openings of the lattice
