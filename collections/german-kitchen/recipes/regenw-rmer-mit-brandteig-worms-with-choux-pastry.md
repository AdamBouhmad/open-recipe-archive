## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas water
- 1 piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
- Fine flour
- 2 to 3 eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Flour for the board
- Butter for the plafond
- Milk

## Directions

1. Let 1/2 maas water and 1 piece of butter the size of a hen's egg boil in a pan
2. Stir in fine flour until it forms a thick dough
3. In a bowl, beat 2 to 3 eggs, salt and sugar together
4. Sprinkle flour on a noodle board and make finger-thick noodles from the dough
5. Brush a plafond with butter, pour in milk to the height of a fingernail
6. Set the noodles side by side, but not too close together
7. Cover the plafond and place it on coals or in a baking oven
8. Let the noodles cook until no more sauce is visible
9. Turn them with a baking spatula and sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
