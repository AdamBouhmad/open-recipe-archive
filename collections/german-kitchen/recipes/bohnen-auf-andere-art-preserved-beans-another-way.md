## Ingredients

- fat beans (sword beans are best)
- salt
- grape leaves
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- flour
- meat broth or bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the beans as for a bean salad (1 handful of salt per bowl of beans)
2. Mix with salt in a clean dish and place in a clean, well-pitched barrel lined with grape leaves on top and bottom
3. Press the beans down firmly, place a well-fitting lid on top and weight it down
4. To cook: let them cook in a dish with boiling water for an hour
5. Pour into a colander and pour cold water over so they stay green
6. Braise in a saucepan with a piece of butter, onion and parsley, sprinkle 3 fingerfulls of flour on top, shake them, add one ladle-full of meat broth or bouillon and cook uncovered so they don't lose their green color
