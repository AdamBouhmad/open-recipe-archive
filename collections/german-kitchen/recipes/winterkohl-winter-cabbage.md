## Ingredients

- cabbage
- fat
- boiling water
- onions
- pork or goose fat
- 3 spoonfuls flour
- meat broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- glazed chestnuts

## Directions

1. Strip, wash the cabbage and add a piece of fat with boiling water.
2. Cook until tender, strain, and let sit in cold water for a quarter hour.
3. Press out firmly and finely mince together with onions.
4. In a casserole with a piece of pork or goose fat in which 3 spoonfuls of flour have been roasted, steam for a quarter hour.
5. Add a ladle of meat broth, salt, and nutmeg.
6. Serve on the platter garnished with glazed chestnuts if desired.
