## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) potatoes without skin, weighed
- 65 g flour
- 2 egg whites or 1 egg
- 1 knife point grated nutmeg
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 10 g salt
- 20 g lard
- 1 white roll
- 2 l water
- 20 g salt

## Directions

1. Peel and boil the potatoes in their skin, then remove the skin and grate them; if using leftover peeled potatoes, press them through a sieve. Cut the white roll into small cubes and fry in 20 g lard.
2. Whisk the egg and stir with salt, fat, and nutmeg; add the bread crumbs and 50 g flour and mix thoroughly. With floured hands, form equal dumplings, roll them in flour, and cook in boiling salted water for 15 minutes with the pot uncovered.
3. Lay them on a colander to drain and serve very hot, usually with stewed fruit or pears or with sauce.
4. Leftover dumplings can be sliced, fried in hot fat in a pan.
