## Ingredients

- potatoes
- six hard-boiled eggs
- half a pint sour cream
- salt
- pepper
- herring
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook good potatoes until not very soft, peel them and cut them into small pieces
2. Press the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve into half a pint of sour cream
3. Add salt and pepper and stir everything together well
4. Butter a plate and layer it with the cut potatoes, then with some herring, then with some of the stirred eggs
5. Continue until all ingredients are on the plate
6. Place some butter on top and put the plate in a roasting pan so the dish becomes golden on top
