## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound rice
- 2 ladle-fuls of fat or 1 piece of butter
- 1 old hen (or young chickens)
- Parmesan cheese
- 1 piece of butter

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 pound rice in boiling water two to three times
2. Wash it clean and put it in a casserolle
3. Add 2 ladle-fuls of fat; if you don't have that, take a piece of butter
4. Place on low coals and let it only come to a boil
5. Take an old hen, cook it until half done, insert it into the rice and let it cook until very tender
6. When serving, carve the hen by the joint
7. Put 1 spoonful of rice on the plate, sprinkle 1 handful of Parmesan cheese over it, place some of the hen on the rice, then rice again and hen again, until it is finished
8. Let a piece of butter become hot in a small pan and brown the rice when serving; you can also add more Parmesan cheese if you wish
