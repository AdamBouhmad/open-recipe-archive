## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- 2 spoons of good beer yeast
- 3 tablespoons of lukewarm milk
- one loth of bitter almonds
- one schoppen of milk
- 10 loths of butter
- 6 eggs
- 4 loths of ground sugar
- one knife point of salt
- sugar and almonds for dusting

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour in a bowl
2. Stir a small dough in the center of the bowl from 2 spoons of good beer yeast and 3 tablespoons of lukewarm milk
3. Let it rise in gentle warmth
4. Grind one loth of bitter almonds very fine with one schoppen of milk
5. Pour through a hair sieve, pressing the almonds firmly
6. Set the almond milk in a warm place
7. Stir 10 loths of butter in a bowl until foamy
8. Gradually stir in 6 eggs, 4 loths of ground sugar, and one knife point of salt
9. Complete the dough with the almond milk
10. Beat for a quarter hour
11. Brush a kugelhopf mold with butter
12. Dust with sugar and almonds
13. Fill the dough into it
14. Bake in the oven
