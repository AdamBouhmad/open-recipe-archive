## Ingredients

- 2 Loth isinglass
- 1 schoppen water
- half Maas milk or cream
- one-quarter pound sugar rubbed with 1 orange
- 6 egg yolks
- milk
- juice of 2 oranges
- half schoppen Arak
- 1 schoppen whipped cream
- anise oil

## Directions

1. Cook 2 Loth isinglass with 1 schoppen water until dissolved
2. Strain the water through a hair sieve into a bowl
3. Cook half a Maas milk (or cream is better) with one-quarter pound sugar rubbed with 1 orange
4. Beat 6 egg yolks smooth with a little milk
5. Slowly stir the boiling milk and isinglass water into the egg yolks
6. Add the juice of 2 oranges
7. Let everything come together once more in the pan
8. Strain the cream through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl
9. Add half a schoppen Arak and 1 schoppen whipped cream
10. Fill the cream into a mold brushed with anise oil
11. Let it cool on ice
