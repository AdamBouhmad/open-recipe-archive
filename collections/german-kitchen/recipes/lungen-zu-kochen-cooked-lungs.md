## Ingredients

- 1/2 lung with heart
- 2 tablespoons soup greens
- 1 spice dose
- 15 g salt
- 2 l water

## Directions

1. Lung and heart are thoroughly washed and cut into hand-sized pieces. The soup greens are cleaned, washed and cut into pieces.
2. Water, salt, lung and heart pieces are placed in a pot and cooked covered. Once foam has formed and has cooked for a while, remove it, add the soup greens and spice dose and let the meat pieces continue cooking covered. To see if they are done, pierce the heart with a stick; if it is soft, remove all the meat and use the broth and lung according to the recipe for Lungenhache.
