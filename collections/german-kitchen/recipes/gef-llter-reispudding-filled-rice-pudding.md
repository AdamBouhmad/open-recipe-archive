## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- boiling water
- 1/2 quarter pound butter
- 1/4 pound ground sugar
- 1/2 quarter pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- half a lemon, zest grated
- egg whites from 6 eggs
- 1 wafer
- preserved fruits
- 3 loth blanched, finely ground almonds
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- 1 schoppen milk
- 1 lemon peel
- a piece of sugar
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald the rice with boiling water, let it stand for a quarter-hour, cook it soft with 1/2 quarter pound butter
2. Stir it with 1/4 pound ground sugar and another 1/2 quarter pound butter until it is cold
3. Beat 6 egg yolks to it, half a lemon with zest grated on a rasp, the snow from the 6 egg whites
4. Stir everything together, fill half of the mass into a lift-pan, lay a wafer on top, on this something preserved, on this the other half of the mass
5. Bake the pudding in the oven
6. For the sauce: let 3 loth blanched, finely ground almonds come to boil with 1 schoppen sweet cream, 1 schoppen milk, 1 lemon peel and a piece of sugar
7. Stir together 3 egg yolks, pour the boiling milk to it, let everything come to a boil once more and pour this sauce over the pudding
