## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas water
- 3 loth butter
- 1 loth sugar
- Flour
- 6 to 8 egg yolks
- Lard
- 1/2 maas milk
- 3 loth sugar
- 1/2 lemon peel
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 fingers-full flour
- Cold milk
- 10 egg whites
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 maas water, 3 loth butter, 1 loth sugar and flour to a thick dough
2. Put it in a bowl and beat 6 to 8 egg yolks in
3. Make walnut-sized balls from the dough and bake them in lard
4. For the crème: cook 1/2 maas milk with 3 loth sugar and 1/2 lemon peel
5. Smooth 5 egg yolks with 3 fingers-full flour and cold milk, stir in the boiling milk
6. Let everything come to the boil once more in the pan
7. Pour part of the crème through a hair sieve onto a plate
8. Make a pyramid from the baked puffs in the crème, pour the other half also on
9. Beat a snow from 10 egg whites and make a hand-high ring of it around the pyramid
10. Sprinkle sugar on it and place the puffs for a short time in the oven
