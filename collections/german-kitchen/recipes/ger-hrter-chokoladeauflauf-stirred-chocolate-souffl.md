## Ingredients

- 6 large spoonfuls sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 knife point of cinnamon
- 1/4 pound grated vanilla chocolate
- 1 loth fine flour
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the dish
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Stir 6 large spoonfuls sugar with 6 egg yolks until the mass is quite thick.
2. Add a knife point of cinnamon, 1/4 pound grated vanilla chocolate, and 1 loth fine flour.
3. Mix everything together, brush a bowl with butter, and fill the mass into it.
4. Bake in the oven until nicely golden and sprinkle sugar on top.
