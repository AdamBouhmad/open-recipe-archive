## Ingredients

- 375 g peeled potatoes
- 125 g mixed vegetables
- 20 g medium barley
- 20 g lean bacon
- 8 g salt
- 1 l water
- 1 pinch salt
- optionally 1 teaspoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into cubes. Rinse the barley twice with cold water and drain.
2. Sauté the barley with bacon cubes and salt in an earthenware pot, but the bacon must not brown. Add the water and cleaned vegetables cut into pieces, as well as raw potatoes cut into cubes and well washed, cover and cook slowly for 2 hours. Season the soup with salt and serve. Optionally add 1 teaspoon chopped parsley.
