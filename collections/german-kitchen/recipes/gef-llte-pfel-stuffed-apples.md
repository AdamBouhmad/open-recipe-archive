## Ingredients

- Apples of equal size
- butter
- sugar
- 2 beaten eggs
- finely grated bread
- 10 loth almonds in their shells
- 1/4 pound ground sugar
- 1 nutmeg
- a knife-tip of ground cloves
- 4 loth grated black bread
- 10 egg yolks
- snow from 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel apples of equal size and remove the core.
2. Stew the apples in a piece of butter and a piece of sugar until half-soft.
3. When cold, turn them in 2 beaten eggs, then in finely grated bread.
4. Place on a baking sheet and bake until golden in the oven.
5. Pound 10 loth almonds in their shells very fine, 1/4 pound ground sugar, one nutmeg, a knife-tip of ground cloves, and 4 loth grated black bread together.
6. Beat 10 egg yolks into this mixture and stir everything together strongly like a cake batter.
7. Finally fold in the snow from 10 egg whites.
8. Place the apples on a plate, fill the mixture on top, and let it bake golden in the oven.
