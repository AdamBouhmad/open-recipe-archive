## Ingredients

- dark bread crust
- wine
- lard
- jus
- lemon juice
- lemons, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the top crust of dark bread soft in wine (but first the bread must be baked yellow in lard)
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Add one ladle of jus
4. Add some lemon juice and finely chopped lemons
5. Let everything boil together
