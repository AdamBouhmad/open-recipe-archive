## Ingredients

- trout
- 2 calf's feet, cleaned
- 1 Loth isinglass
- fish stock
- 1 whole egg
- 1 sheet blotting paper
- clove leaves
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the trout blue and place on a dish, cover tightly so the blue does not disappear
2. Make the following sauce: Chop 2 cleaned calf's feet together with 1 Loth isinglass, add fish stock so it makes 1-1.5 Maas
3. Cook for 2 to 3 hours, strain the jelly through a fine sieve and let it stand in a bowl for a quarter hour so the sediment remains on the bottom
4. Draw a sheet of blotting paper through the jelly so the fat adheres to it, beat a whole egg in a casserole and pour the jelly slowly into it
5. Set over fire and stir constantly with a new cooking spoon until it boils, then remove it and pour into a felt hat
6. Place the trout on a large plate, pour some of the clear jelly over it and let it stand
7. Color part of the jelly green in a dish: take fat clove leaves, pound them in a mortar, press the juice through a cloth and make the jelly green with it
8. When it is half set, pour the green jelly over the trout, let it set completely and pour clear jelly over once more
9. The fish can now be garnished to taste
