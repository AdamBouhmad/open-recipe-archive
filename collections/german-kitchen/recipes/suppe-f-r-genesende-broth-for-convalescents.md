## Ingredients

- one fresh egg
- 1 quart water
- 1 quart wine
- sugar
- cinnamon
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Boil a fresh egg, peel it, and press it through a hair sieve.
2. Cook it in 1 quart water, 1 quart wine, sugar, and cinnamon for a quarter hour.
3. Arrange it over toasted bread slices.
4. If the wine is still too strong for the patient, meat broth may be used instead.
