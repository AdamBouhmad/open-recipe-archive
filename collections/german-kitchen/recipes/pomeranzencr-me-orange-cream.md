## Ingredients

- juice of 3 oranges
- sugar rubbed with zest of 2 oranges
- 1 schoppen white wine
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. Mix the juice of 3 oranges with sugar that has been rubbed with the zest of 2 oranges and 1 schoppen white wine until the sugar dissolves
2. Beat 6 egg yolks and 1 whole egg and add to the mixture
3. Strain the cream through a hair sieve into a designated bowl
4. Place this bowl over boiling water and cover with a lid topped with coals until the cream becomes firm
5. Serve either cold or warm
