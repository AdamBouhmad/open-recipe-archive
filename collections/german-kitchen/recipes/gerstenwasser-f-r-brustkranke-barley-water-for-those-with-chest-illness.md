## Ingredients

- 8 Loth washed and picked malt barley
- 4 Loth washed raisins
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 3 Maas water
- juice of 1 lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 8 Loth washed and picked malt barley, 4 Loth washed raisins and the peel of 1/2 lemon with 3 Maas water for one hour
2. Fill the pot again with water
3. Simmer a little longer
4. Add the juice of 1 lemon and some sugar
5. When it has cooled, give it to the patient to drink
