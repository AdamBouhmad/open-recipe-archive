## Ingredients

- 1 kg Schweinerücken (pork loin)
- 5 g Salz (salt)
- 1 Zwiebel (onion)
- 1 Gewürzdosis (1 spice dose)
- 4 Essl. saure Milch oder leichtes Bier (4 tablespoons sour milk or light beer)
- 10 g Kartoffel- oder Weizenmehl (potato or wheat flour)
- 1/2 l Wasser (water)

## Directions

1. Wash the pork loin quickly, dry, pound, and trim the fat to two straws' width; score diagonal lines first to the right, then to the left to form small squares; dust the meat with salt.
2. Bring the oven to an even, not too strong heat; peel the onion and place with the spice dose in the pan; stir the flour with 4 tablespoons cold water.
3. Place the prepared roast in the pan, pour 4 tablespoons boiling water over it (so the whole surface is covered) and place in the oven.
4. For a roast this small, allow 20 minutes per 1/2 kg; for a larger one, 18 minutes.
5. Baste the roast every 5-8 minutes with the fat that collects; meanwhile add a few spoonfuls of liquid and scrape the pan often to form a brown sauce.
6. The finished roast should be a nice brown color and crisp; remove from the pan, place on the serving dish, and keep warm.
7. Degrease the sauce, cook through with beer or milk, add the dissolved flour while stirring; the sauce must come to a proper boil, taste, and serve through a sieve with the roast.
