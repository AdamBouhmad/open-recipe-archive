## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls of carefully washed chervil
- parsley (optional)
- butter
- 1 small cooking spoonful of flour
- meat broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls of sweet milk
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Finely mince 2 handfuls of carefully washed chervil (parsley can also be added)
2. Braise it for 1/4 hour in a pot in butter
3. Add a small cooking spoonful of flour and let it come to a boil in meat broth
4. Beat 2 egg yolks in the soup bowl
5. Add 2 spoonfuls of sweet milk and serve the soup over it
6. Also add salt and nutmeg
