## Ingredients

- 250 g calf's breast
- 2 tablespoons beef fat
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 5 g salt
- 1/4 bay leaf
- 2 peppercorns
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. The calf meat is washed, dried and beaten, and 1/4 liter water is brought to a boil.
2. The fat is rendered and heated to steaming; the prepared calf meat is rubbed with salt, dusted with flour, placed in the steaming fat and browned on all sides by turning; peppercorns and bay leaf are added; half of the boiling water is poured over it and the meat is covered and braised slowly. The meat is turned frequently and water is added at intervals. When the meat is tender, taste the sauce, remove the spices, arrange the meat and pour the sauce over it.
