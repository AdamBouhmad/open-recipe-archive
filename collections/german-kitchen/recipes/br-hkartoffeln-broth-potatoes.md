## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 pounds) potatoes
- 5 g salt
- 1 liter water
- 3/4 liter broth
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley or 1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram

## Directions

1. Prepare: Wash, peel and cut the potatoes into pieces as evenly as possible. Wash and chop the parsley.
2. Put the potato pieces in water and salt, bring to a boil, drain, add to the broth and cook covered until tender. When soft, let the pot stand open for a while so the broth properly combines with the potatoes, then add parsley or marjoram and arrange the dish in a bowl.
