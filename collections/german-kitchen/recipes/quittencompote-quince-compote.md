## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces and cut them in half
2. Cook them soft in water and place on a hair sieve
3. Cook the peels and cores soft in water and also strain through a hair sieve
4. Combine in a pan with the quinces, sugar, and lemon peel
