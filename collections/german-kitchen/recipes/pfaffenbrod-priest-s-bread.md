## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 3 egg yolks
- 8 loth finely ground sugar
- 8 loth blanched and finely chopped almonds
- 5 loth flour
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- Flour and sugar for the board
- Lard for the baking sheet

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white into stiff peaks
2. Stir in egg yolks and finely ground sugar and beat together for a quarter hour
3. Fold in blanched almonds, flour, and lemon zest
4. Dust the noodle board with flour and sugar and roll out dough knife-back thick
5. Cut into finger-long pieces and two fingers wide
6. Place on a lard-greased sheet and let stand for a quarter hour so the sugar rises
7. Bake in gentle oven heat
