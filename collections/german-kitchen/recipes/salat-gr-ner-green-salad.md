## Ingredients

- 2 heads green lettuce
- 40 g bacon
- vinegar to taste
- 3 g salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar or 1 tablespoon syrup

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into cubes and brown it.
2. Clean the lettuce by removing the thickest stems, wash it several times until it is free of sand, place it in a clean cloth, and shake it dry by gathering the ends of the cloth and swinging it.
3. Mix all ingredients and adjust the seasoning of the liquid.
4. Add the lettuce and mix it with 2 spoons.
5. Taste it; if it is too sour, add 1 more tablespoon of water.
6. Do not assemble the salad long before the meal, as it will otherwise become unattractive and soft.
