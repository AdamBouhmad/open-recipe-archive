## Ingredients

- 250 g veal
- 65 g beef kidney fat
- 8 g salt
- 1/2 egg
- 30 g grated bread roll
- 5 g flour
- 30 g margarine

## Directions

1. Finely chop or pass the meat and fat twice through the meat grinder.
2. Mix the mixture with 6 g salt and divide into 4 equal portions.
3. Using a damp knife on a plate, shape each portion into a cutlet form.
4. Coat with grated bread roll.
5. Fry in brown margarine over 7 minutes, turning three times, until slowly cooked through and nicely browned.
6. Cook a broth from the sinews, which can be mixed with a little meat extract to make a sauce if desired.
7. For the sauce: let the flour sweat gently in the frying fat, add the broth, season with salt, cook the sauce while stirring, strain through a sieve, and serve with the cutlets.
8. Do not cover the cutlets during sauce preparation, otherwise they will become soft.
