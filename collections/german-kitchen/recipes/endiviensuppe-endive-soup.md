## Ingredients

- butter
- endive, finely cut and cleaned
- flour
- meat broth
- Mundbrode bread, cut lengthwise
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place a piece of butter in a pot and braise finely cut, carefully washed, beautiful yellow endive until tender
2. Sprinkle as much flour on top as one can grasp between three fingers
3. Pour meat broth over it
4. Let it come to a boil again
5. Serve over 2 pieces of Mundbrode bread cut lengthwise and roasted golden on the grill; add salt and nutmeg
