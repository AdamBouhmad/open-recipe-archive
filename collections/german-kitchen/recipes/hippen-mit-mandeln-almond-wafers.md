## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 1/4 pound sifted sugar
- 1/4 pound finely ground almonds
- 1 knife tip cinnamon
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 3 loth flour

## Directions

1. Stir egg whites, sugar, almonds, and cinnamon together for a quarter hour
2. Mix in lemon zest, juice, and flour
3. Coat a black baking sheet with lard
4. Form wafers from the mixture
5. Bake until golden
6. While still warm, stretch them over a round piece of wood
