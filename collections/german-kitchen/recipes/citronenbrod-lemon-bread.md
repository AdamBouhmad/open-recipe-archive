## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- salt
- 4 Loth fine sugar
- 8 Loth melted butter
- 2 spoonfuls good beer yeast
- 1/2 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough in a bowl from 1 pound flour, a knife-tip of salt, 4 Loth fine sugar, 8 Loth melted butter, 2 spoonfuls of good beer yeast, the yolk of half a lemon, 2 eggs and 2 yolks
2. Work it for a quarter hour on a noodle board
3. Form a long stick from it and place it on a black sheet sprinkled with flour
4. Let it rise well at gentle warmth
5. When it is ripe, brush it with a beaten egg
6. Bake in the oven
7. The next day, cut the stick into thin slices
8. Toast them beautifully golden on a black sheet in the oven
