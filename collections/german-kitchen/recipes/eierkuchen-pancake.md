## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 105 g flour
- 5 g salt
- 1 pinch baking powder
- 1/4 liter skim milk
- 40 g cooking fat
- 10 g sugar

## Directions

1. Crack the egg, separating yolk and white into different pots
2. Beat the yolk with milk and salt, then gradually stir it into the center of the flour along with the baking powder
3. Beat the egg white until stiff and slowly fold into the mixture, which should not sit
4. Wipe a handled frying pan dry with paper
5. Heat 1 spoon of fat in the pan until hot
6. Slowly pour in the batter and distribute evenly by moving the pan back and forth
7. Brown the first side of the pancake nicely over frequent shaking, slide it onto a lid, add about 10 g fat to the unbaked side, cover the pan with the lid, flip, let the pancake steam covered for a few moments, then remove the lid
8. The cake should have risen nicely; bake to a nice color, serve, and sprinkle with sugar
9. Pancakes should not sit; if necessary, keep them only on a pot of hot water
