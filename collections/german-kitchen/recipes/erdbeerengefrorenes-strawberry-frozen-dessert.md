## Ingredients

- 2 measures of strawberries
- sugar
- 2 knife points of ground cinnamon
- 1 glass of nutmeg wine

## Directions

1. Press the strawberries through a sieve
2. Add sugar, ground cinnamon, and nutmeg wine
3. Mix everything together
4. Let it freeze in the freezing tin
5. Do not cook it over fire and do not work it during freezing
