## Ingredients

- 1 autumn chicken or 1 old chicken
- 56 g soup greens
- 15 g salt
- 1-1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken and tie it with twine; place liver, heart and stomach inside.
2. Heat the water with soup greens and salt, add the chicken, cover and cook only until tender, which for a young chicken takes 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 hours.
3. Remove from the pot, let it drain and immediately place it briefly in cold water so it becomes white and cools. Then portion it as desired for various dishes.
4. The meat in smaller and larger pieces should immediately be marinated with broth, vinegar and oil or white wine depending on intended use, or serve the chicken as its own dish with sauce of choice.
