## Ingredients

- 250 g Rindfleisch, Schild oder Dünnung (beef shoulder or brisket)
- 2 Essl. Suppengrün (2 tablespoons vegetable soup greens)
- 10 g Salz (salt)
- 1-1/4 l Wasser (water)

## Directions

1. Wash and pound the meat; set it on the stove with the water and salt; clean the vegetable soup greens, cut into even pieces, and wash.
2. Cook the meat in the salted water, skim it, add the vegetable soup greens, and cover the pot.
3. The meat must now steep rather than boil for 2-1/2 hours.
4. To use the broth as soup, remove the meat, strain the broth through a sieve or remove the vegetable soup greens, degrease the broth, and pour some spoonfuls of fat over the meat. Return the vegetable soup greens to the degreased broth.
