## Ingredients

- 15 fresh herrings
- 40 g salt
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 75 g flour
- 250 g frying fat
- 1 l vinegar
- 50 g onion slices
- 1 spice portion
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Scale the herrings, gut them, wash them, make a few light cuts along the back and salt them for 1/2 hour.
2. Dry them off and roll them evenly in flour mixed with pepper and salt.
3. Fry or bake the herrings in steaming fat until cooked through and golden.
4. Once cooled, pour over the herrings a sauce made from vinegar, water, salt, spices and onion slices that has been brought to a boil, seasoned to taste, then cooled.
5. Pack them in a stone pot, tie it shut, and use as needed.
6. Note: herrings should only be purchased when the head looks entirely pale; once it has turned red, the herrings are not fresh. The fat should be clarified.
