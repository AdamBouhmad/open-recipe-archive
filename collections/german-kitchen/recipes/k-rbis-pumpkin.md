## Ingredients

- 250 g peeled pumpkin pieces
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 clove
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 g starch flour
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin, cut it into pleasing pieces, wash and drain it. Dissolve the starch flour.
2. Simmer the pumpkin pieces soft covered with vinegar, clove and sugar, stir in the starch flour dissolved in cold water, bring to a boil and arrange it.
