## Ingredients

- 3 schoppen milk
- semolina flour
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound ground sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 1 pound cherries

## Directions

1. Cook 3 schoppen milk and semolina flour together in a brass pan to make a thick paste
2. Pour into a bowl and cut in 1/4 pound butter
3. Add 1/4 pound ground sugar and 1/2 lemon rubbed on a grater
4. Add 6 egg yolks and stir everything strongly for half an hour
5. Mix in the snow from 6 egg whites
6. Finally add 1 pound pitted cherries
7. Bake in a pan thickly coated with butter until beautiful yellow in the oven
