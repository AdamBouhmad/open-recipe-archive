## Ingredients

- Ox tongues
- Salt
- Saltpeter
- Pepper, ground
- Juniper berries
- Thyme
- Basil
- Celery herb
- Various herbs
- Cold water
- Onions

## Directions

1. Cut off the throat from the ox tongues.
2. Roast a part of salt and a part of saltpeter together with ground pepper and juniper berries.
3. Rub the tongues well with this mixture.
4. Lay them in a dish, add all kinds of herbs, thyme, basil, and celery herb.
5. Let them lie in the brine for 4 weeks, press them down firmly with a well-fitting lid.
6. Hang them afterwards in a large casing, into which they are inserted, in smoke.
7. When you wish to cook them, lay them overnight in cold water.
8. Cook them with cold water, onions, and all kinds of herbs until soft.
9. Serve them cold or warm.
