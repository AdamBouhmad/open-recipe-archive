## Ingredients

- ordinary Flädlein (thin pancakes)
- thick sour cream
- washed raisins and Zibeben (dried grapes)
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- 2 spoonfuls of milk

## Directions

1. Make ordinary Flädlein (thin pancakes)
2. Coat them with thick sour cream
3. Sprinkle with washed raisins and Zibeben (dried grapes)
4. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over them
5. Roll them together
6. Coat a dish for serving with butter and lay the filled Flädlein in it crosswise
7. Add 2 spoonfuls of milk
8. Let them rise in the oven
