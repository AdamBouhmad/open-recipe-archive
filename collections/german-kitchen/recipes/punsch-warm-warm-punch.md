## Ingredients

- 5 teaspoons tea
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1/4 bottle rum
- 125 g sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare 3/4 liter of tea according to instructions and pour through a tea sieve
2. Place wine, rum and sugar in an earthenware pot over gentle heat and bring to just before boiling point, that is until white foam appears
3. Mix with the tea
4. Check the finished punch for sweetness
