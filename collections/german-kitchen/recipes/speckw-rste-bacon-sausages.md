## Ingredients

- Half pound fresh lard
- 3 kreuzer rolls of bread
- Water
- Onions
- Parsley
- A piece of fat
- 3 to 4 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mace
- Bratwurst casings
- Hot water

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of fresh lard into small cubes
2. Soak 3 kreuzer rolls of bread in water, press them out firmly
3. Chop onions and parsley and braise them in a piece of fat
4. Remove from heat
5. Take the lard, 3 to 4 eggs, salt, pepper, and mace
6. Mix everything well together
7. Press it into bratwurst casings not too firmly
8. Place the sausages in hot water and fry them until golden brown
