## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- oat flour (Geigenmehl)
- milk
- three whole eggs
- chives
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, peel them, place them in cold water for a while, and cut them into 4 parts.
2. Butter the dish to be served at table and place the hard-boiled eggs in it.
3. Take 3 handfuls of oat flour, stir it smooth with one pint of milk.
4. Beat in 3 whole eggs and a handful of finely-cut chives, stir everything well together.
5. Add salt and nutmeg, carefully pour it over the eggs.
6. Place the dish in a baking oven and let it rise nicely golden.
