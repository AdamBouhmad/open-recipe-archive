## Ingredients

- white cauliflower
- salt water
- flour
- butter
- cold water
- meat broth
- 6 eggs
- 1 cooking spoonful of flour
- 1.5 measures of milk
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut beautiful white cauliflower into florets, remove the green skin and cook it soft in salt water
2. Stir one cooking spoonful of flour into melted butter, turn it over a few times
3. Pour in first a glass of cold water, stir it smooth, then a cooking spoonful of meat broth
4. Add the cauliflower and let it cook slowly
5. For the Eierkäse: Beat 6 eggs into one cooking spoonful of flour in a small bowl and stir well together
6. Pour 1.5 measures of milk to it
7. Butter a dish, pour the mixture into it, and place it in boiling water or in the oven and let the mass become firm
8. When time to serve, place the cauliflower in the tureen, pour meat broth over it, use a spoon to cut dumplings from the Eierkäse and place them in the tureen; also add salt and nutmeg
