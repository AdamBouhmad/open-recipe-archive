## Ingredients

- One piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
- Milk
- 1/4 pound white sago
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Heat milk with a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
2. Cook 1/4 pound white sago in it until one measure of milk has cooked down
3. Add sugar and cinnamon
4. Arrange the soup
