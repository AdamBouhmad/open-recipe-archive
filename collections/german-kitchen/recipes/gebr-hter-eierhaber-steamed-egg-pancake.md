## Ingredients

- one schoppen of milk
- 4 loth of butter
- flour
- 6 eggs
- lard
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Let a schoppen of milk and 4 loth of butter cook together in a brass pan
2. Stir in flour until it becomes a thick paste
3. Put the batter in a bowl and stir in 6 eggs, one after the other
4. Heat lard in an iron pan, let the batter run in slowly, cover it for a while
5. Turn it over, let it set again, pour a little hot lard over it
6. Break or cut it up, but not too fine, and let it finish baking
7. You can salt it or sweeten it
