## Ingredients

- 40 g Kathreiners malt coffee
- 1 l water
- 10 g coffee beans

## Directions

1. The malt coffee is ground and placed with cold water on the fire.
2. It must boil slowly covered for 5 minutes, then the ground bean coffee is poured over with the malt coffee decoction. The mixture must now stand for 15 minutes in a warm place and is then poured through a filter funnel with over-poured filter paper placed in it.
