## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 1 measure of water
- 1 lemon
- 8 to 12 lemons for juice
- 24 loth or 1/2 bottle of fine arak

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 pound sugar with 1 measure of water as described in No. 817
2. Peel the yellow zest very thinly from 1 lemon
3. Let it boil several times with the clarified sugar, then remove it as this frozen dessert acquires a certain bitterness from it
4. Once the sugar is removed from the fire, add the juice of 8 to 12 lemons, depending on how much juice the fruits contain
5. Press the whole mixture through a sieve
6. Once the syrup has cooled, add 24 loth or 1/2 bottle of fine arak
7. Let the mixture freeze as usual
8. It takes always half an hour to freeze when the ice is freshly salted
9. It is more difficult to freeze than all other types due to the spirits in the arak
