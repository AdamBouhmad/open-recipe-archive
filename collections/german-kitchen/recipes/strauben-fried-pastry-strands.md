## Ingredients

- 4 lots butter
- Half a maa water
- Flour
- 6 to 7 eggs

## Directions

1. Let 4 lots butter, half a maa water and flour cook together in a brass pan until the dough peels off from the pan
2. Stir in 6 to 7 eggs until the dough is so thin that it runs
3. Brush a strauben funnel with hot lard
4. Let the dough run through the funnel into hot lard
5. Turn the strauben over and bake on the other side
6. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
