## Ingredients

- herb leaves including: noble liverwort, strawberry plant, chervil, spoonwort, pennywort, starred liverwort, fleawort, goose daisy flowers, ground ivy, pfaffenöhrleins root, sorrel, wood sorrel, yarrow, chicory root
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Pick the herbs carefully without washing them
2. Weigh a handful of each, made as fine as possible
3. Add a pinch of salt
4. Press the juice into a glazed pot
5. Place it in the cellar overnight
6. In the morning, bring 1 Maas milk to a simmer
7. Add 3 to 4 spoonfuls of the juice
