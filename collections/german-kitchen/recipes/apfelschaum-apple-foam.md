## Ingredients

- 6 Borsdorfer apples
- 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 lemon
- wafers (Oblaten)

## Directions

1. Roast the 6 Borsdorfer apples, scrape out the pulp and stir it with 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar for half an hour
2. Beat the whites of 3 eggs to stiff peaks
3. Mix the egg whites with the yolk of half a lemon (grated on a grater) into the apple mass
4. Line a sheet with wafers, place small heaps of the mixture on it
5. Bake in the oven
