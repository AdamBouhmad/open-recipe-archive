## Ingredients

- flour (amount taken by 3 eggs)
- walnut-sized piece of butter
- salt
- hot lard
- chives, finely cut

## Directions

1. Take as much flour on the noodle board as 3 eggs will take, add walnut-sized piece of butter
2. Work the dough together well
3. Roll it out very thin
4. Cut half-finger-width noodles from it
5. Put part of them in a pan with boiling water in which a handful of salt is dissolved
6. Catch the noodles with a skimming spoon onto a plate
7. Let the remaining noodles become beautifully yellow in a small pan with hot lard
8. Pour them over the cooked noodles
9. Sprinkle finely cut chives on top
