## Ingredients

- 250 g eel
- 1/4 liter water
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1-1/2 teaspoons flour
- 1 teaspoon chopped dill
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 10 g salt
- 10 g onions
- 1 spice dose
- 3/8 liter water

## Directions

1. Prepare the eel according to directions, cut into pieces, clean and scald with boiling water
2. Cook the fish with spice dose, salt, onions and water until done
3. Remove from broth and keep hot
4. In an earthen pot, cook the fish broth with flour sautéed in butter into a good white sauce
5. Season with white pepper and chopped dill or parsley
6. Pour over the eel pieces
