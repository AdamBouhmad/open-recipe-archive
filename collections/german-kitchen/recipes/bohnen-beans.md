## Ingredients

- beans
- hot butter
- onions
- pepper
- nutmeg
- beef jus (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from the beans and split them once or twice
2. Cook with boiling water until soft
3. Drain the water
4. Sauté the beans with hot butter and onions
5. Before serving, add pepper and nutmeg, and if desired, a couple of tablespoons of beef jus
