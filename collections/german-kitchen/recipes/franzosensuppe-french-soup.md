## Ingredients

- 1 head of white cabbage
- Savoy cabbage
- 2 kohlrabi
- 2 celery stalks
- 2 yellow turnips
- 1 white turnip
- 1 handful of parsley
- cold water
- meat broth
- liver, stomach and heart of poultry (optional)
- finely cut bread

## Directions

1. Cut all vegetables as finely as noodles
2. Place in cold water and set on fire, letting it cook until half soft
3. Pour through a hair sieve and rinse with fresh cold water, letting it drain well
4. Cook it in a pot in meat broth until tender
5. If available, liver, stomach and heart of poultry cut into pieces can be added and fine cut bread cooked with it
6. Alternatively, everything can be cooked so soft that it can be pushed through a soup strainer, but in this case omit the cabbage and liver
