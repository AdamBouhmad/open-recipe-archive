## Ingredients

- corn cobs
- bay leaves
- coarsely crushed pepper
- salt water

## Directions

1. Blanch the corn cobs when they are as thick as a small finger
2. Place them in salt water for one day
3. Remove and dry them
4. Lay bay leaves and coarsely crushed pepper in a stone pot
5. Place the corn cobs on top, then pepper and bay leaves again
6. Continue alternating until the pot is full
7. Bind it tightly and store it
8. Serve with beef
