## Ingredients

- ribs of a young pig
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the sinew from the bottom of the ribs of a young pig, but do not strip the bone
2. Pound it a little, sprinkle it with salt and pepper
3. Lay it in a roasting pan, pour a glass of vinegar and a glass of water over it
4. Let it braise until tender
5. Serve it with sauerkraut or beans
