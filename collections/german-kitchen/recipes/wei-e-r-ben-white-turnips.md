## Ingredients

- White turnips
- Fat
- Flour
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 2 tablespoons sweet cream

## Directions

1. Scrape the turnips, cut them fine and long like noodles, wash them, and braise them in a casserole with a piece of fat until soft.
2. Dust a little flour over them and pour 2 tablespoons of sour cream and the same amount of sweet cream over them.
