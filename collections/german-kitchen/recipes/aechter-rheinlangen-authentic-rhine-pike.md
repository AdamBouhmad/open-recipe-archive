## Ingredients

- 1 fish
- salt
- 3 bottles Bordeaux wine
- 1 whole lemon, sliced
- cloves
- whole pepper
- mace
- beef kidney fat
- handful of chopped parsley
- 2 shallot onions
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Clean the fish thoroughly, remove the gills, remove the entrails, rub it strongly with salt
2. Place in a fish kettle with 3 bottles Bordeaux wine, one whole lemon sliced, cloves, whole pepper, and mace, cover tightly and cook slowly
3. Finely chop a piece of beef kidney fat and let it melt in a casserole
4. Add a handful of chopped parsley, 2 shallot onions, and some lemon zest to the fat
5. When the fish is tender, place it on a plate and pour the mixture over it
