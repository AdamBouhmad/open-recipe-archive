## Ingredients

- calf's mesentery
- salt water
- eggs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the calf's mesentery carefully
2. Cook it in salt water
3. Remove the glands
4. Cut it into small pieces
5. Turn them in beaten eggs
6. Then turn them in grated bread
7. Fry them golden brown
