## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 4 lemons
- 1/2 measure of water
- 1 schoppen of wine

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 4 lemons into the sugar
2. Cook the zest together with the lemon juice and 1/2 measure of water thoroughly
3. Add 1 schoppen of wine
4. Strain through a cloth
5. Freeze in the freezing tin according to the method given in No. 767
