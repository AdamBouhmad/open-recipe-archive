## Ingredients

- 1 old hen, cleaned
- 2 calf's feet
- 1 bone
- 1/4 pound grated deer horn

## Directions

1. Chop the cleaned hen finely
2. Add 2 calf's feet and 1 bone
3. Bind the 1/4 pound grated deer horn in a cloth and cook it together with the other ingredients for 2 to 3 hours
4. Strain the broth through a cloth
5. Use as soup
