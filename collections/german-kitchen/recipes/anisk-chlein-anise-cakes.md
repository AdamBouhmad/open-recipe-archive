## Ingredients

- 5 egg whites
- Half pound white sugar
- Pure anise for 2 kreuzer
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat 5 egg whites into stiff snow
2. Add half pound white sugar and stir for half an hour
3. Mix in pure anise and enough flour so the dough no longer runs
4. Line a dark baking sheet with wafers
5. Set small mounds of dough on them
6. Bake at gentle heat in the oven so they remain completely white
