## Ingredients

- 1 small leg of mutton, cut short, 2 kg
- 10 g salt
- 20 g fat
- 10 g flour
- 50 g onions
- 1 spice packet
- 1/4 l water or wheat beer

## Directions

1. Wash the leg, pound it, and rub with salt.
2. Heat the fat strongly, turn the leg in flour, and brown on all sides in the fat. Add onions and spices. Slowly add 1/8 l boiling water, cover the pot, and braise the leg very slowly over gentle heat for 3 hours, basting frequently and adding water as needed. After this time it will be very tender. Remove fat from the sauce and strain through a sieve, coat the leg with it and serve. You can also braise some tomatoes with it and strain through the sauce, or add sour cream or 1 tablespoon cognac.
