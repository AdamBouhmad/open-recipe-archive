## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 90 g sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon sugar
- 3/4 l white or brown beer

## Directions

1. The whole eggs are beaten with the sugar. The beer is heated and then beaten with the egg foam and lemon sugar over gentle heat with the whisk and arranged for serving immediately.
