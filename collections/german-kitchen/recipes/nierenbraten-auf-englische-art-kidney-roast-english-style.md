## Ingredients

- kidney roast
- milk
- cloves
- white peppercorns
- mace
- thyme
- basil
- bay leaves
- onions, sliced
- yellow carrots
- parsley
- parsnip
- shallots
- various roots
- flour
- salt
- butter
- clear jus

## Directions

1. Place the kidney roast in a casserole and cover completely with milk.
2. Add cloves, white peppercorns, mace, thyme, basil, several bay leaves, sliced onions, yellow carrots, parsley, parsnip, shallots, and various other roots.
3. Let the roast stand overnight with the herbs.
4. The next day, 2 hours before serving, remove it from the milk and roast it on a spit.
5. Sprinkle it with flour and salt, and baste it frequently with butter so it achieves a beautiful color.
6. Place it on a platter and pour clear jus over it.
