## Ingredients

- 6 Loth lard
- 3 quarter-pound peeled almonds, finely ground
- zest of 2 lemons, grated on a grater
- half a pound finely ground sugar
- quarter-pound fine flour
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Stir the lard in a bowl until it is completely white
2. Grind the peeled almonds finely
3. Add the grated lemon zest and half a pound of finely ground sugar
4. Mix with a quarter-pound of fine flour for half an hour, stirring well
5. Butter a springform pan
6. Put two-thirds of the mixture into it and coat with eggs
7. Make a grid pattern from the remaining third of the mixture and place it on top
8. Coat the grid with eggs as well
9. Bake in the oven until beautifully golden
10. When the cake comes out of the oven, place it on the plate on which it will be served to the table
11. Fill the grid openings with some preserves
