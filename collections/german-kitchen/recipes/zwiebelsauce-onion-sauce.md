## Ingredients

- Two large handfuls of shallot onions
- A piece of butter
- Some sugar
- Three finger-widths of flour
- Vinegar
- Meat broth
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Steam the shallot onions in a small casserole with a piece of butter and some sugar until they turn yellow
2. Sprinkle three finger-widths of flour over the onions
3. Add vinegar, meat broth, and pepper
4. Cook everything together until time to serve
