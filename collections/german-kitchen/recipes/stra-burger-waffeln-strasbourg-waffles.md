## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 6 eggs
- 10 loth flour
- lukewarm milk
- 1 knife-point of salt

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 pound butter until foamy and gradually beat in 6 eggs.
2. Make a thin dough from 10 loth flour with lukewarm milk and 1 knife-point of salt.
3. Stir it under the butter for a quarter hour.
4. Bake the waffles as usual on both sides and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
