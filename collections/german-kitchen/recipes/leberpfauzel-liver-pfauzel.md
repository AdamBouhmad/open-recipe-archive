## Ingredients

- calf's liver, cleaned and skinned
- bacon, half pound
- bread
- water
- onions, 2, finely chopped and braised in fat

## Directions

1. Take a clean, skinned, and washed calf's liver, place it on a cutting board, and grind it very finely with half a pound of bacon.
2. Grate bread worth 2 kreuzer on a grater, soak in water, and wring out.
3. Put the ground liver in a bowl, add the bread, and 2 finely chopped onions braised in fat.
