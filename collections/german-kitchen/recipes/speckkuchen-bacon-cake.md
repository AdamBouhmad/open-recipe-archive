## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- good beer yeast
- milk
- quarter pound butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 knife points salt
- green bacon
- salt
- caraway

## Directions

1. Put one pound flour in a bowl
2. Make a small dough from good beer yeast with milk and let it rise at gentle warmth
3. Stir a quarter pound butter lightly
4. Add 4 egg yolks and 2 knife points salt
5. Add the small dough to the butter with flour
6. Work the dough until it throws bubbles
7. Put it back in the bowl and let it rise
8. When ripe, take it to the pastry board
9. Roll it out 2 fingers thick
10. Fold it in 2 fingers wide at the edge
11. Brush it with beaten egg
12. Cut green bacon into cubes
13. Mix it with salt and caraway
14. Sprinkle it over the cake
15. Coat a sheet of paper with butter
16. Lay the cake on it
17. Put it on a black baking sheet
18. Bake it nicely yellow in the oven
