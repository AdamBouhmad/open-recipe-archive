## Ingredients

- 8 loth chocolate
- 3 tablespoons water
- 2 loth butter
- 3 loth finely ground almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter for the dish

## Directions

1. Melt 8 loth chocolate in a brass pan with 3 tablespoons water.
2. Add 2 loth butter and 3 loth finely ground almonds and let them cook together a little.
3. Put the mixture into a bowl and stir in 6 egg yolks one after another.
4. Beat the egg white to a snow.
5. Brush a dish with butter, put the mass in it, place it in the oven, and let it bake nicely. It will be done in about 15 minutes.
