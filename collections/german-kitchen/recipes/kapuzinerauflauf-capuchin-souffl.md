## Ingredients

- 2 finely cut white rolls
- a glass of boiling wine
- 1 pound butter
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 4 rose apples
- 1/4 pound citrons and raisins
- all kinds of cooked fruits
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Moisten 2 finely cut white rolls with a glass of boiling wine
2. Stir 1 pound of butter until foamy and add the bread with 1/4 pound of sugar grated on a lemon, finely ground
3. Peel 4 rose apples, cut them into small thin slices
4. Add 1/4 pound of citrons and raisins and all kinds of cooked fruits
5. Add 6 egg yolks and finally fold in the snow from the egg whites
6. Mix the whole mass well together and bake the soufflé on a buttered baking sheet in the oven
