## Ingredients

- good peeled bergamot pears or Zweibutzer pears (Lemon pears are best)
- half pound sugar per 2 pounds pulp
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled pears in a pan in water until soft
2. When the water is completely evaporated, force them through a hair sieve
3. Put the preserve back in a pan (for 2 pounds of pulp add half a pound of sugar)
4. Cook it until it is thick, then let it cool
5. Store it in wooden boxes
