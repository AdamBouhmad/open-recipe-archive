## Ingredients

- 3 soaked and squeezed rolls
- one quarter pound of butter
- crayfish butter
- 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- spinach juice

## Directions

1. Grate 3 soaked and squeezed rolls, each separately in a bowl
2. Lightly beat each with one quarter pound of butter in a bowl (one bowl must have crayfish butter)
3. Beat 3 eggs, salt, and nutmeg into each bowl
4. Color the first bowl white, the second (with crayfish butter) red, and the third with previously described spinach juice green
5. Brush a tin mold with butter
6. Fill in the white mixture and spread it evenly
7. Fill in the crayfish mixture, then the green
8. Seal the mold and cook everything for one hour
9. Cut into slices and place on crayfish soup
