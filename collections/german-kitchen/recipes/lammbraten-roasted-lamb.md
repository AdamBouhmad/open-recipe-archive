## Ingredients

- One lamb
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Jus
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Skin the lamb and cut off the head, feet, and breast as you would with hare.
2. Skin it and lard it where it has no fat.
3. Rub with salt and pepper.
4. Arrange it nicely and let it roast golden in a braising pan with onions.
5. Pour a little jus over it, baste frequently with jus, and squeeze lemon juice over it.
6. Lamb roasts are most flavorful in spring, at Easter time.
