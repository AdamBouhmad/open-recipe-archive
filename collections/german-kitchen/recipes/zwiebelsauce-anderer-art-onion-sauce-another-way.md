## Ingredients

- A piece of lard
- About 1 Loth of sugar
- One handful of finely cut onions
- Two tablespoons of flour
- One ladle-full of meat broth
- Good vinegar
- Juice and yolk of half a lemon
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Heat a piece of lard in a casserole until very hot
2. Add about 1 Loth of sugar and let it turn dark brown over the heat
3. Throw in one handful of finely cut onions and brown them very dark
4. Stir in two tablespoons of flour, one ladle-full of meat broth, and some good vinegar
5. Press everything through a hair sieve
6. Add the juice and yolk of half a lemon to the strained mixture
7. Season with pepper, cloves, and a bay leaf
