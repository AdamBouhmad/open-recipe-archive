## Ingredients

- Marrowfat peas
- Onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Meat broth
- Rice

## Directions

1. Shell the marrowfat peas and braise with onion, parsley, and butter until soft
2. Stir in one tablespoon of flour and add some meat broth
3. Let cook for a quarter hour or until soft, then press through a fine sieve
4. Prepare the rice as in recipe No. 35
5. Arrange both together
