## Ingredients

- one calf's liver
- meat broth
- half a pound boiled rice
- a ladleful of meat broth

## Directions

1. Skin a calf's liver and remove the veins from it
2. Cook it in meat broth for one hour
3. When it has cooled, grate it on a grater
4. Cook it half soft in a casserole together with half a pound boiled rice and a ladleful of meat broth
