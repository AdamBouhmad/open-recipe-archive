## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls of sugar
- 2 spoonfuls of thick sour cream
- 6 loth of fine flour
- hot lard for frying
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, sugar, and sour cream in a bowl with the fine flour to make a dough
2. Roll out the dough, fold it over itself, then roll it out again to the thickness of a knife back
3. Cut square pieces from the dough
4. Make a slit in each piece
5. Fry them in hot lard until golden yellow
6. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
