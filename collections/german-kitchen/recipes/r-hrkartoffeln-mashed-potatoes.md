## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) potatoes
- 1/4 l water
- 1/2 l whole milk
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cook them like salted potatoes.
2. Mash the cooked potatoes very finely or pass them through a sieve.
3. Beat them very hot with milk and salt, which is added gradually, until they reach a boil.
4. If desired, a small piece of butter can be beaten in.
