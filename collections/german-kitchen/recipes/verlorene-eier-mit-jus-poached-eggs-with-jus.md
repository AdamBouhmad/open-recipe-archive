## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- vinegar
- strong jus
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. In a brass pan with half water and half vinegar, when the water boils, carefully beat fresh eggs into it.
2. Remove them with a slotted spoon, place them in cold water, and continue until the eggs are done.
3. When serving, remove the eggs, trim them, place them on the plate for serving, pour a slotted spoonful of strong jus over them, let them come together, and place pepper on top.
4. The water must be salted.
