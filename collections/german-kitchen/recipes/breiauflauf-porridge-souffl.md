## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 4 loth butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 4 loth raisins
- 5 egg whites
- butter for the dish

## Directions

1. Cook a child's porridge from 1/2 maas milk.
2. Lightly stir it with 4 loth butter in a bowl.
3. Add 5 egg yolks one after another.
4. Add 4 loth sugar with a lemon zest grated into it, finely ground.
5. Mix in 4 loth raisins and the snow from 5 egg whites.
6. Brush a porcelain bowl with butter, fill the soufflé into it, and bake at low heat.
