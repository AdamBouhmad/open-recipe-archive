## Ingredients

- broccoli peas
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- 6 young chickens
- flour
- sugar
- consommé

## Directions

1. Steam the broccoli peas in a casserole with butter, a handful of chopped parsley and onion for half an hour
2. Steam 6 cleaned young chickens for half an hour
3. Pour the chicken juice over the broccoli peas
4. Add a small spoon of flour and 2 loths of sugar
5. Bring to a boil together
6. Cut the breast and legs from the chickens
7. Arrange the broccoli peas on a platter and garnish with the chicken pieces
8. Brush with melted consommé
