## Ingredients

- bread
- boiling water
- lard
- onions
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut very fine slices of bread, lay them in the bowl
2. Pour boiling water over them
3. Let the soup slowly come to a boil
4. Finish it with hot lard in which onions have been roasted golden beforehand
