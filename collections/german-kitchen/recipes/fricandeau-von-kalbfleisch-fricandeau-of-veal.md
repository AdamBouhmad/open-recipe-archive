## Ingredients

- Veal shank
- Butter
- Onions
- Carrots
- Celery
- Bay leaves
- Lemon
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Beef broth
- Flour
- Jus

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal shank, cut it along the grain, pound it, remove the skin and lard it.
2. Place it in lukewarm water so it stays white.
3. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole and add chopped onions, carrots, celery, bay leaves and lemon.
4. Place the meat in the casserole.
5. Pour in a glass of wine, some vinegar and beef broth.
6. Braise for one hour.
7. Remove the meat and let the sauce turn dark yellow.
8. Sprinkle a little flour and pour in a little broth or jus.
9. Let it cook together for another quarter hour.
10. Strain the broth through a fine sieve, pour it back into the casserole and add the meat.
11. Let it boil a little longer and add the zest and juice of half a lemon.
12. When serving, add lemon slices.
