## Ingredients

- 200 g white beans
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 g salt
- 30 g bacon
- 1 medium onion
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- vinegar to taste
- 30 g flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare: Soak the beans the evening before; dice the bacon and onion; prepare 1/2 liter water for the sauce and season with syrup, vinegar, pepper and salt.
2. Cook the beans with 1 pinch salt and cold, soft water, blanch, drain and cook covered slowly and tender with 1-1/2 liters water and 8 g salt. Meanwhile, fry the bacon cubes in an earthenware pot, remove the cracklings and keep them warm, let the sugar brown in the fat, add the diced onions to the fat and let them brown, as well as the flour. Add the liquid slowly with stirring and it must cook thick with the brown flour. Drain the tender beans, mix with the finished sauce, taste again, pour into a bowl, sprinkle the cracklings over and serve.
