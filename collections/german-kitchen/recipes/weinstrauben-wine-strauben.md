## Ingredients

- 1 schopen wine
- 2 lots butter
- A pinch of cinnamon
- 12 lots flour
- 2 lots sugar
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 schopen wine, 2 lots butter, a pinch of cinnamon, 12 lots flour and 2 lots sugar until smooth
2. Thin the dough with egg white so it runs well
3. Brush the strauben funnel with hot lard
4. Let the dough run through it into a pan with hot lard
5. Bake the strauben yellow, turn them over and bake on the other side
6. Curl them over a wooden stick and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
