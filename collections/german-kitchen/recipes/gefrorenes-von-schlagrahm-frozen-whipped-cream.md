## Ingredients

- 1 Maas cream
- 8 Loth sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Simmer 1 Maas cream with 8 Loth sugar
2. Stir 10 egg yolks smooth in a pot with sour cream
3. Pour the boiling cream slowly to the eggs
4. Let it draw once more in the pan
5. Force it through a hair sieve into another dish
6. Let it cool
7. Place it in the freezing bucket and proceed according to the rule given in No. 767
