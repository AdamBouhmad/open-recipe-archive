## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 woodcock, cleaned and larded with bacon
- butter
- onions
- heart, liver and cleaned intestines of woodcock
- half a lemon zest and pulp
- pepper
- nutmeg flower
- half a nutmeg
- salt
- 4 handfuls flour
- 12 loth butter
- 12 loth lard
- 2 egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Braise 3 to 4 cleaned and bacon-larded woodcock with a piece of butter and some onions until half-cooked
2. Finely chop the heart, liver, cleaned intestines, half a lemon zest and pulp
3. Remove the woodcock from the casserole, turn the chopped mixture in it a few times, remove from heat
4. Add a knife-tip each of pounded pepper, nutmeg flower, half a nutmeg and salt
5. Make the dough: work together 4 handfuls flour, 12 loth butter, 12 loth lard, 2 egg yolks and a little salt into firm dough on a board
6. Roll out finger-thick and make a pastry of desired shape
7. Place the woodcock on it, pour the woodcock preparation inside
8. Cover with a lid made from the same dough
9. Brush the pastry with egg and bake in the oven
