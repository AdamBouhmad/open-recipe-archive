## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Bacon, cut into pieces
- 2 large onions, finely cut
- A piece of hare, wild duck, or smoked meat
- Sour cream
- Flour
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Press the sauerkraut firmly and place it in a casserole with a piece of cut bacon, 2 large finely cut onions, and a piece of hare, wild duck, or smoked meat.
2. Braise everything together; if the sauerkraut runs out of juice, sour cream must be poured over it.
3. A quarter hour before serving, dust 3 fingers' worth of flour and 1 knife-tip of pepper over the sauerkraut and serve it with the meat.
