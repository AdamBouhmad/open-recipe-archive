## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) raw potatoes, peeled and weighed
- 5 g salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon potato starch
- 1/8 l (scant) whole milk
- 1 egg white
- 50 g fat or 60 g margarine

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and place in water. Grate them very quickly on a grater into a bowl with some cold water to preserve the color. Pour the mass onto a cloth, press out the moisture lightly, and stir the paste with salt, potato starch, milk, and egg yolk. Fold in the stiff egg white foam.
2. Heat plenty of pork lard or rendered bacon with the cracklings removed, mixed fat, or margarine in the pan. Spoon the mass with a small ladle into the steaming fat and spread as thin as possible to about the size of a small plate. Turn the pancakes often, fry them brown and crispy, and arrange on a hot plate. The above ingredients should yield 6 pieces.
