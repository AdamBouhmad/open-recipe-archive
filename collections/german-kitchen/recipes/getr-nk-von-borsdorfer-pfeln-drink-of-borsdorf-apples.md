## Ingredients

- 4 Borsdorf apples
- 1 Maas water
- peel of 1 lemon (the yellow)
- a handful of large and small raisins

## Directions

1. Peel and cut 4 Borsdorf apples into 4 parts
2. Pour 1 Maas water over them
3. Let this simmer with the yellow of 1 lemon and a handful of large and small raisins for one hour
4. Pour everything through a linen cloth
5. Let it cool and give it to drink
