## Ingredients

- brain
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- 2 eggs
- bread slices
- wheat meal
- egg white
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the brain
2. Steam the brain in a piece of butter with onions and parsley
3. Mix everything with 2 eggs
4. Spread the mixture on bread slices
5. Sprinkle wheat meal on top
6. Cover with egg white
7. Fry the slices in hot lard
