## Ingredients

- pears
- boiling water
- 1/2 lemon peel
- cinnamon stick
- 6 cloves

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and cut them in half
2. Pour enough boiling water over them to cover by a finger's height
3. Cook them soft with half a lemon peel, a whole cinnamon stick, and 6 cloves
4. Form 2 to 3 artichokes on a plate from the pears
5. Reduce the syrup a bit more
6. Strain it through a hair sieve over the pears
