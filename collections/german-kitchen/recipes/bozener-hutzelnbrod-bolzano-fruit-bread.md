## Ingredients

- 1 vierling medlars
- 1 pound raisins
- 1 pound wine grapes
- 2 pounds pitted and cleaned plums
- 12 figs
- 1 pound blanched and finely cut almonds
- 2 loth cinnamon
- 1/2 loth cloves
- 6 kreuzer cubeb and cardamom
- 1 pound citron and bitter orange peel
- 3 handfuls flour
- water

## Directions

1. Cook medlars until very soft and cut into small pieces
2. Remove pits and stems
3. Mix together raisins, wine grapes, plums, figs, almonds, spices, citron, and orange peel thoroughly
4. Place flour on a pastry board and make a firm dough with water
5. Roll out very thick as a knife back
6. Spread the fruit mixture inside
7. Form into a round loaf no more than 4 fingers high
8. Press lightly and place in the oven
9. Bake until golden
10. For the icing: stir egg whites, finely ground sugar, and lemon juice into a thick icing
11. Coat the bread thickly with the icing
12. Garnish with candied fruits as desired
