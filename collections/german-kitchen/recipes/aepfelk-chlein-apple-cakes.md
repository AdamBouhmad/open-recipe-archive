## Ingredients

- flour
- wine
- 6 tablespoons hot lard
- apple slices for baking

## Directions

1. Make a thin dough (Flädchenteig) from flour and wine
2. Stir in 6 tablespoons hot lard, one after another
3. If the dough becomes too thick, help it along with wine
4. Dip apple slices in the dough
5. Fry very slowly in hot lard with frequent shaking
6. Lay on bread slices to drain
7. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
8. Serve very hot
