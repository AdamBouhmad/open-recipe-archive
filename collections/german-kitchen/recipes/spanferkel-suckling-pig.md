## Ingredients

- suckling pig, 4 to 5 weeks old
- salt
- white pepper
- brown beer, half a maas
- bacon
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape the suckling pig thoroughly and remove the entrails with the spleen.
2. Make 3 to 4 cuts on the forehead and thread the spleen through them.
3. Rub it with salt and white pepper.
4. Roast it on a spit if possible.
5. Heat half a maas of brown beer with a piece of bacon in a casserole and pour this broth over the pig.
6. Be careful that it does not blister; to prevent this, make holes in it with a large carving fork.
7. Dress the feet and place a smaller dish under the roast to catch drippings.
8. Cut an onion into the platter.
9. When the pig is done, make a cut in its neck so it roasts nicely.
10. Serve the jus separately.
11. With good heat, a suckling pig will be done in one hour.
