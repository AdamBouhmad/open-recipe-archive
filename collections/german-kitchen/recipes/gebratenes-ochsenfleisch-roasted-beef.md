## Ingredients

- A rib piece or a piece from the breast, or generally a good, juicy piece of beef, preferably the thick flap
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onions
- Lemons
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Meat broth or water
- Butter paper (if no spit is available)
- Madeira wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Rub the meat with salt and pepper
2. If using a spit, roast it on the spit, or if not available, wrap it in butter paper
3. Place the meat in a roasting pan with onions, lemons, bay leaves, cloves, and pepper
4. Pour in a glass of wine, half a glass of vinegar, and half a ladle-full of meat broth or water
5. Roast until beautifully browned, frequently basting with the sauce
6. Half an hour before serving, drain the broth and skim off the fat, then return it to the roasting pan
7. Optionally, add a glass of Madeira wine
8. Let it come to a boil once more and arrange on a platter
9. Serve the sauce separately
