## Ingredients

- Apples of equal size
- 1 piece butter
- 1 piece sugar
- 2 beaten eggs
- Finely grated bread
- 10 loth almonds in the shell
- 1/4 pound crushed sugar
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 knife-tip crushed cloves
- 4 loth grated black bread
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel apples and remove the core
2. Steam them halfway done in a piece of butter and a piece of sugar
3. When cold, turn them in 2 beaten eggs, then in finely grated bread
4. Place in a cake sheet and bake until golden in the oven
5. Crush 10 loth almonds in the shell very fine, add 1/4 pound crushed sugar, 1 nutmeg, 1 knife-tip crushed cloves, 4 loth grated black bread
6. Beat 10 egg yolks in and stir everything together as strongly as a cake mixture
7. Finally add the snow from 10 egg whites
8. Place the apples on the plate, fill the beaten mixture on top and let it bake golden in the oven
