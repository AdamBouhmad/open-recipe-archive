## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions
- Yellow turnips
- 3 pounds lean beef
- 1 head white cabbage
- Water
- Meat broth
- 1 half Maas water (for dumplings)
- Butter the size of an egg (for dumplings)
- Fine flour (for dumplings)
- 5 to 6 eggs (for dumplings)
- 2 knife points of salt (for dumplings)
- Lard (for frying dumplings)

## Directions

1. Place a piece of butter, cut onions and yellow turnips in a casserole
2. Cut 3 pounds of lean beef into small pieces and brown them nicely in the casserole
3. Add a head of white cabbage cut up
4. Pour a ladle full of water over it
5. When it is brown, cover the casserole for a little while
6. Pour in meat broth and let it cook 2 to 3 hours
7. Pass it through a hair sieve into another casserole and let it come to a boil
8. For the fried peas: bring half a Maas water to a boil in a brass pan with a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
9. Stir in fine flour until it becomes a thick paste
10. Transfer to a bowl and beat in 5 to 6 eggs
11. Add 2 knife points of salt
12. Force this dough through a sparrow sieve into hot lard and fry the peas until nicely yellow
