## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound peeled almonds
- 1/4 pound almonds with rose water
- 12 Loth sugar
- 10 Loth flour
- 1 Quint pounded cinnamon
- 1 Quint cloves
- 1 lemon
- 1 knife-tip potassium carbonate
- 2 to 3 eggs
- wafers (Oblaten)

## Directions

1. Cut 1/4 pound peeled almonds as finely as possible
2. Pound 1/4 pound almonds with rose water as finely as possible
3. Mix both together in a bowl
4. Add 12 Loth sugar, 10 Loth flour, 1 Quint pounded cinnamon, 1 Quint cloves, the finely chopped yolk of 1 lemon, and 1 knife-tip potassium carbonate
5. Beat in 2 to 3 eggs to form a dough
6. Roll out finger-thick and cut into finger-length pieces
7. Flatten slightly with the knife
8. Let them stand on wafers on a black sheet for a quarter hour
9. Bake in the oven
