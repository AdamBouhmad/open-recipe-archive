## Ingredients

- Good, ripe melons
- Good wine vinegar
- Sugar (1 half pound per maas of vinegar)
- 1 quint coarsely ground cinnamon
- 8 to 10 whole cloves
- A piece of whole ginger

## Directions

1. Rub good, ripe melons with a cloth
2. Cut them into slices
3. Lay them in a bowl
4. Pour good wine vinegar over them
5. Let them stand for 3 days
6. Pour the vinegar into a brass pan (measuring 1 maas vinegar to 1 half pound sugar)
7. Cook 1 quint coarsely ground cinnamon, 8 to 10 whole cloves, and a piece of whole ginger together with the melons until soft
8. Let them stand overnight in a porcelain bowl
9. The next day put them on the fire again
10. Cook them completely through
11. Let them cool
12. Preserve them like other preserved goods and store in a dry place
13. Serve with roasted poultry
