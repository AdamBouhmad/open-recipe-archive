## Ingredients

- apricots (not overripe)
- boiling water
- sugar (equal weight to apricots)

## Directions

1. Place apricots in a dish and pour boiling water over them
2. Remove the skin and dry on a cloth
3. In a saucepan or brass pan, cook sugar and water until the sugar forms bubbles
4. With a silver spoon, add the apricots and cook as long as hard-boiled eggs
5. Remove with the same spoon to a clean dish
6. Let the syrup cook down somewhat, pour over the apricots and let stand overnight
7. The next day place again in the pan, cook briefly, carefully remove the apricots, cook the syrup down again, and pour over the apricots
8. Repeat this process for 4 days in succession
9. On the fourth day, place the apricots in the dish where they will be stored, draw paper through cherry brandy, place it on top, prick holes in the paper, tie the dish closed and place in a dry location
