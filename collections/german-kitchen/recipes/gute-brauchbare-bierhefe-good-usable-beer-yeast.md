## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 pounds malt (wheat malt is best)
- 1 pound hops
- 40 to 50 maas water
- 1 pound good yeast
- 3 pounds good wheat flour
- half a schoppen brandy

## Directions

1. Bring 10 to 12 pounds malt (wheat malt is best) together with 1 pound hops into a vat or barrel with a double bottom
2. Let 40 to 50 maas water boil in a kettle and brew the malt with it
3. Let it stand 24 hours, noting that the second bottom must have holes, and draw off the liquid from below
4. Stir 1 pound good yeast under the drawn-off liquid
5. Mix 3 pounds good wheat flour and half a schoppen brandy together in an earthenware pot and combine this with the above
6. This yeast keeps well for 4 to 5 months
