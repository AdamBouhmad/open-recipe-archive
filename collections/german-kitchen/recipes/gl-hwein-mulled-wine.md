## Ingredients

- 1/2 bottle red wine, preferably Burgundy
- 2 g cinnamon
- 4 cloves
- 80 g sugar
- 1/2 finely peeled lemon peel

## Directions

1. All ingredients are mixed, heated on the fire until simmering, poured through a hair sieve and served hot.
