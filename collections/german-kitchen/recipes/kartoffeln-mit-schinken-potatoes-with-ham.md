## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- milk
- half pound ham
- half a pint sour cream
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and place them in a casserole with a piece of butter
2. Pour some milk over them and let them braise soft
3. Chop half a pound ham and add it to the potatoes
4. Add half a pint of sour cream, salt, and nutmeg
5. Shake everything together (but do not stir it) and let it finish cooking
