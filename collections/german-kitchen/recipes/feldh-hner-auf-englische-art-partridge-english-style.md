## Ingredients

- partridges, plucked and cleaned
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- melted butter
- lemons

## Directions

1. Lard the partridge breasts.
2. Mix salt, pepper, and cloves well together and rub into the birds.
3. On a grill, braise half-done with melted butter.
4. Cool, then cut out the breasts and legs neatly.
5. Turn in melted butter.
6. Braise again on the grill until golden.
7. Top with lemons.
8. Serve with sauerkraut, winter cabbage, or blue cabbage.
