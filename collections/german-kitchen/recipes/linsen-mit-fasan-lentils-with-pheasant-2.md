## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- 1/8 good Bohemian lentils
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Roast the pheasant until half-done
2. Cook the cleaned and washed lentils in salt water until soft
3. Press through a strainer
4. Add a glass of vinegar
5. Put half the lentils in a bowl, place the pheasant with the breast, shanks, and legs cut away on top, and cover with the remaining half of the lentils
