## Ingredients

- 375 g pumpkin
- 1 l water
- 2 cloves
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 1 piece lemon zest
- 15 g flour
- 15 g margarine
- 5 g salt
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 50 g sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin, cut into small pieces and wash.
2. Cook the pumpkin with water and spices until soft and press through a hair sieve.
3. Sweat flour and margarine together until it foams.
4. Slowly add the pumpkin puree, let the soup cook through and season with salt, vinegar and sugar.
5. Optionally add white bread cut into small cubes and roasted in fat or margarine.
6. Alternatively, add 40 g of soaked rice to the soup, then preparation in a slow cooker is recommended.
