## Ingredients

- Hare meat, cleaned and skinned
- 1 pound lard
- Several shallot onions
- Basil
- Thyme
- A little lemon zest
- Red wine
- Pepper
- Salt
- Bratwurst casings

## Directions

1. Chop the hare meat, lard, onions, basil, thyme, and lemon zest very finely
2. Pour a small glass of red wine over it
3. Add pepper and salt
4. Mix everything well together
5. Fill into bratwurst casings
6. Before serving, fry them like bratwurst sausages, but not too dry to avoid them becoming brittle
