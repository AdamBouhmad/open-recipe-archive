## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) fat
- 1/8 liter water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef fat into small cubes and soak in water for a day or night so any blood particles can be removed
2. Drain the water, place the fat in a braising pot and pour 1/8 liter water over 1/2 kg (1 pound) fat, cover and let it cook slowly
3. The water should loosen the cells so they release the fat better
4. Once the fat becomes transparent in the heat, uncover it and let it braise openly until the cracklings are evenly browned
5. Let the fat cool somewhat, then strain through a sieve
6. Gently press out the cracklings and set aside covered for use in potato soup, legumes, or finely chopped for meatballs, beefsteak, etc.
7. Store the rendered fat in clean tied stone pots
8. For mutton fat: render the same way; when the fat becomes glassy, add 1 cup of milk, which reduces the mutton flavor
