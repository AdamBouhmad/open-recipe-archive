## Ingredients

- short butter dough
- red currants or raspberries
- egg
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roll out a short butter dough knife-back thick and lay a piece in the waffle iron.
2. Lay a piece of paper on this dough and bake the dough on one side.
3. In the same way, bake another piece of dough on one side.
4. Coat both pieces on the baked side with egg.
5. Lay a tablespoon of red currants or raspberries on the unbaked side of the first piece.
6. Fit the unbaked side of the other piece on top; bring both pieces together with their filling into the waffle iron once more, bake a little more, and then sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
