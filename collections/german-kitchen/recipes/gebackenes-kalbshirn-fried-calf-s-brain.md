## Ingredients

- calf's brain
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the brain
2. Place it on the fire with half water, half vinegar, and salt
3. Cook for one hour
4. Remove it and let it drain
5. Turn it in beaten eggs
6. Then turn it in grated bread
7. Fry it golden brown in hot lard
