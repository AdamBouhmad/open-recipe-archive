## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound pounded almonds
- 9 tablespoons rose water
- 4 tablespoons cherry spirits
- 9 egg whites
- 1/4 pound fine flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter white and foamy in a bowl.
2. Stir in 9 egg yolks, one after another, 1/4 pound pounded almonds, 9 tablespoons rose water, and 4 tablespoons cherry spirits.
3. Beat the whites of 9 eggs to a stiff snow, mix with 1/4 pound fine flour, and combine with the previous mixture.
4. Add the zest of 1/2 lemon grated on a grater.
5. Bake golden in a waffle iron and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
