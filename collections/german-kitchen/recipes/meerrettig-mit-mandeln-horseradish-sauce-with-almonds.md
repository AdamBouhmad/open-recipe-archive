## Ingredients

- good grated horseradish
- 2 loths of blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 2 loths of butter
- 1 loth of sugar
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Boil the grated horseradish and almonds in a casserole with the butter, sugar, and cream or milk
