## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- various roots and onions
- hot lard
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- jus
- vinegar or wine
- sour cream, half a schopp
- flour, 3 finger-widths

## Directions

1. Remove the shank bone and end bone from the haunch, skin it well, and lard it.
2. Place it on a plafond, cut various roots and onions alongside it.
3. Pour hot lard over it, add salt, pepper, and cloves.
4. Roast until beautifully golden, then add a little jus, a glass of vinegar or wine, and baste it frequently.
5. Mix together half a schopp of good thick sour cream and 3 finger-widths of flour.
6. Add this mixture with a little jus to the roast and bring to a boil again.
7. Strain the sauce through a hair sieve and pour it over the roast.
