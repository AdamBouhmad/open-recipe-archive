## Ingredients

- Boiled carp roe
- Pike liver
- Crayfish tails
- Mushrooms
- Morels
- Shallot onions
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut boiled carp roe, pike liver, and crayfish tails into small diced pieces
2. Steam mushrooms and morels, finely cut, with shallot onions, parsley, and thyme in a piece of butter for several minutes
3. Mix the above with it
4. Add salt, pepper, and a few tablespoons of good
