## Ingredients

- 8-15 kohlrabi, depending on size
- 10 g salt
- 1 l water for blanching
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 1/8 l water
- 1 pinch salt
- 8 g flour
- 1 pinch pepper
- Kohlrabi greens

## Directions

1. Set 1 l water with 10 g salt to boil; wash the young greens of the kohlrabi; peel the bulbs and cut into uniform slices.
2. Add the kohlrabi slices to the boiling salt water, blanch uncovered, drain, cook soft in a pot with 1/8 l water, salt and 3/4 of the fat. Blanch the young greens also, drain and mince finely. Sweat the remaining fat on a saucer with the flour, slowly stir three tablespoons kohlrabi broth to it; bring the flour roux, kohlrabi slices and greens back to a boil together and season with pepper before serving.
