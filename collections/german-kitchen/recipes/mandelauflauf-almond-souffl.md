## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound peeled almonds
- milk
- 3 tablespoons fine flour
- 4 Loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 Schoppen milk
- 4 Loth butter
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pound 1/4 pound of peeled almonds very finely in a mortar with milk
2. Stir smooth in a pan with 3 tablespoons of fine flour
3. Add 4 Loth of sugar grated on a lemon
4. Pour in 1 Schoppen of milk and cook a not too thick porridge
5. Cut in 4 Loth of butter and stir in 6 egg yolks
6. Beat the egg whites to snow and mix in
7. Cook everything well together
8. Butter a bowl and fill the mass into it
9. Bake in a not very hot oven and sprinkle sugar on top
