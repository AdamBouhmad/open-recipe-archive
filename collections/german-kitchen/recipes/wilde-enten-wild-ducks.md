## Ingredients

- Wild duck
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Bacon
- Lemon
- Pork fat
- Onions
- Carrots
- Bay leaves
- Tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Pluck the duck, remove the skin, cut off the head and neck
2. Soak it in cold water for 3 days, changing the water daily
3. Dry it carefully with a cloth
4. Rub it with salt, pepper, cloves, and nutmeg
5. Bind it with bacon and lemon
6. Braise it in a covered casserole with pork fat, onions, carrots, bay leaves, and a little tarragon vinegar for half an hour
7. Uncover the casserole and roast the duck until golden yellow
8. Add the juice of half a lemon, arrange the duck on a platter, pour the sauce through a hair sieve over it, and leave the bacon on the duck
9. Note: The sauce should not be treated as with other wild game because the liver and heart are unusable due to their unpleasant fishy moss smell
