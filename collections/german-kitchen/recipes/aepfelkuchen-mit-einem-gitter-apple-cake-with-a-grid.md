## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- butter
- sugar
- quarter-pound raisins and currants
- 2 knife-tips ground cinnamon
- zest of half a lemon, grated
- quarter-pound peeled, finely ground almonds
- quarter-pound butter
- half a pound fine flour
- 3 Loth ground sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- cold water if needed
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook peeled apples in a casserole with a piece of butter, a piece of sugar, a quarter-pound of raisins and currants, 2 knife-tips of ground cinnamon, and the grated zest of half a lemon until very soft
2. Let them cool in a dish
3. Make an almond dough: combine a quarter-pound of peeled, finely ground almonds, a quarter-pound of butter, half a pound of fine flour, 3 Loth of ground sugar, and 3 egg yolks (add a little cold water if necessary)
4. Roll out the dough, fold it over itself, and roll out again
5. Line a buttered cake sheet with the dough
6. Place the apples in it
7. Make a border around the cake as usual
8. Cut 12 finger-wide strips from the same dough (reserved for this purpose)
9. Lay these strips crosswise like a grid over the cake
10. Coat with eggs
11. Bake
