## Ingredients

- Speckschwarten (smoked pork rind)
- water

## Directions

1. Brush the rind clean with water or rub together in water.
2. Soak in cold water for one night.
3. Cook the rinds, using 250 g per 1 liter of water, covered, until soft.
4. Use the broth for legume soups; cut the soft rind into small pieces and add to the soup.
