## Ingredients

- 125 g Margarine
- 125 g Pork lard
- 150 g Brown syrup
- 125 g Sugar
- 1-1/8 kg (2-1/4 pounds) Flour
- 60 g Sweet grated almonds or citron cubes
- 5 Bitter almonds
- 10 Crushed cloves
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
- 10 g Chemically pure potash
- 20 g Baking powder
- 2 tablespoons Warm water
- 1 Piece of bacon rind for the baking sheet

## Directions

1. Bring the margarine, lard, syrup, and sugar to a boil and, after cooling, stir in the flour mixed with the spices.
2. Dissolve the potash and baking powder in warm water, strain through muslin, and combine with the dough.
3. Cover the dough and set it in a warm place for 4 days, then roll it out, cut it into desired shapes, and bake it slowly on a greased baking sheet in the oven.
4. Before baking, you can garnish the cakes with almond halves, or after they have cooled, you can brush them with icing.
5. Icing: 3 tablespoons powdered sugar stirred with 2 teaspoons rose water or water. Must not stand long.
