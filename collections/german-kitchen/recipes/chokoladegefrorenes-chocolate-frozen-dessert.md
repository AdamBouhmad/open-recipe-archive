## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound chocolate
- 1-1/2 schoppen of milk
- 1/8 pound sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate in a brass pan
2. Add the milk and sugar to it
3. Let it cook together
4. Beat 8 egg yolks in a pot and stir smooth with cold milk
5. Slowly add the cooked chocolate
6. Set it over the fire again and let it cook briefly
7. Strain through a sieve
8. Let it become completely cold
9. Put it in the freezing tin
