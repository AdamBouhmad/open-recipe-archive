## Ingredients

- Schöne zeitige Kirschen (Beautiful ripe cherries)
- Zucker (Sugar)
- Essig (Vinegar)
- Zimmt (Cinnamon)
- Nelken (Cloves)
- Kirschengeist (Cherry liqueur)

## Directions

1. Cut the stems of beautiful ripe cherries half off strongly
2. In a brass pan, cook 1/4 pound of sugar with as much vinegar as needed for the cherries, half a loth of crushed cinnamon, and some whole cloves together for a quarter hour
3. Remove from heat and let cool
4. When the vinegar is still lukewarm, pour it over the cherries, cover, and let stand for 2 days
5. On the third day, cook the vinegar again in the pan and pour it lukewarm over the cherries again
6. Draw a paper the size needed through cherry liqueur, place it on top of the dish, tie it shut, and store
