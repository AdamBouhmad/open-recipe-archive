## Ingredients

- 125 g rice
- 1/2 l water for blanching
- 1/2 l water
- 5 g fat or margarine
- 20 g large raisins
- 1 piece orange peel
- 5 g salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the picked-over raisins in water and then drain.
2. Add the raisins to the rice, which is prepared as water rice; season the dish with vinegar and sugar.
