## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/4 pound finely ground almonds
- 1 whole lemon, zested on a grater
- 6 eggs
- 2 handfuls fine, grated violin flour
- 3 fingers' worth white flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Whisk together 1/2 pound sugar, 1/4 pound finely ground almonds, 1 lemon grated on a box grater, and 6 eggs for half an hour
2. Mix in 2 handfuls fine grated violin flour and 3 fingers' worth white flour
3. Butter a long metal form and fill with the mixture
4. Bake at gentle heat
5. On the second day, cut the cake into slices and bake them on a black baking sheet in the oven until nicely golden
