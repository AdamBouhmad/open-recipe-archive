## Ingredients

- 60 medium cucumbers
- 50 g grape leaves
- 30 g dill
- 7-1/2 l water
- 100 g salt
- 1 small barrel

## Directions

1. Soak the cucumbers for 24 hours in spring water, wipe them off, and pack them with the washed leaves and dill into a very clean barrel.
2. Bring water and salt to a boil and pour it hot over the cucumbers; repeat this procedure on the following two days.
3. The liquid must cover the cucumbers well.
4. After cooling, bung the barrel and turn it frequently until use.
