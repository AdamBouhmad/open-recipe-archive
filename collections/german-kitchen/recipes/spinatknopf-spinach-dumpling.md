## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls spinach
- Salt water
- Cold water
- 1/2 pound veal or pork roast
- 1/2 lemon
- 2 rolls
- 1 finely minced steamed onion
- 6 loth butter
- 6 egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Snow from 6 egg whites
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- Cold water
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 ladle spoonful meat broth
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream

## Directions

1. Blanch 2 handfuls spinach in salt water
2. Pour it into a strainer and run cold water over it so it remains green
3. Press it firmly and mince it finely
4. Mince 1/2 pound veal or pork roast with 1/2 lemon very finely
5. Rub off 2 rolls, soak and press them
6. Stir 6 loth butter until foamy in a bowl
7. Add the spinach, roast, rolls, steamed onion, 6 egg yolks, salt, nutmeg and the snow from 6 egg whites
8. Bind the mass in a wet cloth and cook for one hour in boiling salt water
9. For the fricassee: stir 1 cooking spoon flour smooth with cold water in a brass pan
10. Beat 2 egg yolks into it
11. Cook 1 ladle spoonful meat broth and 2 spoonfuls sour cream with it
12. Arrange this over the pudding
