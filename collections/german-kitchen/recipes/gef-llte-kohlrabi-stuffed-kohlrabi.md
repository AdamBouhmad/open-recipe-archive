## Ingredients

- beautiful, half-grown, equal-sized kohlrabi
- cold roast
- 1 soaked and squeezed bread roll
- 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- onions
- parsley
- butter
- meat broth
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi, cut away the heart leaf with the lid at the top, and cook until tender.
2. Pour through a sieve and pour cold water over them.
3. Hollow out the kohlrabi.
4. For the filling: mince the cold roast with the soaked and squeezed bread roll, 3 eggs, salt, nutmeg, finely minced onions and parsley, mix together.
5. In a casserole, steam this mixture in a piece of butter.
6. Fill the kohlrabi heads with this mixture.
7. Place in a roasting pan with a little meat broth on coals.
8. Place the cut-off lids back on top.
9. Steam a little longer and make a butter sauce to serve with it.
