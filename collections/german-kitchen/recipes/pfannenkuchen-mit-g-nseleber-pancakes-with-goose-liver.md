## Ingredients

- goose liver
- 6 to 8 shallot onions
- a handful parsley
- half a lemon
- 4 rolls
- 1 Schoppen milk
- butter
- 3 finely chopped shallot onions
- 1 spoonful flour
- 4 eggs
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Chop the goose liver fine, peel 6 to 8 shallot onions and a handful of parsley, steam these together for a while.
2. Add the chopped goose liver and steam everything once more.
3. Press the juice from half a lemon and the chopped yellow from another half lemon to it.
4. Cut 4 rolls as fine as for soup.
5. In a brass pan let 1 Schoppen milk with a piece of butter boil up.
6. Pour this onto the cut rolls, cover and let them stand a little.
7. Take 3 finely chopped shallot onions and 1 spoonful flour to the rolls, also 4 eggs, one after another, and a little salt.
8. Make lard hot in a flat pan, pour half the batter into it.
9. In the middle comes the goose liver, on top the other half of the batter.
10. Bake all like a cake; when done on one side, turn it and bake it on the other side also.
