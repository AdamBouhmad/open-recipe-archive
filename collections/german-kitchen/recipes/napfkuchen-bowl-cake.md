## Ingredients

- 175 g Flour
- 15 g Yeast
- 6 tablespoons Skim milk
- 30 g Beef fat or margarine
- 1 Egg
- 40 g Sugar
- 1 teaspoon Lemon sugar
- 8 g Salt
- 40 g Large and small raisins

## Directions

1. Prepare the Napfkuchen the same way as Hefepudding, but in an earthenware bowl greased with fat, dip beaten egg white and guide it around the edges of the rounds. Fold over the other side of the dough.
2. Using a glass, cut out small round cakes over the marked mounds and let them rise once more on a floured board, then bake them carefully in steaming fat. A wooden stick inserted will show when they are done.
3. The pancakes must not brown too quickly so they bake through completely. When finished baking, place them on a sieve lined with blotting paper and turn them in sugar (and cinnamon if desired).
4. Always bake only a few pancakes at a time. The fillings can be made from any fruit paste. This must not be thin, as it will run out in the heat.
5. Instead of 30 g yeast, 20 g baking powder (1 packet) can also be used.
