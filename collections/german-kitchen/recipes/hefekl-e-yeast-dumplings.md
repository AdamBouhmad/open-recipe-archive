## Ingredients

- 15 g yeast
- 250 g flour
- 1/8 l lukewarm whole milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon melted fat
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 l water

## Directions

1. Prepare a yeast mixture with yeast, one quarter of the flour, and milk; let rise
2. Beat the risen mixture with remaining ingredients until it becomes airy and bubbly
3. Form apple-sized dumplings and place on a flour-dusted board to rise again in a warm place
4. Cook the dumplings in steam or in boiling salted water by placing them in a colander set over boiling water or on a light cloth stretched over the pot
5. Test with a wooden stick to see if dumplings are cooked
6. Remove with a skimmer, tear slightly open with 2 forks, pour brown butter or fried bacon and onions over them as desired, and serve with stewed fruit
