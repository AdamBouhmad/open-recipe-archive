## Ingredients

- 1 carp
- raspberry vinegar
- salt
- water
- 1 piece gingerbread
- a little black bread
- 2 chopped shallot onions
- whole cloves
- pepper
- brown jus
- 1/2 schoppen wine
- 1/2 lemon, yolk

## Directions

1. Scale the carp and carefully remove the entrails
2. Wash it with raspberry vinegar
3. Cut it into desired pieces
4. Boil it in salt water for half an hour
5. Make the sauce as follows: let gingerbread, a little black bread, 2 chopped shallot onions, whole cloves, and pepper cook together with some brown jus and 1/2 schoppen wine for half an hour
6. Push the sauce through a hair sieve, return it to the casserole
7. Add the carp with the juice and the pounded yolk of 1/2 lemon
