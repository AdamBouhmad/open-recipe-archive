## Ingredients

- 6 veal feet with bones removed
- 4 Maas of water
- 3 juicy bitter oranges
- half a pound of sugar
- juice of 3 lemons
- 2 Schoppen of Markgräfler wine

## Directions

1. Cook the veal feet with 4 Maas of water down to 1 Maas, then draw the aspic through a hair sieve and let it stand overnight.
2. The next day, cook the aspic with 3 juicy bitter oranges rubbed with half a pound of sugar, from which the juice and pulp are taken along with the juice of 3 lemons.
3. Skim the fat from the aspic and cook everything together with 2 Schoppen of Markgräfler wine in a brass pan with a lid covered with coals.
4. Let the jelly stand for a quarter hour and pour it through a felt hat into a dish; if the jelly is still not clear enough, let it run through once more.
5. Dip an aspic mold in cold water, pour the jelly into it, place it on ice and turn it out onto a plate.
