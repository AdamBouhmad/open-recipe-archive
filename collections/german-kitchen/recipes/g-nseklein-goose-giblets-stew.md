## Ingredients

- Heart, stomach, head, wings, feet and neck of goose, cut into pieces
- 30 g onion cubes
- 30 g skimmed goose fat
- 1 spice measure
- 30 g flour
- 10 g salt
- 2 tablespoons parsley
- 56 g soup greens
- 1 pinch pepper
- 3/4 l water

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the goose giblets and parts. Cook them covered in water with soup greens and spices until tender, then clean each piece individually.
2. Sauté the onion cubes in goose fat skimmed from the broth until soft, add the flour and cook until done.
3. Pour the goose broth onto the roux and let it reduce by half with the lid off.
4. Season the sauce with salt and pepper, pass it through a sieve, and let the meat become hot in it.
5. Finally add chopped parsley or, if not available, 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram and serve the goose giblets in a deep bowl.
