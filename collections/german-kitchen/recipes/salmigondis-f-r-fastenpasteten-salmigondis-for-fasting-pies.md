## Ingredients

- cooked carp roe
- pike liver
- crayfish tails
- mushrooms
- morels
- shallot onions
- parsley
- thyme
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- several tablespoons good meat broth
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut cooked carp roe, pike liver, and crayfish tails into small diced pieces.
2. Braise mushrooms and morels, finely cut, with shallot onions, parsley, and thyme in a piece of butter for several minutes.
3. Mix the prepared ingredients together.
4. Add salt, pepper, and several tablespoons of good meat broth.
5. Let everything cook together for several minutes.
6. Stir in egg yolks and some lemon juice.
7. Let it cool and use as needed for pies on fasting days.
