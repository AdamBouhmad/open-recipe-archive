## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely sieved sugar
- 5 eggs
- zest of 1 whole lemon
- 1 pound flour
- fine flour
- white gauze fabric
- bacon
- aniseed
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 egg whites
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir the finely sieved sugar with 5 eggs in a bowl for half an hour.
2. Grate the zest of a whole lemon on a grater and mix it with 1 pound of flour.
3. Bind fine flour in a piece of white gauze fabric, dust the molds well with it, press the dough into the molds, cut off the pressed dough with a knife, lay it on the noodle board, and continue until everything is finished.
4. Let it stand overnight.
5. The next day, coat a black baking sheet with bacon, sprinkle aniseed on it, lay the springerlings on it.
6. Stir a thick icing from 1/4 pound sugar, 2 egg whites, and the juice of 1/2 lemon.
7. Bake the springerlings halfway, coat them with the icing, and let them finish baking in the oven.
