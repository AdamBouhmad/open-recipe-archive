## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the breastbone
- Salt and pepper
- Melted butter
- Lemon juice
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Let a piece from the breastbone cook tender
2. Remove it
3. Sprinkle it with salt and pepper
4. Place it on a grate
5. Pour melted butter and lemon juice over it
6. Let it roast beautifully golden
7. Arrange it on a plate
8. Serve with horseradish or arrange it over the top
