## Ingredients

- meat from a hare roasted the day before
- bread soaked in water
- onions, finely cut and steamed
- zest of one lemon
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- 3 eggs
- butter
- tenderloin of the hare
- olives
- olive stones
- jus

## Directions

1. Take the meat from the hind legs of a hare that was roasted the day before and mince it as finely as possible
2. Mix in bread worth 2 kreuzers that has been soaked in water and pressed out, finely cut and steamed onions, the zest of one lemon, nutmeg, pepper, salt, and 3 eggs
3. Rub everything in a mortar until foamy, put it in a bowl and beat in another egg if necessary, mix it once more
4. Butter a mold, fill it with the mixture and cook it in steam
5. Meanwhile, prepare the ragout: take the tenderloin of the hare, cut small neat pieces from it and make an olive sauce as follows: carefully cut olives so they look whole in the sauce, pound the pits very finely in a mortar
6. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan with the pits and finely cut onions, roast them together for a while, sprinkle 3 finger-fulls of flour over, extinguish it with a spoonful of jus, let it boil once more
7. Add the juice of half a lemon, pour the sauce through a hair sieve, then back into the saucepan with the meat and olives
8. Arrange the plisson on a plate with the ragout arranged piece by piece around the plate and the olives on top
