## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 pound finely ground sugar
- 5 whole eggs
- 2 eggs for making noodles
- hot lard or butter
- 1 lemon
- 4 loth citron
- 4 loth orange peel
- 4 egg whites
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Coarsely ground half of the 1/2 pound blanched almonds; cut the other half lengthwise
2. Press the juice of 1 lemon onto the almonds and stir with 1/2 pound finely ground sugar and 5 whole eggs for a quarter hour
3. Make fine noodles from 2 eggs, cut as finely as possible, and fry golden yellow in hot lard or butter
4. Grate 1 lemon on a grater
5. Finely chop 4 loth citron and orange peel and mix into the mixture
6. Beat 4 egg whites into stiff peaks and fold into the mixture
7. Butter a torte mold with sugar and butter
8. Fill the mixture into the mold and bake the torte at gentle heat until thoroughly done
