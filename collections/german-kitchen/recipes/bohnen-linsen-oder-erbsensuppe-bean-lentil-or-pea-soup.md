## Ingredients

- 250 g beans, lentils or peas
- 1 pinch soda
- 1 tablespoon celery cubes
- 250 g beef
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon chopped celery greens or leek
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon onion cubes
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- 2 l water

## Directions

1. Soak the beans, lentils or peas the evening before according to instructions.
2. Place the legumes in 1 l cold water with soda, heat and drain. Add beef, celery cubes, salt and 1 l water to the legumes, seal the pot tightly and cook the contents slowly for 2 hours. In a small bowl, sweat the onions, chopped greens and flour, add a few spoonfuls of soup and cook the flour mixture with the entire dish, which you season with pepper.
3. Serve the meat separately.
