## Ingredients

- Schöne Kirschen (Beautiful cherries)
- Zucker (Sugar)
- Wasser (Water)
- Zimmt (Cinnamon)

## Directions

1. Pit 2 pounds of beautiful cherries, taking care not to lose too much juice
2. For 2 pounds of cherries, use 1½ pounds of sugar and cook it in a brass pan or saucepan with half a bottle's measure of water until the sugar spins thick threads
3. Add the cherries along with some whole cinnamon and cook as long as it takes for hard-boiled eggs
4. Lift out the cherries with a skimming spoon
5. Cook the syrup until it rises above the pan
6. Place the cherries in their designated dish and pour the syrup, after it has been well reduced, over them
7. Let cool and store like other preserved sugar items; they will keep for 2 years
