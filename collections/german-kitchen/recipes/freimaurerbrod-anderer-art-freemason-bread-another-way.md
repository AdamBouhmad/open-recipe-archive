## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon
- flour
- bacon
- citron

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs and 2 yolks into 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar and stir well until it becomes thick
2. Grate the yolk of half a lemon on a grater and mix in
3. Knead in flour until it can be worked well on a noodle board
4. Form finger-long small sticks from it
5. Place them on a sheet brushed with bacon
6. Make 2 cuts with the back of a knife into each stick
7. Cut citron as long and as wide as the cuts
8. Place it in the cuts
9. Let them stand another quarter hour so the sugar rises up
10. Then bake them
