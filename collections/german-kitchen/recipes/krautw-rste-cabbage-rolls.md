## Ingredients

- cabbage leaves
- butter
- 1 roll soaked and pressed out
- onion and parsley
- 1/2 pound finely chopped beef
- 3 to 4 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs out of the cabbage leaves
2. Cover the leaves with boiling water and blanch them
3. Let them drain
4. In a piece of butter, steam together a grated, soaked, pressed-out roll, onion and parsley, along with 1/2 pound finely chopped beef for half an hour
5. Add 3 to 4 eggs, salt, and nutmeg
6. Coat the cabbage leaves with this filling
7. Roll them together like sausages
8. Fry them until golden in a frying pan with a piece of fat
9. Pour meat broth over them
10. Cook them up
