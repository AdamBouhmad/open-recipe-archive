## Ingredients

- 1 old pigeon or pigeon offal from 10 pigeons or chicken offal from 2-3 chickens or 375 g veal
- 1-1/2 l water
- 12 g salt
- 2 tablespoons soup vegetables
- 40 g flour
- 40 g fat (butter for the ill)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon skim milk

## Directions

1. Preparation: The pigeon is prepared according to instructions and used whole or divided. It, as well as the pigeon offal, is cleaned, the stomachs are cleaned, their skin removed, the hearts and livers cleaned, everything is washed, briefly divided and put up with water and salt. The soup vegetables are cleaned, washed and cut up.
2. After skimming the meat, add the cleaned soup vegetables and let it cook slowly for 1-1/2 hours covered.
3. When the offal is soft, sweat flour with fat thoroughly, gradually pour the broth into the roux while stirring, let the soup cook until slimy, return the offal and soup vegetables, season with salt and add the egg yolk stirred with milk or 1 teaspoon chopped parsley.
4. Optionally, two cleaned asparagus pieces cut into small pieces can also be used.
