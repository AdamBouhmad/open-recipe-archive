## Ingredients

- Snails
- Ash
- Salt
- Anchovies (6 loth cleaned)
- 3 Shallot onions
- Lemon
- Parsley
- 1 spoonful of capers
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the snails with a little ash for a quarter hour
2. Pour them into a strainer and pull the snails out of their shells with a fork
3. Clean them well and remove the black skin from the back
4. Boil the shells once more and rub off the slime from the snails with a handful of salt
5. Finely mince 6 loth of cleaned anchovies, 3 shallot onions, some lemon, parsley, and 1 spoonful of capers
6. Knead the minced mixture with butter
7. Fill the snail shells halfway with this mixture
8. Press 2 snails into each shell and cover with more of the mixture on top
9. Sprinkle a little breadcrumbs over it
10. Place them on a snail plate or in a baking vessel, put this in an oven, and leave it in only as long as until the butter has melted on the snails
