## Ingredients

- 25 g oat gruel
- 50 g dried prunes
- 15 g sugar
- 1 small piece cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon lemon sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/8 l white wine
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Wash the oat gruel and the chopped prunes.
2. In an earthenware pot, cook the oat gruel and prunes covered with water, sugar, cinnamon and lemon sugar until soft, press through a hair sieve or cloth and finish with the egg yolk stirred in wine.
