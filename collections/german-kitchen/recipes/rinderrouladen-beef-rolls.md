## Ingredients

- 200 g beef
- 1 small onion
- 30 g bacon, scraped
- 20 g beef fat
- 1 teaspoon salt
- a pinch of pepper
- 20 g flour
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Beef slices the size of a palm and 3/4 cm thick are beaten, the onions finely chopped; the bacon is scraped. Bacon, onions, salt and pepper are mixed, the slices are brushed with this, then rolled together, bound with cotton and rolled in flour. The beef fat is placed in a pot.
2. The fat in the pot is made steaming, the rolls rolled in flour are placed in it and nicely browned on all sides; 1/4 l water is gradually added, the pot is covered again, and the roulades are slowly braised for 2 hours. The roulades are occasionally turned and basted. If liquid is still missing, a little more water is added. The soft roulades are removed from the pot, placed in a bowl, the cotton is removed, the sauce is seasoned and poured over the roulades; then they are served.
