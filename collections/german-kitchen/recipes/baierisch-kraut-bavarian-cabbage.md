## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- pork fat
- onions
- half glass vinegar
- glass of wine
- meat broth
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage finely and salt it lightly.
2. In a casserole, steam it soft with a piece of pork fat and onions.
3. When soft, pour half a glass of vinegar over it, a glass of wine, and some meat broth.
4. Sprinkle a little flour over it, mix together, and finish cooking.
