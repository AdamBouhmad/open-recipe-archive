## Ingredients

- calf's tongues
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues until soft
2. Remove the skin
3. Cut them in 2 parts
4. Rub them with salt and pepper
5. Turn them in beaten egg
6. Sprinkle with grated bread
7. Place on grill
8. Pour melted butter over them
9. Fry golden brown
