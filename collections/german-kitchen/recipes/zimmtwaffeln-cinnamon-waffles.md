## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 4 loth finely pounded almonds with skin
- 4 loth fine flour
- 1/2 loth finely pounded cinnamon
- bacon

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter to foam.
2. Stir together 2 eggs, 1/4 pound sugar, and 4 loth finely pounded almonds with skin, and 4 loth fine flour and 1/2 loth finely pounded cinnamon.
3. Put portions the size of a walnut in the middle of a hot waffle iron coated inside with bacon and bake on both sides.
4. These waffles can be kept for 2 to 3 weeks.
