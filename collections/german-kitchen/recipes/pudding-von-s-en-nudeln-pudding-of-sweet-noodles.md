## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- flour as much as the egg and yolk will take up
- 1 pound butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 pound chopped citron and orange peel
- half a lemon peel, finely chopped
- 4 loth sugar
- 2 apples, finely cut
- egg whites from 5 eggs
- flour
- a small piece of butter
- wine
- sugar
- cinnamon to taste
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from 1 egg, 1 yolk, and flour
2. Roll out cakes from the dough and cut them 2 knife-backs thick
3. Cook them in salted water, draw through cold water and let drain
4. Stir 1 pound butter until creamy
5. Stir in 5 egg yolks, the noodles, 1 pound chopped citron and orange peel, finely chopped lemon peel, 4 loth sugar, 2 finely cut apples, and the snow from 5 egg whites
6. Fill the mass into a damp cloth, tie it tightly, cook the pudding 5 quarter-hours in boiling water
7. For the wine sauce: let 1 small spoonful of flour with a piece of butter turn golden, deglaze with wine, add sugar, a knife-point of cinnamon and the same of nutmeg
8. Let everything come to boil together and pour this sauce over the pudding
