## Ingredients

- milk
- eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix milk together with eggs, cook everything until it becomes thick, stirring constantly, and if lard cooks out it must be skimmed off.
2. When the pudding is on the plate, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on it.
