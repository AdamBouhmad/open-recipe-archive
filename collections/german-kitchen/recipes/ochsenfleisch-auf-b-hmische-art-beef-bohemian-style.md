## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the breast or from the thick loin
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon of meat broth
- White beer
- Half a Schoppen wine
- Some vinegar
- Yellow zest of half a lemon
- Juice of half a lemon
- 2 Loth (approximately 14 grams) blanched almonds
- A piece of brown gingerbread
- A little sugar

## Directions

1. Rub a piece of beef from the breast or thick loin well with salt and pepper
2. Roast it on a grate until it is half done
3. Let it cook tender in a casserole with 1 tablespoon of meat broth, white beer, half a Schoppen wine, some vinegar, the finely minced yellow zest of half a lemon, and its juice
4. Cut 2 Loth of blanched almonds lengthwise
5. Grate a piece of brown gingerbread
6. Add this and a little sugar to the meat
7. Arrange the meat on a plate and pour the sauce over it
