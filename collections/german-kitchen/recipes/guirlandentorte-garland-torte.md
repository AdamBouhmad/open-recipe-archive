## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds, finely cut
- 1/2 pound almonds, pounded
- 1/2 pound pounded sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- zest of 1/2 lemon, grated
- 8 egg whites
- a handful of potato flour
- butter
- 12 egg whites
- 12 loth pounded sugar
- 1/2 pound cut almonds
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound cut almonds

## Directions

1. Cut the blanched almonds very finely and pound 1/2 pound of them
2. Stir the pounded almonds with 1/2 pound pounded sugar and 12 egg yolks until the mixture is thick
3. Mix in the grated lemon zest, the snow from 8 egg whites, and a handful of potato flour
4. Fill the mixture into a springform brushed with butter and bake the torte
5. Whip a stiff snow from 12 egg whites, mix in 12 loth pounded sugar and 1/2 pound cut almonds
6. Spread this frosting evenly on the baked torte and dry it for another quarter hour in the oven
7. In a brass pan, continuously stir together 1/2 pound sugar and 1/2 pound cut almonds until the sugar becomes liquid and yellow
8. Remove the springform from the torte, make a beautiful garland from the roasted almonds around it and a crown in the middle
