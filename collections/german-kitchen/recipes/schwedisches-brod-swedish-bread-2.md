## Ingredients

- one quarter pound almonds
- rose water
- half pound finely sifted sugar
- one quint cinnamon
- one quint cloves
- one quint cardamom
- mace
- a knife point of pepper
- 4 loth fine flour
- 1 egg
- egg white
- 4 loth sugar
- a few drops of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pound the almonds with rose water not too finely and mix them with the half pound finely sifted sugar, one quint cinnamon, one quint cloves, one quint cardamom, mace, a knife point of pepper and 4 loth fine flour
2. Beat one egg into this mixture and make a dough from it
3. Roll it out finger thick and cut out pieces with a small drinking glass
4. Place them on a baking sheet brushed with wax and bake in the oven
5. For the icing: stir 4 loth sugar and a few drops of lemon juice into one egg white, stirring together for some time
6. Brush the breads with this icing using a brush and let them bake completely in the oven
