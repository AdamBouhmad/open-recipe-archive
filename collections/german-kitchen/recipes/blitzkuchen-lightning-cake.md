## Ingredients

- 12 Loth melted butter
- 12 Loth sugar
- 12 Loth flour
- zest of 1 lemon
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix 12 Loth of melted butter, 12 Loth of sugar, 12 Loth of flour, the zest of 1 lemon, and 5 eggs very well together
2. Butter a springform pan
3. Bake the cake beautifully golden
