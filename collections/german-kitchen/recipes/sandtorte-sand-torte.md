## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound fine sugar
- 1 lemon
- 6 egg whites
- 1/2 pound flour

## Directions

1. Melt 1/2 pound butter in a bowl
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks, 1/2 pound fine sugar, the grated zest of 1 lemon, the beaten whites from 6 eggs, and 1/2 pound flour very thick with the butter
3. Fill the mixture into a mold and bake the torte in the oven
