## Ingredients

- 1 pike
- salt
- pepper
- larding fat
- melted lemon butter
- lemon juice
- jus

## Directions

1. Clean the pike carefully, cut away the gills, and remove the skin
2. Rub it with salt and pepper
3. Lard it nicely and tie it onto the spit
4. Frequently baste it with melted lemon butter
5. After half an hour, remove it from the spit and place it on a serving plate
6. Pour lemon juice and jus over it
