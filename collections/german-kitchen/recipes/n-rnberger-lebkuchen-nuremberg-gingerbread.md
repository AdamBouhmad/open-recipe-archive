## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas honey
- 1/2 pound ground sugar
- 1 pound blanched almonds cut crosswise
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 4 loth citron
- 4 loth orange peel
- 1 loth ground cinnamon
- 1/2 quint cloves
- Cardamom for 1 kreuzer
- Cubeb pepper for 1 kreuzer
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 2 pounds flour
- Egg for glazing

## Directions

1. Heat honey in a brass pan; when it begins to boil add ground sugar
2. Let cook together for 8 minutes
3. Cut blanched almonds crosswise and mix in
4. Let cook for one more minute and transfer to a bowl
5. Add lemon zest, citron, orange peel (not finely chopped), cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, cubeb pepper, and grated nutmeg
6. Stir flour into the honey mixture
7. While still warm, place on a noodle board and dust the mold with flour
8. Press dough into the mold to about the thickness of a small finger, turn out, and place on a flour-dusted dark baking sheet
9. Brush with egg and bake in the oven
10. Optional: add 1 knife point potash to the dough
