## Ingredients

- young leaves of purslane, 3 loth
- young leaves of endive, 3 loth
- young leaves of corn salad, 3 loth
- young leaves of sorrel, 3 loth
- chervil, 6 loth
- a small piece of butter
- 3 fingerfulls flour
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Wash the young leaves of purslane, endive, corn salad, and sorrel (each 3 loth) and chervil (6 loth) in lukewarm water.
2. Press out firmly and chop as finely as possible.
3. Braise it for half an hour with a small piece of butter in a saucepan.
4. Add 3 fingerfulls of flour, deglaze with meat broth, and serve to the patient.
5. Fine dumplings may also be added.
