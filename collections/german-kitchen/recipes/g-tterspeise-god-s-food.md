## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 spoon porridge
- egg yolks from the porridge
- 4 bitter almonds
- 3 loth ground sugar with lemon zest
- egg whites from the porridge
- raspberry or apricot jelly for serving

## Directions

1. Cook a thick porridge from 1/2 maas milk, stir 1/4 pound butter lightly, add 1 spoon of porridge to the butter, beat an egg yolk into it, stir together and continue this way until the porridge is finished
2. Peel 4 bitter almonds, pound them fine in a mortar, stir under the mass
3. Add 3 loth ground sugar with lemon zest grated on it, beat the whites of the eggs used for the porridge to snow, mix everything together with the above
4. Butter a tin mold, sprinkle with sugar, fill the mass in, seal the mold tightly and cook 1 hour in boiling water
5. Turn this Götterspeise onto a plate and serve raspberry or apricot jelly with it
