## Ingredients

- poached eggs
- black bread
- lard
- brown gingerbread (Lebkuchen)
- wine
- broth
- lemon
- anchovies

## Directions

1. Prepare poached eggs as described in the egg preparation instructions and place them on a plate.
2. Bake a piece of black bread nicely golden in lard and cook it in a casserole with a piece of brown gingerbread, one pint of wine, and some broth.
3. Press through a hair sieve, return to the casserole with the juice and finely-chopped lemon zest, and bring to a boil.
4. Take half a pound of cleaned anchovies, remove the bones, cut them into small pieces, and lay them on the plate with the eggs.
5. Arrange the sauce over them and serve at table.
