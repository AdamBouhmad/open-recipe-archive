## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) green beans
- 2 liters water for blanching
- 3/4 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 30 g lean bacon or 25 g fatty bacon or 1 tablespoon fat
- 1/2 teaspoon diced onion
- 2 teaspoons flour
- 1 1/2 tablespoons mild vinegar
- 1 pinch pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare: Remove the beans, break or cut them and wash; dice the bacon and onions.
2. Blanch the beans and cook them tender in fresh salt water. 1/2 hour before serving, fry the bacon until yellow, add the diced onions and fry them tender, sweat the flour with the bacon and onions until done, stir in the vinegar and pour everything over the beans, which are seasoned with pepper. You can also add boiled potatoes.
