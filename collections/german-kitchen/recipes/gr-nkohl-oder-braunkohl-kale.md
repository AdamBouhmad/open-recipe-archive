## Ingredients

- 1 kg kale leaves
- 20 g salt
- 3 l water
- 40 g pork, goose, or duck fat
- 20 g onions
- 3/8 l broth
- 5 g sugar
- 5 g oat groats
- 1/2 spice mixture
- 3 g salt

## Directions

1. Dice the onion. Remove the large leaf ribs from the kale and wash it.
2. Place the kale in boiling salted water and bring it to a boil several times, drain it, pour cold water over it, carefully squeeze it with a spoon and chop it lightly; in many places it is also enjoyed unchopped.
3. Heat the fat; soften the onions in it; add the kale, pour in the boiling broth; sprinkle oat groats, spice, salt and sugar between; toss the kale, add more broth and braise the vegetable slowly covered.
4. Instead of oat groats, one can also use 10 g semolina. Brussels sprouts or sprouting kale are cooked the same way but without oat groats.
5. Leftovers can be reheated several times.
