## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1-2 tablespoons water
- 100 g flour
- 3 g salt
- 20 g fat
- 1/2 kg apples
- water and salt for cooking the noodles
- 30 g sugar
- 1/8 l water
- 20 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the core, cut into pieces. Put the sugar water on the stove.
2. Sift the flour onto a board, place the egg beaten with salt in a depression in the middle, distribute the fat over the flour, mix everything together with a broad knife, and work the dough with the hand until a hole pressed in with a finger closes by itself. Divide the dough into 4 parts; roll each one out on a floured board with a floured rolling pin to the thickness of a knife-back, and let the dough sheets dry on clean cloth for 1/4 hour, then cut strips about 5 cm wide from them, lay 6 on top of each other and cut 5 cm long noodles from them, which laid out on the cloth dry for 1/2 hour. The apples are poured into boiling sugar water, cooked until done and kept hot. The dried noodles are boiled for 5 minutes in salted water, drained, dripped dry, mixed with the apples, reheated and sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon before serving.
