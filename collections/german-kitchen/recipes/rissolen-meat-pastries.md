## Ingredients

- leftover meat
- one onion
- one Kreuzerbrod (small bread roll)
- 2 to 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- 2 to 3 handfuls of flour
- half a schoppen of sour cream
- 2 loth of butter
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the leftover meat finely with one onion
2. Soak a Kreuzerbrod in water, then press it out firmly
3. Braise this with butter in a casserole for a quarter hour
4. Stir in 2 to 3 eggs and sprinkle salt and nutmeg over it
5. On a noodle board, make a dough from 2 to 3 handfuls of flour, 2 eggs, half a schoppen of sour cream, and 2 loth of butter, as for noodles
6. Roll out the dough, fold it over once more, roll it out again, but not as thin as noodles
7. Brush the dough with eggs, lay the meat mixture on top
8. Fold it over, cut it out with a mold, and bake the pieces in lard until golden yellow
