## Ingredients

- One beautiful lamb quarter
- Bacon for larding
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Paper
- Fat meat broth
- One glass wine
- Meat broth or butter
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Sour cream
- Half lemon

## Directions

1. From a beautiful lamb quarter, pull back the skin on the shank slightly.
2. Lard it with bacon.
3. Rub with salt and pepper.
4. Put it on a spit.
5. Brush paper with butter and bind it around the meat.
6. Baste frequently with fat meat broth.
7. Pour one glass of wine, some meat broth or butter into the pan, add cut onions and bay leaves to make a good jus.
8. Half an hour before serving, remove the paper.
9. Baste the roast with sour cream (the roast must look quite glossy).
10. Squeeze the juice from half a lemon over it.
11. Force the jus through a hair sieve and serve separately with the roast.
