## Ingredients

- one old, clean chicken
- 2 Maas meat broth
- 1/4 pound blanched, finely ground almonds

## Directions

1. Take an old, carefully cleaned and washed chicken and braise it until half-cooked.
2. Pound it in a mortar together with the bones very finely.
3. Let it cook in a saucepan for one hour, gradually adding 2 Maas meat broth.
4. Press it through a clean cloth.
5. Let it come to a boil again with 1/4 pound blanched, finely ground almonds.
6. Serve it over toasted bread slices.
