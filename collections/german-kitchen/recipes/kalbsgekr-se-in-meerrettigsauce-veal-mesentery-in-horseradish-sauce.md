## Ingredients

- Veal mesentery (cleaned)
- Cold water
- Horseradish (half a root)
- Butter
- Flour (1 spoon)
- Sweet milk (half a schoppen)
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Set a cleaned veal mesentery with cold water and let it cook until soft.
2. Remove it and place it again in cold water, cut it into pieces of choice.
3. Grate half a root of horseradish on a grater.
4. Let a piece of butter in a casserole with one spoon of flour turn golden, add the horseradish, turn it a couple of times, smooth it with half a schoppen of sweet milk.
5. Add salt, nutmeg and the mesentery and let it come to a boil together.
