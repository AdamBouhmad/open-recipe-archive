## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) carrots
- 3/4 l water
- 10 g flour
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 1 teaspoon parsley
- 5 g salt
- 5 g sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the carrots and cut into uniform strips, rinse quickly again, and place in a pot with 3/4 l boiling water, salt and sugar.
2. Wash the parsley and chop finely.
3. Place the carrots on the stove and cook covered until soft.
4. Make a roux with the fat and flour, add one spoon of carrot broth while stirring and bring to a boil.
5. Pour the roux over the dish, let it come to a boil again, season to taste and add the parsley.
