## Ingredients

- One handful of chervil
- One handful of parsley
- Some tarragon
- Six hard-boiled eggs
- Two tablespoons of mustard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two tablespoons of Provençal oil
- Good herb vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash and clean one handful of chervil, one handful of parsley, and some tarragon
2. Mince everything together finely
3. Press the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve
4. Mix everything together in a bowl with two tablespoons of mustard
5. Add salt, pepper, two tablespoons of Provençal oil, and good herb vinegar
6. Blend everything together
