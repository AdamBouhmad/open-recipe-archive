## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 16 loth flour
- lukewarm milk
- 6 eggs
- 2 knife-points of pounded cinnamon
- 1/2 lemon, minced
- 8 blanched bitter almonds
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter until foamy.
2. Stir 16 loth flour smooth in a bowl with lukewarm milk.
3. Add 6 eggs, one after another, 2 knife-points of pounded cinnamon, half a minced lemon, and 8 blanched bitter almonds.
4. Stir all this together with 1 tablespoon sugar under the butter until nothing of the butter is visible.
5. Bake golden in a waffle iron and sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top.
