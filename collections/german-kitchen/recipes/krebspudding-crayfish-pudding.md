## Ingredients

- 25 crayfish
- 1/4 pound butter
- Water or meat broth
- 2 soaked and pressed rolls
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 grated nutmeg
- Salt
- Snow from 5 egg whites
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- Crayfish broth
- 1 knife tip of nutmeg flower
- 1 spoonful sour cream
- 2 egg yolks (for sauce)

## Directions

1. Remove the gall and tails from 25 crayfish
2. Pound the tails with butter very finely
3. Steam the pounded mixture with 1/4 pound butter in a casserole until it foams
4. Add water or meat broth
5. Remove the crayfish butter from the top
6. Pour it into cold water and stir until white in a bowl
7. Take half the crayfish butter and mix with 2 soaked and pressed rolls, 5 egg yolks, 1 grated nutmeg, salt and the snow from 5 egg whites
8. Place the crayfish tails on a wet cloth
9. Fill the mass on top
10. Bind the cloth and cook the pudding in boiling water for one hour
11. For the sauce: put the second half of the crayfish butter with 1 cooking spoon flour in a casserole
12. Turn it several times in the butter
13. Rinse with water and fill with meat broth
14. Add some crayfish broth, 1 knife tip of nutmeg flower, salt and 1 spoonful sour cream
15. Bring to a boil together
16. When ready to serve, stir in 2 egg yolks and pour the sauce over the pudding
