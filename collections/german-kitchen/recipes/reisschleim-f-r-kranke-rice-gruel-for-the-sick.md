## Ingredients

- 4 Loth cleanly washed Carolina rice
- Water
- Egg yolk
- Sweet cream
- Sugar or salt

## Directions

1. Pound 4 Loth of cleanly washed Carolina rice very finely
2. Let it cook in a small pot with 1 Maas water until it begins to thicken
3. Press the rice through a linen cloth that is not very tight
4. Stir in an egg yolk and a spoonful of sweet cream
5. Let the gruel come together once more
6. Sweeten or salt it and give to the sick person one spoonful at a time
