## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound peeled, finely ground almonds
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 drinking glasses sweet cream
- a handful of grated roll

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound peeled, finely ground almonds, 1/2 pound sugar, 8 egg yolks, 2 drinking glasses sweet cream and a handful of grated roll well together
2. Beat a snow from the egg whites, add it and place in a suitable dish, heat above and below, and soufflé
