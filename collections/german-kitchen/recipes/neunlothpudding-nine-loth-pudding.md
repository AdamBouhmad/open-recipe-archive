## Ingredients

- 1 weak schoppen of water
- 6 loth butter
- 6 loth sugar
- half a lemon, zest grated
- 6 loth finely ground almonds
- 6 loth fine flour
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from 6 eggs

## Directions

1. In a brass pan, cook together the water, butter, sugar with grated lemon zest, ground almonds, and flour into a thick batter
2. Place the mass in a bowl and add the 6 egg yolks one after another
3. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in last
4. Brush a pudding mold with butter and sprinkle with sugar
5. Fill the mass into the mold and cook in boiling water for one hour
6. For the hagebutten sauce: stir together 2 spoonfuls of hagebutten puree and 1 spoonful of fine flour in a brass pan
7. Pour 1 schoppen of good wine to it, add sugar and let it come to boil
8. When the pudding is on the plate, pour the sauce alongside it
