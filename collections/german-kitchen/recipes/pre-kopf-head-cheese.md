## Ingredients

- Bacon
- Pork ears
- Some pork jowl
- 4 calf feet
- 2 pork feet
- Salt
- Pepper
- Minced lemon peel
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Mace

## Directions

1. Cook bacon, pork ears, some pork jowl, 4 calf feet, and 2 pork feet until very soft with salt and pepper
2. Cut everything into small pieces
3. Mix thoroughly in a bowl with minced lemon peel, pepper, cloves, ginger, mace, and salt
4. Fill the mixture into a cleaned pork stomach
5. Sew it shut
6. Simmer for one hour, longer if it is large
7. Place between two boards and let it rest overnight
