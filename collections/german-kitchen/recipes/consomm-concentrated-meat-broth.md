## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 pounds beef
- 4 to 5 pounds veal bones
- 6 veal feet
- 3 old cleaned chickens
- water

## Directions

1. Chop all the meat and bones into pieces and put in a large kettle
2. Pour enough water over it so it rises 3 fingers above the contents
3. Let it cook for 10 to 12 hours, skimming diligently
4. Do not salt it; use few herbs and roots to prevent souring
5. Refill with water often during cooking
6. Let it reduce to half its volume, then strain through a hair sieve
7. Let it stand overnight if time permits
8. The next day, remove the fat from the top and put the remainder in a casserole
9. Let it cook until a sample on a plate remains in drops
10. Skim continuously during cooking
11. Pour through linen into a flat dish and cut into small tablets like chocolate when firm
12. It is especially good for poultry and for glazing, and can be used to prepare the strongest meat broths and cleanest soups
