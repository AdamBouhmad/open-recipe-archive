## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 kg large beans
- 2 liters water
- 8 g salt
- 20 g fat
- 3/8 liter broth
- 15 g onions
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1/2 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 5 g flour
- 1 sprig bean or pepper herb

## Directions

1. Shell and wash the beans; rub the onions; chop the parsley.
2. Half-cook the beans in salted water. Drained, cook them in the broth with the pepper herb until done, add flour, onions sweated in fat and parsley, pepper and salt to taste and serve the beans, thoroughly cooked, very hot. Remove the bean or pepper herb.
