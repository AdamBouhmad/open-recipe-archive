## Ingredients

- 75 g grated bread
- 1 piece lemon or orange peel
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon
- 65 g sugar
- 1 bottle light beer
- 65 g currants

## Directions

1. Grate the bread and let the currants swell.
2. Mix the bread with spices and sugar, pour the beer over it; add the swollen currants; stir everything together and chill.
