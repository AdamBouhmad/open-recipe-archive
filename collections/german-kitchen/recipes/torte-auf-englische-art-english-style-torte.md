## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound sifted sugar
- 1/2 pound almonds
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 pound flour
- 1 egg white
- 1/8 pound sugar
- red sanding sugar
- lemon or orange cream
- apple sauce
- other creams for filling

## Directions

1. Cream the butter in a bowl until foamy
2. Add the sifted sugar, almonds, and grated lemon zest, and stir everything together well
3. Line a dark baking sheet with paper, dust with a little flour
4. Work 1/2 pound flour into the mixture
5. Make 4 to 6 cakes, as large as desired for the torte
6. Bake them in the oven until nicely golden yellow
7. Once all cakes are baked, place one cake on the serving plate
8. Spread it with lemon or orange cream, lay the second cake on top, spread it with apple sauce, the third with another cream, and so forth (but the top one must remain empty)
9. Make the torte nicely round
10. Beat an egg white until stiff, add 1/8 pound sugar, stir together until thick
11. Spread this mixture over the torte, sprinkle red sanding sugar over it
12. Place in the oven and let it dry
