## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) chanterelles or porcini mushrooms
- 20 g salt
- 2 l water for blanching
- 30 g fat or margarine
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 5 g flour
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, rinse quickly, cut small if necessary (but do not soak in water), place in hot salted water, blanch, and drain as dry as possible on a sieve.
2. Pour the blanched mushrooms into hot fat, dust with flour, add pepper and salt, and braise the dish for a few minutes.
3. Stir the parsley with the mushrooms and serve.
