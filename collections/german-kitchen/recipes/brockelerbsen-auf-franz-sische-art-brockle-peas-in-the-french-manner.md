## Ingredients

- Schöne junge Brockelerbsen (Beautiful young brockle peas)
- Petersilie (Parsley)
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean and select beautiful young brockle peas
2. Place them in a saucepan with a small handful of parsley and a piece of butter
3. Braise for a quarter hour
4. Transfer to a tin box and add enough butter so that the peas are completely covered
5. Have the box soldered shut by a tinsmith
6. Cook another quarter hour in steam so that the fat remains on top
7. Preserve in the cellar
8. They will taste like fresh brockle peas
