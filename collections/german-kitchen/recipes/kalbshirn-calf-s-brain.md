## Ingredients

- calf's brain
- water
- vinegar
- onions
- lemon
- bay leaves
- chives
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the calf's brain in lukewarm water and remove the skin
2. Put it in a casserole with water, vinegar, onions, lemon, and bay leaves
3. Cook until as firm as hard-boiled eggs
4. Arrange on a plate
5. Chop chives very finely over it
6. Brown hot butter until light golden and pour over the brain
7. Add a little lemon juice
8. Salt to taste
