## Ingredients

- 6 cut rolls
- 1 pint hot milk
- 1 pound smoked bacon, finely chopped
- 1 pound smoked meat, finely chopped
- 1 handful onions steamed in bacon
- 3 spoonfuls fine flour
- salt
- 7 to 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Moisten 6 cut rolls in a bowl with 1 pint hot milk
2. Add 1 pound finely chopped smoked bacon and 1 pound finely chopped smoked meat
3. Add 1 handful onions steamed in bacon, 3 spoonfuls fine flour, and salt
4. Mix 7 to 8 eggs through everything
5. Let it stand covered for half an hour so it draws
6. Make round balls from the mixture
7. Boil them for half an hour in salt water
8. They can be added to stewed chicken
