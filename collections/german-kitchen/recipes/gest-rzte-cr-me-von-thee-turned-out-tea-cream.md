## Ingredients

- half a maas milk
- 1 piece whole cinnamon or vanilla
- 1 loth tea
- 1-1/2 schoppen calf's jelly
- 8 loth sugar
- cinnamon oil

## Directions

1. In half a maas milk with one piece whole cinnamon or vanilla, boil 1 loth tea in a brass pan for one minute
2. Then put 1-1/2 schoppen calf's jelly on the fire until it is melted
3. Add 8 loth sugar to it
4. Place the calf's jelly in a mold brushed with cinnamon oil on ice until firm
5. Pour the above-described cream over it
