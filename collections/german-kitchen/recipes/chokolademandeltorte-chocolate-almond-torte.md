## Ingredients

- 16 egg yolks
- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 25 loth blanched, finely ground almonds
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 strong loth cinnamon
- zest of 1 lemon, minced
- snow of 8 egg whites
- 10 loth grated chocolate
- mussel flour for the pan
- 1/2 loth citron peel
- 1/2 loth orange peel
- 1 egg white for icing
- 1/8 pound sugar for icing
- 2 loth chocolate for icing

## Directions

1. Stir 16 egg yolks for half an hour
2. Take 1 pound finely sifted sugar, 25 loth blanched finely ground almonds, juice of 1 lemon, 1 strong loth cinnamon, and the minced zest of 1 lemon
3. Add the snow of 8 egg whites
4. Stir everything together for one hour
5. Finally add 10 loth grated chocolate
6. Bake the torte in a mold brushed with butter and sprinkled with mussel flour
7. For a better version, also add 1/2 loth citron peel and 1/2 loth orange peel
8. Make an icing from the snow of 1 egg white, 1/8 pound sugar, and 2 loth chocolate
9. Brush the torte with this icing and let it brown once more in the oven
