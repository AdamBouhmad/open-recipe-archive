## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg apples
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 6 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into pieces.
2. Cook them quickly with the water in a heated pot until soft, press the cooked apples through a sieve and mix the puree with the sugar.
