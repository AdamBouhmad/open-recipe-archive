## Ingredients

- 4 grated milk rolls or 8 toasted rusks
- 1 egg, 1 egg white
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3/8 l skim milk
- 1/4 grated lemon
- 2 tablespoons grated bread crumbs
- 25 g lard, fat or margarine for frying
- 20 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut milk rolls or rusks into finger-thick slices and put them in a neat shape, beat 1 egg, salt and sugar with the milk until clear, add lemon and gradually pour the liquid over the bread slices, which are placed on a flat dish and turned often.
2. When the poor knights are thoroughly soaked, turn them in beaten egg white and bread crumbs and fry or bake them in steaming fat to a beautiful light brown color, sprinkle them thickly with sugar and cinnamon, serve with stewed fruit, lingonberries, sauce over wine sauce or serve with apple rice. The poor knights can also first be softened in milk or sugar water, a thin pancake batter made from 20 g flour and the stated ingredients, the softened slices turned in it and then baked.
