## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Butter
- Two chopped shallot onions
- One tablespoon of flour
- Clear meat broth
- A knife's point of ground mace
- Two egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut off the white part of the asparagus and scrape slightly, bind again in bundles and simmer soft in boiling salt water
2. Remove them, cut off the tips and put in the soup tureen, place the rest in a mortar and pound
3. In a casserole, braise the pounded asparagus with butter, add two chopped shallot onions
4. After braising for a quarter hour, stir in one tablespoon of flour
5. Pour in clear meat broth and let cook together for a quarter hour
6. Press through a fine sieve, return to the casserole and bring to a boil once more
7. Add a knife's point of ground mace
8. In a small dish stir two egg yolks with two tablespoons of sour cream, pour into the soup tureen with the asparagus and arrange the soup
