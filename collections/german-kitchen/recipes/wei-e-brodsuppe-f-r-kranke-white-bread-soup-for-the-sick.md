## Ingredients

- 2 rolls
- 3 lot almonds
- rose water
- 1 measure almond milk
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak 2 rolls and press them out firmly
2. Pound 3 lot almonds very finely with rose water
3. Make 1 measure of almond milk as usual
4. Mix it well with the rolls and cook
5. Sprinkle cinnamon on the soup when serving
