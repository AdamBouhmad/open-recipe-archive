## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- A few onions, one of which is studded with 4 cloves
- A celery root
- 1 bay leaf
- A little mace (nutmeg flower)
- Bacon to cover the meat
- Salt
- 2 tablespoons of water
- Several heads of savoy cabbage
- A little jus

## Directions

1. Place a piece of beef in a dish
2. Add a few onions, one studded with 4 cloves, a celery root, 1 bay leaf, and a little mace
3. Cover the meat with bacon
4. Salt it
5. Pour 2 tablespoons of water over it
6. Let it simmer slowly together
7. Bind several cleaned savoy cabbage heads tightly together with string so they remain whole
8. Blanch them slightly
9. Place them in fresh water for a while
10. Add them to the beef when it is somewhat tender
11. Let everything cook together until only a little jus remains with the meat
12. Place the meat on a plate
13. Let the cabbage drain on a sieve
14. Remove the strings
15. Arrange the heads around the meat
16. Pour a little jus over the dish
