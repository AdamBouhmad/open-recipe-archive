## Ingredients

- ox tongue
- onions
- lemons
- bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- 6 Loth butter
- 1 glass wine

## Directions

1. Clean an ox tongue and simmer it until the skin can be removed
2. Lard it
3. Place it in a casserole
4. Add onions, lemons, bay leaves, salt, pepper and mace
5. Add 6 loth butter and a glass of wine
6. Cover the casserole
7. Let it braise
8. You can also braise this tongue in the oven or on a spit
9. When serving add lemons
