## Ingredients

- one quarter pound finely grated sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 loth ground cinnamon
- a couple drops of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat one egg white into one quarter pound finely grated sugar
2. Stir together for a quarter hour
3. Add 1 loth ground cinnamon and a couple drops of lemon juice
