## Ingredients

- 1 l fish broth
- 30 g flour
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 1 teaspoon diced onion
- 1/8 l skim milk
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- Fish scraps or fried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fish broth or cooking water from good fresh fish is well suited for this soup. To the diced onions and flour sweated in an earthenware pot with the fat, gradually add the fish broth while stirring through a sieve, let simmer for 1 hour, add the milk to balance the salt of the broth, bring to a boil again and season with pepper and parsley, place clean fish scraps in it or serve bread crumbs alongside.
2. Optionally, the soup can be finished with an egg yolk mixed into 2 tablespoons milk or white wine.
