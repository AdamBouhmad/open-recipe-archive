## Ingredients

- calf's head
- bacon
- pork forcemeat
- truffles, finely crushed
- bread, soaked in water and pressed dry
- anchovies
- egg yolk
- salt
- nutmeg
- lard
- mushrooms
- morels
- consommé
- lemon juice
- red Burgundy wine, half a glass

## Directions

1. When the head is soft, cut it into square pieces the size of checkerboard squares.
2. Make small dumplings from bacon, pork forcemeat, finely crushed truffles, bread soaked in water and pressed dry, and anchovies; grind everything thoroughly together in a mortar or stone grinder.
3. Place this mixture in a bowl, beat in an egg yolk, add salt and nutmeg, knead everything together once more, and form into dumplings as small as possible.
4. Fry the dumplings in hot lard until golden.
5. On a platter, layer the calf's head pieces, then a layer of mushrooms, truffles, morels, and fried dumplings; continue layering until everything is arranged.
6. Heat consommé, add lemon juice, pour in half a glass of red Burgundy wine, and slowly pour this sauce over the calf's head using a brush.
