## Ingredients

- Pig lung
- Pig liver
- Pig mesentery
- Pig sweetbreads
- Some calf liver
- 4 to 6 onions
- Finely chopped lemons
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Fat in which the meat was cooked
- White bratwurst casings

## Directions

1. Scald the lung, liver, mesentery, sweetbreads, and some calf liver
2. Chop them very finely on a cutting block together with 4 to 6 onions
3. Mix finely chopped lemons, pepper, salt, nutmeg, and the fat in which the meat was cooked in a bowl
4. Mix everything together until it is not too thin
5. Fill the mixture into white bratwurst casings
6. Place in lukewarm water
7. Heat under the dish in which they lie until the sausages rise to the surface
