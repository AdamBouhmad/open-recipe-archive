## Ingredients

- eggs
- spinach
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- beaten eggs
- breadcrumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard, peel them, and place them in cold water.
2. Cook some spinach as you would for a vegetable.
3. Press the egg yolks into the spinach.
4. Let a piece of butter melt in a casserole, braise the spinach and egg yolks in it, add salt and nutmeg.
5. Fill the egg whites with this mixture, press them together so they look like whole eggs.
6. Roll the eggs in beaten eggs and then in grated breadcrumbs.
7. Bake them in hot lard until nicely golden.
