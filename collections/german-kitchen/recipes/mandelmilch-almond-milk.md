## Ingredients

- 4 Loth blanched almonds
- Water

## Directions

1. Pound the blanched almonds very finely in a mortar with a little water
2. Gradually pour half a Maas of water while pounding
3. Press the almonds through a linen cloth
4. Pound the almonds again
5. Repeat this process several times
6. For an invalid, hemp milk, which cools greatly, can be added
