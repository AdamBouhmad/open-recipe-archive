## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Butter
- Meat broth or water
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Chives
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean the pigeons but do not wash them; cut out the breast
2. Chop the remaining body with liver, heart, and cleaned stomach, along with beef bones
3. Steam with butter as desired for one hour and add meat broth, or water if broth is unavailable
4. Boil the breast, cut it into small pieces, place in a bowl with 3 finely chopped hard-boiled eggs and some chives
5. Strain the mixture through a hair sieve over the bowl and sprinkle nutmeg on top
