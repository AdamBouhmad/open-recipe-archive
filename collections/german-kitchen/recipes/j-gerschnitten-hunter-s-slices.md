## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 whole lemon, zested on a grater
- 4 loth blanched almonds, cut lengthwise
- 6 egg whites
- 6 loth fine flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Whisk together 1/2 pound sugar and 10 egg yolks in a bowl for half an hour
2. Add 1 whole lemon, grated on a box grater
3. Add 4 loth blanched almonds cut lengthwise
4. Fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 6 eggs and 6 loth fine flour
5. Butter a metal form and sprinkle a little sugar in it
6. Pour the mixture in and bake at gentle heat
7. Cut into slices only on the second day
