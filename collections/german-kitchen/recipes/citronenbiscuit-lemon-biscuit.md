## Ingredients

- 1 whole lemon
- sugar (for grating)
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 pound finely pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Grate a whole lemon on a piece of sugar
2. Stir the grated material with 2 egg whites and 1/2 pound finely pounded sugar to a thick icing
3. Roll out this icing on a noodle board
4. Press broad with the hand
5. Dip a biscuit mold in cold water and place the dough in it
6. Bake the biscuits in a cooled oven
