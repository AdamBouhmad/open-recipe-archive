## Ingredients

- Half pound flour
- 4 lots ground almonds
- 4 lots finely ground sugar
- 4 lots melted butter
- Half glass wine
- The cream from half a maa sour milk

## Directions

1. Make a dough from half pound flour, 4 lots ground almonds, 4 lots finely ground sugar, 4 lots melted butter, half glass wine and the cream from half a maa sour milk
2. Roll out to knife back thickness
3. Roll strips onto finger-thick and finger-long wooden sticks that are buttered
4. Tie with thread
5. Bake beautifully yellow in hot lard and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
