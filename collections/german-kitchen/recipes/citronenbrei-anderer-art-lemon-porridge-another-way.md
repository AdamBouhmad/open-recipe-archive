## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 5 to 6 egg yolks
- 2 loth butter
- 3 loth ground sugar with lemon zest
- 2 tablespoons cold milk

## Directions

1. Cook a good thick porridge from 1/2 maas milk
2. Stir 5 to 6 egg yolks in a casserole with 2 loth butter and 3 loth ground sugar with lemon zest rubbed on beforehand pounding
3. Add 2 tablespoons cold milk, let the eggs cook until they begin to thicken
4. Stir in the porridge, let it draw a skin, arrange it on a plate, carefully lift the skin from the pan and place it on top of the porridge
