## Ingredients

- 1 pound good butter
- 1 pound fine flour
- 2 spoons sour cream
- 2 knife-tips salt
- 1 whole and half egg
- cold water

## Directions

1. Weigh 1 pound fine flour for 1 pound good butter and place flour on a pastry board.
2. Make a depression in the center and add 2 spoons sour cream, 2 knife-tips salt, 1 whole and half egg, and some cold water.
3. Work this dough gently.
