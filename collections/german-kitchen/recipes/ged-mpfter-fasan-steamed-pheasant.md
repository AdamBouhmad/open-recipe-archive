## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant, cleaned and prepared
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- mace
- 1/2 schoppen wine
- 1/2 schoppen meat broth
- 3 shallots, minced
- butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 lemon
- chopped lemon peel

## Directions

1. Lard the pheasant breast, truss it, and rub with salt, pepper, cloves, and mace.
2. Place in a casserole and pour 1/2 schoppen wine and 1/2 schoppen meat broth around it.
3. Cover the casserole tightly so no steam escapes and braise slowly for 15 minutes.
4. Remove the pheasant and keep it warm in a covered dish.
5. In a brass pan, braise the minced shallots in a piece of butter until golden.
6. Let 1 tablespoon flour cook briefly in the pan, then add the mixture from the casserole in which the pheasant braised.
7. Cook together for 15 minutes.
8. Add the juice of 1 lemon.
9. Place the pheasant on a platter, pass the sauce through a fine sieve over it, and sprinkle with chopped lemon peel.
10. The sauce should be somewhat thick.
