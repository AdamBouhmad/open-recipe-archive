## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- onions
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the calf's liver into small pieces and cut 2 to 3 onions into it
2. Heat butter in an iron pan with onions for a while
3. Add the liver
4. Turn it often
5. Add 3 finger-widths of flour
6. Turn a couple more times
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Arrange on a plate
9. It is ready in a quarter hour
