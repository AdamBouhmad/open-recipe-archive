## Ingredients

- Borsdorf or Rose apples (6 to 8)
- butter
- sugar
- flour (1 small cooking spoon)
- cold water
- egg yolks (4)
- white wine (1 schoppen)
- sugar (4 loth)
- sugar (for sprinkling)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut finely
2. Stew them very soft with a piece of butter and a piece of sugar
3. Press through a hair sieve
4. For the cream: stir a small cooking spoon of flour with cold water smooth
5. Stir 4 egg yolks into it
6. Pour in one schoppen white wine
7. Cook the cream with 4 loth sugar like a porridge
8. Cook it a bit more with the apples
9. Put it in the designated dish
10. Sprinkle sugar on top
11. Burn it nicely with a glowing shovel
