## Ingredients

- 250 g lingonberries
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 l water

## Directions

1. The lingonberries are sorted through and briefly poured into boiling water so that the tart taste is lost; then the water is drained off.
2. The sugar is added to the drained berries and they are stewed until they are transparent; cooled, they are arranged for serving.
