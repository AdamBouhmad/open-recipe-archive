## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg potatoes
- 1/2 herring or 5 herring bellies
- 25 g onions
- 25 g bacon, fat, or margarine
- 2 peppercorns
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 pinch pepper
- 6 tbsp water

## Directions

1. Place the herring in cold water. Cut the bacon into small cubes. Cook the potatoes, peel them and cut into slices; peel the onions and cut into small cubes; mix water (in which you can previously boil out the herring bones), pepper and salt. Clean the herring, remove the bones, skin it and cut it into small cubes.
2. In a pot, if bacon is used, render it and shake the pot frequently; remove the cracklings with a spoon when they are golden brown. Pour the potato slices into the fat and let them fry quickly, turning them carefully frequently with a wide knife. When they are golden, add the onion and herring cubes and let everything cook for a few more minutes.
