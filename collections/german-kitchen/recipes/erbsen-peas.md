## Ingredients

- peas
- butter or lard
- flour

## Directions

1. Sort and rinse the peas
2. Soak overnight in cold water
3. Cook with cold water until soft
4. Press through a strainer
5. Lightly toast the flour in butter or lard
6. Add to the peas and bring to a boil again
