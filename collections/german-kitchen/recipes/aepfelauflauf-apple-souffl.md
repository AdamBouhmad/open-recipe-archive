## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- good apples
- hard rind from 2 rolls or milk breads
- 1 schoppen milk
- 6 loth sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls sour cream, if needed
- egg whites from 5 eggs
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter lightly, cut good apples into small pieces and put them in the butter
2. Cut the hard rind from 2 rolls or milk breads, cook the soft part, finely cut, in a brass pan with 1 schoppen milk like a porridge
3. Let it cool, take it to the butter, add 6 loth sugar, 5 egg yolks, if necessary 3 spoonfuls sour cream
4. Finally fold in the snow from the 5 egg whites
5. Fill the soufflé into a bowl brushed with butter, brown it lightly and sprinkle sugar over it
