## Ingredients

- Pheasant
- Butter paper
- Wine or vinegar
- Onions
- Lemon
- Celery
- Carrots
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- White pepper
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon
- Paper
- Green ribbon

## Directions

1. Pluck the pheasant but not the head; wrap the head immediately and firmly in butter paper
2. Remove the entrails and rinse inside with wine or vinegar
3. Soak it for a day in wine with onions, lemon, celery, carrots, bay leaves, cloves, and white pepper, turning it frequently
4. The next day, rub it with pepper and salt
5. Bind it well in bacon and wrap it in a sheet of paper
6. Braise it slowly and yellowly in a casserole with the marinade wine, basting frequently
7. Before serving, remove the paper and place the head on the breast, leaving only the bacon on the breast
8. Remove the fat from the roast and pour lemon juice over it
9. Strain the sauce through a hair sieve
10. When the pheasant is on the platter, tie a green ribbon around its neck where the feathers begin
