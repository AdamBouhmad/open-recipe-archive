## Ingredients

- 1 red fish or pike
- small potatoes
- lard
- parsley, finely washed
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Boil a nice red fish or pike blue
2. Peel small potatoes, fry them with lard until golden (they must be half soft boiled beforehand)
3. Garnish the fish with them
4. Mince a handful of finely washed parsley very finely
5. Heat half a pound of butter in a saucepan, add the parsley and cook the fish in it
