## Ingredients

- 1 young chicken
- 10 g salt
- 50 g butter
- 1 bunch parsley
- 20 g bacon
- 1/8 l water or light broth
- 8 g potato starch
- 3 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Prepare, clean and dry the chicken. Rub the inside with salt, place parsley and 10 g butter inside, and tie the chicken into good shape. Tie a bacon strip over the breast or lard the chicken.
2. Heat butter in a braising pot, brown the chicken in it, cover and baste frequently, gradually adding 1/8 l water or light broth. Braise slowly for one hour.
3. Make the sauce creamy by cooking off the browned bits from the pot and stirring in potato starch mixed in cold water, then pass through a sieve.
4. Remove the bacon as desired, glaze the chicken with sauce and serve.
