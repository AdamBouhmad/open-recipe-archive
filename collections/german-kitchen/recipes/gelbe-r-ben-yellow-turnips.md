## Ingredients

- Yellow turnips
- Butter
- Finely minced onions
- Small peeled potatoes
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Scrape the yellow turnips, cut them long and fine, and rub them in a bowl of lukewarm water by hand until all impurities are removed.
2. Braise them with a piece of butter in a casserole together with finely minced onions until soft.
3. Add small peeled potatoes and cook everything together until soft.
4. Pour some meat broth over and arrange the yellow turnips in the middle of the plate with the potatoes arranged around it.
