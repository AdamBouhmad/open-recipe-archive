## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- 4 whole eggs
- half a schoppen of thick sour cream
- 3 loths of ground sugar
- 2 knife points of salt
- one quarter pound of clean small raisins
- one quarter pound of melted butter
- 2 spoons of white beer yeast

## Directions

1. In a bowl, beat 4 whole eggs into 2 pounds of flour
2. Work the dough together with half a schoppen of thick sour cream, 3 loths of ground sugar, 2 knife points of salt, one quarter pound of clean small raisins, and one quarter pound of melted butter
3. Add 2 spoons of white beer yeast
4. Place the dough back in the bowl and let it rise in gentle warmth
5. When ripe, place on a pasta board and shape into round fritters the size of a hen's egg
6. Pull them apart so they become thin as paper in the middle
7. Place in hot lard and fry until golden yellow
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
