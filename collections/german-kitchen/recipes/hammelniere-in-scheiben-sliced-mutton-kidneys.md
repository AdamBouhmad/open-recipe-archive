## Ingredients

- 2 raw mutton kidneys
- 1 pinch salt and pepper
- 1/4 lemon
- 15 g margarine
- 5 g flour
- 1 tablespoon chopped herbs
- 4 tablespoons gravy leftovers or water with 1 knife tip of meat extract
- 1 tablespoon red wine
- 1 pinch sugar

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys thinly and marinate with a little salt, pepper, and a few drops of lemon juice for 1/4 hour.
2. Brown the margarine in a pan. Dry the kidneys with a cloth and toss them quickly in the pan for 1-2 minutes. Remove the slices, place them in a bowl and set in a water bath. Roast 5 g flour in the cooking fat, add herbs and, if desired, sliced mushrooms, gravy sauce or meat extract broth, red wine, lemon juice, and sugar. Let everything cook through and pour the sauce over the kidneys.
