## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg fish
- 50 g fat
- 20 g onions
- 40 g flour
- 1 spice dose
- 2 tablespoons soup greens
- 1 bread crust
- 15 g salt
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 1 bottle brown beer

## Directions

1. Cut the prepared fish into hand-width pieces, then clean them so that the pieces remain round and whole and do not fall apart at the belly during cooking
2. Wash the pieces clean and quickly, dry them and salt with 10 g salt
3. Clean, wash and cut the soup greens into small strips
4. Peel the onions and cut into slices
5. Break up the bread crust
6. Render the fat, pour in the flour and stir well
7. Pour the beer over it, add the spices and let everything cook covered until the soup greens are soft
8. Season the sauce with syrup and 5 g salt
9. Place the fish in the boiling sauce, in which it must slowly cook until done
10. To check if the fish is done, pull out a fin; if it comes off easily without being bloody, the fish is done
11. Serve with the sauce in a bowl
12. Instead of syrup, grated gingerbread can be used, but then use less flour
13. Cook roe separately in boiling salt water and arrange on top
