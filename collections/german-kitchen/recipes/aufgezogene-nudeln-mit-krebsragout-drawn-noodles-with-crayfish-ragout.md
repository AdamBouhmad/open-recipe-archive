## Ingredients

- flour for 2 whole eggs and 3 yolks
- boiling water
- cold water
- 15 large crayfish
- quarter pound butter
- 3 eggs
- melon mold

## Directions

1. Work as much flour as 2 whole eggs and 3 yolks can take on the noodle board.
2. Roll out the dough not very thin, cut noodles as wide as the back of a knife is thick.
3. Boil them in boiling water, pour into a strainer and cold water over.
4. Meanwhile boil 15 large crayfish in boiling water, remove the tails and claws, clean the bodies well.
5. Pound them in the mortar with a quarter pound butter, put into a casserole, place on coals and let them steam well.
6. When the crayfish butter is nicely red, force it through a hair sieve so the butter runs off nicely.
7. Stir it lightly, beat 3 eggs into it, slowly mix the noodles under it.
8. Line a melon mold with butter, pour half the noodles into it.
9. Lay the crayfish tails and claws on the noodles, the other half on top.
10. Place in the oven and let it bake nicely.
