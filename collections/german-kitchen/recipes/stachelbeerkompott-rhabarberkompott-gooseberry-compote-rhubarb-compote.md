## Ingredients

- 250 g unripe gooseberries or rhubarb
- 1 pinch of soda
- 90 g clarified sugar
- 4 tablespoons water
- 1 piece of lemon peel
- 1 g cinnamon
- 2 g cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. The gooseberries are freed from blossom and stem, washed and drained. Rhubarb is peeled if necessary, otherwise cut into pieces about 3 cm long and washed. Both are blanched in boiling water, the berries receive an addition of soda.
2. Gooseberries or rhubarb are poured into the clarified sugar and must steep in it with lemon peel and cinnamon pieces in a covered earthenware pot for 1-1/2 hours. Then they are thickened with cornstarch; the peel and cinnamon are removed when arranging for serving.
