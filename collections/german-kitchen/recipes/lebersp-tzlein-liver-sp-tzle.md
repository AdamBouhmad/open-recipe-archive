## Ingredients

- 1/2 calf's liver
- 2 loth butter or lard
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/2 mäßchen flour
- milk or water as needed
- salt, a handful

## Directions

1. Skin the calf's liver with a knife and remove the veins cleanly
2. Chop the liver very finely and place in a bowl
3. Heat 2 loth butter or lard and pour over the liver
4. Beat in 2 whole eggs and stir together for a while
5. Add 1/2 mäßchen flour and mix thoroughly
6. If the dough is too stiff, add a little milk or water
7. In a pan, bring water to boil with a handful of salt
8. Dip a sieve into the water and press the dough through with a cooking spoon
9. Place the spätzle in a sieve and pour a little water over them
10. Serve with soup or roasted
