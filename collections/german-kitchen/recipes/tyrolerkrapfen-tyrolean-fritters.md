## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound almonds
- 1/4 pound butter
- 6 loth sugar
- 1/4 pound flour
- some aniseed
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon, zest grated
- 2 tablespoons wine

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 1/4 pound almonds, 1/4 pound butter, 6 loth sugar, 1/4 pound flour, some aniseed, 2 egg yolks, and half a lemon zest.
2. Add 2 tablespoons wine.
3. Roll out the dough, cut triangular pieces from it.
4. Lay the fritters on a flour-dusted baking sheet.
5. Coat them with egg and sprinkle coarse pounded sugar on top.
6. Bake them.
