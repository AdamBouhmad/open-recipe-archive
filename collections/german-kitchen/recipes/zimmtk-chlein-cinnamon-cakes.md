## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- sugar
- 1 loth cinnamon
- wax

## Directions

1. Beat 2 egg whites to a stiff snow.
2. Stir sugar and 1 loth cinnamon under it.
3. Coat a black baking sheet with wax.
4. Set small heaps the size of a hazelnut from the mixture on it and bake them in a cooled oven.
