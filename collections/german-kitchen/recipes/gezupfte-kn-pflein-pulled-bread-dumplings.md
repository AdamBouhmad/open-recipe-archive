## Ingredients

- soft crumb from 3 white rolls, torn into small pieces
- 1/2 quarter pound melted butter
- 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- equal parts water and meat broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Tear the soft crumb from 3 white rolls into small pieces.
2. Pour 1/2 quarter pound melted butter over it.
3. Stir in 3 eggs, salt, and nutmeg.
4. Let it stand for half an hour.
5. Place small dumplings, the size of a walnut, made from the dough into half water and half meat broth.
6. Add them to the soup.
