## Ingredients

- eggs
- white bread (Kreuzerbrode)
- butter
- currants
- one egg
- sour cream
- salt
- nutmeg
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, peel them, and place them in cold water.
2. Cut them lengthwise in half and carefully remove the yolk.
3. Cut the hard crust from white bread, soak it in water, and press it firmly out.
4. Let a piece of butter melt in a casserole, add the bread and hard-boiled egg yolks, along with a handful of cleaned small currants.
5. Cook this all together over the fire, beat in one egg, 2 spoonfuls of sour cream, salt and nutmeg.
6. Stir it once more over the fire and fill the hard-boiled egg whites with this mixture.
7. Beat 3 egg yolks in half a pint of sweet cream on the plate intended for serving, pour it over the eggs, and let them become nicely golden in a baking oven.
8. Beat 3 egg yolks in a brass pan, add a small piece of butter and half a pint of sweet cream, let this come together over the fire, and pour the sauce over the eggs.
