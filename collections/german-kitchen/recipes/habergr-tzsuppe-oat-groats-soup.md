## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls of carefully cleaned oat groats
- half a measure of water
- butter
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Let 2 handfuls of carefully cleaned oat groats cook down halfway with half a measure of water
2. Stir in a piece of butter
3. Let it come to a boil again
4. Push it through a hair sieve into another dish
5. Stir in 2 egg yolks, salt and nutmeg
