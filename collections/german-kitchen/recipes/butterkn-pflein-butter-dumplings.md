## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound melted butter
- 6 eggs
- grated breadcrumb flour
- fine flour
- meat broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound melted butter until very foamy.
2. Add 6 eggs, and for each egg add a handful of grated breadcrumb flour and one cooking spoonful of fine flour (the flour is added gradually, not all at once).
3. Form dumplings the size of a walnut.
4. Cook them in boiling meat broth.
5. Add them to the soup.
