## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound cleaned rice
- rich meat broth or butter
- 2 eggs
- butter for coating mold
- lamb meat
- finely chopped onions
- parsley
- lemons
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place 1/4 pound cleaned rice on the fire with rich meat broth or butter and let it cook soft (it should have no more broth)
2. Stir 2 eggs into it in a bowl
3. Coat a melon mold with butter, put the rice in it, press it firmly against the mold so it gets a hollow in the middle, and let it bake in the oven
4. For the ragout: cut lamb meat into pieces and steam it in a covered casserole with finely chopped onions, parsley and lemons for half an hour
5. Sprinkle one cooking spoon of flour over it, add the juice of half a lemon, some meat broth, salt, pepper and nutmeg
6. Arrange the baked rice on a plate and fill the ragout into it
