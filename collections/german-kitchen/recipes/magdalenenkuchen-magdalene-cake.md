## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 2 pounds flour
- 9 egg whites
- preserved fruits
- almonds

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter until creamy
2. Add 9 egg yolks, 1/2 pound sugar, and 1 grated lemon
3. Stir in 2 pounds flour and the snow from 9 egg whites
4. Fill half the mass into a Potageblech
5. Add preserved fruits on top
6. Add the remaining half of the mass over the fruits
7. Sprinkle the cake with almonds
8. Bake at good heat
