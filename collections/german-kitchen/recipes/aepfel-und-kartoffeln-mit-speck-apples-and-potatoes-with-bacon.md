## Ingredients

- Bacon, quantity not specified
- Apples, quantity not specified
- Potatoes, quantity not specified
- 1 onion
- Vinegar to taste
- 20 g sugar
- 1 pinch salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut into quarters. Peel the apples, cut into quarters, and remove the core. Cut the onion into small dice.
2. Cook the potatoes like salted potatoes. Quickly cook the apples in 1/4 liter of water and mix with the drained, dry potato pieces. Fry the onion in the rendered, strained bacon fat; set the cracklings aside in a warm place. Mix the onion, fat, sugar, salt and vinegar with the dish, season it strongly, arrange it mountain-like in a vegetable bowl and sprinkle with the bacon cracklings.
