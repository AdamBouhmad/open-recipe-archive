## Ingredients

- white marshmallow roots, one handful
- lungwort, one handful
- liverwort, one handful
- hearts of blue cabbage, one handful
- 3 spoonfuls fine semolina
- 1 chopped calf's foot and bones
- a knife point of mace
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Cut the marshmallow roots, lungwort, liverwort, and cabbage hearts very finely
2. Add the semolina, chopped calf's foot and bones, mace, and a little butter
3. Cook everything together for 3 to 4 hours
4. Strain through a cloth and let it set in the cellar
5. The next day, remove the fat
6. Give the patient a spoonful morning and evening, mixed with meat broth
