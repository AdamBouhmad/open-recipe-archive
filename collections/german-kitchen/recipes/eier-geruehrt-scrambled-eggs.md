## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons whole milk
- 4 g salt
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 50 g butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with pepper, salt, and milk in a pot
2. Melt the butter in a clean cooking pot over low heat
3. Pour in the egg mixture and push it back and forth on the bottom of the pot with a spoon until a chunky but still creamy mass forms
4. Serve the scrambled eggs on a hot dish
