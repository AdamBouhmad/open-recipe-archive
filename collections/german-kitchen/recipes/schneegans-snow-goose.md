## Ingredients

- snow goose
- herbs
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion with cloves
- lemon
- bacon
- sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck the goose, singe it, and lay it for 4 days with all manner of herbs in vinegar.
2. Cut off the head and neck.
3. Rub the goose with salt and pepper.
4. Place an onion studded with cloves and a piece of lemon inside it.
5. Cut off the feet and stick them into the skin.
6. Wrap the goose in bacon, roast it in the oven until beautifully golden.
7. Place a sheet of paper over it and baste it well with sauce.
