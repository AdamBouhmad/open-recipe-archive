## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 kg (3 lbs) prepared pumpkin
- 4 l vinegar
- 1-1/2 kg (3 lbs) sugar
- 3/4 l water
- Peel of 1/2 lemon, 1 piece ginger and 1 piece cinnamon, 4 cloves (in a muslin bag)
- White paper, 1 tablespoon rum

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin, cut into strips, and weigh it.
2. Pour light vinegar over the prepared pumpkin so that it is completely covered.
3. The next day, cook the sugar with water until it reaches the second degree and let the spice bag cook along with it.
4. The pumpkin, which has meanwhile drained on a colander, cook in the sugar until it appears clear.
5. Remove it with a skimming spoon and cook the juice down thickly, pour it after cooling without the spices over the pumpkin pieces packed in glasses, cover these with rum paper and seal the glasses with parchment paper.
6. The vinegar can be used for preparing sour vegetables or meat or for scrubbing. Leftovers go into the vinegar pot.
