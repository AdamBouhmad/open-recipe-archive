## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- one quarter pound butter
- one quarter pound ground almonds
- half a glass wine
- cinnamon and cloves
- zest of one lemon
- some orange peel
- 2 eggs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Work a smooth dough from one pound flour, one quarter pound butter, one quarter pound ground almonds, half a glass wine, cinnamon and cloves (as desired), zest of one lemon, some orange peel and 2 eggs
2. Make finger-like shapes from it and fry in hot lard
