## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fat
- parsley
- celery
- chives
- onions
- butter (hen-egg size)
- 1 cooking spoonful of flour
- meat broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls of sour cream
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel and wash raw potatoes and braise them soft with a piece of fat in a pot
2. Braise parsley, celery and chives together soft and push through a sieve
3. Finely mince onion and parsley and braise with a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg in a pot
4. Add one cooking spoonful of flour and the potatoes
5. Pour meat broth over the whole thing and let it come to a boil again
6. Place two egg yolks with two spoonfuls of sour cream in the bowl and serve the soup; add nutmeg and salt
