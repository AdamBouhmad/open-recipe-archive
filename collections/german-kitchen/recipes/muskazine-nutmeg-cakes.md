## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound almonds
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 pound sifted sugar
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 3 loth finely chopped citron and orange peel
- 1 loth cinnamon
- 1 quint cloves

## Directions

1. Blanch and finely grind the almonds
2. Mix almonds with eggs, egg white, sifted sugar, lemon zest, citron and orange peel, cinnamon, and cloves
3. Stir the mixture together for half an hour
4. Place dough on a noodle board dusted with sugar and flour
5. Dust the molds with sugar and press dough firmly into them
6. Place wafers on a dark baking sheet
7. Turn the Muskazinen out from the molds onto the wafers
8. Bake
