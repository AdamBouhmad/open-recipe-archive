## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen thick sweet cream, skimmed with a spoon
- 6 loth pounded sugar
- half a glass of Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Mix the pounded sugar and Champagne wine into the thick sweet cream
2. Whip the entire mixture in a wide pot with a chocolate whisk until foamy
3. Bring the foam to a fine sieve with a spoon and continue until all is done
4. Place the foam on a plate and sprinkle with sugar
