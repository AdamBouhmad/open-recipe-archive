## Ingredients

- 3 white rolls
- 1/4 pound blanched almonds
- egg white
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound pounded sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound pitted cherries
- 6 egg whites
- 1 orange
- 1 pint sweet wine
- 1 pint milk
- 1 small glass cherry brandy
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the rind from 3 white rolls, soak them, press them out, pound 1/4 pound blanched almonds with egg white.
2. Beat 1/4 pound butter foamy, add 1/4 pound pounded sugar, the rolls, 6 egg yolks, 1/2 pound pitted cherries and the snow beaten from 6 egg whites, stir everything thoroughly together.
3. Fill the mass into a damp cloth, tie it closed firmly, cook the pudding one hour in boiling water.
4. For the orange sauce: rub an orange over sugar, peel away the white skin, cut the flesh into small pieces, let it cook in a casserole with 1 pint of sweet wine and the grated sugar for a time, then add 1 pint of milk, let it cook again, add 1 small glass full of cherry brandy and 2 egg yolks, press the sauce through a sieve and pour it over the pudding.
