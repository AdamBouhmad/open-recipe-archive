## Ingredients

- thick porridge
- butter
- 2 to 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook a thick porridge, then let a piece of butter melt in a dish, beat 2 to 3 eggs into it
2. Stir them on the fire until the eggs begin to thicken, then stir them with the porridge and let a crust cook on it
