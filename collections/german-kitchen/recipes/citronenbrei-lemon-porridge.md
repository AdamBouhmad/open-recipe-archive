## Ingredients

- porridge
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a good thick porridge, stir 2 egg yolks into it
2. Rub the yellow of 1 lemon on sugar, pound this and mix it under the porridge
