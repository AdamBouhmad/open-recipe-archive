## Ingredients

- 350 g veal loin
- 5 g salt
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 30 g grated bread roll
- 1 egg white
- 60 g butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Cut and strike small pieces of the knuckle until they are evenly sized and not too thin.
2. Lightly salt and pepper them and coat with bread crumbs.
3. Brown the fat in the pan and let it settle.
4. Lay the cutlets in the steaming fat.
5. Once they have a light brown crust on the bottom side, turn them and fry them slowly but quickly enough so the coating does not become soggy from the meat juices that develop.
6. Frying time: 6 minutes per 85 g.
7. The cutlets must be elastic and not become hard in the pan.
8. Do not cover the plated cutlets, otherwise the crust will become soft.
