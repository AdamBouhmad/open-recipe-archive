## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- 20 egg whites
- one lemon
- 24 Loth of cornstarch
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir together one pound of sugar with 20 egg yolks and the snow (beaten whites) of 20 egg whites for half an hour.
2. When the mixture is thick, add one lemon rubbed on a grater along with its juice and 24 Loth of cornstarch to the mixture.
3. Butter a cake form, fill everything in and bake the cake in the oven.
