## Ingredients

- calf sweetbreads
- butter
- onion
- lemon
- bay leaf
- 2 blades of mace
- meat broth
- flour
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Lay the sweetbreads in lukewarm water to remove the blood
2. Put them in cold water and bring to a boil
3. Remove them and pass through cold water again
4. Remove the membrane and lard them nicely
5. Put a piece of butter in a casserole, lay the sweetbreads with sliced onion, lemon, bay leaf, and mace in the butter
6. Pour a little meat broth over them and braise covered for half an hour
7. Sprinkle three finger widths of flour over the sweetbreads, turn them over, pour a little cold water over, fill with meat broth, and let everything cook together for a quarter hour
8. When serving, lay the sweetbreads on a plate, strain the sauce through a fine sieve over them, and salt to taste
