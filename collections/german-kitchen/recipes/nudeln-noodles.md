## Ingredients

- Flour as needed
- 3 egg whites
- 1 knife-tip of salt
- 1 large hen's egg of butter
- Milk
- 1 large piece of butter
- Sour cream (optional)
- Sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take flour as needed on the noodle board, beat 3 egg whites together with 1 knife-tip of salt, 1 large hen's egg of butter and milk until the dough can be worked
2. Roll it with the hand as thin as a quill and 1 ell long
3. Make milk hot in a plafond, add a large piece of butter along with the noodles and let them cook together
4. They should have some sauce; you can also add sour cream along with sugar and cinnamon
