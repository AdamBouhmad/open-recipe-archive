## Ingredients

- 125 g meat scraps
- 1/2 sour cucumber
- 20 g bacon or fat
- 20 g onions
- 20 g flour
- 1 spice mixture, ground
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 drop food coloring
- 5 g sugar
- 5 g salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into clean uniform pieces, dice the cucumber, and grate the onion.
2. In an earthenware pot, sauté bacon or fat with onion and flour until yellow, gradually add the water colored with food coloring while stirring, as well as the spice mixture, let the sauce cook thick, season it with salt, sugar, and vinegar, braise the cucumber dice in it, add the meat which should only be heated and not boiled, and serve immediately.
