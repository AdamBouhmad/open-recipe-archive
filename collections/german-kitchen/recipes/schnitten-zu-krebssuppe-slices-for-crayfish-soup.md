## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- 8 egg yolks
- half a schoppen of thick sour cream
- 8 spoonfuls of fine flour
- salt
- mace
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Lightly beat the butter
2. Gradually add 8 egg yolks
3. Add half a schoppen of thick sour cream in the same manner
4. Add 8 spoonfuls of fine flour, salt, and mace
5. Finally fold in the snow from 8 egg whites
6. Brush a tin mold with butter
7. Bake the mixture in the mold at gentle heat
8. Cut into slices and add to crayfish soup
