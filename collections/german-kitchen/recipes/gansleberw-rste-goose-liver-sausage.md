## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 large goose livers (not soaked or only slightly soaked)
- 3 quarter-pounds of very finely minced ham
- onions
- one handful of parsley
- bacon
- the zest of 2 lemons, finely minced
- the pith of 2 lemons without seeds, finely minced
- white bread for 2 kreuzer, soaked in milk and pressed out
- 3 knife tips of white pepper
- 2 knife tips of nutmeg
- half a pound of clean, peeled, and finely pounded truffles

## Directions

1. Mince 2 of the goose livers finely, cut 2 into cubes
2. Mix all the livers together in a bowl with the finely minced ham, onions, and parsley
3. Braise everything in bacon
4. Add the finely minced lemon zest, the finely minced lemon pith, the soaked and pressed bread, white pepper, and nutmeg
5. Mix in the finely pounded truffles
6. Combine everything with the liver mixture and salt strongly
7. Fill into beef casings of the type usually used for lung sausage
8. Do not tie the casing firmly at the bottom, and fill it with the mass using a tablespoon, not pressing too firmly
