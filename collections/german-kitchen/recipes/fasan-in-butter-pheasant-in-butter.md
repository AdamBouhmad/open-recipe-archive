## Ingredients

- pheasant, meat portions only
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- 1/8 pound butter
- 1 handful parsley, washed
- 2 shallots
- 1/2 lemon, zest and juice

## Directions

1. Rub the pheasant pieces with salt, pepper, and cloves.
2. Brown them in 1/8 pound butter in a casserole until golden.
3. Finely mince the parsley, shallots, and lemon zest.
4. Let this mixture braise in the casserole for a while.
5. Add the juice of 1/2 lemon.
