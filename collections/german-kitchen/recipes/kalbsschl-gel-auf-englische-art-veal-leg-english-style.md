## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Basil
- Bay leaves
- Lime
- Shallot onions
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Flour
- Sour cream
- Capers
- Lime rind

## Directions

1. Place the veal leg in a large bowl, add salt, pepper, thyme, basil, bay leaves, the zest of a lime cut into leaves and a couple of shallot onions.
2. Pour vinegar over it and let it stand overnight.
3. The next day, place the leg with the vinegar in a roasting pan 2 hours before serving, add a piece of fresh butter and baste frequently with the roasting broth so it achieves a nice golden color.
4. Make the sauce as follows: in a casserole, brown a piece of butter with a spoonful of flour, add some chopped shallot onions, pour in a small ladle of jus and half a bottle of sour cream.
5. Let the sauce cook with capers, a little cut lime zest and the juice of a lime.
6. Place the leg on the plate and pour the sauce over it.
