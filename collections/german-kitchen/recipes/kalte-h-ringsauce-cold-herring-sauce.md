## Ingredients

- one fine herring, male
- a small piece of butter
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- the herring milk
- 2 spoonfuls of Provençal oil
- some vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the herring thoroughly, removing the bones
2. Pound the herring together with the small piece of butter and hard-boiled egg yolks in a mortar until very fine
3. Pass everything through a hair sieve
4. Take the milk from the herring and stir it with 2 spoonfuls of Provençal oil, some vinegar, and pepper
5. Mix everything together and serve the sauce with beef
