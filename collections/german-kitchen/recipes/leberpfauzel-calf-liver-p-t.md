## Ingredients

- 1 calf liver, cleaned and skinned
- half a pound of bacon
- 2 kreuzers worth of bread
- water
- 2 onions, finely chopped and steamed in fat
- zest of one lemon
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the calf liver, place it on a chopping block and mince it together with half a pound of bacon very finely
2. Grate bread worth 2 kreuzers on a grater, soak it in water, then press it out
3. Place the minced liver in a bowl with the bread, add 2 finely chopped onions that have been steamed in fat, the zest of one lemon, mace, pepper, salt, and 4 eggs, stirring in one egg after another
4. Stir everything vigorously for a quarter hour
5. Put the mixture in a large calf's caul and bake it at gentle heat; it will be ready in half an hour
6. Can be served as a side dish or as a roast with jus
7. For sauce: roast a good amount of onions in a piece of fat and a little sugar in a saucepan until light yellow, dissolve as much flour as can be held between two fingers with a ladle full of meat broth, add a little raspberry vinegar, let it cook together for a quarter hour, season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste
8. Place the baked liver on a plate, pour the sauce over it, and top with softly cooked morels
