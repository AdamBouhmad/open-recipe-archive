## Ingredients

- 1 measure of cream
- 1 measure of wine
- half a vierling of sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- butter
- diced rolls
- saffron
- salt

## Directions

1. Warm 1 measure of cream and 1 measure of wine separately
2. Cook half a vierling of sugar, mixed with zest of a lemon, together with 4 egg yolks and the wine and cream
3. Roast diced rolls in lard
4. Lay them in the prepared bowl
5. Pour the cooked mixture over them
6. Sprinkle saffron on the soup
