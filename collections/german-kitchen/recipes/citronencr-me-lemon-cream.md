## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1/2 maas wine
- juice of the lemons
- 6 egg yolks
- a little flour
- 2 tablespoons water
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar with the zest of 2 lemons and cook with half a maas of wine and the juice of the lemons for 4 minutes.
2. Stir 6 egg yolks smooth with a little flour and 2 tablespoons of water.
3. Slowly stir the cooked wine into the eggs.
4. Let everything come together in the pan once more until it becomes smooth and thick.
5. Beat the 6 egg whites into stiff snow.
6. Fold the snow under the cream.
7. Once cooled, brown it with a glowing spatula dusted with sugar.
