## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salt water
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the calf's head carefully
2. Break out the teeth
3. Cook the head soft in salt water
4. Remove it
5. Brush it with butter
6. Sprinkle it with grated bread
7. Place it on the grill
8. Let it fry golden brown
