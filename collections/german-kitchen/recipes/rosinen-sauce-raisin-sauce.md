## Ingredients

- 1/3 l water (preferably broth)
- 5 g salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons syrup
- 20 g large raisins
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- fat
- flour
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and cut into cubes. Clean the raisins according to instructions and soak until plump. Season 1/3 l liquid with salt, vinegar and syrup.
2. In an earthenware pot, heat the fat, add the sugar for browning, add the flour and onion cubes and let them brown as well. Stirring constantly, pour in the liquid and bring everything to a boil. Add the drained raisins; season with a bit of salt and pepper, and serve the sauce with beef.
