## Ingredients

- turnips
- sugar
- roasted chestnuts
- pork foot
- pork ear
- roasted bratwurst

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the turnips.
2. Brown them in sugar.
3. Add the roasted chestnuts with a pork foot and pork ear.
4. When everything is tender, cut several roasted bratwursts into small pieces and add them.
5. Arrange the turnips on the plate.
