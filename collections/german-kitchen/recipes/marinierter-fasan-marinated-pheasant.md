## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- onions
- lemons
- celery
- yellow turnips
- bay leaves
- cloves
- white pepper
- salt
- bacon
- wine from the marinade
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Turn the pheasant frequently in a marinade of onions, lemons, celery, yellow turnips, bay leaves, cloves, and white pepper.
2. The next day, rub with pepper and salt.
3. Bind well in bacon, wrap in a piece of paper.
4. In a casserole with the wine from the marinade, braise slowly until golden.
5. Baste frequently.
6. Before serving, remove the paper and tuck the head to the chest (only the bacon on the breast should remain visible).
7. Remove the fat from the braise, pour lemon juice over it.
8. Pass the sauce through a fine sieve.
9. When plated, tie a green ribbon around the pheasant's neck where the feathers begin.
