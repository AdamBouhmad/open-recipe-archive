## Ingredients

- 5 Old bread rolls
- 1/8 l Whole milk
- 1/8 l Water
- 1 pinch Ground cinnamon
- 15 g Salt
- 40 g Sugar
- 15 g Fat or margarine
- 1 Egg
- 5 g Fat or margarine

## Directions

1. Grease a shallow dish with a little fat, bring water to a boil for cooking the dumplings, grate the bread, and melt 15 g fat.
2. Whisk together the milk, water, cinnamon, salt, sugar, melted fat, and egg, stir in the breadcrumbs, pour the mixture into the greased dish, place it in boiling water, cover it, and cook for 1-1/2 hours.
3. Remove the dumplings with a spoon, plate them on a hot dish, and serve with stewed fruit or sauce.
4. You can refine these dumplings with 6 grated almonds.
