## Ingredients

- 250 g cooked lean meat
- 1/16 l cold broth
- 1/2 tsp mustard
- 1/4 tsp grated onion
- 1 tbsp wine vinegar
- 1 tsp chopped herbs
- 1 tsp salt and pepper
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 pickled gherkin

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes or small fillets. Slice the gherkin.
2. Mix the meat with the broth and then with mustard, onions, vinegar, and herbs. Season with pepper, salt, and optionally 4 drops of Maggi seasoning. Cover and let stand for 1-2 hours.
3. Arrange the salad mixed with oil and taste again for seasoning, arranging it in a mound. Garnish with the gherkin sliced into uniform slices, arranging them in an overlapping circle, and sprinkle with the gherkin ends cut into small cubes.
4. This salad can be refined by using pearl onions, capers, etc. as ingredients. Fish or poultry salad are prepared the same way, only the pieces are irregular and of arbitrary size.
