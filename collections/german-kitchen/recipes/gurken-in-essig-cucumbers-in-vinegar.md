## Ingredients

- Kleine Gurken (Small cucumbers)
- Salz (Salt)
- Meerrettig (Horseradish)
- Schalottenzwiebeln (Shallot onions)
- Lorbeerblätter (Bay leaves)
- Pfeffer (Peppercorns)
- Nelken (Cloves)
- Estragon (Tarragon)
- Basilikum (Basil)
- Thymian (Thyme)
- Senfmehl (Mustard flour)

## Directions

1. Rub small cucumbers with a cloth
2. Place them in a large dish and cover with strong salt water
3. Let them stand for 48 hours, stirring often (pump water is better than well water because it is harder)
4. When ready to preserve, lay them on a cloth and let them dry well
5. Cut horseradish into small pieces and peel shallot onions
6. Gather together bay leaves, whole peppercorns, some whole cloves, tarragon, basil, and thyme
7. In the designated dish, layer cucumbers alternately with the mixture of spices
8. Place a long sachet containing some mustard flour on top of the cucumbers
9. Tie the dish shut and store it for the winter
