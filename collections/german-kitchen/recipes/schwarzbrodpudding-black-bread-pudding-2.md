## Ingredients

- 5 loth black bread (not sour)
- 1 whole nutmeg
- 2 knife tips of pounded cinnamon
- 6 loth grated and pounded almonds
- 1 knife tip of pounded cloves
- 1 glass Malaga wine
- 1/4 pound

## Directions

1. Grate 5 loth black bread that is not sour
2. Also grate 1 whole nutmeg
3. Add 2 knife tips of pounded cinnamon, 6 loth grated and pounded almonds and 1 knife tip of pounded cloves
4. Dampen everything with 1 glass of Malaga wine
5. Continue with 1/4 pound (recipe incomplete)
