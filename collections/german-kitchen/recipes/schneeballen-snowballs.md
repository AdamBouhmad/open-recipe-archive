## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 2 spoons crushed sugar
- Grated lemon peel
- 2 spoons thick sour cream
- 6 lots flour

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients together on a noodle board
2. Roll out a dough as for noodles
3. Form about 4 inch large circles with the pastry wheel
4. Draw 6 lines in each circle, leaving a small finger-width border
5. Lift the second, fourth and sixth strips upward so the others sink down
6. Bring the balls into a not too large pan with boiling lard
7. Bake them a beautiful yellow and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
