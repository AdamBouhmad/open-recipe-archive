## Ingredients

- 8 lemons
- 1/4 pound almonds
- rose water
- 8 eggs
- 8 loths fine ground sugar
- 2 grated Kreuzerbrode
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Rub 8 lemons on a grater and remove the white skin
2. Cut the pulp into pieces and press out the pits
3. Mix 1/4 pound almonds with rose water, finely ground, together with the lemon pulp and the yellow from the grated lemons
4. Beat 8 eggs and mix in
5. Stir 8 loths fine ground sugar and 2 grated Kreuzerbrode into the mixture and beat everything together for half an hour
6. Coat a plafond with butter, fill the mass into it
7. Bake the cake in the oven until beautiful yellow
8. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
