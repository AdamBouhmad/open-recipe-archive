## Ingredients

- a strong handful of fine flour
- a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
- one schoppen of milk
- 5 egg yolks and one whole egg
- one loth of sugar
- the yellow of one lemon
- 2 coffee cups of sweet cream (if needed to thin)
- one quarter pound of confectionery (e.g. one quarter pound of macaroons)
- 2 loths of citron and orange peel
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together a strong handful of fine flour and a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg with one schoppen of milk
2. Place on the fire and continue stirring until it becomes a thick paste
3. Let it cool
4. Stir together 5 egg yolks and one whole egg with one loth of sugar and the yellow of one lemon
5. If too thick, thin with 2 coffee cups of sweet cream
6. Cut one quarter pound of confectionery (such as macaroons) and stir in along with 2 loths of citron and orange peel into the mass
7. Grease a springform pan with butter
8. Beat 5 egg whites into stiff peaks and fold into the mass
9. Immediately bake in the oven until beautifully golden yellow
