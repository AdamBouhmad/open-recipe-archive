## Ingredients

- Butter
- One minced onion
- Lemon
- One small tablespoon of flour
- One ladle-full of meat broth
- One tablespoon of mustard
- Two egg yolks
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a casserole with one minced onion and some lemon and let it cook slightly
2. Sprinkle one small tablespoon of flour over it
3. Let it steam for a couple of minutes
4. Pour in one ladle-full of meat broth and one tablespoon of mustard
5. Let everything cook together
6. Beat two egg yolks in a dish with one tablespoon of cold water
7. Slowly strain the sauce through a hair sieve onto the eggs
