## Ingredients

- Yellow turnips
- Finely minced onions
- Parsley
- Butter
- Half a schoppen of good sweet cream
- Flour
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the turnips the same way as the previous recipe.
2. Braise them in a casserole with finely minced onions and parsley in butter until soft.
3. Pour half a schoppen of good sweet cream over them, dust with a little flour and sugar, and cook for another quarter hour.
