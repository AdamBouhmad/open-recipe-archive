## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound blanched, finely ground almonds
- 1/4 pound finely grated chocolate
- 2 loth starch flour
- 6 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Whisk together 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar and 3 egg yolks for a quarter hour
2. Add 1/4 pound blanched, finely ground almonds and 1/4 pound finely grated chocolate
3. Mix in 2 loth starch flour
4. Fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 6 eggs
5. Butter a baking sheet and fill with the mixture
6. Bake until nicely golden
7. Cut into slices only on the second day
