## Ingredients

- 10 g onions
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 20 g flour
- 1/3 l water (better: broth)
- 5 g salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons syrup
- 20 g large raisins
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the onion into cubes. Clean and soak the raisins according to instructions. Season 1/3 l of liquid with salt, vinegar, and syrup.
2. In an earthenware pot, let the fat cook, add the sugar for browning, add the flour and onion cubes and let them brown as well. Stirring, pour in the liquid and bring to a boil.
3. Add the drained raisins, season with salt and pepper, and serve the sauce with beef.
