## Ingredients

- 250 g sweet blanched grated almonds, 12 pieces bitter
- 280 g sifted sugar
- 7-8 egg whites
- 5 g baking powder
- 1 piece of bacon rind for the sheet

## Directions

1. Prepare the ingredients.
2. Stir the almonds, sugar, and 1 egg white over heat; fold the stiffly beaten snow of the remaining egg whites first into the stirred mixture, then add the baking powder. With a teaspoon, place small heaps on the greased baking sheet, slide into the oven immediately, and bake the macaroons pale yellow. Instead of almonds and sugar, one can use marzipan scraps; instead of grated almonds, use grated coconut, 1/2 kg (1 pound) available at the confectioner for M. 0.35. (Coconut Macaroons.)
