## Ingredients

- Bacon
- 2 to 3 onions
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- Milk
- 3 eggs
- Meat of any kind

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into cubes and let it melt in a pancake pan
2. Cut 2 to 3 onions lengthwise and add to the bacon
3. Mix 1 cooking spoon flour with milk and beat 3 eggs into it
4. Cut meat of any kind into small pieces and lay it in the pan
5. Pour the dough over the meat
6. Cover the pan and bake the pancake on both sides
