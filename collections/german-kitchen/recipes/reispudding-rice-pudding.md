## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice, well washed
- 1/2 maas milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 8 loth finely ground almonds
- 6 loth sugar
- egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls apricot jam
- 1 schoppen Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Cook the rice very soft with half a maas milk, press it through a hair sieve
2. Stir 1/4 pound butter lightly in a bowl, add 8 loth finely ground almonds and 6 loth sugar
3. Lay 1 spoonful of rice in the butter and 1 egg yolk to it, continue this way to the end
4. Also stir in the almonds, beat the egg whites to stiff peaks, stir them slowly into the mass
5. Brush a melon mold with butter, put the mass in it, place in the oven and let bake well
6. The pudding is baked in about one hour
7. For the apricot sauce: make from 3 spoonfuls of apricot jam and 1 schoppen Rhine wine, let it come to boil together and pour it over the pudding
