## Ingredients

- feet, neck, and head of a Welsh chicken
- wings from the second joint
- butter
- onion, sliced
- milk bread, sliced, worth 2 kreuzer
- good jus
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the feet, neck, and head of a Welsh chicken, and also the wings from the second joint, and chop everything very finely.
2. Braise sliced onion, the chopped chicken pieces, and the sliced milk bread in a piece of butter for half an hour.
3. Pour good jus over it and let it cook together for one hour.
4. Beat 3 egg yolks with 2 spoonfuls of sour cream in the soup bowl.
5. Pass the soup through a hair sieve once more.
6. Let it heat again in the saucepan, stir it into the soup bowl, and season with salt and nutmeg.
