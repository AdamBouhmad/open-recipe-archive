## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- fresh water
- wine
- vinegar
- various herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the ears out of the fish and gut it
2. Salt it and lay it in fresh water for one hour
3. Salt it again and lay it in water once more
4. Make a good sauce of wine, vinegar and all kinds of herbs and add to it
