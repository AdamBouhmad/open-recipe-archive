## Ingredients

- Capercaillie
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Mace
- 1 Onion
- Half a lemon
- 1 Bay leaf
- Butter
- Pork skin (from half the side of a young pig)
- Wine (1 Schoppen)
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. The bird can be kept dead for 6 days; pluck it, singe it, and clean it carefully with a cloth without washing if possible
2. Remove the entrails carefully so the gall is not crushed
3. Rub it with salt, pepper, cloves, and mace
4. Cut into the breastbone and remove the claws but leave the feet on the body
5. Dress it nicely and fill it with an onion, half a lemon, a bay leaf, and a piece of butter
6. Bind it firmly together and cut off the head and neck so the skin remains on the body
7. Wrap it in the skin of half the side of a young pig and sew it firmly with a strong trussing needle
8. Roast it slowly and yellowly in the oven; if young it takes 2 hours, if old 3 hours
9. Pour a Schoppen of wine over it and baste it frequently
10. Serve it with the skin and garnish with parsley and lemon
11. Treat the sauce as for roasted turkey
