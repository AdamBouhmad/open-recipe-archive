## Ingredients

- half Maas cream
- biscuit slices
- roasted almonds
- 2 Loth vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat half a Maas cream in a bowl with a white beater like egg snow
2. Lay biscuit slices on a plate
3. Sprinkle roasted almonds on top
4. Mix 2 Loth vanilla sugar under the snow
5. Pour this mixture around the slices
