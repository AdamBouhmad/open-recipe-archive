## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 1 sprig mugwort
- 8 apples
- 5 g sugar
- 10 g salt
- 5 g potato starch
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Prepare the duck according to the poultry preparation instructions.
2. Remove the neck, wings and feet, wash the duck, rub it inside and out with salt and fill it with whole apples that have been quartered, peeled and mixed with sugar and mugwort.
3. Tie it into a good shape, place it in a roasting pan, pour some boiling water over it and roast it, basting frequently, for 1-1 1/2 hours until done.
4. Arrange the duck and place the apples around it.
5. Thicken the sauce with potato starch stirred in cold water.
