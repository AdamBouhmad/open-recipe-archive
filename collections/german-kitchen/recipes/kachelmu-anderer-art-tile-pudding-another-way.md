## Ingredients

- half a Maas milk
- sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 spoonful flour
- cold milk
- 3 eggs
- butter
- a small handful raisins

## Directions

1. In a brass pan bring half a Maas milk with a piece of sugar rubbed with lemon peel to a boil.
2. Stir 1 spoonful flour smooth with cold milk.
3. Beat 3 eggs into it and stir into the milk.
4. Coat a tile with butter.
5. Pour the mixture with a small handful of raisins into it.
6. Bake in an oven or between coals.
