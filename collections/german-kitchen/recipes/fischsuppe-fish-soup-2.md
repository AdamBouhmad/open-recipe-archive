## Ingredients

- Fish
- Butter
- Onion
- Fish bones
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the meat from the bones of a fish (usually white fish, pike, or barbel, or eel)
2. Place a piece of butter in a casserole
3. Cut onion into pieces worth 2 Kreuzer of wax
4. Chop the fish bones and add some parsley
