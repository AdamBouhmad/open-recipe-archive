## Ingredients

- Beef or ox meat
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare a meat broth from beef or ox meat, water, and a little salt by cooking for several hours. This is called simple or common meat broth.
2. In larger kitchens, at courts, in inns, and in larger families, much poultry trimmings fall away, which one cleans thoroughly, binds together with thread, and either cooks with the meat or in a separate pot into which simple broth has been poured; one may also add a piece of ham, a veal knuckle, and other clean bones left over from roasting, and cook this always with the ox meat.
