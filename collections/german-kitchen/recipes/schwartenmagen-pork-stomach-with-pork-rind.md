## Ingredients

- 2 pounds pork meat from the jaw
- 2 pounds pork from the neck
- 1 pound pork forcemeat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Marjoram
- Ginger
- 1 glass pork blood
- Pork stomach or wide beef casing

## Directions

1. Simmer the jaw and neck pork until soft
2. Cut into long pieces and place in a bowl
3. Add 1 pound pork forcemeat
4. Season with salt, pepper, cloves, marjoram, and ginger as desired
5. Add a glass of pork blood
6. Mix everything together
7. Fill a pork stomach or wide beef casing with the mixture
8. Tie it securely closed
9. Scald it like any other sausage and prick it a little with a fork
10. When finished, place it between 2 wooden plates, put a stone on top, and hang it in smoke for 4 days
