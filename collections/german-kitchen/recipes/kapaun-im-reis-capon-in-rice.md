## Ingredients

- One capon
- Salt
- White pepper, pounded
- Bacon
- Lemon
- Half pound rice
- Good meat broth
- Butter
- Mace
- Lemon peel
- Four eggs
- One cooking spoon sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the capon for roasting as usual.
2. Place it in lukewarm water.
3. Rub with salt and pounded white pepper.
4. Bind it with bacon and lemon.
5. Braise it in a casserole until half done.
6. Cook half a pound rice with good meat broth.
7. Stir in a piece of butter, add mace and lemon peel.
8. Place half the rice in a bowl, add the capon, pour the other half over it.
9. Mix four eggs with one cooking spoon of sour cream, pour this over the rice.
10. Brush a ring mold with butter and place it over the dish.
11. Set in the oven until the rice has puffed up.
