## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sifted sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- 1/2 lemon
- 8 Loth flour
- wafers (Oblaten)

## Directions

1. Add the snow from 2 egg whites and 2 egg yolks to 1/2 pound sifted sugar
2. Stir for a quarter hour
3. Stir in the yolk of 1 lemon (grated on a grater) and the juice from half a lemon
4. Mix in 8 Loth flour
5. Place long or round loaves from this mass on wafers on a sheet
6. Bake them
