## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 calf thymus
- 50 asparagus
- salt
- lemon
- shallot onion
- butter
- nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cooking spoonful flour
- cold water
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean 2 to 3 thymus, blanch them slightly, place in cold water
2. Clean 50 asparagus, cook them soft in salt water, remove and cut into pieces
3. Finely chop half a lemon peel and a shallot onion and steam them with a piece of butter in a casserole
4. Cut the thymus, add them to the casserole, squeeze the juice of half a lemon over them
5. Add salt and nutmeg, let everything steam for another quarter hour
6. Mix 3 egg yolks with 1 cooking spoonful flour and cold water until smooth
7. Pour this with 1 spoonful of good meat broth to the thymus, cook a little longer
8. Arrange on a plate and serve the asparagus with it
