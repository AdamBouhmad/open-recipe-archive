## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 loth citron and orange peel
- 3 loth blanched almonds
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- 6 loth fine flour
- Sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with finely ground sugar in a bowl for a quarter hour
2. Weigh citron and orange peel, blanched almonds, and lemon zest together, not too finely
3. Mix into the egg mixture
4. Stir in fine flour
5. Place stiff paper on a dark baking sheet and sprinkle with sugar
6. Place spoonfuls of batter on the paper
7. Test bake to see if they spread; if they do, add more flour
8. Bake until done
