## Ingredients

- Semolina
- Milk
- Sugar
- Orange peel
- Salt
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Rinse molds or cups with water
2. Rinse and drain the semolina
3. Bring milk, sugar, orange peel, and a pinch of salt to a boil
4. Mix the semolina with some of the liquid, then stir into the milk and cook for 15 minutes
5. Beat the egg white until stiff and gently fold in after the semolina is cooked
6. Pour the pudding into the rinsed molds to cool
7. Once cooled, unmold the pudding and serve with braised fruit or fruit sauce
8. Optional: refine with grated almonds or vanilla and 1 egg yolk
9. Variation: use cornstarch instead of semolina, stirred cold (Cornstarch Pudding); use red fruit juice with water instead of milk and omit the egg white (Red Fruit Pudding); or use very thin apple puree instead of milk (Apple Pudding)
