## Ingredients

- good butter dough
- grapes
- half pound finely cut almonds
- quarter pound ground sugar
- half loth ground cinnamon
- 12 egg whites snow

## Directions

1. Lay good butter dough in a baking sheet
2. Place picked grapes over it
3. Mix half pound finely cut almonds, quarter pound ground sugar, half loth ground cinnamon, the snow from 12 egg whites together
4. Pour the mass over the grapes
5. Bake the cake in a not too hot oven
