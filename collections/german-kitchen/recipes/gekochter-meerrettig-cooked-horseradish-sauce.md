## Ingredients

- 2 loths of butter
- 1 tablespoon of flour
- horseradish
- 3 tablespoons of sweet cream
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a small pan
2. Add the flour
3. Add the horseradish and steam everything together for 3 minutes
4. Stir smooth with the sweet cream
5. Adjust the consistency with beef broth
