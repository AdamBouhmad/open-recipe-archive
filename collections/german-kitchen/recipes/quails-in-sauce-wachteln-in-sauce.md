## Ingredients

- quails, cleaned and plucked
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- 1 to 2 glasses wine
- flour
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Remove the breastbone from each cleaned and well-prepared quail
2. Fill each with ham that has been turned in salt and pepper
3. Dress them nicely, place them in a casserole
4. Pour 1 to 2 glasses of wine over them (depending on how many quails)
5. Cover and let them steam until soft
6. Arrange them on a plate
7. Add 2 knife points of flour to the sauce, the juice of 1 lemon and half a grated nutmeg
8. Strain the sauce through a sieve over the quails
