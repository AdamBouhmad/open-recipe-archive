## Ingredients

- 200 g Rindfleisch (beef)
- 3/4 kg geschälte Kartoffeln (peeled potatoes)
- Wasser und Salz zum Abwellen (water and salt for blanching)
- 15 g Suppengrün (vegetable soup greens)
- 3/4 l Wasser (water)
- 10 g Salz (salt)
- 1 Teel. Petersilie oder 1/4 Teel. getrockneter Majoran (1 teaspoon parsley or 1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram)
- 1 Prise Salz (pinch of salt)

## Directions

1. Wash and pound the meat; blanch the sliced potatoes in salted water and drain on a sieve.
2. Put 3/4 l water and 10 g salt on the stove to heat.
3. Place the meat in the boiling salted water; after it has cooked for 1/2 hour, skim it; add the vegetable soup greens and cook slowly with the meat for another 1/2 hour.
4. Add the blanched potatoes, parsley or marjoram; cook everything together for another 1/2 hour.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
