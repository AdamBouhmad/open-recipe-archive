## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- one quarter pound finely sifted sugar
- a piece of pounded vanilla
- one quarter pound fine flour
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Stir together the egg, egg yolks, finely sifted sugar and pounded vanilla for a strong quarter hour
2. Mix in one quarter pound fine flour
3. Place stiff paper on a black baking sheet and sprinkle it with a little sugar
4. Using a coffee spoon, place small heaps of the mixture onto it
5. Let them bake in a cooled oven
