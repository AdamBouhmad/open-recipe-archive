## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour
- 2 spoonfuls thick good beer yeast
- warm milk
- 1 knife point salt
- 1/2 pound butter
- 6 eggs
- bacon rind for brushing the waffle iron
- sugar and cinnamon for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 1/2 pound flour, 2 spoonfuls thick good beer yeast, warm milk, and 1 knife point salt
2. Let it rise in gentle warmth
3. In the meantime, lightly stir 1/2 pound butter in a bowl
4. Add 6 eggs and knead under the dough
5. Beat the dough strongly, let it rise once more
6. Bake it in hot waffle irons brushed with bacon rind on both sides
7. Sprinkle the waffles on the plate with sugar and cinnamon
