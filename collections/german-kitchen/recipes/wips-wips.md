## Ingredients

- One Maas wine
- One pound sugar
- 24 eggs

## Directions

1. Put one Maas wine, one pound sugar, and 24 eggs in a pan
2. Beat this on the fire with a small whisk until it begins to boil
3. Remove the drink from the fire
4. Continue beating for a quarter hour
5. The Wips is then ready
6. Champagne can be used instead of ordinary wine
