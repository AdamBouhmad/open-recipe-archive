## Ingredients

- whitefish
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lemon
- parsley
- onion
- flour
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Scale the fish and clean it out
2. Dry it thoroughly with a cloth
3. Cut it into pieces
4. Rub well with salt and pepper
5. Place it in a casserole with a piece of butter
6. Add chopped lemon, parsley, and onion
7. Cover the casserole and place it on low coals
8. Let the fish simmer for half an hour
9. Sprinkle 3 finger-widths of flour over it
10. Add a little meat broth
11. Let it come to a boil again
12. Place the fish on a platter and pour the sauce over it
