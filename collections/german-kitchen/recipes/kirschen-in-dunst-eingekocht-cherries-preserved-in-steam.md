## Ingredients

- Beautiful, ripe, not overripe cherries
- Pork or beef bladder

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from beautiful, ripe, not overripe cherries
2. Fill them into a pork or beef bladder that has been previously placed in lukewarm water, rubbed by hand, pulled through cold water, and pressed out firmly
3. Tie the bladder firmly at the top so no water can run in
4. Make a loop of twine for hanging
5. Push a stick through the loop and let the bladder cook very slowly in a pot or kettle filled with boiling water for a quarter hour
6. Leave the bladder in the kettle until the water has cooled
7. Let it drain and hang it in the air in a dry place
8. The cherries will keep well for 2 years and can be used for all baked goods and compotes
