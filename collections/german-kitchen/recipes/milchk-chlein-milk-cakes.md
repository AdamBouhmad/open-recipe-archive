## Ingredients

- thick curdled milk
- cream
- flour
- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Let the thick curdled milk with cream run together in a pan
2. Pour into a colander and let the water drain well
3. In a bowl, stir the curds with 8 loth of flour, 2 whole eggs, and 2 knife points of salt
4. Make cakes as large as a big walnut
5. Fry them in a pan with hot lard until golden yellow
6. They can be served with spinach or other vegetables
