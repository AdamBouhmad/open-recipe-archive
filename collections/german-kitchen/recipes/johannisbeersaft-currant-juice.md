## Ingredients

- ripe red currants
- sugar (equal measure to currant juice in quarter-pounds)

## Directions

1. Pluck ripe red currants and place them in a firmly covered clean pot on hot ashes
2. Let it stand overnight and the next day pour off the clear liquid
3. For as many quarter-pounds of juice as you have, let the same amount of quarter-pounds of sugar cook together for a quarter hour
4. Let it cool, fill it into bottles and seal them tightly
5. This juice is very good for the sick to drink in water
