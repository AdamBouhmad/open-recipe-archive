## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 beautiful peeled quinces
- 1 half pound quince pulp
- 1 half pound yellow mustard flour
- Cooked must
- 1 quint coarsely ground cloves
- Fennel
- Anise
- Coriander

## Directions

1. Cook 3 to 4 beautiful peeled quinces very soft in water
2. Scrape off the pulp
3. Take 1 half pound quince pulp and 1 half pound yellow mustard flour
4. Cook the mustard flour in cooked must
5. Let the quince pulp also cook along for 6 minutes
6. Stir in 1 quint coarsely ground cloves, fennel, anise, and coriander thoroughly
7. Put it in a stone pot and preserve it
