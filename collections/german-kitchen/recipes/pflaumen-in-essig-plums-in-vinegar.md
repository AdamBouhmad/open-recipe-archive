## Ingredients

- 3 kg (6 lbs) plums
- 1-1/4 l wine vinegar
- 1-1/4 kg (2-1/2 lbs) sugar
- 16 g cinnamon in a muslin bag
- 8 g cloves in a muslin bag

## Directions

1. Wipe the plums clean, prick them several times with a new wooden toothpick, and place them in a stone pot.
2. Cook the vinegar with sugar and spices, skim, and pour over the fruit after cooling, which you leave covered for 8-14 days.
3. Then pour them onto a colander, let the juice drain, and heat it.
4. Cook the plums in the juice for a few minutes until they get light cracks.
5. Then place them in glasses or pots, cook the juice until the second degree, and pour it over the fruit after cooling, place small cross-sticks over them to keep them under the juice, and bind the vessels with parchment paper.
