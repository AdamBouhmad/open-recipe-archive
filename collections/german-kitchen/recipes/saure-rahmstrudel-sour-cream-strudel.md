## Ingredients

- approximately 1/2 pound flour
- 2 egg whites
- butter the size of a walnut
- lukewarm milk
- thick sour cream
- 2 knife-tips of salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 schoppen of milk
- 1 small piece of butter

## Directions

1. Put approximately 1/2 pound flour on a noodle board, add 2 egg whites, butter the size of a walnut, and a little lukewarm milk
2. Make a dough like for noodles but not as stiff
3. Work it very thoroughly for a quarter hour and let it rest for half an hour if time allows
4. Roll out the sheets as fine as possible and stretch the strudel on a cloth as fine as postpaper
5. Let it dry well
6. Mix 2 knife-tips of salt into thick sour cream
7. Beat 4 eggs into stiff snow and fold it into the cream
8. Place the strudel on the noodle board, coat it with cream, and roll it up
9. Put 1 schoppen of milk and a small piece of butter in a frying pan and let them boil together
10. Place the strudel in it, put the dish in an oven and let it rise
