## Ingredients

- 10 g margarine
- 10 g flour
- 1/8 l vegetable or fish water
- 5 g sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
- salt to taste
- 1 egg yolk, beaten
- 1 tablespoon whole milk

## Directions

1. Make a white, smooth sauce from the margarine, flour, and liquid.
2. Beat in the sugar, lemon juice, salt, and a little nutmeg if desired, along with the beaten egg yolk, but do not let it boil.
3. The sauce can be mixed with mushrooms or capers as desired.
4. For sauce to chicken with rice or veal with rice, omit the lemon juice and sugar.
5. The sauce can be refined as desired by adding more eggs or butter beaten in at the end.
