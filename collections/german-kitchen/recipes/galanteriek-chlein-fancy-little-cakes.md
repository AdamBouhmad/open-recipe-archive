## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1 pound butter
- 1/2 pound sifted sugar
- 2 eggs
- coarsely ground cinnamon and sugar

## Directions

1. Rub flour and butter together by hand until no butter is visible
2. Add sifted sugar and eggs
3. Roll out the dough and fold it over itself
4. Roll out finger-thick
5. Cut out little cakes with a tin mold
6. Brush with egg white
7. Sprinkle with coarsely ground cinnamon and sugar
8. Bake until golden yellow
