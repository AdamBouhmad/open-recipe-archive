## Ingredients

- 100 g medium or strong pearl barley
- 5 g butter
- 3/4 l water
- 5 g salt
- 90 g dried prunes
- 25 g sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 1 tsp lemon sugar
- 1/4 l water
- 25 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare the pearl barley as indicated (see Pearl Barley Soup). Sauté the barley with butter while stirring and after adding 3/4 l water, allow it to swell completely and slowly with salt in an earthenware pot.
2. Prepare the prunes by adding them with sugar, cinnamon, lemon sugar, and the soaking water and allow them to swell slowly to full size.
3. Mix the prunes with the barley and arrange with sugar and cinnamon.
