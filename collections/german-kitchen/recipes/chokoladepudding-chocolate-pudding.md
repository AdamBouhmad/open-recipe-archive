## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound chocolate
- 2 rolls or milk breads
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound blanched, finely ground almonds
- 1/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- snow from 6 egg whites
- 3 loth chocolate
- milk as needed
- 1 knife-point ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate on a grater
2. Remove the crusts from the rolls and soak and squeeze out the bread
3. Lightly stir the butter
4. Mix in the blanched almonds, ground sugar, and egg yolks
5. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
6. Coat a metal capsule with butter and dust with sugar
7. Fill the mass into the capsule
8. Cook in steam for one hour
9. For the sauce: stir 3 loth chocolate with as much milk as needed in a brass pan
10. Add one knife-point ground cinnamon
11. Bring to a boil, let it foam and bubble, and pour over the pudding
