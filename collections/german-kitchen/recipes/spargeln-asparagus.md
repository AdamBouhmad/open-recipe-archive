## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Salt
- Flour
- Cold water
- 3 egg yolks
- Juice of half a lemon
- 3 tablespoons thick sour cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape the asparagus, cut off the white part, and cook soft in boiling salted water in a brass pan, then arrange on a plate.
2. Make the following sauce: stir 2 cooking spoons of flour smooth with cold water in a brass pan, beat in 3 egg yolks, the juice of half a lemon, three spoons of thick sour cream, salt, nutmeg, some meat broth, and bring to a boil on the coals while stirring constantly so it does not curdle.
