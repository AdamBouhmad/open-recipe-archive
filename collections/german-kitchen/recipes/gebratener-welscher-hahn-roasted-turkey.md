## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- cherry spirit (Kirschengeist)
- salt
- pepper
- turkey liver and gizzard
- onion
- parsley
- butter
- 1 fresh roll (Mundbrod)
- 1/2 schoppen thick sour cream
- 3 eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Before slaughter, pour a glass of cherry spirit into the bird so it runs well into the body.
2. After bleeding out, let it twitch on the ground.
3. Pluck warm but do not eviscerate.
4. Wrap in cloth and set aside until the next day.
5. Carefully remove the crop with innards, loosen the breastbone, and cut the legs from both sides at the joint, loosening carefully at the breast.
6. Wash clean and half-chop the feet.
7. Salt and pepper well.
8. Using a trussing needle, truss so the breast stands up nicely.
9. Prepare the stuffing for the crop: cut liver and gizzard (removing the tough skin) into small square pieces.
10. Braise these with onion and parsley in a piece of butter in a casserole for half an hour.
11. Meanwhile, cut a fresh roll (removing the crust).
12. Mix the braised mixture with the roll, add 1/2 schoppen thick sour cream, 3 beaten eggs, and nutmeg to taste.
13. Mix well and fill the crop with this stuffing.
