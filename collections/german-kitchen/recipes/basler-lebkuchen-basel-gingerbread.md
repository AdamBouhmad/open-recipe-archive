## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound flour
- 1/8 pound blanched, coarsely ground almonds
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 2 loth coarsely chopped citron
- 1/2 loth cinnamon
- 2 knife points cloves
- 2 knife points mace
- 2 egg whites beaten stiff
- 1/4 pound sugar for the glaze
- 2 spoonfuls water for the glaze
- Juice of 3 to 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, flour, almonds, lemon zest, citron, cinnamon, cloves, and mace together
2. Fold in beaten egg whites to form a dough
3. Roll out dough finger-thick on a noodle board
4. Cut out pieces with a tin mold
5. Bake on a flour-dusted sheet until not quite done
6. For the glaze, cook sugar and water together until it forms threads
7. Add lemon juice and bring to a boil again
8. Brush the glaze on the gingerbread and dry in the oven
