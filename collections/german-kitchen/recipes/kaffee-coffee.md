## Ingredients

- 15 g coffee beans
- 1 pinch of chicory
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. The coffee beans are ground, mixed with finely crumbled chicory (which can also be omitted) and poured into a porcelain pot.
2. Then 1/2 l boiling water is poured on. The coffee is allowed to steep covered for 20 minutes and is then poured through a coffee filter, which like the coffee pot is first rinsed with boiling water, into the prepared pot. If one wants to keep coffee hot or reheat it, it is placed covered in a porcelain vessel in hot water.
