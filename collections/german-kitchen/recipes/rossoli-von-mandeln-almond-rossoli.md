## Ingredients

- 4 Loth sweet almonds
- 4 Loth bitter almonds or 4 Loth peach kernels
- Half a Loth galangal
- One orange peel
- Red sandalwood (one Kreuzer worth)
- One Maas first runoff or strong fruit brandy
- One Schoppen cinnamon water

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from bitter almonds or peach kernels
2. Pound them finely with the sweet almonds
3. Cut half a Loth galangal into small pieces
4. Place all of this with one orange peel and red sandalwood in a glass flask
5. Pour one Maas first runoff or strong fruit brandy and one Schoppen cinnamon water into the flask
6. Bind the flask tightly
7. Leave it in the sun or warmth for 12 to 14 days
8. Strain the Rossoli through a felt hat
9. Store it
