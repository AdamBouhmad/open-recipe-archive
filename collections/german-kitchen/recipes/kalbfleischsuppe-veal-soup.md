## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal, finely cut
- Butter
- Celery
- Parsnip
- Parsley
- Flour
- Meat broth
- Egg yolks
- Nutmeg
- Chives

## Directions

1. In a casserole, steam the finely cut veal very gently with a piece of butter, celery, parsnip, and parsley
2. Sprinkle 3 tablespoons of flour on top, pour 2 tablespoons of meat broth over it
3. Cook together for another half hour
4. Press through a hair sieve and arrange on 2 egg yolks in the soup tureen
5. Add some nutmeg and chives on top
