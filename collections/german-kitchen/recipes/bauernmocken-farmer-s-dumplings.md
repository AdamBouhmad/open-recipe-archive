## Ingredients

- flour
- yeast
- milk
- curd from 1 measure of sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 tablespoons thick sour cream
- 1/8 pound sugar
- salt
- chives
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a pre-dough (Zusang) from flour, yeast, and milk
2. Add curd from 1 measure of sour milk to it
3. Beat in 2 eggs and 1 egg yolk
4. Add 3 tablespoons thick sour cream and 1/8 pound sugar
5. Mix well with salt and chives
6. Beat the dough for another quarter hour until it releases from the bowl
7. Let it stand until it is ripe
8. Use a spoon to remove long cakes and place them in hot lard
9. Fry them slowly
