## Ingredients

- 1 maas pure raspberries
- 2 loth isinglass
- water
- half a maas raspberry juice
- 1 schoppen white Burgundy wine
- half a pound sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. From 1 maas pure raspberries select 30 to 40 very large pieces and set aside
2. Put the others in a pot, tie it with cloth, place in a pan in boiling water and let cook for one hour
3. Take out and let the juice run through cloth, which should yield half a maas juice
4. Dissolve 2 loth isinglass in water, press through a fine sieve, put in a brass pan
5. Pour the raspberry juice to it along with 1 schoppen white Burgundy wine, half a pound sugar, and 2 egg whites
6. Beat with a whisk until it begins to boil, remove from fire and let stand a little
7. Run the jelly through a felt hat; if not clear enough the first time, it must be poured over 2 to 3 times
8. Take the designated mold and let a little run in, let it set
9. Lay some of the selected raspberries around in the mold as desired, pour some jelly to it and let it set again
10. Continue until the mold is full, place the jelly on ice and let it set there
