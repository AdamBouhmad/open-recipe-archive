## Ingredients

- 8 lemons
- quarter-pound almonds
- rose water
- 8 eggs
- 8 Loth finely ground sugar
- 2 grated Kreuzer breads
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate the zest of 8 lemons on a grater
2. Remove the white skin from the zest
3. Cut the pulp into pieces
4. Squeeze out the seeds
5. Finely grind a quarter-pound of almonds with rose water
6. Mix with the lemon pulp and the zest from the grated lemons
7. Add 8 eggs
8. Stir in 8 Loth of finely ground sugar and 2 grated Kreuzer breads
9. Mix everything together for half an hour
10. Butter a springform pan
11. Fill the mixture into it
12. Bake the cake beautifully golden in the oven
13. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
