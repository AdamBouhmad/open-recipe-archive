## Ingredients

- large oranges
- 2 schoppen white wine
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 egg
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 loth almonds
- 3 to 4 drops cochineal

## Directions

1. Make a shallow cross-cut at the top of each orange so the flesh can be removed with a finger, leaving nothing inside
2. Press the removed flesh through a hair sieve into a brass pan
3. Pour 2 schoppen white wine into the pan and bring to a boil with 1/4 pound sugar
4. Add 1 egg and beat it with a whisk until it begins to boil
5. Remove the jelly from the heat and add the juice of 2 lemons
6. Strain through a felt hat; if not clear enough the first time, boil again
7. Stand the oranges upright in a dish so they cannot fall over
8. Pour some of the clear jelly over them and let it set
9. Make a thick milk from 2 loth almonds and mix it with some of the clear jelly
10. When the oranges have set, pour the almond milk over them and let it set again
11. Color some clear jelly with 3 to 4 drops of cochineal
12. When the almond milk has set, pour the red jelly over it and let it set
