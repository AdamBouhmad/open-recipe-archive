## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- flour
- half a Maas milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 Loth sugar
- half a lemon
- 6 egg yolks
- a handful of raisins and citrons
- 2 Loth cut citron peel
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix 2 egg yolks with as much flour as they will absorb, ground as finely as possible
2. Cook this into a thick porridge in half a Maas of milk
3. Let it cool in a bowl
4. Stir 1/4 pound of butter until light
5. Take 4 Loth of sugar grated on half a lemon, 6 egg yolks, a handful of raisins and citrons, 2 Loth of cut citron peel, and the snow from the 6 egg whites, and add to the butter
6. Add the porridge to this mixture and blend everything well together
7. Butter a dish and fill the mass into it
8. Let the soufflé rise beautifully golden in the oven and sprinkle sugar on top
