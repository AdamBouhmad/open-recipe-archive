## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon cocoa
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 3/8 l water or milk

## Directions

1. 1 tablespoon cocoa and 1 teaspoon sugar are stirred with 1 tablespoon water and boiled with the whole amount of liquid (3/8 l water or milk per tablespoon cocoa) for 5 minutes.
