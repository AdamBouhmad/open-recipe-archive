## Ingredients

- 30 snails (garden snails are best)
- 3 crayfish, roe and gall removed
- 1/2 calf's lung
- 1 skinned calf's foot
- 1/2 pound chervil
- salt
- mace
- 2 maas water

## Directions

1. Pound the snails and crayfish very finely
2. Cut the calf's lung and foot into small pieces
3. Cook together with the snails and crayfish, finely weighed chervil, salt, mace, and 2 maas water
4. Reduce by cooking until 1 maas of liquid remains
5. Strain through a wool cloth
6. Give the patient 2 spoonfuls to drink in the morning when fasting, at midday, and in the evening
7. The jelly can be cooked with meat broth if serving it warm
