## Ingredients

- Goose liver
- Butter
- Lemon
- Nutmeg
- Shallots
- Anchovies
- Flour
- Beef broth
- Bouillon
- Wine
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. In a casserole, braise a goose liver with 3 lots of butter, half a lemon cut into slices, some whole nutmeg and 4 shallots.
2. Clean and remove the insides from 4 anchovies.
3. Pound them finely in a mortar with a little butter.
4. Knead 3 finger-widths of flour into the anchovy butter.
5. Add a little beef broth or preferably strong bouillon and 2 to 3 spoonfuls of wine.
6. Let everything cook together.
7. Add salt, pepper and juice of half a lemon.
8. Place the liver on the serving platter.
9. Strain the sauce through a fine sieve and pour over.
10. Sprinkle some chopped lemon zest on top.
