## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound beef marrow
- 5 eggs
- grated breadcrumb flour
- meat broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Let 1/4 pound beef marrow render, pour it through a hair sieve onto cold water.
2. Stir it with the water for a quarter hour, drain the water, let the fat render.
3. Stir it again with water until it is completely white.
4. Beat 5 eggs into it gradually, adding with each egg a handful of grated breadcrumb flour.
5. Let everything stand for half an hour.
6. Place small dumplings made from the dough into boiling meat broth.
7. Let them cook for half a quarter hour and add them to the soup.
