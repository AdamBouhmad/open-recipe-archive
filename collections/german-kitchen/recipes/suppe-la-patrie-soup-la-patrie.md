## Ingredients

- 1 half Schoppen cream
- 1 whole egg
- Yolk of 1 egg
- Flour
- Lard
- Semolina flour
- Spinach
- Fish sausages
- Spinach dumplings
- Beef forcemeat
- Italian macaroni
- Butter
- Fine flour (for sauce)
- Water
- Meat broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir together half a Schoppen cream, one whole egg and the yolk of one egg with enough flour to make a thick dough
2. Roll it out knife-back thick on a noodle board
3. Cut out various larger and smaller figures with small cookie cutters and fry them golden in hot lard
4. Make boiled and cooked dumplings from semolina flour, spinach fritters, fried rosettes, small fish sausages, green spinach dumplings, crayfish slices, fried egg slices, boiled beef forcemeat dumplings, fried Italian macaroni, egg cheese
5. Place all these properly boiled, cooked and fried items together in the soup bowl
6. For the sauce: melt 1 quarter pound butter in a casserole
7. Stir in 4 tablespoons of fine flour and let it brown slightly
8. Pour in half a ladle full of water and stir smooth
9. Add meat broth as needed
10. Let it come to a boil once more and pour into the soup bowl with salt and nutmeg
