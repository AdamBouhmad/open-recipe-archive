## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- butter
- 4 Loth butter
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 6 Loth ground sugar
- 1 lemon
- 5 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make very fine noodles from 3 eggs
2. Brown them in a piece of butter and let them drain in a hair sieve
3. Melt 4 Loth of butter in a bowl
4. Mix this with 3 tablespoons of sour cream, 6 Loth of ground sugar grated on a lemon, 5 egg yolks, and finally the snow from these
5. Combine everything with the noodles
6. Fill the mass into a buttered bowl
7. Bake the soufflé beautifully golden and sprinkle sugar on top
