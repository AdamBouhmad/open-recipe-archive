## Ingredients

- 150 g cooked fish or fish scraps (deboned)
- 15 g chopped onions
- 15 g margarine
- 15 g flour
- 1/8 liter skim milk
- 1 pinch salt and pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 5 g flour
- 1 egg white
- 20 g grated bread crumbs
- frying fat for temporary use
- 1/2 sheet blotting paper

## Directions

1. Sauté the onions in margarine until soft
2. Add the flour and stir; when done, stir in milk, salt and pepper to make a thick sauce
3. Remove from heat with the beaten egg yolk
4. Add the fish in small pieces and optionally some finely cut mushrooms; everything must become hot but not boil
5. Cool the mixture and form small cutlets on a floured board with a floured wooden palette when it is stiff
6. Coat with beaten egg white and bread crumbs
7. Fry in steaming fat to a beautiful even color
8. Degrease on blotting paper and arrange neatly
