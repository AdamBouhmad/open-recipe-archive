## Ingredients

- medium-sized potatoes
- half a quarter pound of cut bacon
- onions
- parsley
- one soaked and squeezed bread roll, grated
- half pound finely chopped leftover meat
- two to three eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- lemon
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, equal-sized potatoes of medium size and peel them
2. Cut off a small lid from the top and hollow them out
3. In a small pot, melt the bacon and braise finely chopped onions and parsley in it
4. Add one grated, soaked, and firmly squeezed bread roll
5. Add half pound of finely chopped leftover meat, two to three eggs, salt, nutmeg, pepper, lemon, and lemon juice
6. Fill the potatoes with this filling
7. Cover with the small lid and cook them soft in a roasting pan with a piece of butter
8. It is even better if one places a lid with coals on the roasting pan
