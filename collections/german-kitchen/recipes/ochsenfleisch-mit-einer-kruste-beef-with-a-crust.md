## Ingredients

- Breast or rib piece of beef
- One-quarter pound of rice
- Fatty meat broth
- One-quarter pound Parmesan cheese
- One-quarter pound crayfish butter
- Three cleaned and boned anchovies
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Remove the flap from the bottom and boil the meat until very tender
2. Prepare crayfish butter as described elsewhere in the cookbook
3. Heat one-quarter pound of rice in a casserole with a little fatty meat broth and let it swell
4. Add a little more meat broth and when soft, pound it in a mortar to a paste
5. Transfer to a bowl and mix in one-quarter pound Parmesan cheese and one-quarter pound crayfish butter
6. Pound three cleaned and boned anchovies in a mortar and add them to the mixture
7. Stir everything together for seven and a half minutes
8. Rub the beef on a board with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
9. Spread the rice mixture the thickness of a knife-back onto the beef, on all sides and on top
10. Place the meat on wooden pieces in a roasting pan
11. Roast until beautifully browned in the oven and pour a tablespoon of meat broth over it
