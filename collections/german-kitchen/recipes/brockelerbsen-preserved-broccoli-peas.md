## Ingredients

- half-ripe broccoli peas
- salt
- pig's bladder or bottle

## Directions

1. Shell the half-ripe broccoli peas and remove the wormy ones
2. To one measure of kernels add 2 handfuls of salt and let stand overnight
3. Fill a pig's bladder or bottle with the broccoli peas and salt, tie the bladder tightly closed and make a loop of twine so they can be hung
