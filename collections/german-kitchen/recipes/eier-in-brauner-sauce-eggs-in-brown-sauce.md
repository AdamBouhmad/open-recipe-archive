## Ingredients

- eggs
- lard
- handful of grated black bread
- 2 loth brown gingerbread
- glass of wine
- half glass vinegar
- meat broth or water
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry as many eggs as needed in hot lard, but keep the yolk soft
2. For the sauce: take a handful of grated black bread and 2 loth brown gingerbread together in a casserole
3. Pour in a glass of wine, half a glass of vinegar, a ladle-full of meat broth or water on fasting days
4. Add the juice of half a lemon, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
5. Let it boil together for a quarter hour
6. Strain through a hair sieve
7. Place the eggs on a plate, pour the sauce over them, and let everything become hot together again
