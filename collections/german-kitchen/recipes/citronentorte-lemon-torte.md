## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- zest of 2 lemons, minced
- pulp of 6 lemons, seeds removed
- 1/4 pound citron peel
- 1/4 pound orange peel
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1 cup water
- butter dough
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Finely cut the blanched almonds
2. Mix together the lemon zest, lemon pulp, citron peel, and orange peel
3. Cook 1/2 pound sugar with 1 cup water in a brass pan
4. Skim the sugar carefully
5. Cook the almond and fruit mixture in the sugar together
6. Let the mass cool
7. Line a springform with good butter dough
8. Fill the mass into it
9. Make a thin lid of butter dough over it
10. Brush the lid with egg yolk
11. Bake the torte in a not-too-hot oven
