## Ingredients

- large bitter oranges
- pulp from the oranges
- 2 schoppen of white wine
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 egg
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Make a shallow crosscut at the top of each orange so that the pulp can be extracted with a finger; remove all the pulp.
2. Press the extracted pulp through a fine sieve into a brass pan. Add 2 schoppen of white wine and bring to a boil with 1/4 pound of sugar.
3. Add 1 egg and whisk with a whisk fork until it begins to boil.
4. Remove the aspic from the heat, add the juice of 2 lemons, and strain through a felt hat.
