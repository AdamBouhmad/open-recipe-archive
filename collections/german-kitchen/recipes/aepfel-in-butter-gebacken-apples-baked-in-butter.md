## Ingredients

- Borsdorf apples
- Small and large raisins
- Wine
- Fine butter dough
- Egg

## Directions

1. Peel Borsdorf apples, cut off a small lid at the top and remove the core
2. Cook small and large raisins for half a quarter hour with wine
3. Weigh them and fill the apples with them
4. Roll out fine butter dough to knife back thickness
5. Cut 2 finger-width strips
6. Lay them crosswise and place an apple on top
7. Lift the 2 strips up around the apple
8. Lay 2 other strips on top and bend them downward and glue them to the lower ones with egg
9. Brush all strips with egg
10. Set the apples on a dark baking sheet, bake them and sprinkle with sugar
