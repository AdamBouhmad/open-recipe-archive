## Ingredients

- 1/2 egg white
- 1/2 teaspoon water
- 1 scant tablespoon flour
- 2 tablespoons grated breadcrumbs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 20 g lard or margarine
- bacon

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into 1/2 cm thick slices so that the strips run lengthwise, and soak in 3/8 l water.
2. Beat egg white with 1/2 teaspoon water using a fork on a saucer.
3. Mix grated breadcrumbs with salt and flour; place the fat in a pan and set on the stove.
4. Dry the prepared bacon, roll it in the beaten egg white and then coat evenly in the breadcrumb mixture, fry in steaming fat from the pan, turning frequently, to a golden-brown color and arrange on a plate.
