## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 5 eggs
- 1 whole lemon, zested on a grater
- 1 pound flour
- fine flour
- white silk gauze
- bacon fat
- anise seeds

## Directions

1. Whisk together 1 pound finely sifted sugar and 5 eggs in a bowl for half an hour
2. Add the zest of 1 whole lemon grated on a box grater and mix with 1 pound flour
3. Wrap fine flour in a piece of white silk gauze and use to dust the molds well
4. Press dough into the molds and cut away excess with a knife
5. Transfer to a pastry board and continue until all dough is used
6. Let stand overnight
7. On the second day, brush a black baking sheet with bacon fat and sprinkle anise seeds on it
8. Place the springerlein on the sheet and bake
