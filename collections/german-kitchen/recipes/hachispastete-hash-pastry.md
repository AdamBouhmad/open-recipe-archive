## Ingredients

- 1 pound roasted meat
- 2 spoonfuls capers
- 1 cleaned herring
- zest of 1 lemon
- marrow of 2 lemons
- Mundbrod (bread roll)
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- short pastry
- cold water
- butter pastry strip
- eggs

## Directions

1. Weigh 1 pound roasted meat, 2 spoonfuls capers, 1 cleaned herring, the zest of 1 lemon and the marrow of 2 lemons not very finely
2. Cut the crust from a Mundbrod, soak it in water, press it out firmly, add it to the minced meat
3. Add 2 eggs, salt, pepper and mace and mix everything together
4. Make a short pastry, roll it out knife-back thick to a round cake
5. Place the mass on it, spread it smooth, leaving 2 fingers width empty
6. Make another round cake, pull it over, cut it smooth with a warm knife
7. Brush the pastry with cold water, fasten it with a butter pastry strip, brush it with eggs and bake it in the oven
8. Serve it warm at table
