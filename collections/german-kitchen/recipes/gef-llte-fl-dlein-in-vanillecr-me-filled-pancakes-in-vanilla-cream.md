## Ingredients

- 1 handful of fine flour
- cold milk
- 4 eggs
- bacon for coating the pan
- 1 pint milk
- 1 piece vanilla
- 2 loth sugar
- 3 fingers full of flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls sweet cream
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 1 handful of fine flour, stir it smooth with cold milk, and beat 4 eggs into it
2. Coat a medium Flädleinspfanne (thin pancake pan) with bacon and bake the Flädlein in it
3. Lay them out separately on a noodle board
4. For the Vanillecrême: cook together 1 pint milk, 1 piece vanilla, 2 loth sugar, and 3 fingers full of flour
5. Beat 4 egg yolks and stir them into the boiling milk
6. Let everything draw once more on the fire
7. Pour the cream through a hair sieve into a dish and let it cool
8. Spread 2 spoonfuls of it around on the Flädlein
9. Roll them together
10. Lay them in a dish coated with butter crosswise on top of each other
11. Pour 2 spoonfuls of sweet cream over them
12. Draw them up in the oven and sprinkle sugar on top
