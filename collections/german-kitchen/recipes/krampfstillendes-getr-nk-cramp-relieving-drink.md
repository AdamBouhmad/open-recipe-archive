## Ingredients

- a handful of carefully picked and washed barley
- Scorzonera
- 2 Loth grated hartshorn
- 1 Loth chicory root
- 3 Maas water
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook a handful of carefully picked and washed barley, Scorzonera, 2 Loth grated hartshorn and 1 Loth chicory root with 3 Maas water until 2 Maas remain
2. Sugar may be cooked in if desired
3. Give it cold to drink as thirst dictates
