## Ingredients

- 200 g mutton
- 3/4 l water
- 8 g salt
- 3/4 kg rutabagas
- 1-1/2 l water for blanching
- 250 g peeled potatoes
- water and salt
- 1 spice packet
- 10 g fat or margarine
- 10 g flour
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Place 3/4 l water with 8 g salt on the stove. Wash and pound the meat quickly. Brush rutabagas clean in water, peel them, and cut into uniform strips. Cut potatoes in half or quarters depending on size.
2. Blanch the rutabagas in boiling water uncovered and drain on a sieve. Treat the potatoes the same way but in salted water. When the salted water comes to a boil with the meat, add the meat and cook slowly until foam forms and cook for several more minutes. Remove the foam, add potatoes, rutabagas, and spice packet to the meat and cook together for another 1-1/2 hours. Sweat the fat and flour together, cook with some broth, add to the dish and shake thoroughly to combine. Before serving, taste and adjust seasoning with pepper and salt.
