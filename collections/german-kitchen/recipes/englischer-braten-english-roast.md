## Ingredients

- Piece of loin roast with fat from a fat ox slaughtered 8 days prior
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pure clear butter

## Directions

1. Season a piece of loin roast with fat with salt and pepper
2. Put it on a spit and wrap it double with 4 sheets of paper
3. Roast it slowly over weak fire for 4 hours, basting constantly with pure clear butter
4. Half an hour before serving, remove the paper so the jus runs into the roasting pan
5. Leave the roast on the fire and baste several more times with melted pure butter
6. Place it on the platter, strain the jus through a sieve, remove the fat, and pour the jus over the roast
