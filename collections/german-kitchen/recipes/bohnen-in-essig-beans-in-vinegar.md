## Ingredients

- young beans without kernels
- salt
- pepper
- grape leaves
- strong wine vinegar
- lard
- Provence oil (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the young beans small
2. Cook water in a kettle, add the beans and let boil twice, quickly remove them and place in cold water
3. Let lie for a quarter hour, pour into a colander and let drain well, then dry on a cloth
4. Line the bottom of the intended dish with grape leaves and coarsely pounded pepper mixed with salt
5. Make a layer of beans, press firmly by hand, sprinkle some salt and pepper on top and another layer of beans, continue until finished
6. Pour a bottle of strong wine vinegar over them
7. Melt lard, pour over the beans (must be half a finger's height above them; Provence oil can be used instead of lard)
8. The beans can be used as a salad or served with beef
9. Bind the dish tightly closed and place in a cool location
