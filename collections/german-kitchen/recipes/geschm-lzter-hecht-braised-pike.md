## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- pepper
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- herbs
- cloves
- parsley
- butter
- lemon
- small potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut off the pike's ears and make a small opening
2. Remove the innards and wash thoroughly
3. Rub strongly with salt and pepper
4. Prepare a broth from 1 part wine, 1 part vinegar, and 1 part water
5. Add all kinds of herbs, whole cloves, and pepper
6. If the pike is large, simmer it in the broth for 1 hour (the broth should not completely cover the fish or it will not be flavorful)
7. Place the pike on a long platter
8. Braise a handful of parsley in a piece of butter
9. Pour this over the pike
10. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon over it
11. Garnish the platter with small roasted potatoes or serve potatoes in their skins with butter
