## Ingredients

- cold roast
- 4 loth butter
- 2 cooking spoonfuls fine flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon juice and zest
- half a pint sweet cream
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the cold roast into desired pieces
2. Melt 4 loth butter in a casserole and stir in 2 cooking spoonfuls of fine flour
3. Add salt, nutmeg, juice and grated zest of half a lemon, and half a pint of sweet cream
4. Stir smooth with some meat broth and let it come to a boil together
5. Place the meat in it and let everything cook for another quarter hour
