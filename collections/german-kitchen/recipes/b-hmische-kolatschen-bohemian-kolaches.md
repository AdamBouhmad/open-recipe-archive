## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 3 spoons good white beer yeast
- 1 schoppen warm milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 2 loth ground sugar
- 12 loth butter
- apricot jam
- egg white snow
- sugar and vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a starter dough from flour, yeast, and warm milk in a bowl
2. Let it rise well in gentle warmth
3. Work in egg yolks and ground sugar by hand
4. Roll out on a pastry board
5. Distribute 12 loth butter on the dough and fold it over
6. Roll out again; repeat this process until no butter is visible
7. Return dough to bowl and let it rise
8. Form into round heaps and place on a baking sheet
9. Let them rise slowly
10. Press a depression with an egg
11. Fill with apricot jam
12. Top with a little egg white snow
13. Sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
14. Bake for half an hour, not too hot
