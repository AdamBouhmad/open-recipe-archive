## Ingredients

- 140 g bratwurst
- 15 g fat or margarine
- 3 g salt
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Wash the bratwurst and divide it into two equal pieces.
2. If the bratwurst is fatty enough, it does not need to be fried in fat, instead place it in the pan, pour a spoonful of water over it and fry the sausage in its own fat under frequent turning to a nice color, place the finished sausage on a plate and pour the available fat over it.
