## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls sugar
- water
- ground sugar
- egg white
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough from one pound butter, one pound flour, 4 egg yolks and 2 spoonfuls sugar (if it is too stiff, a little water can be added)
2. Work this together on a pastry board, fold it over, repeat this 4 times
3. The fifth time, roll it out leaf thin
4. Sprinkle ground sugar on it and fold it over 4 times
5. Cut pieces one finger long and two fingers wide from it
6. Place them on a black baking sheet
7. Brush with egg white
8. Sprinkle sugar on top and bake them in the oven until golden yellow
