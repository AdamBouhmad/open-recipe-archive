## Ingredients

- 6 spoonfuls sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 2 lemons
- 6 egg whites
- 2 loth flour
- butter for the dish
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Stir 6 spoonfuls sugar, 6 egg yolks, salt, 2 lemons grated on a grater, and the juice of these lemons in a bowl until the mass is thick.
2. Beat a stiff snow from the 6 egg whites and mix 2 loth flour into it.
3. Brush a bowl with butter and fill the mass into it.
4. Bake the soufflé in a not very hot oven until nicely golden and sprinkle sugar on top.
