## Ingredients

- Brain
- Water
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Onion
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Soak and clean the brain.
2. Let it set firm in equal parts water and vinegar.
3. For the sauce: place a piece of butter in a casserole.
4. Chop an onion with the zest of half a lemon and add to the butter.
