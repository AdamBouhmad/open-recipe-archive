## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- egg whites
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound fine ground sugar
- 2 handfuls fine grated flour
- egg whites
- oblaten
- raisins and currants

## Directions

1. Grind 1/2 pound blanched almonds very finely in a mortar with egg white
2. Melt 1/4 pound butter in a bowl
3. Heat the mixture with 1/2 pound fine ground sugar for half an hour
4. Add 2 handfuls fine grated flour
5. Slowly mix in the snow from some egg whites
6. Line a baking sheet that has been coated with butter with oblaten
7. Spread the mass into it
8. Bake the cake in the oven
9. If desired, scatter raisins and currants on top
