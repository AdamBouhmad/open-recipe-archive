## Ingredients

- 4 strong cooking spoonfuls flour
- one schoppen milk
- 6 egg whites
- quarter pound butter
- sugar to taste
- lemon zest (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir 4 strong cooking spoonfuls flour with one schoppen milk cold
2. Then stir thick on the fire
3. Add 6 egg whites, quarter pound butter, sugar to taste (one can also add some lemon zest)
4. Fill the mass into any desired form
5. Bake it
