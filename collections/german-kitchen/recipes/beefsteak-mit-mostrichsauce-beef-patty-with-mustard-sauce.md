## Ingredients

- 100 g ground beef
- 25 g beef fat cracklings
- 1 large cooked potato
- 20 g margarine or mixed fat
- 15 g onion dice
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/4 l water
- pepper and salt for the sauce

## Directions

1. Grate the potato and weigh the fat cracklings.
2. Mix the beef, cracklings, potato, pepper and salt and form 2 beef patties of 1-1/2 cm thickness using two spoons on a plate. Press diagonal squares into them with a knife, place the patties in hot smoking fat in a skillet and fry them for 6 minutes, turning frequently on both sides.
3. When the patties are brown, brown the onion dice in the fat and place them on the patties, then sweat the flour in the fat until yellow, add 1/4 l water mixed with mustard and vinegar, let the sauce come to a boil, season with salt and pepper and serve separately as mustard sauce.
