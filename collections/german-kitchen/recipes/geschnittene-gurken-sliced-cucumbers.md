## Ingredients

- Gurken (Cucumbers without seeds)
- Salz (Salt)
- Schalottenzwiebeln (Shallot onions)
- Pfeffer (Peppercorns)
- Gelber Senf (Yellow mustard)
- Weinessig (Wine vinegar)
- Schmalz (Lard)

## Directions

1. Select cucumbers that have no seeds yet and peel them clean
2. Slice them as for salad and salt them well in a bowl
3. After one hour, press them out firmly
4. Slice shallot onions finely
5. Combine with coarsely crushed peppercorns and one tablespoon of yellow mustard
6. Mix this with the cucumbers
7. Press them firmly into a stone pot
8. Pour enough good wine vinegar over them so that it covers them
9. Seal with melted lard and store
