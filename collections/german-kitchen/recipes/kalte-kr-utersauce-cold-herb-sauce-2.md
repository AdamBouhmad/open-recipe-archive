## Ingredients

- One handful of chives
- One handful of chervil
- One handful of parsley
- Two shallot onions
- Two hard-boiled egg yolks
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut one handful of chives very finely
2. Mince one handful of chervil, one handful of parsley, and two shallot onions together finely
3. Mix two hard-boiled egg yolks with vinegar and oil until smooth
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add the minced herbs
6. Arrange in a sauce bowl and serve with beef
