## Ingredients

- one large finely chopped onion
- fresh butter
- half a maas of pork blood
- half a maas of sweet cream
- half a pound of fresh, half-cooked bacon cut into small cubes
- finely chopped basil
- finely chopped thyme
- finely chopped marjoram

## Directions

1. Braise the finely chopped onion in fresh butter in a casserole
2. Pour half a maas of pork blood and half a maas of sweet cream over it
3. Add half a pound of fresh, half-cooked, finely diced bacon and finely chopped basil, thyme, and marjoram
4. Set over the fire and stir until warm
5. Fill the mixture into casings and place the sausages in boiling water
6. Let them simmer twice and then cool
7. Before serving, warm the sausages in hot water or weak broth, then brown them on both sides in a casserole with butter or good fat over strong heat
