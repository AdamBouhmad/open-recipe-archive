## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- pork
- bread
- pepper
- salt
- onions
- lemons
- 1 egg
- flour
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut out a large shoulder nicely, loosen the blade along with the first and second bones, and cut off the bones except for the outer knob
2. Make a filling of pork, some bread, pepper, salt, onions, and lemons; mix this together with an egg
3. Fill the shoulder with this mixture, roll it together, sew it shut
4. Turn the collar with a wooden peg onto the breast
5. Make a drawn sauce in a casserole with it, adding onions and yellow turnips
6. Let the sediment in which the shoulder cooked reduce completely, lift out the meat, sprinkle as much flour as can be held with 3 fingers, pour 1 ladleful of meat broth over it, add the juice of half a lemon, let it cook together for half a quarter hour
7. Pour it through a fine sieve, place the meat back in, and serve with lemons
