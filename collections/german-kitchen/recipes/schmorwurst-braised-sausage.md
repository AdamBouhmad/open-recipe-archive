## Ingredients

- 300 g Bratwurst (bratwurst sausage)
- 10 g Mehl (flour)
- 15 g Fett oder Margarine (fat or margarine)
- 25 g Brotrinde (bread crust)
- 1 Gewürzdosis (1 spice dose)
- 15 g Zwiebeln (onions)
- 1 Prise Salz (pinch of salt)
- 1/4 l Weissbier oder Wasser (white beer or water)
- 5 g Kartoffelmehl (potato starch)
- 2 Tropfen Speisefarbe (2 drops food coloring)

## Directions

1. Brown the bratwurst in the fat, dust with flour, then braise in a braising pot in white beer or water with the remaining ingredients (except potato starch and food coloring) for 3/4 hour over low heat.
2. Place the sausage on a serving dish.
3. Degrease the sauce and pass through a sieve; cook 3 tablespoons of it with potato starch mixed in water, and pour over the arranged sausage.
4. Color the remaining sauce with food coloring and serve on the side.
