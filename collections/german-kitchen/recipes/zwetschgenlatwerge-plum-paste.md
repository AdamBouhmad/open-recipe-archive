## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe plums
- sugar
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over beautiful ripe plums and peel off the skin
2. Let them steam in a covered casserole for a quarter hour
3. Force them through a hair sieve (for every pound of pulp, add the same amount of sugar)
4. Cook the sugar in a brass pan until it spins threads
5. Cook the pulp with it until everything is thick, then let it cool
6. Store it in a wooden box
