## Ingredients

- semi-short dough
- bacon
- 3 goose livers
- 1 pound pork forcemeat
- quarter pound bacon
- 1 rusk
- quarter pound cleaned truffles
- 2 goose livers
- 2 eggs
- 4 loth lean ham
- a handful of chopped onions and parsley
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace
- pepper
- 3 chopped lemon peels
- marrow from lemons
- butter dough

## Directions

1. Make a semi-short dough and brush a melon mold with bacon.
2. Line the mold with the dough.
3. Weigh 3 goose livers finely.
4. Rub 1 pound pork forcemeat and quarter pound bacon on a grater.
5. Peel 1 rusk, soak it, and press it out.
6. Pound quarter pound cleaned truffles finely.
7. Cut 2 goose livers into cubes.
8. Beat 2 eggs together.
9. Weigh 4 loth lean ham.
10. Braise a handful of chopped onions and parsley.
11. Mix all of this together with salt, nutmeg, mace, pepper, 3 chopped lemon peels, and the marrow from the lemons in a bowl.
12. Fill the mass into the melon mold.
13. Make a smooth butter dough lid on top.
14. Cut some holes in it, brush the lid with eggs.
15. Bake the pie for 2 hours in the oven.
16. Let it cool and turn it out onto a plate.
