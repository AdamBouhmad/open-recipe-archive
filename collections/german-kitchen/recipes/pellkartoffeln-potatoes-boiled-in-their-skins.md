## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 lb) potatoes
- 1/4 l water
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes (preferably of equal size) thoroughly with cold water and place in a pot (approximately 1-1/2 l capacity) with cold water and salt.
2. Cook covered until done, drain, shake in the pot, and place for 1/2 minute on the hot plate with the lid half-open so the potatoes dry and burst.
3. New potatoes require about 20 minutes to cook, old potatoes 1/2 hour or more. Test by piercing with a fork to ensure they are soft throughout.
