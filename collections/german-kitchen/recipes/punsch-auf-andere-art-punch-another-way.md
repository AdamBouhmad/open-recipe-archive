## Ingredients

- One Maas water
- 8 lemons
- 2 oranges
- Sugar
- One Schoppen Arak

## Directions

1. Rub 4 lemons on sugar
2. Scrape the sugar into the water
3. Boil the remaining sugar in a brass pan with one spoonful of water until it turns yellowish
4. Pour the juice from the lemons and oranges through a hair sieve onto the sugar
5. Add one Maas of water
6. Let it boil together
7. Pour into the bowl and add one Schoppen Arak
