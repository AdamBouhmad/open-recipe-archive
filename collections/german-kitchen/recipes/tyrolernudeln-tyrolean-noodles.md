## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pounds flour
- 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 schoppen of sour cream
- 3 loth of sugar
- 1/4 pound of small raisins
- hot lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough from 1-1/2 pounds flour, 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast, 3 egg yolks, 1/2 schoppen of sour cream, 3 loth of sugar, and 1/4 pound of small raisins
2. Work it for a quarter hour and let it rise in warmth
3. Make small cakes with a spoon, place them on a floured cloth
4. Form them into finger-long batons and bake them in hot lard
5. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over them
