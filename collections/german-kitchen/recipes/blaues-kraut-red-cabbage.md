## Ingredients

- cabbage
- onion
- vinegar
- flour
- meat broth
- sugar
- wine
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage finely.
2. In a casserole, steam a finely cut onion.
3. Add the cabbage, pour a small glass of vinegar over it.
4. Steam for one hour.
5. When quite tender, sprinkle a little flour over it, pour meat broth over it, and add sugar to taste.
6. Add half a glass of wine, pepper, and salt to taste.
