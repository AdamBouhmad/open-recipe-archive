## Ingredients

- butter dough
- preserved currants
- apple jam
- preserved raspberries
- apricot jam
- wafer
- egg
- 4 Loth sugar
- 2 small tablets chocolate
- 1 egg white
- quarter-pound finely ground sugar
- juice of half a lemon
- fruits for garnish

## Directions

1. Bake 5 to 6 plate-sized cakes from good butter dough
2. Place one on the plate to be served at table
3. Coat it not very thickly with preserved currants
4. Place the second cake on top and coat with apple jam
5. Coat the third with preserved raspberries
6. Coat the fourth with apricot jam
7. Place the fifth on top
8. Coat a piece of wafer with egg and arrange it beautifully around the cake
9. Prepare chocolate icing by cooking 4 Loth sugar in a brass pan with 2 small tablets of chocolate
10. Coat the cake with this chocolate icing in a ring around the top
11. Prepare lemon icing by whisking together 1 egg white, quarter-pound finely ground sugar, and the juice of half a lemon until white
12. Pour this lemon icing into the inner edge after the chocolate icing using a piping device
13. Dry the cake in the oven and garnish with fruits
