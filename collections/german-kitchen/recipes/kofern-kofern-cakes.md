## Ingredients

- 6 loth butter
- 4 loth sugar
- 1/2 lemon grated on a grater
- 1 whole egg
- 8 loth fine flour
- 1 spoon sweet cream
- 1/2 loth bitter orange peel
- cinnamon and cloves to taste

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients together until very smooth
2. Coat a kofern iron with lard and heat it
3. Pour a spoonful of batter into the iron
4. Close the iron tightly
5. Bake and press onto a round piece of wood while still hot
