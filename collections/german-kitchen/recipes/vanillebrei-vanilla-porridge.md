## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons grated roll flour
- 2 tablespoons fine flour
- cold milk
- 1 schoppen cream
- a piece of vanilla
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stir 2 tablespoons grated roll flour and 2 tablespoons fine flour smooth with cold milk
2. Let 1 schoppen cream and a piece of vanilla cook together for a quarter hour, pour the stirred mixture to it, let it cook well together
3. Arrange it on a plate and sprinkle sugar on top
