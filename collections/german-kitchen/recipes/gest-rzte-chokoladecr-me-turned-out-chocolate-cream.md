## Ingredients

- calf's feet (4, cleaned, bones removed)
- water (2 maas)
- chocolate (12 loth)
- water (half schoppen)
- sugar (12 loth)
- vanilla (1 piece)
- sweet cream (1 schoppen)
- eggs (8)
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Set 4 cleaned calf's feet with bones removed with 2 maas water on the fire
2. Reduce to half a maas
3. Pour the aspic through a woolen cloth onto a porcelain bowl
4. Let it sit for a quarter hour
5. Dissolve 12 loth chocolate in a brass pan with half a schoppen water (if still too thick, another half glass of water can be added)
6. Add 12 loth sugar and one piece of vanilla
7. Carefully add the aspic
8. Stir smooth with one schoppen sweet cream
9. Stir the cooked chocolate into 8 beaten eggs
10. Let it draw once more in the pan, stirring constantly
11. Strain the cream through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl
12. Let it cool
13. Brush an aspic mold with almond oil, turn it over and let it run out well
14. Pour the cold cream into it
15. Before serving at table, arrange it beautifully on a plate
