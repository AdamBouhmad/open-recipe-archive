## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ripe cherries
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- half a Schoppen water
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pit 2 pounds of ripe cherries
2. Let them sweat in a ceramic pot for a quarter hour
3. Force them through a hair sieve
4. Take 1.5 pounds of sugar in a casserole and pour half a Schoppen of water over it
5. Cook it until it reaches the largest thread stage
6. Pour the cherry pulp into it
7. Cook it with 3 knife-tips full of cinnamon until thick (it must be cooked very carefully as it burns easily)
8. Store the paste in a porcelain, bound pot
