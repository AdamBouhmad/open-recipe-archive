## Ingredients

- 3 loths of butter
- 1 schoppen of sour cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 nutmeg
- 1 knife point of salt
- 6 egg whites
- 6 loths of flour

## Directions

1. Stir together the butter and sour cream lightly
2. Stir in the egg yolks
3. Add the nutmeg and salt
4. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
5. Mix the flour into the mass
6. Brush small pastry molds with butter
7. Fill with the mixture and bake until golden
8. Serve with venison or braised veal
