## Ingredients

- A piece of beef
- 6 shallot onions
- Yellow zest and marrow of 1 lemon
- Basil
- Thyme
- Tarragon
- 2 tablespoons of capers
- Butter
- 3 fingers' breadth of flour
- Half a tablespoon of meat broth
- Melted consommé

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of beef tender
2. Mince finely 6 shallot onions, the yellow zest and marrow of 1 lemon, basil, thyme, tarragon, and 2 tablespoons of capers
3. Braise the minced mixture with a piece of butter and 3 fingers' breadth of flour
4. Place the meat on the herbs
5. Let everything braise together for half an hour
6. Pour half a tablespoon of meat broth over it
7. Arrange the herbs on a plate
8. Place the meat on top
9. Coat it with melted consommé
