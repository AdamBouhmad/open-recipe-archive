## Ingredients

- 1 l water
- 1 pinch salt
- 30 g sugar
- 10 g butter
- 1/4 lemon peel
- 1 small piece cinnamon
- 40 g semolina, 30 g pearl barley, rice, flour or cornstarch
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Clean the pearl barley, rice and semolina by rinsing in cold water. Mix flour or cornstarch with 6 tablespoons cold water.
2. Bring 1 l water to a boil with salt, sugar, butter and spices, add the ingredients and cook until done under frequent stirring.
3. Pass the stirred egg yolk through a sieve into the soup; it can also be omitted.
