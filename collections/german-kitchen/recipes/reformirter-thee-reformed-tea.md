## Ingredients

- Milk
- Cinnamon
- Lemon peel
- Green tea
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pour milk into a pan as much as needed
2. Add a piece of cinnamon and some lemon peel
3. Take as much green tea as can be grasped with three fingers
4. Let it boil together for 6 minutes
5. Beat 6 egg yolks in a pot
6. Stir them smooth with cold milk
7. Slowly pour the hot milk through a hair sieve into the egg yolks
8. Put it back in the pan and let it set
