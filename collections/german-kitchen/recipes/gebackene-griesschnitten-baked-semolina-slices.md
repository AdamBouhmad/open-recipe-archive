## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 4 to 5 handfuls semolina flour
- 6 eggs
- Chives
- Nutmeg
- Salt
- Butter for the form

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 maas milk with 4 to 5 handfuls semolina flour to a thick porridge
2. Gradually stir in 6 eggs in a bowl
3. Add considerable chives, nutmeg and salt
4. Bake the dough in a butter-brushed form
5. Cut into small slices and serve in soup - you can serve them in meat soups or in soups à la Reine
