## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen of sour cream
- flour
- eggs
- 1 maas of milk
- butter
- 2 eggs
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Let 1 schoppen of sour cream simmer in a brass pan
2. Stir in flour until it becomes a thick porridge and remove from heat
3. Beat in eggs until it becomes a firm sparrow dough
4. Set 1 maas of milk on the fire
5. Scrape noodles from the dough (laid on a sparrow board) into the boiling milk
6. Cover and let them cook well
7. Remove with a skimming spoon and let them drain well on a board
8. Coat a dish to be served with butter and lay the noodles crosswise over the top
9. Stir 2 eggs together with a little milk and sour cream, pour over the noodles and bake them beautifully golden in the oven
