## Ingredients

- 100 g lean bacon
- 3/8 l water
- 1/2 egg white, 1/2 teaspoon water
- 1 scant tablespoon flour
- 2 tablespoons grated breadcrumbs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 20 g lard or margarine

## Directions

1. Cut bacon into 1/2 cm thick slices so that the stripes run lengthwise and soak in 3/8 l water. Beat the egg white with 1/2 teaspoon water with a fork on a saucer. Mix the grated breadcrumbs with salt and flour; put the fat in a pan and place on the stove.
2. Dry the prepared bacon, roll it in the beaten egg white and then coat it evenly in the breadcrumb mixture, fry it in steaming fat from the pan while turning until golden brown in color and serve.
