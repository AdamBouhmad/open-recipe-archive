## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- butter
- sugar
- flour
- meat broth or jus

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage finely and remove the ribs.
2. Put a piece of butter and a piece of sugar, as large as a hen's egg, in a casserole.
3. When the sugar and butter are nicely golden, put in the cabbage.
4. Let it steam until soft, sprinkle flour over it.
5. Pour half a ladle of meat broth or jus over it and finish cooking.
