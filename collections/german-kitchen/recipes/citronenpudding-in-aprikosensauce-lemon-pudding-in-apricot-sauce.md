## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen water
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound sugar
- zest of 2 lemons
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 loth fine flour
- 2 loth bitter almonds
- 2 loth sweet almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- snow from egg whites
- 8 pitted apricots
- 1 schoppen wine
- 1 schoppen water
- 6 loth sugar

## Directions

1. Cook one schoppen water with one quarter pound butter in a brass pan for a quarter hour
2. Add the sugar with grated lemon zest, lemon juice, fine flour, and the ground almonds
3. Cook until the mass separates from the pan
4. Transfer to a bowl
5. Beat in the egg yolks gradually
6. Fold in the beaten egg whites slowly
7. Coat a metal capsule with butter and dust with sugar
8. Fill in the mass and seal the capsule
9. Place in a covered casserole with simmering water
10. Cook the pudding for 1-1/2 hours
11. For the sauce: cook 8 pitted apricots in a small casserole with 1 schoppen wine, 1 schoppen water, and 6 loth sugar for a quarter hour
12. Pass through a sieve
13. Heat again and pour over the arranged pudding
