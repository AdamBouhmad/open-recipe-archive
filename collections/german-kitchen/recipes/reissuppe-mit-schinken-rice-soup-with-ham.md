## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of rice
- Meat broth
- 1/2 pound of ham
- 1/4 pound of Parmesan cheese
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the rice and let stand for half an hour
2. Drain and pour boiling water over again, let stand another quarter hour
3. Wash it thoroughly
4. Pour meat broth over it and cook until soft
5. Grate the Parmesan cheese on a grater
6. Place 1/2 pound ham and 1/4 pound grated Parmesan cheese together in the soup bowl
7. Arrange the rice over it and grate nutmeg on top
