## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- sour cream, 2 spoonfuls
- calf's brain
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- fat broth

## Directions

1. Shell as many hard-boiled eggs as needed, cut in half and remove the yolks
2. Press the yolks through a hair sieve
3. Add 2 spoonfuls sour cream to the yolks
4. Skin a calf's brain, melt a piece of butter in a saucepan and braise the brain in it
5. Mix this with salt and pepper into the egg yolks
6. Fill the egg whites with the mixture
7. Place in a roasting pan, pour a little fat broth over, place in the roasting oven for a quarter hour and bake golden yellow
