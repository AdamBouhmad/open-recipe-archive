## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt water
- 1 onion
- 2 spoonfuls of flour
- Butter
- 1 spoonful of meat broth
- Nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the spinach
2. Cook it in salt water until soft
3. Soak it in cold water for one hour
4. Press it firmly
5. Mince it finely with one onion
6. Roast 2 spoonfuls of flour in a piece of butter until light yellow
7. Steam the spinach in this mixture
8. Pour 1 spoonful of meat broth over it and cook
9. Grate nutmeg over it if desired
