## Ingredients

- beautiful large plums
- boiling water
- 1.5 pounds sugar per 2 pounds of plums
- cherry schnapps

## Directions

1. Place beautiful large plums in a bowl and pour boiling water over them
2. Peel off the blue skin
3. For 2 pounds of plums, take 1.5 pounds of sugar
4. Clarify the sugar with water and cook it until it spins threads
5. Add the plums and cook them as long as a soft-boiled egg
6. Place them in a container, cover it and let it stand overnight
7. The next day cook them again for the same length of time, and on the third day do so once more
8. Place them in a container, lay paper on top treated with cherry schnapps
9. Bind it and poke holes in the paper
10. Store them
