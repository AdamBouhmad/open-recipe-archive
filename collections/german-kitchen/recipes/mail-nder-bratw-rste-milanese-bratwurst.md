## Ingredients

- tame pork, half fat
- a little basil
- a little thyme
- a little rye bread
- fine pepper
- a little crushed cloves
- salt
- a small glass of water

## Directions

1. Mince the pork finely until it resembles dough
2. Mince basil and thyme very finely
3. Mix everything together with a little rye bread, fine pepper, a little crushed cloves, and salt
4. Add a small glass of water and mix very well
5. Fill into bratwurst casings
6. These sausages can be grilled or, as the Italians do, simmered in rice or other vegetables
