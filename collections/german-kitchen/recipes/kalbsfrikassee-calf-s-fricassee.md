## Ingredients

- 375 g calf's breast
- 65 g pork, weighed
- 65 g beef, weighed
- 10 g salt
- 20 g flour
- 25 g margarine
- 1 spice measure
- 10 g soup greens
- 15 g onions
- 20 g dried mushrooms
- 12 g milk bread
- 1 knife point grated onions
- 1/2 egg
- 8 g salt and pepper
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. The calf's breast is beaten, cut into evenly-sized pieces (about 4 cm square; the cartilage remains in it) and poured over on a sieve. The dried mushrooms are soaked 2 hours beforehand in cold water.
2. Salted, dusted with flour, and covered with 20 g melted margarine or calf fat, the meat is braised until the flour is cooked through; then water is added to it, spices, soup greens and onions are tied in a cheesecloth bag, the bag is placed with the meat, everything is cooked until the meat is tender and it is skimmed once, then the mushrooms are added to the calf meat and allowed to swell slowly or use well-prepared fresh mushrooms such as champignons, porcini, or chanterelles. From the beef and pork, the soaked milk bread fried in 5 g margarine, the grated onions, 1/2 egg, pepper and salt, a dumpling dough is stirred together. With 2 ladles, evenly-shaped round dumplings are formed, which must cook in boiling salted water until done but not dry.
