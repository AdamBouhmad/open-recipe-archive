## Ingredients

- 1 finger-length piece vanilla
- 1 schoppen milk
- 4 spoonfuls flour
- cold milk
- 4 loth sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook 1 finger-length vanilla piece with 1 schoppen milk for a quarter hour
2. In a brass pan, stir 4 spoonfuls flour smooth with cold milk
3. Add 4 loth sugar, 6 egg yolks and 1 more schoppen milk, as well as the vanilla milk
4. Cook everything to a thick paste on the fire
5. Slowly fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Butter a dish, fill with the cream and bake until golden yellow
