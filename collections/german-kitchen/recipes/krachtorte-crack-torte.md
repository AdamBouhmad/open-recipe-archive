## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound refined sugar
- almond oil
- 1 schoppen cream
- 3 loth vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch 1 pound almonds, peel them, cut them finely and lengthwise, and roast them with 1 pound refined sugar in a brass pan under constant stirring until the sugar becomes yellow
2. Brush a melon mold with almond oil
3. Press the seams well with some of the roasted almonds
4. Warm the rest and fill the mold completely with them
5. When the torte has cooled, turn it upright onto a plate so that the opening faces upward
6. Whip 1 schoppen cream in a bowl with a stiff whisk until it resembles egg snow
7. Let it drain a little on a hair sieve and mix in 3 loth vanilla sugar
8. Pour this sauce over the torte
