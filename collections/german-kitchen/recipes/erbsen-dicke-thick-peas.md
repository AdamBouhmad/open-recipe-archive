## Ingredients

- 250 g yellow peas
- 1/2 liter ham or bacon broth
- 10 g onions
- 15 g lard
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the peas as for pea soup, skim and drain (after removing the husks that float on top), cook with ham or bacon broth, and when the liquid is mostly absorbed, stir to a puree with a wooden pestle; optionally add some marjoram or onions. A finely cut onion is sweated until light brown in lard and poured over the peas seasoned with pepper and salt.
