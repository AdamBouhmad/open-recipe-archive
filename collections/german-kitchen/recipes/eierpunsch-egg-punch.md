## Ingredients

- 1 Schoppen egg punch
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 large tablespoons sugar
- boiling water
- Arak

## Directions

1. To 1 Schoppen egg punch take 2 egg yolks and 2 large tablespoons of sugar
2. Stir everything together in a glass with a silver spoon until it is like porridge
3. Then pour boiling water to it with constant stirring until the glass is almost full
4. Finally fill it up completely with Arak
