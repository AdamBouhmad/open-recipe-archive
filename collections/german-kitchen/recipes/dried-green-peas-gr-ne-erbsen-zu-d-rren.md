## Ingredients

- Green peas, not very old
- Warm water

## Directions

1. Shell the peas.
2. Let boiling water pass over them twice in a pan.
3. Pour them into a strainer.
4. Lay them on paper and let them dry.
5. Store them for winter.
6. To cook, pour lukewarm water over them.
7. Let them stand overnight.
8. In the morning, drain them.
9. Pour fresh lukewarm water over them and cook them like other green peas.
