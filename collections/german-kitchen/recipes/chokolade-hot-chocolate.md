## Ingredients

- 1/2 Maas milk (or cream, which is better)
- 6 Loth chocolate
- 2 Loth sugar
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 3 egg yolks
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Calculate 6 Loth chocolate to 1/2 Maas milk
2. In a saucepan or brass pan, stir the chocolate smooth with a little water over the fire
3. Gradually stir in the milk along with 2 Loth sugar and 1 piece of whole cinnamon
4. Let this cook for a time
5. Stir 3 egg yolks smooth in a pot with cold milk
6. Slowly stir the chocolate to it
7. Let it draw once more in the pan and whisk vigorously in the pot with the chocolate until it foams
