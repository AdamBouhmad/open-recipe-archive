## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) string beans
- 2 l water for blanching
- 1/2 l mutton broth
- 1 stalk parsley
- 1 tablespoon melted beef fat or margarine
- 6 g flour
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut the string beans instead of breaking them, and prepare them otherwise the same way as green beans.
