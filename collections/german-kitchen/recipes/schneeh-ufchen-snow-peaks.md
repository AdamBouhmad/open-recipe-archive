## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 3 to 4 drops anise oil
- wax

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Mix in 1/2 pound sugar and 3 to 4 drops anise oil (the mixture should remain firm, not liquid)
3. Brush a black baking sheet with wax
4. Drop small mounds of the mixture onto the sheet
5. Bake in a cooled oven
