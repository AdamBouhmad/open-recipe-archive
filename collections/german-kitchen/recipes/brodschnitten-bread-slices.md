## Ingredients

- Mundbrode (as much as needed)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 maas milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 schoppen milk

## Directions

1. Grate Mundbrode on a grater and cut into slices
2. Place in a plafond
3. Beat 4 eggs with 1/2 maas milk and pour over the slices
4. Let stand for half an hour
5. Beat 2 more eggs with 1 schoppen milk
6. Turn the slices in this mixture and bake until golden
