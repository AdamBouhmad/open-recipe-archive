## Ingredients

- broccoli peas
- parsley
- onion
- butter
- flour
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Clean and sort the broccoli peas, discarding any damaged by worms
2. Steam parsley and onion in butter in a casserole
3. Add the broccoli peas and steam until soft
4. Sprinkle flour over them
5. Add some beef broth and let cook for a quarter hour
