## Ingredients

- 6 partridges
- half pound pork sausage meat
- Kreuzerbrod (bread roll)
- 2 eggs
- 3 Loth cleaned anchovies
- 3 Loth butter
- zest of 1 lemon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- mace
- bacon
- onions
- juniper berries
- carrots
- celery
- capers
- 2 to 3 cleaned truffles
- butter pastry
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the breasts from 6 partridges and remove bones from 2 breasts
2. Pound the meat finely in a mortar
3. Rub half a pound pork sausage meat, a soaked and firmly pressed out Kreuzerbrod and 2 eggs on a grater until foamy
4. Put the grated mixture in a bowl
5. Pound 3 Loth cleaned anchovies with 3 Loth butter, the minced zest of 1 lemon, salt, pepper, nutmeg and mace and add it
6. Lard 4 chicken breasts and the legs, place them with cut bacon, butter, onions, juniper berries, carrots and celery in a casserole
7. Cover and braise them for half an hour
8. Chop the bodies of the partridges together and braise them as well
9. Line a melon mold with butter pastry
10. Place half of the stirred mass in it
11. Place the breasts and legs on top
12. Place a spoonful of capers and 2 to 3 cleaned truffles over it
13. Cover with the other half of the stirred mixture
14. Pour the fat in which they were braised over it
15. Make a lid of butter pastry on top, brush it with egg, pinch it well together and bake the pastry in the oven
