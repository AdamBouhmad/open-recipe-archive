## Ingredients

- one strong handful fine flour
- one piece butter size of a hen's egg
- one schoppen milk
- 5 egg yolks
- one whole egg
- one loth sugar
- yellow of one lemon
- 2 coffee cups sweet cream (optional)
- quarter pound confections such as macarons
- 2 loth citron and orange peel
- 5 egg whites snow

## Directions

1. Stir a strong handful fine flour and one piece butter in the size of a hen's egg with one schoppen milk well
2. Place it on the fire and continue stirring until it is a thick porridge
3. Let it cool
4. Stir 5 egg yolks and one whole egg with one loth sugar and the yellow of one lemon into it
5. If too thick, thin with 2 coffee cups sweet cream
6. Cut a quarter pound confections (such as macarons)
7. Stir 2 loth citron and orange peel under the mass
8. Coat a springform with butter
9. Stir a stiff snow from 5 egg whites under the mass
10. Put it in the oven immediately
