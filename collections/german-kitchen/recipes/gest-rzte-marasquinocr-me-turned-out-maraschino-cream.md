## Ingredients

- a strong 1/2 maas milk
- 6 loth sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 knife points fine flour
- 1 glass maraschino
- 1 strong schoppen pure calf meat
- calf meat
- anise oil
- chocolate
- cochineal dye
- green clove leaves

## Directions

1. Let a strong 1/2 maas milk and 6 loth sugar boil together, then let cool slightly.
2. Beat 8 egg yolks in a bowl, add 2 knife points of fine flour and stir smooth.
3. Stir the cooked milk into the eggs along with 1 glass of maraschino and 1 strong schoppen of pure calf meat.
4. Let it boil together.
5. Press it through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl.
6. Brush a jelly mold with anise oil and make three differently colored jellies: one from chocolate, one from cochineal dye, the third from green clove leaves, using calf meat for all.
7. Pour out the decorations nicely and let set.
8. Pour the cooked cream into it and place on ice.
9. When serving, turn it out onto a plate and garnish to taste.
