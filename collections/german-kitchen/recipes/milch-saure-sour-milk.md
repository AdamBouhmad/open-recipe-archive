## Ingredients

- 3/4 l Whole milk
- 20 g Grated bread
- 30 g Sugar

## Directions

1. Distribute the milk among 2 deep soup bowls or small dishes, place them in a dust-free, warm room, and let them stand undisturbed for about 36 hours.
2. Serve sprinkled with bread and sugar, or serve the bread and sugar on the side.
