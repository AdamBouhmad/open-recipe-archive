## Ingredients

- White turnips
- Lard
- Bouillon or jus
- Flour
- Sugar
- Optional: carbonade, mutton, or pork

## Directions

1. After peeling and cutting the turnips, fry them in hot lard.
2. Cook them in a casserole with bouillon or jus, dust a little flour over them, add sugar and bring to a boil once more.
3. Carbonade, mutton, or pork can be added if desired.
