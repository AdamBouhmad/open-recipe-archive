## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 pinches ground cinnamon
- 4 loth small black bilberries
- 1 pinch cloves
- flour
- bacon fat

## Directions

1. Whisk together 1/2 pound sugar and 2 whole eggs until thick
2. Add 3 pinches ground cinnamon, 4 loth small black bilberries, and 1 pinch cloves
3. Work in enough flour until it forms a not-very-firm dough
4. Form a long sausage-like loaf on a pastry board
5. Place on a black baking sheet brushed with bacon fat
6. Bake slowly at gentle heat in a baking oven
7. Cut into slices when cooled
8. Keep for 2 to 3 weeks
