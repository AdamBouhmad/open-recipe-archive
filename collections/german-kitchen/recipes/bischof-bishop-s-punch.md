## Ingredients

- 1/2 Maas good red wine
- 3 sweet Seville oranges
- 3 bitter Seville oranges
- 1 Loth whole cinnamon
- 6 cloves
- 12 Loth sugar

## Directions

1. Take 3 sweet and 3 bitter Seville oranges and cut lines around them
2. Broil them on the grate
3. Place the wine on coals
4. Add the whole cinnamon, cloves, sugar and the broiled oranges
5. Let everything cook together for half an hour
6. Strain the wine through a hair sieve into the serving dish
7. It can be served cold or warm
