## Ingredients

- plafond with short dough
- gooseberries
- quarter pound finely ground sugar
- 2 knife points cinnamon
- 6 egg whites
- half a lemon juice

## Directions

1. Line a plafond with short dough
2. Cut the stems, cores and fuzz from the gooseberries
3. Cover the sheet with them
4. Stir a beautiful white icing from quarter pound finely ground sugar, 2 knife points cinnamon, 6 egg whites and the juice of half a lemon
5. Pour it over the berries
6. Bake the cake in the oven
