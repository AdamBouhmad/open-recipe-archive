## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fine flour
- 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast
- 1 schoppen of lukewarm milk
- 1/4 pound blanched almonds
- egg white
- 3 loth of ground sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- zest of 1 lemon
- 4 loth of butter
- sour cream
- melted butter

## Directions

1. With 2 pounds fine flour, 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast, and 1 schoppen of lukewarm milk make a dough in a bowl and let it rise in warmth
2. Pound 1/4 pound of blanched almonds finely with egg white
3. Add 3 loth of ground sugar, 2 egg yolks, and the zest of 1 lemon and stir everything together for a quarter hour
4. Add 4 loth of butter and work everything together with the dough well (if still too stiff, pour in sour cream)
5. Beat it for a quarter hour, cover it and let it rise well
6. When ripe enough, make noodles the size of a hen's egg, stretch them out so they become finger-long
7. Coat them first with melted butter and the stirred almonds knife-back thick, roll them together like spirals
8. Place them in a dish where butter has been melted beforehand, let them stand in it for another quarter hour and bake them in an oven until beautifully golden
9. Serve with compote of sloes
