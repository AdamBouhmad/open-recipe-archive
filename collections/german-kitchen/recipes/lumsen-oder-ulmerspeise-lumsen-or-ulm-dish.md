## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 eggs
- walnut-sized piece of butter
- 4 loth butter and milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 4 or 5 eggs, add walnut-sized piece of butter
2. Roll it out like ordinary noodle dough
3. Let 4 loth butter and milk cook together as you see fit
4. Tear this noodle cake into pieces
5. Lay the pieces in the cooked milk
6. Put them in a baking oven so they get color on both sides
7. Then arrange them on a plate
8. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
