## Ingredients

- 100 g lean bacon
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 kg pears
- 40 g sugar
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 5 g potato starch
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Peel the pears. Clean the bacon by rubbing it.
2. Half-cook the bacon in water. Cook the pears gently in the bacon broth, season with sugar and vinegar, and make creamy with the potato starch dissolved in cold water. You can also add 375 g peeled and blanched potatoes and cook together. Season the dish with salt.
