## Ingredients

- one-eighth pound biscuit
- 4 Loth bitter and sweet almond bread
- 3 schoppen milk
- 10 Loth sugar rubbed with 1 lemon
- 2 teaspoons pounded cinnamon
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 to 4 spoonfuls raspberry jelly

## Directions

1. Cook one-eighth pound biscuit and 4 Loth bitter and sweet almond bread in a brass pan with 3 schoppen milk, 10 Loth sugar rubbed with 1 lemon, and 2 teaspoons pounded cinnamon for 4 minutes while stirring constantly
2. Meanwhile beat 6 egg yolks in a bowl
3. When the cooked mixture has cooled slightly, stir it into the eggs and pass through a hair sieve
4. Dip a coffee cup or proper cream cup in cold water
5. Fill the cream into it
6. Place the cup on the fire and cover with a lid topped with weak coals until the cream is firm, which takes about half an hour with ordinary cups
7. Turn the cream out onto a plate and pour 3 to 4 spoonfuls of raspberry jelly over it
