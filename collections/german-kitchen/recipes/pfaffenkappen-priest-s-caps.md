## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound blanched almonds
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Butter dough
- Egg for glazing
- Coarsely ground sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and egg yolks with almonds and sugar and mix with lemon zest until thick like an almond cake
2. Roll out butter dough knife-back thick
3. Cut with a warm knife into strips 3 fingers wide and half a quarter-ell long
4. Place a spoonful of almond mixture in the center of each strip
5. Fold the two opposite corners diagonally over the center
6. Brush with egg
7. Fold the other two corners diagonally over the center to form a cap shape
8. Brush again with egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar
9. Place on a dark baking sheet and bake
