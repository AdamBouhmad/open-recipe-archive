## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound rice
- half Maas milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 Loth pounded sugar
- 1 lemon
- 2 Loth blanched bitter almonds
- milk
- red wine
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald one-quarter pound rice and strain through a hair sieve
2. Cook with half a Maas milk until thick
3. Add 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 4 Loth pounded sugar, 1 lemon rubbed with sugar, and 2 Loth blanched bitter almonds that have been pounded fine with milk
4. Stir everything together
5. Fill the mass into chocolate cups
6. Let the cream cool thoroughly
7. When serving, turn it out onto a plate
8. Pour red wine over it and sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
