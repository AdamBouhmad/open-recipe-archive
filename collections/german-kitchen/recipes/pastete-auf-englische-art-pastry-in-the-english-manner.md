## Ingredients

- hare meat
- mutton fat from a leg
- half a pound beef marrow
- 1 pound tender veal
- 2 lemons
- pepper
- nutmeg
- nutmeg flower
- 2 to 3 cleaned truffles
- 4 to 6 mushrooms
- 2 eggs
- salt
- butter pastry
- bacon strips
- game salami sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the hare meat
2. Cut the fat from a mutton leg
3. Chop half a pound of beef marrow and 1 pound of tender veal together into a paste and put in a bowl
4. Peel 2 lemons, finely chop the zest and pulp and add to the mixture
5. Add 2 knife-tips each of pepper, nutmeg and nutmeg flower
6. Add 2 to 3 cleaned truffles, not too finely pounded, and 4 to 6 chopped mushrooms
7. Beat in 2 eggs, salt to taste and work everything together well
8. Line a melon mold with butter pastry
9. Fill in the mixture, cover the top with bacon strips
10. Cover with a lid, seal with a pastry crimper and bake in the oven until golden
11. Turn onto a plate and make a small opening on top
12. Cook a good game salami sauce, pour it around the pastry and serve warm
