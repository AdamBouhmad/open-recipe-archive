## Ingredients

- brain
- butter
- onion
- lemon
- bread soaked in milk
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- zest of one lemon
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Skin the brain carefully
2. Place it with a piece of butter in a saucepan, mince an onion and a lemon very finely and let them steam with the brain for a quarter hour
3. Put the brain in a bowl, add bread worth 3 kreuzers that has been grated, soaked in milk and pressed out firmly
4. Beat 5 egg yolks, one after another, and add salt
5. Mince the zest of one lemon
6. Beat the whites of the 5 eggs into stiff snow
7. Mix everything together lightly
8. Butter a tin mold, fill it with the mixture, place it in a saucepan with boiling water
9. Cover with a lid with coals on top and let it cook for half an hour
10. Can be served with chicken fricassée
