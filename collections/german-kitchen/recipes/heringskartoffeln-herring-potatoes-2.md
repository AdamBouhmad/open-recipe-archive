## Ingredients

- Potatoes, sliced
- Herring, cleaned and diced
- Onions, peeled and diced
- Water (in which herring bones may be cooked)
- Pepper and salt
- Bacon (optional)

## Directions

1. If using bacon, render it in a pot and shake the pot frequently; remove the cracklings with a spoon when they turn golden brown.
2. Add potato slices to the fat and fry them quickly, turning them carefully with a broad knife frequently. When they turn yellow, add onion and herring pieces and let everything fry for a few more minutes while turning until golden brown.
3. Pour the potatoes into a bowl, pour the prepared liquid over them and let the dish covered rest in a warm place for 1/4 hour.
4. Sprinkle the hot bacon cracklings over the potatoes and serve.
