## Ingredients

- 16 egg whites
- whole lemon
- half lemon
- handful of potato flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until thick (this is added only when the mixture is already thick)
2. Grate a whole lemon on a grater and add the juice from half a lemon along with a handful of potato flour
3. Butter a tin form, fill the mixture in, and bake the torte in the oven
