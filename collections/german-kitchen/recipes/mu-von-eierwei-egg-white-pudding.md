## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 1 spoonful sugar
- 1 Schoppen milk

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites with 1 spoonful sugar into stiff peaks (you can rub the sugar on a lemon).
2. Cook in a brass pan with 1 Schoppen milk until it begins to thicken.
