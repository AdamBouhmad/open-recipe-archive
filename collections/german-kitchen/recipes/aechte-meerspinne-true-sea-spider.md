## Ingredients

- 1 boiled sea spider
- white meat from the sea spider
- eggs from the sea spider
- butter
- 1 glass wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 lemon slice

## Directions

1. After the sea spider is boiled, let it cool
2. Break off all the long legs and remove the inner white meat, keeping it tender
3. Clean the body and wash it out carefully
4. Place the meat back in the body with the eggs
5. Roast everything until nicely golden
6. Pour in a glass of wine and let it come to a boil
7. Add 1 bay leaf and 1 lemon slice
8. Let it steam until soft
