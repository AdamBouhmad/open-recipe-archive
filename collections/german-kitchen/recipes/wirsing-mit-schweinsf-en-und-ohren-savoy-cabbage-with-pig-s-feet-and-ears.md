## Ingredients

- savoy cabbage
- butter or lard
- shallot onions
- bay leaves
- cloves
- raw ham
- meat broth
- flour
- nutmeg
- salt
- pig's feet and ears

## Directions

1. Cut the stem and ribs from the savoy cabbage
2. Boil it in water for only two broths, then place it in cold water
3. In a casserole with butter or lard, add chopped shallot onions, lay them with two bay leaves, four cloves and the squeezed-out cabbage heads cut into four parts
4. Cover and braise strongly on coals
5. Pour in one spoonful of bouillon, add a piece of raw ham and let everything cook soft together
6. Add another piece of butter and another spoonful of flour, some nutmeg and salt
7. Arrange the cabbage on a plate, pass the sauce through a hair sieve onto the cabbage and garnish the plate with pig's feet and ears
