## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- 30 g margarine
- 3 tablespoons oil
- 20 g salt
- 1 teaspoon powdered marjoram and thyme
- 1 spice seasoning
- 20 g chopped onions
- 25 g flour
- 1 broth or meat extract broth
- 1/8 l apple wine
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the rabbit as directed for hare, then cut into 4 cm pieces.
2. Salt the rabbit meat and fry with the spice for 20 minutes in margarine and oil.
3. Remove the meat from the pan, sweat the onions with the flour in the butter until done, add broth and wine, season the sauce, let the meat warm through in it without boiling, and sprinkle parsley before serving.
