## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt water
- meat
- onions
- parsley
- two bread rolls, grated and squeezed dry
- three to four eggs
- butter sauce or jus

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the cabbage and cook it in salted water
2. Chop the meat, onions, and parsley finely
3. Add two grated, soaked, and firmly squeezed bread rolls
4. Stir in three to four eggs
5. Dip a cloth in cold water and lay the cabbage leaves one after another on it
6. Spread one spoonful of the filling on each leaf
7. Tie the cloth tightly together and cook the cabbage soft in salted water
8. Serve with either a butter sauce or jus
