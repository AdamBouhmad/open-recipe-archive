## Ingredients

- 1/2 l whole milk
- 40 g semolina or rice flour
- 6 tablespoons whole milk
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 piece lemon peel

## Directions

1. Whisk 6 tablespoons milk with the flour.
2. Boil 1/2 l milk with lemon peel and sugar, pour in the whisked flour, and let everything cook while stirring until it appears clear. Pour the jelly into a glass bowl and serve with fruit sauce when cooled.
3. Remark: Instead of rice flour, one can use 9 sheets of white gelatin.
