## Ingredients

- 125 g lean beef or veal
- 1/4 l water
- 3 g salt

## Directions

1. The washed meat is finely chopped, better still minced, cold water is poured over it, salt is added.
2. This mixture is cooked covered slowly for 3/4 hour and poured through a sieve. The broth can be served in the nursing bottle alone or with milk, and is also used for softening rusks or old rolls for a small child.
