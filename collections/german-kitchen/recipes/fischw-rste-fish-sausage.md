## Ingredients

- fish (whitefish or barbel)
- milk
- onions
- parsley
- lemons
- butter
- 2 to 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, cutting the skin open along the back and peeling it off
2. Scrape the flesh from the bones and soak it for half an hour in milk to make it white
3. Weigh onions, parsley, and lemons and braise them in a piece of butter
4. Mince the fish meat as finely as possible
5. Stir it in a bowl with milk until it is completely white
6. Add 2 to 3 eggs and stir for another quarter hour
7. Add the braised vegetables with salt and nutmeg
8. Press the mass into casings and place the sausages in warm water
9. Fry them on a grill or in a pan
