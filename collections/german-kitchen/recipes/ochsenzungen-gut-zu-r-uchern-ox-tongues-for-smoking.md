## Ingredients

- Ox tongues
- Rock salt
- Saltpeter
- Pounded pepper
- Juniper berries
- Thyme
- Basil
- Celery
- All kinds of herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the gullet from the ox tongues
2. Roast rock salt and saltpeter together with pounded pepper and juniper berries
3. Rub the tongues thoroughly with this mixture
4. Place them in a dish
5. Add all kinds of herbs, thyme, basil, and celery
6. Leave them in the brine for 4 weeks
7. Weight them down with a well-fitting lid
8. Hang them in a large intestine casing in smoke to be smoked
9. To cook them, soak overnight in cold water
10. Cook them with cold water, onions, and all kinds of herbs until tender
11. Serve cold or warm
