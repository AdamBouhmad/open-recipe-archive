## Ingredients

- 20 g lean bacon
- 15 g onions
- 20 g flour
- 1/4 l water
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 crushed spice portion
- salt to taste
- 15 g sugar or 1 1/2 tablespoons syrup
- 1-2 drops food coloring

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into small cubes, chop the onions finely.
2. In an earthenware pot, fry the bacon with the onion until light yellow, sweat it through, then cook the flour in it and cook down with the water. Season the sauce with vinegar, spice, salt, sugar or syrup and serve it.
3. Remark: This sauce is suitable as an accompaniment to potatoes in their skins and also to stewed cucumbers. Mixed with 2 tablespoons diced pickles: cucumber sauce.
