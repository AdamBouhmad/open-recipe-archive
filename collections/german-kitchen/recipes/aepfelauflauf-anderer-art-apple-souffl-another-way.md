## Ingredients

- 14 to 16 Borsdorfer apples
- a piece of butter
- a piece of sugar
- butter for the dish
- equal weight of eggs, sugar, and almonds
- finely ground sugar
- peeled almonds
- egg whites
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Steam 14 to 16 Borsdorfer apples with a piece of butter and a piece of sugar in a casserole until not very soft.
2. Brush the dish in which you will serve the soufflé with butter and place the apples in it.
3. Take equal weight of eggs, sugar, and almonds. Finely grind the sugar and peel the almonds and grate them with egg white on a grater to a paste.
4. Stir together the sugar, egg yolks, and almonds ground with egg white as for an almond cake.
5. Beat the remaining egg whites to a stiff snow and mix into the mass.
6. Pour this over the apples and bake in the oven until nicely golden.
