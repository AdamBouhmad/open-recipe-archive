## Ingredients

- 20 g oil
- 20 g onions
- 20 g flour
- 3/4 l broth
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon herbs, chives, parsley
- salt to taste
- 1 pinch white pepper

## Directions

1. Weigh the herbs and blanch them. Cut the onions into small pieces.
2. Sweat the oil, onions, and flour together, cook with 1/4 l broth and 1 tablespoon vinegar, strain through a sieve, and cool while stirring. Add the herbs and enough broth so the sauce has the right thickness.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and vinegar.
4. The sauce can be refined with chopped capers, pickles, mustard, and beaten egg yolk.
