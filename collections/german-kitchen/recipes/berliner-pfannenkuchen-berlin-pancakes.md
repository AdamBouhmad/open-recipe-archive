## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 2 spoonfuls good thick beer yeast
- lukewarm milk
- 8 loth butter
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- lukewarm cream
- 1/4 pound large and small raisins
- cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a small pre-dough from 1 pound flour, 2 spoonfuls good thick beer yeast, and lukewarm milk, and let it stand and rise
2. Stir 8 loth butter in a bowl until foamy
3. Beat in 4 egg yolks one after another
4. Work the stirred butter and flour together
5. Add salt and sugar, then beat the dough for another quarter hour; lukewarm cream can be added if it is still too stiff
6. Set the dough in warmth and let it rise, but not until completely ripe
7. Meanwhile, take 1/4 pound large and small raisins
8. Roll out half the dough on the noodle board to knife-back thickness
9. Brush with melted butter and cover completely with raisins and currants
10. Roll out the other half of the dough equally thin and lay it on the first cake
11. Cut or punch out cakes with a round model
12. Fry beautifully golden in a pan with hot lard
13. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
