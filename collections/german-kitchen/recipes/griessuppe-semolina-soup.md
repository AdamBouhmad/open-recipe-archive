## Ingredients

- meat broth
- 4 handfuls of semolina flour
- butter (hen-egg size)
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls of sour cream
- chives
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. In a pot of simmering meat broth, stir in 4 handfuls of semolina flour
2. Add butter the size of a hen's egg
3. Let this cook together for a while
4. Beat 2 egg yolks in a soup bowl
5. Stir them smooth with 2 spoonfuls of sour cream
6. Stir the soup into them and sprinkle chives and nutmeg on top
