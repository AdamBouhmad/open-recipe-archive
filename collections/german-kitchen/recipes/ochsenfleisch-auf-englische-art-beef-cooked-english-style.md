## Ingredients

- beef from the brisket
- 2 Maas water
- 3 onions
- 2 bay leaves
- celery root, cut into pieces
- yellow turnips
- whole cloves
- 3 Maas sweet cream
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of beef brisket in 2 Maas water.
2. Add 3 onions, 2 bay leaves, a celery root cut into pieces, yellow turnips, and whole cloves.
3. When the meat is half tender, pour 3 Maas sweet cream over it.
4. Let it finish cooking in the cream.
5. Arrange it on a platter.
6. Pass the sauce through a hair sieve over it and garnish the platter with parsley.
