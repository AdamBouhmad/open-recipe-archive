## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) asparagus spears
- 5 g salt
- 1 l water
- 3 g sugar
- 50 g butter
- 1 tablespoon grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash the asparagus and peel it with a fine knife, starting from the tip and peeling more strongly toward the bottom, keeping it nicely round.
2. Bundle asparagus of equal length and thickness, about 6-8 together.
3. Put thinner asparagus in the cooking water later than thicker ones.
4. Cook the asparagus in boiling salted water with sugar for 20-30 minutes until soft.
5. Longer cooking makes it yellow and unpalatable.
6. Remove it carefully with 2 ladles.
7. Remove the string and arrange the asparagus with the tips overlapping.
8. Serve with melted or brown butter in which some bread crumbs have been fried, alongside or on top.
9. For broken asparagus, divide the spears into equal pieces, cook as for whole asparagus spears, drain, and mix with parsley sauce prepared without onion using asparagus water.
