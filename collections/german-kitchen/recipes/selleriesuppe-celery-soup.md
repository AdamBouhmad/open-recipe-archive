## Ingredients

- A piece of butter
- 3 to 4 clean washed celery roots
- Two finely chopped shallot onions
- Two chopped leek stalks
- As much flour as can be held between three fingers
- Meat broth or jus
- Toasted bread crumbs
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole
2. Cut the celery as finely as noodles and place with the shallot onions and leek stalks in the casserole
3. Cover and let the celery braise until soft
4. Sprinkle with as much flour as can be held between three fingers
5. Pour in meat broth or jus as needed for the soup
6. Let cook another quarter hour
7. Arrange over toasted bread crumbs and grate nutmeg on top
