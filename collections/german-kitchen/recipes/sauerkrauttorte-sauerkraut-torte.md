## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sauerkraut
- cold water
- zest of 3 lemons
- 3 loth blanched almonds
- 3 loth citron
- 3 loth orange peel
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 glass water
- butter dough
- egg
- raw potatoes
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from 1/2 pound sauerkraut, place it in cold water, and add fresh water daily, repeating this 5 to 6 days
2. Cook the sauerkraut soft, let it drain in a sieve, and press it firmly
3. Chop the zest from 3 lemons, cut 3 loth blanched almonds, 3 loth citron, and orange peel lengthwise
4. Cook 1/2 pound sugar with 1/2 glass water in a brass pan until the sugar forms bubbles
5. Add the sauerkraut and the chopped ingredients, let everything cook 2 to 3 minutes
6. Line a plafond with butter dough, brush with egg, place raw potatoes in the sheet so the dough does not shrink, and bake golden yellow
7. Press the juice from 2 lemons on it, remove the potatoes, fill the mixture into the sheet, and let the torte bake a quarter hour in the oven
