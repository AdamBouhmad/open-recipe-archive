## Ingredients

- Beautiful green reinesclaudes, not overripe
- Sugar (same weight as the reinesclaudes)

## Directions

1. Cook beautiful green reinesclaudes that are not overripe in a non-tinned casserole with as much sugar as the reinesclaudes weigh until it reaches the bubble stage, or as long as it takes to hard boil eggs
2. Take a silver spoon and lift them out so they do not break
3. Let the syrup cook down a little
4. Pour it over them
5. Let it stand overnight
6. Repeat this process for 3 days in a row
7. Each time a non-tinned casserole must be used, otherwise they will lose their beautiful green color
