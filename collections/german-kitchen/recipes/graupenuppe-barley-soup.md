## Ingredients

- 40 g barley
- 1 l mutton broth or water
- 15 g beef fat or margarine
- 1/2 egg
- 8 g salt
- 1 tablespoon celery and onion pieces

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley in an earthenware pot with water and put it on the stove with water and salt to soften. Clean celery and onion and cut into small pieces; finely chop the beef fat.
2. Drain the barley and brown it with celery, onion pieces and the fat; gradually pour in broth or water and salt while stirring and cook the barley slowly until soft covered. When the barley is done, mix the egg with a few tablespoons of soup and pour it into the whole liquid. Adding a few potatoes cooked together and 1 teaspoon parsley makes the soup tastier. For children and the sick it can also be pressed through a fine sieve to make 'barley slime soup,' but then butter is preferable.
