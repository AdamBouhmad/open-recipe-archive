## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas honey
- 3 spoonfuls cherry spirit
- ground fennel
- anise
- ginger
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 pinch potash
- flour
- egg for brushing
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Let 1/2 maas honey cook in a brass pan for a quarter hour
2. Transfer to a bowl and add 3 spoonfuls cherry spirit
3. Add ground fennel, anise, ginger, 1 pinch pepper, and 1 pinch potash
4. Add enough flour to make a workable dough
5. Transfer to a pastry board and press into the molds
6. Place the gingerbread on a black baking sheet dusted with flour
7. Brush with egg and place a blanched almond on each of the four corners
8. Bake in the oven
