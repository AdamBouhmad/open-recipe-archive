## Ingredients

- half a pound blanched almonds
- rose water
- quarter pound sugar
- one-eighth pound butter
- almonds
- flowers

## Directions

1. Pound half a pound blanched almonds with rose water very fine.
2. Stir in a quarter pound sugar and one-eighth pound butter.
3. Stir everything together for half an hour.
4. Form a mountain on a not very large dish.
5. Stick almonds and flowers into it.
6. Serve at table; you can also sprinkle cinnamon over it.
