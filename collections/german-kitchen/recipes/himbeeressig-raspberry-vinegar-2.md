## Ingredients

- raspberries (measured in Maas)
- authentic wine vinegar (equal measure to raspberries)
- sugar (1 pound per 1 Maas vinegar)

## Directions

1. Pour as many Maas of authentic wine vinegar as raspberries over the berries
2. Bind it up, place it in the cellar for 3 to 4 days and strain the clear liquid through a cloth
3. Pour the vinegar with sugar into a pan (calculate 1 pound of pure sugar per 1 Maas vinegar)
4. Let it cook on the fire for a quarter hour
5. Skim the foam carefully
6. Pour it into a pot and let it stand overnight
7. The next day fill it into bottles and store it for use
