## Ingredients

- piece of loin roast (from the loin)
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- parsley
- anchovy
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of loin roast into finger-thick pieces
2. Beat them a little
3. Rub them with salt and pepper
4. Lay them over good coals on a grill and pour melted butter over them
5. Meanwhile make an anchovy butter as follows: weigh parsley with a carefully cleaned and boned anchovy
6. Work this into a piece of butter
7. When the beefsteak on the grill is done, arrange it on a plate
8. Put a little of the butter on each piece, pour lemon juice over it and serve quickly
9. Note: these beefsteaks should still be juicy in the middle and are done in a quarter hour
