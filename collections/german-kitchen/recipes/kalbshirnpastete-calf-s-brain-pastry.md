## Ingredients

- short pastry dough
- butter
- 4 calf brains
- water
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- 3 shallot onions
- cold water
- light meat broth
- juice of half a lemon
- 1 glass white wine
- half a nutmeg
- nutmeg flower
- zest of half a lemon
- egg

## Directions

1. Fold the short pastry dough over itself 3 to 4 times, roll it out 2 fingers thick and place it on a black baking sheet
2. Place a large plate on the rolled-out dough and cut around it with a hot knife to remove the excess dough
3. Cut a second piece of dough around the plate that will be served at table
4. Pierce the lid or smaller piece of dough with a hot knife and press the edges together with a hot knife
5. Brush both pieces with egg and bake them on a black baking sheet in the oven until golden, then carefully remove the lid
6. Cook the 4 cleaned calf brains in a copper pan or casserole with half water and half vinegar until as firm as hard eggs
7. In a casserole, melt a piece of butter, sprinkle in a small spoonful of flour, add 3 finely chopped shallot onions, and braise this for a while in the butter
8. Stir it smooth with half a small ladle of cold water, pour in 1 ladle of light meat broth, add the juice of half a lemon, 1 glass of white wine, half a nutmeg, a knife-tip of nutmeg flower
9. Let everything cook together for a quarter hour, add the zest of half a lemon and let it cook a while longer
10. When ready to serve, put the pastry back in the oven to warm it, place it on a plate, fill it with the brain, cover with the lid and serve
