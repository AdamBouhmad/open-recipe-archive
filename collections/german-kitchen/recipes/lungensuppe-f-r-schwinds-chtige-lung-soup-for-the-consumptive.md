## Ingredients

- 1 veal lung and some heart, cut into small pieces
- 6 crayfish
- 1 handful of chervil
- 1 handful of speedwell
- A few cow's parsley leaves
- Water
- Egg yolks (optional)
- Toasted bread slices (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the veal lung and heart into small pieces; the lung should be taken fresh from the calf if possible
2. Kill 6 crayfish alive and add them to the lung
3. Place in a pot with 1 handful of chervil, 1 handful of speedwell, and several leaves of cow's parsley, all well washed
4. Pour 2 measures of water over it and let half of it reduce
5. Strain through a hair sieve
6. Can be served with toasted bread slices or finished with egg yolks
