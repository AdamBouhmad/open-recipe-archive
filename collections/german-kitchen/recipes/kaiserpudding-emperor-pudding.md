## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound almonds
- egg white
- 1 lemon
- 1/4 pound citron and orange peel
- 1/2 pound butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 white Kreuzer roll
- 8 egg whites
- butter
- sugar
- 1-1/2 pints wine
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 fingers full flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pound 1/2 pound almonds with egg white as finely as pap.
2. Chop the lemon peel, 1/4 pound citron and orange peel very finely.
3. Beat 1/2 pound butter very foamy, stir together the above with 10 egg yolks, a white Kreuzer roll rubbed on the grater and pressed through a sieve, and the snow beaten from 8 egg whites very well together, like an almond torte.
4. Brush a pudding mold with butter, sprinkle it with sugar, fill the mass into it, brush the mold's cover with butter, seal it tightly, place it in boiling water and cook it a good half hour.
5. For the sauce: cook 1-1/2 pints of wine with a little lemon peel and whole cinnamon, stir 3 egg yolks and 3 fingers full of flour smooth with cold water, pour the boiling wine to it, spurt everything with the chocolate spurtle until it rises over itself, arrange the pudding and pour the sauce through a sieve over it.
