## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely ground sugar
- 10 egg whites
- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- 1/2 pound unblanced almonds
- zest of 2 lemons
- 1/4 pound citron
- 1/4 pound orange peel
- butter
- wafers

## Directions

1. Beat together 1 pound finely ground sugar and 10 egg whites into a thick icing
2. Cut 1/2 pound blanched almonds lengthwise as finely as possible
3. Grate 1/2 pound unblanced almonds together with the zest of 2 lemons on a grater or nutmeg grater
4. Cut 1/4 pound citron and orange peel finely
5. Slowly fold all ingredients into the icing
6. Butter a springform or ring and line it with wafers
7. Fill the mixture into the form and bake the torte at low heat until beautifully done
