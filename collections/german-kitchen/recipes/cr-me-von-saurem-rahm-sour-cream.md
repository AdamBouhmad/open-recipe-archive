## Ingredients

- thick sour cream
- 1 lemon
- one-eighth pound sugar
- grated biscuit

## Directions

1. Beat thick sour cream until very foamy
2. Rub 1 lemon with one-eighth pound sugar
3. Pound the sugar and mix with the rubbed zest under the cream
4. Arrange the cream and sprinkle grated biscuit over it
