## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) potatoes (boiled)
- 50 g bacon or 40 g lard = 2-1/2 tablespoons melted fat
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. The boiled and slightly cooled unpeeled potatoes are peeled and cut into even slices. The bacon is cut into cubes and fried to a good color in a pan; the cracklings are removed; fat can also be used instead of bacon. The potatoes are put in the fat, when it steams, salt is sprinkled over and the potatoes are fried to a nice brown, stirring frequently with a spoon.
