## Ingredients

- 60 g dried snap or breaking beans
- Water for cooking
- 15 g fat
- 8 g flour
- 1/4 liter water
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 sprig pepper herb
- 250 g peeled potatoes
- Water and salt for blanching
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes into pieces.
2. Put the beans on with cold water, bring to a boil, drain and finish like fresh beans; drain the blanched potatoes, lay them on the beans; both cook together covered for 3/4 hour until soft and are then mixed together.
