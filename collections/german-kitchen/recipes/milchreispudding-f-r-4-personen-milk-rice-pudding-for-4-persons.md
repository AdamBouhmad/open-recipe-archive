## Ingredients

- 125 g Rice
- 1 pinch Baking soda
- 1/2 l Water for parboiling
- 1/2 l Whole milk
- 1 pinch Salt
- 65 g Butter or margarine
- 125 g Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 1 tablespoon Lemon sugar
- 5 g Margarine
- 1 tablespoon Grated breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Wash the rice, parboil it, and drain it.
2. Cook the rice in the milk with salt slowly until soft and thick, then chill it.
3. Whip the fat into a cream, add sugar and egg yolks, then add the rice and lemon sugar. Finally, fold in the beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mixture into a greased and breadcrumb-coated mold, cook it in boiling water for 1 hour, and turn it out onto a hot plate. Serve with fruit or wine foam sauce.
5. You can also use leftover milk rice for the pudding.
