## Ingredients

- Tarragon, tender leaves (1 strong handful)
- Good wine vinegar
- Pimpinella (6 pieces)
- Shallot onions (6)
- Cloves (8)
- White peppercorns (8)
- Ginger, whole (1 piece)
- Cinnamon, whole (1 piece)
- Lemon (a piece)

## Directions

1. Take a strong handful of tender tarragon leaves.
2. Pour good wine vinegar over them in a clean dish, together with 6 pimpinellas, 6 shallot onions, 8 cloves, 8 white peppercorns, a piece of whole ginger, a piece of whole cinnamon, and a piece of lemon.
3. Bind the dish tightly closed.
4. Place it in the sun for 14 days or 3 weeks, or otherwise in a warm place.
5. After 3 weeks, the vinegar is exceptionally good.
6. Fill it into bottles, close them tightly.
7. The vinegar can be used for sauces and salads.
