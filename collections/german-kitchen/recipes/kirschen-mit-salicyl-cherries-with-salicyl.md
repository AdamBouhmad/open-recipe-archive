## Ingredients

- 3 kg (6 Pfd.) sour cherries
- 1-1/2 kg (3 Pfd.) granulated sugar
- 3 g salicyl

## Directions

1. Preparation: Pit very fleshy sour cherries. Mix them in a stone pot with the sugar and set aside covered and cool for one night.
2. The next day, dissolve the salicyl with 3 tablespoons of the juice, stir it into the cherries, and tie up the pot with parchment paper. Every other day the cherries must be stirred until the juice covers the fruit. Prepared this way, the compote keeps excellently.
3. More palatable and economical: take the necessary compote from the pot, dilute it slightly with water, poach the cherries, and cook the sauce with a little cornstarch to a thick consistency.
