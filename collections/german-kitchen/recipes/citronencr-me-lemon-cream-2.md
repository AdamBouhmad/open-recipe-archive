## Ingredients

- sugar (quarter pound)
- lemons (2)
- wine (half a maas)
- egg yolks (6)
- flour
- water (2 tablespoons)

## Directions

1. Cook a quarter pound sugar rubbed with 2 lemons with half a maas wine and the juice of the lemons for 4 minutes
2. Stir 6 egg yolks smooth with a little flour and 2 tablespoons water
