## Ingredients

- walnuts with still green shells
- dry salt

## Directions

1. Lay the walnuts, which still have green shells, in a pot on dry salt
2. Press them together with the hand
3. Cover them with salt
4. Bind a cloth over it
5. Bury the pot in the earth, but in such a way that frost does not reach it
6. Thus one can preserve them for a long time
