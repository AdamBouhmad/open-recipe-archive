## Ingredients

- 65 g beef
- 30 g rice
- 1/4 bunch celery
- 1 liter water
- 8 g salt
- 1 small carrot

## Directions

1. Prepare: Wash the meat and cut into cubes, peel celery, wash and cut into cubes, clean and grate the carrot. Wash the rice three times in lukewarm water.
2. Put the meat and celery cubes, salt, and carrot in 1 liter water covered; add the rice 3/4 hour before serving and cook until tender. You can also add washed potato pieces to this soup and cook them for 3/4 hour.
