## Ingredients

- 1 l whole milk
- 50 g sugar
- 2 g cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon lemon sugar or 1 piece orange peel
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 5 g cornstarch
- 6 tablespoons whole milk
- 2 rusks

## Directions

1. Cook milk, sugar, spices and salt.
2. Whisk the egg yolk mixed with cornstarch and 6 tablespoons of cold milk into the hot milk, stir the mixture on the heat until slightly slimy and let it cool.
3. Before serving, pour the soup through a sieve.
4. Add rusk crumbles.
5. Optional variations: Strawberry milk cold soup - add 125 g fresh strawberries mixed with 40 g sugar when serving; Elder milk cold soup - steep 1 flower cluster of elder or elderflower for 2 minutes in the milk; Peach milk cold soup - let 4 peach leaves steep for 2 minutes in the milk instead of lemon sugar; Vanilla cold soup - use 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar instead of cinnamon and lemon sugar.
