## Ingredients

- 300 g magerer Schweinebauch (lean pork belly)
- 8 g Salz (salt)
- 15 g Zwiebel (onion)
- 1 Gewürzdosis (1 spice dose)
- 1 Stiel Majoran (1 sprig marjoram)
- 3/4 l Wasser (water)

## Directions

1. Scrub the pork belly thoroughly in water like all pork and rub well with salt.
2. Place in cold water, bring to a boil and skim; after adding onion, spice dose, and dried marjoram, cook covered slowly for 2 hours.
3. Slice the meat and serve with vegetables; the broth is tasty with all root vegetable dishes, sauerkraut, and legumes; degrease before use.
