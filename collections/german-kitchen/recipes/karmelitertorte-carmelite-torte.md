## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds, cut lengthwise very finely
- 7 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound sifted sugar
- zest of 2 lemons
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 pound flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the blanched almonds lengthwise as finely as possible
2. Whip 7 egg yolks in a deep bowl into a stiff foam
3. Add 1/2 pound sifted sugar and stir vigorously for a quarter hour
4. Add the zest of 2 lemons, the juice of 1 lemon, 1/4 pound flour, and half of the almonds to the mixture
5. Immediately fill this into a torte form brushed with butter
6. Sprinkle the remaining almonds on top and bake until nicely golden yellow
7. Turn the torte out onto a plate
