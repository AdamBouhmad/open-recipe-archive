## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1.5 pounds) raw unpeeled potatoes
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. After cleaning, peel the potatoes in water, round in shape; leave smaller ones whole and halve larger ones.
2. After peeling, wash the potatoes and place them in cold water with salt.
3. Cook the potatoes well covered for 20 minutes to an hour, depending on age and variety.
4. Drain the water completely.
5. Dry-steam the potatoes for several more minutes covered on a hot plate with frequent shaking.
6. Open the pot briefly and serve the potatoes.
