## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Pork fat
- Onions
- 1 bottle Champagne
- Larks
- Fat
- Bay leaf
- Whole pepper
- Cloves

## Directions

1. Cook the sauerkraut soft with pork fat, onions, and a bottle of Champagne.
2. Pluck the larks, remove the eyes, place the head on the breast, twist the feet, thread 6-7 larks onto a wooden skewer and cook them in a casserole with a piece of fat, onions, bay leaf, whole pepper and cloves for an hour.
3. When they are beautiful yellow, arrange the sauerkraut, place the larks nicely on top so their heads are visible, and cover with sauerkraut again, but the heads must always remain visible.
