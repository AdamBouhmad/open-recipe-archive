## Ingredients

- 50 snails
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Sorrel
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth
- Egg yolks
- Sour cream
- Toasted bread slices
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the snails as usual, clean them, remove the slime, and cook for another half hour, then chop
2. Finely chop 1 handful each of parsley, chervil, and sorrel together
3. Steam the snails with the chopped herbs in melted butter for a quarter hour
4. Bring to a boil with 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 ladle of meat broth, adding more meat broth as needed
5. Arrange the soup over toasted bread slices
6. Stir in 2 egg yolks with 2 tablespoons of sour cream
7. Season to taste with salt, nutmeg, etc.
