## Ingredients

- fresh plums
- coarsely crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place fresh plums in a sugar glass so that all stems point upward
2. Add coarsely crushed cinnamon and cloves
3. Fill the glass with plums
4. Cook as much vinegar as needed with sugar (for half a Maas of vinegar, 12 Loth of sugar are required)
5. When the vinegar is still lukewarm, pour it over the plums
6. Let them stand overnight
7. The next day, pour off the vinegar, cook it again in the pan, and repeat this a third time
8. Pull a paper through cherry schnapps, lay it on the plums
9. Bind the glass and store it in a dry place
