## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Onion
- Lemon
- Bacon
- Carrots
- Bay leaves
- Juniper berries
- Fat
- Bread crust
- Lard
- Red wine
- Jus
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and pluck the partridges, singe them, cut off the wings at the second joint and remove the heads and innards.
2. If the partridges are fresh, do not wash them but rub them inside with salt and pepper.
3. Insert an onion studded with cloves and a piece of lemon into the opening.
4. Wrap the partridges with bacon and place them in a casserole with chopped onion, carrots, bay leaves, juniper berries, a piece of fat, and the heads, wings, stomach and liver.
5. Cover the casserole and let it brown well.
6. When cooked, place the partridges on a platter to keep warm.
7. Remove the other ingredients from the casserole and pound them finely in a mortar.
8. Pour the pounded mixture back into the casserole.
9. Add a bread crust fried in lard.
10. Pour in a schoppen of red wine and a spoonful of jus.
11. Let it boil for a quarter hour, strain through a fine sieve and pour back into the casserole.
12. Add the partridges and chopped lemon, cloves, nutmeg, pepper and salt to taste.
