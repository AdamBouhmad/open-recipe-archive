## Ingredients

- 140 g beef without fat and bones
- 1 g caraway
- 10 g fat
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 5 g flour
- 10 g onions
- 5 g salt
- 1/8 liter hot water

## Directions

1. Cut the clean washed beef into 1-1/2 cm cubes, dice the onion, and soften it slowly in fat in an earthenware pot.
2. Add the meat to the softened onion dice, fry it slowly until it is somewhat browned, dust flour over it, and add 1 pinch salt.
3. When the flour is cooked, add hot water, add the prepared caraway, cover the pot, and let the dish simmer slowly for 3 hours under frequent shaking and addition of some water, skim fat if necessary, add pepper, check the consistency of the sauce, and serve the dish.
4. Also use leftovers, especially dry pieces and trimmings from braised or beef roasts for this flavorful and hearty dish.
