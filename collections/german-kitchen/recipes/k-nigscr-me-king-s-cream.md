## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1/2 maas milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- 1 loth blanched, finely pounded bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Cook 1/4 pound sugar in a brass pan with 2 tablespoons water until the sugar becomes light yellow.
2. Pour it into any desired form and let it run all around.
3. Stir together 1/2 maas milk, 2 eggs, 1 cooking spoon flour, and 1 loth blanched, finely pounded bitter almonds.
4. Pour everything into the sugared form.
5. Place it in boiling water.
6. Cover with a lid topped with coals.
7. Let it cook for 2 to 3 hours and then turn it out onto a plate.
