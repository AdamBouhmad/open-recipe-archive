## Ingredients

- 125 g millet
- water and salt
- 1/2 l skim milk
- 1 pinch salt
- 5 g fat
- 1 piece orange peel
- 20 g fat for pouring over
- 1 teaspoon sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Sort the millet and bring to a boil three times with cold water, pouring off the water each time when hot
2. After draining, brown the millet with 5 g fat, add the milk, salt and orange peel
3. Let the millet swell while stirring frequently; when done, serve in a bowl
4. Pour 20 g browned fat over it and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
