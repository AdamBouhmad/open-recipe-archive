## Ingredients

- 90 g Sugar
- 4 Egg yolks
- 1/2 Grated lemon
- 2 tablespoons Skim milk
- 220 g Margarine
- 1/2 kg (1 pound) Flour
- 1 Egg for brushing
- 1 Piece of bacon rind for the baking sheet

## Directions

1. Combine the sugar, lemon, milk, and egg yolks, then rub them into the margarine beaten to a foam. Add the flour last.
2. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a knife back, cut out small cakes or tartlets (these must be provided with a rim), brush them with egg, and sprinkle them, depending on how you wish to use them, with lengthwise-cut almonds, currants, or sugar and cinnamon.
3. Bake them on a greased baking sheet to a good color.
