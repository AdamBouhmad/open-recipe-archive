## Ingredients

- 1 cucumber
- 1 tbsp oil
- 5 g salt
- 1 strong pinch white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp chives
- 1 pinch sugar

## Directions

1. Wipe the cucumber and peel it from the tip to the stem end until all the green is removed, taste it, and if it is bitter, discard it wholly or partially. Shortly before serving, slice it thinly. Chop the chives.
2. Mix the cucumber slices with oil, salt, pepper, chives, sugar and acid and, because they quickly draw juice, serve immediately to the table.
3. Salad of pickled cucumbers is made exactly the same way, especially mixed with potato slices.
4. Radish salad is prepared the same way.
5. Cucumber salad with sour cream: 4 tbsp thick sour cream beaten clear with 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tsp salt, 1 pinch pepper (optionally also 1 tsp sugar) and 1 tsp chives are mixed with the cucumber slices.
