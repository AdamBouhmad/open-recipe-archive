## Ingredients

- Rice
- 5 g Beef fat or margarine
- 5 g Flour
- 1/4 l Water
- 1/4 l Milk
- Orange or lemon zest
- Salt
- 20 g Sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice thoroughly with cold water, drain it, bring it to a boil with cold water in an earthenware pot, and drain again.
2. Sauté it with 5 g fat, sprinkle flour over it, and cook briskly for several minutes in an open pot with 1/4 l water, 1/4 l milk, orange or lemon zest, and salt, shaking frequently.
3. Cover it and let it simmer slowly, shaking frequently but not stirring, until the rice is tender. Remove the zest.
4. Plate the rice and sprinkle it with sugar and cinnamon.
