## Ingredients

- 4 cooking spoonfuls flour
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 schoppen milk
- 4 loth finely ground sugar
- 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Stir 4 egg yolks into 4 cooking spoonfuls flour in a brass pan, pour 1/2 schoppen milk into it, and cook to a thick cream.
2. Put the cream into a bowl and add 4 loth finely ground sugar and 1/2 lemon grated on a grater.
