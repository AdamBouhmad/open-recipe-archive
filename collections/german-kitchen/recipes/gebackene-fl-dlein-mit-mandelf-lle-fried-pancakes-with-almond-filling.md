## Ingredients

- flour
- milk or water for pancakes
- sugar
- 1/4 pound blanched almonds, finely pounded with egg white
- 6 loth finely pounded sugar
- 1/2 lemon, zested on a grater
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 egg, beaten
- lard
- wine or milk
- arak

## Directions

1. Prepare pancakes (Flädlein) as usual, but add some sugar to the batter
2. Mix 1/4 pound blanched almonds with egg white and pound finely
3. Beat together with 6 loth finely pounded sugar, 1/2 lemon zest, and 3 whole eggs until thick
4. Separate the pancakes and spread the filling on each one
5. Roll them together
6. Cut each roll in half
7. Turn in beaten egg
8. Fry well in lard
9. Make a wine or milk sauce with arak to serve alongside
