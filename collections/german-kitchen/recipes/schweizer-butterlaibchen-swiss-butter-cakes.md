## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- one quarter pound sugar
- one quarter pound almonds
- one quarter pound butter
- 1.5 quarter pounds flour
- lemon peel
- ground cinnamon
- 3 eggs
- half pound flour
- 8 to 10 blanched almonds
- 3 loth candied lemon peel

## Directions

1. Stir together 2 eggs, one quarter pound sugar, one quarter pound almonds and one quarter pound butter
2. Add 1.5 quarter pounds flour, and if desired, finely cut lemon peel and ground cinnamon
3. Sprinkle a baking sheet with flour and place cakes from the dough, somewhat larger than a pepper nut, onto it using a spoon
4. Bake in the oven
5. They can be brushed with egg, sprinkled with sugar and ground almonds, or alternatively the almonds can be left out of the dough and instead 3 eggs, half pound flour, 8 to 10 blanched almonds and 3 loth cut candied lemon peel can be added to the butter and sugar; otherwise they are made like the previous cakes
