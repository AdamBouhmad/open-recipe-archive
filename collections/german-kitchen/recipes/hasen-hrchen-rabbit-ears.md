## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 spoonfuls of sugar
- 2 spoonfuls of thick sour cream
- 6 loth of fine flour

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 3 egg yolks, 3 spoonfuls of sugar, 2 spoonfuls of thick sour cream, and 6 loth of fine flour in a bowl
2. Roll it out, fold it over, and roll it out again knife-back thick
3. Cut square pieces from it
