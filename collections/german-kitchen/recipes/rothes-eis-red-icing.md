## Ingredients

- one quarter pound grated fine sugar
- 1 egg white
- cochineal dye or peas juice

## Directions

1. Beat one egg white into one quarter pound grated fine sugar
2. Stir together for a quarter hour
3. Add cochineal dye or pea juice drop by drop until it is beautifully red and use as desired
