## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound Italian macaroni
- 1 pound lean, finely chopped ham
- 1 schoppen sour cream
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 pound grated Parmesan cheese
- butter dough

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 pound Italian macaroni until quite soft.
2. Place in a bowl and mix with 1 pound lean, finely chopped ham, 1 schoppen sour cream, 3 eggs, and 1/4 pound grated Parmesan cheese.
3. Line a melon mold with butter dough.
4. Fill with the mixture, bake in the oven, and then turn out onto a plate.
