## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- snow of 20 egg whites
- 1 lemon, zest grated
- juice of 1 lemon
- 24 loth starch flour
- butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the 20 egg yolks and the snow of 20 egg whites for half an hour
2. When the mass is thick, add the lemon zest that has been grated on a grater, along with its juice
3. Add 24 loth starch flour to the mass
4. Brush a cake pan with butter
5. Fill the entire mixture into the pan
6. Bake the torte in the oven
