## Ingredients

- half a pound butter
- 1 bitter orange
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 4 Loth sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- 2 tablespoons fine flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir half a pound of butter until foamy
2. Grate 1 bitter orange on 1/4 pound of sugar, grind this finely, and cook with the above
3. Then let 4 Loth of sugar in a brass pan cook until it spins threads
4. Cook this again with the above for a quarter hour
5. Beat in 6 egg yolks and add the snow from the 6 egg whites along with 2 tablespoons of fine flour
6. Butter a porcelain dish, fill the soufflé into it and bake it beautifully golden in the oven
