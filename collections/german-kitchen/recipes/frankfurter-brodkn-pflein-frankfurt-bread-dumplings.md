## Ingredients

- 4 rolls
- butter
- onions and parsley
- 6 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls fine flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- bread for braising

## Directions

1. Cut 4 rolls into cubes and roast half of them in butter
2. Steam finely cut onions and parsley also in butter
3. Beat 6 eggs into them, add 2 spoonfuls fine flour, the unroasted roll cubes, salt, and nutmeg
4. Mix everything together
5. Make dumplings the size of a hen's egg from this mixture
6. Let them cook for a quarter hour in salt water
7. Braise them with butter and bread
8. Serve them with game
