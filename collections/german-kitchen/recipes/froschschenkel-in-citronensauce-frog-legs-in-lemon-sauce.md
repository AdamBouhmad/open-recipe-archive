## Ingredients

- Frogs
- Lemon
- Onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the frogs thoroughly and cut off the paws.
2. Draw one foot over the other.
3. Chop half a lemon with its pulp and onions.
4. Put butter in a casserole, add the chopped mixture and frog legs.
5. Cover and braise for a quarter hour.
6. Add as much flour as you can hold between two fingers.
7. Pour in a spoonful of water and salt.
8. Make sure no lumps form.
