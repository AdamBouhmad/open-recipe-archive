## Ingredients

- 2 Wecken (bread rolls)
- 3 Loth almonds
- Rosewater
- 1 Schoppen almond milk
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the bread rolls and press them out firmly
2. Pound the almonds very finely with rosewater
3. Make 1 Schoppen almond milk in the usual way
4. Stir it well with the bread rolls and cook
5. When the soup is served, sprinkle cinnamon on top
