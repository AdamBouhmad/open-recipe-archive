## Ingredients

- boiling water
- black bread, finely cut
- lard
- eggs (as many as persons at table)
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over finely cut black bread that has been roasted golden in lard
2. Bring to a boil
3. Break as many eggs as there are persons at table into the soup
4. Let the soup draw a little, salt it and sprinkle nutmeg on top
