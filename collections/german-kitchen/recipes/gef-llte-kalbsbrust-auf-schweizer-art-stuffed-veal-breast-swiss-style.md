## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Cleaned veal liver (or goose liver)
- One quarter pound beef kidney fat
- 6 shallot onions
- Zest of one lemon
- Basil
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Tarragon
- Butter
- 2 white bread rolls
- 3 to 4 eggs
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Bacon or pork fat
- Onions
- Bay leaf
- Pepper
- Jus

## Directions

1. Remove the breast bones and soak the breast in lukewarm water for an hour until it turns white.
2. Mince the cleaned veal liver and one quarter pound of beef kidney fat as finely as possible and put them in a bowl.
3. Mince 6 shallot onions, the zest of one lemon, basil, thyme, parsley, and tarragon very finely and steam them in a piece of butter.
4. Grate 2 white bread rolls, soak them, and squeeze them out again; mix with 3 to 4 eggs, salt, nutmeg, and the steamed mixture, stir for a quarter hour, add the minced liver and stir again.
5. Fill the breast with the mixture, sew it firmly shut, cook it in boiling water for a quarter hour, place it in a casserole with bacon or pork fat, onions, bay leaf, pepper and salt in the oven, having first tied some bacon pieces on top of the breast.
6. Pour a little jus over it, place the breast on a platter, and pour the sauce (passed through a fine sieve) over it.
