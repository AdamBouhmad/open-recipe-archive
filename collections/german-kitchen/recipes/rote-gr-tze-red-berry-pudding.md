## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1-1/2 lb) currants or other fruit
- 100 g sugar according to taste
- 65 g coarse semolina or ground rice
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Wash and stem the currants and place in a pot with 1/4 l water, covered
2. Prepare cups or bowls by rinsing with water
3. Stir semolina with cold water and let drain
4. Cook the currants for 1/2 hour, then strain through a sieve or cloth and press
5. Mix the strained fruit with sugar and return to the stove
6. The liquid should measure about 1/2 l; bring to a boil and pour in semolina or rice
7. Cook slowly for 10-15 minutes while stirring, pour into moulds or cups and let cool before turning out
