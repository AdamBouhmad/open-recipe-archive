## Ingredients

- quarter pound almonds
- one egg
- quarter pound sugar
- 8 egg yolks gradually added
- half pound melted butter
- one spoonful flour
- some arak
- 8 egg whites snow

## Directions

1. Pound quarter pound almonds with one egg
2. Stir in quarter pound sugar and gradually 8 egg yolks
3. Stir half pound melted butter with one spoonful flour until foamy
4. Add some arak
5. Add the snow from the 8 egg whites
6. Mix with the almonds and sugar
7. Coat a springform with butter
8. Fill with the mass
9. Bake at gentle heat
