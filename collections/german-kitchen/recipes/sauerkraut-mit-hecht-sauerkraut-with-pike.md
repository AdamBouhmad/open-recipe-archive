## Ingredients

- Sauerkraut
- Wine, cognac, or arak
- Pike
- Freshly boiled potatoes
- Half a Vierling of butter
- 1 cooking spoon of flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cook the sauerkraut soft with wine, cognac, or arak, then drain it well in a sieve.
2. If you have leftover pike, use it; otherwise, cook a pike blue, remove the bones and cut it into small pieces.
3. Peel fresh boiled potatoes, cut them into slices, and layer them in a baking pan alternately with sauerkraut and pike, repeating until full.
4. Stir together half a Vierling of butter and 1 cooking spoon of flour with an egg, pour this over the sauerkraut, bake in the oven, and turn it out onto a plate before serving.
