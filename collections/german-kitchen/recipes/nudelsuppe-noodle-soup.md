## Ingredients

- 40 g shaped noodles
- 1 l water
- 1 pinch salt
- 5 g butter
- 6 drops Maggi seasoning

## Directions

1. Rinse the noodles in cold water.
2. Cook the noodles in salted water to which the butter has been added until done and drain.
3. Before serving, add the Maggi seasoning to the soup without cooking again.
