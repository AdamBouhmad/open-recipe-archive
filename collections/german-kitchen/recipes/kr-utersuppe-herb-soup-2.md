## Ingredients

- chervil
- parsley
- sorrel
- garden cress
- chives
- butter
- 1 cooking spoonful of flour
- meat broth
- fried bread rolls
- 3 spoonfuls of sour cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash chervil, parsley, sorrel, garden cress and chives clean and after squeezing out water, mince finely
2. Braise with a piece of butter in a pot for one quarter hour
3. Add one cooking spoonful of flour and pour on meat broth
4. Let everything cook together for another quarter hour
5. Serve over fried bread rolls
6. Stir together 3 spoonfuls of sour cream with 2 egg yolks and add on top
