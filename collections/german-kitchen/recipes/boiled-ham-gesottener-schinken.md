## Ingredients

- Ham
- Cold water
- Various herbs
- Parsley, fresh
- Breadcrumb flour, grated
- Pepper
- Wine (optional)
- Coarse flour and water (for baking variation)

## Directions

1. Wash the ham thoroughly.
2. Lay it overnight in cold water.
3. In the morning, place it in a large pot or kettle over the fire and let it boil, adding all kinds of herbs.
4. When it is half cooked, remove it from the fire and let it sit in the pot until cold.
5. Remove the rind.
6. Weigh a handful of fresh parsley and an equal amount of grated breadcrumb flour, add 2 knife-tips of pepper, mix together, and sprinkle over the ham.
