## Ingredients

- Venison saddle
- Fat
- Roots of various kinds
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Jus
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 4 juniper berries
- Grated bread
- Sour cream
- Lemons

## Directions

1. Clean the saddle carefully and skin it beautifully; cut off the long bones and lard it nicely.
2. Place it in a roasting dish, heat a piece of fat in a pan, pour it over the saddle, add all kinds of roots, pour a glass of wine and a glass of vinegar with some jus, salt, and pepper to taste.
3. Place it in an oven and let it roast until beautifully golden.
4. Take 4 juniper berries and a handful of grated bread; each time the roast is basted, sprinkle some of this bread on it.
5. Finally, pour half a quart of sour cream over the saddle and let it become beautifully golden.
6. When serving, add finely chopped lemon on top, pass the sauce through a fine sieve, and serve it in a separate dish.
