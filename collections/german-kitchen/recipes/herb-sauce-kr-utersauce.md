## Ingredients

- chives
- chervil
- basil
- tarragon
- 2 handfuls dark grated bread
- 6 to 8 pounded juniper berries
- 1 loth sugar
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 1 spoon French mustard
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop chives, chervil, basil and tarragon very finely together
2. Add 2 handfuls of dark grated bread, 6 to 8 pounded juniper berries, 1 loth sugar, finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, 1 spoon French mustard, salt and pepper
