## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- flour
- leftover veal roast
- white bread
- spinach
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Take 3 eggs and as much flour as they can take and work them together
2. Roll it out very nicely
3. For the filling: take leftover veal roast and pound it as fine as possible
4. Cut the crust off white bread (worth 2 kreuzer) and soak it in water
5. Scald a handful of cleanly washed spinach and pound it as fine as possible
6. Take it together with the meat and also the yolk of 3 hard-boiled eggs
7. Let a piece of butter melt in a casserole, put everything together in the casserole, press the bread out firmly and take it too, add 2 whole eggs, salt and nutmeg
8. Cook everything together on the fire for 6 minutes
9. Spread it on the cakes very thinly, roll them together but not thicker than a quill, cut them into finger-length pieces (they must look just like Italian macaroni)
10. Simmer them in meat broth, pour the broth into a soup bowl, add the macaroni to it and serve it at table
