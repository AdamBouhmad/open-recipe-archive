## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salt water
- butter
- water
- bread
- milk
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- 3 eggs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Wash the crayfish and kill them by removing the middle of the 5 tail fins and the attached thread
2. Boil them in salted water
3. Break out the tail and remove the shells
4. Take out the gall from the top of the body
5. Pound the remainder with half a pound of butter in a mortar
6. Steam the pounded mixture in a casserole until it foams
7. Pour water on it and cook for a quarter hour
8. Pour the butter onto cold water and when firm, beat it foamy in a bowl
9. Grate 2 pieces of bread at the grater, soak in milk, press out firmly, and add to the butter
10. Add salt and nutmeg flower
11. Mince the tails and claws finely, add to the mass, and bind with 3 eggs
12. Make round cakes from this dough and fry them in lard
