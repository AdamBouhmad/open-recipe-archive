## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- eggs
- lard
- bratwurst sausages

## Directions

1. Make a batter from flour, milk, and eggs
2. Pour 2 spoonfuls of hot lard into it
3. Put the bratwurst sausages in hot water
4. Cut them into finger-long pieces
5. Mix them into the batter
6. Fry the batter in lard until golden
