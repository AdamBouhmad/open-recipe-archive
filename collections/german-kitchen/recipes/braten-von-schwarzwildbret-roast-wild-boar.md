## Ingredients

- leg of young wild boar
- wine
- vinegar
- roots and herbs
- coarsely crushed spices
- bacon
- capers
- smoked herring
- onion
- lemon
- egg whites
- cinnamon
- mace

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the leg of young wild boar and cut the lower bone so that the hooves and skin remain attached.
2. Place it in a mixture of equal parts wine and vinegar with all sorts of roots, herbs, and coarsely crushed spices, turning it often.
3. On the day it is to be served, remove it from the marinade.
4. Make holes in the bottom with a knife and insert a piece of bacon into each hole.
5. Roast it in a casserole in the oven until beautifully golden, along with the marinade.
6. Finely chop one tablespoon of capers, one smoked herring, one onion, and the zest of half a lemon.
7. Mix together two egg whites, a knife-point of cinnamon, the same amount of mace, and the juice of half a lemon in a bowl.
8. When the leg is tender, spread this mixture on top of it to the thickness of a knife-back.
9. Roast it in the oven until golden, basting it 2 to 3 times with the fat.
10. Arrange the roast on a plate, garnish with lemons, place the shank with hooves on top, and serve the jus strained through a fine sieve separately.
