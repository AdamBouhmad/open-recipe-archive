## Ingredients

- coffee (quarter pound)
- milk (3 schoppen)
- sugar (8 loth)
- veal stock (1 schoppen, thickly cooked)
- cream (1 schoppen)
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Roast a quarter pound coffee light yellow
2. Crush it in a mortar
3. Let it cook in a brass pan with 3 schoppen milk
4. Let 8 loth sugar and one schoppen thickly cooked veal stock make a decoction with the coffee
5. Strain everything through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl
6. Let it cool
7. Whip one schoppen cream stiff in a bowl
8. When the cream is cold, slowly mix it into the whipped cream
9. Pour this mass into an aspic mold brushed with almond oil
10. Let it stand on ice
11. Garnish to taste
