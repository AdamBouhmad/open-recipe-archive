## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Whip egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Stir in grated chocolate
3. Make sleeves from white paper and place on a black baking sheet
4. Fill with the mixture
5. Dry in the oven for 4 minutes
