## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the ribs or the so-called baron
- Various herbs and roots
- 1 glass of wine
- Half a glass of vinegar
- Half a tablespoon of meat broth or water
- Meat broth for finishing
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef from the ribs or the so-called baron
2. Remove the bones nicely
3. Place the meat in a casserole in which a piece of fat, whatever fat it may be, has melted
4. Add various herbs and roots
5. Pour 1 glass of wine, half a glass of vinegar, and half a tablespoon of meat broth or water over it
6. Let it cook slowly over weak coals
7. Turn it often during cooking
8. When it is done, remove the fat
9. Pour another tablespoon of meat broth over it
10. Pass the broth through a fine sieve
11. Let it come to a boil once more in the casserole
12. Add lemon when arranging
