## Ingredients

- raw butter dough
- raw potatoes
- preserved currants, raspberries, or cherries
- 1/4 pound sugar
- zest of 2 lemons
- 1 tablespoon flour
- sweet milk
- 1 schoppen milk
- 6-7 egg yolks
- snow of 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Form a torte of raw butter dough
2. Lay raw potatoes on it
3. Bake it so that it does not shrink
4. When it has cooled, brush it with preserved currants, raspberries, or cherries
5. For the cream: grate 1/4 pound sugar with 2 lemons onto paper and dry it
6. Stir 1 tablespoon flour smooth in a pan with sweet milk
7. Pour in another schoppen of milk
8. Add 6-7 egg yolks to it
9. Cook this like a porridge and stir until it is cold
10. Carefully spread it on the preserved fruit
11. Beat the snow stiffly from 6 egg whites
12. Mix the dried lemon sugar with it
13. Put the snow on the cream
14. Place the torte in the oven at low heat
