## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish
- 4 tablespoons broth
- 15 g margarine
- 10 g onions
- 10 g flour
- 1/4 l broth
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Weigh the onions. Peel and grate the horseradish quickly.
2. Let the horseradish steep slowly with 4 tablespoons of broth in an earthenware pot. Make a smooth sauce from the fat, onions, flour, and broth.
3. Mix the horseradish into the sauce and season with salt. If it is very sharp, add 2 tablespoons of milk.
4. Note: Grated horseradish must not be allowed to dry out; it should be mixed with a little milk or vinegar so it does not turn black.
