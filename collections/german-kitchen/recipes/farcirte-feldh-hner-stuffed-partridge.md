## Ingredients

- 2 partridges
- 1/4 pound bacon
- onions
- lemons
- truffles
- salt
- mace
- 1 egg
- white bread (for 1 kreuzer), grated
- milk
- bacon for binding
- fat for braising
- juniper berries
- yellow turnips

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe one partridge, cut off the head (unplucked), remove the crop, split the back, and remove the breast so all meat remains attached to the skin.
2. Carefully remove all bones from the body (keeping only the leg bone).
3. Cut the feet at the second joint.
4. From a second partridge, remove all meat and mince finely with 1/4 pound bacon, adding onions and lemons.
5. Pound in a mortar or on a rubbing stone until very fine.
6. Add truffles, salt, and mace.
7. Mix in a bowl with 1 egg.
8. Add grated white bread (for 1 kreuzer) that has been soaked in milk and firmly pressed out, and mix well again.
9. Fill the first bird with this mixture.
10. Sew the back closed.
11. Bind with bacon.
12. In a casserole with a piece of fat, onions, juniper berries, and yellow turnips, braise slowly and beautifully.
13. Serve hot or cold, best with mayonnaise sauce.
