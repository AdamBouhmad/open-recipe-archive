## Ingredients

- eel
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Skin the eel and remove the hair, head, and tail
2. Remove the gallbladder
3. Cut the eel into pieces two fingers wide
4. Rub with pepper and salt
5. Wrap each piece with sage and lemon slices
6. Fry in a frying pan with a piece of butter
7. Before serving, remove the thread that bound the sage and lemon, and arrange lemon slices alongside
