## Ingredients

- good Gruber potatoes
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 3/4 pound potatoes
- 7 whole eggs
- 7 egg yolks
- 2 loth blanched, finely pounded bitter almonds
- 1 lemon, grated on a grater
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook good Gruber potatoes in steam the day before or roast them in the oven
2. Grate them (for 1/2 pound sugar, use 3/4 pound potatoes)
3. Stir them with the sugar, 7 whole eggs, and 7 egg yolks for half an hour
4. Add 2 loth blanched, finely pounded bitter almonds, a lemon grated on a grater, and the juice of 1/2 lemon
5. Brush a metal form with butter, dust with sugar, and bake the torte in it
