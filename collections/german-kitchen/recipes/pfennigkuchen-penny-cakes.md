## Ingredients

- 125 g Sugar
- 125 g Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 pinch Cardamom
- 1 Piece of bacon rind

## Directions

1. Line a baking sheet with paper and then grease it with bacon rind. Break the eggs into a bowl.
2. Whip the eggs in the bowl with the finely sifted sugar for about 1/4 hour until foamy, then add the flour and cardamom and stir well with the foamy egg.
3. Using a teaspoon, drop walnut-sized portions onto the baking sheet and let the cakes stand outside the oven for 5 minutes so a thin skin forms on the surface.
4. Place the cakes in a not-too-hot oven and bake them in 10-15 minutes to a nice light brown color.
5. You can also use anise, vanilla sugar, or lemon sugar instead of cardamom as the spice.
