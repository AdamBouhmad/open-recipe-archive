## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 9 tablespoons salt
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs in boiling water with the salt for 10 minutes
2. Crack the tips of the eggshells
3. Place the eggs in cooled salt water where they will keep for a long time
4. After 2-3 days they can be used
5. Optional: place the pickled eggs in cooled ham or curing brine for 1 to 3 days in winter
