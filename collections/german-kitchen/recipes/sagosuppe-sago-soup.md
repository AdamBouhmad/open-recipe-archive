## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound sago
- Clear meat broth
- Two egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of sweet cream
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pour clear meat broth into a casserole and slowly let the sago run into it
2. Cover and let cook slowly, stirring often
3. Pour in as much meat broth as needed for the soup
4. Beat two egg yolks into the soup bowl and add two tablespoons of sweet cream
5. Arrange the sago over it and sprinkle nutmeg on top. 1/4 pound of sago is sufficient for 8 people
