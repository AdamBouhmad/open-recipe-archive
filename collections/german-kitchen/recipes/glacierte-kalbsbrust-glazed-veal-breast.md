## Ingredients

- Veal breast (beautiful, fat, white)
- Pork fat
- Onions (chopped)
- Bay leaves
- Lemons
- Yellow turnips
- Meat broth or water (half a ladle)
- Flour (1 cooking spoon)
- Tarragon or raspberry vinegar (half a glass)
- Wine (1 glass)
- Jus (1 ladle)
- Lemon (yellow part and juice)
- Nutmeg (grated)
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Bone the veal breast and cut it in two parts so they become nicely round.
2. Place them in a casserole with boiling water and let it come to a boil.
3. Place them in cold water, lard them well and set them in a casserole with a piece of pork fat, add chopped onions, bay leaves, lemons and yellow turnips.
4. Pour in half a ladle of meat broth or water and let them steam for an hour.
5. If the meat does not have enough color, place it in a roasting pan and put it in the oven.
6. When the sauce has set, stir in one cooking spoon of flour, pour in half a glass of tarragon or raspberry vinegar, a glass of wine and one ladle of jus, let it cook for half an hour.
7. Press it through a hair sieve, add the meat back and season it with the minced yellow part and juice of a lemon, grated nutmeg, and salt and pepper to taste.
