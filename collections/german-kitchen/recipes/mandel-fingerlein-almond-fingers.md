## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound ground almonds
- 1/2 glass wine
- cinnamon and cloves to taste
- zest of 1 lemon
- some bitter orange peel
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients into a smooth dough
2. Form into small finger-shaped pieces
3. Fry in hot lard until golden
