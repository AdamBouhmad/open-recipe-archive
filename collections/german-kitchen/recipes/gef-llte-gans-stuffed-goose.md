## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 2 rolls (Wecken)
- water
- goose liver, heart, and gizzard
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/4 pound bacon, finely sliced
- parsley
- 1 lemon, minced

## Directions

1. Pluck, wash, and clean out the goose.
2. Soak 2 rolls in water and press out.
3. Mince the liver, heart, and gizzard finely.
4. Mix with salt, pepper, nutmeg, 2 eggs, 1/4 pound finely sliced bacon, parsley, and 1 minced lemon.
5. Fill the mixture into the goose.
6. Rub with pepper and salt.
7. Braise until golden.
