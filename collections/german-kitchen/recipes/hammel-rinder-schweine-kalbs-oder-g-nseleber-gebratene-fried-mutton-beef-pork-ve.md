## Ingredients

- 200 g mutton or beef liver
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 tablespoons grated breadcrumbs
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 40 g fat or margarine
- 5 g salt and pepper
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 4 tablespoons water or broth

## Directions

1. Wash the liver, skin it, and cut diagonally into 4-5 slices. Mix breadcrumbs and flour together (breading). Melt the fat for frying.
2. Salt and pepper the sliced liver, coat in breading, and fry in steaming fat, turning four times, for 4-6 minutes until a beautiful brown color. Lay the slices on a dish side by side, not on top of each other.
3. Add 1 teaspoon flour to the fat in the pan, stir until cooked, pour in several spoonfuls of water or broth and 1/2 tablespoon vinegar, taste and adjust seasoning with salt, and serve the sauce with the liver.
