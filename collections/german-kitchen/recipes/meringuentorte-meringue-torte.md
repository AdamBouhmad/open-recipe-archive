## Ingredients

- 12 egg whites
- 1 pound finely ground and sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 12 egg whites into stiff dry peaks
2. Mix with 1 pound finely ground and sifted sugar
3. Fill part of the mixture into a piping bag with a finger-thick nozzle
4. Pipe 4 to 6 columns onto a very clean board
5. Let them set in the oven at gentle heat
6. Form the remaining mixture into a round the size and shape of a plate
7. Place it around the baked columns so they protrude above it
8. Bake the whole thing in the oven until beautifully and thoroughly baked
