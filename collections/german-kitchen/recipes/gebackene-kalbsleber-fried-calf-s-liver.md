## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- flour
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from a calf's liver
2. Cut out the veins
3. Cut the liver into slices
4. Turn them in flour
5. Fry in butter
6. Season with salt only just before serving
