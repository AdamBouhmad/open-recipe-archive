## Ingredients

- A good piece of beef
- Butter
- Yellow zest of 1 lemon
- Marrow of 1 lemon
- A handful of black bread
- A handful of chervil
- Parsley
- Celery
- 1 Schoppen red wine
- 1 tablespoon of jus or meat broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut a good piece of beef into finger-thick pieces
2. Pound them slightly
3. Place them with a piece of butter in a casserole
4. Finely mince the yellow zest and marrow of 1 lemon, leaving away only the white skin
5. Grate a handful of black bread
6. Mince finely a handful of chervil with parsley and celery
7. Mix this with the grated bread
8. Add it to the meat
9. Pour 1 Schoppen red wine and 1 tablespoon of jus or meat broth over it
10. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg, but very little
11. Set it to the fire and let it cook for one hour
12. After arranging, pour the sauce over it
13. Note: all meat prepared in this or similar manner is more flavorful if you do not cook it too long and do not salt it heavily
