## Ingredients

- Capon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- Butter
- Paper

## Directions

1. Dry pluck the capon and, if time permits, it can remain undrawn until the next day.
2. Before roasting, draw it, wash it clean, rub it well with salt and pepper.
3. Stuff an onion inside, cut off the neck and head but leave the skin on the body.
4. Spread a sheet of paper with butter, wrap the capon in it and roast it in the oven until golden.
5. The feet must remain on, only the claws are cut off.
