## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1 pound blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 16 egg yolks
- 16 egg whites
- 1 whole lemon, grated on a grater
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- a handful of potato flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pound finely sifted sugar, 1 pound blanched, finely pounded almonds, 16 egg yolks until thick
2. Add the snow from 16 egg whites (this is added only after the mixture is already thick)
3. Add a whole lemon grated on a grater, the juice of 1/2 lemon, and a handful of potato flour
4. When the mixture is together, brush a metal form with butter and bake the torte in it in the oven
