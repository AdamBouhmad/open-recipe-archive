## Ingredients

- one handful of fresh, cleaned parsley
- half a stirring spoonful of flour
- a piece of butter
- good meat broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop the parsley very finely
2. Knead the flour into a piece of butter
3. Mix the parsley into the butter mixture
4. Stir everything over the fire with good meat broth
5. Let it cook a little and finish with egg yolks before serving
6. This sauce is useful for many meat dishes
