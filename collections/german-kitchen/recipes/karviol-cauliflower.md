## Ingredients

- cauliflower florets
- salt water
- flour
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the florets from the stem, peel them and cook them soft in salted water
2. For the sauce: stir three spoonfuls of flour smooth in a casserole with cold water
3. Add three eggs and a small piece of butter, salt and nutmeg
4. Pour this sauce over the cauliflower
