## Ingredients

- kidneys
- onions
- butter
- flour
- half a glass of Burgundy wine
- meat broth
- zest and juice of half a lemon
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys very finely
2. Cut a good amount of onions
3. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan with the onions and kidneys
4. Cover the saucepan and let it steam for half a quarter hour
5. Sprinkle 3 strong finger-fulls of flour over it, turn it a couple of times
6. Pour in half a glass of Burgundy wine and some meat broth
7. Add the finely chopped zest and juice of half a lemon, salt, and nutmeg
8. Let it cook together once more
