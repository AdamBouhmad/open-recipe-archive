## Ingredients

- 1 calf's liver
- 1 large handful onions
- fat
- 3 eggs
- 2 rolls
- butter
- onions for braising

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the calf's liver, cut out the veins, and chop as finely as possible
2. Steam 1 large handful onions in a piece of fat
3. Take 3 eggs and 2 rolls to the liver
4. Stir this all together for half an hour
5. Cook dumplings made from the mixture in salt water
6. Braise them with butter and onions
