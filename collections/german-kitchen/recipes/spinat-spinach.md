## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 pounds) spinach
- 8 g salt
- 2 l boiling water
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 15 g fat or margarine
- 5 g grated onions
- 1/8 l broth
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the bottom of the leaf stems; sort the spinach, wash it often, blanch it several times in boiling salted water, skim it, drain it, cool it with fresh water, drain it well, press it gently on a colander, and pass it through a fine sieve or weigh it.
2. Sauté the onions in fat, then cook the flour with it, but do not brown it.
3. Stir the spinach puree into it and add the broth.
