## Ingredients

- eggs
- a roll of bread (Wecken)
- milk
- butter
- one whole egg
- soft bread
- sugar
- anchovies
- ham
- sweet cream
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, peel them, and place them in cold water.
2. Cut the crust from a roll and soak it in milk, then press it out.
3. Let a piece of butter melt in a casserole and braise the roll and egg yolks in it for a quarter hour.
4. Beat in one whole egg and fill the egg whites with this mixture.
5. Cut soft bread into slices, pour a little milk on them so they become soft.
6. Butter a baking sheet, sprinkle it with sugar, and lay the soft bread slices on it.
7. Place the eggs on the bread slices.
8. Cut 6 cleaned and deboned anchovies and some ham into small pieces and lay them around the eggs.
9. Beat 3 whole eggs with one pint of sweet cream, salt it, grate in 4 lots of Parmesan cheese, stir everything together, and pour it over the eggs.
10. Place the baking sheet in a baking oven and let them rise nicely.
