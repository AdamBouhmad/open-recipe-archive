## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg potatoes
- 1/8 l hot salt water
- 2 tablespoons oil or 25 g lard or goose fat
- 10 g onions
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 3 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Grate the onion and mince the parsley. Cook the potatoes, peel them and cut into slices.
2. Always dress the potato slices while lukewarm; for this purpose first pour hot salt water and vinegar over them and let rest covered on a warm spot for a while. Then mix them with oil and vinegar, salt, pepper, onion and parsley, which must be done carefully, and season well to taste.
