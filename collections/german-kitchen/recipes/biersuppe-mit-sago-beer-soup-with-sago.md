## Ingredients

- 1 bottle light beer
- 65 g sugar
- 1 piece lemon peel
- 3 cloves
- 1 pinch salt
- 30 g sago

## Directions

1. Bring the beer to a boil with sugar and spices, sprinkle in the sago and cook for 15-25 minutes in a covered pot depending on the grain firmness; season with salt and remove the spices.
