## Ingredients

- beautiful white cutlets
- lemon zest
- onion
- parsley
- pork forcemeat
- 2 egg whites
- salt
- bacon
- butter
- parchment paper

## Directions

1. Pound beautiful white cutlets well, no thicker than a knife, and shape them somewhat lengthily
2. Chop the zest of half a lemon finely with some onion and parsley
3. Mix this finely with pork forcemeat, combine with 2 egg whites, and salt to taste
4. Spread this mixture on the cutlets above and below, knife-back thick
5. Cut a piece of bacon as thin as possible and lay it above and below the cutlet
6. Brush a sheet of parchment paper with butter, wrap the cutlet in it so the bone sticks out at the top
7. Cut off the remaining paper, seal it tightly with your hand so nothing can leak out
8. Place on a grate and turn it; the cutlets are done in a quarter hour
9. Serve them with the paper on the table
