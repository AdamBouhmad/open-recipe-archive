## Ingredients

- 1 hazelhen
- wine
- pepper
- salt
- 1 onion
- lemon
- basil
- bacon
- goose liver
- onions
- bay leaves
- celery
- yellow turnips

## Directions

1. It is always better if the hazelhen, after being plucked dry, soaks in wine for a time
2. Cut off the head and neck
3. Rub the hen well with pepper and salt
4. Stuff the hen with an onion, some lemon, and basil
5. Dress it nicely, bind it thickly with bacon, and roast it until golden yellow in the oven or on the spit
6. The claws must be cut off
7. Put onions, bay leaves, celery, and yellow turnips into the casserole with the roast
8. Pound together goose liver and a little lemon, add them as well
9. Remove the fat from the sauce, press it through a fine sieve, and pour it with a little jus over the roast
