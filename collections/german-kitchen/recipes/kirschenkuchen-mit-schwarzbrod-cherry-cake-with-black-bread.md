## Ingredients

- 16 loths grated black bread
- 1 glass good, old wine
- 1 whole nutmeg
- 1 loth ground cinnamon
- 1 quint ground cloves
- 2 knife tips ground mace
- 1 lemon peel
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound ground almonds
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 1 pound black pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Moisten 16 loths grated black bread with 1 glass good, old wine
2. Grate 1 whole nutmeg over it
3. Add 1 loth ground cinnamon, 1 quint ground cloves, 2 knife tips ground mace
4. Add the finely chopped peel of 1 lemon
5. Add 1/2 pound sugar and 1/2 pound ground almonds
6. Add 12 egg yolks and stir everything together for half an hour
7. Mix in the snow from 6 egg whites last
8. Add 1 pound black pitted cherries
9. Bake in a pan coated with butter in the oven
