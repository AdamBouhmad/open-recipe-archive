## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg fish
- 40 g fat
- 1 medium onion
- 2 tablespoons soup greens
- 10 g salt
- 40 g flour
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 pinch pepper
- 3/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, wash it, cut it, clean it and salt it
2. Cut the soup greens
3. Cook the soup greens covered in 3/4 liter salt water until soft
4. Add the fish pieces and cook them until done in a few minutes
5. In a small pot, cook the finely chopped onion in fat until done but not brown
6. Add the flour and thin with a few spoonfuls of fish water
7. When the flour mixture bubbles, add it to the fish dish
8. Add pepper and chopped parsley and arrange, after everything has cooked into an even slimy consistency, the fish with sauce
