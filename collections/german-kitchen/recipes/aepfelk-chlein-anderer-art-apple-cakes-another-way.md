## Ingredients

- flour
- lukewarm wheat beer
- 5 tablespoons boiling hot lard
- good baking apples
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a thin dough (Flädchenteig) from flour and lukewarm wheat beer in a bowl
2. Stir in 5 tablespoons boiling hot lard, one after another
3. Peel slices of good baking apples and remove the core
4. Mix the apple slices into the dough
5. Fry slowly in hot lard with frequent shaking of the pan
6. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over them
