## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- Parmesan cheese
- 3 spoonfuls sour cream
- 2 knife points salt
- 2 spoonfuls beer yeast
- 2 spoonfuls cherry spirits

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 1 pound flour, Parmesan cheese, 3 spoonfuls sour cream, 2 knife points salt, 2 spoonfuls beer yeast, and 2 spoonfuls cherry spirits
2. Work it for half an hour
3. Let it rise in warmth
4. Make small cakes with a spoon and bake them in hot lard
