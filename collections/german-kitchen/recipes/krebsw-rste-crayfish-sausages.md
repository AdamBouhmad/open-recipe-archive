## Ingredients

- Small cooked crayfish
- Crayfish shells
- Half a loaf of bread without crust
- Sweet cream
- Calf sweetbread
- 2 calf udders
- Basil
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Small onion
- Egg yolks
- Pepper
- Salt
- Sausage casings
- Milk

## Directions

1. Remove the tails from the boiled crayfish and make crayfish butter from the shells
2. Cut half a loaf of bread into small pieces and soak it in sweet cream for one hour
3. Blanch the crayfish tails, calf sweetbread, and calf udders finely together and add to the soaked bread
4. Add a little basil, thyme, marjoram, and a small finely chopped onion that has been cooked in crayfish butter
5. When the crayfish butter is completely cold, add some egg yolks, a little pepper, and salt
6. Mix everything well together
7. Fill the casings using a sausage maker
8. Slowly bring the sausages to a boil in hot milk in a pot
9. Half an hour before serving, brush paper with crayfish butter and fry the sausages on a grate
