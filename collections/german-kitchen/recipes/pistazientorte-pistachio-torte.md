## Ingredients

- 8 loth pistachios
- 12 loth blanched and finely ground almonds
- 1/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 16 egg yolks
- 4 loth citron
- 4 loth orange peel
- zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 8 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak 8 loth pistachios in cold water overnight
2. Beat together 12 loth blanched and finely ground almonds and 1/4 pound finely ground sugar with 16 egg yolks and stir together for half an hour
3. Peel the pistachios and chop them on a chopping board
4. Chop 4 loth citron and 4 loth orange peel together, not very finely
5. Finely chop the zest of 1 lemon and add its juice
6. Add the pistachios to this mixture and stir for a while longer
7. Beat the whites from 8 eggs into stiff peaks and slowly fold into the mixture
8. Butter a tin form and bake the mixture in the oven
9. Bake for one hour
