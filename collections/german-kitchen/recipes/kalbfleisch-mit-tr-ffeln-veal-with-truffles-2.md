## Ingredients

- Beautiful piece of veal from the hind leg
- Onions
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Pound a nice piece of veal from the hind leg well, lard it, and place it in a casserole.
2. Chop onions and lemon very finely and add to the casserole, cover it, and let it steam for half an hour.
