## Ingredients

- 1 carp
- good broth
- salt
- pepper
- 1 half-boiled egg, yolk and white separated
- hot butter
- 3 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a good broth for the carp
2. Scale the carp, cut out the ears (gills), remove the entrails
3. Rub with salt and pepper
4. Place in the broth
5. Cook thoroughly for half an hour
6. Place on a serving plate
7. Pound 1 whole half-boiled egg, keeping yolk and white separate
8. Lay this in strips over the fish
9. Pour hot butter and 3 tablespoons vinegar over it
