## Ingredients

- butter
- 1 cooking spoonful of flour
- cold water
- 1 measure of wine
- 3 lots of sugar
- cinnamon stick
- zest of 1 lemon
- roasted bread pieces

## Directions

1. Let a piece of butter melt in a pot
2. Add 1 cooking spoonful of flour and let it brown
3. Deglaze with cold water
4. Pour in one measure of wine
5. Add 3 lots of sugar, a piece of cinnamon and the zest of a lemon
6. Let it all come to a boil together
7. Serve over roasted bread pieces
