## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 half- or three-quarter pound pike
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onions
- lemons
- calf sweetbread
- 8 large boiled crayfish
- quarter pound of butter

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare 2 to 3 pike weighing half to three-quarters of a pound each
2. Cut them into finger-wide pieces and rub them well with salt and pepper
3. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan with the fish, some onions and lemons, cover and let it steam for half an hour
4. Meanwhile, take the throat from a piece of calf sweetbread, blanch it slightly, cut it into pieces and add it to the fish
5. From 8 large boiled crayfish, take the tails and claws, clean the bodies carefully so that the upper shell remains intact
6. Pound the remainder together with a quarter pound of butter
7. Put this in a saucepan, steam it until it foams
8. Pour it through a hair sieve
