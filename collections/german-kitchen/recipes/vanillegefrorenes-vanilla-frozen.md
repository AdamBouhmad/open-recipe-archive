## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 Schoppen milk
- 1 piece vanilla
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a brass pan, let 1-1/2 Schoppen milk with a piece of vanilla cook as long as hard-boiled eggs
2. Beat 12 egg yolks in a pot
3. Stir the boiling milk to them
4. Let it draw once more in the pan
5. Force it through a hair sieve
6. Let it cool
7. Place it in the freezing bucket and work it according to the rule given in No. 767
