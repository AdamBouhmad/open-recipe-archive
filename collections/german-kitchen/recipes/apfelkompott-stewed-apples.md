## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg apples
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples cleanly and remove the cores.
2. Stew them soft and clear in the sugar water, arrange them, cook the juice down a bit more and pour it over the apples.
