## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 8 loth black bread
- 1 glass red wine
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 knife point of cinnamon
- 1 knife point of mace
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 4 loth citron peel and orange peel, finely chopped
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter in a bowl until foamy and gradually stir in 6 egg yolks.
2. Grate 8 loth black bread on a grater and moisten it with 1 glass of red wine.
3. Add a grated nutmeg, a knife point of cinnamon, the same amount of mace, 1/4 pound sugar with a lemon zest grated into it, 4 loth citron peel and orange peel finely chopped.
4. Beat the egg whites to a stiff snow and add to the mixture.
5. Mix the mass well together and bake the soufflé in a porcelain bowl brushed with butter in the oven until nicely golden.
