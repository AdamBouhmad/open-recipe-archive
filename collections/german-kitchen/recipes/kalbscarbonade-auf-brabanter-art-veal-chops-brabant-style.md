## Ingredients

- butter
- veal chop
- flour
- salt
- onion
- bay leaf
- thyme
- wine
- jus
- lemon

## Directions

1. Make a piece of butter golden in a casserole
2. Turn the chop in flour and lay it in the casserole with salt, an onion, bay leaf, and thyme
3. Fry golden on both sides
4. Pour a small glass of good wine and some jus over it
5. Let it cook together for a while
6. Before serving, squeeze the juice of half a lemon over the chop
