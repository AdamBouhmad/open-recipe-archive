## Ingredients

- 1 goose of 4 kg
- 1 kg apples
- 40 g sugar
- 30 g salt
- 1 bunch mugwort
- 30 g onions
- spice mixture
- 8 g potato starch
- 1 l water

## Directions

1. Prepare the goose according to instructions; remove wings, neck, head and feet.
2. Carefully separate the intestinal fat from the other fat and soak both separately.
3. After the goose is washed and dried, rub it with salt.
4. Peel or leave unpeeled the apples as desired, cut them or if small leave them whole, toss with sugar and salt and fill the goose with them along with the rinsed mugwort; sew it closed and rub salt on the outside.
5. Keep the oven not too hot in the first hour, pour 1/2 l boiling water over the goose; add onions and spice mixture.
6. Baste the goose with water for 1 hour in the oven. In the first hour you should notice neither color nor roasting aroma; if the oven temperature rises, cover the goose with parchment paper.
7. After about 1 hour, the goose will have rendered enough of its own fat to baste with; fill with spoonfuls of water from time to time so the fat doesn't brown.
8. After 1-1/2 hours, occasionally skim fat from the sauce and roast the goose to a nice brown color.
9. When tender, sprinkle it with 1 spoonful of cold water so the skin becomes crispy, arrange the goose and cook the sauce in the pan with some water and potato starch stirred in water to sufficient consistency, and can also color with food coloring.
10. Pour the sauce through a fine sieve.
