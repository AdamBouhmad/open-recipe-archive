## Ingredients

- 1 old hen's breast
- Remaining body of the hen, chopped
- Some veal
- Parsnip
- Celery
- Carrots
- Leek
- Onions
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth
- 1 egg
- Nutmeg
- Chives

## Directions

1. Cut the breast from an old hen and set aside
2. Chop the remaining body and combine with some veal, and various root vegetables: parsnip, celery, carrots, leeks, and onions
3. Steam in 1/8 pound of butter in a covered casserole for 1 hour
4. Sprinkle a small handful of flour on top, turn, and pour meat broth over it
5. Let the whole thing cook for another half hour
6. Finely chop the hen's breast, pound it with an egg and some butter in a mortar, and form small dumplings
7. Cook the dumplings for 1/8 hour
8. Strain the glaze through a hair sieve and add the dumplings, some nutmeg, and chives
