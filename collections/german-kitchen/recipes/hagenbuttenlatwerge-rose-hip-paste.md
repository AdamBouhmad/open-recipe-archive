## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 Maas of rose hips
- 20 Maas water per 10 Maas rose hips

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds and stems from 10 to 12 Maas of rose hips
2. Place them together in a cleaned pot with 20 Maas of water (for 10 Maas rose hips)
3. Let them cook together for 2 to 3 hours, always adding more water as needed
4. Remove the rose hips and press them well
5. Let the juice simmer down (20 Maas of water must reduce to one)
6. Skim it frequently
7. Store it in a porcelain container
8. It is very good for coughs
