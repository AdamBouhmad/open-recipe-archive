## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 40 g sugar
- 1/8 l apple wine, white wine or malt wine
- 1 teaspoon lemon sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stir eggs with sugar cold, add wine, lemon sugar and lemon juice, place in water bath and beat until thick. To increase the amount of sauce, one can also cook 10 g flour separately with 1/8 l wine and stir into the above mixture. The sauce is to be served warm or cold.
2. Remarks: Instead of lemon, orange or both mixed can be used. If 3 tablets of dissolved gelatin are added and the mixture is then poured into a bowl, when cooled you have wine foam dessert.
