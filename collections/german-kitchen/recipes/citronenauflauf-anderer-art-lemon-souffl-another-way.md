## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 8 loth butter
- 8 loth ground sugar
- 2 lemons
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites
- butter for the dish
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Cook a thick porridge from 1/2 maas milk and put into a bowl.
2. Add 8 loth butter, 8 loth ground sugar, 2 lemons grated on a grater, and the pith cut into strips.
3. Stir in 5 egg yolks and stir everything well for 15 minutes.
4. Beat the 5 egg whites to a stiff snow and mix with the porridge.
5. Brush the bowl that you will serve with butter, pour the mass onto it, let the soufflé bake nicely golden, and sprinkle sugar on top.
