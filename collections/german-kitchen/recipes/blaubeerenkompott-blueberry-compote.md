## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg blueberries
- 50 g sugar
- 1 piece orange peel
- 2 g starch flour
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Sort the blueberries, wash them and toss them with sugar in an earthen pot. Dissolve the starch flour.
2. Place the prepared blueberries on the stove in the earthen pot and simmer slowly with the orange peel for 1/4 hour. Then stir in the starch flour dissolved in water while stirring, let everything boil and arrange the dish.
