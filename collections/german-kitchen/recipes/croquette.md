## Ingredients

- veal
- onions
- lemons
- fat
- a penny roll
- water
- 2 to 3 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- a roll
- lard

## Directions

1. Weigh veal with onions and lemons, braise it in a piece of fat
2. Soak a penny roll in water, press it out firmly, and add it to the above
3. Braise everything together for another quarter hour
4. Add 2 to 3 eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste
5. Cut a roll (with the bottom crust removed) into pieces
6. Spread the mass onto the pieces and fry them golden yellow in hot lard
