## Ingredients

- lake trout
- Burgundy wine
- lesser wine
- vinegar
- lemon
- cloves
- pepper
- bay leaves
- herbs
- flour
- water
- egg yolks
- butter
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Scale the fish thoroughly and remove the gills and innards
2. Rub strongly with salt
3. Place in a fish kettle
4. Pour 1 bottle of Burgundy, 1 bottle of lesser wine, and vinegar over it
5. Add lemon, cloves, pepper, bay leaves, and all kinds of good herbs
6. Cover the kettle tightly and let the fish simmer for 1 hour
7. Place on a long platter
8. For the Dutch sauce: mix 1 cooking spoon of flour smoothly with cold water
9. Beat in 3 egg yolks, butter the size of a chicken egg, and some of the fish stock
10. Cook this sauce well with meat broth and pour over the fish or serve separately
