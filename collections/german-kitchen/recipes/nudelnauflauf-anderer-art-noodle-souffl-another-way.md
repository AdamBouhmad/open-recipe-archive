## Ingredients

- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- butter
- noodle dough
- milk
- 6 peeled good apples
- 4 Loth raisins
- 4 Loth ground sugar
- butter
- 3 eggs
- half a Schoppen sour cream
- half a Schoppen sweet milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from one whole egg, one yolk, and butter the size of a hen's egg
2. Roll out the cakes as finely as possible but do not cut them very finely
3. Cook them in milk so they are moist
4. Cut 6 peeled good apples into small pieces
5. Take 4 Loth of raisins and 4 Loth of ground sugar
6. Butter a dish and lay noodles in the bottom, then cut apples and raisins, noodles, apples and raisins, and continue until everything is used
7. Beat 3 eggs in a dish, add half a Schoppen of sour cream and half a Schoppen of sweet milk
8. Stir it together, pour it over the noodles
9. Bake them and sprinkle sugar on top
