## Ingredients

- 125 g dried fruit
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 1 piece orange peel
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 g potato flour
- 1 tablespoon water
- 3/8 liter water

## Directions

1. Wash the dry fruit preferably the evening before use and place it in an earthen pot with 3/8 liter fresh water to soak.
2. The next morning, without changing the water, bring the dried fruit to a boil in the same pot with orange peel, cinnamon and half the sugar, then cover, push back and simmer very slowly for 2-3 hours to soften, without stirring; only shake the pot occasionally. If necessary, add some water so that there is plenty of broth. Lift out the fruit, mix the remaining sugar with the juice, cook this down to 1/8 liter and mix with potato flour dissolved in water; arrange the fruit with the sauce poured over it.
