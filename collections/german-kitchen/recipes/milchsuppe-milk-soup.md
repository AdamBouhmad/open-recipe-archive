## Ingredients

- 1 l whole milk
- 1 piece lemon or orange peel
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 6 g salt
- 30 g rice, 40 g semolina, flour or cornstarch

## Directions

1. Wash and drain the rice, wash the semolina, and mix the flour with liquid.
2. Bring the milk with spices, sugar and salt to a boil, add the rice, semolina, flour or cornstarch and let it swell under frequent stirring.
3. Before serving, strain the soup through an egg yolk if desired.
