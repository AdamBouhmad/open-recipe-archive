## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- lukewarm wine
- 2 knife-points of cinnamon
- 4 loth pounded sugar
- snow from 6 eggs
- bacon

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound flour smooth in a bowl with lukewarm wine.
2. Slowly mix in 2 knife-points of cinnamon, 4 loth pounded sugar, and the beaten whites of 6 eggs.
3. Heat a waffle iron in fire or, better yet, in coals, and coat the inside with bacon.
4. Fill it with dough, close the iron slowly, bake the waffle golden on both sides, and sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top.
