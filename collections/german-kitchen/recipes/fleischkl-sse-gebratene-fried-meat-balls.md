## Ingredients

- 70 g scraped beef
- 50 g scraped pork
- 1 peeled bread roll
- 1/8 liter water for soaking
- 1/2 egg
- 1 pinch pepper, 5 g salt
- 1 knife point grated onions
- 10 g grated bread roll
- 25 g fat or margarine for frying

## Directions

1. Peel the bread roll, cut it into pieces, and soak it.
2. Mix beef, pork, egg, squeezed-out bread roll, pepper, salt, and onion thoroughly together in a bowl; form this mixture into 4 uniform balls, press them smooth, roll them in grated bread roll, and fry them in steaming fat for 7 minutes slowly with frequent turning.
3. Alternatively, form the entire mixture into an oblong shape and fry it in a pan with 1 tablespoon fat for 1/2 hour under frequent basting; gradually pour a few spoons of water under the roast and thicken the sauce after removing it with 3 g potato flour mixed in 2 tablespoons water.
4. Alternatively (Fleischpudding): In a greased, breadcrumb-lined sealed mold, the well-pressed mixture is cooked for 1 1/2 hours in a water bath or in a pressure cooker, turned out, and served with sauce of choice. The frying fat is then omitted.
