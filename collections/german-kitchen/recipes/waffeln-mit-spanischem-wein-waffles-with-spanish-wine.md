## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- Spanish wine
- 4 loth melted butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 knife-point of pounded cinnamon
- 1 knife-point of salt
- 4 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- bacon

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound flour smooth with Spanish wine.
2. Pour 4 loth melted butter on it and stir in 4 egg yolks, one after another, along with 1 knife-point of pounded cinnamon and as much salt.
3. Add the stiff snow of 4 egg whites and 2 tablespoons sour cream.
4. Coat the hot waffle iron inside with a strip of bacon and fill with dough.
5. Bake the waffle on both sides and sprinkle with sugar.
