## Ingredients

- 3 to 5 pieces of Mundbroden (sweet bread)
- milk
- 1 schoppen lukewarm wine
- flour
- 6 to 8 apples
- 1 piece of butter
- 2 loth sugar
- 1/8 pound small raisins
- cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut off the hard rind from the bottom of 3 to 5 pieces of Mundbroden
2. Dip 3 to 4 slices at a time in milk, lay them on top of each other, and continue until all are used
3. Make a thin dough (Flädchenteig) from 1 schoppen lukewarm wine and flour
4. Peel 6 to 8 apples, put a piece of butter in a casserole with the apples, 2 loth sugar, and 1/8 pound small raisins
5. Cook them soft, add 2 knife points of cinnamon, stir all together like a porridge, and let it cool on a plate
6. Heat lard, take half a spoonful of the apple mixture and stir it into the dough
7. Take 2 of the bread slices, spread a tablespoonful of the cooked apples on one, cover with another slice, dip in the dough
8. Fry until golden and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
