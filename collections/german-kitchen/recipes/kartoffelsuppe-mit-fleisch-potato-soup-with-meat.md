## Ingredients

- 125 g beef
- 1/2 kg raw peeled potatoes
- 2 tablespoons soup vegetables
- 10 g salt
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 l water

## Directions

1. Wash the beef, cut it into bite-sized cubes and place in an earthenware pot. Cut the potatoes into quarters, cut any cleaned vegetables such as beets, leeks, cabbage, celery into cubes and pour everything over with 1 l boiling water. Fill this into the pot with the meat using a skimming spoon, sprinkle salt over it and add 1 l water.
2. Cover the pot tightly; after 1/2 hour skim the soup and serve it, after 2-1/2 hours of slow cooking, mixed with fresh chopped parsley and seasoned with pepper.
