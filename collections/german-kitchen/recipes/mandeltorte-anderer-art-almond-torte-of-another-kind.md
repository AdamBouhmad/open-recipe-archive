## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 loth finely pounded almonds
- 12 loth finely pounded sugar
- 4 loth finely cut citron
- 4 loth finely cut orange peel
- zest of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Whip 8 egg whites into a stiff snow
2. Take 8 egg yolks with 12 loth finely pounded almonds, 12 loth finely pounded sugar, 4 loth finely cut citron, and 4 loth finely cut orange peel
3. Stir everything together very thick with the zest of 1/2 lemon
4. Fill into a metal form and prepare according to standard baking methods
