## Ingredients

- quarter pound flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 loth butter
- 3 loth finely ground sugar
- half a lemon zested
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 loth ground sugar
- 4 loth melted butter
- half a lemon zested
- 8 loth fine flour
- 10 egg whites snow
- 3 loth citron cut into cubes
- one loth coarsely ground rock sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough from quarter pound flour, 2 egg yolks, 4 loth butter, 3 loth finely ground sugar, half a lemon zested and 2 spoonfuls sour cream on the pastry board
2. Work it until no butter is visible
3. Work it together
4. Roll out a cake from it
5. Coat a cake sheet with butter
6. Lay the dough in it
7. Press a rim with fingers as usual
8. Stir together 10 egg yolks, 10 loth ground sugar, 4 loth melted butter, half a lemon zested, 8 loth fine flour with the snow from 10 egg whites
9. Fill the mass onto the dough
10. Bake the cake in a not too hot oven half way
11. Sprinkle it with 3 loth citron cut into cubes mixed with one loth coarsely ground rock sugar
12. Bake it until completely done
