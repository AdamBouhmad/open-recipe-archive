## Ingredients

- 1 piece pumpkin
- 1/2 l water for blanching
- 125 g rice
- 5 g salt
- 10 g sugar
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 5 g margarine
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Peel the pumpkin, cut into cubes and rinse; wash the rice.
2. Cook both together in water, fat, salt and sugar until done and season with vinegar.
