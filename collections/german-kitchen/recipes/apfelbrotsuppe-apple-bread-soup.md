## Ingredients

- 100 g old bread
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 crushed cloves
- 40 g sugar
- 4 medium-sized apples (or apple scraps)
- 1 l water

## Directions

1. Soften the bread in the water.
2. Cook the apples or apple scraps, bread mixture, spices, salt and sugar together, strain through a sieve, reheat once more and serve.
