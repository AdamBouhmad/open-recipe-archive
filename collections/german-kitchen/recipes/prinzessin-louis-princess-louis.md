## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen water
- 4 loth fresh butter
- 6 loth sugar
- fine flour
- 1 lemon
- 12 egg yolks
- preserved gooseberries
- apricot jam
- raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook 1 schoppen water, 4 loth fresh butter, and 6 loth sugar together in a brass pan.
2. Stir in fine flour until thick, transfer to a bowl, and grate 1 whole lemon over a grater and mix with 12 egg yolks into the batter.
3. Bake 1 ladle-full of batter in an iron pan the size of a plate until golden on one side, turn the cake and bake the other side.
4. Grease the plate for serving with butter and place the cake on it.
5. Spread with preserved gooseberries, bake a similar cake and place on the first.
6. Spread the second with apricot jam, bake the third and place it on the second, spread with raspberries, and continue this way until the batter is finished (making approximately 4 to 5 cakes).
7. Beat stiff snow from 12 egg whites, make a wreath around the cakes, sprinkle sugar over for a beautiful shine, let everything bake golden in the oven for half an hour, and sprinkle with sugar.
