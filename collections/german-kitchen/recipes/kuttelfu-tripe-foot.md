## Ingredients

- One ox snout
- One ox foot
- Vinegar
- Salt
- One lemon peel, minced
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Simmer the ox snout and foot until very soft
2. Let them cool
3. Cut them into thin slices
4. Cook them further for some time with vinegar, one spoonful of the broth in which they were first cooked, salt, the minced lemon peel, and pepper
5. Cook until little broth remains
6. Pour everything into a mold
7. Let it set and then unmold onto a plate
