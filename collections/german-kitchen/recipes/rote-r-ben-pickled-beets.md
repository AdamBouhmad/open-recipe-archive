## Ingredients

- 2-1/2 kg (5 Pfd) rote Rüben (beets)
- 8 g Kümmel (caraway seeds)
- 3 tablespoons horseradish chunks
- 1/2 l vinegar
- 10 g sugar
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. Clean the beets carefully with a brush, being careful not to tear the skin or break off the root end, otherwise the beets will become pale in cooking water.
2. Place them carefully in boiling water and cook until soft, which takes varying time depending on the size of the beets.
3. Place them briefly in cold water and slip the skin off all the beets smoothly.
4. Before cutting the prepared beets, wash your hands so nothing from the outside of the beet gets onto the beet slices.
5. Cut the beets over the pot in which they should remain, in slices the thickness of a coin, and sprinkle cleaned caraway seeds and small horseradish chunks thinly between the layers.
6. Bring the vinegar, which should not be too sharp, to a boil with sugar and salt, and after cooling pour it over the beets, which should be weighted down so they remain under the liquid.
7. Tie the pot with parchment paper. The small heart leaves are suitable for salad.
