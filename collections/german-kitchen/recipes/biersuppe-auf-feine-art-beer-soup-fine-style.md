## Ingredients

- 1/2 measure beer
- 6 lot sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- butter
- 1 small spoon flour
- 1/2 measure good sweet cream
- 2 egg yolks
- bread
- lard

## Directions

1. Let 1/2 measure beer cook in a brass pan with 6 lot sugar, a piece of cinnamon, and the zest of 1/2 lemon
2. Put a piece of butter in a casserole and let 1 small spoon of flour brown in it
3. Pour in 1/2 measure of good sweet cream
4. Add 2 egg yolks and strain the beer through a hair sieve into the casserole
5. Let everything come to a boil together
6. Cut bread into small pieces, toast them golden in lard, and arrange the soup over them
