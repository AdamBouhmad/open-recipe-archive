## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound sugar
- 6 egg whites
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- 8 loth butter
- 4 loth flour
- 2 handfuls ground hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients together thoroughly
2. Bake in a waffle iron until golden
3. Sprinkle with sugar
