## Ingredients

- Veal (from the knuckle, cut in strips 3 fingers wide)
- Onions
- Parsley
- Lemons
- White bread (a little, grated)
- Butter
- Bay leaf
- Lemon herb
- Flour (1 spoon)
- Cold water
- Light meat broth (1 ladle)
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Lemon juice (from half a lemon)

## Directions

1. Cut strips from the knuckle 3 fingers wide and pound them a little.
2. Mince onions, parsley and lemons, grate in a little white bread, lay out the strips and place some of the minced mixture on each strip.
3. Roll them together, bind them with thread, place a piece of butter in a casserole, add the meat, a chopped onion, a bay leaf and lemon herb and let them steam covered for half an hour.
4. Sprinkle one spoon of flour over them, pour a little cold water and one ladle of light meat broth, add salt, nutmeg, pepper to taste and the juice of half a lemon.
