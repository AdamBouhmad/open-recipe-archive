## Ingredients

- 140 g ground beef
- 60 g ground pork
- 1 teaspoon melted fat
- 1 egg white or egg yolk
- 1/2 roll
- 1 tablespoon water
- a pinch of salt and pepper
- 1 knife point grated onions
- 1 l water
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. Set the water for cooking the meatballs to boil with salt. Cut the roll into cubes, soak and squeeze out.
2. Place ground beef and ground pork in a bowl; add egg white or egg yolk, salt, pepper, roll, onions and fat, mix everything thoroughly together, form 4 meatballs from the mixture, which must cook slowly in the salt water for 10-15 minutes in an open pot. Serve the meatballs with herring sauce. Remove the meatballs from the water, let them drain, place them in a bowl and keep warm until the sauce, which will then be poured over them, is ready.
