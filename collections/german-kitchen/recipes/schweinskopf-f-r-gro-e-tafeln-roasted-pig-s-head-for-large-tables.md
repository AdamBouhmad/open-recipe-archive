## Ingredients

- 5 pounds butter
- 5 pounds pork lard
- chocolate
- saffron
- spinach cheese

## Directions

1. Stir 5 pounds butter and 5 pounds pork lard together for half an hour until they become like pomade
2. On a large long platter, build a kind of altar from the majority of this mass
3. Decorate it with columns formed from the rest of the mass rolled round, which can be colored brown with chocolate, yellow with saffron, or green with spinach cheese
4. Between two such columns you can also place a figure of a hunter, a pilgrim, or some other form shaped from the same mass
5. Place the pig's head on this altar
