## Ingredients

- 1 quarter pound blanched almonds, of which 2 loth must be bitter
- water
- 3 schoppen milk
- meat from 3 calf's feet
- 1 quarter pound sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 knife points flour
- good oil

## Directions

1. Pound 1 quarter pound blanched almonds (of which 2 loth must be bitter) with water as fine as flour
2. Prepare with 3 schoppen milk
3. Let the almond milk cook with the meat from 3 calf's feet and a quarter pound sugar
4. Mix 6 egg yolks with 2 knife points flour smooth, stir this with the cooked milk on the fire until very smooth
5. Press the mixture through a fine sieve and let it cool
6. Brush a mold with good oil, fill the cream into it and place on ice until it sets
