## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fine flour
- 3 spoonfuls of good beer yeast
- 1 schoppen of lukewarm cream
- 1/2 pound crayfish butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 loth of ground sugar
- 1 knife-tip of salt
- 1 schoppen of milk
- 1/2 maas of milk
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1 piece of whole cinnamon
- 2 loth of sugar
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a dough with 2 pounds fine flour, 3 spoonfuls of good beer yeast, and 1 schoppen of lukewarm cream, and let it rise in warmth
2. Prepare 1/2 pound crayfish butter as previously described
3. Stir 1/4 pound of it in a bowl with 3 egg yolks, 2 loth of ground sugar, and 1 knife-tip of salt until light
4. Add the prepared dough with the flour to the crayfish butter and make a good dough
5. Beat it for a quarter hour, set it in warmth and let it rise
6. In a steamed noodle dish, melt the remaining 1/4 pound crayfish butter, pour in 1 schoppen of milk, place the noodles in it, cover and bake until done
7. For the sauce: cook together 1/2 maas of milk, zest of 1 lemon, 1 piece of whole cinnamon, and 2 loth of sugar
8. Beat 3 egg yolks in a pot and stir smooth with 3 finger-widths of flour and cold milk
9. Gradually pour the boiling milk to it and let everything boil together once more
10. Strain through a fine sieve and serve separately
11. Arrange the steamed noodles on a platter
