## Ingredients

- A piece from the thin breast or flap of beef
- White bread for 1 Kreuzer
- Meat broth
- Half a pound of ground pork
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Half a Vierling of cleaned anchovies
- Capers
- Olives
- Mushrooms
- Lemon peel
- Water
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Cream or truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of meat from the thin breast or flap open in the middle
2. Grate white bread on a grater and cut it into fine strips
3. Pour hot meat broth over the strips so they become moist and soft
4. Mix this with half a pound of well-worked ground pork and season with pepper and nutmeg
5. Spread half of this mixture on the meat
6. Place half a Vierling of cleaned anchovies on the meat and mixture
7. Mix the other half of the pork mixture with minced capers, olives, mushrooms, and lemon peel
8. Spread this over the meat, roll it up, wrap in a white cloth, and sew it closed
9. Cook in a casserole with two parts water, one part wine, and one part vinegar for two hours
10. The meat should remain beautifully white when done
11. Serve with a cream or truffle sauce
