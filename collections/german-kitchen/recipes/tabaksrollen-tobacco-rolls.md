## Ingredients

- Butter dough or other short pastry
- Eggs
- Quarter pound dried grapes and raisins
- 1 glass wine
- 2 lots blanched almonds
- 1 lot citron peel

## Directions

1. Roll out the dough strongly to knife back thickness
2. Cut into strips 3 fingers wide and half a quarter long
3. Brush with eggs
4. Cook quarter pound dried grapes and raisins in a small dish with 1 glass wine for a quarter hour until no liquid remains
5. Mix this with 2 lots blanched almonds and 1 lot citron peel
6. Spread the mixture on the strips
7. Brush the strips with egg and roll them on wooden sticks that are also buttered
8. Tie them together with thread and bake them quickly in hot lard until yellow
9. Remove the thread, let them drain on bread slices, pull out the sticks and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
