## Ingredients

- coffee beans
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar per 1 kg (2 pounds) coffee

## Directions

1. Remove all impure beans from the coffee
2. Wash the coffee lukewarm
3. Drain on a sieve and dry on a cloth
4. Pour into the coffee roaster and roast for 5 minutes
5. Shake the roaster, then continue roasting while slowly turning
6. Every 5 minutes shake the coffee roaster, opening it out the window the second and third times so the loosening husks do not dirty the kitchen
7. Continue roasting until the coffee has a beautiful dark chestnut brown color, checking frequently
8. In the last minute, add 1 teaspoon powdered sugar per 1 kg (2 pounds) coffee, which coats the coffee with a light coating that holds the aroma in the bean
9. Pour out the coffee and stir frequently until it cools so it does not continue roasting and become too dark
10. Store the coffee in earthenware or tin containers on top, which should always be kept closed
