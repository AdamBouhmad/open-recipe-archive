## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
- half a pound of finely ground sugar
- one lemon, grated on the grater

## Directions

1. Stir together 12 egg yolks, the stiffly beaten egg whites, half a pound of finely ground sugar, and one grated lemon for half an hour until the mass becomes very thick
2. Gradually stir in half a pound of very fine flour
3. Brush a kugelhopf mold (or Turkish mold) with butter
4. Fill the mass into it
5. Bake until beautifully golden yellow
