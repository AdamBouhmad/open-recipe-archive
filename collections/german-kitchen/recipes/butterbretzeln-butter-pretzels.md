## Ingredients

- one quarter pound butter
- half pound flour
- one quarter pound sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 yolk
- 1 tablespoon sweet or sour cream
- bitter ground almonds

## Directions

1. Mix together one quarter pound butter, half pound flour, one quarter pound sugar, one egg, one yolk and one tablespoon sweet or sour cream in a bowl or on the pastry board
2. Form small pretzels or rings from it
3. Brush with egg yolk
4. Sprinkle bitter ground almonds on top and bake
