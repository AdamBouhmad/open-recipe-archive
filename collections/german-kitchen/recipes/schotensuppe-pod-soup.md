## Ingredients

- 1 kg (2 lbs) pods
- 30 g fat
- 40 g flour
- 8 g salt
- 1-1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Split the pods, break the pod shells, wash them and put them in a pot.
2. Cook the washed pod shells and the thick pod kernels with water and salt until soft and press the mixture through a sieve.
3. Meanwhile, make a flour roux. Slowly add the strained pod water to the roux with stirring.
4. Cook the fine pod kernels in the soup until soft, optionally cook flour dumplings in it as well and serve everything.
