## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- butter
- 1 onion
- fine flour
- good bouillon
- parsley roots
- water
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Lightly boil lamb breast
2. Cut into small pieces
3. Braise in a casserole with butter and 1 whole onion
4. Cook with fine flour and good bouillon
5. Cut parsley roots into small pieces, boil slightly in water
6. Add to the meat along with a little nutmeg and salt
7. Ensure the sauce and meat are white and fill into the pastry
