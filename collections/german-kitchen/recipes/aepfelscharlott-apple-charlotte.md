## Ingredients

- apples
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound raisins and dried fruit
- 2 knife points of ground cinnamon
- half the peel of a finely chopped lemon
- white bread or milk bread slices
- clarified fat for frying
- butter for the baking sheet
- sugar
- preserved fruits
- 2 spoonfuls of raspberry jelly

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cook them in a casserole with 1/4 pound butter, 1/4 pound raisins and dried fruit, 2 knife points of ground cinnamon, and half the peel of a finely chopped lemon until they form a paste and let cool.
2. Cut white bread or milk bread slices and fry them golden in clarified fat.
3. Brush a fairly deep baking sheet with butter, sprinkle it with sugar, and line the bottom with some of the fried bread slices.
4. Lay a layer of the cooked apples on top, then preserved fruits, then again apples and again fruits, continuing until the apples are used up.
5. Finally, cover the top of the dish with fried bread slices.
6. Place the dish in the oven for 1.5 hours.
7. Pour 2 spoonfuls of raspberry jelly over it and the same amount at the side of the dish so that the bread slices become soft.
