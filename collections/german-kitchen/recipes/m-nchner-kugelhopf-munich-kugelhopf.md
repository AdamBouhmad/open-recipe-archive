## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- 12 eggs (warmed in lukewarm water)
- 4 loths of finely ground sugar
- the yellow of one lemon, grated on the grater
- half a schoppen of thick sweet cream
- 12 tablespoons of very fine flour
- 3 loths of raisins
- 2 loths of finely cut citron
- 3 spoons of good beer yeast

## Directions

1. Let half a pound of butter melt in a bowl
2. Stir until very foamy
3. Gradually stir in 12 eggs (that have been warmed in lukewarm water) along with 4 loths of finely ground sugar
4. Add the yellow of one lemon grated on the grater
5. Stir in half a schoppen of thick sweet cream
6. Mix in 12 tablespoons of very fine flour, 3 loths of raisins, 2 loths of finely cut citron, and 3 spoons of good beer yeast
7. Mix everything well together
8. Brush a kugelhopf mold with butter
9. Fill in the mass
10. Let it rise well in gentle warmth
11. Bake the kugelhopf in the oven at not high heat
