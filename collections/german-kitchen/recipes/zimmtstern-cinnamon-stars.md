## Ingredients

- 1 pound unpeeled almonds
- 6 egg whites
- 1 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1 loth cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the almonds with a cloth, grind them finely and pass through a fine sieve, grinding again what remains
2. Beat the egg whites into snow
3. Mix the almonds and snow together with the finely sifted sugar and cinnamon
4. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a knife back and cut out stars with a medium-sized star mold
5. Brush a black baking sheet with bacon fat, place the stars on it not too close together
6. Bake in gentle heat until yellow
7. Can be served with lemon ice or other ice, or placed on wafer cookies if desired
