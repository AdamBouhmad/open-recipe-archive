## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound fresh butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 6 egg whites
- 6 spoonfuls fine flour

## Directions

1. Stir the fresh butter in a bowl into foam
2. Gradually stir in the 6 egg yolks, 1/4 pound finely sifted sugar, and the zest of half a lemon (grated on a grater)
3. Mix the stiff snow from 6 egg whites with 6 spoonfuls of fine flour and fold into the mixture
4. Mix everything well together
5. Fill the dough into finger-long and 2 finger-wide capsules made of stiff paper, moistened inside with melted butter
6. Bake in the oven
