## Ingredients

- veal, cut into small pieces
- butter
- onion
- yellow turnips
- celery
- 3 handfuls flour
- 4 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter for greasing the mold
- eggs for coating
- grated bread for coating
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cut veal into small pieces and braise it gently in a saucepan with a piece of butter, onion, yellow turnips, and celery.
2. Add 3 handfuls of flour and braise together again.
3. Press everything through a sieve.
4. Beat 4 eggs into it.
5. Stir it together well with salt and nutmeg in a bowl.
6. Butter a long mold and fill it with the mixture.
7. Poach it in a water bath.
8. Cut or shape small cakes from it, dip them in eggs and grated bread, and fry them in hot lard.
