## Ingredients

- 4 pig ears
- 4 pig skins with some fat still attached
- 1 glass muscatel wine
- chopped basil
- thyme
- lemon peel
- pepper
- cloves (coarsely ground)
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop 4 pig ears and 4 pig skins with fat attached
2. Pour a glass of muscatel wine over them
3. Add some chopped basil, thyme, lemon peel, pepper, coarsely ground cloves, and salt
4. Mix everything well together
5. Fill into intestines very firmly like salami and tie tightly
6. Hang them in a dry place with air circulation until they are quite hard
7. Before serving, simmer them with 2 bay leaves until soft and serve with vegetables
8. They can be kept a long time and sent far distances
