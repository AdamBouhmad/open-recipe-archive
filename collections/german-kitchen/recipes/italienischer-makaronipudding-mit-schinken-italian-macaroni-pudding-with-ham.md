## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound macaroni
- Salt water or meat broth
- Cold water
- 1/2 pound lean ham
- 1 handful grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 schoppen sour cream
- Grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook 1/4 pound macaroni in salt water or meat broth
2. Draw it through cold water and let it drain
3. Mince 1/2 pound lean ham
4. Mix everything together in a bowl with 1 handful grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 schoppen sour cream and grated nutmeg
5. Bind the mass in a wet cloth
6. Cook it for one hour in salt water
7. Arrange the pudding on a plate and brown it with wet butter
