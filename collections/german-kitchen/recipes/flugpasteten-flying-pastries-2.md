## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 1 tablet grated chocolate
- white paper

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg whites to a stiff snow
2. Stir one tablet grated chocolate into it
3. Make small paper cuffs
4. Place them on a black baking sheet
5. Fill them with the mixture and dry in the oven for 4 minutes
