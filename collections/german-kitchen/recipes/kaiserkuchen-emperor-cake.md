## Ingredients

- four rolls (Mundbroten)
- water
- butter
- almonds
- sugar
- lemon
- egg yolks
- currants
- raisins
- egg whites
- cherries
- cherry stones
- wine
- flour
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the crust from 4 rolls, soak the bread in water and press it out.
2. Braise it in a brass pan with a piece of butter until quite dry, place it in a bowl.
3. Stir in 10 lots of blanched, finely-pounded almonds, a quarter pound of sugar also finely-pounded, one lemon grated on a grater, and 6 egg yolks, mixing well together.
4. Add a quarter pound of currants and raisins along with the snow of egg whites to the mixture.
5. Bake the cake in a ring mold (Gogelhopfenmodel) brushed with butter and sprinkled with sugar until nicely golden.
6. For the cherry sauce: pound half a pound of cherries together with their stones in a mortar, let them cook for a quarter hour with a glass of water, pass them through a sieve with 1 pint of wine.
7. In another dish, stir 2 spoonfuls of flour smooth with wine, add the strained cherries, let all cook together with 4 lots of sugar, 2 knife-tips of cinnamon and the same amount of cloves.
8. Place the emperor cake on the plate and pour the sauce over it.
