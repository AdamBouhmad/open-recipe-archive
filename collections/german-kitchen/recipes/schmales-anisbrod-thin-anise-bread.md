## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites
- 1 lemon
- 1 pound flour
- 2 handfuls anise, cleaned

## Directions

1. Stir together 1 pound sugar, 10 egg yolks, and the snow from 10 egg whites strongly for half an hour
2. Add the zest of 1 lemon (grated on a grater), 1 pound flour, and 2 handfuls of clean anise
3. Sprinkle flour on a black sheet
4. Form long, hand-width strips of dough and place them on the sheet
5. Bake in the oven
6. When cooled, cut into slices
7. Toast in a not-too-hot oven
