## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 sweetbreads
- handful of chopped onions
- parsley
- lemon peel
- piece of butter
- 2 Mundbrod (bread rolls)
- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut 2 to 3 sweetbreads into square pieces
2. Braise them with a handful of chopped onions, parsley, lemon peel and a piece of butter for a quarter hour
3. Grate 2 Mundbrod, soak them, press them out firmly
4. Stir 1/4 pound butter lightly, add the bread, 4 eggs, salt and nutmeg
5. Stir everything together until quite foamy
6. Brush pastry molds with butter, fill the mass into them
7. Place a spoonful of the braised sweetbreads on each mold and bake them until golden
