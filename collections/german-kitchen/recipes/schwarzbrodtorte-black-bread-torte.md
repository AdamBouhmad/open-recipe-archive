## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely ground sugar
- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- 1/2 pound unblanched almonds
- 12 loth stale black bread, grated
- 1 grated nutmeg
- zest of 1 lemon, minced
- 2 knife-points of ground cinnamon
- 2 knife-points of mace
- 2 knife-points of ground cloves
- 2 knife-points of cardamom
- 2 knife-points of cubeb
- 24 egg yolks
- snow of 10 egg whites
- 1/4 pound citron peel
- 1/4 pound orange peel
- butter and bread crumbs for the pan

## Directions

1. Finely pound the blanched almonds with egg white
2. Pound the unblanched almonds less finely
3. Stir together the sugar, both types of almonds, grated bread, nutmeg, lemon zest, cinnamon, mace, cloves, cardamom, and cubeb with the 24 egg yolks for half an hour with strong stirring
4. Mix in the snow of 10 egg whites along with the citron peel and orange peel
5. Brush a cake pan with butter and sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Fill the mass into the pan
7. Bake the torte in a hot oven
