## Ingredients

- 2 large herrings
- 4 Borsdorfer apples
- 4 potatoes
- onions
- pepper
- milk from the herring
- Provençal oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the herrings, take out the bones, cut them into desired pieces.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into pieces the same size as the herring.
3. Peel the potatoes and cut them.
4. Stir the milk from the herring with Provençal oil, put all the cut ingredients in a salad bowl, pour the mixture over it, add some vinegar, mix it together and lay it on top of the salad.
