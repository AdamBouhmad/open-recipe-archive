## Ingredients

- 1 quarter pound butter
- 1 quarter pound blanched, finely pounded almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 6 eggs
- 8 loth fiddle flour
- 1 lemon
- butter
- sugar
- 1/2 pint milk
- 1/2 lemon
- 3 loth sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 glass Maraschino

## Directions

1. Lightly beat 1 quarter pound butter in a bowl, add 1 quarter pound blanched, finely pounded almonds, stir 8 egg yolks in one after another, beat stiff snow from 6 eggs, mix 8 loth fiddle flour with the snow under the mass, grate 1 lemon on the grater and add it as well.
2. Brush a pudding mold with butter and sprinkle with sugar, fill the mass into it and leave it in boiling water 3 quarters of an hour.
3. For the sauce: let 1/2 pint of milk, the zest of half a lemon and 3 loth sugar cook together in a brass pan.
4. Beat 3 egg yolks in a dish, pour 1/2 glass of Maraschino and the boiling milk slowly to it, put it back in the pan and let it thicken, then turn the pudding onto a plate and serve the sauce with it.
