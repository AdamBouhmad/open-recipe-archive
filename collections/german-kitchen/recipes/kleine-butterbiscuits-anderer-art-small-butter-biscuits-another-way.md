## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 pound finely pounded sugar
- raisins (optional)
- 6 Loth flour

## Directions

1. Stir the butter in a bowl until foamy
2. Stir in the eggs one after another
3. Stir in 1/2 pound finely pounded sugar
4. If desired, add a small handful of raisins
5. Stir in 6 Loth flour
6. Fill the mass into small greased metal molds
7. Bake in the oven
