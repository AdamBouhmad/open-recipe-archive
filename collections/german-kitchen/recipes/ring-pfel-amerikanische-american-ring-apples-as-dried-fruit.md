## Ingredients

- 65 g American ring apples
- 25 g sugar
- 4 g potato flour
- 1 piece of orange peel
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. American ring apples must be cooked alone, because they require less time and would also discolor; they are only washed several times while warm. They are not soaked.
2. Otherwise they are prepared like other dried fruit; for example, they make a good compote for the sick.
