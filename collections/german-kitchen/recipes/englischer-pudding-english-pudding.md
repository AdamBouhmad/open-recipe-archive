## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef kidney fat, cleaned and finely chopped
- 1/2 pound large raisins
- 1/2 pound small raisins
- 1/2 pound ground sugar
- zest of 1 lemon, chopped
- 8 loth grated rolls
- 1 cooking spoon fine flour
- 1/2 glass Arak
- 5 eggs
- flour for dusting
- preserved fruits of all kinds
- approximately 8 peeled apples, sliced

## Directions

1. On a noodle board dusted with flour, make a well-worked dough from the kidney fat, raisins, sugar, lemon zest, grated rolls, flour, Arak, and eggs
2. Roll it out as well as possible
3. Lay a clean cloth on a pillow and stretch it out as finely as possible on it
4. Let it dry slightly
5. Put it back on the noodle board
6. Spread half of the mixture over it
7. Add all kinds of preserved fruits and the sliced apples
8. Spread the other half of the mixture on top
9. Roll up the pudding
10. Form it into a spiral shape
11. Bind it tightly in a damp cloth, leaving a finger-width empty at the top
12. Cook in boiling water for 4 hours
