## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- butter
- flour
- meat broth
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and wash them, cut into leaves or square pieces.
2. In a piece of butter, steam them soft.
3. Sprinkle flour over them, pour meat broth over them, and cook a while longer.
4. If desired, add a little sugar.
