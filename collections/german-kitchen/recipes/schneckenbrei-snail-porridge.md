## Ingredients

- snails
- 1/2 lemon peel
- 1 onion
- a handful of parsley
- 1/8 pound butter
- 3 fingers flour
- 1 spoon meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the snails as previously described, then weigh them very fine with 1/2 lemon peel, 1 onion and a handful of parsley
2. Let 1/8 pound butter melt in a casserole, braise the weighed snails in it, sprinkle 3 fingers flour on top
3. Pour 1 spoon meat broth to it, add salt and pepper along with the juice of 1/2 lemon and let everything come to a boil together once more
