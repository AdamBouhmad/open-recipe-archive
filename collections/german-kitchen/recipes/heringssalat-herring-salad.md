## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cooked potatoes
- 1 apple
- 1 herring
- 1 small onion
- 1/2 salt cucumber
- 3 tablespoons each vinegar and water
- 1 tablespoon oil or goose fat
- 1 pinch pepper
- 2-1/2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix water, vinegar, sugar, oil, pepper, salt and one cooked, grated potato in a bowl to make a marinade.
2. Peel the onion and cut it into very small pieces or mince finely. Peel the apple, potatoes and salt cucumber and cut into uniform pieces; soak the herring, clean, skin, bone and cut finely; add everything to the marinade.
3. Mix the entire mass thoroughly with a wooden spoon and let it steep covered. Taste the salad and arrange it on a platter in a mound shape.
