## Ingredients

- 15 fresh herrings
- 40 g salt
- 1 pinch pepper and salt
- 75 g flour
- 250 g frying fat
- 1 liter vinegar
- 50 g onion slices
- 1 spice dose
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Scale the herrings, clean them, wash them and make light scratches a few times across the back
2. Salt them and let stand for 1/2 hour
3. Dry them and roll them evenly in flour
