## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef kidney fat
- 1 pound grated white bread
- 3/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 3/4 pound small raisins
- 3/4 pound washed large raisins
- zest of 1 lemon, chopped
- 1 quint ground cinnamon
- 2 knife-points ground nutmeg
- 1 glass cognac
- 13 eggs
- salt to taste
- sugar for serving
- arak for serving

## Directions

1. Carefully skin the beef kidney fat, cut it into very small pieces
2. Add 1 pound grated white bread, 3/4 pound finely ground sugar, 3/4 pound small raisins and the same amount of washed large raisins, the chopped zest of 1 lemon, 1 quint ground cinnamon, 2 knife-points ground nutmeg, and 1 glass cognac
3. Mix everything together with 13 eggs
4. Bind the pudding in a damp cloth and cook it in boiling, lightly salted water for 4 to 5 hours
5. Turn it out onto a plate, cut a small opening in the top of the pudding
6. Pour sugar into the opening, pour in arak, light it, and serve the pudding burning
