## Ingredients

- half a pound blanched almonds
- egg white
- quarter pound butter
- half pound finely ground sugar
- 2 handfuls finely grated potato flour
- egg white snow from several eggs
- butter for coating baking sheet
- wafers
- raisins and dried plums (optional)

## Directions

1. Pound the blanched almonds very finely in a mortar with egg white
2. Let a quarter pound butter melt in a bowl
3. Boil the above with half a pound finely ground sugar for half an hour
4. Add 2 handfuls finely grated potato flour
5. Slowly mix in the snow from several egg whites
6. Line a baking sheet first coated with butter with wafers
7. Pour the mass onto it
8. Bake the cake in the oven
9. If desired, sprinkle raisins and dried plums on top
