## Ingredients

- Tender butter dough
- A piece of ling
- Salt water, boiling
- 2 to 3 handfuls Bohemian peas
- Potatoes, baked
- Parsley, 1 handful
- Shallot onions
- Lard

## Directions

1. Roll out tender butter dough finger-thick
2. Make a bottom and a lid for a pastry from it
3. Brush the lid with egg
4. Bake the pastry golden
5. When it comes from the oven, remove the lid
6. Prepare the ragout: treat a piece of ling as described in No. 286
7. Cook 2 to 3 handfuls of Bohemian peas until the skins come off
8. Pour off the water
9. Set them to warmth
10. Pour the ling into a sieve
11. Clean it
12. Lay the pastry on a plate
13. Put some of the ling in it, then some of the peas, some baked potato, and continue until everything is used
14. Render the ling with a handful of parsley and shallot onions that have been steamed in hot lard
15. Lay the lid on the pastry
16. Serve it at table
