## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 loth isinglass
- 1 schoppen water
- 4 to 6 spoonfuls rosehip pulp
- 6 eggs
- 1 schoppen good wine
- 1/2 schoppen water
- 1/2 pound sugar
- grated zest of 1 lemon
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1-1/2 loth isinglass in 1 schoppen water.
2. Place 4 to 6 spoonfuls of rosehip pulp in a brass pan.
3. Beat 6 eggs into it.
4. Add 1 schoppen good wine, 1/2 schoppen water, 1/2 pound sugar, and the grated zest of 1 lemon.
5. Cook everything together with constant stirring.
6. Fill the cream into a jelly mold brushed with almond oil.
7. Place it in the cellar and let the cream set in it.
