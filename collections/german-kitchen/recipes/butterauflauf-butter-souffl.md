## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 whole lemon
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- fine flour
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 14 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter until foamy.
2. Grate a whole lemon into 1/4 pound sugar, finely grind it, and stir in with 12 egg yolks, each time adding a knife point of fine flour.
3. When all the eggs are mixed in, add 3 tablespoons sour cream.
4. Beat the 14 egg whites to a snow and mix into the mass well.
5. Bake in a baking sheet brushed with butter.
