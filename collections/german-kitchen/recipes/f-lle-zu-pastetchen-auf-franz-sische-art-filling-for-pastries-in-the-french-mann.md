## Ingredients

- 1 pound of skinned veal
- 1/2 pound of beef kidney fat
- zest of 1 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- steamed onions
- 1 soaked and squeezed roll
- 4 eggs
- 1 knife point of ground pepper
- 1 knife point of nutmeg
- 1 knife point of cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons of cold water
- puff pastry
- bacon
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop together the veal, kidney fat, and lemon zest like a dough
2. Add the lemon juice, steamed onions, and the soaked and squeezed roll
3. Put everything in a mortar and stir with the eggs until frothy, adding the pepper, nutmeg, cinnamon, and cold water
4. Make puff pastry and roll it out two knife backs thick
5. Place on a floured black baking sheet and form a round base
6. Cover with bacon and spread the mixture on top
7. Cover again with bacon, leaving a 2-finger wide border empty
8. Cover with a puff pastry lid brushed with cold water
9. Frame the lid with a 2-finger wide strip of puff pastry
10. Cut various flowers into it with a hot knife
11. Brush with egg and bake
12. Serve cold
