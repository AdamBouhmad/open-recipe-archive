## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- zest of 4 lemons
- 8 egg yolks
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 loth fine flour
- snow of 8 egg whites
- butter dough for lining

## Directions

1. Finely pound the sugar with the lemon zest in a bowl
2. Beat 8 egg yolks into it
3. Stir this together for half an hour
4. Mix in the juice of 2 lemons, 2 loth fine flour, and the snow of the 8 egg whites into the mass
5. Fill this into a plafond lined with butter dough
6. Bake the torte in the oven
