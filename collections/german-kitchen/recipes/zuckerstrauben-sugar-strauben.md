## Ingredients

- Quarter pound flour
- 6 lots sugar
- 3 spoons wine
- 3 spoons rose water
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Stir together quarter pound flour, 6 lots sugar, 3 spoons wine and 3 spoons rose water with egg white
2. Make the dough somewhat thinner than usual strauben dough
3. Let hot lard run through the strauben funnel
4. Bake the strauben through the funnel in hot lard
5. While still hot, press them over a corrugated rolling pin
6. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
