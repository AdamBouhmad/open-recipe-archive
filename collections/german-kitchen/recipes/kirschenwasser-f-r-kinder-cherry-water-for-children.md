## Ingredients

- 1/2 Maas cherries
- 1 Maas water
- 1 piece cinnamon
- peel of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Pound 1/2 Maas cherries very well in the mortar
2. Let them cook with 1 Maas water, 1 piece cinnamon and the peel of 1/2 lemon for half an hour
3. Pour the water through a hair sieve and give it cold to drink
