## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 12 loth finely ground almonds
- 12 loth finely ground sugar
- 4 loth finely cut citron
- 4 loth finely cut orange peel
- zest and juice of half a lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the 8 egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Mix the 8 egg yolks with the finely ground almonds, finely ground sugar, finely cut citron, finely cut orange peel, and zest and juice of half a lemon
3. Stir all together very thick, then fold in the egg white peaks
4. Butter a tin form
5. Fill the mixture into the form and bake in the oven at low heat until golden yellow
