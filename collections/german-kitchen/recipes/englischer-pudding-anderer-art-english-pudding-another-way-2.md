## Ingredients

- 9 loth beef kidney fat
- 20 loth flour
- 8 loth finely pounded sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 8 loth citron and orange peel
- 8 loth small raisins
- 8 loth large raisins
- 4 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the beef kidney fat very finely.
2. Rub 20 loth flour with the hand and mix into the chopped fat.
3. Add 8 loth finely pounded sugar, zest of half a lemon grated on the grater, 8 loth citron and orange peel finely chopped, 8 loth small raisins, 8 loth large raisins washed clean.
4. Beat 4 whole eggs and mix everything with a cooking spoon together like a sparrow dough.
5. Tie the mass firmly in a cloth dipped in cold water and put it in boiling water.
6. Cook for three hours.
7. Serve with flaming Arak or any desired sauce.
