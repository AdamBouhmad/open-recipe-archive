## Ingredients

- eggs
- spinach
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- beaten eggs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, shell them and place in cold water
2. Cook spinach as for a vegetable
3. Press the egg yolks into the spinach
4. In a saucepan, melt a piece of butter and braise the spinach and egg yolks in it with salt and nutmeg
5. Fill the egg whites with this, press them together so they look like whole eggs
6. Turn the eggs in beaten eggs and then in grated breadcrumbs and bake
