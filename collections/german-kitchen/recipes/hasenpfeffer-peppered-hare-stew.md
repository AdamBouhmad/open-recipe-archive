## Ingredients

- 250 g hare meat: the split head, breast, neck, lungs and heart
- 50 g bacon cubes
- 20 g onions
- 20 g flour
- 1 spice measure
- 1/4 liter meat extract broth
- 4 tablespoons red or malt wine
- 15 g sugar
- 8 g salt and pepper
- 2 drops food coloring
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. The small hare pieces are minced and washed thoroughly.
2. Cook them in 1/2 liter water, then cut into cubes and remove the bones. The bacon is browned in an earthenware pot; the meat and onion cubes are added. After everything is browned, dust flour over it, add spices, broth, wine, sugar, salt and pepper and braise the dish slowly until done. The appearance is enhanced by food coloring; the wine can be omitted.
