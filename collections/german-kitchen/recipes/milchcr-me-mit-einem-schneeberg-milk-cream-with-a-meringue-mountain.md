## Ingredients

- 1 Maas milk
- 4 Loth sugar
- half a lemon peel
- 8 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 small spoonful flour
- red sanding sugar

## Directions

1. Let 1 Maas milk with 4 Loth sugar and half a lemon peel boil in a brass pan.
2. Beat 8 egg whites into stiff peaks by hand until very high.
3. Let the meringue cook 3 to 4 minutes in the boiled milk, turning carefully.
4. Let it cook that long again, then remove carefully and place in a hair sieve to drain.
5. Stir the 8 egg yolks with one small spoonful flour until completely smooth.
6. Pour the boiling milk onto it, let it draw up once more in the pan.
7. Pass half the milk through a hair sieve onto the plate.
8. Set the meringue mountain in the middle.
9. Pour the remaining milk over it.
10. Sprinkle red sanding sugar on top.
11. Serve cold in summer, warm in winter.
