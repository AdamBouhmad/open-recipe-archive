## Ingredients

- 4 to 5 handfuls semolina
- 1/2 Maas milk
- 4 eggs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cook 4 to 5 handfuls of semolina in 1/2 Maas milk for a quarter hour until very thick.
2. Stir 4 eggs into it in a bowl (adding gradually).
3. Fry small dumplings in hot lard.
