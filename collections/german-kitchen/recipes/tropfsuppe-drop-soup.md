## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls of flour
- milk or water
- meat broth or water
- chives
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 2 eggs, 2 spoonfuls of flour and milk or water
2. Heat meat broth or water until simmering
3. Let the dough run into the pot over a cooking spoon or through a skimming spoon
4. When serving, add chives, nutmeg and salt
