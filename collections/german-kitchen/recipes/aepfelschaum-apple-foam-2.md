## Ingredients

- Apples (6 to 8)
- cinnamon (1 knife point)
- sugar (8 loth, ground)
- egg whites (12)
- butter

## Directions

1. Roast the apples either in hot ashes, on a dish in the oven, or in the roasting oven
2. Scrape out the pulp
3. Stir it with a knife point of cinnamon and 8 loth ground sugar until very foamy
4. Whip a stiff foam from 12 egg whites
5. Slowly mix it with the apple mixture
6. Pour into a deep plate brushed with butter, shaped like a pyramid
7. Place the plate on a black sheet thickly covered with sand or salt
8. Put in a roasting oven and sprinkle with sugar
9. It is ready in half an hour
