## Ingredients

- 4 strong spoonfuls pounded sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- half a lemon
- 4 egg whites
- 3 fingers full flour
- lard, size of a hen's egg

## Directions

1. Stir 4 strong spoonfuls pounded sugar, 4 egg yolks and half a lemon grated on a grater together until very thick.
2. Mix in the snow from 4 egg whites and 3 fingers full flour.
3. Make a piece of lard the size of a hen's egg hot in a pancake pan and let it cool a little.
4. Pour the mass slowly into it.
5. Cover the pan and let the omelet bake slowly on hot ashes.
6. Turn it and bake it on the other side also and fold it over.
7. When all are baked, place them for a while on a long plate in the baking oven and finish them.
