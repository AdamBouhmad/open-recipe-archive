## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- half a Maas of wine vinegar
- 4 bay leaves
- 6 cloves
- 6 coarsely crushed peppercorns
- 4 Loth brown mustard flour
- 4 Loth yellow mustard flour

## Directions

1. Chop the onions and garlic cloves very finely
2. Pour half a Maas of wine vinegar into a pan, add 4 bay leaves, 6 cloves, and 6 coarsely crushed peppercorns
3. Let it cook for half a quarter hour
4. Take 4 Loth brown mustard flour and the same amount of yellow mustard flour in a bowl
5. Remove the vinegar from the fire and strain it through a hair sieve onto the flour
6. Stir the mustard for half an hour, as this makes it finer
7. Fill it into a container as desired, bind it and serve with beef
