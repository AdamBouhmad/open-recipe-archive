## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound blanched bitter almonds
- cream
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 measure of water
- 10 to 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pound the blanched bitter almonds finely in a mortar with some cream
2. Place them in a dish and pour enough cream over them to make 1/2 measure total
3. Cover and let stand for 1/2 hour
4. Cook 1/2 pound sugar in a brass pan with 1/2 measure water
5. Remove the dark scum completely
6. Let it cook until it forms threads
7. Press the almonds through linen cloth so they are completely dry
8. Pour the cream into the sugar
9. Let everything cook together for 2 minutes
10. Beat 10 to 12 egg yolks in a pot and slowly stir in the cream
11. Put it in the freezing tin
12. Once it is slightly cold, proceed according to the method given in No. 767
