## Ingredients

- One Schoppen measure
- One lemon, rubbed on sugar
- One lemon, juice pressed
- Fresh spring water
- 3 Loth sugar per Schoppen

## Directions

1. Rub one lemon on the sugar
2. Press the juice from one lemon
3. Fill with fresh spring water
4. Use 3 Loth sugar per Schoppen
5. For wine lemonade: use half wine and half water, juice from half a lemon, and rub the other half on sugar
