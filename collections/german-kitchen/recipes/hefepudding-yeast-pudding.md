## Ingredients

- 175 g flour
- 15 g yeast
- 1/8 l whole milk
- 20 g beef fat or margarine
- 1 egg
- 2 cloves
- 40 g sugar
- 8 g salt
- 40 g large and small raisins
- 5 g margarine for the mould
- 5 g sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and sift the flour; warm the flour as well
2. Crumble the yeast into the milk and stir
3. Sort and soak the raisins; crush the cloves; grease the mould or cloth and prepare a water pot for cooking
4. Melt 20 g fat
5. For the yeast piece: place 125 g flour in a bowl, make a well in the center, gradually stir in the yeast mixed with milk until a thick smooth batter forms; dust with flour, cover with a cloth and set in a warm place to rise to twice its height
6. Pour the melted but not hot fat into a bowl and mix with the other prepared ingredients except flour and yeast piece
7. When the yeast piece reaches the proper height, add it along with the remaining flour and mix well
8. Place in the mould or loosely bind in cloth and let rise another 1/4 hour
9. Place the sealed mould or cloth pudding in strongly boiling water and cook; place an inverted plate under the cloth
10. Maintain constant water level by adding boiling water as needed
11. After 1 hour, remove the pudding mould, dry it, let stand a moment, open it, place a plate on top, turn the mould over, lift it off and sprinkle the pudding with sugar; for cloth pudding, untie the cloth, place a dish on the pudding and turn over
