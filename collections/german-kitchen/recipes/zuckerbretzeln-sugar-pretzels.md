## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound flour
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 spoonful thick sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix butter, flour, sugar, egg and thick sour cream into a dough
2. Make small pretzels from the dough
3. Place on a black baking sheet
4. Brush with egg
5. Sprinkle with finely pounded almonds and some sugar
6. Bake
