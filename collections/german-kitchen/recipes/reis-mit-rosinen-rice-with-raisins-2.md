## Ingredients

- 1/2 l water for blanching
- 1/2 l water
- 5 g fat or margarine
- 20 g large raisins
- 1 piece orange peel
- 5 g salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the cleaned raisins in water and then drain them.
2. Add the raisins to the rice, which is prepared as water rice.
3. Season the dish with vinegar and sugar.
