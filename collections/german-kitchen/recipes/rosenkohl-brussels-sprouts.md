## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- boiling water
- onions
- parsley
- fat
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the Brussels sprouts carefully.
2. Add boiling water and cook until tender.
3. Pour into a colander, pour cold water over them.
4. In a casserole, steam the onions and parsley in a piece of fat.
5. Cook the Brussels sprouts in this for a quarter hour.
6. Add flour, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and meat broth.
7. Finish cooking until done.
