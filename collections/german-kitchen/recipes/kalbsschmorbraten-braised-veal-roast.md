## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1 1/2 Pfd.) kidney piece
- 50 g fat or margarine
- 8 g salt
- 4 g potato flour
- 1/8 l water
- 4 tablespoons sour milk

## Directions

1. Brush the kidney piece clean in water, strike it, and salt it.
2. Pour boiling water over the meat, then drain it.
3. In browned fat, brown the meat and braise it gently in a braising pot, pouring and adding milk and water until tender.
4. Coat the meat with sauce, which is made silky after removing the roast by stirring potato flour mixed with a little water into the cooking liquid.
