## Ingredients

- pheasant
- one eighth good Bohemian lentils, cleaned and washed
- salt water
- one glass vinegar

## Directions

1. Roast the pheasant until half done
2. Cook one eighth of good Bohemian lentils, which have been carefully cleaned and washed, until soft in salted water
3. Press them through a sieve
4. Pour one glass of vinegar over them
5. Put half of the lentils into a bowl
6. Remove the breast along with the legs and thighs from the pheasant and lay it in the bowl
7. Add the remaining half of the lentils on top
8. Place it in a roasting pan and let it form a crust
