## Ingredients

- 8 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 onions
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg
- sour cream
- 3 shallot onions
- lemon zest
- flour
- meat broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. For 4 persons, allow 8 hard-boiled eggs
2. Press the yolks of hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve
3. Slice 2 onions and steam them in a casserole with a piece of butter until soft but not yellow
4. Add the egg yolks to the onions with salt, nutmeg, and a spoonful of sour cream
5. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
6. For the sauce: let 3 chopped shallot onions and the zest of half a lemon lightly color in a casserole with melted butter and chopped ingredients, with 3 fingers-worth of flour
7. Pour in a spoonful of meat broth, add salt, nutmeg, and the juice of half a lemon
8. Let this come to a boil together
9. Arrange the sauce on a plate and place the eggs in it
