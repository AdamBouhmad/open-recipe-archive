## Ingredients

- inferior fish forcemeat
- milk
- 3 eggs
- onion
- half a vierling of butter
- lemon
- salt
- grated bread
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Make forcemeat from inferior fish and lay it in milk for half an hour
2. Chop it finely, stir it strongly with a schoppen of milk, beat in 3 eggs and some chopped onion
3. Braise onion and lemon in half a vierling of butter, which also goes into the forcemeat
4. Add salt to taste
5. Stir everything together once more, form into beautiful small cutlets
6. Turn them in eggs, then in grated bread
7. Fry them in an iron pan with plenty of fat until golden yellow
8. Do not let them stand long or they will become tough
