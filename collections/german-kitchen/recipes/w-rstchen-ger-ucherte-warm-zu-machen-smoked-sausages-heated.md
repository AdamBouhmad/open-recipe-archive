## Ingredients

- 2 pairs sausages
- 1/2 l cold water

## Directions

1. Place Jauersche, garlic or Wiener sausages in a covered pot with plenty of cold water.
2. When the water is hot, just before boiling, the sausages are ready. If the sausages boil, they become hard and dry.
