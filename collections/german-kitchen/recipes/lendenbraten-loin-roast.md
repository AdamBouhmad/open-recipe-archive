## Ingredients

- One piece loin roast
- Bacon for larding
- One large spoon meat broth
- Three finely cut celery roots

## Directions

1. Remove skin and fat from the piece.
2. Lard it with bacon.
3. Place in a braising pan and pour one large spoon of meat broth over it.
4. Roast in the oven.
5. When nearly done, add three finely cut celery roots and let them cook until tender.
6. Once soft, serve the roast.
