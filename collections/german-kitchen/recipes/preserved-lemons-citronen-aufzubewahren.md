## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Dry salt

## Directions

1. Wrap the lemons in paper.
2. Lay dry salt in a dish, then lemons, then salt and lemons again.
3. Fill the dish in this manner.
4. Bind it closed and place it in a dry location.
5. In this way, you always have fresh lemons.
