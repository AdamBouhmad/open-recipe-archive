## Ingredients

- Butter dough
- 1/4 pound blanched almonds, not very finely ground
- Egg yolks
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 4 loth sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out butter dough to the thickness of a knife back and cut into strips half a quarter-ell long
2. Mix blanched almonds with egg yolks, lemon zest, lemon juice, and sugar
3. Spread the mixture onto the strips
4. Roll the strips into spiral shapes
5. Bake on a flour-dusted sheet until golden
