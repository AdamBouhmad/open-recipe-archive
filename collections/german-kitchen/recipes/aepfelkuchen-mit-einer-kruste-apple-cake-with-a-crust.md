## Ingredients

- peeled Breitling apples
- butter dough
- quarter-pound butter
- 3 handfuls grated bread
- 4 Loth ground sugar
- 3 Loth ground almonds
- 2 knife-tips cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place peeled Breitling apples in a cake sheet lined with butter dough
2. Toast 3 handfuls of grated bread in a quarter-pound of butter
3. Add 4 Loth of ground sugar, 3 Loth of ground almonds, and 2 knife-tips of cinnamon
4. Mix together
5. Sprinkle this mixture thickly over the cake
6. Bake
