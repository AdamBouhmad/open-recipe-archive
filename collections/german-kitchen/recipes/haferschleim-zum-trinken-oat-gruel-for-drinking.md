## Ingredients

- 60 g oat groats
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1-1/2 l water

## Directions

1. The oat groats are stirred thoroughly, swelled with cold water and drained off.
2. In an earthenware pot, 1/4 l water is filled in while frequently beating with the wooden spoon, this is allowed to evaporate and the process continues until the groats are quite soft and the liquid is slimy. Then the oat gruel is rubbed through a hair sieve, or in the absence of one through a hot-washed cloth, and heated with a small amount of salt. Sugar is added according to taste and the gruel is diluted according to use with boiling water. For the sick, a little raspberry juice or red wine is gladly added.
