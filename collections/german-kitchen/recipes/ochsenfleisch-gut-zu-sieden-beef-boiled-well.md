## Ingredients

- beef from the brisket or chuck
- cold water
- onions
- yellow turnips
- celery
- salt
- jus

## Directions

1. Pound the beef slightly and tie it with twine.
2. Place it in cold water on the fire and cover it.
3. When it is time to skim, remove the foam from the top until it no longer foams.
4. Cut onions, yellow turnips, and celery into it and let it cook very slowly until tender.
5. Salt it to taste half an hour before serving.
6. Pour a ladleful of jus onto the platter before arranging the meat on it.
