## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- 4 loths of sugar
- one egg
- 2 spoons of thick beer yeast
- lukewarm milk
- melted butter
- lemon peel
- anise

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of flour, 4 loths of sugar, one egg, and 2 spoons of thick beer yeast with lukewarm milk to form a firm dough
2. Mix in some melted butter, lemon peel, and anise
3. Knead thoroughly on a pasta board
4. Form 2 loaves
5. Roll each one out the length of a shoe
6. Let them rise in gentle warmth
7. Brush with beaten egg
8. Bake in the oven
