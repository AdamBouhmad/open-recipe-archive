## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked cold potatoes
- 1/4 pound peeled almonds
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1/4 pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- half a Schoppen sour cream
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grate 1 pound of cooked cold potatoes
2. Pound 1/4 pound of peeled almonds and 1/4 pound of sugar grated on a lemon very finely
3. Add this to the potatoes
4. Melt 1/4 pound of butter in a bowl and add the above mixture with 6 egg yolks and half a Schoppen of sour cream
5. Stir everything together for a quarter hour
6. Fold in the snow from the 6 egg whites
7. Let the soufflé rise golden in a buttered bowl and sprinkle sugar on top
