## Ingredients

- 250 g fresh or dried plums
- 100 g bacon
- 1/2 kg (1 lb) potatoes
- 1 l water
- 40 g sugar
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the plums according to instructions.
2. Prepare as for Pears with Bacon.
