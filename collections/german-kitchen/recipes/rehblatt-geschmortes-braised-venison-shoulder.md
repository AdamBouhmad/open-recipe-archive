## Ingredients

- 1 large venison shoulder
- 1 medium-sized onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 spice dose
- 1/4 l broth, milk or water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 30 g bread crust
- 50 g margarine
- 30 g bacon
- 5 g salt

## Directions

1. The venison shoulder is freed as much as possible from the bones (except the leg, which is only shortened), beaten, washed, skinned, drawn together with needle and thread into good shape, larded and salted. The bones are broken into small pieces.
2. The bones are placed in the braising pot after the shoulder is browned in hot fat, add onion, carrot slices, spices and bread crust, gradually pour vinegar and broth or milk or water after browning and braise the shoulder slowly in 1 1/2-2 hours under frequent basting until soft. Strain the sauce through a sieve, coat the finished shoulder with sauce and serve the remaining sauce on the side.
