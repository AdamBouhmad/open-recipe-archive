## Ingredients

- 2 spoons flour
- lard
- cold water
- salt
- nutmeg
- bread

## Directions

1. Roast 2 spoons of flour yellow in hot lard in a pan
2. Extinguish it with cold water
3. Stir it smooth
4. Pour on as much water as needed
5. Add salt and nutmeg
6. Cut bread into small cubes and let it come to a boil, or cut the pieces directly into the serving dish and let the broth run through a sieve over them
