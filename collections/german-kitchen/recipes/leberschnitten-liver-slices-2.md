## Ingredients

- one calf's liver
- half a pound of cooked rice
- one ladle full of meat broth
- half a pound of beef kidney fat
- 6 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace

## Directions

1. Skin the calf's liver and remove the veins
2. Cook it for one hour in meat broth
3. When cooled, grate it on a grater
4. Cook the grated liver together with half a pound of cooked rice in a casserole and one ladle full of meat broth until half soft (it must not have broth)
5. Add half a pound of beef kidney fat, finely chopped, 6 eggs, salt, nutmeg, and mace
6. Stir everything together again
7. Bind the mixture in a damp cloth
8. Cook for 4 hours in water
9. When slightly cooled, cut into slices and add to soup à la Reine
