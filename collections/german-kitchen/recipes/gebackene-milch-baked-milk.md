## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/2 measures milk
- sugar
- 1 lemon
- 3 spoonfuls flour
- hot lard
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir together 5 whole eggs, 5 egg yolks, and 1/2 measures milk well
2. Take sugar on which a lemon has been grated
3. Roast 3 spoonfuls flour in hot lard until light yellow
4. Pour the milk with the eggs to it
5. Cook everything until it becomes thick, stirring constantly
6. Should the lard cook out, it must be skimmed off
7. When the porridge is on the plate, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on it
