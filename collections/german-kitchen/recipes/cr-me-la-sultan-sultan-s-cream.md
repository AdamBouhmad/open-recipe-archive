## Ingredients

- 1 quarter pound sugar
- half a glass water
- half a maas milk
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 6 loth sugar rubbed on a lemon
- 3 fingers full flour
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the quarter pound sugar and half glass water on the fire until the sugar is dark yellow
2. Pour half a maas milk to it, add one piece whole cinnamon, and let it cook together
3. Take 6 loth sugar which has been rubbed on a lemon, 3 fingers full flour, and 8 egg yolks, and stir slowly into the cooked milk
4. Let everything on the fire come to a boil once more
5. Press the cream through a fine sieve onto a dish that will be served at table
6. Beat the 8 egg whites into stiff snow, make a wreath of it around the cream, place a round ball in the middle
7. Sprinkle coarsely pounded sugar over it, place it in an oven, and let it become a nice yellow
8. Serve the cream warm
