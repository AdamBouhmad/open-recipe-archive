## Ingredients

- half a Vierling finely grated sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 tablespoonful water
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir into half a Vierling finely grated sugar, 1 egg white and 1 tablespoonful water for half an hour so much flour that it becomes as thick as pap.
2. Coat a tile with butter.
3. Fill the stirred mixture into it.
4. Place it in an oven or between coals and bake it a nice yellow.
