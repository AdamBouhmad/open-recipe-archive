## Ingredients

- 250 g apples (sour)
- 250 g flour
- 15 g margarine
- 1-1/2 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon sugar
- 1 knife-tip baking powder
- 1/8 l skim milk (scant)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the core and cut into cubes.
2. Make a good dumpling dough from the remaining ingredients, mixing in the apple cubes last. Dumplings are spooned from the dough, either placed on the dish to be prepared and covered tightly to steam until cooked, or boiled in salted water.
