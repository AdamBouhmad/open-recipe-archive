## Ingredients

- flour
- egg whites
- salt
- butter
- milk
- sour cream
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take flour as needed on a noodle board, beat in 3 egg whites with one knife-tip of salt, egg-sized butter and milk, until you can work the dough.
2. Roll it out by hand as thin as a quill feather and one ell long.
3. Heat milk in a pan, add a large piece of butter along with the noodles and let them cook together.
4. They should have some broth; you can also add sour cream along with sugar and cinnamon.
