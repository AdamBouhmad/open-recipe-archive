## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 half maas wine vinegar
- 4 bay leaves
- 6 cloves
- 6 coarsely ground peppercorns
- 4 loth brown mustard flour
- 4 loth yellow mustard flour

## Directions

1. Cut 2 onions and 2 garlic cloves very small
2. Pour 1 half maas wine vinegar in a pan
3. Add 4 bay leaves, 6 cloves, 6 coarsely ground peppercorns
4. Let it cook for half a quarter hour
5. Take 4 loth brown and as much yellow mustard flour in a bowl
6. Remove the vinegar from the fire
7. Pull it through a hair sieve into the flour
8. Stir the mustard for half an hour as this makes it finer
9. Fill it in a vessel as desired
10. Bind it up and serve it with beef
