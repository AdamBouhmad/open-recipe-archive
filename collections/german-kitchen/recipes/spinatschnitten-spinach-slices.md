## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 handfuls of spinach
- salt
- 2 rolls
- one cleaned calf's brain
- butter
- 4 loth of melted butter
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch 3 to 4 handfuls of spinach in salted water
2. Pour into a sieve and pour cold water over it
3. Press it firmly dry
4. Grate 2 rolls, soak them, press them out and add to the spinach
5. Braise one cleaned calf's brain in butter
6. Beat 4 loth of melted butter and 5 eggs with everything for half an hour
7. Cook this mixture in a tin mold brushed with butter in a steam pot
8. When cooled, cut into slices and add to soup
