## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt water
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Using a cutter, make small round potatoes of even size
2. Boil them in salted water until half-cooked
3. Pour into a colander
4. Fry in a pan with hot lard until golden yellow, but do not put too many in the pan at once
5. These potatoes are used to garnish vegetables, such as cabbage and spinach
