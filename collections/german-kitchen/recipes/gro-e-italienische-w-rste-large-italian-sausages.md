## Ingredients

- all intestines from one large fat pig
- salt
- pepper
- cloves (half-crushed)
- mace
- sweet caraway seed

## Directions

1. Take all intestines from a large fat pig; set aside the large fat intestine
2. Cut the remaining intestines into small pieces and salt them well
3. Let them lie in salt and drain the liquid they release themselves
4. Remove them from the salt water
5. Add pepper, half-crushed cloves, a little mace and sweet caraway seed
6. Fill the mixture very firmly into the fat intestine
7. Tie it off and salt it again
8. Hang it in a dry place with air circulation until quite dry
9. Afterwards these sausages can be simmered with green vegetables or alone, or also grilled
