## Ingredients

- Hare head and feet
- Hare breast
- Onions, sliced
- Lemons
- Bay leaves
- Juniper berries
- Meat broth
- Salt and spices to taste
- Boiling vinegar with onions (if hare has not been marinated)

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and feet of the hare; cut the breast not too close to the saddle so it has a nice shape.
2. Skin the roast, lard it, and place it in a roasting pan with sliced onions, lemons, bay leaves, and juniper berries along with a spoonful of meat broth.
3. Roast until beautifully golden brown; add salt and spices to taste.
4. Note: If the game has not been marinated, it must be poured over with boiling vinegar containing onions.
