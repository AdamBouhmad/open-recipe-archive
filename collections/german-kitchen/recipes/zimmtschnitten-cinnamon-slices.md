## Ingredients

- yeast dough
- 3 loth finely powdered sugar
- 3 knife points cinnamon
- eggs for brushing
- sugar and cinnamon for sprinkling
- 1/2 pound finely powdered sugar
- 1 loth powdered cinnamon
- 1 whole grated nutmeg
- 1 knife point cardamom

## Directions

1. Make a not very strong yeast dough as previously described
2. Let it rise, take 3 loth finely powdered sugar and 3 knife points cinnamon
3. Work the dough with this
4. Let it rise until ripe
5. Make 3 sausages on a noodle board
6. Place them on a black flour-dusted sheet, let them rise once more in warmth
7. Brush with eggs, scatter sugar and cinnamon on top, and bake until beautifully golden
8. Let them cool
9. Cut them into slices 2 knife-back thicknesses thick
10. Sprinkle each with a mixture of 1/2 pound finely powdered sugar, 1 loth powdered cinnamon, 1 whole grated nutmeg, and 1 knife point cardamom, and rub in well
11. Layer them all on top of each other, let sit for a quarter hour
12. Brush again, but only very lightly, with the above-described mixture
13. Place on a black sheet with paper on it and dry in a cooled oven
14. The slices can be served for dessert or with cold punch
