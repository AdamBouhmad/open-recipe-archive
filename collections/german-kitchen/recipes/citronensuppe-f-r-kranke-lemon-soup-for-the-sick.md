## Ingredients

- 4 fresh egg yolks
- A piece of sugar rubbed on a lemon rind
- Juice from 2 lemons
- 1 schoppen of borage water
- 1 schoppen of black cherry water
- Wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks in a pot
2. Add the sugar rubbed on lemon rind and the juice from 2 lemons
3. Add 1 schoppen of borage water and 1 schoppen of black cherry water
4. Heat on a coal fire until it begins to boil
5. Wine may be added if the illness permits
