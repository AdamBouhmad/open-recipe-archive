## Ingredients

- a strong handful of tender tarragon leaves
- good wine vinegar
- 6 pieces pimpernelle
- 6 shallot onions
- 8 cloves
- 8 white peppercorns
- a piece of whole ginger
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- some lemon

## Directions

1. Take a strong handful of tender tarragon leaves and pour good wine vinegar over them together with 6 pieces pimpernelle, 6 shallot onions, 8 cloves, 8 white peppercorns, a piece of whole ginger, a piece of whole cinnamon and some lemon in a clean container
2. Bind the container firmly shut
3. Set it in the sun for 14 days or 3 weeks, or otherwise in a warm place
4. After 3 weeks the vinegar is excellently good
5. Fill it into bottles, seal these firmly and the vinegar can be used for sauces and for salad
