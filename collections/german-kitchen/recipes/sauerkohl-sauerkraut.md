## Ingredients

- 1 small head cabbage or 1/2 kg (1 pound) sauerkraut
- 50 g pork or goose fat or lard
- 15 g onions
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 8 g salt
- 5 g flour
- 1 pinch pepper
- 5 g sugar
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Prepare fresh cabbage as for red cabbage. Loosen sauerkraut with a fork while washing it.
2. In an earthenware pot in which the fat with an onion has been brought to a boil, pour the cabbage over with salt water and some fresh vinegar and braise it covered for several hours.
3. Whisk a teaspoon of the fat with flour, mix with vinegar and pepper, pour it over the cabbage, and cook it through.
4. The cabbage must look completely white and have a beautiful sour taste, which is only subdued by sugar.
5. The cabbage must be white and have a nice sour flavor, which is only tempered by sugar.
