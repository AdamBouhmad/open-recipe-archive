## Ingredients

- 2 pounds grated black bread
- 2 pounds grated brown Nuremberg gingerbread
- 3 knife points of ground cloves
- 1 loth ground cinnamon
- Cardamom for 6 kreuzer
- Finely ground cubeb pepper
- 1/2 schoppen honey
- Flour

## Directions

1. Toast the grated black bread on a dark baking sheet in the oven until golden
2. Grate the Nuremberg gingerbread on a grater
3. Mix bread, grated gingerbread, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, cubeb pepper, and honey in a bowl to form a stiff dough
4. Let dough rest covered
5. Make a dough on a floured noodle board like cut noodles
6. Roll it out knife-back thick
7. Place a spoonful of the bread mixture on the dough, leaving a finger's width empty around it
8. Fold the dough over and seal
9. Cut into round fritters with a pastry wheel
10. Place on a dark baking sheet
11. Dry in the oven; they should remain completely white
