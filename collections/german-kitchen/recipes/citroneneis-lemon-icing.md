## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- one quarter pound sugar
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of one lemon on one quarter pound sugar
2. Finely pound and sieve the remaining sugar
3. Press the juice of the lemon into it
4. Beat one egg white to snow
5. Stir it together for a quarter hour and use as desired
