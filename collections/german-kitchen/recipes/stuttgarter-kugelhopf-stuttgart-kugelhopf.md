## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- 3 spoons of good beer yeast
- half a schoppen of cream
- one quarter pound of butter
- 4 egg yolks
- one loth of ground sugar
- one knife point of salt
- lukewarm milk (as needed)
- 4 loths of melted butter
- 10 loths of large and small raisins

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour in a bowl
2. Make a small dough in the center of the bowl from 3 spoons of good beer yeast and half a schoppen of cream
3. Let it rise
4. Cut in one quarter pound of butter
5. Add 4 egg yolks, one loth of ground sugar, and one knife point of salt
6. Make a good, somewhat firm dough from everything
7. If necessary, add a little more lukewarm milk
8. Work it for a quarter hour
9. Let it rise beautifully
10. When ripe, roll out a little on a pasta board
11. Brush the dough with 4 loths of melted butter
12. Sprinkle 10 loths of large and small raisins over it
13. Roll it up like a sausage
14. Brush a Turkish mold with butter
15. Place the sausage in it
16. Let it rise well
17. Bake
