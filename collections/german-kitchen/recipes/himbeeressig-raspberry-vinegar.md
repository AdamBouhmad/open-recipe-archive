## Ingredients

- 2 kg (4 Pfd.) raspberries
- 1 l wine vinegar
- 1-1/2 kg (3 Pfd.) sugar
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Preparation: Crush the raspberries with a spoon, cover them for 12 hours with the wine vinegar, and strain through a cloth.
2. Preparation: Clarify the sugar, cook it into a syrup, cook it with the vinegar for another 20 minutes, and fill into bottles. Cork and seal the bottles, and the raspberry vinegar is mixed with fresh water or seltzer water for use.
