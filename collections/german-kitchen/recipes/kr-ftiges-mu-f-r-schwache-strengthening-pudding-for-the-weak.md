## Ingredients

- milk bread or roll slices, baked
- cinnamon
- rose water
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook baked milk bread or roll slices with cinnamon, rose water and a small piece of butter.
2. Stir smooth with rose water.
3. Sprinkle cinnamon over when the pudding is arranged.
