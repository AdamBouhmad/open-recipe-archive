## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon cocoa
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 3/8 liter water or milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1 tablespoon cocoa and 1 teaspoon sugar with 1 tablespoon water
2. Boil with the entire liquid (3/8 liter water or milk per 1 tablespoon cocoa) for 5 minutes
