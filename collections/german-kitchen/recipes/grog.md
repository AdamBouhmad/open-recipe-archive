## Ingredients

- 1/8 l cognac, arrack or rum
- 80 g sugar
- 3/4 l boiling water

## Directions

1. Sugar and cognac are quickly mixed with the boiling water and served immediately. The strength and sweetness of the drink are to be changed according to taste.
