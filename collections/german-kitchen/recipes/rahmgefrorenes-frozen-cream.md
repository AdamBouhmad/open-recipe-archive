## Ingredients

- 1/2 Maas thick cream
- 1/2 Maas raspberries
- 8 Loth sugar (or more to taste)

## Directions

1. Beat the cream very firmly with a small whisk
2. Lay it on a hair sieve
3. Force the raspberries through the sieve
4. Add 8 Loth of sugar or as much as is needed until they are sweet enough
5. Mix the whipped cream underneath
6. Place it in the freezing bucket, but do not churn it
