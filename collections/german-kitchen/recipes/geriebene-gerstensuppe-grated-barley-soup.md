## Ingredients

- 1 to 2 eggs
- flour
- meat broth
- chives

## Directions

1. Make a firm dough from 1 to 2 eggs and flour
2. Grate this on a grater
3. As soon as the grated dough has dried a little, sprinkle it into simmering meat broth while constantly stirring
4. When serving, sprinkle chives on top
