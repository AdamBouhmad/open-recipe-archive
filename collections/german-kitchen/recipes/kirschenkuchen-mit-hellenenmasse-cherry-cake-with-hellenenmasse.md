## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 pound flour
- 9 egg whites
- 1 pound pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter until creamy
2. Stir in 9 egg yolks and the yellow of 1/2 lemon rubbed on sugar
3. Stir in 1/2 pound flour and the snow from 9 egg whites
4. Coat a plafond with butter and put the mass into it
5. Lay 1 pound pitted cherries on top and press gently into the mass with a finger
6. Bake in a roasting oven
