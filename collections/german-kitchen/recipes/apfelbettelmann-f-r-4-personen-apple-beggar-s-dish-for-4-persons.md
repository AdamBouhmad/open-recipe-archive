## Ingredients

- 250 g grated bread
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 ground cloves
- 1/2 grated lemon
- 40 g sugar
- 1/2 kg apples
- 65 g sugar
- 4 tablespoons water
- 125 g lard
- 30 g currants
- 20 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate the bread, mix with the spices and 40 g sugar; peel the apples and cut into pieces, clean the currants and let them soak.
2. Stew the apples with 65 g sugar in a little water. Then brown half of the lard in a pan, press half of the bread mixture onto it with a spoon, place the apple compote and currants over it, sprinkle the other half of bread over and press down. After the dish has baked slowly for 1/4 hour, drizzle the remaining lard over it, turn the dish like a pancake, bake it for another 1/4 hour over low heat and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon after serving.
