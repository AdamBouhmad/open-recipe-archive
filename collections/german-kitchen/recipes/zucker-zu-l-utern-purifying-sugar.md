## Ingredients

- half a pound sugar
- half a schoppen water
- one egg white

## Directions

1. Take half a pound sugar in a brass pan and break it into 3 to 4 pieces
2. Add half a schoppen water and let it boil until it throws bubbles
3. Beat one egg white to snow, add it under the sugar, and add a spoonful of water if the sugar wants to rise
4. When the sugar is quite clear, wet a cloth that is not very tight, pour the sugar into it and let it run through
5. Then preserve it
