## Ingredients

- potatoes (boiled the day before)
- 1 measure flour
- good beer yeast
- milk
- 1 pound butter
- 1/2 schoppen thick sour cream
- chives
- salt
- nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- lard

## Directions

1. Grate potatoes that were boiled the day before
2. Make a pre-dough or yeast dough from 1 measure flour, good beer yeast, and milk, and let it stand in moderate warmth
3. Melt 1 pound butter in a bowl and add the potatoes
4. Pour in 1/2 schoppen thick sour cream and stir well together
5. Chives can be added; also salt and nutmeg
6. Knead everything together with the yeast dough and 3 egg yolks
7. When the dough is ripe, form cakes and fry in lard
