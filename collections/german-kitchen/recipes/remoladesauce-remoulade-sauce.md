## Ingredients

- some peeled shallots
- 2 spoonfuls of capers
- 4 clean washed anchovies
- the yolk of 2 hard-boiled eggs
- one spoonful of Provençal oil
- some tarragon
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Mince the peeled shallots, capers, anchovies, and hard-boiled egg yolks together
2. Take one spoonful of Provençal oil, some tarragon, vinegar, pepper, and salt to taste
3. Stir everything well together
4. Serve with any meat
