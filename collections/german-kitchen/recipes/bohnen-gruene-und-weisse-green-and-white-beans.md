## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg green beans
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 100 g white beans
- 1 liter water
- 3/4 liter mutton broth
- 1 sprig pepper herb
- 1 scant tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Soak white, dried beans overnight.
2. The next morning cook them with cold water until soft and pour them into the green bean vegetable prepared according to the previous recipe.
