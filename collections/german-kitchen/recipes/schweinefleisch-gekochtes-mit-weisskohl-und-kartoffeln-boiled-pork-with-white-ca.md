## Ingredients

- 200 g Schweinekamm (pork neck)
- 8 g Salz (salt)
- 3/4 l Wasser (water)
- 3/4 kg Weisskohl (white cabbage)
- Wasser und 1 Prise Salz (water and 1 pinch salt)
- 250 g geschälte Kartoffeln (peeled potatoes)
- Wasser und 1 Prise Salz (water and 1 pinch salt)
- 1 Gewürzdosis (1 spice dose)
- 10 g Fett (fat)
- 10 g Mehl (flour)
- Salz und Pfeffer (salt and pepper)

## Directions

1. Wash and pound the meat; put 3/4 l water with 8 g salt on the stove to heat.
2. Remove the outer leaves and thick ribs from the cabbage; after dividing the head into 8 or 16 pieces, blanch in salted water and drain; cook the potatoes whole or cut almost until done.
3. Place the meat in the boiling salted water; after it begins to cook, continue as directed in the preparation.
