## Ingredients

- red beets
- potatoes
- beans
- bean kernels
- endive
- head lettuce
- ox mouth
- pickled cucumbers
- celery
- yellow beets
- soft corn
- yolk of 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 spoonfuls of Provençal oil
- good vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put red beets, potatoes, beans, bean kernels, endive, head lettuce, ox mouth, pickled cucumbers, celery, yellow beets, and soft corn in a bowl.
2. Press the yolk of 3 hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve and dress the salad with 3 spoonfuls of Provençal oil, some good vinegar, salt and pepper.
