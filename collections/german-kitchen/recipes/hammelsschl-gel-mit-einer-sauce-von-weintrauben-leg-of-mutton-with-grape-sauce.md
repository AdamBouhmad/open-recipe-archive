## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- butter
- verjus or meat broth

## Directions

1. Roast the leg of mutton 1 to 1.5 hours before serving, basting frequently with butter
2. Place it warm on a dish
3. Boil a little verjus (juice of sour unripe grapes) or a little meat broth in a casserole and pour hot over the leg
4. Alternatively, the leg of mutton can be treated like a quarter of lamb, larded with parsley and served with a sauce hachée, or larded with bacon and served with another sauce
