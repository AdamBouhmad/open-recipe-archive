## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen thick sweet cream
- 6 Loth pounded sugar
- half glass champagne wine

## Directions

1. Mix 6 Loth pounded sugar and half a glass champagne wine into 1 schoppen thick sweet cream that has been lifted with a spoon
2. Whip the whole mixture in a wide pot with a chocolate whisk
3. Bring the foam to a hair sieve with a spoon and continue until everything is done
