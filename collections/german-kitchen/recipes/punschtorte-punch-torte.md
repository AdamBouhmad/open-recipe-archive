## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound butter
- 3/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 18 egg yolks
- egg whites from 18 eggs
- zest of 1 lemon
- 26 loth flour
- 2 tablespoons arrack
- raspberries
- 2 tablespoons arrack
- currants
- 2 tablespoons arrack
- apple preserves
- wafers
- arrack
- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- arrack
- candied fruits

## Directions

1. Stir 3/4 pound butter until foamy
2. Add 3/4 pound finely ground sugar, 18 egg yolks, the beaten whites from the eggs, grated zest of 1 lemon, and 26 loth flour to the butter
3. Fill one tablespoon into 4 plate-sized equal forms and bake them golden yellow, then let cool
4. Mix 2 tablespoons arrack with raspberries in one dish, 2 tablespoons arrack with currants in a second dish, and arrack with apple preserves in a third dish
5. Place a piece of paper on a springform, put one baked small cake on it, spread with raspberries
6. Place the second cake on the first, spread with currants, place the third on the second, spread with apple preserves, place the fourth cake on top
7. Cut the torte into a round shape; if necessary, moisten a piece of wafer with arrack and arrange it nicely around the torte
8. Make an arrack icing: pour arrack into 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar until it is liquid enough to coat the torte, but the icing should not run
9. Pour the icing over the torte and garnish with candied fruits
