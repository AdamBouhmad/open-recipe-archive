## Ingredients

- 250 g white or red cabbage, cut
- 1-1/2 l water
- 5 g salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 pinch sugar
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 5 g salt
- 1 pinch white pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the finely cut cabbage in boiling water to which salt and 1 tablespoon vinegar has been added, using a colander, and let it drain.
2. Mix together oil, sugar, 1 tablespoon vinegar, salt and pepper.
3. Pour the sauce over the cabbage and mix thoroughly.
4. Serve cold with goose roast, etc.
