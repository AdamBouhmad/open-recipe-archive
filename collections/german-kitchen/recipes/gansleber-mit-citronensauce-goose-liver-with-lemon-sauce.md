## Ingredients

- Goose liver
- Butter
- Onion
- Cloves
- Shallots
- Lemon
- Flour
- Wine
- Beef broth

## Directions

1. Soak the goose liver in water beforehand and let it drain on a cloth.
2. Place 4 lots of butter, a cleaned onion studded with 3 cloves, 3 shallots and half a lemon cut into slices in a casserole.
3. Place the liver on top and braise slowly.
4. If the liver is large it needs half an hour.
5. Place the liver on the serving dish.
6. For the sauce: take as much flour as you can grasp firmly with 3 fingers and add to the casserole.
7. Add a glass of wine, juice of a lemon and some beef broth.
8. Let it cook together for a quarter hour.
9. Strain through a fine sieve and pour over the liver.
10. Sprinkle the finely chopped zest of a lemon on top.
