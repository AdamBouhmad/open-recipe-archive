## Ingredients

- 200 g beans or lentils
- 10 g salt
- 1 pinch baking soda
- 1 liter water
- 30 g bacon
- 20 g onions
- 1 spice mixture
- 1/4 liter water
- 1 tablespoon syrup
- 15 g flour
- 1-2 tablespoons wine vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the legumes the evening before; dice the bacon and onions.
2. Cook the beans or lentils in salted water with a pinch of baking soda until soft and pour onto a sieve. Brown the bacon; remove the cracklings and keep warm; soften the onion in the fat, add the flour, stir and brown, fill the sauce with the spices, vinegar, syrup and water, cook 1/2 hour open in an earthen pot; mix the soft legumes with it and heat. When serving, sprinkle the dish with the bacon cracklings.
