## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg (1-1/2 lbs) rutabaga
- 10 g salt
- 1-1/2 l water
- 1/4 l hot broth
- 1/2 spice dose
- 1 sprig marjoram
- 5 g sugar
- salt and pepper to taste
- 10 g fat or margarine
- 5 g flour

## Directions

1. Scrub the rutabagas in water, peel them, and cut into uniform strips.
2. Blanch in boiling salted water uncovered, then drain thoroughly.
3. Cook the rutabagas covered with broth, spice dose, marjoram, sugar, salt and pepper until soft.
4. Make a roux with the fat and flour, cook it with the vegetable broth, and thoroughly shake together with the vegetables.
