## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sweet cream
- 5 tablespoons flour
- meat broth
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter in a bowl until very light
2. Gradually stir in the eggs, sweet cream, and flour
3. Drop this batter by spoonfuls into boiling meat broth
4. Season with salt and nutmeg
