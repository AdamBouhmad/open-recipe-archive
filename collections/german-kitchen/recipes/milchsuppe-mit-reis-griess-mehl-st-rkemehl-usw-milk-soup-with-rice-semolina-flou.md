## Ingredients

- 1 l whole milk
- 1 piece lemon or orange peel
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 6 g salt
- 30 g rice, 40 g semolina, flour or cornstarch

## Directions

1. Preparation: The rice is washed and drained, the semolina is washed, the flour is mixed with liquid.
2. To the boiling milk with spices, sugar and salt, add rice, semolina, flour or cornstarch and let it swell under frequent stirring.
3. Before serving, optionally finish the soup with egg yolk.
4. Optional: Chocolate soup - soften 125 g chocolate in the milk, bring to a boil and finish with 20 g stirred cornstarch; the cinnamon can be omitted.
