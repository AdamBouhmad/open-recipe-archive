## Ingredients

- 1 pike, cleaned and gutted
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- lemon butter
- 1 handful parsley
- 1 lemon
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Rub the cleaned and gutted pike with salt and pepper
2. Let it rest for one hour
3. Make 3 to 4 diagonal cuts in it, then sew the fish shut again and cut out the ears
4. Place it on a grill and frequently baste with melted butter, also pouring hot butter into the ears
5. Turn the fish and baste the other side
6. Roast very carefully so the skin does not stick to the grill
7. For serving, make lemon butter as follows: finely chop 1 handful of parsley, let a piece of butter become hot in a casserole, steam the parsley in it, squeeze the juice of 1 lemon into it, let it come to a boil together, and pour this over the fish
8. Lemon slices can be placed in the cuts
