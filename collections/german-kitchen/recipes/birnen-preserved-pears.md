## Ingredients

- muscat pears
- clove
- cinnamon
- water
- sugar (7/4 pound per 3 pounds pears)

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, remove the core and insert a clove, and whole cinnamon in place of the stem
2. Cook them soft in water, place on a hair sieve and let drain
3. In a brass pan, cook sugar with water from the pears until it spins a thick thread
4. Add the pears and cook as long as hard-boiled eggs
5. Repeat this process for 4 days
6. On the fourth day, fill the pears into a vessel, soak card paper in cherry brandy, place on the pears and bind the vessel tightly closed
