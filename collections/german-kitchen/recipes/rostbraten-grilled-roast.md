## Ingredients

- piece from the rump, hind rib
- salt
- pepper
- lemon butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a piece from the rump (hind rib), remove the rib bone and the shank below.
2. Cut the meat into half-finger-thick slices.
3. Pound them lightly, rub them with salt and pepper.
4. Roast them with lemon butter on the grill until beautifully golden.
5. They will be done in a quarter hour.
6. Place them on a platter and serve with lemon.
