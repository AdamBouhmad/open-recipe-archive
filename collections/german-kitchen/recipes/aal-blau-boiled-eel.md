## Ingredients

- 250 g eel
- 10 g salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1/2 l water
- 30 g onion slices
- 1 spice measure
- 1 tablespoon soup greens
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Divide the killed eel into uniform pieces of desired length. Clean each piece individually by pushing out the entrails with a whisk handle and cleaning it from the inner skin, rub with salt and wash.
2. Pour boiling water mixed with 1/2 tablespoon vinegar over the fish and cook it in a broth of water, soup greens, spices, onion and some vinegar for a good 1/4 hour until done. Arrange the blue eel pieces warm or cold and serve with the eel broth.
