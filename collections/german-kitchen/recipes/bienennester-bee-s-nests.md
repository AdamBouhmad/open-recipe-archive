## Ingredients

- 2 pounds flour
- 3 spoonfuls brewer's yeast
- 1 schoppen milk
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/4 pound small raisins
- 1/4 pound large raisins
- sugar
- good pears
- 1 schoppen wine
- 1 lemon, zest of
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 3 loth sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough with 2 pounds flour, 3 spoonfuls brewer's yeast and 1 schoppen milk, let it rise in warmth
2. Cut in 1/4 pound butter, 2 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs
3. Beat the dough for a quarter hour, let it rise again
4. Dust a cloth with flour, form the dough into noodles as large as a chicken egg
5. Pull them apart on the cloth, brush with butter
6. Fill with 1/4 pound small and 1/4 pound large raisins
7. Roll up the noodles, bake them golden yellow in a buttered dish sprinkled with sugar
8. For the compote: peel good pears, place in hot water in an earthen dish
9. Pour 1 schoppen wine over, add the yellow of 1 lemon, a piece of whole cinnamon and 3 loth sugar
10. Cook with these, and when the pears are soft, serve with the nests
