## Ingredients

- 9 loths of butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 loths of finely ground sugar
- 12 loths of fine flour
- 2 tablespoons of good beer yeast
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 9 loths of butter in a bowl until creamy
2. Gradually stir in 6 egg yolks, 3 loths of finely ground sugar, 12 loths of fine flour, and 2 tablespoons of good beer yeast
3. Fold in the stiffly beaten snow from 6 egg whites
4. Let the dough rise
5. Fill into a kugelhopf mold brushed with butter
6. Bake
