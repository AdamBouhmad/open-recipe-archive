## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 4 Loth sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- flour
- melted butter
- bacon
- coarse pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Press the yolk from 6 hard-boiled eggs through a hair sieve onto a noodle board
2. Mix in 4 Loth sugar, 2 egg yolks, some yolk from 1 lemon, and flour
3. Pour melted butter on and work like a noodle dough
4. Form quarter-long sticks from this
5. Place them on a black sheet brushed with bacon
6. Brush with an egg and coarse pounded sugar
7. Bake golden in the oven
8. Serve with tea
