## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 3/4 pound flour
- 3 loths fine ground sugar
- milk

## Directions

1. Lightly stir 1/2 pound butter
2. Mix with 5 eggs, 3/4 pound flour, and 3 loths fine ground sugar into a dough
3. If too firm, add some milk
4. Roll out the dough on a pastry board and fold over several times
5. Roll out finger thick
6. Bake in a cake pan coated with butter
7. Can be topped with lemon or chocolate ice
