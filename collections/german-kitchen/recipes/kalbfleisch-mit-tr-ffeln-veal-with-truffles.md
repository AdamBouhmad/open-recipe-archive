## Ingredients

- A nice piece of veal from the leg
- Onions
- Lemon
- 3 truffles
- Flour

## Directions

1. Pound a nice piece of veal from the leg well and lard it.
2. Place it in a casserole, add finely cut onions and lemon, cover the casserole and let it braise for half an hour.
3. Cut the rough parts from 3 carefully cleaned truffles, pound one of them in a mortar, mix a little flour with it, cut the remaining 2 truffles into leaves.
4. Add everything to the meat and let it cook together for another quarter hour.
