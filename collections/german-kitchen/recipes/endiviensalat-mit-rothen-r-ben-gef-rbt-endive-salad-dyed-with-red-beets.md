## Ingredients

- beautiful endive
- potatoes
- red beet broth

## Directions

1. Chop the endive finely, place it in lukewarm water.
2. Peel the potatoes and lay them in red beet broth for half an hour.
3. Dress the endive, place it in the bowl, cut the potatoes into wheels and garnish the salad with them as desired.
