## Ingredients

- Boiled cold potatoes
- 1/2 pound lean ham
- 1 soaked and pressed roll
- Finely minced onions
- 1/4 pound butter
- 5 to 6 eggs
- Chives
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate the boiled cold potatoes on a grater
2. Mince the lean ham finely
3. Soak and press the roll
4. Melt 1/4 pound butter in a bowl
5. Mix together the potatoes, ham, roll, finely minced onions, 5 to 6 eggs, chives, salt and nutmeg
6. Bind the mass tightly in a wet cloth
7. Cook it for one hour in salt water
8. Arrange the dumpling on a plate and brown it with butter and bread crumbs
