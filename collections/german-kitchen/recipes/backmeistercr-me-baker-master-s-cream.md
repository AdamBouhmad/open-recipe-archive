## Ingredients

- fine flour
- egg yolks (8)
- sweet cream (half a maas)
- sugar (4 loth)
- lemon (zest)
- fresh butter (3 loth)
- lemon (half, juice)
- sugar (quarter pound)
- water (half a glass)

## Directions

1. Take as much fine flour as can be stirred with 8 egg yolks
2. Pour half a maas sweet cream to it
3. Add 4 loth sugar rubbed from a lemon peel and 3 loth fresh butter
4. Stir the cream on the fire
5. Press the juice of half a lemon to it when removed from the fire
6. Cook a quarter pound sugar with half a glass of water to a caramel
7. Pour the cream into it
8. Stir together so it remains somewhat marbled (white and brown)
