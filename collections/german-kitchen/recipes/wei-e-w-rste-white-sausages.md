## Ingredients

- Ingredients as in recipe 369
- White and soft-boiled offal
- Onions
- Butter
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Make the sausages as in recipe 369, but instead of sweetbreads and udder use a beautiful white and properly soft-boiled offal, but no butter except that in which the onions have been fried
2. Add a little chopped marjoram
3. Serve them warm
