## Ingredients

- thick muscles of a roe deer leg
- bacon
- onions
- bay leaves
- pepper
- salt
- 1 pound pork forcemeat
- half pound tender veal
- half pound lean bacon
- 2 cleaned herrings
- yellow of 1 lemon
- 4 shallot onions
- 2 ordinary chopped and braised onions
- 1 peeled cross roll
- fine spices
- 2 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls chopped capers
- 12 peeled olives
- quarter pound cleaned truffles

## Directions

1. Make beautiful pieces from the thick muscles of a roe deer leg.
2. Beat them and lard them with bacon.
3. Braise them in a covered saucepan with finely cut onions, bay leaves, pepper, and salt.
4. When the meat is golden, let it cool on a plate.
5. Skin the remaining meat from the roe deer leg and pound it with 1 pound pork forcemeat, half pound tender veal, half pound lean bacon, 2 cleaned herrings, and the yellow of 1 lemon into a dough.
6. Add 4 shallot onions and 2 ordinary chopped and braised onions.
7. Soak 1 peeled cross roll, press it out, and add it along with salt and all kinds of fine spices to the meat.
8. Rub everything together on a grater to a foam.
9. Mix 2 eggs, 2 spoonfuls chopped capers, 12 peeled olives, and quarter pound cleaned truffles cut into pieces.
