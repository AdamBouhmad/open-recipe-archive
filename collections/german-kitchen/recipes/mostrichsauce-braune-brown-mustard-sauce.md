## Ingredients

- 20 g fat
- 20 g onions
- 20 g flour
- 1 spice dose
- 1 l broth or water
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 pinch salt and pepper
- 2 drops food coloring

## Directions

1. Weigh the onions.
2. Brown the onions in fat, then sweat them with the flour until light brown. Add the spice and liquid and cook a smooth sauce.
3. Mix the mustard with sugar and vinegar along with a few spoonfuls of the finished sauce so it can blend, then stir it into the whole sauce where it comes to a boil.
4. Strain the sauce through a sieve and season with salt, pepper, and food coloring. Heat once more.
5. This mustard sauce is used for hard eggs (mustard eggs) and also for beef. It can be improved by adding leftover gravy sauce.
