## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound sweet almonds
- 6 to 8 bitter almonds
- rose water
- 2 lemon peels
- 3 dried apricots
- 3 peeled, sliced apples
- 1/4 pound beef marrow
- 10 eggs
- 4 loths ground sugar
- 1 schoppen sweet cream

## Directions

1. Grind 1/4 pound sweet almonds with 6 to 8 bitter almonds finely with rose water in a mortar
2. Finely chop the peels of 2 lemons
3. Add 3 sliced dried apricots and 3 peeled, sliced apples
4. Mix in 1/4 pound beef marrow, 10 eggs, 4 loths ground sugar, and 1 schoppen sweet cream
5. Line a cake pan with oblaten
6. Spread the mass on it
7. Bake in the oven
