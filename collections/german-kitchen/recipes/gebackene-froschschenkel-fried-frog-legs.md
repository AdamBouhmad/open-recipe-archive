## Ingredients

- frog legs
- salt
- flour
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Wash the frog legs clean
2. Cut off the front joints
3. Salt the remaining legs
4. Clean them well so they lie nicely on the plate
5. Dip them in flour, then in eggs, then in grated bread
6. Fry them in lard
7. Do not put too many in the pan at once, otherwise they will not roast properly
