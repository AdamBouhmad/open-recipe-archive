## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- 2 spoons of beer yeast
- half a schoppen of lukewarm milk
- 10 loths of butter
- one quarter pound of clean small raisins
- 2 loths of large pitted raisins, cut in half
- one loth of ground sugar
- one knife point of salt
- 4 blanched bitter almonds, coarsely ground
- 4 whole eggs

## Directions

1. In a bowl, make a small dough with one pound of flour, 2 spoons of beer yeast, and half a schoppen of lukewarm milk
2. Let it rise in gentle warmth
3. Cut in 10 loths of butter
4. Add one quarter pound of clean small raisins, 2 loths of large pitted raisins cut in half, one loth of ground sugar, one knife point of salt, 4 blanched bitter almonds coarsely ground, and 4 whole eggs
5. Work the dough together with all ingredients
6. Beat for a quarter hour
7. Brush a kugelhopf mold with butter
8. Sprinkle a little sugar into it
9. Fill in the mass
10. Let the kugelhopf rise a little more in gentle warmth
11. Bake in the oven until golden yellow
