## Ingredients

- 1 stale biscuit
- butter
- raisins
- preserved nuts
- apricots
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- 2 loth blanched almonds
- milk
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- 2 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1/2 glass maraschino

## Directions

1. Cut 1 stale biscuit into finger-length pieces.
2. Grease a metal mold with butter and lay alternating layers: biscuit and raisins, biscuit and preserved nuts, biscuit and apricots, continuing until the form is full but leaving a finger's breadth empty at the top.
3. Mix 2 egg yolks with 1 schoppen sweet cream and pour over the mixture.
4. Seal the cover tightly and boil the pudding for half an hour.
5. For the sauce: pound 2 loth blanched almonds very fine with milk, add 1 schoppen sweet cream and 2 loth sugar grated from a lemon, bring to a boil, add 1/2 glass maraschino, and pour over the pudding.
