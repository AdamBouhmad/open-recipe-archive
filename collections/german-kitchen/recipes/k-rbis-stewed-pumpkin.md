## Ingredients

- 250 g peeled pumpkin pieces
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 clove
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 g cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. The pumpkin is peeled, cut into pleasing pieces, washed and drained. The cornstarch is dissolved.
2. The pumpkin pieces are stewed covered with vinegar, clove and sugar until soft, stirred with the cornstarch dissolved in cold water, brought to a boil and arranged for serving.
