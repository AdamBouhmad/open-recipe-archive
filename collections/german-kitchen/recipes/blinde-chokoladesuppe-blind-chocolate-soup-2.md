## Ingredients

- Butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Good milk
- 3 Loth sugar
- Ground cinnamon
- Bread

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole
2. Roast 2 tablespoons of flour in it until light yellow
3. Stir smooth with good milk
4. Add more milk as needed
5. Add 3 Loth sugar and ground cinnamon
6. Let it come to a boil
7. Cut bread into fine pieces for dunking and arrange the soup over the dunked slices
