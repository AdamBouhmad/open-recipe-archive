## Ingredients

- 3 quarter-pound sugar
- 1 pound peeled, ground almonds
- 14 egg yolks
- zest of 2 lemons, grated on a grater
- 1 Loth ground cinnamon
- 6 Loth grated bread
- snow from 6 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix 3 quarter-pound of sugar, 1 pound of peeled, ground almonds, 14 egg yolks, and the grated zest of 2 lemons in a bowl, stirring strongly for half an hour
2. Add 1 Loth of ground cinnamon
3. Stir everything together again
4. Add 6 Loth of grated bread and the snow from 6 egg whites
5. Fill the mixture into a buttered baking sheet
6. Bake the cake in the oven
