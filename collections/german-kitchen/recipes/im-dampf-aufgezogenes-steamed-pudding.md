## Ingredients

- 3 handfuls fine flour
- 1 schoppen milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons maraschino
- a handful of finely ground sugar
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir 3 handfuls fine flour smooth with 1 schoppen milk and cook to a thick porridge, let it cool
2. Stir in 6 egg yolks, 4 tablespoons maraschino, a handful of finely ground sugar and the snow of 6 egg whites, stir the mass well together
3. Fill it into a dish suitable for table service, place this in a dish with boiling water, cover it tightly, place glowing coals on the lid and serve the dish after it has cooked for half an hour
