## Ingredients

- 2 large herrings
- 6 to 8 potatoes
- 4 peeled Borsdorfer apples
- 2 large spoonfuls of capers
- 2 large spoonfuls of olives
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 clean, soft-boiled celery roots
- endive
- vinegar
- Provençal oil
- red beets

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, remove the bones, cut them into pieces.
2. Cut 6 to 8 potatoes into square pieces and 4 peeled Borsdorfer apples into pieces.
3. Stone the olives.
4. Cut 2 clean, soft-boiled celery roots.
5. Combine all with endive in a bowl and dress with vinegar and Provençal oil.
6. Cut the hard-boiled eggs into long pieces, arrange the salad beautifully from the bowl with herring pieces and make a beautiful star of red beets in the middle; from hard-boiled eggs make the white and yolk into the star and from capers the green.
