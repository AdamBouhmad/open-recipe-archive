## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- half pound flour
- 4 spoonfuls sugar
- 3 spoonfuls wine
- 1 egg yolk
- ground almonds

## Directions

1. Make a dough from half pound butter, half pound flour, 4 spoonfuls sugar, 3 spoonfuls wine and one egg yolk on the pastry board
2. Roll it out strong knife-back thick
3. Cut out figures using all forms of small metal molds
4. Place them on a black baking sheet
5. Brush them with egg
6. Sprinkle them with ground almonds and bake
