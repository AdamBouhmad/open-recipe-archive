## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp finely chopped soup vegetables (plenty of parsley root)
- 1 small onion
- 1/2 spice mixture
- 1 pinch pepper
- 30 g fat or margarine
- 30 g flour
- 1/2 tbsp chopped dill
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp skim milk
- 15 g salt
- 1/2 kg potatoes
- 1/2 l water

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and cut the soup vegetables; peel the onion and cut into cubes; wash the parsley and dill and chop them; wash the potatoes and put them in a pot, add water and salt.
2. Cook the soup vegetables, spice mixture, salt and water in a covered pot for 20 minutes to 1/2 hour. Cook the potatoes until done, drain, peel and cut into slices.
3. Brown the onion cubes in the fat until golden, add the flour and stir until cooked, pour in the vegetable water, cook for 1/4 hour, strain through a sieve, season with parsley, dill, pepper, salt and milk, mix with the potatoes, and serve the dish in a bowl.
4. The same sauce can be prepared for green fish; the fish is cooked first in the vegetable water and later placed in the sauce. Double the amount of liquid poured over the roux makes a good soup, fish soup, which can be made even more delicious with any chopped herbs. An egg yolk beaten into the finished soup makes it richer.
