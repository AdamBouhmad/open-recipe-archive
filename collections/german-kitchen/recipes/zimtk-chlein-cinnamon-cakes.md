## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- sugar
- 1 loth cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites into a stiff snow
2. Stir sugar and cinnamon underneath
3. Brush a black baking sheet with wax
4. Place spoonfuls of the mixture the size of a hazelnut on the sheet
5. Bake in a cooled oven
