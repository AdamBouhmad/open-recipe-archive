## Ingredients

- 2 Loth blanched almonds
- 3 Loth sugar
- Cinnamon
- 1 half Maas milk
- Fresh bread

## Directions

1. Take 2 Loth blanched almonds, pound them very finely
2. Stir them together with 3 Loth sugar and some cinnamon with half a Maas milk in a brass pan
3. Let it come to a boil together
4. Cut fresh bread into fine slices and serve the milk over them
