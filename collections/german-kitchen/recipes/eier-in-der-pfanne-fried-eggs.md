## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 15 g margarine
- 1 pinch white pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter on a plate or enameled dish
2. Carefully open the fresh eggs, pour them onto the plate, and sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Let them coagulate slowly
4. The egg white around the yolk should be white, but still translucent over the yolk
5. Use a glass to remove the irregular egg white parts
6. Serve the fried eggs
