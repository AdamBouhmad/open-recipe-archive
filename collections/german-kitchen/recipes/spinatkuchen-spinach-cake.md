## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls spinach
- 2 mouth-sized pieces bread
- boiling milk
- 4 egg yolks
- salt, 1 knife point
- 2 loth sugar
- 1/2 nutmeg, grated
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Chop 2 handfuls spinach very finely
2. Scald 2 pieces of bread with boiling milk, then drain the milk
3. Add the spinach to the bread
4. Beat 4 egg yolks, 1 knife point salt, 2 loth sugar and 1/2 grated nutmeg with the spinach mixture until foamy
5. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Butter a baking sheet, fill with the mixture and bake the cake in the oven
