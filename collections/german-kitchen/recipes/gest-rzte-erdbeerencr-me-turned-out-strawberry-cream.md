## Ingredients

- 3 schoppen strawberries
- 2 loth isinglass
- 1/2 maas cream
- sugar
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Press 3 schoppen strawberries through a hair sieve.
2. Dissolve 2 loth isinglass in water.
3. Beat half a maas of cream as stiff as egg snow.
4. Let it drain on a hair sieve.
5. Pour the isinglass into the strawberry pulp.
6. Add as many spoonfuls of sugar as you have pulp.
7. Stir the cream on ice until it begins to become gelatinous.
8. Brush a jelly mold with almond oil.
9. Slowly fold the whipped cream and snow from 2 egg whites into the mixture.
10. Pour the cream into the mold.
11. Place it in the cellar or on ice.
12. Carefully turn the cream out onto a plate and garnish beautifully with strawberries.
