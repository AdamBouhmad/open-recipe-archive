## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound bacon
- 2 kreuzer worth white bread
- 4 eggs
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- 2 small cooking spoons flour
- Hot meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut 1/2 pound bacon into small cubes and place half in a bowl
2. Let the other half melt in a casserole and roast 2 kreuzer worth white bread cut into cubes in it
3. Cut another 2 kreuzer worth white bread into cubes
4. Dampen them with hot meat broth
5. Mix together the roasted bread, 4 eggs, salt, nutmeg and 2 small cooking spoons flour
6. Bind everything in a wet cloth
7. Cook the dumpling for one hour
8. Serve it with braised veal
