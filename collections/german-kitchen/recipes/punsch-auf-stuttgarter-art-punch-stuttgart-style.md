## Ingredients

- 6 large lemons
- 6 oranges
- One Schoppen water
- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 2 bottles white Burgundy wine
- 2 Schoppen Arak

## Directions

1. Press the juice from 6 large lemons and 6 oranges
2. In a brass pan, boil one Schoppen water and 1.5 pounds sugar together until the sugar spins threads
3. Skim off the black foam carefully
4. Pour the pressed juice through a hair sieve onto the sugar
5. Let it boil together again
6. Add 2 bottles of white Burgundy wine
7. Let it heat in the pan until it begins to boil
8. Pour everything into the bowl
9. Add 2 Schoppen Arak
10. If not strong enough, strengthen it further with Arak
