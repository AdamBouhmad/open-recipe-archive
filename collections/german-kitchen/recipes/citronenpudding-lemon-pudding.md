## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound blanched almonds
- egg white or rose water
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound finely ground sugar
- 7 egg yolks
- zest of 1 whole lemon, grated
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 loth Geigenmehl
- 2 knife-points fine flour
- snow from 5 egg whites
- butter dough
- 1 schoppen wine
- 4 loth sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 knife-points fine flour
- cold water
- 1/2 glass Arak

## Directions

1. Grind the blanched almonds very fine with egg white or rose water
2. Lightly stir the butter in a bowl
3. Add the ground sugar and almonds
4. Beat in 7 egg yolks
5. Stir together for a quarter hour
6. Add the grated lemon zest and juice, plus Geigenmehl and fine flour
7. Beat the egg whites from 5 eggs to stiff peaks and fold in
8. Roll out butter dough knife-back thick
9. Cut finger-wide strips with a pastry wheel
10. Line a casserole so the strips hang over the edge and are woven together
11. Fill with the mass
12. Trim the strips around the edges and bake in the oven until golden (takes 1-1/2 hours)
13. Turn onto a plate
14. For the sauce: put 1 schoppen wine in a brass pan with 4 loth sugar and cook
15. Beat 2 egg yolks in a pot, add 2 knife-points fine flour, stir smooth with cold water
16. Add half a glass Arak
17. Stir in the boiling wine, put back in the pan and let it set
18. Pass through a sieve and serve separately
