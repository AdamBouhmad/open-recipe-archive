## Ingredients

- Ham
- Salt
- Pepper, ground
- Juniper berries
- Saltpeter (4 loth)
- Garlic
- Onions

## Directions

1. Remove the hip bone from the ham.
2. Roast in an iron pan salt as much as needed, together with ground pepper and juniper berries.
3. Add 4 loth of saltpeter and rub the ham with it, especially where the hip bone was removed.
4. The salt must be as warm as one can bear it in the hand.
5. Lay the hams next to each other in a stand with a tap at the bottom (pork can be laid between them in the gaps).
6. Cut garlic and onions and add to it.
7. Close the stand firmly and press it down.
8. Let everything stand for 3 days.
9. Cook 2 pounds of salt in 2 maas of water, let it cool, pour it over the hams, and let it stand for another 8 days.
10. Repeat this for 4 to 5 weeks, cooking the brine fresh every 8 days.
11. After 5 weeks, hang the hams in the smoke chamber.
