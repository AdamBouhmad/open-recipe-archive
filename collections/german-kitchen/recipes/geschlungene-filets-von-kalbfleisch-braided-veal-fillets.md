## Ingredients

- Veal fillets
- Farce (forcemeat)
- Egg white
- Bacon strips
- Paper
- Lemon or lime sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the fillets two knife-back thicknesses thick and marinate them as previously described.
2. Line the bottom of the serving dish with a fine forcemeat and coat with egg white.
3. Lay the fillets on top in a chain-like braided pattern.
4. Remove any forcemeat not covered by fillets.
5. Cover the fillets with bacon strips and paper.
6. Bake in a not-too-hot oven.
7. Before serving, remove the bacon and fat as best as possible.
8. Pour a lemon or lime sauce over top.
