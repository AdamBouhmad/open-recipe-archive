## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 3 spoonfuls good beer yeast
- half a schoppen cream
- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 loth powdered sugar
- 1 knife point salt
- warm milk as needed
- 4 loth melted butter
- 10 loth large and small raisins

## Directions

1. Take 1 pound flour in a bowl and make a small dough in the middle with 3 spoonfuls of good beer yeast and half a schoppen cream
2. Let it rise, add 1/4 pound butter, 4 egg yolks, 1 loth powdered sugar, and 1 knife point salt
3. Make a good, somewhat stiff dough from all ingredients, add a little warm milk if necessary
4. Work it for a quarter hour and let it rise beautifully
5. When it is ripe, roll it out somewhat broad on a noodle board
6. Brush the cake with 4 loth melted butter and scatter 10 loth large and small raisins on top
7. Roll it together like a sausage
8. Brush a Turkish mold with butter, place the sausage in it, let it rise well, and bake the Kugelhopfen in fairly strong heat until beautifully golden
