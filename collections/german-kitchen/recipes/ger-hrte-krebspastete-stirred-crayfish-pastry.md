## Ingredients

- 24 crayfish
- salt water
- crayfish butter
- 2 soaked, squeezed and trimmed breadcrumbs
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the crayfish in salted water
2. Remove the tails and claws, make crayfish butter
3. Lightly stir one part of the crayfish butter in a bowl
4. Stir in 2 soaked, squeezed and trimmed breadcrumbs and 5 eggs
5. Coat small pastry molds with the remaining crayfish butter
6. Fill the mixture into the molds
7. Neatly place the crayfish tails and claws on top of the pastries
8. Bake until golden
