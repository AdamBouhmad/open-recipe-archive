## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 cucumbers without seeds
- 2 onions
- handful of parsley
- butter
- flour
- cold water
- meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons herb vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut 2 to 3 seedless cucumbers into salad
2. Chop 2 onions with a handful of parsley
3. Steam all of this in a casserole with a piece of butter for a quarter hour
4. Sprinkle 3 finger widths of flour on it
5. Add a little cold water, one ladle of meat broth, salt, pepper and 2 tablespoons of herb vinegar
6. Let everything boil together
