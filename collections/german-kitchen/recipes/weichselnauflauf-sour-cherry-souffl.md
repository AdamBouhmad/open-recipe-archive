## Ingredients

- 6 loth almond macaroons
- 3 cooking spoonfuls fine flour
- 3 loth sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 schoppen sweet cream
- 4 loth butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon
- pitted sour cherries

## Directions

1. Place 6 loth almond macaroons in warmth to be roasted, then pound them fine in a mortar, place them in a brass pan
2. Stir them smooth with 3 cooking spoonfuls fine flour, 3 loth sugar, 4 egg yolks and 1/2 schoppen sweet cream and cook to a thick crème
3. Stir 4 loth butter and the crème together in a bowl, then add 5 egg yolks one after another, beat the whites of these eggs to snow and fold into the mass
4. Add 1/2 lemon grated on a grater, butter a baking sheet, place some of the mass in it, a layer of pitted sour cherries on top, pour some of the crème again, sour cherries again on top, the remaining crème over it
5. Place in oven and let it rise
