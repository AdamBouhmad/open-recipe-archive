## Ingredients

- 3 spoonfuls sieved sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls Arak
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 3 spoonfuls sieved sugar and 3 egg yolks together very thick
2. Pour 2 spoonfuls Arak to it
3. Stir for another quarter hour
4. Finally mix in the snow from 3 egg whites
5. Arrange the cream on a salad bowl
6. Whipped cream may be added if desired
