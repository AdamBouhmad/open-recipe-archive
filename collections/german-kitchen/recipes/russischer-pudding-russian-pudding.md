## Ingredients

- 1 pound cleaned beef marrow
- 6 loth grated white bread
- 7 cooking spoonfuls fine flour
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound large raisins
- 1/2 pound small raisins
- 1 glass strong arak
- zest of 1 lemon, chopped
- cinnamon to taste
- 10 eggs
- 1 orange
- zest of 1 orange
- 1 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 bottle wine
- 3 egg yolks
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned beef marrow into small pieces
2. Mix it well with 6 loth grated white bread, 7 cooking spoonfuls fine flour, 1/2 pound sugar, 1/2 pound large raisins and the same amount of small raisins, 1 glass strong arak, the chopped zest of 1 lemon, cinnamon, and 10 eggs
3. Bind it in a damp cloth, but not tightly, and cook 2 hours in boiling water
4. For the sauce: grate 1 orange on sugar, pull off the white skin, remove the pits, cut the flesh into pieces
5. Let this come to boil together with the grated zest, the juice of 1 lemon and 1 bottle of wine
6. Beat 3 egg yolks in a pot, add 3 fingers-full of flour, stir it smooth with cold water
7. Add the boiling spiced wine to it, let it come to boil once more while it bubbles strongly and pour the sauce over the arranged pudding
