## Ingredients

- liver
- fresh calf's mesentery
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Treat the liver the same way as fried liver, with the difference that it is cut into triangular pieces
2. Take fresh calf's mesentery, place it in lukewarm water
3. Cut pieces from it
4. Wrap the liver in it
5. Place it on the grill
6. Let it fry well
7. When serving add lemon juice and salt
