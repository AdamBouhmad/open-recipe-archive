## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/4 pound blanched and finely pounded almonds
- 3 loth finely pounded sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- fine flour
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 schopp thick sour cream
- 8 egg whites
- bacon

## Directions

1. Stir 1/2 pound butter in a bowl until foamy.
2. Mix in 1/4 pound blanched and finely pounded almonds and 3 loth finely pounded sugar.
3. Stir everything together, add 8 egg yolks, one after another, and with each yolk a tablespoon of fine flour, the grated zest of 1/2 lemon, and 1/2 schopp of thick sour cream.
4. Beat the whites of 8 eggs to a stiff white snow and very slowly mix under the batter.
5. Bake well in a hot waffle iron coated inside with bacon and sprinkle the waffles with sugar and cinnamon.
