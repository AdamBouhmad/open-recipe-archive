## Ingredients

- all intestines from 2 pigs
- pig ears
- pig tongues
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- caraway seeds
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Wash all the intestines from 2 pigs in salt water
2. Weigh the ears and tongues of the pigs with the intestines
3. Add salt, pepper, cloves and caraway seeds
4. Mix everything well together
5. Fill the mixture into intestines and tie them well
6. Place the sausages with some feet into a braise (broth)
7. Fill this with bouillon
8. Let them cook on steady fire and afterwards cool
9. They can be kept in the cellar for 14 days
10. Before use, remove the aspic from them, wrap each sausage separately in paper brushed with butter, fry them on the grill until golden and serve warm
