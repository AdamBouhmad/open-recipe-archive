## Ingredients

- 6 loth finely ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- zest and juice of half a lemon
- 6 egg whites
- 6 loth fine flour
- butter
- 2 tablespoons raspberries
- 2 tablespoons arrack
- 4 loth sugar
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 6 loth finely ground sugar with 6 egg yolks and stir together for a quarter hour
2. Add the zest and juice of half a lemon
3. Beat 6 egg whites into stiff peaks
4. Fold 6 loth fine flour into the mixture
5. Butter a baking sheet, spread one quarter of the mixture on it, and let it half-cook in the oven
6. Mix 2 tablespoons raspberries with 2 tablespoons arrack, remove the sheet from the oven, spread the raspberries over it, spread the remaining mixture on top, and bake completely in the oven
7. For the icing: cook 4 loth sugar and 2 tablespoons cold water in a brass pan until it reaches the thread stage
8. Beat 3 egg whites into stiff peaks, stir into the cooked sugar
9. Turn the torte out onto a plate, cover with the egg white icing, and smooth the surface with a knife
