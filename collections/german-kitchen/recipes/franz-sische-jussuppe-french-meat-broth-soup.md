## Ingredients

- Butter or other fat
- Onions
- Yellow turnips
- Celery
- 2 pounds beef
- Bones (optional)
- Water
- Meat broth
- Black bread
- Eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter or fat in a casserole
2. Line the bottom with onions, yellow turnips and celery
3. Cut 2 pounds of beef into small pieces and place in the casserole
4. Add any leftover bones if available
5. Cover the casserole and let it steam for one hour
6. Uncover and let the steamed mixture brown well
7. When it is brown, add a ladle full of water
8. Fill the casserole with meat broth and let it cook 2 hours
9. Cut slices from black bread and add to the casserole
10. After everything has cooked together for half an hour, pass it through a hair sieve
11. Let it come to a boil once more in the casserole and serve
12. If desired, eggs can be beaten into the soup bowl
