## Ingredients

- Dried morello cherries
- Warm water
- Sugar
- Lemon
- Wine
- Cinnamon
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Wash dried morello cherries thoroughly in warm water
2. Pound them very finely in a mortar
3. Place in a casserole, pour water over as much as needed for the soup
4. Let cook together for half an hour
5. Press through a fine sieve, return to casserole and bring to a boil once more
6. Rub a piece of sugar slightly on the lemon
7. If the illness permits, wine and cinnamon can be added
8. Arrange over poached bread slices
