## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Milk or water
- Meat broth or water
- Chives
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make a dough from 2 eggs, 2 tablespoons of flour and milk or water
2. Bring meat broth or water to a boil
3. Let the dough run into the casserole over a cooking spoon or through a skimming spoon
4. When serving add chives, nutmeg and salt
