## Ingredients

- one pound of potatoes, cooked the day before
- butter
- a handful of onions
- chives
- parsley
- 6 eggs
- one quarter schoppen of sour cream
- salt
- nutmeg
- a handful of grated Parmesan, Emmental, or Swiss cheese
- two handfuls of grated bread

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes that were cooked the day before
2. In a piece of butter, braise a handful of onions, chives, and parsley (finely cut)
3. Add to the grated potatoes with 6 eggs, one quarter schoppen of sour cream, salt, nutmeg, a handful of grated cheese, and two handfuls of grated bread
4. Stir everything together again
5. Bake on a baking sheet
6. Cut into slices and add to brown meat sauce soup or white glace soup
