## Ingredients

- 1 old chicken or 2 old partridges
- 125 g rice, washed several times
- 20 g butter
- 10 g flour
- 1 spice measure
- 1 onion
- 1 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Cut the raw chicken or partridges into pieces. Sauté the rice, butter and flour together, add the spice measure and onion, then cook in water until done.
