## Ingredients

- trout
- wine
- vinegar
- lemon
- whole pepper
- herbs
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Make a court bouillon from equal parts wine and vinegar
2. Add lemon, whole pepper, all kinds of herbs, and a handful of salt
3. Let everything simmer together for a quarter hour
4. Cut open the trout at the throat just enough to remove the gallbladder
5. Place the trout in the court bouillon
6. Remove from the fire
7. Place the fish on a platter and pour vinegar and oil over it
8. Note: all types of trout must not be handled or washed extensively to preserve their beautiful blue color; only wipe away the blood with a cloth
