## Ingredients

- 18 to 20 Borsdorf apples
- 1 maas water
- 1 schoppen wine
- 8 loth sugar
- cooked cherries

## Directions

1. Cut the apples in half and remove the cores
2. Cook the cores and peels in a pan with 1 maas water until reduced to half
3. Strain through a hair sieve and press out the peels
4. Pour 1 schoppen wine into the juice
5. Add 8 loth sugar and put the apples in to cook until soft
6. Drain the apples on a hair sieve
7. Arrange them on a dish in the form of a beehive, placing cooked cherries between the gaps
8. Reduce the syrup until it spins and pour it over the apples
