## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Onions
- Cold roasted meat or two hard-boiled eggs
- Two eggs
- Flour for noodle dough
- One egg
- One egg yolk
- Meat broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash spinach, press out and finely chop with onions
2. Alternatively, finely chop some cold roasted meat or two hard-boiled eggs if roasted meat is not available
3. Make a noodle dough from two eggs and roll out finely
4. Stir the spinach with one egg and the yolk of one egg
5. Place one tablespoon of the mixture on each noodle cake, fold over, cut out and cook in meat broth
6. Add salt and nutmeg to the spinach
