## Ingredients

- 125 g rice
- 1/4 l whole milk
- 1/2 l water for soaking
- 1 pinch soda
- 1/4 l water
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 piece orange or lemon peel
- 5 g beef fat or margarine
- 5 g flour
- 20 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice well with cold water, drain it, bring to a boil in cold water in an earthenware pot, and drain again.
2. Sauté it with 5 g fat, sprinkle the flour over it, and cook briskly uncovered with 1/4 l water, 1/4 l milk, orange or lemon peel, and salt for a few minutes while shaking occasionally. Cover it and let it swell slowly while shaking occasionally, not stirring, until done. Remove the peel, arrange the rice, and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
3. Leftover uses: Milk Rice Soup, Milk Rice Pudding.
