## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound Icelandic moss
- 3 maas boiling water
- 1/2 pound candy sugar

## Directions

1. Pour 3 maas boiling water over 1/4 pound Icelandic moss
2. Let it stand for half an hour
3. Push the moss through a hair sieve
4. Pour the same amount of boiling water over it again and let stand another half hour
5. Push through the sieve again and press out the liquid
6. Let the liquid simmer in the pan for a quarter hour
7. Strain through a hair sieve again
8. Cook the liquid with 1/2 pound candy sugar until only 1 strong schoppen remains
9. Skim off the white foam from the top as it cooks
10. Let the jelly cool in a porcelain dish, cover it, and give to the chest patient one spoonful in the morning when fasting, at midday one hour after eating, and at night before bed
