## Ingredients

- Butter pastry dough
- 2 pounds dried cod or a piece of ling
- Salt water, boiling
- Onions, minced, 1 handful
- Parsley, 1 handful
- Butter
- Flour, 1 spoon full
- Light pea broth, 1/2 ladle full
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Sour cream, 1/2 pint

## Directions

1. Make a pastry from butter dough as previously explained
2. Place 2 pounds dried cod or a piece of ling in a casserole with boiling salt water
3. Cover the casserole and set it to warmth
4. For the sauce: steam a handful of minced onions and parsley in butter
5. Let it brown lightly with 1 spoon full of flour
6. Pour 1/2 ladle full of light pea broth to it
7. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg
8. Stir it smooth with 1/2 pint of sour cream
9. Let everything cook together
10. Pour the dried cod into a sieve
11. Clean it
12. Cook it a couple of minutes in the sauce
13. Fill it with the sauce into the pastry
14. Cover with the lid
15. Serve it at table
