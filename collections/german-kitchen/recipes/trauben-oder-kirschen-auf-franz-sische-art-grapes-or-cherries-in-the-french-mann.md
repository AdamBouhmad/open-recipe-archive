## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe grapes (black Muscat are the best) or cherries
- quarter pound of crushed sugar per half Maas glass
- coarsely crushed cinnamon
- cloves
- cherry schnapps

## Directions

1. Cut the stems from the grapes or cherries
2. Place them in a sugar glass
3. For each half Maas glass, add one quarter pound of crushed sugar, some coarsely crushed cinnamon and cloves
4. Fill the glass completely with cherry schnapps
5. Bind it with a bladder and store it
6. This preserve is very good for baking
