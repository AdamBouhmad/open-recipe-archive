## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Carefully sort as many raspberries as you need to use
2. Tie them firmly in a pot and let it stand overnight
3. The next day place it in another pot or kettle with boiling water, cover it tightly so the steam cannot escape
4. Draw the clear juice through a fine sieve and mix sugar with it (for 1 schoppen juice count 3 quarter pounds sugar)
5. Let it cook together, skim diligently
6. To test if it is done, take a spoonful in a coffee cup and let it cool; if it is gelatinous, it is good
7. Store it in a sugar glass
