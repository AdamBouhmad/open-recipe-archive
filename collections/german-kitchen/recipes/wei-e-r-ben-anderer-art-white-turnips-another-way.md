## Ingredients

- White turnips
- 3 Loth sugar
- Fat
- Flour
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the turnips as in recipe No. 127.
2. Toast 3 Loth of sugar in a casserole with fat until beautiful yellow, add the turnips and let them cook for an hour.
3. When soft, dust a spoonful of flour over them and mix with a cooking spoon, pour a spoonful of meat broth over them and let them cook for another hour.
