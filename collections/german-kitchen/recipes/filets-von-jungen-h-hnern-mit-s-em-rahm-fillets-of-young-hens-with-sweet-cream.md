## Ingredients

- Young hens (Poulards)
- Béchamel sauce
- Glace
- Veal from the shank
- Butter
- Onions
- Parsley
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Roast the young hens half-done.
2. When cold, remove the skin.
3. Cut the white meat into fine fillets and cook with Béchamel sauce and some glace.
4. For the Béchamel: cut one pound of veal shank into small pieces.
5. In a covered casserole, gently braise the veal with one pound of butter, onions and parsley.
6. Sprinkle two handfuls of flour over it, stir a few times, cover and braise for a few more minutes.
7. Pass everything through a fine sieve using a vegetable mill.
8. Place a piece of butter and the Béchamel in a casserole.
9. Stir in 3 egg yolks, salt and nutmeg, and stir several times.
10. Pour the Béchamel onto the serving platter.
11. Place the young hens on top and squeeze the juice of half a lemon over them.
