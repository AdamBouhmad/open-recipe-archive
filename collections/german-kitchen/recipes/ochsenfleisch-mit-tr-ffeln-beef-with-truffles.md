## Ingredients

- Beef
- Potatoes
- Butter
- One Schoppen of sour cream
- Three to four large truffles

## Directions

1. Roast the beef beautifully in a casserole or in the oven
2. Peel potatoes raw and wash them clean
3. Cook them half-soft with a piece of butter
4. Pour one Schoppen of sour cream over them
5. Clean three to four large truffles with a brush
6. Cut off the rough skin and slice the truffles into thin pieces
7. Add the truffles to the potatoes
8. Let everything cook for another fifteen minutes
9. Arrange on a platter, place the meat on top, and pour some of the sauce over the meat
