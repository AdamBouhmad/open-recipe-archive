## Ingredients

- 9 loth sweet almonds
- 1 loth bitter almonds
- cinnamon water
- 1/2 maas cream
- 8 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 3 to 4 spoonfuls cinnamon water
- 1 schoppen calf jelly
- 6 egg yolks
- a little milk
- orange oil

## Directions

1. Blanch 9 loth sweet and 1 loth bitter almonds.
2. Pound them very fine with a little cinnamon water.
3. Cook the almonds with 1/2 maas cream, 8 loth sugar rubbed with lemon zest, and 3 to 4 spoonfuls cinnamon water together with constant stirring.
4. Add 1 schoppen calf jelly and cook again with it.
5. Stir 6 egg yolks smooth with a little milk.
6. Finally pour this into the cream.
7. Let everything come together once more in the pan.
8. Pour the cream into a jelly mold brushed with orange oil.
9. Place it on ice or in the cellar.
10. Turn the cream out onto a plate and garnish to taste.
