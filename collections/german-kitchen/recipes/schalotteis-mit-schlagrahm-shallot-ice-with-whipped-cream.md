## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound bacon rinds
- Cold water
- 1 maas water
- 6 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 3 schoppen thick sweet cream
- 1 schoppen whipped cream
- Sugar bread (Zuckerbrod)

## Directions

1. Place 1/2 pound bacon rinds with cold water on the fire and let cook for two minutes
2. Pour off the water, place the rinds on a clean board and scrape off the fat
3. Put the rinds again in a clean dish and pour 1 maas water over
4. Let it reduce to 3 schoppen
5. Strain through a hair sieve or woolen cloth and let stand for a quarter hour
6. Pour into a brass pan, add 6 loth sugar with a lemon rind grated into it
7. Add 3 schoppen thick sweet cream and let cook together as long as a hard egg
8. Strain through a hair sieve and let cool
9. Meanwhile, beat 1 schoppen whipped cream in a bowl with a white whisk or chocolate whisk until very thick
10. Place on a hair sieve and let drain
11. When the crème is cold, fold in the snow
12. Line a smooth deep form throughout with sugar bread so that one piece touches another, as with apple shallot
13. Fill the crème into it, let it set firm and turn it out onto a plate
