## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- flour, sufficient amount
- milk
- 4 loth butter
- salt or sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 3 eggs into sufficient flour in a bowl and make a spätzle dough with milk
2. In a pan, bring milk to boil
3. Dip a colander in cold water and press the dough through it into the boiling milk
4. Let it run slowly into the boiling milk, stirring the spätzle often to prevent lumps from forming
5. Add 4 loth butter and let cook well, but keep them moist
6. Season with salt or sugar as desired
