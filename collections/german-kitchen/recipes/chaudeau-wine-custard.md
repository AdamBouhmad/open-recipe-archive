## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- 1/2 Maas wine
- sugar (totaling 1/2 pound with the lemon zest)
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 4 egg yolks
- cold water

## Directions

1. Rub 2 lemons on sugar to release the zest
2. Pour 1/2 Maas wine into a brass pan
3. Add the rubbed sugar, the juice of the 2 lemons, and enough additional sugar that it makes 1/2 pound total with the zest
4. Add 1 piece of whole cinnamon
5. Let everything cook a little together
6. Beat 4 egg yolks in a dish with a spoonful of cold water
7. Stir them smooth
8. Stir the boiling wine slowly to them
9. Set it back on the fire and let it draw
10. Pour it into the pot and whisk it until it is quite fine
