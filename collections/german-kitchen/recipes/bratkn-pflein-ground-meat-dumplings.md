## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal or beef forcemeat
- 1/2 Maas milk (if not already mixed in by the butcher)
- zest of half a lemon, finely minced
- 1/2 quarter pound rendered butter
- lukewarm water for cooking

## Directions

1. If the 1 pound veal or beef forcemeat is not already prepared with milk by the butcher, stir it with 1/2 Maas milk.
2. Mince the zest of half a lemon finely.
3. Stir 1/2 quarter pound rendered butter with the forcemeat and lemon zest thoroughly.
4. Place the dumplings in lukewarm water but do not cook them in it; if they turn rough, they are overcooked.
5. When they throw up a white foam, they are done.
6. Serve them in meat broth.
