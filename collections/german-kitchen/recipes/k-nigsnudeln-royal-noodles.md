## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen milk
- flour
- fresh butter
- 4 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 spoonfuls good beer yeast
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lard

## Directions

1. Bring 1 schoppen milk to a boil
2. Stir in flour until it becomes a thick porridge
3. Add some fresh butter
4. Beat in 4 eggs one after another and 4 egg yolks
5. Cook until completely dry
6. Place in a bowl with 2 spoonfuls good beer yeast and let stand in warmth for a time
7. Form noodles from it
8. Fry well in hot lard
9. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
