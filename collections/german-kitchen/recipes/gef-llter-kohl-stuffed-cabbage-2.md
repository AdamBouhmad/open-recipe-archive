## Ingredients

- cabbage
- meat
- onions
- parsley
- 2 soaked and squeezed bread rolls (Wecken)
- 3 to 4 eggs
- salt water
- butter sauce or jus

## Directions

1. Pick over, wash, and cook the cabbage in salt water.
2. For the filling: finely mince the meat with onions and parsley, add 2 grated, soaked, and firmly squeezed bread rolls, stir in 3 to 4 eggs.
3. Dip a cloth in cold water and lay the cabbage leaves one after another upon it.
4. Spread one spoonful of the filling on each leaf, bind the cloth firmly together, and cook the cabbage so in salt water until tender.
5. Serve with either butter sauce or jus.
