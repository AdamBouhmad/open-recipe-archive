## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- 1/2 maas milk
- 4 loth butter
- 12 Borsdorfer apples
- 1 glass wine
- 2 loth orange peel and citron
- some lemon peel
- 6 peeled almonds
- a handful of raisins
- 5 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 4 loth sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/4 pound rice in boiling water 2 to 3 times, drain it in a hair sieve, cook it with 1/2 maas milk and 4 loth butter in a casserole
2. Peel 12 Borsdorfer apples, remove the core, braise the apples in a casserole with 1 glass wine in butter until half soft, place them on a plate
3. For the filling: weigh 2 loth orange peel and citron, some lemon peel, 6 peeled almonds not very fine, add a handful of raisins, fill the apples with this mixture
4. Stir 5 egg yolks, the snow of 6 egg whites, 4 loth ground sugar into the mass
5. Place half of the mixture on a buttered baking sheet, lay the filled apples on it, place the other half on top, and let the soufflé rise in an oven
6. When ready, place it on a plate, spread a snow of 4 egg whites mixed with sugar on top, and place it once more in the oven on a black baking sheet with sand or salt underneath
