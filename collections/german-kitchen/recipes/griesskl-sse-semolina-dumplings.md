## Ingredients

- 125 g semolina
- 1/4 liter skim milk
- 20 g margarine
- 1 pinch salt
- 30 g sugar
- 1 egg
- Water and salt for cooking the dumplings

## Directions

1. Bring milk, fat, salt, and sugar to a boil
2. Add the semolina and stir constantly to form a shiny mass
