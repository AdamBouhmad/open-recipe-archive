## Ingredients

- 1 Bratwurst
- 3 g salt
- 1 tablespoon water
- 10 g flour (optional)
- 1 pinch pepper (optional)
- 1/4 liter broth or wheat beer (optional)
- 1 drop brown sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the bratwurst and divide it into two equal pieces.
2. If the bratwurst is fatty enough, place it in the pan without additional fat, pour one spoon of water over it, and fry the sausage under frequent turning in its own fat to a nice color.
3. Place the finished sausage on a plate and pour the available fat over it.
4. If a vegetable with sauce is added, this is sufficient. Otherwise, add to the fat in the pan 10 g flour, 1 pinch pepper and salt, make a brown roux from it, add 1/4 liter broth or wheat beer while stirring, color the sauce with a drop of brown sugar and adjust the seasoning.
5. Alternatively, prepare the sauce first and let the sausage simmer in it for 1/2 hour.
