## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- 4 eggs
- sour cream
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Put 2 spoonfuls of flour in a bowl (for one person, use one cooking spoon of flour and 2 eggs)
2. Stir it smooth with lukewarm milk, beat in 4 eggs and 1 spoonful of sour cream
3. Stir everything well together with milk
4. Heat lard in a pan, let the batter run into it, cover it
5. Let it set a little, turn it over, let it set again, tear it apart
6. Pour more lard over and let it bake until done
