## Ingredients

- 6 veal feet
- Celery
- Carrots
- Parsley
- Fish meat dumplings (optional)
- Egg yolks
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the veal feet, remove the skin, place in a casserole or pot
2. Cook with celery, carrots, and parsley until tender
3. Cut into small pieces and return to the casserole or pot
4. Strain the broth through a sieve back over the pieces and bring to a boil again
5. Small dumplings can be made from fish meat if desired
6. Place 2 egg yolks in the soup bowl and arrange the soup over them
7. Season with nutmeg and salt
