## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- 6 to 8 bitter almond kernels
- a piece of cinnamon
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put 1/2 maas of milk over the fire.
2. Pound 6 to 8 bitter almond kernels and add them along with a piece of cinnamon, 4 loth sugar with lemon zest rubbed in, into the milk.
3. Let everything cook together for a while.
4. Finally stir in the snow from 4 egg whites.
5. Once cooled, press the cream through a hair sieve into a bowl.
6. Place it on hot ashes.
7. Cover with a lid topped with weak coals and let the cream set.
8. Serve cold or warm.
