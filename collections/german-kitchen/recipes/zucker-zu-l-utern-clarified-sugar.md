## Ingredients

- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Dip the sugar in water until no more bubbles rise.
2. Place the pieces thoroughly soaked with water in a clean pot or kettle.
3. Heat gently until the sugar is completely melted.
4. Only then bring the mixture to a boil; if done earlier, the sugar will crystallize.
5. Bring the sugar to a boil, skim, and use further.
