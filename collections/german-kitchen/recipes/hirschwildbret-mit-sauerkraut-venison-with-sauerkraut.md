## Ingredients

- Deer loin roast or another tender piece
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Lemons
- Juniper berries
- Wine
- Pork lard
- Sugar
- Sauerkraut
- Champagne or good wine
- Flour
- Sour cream
- Ground cinnamon
- Puff pastry
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Skin the deer meat, lard it, place it in an earthenware roasting dish, add sliced onions, bay leaves, lemons, and juniper berries.
2. Pour half a quart of wine over it, place the dish in an oven, and let the roast become half done.
3. In a casserole, make pork lard and 2 loths of sugar golden; let sauerkraut that has been firmly squeezed out steam in the casserole for half an hour, stirring often.
4. Pour a glass of champagne or other good wine over it, heat lard in a dish, let a handful of chopped onions become golden in it, sprinkle half a kitchen spoonful of flour into it and let it take color, pour this onto the sauerkraut along with 3 spoonfuls of sour cream.
5. Stir it together and let it cook until no more sauce is visible.
6. Place half of the sauerkraut on a platter, place the venison roast on it, cover with the remaining sauerkraut, sprinkle ground cinnamon on it.
7. Make a border around the platter with puff pastry, brush it with eggs, place the platter in the oven, and let the sauerkraut become beautifully golden brown.
