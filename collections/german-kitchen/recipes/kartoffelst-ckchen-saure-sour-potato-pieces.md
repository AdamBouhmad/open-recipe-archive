## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg raw peeled potatoes
- 20 g bacon, fat or margarine
- 15 g onions
- 1/2 l broth or water
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 1/2 spice mixture
- 1/2 pinch pepper and salt
- 25 g sugar or syrup

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into quarters.
2. Bring them to a boil with cold water and drain. Render 20 g bacon or fat with the finely cut onions, cook with the heated liquid, vinegar, spices, salt and pepper, cook the potatoes through in it, season with sugar or syrup and, if necessary, as is the case with new potatoes, thicken with a flour roux made from 10 g fat and 10 g flour and bring to a boil.
