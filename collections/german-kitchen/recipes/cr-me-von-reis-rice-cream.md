## Ingredients

- one-quarter pound rice
- boiling water
- half Maas milk
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- 4 Loth pounded sugar
- 2 Loth diced citron

## Directions

1. Scald one-quarter pound rice with boiling water and let it stand for a quarter hour
2. Strain through a hair sieve
3. Cook it with half a Maas milk until very soft
4. Add 1 schoppen sweet cream, 6 egg yolks, 1 lemon rubbed with sugar, 4 Loth pounded sugar, and 2 Loth diced citron
5. Let everything come together slightly in a pan
6. Place a bowl in hot water
7. Fill the mass into it
8. Cover with a lid topped with coals and let the cream become firm
9. Serve cold or warm
