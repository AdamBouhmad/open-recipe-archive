## Ingredients

- Pears
- boiling water
- half a lemon peel
- whole cinnamon
- 6 cloves

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and cut them in half
2. Pour boiling water over them such that it reaches a finger's height above the pears
3. Cook them in the water with half a lemon peel, some whole cinnamon and 6 cloves until soft
4. Form 2 to 3 artichokes from the pears on a plate
5. Let the syrup reduce a bit more
6. Pour the syrup through a hair sieve over the pears
