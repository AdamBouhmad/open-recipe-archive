## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 quarter-pound peeled almonds with rose water
- 1-1/2 quarter-pound sugar
- 4 Loth finely chopped citron
- 2 Loth finely chopped orange peel
- 1/2 Quint pounded cinnamon
- 1 Quint cloves
- 2 to 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Stir together in a bowl for a quarter hour: 1-1/2 quarter-pound peeled almonds pounded with rose water, 1-1/2 quarter-pound sugar, 4 Loth finely chopped citron, 2 Loth finely chopped orange peel, 1/2 Quint pounded cinnamon, 1 Quint cloves, and the snow from 2 to 3 egg whites
2. Form finger-long sticks from the mixture
3. Place them on a sheet brushed with bacon or wax
4. Bake in the oven
