## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 pieces of Breitling apples
- a piece of butter
- a piece of sugar
- 10 loth butter
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 lemon
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 teaspoons of flour
- 8 egg whites
- butter for the dish
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Peel and cut 6 to 8 Breitling apples and steam them in a piece of butter with a piece of sugar until not quite soft.
2. In a bowl, lightly stir 10 loth butter, 4 loth sugar with a lemon zest grated into it.
3. Add 8 egg yolks one after another to the butter mixture.
4. To each egg yolk add a small teaspoon of flour.
5. Beat the 8 egg whites to a snow.
6. Mix everything together, fill into a bowl brushed with butter.
7. Bake until golden and sprinkle sugar on top.
