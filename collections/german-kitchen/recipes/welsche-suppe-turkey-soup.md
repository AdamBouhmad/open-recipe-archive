## Ingredients

- Turkey feet, neck, head, and wings from the second joint
- Butter
- Onions
- Milk bread
- Meat jus
- Egg yolks
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Take the feet, neck, head, and wings from the second joint of a turkey and chop everything very finely
2. Steam cut onions, the chopped mixture, and milk bread cut into 2 kreuzer worth of pieces with butter for half an hour
3. Pour in good jus and let it cook together for one hour
4. Beat 3 egg yolks with 2 tablespoons of sour cream in the soup bowl
5. Strain the soup through a hair sieve once more
