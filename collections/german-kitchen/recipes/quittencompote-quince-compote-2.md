## Ingredients

- Quinces
- water
- sugar (12 loth)
- lemon peel
- wine (half a schoppen)
- raisins

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces and cut them in half
2. Cook them soft in water
3. Place them on a hair sieve
4. Cook the skins and seeds soft in water and also pour through a hair sieve
5. In a saucepan combine the quinces, the seed/skin mixture, 12 loth sugar, a lemon peel and half a schoppen wine
6. Cook together until the syrup is thick
7. Form a melon shape from the quinces on a plate
8. Pour the syrup over it
9. Insert large raisins that have been cooked in the syrup into the figure
