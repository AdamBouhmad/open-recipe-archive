## Ingredients

- Salsify roots
- Salt water
- Potatoes
- Butter
- Sweet milk
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Half a schoppen sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash, clean and prepare the salsify, and cook it soft in salted water.
2. Peel potatoes, cut them into 4 parts, place a piece of butter in the casserole with the potatoes and a little sweet milk and braise them soft, adding salt and nutmeg to taste.
3. When everything is soft, add the salsify to the potatoes and pour half a schoppen of sour cream over.
