## Ingredients

- 1 pound pork fat from the loin
- 8 large onions
- Half pound butter
- Half a maas of blood
- One schoppen sweet cream
- Basil
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Pepper
- Salt
- Sausage casings
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Skin the pork fat and cut it into small cubes
2. Cut 8 large onions very finely and let them brown over steady heat with half a pound of butter
3. Pour half a maas of blood and one schoppen of sweet cream over them
4. Add the cut fat and chopped basil, thyme, and marjoram
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Mix everything well together
7. Fill the casings with this mixture
8. Put the sausages in boiling salted water and let a broth cook over them
9. Allow them to cool again
10. Before serving, brush them with fresh butter and grill them on a grate
