## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar, finely ground
- 20 egg yolks
- snow of 16 egg whites
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 1/2 loth ground cinnamon
- 12 loth starch flour
- butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar with the 20 egg yolks and the snow of 16 egg whites for a quarter hour
2. Slowly stir in the grated chocolate, ground cinnamon, and 12 loth starch flour into the mass
3. Bake the torte in a tin pan brushed with butter
