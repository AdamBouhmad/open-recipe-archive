## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound blanched almonds
- egg white
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 white roll, soaked and firmly squeezed out
- 6 egg yolks
- egg whites from 6 eggs, beaten to snow
- 1/4 pound currants
- 1/4 pound raisins
- 1 apricot half, pitted
- large currants
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat 1/4 pound butter in a bowl until very foamy.
2. Pound 1/4 pound blanched almonds with egg white very finely.
3. Mix together 1/4 pound sugar with lemon zest grated on it, the squeezed-out soaked white roll, 6 egg yolks, the beaten egg white snow, 1/4 pound currants and raisins with the pounded almonds, beating very foamy (the snow must be added last).
4. Spread out a damp cloth, place half a pitted apricot in the center, arrange large currants around it, fill the mass onto it.
5. Tie the cloth closed leaving a finger's width of space, cook it one hour in lightly salted water.
6. For the lemon sauce: rub 2 lemons over sugar, mix with a cooking spoon of flour and cold water, add 1 pint of wine, the lemon sugar, and the juice, and pour this sauce over the pudding.
