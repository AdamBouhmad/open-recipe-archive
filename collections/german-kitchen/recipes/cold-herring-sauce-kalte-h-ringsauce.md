## Ingredients

- 1 beautiful herring, male fish
- small piece butter
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- herring roe
- 2 spoons Provençal oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and bone a beautiful male herring carefully
2. Pound it with a small piece of butter and 6 hard-boiled egg yolks very finely in a mortar
3. Strain everything through a sieve
4. Take the roe from the herring and stir it with 2 spoons of Provençal oil, some vinegar and pepper
5. Stir everything together and serve the sauce with beef
