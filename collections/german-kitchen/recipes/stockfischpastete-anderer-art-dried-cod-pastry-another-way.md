## Ingredients

- Tender butter dough
- Dried cod
- Salt water, boiling
- 2 cleaned herrings
- Roe and milk
- Parsley, 1 handful
- 1 onion
- Butter
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- Thick sour cream, 1/2 pint
- Red beet broth

## Directions

1. Make a ring from tender butter dough
2. Lay it on a black sheet and bake it golden
3. Treat the dried cod as in No. 286
4. Cut 2 cleaned herrings into small pieces with roe and milk
5. Mince a handful of parsley with 1 onion finely
6. Lay a piece of butter in a casserole
7. Add the herrings to it
8. Pour the dried cod into a sieve
9. Clean it
10. Lay it with the herrings
11. Shake them often so everything mixes together
12. Press the yolk of 3 hard-boiled eggs through a sieve
13. Stir 1/2 pint of thick sour cream under it
14. Arrange the dried cod on a plate
15. Lay the ring around it
16. Arrange the stirred mixture over the dried cod
17. Cut the white of the eggs into leaves and cut it out with a tin cutter as desired
18. Lay the cut-outs in red beet broth
19. Put the pastry in the oven and let it brown
20. When it is time to serve, remove the pastry from the oven and garnish it with the eggs lying in red beet broth
