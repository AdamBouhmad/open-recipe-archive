## Ingredients

- 125 g rice
- 1/2 l water for cooking rice
- 2 large apples or 40 g dried apples and 1/4 l water
- 5 g margarine or fat
- 1 small piece of orange peel
- 10 g sugar
- 5 g salt
- 10 g sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel 2 large apples and cut them into pieces. Cook the apple scraps with 1/4 l water; use this water for cooking the rice.
2. Add 10 g sugar to the apples and cook them with orange peel, salt, and the cooked rice that has been mixed with fat until everything is tender.
3. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over it. You can also use 40 g dried apples, but these should be cooked separately until soft before mixing with the rice.
