## Ingredients

- 375 g sour cherries
- 40 g sugar
- 2 g starch flour
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Wash the cherries, remove the stems, pit them with a new hairpin. Half of the pits are crushed, bound in a rinsed muslin bag and the cherries are tossed with sugar. Dissolve the starch flour.
2. Bring the prepared cherries to the heat with the pits and simmer slowly soft covered; then remove the muslin bag, make the sauce silky with the dissolved starch flour, shake the compote a little so that the cherries fill, and arrange it.
