## Ingredients

- 3 rolls soaked in water and firmly pressed out
- 1 piece fat
- 1 handful steamed onions and parsley
- 4 to 5 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Steam 3 water-soaked, firmly pressed-out rolls in a pan with 1 piece fat
2. Take 1 handful steamed onions and parsley, 4 to 5 eggs, salt, and nutmeg
3. Make small dumplings from the dough, as large as a hen's egg
4. Cook them for a quarter hour in boiling water
5. Braise them with butter and grated bread
