## Ingredients

- leftover meat of any kind
- onions
- parsley
- bread, 2 pieces
- fat
- eggs, 4
- salt
- nutmeg
- calf's caul

## Directions

1. Chop leftover meat with onions and parsley.
2. Soak bread in water, wring out, and add to the meat mixture.
3. Braise everything in a casserole with a piece of fat for fifteen minutes.
4. Add eggs, salt, and nutmeg to the mixture.
5. Wrap the mixture in calf's caul, sew it closed, and roast in the oven until golden brown.
