## Ingredients

- Chervil
- Parsley
- Sorrel
- Garden cress
- Chives
- A piece of butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Meat broth
- Toasted bread slices
- Three tablespoons of sour cream
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash chervil, parsley, sorrel, garden cress, and chives thoroughly and finely chop after pressing out water
2. Braise everything together with a piece of butter in a casserole for a quarter hour
3. Add one tablespoon of flour and pour in meat broth
4. Let everything cook together for another quarter hour
5. Arrange over toasted bread slices
6. Stir together three tablespoons of sour cream with two egg yolks and add to the soup
