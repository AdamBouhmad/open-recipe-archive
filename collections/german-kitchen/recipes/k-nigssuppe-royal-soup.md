## Ingredients

- 2 cleaned and washed chickens, finely chopped
- 1 pound lean beef, cut into small pieces
- Butter
- Celery root
- Parsnip root
- Flour
- Meat broth or jus
- Egg yolks
- Thick sweet cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. In a casserole with a piece of butter the size of an egg, combine the chopped chickens and beef pieces with celery and parsnip root
2. Steam for 2 to 3 hours
3. Sprinkle a tablespoon of flour on top, pour in meat broth or jus, and cook for another half hour
4. Press through a hair sieve with meat broth and bring to a boil once more
5. Beat 4 egg yolks in the soup tureen, add half a schoppen of thick sweet cream
6. Arrange the soup over the yolks and cream
7. Season with salt and nutmeg
