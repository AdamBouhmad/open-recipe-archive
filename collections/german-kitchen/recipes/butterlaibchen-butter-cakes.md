## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- one quarter pound butter
- one quarter pound sugar
- one quarter pound finely ground almonds
- 1 to 1.5 quarter pounds flour
- zest of half a lemon
- a knife point of ground cinnamon
- lard
- coarsely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Stir together 2 eggs, one quarter pound butter, one quarter pound sugar and one quarter pound finely ground almonds for a quarter hour
2. Work in 1 to 1.5 quarter pounds flour, the zest of half a lemon grated on a grater, and a knife point of ground cinnamon
3. Place cakes the size of a walnut from this dough onto a baking sheet brushed with lard
4. Brush with egg
5. Sprinkle with coarsely ground sugar and bake
