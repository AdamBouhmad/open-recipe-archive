## Ingredients

- 250 g pork kidneys
- 5 g salt
- 10 g flour
- 25 g margarine
- 1/4 l broth or meat extract broth
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the kidneys thoroughly, cut them lengthwise into 1/2 cm thick pieces, remove the sinews and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Make a roux with flour and margarine in a saucepan until golden, add the kidneys, brown them, add the broth and braise while stirring constantly, as they easily become tough, for approximately 2-3 minutes until they no longer look red. Then add the vinegar and serve quickly, preferably with fried potatoes.
