## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- salt
- onions
- parsley
- 2 mouth-sized pieces bread
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 vierling butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the spinach carefully, place in boiling water on the fire, add salt and cook through
2. Let drain through a sieve, pour cold water over, press firmly dry
3. Chop as finely as possible with onions and parsley
4. Rub 2 pieces of bread on a grater, soak in water, press dry and mix with the spinach
5. Beat in 4 eggs one by one
6. Melt 1/2 vierling butter and pour under the mixture
7. Add salt and nutmeg, stir for a quarter hour
8. Butter a baking sheet (pottage sheet), fill with the mixture and bake on coals or in the oven
