## Ingredients

- 1/4 l whole milk
- 15 g sugar
- 1 small pinch salt
- 5 g starch flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons whole milk
- cocoa, almonds, or flavorings as desired

## Directions

1. Heat the sugar, salt, and 1/4 l milk with either 6 bitter almonds, 1/2 packet vanilla sugar, 15 g cocoa, or 1 tablespoon lemon sugar according to taste.
2. Mix the starch flour with the egg yolks and 4 tablespoons of milk, then stir this into the heated mixture while cooking until thickened.
3. Strain through a sieve and stir frequently as it cools to prevent a skin from forming.
4. Note: The egg yolks can be omitted if you increase the starch flour to 6 g instead.
