## Ingredients

- Halbgewachsene Gurken (Half-grown cucumbers)
- Salz (Salt)
- Pfeffer (Peppercorns)
- Lorbeerblätter (Bay leaves)
- Traubenlaub (Grape leaves)
- Grüner Fenchel (Green fennel)
- Citronenkraut (Lemon balm)
- Wasser (Water)

## Directions

1. Soak half-grown cucumbers in salt water for 24 hours
2. Dry them with a clean cloth
3. Line the bottom of the designated small barrel with coarsely crushed peppercorns, bay leaves, and grape leaves
4. Layer cucumbers, then a layer of the spice mixture, alternating until all is used
5. Top with green fennel, lemon balm, and bay leaves
6. Cook water and salt together (for one measure of water, use one large handful of salt)
7. Let cool, then pour over the cucumbers
8. Seal the barrel securely and pitch it inside and out
9. Place in the sun for 8 days and shake daily
10. In 14 days the cucumbers are ready to eat and will keep well for a whole year
