## Ingredients

- pork cheeks, ears, and a piece of belly flap
- marjoram
- pepper
- spice blend
- salt
- 4 maas of blood
- 1 maas of milk
- fat from the above
- bacon

## Directions

1. Simmer the pork cheeks, ears, and belly flap until very tender
2. Cut into small cubes and mix with marjoram, pepper, spice blend, and salt
3. Mix 4 maas of blood with 1 maas of milk
4. Take the fat from the cooked meat and add it to the blood
5. Pass the blood through a hair sieve into a clean bowl
6. Fill clean prepared pork casings: put one handful of the cut bacon pieces into each, pour blood over it, and fill the casing
7. Tie the casings firmly and continue until all blood and bacon are in casings
8. Place the sausages in lukewarm water; when they rise to the surface, they are done
