## Ingredients

- one quarter pound butter
- half pound flour
- 6 loth sugar
- zest of one lemon
- 6 egg yolks
- lemon or chocolate icing

## Directions

1. Mix together one quarter pound butter, half pound flour, 6 loth sugar, lemon zest grated on a grater, and 6 egg yolks to form a dough
2. Form finger-long sticks from this dough
3. Tear them onto a black baking sheet brushed with lard using a knife
4. Bake in the oven
5. Pour lemon or chocolate icing over them
