## Ingredients

- 1 schopen water
- 1 schopen milk
- 3 lots butter
- Flour
- 6 to 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Let 1 schopen water, 1 schopen milk and 3 lots butter cook in a brass pan
2. Stir in flour until as thick as dough
3. Dry it well over the fire
4. Transfer to a bowl and beat in 6 to 8 eggs
