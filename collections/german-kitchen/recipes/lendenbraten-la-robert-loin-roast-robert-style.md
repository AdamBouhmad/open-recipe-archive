## Ingredients

- One beautiful piece loin roast
- Half pound fresh bacon
- Four anchovies
- Half lemon
- One kreuzer worth of bread
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mace
- Cloves
- Two eggs
- Cut onions
- Bay leaves
- Lemon
- Basil
- Tarragon
- One glass wine
- Half cooking spoon flour
- One glass Bordeaux wine
- One spoon meat broth

## Directions

1. From a beautiful piece of loin roast, remove fat and skin.
2. Cut it open at the side but not all the way through.
3. Cut out some flesh but leave no hole in the roast.
4. Mince the removed flesh with half a pound fresh bacon.
5. Mince four anchovies (which must be cleaned and boned beforehand) and half a lemon with its marrow very finely.
6. Grate one kreuzer worth of bread on a grater, soak in water, press out firmly.
7. Mix with the minced and chopped ingredients, salt, pepper, mace, cloves, and two beaten eggs.
8. Fill the entire mass into the loin roast, sew it closed again.
9. Place in a casserole with cut onions, bay leaves, lemon, basil, tarragon, and one glass of wine.
10. Let braise slowly over weak charcoal fire.
11. Half an hour before serving, remove the roast.
12. Add half a cooking spoon flour to the casserole, one glass Bordeaux wine and one spoon meat broth, plus juice from half a lemon.
13. Let simmer together for half a quarter hour.
14. Force through a hair sieve back into the casserole.
15. Return the roast and let finish cooking.
