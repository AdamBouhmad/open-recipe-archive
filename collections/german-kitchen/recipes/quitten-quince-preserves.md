## Ingredients

- Ripe quinces
- Sugar (same weight as the quinces)
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the core from ripe quinces
2. Peel them
3. Cut them into 4 parts
4. Simmer them soft in water
5. Lay them on a hair sieve
6. Take as many pounds of sugar as you have pounds of quinces
7. Simmer the sugar in the water in which the quinces were cooked soft until it spins threads
8. Put the quinces in
9. Let them cook with the sugar
10. Preserve them in a stone pot
