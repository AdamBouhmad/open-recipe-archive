## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 200 g sugar
- 6 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon sugar
- 3 tablets white gelatin
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar for 1/2 hour until foamy, gradually combine with lemon juice and lemon sugar, finally add the prepared gelatin and carefully fold in the snow of the egg whites. Fill into a glass bowl.
