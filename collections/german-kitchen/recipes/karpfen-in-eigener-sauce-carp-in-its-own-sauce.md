## Ingredients

- 1 carp
- salt
- pepper
- 3 cleaned anchovies
- 1/2 lemon, yolk and marrow
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 piece butter
- grated bread
- 1/2 ladle jus

## Directions

1. Scale the carp, clean it, remove the entrails, cut it in 2 parts
2. Rub with salt and pepper (not very strong)
3. Pound 3 cleaned anchovies, the yolk and marrow of 1/2 lemon, and 1 tablespoon capers
4. Place the fish in a roasting pan with a piece of butter and the pounded mixture
5. Let it cook for half an hour
6. Sprinkle grated bread over it
7. Pour in 1/2 ladle of jus
8. Let everything cook together for another half hour
9. Place the fish on a plate and pour the sauce over it
