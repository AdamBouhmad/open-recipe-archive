## Ingredients

- Half-roasted poultry (capon or poulard)
- Béchamel
- Egg yolks
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- Milk

## Directions

1. Skin the half-roasted poultry and remove all meat
2. Cut the meat into fine fillets
3. Mix with a good amount of béchamel, some egg yolks, and a little pepper and salt
4. Fill the mixture into casings
5. Poach the sausages gently in salted water
6. Cool in milk
7. Place on paper brushed with butter
8. Fry until golden brown over low heat
