## Ingredients

- 1/2 lung and heart (cooked)
- 20 g fat
- 10 g flour
- 20 g onions
- a pinch of pepper and salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Free the meat pieces from skins, sinews and cartilage and chop not too finely. Peel the onions, cut into small cubes or chop. Heat 1/8 l of lung broth.
2. Heat the fat; soften the onions in it, braise the chopped lung and flour sprinkled over it in it and then add the broth and vinegar and cook it through. Season the hash with pepper and salt. Some chopped herbs and roast sauce, if you have them on hand, make the hash more palatable. It is advantageous to cook the lung the day before. The remaining lung broth can be used for lung soup. For hash one can also use all sorts of roasted or cooked meat scraps (meat hash). Meat hash is prepared exactly like lung hash.
