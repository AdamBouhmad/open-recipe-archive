## Ingredients

- 30 g green spelt flour
- 30 g fat or margarine
- 1 l water
- 10 g onions
- 1/2 egg
- 8 g salt

## Directions

1. Dice the onion.
2. Braise the onion cubes and fat, add the green spelt flour, toast it with the onion fat while stirring, gradually add the water. After the soup has cooked for 1-1/2 hours, mix a few spoonfuls of it with the egg, though this can also be omitted, add it to the mass, season with salt, press the soup through a sieve and serve it reheated.
