## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- salt
- boiling water
- cold water
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- 3 finger-lengths of flour
- white bouillon
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and peel the kohlrabi, cut finely, salt them, cover, and let stand for one hour.
2. Add boiling water and cook until tender.
3. Strain and pour cold water over them so they remain white.
4. In a casserole, steam finely minced onions and parsley in a piece of butter.
5. Let the kohlrabi cook in this for one hour.
6. Sprinkle 3 finger-lengths of flour over them, stir, and pour 1 spoonful of white bouillon over them.
7. Add pepper and nutmeg.
