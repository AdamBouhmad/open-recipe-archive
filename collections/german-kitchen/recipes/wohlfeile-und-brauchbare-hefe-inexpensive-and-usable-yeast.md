## Ingredients

- 4 loth grated, mealy, cooked potatoes
- 1 loth pounded sugar
- a teaspoon full of yeast
- a schoppen of water

## Directions

1. Place 4 loth grated, mealy, cooked potatoes, 1 loth pounded sugar, and a teaspoon full of yeast in a pot together with a schoppen of water
2. Set it in gentle warmth
3. After one hour the yeast can be used
