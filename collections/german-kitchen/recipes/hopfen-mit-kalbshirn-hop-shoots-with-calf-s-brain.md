## Ingredients

- two to three handfuls hop shoots tied in bundles
- one handful salt
- four calf's brains
- half vinegar and half water
- parsley, one handful
- chervil, one handful
- chives
- shallot onions
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- meat broth
- juice of half a lemon
- three fingers-full flour

## Directions

1. Cook the hop shoots tied in bundles with one handful of salt until soft
2. Cook the four calf's brains in a pan with half vinegar and half water so that only a single broth covers them
3. Chop the parsley, chervil, chives, and shallot onions together
4. In a casserole with melted butter, braise the herbs and brain with salt, pepper, and nutmeg for a quarter hour
5. Pour in some meat broth and the juice of half a lemon
6. Sprinkle three fingers-full of flour over it and shake the whole together
7. When serving, place the brain in the middle of the plate, arrange the hop shoots around it, and pour the sauce over
