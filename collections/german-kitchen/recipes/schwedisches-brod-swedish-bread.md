## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound almonds with rose water
- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1 Quint cinnamon
- 1 Quint cloves
- 1 Quint cardamom
- mace
- pepper
- 4 Loth fine flour
- 1 egg
- wax
- 1 egg white
- 4 Loth sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pound 1/4 pound almonds with rose water not too finely and mix with 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar, 1 Quint cinnamon, 1 Quint cloves, 1 Quint cardamom, mace, a knife-tip of pepper, and 4 Loth fine flour
2. Beat in 1 egg to form a dough
3. Roll it out finger-thick
4. Cut pieces with a small drinking glass
5. Place them on a sheet brushed with wax
6. Bake in the oven
7. For the icing: Stir 4 Loth sugar and some drops of lemon juice into 1 egg white
8. Stir this together for a time
9. Brush the breads with it using a brush
10. Place in the oven to set
