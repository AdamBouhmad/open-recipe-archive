## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- boiling water
- half a Maas milk
- 4 Loth butter
- 12 Borsdorfer apples
- a glass of wine
- butter
- 2 Loth orange peel and citron
- citron peel
- 6 peeled almonds
- a handful of raisins
- 5 egg yolks
- egg whites from the eggs
- 4 Loth ground sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/4 pound of rice in boiling water 2-3 times and let it drain through a hair sieve
2. Cook it with half a Maas of milk and 4 Loth of butter in a casserolle
3. Peel 12 Borsdorfer apples and remove the core
4. Braise the apples in a casserolle with a glass of wine in butter until half soft and lay them on a plate
5. Make a filling: weigh 2 Loth of orange peel and citron, some citron peel, 6 peeled almonds not very finely, add a handful of raisins and fill the apples with this
6. Stir 5 egg yolks, the snow from 6 egg whites, and 4 Loth of ground sugar into the rice mass
7. Put half of this into a buttered baking sheet and lay the filled apples on top
