## Ingredients

- a small knife point of saffron
- 1 spoonful boiling water
- one quarter pound finely grated sugar
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Let a small knife point of saffron and one spoonful boiling water stand for a moment
2. Add one quarter pound finely grated sugar to it
3. Beat one egg white into it
4. Stir together for a quarter hour
5. Add the infused saffron drop by drop until it is a beautiful yellow and use as desired
