## Ingredients

- wild boar meat
- meat broth
- wine
- vinegar
- herbs and roots
- onions
- sugar
- fat
- flour
- red wine
- jus
- spices
- lemon
- tarragon vinegar or raspberry vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of wild boar in meat broth, wine, and vinegar with various herbs and roots until not very tender.
2. Chop 3 to 4 large onions.
3. In a casserole, let a piece of sugar with fat become golden, then add the onions and let them brown with the sugar.
4. Add flour according to how much sauce is needed.
5. Deglaze with the broth in which the meat was cooked and let everything cook together for one hour.
6. Add a pint of red wine and some jus to it.
7. Add various spices, the zest of one grated lemon, and some tarragon or raspberry vinegar.
8. Let it cook for a long time.
