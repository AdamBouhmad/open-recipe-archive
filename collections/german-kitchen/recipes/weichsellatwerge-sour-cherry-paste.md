## Ingredients

- 2 pounds genuine, very ripe sour cherries
- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 Schoppen water

## Directions

1. Pit 2 pounds of genuine, very ripe sour cherries
2. Force them through a fine hair sieve
3. Cook 2 pounds of sugar with 1 Schoppen of water in an earthenware container until the sugar spins a very thick thread
4. Add the cherry pulp and cook it with the sugar under constant stirring until no juice is visible
5. Store the paste in a porcelain, bound pot like all preserves
6. It is particularly good for the sick
