## Ingredients

- piece of loin roast
- salt
- pepper
- fine oil
- butter
- onion
- gravy jus

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of loin roast into small pieces, but not as thick as English beefsteaks
2. Beat them flat with your hand
3. Rub them with salt and pepper
4. Pour fine oil into a vessel and turn the beefsteaks in it
5. Place them in a pan with a piece of butter
6. Add a weighed onion
7. Let them fry golden brown
8. Arrange the whole on a plate
9. Pour the sauce and a tablespoon of roast jus over them
10. Serve with potatoes
