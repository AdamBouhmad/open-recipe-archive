## Ingredients

- 200 g cooked or fried fish (deboned)
- 375 g ready-cooked sauerkraut
- 20 g margarine
- 1/8 liter cream
- 5 g salt
- 10 g grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Layer the sauerkraut on a buttered dish or in a form
2. Over it place a layer of the deboned fish, broken into small pieces
3. Continue alternating layers, ending with sauerkraut
4. Pour sweet cream mixed with salt over the dish
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and drizzle with margarine
6. Bake for 1/2 hour in the oven until browned
