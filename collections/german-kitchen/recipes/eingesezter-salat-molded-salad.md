## Ingredients

- sour aspic
- endive salad
- green beans
- bean kernels
- potatoes
- 4 tablespoons of fine Provençal oil
- 2 tablespoons of mustard
- yolk of 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons of vinegar

## Directions

1. In a beautiful aspic mold, let sour aspic run around knife-back thick and then set.
2. Line the mold beautifully with flowers, with endive salad that should be neither too large nor too small, with green beans, bean kernels, potatoes, press everything firmly in.
3. Stir together 4 tablespoons of fine Provençal oil, 2 tablespoons of mustard, the yolk of 2 hard-boiled eggs with 2 tablespoons of vinegar.
4. Pour it over the salad, let it stand for a while and turn it out onto the intended plate when serving.
