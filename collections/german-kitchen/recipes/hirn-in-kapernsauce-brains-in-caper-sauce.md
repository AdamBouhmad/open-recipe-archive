## Ingredients

- Brains
- Water
- Vinegar
- Butter
- Onion
- Lemon (yellow part and juice)
- Flour (1 cooking spoon)
- Cold water
- Meat broth (1 ladle)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Capers

## Directions

1. Soak and clean the brains.
2. Let them set firmly in equal parts water and vinegar.
3. In a casserole, add butter and place in the onion mixed with the yellow part of half a lemon; let it steam for a time.
4. Stir in one cooking spoon of flour, extinguish it with cold water, add one ladle of meat broth and let it cook for half an hour.
5. Add the brains, press in a little lemon juice and let it finish cooking.
6. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
