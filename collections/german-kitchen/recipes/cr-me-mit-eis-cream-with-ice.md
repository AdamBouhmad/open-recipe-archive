## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas milk
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- 1/8 pound sugar
- 9 egg yolks
- a little flour
- 6 egg whites
- 6 drops orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Cook half a maas of milk, a piece of whole cinnamon, and 1/8 pound sugar together in a brass pan.
2. Stir 9 egg yolks smooth with a little flour.
3. Stir the cooked milk into it.
4. Let everything come together once more in the pan.
5. Pour the cream through a hair sieve into a bowl and let it cool.
6. Beat 6 egg whites into stiff snow.
7. Stir 6 drops of orange blossom water and sugar into the snow until the ice is thick.
8. Pour the ice around the cream.
9. Make a spatula glowing and hold it over the cream until it is dry.
10. Serve cold.
