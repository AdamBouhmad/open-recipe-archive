## Ingredients

- 16 to 18 peeled Borsdorf apples
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 schoppen white wine
- 3 schoppen milk
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 cooking spoon flour
- 1/2 glass Maraschino
- Pastry dough
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Steam 16 to 18 peeled Borsdorf apples, from which the core has been removed, in a casserolle with 4 loth sugar and 1 schoppen white wine
2. Make a crème with Maraschino as follows: let 3 schoppen milk, 4 loth sugar and 1 piece cinnamon cook together for a time
3. Smooth 8 egg yolks and 1 cooking spoon flour together, pour the boiling milk over it along with 1/2 glass Maraschino
4. Let everything come to the boil once more in the pan, spurt it vigorously in a pot
5. Place the apples on a plate, make a firm water pastry dough and make a ring of it around the plate
6. Brush with an egg, pour the crème over the apples and let them bake golden in a baking oven
