## Ingredients

- 1 pint water
- 1/4 pound butter
- flour
- 1/2 quarter pound small raisins
- 3 loth pounded sugar
- cooked fruit
- prunes
- cherries
- apricots

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pint water and 1/4 pound butter together in a brass pan.
2. Stir it in a bowl with a little flour loosely so that it gets no lumps.
3. Add 1/2 quarter pound small raisins and 3 loth pounded sugar, mix everything together.
4. Tie it in a damp cloth, cook it half an hour in boiling water.
5. Serve with cooked fruit such as prunes, cherries, or apricots.
