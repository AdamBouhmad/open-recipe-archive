## Ingredients

- 1 pound finely ground sugar
- 20 egg yolks
- snow of 16 egg whites
- zest of 1 lemon, grated
- juice of 1 lemon
- 16 loth starch flour
- butter and sugar for the pan
- 1-1/2 loth fish glue
- 1 schoppen water
- zest of 1 orange
- 6 loth sugar
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- juice of 2 oranges
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 schoppen sweet cream for whipping
- citron or chocolate icing

## Directions

1. Stir 1 pound finely ground sugar with 20 egg yolks and the snow of 16 egg whites until the mass is thick
2. Add the grated zest and juice of 1 lemon
3. Mix in 16 loth starch flour
4. Brush a plafond 3 fingers high and not very wide with butter, sprinkle with sugar, and bake the torte in it
5. For the cream: dissolve 1-1/2 loth fish glue in 1 schoppen water
6. Pour the water through a hair sieve
7. Grate the zest of 1 orange with 6 loth sugar
8. Pour the fish glue water into a brass pan
9. Take the sugar, 1 schoppen sweet cream, juice of 2 oranges, and 6 egg yolks
10. Cook everything on the fire into a cream
11. Pour it through a hair sieve into a porcelain bowl and let it cool
12. Beat 1 schoppen sweet cream in a bowl like snow
13. Let it drain in a hair sieve and mix it into the cold cream
14. Place the torte on a plate and remove the lid from the top
15. Cut a 2-inch deep opening into the torte and hollow it out
16. Pour the cream into it
17. Replace the lid
18. Make a lemon or chocolate icing on the lid as has been described before
19. Decorate the torte with citron peel and preserved fruits
