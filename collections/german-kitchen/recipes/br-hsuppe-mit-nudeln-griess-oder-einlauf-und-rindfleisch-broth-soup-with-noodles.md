## Ingredients

- 250 g beef
- 1 tablespoon soup vegetables
- 1 l water
- 8 g salt
- 1 grated carrot or peel of a red onion

## Directions

1. Place the water with salt and any available cleaned bones on the stove covered; clean, wash and cut the soup vegetables; wash and pound the beef.
2. Once the water with the bones boils, add the meat; after it has cooked with the scum for some time, remove the scum, add the cut soup vegetables, and to color the broth a grated carrot or raw onion peel, seal the pot tightly and let the meat simmer slowly for 2-2½ hours. When the meat is tender, remove it and keep it warm, season the broth, remove the bones, skim the fat from the broth and add noodles, semolina or egg drop, cook the soup for another 10 minutes and serve soup and meat.
