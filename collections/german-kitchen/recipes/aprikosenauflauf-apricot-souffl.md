## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen sour cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 3 loth flour
- 4 egg whites
- butter for the dish
- apricots (fresh or preserved)
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Stir 1 schoppen sour cream with 4 egg yolks and 1/4 pound sugar until very thick.
2. Mix 3 loth flour and the snow from 4 egg whites into the mass.
3. Brush the dish that you will serve with butter and line it with apricots (fresh or preserved).
4. Fill the mass on top, bake the soufflé until golden, and sprinkle sugar on top.
