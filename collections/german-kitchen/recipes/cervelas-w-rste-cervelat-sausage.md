## Ingredients

- lean meat from a pork leg
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- basil
- coriander
- 1 pound of bacon to 4 pounds of meat

## Directions

1. Cut the lean meat from the pork leg
2. Mince this together with salt, pepper, cloves, basil, and coriander very finely
3. Pound it further in a mortar
4. Work it together in a bowl
5. Press the mass into a beef casing
6. Make sausages of desired size and hang them to smoke
7. These sausages can be served with peas and lentils
