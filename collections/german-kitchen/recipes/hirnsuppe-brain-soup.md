## Ingredients

- 2 veal brains
- Onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- Flour
- Meat broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place the veal brains in lukewarm water and remove the skin
2. Chop onion and parsley together and steam the brains with the chopped mixture in a casserole with butter for one quarter hour
3. Stir in half a tablespoon of flour to the brains without completely covering them
4. Pour in meat broth and bring to a boil
5. Season with salt and nutmeg
