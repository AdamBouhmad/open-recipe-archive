## Ingredients

- Half a Maas Arak
- 8 lemons
- Sugar
- 2 bottles wine
- One Schoppen good green tea
- One pound sugar

## Directions

1. Rub half of the lemons on sugar
2. Squeeze the juice from the other half of the lemons
3. Pour 2 bottles of wine, one Schoppen good green tea, and one pound sugar into a brass pan
4. Heat only until it begins to boil
5. Pour through a hair sieve directly into the punch bowl
6. Add another half Maas of Arak
