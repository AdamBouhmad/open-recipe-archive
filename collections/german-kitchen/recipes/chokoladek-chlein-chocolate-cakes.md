## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- half pound finely sifted sugar
- half pound grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat 2 egg whites to a stiff snow
2. Stir in half pound finely sifted sugar and half pound grated chocolate
3. Mix together
4. Place heaps of it on a black baking sheet brushed with wax and bake in the oven
