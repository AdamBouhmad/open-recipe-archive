## Ingredients

- white potatoes
- butter
- water
- milk

## Directions

1. Peel the white potatoes raw and place them in a casserole with a piece of butter
2. Add a ladle-full of water and let them cook soft
3. Rub them through a hair sieve
4. Put them back into a casserole, pour in half a measure of milk and a piece of butter
5. Stir the puree together for a quarter hour and place it back on coals so it becomes hot
6. When serving, let butter become hot in a small pan, arrange the potato puree on a plate and pour the butter over it
