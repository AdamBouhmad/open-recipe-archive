## Ingredients

- 30 g onion slices
- 1 spice dose
- 1 tablespoon soup greens
- 1/2 liter water
- eel (prepared as described above)
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the prepared eel into uniform pieces of any desired length
2. Clean each piece individually by pushing out the entrails with a whisk handle and then cleaning it of the inner skin
3. Rub with salt and wash
4. Scald the fish with boiling water mixed with 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
5. Cook it in a broth made from water, soup greens, spices, onion and some vinegar for approximately 1/4 hour until done
6. Arrange the blue eel pieces warm or cold
7. Serve with the eel broth
