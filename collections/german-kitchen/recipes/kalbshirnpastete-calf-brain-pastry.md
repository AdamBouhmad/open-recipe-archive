## Ingredients

- short butter pastry
- butter

## Directions

1. Fold a short butter pastry 3 to 4 times on top of each other
2. Roll it out 2 fingers thick and place it on a black sheet
3. Turn a large plate onto the rolled out cake and cut around the plate with a hot knife
4. Cut a second cake around the plate that will be served at table
5. Poke the lid or the smaller cake with a hot knife and press the edge with a hot knife
