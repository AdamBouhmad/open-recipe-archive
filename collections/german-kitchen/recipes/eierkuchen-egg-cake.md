## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound peeled, finely ground almonds
- 6 to 8 bitter almonds
- 1 lemon
- 1 schoppen sweet cream
- 1/4 pound beef marrow
- 2 knife points ground cinnamon
- some rose water
- 1 knife point salt
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Weigh 1/4 pound peeled, finely ground almonds with 6 to 8 bitter almonds among them, and the yellow of 1 lemon very fine, pour 1 schoppen sweet cream to it
2. Cut 1/4 pound beef marrow very fine, add 2 knife points ground cinnamon, some rose water, 1 knife point salt, 8 eggs
3. Stir everything together, butter a cake pan, fill the mass into it and bake the cake in the oven until golden
