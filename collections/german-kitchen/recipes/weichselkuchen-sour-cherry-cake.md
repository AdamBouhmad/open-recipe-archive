## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pound sour cherries
- 10 loth melted butter
- 6 rolls or milk breads
- water
- 10 loth ground almonds
- 6 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- cinnamon
- cloves
- 4 egg whites snow
- plafond with butter dough

## Directions

1. Remove stems and stones from 1-1/2 pound sour cherries
2. Put 10 loth melted butter in a bowl
3. Cut the crust from 6 rolls or milk breads
4. Soak them in water and press out firmly
5. Stir 10 loth ground almonds, 6 whole eggs, 4 egg yolks, a little cinnamon and cloves with all the above for half an hour
6. Mix the snow from 4 egg whites with it
7. Line a plafond with butter dough
8. Pour the stirred mixture onto it
9. Bake the cake in the oven
