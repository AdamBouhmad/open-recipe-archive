## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 eggs
- 4 lots butter

## Directions

1. Make a noodle dough from 3 to 4 eggs
2. Work 4 lots butter into it
3. Roll out strongly to the thickness of a knife back
4. Cut out 4 pieces with a tin rose mold
5. Brush each piece in the middle
6. Lay the 4 pieces on top of each other
7. Press them firmly together in the middle
8. Bake them in hot lard
9. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
