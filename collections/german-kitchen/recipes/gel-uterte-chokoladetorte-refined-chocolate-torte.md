## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound almonds
- egg white
- 4 eggs and yolks
- 1/2 glass water
- 1/2 pound fine sugar
- 6 loth finely grated chocolate
- 1/2 loth cinnamon
- butter and sugar for the pan

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 pound almonds and grate them very finely with egg white
2. Stir them in a bowl with 4 eggs and yolks for half an hour
3. Pour 1/2 glass water and 1/2 pound fine sugar into a brass pan
4. Cook until the sugar spins thick threads
5. Mix 6 loth finely grated chocolate with the cooked sugar and cinnamon
6. Add this to the stirred almond mass
7. Stir everything well together
8. Brush a cake mold with butter and sprinkle with sugar
9. Fill the mass into it
10. Bake the torte in the oven
