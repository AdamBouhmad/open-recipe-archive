## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- 4 eggs
- water or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. In a bowl, beat 4 eggs into 1/4 pound flour and mix with water or milk (do not beat the dough as it will become tough)
2. In a pan, bring water to boil and add a handful of salt
3. Dip a sieve into the water and press the dough through it with a cooking spoon
4. Place the spätzle in a sieve and pour cold water over them
5. If serving with soup, pour broth instead of cold water
6. If serving roasted, fry them in hot lard in a pan on both sides until golden yellow
7. Mix 2 eggs with 3 spoonfuls of sour cream, pour over the spätzle, let set, turn once more and serve
