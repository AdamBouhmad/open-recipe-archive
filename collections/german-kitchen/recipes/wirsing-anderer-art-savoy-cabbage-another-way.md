## Ingredients

- savoy cabbage
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- flour
- pepper
- nutmeg
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean the savoy cabbage and cut off the ribs and wash it
2. Braise it for a quarter hour in a piece of butter with onions and parsley
3. Sprinkle a little flour, pepper, and nutmeg over it
4. Pour meat broth over it
