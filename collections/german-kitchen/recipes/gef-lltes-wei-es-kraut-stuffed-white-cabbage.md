## Ingredients

- 1 head white cabbage
- salt water
- 1 pound pork forcemeat
- 1 soaked and squeezed bread roll
- onions
- parsley
- 3-4 eggs
- meat broth
- butter sauce or jus

## Directions

1. Cook 1 head of white cabbage half tender in salt water.
2. Prepare a filling of: 1 pound pork forcemeat mixed with 1 soaked and squeezed bread roll, finely minced onions and parsley, and 3-4 eggs.
3. Remove leaves from the head down to the heart leaf.
4. Spread one spoonful of filling on each leaf.
5. Reassemble the head and bind firmly with string.
6. Place in a roasting pan, pour a little meat broth over it, and bake in the oven.
7. Serve with either butter sauce or jus.
