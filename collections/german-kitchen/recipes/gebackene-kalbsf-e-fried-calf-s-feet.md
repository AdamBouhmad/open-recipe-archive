## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- salt water
- 1 Schoppen water
- butter (hen egg-sized)
- flour
- 3 to 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the feet
2. Cook them soft in salt water
3. Cut them into small pieces
4. Make a batter: let 1 schoppen water and hen egg-sized butter cook together
5. Add flour until it becomes a thick porridge
6. Let it cook until it separates from the pan
7. Remove from fire
8. Stir in 3 to 4 eggs
9. Dip the pieces one after another into the batter
10. Fry them golden brown
