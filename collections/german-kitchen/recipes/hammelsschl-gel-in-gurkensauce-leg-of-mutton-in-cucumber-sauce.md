## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- meat broth
- vinegar
- onions
- basil
- lemon herb
- parsnip root
- 2 cucumbers
- butter
- parsley
- 1 lemon
- capers
- 2 anchovies
- flour
- wine
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove fat and end bone from the leg of mutton and cut off the lower bone
2. Pound the leg well and braise it nicely golden in a covered casserole with 1 spoonful of meat broth, some vinegar, onions, basil, lemon herb, and parsnip root
3. Peel 2 cucumbers, cut them as for a salad, remove the seeds, and braise them soft in a casserole with butter
4. Pound together a handful of clean washed parsley, 1 whole lemon with its yellow zest and pulp (but not the white skin), 1 spoonful of capers, and 2 clean washed anchovies
5. Add them to the cucumbers and let everything braise together for another quarter hour
6. Thicken with 1 spoonful of flour, add 1 spoonful of jus and some wine, pepper, and salt
7. Lift out the meat, place it on a dish, strain the sauce through a fine sieve, return it to a casserole with the cucumbers, and let it cook a while longer
