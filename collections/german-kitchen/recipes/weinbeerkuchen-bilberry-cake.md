## Ingredients

- 16 loth grated black bread
- one grated nutmeg
- 2 knife points cloves
- half loth ground cinnamon
- butter dough
- 12 egg yolks
- half pound ground sugar
- 8 loth melted butter
- half pound raisins
- 12 egg whites snow

## Directions

1. Mix 16 loth grated black bread, one grated nutmeg, 2 knife points cloves, half loth ground cinnamon together
2. Cover a cake sheet with butter dough
3. Stir 12 egg yolks, half pound ground sugar and 8 loth melted butter together for a quarter hour
4. Add half pound raisins and the snow from the 12 egg whites
5. Mix the bread with the rest
6. Fill everything onto the butter dough
7. Bake the cake in the oven
