## Ingredients

- snails
- 4 pieces anchovies
- 1 onion
- zest of 1 lemon
- butter
- flour
- meat broth
- 1/2 glass wine
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the snails as described in the 'filled snails' recipe
2. Chop finely 4 anchovies, 1 onion and the zest of 1 lemon
3. Let a piece of butter with a little flour turn yellow in a casserole
4. Add the chopped mixture and turn it a couple of times in the butter
5. Pour on one spoon of meat broth, 1/2 glass wine and the juice of 1 lemon
6. Add the snails to the sauce and let everything boil together once more
