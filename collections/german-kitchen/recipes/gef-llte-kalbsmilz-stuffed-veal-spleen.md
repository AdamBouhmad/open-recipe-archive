## Ingredients

- Veal spleen
- Half a pound of fatty pork
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fat
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Juniper berries

## Directions

1. Do not cut the spleen completely open.
2. Cut half a pound of fatty pork into pieces and mix it with salt and pepper.
3. Stuff the spleen with this mixture, sew it closed again.
4. Let it braise beautifully golden in a covered casserole with a piece of fat, onions, bay leaves, juniper berries and salt.
