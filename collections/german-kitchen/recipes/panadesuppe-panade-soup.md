## Ingredients

- 4 Mundbrot (mouth bread)
- Cold water
- 3 Loth butter
- Eggs
- Milk
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Chives

## Directions

1. Cut 4 Mundbrot as for a soup
2. Put it in a casserole, pour cold water over it and let it cook with 3 Loth butter for half an hour
3. Pass it through a hair sieve into the casserole and let it come to a boil once more
4. Add salt and nutmeg
5. Beat 2 eggs into the soup bowl
6. Pour a little milk over them
7. Serve the soup over and sprinkle with cut chives and nutmeg
