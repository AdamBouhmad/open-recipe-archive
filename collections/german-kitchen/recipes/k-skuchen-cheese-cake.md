## Ingredients

- yeast or puff pastry dough
- whey or non-sour curd cheese
- 1/2 schoppen thick, sour cream
- 4 whole eggs
- 2 spoons fine flour
- 1 handful chives
- salt

## Directions

1. Line a cake pan with yeast or puff pastry dough
2. Mix whey or curd cheese that is not too sour with 1/2 schoppen thick, sour cream
3. Add 4 whole eggs and 2 spoons fine flour
4. Add 1 handful chives and a knife tip of salt
5. Stir everything together for a quarter hour
6. Pour the mass onto the cake
7. Cut a little butter on top
8. Bake
