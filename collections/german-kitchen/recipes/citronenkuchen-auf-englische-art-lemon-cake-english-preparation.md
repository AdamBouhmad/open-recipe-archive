## Ingredients

- 6 lemon peels
- lemon pulp
- 1 orange
- orange juice
- 3/4 pound ground sugar
- 1/2 pound blanched, roughly ground almonds
- 2 handfuls flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 spoon ground sugar
- butter
- rose water
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Finely chop the peels of 6 lemons
2. Mix the pulp with an orange rubbed on sugar and the orange juice
3. Add 3/4 pound ground sugar and 1/2 pound blanched, roughly ground almonds
4. Let stand for one hour
5. Make a dough with 2 handfuls flour, 2 egg yolks, 1 spoon ground sugar, butter the size of a chicken egg, and rose water on a pastry board
6. Roll out the dough and place it in a springform pan coated with butter
7. Beat 6 eggs and add to the mass described above
8. Fill into the pan and bake the cake
9. After baking, it can be glazed or topped with lemon ice
