## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/4 pound finely ground almonds
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 4 loth finely ground sugar
- 1/4 pound fine flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients together into a dough
2. Form thumb-sized sticks pointed at both ends
3. Make crosshatch marks with a knife
4. Place on a black baking sheet coated with lard
5. Brush with egg white
6. Sprinkle with coarsely ground sugar
7. Bake in the oven
