## Ingredients

- beans
- onions
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as in the previous recipe but not quite as soft
2. Steam onions and parsley in butter in a casserole
3. Add the beans and steam everything together until soft
4. Sprinkle a little flour over them
5. Add a little beef broth, nutmeg, and some sugar
6. The beans are very tasty and good prepared this way
