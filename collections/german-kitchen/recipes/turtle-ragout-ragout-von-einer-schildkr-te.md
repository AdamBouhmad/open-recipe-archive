## Ingredients

- 1 turtle, head and feet
- hot water
- roots
- onions
- salt
- cloves
- 1/8 pound butter
- 6 to 8 onions, cut
- flour
- 1 drinking glass wine
- meat broth or pea broth
- 1 schoppen Madeira wine
- Spanish pepper
- 1/4 pound truffles, cleaned and prepared

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and feet of the turtle
2. Scald the turtle with hot water and clean the shell well
3. Cook the turtle on the fire with some roots, onions, salt and cloves until the shell can be lifted (takes about one strong hour)
4. Remove the hard skin from the top, also the liver and eggs
5. In a casserole, let 6 to 8 cut onions turn yellow in 1/8 pound butter, stir in one cooking spoon of flour
6. Let it turn yellow, pour on 1 drinking glass of wine and some meat broth (pea broth is also good)
7. Let it boil together for half an hour, press through a sieve
8. Pour it back into the casserole with 1 schoppen Madeira wine, a knife point of Spanish pepper, 1/4 pound cleaned and prepared truffles cut into leaves
9. Let it cook together for another quarter hour
10. Arrange the turtle on a plate and serve the sauce separately
