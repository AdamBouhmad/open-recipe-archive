## Ingredients

- white pike
- lemon juice
- butter
- lemon
- fish forcemeat
- eggs
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Scale a fine white pike thoroughly and wash it well
2. Clean it out and cut off the head and tail
3. Split lengthwise and remove the backbone
4. Clean thoroughly with a cloth and cut into 4 pieces
5. Place the pieces in lemon juice, turning them frequently, and if time permits, let them stand overnight
6. The next day, place the pike on a platter with a large piece of butter and lemon juice
7. Put a lemon slice on each piece
8. Place paper thickly buttered over the pike
9. Finely chop 1 pound of fish forcemeat
10. Let 1 quarter-pound of butter melt in a bowl
11. Add the fish forcemeat to it and beat in 3 eggs
12. Add the yellow of half a lemon grated on a grater, nutmeg, and salt
13. Stir until very foamy
14. Place 2 buttered springforms on the platter where the pike lies
15. Fill with the fish forcemeat
