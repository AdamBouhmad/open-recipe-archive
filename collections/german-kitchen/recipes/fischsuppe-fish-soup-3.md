## Ingredients

- Fish (usually whitefish, pike perch, or barbel, or also eel)
- Butter
- Onion for 2 kreuzer worth
- Fish bones
- Parsley
- Water or meat broth (on fast days, water)

## Directions

1. Scrape the flesh from a fish of any kind, removing it from the bones
2. Place a piece of butter in a casserole, cut onion, chop the fish bones, add some parsley
3. Steam everything together in the casserole for one hour
4. On fast days pour water over it, let it cook for one hour, then strain through a hair sieve
5. Return it to the casserole and let it come to a boil again
6. Make small dumplings from the scraped fish meat, as has been described before, and arrange the cooked mixture over them
