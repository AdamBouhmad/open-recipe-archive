## Ingredients

- medlars and plums as needed
- raisins to taste
- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- 50 to 60 pieces walnuts
- cinnamon, cloves, anise, figs, 1 glass cherry spirit
- wheat flour and some beer yeast
- medlar broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil medlars and plums
2. Add raisins as desired
3. Cut blanched almonds lengthwise
4. Break walnuts into 50-60 pieces
5. Combine with spices and cherry spirit
6. Mix wheat flour and beer yeast with cooked medlar broth
7. Make a starter dough and let it rise in warmth
8. When ripe, work it together thoroughly and salt it
9. Let it rise again until half-risen
10. Form into loaves
11. Work in the spiced medlars
12. Brush loaves with medlar broth
13. Let them rise once more
14. Bake in the oven
