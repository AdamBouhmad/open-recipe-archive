## Ingredients

- 3 Schoppen milk
- One finger-long piece of vanilla
- 4 Loth blanched almonds
- 2 Loth sugar
- Bread

## Directions

1. Put 3 Schoppen milk on the fire
2. Add a finger-long piece of vanilla
3. Let it cook until 1 Schoppen has cooked down
4. Remove the vanilla with a skimming spoon
5. Add 4 Loth blanched and finely pounded almonds and 2 Loth sugar to the milk
6. Remove the hard crust from a piece of bread and cut it into fine soup slices
7. Serve the milk over the bread
