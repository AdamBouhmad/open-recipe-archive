## Ingredients

- salmon trout
- salt
- wine
- vinegar
- lemon
- bay leaves
- cloves
- pepper
- 8 hard-boiled eggs
- 8 fresh egg yolks
- light jelly
- 2 tablespoons Provence oil
- 1 tablespoon jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the salmon trout, cut out the ears and gut it
2. Rub with salt and season with wine, vinegar, lemon, bay leaves, cloves, and pepper
3. Cook slowly so it does not burst, then place on the designated plate
4. For the sauce, press the yolks from 8 hard-boiled eggs through a fine sieve, beat in 8 fresh egg yolks
5. Add one spoon of light jelly and stir constantly to one side for half an hour
6. Add 2 tablespoons Provence oil drop by drop, stirring constantly for 2 hours
7. Add another tablespoon of jelly (if you have ice, you can stir the jelly over it)
8. The sauce must be as thick as pomade, then spread it on the fish, but not on its head
