## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 kg (3 Pfd.) pears
- 3/4 kg (1-1/2 Pfd.) sugar
- 1/2 l wine vinegar, not too sharp
- 1 piece lemon zest
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 10 cloves
- 2 tablespoons wine vinegar
- 125 g sugar
- 1/4 l water

## Directions

1. Preparation: Prepare smaller pears by peeling them whole, larger pears by halving them and carefully coring the seed cavity, or table pears by peeling and cutting into quarters. While preparing the pears, dissolve 3/4 kg (1-1/2 Pfd.) sugar in vinegar and skim.
2. Preparation: Cook the spices (in a muslin bag) with the vinegar and sugar. Blanch the pears in water, sugar and vinegar, then lay them drained into the sugar vinegar and cook them until soft.
3. Fill the pears into glasses or pots and reduce the sugar vinegar until it falls in broad drops from the spoon. After it cools, pour it over the pears (leaving the spices behind) and seal the containers as directed.
4. After a few days, pour the pears onto a colander, cook the thinned vinegar down to the second degree, and after it cools, pour it over the pears returned to the containers.
5. If the liquid is still thinned by the juice from the pears, it must be reduced again; if it has reduced too much, sugar and vinegar must be added.
