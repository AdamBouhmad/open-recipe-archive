## Ingredients

- Bread slices
- Fat
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Dried fruit
- Lingonberries
- Apple puree
- Wine sauce

## Directions

1. Fry bread slices in fat until dark brown color
2. Sprinkle thickly with sugar and cinnamon
3. Add dried fruit, lingonberries, and apple puree or serve with wine sauce
4. Alternatively, soften bread slices first in milk or sugar water, prepare a thin pancake batter from 20 g flour and the stated ingredients, dip the softened slices in the batter, and bake
