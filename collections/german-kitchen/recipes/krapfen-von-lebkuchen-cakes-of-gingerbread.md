## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound grated brown gingerbread
- 2 loth finely pounded sugar
- 3 spoonfuls honey
- some fine spices
- 2 loth grated almonds
- 1 egg
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. From 1/4 pound grated brown gingerbread, 2 loth finely pounded sugar, 3 spoonfuls honey, some fine spices, 2 loth grated almonds, 1 egg, and 1 egg white, make a firm dough on the noodle board
2. Roll it out
3. Cut it out with a jagged mold
4. Lay the cut pieces on a black sheet
5. Bake them at mild heat in the oven so that they remain still white, almost only dried
