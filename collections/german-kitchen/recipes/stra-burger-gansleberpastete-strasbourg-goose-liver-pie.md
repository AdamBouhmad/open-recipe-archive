## Ingredients

- 6 goose livers
- salt
- half pound pork forcemeat
- 2 eggs
- 1 soaked and pressed rusk
- half pound crushed truffles
- half pound cut truffles
- 1 quint crushed mace
- 1 crushed nutmeg
- half loth pepper
- a handful of chopped and yellow-braised shallot onions
- 3 lemons
- half Württemberg Metze flour
- three quarters pound lard
- half pound butter
- cold water
- 4 loth grated Parmesan cheese
- bacon slices
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Place 6 goose livers on a sheet of paper on a rack.
2. Sprinkle salt on the livers to draw out water.
3. Pound 3 of them as fine as a paste and cut the other 3 into small pieces, placing them in a dish.
4. Rub half pound pork forcemeat, 2 eggs, and 1 soaked and pressed rusk on a grater to a foam.
5. Add this to the pounded goose liver, half pound crushed and half pound cut truffles, 1 quint crushed mace, 1 crushed nutmeg, half loth pepper, salt, a handful of chopped and yellow-braised shallot onions, the juice from 3 lemons.
6. Mix everything together well and set aside.
7. Make a dough from half Württemberg Metze flour, three quarters pound lard, half pound butter, salt, and cold water.
8. Work it together firmly.
9. Roll out a base the thickness of a small finger and make it plate-sized.
10. Press the edge down firmly with the finger, keeping the dough finger-thick.
11. Make the pie 2 to 3 hands high.
12. Sprinkle 4 loth grated Parmesan cheese on the pie.
13. Place bacon slices and a handful of the pounded goose liver on top.
14. Then a layer of truffles.
15. Place the cut goose livers on the truffles, putting a mushroom on each piece, and continue until all is used.
16. Make a finger-thick lid from the butter dough.
17. Crimp it firmly with a pie crimper, but leave the edge 2 fingers width empty.
18. Make a small opening in the top of the lid and insert a plug, which remove when the pie is baked.
19. It is better to bake the pie the next day.
20. Brush it with eggs, make various flowers in the lid with the pie crimper.
21. Place it in a plafond so the fat does not run in the oven.
22. Let it bake 2 to 3 hours and serve it cold.
