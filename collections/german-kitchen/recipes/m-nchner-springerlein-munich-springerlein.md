## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound blanched almonds
- rose water
- 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar
- 1/8 pound starch flour
- flour and sugar for dusting
- wax
- lemon or cinnamon icing

## Directions

1. Grind 1/2 pound blanched almonds finely with rose water
2. Stir them together with 1/2 pound finely sifted sugar and 1/8 pound starch flour in a brass pan until the dough pulls away from the sides
3. Transfer to a pastry board and dust with flour and sugar
4. Dust the wooden molds with sifted sugar and press the dough into them
5. Turn out the molds and place the springerlein on a black baking sheet brushed with wax
6. Let stand overnight or for at least 2 to 3 hours
7. Bake at gentle heat and top with lemon or cinnamon icing if desired
