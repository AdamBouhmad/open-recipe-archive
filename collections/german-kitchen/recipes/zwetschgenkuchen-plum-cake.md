## Ingredients

- butter dough
- plums
- 3 loths of ground sugar
- 2 knife points of cinnamon
- 2 loths of butter

## Directions

1. Line a baking sheet with butter dough
2. Pit the plums
3. Cover the cake thickly with plums, arranged so that the cut side faces upward
4. Sprinkle with 3 loths of ground sugar and 2 knife points of cinnamon
5. Cut 2 loths of butter into small pieces over the top
6. Bake the cake
