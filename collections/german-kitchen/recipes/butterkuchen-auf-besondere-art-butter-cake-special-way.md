## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 3 quarter-pound butter
- 3 spoonfuls beer yeast
- 2 knife-tips mace
- 3 tablespoons rose water
- 2 Loth finely ground sugar
- quarter-pound butter
- 6 eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough from 1 pound of fine flour, 3 quarter-pound of butter, 3 spoonfuls of beer yeast, 2 knife-tips of mace, 3 tablespoons of rose water, and 2 Loth of finely ground sugar
2. Work it for a long time
3. Set it to a gentle warmth and let it rise
4. Place it on a noodle board and roll it out thumb-thick
5. Work a quarter-pound of butter into it
6. Coat a piece of paper with butter and place the cake on it
7. Put it in a springform pan
8. Beat 6 eggs and pour them over the cake
9. Place it in the oven
10. Bake it beautifully golden
11. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over it
