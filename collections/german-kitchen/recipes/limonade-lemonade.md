## Ingredients

- 1/4 lemon or orange peel
- 25 g sugar
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice or 6 tablespoons orange juice

## Directions

1. Finely peel the lemon or orange peel
2. Add the sugar and pour boiling water over it
3. Once cooled, add lemon or orange juice through a sieve
4. Remove the peel and serve the lemonade cold or add a piece of artificial ice; it can also be served warm as a sweat-inducing remedy
