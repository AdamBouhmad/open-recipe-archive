## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe quinces
- three-quarters pound sugar per pound of pulp
- water

## Directions

1. Peel beautiful ripe quinces
2. Cook them in water until soft
3. Scrape off the pulp
4. Force it through a hair sieve (to one pound of pulp add three-quarters pound of sugar)
5. Cook everything together in a pan until no juice is visible
6. Store it in a porcelain container
