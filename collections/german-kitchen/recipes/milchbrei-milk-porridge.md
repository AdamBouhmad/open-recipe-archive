## Ingredients

- 1 cooking spoon flour
- sweet cream
- 6 whole eggs
- sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1/2 maas milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir 1 cooking spoon flour smooth with sweet cream, beat 6 whole eggs into it, some sugar grated from a lemon
2. Pour 1/2 maas milk to it, butter a bowl, fill the mass into it and bake it a nice yellow
