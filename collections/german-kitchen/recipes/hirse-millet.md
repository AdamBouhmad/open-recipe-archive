## Ingredients

- 100 g millet
- 10 g butter
- 3/4 l skim milk
- 8 g salt

## Directions

1. Pick over the millet and rinse it three times with cold water, draining each time when hot.
2. Fry the millet with butter, pour the milk over it, salt the dish and let it swell while stirring frequently. Alternatively, you can swell the millet with water and 20 g butter and add 30 g brown butter.
