## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound oat groats
- water
- peel and juice of 2 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour 1 schoppen water onto 1/4 pound oat groats
2. Let it stand for 3 days, adding 2 maas fresh water each day
3. On the fourth day, push it through a hair sieve
4. Cook the orange peel and juice, lemon juice, and a piece of sugar with the strained liquid for a quarter hour
5. Strain the jelly through a hair sieve
6. Give to the patient one spoonful at a time
