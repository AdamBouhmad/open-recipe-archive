## Ingredients

- Schöne reife Johannisbeeren (Beautiful ripe currants)
- Zucker (Sugar)
- Wasser (Water)

## Directions

1. Strip beautiful ripe currants from their stems (for 2 pounds of currants, use 1½ pounds of sugar by weight)
2. Place the sugar in a brass pan and add one bottle's measure of water
3. Cook until it gives off bubbles
4. Add the currants and let them cook, skimming off the foam carefully
5. Test a drop on a plate to see if they are done
6. If done, place them in a dish and store
