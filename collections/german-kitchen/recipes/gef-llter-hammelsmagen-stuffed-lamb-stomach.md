## Ingredients

- Lamb stomach
- 2 milk rolls
- Water
- Onions
- Parsley
- Piece of lard
- 2 lamb kidneys
- Salt
- Chives
- 3 eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape the stomach thoroughly and wash it
2. Grate 2 milk rolls on a grater, soak them in water, press them out firmly, and put them in a bowl
3. Chop onions and parsley, braise them in a piece of lard, and add to the bread
4. Chop 2 lamb kidneys and add them
5. Mix everything together with salt, chives, and 3 eggs
6. Fill the mixture into the stomach and tie it closed at the top
7. Simmer it in salted water for half an hour
8. Bake it in butter until golden brown
