## Ingredients

- leftover meat
- onion
- Kreuzer bread
- water
- butter
- 2 to 3 eggs
- salt
- nutmeg
- 2 to 3 handfuls flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 half Schoppen sour cream
- 2 Loth butter

## Directions

1. Take leftover meat and weigh it finely with an onion
2. Soak a Kreuzer bread in water and press it out firmly
3. Braise this with butter in a casserole for a quarter hour
4. Stir in 2 to 3 eggs
5. Sprinkle salt and nutmeg over it
6. Make a dough on a noodle board from 2 to 3 handfuls flour, 2 eggs, half a schoppen sour cream and 2 loth butter, like for noodles
7. Roll it out, fold it over again, roll it out again, but not as fine as noodles
8. Brush the sheet with eggs
9. Place the mixture on it
10. Fold it over
