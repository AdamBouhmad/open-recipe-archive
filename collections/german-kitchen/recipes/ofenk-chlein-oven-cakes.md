## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen water
- 4 loth butter
- 2 loth sugar
- 10 loth fine flour
- 4 to 5 eggs
- Jam or chopped almonds
- Sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Boil water, butter, and sugar together in a brass pan
2. Stir in fine flour and cook until the dough pulls away from the pan
3. Transfer dough to a bowl and stir in eggs one at a time
4. Place on a dark, flour-dusted baking sheet
5. Sprinkle with jam, chopped almonds, and sugar
6. Bake in gentle heat
