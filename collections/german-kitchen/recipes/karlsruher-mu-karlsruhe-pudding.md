## Ingredients

- 6 spoonfuls fine flour
- 8 Loth pounded sugar
- milk
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 lemon
- half a Maas milk
- 7 egg whites
- coarsely pounded sugar
- finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. In a brass pan stir 6 spoonfuls fine flour and 8 Loth pounded sugar smooth with milk.
2. Take 7 egg yolks and 1 lemon grated on a grater.
3. Pour half a Maas milk to it.
4. Cook a thick cream from it.
5. Remove from fire and stir until somewhat cooled.
6. Mix in the snow from 7 egg whites.
7. Pour all into a butter-coated plate.
8. Sprinkle coarsely pounded sugar and finely chopped almonds over it.
9. Let the pudding bake nicely. It is ready in half an hour.
