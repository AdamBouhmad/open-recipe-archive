## Ingredients

- Young beans
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from young beans.
2. Tie them to a strong thread.
3. Heat water in a kettle or pan.
4. Put the beans in, let boiling water pass over them.
5. Remove them and hang them in a dry place where they have air.
6. Preserve them for winter; they can also be dried or smoked.
