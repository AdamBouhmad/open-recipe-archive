## Ingredients

- hare
- onions
- lemons
- bay leaves
- juniper berries
- meat broth
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and feet of the hare
2. Cut the breast not too close to the saddle so it gets a nice shape
3. Skin the roast, lard it, and roast it nicely golden in a roasting pan with cut onions, lemons, bay leaves, and juniper berries along with 1 spoonful of meat broth
4. Add salt and spices as desired
5. Note: if the game has not been marinated, it must be poured over with boiling vinegar in which onions are
