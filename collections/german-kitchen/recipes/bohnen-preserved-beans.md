## Ingredients

- very small, half-grown beans
- salt
- grape leaves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from the beans and cut them as desired, large or small
2. Boil water in a kettle, salt it well and add the beans
3. Cook for 4 minutes, quickly remove them and place in cold water for a quarter hour
4. Place in a basket to drain well and dry on a noodle board
5. Preserve in a well-pitched small barrel as follows: lay grape leaves on the bottom, some coarsely pounded pepper and salt, then a layer of beans, pepper and salt on top, then again beans, continuing until the barrel is full
6. Press the beans down firmly by hand and cover the top of the barrel again with grape leaves
