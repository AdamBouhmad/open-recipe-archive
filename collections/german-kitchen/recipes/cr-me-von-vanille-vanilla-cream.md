## Ingredients

- half a maas milk
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 1 piece whole vanilla
- 4 loth sugar
- 3 fingers full flour
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Let half a maas milk, one piece whole cinnamon, one piece whole vanilla, and 4 loth sugar cook together for a while
2. Mix 3 fingers full flour and 8 egg yolks together, stir slowly into the cooked milk
3. Let everything come to a boil once more in the pan
4. Press the cream through a fine sieve into a dish, put a cover with weak coals on it
5. Place the dish in boiling water and let the cream become firm
6. Burn it with a glowing shovel, sprinkle sugar on it and serve warm
