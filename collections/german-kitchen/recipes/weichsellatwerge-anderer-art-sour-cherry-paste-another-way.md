## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe sour cherries (wine cherries are even better)
- half pound sugar per pound of pulp

## Directions

1. Pit beautiful ripe sour cherries (wine cherries are even better)
2. Force them through a hair sieve
3. For every amount of pulp, take the same amount in half-pounds of sugar
4. Cook them together in a pan until no juice is visible, then let it cool
5. Store it in wooden boxes or in a porcelain container
