## Ingredients

- 1 pound logwood
- 2 maas water
- 1 maas vinegar
- 3/4 pound coarsely pounded gall nuts
- 4 loth alum
- 12 loth dried iron vitriol
- 8 loth gum arabic

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound logwood with 2 maas water until only 1 maas remains
2. When the logwood has settled to the bottom, pour this off
3. In an earthenware container, stir together 1 maas vinegar, 3/4 pound coarsely pounded gall nuts, 4 loth alum, 12 loth dried iron vitriol and 8 loth gum arabic together with the decocted liquid using a wooden stick thoroughly
4. Let it stand for 14 days and the ink is then ready for use
5. The liquid ink poured off and the residue consisting of the above ingredients, boiled again, gives again good ink which, mixed with the first ink, further enhances its quality
