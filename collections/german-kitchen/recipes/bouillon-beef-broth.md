## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef
- 2 pounds veal bones
- 1 old hen
- cold water
- salt
- 2 scraped and cut celery roots
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 yellow carrots
- leek
- parsley root
- 1 onion studded with 4 cloves

## Directions

1. Put the beef, veal bones, and hen together in a three-measure pot with cold water and some salt
2. Bring to a boil and skim off the foam diligently
3. Add the scraped and cut celery roots, bay leaves, yellow carrots, leek, parsley root, and the onion studded with cloves
4. Let it cook over gentle heat until the meat is tender
5. Remove the meat, pour the broth through a hair sieve, skim off the fat, and set it in a cool place
6. This bouillon is used for all white sauces
