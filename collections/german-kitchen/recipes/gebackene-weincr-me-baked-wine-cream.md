## Ingredients

- half a maas wine
- 1 lemon peel
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 1 quarter pound sugar
- a little flour
- cold water
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- finely cut almonds
- coarsely pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Let half a maas wine cook with the peel of a lemon, one piece whole cinnamon, and a quarter pound sugar
2. Stir a little flour smooth with cold water, beat 8 egg yolks into it
3. Stir this into the boiling wine and let everything cook once more in the pan
4. Pour the cream through a fine sieve onto the plate
5. Beat the 8 egg whites into snow and form round balls the size of a hen's egg
6. Sprinkle them with finely cut almonds and coarsely pounded sugar
7. Place the cream in the oven for a quarter hour before serving
