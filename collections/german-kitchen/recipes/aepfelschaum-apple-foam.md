## Ingredients

- 6 Borsdorf apples
- Quarter pound finely sifted sugar
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Roast 6 Borsdorf apples
2. Scrape out the pulp and stir it with quarter pound finely sifted sugar for half an hour
3. Beat the whites of 3 eggs into stiff snow and mix it with the yolks
