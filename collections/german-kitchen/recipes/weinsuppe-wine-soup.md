## Ingredients

- 1 measure of wine
- 1 cooking spoonful of flour
- cold water
- 4 eggs
- 3 lots of sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- roasted bread pieces

## Directions

1. Pour one measure of wine into a pan
2. Stir one cooking spoonful of flour mixed with cold water into it
3. Beat in 4 eggs
4. Add 3 lots of sugar with the zest of half a lemon mixed in
5. Pour in half a measure of cold water
6. Let it cook while constantly stirring
7. Serve over roasted bread pieces
