## Ingredients

- 125 g carrots
- 125 g snap beans
- 250 g potatoes
- 1 l water
- 1 tablespoon lean bacon cubes
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 5 g salt
- 1 pinch pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the carrots as described above, but cut into cubes, as well as the peeled broken beans.
2. Cook the carrots, beans and potatoes in salted water until soft.
3. Mix the vegetables with the fried bacon and vinegar and season with pepper and salt.
