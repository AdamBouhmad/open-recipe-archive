## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) raw peeled potatoes
- 5 g salt
- 1/4 l water
- 375 g apples
- 1/4 l water
- 10 g onions
- 20 g fatty bacon
- vinegar to taste
- 20 g sugar
- 1 pinch salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into quarters. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters and remove the core. Cut the onion into small cubes.
2. Cook the potatoes like salted potatoes. Cook the apples quickly with 1/4 l water and mix with the drained, dry potato pieces.
3. Fry the onion in the rendered bacon fat from which the cracklings have been removed; set the cracklings aside in a warm place.
4. Mix the onion, fat, sugar, salt and vinegar into the dish, season it strongly to taste, arrange it in the vegetable bowl in a mound shape and sprinkle the bacon cracklings over it.
