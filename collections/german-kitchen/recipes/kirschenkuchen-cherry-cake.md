## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/2 pound ground sugar
- 1/2 pound ground almonds, unpeeled
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- 1/4 pound citron and orange peel
- 2 rolls with crusts removed, soaked and pressed
- 1 loth ground cinnamon
- 1 quint ground cloves
- 4 egg whites for snow
- 1 pound pitted cherries

## Directions

1. Melt 1/2 pound butter in a bowl
2. Add 1/2 pound ground sugar, 1/2 pound ground unpeeled almonds, 12 egg yolks, and 6 egg whites
3. Stir everything for 3/4 hour
4. When thick, add 1/4 pound citron and orange peel, not too finely chopped
5. Add 2 rolls with crusts removed, soaked and pressed
6. Add 1 loth ground cinnamon and 1 quint ground cloves
7. Stir everything again
8. Beat snow from 4 egg whites and fold in
9. Press 1 pound pitted cherries gently into the cake with a spoon just before baking
10. Bake in a pan coated with butter until beautiful yellow
