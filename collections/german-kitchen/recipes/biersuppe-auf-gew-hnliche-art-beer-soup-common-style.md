## Ingredients

- white beer
- butter
- 1 spoon flour
- 1 measure of good cooked milk
- bread
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Let the white beer cook in a brass pan
2. Skim off the white foam with a skimming spoon
3. Melt a piece of butter in a casserole
4. Add 1 spoon of flour and let it barely brown
5. Stir it smooth with the beer
6. Pour in 1 measure of good cooked milk
7. Let everything come to a boil together
8. Cut bread into cubes and arrange the soup over it
9. Do not forget salt and nutmeg
