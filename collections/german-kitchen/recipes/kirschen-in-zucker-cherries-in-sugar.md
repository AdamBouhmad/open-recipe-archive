## Ingredients

- Nicht überreife Kirschen (Not overripe cherries)
- Zucker (Sugar)
- Wasser (Water)
- Kirschengeist or Arak (Cherry liqueur or arak)

## Directions

1. Cut the stems half off non-overripe cherries
2. In a brass pan, cook 3/4 pound of sugar with half a bottle's measure of water until it reaches the small thread stage
3. Place the cherries in the sugar and cook them as long as it takes to hard boil eggs
4. Lift the cherries out with a skimming spoon into the vessel in which they are to be stored
5. Reduce the syrup in the pan until thick
6. Pour the syrup over the cherries and let cool
7. Cover with stiff paper and pour some cherry liqueur or arak on top
8. Tie the dish shut, prick small holes in the cover with a fork, and store
