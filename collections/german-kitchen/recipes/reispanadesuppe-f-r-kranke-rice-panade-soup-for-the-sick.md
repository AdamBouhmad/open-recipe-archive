## Ingredients

- 4 Loth cleanly washed rice
- Water
- Meat broth
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon sweet cream

## Directions

1. Tie 4 Loth of cleanly washed rice in a linen cloth, leaving 3 finger-widths empty
2. Boil the rice in a pot with half water and half meat broth for 2 to 3 hours
3. Stir it very finely with 1 egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of sweet cream
4. Let it come together once more and give to the sick person one spoonful at a time
