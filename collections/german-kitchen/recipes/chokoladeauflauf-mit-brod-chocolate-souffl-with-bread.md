## Ingredients

- 3 white bread rolls or milk bread
- 1 schoppen milk
- 6 loth butter
- 4 loth ground sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound grated chocolate
- 5 egg whites
- butter for the dish
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Cut 3 white bread rolls or milk bread finely as for soup and cook with 1 schoppen milk like porridge.
2. Put into a bowl and stir 6 loth butter, 4 loth ground sugar, 5 egg yolks, and 1/4 pound grated chocolate with the porridge for about 15 minutes.
3. Beat the 5 egg whites to a snow and mix into the mass.
4. Brush a bowl with butter, pour the mass into it, let the soufflé bake nicely golden, and sprinkle sugar on top.
