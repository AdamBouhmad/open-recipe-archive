## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg (1 pound) potatoes
- water and salt
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 20 g flour
- 20 g onions
- 30 g ham or bacon scraps or bacon rind without smoke edges
- 1/2 l skim milk, pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in salted water until done, then peel them. Dice the onions and ham, bacon rind, or bacon.
2. In an earthenware pot, sauté the bacon, rind, or ham and onion cubes, add the flour, then stir in milk, pepper, and salt while stirring. Let the sauce cook down slowly, pass it through a sieve, slice the warm potatoes into it, let them draw in the sauce covered for a while without boiling, and adjust the seasoning with pepper and salt before serving.
3. Remark: Instead of milk, water and 30 g white cheese pressed through and stirred in can be used. (Cheese Potatoes.)
