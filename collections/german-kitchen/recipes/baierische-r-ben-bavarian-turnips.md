## Ingredients

- turnips
- pork fat
- meat broth or warm water
- pork meat
- flour
- fat

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the turnips, scrape them.
2. In a casserole with pork fat, some meat broth or warm water, cook them for one hour.
3. When tender, add a piece of pork meat.
4. In a pan, roast a couple of spoonfuls of flour in a piece of fat until brown.
5. Deglaze it with meat broth, stir it smooth, pour it over the turnips.
6. Let them cook another half hour.
