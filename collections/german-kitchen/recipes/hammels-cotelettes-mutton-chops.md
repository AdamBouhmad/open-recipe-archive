## Ingredients

- beautiful young mutton ribs
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the sinew from the bottom of beautiful young mutton ribs, cut off the fatty skin from the top, strip the bone, remove the membrane
2. Pound the ribs a little and sprinkle them with a mixture of onion, parsley, salt, and pepper
3. Lay them in a roasting pan, pour a little water over them, cover them, and let them fry quickly
4. They must be done in half a quarter hour
5. Serve them with savoy cabbage or kohlrabi
