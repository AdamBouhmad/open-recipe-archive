## Ingredients

- 1 schoppen of cream
- 4 whole eggs
- fine flour
- 1 knife-tip of salt
- 2 eggs
- hot lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir fine flour into 1 schoppen of cream and 4 whole eggs until it becomes a dough
2. Add a knife-tip of salt and stir in 2 more eggs
3. When the dough is so thin that it flows through a fritter funnel, let it run into a pan of hot lard
4. Bake it until beautifully golden and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
