## Ingredients

- Frog legs
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Grated bread
- Lard

## Directions

1. Wash the frog legs clean and cut off the front joints
2. Salt the remaining legs, clean them well so they lie nicely on the plate
3. Turn them in flour, then in eggs, and in grated bread
4. Fry them in lard
5. Do not put too many in the pan at once, otherwise they will not be roasted properly
