## Ingredients

- 1 bread roll or salted roll
- 15 g butter

## Directions

1. Cut the roll lengthwise or around into 3 slices
2. Spread each slice evenly with soft butter, or if butter is forbidden, with plum puree or sauerkraut
3. On a plate, cut each slice lengthwise and, if the patient cannot bite, also cut into pieces crosswise
4. Optional: toast the white bread slices on a fork over an open flame and then spread
5. If topping with minced ham, a meat slice, or chopped egg yolk, trim the overhanging edges neatly so the rolls look attractive
