## Ingredients

- 100 g old bread
- 1 l buttermilk
- 15 g flour
- 5 g salt
- 3 tablespoons syrup or 35 g sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into small pieces and soften it with 3/4 l buttermilk.
2. Bring to a boil while stirring, then bind with the flour mixed into 1/4 l buttermilk, season the mixture with syrup or sugar and salt, bring to a boil, pass through a fine sieve while stirring, heat again and serve.
