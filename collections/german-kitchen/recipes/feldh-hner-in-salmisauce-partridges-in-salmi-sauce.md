## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- onion
- lemon
- bacon
- yellow turnips
- bay leaves
- juniper berries

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges thoroughly, pluck them, singe them
2. Cut off the wings at the second joint and the heads, then draw them
3. If the partridges are fresh, do not wash them but rub them inside with salt and pepper
4. Insert an onion studded with cloves and a piece of lemon in the cavity
5. Wrap them with bacon, place them in a casserole with sliced onion, yellow turnips, bay leaves, and juniper berries
