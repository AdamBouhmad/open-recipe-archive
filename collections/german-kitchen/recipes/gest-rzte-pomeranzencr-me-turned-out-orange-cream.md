## Ingredients

- 4 calf's feet
- 2 Maas water
- 3 oranges
- half pound sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 schoppen cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 schoppen cream (for whipping)
- citron oil

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from 4 calf's feet and place the meat with 2 Maas water on the fire
2. Let it reduce to 1-1/2 Maas and strain through a woolen cloth
3. Let it stand for a time
4. Rub 3 oranges with half a pound of sugar
5. Press the juice of the oranges into a brass pan
6. Add the rubbed sugar, juice of 1 lemon, the calf stock, and 1 schoppen cream
7. Let everything cook together on the fire
8. Beat 8 egg yolks smooth with a little water and slowly stir the cooked mixture into the eggs
9. Let everything come to a boil once more in the pan
10. Strain through a woolen cloth into a porcelain dish
11. Whip 1 schoppen cream until very stiff, let it drain on a hair sieve, and slowly mix it under the cream
12. Coat a mold with lemon oil
13. Pour the cream in, place the mold on ice, and when the cream is completely set, turn it out onto a plate
