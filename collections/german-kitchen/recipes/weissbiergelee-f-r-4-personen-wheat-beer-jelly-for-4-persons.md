## Ingredients

- 9 Sheets Red gelatin
- 1 Bottle Wheat beer
- 1/2 cm Cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon Lemon sugar
- 100 g Sugar
- 1 tablespoon Rum

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatin in 8 tablespoons wheat beer at warm temperature.
2. Add cinnamon to the remaining wheat beer, add lemon sugar, sugar, rum, and the gelatin strained through a sieve, stir everything well, remove the cinnamon, pour the jelly into small glasses, a porcelain or glass dish, and chill thoroughly.
3. Instead of wheat beer, you can also use cider or light white wine (wine jelly). The rum can also be omitted.
