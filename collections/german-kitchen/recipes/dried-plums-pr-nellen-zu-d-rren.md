## Ingredients

- Beautiful ripe plums
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the plums.
2. Remove the skin.
3. Take out the pits.
4. Lay them on paper, or better yet on a wire mesh grate.
5. Place this in a baking oven that is no longer hot.
6. When well dried, preserve them in a wooden box.
