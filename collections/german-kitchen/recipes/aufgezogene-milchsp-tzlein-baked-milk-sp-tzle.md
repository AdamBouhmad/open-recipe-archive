## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 4 or 5 eggs
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour per egg
- milk, 1 schoppen
- 4 loth sugar
- 1 lemon, zest
- 3 eggs for sauce

## Directions

1. Stir 1/4 pound butter in a bowl, adding 4 or 5 eggs one by one, adding 1 heaping tablespoon flour for each egg
2. In a saucepan, bring milk to boil
3. Dip a sieve into the milk and press the dough with a cooking spoon into the boiling milk
4. Catch the spätzle with a skimming spoon and place in a bowl
5. Butter a baking sheet and lay the spätzle on it
6. Pound 4 loth sugar rubbed with lemon zest very finely
7. Pour 1 schoppen good milk into it, beat in 3 eggs and mix well
8. Pour a little over the spätzle, add the remaining spätzle sauce, pour the rest over and place in the oven
9. Bake for half an hour until done
