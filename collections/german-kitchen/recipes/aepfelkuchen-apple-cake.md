## Ingredients

- cake sheet with butter dough
- good apples, sliced
- zest of half a lemon, grated
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream
- 1 large handful ground sugar
- 2 knife-tips ground cinnamon
- half a schoppen milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Line a cake sheet with butter dough
2. Cover with good, sliced apples
3. Mix the grated zest of half a lemon, 2 spoonfuls of sour cream, a large handful of ground sugar, 2 knife-tips of ground cinnamon, and half a schoppen of milk together well
4. Pour this mixture over the apples
5. Cut some butter over the top
6. Bake the cake in the oven
