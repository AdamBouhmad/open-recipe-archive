## Ingredients

- 50 g flour
- 10 g beef fat or margarine
- 2 g salt
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Directions

1. Bring fat and water to a boil and pour into the flour and salt while stirring rapidly. Using a teaspoon that has been held in boiling water beforehand, drop small dumplings into the mixture before the dough cools, cook them about 15 minutes slowly in the soup or salted water with the pot uncovered, and serve with them.
2. If the flour dumplings are to be served with brown butter or stewed fruit, use the ingredients threefold, drop with a tablespoon, and cook in salted water until done. Similarly for finer flour dumplings, which improve the above mixture by adding an egg and using 1-1/2 tablespoon of lean fried bacon cubes instead of the fat.
