## Ingredients

- Peeled rind of 3 Wecken or milk breads
- Hot lard
- 1 schoppen wine
- 6 loth finely crushed sugar
- 1 orange
- 4 to 5 egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Puff pastry
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 4 egg whites
- 1 orange

## Directions

1. Bake the peeled rind of 3 Wecken or milk breads in hot lard and cook it with 1 schoppen wine in a casserolle like a porridge
2. In a bowl, beat it with 6 loth finely crushed sugar, 1 orange rind grated on a grater, 4 to 5 egg yolks and the snow from the eggs together
3. Bake this mass in a baking sheet into which puff pastry has been placed beforehand
4. Let 1/4 pound sugar cook with 2 tablespoons water until it spins threads
5. Beat a stiff snow from 4 egg whites, add the juice of 1 orange, stir together
6. Turn the baked cake onto a plate and pour the ice over it
7. This dish can also be cooked as a pudding in steam and an orange sauce added to it
