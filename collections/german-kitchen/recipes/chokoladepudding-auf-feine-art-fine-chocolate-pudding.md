## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound vanilla chocolate
- 1/2 cup good sweet cream
- 8 loth butter
- 4 loth fine flour
- 1/4 pound blanched, ground almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound ground sugar
- snow from 6 egg whites
- 1/2 measure milk
- 1 piece vanilla
- 1 piece sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 3 fingers full flour
- cold milk as needed

## Directions

1. Cook the vanilla chocolate, cream, butter, and fine flour in a brass pan with constant stirring until it separates from the pan
2. Let it cool in a bowl
3. Stir in the blanched almonds, egg yolks, ground sugar, and beaten egg whites
4. Mix this with the above
5. Coat a pudding capsule with butter and dust with sugar
6. Place in a pot of boiling water
7. Cook the pudding for one hour
8. For the sauce: cook half a measure milk with one piece vanilla and one piece sugar together
9. Beat 2 egg yolks, whisk with 3 fingers full flour and cold milk until smooth
10. Pour this into the boiling vanilla milk
11. Cook everything together with constant stirring
12. Pour through a sieve and pour over the pudding
