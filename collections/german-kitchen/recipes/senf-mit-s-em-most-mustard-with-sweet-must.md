## Ingredients

- Sweet must
- Yellow mustard flour (8 loth)
- Brown mustard flour (8 loth)

## Directions

1. In autumn take completely sweet must
2. Cook it down well (from 3 maas must, 1 maas can be cooked down)
3. Pour it into a pot
4. Let it stand overnight
5. In the morning put it in a bottle
6. Cork it well and set it aside for use
7. To make mustard, take some of it in a brass pan
8. Let it cook
9. Take 8 loth yellow mustard flour in a bowl, 8 loth brown mustard flour
10. Scald this with the boiling must
11. Stir it thoroughly
12. Heat a small arch piece of steel until glowing
13. Dip it in the mustard
14. Stir this until it is completely cold
15. Preserve it in a stone pot, bind it up and serve it with beef
