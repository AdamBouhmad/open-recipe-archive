## Ingredients

- smoked ham
- shallot onions
- bay leaves
- basil
- thyme
- cloves
- garlic
- mace
- champagne wine, one bottle
- butter
- coulis
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the ham on a grill and clean it thoroughly, removing any blackened parts.
2. Place it in a long dish and add sliced shallot onions, bay leaves, basil, thyme, several cloves, garlic, and mace.
3. Pour a bottle of champagne over it, cover the dish tightly, and let it stand until the next day.
4. 3 hours before serving, place it on a spit.
5. Baste it frequently with the sauce it marinated in, to which a piece of butter has been added, until it has absorbed all the liquid.
6. Before serving, bring a couple spoonfuls of coulis and the juice of a lemon to a boil and pour this over the haunch.
