## Ingredients

- 1 fish
- salt
- 3 bottles Bordeaux wine
- 1 whole lemon, sliced
- cloves
- whole pepper
- nutmeg blossom
- ox kidney fat
- 1 handful chopped parsley
- 2 shallot onions
- 1 lemon, yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the fish thoroughly, cut out the gills, remove the entrails
2. Rub strongly with salt
3. In a fish kettle, cook it with 3 bottles Bordeaux wine, 1 whole lemon cut into slices, cloves, whole pepper, and nutmeg blossom, tightly covered and slowly
4. Finely pound a piece of ox kidney fat, let it melt in a casserole
5. Add 1 handful chopped parsley, 2 shallot onions, and some lemon yolk
6. When the fish is soft, place it on a plate and pour the steamed mixture over it
