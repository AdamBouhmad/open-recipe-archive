## Ingredients

- 1/2 maas raspberries
- sugar
- 12 egg whites
- blue sugar sand
- rosehips
- butter

## Directions

1. Press 1/2 maas of raspberries through a hair sieve using a pea-pusher.
2. Stir together as many spoonfuls of pulp with as many spoonfuls of sugar, mixing thoroughly.
3. Beat 12 egg whites into snow.
4. Mix the snow into the previous mixture.
5. Pour everything into a plate brushed with butter.
6. Shape it high and attractive.
7. Sprinkle blue sugar sand on top.
8. Garnish the plate with rosehips.
9. Treat the foam as indicated for apple foam.
10. It will be ready in a quarter hour.
