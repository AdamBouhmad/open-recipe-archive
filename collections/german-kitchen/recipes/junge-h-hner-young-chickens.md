## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pierce the chickens and place them in cold water so they become nice and white
2. Dip one after another into boiling water
3. Pluck them clean so they do not get holes
4. Wash them once more in lukewarm water
5. Poke out the eyes and pull the skin away from the leg so it becomes nice and white
6. Loosen the breastbone
7. Rub them well with salt and pepper
8. Dress them nicely together
9. Cut the feet halfway off
10. Roast them on the spit or in an iron baking pan
