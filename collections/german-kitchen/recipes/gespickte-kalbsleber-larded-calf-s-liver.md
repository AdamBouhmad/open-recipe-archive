## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- bacon, sliced
- onions
- lemons
- bay leaf
- stock (Jus)
- gingerbread
- wine, half a glass
- salt
- cloves
- mace

## Directions

1. Skin the liver and lard it beautifully.
2. Place it in a casserole with sliced bacon, onions, lemons, and bay leaf, and lay more bacon on top.
3. Pour a little stock over it, cover the casserole with a lid topped with coals, and braise for one hour.
4. Remove the fat, add a piece of gingerbread to the liver, pour in half a glass of wine, add salt, cloves, and mace.
5. Bring to a boil and strain the sauce through a fine sieve before serving.
