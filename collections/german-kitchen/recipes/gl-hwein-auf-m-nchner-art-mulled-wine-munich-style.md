## Ingredients

- 2 Maas good wine (Affenthaler is best)
- 1-1/2 pounds sugar
- finest cinnamon
- several cloves

## Directions

1. Cook 2 Maas good wine, 1-1/2 pounds sugar, finest cinnamon and several cloves together well covered in a brass pan until 1 Maas of it has cooked down
2. Strain the wine through a hair sieve into the serving dish
3. Cover it very tightly
