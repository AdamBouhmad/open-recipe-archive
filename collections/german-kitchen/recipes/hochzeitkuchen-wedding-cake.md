## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 pound butter
- 3 spoons beer yeast
- lukewarm milk
- 4 loths ground sugar
- 10 loths butter
- peeled apples
- raisins
- 1 loth blanched almonds
- 2 loths ground candied sugar
- eggs for brushing

## Directions

1. Stir 4 eggs and 1/2 pound butter until white
2. Stir 3 spoons beer yeast mixed with some lukewarm milk into the mixture
3. Add 4 loths ground sugar and work the dough together
4. Beat the dough for a quarter hour
5. Set aside to warm and let it half rise
6. Roll out the dough and cut 10 loths butter onto the cake
7. Fold it over like puff pastry, roll out 2 fingers thick
8. Arrange peeled apples and raisins on the edge of the cake
9. Fold the cake over 3 fingers wide
10. Coat a sheet of paper with butter, place the cake on it and let it rise
11. Brush with beaten eggs
12. Mix 1 loth blanched almonds with 2 loths ground candied sugar and scatter over the cake
13. Bake until beautiful yellow
