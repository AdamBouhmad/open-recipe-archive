## Ingredients

- half a Maas milk or water
- one quarter pound butter
- one quarter pound sugar
- 1 lemon
- 12 loth flour
- 6 eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook half a Maas milk or water, one quarter pound butter and one quarter pound sugar (with one lemon zested into it) together for 4 minutes
2. Stir 12 loth flour into it and cook everything together until the dough is dry
3. Beat 6 eggs into it
4. Brush a black baking sheet with lard and sprinkle a little flour on it
5. Place heaps of the dough on it with a spoon and bake them in the oven until golden yellow
