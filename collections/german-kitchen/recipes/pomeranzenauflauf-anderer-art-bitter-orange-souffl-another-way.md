## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons flour
- milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 bitter orange
- 4 Loth sugar
- 1 Schoppen milk
- egg whites from the eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir 4 tablespoons of flour smooth in a brass pan with milk
2. Beat in 6 egg yolks
3. Grate 1 bitter orange on 4 Loth of sugar and add this to the pan along with the juice of the bitter orange
4. Pour in 1 Schoppen of milk and cook a thick porridge of everything
5. Stir it (removed from the fire) until it cools a little
6. Finally add the snow from the 6 egg whites and bake the soufflé in a buttered porcelain dish beautifully golden in the oven
