## Ingredients

- 5 loth black bread, not sour
- 1 whole nutmeg
- 2 knife-points ground cinnamon
- 6 loth grated, ground almonds
- 1 knife-point ground cloves
- 1 glass Malaga wine
- 1/4 pound butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/4 pound ground sugar
- zest of half a lemon, grated
- snow from 6 egg whites
- 1 bottle wine
- 8 loth sugar
- 1 piece whole cinnamon
- 6 whole cloves

## Directions

1. Grate the black bread and nutmeg together
2. Mix in the cinnamon, ground almonds, and cloves
3. Moisten everything with a glass of Malaga wine
4. Lightly stir the butter in a bowl
5. Add all the above to the butter, then add the egg yolks, ground sugar, and grated lemon zest
6. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
7. Bake a thin pancake
8. Cut this into strips and lay them crosswise in a damp cloth
9. Fill the mass onto the strips and bind the cloth tightly
10. Leave in boiling water for one hour
11. For the mulled wine sauce: reduce one bottle of wine with 8 loth sugar to one schoppen in a pot
12. Arrange the pudding and pour the wine through a sieve containing one piece whole cinnamon and 6 cloves over the pudding
