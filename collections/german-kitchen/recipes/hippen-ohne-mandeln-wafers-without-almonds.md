## Ingredients

- 4 loth flour
- wine or white beer
- 4 egg whites
- 3 loth ground sugar
- 1 quint cinnamon
- 1/2 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir flour smooth with wine or white beer
2. Add egg whites, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg
3. Stir everything together thoroughly
4. Coat a wafer iron with lard and heat it
5. Pour a spoonful of batter into the iron
6. Bake until golden and immediately stretch over a round piece of wood while hot
