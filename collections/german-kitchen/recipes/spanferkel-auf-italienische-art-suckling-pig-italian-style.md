## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- salt
- pepper
- good rice
- bouillon
- ham
- Parmesan cheese
- bratwurst sausages
- Provençe oil

## Directions

1. Bone out the suckling pig and rub it with salt and pepper.
2. Cook good rice in bouillon with a piece of ham; the rice must be quite hard and dry.
3. Remove it from the heat and when it is cold, grate some Parmesan cheese into it.
4. Fry some bratwurst sausages, cut them into small pieces, and remove their casings.
5. Remove the ham from the rice, cut it small, and stir the rice well.
6. Add the sausage pieces along with the fat they were fried in.
7. Fill the suckling pig with this mixture.
8. Place it in a casserole with a piece of fat and put it in the oven for one hour before serving.
9. Brush it with Provençe oil so it gets a beautiful color.
10. Place the roasted pig on a platter.
