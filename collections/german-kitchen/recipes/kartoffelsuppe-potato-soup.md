## Ingredients

- 375 g raw potatoes
- 2 tablespoons soup vegetables
- 20 g fat or margarine
- 5 g flour
- 5 g salt
- 1/2 seasoning portion
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley or celery or leek
- 1 pinch pepper
- 1 l water

## Directions

1. Preparation: The potatoes are peeled, the soup vegetables are cleaned and not cut too small.
2. Set the potatoes with cold water and when they boil, drain them. Then pour 1 l water over them, add spices, soup vegetables and salt; the whole must cook covered for 1 hour.
3. Meanwhile, in an earthenware pot, sweat fat with flour until it foams.
4. Remove the soup vegetables from the soup, press the remaining mass through a sieve and, while stirring with a wooden spoon, pour into the roux, with which it must cook in an open earthenware pot for at least 10 minutes.
5. Add chopped parsley (or celery greens) to the soup and season with a pinch of pepper.
