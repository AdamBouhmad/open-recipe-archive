## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. Flour
- 3 oz. Suet
- 1 Egg
- 2 Lemons
- 2 oz. Sugar
- 1/4 gill Water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour
2. Chop the suet finely and mix together
3. Stir in the sugar and the grated rind of the lemons
4. Beat up the egg, add the juice of one lemon, and mix the pudding into a dough with this and a little water if required
5. Dip a cloth in boiling water
6. Tie the pudding in it
7. Plunge into boiling water and boil two hours
8. Take out of the cloth and turn on to a hot dish
9. Pour round it sauce made by squeezing the juice of the other lemon into a small saucepan, stirring in some sugar and a gill of water, and boiling up
