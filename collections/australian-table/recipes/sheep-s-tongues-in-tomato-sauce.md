## Ingredients

- 6 Tongues
- 1 pint Tomato Sauce
- 1 doz. Peppercorns
- 1 Onion
- 1 fagot Herbs
- 1 Carrot
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the tongues in cold water, put them into a saucepan, cover them with cold water or stock, and bring to the boil, then skim well.
2. Either corned or fresh tongues will do for this dish. If corned, no salt is required; but if fresh ones are used, put in a dessertspoonful of salt.
3. Put in the vegetables and peppercorns and simmer gently for two hours.
4. Take them up, plunge them into cold water and remove the skin.
5. Trim them off and cut in half.
6. Make some tomato sauce by recipe given elsewhere. The liquor in which the tongues were boiled may be used for this if it is not too salt.
7. Lay the tongues in and simmer for another hour.
8. Dish carefully, boil up the sauce and pour over.
9. Garnish with chopped parsley.
