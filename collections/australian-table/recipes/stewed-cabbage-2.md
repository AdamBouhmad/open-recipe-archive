## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage as directed, and squeeze very dry
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, season with pepper, salt, and a drop or two of lemon juice
3. Put in the cabbage and cook in the butter for ten minutes, stirring frequently
4. Arrange neatly in a hot dish, and serve
