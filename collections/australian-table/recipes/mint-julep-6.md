## Ingredients

- about a dozen sprigs of the tender shoots of mint
- a spoonful of white sugar
- equal proportions of peach and common brandy
- rasped or pounded ice

## Directions

1. Put into a tumbler about a dozen sprigs of the tender shoots of mint
2. upon them put a spoonful of white sugar, and equal proportions of peach and common brandy, so as to fill up one-third, or perhaps a little less
3. Take rasped or pounded ice, and fill up the tumbler
4. As the ice melts you drink
5. Epicures may rub the lips of the tumbler with a piece of fresh pineapple, and the tumbler itself is often encrusted outside with stalactites of ice
