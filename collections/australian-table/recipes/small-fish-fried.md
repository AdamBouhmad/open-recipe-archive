## Ingredients

- small fish
- dripping or lard

## Directions

1. Clean them well, and let them dry.
2. Fry them plain or egg them.
3. Put plenty of dripping or lard into the pan, and as soon as the fat boils put them in and brown them.
4. They will hardly take a couple of minutes.
5. Drain them in a hair sieve, and serve.
