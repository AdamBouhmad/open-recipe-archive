## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Boiled Fish
- 1 lb. Cold Boiled Potatoes
- 1/2 of an Onion
- 2 oz. Fat Bacon
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Milk or Gravy
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Flake up the fish free from skin and bone, mash the potatoes and mix them together
2. Season with half the parsley, pepper and salt
3. Mince the bacon and onion very finely
4. Put them into a frying pan with the butter and fry for a few minutes
5. Stir in the fish and potatoes and turn about until thoroughly hot through
6. Pour over the gravy or milk and again make thoroughly hot
7. Heap on to a dish, and garnish with the rest of the parsley
8. Serve very hot
