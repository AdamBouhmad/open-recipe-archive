## Ingredients

- pig meat from griskins
- veal
- beef suet
- powdered sage
- pepper
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take one pound and a half of pig meat, cut from the griskins, without any skin
2. Add half a pound of veal, and a pound and a half of beef suet
3. Mince these meats very finely
4. Mix them with a dessert-spoonful of powdered sage, pepper, and salt, to taste
5. Add the well-beaten yolks and whites of four eggs
6. Beat the whole together well, as much depends on the mixing
