## Ingredients

- twenty-four pounds of raisins
- three gallons of boiling water
- three pints of elderberry juice
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Pick and shred twenty-four pounds of raisins, and cover them with three gallons of boiling water
2. Stir the liquor every day, and add three pints of elderberry juice and one pound of sugar
3. Let it ferment its usual time
4. Close the cask, and bottle
