## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. Cold Fish
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Milk
- Bread Crumbs
- Hot Fat
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 teaspoonful Anchovy
- 1/2 teaspoonful Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pick the fish free from skin and bone, and chop it up.
2. Make a smooth thick sauce with the flour, butter, and water, by directions given elsewhere.
3. Flavour it with anchovy, parsley, pepper, and salt; stir in the fish, and mix well.
4. Turn on to a plate till cold.
5. Make up into small balls, cover with egg and bread crumbs, and fry in hot fat.
6. Drain for a few minutes on kitchen paper, arrange carefully on a dish, and garnish with parsley.
