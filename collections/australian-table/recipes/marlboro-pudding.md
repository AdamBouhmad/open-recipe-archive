## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter melted
- four ounces of sugar
- four eggs well beaten
- paste
- a layer of preserve

## Directions

1. Mix the butter, sugar, and eggs well together
2. Line the dish with paste and a layer of preserve
3. Bake half an hour
