## Ingredients

- six ounces of flour
- a pint of new milk
- six ounces of sugar
- six ounces of butter
- six ounces of currants
- six eggs
- a little brandy
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix six ounces of flour to a smooth batter with a pint of new milk
2. Add six ounces of sugar, six ounces of butter, six ounces of currants, six eggs, a little brandy and grated nutmeg
3. When well mixed put it into moulds
4. Bake for an hour and turn the puddings out in a dish
5. Serve with sweet sauce
