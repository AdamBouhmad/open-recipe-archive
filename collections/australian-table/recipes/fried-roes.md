## Ingredients

- 3 Roes
- Frying Batter
- Hot Fat
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Put the roes on in cold water and boil for about half an hour
2. Take them up and let them get quite cold, then cut into slices
3. Make some frying batter by recipe given elsewhere
4. Season it with salt and pepper, dip in the slices, and fry a good colour
5. Pile high on a dish and garnish with fried parsley
