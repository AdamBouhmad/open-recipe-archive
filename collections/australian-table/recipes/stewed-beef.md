## Ingredients

- three or four pounds beef (rump or any part)
- sweet herbs chopped fine
- a little shalot
- spice whole
- a little bacon cut in slips
- flour
- a small bit of butter

## Directions

1. Take three or four pounds and trim it
2. Add sweet herbs chopped fine, a little shalot, and spice whole
3. Add a little bacon cut in slips
4. Lard the beef and rub it over with the herbs and spices, flour, and a small bit of butter
5. The beef can either be stewed or baked in an oven
6. Serve hot or cold, garnished with pickles
