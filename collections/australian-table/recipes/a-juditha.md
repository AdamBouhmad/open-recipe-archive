## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- a little water
- well-beaten yolks of eggs
- white sugar
- biscuit ice or other cream ice

## Directions

1. Put some gooseberries into a saucepan with a little water.
2. When they are soft, pulp them through a sieve.
3. Add several well-beaten yolks of eggs, and sweeten with white sugar.
4. Have ready a shape of biscuit ice, or any other cream ice that may be preferred.
5. Take off a thick slice of the ice from the top carefully, and without breaking, so that it may be replaced on the ice.
6. Scoop out a large portion of the ice, which may be mixed with the gooseberry cream.
7. Fill up the hollow with it.
8. Cover the shape with the piece that was removed.
9. Serve.
