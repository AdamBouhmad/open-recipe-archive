## Ingredients

- juice of oranges, three pints
- a pound of white sugar
- a gallon of rum

## Directions

1. Put the juice of oranges, three pints, and a pound of white sugar to a gallon of rum
2. Let it remain in a cask for a few weeks, and it will be fit for use
