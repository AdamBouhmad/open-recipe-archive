## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a quart of milk
- a little salt
- six eggs
- half a pint of flour

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of rice to soak over night
2. Early in the morning boil it very soft
3. Drain it from the water
4. Mix with it a quarter of a pound of butter
5. Set it away to cool
6. When cold stir in a quart of milk, and add to it a little salt
7. Beat up six eggs and mix with half a pint of flour
8. Stir the eggs and flour into the rice and milk
9. Bake it on the girdle in cakes the size of a dessert-plate
10. Butter them, and send to table hot
