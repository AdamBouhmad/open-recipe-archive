## Ingredients

- lobster or crayfish
- yolk of an egg, boiled hard
- a mealy potato
- a table-spoonful of vinegar
- two spoonfuls of oil
- a dessert-spoonful of mustard
- half a tea-spoonful of salt
- half a tea-spoonful of cayenne
- a couple of medium-sized lettuces
- a table-spoonful of cream

## Directions

1. Cut lobster or crayfish into small pieces
2. Add the yolk of an egg, boiled hard, a mealy potato, a table-spoonful of vinegar, two of oil, a dessert-spoonful of mustard, half a tea-spoonful of salt, the same of cayenne
3. Slice into pieces a couple of medium-sized lettuces
4. A table-spoonful of cream may be added
5. Mix the whole well together
