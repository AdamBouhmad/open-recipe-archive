## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Split Peas
- 2 Onions
- 1 Head of Celery
- 1 oz. Butter or Dripping
- 2 Carrots
- 2 doz. Peppercorns
- 3 quarts Water
- Salt
- dried mint
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Wash the peas well in cold water, and put them into a saucepan with the vegetables sliced up, the peppercorns and the water.
2. Bring to the boil and boil steadily for four hours, then rub through a sieve and return to the saucepan.
3. Season well with salt, and stir in 1 oz. butter or dripping.
4. Bring to the boil and pour into a warm tureen.
5. Send some dried mint and fried bread to table with it.
