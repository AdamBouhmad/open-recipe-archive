## Ingredients

- a pulp of apples
- sugar
- lemon
- half a pint of milk
- half a pint of cream
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Place a pulp of apples at the bottom of the trifle-dish, flavoured with sugar and lemon
2. Mix half a pint of milk, half a pint of cream, and the yolk of an egg
3. Give it a scald over the fire, with a little sugar in it
4. Let it cool, then cover the apples with it, and put a whip on the top
