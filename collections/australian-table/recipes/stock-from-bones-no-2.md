## Ingredients

- Bones from joints of meat, whether roasted or boiled
- Beef bones
- Mutton and veal bones
- Bones and trimmings of poultry, game, and rabbits
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Remove all pieces that may be burnt
2. Chop the bones very small
3. Well cover with cold water
4. When the pot boils put in a teaspoonful of salt and skim well
5. Boil steadily for six hours or longer
6. Strain off and remove the fat
7. Let it stand till the next day before converting it into soup or gravy
