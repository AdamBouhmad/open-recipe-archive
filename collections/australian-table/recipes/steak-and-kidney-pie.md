## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Steak
- 2 Kidneys
- 1 lb. Flour
- 1/2 lb. Dripping
- 1 gill Water
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Mix a teaspoonful of flour in a plate with some pepper and salt
2. Slice up the meat into pieces about three inches long by two broad
3. Dip each piece lightly in the flour
4. Skin and slice up the kidneys, and cut the fat into small pieces
5. Roll a piece of kidney and a piece of fat alternately in the slices of meat
6. Pile high in a dish, and pour in a gill of water or stock
7. Make a short crust by directions given for short pastry
8. Wet the edge of the dish and line it with a strip of the paste
9. Wet this strip again with water and cover the dish with paste
10. Trim off the edge, cut a small piece out of the centre of the pie, and ornament it with a few leaves cut out of the paste trimmings
11. Brush over with water and bake in a moderate oven for one hour and a half
12. As soon as the crust has acquired some colour, cover with a piece of paper well rubbed with dripping
