## Ingredients

- peas
- cloth
- an ounce of butter
- one egg
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the peas for ten or twelve hours
2. Tie them loosely in a cloth, leaving room for them to swell
3. Simmer for a couple of hours
4. When tender, drain them
5. Rub them through a colander with a wooden spoon
6. Add an ounce of butter, one egg beat up, and pepper and salt to taste
7. Beat them well together
8. Tie lightly in a cloth
9. Boil for half an hour
