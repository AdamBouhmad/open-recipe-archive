## Ingredients

- sheep's feet
- vinegar
- salt
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the sheep's feet well, and boil them in water
2. Steep them a day in vinegar and salt
3. Drain them, and fry them with egg and bread crumbs, until of a good colour
