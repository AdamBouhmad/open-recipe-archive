## Ingredients

- fruit prepared as in Lamplich recipe
- rich paste
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Line the dish with the paste.
2. Lay the fruit with alternate layers of paste till the dish is filled.
3. The paste must form the top layer.
4. Pour clarified sugar over before it is put in the oven.
