## Ingredients

- 4 Young Turnips
- 2 Spring Onions
- 2 Boiled Potatoes
- Half a Lettuce
- Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the turnips and boil them for twenty minutes, or until soft.
2. Let them get quite cold.
3. Shred up very small the onions.
4. Slice up the potatoes.
5. Break up half a lettuce.
6. Arrange these neatly in a bowl.
7. Pour over a simple salad dressing or remoulade sauce.
