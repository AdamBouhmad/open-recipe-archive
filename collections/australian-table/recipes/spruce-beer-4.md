## Ingredients

- four gallons of cold water
- four gallons of boiling water
- six pounds of molasses
- eight table-spoonfuls of the essence of spruce
- about half a pint of yeast

## Directions

1. Put four gallons of cold water into a nine-gallon keg
2. Add four more gallons of boiling water
3. Add six pounds of molasses and eight table-spoonfuls of the essence of spruce
4. When cool, put to it about half a pint of yeast
5. Let it work
6. Just before it has done working, bottle in stone bottles
