## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- a pound of white sugar
- the peel of a grated lemon
- a pound of flour
- a tea-spoonful of rose-water

## Directions

1. Beat eggs to a froth
2. Add white sugar and grated lemon peel
3. Whisk the whole well together
4. Add flour and rose-water
5. Divide it into biscuits
6. Sugar them over
7. Bake in tins
