## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Potatoes
- 1 pint White Sauce
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the potatoes, put them in water seasoned with salt, and boil for five minutes; strain off the water.
2. Make some white sauce by directions given elsewhere.
3. Lay in the potatoes and simmer gently till they are soft, but not broken.
4. Place them on a hot dish and pour the sauce over.
