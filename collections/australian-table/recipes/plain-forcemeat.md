## Ingredients

- equal quantity of meat and beef suet
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- pounded lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- parsley
- sweet herbs
- a little shalot
- onion
- bread crumbs
- yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Take an equal quantity of meat and beef suet
2. Mince and chop small
3. Add pepper, salt, cloves, pounded lemon-peel, and nutmeg, grated, parsley and sweet herbs, a little shalot and onion, a few bread crumbs, grated fine
4. Add yolk of egg sufficient to work it light
5. Roll it into balls
6. If for roast meat, put them in the dripping-pan to brown; for white sauce, boil them; for brown sauce, fry them
