## Ingredients

- one ounce of black pepper
- half an ounce of allspice
- one ounce of salt
- one ounce of grated horseradish and shalots
- one pint of mushroom ketchup

## Directions

1. Pound the black pepper, allspice, salt, and grated horseradish and shalots
2. Put these articles to a pint of mushroom ketchup
3. Let them infuse for a month
