## Ingredients

- one pound of fruit
- one quart of the best white wine vinegar
- one pound of fresh raspberries

## Directions

1. Put one pound of fruit into a bowl
2. Pour upon it one quart of the best white wine vinegar
3. Next day strain the juice on one pound of fresh raspberries
4. The following day do the same
5. Bottle and cork well
