## Ingredients

- 0.5 lb. Blue Cod
- 1 lb. Potatoes
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 Egg
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Use cold fish and potatoes, if there are any in the larder; if not, boil a piece of blue smoked cod in some water for five minutes.
2. Flake it up free from skin and bone and put it into a basin; mash up the potatoes and mix them in with the pepper and salt.
3. Bind into a paste with an egg; rub some dripping on a baking sheet, turn the mixture on to it and shape into the letter S.
4. Brush over with egg or milk, and bake till brown.
5. Slip it off on to a hot dish, and garnish with parsley.
