## Ingredients

- Slices of Cold Roast Beef (underdone)
- 1/2 gill Melted Butter Sauce
- 1/2 gill Gravy or Water
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 tablespoonful Walnut Ketchup or Vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful Red Currant Jelly

## Directions

1. Cut some thin slices of beef and lay them in a saucepan or basin.
2. Mix the melted butter sauce, gravy, jelly, and ketchup together, and pour over them.
3. Cover down closely and stand the saucepan in a larger one, half full of boiling water, and steam for half an hour.
4. Put the meat into a dish and pour the sauce over it.
