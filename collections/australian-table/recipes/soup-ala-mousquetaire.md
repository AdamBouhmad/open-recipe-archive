## Ingredients

- a pint of stock
- a pint of green peas
- a handful of sorrel
- a quart of water
- a few pounds of mutton

## Directions

1. Boil in a pint of stock a pint of green peas and a handful of sorrel
2. Add a quart of water, and boil a few pounds of mutton in it
3. When done, glaze as africandeau, and serve together
