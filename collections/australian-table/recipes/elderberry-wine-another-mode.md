## Ingredients

- six gallons of berries
- seven gallons of water
- a quarter of a pound of allspice
- two ounces of ginger
- a few cloves
- three pounds and a half of sugar per gallon
- thick yeast

## Directions

1. To six gallons of berries add seven of water, and a quarter of a pound of allspice, two ounces of ginger, and a few cloves
2. Boil for half an hour
3. Squeeze the berries through a sieve, adding to every gallon three pounds and a half of sugar
4. Boil until the liquor becomes pretty clear, taking off the scum
5. Put it into the cask, and remove it to a cool place
6. When it becomes lukewarm, ferment it by toasted bread being dipped in thick yeast
7. When the fermentation has subsided, bung up the cask
8. It will be fit to drink in three months, and will keep for years
