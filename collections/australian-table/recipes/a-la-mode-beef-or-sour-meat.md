## Ingredients

- piece of ribs of beef, boned and filleted, or piece of round
- vinegar diluted with water
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- whole allspice
- three or four bay-leaves
- cupful of raspings

## Directions

1. Cover meat with vinegar diluted with water
2. Season with onions, pepper, salt, whole allspice, and three or four bay-leaves
3. Add a cupful of raspings
4. Let the whole stew gently for three or four hours, according to the weight of the meat
5. This dish is excellent when cold
6. A rump-steak stewed in the same way will be found exceedingly fine
