## Ingredients

- three dozen sticks of rhubarb
- a little lemon
- cinnamon
- moist sugar
- paste

## Directions

1. Peel and wash three dozen sticks of rhubarb, put into a stewpan with a little lemon, cinnamon, and moist sugar to sweeten
2. set it over the fire to marmalade
3. when done put paste round a dish, insert the rhubarb, and bake
