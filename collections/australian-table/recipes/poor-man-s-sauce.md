## Ingredients

- a handful of parsley leaves
- salt
- half a dozen young green onions
- three table-spoonfuls of oil
- five table-spoonfuls of vinegar
- black ground pepper

## Directions

1. Pick a handful of parsley leaves from the stalks, mince them very fine, strew over them a little salt
2. shred fine half a dozen young green onions, add them to the parsley
3. put them into a sauceboat, with three table-spoonfuls of oil and five of vinegar
4. add some black ground pepper and salt
5. stir together, and serve
