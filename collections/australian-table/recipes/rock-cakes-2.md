## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- three eggs
- half a pound of currants
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. beat up the eggs thoroughly, and mix in with the butter and sugar
3. stir in the flour very gradually
4. lastly stir in the currants
5. Keep a little of the white of egg out to froth the top with
6. Drop the buttons on buttered paper, and bake quickly
