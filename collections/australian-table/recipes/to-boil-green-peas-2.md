## Ingredients

- peas
- cold water
- salt
- sugar
- mint

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and wash them well
2. Just cover them with cold water, season it with a little salt, sugar, and mint
3. Bring quickly to the boil and cook for about twenty minutes
4. When soft, but not broken, strain off the water and put them into a vegetable dish
