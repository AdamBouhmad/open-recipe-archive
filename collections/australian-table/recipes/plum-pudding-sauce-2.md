## Ingredients

- a glass of sherry
- half a glass of brandy
- two tea-spoonfuls of white sugar
- a little grated lemon-peel
- a quarter of a pint of thick melted butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix sherry, brandy, white sugar, and grated lemon-peel together
2. Add the mixture to a quarter of a pint of thick melted butter
3. Grate nutmeg on the surface
