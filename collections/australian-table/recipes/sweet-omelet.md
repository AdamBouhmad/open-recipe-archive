## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- ½ oz. Butter
- 1 teaspoonful Jam
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of the eggs into a basin and beat in half the sugar, put the whites on to a plate with a little sugar, and whip till stiff; mix with the yolks.
2. Put the butter into a small frying-pan, and when it is dissolved pour in the mixture.
3. Leave over the fire for about three minutes.
4. Hold the pan in front of the fire for a minute or two to brown the top.
5. Put the jam on to a hot plate, slip the omelet on the top.
6. Serve at once.
