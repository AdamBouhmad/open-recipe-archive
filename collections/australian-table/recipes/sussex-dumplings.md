## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- a little salt
- water

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of flour, a little salt, and water to a paste
2. Form into small round dumplings
3. Drop them into boiling water and boil for half an hour
4. Serve with roast or boiled meat, or if with the latter, boiled in the same water
