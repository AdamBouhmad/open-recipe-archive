## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- milk
- a pound of flour

## Directions

1. Warm the butter in milk
2. Convert the flour into a stiff paste
3. Beat it smooth
4. Roll them out thin
5. Cut them into round biscuits
6. Prick them with a fork
7. Bake
