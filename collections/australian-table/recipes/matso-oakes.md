## Ingredients

- biscuit-powder
- milk and water
- a little butter
- yolk of an egg
- a little white sugar

## Directions

1. Make a stiff paste with biscuit-powder and milk and water.
2. Add a little butter, the yolk of an egg, and a little white sugar.
3. Cut into pieces, and mould with the hand.
4. Bake in a brisk oven.
5. These cakes should not be too thin.
