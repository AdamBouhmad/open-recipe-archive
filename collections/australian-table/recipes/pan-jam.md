## Ingredients

- kangaroo tails
- fat bacon
- mushrooms
- pepper

## Directions

1. Roast them in the ashes with the skin on.
2. When nearly done, scrape them well, and divide at the joints.
3. Put them into a pan, with a few slices of fat bacon, to which add a few mushrooms, pepper, &c.
4. Fry gently, and serve.
