## Ingredients

- one part honey
- three parts boiling water
- spices
- ground malt
- toast
- yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve one part of honey in three parts of boiling water
2. Flavour with spices
3. Add a portion of ground malt to a piece of toast dipped in yeast, to allow the malt to ferment
