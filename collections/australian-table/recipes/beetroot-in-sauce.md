## Ingredients

- beetroots
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the beetroots, but do not cut them
2. Put them in cold water, and boil till they feel soft if pressed
3. Take them up, peel and slice them
4. Make the sauce by directions given elsewhere
5. Put in the beetroot and simmer for about half an hour
6. Dish the beets and pour the sauce over
