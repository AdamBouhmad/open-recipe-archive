## Ingredients

- Mustard seed, one ounce and a half
- Coriander, four ounces
- Turmeric, four and a quarter ounces
- Black pepper, two and a half ounces
- Cayenne, one ounce and a quarter
- Ginger, half an ounce
- Cinnamon, quarter of an ounce
- Cloves, quarter of an ounce
- Mace, quarter of an ounce

## Directions

1. Well pound all ingredients
2. Mix together
3. Keep in a stoppered bottle in a dry place
