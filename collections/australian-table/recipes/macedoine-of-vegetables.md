## Ingredients

- equal quantity of carrots and turnips
- dozen button onions
- butter
- gravy
- mushrooms
- young beans
- French beans
- haricots
- Brussels sprouts
- asparagus plants
- tops of a cauliflower
- teaspoon of sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. cut carrots and turnips of the size of almonds
2. peel a dozen button onions
3. put all in a saucepan with a piece of butter
4. let them brown gently
5. add a little gravy and some mushrooms, young beans, French beans, haricots, and some Brussels sprouts (the last must be parboiled), some asparagus plants, and the tops of a cauliflower, with a teaspoon of sugar
6. let them all boil gently
7. just before serving thicken the sauce with a little flour
8. serve
