## Ingredients

- lampreys
- strong beef gravy
- allspice
- mace
- cloves
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- port wine
- sherry
- horseradish vinegar
- anchovies
- lemon
- butter
- flour
- sliced lemon
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cleanse the fish and remove the tough membrane from the back
2. Put the lampreys into a stewpan and cover them with strong beef gravy
3. Add a dessert-spoonful of mixed allspice, mace, and cloves in powder, a spoonful of salt, a few grains of cayenne pepper, a gill of port wine, the same of sherry, and a table-spoonful of horseradish vinegar
4. Cover the pan and stew gently till the fish are tender
5. Take them out and add to the gravy two anchovies beaten to paste and the juice of a lemon
6. Boil it up and strain it, and if requisite, thicken it with butter and flour
7. Warm the lampreys in this sauce before serving
8. Garnish with slices of lemon and sippets of toasted bread
