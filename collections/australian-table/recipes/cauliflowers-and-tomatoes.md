## Ingredients

- 2 Cauliflowers
- ½ pint Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflowers and make the sauce by directions given elsewhere.
2. Dish the cauliflowers carefully and pour over them the sauce, leaving just the centre of the flowers clear.
3. Put into the oven for five minutes, and serve.
