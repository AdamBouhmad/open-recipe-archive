## Ingredients

- a quarter to half a pound of hops
- two and a half gallons of water
- six pounds and a quarter of brown sparkling sugar
- a quarter of a pound of ginger
- two and a half gallons of cold water

## Directions

1. Boil from a quarter to half a pound of hops, according to taste, in two and a half gallons of water
2. Strain the water from the hops on six pounds and a quarter of brown sparkling sugar to dissolve it
3. Bruise and boil a quarter of a pound of ginger
4. add to it two and a half gallons of cold water, and fill up a five-gallon keg
5. Put in the tap, and bung it up tight
6. in a week the beer will be fit to drink
