## Ingredients

- meat
- garlic
- brown sugar
- salt
- saltpetre
- allspice
- mace
- white pepper
- preserved bay-leaf
- lard
- peppercorns
- pickled chitterlings

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and rub with garlic
2. Put in a mortar and pound until it becomes a paste
3. Sprinkle with brown sugar, and work up again
4. Work up with the following ingredients: two parts of salt, and one of saltpetre, well pounded with allspice, mace, and white pepper, and a small quantity of preserved bay-leaf
5. Lay the meat on the table, and roll out
6. Lay strips of lard on it with peppercorns
7. Roll the sausages, place in pickled chitterlings, tie very tight at each end
8. Hang up to dry
