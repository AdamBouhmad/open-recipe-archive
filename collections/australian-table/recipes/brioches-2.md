## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- a little hot water
- a spoonful of yeast
- a pound of flour
- three-quarters of a pound of butter
- five eggs
- a little water
- a few grains of salt

## Directions

1. Make a paste with half a pound of flour and a little hot water, with a spoonful of yeast
2. Wrap up this paste in a cloth, and put it in a warm place, for twenty minutes in summer and an hour in winter
3. Put a pound of flour on the board, mix the paste that you have leavened with it, and three-quarters of a pound of butter, five eggs, a little water, and a few grains of salt
4. Knead all together three times
5. Wrap up warmly, and let it remain nine or ten hours
6. Cut the paste into pieces, the size you wish to make your brioches
7. Mould them into the form you wish, egg them over, and bake small ones half an hour; those of a larger size an hour and a half
