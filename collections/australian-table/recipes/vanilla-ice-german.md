## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- one quart of good cream
- half a drachm of bruised vanilla
- four or five yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of sugar into a syrup
2. add one quart of good cream
3. having tied up half a drachm of bruised vanilla in a muslin slip, boil the whole together
4. having beaten up four or five yolks of eggs, add them to the cream
5. press the vanilla, and take the slip out, and place it in a refrigerator to cool
