## Ingredients

- one pound of lean beef
- cold water
- salt
- roasted onions or burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of lean beef, free from fat, and separated from the bones, in the finely-chopped state in which it is used for sausages or mincemeat
2. Uniformly mix it with its own weight of cold water
3. Slowly heat to boiling
4. Boil briskly for a minute or two
5. Strain through a sieve, from the coagulated albumen and the fibrin
6. Mix with salt, and other additions by which soup is usually seasoned
7. Tinge somewhat darker by means of roasted onions, or burnt sugar
