## Ingredients

- one pint of beef gravy thickened
- a wine-glassful of sherry
- the juice and peel of a lemon
- a few leaves of basil
- a shalot
- a few grains of cayenne
- a little essence of anchovy

## Directions

1. Put into a pan a pint of beef gravy thickened
2. Add a wine-glassful of sherry, the juice and peel of a lemon, a few leaves of basil, a shalot divided, a few grains of cayenne, and a little essence of anchovy
3. Let them simmer for five minutes
4. Strain through a tammy
