## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- milk
- flour
- rice
- apple
- onion
- curry powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Mince the onion and apple finely, and fry in the butter till a good colour
2. Sprinkle over it the curry powder and flour, and mix well
3. Pour in the milk and stir until it boils
4. Slice the tomatoes and put them in and simmer very gently for half an hour
5. Season with salt, dish carefully and serve either in a border of rice, or with rice moulds on a separate dish
