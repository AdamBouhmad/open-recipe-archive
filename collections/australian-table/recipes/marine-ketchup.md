## Ingredients

- Strong beer, one gallon
- Anchovies, a pound and a half
- Peeled shalots pounded, one pound
- Mace, half an ounce
- Mustard-seed, half an ounce
- Cloves, half an ounce
- Bruised pepper, a quarter of an ounce
- Bruised ginger, a quarter of an ounce
- Mushroom ketchup, one quart
- Vinegar, one quart

## Directions

1. Combine strong beer one gallon, anchovies a pound and a half, and peeled shalots pounded one pound
2. Add half an ounce of mace, mustard-seed, and cloves
3. Add bruised pepper and ginger, a quarter of an ounce
4. Add mushroom ketchup and vinegar, one quart
5. Proceed in the same manner as camp ketchup
