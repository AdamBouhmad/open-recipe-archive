## Ingredients

- 3 Whiting or Bream
- 1.5 oz. Butter
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley, Pepper, and Salt

## Directions

1. Fillet the fish and cut them into strips, wash them well in cold water and dry in a cloth; twist them round, and lay in a buttered soup plate.
2. Sprinkle with white pepper and salt, and chopped parsley.
3. Put in the rest of the butter, cover with another soup plate, and stand over a saucepan of boiling water for three-quarters of an hour; reverse the plates once while it is cooking.
4. Place in a hot dish, and pour over it the butter and parsley in which it was cooked.
