## Ingredients

- the curd of a quart of milk
- four ounces of butter
- eight ounces of ground white sugar
- the yolks of four eggs
- one mealy potato of medium size
- one ounce each of sweet and bitter almonds
- the strained juice of a lemon
- the rind of one lemon grated
- nutmeg
- a wine-glass of brandy
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Take the curd of a quart of milk, and drain dry
2. rub it through a sieve into a pan
3. mix with it four ounces of butter, eight ounces of ground white sugar, the yolks of four eggs, and one mealy potato of medium size
4. when well mixed, add one ounce each of sweet and bitter almonds (using the strained juice of a lemon to prevent their oiling while being pounded), the rind of one lemon grated, some nutmeg, and a wine-glass of brandy, and stir in with the rest
5. Line the pans with puff paste
6. fill them with the mixture, and bake
