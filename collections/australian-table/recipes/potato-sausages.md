## Ingredients

- 3 Cold Potatoes
- 1 lb. Cold Meat
- Nutmeg, Pepper, and Salt
- 1 Egg
- Bread Crumbs
- Hot Fat

## Directions

1. Mash up the potatoes, and mince the meat.
2. Mix together, season nicely, and mix into a paste with half the egg.
3. Roll into sausages, egg and bread crumb, and fry in hot fat.
4. Dish in a pyramid, and garnish with fried parsley.
