## Ingredients

- half a tea-cupful of capillaire
- sugar
- a few drops of orange-flower water
- a pint of brown sherry
- one bottle of ale
- a bottle of soda-water
- grated nutmeg
- a piece of toast

## Directions

1. Combine half a tea-cupful of capillaire, sugar, a few drops of orange-flower water, a pint of brown sherry, and one bottle of ale
2. Just before placing it on the table, add a bottle of soda-water
3. Sprinkle some grated nutmeg on the top of the liquor and put a piece of toast in it
4. Place a napkin on the handle of the cup
