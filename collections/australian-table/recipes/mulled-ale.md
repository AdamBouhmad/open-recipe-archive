## Ingredients

- a pint of good sound ale
- a little nutmeg
- sugar
- three eggs
- a little cold ale
- a table-spoonful of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of good sound ale with a little nutmeg and sugar
2. Beat up three eggs and mix them with a little cold ale
3. Add the hot ale to it gradually and pour backwards and forwards from one vessel to another to prevent its curdling
4. Warm and stir till it thickens
5. Add a table-spoonful of brandy and serve hot
