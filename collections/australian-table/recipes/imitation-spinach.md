## Ingredients

- Young green shoots of the pumpkin plant
- Salt
- Carbonate of soda
- 1 ounce Butter
- Pepper
- Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Wash the very young green shoots of the pumpkin plant well and put them into a large saucepan, with a very little water seasoned with salt and a pinch of carbonate of soda; keep pressing them down into the water and boil till soft.
2. Turn into a colander and squeeze very dry, put into a saucepan with one ounce of butter, pepper, salt, and a few drops of lemon juice.
3. Stir about till thoroughly hot through, dish neatly, and serve.
