## Ingredients

- scraps of bread
- a little milk
- three eggs
- three ounces of sugar
- a little nutmeg
- ginger
- allspice
- a few currants

## Directions

1. Put scraps of bread in a saucepan, with a little milk
2. break in three eggs, three ounces of sugar, a little nutmeg, ginger, and allspice
3. stew well together
4. This pudding may be either boiled or baked
