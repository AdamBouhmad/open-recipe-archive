## Ingredients

- two bottles of porter or ale
- half a pint of rum
- from half a pound to a pound of white sugar
- the whites and yolks of six eggs

## Directions

1. Boil two bottles of porter or ale
2. Add half a pint of rum and from half a pound to a pound of white sugar
3. After this has boiled remove it from the fire and put into the mixture the whites and yolks of six eggs previously well whisked
4. Stir it for a minute and serve in a punch-bowl
