## Ingredients

- end of neck of mutton or fillet from leg or shoulder
- water
- carrot
- turnip
- salt
- pepper
- forcemeat balls
- celery
- yolks of two eggs
- flour
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Take the end of a neck of mutton or a fillet taken from the leg or shoulder
2. Place it in a stewpan with enough water to cover it
3. Throw in a carrot and a turnip and season, but not too highly
4. When nearly done, remove the meat and strain off the gravy
5. Return both to the stewpan with forcemeat balls and some fine celery cut in small pieces
6. Let all stew together till perfectly done
7. Stir in the yolks of two eggs, a little flour, and the juice of half a lemon, which must be mixed with a little of the gravy before pouring into the stewpan
8. Take care to prevent its curdling
