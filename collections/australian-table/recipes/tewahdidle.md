## Ingredients

- a pint of small beer
- a tablespoon of brandy
- a teaspoonful of brown sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- a roll of thin lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Combine a pint of small beer, a tablespoon of brandy, and a teaspoonful of brown sugar with a little grated nutmeg and a roll of thin lemon-peel
