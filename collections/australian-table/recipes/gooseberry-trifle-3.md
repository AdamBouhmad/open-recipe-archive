## Ingredients

- a quart of gooseberries
- sugar
- nutmeg
- half a pint of cream
- half a pint of milk
- the yolk of an egg
- a pint of cream
- two eggs
- lemon-peel
- sugar

## Directions

1. Proceed with a quart of gooseberries and pass them through a sieve
2. Place them at the bottom of a dish
3. Add sugar and a little nutmeg
4. Mix half a pint of cream with the same quantity of milk, and the yolk of an egg
5. Scald it over the fire, and stir it
6. Add a little sugar, and let it cool
7. Pour over the gooseberries a whip made the day before of a pint of cream, two eggs, lemon-peel to flavour a little, and sugar
