## Ingredients

- puff paste
- white of an egg
- sugar
- preserves

## Directions

1. Roll out puff paste about half an inch thick
2. Stamp out what will be used, work it up again and roll it the same thickness
3. Stamp out smaller pieces to correspond with the larger ones
4. Stamp out the centre of the smaller rings
5. Brush over the larger pieces with white of an egg
6. Place a small ring on the top of every large circular piece of paste
7. Egg over the tops and bake sharp for less than a quarter of an hour
8. Sift sugar over them and put them back in the oven to colour
9. Fill in the rings with preserves
10. Dish them up and serve
