## Ingredients

- 2 quarts Stock
- 1 Apple
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 1/2 oz. Curry Powder
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 oz. Butter

## Directions

1. Slice up the apple, onion, and carrot, and fry them in the butter
2. Sprinkle over the curry powder and flour and brown that too
3. Pour over the boiling stock and stir until it boils up
4. Simmer gently for one hour
5. Rub through a sieve and return to the saucepan
6. Bring to the boil, flavour with salt and lemon juice
7. Pour into a warm tureen and serve
8. Send well-boiled rice to the table with this soup
