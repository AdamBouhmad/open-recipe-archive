## Ingredients

- 1 doz. Large Oysters
- 3 Rashers Bacon
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lime Juice

## Directions

1. Mix some pepper, salt, and lemon juice together, and lay the oysters in this.
2. The bacon should be cut very thin, and then into strips about 1 inch broad and 3 inches long.
3. Roll these up, and thread on a skewer first a roll of bacon and then an oyster, until the skewer is full.
4. Lay on a baking sheet and cook in the oven for about ten minutes.
5. Have ready a hot dish, slip the bacon and oysters off the skewers on to this, and serve hot.
