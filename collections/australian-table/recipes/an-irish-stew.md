## Ingredients

- two pounds of mutton
- four pounds of potatoes
- six onions
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of mutton cut into fair pieces, and four pounds of potatoes
2. Peel the potatoes and cut them in half
3. Peel and slice six onions
4. First put in the bottom of the saucepan a layer of potatoes, and then some of the meat and onions, a small spoonful of pepper, and two of salt
5. This dish must simmer, but not boil, for a couple of hours
