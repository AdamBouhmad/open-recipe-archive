## Ingredients

- a pint of peas
- two quarts of water
- mutton bones
- two heads of celery
- a sprig of savory
- a little sweet marjoram
- pepper
- salt
- an onion
- a pound of bacon
- toasted bread
- powdered mint

## Directions

1. Soak the peas over night
2. Put them on to simmer in two quarts of water, with some mutton bones, two heads of celery, a sprig of savory, and a little sweet marjoram, pepper, and salt
3. Let it simmer for an hour
4. Add an onion, cut small
5. When nearly done, take out the mutton bones, and add a pound of bacon, cut into little pieces
6. Serve with toasted bread and powdered mint
