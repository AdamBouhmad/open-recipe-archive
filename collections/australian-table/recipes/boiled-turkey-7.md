## Ingredients

- turkey
- liver
- gizzard
- flour
- napkin
- crumbs of bread
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon-peel
- anchovy
- oysters
- shredded mushrooms
- suet
- butter
- egg
- lemon juice
- salt
- celery
- oyster sauce
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Truss the turkey, with the liver and gizzard in the wings
2. Well dredge with flour
3. Fasten in a napkin
4. Fill the crop with forcemeat made of crumbs of bread, parsley, pepper, salt, nutmeg, lemon-peel, anchovy, oysters chopped, shredded mushrooms, a little suet, butter, and bound together with an egg
5. In the water in which the turkey is boiled put the juice of three lemons, two ounces of butter, and a handful of salt
6. Let it simmer
7. Serve with celery, oyster, or brown sauce
