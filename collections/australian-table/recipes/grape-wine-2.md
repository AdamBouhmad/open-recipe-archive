## Ingredients

- ripe grapes
- water
- loaf sugar
- white of eggs or isinglass
- brandy
- crude tartar

## Directions

1. Take any given quantity of ripe grapes, macerate and strain them
2. Add two gallons of water to one gallon of juice, and three pounds and a half of loaf sugar
3. Let it ferment for six weeks
4. Clear it with white of eggs or isinglass, and barrel it
5. Add some brandy
6. A little crude tartar is an improvement
