## Ingredients

- 6 Tomatoes
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1.5 oz. Flour
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. If the tomatoes are ripe they need not be cooked; but if at all hard, boil them for five minutes
2. Slice up and rub through a sieve
3. Put the butter into a small saucepan, and when it is dissolved stir in the flour and sugar
4. Pour in the tomato juice and stir until it boils
5. Season with salt to taste
