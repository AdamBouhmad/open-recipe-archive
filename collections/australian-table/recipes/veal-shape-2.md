## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Neck of Veal
- 1 Lemon
- Pepper and Salt
- 1/2 lb. Ham or Bacon
- 3 Eggs
- 1/2 pint Stock

## Directions

1. Put the meat into a saucepan with the rind of the lemon cut very thinly.
2. Pour in the stock and simmer very gently for three hours.
3. If the bacon is not cooked put it in and stew it for the last half-hour.
4. Take up the meat and ham, cut it off the bones.
5. Put these back in the saucepan and let them boil quickly without the lid.
6. Boil the eggs hard and cut them in slices and arrange in a plain mould or dish.
7. Lay in the veal and ham, and season with pepper
