## Ingredients

- half a pint of water
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- four table-spoonfuls of flour
- six yolks and four whites of eggs
- boiling clarified dripping or lard

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan half a pint of water and a quarter of a pound of butter
2. Stir it until it boils, and mix with it four table-spoonfuls of flour
3. Stir it together, and add six yolks and four whites of eggs, two at a time
4. Let it cool, and, with a dessert-spoon, drop it into boiling clarified dripping or lard
