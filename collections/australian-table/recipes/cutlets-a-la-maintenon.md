## Ingredients

- veal cutlets or mutton or lamb-chops
- egg yolk
- bread crumbs
- sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Cut veal cutlets, mutton, or lamb-chops, about half an inch thick, and flatten them
2. Brush them over with the yolk of an egg
3. Put over them crumbs of bread and sweet herbs, quant. suff.
4. Fold each cutlet in a separate piece of paper
5. Fry them until nearly done
6. Take them out of the pan, and put them on the gridiron to finish and dry
