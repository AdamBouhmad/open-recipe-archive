## Ingredients

- ripe bananas
- oranges
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up some ripe bananas and oranges, removing the pips from the oranges, but saving the juice
2. Take a deep glass dish, lay at the bottom some bananas, then a layer of oranges
3. Sprinkle well with sugar, then some more bananas and oranges and sugar, until all the materials are used up
4. Cover and let it stand for an hour, then serve as a sweet
