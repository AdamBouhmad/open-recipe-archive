## Ingredients

- one bottle of cider
- half a bottle of perry
- half a bottle of sherry
- half a gill of brandy
- one sliced lemon with rind pared thin
- one toasted biscuit

## Directions

1. Mix cider, perry, and sherry with half a gill of brandy
2. Add a sliced lemon with rind pared thin
3. Add a toasted biscuit
4. Drink iced or cooled
