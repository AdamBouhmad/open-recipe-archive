## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of white sugar
- one pound of fresh butter
- ten eggs
- half a pound of currants
- a little white wine

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, one pound of white sugar, and one pound of fresh butter
2. Work it well up until it crumbles
3. Add ten eggs and half a pound of currants with a little white wine
4. Bake in tins only half filled
