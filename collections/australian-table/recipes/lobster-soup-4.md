## Ingredients

- 1 Lobster, Crayfish, or Tin of Lobster
- 2 quarts Fish Stock
- 1 pint of Milk
- 1 oz. Cornflour
- Lemon Juice
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. The fish stock for this soup should be well flavoured with vegetables.
2. If a crayfish be used, remove all the white meat and boil the shells in the stock for half an hour and strain them out; thicken with the cornflour, pour in the milk, and boil up.
3. Cut the lobster into small pieces and put into the soup; simmer for ten minutes.
4. Flavour with lemon juice and salt, pour into a warm tureen, and serve with fried bread.
5. Wash the shells well in cold water before putting them into the soup.
