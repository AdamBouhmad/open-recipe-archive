## Ingredients

- mangold
- grated carrot, about one-third of the bulk of mangold juice
- essence of lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Wash and pare the mangold
2. Grate on a coarse grater
3. Express the juice through a cheese cloth
4. Add to the juice obtained about one-third of its bulk of grated carrot
5. Simmer gently for about forty-eight hours
6. Add a drop or two of essence of lemon or vanilla to improve it
