## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- four eggs
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- cold milk
- one teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the butter with the flour
2. Beat the eggs together with cold milk
3. Pour gradually into the flour
4. Add one teaspoonful of salt
5. Work it for a few minutes
6. Cut the dough the size of a breakfast-plate
7. Bake in a quick oven
