## Ingredients

- pepper
- salt
- mustard
- pint of broth or stock
- couple of shredded onions
- vinegar
- toasted bread sippets

## Directions

1. Cut into joints, and flour them with a little pepper, salt, and mustard
2. Put into a stewpan with a pint of broth, or stock, and a couple of shredded onions
3. Let the joints simmer, and add a little more thickening, if necessary, and a table-spoonful of vinegar
4. Serve, and garnish with toasted bread sippets
