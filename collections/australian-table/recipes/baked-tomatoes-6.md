## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- bread crumbs
- butter
- mustard
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Slice up the tomatoes, spread with a very little made mustard
2. Season some brown bread crumbs with pepper and salt, and sprinkle the slices well
3. Put into a buttered dish and bake till soft
4. Serve hot
