## Ingredients

- strawberries
- raspberries
- currants
- morella cherries
- white sugar
- a gill of brandy
- cherry and apricot kernels

## Directions

1. Extract the juice from strawberries, raspberries, currants, and morella cherries separately
2. Add to each a small proportion of white sugar so as to make it sweet and rich, but not a thick syrup
3. Strain it off as clear as possible
4. Mix the juices of the four fruits together, observing to put in a smaller proportion of currant and raspberry juice than of the cherry and strawberry
5. To a pint of juice add a gill of brandy
6. Bottle it with the addition of some cherry and apricot kernels
