## Ingredients

- the whites and yolks of six eggs
- a spoonful of rose-water
- six ounces of powdered loaf sugar
- one ounce of bruised carraway seeds
- six ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Beat up the whites and yolks of six eggs separately with a spoonful of rose-water
2. Add six ounces of powdered loaf sugar
3. Beat the whole well
4. Add one ounce of bruised carraway seeds and six ounces of flour
5. Drop them on wafer-paper
6. Bake in a moderate oven
