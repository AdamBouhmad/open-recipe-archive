## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 2 oz. dripping
- 1 oz. sugar
- 1 pint sour milk
- 1 teaspoonful cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Rub the dripping into the flour, stir in the sugar, cream of tartar, and soda.
2. Mix into a very light dough with the milk, turn on to a floured board; divide into two parts.
3. Flatten these out into two cakes, divide each one into four pieces, brush over with milk.
4. Put on to a floured tin and bake in a hot oven from fifteen to twenty minutes.
