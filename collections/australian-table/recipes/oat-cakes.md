## Ingredients

- a pound of oatmeal
- a pint of buttermilk
- a quarter of an ounce of carbonate of soda
- a little salt
- half a pound of flour

## Directions

1. Soak a pound of oatmeal in a pint of buttermilk
2. Rub a quarter of an ounce of carbonate of soda and a little salt in half a pound of flour, and mix with the oatmeal
3. Roll out any thickness, and bake in a moderate oven
