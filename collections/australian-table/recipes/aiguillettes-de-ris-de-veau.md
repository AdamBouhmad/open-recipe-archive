## Ingredients

- three throat sweet-breads
- water
- one onion
- one carrot
- one turnip
- two bay-leaves
- one pint of stock
- salt
- pepper
- one ounce of butter
- white sauce
- yolks of eggs
- juice of a lemon
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Boil sweet-breads in water ten minutes
2. Pour off the liquor
3. Add one onion, one carrot, one turnip, two bay-leaves, and a pint of stock
4. Simmer twenty minutes
5. Cut them into round pieces a little larger than a shilling
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Put them into a stewpan with an ounce of butter
8. Dress them white
9. Dip each piece with a fork into a mixture of white sauce, yolks of eggs, and the juice of a lemon
10. When cold, run the skewer through the pieces, putting several on each skewer
11. Dip them into egg well beaten, then into bread crumbs
12. Fry in hot lard
13. Serve on a napkin
