## Ingredients

- celery
- water
- slice of lemon
- mace
- butter
- flour
- egg or two
- nutmeg
- table-spoonful of cream
- half a cupful of gravy or stock

## Directions

1. Take off the outside and the green ends of the celery, boil them in water till tender
2. put in a slice of lemon, a little mace, thicken with butter and flour
3. Beat up an egg or two, grate in some nutmeg, mix with a table-spoonful of cream, put to it half a cupful of gravy or stock
4. let it simmer until sufficiently thick
5. Serve hot
