## Ingredients

- beef suet chopped
- six ounces
- Malaga raisins stoned
- six ounces
- currants washed and picked
- eight ounces
- bread crumbs
- three ounces
- flour
- three ounces
- eggs
- three ounces
- sixth of a nutmeg
- small blade of mace
- cinnamon
- half a tea-spoon of salt
- sugar
- four ounces
- candied lemon
- one ounce
- citron
- one ounce
- half a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and spice together
2. Mix the milk with them by degrees, then the rest of the ingredients
3. Dip a clean linen cloth into boiling water and put it in a hair sieve
4. Flour it a little and pour in the mixture
5. Tie it up close
6. Put it into a saucepan containing six quarts of boiling water
7. Keep a kettle of boiling water alongside of it to fill up your pot as it wastes
8. Keep it boiling six hours, at least
