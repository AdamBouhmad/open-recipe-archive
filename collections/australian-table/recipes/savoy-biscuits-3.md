## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three ounces of white sugar
- lemon-peel
- four ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs first
2. Mix the other articles
3. Drop on paper
4. When they have been a short time in the oven, sprinkle a little sugar over them
