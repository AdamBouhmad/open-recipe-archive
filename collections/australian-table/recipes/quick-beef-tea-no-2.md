## Ingredients

- 1 pound gravy beef
- 1 pint water
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Pass the meat twice through a sausage machine
2. Put it into a saucepan and pour over the cold water
3. Stand on the stove and stir constantly until it comes to boiling point but do not allow it to boil
4. As soon as it changes colour from red to brown strain through a colander
5. Add salt to taste
