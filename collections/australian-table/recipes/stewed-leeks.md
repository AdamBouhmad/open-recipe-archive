## Ingredients

- 1 bunch Leeks
- 1/2 pint Stock
- Pepper and Salt
- 1/2 oz. Butter
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Cut off the roots and green tops of the leeks and wash well.
2. Put them into a saucepan with the stock and stew very gently till soft; take them up and put on to a hot dish.
3. Put the butter into a saucepan, and when it is dissolved stir in the flour, mix well, and strain in the stock.
4. Stir until it boils.
5. Season with some pepper, salt, and a few drops of lemon juice.
6. Pour over the leeks, and serve.
