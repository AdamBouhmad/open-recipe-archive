## Ingredients

- calf's head
- brains
- parsley
- sage leaf
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- tongue
- bacon
- ham
- pickled pork
- lemon

## Directions

1. Tie the head in a cloth, and boil for two hours in plenty of water
2. Tie the brains up in a separate cloth, with a little parsley and a leaf of sage
3. Boil the brains one hour
4. Chop the brains small, warm them up in a saucepan, with a little butter, pepper, and salt
5. Lay the tongue, which has been boiled at the same time and peeled, in the middle of a small dish
6. Place the brains round it
7. Or mince the brains and put them into parsley and butter, and pour over the head
8. Serve with bacon, ham, or pickled pork in another dish
9. Garnish with lemon
