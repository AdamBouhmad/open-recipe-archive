## Ingredients

- six ounces of hops
- three gallons of water
- half a peck of ground malt
- three pints of patent yeast

## Directions

1. Boil six ounces of hops in three gallons of water for three hours
2. Strain it off, and let it stand ten minutes
3. Add half a peck of ground malt, stir it well up, and cover it over
4. Return the hops, and put the same quantity of water to them again, boiling them the same time as before
5. Strain it off to the first mash
6. Stir it up and let it remain four hours
7. Strain it off, and set it to work at 90° with three pints of patent yeast
8. Let it stand about twenty hours
9. Take the scum off the top, and strain it through a hair sieve
10. It will then be fit for use. One pint is sufficient to make a bushel of bread.
