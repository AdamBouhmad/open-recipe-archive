## Ingredients

- ham
- parsley
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. When skinned, chop parsley (free from grit, but not wetted or washed) impalpably fine
2. Scatter it over the ham
3. Grate a whole nutmeg over it
4. This will keep it almost any length of time, and adds a nice flavour
