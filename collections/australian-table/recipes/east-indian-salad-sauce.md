## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 1 teaspoonful Curry Powder
- 1/4 gill Oil
- 1/4 gill Vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard.
2. Put the yolks into a bowl and work them till they are quite smooth.
3. Work in gradually the curry powder, oil, and vinegar.
4. Blend well, and it is ready.
