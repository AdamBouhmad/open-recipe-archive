## Ingredients

- Fish
- 1 spoonful of Flour
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 Egg
- Water
- Oil
- Dry fine crumbs
- Hot Fat
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Wash fish well in cold water, but not soaked; dry in a cloth.
2. Mix on a plate a spoonful of flour, pepper, and salt.
3. Beat on another plate an egg, with a spoonful each of water and oil, and have plenty of dry fine crumbs on a sheet of paper.
4. Dip the fish in the flour and dust off again; put at once into the egg and cover well.
5. Drop into the crumbs, shake them all over it; toss in the hands to shake all the loose crumbs off.
6. Lay on a plate separately, and either fry at once or leave in a cool place for an hour or two.
7. Plunge into plenty of hot fat and fry till crisp and brown; drain for a few minutes on kitchen paper.
8. Pile on a dish, and garnish with either fresh or fried parsley.
