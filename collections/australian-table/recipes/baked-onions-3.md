## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Onion
- 1 pint Thick Gravy
- 1/2 pint Water
- Pepper and Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, put them on in cold water, and bring to the boil.
2. Strain the water off, butter a baking dish, put in the onions, pour in the water, cover with a plate, and stand in a moderate oven.
3. Stew until soft, place in a hot dish and pour over them, either a nice gravy thickened with a little butter and flour, or some plain melted butter.
4. Serve hot.
