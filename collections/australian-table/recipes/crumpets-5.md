## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of flour
- three pints of milk
- two table-spoonfuls of yeast
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Add three pints of milk to a pound and a half of flour
2. Add two table-spoonfuls of yeast, and two eggs
3. Mix the milk lukewarm
4. Beat it into a batter
5. Let it stand until it rises into bladders
6. Bake on polished iron in the tin rims
