## Ingredients

- mutton, fowls, or pigeons
- butter
- rice
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- saffron
- pomegranate juice

## Directions

1. According to the number of guests, take mutton, fowls, or pigeons, boil them until half done, then put the meat and broth into a basin
2. Cut the half-cooked meat into pieces, and put them into a pot, with some butter, and fry them of a light brown colour
3. Wash the rice well, then place it over the meat in the pot, and pour the broth over the rice till it is covered to a full finger's depth
4. Cover the pot, and keep a clear fire under it, until the rice is done
5. Cover the top of the pot with a cloth folded, and melt some butter and pour in
6. Cover it again, and let it simmer
7. Pepper and salt to taste
8. Serve in dishes, with the meat arranged at the top. One dish may be of its natural colour, white; another made yellow with saffron; and a third red, with pomegranate juice
