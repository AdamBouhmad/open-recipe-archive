## Ingredients

- ½ lb. Flour
- 1 gill Milk
- 1 oz. Butter
- ⅛ saltspoonful Salt

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour, sprinkle in the salt, and make into a dough with the milk.
2. Knead till smooth, roll out very thin.
3. Cut into small rounds, prick well with a fork, put on to a floured tin and bake for about ten minutes.
4. They should not get brown.
