## Ingredients

- half a pound of cold beef
- a couple of lettuce
- half a tea-spoonful of salt
- one spoonful of vinegar
- three spoonfuls of good salad oil
- a tea-spoonful of black pepper
- garlic or a shalot

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of cold beef into thin slices
2. Put the slices into a salad bowl with a couple of lettuce cut into pieces
3. Season with half a tea-spoonful of salt, one spoonful of vinegar, three of good salad oil, and a tea-spoonful of black pepper, with a little garlic or a shalot cut up fine
4. Mix well together
