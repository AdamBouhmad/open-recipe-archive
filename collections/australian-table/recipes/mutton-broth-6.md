## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 scrags of mutton and shank bones
- Cold water
- Teaspoonful of salt
- 1 dozen peppercorns
- One onion and carrot (if vegetables are allowed)

## Directions

1. Carefully trim the scrags of mutton and remove the pith from the bones
2. Wipe with a damp cloth
3. Break these and the shank bones into very small pieces
4. Put them into an enamelled saucepan well covered with cold water
5. Add a teaspoonful of salt and stand on the stove
6. When it boils up remove the scum very carefully
7. Add 1 dozen peppercorns and an onion and carrot if vegetables are allowed the patient
