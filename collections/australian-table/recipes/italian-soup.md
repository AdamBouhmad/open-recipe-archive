## Ingredients

- 2 ounces macaroni
- 2 quarts water or pot boilings
- 3 tomatoes
- 1 ounce butter
- 2 ounces cheese rind
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Put the water or stock on to boil
2. When it boils put in the macaroni and boil from twenty-five to thirty minutes
3. While it is boiling grate up a dry piece of cheese
4. Put the tomatoes into boiling water and remove the skin
5. Slice them up and put them into a saucepan with the butter and some pepper and salt, and cook them for a few minutes
6. When the macaroni is soft, cut it into pieces one inch long
7. Put a layer of tomatoes at the bottom of the soup tureen, then a layer of grated cheese, then one of macaroni
8. Repeat this until all the materials are used up
9. Pour over it boiling the liquor in which the macaroni has been cooked
10. Cover down for a few minutes, and serve
