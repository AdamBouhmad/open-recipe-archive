## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Oysters
- 1 pint of Milk
- Cornflour
- Vegetables
- 2 quarts Fish Stock
- 1 onion
- 6 cloves
- 2 doz. white peppercorns
- a fagot of herbs
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- salt
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Put the stock and the liquor from the bottle of oysters into a saucepan with an onion stuck with six cloves, 2 doz. white peppercorns, and a fagot of herbs, and boil together for half an hour
2. Strain off and return to the saucepan with the milk
3. When nearly boiling thicken with a tablespoonful of cornflour and boil two or three minutes
4. Put in the oysters and simmer for five minutes
5. Flavour with a little lemon juice, nutmeg, and salt
6. Pour into a warm tureen, and send fried bread to table with it
