## Ingredients

- ten or more cutlets
- larding
- good braise
- small stewed onions
- puree of onions

## Directions

1. Take ten cutlets, or more, according to your party; do not pare or shape them, and take care that each cutlet has a bone
2. Lard them, and season your larding before you use it
3. Stew them in good braise for three or four hours
4. When done put them on a tin plate, with another cover on it, on which you place a weight to make the cutlets a good shape
5. When they are cold trim them neatly and fry them
6. When you serve them, place them either in a crown, or one bone over the other in a sort of pyramid, with small stewed onions round, and in the middle a puree of onions
