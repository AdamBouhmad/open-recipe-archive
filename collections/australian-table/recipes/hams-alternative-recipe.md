## Ingredients

- brown sugar
- salt
- saltpetre

## Directions

1. One pound and a half of brown sugar, two pounds and a half of salt, and half a pound of saltpetre
2. Rub it dry on the hams, bacon, and cheeks
3. Turn every day for the first ten days, and afterwards every other day
4. Use the acid, and bran it as before
