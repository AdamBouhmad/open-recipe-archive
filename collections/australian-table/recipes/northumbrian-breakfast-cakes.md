## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- four ounces of butter
- a little salt
- a tea-spoonful of sugar
- a proportionate quantity of milk or cream

## Directions

1. Rub butter well into flour
2. Add salt, sugar, and milk or cream
3. Knead it well together
4. Bake on a girdle
5. When done, open the cakes, and butter
6. Send them up hot to the breakfast-table, enveloped in a napkin
