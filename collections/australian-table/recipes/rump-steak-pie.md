## Ingredients

- two pounds of rump-steaks
- shalots
- half an ounce of pepper and salt mixed
- mushroom ketchup
- thickening
- hard-boiled eggs
- oysters

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of rump-steaks into pieces
2. Chop very fine a few shalots, and mix them with half an ounce of pepper and salt mixed
3. Strew some of the mixture on each layer of steaks, and so on until the dish is full
4. Add a mushroom ketchup and thickening, with a few hard-boiled eggs
5. A few oysters is an improvement
6. The paste is elsewhere given
