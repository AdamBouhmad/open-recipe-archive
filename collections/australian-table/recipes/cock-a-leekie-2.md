## Ingredients

- four pounds of lean beef
- a fowl
- leeks
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil four pounds of lean beef until the liquor is good
2. Take out the meat, and put into the soup a fowl cut into pieces, with half the quantity of leeks intended to be used
3. Let it simmer
4. Half an hour before serving, add the remainder of the leeks, and the seasoning of pepper and salt
