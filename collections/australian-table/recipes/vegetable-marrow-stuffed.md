## Ingredients

- 1 Vegetable Marrow
- 1/4 lb. Veal Forcemeat
- 1/4 pint Melted Butter Sauce
- Salt
- Tape

## Directions

1. Peel a marrow and cut it in half length-ways.
2. Prepare some veal forcemeat by recipe given elsewhere, and make it hot in a saucepan.
3. Remove the seeds from the marrow and put in their place the forcemeat; put the pieces together and bind round with tape.
4. Have ready a fish kettle full of boiling water seasoned with salt; lay the marrow on the drainer and plunge into the water.
5. Boil gently for about twenty-five minutes.
6. Slip the marrow carefully into a dish and pour over some melted butter sauce.
