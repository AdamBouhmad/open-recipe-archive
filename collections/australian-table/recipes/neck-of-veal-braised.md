## Ingredients

- a little lean bacon or ham
- an onion
- two carrots
- two heads of celery
- a glass of sherry or Madeira
- neck of veal
- a little water
- a little butter and flour
- a little lemon

## Directions

1. Put a little lean bacon or ham into a stewpan, with an onion, two carrots, two heads of celery, and a glass of sherry or Madeira
2. Lay on them the neck and a little water, and stew until it is tender
3. Strain off the liquor, mix a little butter and flour in a stewpan until brown, stir some of the liquor in, and boil it up
4. Skim it, and use a little lemon, and serve with the meat
5. The bacon must be browned in a salamander
