## Ingredients

- two pounds of orange-flowers
- two quarts of white wine

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of orange-flowers, as fresh as you can get them
2. Infuse them in two quarts of white wine
3. Distil them
4. It will yield a curious perfuming spirit
