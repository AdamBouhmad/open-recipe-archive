## Ingredients

- egg
- grated nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- sweet herbs
- crumbs of bread
- flour
- ketchup
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut them in quarters
2. Beat up an egg, with some grated nutmeg, pepper, salt, and parsley fine, a few sweet herbs, and crumbs of bread
3. Mix all together, and cover the fowl, &c.
4. Fry in a pan until well warmed
5. Thicken a little gravy with flour and ketchup
6. Lay the poultry fry on the dish, and garnish with slices of lemon
