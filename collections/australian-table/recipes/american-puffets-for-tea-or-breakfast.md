## Ingredients

- one pint of cold milk, that has been boiled
- a quarter of a pint of yeast
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- five eggs

## Directions

1. Make into a stiff paste
2. Put on the baking-tin to rise
3. Bake in the usual manner without taking off
