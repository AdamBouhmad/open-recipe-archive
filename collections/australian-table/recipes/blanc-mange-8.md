## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- an ounce of isinglass
- a quarter of a pound of the best sugar
- a quarter of an ounce of cinnamon
- a little grated nutmeg
- half the peel of a lemon
- a bay leaf

## Directions

1. Simmer over a slow fire till the isinglass is dissolved
2. Strain it through a napkin into a mould for the table
3. It can be coloured or flavoured any way, as long as no ingredient is used that does not curdle the milk
