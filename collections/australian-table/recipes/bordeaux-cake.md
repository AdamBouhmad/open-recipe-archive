## Ingredients

- paste
- butter
- flour
- choice preserve

## Directions

1. Roll paste out less than a quarter of an inch thick
2. Cut it into six or seven portions of equal size
3. Lay them on buttered tins floured
4. Bake them in an oven until they are firm and crisp and of a pale brown
5. When cold, spread upon each a different kind of choice preserve
6. Pile the whole evenly in the form of a cake
7. The top may be iced and decorated
