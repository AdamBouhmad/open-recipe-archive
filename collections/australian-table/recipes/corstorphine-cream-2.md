## Ingredients

- milk of three or four days
- cream
- sugar
- strawberry fruit (in season)

## Directions

1. Put the milk of three or four days together, with the cream, into a vessel
2. Allow it to remain there until it becomes sour and coagulated
3. Draw off the whey
4. Add fresh cream
5. When brought to table, eat with sugar, and in the strawberry season with that fruit
