## Ingredients

- brioche paste
- the size of a nut of saffron in powder
- a quarter of a pound of the best raisins stoned
- double the quantity of currants
- sugar
- some slices of candied citron cut in small pieces

## Directions

1. Make with the brioche paste
2. Add thereto the size of a nut of saffron in powder, a quarter of a pound of the best raisins stoned, and double the quantity of currants, sugar, and some slices of candied citron cut in small pieces
3. Mix and knead all together
4. Let the paste be soft and well mixed
5. Put it into a buttered pan, and let it stand eight hours in winter and four in summer
6. Without touching it, let it bake in a slow oven for an hour
7. It will be done enough when it has taken a good colour
