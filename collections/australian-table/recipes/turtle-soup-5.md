## Ingredients

- turtle
- stock
- shalots
- thyme
- marjoram
- spice
- butter

## Directions

1. Hang up the turtle the night before it is to be dressed
2. Cut off its head, or a weight may be placed on its back to make it extend itself
3. When dead, cut the belly part clear off
4. Sever the fins at the point and take away the white meat, and put it into water
5. Draw, cleanse, and wash the entrails
6. Scald the fins, the head, and the belly shells
7. Saw the shell about two inches deep all round, scald and cut it into pieces
8. Put the head, shell, and fins in a pan and cover them with stock
9. Add shalots, thyme, marjoram, and spice, chopped
10. Stew it till tender
11. Take out the meat and strain the liquor through a sieve
12. Cut the fins in three pieces and take out all the brown from the bones, and cut it in square pieces
13. Melt some butter in a stewpan and put the white meat to it
14. Simmer it till nearly done
