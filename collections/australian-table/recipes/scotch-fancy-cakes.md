## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- a pound and a half of flour
- four eggs
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- half a gill of cream

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of butter into a pound and a half of flour
2. Break four eggs into an earthen pan with three quarters of a pound of sugar
3. Whisk them for five minutes
4. Make a print with the butter and flour
5. Pour in the eggs and sugar and add half a gill of cream
6. Mix all into a dough
7. Cut into pieces two inches across and an eighth of an inch in thickness
8. Cover tins with wafer paper
9. Lay cakes on it
10. Bake
