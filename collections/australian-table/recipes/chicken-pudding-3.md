## Ingredients

- beef-suet paste
- chicken
- chorissa or sliced smoked veal
- beef gravy
- mushroom essence or powder
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace

## Directions

1. Line a basin with a good beef-suet paste
2. Cut up a chicken and lightly fry the pieces
3. Place them in a stewpan with thin slices of chorissa or, if at hand, slices of smoked veal
4. Add enough good beef gravy to cover them
5. Season with mushroom essence or powder, pepper, salt, and a very small quantity of nutmeg and mace
6. Simmer gently for a quarter of an hour and fill the pudding
7. Pour over part of the gravy and keep the rest to be poured over the pudding when served in the dish
8. Cover the pudding closely with the paste, the end of which should be wetted with a paste-brush to make it adhere closely
