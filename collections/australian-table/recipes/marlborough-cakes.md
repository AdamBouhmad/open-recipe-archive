## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one pound of ground sugar
- one pound of flour
- two ounces of carraway seeds

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs and one pound of ground sugar well
2. by degrees mix in one pound of flour and two ounces of carraway seeds
3. bake in tin pans in a quick oven
