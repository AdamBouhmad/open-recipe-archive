## Ingredients

- kangaroo
- fat bacon

## Directions

1. Divide a piece of kangaroo nicely into cutlets, two or three inches broad and a third of an inch thick.
2. Take a straight clean stick, about four feet long, sharpened at both ends.
3. On the narrow part of this, for the space of a foot or more, spit the cutlets at intervals, and on the end place a piece of delicately rosy fat bacon.
4. Stick the strong end of the stick-spit fast and erect in the ground, close by the fire, to leeward, taking care that it does not burn.
5. Allow the bacon on the summit of the spit to soften in the blaze, dropping a lubricating shower of rich and savoury fat upon the leaner kangaroo cutlets below.
