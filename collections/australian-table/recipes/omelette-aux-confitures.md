## Ingredients

- eggs
- lemon-peel
- cream
- orange or apple marmalade, apricot or gooseberry jam, or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks and whites of six eggs separate.
2. Add to the yolks a little grated lemon-peel.
3. Add the whites, and beat the whole well together, adding a little cream, two spoonfuls of orange or apple marmalade, apricot or gooseberry jam, currant jelly, or any preserve.
4. Fry and finish in the same way as a sugar omelette.
