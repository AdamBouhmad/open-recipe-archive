## Ingredients

- half a pint of milk
- half a pint of water
- an ounce of butter
- two lumps of sugar
- a little salt
- the yolks of two eggs and the white of one
- a table-spoonful of yeast
- one pound of flour

## Directions

1. Put half a pint of milk and the same quantity of water into a saucepan, with an ounce of butter, two lumps of sugar, and a little salt
2. stir it over the fire till rather warmer than new milk
3. beat up the yolks of two eggs and the white of one, and add a table-spoonful of yeast
4. Put them into a jug and mix the whole together
5. add one pound of flour, and stir it in free from lumps
6. Let the jug remain by the fire to rise
7. rub a little butter on the baking-iron, then pour on the batter to bake
