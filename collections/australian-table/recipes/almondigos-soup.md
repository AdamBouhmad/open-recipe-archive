## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a calf's foot
- two quarts of water
- a blade of mace
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- a turnip
- white pepper
- salt
- balls of forced meat and egg-balls
- the yolks of four eggs
- a dessert-spoonful of lemon-juice
- three ounces of sweet almonds
- a spoonful of powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Put a knuckle of veal and a calf's foot into two quarts of water, with a blade of mace and a bunch of sweet herbs, a turnip, a little white pepper, and salt
2. When sufficiently done, strain and skim it, and add balls of forced meat and egg-balls
3. A quarter of an hour before serving beat up the yolks of four eggs, with a dessert-spoonful of lemon-juice, and three ounces of sweet almonds, blanched, and with a spoonful of powdered white sugar
4. Beat this mixture into the soup until it thickens, taking care to prevent its curdling
