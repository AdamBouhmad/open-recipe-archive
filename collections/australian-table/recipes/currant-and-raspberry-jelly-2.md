## Ingredients

- two pounds of ripe currants
- a pound of raspberries
- a spoonful of water
- sugar
- a little isinglass

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of ripe currants and a pound of raspberries, put them on a stone in a preserving-pan, with a spoonful of water
2. slightly mash them with a wooden spoon, and strain them through a napkin
3. Mix this juice with sugar and a little isinglass
4. boil and skim, and put it in a mould to set
