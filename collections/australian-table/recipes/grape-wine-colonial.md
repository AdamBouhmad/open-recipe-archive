## Ingredients

- a gallon of water
- a gallon of grapes (bruised)
- three pounds of loaf sugar per gallon of wine
- a bottle of brandy per ten gallons of wine
- isinglass

## Directions

1. To a gallon of water add a gallon of grapes (bruised), and let them stand a week without stirring
2. Draw it off fine, and to every gallon of wine add three pounds of loaf sugar
3. Put it in a cask; do not bung it until the fermentation has ceased
4. Add a bottle of brandy to every ten gallons of wine
5. Bottle it on a clear day in July or August
6. Try it previously; and if not fine, dissolve an ounce of isinglass and put in the cask, and let it remain for two or three weeks
