## Ingredients

- half a drachm of mace
- ginger
- cloves
- nutmegs
- three quarts of Lisbon wine
- cinnamon
- twenty ounces of white sugar

## Directions

1. Add half a drachm of mace, ginger, cloves, and nutmegs to three quarts of Lisbon wine; also cinnamon
2. Let it stand for a few days, when strain, and add twenty ounces of white sugar to the liquor
