## Ingredients

- turkey
- slices of bacon
- puree of chicory, celery, or turnips

## Directions

1. Wrap round the turkey prepared for roasting some slices of bacon, and dress it of a pale colour
2. Prepare a puree of chicory, celery, or turnips; celery is preferable
3. Put the roast turkey into it, to give the puree a flavour, and serve
4. Or stew the vegetables, and surround the turkey in the dish
