## Ingredients

- half a pint of peas
- half a lettuce
- a little mint
- a small onion
- two tablespoonfuls of oil
- a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Put half a pint of peas into a stewpan
2. Add half a lettuce chopped small, a little mint, a small onion cut up, two tablespoonfuls of oil, and a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar, with water sufficient to cover the peas
3. Watch from time to time that they do not become too dry
4. Stew gently, taking care that they do not burn, till they are perfectly soft
5. When done they should look of a yellowish brown
