## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Throw the garlic into five boiling waters, with a little salt
2. Boil five minutes in each water
3. Drain the garlic
4. Put it into the dripping-pan under the roasting mutton
5. Roast until done
