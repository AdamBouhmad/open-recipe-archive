## Ingredients

- gooseberries, damsons, morello cherries, currants mixed with raspberries, plums of all kinds, cranberries, and mulberries
- half a pound of moist sugar to a quart of fruit
- yolks of eggs
- paste
- whites of two eggs
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Lay fresh picked and washed fruit in the dish, with the centre highest, and half a pound of moist sugar to a quart of fruit
2. rub the edges of the dish with yolks of eggs, cover with paste in the usual way, and bake in a quick oven
3. For icing, beat up the whites of two eggs to a froth, lay it over the pie or tart, sift over it pounded sugar, and return it to the oven to set
