## Ingredients

- 2 Kidneys
- 1/2 oz. Butter
- 1/2 Slice of Toast
- Parsley
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Skin and chop the kidneys small, put into a saucepan with the butter, and cook for two or three minutes.
2. Season with pepper and salt.
3. Spread it on the toast, sprinkle over some chopped parsley, and serve.
