## Ingredients

- a nip of brandy
- a table-spoonful of port wine
- a table-spoonful of ice
- sugar and water to taste
- a sprig of mint

## Directions

1. Combine a nip of brandy, a table-spoonful of port wine, and a table-spoonful of ice
2. Add sugar and water to taste
3. Add a sprig of mint
