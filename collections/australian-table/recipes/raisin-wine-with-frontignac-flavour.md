## Ingredients

- twelve pounds of Malaga raisins
- twelve gallons of water
- twenty-four pounds of loaf sugar
- half a pint of yeast
- half a peck of elder-flowers

## Directions

1. Boil twelve pounds of Malaga raisins in twelve gallons of water till they are quite absorbed
2. Take them out, extract the stones, and return the pulp to the water
3. Pour on twenty-four pounds of loaf sugar, and ferment it with half a pint of yeast
4. When the fermentation begins to subside, tie up in a muslin bag half a peck of elder-flowers, and suspend the bag for some days in the liquor
5. Remove it, and when clear draw off and bottle
