## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half a pound of moist sugar
- half a gill of good yeast
- half a pint of lukewarm milk
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. To two pounds of flour put half a pound of moist sugar
2. Make a cavity in the centre, and stir in half a gill of good yeast, half a pint of lukewarm milk, with enough flour to thicken it
3. Let it rise for half an hour or more
4. Melt half a pound of butter lukewarm
5. Stir it into the other ingredients with enough warm milk to make it a soft paste
6. Throw some flour over it, and let it rise for three-quarters of an hour
7. Have ready a baking-tin, mould the buns into sizes, and bake
8. When drawn from the oven, milk them over
