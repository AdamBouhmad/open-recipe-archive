## Ingredients

- 2 Rabbits
- 1/2 lb. Pickled Pork
- 1 Onion
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 pint Gravy
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- 1 tablespoonful Red Currant Jelly

## Directions

1. Wash and joint up the rabbits and cut the pork into slices.
2. Lay some of the pork over the bottom of a baking jar, and on this some joints of rabbit.
3. Continue in layers until all the meat is in, then put in the onion, sliced up, the fagot of herbs, and a few peppercorns.
4. Cover down closely, stand in a moderate oven, and cook for two hours.
5. Take up the meat and arrange nicely on a hot dish, strain the gravy into a saucepan, thicken with the flour, and when it boils stir in the jelly.
6. Flavour to taste, pour it over the rabbits, and serve.
