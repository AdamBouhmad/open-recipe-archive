## Ingredients

- beans
- boiling water
- salt
- carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Slice up the beans and wash in cold water
2. Put them into plenty of boiling water, seasoned with salt and a quarter of a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
3. Boil quickly without the lid for about ten minutes or a quarter of an hour
4. When soft, strain off and shake the water out thoroughly
5. Put into a hot dish, and serve plainly or with melted butter
