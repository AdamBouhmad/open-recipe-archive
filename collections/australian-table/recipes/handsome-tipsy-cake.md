## Ingredients

- sponge cakes
- jam
- three glasses of sherry
- two glasses of brandy
- rich custard

## Directions

1. Provide some sponge cakes, place four close upon the dish upon which the cake is to be served
2. spread over them a thick layer of jam
3. upon this place three sponge cakes close together, spread over a thick layer of jam
4. complete the pyramid with one
5. Pour over the whole three glasses of sherry, so that the cakes may absorb all the wine
6. when this is done, add two glasses of brandy slowly, so that in absorbing the spirits, the cakes do not become too moist and break
7. Make a rich custard and pour over the whole
