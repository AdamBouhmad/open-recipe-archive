## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Almonds
- 2 oz. Sugar
- 1 1/2 oz. Arrowroot

## Directions

1. Blanch and chop the almonds very small
2. Mix them with the sugar and arrowroot
3. Put the milk on to boil
4. When it boils pour it on to the arrowroot and stir
5. If it does not get thick enough, pour back into the saucepan and boil for a minute
6. Turn into a wet mould and stand away till firm
7. Turn out and serve with jam or custard sauce, or it may be served plainly
