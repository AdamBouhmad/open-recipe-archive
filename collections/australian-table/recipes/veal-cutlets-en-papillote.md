## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- egg
- bread crumbs
- bacon
- parsley
- chives
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- white paper

## Directions

1. Cover the cutlets on both sides with an egg
2. Add a forcemeat of bread crumbs, bacon, parsley, chives, and mushrooms, all chopped fine
3. Add salt and pepper
4. Lay thin slices of bacon over this seasoning
5. Envelope the whole carefully in white paper, well buttered
6. Let them fry gently over a slow fire
7. Serve them in the paper
