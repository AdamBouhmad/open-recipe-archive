## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. Cold Potatoes
- 2 Eggs
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Milk

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes, beat the butter to a cream, then beat in the eggs, pepper, salt, and milk.
2. Stir up the potatoes, pour into a buttered pie-dish, and bake for about half an hour.
3. Serve hot.
