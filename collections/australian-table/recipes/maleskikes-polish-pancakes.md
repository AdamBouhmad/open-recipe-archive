## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- a pint and a half of milk or cream
- two ounces of melted fresh butter
- some grated nutmeg
- lemon-peel rasped upon loaf sugar
- a little salt
- ten ounces of flour
- butter or lard for frying
- a few currants

## Directions

1. Beat up eggs and mix them with milk or cream, melted butter, nutmeg, lemon-peel, salt, and flour to make a smooth batter
2. Put butter or lard into a frying-pan and when it boils pour in some of the batter
3. Sprinkle in a few currants
4. Fry nicely and sprinkle the pancake with sugar
5. Roll it with a two-pronged fork and serve hot
