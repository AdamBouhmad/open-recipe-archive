## Ingredients

- One glass of Outard brandy
- half a glass of Jamaica rum
- a table-spoonful of arrack
- a quarter of a lemon
- a table-spoonful of white sugar
- water and ice

## Directions

1. Let it be thoroughly mixed, and serve with a straw
