## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Potatoes
- 1/2 pint Onion Sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel and boil the potatoes in the usual way, slice them up and put them into a hot dish.
2. Make some rather thin onion sauce by directions given elsewhere, season with a few drops of lemon juice, and pour over the potatoes.
3. Serve hot.
