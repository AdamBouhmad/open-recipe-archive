## Ingredients

- a tea-cupful of biscuit-powder
- beaten eggs
- white sifted sugar
- grated lemon-peel
- a spoonful of orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Mix into a smooth batter a tea-cupful of biscuit-powder, with beaten eggs, and sweeten with white sifted sugar.
2. Add grated lemon-peel, and a spoonful of orange-flower water.
3. Fry of a light brown.
4. The flavour may be varied by substituting a few beaten almonds, with one or two bitter, instead of the orange-flower water.
