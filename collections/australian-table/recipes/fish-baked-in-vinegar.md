## Ingredients

- 2 Mullet
- 1/2 pint Vinegar
- 1 gill Water
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 doz. Peppercorns
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the fish, dry them on a cloth, and rub them with a little salt
2. Lay them in a deep dish, put in the herbs and peppercorns, pour over the vinegar and water
3. Cover with a tin, and stand in a cool oven, and bake very slowly for an hour
4. Take them out and let them get quite cold in the vinegar, then lay them in a dish, and strain the sauce over
5. Garnish with sprigs of parsley
