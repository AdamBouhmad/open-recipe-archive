## Ingredients

- six pounds of moist sugar
- five ounces of bruised ginger
- four ounces of cream of tartar
- four lemons
- seven gallons of boiling water
- half a pint of yeast
- a pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Put into a nine-gallon cask six pounds of moist sugar, five ounces of bruised ginger, four ounces of cream of tartar, four lemons, and seven gallons of boiling water
2. When cool, add half a pint of yeast
3. Let it work a couple of days
4. then strain, and add a pint of brandy
5. In a few days bottle and wire down
