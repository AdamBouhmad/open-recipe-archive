## Ingredients

- raspberries
- water
- loaf sugar
- brandy

## Directions

1. To every quart of raspberries put the same quantity of water
2. Bruise, and let them stand two days
3. Strain off the liquor, and to every gallon put three pounds of loaf sugar
4. Put the liquor in a cask
5. When ready to bottle, add a table-spoonful of good brandy to each bottle
