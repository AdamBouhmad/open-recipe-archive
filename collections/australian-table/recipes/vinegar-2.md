## Ingredients

- eleven gallons of water
- fourteen pounds of sugar
- yeast

## Directions

1. Boil eleven gallons of water and fourteen pounds of sugar for twenty minutes
2. Pour it into a vessel to cool
3. When milk-warm, add yeast, and let it ferment
4. Place it in a warm place, and in a few months you will have good vinegar
