## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of raisins, stoned and cut
- one pound of currants
- three ounces of orange-peel
- three ounces of citron
- three ounces of almonds blanched and cut
- mixed spice
- three-quarters of a pound of butter
- warm water
- a quarter of a pint of yeast
- half a gill of yeast

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of flour, one pound of raisins stoned and cut, and one pound of currants
2. Add three ounces of orange-peel, the same of citron, and of almonds blanched and cut
3. Mix together and add some mixed spice
4. Make a hole in the flour and break in three-quarters of a pound of butter
5. Pour warm water on the butter to soften it
6. Work the flour and butter together
7. Add a quarter of a pint of yeast and work it smooth
8. Cut a third part of the paste for sheets
9. Spread out the rest of the paste on the table and put the fruit in
10. Pour about half a gill of yeast over the fruit and paste and work together
11. Make it round and roll out the sheet in a circular form
12. Lay the bun in the middle and gather the sheet round it
13. Roll it out the desired thickness
14. Run a fork through in different parts down to the bottom and pinch it on the top
15. Flour double paper and put the bun on it and give a cut round the side
16. Bake moderately
