## Ingredients

- two pounds flour
- quarter of an ounce bi-carbonate of soda
- quarter of an ounce salt
- one pint sour buttermilk

## Directions

1. Mix to the consistence of light dough
2. Roll out about half an inch thick, or less
3. Cut them in any shape you please
4. Bake them on a girdle, turning, to brown on each side
