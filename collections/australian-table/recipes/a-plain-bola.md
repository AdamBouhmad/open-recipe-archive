## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of white sugar
- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- two eggs
- one pound and a half of flour
- three spoonfuls of yeast
- a little milk
- two ounces of citron
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take three-quarters of a pound of white sugar, three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter, two eggs, one pound and a half of flour, three spoonfuls of yeast, a little milk, and two ounces of citron, cut thin.
2. Mix into a light paste.
3. Bake in a tin.
4. Strew powdered sugar and cinnamon over it before baking.
5. The above ingredients are often baked in small tins or cups.
