## Ingredients

- flour
- six table-spoonfuls of sugar
- three eggs, well beaten
- two ounces of fresh butter
- a little grated lemon-peel
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough with flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and lemon-peel
2. Mix them in a pan with a wooden spoon
3. If the dough is not firm, add more flour and sugar
4. Turn it out of the pan, and work it slightly
5. Cut it into the shape of round long biscuits
6. Glaze them with the white of an egg
