## Ingredients

- haunch of venison
- salt
- water
- clean dripping or butter
- flour
- white paper ruffle
- plain gravy
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Take a haunch of venison and set it before a slow fire, taking care to paper it to prevent its scorching.
2. Baste it well with salt and water at first, but afterwards use clean dripping or butter.
3. Dredge it with flour.
4. When it is done it will be frothed and browned on both sides.
5. Wrap a white paper ruffle round the knuckle-bone, and send it to table with plain gravy.
6. Serve with currant jelly.
7. If the venison has hung long, the outer skin had better be removed.
