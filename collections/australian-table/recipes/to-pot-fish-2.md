## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- cayenne
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- oil
- vinegar
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Pull the fish to pieces, and pick out every bone
2. Mix with a little butter, cayenne, pepper, salt, mace, oil, and vinegar
3. Place the fish in pots, and pour clarified butter over it
