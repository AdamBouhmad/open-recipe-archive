## Ingredients

- a dessert-spoonful of mustard
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a little cayenne
- a large glassful of port wine or claret
- a table-spoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. Mix a dessert-spoonful of mustard, a salt-spoonful of salt, and a little cayenne in a large glassful of port wine or claret
2. Pour it on the meat just before serving
3. A table-spoonful of ketchup may be added
