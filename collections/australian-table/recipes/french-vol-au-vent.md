## Ingredients

- puff paste
- short paste
- yolk of an egg
- ragout of fowl, fish, or veal

## Directions

1. Prepare puff paste
2. Make an under-crust of short paste as thick as a penny piece
3. Moisten it a little
4. Over this crust put some puff paste, well rolled
5. Put these two crusts at the bottom of a pie-dish
6. Cover it with a lid the size intended for the vol-au-vent
7. Cut the puff paste all round the lid
8. Brush the surface with the yolk of an egg
9. With a knife cut the vol-au-vent within an inch and a half of the edge, striking the knife into the paste three-quarters of its thickness, to form the cover
10. Bake it in a moderate hot oven
11. When the crust is well browned take it out
12. Fill it with whatever you like; ragout of fowl, fish, veal, or any morsel
