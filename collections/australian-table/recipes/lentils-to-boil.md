## Ingredients

- Lentils
- Salt
- Water
- Butter

## Directions

1. Wash the lentils well in cold water, cover them with cold water seasoned with salt, and boil for one hour and a half.
2. Strain all the water off, put into a hot dish with about half an ounce of butter, and serve.
