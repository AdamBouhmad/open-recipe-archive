## Ingredients

- cold tea
- sliced lemon
- a wine-glass of good brandy
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Properly make the tea by pouring boiling water on the leaves
2. Allow to stand five minutes
3. Pour into a jug with slices of lemon at the bottom
4. Add a wine-glass of good brandy when cool
5. Add sugar to taste
6. Do not add milk
