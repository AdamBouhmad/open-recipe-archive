## Ingredients

- lamb
- bacon
- bread crumbs
- salt
- parsley
- sharp sauce

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb on the thin side with small pieces of bacon
2. Sprinkle the other part with bread crumbs
3. When almost done take it from the fire
4. Sprinkle it a second time with crumbs of bread, seasoned with salt and parsley
5. Put the joint down to brown
6. When done serve with sharp sauce
