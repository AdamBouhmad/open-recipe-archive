## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 3 Eggs
- 1 1/2 oz. Sugar
- Flavouring

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of the eggs into a basin and whisk them
2. Put the milk into a saucepan, and when it is boiling pour it over the eggs, stirring all the time
3. Strain back into the saucepan and whisk well till it comes to boiling point
4. Draw away from the fire, but continue whisking for a few minutes
5. Pour into a basin, sweeten and flavour to taste
