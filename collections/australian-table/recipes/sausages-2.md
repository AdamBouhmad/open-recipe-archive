## Ingredients

- pork
- veal
- fat
- bread-crumbs
- thyme
- parsley
- sage
- leeks
- garlic or shalot
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg

## Directions

1. To each pound of pork allow a pound of veal and one pound of fat
2. Mix them well
3. Allow a pound of bread-crumbs, thyme, parsley, an ounce of sage, two heads of leeks, a little garlic or a shalot, chopped fine, with pepper, salt, and nutmeg
4. To each pound allow one egg, the yolks and whites separate
5. Beat both well, mix in the yolks, and as much of the whites as necessary to moisten the bread
6. Add all the ingredients together
