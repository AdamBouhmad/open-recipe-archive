## Ingredients

- 6 Small Fish
- Vegetables
- Salt and Pepper
- Lemon Juice
- 1 doz. peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 1 small turnip
- 1 onion
- A small piece of celery
- A fagot of herbs

## Directions

1. Choose small fish of different kinds and fillet them
2. Wash the bones in cold water and remove the black substance from them
3. Put them into two quarts of cold water with a teaspoonful of salt
4. When it boils remove the scum and add 1 doz. peppercorns, one carrot, one small turnip, one onion, a small piece of celery, and a fagot of herbs
5. Put the vegetables in whole
6. Boil this together for one hour
7. Strain off through a hair sieve and return to the saucepan
8. Wash the vegetables that have been boiled in it, slice them up and put them into the liquor
9. Cut the fillets of fish into small pieces and put them in
10. Simmer for half an hour
11. Put in a little lemon juice
12. Pour into a tureen, and sprinkle a little chopped parsley on the top
13. Send brown bread and butter to table with it and a lemon
