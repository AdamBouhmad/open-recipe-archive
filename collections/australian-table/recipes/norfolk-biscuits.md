## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of butter
- three pounds and a half of flour
- a quarter of a pint of yeast
- water

## Directions

1. Melt the butter with water
2. Knead it well
3. Use an ounce for each biscuit
4. Bake on buttered paper
