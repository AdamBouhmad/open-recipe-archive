## Ingredients

- sixteen lemons
- one pound and a half of white sugar
- one gallon of boiling water
- one wine-glass of sherry wine

## Directions

1. Take sixteen lemons, pare them, cut in halves, squeeze well, and throw them into a pan
2. Add a pound and a half of white sugar, a gallon of boiling water, and a wine-glass of sherry wine
3. Mix, strain, and cool
