## Ingredients

- two quarts shelled green peas
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- butter or gravy

## Directions

1. Put peas into boiling water
2. Let them boil with a little parsley, chives, and salt
3. Strain them and rub them through a sieve
4. Add a little butter or gravy
5. Warm them up again and serve
