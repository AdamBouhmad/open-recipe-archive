## Ingredients

- Half a Calf's Liver
- 1 1/2 oz. Butter
- 1 Carrot
- Lemon Juice
- 1 Onion
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 pint of Gravy
- Parsley
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Wash and slice up the liver, and dip in the flour.
2. Fry very lightly and quickly in the butter and lay in a saucepan.
3. Slice up the carrot and fry in the same butter.
4. Stir in the gravy, boil up, and pour over the liver.
5. Simmer very gently for one hour, then dish carefully.
6. Season the gravy with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
7. Boil up and pour over it.
8. Serve hot.
