## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of dripping
- a dozen turnips
- a quarter of a pound of flour
- three pounds of mutton
- water
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- one tea-spoonful of pepper
- one tea-spoonful of brown sugar
- a little garlic

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a quarter of a pound of dripping
2. When hot, cut into small pieces a dozen turnips, put them into the dripping, and brown
3. Take them out, and put into the fat a quarter of a pound of flour; stir it until brown
4. Cut up three pounds of mutton into small pieces, and put in the saucepan, and add sufficient water to cover the meat
5. When the mutton is nearly done, add the turnips, season with a tea-spoonful of salt, one of pepper, and one of brown sugar, and a little garlic
