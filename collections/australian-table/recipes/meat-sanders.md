## Ingredients

- beef or mutton
- onion
- shalot
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- mashed potatoes
- fat

## Directions

1. Mince beef or mutton small with onion, shalot, pepper and salt, adding a little gravy
2. Put into scallop shells or tins, with a little gravy, making them about half full
3. Fill up with mashed potatoes
4. Put a little fat on the top of each, and brown in a Dutch oven
