## Ingredients

- half a pint of gin
- outer peel of a lemon
- lemon-juice
- sugar
- a glass of maraschino
- about a pint and a quarter of water
- two bottles of iced soda-water

## Directions

1. Pour half a pint of gin on the outer peel of a lemon
2. Add a little lemon-juice, sugar, a glass of maraschino, about a pint and a quarter of water, and two bottles of iced soda-water
3. The result will be three pints of punch
