## Ingredients

- fifteen ripe tomatoes
- a capsicum
- a few spoonfuls of gravy
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- a shalot

## Directions

1. Take fifteen ripe tomatoes, cut them in half, and squeeze them
2. put them into a stewpan, with a capsicum and a few spoonfuls of gravy
3. simmer for an hour
4. run them through a sieve
5. add pepper, salt, and onion cut small, or a shalot
