## Ingredients

- herrings
- sourish apples
- pickled gherkins
- boiled beetroot
- capers
- cold roast veal
- potatoes
- mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Soak herrings in water or milk for three hours
2. Remove herring heads and tails and discard
3. Gut, skin, and wash the herring bodies
4. Remove bones and cut flesh into small dice-like cubical pieces
5. Peel and core two sourish apples and cut them into small cubes like the herrings
6. Add two pickled gherkins, boiled beetroot, and a few capers, dividing them into the same small pieces
7. If desired, add a quarter of a pound of cold roast veal in small pieces
8. Mix all ingredients together
9. Boil two potatoes with their jackets on
10. Peel the potatoes and cut into small pieces while hot
11. Add the warm potato to the preceding ingredients and mix thoroughly
12. Pour milt or roe of the herring over the salad as previously indicated
13. Pour mayonnaise sauce over the herring salad to complete
