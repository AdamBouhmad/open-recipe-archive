## Ingredients

- lean meat
- curry stuff
- half a shalot between each piece
- yolk of an egg
- bread crumbs
- mixed parsley
- sweet herbs
- salt
- cayenne
- a little salad oil

## Directions

1. Take lean meat, cut it into pieces about an inch square, and a quarter of an inch thick
2. Rub these pieces with a little curry stuff, and put them upon skewers, with half a shalot between each
3. Dip the skewered meat into the yolk of an egg, and then into bread crumbs, mixed parsley, sweet herbs, salt, and cayenne
4. Dip them again in the egg, and a second time cover them with bread crumbs
5. Put these sticks of meat on a spit, and roast them, basting them with a little salad oil
6. Serve them up brown, with or without sauce
