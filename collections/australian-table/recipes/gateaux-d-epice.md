## Ingredients

- a pint of treacle
- half a pound of butter
- an ounce of powdered ginger
- an ounce of powdered cinnamon
- a quarter of an ounce of allspice
- two ounces of candied lemon-peel chopped fine
- six drops of tincture of vanilla
- flour as much as necessary

## Directions

1. Place the treacle over the fire
2. add the butter, and successively all the other things but the flour
3. when well mixed, take a single boil, stirring them, and then let them cool
4. When cold, mix in, with a wooden spoon, enough flour to make it into a pretty stiff paste
5. butter a tin baking-dish, and lay on it with the spoon the paste, in the size necessary to form the nuts
6. set the tin in the oven, and bake
7. When cold they are hard, if done
