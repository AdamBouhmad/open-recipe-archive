## Ingredients

- hundred chestnuts
- half a pound of sausage-meat
- liver
- butter
- parsley
- chives
- shalot
- clove of garlic
- glass of wine
- stock
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a hundred chestnuts, and put them on the fire in a poele with holes
2. After having taken off the first skin, put them again in poele till you remove the second skin
3. Chop half the chestnuts, and put them into a stewpan with half a pound of sausage-meat, the liver chopped, a little butter, some parsley, chives, shalot, and a clove of garlic, finely chopped
4. Put this on the fire for a quarter of an hour
5. Stuff the goose with it, and roast
6. Put the rest of the chestnuts into a stewpan, with a glass of wine, a little stock, pepper, and salt
7. When done serve round the goose
