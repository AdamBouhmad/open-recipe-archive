## Ingredients

- Jones's patent flour
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Mix lightly Jones's patent flour with cold milk or water.
2. As soon as mixed, put them into boiling water.
3. When done, take out and serve with melted butter and sweet sauce.
4. They won't take much more than five minutes boiling.
