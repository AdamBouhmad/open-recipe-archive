## Ingredients

- 1 tin Sardines
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Lettuce
- Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Split the sardines open and remove the bone.
2. Break some of the lettuce into a bowl, lay on this the sardines.
3. Chop up one of the eggs and sprinkle over them, pour on the dressing.
4. Cover with the rest of the lettuce, and garnish with the other egg cut in slices, and a little watercress or beetroot.
