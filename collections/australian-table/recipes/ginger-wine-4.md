## Ingredients

- half a pound of bruised ginger
- fourteen pounds of loaf sugar
- six gallons of water
- rind of a dozen lemons
- lemon-juice
- half a cup of yeast
- a pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of bruised ginger and fourteen pounds of loaf sugar in six gallons of water
2. Pour the liquor on the rind of a dozen lemons
3. Let it stand until cool, and then add the lemon-juice and half a cup of yeast
4. Let the fermentation proceed
5. Before you bottle the wine, add a pint of brandy
