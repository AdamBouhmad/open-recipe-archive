## Ingredients

- 1 Vegetable Marrow
- 1/2 pint Gravy
- 1 oz. Dripping
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the marrow and cut into pieces, remove the seeds, put on to a baking sheet with some beef dripping, and bake till soft and rather brown.
2. Thicken a little gravy with some flour, and season and flavour it nicely; dish the marrow and pour this sauce over.
