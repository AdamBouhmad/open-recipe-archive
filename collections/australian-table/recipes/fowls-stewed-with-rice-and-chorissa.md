## Ingredients

- fowl
- water or gravy
- half a pound of rice
- one pound of chorissa
- white pepper
- salt
- saffron

## Directions

1. Boil a fowl in sufficient water or gravy to cover it
2. When boiling for ten minutes, skim off the fat
3. Add half a pound of rice and one pound of chorissa cut in about four pieces
4. Season with a little white pepper, salt, and a pinch of saffron to colour it
5. Stew till the rice is thoroughly tender
6. The rice should be perfectly moist when served
