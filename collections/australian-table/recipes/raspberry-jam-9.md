## Ingredients

- raspberries
- a pound of sugar for each pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Weigh your fruit, and put it in the preserving pan to simmer or boil
2. When the fruit has set, add a pound of sugar for each pound of fruit
3. Let it boil ten or twelve minutes
4. Skim occasionally
5. When it sticks to the spoon it is fit for the jar or pot
