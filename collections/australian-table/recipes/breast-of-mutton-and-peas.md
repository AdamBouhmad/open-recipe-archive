## Ingredients

- 2 Breasts of Mutton
- 2 Onions
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Egg
- Bread Crumbs
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 pint Peas
- Salt and Pepper
- Hot Fat
- 12 Peppercorns

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat with a warm damp cloth, and put it into a saucepan with the vegetables.
2. Bring to the boil and stew very gently for two hours.
3. Take it up and remove all the bones, put it between two boards and stand some heavy weights on it till quite cold.
4. Cut into neat-shaped pieces, egg and bread crumb them.
5. Fry a good colour.
6. Boil the peas by recipe given elsewhere.
7. Pile the mutton on a dish and put the peas round.
