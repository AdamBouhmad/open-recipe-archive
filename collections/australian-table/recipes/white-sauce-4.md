## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Butter
- 0.5 oz. Flour
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a small saucepan, and when it is dissolved put in the flour
2. Mix well and pour on the cold milk and stir till it boils
3. Let it boil for two minutes and it is ready
4. It may be served either as a sweet or savoury sauce, putting either sugar or pepper and salt, as required
