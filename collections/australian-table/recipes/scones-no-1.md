## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 pint milk
- 1 oz. butter
- 2 teaspoonsful baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour, stir in the baking powder, and make into a very light dough with the milk; turn on to a floured board, knead for a few minutes, roll out about half an inch thick.
2. Cut into shapes, put on to a floured tin, and bake in a quick oven for about ten minutes.
3. Serve either hot or cold.
