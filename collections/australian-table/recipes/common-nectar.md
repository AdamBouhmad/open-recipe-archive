## Ingredients

- half a pound of loaf sugar
- one pint of cold water
- a bottle of hock
- one bottle of Madeira
- half a nutmeg
- a drop or two of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of loaf sugar into a large jug
2. add one pint of cold water
3. stir the sugar till dissolved
4. pour over it a bottle of hock and one bottle of Madeira
5. Mix them well together, and grate in half a nutmeg, with a drop or two of essence of lemon
6. Set the jug in a bucket of ice for an hour
