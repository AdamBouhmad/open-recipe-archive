## Ingredients

- salad oil
- eggs

## Directions

1. Pour into a Dutch oven or cheese-toaster two table-spoonfuls of salad oil.
2. Break the eggs into the tin, which ought to take out, and when the yolk is set send up the tin, with the eggs in it, hot.
