## Ingredients

- pint of wine
- sugar to taste
- little cinnamon
- yolks of eight eggs
- slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a pint of wine, put in sugar to taste and a little cinnamon
2. Boil it half an hour
3. Mix it lukewarm with the yolks of eight eggs, strained after being well beaten
4. When cool, put it in a bowl, in a saucepan of hot water till done enough
5. Pour it into the dish on which you serve it, cold, garnished with slices of lemon
