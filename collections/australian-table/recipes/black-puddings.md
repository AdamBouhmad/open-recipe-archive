## Ingredients

- blood
- salt
- grits or boiled rice
- bread
- milk
- winter savory
- thyme
- pennyroyal
- pepper
- ground spice
- beef suet or pig fat
- eggs

## Directions

1. Stir the blood with salt until cold
2. Put a quart of it to a quart of grits or boiled rice, and soak some bread in milk
3. Clean the grits well with salt and water
4. Chop winter savory, thyme, pennyroyal, pepper, and salt, and a little ground spice
5. Mix these about with three pounds of beef suet, or the fat of the pig
6. Add six eggs well beaten up
7. Add the rice, or grits, and bread, and fill your skins
