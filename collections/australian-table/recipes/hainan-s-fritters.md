## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of the best Florence oil
- one pound of flour
- four beaten eggs
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Take two spoonfuls of the best Florence oil, scald it
2. When hot, mix with it one pound of flour
3. Add four beaten eggs, and make it into a paste
4. Roll it out thin, and cut it into pieces about four inches square
5. Let them dry, and fry them in oil
6. The moment the pieces are put into the frying-pan they must be drawn up with silver skewers into different forms, according to fancy
7. Fry them a few minutes; they should be crisp when done
