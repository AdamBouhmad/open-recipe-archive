## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Water
- Carbonate of Soda

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage, then cut it into quarters and cut out the stalk.
2. Wash it well in salt and water, and leave in the water for half-an-hour.
3. Put it into a colander and shake all the water from it.
4. Place on the fire a large saucepan of water, and when it boils, put in two teaspoonsful of salt and a quarter of a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda.
5. Put in the cabbage and cover down till it boils up; then remove the lid and boil very quickly, pressing it down into the water from time to time.
6. Boil for from fifteen to twenty minutes; try it with a fork, and if soft turn into a colander, and very carefully press all the water from it.
7. Slip into a vegetable dish and cut into neat pieces.
