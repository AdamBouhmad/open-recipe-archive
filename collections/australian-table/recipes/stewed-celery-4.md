## Ingredients

- 3 heads of Celery
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Milk
- 1 oz. Flour
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Take only the white and best part of celery for this dish, pull it to pieces, wash well in salt and water, and tie in a bundle.
2. Put it into boiling water seasoned with salt, and boil for about half an hour, or until the fork will go through easily.
3. Take half a pint of the water in which it was boiled and mix it with the milk; make a sauce with this and the butter and flour by directions given for sauces.
4. Dish the celery and pour the sauce over.
