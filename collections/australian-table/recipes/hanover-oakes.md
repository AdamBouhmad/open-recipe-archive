## Ingredients

- yolks of eight eggs
- whites of four eggs
- a pound of finely-powdered white sugar
- rind and juice of two lemons
- a pound of blanched almonds, coarsely bruised
- three-quarters of a pound of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of eight eggs and the whites of four well together
2. Add a pound of finely-powdered white sugar, the rind and juice of two lemons
3. Beat them well together with a pound of blanched almonds, coarsely bruised, and three-quarters of a pound of flour
4. Shape and put them on a tin to bake
