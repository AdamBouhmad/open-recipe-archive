## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of suet
- a quarter of a pound of currants
- a quarter of a pound of raisins stoned
- a table-spoonful of moist sugar
- half a tea-spoonful of ginger
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put the flour, suet, currants, raisins, sugar, ginger, and salt together
2. Put the mixture into a pudding-cloth
3. Boil for two hours, or a little more
4. Turn the pudding out of the cloth
5. Serve with sifted sugar
