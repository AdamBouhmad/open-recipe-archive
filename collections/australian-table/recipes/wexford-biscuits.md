## Ingredients

- ten ounces of butter
- four ounces of flour
- three ounces of white sugar
- yolks of two eggs
- one table-spoonful of brandy
- white of the eggs
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Rub butter into flour and sugar
2. Mix in the yolks of eggs and brandy
3. Roll the paste out thin
4. Cut with a cutter into round shapes
5. Egg over the tops of each with the white of the eggs
6. Sift on white sugar
7. Bake in a quick oven
