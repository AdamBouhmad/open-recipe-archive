## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Roast Beef
- 1 lb. Bread Crumbs
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 Egg
- 1 pint Gravy
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mince up the beef finely and mix it with the bread crumbs.
2. Season with pepper, salt, nutmeg, or parsley.
3. Beat up the egg, mix it with the gravy, and pour over the meat and crumbs.
4. Butter a basin, sprinkle well with brown bread crumbs, put in the mince.
5. Cover over with a plate and bake for an hour.
6. Turn on to a hot dish and pour a little nice gravy round it.
