## Ingredients

- ten pounds of spirit (pale brandy)
- four pounds of white sugar
- four pints of water
- four drachms of powdered cinnamon
- forty-eight grains of cloves
- forty-eight grains of mace
- caramel

## Directions

1. To ten pounds of spirit add four pounds of white sugar and four pints of water
2. Add four drachms of powdered cinnamon, forty-eight grains of cloves, and the same quantity of mace
3. Colour with caramel
