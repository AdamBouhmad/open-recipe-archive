## Ingredients

- two ounces of peach, apricot, or other kernels
- half a pound of white sugar-candy
- a cup of cold water
- brandy

## Directions

1. Blanch two ounces of peach, apricot, or other kernels, and put them in a bottle
2. Dissolve half a pound of white sugar-candy in a cup of cold water
3. Add to the brandy after it has stood a month on the kernels
4. Strain, and bottle for use
