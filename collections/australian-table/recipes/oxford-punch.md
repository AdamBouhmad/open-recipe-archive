## Ingredients

- three fresh lemons
- loaf sugar
- two lemons
- two Seville oranges
- ten lemons
- four Seville oranges
- six glasses of calf's foot jelly
- four quarts of boiling water
- a bottle of capillaire
- half a pint of sherry
- a pint of brandy
- a pint of old rum
- a quart of orange shrub

## Directions

1. Rub the rind of three fresh lemons with loaf sugar so as to extract the oil
2. Peel finely two lemons more and two Seville oranges
3. Use the juice of ten lemons and four Seville oranges
4. Add six glasses of calf's foot jelly and put it into a large jug and stir the whole
5. Pour in four quarts of boiling water and set the jug on the hob for twenty minutes
6. Strain the liquor into a large bowl
7. Pour in a bottle of capillaire, half a pint of sherry, a pint of brandy, a pint of old rum, and a quart of orange shrub
8. Stir it well as you pour in the spirits
