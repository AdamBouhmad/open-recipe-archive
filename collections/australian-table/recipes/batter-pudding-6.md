## Ingredients

- three eggs
- a salt-spoon of salt
- about four ounces of flour
- half a pint of milk
- a little powdered ginger

## Directions

1. Break three eggs into a basin, with a salt-spoon of salt, beat them well
2. add about four ounces of flour, and, by degrees, half a pint of milk and a little powdered ginger
3. Boil in a cloth or mould well floured, or bake
4. Serve with sweet sauce
