## Ingredients

- ham
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put the ham in cold water to soak for twenty-four hours before boiling
2. Scrape it well
3. Place in your copper, well covered with cold water
4. Let it come to boil slowly
5. After it has boiled for twenty minutes take all the fire from under the copper
6. Let it remain until the water is cool, or until it is convenient to remove it out of the water
