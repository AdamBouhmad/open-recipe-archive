## Ingredients

- sheep's head
- liver
- heart
- tongue
- stock
- gravy
- pepper
- salt
- lemon
- ketchup
- egg
- bread crumbs
- brains
- batter
- bacon

## Directions

1. Take the head, and after being well cleaned, parboil it
2. Egg and crumb it well, and put it before the fire to brown, or in an oven
3. Mince the liver, the heart and tongue, and add to it a small quantity of stock, or good gravy
4. Add pepper and salt, with the squeeze of a lemon, a little ketchup, and let it simmer
5. Fry the brains in thick batter
6. Put the mince in the dish, the head in the centre, and round the dish the brain fritters
