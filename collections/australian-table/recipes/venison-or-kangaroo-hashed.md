## Ingredients

- meat
- stock gravy
- gill of port wine
- table-spoonful of ketchup
- lemon
- salt
- cayenne
- currant jelly sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in slices
2. Put it in a stewpan, with a small quantity of stock gravy, a gill of port wine, a table-spoonful of ketchup, some lemon, with salt and cayenne in moderation
3. Let it simmer
4. Serve in a hot dish
5. Serve with currant jelly sauce
