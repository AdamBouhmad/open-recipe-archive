## Ingredients

- two pounds of fine flour
- two table-spoons of yeast
- a little warm water
- two ounces of butter
- the yolk of an egg
- new milk

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of fine flour and two table-spoons of yeast with a little warm water
2. Put to rise in half an hour
3. Put two ounces of butter and the yolk of an egg in as much new milk as will make it a proper stiffness
4. Mix all well up
5. When risen, bake in a quick oven
