## Ingredients

- chops or slices of mutton
- gravy or thickening
- potatoes
- salt

## Directions

1. Put a few chops or slices of mutton at the bottom of a pie-dish
2. Add a little gravy or thickening on the meat
3. Fill up the dish with potatoes sliced green
4. Add salt
5. Bake
