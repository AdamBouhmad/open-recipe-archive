## Ingredients

- ½ doz. Tomatoes
- 1 Carrot
- 2 Small Onions
- 12 Peppercorns
- 1 fagot Herbs
- ½ teaspoonful Salt
- 2 quarts Stock
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 oz. Cornflour
- 1 oz. Tapioca
- 1 cup of Green Peas
- Curry Powder
- ½ teaspoonful of Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a saucepan, slice up the onions and carrot and fry them in it with the herbs, peppercorns, and a good pinch of curry powder.
2. Mix the cornflour with a little stock and pour it over.
3. Slice up the tomatoes and add them with the boiling stock; stir until it boils, and then simmer slowly for an hour.
4. Rub through a sieve and return to the saucepan.
5. Add the salt, sugar, and the tapioca; stir until this becomes transparent and thickens the soup.
6. Put in a cupful of cold boiled peas; boil up and serve.
