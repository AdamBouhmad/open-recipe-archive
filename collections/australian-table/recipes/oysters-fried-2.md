## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- oysters
- nutmeg
- bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a batter of eggs, milk, and flour
2. season it with pepper and salt
3. dip the oysters into the batter, which ought to be rather thick
4. fry them of a light brown
5. add a little nutmeg and bread-crumbs to the seasoning
