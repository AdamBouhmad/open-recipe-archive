## Ingredients

- biscuit-powder
- milk
- water
- a small piece of butter
- yolk of an egg
- a little white sugar

## Directions

1. Make a stiff paste with biscuit-powder, milk, and water
2. Add a small piece of butter, the yolk of an egg, and a little white sugar
3. Cut it into pieces and mould with the hand
4. Bake in a crisp oven
