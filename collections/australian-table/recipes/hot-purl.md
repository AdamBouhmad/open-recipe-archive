## Ingredients

- a quart of mild ale
- a tablespoonful of grated nutmeg
- a little cold ale
- sugar to taste
- two eggs
- a quarter of a pint of whisky

## Directions

1. Put a quart of mild ale into a saucepan and add a tablespoonful of grated nutmeg
2. Place it over a slow fire until it nearly boils
3. Mix a little cold ale with sugar to taste and gradually two eggs well beaten
4. Add the hot ale, stirring one way to prevent curdling, and a quarter of a pint of whisky
5. Warm the whole again and pour it from one vessel into another until it becomes smooth
