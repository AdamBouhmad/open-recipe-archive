## Ingredients

- four ounces of ground rice
- two ounces of sugar
- a few drops of essence of lemon
- two ounces of butter
- a quart of milk

## Directions

1. Put into a stewpan four ounces of ground rice, two ounces of sugar, a few drops of essence of lemon, with two ounces of butter
2. Add a quart of milk
3. Boil for about twenty minutes, until it is smooth, but not too thick
4. Pour it into a mould, or basin, which has been rinsed with butter, to let it set and cool
