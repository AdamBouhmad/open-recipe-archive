## Ingredients

- 6 apples
- 1.5 oz. tapioca
- 1 lemon
- 2 oz. sugar
- 1.5 pints water
- A few drops of cochineal

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter the apples and remove the core, put them into a saucepan with the lemon juice, sugar, and a spoonful of water, and stew till soft but not broken.
2. Place them in a glass dish.
3. Wash the tapioca in cold water, put it in a saucepan, pour over it 1.5 pints of water, and stir till it boils.
4. Cook it till quite clear, sweeten and flavour with a few drops of lemon juice, and colour with cochineal.
5. Pour over the apples and put away till cold; it is then ready to serve.
