## Ingredients

- 4 slices Toast
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 pint White Sauce

## Directions

1. Make the toast and lay it in a dish.
2. Make the sauce by directions given for white sauce.
3. Season with pepper and salt, and pour over it; serve hot.
4. If a richer dish is desired, a little butter may be put on the toast.
