## Ingredients

- ox tongue
- cloves
- port wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel the outside skin off before putting the tongue down
2. Trim the root
3. Put before the fire in a cradle spit
4. Baste it well when being roasted
5. When done, stick cloves in the tongue, in the part uppermost in the dish
6. Make some gravy with port wine, rather thick
7. Add the juice of a lemon
8. Serve hot
