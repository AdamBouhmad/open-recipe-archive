## Ingredients

- leg of pork
- sage
- onion
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- salad oil
- apple sauce

## Directions

1. Pork must invariably be scored
2. The leg must be stuffed with sage and onion, mixed fine, and bread crumbs, with pepper and salt
3. Rub a little salad oil on the skin to crisp the crackling
4. Do not put the meat too near the fire at first
5. Roast for full three hours
6. Serve with apple sauce
