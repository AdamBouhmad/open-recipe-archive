## Ingredients

- four eggs
- three ounces of butter
- three ounces of cheese
- a gill of cream

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Mix well with the butter, cheese, and cream
3. Bake for twenty minutes in small shapes or in a dish
4. Serve hot
