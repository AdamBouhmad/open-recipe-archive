## Ingredients

- pound and a half of apples
- quart of water
- half an ounce of isinglass per pint of juice
- juice and peel of a lemon
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Pare and core a pound and a half of apples
2. Put them in a quart of water
3. Boil them till the apples are in pulp
4. Put them into a hair sieve to drain
5. To every pint of juice add half an ounce of isinglass, the juice and peel of a lemon, sugar to taste
6. Boil ten minutes
7. Pass it through a flannel bag into a mould
