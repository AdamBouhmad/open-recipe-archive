## Ingredients

- 2 heads of Celery
- 2 quarts Pot Boilings
- 1 pint of Milk
- 1 oz. Sago

## Directions

1. If vegetables have been boiled with the meat the stock will be sufficiently flavoured; if not, boil an onion and carrot in it and strain out
2. Wash the celery thoroughly and cut it into pieces one inch long
3. Put it into the boiling stock and boil for half an hour
4. Sprinkle in 1 oz. of sago and stir until it is transparent
5. Pour in the milk and bring to boiling point
