## Ingredients

- kangaroo meat
- milk
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- salt pork or bacon
- ketchup
- jelly

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in pieces of about a quarter of an inch square
2. Put it into a pan with a well-covered lid
3. Add a spoonful of milk, an onion shredded into small pieces, and some pepper and salt to taste
4. When it has been on the fire a short time add about a tenth in quantity of salt pork or bacon cut to the same size as the kangaroo
5. Add a spoonful of ketchup
6. Serve hot, with jelly
