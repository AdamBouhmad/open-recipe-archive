## Ingredients

- six baking apples
- a table-spoonful of cold water
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core six baking apples
2. put them into a saucepan, with a table-spoonful of cold water
3. let them simmer until done
4. add a little butter and sugar to sweeten them
