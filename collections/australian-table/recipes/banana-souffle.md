## Ingredients

- 6 Bananas
- 2 oz. Sugar
- 1/2 pint Milk
- 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Choose ripe bananas, peel and slice them up, and lay them in a glass dish
2. Sprinkle with sugar
3. Make a custard with the milk and yolks of the eggs by directions for boiled custard
4. Flavour with a pinch of ginger, and pour it over the bananas
5. Let it stand till quite cold
6. Whip the whites to a very stiff froth and heap them on top
7. Sprinkle with sugar, and serve
