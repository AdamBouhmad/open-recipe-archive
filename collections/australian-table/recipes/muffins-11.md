## Ingredients

- five eggs
- a quart of milk
- two ounces of butter
- a teaspoonful of salt
- two large table-spoonfuls of yeast
- enough flour to make a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and butter
2. Cover the mixture, and set it to rise in a warm place
3. When it has risen sufficiently grease your baking-iron and muffin rings
4. Bake them a light brown
5. Tear them asunder, and butter
