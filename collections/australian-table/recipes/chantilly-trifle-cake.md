## Ingredients

- a savoury cake
- half a pint of sherry wine
- whipped cream
- vanilla or lemon
- a little brandy and wine
- preserved fruits or blanched sweet almonds
- custard

## Directions

1. Take a savoury cake of the size required
2. Cut off the top and scoop out the inside to about three-quarters of an inch from the edge, having about the same thickness at the bottom
3. Put it on the dish on which it is to be served
4. Pour about half a pint of sherry wine over the sides and bottom of the cake
5. As it soaks in, pour it over until it has absorbed
6. Just before serving fill the cake with whipped cream flavoured with vanilla or lemon, or a little brandy and wine
7. Garnish the edge of the cake with preserved fruits or blanched sweet almonds cut in thin slices lengthways and stuck round
8. Custard may be substituted for the cream in filling the cake
