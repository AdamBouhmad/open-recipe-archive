## Ingredients

- pounded loaf sugar
- rasped orange-peel
- orange-juice

## Directions

1. Put a quantity of pounded loaf sugar, with some rasped orange-peel, into a saucepan
2. Add a quantity of orange-juice, to give it flavour
3. Dry the whole over the fire, until it is of a proper consistence
4. Drop the confection from the point of a knife on clean paper
