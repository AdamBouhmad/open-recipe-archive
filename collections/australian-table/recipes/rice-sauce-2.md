## Ingredients

- a little rice
- a pint of milk
- onion
- white pepper
- salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil a little rice in a pint of milk
2. add onion and white pepper and salt
3. Rub it through a tamis sieve, and, if necessary, thicken it with cream
