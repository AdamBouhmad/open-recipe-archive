## Ingredients

- mulberry jam
- Aldridge plum jam
- morella jam
- quince jam
- sugar
- a tablespoon of vinegar per pint

## Directions

1. In making jam — mulberry, Aldridge plum, morella, and quince — after the sugar has been added, skim off and strain the surplus syrup
2. Keep in a dry place till all are ready
3. Add a tablespoon of vinegar to every pint
