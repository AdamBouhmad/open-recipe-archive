## Ingredients

- beef
- flour
- stock
- onion
- ketchup
- pepper
- salt
- lemon acid

## Directions

1. Cut beef in pieces of about half an inch square
2. Flour them and fry until half done
3. Remove the collops and put them in a stewpan with a small quantity of stock
4. Add an onion cut up small, ketchup, pepper, and salt, with a dessert-spoonful of lemon acid
5. Let it simmer until done
