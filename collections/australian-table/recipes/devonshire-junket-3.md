## Ingredients

- lukewarm milk
- rennet
- clouted cream
- sugar
- cinnamon
- wine or brandy
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put lukewarm milk into a bowl, and turn it with rennet
2. then add some clouted cream, sugar, and cinnamon on the top, without breaking the curd
3. then drain the milk, and add a little wine or brandy
4. When turned, put sugar and nutmeg on the top, and serve
