## Ingredients

- beef shin
- beef leg bone
- beef neck
- carrots
- parsnips
- turnips
- leeks
- celery
- onion
- water

## Directions

1. Select the best pieces of beef: the shin, the leg bone, and the neck
2. Cut the meat in pieces and put in a digester with a sufficient quantity of water
3. Skim the meat sufficiently
4. Add carrots, parsnips, turnips, leeks, celery, and onion which have been browned in a pan
5. Simmer for four or five hours
6. Take out the meat and strain the stock for use
