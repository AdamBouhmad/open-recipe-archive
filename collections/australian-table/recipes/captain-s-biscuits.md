## Ingredients

- three pounds and a half of flour
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Mix them together till they make a hard, even dough
2. Cut it into pieces
3. Roll it into paste half an inch thick
4. Mould them into shape
5. Bake in a crisp oven
