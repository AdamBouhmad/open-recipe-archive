## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 6 or 8 oz. dripping
- 1 gill water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin, rub in the dripping very lightly until it is quite fine, mix into a very stiff dough with the water, turn on to a floured board, and knead into a smooth paste.
2. Roll out to the required thickness, and it is ready at once.
3. The dripping should be clarified, directions for which are given elsewhere.
