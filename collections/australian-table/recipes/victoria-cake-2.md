## Ingredients

- yolks of twelve eggs
- whites of six eggs
- one pound of white sugar
- juice of one lemon
- peel of two lemons
- twelve ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Whisk the yolks of twelve eggs, leaving out the whites of six, one pound of white sugar, the juice of one lemon, and the peel of two cut very fine together for half an hour
2. Add twelve ounces of flour
3. Put the cake in the oven and bake an hour
