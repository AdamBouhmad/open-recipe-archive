## Ingredients

- One pound of white sugar
- One pound of fresh butter
- A pound and a half of flour
- Half a gill of milk
- One egg beaten

## Directions

1. Rub together one pound of white sugar, one pound of fresh butter, and a pound and a half of flour
2. Mix it into a paste, with half a gill of milk and one egg beaten
3. Let it lie half an hour
4. Roll it out thin, cut it into cakes
5. Bake them in a moderate oven
