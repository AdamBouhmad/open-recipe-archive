## Ingredients

- a quart of gooseberries
- sufficient water
- six eggs
- a pint of cream or milk
- a table-spoonful of orange-flower water
- spice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch a quart of gooseberries, closely covered with sufficient water to pulp them
2. Beat six eggs, and add them to a pint of cream (or milk)
3. Add a table-spoonful of orange-flower water, spice and sugar to sweeten
4. Stir it over a fire till of a proper thickness
5. Dish, and sift powdered white sugar on the top
