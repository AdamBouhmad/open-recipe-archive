## Ingredients

- a bit of butter as big as an egg
- two or three onions
- carrots
- turnips
- a clove of garlic
- two shalots
- two cloves
- a bay leaf
- thyme and basil
- some flour
- a glass of red wine
- a glass of water
- a spoonful of vinegar
- a little pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Put a bit of butter as big as an egg into a stewpan with two or three onions, carrots, and turnips cut in slices, a clove of garlic, two shalots, two cloves, a bay leaf, thyme and basil
2. Keep turning them in the pan until they get a colour
3. Shake in some flour
4. Add a glass of red wine, a glass of water, and a spoonful of vinegar, with a little pepper and salt
5. Boil half an hour
6. Skim and strain it
