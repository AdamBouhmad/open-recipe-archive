## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Semolina
- 1 pint of Milk
- 3 pints Bone Stock
- Salt and Pepper
- onion
- carrot
- fagot of herbs
- dozen peppercorns

## Directions

1. If the stock has been made without vegetables, boil an onion, carrot, fagot of herbs, and a dozen peppercorns in it for half an hour
