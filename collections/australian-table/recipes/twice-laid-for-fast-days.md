## Ingredients

- any kind of fish, either fresh or salt
- mashed potatoes
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Mix fish with mashed potatoes, with a little butter, in the proportion of about one-fourth fish to three-fourths potatoes.
2. Brown before the fire.
