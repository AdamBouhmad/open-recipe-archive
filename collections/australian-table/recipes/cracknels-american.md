## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- half a nutmeg
- yolks of four eggs
- two spoonfuls of rose-water
- cold water
- one pound of butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with grated nutmeg
2. Beat the yolks of eggs with two spoonfuls of rose-water
3. Make into a stiff paste with sufficient cold water
4. Roll in a pound of butter
5. Make them into cracknel shapes
6. Put them into a kettle of boiling water
7. Boil them until they can swim
8. Take them out and place them in cold water
9. When hardened, lay them out to dry
10. Bake on tin plates
