## Ingredients

- two pounds of bitter almonds
- a gallon of brandy
- two pounds of white sugar
- a little spice

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound two pounds of bitter almonds, and put to them a gallon of brandy, with two pounds of white sugar and a little spice
2. Stir it, and let the spirit remain in the almonds for a month, when the noyeau may be bottled for use
