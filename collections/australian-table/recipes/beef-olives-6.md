## Ingredients

- slices of beef
- forcemeat of crumbs of bread
- shalot
- suet or dripping
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut slices of beef of about a quarter of an inch thick
2. Lay on each piece a forcemeat of crumbs of bread, shalot, a little suet or dripping, pepper, and salt
3. Roll each piece separate, and fasten with a skewer
4. Put them into a stew-pan, with some gravy, and stew till tender
5. Serve with slices of lemon
