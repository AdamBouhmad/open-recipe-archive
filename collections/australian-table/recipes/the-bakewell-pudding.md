## Ingredients

- thin puff paste
- any kind of jam
- eight egg yolks
- ten egg whites
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter melted
- almond flavour to taste

## Directions

1. Cover a dish with thin puff paste
2. put a layer of any kind of jam, about half an inch thick
3. take the yolks of eight eggs and ten whites, half a pound of sugar, half a pound of butter melted, and almond flavour to taste, beat all well together
4. pour the mixture into a dish, an inch thick
5. bake an hour in a moderate oven
