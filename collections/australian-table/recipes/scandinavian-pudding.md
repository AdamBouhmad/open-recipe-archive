## Ingredients

- 2 bream
- 2 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 1 lb. flour
- pepper
- salt
- 1 pint fish sauce
- butter
- melted butter sauce
- anchovy

## Directions

1. Fillet the fish, skin and chop very fine
2. Sift the flour into a basin
3. Drop in the eggs and make into a batter with the milk
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Stir in the chopped fish
6. Butter a basin and pour in the mixture
7. Boil for one hour
8. Turn out of the basin and serve with melted butter sauce, flavoured with anchovy, or with any other fish-sauce that may be preferred
