## Ingredients

- 1 Bone of Mutton or Ham
- 1 Onion
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 pint Water or Stock
- 1 lb. Potatoes
- Salt
- 2 Cloves

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a saucepan, and when it is hot put in the flour; mix together smoothly, pour in the water or stock, and stir until it boils.
2. Joint up a mutton or ham bone and lay it in; if it is mutton, add a little salt.
3. Bring it to the boil, put in the onion whole stuck with two cloves, and simmer for half an hour or longer.
4. Peel the potatoes, cut them in half and put them in, and cook until they are soft.
5. Take out the bones and place on a dish, put the potatoes round, and pour the sauce over.
