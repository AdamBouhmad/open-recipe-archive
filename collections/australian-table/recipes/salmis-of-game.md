## Ingredients

- under-done partridges
- butter
- ham
- shalots
- dried carrot
- mushrooms
- parsley
- thyme
- bay-leaf
- cloves
- peppercorns
- allspice
- sherry
- brown gravy
- mushroom ketchup
- pepper
- cayenne
- sugar
- fried bread sippets

## Directions

1. Take off the legs, wings, and breasts of as many under-done partridges as you intend to use
2. Remove the skin from them very carefully
3. Put into a stewpan, with a bit of butter, a few slices of ham, four or five shalots, a dried carrot, a few mushrooms, a little parsley, sprig of thyme, a bay-leaf, two cloves, three or four peppercorns, and some allspice
4. Fry the vegetables slightly
5. Add two wine-glassfuls of sherry, four or five spoonfuls of brown gravy, and a spoonful of mushroom ketchup
6. Add the parts of the bird not intended to be eaten, and let them simmer for a couple of hours
7. Season with pepper and cayenne
8. Skim off the fat and add a lump of sugar
9. When the sugar is dissolved, strain this gravy over the wings, legs, and the breast of the partridge
10. Put the partridge parts into a clean saucepan
11. Let it simmer by the fire until the meat is saturated with the sauce
12. Serve with fried bread sippets
