## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- a little salt
- two ounces of butter
- one ounce of sugar
- two eggs, well beaten up
- half a pint of milk, lukewarm
- two table-spoonfuls of yeast

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt, butter, sugar, eggs, milk, and yeast
2. Let the dough rise
3. Make it into six rolls
4. Set them to rise again
5. Bake in tins
