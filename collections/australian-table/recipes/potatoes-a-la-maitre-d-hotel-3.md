## Ingredients

- Cold Potatoes
- 1/2 pint Maitre d'Hotel Sauce
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make the sauce by recipe given elsewhere, flavour nicely with lemon juice, pepper, and salt.
2. Slice up the potatoes, and put them into it; simmer for five minutes, dish, and serve.
