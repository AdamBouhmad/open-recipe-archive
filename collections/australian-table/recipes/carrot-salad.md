## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 Cold Boiled Carrots
- 1/4 lb. Cold Boiled Mutton
- 1 stalk Celery
- 6 Capers
- Half a teaspoonful Parsley
- Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Cut up some cold boiled mutton into small pieces and lay them in a salad bowl.
2. Mince up the celery and capers and strew over it.
3. Pour over the dressing.
4. Slice up the cold carrots and lay them on top.
5. Garnish with the chopped parsley, and serve.
