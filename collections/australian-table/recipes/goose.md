## Ingredients

- goose
- onion
- sage
- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- liver
- yolk of an egg
- apple sauce
- lemons

## Directions

1. Roast goose
2. Stuff it well with onion, sage, bread-crumbs, pepper, salt, and a little cayenne; with the liver, and the yolk of an egg
3. Ensure it is well done
4. Serve up with apple sauce
5. Garnish the dish with cut lemons
