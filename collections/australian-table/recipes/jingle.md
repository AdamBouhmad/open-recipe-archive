## Ingredients

- a few apples
- nutmeg
- sugar to taste
- some slices of toasted bread
- some ale

## Directions

1. Roast a few apples and grate some nutmeg on them with sugar to taste
2. Place them in a jug with some slices of toasted bread
3. Heat some ale and fill the jug
