## Ingredients

- cold vegetables such as potatoes, cauliflowers, peas, beans, haricots
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take any cold vegetables that there may be in the larder
2. Slice up the potatoes, branch the cauliflower, and mix in the peas and beans
3. Put all into a salad bowl
4. Take oil and vinegar in the proportion of one of oil to two of vinegar, blend them together and season with salt and pepper
5. Pour this over the vegetables
6. Slice up one or two hard boiled eggs into very thin slices, and lay round as a garnish
