## Ingredients

- best white sugar, five pounds
- lemon-juice, a quarter of a pint
- bruised ginger, five ounces
- honey, a quarter of a pound
- water, four gallons and a half
- white of an egg
- essence of lemon, a dozen drops

## Directions

1. Boil the ginger in three quarts of the water for half an hour
2. Add the sugar, lemon-juice, and honey, with the remainder of the water
3. Strain through a cloth or strainer
4. When cold add the white of an egg, and a small quantity of essence of lemon
5. Let the whole stand four days, and then bottle
