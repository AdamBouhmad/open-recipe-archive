## Ingredients

- two cups of Indian corn meal
- one cup of flour
- two eggs
- large spoonful of melted butter
- two small tea-spoons of cream of tartar
- one tea-spoon of soda
- one tea-spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix
2. Bake
