## Ingredients

- leaves of sorrel
- leeks
- lettuce
- purslain
- chervil
- about two handfuls of each
- salt
- some fresh butter
- three pints of water
- the yolks of two eggs
- a spoonful of cream

## Directions

1. Take leaves of sorrel, leeks, lettuce, purslain, and chervil, about two handfuls of each.
2. Pick and wash them.
3. Add salt, some fresh butter.
4. Boil together in three pints of water till it is reduced to half.
5. Take it off and strain it.
6. If you like you can add the yolks of two eggs, beaten with a spoonful of cream.
