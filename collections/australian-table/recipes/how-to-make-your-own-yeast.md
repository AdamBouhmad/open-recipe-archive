## Ingredients

- two ounces of the best hops
- four quarts of water
- a small handful of salt
- half a pound of brown sugar
- one pound of the best flour
- three pounds of potatoes

## Directions

1. Monday morning boil two ounces of the best hops in four quarts of water for half an hour
2. Strain it, and let the liquor cool down to new milk warmth
3. Put in a small handful of salt and half a pound of brown sugar
4. Beat up one pound of the best flour with some of the liquor, and then mix all together
5. On Wednesday add three pounds of potatoes boiled and then mashed, to stand till Thursday
6. Strain it and put it into bottles, and it is ready for use
7. Stir frequently while it is making, and keep near the fire
8. Before using shake the bottle well up
