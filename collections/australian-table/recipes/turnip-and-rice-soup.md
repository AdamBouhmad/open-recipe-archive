## Ingredients

- 4 Turnips
- 1/2 lb. Rice
- 2 quarts Water
- 1 pint Milk
- Onion and Salt
- 1 doz. white peppercorns

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the turnips
2. Wash the rice and put into a saucepan with the onion and 1 doz. white peppercorns
3. Pour over the water and boil for an hour
4. Rub through a sieve and return to the saucepan
5. Add the milk and a seasoning of salt and pepper
6. Stir until it boils
7. Pour into a warm tureen and sprinkle some chopped parsley on top
8. This soup is much improved by putting one ounce of butter into the water in which the rice and turnips are boiled
