## Ingredients

- One ounce and a half of gentian-root
- One ounce and a half of lemon-peel
- One ounce and a half of orange-peel
- One quart of sherry

## Directions

1. Steep these ingredients for about a month in a quart of sherry
2. Strain and bottle for use
