## Ingredients

- beef
- spring water
- powdered saltpetre
- common salt
- a quarter of a pound of allspice
- two ounces of pepper

## Directions

1. Put the beef into spring water and allow it to remain there a few hours
2. Take it out and drain well
3. Rub a few ounces of powdered saltpetre well into the meat
4. Repeat this two or three times
5. Rub in common salt several times for the next twenty-four hours
6. Rub in a mixture of a quarter of a pound of allspice and two ounces of pepper for another twenty-four hours
7. Let the beef remain ten days in the pickle, turned every day
8. Bake it in a pan for four hours, covered with crust, and round the bottom of the baking-pan
