## Ingredients

- three eggs, well beaten
- a tablespoonful of milk
- a spoonful of sugar
- a little salt
- two ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Mix the well beaten eggs with milk, sugar, and salt
2. Put two ounces of butter into the frying-pan
3. When it boils pour in the batter and fry
4. Fry of a light brown
5. Serve with sifted sugar over them
