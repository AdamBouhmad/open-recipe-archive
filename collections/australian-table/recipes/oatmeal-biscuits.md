## Ingredients

- one pound of the finest oatmeal
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of loaf sugar
- two eggs
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pound the sugar
2. Mix all the ingredients into a stiff paste with cold water
3. The eggs are not to be beaten for these biscuits
4. Make the paste into small balls
5. Flatten them
6. Bake on tins
