## Ingredients

- pound of beef
- pepper
- salt
- couple of carrots
- four cloves of garlic
- quarter of a pound of ham
- little water
- flour
- quart of stock
- bunch of sweet herbs
- little spice
- slice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut a pound of beef into small pieces and pepper and salt it
2. Put it into a stewpan with carrots and sliced garlic cloves, a quarter pound of ham, and a little water
3. Put it over a slow fire and turn the meat when it sticks
4. When browned, dredge it with flour
5. Add a quart of stock, a bunch of sweet herbs, a little spice, and the slice of a lemon
6. Let it simmer for an hour
7. Take off the fat and strain
