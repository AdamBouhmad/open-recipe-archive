## Ingredients

- small piece of rump of beef or under-cut of sirloin
- three pints of vinegar
- two ounces of caraway-seeds
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Place meat in a deep pan with three pints of vinegar, two ounces of caraway-seeds tied in a muslin bag, salt, pepper, and spices
2. Cover it down tight
3. Bake thoroughly in a slow oven
