## Ingredients

- about a dozen sprigs of tender shoots of mint
- a spoonful of white sugar
- equal proportions of peach and common brandy
- rasped or pounded ice
- fresh pineapple

## Directions

1. Put into a tumbler about a dozen sprigs of the tender shoots of mint
2. Upon them put a spoonful of white sugar
3. Add equal proportions of peach and common brandy, so as to fill up one-third, or perhaps a little less
4. Take rasped or pounded ice, and fill up the tumbler
5. Rub the lips of the tumbler with a piece of fresh pineapple
6. As the ice melts you drink
