## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- grated rind of a lemon
- a little cinnamon
- flour
- spice
- eggs
- candied orange or lemon peel

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of butter and sugar, say half a pound of each.
2. Grate the rind of a lemon, add a little cinnamon, and as much flour as will form it into a paste, with spice and eggs.
3. Roll it out.
4. Cut it into small cakes.
5. Bake.
6. A piece of candied orange or lemon peel may be put on the top of each cake.
