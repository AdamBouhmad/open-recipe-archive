## Ingredients

- 4 Onions
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1½ oz. Flour
- 1 gill of Milk
- 2 quarts of Stock
- Salt and Pepper
- lemon juice
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the onions and fry them in the butter till they are a good brown colour
2. Sprinkle over the flour and brown that too
3. Pour on the boiling stock and boil steadily till the onions are very soft, then rub through a sieve
4. If there is any fat on it remove it carefully
5. Pour back into the saucepan, add the milk, pepper, and salt, and boil up
6. Just before serving put in a few drops of lemon juice
7. Send fried bread to table with it
