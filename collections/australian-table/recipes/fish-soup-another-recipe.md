## Ingredients

- five pounds of fish, assorted kinds
- water or weak broth, about a quart
- cayenne
- common pepper
- salt
- a bundle of sweet herbs
- parsley
- a couple of onions
- flour
- butter
- ketchup
- soy
- sauce
- macaroni or vermicelli

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces, and fry until half done
2. Place them in a boiler, in about a quart of water or weak broth, having first seasoned the pieces of fish with cayenne, common pepper, and salt, a bundle of sweet herbs, and parsley
3. Let the whole stew for about three hours, so that all the goodness may be extracted from the fish
4. Whilst stewing, fry a couple of onions, well coated with flour, in the fat the fish was previously fried in, then put them into the soup
5. Thicken it with a little flour and butter, and ketchup, soy, and sauce
6. Strain the contents through a hair sieve, and, when cold, take off the fat
7. When wanted for use, warm it up, and add macaroni or vermicelli
