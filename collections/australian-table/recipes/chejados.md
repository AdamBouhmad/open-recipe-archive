## Ingredients

- a pound of sugar
- half a pint of water
- a moderately-sized cocoa-nut
- rose-water
- the beaten yolks of six eggs
- thin nouilles pastry

## Directions

1. Clarify a pound of sugar in half a pint of water
2. Peel and grate a moderately-sized cocoa-nut, add it to the syrup
3. Let it simmer till perfectly soft, putting rose-water occasionally to prevent its becoming too dry
4. Stir it to prevent burning
5. Let it cool, and mix it with the beaten yolks of six eggs
6. Make a thin nouilles pastry, cut it into rounds the size of a tea-cup
7. Pinch up the edges deep enough to form a shape
8. Fill them with the sweetmeat, and bake of a light brown
