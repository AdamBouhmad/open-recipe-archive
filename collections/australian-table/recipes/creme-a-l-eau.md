## Ingredients

- rind of a lemon
- sugar
- a pint of water
- yolks of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Grate a rind of a lemon, and add to it, with sugar, a pint of water
2. boil, and then add the yolks of eight eggs, well beaten
3. when cool, put in a bowl in a saucepan of hot water, till done enough
4. pour into the dish on which you serve it, garnished with slices of lemon
