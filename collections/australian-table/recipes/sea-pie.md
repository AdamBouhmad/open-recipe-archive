## Ingredients

- thick crust
- sliced onions
- salt beef
- potatoes
- pork
- pepper
- cloth
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a thick crust, as for a pudding
2. Line a dish with it
3. Put in a layer of sliced onions
4. Add a layer of salt beef cut in slices
5. Add a layer of potatoes sliced
6. Add a layer of pork
7. Add another layer of onions
8. Strew pepper over all
9. Cover with the crust
10. Tie with a cloth, well floured, so that it will not stick when done
11. Boil for a couple of hours
12. Serve
