## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of butter
- one pint of new milk
- three-quarters of a pound of white sugar
- half a dessert-spoonful of yeast
- one whole dessert-spoonful of caraway seeds
- flour

## Directions

1. Melt butter in new milk
2. Pour it on white sugar
3. When cool, put in half a dessert-spoonful of yeast and a whole one of caraway seeds
4. Add flour sufficient to make it into a stiff paste to roll thin
5. Prick and cut into shapes
6. Bake them
