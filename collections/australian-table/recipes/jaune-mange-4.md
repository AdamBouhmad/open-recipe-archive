## Ingredients

- ounce of isinglass
- half a pint of boiling water
- yolks of six eggs
- half a pint of sherry
- juice of a lemon
- sugar to taste
- peel of the lemon

## Directions

1. Dissolve an ounce of isinglass in half a pint of boiling water
2. Beat the yolks of six eggs, and mix them with half a pint of sherry, the juice of a lemon, and sugar to taste
3. Rub the peel of the lemon on some of the sugar to extract the flavour
4. Stir all well together, and boil ten minutes
5. Strain, and put in a mould for shape
