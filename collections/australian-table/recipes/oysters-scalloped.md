## Ingredients

- oysters
- shells
- butter
- bread-crumbs
- mace

## Directions

1. Open the shells carefully, and let the oysters remain in the scallop-shell
2. put some butter, bread-crumbs, and a little mace into the shells
3. brown in a Dutch oven before the fire
4. Send them to table hot
