## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. Flour
- 1/4 lb. Bread Crumbs
- 1/4 lb. Raisins
- 6 oz. Suet
- 1/4 lb. Sultanas
- 1/4 lb. Sugar
- 2 Apples

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and bread crumbs together
2. Shred the suet finely and mix it in with the sugar
3. Stone the raisins and pull them in half
4. Clean the sultanas and mix these in
5. Peel and core the apples
6. Put in the pips, chop the apples finely, and add them
7. Let it stand for an hour and then mix it into a paste
8. Press into a basin
9. Tie down tightly and boil at least six hours
