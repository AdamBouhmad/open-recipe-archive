## Ingredients

- bones
- paste

## Directions

1. Let the bones be sawn even
2. Put paste at the ends, and boil them until they are done, which will require nearly two hours
3. Serve on toasted bread, and send up a silver marrow-spoon and napkin for the helper to use
4. The dish must be sent up hot; cold marrow is decidedly objectionable
