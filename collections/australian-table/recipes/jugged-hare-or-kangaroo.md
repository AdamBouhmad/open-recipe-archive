## Ingredients

- hare or kangaroo
- pound of bacon
- flour
- pepper
- allspice
- onions
- winter savory
- pint of stock
- currant jelly
- sharp sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the hare or kangaroo into pieces
2. Cut up a pound of bacon into small squares, and fry it in a pan for five minutes
3. Add the hare or kangaroo, and stir them in a stewpan until a little brown
4. Add some flour, pepper, allspice, onions, and a little winter savory
5. Moisten the stew with a pint of stock, and allow it to simmer for half an hour
6. Serve with currant jelly and sharp sauce
