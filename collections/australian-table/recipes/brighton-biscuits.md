## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one drachm of carbonate of ammonia in fine powder
- four ounces of white sugar
- one ounce of arrowroot
- four ounces of butter
- one egg
- new milk

## Directions

1. Mix together flour, carbonate of ammonia, sugar, arrowroot, butter, and egg
2. Add new milk so as to form a stiff paste
3. Beat the mass with a rolling-pin
4. Roll it out
5. Bake
