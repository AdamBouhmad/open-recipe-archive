## Ingredients

- a quart of cream
- cinnamon or mace
- half a pound of rice flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scald a quart of cream with a little cinnamon or mace
2. Mix this gradually into half a pound of rice flour
3. Stir it over a gentle fire until it is of the thickness of jelly
4. Sweeten it to taste
5. Pour it into cups or shapes
6. Turn it out when cold, and serve up
