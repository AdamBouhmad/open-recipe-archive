## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- two pounds of flour
- half a pint of warm cream
- a quarter of a pint of good yeast
- grated nutmeg
- mace
- cloves
- a quarter of an ounce of caraway-seeds
- a quarter of a pound of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Rub a quarter of a pound of fresh butter into two pounds of flour till none of it is visible
2. Add half a pint of warm cream and a quarter of a pint of good yeast
3. Mix the whole into a light paste, and set it to rise
4. When the dough has risen, work in the grated nutmeg, mace, cloves, caraway-seeds, and fine sugar
5. Roll the paste out thin, and divide into cakes
6. Put the cakes on tins to rise, and then bake
