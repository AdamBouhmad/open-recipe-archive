## Ingredients

- the whites of eight eggs
- a pint and a half of milk
- a bit of lemon-peel
- sugar
- the yolks of eight eggs
- a table-spoonful of orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Whip the whites of eight eggs until they form a very thick froth
2. Put a pint and a half of milk on to boil, and when it boils place on its surface as many table-spoonfuls of the whipped whites of eggs as will stand on it without touching each other
3. As each spoonful becomes cooked and assumes the appearance of snow, take it off and put on another, until all the whipped eggs are done
4. As you take off the snow from the milk, put it on a hair sieve to drain
5. Add to the milk a bit of lemon-peel, and sugar enough to sweeten it well
6. As soon as it has acquired the flavour of the lemon-peel, stir into it the yolks of eight eggs beaten up with a table-spoonful of orange-flower water
7. When of proper consistency, but not so thick as cream, pour it into a cream-dish
8. After it is cold, put the snow on the surface to make the sauce
