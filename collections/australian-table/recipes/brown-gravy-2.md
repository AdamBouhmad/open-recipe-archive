## Ingredients

- six or eight onions
- ounce of butter
- half an ounce of flour
- stock
- pepper
- salt
- tablespoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut onions into a stewpan with an ounce of butter
2. Set it on a slow fire and turn the onions until lightly browned
3. Stir in gradually half an ounce of flour with a little stock, pepper and salt
4. Simmer for a short time
5. Add a tablespoonful of ketchup
