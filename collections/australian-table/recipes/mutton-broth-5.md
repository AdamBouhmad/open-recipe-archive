## Ingredients

- two pounds of mutton
- sufficient cold water
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a small quantity of pepper
- a table-spoonful of the best grits
- an onion
- three turnips

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of mutton, any part; put it into a stewpan, with sufficient cold water, a tea-spoonful of salt, a small quantity of pepper, a table-spoonful of the best grits, and an onion.
2. Let it simmer, and skim it often.
3. Put into it three turnips and an onion, let it simmer for a couple of hours.
4. It can be thickened with oatmeal, rice, or Scotch barley.
