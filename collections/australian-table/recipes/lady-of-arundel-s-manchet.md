## Ingredients

- a bushel of white wheat flour
- twenty eggs
- three pounds of fresh butter
- salt
- barm
- new milk

## Directions

1. Take a bushel of white wheat flour, twenty eggs, three pounds of fresh butter
2. Take as much salt and barm as the ordinary manchet
3. Temper it together with new milk pretty hot
4. Let it lie the space of half an hour to rise
5. Work it into bread, and bake it
6. Let not your oven be too hot
