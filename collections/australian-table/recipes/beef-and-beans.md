## Ingredients

- piece of brisket of beef
- water
- one quartern of fresh beans
- two onions
- pepper
- salt
- dessert-spoonful of flour
- coarse brown sugar
- large tea-cupful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cover beef with water
2. When boiling, skim off the fat
3. Add one quartern of fresh beans cut small and two onions cut in quarters
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. When nearly done, take a dessert-spoonful of flour, one of coarse brown sugar, and a large tea-cupful of vinegar
6. Mix them together and stir in with the beans
7. Continue stirring for about half an hour longer
