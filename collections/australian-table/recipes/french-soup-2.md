## Ingredients

- 3 Potatoes
- 3 Carrots
- 2 Turnips
- 2 quarts Bone Stock
- Pepper
- 2 Onions
- 1/2 stalk Celery
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 teaspoonful Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the vegetables and sprinkle them with the sugar and salt
2. Put them into a saucepan with the butter, and sweat for five minutes
3. Pour over the boiling stock and stir until it boils
4. Boil slowly for an hour
5. Rub through a sieve
6. If it is too thick, reduce it with a little more stock or milk
7. Return to a saucepan, and bring to the boil
8. When tomatoes are in season slice up two with the other vegetables; these will make the soup a good colour and improve the flavour
