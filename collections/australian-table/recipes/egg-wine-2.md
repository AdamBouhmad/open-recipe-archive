## Ingredients

- an egg
- a tablespoonful of water
- a wine-glass of wine
- half a glass of water
- sugar and nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Beat up an egg and mix it with a tablespoonful of water
2. Put a wine-glass of wine, half a glass of water, and sugar and nutmeg to taste into a small saucepan and place over a slow fire
3. When it boils add it gradually to the egg, stirring well
4. Return the whole to the saucepan and place over the fire again and stir for a minute
5. Remove and serve with toast. If it boils a second time, it will curdle
