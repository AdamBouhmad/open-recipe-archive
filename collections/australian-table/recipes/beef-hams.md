## Ingredients

- a leg of beef
- saltpetre
- salt
- an ounce of bay-salt
- an ounce of saltpetre
- a pound of coarse brown sugar
- a pound of common salt
- bran or sawdust

## Directions

1. Select a leg of beef and rub it well with saltpetre and salt
2. Make a pickle of an ounce of bay-salt, an ounce of saltpetre, a pound of coarse brown sugar, and a pound of common salt
3. Rub this well in every day for a week
4. Rub it every other day for about three weeks
5. Roll it in bran or sawdust and smoke
