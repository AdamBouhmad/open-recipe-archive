## Ingredients

- three table-spoonfuls of flour
- two table-spoonfuls of finely-minced beef suet
- two table-spoonfuls of currants
- a little salt
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Mix flour, suet, and currants with salt and as much milk or water as will make a batter of the ingredients.
2. Tie in a well-floured cloth and boil for an hour.
3. Serve plain or with sweet sauce.
