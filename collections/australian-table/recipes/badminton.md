## Ingredients

- half a moderate-sized cucumber
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- a little nutmeg
- a bottle of claret
- a bottle of soda-water

## Directions

1. Peel half a moderate-sized cucumber, and put it into a mug, silver or crockery
2. with four ounces of powdered sugar, a little nutmeg, and a bottle of claret
3. When the sugar is dissolved, add a bottle of soda-water, and it is then fit for use
