## Ingredients

- one handful of hops
- three quarts of water
- a pint of flour
- a tea-cupful of moist sugar
- a tea-cupful of yeast

## Directions

1. Put one handful of hops to three quarts of water, and let it boil two hours
2. Strain the hops away, mixing a pint of flour with the liquor
3. While hot add a tea-cupful of moist sugar
4. Let it stand and get luke-warm
5. Work it with a tea-cupful of yeast, stirring it often
6. Let it stand one day, and then put it into jars for use
