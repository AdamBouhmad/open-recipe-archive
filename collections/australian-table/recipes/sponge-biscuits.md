## Ingredients

- whites and yolks of eight eggs
- a pound of finely-pounded sugar
- a pound of fine flour
- rind of two lemons

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of eggs well
2. Add to finely-pounded sugar
3. Add a pound of fine flour and the rind of two lemons grated
4. Put it into shapes
5. Sift some white sugar over them
6. Bake in tin moulds for some time
