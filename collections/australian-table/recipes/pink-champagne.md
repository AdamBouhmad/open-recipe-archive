## Ingredients

- nine pounds of loaf sugar
- three gallons of water
- a gallon of red and white currants
- a tea-cupful of yeast
- isinglass or white of egg

## Directions

1. Dissolve nine pounds of loaf sugar over the fire in three gallons of water, remove the scum
2. Have ready picked a gallon of red and white currants, and pour over them the boiling liquor
3. Let it stand until cool, then add a tea-cupful of yeast
4. Allow it to ferment for two days, then put it in a cask, and add some isinglass or white of egg to clear
5. Bung up the cask, but don't do this too soon, or there will be an explosion
6. When ready for bottling add a small lump of sugar to each bottle
