## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- two ounces of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- one ounce of carraway seeds
- half an ounce of ground coriander seed
- half a tea-spoonful of carbonate of soda
- a table-spoonful of arrowroot
- warm milk

## Directions

1. Mix the whole together
2. Make a stiff paste with warm milk
3. Cut into thin cakes
4. Prick with a fork
5. Bake
