## Ingredients

- short paste
- hashed meat or forcemeat
- very hot fat

## Directions

1. Take short paste and roll very thin
2. Cut it into small round pieces
3. Put into them some hashed meat or forcemeat
4. Gather up the paste over it and moisten the edges
5. Press them securely together
6. Fry them in a piece of very hot fat
7. Drain them and serve them hot
