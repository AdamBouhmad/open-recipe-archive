## Ingredients

- turbot
- salt
- cold water
- red spawn of the lobster
- parsley
- lemon
- horseradish
- fried smelts
- lobster or crayfish sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the fish in cold water a couple of hours before you dress it, with some salt in it
2. Score the skin across the back
3. Put a handful of salt into a fish-kettle with cold water
4. Lay your fish on a strainer
5. When coming to the boil, skim it well
6. Set the kettle on the side of the fire to simmer for about fifteen minutes
7. Rub some of the red spawn of the lobster through a sieve, and sprinkle over
8. Garnish with parsley, lemon, and horseradish; or it may be served with fried smelts
9. Serve with lobster or crayfish sauce
