## Ingredients

- a leg of mutton
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- onion
- thyme
- a bay-leaf
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bone the leg of mutton and put it into a pan
2. Pour over it a marinade made of oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, garlic, onion, thyme, a bay-leaf, and parsley
3. Let it marinade three days
4. Roast it or stew it
