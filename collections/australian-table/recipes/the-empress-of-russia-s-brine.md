## Ingredients

- Six pounds of common salt
- Two pounds of powdered loaf sugar
- Three ounces of saltpetre
- Three gallons of water

## Directions

1. Boil together over a slow fire
2. Skim it while boiling
3. When cold, pour it over the meat, ensuring every part is covered with the brine
4. Small pork will be sufficiently cured in four or five days, and hams intended for drying in two weeks
5. This pickle may be used frequently if boiled up, skimmed, and more ingredients used
6. Before putting the meat into the water, wash it well and dry it
