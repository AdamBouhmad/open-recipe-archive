## Ingredients

- common ship's biscuit or captain's biscuit
- lukewarm water
- butter
- cayenne
- anchovy or herring paste

## Directions

1. Soak the biscuit for a quarter of an hour in lukewarm water
2. Toast them and butter well
3. Add cayenne and anchovy or herring paste
4. Hold before the fire in a cheese-toaster for a few minutes
