## Ingredients

- 1 slice of Dry Bread
- 2 Eggs
- 1 oz. Sugar
- Half a Lemon
- 1 1/2 pints of Milk
- 1 tablespoonful Jam
- 1/2 tablespoonful Cornflour

## Directions

1. Take a piece of very stale bread and cut it into small squares
2. Bake it in the oven till a good colour
3. Break the eggs into a pie-dish
4. Beat in the sugar and grated rind of the lemon
5. Pour in one pint of milk, and mix well
6. Drop in the rusks and put into a cool oven and bake till firm
7. Spread on the top a layer of jam
8. Put half a pint of milk into a saucepan, and when it nearly boils, stir in the cornflour which has been mixed with a little lemon peel and sugar
9. Pour it on top of the pudding
10. Put it back in the oven for a few minutes
11. Stand away till cold
