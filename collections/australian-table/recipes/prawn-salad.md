## Ingredients

- 1 pint Prawns
- 6 Tomatoes
- Mayonnaise or Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Pick the prawns, leaving the skin on a few fine ones for a garnish.
2. Peel and slice up the tomatoes and arrange them on a dish; put over them the prawns, and pour over all some mayonnaise or salad dressing.
3. Place the other prawns round as a garnish with a few lettuce leaves broken up.
