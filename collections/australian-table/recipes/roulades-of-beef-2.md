## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. Fillet of Beef
- 1/2 lb. Cold Boiled Bacon
- 1 Egg
- 1 1/2 oz. Dripping
- 1/2 pint Gravy
- Pepper and Mustard
- 2 oz. Crumbs

## Directions

1. Trim away the fat from the fillet and cut it into very small thin slices.
2. Cut the bacon also into thin slices, but smaller.
3. Spread the side of the beef with mustard and pepper.
4. Cover with the bacon, and roll up as lightly as possible.
5. When all are rolled beat up an egg, mix it with a spoonful of water.
6. Brush over the rolls.
7. Cover them with crumbs and thread on a small skewer.
8. Put the dripping into a frying-pan, and when quite hot lay in the rolls and fry until a good colour.
9. Place on a hot dish and slip out the skewers.
10. Make the gravy hot, season and flavour, and pour boiling round the roulades.
