## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Tripe
- 1 pint Milk
- Pepper and Salt
- 2 Onions
- 1 oz. Flour
- ½ pint Water

## Directions

1. Prepare the tripe as in the preceding recipe.
2. Mix the milk and water together, pour it into a saucepan.
3. Lay in the tripe.
