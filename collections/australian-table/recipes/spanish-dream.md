## Ingredients

- half a pint of water
- an ounce of isinglass
- a quart of cream or good milk
- the beaten yolks of six eggs
- a glass of white wine
- loaf sugar
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Boil in half a pint of water an ounce of isinglass till dissolved
2. Strain, and mix with it a quart of cream or good milk (if cream, do not use so much isinglass)
3. Stir it over the fire till it comes to a boil
4. When a little cooled, add gradually the beaten yolks of six eggs and a glass of white wine
5. Pour it into a deep dish
6. Sweeten with loaf sugar
7. Stir it till cold
8. Put it into a shape
9. It may be flavoured with the peel of a lemon instead of the wine
