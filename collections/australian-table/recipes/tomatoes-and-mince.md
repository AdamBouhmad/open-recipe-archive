## Ingredients

- 8 Tomatoes
- 8 pieces Toast
- 1/2 lb. Minced Meat
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Take any remains of cold mince or hash, add more flavouring if necessary, and make it hot in the saucepan.
2. Wipe the tomatoes and scrape out the centre, fill it up with the mince, and stand in the oven for ten minutes.
3. Have ready some rounds of toast about the same size as the tomatoes.
4. When the tomatoes are cooked enough, stand them on the toast, and serve.
