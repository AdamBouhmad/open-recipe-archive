## Ingredients

- apples, pears, or quinces
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Peel and core them, and cut them in thin slices
2. Dry them in the sun
3. When used in pies or puddings, soak them sufficiently in lukewarm water, and the flavour of the apple will be found excellent
