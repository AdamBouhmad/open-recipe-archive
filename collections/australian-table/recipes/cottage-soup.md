## Ingredients

- six quarts of water
- one pound of bacon
- a proportionate quantity of carrots
- turnips
- cabbages
- leeks
- onions
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Fill an earthen pot or saucepan with six quarts of water
2. Add one pound of bacon with a proportionate quantity of carrots, turnips, cabbages, leeks, and onions
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Simmer for five or six hours
