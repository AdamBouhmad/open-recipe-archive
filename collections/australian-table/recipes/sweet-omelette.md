## Ingredients

- the yolks and whites of three eggs
- a little ginger
- salt
- a little cream or new milk

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites of eggs separately
2. Grate on them a little ginger with salt
3. Add a little cream or new milk
4. Fry the omelette
5. Serve with white sugar
