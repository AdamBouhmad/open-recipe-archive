## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Fish
- 1 lb. Boiled Rice
- 2 Hard Boiled Eggs
- 1 oz. Butter
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Flake up the fish and mix it with the rice
2. Shell the eggs and cut them in half, put the yolks on one side
3. Chop the whites and mix them with the rice and fish
4. Season nicely and put into a saucepan with the butter, and stir until thoroughly hot
5. Pile on a dish, and either chop the yolks and sprinkle them over, or hold a sieve over the kedgeree and rub them lightly through
6. Serve hot
