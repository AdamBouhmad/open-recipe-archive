## Ingredients

- 1 small Pumpkin
- 2 oz. Butter
- 1 pint of Milk
- 2 Onions
- 1 Carrot
- 2 quarts of Water
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the pumpkin, onions, and carrot
2. Put them into a saucepan with half the butter, and sweat the vegetables in it for five minutes
3. Pour over the boiling water and boil until the vegetables are very soft
4. Rub through a sieve and return to the saucepan with the milk and some pepper and salt
5. Stir until it boils up
6. Just before serving, stir in, in tiny pieces, the rest of the butter and a little lemon juice
