## Ingredients

- salmon cutlets
- salad oil
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- paper
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut them in slices
2. Rub them with salad oil, and season with cayenne pepper and salt
3. Wrap them in paper, and fry in boiling fat
4. When nearly done, lay them in their paper shrouds on a gridiron, to dry; or place them in a hot oven for a similar purpose
