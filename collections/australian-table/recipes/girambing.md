## Ingredients

- four ounces and a half of powdered ginger
- fourteen quarts of water, wine measure
- whites of four eggs
- nine pounds of white sugar
- nine lemons
- a little yeast

## Directions

1. Boil four ounces and a half of powdered ginger in fourteen quarts of water
2. Beat up the whites of four eggs to a froth, and mix them together, with nine pounds of white sugar
3. Add to the preceding water and ginger
4. Take nine lemons, and add the juice and the rind
5. Put the whole in a barrel, with a little yeast
6. In a few days bottle it off, and it will be soon fit for drinking
