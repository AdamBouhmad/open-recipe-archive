## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of butter melted
- half a pound of currants
- one pound of moist sugar
- four yolks of eggs
- a table-spoonful of brandy
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour, one pound of butter melted, half a pound of currants, one pound of moist sugar, four yolks of eggs, and a table-spoonful of brandy
2. Add a little cinnamon
3. Bake
