## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of fresh butter
- warm water
- three-quarters of a pint of good yeast
- a wine-glassful of rose-water
- two pounds of flour
- three-quarters of a pound of sifted sugar
- half a pound of carraway comfits

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a little warm water
2. Add three-quarters of a pint of good yeast and a wine-glassful of rose-water
3. Pour this mixture on two pounds of flour mixed with three-quarters of a pound of sifted sugar
4. Knead the whole into a light paste with half a pound of carraway comfits
5. Make it into buns and put a few comfits on the top of each
6. Bake
