## Ingredients

- a glass of rum-grog
- honey
- nutmeg
- lemon-juice
- a piece of fresh butter about the size of half a walnut

## Directions

1. Mix a glass of rum-grog pretty strong and hot
2. Sweeten to taste with honey
3. Flavour with nutmeg and lemon-juice
4. Add a piece of fresh butter about the size of half a walnut
