## Ingredients

- fowl
- salt
- Lucca oil
- fine herbs
- pepper
- cayenne
- slice of ham or bacon

## Directions

1. Cut up a fowl as for fricassee, and put it in a stewpan
2. First put the legs, as they take longer doing, and then the other parts, with a little salt, and a quantity of Lucca oil
3. Let the joints brown, and, when half done, add some fine herbs, with pepper and cayenne
4. Serve with sauce, and the dish garnished, but if the fowl is tender it will require no addition, except a slice of ham or bacon
