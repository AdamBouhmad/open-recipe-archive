## Ingredients

- beef, veal, or mutton
- anchovies
- parsley
- basil
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- bacon
- melted butter
- flour
- bouillon
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef, veal, or mutton, and cut it into thin slices
2. Beat them, and spread one of the sides of each slice with anchovies, parsley, basil, the whole chopped very fine
3. Add salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Cut as many pieces of bacon as you have slices of meat, and of the same length
5. Place them on the edge of the meat, and roll up the whole, so that the slice of bacon may be in the centre
6. Tie them up with a string, and put them into a saucepan with some melted butter
7. When they are well browned take them out, put into the fat a spoonful of flour, let it brown, and then add some bouillon, pepper, and salt
8. Put the roulades (from which you have taken the string) into this sauce to finish
9. Add a few slices of lemon, and serve hot
