## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- Two pounds of white sugar
- Two pounds of butter
- Eighteen eggs
- Four pounds of currants, washed and cleaned
- Half a pound of almonds, blanched and cut into pieces
- One pound of candied orange and lemon-peel
- Half a pound of citron
- Half a nutmeg, grated
- Half an ounce of mixed spice
- A gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a stewpan, and work it smooth with a wooden spatula, and mix it with the sugar and spice in a pan for a time
2. Break in the eggs by degrees, and beat the mixture
3. Stir in the brandy, and then the flour, and work it a little
4. Add the fruit, sweetmeats, and almonds, and mix all lightly together
5. Put it in a hoop, and bake on a baking-tin for four hours
6. When nearly cold, ice it
