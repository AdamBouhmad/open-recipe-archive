## Ingredients

- a pint of new milk
- a slice of toasted bread
- a quart of ale
- nutmeg
- ginger

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of new milk with a slice of toasted bread
2. Sweeten a quart of ale and pour it into a basin with nutmeg and ginger
3. Add the boiling milk to it
4. When the head rises, serve
