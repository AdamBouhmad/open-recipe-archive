## Ingredients

- Beef bones
- Two teaspoonsful of salt
- One onion
- One carrot
- Half a turnip
- A little piece of the outside stalk of celery
- 1 doz. peppercorns
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Break beef bones up very small with a chopper
2. Put them into a large saucepan and cover well with cold water
3. Add two teaspoonsful of salt
4. When it boils up remove the scum carefully
5. Put in one onion, one carrot, half a turnip, a little piece of the outside stalk of celery, and 1 doz. peppercorns
6. Boil steadily for six hours, or longer
7. Strain off through a colander or sieve
8. Stand in a cool place till the next day
9. Carefully remove the fat and it is ready for use
10. In very hot weather omit all the vegetables
