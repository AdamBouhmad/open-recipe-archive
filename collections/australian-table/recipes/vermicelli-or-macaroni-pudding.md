## Ingredients

- sufficient vermicelli or macaroni to fill three-fourths of pie-dish
- a little sugar
- butter
- spice
- a custard

## Directions

1. Boil sufficient vermicelli or macaroni to fill three-fourths of your pie-dish, with a little sugar, butter, and spice, on the top of which put a custard
2. bake
