## Ingredients

- fruits in season such as oranges, mandarins, passion fruit, apricots, nectarines, pine-apples, bananas
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take any fruits in season, such as oranges, mandarins, passion fruit, apricots, nectarines, pine-apples, bananas, &c.
2. Peel and slice them up, and put them into a glass dish in layers, with plenty of sugar between each layer
3. Stand in a cool place for an hour covered over, and it is ready to serve
