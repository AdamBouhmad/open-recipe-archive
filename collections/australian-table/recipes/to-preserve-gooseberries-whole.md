## Ingredients

- full grown but not ripe gooseberries
- new corks
- resin

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries
2. Put the fruit into wide-mouthed bottles
3. Gently cork them with new corks
4. Put them into a pan of boiling water
5. Let them remain in the water until they have shrunk to a fourth
6. Beat the corks in tight, and resin the tops
7. Set the bottles in a dry place
