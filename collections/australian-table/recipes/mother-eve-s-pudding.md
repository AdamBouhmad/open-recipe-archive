## Ingredients

- two pennyworth of eggs
- half a dozen apples, well pared and well chopped
- six ounces of bread crumbs, grated fine
- six ounces of currants
- five ounces of sugar
- some salt
- some nutmeg

## Directions

1. Combine eggs, chopped apples, bread crumbs, currants, sugar, salt, and nutmeg
2. Boil for three hours without hurry
3. Serve without sugar or butter
