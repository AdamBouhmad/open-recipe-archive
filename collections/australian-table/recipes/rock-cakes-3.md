## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. Flour
- 2 oz. Dripping
- 1/4 lb. Sugar
- 2 oz. Currants
- 1 Egg
- 1 oz. Peel
- 1 teaspoonful Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Rub the dripping and flour together
2. Stir in the sugar, currants (well cleaned), the baking powder, and the peel
3. Beat up the egg and pour it in
4. Make into a very stiff dough
5. Take up in rough pieces and lay on a greased tin
6. Bake in rather a quick oven for fifteen minutes
