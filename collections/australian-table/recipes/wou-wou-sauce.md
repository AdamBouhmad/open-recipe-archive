## Ingredients

- parsley leaves
- two or three pickled walnuts or cucumbers
- butter
- a table-spoonful of flour
- about half a pint of broth
- a table-spoonful of vinegar
- a table-spoonful of ketchup or port wine
- a tea-spoonful of mustard

## Directions

1. Chop some parsley leaves fine, and add two or three pickled walnuts or cucumbers
2. Put into a pan a bit of butter
3. when it has melted stir in a table-spoonful of flour, and about half a pint of broth, a table-spoonful of vinegar, the same quantity of ketchup, or port wine, and a tea-spoonful of mustard
4. Let it simmer till thick, and then add the parsley leaves and pickles
