## Ingredients

- 2 Bream
- 1 gill Melted Butter
- 1/2 lb. Suet
- 1/2 teaspoonful Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt
- 1/2 lb. Flour

## Directions

1. Skin and fillet the fish and cut into small pieces.
2. Make a dry crust of the suet and flour and line a pudding basin with it.
3. Lay the fish in lightly, and season with the parsley, pepper, and salt.
4. Pour over the melted butter; this should be made with 1/2 oz. butter, 1/2 oz. flour, and 1 gill of water.
5. Cover the top of the pudding with crust, tie down securely with a cloth and string, and plunge into plenty of boiling water.
6. Boil for one hour and a half, turn out of the basin, and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
7. Serve hot.
