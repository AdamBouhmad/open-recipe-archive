## Ingredients

- one or more sets of giblets
- veal or chicken
- good gravy
- pepper
- salt
- browning
- mushroom powder
- lemon-juice
- one glass of white wine
- forcemeat balls
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Scald one or more sets of giblets
2. Set them on the fire with a little veal or chicken, or both, in a good gravy
3. Season to taste
4. Thicken the gravy, and colour it with browning
5. Flavour with mushroom powder and lemon-juice, and one glass of white wine
6. Add forcemeat balls a few moments before serving
7. Garnish with thin slices of hard-boiled eggs
