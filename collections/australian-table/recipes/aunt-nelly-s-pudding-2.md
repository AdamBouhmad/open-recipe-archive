## Ingredients

- half a pound of suet
- half a pound of flour
- half a pound of treacle
- a few shreds of candied lemon-peel
- three table-spoonfuls of cream
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Chop half a pound of suet fine
2. Mix with it half a pound of flour, half a pound of treacle, a few shreds of candied lemon-peel, three table-spoonfuls of cream, and two eggs well beaten
3. Beat the ingredients well
4. Put it into a buttered basin
5. Tie it down with a cloth and boil for a couple of hours
