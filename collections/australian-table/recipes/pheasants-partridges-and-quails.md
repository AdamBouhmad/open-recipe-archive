## Ingredients

- pheasants
- partridges
- quails
- bread sauce
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Let them hang for a short time, not too long
2. Draw, singe, and truss them
3. Put them down to a clear fire, and let them be quickly done
4. Baste them incessantly
5. Serve with bread sauce, or good brown gravy
