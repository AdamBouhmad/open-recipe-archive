## Ingredients

- 2 bream
- lemon juice
- parsley
- half an onion
- 1 pint tomato sauce
- pepper
- salt
- batter or flour

## Directions

1. Fillet the fish and lay them in a dish
2. Sprinkle them well with lemon juice, pepper, salt, and parsley
3. Lay over them some slices of onion
4. Leave them for an hour
5. Fry them either in batter or flour
6. Drain them for a few moments on kitchen paper
7. Serve on a dish very hot with some good thick tomato sauce in a sauce-boat
