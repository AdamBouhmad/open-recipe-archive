## Ingredients

- 2 oz. macaroni
- 1 bottle oysters
- 1 gill milk or melted butter sauce
- cayenne
- salt
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in the oyster liquor or in weak stock till quite soft
2. Rub a little butter on a dish
3. Cut the macaroni into pieces two inches long and lay it at the bottom
4. On this place the oysters
5. Season them with cayenne, salt, and a little lemon juice or nutmeg
6. Pour over the milk or sauce
7. Cover with bread crumbs
8. Brown it in a quick oven
9. A few little pieces of butter laid on top of the crumbs make a richer dish
10. Serve very hot
