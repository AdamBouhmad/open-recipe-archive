## Ingredients

- half a quartern of dough
- one gill of the best Florence oil
- half a pound of currants
- half a pound of moist sugar
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take half a quartern of dough, one gill of the best Florence oil, half a pound of currants, the same of moist sugar, and a little cinnamon.
2. Mix all well together.
3. Make it up in the form of a twist.
4. Bake it.
