## Ingredients

- one bottle of ordinary claret
- a bottle of soda-water
- a glass of sherry or curacoa
- a pint of ripe raspberries or four or five peaches or nectarines
- powdered sugar
- lumps of clear ice

## Directions

1. To each bottle of ordinary claret add a bottle of soda-water
2. Add a glass of sherry or curacoa
3. Substitute a pint of ripe raspberries or four or five peaches or nectarines cut in slices for the lemon-peel
4. Add powdered sugar according to taste
5. Let the whole remain an hour or two before serving
6. Then add some lumps of clear ice
