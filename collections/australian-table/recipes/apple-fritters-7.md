## Ingredients

- 3 Apples
- Frying Batter
- Hot Fat
- Sugar
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Peel and slice up the apples into rounds, take out the core with a small round cutter.
2. Make frying batter by directions given elsewhere, and flavour with lemon juice.
3. Dip in the pieces of apple, plunge into plenty of hot fat, and fry till a good colour.
4. Drain on kitchen paper, pile high on a dish, and sprinkle well with sugar.
5. Serve very hot.
