## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of currants
- three eggs beaten
- half a pint of milk
- two glasses of white wine
- one glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour, one pound of butter, one pound of sugar, and one pound of currants
2. Wet them with three eggs beaten, half a pint of milk, two glasses of white wine, and one glass of brandy
3. Work them together
4. Bake in a tin plate
