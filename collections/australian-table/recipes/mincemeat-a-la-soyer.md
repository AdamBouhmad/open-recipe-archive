## Ingredients

- four pounds and a half of beef suet
- a quarter of a pound of candied lemon and orange-peel
- a quarter of a pound of citron
- a pound and a half of lean cooked beef
- three pounds and a half of apples, cored and peeled
- four pounds and a half of currants, well washed and picked
- two ounces of mixed spice
- two pounds of moist sugar
- juice of eight lemons
- a pint of brandy
- a pound and a half of raisins, well stoned and chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Skin and chop the beef suet very fine
2. Chop the candied lemon and orange-peel, citron, lean cooked beef, and apples fine
3. Put all ingredients into a large pan with the currants, mixed spice, and moist sugar
4. Mix the whole well together with the juice of eight lemons and a pint of brandy
5. Place it in jars and tie down for use
