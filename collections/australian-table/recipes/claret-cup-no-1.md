## Ingredients

- one bottle of ordinary claret
- a bottle of soda-water
- a glass of sherry or curacoa
- the peel of a lemon
- powdered sugar
- lumps of clear ice

## Directions

1. To each bottle of ordinary claret add a bottle of soda-water
2. Add a glass of sherry or curacoa and the peel of a lemon cut very thin
3. Add powdered sugar according to taste
4. Let the whole remain an hour or two before serving
5. Then add some lumps of clear ice
