## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- dripping
- one or two eggs
- warm water

## Directions

1. Take flour, and butter, and dripping enough to make the crust—half butter and half dripping, and to one pound of flour put half the weight of butter
2. Rub the flour and butter well together
3. Beat up one or two eggs, with sufficient warm water to mix the flour
4. Roll once
