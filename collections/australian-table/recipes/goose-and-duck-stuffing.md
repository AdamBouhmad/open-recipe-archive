## Ingredients

- two ounces of onion
- an ounce of green sage
- four ounces of bread crumbs
- the yolk and white of an egg
- a little minced apple
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Chop fine two ounces of onion, an ounce of green sage, four ounces of bread crumbs, the yolk and white of an egg, and a little minced apple and cayenne
