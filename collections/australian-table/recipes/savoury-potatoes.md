## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 Large Potatoes
- 2 oz. Cheese
- 1 spoonful of Milk
- 1 Egg
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Scrub the potatoes and bake them in the oven.
2. Cut off the end, scoop out all the meal; grate up some dry pieces of cheese, beat it into the potatoes with the yolk of the egg, and some seasoning.
3. Whip the white till stiff and stir lightly in; fill the potatoes with this mixture.
4. Lay in a baking sheet and bake for about twenty minutes.
5. Garnish with parsley, and serve.
