## Ingredients

- 1 Leg of Mutton
- 1 Rasher of Ham
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 20 Peppercorns
- 1 1/2 oz. Butter
- 2 Carrots
- 1 Turnip
- 1 Onion
- 1 quart Stock

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a saucepan, and when it is dissolved put in the mutton and brown it all over
2. Then lay the ham and vegetables round it, pour in the stock, and bring it to the boil
3. Cover down closely, and stand the saucepan in a moderate oven where it will cook slowly
4. If the braising is being done by a coal fire the lid of the stewpan may be reversed and some hot coals placed in it; these will want renewing from time to time
5. In any case cook very slowly, then dish the meat, strain the gravy, remove the fat carefully, and boil to a sort of half glaze
6. Pour round the dish, serve with Julienne or plain vegetables
