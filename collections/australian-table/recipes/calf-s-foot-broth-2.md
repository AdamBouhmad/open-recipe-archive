## Ingredients

- a calf's foot
- water
- a little lemon-peel
- butter
- sugar
- nutmeg
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Simmer the foot in water, with a little lemon-peel, taking off the scum.
2. Put it into a basin, and, when cold, take off the fat.
3. When wanted, warm it up again with a little of the broth, adding butter, sugar, and nutmeg.
4. Add the yolk of an egg and serve.
