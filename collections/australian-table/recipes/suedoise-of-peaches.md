## Ingredients

- four ripe peaches
- a third of a pint of water
- three ounces of white sugar
- a couple of pounds of ripe peaches
- a blanched peach kernel
- macaroons
- candied citron
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Pare and divide four ripe peaches
2. Let them simmer for about five minutes in a syrup made with the third of a pint of water and three ounces of white sugar
3. Lift them out carefully into a deep dish, and pour half the syrup over them
4. Into the remaining half put a couple of pounds of ripe peaches, and boil them to marmalade
5. Lift the other peaches from the syrup, and reduce it by boiling more than half
6. Spread a deep layer of marmalade in a dish, and arrange the peaches round it, and fill the places with the marmalade
7. Place the half of a blanched peach kernel on each
8. Pour the reduced syrup equally over the surface
9. Form a border round the dish with macaroons or, instead, candied citron, sliced thin, and cut into leaves with a paste-cutter
10. Add a little lemon-juice
