## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs. of Steak
- 1 dessertspoonful Curry Powder
- 1 dessertspoonful Worcester Sauce
- 1 dessertspoonful Vinegar
- ½ pint Stock
- 1 tablespoonful Flour
- 1 tablespoonful Chutney
- ½ lb. Rice
- Salt
- Saffron

## Directions

1. Slice up the steak into pieces about three inches long and two broad.
2. Mix the curry powder, sauce, vinegar, flour, and chutney together and spread this over the steak.
3. Roll up and thread on small wooden skewers.
4. Lay them in a saucepan, pour over the stock, bring to the boil and simmer one hour and a half.
5. While they are cooking, well wash the rice in cold water and let it soak for half an hour.
6. Throw it into boiling water for three minutes and strain off.
7. Put a pinch of saffron in some fresh water, season with salt, and finish cooking in this.
8. Strain off and dry in the saucepan.
9. Pile this on a dish and lay the kabobs over it.
10. Boil up the gravy, season and flavour, and strain round the dish.
