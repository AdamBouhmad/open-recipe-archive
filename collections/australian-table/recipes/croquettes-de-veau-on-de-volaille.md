## Ingredients

- two pounds of veal, chicken, or turkey
- ten ounces of butter
- a table-spoonful of fine flour
- yolks of two eggs
- a little milk
- the juice of a lemon
- a tea-cupful of stock
- pepper
- salt
- a little nutmeg grated
- a little lemon-peel
- bread crumbs
- lard
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mince two pounds of veal, chicken, or turkey
2. Melt ten ounces of butter in a stewpan
3. When hot add a table-spoonful of fine flour and mix
4. Beat the yolks of two eggs with a little milk and the juice of a lemon
5. Mix it with the flour and butter by degrees
6. Add the minced meat with a tea-cupful of stock
7. Season with pepper, salt, a little nutmeg grated, and a little lemon-peel
8. Stir it together and let it boil till of proper thickness
9. Pour it into a basin till cold
10. Grate some bread on a pasteboard
11. Cut the mince into pieces of the same size and roll them lightly in the bread crumbs
12. Beat the yolk of an egg or two and dip the croquettes into this
13. Roll them again in the bread crumbs, making them a neat shape like a pear
14. Have some lard melted and put the croquettes in
15. Fry them a light brown
16. Drain them on a colander and keep them hot
17. Serve with garnished parsley
