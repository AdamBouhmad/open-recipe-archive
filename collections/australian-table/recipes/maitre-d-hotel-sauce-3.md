## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Butter
- Lemon Juice
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a small saucepan, and when it is melted stir in the flour, and mix smoothly; pour in the milk and stir until it boils.
2. Take the saucepan from the fire, add a few drops of lemon juice, a pinch of pepper and salt to taste, last of all the parsley.
3. It is then ready to serve.
