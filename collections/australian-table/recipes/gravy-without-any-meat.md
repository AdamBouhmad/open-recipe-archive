## Ingredients

- half a pint of beer
- half a pint of water
- pepper
- salt
- lemon peel
- couple of cloves
- two spoonfuls of walnut or mushroom ketchup
- onion or two
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix half a pint of beer, the same quantity of water, with pepper, salt, lemon peel, a couple of cloves, and two spoonfuls of walnut or mushroom ketchup together
2. Flour and skin an onion or two and fry it in butter until brown
3. Put the mixture into a stewpan and simmer it for a quarter of an hour
4. It is fit for use
