## Ingredients

- a pound of butter
- a little milk
- a pound of flour
- six beaten eggs
- a quarter of a pound of beaten sweet almonds
- two table-spoonfuls of yeast
- egg prepared as for bola d'amor
- slices of citron
- egg marmalade
- cinnamon
- a rich puff paste
- clarified sugar with orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Warm a pound of butter over the fire with a little milk
2. Put it into a pan with a pound of flour, six beaten eggs, a quarter of a pound of beaten sweet almonds, and two table-spoonfuls of yeast
3. Make these ingredients into a light paste, and set it before the fire to rise
4. Grease a deep dish, and place in a layer of the paste
5. Then some egg prepared as for bola d'amor
6. Then slices of citron, and a layer of egg marmalade
7. Sprinkle each layer with cinnamon, and fill the dish with alternate layers
8. Line the dish with a rich puff paste
9. Bake in a brisk oven
10. Pour clarified sugar with orange-flower water over till the syrup has thoroughly penetrated the bola
