## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 1 gill Oil
- 1/2 gill Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of the eggs into a basin, sprinkle over the salt, begin to stir them with a wooden spoon, dropping in the oil very slowly
2. The sauce must be kept thick, and the oil added very slowly
3. When it is quite thick and smooth, pour in the vinegar slowly, and it is ready for use
4. If made some time before it is required for table, it must be kept cool
5. It ought to stand in ice, and the vinegar should be added just before serving
