## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Flour
- 1/2 lb. Bread Crumbs
- 1/2 lb. Suet
- 1 oz. Sugar
- 1 tablespoonful Vinegar
- 1 gill Milk
- 1 tablespoonful Raspberry Jam
- 1 Egg
- 1/2 teaspoonful Carbonate of Soda

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, crumbs, finely chopped suet, and sugar in a basin
2. Stir in the jam
3. Beat up the egg and milk and stir it in
4. Mix up the carbonate of soda and the vinegar together
5. Beat it in
6. When well mixed pour it into a buttered basin
7. Tie up carefully and boil for two hours
8. Turn out on to a hot dish and serve either with sifted sugar or custard sauce
