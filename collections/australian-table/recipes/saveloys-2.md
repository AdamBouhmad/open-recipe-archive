## Ingredients

- pork
- saltpetre
- common salt
- pepper
- sage
- grated bread
- eggs

## Directions

1. Pork, free from bone and skin, three pounds
2. Salt it with an ounce of saltpetre, and half a pound of common salt for about forty-eight hours
3. Mince it fine
4. Add three spoonfuls of pepper, a dozen leaves of sage, and a pound of grated bread
5. Mix well
6. Fill the skins, and boil or bake them slowly
7. Or the ingredients may be made flat and round, without being put into skins, and cooked in that manner, after being mixed with beaten eggs, in order to bind the meat together
