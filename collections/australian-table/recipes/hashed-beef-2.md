## Ingredients

- beef
- an onion
- water or stock
- flour thickening
- ketchup
- other sauce
- bread or toast sippets

## Directions

1. Cut the beef in slices, and put it into a stewpan, with an onion, and a little water or stock
2. Let it simmer, then add a little flour thickening, with ketchup and other sauce
3. Let it simmer for a quarter of an hour, and serve with bread or toast sippets
