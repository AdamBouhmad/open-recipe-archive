## Ingredients

- oysters
- their liquor
- lemon-juice or vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- batter

## Directions

1. Put the oysters into a saucepan for a few minutes, to blanch with their liquor
2. take them out
3. lay them on a linen cloth to drain
4. place them for an hour in lemon-juice or vinegar, pepper and salt, and a little nutmeg
5. dip them into batter
6. fry them
