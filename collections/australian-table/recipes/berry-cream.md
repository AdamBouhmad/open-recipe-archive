## Ingredients

- a pottle of strawberries
- half a pound of white sugar
- a pint of whipped cream
- one ounce and a half of dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Mix strawberries with half a pound of white sugar
2. Rub this through a sieve
3. Add to it a pint of whipped cream and one ounce and a half of dissolved isinglass
4. Pour it into the mould, which must be immersed in ice until ready to serve
5. Carefully turn out on the dish and garnish according to fancy
