## Ingredients

- a cupful of pearl barley
- about three quarts of cold water
- about two pounds of neck of mutton
- two carrots
- two turnips
- a few small onions
- pepper
- salt
- a cabbage or a similar quantity of greens

## Directions

1. Put a cupful of pearl barley with about three quarts of cold water in a pot and allow it to simmer
2. Put into the pot about two pounds of neck of mutton and let it continue simmering for an hour, skimming occasionally
3. Add two carrots, two turnips, and a few small onions cut in pieces, with some pepper and salt
4. Add a cabbage cut into pieces or a similar quantity of greens
5. Simmer another hour and add a little more boiling water if necessary
6. Serve the meat in a separate dish garnished with carrots and turnips, and serve the broth in a tureen
