## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- lemon-peel
- six ounces of white sugar
- two tea-spoonfuls of flour
- the juice of two lemons
- a quarter of a pound of macaroons
- a glass of white wine
- sliced citron or iced lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Flavour with lemon-peel a pint of cream
2. Add six ounces of white sugar
3. Put the cream and sugar into a saucepan
4. When simmering, add, by degrees, two tea-spoonfuls of flour
5. Let it simmer for five minutes, stirring it all the time
6. Pour it out, and when cold mix with it, by degrees, the juice of two lemons
7. Take a quarter of a pound of macaroons, put them in a dish, and pour over them a glass of white wine
8. Add part of the cream, then the macaroons, and cream again
9. Ornament the dish with sliced citron or iced lemon-peel
