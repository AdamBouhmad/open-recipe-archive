## Ingredients

- one bottle of still or sparkling Moselle
- one bottle of soda-water
- sprigs of woodruffe
- the peel of half a lemon
- powdered sugar
- lumps of clear ice

## Directions

1. To each bottle of still or sparkling Moselle add one bottle of soda-water
2. Add sprigs of woodruffe instead of fruit
3. Add the peel of half a lemon cut very thin and powdered sugar according to taste
4. Let the whole stand about an hour
5. Before serving add some lumps of clear ice
