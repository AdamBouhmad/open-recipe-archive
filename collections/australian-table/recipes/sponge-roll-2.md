## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonsful flour
- 3 tablespoonsful sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoonsful baking powder
- 3 teaspoonsful jam

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar together for five minutes, mix the flour and baking powder together and stir them lightly in.
2. Pour into a well-buttered tin and bake in a quick oven for eight or ten minutes.
3. Turn on to a damp cloth and roll up directly; warm the jam in a saucepan while the roll is cooking, and if it is very stiff mix in a spoonful of water.
4. Take the roll out of the cloth and lay flat on a piece of sugared paper, spread the jam on quickly and roll up again; place on a sieve till cold.
