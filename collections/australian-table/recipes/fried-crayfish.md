## Ingredients

- 1 Crayfish
- French Frying Batter
- 1 teaspoonful Anchovy
- Frying Fat
- Lemon Juice
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pick all the white meat from a crayfish, and cut it into pieces about two inches long and one inch broad.
2. Make a frying batter by recipe given elsewhere, and season with anchovy, lemon juice, pepper, and salt.
3. Dip the pieces of crayfish into this and plunge into plenty of very hot fat.
4. Fry a good colour, drain on kitchen paper for a few minutes, pile high on a dish, and garnish with fried parsley.
