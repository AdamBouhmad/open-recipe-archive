## Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes
- 1 Cucumber
- 1 tablespoonful Oil
- 1 Spring Onion
- Half a Lettuce
- 2 tablespoonsful Vinegar
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Scald the tomatoes and take off the skin.
2. Put them into cold water or on to the ice until quite cold.
3. Cut them up the same as an orange.
4. Peel and cut up the cucumber into very thin slices.
5. Mince up the onion.
6. Sprinkle these with pepper and salt.
7. Pour over the oil and vinegar.
8. Shred up the lettuce and lay on the top.
9. It is then ready to serve.
