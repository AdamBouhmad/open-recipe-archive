## Ingredients

- meats, fowls, or rabbits
- onion
- curry stuff
- flour
- lemon
- butter
- ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut meats, fowls, or rabbits into small joints, and fry them brown in a pan with an onion chopped up
2. When half done take them out and put them into a stewpan, with two table-spoonfuls of curry stuff and a dredge of flour, and let it simmer with the juice of half a lemon, a table-spoonful of butter, and a little ketchup
