## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- half a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of currants
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Beat half a pound of butter to a cream
2. Strew in by degrees half a pound of powdered sugar and mix both together
3. Dredge in half a pound of flour and add a quarter of a pound of currants
4. Moisten with four eggs well beaten
5. When all the ingredients are well stirred and mixed, butter a mould that will hold the mixture
6. Tie it down with a cloth and put the pudding into boiling water
7. Boil for a couple of hours
8. When turned out, put on it some powdered sugar and serve
