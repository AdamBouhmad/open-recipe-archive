## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Rub stale bread through a colander
2. Put them in a pan, with two ounces of butter
3. Place them over a moderate fire
4. Stir them with a wooden spoon, until they are slightly brown
5. Spread them on a sieve to drain
