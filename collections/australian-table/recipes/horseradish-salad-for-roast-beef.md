## Ingredients

- scraped horseradish
- vinegar
- moist sugar
- spoonful of mustard
- yolk of an egg
- dust of cayenne
- cream

## Directions

1. Moisten scraped horseradish with a small quantity of vinegar
2. Add a little moist sugar, a spoonful of mustard, and the yolk of an egg, with a dust of cayenne
3. Soften the whole with cream to the consistence required
