## Ingredients

- one salt-spoonful of salt
- one salt-spoonful of cayenne
- one dessert-spoonful of lemon-juice
- one dessert-spoonful of ketchup
- two dessert-spoonfuls of Harvey
- three dessert-spoonfuls of port wine
- three dessert-spoonfuls of claret

## Directions

1. Mix the ingredients
2. Warm them
3. Pour over before the dish comes on the table
4. Keep away all fat from the bird when done
