## Ingredients

- vegetables
- a piece of butter
- the yolks of three eggs
- water

## Directions

1. Make a soup maigre by using vegetables boiled tender enough to be rubbed through a tamis
2. Add water to the soup before the eggs are put in
3. Add a piece of butter, and the yolks of three eggs well beaten
