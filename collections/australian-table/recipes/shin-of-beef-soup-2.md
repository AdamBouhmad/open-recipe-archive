## Ingredients

- shin of beef, about six pounds
- two carrots
- two turnips
- a head of celery
- a little allspice
- cloves
- savory
- thyme
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take a shin of beef, of about six pounds; chop the bone in three pieces
2. Put it into a soup-kettle to simmer and cover it well with cold water
3. After about an hour, add two carrots, two turnips, a head of celery, a little allspice, cloves, savory, thyme, and parsley
4. Let it continue simmering for two or three hours
5. Add pepper and salt
6. Strain the soup and serve the vegetables and soup served up thick, and the boulli and gristle in a separate dish with a small quantity of parsley and butter over it
