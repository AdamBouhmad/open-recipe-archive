## Ingredients

- one ounce and a half of tamarinds
- three ounces of currants
- two ounces of stoned raisins
- three pints of water
- lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Boil tamarinds, currants, and stoned raisins in three pints of water
2. Strain
3. Add a little lemon-peel
