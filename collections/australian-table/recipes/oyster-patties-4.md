## Ingredients

- paste
- bread
- oysters
- nutmeg
- white pepper
- salt
- lemon
- cream
- oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Put paste into patty-pans, and cover them, with a bit of bread on each
2. bake them
3. Take off the beards of the oysters
4. cut the other parts in small bits
5. put them into a small saucepan, with a little nutmeg, white pepper and salt, lemon, cream and a small quantity of the oyster liquor
6. Simmer for a few minutes
7. fill the patty-pans
