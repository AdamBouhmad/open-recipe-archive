## Ingredients

- Cold potatoes
- Dripping
- 1 oz. Butter
- Lemon juice
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut up any cold potatoes that may be left into strips, not too thin, put some dripping into a frying pan, and when very hot put in the potatoes and fry them a pale colour.
2. Place them on a hot dish; melt one ounce of butter in a saucepan.
3. Season with lemon juice, parsley, pepper, and salt.
4. Pour this over the potatoes, and serve very hot.
