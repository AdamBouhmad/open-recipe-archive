## Ingredients

- lamb fore-quarter
- gravy
- mint sauce
- lemon
- salad

## Directions

1. Roast the fore-quarter before a clear fire
2. Bast from the time it is warmed until it is thoroughly done
3. Never place so near the fire as to scorch the fat
4. Serve with a little gravy made in the pan
5. Have mint sauce in a tureen, with a lemon cut ready for use
6. A salad is a favourite addition
