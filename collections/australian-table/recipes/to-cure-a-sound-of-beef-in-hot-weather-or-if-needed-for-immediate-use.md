## Ingredients

- sound of beef
- fine Liverpool salt
- saltpetre
- cold brine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rub in thoroughly a rather large quantity of fine Liverpool salt, with a little saltpetre
2. Place it over a tub or pan of water on two sticks
3. Place a good quantity of salt on the upper side
4. Turn it once after twelve hours, laying additional salt on the turned-up side
5. It will be cured in twenty-four hours
6. Should it be required at once, with a small squirt or syringe, if cold brine be at hand, it might be squirted into all the crevices, and then it could be cooked in twelve hours
7. If beef is not required in a hurry, a little sugar and less salt very much improves the flavour
