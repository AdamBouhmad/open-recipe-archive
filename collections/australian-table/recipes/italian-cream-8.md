## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of milk
- the peel of an orange and lemon
- a little salt
- three ounces of pounded lump sugar
- the yolks of six eggs
- a sufficient quantity of isinglass, dissolved

## Directions

1. Boil a pint and a half of milk, and when it is boiling infuse the peel of an orange and lemon until the milk is flavoured
2. Put in a little salt, three ounces of pounded lump sugar, and the yolks of six eggs
3. Add a sufficient quantity of isinglass, dissolved, to make it tenacious
4. Put it into moulds until cold
5. To take it from the mould, dip a cloth in hot water, and rub round the mould; the cream will then turn out entire
