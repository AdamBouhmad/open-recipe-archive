## Ingredients

- one pint of claret
- one pint of port wine
- one pint of mushroom ketchup
- half a pint of walnut liquor
- four ounces of pounded anchovies
- one ounce of fresh lemon-peel finely pared
- one ounce of sliced shalots
- one ounce of scraped horseradish
- half an ounce of allspice and black pepper
- one drachm of cayenne
- three drachms of curry-powder
- one drachm of celery seed bruised

## Directions

1. Put into a wide-mouthed bottle
2. Shake every day for a fortnight
