## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- toast or spinach

## Directions

1. Put in a stewpan a pint of water, with half a table-spoonful of salt, and three of vinegar.
2. When simmering break in the eggs, and let them remain until the yolk is set.
3. Serve on toast or spinach.
