## Ingredients

- one gallon of boiling water
- one pound of loaf sugar
- one ounce of best ginger, bruised
- one ounce of cream of tartar, or a sliced lemon
- a table-spoonful of good yeast

## Directions

1. Stir them until the sugar is dissolved
2. Let it remain until it cools to the heat of milk, then add a table-spoonful of good yeast
3. Cover and let it stand for twenty-four hours
4. Bottle it, not filling the bottles
5. Cork well and tie down, and it will be ready for use in two days
