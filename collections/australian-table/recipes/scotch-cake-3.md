## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of flour
- a pound and a half of butter
- a pound and a half of white sugar
- six ounces of blanched sweet almonds
- three-quarters of a pound of candied orange-peel
- half a pound of citron, cut small
- half a nutmeg grated
- a tea-spoonful of caraway-seeds
- twelve eggs
- a glass of brandy
- carraway comfits

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, add the sugar and eggs gradually
2. Mix the flour by degrees, and then the sweetmeats, almonds, and spice
3. Stir in a glass of brandy
4. Butter the hoop or tin, and pour in the cake so as to nearly fill it
5. Smooth the top, and strew over it carraway comfits
6. Bake in a moderate oven
