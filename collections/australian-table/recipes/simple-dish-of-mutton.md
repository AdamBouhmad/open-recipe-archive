## Ingredients

- mutton steaks
- quarter of a pound of maccaroni
- water
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- chutney
- table-spoonful of vinegar or juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Put a few steaks of mutton on to simmer for an hour, with a quarter of a pound of maccaroni, and a little water
2. Season with pepper, salt, cayenne, and chutney, and a table-spoonful of vinegar, or the juice of half a lemon
