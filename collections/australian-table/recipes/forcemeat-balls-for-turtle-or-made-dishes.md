## Ingredients

- beef or veal
- a third of its weight in butter
- the same weight of bread crumbs
- milk
- chopped parsley
- shalot
- the yolks of three eggs, boiled hard
- salt
- pepper
- curry-powder
- the yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Pound some beef or veal, and a third of its weight in butter
2. Put into a stewpan the same weight of bread crumbs, moistened with milk
3. Add chopped parsley and shalot
4. Rub them in a mortar until they form a smooth paste
5. Put it through a sieve
6. When cold, pound and mix all together, with the yolks of three eggs, boiled hard
7. Season with salt, pepper, and curry-powder
8. Add the yolks of two eggs
9. Rub together and make small balls
10. Put into the soup a few minutes before it is served
