## Ingredients

- 1 bottle oysters
- 4 cold potatoes
- 1 egg
- 2 oz. flour
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- milk

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes and make them into a paste with the flour and egg
2. Roll out and cut into small pieces
3. Season the oysters with lemon juice, pepper, and salt
4. Put three or four oysters into each piece of potato crust
5. Roll it up and brush over with milk
6. Bake for twenty minutes
7. Pile high on a dish and serve hot
