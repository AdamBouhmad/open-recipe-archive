## Ingredients

- two pounds of rump-steak
- two or three onions
- good crust
- pepper
- salt
- a dozen oysters
- mushroom ketchup
- flour
- water
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut the rump-steak into seasonable pieces
2. Cut into shreds two or three onions
3. Paste the pudding-basin with good crust, not too rich nor too poor
4. Put the meat into the basin, with some pepper and salt, and a dozen oysters
5. Add a little thickening, composed of mushroom ketchup, flour and water, and mustard
6. Simmer for an hour and a half
7. Serve in the basin or turn it out, if the gravy in the pudding can be retained
