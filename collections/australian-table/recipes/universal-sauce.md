## Ingredients

- one pint of broth
- a glass of white wine
- salt
- pepper
- grated lemon
- a bay leaf
- a spoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Into a pint of broth put a glass of white wine, salt, pepper, grated lemon, a bay leaf, and a spoonful of lemon juice
2. Let them macerate by the side of the fire for ten hours
3. When served, pour over either game, fish, or vegetables, or send to table in a sauceboat
