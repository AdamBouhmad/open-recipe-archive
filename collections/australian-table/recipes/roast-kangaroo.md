## Ingredients

- hind quarters of a medium-sized brush kangaroo
- bacon
- good veal stuffing
- forcemeat balls
- good gravy
- currant jelly
- good dripping or milk

## Directions

1. Lard the hind quarters of a medium-sized brush kangaroo with bacon
2. Stuff with good veal stuffing
3. Put down to roast
4. Make some forcemeat balls and put in the dripping-pan to warm
5. Well baste the kangaroo during roasting with either good dripping or milk (the latter is best)
6. Serve with some good gravy, properly made, and currant jelly
7. This dish must be well but not over-done
