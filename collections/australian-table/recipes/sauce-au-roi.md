## Ingredients

- two quarts of vinegar
- a pint of soy
- a pint of walnut ketchup
- six cloves
- six shalots
- a quarter of an ounce of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Mix two quarts of vinegar, a pint of soy, and a pint of walnut ketchup, with six cloves, six shalots, and a quarter of an ounce of cayenne pepper
2. let it stand for use
