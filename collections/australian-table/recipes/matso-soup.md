## Ingredients

- half a shin of beef
- four pounds of gravy beef
- a calf's foot
- four quarts of water
- celery
- carrots
- turnips
- pepper
- salt
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- half a pound of matso flour
- two ounces of chopped suet
- ginger
- nutmeg
- four beaten eggs
- a small onion
- a dessert-spoonful of oil

## Directions

1. Boil down half a shin of beef, four pounds of gravy beef and a calf's foot may be added if approved in four quarts of water
2. Season with celery, carrots, turnips, pepper and salt, and a bunch of sweet herbs
3. Let the whole stew gently for eight hours, then strain, and let it stand to get cold, when the fat must be removed
4. Return it to the saucepan to warm up
5. Take half a pound of matso flour, two ounces of chopped suet, season with a little pepper, salt, ginger, and nutmeg
6. Mix with this four beaten eggs, and make it into a paste with a small onion shred and browned in a dessert-spoonful of oil
7. Make the paste into rather large balls, taking care to make them very light
8. Ten minutes before serving throw in the balls
