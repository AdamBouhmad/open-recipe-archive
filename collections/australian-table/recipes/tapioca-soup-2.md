## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Tapioca
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 3 quarts Bone Stock
- Salt
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the onion and carrot in the stock for twenty minutes
2. If the stock is not a good colour put in half a teaspoonful of burnt sugar
3. Strain out the vegetables
4. Wash the tapioca in cold water and stir it in
5. Continue stirring until the tapioca is quite clear
6. Flavour with salt and lemon juice, and serve very hot
