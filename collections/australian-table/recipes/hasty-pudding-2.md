## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Butter
- 3 oz. Flour
- 2 oz. Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the milk on the fire to boil, and when boiling stir in the flour quickly; it should be rather lumpy.
2. Pour it into a dish, melt the butter and sugar, and pour it in the middle of the pudding.
3. A little flavouring of grated lemon peel may be put into the milk, or jam served with the pudding.
