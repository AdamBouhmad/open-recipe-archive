## Ingredients

- a dessert-spoonful of arrowroot or potato starch
- a small quantity of cold water
- a pint and a half of milk
- a little salt
- three ounces of pounded white sugar
- the yolks of eight eggs
- a table-spoonful of orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Dilute a dessert-spoonful of arrowroot or potato starch in a small quantity of cold water
2. Put a pint and a half of milk to boil in a saucepan
3. When it boils, add a little salt, three ounces of pounded white sugar, and the diluted arrowroot or starch
4. When the milk has sufficiently thickened, put to it the yolks of eight eggs, well beaten, and a table-spoonful of orange-flower water
5. Stir the whole sufficiently, and pour into a cream-dish
