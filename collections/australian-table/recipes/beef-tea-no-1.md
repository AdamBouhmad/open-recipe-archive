## Ingredients

- 1 pound gravy beef
- 1 pint water
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Remove all fat and skin from the meat
2. Put it twice through a sausage machine or scrape it into a pulp with a sharp knife
3. Pour over the cold water and let it stand for an hour
4. Pour it into a brown baking jar and put it into a cool oven
5. Keep it below boiling point for an hour or longer according to the heat of the oven
6. It should look brown, thick, and rich when sufficiently cooked
7. Strain through a colander and add salt to taste
