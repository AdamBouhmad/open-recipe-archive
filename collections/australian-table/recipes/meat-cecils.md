## Ingredients

- meat
- crumbs of bread
- onions
- shalot
- lemon-peel
- salt
- nutmeg
- anchovies
- butter
- egg
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Mince any kind of meat, add crumbs of bread, onions, shalot, lemon-peel, salt, nutmeg, a couple of anchovies boned, and a small piece of butter
2. Mix well, and make into balls, with an egg
3. Sprinkle over them crumbs of grated bread, and fry them brown
