## Ingredients

- six ounces of butter or clarified dripping
- one pound of flour
- half a pint of water
- flour for dredging

## Directions

1. Work the flour to a paste with half a pint of water
2. Roll out the crust rather thin
3. Place the butter over it in small pieces
4. Dredge over it some flour
5. Fold the paste over
6. Repeat the rolling
