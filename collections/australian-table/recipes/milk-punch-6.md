## Ingredients

- six oranges and lemons
- sugar
- a bottle of rum or brandy
- two pounds of sugar
- four quarts of water
- one quart of new milk, boiling hot

## Directions

1. Pare six oranges and lemons as thin as you can
2. Grate them over with sugar to get the flavour
3. Steep the peels in a bottle of rum or brandy for twenty-four hours
4. Squeeze the fruit on two pounds of sugar
5. Add to it four quarts of water and one of new milk boiling hot
6. Add the rum
7. Run it through a jelly-bag, bottle and cork
