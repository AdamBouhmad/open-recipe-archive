## Ingredients

- a pound of French roll dough
- six ounces of fresh butter
- two eggs
- flour
- Florence oil
- clarified sugar
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a pound of French roll dough, six ounces of fresh butter, two eggs, as much flour as will be requisite
2. Knead together and roll it in the form of a long French roll
3. Cut it in thin long slices
4. Set them at a short distance from the fire to rise
5. Fry them in the best Florence oil
6. When nearly cold, dip them in clarified sugar, flavoured with essence of lemon
