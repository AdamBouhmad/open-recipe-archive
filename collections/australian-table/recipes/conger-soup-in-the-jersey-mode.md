## Ingredients

- five pounds of conger
- three pints of water
- new milk
- green peas

## Directions

1. Take five pounds of conger to three pints of water
2. Cut the conger into small pieces and place them in a boiler with the water until the liquid is reduced to two-thirds of its original quantity
3. Strain it
4. Add to it the same quantity of new milk as there is of soup
5. Warm this up again
6. Throw in some green peas, which should be boiled up in the soup until thoroughly done
