## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Mixed Vegetables
- 2 oz. Butter
- ½ lb. Haricot Beans
- Peppercorns
- Salt
- Sugar
- 4 quarts of Water
- carrots
- turnips
- leeks
- onions
- celery

## Directions

1. Take any vegetables that may be in season, such as carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, and celery, and slice them up
2. Put them into a saucepan with the haricot beans and the butter, and turn them all about till the butter is all absorbed
3. Sprinkle over them a teaspoonful each of salt and sugar, add the peppercorns and the water
4. Boil until the vegetables are very soft
5. Rub them through a sieve, return to the saucepan and make thoroughly hot
6. It is ready to serve
