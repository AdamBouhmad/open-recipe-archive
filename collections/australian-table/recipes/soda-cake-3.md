## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Flour
- 1/2 lb. Currants
- 1/4 lb. Sugar
- 6 oz. Dripping
- 1 1/2 teaspoonsful Carbonate of Soda
- 1/2 pint Milk

## Directions

1. Rub the dripping and the flour together
2. Clean and stir in the currants and sugar
3. Stir in the soda and mix into a dough with the milk
4. Beat for a few minutes
5. Pour into a tin which has been well rubbed with dripping
6. Bake in a moderate oven for two hours
