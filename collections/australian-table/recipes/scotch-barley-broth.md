## Ingredients

- a neck of mutton
- half a gallon of water
- three table-spoonfuls of Scotch barley
- some turnips
- onions

## Directions

1. Put a neck of mutton on the fire in a saucepan, with half a gallon of water, and three table-spoonfuls of Scotch barley, and let it simmer.
2. After it has been on some time, add some turnips, and onions cut into dice.
3. Skim well, and allow it to simmer about a couple of hours.
