## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of savoury biscuits
- a quart of milk
- six ounces of fresh butter
- four eggs
- one ounce of candied orange-peel
- one ounce of lemon-peel
- one ounce of citron
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Soak three-quarters of a pound of savoury biscuits in a quart of milk
2. Add six ounces of fresh butter, four eggs, one ounce of candied orange-peel, the same quantity of lemon-peel, and one ounce of citron
3. Mix all well together
4. Sweeten with white sugar, and bake in a quick oven
5. When nearly done, spread over the top the whites of the eggs well whisked
6. Return it to the oven
