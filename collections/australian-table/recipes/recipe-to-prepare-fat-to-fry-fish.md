## Ingredients

- ten pounds of beef suet
- five pounds of fresh butter
- three pounds of hog's lard
- two turnips
- four carrots
- an onion stuck with cloves
- a bunch of parsley
- half a dozen shalots
- a small bit of thyme
- two bay-leaves
- a little sweet basil
- baysalt
- whole pepper

## Directions

1. Carefully pick ten pounds of beef suet
2. When ready put it into an iron pan to melt
3. As soon as melted add five pounds of fresh butter and three pounds of hog's lard
4. The moment the whole is in a boiling state, throw in two turnips, four carrots, an onion stuck with cloves, a bunch of parsley, half a dozen shalots, a small bit of thyme, two bay-leaves, and a little sweet basil
5. Let the herbs and roots fry until they are dried up
6. Strain the melted fat through a sieve
7. When nearly cold, stir in some baysalt and some whole pepper
8. Put in jars for use
