## Ingredients

- flounders
- cold water
- salt
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash and clean them well, and cut the back side of them, the same as you do turbot
2. Put them into a kettle, with cold water and salt
3. When it comes to a boil, skim them
4. Let them simmer for five minutes, and they are ready
5. Serve eaten with plain melted butter
