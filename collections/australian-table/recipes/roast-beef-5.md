## Ingredients

- 10 pounds of sirloin
- fat
- flour
- salt
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Put the joint at first at a distance from the fire, which must be brisk and clear
2. As the meat warms, bring it nearer
3. During the time it is roasting baste it well
4. Keep the fat paper-covered
5. When nearly done dredge it with flour
6. A few minutes before taking it up, sprinkle some salt on it
7. Put horseradish round the dish
8. Serve with its own gravy, quite devoid of fat
