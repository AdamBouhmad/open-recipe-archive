## Ingredients

- 6 teaspoonsful of coffee, heaped up
- a very small teaspoonful of chicory, or none at all
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Pour enough boiling water into the upper part of the cafetiere
2. Add the specified proportions of coffee and chicory
3. Allow to brew
4. Pour the coffee into a cup
