## Ingredients

- lettuce or any other salad
- vinegar
- water
- pepper and salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Take the lettuce, or any other salad that is to be procured, and tear the leaves off from the stem
2. Put into a bowl equal quantities of vinegar and water, a tea-spoonful of pepper and salt, and four times as much oil as vinegar and water
3. Mix well together
