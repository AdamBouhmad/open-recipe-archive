## Ingredients

- fish
- bread crumbs or oatmeal
- egg
- clarified fat or salad oil

## Directions

1. Wash the fish thoroughly, and dry them well.
2. Prepare some bread crumbs, by rubbing stale bread through a colander; or oatmeal may be used instead.
3. Beat an egg up, and use it on the fish on both sides.
4. Put clarified fat or salad oil into the pan, and then put in the fish, which must literally boil in the fat.
5. When done, take them off and dry them; serve with a napkin laid on the dish, or without a napkin.
