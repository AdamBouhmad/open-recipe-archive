## Ingredients

- shin or leg of beef
- good vinegar
- onions (optional)
- other flavouring or vegetable substances (optional)

## Directions

1. Take shin or leg of beef and cut it into slices of two or three ounces each
2. Dip it in good vinegar
3. With or without onions, or any other flavouring or vegetable substances, put it into a stewpan without water
4. Let it stand on the hot hearth, or by a slow fire, for two, three, or four hours
5. The meat will be thoroughly done, will have yielded plenty of gravy, and be as tender as a chicken
6. Care must be taken that the heat is moderate
