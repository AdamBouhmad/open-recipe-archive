## Ingredients

- 1 Flathead
- 1/2 pint Brown Sauce
- 3 oz. Fish Forcemeat
- 1 oz. Dripping

## Directions

1. Make a forcemeat and sew it into the fish
2. Rub some dripping over a baking sheet, truss the fish into shape, and lay it on
3. Rub the rest of the dripping on to a piece of paper, cover the fish carefully, bake in rather a hot oven for half an hour or longer, according to size
4. Take off the paper, dish it, and pour round a nice brown sauce
