## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Boiled Fish
- 1/2 lb. Cold Boiled Potatoes
- Pepper and Salt
- Frying Fat
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 Egg
- 1 tablespoonful of Milk
- Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. Free the fish from skin and bone and flake it up; mash the potatoes smoothly, mix together and season with pepper and salt.
2. Put the milk and butter into a saucepan, and when it is quite hot put in the fish and the potatoes.
3. Beat up the egg, and put half in, and mix together till hot through; spread on to a plate and stand away to cool.
4. Add a teaspoonful each of water and oil to the egg.
5. Make some bread crumbs on a sieve, and put them on to a piece of paper.
6. Shape the fish mixture into cakes about one inch high and two inches across; brush them over with the egg, and toss them into the crumbs.
7. Shape again and fry in very hot fat, arrange in the form of a wheel on a dish paper, garnish with fresh or fried parsley, and serve hot.
