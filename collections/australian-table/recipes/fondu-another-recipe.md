## Ingredients

- Yolks of eight eggs
- A quarter of a pound of melted butter
- Half a pound of grated cheese of good flavour
- Whites of the eggs whisked to a froth

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of eight eggs together with a quarter of a pound of melted butter, and half a pound of grated cheese of good flavour
2. Mix the ingredients with the whites of the eggs whisked to a froth
3. Bake in small dishes
4. Serve hot
