## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful Jam
- 1 pint Water
- 1 oz. Sugar
- 1 teaspoonful Cornflour
- 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Put the water, jam, lemon juice, and sugar into a small saucepan and boil it for five minutes.
2. Mix the cornflour with a little cold water and pour it in; stir till it boils up.
3. Strain the jam out, and it is ready to serve; a few drops of cochineal improve the colour.
