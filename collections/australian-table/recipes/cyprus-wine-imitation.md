## Ingredients

- ten quarts of syrup of elderberry
- eighty pints of water
- two ounces of ginger
- two ounces of cloves
- a few bruised grapes

## Directions

1. To ten quarts of syrup of elderberry add eighty pints of water, two ounces of ginger, and two ounces of cloves, and boil together
2. Add a few bruised grapes and strain
