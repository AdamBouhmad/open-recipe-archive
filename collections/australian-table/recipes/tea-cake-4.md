## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Flour
- ½ pint Milk
- 2 oz. Butter
- 1 Egg
- 2 teaspoonsful Baking Powder
- 1 teaspoonful Sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour, stir in the sugar and baking powder.
2. Beat up the egg and milk, and mix the dry ingredients into a dough with them ; divide into two pieces and form each into a flat cake.
3. Cut lightly across into four with a knife, put on to a buttered tin, and bake twenty minutes.
4. Cut open, butter, and serve.
