## Ingredients

- pint of well-boiled peas or beans
- six spoonfuls of brown sauce
- three spoonfuls of brown gravy
- two onions
- parsley
- quarter of a pound of lean bacon
- ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Put a pint of well-boiled peas or beans into a stewpan, with six spoonfuls of brown sauce, three of brown gravy, two onions, and a little parsley
2. Let it boil ten minutes
3. Have ready about a quarter of a pound of lean bacon cut into pieces, add it to the peas or beans
4. Season with an ounce of butter, and stew fifteen minutes, and serve
