## Ingredients

- two ounces of currants
- four or five layers of bread and butter
- a custard

## Directions

1. Wash and clean two ounces of currants, get ready four or five layers of bread and butter
2. put the latter in the dish, and between the layers put the currants
3. over all put in a custard
4. Bake about three-quarters of an hour
