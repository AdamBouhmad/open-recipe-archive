## Ingredients

- salt fish
- cold water
- fresh water
- parsley
- lemon
- boiled parsnips
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Soak salt fish in cold water for twelve hours
2. Well wash in fresh water, scrape and clean
3. Lay it in a fish-kettle and cover with cold water
4. Simmer very gently from one hour to one hour and a half, according to the thickness of the fish
5. Dish on a serviette and garnish with sprigs of parsley and slices of lemon
6. Send it to table with boiled parsnips and egg sauce
