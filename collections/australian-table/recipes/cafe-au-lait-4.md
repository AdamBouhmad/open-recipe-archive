## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 teaspoonsful of coffee
- a sixth part of chicory
- 1 pint of water
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Pour enough boiling water into the upper part of the cafetiere
2. Add the specified proportions of coffee and chicory
3. Allow to brew
4. Half fill the cup with hot milk
5. Pour the coffee into the cup
