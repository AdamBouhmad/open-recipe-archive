## Ingredients

- thin nouilles paste
- beaten eggs
- white sugar
- a little fresh butter
- grated lemon-peel
- preserved cherries
- custard or cream
- brandy

## Directions

1. Make thin nouilles paste, cut into strips of about two inches wide
2. Leave it to dry
3. Boil the strips in a little water, and drain through a colander
4. When the water is strained off, mix it with beaten eggs, white sugar, a little fresh butter, and grated lemon-peel
5. Bake or boil in a shape lined with preserved cherries
6. When turned out, pour over a fine custard, or cream, flavoured with brandy, and sweetened to taste
