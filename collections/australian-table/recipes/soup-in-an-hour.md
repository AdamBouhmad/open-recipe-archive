## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef
- a carrot
- a turnip
- an onion
- some celery
- pepper
- salt
- water
- fine herbs or parsley and butter

## Directions

1. Chop two pounds of beef into pieces
2. Add a carrot, turnip, an onion, and some celery, all cut up
3. Put with the meat into a saucepan
4. Season it with pepper and salt and add a quantity of water
5. Let it boil an hour
6. Strain it through a tamis sieve
7. You can serve the meat with gravy, adding a few fine herbs, or parsley and butter
