## Ingredients

- two wine-glassfuls of Scotch whisky
- one wine-glassful of heather-honey
- one well-beaten egg

## Directions

1. Add two wine-glassfuls of Scotch whisky to a wine-glassful of heather-honey
2. Mix well, and stir in a well-beaten egg
