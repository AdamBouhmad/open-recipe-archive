## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- a pound of good sugar
- one ounce of best ginger
- three-quarters of a pound of lard or butter
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Add a pound of good sugar to two pounds of flour
2. Mix one ounce of best ginger well with the flour
3. Melt three-quarters of a pound of lard or butter
4. Beat four eggs well
5. Mix the lard and eggs together
6. Stir in the flour, which will form a paste
7. Roll out in thin cakes
8. Bake in a moderately-heated oven
