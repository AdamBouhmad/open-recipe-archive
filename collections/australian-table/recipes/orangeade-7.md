## Ingredients

- juice of an orange
- peel of an orange
- boiling water
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice of an orange
2. Pour boiling water on some of the peel, and cover it close
3. Boil water and sugar to a thin syrup and skim it
4. When all are cold, mix the juice, the infusion, and the syrup with as much water as will make a rich drink
5. Strain through a jelly-bag, and ice
