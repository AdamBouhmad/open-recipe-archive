## Ingredients

- 3 Whiting
- Pepper and Salt
- 1.5 oz. Butter
- 1 Lemon
- flour

## Directions

1. Wash the whiting, dry them in a cloth, mix a little flour, pepper, and salt together, cover the fish thoroughly with this.
2. Butter a tin dish, lay the whiting in and put the rest of the butter over them in small pieces, and put them into a hot oven; baste constantly with the butter.
3. This must not be allowed to get black; it should be brown.
4. When the whiting are done, which will be in from fifteen to twenty minutes, according to the thickness of the fish, place them in a hot dish and pour the butter in which they have been cooked over them.
