## Ingredients

- hare
- milk or cream
- butter
- hare liver
- flour
- red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Put the hare down to roast, away from the fire at first, but afterwards draw nearer
2. Keep eternally basting with milk or cream, and subsequently with butter
3. Mince the liver
4. Flour the hare well when nearly done, so as to raise a froth on the joint
5. Serve hot with red currant jelly
