## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint Milk
- 1 Egg
- 1 dessertspoonful Cornflour
- Sugar
- Flavouring

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a saucepan to boil
2. Mix the cornflour with a spoonful of cold milk
3. When the milk in the saucepan is nearly boiling, stir it in and continue stirring till it boils
4. Let it boil two or three minutes
5. Draw the saucepan away from the fire, beat in the yolk of the egg and flavouring
6. Put back on the fire and bring to boiling point
