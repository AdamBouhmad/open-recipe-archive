## Ingredients

- rind of three or four lemons
- rind of eight Seville oranges
- three pounds of sugar-candy, powdered
- one gallon of brandy

## Directions

1. Peel the rind of lemons and oranges thin
2. Powder the sugar-candy
3. Steep the whole in a gallon of brandy for four or five days, stirring it frequently
4. Run it through a filterer to clear it
