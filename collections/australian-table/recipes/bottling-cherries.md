## Ingredients

- cherries
- six ounces of loaf sugar to every pound of fruit

## Directions

1. To every pound of fruit for tarts through the winter, add six ounces of loaf sugar
2. Fill the jars with fruit
3. Shake the sugar over, and tie down with two bladders, as there is danger of one bursting through the boiling
4. Place the jars in a boiler of cold water
5. After the water has boiled, let them remain three hours
6. Take them out, and, when cool, put them in a dry place, where they will keep over a year
