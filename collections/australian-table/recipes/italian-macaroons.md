## Ingredients

- a pound of Jordan or Valencia almonds
- whites of four eggs
- two pounds and a half of the best white sugar
- whites of six more eggs
- slips of blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Blanch a pound of Jordan or Valencia almonds
2. Pound them fine with the whites of four eggs
3. Add two pounds and a half of the best white sugar
4. Pound all together
5. Add the whites of six more eggs
6. Add slips of blanched almonds on the top of each macaroon
7. Bake
