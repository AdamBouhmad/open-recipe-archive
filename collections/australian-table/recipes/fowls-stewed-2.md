## Ingredients

- fowls
- Bechamel sauce
- cream
- white pepper
- salt
- onion
- ham or bacon
- lemon or bread sippets

## Directions

1. Divide fowls in joints
2. Stew in Bechamel sauce or done in cream with white pepper, salt, and an onion cut in shreds
3. Serve the dish garnished with slices of ham or bacon, and lemon or bread sippets
