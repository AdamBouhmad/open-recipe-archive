## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- a pound of flour
- half a pound of currants
- half a pound of sugar
- one egg
- a quarter of a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of butter into a pound of flour
2. Put in half a pound of currants, half a pound of sugar, and one egg
3. Mix all together with a quarter of a pint of milk
4. Cut them into round cakes
5. Bake
