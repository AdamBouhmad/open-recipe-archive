## Ingredients

- part of a pumpkin
- some water
- about half a quarter of a pound of butter
- a little salt
- a quart of milk
- bread

## Directions

1. Take part of a pumpkin, pare off the skin and remove the seeds
2. Cut it into small pieces, and place them in a stewpan on the fire, with some water
3. When the pumpkin is pulped, put in about half a quarter of a pound of butter, and a little salt
4. Boil a quart of milk, and mix it with the pumpkin pulp
5. Put some bread, cut in thin slices, in the tureen, and pour the soup hot over it
