## Ingredients

- ginger, powdered
- curry-powder
- mustard
- salad oil
- cayenne pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix ginger, powdered, curry-powder, and mustard, of each two ounces, well mixed
2. Dilute with three or four table-spoonfuls of salad oil
3. Add a little cayenne pepper, and the requisite quantity of vinegar
4. Pour it warm on the article that you are desirous of pickling
