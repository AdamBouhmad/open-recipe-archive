## Ingredients

- 1 bundle Rhubarb
- 6 oz. Sugar
- 1/4 lb. Sago
- 1 pint Water

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut up the rhubarb and put it on to boil with one gill water
2. Boil for about ten minutes
3. Wash the sago and soak it in one gill warm water
4. Add to the rhubarb
5. Stir in also the sugar and boil for about ten minutes or longer, stirring constantly
6. Pour into a basin or mould which has been dipped in cold water
7. Stand away till cold and firm
8. Turn out and serve
