## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Roast Beef
- 8 Tomatoes
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 gill Gravy
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful Sugar
- Salt and Pepper
- Toast

## Directions

1. Slice up the tomatoes and put them into a saucepan with the butter, herbs, pepper, salt, sugar, and gravy.
2. Stir about until it becomes quite a pulp; then rub through a sieve, season to taste, and let it get quite cold.
3. Cut the beef into thin slices and lay it in a saucepan, pour over the cold sauce and let it get hot through, very slowly.
4. Arrange on a hot dish, and garnish with fried sippets of bread or toast.
