## Ingredients

- three eggs, well beaten
- one shalot
- parsley
- a little ham or bacon
- a little cayenne
- salt to taste
- two ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Chop the shalot, parsley, and ham or bacon very fine all together
2. Mix with the well beaten eggs, cayenne, and salt to taste
3. Put two ounces of butter into the frying-pan
4. When it boils pour in the batter and fry
5. Fry for about five minutes
6. Serve with brown gravy, vinegar, and cayenne
