## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1½ oz. Ground Rice
- 1 oz. Sugar
- Flavouring

## Directions

1. Put the milk on to boil with a strip of lemon peel in it ; when nearly boiling mix the rice in a spoonful of cold milk and pour it in.
2. Stir till it boils, and let it boil two or three minutes ; pour into a mould which has been dipped in cold water, and stand away till firm.
3. Turn out when cold, and serve with jam, stewed fruit, or custard sauce.
