## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- two pounds of treacle
- half a pound of candied orange-peel
- an ounce and a half of ginger
- an ounce of ground allspice
- carraways
- corianders
- three-quarters of a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour, three-quarters of a pound of sugar, two pounds of treacle, half a pound of candied orange-peel cut small, an ounce and a half of ginger, an ounce of ground allspice, carraways, and corianders mixed, and three-quarters of a pound of butter, oiled
2. Mix all together, and set it aside for six hours
3. Roll it out in pieces the size of a walnut
4. Press them flat and bake
