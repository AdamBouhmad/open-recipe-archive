## Ingredients

- one pound of currants
- one pound of raisins
- one pound of sugar
- a little citron
- ground cloves
- cinnamon
- eight apples
- rich paste
- one ounce of fresh butter
- a tea-cupful of clarified sugar
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a pound of currants, the same quantity of raisins and sugar, a little citron, ground cloves and cinnamon, with eight apples, finely chopped.
2. Mix all together.
3. Have ready a rich paste; cut into small triangles.
4. Fill them with the fruit, like puffs, and lay them in a deep dish.
5. Let the pieces be placed closely.
6. When the dish is full, pour over one ounce of fresh butter, melted in a tea-cupful of clarified sugar, flavoured with essence of lemon.
7. Bake in an oven not too brisk.
