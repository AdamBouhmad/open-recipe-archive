## Ingredients

- one bottle of light hock
- about a dozen sprigs of woodruffe
- a quarter of an orange
- about two ounces of powdered sugar
- a bottle of sparkling wine added to four or five bottles of still hock
- ice

## Directions

1. Take with each bottle of light hock about a dozen sprigs of woodruffe and a quarter of an orange cut in small slices
2. Add about two ounces of powdered sugar
3. Remove the herbs after having been in the wine half an hour or longer, according to taste
4. Add a bottle of sparkling wine added to four or five bottles of still hock as a great improvement
5. Add a little ice
