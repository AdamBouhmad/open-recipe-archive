## Ingredients

- 1 lb. New Potatoes
- 1 oz. Butter
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes and put them into boiling salt and water, and boil for ten minutes.
2. Take up, peel and cut them in half.
3. Melt the butter in a saucepan, and when quite hot, put in the potatoes and toss over the fire.
4. Sprinkle over the parsley, pepper, salt, and a few drops of lemon juice; cover down and cook gently till the potatoes are soft but not broken.
5. Put into a hot dish and serve.
