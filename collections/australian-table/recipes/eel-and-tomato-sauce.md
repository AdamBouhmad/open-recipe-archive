## Ingredients

- 1 Eel
- 6 Tomatoes
- 2 oz. Veal Seasoning
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 oz. Flour
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Stuff and cook the eel as in the preceding recipe, and strain off the liquor
2. Rub the tomatoes through a sieve
3. Mix with half a pint of the liquor in which the fish was cooked
4. Make a sauce of this with the butter and flour, season with pepper and salt, and pour it over the fish
5. Garnish with parsley, and serve
