## Ingredients

- one bottle of hock
- about half a pint of highly-flavoured strawberries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take to each bottle of hock about half a pint of highly-flavoured strawberries
2. Add powdered sugar
3. Take the fruit with the wine after having been in it about an hour
