## Ingredients

- tripe
- onions
- sweet herbs
- pint of milk or stock
- ounce of butter
- flour
- white pepper
- salt
- spice

## Directions

1. Put the tripe into a stewpan, with onions whole (at least a dozen), and some sweet herbs
2. Add a pint of milk or stock, and let it simmer for a time
3. Add an ounce of butter mixed with flour, white pepper, salt, and a little spice
