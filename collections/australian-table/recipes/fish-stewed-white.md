## Ingredients

- an onion
- a little oil
- half a pint of water
- fish
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- ground allspice
- nutmeg
- ginger
- the beaten yolks of four eggs
- the juice of two lemons
- a tea-spoonful of flour
- a table-spoonful of cold water
- a little saffron
- the liver and the roe
- chopped parsley
- warm butter
- crumbs of bread
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put an onion, finely chopped, into a stewpan, with a little oil, till the onion becomes brown
2. Add half a pint of water, and place the fish in the stewpan, seasoning with pepper, salt, and mace, ground allspice, nutmeg, and ginger
3. Let it stew gently until the fish is done
4. Prepare the beaten yolks of four eggs, with the juice of two lemons, a tea-spoonful of flour, a table-spoonful of cold water, and a little saffron
5. Mix well in a cup, and pour it into the stewpan, stirring it carefully one way until it thickens
6. Balls should be thrown in about twenty minutes before serving
7. For the balls: Take a little of the fish, the liver and the roe, if there is any, beat it up finely with chopped parsley, and spread warm butter, crumbs of bread, and seasoning according to taste
8. Form this into a paste with eggs, and make it into balls of a moderate size
