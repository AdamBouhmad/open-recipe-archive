## Ingredients

- a slice of bread
- butter
- a slice of mellow cheese
- mustard

## Directions

1. Toast a slice of bread on both sides, and butter it
2. Toast a slice of mellow cheese on one side, and lay that next the bread
3. Toast the other side with a salamander
4. Rub mustard over
5. Serve hot and covered
