## Ingredients

- Parsley
- Fat

## Directions

1. Pick the top or flower of parsley carefully and rub well in a damp cloth, and then in a dry cloth
2. Put into a frying basket and plunge into the fat when the fish, or whatever it is to be served with, has been fried
3. Leave it in not more than one minute
4. Turn it on to some kitchen paper and stand for a minute on the stove to dry
5. It is then ready
