## Ingredients

- Old beer, two quarts
- White wine, one quart
- Anchovies, four ounces
- Peeled shalot, three ounces
- Mace, half an ounce
- Nutmegs
- Ginger
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Take old beer two quarts and white wine one quart with anchovies four ounces
2. Mix and heat to the boiling point
3. Remove from the fire
4. Add three ounces of peeled shalot, half an ounce of mace, nutmegs, ginger, and black pepper
5. Macerate for fourteen days
6. Strain
