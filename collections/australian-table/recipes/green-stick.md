## Ingredients

- one pint of biscuit-powder
- a little brown sugar
- grated lemon-peel
- six eggs
- three-quarters of a pound of warmed fresh butter
- four apples
- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds blanched and chopped
- half a pound of stoned raisins
- a little nutmeg grated
- half a pound of coarse brown sugar
- a glass of white wine or a little brandy
- brown sugar-and-water syrup
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Make into a stiff paste one pint of biscuit-powder, a little brown sugar, grated lemon-peel, six eggs, and three-quarters of a pound of warmed fresh butter
2. Prepare four apples chopped finely, a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds blanched and chopped, half a pound of stoned raisins, a little nutmeg grated, half a pound of coarse brown sugar, and a glass of white wine, or a little brandy
3. Mix the above ingredients together
4. Put them on a slow fire, to simmer for half an hour
5. Place in a dish to cool
6. Make the paste into the form of small dumplings
7. Fill them with the fruit
8. Bake them
9. When put in the oven, pour over a syrup of brown sugar-and-water, flavoured with lemon-juice
