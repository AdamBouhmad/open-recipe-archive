## Ingredients

- red currants
- table-spoonful of water
- a pound of sugar to every pint of juice

## Directions

1. Put the fruit on the fire or hot plate, with a table-spoonful of water at the bottom of the pan, and boil for twenty to twenty-five minutes
2. Pass the juice through a flannel jelly bag
3. To every pint of juice add a pound of sugar of the best kind
4. Return it to the pan, and boil quickly for a little better than ten minutes, or until the preserve sticks to the spoon
5. Run it into pots or jelly glasses
6. Cover with paper, steeped in spirits of any kind, and use bladder for top covering
