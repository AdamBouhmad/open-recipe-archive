## Ingredients

- Calves' feet
- Salad dressing or oil and vinegar
- White lettuce
- Sliced beetroot

## Directions

1. Boil calves' feet very thoroughly for at least eight hours.
2. Strain off the stock and remove the bones.
3. Put the meat on one side till quite cold.
4. Cut up into neat pieces and put into a salad bowl.
5. Pour over a salad dressing or just oil and vinegar.
6. Shred over it a nice white lettuce.
7. Garnish with sliced beetroot.
