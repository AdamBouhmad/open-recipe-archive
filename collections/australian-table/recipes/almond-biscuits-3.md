## Ingredients

- four ounces of sweet almonds
- two ounces of bitter almonds
- one pound of loaf sugar
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Blanch and beat almonds till smooth
2. Add one pound of loaf sugar
3. Mix all together
4. Beat the white of an egg to a froth and mix it with the rest
5. Bake in lumps on wafer-paper to prevent their sticking
6. A quick oven is necessary
7. They should be done of a light brown
