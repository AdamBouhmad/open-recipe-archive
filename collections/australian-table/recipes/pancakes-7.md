## Ingredients

- three spoonfuls of flour
- three eggs, well beaten
- a little salt
- small sprinkling of ginger

## Directions

1. Make a light batter of flour, eggs, salt, and ginger
2. Fry in thin layers in a pan, and turn
3. Roll and serve with sugar and lemon
