## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal
- a calf's foot
- one pound of chorissa
- a large fowl
- four quarts of water
- a piece of fresh lemon-peel
- six Jerusalem artichokes
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- salt
- white pepper
- a nutmeg
- a blade of mace
- flour or ground rice

## Directions

1. Stew a knuckle of veal, a calf's foot, one pound of chorissa, and a large fowl, in four quarts of water
2. Add a piece of fresh lemon-peel, six Jerusalem artichokes, a bunch of sweet herbs, a little salt and white pepper, a nutmeg, and a blade of mace
3. When the fowl is thoroughly done remove the white parts to preserve for thickening, and let the rest continue stewing till the stock is sufficiently strong
4. Pound the white parts of the fowl and sprinkle with flour or ground rice, and stir in the soup after it has been strained, until it thickens
