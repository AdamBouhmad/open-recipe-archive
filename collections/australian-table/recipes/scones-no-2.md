## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 pint sour milk
- 3 teaspoonsful baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt together, mix into a very light dough with the milk, adding a little more milk if necessary; turn on to a floured board and knead till smooth, roll out half an inch thick.
2. Cut into small rounds and bake for about five minutes.
