## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 Bream
- 1 gill Milk or Melted Butter
- Short Pastry
- Pepper and Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cold fish will do very well for this dish. If fresh is used, fillet it and cut into small pieces; if cooked, flake up into small pieces.
2. Lay in a buttered pie-dish, season with pepper, salt, and chopped parsley.
3. Pour over the sauce and cover with a short pastry made with 0.5 lb. flour and 0.5 lb. dripping.
4. Brush over with egg or milk, and bake for three-quarters of an hour; garnish with parsley, and serve.
