## Ingredients

- 1 pound gravy beef
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very small
2. Put it into a brown baking jar and cover down with a closely-fitting lid or with brown paper
3. Stand in a saucepan of boiling water for one hour
4. Pour off the essence and add a little salt
