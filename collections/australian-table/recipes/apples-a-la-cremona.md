## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar (half their weight)
- lemon-peel
- three or four table-spoonfuls of cider or perry

## Directions

1. Cut the apples into small squares, and strewed over with half their weight in sugar and a few pieces of lemon-peel
2. After remaining covered up until the next day, lightly drop into a small stewpan, with three or four table-spoonfuls of cider or perry
3. Simmer gently until they become clear
4. Take them out, and when cold build in a wall-like form round a small china or glass dish
5. Pour the syrup into the middle, and place the strips of lemon on the top
