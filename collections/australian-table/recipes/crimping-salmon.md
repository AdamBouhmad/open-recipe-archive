## Ingredients

- salmon
- cold spring water
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Stun the fish by a blow on the head
2. Cut cross-wise just below the gills
3. Crimp by cutting to the bone on each side, so as almost to divide into slices
4. Put into cold spring water for ten minutes
5. Put slice by slice into a pot of salt and water, boiling furiously, allowing time for the water to recover its heat after the throwing in of each slice
6. Leave the head out, and throw in the thickest pieces first
