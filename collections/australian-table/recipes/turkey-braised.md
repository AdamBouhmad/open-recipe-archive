## Ingredients

- turkey
- slices of bacon
- chopped carrots
- onions
- sweet herbs
- salt
- pepper
- allspice
- mace

## Directions

1. Truss and stuff the turkey in the usual manner
2. Cover the bottom of a stewpan with slices of bacon, chopped carrots, onions, sweet herbs, salt, pepper, allspice, and mace
3. Lay the turkey on these ingredients with its breast downwards
4. Place a layer of the same materials over the bird
5. Cover the top close and put the pan in an oven or on a hot-plate for three or four hours until done
6. Let the dish gently simmer in its own steam until mellow and tender
