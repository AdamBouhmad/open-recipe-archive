## Ingredients

- slice of bacon or ham
- four or five pounds of gravy beef
- carrot
- onion
- couple of cloves
- head of celery
- quart of water

## Directions

1. Put the bacon or ham, gravy beef cut into pieces, carrot, onion, cloves, and celery into a stewpan
2. Put a quart of water to it, or a little more
3. Let it simmer for three hours
4. Strain through a tamis
