## Ingredients

- Dry crusts of bread
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 tablespoonful jam
- 2 eggs
- 15 oz. sugar
- Grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in cold water till quite soft, put it into a cloth and squeeze all the water out of it; turn into a basin and beat it smooth with a spoon.
2. Beat in the yolks of the eggs, sugar, milk, and a little grated lemon peel.
3. Pour into a pie dish and bake till quite firm, then take from the oven and spread the jam on the top.
4. Whip the whites to a stiff froth and spread over the jam; put back in the oven for a few minutes till brown, then sprinkle with sugar and serve either hot or cold.
