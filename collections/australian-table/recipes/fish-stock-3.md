## Ingredients

- Fish bones
- Vegetables
- Peppercorns
- Teaspoonful of salt
- 1 dozen white peppercorns
- A fagot of herbs
- One onion
- One carrot

## Directions

1. Wash the bones well in cold water and cut away any black substance adhering to them
2. Break them up and put into a saucepan with a teaspoonful of salt
3. When it boils remove the scum and put in 1 dozen white peppercorns, a fagot of herbs, one onion, and one carrot
4. Boil steadily for two hours or longer
5. Strain through a sieve into a basin
