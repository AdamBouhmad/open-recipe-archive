## Ingredients

- 1 pint Haricot Beans
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1/2 lb. Bacon
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Soak the haricot beans and boil them by directions already given.
2. Rub them through a wire sieve.
3. The bacon should be in thin rashers and very fat. Cook it carefully in a small clean frying-pan, and as the fat runs from it, pour it on the beans.
4. Mash them up with this and a little pepper and salt, and put them into a hot dish.
5. Sprinkle over with parsley and lay the bacon rashers on top.
6. Serve hot.
