## Ingredients

- a shalot
- chervil
- chives
- a head of garlic
- salt and pepper
- mustard
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put into a sauceboat a shalot, chervil, chives, a head of garlic, all chopped very fine
2. Add salt and pepper
3. Beat it up with mustard, oil, and vinegar
