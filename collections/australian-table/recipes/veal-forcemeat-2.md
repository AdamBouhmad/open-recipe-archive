## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Suet (Beef)
- 3 oz. Bread Crumbs
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 Egg
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 0.5 teaspoonful Sweet Herbs
- Half a Lemon

## Directions

1. Shred the suet and mix it with the bread crumbs
2. Chop the parsley and sweet herbs very finely and stir them in
3. Stir in the grated rind of half a lemon, and the pepper and salt
4. Drop in the egg and bind into a paste
5. It is ready to use
