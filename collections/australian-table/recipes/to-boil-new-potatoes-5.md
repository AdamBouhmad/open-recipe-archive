## Ingredients

- New potatoes
- Salt
- Water
- Mint

## Directions

1. New potatoes may be either scraped while raw, or peeled after boiling; they are a better flavour if cooked in their skins.
2. Well wash in cold water, plunge into boiling water seasoned with salt and a sprig of mint.
3. Boil quickly until a fork will go through easily; then strain off the water, dry, and serve.
