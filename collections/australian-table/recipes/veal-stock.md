## Ingredients

- Knuckle of veal
- Peppercorns and vegetables
- Cold water
- Dessertspoonful of salt
- 2 dozen white peppercorns
- One onion stuck with six cloves
- A fagot of herbs made with sprigs of parsley, marjoram, and thyme, tied up with a bay or peach leaf

## Directions

1. Have the butcher chop the bones very small
2. Cut the meat across in several places and lay it in a very clean stock pot
3. Cover well with cold water and bring to the boil slowly
4. Put in a dessertspoonful of salt and skim very carefully
5. Draw away from the fire and place it where it will boil steadily
6. Put in 2 dozen white peppercorns, one onion stuck with six cloves, and a fagot of herbs
7. Boil steadily for six hours and strain off
