## Ingredients

- a slice of bread about an inch thick
- butter
- cayenne pepper
- ginger
- a pint of warm ale
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cut a slice of bread about an inch thick, toast and butter it
2. Sprinkle it with cayenne pepper and ginger and place in the bottom of a jug
3. Add a pint of warm ale and sugar to taste
