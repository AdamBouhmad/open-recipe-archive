## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Chops
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- 1 gill Gravy
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Trim some of the fat from the chops, put the butter into a saucepan, and when it is melted stir in the flour.
2. Mix well, and pour in the gravy; stir until it boils.
3. Lay in the chops, and simmer very gently for one hour.
4. Dish the chops in a circle, boil up and season the gravy, and pour over the stew.
