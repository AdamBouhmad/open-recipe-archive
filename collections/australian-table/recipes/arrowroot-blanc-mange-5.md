## Ingredients

- a quart of new milk
- a quarter of a pound of arrowroot
- a little cinnamon water or lemon

## Directions

1. To a quart of new milk add a quarter of a pound of arrowroot, sweetened to taste
2. Add a little cinnamon water or lemon for flavour
3. Stir it well on the fire, and boil for about fifteen minutes
4. Pour it into a mould to cool
5. When served add milk, cream, jam, or a custard over it
