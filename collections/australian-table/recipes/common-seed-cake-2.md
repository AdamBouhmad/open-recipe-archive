## Ingredients

- Two pounds and a half of flour
- Half a pound of white sugar
- Half a pint of lukewarm milk
- Two tablespoonfuls of yeast
- Half a pound of fresh butter
- An ounce of carraway seeds
- Warm milk to make it a middling stiffness

## Directions

1. Take two pounds and a half of flour, and half a pound of white sugar; put it into a bowl, and make a cavity in the centre, and pour in half a pint of lukewarm milk, and two tablespoonfuls of yeast
2. Set it in a warm place for an hour
3. Melt to an oil half a pound of fresh butter, and add to the other ingredients, with an ounce of carraway seeds, and warm milk to make it a middling stiffness
4. Put it into a round tin, and allow it to rise again a short time
5. Bake
