## Ingredients

- 1 ounce vermicelli
- 2 quarts bone stock
- 3 or 4 threads of saffron
- 1 onion or leek
- 6 cloves
- 1 dozen white peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Put the stock into a saucepan with three or four threads of saffron, an onion or leek stuck with six cloves, 1 dozen white peppercorns and some salt, and boil all together for half an hour
2. Strain out the vegetables and put it back into the saucepan
3. It should be of a bright straw colour; if it is not, a thread more saffron may be added before straining
4. Put in the vermicelli broken small, and simmer for twenty minutes
5. It is then ready to serve
