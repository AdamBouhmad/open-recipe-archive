## Ingredients

- loin of mutton
- veal stuffing
- brown gravy
- port wine

## Directions

1. Take the skin off a loin of mutton with the flap on
2. Bone it neatly
3. Fill the inside of the loin, where the bones were removed, with veal stuffing
4. Roll it up tight, skewer the flap, and bind it together
5. Put the outside skin over it till nearly roasted, and then remove it that the mutton may brown
6. Serve with brown gravy, with a little port wine in it
