## Ingredients

- cayenne
- lemon-peel
- celery
- ginger
- allspice
- nutmeg
- cloves
- mace
- cinnamon
- marjoram
- herbs of any kind
- rectified spirits or brandy

## Directions

1. Infuse the different ingredients in rectified spirits or brandy
2. Allow them to steep for a month or more
3. Strain the liquor for use
4. The various articles must be good of their kind, and not adulterated
