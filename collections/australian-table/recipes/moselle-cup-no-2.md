## Ingredients

- one bottle of still or sparkling Moselle
- one bottle of soda-water
- a glass of sherry or brandy
- a pint of fresh strawberries or three or four peaches or nectarines
- the peel of half a lemon
- powdered sugar
- lumps of clear ice

## Directions

1. To each bottle of still or sparkling Moselle add one bottle of soda-water
2. Add a glass of sherry or brandy
3. Substitute a pint of fresh strawberries, or three or four peaches or nectarines for the pineapple
4. Add the peel of half a lemon cut very thin and powdered sugar according to taste
5. Let the whole stand about an hour
6. Before serving add some lumps of clear ice
