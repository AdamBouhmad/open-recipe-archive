## Ingredients

- three ounces of cream of tartar
- one ounce of bruised ginger
- one pound and a half of white sugar
- one ounce of lemon-juice
- one gallon and a half of boiling water
- two table-spoons of yeast

## Directions

1. Take three ounces of cream of tartar, an ounce of bruised ginger, a pound and a half of white sugar, an ounce of lemon-juice
2. Pour a gallon and a half of boiling water on them, and, when cool, add two table-spoons of yeast
3. Strain and bottle
