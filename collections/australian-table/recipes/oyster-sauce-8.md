## Ingredients

- three dozen bearded oysters
- half a pint of melted butter
- milk
- mace
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Put three dozen bearded oysters into half a pint of melted butter
2. Let them just come to the boiling point
3. put the whole into a basin
4. Strain the liquor from them
5. add sufficient milk and a little mace and cayenne
6. Set by fire to become hot, but not to boil
