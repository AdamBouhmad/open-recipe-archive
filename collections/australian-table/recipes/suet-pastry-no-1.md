## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 10 oz. beef suet
- 0.5 pint water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin, and make it into a firm paste with the water.
2. Free the suet from skin, and put it twice through a sausage machine.
3. Roll the paste out, and put half over it in very tiny pieces; sprinkle with flour and fold into three.
4. Double the ends over till they meet, roll out again, and put on the rest of the suet and proceed as before.
5. It is then ready for use, but is much improved by standing for an hour in a cold place.
6. This is a very wholesome pastry, and particularly nice for meat pies.
