## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Flour
- 1 gill Tepid Water
- White of Egg
- 1 dessertspoonful Oil

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin, pour over it the oil, then the water, and beat into a smooth batter
2. Stand away for an hour, if possible in a cool place
3. Whip the white of the egg to a stiff froth, and stir it in
4. It is ready to use
