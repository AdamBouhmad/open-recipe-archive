## Ingredients

- four ounces of Scotch barley
- four ounces of sliced onions
- five quarts of water
- two ounces of beef or mutton dripping
- three ounces of fat bacon
- four ounces of oatmeal
- black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of Scotch barley and four ounces of sliced onions into five quarts of water
2. Boil gently for one hour
3. Pour it into a pan
4. Put into the saucepan two ounces of beef or mutton dripping, and three ounces of fat bacon
5. When melted, stir into it four ounces of oatmeal
6. Rub these until you make a paste
7. Add the paste to the barley broth
8. Season with black pepper, salt, &c.
