## Ingredients

- four pigeons
- bread
- bacon
- thyme
- parsley
- onion
- lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- egg
- stock
- butter
- flour
- forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Truss four pigeons as for boiling
2. Grate some bread
3. Scrape some bacon, or cut it into small slices
4. Chop some thyme, parsley, and onion, and some lemon-peel
5. Grate some nutmeg, and season with pepper and salt
6. Mix it up with an egg
7. Put this forcemeat into the craw of the pigeons
8. Lard the breasts, and fry them brown
9. Place them in a stewpan, with some stock, and simmer them for three-quarters of an hour
10. Thicken the gravy with a piece of butter rolled into some flour
11. Serve with forcemeat balls, and pour the gravy over the pigeons
