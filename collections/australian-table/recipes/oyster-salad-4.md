## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Oysters
- 1 Lettuce
- Half a Lemon
- Mayonnaise or Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Strain away the liquor from a bottle of oysters; put it into a saucepan, and when it boils put in the oysters and cook for five minutes; let them get cold in the liquor.
2. Wash and break up the lettuce and put some at the bottom of a bowl.
3. Strain the liquor from the oysters and mix a little with the dressing, stir in the oysters and spread over the lettuce.
4. Cover with more lettuce and garnish with slices of lemon and red radishes.
