## Ingredients

- two wine-glassfuls of port
- two wine-glassfuls of walnut pickle
- four wine-glassfuls of ketchup
- half a dozen pounded anchovies
- a few shalots sliced and pounded
- a table-spoonful of soy
- half a drachm of cayenne

## Directions

1. Let these ingredients simmer for ten minutes
2. Strain it into bottles for use
