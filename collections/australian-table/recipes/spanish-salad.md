## Ingredients

- lettuce or other salad
- vinegar
- water
- tea-spoonful of pepper and salt
- oil
- fine herbs, especially tarragon and chervil

## Directions

1. Take the lettuce, or any other salad that is to be procured, and tear the leaves off from the stem
2. Put into a bowl equal quantities of vinegar and water, a tea-spoonful of pepper and salt, and four times as much oil as vinegar and water
3. Mix well together
4. Chop small on a plate any fine herbs that can be got, especially tarragon and chervil
5. Immediately before eating pour the sauce over the salad and herbs
