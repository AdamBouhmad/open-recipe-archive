## Ingredients

- a pound and a quarter of bitter almonds
- half a pound of sweet almonds, blanched
- nine pounds of loaf sugar
- six pints of water
- rinds of three lemons
- orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Pound the almonds in a mortar with the sugar
2. Add the water a little by degrees
3. Put the mixture on the fire with the lemon-peel
4. After a boil pour off the syrup and press the almonds, to extract the milk
5. Add the milk to the syrup
6. Strain the whole through a sieve
7. When cold add a little orange-flower water
8. Bottle the mixture
9. The orgeat is used as a summer drink, mixed with water, according to taste
