## Ingredients

- Slices of Corned Beef
- 1 Lettuce
- 2 Eggs
- Mayonnaise or Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Take some slices of cold corned beef, dip them in a salad dressing, and lay them in a dish with alternate layers of lettuce leaves.
2. Garnish with hard boiled eggs cut in slices.
