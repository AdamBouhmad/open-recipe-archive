## Ingredients

- sugar, four ounces
- sack, a pint
- nutmeg
- eggs, ten
- milk, a quart

## Directions

1. Heat sugar and sack over flaming coals until the sack dissolves the sugar
2. Over such another fire put ten eggs
3. Stir them with steady hand
4. Boil milk and when cold, put milk and sack to eggs
5. Unite them firmly
6. Put them together on the fire until Miss sing twice, 'You must not kiss and tell'
7. Each lad and lass take up a silver spoon and fall on fiercely
