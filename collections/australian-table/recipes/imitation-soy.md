## Ingredients

- one pound of salt
- two pounds of dark brown sugar
- two pints of boiling water
- a quarter of a pint of essence of anchovies
- a few herbs

## Directions

1. Fry and brown the salt and dark brown sugar over a fire
2. Add two pints of boiling water, a quarter of a pint of essence of anchovies, and a few herbs
3. Boil
4. When cold, bottle
