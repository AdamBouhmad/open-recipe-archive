## Ingredients

- four pounds of fine flour
- one quart of lukewarm milk
- salt
- one quarter of a pound of melted butter
- three beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the milk and butter together
2. Add the beaten eggs
3. Handle as little as possible
4. Let the dough rise
5. Mould it into shape
6. Bake in a quick oven
