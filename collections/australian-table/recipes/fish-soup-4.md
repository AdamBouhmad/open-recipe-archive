## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of fish
- two quarts of veal, beef, or mutton broth
- a few slices of lean ham
- two or three carrots
- onions
- heads of celery
- some sweet herbs
- salt
- cayenne
- a piece of butter
- some flour
- two glassfuls of white wine
- a tablespoonful of garlic vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut fish into pieces and put into a stewpan with two quarts of veal, beef, or mutton broth
2. Add a few slices of lean ham, two or three carrots, onions, and heads of celery, some sweet herbs, salt, and cayenne
3. Stew the fish until it will pass through a coarse sieve
4. Return it to the stewpan with a piece of butter and some flour to thicken
5. Add two glassfuls of white wine and a tablespoonful of garlic vinegar
