## Ingredients

- veal
- stuffing
- force-meat
- melted butter
- bacon or ham
- lemon

## Directions

1. Roast the veal and continually bast it
2. The fillet will require stuffing and force-meat
3. Paper the kidney and fat, serve hot on toast for the veal gourmet
4. Roast the shoulder or breast
5. Parboil the sweetbread before roasting
6. Put good melted butter over it
7. Ensure it is well browned
8. Accompany with a little bacon or ham, either as a garnish or separate dish
9. A little lemon is an improvement
