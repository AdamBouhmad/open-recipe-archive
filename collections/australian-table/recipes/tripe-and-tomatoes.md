## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Tripe
- 1 doz. Tomatoes
- ½ pint Water or Stock
- 1 oz. Cornflour
- ½ Onion
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the tripe into neat pieces, put it on in cold water and bring to the boil.
2. Let it boil for five minutes, put it into cold water, and wash and scrape it well.
3. Slice up the tomatoes and rub them through a sieve.
4. Mix them with the stock or water, and season with pepper and salt.
5. Pour this into a saucepan, slice in the onion, put in the tripe, and let it boil up.
6. Simmer gently for four hours.
7. Mix the cornflour smoothly with a little water or stock, and pour it in.
8. Stir until it boils, dish the tripe carefully, season and flavour the sauce to taste, and pour it over.
