## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- four ounces of butter
- two pounds of flour
- three table-spoonfuls of yeast
- two eggs well beaten

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pint of milk
2. Add flour, yeast, and eggs
3. Let rise
4. Knead them lightly, and make into cakes
5. Let them rise on the tins
6. Bake in a moderately heated oven
