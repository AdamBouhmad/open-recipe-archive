## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. Flour
- 1/4 lb. Cornflour
- 1 gill Milk
- 1/4 teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 2 oz. Butter
- 1 Egg
- Flavouring
- 2 oz. Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, cornflour, and baking powder together
2. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
3. Beat in the egg, flavouring, and milk
4. Beat in the flour, &c., and continue to beat for five minutes
5. Butter some small bun tins, half fill them with the mixture
6. Put into a moderate oven and bake for about twenty minutes
7. Stand on a sieve till cold
