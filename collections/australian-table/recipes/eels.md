## Ingredients

- eels
- beef gravy or stock, about a quart
- flour
- two table-spoonfuls of port wine
- one table-spoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. Clean them well, wipe them dry, and fry them until brown
2. Have ready some beef gravy, or stock, about a quart; put the eels into it when cold, and let it simmer for about fifteen minutes
3. Take them out of the stewpan, and place them in a deep dish
4. Thicken the sauce with flour, and put into it two table-spoonfuls of port wine, and one of ketchup
5. Let it simmer for about ten minutes
6. Pour on the fish
