## Ingredients

- whites of eight eggs
- half a pound of white sugar
- essence
- fine preserved sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk the whites of eight eggs up to a strong froth
2. Stir in half a pound of white sugar, as lightly as possible
3. Flavour them with any essence you please
4. Lay them on white paper the shape of half an egg
5. Sift fine preserved sugar on them, and blow off all that does not stick
6. Bake them on a board on a slow oven
7. When of a pale brown, they are done
8. Take them off the paper, and beat in the under part with a spoon, to form a hollow, and dry them
9. Fill them with cream, or any preserved fruit, and stick two together, which will form an egg shape
