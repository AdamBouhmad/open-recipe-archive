## Ingredients

- three ounces shalots
- three ounces sweet savory
- three ounces chives
- three ounces tarragon
- two table-spoonfuls of dried mint leaves
- two table-spoonfuls of balm
- strong white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat shalots, sweet savory, chives, and tarragon, of each three ounces, two table-spoonfuls of dried mint leaves, and the same of balm together in a mortar
2. Put them into a stone gallon bottle
3. Fill up with strong white wine vinegar
4. Cork it securely
5. Let it stand a fortnight exposed to the sun
6. Filter it
