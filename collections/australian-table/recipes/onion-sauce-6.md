## Ingredients

- 3 Small Onions
- 1 oz. Butter
- Lemon Juice
- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Bread Crumbs
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, put them into cold water, and let them boil for a minute.
2. Strain away the water, cover again with cold water, boil up and cook till soft; take out of the water, chop small.
3. Put the butter and milk into a saucepan, and when it boils put in the bread crumbs and onions.
4. Cook slowly for five minutes, season with pepper, salt, and a few drops of lemon juice, and it is ready to serve.
