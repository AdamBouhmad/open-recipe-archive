## Ingredients

- beef (bones removed)
- a pound of salt
- two ounces of saltpetre
- an ounce of sal prunella
- half a pound of coarse brown sugar
- parsley
- shalots
- thyme
- bay-leaves
- marjoram
- sweet basil
- winter savory
- coriander-seed
- a tablespoonful of juniper berries
- three cloves of garlic
- vinegar
- a couple of onions
- a few cloves
- nutmeg
- herbs

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the meat and put it in a pan
2. Rub well with a pickle composed of a pound of salt, two ounces of saltpetre, an ounce of sal prunella, and half a pound of coarse brown sugar, well mixed together
3. Cover with parsley, shalots, thyme, bay-leaves, marjoram, sweet basil, winter savory, a little coriander-seed, a tablespoonful of juniper berries, and three cloves of garlic
4. Turn every day for ten days in the brine, and put the herbs on the top
5. Hang up and smoke
6. When boiling, put a little vinegar in the water, a couple of onions cut, a few cloves, some nutmeg, and herbs
7. When done, let it remain in the same liquor until cold
