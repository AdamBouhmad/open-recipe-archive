## Ingredients

- two table-spoonfuls of flour
- a quarter of a pint of cream
- two eggs
- some grated nutmeg
- four bitter almonds pounded
- two tea-spoonfuls of ratafia
- an ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Mix two table-spoonfuls of flour with a quarter of a pint of cream, two eggs well beaten, some grated nutmeg, four bitter almonds pounded, two tea-spoonfuls of ratafia, and an ounce of butter beaten to a cream
2. Bake these ingredients in small buttered cups for half an hour
3. Turn them out in a dish, and serve immediately with sweet sauce poured over them
