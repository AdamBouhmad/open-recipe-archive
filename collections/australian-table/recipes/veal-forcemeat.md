## Ingredients

- four ounces of lean veal
- two ounces of bread crumbs
- two ounces of suet
- some parsley and sweet herbs
- an onion
- spice
- the yolk of an egg
- pepper and salt
- a little ham, bacon, or salt tongue

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar four ounces of lean veal, two ounces of bread crumbs, two ounces of suet, and some parsley and sweet herbs, an onion, and spice
2. Break into it the yolk of an egg
3. Mix all together
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. A little ham, bacon, or salt tongue would make it more savoury
