## Ingredients

- beef
- brown sugar
- a pound of salt
- a quarter of a pound of saltpetre
- an ounce of juniper berries

## Directions

1. Rub the beef well with brown sugar
2. Mix a pound of salt with a quarter of a pound of saltpetre and an ounce of juniper berries
3. Rub the beef well in with this mixture in the pan for ten days
4. Press the beef
5. Dry and smoke it
6. Boil it in a cloth and do not remove it until cold
