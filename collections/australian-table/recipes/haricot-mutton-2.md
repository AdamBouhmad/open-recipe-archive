## Ingredients

- mutton
- turnips
- carrots
- onions
- stock
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry in a pan a sufficient quantity of mutton, cut in slices
2. When nearly done take the meat out, and fry of a brown colour equal quantities of turnips and carrots, cut into small pieces, and a few onions
3. When done put the mutton and vegetables in a stewpan, with a small quantity of stock, and pepper and salt
4. Simmer and serve
