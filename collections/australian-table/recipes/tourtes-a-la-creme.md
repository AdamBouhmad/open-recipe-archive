## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pint of thick cream
- a pinch of salt
- half a pound of butter
- yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Put a pound of flour on a paste-board, make a hole in the middle, and put in a quarter of a pint of thick cream and a pinch of salt
2. Mix it lightly, and let it stand half an hour
3. Put in it half a pound of butter, at five times, letting it stand a quarter of an hour twice
4. Make it into several cakes
5. Feather with yolk of egg, and bake
