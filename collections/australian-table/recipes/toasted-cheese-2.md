## Ingredients

- mild cheese
- butter
- ale

## Directions

1. fit six or eight movable small tin dishes into the toaster
2. put the least bit of butter in the tin at the bottom, with the cheese, or a little ale
3. expose to the fire until done
4. Serve hot
