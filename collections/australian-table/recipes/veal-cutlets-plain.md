## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- shalots
- dripping
- water
- flour
- broiled ham or bacon

## Directions

1. Put the cutlets in the frying-pan, with salt and pepper, parsley, shalots, chopped fine
2. Fry in the best dripping, over a quick fire
3. When the cutlets are done on one side, turn them, and brown them
4. Take out the cutlets and pour all the fat out of the pan but a couple of spoonfuls
5. Add half a pint of water, thickened with flour
6. Let this gravy brown, and pour over the cutlets
7. Garnish the dish with broiled ham, or bacon toasted
