## Ingredients

- 3 Bream
- 0.5 pint Curry Sauce
- 1 lb. Rice

## Directions

1. Make the curry sauce by recipe given elsewhere.
2. Fillet the fish and cut each fillet in two pieces, butter a saucepan and lay in the fish.
3. Pour over the sauce, bring it up to the boil, and cook on the stove very slowly for an hour.
4. Just before serving, season with salt and lemon juice to taste.
5. Boil the rice and dry thoroughly; press into little cups or moulds.
6. Dish the fish carefully and pour the sauce over it; garnish with the moulds of rice.
