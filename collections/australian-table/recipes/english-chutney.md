## Ingredients

- two pounds of apples
- a quarter of a pound of green mint
- juice of two lemons
- half a pound of bird's-eye chillies
- half a pound of salt
- a quarter of a pound of onions
- a quarter of a pound of garlic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and core the apples
2. Pound the apples
3. Chop the mint fine
4. Pound all ingredients together
5. Add a small quantity of vinegar
6. If you cannot procure the green chillies, use cayenne
