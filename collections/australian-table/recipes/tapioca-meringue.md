## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1½ oz. Tapioca
- 1 oz. Sugar
- Whites of 2 Eggs
- Flavouring
- 1 oz. Beef Suet

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca well in cold water, strain off the water, and put it into a pie dish.
2. Chop the suet very finely and mix it in with the sugar.
3. Flavour with grated lemon peel or nutmeg, pour over the milk and mix well.
4. Stand in a very cool oven for two hours.
5. Whip the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth, flavour the same as the pudding, spread these on top, sprinkle with sugar, and stand in the oven till set.
6. Serve cold. This meringue is very much improved if a few macaroons are broken up and laid on the top before the eggs are put on, or if a spoonful of raspberry jam is spread over.
