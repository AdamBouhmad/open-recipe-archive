## Ingredients

- a sheep's head
- a cupful of pearl barley
- two carrots
- two turnips
- an onion
- some parsley
- salt
- pepper
- parsley and butter
- parsley leaves or boiled beet

## Directions

1. Clean the head properly and put it on to simmer with a cupful of pearl barley
2. Skim it carefully, and when it has simmered an hour, add two carrots, two turnips, an onion cut into slices, and some parsley
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. When the head is sufficiently tender, serve
5. The head can be put on a separate dish and parsley and butter poured over it and garnished with parsley leaves or boiled beet
