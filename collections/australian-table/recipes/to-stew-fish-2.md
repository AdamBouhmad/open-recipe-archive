## Ingredients

- fish
- half a pint of claret or port wine
- a quart of stock or gravy
- a large onion
- a few cloves
- a little mace
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- anchovy
- mushroom ketchup
- chilli vinegar

## Directions

1. When the fish have been well washed, lay them in a stewpan, with half a pint of claret or port wine, and a quart of stock or gravy, a large onion sliced, and a few cloves and a little mace.
2. Cover the stewpan, and let it simmer for about twenty minutes, according to the size of the fish.
3. Take the fish out, and lay it on a hot hearth in a dish.
4. Thicken the liquor that it was stewed in with a little flour, and season with pepper, salt, anchovy, and mushroom ketchup, and chilli vinegar.
5. When it has boiled about ten minutes, either pour it over the fish or return the fish into the stewpan to warm.
