## Ingredients

- 1 bottle of Oysters
- 1 pint of Milk
- 6 Soda Biscuits
- 1 oz. Butter
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Put the milk and butter into a saucepan; when it boils put in the oysters and simmer for five minutes.
2. Season with pepper and salt; break up the biscuits and throw them in.
3. Boil up and pour into a deep dish, and it is ready to serve.
