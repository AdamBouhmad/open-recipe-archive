## Ingredients

- a quart of mild ale
- a wine-glassful of white wine
- a wine-glassful of brandy
- a wine-glassful of capillaire
- the juice of a lemon
- some of the rind
- a sprig of balm or borage
- some toasted bread
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put to a quart of mild ale a wine-glassful of white wine, the same of brandy and capillaire, the juice of a lemon, and some of the rind
2. Add a sprig of balm or borage, some toasted bread, and nutmeg grated on the top
