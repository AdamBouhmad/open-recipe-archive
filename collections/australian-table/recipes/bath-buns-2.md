## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- a pound of flour
- six eggs
- two table-spoonfuls of yeast
- half a cupful of lukewarm milk
- six ounces of white sugar
- a few carraway-seeds
- six or eight carraway comfits

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of butter into a pound of flour
2. Beat up six eggs, and add them to the flour, with two table-spoonfuls of yeast
3. Mix them together with half a cupful of lukewarm milk
4. Set it to rise
5. Add six ounces of white sugar, and a few carraway-seeds
6. Mould into buns
7. Put on the top of each six or eight carraway comfits
8. Bake
