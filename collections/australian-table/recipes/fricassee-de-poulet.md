## Ingredients

- chickens
- a piece of butter
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- some mushrooms
- chives
- two shalots
- a little flour
- a glass of white wine
- a glass of pale stock
- salt
- pepper
- yolks of three eggs
- a spoonful of vinegar
- a spoonful of stock

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into small pieces after they are semi-boiled
2. Put them into a stewpan with a piece of butter, a bunch of sweet herbs, some mushrooms, chives, two shalots
3. Add a little flour, a glass of white wine, and the same of pale stock
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Stew for half an hour slowly
6. Take out the herbs
7. Add to the sauce the yolks of three eggs, to which add a spoonful of vinegar and the same of stock
