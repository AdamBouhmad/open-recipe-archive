## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- onions
- a carrot
- some parsley and mushrooms
- three spoonfuls of flour
- half a pint of cream
- salt
- white pepper
- nutmeg
- a small piece of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan a piece of butter, onions, and a carrot shred, some parsley and mushrooms
2. Set it on the fire
3. Put in three spoonfuls of flour, half a pint of cream, salt, white pepper, and nutmeg
4. Keep it stirred until it boils
5. Let it simmer for half an hour
6. Strain it
7. When ready to serve add a small piece of fresh butter
