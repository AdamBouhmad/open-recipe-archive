## Ingredients

- the peel of four lemons
- two gallons of boiling water
- the strained juice of the lemons
- a pound of raisins, stoned and chopped fine
- brandy

## Directions

1. Cut the peel of four lemons thin, pour on it two gallons of boiling water
2. when cold, add the strained juice of the lemons, and a pound of raisins, stoned and chopped fine
3. Let it stand a few days, and then strain it and bottle for use
4. A small addition of brandy is an improvement
