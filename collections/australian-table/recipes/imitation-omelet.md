## Ingredients

- 1 Egg
- ⅛ gill Milk
- 1 teaspoonful Sugar
- 1 teaspoonful Jam
- 1 teaspoonful Flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolk and white of egg separately.
2. Beat the flour and milk together, and mix in the sugar and yolk of egg.
3. Stir in the white, butter a saucer, put the jam at the bottom.
4. Pour in the mixture, bake in the oven for five minutes, sprinkle with sugar, and serve.
