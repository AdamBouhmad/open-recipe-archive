## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 Parsnips
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Milk
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Scrape and cut up the parsnips (or cold ones will do).
2. If raw, boil them in water seasoned with salt for three-quarters of an hour.
3. Make the butter, flour, and milk into a sauce by directions given, and season nicely.
4. Stir in the parsley, put in the parsnips, bring to the boil and simmer for ten minutes.
5. Arrange them on a hot dish, pour the sauce over, and serve.
