## Ingredients

- a medium-sized lobster or crayfish
- half an anchovy
- a large piece of boiled celery
- the yolk of an egg, hard-boiled
- a little cayenne
- mace
- salt
- pepper
- two table-spoonfuls of bread crumbs
- two ounces of butter
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the flesh and soft parts of a medium-sized lobster or crayfish, half an anchovy, a large piece of boiled celery, the yolk of an egg, hard-boiled, a little cayenne, mace, salt, and pepper, with two table-spoonfuls of bread crumbs, two ounces of butter, and two eggs, well beaten up
2. Make into balls
3. Fry of a light brown
