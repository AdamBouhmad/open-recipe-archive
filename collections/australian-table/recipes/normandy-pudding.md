## Ingredients

- 2 Stale Buns
- 1 Egg
- 1 pint Milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and pour it over the beaten egg
2. Sweeten to taste
3. Put the buns into a pie-dish
4. Pour over the custard, cover and leave for an hour
5. Put into a moderate oven and bake for about half an hour
6. Serve hot
