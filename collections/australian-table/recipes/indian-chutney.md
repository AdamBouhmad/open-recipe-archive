## Ingredients

- two pounds of green tamarinds
- two pounds of unripe mangoes
- two pounds of salt
- two pounds of ginger roots
- one pound of onions
- one pound of garlic
- one pound and a half of raisins
- six parts of good vinegar

## Directions

1. Stone the tamarinds
2. Stone the raisins
3. Well pound all the ingredients together
4. Add to six parts of good vinegar
5. Jar them for use
