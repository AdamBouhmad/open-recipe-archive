## Ingredients

- 6 Carrots
- 1 oz. Butter
- Sugar, Salt, and Pepper
- 3 quarts Bone Stock

## Directions

1. Scrape and slice up the carrots and put them into a saucepan with the butter.
2. Sprinkle over a teaspoonful each of salt and sugar and a quarter of a teaspoonful of pepper.
3. Turn them about in the butter for five minutes, pour over the boiling stock and boil for an hour.
4. Rub through a sieve, return to the saucepan and boil up, season to taste, and serve very hot.
