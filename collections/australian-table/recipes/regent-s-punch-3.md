## Ingredients

- three bottles of champagne
- one bottle of hock
- one bottle of curaçoa
- a quart of brandy
- a pint of rum
- two bottles of Madeira
- two bottles of seltzer-water
- four pounds of bloom raisins, stoned
- Seville oranges
- lemons
- white sugar-candy
- green tea

## Directions

1. Mix three bottles of champagne, one bottle of hock, one bottle of curaçoa, a quart of brandy, a pint of rum, two bottles of Madeira, and two bottles of seltzer-water together
2. Add four pounds of bloom raisins (stoned), Seville oranges, lemons, and white sugar-candy
3. Use green tea instead of water
4. Ice in summer and warm in winter
