## Ingredients

- two ounces of the tops of wormwood
- four quarts of boiling water
- the white of an egg
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Put in a basin two ounces of the tops of wormwood, and pour over it four quarts of boiling water
2. Let it steep four hours on a hot hearth
3. Strain the decoction through linen
4. Beat the white of an egg, melt two pounds of sugar, and simmer until done
5. Bottle for use
