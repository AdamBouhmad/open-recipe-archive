## Ingredients

- 1 Sole
- 1 teaspoonful of Parsley
- 4 teaspoonsful Bread Crumbs
- 1 Small Onion
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 gill Good Gravy
- 1 oz. Fat Bacon

## Directions

1. Mince the onion, parsley, and bacon very finely, and put them into a basin with the seasoning and crumbs, and mix thoroughly
2. Butter a dish in which the fish can be both cooked and served
3. Spread half the seasoning on it, wash and dry the fish and lay it on this bed of seasoning
4. Spread the rest of the seasoning on the top, pour over gently the gravy
5. Cover with a few brown raspings and put the butter on in tiny pieces
6. Put it into a quick oven and bake from 15 to 20 minutes, according to the thickness of the fish
7. Pin a paper collar round the dish, and serve at once
