## Ingredients

- salted fish
- cold water
- egg sauce
- boiled beet
- yolks of hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Salted fish generally requires soaking, according to the time they are salted.
2. Put it into plenty of cold water, and let it simmer till done.
3. Serve with egg sauce and boiled beet as an accompaniment, with yolks of hard-boiled eggs.
