## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of the best lard
- three quarters of a pint of new milk
- four pounds of flour
- pork or veal
- white pepper
- salt
- dried sage
- egg

## Directions

1. Boil the lard and milk together
2. Stir the boiling mixture into four pounds of flour
3. Knead the paste well
4. Set it down before the fire for twenty minutes before using
5. Make your paste warm, roll it out, and mould it in a mould
6. When cold take it off
7. Fill it up with pork or veal, which must be cut up small, seasoned with white pepper, salt, and dried sage
8. Put egg on the top if you like
9. Lay fancy leaves on the top to make it ornamental
10. Bake well
