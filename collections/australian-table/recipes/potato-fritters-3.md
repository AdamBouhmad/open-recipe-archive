## Ingredients

- Cold Potatoes
- 1 Egg
- 2 oz. Sugar
- Nutmeg or Lemon Peel
- Hot Fat

## Directions

1. Mash up the potatoes very smoothly, beat in the egg and a flavouring of nutmeg or grated lemon peel.
2. Beat up the egg and pour over the potatoes and mix into a paste.
3. Form into small round cakes.
4. Fry in very hot fat till brown.
5. Pile high on a dish, sprinkle with sugar, and serve. One Egg is sufficient for about 1 lb. potatoes.
