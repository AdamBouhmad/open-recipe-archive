## Ingredients

- rind and juice of a lemon
- a lump of sugar
- a small piece of bruised ginger
- about one pint and a half of boiling water
- one wine-glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Take the rind and juice of a lemon, a lump of sugar, a small piece of bruised ginger, and pour on them about a pint and a half of boiling water
2. When cold strain, and add a wine-glass of sherry
