## Ingredients

- one pound of blanched almonds
- whites of four eggs
- one pound of sifted white sugar
- grated lemon-peel
- yolks of twelve eggs
- whites of twelve eggs
- a quarter of a pound of flour

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar one pound of blanched almonds, with the whites of four eggs
2. Add one pound of sifted white sugar, some grated lemon-peel, and the yolks of twelve eggs
3. Work them well together
4. Beat to a froth the whites of the dozen eggs
5. Stir them into the other ingredients, with a quarter of a pound of flour
6. Put the mixture in a mould
7. Bake an hour
8. Take the cake out of the mould, and set it on a sieve
