## Ingredients

- fourteen pounds of flour
- one quart of warm water
- one quarter of a pint of good yeast
- three teaspoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Put fourteen pounds of flour into a pan
2. Add to one quart of warm water a quarter of a pint of good yeast
3. Make a hole in the flour, pour in the water and the yeast
4. Stir it well with a wooden spoon until it forms a thickish paste
5. Throw a little flour over, and leave it in a warm place
6. In about an hour when it has risen and burst through the covering of flour, add more warm water and three teaspoonfuls of salt
7. Knead until it forms a rather stiff dough, working it as much as possible
8. Let it remain, covered with a cloth, for an hour
9. Divide it into loaves
10. Bake for an hour and a quarter
