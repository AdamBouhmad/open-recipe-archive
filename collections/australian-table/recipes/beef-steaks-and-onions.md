## Ingredients

- beef-steaks
- clarified dripping
- onions
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Have the steaks cut rather thinner than for broiling
2. Put some clarified dripping into the pan, and fry the steaks until done
3. Put in the onions, having been previously sliced, and fry them of a light brown
4. Serve with brown sauce
