## Ingredients

- 4 Lettuces
- 1 oz. Butter
- Nutmeg
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the lettuces very thoroughly and lay them in salt and water for half an hour.
2. Plunge them into plenty of boiling water seasoned with salt and a quarter of a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda.
3. Boil quickly without the lid from fifteen to twenty minutes, take up and squeeze all the water from them.
4. Chop them up and put into a saucepan with some butter, nutmeg, pepper and salt, and a few drops of lemon juice; stir them about and cook for about five minutes.
5. Turn into a hot dish and serve.
