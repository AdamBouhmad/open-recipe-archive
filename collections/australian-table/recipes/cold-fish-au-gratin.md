## Ingredients

- scraps of cold fish
- 1 gill of milk or water
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1 oz. of flour
- brown bread crumbs

## Directions

1. If any fish sauce is left, warm it in; if not, make a little with 1 gill of milk or water, 1 oz. of butter, and 1 oz. of flour.
2. Flake the fish up, butter a plate, put the fish in and pour the sauce over.
3. Sprinkle with brown bread crumbs, and bake in the oven for a quarter of an hour.
