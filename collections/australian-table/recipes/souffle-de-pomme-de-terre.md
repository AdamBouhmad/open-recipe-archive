## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- six spoonfuls of potato flour
- four yolks of eggs
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a little lemon-peel
- six yolks of eggs
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Dilute the potato flour with the four egg yolks and cream.
2. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg and a little lemon-peel.
3. Put this mixture on the fire and let it boil a few minutes, stirring it.
4. Allow it to cool.
5. Add six yolks of eggs and beat all together.
6. Whisk the whites of four eggs and mix them quickly and lightly with the rest.
7. Put all in a pan and bake.
8. Serve quickly.
