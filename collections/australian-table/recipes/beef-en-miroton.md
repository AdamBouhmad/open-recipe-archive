## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- flour
- broth
- white wine
- boiled beef
- mustard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice a few onions, fry them with a piece of butter and a little flour, until they are of a fine brown colour
2. Add a little broth, a glass of white wine, and stew the whole until the onions are well done
3. Add boiled beef cut in small thin slices, called miroton
4. Stew them until they are well flavoured with onion
5. Flavour them with mustard or a dash of vinegar, and serve
