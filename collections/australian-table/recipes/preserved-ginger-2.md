## Ingredients

- large pieces of common ginger
- white sugar (sugar-candy is best)

## Directions

1. Simmer ginger in water until tender
2. Put in cold water
3. Take it out and drain
4. To equal quantities in weight of ginger add white sugar
5. Let the sugar be clarified, and put to the ginger
6. Simmer constantly, until the sugar has penetrated right through the ginger
