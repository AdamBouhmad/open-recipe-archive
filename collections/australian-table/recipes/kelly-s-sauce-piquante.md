## Ingredients

- a table-spoonful of parsley leaves
- a table-spoonful of capers
- a table-spoonful of fresh mustard
- three hard yolks of eggs
- six anchovies
- a table-spoonful of vinegar
- two table-spoonfuls of oil
- a finely-chopped shalot
- half a pint of melted butter or strong gravy

## Directions

1. Put a table-spoonful of parsley leaves, and the same of capers, into a mortar, and pound them
2. add a table-spoonful of fresh mustard, and three hard yolks of eggs, and properly mix the whole
3. Then add six anchovies, boned and forced through a sieve, a table-spoonful of vinegar, two of oil, and a finely-chopped shalot, and mix the whole
4. When to be used, stir the sauce into half a pint of melted butter or strong gravy
