## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat to a froth the whites of six eggs which have been seasoned as for an omelette.
2. Pour this froth into a buttered baking-tin.
3. Pour across it four spoonfuls of cream, dropped into it at equal distances, the yolks whole.
4. Bake, but not too briskly, and serve.
