## Ingredients

- one quart of oatmeal
- three pounds of treacle
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- a little butter and flour
- sweetmeats or carraways ad libitum

## Directions

1. Mix all together
2. Roll it out thin, and cut into round cakes
3. Bake in a tin
