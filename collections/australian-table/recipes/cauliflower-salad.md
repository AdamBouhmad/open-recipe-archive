## Ingredients

- 1 Cauliflower
- Half a Lettuce
- 2 Eggs
- 1/4 gill Oil and Vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower and branch it carefully.
2. Boil the eggs hard.
3. Separate the whites from the yolks.
4. Chop the whites small and cut the yolks in slices.
5. Shred up the lettuce in a bowl.
6. Put the branches of cauliflower all round it.
7. Put the slices of yolk of egg outside as a border.
8. Pour on the salad dressing.
9. Put the white of egg in little heaps on the lettuce.
10. It is then ready to serve.
