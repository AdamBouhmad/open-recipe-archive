## Ingredients

- six tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt
- a pint of milk
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Put six tablespoonfuls of flour into a basin, with a little salt
2. Stir in gradually a pint of milk to make a thin batter, and make sure it is not lumpy
3. When it is well mixed, put in three eggs, well beaten up, and a little more milk
4. Place it under the meat, so that the dripping may fall on it
5. Serve as free from fat as possible
