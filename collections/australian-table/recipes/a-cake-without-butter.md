## Ingredients

- five eggs
- six ounces of flour
- beaten almonds
- thin slices of citron

## Directions

1. Beat well five eggs.
2. Add six ounces of flour.
3. Flavour with beaten almonds, and add, if liked, thin slices of citron.
4. Bake in a mould in a moderate oven.
