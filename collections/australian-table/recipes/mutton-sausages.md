## Ingredients

- mutton
- beef suet
- anchovies
- seasoning
- herbs

## Directions

1. To each pound of mutton add a pound of beef suet
2. Add anchovies, for relish, chopped fine
3. Add what seasoning you choose and herbs or not, as you please
