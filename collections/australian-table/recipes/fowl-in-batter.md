## Ingredients

- fowl
- handful of mushrooms
- rasped bacon
- bleached artichoke bottoms
- truffles
- beef suet
- anchovy
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- mixed spice
- butter
- glass of white wine
- yolks of two eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Truss the fowl
2. Make forcemeat by chopping together mushrooms, rasped bacon, artichoke bottoms, truffles, beef suet, anchovy, and parsley
3. Mix these together with bread crumbs, pepper, salt, and mixed spice
4. Put a lump of butter into a stewpan; when warm add the mixture and let it fry a little
5. Moisten it with a glass of white wine and stew for a quarter of an hour
6. Take it off the fire and add the yolks of two eggs
7. Put this forcemeat into the body of the fowl and cover it with paper
8. Roast the fowl; when half done remove the paper
9. Make a light batter with a little salt and grated nutmeg
10. Baste the fowl with the batter to give it a thin coating
11. When the first coating is dry, add a second and third coating until a delicate coat of light-brown colour is formed
12. Serve with sauce of any kind, thickened with cream
