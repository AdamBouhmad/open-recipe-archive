## Ingredients

- Two tea-spoonfuls of white sugar
- one tea-spoonful of raspberry jam
- the whites of two eggs
- the juice of one lemon
- fancy biscuits

## Directions

1. Beat well for half an hour
2. serve up, sprinkled with fancy biscuits
