## Ingredients

- half a pound of ham or lean bacon
- six pounds of lean beef
- two onions
- two turnips
- two carrots
- a head of celery
- a few cloves
- a blade of mace
- hot water
- cold water
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Cut half a pound of ham, or the same quantity of lean bacon, into slices, and lay them at the bottom of a digester, with six pounds of lean beef, cut into pieces
2. Clean two onions, two turnips, and two carrots, and a head of celery, a few cloves, and a blade of mace
3. Cover the digester close, and set it over the fire
4. When the meat adheres to the bottom of the digester, turn it
5. When there is a brown glaze on the meat, cover with hot water
6. When coming to a boil, put in half a pint of cold water, take off the scum
7. Put in some more cold water, and again skim it
8. Set it aside to simmer for full three hours
9. When done, strain it through a tamis, turn it into a pan
10. When cold, take off the fat
11. If thick, it must be clarified with the whites of two eggs
