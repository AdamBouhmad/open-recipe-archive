## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- One pound of sugar
- Eight eggs
- A few drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and the whites of the eggs separate sufficiently
2. Add the two together, and put in the sugar
3. When mixed, add the flour by degrees
4. Add a few drops of essence of lemon as an improvement
5. Butter a dish and bake
