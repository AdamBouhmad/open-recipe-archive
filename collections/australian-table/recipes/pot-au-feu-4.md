## Ingredients

- 3 pounds leg of beef
- 2 quarts water
- 1 fagot of herbs
- salt and pepper
- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 1 dozen peppercorns
- tape
- sippets of toast

## Directions

1. Tie the meat up into a nice shape with a piece of tape
2. Put it into cold water
3. Bring slowly to the boil, and very carefully remove the scum
4. Peel and slice up the vegetables, and put them in with the fagot of herbs and the peppercorns tied in a piece of muslin
5. Bring to simmering point, and keep it so for five hours
6. The liquor can then be served as a soup with part of the vegetables and some sippets of toast
7. Take the tapes off the meat, and serve with the rest of the vegetables round the dish as a border or garnish
8. The remains of the beef can be pressed between heavy weights till cold, or put into a brawn tin and served cold with a salad
