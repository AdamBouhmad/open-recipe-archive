## Ingredients

- herring or anchovies
- warm beer or water
- a small quantity of butter
- good cayenne
- a little vinegar
- a little powdered mace

## Directions

1. Soak herring or anchovies in warm beer or water
2. Take out all the bones and skins
3. Pound them with a small quantity of butter, some good cayenne, and a little vinegar
4. A little powdered mace may be added
5. Pot them
