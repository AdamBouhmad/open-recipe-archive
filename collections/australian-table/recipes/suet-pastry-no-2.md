## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 8 oz. suet
- 0.5 pint water
- Pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin; prepare the suet by cutting it into very thin slices and then shredding it up very fine indeed; mix it in with the flour.
2. Stir in the water until it is of a firm consistency, but do not use too much water, or the paste will be tough.
3. Turn it on to a floured board and knead for a few minutes.
4. It is then ready for use; this crust is suitable for all kinds of boiled puddings, such as meat, apple, jam, &c.
5. These puddings require to be boiled for a very long time and must always be plunged into boiling water, and kept boiling and covered with water all the time they are cooking.
