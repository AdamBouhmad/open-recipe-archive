## Ingredients

- Fat mutton
- Two ounces of sugar
- An ounce of common salt
- Half a spoonful of saltpetre

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with salt and saltpetre
2. Well rub the meat with this mixture
3. Place in a pan
4. Beat and turn once a day for ten days
5. Dry and smoke
6. Cut rashers thin and serve hot, or serve the hams cold after having been previously simmered sufficiently
