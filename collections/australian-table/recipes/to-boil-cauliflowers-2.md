## Ingredients

- cauliflowers
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the cauliflowers in plenty of salt and water, with the flower downwards
2. Cook in plenty of boiling water seasoned with salt, putting the flower to the bottom of the saucepan
3. Keep uncovered all the time of cooking
4. Take up with a slice and strain in a colander
5. Turn carefully into a vegetable dish, and serve with or without sauce
