## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a tea-cupful of water
- three pounds of good moist sugar
- grated peel of one lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of butter and half a tea-cupful of water into a pan
2. Add three pounds of good moist sugar, the grated peel of one lemon
3. Boil in water till it becomes brittle
4. Pour on a tin
