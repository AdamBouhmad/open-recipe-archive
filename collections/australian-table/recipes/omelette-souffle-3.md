## Ingredients

- eggs
- lemon-peel
- orange-flower water
- powdered sugar
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of six eggs into a basin and the whites into another, with a little rasped lemon-peel, a spoonful of orange-flower water, some powdered sugar, and a little salt.
2. Whip the yolks into a froth.
3. Mix the yolks and whites lightly together.
4. Put a lump of fresh butter into an omelette pan, and, when it boils, pour the omelette into the pan.
5. When the bottom of it has become firm, turn it over into the dish to be baked in, the sides of which may be raised with paper.
6. Bake it in an oven or before the fire.
7. The moment it has risen, it must be sent to table, or it will sink.
