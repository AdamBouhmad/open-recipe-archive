## Ingredients

- beef or mutton slices
- eggs
- potatoes
- greens

## Directions

1. Cut slices of beef or mutton of equal thickness, and either broil, fry, or brown, in an oven before the fire
2. Lay them in a dish to keep warm, while you poach eggs and mash potatoes; or you may fry potatoes and greens
3. Place the vegetables on the dish, the meat on the top, and garnish with the poached eggs
