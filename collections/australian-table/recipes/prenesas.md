## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- flour
- ten eggs
- clarified butter
- clarified sugar
- lemon essence

## Directions

1. Stir flour into one pint of milk as much as will bring it to the consistency of hasty pudding
2. Boil it till it becomes thick
3. Let it cool, and beat it up with ten eggs
4. When smooth, take a spoonful at a time, and drop it into a frying-pan in which there is a good quantity of boiling clarified butter
5. Fry of a light brown, and serve with clarified sugar flavoured with lemon essence
