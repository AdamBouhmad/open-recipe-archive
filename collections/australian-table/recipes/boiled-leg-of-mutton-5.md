## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- mashed turnips

## Directions

1. Simply simmer until done
2. Take off the scum occasionally
3. Ensure the dish is done but not over-done
4. Serve with mashed turnips which must be neither stringy nor bitter
5. If turnips are bitter, a little sugar put in the water will absorb that unpleasant taste
