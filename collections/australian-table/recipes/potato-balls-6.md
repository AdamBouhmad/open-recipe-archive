## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Boiled Potatoes
- Bread Crumbs
- 2 Eggs
- 1 oz. Butter
- Hot Fat

## Directions

1. Rub the potatoes through a sieve or mash them smoothly.
2. Put the butter into a saucepan, and, when melted, season with pepper and salt; put in the potatoes and turn them about till hot through.
3. Drop in the egg and mix into a paste, turn on to a plate to cool, and roll into balls.
4. Beat up an egg and brush over the balls, cover well with crumbs, and fry in hot fat.
