## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- One pound of butter
- Eight eggs
- One pound and a quarter of flour
- Two ounces of currants
- Half a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix the butter with sugar and spice
2. Add half the eggs, and beat for a few minutes
3. Add the rest of the eggs, and work for five minutes longer
4. Stir in the flour and currants
5. Bake in shapes
