## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- six ounces of good sugar
- three-quarters of a pound of lard or butter
- two eggs
- about eight drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Add sugar to flour
2. Melt the lard or butter
3. Beat the eggs well
4. Mix the lard and eggs together
5. Stir in the flour to form a paste
6. Add about eight drops of essence of lemon
7. Roll out into thin cakes
8. Bake in a moderately heated oven
