## Ingredients

- a pheasant
- a hare
- a capon
- two pigeons
- two rabbits
- paste
- livers and hearts
- two mutton kidneys
- forcemeat
- egg balls
- seasoning
- spice
- ketchup
- mushrooms
- gravy made from the different bones

## Directions

1. Bone the pheasant, hare, capon, pigeons, and rabbits
2. Put them into the paste in the shape of a bird
3. Add the livers and hearts, two mutton kidneys, forcemeat and egg balls, seasoning, spice, ketchup, and mushrooms
4. Fill up with gravy made from the different bones
5. Bake sufficiently
