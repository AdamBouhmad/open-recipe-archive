## Ingredients

- half a pint of green gooseberries
- the same quantity of melted butter
- lemon or ginger

## Directions

1. Top and tail and scald half a pint of green gooseberries
2. drain them, and put them to the same quantity of melted butter
3. a little lemon or ginger is an improvement
