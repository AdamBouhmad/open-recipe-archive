## Ingredients

- Dry, hard cheese
- Dry crusts of bread
- Boiling milk
- 1 egg
- Pepper and salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pour a little boiling milk over the bread.
2. Cover it down till quite soft.
3. Beat it with a fork.
4. Grate up the cheese and beat it in with the yolk of an egg and some pepper and salt.
5. Beat the white of the egg to a stiff froth and stir it lightly in.
6. Pour into a buttered pie-dish.
7. Bake in a quick oven for twenty minutes.
8. Serve hot.
