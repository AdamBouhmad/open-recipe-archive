## Ingredients

- fine thick piece of brisket of beef
- pickle
- ground allspice
- black pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- nutmegs
- brown sugar
- dry sweet herbs
- water

## Directions

1. Let a fine thick piece of brisket of beef, not fat, lay three days in pickle
2. Take it out and rub in a mixture of spices consisting of equal quantities of ground allspice, black pepper, cloves, ginger, and nutmegs, and a little brown sugar
3. Repeat this daily for a week
4. Cover it with pounded dry sweet herbs
5. Roll or tie it tightly
6. Put it into a pan with a little water
7. Bake slowly for eight hours
8. Take it out and untie it
9. Put a heavy weight upon it
10. This is a fine relish when eaten cold
