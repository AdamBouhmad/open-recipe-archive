## Ingredients

- 1½ oz. Macaroni
- 1 oz. Butter
- Vegetables
- Cornflour
- 2 quarts Bone Stock
- 1 doz. peppercorns
- onions or leeks
- 1 carrot
- a fagot of herbs
- sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice up the onions or leeks, one carrot, and make a fagot of herbs
2. Fry them in the butter with 1 doz. peppercorns till they are quite brown, but not burnt
3. Sprinkle over a tablespoonful of cornflour, and when brown pour over the boiling stock and stir till it boils up
4. Let it simmer for an hour
5. If it is not brown enough, burn a little sugar in a spoon and stir it in
6. When the vegetables are soft rub the soup through a wire sieve and return to the saucepan
7. Boil the macaroni in salt and water for twenty minutes, strain off, and cut into pieces one inch long
8. Put these into the soup and simmer for a quarter of an hour
9. Flavour with a little salt and pepper if necessary, and pour into a hot tureen
