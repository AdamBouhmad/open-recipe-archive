## Ingredients

- 4 Mullet or Jew-fish
- 2 oz. Bread Crumbs
- 1 oz. Butter
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1 teaspoonful Sweet Herbs
- 1/2 Lemon
- 2 oz. Suet

## Directions

1. Split open the fish and remove the head and backbone, wash well in cold water and dry in a cloth.
2. Chop the parsley, herbs, and suet, and mix these together; add half the crumbs, the rind of half a lemon, and pepper and salt.
3. Butter a baking tin, lay on a fish skin downwards.
4. On this place a layer of seasoning, a little lemon juice, and a few pieces of butter; on this another fish with the cut part next the seasoning.
5. Do the rest in the same way, piling one on top of another; over all put the rest of the crumbs and butter, bake in a moderate oven for half an hour.
6. Slip into a hot dish, and serve.
