## Ingredients

- Cold boiled cabbage
- Cold boiled potatoes
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take any remains of cold boiled cabbage and potatoes, and cut them into small pieces, season with pepper and salt.
2. Put a small piece of butter into a frying pan; put in the vegetables and fry them until they are thoroughly hot through and well mixed.
3. Turn them on to a hot dish, make into a neat pile, and serve.
