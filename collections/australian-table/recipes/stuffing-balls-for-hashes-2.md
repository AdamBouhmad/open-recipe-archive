## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of bread crumbs
- half a pound of beef suet
- a little lemon
- thyme
- parsley
- marjoram
- savory
- nutmeg
- mace
- pepper
- cayenne
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Take three-quarters of a pound of bread crumbs and half a pound of beef suet, finely chopped
2. Add a little lemon, thyme, parsley, marjoram, and savory, also chopped, nutmeg, mace, pepper, and cayenne
3. Mix with two well-beaten eggs
4. Divide into balls
