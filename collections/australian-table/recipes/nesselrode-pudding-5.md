## Ingredients

- forty chestnuts
- syrup
- a pound of sugar
- vanilla
- a pint of cream
- yolks of a dozen eggs
- a glass of maraschino
- an ounce of citron
- two ounces of currants
- two ounces of stewed raisins
- whites of three eggs
- a little syrup

## Directions

1. Blanch about forty chestnuts in boiling water and remove the husks
2. Pound them in a mortar, adding a little syrup
3. Rub them through a fine sieve
4. Mix in a basin with a pint of syrup made from a pound of sugar clarified and flavoured with vanilla, a pint of cream, and the yolks of a dozen eggs
5. Set this mixture over the fire and keep stirring it
6. Just as it begins to boil take it off and pass it through a tamis
7. When it is cold put it into a freezing-pot, adding a glass of maraschino, and let the mixture set
8. Add an ounce of citron, two ounces of currants, and two ounces of stewed raisins
9. Add a plateful of whipped cream to the whites of three eggs beaten to a froth with a little syrup
10. When the pudding is frozen, put it into a pine-apple mould and close the lid
11. Place it again in the freezing-pan and cover over with pounded ice and saltpetre
12. Let it remain until required for table, then turn out the pudding and serve
