## Ingredients

- half a tumbler of tea
- sugar
- milk
- a slice of lemon
- a wine-glass of new milk
- a wine-glass of rum or brandy
- a new-laid egg

## Directions

1. Make half a tumbler of tea as usual with sugar and milk
2. Add a slice of lemon and a wine-glass of new milk
3. Add a wine-glass of rum or brandy
4. Beat up a new-laid egg and add to the mixture
