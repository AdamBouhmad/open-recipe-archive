## Ingredients

- a small quantity of fresh mint
- parsley
- a tea-spoonful of moist sugar
- a quarter of a pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash a small quantity of fresh mint with a little parsley
2. pick the stalks out, mince them fine
3. put in a sauceboat, with a tea-spoonful of moist sugar, and a quarter of a pint of vinegar
