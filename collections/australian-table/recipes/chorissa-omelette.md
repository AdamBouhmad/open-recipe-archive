## Ingredients

- eggs
- half a tea-cupful of finely-minced chorissa

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Add half a tea-cupful of finely-minced chorissa to the eggs
3. Lightly fry on both sides, or hold the salamander over long enough to dress the chorissa
