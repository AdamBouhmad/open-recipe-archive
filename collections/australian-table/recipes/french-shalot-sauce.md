## Ingredients

- some shalots
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- good stock

## Directions

1. Chop fine some shalots
2. Boil them in vinegar with pepper and salt to taste
3. Use about a wine-glassful of vinegar
4. Add some good stock, thickened lightly
