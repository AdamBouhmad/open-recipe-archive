## Ingredients

- 2 Mullet
- Frying Batter
- Hot Fat
- Flour
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Fillet the mullet and cut into small pieces; dip in flour seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Cover with French frying batter, the recipe for which is given elsewhere.
3. Plunge into hot fat.
