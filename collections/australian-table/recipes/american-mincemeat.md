## Ingredients

- pieces of vegetables and cold meat
- gravy or dripping
- a few apples
- a little sage
- slices of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Mash the vegetables and chop the meat fine
2. Warm it with gravy or dripping
3. Fry and slice a few apples and mix with it, with a little sage
4. After it has been sufficiently warmed, lay it on slices of toasted bread and serve
