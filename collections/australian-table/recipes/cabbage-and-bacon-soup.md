## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- 1 pound bacon
- 1 dozen peppercorns
- 2 turnips
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 2 quarts water
- pieces of stale bread

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of water into a saucepan
2. When it boils put in a pound of bacon neither too lean nor too fat
3. Let it boil slowly for one hour
4. The bacon must be well washed and scraped before cooking, and when it boils skim the pot thoroughly
5. Well wash the cabbage and soak it in hot water for half an hour
6. Take all the water away and put the cabbage into the saucepan with the bacon and vegetables cut up, and the peppercorns tied in a piece of muslin
7. Let them simmer together for two and a half hours
8. Take up the cabbage, and cut it into quarters
9. Take one quarter and cut it into small pieces and put it into a soup tureen
10. Cut some stale pieces of bread into thin slices and lay on the top
11. Pour over the boiling liquor, and serve
12. Dish the bacon, pull off the rind, and put the rest of the cabbage round the dish
