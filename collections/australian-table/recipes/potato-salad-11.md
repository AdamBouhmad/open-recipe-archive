## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice up some cold boiled potatoes
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. Mix the oil and vinegar together in the proportion of two of oil to one of vinegar
4. Pour this over, let it stand for an hour, and serve
