## Ingredients

- Good pale vinous cider, one hogshead
- Pale proof spirit, three gallons
- Honey or sugar, fourteen pounds
- Orange-flower water, three pints
- Skimmed milk, half a gallon

## Directions

1. Mix the cider, spirit, and honey or sugar well
2. Let them remain together in a temperate situation for a month
3. Add three pints of orange-flower water
4. In a few days fine it down with half a gallon of skimmed milk
