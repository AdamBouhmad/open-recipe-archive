## Ingredients

- a boiled lobster
- mustard
- vinegar
- cayenne
- butter
- a glass of sherry or madeira
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Cut and break the boiled lobster, but not into small pieces
2. Prepare a mixture of mustard and vinegar, seasoned with cayenne
3. Put the mixture with the lobster and a good-sized piece of butter into a stewpan
4. Keep the cover close for about five minutes
5. Put in a glass of sherry or madeira, and let it boil for about five minutes more
6. Serve it up, garnished with sliced lemon
