## Ingredients

- 1 Small Bream
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 teaspoonful Anchovy Sauce
- 1 gill Milk
- Pepper, Salt, and Lemon Juice
- Flaky Pastry

## Directions

1. Bake the fish in the oven, unless there is cold fish in the larder, which will do just as well; take away the skin and bone, and flake it up.
2. Make a sauce of the butter, flour, and milk; season with anchovy, pepper, salt, and lemon juice; stir in the fish and mix well.
3. Line some small patty pans with flaky pastry, put a spoonful of the mixture in the centre, cover with a round of pastry, press the edges together, and trim into a neat shape.
4. Make a small hole in the centre with a skewer, brush over with egg or milk, put into a quick oven, and bake for about twenty minutes.
5. Dish on a fancy paper, and garnish each patty with a tiny sprig of parsley.
