## Ingredients

- one pound of mutton
- one pound of beef
- half a pound of lean pork
- two quarts of water
- a good-sized carrot
- two turnips
- two onions
- a shalot
- a few lettuces
- half a dozen potatoes
- a pint of green peas
- pepper
- salt
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton, beef, and pork into pieces, and put the meat into two quarts of water, and set it on the fire to simmer for an hour
2. Add a good-sized carrot, two turnips, two onions cut in pieces, a shalot, and a few lettuces, and again simmer
3. Add half a dozen potatoes cut up, and a pint of green peas, pepper and salt, and the squeeze of a lemon
4. Simmer till done, and serve in a tureen
