## Ingredients

- 1 quart Milk
- 1 tablespoonful Rennet
- 1 oz. Sugar
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make the milk tepid, stir in the sugar and a spoonful of rennet or a rennet tablet ; pour into a dish and stand on the stove till solid.
2. Grate a little nutmeg on top and serve cold.
