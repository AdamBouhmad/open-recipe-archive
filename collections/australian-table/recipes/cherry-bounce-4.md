## Ingredients

- six pounds of morella cherries
- three pounds of loaf sugar
- two ounces of powdered bitter almonds
- two quarts of the best brandy

## Directions

1. To six pounds of morella cherries add three pounds of loaf sugar and two ounces of powdered bitter almonds
2. Pour over them two quarts of the best brandy
3. Let it remain steeped, and afterwards bottle according to taste
