## Ingredients

- 1 pair Sweetbreads
- 1 pint Gravy
- Salt and Pepper
- 1/2 Onion
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- 1 Carrot

## Directions

1. Put the sweetbreads in cold water, bring to the boil.
2. Strain away the water, scrape and clean them and remove the pieces of skin.
3. Put the butter into a stewpan and flour the sweetbreads.
4. Dry very lightly and quickly, take them out.
5. Slice up and fry the onion and carrot.
6. Stir in the flour and gravy, and bring to the boil.
7. Lay in the sweetbreads and simmer very gently for one hour.
8. Take them up on a hot dish, season and flavour the gravy.
9. Remove the fat, boil up and pour round them.
10. Serve hot.
