## Ingredients

- one half pound of rice
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- one quart of milk
- a little salt
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of rice to soak over night
2. Early in the morning boil it very soft
3. Drain it from the water
4. Mix with it a quarter of a pound of butter
5. Set it away to cool
6. When cold stir in one quart of milk
7. Add to it a little salt
8. Beat up six eggs and mix
