## Ingredients

- 6 Tomatoes
- 6 Potatoes
- 1 lb. Short Pastry
- Dripping
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1½ teaspoonful Sweet Herbs
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Peal and slice up the potatoes and tomatoes; lay them alternately in a pie-dish and sprinkle over them some parsley, herbs, salt, and pepper.
2. When the dish is full, cover with a short pastry and bake for one hour; serve hot.
