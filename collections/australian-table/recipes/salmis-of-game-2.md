## Ingredients

- under-done partridges
- butter
- ham
- shalots
- dried carrot
- mushrooms
- parsley
- thyme
- bay-leaf
- cloves
- peppercorns
- allspice

## Directions

1. Take off the legs, wings, and breasts of as many under-done partridges as you intend to use
2. Remove the skin from them very carefully
3. Put into a stewpan with a bit of butter, a few slices of ham, four or five shalots, a dried carrot, a few mushrooms, a little parsley, sprig of thyme, a bay-leaf, two cloves, three or four peppercorns, and some allspice
4. Fry the vegetables
