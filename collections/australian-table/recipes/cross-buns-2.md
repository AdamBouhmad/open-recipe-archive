## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half a pound of moist sugar
- half a gill of good yeast
- half a pint of lukewarm milk
- half a pound of butter
- an ounce of spice

## Directions

1. Make similar to plain buns
2. Add an ounce of spice to the mixture
3. Cross when baked
