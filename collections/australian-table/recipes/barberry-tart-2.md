## Ingredients

- barberries
- sugar
- a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit
- paste

## Directions

1. Pull the barberries from the stalks and put the fruit into a stone jar in boiling water
2. Let it simmer until the fruit is soft
3. Put it into a preserving-pan with the sugar (a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit) and boil gently for fifteen minutes
4. Line a tartlet-pan with paste and bake it
5. When the paste is cold fill in with fruit
