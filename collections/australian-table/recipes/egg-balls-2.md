## Ingredients

- four eggs, boiled till hard
- a small quantity of salad oil
- a tea-spoon of flour
- some chopped parsley
- a little salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. When the eggs are cold put the yolks into a mortar, with a small quantity of salad oil, a tea-spoon of flour, some chopped parsley, a little salt and cayenne
2. Rub them well together
3. Roll into small balls
4. Boil two or three minutes
