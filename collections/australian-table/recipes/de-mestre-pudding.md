## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 lb. raisins
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 oz. dripping
- 1 teaspoonful carbonate of soda
- 1 gill boiling water

## Directions

1. Put the flour into a basin; stone the raisins and cut them in half, mix in the sugar and carbonate of soda.
2. Dissolve the dripping in the water, pour in and make into a dough, leave it to stand all night.
3. Dip a cloth in boiling water and tie the pudding up tightly.
4. Plunge into plenty of boiling water, and keep it boiling steadily for three hours; turn into a hot dish.
5. A little custard sauce served with this pudding is a great improvement.
