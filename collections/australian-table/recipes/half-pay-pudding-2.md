## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of suet
- a quarter of a pound of currants
- a quarter of a pound of stoned raisins
- a quarter of a pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of bread-crumbs
- two table-spoonfuls of treacle
- half a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Chop a quarter of a pound of suet fine
2. Mix it with a quarter of a pound of currants well washed, a quarter of a pound of stoned raisins, a quarter of a pound of flour, the same of bread-crumbs, two table-spoonfuls of treacle, and half a pint of milk
3. Beat up the ingredients until they are well mixed
4. Put them into a buttered basin and boil for a couple of hours
