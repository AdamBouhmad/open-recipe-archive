## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 1½ oz. Rice
- 1 oz. Sugar
- Rind of Half a Lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well, strain off the water and put it into a pie-dish.
2. Mix in the sugar and the rind of the lemon.
3. Pour over the milk, and let it stand for half an hour.
4. Put it into a very slow oven, and bake till firm. This is a very delicious pudding if properly made; it should be firm, but not dry.
