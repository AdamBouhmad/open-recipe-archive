## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Fish
- 1 gill Thick Sauce
- 1 teaspoonful Anchovy
- 1 pint Melted Butter
- 2 oz. Fat Bacon
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- 1 Egg
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 oz. Flour
- 1 oz. Butter

## Directions

1. Chop the fish, bacon, and parsley finely, and mix them together with the seasoning
2. Make a thick sauce with 1 gill water, 1 oz. flour, and 1 oz. butter
3. Flavour with anchovy and stir the fish in
4. Simmer for a few minutes, stir in the yolk of the egg, and turn on to a plate to cool
5. Make up into small balls
6. Fill a frying pan with boiling water, put in the balls
7. Cover over and simmer gently for ten minutes
8. Dish the balls in a circle and pour over the melted butter, which has been nicely flavoured with anchovy
9. Garnish with parsley, and serve
