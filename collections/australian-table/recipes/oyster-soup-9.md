## Ingredients

- liquor of a hundred oysters
- a quart of new milk
- a quart of water
- pepper
- salt
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- flour
- a little nutmeg
- a little mace

## Directions

1. Put the liquor of a hundred oysters into a stewpan, with a quart of new milk, and the same quantity of water
2. Season with pepper and salt, with a quarter of a pound of fresh butter and flour
3. Let this boil for a short time, after which set it to cool
4. Beard the oysters, and add them to the liquid, and let them boil for two minutes
5. A little nutmeg and mace may be added
