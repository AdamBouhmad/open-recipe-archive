## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- the peel of a lemon
- a little grated nutmeg
- a bay or laurel leaf
- a stick of cinnamon
- eight egg yolks
- four egg whites
- a little brandy or white wine

## Directions

1. Put a quart of milk into a stewpan, with the peel of a lemon cut thin, a little grated nutmeg, a bay or laurel leaf, a stick of cinnamon
2. set it over a quick fire, and mind it does not boil over
3. when it boils set it beside the fire, and simmer ten minutes
4. break the yolks of eight and the whites of four eggs into a basin, beat them well
5. pour in the milk a little at a time, set it on the fire again, and stir it with a wooden spoon
6. let it have one boil, pass it through a tamis
7. when cold, add a little brandy or white wine, and serve in cups
