## Ingredients

- two dozen onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- half a tea-spoonful of salt
- about half a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Take two dozen onions and peel them
2. put them in salt and water for an hour, and then boil them until tender
3. drain them, and chop them up
4. Put them in a clean saucepan, with some butter and flour, half a tea-spoonful of salt, and about half a pint of milk
5. simmer till done
