## Ingredients

- duck
- lemon
- butter
- stock
- olives

## Directions

1. Give the duck a round form
2. Rub it with lemon and brown it in a pan with butter
3. When it has taken a good colour add some stock
4. When nearly done scald some olives and stone them without breaking
5. Add the stoned olives into the sauce in which the duck was cooked
6. Serve the duck with olives round it
