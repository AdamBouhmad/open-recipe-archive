## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of fine Liverpool salt
- three ounces of coarse brown sugar
- one ounce of pounded allspice

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a pound of fine Liverpool salt, add three ounces of coarse brown sugar, and one ounce of pounded allspice
2. Mix them well together
3. Rub the whole well on
