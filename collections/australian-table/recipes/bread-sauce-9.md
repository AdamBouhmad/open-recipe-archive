## Ingredients

- two onions
- milk
- stale bread
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil two onions, cut in parts, in milk until reduced to pulp
2. Pour the milk on stale bread, and cover it close
3. In half an hour put it into a saucepan, with a piece of butter, and add a very small quantity of flour
4. add pepper and salt, and let it simmer for ten minutes
