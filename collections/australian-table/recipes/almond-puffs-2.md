## Ingredients

- two ounces of sweet almonds
- four ounces of bitter almonds
- two ounces of butter
- two table-spoonfuls of flour
- two ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound two ounces of sweet and four ounces of bitter almonds
2. Melt two ounces of butter
3. Dredge in two table-spoonfuls of flour and add two ounces of sugar and the pounded almonds
4. Beat the mixture well and bake in a moderate oven
5. Turn them out on a dish with the bottom of the puff uppermost and serve
