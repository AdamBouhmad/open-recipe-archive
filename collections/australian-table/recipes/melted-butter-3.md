## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- two table-spoonfuls of milk
- six table-spoonfuls water

## Directions

1. Cut about two ounces of butter into pieces
2. Put it into a stewpan with a dessert-spoonful of flour and two table-spoonfuls of milk
3. When well mixed add six table-spoonfuls more water
4. Hold it over the fire and let it simmer till thick, turning it one way
