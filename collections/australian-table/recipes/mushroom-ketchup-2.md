## Ingredients

- Fresh gathered mushrooms
- Common salt
- Cloves, half an ounce per gallon
- Mustard-seed, half an ounce per gallon
- Allspice, one ounce per gallon
- Black pepper, one ounce per gallon
- Ginger bruised, one ounce per gallon

## Directions

1. Sprinkle mushrooms with common salt for three days
2. Squeeze out the juice
3. To each gallon of juice add cloves and mustard-seed of each half an ounce
4. Add allspice, black pepper, and ginger bruised, one ounce
5. Boil sufficiently
