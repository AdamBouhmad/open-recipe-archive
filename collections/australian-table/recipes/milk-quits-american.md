## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour, sifted
- half a pound of butter
- two eggs
- six wine-glasses of milk
- two wine-glasses of yeast

## Directions

1. Cut the butter into the milk, and warm it lightly in the stove
2. Sift the flour into the pan and pour the milk and butter into it
3. Beat the eggs and pour them in also, lastly the yeast
4. Mix all well together, flour your paste-board, put the dough on it and knead it
5. Cut the dough in small pieces and knead them in round balls
6. Stick the tops of them with a fork, lay them in butter-pans, and set them to rise
7. When they are light, put them in a moderate oven and bake them
