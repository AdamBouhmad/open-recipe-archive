## Ingredients

- fowl
- small onion
- chopped parsley
- two sticks of celery
- pound of sausages
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- water
- butter
- jelly

## Directions

1. Put a fowl, breast downwards, in an earthen pan with a cover
2. Shred over it a small onion, a table-spoonful of chopped parsley, and two sticks of celery
3. Fill in the hollow with a pound of sausages
4. Season with pepper, salt, and cayenne to taste
5. Cover it close
6. Put in the oven just after breakfast and forget it till dinner
7. If it should be forgotten till night, take off the cover before putting it away in the larder, and in the morning turn it out in a mould of jelly
8. If the celery is bought, i.e., long gathered, add a little water
9. If you have no sausages, add lumps of butter and more water
