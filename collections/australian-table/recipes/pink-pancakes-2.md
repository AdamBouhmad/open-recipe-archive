## Ingredients

- a large red beetroot
- the yolks of five eggs
- two table-spoonfuls of flour
- four table-spoonfuls of cream
- powdered sugar
- nutmeg
- a wineglass of brandy
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Boil a large red beetroot until it is very tender
2. Peel it, cut it into thin slices, and pound it to a pulp in a marble mortar
3. Add the yolks of eggs, flour, cream, powdered sugar, nutmeg, and brandy
4. Rub the whole into a batter
5. Fry the pancakes with lard
6. Serve hot, garnished with sweetmeats
