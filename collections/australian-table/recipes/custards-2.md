## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of milk
- a bit of lemon-peel
- a couple of peach-leaves
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- two ounces and a half of powdered white sugar
- the yolks of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Put a pint and a half of milk on the fire in a clean saucepan
2. When it boils add a bit of lemon-peel, a couple of peach-leaves, a salt-spoonful of salt, and two ounces and a half of powdered white sugar
3. Let the milk simmer until it has acquired the flavour of the lemon-peel and peach-leaves, but keep stirring it all the time, or it will burn
4. Use a wooden spoon for this purpose
5. Have the yolks of eight eggs ready beaten up, put them into the milk, and stir the whole over the fire until the custard is thick
6. If it boils or becomes too hot it will curdle
7. When cold put into glasses
