## Ingredients

- two ounces of beef suet, chopped fine
- three ounces of bread crumbs
- some parsley
- shalot
- marjoram
- thyme
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- a little cayenne
- the yolk and white of an egg

## Directions

1. Mix two ounces of beef suet, chopped fine, three ounces of bread crumbs, some parsley, shalot, marjoram, thyme, nutmeg, pepper, salt, a little cayenne thick with the yolk and white of an egg
