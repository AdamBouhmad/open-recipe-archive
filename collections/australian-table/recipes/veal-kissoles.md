## Ingredients

- veal
- cooked ham
- Bechamel sauce
- pastry

## Directions

1. Mince and pound veal fine
2. Grate into it some remains of cooked ham
3. Mix them together with Bechamel sauce
4. Form into balls
5. Inclose each in pastry
6. Fry them of a nice brown
