## Ingredients

- sherry, six table-spoonfuls
- brandy, two table-spoonfuls
- sugar, an ounce and a half
- two or three shreds of lemon-peel
- one bottle of claret
- a bottle of soda-water
- a lump of ice
- a little borage

## Directions

1. Add sherry, brandy, sugar, and lemon-peel to one bottle of claret and a bottle of soda-water
2. Keep in a cool place or refrigerator and only open before drinking
3. Add a lump of ice and a little borage as improvements
