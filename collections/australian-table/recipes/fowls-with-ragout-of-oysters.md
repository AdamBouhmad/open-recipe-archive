## Ingredients

- forcemeat
- one dozen oysters
- fowls
- thin slices of bacon
- writing paper
- gravy
- oysters
- ketchup
- lemon juice
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a forcemeat, and add to it one dozen oysters
2. Fill the craw of the fowls with it
3. Truss them
4. Put on their breasts thin slices of bacon with writing paper over it
5. Roast them
6. Make some gravy, and put into it some oysters, a little ketchup, and the juice of a lemon
7. Thicken with flour and butter
8. Take away the bacon and paper
9. Serve the fowls in the gravy
