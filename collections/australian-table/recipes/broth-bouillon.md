## Ingredients

- a pound of beef or veal
- an onion
- a carrot
- a bit of bacon
- a glass of water
- another glass of water
- some salt
- about a quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Take a pound of beef or veal, cut into dice, chop them, and put them into a stewpan, with an onion, a carrot, a bit of bacon, and a glass of water.
2. Just as it comes to the boil throw in another glass of water, add some salt, boil it a little longer, and add about a quart of boiling water.
3. Take it up, strain, and serve.
