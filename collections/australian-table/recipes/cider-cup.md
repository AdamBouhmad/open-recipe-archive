## Ingredients

- two quarts of best bottled cider
- sugar
- half a nutmeg
- a glass of brandy
- a glass of noyeau
- a lemon

## Directions

1. Mix together two quarts of best bottled cider
2. Sweeten to taste, taking care that the sugar is perfectly melted
3. Add half a nutmeg grated and a glass of brandy
4. Add a glass of noyeau
5. Cut a lemon in moderately thin slices and let them remain there
6. Make it two hours before wanted and stand it in some ice
