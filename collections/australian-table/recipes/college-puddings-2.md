## Ingredients

- four eggs
- two ounces of flour
- a little nutmeg and ginger
- three ounces of white sugar
- six ounces of suet, chopped fine
- six ounces of currants, well washed and cleaned
- a glass of white wine or brandy

## Directions

1. Beat up four eggs with two ounces of flour, a little nutmeg and ginger, and three ounces of white sugar.
2. Beat to a batter.
3. Add six ounces of suet, chopped fine, six ounces of currants, well washed and cleaned, with a glass of white wine or brandy.
4. Bake in an oven or patty-pan, or boil and serve with wine sauce.
