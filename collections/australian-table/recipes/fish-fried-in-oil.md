## Ingredients

- soles, plaice, or salmon
- beaten eggs
- flour
- frying-oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. When prepared by salting or drying, have a dish ready with beaten eggs
2. Turn the fish well over in them, and sprinkle it freely with flour, so that the fish may be covered entirely with it
3. Place it in a pan with a quantity of the best frying-oil, at boiling heat
4. Fry the fish in it gently till of a fine, equal, brown colour
5. When done it should be placed before the fire for the oil to drain off
6. Take care that the oil should have ceased to bubble when the fish is put in, otherwise it will be greasy
7. The oil will serve again if strained off and poured into a jar
8. Fish preserved in this way is usually served cold, garnished with parsley
