## Ingredients

- a lump of ice
- two glasses of cool sherry
- a spoonful of white sugar
- a few small pieces of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a lump of ice and fix it at the edge of a board
2. rasp it with the proper tool
3. collect the fine raspings in a capacious tumbler
4. pour thereon two glasses of cool sherry, and a spoonful of white sugar, with a few small pieces of lemon
5. Stir with a macerator, and drink through a tube of macaroni
