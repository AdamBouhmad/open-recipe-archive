## Ingredients

- one pound of the finest powdered loaf sugar
- half a pint of water
- the white of an egg

## Directions

1. Whisk the mixture till it boils, taking off the scum as it rises
2. Let it boil five minutes, then run it through a tamis
3. Put it again on the fire and boil it up
4. When it has reached caramel height, it must be taken off
5. To ascertain the latter, when the syrup has become thick, dip into it the handle of a spoon, and immediately put it into cold water: if the sugar on it becomes crisp, it is done
6. To spin a basket or any other ornament, when the caramel begins to cool take some up with a spoon, and run it cleverly in thin threads over the mould, which must have been oiled
