## Ingredients

- two pounds of steaks
- water
- six large onions
- pepper
- salt
- a carrot
- two table-spoonfuls of flour
- ketchup
- a tea-spoonful of mustard

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of steaks, and put them into a stewpan with just sufficient water to cover them, and let them simmer for half an hour
2. then add six large onions whole, with a little pepper and salt, a carrot cut up, and some thickening, consisting of two table-spoonfuls of flour and some ketchup, and a tea-spoonful of mustard
3. Let it simmer for an hour longer, and the steaks will be done
4. They must be sufficiently cooked, and at the same time not done to rags, or they are spoilt
