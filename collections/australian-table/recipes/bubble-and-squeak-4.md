## Ingredients

- cold salt meat
- cold cabbage
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slightly fry cold salt meat in a pan
2. Cut cold cabbage into pieces
3. Put a little salt and pepper on it
4. Warm the cabbage
5. Dish up the fried cabbage, and garnish with the beef
