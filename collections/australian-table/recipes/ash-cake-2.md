## Ingredients

- meal
- water

## Directions

1. Scald the meal, and put it in little heaps to cool
2. Mix it with more water (warm) into dough, and mould it into flat cakes rather larger than a breakfast saucer
3. Open a place in the side of a wood fire on the hearth, and put in the cakes, each between two cabbage leaves
4. Lay them on the hot hearth, sprinkle some ashes lightly over first
5. Put hot coals on the top, and if these appear to cool fast, remove them from time to time, and replace them with hotter coals from the fire
