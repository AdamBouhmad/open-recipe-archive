## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. of Beefsteak
- 1/2 lb. Potatoes
- 2 oz. Dripping
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 tablespoonful Milk
- 1 Onion
- 1/2 teaspoonful Sage

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the potatoes with the butter, milk, and salt.
2. Lay the steak flat and spread the potatoes over it.
3. Chop the onion very fine and powder the sage, and sprinkle over the potatoes.
4. Roll up and tie with a tape or string.
5. Rub some dripping over a baking sheet, put in the steak, and plenty of dripping on the top.
6. Put into a moderate oven and bake for an hour, basting frequently.
7. Put on to a hot dish, take off the tapes, and pour round it some nice gravy.
8. Send mashed potatoes to table with it.
