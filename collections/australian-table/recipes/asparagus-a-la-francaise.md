## Ingredients

- asparagus
- boiled onion
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolk
- toasted bread
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil some asparagus
2. Chop the heads and tender parts of the stalks together with a boiled onion into small pieces
3. Add a little salt and pepper and the beaten yolk of an egg
4. Beat up and serve on sippets of toasted bread with melted butter poured over
