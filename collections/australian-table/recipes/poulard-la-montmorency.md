## Ingredients

- fowl
- liver
- bacon
- hard eggs
- pale stock
- flour
- butter
- onions
- carrots
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Lard the fowl, and stuff it with the liver, cut in pieces, some bacon, and hard eggs
2. Stew it in pale stock, with flour browned in butter, some onions, carrots, and a few fine herbs
3. Reduce the sauce to a glaze, and serve with the fowl
