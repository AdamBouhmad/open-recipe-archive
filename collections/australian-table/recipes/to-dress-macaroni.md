## Ingredients

- macaroni
- milk or water
- salt
- cheese, grated or cut into small pieces
- butter
- bread-crumbs
- pepper or cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the quantity required on the fire to boil, either in milk or water, with a little salt
2. When done, take the macaroni out of the liquid, and put into a tin dish that will fit into a toaster or salamander
3. Have ready cheese grated, or cut up into suitable small pieces
4. Place cheese on the macaroni, with a small quantity of butter and a few bread-crumbs
5. Put a little salt in, with grated cheese on the top
6. Brown before the fire, and serve hot
7. Let the consumer use either pepper or cayenne, according to fancy; put none in the dish
