## Ingredients

- one pound of white sugar
- a quarter of a pint of water
- eight well-beaten eggs
- one pound of matso flour
- a little grated lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Simmer one pound of white sugar in a quarter of a pint of water
2. Pour hot upon eight well-beaten eggs
3. Beat till cold
4. Add one pound of matso flour and a little grated lemon-peel
5. Bake in a prepared tin, or in small tins
6. Remove the cake while hot
