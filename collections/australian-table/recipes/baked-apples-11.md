## Ingredients

- apples
- fresh butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Scoop out the inside of the apple by the stalk, leaving the eye impierced
2. Fill the hole with fresh butter
3. Place the apple on a plate (not tin), crown downwards
4. Sprinkle it thickly with powdered sugar
5. Set it to bake very slowly in an oven
6. Eat it before it cools
