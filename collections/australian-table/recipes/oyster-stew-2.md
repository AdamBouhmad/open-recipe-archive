## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Oysters
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1/2 pint Milk
- 1/2 oz. Flour
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Make a sauce by directions given, using a little of the oyster liquor mixed with the milk; flavour with salt and pepper, and a little nutmeg and lemon juice.
2. Stir in the oysters and simmer for five minutes, it is then ready to serve.
