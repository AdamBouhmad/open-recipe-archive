## Ingredients

- light suet crust
- jam

## Directions

1. Make a light suet crust and roll it out thin
2. Spread the jam equally over it, leaving a small margin of paste without any where the pudding joins
3. Roll it up and fasten the ends securely
4. Tie it in a floured cloth and boil for two hours
