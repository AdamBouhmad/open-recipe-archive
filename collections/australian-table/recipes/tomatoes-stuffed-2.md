## Ingredients

- 6 Tomatoes
- 1 lb. Veal Forcemeat
- 1 oz. Cheese
- 6 pieces Fried Bread

## Directions

1. Choose tomatoes of a good colour, and all about the same size; scoop out the centre.
2. Grate up the cheese and mix it with the forcemeat, put this into the tomatoes.
3. Place on a buttered tin, and bake in the oven for ten minutes.
4. Put each tomato on to a round of fried bread, and serve.
