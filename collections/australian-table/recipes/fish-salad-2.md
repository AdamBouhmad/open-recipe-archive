## Ingredients

- Cold Boiled Fish
- 1 Lettuce
- 1 Egg
- Salad Dressing, or Remoulade Sauce

## Directions

1. Make a salad dressing the same as that given for lettuce salad
2. Flake up the fish free from skin and bone
3. Wash and dry the lettuce and shred it up, mix the fish with the dressing
4. Put a layer of lettuce at the bottom of the bowl, then one of fish and dressing
5. Do this alternately, leaving plenty of lettuce for the top
6. Garnish with hard boiled eggs cut into slices
