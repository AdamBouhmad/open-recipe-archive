## Ingredients

- meat
- broth or gravy
- fried onion
- pepper
- salt
- ketchup or other sauce
- flour
- sippets of toast
- port wine
- saffron
- chorissa

## Directions

1. Put meat on the fire in a little broth or gravy with a little fried onion, pepper, salt, and a spoonful of ketchup or any other sauce at hand
2. Let it simmer for about ten minutes
3. Mix in a cup a little flour with a little of the gravy
4. Pour it into the stewpan to thicken the rest
5. Serve sippets of toast with hashes
6. A little port wine, a pinch of saffron, or a piece of chorissa may be considered great improvements
