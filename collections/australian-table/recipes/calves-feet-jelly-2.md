## Ingredients

- four calves' feet
- water
- pound of lump sugar
- peel of two lemons
- juice of six lemons
- six whites of eggs
- bottle of sherry

## Directions

1. Slit the calves' feet in two and wash them in lukewarm water
2. Put them into simmer for six hours in sufficient water to cover them
3. When reduced to half the quantity, after skimming it, strain it through a sieve
4. Skim off all the oily part on the top of the liquor
5. Put the liquor in a stewpan to melt, with a pound of lump sugar, the peel of two lemons, the juice of six, six whites of eggs and shells beat well together, and a bottle of sherry
6. Whisk the whole together until it simmers for half an hour
7. Strain through a jelly-bag
