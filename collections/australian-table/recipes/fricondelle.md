## Ingredients

- cold veal or poultry
- French roll
- white gravy
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- essence of lemon
- two or three eggs
- crumbs of bread

## Directions

1. Prepare cold veal or poultry
2. Add a French roll soaked in white gravy with sweet herbs, pepper, salt, parsley, and essence of lemon
3. Mix all well with two or three eggs
4. Form it into shapes
5. Sprinkle with crumbs of bread
6. Place in a frying-pan as deep as a shallow saucepan
7. When they have fried enough to become set, pour enough white gravy in the pan to cover the fricondelles
8. Let them stew in it gently
9. Place them both in the same dish and pour over any well-thickened sauce that may be selected
