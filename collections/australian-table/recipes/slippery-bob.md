## Ingredients

- kangaroo brains
- flour
- water
- pepper
- salt
- emeu fat

## Directions

1. Take kangaroo brains, and mix with flour and water, and make into batter.
2. Well season with pepper, salt, &c.
3. Pour a table-spoonful at a time into an iron pot containing emeu fat.
4. Take them out when done.
