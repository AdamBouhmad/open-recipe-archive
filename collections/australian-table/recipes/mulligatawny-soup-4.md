## Ingredients

- a few onions
- a few slices of bacon
- a fowl
- a little garlic
- about a quart and a half of stock
- two tablespoonfuls of curry
- salt
- lemon pickle or mango juice
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Fry a few onions of a brown colour
2. Put a few slices of bacon into the stewpan and cut a fowl up in moderate pieces, then brown them
3. Put in the fried onions, a little garlic, and about a quart and a half of stock
4. Simmer till the fowl is tender
5. Skim carefully, and when the fowl is nearly done, rub two tablespoonfuls of curry to the soup
6. Add salt and lemon pickle or mango juice
7. Send up boiled rice with the mulligatawny in a separate dish
