## Ingredients

- a table-spoonful of flour
- a table-spoonful of brandy or orange-flower water
- eight yolks of eggs
- a little salt
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Mix a table-spoonful of flour with a table-spoonful of brandy or orange-flower water, eight yolks of eggs, and a little salt
2. When well mixed, add a quarter of a pound of sugar to a pint of milk, which pour over the eggs, stirring all the time
3. Put the mixture into a buttered tart-pan, and bake in a moderate oven
4. Powder it with sugar and serve
