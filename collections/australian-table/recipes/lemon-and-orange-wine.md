## Ingredients

- ten pounds of loaf sugar
- four gallons of water
- rind of eighteen oranges and lemons
- eighteen oranges and lemons
- two pounds of white sugar
- a little yeast

## Directions

1. Boil ten pounds of loaf sugar in four gallons of water for half an hour
2. Pour the liquor on the rind of eighteen oranges and lemons
3. Squeeze the fruit, and boil it in a syrup with two pounds of white sugar
4. Mix the syrup with the liquor, and let it cool
5. Ferment with a little yeast
6. When the fermentation subsides, bung it down, and bottle in three or four months
