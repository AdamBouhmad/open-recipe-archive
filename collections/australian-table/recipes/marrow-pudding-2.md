## Ingredients

- a penny loaf
- a pint of boiling cream or milk
- a pound of beef marrow
- four eggs
- a wineglass of brandy or sherry
- sugar and nutmeg to taste
- two ounces of candied citron

## Directions

1. Grate the penny loaf into crumbs and pour the boiling cream or milk on it.
2. Cut the beef marrow very thin.
3. Beat the four eggs well and add the wineglass of brandy or sherry, with sugar and nutmeg to taste.
4. Mix all well together.
5. Either boil or bake for three-quarters of an hour.
6. Cut the candied citron thin and stick the pieces all over it when served up.
