## Ingredients

- a quart of good lemonade
- the yolks of eight eggs
- a small quantity of dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Make a quart of good lemonade sweet
2. Strain it, and put it into a stewpan over the fire
3. Add the yolks of eight eggs, well beaten up, and stir the cream well
4. Put it into cream-glasses, or a cream-dish
5. If to be put in moulds, add a small quantity of dissolved isinglass
