## Ingredients

- 4 Eggs
- 1 pint Tomato Sauce
- Fried Bread
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley

## Directions

1. Take some thick tomato sauce and pour it on to a hot dish
2. Poach the eggs carefully and lay them on the sauce
3. Garnish with parsley and fried bread, and serve hot
