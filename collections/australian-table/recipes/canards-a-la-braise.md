## Ingredients

- pair of ducks
- couple of slices of bacon or ham
- two onions
- salt
- pepper
- glass of claret or port
- morels, capers, or artichokes
- consomme or stock

## Directions

1. Lard a pair of ducks
2. Place them in a stewpan breasts downwards
3. Lay a couple of slices of bacon or ham in the stewpan with two onions sliced, salt, pepper, and a glass of claret or port
4. Let it stew gently
5. When done serve with sauce; morels, capers, or artichokes may be sliced and added with a small quantity of consomme or stock
