## Ingredients

- haggis bag
- drought
- liver
- beef
- suet
- onions
- dried meal
- salt
- mixed spices
- water

## Directions

1. Make the haggis bag perfectly clean
2. Parboil the drought, boil the liver very well, so as it will grate, dry the meat before the fire
3. Mince the drought and a pretty large piece of beef very small
4. Grate about half the liver
5. Mince plenty of the suet and some onions small
6. Mix all these ingredients very well together, with a handful or two of the dried meal
7. Spread them on the table, and season them properly with salt and mixed spices
8. Take any of the scraps of beef that is left from mincing, and some of the water that boiled the drought, and make about a chopin (i.e., a quart) of good stock of it
9. Put all the haggis meat into the bag, and that broth in it
10. Sew the bag, but be sure to put out all the wind, before you sew it quite close
11. If you think the bag is thin, you may put it in a cloth
12. If it is a large haggis, it will take at least two hours boiling
