## Ingredients

- peacock
- truffles
- mushrooms
- livers of fowls
- bacon
- salt
- spice
- thyme
- crumbs of bread
- bay-leaf
- butter
- water
- lemon
- flowers

## Directions

1. Take off the skin with the greatest care, so that the feathers do not get detached or broken
2. Stuff it with truffles, mushrooms, livers of fowls, bacon, salt, spice, thyme, crumbs of bread, and a bay-leaf
3. Wrap the claws and head in several folds of cloth
4. Envelope the body in buttered paper
5. Baste the head and claws with water during the cooking, to preserve them, and especially the tuft
6. Before taking it off the spit, brown the bird by removing the paper
7. Garnish with lemon and flowers
8. If to come on the table cold, place the bird in a wooden trencher, in the middle of which is fixed a wooden skewer, which should penetrate the body of the bird, to keep it upright
9. Arrange the claws and feathers in a natural manner, and the tail like a fan, supported with wire
