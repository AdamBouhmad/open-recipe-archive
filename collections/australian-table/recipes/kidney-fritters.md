## Ingredients

- 6 Kidneys
- 1 teaspoonful Minced Herbs
- 1 teaspoonful Onion
- Frying Batter
- Pepper and Salt
- Hot Fat

## Directions

1. Skin the kidneys and cut up each one into three or four slices.
2. Make a frying batter by directions given elsewhere.
3. Stir in the minced onions and herbs, and season with pepper and salt.
4. Dip the slices of kidney into this and plunge into very hot fat.
5. Fry a good colour, pile high on a dish, garnish with fried parsley, and serve very hot.
