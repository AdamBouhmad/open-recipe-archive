## Ingredients

- pound of sweet almonds
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar in powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds
2. Cut each almond into four or five slices
3. Dry them on a slow fire, taking care not to let them burn
4. Put three-quarters of a pound of sugar in powder into a pan
5. When melted through put in the almonds hot
6. Mix them well together
7. Put a layer round a buttered mould
8. Smooth them, round equally with a lemon
9. When small let it cool
10. If not quite hard when cold, put your shapes into a cool oven to dry
