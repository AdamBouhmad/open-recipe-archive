## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of flour
- half a salt-spoon of salt
- a little lemon-juice
- about a quarter of a pint of the coolest water
- the yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Put the flour on the paste-board and make a hole in the centre
2. Put the yolk of an egg, lemon-juice, and salt in the hole
3. Mix the whole with cold water into a flexible paste with the right hand, handling it as little as possible
4. Squeeze all the buttermilk from the butter and wring it through a cloth
5. Roll out the paste and place the butter on this
6. Fold the edges of the paste over to hide the butter
7. Roll it out again to the thickness of a quarter of an inch
8. Fold over one-third, over which again pass the rolling-pin
9. Fold over the other third, thus forming a square
10. Place it with the ends, top, and bottom before you
11. Repeat the rolls and turns twice more
12. Flour a baking-sheet and put the paste on this
13. Let it remain in a cool place for half an hour
14. Roll twice more, turning it as before
15. Place it again in a cool place for a quarter of an hour
16. Give two more rolls, making seven in all
