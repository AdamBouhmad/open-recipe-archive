## Ingredients

- chocolate
- boiling water
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Do not grate the chocolate, but cut the quantity required
2. Boil it in water
3. To an ounce of chocolate put an ounce and a half of boiling water
4. Add cream or milk
5. Do not allow it to boil over if you desire the flavour to be preserved
6. It must not be boiled a second time
