## Ingredients

- lean beef
- pork
- common salt
- saltpetre
- sugar
- black pepper
- allspice
- cayenne
- garlic
- shalot

## Directions

1. Salt a piece of lean beef and pork with common salt, a little saltpetre, sugar, black pepper, and allspice, for two days
2. Season with pepper, cayenne, garlic, and a shalot
3. Mince fine and put into clean gut
4. Smoke them
5. When wanted for use they may be boiled and eaten cold
