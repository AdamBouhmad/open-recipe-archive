## Ingredients

- oysters
- their shells
- their liquor
- pepper
- butter
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Take them from the shells and beard them
2. put them with their liquor into scallop-tins, with a little pepper and butter
3. Put the shells upon a gridiron, over a good fire
4. serve them when plump and hot
5. Squeeze a little lemon-juice on them as they come from the fire
