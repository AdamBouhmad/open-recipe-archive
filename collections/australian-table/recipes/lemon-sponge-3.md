## Ingredients

- half an ounce of isinglass
- boiling water
- juice of eight lemons
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take half an ounce of isinglass and dissolve it in a little boiling water
2. Take the juice of eight lemons, and put sugar to your taste
3. Whisk it together until it becomes a sponge
4. Wet the mould and put it in
5. When set, turn it out
