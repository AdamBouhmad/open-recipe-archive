## Ingredients

- veal
- fat
- two spoonfuls of shalot
- one spoonful of salt
- half a spoonful of pepper
- nutmeg
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Chop up some veal, with a little fat
2. Add two spoonfuls of shalot, one of salt, half a one of pepper, a little nutmeg
3. Chop the whole, to give the shape of a cutlet
4. Egg well with bread crumbs
5. Fry it of a light brown, and serve with any sauce you please
