## Ingredients

- 2 Mullet
- 6 Tomatoes
- Bread Crumbs
- 1 teaspoonful Parsley
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 oz. Butter

## Directions

1. Fillet and slice up the mullet, season each slice with parsley, pepper, and salt
2. Dip the tomatoes in boiling water, skim and slice them up
3. Butter a pie-dish, lay in the slices of fish and tomatoes alternately
4. Cover the top with bread crumbs and little pieces of butter
5. Cover with buttered paper and bake in a moderate oven for half an hour
6. Take off the paper, and serve hot
