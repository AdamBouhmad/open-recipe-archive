## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Pastry
- 1 oz. Currants
- 1 oz. Raisins
- Half a Lemon
- Half an Orange
- 1 oz. Cake or Bread Crumbs
- 1 oz. Sugar

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins and chop them lightly, put them into a basin with the currants cleaned, the sugar, and the cake or bread crumbs.
2. Mix together, grate over the rind of half a lemon, and half an orange.
3. Strain in the juice, and let it stand for an hour.
4. Roll out the pastry and cut into rounds about three inches long.
5. Lay a little of the mixture in the centre, close over the pastry, turn the cake over, flatten it out in the middle.
6. Brush over with sugar, and bake in rather a quick oven.
7. Serve warm.
