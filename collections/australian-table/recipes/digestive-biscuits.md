## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. Brown Meal
- 1/4 lb. Flour
- 1 1/2 gills Water
- 1 oz. Butter or Lard
- 1 oz. Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the meal and flour together
2. Rub in the butter or lard and the sugar
3. Mix into a dry paste with water
4. Knead till smooth
5. Roll out very thin
6. Cut into rounds
7. Bake in rather a slow oven
