## Ingredients

- pheasants
- partridges
- game birds
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- herbs
- shalots
- flour
- salt
- spice
- stock
- wine

## Directions

1. Split the birds in halves
2. Stew them in butter, until nearly half done
3. Make a sauce of the butter, with mushrooms, chopped parsley, a few herbs and shalots
4. Add a little flour, salt, and spice; with some stock, and a glass of wine
5. Stew this sauce
6. Pour it over the halves of the birds
7. Wrap them in paper
8. Broil for about fifteen minutes
9. Serve in the paper, with the sauce as an accompaniment
