## Ingredients

- flour
- apples mashed
- yeast

## Directions

1. Take apples and flour, in the proportion of two parts of flour to one of apples mashed well together
2. Add a quantity of yeast, and allow it to rise a sufficient time
3. Use very little water if the apples are juicy
4. Bake in long loaves
