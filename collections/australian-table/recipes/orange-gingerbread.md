## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- a pound and three-quarters of treacle
- six ounces of candied orange-peel
- three-quarters of a pound of moist sugar
- one ounce of ground ginger
- one ounce of allspice
- half or three-quarters of a pound of butter
- yolk of eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Add to two pounds of flour a pound and three-quarters of treacle, six ounces of candied orange-peel cut small, three-quarters of a pound of moist sugar, one ounce of ground ginger, and one ounce of allspice
2. Melt half or three-quarters of a pound of butter
3. Mix the whole well together and lay it by for six hours
4. Roll it out into pieces in the form of chequers
5. Bake an inch apart
6. Rub them over with a brush dipped in yolk of eggs and milk
7. When baked, rub over a second time
