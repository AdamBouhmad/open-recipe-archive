## Ingredients

- 1 tin sardines
- 1 oz. mustard
- vinegar
- cayenne
- salt
- dripping
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Make the mustard with vinegar instead of water
2. Stir into it some cayenne and salt
3. Rub the sardines over with this mixture
4. Either fry them in a little dripping or grill them
5. Cut the toast into fingers
6. Lay a sardine on each piece and serve hot
