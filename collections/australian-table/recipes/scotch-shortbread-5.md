## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- twelve ounces of fine sugar
- two pounds of flour
- four eggs
- slices of candied lemon-peel
- carraway comfits

## Directions

1. Rub one pound of butter and twelve ounces of fine sugar into two pounds of flour, with the hand
2. Make it into a stiff paste with four eggs
3. Roll it out to double the thickness of a penny-piece
4. Cut it into cakes, pinch the edges
5. Stick slices of candied lemon-peel and carraway comfits on the top
6. Bake on plates in a moderate oven
