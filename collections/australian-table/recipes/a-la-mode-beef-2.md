## Ingredients

- ten pounds of lean beef
- two ounces of beef dripping
- a couple of onions
- flour
- boiling water
- one drachm of black pepper
- two of allspice
- four bay leaves

## Directions

1. Take ten pounds of lean beef of any kind, cut it into small pieces
2. put two ounces of beef dripping and a couple of onions into a stewpan
3. As soon as it is hot, flour the meat and put it into the pan
4. keep stirring it about
5. When it has been some few minutes in the pan, dredge it with flour, and cover it with boiling water
6. Skim it when it boils, and put in one drachm of black pepper, two of allspice, and four bay leaves
7. Set the pan by the side of the fire, and let it simmer for three hours
8. When you find the meat sufficiently tender serve it in a tureen
9. when cold it ought to jelly
