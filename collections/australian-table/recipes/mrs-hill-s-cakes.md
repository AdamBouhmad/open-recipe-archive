## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- a pound of sugar
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- the yolks of two eggs
- the peel of two lemons

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of flour, a pound of sugar, a quarter of a pound of butter, the yolks of two eggs, and the peel of two lemons cut very small
2. Roll into thin cakes
3. Bake in the oven after the bread is drawn
