## Ingredients

- loin of pork
- onions
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the skin of the loin across at the distance of half an inch
2. Roast as usual
3. Cut two onions up small, and put them into the dripping-pan, with a pint of vinegar
4. Baste well with this, and serve hot
