## Ingredients

- four pounds of the tops of wormwood
- one ounce root of angelica
- one ounce calamus aromaticus
- one ounce aniseed
- one ounce leaves of dittany
- four gallons alcohol
- water
- oil of aniseed

## Directions

1. Take of the tops of wormwood, four pounds; root of angelica, calamus aromaticus, aniseed, leaves of dittany, of each one ounce
2. Macerate these substances during eight days
3. Add a little water
4. Distil by a gentle fire until two gallons are obtained
5. Reduce to a proof spirit
6. Add a few drops of the oil of aniseed
