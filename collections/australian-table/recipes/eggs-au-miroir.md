## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Spread a piece of butter upon a dish that can be set on the fire.
2. Break the eggs over it, adding salt, pepper, and two spoonfuls of milk.
3. Place it on a slow fire, with a red-hot shovel over it, and serve when the eggs are set.
