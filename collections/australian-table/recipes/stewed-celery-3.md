## Ingredients

- celery
- water
- lemon
- mace
- butter
- flour
- egg
- nutmeg
- cream
- gravy or stock

## Directions

1. Take off the outside and the green ends of the celery
2. Boil them in water till tender
3. Put in a slice of lemon and a little mace
4. Thicken with butter and flour
5. Beat up an egg or two and grate in some nutmeg
6. Mix with a table-spoonful of cream
7. Put to it half a cupful of gravy or stock
8. Let it simmer until sufficiently thick
9. Serve hot
