## Ingredients

- 2 Mullet
- Frying Batter
- Hot Fat
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet the mullet and cut into small pieces
2. Dip in flour seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Cover with French frying batter
4. Plunge into plenty of hot fat and fry until a good colour
5. Drain for a few minutes on kitchen paper
6. Pile high on a dish, garnish with parsley, and serve hot
