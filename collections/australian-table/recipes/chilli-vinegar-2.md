## Ingredients

- an ounce of cayenne pepper or green birds'-eye chillies
- a pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put an ounce of cayenne pepper, or the same quantity of the green birds'-eye chillies, to a pint of vinegar, and allow the infusion to remain a month
2. You may filter it if you please, or not
