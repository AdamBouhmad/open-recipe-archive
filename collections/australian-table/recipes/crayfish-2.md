## Ingredients

- crayfish
- milk
- mace
- onion
- cayenne
- salt
- lemon
- butter
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil, in a pint of milk, two blades of mace, one onion sliced, cayenne, and salt, with lemon, a little butter and flour, and a gill of cream
2. Serve with crayfish
