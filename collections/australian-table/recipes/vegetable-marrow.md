## Ingredients

- marrow
- white sauce
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the marrow, take out the seeds, and cut it into small pieces
2. Put into boiling water nicely seasoned with salt, and boil gently for about fifteen minutes
3. Take up with a slice and strain in a colander
4. Place in a hot dish, and pour over the sauce
