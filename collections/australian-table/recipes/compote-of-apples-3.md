## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar
- a glass of wine
- a little lemon acid

## Directions

1. Cut apples in halves or quarters, according to their size
2. Put them in to stew with sufficient water and sugar to taste
3. Add a glass of wine, and a little lemon acid
4. Simmer, and when sufficiently done, serve in their own syrup
