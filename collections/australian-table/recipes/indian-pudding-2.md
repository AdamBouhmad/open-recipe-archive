## Ingredients

- four large apples
- grated nutmeg
- two table-spoonfuls of sugar
- a tea-spoonfal of minced lemon-peel
- crust
- six ounces of currants
- three-quarters of a pound of minced suet

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and cut four large apples into slices
2. Put them into a saucepan with some grated nutmeg, two table-spoonfuls of sugar, and a tea-spoonfal of minced lemon-peel
3. Stir them over the fire until soft
4. Have ready some crust and roll it out thin
5. Spread the apples over the paste
6. Sprinkle over the apples six ounces of currants and three-quarters of a pound of minced suet
7. Roll the pudding up, closing the ends
8. Tie it in a floured cloth and boil a couple of hours
