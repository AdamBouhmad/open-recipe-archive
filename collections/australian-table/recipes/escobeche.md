## Ingredients

- cold fried fish
- vinegar
- water
- grated ginger
- allspice
- cayenne pepper
- bay-leaves
- salt
- lemon-juice
- sliced onions

## Directions

1. Take some cold fried fish, and place it in a deep pan
2. Boil half a pint of vinegar with two table-spoonfuls of water, a little grated ginger, allspice, cayenne pepper, two bay-leaves, a little salt, and a table-spoonful of lemon-juice with sliced onions
3. When boiling, pour it over the fish
4. Cover the pan, and let it stand twenty-four hours before serving
