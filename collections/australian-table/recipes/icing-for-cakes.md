## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- the whites of six eggs
- powder blue
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Take one pound of sugar; put into a pan
2. break in the whites of six eggs, and as much powder blue as will lie on a sixpence
3. beat it well with a wooden spoon for ten minutes, then squeeze in the juice of a lemon, and beat it till it becomes transparent
4. Set the cake you intend to ice in an oven or warm place for five minutes
5. then spread over the mixture as smooth as possible
6. A plain ice, or ornament the cake with fancy articles of any description
