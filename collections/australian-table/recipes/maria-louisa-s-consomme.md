## Ingredients

- two quarts of water
- two pounds of lean beef
- a fowl
- two large carrots
- two onions
- a bay leaf
- two cloves

## Directions

1. In about two quarts of water put two pounds of lean beef, cut in slices, a fowl divided, two large carrots, two onions, a bay leaf, and two cloves
2. Let it simmer for six hours
3. Skim off the fat, and serve
