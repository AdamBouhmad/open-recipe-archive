## Ingredients

- 1 Chop
- Pepper and Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Choose a nice loin chop with an undercut.
2. Rub a little butter in a soup plate, lay in the chop, cover with another plate, and stand in a cool oven for an hour.
3. Put on a very hot plate and pour over the gravy which has run from it.
4. Serve very hot.
