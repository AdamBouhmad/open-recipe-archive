## Ingredients

- an ounce of onion
- half an ounce of green sage leaves
- water
- a tea-spoonful of pepper and salt
- an ounce of bread crumbs
- a pint of gravy or broth

## Directions

1. Chop fine an ounce of onion and half an ounce of green sage leaves
2. put them into a stewpan, with a little water
3. simmer for ten minutes
4. then put in a tea-spoonful of pepper and salt and an ounce of bread crumbs
5. mix together
6. then pour in a pint of gravy or broth, and simmer a little longer
