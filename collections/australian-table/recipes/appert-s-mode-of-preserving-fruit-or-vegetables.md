## Ingredients

- Wide-mouthed glass bottles
- Finest corks
- Cold water
- Resin

## Directions

1. Put the article into wide-mouthed glass bottles
2. Use the finest corks and ensure the bottles are dry and when corked, air-tight
3. Put the corked bottles into a saucepan, fill it with cold water, and set it on the fire
4. Boil the bottled articles in the bain marie
5. Remove the saucepan from the fire and let it get almost cold before taking out the bottles
6. Dip the cork in resin
7. Put the bottles in the cellar
8. Fill the bottles to within two inches of the necks only, and not even so high if the bottle only contains liquid
