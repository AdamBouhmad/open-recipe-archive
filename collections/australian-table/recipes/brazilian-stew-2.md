## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Leg of Beef
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 1 tablespoonful Vinegar
- 1 doz. Peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cut the beef up in small pieces, dip them in the vinegar, and lay in a jar.
2. Slice in the vegetables, add the peppercorns, and tie a paper over the top of the jar.
3. Stand in a saucepan of boiling water for three hours.
4. Dish up the meat, garnish with the carrot, strain the gravy, season and flavour, boil up and pour over.
5. Serve hot.
