## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- port wine sauce
- lemon
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Keep them sufficiently
2. Roast them rather under-done, not forgetting to baste well
3. Serve with lemon and port wine sauce, and cayenne pepper
