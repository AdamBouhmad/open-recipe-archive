## Ingredients

- lemons
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Cut off the peel of the lemons, and squeeze them until all the juice is out into a basin
2. strain it through muslin
3. Have ready dry bottles, fill them with the juice until near the top
4. Pour into the bottles after a teaspoon of olive oil
5. Cork them, and place them upright in a cool place
6. Dry the peel, and string it for use
7. When you require to use the juice, open the bottle, and put in a skewer, with some cotton at the end, which will absorb the oil
