## Ingredients

- six ounces of suet
- six ounces of flour
- two ounces of bread crumbs
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- two eggs
- six table-spoons of milk

## Directions

1. Chop six ounces of suet fine, put it into a basin, with six ounces of flour, two ounces of bread crumbs, and a tea-spoonful of salt
2. stir well together, beat up two eggs, and add to them six table-spoons of milk
3. put by degrees into the basin, and stir well together
4. Divide into dumplings, or boil in a cloth floured
5. Boil an hour
