## Ingredients

- The yolks of twelve eggs, leaving out the whites of six
- One pound of white sugar
- The juice of one lemon
- The peel of two lemons cut very fine
- Twelve ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Whisk these ingredients together for half an hour
2. Add twelve ounces of flour
3. Put the cake in the oven and bake an hour
