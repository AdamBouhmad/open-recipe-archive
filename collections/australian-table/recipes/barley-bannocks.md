## Ingredients

- barley-meal
- water
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix barley-meal with water and a little salt
2. Roll them out into a paste, three quarters of an inch thick
3. Divide it into cakes
4. Bake in an oven or on the girdle of a light brown colour
