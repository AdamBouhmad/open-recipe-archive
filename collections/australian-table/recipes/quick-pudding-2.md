## Ingredients

- 1 Egg
- 1 tablespoonful Flour
- 1 tablespoonful Jam
- 1 teaspoonful Sugar
- 1 teaspoonful Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and baking powder together, beat the egg till very light, whisk in the sugar, and stir in the flour.
2. Pour into a buttered tin, and bake five minutes.
3. Turn on to a sugared paper spread with jam.
4. Roll up and serve. Custard sauce is nice with this.
