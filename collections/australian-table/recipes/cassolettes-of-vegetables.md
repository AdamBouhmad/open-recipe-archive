## Ingredients

- Turnips
- Salt
- Water
- Cold vegetables such as beans, peas, carrots, and parsnips
- Butter

## Directions

1. Peel some turnips and scoop out the centre; boil them in salt and water till soft, but quite whole.
2. If there are any cold vegetables in the larder, such as beans, peas, carrots, and parsnips, make them hot; if not, cut some into small pieces and boil separately.
3. Stir them into any cold sauce that may be left, or toss them in a little butter.
4. Fill the turnip cups with these, arranging them on a dish, alternately red and green.
5. Serve hot.
