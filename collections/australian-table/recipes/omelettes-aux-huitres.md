## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- their own liquor
- coulis
- pepper
- eggs
- salt
- parsley
- bread
- cream

## Directions

1. Scald some oysters
2. put two-thirds in a stewpan, with a little butter
3. moisten them with some of their own liquor and some coulis, with a little pepper
4. Take care the oysters are well done
5. break a dozen eggs
6. season them with salt, parsley, chopped, and pepper
7. have some sippets of bread, about the size of half-a-crown
8. give two or three blows of a knife to the oysters that remain
9. put into the beaten eggs, with some cream
10. beat them all together
11. pour your omelette into a pan, with melted butter
12. stir it till well done
13. put the oysters and their sauce in the dish
14. roll your omelette in the fryingpan
15. serve in the middle of the ragout of oysters
