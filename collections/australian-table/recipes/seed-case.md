## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 6 oz. dripping
- 6 oz. sugar
- 1.5 teaspoonsful caraway seeds
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoonsful baking powder
- 1 gill milk or water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin and rub in the dripping carefully, stir in the sugar, baking powder, and caraway seeds.
2. Beat up the egg and milk or water, and mix the dry ingredients into a dough; beat for two or three minutes.
3. Turn into a cake tin which has been well rubbed with dripping, stand on a baking sheet and place in a moderate oven.
4. Bake for one hour and a half or longer, test it by running a skewer right through the centre; if it comes out clean the cake is done.
5. Turn it out of the tin carefully and stand on a sieve till cold.
