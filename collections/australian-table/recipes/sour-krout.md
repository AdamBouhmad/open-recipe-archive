## Ingredients

- seven or eight pounds of beef (brisket or fillet off the shoulder)
- water
- sour-krout

## Directions

1. Boil about seven or eight pounds of beef, either brisket or a fillet off the shoulder, in enough water to cover it
2. When it has boiled for an hour, add as much sour-krout as may be approved
3. It should then stew gently for four hours
4. Be served in a deep dish
5. Remove the fat for a more delicate dish
