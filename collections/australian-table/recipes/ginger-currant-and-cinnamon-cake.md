## Ingredients

- six ounces of butter
- four pounds of flour
- one pound of sugar
- an ounce and a half of volatile salts
- four eggs well beaten
- as much butter as will make into dough
- an ounce of ground ginger
- two ounces of currants
- half an ounce of ground cinnamon
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Rub six ounces of butter into four pounds of flour
2. make a paste, and add one pound of sugar, an ounce and a half of volatile salts, four eggs well beaten, and as much butter as will make into dough
3. divide it into three equal parts
4. add to one half an ounce of ground ginger, to another two ounces of currants, and to the third half an ounce of ground cinnamon
5. Bake on buttered tins, and sprinkle the top with sugar
