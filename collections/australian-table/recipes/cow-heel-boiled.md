## Ingredients

- cow-heel
- onions
- milk
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- ham, bacon, or corned pork

## Directions

1. Let it be perfectly clean.
2. Boil it well for five or six hours, together with five or six onions and a quart of milk.
3. Add a little salt.
4. Serve with parsley and butter, and a little ham, bacon, or corned pork.
