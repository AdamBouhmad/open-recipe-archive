## Ingredients

- boiling water
- sugar
- bottle of rum
- limes
- guava jelly
- green tea, two ounces
- six glasses of cognac
- two glasses of Madeira
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Brew about a pint of green tea (two ounces)
2. Dissolve two-thirds of the sugar in the tea infusion
3. Throw aside the tea-leaves
4. Rub the remainder of the sugar on the rind of the limes
5. Add the delicious acid of the fruit to the already impregnated sugar
6. Dissolve the guava jelly in a pint or so of boiling water
7. Commingle the tea, the sweets, and acids
8. Taste the foundation or sherbet
9. Add six glasses of cognac, two of Madeira, and the bottle of old rum
10. Add about a quart more of boiling water
11. Add the slightest possible sprinkling of nutmeg
