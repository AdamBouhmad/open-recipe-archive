## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Artichokes
- 1/2 pint White Sauce
- Vinegar
- Milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash and peel the artichokes and put them into some water; add a tablespoonful of vinegar and leave them for half an hour.
2. Drain the water all away and put them into a saucepan, cover with cold water; add one gill of milk and some salt.
3. Bring to the boil and cook slowly for about an hour.
4. Take half a pint of the liquor in which the artichokes were boiled, and make a sauce; dish them and pour this over.
