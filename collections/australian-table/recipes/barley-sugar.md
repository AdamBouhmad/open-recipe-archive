## Ingredients

- clarified syrup
- rasped lemon-peel
- butter

## Directions

1. Put clarified syrup, containing rasped lemon-peel, into a saucepan with a lip as for melting butter
2. Boil it to caramel height, carefully skimming as it boils
3. Have ready a marble slab, well buttered
4. Pour the syrup on it of the thickness required for the sticks of barley sugar
5. Twist every stick at each end when hot, and it will assume the form required
