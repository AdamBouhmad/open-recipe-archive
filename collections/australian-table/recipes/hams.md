## Ingredients

- black pepper
- allspice
- saltpetre
- salt
- sugar
- beer

## Directions

1. If the weather will allow it, let them hang for a few days
2. Mix in proportion one ounce of black pepper, one ounce of allspice, one ounce of saltpetre, half a pound of salt, and half a pound of sugar with a quart of beer
3. Boil them together, and pour them hot on the hams
4. Turn them in this pickle for three weeks
5. Let the ham drain, and brush it over with pyroligneous acid, and sprinkle over it some sharps, bran, or pollard
