## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. The water must boil well before the fish is put in, and be seasoned with salt and a teaspoonful of vinegar or lemon juice; lay the fish carefully in, and bring the water to the boil again.
2. Then draw it away from the fire, cover down closely, and keep it just below the boil.
3. About ten minutes to every lb. will be sufficient.
4. It is always done when it begins to leave the bone.
5. Take it out of the water directly it is cooked, and if it is not wanted just at the time, cover it with a cloth and keep it hot.
6. Any kind of fish sauce can be served with it, such as plain melted butter, parsley, or egg sauce.
