## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes thinly, and well wash in cold water, but not soak; put them into a saucepan kept for this vegetable only.
2. Just cover them with cold water seasoned with salt, and bring to a boil.
3. Simmer very gently for about twenty minutes; test them with a fork, and if soft, strain off the water and toss them in a saucepan over the fire until they are dry.
4. If potatoes begin to break soon after they boil up, pour off nearly all the water, leaving only one inch at the bottom of the saucepan, and cook the potatoes slowly in this, then strain off and dry.
5. Potatoes that are very troublesome to boil often steam well; allow from an hour to one hour and a half, according to the quantity of water over which they are cooking.
