## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- half a pint of white wine
- a little strong ale
- sugar
- a pint of milk
- a little nutmeg
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take eight eggs, leave out the whites of four, and beat the remainder well together in a basin
2. Add half a pint of white wine, a little strong ale, and sugar
3. Put it into a saucepan and set it over a slow fire, stirring it all the time
4. Boil a pint of milk with a little nutmeg and cinnamon, just enough to flavour it
5. When the eggs and wine are hot, add the boiling milk to it
6. Remove it from the fire, pour into a punch-bowl, and serve
