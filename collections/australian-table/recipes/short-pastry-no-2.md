## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 8 oz. butter
- 1 gill water
- Juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a basin, rub the butter lightly in, until it is as fine as bread crumbs; make a well in the centre, and strain the lemon juice.
2. Mix into a stiff paste with the water, knead for a few minutes until it is quite smooth, it is then ready for use.
3. A crust may be made with lard just in the same way; this is much lighter of digestion than a butter crust, and should always be given to anyone suffering from a weak digestion.
