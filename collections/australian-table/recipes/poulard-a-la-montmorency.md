## Ingredients

- fowl
- lard
- liver
- bacon
- hard eggs
- pale stock
- flour
- butter
- onions
- carrots
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Lard the fowl
2. Stuff it with the liver cut in pieces, some bacon, and hard eggs
3. Stew it in pale stock with flour browned in butter, some onions, carrots, and a few fine herbs
4. Reduce the sauce to a glaze
5. Serve with the fowl
