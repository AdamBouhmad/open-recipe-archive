## Ingredients

- two pints of boiling water
- loaf sugar
- lime or lemon-juice
- one pint of ale or porter
- one pint of rum
- half a pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Combine two pints of boiling water with quantum sufficit of loaf sugar and lime or lemon-juice, one pint of ale or porter, one pint of rum, and half a pint of brandy
