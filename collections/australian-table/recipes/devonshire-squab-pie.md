## Ingredients

- mutton-chops or mutton
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- water
- apples
- potatoes
- crust

## Directions

1. Lay mutton-chops or mutton at the bottom of the dish
2. On the meat strew some onions, with pepper, salt, a little sugar, and half a tea-cupful of water
3. Place on the top apples and potatoes, in layers, cut thin
4. Cover the sides and top of the dish with crust
5. Bake well
