## Ingredients

- fat
- a couple of table-spoonfuls
- half a pint of flour and water
- a table-spoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. When meat of any kind has been fried in fat in a pan — whether chops, steaks, or cutlets — when they are done remove them
2. pour out of the pan all but a couple of table-spoonfuls
3. Mix up, say half a pint of flour and water, pour it into the pan, and let it simmer until done
4. add a table-spoonful of ketchup
