## Ingredients

- green peas
- butter
- mint

## Directions

1. Boil green peas in plenty of water
2. When done, remove from the fire
3. Drain them on a hair sieve
4. Put them in the dish with the least bit of butter
5. A sprig of mint boiled with them is usually sent up with the peas
