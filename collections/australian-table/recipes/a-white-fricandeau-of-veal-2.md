## Ingredients

- four or five pounds of breast of veal or fillet from shoulder
- veal stuffing
- water
- calf's foot
- onion
- blade of mace
- white pepper
- salt
- sprig of parsley

## Directions

1. Take four or five pounds of breast of veal or fillet from the shoulder
2. Stuff it with a fine-flavoured veal stuffing
3. Put it into a stewpan with water sufficient to cover it
4. A calf's foot cut in pieces is sometimes added
5. Season with one onion, a blade of mace, white pepper and salt, and a sprig of parsley
6. Stew the meat
