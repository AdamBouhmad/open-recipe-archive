## Ingredients

- haddock, cod, or any other solid fish
- one pound of fat salt pork
- onions
- flour
- water
- black pepper
- salt
- crackers or Philadelphia pilot bread

## Directions

1. Cut a haddock, cod, or any other solid fish into pieces three inches square
2. Pound a pound of fat salt pork in strips into the pot, set it on the fire and fry out the oil
3. Take out the pork and put in a layer of fish, over that a layer of onions in slices, then a layer of fish with slips of salt pork, then another layer of onions, and so on alternately until the fish is consumed
4. Mix some flour with as much water as will fill the pot, season with black pepper and salt to taste
5. Boil for half an hour
6. Have ready some crackers soaked in water until a little softened
7. Throw them into the chowder five minutes before you take it up
8. Serve in a tureen
