## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 Tomatoes
- 1 lb. Chops
- 1 oz. Butter
- 4 Cold Potatoes
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 tablespoonful Milk

## Directions

1. Skin and slice up the tomatoes, put a layer at the bottom of a pie-dish, then lay in the chops.
2. Season with pepper and salt, and cover with the rest of the tomatoes.
3. Mash up the potatoes until very smooth.
4. Warm the butter and milk, and pour it over them and make into a paste.
5. Cover the dish with this crust, brush the top over with a little milk, put into the oven and bake for about one hour and a quarter.
