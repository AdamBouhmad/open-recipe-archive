## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- celery heads
- onions
- leeks
- a little butter
- lettuces
- sorrel
- celery
- sufficient stock
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut up small, carrots, turnips, and celery heads, onions, and leeks, and put them into a stewpan, with a little butter
2. Then add to them lettuces, sorrel, and celery, cut in pieces
3. To these vegetables put sufficient stock, and simmer until the vegetables are well done
4. Serve with or without bread
