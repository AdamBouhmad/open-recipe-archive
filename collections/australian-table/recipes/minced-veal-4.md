## Ingredients

- veal
- bechamel sauce
- a little milk
- a small quantity of floured butter
- a small piece of lemon peel
- bread sippets

## Directions

1. Cut the veal in small pieces, and put it in a stewpan with some bechamel sauce, a little milk, and a small quantity of floured butter, a small piece of lemon peel, and warm
2. Serve with bread sippets
