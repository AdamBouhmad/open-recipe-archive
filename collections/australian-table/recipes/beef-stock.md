## Ingredients

- Leg of beef
- Vegetables
- Cold water
- Dessertspoonful of salt
- 2 dozen peppercorns
- One onion
- Two carrots

## Directions

1. Have the butcher chop the bone small
2. Remove the marrow from the bones and cut the meat into small pieces
3. Put all together into a stock pot or digester
4. Cover well with cold water and bring it to the boil
5. Add a dessertspoonful of salt
6. Remove the scum carefully when it is thrown up
7. Put in 2 dozen peppercorns, an onion, and two carrots
8. Draw away from the fire and let it boil steadily for five or six hours or longer
9. Strain off through a colander and stand away in a cool place
