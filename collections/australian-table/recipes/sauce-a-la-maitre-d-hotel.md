## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- a little flour
- parsley and chives
- half a glass of water
- a little salt and pepper
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter, a little flour, parsley and chives chopped small into a saucepan
2. Add half a glass of water, a little salt and pepper
3. Just before serving, set on the fire
4. Keep turning it till all the ingredients are well incorporated
5. Add the juice of a lemon and serve
