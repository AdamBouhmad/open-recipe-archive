## Ingredients

- part of a leg of beef
- half a gallon of water
- a few blades of mace
- a bunch of parsley
- a crust of bread

## Directions

1. Take part of a leg of beef and crack the bone in two or three places
2. Put it into half a gallon of water and let it simmer
3. Skim well and add a few blades of mace, a bunch of parsley, and a crust of bread
4. Let it simmer for two hours and serve with toasted bread
