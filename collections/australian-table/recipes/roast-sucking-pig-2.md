## Ingredients

- sucking pig
- salad oil
- onion
- sage
- bread crumbs
- egg
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Rub the skin all over with salad oil
2. Stuff the pig with onion, sage, and bread crumbs, mixed together with egg, some pepper and salt
3. Baste the pig well, as everything depends on this attention
4. The pettitoes, liver, and brains are generally made into gravy, and served up in a sauce tureen with the pig
