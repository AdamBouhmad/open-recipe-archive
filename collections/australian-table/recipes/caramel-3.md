## Ingredients

- Half a pound of Sugar
- Half a pint of Water

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of sugar into a frying-pan and let it get very brown
2. Pour over half a pint of water and stir till it boils
3. Strain into a bottle
4. It will keep good a long time, and is very useful for colouring soup and gravies
