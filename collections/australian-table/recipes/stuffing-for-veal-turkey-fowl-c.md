## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of beef stuffing in marrow
- the same weight of bread crumbs
- two drachms of parsley
- an onion
- sweet marjoram
- grated nutmeg
- pepper and salt
- the yolks and whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Mix a quarter of a pound of beef stuffing in marrow and the same weight of bread crumbs
2. Add two drachms of parsley, an onion, sweet marjoram, grated nutmeg, pepper and salt
3. Pound them with the yolks and whites of two eggs
4. They can be made into balls or sausages, and floured when so required
5. They may be fried or boiled
