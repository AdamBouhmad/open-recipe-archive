## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- a little milk
- one pound of flour
- six eggs
- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
- two table-spoonfuls of yeast
- pounded almonds
- sugar
- citron
- cinnamon
- clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of butter, warm it over the fire with a little milk.
2. Pour it into a pan with a pound of flour, six eggs, a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds, finely powdered, and two table-spoonfuls of yeast.
3. Beat these ingredients well together into a light paste.
4. Set it before the fire to rise.
5. Butter the inside of a pan, and fill it with alternate layers of the paste, and of pounded almonds, sugar, citron, and cinnamon.
6. Bake.
7. When baked and while hot, make holes through the siesta with a small silver skewer, taking care not to break it.
8. Pour over clarified sugar until it is perfectly soaked through.
