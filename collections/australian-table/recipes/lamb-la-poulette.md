## Ingredients

- any part of a quarter of lamb
- a bit of butter
- a spoonful of flour
- two or three glasses of boiling water
- pepper
- salt
- a bunch of mixed herbs
- some small onions
- some mushrooms

## Directions

1. Blanch any part of a quarter of lamb, put it into a saucepan with a bit of butter, and a spoonful of flour
2. when well mixed pour over it by degrees two or three glasses of boiling water, in order that it may mix well with the butter
3. When quite smooth put in the lamb, with pepper, salt, a bunch of mixed herbs, and some small onions
4. Stew the whole gently, and half an hour before serving add some mushrooms
5. take out the meat, skim the fat off the sauce, and serve
