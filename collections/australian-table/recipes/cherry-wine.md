## Ingredients

- morella cherries
- loaf sugar
- brandy

## Directions

1. Pound morella cherries in a mortar, and let them be in a cool place for twenty-four hours
2. Press the pulp through a fine tin strainer, and to every quart of juice add a pound of loaf sugar
3. Allow it to ferment the proper time, and add a little brandy
