## Ingredients

- 2 Bream
- 2 Tomatoes
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 Carrot
- 1 oz. Flour
- Pepper
- Salt
- 1 Onion
- 1 doz. Peppercorns
- Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Fillet the fish, put the bones in a saucepan, and just cover them with water.
2. When they boil, skim well, and add the tomatoes sliced up, the peppercorns and vegetables; boil quickly without the lid for half an hour, then strain, rubbing the pulp of the tomatoes through with the liquor.
3. Make a smooth sauce with half a pint of this liquor, the butter, and the flour; if the colour is not good add a few drops of cochineal.
4. Fold the fillets of fish neatly, and bake in the oven with a little lemon juice, and covered with a buttered paper.
5. Arrange them on a dish and pour the sauce over.
6. Serve hot.
