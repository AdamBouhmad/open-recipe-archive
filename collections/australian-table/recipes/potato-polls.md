## Ingredients

- two pounds of potatoes
- an ounce of butter
- milk
- half a pint of yeast
- a quarter of a pint of lukewarm water
- two pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Boil two pounds of potatoes, mash them, and work them with an ounce of butter and as much milk as will pass them through a colander
2. Mix with the potatoes half a pint of yeast, and a quarter of a pint of lukewarm water
3. Pour the whole on two pounds of flour, knead it well
4. Let it stand before the fire to rise, and make it into rolls
5. Toast and butter them after baking
