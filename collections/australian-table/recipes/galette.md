## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of butter
- a pound of flour
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- yolks of two eggs
- a tea-cupful of cream or water

## Directions

1. Work lightly three-quarters of a pound of butter into a pound of flour, add a salt-spoonful of salt
2. Make it into a paste with the yolks of two eggs, mixed with a tea-cupful of cream or water
3. Roll round, three-quarters of an inch thick, score it in small diamonds
4. Brush yolks of eggs over the top, and bake it for about half an hour in a quick oven
