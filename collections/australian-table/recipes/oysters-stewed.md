## Ingredients

- a pint of oysters
- their own liquor
- a quarter of a pint of cream or milk
- mace
- cayenne
- salt
- an ounce of butter
- flour
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put a pint of oysters into a stewpan, with their own liquor
2. add to the liquor a quarter of a pint of cream or milk, some mace and cayenne, and a little salt
3. when it boils stir in an ounce of butter mixed smoothly with a little flour
4. put in the oysters, bearded, or not, as you choose
5. simmer for a few moments
6. when ready to serve add a little lemon-juice
