## Ingredients

- 1 Eel
- 3 oz. Veal Seasoning
- 1 1/2 oz. Flour
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 1/2 oz. Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Make the veal seasoning by recipe given elsewhere
2. Sew it into the eel and put it into a deep dish
3. Just cover it with water, and bake it in a good oven for about one hour
4. Take it up and keep hot, strain the liquor in which it has been cooked
5. Take about one pint and make into a brown sauce with the butter and flour
6. Colour it with a few drops of caramel, let it boil for a few minutes, season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
7. Pour over the fish, and serve very hot
