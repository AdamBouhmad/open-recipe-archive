## Ingredients

- 2 quarts White Stock
- 1 pint Milk
- 1 oz. Sage
- 1 Leek
- 1 Fagot of Herbs
- 1 doz. White Peppercorns
- Salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put the stock into a stewpan; slice in the leek and add the fagot of herbs and the peppercorns.
2. Boil them together for half an hour, strain out the vegetables and return to the saucepan.
3. Stir in the sage and continue stirring until it is clear and the soup is thick.
4. Pour in the boiling milk, boil up and pour into a tureen.
5. Sprinkle finely chopped parsley on the top before serving.
