## Ingredients

- two quarts of new milk
- two table-spoonfuls of rennet or less quantity of patent rennet
- four ounces of sugar
- three ounces of butter
- four eggs
- a little nutmeg grated
- lemon-peel
- a glass of brandy
- paste

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of new milk into a stewpan and set it near the fire.
2. Stir in two table-spoonfuls of rennet or less quantity of patent rennet and let it stand until set, about an hour.
3. Break it well when set and pour off the whey.
4. Put the curd to drain.
5. When dry, add four ounces of sugar and three ounces of butter to be oiled and stir well together.
6. Beat the yolks of four eggs in a basin with a little nutmeg grated, lemon-peel, and a glass of brandy.
7. Add this to the curd and stir well together.
8. Line tins with paste and bake.
