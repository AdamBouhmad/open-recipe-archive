## Ingredients

- four eggs
- four ounces of sugar
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a lemon
- paste

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs to a froth
2. Mix them with four ounces of sugar and a quarter of a pound of warmed butter
3. Mix well together, putting in the strained juice and grated rind of a lemon
4. Line a shallow dish with paste and bake in a moderate oven for half an hour
5. Turn the pudding out and strew over it sifted sugar
