## Ingredients

- cod
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the fish, and rub some salt on the inside
2. Put the fish in the kettle, so that it may be covered
3. Put in a handful of salt, and then put in the fish
4. A small fish will be done in about fifteen minutes, and a larger one will take longer time
5. Garnish the dish with the roe and liver
