## Ingredients

- 6 oz. Flour
- 2 oz. Sugar
- 1/4 teaspoonful Carbonate of Soda
- 1 oz. Butter
- 1 teaspoonful Cream of Tartar
- 6 drops Essence of Almonds
- 1 Egg

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour
2. Stir in the sugar, carbonate of soda, and cream of tartar
3. Mix into a stiff dough with the egg and flavouring
4. Roll into small balls about the size of a marble
5. Toss in coarse sugar
6. Put on to a greased baking sheet, and bake from five to eight minutes
