## Ingredients

- six ounces of butter
- one pound of flour
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of currants
- three-quarters of an ounce of carraway-seeds
- a tea-spoonful of soda
- a little warm water
- new milk

## Directions

1. Rub six ounces of butter into one pound of flour
2. Mix in half a pound of sugar, half a pound of currants, and three-quarters of an ounce of carraway-seeds
3. Dissolve a tea-spoonful of soda in a little warm water, and mix with new milk to make a pint
4. Combine all ingredients
5. Bake an hour, putting it in the oven as soon as possible after mixing
