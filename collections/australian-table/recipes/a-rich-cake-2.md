## Ingredients

- Two pounds and a half of Jones's patent flour
- A pint and a half of cold milk
- A pound of butter, oiled
- A pound of sifted sugar
- A pound of currants
- A quarter of a pound of lemon and orange-peel
- A little grated nutmeg, cinnamon, and allspice

## Directions

1. Put these ingredients in a round tin and bake an hour
2. This cake may be iced or ornamented
