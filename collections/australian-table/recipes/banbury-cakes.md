## Ingredients

- a gill of warm milk
- a pound of flour
- three spoonfuls of yeast
- half a pound of currants
- a quarter of a pound of orange and lemon-peel
- an ounce of mixed spice
- half a pound of honey

## Directions

1. Put a gill of warm milk to a pound of flour, and three spoonfuls of yeast
2. When it has worked a little, mix with it half a pound of currants, washed and cleaned
3. Add a quarter of a pound of orange and lemon-peel, cut small, and an ounce of mixed spice
4. Mix the whole together with half a pound of honey
5. Roll out some paste thin
6. Make it in the form of an oval
7. Put into each the quantity of the mixture
8. Close it round and soft
9. Sugar over
10. Bake
