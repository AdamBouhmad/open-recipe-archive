## Ingredients

- six ounces of Brown and Poison's patent corn-flour
- six ounces of best white sugar
- a quart of milk
- four ounces of fresh butter
- a pinch of salt
- a few drops of essence of vanilla or lemon
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Put six ounces of corn-flour into a stewpan with six ounces of white sugar
2. Mix well with a quart of milk, four ounces of fresh butter, a pinch of salt, and a few drops of essence of vanilla or lemon
3. Stir well until it boils
4. Work in the yolks of six eggs
5. Whisk the whites into a froth and lightly incorporate with the butter
6. Pour into a buttered pie-dish
7. Bake in a moderately heated oven for half an hour
8. Sugar the top and serve when risen
