## Ingredients

- 2 fish
- 1 pint stock
- 1 blade of mace
- 2 cloves
- 1 oz. flour
- 2 tablespoonsful ketchup
- 1 onion
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- 1 doz. peppercorns

## Directions

1. Fillet the fish and fry them in egg and bread crumbs
2. Slice and fry the onion
3. Lay the onion and the fish in a tin dish
4. Cover with stock
5. Put in the cloves, peppercorns, and mace
6. Cover over and put into a moderate oven for an hour
7. Mix the flour and ketchup together and stir it in
8. Put back into the oven for ten minutes
9. Dish the fish and strain the sauce over it
