## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 2 Eggs
- 1 oz. Currants
- 1/4 lb. Flour

## Directions

1. Put the eggs into a basin
2. Beat in the flour and then the milk
3. Pour into a buttered basin
4. Clean the currants and drop them in
5. Steam for one hour
6. Turn out of the basin
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
