## Ingredients

- breast of veal
- onion
- stock
- green peas
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the breast into pieces, and fry of a light brown, with a shredded onion
2. When browned put the pieces into a stewpan, with a little stock, and allow it to simmer
3. When nearly done add a quantity of green peas, with pepper, salt, and the juice of half a lemon, or a little vinegar
4. Keep the cover of the stewpan off, and serve as soon as the peas are done
