## Ingredients

- round of beef
- salt
- fat

## Directions

1. Well and properly salt the beef
2. Skewer it tightly, and as round and evenly as possible
3. Put more fat in the centre, if the meat is too lean
4. Use a roomy pot
5. Heat gradually and allow it to simmer until done
6. Take off the scum
7. When dished, see that the silver skewers hold the meat well together
8. Ladle a small quantity of the liquor over the meat
9. Garnish with sliced carrots
10. If to be eaten cold, let it remain in the water until it cools, and the meat will be more mellow
