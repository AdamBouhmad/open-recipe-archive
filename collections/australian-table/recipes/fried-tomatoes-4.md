## Ingredients

- 1 doz. Tomatoes
- 1 gill Milk
- 1 oz. Butter
- Pepper and Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Slice up the tomatoes, mix a spoonful of flour with some pepper and salt; dip in the slices and fry quickly, pile on a dish.
2. Pour the milk into the pan in which they were fried, stir until it boils, and pour over.
3. Garnish with fried bread and serve hot.
