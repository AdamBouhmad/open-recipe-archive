## Ingredients

- four ounces of apples
- four ounces of currants
- four ounces of grated bread
- two ounces of sugar
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and mince four ounces of apples
2. Add to these four ounces of currants well cleaned, four ounces of grated bread, and two ounces of sugar
3. Whisk three eggs and beat them up with the rest of the ingredients
4. When all are thoroughly mixed put the pudding in a buttered basin
5. Tie it down with a cloth and boil for a couple of hours
