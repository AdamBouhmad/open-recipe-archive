## Ingredients

- three pints of fruit
- one quart of water
- a pound of the best sugar per quart
- brandy

## Directions

1. Bruise three pints of fruit, and add one quart of water
2. Let it stand, and after twenty-four hours strain the liquor, and put to every quart a pound of the best sugar
3. It will work of its own accord
4. Put a bottle of brandy to every five gallons
5. Keep it for a time and bottle it
