## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 2 lbs. Chops
- 1 pint Milk
- 1 lb. Flour
- Salt and Pepper
- Dripping

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a basin, beat in the flour with a fork, then add gradually the milk, season with a little pepper and salt.
2. Rub some dripping on a baking dish, pour in the batter, lay in the chops.
3. Put into a moderate oven and bake for about one hour and a quarter.
4. Serve hot.
