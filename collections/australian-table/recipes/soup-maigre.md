## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of butter
- half a dozen sliced onions
- a few heads of celery
- two handfuls of spinach
- two cabbage lettuces
- parsley
- two quarts of water
- crusts of bread
- a tea-spoonful of pepper
- a few blades of mace
- a handful of white beet leaves
- the yolks of two eggs
- a table-spoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt a quarter of a pound of butter in a stewpan
2. Shake it well, and throw in half a dozen sliced onions
3. Shake the pan for a few minutes, then put in a few heads of celery, two handfuls of spinach, two cabbage lettuces, cut small, and some parsley
4. Shake the pan well for five minutes
5. Put in two quarts of water, some crusts of bread, a tea-spoonful of pepper, and a few blades of mace, also a handful of white beet leaves, cut small
6. Let it simmer for an hour
7. Before serving, beat up and add the yolks of two eggs, and a table-spoonful of vinegar
