## Ingredients

- chops or steaks
- pepper
- salt
- butter (optional)
- tomato sauce (optional)
- minced shalot (optional)
- dried mint (optional, for pork-chops)
- tarragon (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the chops a little over half an inch thick
2. broil them over a clear fire until done, turning them with the beef-steak tongs about three times
3. do not pierce them with a fork
4. sprinkle pepper and salt over them in moderation
5. Serve hot
