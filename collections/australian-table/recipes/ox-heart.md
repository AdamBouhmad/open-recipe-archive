## Ingredients

- ox heart
- veal stuffing
- paper
- flour
- hot water
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Stuff the heart in the centre with veal stuffing
2. Have a few stuffing balls in the dripping-pan
3. Put paper on the heart at first
4. Afterwards take away the paper, and flour it so as to put a froth on
5. Serve hot, with hot water plates and currant jelly
