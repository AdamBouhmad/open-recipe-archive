## Ingredients

- dessert-spoonful of mustard
- salt-spoonful of salt
- little cayenne
- large glassful of port wine or claret
- tablespoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. Mix the mustard, salt, and cayenne in the large glassful of port wine or claret
2. Pour it on the meat just before serving
3. A tablespoonful of ketchup may be added
