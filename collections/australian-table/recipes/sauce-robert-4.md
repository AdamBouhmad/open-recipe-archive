## Ingredients

- flour
- a couple of onions
- a table-spoonful of ketchup
- pepper
- salt
- a tea-spoonful of mustard
- the juice of half a lemon or a table-spoonful of vinegar
- half a pint of stock

## Directions

1. Put a quantity of flour into a dry pan, and brown it
2. When browned cut a couple of onions up and brown them
3. then add a table-spoonful of ketchup, a little pepper and salt, a tea-spoonful of mustard, the juice of half a lemon, or a table-spoonful of vinegar, and half a pint of stock
4. let it simmer for ten minutes
