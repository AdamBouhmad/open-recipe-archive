## Ingredients

- shoulder of veal
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- a piece of butter
- a glass of water
- a bay-leaf
- broth

## Directions

1. Bone the shoulder, and season it with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
2. Roll it, and give it an oblong shape, then tie it up
3. Put it into an oval stewpan, with a piece of butter, and stew it over a slow fire
4. Let it take a fine light colour, then add a glass of water, and sprinkle it with a little salt, adding a bay-leaf
5. Place fire above and below till a glaze is formed
6. Dress the shoulder on the dish, and drain the butter, detaching what remains with a spoonful of broth and pour the gravy on the veal
