## Ingredients

- two quarts of good vinegar
- light French wine

## Directions

1. Have a barrel to hold from thirty to forty quarts
2. Boil two quarts of good vinegar, pour it into the barrel, stop the bung-hole, and roll it about well
3. Half fill the barrel with light French wine, and leave it for a time in a warm place
4. At the end of a week boil two more quarts of vinegar, pour it into the barrel, and fill it up with wine and set it by
5. The vinegar may be used in a month, and it will acquire strength by keeping
6. If the vinegar should at any time be getting weak, add two quarts of vinegar to the cask, after boiling
