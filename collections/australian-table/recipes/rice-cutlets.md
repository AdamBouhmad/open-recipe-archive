## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Cold Meat
- 2 oz. Rice
- Pepper and Salt
- 1 Egg
- Bread Crumbs
- Hot Fat
- Nutmeg or Lemon Peel

## Directions

1. Mince the meat finely; if there is any cold rice in the larder it will do; if not, boil some.
2. Mix the rice and meat well together, season and flavour with a little nutmeg or lemon peel; if the meat is very lean add 2 oz. fat or beef suet.
3. Shape into cutlets, egg and bread crumb them, and fry in hot fat.
4. Dish in a circle and garnish with fried parsley.
