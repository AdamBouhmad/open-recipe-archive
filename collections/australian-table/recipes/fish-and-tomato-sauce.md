## Ingredients

- 2 Bream
- 1/2 pint of Tomato Sauce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet the bream; cut each fillet into two pieces, wash and trim them.
2. Make some tomato sauce by recipe given.
3. Butter a pie dish, lay in the fillets, and season them; pour over the sauce, and bake in a moderate oven for twenty minutes.
4. Garnish with a little chopped parsley, and serve in the dish in which they were cooked.
