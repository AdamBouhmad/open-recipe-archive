## Ingredients

- fish
- a shredded onion
- a few cloves
- allspice
- pepper
- boiling water
- butter
- flour
- a glass of wine
- a little ketchup and anchovy
- cayenne
- sippets of bread

## Directions

1. Put the fish into a stewpan, with a shredded onion, a few cloves, allspice, and pepper.
2. Just cover it with boiling water.
3. Let it simmer for about twenty minutes.
4. Strain the liquor into another pan, leaving the fish to keep warm, until the sauce is ready.
5. Rub well together as much butter and flour as will thicken.
6. To each pint of sauce add a glass of wine, a little ketchup and anchovy, and cayenne.
7. Let it simmer for a time, and pour over the fish.
8. Garnish with sippets of bread toasted or fried.
