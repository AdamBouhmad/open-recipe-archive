## Ingredients

- halibut, plaice, or soles
- potatoes
- short crust paste
- brown sugar
- small pickles
- pepper
- gherkins
- West India pickles
- water or butter

## Directions

1. Cut fish in small pieces
2. Place them in a deep dish in alternate layers with slices of potatoes and dumplings made of short crust paste, sweetened with brown sugar
3. Season well with small pickles, pepper, gherkins, or West India pickles
4. Throw over a little water or butter, warmed
5. Bake it thoroughly
