## Ingredients

- one pint of apricot juice
- half a pound of sugar
- one ounce of tartaric acid

## Directions

1. Take one pint of apricot juice and add half a pound of sugar
2. Add one ounce of tartaric acid
3. Bottle and cork for use
4. Use it with water and a small quantity of carbonate of soda
