## Ingredients

- twelve full-sized potatoes
- a handful of hops
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- six pints of water
- about half a pint of stale yeast

## Directions

1. Take about twelve full-sized potatoes, and when boiled, mash them
2. Boil a handful of hops and three tablespoonfuls of sugar in six pints of water
3. When nearly cold, mix the whole with about half a pint of stale yeast
4. Set it near the fire to work
5. It is ready for use when done working, and will keep a fortnight
