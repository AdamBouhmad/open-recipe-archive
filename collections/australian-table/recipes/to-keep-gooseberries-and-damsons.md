## Ingredients

- gooseberries or damsons
- rosin

## Directions

1. Pick the fruit, not too ripe, and put them into wide-mouthed bottles
2. Cork them gently
3. Put them in the oven — not over-warm — until they have shrunk a third of their weight
4. Take them out of the oven, tighten the corks, and rosin them down close
