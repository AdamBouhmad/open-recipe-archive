## Ingredients

- 1 pint Milk
- 2 oz. Flour
- 1 oz. Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 spoonful Jam

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a saucepan, mix the flour with a little cold milk.
2. When the milk in the saucepan is nearly boiling, stir it in, and let it boil up.
3. Pour into a basin and beat in the yolks of the eggs and the sugar.
4. Turn into a pie-dish and bake till firm.
5. Spread a spoonful of jam on the top.
6. Whip the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and spread them over, sprinkle with sugar, and put back in the oven to set.
7. Serve cold.
