## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- stock
- pepper
- salt
- ketchup
- pint of green peas
- fried eggs

## Directions

1. Stew a leg of lamb in a simmer of stock until half done
2. Add seasoning of pepper, salt, a little ketchup, and a pint of green peas
3. Put it again on the fire, and simmer till done
4. This dish can be garnished with fried eggs
