## Ingredients

- half a pint of lemon-juice
- half a pint of white wine
- three-quarters of a pound of white sugar
- one quart of boiling water
- one pint of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Mix lemon-juice, white wine, sugar, and one quart of boiling water
2. When cold, add one pint of boiling milk
3. Let it stand twelve hours
4. Pour through a jelly-bag
5. This will make two quarts
