## Ingredients

- apple pulp
- sugar
- lemon
- half a pint of milk
- half a pint of cream
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Place apple pulp flavoured with sugar and lemon at the bottom of the trifle-dish
2. Mix half a pint of milk, half a pint of cream, and the yolk of an egg
3. Give it a scald over the fire, with a little sugar in it
4. Let it cool
5. Cover the apples with it
6. Put a whip on the top
