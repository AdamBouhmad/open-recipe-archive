## Ingredients

- two onions
- tomatoes
- a handful of green pimento
- a cucumber
- a clove of garlic
- parsley
- chervil
- crumbed bread
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Take two onions, some tomatoes, a handful of green pimento, a cucumber, a clove of garlic, parsley, and chervil
2. Cut the whole into small pieces, and put them into a salad bowl
3. Add as much crumbed bread as will form double the quantity which the dish already contains
4. Season with salt, pepper, oil, and vinegar, like a salad
5. Complete the gaspacho with a pint of water to make the bouillon
