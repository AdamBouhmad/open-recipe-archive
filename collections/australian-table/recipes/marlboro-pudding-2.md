## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- four ounces of sugar
- four eggs
- paste
- preserve

## Directions

1. Melt four ounces of butter
2. Mix with four ounces of sugar and four eggs well beaten
3. Mix all well together
4. Line the dish with paste and a layer of preserve
5. Bake half an hour
