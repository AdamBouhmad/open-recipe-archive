## Ingredients

- one pint of cider
- one pint of warm ale
- sugar
- nutmeg
- ginger
- one wine-glass of whisky

## Directions

1. Take a pint of cider, and add to a pint of warm ale
2. Sweeten with sugar
3. Grate in some nutmeg and ginger, and add a wine-glass of whisky
