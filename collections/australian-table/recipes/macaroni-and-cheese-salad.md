## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. Macaroni
- 1/4 lb. Cheese
- 1 teaspoonful French Mustard
- 3 tablespoonsful Oil
- 3 tablespoonsful Vinegar
- 1 Head of Celery
- 1 Lettuce
- Beetroot

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni, or use any cold that may be in the larder.
2. Cut it into pieces about three inches long.
3. Cut the cheese into very thin slices.
4. Cut the celery into very small pieces.
5. Lay these alternately in a bowl with some shredded lettuce.
6. Make a dressing of the mustard, oil, and vinegar.
7. Pour it over.
8. Garnish with a little beetroot, and serve.
