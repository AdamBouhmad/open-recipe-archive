## Ingredients

- the yolks of four eggs
- one pound of sugar
- the whites of four eggs
- half a pound of flour

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of four eggs with one pound of sugar
2. Whip the whites separately
3. When they are well frothed, mix them with the yolks and sugar
4. Stir in half a pound of flour lightly
5. Have ready some tin moulds, buttered
6. Put in the biscuit paste, filling the moulds only half
7. Throw some sugar over them
8. Bake
