## Ingredients

- ladies' finger cakes
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Place in a mould, in a circle, some ladies' finger cakes, and let them lie closely together
2. line the sides of the mould with them by placing them upright
3. Pour into the middle a whipped cream
4. turn it upside down in the dish
5. If made in warm weather, it must be iced
