## Ingredients

- Four eggs well beaten
- Three ounces of butter
- Three ounces of cheese
- A gill of cream

## Directions

1. Mix well
2. Bake for twenty minutes in small shapes or in a dish
3. Serve hot, for cold fondu is unbearable
