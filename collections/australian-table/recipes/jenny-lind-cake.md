## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a quarter of a pound of moist sugar
- a little spice
- two tea-spoonfuls of soda
- three-quarters of a pint of fresh-churned buttermilk
- candied peel

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, a quarter of a pound of butter, and a quarter of a pound of moist sugar with a little spice
2. Mix with two tea-spoonfuls of soda in three-quarters of a pint of fresh-churned buttermilk
3. Add candied peel to taste
4. Bake
