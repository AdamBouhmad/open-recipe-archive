## Ingredients

- anchovies
- parsley or other minced garnish
- hard egg
- oil

## Directions

1. In the middle of a plate place the anchovies, cut in fillets, and lay them transversely
2. Around them place a wreath of parsley or other minced garnish
3. Upon them place quarters of hard egg
4. Serve with oil
