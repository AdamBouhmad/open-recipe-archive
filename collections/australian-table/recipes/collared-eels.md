## Ingredients

- 2 Eels
- 1 Egg
- 1 oz. Gelatine
- 1 fagot of Herbs
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 1 spoonful Vinegar
- Pepper and Salt
- Salt
- 2 dozen peppercorns

## Directions

1. Clean the eels, cut them into pieces 2 inches long
2. Put them in cold water well seasoned with salt, 2 dozen peppercorns and the vegetables, and a spoonful of vinegar
3. Bring to the boil, and skim well
4. Boil steadily for an hour, or longer if the eels are large
5. Take out the fish, slip out the bones, and cut the meat into small pieces
6. Put back the bones and boil the liquor quickly without the lid for half an hour, then strain off
7. Dissolve the gelatine in a little cold water or gravy and stir in
8. Rinse a mould in cold water, arrange in it the pieces of eel and a hard boiled egg cut into slices with a few sprigs of parsley
9. Strain the liquor over and stand away till cold
10. Turn out and serve with a salad
