## Ingredients

- the cheek
- two onions
- two cloves
- three turnips
- three carrots
- two bay leaves
- a couple of heads of celery
- allspice
- pepper
- salt
- sweet herbs
- an ounce of butter
- a half-pint of the soup
- flour
- a table spoonful of ketchup
- tarragon vinegar
- a wineglass of port wine
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the cheek into a stewpan, with two onions, two cloves, three turnips, three carrots cut up, two bay leaves, a couple of heads of celery, and a little allspice, pepper, and salt, with a few sweet herbs
2. Let it simmer for three hours, and skim it frequently
3. Take out the cheek, and melt an ounce of butter in a stewpan, add to it a half-pint of the soup, and thicken it with some flour and a table spoonful of ketchup, tarragon vinegar, and a wineglass of port wine, with a little cayenne, and let it come to near a boil
4. Serve with the head on a dish, divided, and the soup in a tureen
