## Ingredients

- half a chicken
- about a quart of water
- salt
- a table-spoonful of rice

## Directions

1. Put half a chicken, cut into pieces, into a stewpan, with about a quart of water.
2. Let it simmer for an hour, skim well, add salt and a table-spoonful of rice.
3. Put it on the fire again for half an hour, and it will be found to be excellent broth.
