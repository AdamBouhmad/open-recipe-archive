## Ingredients

- cream
- wine
- sugar
- lemon juice
- lemon peel
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a strong whip as for trifle
2. Mix a pint of cream with half a pint of wine
3. Add sugar and flavour with the juice and grated peel of a lemon, and a little cinnamon
4. Stir briskly
5. Fill the glasses sufficiently to put some of the whip on the top of each glass
