## Ingredients

- peaches, apricots, plums, pears, or other ripe fresh fruit
- white sugar
- benzoate (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the fruit
2. Pit the fruit
3. Place in a jar alternating layers of fruit and white sugar until the jar is full
4. Seal tightly so no water enters
5. Place in a water bath for three hours over even heat
6. Lower heat and let cool
7. Optional: add a little benzoate to preserve longer
