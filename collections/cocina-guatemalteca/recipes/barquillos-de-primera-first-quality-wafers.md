## Ingredients

- half a liter of milk
- half a pound of sugar
- one pound of sifted bread flour
- two ounces of melted butter
- half an ounce of vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Place in a bowl half a liter of milk with half a pound of sugar and beat until there is not a single grain remaining
2. Add one pound of sifted bread flour and beat so it does not become lumpy
3. Add two ounces of melted butter
4. If too thick add a little more milk and half an ounce of vanilla essence
5. Have the wafer iron ready over charcoal fire and drop one large spoonful; if the wafer iron is large three or four can be made at once
6. Close the wafer iron carefully so they do not spread too much
7. When they are golden remove them
