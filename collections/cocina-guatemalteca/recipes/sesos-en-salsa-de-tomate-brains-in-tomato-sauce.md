## Ingredients

- tomato
- guaque chile
- cumin (optional)
- thyme
- achiote (annatto)
- calf brains, cooked and sliced

## Directions

1. Cook a little tomato with a guaque chile
2. Grind, if desired, with a little cumin; if not, with a sprig of thyme and a small piece of achiote
3. Fry this and add the brain, which should already be well cooked and sliced into even slices
4. Serve hot
