## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- one teaspoon of flour
- one glass of water
- a little pepper powder
- a little nutmeg powder
- one lemon or a little vinegar
- tender mushrooms (if available)
- chicken quarters
- salt to taste
- three egg yolks
- a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar
- artichoke hearts, cooked (if no mushrooms)
- lemon

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of butter with one teaspoon of flour in a pan; as soon as it melts add one glass of water.
2. Add a little pepper powder, a little nutmeg powder, and one lemon or a little vinegar.
3. If there are tender mushrooms, add them.
4. Add the chicken quarters with salt to taste.
5. Let it boil for three quarters of an hour.
6. Just before serving, add three egg yolks well beaten with a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar.
7. If using mushrooms, add them when the chicken is half cooked; if not, use cooked artichoke hearts.
8. To keep the chicken meat white, brush it with lemon.
