## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Salt
- Pork loin
- Tomato
- Onion
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Sweet sherry
- Bread powder
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Flour
- Eggs
- Sweet orange juice
- Butter
- Colored tissue paper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with salt
2. Cut bones to a span in length and scrape away all meat until the bone is very clean
3. Fry this meat with one pound of cooked pork loin, chopped tomato and onion
4. Add raisins and almonds sliced lengthwise, salt, one cup of sweet sherry, a little bread powder mixed with clove, pepper and cinnamon
5. Prepare a dough with one pound of flour, four eggs, juice of three sweet oranges, half a teaspoon of salt, and one tablespoon of butter
6. Knead for half an hour until it crackles and is very fine
7. Roll out with a rolling pin, neither thin nor thick
8. Cut circles with the rim of a glass
9. Place a bone on one circle with a large spoonful of the meat mixture
10. Cover with another circle, sealing the edges very carefully so the filling does not escape
11. Have a pan with sufficient hot butter ready
12. Fry the cutlets until golden
13. Dust with fine sugar powder
14. Have colored tissue paper cut very fine ready
15. Use this to wrap the bone protruding from the dough
16. Arrange on a platter
