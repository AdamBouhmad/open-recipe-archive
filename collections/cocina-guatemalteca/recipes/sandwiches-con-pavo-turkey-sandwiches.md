## Ingredients

- turkey
- French mustard
- pepper powder
- hard egg yolk
- German bread
- butter
- tomato
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Chop the turkey very finely, more than one can of devil ham.
2. Mix with one tablespoon of French mustard, pepper powder, and one hard egg yolk, well crumbled, forming a paste.
3. Cut slices of German bread into rounds.
4. Coat with butter and cover with paste.
5. Place a slice of tomato (without seeds) on top and cover with mayonnaise.
