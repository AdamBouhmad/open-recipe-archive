## Ingredients

- five pounds of meat
- half an ounce of salt
- a quarter ounce of pepper
- a quarter ounce of cinnamon powder
- four eggs
- one teaspoon of flour
- one deboned turkey
- one or two bottles of fruits

## Directions

1. Grind five pounds of meat and add half an ounce of salt, a quarter ounce of pepper, a quarter ounce of cinnamon powder, four eggs and one teaspoon of flour
2. Have ready a deboned turkey
3. Stuff the turkey with the meat mixture, placing one or two bottles of fruits inside
4. Sew closed with thick thread, wrap in cloth, cook and press as previously described
