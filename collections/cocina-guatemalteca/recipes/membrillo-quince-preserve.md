## Ingredients

- quince
- sugar and honey (amount not specified)
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the quince
2. Remove the seeds and skin
3. Parboil it, cutting it to the desired size
4. Remove it and place in cold water
5. Then place it in the honey to cook through
