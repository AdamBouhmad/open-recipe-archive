## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- sour orange juice
- salt
- flour
- lard
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- thyme powder
- onion
- garlic

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and cut into pieces.
2. Parboil with a little water and sour orange juice with salt.
3. Remove and dry with cloth.
4. Coat in flour and fry in hot lard.
5. Once fried, remove and place in a dish keeping pieces separate.
6. Bathe with one cup of vinegar mixed to taste with oil, salt, pepper powder, oregano powder, and thyme powder, chopped onion and garlic.
7. Place over heat for a very light boil.
8. Cover and let rest until the following day.
9. Add a small glass of vinegar and oil and give it another light boil.
10. Serve with onion slices.
