## Ingredients

- 0.5 pounds sugar
- 1.5 glasses milk
- 3 ounces melted butter
- 0.25 ounce cinnamon powder
- 1 ounce raisins
- figs
- chilacayotes
- candied oranges cut lengthwise
- 0.5 pounds crushed sponge cakes
- 9 eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a dish 0.5 pounds of sugar, 1.5 glasses of milk, 3 ounces of melted butter, 0.25 ounce of cinnamon powder, 1 ounce of raisins, some figs, chilacayotes, candied oranges cut lengthwise, and 0.5 pounds of crushed sponge cakes
2. Beat 9 eggs as for marquesote
3. Mix everything very well
4. In a butter-greased mold, pour the mixture
5. Put in the oven for about 20 minutes
