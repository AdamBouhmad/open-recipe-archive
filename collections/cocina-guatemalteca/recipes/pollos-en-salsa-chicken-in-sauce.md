## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- roasted tomatoes
- oregano
- bread fried in lard
- ten fried almonds
- handful of toasted and peeled melon seeds

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with salt
2. Grind the roasted tomatoes with a little oregano and a small piece of bread fried in lard, ten fried almonds, and a handful of toasted and peeled melon seeds
3. Dissolve this mixture in the same broth
4. Add the chicken pieces cut into quarters to the mixture
5. Serve hot
