## Ingredients

- One bottle of white honey
- One pound of sugar
- A small piece of ginger
- One-eighth ounce of cloves
- One-eighth ounce of Castilian pepper
- One-eighth ounce of Chiapa pepper
- One quarter ounce of cinnamon
- Four ounces of peanut
- Four ounces of toasted sesame seeds
- Two pounds of French sponge cake, ground

## Directions

1. Put the white honey with one pound of sugar and the ginger over the fire and boil for five minutes only, because it will become caramelized
2. While hot, add the cloves, Castilian pepper, Chiapa pepper, and cinnamon, all ground dry
3. Mix with four ounces of peanut, four ounces of toasted sesame, and two pounds of ground French sponge cake
4. Pour into a container or small boxes as desired
