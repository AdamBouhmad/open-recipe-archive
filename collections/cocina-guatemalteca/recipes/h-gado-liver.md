## Ingredients

- liver, one pound
- lard or oil
- onion, two ounces
- water, very little
- potatoes, optional
- carrot, chopped and cooked, optional
- peas, optional

## Directions

1. Wash the liver well, peel and cut into pieces
2. Heat lard or oil in a pan on the fire
3. Lightly fry the liver in this
4. For one pound of liver, have two ounces of onion parboiled with very little water, letting it boil just a few times so it comes out tender
5. If desired add potatoes, chopped and cooked carrot, and also peas
