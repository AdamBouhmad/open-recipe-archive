## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 ochavo of salt
- 1 tablespoon lard
- melted butter for layering
- 1 pound pork
- peeled and chopped tomato
- onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tablespoon good vinegar
- salt
- powder of pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- chopped olives
- capers
- lard for frying
- tender lettuce leaves
- potatoes, cooked and sliced
- butter for frying potatoes
- bread powder
- stewed lamb broth

## Directions

1. Knead flour, eggs, salt, and lard very well
2. Flatten with a rolling pin and brush with melted butter, then fold; repeat this process three or four times
3. Flatten very thin and cut into small rounds
4. Chop one pound of pork with peeled and chopped tomato, onion, and garlic
5. Add one tablespoon of good vinegar, salt, powder of pepper, clove, and cinnamon
6. When half-cooked, remove from heat and add chopped olives and capers
7. Fill the rounds with this mixture and fold edges like empanadas
8. Fry in very hot lard
9. Arrange on a platter with tender lettuce leaves and potato slices fried in butter with bread powder
10. Cover the ravioli with stewed lamb broth
