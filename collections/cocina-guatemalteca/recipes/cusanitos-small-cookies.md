## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- four egg yolks
- four ounces of butter
- four ounces of lard
- a pinch of salt
- four ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, four egg yolks, four ounces of butter, four ounces of lard, a pinch of salt, and four ounces of sugar.
2. Knead well.
3. Place in a mold and make small rings.
4. Put in a hot oven.
