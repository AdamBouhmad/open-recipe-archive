## Ingredients

- Water
- Sheep head
- Fat hen
- Sheep brain
- Chayote
- Green beans
- Carrot
- Potato
- Sheep liver
- Chicken meat
- Sheep tongue
- Fried seasoning
- Pepper powder
- Chopped parsley
- Flour
- Butter
- Saffron water
- French bread slices
- Lard
- Egg

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot with the necessary water according to the number of people dining
2. When it reaches its first boil, drop in a sheep head and a fat hen well prepared
3. Cook a sheep brain
4. Let everything cook for four hours
5. When everything is cooked, remove it
6. Prepare in advance chayote, green beans, carrot, and potato cut into very small pieces
7. When the vegetables are half-cooked, mince all the liver, chicken meat, and sheep tongue and drop it into the broth
8. Before adding the vegetables, strain the broth through a filter
9. Add sufficient fried seasoning, pepper powder, chopped parsley, a little flour fried in butter until well browned, and half a cup of saffron water
10. Prepare small fritters by slicing very small and thin pieces of French bread and frying them in lard
11. Fill them with the brain that will be finely minced with pepper powder and chopped parsley
12. Add a pinch of salt, mix very well and fill a small bread round
13. Cover with another piece of fried bread and wrap in well-beaten egg
14. Fry over very gentle heat
15. Once all fritters are made, drop them into the soup for a light boil, as excessive boiling will break them down
