## Ingredients

- apples, split
- a little water
- sugar equal in weight to the cooked apple

## Directions

1. Cook the split apples with a little water
2. When cooked, remove and strain dry
3. Weigh the apple and for each unit of fruit weight add the same weight of sugar
4. Put on the fire with the water in which they cooked until they reach the paste stage
