## Ingredients

- marquesote soaked in honey and muscatel wine
- one pound of sugar
- four egg yolks
- one half glass of milk or wine
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Place wet marquesote in honey and muscatel wine on a platter
2. Place one pound of sugar over heat until it reaches the soft-ball stage
3. Remove when cold and add four egg yolks beaten in one half glass of milk or wine
4. Place back over heat stirring constantly until it becomes hard
5. Place this on the marquesote with cinnamon powder on top
