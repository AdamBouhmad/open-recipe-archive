## Ingredients

- avocados
- mustard powder
- salt
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Prepare the avocados by removing them from the shell
2. Place in a bowl and mash with a masher
3. Add a little foreign mustard powder, salt, and pepper powder
4. Beat well and serve immediately, as everything should be done close to serving time
