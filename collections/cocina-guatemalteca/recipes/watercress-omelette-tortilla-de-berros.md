## Ingredients

- Watercress
- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the watercress by removing everything that is stem.
2. Beat the egg (white first and then yolk).
3. Mix in the raw watercress leaves, seasoned with salt.
4. Fry in hot butter.
