## Ingredients

- twenty ounces fine rice flour
- twelve ounces powdered sugar
- half pound grated and well-aired cheese
- half cup cream from milk
- half pound butter
- seven eggs

## Directions

1. Mix twenty ounces of fine rice flour with twelve ounces of powdered sugar, half a pound of grated and well-aired cheese, half a cup of cream from milk, and half a pound of butter.
2. Add the seven eggs one by one, kneading for five minutes with each egg.
3. Put in a lard-coated casserole and bake in the oven.
