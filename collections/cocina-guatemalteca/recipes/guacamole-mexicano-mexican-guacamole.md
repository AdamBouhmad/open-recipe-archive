## Ingredients

- avocado
- tomato
- onion
- cilantro
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind the avocado.
2. Add finely chopped tomato, onion and cilantro.
3. Add salt last so it does not blacken.
4. Top with tomato slices, onion slices and chile for garnish.
