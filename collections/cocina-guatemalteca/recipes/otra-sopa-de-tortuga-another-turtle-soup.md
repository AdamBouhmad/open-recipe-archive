## Ingredients

- turtle
- lemon
- half a teaspoon of baking soda
- onion stems
- parsley
- celery
- bay leaf
- thyme
- chicken broth
- vegetables, finely chopped
- soffritto
- turtle meat
- chicken
- chicken giblets
- chopped parsley
- toasted or fried flour with butter
- turtle eggs

## Directions

1. Hold the turtle over fire; when it puts out its head, tie and hang it to cut off the head
2. Wrap in cloth and leave for half an hour
3. With a very sharp knife, remove all the meat and wash very well with lemon
4. Have water boiling with half a teaspoon of baking soda; after a quarter hour, remove from that water and return to fire in little water with onion stems, parsley, celery, bay leaf, and a sprig of thyme
5. Have ready a good chicken broth with finely chopped vegetables and sufficient soffritto
6. Add the chopped turtle meat, the chicken, and giblets to the broth, along with chopped parsley and some toasted or fried flour in butter
7. Add the turtle eggs whole or chopped
