## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of sugar
- little water
- half a pound of almonds
- one egg white
- a little milk
- three egg whites
- two egg yolks
- two ounces of sugar
- four ounces of flour
- one full teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Put the sugar on the fire with very little water
2. When it reaches hard crack stage, add the half pound of almonds ground with one egg white and a little milk
3. When it reaches the consistency of before, let it cool
4. Add two egg whites
5. For the dough, beat three egg whites until stiff peaks form
6. When well set, mix in the egg yolks with two ounces of sifted sugar, four ounces of flour and one full teaspoon of Royal
7. Once well mixed, put in a greased flat baking pan and bake in a hot oven for five minutes
8. As soon as it is cooked, cover the dough with the almond mixture, fold in two, cover with the rest of the almond mixture and put in the oven to brown
