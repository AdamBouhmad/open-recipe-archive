## Ingredients

- pork skin and fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove all lard from the clean pork skin and set aside
2. Cut the lard into pieces of desired size and season with salt
3. Cut the skin lengthwise, pound it, and place in the sun for one full day
4. The next day, fry the lard and when it is half-fried, add pieces of skin of desired size
5. When the fat pieces are fried, remove them and let the skin fry a bit more without burning
6. The following day, put a pan with a little lard on the fire and add the skin pieces one by one, basting them with hot lard to make them puff up
