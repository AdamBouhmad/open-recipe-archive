## Ingredients

- duck
- parsley
- garlic
- onion
- bay leaf
- thyme
- tomato
- three spices powder
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- one glass of wine
- one glass of vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the duck well and place it on ice, preferably for two or three days.
2. Then plunge it into boiling water for five minutes, remove it, dry it well.
3. Fry it in oil until it takes on color.
4. Add parsley, crushed garlic, onion, bay leaf, thyme, peeled and chopped tomato, three spices powder, cinnamon, clove, and pepper.
5. Add one glass of wine and one of vinegar.
6. When the duck is cooked, strain the sauce and serve hot.
