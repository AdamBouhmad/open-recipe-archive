## Ingredients

- lard and oil
- plenty of garlic
- two chopped and peeled tomatoes
- two lettuce leaves
- two pasilla chiles, soaked in hot water
- three ounces of toasted shrimp
- water or broth
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Fry plenty of garlic in lard and oil
2. Remove the garlic and fry two chopped and peeled tomatoes with two lettuce leaves in the same fat
3. Grind two pasilla chiles soaked in hot water and three ounces of toasted shrimp and mix everything well
4. When everything is well fried, add water if it is Lent, if not add broth
5. Have fried bread in the soup bowl and pour this mixture over it
