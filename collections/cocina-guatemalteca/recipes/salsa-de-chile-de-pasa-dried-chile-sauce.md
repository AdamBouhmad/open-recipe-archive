## Ingredients

- four dried pasilla chiles
- four roasted tomatoes
- butter
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from and soak four dried chiles, grind with four roasted tomatoes
2. fry in a small spoonful of butter, add one spoonful of vinegar and salt
3. serves with any meat
