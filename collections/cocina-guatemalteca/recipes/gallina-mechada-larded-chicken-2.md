## Ingredients

- one chicken
- ham
- chorizo
- olives
- garlic
- pepper powder
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- oil
- lard or butter
- tomatoes
- onion
- capers

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken well, lard it with ham, chorizo, olives, chopped garlic and pepper powder
2. Place in a serving dish and cover with water and one cup of vinegar with salt
3. Cook for two hours, remove and let it drain
4. Then fry in oil, lard or butter
5. When it has taken color, grind about three roasted tomatoes and one roasted onion head and dissolve with the broth in which the chicken was cooked
6. Just before serving, add olives and capers
