## Ingredients

- 1 cup of flour
- half a tablespoon of Royal baking powder
- half a teaspoon of salt
- quarter teaspoon of paprika
- 2 eggs
- half a cup of milk
- 1 cup of cooked chicken cut into pieces
- 1 cup of cooked peas
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Sift all dry ingredients
2. Beat egg yolks until thick
3. Mix in the milk
4. Add the chicken and peas
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
6. Drop spoonfuls into hot fat in a skillet
7. Remove and drain on waxed paper
