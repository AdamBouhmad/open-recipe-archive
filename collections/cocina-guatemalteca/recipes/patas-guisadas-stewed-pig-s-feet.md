## Ingredients

- pig's feet halved
- carrot
- minced ham
- parsley sprigs
- glass of white wine
- pepper powder
- a teaspoon fried flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the feet halved in half and add a carrot, minced ham piece, parsley sprigs, a glass of white wine and pepper powder
2. When cooked add a small teaspoon of flour fried in butter
3. When serving remove all the vegetables
