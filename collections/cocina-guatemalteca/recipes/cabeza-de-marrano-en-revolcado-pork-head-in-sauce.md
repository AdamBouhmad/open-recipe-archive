## Ingredients

- Pork head
- Salt
- Tomatillo
- Cooked tomato
- Garlic clove
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Castilian peppers
- Guache chile
- Achiote
- Onion
- French bread

## Directions

1. Cook the head well with salt
2. Grind together a little tomatillo, cooked tomato, one garlic clove, a little oregano, cumin, some Castilian peppers, a guache chile, a small piece of achiote, a roasted onion head, and a soaked piece of French bread
3. Fry this mixture in a little lard
4. Thin it with the same broth in which the head was cooked
5. Add pieces of meat of desired size
6. Serve hot
