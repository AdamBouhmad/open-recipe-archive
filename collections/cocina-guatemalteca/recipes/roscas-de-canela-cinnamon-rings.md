## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- Four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- One teaspoon of cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify one pound of sugar
2. When it reaches the soft ball stage, remove from heat
3. Add four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
4. Add one teaspoon of cinnamon powder
5. Return to heat for five minutes
6. When cooled, place in a pastry bag to form rings
7. Place on a baking sheet and bake in the oven
