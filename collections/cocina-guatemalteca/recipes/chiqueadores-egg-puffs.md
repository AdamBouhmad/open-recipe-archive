## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- egg yolks
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 5 ounces of sifted flour
- butter for coating
- cinnamon powder (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites as for nougat
2. Beat the yolks separately with four ounces of sugar
3. Combine both, mix well
4. Add five ounces of sifted flour, mix well
5. Place papers well-coated with butter on tins
6. Drop small spoonfuls of the batter in desired shapes, long or round, with great lightness so it doesn't settle
7. If desired, dust with cinnamon powder
8. Place in a not-very-hot oven
