## Ingredients

- venado (venison)
- salt
- cooked tomato
- chile guaque
- cumin
- garlic clove
- oregano
- achiote (annatto)
- french bread, soaked

## Directions

1. Once the venison is well shredded, place it to cook with salt.
2. Grind some cooked tomato with a chile guaque, a little cumin, a garlic clove, a little oregano, a small piece of achiote, and a soaked french bread.
3. Dissolve this mixture and put it to fry.
4. Add the well-cooked venison to this preparation.
