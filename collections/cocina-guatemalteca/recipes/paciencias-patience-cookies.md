## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- nine ounces of ground sugar
- six ounces of sifted flour
- zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff like nougat
2. Mix in the ground sugar, sifted flour, and lemon zest
3. Place in a paper cone and pipe onto buttered trays in desired sizes
4. Allow to form a crust
5. Place in the oven until browned
