## Ingredients

- whole green beans
- salt
- bicarbonate
- pepitoria
- piece of cinnamon
- Castilian peppercorns
- piece of tortilla or tamalito soaked
- Chiapa peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cook the whole green beans with salt and a little bicarbonate
2. Toast some pepitoria, grind it with a piece of cinnamon and some Castilian peppercorns, with a piece of soaked tortilla or tamalito
3. If preferred, grind only some Chiapa peppercorns, always adding hot pepper to taste
4. Dissolve with a little of the same water in which the green beans were cooked
5. Add the green beans to this
6. If desired serve raw seasoned with salt, otherwise fry to eat it hot
