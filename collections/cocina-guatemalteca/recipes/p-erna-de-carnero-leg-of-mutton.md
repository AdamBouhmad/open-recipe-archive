## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Butter
- Water
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. One day before, place the leg with a little vinegar, salt and pepper powder
2. The next day place in the oven well brushed with butter; if desired cover with water
3. When cooked, brown and serve with boiled potatoes
