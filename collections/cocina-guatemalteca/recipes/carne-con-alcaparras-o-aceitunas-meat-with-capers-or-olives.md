## Ingredients

- Loin, brisket, or chuck
- Finely minced onion
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Parsley
- Butter or margarine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four ounces of capers or olives
- A piece of fried bread
- Broth
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Slice the loin, brisket, or chuck as for steak
2. Make a minced mixture of very finely chopped onion, garlic, tomato, and plenty of parsley; the tomato should be sufficient and peeled
3. Coat a pan with butter or margarine
4. Layer in the pan: a layer of the minced mixture, then a layer of meat with salt and pepper, repeating until full
5. Cover and let sit overnight
6. Place over low heat, stirring very carefully
7. Grind four ounces of capers or olives and a piece of fried bread and strain through broth over the meat
8. Leave over low heat and serve very hot with potatoes
