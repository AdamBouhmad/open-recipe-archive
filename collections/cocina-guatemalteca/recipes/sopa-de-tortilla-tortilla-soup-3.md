## Ingredients

- tortilla
- lard
- annatto (achiote)
- fried seasoning
- salt
- mint leaves
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Fry the tortilla in lard with a bit of annatto, when fried remove it and place it in a pan
2. Add fried seasoning and cover with hot salted water, mint leaves and slices of fresh cheese
