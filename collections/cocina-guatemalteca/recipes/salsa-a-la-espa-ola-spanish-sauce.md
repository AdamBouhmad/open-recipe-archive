## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- onion
- parsley
- bread flour
- salt
- pepper powder
- milk

## Directions

1. In a spoonful of butter or lard fry a chopped onion, parsley, one spoonful of bread flour
2. add salt, a little pepper powder and one cup of milk
3. let it boil ten minutes
