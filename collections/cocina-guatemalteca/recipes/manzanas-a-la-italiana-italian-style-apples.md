## Ingredients

- ten large ripe apples
- one pound of clarified sugar at medium-firm stage
- ten peaches
- four ounces of rice cooked in milk
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cook them in the clarified sugar
2. When tender, remove and let the syrup thicken more
3. Cook the peaches separately in the same manner, or prepare five peaches first
4. When done, remove and cook five apples in the same syrup
5. When tender, place both fruits on a platter
6. Prepare the rice cooked in milk with egg yolks and butter
7. Mix this with the thick syrup and cover the apples
8. Place in the oven to brown
