## Ingredients

- chiles pimientos, about eighteen
- roasted tomatoes, twelve
- onion head, one
- lard, two ounces
- salt
- rice, one pound
- milk, two liters
- lard, fourteen ounces
- corn dough
- chicken pieces
- cheese pieces
- butter pieces
- corn husks
- twine

## Directions

1. Grind the red chiles pimientos, roasted tomatoes, and ground onion head
2. Fry in two ounces of lard and season with salt
3. Grind one pound of rice that has been soaked the day before
4. When well ground, cook in two liters of milk and fourteen ounces of lard with salt to taste
5. When the dough is well cooked, place a little white corn dough on the husk
6. Add in the middle a piece of chicken and the prepared mixture with cheese and butter pieces
7. Wrap well, tie with twine, and cook like regular tamales
