## Ingredients

- calf brains
- salt
- Castilian pepper powder
- french mustard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking the brains, peel and slice them very evenly
2. Place on a platter and season with salt and a little pepper powder
3. Prepare a sauce by dissolving a little french mustard in a bit of water with a little salt, a little Castilian pepper powder, and a tablespoon of vinegar
4. Bathe the brains with this sauce
5. Serve cold
