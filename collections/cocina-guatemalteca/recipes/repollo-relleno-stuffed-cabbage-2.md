## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Pork, partially cooked, chopped
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic, well chopped
- Vinegar, a bit
- Salt
- Sherry, one tablespoon
- Bread powder, ground
- Pepper
- Clove
- Broth

## Directions

1. Chop the partially cooked pork and fry with seasoning of tomato, onion and well chopped garlic.
2. Add a bit of vinegar, salt, one tablespoon of sherry, ground bread powder with pepper and clove.
3. Fill all the white part of the cabbage with this, tie it and place in a cooking pot with broth in the oven.
4. When cooked, serve hot.
