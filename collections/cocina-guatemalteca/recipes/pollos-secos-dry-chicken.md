## Ingredients

- chickens
- water
- salt
- parsley
- leeks
- thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 sprig of mint
- 1 tablespoon of lard or butter
- plenty of peeled and chopped tomato
- raw tomatillo
- 3 peeled garlic cloves
- 1 green chile
- pepper powder
- cinnamon
- chopped almonds
- raisins
- olives
- capers
- 1 tablespoon of vinegar
- 1 jigger of dry sherry

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and boil them with water, salt, parsley, leeks, thyme, 2 bay leaves and 1 sprig of mint
2. When cooked, remove and cut into regular-sized pieces
3. Put a pan on the fire with 1 tablespoon of lard or butter
4. Chop plenty of peeled tomato and raw tomatillo
5. Add 3 peeled garlic cloves and 1 green chile, all chopped
6. Add salt, pepper powder and cinnamon
7. Add chopped almonds, raisins, olives, whole capers and 1 tablespoon of vinegar
8. Mix everything well and remove from fire
9. Grease a casserole with butter
10. Alternate layers of chicken pieces and sauce, finishing with a layer of sauce on top
11. Put on fire and when it boils, add 1 jigger of dry sherry
12. Place over two fires to reduce
