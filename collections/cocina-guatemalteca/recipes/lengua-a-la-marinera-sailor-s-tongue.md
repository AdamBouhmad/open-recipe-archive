## Ingredients

- cooked tongue
- butter or lard
- cooked potatoes
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- broth

## Directions

1. Once the tongue is well cooked, slice it.
2. Place it in a dish with butter or lard.
3. Make a layer of cooked potatoes salted and sliced.
4. Add a layer of tongue and a preparation made of peeled tomato, onion, garlic and chopped parsley with a little of the broth in which the tongue was cooked.
5. Add another layer.
