## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Eggs
- Cheese
- Shrimp
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. After cooking the potatoes, peel and grind them.
2. For half a pound of potatoes, mix in three eggs and a piece of cheese.
3. If available, add one ounce of shrimp, peeled and blanched.
4. Season with salt.
5. Form the cakes and fry in hot butter over low heat.
