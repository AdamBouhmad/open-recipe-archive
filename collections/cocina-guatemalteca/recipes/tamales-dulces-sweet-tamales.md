## Ingredients

- two pounds corn dough
- half pound lard
- four ounces melted butter
- six egg yolks
- salt and sugar to taste
- milk (if needed)
- cinnamon powder
- raisins
- blanched almonds
- dobladores (corn husk wrappers)

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of corn dough
2. Grind and sift it
3. Grind again without moistening
4. Add half pound of lard and four ounces of melted butter
5. Add six egg yolks, salt and sugar to taste
6. If too thick, add a little milk
7. Place the dough in the wrapper
8. In the middle, add a little cinnamon powder, one raisin, and half a blanched almond
9. Wrap and tie
10. Cook by steaming
