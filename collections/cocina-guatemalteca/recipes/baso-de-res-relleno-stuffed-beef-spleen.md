## Ingredients

- beef spleen
- garlic
- potato
- peas
- carrot
- half pound pork meat
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- tomato
- sweet bell pepper
- vinegar
- mint

## Directions

1. Place the spleen in boiling water.
2. Prepare garlic, potato, peas, carrot and half a pound of chopped pork meat, with the vegetables parboiled and meat minced.
3. Open the spleen and remove the blood.
4. Fry the blood with salt, parsley, garlic, onion, tomato, sweet bell pepper, vinegar and the vegetables, mixing everything well.
5. Fill the spleen with this mixture, sew it closed, and place it on the fire with water, salt and a sprig of mint.
6. Turn it and let it brown as the liquid reduces.
