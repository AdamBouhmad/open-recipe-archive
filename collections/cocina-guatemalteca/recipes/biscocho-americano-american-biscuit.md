## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- three egg yolks
- three egg whites
- half a pound of flour
- six ounces of sifted sugar
- zest of one lemon
- nutmeg powder
- cinnamon powder
- a small glass of rum or dry sherry

## Directions

1. Beat the butter for one hour
2. Add the egg yolks and beaten egg whites and beat another hour
3. Add the flour, sifted sugar, lemon zest, nutmeg, cinnamon, and rum or sherry
4. Place in buttered molds or paper boxes
5. Bake in the oven
