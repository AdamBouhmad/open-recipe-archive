## Ingredients

- three egg whites
- fifteen egg yolks
- half pound of ground almonds
- two egg whites
- two and a half pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three egg whites and fifteen egg yolks well until they reach the consistency of rompope.
2. Add half pound of ground almonds mixed with two egg whites.
3. Grease a mold lightly with butter and place the mixture either in the oven or in a water bath.
4. The next day, remove the crust from the bottom, break into pieces.
5. Clarify two and a half pounds of sugar and place on the fire to harden.
