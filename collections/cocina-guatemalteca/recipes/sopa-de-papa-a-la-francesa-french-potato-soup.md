## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil peeled potatoes until well cooked
2. Pass them through a strainer
3. Add a little milk, salt to taste, and a little butter
4. Let it come to a boil a few times
5. Serve hot
