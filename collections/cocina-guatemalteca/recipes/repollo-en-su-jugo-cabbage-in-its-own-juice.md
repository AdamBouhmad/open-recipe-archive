## Ingredients

- cabbage
- butter
- onion slices
- salt
- pepper powder
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage finely and place it to fry in butter with onion slices, salt and pepper powder
2. When fried, let it cook and if it is tough add very little broth or water
3. Place over very gentle heat
