## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- onion, chopped
- butter
- egg yolk
- parsley, chopped
- white sauce
- lemon juice, a few drops

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with salt and chopped onion
2. Place them whole or cut in a serving dish
3. Prepare a little butter, an egg yolk, chopped parsley, a little white sauce, a few drops of lemon juice and place over the fire without letting it boil
4. Cover the potatoes with this mixture
