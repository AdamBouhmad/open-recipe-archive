## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound peeled almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 6 ounces cornstarch

## Directions

1. Grind 0.5 pound of peeled almonds dry
2. Beat 8 egg yolks with 0.5 pound of sugar and 4 egg whites, like for nougat
3. When very thick, mix together
4. Mix in the almonds and 6 ounces of cornstarch
