## Ingredients

- mutton
- lard
- salt
- ham
- parsley
- garlic
- lemon juice
- white wine
- oil
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of meat and place in a pan with lard and salt
2. When half browned, add some chopped ham, some crushed parsley and crushed garlic
3. Add the juice of two lemons, one cup of white wine and two tablespoons of oil
4. Cover with broth or water
5. Serve hot
