## Ingredients

- small molletes (rolls)
- manjar blanco or fresh butter for filling
- two eggs for eight molletes
- one teaspoon of bread
- lard for frying
- one and a half pounds of clarified sugar or half a loaf of very white panela sugar
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Take small molletes and remove a small cap from each
2. Hollow them out without breaking them
3. Fill them with manjar blanco or fresh butter
4. Put the cap back on
5. Beat two eggs for eight molletes, first the whites and then the yolks
6. Add one teaspoon of bread
7. Wrap the molletes in this mixture
8. Fry them in hot lard over very gentle heat
9. Once fried, remove and wash in a little hot water
10. Squeeze and dry with a very clean cloth
11. Have ready one and a half pounds of clarified sugar or half a loaf of very white panela sugar
12. Put the molletes in this, making sure the vessel is wide so they do not pile on top of each other
13. Add cinnamon sticks and let them heat
14. Remove with a skimmer very carefully so they do not break
15. Cover with the syrup
