## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- one-quarter ounce of pepitoria
- one ounce of toasted and ground sesame
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- small toasted tomatoes
- toasted chile
- lard
- bread
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and fry it whole
2. When it has browned, add one-quarter ounce of pepitoria and one ounce of toasted and ground sesame with pepper, clove, and a small piece of cinnamon
3. Add small toasted tomatoes with a toasted chile fried in lard and a small piece of bread
4. Dissolve all this in a cup of water and add to the tongue
5. Let it simmer for two hours
6. Serve in its hot sauce
