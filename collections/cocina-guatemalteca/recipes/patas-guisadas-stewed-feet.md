## Ingredients

- feet
- salt
- water
- chickpeas
- garlic head
- onion heads
- parsley sprigs
- mint sprigs
- ham
- chorizo
- paprika

## Directions

1. Clean the feet well.
2. Cut into pieces and cook with salt, sufficient water and half an ounce of chickpeas (which should have been soaked the day before).
3. When the feet and chickpeas are almost cooked, add a head of garlic, two heads of onion, two sprigs of parsley, one sprig of mint, ham and chorizo in slices.
4. If available, add one tablespoon of paprika.
5. Let it simmer until everything is well cooked.
6. Before serving, remove the garlic, parsley, onion and mint.
