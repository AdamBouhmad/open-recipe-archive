## Ingredients

- one pound of peeled, cooked and ground potato
- three eggs
- four ounces of butter
- little salt
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. To one pound of peeled, cooked and ground potato add three eggs (white and yolk), four ounces of butter, a few grains of salt and sugar to taste
2. Mix everything very well and put in a pan coated with lard in the oven
