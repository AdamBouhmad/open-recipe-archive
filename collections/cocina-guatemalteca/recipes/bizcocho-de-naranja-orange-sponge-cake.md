## Ingredients

- Half pound of dry sugar
- Eight egg yolks
- Eight egg whites
- Half pound of sifted flour
- Zest of one sweet orange
- One teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Beat half pound of dry sugar with eight egg yolks until thick
2. Add eight well-beaten egg whites with half pound of sifted flour, orange zest, and one teaspoon of Royal baking powder
3. Pour into a buttered mold
4. Place in a hot oven
