## Ingredients

- Shrimp, well peeled and dried
- Lard
- Three roasted tomatoes
- One small glass of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Fried bread slices, thin

## Directions

1. Soak the well-peeled and dried shrimp with hot water.
2. Fry them in lard.
3. Grind three roasted tomatoes and fry them with the shrimp.
4. Add one small glass of white wine.
5. When well thickened, season with salt and a pinch of pepper.
6. Serve over thin slices of fried bread.
