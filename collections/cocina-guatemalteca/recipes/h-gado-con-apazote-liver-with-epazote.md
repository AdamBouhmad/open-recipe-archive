## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- Castilian peppercorns
- achiote
- chile
- masa
- butter
- apazote

## Directions

1. Put the liver to cook with salt, cut into pieces
2. Grind some Castilian peppercorns, a small piece of achiote, a small piece of chile and a little masa, as fine as possible
3. Dissolve this in the same broth in which the liver cooked and place it in a dish with a little butter
4. Add the liver with a branch of apazote that has been previously washed with boiling water
5. This can also be made by grinding only Chiapa pepper, without adding epazote or Castilian peppercorns
