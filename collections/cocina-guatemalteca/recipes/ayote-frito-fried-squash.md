## Ingredients

- squash
- one tablespoon lard
- peeled tomato
- garlic clove
- one tablespoon ground breadcrumb powder
- piece of achiote
- Castilian peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cook the squash as the others
2. Fry in one tablespoon of lard, peeled tomato and one chopped garlic clove
3. Add the squash to this
4. Add one tablespoon of ground breadcrumb powder with a piece of achiote and some Castilian peppercorns
