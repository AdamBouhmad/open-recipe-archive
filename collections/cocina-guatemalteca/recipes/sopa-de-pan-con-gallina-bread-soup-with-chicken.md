## Ingredients

- bread slices
- oil
- lard
- cooked chicken breast
- egg yolks
- roasted tomatoes
- Castilian pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Fry bread slices in oil and lard and place in the tureen
2. Remove the breast from a well-cooked chicken
3. Grind all the breast meat with four hard-cooked egg yolks, two roasted tomatoes, eight Castilian peppercorns, and three cloves
