## Ingredients

- Butter
- Cooked ham slices
- Eggs
- Salt
- Cheese powder

## Directions

1. Grease a pan with butter and place in it a layer of cooked ham slices.
2. Break the eggs on top, ensuring they remain whole.
3. Add salt and cheese powder.
4. Put in a moderate oven.
5. When the whites are cooked, serve the eggs.
