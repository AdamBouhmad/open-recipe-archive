## Ingredients

- Cooked potatoes
- Butter or lard, one spoon
- Cooked fish, cut into pieces
- Tomato, roasted and ground
- Garlic clove
- Shrimp (optional alternative)

## Directions

1. Cook and partition the potatoes
2. Spread a pan with a spoon of butter or lard
3. Place a layer of potatoes, one of cooked fish cut into pieces and one of roasted tomato ground with a garlic clove
4. Alternate the layers making sure the last is potato
5. Then place in the oven or over two fires to give a light boil
6. If desired, shrimp can be used instead of fish
