## Ingredients

- Cleaned squabs
- 1 glass of red wine (for five squabs)
- A piece of cinnamon
- A few cloves
- Castilian pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 sprig of thyme
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon of butter per squab
- Water
- Bread, fried in butter

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned squabs in a dish according to the quantity
2. For five squabs, add a glass of red wine, a piece of cinnamon, a few cloves, Castilian pepper (whole if preferred, but powder is preferable), 2 bay leaves, a sprig of thyme, salt and a tablespoon of butter per squab
3. Cover with water
4. After they are well cooked, remove them and carefully remove all the bones
5. Place each squab on a piece of paper spread with butter in a warm oven
6. To serve, place each on a slice of bread well fried in butter
