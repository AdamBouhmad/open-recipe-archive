## Ingredients

- chickens
- ham
- pepper powder
- nutmeg
- two cloves of garlic
- peeled tomato
- parsley
- oil
- two roasted tomatoes
- capers
- fried bread
- onion
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the chickens with ham, pepper powder, nutmeg, two cloves of garlic, peeled tomato and chopped parsley
2. Brown them in oil and when they take on color, add two roasted tomatoes, some capers, a piece of fried bread, sufficient chopped onion
3. Dissolve with water and salt and add to the chickens
4. If they are very tender, let them simmer half an hour; if not, one hour
