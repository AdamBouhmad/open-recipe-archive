## Ingredients

- one cup of well-ground banana
- one half cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of Royal baking powder
- two beaten eggs
- three tablespoons of sour milk
- one tablespoon of vanilla
- one half cup of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar until it becomes sandy
2. Add the eggs and salt
3. Add the flour sifted with the Royal baking powder
4. Add the milk and finally the banana
5. Place in a greased mold and put in a hot oven
