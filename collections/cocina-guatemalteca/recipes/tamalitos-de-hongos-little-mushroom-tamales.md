## Ingredients

- corn (maíz)
- salt
- half pound melted lard
- miltomate, ground
- dried chile, cooked
- a small piece of achiote
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cook and grind the corn.
2. Add salt, half pound of melted lard, a little ground miltomate with cooked dried chile to taste, and a small piece of achiote.
3. Mix everything very well.
4. Prepare the mushrooms by cutting them and soaking them in water overnight, changing the water many times to ensure no insects remain.
5. Wrap in corn husks and cook like the previous tamales.
