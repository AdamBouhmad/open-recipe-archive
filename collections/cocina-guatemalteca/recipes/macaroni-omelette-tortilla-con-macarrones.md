## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Eggs
- Cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni broken up.
2. When cooked, remove and drain all the water.
3. Have the egg beaten as in the previous recipes.
4. For three eggs use one ounce of macaroni.
5. Mix well with cheese powder.
6. Mix with the egg.
7. Make the omelette and fry.
