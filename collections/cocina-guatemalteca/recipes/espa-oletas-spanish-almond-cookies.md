## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 8 egg whites
- half a pound of sifted flour
- dry sifted sugar for topping
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat eight yolks with four ounces of sugar
2. When very thick, mix with the eight egg whites that will be very well beaten as for nougat
3. Add half a pound of sifted flour, moving it very gently so it doesn't fall
4. Have papers coated with butter on tin sheets
5. Drop spoonfuls in desired size
6. Dust with dry sifted sugar and pieces of chopped almonds on top
