## Ingredients

- tongue
- saltpeter
- salt
- water
- bay leaves
- leeks
- parsley
- thyme
- orange leaves
- Castilian pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the tongue on a stone, place it over coals and when the skin inflates remove it and scrape the tongue with a knife until very clean.
2. Finish cleaning with a cloth without wetting it.
3. Prick it with a fork and rub the tongue vigorously with half an ounce of saltpeter and one ounce of salt.
4. Beat it with a mallet so it comes out very tender.
5. The next day rub it again with more salt.
6. Continue rubbing with salt for eight consecutive days, keeping it in a well-covered container.
7. After eight days, cook it with sufficient water, four or six bay leaves, a bunch of leeks, a bunch of parsley, a branch of thyme, four or six orange leaves and Castilian pepper powder.
8. When well cooked, which will be after six hours, remove it and serve cold with a salad of your choice, preferably potato salad.
