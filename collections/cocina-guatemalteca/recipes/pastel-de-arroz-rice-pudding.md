## Ingredients

- rice
- half a pound of rice
- 4 eggs
- 4 ounces of Parmesan cheese
- 1 glass of cream
- cracker crumbs
- butter
- a glass or jar for the center

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice as usual; when cold, mix half a pound of rice, 4 eggs, 4 ounces of Parmesan cheese, 1 glass of cream, and a little cracker crumbs
2. Butter a mold and dust with cracker crumbs
3. If the mold has no center hole, place a glass or jar in the center
