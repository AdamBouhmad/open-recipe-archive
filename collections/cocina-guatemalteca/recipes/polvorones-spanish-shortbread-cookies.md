## Ingredients

- half a pound of ground bread
- half a pound of bread flour
- four ounces of lard
- three ounces of sugar
- one eighth of an ounce of baking soda
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon powder
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of ground bread with half a pound of bread flour, four ounces of lard, three ounces of sugar, one eighth of an ounce of baking soda, one teaspoonful of cinnamon powder and a little milk
2. Knead and then stretch with the wooden pin and cut with the mouth of a glass or cup
3. Place on baking sheets in the oven
