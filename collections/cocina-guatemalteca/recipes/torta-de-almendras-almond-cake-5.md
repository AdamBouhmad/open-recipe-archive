## Ingredients

- 0.5 pounds peeled almonds
- 7 egg whites
- egg yolks
- marquesote powder or egg bread
- butter
- sweet bread powder

## Directions

1. Grind 0.5 pounds of peeled almonds
2. Beat 7 egg whites until very stiff
3. Blend in the yolks, the almonds and marquesote powder or egg bread
4. Have a mold greased with butter and dusted with sweet bread powder so it does not stick
5. Pour the batter into it and put in the oven
