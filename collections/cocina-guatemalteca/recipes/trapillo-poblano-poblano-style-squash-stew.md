## Ingredients

- calabaza or güicoy squash, not too tender or too ripe
- butter
- peeled tomato
- chopped onion
- garlic clove
- tender elote (corn)
- green chile pimiento, finely chopped
- cream from milk

## Directions

1. Chop the calabaza or güicoy very finely.
2. Make a fried mixture in butter with peeled tomato, chopped onion, and a garlic clove.
3. Add the chopped squash with a tender elote well scraped and a finely chopped green chile pimiento.
4. Add a little cream from milk and let it boil on very low heat and serve hot.
