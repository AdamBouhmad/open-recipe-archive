## Ingredients

- Oil
- Onions
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Oregano powder
- Pepper powder
- Thyme powder
- Parsley
- Cooked fish
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Fry in a tablespoon of oil three or four onions with salt (only the heads).
2. Add two peeled and chopped tomatoes.
3. Add oregano powder, pepper powder and thyme powder.
4. Add a little chopped parsley and a piece of cooked fish.
5. Mix everything well and stir with the egg, which will have been previously prepared as in the others.
6. Fry.
