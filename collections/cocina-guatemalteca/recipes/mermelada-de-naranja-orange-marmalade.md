## Ingredients

- two large ripe oranges
- water
- one pound of clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Place the two large ripe oranges in hot water
2. After boiling for a quarter of an hour, remove that water and place them again in boiling water
3. At the same time remove that water and put another one for a third time
4. After boiling the same amount of time, split them and cut the skin very thin
5. Have one pound of sugar clarified, put the orange into it and then remove the white pith from the inside and chop it finely and put it with the orange to soak well
