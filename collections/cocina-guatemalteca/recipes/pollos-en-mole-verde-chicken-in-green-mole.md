## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- lard
- squash seed
- about 12 grains of popcorn
- green chile
- a little parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with salt
2. Fry a bit of squash seed in lard with about 12 grains of popcorn
3. Grind with a little salt until very fine
4. Grind 1 green chile and a little parsley
5. Dissolve in the same broth
6. Fry and add the chicken pieces
