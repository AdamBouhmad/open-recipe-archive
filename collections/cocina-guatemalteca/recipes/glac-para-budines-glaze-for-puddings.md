## Ingredients

- Powdered sugar (one and a half cups)
- Butter (half a tablespoon)
- Hot milk (two tablespoons)
- Vanilla (half a teaspoonful)

## Directions

1. Add butter to the hot milk.
2. Slowly add the powdered sugar.
3. Add the vanilla and, without letting it reach too high a point, remove from the heat and beat until it forms a cream.
4. This can be used to cover any pastry.
