## Ingredients

- cooked pig's feet
- salt
- vinegar
- two bay leaves
- pepper powder
- roasted tomatoes
- onions in vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking with salt, prepare a little vinegar with two bay leaves, pepper powder and salt
2. Put the feet in this pressing with a weight, and after three or four hours place on a platter
3. Have some roasted and not very ground tomatoes, add a little of the vinegar in which the feet were and with this sauce cover the feet, adding pickled onions
4. Serve cold
