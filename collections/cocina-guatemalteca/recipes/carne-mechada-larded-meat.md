## Ingredients

- Loin
- Pieces of ham
- Garlic
- Pepper powder
- Pieces of red peppers
- Water
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- One sprig of thyme
- Two bay leaves
- Lettuce
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Beat a loin very well
2. Lard it with small pieces of ham, garlic, pepper powder, and pieces of red peppers
3. Put to cook with water and vinegar
4. Add pepper powder, clove, a sprig of thyme, and two bay leaves
5. When well cooked, cover and leave in the air overnight
6. The next day clean it, cut into slices, and serve with lettuce, olives, and capers
