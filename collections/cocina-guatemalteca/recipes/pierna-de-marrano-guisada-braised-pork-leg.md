## Ingredients

- 1 pork leg
- parsley
- pepper powder
- cloves
- butter or lard
- tomato slices, peeled
- garlic
- 3 bay leaves
- thyme
- 1 glass water
- salt
- 1 glass dry sherry

## Directions

1. Lard the leg with parsley, pepper powder and cloves
2. Place in a pan greased with butter or lard; the leg may be whole or in slices
3. Add peeled tomato slices, garlic, 3 bay leaves, thyme and 1 glass of water with salt
4. When more than cooked through, add 1 glass of dry sherry and place in the oven or over two fires
