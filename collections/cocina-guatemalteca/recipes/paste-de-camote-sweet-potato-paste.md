## Ingredients

- yellow sweet potato (1 pound)
- sugar (1.5 pounds)
- milk, cooked (2 glasses)

## Directions

1. Cook the sweet potato until tender, strain and weigh it.
2. Mix the sweet potato with 2 glasses of cooked milk.
3. Place on fire and cook until it reaches the point of ante (a thick, spreadable consistency).
4. For each pound of sweet potato, use 1.5 pounds of sugar.
