## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- three eggs
- four ounces butter
- two ounces lard
- four ounces sugar
- one tablespoon Royal baking powder
- one cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, three eggs, four ounces of butter, two ounces of lard, four ounces of sugar, one tablespoon of Royal, and one cup of milk together well.
2. Roll out the dough several times with a rolling pin to regular thickness.
3. Cut out crackers and place in the oven.
