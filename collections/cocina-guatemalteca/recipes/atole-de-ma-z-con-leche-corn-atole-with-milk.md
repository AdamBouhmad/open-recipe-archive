## Ingredients

- a little salpor
- lime
- milk
- cinnamon stick
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook a little salpor as for small tamales, with lime
2. Wash it and remove the germ
3. Give it one pass on the grinding stone, so it is neither whole nor very fine
4. Place on the fire with milk, a small stick of cinnamon and sugar to taste and let it boil for half an hour
