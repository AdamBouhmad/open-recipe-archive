## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Mantequilla (butter), one tablespoon
- Bread flour, one tablespoon
- Water, half a cup
- Bread powder
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower in pieces with salt.
2. Fry in one tablespoon of butter one tablespoon of bread flour.
3. Add half a cup of water without stopping to stir.
4. Put in the cauliflower and serve with bread powder and chopped parsley.
