## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil, lard or butter
- salt
- pepper powder
- 1 glass of sherry
- champagne (if available)
- 1 cup of water
- 3 or 4 ground tomatoes
- 2 or 3 cooked artichoke hearts
- truffles

## Directions

1. Cut chickens into pieces
2. Fry them in oil, lard or butter with salt and pepper powder
3. When they turn golden, add 1 glass of sherry, and another of champagne if available
4. When it has consumed half of the liquid, add 1 cup of water
5. Add 3 or 4 ground tomatoes
6. Add 2 or 3 cooked artichoke hearts and a bit of truffles
7. Let it boil for half an hour
8. Serve as a truly exquisite dish
