## Ingredients

- one veal leg
- salt
- half pound of chopped ham
- one chicken
- pepper powder
- cloves
- cinnamon
- garlic
- one glass of white wine
- two carrots
- six onions
- four ounces of lean ham cut into small pieces

## Directions

1. Cook a veal leg with salt until more than cooked, then remove it
2. Place half pound of chopped ham in a serving dish
3. Put the well-cleaned chicken in this and let it fry for about ten minutes
4. Add the deboned veal leg, pepper powder, cloves, cinnamon, salt, chopped garlic and one glass of white wine
5. Cover and let cook over very even heat
6. After one and a half hours of boiling, add two carrots cut in pieces, about six chopped onions and about four ounces of lean ham cut into small pieces
7. When serving, remove all the fat
