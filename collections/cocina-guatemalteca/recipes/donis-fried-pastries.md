## Ingredients

- half a glass of cream
- one cup of sugar
- one egg
- flour as needed
- two teaspoons of Royal baking powder
- sugar powder
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the cream with one cup of sugar and one whole egg.
2. Add flour as needed.
3. Add two teaspoons of Royal baking powder.
4. Make a soft dough.
5. Roll out with a rolling pin and cut in the shape of rings.
6. Fry the pastries.
7. When done, coat with sugar powder and cinnamon.
