## Ingredients

- good broth
- noodles, vermicelli, or any pasta, parboiled
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- two eggs
- rice powder
- chopped parsley
- fried flour

## Directions

1. Prepare very good broth
2. Add noodles, vermicelli, or any parboiled pasta to the broth along with fried seasoning and pepper powder
3. Prepare small fritters in advance by beating two eggs (first the white, and when it is stiff add the yolk) with rice powder
4. Fry the fritters in desired size, preferably very small
5. Just before serving, add the fritters to the soup
6. Add chopped parsley and fried flour
7. Serve hot
