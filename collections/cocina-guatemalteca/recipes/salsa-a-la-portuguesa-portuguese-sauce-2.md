## Ingredients

- oil
- four garlic cloves
- three egg yolks
- salt
- lemon juice
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Put a spoonful of oil in a pan, add four garlic cloves
2. dissolve three egg yolks with salt and a little lemon juice, pepper powder
3. add to the oil and give a slight boil
4. when serving, remove the garlic cloves
