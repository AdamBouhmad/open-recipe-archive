## Ingredients

- one spoonful of lard or butter
- one garlic clove
- two onions, chopped
- two ground tomatoes
- one spoonful of flour fried in butter
- chicken broth
- two white turnip roots, cooked and diced
- cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Brown one garlic clove and two chopped onions in one spoonful of lard or butter
2. When it takes color, add two ground tomatoes and one spoonful of flour fried in butter
3. Dissolve this in a little broth in which the chicken was cooked
4. Add two cooked white turnip roots chopped into cubes
5. Place the cooked chicken, whole or cut as desired
6. Serve hot
