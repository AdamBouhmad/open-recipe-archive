## Ingredients

- 1 glass milk
- 1 glass cream
- 4 ounces sugar
- ladyfingers (chiquiadores)
- dragées

## Directions

1. Beat 1 glass of milk and 1 glass of cream very well for half an hour with 4 ounces of sugar
2. Put a layer of ladyfingers in a platter and a layer of this cream
3. Alternate the layers, trying to make the last one cream
4. Place dragées on top and serve
