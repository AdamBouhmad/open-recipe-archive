## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Milk
- Salt
- Butter
- Parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni with milk and a few grains of salt
2. When cooked, remove and butter a pan
3. Place a layer of macaroni, a layer of Parmesan cheese powder and alternate the layers, making sure the last one is cheese
4. Cover with the rest of the milk and add two ounces of melted butter
5. Place in the oven or over two fires
