## Ingredients

- lard
- tomato
- onion
- parsley, chopped
- capers, finely chopped
- a little water
- a little flour
- a little water
- chicken quarters, cooked
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Heat a pan with lard.
2. Make a fried mixture of tomato, onion in slices, and chopped parsley.
3. When half fried, add the finely chopped capers and a little water.
4. Thicken with a little flour dissolved in a little water.
5. Add the chicken quarters that have been previously cooked.
6. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.
