## Ingredients

- ichintales
- salt
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and clean the ichintales well
2. Cook them with salt
3. When done, remove and let all the water drain
4. Grind them, mixing in eggs like the previous ones
5. Form the cake
6. Fry it in butter over very low heat
