## Ingredients

- avocados
- tomato
- cream cheese
- small onions
- vinegar
- sweet bell pepper
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Slice the avocados into rounds.
2. Slice tomato without seeds.
3. Cut cream cheese into strips.
4. Prepare small onions cooked in vinegar.
5. Peel and slice the bell pepper into rings.
6. Place everything in a salad bowl and dress with vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.
7. Garnish with lettuce.
