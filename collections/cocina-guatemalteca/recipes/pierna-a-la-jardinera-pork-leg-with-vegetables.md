## Ingredients

- 1 pork leg
- 3 chile pods without seeds
- 3 roasted tomatoes
- 1 garlic clove
- salt
- all kinds of cooked and minced vegetables
- butter
- salt
- vinegar
- minced onion
- 2 bay leaves

## Directions

1. Cut or lift a layer from where the leg has the most meat; if possible, 2 or 3 layers
2. Grind 3 chile pods without seeds with 3 roasted tomatoes and 1 garlic clove
3. Season with salt and coat the first layer with this sauce on both sides
4. Have all kinds of cooked and minced vegetables ready and fill with this
5. Coat the second layer with the sauce and add more vegetables
6. Continue in this manner until finished
7. Sew together with thick thread
8. Rub very well with butter, salt and vinegar
9. Add minced onion and 2 bay leaves
10. Place everything in a well-covered pan and leave overnight
11. The next day, put it in the oven for two hours
