## Ingredients

- 12 ounces sugar
- 4 ounces ground almonds
- 4 ounces melted butter
- ladyfingers (chiquiadores)
- sherry
- sweetened milk
- cream
- raisins and almonds or cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify 12 ounces of sugar
2. When it reaches high point, add 4 ounces of ground almonds and 4 ounces of melted butter
3. Put to boil lightly
4. Put in a platter a layer of ladyfingers soaked in sherry and sweetened milk and a layer of cream
5. Alternate the layers trying to make the last one cream
6. Decorate with raisins and almonds or cinnamon powder
