## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- three dried chiles, fried in lard
- one ounce peeled and toasted almonds
- two cloves
- Castilian peppercorns
- a small piece cinnamon
- abundant lard
- half cup water from the cooked fish
- three spoonfuls good vinegar
- oregano powder
- a little oil

## Directions

1. Soak and cook the cod as the previous recipe
2. Grind three dried chiles fried in lard with one ounce of peeled almonds toasted with hot water
3. Grind this with two cloves, some Castilian peppercorns and a small piece of cinnamon
4. Fry in abundant lard until it rises to the top
5. When well boiled, remove the fat and add half a cup of water in which the fish was cooked, three spoonfuls of good vinegar, oregano powder, a little oil and the cod
6. Let it boil a bit and serve
