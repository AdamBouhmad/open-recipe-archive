## Ingredients

- Twelve egg whites
- Twelve egg yolks
- Six ounces of ground and dry sugar
- One pound of sifted flour
- Butter for greasing
- Crema de Leche (milk cream filling)

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites and yolks with six ounces of ground and dry sugar
2. When well beaten, fold in one pound of sifted flour
3. Pour into a greased small dish, leaving some space
4. Bake in the oven
5. As soon as it is cooked, flip it over
6. Cut into three-finger height pieces
7. Cover on top with milk cream filling
