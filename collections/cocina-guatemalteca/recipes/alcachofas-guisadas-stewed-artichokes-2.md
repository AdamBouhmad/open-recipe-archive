## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Oil
- Garlic, one clove
- Onion, chopped
- Tomatoes, three, roasted and ground
- Pepper
- Capers, about ten
- Vinegar, a bit
- Water, a bit
- Bread powder, white, one teaspoon

## Directions

1. Remove the tip and stem, cook in water with salt.
2. Fry in oil one clove of garlic, one chopped onion, three roasted and ground tomatoes with pepper, about ten capers, a bit of vinegar, a bit of water and one teaspoon of white bread powder.
3. Cut the artichokes in half and let them simmer in this.
