## Ingredients

- cooked chickens
- oil, lard or butter
- about 10 olives
- 10 roasted almonds
- broth
- 1 tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked chickens into quarters
2. Fry them in oil, lard or butter
3. When they turn golden color, add about 10 olives
4. Add 10 roasted almonds ground with a little broth and 1 tablespoon of vinegar
