## Ingredients

- rice
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- chorizo
- pork ribs
- pork loin
- chicken or hen
- chicken broth
- peas
- carrot
- potato
- tender green beans
- bell peppers
- snails
- pepper
- clove
- saffron water
- long green chiles (guaques)

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice exactly as in the previous recipe
2. Separately, fry plenty of finely minced tomato, onion and garlic
3. Drop pieces of chorizo, pieces of ribs and pork loin into this (the chorizo should be sliced), with all meat, chicken or hen parboiled and cooked separately
4. Mix all of this into the rice with the chicken broth, cover and add peas, small carrot slices, potato slices, tender green beans, finely chopped bell peppers, snails, pepper powder, clove and a small cup of saffron water
5. Top with long green chiles or guaques, cover and let it cook over very low heat
