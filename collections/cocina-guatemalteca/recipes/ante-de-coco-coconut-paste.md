## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- flesh of one coconut, well ground
- three ounces of soaked, ground, and strained rice
- three ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- one liter of milk
- raisins and almonds for decoration

## Directions

1. Clarify three pounds of sugar
2. Add the well-ground flesh of one coconut, three ounces of soaked, ground, and strained rice, three ounces of peeled and ground almonds, and one liter of milk
3. Place over fire and let it reach the consistency of paste
4. Decorate with raisins and almonds
