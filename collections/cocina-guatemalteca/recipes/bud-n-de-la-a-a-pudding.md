## Ingredients

- 7 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter
- 7 egg whites
- 1 pound bread flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder
- 2 ounces chocolate powder (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat 7 egg yolks with 0.5 pound of sugar and 0.5 pound of butter
2. When everything is well beaten, mix with the 7 egg whites that will have been beaten like for nougat
3. Add 1 pound of bread flour, 1 cup of milk and 1 teaspoon of Royal
4. Place in a mold greased with lard and bake hot
5. If desired, this batter can be made in two parts and in one of them put 2 ounces of chocolate powder; place one layer of batter and one of chocolate
