## Ingredients

- potatoes
- pork meat
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- vinegar
- pepper
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw or cooked and open a hole in them
2. Prepare the filling with blanched and chopped pork meat
3. Fry this with tomato, onion and a clove of peeled and chopped garlic
4. Add a little vinegar, pepper powder and half a tablespoon of white bread powder
