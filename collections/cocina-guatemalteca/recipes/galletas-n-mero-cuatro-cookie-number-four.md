## Ingredients

- Half pound of flour
- Four ounces of sugar
- Two eggs
- One level teaspoon of Royal
- Half glass of milk
- A few grains of salt
- One tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin
2. Cut cookies
3. Bake like the previous ones
