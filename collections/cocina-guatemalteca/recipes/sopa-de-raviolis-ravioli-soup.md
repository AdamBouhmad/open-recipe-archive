## Ingredients

- Two bundles of spinach
- A little water
- A little saltpetre
- Two ounces of ham
- One brain
- One ounce of marrow
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Two tablespoons of grated Flemish cheese
- Four ounces of flour
- Two eggs
- Salt
- Butter
- Lard
- Good broth
- Toasted bread powder
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- One cup of fresh cream

## Directions

1. Cook two bundles of spinach in a little water with a little saltpetre
2. When cooked, drain very well
3. Separately, cook two ounces of ham, one brain and one ounce of marrow
4. When all is cooked, chop with the spinach and add pepper powder, clove, cinnamon and two tablespoons of grated Flemish cheese
5. Take four ounces of flour and knead with two eggs; if the dough is too stiff add another, with a little salt
6. Mix very well and stretch this dough with a rolling pin on a table greased with butter
7. Cut out small circles of the desired size and fill with the previously chopped mixture
8. Seal the edges or cut with a knife, dust flour on the edge and fry in lard with salt over very gentle heat
9. Place on a platter and cover with very good broth thickened with toasted bread powder, pepper powder, clove, cinnamon, one cup of fresh cream and enough broth to boil once, if desired as a soup
10. If desired dry, only cover the ravioli and let it reduce over two fires
