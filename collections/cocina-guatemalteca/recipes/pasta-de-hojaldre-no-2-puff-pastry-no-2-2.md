## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two egg yolks
- one egg white
- water as needed
- half a pound of butter, melted

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add two egg yolks, one egg white and the necessary water so the dough becomes soft
2. Sprinkle flour on a board, stretch the dough with the wooden pin
3. Have half a pound of butter melted, spread this on the dough and close the four corners, roll again with the wooden pin and add more butter, repeating this operation
