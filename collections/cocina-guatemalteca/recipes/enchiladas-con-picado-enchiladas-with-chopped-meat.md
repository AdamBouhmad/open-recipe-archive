## Ingredients

- Tortillas
- Salt
- Pork meat, partially cooked and chopped
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Clove

## Directions

1. Fry tortillas with salt if not made from masa as in previous recipes.
2. Once fried, remove them.
3. Chop a little partially cooked pork meat.
4. Half-fry it with one tablespoon of vinegar, pepper powder, and clove seasoning.
