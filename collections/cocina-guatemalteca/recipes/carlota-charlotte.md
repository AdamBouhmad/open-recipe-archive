## Ingredients

- one liter of milk
- one pound of sugar
- eight egg yolks
- one teaspoon of potato starch or cornstarch
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- one packet of gelatin dissolved in cold water or six sheets of isinglass dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan with one liter of milk and one pound of sugar on the fire and let it boil until it thickens
2. Lower heat and when it is fried, add eight egg yolks and one teaspoon of potato starch or cornstarch dissolved in one cup of milk and one teaspoon of vanilla
3. Put back on the fire with a small packet of gelatin dissolved in cold water or six sheets of isinglass dissolved in hot water
4. Strain and put in a mold to set
