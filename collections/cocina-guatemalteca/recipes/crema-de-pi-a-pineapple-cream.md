## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sugar
- One pineapple, ground
- Six egg yolks
- Half pound of ground almond

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar
2. When at point, add the ground pineapple
3. When it sets, remove and when cool mix in six well-beaten egg yolks and half pound of ground almond
4. Return to the fire until it reaches the consistency of before
5. Place in a platter and in the oven to brown
