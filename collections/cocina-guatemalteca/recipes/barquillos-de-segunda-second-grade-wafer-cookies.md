## Ingredients

- Half a pound of sugar
- Half a liter of water
- One pound of sifted bread flour
- One tablespoon of melted lard
- Juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of sugar in half a liter of water and beat very well
2. Add one pound of sifted bread flour
3. Add one tablespoon of melted lard and the juice of two lemons
4. If too thick, add a little more water; if not, leave as is
5. Prepare the griddle and cook like the previous wafer cookies
