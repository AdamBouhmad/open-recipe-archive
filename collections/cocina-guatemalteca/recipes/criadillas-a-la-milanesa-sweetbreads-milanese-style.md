## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- egg
- bread powder
- lard
- lettuce
- peas
- tomato
- onion

## Directions

1. After cooking, remove the thick skin that covers them
2. Cut lengthwise
3. Dry and coat in beaten egg, then in bread powder
4. Fry in hot lard
5. Place on tender lettuce leaves, garnish with well-cooked green peas, slices of peeled tomato without seeds and very thin onion slices
