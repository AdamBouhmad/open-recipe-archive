## Ingredients

- half pound of chickpeas
- salt
- one large tablespoon of lard
- cabbage
- carrot
- chayote (güisquil)
- potatoes
- a small piece of plantain
- one dried stockfish (zunte)
- three or four roasted tomatoes
- two roasted onions
- three roasted garlic cloves
- a bit of cumin
- a bit of Castilian pepper
- a bit of oregano
- a small piece of achiote
- one French bread soaked in water and salt

## Directions

1. Soak half a pound of chickpeas in water the day before
2. The next morning early put on fire with the same soaking water
3. When half cooked add salt and one large tablespoon of lard
4. After boiling five hours on slow even heat add cabbage, carrot, chayote, potatoes and a small piece of plantain, all finely chopped
5. Soak dried stockfish in very hot water and after ten minutes of soaking remove all the bones and add to the vegetables and chickpeas to boil for half an hour
6. Grind three or four roasted tomatoes, two roasted onions and three roasted garlic cloves
7. Take a bit of cumin, a bit of Castilian pepper, a bit of oregano, a small piece of achiote and grind with all the above and one French bread soaked in water and salt
8. Dissolve with a bit of cold water and add to the broth
9. After boiling half an hour serve hot
