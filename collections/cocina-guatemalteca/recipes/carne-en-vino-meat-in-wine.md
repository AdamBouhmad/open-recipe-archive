## Ingredients

- carne (meat)
- manteca o mantequilla (lard or butter)
- ajo (garlic)
- caldo (broth)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- vino jerez (sherry wine)
- hoja de laurel (bay leaf)
- pimientas de Castilla (Castilian peppers)
- clavos de especia (cloves)

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of meat in lard or butter with three or four mashed garlic cloves
2. When golden, add half broth and half water until the meat is covered, adding salt
3. When half this liquid has been consumed, add a glass of sherry wine, two bay leaves, two Castilian peppers and two cloves
4. Let it reduce and when serving, remove the garlic and spices
