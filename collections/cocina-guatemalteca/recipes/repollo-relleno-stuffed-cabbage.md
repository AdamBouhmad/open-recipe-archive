## Ingredients

- Pork meat, partially cooked
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Salt
- One tablespoon of sherry
- Bread powder, ground with pepper and clove
- Cabbage
- Broth

## Directions

1. Chop the partially cooked pork meat
2. Fry it with a mixture of tomato, onion, and finely chopped garlic
3. Add a little vinegar, salt, one tablespoon of sherry, and bread powder ground with pepper and clove
4. Fill the white part of the cabbage with this mixture
5. Tie the cabbage
6. Place it in a casserole with broth
7. Bake it in the oven
8. When it is cooked, serve it hot
