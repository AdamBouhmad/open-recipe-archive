## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Oil or manteca, two to three tablespoons
- Garlic, three or four cloves
- Salt
- Egg, beaten
- White bread powder
- Lettuce leaves, green
- Olives
- Onions, cooked and separated into leaves
- Oregano powder, abundant
- Avocado, sliced

## Directions

1. In two to three tablespoons of oil or manteca, fry three or four cloves of garlic.
2. Have the cauliflower already cut into pieces and cooked with salt; dry it with a cloth.
3. Coat in beaten egg, then in white bread powder.
4. Fry in the same manteca where the garlic was fried.
5. When golden, place on green lettuce leaves on the platter.
6. Decorate with olives, cooked and separated onions, abundant oregano powder and avocado slices.
