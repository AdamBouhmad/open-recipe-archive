## Ingredients

- mutton
- vinegar
- salt
- garlic
- paprika
- bay leaves
- oregano powder
- water
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Take the meat well washed and place in a dish with vinegar, salt, crushed garlic, one tablespoon of paprika, bay leaves and oregano powder
2. Leave overnight
3. The next day add water to cover the meat and about three tomato halves
4. Let cook
5. When serving, strain the sauce
