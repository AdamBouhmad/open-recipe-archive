## Ingredients

- White beans
- Salt
- Garlic head
- Water
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Pepper powder
- Onion

## Directions

1. Cook the white beans with salt and one garlic head
2. When well cooked, remove it and prepare a dressing with half water and half vinegar, salt, two large spoonfuls of oil, pepper powder and plenty of onion in very thin slices blanched in boiling water
3. Place the beans in this dressing, mix very well, cover it and let it soak in this infusion for two hours before serving immediately
