## Ingredients

- brains
- water
- vinegar
- six eggs
- one glass milk
- two tablespoons flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- green beans

## Directions

1. Place brains in water; after ten minutes, remove the skin and cook in vinegar.
2. When cooked, let cool and slice.
3. Coat a baking pan with butter and place the brain slices in it.
4. Beat six eggs, one glass of milk, and two tablespoons of flour together.
5. Beat the eggs to stiff peaks and then mix in the milk and flour; season with salt and a bit of nutmeg.
6. Cover the brains with this mixture and place in the oven for ten minutes.
7. Serve with string bean salad.
