## Ingredients

- pepitoria
- sesame
- chile
- pasa or guaque
- miltomate
- small peppers
- cinnamon
- cloves
- white bread
- lard
- cooked tongue
- egg

## Directions

1. Toast pepitoria, sesame, chile, pasa or guaque and a little miltomate.
2. Grind all this with small peppers, a small piece of cinnamon, two cloves and half a white bread.
3. Fry this in a little lard.
4. Once the tongue is cooked, make slices not very thin, dry them well and wrap them in beaten egg.
5. Fry in hot lard over low heat, ensuring they come out golden.
6. Leave them in the sauce to give a light boil and serve hot.
