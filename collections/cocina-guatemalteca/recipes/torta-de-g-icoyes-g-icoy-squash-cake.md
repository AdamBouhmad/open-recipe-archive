## Ingredients

- tender güicoyes
- butter
- salt
- green chile pimiento
- fresh cheese or butter

## Directions

1. Slice the tender güicoyes.
2. Butter a pan and put a layer of güicoy slices with a little salt and small pieces of chile pimiento chopped lengthwise.
3. Alternate layers, some of güicoy and others of small pieces of fresh cheese or butter.
4. Put in the oven or on very low heat; when it has boiled for a quarter hour, cover with a beaten egg; if there is no oven, place a tin with hot coals on top to brown it.
