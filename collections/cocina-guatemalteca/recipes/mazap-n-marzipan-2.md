## Ingredients

- Two pounds of peanut
- Water for blanching
- One egg white
- Three glasses of cooked milk
- Three pounds of well-clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Put two pounds of peanut in a little lime water
2. When it has boiled sufficiently and peels, remove from heat
3. The next day, wash in a fine-mesh basket
4. Change the water many times until all three skins covering it are removed
5. Grind in a very clean stone mortar and wet with a little water where an egg white has been beaten so it doesn't stick
6. Dissolve this with three glasses of cooked milk
7. Add to three pounds of well-clarified sugar at high point
8. When it reaches the consistency of before, remove from heat and decorate with dragées
