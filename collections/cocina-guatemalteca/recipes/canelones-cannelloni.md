## Ingredients

- one pound bread flour
- five eggs
- two ounces butter
- water
- salt
- onion stalks
- white sauce
- chicken
- ham
- brains
- pâté
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a kneading board and add five eggs and two ounces of butter.
2. Knead very well, let rest for some hours, or better if moistened the day before.
3. Roll out the dough and cut cannelloni four inches long by two inches wide.
4. Prepare a pan with boiling water, salt and onion stalks.
5. Boil the cannelloni for ten minutes, then remove them.
6. Prepare a thick white sauce and mix with minced chicken, ham, brains and pâté.
7. Fill the cannelloni and roll them into cannelloni shape.
8. Place them in a buttered pan, cover with the remaining sauce and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
9. If desired, place in the oven for a while.
