## Ingredients

- One cup of milk
- One cup of butter
- Two cups of powdered sugar
- Two cups of flour
- Five egg yolks
- Three egg whites
- One teaspoon of Royal
- One teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat sugar with butter until very thick
2. Add flour little by little
3. Add milk
4. Add one egg yolk and beat for five minutes
5. Add another egg yolk and beat five minutes more
6. Repeat with the remaining three yolks, beating five minutes each time
7. Finally add three beaten egg whites as for nougat, one teaspoon of sifted Royal, and one teaspoon of vanilla
8. Pour into a buttered mold
9. Put in a hot oven
