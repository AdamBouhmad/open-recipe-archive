## Ingredients

- half pound flour
- two eggs
- salt
- four ounces butter or lard
- three lemons
- six egg yolks
- sugar
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Make puff pastry dough with half a pound of flour, two eggs, a pinch of salt, and four ounces of butter or lard; knead well and roll out with a rolling pin.
2. Put a very thin layer on a pan or plate and bake in the oven just until cooked.
3. Beat four ounces of butter with the juice of three lemons, put on heat, and gradually add six tablespoons of sugar and six egg yolks without stopping to stir.
4. When thickened, cover the pastry with this mixture.
5. Beat the six egg whites as for turrón, add three tablespoons of powdered sugar and the zest of three lemons, cover the cream with this and bake in the oven until golden.
