## Ingredients

- one pound of bread flour or salporrita flour
- three eggs
- salt grains
- two egg yolks
- four ounces of sugar
- five ounces of lard
- anise grains

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, eggs, salt grains, yolks, sugar, lard, and anise grains
2. Knead very well for half an hour
3. Place small balls on baking trays with sprinkles on top
4. Bake in the oven for ten minutes
