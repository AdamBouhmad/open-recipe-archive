## Ingredients

- three hard egg yolks
- oil
- bread flour
- broth
- capers
- pepper powder
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop three hard egg yolks, put two spoonfuls of oil in a pan
2. add one spoonful of bread flour, gradually add broth while stirring until it becomes a thin sauce
3. add the egg yolk and about ten chopped capers, pepper powder, salt and one large spoonful of vinegar
