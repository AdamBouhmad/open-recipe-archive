## Ingredients

- Six ounces of cinnamon
- One ounce of palo de la vida
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. The day before, bring six ounces of cinnamon with one ounce of palo de la vida to a boil
2. Strain and add sugar to taste
3. This quantity yields twenty-five glasses of horchata
