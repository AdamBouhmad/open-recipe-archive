## Ingredients

- potatoes
- eggs
- cheese
- shrimp (optional)
- salt
- butter
- tomato
- onion
- breadcrumb powder
- annatto
- black pepper

## Directions

1. After the potatoes are cooked, peel and grind them
2. For half a pound of potatoes, mix in three eggs, a piece of cheese, and if available one ounce of shrimp, peeled and blanched
3. Season with salt
4. Form the small cakes
5. Fry them in hot butter over low heat
6. Serve alone or with a sauce made from peeled and chopped tomato with onion
7. When fried, add a little water, salt, and a little breadcrumb powder with a small piece of annatto and some black pepper
