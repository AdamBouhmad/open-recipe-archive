## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- carrot
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- thyme
- salt
- white wine

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, place in a dish with one tablespoon of oil.
2. Add slices of peeled carrot, onions, chopped garlic and parsley and a sprig of thyme.
3. Season with salt and one glass of white wine.
4. When serving, remove the thyme.
