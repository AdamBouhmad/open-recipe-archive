## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- a little baking soda
- watercress
- hot water
- tomato
- onion

## Directions

1. Put a pot with water on the fire, salt and a little baking soda
2. Prepare the watercress by removing all the stems
3. Wash very well with hot water
4. Add to the pot and let it boil for ten minutes on moderate heat
5. Remove immediately, as if it takes more time it will turn black
6. If desired, before removing from the fire add a halved tomato and an onion to eat thus alone, if not, remove all the water and fry what is greens with tomato and chopped onion
