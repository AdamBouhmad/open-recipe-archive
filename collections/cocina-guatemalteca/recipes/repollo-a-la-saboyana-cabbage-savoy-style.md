## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- pork loin sliced
- lard or butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Chop and cook the cabbage in salted water, drain in a colander
2. In a mold put a layer of cabbage and a layer of pork loin made into slices and fried very well with its fat
3. Put another layer of cabbage and continue alternating until the mold reaches the desired height
4. Add one spoonful of lard or butter, put in the oven for one hour and when ready to serve, put a little tomato sauce on top
