## Ingredients

- chayotes
- salt
- one tablespoon butter
- peeled tomato
- garlic clove
- chopped onion
- cheese
- pepper powder
- one tablespoon butter
- white breadcrumb powder

## Directions

1. Cook whole with salt
2. When cooked, open a small square on each, remove all the inside, chop it and fry in one tablespoon of butter with peeled tomato, one garlic clove and chopped onion
3. When well fried, add plenty of cheese and a little pepper powder
4. Stuff the chayote with this mixture, cover with the same small square
5. Rub with one tablespoon of butter and white breadcrumb powder
6. Place in the oven for ten minutes
