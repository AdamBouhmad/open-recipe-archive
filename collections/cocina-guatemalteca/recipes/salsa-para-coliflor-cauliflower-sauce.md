## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- three roasted tomatoes
- Castilian pepper
- oregano
- onion
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower in pieces in salted water and remove it
2. place in half a cup of vinegar and two spoonfuls of oil for two hours
3. grind about three roasted tomatoes with Castilian pepper and a little oregano
4. dissolve this with the same vinegar in which the cauliflower is
5. arrange on a platter and well seasoned with salt, cover the cauliflower with this sauce
6. add onion sliced and blanched with boiling water, olives and capers
