## Ingredients

- beans
- two tablespoons of butter
- one onion, chopped
- Parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. Soak the beans in water the night before
2. When well cooked, grind them very fine
3. Heat a pan with two tablespoons of butter and one chopped onion
4. Add the beans
5. If too dry, add another tablespoon of butter
6. Serve with Parmesan cheese powder around the plate
