## Ingredients

- one garlic clove
- onion
- four roasted ground tomatoes
- oil
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- parsley, chopped
- dry cheese powder
- lard
- tortillas
- cooked brains

## Directions

1. Fry one garlic clove, onion, and four roasted ground tomatoes in one spoonful of oil
2. Add pepper powder, oregano powder, chopped parsley, and dry cheese powder
3. Coat a pan with one spoonful of lard
4. Have tortillas well fried
5. Dip tortillas in the sauce
6. Layer tortillas, then cooked brains sliced, then tortillas, alternating layers and ensuring the last layer is tortillas
7. Pour the remaining sauce on top
8. Place in the oven or over two fires to cook
