## Ingredients

- Cleaned ducks
- Oil
- Salt
- Half a bottle of white, red or sherry wine
- Powder of cinnamon
- Pepper
- Clove
- Laurel
- Thyme
- Water
- Tender lettuces

## Directions

1. Well cleaned ducks are fried in oil and salt
2. When they have taken on a golden color, add half a bottle of white, red or sherry wine, whichever is preferred, powder of cinnamon, pepper, clove, laurel and thyme
3. Cover completely with water and salt
4. Let them boil
5. When they are reduced, brown them and serve with tender lettuces
