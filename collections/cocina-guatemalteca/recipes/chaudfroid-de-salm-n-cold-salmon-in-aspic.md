## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt
- pepper powder
- a few drops of onion juice
- 2 tablespoons gelatin
- 1 tablespoon water
- cucumber strips
- ice water
- salmon

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoons of butter, three of flour, one cup of heavy cream and one beaten egg yolk very well
2. Add one and one-half tablespoon of lemon juice, a little salt, pepper powder and a few drops of onion juice
3. Dissolve two tablespoons of gelatin in one tablespoon of water and add to the sauce when it is already cold
4. Let it set; use this to cover the salmon and decorate with cucumber strips, which have been placed in ice water beforehand
