## Ingredients

- one cleaned turkey
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- three to four garlic cloves
- parsley
- three to four onions
- thin slices of ham
- cloth
- water
- ham
- sherry wine
- three bay leaves
- four to six slices of bitter orange
- three onion heads
- thyme
- half cup of oil
- half cup of vinegar
- lettuce
- fresh pickles

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and add salt
2. Grind a little pepper, clove, a small piece of cinnamon and a small piece of nutmeg
3. Chop three to four peeled garlic cloves, a little parsley and three to four onions, all finely
4. Mix together and rub the turkey inside and outside with this mixture
5. Cover with very thin slices of ham and wrap in a very thin cloth
6. Place in a pan and cover with water
7. After two hours of boiling over even heat, remove the turkey and lard it with ham chopped with three garlic cloves and pepper powder
8. Then place in a pan with one glass of dry sherry wine, three bay leaves, salt, four to six slices of peeled bitter orange without seeds, three peeled onion heads, a sprig of thyme, half a cup of oil and half a cup of vinegar
9. Keep on the fire for one hour
10. Serve with lettuce leaves and fresh pickles
