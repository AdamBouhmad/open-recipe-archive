## Ingredients

- split backbone
- salt
- some raw tomatillo
- garlic
- one cooked guaque chile
- oregano
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- a small piece of achiote
- one soaked French roll

## Directions

1. Cook the split backbone with salt
2. Grind some raw tomatillo, better if small, with garlic, one cooked guaque chile, oregano, cumin, Castilian pepper, a small piece of achiote and one soaked French roll
3. Thin with the broth
4. Put a pan on the fire, remove all the broth in which the backbone was cooked and in this fry the recado, adding the meat pieces
