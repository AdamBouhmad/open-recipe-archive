## Ingredients

- squashes, chopped
- a little water
- onion
- tomato
- one clove of garlic, chopped
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt to taste
- ground bread powder with cinnamon
- one small clove
- Castilian peppers
- half a teaspoonful of vinegar
- puff pastry dough
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the squashes and place in a dish with a little water; add onion, tomato, one chopped clove of garlic, one tablespoon of butter and salt to taste
2. When cooked and without water, add ground bread powder with cinnamon, one small clove, some Castilian peppers, half a teaspoonful of vinegar
3. Remove from the fire and have a puff pastry dough prepared
4. Cut to the desired size, stretch with the wooden pin, fill with the squash, fold, crimp and place in the oven
5. If desired with more flavor, fry in hot lard
