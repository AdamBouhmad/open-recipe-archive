## Ingredients

- Two-month-old piglet
- Milk with bread (sweetened)
- Chopped parsley
- Sweet wine
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Butter
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Garlic head
- Parsley
- Mint
- Bundle of onions
- Leek (one, if available)
- Pork meat (two pounds for stuffing)
- Salt
- Green beans
- Carrots
- Potatoes
- Peas
- Onion
- Tomato
- Raisins (two ounces, pitted)
- Almonds (one ounce)
- Olives
- Capers
- Pitted prunes
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Clove powder
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Sherry wine (one small glass)

## Directions

1. Prepare a two-month-old piglet and feed it sweetened milk with bread for eight days.
2. Two days before killing, give it chopped parsley with sweet wine.
3. The day before, give it only water.
4. To kill the animal, slaughter it carefully without opening it much.
5. When it dies, be careful to sew up the wound.
6. Have boiling water ready and pour it on the animal.
7. When clean, prick it with a fork and rub it with salt, vinegar, and pepper powder inside and out.
8. If making it without stuffing: sew it closed and put it in the oven brushed with butter, with two cups of water, bay leaves, a sprig of thyme, a garlic head, parsley, mint, a bundle of onions, and puerro if available.
9. Put it in the oven and take it out every half hour to baste it with its juices.
10. If making it with stuffing: parboil two pounds of pork meat with salt; when cooked, mince it. Separately, parboil green beans, carrots, potatoes in small pieces, and peas.
11. Make a fried mixture with plenty of onion and tomato; add the meat, vegetables, about two ounces of pitted raisins, one ounce of almonds, olives, capers, pitted prunes, a little vinegar and sugar to taste, a little clove powder, pepper and cinnamon, and one small glass of sherry wine.
12. Stuff the piglet and prepare it as above.
13. The animal should be killed one day before.
