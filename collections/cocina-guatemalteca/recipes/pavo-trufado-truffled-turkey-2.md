## Ingredients

- turkey, boned
- vinegar
- dry wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- 3 and a half pounds of pork loin, ground
- powdered pepper
- powdered toasted bay leaf
- 4 ounces of fresh ham
- chopped truffles
- a pinch of saltpeter
- a little ginger water
- olives
- capers
- onion stems
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- mint
- leek

## Directions

1. After boning the turkey, bathe it with vinegar and add dry wine, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon, all ground
2. For the filling: grind 3 and a half pounds of pork loin and add vinegar, salt, sufficient powdered pepper, powdered toasted bay leaf, 4 ounces of fresh ham, chopped truffles, a pinch of saltpeter, and a little ginger water
3. Leave in brine the day before; the next day fill the turkey and add olives and capers
4. Cook in sufficient water with onion stems, bay leaf, thyme, parsley, mint, and leek, and wrap in cloth
