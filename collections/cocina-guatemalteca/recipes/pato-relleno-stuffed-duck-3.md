## Ingredients

- one duck
- duck meat from the thickest parts
- duck livers
- two pounds pork loin
- salt
- onion
- chopped parsley
- three or four mushrooms (if available)
- butter
- onions
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Bone the duck as you would a turkey
2. Remove some meat from the thickest parts and chop it together with the livers and two pounds of pork loin
3. Season with salt, onion, and chopped parsley; if mushrooms are available, chop three or four and add
4. Mix everything together well
5. Stuff the duck with this mixture and sew it closed with thick thread
6. Coat well with butter and place in a pan with quartered onions and two bay leaves
7. Place in the oven, basting frequently so it does not dry out
