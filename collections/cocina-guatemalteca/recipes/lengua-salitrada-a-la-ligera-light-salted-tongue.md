## Ingredients

- tongues
- water
- salt
- saltpeter
- bay leaves
- leeks
- parsley
- thyme
- orange leaves
- Castilian pepper
- wine

## Directions

1. Clean the tongues as in the previous recipe.
2. Place them in a container and cover with very clean water, four pounds of salt and two ounces of saltpeter.
3. Four to six tongues can be cured this way.
4. Keep them thus for three days, ensuring they are well covered and the container is left in the open air.
5. Add all the same ingredients as in the previous recipe but cook with the same water and one bottle of wine.
