## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Oil for frying
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Bread slices, browned in butter
- Tender lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in oil, making sure they come out very whole; add salt and pepper powder, remove with a skimmer with great care, place them on slices of bread browned in lard, place them on a platter on leaves of tender lettuce and add pepper powder.
