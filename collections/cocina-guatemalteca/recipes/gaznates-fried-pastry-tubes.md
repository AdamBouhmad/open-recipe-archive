## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- juice of 2 sweet oranges
- 5 eggs
- a little milk
- a pinch of salt
- lard for frying
- marzipan, candied yam, or candied citron for filling
- honey beaten to stiff peaks
- colored tissue paper

## Directions

1. Moisten one pound of flour with the juice of two sweet oranges, five eggs, a little milk, and a pinch of salt
2. Knead very well and let rest for half an hour
3. Roll out with the rolling pin and cut strips the width of a finger
4. Wrap them around cardboard tubes not too thick to give them a curled shape and fry them in hot lard
5. When fried, carefully remove the tube and fill with marzipan, candied yam, or candied citron
6. Have honey beaten to a very stiff peak, frost with this and cover the holes with finely chopped colored tissue paper for a nice appearance
