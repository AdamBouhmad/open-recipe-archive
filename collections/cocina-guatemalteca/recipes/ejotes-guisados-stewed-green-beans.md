## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- bicarbonate
- one tablespoon oil
- peeled tomato
- chopped onion
- piece of green chile
- sardines
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Cook the green beans in boiling water with salt and a little bicarbonate
2. Fry peeled tomato, chopped onion and a piece of green chile in one tablespoon of oil
3. Add the green beans to this mixture
4. Place on the platter
5. Cover with sardines and pepper powder
