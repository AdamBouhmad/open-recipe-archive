## Ingredients

- good broth
- one tablespoon of flour fried in lard or butter
- fried seasoning paste of tomato, onion, and garlic
- minced parsley
- half a pound of loin or very tender beef
- small piece of cinnamon
- small amount of cumin
- small amount of saffron
- eight Castilian peppercorns
- two eggs
- two eggs beaten
- one ounce of rice flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare a good broth and add one tablespoon of flour fried in lard or butter, fried seasoning paste of tomato, onion, and garlic, and minced parsley
2. Grind half a pound of loin or very tender beef and mix in a small piece of cinnamon, a small amount of cumin, a small amount of saffron, and eight Castilian peppercorns
3. Grind everything well and mix with two eggs
4. Using the tip of a knife, drop meatballs into the broth
5. Make small fritters with two beaten eggs mixed with one ounce of rice flour
6. Fry them by small spoonfuls in hot lard
7. Just before serving, add them to the broth for a light boil
