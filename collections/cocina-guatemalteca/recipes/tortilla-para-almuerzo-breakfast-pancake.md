## Ingredients

- two eggs
- four tablespoons cornmeal or corn flour
- five tablespoons wheat flour
- one small glass milk
- sugar
- half teaspoon baking soda
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs well.
2. Mix in four tablespoons of cornmeal or corn flour, five tablespoons of wheat flour, one glass of milk and sugar to taste.
3. Beat everything very well together.
4. Heat lard or butter in a pan.
5. When the mixture is beaten, add half a teaspoon of baking soda.
6. Fry by spoonfuls.
7. As they cook, place them on a butter-greased plate and continue with the rest.
