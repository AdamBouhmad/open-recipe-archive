## Ingredients

- one chicken
- wooden sticks
- chicken breast
- chicken liver
- chicken gizzard
- beef tongue
- ham
- almonds
- salt
- pepper powder
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- garlic head
- leeks
- onion
- butter

## Directions

1. After plucking and cleaning the chicken well, remove the skin with great care so it does not break
2. Place wooden sticks in the middle in a cross so it does not dry out
3. Cut into pieces the breast, liver, gizzard and all the meat of the chicken with small pieces of cooked beef tongue, pieces of ham cut lengthwise, some peeled and chopped almonds, and season with salt, pepper powder and a little vinegar
4. Fill the chicken with this mixture and remove the sticks
5. Sew it closed, giving it the desired shape, and wrap it tightly in a cloth so it keeps the shape given to it
6. Place in a serving dish covering it with water, bay leaves, thyme, a garlic head, a bunch of leeks and onion
7. When cooked, remove it; whether you want to serve it cold or hot, always press it; if serving hot, after pressing rub it with butter and place in the oven to brown
