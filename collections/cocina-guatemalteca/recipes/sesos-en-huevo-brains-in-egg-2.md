## Ingredients

- brains
- water
- lemon juice
- onion
- parsley
- bay leaf
- salt
- egg
- lard
- fried potatoes
- raw tomato

## Directions

1. Cook the brains in water with lemon juice (half a lemon), onion, parsley, a bay leaf and salt.
2. When cooked, remove them and cut into desired shape.
3. Wrap them in beaten egg and fry in very hot lard over low heat.
4. Serve with fried potatoes, lemon slices, and slices of raw peeled tomato.
