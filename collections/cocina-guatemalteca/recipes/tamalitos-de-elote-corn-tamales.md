## Ingredients

- corn ears (6 to 8, not very ripe)
- cheese (1)
- salt (to taste)
- sugar (to taste)
- lard or butter (1 large spoon)

## Directions

1. Shell 6 to 8 ears of corn that are not very ripe, grinding until the masa is very fine.
2. Grind cheese with salt and sugar to taste.
3. Add 1 large spoon of lard or butter.
4. Wrap the mixture in the corn husks.
5. Cook in a steamer.
6. The next day, reheat well and serve.
