## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Salt
- Pork meat
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic clove
- Vinegar
- Dry sherry
- White bread powder
- Peppers
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Butter
- Paper

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with salt
2. Cut the bones to a span in length, scraping the meat so most of the bone is clean
3. Have one pound of cooked and finely chopped pork meat ready
4. Fry with peeled and chopped tomato, onion, and a garlic clove
5. When half fried, add two tablespoons of vinegar and two of dry sherry
6. Season with salt, a little white bread powder, about four peppers, two cloves, and a small piece of cinnamon
7. Place the bone on a piece of white paper spread with butter
8. Fold the paper or tie with twine
9. Place on a platter in a low oven or over a grill with low heat
