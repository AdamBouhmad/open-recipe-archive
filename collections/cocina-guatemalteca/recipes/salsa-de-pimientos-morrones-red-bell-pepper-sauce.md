## Ingredients

- six tomatoes
- six red bell peppers
- oil
- vinegar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook or roast six tomatoes, grind them with six red bell peppers
2. put a pan on the fire with two spoonfuls of oil
3. fry the tomato in this, adding half a cup of vinegar and half a cup of water, seasoned with salt
4. serve hot
