## Ingredients

- Butter
- Parboiled and chopped mushrooms
- Pepper powder
- Four hard egg yolks
- Ten or twelve fresh oysters
- White bread powder

## Directions

1. Place a pan with one tablespoon of butter, if available, some parboiled and chopped mushrooms, pepper powder, four chopped hard egg yolks and ten or twelve fresh oysters
2. Fry everything well and fill the shells with this
3. Cover with white bread powder and place in the oven
