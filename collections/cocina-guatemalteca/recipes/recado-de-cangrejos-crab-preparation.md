## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- small crab claws
- roasted tomatoes
- onion head
- dried chile
- achiote (annatto)
- rice or masa

## Directions

1. Clean the crabs well and remove the small claws.
2. Cook the crabs with salt.
3. Grind the small claws very well with two roasted tomatoes, one onion head, and if desired, a dried chile and a small piece of achiote for color.
4. Grind a little rice or masa to thicken.
5. Serve after boiling well, either thick or thin as desired.
