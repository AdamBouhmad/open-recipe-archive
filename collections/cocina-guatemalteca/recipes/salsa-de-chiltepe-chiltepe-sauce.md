## Ingredients

- six ounces chiltepe
- two pounds tomatillo
- eight cloves garlic
- eight onion heads
- one small bunch fresh cilantro
- vinegar
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Toast the tomatillo and all other ingredients.
2. Grind with the cilantro.
3. Mix in good vinegar and salt.
4. Add oil if desired.
5. Store in well-sealed jars.
