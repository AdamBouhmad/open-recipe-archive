## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil
- two carrots
- flour
- water
- red wine
- bay leaf
- parsley
- garlic
- mint
- mushrooms
- six to eight heads of onion
- bread

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and place in a dish with oil to take on color
2. Add two chopped and halved carrots and a spoonful of flour
3. Cover half with water and half with red wine, a bay leaf bundle, a sprig of parsley, a clove of garlic, a sprig of mint and if there are mushrooms, add some raw and chopped, and six to eight heads of onion
4. Let consume
5. When serving, remove all the herbs and at the edge of the platter place very thin slices of fried bread
