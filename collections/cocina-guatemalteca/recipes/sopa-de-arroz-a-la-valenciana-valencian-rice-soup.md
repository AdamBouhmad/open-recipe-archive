## Ingredients

- 1 pound rice
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- 1 clove garlic
- bell peppers
- 1 tender fat chicken
- saffron water
- pepper
- green peas
- olives
- capers
- sardines
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pass the rice through a strainer so it comes out very whole, weigh one pound of rice and wash it lightly, better if washed a day before; if it is local rice, wash it, if it is bomb rice only clean it and fry it in oil
2. When fried, add sufficient fried seasoning of peeled tomato, onion, one clove of garlic and a small jar of finely chopped bell peppers
3. Meanwhile, have parboiled a tender fat chicken, add the chicken pieces and broth in which it was cooked
4. Add one cup of saffron water and pepper powder, and if desired, some green peas
5. Use no more than one liter of water per pound of rice, cover and let it boil over very low heat so the rice comes out fluffy, do not stir
6. When cooked, after about half an hour, place on a platter and top with olives, capers, sardines and hard-boiled egg slices
