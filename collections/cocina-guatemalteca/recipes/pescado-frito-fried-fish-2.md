## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- flour
- oil or lard
- garlic
- vinegar
- chile verde (green chile)
- onion
- hard-boiled egg
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- lettuce

## Directions

1. After washing the fish well, cut it into pieces of desired size.
2. Season with salt and coat in flour.
3. Fry crushed garlic in hot oil or lard.
4. When the fish is well browned, place it on a platter.
5. Bathe the fish with a sauce made of one cup of vinegar, another of oil, a green chile and finely chopped onion and pieces of hard-boiled egg.
6. Wash the onion with boiling water.
7. Add pepper powder and oregano powder.
8. Arrange chopped lettuce around the fish.
