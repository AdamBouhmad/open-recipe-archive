## Ingredients

- güisquil
- salt
- one teaspoon lard
- peeled tomato
- chopped onion

## Directions

1. Peel the güisquil, chop into small squares or marquesotitos, cook with salt
2. Fry in one teaspoon of lard, peeled tomato with chopped onion
3. Add the güisquil to this
