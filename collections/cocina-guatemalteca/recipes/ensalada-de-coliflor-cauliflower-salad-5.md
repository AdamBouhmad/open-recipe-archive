## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Oil
- Onion
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower in large pieces or very finely chopped, as desired
2. Once cooked, place it in the platter and bathe it with a dressing composed of half water and half vinegar, salt, two spoonfuls of oil
3. Decorate with onion slices blanched in boiling water and sprinkle with oregano powder
4. The same is done for beet salad and carrot salad
