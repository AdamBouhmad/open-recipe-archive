## Ingredients

- cooked tongue
- cooked tomato
- guache or pasa chile
- thyme
- lard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Once the tongue is well cooked, slice it.
2. Grind a little cooked tomato with a guache or pasa chile and a sprig of thyme.
3. Fry it in a little lard and add the tongue slices.
4. Serve with a little vinegar.
