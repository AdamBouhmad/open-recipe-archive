## Ingredients

- beans
- two spoonfuls of lard
- salt
- one pound of beans
- six ounces of rice
- water
- one tablespoon of lard
- tomato and onion, chopped
- four bundles of chipilín leaves
- another tablespoon of lard

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. When the beans are half-cooked, add two spoonfuls of lard and salt
3. The day before serving, in a dish combine for one pound of beans: six ounces of rice with water, salt, one tablespoon of lard, tomato and chopped onion
4. Parboil about four bundles of chipilín leaves, remove the water, and add to the rice
5. Take care not to stir it so it does not fall apart
6. Leave it and the next day add the beans when they are already cooked, with another tablespoon of lard
7. Let it boil on very low heat
8. After half an hour of cooking it can be served
