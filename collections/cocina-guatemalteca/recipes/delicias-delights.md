## Ingredients

- 1 pound of peeled almonds (dry)
- 3 ounces of sifted sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk (if needed)
- juice of 1 lemon
- syrup at proper stage for coating

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of peeled almonds (dry)
2. Add three ounces of well-sifted sugar
3. Mix this with one egg and if too stiff add one yolk with the juice of a lemon
4. Place on tins in the form of mantecado
5. Bake in the oven
6. When golden, coat with proper stage syrup
