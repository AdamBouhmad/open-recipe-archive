## Ingredients

- white beans
- water
- salt
- peeled tomato
- onion
- garlic clove
- breton vegetables
- achiote (annatto)
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook the white beans and after boiling for one hour, drain the water and add fresh boiling water with salt.
2. When the beans are well cooked, make a fried mixture of peeled tomato, onion, and a garlic clove.
3. Add the beans and the breton vegetables that have been cooked beforehand to this mixture.
4. Add a bit of achiote for color and half a teaspoon of flour dissolved in cold water.
