## Ingredients

- ten crabs
- salt
- half pound of well-washed rice
- fried seasoning of tomato, onion and garlic
- saffron water
- a teaspoon of lard

## Directions

1. Wash ten crabs very well with boiling water, removing the thin parts from their legs
2. Cook the crabs with salt
3. After they have boiled for half an hour, add half a pound of well-washed rice without soaking it
4. Add fried seasoning made from tomato, onion and finely chopped garlic, a bit of saffron water and a teaspoon of lard
5. Leave until the rice is cooked, making sure it comes out whole; do not stir, only move the pot so it does not stick
6. Serve hot
