## Ingredients

- 1 glass of wine
- 4 egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- 12 ounces of lard
- 1.5 pounds of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put one glass of wine and add four egg yolks, a pinch of salt, and twelve ounces of lard
2. Mix everything well together
3. Add one and a half pounds of sifted flour and stir without kneading
4. Form small balls of the desired size, coat them in beaten egg, dust with powdered sugar, and place in the oven
