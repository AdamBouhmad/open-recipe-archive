## Ingredients

- Dried shrimp
- Small fish
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Grind two ounces of dried shrimp and one of small fish.
2. Beat four eggs as in the previous recipes.
3. Mix everything well.
4. Form the omelette in hot butter.
5. Fry over very low heat.
