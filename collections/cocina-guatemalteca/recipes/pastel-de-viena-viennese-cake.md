## Ingredients

- Four cups of flour
- Two cups of sifted sugar
- One cup of milk with cream
- One cup of melted butter
- Nine egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in a bowl
2. Mix everything well and beat
3. Pour into a buttered mold
4. Put in a hot oven
