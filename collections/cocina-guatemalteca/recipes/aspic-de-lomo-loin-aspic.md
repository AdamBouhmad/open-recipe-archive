## Ingredients

- one pound loin
- beef gelatin
- hard-boiled eggs
- cooked ham
- lettuce
- cucumber
- lemon

## Directions

1. Braise one pound of loin, then press it immediately.
2. Cut into very thin slices.
3. Place in a mold with two glasses of beef gelatin, alternating slices of hard-boiled egg and cooked ham with the meat slices.
4. Cover with gelatin and refrigerate on ice.
5. When unmolded, place on a salad of finely cut lettuce, decorated with cucumber slices and lemon slices.
