## Ingredients

- Two glasses of cooked milk
- Sugar to taste
- Salt to taste
- Three egg yolks
- One ounce of cornstarch or rice flour, soaked and ground
- Half teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Put two glasses of cooked milk with sugar to taste, salt, three egg yolks, and one ounce of soaked and ground cornstarch or rice flour
2. Strain through fine cloth
3. Place on heat, stirring constantly so it does not stick
4. Add half teaspoon of vanilla
5. Pour onto a plate; can be used for many pastries
