## Ingredients

- sweet potato (preferably yellow variety)
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- a small teaspoon of lime lye
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the sweet potato, preferably if it is of the yellow variety
2. Place it in water with a small teaspoon of lime lye for a quarter of an hour
3. Have one and a half pounds of sugar clarified
4. Place the sweet potato in the clarified sugar to cook through
