## Ingredients

- half a pound of peeled, dry, ground almonds
- six ounces of sifted sugar
- two egg whites beaten until stiff

## Directions

1. Mix the ground almonds with the sifted sugar
2. Add the stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Place in a paper cone or in a small metal tube in cloth
4. Form rings on trays
5. Bake in the oven
