## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Lard or butter
- Garlic
- Onion
- Parsley, chopped
- Broth in which the squabs have been cooked
- 1 tablespoon of well-fried flour
- Powder of pepper
- Clove
- 1 glass of white wine
- A little vinegar
- 6 to 8 blanched and chopped almonds
- Capers

## Directions

1. Fry garlic, onion, and chopped parsley in lard or butter
2. Add a little of the broth in which the squabs have been cooked, a tablespoon of well-fried flour, a little powder of pepper and clove, a glass of white wine, a little vinegar, 6 to 8 blanched and chopped almonds and some capers
