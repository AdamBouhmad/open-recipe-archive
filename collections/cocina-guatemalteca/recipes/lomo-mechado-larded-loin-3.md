## Ingredients

- Strips of ham
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Butter or margarine for browning
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Dried chile pepper
- Oregano
- Thyme
- One bay leaf
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare strips of ham with salt and pepper powder
2. Lard the loin with these strips and place in a pot to brown in butter or margarine
3. Once it has taken on a golden color, grind a little pepper, cinnamon, dried chile pepper, oregano, thyme, and a bay leaf
4. Toast all spices and dissolve in a little water and vinegar
5. Add to the meat
6. Leave overnight and the next day put to cook over two fires
