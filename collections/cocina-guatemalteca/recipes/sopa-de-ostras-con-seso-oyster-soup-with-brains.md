## Ingredients

- brains
- egg
- bread powder
- lard
- one tablespoon flour
- chopped parsley
- pepper powder
- milk
- one can of oysters
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cook the brains and when cold cut into small pieces
2. Coat in beaten egg and then in bread powder
3. Fry in hot lard and in that same lard fry one tablespoon of flour, chopped parsley and pepper powder
4. Add milk to make a thin sauce and add one can of oysters
5. Serve with the brains and slices of fried bread
