## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- two dried chiles, soaked and pitted
- roasted garlic clove
- six grains of Castilian pepper
- six cloves
- piece of bread, fried
- cinnamon
- water
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and brown in lard
2. When it browns, add two soaked and pitted dried chiles with a roasted garlic clove, six grains of Castilian pepper, six cloves, and a fried piece of bread with cinnamon, all well ground
3. Dissolve in a cup of water and add to the tongue
4. Let it simmer for two hours
5. When serving, remove all the spices; if desired, add whole peeled potatoes
