## Ingredients

- rice
- 1 pound pork meat
- lard
- fried seasoning
- pepper
- cinnamon
- clove
- salt
- vinegar
- sherry
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- capers
- chicken or squab
- eggs

## Directions

1. Make the day before some rice as for daily use, parboil one pound of pork meat, mince it very well and fry it with a little lard, sufficient fried seasoning, pepper powder, cinnamon, clove and salt; a little vinegar and a little sherry (if there is none it is not necessary)
2. Add raisins, well-peeled almonds, olives, capers and small pieces of chicken or whole squabs that will have been previously made in a stew
3. Line a pan with lard or butter, put one layer of rice and on top of this place all the filling that was explained, cover this filling with another layer of rice and cover this with three beaten eggs
