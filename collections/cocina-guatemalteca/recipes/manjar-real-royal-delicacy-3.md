## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one ounce of rice or rice flour, well-sifted
- two liters of milk
- one ounce of bread flour
- four ounces of ground and peeled almonds
- cinnamon powder
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar
2. When it reaches the proper stage, lower heat and grind one ounce of rice or use well-sifted rice flour
3. Place in two liters of milk with one ounce of bread flour
4. Mix well and place over fire, stirring so it does not stick
5. When it begins to thicken, add the sugar mixture with four ounces of ground and peeled almonds
6. Leave until it reaches the soft-ball stage (punto de ante)
7. Place in a platter and decorate with cinnamon powder and chopped almonds
