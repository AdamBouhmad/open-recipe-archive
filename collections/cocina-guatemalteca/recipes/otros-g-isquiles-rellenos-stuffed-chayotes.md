## Ingredients

- Güisquiles (chayotes), whole
- Mantequilla (butter), one tablespoon
- Tomato
- Onion, peeled and chopped
- Salt and sugar to taste
- Raisins
- Almonds, chopped
- Bread powder, sweet
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the güisquiles whole in steam.
2. When cooked, cut them in half lengthwise and remove all the flesh, leaving the shell very whole.
3. Put in a pan one tablespoon of butter, tomato and peeled and chopped onion.
4. Add the güisquil, well broken up, with salt and sugar to taste, raisins and chopped almonds.
5. Once well seasoned, fill the shells with this mixture, keeping them very even.
6. Cover with sweet bread powder and place in a hot oven for ten minutes.
