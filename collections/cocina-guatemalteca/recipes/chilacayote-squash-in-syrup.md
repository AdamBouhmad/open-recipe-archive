## Ingredients

- chilacayote
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified per pound of fruit
- white honey
- about twelve cloves

## Directions

1. Beat the chilacayote very well with a mallet
2. Peel it, remove all the fibrous parts, and chop in whatever form is desired
3. Blanch in very clean water
4. When blanched, remove and place in cold water
5. For one pound of fruit clarify one and a half pounds of sugar
6. Put the chilacayote in the sugar and when it begins to reach medium heat add one cup of white honey and about twelve cloves
7. Remove the cloves before serving
8. If desired to make it covered, do not add white honey or cloves; remove and when dry, coat like the figs
