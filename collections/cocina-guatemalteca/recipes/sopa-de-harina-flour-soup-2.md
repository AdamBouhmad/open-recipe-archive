## Ingredients

- milk
- flour
- egg yolks
- salt
- egg
- flour for coating
- lard
- broth
- peas
- potatoes
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- parsley

## Directions

1. In a glass of milk put two ounces of flour, two egg yolks, a few grains of salt and cook
2. As soon as it becomes a paste, remove it and let it cool
3. Then knead with an egg and form balls wrapped in flour
4. Fry them in very hot lard, place in the tureen and cover with a broth prepared in advance
5. The broth should have peas and chopped potatoes, fried seasoning, pepper powder, and parsley
6. Thicken the broth with a teaspoon of flour fried in butter
