## Ingredients

- pork tongues
- ground pineapple
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Fry the tongues and when they have browned, add ground pineapple with a cup of water
2. Add half a cup of vinegar, pepper, clove, cinnamon, salt, and a bay leaf
3. When cooked, remove all the spices
4. Serve either dry or in its juice
