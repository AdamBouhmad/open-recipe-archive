## Ingredients

- white bread
- small peppers
- cloves
- salt
- parsley
- lard
- butter
- oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Make white bread powder with small peppers and cloves.
2. Mix in salt and some chopped parsley.
3. Grease a pan with a little lard.
4. Place a layer of this bread powder, then a layer of cooked and sliced brains, another layer of bread, and another layer of brains, ensuring the last layer is bread.
5. Add a tablespoon of butter, one of oil, and a few drops of lemon.
6. Cook over two fires.
