## Ingredients

- eight egg whites
- egg yolks
- seven ounces powdered sugar
- half pound rice flour, well sifted
- lard

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg whites; when they are very stiff, mix in the yolks with seven ounces of powdered sugar and half a pound of well-sifted rice flour.
2. Mix well and put into molds well coated with lard and bake in the oven.
