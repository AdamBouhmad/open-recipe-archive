## Ingredients

- 7 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter
- 1 pound flour, sifted
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon Royal baking powder
- 7 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 7 egg yolks with 0.5 pound of sugar and 0.5 pound of butter
2. After half an hour of beating, add 1 pound of sifted flour
3. Beat for five minutes with 1 cup of milk
4. Add 1 tablespoon of Royal and the well-beaten egg whites
