## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons of butter
- 2 tablespoons of bread flour
- pepper powder
- nutmeg
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon (of milk, inferred from context)

## Directions

1. Put 2 tablespoons of butter and 2 of bread flour together
2. Stir until it becomes a cream
3. Add pepper powder, nutmeg, and a bay leaf
