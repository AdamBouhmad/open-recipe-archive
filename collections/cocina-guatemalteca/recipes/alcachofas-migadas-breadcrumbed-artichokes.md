## Ingredients

- artichokes
- garlic
- oil
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. After cooking them whole, lift the top part and remove the spines
2. Chop garlic, fry it in oil and add sufficient bread crumbs
3. Fill the artichokes with this, brush them with oil and leave them in a warm place
