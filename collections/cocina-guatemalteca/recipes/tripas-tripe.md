## Ingredients

- tripe (panza)
- salt
- pepitoria (ground seeds)
- sesame
- clove
- miltomate
- cinnamon
- cumin
- Castilian peppers
- beef liver, well cooked
- cold tortillas
- french bread or white bread
- achiote
- manteca
- onion

## Directions

1. After cleaning the tripe very well, put it to cook
2. After half an hour of boiling, remove the water and replace with an equal amount of fresh, clean water
3. Put it back on the fire and add salt
4. When well cooked, cut into large or small pieces
5. Grind together a little pepitoria, sesame, a toasted clove (if desired) or one cooked with miltomate, a little cinnamon, cumin, Castilian peppers, and a piece of well-cooked beef liver
6. Thicken with cold tortillas and a piece of french bread or white bread and a small piece of achiote
7. Dissolve this mixture in the water in which the tripe was cooked
8. Put a pan with sufficient manteca and fry some chopped onion
9. When it takes on a golden color, add this mixture and the pieces of tripe, letting it boil until a thick sauce results
10. Serve hot
