## Ingredients

- one deboned turkey
- five pounds of loin
- one or two beef tongues
- ham

## Directions

1. Debone the turkey
2. Cut five pounds of loin into long, very thin strips, removing the skins
3. Have one or two beef tongues (either salted or fresh), according to the size of the turkey
4. Cut the tongues into very thin strips
5. Stuff the turkey by layering: one layer of loin strips, one of devil ham, one of tongue, and so on until finished
6. Sew closed, wrap in cloth, cook and press as described before
