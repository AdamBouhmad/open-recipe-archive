## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of sugar
- one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
- eight egg yolks
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify one and one-half pounds of sugar to the soft-ball stage (punto de bolita)
2. Lower the heat and let cool
3. Add one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds and eight egg yolks
4. Return to fire and when it reaches the proper stage, lower the heat and beat until a paste forms
5. Place on a table, cut into desired shapes
6. Roll in cinnamon powder and serve
