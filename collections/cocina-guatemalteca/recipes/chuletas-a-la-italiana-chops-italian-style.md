## Ingredients

- backbone with meat
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Ensure the backbone remains well fleshed
2. Cut into round pieces
3. Flatten very well with a machete
4. Season with salt and lemon juice
5. Leave for a quarter hour
6. Then place on a grill, turning frequently with great care so they do not dry out
7. Serve juicy
