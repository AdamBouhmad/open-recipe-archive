## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- oil
- salt
- vinegar
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Beat four raw egg yolks, letting a large spoonful of oil fall drop by drop
2. When it begins to thicken, let it fall in a thin stream
3. When well thick, let sit for half an hour and then add salt, a tablespoon of very good vinegar, and if desired, a tablespoon of French mustard
