## Ingredients

- Shrimp, well peeled
- Salt
- Flour
- Pepper powder
- Oil
- Garlic
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped parsley
- Vinegar
- Chopped pickles
- One or two tablespoons of mustard

## Directions

1. After peeling the shrimp well, cook them with salt.
2. Let them boil for a quarter of an hour, then remove.
3. Coat them in flour and pepper powder.
4. Fry them in oil, and when they turn golden, remove them.
5. In the same oil, fry garlic, peeled tomato, and chopped parsley.
6. Add a little vinegar, chopped pickles, and one or two tablespoons of mustard.
7. Place the shrimp on a platter and cover with this sauce.
