## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- butter
- oil
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- parsley
- pimientos morrones (red peppers)
- pepper powder
- potato

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, season with salt.
2. Place on paper well spread with melted butter.
3. Fry in hot oil until golden.
4. Remove the fish.
5. In the same oil fry parboiled onion heads, peeled tomato, chopped garlic and parsley.
6. When half fried, add a jar of chopped red peppers and pepper powder, well seasoned with salt.
7. Cover the fish with this mixture.
8. When serving, sliced cooked potato can be added.
