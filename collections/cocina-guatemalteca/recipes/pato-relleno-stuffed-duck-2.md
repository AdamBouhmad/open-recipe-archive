## Ingredients

- Four ounces of prune
- Ten or twelve apples
- Two ounces of almond
- Sweet bread powder
- Pepper powder
- Salt
- Three raw egg yolks
- Duck, deboned if desired
- Butter
- Two onion heads
- Two bay leaves
- One sprig of thyme
- One cup of vinegar
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Cook four ounces of prune; peel ten or twelve apples, removing all seeds and chop them well
2. Peel and chop two ounces of almond, mix well with the pitted prune and add sweet bread powder, a little pepper powder, salt and three raw egg yolks
3. Mix everything well and fill the duck which should be deboned beforehand if desired, or fill it as is
4. Rub well with butter, salt, pepper powder, two onion heads, two bay leaves, one sprig of thyme, one cup of vinegar
5. Place in the oven; if desired cover with water, one glass of white wine, salt and some bay leaves
6. When cooked, brush with butter and place in the oven to brown
