## Ingredients

- 1 pound pitted plums
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 glass of water
- 1 pound flour
- 5 eggs
- a pinch of salt
- 0.5 pound butter

## Directions

1. Put 1 pound of pitted plums in 1 pound of sugar with 1 glass of water until it reaches the point of "ante" (soft ball stage)
2. Meanwhile, work 1 pound of flour with 5 eggs, a pinch of salt and 0.5 pound of butter
3. Knead for half an hour and let rest for a quarter hour
4. Then stretch with a rolling pin and cut the dough to the size of the plate in which the cake will be made
5. Place on the plate very well adjusted, put in the oven and as soon as it bakes without taking color, remove it and place the plum on top
6. Take out some thin strips very straight and make on the plate a weave like a petate pattern
7. Put in the oven, when it browns remove and serve
