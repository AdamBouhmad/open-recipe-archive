## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- four ounces of lard
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- twelve egg yolks
- egg whites
- optionally half a pound of tapioca flour or rice flour

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, lard, sugar, flour, and egg yolks together
2. When everything is well beaten, beat the egg whites as for nougat
3. Mix everything very well
4. Put in greased small casseroles and place in the oven
5. If desired, add half a pound of tapioca flour or rice flour
