## Ingredients

- venison
- salt
- cooked tomato
- guaque chile
- cumin
- garlic clove
- oregano
- achiote
- dried chile

## Directions

1. Clean the venison well and cook with salt
2. Grind some cooked tomato with a guaque chile, a little cumin, one garlic clove, a little oregano, a small piece of achiote, and a dried chile
3. Dissolve and fry this mixture
4. Add the well-cooked venison to cook in this sauce
