## Ingredients

- one leg, well cleaned and cooked with salt
- one tablespoon of butter
- onion
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- one tablespoon of capers
- ten or twelve pitted olives
- salt
- a little of the broth in which the leg cooked

## Directions

1. Well cleaned and cooked, the leg is cut into pieces, separating the bones
2. Put in a pan one tablespoon of butter; add onion and peeled, chopped tomato
3. Into this add the leg with one tablespoon of capers, ten or twelve pitted olives and add salt and a little of the broth in which the leg cooked
4. Let it boil about ten minutes and serve hot
