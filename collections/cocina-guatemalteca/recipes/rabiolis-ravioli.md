## Ingredients

- flour, one pound
- eggs, three
- salt
- brains, cooked and chopped
- spinach, cooked and chopped
- pepper powder
- butter
- beef or chicken pieces
- onion, chopped
- garlic clove, one
- tomatoes, about ten, peeled and chopped
- salt
- parmesan or Zacapa cheese powder

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of flour with three eggs and a little salt until well worked
2. Cut into small squares of desired size
3. Prepare a filling with cooked chopped brains, double the amount of cooked chopped spinach, a little pepper powder, fried in butter
4. Fill the dough squares, pinch the edges, and drop into boiling salted water
5. Boil for a quarter hour
6. Prepare a sauce by frying pieces of beef or chicken until golden without burning
7. Add a little chopped onion with a garlic clove and about ten peeled and chopped tomatoes
8. Place the ravioli in a platter, cover with sauce, and sprinkle with parmesan or Zacapa cheese powder
9. Serve as is or put in the oven for a quarter hour
