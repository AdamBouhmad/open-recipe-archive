## Ingredients

- turnip greens
- water
- salt
- bicarbonate of soda
- white turnip roots
- one tablespoon lard
- two peeled tomatoes
- one onion
- one garlic clove

## Directions

1. Chop all the green parts and place in a pot with boiling water, salt, and a little bicarbonate of soda
2. Peel the white roots and cook separately in salted water
3. Once everything is cooked, drain well
4. Heat one tablespoon of lard in a pan and chop two peeled tomatoes, one onion, and one garlic clove
5. Add the turnips to the pan, and once fried (after about five minutes), serve immediately so it does not discolor
