## Ingredients

- sixteen egg whites
- one pound of flour
- one pound of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Beat sixteen egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Have one pound of flour and one pound of ground sugar sifted
3. Once the egg whites are well beaten, add the sugar and the egg yolks and finally the flour, mixing without beating
4. Place in greased molds and put in a gentle oven
