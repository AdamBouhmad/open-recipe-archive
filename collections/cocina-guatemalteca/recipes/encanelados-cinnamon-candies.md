## Ingredients

- half pound of sugar
- four egg yolks
- half pound of well-ground almonds
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Put half pound of sugar on the fire to reach thin-thread stage.
2. Add four egg yolks and half pound of well-ground almonds, stirring constantly.
3. When it pulls away from the pan, place on a bed of sugar and cinnamon powder.
4. Grind and form into pieces.
