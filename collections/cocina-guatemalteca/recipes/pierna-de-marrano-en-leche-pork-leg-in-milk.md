## Ingredients

- a little butter
- one sprig of parsley
- one pork leg
- salt to taste
- one to one and a half liters of milk

## Directions

1. Put a little butter and one sprig of parsley in a pan over low heat.
2. Place the pork leg in it to brown, with salt to taste.
3. As soon as it is well browned, add one to one and a half liters of milk in the same pan.
4. Cook over low heat, basting with the same juices.
5. When cooked, remove from heat.
6. Serve hot or cold, with each slice accompanied by a spoonful of the juices in which the leg was cooked.
