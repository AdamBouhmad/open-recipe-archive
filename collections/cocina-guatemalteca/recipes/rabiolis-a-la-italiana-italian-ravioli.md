## Ingredients

- half pound bread flour
- two eggs
- one eighth of an ounce salt
- two tablespoons melted butter
- two cooked spinaches (well drained)
- cooked and chopped ham
- one ounce bone marrow
- one cooked brain (chopped)
- salt, pepper powder, cloves, and cinnamon to taste
- two tablespoons grated Flemish or Parmesan cheese
- flour (for dusting)
- two tablespoons lard
- one cup very good broth
- two tablespoons powder of toasted bread (well ground)
- six Castilian peppercorns
- two cloves
- one small piece of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful butter

## Directions

1. Knead all the flour, eggs, salt, and melted butter together
2. Roll out with a rolling pin
3. Grease the work surface with lard; repeat this operation two or three times
4. Cut out small circles of desired size
5. Prepare filling: cook and drain spinaches, chop them
6. Mix with chopped cooked ham, one ounce of bone marrow, and one chopped cooked brain
7. Add salt, pepper powder, cloves, and cinnamon
8. Mix in two tablespoons of grated Flemish or Parmesan cheese
9. Fill the circles with this mixture
10. Seal in the shape of pastries
11. Dust lightly with flour and cover with a clean cloth; let sit overnight
12. The next day, place in a frying pan with two tablespoons of lard on very low heat
13. Cover and turn carefully
14. When cooked, remove and let drain
15. In another frying pan, put one cup of very good broth with two tablespoons of powder of toasted and well-ground bread, six Castilian peppercorns, two cloves, and one small piece of cinnamon
16. Add one teaspoonful of butter
17. Place the pastries in this broth to give one gentle boil
