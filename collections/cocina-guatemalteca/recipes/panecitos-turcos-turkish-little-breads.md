## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one tablespoon of Royal
- four eggs
- one small cup of sugar
- half a cup of milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs as for nougat in two cups of flour with one tablespoon of Royal
2. Beat the yolks with one small cup of sugar
3. When everything is well beaten mix with the flour and half a cup of milk
4. Grease some papers very well with butter and put them on tin plates
5. Put two tablespoons of the batter on these and put in a hot oven
6. When cool, put cream or whatever you wish on top and stack them one on top of another to whatever height you wish
