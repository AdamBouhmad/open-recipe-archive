## Ingredients

- rice
- Parmesan cheese powder
- butter
- onion
- flour
- broth or milk
- ham pieces
- mushrooms
- truffles
- chicken livers
- roasted meat
- chicken
- sherry wine
- 2 egg yolks
- lemon juice
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare rice as for everyday rice, but before it is done add Parmesan cheese powder, taking care that it remains white
2. Prepare a hollow mold, grease it and put the rice in it and put in the oven
3. Prepare a béchamel as follows: put a little butter, a little onion, flour, broth or milk; when it has boiled add pieces of ham, mushrooms, truffles, chicken livers and roasted meat
4. Chop the chicken as everything else and add to the sauce with a little sherry wine and two egg yolks with lemon juice
5. Remove the rice from the mold and in the interior put the béchamel and on the edge fine tomato sauce
