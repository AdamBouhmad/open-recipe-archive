## Ingredients

- camote
- water with lime lye
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified

## Directions

1. Peel the camote, better if yellow colored
2. Soak for a quarter hour in water with one teaspoon of lime lye
3. Have already clarified one and a half pounds of sugar
4. Put the camote in the syrup to heat through
