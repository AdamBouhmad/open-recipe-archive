## Ingredients

- Thirty egg yolks
- One bottle of high-point syrup made with two and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Beat thirty egg yolks for two hours
2. When well thickened, add one bottle of high-point syrup made with two and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar
