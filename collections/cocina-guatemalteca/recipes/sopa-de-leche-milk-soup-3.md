## Ingredients

- milk
- flour
- butter
- nutmeg
- eggs

## Directions

1. In a glass of milk, dissolve two and a half ounces of flour, two ounces of butter, and a little nutmeg
2. Cook until it forms a ball and let it cool
3. When cold, mix in three egg whites and three egg yolks
4. When well mixed, form little balls and wrap in flour
5. Drop them into broth prepared in advance
