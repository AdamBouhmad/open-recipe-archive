## Ingredients

- Two ounces of cinnamon
- A quarter ounce of palo de la vida
- One liter of water
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. The day before, bring two ounces of cinnamon with a quarter ounce of palo de la vida to a boil in one liter of water
2. The following day, strain it with twelve ounces of sugar
3. Put in the ice cream maker and set like the others
