## Ingredients

- rice
- oil
- fried seasoning of peeled tomato, onion, garlic and roasted peppers
- one young fat chicken
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Pass the rice through a colander so it comes out very whole
2. Weigh one pound of rice and wash lightly, better if washed the day before
3. If local rice, wash it; if of the bomba variety only clean it and fry in oil
4. When fried add sufficient fried seasoning made from peeled tomato, onion, one garlic clove and a small can of finely chopped roasted peppers
5. Have pre-simmered one young fat chicken beforehand
6. Add the chicken pieces and the broth in which it was cooked
