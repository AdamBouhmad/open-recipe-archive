## Ingredients

- mixed vegetables
- potato
- noodles
- pasta
- bread
- lard
- butter
- rice
- cheese
- hard-boiled eggs
- meat broth
- saffron water

## Directions

1. Prepare all kinds of well-minced and cleaned vegetables, potato sliced, parboil noodles and pasta, fry bread slices in lard, have some common rice cooked in lard, line a pan with a little lard or butter
2. Place one layer of rice, one of vegetables, one of fried bread, one of noodles, one of fresh cheese slices, one of hard-boiled egg slices, begin with another layer of rice and so on as before, making sure the last layer is bread slices, fresh cheese and hard-boiled egg slices
3. Add meat broth and a little saffron water just enough to cover the layers, letting it boil for a quarter hour
