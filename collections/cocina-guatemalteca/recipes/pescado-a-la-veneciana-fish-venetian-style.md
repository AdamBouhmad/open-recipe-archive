## Ingredients

- fish, clean
- salt
- white bread powder
- onion, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- pepper powder
- clove
- thyme powder
- butter
- white paper
- half cup vinegar
- half cup oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning, cut the fish into pieces.
2. Add salt and coat with white bread powder.
3. Chop plenty of onion and parsley and add pepper powder, clove, and thyme powder.
4. Place a cazuela with white paper well coated with butter.
5. Alternately layer fish and the chopped mixture, ensuring the last layer is fish.
6. Finally, add half a cup of vinegar and half a cup of oil.
