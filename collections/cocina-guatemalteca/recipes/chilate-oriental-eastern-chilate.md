## Ingredients

- twenty cacao beans
- twenty pataxtes
- one sapuyulo
- one quarter ounce of anise
- one quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper
- about ten chili strings
- a small piece of ginger
- half a pound of chan
- about ten cold tamales
- water
- four cinnamon chocolates
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Toast twenty cacao beans, twenty pataxtes, one sapuyulo, one quarter ounce of anise, one quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper, about ten chili strings, one small piece of ginger, half a pound of chan, all well toasted without burning
2. Grind about ten cold tamales
3. Dissolve everything with a little cold water
4. Add four beaten cinnamon chocolates and sugar to taste
5. Serve cold as a refreshment
