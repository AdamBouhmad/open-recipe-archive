## Ingredients

- green chile
- tomato, peeled
- parsley, chopped
- four to six egg yolks, well cooked
- clove
- pepper
- capers
- olives
- cooked vegetables: chayote, potatoes, carrots, green beans, peas, and fava beans
- one teaspoon vinegar
- oil
- fish

## Directions

1. Fry a green chile, peeled tomato, and chopped parsley.
2. Dissolve four to six well-cooked egg yolks with clove and pepper, capers, olives, cooked and chopped vegetables such as chayote, potatoes, carrots, green beans, tender peas, and fava beans.
3. Mix everything well with one teaspoon of vinegar.
4. Fill the fish with this mixture.
5. Place on an oiled paper on a baking sheet in the oven to brown.
6. Serve with salad or sauce of choice.
