## Ingredients

- salmon
- butter
- salt
- pepper powder
- onion
- parsley, chopped
- nutmeg
- capers
- cooked and ground tomato
- lemon juice to taste

## Directions

1. Place the salmon in a serving dish with a little butter, salt, pepper powder, onion, chopped parsley, a little nutmeg, some capers and cooked ground tomato
2. Mix everything well and place it on the salmon with lemon juice to taste
