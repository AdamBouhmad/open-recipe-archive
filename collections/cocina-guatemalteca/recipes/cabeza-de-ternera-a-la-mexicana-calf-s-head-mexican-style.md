## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salt
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- vinegar
- garlic head, peeled
- dried chiles (four to six), cooked
- potato salad
- lettuce

## Directions

1. After peeling and cleaning well, split the head in half to remove the brains
2. Rub with salt, pepper powder, oregano powder, and vinegar
3. Grind a whole head of garlic with four or six cooked dried chiles
4. Dissolve this with a little more vinegar and rub inside and outside the head
5. Leave overnight in this brine
6. Early the next morning put it in the oven, carefully basting it with its juices every moment
7. After three hours in the oven, remove and serve with a salad of potatoes and lettuce
