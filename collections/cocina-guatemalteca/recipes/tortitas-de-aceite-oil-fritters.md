## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- half a pound of sugar
- 1 small glass of white wine
- 1 small glass of very fine oil
- a little anise
- 1 small teaspoon of salt
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with half a pound of sugar until thick
2. Add one small glass of white wine
3. Add one small glass of very fine oil
4. Add a little anise
5. Add one small teaspoon of salt
6. Add one tablespoon of butter
7. Add flour, ensuring the dough is not too stiff
8. Roll out thin with a rolling pin
9. Cut out figures as desired
10. Place on a tin and bake in the oven
11. When cooked, coat with half-thread stage syrup
