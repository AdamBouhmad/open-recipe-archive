## Ingredients

- lomo, posta negra o badilla (loin, black brisket or shoulder cut)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- miltomate crudo (raw tomatillo)
- tomates (tomatoes)
- orégano (oregano)
- comino (cumin)
- pimientas de Castilla (Castilian peppers)
- ajo (garlic)
- chile de pasa (dried chile)
- achiote (annatto)
- papas (potatoes)
- lechuga verde (green lettuce)

## Directions

1. Place a piece of loin, black brisket or shoulder cut with water, salt and a little vinegar
2. Grind some raw tomatillo, two tomatoes, a little oregano, cumin, ten Castilian peppers, one garlic clove and one dried chile
3. If using dried chile, grind a small piece of annatto with it
4. Dissolve this in one liter of water and add to the meat
5. When most of the water has been consumed, add some potatoes
6. Cook on very gentle heat
7. Serve with green lettuce leaves
