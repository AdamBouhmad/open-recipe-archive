## Ingredients

- Green fava beans
- Salt
- Water
- Ham slices
- Chorizo slices
- Pork loin slices
- One tablespoon oil
- Peeled tomato
- Onion
- Green chile, chopped
- French bread
- Ten capers

## Directions

1. Peel the green fava beans, split them in half and cook in salted water, making sure the water is boiling when they are added
2. When cooked, remove them and in that same water cook ham, chorizo, and pork loin, all sliced
3. In one tablespoon of oil, fry peeled tomato, onion and chopped green chile
4. Grind a piece of fried French bread with about ten capers, dissolve with a little water and add to the fried preparation
5. Add the fava beans and all the meats that have been cooked
6. Serve hot
