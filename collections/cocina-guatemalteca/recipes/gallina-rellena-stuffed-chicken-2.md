## Ingredients

- one chicken
- ham
- meat
- onion
- mint leaves
- cilantro leaves
- two hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- saffron
- salt
- lemon or sour orange juice
- two raw eggs
- butter
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken well, debone it leaving all the meat
2. Chop a little ham and fry it with pieces of meat, onion cut lengthwise, about two mint leaves, some cilantro leaves, two chopped hard-boiled eggs, a little pepper ground with saffron
3. Remove from heat, season with salt, a little lemon or sour orange juice and add two raw eggs
4. Fill the chicken with this mixture, rub it with salt, butter, two bay leaves and chopped onion and place in the oven, taking care to baste it so it comes out juicy
