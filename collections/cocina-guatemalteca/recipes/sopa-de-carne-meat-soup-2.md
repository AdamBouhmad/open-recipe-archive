## Ingredients

- One pound of very tender meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two roasted tomatoes
- One roasted onion
- Flour
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Prepare one pound of very tender meat by half-roasting it
2. Grind it with salt, pepper, two roasted tomatoes and one roasted onion
3. Let this well-ground mixture fall into a well-prepared broth
4. Add one teaspoon of flour well-browned and when it has boiled two or three times serve with toasted bread
