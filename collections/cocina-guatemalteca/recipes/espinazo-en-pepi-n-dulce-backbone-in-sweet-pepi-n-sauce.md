## Ingredients

- half ounce pepitoria seeds
- half ounce sesame seeds
- some sweet bread
- two guaque chiles or dried chiles
- some tomatillo
- a large piece of cinnamon
- twelve cloves
- equal quantity of Castilian pepper
- a small piece of very burned banana peel
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Toast half an ounce of pepitoria, half ounce of sesame seeds, some sweet bread, two guaque or dried chiles and some tomatillo
2. Grind all with a large piece of cinnamon, twelve cloves, equal amount of Castilian pepper, a small piece of very burned banana peel
3. Thin with the same broth
4. Fry and add the backbone, seasoning with salt and sugar to taste
