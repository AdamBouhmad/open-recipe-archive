## Ingredients

- fish
- bread powder
- lard
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- pickles in mustard
- fried bread
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and coat in bread powder.
2. Wrap in paper well spread with lard and fry in very hot oil.
3. When golden, remove the paper and place the fish on a platter.
4. In the same oil, fry peeled tomato, onion and finely chopped garlic.
5. Chop some pickles in mustard, adding a little of the same sauce with a piece of fried bread, ground and dissolved in a little vinegar and water.
6. Cover the fish with this mixture.
7. Serve hot or cold.
