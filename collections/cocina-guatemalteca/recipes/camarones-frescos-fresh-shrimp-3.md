## Ingredients

- fresh shrimp, cleaned
- two glasses white wine
- salt
- parsley, minced
- onion, minced
- garlic, minced
- two cloves
- half glass oil
- two bay leaves
- pepper powder
- two or three slices peeled and seeded lemon

## Directions

1. Place cleaned shrimp in a dish with white wine, salt, minced parsley, onion and garlic
2. Add cloves, oil, bay leaves, pepper powder and lemon slices
3. Let boil and when reduced, remove the bay leaves and lemon
4. Serve juicy
