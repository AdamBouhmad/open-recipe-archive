## Ingredients

- feet
- salt
- miltomate
- tomato, cooked
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- garlic clove
- achiote
- guaque chile
- piece of French bread, fried
- lard
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook the feet with salt.
2. When well cooked, chop them, removing all the small bones.
3. Grind miltomate and cooked tomato, cumin, Castilian pepper, a garlic clove, a little achiote, a guaque chile and a piece of fried French bread.
4. Fry in a little lard, thinning it with the same broth.
5. Add the feet to this mixture.
6. Serve hot.
