## Ingredients

- One hen or chicken, not too cooked nor too tough
- Peas
- Carrot pieces
- Potato pieces
- Butter, oil or lard (sufficient)
- Chopped tomato
- Onion in very thin slices
- One can of diced pimientos morrones with all its juice
- Broth in which the hen or chicken was cooked
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a hen or chicken that is not too cooked nor too tough.
2. Parboil peas, carrot pieces, and potato pieces.
3. In sufficient butter, oil or lard, fry the chopped tomato, onion in very thin slices, and one can of diced pimientos morrones with all its juice; fry it very well.
4. Add the broth in which the hen or chicken was cooked and the quarters of it.
5. Let it simmer for half an hour well covered and add the vegetables with the chicken and serve hot.
