## Ingredients

- half a pound of lard or butter
- six ounces of sugar
- nine eggs
- one pound of flour
- one small teaspoon of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Work the lard or butter for half an hour with the sugar
2. Beat the egg yolks with the butter and one pound of flour
3. Beat the egg whites separately until very stiff, like for nougat
4. When the whites are well set, mix everything together very well
5. Add one small teaspoon of Royal
6. Put in greased small casseroles and place in the oven
