## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 6 glasses milk
- 6 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 1 pineapple, well ground

## Directions

1. Place sugar in a vessel with six glasses of milk and peeled and ground almonds
2. When it begins to thicken, add the well-ground pineapple
3. Let it reach proper consistency
