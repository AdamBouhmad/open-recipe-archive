## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Turnips
- Carrots
- Cabbage
- Peas
- Butter
- Bay leaves
- Chiapas peppers
- Cloves
- Thyme branch
- Chicken or plump fowl
- Good bone (six pounds if for over twenty people)
- Sheep head
- Garbanzo beans
- Leeks
- Celery branch (if desired)
- Flour
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Mince very finely: green beans, turnips, carrots and cabbage, adding peas
2. Lightly fry everything in butter with two bay leaves, two Chiapas peppers, crushed cloves and a thyme branch
3. Put all this to cook with a broth prepared as follows: cook a plump hen or chicken
4. If the soup is for more than twenty people, add six pounds of very good bone and a very clean sheep head
5. Add a handful of garbanzo beans, a bundle of leeks and if desired a celery branch
6. This broth should boil for six hours and into it drop everything above
7. Thicken the soup with a little flour fried in butter with chopped parsley
8. Serve at the third boil
