## Ingredients

- half a liter of milk
- one pound of dry sugar
- one small glass of white honey

## Directions

1. In half a liter of milk put one pound of dry sugar
2. When it is already reaching the thread stage, add one small glass of white honey
3. Let it boil until it reaches the caramel stage
4. Grease a wooden board with a little lard, pour the caramel into it and when cold cut it into the size and shape desired
