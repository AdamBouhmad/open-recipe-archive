## Ingredients

- green guaque chile
- garlic
- roasted tomatoes
- oregano
- pepper
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the green guaque chile; once cooked, grind with a garlic clove, three roasted tomatoes, a bit of oregano, a few peppercorns
2. Dissolve this with a bit of vinegar, water and salt and add some capers
