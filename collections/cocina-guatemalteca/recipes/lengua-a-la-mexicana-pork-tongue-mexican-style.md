## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- two dried chiles, cooked and pitted
- three roasted tomatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the tongue as in the previous recipe
2. Grind two cooked dried chiles without seeds with three roasted tomatoes
3. Add to the tongue with a cup of water
4. Let it cook for two hours
