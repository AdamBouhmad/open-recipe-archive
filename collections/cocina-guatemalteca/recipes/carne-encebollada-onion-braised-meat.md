## Ingredients

- meat, in pieces
- onions, peeled, three
- tomatoes, three
- bay leaves, two
- pepper
- cinnamon, a small piece
- vinegar, half cup
- oil, half cup

## Directions

1. Place meat pieces in a pot
2. Add three peeled onions, three tomatoes, two bay leaves, pepper, a small piece of cinnamon, half cup vinegar, and half cup oil
3. Let it cook and serve with whatever is desired
