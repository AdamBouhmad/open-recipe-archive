## Ingredients

- beef broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper powder
- lemon peel
- bay leaf
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a glass of broth, half a glass of white wine, salt, pepper powder, a piece of lemon peel, a bay leaf, half a cup of vinegar
2. Let boil for four to six hours and strain
3. Serves for all kinds of meats
