## Ingredients

- beans
- some large pork rinds, preferably pine cone shaped and ears
- one tablespoon of flour
- water
- one branch of epazote
- salt
- alternatively, salted pig's feet prepared two or three days in advance

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. When half-cooked, add some large pork rinds, preferably pine cone shaped ones and ears
3. When everything is cooked, add one tablespoon of flour dissolved in a little water
4. Add one branch of epazote washed with boiling water
5. Season well with salt
6. Serve hot
7. Alternatively, pig's feet that have been salted two or three days in advance can be used instead of pork rinds
