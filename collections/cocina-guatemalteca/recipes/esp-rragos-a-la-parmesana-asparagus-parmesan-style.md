## Ingredients

- butter
- asparagus
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Put a dish coated with butter, place a layer of asparagus and a layer of grated Parmesan cheese
2. Continue putting layers of both things, making sure the last one is cheese
3. Cook in the oven or over two fires
