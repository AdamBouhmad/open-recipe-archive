## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one small glass of oil
- two cooked egg yolks, ground
- two raw egg yolks
- a little pepper powder
- salt to taste
- a teaspoon of vinegar
- a little tender lettuce, finely chopped
- salmon
- lobster or cooked chicken, chopped

## Directions

1. Beat together half a pound of butter with one small glass of oil, two cooked and ground egg yolks, two raw egg yolks, a little pepper powder and salt to taste.
2. When well beaten, add a teaspoon of vinegar drop by drop.
3. Add a little tender lettuce, finely chopped.
4. Place a layer of salmon on a platter, then a layer of mayonnaise, then a layer of lobster or cooked chicken, chopped.
5. Alternate the layers, making sure the last layer is mayonnaise.
6. Serve decorated with tender lettuce.
