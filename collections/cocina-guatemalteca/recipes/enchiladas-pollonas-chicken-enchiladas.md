## Ingredients

- tortillas
- lard
- four pasilla chiles
- four mulato chiles
- four ancho chiles
- three roasted onions
- two ounces sesame seeds
- toasted French bread
- four roasted tomatoes
- ten capers
- chile in vinegar
- two cloves garlic
- one pound pork meat
- salt
- olives
- lettuce
- radishes

## Directions

1. Fry the tortillas in sufficient lard.
2. Grind four pasilla chiles, four mulato chiles, four ancho chiles, three roasted onions, two ounces of sesame seeds, toasted French bread, four roasted tomatoes, ten capers, four pieces of chile in vinegar and two cloves of garlic, along with toasted sesame seeds.
3. Cook one pound of pork meat with salt, then chop it finely.
4. Once the meat is fried, place it on the tortillas and cover with the sauce.
5. Top with chopped olives and garnish with fresh lettuce and radishes.
