## Ingredients

- meat
- salt
- saltpeter
- fat pieces
- peppers
- thick intestines

## Directions

1. Salt the meat with salt and saltpeter and grind very finely
2. Add fat pieces and peppers
3. Fill thick intestines with the mixture, pricking them with a pin
4. Cook the sausages
5. Hang them to smoke
