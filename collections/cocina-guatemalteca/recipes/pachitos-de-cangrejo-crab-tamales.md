## Ingredients

- corn (ground)
- salt
- twelve ounces lard
- small crab legs
- one cup cooked miltomate
- a little oregano
- a little cumin
- a piece of achiote
- dried chile cooked to taste
- chayote (chopped)
- carrot (chopped)
- green beans (chopped)
- potatoes (chopped)
- one cup water
- one crab (well washed)
- two large crab claws

## Directions

1. Grind the corn as with the others
2. Add salt and twelve ounces of lard
3. Remove the thin tips from the small crab legs and grind the rest with one cup of cooked miltomate, a little oregano, a little cumin, a piece of achiote, and dried chile cooked to taste
4. Chop chayote, carrot, green beans, and potatoes, mix everything well together
5. Add one cup of water
6. Place one spoonful of the mixture on a leaf with a well-washed crab and the two large claws
7. Wrap and cook the same as the others
