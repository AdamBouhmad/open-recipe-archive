## Ingredients

- meat
- onion stalks
- handful of tomatillos
- two cooked chiles
- handful of soaked rice
- Castilian peppercorns
- cumin grains
- small piece of achiote
- potato pieces
- güisquil

## Directions

1. Put the meat to cook in sufficient water with onion stalks cut into pieces
2. When the meat is cooked, add a sauce made as follows: grind a handful of tomatillos with two cooked chiles, a handful of soaked rice, some Castilian peppercorns, some cumin grains and a small piece of achiote
3. Let it boil until the rice is calculated to be cooked
4. When desired, add small pieces of potato and güisquil
