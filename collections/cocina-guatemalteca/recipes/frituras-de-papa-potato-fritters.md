## Ingredients

- one pound of potatoes
- salt
- two and one half ounces of flour
- equal quantity of powdered sugar
- drops of lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of potatoes with salt
2. When cooked and cold, grind them and add two and one half ounces of flour, equal quantity of powdered sugar, a few drops of lemon or orange juice
3. Knead everything very well for ten minutes, roll out with a rolling pin, cut with small molds and fry in hot lard
