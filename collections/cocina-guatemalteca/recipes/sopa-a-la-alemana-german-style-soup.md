## Ingredients

- four ounces of flour
- two eggs
- one cup of milk
- a few grains of salt
- one tablespoon of butter
- chicken
- chorizo
- pitted olives
- green chile
- pre-cooked noodles
- broth
- minced parsley
- fried seasoning paste
- pepper powder
- one tablespoon of flour fried in butter

## Directions

1. Take four ounces of flour and mix it with two eggs, one cup of milk, a few grains of salt, and one tablespoon of butter
2. Knead it very well
3. Using a mold or the rim of a glass, cut out small rounds and fill them with chicken, chorizo, chopped olives, and green chile
4. Seal the edges and fry them in very hot butter
5. Place a layer of these tartlets in the soup tureen and a layer of noodles that have been pre-cooked
6. Cover with broth that has been seasoned with minced parsley, fried seasoning paste, pepper powder, and one tablespoon of flour fried in butter
