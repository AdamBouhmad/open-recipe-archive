## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites

## Directions

1. Work together very well 0.5 pound of sugar with 0.5 pound of butter and 12 egg yolks
2. After half an hour of beating, add the 12 well-beaten egg whites
3. Place in a mold greased with butter and bake in the oven
