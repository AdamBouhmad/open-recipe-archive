## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 8 egg yolks
- half a pound of ground and peeled almonds
- clarified sugar at half-thread stage
- 1 glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Beat the four egg whites and eight yolks separately
2. When both are well beaten, combine them
3. Add half a pound of ground and peeled almonds
4. Pour into a buttered mold
5. Place in the oven or in a bain-marie
6. When cooked, remove and place in a clean mold
7. Have the clarified sugar at half-thread stage with a glass of sherry
8. Coat the cake with this mixture and return to the oven
