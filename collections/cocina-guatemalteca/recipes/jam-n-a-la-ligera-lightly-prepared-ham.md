## Ingredients

- one pig's leg
- salt
- saltpeter
- pepper powder
- juice of six sweet oranges
- six bay leaves
- one small bunch of thyme
- one bunch of leeks (if available)
- tomato
- one dried chile, seeded

## Directions

1. Take one pig's leg and for two days rub it thoroughly with salt and saltpeter.
2. On the third day, put it to cook with sufficient water, pepper powder, the juice of six sweet oranges, six bay leaves, one small bunch of thyme and, if available, one bunch of leeks.
3. After it is well cooked, if desired, place it in the oven.
4. Prepare a sauce by cooking or roasting sufficient tomato, grind it very well with one seeded dried chile, and after grinding well, fry it in oil or lard.
5. Serve the leg with this sauce.
