## Ingredients

- tongue
- water
- vinegar
- onions
- tomatoes
- thyme
- bay leaves
- Castilian pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pickles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue as in the previous recipes.
2. Place it in a dish with water, one cup of vinegar, four or five onions cut in cross, four peeled and halved tomatoes, a sprig of thyme, two bay leaves, eight or ten Castilian peppers, the same amount of cloves, a small piece of cinnamon and some pickles in vinegar.
3. Let it boil for five or six hours.
4. Remove all spices, let it brown and serve with a salad of your choice.
