## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- tomatillo
- guaque chile
- cumin
- Castilian peppercorns
- saffron
- cinnamon
- achiote
- French bread

## Directions

1. Cook the liver with salt
2. Grind some tomatillo with a guaque chile, a little cumin, six Castilian peppercorns, a little saffron, a small piece of cinnamon, a small piece of achiote, a piece of cooked liver and a piece of soaked French bread
3. Dissolve everything with a little of the broth in which the liver cooked
4. Fry everything and add the liver chopped into small pieces
5. Serve hot
