## Ingredients

- yellow corn ears (same quantity as previous recipe)
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Shuck the same quantity of corn ears
2. Dissolve them with an equal quantity of water and strain only with water
3. Cook as the previous recipe, calculating the water in small quantities so it does not come out thin
