## Ingredients

- fish, clean
- oil
- salt
- fine white bread powder
- melted butter
- paper
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- one teaspoon vinegar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, dry it thoroughly so no water remains.
2. Place a frying pan with oil.
3. Rub the fish with salt and coat with fine white bread powder.
4. Wrap in paper well coated with melted butter and place in hot oil.
5. When golden, remove it.
6. In the same oil, fry garlic, onion, parsley, peeled chopped tomato, and one teaspoon of vinegar, or if preferred, just a little lemon juice.
