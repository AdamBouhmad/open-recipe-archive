## Ingredients

- rice or salpor
- water
- saffron
- sugar
- salt
- 2 ounces of pepitoria
- 2 ounces of sesame
- 1 cup of miltomate
- 2 dried chiles
- lard
- 4 French beans
- Castilian pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Cook and grind rice or salpor as in the previous version
2. Simmer the dough with water, saffron, sugar and salt in the same quantities as before
3. For the sauce, toast 2 ounces of pepitoria, 2 ounces of sesame, 1 cup of miltomate, 2 dried chiles browned in lard, 4 well-fried French beans
4. Grind with half an eighth of Castilian pepper and half an eighth of clove
