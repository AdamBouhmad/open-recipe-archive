## Ingredients

- Butter
- Bread powder
- Cheese powder
- Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Nutmeg
- Butter

## Directions

1. Grease a pan with butter.
2. Place in it a layer of bread powder and cheese powder (half of each).
3. Break the eggs on top.
4. Add salt, pepper powder and nutmeg.
5. Add cheese powder and a tablespoon of butter.
6. Place in the oven or between two fires.
