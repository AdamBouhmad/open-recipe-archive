## Ingredients

- apples (12 to 15 pounds)
- water (20 liters)
- panela (sugar cakes) (3 pieces, added after 5 days)

## Directions

1. Crush 12 to 15 pounds of apples very well.
2. Place in a barrel or large covered jug with 20 liters of water.
3. After 5 days, add 3 pieces of panela and cover again.
4. After 10 days, stir gently with a clean paddle without touching the apples, as they will be ruined if touched.
5. After 30 days, strain, bottle, and it is ready to use.
