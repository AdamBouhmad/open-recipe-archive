## Ingredients

- meat
- oil
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- vinegar, a little
- salt
- pepper powder
- clove
- cinnamon
- eggs, two
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Make meat slices and coat with oil
2. Place in a dish with onion, garlic, parsley, a little vinegar, salt, pepper powder, clove, and cinnamon
3. Let infuse for four hours
4. Beat two eggs (whites and yolks) and add salt
5. Coat the meat in the beaten egg
6. Wrap in bread powder and fry in hot lard
