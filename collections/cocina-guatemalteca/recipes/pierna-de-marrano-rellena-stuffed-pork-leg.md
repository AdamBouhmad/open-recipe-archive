## Ingredients

- 1 pork leg
- mustard from pickles
- pickled vegetables
- pimientos morrones (roasted red peppers)
- ham, parboiled and sliced
- chorizo slices
- salami slices
- tender peas, cooked
- mixed cooked vegetables
- salt
- 1 ounce butter
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- 2 onion heads
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 cup dry sherry

## Directions

1. Cut the leg where it is thickest and make 2 or 3 layers without breaking them
2. Coat each layer with mustard from pickles
3. Slice the pickled vegetables and pimientos morrones lengthwise
4. Place a layer of these with slices of parboiled ham
5. Cover the next layer with the same mustard
6. Add a layer of chorizo slices, salami slices and cooked tender peas
7. Cover the last layer with mustard, pickles lengthwise and all kinds of cooked vegetables
8. Sew each layer together with thick thread
9. Place in a pan, rub well with salt, coat with 1 ounce of butter, pepper powder, plenty of oregano powder, 2 onion heads, 2 garlic cloves, 1 cup of vinegar and 1 cup of dry sherry
10. Put it in the oven for two hours, basting it with its juices constantly
