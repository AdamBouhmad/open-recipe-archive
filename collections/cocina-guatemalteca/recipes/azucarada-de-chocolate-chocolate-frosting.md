## Ingredients

- half a teaspoon of butter
- 3 tablespoons of milk
- 2 ounces of dark chocolate
- 2 cups of powdered sugar
- half a teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the milk, butter, and chocolate in a water bath
2. When hot, add the sugar without stopping beating
3. Add the vanilla and continue beating until it reaches a cream consistency
4. If too thick, add a little milk so it can be spread on the cake
