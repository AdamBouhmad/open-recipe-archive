## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two ounces of sugar
- four ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar for a quarter hour
2. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form
3. Mix everything together
4. Add the flour, well sifted, being careful to only mix it
5. Immediately make the chiqueadores
