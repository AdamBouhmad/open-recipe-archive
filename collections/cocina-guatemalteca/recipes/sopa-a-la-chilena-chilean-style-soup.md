## Ingredients

- 1 fat hen
- 3 cloves spice
- 3 bay leaves
- onions
- butter
- potatoes
- petit pois
- various vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean a fat hen and cook it in very low heat with three cloves spice, three bay leaves and onions well browned in butter
2. When the hen is tender, remove it and add the broth and sufficient potatoes until they fall apart
3. Add a little petit pois and various vegetables in very small pieces
4. Debone the hen and make meatballs from the meat as follows: finely chop an onion head, add a little powdered pepper, two eggs and a little flour or white bread powder, mix very well and make small meatballs
5. Drop them into the broth; as soon as it boils, remove immediately and serve very hot
6. If desired, add a glass of dry sherry
