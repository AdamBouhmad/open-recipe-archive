## Ingredients

- Loin or very tender brisket
- Ground garlic
- Roasted tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- A small piece of chile
- Water

## Directions

1. Make slices of loin or very tender brisket
2. Fry with ground garlic
3. Grind roasted tomatoes with clove, pepper, and a small piece of chile
4. Add to the meat with the necessary water to cook it over two fires
