## Ingredients

- one duck
- salt
- one tablespoon lard or butter
- two garlic cloves
- two onions
- a little parsley
- three roasted tomatoes
- about ten Castilian peppercorns
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- a piece of bread fried in lard
- broth from cooking the ducks
- slices of cooked ham
- one tablespoon oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning the duck well, cook it with salt
2. Once cooked, remove it and place it in a pan over heat with a tablespoon of lard or butter
3. Chop two garlic cloves, two onions, and a little parsley and add to the lard
4. Grind three roasted tomatoes, about ten Castilian peppercorns, three hard-boiled egg yolks, and a piece of bread fried in lard
5. Mix everything in the same broth in which the ducks were cooked
6. Pour into the pan with slices of cooked ham and one tablespoon of oil
7. Add the whole duck or as many as desired to cook in this sauce
