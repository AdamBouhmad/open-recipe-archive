## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour
- salt
- Royal baking powder
- lard
- goose liver or fried spinach or salmon

## Directions

1. Beat eggs according to the number of people, add one tablespoon of flour, salt to taste, and half a teaspoon of Royal baking powder.
2. Fry in hot lard and fill with goose liver or spinach fried in butter or salmon.
3. Serve hot.
