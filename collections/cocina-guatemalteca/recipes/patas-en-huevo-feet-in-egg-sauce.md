## Ingredients

- feet
- cloth
- flour
- beaten egg
- lard
- tomato
- dry or fresh chile
- onion heads
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the feet well.
2. Dry them with a cloth.
3. Coat in flour and then in beaten egg.
4. Fry in hot lard.
5. Cook two tomatoes, a dry or fresh chile, two heads of onion and salt.
6. When well cooked, mash it.
7. Baste the feet with this sauce.
8. Serve hot.
