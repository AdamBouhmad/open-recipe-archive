## Ingredients

- carne suave o lomo chiquito (tender meat or small loin)
- limón (lemon)
- sal (salt)
- mantequilla (butter)
- papas (potatoes)
- tomate asado (roasted tomato)
- chile tostado (toasted chile)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion)
- hierbabuena (mint)

## Directions

1. Cut meat into slices
2. Squeeze lemon drops on it, add salt, coat with butter and let sit
3. After ten minutes, place on the grill
4. For tenderness, turn three times; otherwise let it roast longer
5. Serve with boiled or fried potatoes, sauce or chirmolito made as follows: roast tomato and toast chile in lard or on hot coals, wash the chile and mash it well after removing seeds with a little salt, when well mashed add the roasted peeled tomato with a little hot water, ensuring it is not too thin, chop onion and mint and serve
