## Ingredients

- Tortillas with good skin
- Fresh cheese, in good quantity
- Salt
- Lard for frying
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped onion
- Pepper powder of Castile
- Capers
- Four to six chopped olives
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Take tortillas with good skin, remove a small piece and through that opening fill them with plenty of fresh cheese, sprinkle with salt and fry in hot lard.
2. Place them on the platter and in the same lard (if there is much, remove some, keeping a small tablespoon), fry the peeled tomato and chopped onion.
3. When well fried, add a little salt, Castile pepper powder, some capers, four or six chopped olives; cover the enchiladas with this, making sure the sauce is not too thick, and serve with a little chopped parsley.
