## Ingredients

- cooked tomato
- tomatillo
- garlic
- oregano
- peppercorns
- cumin
- chile
- achiote
- French bread
- liver

## Directions

1. Grind cooked tomato and tomatillo, one clove of garlic, a little oregano, some peppercorns, a little cumin, chile to taste, a small piece of achiote and a soaked French bread
2. Dissolve this in the broth in which the liver cooked
3. Fry it and add the liver in small pieces or in long slices
