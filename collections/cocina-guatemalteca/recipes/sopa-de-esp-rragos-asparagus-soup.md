## Ingredients

- six pounds of beef bone
- salt
- leeks
- onion
- one hen or two plump chickens
- finely chopped vegetables: carrots, tender green beans, chayote, finely chopped cabbage, broad beans, and peas
- asparagus
- corn (canned)
- fried seasoning made of finely chopped tomato, onion, and one clove of garlic
- chopped parsley
- pepper powder
- one large spoonful of flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash six pounds of beef bone very well and place in a pot of boiling water
2. Add salt, leeks, and onion, and skim until the broth is very clear
3. Add one hen or two plump chickens
4. Let the broth simmer for three or four hours
5. Remove the meat and strain the broth
6. Drop finely chopped vegetables into the strained broth: carrots, tender green beans, chayote, very finely chopped cabbage, broad beans, and peas
7. When well cooked, finely chop the hen meat and the asparagus
8. If there are no asparagus pieces, add a can of corn
9. Prepare fried seasoning made of finely chopped tomato, onion, and one clove of garlic with chopped parsley and pepper powder
10. Fry one large spoonful of flour in butter and add to the soup
11. Let it come to a boil two or three times and serve hot
