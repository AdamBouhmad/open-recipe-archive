## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 ounces sugar
- 4 ounces butter
- 4 ounces salpor flour
- 4 ounces bread flour
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder
- 2 ounces candied fruits: fig, chilacayote, orange and lemon, chopped
- 1 ounce raisins
- peeled and chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites like for nougat
2. Add the egg yolks beaten with 4 ounces of sugar and 4 ounces of butter
3. Mix in 4 ounces of salpor flour and 4 ounces of bread flour, mixing everything very well
4. Mix in 1 teaspoon of Royal, 2 ounces of candied fruits (fig, chilacayote, orange and lemon), all chopped, 1 ounce of raisins and peeled and chopped almonds
5. After mixing everything well, pour into a mold greased with butter
