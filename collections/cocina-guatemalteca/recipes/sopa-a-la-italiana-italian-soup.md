## Ingredients

- Various vegetables in small pieces
- Water
- Salt
- Bacon (two ounces if available)
- Onion (equal quantity to the bacon)
- Parsley (equal quantity to the bacon)
- Garlic clove (one, minced)
- Cooked or minced meat (half a pound)
- Salt and pepper
- Cooked and ground tomato
- Pasta or rice

## Directions

1. Cook various vegetables in small pieces.
2. When parboiled, change the water and add the necessary salt and two ounces of bacon if available.
3. In a pan, fry equal quantities of onion, parsley, and one minced garlic clove.
4. When the onion is golden, add half a pound of well-cooked or minced meat with salt and pepper.
5. When the meat releases its juices, add sufficient cooked and ground tomato.
6. When this is cooked, add it to the vegetables along with a little pasta or rice and serve hot.
