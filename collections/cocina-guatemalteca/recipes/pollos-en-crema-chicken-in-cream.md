## Ingredients

- chickens
- butter
- half a liter of cream per chicken (or 1 glass of cream if cooked)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chickens, brown them in butter just until they color
2. Add half a liter of cream for each chicken with salt and a little pepper
3. Alternatively, they can be made with cooked chickens; for 1 chicken use 1 glass of cream
