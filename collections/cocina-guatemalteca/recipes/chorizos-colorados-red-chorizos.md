## Ingredients

- 6 pounds meat
- 1/2 ounce salt
- 1/4 ounce pepper
- 1/4 ounce cumin
- 1/4 ounce oregano
- 4 garlic cloves
- 3 pieces achiote
- 4 cooked guajillo chiles without seeds
- 1 cup cooked miltomate
- 1/2 bottle vinegar
- intestines

## Directions

1. Grind 6 pounds of meat
2. Add 1/2 ounce salt, 1/4 ounce pepper, 1/4 ounce cumin, 1/4 ounce oregano, 4 garlic cloves, 3 pieces achiote, 4 cooked guajillo chiles without seeds
3. Dissolve everything in 1/2 bottle vinegar and add to the meat, mixing very well
4. Let sit in this infusion for three days
5. Fill intestines with the mixture and tie them as with the previous chorizo recipe
