## Ingredients

- good broth, preferably from hen
- French bread
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare good broth, preferably from hen.
2. Fry slices of French bread in butter and put them in special pans for this.
3. Cover with broth and put two eggs on each one, making sure they do not break but come out whole.
