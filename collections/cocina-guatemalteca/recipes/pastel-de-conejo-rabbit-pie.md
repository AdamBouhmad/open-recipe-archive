## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- salt
- lard and butter
- ham pieces
- pepper powder
- one teaspoon flour
- one glass white wine or sherry
- broth
- rabbit livers
- puff pastry dough
- olives
- capers
- truffles and mushrooms (if available)
- salchichón slices
- Extremadura chorizo with fat
- hard-boiled egg slices

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit well, cut it into pieces, and fry in lard and butter with salt
2. When it takes color, add ham pieces, pepper powder, and one teaspoon of flour
3. When browned, add one glass of white wine or sherry and a little broth
4. Once cooked, remove the rabbit and chop the livers that should be precooked, adding them to the sauce until it reduces to one quarter
5. Prepare a puff pastry dough, roll it with a pin, and use to cover a baking pan
6. Place in the oven to cook without browning
7. Layer the stewed rabbit at the desired height, adding olives, capers, truffles and mushrooms if available, salchichón slices, pieces of Extremadura chorizo with their fat, and hard-boiled egg slices
8. Cover with the sauce and top with another thin layer of puff pastry, decorating with small designs
9. Place in the oven to brown
