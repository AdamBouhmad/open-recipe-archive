## Ingredients

- lomo (loin)
- mantequilla (butter)
- cebolla (onion)
- polvo de orégano (ground oregano)
- jugo de naranja dulce (sweet orange juice)
- tomillo (thyme)
- hoja de laurel (bay leaf)
- agua (water)
- aceite de oliva (olive oil)
- pan frito (fried bread)
- manteca (lard)
- aceitunas (olives)
- alcaparras (capers)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- lechuga (lettuce)

## Directions

1. Make some small cuts in the loin with a pointed knife and fry in butter
2. When colored, add sliced onion, ground oregano, a little sweet orange juice, thyme, two bay leaves, water to cover the meat and two tablespoons of olive oil
3. Grind a small piece of fried bread in lard with six olives and six capers, dissolve this in a cup of vinegar and add to the meat, letting it reduce
4. Serve with lettuce or as desired
