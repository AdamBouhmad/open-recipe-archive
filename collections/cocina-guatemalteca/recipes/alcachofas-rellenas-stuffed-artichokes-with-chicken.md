## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Chicken, cooked, in small pieces
- Ham
- Parsley, chopped
- Manteca or mantequilla, one tablespoon
- Tomato
- Garlic, one clove
- Onion, peeled and chopped
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cook in water with salt.
2. Remove all the fuzz very well and let all water drain.
3. Dry with a cloth.
4. Prepare a filling of cooked chicken made into small pieces, ham, chopped parsley.
5. Fry all in one tablespoon of manteca or mantequilla with tomato, one clove of garlic, peeled and chopped onion with pepper powder, clove and nutmeg.
6. Fill the artichokes with this, tie them and place in a pan with a bit of broth in the oven.
