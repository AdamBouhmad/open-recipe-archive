## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- six eggs
- one teaspoon of salt
- one ounce of cinnamon powder
- half an ounce of Chiapa pepper
- one pound of lard
- half a liter of milk
- one large tablespoon of Royal
- half a cup of panela
- one ounce of ginger

## Directions

1. The night before, put half a cup of panela to boil with one ounce of well-pounded ginger
2. When it reaches the right point, remove the ginger
3. Strain the honey well and mix it with the flour and all the other ingredients well combined
4. Put in greased baking dishes in the oven
