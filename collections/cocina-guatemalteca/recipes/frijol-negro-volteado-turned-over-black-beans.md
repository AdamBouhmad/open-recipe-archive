## Ingredients

- black beans, cooked
- two ounces of lard or two large spoonfuls
- four or five tablespoons of the bean broth
- fried plantain slices
- washed butter or slices of fresh cheese

## Directions

1. After the beans are well cooked, grind them very fine
2. Heat a pan with two ounces of lard or two large spoonfuls
3. Put the beans in with about four or five tablespoons of the same broth
4. Cook on very low heat until dry
5. If necessary, add a bit more lard
6. When well fried, place on a plate, turning it so it falls whole
7. Serve with fried plantain slices, washed butter or slices of fresh cheese
