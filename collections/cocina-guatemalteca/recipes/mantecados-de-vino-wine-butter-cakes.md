## Ingredients

- Nine ounces of butter
- Half glass of sweet sherry
- Nine ounces of bread flour
- Powdered sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until it whitens
2. Add the sherry little by little
3. Add the bread flour and beat again
4. If too wet, add a bit more flour
5. Place on greased baking sheets
6. Place in the oven
7. When very hot, sprinkle sifted powdered sugar on top
