## Ingredients

- Six ounces of hazelnut
- Six ounces of peeled and toasted almond
- Six ounces of sifted sugar
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix everything very well
2. Form the rosquetes using a paper cone or tin tube onto baking sheets
3. Place in the oven
