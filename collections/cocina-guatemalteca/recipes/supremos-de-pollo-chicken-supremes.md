## Ingredients

- fat chicken
- pork meat
- salt
- pepper powder
- nutmeg
- parsley
- thin membrane from lamb belly
- lard
- carrot

## Directions

1. Debone a fat chicken and cut into two-inch square pieces
2. Grind a little pork meat and season with salt, pepper powder, nutmeg and chopped parsley
3. Use this to fill the small squares which are wrapped in thin membrane from the lamb belly
4. Sew and place in a dish with lard and a halved carrot
5. Let boil for half an hour
