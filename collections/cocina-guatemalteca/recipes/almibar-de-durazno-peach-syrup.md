## Ingredients

- water
- sifted ash
- ten large peaches
- one pound of sugar clarified
- cinnamon strips or fig leaf

## Directions

1. Put some water with a ladle of sifted ash in a vessel, and when it comes to a boil throw in ten peaches
2. When they peel, remove them and wash in very clean water
3. Make three cuts with a knife down the middle of each peach
4. Have one pound of sugar already clarified
5. Put the peaches in the clarified sugar to heat
6. They should boil for two hours over moderate heat
7. When they begin to reach the proper consistency, add cinnamon strips or a fig leaf
8. Remove when the syrup has reached medium point
