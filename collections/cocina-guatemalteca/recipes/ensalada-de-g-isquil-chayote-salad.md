## Ingredients

- Chayote
- Salt
- Baking soda
- Vinegar
- Water
- Oil
- Pepper powder
- Onion
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Peel the chayote, chop it into small cubes or in marquesotes, as desired
2. Cook it in water with salt and a little baking soda so it comes out green
3. Prepare in the salad bowl half a cup of vinegar and half water, salt, pepper powder and two spoonfuls of oil
4. Place the cooked chayote in this dressing
5. Place on top onions that have been partially cooked in water with salt and vinegar, halved and sprinkled with oregano powder
