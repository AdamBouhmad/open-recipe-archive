## Ingredients

- venison leg
- chopped parsley
- clove powder
- ham
- chopped garlic
- lard or butter
- onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Lard the leg with chopped parsley, clove powder, ham, and chopped garlic
2. Place in a pan coated with lard or butter with onion
3. Cover and let brown very slowly, basting with butter
