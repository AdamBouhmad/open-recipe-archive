## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), in pieces
- salt
- miltomate and tomato (or tomatillo snake variety)
- 1 guaque chile, cooked
- garlic
- oregano
- cumin
- 1 small piece of achiote
- 1 French (pepper?), chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey in pieces with salt.
2. Grind miltomate, tomato, cooked guaque chile, garlic, oregano, cumin, achiote, and French together.
3. Dissolve this mixture in the same broth.
4. Fry and add the turkey pieces.
5. Serve hot.
