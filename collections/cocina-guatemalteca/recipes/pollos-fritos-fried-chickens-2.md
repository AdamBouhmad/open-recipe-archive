## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil or lard
- salt
- hot water
- 2 bay leaves
- mashed garlic
- cinnamon powder
- clove
- pepper
- half cup of vinegar
- chopped and blanched onion
- hot water
- green lettuce

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and fry them in oil or lard with salt
2. When they turn color, cover with hot water, salt, 2 bay leaves, mashed garlic, cinnamon powder, clove and pepper
3. When the liquid is consumed, add half cup of vinegar
4. Add a little chopped onion that has been blanched in hot water
5. Let it boil and serve with green lettuces
