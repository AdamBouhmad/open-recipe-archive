## Ingredients

- 2 glasses cooked milk
- 2 tablespoons bread flour
- 0.5 pounds white sugar
- 4 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 drop bitter almond essence

## Directions

1. Put in 2 glasses of cooked milk 2 tablespoons of bread flour, 0.5 pounds of white sugar, 4 ounces of peeled and ground almonds, 4 egg yolks, and 1 drop of bitter almond essence
