## Ingredients

- carrots
- salt
- flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- onion slices
- hot water

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots in salted water and let them drain
2. Cut and coat in flour, heat one tablespoon of butter in a pan, add some onion slices
3. When they are golden (not burned) add the carrots
4. When fried, add one glass of hot water, salt to taste and let boil for five minutes
5. Serve hot
