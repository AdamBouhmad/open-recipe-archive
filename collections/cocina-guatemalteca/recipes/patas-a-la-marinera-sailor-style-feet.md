## Ingredients

- Feet
- salt
- thyme
- chopped parsley
- slices of chorizo or sausage and ham
- toasted bread
- garlic cloves
- Castilian peppercorns
- tablespoon of paprika powder
- broth from cooking

## Directions

1. Split the feet and put them to cook with salt, thyme and chopped parsley
2. When they are almost cooked, add slices of chorizo or sausage and ham
3. Grind a little toasted bread with a garlic clove, a few Castilian peppercorns and a tablespoon of paprika powder
4. Fry this, dissolving it with a little of the same broth, and add the feet with the chorizo and ham
