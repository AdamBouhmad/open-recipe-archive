## Ingredients

- duck
- two spoonfuls of lard
- salt
- water
- two bay leaves
- two onion heads
- pepper powder
- three white turnip roots
- half a teaspoon of burned sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the duck and fry in two spoonfuls of lard with salt
2. When it takes color, cover with water
3. Add two bay leaves, two onion heads, and pepper powder
4. When it has boiled for half an hour, add three peeled and cut white turnip roots
5. Add half a teaspoon of burned sugar
