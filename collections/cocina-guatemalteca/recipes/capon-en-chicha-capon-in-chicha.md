## Ingredients

- 1 capon, cleaned
- 1 onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 sprig of thyme
- Castilian pepper
- A few cloves
- Salt
- Chicha (not too strong)

## Directions

1. After the capon is well cleaned, place it in a dish with onion, tomato, thyme, Castilian pepper, a few cloves and salt
2. If desired, keep everything whole, or alternatively grind everything to powder
3. Cover with a little chicha that is not too strong
4. Leave until it falls apart
