## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- salt
- pepper
- oysters
- butter
- 2 egg yolks, cooked
- 1 sprig of thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon of starch or 1 teaspoon of flour fried in butter
- water

## Directions

1. Rub the chicken with salt and pepper
2. Stuff it inside and outside with oysters
3. Rub with butter
4. Place in a hermetically sealable vessel
5. Cook in a water bath for two hours
6. Remove and collect the juice it has released
7. Fry in a dish with a little lard
8. Add 3 cooked and chopped egg yolks
9. Add 1 sprig of thyme and 1 bay leaf
10. Add 1 teaspoon of starch or 1 teaspoon of flour fried in butter
11. Add water to make a very thin sauce
12. Let it boil twice
13. Pour this sauce over the chicken and serve
