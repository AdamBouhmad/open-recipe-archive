## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- 1 ounce of butter
- 1 ounce of lard
- a little milk

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of flour, one ounce of butter and one of lard very well with a little milk
2. Make rings
3. Place in the oven
4. When they take color, remove and coat with butter
5. Return to the oven to brown
