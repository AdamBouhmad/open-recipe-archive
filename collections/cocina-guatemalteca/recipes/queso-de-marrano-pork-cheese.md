## Ingredients

- pig's head
- feet
- bay leaves
- thyme sprig
- salt
- Castilian pepper
- parsley sprig
- broth
- pepper powder or parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Clean the head well.
2. Cook with the feet, two bay leaves, a thyme sprig, salt, some Castilian pepper and a parsley sprig.
3. When well cooked, chop everything finely, ensuring no bones remain; discard brains and bones.
4. Put all the chopped meat in a pan with a little of the broth in which the head was cooked.
5. When well heated, add pepper powder or chopped parsley.
6. Pour into a clean cloth and press.
7. The next day serve as desired.
8. If desired, cook a beef foot with salt, and when well cooked, chop it and mix with the head before pressing.
