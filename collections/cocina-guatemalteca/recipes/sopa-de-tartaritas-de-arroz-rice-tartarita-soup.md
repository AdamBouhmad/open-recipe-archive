## Ingredients

- Three ounces of rice
- Two egg whites
- Rice flour
- One tablespoon of grated cheese
- Lard for frying
- Half a pound of very tender loin
- Cinnamon
- Eight Castilian peppercorns
- A little saffron
- A small piece of cinnamon
- Two eggs
- Salt
- Bread or flour powder
- Pepper powder
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Soak three ounces of rice; when well-soaked wash it and place in the sun for a moment
2. Grind it, obtaining a fine powder from it
3. Beat two egg whites and when well-stiff mix in the yolks, rice flour and one tablespoon of grated cheese
4. Fry in very small cakes in hot lard
5. Have good broth prepared as the others
6. Grind half a pound of very tender loin with a little cinnamon, eight Castilian peppercorns, a little saffron and a small piece of cinnamon
7. When all is well-ground, mix in two eggs, a little salt, the fried mixture and one teaspoon of bread or flour powder
8. With the tip of a spoon or knife make small meatballs and drop into the broth
9. When it boils twice add the tartaritas to boil once so they do not fall apart and serve with pepper powder and chopped parsley
