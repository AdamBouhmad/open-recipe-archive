## Ingredients

- oil or lard
- four strained tomatoes
- pepper powder
- salt
- one large spoonful of vinegar
- broth of chicken or good stew
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a spoonful of oil or lard in a pan
2. add four strained tomatoes, pepper powder, salt, one large spoonful of vinegar and a little chicken broth or good stew broth
3. finally, add four egg yolks to serve as a light sauce
