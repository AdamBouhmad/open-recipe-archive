## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Onion
- Tomato
- Flour or bread without sugar
- Broth
- Dry sherry (optional)

## Directions

1. Debone the chicken and mince the meat until ground
2. Make a mixture of onion and peeled and finely minced tomato
3. Add the chicken meat with a little flour or a piece of bread without sugar
4. Mix very well with a small spoon
5. Take small amounts and drop them into the broth
6. As soon as it boils, remove and serve hot
7. If desired, add a small glass of dry sherry
