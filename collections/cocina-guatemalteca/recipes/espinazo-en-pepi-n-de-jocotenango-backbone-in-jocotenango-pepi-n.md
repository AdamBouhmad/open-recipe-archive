## Ingredients

- split backbone
- salt
- rice
- tomatillo
- green cilantro
- twenty Chiapa peppers
- same amount of Castilian pepper
- one toasted guaque chile
- one roasted onion

## Directions

1. Cook the split backbone with salt
2. Toast a little rice according to the amount of meat and some tomatillo
3. Once toasted, grind with some green cilantro, twenty Chiapa peppers and the same amount of Castilian pepper, one toasted guaque chile and one roasted onion
4. Grind the rice with water but very finely, thin with a little broth and add to the pot where the backbone is, making sure it is not too thin and not too thick
