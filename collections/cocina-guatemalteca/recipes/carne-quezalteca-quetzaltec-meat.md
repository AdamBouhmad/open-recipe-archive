## Ingredients

- carne (meat)
- jamón (ham)
- manteca o mantequilla (lard or butter)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- agua (water)
- tomate (tomato)
- cebolla (onion)
- polvo de pimienta (ground pepper)

## Directions

1. Open the meat lengthwise
2. Place a piece of ham inside
3. Brown in lard or butter
4. When golden, add half a cup of vinegar and the rest water until the meat is covered
5. Cook some tomato with two or three onion heads
6. Once cooked, mash with a spoon
7. Add a little ground pepper
8. Pour onto the meat to keep it well and serve with its own sauce
