## Ingredients

- One glass of hot water
- Salt to taste
- Two tablespoons of sugar
- Two ounces of butter
- One fresh lemon peel
- Four ounces of sifted bread flour
- Four eggs
- Powdered sugar for topping
- Cream filling (Crema de Leche)

## Directions

1. Put one glass of hot water with salt, two tablespoons of sugar, two ounces of butter, and one fresh lemon peel in a pan
2. At the first boil, remove from heat, remove the lemon peel
3. Gradually add four ounces of sifted bread flour, stirring very well to avoid lumps
4. Place over very low heat, stirring constantly until a compact ball forms
5. Remove from heat and stir until cool
6. Mix in four eggs one at a time, ensuring each is well combined before adding the next
7. Place on a baking sheet in desired sizes, sprinkle with powdered sugar
8. Bake until cooked
9. Split in half and fill with cream filling (Crema de Leche)
