## Ingredients

- cooked liver
- cooked tomatillo
- chile
- hard cooked liver
- a small piece of cinnamon
- Castilian peppers
- a small piece of achiote
- a soaked French roll

## Directions

1. Cook the liver
2. Grind cooked tomatillo, chile, a piece of hard cooked liver, a small piece of cinnamon, some Castilian peppers, a small piece of achiote and a soaked French roll
3. Thin with the same broth
4. Fry and add the finely minced liver
