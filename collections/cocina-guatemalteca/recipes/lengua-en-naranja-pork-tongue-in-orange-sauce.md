## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- juice of two sweet oranges
- vinegar
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- bay leaf
- sprig of thyme
- tender green beans

## Directions

1. Fry the tongue and when it browns, add juice of two sweet oranges, a little vinegar, clove, pepper, a small piece of cinnamon, a bay leaf, and a sprig of thyme
2. When cooked, remove all the spices
3. Serve with tender cooked green beans
