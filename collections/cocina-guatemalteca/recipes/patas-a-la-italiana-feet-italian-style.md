## Ingredients

- feet
- salt
- bay leaves
- clove
- pepper
- parsley sprig
- lard
- bread powder
- Parmesan cheese powder
- butter or melted lard

## Directions

1. Clean the feet well.
2. Cook with salt, two bay leaves, clove, pepper and a parsley sprig.
3. When cooked, cut them into pieces, removing all the bones.
4. Grease a pan with lard and add bread powder.
5. Layer with pieces of feet, then Parmesan cheese powder, then more feet pieces, then bread powder.
6. Drizzle with a little melted lard or butter.
7. Put in the oven for ten minutes; if there is no oven, it can be placed over two fires.
