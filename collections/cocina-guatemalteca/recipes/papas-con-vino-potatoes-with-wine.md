## Ingredients

- butter
- onions
- red wine or dry sherry
- potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Put butter in a serving dish
2. Add one or two chopped onions and a glass of red wine or dry sherry
3. Put the potatoes in sliced or whole, with the necessary water for them to cook
