## Ingredients

- chicken
- tomato
- garlic
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- orange juice
- salt
- two pounds potato
- four ounces butter
- milk
- sweet bell pepper
- peas
- olives
- capers
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as a stew with tomato, garlic, onions, bay leaf, thyme, cloves, pepper and cinnamon, all in powder form.
2. Use orange juice instead of vinegar and add salt.
3. Prepare a good potato purée by cooking two pounds of potato in little water, passing them through a strainer while hot, and mixing with four ounces of butter, salt and milk so it is not too watery.
4. In a buttered baking dish, place the chicken with strips of peeled sweet bell pepper and some cooked peas, and if desired, olives and capers.
5. Cover with the remaining purée, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and place in the oven to brown.
