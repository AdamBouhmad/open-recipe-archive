## Ingredients

- Loin
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Lemon juice
- Ham
- Garlic
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Thyme
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Vinegar
- Ginger
- Pickles
- Oil
- Olives
- Capers
- Lettuce
- Radishes

## Directions

1. Rub a loin very well with salt, pepper powder, and a bit of lemon juice
2. Lard it with ham, garlic, pepper powder, cloves, and cinnamon
3. Cook with a sprig of thyme, salt, water, two bay leaves, vinegar, ginger, cloves, and pepper
4. When cooked, let it cool and slice
5. Add pickles, plenty of oil, vinegar, salt, pepper powder, olives, and capers
6. Garnish with lettuce and tender radishes
