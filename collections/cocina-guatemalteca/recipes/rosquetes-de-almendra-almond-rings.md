## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- three eggs
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify half a pound of sugar to a very high point
2. Mix in four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
3. Let it cool down and mix in three eggs and cinnamon powder
4. Form the rings and put in the oven
