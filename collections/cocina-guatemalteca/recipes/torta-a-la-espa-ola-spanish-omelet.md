## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onion
- two eggs
- parsley
- oil or lard

## Directions

1. Fry potatoes sliced so they do not brown.
2. Fry chopped onion.
3. Mix everything with two eggs and chopped parsley.
4. Fry as an omelet.
