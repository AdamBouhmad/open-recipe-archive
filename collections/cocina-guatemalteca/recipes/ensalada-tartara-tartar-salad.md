## Ingredients

- peas
- green beans
- chayote
- cabbage
- carrots
- potatoes
- turnips
- one chicken
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- one glass of cream
- two egg yolks
- olives
- capers
- pickled foreign onions
- lettuce
- parsley
- sweet peppers

## Directions

1. Cook peas, green beans, chayote, cabbage, carrots, potatoes, turnips, and one chicken.
2. Season everything with salt, pepper, oil, and vinegar.
3. Beat one glass of cream with two egg yolks.
4. Cover the vegetables with this cream mixture.
5. Add olives, capers, pickled foreign onions, and chopped chicken.
6. Garnish with lettuce, parsley, and sweet peppers.
