## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- six egg yolks
- six ounces of sifted flour
- four ounces of sifted sugar
- one ounce of melted lard

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff like nougat
2. Mix in the yolks with the sifted flour, sifted sugar, and melted lard
3. Place in buttered molds
4. Bake in the oven
