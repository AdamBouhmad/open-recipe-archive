## Ingredients

- two plantains
- a small piece of cinnamon
- beans, well-fried and sweetened to taste
- lard for frying
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cook two plantains by steam
2. When already fried, grind them with a small piece of cinnamon
3. Make small tortillas of the desired size
4. Fill them with beans that have been well-fried and sweetened to taste beforehand
5. Fold the tortilla and fry in hot lard
6. Serve with powdered sugar on top
