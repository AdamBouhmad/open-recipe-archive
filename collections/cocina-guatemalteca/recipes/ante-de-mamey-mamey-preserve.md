## Ingredients

- one and one-quarter pounds of sugar
- one pound of mamey, well ground
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon powder mixed with six cloves
- marquesote cake soaked in sherry
- raisins and chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Clarify one and one-quarter pounds of sugar
2. Add one pound of well-ground mamey
3. When it begins to set, add one teaspoon of ground cinnamon powder mixed with six cloves
4. Serve alone if desired, or place a layer of marquesote soaked in sherry on a platter, then a layer of the preserve
5. Decorate with raisins and chopped almonds
