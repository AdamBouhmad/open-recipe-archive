## Ingredients

- Cooked tongue
- Lard or butter
- Peeled tomatoes
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Water
- Vinegar
- Black pepper powder
- Capers
- Olives
- Cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Place lard or butter in a pan
2. Chop two or three peeled tomatoes, onion, and one peeled garlic clove
3. Add finely chopped parsley
4. When fried, add a little water, one teaspoon of vinegar, and black pepper powder
5. Add the sliced tongue to this mixture
6. Add capers, chopped olives, and slices of cooked potatoes
7. Serve hot
