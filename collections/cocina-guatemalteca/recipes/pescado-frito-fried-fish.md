## Ingredients

- fish, well washed
- salt
- flour
- oil or lard
- crushed garlic cloves
- one cup vinegar
- one cup oil
- green chile
- chopped onion
- hard-boiled egg pieces
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- chopped lettuce

## Directions

1. After washing the fish well, cut into pieces of desired size.
2. Add salt and coat with flour.
3. Fry crushed garlic cloves in hot oil or lard.
4. When well browned, place the fish on a platter.
5. Bathe with a sauce made of one cup of vinegar, one cup of oil, a green chile, and finely chopped onion with hard-boiled egg pieces.
6. Scald the onion with boiling water.
7. Add pepper and oregano powder, and chopped lettuce around the edge.
