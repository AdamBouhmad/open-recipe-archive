## Ingredients

- large lemons with thick rind
- water
- salt
- bicarbonate of soda
- clarified honey
- cinnamon strips

## Directions

1. Peel the lemons with a piece of glass or a grater, taking care to remove only the fuzz and keeping them green
2. Cut in half and remove the juice and pith
3. Blanch in hot water with one tablespoon of salt and one teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda
4. When well blanched remove and place in cloth
5. Keep under the stream of water for six days
6. Remove and place to heat in clarified honey as with the others
7. Add cinnamon strips
