## Ingredients

- cooked, chopped meat
- three raw eggs per half pound of meat
- fried recado
- bread powder
- Castilian pepper
- a little sherry optional
- butter
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- achiote coloring optional

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat and mix with three raw eggs, fried recado and bread powder with Castilian peppers; if desired add a little sherry
2. Form the patties and fry in hot butter
3. Once fried, prepare a fried recado with onion, tomato and garlic
4. Add water, bread powder or achiote coloring to make a thin sauce
5. Serve the patties with this sauce
