## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- three eggs
- three ounces of butter
- a little milk
- half a teaspoon of salt
- meat or brain and spinach for filling
- egg white
- salt
- tomato
- dried chili pepper
- onion heads
- garlic heads
- oil
- parmesan cheese
- mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. The day before, moisten one pound of flour with three eggs, three ounces of butter, a little milk, and half a teaspoon of salt
2. Knead very well leaving a dough that is neither hard nor watery
3. The following day, stretch it as thin as possible and cut the ravioli
4. Fill them with meat or brain and spinach
5. Seal the ravioli with egg white
6. Put them in a pot with boiling salted water
7. Prepare a sauce by cooking tomato with a dried chili pepper, two onion heads, and two ground garlic heads
8. Strain and add this sauce to oil where two other garlic heads have been fried until golden, then remove them
9. Fry the sauce in this oil
10. Place the ravioli in a dish, cover with the sauce and sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese
11. If desired, put in the oven for a quarter hour to brown, or serve just hot
12. If desired, mushrooms can also be added
