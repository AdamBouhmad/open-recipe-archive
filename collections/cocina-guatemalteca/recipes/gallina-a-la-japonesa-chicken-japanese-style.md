## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, cut in pieces
- 1 tablespoon of butter or lard
- ham, chopped
- garlic cloves
- parsley, chopped
- water and salt
- 1 ounce of toasted almonds
- 1/2 ounce of ground almonds
- 1 tablespoon of toasted flour, lightly browned

## Directions

1. Place the chicken pieces in a pan with butter or lard.
2. Add chopped ham.
3. When half fried, add garlic cloves and chopped parsley.
4. Cover with water and salt.
5. Add toasted almonds and half ground, along with the toasted flour.
6. When cooked, serve hot.
