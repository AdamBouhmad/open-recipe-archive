## Ingredients

- Feet
- flour
- beaten egg
- lard or butter
- peeled and chopped tomato
- onion
- water
- salt
- bread powder
- achiote
- tomatoes
- peeled onions
- green chiles

## Directions

1. After the feet are well cooked, dry them, coat them in flour, then wrap them in beaten egg and place in the oven to remove the fat
2. In a little lard, fry some peeled and chopped tomato with onion
3. Add a little water, salt and bread powder ground with a little achiote, and bathe the feet with this
4. Alternatively, cook some tomatoes with peeled onions, green chiles and salt, break this up well while hot and serve with the feet
