## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Hard-boiled egg
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Oil

## Directions

1. Chop the lettuce as finely as possible
2. Peel large tomatoes, remove the seeds and cut them into slices
3. Place in a bowl a layer of lettuce, one of tomato, another of lettuce and another of onion; alternate the layers this way, making sure the last one is of tomato and onion
4. Add slices of hard-boiled egg
5. Moments before serving, bathe it with a dressing composed of half a cup of water and half vinegar, salt, pepper powder and two spoonfuls of oil; if this is added before, the lettuce discolors and will look very bad
