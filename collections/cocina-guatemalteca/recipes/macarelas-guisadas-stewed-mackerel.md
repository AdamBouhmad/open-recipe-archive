## Ingredients

- mackerel
- oil from the mackerel
- peeled tomato, chopped
- onion, chopped
- garlic, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pepper powder
- olives
- capers
- cooked potato slices

## Directions

1. Remove the oil from the mackerel and use it to fry the peeled chopped tomato, onion, garlic and parsley
2. When well fried, add a spoonful of vinegar and a little water
3. Season with salt and pepper powder and add olives and capers
4. Cover the mackerel with this mixture
5. Serve if desired with sliced cooked potatoes
