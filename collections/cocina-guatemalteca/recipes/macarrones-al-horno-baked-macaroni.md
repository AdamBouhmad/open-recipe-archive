## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- butter
- cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni with salt
2. Butter a pan and alternately layer powdered cheese and macaroni until finished
3. Top with another layer of butter
4. Bake for ten minutes or cook over two fires
