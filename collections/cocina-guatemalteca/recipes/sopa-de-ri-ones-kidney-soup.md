## Ingredients

- good broth
- kidney
- finely chopped vegetables
- chard, finely chopped
- brain slices, well cooked and fried
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Prepare very good broth
2. Wash the kidney very well and chop it very finely
3. Drop into the broth with finely chopped vegetables and a little finely chopped chard
4. Just before serving, add slices of well-cooked brain fried with bread powder
5. Serve hot
