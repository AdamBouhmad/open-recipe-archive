## Ingredients

- cidra
- water
- salt
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified per pound of fruit
- cinnamon strips

## Directions

1. Peel the cidra and chop into very small cubes imitating hail
2. Throw into a little boiling water and when it has boiled a few times remove that water and return to the fire with hot clean water
3. Add one tablespoon of salt
4. When blanched, remove and place in cloth and tie well under the stream of water for three days
5. After three days remove and place in clarified honey
6. When the fruit has heated through add cinnamon strips
7. Always take care to clarify one and a half pounds of sugar for one pound of fruit
