## Ingredients

- pork rib
- salt
- two tomatoes
- two green chiles
- peas

## Directions

1. Cook the pork rib with salt
2. In the same broth, cook two tomatoes and two green chiles
3. Cook the peas separately with salt
4. When the tomato is cooked, grind it with the chile and about four tablespoons of cooked peas
5. Dissolve this mixture with a little of the broth in which the rib was cooked
6. Add the peas and the rib, fry everything well, ensuring it is not too thin
7. Serve hot
