## Ingredients

- feet
- lard
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- onion
- garlic clove
- white bread
- pepper powder
- parsley, chopped
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking the feet, cut them into pieces of desired size.
2. Put a pan on the fire with lard.
3. Add peeled and chopped tomato, onion and a garlic clove.
4. When half fried, add the feet with a little white bread, pepper powder, a little chopped parsley and a teaspoon of vinegar.
5. Serve dry.
