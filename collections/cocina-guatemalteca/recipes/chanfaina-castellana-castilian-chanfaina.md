## Ingredients

- cooked liver
- cooked jowls
- oil
- onion
- garlic
- minced parsley
- pepper powder
- cloves
- one tablespoon flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the liver and cut the jowls into pieces after cooking them beforehand
2. Put everything in a pan with a little oil, onion, garlic, minced parsley, pepper powder and cloves
3. When well fried, add one tablespoon of flour well browned in butter
4. Let it cook and serve in a thin sauce
