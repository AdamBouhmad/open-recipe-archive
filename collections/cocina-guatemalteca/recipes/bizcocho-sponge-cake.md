## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 3 ounces of potato starch
- 2 ounces of sifted bread flour

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites to stiff peaks
2. When well firm, mix in the yolks beaten with four ounces of sugar
3. Then add three ounces of potato starch and two ounces of well-sifted bread flour
4. Pour into a mold coated with butter or paper boxes
