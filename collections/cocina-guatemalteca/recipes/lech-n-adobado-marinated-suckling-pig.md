## Ingredients

- Four to six pasilla chiles
- three or four roasted garlic cloves
- cumin
- oregano
- piece of French bread soaked in vinegar
- water
- suckling pig

## Directions

1. Soak four to six pasilla chiles and remove the seeds and grind them with three or four roasted garlic cloves, cumin, oregano and a piece of French bread soaked in vinegar
2. Dissolve everything with half a liter of water and place the piglet, already cleaned like the previous recipes, in this
3. Put on very low heat until it consumes
