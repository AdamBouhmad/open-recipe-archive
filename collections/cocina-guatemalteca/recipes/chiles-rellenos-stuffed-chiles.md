## Ingredients

- Green guaque chiles
- Twelve ounces of partially cooked pork meat (for ten chiles)
- One tablespoon of lard
- Tomato
- Onion
- One garlic clove
- One teaspoon of vinegar
- Salt
- Bread powder ground with pepper, cinnamon, and clove
- Chopped almonds and raisins
- A touch of sweetness (optional)
- Zucchini, carrots, green beans, all chopped finely and cooked with salt (optional, instead of almonds and raisins)
- Capers (if using vegetables)
- Flour
- Two eggs
- Hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Place the green guaque chiles over coals that do not burn them
2. Put them in a pot and cover with boiling water
3. Cover and let them sit for ten minutes
4. Peel them easily
5. Open them in the middle and carefully remove the seeds with the tip of a knife
6. Leave them in water, changing it every two hours
7. If they are very spicy, you can add one tablespoon of sweetened vinegar
8. After one or two days in water (depending on preference), remove them and let them drain
9. Dry them with cloth
10. Chop partially cooked pork meat (twelve ounces for ten chiles)
11. Fry in one tablespoon of lard with tomato, onion, and one peeled and chopped garlic clove
12. Add one teaspoon of vinegar, salt, bread powder ground with pepper, cinnamon, and clove, chopped almonds and raisins, and if desired, a touch of sweetness
13. Alternatively, add finely chopped and cooked zucchini, carrots, and green beans with salt, in which case omit raisins and almonds but add capers
14. Fill the chiles with this mixture
15. Dust with flour
16. Dip in beaten egg
17. Fry in hot lard
18. Beat two eggs (first the whites, then the yolks) for ten chiles
19. Serve with a sauce of peeled and chopped tomato, onion, and one fried garlic clove, with one tablespoon of bread powder or one teaspoon of flour dissolved in water, with a touch of achiote color, and one cup of water with salt
20. Alternatively, serve with a sauce of cooked tomato ground with about ten or twelve capers, fried in a little lard, with water to make a thin sauce, seasoned with salt, and serve hot
