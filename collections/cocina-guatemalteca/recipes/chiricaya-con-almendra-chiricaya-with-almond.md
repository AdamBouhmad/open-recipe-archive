## Ingredients

- 3 glasses milk
- 3 ounces sugar
- 3 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 8 egg yolks
- a little cinnamon powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Put in 3 glasses of milk 3 ounces of sugar and 3 ounces of peeled and ground almonds
2. Boil until it thickens
3. Lower the heat and when cold, add 8 egg yolks with a little cinnamon powder
4. Grease a mold with a little butter
5. Pour everything into it and put in the oven in a water bath
