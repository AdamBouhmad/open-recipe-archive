## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salt
- garlic
- oregano
- chile pasa (dried chile)
- Castilian peppers
- cumin
- roasted tomatoes
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After peeling the head, split it in half and remove the brains
2. After washing well, rub with salt
3. Grind garlic, oregano, dried chile, some Castilian peppers, a little cumin, and some roasted tomatoes
4. Dissolve this with vinegar and a little salt
5. Rub this mixture inside and outside the head
6. Place in a cazuela (earthenware dish) and put in the oven for three hours, continuously turning it and basting with its juices
7. Can be served as a table course with vinaigrette sauce
