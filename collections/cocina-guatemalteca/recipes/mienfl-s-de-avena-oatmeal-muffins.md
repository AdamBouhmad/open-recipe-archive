## Ingredients

- one cup quick oats
- one cup sour or curdled milk
- one egg
- half cup brown sugar
- one cup wheat flour
- half teaspoon salt
- one tablespoon Royal baking powder
- half teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak the oats in milk for one hour.
2. Add the egg and beat well.
3. Then add the sugar and flour sifted with Royal and bicarbonate of soda and melted butter; mix well.
4. Coat molds with butter and place in a hot oven for ten minutes.
