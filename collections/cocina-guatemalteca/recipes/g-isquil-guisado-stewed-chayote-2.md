## Ingredients

- Güisquil (chayote)
- Salt
- Manteca (lard)
- Tomato, peeled
- Onion, chopped
- Vinegar, half a teaspoon
- Bread powder
- Fresh cheese, abundant

## Directions

1. Peel the güisquil and chop it into small cubes or marquesote pieces.
2. Cook with salt.
3. Fry in a teaspoon of manteca, peeled tomato with chopped onion.
4. Add the güisquil with half a teaspoon of vinegar and bread powder.
5. When serving, cover with abundant fresh cheese.
