## Ingredients

- ducks
- sufficient oil
- onion slices
- one glass red wine
- juice of half a lemon
- a very small lemon peel
- two bay leaves
- chopped parsley
- a little pepper powder
- water

## Directions

1. After cleaning the ducks well, fry them in sufficient oil
2. When they have taken on color, remove them and fry onion slices in the same oil
3. Add one glass of red wine, the juice of half a lemon, a very small lemon peel, two bay leaves, chopped parsley, and a little pepper powder
4. Return the ducks, cover them with water, and let them boil for two or three hours
