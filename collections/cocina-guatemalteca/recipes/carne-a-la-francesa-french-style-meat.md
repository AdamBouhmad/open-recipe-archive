## Ingredients

- Loin, brisket, or chuck meat
- Two turnips
- Two carrots
- Chopped parsley
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- One glass of Sherry wine
- Water
- Butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Fry the meat in butter
2. Add two turnips, two carrots, chopped parsley, pepper powder, clove, and cinnamon
3. Pour in one glass of Sherry wine and enough water to cover the meat
4. Let it reduce
5. Serve with plain potatoes
