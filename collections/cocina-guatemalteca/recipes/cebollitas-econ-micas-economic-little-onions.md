## Ingredients

- onions
- one glass vinegar
- half glass water
- salt
- a piece of rapadura (unrefined sugar)
- six large tomatoes
- two dried chiles or guaques

## Directions

1. Prepare the onions as for pickling, well cleaned.
2. Place them in a pot with one glass of vinegar and half glass of water, salt, and a piece of rapadura.
3. Grind for fifty onions: six large tomatoes and two dried chiles or guaques.
4. Serve hot.
