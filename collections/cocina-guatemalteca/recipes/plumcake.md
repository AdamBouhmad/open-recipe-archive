## Ingredients

- Seven and a half ounces of flour
- Six ounces of sifted sugar
- Four ounces of currants
- Six ounces of pitted and seedless raisins
- Four ounces of melted butter
- Four ounces of candied peel like fig, orange, chilacayote
- One teaspoon of gum arabic powder
- Four egg whites
- Four egg yolks
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, sifted sugar, currants, raisins, melted butter, candied peel, and gum arabic powder
2. Beat the four egg whites
3. Mix the egg yolks with a pinch of salt and combine with the dry ingredients
4. Mix everything together
5. Pour into a greased mold
6. Place in the oven to bake
