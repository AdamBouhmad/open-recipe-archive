## Ingredients

- one pound and one half of sugar
- nine egg yolks
- six ounces of butter
- juice of one pineapple

## Directions

1. Place one pound and one half of sugar over heat
2. When it reaches the point, let it cool
3. Stir in nine egg yolks passed through a strainer
4. When at medium point add six ounces of butter and the juice of one pineapple
5. Remove when it detaches from the pan
