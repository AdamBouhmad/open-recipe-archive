## Ingredients

- Guava
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the guava with little water, removing all the dark part at the tip
2. Strain without water and weigh the fruit
3. Add an equal amount of sugar
4. Place over the fire with the water in which the guava was cooked, strained
5. When it doesn't stick to the pan, remove from heat
6. Pour into a container or spread fine powdered sugar on a board
7. Place the paste on it and wrap well to store
