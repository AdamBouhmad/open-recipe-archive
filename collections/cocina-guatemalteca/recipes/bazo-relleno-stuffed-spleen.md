## Ingredients

- spleen
- salt
- oregano powder
- sheep blood, partially cooked
- lard
- onion
- tomato, chopped
- capers
- olives
- pickled vegetables
- ham
- chorizo slices
- pepper powder
- vinegar
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the spleen well and cut it open lengthwise.
2. Season with salt and oregano powder.
3. Have some partially cooked sheep blood, chopped.
4. In a pan with hot lard, add onion, chopped tomato, capers, olives, pickled vegetables, ham cut lengthwise, chorizo slices, pepper powder and three tablespoons of vinegar.
5. Mix everything well and fry until half done.
6. Fill the spleen with this mixture.
7. Sew it closed with thick thread.
8. Coat well with butter and salt.
9. Put in the oven, stirring it continuously.
10. Baste with its own juice so it does not dry out.
11. Serve with a vegetable salad.
