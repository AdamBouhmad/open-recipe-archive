## Ingredients

- Chickpeas
- Water
- Lye water
- Ham slices
- Chorizo slices
- Tender green beans
- Güisquil pieces
- Carrot, peeled and split lengthwise
- Cabbage
- Salchichón slices
- Peeled tomato
- Onion, chopped

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas in sufficient water the night before cooking
2. The next day, put a little lye water on the fire
3. Add the chickpeas and let them boil for ten minutes, then lower heat and let cool a bit
4. Wash with cold water and put in very clean water on even heat
5. When cooked, remove the chickpeas
6. In the broth in which they cooked, cook slices of ham, slices of chorizo, tender green beans split lengthwise, pieces of güisquil, carrot peeled and split lengthwise, and cabbage
7. When everything is well cooked, mix it with the chickpeas along with salchichón slices and a fried preparation of peeled tomato and chopped onion
8. With the chickpeas, it should only give one boil so everything comes out whole, and serve hot
