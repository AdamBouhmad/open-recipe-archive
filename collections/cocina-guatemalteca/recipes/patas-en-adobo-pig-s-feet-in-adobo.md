## Ingredients

- well cooked pig's feet
- some cooked tomato
- oregano
- cumin
- a soaked bread piece
- a small piece of achiote

## Directions

1. Well cooked feet are minced
2. Grind some cooked tomato, oregano, cumin, a soaked bread piece and a small piece of achiote
