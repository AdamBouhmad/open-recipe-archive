## Ingredients

- Lamb liver
- Salt
- Flour or bread powder
- Lard
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Take the hard part of the liver and slice it
2. Season with salt, coat with flour or bread powder
3. Fry in hot lard
4. Serve with potatoes cooked plain
