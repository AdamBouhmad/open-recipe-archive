## Ingredients

- loin
- salt
- French mustard
- chayote
- green beans
- carrot
- potato
- peas
- tomato
- onion
- ground white bread
- pepper
- clove
- vinegar
- olive oil
- bay leaves
- thyme

## Directions

1. Open a loin down the middle and rub lightly with salt and French mustard
2. Chop chayote, green beans, carrot, and potato; cook with tender peas
3. Fry everything with tomato and onion
4. When half-fried, add ground white bread with pepper, clove, and a tablespoon of good vinegar
5. Fill the loin with this mixture, then rub with salt and a small glass of oil
6. Place in a dish with half a cup of vinegar, two onion heads, two bay leaves, and a sprig of thyme
7. Bake in the oven, basting frequently
