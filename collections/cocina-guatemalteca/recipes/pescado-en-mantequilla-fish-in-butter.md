## Ingredients

- butter
- chopped onion
- fish, clean, whole or cut
- salt
- pepper powder
- chopped parsley
- one cup water
- a little vinegar
- a little white bread powder

## Directions

1. In a cazuela or fish pan, place butter and chopped onion.
2. Add the clean fish, whether whole or cut.
3. Cover with more butter, chopped onion, salt, pepper powder, and chopped parsley.
4. Add one cup of water with a little vinegar and a little white bread powder.
5. Let it cook over two fires.
