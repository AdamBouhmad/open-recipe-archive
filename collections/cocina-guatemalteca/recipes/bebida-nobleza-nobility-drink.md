## Ingredients

- One pineapple
- Five glasses of water
- One pound of peeled tamarind
- Water
- Ten to fifteen glasses of water
- One tablespoon of ground cinnamon powder
- About ten cloves
- Juice of six lemons
- Sugar to taste
- One bottle of red wine
- Thin lemon slices

## Directions

1. Grind one pineapple and put it in infusion for three days with five glasses of water
2. At the same time, put one pound of peeled tamarind with the same amount of water
3. After that time, strain it
4. Add ten to fifteen glasses of water, one tablespoon of ground cinnamon powder with about ten cloves and the juice of six lemons
5. Add sugar to taste and let sit
6. The following day, add one bottle of red wine and serve with very thin lemon slices
