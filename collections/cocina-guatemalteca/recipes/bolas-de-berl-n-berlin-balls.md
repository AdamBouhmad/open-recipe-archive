## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sifted flour
- three eggs
- two and a half cups of milk
- a pinch of salt
- two ounces of sugar
- one and a half cups of bread yeast

## Directions

1. Mix everything at eight o'clock in the evening
2. The next morning, stretch out the dough with a rolling pin
3. Cut rounds the size desired
4. Place jelly in the middle and put egg white on the edges to seal the two halves
5. Let rest for half an hour to an hour
6. Then fry in very hot lard
7. Before serving, sprinkle sifted sugar over them
