## Ingredients

- Feet
- salt
- vinegar
- two bay leaves
- black pepper powder
- roasted tomatoes
- onions in vinegar

## Directions

1. After the feet are cooked with salt, prepare a little vinegar with two bay leaves, black pepper powder and salt
2. Place the feet in this mixture, pressing them with a weight, and after three or four hours place them on the platter
3. Have some roasted tomatoes that are not too finely ground, add a little of the vinegar in which the feet were soaked and cover the little feet with this sauce
4. Add pickled onions and serve cold
