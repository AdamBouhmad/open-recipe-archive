## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- oil
- salt
- onion, chopped
- pepper
- nutmeg
- a small piece of ginger
- water
- a little vinegar
- a little saffron water
- bread, fried

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit well and fry it in oil with salt
2. When it takes color, fry plenty of chopped onion in the same oil
3. Grind a little pepper, nutmeg, and a small piece of ginger and add with water to cover the rabbit
4. When somewhat tender, add a little vinegar and a little saffron water
5. Let it reduce and place on a platter, surrounding with fried bread slices
