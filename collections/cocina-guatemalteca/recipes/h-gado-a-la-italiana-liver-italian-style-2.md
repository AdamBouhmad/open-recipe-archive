## Ingredients

- liver
- white wine
- vinegar
- oil
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- pepper
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the liver in water, peel and wash it very well.
2. Cut into small pieces and add sufficient white wine and a little good vinegar.
3. Heat a pan with oil, onion, tomato, garlic, pepper, parsley and salt.
4. Add the liver when the pan is hot, being careful not to let it boil much as it becomes tough.
