## Ingredients

- garlic cloves, crushed
- oil
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- french bread, fried and ground
- fish, clean
- one tablespoon vinegar
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- cooked green chile

## Directions

1. Crush some garlic cloves and fry in oil with peeled chopped tomato and a piece of fried and ground french bread.
2. Add the clean fish with one tablespoon of vinegar, pepper powder, oregano powder, and a cooked green chile.
