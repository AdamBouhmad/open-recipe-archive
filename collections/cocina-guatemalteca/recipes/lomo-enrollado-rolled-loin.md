## Ingredients

- Loin
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Onion
- One sprig of thyme
- Salt
- A little lemon juice
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Tenderize a loin well
2. Grind garlic, oregano, onion, and a sprig of thyme
3. Add salt and a little lemon juice
4. Coat the loin very well with this mixture
5. Roll up well, tying with twine
6. Put to cook with equal parts water and vinegar
7. When cold, cut into slices and serve with any salad
