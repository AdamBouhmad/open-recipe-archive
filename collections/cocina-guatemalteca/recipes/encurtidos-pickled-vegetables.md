## Ingredients

- Small onions
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Cloves
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Beet
- Carrot
- Green beans
- Pacaya
- Chayote
- Cauliflower
- Peas
- Tender green beans
- Green beans
- Cabbage
- Escarole
- Ginger
- Laurel powder
- Oregano
- Green chiles

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into a cross shape a few small onions
2. Place them in boiling water; the next day place them in the sun
3. Then put them in very good vinegar with salt, four peppercorns, three cloves, a sprig of thyme, a bay leaf and let them soak for one or two days
4. Cook beet in steam, chop carrots, green beans, pacaya, chayote, cauliflower, peas, tender green beans, peeled green beans
5. Cook all in water with salt so they do not fall apart, remove them and parboil chopped cabbage and tender escarole
6. When they have drained all the water, place them in a dish with half water and half vinegar, salt, a piece of crushed ginger and a sprig of thyme
7. After two days add one spoonful of bay leaf powder, oregano and peppercorns, add some whole green chiles or two
