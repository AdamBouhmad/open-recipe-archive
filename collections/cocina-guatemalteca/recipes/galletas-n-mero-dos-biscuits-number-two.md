## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of butter
- 3 eggs
- zest of 1 lemon
- 6 ounces of finely ground sugar
- 0.5 pound of bread flour

## Directions

1. Beat four ounces of butter until beaten
2. Add three eggs, the zest of a lemon, six ounces of finely ground sugar and half a pound of bread flour
3. Knead everything very well, roll out with the rolling pin
4. Cut the biscuits into small squares or whatever shape desired, place them on baking sheets
5. With a clean stick form little holes and place in the oven
