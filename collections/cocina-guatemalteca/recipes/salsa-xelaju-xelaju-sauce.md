## Ingredients

- tomato
- three dried chiles
- two garlic cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook sufficient tomato with three dried chiles, grind with about two garlic cloves
2. add salt and mix well
3. serve with potatoes or meat; it is more suitable for traveling
4. if desired, fry small fish and cover with this sauce
