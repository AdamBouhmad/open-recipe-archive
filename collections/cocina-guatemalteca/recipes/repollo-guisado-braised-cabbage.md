## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- One tablespoon of lard
- Three peeled tomatoes
- Onion
- One garlic clove
- One tablespoon of vinegar
- White bread powder, finely ground with Castilian pepper
- Fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the chopped cabbage as in previous recipes
2. Fry in one tablespoon of lard three peeled tomatoes, onion, and one garlic clove
3. Add the cabbage with one tablespoon of vinegar and a little white bread powder finely ground with Castilian pepper
4. Just before serving, cover with plenty of fresh cheese
