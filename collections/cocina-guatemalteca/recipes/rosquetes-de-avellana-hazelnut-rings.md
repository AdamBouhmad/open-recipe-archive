## Ingredients

- six ounces of hazelnut
- six ounces of peeled and toasted almonds
- six ounces of sifted sugar
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the hazelnuts, almonds, sifted sugar, and egg yolks very well
2. Using a paper cone or small metal tube, form rings on trays
3. Bake in the oven
