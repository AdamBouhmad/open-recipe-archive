## Ingredients

- baking soda (4 ounces)
- cream of tartar (8 ounces)
- cornstarch (4 ounces)

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients very well in dry form.
2. Store in a covered jar.
3. Use in the same way as commercial baking powder.
