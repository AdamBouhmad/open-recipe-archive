## Ingredients

- large potatoes
- butter
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- oil
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook large potatoes, peel and cut them into slices
2. Put in a pan one tablespoon of butter, add garlic and finely chopped parsley, salt and a teaspoon of oil
3. Put the potatoes in this, mixing them lightly so they do not break
4. Serve with plenty of Parmesan cheese powder
