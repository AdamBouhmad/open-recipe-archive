## Ingredients

- backbone
- salt
- one quarter ounce of pepitoria seeds
- one ounce sesame seeds
- some tomatillo
- two toasted French rolls
- Castilian pepper
- a small piece of cinnamon
- some green cilantro
- one guaque chile

## Directions

1. Cook the backbone with salt
2. Toast one quarter ounce of pepitoria and one ounce of sesame seeds, some tomatillo, about two toasted French rolls according to the amount of meat and grind all with Castilian pepper, a small piece of cinnamon, a little green cilantro, one guaque chile
3. Thin with the same broth
4. Fry and add the backbone
