## Ingredients

- Pork loin, parboiled and chopped
- Lard or butter
- Garlic
- Onion
- Green chile, chopped
- Tomato
- Chorizo
- Ham
- Chopped parsley
- Almonds
- Capers
- Hard-boiled egg
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the chopped parboiled pork loin in lard or butter with garlic, onion, chopped green chile, and tomato
2. Add to this mixture the chorizo, ham, chopped parsley, almonds, capers, hard-boiled egg, a little oil, vinegar, and a little wine
3. Add a bit of sugar if desired, otherwise only acid
4. Butterfly the loin and fill it with this mixture, sewing it with long stitches
5. Cook with water, a glass of red wine or sherry, and a tablespoon of butter
6. When well cooked, serve with a sauce made as follows: fry in oil or butter garlic, chopped green chile, roasted tomato, and ground capers; when fried, add pepper powder, salt, a small piece of French bread fried in lard, a bit of vinegar, and water so that the sauce comes out thin
7. Serve cold or hot
