## Ingredients

- One liter of cooked milk
- Ten fresh egg yolks
- Twelve ounces of sugar
- Two ounces of unsalted butter
- Two ounces of ground almond
- Half an ounce of vanilla
- Ice and two pounds of salt for the freezer

## Directions

1. Strain the cooked milk and put the ten egg yolks and twelve ounces of sugar into it
2. Add two ounces of unsalted butter
3. Strain and bring to a boil for ten minutes
4. Let cool
5. Add two ounces of ground almond and half an ounce of vanilla
6. Put in the ice cream maker covered with ice and two pounds of salt
7. Turn until the ice creams are well set
8. Serve plain if desired, or with a tablespoon of strawberry ice cream on top, according to taste
9. Alternatively, use only four egg yolks and half an ounce of cornstarch per liter of milk for a more economical version, though less flavorful
