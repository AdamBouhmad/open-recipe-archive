## Ingredients

- 1 hen
- pork loin
- cooked ham cut in long strips
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Debone the hen as described previously
2. Chop a bit of pork loin with a piece of cooked ham cut in long strips
3. Add pepper powder, oregano powder, and a teaspoon of salt
4. Mix everything very well with the meat
5. Stuff the hen with this mixture
6. Wrap it and put it to cook with water, salt and plenty of oregano powder
7. After two hours, press it and serve cold
