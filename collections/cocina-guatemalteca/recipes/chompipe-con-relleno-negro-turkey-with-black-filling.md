## Ingredients

- Loin meat (4 libras)
- Ham (2 libras)
- Clove
- Castilian pepper
- Bay leaves
- Cinnamon
- Thyme
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Turkey

## Directions

1. Buy four libras of loin and two of ham and cut both meats into very thin pieces
2. Toast a quarter ounce of clove, the same amount of Castilian pepper, two bay leaves, a quarter ounce of cinnamon and a sprig of thyme
3. Once well toasted and ground with half an ounce of salt, dissolve in a cup of vinegar and add to the meat
4. Stir during the two days it sits in brine
5. On the third day, debone the turkey and fill it by alternating a layer of meat and a layer of ham until full
6. Sew with thick thread, wrap in cloth, cook and press as the previous recipes
