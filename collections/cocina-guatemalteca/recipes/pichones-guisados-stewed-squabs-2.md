## Ingredients

- squabs
- hot lard
- two garlic cloves
- two peeled and chopped tomatoes
- a little chopped parsley
- saffron
- salt
- pepper powder
- one spoonful of toasted flour

## Directions

1. Fry well-cleaned squabs in a pan with hot lard
2. When they take color, remove them
3. In the same fat, fry two garlic cloves, two peeled and chopped tomatoes, and a little chopped parsley
4. Place the squabs in a pot and pour this sauce over them
5. Cover with a little saffron, salt, pepper powder, and one spoonful of toasted flour
6. Let simmer on very gentle heat
7. If the squabs are tender, one hour of cooking is sufficient; if not, cook longer
