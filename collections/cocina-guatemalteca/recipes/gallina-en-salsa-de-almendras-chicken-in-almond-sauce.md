## Ingredients

- one chicken
- twenty roasted almonds
- four well-cooked egg yolks
- three roasted and seeded pasilla chiles
- three cloves
- about eight Castilian peppers
- one small piece of cinnamon
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind the roasted almonds, well-cooked egg yolks, roasted and seeded pasilla chiles with three cloves, about eight Castilian peppers and a small piece of cinnamon
2. Dissolve with the broth in which the chicken was cooked, fry in oil and add one tablespoon of vinegar
