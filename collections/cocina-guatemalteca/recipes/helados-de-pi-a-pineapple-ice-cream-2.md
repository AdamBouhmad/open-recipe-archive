## Ingredients

- One pineapple, preferably yellow
- One liter of water
- One pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Grind one pineapple, making sure it is yellow
2. Strain it with one liter of water and one pound of sugar
3. If too acidic, add more water
4. Put in the ice cream maker and set
5. The same is done with soursop ice cream, except beat it with the water very well and strain with the sugar like the previous ones
