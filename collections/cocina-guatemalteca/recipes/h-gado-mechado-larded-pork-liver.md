## Ingredients

- hard part of pork liver
- ham
- garlic
- pepper powder
- parsley
- salt
- butter
- hot sauce

## Directions

1. Take the hard part of the liver and lard with ham, garlic, pepper powder, and chopped parsley
2. Rub with salt and butter and bake in the oven
3. Serve with hot sauce
