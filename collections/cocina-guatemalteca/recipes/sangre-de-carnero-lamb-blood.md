## Ingredients

- Lamb blood
- Water
- Salt
- Fat pieces and scraps
- Lard
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic clove
- Hierbabuena (mint)
- Parsley
- Black pepper powder
- Pickled vegetables
- Capers
- Oregano powder

## Directions

1. Put all the fat pieces and scraps in water with salt
2. After one hour of boiling, add the lamb blood, stirring from the bottom so it does not stick
3. After ten minutes, remove and drain in a colander
4. Put in a pan on the fire with two spoonfuls of lard
5. Add plenty of peeled and minced tomato, onion, a garlic clove, hierbabuena, parsley, the finely minced blood, pickled vegetables, capers, black pepper powder, salt and oregano powder
