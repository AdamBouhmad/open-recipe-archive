## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound of sugar
- 1 pound of flour
- zest of 1 lemon
- egg whites as needed for a soft dough
- a pinch of salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of sugar on a clean table with one pound of flour, the zest of a lemon and enough egg whites to make a soft dough
2. Add a pinch of salt, knead and then roll out with the rolling pin
3. Place on a baking sheet in the oven, and when partially cooked coat with egg yolk and return to the oven
4. When baked, remove, cut to the desired size and return to the oven to brown
