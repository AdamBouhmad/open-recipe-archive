## Ingredients

- Tail
- Chickens or fat hen
- One sheep's head
- Green tender peas
- Green beans
- Chayote
- Carrots
- Potatoes
- Fried mixture
- Two tablespoons of flour browned in butter
- Chopped parsley
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Prepare a broth with tail, chickens or fat hen and one sheep's head
2. When all this has boiled for three hours remove the broth, strain it and put it back on the heat
3. Add green tender peas, green beans, chayote, carrots and potatoes, all very finely chopped
4. When everything is cooked add the fried mixture, two tablespoons of flour browned in butter, chopped parsley and pepper powder
