## Ingredients

- meat
- lard
- tomatillo
- one or two tomatoes
- sprig of thyme
- water
- salt
- potatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the meat in a little lard
2. Grind a bit of tomatillo, one or two tomatoes, and a sprig of thyme
3. Add this ground mixture to the meat with sufficient water to cook it
4. Add salt to taste
5. If desired, add peeled whole potatoes
6. Garnish with lettuce and serve hot
