## Ingredients

- meat
- lemon
- pepper
- salt
- onion, chopped
- lard, a little
- wine

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices and place in a dish with lemon, pepper, and salt
2. Let sit for three hours
3. Add chopped onion, a little lard, and wine
4. Place in the oven
