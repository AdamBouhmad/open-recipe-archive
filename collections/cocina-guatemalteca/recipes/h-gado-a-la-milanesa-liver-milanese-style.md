## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- pepper powder
- flour
- butter
- lettuce leaves
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and season it with salt and pepper powder
2. Coat it in flour and fry it in hot butter over very low heat
3. When golden, remove it and fry it on tender lettuce leaves and potatoes cooked plain
