## Ingredients

- Masa dough, well ground with salt
- Lard
- Pork meat, well fried
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped onion
- A little vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Three roasted tomatoes
- One guaque chile, fried
- Castilian peppercorns
- One teaspoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind the masa well with salt, form into tortillas neither thick nor thin.
2. Fry them in hot lard, remove when golden, and sprinkle lightly with water.
3. Prepare a mixture of well-fried pork meat with peeled tomato and chopped onion.
4. Season with a little vinegar, salt, and pepper powder.
5. Place the meat mixture on the tortilla to desired thickness.
6. Grind three roasted tomatoes with one fried guaque chile and some Castilian peppercorns.
7. Fry this sauce with salt and one teaspoon of vinegar.
8. Coat the enchiladas with this sauce and serve hot.
