## Ingredients

- pigeon meat
- bread soaked in milk
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- lard
- roasted onion
- roasted tomato
- parsley
- broth
- vegetables of all kinds
- peas
- chard

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of pigeon meat with a piece of bread soaked in milk, a little pepper, and nutmeg
2. Fry a little flour in lard and when it changes color add roasted onion, squeezed roasted tomato, and chopped parsley
3. When well fried, add sufficient broth, pepper powder, nutmeg, vegetables of all kinds, peas, and chard
4. Add the little pieces of meat gradually until cooked
