## Ingredients

- one glass of milk
- one pound of sugar
- three ounces of peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Sweeten one glass of milk with one pound of sugar
2. Add three ounces of peeled and ground almonds
3. Place over fire and let it reach the consistency of paste
