## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- salt
- almonds
- pepitoria (pumpkin seeds)
- Castilian pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- bread
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the fish in little water, in pieces or whole, with salt, taking care it does not break apart.
2. Toast one ounce of almonds and a little pumpkin seeds.
3. Grind everything with Castilian peppers, three cloves, a small piece of cinnamon and a small piece of fried bread in lard.
4. Dissolve everything in a little of the water in which the fish was cooked, seasoned with salt.
5. Add the fish and fry in one tablespoon of oil with a light boil.
