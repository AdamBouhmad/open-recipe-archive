## Ingredients

- Watercress
- Water
- Salt
- A little baking soda
- One tomato (optional)
- One onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Put a pot with water on the fire, salt, and a little baking soda
2. Prepare the watercress by removing all the stems
3. Wash it very well with hot water
4. Add it to the pot
5. Let it boil for ten minutes on even heat
6. Remove it immediately, because if it stays longer it will turn black
7. If desired, before removing it from the fire, add one halved tomato and one onion
