## Ingredients

- Plantains
- Lard
- Hot water
- White panela or four ounces of sugar
- Cinnamon strips
- White honey

## Directions

1. Fry the plantains as before, wash them one or two times with hot water to remove the fat
2. Place in a dish and cover with hot water, add a piece of crushed white panela, cinnamon strips, or if not four ounces of sugar instead of the panela
3. When it begins to set, add a small glass of white honey and let it caramelize
