## Ingredients

- very good broth
- vinegar
- pepper powder
- salt
- garlic
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put in a pot a quantity of very good broth, equal quantity of vinegar, pepper powder, salt, six or eight well-chopped garlic cloves, a bundle of parsley
2. Let boil until the garlic is well cooked
