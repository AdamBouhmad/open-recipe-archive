## Ingredients

- cooked tomato
- tomatillo
- oregano
- cumin
- Castilian peppercorns
- achiote
- guaque chile
- water
- butter
- kidney
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Grind cooked tomato and tomatillo with a little oregano, a little cumin, some Castilian peppercorns, a small piece of achiote and a guaque chile
2. Dissolve this with a little water and fry it in a little butter
3. Add the kidney chopped into pieces and let it boil for ten minutes
4. Serve hot with slices of cooked potatoes
