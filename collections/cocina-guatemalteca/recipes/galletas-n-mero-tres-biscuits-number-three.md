## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound of flour
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 ounces of ground sugar
- 2 ounces of butter
- 3 ounces of lard
- 1 level teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- 0.5 glass of milk cooked with plenty of cinnamon
- 2 ounces of chopped and peeled almonds

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of flour on a table with five yolks, five ounces of ground sugar, two ounces of butter, three ounces of lard, one level teaspoon of Royal baking powder, half a glass of milk cooked with plenty of cinnamon, and two ounces of chopped and peeled almonds
2. Knead very well, roll out with the rolling pin, cut the biscuits and with a stick form the little holes
3. Then place in the oven
