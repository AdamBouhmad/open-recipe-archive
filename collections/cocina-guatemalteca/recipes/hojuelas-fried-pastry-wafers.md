## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 5 eggs
- a little salt
- 1 teaspoon of butter
- butter for frying
- half water and half white honey for coating or half a pound of clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Make a well in one pound of flour
2. Place five eggs, a little salt, and one teaspoon of butter
3. If too stiff, add another egg
4. Knead for half an hour until the dough is very fine
5. Roll out with a rolling pin
6. Cut with a small mold in desired shapes
7. Fry in hot butter
8. Place on a platter with a coating of half water and half white honey, or if not, clarify half a pound of sugar; when at proper stage coat the wafers with this
