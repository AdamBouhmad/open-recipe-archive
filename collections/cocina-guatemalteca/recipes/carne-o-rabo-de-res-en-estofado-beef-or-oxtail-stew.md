## Ingredients

- Pieces of meat or oxtail
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Pepper powder
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Tomato
- Garlic (whole heads)
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Bread (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of meat or oxtail
2. In a pot with water, add salt, vinegar, lard, pepper powder, cloves, and cinnamon (if preferred, add whole spices and remove them before serving)
3. Chop tomato, garlic (whole heads), ham, and chorizo and add to the pot
4. Cook over low heat
5. If desired, let it reduce; if not, grind bread and serve juicy
