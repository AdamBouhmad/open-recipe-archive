## Ingredients

- 1 pound of butter
- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 ounce of cinnamon powder
- 6 eggs
- flour as needed
- zest of 1 lemon
- sugar for topping (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat one pound of butter with one of sugar until white
2. Add one ounce of cinnamon powder
3. Add six eggs and beat again for a long time
4. Add flour, as much as needed to achieve a consistency like buñuelo dough
5. Add the zest of a lemon and beat well again to achieve good results
6. Make small white paper boxes coated with butter
7. Place one small spoonful of the dough in each and bake in the oven
8. If desired, sprinkle sugar on top
