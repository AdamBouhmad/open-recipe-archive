## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper powder
- 4 teaspoons hot water
- egg whites
- butter
- optional toppings: chopped ham, cooked chicken mixed with cream, grated cheese, chopped onions, pieces of stewed meat, peas with fish or cream, or tomato slices

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks with salt to taste, a little pepper powder and four teaspoons of hot water
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and when well beaten, mix with the yolks
3. Grease a pan with butter; when very hot, pour in the mixture, put on low heat and when already golden on the bottom, put in the oven to brown on top
4. If desired, top with chopped ham, cooked chicken mixed with cream, grated cheese, chopped onions, pieces of stewed meat, peas with fish or cream, or tomato slices, all to taste
