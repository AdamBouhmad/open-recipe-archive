## Ingredients

- cooked pork tongues
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- lard
- vegetables or fried potatoes

## Directions

1. After the tongues are well cooked, remove them and coat in beaten egg
2. Then coat in bread crumbs
3. Fry in hot lard
4. When golden, serve with vegetables or fried potatoes
