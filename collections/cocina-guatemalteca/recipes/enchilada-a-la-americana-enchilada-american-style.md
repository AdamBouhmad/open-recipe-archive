## Ingredients

- Four dried pasilla chiles, seeded and soaked
- Four Castilian peppercorns
- Four cloves of spice
- A small piece of cinnamon
- Water
- One tablespoon of lard
- One pound of beef sirloin, partially cooked and chopped
- Peeled tomato
- Onion
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- One teaspoon of vinegar
- Thin tortillas
- Grated hard cheese
- Sliced radish
- Tender lettuce, chopped
- Whole lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Grind four dried pasilla chiles, seeded and soaked, with four Castilian peppercorns, four cloves of spice, and a small piece of cinnamon.
2. Mix this with a little water.
3. Fry in one tablespoon of lard.
4. Chop one pound of partially cooked beef sirloin and fry it with peeled tomato and onion.
5. Season with pepper powder, clove, cinnamon, and one teaspoon of vinegar.
6. Fill thin tortillas with this mixture, roll them into tubes, and thoroughly coat with the first sauce.
7. Arrange on a platter to maintain their shape.
8. Cover with the remaining sauce.
9. Sprinkle with grated hard cheese, radish slices, and chopped tender lettuce.
10. Surround the platter with whole lettuce leaves.
