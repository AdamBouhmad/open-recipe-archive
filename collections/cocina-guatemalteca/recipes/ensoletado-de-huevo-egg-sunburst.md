## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 4 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 0.5 cup milk
- ladyfingers (chiquiadores)
- honey
- dry sherry
- cream
- toasted and chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Clarify 1 pound of sugar and strain it
2. Return to the fire
3. When it reaches the point, lower the heat and let it cool
4. Then add 10 egg yolks
5. Return to the fire until it reaches the point of before (ante)
6. Add 4 ounces of peeled and ground almonds dissolved in 0.5 cup of milk
7. Cover the platter with ladyfingers soaked in honey and dry sherry
8. Put a layer of cream, then a layer of ladyfingers and alternate the layers
9. Try to make the last layer cream
10. Decorate with toasted and chopped almonds
