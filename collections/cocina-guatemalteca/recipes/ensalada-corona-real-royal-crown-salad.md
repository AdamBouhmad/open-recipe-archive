## Ingredients

- lettuce
- mayonnaise sauce
- green pepper slices
- carrot rounds
- green beans

## Directions

1. Wash a lettuce carefully so it does not fall apart
2. Cut the tip very straight so it can stand upright
3. Cover with mayonnaise sauce and decorate the whole with green pepper slices, round pieces of carrots and green beans
