## Ingredients

- butter
- onion
- parsley, chopped
- fish, clean
- salt
- pepper powder
- one cup clear broth

## Directions

1. Coat a cazuela (cooking pot) with butter, onion, and chopped parsley.
2. Place on the fire and when everything takes on color, add the clean fish with salt and a little pepper powder.
3. Add one cup of very clear broth.
4. Let it boil over two fires (double boiler) without the fish falling apart.
