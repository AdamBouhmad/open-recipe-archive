## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- oregano powder
- thyme powder
- bread
- Castilian pepper
- sherry
- vinegar
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, fry in oil.
2. Remove the fish.
3. In the same oil fry peeled tomato, onion and chopped parsley, oregano powder and thyme powder with a piece of fried bread ground fine, and some Castilian peppers.
4. Dissolve in a small glass of sherry and one of vinegar.
5. Add to the frying mixture.
6. Add olives and capers.
7. Bathe the fish with this sauce.
