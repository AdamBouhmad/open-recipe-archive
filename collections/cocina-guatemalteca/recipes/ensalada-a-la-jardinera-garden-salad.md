## Ingredients

- Carrot
- Chayote
- Green beans
- Beet
- Potato
- Onion
- Lettuce leaves
- Escarole
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Oil
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Chop the carrot very finely, chayote, green beans, and beet
2. Cook each vegetable separately with salt
3. Chop the potato very finely; once cooked, place it in a round platter with tender lettuce leaves
4. Then place a slice of carrot, a slice of green bean that should be very green, a slice of potato and one of beet; if the platter is round, arrange them in circles; if it is long, place the rows lengthwise, making sure they are very well arranged with varied colors
5. The day before, cooked escarole pieces should have been placed in vinegar and salt; these whole pieces, without losing their shape, are placed in the middle of the colors
6. Prepare one cup half water and half vinegar, and three spoonfuls of oil with salt and pepper powder
7. Cover the salad with this dressing and serve as desired
