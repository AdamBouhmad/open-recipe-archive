## Ingredients

- calf brains
- salt
- broth from boiled meat (optional)
- egg, beaten
- bread powder
- manteca (lard)
- tender lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Remove the brains with great care so they do not break
2. Place in a cloth with salt or, if not, in the broth from boiled meat
3. Once cooked, let all the water drain from them
4. Split and coat in beaten egg and then in bread powder
5. Fry in hot manteca over very low heat
6. When well browned, place on a tender lettuce leaf and serve with whatever you wish
