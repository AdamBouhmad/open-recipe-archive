## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Tomato
- Onion
- Lettuce
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Chop a piece of pineapple very well and place it in the salad bowl with half a cup of water and half vinegar, salt, pepper powder, peeled tomato and chopped onion
2. Place the lettuce, which should already be finely chopped, in this dressing
3. If desired, add a little sugar, according to the taste of the people
