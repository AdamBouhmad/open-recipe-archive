## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- four ounces butter
- four ounces bread flour or cornmeal
- canned asparagus tips
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs by boiling them for ten minutes, then place in cold water and peel.
2. Prepare a white sauce with one quarter glass of milk, four ounces of butter and four ounces of flour or cornmeal, adding the juice from a can of asparagus tips.
3. Place some white sauce in a baking dish, add the asparagus tips and cover with the remaining white sauce.
4. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and place in the oven to brown.
5. Arrange the whole or halved eggs around it.
