## Ingredients

- three cups of sugar
- three egg whites
- one pound of peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Place three cups of sugar and three egg whites in a container
2. Beat until it becomes hard
3. When well hardened, add one pound of peeled and ground almonds
