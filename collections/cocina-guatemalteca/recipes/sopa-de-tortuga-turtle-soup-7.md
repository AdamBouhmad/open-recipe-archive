## Ingredients

- carrot
- onion
- bay leaf
- tomato
- parsley
- beef pieces
- broth
- turtle broth
- flour
- Madeira wine
- meat juice
- lemon juice
- turtle

## Directions

1. In a saucepan put carrot, onion, bay leaf, tomato, parsley and some pieces of beef
2. Fill the saucepan with broth, using half turtle broth
3. Add a little flour and let everything cook for five hours
4. Strain this broth through a colander, adding some Madeira wine, meat juice and lemon juice
5. Cut the turtle into small pieces and add to the rest
