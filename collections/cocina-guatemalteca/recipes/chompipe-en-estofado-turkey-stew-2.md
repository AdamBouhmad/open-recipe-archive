## Ingredients

- one cleaned turkey
- four large tomatoes
- four to six onions
- one garlic head
- a quarter ounce of clove
- a quarter ounce of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and cut it into quarters or pieces of the desired size
2. Place in a pan and add four large peeled and halved tomatoes, four to six onions, one peeled and crushed garlic head, a quarter ounce of clove and a quarter ounce of cinnamon
