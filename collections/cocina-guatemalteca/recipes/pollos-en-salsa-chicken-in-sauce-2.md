## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- roasted tomatoes
- a little oregano
- a small piece of fried bread in lard
- 10 roasted almonds
- a handful of roasted and peeled melon seeds

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with salt
2. Grind roasted tomatoes with a little oregano and a small piece of bread fried in lard
3. Add 10 roasted almonds
4. Add a handful of roasted and peeled melon seeds
5. Dissolve this with the same broth
6. Add the chickens cut into quarters and serve hot
