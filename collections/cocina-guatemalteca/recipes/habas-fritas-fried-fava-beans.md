## Ingredients

- Green fava beans, cooked and peeled
- One small teaspoon lard
- Tomato
- Onion, chopped
- One small teaspoon grated white bread
- A little vinegar

## Directions

1. After cooking and peeling the green fava beans, fry them in a pan with one small teaspoon of lard, tomato and chopped onion
2. Add the fava beans with one small teaspoon of grated white bread and a little vinegar
3. Serve hot
