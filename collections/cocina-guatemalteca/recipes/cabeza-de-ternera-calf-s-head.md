## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salt
- parsley
- garlic
- ham
- pepper powder
- butter, melted
- water
- parsley bundle
- leeks
- orange leaves
- bay leaves
- dry sherry wine

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, split the head in half and sprinkle with salt
2. Chop a little parsley, very finely minced garlic, and cut ham into long thin strips
3. Mix with pepper powder, a little salt, and a tablespoon of melted butter
4. Stuff the head with this mixture, rubbing the outside with butter and salt
5. Place in a vessel and cover with water
6. Add a bundle of parsley, some leeks, some orange leaves, two bay leaves, salt, and half a bottle of dry sherry
