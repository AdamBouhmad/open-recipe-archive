## Ingredients

- One glass of water
- Two glasses of whisky or Old Tom
- One small glass of Angostura bitters
- A few drops of lemon
- Sugar, very white and sifted
- Lemon peels

## Directions

1. Put one glass of water and two glasses of whisky or Old Tom in a glass
2. Add one small glass of Angostura bitters and a few drops of lemon
3. Moisten the small glasses with the same cocktail or pass them over a little very white and sifted sugar
4. Serve the cocktails in these glasses with their lemon peels
