## Ingredients

- a piece of cold meat
- butter or lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cold meat, coat with butter or lard and salt
2. Place in the oven on a roasting pan and baste with its own juice constantly, avoiding that it dries out
3. Serve with tender lettuces and potatoes in white
