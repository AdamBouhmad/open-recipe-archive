## Ingredients

- Loin
- Garlic
- Pepper
- A small piece of ginger
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Chorizo
- Ham
- Chile
- Pickles
- Oregano powder
- Thyme
- Clove
- Pepper
- Lemon slices
- Vegetables
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Tenderize the loin very well
2. Grind garlic, pepper, and a small piece of ginger
3. Dissolve this in vinegar with salt
4. Rub the loin with this mixture
5. Mince chorizo, ham, chile, and pickles
6. Fill the loin with this mixture
7. Roll it up well and sew with twine
8. Place in a pot with vinegar, garlic, crushed ginger, oregano powder, thyme, clove, pepper, and some lemon slices
9. Ensure the meat is well covered with vinegar
10. Cover well and after three days put to cook
11. When cold, serve with vegetables, olives, and capers
