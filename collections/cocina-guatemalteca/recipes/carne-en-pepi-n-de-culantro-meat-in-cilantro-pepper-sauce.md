## Ingredients

- Beef or pork
- Salt
- Cabbage
- Güisquil
- Green beans
- Pacayas
- Miltomate
- Rice, toasted
- Chile zambo or other chile
- Onion head, roasted
- Garlic clove
- Chiapa pepper
- Castilian pepper
- Plantain skin, burned (for color, optional)
- Cilantro
- Water

## Directions

1. If using beef, cook rib and flank cut into pieces with salt
2. When the meat is halfway cooked, add cabbage, güisquil, green beans, and pacayas, all cut into small pieces
3. Grind miltomate and toasted rice, chile zambo or other chile, a roasted onion head, a clove of garlic, and a bit of Chiapa and Castilian pepper
4. If desired, give it color with a small piece of burned plantain skin
5. Dissolve this in cold water and grind in a bit of fresh cilantro
6. Let it boil for half an hour so the rice cooks well and ensure it is not too thick
7. Can be served with white rice soup
