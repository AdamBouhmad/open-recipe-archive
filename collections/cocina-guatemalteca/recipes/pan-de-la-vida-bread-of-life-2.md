## Ingredients

- two pounds of common bread dough
- half a pound of sugar
- seven egg yolks
- four egg whites
- half a pound of lard or butter
- four tablespoons of yeast

## Directions

1. Weigh two pounds of common bread dough and add half a pound of sugar, seven egg yolks, four egg whites beaten as for nougat and half a pound of lard or butter
2. Knead with four tablespoons of yeast
3. Knead until it blisters
4. Put in greased baking dishes and place in the oven
5. This is prepared at night and put in the oven the next day
