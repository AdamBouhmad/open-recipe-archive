## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Lettuce leaves
- Onion
- Water

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage very finely
2. Cook it with salt so that it does not fall apart
3. Prepare in a bowl a dressing of oil and vinegar with salt and pepper powder
4. Place the cabbage in this dressing
5. Decorate with green lettuce leaves and onion slices that have been blanched in boiling water
