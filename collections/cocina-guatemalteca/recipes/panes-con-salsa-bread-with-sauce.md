## Ingredients

- four roasted tomatoes
- one guaque chile cooked or one pasilla chile fried
- one sprig of thyme
- oil
- salt
- meat (ham, goose liver, or other previous varieties)
- cold loin, pork leg, or sausage (optional)

## Directions

1. Grind four roasted tomatoes with one cooked guaque chile or one fried pasilla chile and one sprig of thyme
2. Fry in one spoonful of oil
3. Season with salt
4. Spread one spoonful of this sauce on bread
5. Add a slice of any of the previous meats, or if desired, a slice of cold loin, pork leg, or sausage
