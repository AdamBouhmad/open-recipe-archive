## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- salt
- half an ounce of saltpeter
- 1 bottle of vinegar
- cloves
- pepper
- bay leaf
- basil
- thyme
- onion
- garlic
- leeks
- orange leaves
- bitter orange juice
- sweet orange juice
- water or red wine and sherry
- egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the leg well with salt and half an ounce of saltpeter
2. Place in a dish with vinegar, cloves, pepper, bay leaf, basil, thyme, onion, crushed garlic, leeks, orange leaves, bitter and sweet orange juice
3. Cover with water or red wine and sherry
4. On the second day, place over heat for three hours
5. When done, coat with beaten egg mixed with flour and place in the oven to brown
