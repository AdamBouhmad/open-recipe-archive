## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pineapple, ground
- six egg yolks
- one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar
2. When it reaches the proper stage, add one ground pineapple
3. Let boil until it takes on the consistency of preserve (punto de conserva)
4. Remove and when cold, add six egg yolks and one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
5. When it reaches the high stage (punto alto), place in the oven to brown
