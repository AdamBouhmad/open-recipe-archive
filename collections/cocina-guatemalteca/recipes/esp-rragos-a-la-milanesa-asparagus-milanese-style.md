## Ingredients

- asparagus
- one large tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon breadcrumb flour
- water
- salt
- nutmeg powder
- pepper

## Directions

1. Lightly fry the asparagus in one large tablespoon of butter, ensuring they remain very whole, and remove them and place on the platter
2. In the same butter fry one tablespoon of breadcrumb flour, add water, stirring so it does not become lumpy
3. Season with salt, nutmeg powder and pepper
4. Cover the asparagus with this sauce
