## Ingredients

- tender onion stalks
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the tender onion stalks into pieces
2. Blanch them lightly
3. Squeeze them very well so they have no water
4. Mix them in beaten egg
5. Fry them in hot butter like the previous recipe
