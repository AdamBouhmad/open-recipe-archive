## Ingredients

- rice
- lard
- garlic clove
- ripe tomato
- saffron water
- achiote

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well without soaking and remove excess water
2. For one pound of rice use half a pound of lard and one garlic clove
3. Fry very well without browning
4. When fried add a piece of ripe tomato and then cover with saffron water
5. Make on very low heat so the rice comes out fluffy
