## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- Lard
- Tomato, peeled
- Onion, chopped
- Bread flour
- Water
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with salt
2. Peel them and cut into slices
3. Fry in a pan with very little lard, peeled tomato and chopped onion
4. Dissolve a teaspoon of bread flour
5. Add to the mixture with water so that a thin sauce forms
6. Add the potatoes
7. Serve hot with chopped parsley
