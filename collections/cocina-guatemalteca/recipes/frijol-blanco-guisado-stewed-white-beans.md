## Ingredients

- White beans
- Cold water
- Boiling water
- Garlic head with salt
- One tablespoon lard
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped onion
- Two or three tablespoons grated white bread powder
- Castilian peppercorns

## Directions

1. Put the beans in cold water and after boiling for one hour on even heat, remove that water
2. Add boiling water with a head of garlic and salt
3. When cooked, remove the beans
4. Fry in one tablespoon of lard, peeled tomato and chopped onion
5. When fried, add a little of the water in which the beans cooked and two or three tablespoons of grated white bread powder mixed with some Castilian peppercorns
6. Serve hot
