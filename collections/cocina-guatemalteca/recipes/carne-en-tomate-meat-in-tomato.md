## Ingredients

- meat sliced
- oil
- two tomatoes, chopped
- one onion
- minced garlic
- white wine, sherry or red wine
- pepper powder
- cinnamon
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Slice the meat and coat with oil and place in a dish
2. Add two chopped tomatoes, one onion, minced garlic, white wine, sherry or red wine, pepper powder, cinnamon, salt and water to cover the meat
3. When it has boiled, remove everything except the tomato
