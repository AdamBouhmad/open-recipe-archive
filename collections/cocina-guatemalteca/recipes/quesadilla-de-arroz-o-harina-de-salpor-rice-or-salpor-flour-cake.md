## Ingredients

- flour, rice, salpor, or bread flour (1 pound)
- butter or lard (1 pound)
- salt (a pinch, if butter is unsalted)
- powdered sugar (10 ounces)
- eggs (9)

## Directions

1. Work 1 pound of flour with 1 pound of butter or lard until well combined.
2. If the butter lacks salt, add a small amount.
3. Let rest for half an hour.
4. After this time, mix in 10 ounces of powdered sugar.
5. Add the 9 eggs one by one, working the mixture well with each addition.
6. After everything is very well worked, place in small greased baking dishes.
7. If desired, sprinkle a little flour on top before placing in the oven.
8. Bake in the oven.
