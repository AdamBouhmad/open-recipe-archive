## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter, oil, or lard
- a piece of tender meat
- onion sliced very thin
- two bay leaves
- a sprig of thyme
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan on the fire with one tablespoon of butter, oil, or lard
2. In this fry a piece of tender meat and add a quantity of very thinly sliced onion
3. Add two bay leaves and a sprig of thyme with salt to taste
4. When well fried, cover with water
5. Seal the saucepan hermetically and place on very low heat
6. Shake the saucepan without opening it
