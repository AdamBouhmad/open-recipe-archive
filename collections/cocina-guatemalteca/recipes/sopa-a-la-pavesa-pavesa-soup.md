## Ingredients

- Good broth, preferably chicken
- French bread slices
- Butter
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a good broth, preferably chicken broth.
2. Fry slices of French bread in butter.
3. Place them in special pans for this purpose.
4. Cover with the broth and place two eggs on each one, being careful that they do not break but come out whole.
