## Ingredients

- macarrones (macaroni)
- sal (salt)
- mantequilla (butter)
- polvo de queso (grated cheese)

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni with salt
2. Butter a pan
3. Place alternately a layer of grated cheese and a layer of macaroni, continuing until finished
4. Top with another layer of butter
5. Place in the oven or over two fires for ten minutes
