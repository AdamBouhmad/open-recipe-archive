## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 1 pound sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon potato starch or cornstarch
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 packet gelatin dissolved in cold water or 6 sheets isinglass dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan on the fire with one liter of milk and one pound of sugar and let it boil until it thickens
2. Remove from heat and when it is fried, add eight beaten egg yolks and one teaspoon of potato starch or cornstarch dissolved in one cup of milk and one teaspoon of vanilla
3. Put back on the fire with a small packet of gelatin dissolved in cold water or six sheets of isinglass dissolved in hot water
4. Strain and put in a mold to set
