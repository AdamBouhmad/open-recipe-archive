## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Sugar
- Butter
- Rum

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs (whites and yolks) with a small teaspoon of sugar.
2. Form the omelette and fry in hot butter.
3. Fold it and cover with white sugar.
4. Pour a glass of rum over it.
5. Set it on fire and serve burning. This can be served as a pancake.
