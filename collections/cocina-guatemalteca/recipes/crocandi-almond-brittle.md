## Ingredients

- One pound of brown sugar
- One teaspoon of butter
- Half pound of almonds
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Place one pound of brown sugar with one teaspoon of butter on heat
2. Peel and toast half pound of almonds, then chop them
3. When the sugar no longer shows grains, add the almonds
4. Remove from heat and pour onto a buttered board
5. Shape and cut into desired figures
