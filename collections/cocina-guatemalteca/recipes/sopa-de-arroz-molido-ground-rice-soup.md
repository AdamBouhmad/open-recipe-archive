## Ingredients

- three ounces of rice (for ten persons)
- three eggs
- one small glass of milk
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- one clove of garlic
- broth
- pepper powder
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Soak three ounces of rice for two hours in water
2. Remove and let it air dry
3. Grind it very finely
4. Mix in three eggs (white and yolk), one small glass of milk, and salt
5. Make fritters of desired size and fry them
6. Fry tomato, onion, and one clove of garlic
7. Add broth and pepper powder to this fried mixture
8. Add the fritters to the broth
9. When it comes to a boil, add chopped parsley
10. Serve hot
