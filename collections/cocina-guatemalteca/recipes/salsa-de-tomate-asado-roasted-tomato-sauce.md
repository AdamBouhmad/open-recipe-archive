## Ingredients

- three or four tomatoes
- garlic clove
- cumin
- oregano
- pepper
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Roast three or four tomatoes, peel and chop very finely
2. grind one garlic clove and some cumin, oregano, pepper
3. add water, salt, a little vinegar and fry in a hot spoonful of oil
4. if desired it can be served cold
