## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- two bay leaves
- water
- oil
- lard
- lettuce
- tomatoes
- two dried chiles, seeded

## Directions

1. Season the pork loin with salt, pepper, clove and two bay leaves.
2. The next day, add a glass of water to it and, with the pot well covered, cook it.
3. When well cooked, fry it in oil and lard.
4. When golden, place it on a platter, either whole if desired or in slices.
5. Garnish with lettuce.
6. Fry some well-chopped tomatoes and grind two seeded dried chiles.
7. Dissolve the chiles with a little of the broth in which the loin was cooked and cover the meat with this sauce to serve hot.
