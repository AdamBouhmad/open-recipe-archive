## Ingredients

- peas
- two cooked tomatoes
- one garlic clove
- oregano
- one dried cooked chile
- a small piece of achiote
- one piece of French bread soaked
- pork loin

## Directions

1. Cook the peas as in previous recipes
2. Grind two cooked tomatoes, one garlic clove, a little oregano, one dried cooked chile, a small piece of achiote, and one piece of soaked French bread
3. Dissolve this mixture with the peas' cooking broth, fry it, and add the cooked peas
4. If desired, add pieces of cooked pork loin
5. Serve hot
