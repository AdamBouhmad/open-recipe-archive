## Ingredients

- thick carrots
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- sardines
- egg
- hot lard
- white bread
- achiote
- vinegar
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cook thick carrots and once cooked, peel them and scoop out enough to fill them
2. Add salt while cooking
3. Prepare a fried mixture of tomato, onion, and chopped parsley fried in a little oil
4. Add sardines to this mixture and use it to stuff the carrots, covering with the scooped out part
5. If desired, wrap in beaten egg and fry in hot lard
6. Serve with a sauce made from chopped tomato and onion, well fried, with a teaspoon of white bread and a little achiote, leaving a thin sauce
7. Alternatively, place the carrots in a pan with one tablespoon of vinegar, a little salt, and a bay leaf, cover and heat in the oven to keep warm
8. Serve with chopped parsley
