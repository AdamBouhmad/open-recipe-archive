## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- water
- bicarbonate of soda
- good bone
- salt
- chayote or squash
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- sweet pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas one day before in cold water with a teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda.
2. The next day, add to a pot or cook with a piece of good bone and salt.
3. Cut chayote or squash into small pieces.
4. Place a layer of chickpeas in a pot, then a layer of chayote.
5. Cover with a fried mixture of tomato, onion, garlic, sweet pepper, and broth.
6. Cook over low heat.
