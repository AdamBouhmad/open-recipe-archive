## Ingredients

- ichintal
- salt
- two eggs, beaten
- tomato, cooked
- one head of onion, peeled and scored
- one dry green chile
- lard

## Directions

1. Peel the ichintal and cook with salt
2. Remove and dry
3. Wrap in beaten egg (for ten slices of ichintal, beat two eggs)
4. Prepare a sauce of cooked tomato with one peeled onion head scored and one dry green chile
5. After wrapping the ichintal in beaten egg, fry in hot lard
