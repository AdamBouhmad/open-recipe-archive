## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one and a half tablespoons of beer yeast
- three and a half eggs
- half a glass of warm milk
- seven more eggs
- six ounces of melted butter
- two ounces of ground sugar
- butter for greasing molds
- clarified honey at the thread stage
- plenty of cognac

## Directions

1. Put one pound of flour with one and a half tablespoons of beer yeast, add three eggs and half a glass of warm milk
2. One by one mix in seven more eggs with six ounces of melted butter and two ounces of ground sugar
3. Knead until it becomes very white
4. Place in small molds greased with butter in the oven
5. When cooked, remove and give them a bath in honey at the thread stage mixed with plenty of cognac
