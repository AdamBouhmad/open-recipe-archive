## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- flour
- lard
- pig's feet
- onion
- salt
- one cup of broth

## Directions

1. Split the artichokes in half
2. Place them to cook with salt
3. Remove the spines, drain them, coat them in flour and fry them in lard
4. Separately cook pig's feet with onion and salt
5. When the artichokes are fried add the pig's feet and one cup of broth and let them boil together
6. If it is too thin add a little flour
