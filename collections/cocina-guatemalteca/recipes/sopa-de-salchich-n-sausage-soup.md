## Ingredients

- ham, lean from the leg
- peeled and chopped tomato
- onion
- garlic
- broth
- green beans
- fava beans
- güisquil squash
- carrot
- peas
- flour
- butter
- cooked sausage

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of ham
2. When fried, add peeled and chopped tomato, onion, and garlic
3. Have a good broth prepared
4. Add this seasoning with green beans, fava beans, güisquil, carrot, and tender peas, which should be chopped and parboiled in advance
5. Add a tablespoon of flour fried in butter
6. Serve in a tureen with slices of cooked sausage
