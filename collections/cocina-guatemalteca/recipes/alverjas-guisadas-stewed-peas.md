## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- bicarbonate of soda
- longaniza sausage
- chorizo
- one tablespoon oil
- one garlic clove
- one onion
- one peeled and chopped tomato
- a small piece of fried French bread
- two Castilian peppers
- one clove

## Directions

1. Cook the peas with salt and a little bicarbonate of soda
2. Once cooked, remove the peas
3. In the same water, cook longaniza and chorizo cut into slices
4. Fry one tablespoon of oil with one garlic clove, one onion, and one peeled and chopped tomato
5. Once fried, add the peas, chorizo, and longaniza
6. Grind a small piece of fried French bread with two Castilian peppers and one clove
7. Dissolve this with a little water, add to the stew, and let it come to a boil before serving
