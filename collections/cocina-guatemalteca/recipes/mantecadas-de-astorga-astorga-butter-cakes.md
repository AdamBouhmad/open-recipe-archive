## Ingredients

- Half pound of sugar
- Half pound of butter
- Half pound of peeled, toasted, and ground almonds
- A bit of ammonium bicarbonate or Royal baking powder
- Twelve ounces of toasted flour
- One egg white, well beaten

## Directions

1. Mix everything very well
2. Place in paper boxes or chat shapes on baking sheets
3. Top with well-beaten egg white
4. Place in the oven
