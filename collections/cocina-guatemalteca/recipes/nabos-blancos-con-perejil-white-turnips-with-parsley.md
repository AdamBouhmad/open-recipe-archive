## Ingredients

- turnips
- salt
- one tablespoon oil
- garlic cloves
- parsley
- one clove
- a small piece of cinnamon
- a small piece of fried French bread
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips, cut them into pieces, and cook with salt
2. Once cooked, remove them
3. Heat one tablespoon of oil in a pan, add peeled garlic cloves, and remove once they turn golden
4. Grind a little parsley with one clove, a small piece of cinnamon, and a small piece of fried French bread
5. Dissolve this with water and a teaspoon of vinegar, add to the oil
6. Add the turnips to the mixture, let it come to a boil, and serve hot
