## Ingredients

- sweet potato, yellow
- milk, cooked
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the yellow sweet potato, strain and weigh it
2. For one pound of sweet potato, use one and a half pounds of sugar; the sweet potato dissolved in two glasses of cooked milk
3. Place on the fire to reach the paste stage
