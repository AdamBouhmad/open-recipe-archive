## Ingredients

- A good piece of meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Water
- One cup of vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Oregano
- Clove
- A small piece of ginger
- Herbs salad or potato salad

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of meat and beat it with a mallet
2. Add salt, pepper, and clove
3. Place in a press for one night
4. The next day put to cook with water, one cup of vinegar, pepper powder, oregano, clove, and a small piece of ginger
5. When cooked, place in a press again
6. The next day cut into slices
7. Serve with herb salad or potato salad
