## Ingredients

- lomo de res (beef loin)
- marrano (pork)
- pimienta (pepper)
- jengibre (ginger)
- tomillo (thyme)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)
- rebanada de mantequilla (slice of butter)
- copita de vino tinto (small glass of red wine)
- mantequilla (butter)
- caldo (broth)
- mostaza (mustard)

## Directions

1. Grind equal quantities of beef loin and pork
2. Add small amounts of pepper and ginger, the same of thyme, egg yolks, a slice of butter and a small glass of red wine
3. Make small pieces and place in a buttered dish with red wine
4. Cook over fire with a little broth and serve with mustard
