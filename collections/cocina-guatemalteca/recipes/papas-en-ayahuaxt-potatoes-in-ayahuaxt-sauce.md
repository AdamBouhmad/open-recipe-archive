## Ingredients

- Pepitoria, half an ounce
- Chiapa peppers
- Castilian peppers
- Tortilla or tamalito, cold
- Water
- Salt
- Potatoes, cooked and chopped
- Cooked tomatillo
- Bread, soaked
- Cooked string beans, izote flower, cauliflower (optional)
- Castilian pepper and cinnamon (alternative)

## Directions

1. Toast half an ounce of pepitoria
2. Grind with some Chiapa peppers and some Castilian peppers, a cold tortilla or tamalito
3. Dissolve with a little salted water
4. Add the cooked and chopped potatoes
5. Serve cold
6. If you want it hot, fry the mixture grinding in a little cooked tomatillo and a piece of soaked bread
7. To this you can also add cooked string beans, izote flower, cauliflower, all with the same mixture
8. If you prefer, grind only Castilian pepper and cinnamon without the Chiapa, with a little spice to taste
