## Ingredients

- calf brains
- salt
- red wine
- water
- thyme sprig
- onions (two)
- bay leaf
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the brains with great care so they come out whole
2. Place in a serving dish and cover with equal parts water and red wine, adding salt, a sprig of thyme, two onions, and a bay leaf
3. Let the brains cook in this
4. In hot butter fry a tablespoon of flour and add the broth in which the brain cooked, making sure it becomes a thin sauce
5. Add the brain in slices to this sauce, let it come to a light boil, and serve hot
