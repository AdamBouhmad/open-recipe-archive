## Ingredients

- Raw squabs
- Salt
- Lard or butter
- 1 glass of white wine
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- 4 to 6 cloves of garlic
- 2 ounces of ground and blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Fry the raw squabs with salt in lard or butter
2. When they are fried, add a glass of white wine, clove, pepper and cinnamon
3. Let them boil for half an hour if they are tender, or longer if not
4. In a separate pan with a little lard, fry 4 to 6 cloves of garlic until golden and remove them
5. Add to the butter about 2 ounces of ground and blanched almonds
6. Place the squabs in this sauce to serve hot
