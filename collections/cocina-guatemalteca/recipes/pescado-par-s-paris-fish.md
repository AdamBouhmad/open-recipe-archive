## Ingredients

- fish
- vinegar
- salt
- oregano
- pepper powder
- bay leaves
- egg
- oil

## Directions

1. Slice the cleaned fish and place in a serving dish with vinegar, salt, oregano, pepper powder, and bay leaves.
2. The next day, remove the fish and coat in beaten egg (white and yolk).
3. Fry in hot oil.
