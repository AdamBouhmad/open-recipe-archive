## Ingredients

- three pounds corn dough
- one glass clean cooked milk
- one pound melted lard
- salt and sugar to taste
- small candies (gragea)
- raisins
- blanched almonds
- dobladores (wrappers, clean and white)

## Directions

1. Cook three pounds of corn dough as for tamales
2. Sift and grind twice more dry
3. Add one glass of clean cooked milk and one pound of melted lard
4. Add salt and sugar to taste
5. Knead for half an hour until it sounds well-beaten
6. Have ready clean, white, well-washed wrappers
7. Put a little gragea on each wrapper, then two separate spoonfuls of dough on top; put one raisin in one spoonful and half a blanched almond in the other
8. Wrap and tie very tightly so the tamale comes out very round
9. Cut the ends of the wrapper
10. Cook in a very clean pot with a good base of clean wrappers so they do not burn
