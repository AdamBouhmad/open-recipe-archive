## Ingredients

- butter
- bread flour
- broth
- pepper powder
- salt
- mustard powder or French mustard

## Directions

1. Put a spoonful of butter in a pan, add a spoonful of bread flour
2. when well browned, add the necessary broth so it becomes a thin sauce
3. add pepper powder and salt
4. let it boil ten minutes, remove and mix in one spoonful of mustard powder or French mustard, according to taste
