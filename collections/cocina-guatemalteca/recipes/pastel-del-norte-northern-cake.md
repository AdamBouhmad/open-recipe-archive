## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds flour
- 0.5 pound butter or melted lard
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 1 small glass of cognac
- 9 eggs, beaten like for nougat
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. To 1.5 pounds of flour add 0.5 pound of melted butter or lard, 0.5 pound of sugar, 1 small glass of cognac, 9 eggs beaten like for nougat, and 1 teaspoon of Royal
2. Place in greased molds and bake in the oven
