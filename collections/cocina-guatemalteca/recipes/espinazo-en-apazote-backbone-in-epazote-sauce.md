## Ingredients

- split backbone
- salt
- two cooked tomatoes
- peppers
- a small piece of achiote
- one dried chile
- fine cornmeal
- broth
- washed epazote branch

## Directions

1. Cook the split backbone with salt
2. Grind two cooked tomatoes with peppers, a small piece of achiote and a dried chile
3. Thicken with a little finely ground cornmeal and thin with a little broth
4. Add to the backbone with a branch of epazote washed in boiling water
