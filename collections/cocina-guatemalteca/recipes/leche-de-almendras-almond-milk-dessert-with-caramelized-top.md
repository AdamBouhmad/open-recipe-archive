## Ingredients

- 3 glasses milk
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 2 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 ounces peeled and toasted almonds, finely chopped
- powdered sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Place milk, sugar, peeled and ground almonds, and egg yolks over fire
2. When it begins to thicken, add the peeled and toasted chopped almonds
3. When it reaches proper consistency, pour into a platter
4. Once cold, sprinkle with very dry powdered sugar
5. Using a very hot iron and cinch, caramelize the top
