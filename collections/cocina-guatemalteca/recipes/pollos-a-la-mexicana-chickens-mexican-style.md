## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- French bread
- milk
- liver and gizzard of the chickens cooked
- ham
- cooked brain
- butter
- nutmeg powder
- thyme powder
- tomato slices
- onion slices

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with salt
2. Soak French bread in milk and chop it with the cooked liver and gizzard of the chickens, a piece of ham and a cooked brain, chopping carefully so it does not fall apart
3. Fry all this in a spoonful of butter
4. Add nutmeg powder and thyme powder
5. Use this mixture to stuff the chicken
6. Rub with butter and salt
7. Place in the oven with tomato and onion slices
