## Ingredients

- six hard egg yolks
- three red bell peppers
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Chop six hard egg yolks very finely, three red bell peppers, parsley and onion
2. mix everything with salt, some spoonfuls of vinegar and two spoonfuls of oil
