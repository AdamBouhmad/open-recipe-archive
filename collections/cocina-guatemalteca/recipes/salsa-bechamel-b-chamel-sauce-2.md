## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons bread flour
- pepper powder
- nutmeg
- one bay leaf
- one tablespoon vinegar
- salt to taste
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place the butter and bread flour in a pan and stir until it becomes a cream
2. Add pepper powder, nutmeg, and a bay leaf
3. Add one tablespoon of vinegar and salt to taste
4. Remove from heat and add two egg yolks before serving
