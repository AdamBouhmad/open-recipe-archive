## Ingredients

- kidney
- flour
- salt
- pepper powder
- onion
- parsley
- white wine

## Directions

1. Well wash the kidney and cut it into very thin strips
2. Coat the strips in flour with salt, pepper powder, onion and chopped parsley
3. Add one cup of white wine, let it boil and serve immediately
