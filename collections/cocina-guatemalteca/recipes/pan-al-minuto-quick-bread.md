## Ingredients

- six ounces of flour
- one cup of milk
- one pound of flour
- three ounces of butter
- four ounces of melted lard
- four eggs
- one full teaspoon of Royal

## Directions

1. Put six ounces of flour in one cup of milk, one pound of flour
2. Knead very well and add three ounces of butter and four ounces of melted lard and four eggs
3. Knead until it sounds beaten, add one full teaspoon of Royal
4. Put in greased molds in a very hot oven
5. If you wish to make it simpler, use only two ounces of butter and four ounces of lard
