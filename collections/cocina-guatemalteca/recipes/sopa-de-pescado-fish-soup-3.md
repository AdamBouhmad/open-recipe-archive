## Ingredients

- carrots
- onions
- sufficient oil
- four to six sprigs of parsley
- two bay leaves
- one head of garlic crushed
- salt
- pepper powder
- pieces of fish
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut carrots into very thin slices and onions
2. Fry with sufficient oil, add four to six sprigs of parsley, two bay leaves and one crushed head of garlic
3. Add sufficient salted water and pepper powder
4. When it has boiled enough, strain through a colander and add pieces of fish (if dried fish, soak it in water the night before)
5. Toast bread, place in the soup bowl and pour this thin broth over it
