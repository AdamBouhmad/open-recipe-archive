## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sugar
- One pineapple, ground
- Six egg yolks, well beaten
- Half pound of ground almonds
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar
2. When it reaches the proper point, add the ground pineapple
3. When preserved, remove and let cool
4. Mix in six well-beaten egg yolks and half a pound of ground almonds
5. Return to heat until it reaches the consistency of ante
6. Place on a platter and in the oven to brown
