## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- four cloves of garlic
- two tomatoes
- parsley
- salt
- pepper powder
- saffron water
- toasted flour

## Directions

1. If tender, brown them whole; if not, in pieces
2. In the same lard, brown four cloves of garlic
3. When they take on color, add two chopped and peeled tomatoes, chopped parsley, and salt
4. Add pepper powder, a little saffron water and a spoonful of toasted flour
