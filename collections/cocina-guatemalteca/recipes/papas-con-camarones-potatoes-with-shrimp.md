## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- butter
- tomato
- onion
- annatto
- shrimp
- cheese
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with salt, peel and break them well
2. Put them in a pan with butter, tomato and chopped onion and a light color of annatto
3. Have two ounces of shrimp peeled and cooked for each pound of potatoes
4. When everything is well fried, add plenty of cheese
5. Serve with chopped parsley
6. They are also suitable for traveling and can be eaten cold
