## Ingredients

- bananas
- one half ounce of butter per banana
- one ounce of sugar per banana
- one tablespoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Place the bananas in a dish
2. Open them in the middle without splitting them completely
3. Put to each banana one half ounce of butter and one of sugar and one tablespoon of vanilla
4. If there is an oven place them in the oven, if not in gentle heat
