## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- tomatoes
- half bottle oil
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Soak and cook the cod as previous recipes
2. Roast a quantity of tomatoes on a griddle
3. Remove, peel and remove all seeds, then roughly chop the tomato
4. Place in half a bottle of very hot oil with a little pepper powder and the cod
5. Strain the tomato water and add to this to boil until it reduces
