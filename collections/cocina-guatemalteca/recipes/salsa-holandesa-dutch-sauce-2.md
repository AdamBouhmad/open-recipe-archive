## Ingredients

- oil
- tomatoes
- onions
- oregano
- cloves
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put in a pan a large spoonful of oil, add four whole tomatoes, four onions
2. After boiling five minutes, grind the tomato with oregano and two cloves and return to the oil with sufficient water and a bit of vinegar
