## Ingredients

- loin with rib attached
- salt
- pepper powder
- egg
- bread powder
- lard
- lettuce
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Keep the loin attached to the rib
2. Cut the bones about a hand's length long
3. Flatten with a mallet
4. Season with salt and pepper powder
5. Coat in beaten egg and then in bread powder
6. Fry in hot lard
7. Serve on very tender lettuce leaves with spicy sauce
