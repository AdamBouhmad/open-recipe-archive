## Ingredients

- granadillas
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified per ten granadillas
- coconut and pineapple paste
- fish roe
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Peel the granadillas and remove everything inside, making sure not a single seed remains
2. After washing very well, put them on the fire in boiling water, turning them on all sides so they blanch well
3. Remove and place in cold water
4. Have clarified one and a half pounds of sugar for ten granadillas
5. Put the granadillas in the syrup until they heat through
6. If desired serve in the syrup; if not, remove and when all the honey has drained, fill with coconut and pineapple paste or fish roe, sprinkling cinnamon powder on top
