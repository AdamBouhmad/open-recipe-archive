## Ingredients

- hard part of liver
- ham
- garlic
- pepper powder
- minced parsley
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Take the hard part of the liver and stuff it with ham, garlic, pepper powder and minced parsley
2. Rub with salt and butter
3. Put in the oven
4. Serve with spicy sauce
