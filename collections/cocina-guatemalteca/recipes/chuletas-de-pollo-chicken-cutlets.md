## Ingredients

- very tender chickens
- lemon
- salt
- pepper
- egg, beaten
- bread crumbs
- lard or butter, hot
- lettuce
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Clean very tender chickens and cut into pieces
2. Place in a brine of lemon, salt, and pepper
3. Pass the pieces through beaten egg and then through bread crumbs
4. Fry in hot lard or butter
5. Garnish with lettuce and chopped parsley
