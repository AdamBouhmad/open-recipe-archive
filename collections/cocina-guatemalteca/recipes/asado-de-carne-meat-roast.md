## Ingredients

- carne (meat)
- agua (water)
- ajo (garlic)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- cebolla (onion)
- tomate (tomato)

## Directions

1. Slice the meat and fry it, being careful not to brown too much
2. Add water, mashed garlic clove and a little vinegar
3. When tender, add raw onion in small pieces and raw tomato
