## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of flour
- one pound of ground and sifted sugar
- eight egg yolks and four egg whites
- six ounces of butter
- two cloves
- one ounce of cinnamon powder
- zest of one lemon
- one teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- chopped almonds
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one and a half pounds of flour, one pound of ground and sifted sugar, eight egg yolks and four egg whites.
2. Add six ounces of butter, two cloves, one ounce of cinnamon powder, and lemon zest.
3. Work well with one teaspoon of Royal baking powder.
4. Roll out with a rolling pin and cut with a mold that has a little hole.
5. Place on greased baking sheets.
6. Sprinkle chopped almonds and granulated sugar on top and bake.
