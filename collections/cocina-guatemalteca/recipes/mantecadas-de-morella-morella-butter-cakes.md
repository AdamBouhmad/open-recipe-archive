## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of lard
- half a pound of peeled and toasted almonds
- a little ammonium bicarbonate or one teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- twelve ounces of toasted bread flour

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar, lard, toasted almonds, baking powder, and toasted flour
2. If too soft, add two more ounces of flour
3. Place in buttered molds or paper boxes
4. Bake in the oven
