## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Salt
- Black pepper powder
- White bread powder
- Lard
- Rice
- Miltomate sauce, spicy

## Directions

1. Split the sweetbreads in half, being careful they come out very whole
2. Rub with salt and black pepper powder
3. Let sit for a quarter hour
4. Coat in white bread powder and fry in lard over very low heat
5. When golden, remove and serve with dry rice and a spicy miltomate sauce
