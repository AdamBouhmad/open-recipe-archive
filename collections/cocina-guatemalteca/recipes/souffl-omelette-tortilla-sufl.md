## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Sugar
- Cognac
- Butter

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs, the yolks with one ounce of sugar and a small glass of cognac.
2. Beat both things well and then combine them.
3. Form the omelette and fry in hot butter.
