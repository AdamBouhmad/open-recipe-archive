## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- eight egg yolks
- one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
- butter
- clarified sugar at medium stage
- one glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites and eight egg yolks separately
2. When both are well beaten, combine them
3. Add one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
4. Place in a buttered mold and pour the mixture into it
5. Bake in the oven or in a water bath (bain-marie)
6. When baked, remove and place in another clean mold
7. Have the sugar clarified and at medium stage with one glass of sherry, coat the cake with this mixture and return to the oven
