## Ingredients

- meat prepared as before
- butter
- chile
- two roasted tomatoes
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as in the previous recipe
2. Make meatballs of desired size and fry them
3. When done, remove them and in the same butter add a chile that has been ground beforehand with two roasted tomatoes
4. Season with salt and a little vinegar and put the meatballs in to give two boils
5. Then serve hot
