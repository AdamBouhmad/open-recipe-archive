## Ingredients

- chicken or fat poultry broth
- rice
- oil or lard
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- saffron water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Heat the chicken or poultry broth in advance
2. Fry the rice well in oil or lard
3. Add the fried rice to the broth
4. Remove the chicken and leave the poultry pieces
5. Add the fried seasoning, pepper powder, and saffron water
6. Cook on very low heat without stirring so the rice comes out whole
7. Just before serving, add a little vinegar
