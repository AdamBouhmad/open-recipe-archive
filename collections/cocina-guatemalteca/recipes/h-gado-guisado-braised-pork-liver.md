## Ingredients

- cooked pork liver
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- cooked ham
- pepper powder
- salt
- vinegar
- cooked potato slices

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked liver and fry with tomato, onion, parsley, chopped garlic, and cooked ham
2. Add pepper powder, salt, and a tablespoon of vinegar
3. Serve with slices of cooked potato
