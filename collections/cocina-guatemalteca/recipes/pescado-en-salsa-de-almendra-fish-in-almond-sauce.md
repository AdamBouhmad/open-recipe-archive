## Ingredients

- four to six garlic cloves
- oil
- parsley
- green chile
- one ounce peeled and toasted almond
- french bread, golden in butter
- water
- pepper powder
- clove
- cinnamon
- fish, clean
- chopped olives
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown four to six garlic cloves in oil.
2. Chop parsley and a green chile and fry with the garlic.
3. Grind one ounce of peeled and toasted almond with a piece of french bread golden in butter.
4. Dissolve with a little water and add to the frying mixture with a little pepper powder, clove, and cinnamon in small quantity.
5. Place the very clean fish on top with some chopped olives and a little vinegar.
6. Let it boil over two fires.
7. When serving, remove the garlic cloves.
