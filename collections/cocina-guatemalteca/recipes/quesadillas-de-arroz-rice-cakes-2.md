## Ingredients

- One pound of butter
- One pound of rice flour or salpor flour
- One pound of ground sugar
- One tablespoon of lard
- Two whole eggs
- Salt to taste
- Nine egg yolks
- Nine egg whites
- Ground sugar and flour for topping (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat one pound of butter with one pound of rice flour and one pound of ground sugar until very well beaten
2. Add one tablespoon of lard with two whole eggs; add salt if needed
3. Beat for half an hour
4. Add nine egg yolks and beat five minutes more
5. Fold in nine well-beaten egg whites gently, stirring very well but not kneading
6. Pour into greased small dishes
7. Place in the oven; if desired, sprinkle with ground sugar and flour on top
