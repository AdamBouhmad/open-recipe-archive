## Ingredients

- young squash
- salt
- fine masa
- piece of achiote
- Castilian peppercorns
- chile
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Chop the young squash into small squares and cook with salt
2. Grind a little very fine masa with a piece of achiote, some Castilian peppercorns and chile to taste
3. Dissolve this with the same water and add to the pot where the squash is
4. Add a sprig of cilantro and serve hot
