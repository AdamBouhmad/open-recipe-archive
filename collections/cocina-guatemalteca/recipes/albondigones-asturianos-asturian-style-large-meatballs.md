## Ingredients

- meat ground and prepared
- a little sherry
- one hard boiled egg
- capers
- almonds
- olives
- green chile or pickled chile
- a few potatoes
- bread powder
- butter
- onion
- white wine
- tomatoes
- salt
- vinegar
- tender lettuces
- fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Grind and prepare the meat as in previous recipes, but add a little sherry
2. Chop one hard boiled egg, capers, almonds and olives; also add green or pickled chile and a few potatoes, all well mixed
3. Spread out the meat to desired size, place all this chopped filling in the middle, cover and form into a ball shape, either round or long
4. Coat with bread powder, fry in very hot butter over low heat
5. When fried, remove and place on a platter with tender lettuces and very thin slices of fried potatoes
6. Prepare a sauce as follows: in very hot butter, well chopped onion with a little white wine, water, salt, vinegar, two roasted and ground tomatoes, and serve
