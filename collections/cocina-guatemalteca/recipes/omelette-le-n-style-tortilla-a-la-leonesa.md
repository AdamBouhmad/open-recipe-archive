## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter
- Fresh cheese
- Parsley
- Tomatoes
- Chile de pasa
- Water
- Or alternatively: cooked raisins, butter, salmon, sardines, ground meat, or sausage slices

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs according to the number of persons (white and yolk together).
2. Season with salt.
3. Fry in very hot butter and turn over with great lightness.
4. When fried, remove it and without letting it cool, cover completely with plenty of fresh cheese.
5. Roll it up and serve with chopped parsley.
6. Alternatively, grind two roasted tomatoes with a seeded and soaked chile de pasa.
7. Fry with about two tablespoons of water and salt and place this sauce around the egg.
8. If desired, cover the omelette instead of cheese with cooked raisins fried in butter, salmon, sardines, chopped meat or very thin slices of sausage.
