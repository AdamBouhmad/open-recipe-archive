## Ingredients

- Potatoes, raw or cooked
- Pork, cooked and chopped
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic clove, chopped and peeled
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Bread powder, half a spoon
- Salt
- Butter
- Cheese or cooked brains mixed with chopped parsley and pepper powder
- Egg

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and open a hole in them
2. Prepare the filling with cooked and chopped pork, fried with tomato, onion, a chopped and peeled garlic clove
3. Add a little vinegar, pepper powder and half a spoon of white bread powder
4. Fill the potatoes with this mixture, seasoned with salt
5. Cover the hole with a little potato from what was removed
6. Spread with butter and salt and place in the oven
7. They can be filled with cheese, or with cooked brains mixed with chopped parsley and pepper powder
8. If the potatoes are cooked they can be fried, and if not, wrapped in egg to serve with fried tomato sauce
