## Ingredients

- thin slices French bread
- hen giblets
- hazelnuts
- hen broth
- bay leaf
- oregano
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- chorizo
- brains
- egg

## Directions

1. Fry thin slices of French bread, mince the giblets of a hen that will have been well cooked, and place in a pan alternately one layer of bread and one of liver, making sure the last layer is bread
2. Peel about twenty hazelnuts, dissolve them in a little of the hen broth and cover the soup with this, making sure the broth and hazelnut mixture boils first with a bay leaf and a bit of oregano powder
3. Let the soup boil over two fires and when serving, garnish with hard-boiled egg slices and chopped parsley, chorizo slices and brain slices breaded in egg
