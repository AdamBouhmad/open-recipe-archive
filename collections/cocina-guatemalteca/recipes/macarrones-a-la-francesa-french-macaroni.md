## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- water
- gruyère or parmesan cheese, grated
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in salted water
2. When cooked, place in a strainer to drain
3. Place in a platter
4. Add a little grated gruyère or parmesan cheese and mix well
5. Add bread powder and another little amount of cheese powder
6. Put in the oven
