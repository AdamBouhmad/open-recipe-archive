## Ingredients

- leg of pork
- vinegar
- salt
- beer, one liter
- tomatoes, raw and ground

## Directions

1. Take a leg of pork and rub with vinegar and salt
2. Let sit overnight
3. The next day, add one liter of beer and bring to a boil
4. When it has boiled sufficiently, add ground raw tomatoes
5. Serve hot
