## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Garlic
- Oil or lard
- Onion
- Green chile
- Three tomatoes
- Parsley
- Pepper powder
- Water
- Two tablespoons of milk (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the chopped cabbage with salt and plenty of garlic
2. When cooked, remove and let it drain
3. Fry in one tablespoon of oil or lard the onion and chopped green chile
4. Add three peeled and roasted tomatoes and parsley, all chopped, with pepper powder and a little water
5. Add the cabbage to this mixture
6. If desired, add two tablespoons of milk
7. Serve hot
