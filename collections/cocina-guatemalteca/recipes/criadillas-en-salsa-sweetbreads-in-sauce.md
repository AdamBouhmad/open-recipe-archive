## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- tomato
- thyme
- dried chile
- vinegar
- water
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook as above and cut into slices
2. Grind some raw tomatoes with a sprig of thyme and a seeded and soaked dried chile
3. Dilute with a teaspoon of vinegar and water
4. Fry in one tablespoon of oil
5. When it has boiled sufficiently add the sweetbread slices
6. Serve hot
