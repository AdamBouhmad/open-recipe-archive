## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Salt
- Onion, thin slices, abundant
- French mustard, one teaspoon (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the first leaves, cook in water with salt.
2. Once cooked, place in cold water.
3. Prepare oil mixed with equal parts water and vinegar, pepper powder and salt.
4. Place the artichokes in this with abundant thin onion slices.
5. If desired, add one teaspoon of French mustard.
