## Ingredients

- cooked pig's liver
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- minced garlic
- cooked ham
- pepper powder
- salt
- one tablespoon vinegar
- cooked potato slices

## Directions

1. Mince the cooked liver and fry it with tomato, onion, parsley, minced garlic and cooked ham
2. Add pepper powder, salt, one tablespoon of vinegar
3. Serve with slices of cooked potato
