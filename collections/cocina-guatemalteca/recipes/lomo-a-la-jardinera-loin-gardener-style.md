## Ingredients

- cooked loin
- tomato
- onion
- broth
- mixed vegetables, chopped and cooked
- ground white bread
- pepper
- achiote
- green chiles

## Directions

1. After cooking the loin, cut it as before and fry
2. Fry tomato and onion; when well fried, add some broth, the loin, and all kinds of chopped and cooked vegetables
3. Add ground white bread with pepper and a small piece of achiote
4. Add green chiles and serve hot
