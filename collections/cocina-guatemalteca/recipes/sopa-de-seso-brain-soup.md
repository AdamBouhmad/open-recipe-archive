## Ingredients

- chard
- brain
- beef tail or hip bone
- dry grated cheese
- pepper powder
- flour
- lard
- garlic
- parsley
- onion
- tomato
- red wine
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cook chard and brain in sufficient beef tail or hip bone
2. When the brain is cooked, remove it and let it drain well
3. Drain the chard and dry it very well with a cloth
4. Grind the chard and mix well with the brain
5. Add grated dry cheese and pepper powder
6. Form balls wrapped in flour
7. Fry in lard without browning and place in the tureen
8. Fry well-crushed garlic in lard, remove it, and in the same lard fry chopped parsley, chopped onion, and two or three ground tomatoes
9. Add sufficient broth and pour into the tureen with the balls
10. Also add pieces of fried bread and grated cheese; if desired, a little red wine
