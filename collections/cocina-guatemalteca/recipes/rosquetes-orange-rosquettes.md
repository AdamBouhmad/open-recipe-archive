## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- twelve egg yolks
- two egg whites
- two sweet oranges, juiced
- three ounces butter

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients very well and let rest for a moment.
2. Form rings of one ounce each, make an opening in the middle with a sharp knife, put in a hot oven, and coat with stage honey.
