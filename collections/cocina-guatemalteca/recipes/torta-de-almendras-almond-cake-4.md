## Ingredients

- half a pound of peeled almonds
- seven egg whites
- egg yolks
- marquesote powder or egg bread
- butter
- sweet bread powder
- one pound of clarified sugar
- a small glass of dry sherry

## Directions

1. Grind half a pound of peeled almonds dry
2. Beat seven egg whites until very stiff
3. Mix in the egg yolks, almonds, and marquesote or egg bread powder
4. Coat a mold with butter and sweet bread powder so it does not stick
5. Pour the batter into the mold and place in the oven
6. When cooked, add one pound of clarified sugar at the right point with a small glass of sherry
7. Pour over the cake and leave
8. Serve cold the next day
