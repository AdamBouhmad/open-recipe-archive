## Ingredients

- Cecina (dried meat), cut into pieces, not fatty
- Cabbage
- Salt
- Three roasted tomatoes
- One soaked and seeded pasilla chile
- One tablespoon of lard
- Two onions
- One cup of water
- One tablespoon of white bread powder

## Directions

1. Cook the cecina pieces
2. Cook the chopped cabbage separately with salt
3. Grind three roasted tomatoes with one soaked and seeded pasilla chile
4. Fry two finely chopped onions in one tablespoon of lard
5. Add the ground tomato mixture, one cup of water, salt, and one tablespoon of white bread powder
6. Add the cecina and cabbage
7. Let it boil a few times and serve hot
