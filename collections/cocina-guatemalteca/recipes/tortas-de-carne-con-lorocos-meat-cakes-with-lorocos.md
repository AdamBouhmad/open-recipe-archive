## Ingredients

- raw meat
- fried seasoning
- breadcrumb powder
- lorocos
- salt
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the raw meat, using half beef and half pork if desired
2. Add fried seasoning, one teaspoon of breadcrumb powder and the lorocos which should have been cooked with salt beforehand
3. Mix in some raw eggs according to the quantity of meat
4. Season everything with salt
5. Form the small cakes
6. Fry them in hot butter
