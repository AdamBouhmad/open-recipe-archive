## Ingredients

- Eight egg yolks
- Six ounces of well-ground sugar
- Six beaten egg whites
- Half pound of ground almonds
- Six ounces of well-sifted starch
- Half teaspoon of Royal

## Directions

1. Place egg yolks with six ounces of well-ground sugar
2. Beat for half an hour
3. Mix in six beaten egg whites and half pound of ground almonds, six ounces of well-sifted starch, and half teaspoon of Royal
4. Pour into a well-buttered mold
5. Put in a hot oven
