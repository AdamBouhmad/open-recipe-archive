## Ingredients

- leg of pork
- vinegar
- salt
- ground tomatillos
- ground onion
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the leg the same way as the previous recipe
2. Grind plenty of tomatillos and onion
3. Leave the leg in this infusion for three days
4. Then put it to cook, adding water in small quantity as needed
5. Take care to turn it frequently until it is cooked
6. Serve hot
