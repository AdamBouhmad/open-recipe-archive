## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Water
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Peel large tomatoes and remove the seeds
2. Place in the salad bowl a layer of tomatoes and one of onion slices blanched in boiling water; alternate the layers this way, making sure the last one is of onion
3. Cover with chopped parsley and a little water, vinegar, two spoonfuls of oil, salt and pepper powder
