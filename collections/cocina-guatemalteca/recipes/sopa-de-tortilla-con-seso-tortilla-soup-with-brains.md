## Ingredients

- roasted tomato
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- grated dry cheese
- pepper
- cooked brains
- tortillas
- lard
- salt
- 1 clove garlic
- 4-5 cooked tomatoes
- oregano

## Directions

1. Fry roasted tomato, onion and parsley; add one tablespoon of oil, grated dry cheese and pepper powder
2. Drop thin slices of previously cooked and well-cleaned brains into this
3. Have tortillas fried in lard with salt and in the same lard fry one minced clove of garlic, onion, about four or five cooked and ground tomatoes
4. Add pepper powder, oregano and one tablespoon of oil; soak the tortillas in this sauce and arrange them alternately in a dish, one layer of tortillas and one of brain slices, until finished
5. Cover with the sauce in which the brains were cooked and place over two fires
