## Ingredients

- beef leg
- beef meat
- Castilian pepper
- saffron
- egg yolks
- nutmeg powder
- salt
- flour
- broth
- red wine

## Directions

1. Cook a beef leg until well done, debone it and chop finely
2. Grind one pound of beef meat with a little Castilian pepper and a little saffron
3. Mix the ground meat with the leg
4. Add three egg yolks, nutmeg powder, and salt
5. Mix everything well and form balls wrapped in flour
6. Drop them into a good broth prepared in advance
7. In the tureen, add three well-cooked and ground egg yolks and a little red wine
