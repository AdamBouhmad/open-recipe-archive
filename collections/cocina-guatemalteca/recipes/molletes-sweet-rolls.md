## Ingredients

- four pounds of flour
- twelve egg yolks and six egg whites (or six whole eggs and six yolks)
- twelve ounces of ground sugar
- half pound of butter
- one pound of yeast
- one glass of Muscatel wine
- milk as needed

## Directions

1. Mix four pounds of flour with twelve egg yolks and six egg whites (or six whole eggs and six yolks).
2. Add twelve ounces of ground sugar, half pound of butter, and one pound of yeast.
3. Add one glass of Muscatel wine and as much milk as needed to make a soft dough.
4. Form the molletes.
5. When they rise, place in a gentle oven.
