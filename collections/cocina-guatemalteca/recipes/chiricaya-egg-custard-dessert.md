## Ingredients

- 3 glasses cooked milk
- 12 egg yolks, strained
- 4 ounces sugar
- cinnamon strips

## Directions

1. Strain twelve egg yolks into three glasses of cooked milk with sugar and cinnamon strips
2. Pour into a large china cup or mold
3. Place in the oven or in a water bath, which consists of placing clean water in a vessel and introducing the mold while being careful no water enters it, covering with metal and placing coals on top
4. As soon as it browns, remove the heat and let it simmer for two hours over even fire
5. Insert a knife and if it comes out clean, it can be served either cold or hot
