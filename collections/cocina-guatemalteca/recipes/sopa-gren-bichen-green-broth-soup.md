## Ingredients

- flour
- egg
- butter, one tablespoon
- good chicken broth or with beef leg
- green beans, parboiled
- carrot, parboiled
- peas, parboiled
- potato, sufficient
- ground tomato
- onion
- garlic, crushed and fried
- parsley, chopped
- flour, toasted and fried in butter
- pork meat, finely chopped
- chicken
- sweet pepper, chopped

## Directions

1. Knead a little flour with egg and one tablespoon of butter, let it rest
2. Have a good chicken broth ready, if desired with beef leg
3. To this broth add parboiled vegetables: green beans, carrot, peas, sufficient potato, ground tomato, onion, crushed and fried garlic, chopped parsley
4. If it is too thin, add a little flour toasted and fried in butter
5. Roll out the dough very thin and cut into small squares
6. Fill with finely chopped pork meat or chicken with its seasoning fried
7. Seal the edges very well and just before serving drop them into the broth, letting them boil only once
8. Add chopped sweet pepper to the broth
9. The small empanadas can also be filled with brains, mushrooms or vegetables
10. Alternatively, filled ravioli cooked the same way and wrapped in egg can be used instead
