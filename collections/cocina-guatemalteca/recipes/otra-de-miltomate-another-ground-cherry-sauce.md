## Ingredients

- miltomate
- chile chiltepe
- cumin
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- onion

## Directions

1. Toast the miltomate and grind with a quantity of chile chiltepe and a little cumin
2. dissolve with a little water and one spoonful of vinegar
3. add salt and plenty of chopped onion
