## Ingredients

- Tortillas
- Pork meat, partially cooked
- Vinegar, one tablespoon
- Pepper powder
- Clove powder
- Salt
- Tender pacaya buds in vinegar
- Olives
- Capers
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Fry the tortillas with salt, or if not made from masa as in the previous recipes, fry them and remove them.
2. Mince some partially cooked pork meat; fry it halfway, adding one tablespoon of vinegar, pepper powder and clove, seasoned with salt.
3. Cover the enchiladas with this meat, add tender pacaya buds in vinegar, olives, capers and slices of hard-boiled egg.
