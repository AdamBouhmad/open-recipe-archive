## Ingredients

- meat sliced like for steak
- butter or oil
- peeled tomato, cut into pieces
- garlic, thinly minced
- parsley
- pepper powder
- a little water
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat butter or oil
2. Add the meat with the peeled tomato in pieces, thinly minced garlic, parsley, pepper powder, a little water and salt
