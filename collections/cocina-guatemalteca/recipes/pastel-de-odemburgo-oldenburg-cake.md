## Ingredients

- Twelve egg whites
- Twelve egg yolks
- Half pound of butter
- Half pound of powdered sugar
- One pound of sifted flour
- One full teaspoon of Royal
- One teaspoon of vanilla
- Lemon zest if desired
- Raisins
- Chopped almonds
- Sweet bread powder

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites as for nougat
2. Beat twelve egg yolks separately with half pound of butter and half pound of powdered sugar
3. When everything is well beaten, combine the two mixtures
4. Add one pound of sifted flour, one teaspoon of Royal, one teaspoon of vanilla, lemon zest if desired, raisins, and chopped almonds
5. Pour into a mold buttered and dusted with sweet bread powder
6. Put in a hot oven
7. When it rises, cover with paper and after ten minutes insert a knife; if it comes out clean it is done
8. If for tea, serve plain; otherwise serve with syrup or chocolate cream
