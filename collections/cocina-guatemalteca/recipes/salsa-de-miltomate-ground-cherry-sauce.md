## Ingredients

- miltomate
- cumin
- Castilian peppercorns
- French mustard
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook a quantity of miltomate, grind with a little cumin, some Castilian peppercorns, a small piece of French mustard fried in butter
2. dissolve with vinegar and fry in a small spoonful of butter or margarine
