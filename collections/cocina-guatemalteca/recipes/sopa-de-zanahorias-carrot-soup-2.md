## Ingredients

- Four ounces of flour
- Three egg yolks
- Water with salt
- One tablespoon of butter or lard
- Lard for frying
- Cheese, grated
- Young chayote
- Well-cooked carrots
- Green peas
- Well-prepared broth
- Pepper
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. To four ounces of flour add three egg yolks, a little water with salt and one tablespoon of butter or lard
2. Knead very well for a quarter hour and let rest for another quarter hour
3. After this, stretch the dough with a rolling pin and cut out shapes with a mold as desired
4. Brown in hot lard
5. In the soup tureen place a layer of tartaritas, one of grated cheese, one of tender chayote and well-cooked carrots, another of tartaritas and another of peas
6. Pour very well-prepared broth over it and add pepper and very finely chopped parsley
