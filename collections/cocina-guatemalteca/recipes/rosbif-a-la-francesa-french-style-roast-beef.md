## Ingredients

- a very tender piece of meat
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- minced parsley
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Take a very tender piece of meat and season a little oil with salt, pepper and minced parsley
2. Coat the loin with this, place in a dish in the oven with two bay leaves, basting it constantly with its juice
