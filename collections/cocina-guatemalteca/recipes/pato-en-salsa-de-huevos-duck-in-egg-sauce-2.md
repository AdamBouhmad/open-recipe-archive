## Ingredients

- Cleaned duck
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon of lard or butter
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 2 onions
- A little parsley
- 3 roasted tomatoes
- About 10 Castilian peppers
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 piece of bread fried in butter
- Broth in which the duck was cooked

## Directions

1. After the duck is well cleaned, cook it with salt
2. Once cooked, remove it and place it in a pan on the fire with a tablespoon of lard or butter
3. Chop 2 cloves of garlic, 2 onions and a little parsley and add to the butter
4. Grind, for one duck, 3 roasted tomatoes, about 10 Castilian peppers, 3 hard-boiled egg yolks and a piece of bread fried in butter
5. Dissolve everything in the same broth in which the duck was cooked
