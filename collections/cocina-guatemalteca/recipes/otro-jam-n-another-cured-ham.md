## Ingredients

- 1 ham leg
- salt
- saltpeter
- bay leaves
- parsley
- other herbs

## Directions

1. Proceed as in the previous recipe, except leave it in the open air for three nights, rubbing it during the day with salt and saltpeter
2. After three days, cook it with the same herbs as the previous recipes for the same length of time
