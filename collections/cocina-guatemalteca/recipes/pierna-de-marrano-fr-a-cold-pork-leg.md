## Ingredients

- 1 boned pork leg
- 1 pound salt
- 1/2 ounce saltpeter
- pepper powder
- cloves
- bay leaves
- thyme
- oregano
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1 tablespoon salt
- water

## Directions

1. Bone the leg and rub it with 1 pound of salt and 1/2 ounce of saltpeter until incorporated
2. Press it for one night
3. The next day place it in a wide pan and sprinkle with pepper powder, cloves, bay leaves, thyme and oregano, all in powder form
4. Keep it this way for three days, turning it daily
5. On the fourth day, cook it in a pan with 1 bottle of red wine and 1 more tablespoon of salt, covered with water
6. Let it boil for three hours, turning it over
