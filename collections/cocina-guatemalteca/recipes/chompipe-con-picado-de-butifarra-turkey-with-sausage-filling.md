## Ingredients

- Pork meat (5 libras)
- Salt
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Turkey

## Directions

1. Grind five libras of meat and add half an ounce of salt, a quarter ounce of cinnamon, the same amount of clove, the same amount of pepper, a glass of vinegar and let sit for two days, stirring to ensure even color
2. After two days, debone the turkey, cook and press the same as the previous recipe
