## Ingredients

- oil
- six roasted and ground tomatoes
- one garlic clove
- pepper powder
- parsley
- capers, chopped
- cooked and chopped onions
- salt
- one tablespoon flour, fried in butter
- water
- fish, clean
- one teaspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry six roasted and ground tomatoes in oil with one garlic clove.
2. Add pepper powder, parsley, chopped capers, cooked and chopped onions, salt, and one tablespoon of flour fried in butter.
3. Add a little water.
4. Place the clean fish in this and let it boil for half an hour.
5. When serving, add one teaspoon of vinegar.
