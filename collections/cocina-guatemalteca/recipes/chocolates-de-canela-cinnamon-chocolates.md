## Ingredients

- three pounds of cacao
- five pounds of dry sugar
- three ounces of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Toast three pounds of cacao and peel it, then weigh it; it will yield two and a half pounds
2. Add five pounds of dry sugar
3. Grind three ounces of cinnamon first and when well ground wrap it in paper
4. Then grind the sugar and cacao well mixed together
5. When very well ground mix in the cinnamon
6. Give it two more passes
7. Then remove the chocolate and weigh by pound
8. Form it by hand and make eight chocolates from each half pound
9. Cover them and the next day store them
