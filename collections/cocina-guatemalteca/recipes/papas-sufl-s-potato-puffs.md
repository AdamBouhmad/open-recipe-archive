## Ingredients

- large potatoes
- oil
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel large potatoes and cut them into slices the thickness of chalk
2. Have very hot oil in a pan and put the potatoes in it
3. As soon as they are soft without browning, remove them
4. Have plenty of hot butter in a serving dish
5. Put the potatoes in after they have been five minutes in the air
6. Make sure the potatoes do not touch so they will puff up
