## Ingredients

- butter or margarine
- onion
- water
- potatoes
- salt
- bay leaves
- pepper
- parsley
- cheese

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion in butter or margarine
2. Add water and put in the peeled potatoes with salt and two bay leaves
3. When cooked, remove with great care
4. Serve with pepper powder, chopped parsley and grated cheese
