## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- spearmint
- garlic cloves
- green chile
- oregano powder
- pepper powder
- parsley
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop together plenty of spearmint, two garlic cloves and green chile
2. Mix with oregano powder, pepper powder, chopped parsley and salt
3. Lard the leg on all sides with this mixture
4. Rub with salt and vinegar
5. Place in the oven for three hours
