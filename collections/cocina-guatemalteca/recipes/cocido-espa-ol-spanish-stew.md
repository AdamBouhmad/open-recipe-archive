## Ingredients

- one good hen broth
- one pound rib
- two beef heels
- four ounces ham
- two or three red chorizos
- half pound chickpeas (soaked since the day before)
- four white turnips
- three or four carrots
- chopped cabbage
- handful of washed rice
- sufficient tomatoes
- four or five onion heads
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare a good hen broth and add one pound rib, two beef heels, four ounces ham, and two or three red chorizos
2. Boil until everything is cooked
3. Strain the broth and finely chop all the meat
4. Return the meat to the strained broth and add half pound chickpeas that have been soaked since the day before
5. Add sufficient water until everything is cooked
6. Add four white turnips and three or four carrots cut into pieces and cooked, along with chopped cabbage and a handful of washed rice
7. Roast a sufficient amount of tomato, then grind it
8. Slice four or five onion heads and fry them with the tomato in a bit of lard
9. Add this to the stew
10. Let it boil for ten or fifteen minutes and serve hot
