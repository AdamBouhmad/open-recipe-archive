## Ingredients

- macarrones (macaroni)
- leche (milk)
- mantequilla (butter)

## Directions

1. Lightly parboil the macaroni
2. When parboiled, place in a glass of milk with two tablespoons of butter
3. Let boil until the macaroni finishes cooking in the milk
