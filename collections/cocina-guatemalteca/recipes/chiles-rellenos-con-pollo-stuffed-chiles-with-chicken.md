## Ingredients

- Green guaque chiles
- Chicken
- Salt
- One tablespoon of lard or butter
- One peeled and chopped tomato
- One garlic clove
- One onion head, chopped
- One teaspoon of vinegar
- One teaspoon of white bread powder
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as in the previous recipe
2. Cook the chicken with salt
3. When cooked, chop all the meat into small pieces
4. Put one tablespoon of lard or butter in a pan
5. Add one peeled and chopped tomato, one garlic clove, and one chopped onion head
6. Add the chicken with one teaspoon of vinegar, one teaspoon of white bread powder, salt, and a little pepper powder
7. Fill the chiles with this mixture
8. Wrap them in beaten egg
9. Fry in hot lard and serve like the previous recipe
