## Ingredients

- Apricots (a quantity)
- Sugar
- Apollinaris (one bottle)
- Maraschino (a little)
- White wine (one bottle)
- Champagne (one bottle)
- Ice
- Grapes or cherries

## Directions

1. Cook a quantity of apricots.
2. When cooked and cold, strain them.
3. Add sugar according to the quantity desired.
4. Add one bottle of Apollinaris, a little maraschino, one bottle of white wine, and one of champagne.
5. Add pieces of ice and serve with grapes or cherries.
