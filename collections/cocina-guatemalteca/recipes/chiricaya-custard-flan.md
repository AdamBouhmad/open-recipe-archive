## Ingredients

- sugar
- white honey
- one glass of cream
- five egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Burn a little sugar and mix it with white honey, use this to coat the mold
2. Arrange one glass of cream with five egg yolks, sugar to taste and lemon zest
3. Mix well and cook in a water bath
