## Ingredients

- meat
- water
- onion stalks
- handful of miltomate
- 2 cooked chiles
- handful of rice, soaked
- Castilian peppers
- cumin seeds
- small piece of achiote
- potato pieces (optional)
- güisquil pieces (optional)

## Directions

1. Put the meat to cook in sufficient water with onion stalks cut in pieces
2. When the meat is already cooked, add a sauce made as follows: grind a handful of miltomate with two cooked chiles, a handful of soaked rice, some Castilian peppers, some cumin seeds and a small piece of achiote
3. Let it boil until the rice is calculated to be cooked
4. When desired, add small pieces of potato and güisquil
