## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one glass of milk
- half a pound of butter
- one teaspoon of salt
- one large tablespoon of Royal

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with one glass of milk and half a pound of butter, one teaspoon of salt and one large tablespoon of Royal
2. Stir very well without kneading
3. Spread with a rolling pin
4. Cut with a mold of whatever shape you wish
5. Put in a very hot oven
