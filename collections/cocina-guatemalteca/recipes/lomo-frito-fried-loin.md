## Ingredients

- loin
- tomatoes
- onion
- green chiles
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the loin and fry it
2. When golden, remove the lard
3. Cook tomatoes with onion, green chiles and some potatoes if available, or just potatoes if not
4. Bathe the loin with this sauce and serve hot
