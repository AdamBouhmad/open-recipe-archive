## Ingredients

- Pieces of ice
- One bottle of champagne
- Four small glasses of very fine cognac
- One small glass of maraschino
- One bottle of Apollinaris
- One small glass of Angostura bitters
- One bottle of white wine
- Pieces of frozen ripe pineapple
- One jar of grapes with all their liquid

## Directions

1. Put pieces of ice in a container
2. Add one bottle of champagne, four small glasses of very fine cognac, one small glass of maraschino, one bottle of Apollinaris, one small glass of Angostura bitters, one bottle of white wine, pieces of frozen ripe pineapple and one jar of grapes with all their liquid
3. Serve with pieces of ice
