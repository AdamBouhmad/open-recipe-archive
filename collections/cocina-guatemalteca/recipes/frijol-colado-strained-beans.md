## Ingredients

- beans, cooked with salt
- one head of garlic
- two spoonfuls of lard
- one onion, well chopped
- onion head and stalk

## Directions

1. Cook beans well with salt and one head of garlic
2. Remove and grind or mash with a masher
3. Strain with the same broth
4. Heat a pan over the fire with two spoonfuls of lard and one well-chopped onion (head and stalk)
5. As soon as it takes on color without burning, add the beans
6. Let it boil until the fat disappears
