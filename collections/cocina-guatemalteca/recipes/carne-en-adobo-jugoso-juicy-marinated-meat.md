## Ingredients

- tomato
- miltomate (ground cherry)
- onion head, roasted
- cumin, a little
- oregano
- garlic clove
- achiote (annatto), a small piece
- chile, well toasted (if desired for spice)
- meat, already fried

## Directions

1. Grind tomato and miltomate, toasted or cooked with a roasted onion head, a little cumin, oregano, a small garlic clove, and a small piece of achiote
2. If spice is desired, grind a well-toasted chile
3. Place this mixture on the meat that is already fried
4. Let it simmer for a few boils so that it does not dry out
