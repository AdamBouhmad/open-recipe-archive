## Ingredients

- One ounce of toasted cacao
- One ounce of soaked rice
- A quarter ounce of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind one ounce of toasted cacao, one ounce of soaked rice with a quarter ounce of cinnamon
2. Strain and sweeten to taste
