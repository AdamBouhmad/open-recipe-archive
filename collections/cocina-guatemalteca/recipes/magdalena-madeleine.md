## Ingredients

- three ounces of sugar
- three ounces of flour
- two ounces of lard
- six eggs
- honey
- filling of choice

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with the sugar and let warm slightly
2. When it has risen sufficiently, add melted lard and sifted flour
3. Stir with a skimmer without beating so it does not become firm
4. Put in a greased mold and place in the oven
5. Cut in half, brush with honey and fill with whatever is desired
