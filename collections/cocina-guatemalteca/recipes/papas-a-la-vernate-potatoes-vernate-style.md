## Ingredients

- butter
- potatoes
- Parmesan or dry cheese powder
- broth or milk

## Directions

1. Coat a pot with butter, put a layer of well peeled and sliced potatoes, a layer of Parmesan or dry cheese powder
2. Continue putting layers like the previous ones as far as desired
3. Moisten with broth or milk, put in the oven and remove when cooked
