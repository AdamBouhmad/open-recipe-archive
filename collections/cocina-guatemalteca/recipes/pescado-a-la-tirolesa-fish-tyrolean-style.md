## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- oil
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- Castilian pepper
- French mustard

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, fry whole or in pieces with salt.
2. Remove without allowing it to brown.
3. In the same oil fry finely chopped garlic and onions.
4. Grind three or four roasted tomatoes with one garlic clove, some Castilian peppers and one small teaspoon of French mustard.
5. Add the fish to this mixture for a very light boil.
6. Serve hot.
