## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), cleaned
- 4 large tomatoes, peeled and halved
- 4 to 6 onions
- 1 head of garlic, peeled and mashed
- 1/4 ounce of clove
- 1/4 ounce of cinnamon
- 1/4 ounce of Castilian pepper
- 4 bay leaves
- 4 orange leaves
- 1 tablespoon of salt
- 1 glass of vinegar
- 1 piece of ground pineapple
- water to cover the meat

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and cut into quarters or pieces of desired size.
2. Place in a pan and add the peeled and halved tomatoes, onions, peeled and mashed garlic head, clove, cinnamon, Castilian pepper, bay leaves, orange leaves, salt, vinegar, and ground pineapple.
3. Cover with water until the meat is covered.
4. Place over gentle and even heat.
5. If the turkey is very large, it must simmer for three hours; if not, only two hours.
6. If you want it juicy, add bread powder; otherwise, let it dry and serve with salad or sauce.
