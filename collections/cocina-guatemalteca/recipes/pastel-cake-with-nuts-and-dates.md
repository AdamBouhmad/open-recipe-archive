## Ingredients

- five egg yolks
- one cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of flour
- one teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- one cup of chopped walnuts
- one cup of chopped dates
- a little vanilla
- five egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks with sugar, flour, Royal, the walnuts and the dates
2. Add a little vanilla
3. Finally, add the five beaten egg whites
4. Grease a mold with lard
5. Pour the batter in and put it in the oven
