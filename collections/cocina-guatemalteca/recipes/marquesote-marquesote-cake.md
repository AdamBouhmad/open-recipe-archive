## Ingredients

- Two cups of flour
- One cup of sugar
- One tablespoon of Royal
- Four ounces of butter
- Two eggs
- One cup of milk

## Directions

1. Sift everything very well
2. Add one cup of milk
3. Beat the two eggs as for marquesote
4. Mix everything very well
5. Stir in a circular motion
6. Pour into a buttered small pan
7. Put in the oven
