## Ingredients

- jamón (ham)
- chorizo (sausage)
- tomate pelado (peeled tomato)
- ajo (garlic)
- cebolla (onion)
- perejil (parsley)
- polvo de pimienta (ground pepper)
- sal (salt)
- manteca o mantequilla (lard or butter)
- agua (water)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- vino (wine)
- hoja de laurel (bay leaf)
- ramita de tomillo (sprig of thyme)
- clavo (clove)
- canela (cinnamon)
- jengibre (ginger)
- tomates (tomatoes)

## Directions

1. Chop ham, sausage, peeled tomato, garlic, onion and parsley
2. Mix with ground pepper and salt
3. Use this mixture to lard the meat by making cuts with a pointed knife
4. Brown in lard or butter
5. When colored, add one liter of water, salt, one glass of vinegar and another of wine if available
6. Add a bay leaf, a sprig of thyme, pepper, clove, a small piece of cinnamon, a small piece of ginger, some diced tomatoes and two or three garlic cloves
7. Let it conserve; serve juicy if desired or save to serve with sauce or salad
