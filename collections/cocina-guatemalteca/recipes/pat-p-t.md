## Ingredients

- one hen
- twelve ounces of veal
- nine ounces of pork
- two ounces of cooked ham
- one small can of liver pâté
- three tablespoons of cognac
- two tablespoons of oil
- four eggs
- salt
- pepper powder
- nutmeg
- thyme
- bay leaf
- water

## Directions

1. Debone the hen leaving only the skin.
2. Grind the hen meat with twelve ounces of veal and nine ounces of pork.
3. Place in a container and add two ounces of chopped cooked ham, one small can of liver pâté, three tablespoons of cognac, two tablespoons of oil, and four beaten eggs.
4. Mix everything with salt, pepper powder, and nutmeg.
5. Season the hen's breast well with salt, pepper powder, and cognac.
6. Place on the skin the mixture, the breast, and on top the rest of the meat.
7. Close and sew or tie it, giving it the shape of a small piece.
8. Place in cloth with water, salt, a sprig of thyme and one of bay leaf for two hours.
9. If the hen is tender, dry and press it.
