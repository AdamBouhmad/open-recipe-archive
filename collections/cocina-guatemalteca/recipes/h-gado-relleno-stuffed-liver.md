## Ingredients

- Four ounces of pork meat
- Two ounces of beef
- Onion
- Tomato
- Chorizo
- Ham
- Hard-boiled egg
- Black pepper powder
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Capers
- Pickled vegetables
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Lard or butter
- Liver

## Directions

1. Chop four ounces of pork meat and two ounces of beef that have been parboiled
2. Fry with onion and tomato
3. Add chorizo, parboiled ham, chopped hard-boiled egg, black pepper powder, cinnamon, clove, capers, pickled vegetables, wine, vinegar, and salt
4. Fry partially
5. Stuff the liver (whether lamb, pork, or beef) with this mixture
6. Sew with thick thread
7. Coat with lard or butter and salt
8. Bake in the oven
