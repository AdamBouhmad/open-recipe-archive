## Ingredients

- sufficient oil
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- olives
- capers
- cod

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped onion in sufficient oil, then add peeled and chopped tomato, a little ground garlic, chopped parsley, pepper, olives, capers, and cod pieces.
2. Serve hot.
