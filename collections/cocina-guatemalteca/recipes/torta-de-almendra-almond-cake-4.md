## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of sugar
- one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
- four egg whites
- biscotela dough
- two egg whites
- sugar powder
- four drops of lemon
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Clarify one and one-half pounds of sugar and bring to syrup stage (punto de almíbar)
2. Add one-half pound of peeled and ground almonds
3. Place over fire until it reaches the dry stage (punto seco)
4. Lower heat and when cold, add four beaten egg whites
5. Place a mass of biscotela dough on a platter and bake in the oven
6. Pour the almond preserve over the baked dough and return to the oven
7. When baked, coat with two beaten egg whites mixed with sugar powder, four drops of lemon, and a little cinnamon powder
8. Return to the oven until the glaze is golden
