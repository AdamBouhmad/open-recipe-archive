## Ingredients

- one large spoonful of oil
- four whole tomatoes
- four onions
- oregano
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Put a large spoonful of oil in a pan
2. add four whole tomatoes and four onions
3. after boiling five minutes, grind the tomato with oregano and two cloves
4. return to the oil with sufficient water and a little vinegar
