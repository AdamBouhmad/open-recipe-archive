## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Two ounces of butter
- One level teaspoon of salt
- One tablespoon of Royal
- One glass half water and half milk

## Directions

1. Mix everything very well
2. Cut cookies
3. Form small holes with a stick
4. Put in the oven
