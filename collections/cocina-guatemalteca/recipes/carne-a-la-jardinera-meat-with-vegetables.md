## Ingredients

- Meat
- Ham
- Butter or margarine
- Water
- Salt
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Peas
- Green beans
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Pepper powder
- Nutmeg powder

## Directions

1. Lard with ham and put to fry in butter or margarine
2. When it has taken on some color, add water, salt, oregano, thyme, and laurel, all ground
3. While the meat cooks, put carrots, turnips, peas, and tender green beans to cook
4. Once cooked, let them drain
5. Fry a little butter and in it two tablespoons of flour with a little milk so the sauce is quite thin
6. Add pepper powder and nutmeg powder
7. When it boils, mix in the vegetables
8. Cut the meat into very thin slices and place on the platter, pouring this sauce over it
