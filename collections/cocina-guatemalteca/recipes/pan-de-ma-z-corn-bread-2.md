## Ingredients

- one glass of liquid (two parts milk and one part water)
- half a tablespoon of salt
- one pound and two ounces of sugar
- ten ounces of lard and half of butter
- two pounds of bread flour and one of cornmeal
- half an ounce of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Put in a bowl one glass of liquid (two parts milk and one part water), half a tablespoon of salt, one pound and two ounces of sugar, ten ounces of lard and half of butter
2. Mix everything well and add two pounds of bread flour and one of cornmeal
3. Knead for half an hour
4. Let rest for a quarter hour
5. Then add half an ounce of Royal
6. Place in greased bowls and put in a hot oven for three quarters of an hour
