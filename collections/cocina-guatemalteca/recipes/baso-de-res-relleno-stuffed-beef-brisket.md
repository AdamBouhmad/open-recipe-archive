## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- garlic
- potato
- peas
- carrot
- half pound of pork

## Directions

1. Place the beef brisket in boiling water.
2. Prepare garlic, potato, peas, carrot, and half pound of pork.
3. Par-cook the vegetables and chop the meat.
4. Open the brisket to fill.
