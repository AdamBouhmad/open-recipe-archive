## Ingredients

- Dried fava beans
- Water
- Salt
- One tablespoon lard
- Roasted tomato
- Castilian peppercorns
- Achiote
- Masa, finely ground
- Epazote
- Hot water

## Directions

1. Leave the fava beans in water overnight
2. The next day, peel them and, very well washed, put in cold salted water, taking care not to stir so they do not become hard
3. When they burst, add one tablespoon of lard
4. Grind a roasted tomato with some Castilian peppercorns, a piece of achiote and a piece of finely ground masa
5. Dissolve with a little water and add to the fava beans with a sprig of epazote washed with boiling water
6. Stir and leave neither very thick nor very thin
