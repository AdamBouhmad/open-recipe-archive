## Ingredients

- ichintal
- salt
- two tomatoes, peeled
- slices of onion
- green or dry chile

## Directions

1. Peel the ichintal as in the previous recipe
2. Cook with salt along with two peeled tomatoes, slices of onion, and green or dry chile
3. When everything is cooked, mash the tomato
4. Leave the chile whole to eat as desired
