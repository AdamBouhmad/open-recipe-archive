## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 4 ounces sugar
- 4 ounces lard
- 2 eggs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, four ounces of sugar, four ounces of lard and two eggs very well with a little water and a pinch of salt
2. Stretch with a roller, prick with a knife
3. Cook on a comal, on the stove plate or in the oven
