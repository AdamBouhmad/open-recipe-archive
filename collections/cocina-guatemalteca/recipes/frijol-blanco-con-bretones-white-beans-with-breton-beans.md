## Ingredients

- white beans
- water
- salt
- tomato, peeled
- onion
- garlic clove, one
- Breton beans, pre-cooked
- achiote
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Boil the white beans and after boiling for one hour, drain the water
2. Add fresh boiling salted water
3. When the beans are well cooked, make a fried mixture of peeled tomato, onion, and a garlic clove
4. Add the beans with the pre-cooked Breton beans
5. Add a little achiote for color
6. Add half a teaspoon of flour dissolved in cold water
