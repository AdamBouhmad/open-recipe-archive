## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half a teaspoon of salt
- six eggs
- four ounces of lard
- a little water if needed

## Directions

1. Put one pound of flour, half a teaspoon of salt, and six eggs together
2. Add four ounces of lard, and if it is too stiff add a little water so that it becomes a smooth dough
3. Knead very well and let rest for half an hour
4. This dough serves for many pastries
