## Ingredients

- pepitoria (pumpkin seeds)
- sesame
- guaque chile
- miltomate
- two or three french peppers
- Castilian pepper
- cinnamon, a small piece
- cilantro, a little green
- meat broth (beef or pork)
- lard

## Directions

1. Toast pepitoria, sesame, a guaque chile, miltomate, and two or three french peppers
2. Once well toasted, grind with Castilian pepper, a small piece of cinnamon, and a little green cilantro
3. Dissolve this mixture with the meat broth
4. Fry in lard and add the meat in pieces
5. Let it simmer for a while and serve hot
