## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- saffron water
- fried seasoning of tomato, onion and garlic
- two ounces of peeled shrimp
- one teaspoon of lard

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well without soaking
2. Add to a pot with sufficient water to cover the rice
3. Add salt, a bit of saffron water, fried seasoning made from tomato, onion and finely chopped garlic
4. Add two ounces of peeled shrimp and one teaspoon of lard
5. Boil with only one light stir so the rice does not break apart
