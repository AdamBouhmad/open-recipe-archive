## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- four eggs
- four ounces Parmesan cheese
- one glass cream
- biscuit powder
- butter
- ham
- tomato sauce
- ground meat or chicken

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice as usual; when it is cold, mix in half pound of rice, four eggs, four ounces of Parmesan cheese, one glass of cream, and a little biscuit powder.
2. Coat a mold with butter and biscuit powder.
3. If the mold has a hollow center, place a glass or jar in the middle, coated with butter.
4. Place in a bain-marie; when set, remove the glass and fill the hollow with ham mixed with tomato sauce and ground meat or chicken, all well seasoned.
