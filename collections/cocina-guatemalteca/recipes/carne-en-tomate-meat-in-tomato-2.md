## Ingredients

- meat
- oil
- tomatoes, chopped, two
- onion
- garlic, chopped
- white wine, sherry, or red wine

## Directions

1. Cut meat into slices and coat with oil
2. Place in a dish
3. Add two chopped tomatoes, one onion, chopped garlic, and white wine, sherry, or red wine
