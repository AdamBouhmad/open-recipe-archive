## Ingredients

- cooked meat
- tomato
- onions
- breadcrumb powder
- eggs
- salt
- sherry (optional)

## Directions

1. After the meat is cooked, chop it very well
2. Add a fried mixture of tomato and onions, a little breadcrumb powder
3. For half a pound of meat, mix in three eggs
4. Season with salt
5. Add a little sherry if desired
6. Form the small cakes
7. Fry them in hot butter
8. Can also be made with raw ground meat, or half beef and half pork meat
