## Ingredients

- 6 pounds meat
- 1/2 ounce salt
- 1/8 ounce Castilian pepper
- 1/8 ounce cinnamon
- 1/4 ounce clove
- 1/2 bottle vinegar
- 2 ounces ham
- intestines

## Directions

1. Grind 6 pounds of meat
2. Add 1/2 ounce salt, 1/8 ounce Castilian pepper, 1/8 ounce cinnamon, and 1/4 ounce clove
3. Dissolve everything in 1/2 bottle vinegar and mix well with the meat
4. Leave in brine for four days, stirring daily
5. Mince 2 ounces ham and mix very well
6. Fill well-washed intestines with the mixture and tie them about a hand's width long
7. Place in the sun for one day
8. Store in a fly trap to prevent spoilage
