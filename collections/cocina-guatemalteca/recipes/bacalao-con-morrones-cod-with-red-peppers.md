## Ingredients

- cod
- oil
- three heads garlic, peeled and crushed
- two or three chopped onions
- eight or ten large chopped tomatoes
- one jar red peppers
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Prepare the cod as in previous recipes
2. Fry in a little hot oil
3. When fried, remove and fry three heads of peeled and crushed garlic in the same oil
4. When they take color, remove them keeping the oil very clean
5. Have two or three chopped onions ready and add to the oil eight or ten large chopped tomatoes with one jar of red peppers in their juice
6. Place the cod in this and let boil sufficiently
7. When serving, add olives and capers
