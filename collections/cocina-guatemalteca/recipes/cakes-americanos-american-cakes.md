## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons lard
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder
- 2 eggs
- chopped almond pieces

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 pound of flour, 4 tablespoons of sugar, 4 tablespoons of lard, 1 teaspoon of Royal and 2 eggs
2. Mix everything well and by spoonfuls place on a baking sheet with pieces of chopped almonds on top
3. Bake in a hot oven
