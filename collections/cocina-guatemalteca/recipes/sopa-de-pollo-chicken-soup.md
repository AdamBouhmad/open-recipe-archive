## Ingredients

- beef tail or hip
- fat chicken
- vegetables of all kinds
- flour
- butter
- pepper powder
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare very good broth with beef tail or hip
2. Add a clean fat chicken to it
3. When the chicken is well cooked, remove it
4. Strain the broth through a filter
5. Add vegetables of all kinds, parboiled and chopped
6. When everything is cooked, add a teaspoon of flour fried in butter and pepper powder
7. Add all the chicken meat cut into little pieces
8. Serve hot with chopped parsley
