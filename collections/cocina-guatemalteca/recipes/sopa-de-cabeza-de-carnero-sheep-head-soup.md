## Ingredients

- Sheep head
- Sheep feet
- Sheep offal
- Cooked ham
- Cooked egg yolks
- Castilian peppers
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg powder
- Raw eggs
- Flour or bread powder
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- Lard
- Broth
- Fried seasoning
- Butter
- Toasted bread slices
- Dry sherry

## Directions

1. Cook a sheep head, feet, and offal
2. Once everything is well cooked, remove it and mince the brains with everything else
3. Mix in finely minced cooked ham
4. Stir in three cooked and ground egg yolks with Castilian peppers, two cloves, a small piece of cinnamon, and a little nutmeg powder
5. Mix in two raw eggs, a little flour or sweetless bread powder, salt to taste and chopped parsley
6. Well mixed, make small fritters in hot lard
7. Prepare in advance a good broth like the previous soups, adding fried seasoning, chopped parsley, and flour fried in butter
8. Drop the fritters into it and let them boil five minutes in the tureen
9. Prepare toasted bread slices and pour the soup over them, adding a small glass of dry sherry
