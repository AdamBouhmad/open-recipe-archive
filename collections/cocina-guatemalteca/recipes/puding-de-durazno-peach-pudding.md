## Ingredients

- salpor flour (0.5 cup)
- bread flour (0.5 cup)
- Royal baking powder (1 teaspoon)
- sugar (2 tablespoons)
- butter (1 tablespoon)
- milk (1 cup)
- eggs (3)
- nutmeg (a pinch)
- salt (a pinch)
- seedless raisins (0.5 cup, halved)
- peach pieces (1 cup)

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
2. Mix in the egg yolks.
3. Add the salpor flour, bread flour, Royal, sugar, butter, milk, nutmeg, and salt.
4. Fold in the halved raisins and peach pieces.
5. Mix everything well.
6. Pour into a greased mold and bake in the oven.
