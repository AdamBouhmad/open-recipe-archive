## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- saffron water
- fried seasoning
- epazote (apazote)

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice as in the previous recipe without saffron
2. When the rice is cooked add a sprig of epazote that has been washed with very hot water so it gives a very light boil
3. When serving, leave the epazote if desired or remove it
