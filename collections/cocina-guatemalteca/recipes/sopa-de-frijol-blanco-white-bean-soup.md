## Ingredients

- white beans, well cooked
- sufficient seasoning for frying
- good broth
- pepper powder
- thin slices of bread, well fried
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. When the beans are well cooked, grind them and strain
2. Fry with sufficient seasoning
3. Add very good broth and pepper powder
4. In the soup tureen, place thin slices of well-fried bread
5. Add chopped parsley
6. Serve very hot
