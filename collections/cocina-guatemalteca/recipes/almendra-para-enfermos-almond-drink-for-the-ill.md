## Ingredients

- one ounce of melon seed
- one ounce of rice
- half an ounce of almond
- cinnamon stick
- sugar to taste
- milk

## Directions

1. Soak one ounce of melon seed, one ounce of rice, half an ounce of almond
2. Grind the almond well after peeling, the rice well washed, everything well ground
3. Strain with little water
4. Place on the fire with a small stick of cinnamon and sugar to taste
5. When it thickens, add the necessary milk to make a thin atole
