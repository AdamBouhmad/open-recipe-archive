## Ingredients

- rib pieces
- backbone
- brisket
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- thyme
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of rib, backbone and brisket
2. Place in a dish with plenty of diced tomato, onions cut in quarters, peeled garlic, a sprig of thyme, clove, pepper, cinnamon, equal parts water and vinegar to cover the meat and one tablespoon of salt
3. Leave overnight
4. The next day place on very low heat
5. If desired leave it juicy and sprinkle with bread powder; if not, serve it dry
6. This can also be made with all the spices ground
