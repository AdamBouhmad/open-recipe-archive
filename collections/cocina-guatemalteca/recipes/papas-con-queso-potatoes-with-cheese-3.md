## Ingredients

- Potatoes, well cooked
- Butter
- Tomato
- Onion and garlic, chopped
- Achiote
- Cheese, plenty
- Butter, washed or fresh

## Directions

1. Well cooked potatoes are mashed
2. Place in a pan with butter, tomato, chopped onion and garlic and a little achiote
3. Add plenty of cheese
4. Put on a platter and place washed or fresh butter all around the edge
