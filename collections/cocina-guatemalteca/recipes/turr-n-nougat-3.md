## Ingredients

- nine egg whites
- two pounds of sugar
- one and a half glasses of water
- juice of half a lemon
- lemon zest
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Beat nine egg whites for half an hour
2. Meanwhile, put two pounds of sugar with one and a half glasses of water over the fire and let it reach a very high point
3. Pour this in a stream to the whites while the syrup is very hot, beating continuously
4. Add the juice of half a lemon and the lemon zest
5. When it is cold and very thick, pour onto a platter with cinnamon powder on top
