## Ingredients

- kidney
- salt
- pepper powder
- butter
- tomato sauce with dried chile

## Directions

1. Wash the kidney very well, removing the skin and fat
2. Split it open, rub it with salt, pepper powder and a little butter and let it sit in this brine for a quarter hour
3. Then place it on the grill, turn it two or three times so it does not become tough and serve it with a good tomato and dried chile sauce
