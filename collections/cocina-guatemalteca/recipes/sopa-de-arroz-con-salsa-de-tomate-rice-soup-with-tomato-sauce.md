## Ingredients

- 3 ounces rice
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 4 to 6 tomatoes
- 6 Castilian peppers

## Directions

1. Soak three ounces of rice; when well soaked, wash and grind it, moistening with a little water or milk if desired
2. Once ground, add two eggs (white and yolk), mix very well with a little salt
3. Fry the mixture in small curdles, as small as possible
4. Grind four or six tomatoes with six Castilian peppers
5. In a pan, layer the fried rice cakes and sauce alternately
6. Bring to a boil for ten minutes and serve
