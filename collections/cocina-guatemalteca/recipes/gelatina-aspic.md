## Ingredients

- two chickens
- salt
- one bay leaf
- a few drops of lemon
- one packet of gelatin or two sheets of isinglass
- cooked chicken, cut into pieces
- vegetables, finely chopped and dried
- shrimp, well cleaned
- salted tongue, sliced

## Directions

1. Cook two chickens with salt and one bay leaf.
2. When well cooked, remove the chickens and reserve the broth.
3. The next day, skim the fat, bring to a boil with a few drops of lemon.
4. Add one packet of gelatin or two sheets of isinglass.
5. Prepare in a mold small pieces of cooked chicken, finely chopped and dried vegetables, well-cleaned shrimp, and sliced salted tongue.
6. Pour the aspic over everything and if urgent, place on ice to set.
