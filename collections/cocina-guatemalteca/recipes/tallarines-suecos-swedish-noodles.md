## Ingredients

- pork
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- garlic
- onion slices
- pepper powder
- sweet chile
- chorizo
- longaniza (Spanish sausage)
- ham
- roasted tomato
- dried guaque or pasilla chile
- butter
- noodles or spaghetti
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Place a piece of pork with salt, thyme, bay leaf, crushed garlic, onion slices, pepper powder, sweet chile, chorizo, longaniza, and ham to cure overnight.
2. The next day, chop the meat and add roasted tomato and well-ground dried guaque or pasilla chile.
3. Fry with the previous meats.
4. Coat a pot with sufficient butter.
5. Layer noodles or spaghetti, then Parmesan cheese powder, then sauce; make the top layer cheese.
6. Place in the oven or between two fires to cook.
