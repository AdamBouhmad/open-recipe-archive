## Ingredients

- lomo (loin)
- manteca o mantequilla (lard or butter)
- ajo (garlic)
- ramita de tomillo (sprig of thyme)
- hoja de laurel (bay leaf)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Make some cuts in the loin that go through to the other side
2. Fry in lard or butter
3. When golden, add a garlic clove, a sprig of thyme, a ground bay leaf and dissolve with enough water to cover the meat
4. Season with salt and let reduce
5. Serve dry if desired or in its own juices
