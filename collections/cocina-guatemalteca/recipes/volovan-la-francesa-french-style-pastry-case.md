## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Water
- Salt to taste
- One pound of butter, melted beforehand
- One tablespoon of butter
- One garlic clove
- Small pieces of pork loin
- Tender pieces of chicken meat

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with water and a little salt, so the dough is not very hard
2. Let rest half an hour
3. Roll out with a roller and spread melted butter on it
4. Roll out again and spread more butter
5. Repeat this operation five or six times
6. Let rest ten minutes
7. Roll out the sheets with the roller to the size of the mold in which they will be made
8. Cover the mold with the dough very evenly
9. Place in the oven to bake
10. When cooked, remove very carefully so it does not break, whether large mold or small boxes
11. Make lids the size of the mold and bake the same way
12. Prepare a sauce by putting a saucepan with one tablespoon of butter, one garlic clove, and very small pieces of pork loin and tender chicken meat on heat
13. When it has taken on golden color, continue with the filling preparation
