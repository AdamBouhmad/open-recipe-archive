## Ingredients

- Potatoes, peeled
- Salt
- Water
- Parsley, chopped
- Hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- Butter, washed and cut into pieces
- Butifarra, sliced

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, removing all the dark parts with the tip of the knife
2. Place in a dish with salt and a little water
3. Cook on very low heat
4. Once cooked, remove very carefully so they do not fall apart
5. Serve with chopped parsley, slices of hard-boiled egg, pieces of washed butter and slices of butifarra
