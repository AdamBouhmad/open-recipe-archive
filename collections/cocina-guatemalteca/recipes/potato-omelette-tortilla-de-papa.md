## Ingredients

- Potato
- Salt
- Oil or Butter
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the potato and fry it with salt in one or two tablespoons of oil.
2. Stir so it breaks up.
3. For four ounces of potato, beat three eggs (white first and then yolk).
4. Mix the potato with this beaten mixture forming the omelette the size desired.
5. Fry in hot butter.
