## Ingredients

- young squash
- salt
- beaten egg
- lard and chile
- tomato
- onion

## Directions

1. Peel the young squash, cut into slices, cook with salt
2. When cooked remove and dry with a cloth
3. Wrap in beaten egg
4. Fry in lard and chile
5. Dissolve the tomato and onion
6. Leave the chile whole to eat as desired
