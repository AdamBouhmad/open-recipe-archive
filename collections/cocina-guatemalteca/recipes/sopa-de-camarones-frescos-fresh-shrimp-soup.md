## Ingredients

- shrimp
- three cooked egg yolks
- pepper
- salt
- lard
- chopped parsley
- fish broth or beef broth
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- piece of French bread soaked in milk
- dry sherry

## Directions

1. Cook the shrimp and when done carefully remove the shell and meat
2. Grind the shrimp meat with three cooked egg yolks and mix with pepper, salt, a bit of lard and chopped parsley
3. Fill the shrimp shells with this mixture and place on a platter
4. Cover with fish broth if it is Lent, if not use beef broth made with fried seasoning, pepper powder and a piece of French bread soaked in milk
5. Bring to a boil and when ready to serve add a glass of dry sherry
