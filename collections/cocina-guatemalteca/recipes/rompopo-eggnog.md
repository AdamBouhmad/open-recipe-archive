## Ingredients

- thirty egg yolks
- one bottle of high-point syrup made with two and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar
- one pound of rum
- two ounces of ground almond

## Directions

1. Beat thirty egg yolks for two hours
2. When they are well thickened, add one bottle of high-point syrup made with two and a half pounds of well-clarified sugar, one pound of rum, and two ounces of ground almond
3. Mix everything well and pour into clean white bottles
4. Cork and serve the following day
5. Alternatively, instead of rum, use one bottle of arrack with half an ounce of cinnamon and the powder of one nutmeg added eight days before use
