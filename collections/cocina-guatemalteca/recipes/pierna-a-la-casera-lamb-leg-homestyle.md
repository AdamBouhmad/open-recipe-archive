## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- raw potatoes
- thyme branch
- bay leaves
- tomatoes
- clove
- parsley bundle
- garlic head
- water

## Directions

1. Pound the leg and insert ham larding throughout with long strips that go from side to side
2. Place in a baking dish with salt, 3 or 4 onions, slices of raw potatoes, a branch of thyme, 3 bay leaves, 4 tomatoes cut in half, clove, pepper, a bundle of parsley, and a head of peeled garlic
3. Cover with water and simmer for three hours on low heat, turning it frequently so it cooks evenly
4. When cooked, place in the serving platter and strain the sauce well through a filter over it
