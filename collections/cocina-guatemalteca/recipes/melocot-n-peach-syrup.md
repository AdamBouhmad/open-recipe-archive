## Ingredients

- peaches
- water with lime lye
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified per pound of fruit
- cinnamon strips

## Directions

1. Peel the peach and remove all the pit
2. Soak for half an hour in water with two teaspoons of lime lye
3. Blanch in clean water
4. When blanched place in cold water
5. Have clarified one and a half pounds of sugar for one pound of fruit
6. Put the peach in the syrup to reach the proper point
7. Add cinnamon strips and serve as syrup
8. If desired remove and let dry in the sun for one or two days, then coat with high-point honey
