## Ingredients

- 6 ounces sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 glass cooked milk
- 1 full teaspoon potato starch
- 2 ounces peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Put 6 ounces of sugar in a pot with 5 egg yolks strained with 1 glass of cooked milk
2. Add 1 full teaspoon of potato starch and 2 ounces of peeled and ground almonds
3. Put on the fire to reach the point of before (ante)
