## Ingredients

- two chickens
- salt
- bay leaf
- lemon juice
- gelatin packet or two isinglass leaves
- cooked chicken pieces
- finely minced and dried vegetables
- cleaned shrimp
- slices of salted tongue

## Directions

1. Cook two chickens with salt and a bay leaf until well cooked
2. Remove the chickens and save the broth
3. The next day, degrease the broth
4. Bring to a boil with a few drops of lemon juice
5. Add a packet of gelatin or two isinglass leaves
6. Prepare a mold with pieces of cooked chicken, finely minced and dried vegetables, well-cleaned shrimp, and slices of salted tongue
7. Pour the gelatin over these ingredients
8. If urgent, place on ice to set; otherwise, let set overnight
9. Alternative: the aspic can also be made with a piece of pig's head, a chicken, and a beef leg
