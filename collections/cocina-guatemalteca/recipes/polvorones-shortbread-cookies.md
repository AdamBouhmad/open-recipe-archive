## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of sifted flour
- six ounces of sifted sugar
- six ounces of lard

## Directions

1. Place the sifted flour, sifted sugar, and lard on a table
2. Mix very well until the dough is silky
3. Roll out with a rolling pin
4. Cut into desired shapes
5. Place on a hot oven to cook quickly
