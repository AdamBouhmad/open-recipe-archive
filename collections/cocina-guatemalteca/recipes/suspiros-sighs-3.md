## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- very little water
- 2 egg whites
- half a pound of very dry peeled almonds, cut in strips or ground
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Place half a pound of sugar on the fire with very little water
2. When at thread stage, pour hot into two egg whites that will be well beaten as for meringue
3. Continue beating and when very stiff
4. Add half a pound of very dry peeled almonds, cut in strips or ground
5. Add the juice of a lemon
6. Place on butters-coated tins in desired sizes
