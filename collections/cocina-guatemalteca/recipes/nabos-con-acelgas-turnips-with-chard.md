## Ingredients

- three chard leaves
- ten peeled fried almonds
- water
- a small teaspoon of oil or lard
- nutmeg powder
- Castilian pepper
- turnip roots
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook three chard leaves without the white stalk
2. Once cooked, grind them with ten peeled and fried almonds
3. Dissolve this with a little water and fry in a small teaspoon of oil or lard
4. Add nutmeg powder and Castilian pepper
5. Add the turnip roots that have been previously cooked with salt
