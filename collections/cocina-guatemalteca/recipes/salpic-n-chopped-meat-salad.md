## Ingredients

- Cooked and cold badilla meat
- Onion
- Mint
- Tomato
- Green chile
- Salt
- Sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked and cold badilla meat very well, chop onion, mint, tomato, green chile, season with salt and sour orange juice
2. Serve with white rice
