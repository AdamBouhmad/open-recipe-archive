## Ingredients

- pork loin
- large tomatoes
- salt
- onion
- mint leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the pork loin on a grill, turning it until it is browned
2. Roast some large tomatoes, remove the skin, and mash them with a masher
3. Add salt, onion, and some chopped mint leaves to the tomatoes
4. Add vinegar if desired
5. Slice the pork loin and place it in the sauce
