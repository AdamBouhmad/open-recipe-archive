## Ingredients

- oil, a little
- garlic, crushed
- meat
- onions, in slices
- tomatoes, chopped
- pepper, ground
- cinnamon, a small strip
- saffron, five threads
- vinegar, a little
- bay leaves, two
- potatoes for serving (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry a little oil and fry crushed garlic until cooked; remove it
2. In the same pan, fry a piece of meat until it takes on a yellow color
3. Add sliced onions, chopped tomatoes, ground pepper, a small strip of cinnamon, and five threads of saffron
4. Dissolve all this in a little vinegar and add two bay leaves
5. Once fried, serve with potatoes if desired
