## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound of flour
- 4 egg whites
- a pinch of salt
- cold water as needed
- butter equal in weight to the dough
- 2 egg whites beaten
- 6 ounces of sugar
- a few drops of lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of flour with four egg whites, a pinch of salt, and enough cold water to make a soft dough
2. Work it well, weigh it, and add the same weight of very firm butter
3. Roll out, place the butter in the middle and close the four corners
4. Roll out with the rolling pin, dust with flour and leave in the air for about five minutes
5. Fold again, roll out the same way four or five times
6. On the last fold, frost with two well-beaten egg whites, six ounces of sugar and a few drops of lemon
7. Cut into small pieces and place on baking sheets in the oven
