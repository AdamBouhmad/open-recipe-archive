## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- nine egg whites
- half a pound of sifted flour
- half a teaspoon of bitter almond water
- one ounce of currants
- one ounce of raisins
- one teaspoon of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat half a pound of butter for half an hour
2. Then add half a pound of powdered sugar and beat for half an hour more
3. While this is being beaten, have nine egg whites beating
4. When the whites are very stiff, add them to the butter mixture
5. Add half a pound of sifted flour, half a teaspoon of bitter almond water, one ounce of currants, one ounce of raisins, and one teaspoon of Royal baking powder
6. Pour into a mold coated with butter and bread powder so it does not stick
7. Put in the oven for fifteen minutes
