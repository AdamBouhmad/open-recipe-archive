## Ingredients

- güisquil (chayote)
- green beans
- tender beans
- noodles
- pasta
- tender peas
- tortillas
- lard
- cheese
- water or broth
- fried seasoning

## Directions

1. Mince güisquil, green beans and tender beans, parboil noodles and pasta, fry small rounds of tender peas; once cooked, drain the water and have tortilla pieces, well fried, prepared
2. Put in a lard-lined pan, one layer of tortilla, one of vegetables, one of cheese and so on successively, making sure the last layer is tortilla
3. Cover with a little hot water with fried seasoning or broth if desired, letting it boil for a quarter hour over very low heat
