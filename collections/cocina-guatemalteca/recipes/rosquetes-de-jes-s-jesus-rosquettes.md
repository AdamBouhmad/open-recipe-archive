## Ingredients

- one and a half cups milk
- two eggs
- three cups flour
- three ounces butter
- lard
- one cup milk
- one tablespoon cornstarch
- one egg
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well in a mold coated with lard and bake in the oven.
2. When cooked, cut in half.
3. Prepare a cream made with one cup of milk, one tablespoon of cornstarch, one egg, sugar to taste, and vanilla; put on heat.
4. As soon as it thickens, fill the pastry with this and cover with turrón.
