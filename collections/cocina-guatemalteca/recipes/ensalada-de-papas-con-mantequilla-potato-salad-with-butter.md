## Ingredients

- Large potatoes
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Wash very large potatoes, peel them and slice them
2. Prepare in a bowl half a cup of vinegar and one of oil, two large spoonfuls of melted butter and a small bunch of very finely chopped parsley
3. Place the well-drained potatoes in this dressing
