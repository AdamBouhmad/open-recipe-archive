## Ingredients

- Beef
- Pork
- Chorizo
- Ham
- Capers
- Fried seasoning mixture
- Water
- Red wine

## Directions

1. Mince beef and pork, plenty of crumbled chorizo, and minced ham
2. Mix in capers and fried seasoning
3. Fill the loin with this mixture
4. Tie very well with twine
5. Place in a pot with equal parts water and red wine
6. Once cooked, serve with lettuce and tender radishes
