## Ingredients

- three ounces of rice
- water
- half a liter of milk
- three egg yolks
- a few grains of salt
- four ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak three ounces of rice overnight
2. The following day grind it with water
3. Strain with half a liter of milk and three egg yolks
4. Place on the fire with a few grains of salt and four ounces of sugar
5. If it is too thick add more milk to make a thin atole
6. If desired, serve alone; if not, serve half atolillo and half rice with milk
