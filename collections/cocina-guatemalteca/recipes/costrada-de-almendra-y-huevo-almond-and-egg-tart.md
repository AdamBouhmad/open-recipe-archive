## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- water
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 pound almond, peeled and ground
- 1/2 glass sherry wine
- 1/2 ounce cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put one pound of sugar with a little water on the fire; when it reaches the hard-crack stage, let it cool
2. Add twelve well-beaten egg yolks and one pound of peeled and ground almond
3. Mix everything well and put back on the fire with one-half glass of sherry wine and one-half ounce of cinnamon
4. Remove when it pulls away from the pan, and if desired, put in the oven to brown
