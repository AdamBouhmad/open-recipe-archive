## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one egg
- two egg yolks
- water
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add one egg, two egg yolks and the necessary water to make a tender dough
2. Spread it on a board with a rolling pin
3. Brush with half a pound of melted butter
4. Repeat this operation three or four times
5. Then use it for whatever you wish
