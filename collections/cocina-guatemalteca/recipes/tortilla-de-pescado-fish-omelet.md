## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 cup of flour
- salt
- parsley, chopped
- garlic
- shredded salt cod
- oil or lard

## Directions

1. Beat well 2 eggs with 1 cup of milk
2. Mix in 1 cup of flour
3. Add salt, plenty of chopped parsley, garlic, and shredded salt cod
4. Drop by spoonfuls into hot oil or lard so they cook evenly
