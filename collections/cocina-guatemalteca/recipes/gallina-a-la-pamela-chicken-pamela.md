## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, well cleaned
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- onion, chopped
- butter or lard
- water and salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 drops of vinegar

## Directions

1. Heat butter or lard and brown the chopped garlic and onion.
2. Add the well-cleaned chicken, cover with water and salt, and let simmer until cooked.
3. Before serving, beat 3 egg yolks with 3 drops of vinegar.
4. Serve hot with the egg mixture.
