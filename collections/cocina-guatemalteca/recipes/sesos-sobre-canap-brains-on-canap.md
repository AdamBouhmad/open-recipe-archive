## Ingredients

- bread
- lard
- butter
- cooked brains
- egg
- breadcrumb powder
- chard
- tomato
- onion
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry very thin slices of bread in very hot lard and butter.
2. In this same lard, fry the cooked brain coated in egg and then in breadcrumb powder.
3. When these slices are well fried, place them on the bread slices.
4. Arrange on a platter and around the edge place chard fried in butter with tomato, onion and half a tablespoon of vinegar.
5. Serve hot.
