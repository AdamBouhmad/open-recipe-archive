## Ingredients

- Potatoes, sliced and cooked with salt
- Castilian peppers, three
- Cloves, four
- Butter
- Pickled vegetables, chopped
- Hard-boiled eggs, pieces
- Parsley, chopped
- Pepper powder
- Black chorizo, sliced
- Cooked ham, pieces
- Tomatoes, three or four roasted
- Chile de pasa, pitted and soaked
- Garlic clove
- Water, half a cup
- Salt
- Dry cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes sliced with salt, three Castilian peppers and four cloves
2. Remove carefully when cooked so they do not break
3. Place in a casserole a layer of butter, a layer of potatoes, another of chopped pickled vegetables with pieces of hard-boiled egg, chopped parsley, pepper powder, slices of black chorizo, pieces of cooked ham, another layer of potatoes and so on alternating layers, making sure the last is potatoes
4. Grind three or four roasted tomatoes with a pitted and soaked chile de pasa and a garlic clove
5. Dissolve this in half a cup of water
6. Add salt and with this cover the potatoes so they boil two or three times
7. Empty carefully onto the platter and cover with dry cheese powder
