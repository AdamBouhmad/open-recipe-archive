## Ingredients

- Large potatoes, cooked
- Butter, one spoon, melted
- Pepper powder
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Parmesano cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook large potatoes
2. Keep in hot water until the moment of serving
3. Cut into wheels
4. Have a spoon of melted butter with pepper powder, chopped parsley and salt
5. Place the potatoes, very hot, in this
6. Cover with parmesano cheese powder
