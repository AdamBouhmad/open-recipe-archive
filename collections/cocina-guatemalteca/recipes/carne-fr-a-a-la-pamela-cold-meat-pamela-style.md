## Ingredients

- a piece of meat
- salt
- pepper powder
- one egg per pound of meat
- pieces of cooked ham
- hard boiled egg
- cheese
- bay leaves
- thyme

## Directions

1. Take a piece of meat; if desired with the color of sausage, leave it one day with salt; if not, grind it the same day in a machine until very fine
2. Add salt and pepper powder
3. For one pound of meat add one beaten egg and mix very well
4. Spread it out and place long pieces of cooked ham, hard boiled egg and cheese cut lengthwise
5. Wrap it, giving it the shape desired, either round or long
6. Place in very fine cloth, tie it and cook in water for three hours with two bay leaves, thyme and salt
7. The next day slice it and serve with salad or sauce
