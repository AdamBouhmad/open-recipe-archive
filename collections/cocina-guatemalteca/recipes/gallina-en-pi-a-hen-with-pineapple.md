## Ingredients

- 1 hen, cut into pieces
- butter or lard
- pineapple slices
- pepper powder
- clove
- cinnamon
- 1 pound of ham
- sausage
- pickles
- chopped almonds
- whole olives
- 1 glass of red wine
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Grease a dish with butter or lard
2. Place a layer of hen pieces of desired size
3. Add a layer of pineapple slices, removing the hard center
4. Add pepper powder, clove and cinnamon
5. Add 1 pound of ham, sausage, pickles, chopped almonds, whole olives and 1 glass of red wine
6. Cover with water and salt
7. Let it boil for two hours over very gentle heat
8. If making with cooked hen, add only the glass of red wine without water and let it boil for half an hour
