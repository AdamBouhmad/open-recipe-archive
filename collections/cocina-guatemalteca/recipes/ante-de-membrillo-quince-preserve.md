## Ingredients

- quince
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the quince
2. When cooked, drain the water in which it was cooked and strain it separately to make a jelly
3. Strain the quince without water and weigh it
4. For every pound of quince, use two pounds of sugar that have been clarified beforehand and brought to medium syrup stage
5. Add the strained quince and let boil until it separates from the pot
6. If desired, store in a well-covered earthenware vessel where it will keep for a long time
