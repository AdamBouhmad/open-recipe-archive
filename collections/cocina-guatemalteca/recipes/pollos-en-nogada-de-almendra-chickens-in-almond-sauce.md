## Ingredients

- chickens
- ten to fifteen fried almonds
- two roasted tomatoes
- guaque or dried chile peppered
- liver of the chickens cooked
- small piece of cinnamon
- ten Castilian peppers
- a little saffron
- water
- green lettuce
- pickles

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and fry them
2. When they take on color, grind ten to fifteen fried almonds, two roasted tomatoes, one guaque or dried chile without seeds, the cooked liver of the chickens, a small piece of cinnamon and ten Castilian peppers, and a little saffron
3. Dissolve this with a little water and add to the chickens
4. Let cook
5. Serve with green lettuce and pickles
