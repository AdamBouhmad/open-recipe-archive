## Ingredients

- Cleaned tongue
- Salt
- One quarter ounce of saltpeter
- Bay leaves
- Black pepper powder
- Nutmeg
- Thyme
- One cup of good quality vinegar
- Oil
- Vegetable salad greens

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and cook with salt until well done
2. Coat with one quarter ounce of saltpeter
3. Place in a serving dish with two bay leaves, black pepper powder, a little nutmeg, a sprig of thyme, one teaspoon of salt, and one cup of good quality vinegar
4. Cover and leave out in the night air for three nights, turning it every day
5. On the fourth day, place under a press
6. Serve sliced with a vegetable salad prepared with plenty of oil and vinegar
