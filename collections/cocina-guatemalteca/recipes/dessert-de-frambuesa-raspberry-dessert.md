## Ingredients

- raspberries without liquid (2 cups)
- bread crumbs (2 cups)
- cinnamon powder (0.5 teaspoon)
- butter (1 tablespoon)
- sugar (2 tablespoons)
- whipped cream sweetened and thick (for serving)

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and cinnamon together.
2. In a buttered platter, spread half of the bread crumbs.
3. Cover with raspberries.
4. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon mixture and dot with pieces of butter.
5. Cover with the remaining bread crumbs, sugar, and butter.
6. Place in the oven until golden brown.
7. Serve with sweetened, thick whipped cream.
