## Ingredients

- turtle
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- sweet pepper
- powdered pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- fried bread slices with butter
- soda cracker crumbs
- 10 tomatoes
- 1 onion head
- 1 cup of good broth
- 2 beaten eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the turtle as for a broth soup and chop the meat
2. Fry the meat in a soffritto made of tomato, garlic, onion, sweet pepper, powdered pepper, and salt
3. Prepare lemon juice in fried bread slices with butter, some soda cracker crumbs, and a sauce made from 10 tomatoes and 1 ground and fried onion head dissolved in 1 cup of good broth
4. In a baking dish, layer butter, bread and crumbs, turtle meat, and tomato sauce until full
5. Cover with 2 beaten eggs as if to coat in egg and place in a gentle oven or over two fires
