## Ingredients

- 1 pound sugar
- drops of lemon juice
- 8 very ripe peaches, peeled with small cuts on sides
- 1.5 glasses cooked milk
- 4 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 small glass sherry
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Place sugar over fire and clarify with drops of lemon juice
2. Return to fire and add eight very ripe peeled peaches with small cuts on sides
3. Leave until the syrup is very thick
4. Remove peaches and arrange well-drained on a platter
5. In a saucepan, place 1.5 glasses cooked milk with peeled and ground almonds and egg yolks
6. Place over fire and after boiling ten minutes, add the peach syrup
7. When it reaches proper consistency, add one small glass of sherry
8. Cover the peaches with this mixture, sprinkle with cinnamon powder, and place in the oven with heat on top
