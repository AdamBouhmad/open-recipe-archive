## Ingredients

- peas
- hot water
- one tablespoon lard
- a small piece of sugar
- salt
- one onion head

## Directions

1. Place well-washed peas in a pot with one cup of water, one tablespoon of lard, a small piece of sugar, salt, and one peeled onion head cut in half
2. Once cooked, serve hot
