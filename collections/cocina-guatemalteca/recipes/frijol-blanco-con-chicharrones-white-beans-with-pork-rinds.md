## Ingredients

- white beans
- large white pork rinds
- salt
- butter
- pickled vegetables, chopped
- slices of black chorizo
- pieces of cooked ham
- oregano powder
- two roasted tomatoes
- a sprig of thyme
- one teaspoon of vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. Cook separately some large white pork rinds with salt
3. Grease a pan with butter and layer white beans in it
4. Add a layer of pork rinds mixed with chopped pickled vegetables, slices of black chorizo, pieces of cooked ham, and oregano powder
5. Add another layer of white beans, ensuring this is the last layer
6. Grind two roasted tomatoes with a sprig of thyme
7. Dissolve with one teaspoon of vinegar and a little water seasoned with salt
8. Cover the beans with this mixture
9. Place over two fires to bring to a boil
10. Serve hot
