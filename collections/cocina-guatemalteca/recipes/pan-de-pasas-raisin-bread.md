## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- four eggs
- half a pound of sifted flour
- one small glass of milk
- one large tablespoon of Royal
- one glass of sherry
- half a pound of pitted raisins
- lemon zest if desired

## Directions

1. Beat half a pound of sugar with half a pound of butter
2. Mix in four eggs and beat well
3. Mix in half a pound of sifted flour, one small glass of milk, one large tablespoon of Royal, one glass of sherry and half a pound of pitted raisins
4. Add lemon zest if desired
5. Put in greased baking dishes in the oven
