## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Parsley
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for fifteen minutes.
2. Remove them and place in cold water.
3. Peel them and cut them into whole slices.
4. Put a pan on the fire with a small teaspoon of butter.
5. Add a small teaspoon of flour, stirring constantly so it does not lump.
6. Chop a little parsley and add pepper powder.
7. Cover the egg slices with this mixture and serve hot.
