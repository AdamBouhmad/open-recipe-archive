## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Water
- Salt
- Butter
- Hard cheese powder
- Truffles or Extremadura chorizo (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni in water with salt
2. When cooked, remove and butter a pan with one tablespoon of butter
3. Place a layer of macaroni and a layer of hard cheese powder
4. If desired add a layer of truffles or Extremadura chorizo
5. Alternate the layers so the last one is cheese and place in the oven
