## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- two bay leaves
- two dried chiles, soaked and seeded
- two cloves garlic, fried
- oregano
- Castilian peppercorns
- sprig thyme
- piece of bread, golden in lard
- two spoonfuls vinegar
- one cup broth
- one glass oil

## Directions

1. Soak the cod and cook in water with two bay leaves
2. Grind two soaked and seeded dried chiles with two fried cloves of garlic, a little oregano, some Castilian peppercorns, a sprig of thyme and a piece of bread browned in lard
3. Mix all this with two spoonfuls of vinegar and one cup of the same broth
4. Fry in one glass of very hot oil
