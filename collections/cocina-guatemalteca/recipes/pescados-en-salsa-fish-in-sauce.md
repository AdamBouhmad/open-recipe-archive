## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- two onion heads
- fish, clean and scaled
- four to six roasted tomatoes
- oil
- onion heads, chopped
- oregano powder
- one teaspoon vinegar
- olives
- onions in vinegar

## Directions

1. Place water, salt, and two onion heads in a dish.
2. Put the very clean and scaled fish in this.
3. When the fish is cooked without falling apart, grind four to six roasted tomatoes.
4. Fry chopped onion heads in oil.
5. Add the tomato with oregano powder, one teaspoon of vinegar, and salt.
6. Place a layer of this sauce on the platter.
7. Place the fish on top and cover with another layer of sauce.
8. Garnish with olives and onions in vinegar.
