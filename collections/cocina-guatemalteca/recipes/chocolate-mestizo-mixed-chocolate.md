## Ingredients

- three pounds of cacao
- one pound of rice (well washed)
- five and a half pounds of sugar
- one quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper
- one ounce of anise
- eight chili strings
- one small piece of ginger
- one very small piece of chuchupate

## Directions

1. Toast three pounds of cacao and one pound of very well washed rice
2. Add five and a half pounds of sugar, one quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper, one ounce of anise, eight chili strings, one small piece of ginger and one very small piece of chuchupate, all well toasted without burning
3. Grind the rice very well first with all these ingredients, the cacao and sugar together
4. When well ground mix in the rice and give it two or three more passes until everything is very well mixed
5. Then finish the chocolate as the previous recipe
6. When consumed it must be boiled very well so that the rice cooks
