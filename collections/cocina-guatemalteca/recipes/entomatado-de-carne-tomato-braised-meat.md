## Ingredients

- cooked meat
- lard
- tomatoes, peeled
- parsley, a little
- garlic, small cloves, roasted

## Directions

1. Quarter the cooked meat and lightly soften it
2. Fry in lard
3. Chop peeled tomatoes and add to the meat with a little parsley and some roasted small garlic cloves
4. When it has simmered, remove the parsley and garlic
5. Serve hot
