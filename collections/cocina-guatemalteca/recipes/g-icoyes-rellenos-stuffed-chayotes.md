## Ingredients

- tender chayotes
- white beans cooked
- small shrimp
- cumin
- achiote

## Directions

1. Cook the small chayotes and hollow them out
2. Have white beans cooked with small shrimp
3. Fry them and in their prepared sauce grind cumin and achiote
4. Fill the chayotes with this
5. If desired, grind a little bean to make a sauce and serve it
