## Ingredients

- chicken livers
- ham
- parsley
- onion
- pepper powder
- butter
- salt
- thick thread
- oil

## Directions

1. Chop the chicken livers with ham, parsley and onion
2. Add pepper powder
3. Remove all bones from the chickens
4. Rub with butter and salt
5. Stuff with the previous mixture adding four ounces of ham cut lengthwise
6. Sew with thick thread
7. Rub with a spoonful of oil
8. Place in a dish in the oven or wrap in paper spread with butter, and place on a rotisserie over very low heat
