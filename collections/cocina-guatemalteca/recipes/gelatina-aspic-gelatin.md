## Ingredients

- chickens, three
- pork feet, four, very white
- lemon juice, from two lemons
- fish glue, one sheet
- foreign gelatin, one tablespoon
- chicken pieces
- assorted vegetables, well cooked
- shrimp, well cleaned
- hard-boiled eggs
- asparagus tips

## Directions

1. Boil three chickens and four very white pork feet until they fall apart
2. Remove and let cool in a very cool place
3. The next day, remove all the fat and strain through a fine cloth
4. Place on the fire, add juice from two lemons, one sheet of fish glue, and one tablespoon of foreign gelatin
5. When well thickened, pour into a mold with small chicken pieces, all kinds of well-cooked vegetables, well-cleaned shrimp, hard-boiled egg pieces, and asparagus tips
6. Place the mold on ice until this jelly sets
7. Can be placed on fried eggs for a very nice presentation and exquisite flavor
