## Ingredients

- ten eggs
- sugar equal to the weight of five eggs
- flour equal to the weight of seven eggs
- one tablespoon of Royal
- two ounces of melted lard or butter

## Directions

1. Take ten eggs and weigh the sugar to equal the weight of five eggs; flour equal to the weight of seven eggs and one tablespoon of Royal
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar, beat the whites as for nougat
3. Mix everything with two ounces of melted lard or butter
4. When well mixed put in the oven in a greased mold
