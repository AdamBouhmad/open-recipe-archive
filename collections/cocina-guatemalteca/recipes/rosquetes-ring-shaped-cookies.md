## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- one ounce of butter
- one ounce of lard
- a little milk
- butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, butter, and lard very well with a little milk
2. Form the rings and place them in the oven
3. When they take color, remove them and brush with butter
4. Return them to the oven to brown
