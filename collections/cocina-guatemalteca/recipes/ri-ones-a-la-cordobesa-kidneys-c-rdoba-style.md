## Ingredients

- kidney
- butter or lard
- flour
- onion
- parsley
- broth
- dry sherry

## Directions

1. After washing the kidney, cut it into slices and fry them in butter or lard
2. Remove the kidney and in that same fat fry one tablespoon of flour with onion and parsley, both chopped
3. Add a little broth from the pot so that a thin sauce forms
4. Add one tablespoon of dry sherry and put the kidneys in to give a slight boil, as if they boil too much they become tough
5. Serve hot
