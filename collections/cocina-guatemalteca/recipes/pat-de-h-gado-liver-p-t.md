## Ingredients

- one pound of beef liver
- one half pound of pork liver
- three cooked egg yolks
- one cup of milk
- two ounces of butter
- pepper powder
- chopped onion
- salt
- a little Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of beef liver and one half pound of pork liver
2. Add three cooked egg yolks, one cup of milk and two ounces of butter, sufficient pepper powder, finely chopped onion, salt and a little Worcestershire sauce
3. Place in a dish in a water bath
4. This is good for making sandwiches
