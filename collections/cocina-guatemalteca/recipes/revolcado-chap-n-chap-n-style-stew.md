## Ingredients

- pig's head
- lard from the pig's head
- tomato
- tomatillo
- guaque chile
- green chile
- two cold tortillas
- achiote

## Directions

1. Cook the pig's head
2. Let it cool and remove the lard
3. In that same lard, fry a mixture that has been cooked beforehand consisting of plenty of tomato and tomatillo, one guaque chile and one green chile
4. Grind two cold tortillas and a small piece of achiote and add to the mixture
5. Add the pig's head cut into pieces
