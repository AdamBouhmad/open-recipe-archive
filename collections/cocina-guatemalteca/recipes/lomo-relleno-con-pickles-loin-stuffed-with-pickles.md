## Ingredients

- Loin
- Pickle mustard
- Pickles
- Bell peppers
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Bay leaf
- Tomato
- Onion

## Directions

1. Butterfly a loin well and spread the mustard from the pickles
2. Cut the pickles lengthwise along with bell peppers
3. Fill the loin with this mixture, sew it closed
4. Cook with water, vinegar, salt, bay leaf, tomato, and onion
5. Serve with sauce or salad
