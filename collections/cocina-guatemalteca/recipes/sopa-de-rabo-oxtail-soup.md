## Ingredients

- Three to four pounds of raw oxtail
- Lard
- Cold water
- Bay leaves
- Thyme branch
- Chopped parsley
- Fried seasoning
- Flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take three to four pounds of raw oxtail cut into very small pieces
2. Once everything is finely minced, drop in a spoonful of well-heated lard and be careful to stir it so it does not stick
3. When browned, cover with cold water, two bay leaves and a thyme branch, a little chopped parsley, fried seasoning, and a spoonful of flour fried in butter
4. Cover and let it boil for four hours (charcoal fire should frequently be used)
