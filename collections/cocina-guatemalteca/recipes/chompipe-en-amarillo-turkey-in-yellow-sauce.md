## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), in pieces
- salt
- carrots
- green beans
- chayote squash
- potatoes
- peas
- green fava beans
- bicarbonate of soda
- 1 tablespoon of lard
- 2 to 3 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 to 3 onions
- bread crumbs
- Castilian pepper
- 1 small piece of achiote

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey in pieces with salt.
2. Chop carrots, green beans, chayote, and potatoes.
3. Cook these vegetables separately, with salt. Cook carrots and potatoes separately, while green beans, peas, and fava beans are cooked together with a little bicarbonate of soda to bring out a good color.
4. Once all is cooked, place a pan over heat with lard, peeled and chopped tomatoes, garlic clove, and onions.
5. Once well fried, add a little of the same broth and the turkey, which should be well cooked.
6. Add bread crumbs mixed with Castilian pepper and a small piece of achiote.
7. Before serving, add the vegetables so they do not discolor.
