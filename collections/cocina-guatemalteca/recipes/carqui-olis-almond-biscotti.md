## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- 1 pound of dry sugar
- half a glass of water
- half an ounce of ammonium bicarbonate dissolved in water
- pieces of almonds

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of flour on a very clean table
2. Make a well
3. Add one pound of dry sugar
4. Add half a glass of water
5. Add half an ounce of ammonium bicarbonate dissolved in the water
6. Mix everything very well
7. Pour into pans coated with butter, inserting small pieces of almonds all around
8. When cooked, remove and cut into long pieces
9. Place on tins and return to the oven until golden
