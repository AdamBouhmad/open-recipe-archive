## Ingredients

- good broth
- half a pound of ground meat
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- saffron
- cinnamon stick, finely ground
- three eggs
- fried bread
- chopped parsley
- rice fritters (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare very good broth
2. Grind meat with cumin, Castilian pepper, saffron, and finely ground cinnamon
3. Mix in three eggs and blend very well
4. Drop meatballs of desired size into the prepared broth
5. Just before serving, add fried bread and chopped parsley
6. If desired, add rice fritters
7. Serve hot
