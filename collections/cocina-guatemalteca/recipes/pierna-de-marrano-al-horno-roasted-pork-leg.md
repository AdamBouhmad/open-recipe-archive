## Ingredients

- parsley, ground
- onion stems
- powdered pepper
- salt
- 1 glass of vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind plenty of parsley and onion stems with powdered pepper
2. Leave the pork leg in this brine for a day and a night
3. The next day, place in the oven with 1 glass of vinegar
