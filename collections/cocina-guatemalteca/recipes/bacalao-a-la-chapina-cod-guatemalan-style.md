## Ingredients

- Soaked cod
- Four to six large tomatoes per pound of cod
- Four cloves of garlic
- Three onion heads
- One guaque chile, fried
- A little oregano
- A little cumin
- Castilian peppercorns
- A small piece of annatto
- Half a French bread, fried in lard
- Three tablespoons of good vinegar
- One cup of cod cooking broth
- One glass of oil
- Pickled vegetables, optional
- Green peas, well cooked, optional

## Directions

1. Soak and cook the cod as with previous recipes.
2. Roast four to six large tomatoes, four garlic cloves, three onion heads, and one fried guaque chile.
3. Grind this roasted mixture with a little oregano, a little cumin, some Castilian peppercorns, a small piece of annatto, and half a French bread fried in lard.
4. Mix this ground mixture with three tablespoons of good vinegar and one cup of the cod cooking broth.
5. Fry this mixture in one glass of oil.
6. Place the cod to boil for half an hour in this sauce.
7. When serving, if desired, add chopped pickled vegetables and well-cooked green peas.
