## Ingredients

- Chickpeas
- Beef tripe, clean and cooked with salt
- One tablespoon lard
- Four to six peeled and chopped tomatoes
- Large onion slices, very thin
- Pepper powder
- Chopped parsley
- Vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickpeas as previously described
2. Have beef tripe cooked with salt
3. Fry in a pan with one tablespoon of lard the peeled and chopped tomatoes with slices of large onion, very thin
4. When everything is well fried, mix in the chickpeas and tripe cut into small pieces
5. Add pepper powder and chopped parsley, and if desired, a small teaspoon of vinegar
