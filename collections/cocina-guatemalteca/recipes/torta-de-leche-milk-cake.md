## Ingredients

- three glasses of milk sweetened to taste
- three egg yolks
- egg bread
- raisins
- chopped almonds
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. In three glasses of sweetened milk place three egg yolks and enough crumbled egg bread to form the cake
2. Add raisins and chopped almonds
3. Mix everything well and place in a well-buttered cooking pot
4. Put in the oven until it is cooked
