## Ingredients

- leeks
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Slice the leeks without the stems
2. Blanch in salted water
3. When done, arrange in a salad bowl with a little vinegar, oil, salt to taste
4. Add pepper powder if desired
