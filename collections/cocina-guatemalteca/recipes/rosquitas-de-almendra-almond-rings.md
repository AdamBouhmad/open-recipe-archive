## Ingredients

- Half pound of peeled and dry almonds
- One egg white
- Five ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Grind half pound of peeled and dry almonds
2. Add one egg white and five ounces of powdered sugar
3. Wrap in a damp napkin and leave overnight
4. The next day, place in a pastry bag to form rings
5. Bake in a hot oven
