## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- salt
- three tablespoons of butter
- juice of one lemon
- pepper powder
- five hard boiled eggs
- three eggs for sauce
- egg yolks
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- one quarter part of milk
- one quarter part of cream

## Directions

1. Cook the spinach with water and salt, when well cooked remove the water until it drains well, chop very finely
2. Put three tablespoons of butter, one of lemon juice, salt and pepper powder to taste
3. Cook five minutes on low heat, empty into a platter and garnish with chopped hard boiled eggs and serve with the following sauce:
4. Cook three eggs, immediately remove the yolks, break them up very well with salt to taste, one tablespoon of melted butter, one quarter part of milk and one quarter part of cream
