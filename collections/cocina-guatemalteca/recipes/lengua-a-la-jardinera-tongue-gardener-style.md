## Ingredients

- Green beans
- Carrot
- Chayote
- Peas
- Fava beans
- Potatoes
- Salt
- Baking soda
- Lard or butter
- Flour
- Cooked milk
- Black pepper powder
- Nutmeg
- Cooked tongue

## Directions

1. Prepare tender green beans, carrot, chayote, peas, fava beans, and potatoes, cut into small pieces as desired
2. Cook with salt, adding a little baking soda to keep the vegetables green
3. Drain when cooked
4. Heat some lard or butter in a pan and add one tablespoon of flour
5. Before it browns, add one cup of cooked milk, black pepper powder, and nutmeg
6. Add the cooked vegetables and bring to a light boil
7. Arrange the sliced tongue around the edge of a platter
8. Place the sauce in the center
9. Serve hot
