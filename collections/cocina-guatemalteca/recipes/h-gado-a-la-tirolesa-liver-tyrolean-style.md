## Ingredients

- two slices French bread
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- tomatoes
- parsley
- liver
- salt
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Fry two slices of French bread in butter until golden, then remove them
2. In the same butter fry chopped onion, one clove of garlic, three peeled chopped tomatoes and a little parsley
3. Grind the well-fried French bread and dissolve it with water
4. Let it boil
5. Have ready well-fried slices of liver with salt and some well-browned slices of potato
6. Place the liver on a platter and arrange the potatoes around it
7. Cover everything with the hot sauce
