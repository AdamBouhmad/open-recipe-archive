## Ingredients

- cooked livers
- cooked tomato
- cooked tomatillo
- garlic
- oregano
- chile
- a small piece of achiote
- one soaked French roll

## Directions

1. Cook the livers and mince them to desired size
2. Grind cooked tomato and tomatillo with garlic, oregano, chile, a small piece of achiote and a soaked French roll
3. Thin this mixture with the same broth
4. Fry it and add the liver to the sauce
