## Ingredients

- venison pieces
- lard
- salt
- two garlic cloves, crushed
- two tablespoons flour
- pepper powder
- chopped parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the venison pieces and clean them well
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. Add two crushed garlic cloves and two tablespoons of flour according to the amount of meat
4. When the flour is well browned, add pepper powder and chopped parsley
5. Cover with water and let cook until tender, serving it juicy
