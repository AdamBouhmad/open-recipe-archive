## Ingredients

- Large potatoes
- One tablespoon butter, melted
- Pepper powder
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cook large potatoes
2. Keep them in hot water until the moment of serving
3. Cut them into slices
4. Have one tablespoon of melted butter with pepper powder, chopped parsley and salt ready
5. Put the potatoes, while very hot, into this mixture
6. Cover with parmesan cheese powder
