## Ingredients

- Large tomatoes
- Half ground beef
- Half ground pork
- Pepper powder
- Salt
- Chopped parsley
- One tablespoon of butter
- One cup of broth

## Directions

1. Peel the large tomatoes with boiling water
2. Carefully remove the seeds
3. Grind half beef and half pork and mix with a little pepper powder, salt, chopped parsley, and one tablespoon of butter
4. Fill the tomatoes with this mixture
5. Place them in a casserole with one cup of broth
6. Bake in the oven
