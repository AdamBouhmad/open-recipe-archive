## Ingredients

- Potatoes, peeled
- Milk
- Salt
- Butter, cooked, one spoon

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, taking care that they remain very white
2. Place in a dish and cover with milk
3. Add a few grains of salt and a spoon of cooked butter
4. When done, serve hot with its broth
