## Ingredients

- peeled and seeded tomato, chopped
- onion, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- vinegar
- abundant oil
- salmon
- very thinly sliced onion

## Directions

1. Place chopped peeled and seeded tomato, onion and parsley in a dish with a little vinegar and abundant oil according to quantity
2. Place the salmon in this mixture, carefully mixing so it does not fall apart
3. Serve with very thinly sliced onion
