## Ingredients

- twelve egg whites
- six egg yolks
- six ounces of sugar
- six ounces of cornstarch
- two ounces of sifted bread flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff like nougat
2. Beat the yolks with sugar until well beaten
3. Combine both mixtures
4. Add the cornstarch and sifted bread flour
5. Place in a buttered pan
6. Bake in a moderate oven
