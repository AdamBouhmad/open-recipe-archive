## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- one can of squid
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Fry the rice very well.
2. Add tomato, garlic, and onion.
3. Add the can of squid and cover with water or broth.
4. When it has burst and the liquid is consumed, place on low heat.
