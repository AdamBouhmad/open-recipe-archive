## Ingredients

- preserved plums
- four ounces of sugar
- a little cream of tartar
- eight egg yolks
- fine sugar
- honey caramel glaze

## Directions

1. Open the preserved plums carefully, removing the pit.
2. Put four ounces of sugar on the fire to reach soft-ball stage.
3. Add a little cream of tartar and eight egg yolks.
4. Place on the fire, stirring constantly.
5. When it pulls away from the pan, remove from heat.
6. Once cool, knead with a little fine sugar until it becomes consistent.
7. Fill the plums with this mixture.
8. Coat the filled plums with caramel honey glaze.
