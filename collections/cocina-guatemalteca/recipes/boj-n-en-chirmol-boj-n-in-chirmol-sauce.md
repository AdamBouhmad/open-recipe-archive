## Ingredients

- bojón
- two tomatoes, peeled
- onion
- chile

## Directions

1. Peel the bojón, removing all the skin to chop only the center which is the most tender part
2. Cook with about two peeled tomatoes, onion, and chile
3. When well cooked, mash to serve as the previous recipe
4. The same is done with palmito
