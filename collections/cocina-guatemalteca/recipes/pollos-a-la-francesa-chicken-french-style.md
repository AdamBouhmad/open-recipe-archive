## Ingredients

- chickens
- flour
- butter or oil
- salt
- 4 or 5 roasted tomatoes
- 3 cloves of garlic
- raw onion
- 1 glass of Muscatel wine

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and cut them into quarters
2. Soak for a quarter hour and dry very well
3. Roll in flour and fry in butter or oil with salt
4. Roast 4 or 5 tomatoes and 3 cloves of garlic, chop raw onion
5. Fry everything in the fat in which the chickens were fried with 1 glass of Muscatel wine
