## Ingredients

- one turkey
- salt
- butter
- pepper powder
- four to five tablespoons of vinegar
- two bay leaves
- onion

## Directions

1. Kill the turkey one day before cooking it, otherwise it will not be tender
2. Clean it very well and prick it with a fork
3. Rub with salt, butter and pepper powder
4. Place in a pan with four to five tablespoons of vinegar, two bay leaves and chopped onion
5. Put in the oven leaving it there for three hours, basting with its juices every five minutes
