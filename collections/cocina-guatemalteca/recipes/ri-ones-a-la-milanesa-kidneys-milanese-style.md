## Ingredients

- kidney
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper powder
- butter or lard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the kidney very well and cut it into very thin slices
2. Mince one clove of garlic very thin and parsley very finely, and mix everything very well with a little salt and pepper powder
3. Have a pan on the fire with butter or lard and fry the kidney over very low heat
4. The diners should already be seated at the table so they can eat the dish very hot after it has been on the fire for five minutes
5. Serve with potatoes cooked plain
