## Ingredients

- Ham (York or domestic), cooked
- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop some ham, whether York or domestic, very well cooked.
2. Mix it with two eggs per person.
3. Season with salt.
4. Add it to a tablespoon of very hot butter.
5. Stir with great lightness so they mix well.
6. Ensure they come out tender but not raw.
