## Ingredients

- Half pound of flour
- Five egg yolks
- Five ounces of ground sugar
- Two ounces of butter
- Three ounces of lard
- One level teaspoon of Royal
- Half glass of milk cooked with plenty of cinnamon
- Two ounces of chopped and peeled almonds

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board
2. Add egg yolks, ground sugar, butter, lard, Royal, cinnamon milk, and chopped almonds
3. Knead very well
4. Roll out with a rolling pin
5. Cut cookies
6. Form small holes with a stick
7. Put in the oven
