## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- two ounces of almonds
- two ounces of peeled hazelnuts
- Castilian pepper
- piece of nutmeg
- water
- thread
- cloth
- platter
- bottle of dry sherry
- two orange leaves
- sprig of thyme
- two bay leaves
- butter
- salad or sauce

## Directions

1. Clean it thoroughly like the previous recipe; debone it if desired, or leave it without deboning
2. Grind two ounces of almonds and two of peeled hazelnuts with Castilian pepper and a piece of nutmeg, wash the mortar with a little water, rub the piglet with this mixture and sew with thread
3. Place in a cloth very tightly wrapped, put it in a platter where it has plenty of room and cook with a bottle of dry sherry, two orange leaves, a sprig of thyme and two bay leaves
4. After two hours of simmering, remove it, rub with butter and put in the oven to brown
5. Serve with salad or sauce
