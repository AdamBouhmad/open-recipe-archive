## Ingredients

- good piece of meat
- onion heads, peeled and split
- tomatoes
- garlic
- Castilian pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Fry a good piece of meat
2. Make several small cuts in it
3. Add peeled onion heads split in half, tomatoes, garlic, Castilian pepper, and clove
4. Remove the foam from the pot
5. Let it cook very well
6. Remove the spices before serving
