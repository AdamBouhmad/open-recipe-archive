## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- six ounces of white dry sugar
- half a pound of sifted flour
- six egg yolks
- six egg whites
- one quarter glass of milk
- one teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the butter with the sugar until very fine
2. Add the sifted flour and egg yolks, mix very well
3. Fold in the beaten egg whites, milk, and vanilla
4. Pour into a buttered mold
5. Bake in the oven
