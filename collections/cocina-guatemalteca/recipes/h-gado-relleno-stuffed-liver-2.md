## Ingredients

- four ounces pork meat
- two ounces beef
- onion
- tomato
- chorizo
- ham
- hard-boiled egg
- pepper powder
- cinnamon
- clove
- capers
- pickled vegetables
- wine
- vinegar
- salt
- lard or butter
- liver (lamb, pork, or beef)

## Directions

1. Chop the pork and beef meat, which should be parboiled, and fry them with onion and tomato
2. Add chorizo, parboiled ham, chopped hard-boiled egg, pepper powder, cinnamon, clove, capers, pickled vegetables, a little wine, vinegar and salt
3. When half fried, stuff the liver with this mixture, whether it be lamb, pork or beef liver
4. Sew the liver with thick thread
5. Coat it with lard or butter with salt and place it in the oven
