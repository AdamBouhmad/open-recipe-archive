## Ingredients

- Tomato
- Miltomate, roasted or cooked
- Onion head, roasted
- Cumin
- Oregano
- Garlic clove
- Achiote
- Chile, well toasted (optional for spicy version)

## Directions

1. Grind tomato and miltomate (roasted or cooked) with a roasted onion head, a bit of cumin, oregano, a small garlic clove, and a small piece of achiote
2. Add ground well-toasted chile if a spicy version is desired
3. Add this mixture to meat that is already fried
4. Let it come to a boil a few times so it does not dry out
