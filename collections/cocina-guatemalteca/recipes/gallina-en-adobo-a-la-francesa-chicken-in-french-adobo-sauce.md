## Ingredients

- 1 calf's foot
- salt
- 1/2 pound of chopped ham
- 1 chicken, well cleaned
- Castilian pepper powder
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook a calf's foot with salt until more than cooked.
2. Remove it and debone.
3. Place 1/2 pound of chopped ham in a pan, put the well-cleaned chicken in it, and let fry for about ten minutes.
4. Add the deboned calf's foot, Castilian pepper powder, clove, cinnamon, and salt.
