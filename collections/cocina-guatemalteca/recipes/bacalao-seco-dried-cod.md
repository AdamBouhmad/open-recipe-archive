## Ingredients

- dried cod
- water
- one cup oil
- four or six onions, quartered
- two or three peeled and chopped tomatoes
- two raw ground tomatoes
- two spoonfuls vinegar
- salt
- pepper powder
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the cod overnight in water
2. The next day cook in very clean water
3. When cooked, remove and cut into pieces lengthwise as desired
4. Place a pot on the fire with one cup of oil and four or six onions cut into quarters
5. When they take color, add two or three peeled and chopped tomatoes, two raw ground tomatoes and two spoonfuls of vinegar
6. Season with salt, pepper powder and minced parsley
7. Add the cod with half a cup of the broth in which it was cooked
8. Serve hot or cold
