## Ingredients

- Pork head
- Pork brains
- Pork ears
- Pork sweetbreads
- Pepper powder
- Dried thyme powder
- Parsley
- Bay leaf powder
- Salt
- Dry sherry
- Lemon juice and zest
- Nutmeg powder
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Leeks

## Directions

1. Split the head in half and remove the brains and bones, being careful to keep the skin intact
2. Chop all the meat
3. Cut the ears very carefully so the skin does not break
4. Chop some sweetbreads
5. Mix the chopped meat with pepper powder, dried thyme powder, parsley, bay leaf powder, and salt
6. Mix in one cup of dry sherry, lemon juice, lemon zest, and a small piece of nutmeg powder
7. Place the mixture on the skin and sew it closed with thick thread
8. Cook in water with bay leaves, thyme, a bundle of leeks, and salt for three hours
9. Place in a tin mold and press with a stone to give it a pleasant shape
