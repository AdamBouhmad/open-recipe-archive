## Ingredients

- one chicken
- salt
- two red tomatoes
- one guaque chile
- two egg yolks
- chicken liver
- Castilian peppers
- cinnamon
- saffron
- achiote
- one francés soaked in vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken with salt
2. Roast two red tomatoes and grind them with one cooked guaque chile, two egg yolks and the well-cooked chicken liver with some Castilian peppers, a small piece of cinnamon, a little saffron, a small piece of achiote, and one francés soaked in vinegar
3. Dissolve all this with the same broth, fry it and add the pieces of chicken
4. When serving, put chopped parsley on top
