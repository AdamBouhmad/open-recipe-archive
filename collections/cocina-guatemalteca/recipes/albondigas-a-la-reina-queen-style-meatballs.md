## Ingredients

- beef and pork in equal proportions or only beef
- saffron
- Castilian pepper
- a piece of cinnamon
- a little cumin
- white bread or bread powder
- three raw eggs per pound of meat
- salt
- raisins
- almonds
- cooked ham
- chorizo
- olives
- raw tomato
- parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Grind beef and pork in equal proportions or only beef with a little saffron, Castilian pepper, a piece of cinnamon, a little cumin and white bread or bread powder; grind first in a machine then with all the spices to refine further
2. Mix with three raw eggs and a little salt per pound of meat
3. Take one tablespoon of meat and make a patty not too thin
4. Place inside a raisin, half an almond, a piece of cooked ham, a small slice of chorizo and an olive
5. Cover everything forming it into a ball shape
6. Fry in very hot butter over low heat
7. Grind a little raw tomato and add minced parsley and a little water to taste
8. As soon as it boils, remove and bathe the meatballs with this and serve hot
