## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- ham strips
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- parsley
- bay leaves
- clove
- thyme
- leeks
- spearmint
- onion
- water
- rose-colored aspic

## Directions

1. Lard the leg with ham strips, salt and pepper
2. Place in a dish with vinegar, pepper, parsley, bay leaves, clove, thyme, leeks, spearmint and onion pieces
3. Leave in this brine, turning it every day, for four days
4. After four days, cook covered with water
5. Once cooked, remove and let cool
6. The next day, cover with an aspic that has a rose color
