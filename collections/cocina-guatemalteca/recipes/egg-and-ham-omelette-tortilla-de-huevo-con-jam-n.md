## Ingredients

- Ham
- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut into long, thin pieces a piece of ham, whether domestic or foreign.
2. Beat the eggs (white and yolk).
3. Season with salt.
4. Form the omelette and fry in hot butter.
