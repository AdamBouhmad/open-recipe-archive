## Ingredients

- six liters of water
- one large tablespoon of salt
- eight pounds of very good bone
- one fat hen or three large fat chickens
- eight to ten leeks
- one sheep's head
- two bay leaves
- ten Castilian peppercorns
- one glass of dry sherry (optional)
- four egg yolks (optional) or one yolk per person (optional)

## Directions

1. Place six liters of water in a pot with one large tablespoon of salt, eight pounds of very good bone, one fat hen or three large fat chickens, eight to ten leeks, one sheep's head, two bay leaves, and ten Castilian peppercorns
2. Let it boil for four hours over steady heat
3. Remove from heat and let it cool
4. Skim off the fat very well and leave it in a cool place
5. The next day, heat it and serve it
6. If desired, add one glass of dry sherry or four egg yolks to make it a thin broth, or if preferred, add one yolk per person
