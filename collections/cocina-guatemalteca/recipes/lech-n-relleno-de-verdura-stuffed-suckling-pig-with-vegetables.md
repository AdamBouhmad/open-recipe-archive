## Ingredients

- 2 ounces almonds
- 2 ounces hazelnuts
- 2 ounces peeled walnuts
- Castilian peppers
- salt
- a little nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- chayote
- string beans
- potatoes
- carrots
- tender peas
- lard
- peeled and chopped tomato
- onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- breadcrumb powder
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- cooked chicken pieces
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 additional ounce ground almond

## Directions

1. Clean the suckling pig exactly as described in previous recipes
2. Grind together 2 ounces almonds, 2 ounces hazelnuts, 2 ounces peeled walnuts with Castilian peppers, salt, and a little nutmeg
3. Mix this with 2 tablespoons melted butter
4. Prick the suckling pig all over with a fork and rub it inside and out with this mixture, leaving it overnight
5. The next day, cut into very small pieces: chayote, string beans, potatoes, and carrots in sufficient quantity and cook with salt and tender peas
6. Once cooked, remove and place in a strainer to drain
7. Put a pan with lard on the fire and add plenty of peeled and chopped tomato with onion
8. Add the vegetables with 1 tablespoon butter, breadcrumb powder, pepper, clove, and cinnamon
9. Add pieces of cooked chicken and 1 tablespoon vinegar to the filling
10. Fill the suckling pig with this mixture and sew it closed with thick thread
11. Place in a baking dish well coated with butter, with 1 additional ounce ground almond, basting it constantly with its juices while cooking
