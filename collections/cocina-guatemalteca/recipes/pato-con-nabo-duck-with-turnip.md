## Ingredients

- duck
- turnip
- sugar
- achiote
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Clean the duck and place in a pan to fry
2. When it takes color, remove it
3. In the same fat, fry the turnip cut into small pieces
4. When the turnip takes color, remove it and add one tablespoon of sugar
5. Make a seasoning paste with achiote, salt, parsley, onion, half a head of garlic, and a bay leaf
6. Place the duck in this mixture and when it is tender, add the turnips, taking care that they do not fall apart
7. Serve hot
