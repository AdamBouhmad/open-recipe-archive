## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Water
- Lard or butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Broth
- Chopped oysters
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots, grind them and strain in a little water
2. Fry in lard or butter and a small amount of flour
3. Add milk to the carrots
4. Add broth and some chopped oysters with a little butter
