## Ingredients

- Lamb liver
- Salt
- Castilian pepper
- Dried chile
- Achiote
- Epazote

## Directions

1. Cook the liver with salt, cut into pieces
2. Grind a very fine paste with Castilian pepper, a dried chile and a small piece of achiote
3. Dissolve this in the same broth and add to the liver with a branch of epazote washed in hot water
