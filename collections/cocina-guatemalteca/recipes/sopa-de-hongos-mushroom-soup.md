## Ingredients

- sufficient meat for broth
- chayote, carrot, cabbage, green beans, broad beans, peas (all well washed and finely chopped)
- sufficient quantity of mushrooms
- fried seasoning
- saffron water
- pepper powder
- one spoonful of flour
- butter
- chicken, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Prepare a good broth with sufficient meat
2. Chop chayote, carrot, cabbage, green beans, broad beans, and peas very finely
3. Add a very sufficient quantity of mushrooms
4. When everything is well cooked, add fried seasoning, a little saffron water, pepper powder, and one spoonful of flour fried in butter
5. Add the finely chopped chicken
6. Serve the soup hot
