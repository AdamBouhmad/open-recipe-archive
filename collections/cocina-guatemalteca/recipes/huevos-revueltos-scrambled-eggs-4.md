## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Chopped tomato
- Salt
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Put a pan on the fire with one tablespoon of lard, chop a tomato and when it is half fried, break the eggs with salt and a little pepper powder and scramble with much lightness in the lard so they come out tender, cooked, but not hard.
