## Ingredients

- three ounces of dried salpor
- one ounce of melon seed
- half an ounce of peeled almond
- water
- milk
- cinnamon stick
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook three ounces of dried salpor with only water
2. When it is cooked, grind one ounce of melon seed and half an ounce of peeled almond
3. Strain with a glass of water
4. Place on the fire and when it thickens add milk and a small stick of cinnamon and sugar to taste
