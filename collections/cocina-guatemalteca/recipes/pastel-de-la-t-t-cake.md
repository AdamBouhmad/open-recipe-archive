## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter
- 8 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 0.5 pound fine yucca flour, sifted
- 0.5 pound bread flour
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder
- 1 ounce chopped raisins and almonds
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 0.5 pound of sugar and 0.5 pound of butter
2. When well beaten, mix with the 8 egg whites that will have been beaten like for nougat
3. Add 1 tablespoon of cream, 0.5 pound of fine sifted yucca flour, 0.5 pound of bread flour, 1 teaspoon of Royal and 1 ounce of chopped raisins and almonds
4. When everything is well mixed, add a dash of cornstarch
5. Place in a mold greased with lard and bake
