## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- onion, chopped
- tomato sauce, cooked
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper powder
- parsley, chopped
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Hard-boil the eggs, peel them and cut in half
2. Heat butter in a pan with chopped onion, when it takes on the color of tea add a little cooked tomato sauce, finely chopped mushrooms, salt, pepper powder, chopped parsley and chopped egg yolks
3. Mix everything well and fill the egg whites with this mixture
4. Put a little butter and bread crumbs on top
5. Bake in the oven for five minutes
