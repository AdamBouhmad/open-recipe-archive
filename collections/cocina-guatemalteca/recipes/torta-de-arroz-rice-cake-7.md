## Ingredients

- 5 glasses milk
- 0.5 pounds very well washed rice
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 4 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 1 pound sugar
- 2 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks
- a little marquesote powder or egg bread

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 pounds of very well washed rice in 5 glasses of milk with 1 cinnamon stick
2. When well cooked, add 4 ounces of peeled and ground almonds and 1 pound of sugar
3. When it reaches the point, lower the heat and when cool, add 2 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks
4. Add a little marquesote powder or egg bread
