## Ingredients

- pineapple
- meat of one coconut
- water from the coconut
- three pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and grind the pineapple with the meat of one coconut if it is not too acidic
2. Dissolve everything with the water from the coconut
3. Have three pounds of sugar clarified beforehand to medium syrup stage
4. Add the coconut mixture and cook until it reaches the soft-ball stage (punto de ante)
5. Decorate with raisins and almonds
