## Ingredients

- thin slices of French bread
- garlic
- lard
- cooked belly pork
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry very thin slices of French bread
2. Spread with ground garlic
3. Line a pan with lard and place a layer of French bread and a layer of thin strips of well-cooked belly pork alternately, making sure the last layer is French bread
4. Season with a bit of salt, pepper, vinegar, and oil
5. Place on very low heat
