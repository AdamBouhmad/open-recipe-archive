## Ingredients

- feet
- salt
- miltomate, toasted
- Chiapas peppers
- zambo chile, toasted
- piece of French bread, soaked
- broth
- lard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the feet well with salt.
2. Chop them, removing all the small bones.
3. Grind a bit of toasted miltomate with ten to fifteen Chiapas peppers, a toasted zambo chile and a piece of soaked French bread.
4. Dissolve all this with a little of the broth in which the feet were cooked.
5. Fry in a little lard.
6. Add the feet to this mixture.
7. Serve with plain potatoes.
