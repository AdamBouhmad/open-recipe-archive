## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Manteca (lard), one tablespoon
- Lemon juice, from one lemon
- Onions, two
- Broth, one ladle
- Flour, one teaspoon, dissolved in a bit of water
- Pepper powder
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes in water with salt.
2. Once cooked, cut into four pieces and let drain.
3. Place in a pan with one tablespoon of manteca, the juice of one lemon, two onions, one ladle of broth, one teaspoon of flour dissolved in a bit of water.
4. Let simmer about ten minutes.
5. Remove the artichokes, strain the sauce and return the artichokes to it so they give a light simmer.
6. Serve with pepper powder and chopped parsley.
