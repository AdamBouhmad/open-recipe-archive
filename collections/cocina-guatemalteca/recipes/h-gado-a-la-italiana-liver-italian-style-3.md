## Ingredients

- liver
- white wine
- Castilian vinegar
- oil
- peeled tomatoes
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Clean the liver well and cut it into pieces
2. Place in a bowl and dissolve it by adding white wine little by little to the desired consistency
3. Add a little Castilian vinegar
4. Prepare a pan with sufficient oil, peeled tomatoes, onion, garlic, parsley, and pepper powder
5. Fry this mixture and when done, add the liver
6. Let it boil for just a few minutes so it does not become tough
