## Ingredients

- large onions
- salt
- boiling water
- vinegar, one cup
- oil, half cup
- cooked guaque chiles, eight, ground
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Clean large onions and cut the top
2. Pour boiling water with one tablespoon of salt
3. The next day, put on the fire with one cup of vinegar and half cup of oil, salt to taste, and eight cooked and well-ground guaque chiles
4. Add a pinch of pepper powder if desired
5. After half an hour, remove and serve hot
