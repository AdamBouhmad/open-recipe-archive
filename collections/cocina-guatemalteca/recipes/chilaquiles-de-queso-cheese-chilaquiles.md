## Ingredients

- Tortillas
- Fresh cheese
- Eggs, two for ten tortillas
- Salt
- Half a teaspoon of flour
- Two peeled tomatoes
- Two onions
- One or two green chiles

## Directions

1. Take the tortillas, add fresh cheese and fold them in two, wrap them in beaten egg; for ten tortillas, use two eggs.
2. Beat the egg whites first and when they are very stiff add the yolks with a few grains of salt and half a teaspoon of flour.
3. When well fried, put them in the oven to drain the grease.
4. Cook two peeled tomatoes, two onions cut in half, one or two green chiles; this is broken down and the enchiladas are bathed with it, serving them hot.
