## Ingredients

- One yellow pineapple
- Six to eight glasses of water
- A quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper powder
- One pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Chop one yellow pineapple very finely
2. Put it in a container with six to eight glasses of water and a quarter ounce of Chiapa pepper powder
3. Let it infuse with one pound of sugar for one night
4. The following day, if too acidic, add more water and sugar
