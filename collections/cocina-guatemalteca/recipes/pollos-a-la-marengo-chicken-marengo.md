## Ingredients

- chicken
- one glass of white wine
- one glass of oil
- little powder of pepper
- juice of one lemon
- butter or lard
- little chopped parsley
- little fried flour

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken, quarter it
2. Place the chicken in an infusion made as follows: in a pot put one glass of white wine, one glass of oil, a little powder of pepper and the juice of one lemon
3. Leave the chicken in the infusion for one night
4. The next day, put a pot with a little butter or lard on the fire
5. When hot, add the chicken with all the broth in which it was kept the night before
6. When half cooked, add a little chopped parsley and a little fried flour
7. Let it boil until cooked and serve hot
