## Ingredients

- ten or twelve apples
- one teaspoonful of lime water (lejía de cal)
- one pound and a half of sugar, clarified
- a handful of cloves
- almonds and raisins, chopped, or cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Peel ten or twelve apples, place in water with one teaspoonful of lime water for one hour
2. Have one pound and a half of sugar clarified, wash the apples, make three small cuts with the knife and place in the honey on the fire with a handful of cloves
3. When they have soaked through, remove and place on the platter; if the honey is still thin, let it boil a quarter hour more
4. Cover the apples with this, top with chopped almonds and raisins, or if not, cinnamon powder
