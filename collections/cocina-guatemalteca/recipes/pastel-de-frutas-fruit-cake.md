## Ingredients

- About 15 chilacayotes
- 15 figs
- 10 oranges
- 15 coconut candies
- Candied lemons if available
- Three ten-cent yolk pastries
- Raisins

## Directions

1. Chop about 15 chilacayotes, 15 figs, 10 oranges, 15 coconut candies, and candied lemons if available very finely
2. Chop three ten-cent yolk pastries
3. Butter a mold
4. Place a layer of chopped bread
5. Place a layer of chopped sweets with raisins
