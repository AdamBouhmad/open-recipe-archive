## Ingredients

- twenty corn cobs, shelled
- salt
- one pound lard
- two pounds sugar
- one cup milk (if corn is very ripe)

## Directions

1. Shell and grind the corn, then strain it
2. For twenty corn cobs, add one teaspoon salt, one pound lard, and two pounds sugar
3. Mix everything very well
4. If the corn is very ripe, stir in one cup of milk
5. Ensure the corn is tender
