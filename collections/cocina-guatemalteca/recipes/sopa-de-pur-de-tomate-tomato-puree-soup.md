## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- broth
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- cornstarch
- heavy cream

## Directions

1. Roast the tomatoes
2. Pass them through a strainer
3. Add the necessary broth prepared in advance
4. In a separate saucepan with a little butter, brown chopped onion and parsley
5. Add one or two tablespoons of cornstarch and let it brown for a moment
6. Mix into the soup
7. Just before serving, add a glass of heavy cream
