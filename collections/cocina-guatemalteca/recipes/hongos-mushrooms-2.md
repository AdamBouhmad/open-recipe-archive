## Ingredients

- mushrooms, umbrella or button mushrooms
- oil
- onion
- garlic, one clove
- salt
- pepper powder
- broth or water, small quantity

## Directions

1. Take the mushrooms, whether umbrella or button mushrooms, wash them well
2. Heat oil in a pan on the fire
3. Fry onion and one clove of garlic in this oil, when it browns add the mushrooms with salt and pepper powder
4. Add broth or water in very small quantity
5. After ten minutes they can be served with stewed meat, chicken, kidneys, liver or alone
