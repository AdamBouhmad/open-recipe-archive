## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- two tomatoes, peeled and halved
- two onions, peeled and quartered
- green chile or one dried chile, seeded
- sliced peeled potatoes
- fresh fish, whole or cut as desired

## Directions

1. Place a little salted water in a dish
2. Add the two peeled and halved tomatoes, two peeled onions quartered, green chile or dried chile without seeds, and sliced peeled potatoes
3. When half cooked, add the fresh fish whole or cut as desired, being careful it does not fall apart
4. After a quarter hour of boiling, mash the tomato and onion
5. Serve with its hot broth, noting that if dried chile is used, remove it at the first boils to prevent it from falling apart
