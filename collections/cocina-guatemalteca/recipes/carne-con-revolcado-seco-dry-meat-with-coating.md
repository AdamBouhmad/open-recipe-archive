## Ingredients

- Meat
- Salt
- Miltomate, cooked
- Cumin
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Achiote
- Chile (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the meat, cut into slices, pound them, and fry with salt
2. Have ready ground cooked miltomate with a bit of cumin, a small clove of garlic, a bit of oregano, and a small piece of achiote
3. Add ground chile if a spicy version is desired
4. Add this mixture to the meat and serve after turning it several times until it is dry
