## Ingredients

- Tail or hip bone
- One fat hen
- Leeks (or onion if not available)
- Turnips
- Carrot
- Cabbage
- Chickpeas
- Tomato, finely chopped
- Onion
- Four egg yolks, hard-cooked and well-ground
- Sherry wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook tail or hip bone and one fat hen
2. Add plenty of leeks to the broth and if not available, use onion
3. Add turnips, carrot, cabbage and chickpeas
4. When everything is well-cooked, separate the broth and add to it a fried mixture composed of very finely chopped tomato, onion and four hard-cooked egg yolks well-ground
5. In this broth add all the hen meat finely chopped along with the well-cooked chickpeas
6. Serve adding a small glass of sherry wine if desired
