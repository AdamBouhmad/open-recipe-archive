## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- water
- salt
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Make bread slices and fry in butter
2. When fried, place in a pan
3. Add water, salt to taste, and a little chopped parsley
4. When it comes to a boil, serve hot
