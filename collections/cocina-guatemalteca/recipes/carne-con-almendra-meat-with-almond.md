## Ingredients

- 2 ounces peeled almonds
- 1 sprig thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 stick cinnamon
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Loin or a piece of very tender brisket

## Directions

1. Clean the loin or brisket well
2. Grind together the peeled almonds, thyme sprig, bay leaf, and cinnamon stick
3. Dissolve the ground mixture in vinegar and salt
4. Rub the loin very well with this mixture and let it sit overnight
5. The following day, place it in the oven
6. Serve with salad of your choice
