## Ingredients

- fresh corn (not too ripe, ground)
- sugar or salt to taste
- ground cheese
- one ounce butter
- one ounce lard
- corn husks

## Directions

1. Grind fresh corn that is not too ripe
2. Add sugar or salt to taste, ground cheese, one ounce of butter, and one ounce of lard
3. Mix everything well
4. Wrap the tamales in the same corn husks
5. Cook by steaming
