## Ingredients

- Cleaned tongue
- Water
- Salt
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Garlic
- Cinnamon
- Half pound of pork meat
- Butter
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Vinegar
- Sherry
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Sweet optional
- Pitted olives
- Potatoes
- Almonds
- Green beans
- Chayote
- Carrot
- Green peas

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and cook with sufficient water, salt, thyme, oregano, pepper, clove, garlic, and cinnamon
2. When well cooked, remove and cool
3. Split the tongue lengthwise
4. For the filling: parboil half a pound of pork meat, chop finely, and fry in a pan with one tablespoon of butter, two or three chopped tomatoes, onion, and garlic
5. Add two tablespoons of vinegar, two of sherry, black pepper powder, cinnamon, and clove; add a touch of sweetness if desired
6. Add pitted olives, potatoes, and sliced almonds
7. Mix in parboiled and chopped potatoes, green beans, chayote, carrot, and green peas
8. Fill the tongue with this mixture
9. Sew closed with thick thread, coat well with butter, and place in the oven
