## Ingredients

- loin
- vinegar
- one head of garlic
- oregano powder
- pepper powder
- clove
- two bay leaves
- salt
- one glass of water
- oil
- lard
- lettuce
- tomatoes
- two dried chiles

## Directions

1. The day before, clean the loin very well and place in a pot covered with vinegar, one peeled and crushed head of garlic, a little oregano powder, pepper powder, clove, two bay leaves and salt to taste
2. The next day add one glass of water and with the pot well covered, put to cook
3. When well cooked, fry in oil and lard
4. When golden, place on a platter, whole if desired or in slices, garnish with lettuce
5. Fry some finely chopped tomatoes, grind two seeded dried chiles, dissolve with a little broth in which the loin was cooked and bathe with this to serve hot
