## Ingredients

- cooked turkey meat, more than one large can of deviled ham
- mustard, one tablespoon
- pepper powder
- hard-boiled egg yolk, one
- bread, round slices
- butter
- tomato, peeled and seedless
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked turkey meat which should be more than one large can of deviled ham
2. Mix with one tablespoon of mustard, pepper powder and one hard-boiled egg yolk well crumbled
3. Mix well to form a paste
4. Cut round slices of bread the size of a tomato
5. Spread with butter
6. Put the paste on top, a slice of peeled tomato without seeds and cover with mayonnaise
