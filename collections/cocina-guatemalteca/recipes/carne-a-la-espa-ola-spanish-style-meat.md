## Ingredients

- piece of beef or pork
- salt
- pepper
- onion, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- celery, chopped
- three hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Pound a piece of beef or pork meat
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Chop onion, parsley, celery and three hard-boiled eggs
4. Season with salt, pepper powder and a little butter
5. Place this mixture on the meat, roll it well and tie it
6. Brown in butter
7. Add broth and serve hot
