## Ingredients

- Lamb liver
- Salt
- Miltomate
- Cooked tomato
- Guaque or dried chile
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Castilian pepper
- Achiote
- French bread

## Directions

1. Cook the liver with salt
2. Grind miltomate and cooked tomato with a guaque or dried chile, oregano, cumin, Castilian pepper, a small piece of achiote and a soaked piece of French bread
3. Dissolve this mixture in the same broth
4. Fry and add the liver cut into pieces
