## Ingredients

- Soaked cod
- Two bay leaves
- Two dried pasilla chiles, soaked and seeded
- Two cloves of garlic, fried
- A little oregano
- Castilian peppercorns
- A sprig of thyme
- A piece of French bread, browned in lard
- Two tablespoons of vinegar
- One cup of cod cooking broth
- One glass of very hot oil

## Directions

1. After soaking the cod, cook it in water with about two bay leaves.
2. Grind two dried pasilla chiles, soaked and seeded, with two fried garlic cloves, a little oregano, some Castilian peppercorns, a sprig of thyme, and a piece of French bread browned in lard.
3. Mix this ground mixture with two tablespoons of vinegar and one cup of the cooking broth.
4. Fry this in one glass of very hot oil.
5. Place the cod in this mixture and let it reduce.
