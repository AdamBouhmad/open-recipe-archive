## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- three eggs
- four ounces of butter
- two ounces of lard
- four ounces of sugar
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Put together the flour, eggs, butter, lard, sugar, milk and Royal
2. If there is no milk, use six eggs instead of three
3. Work everything very well
4. Stretch out with a rolling pin
5. Cut the crackers in the shape desired
6. Put in the oven
