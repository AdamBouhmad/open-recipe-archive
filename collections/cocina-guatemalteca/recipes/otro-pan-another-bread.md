## Ingredients

- four ounces of melted butter
- six eggs
- flour
- three tablespoons of cream or milk
- one tablespoon of beer yeast
- four tablespoons of ground sugar
- one ounce of currants
- two ounces of peeled and lengthwise-cut almonds

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of melted butter in a saucepan and beat until it becomes like cream
2. Add six eggs, putting in one egg and one tablespoon of flour and so on until all six eggs are done
3. Then three tablespoons of cream or milk, putting in one tablespoon of this and one of flour until three tablespoons are done
4. Add one tablespoon of beer yeast, three more tablespoons of flour and four of ground sugar
5. Beat everything for half an hour
6. Add one ounce of currants and two ounces of peeled almonds cut lengthwise
7. Put in greased molds of lard or butter and when it rises put in a hot oven
