## Ingredients

- carne de ternera (veal)
- tomate pelado (peeled tomato)
- perejil (parsley)
- chile verde (green chile)
- polvo de pimienta (ground pepper)
- manteca o mantequilla (lard or butter)
- agua (water)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Cook the meat and slice it
2. Prepare a mixture of peeled tomato, parsley, green chile and ground pepper
3. Coat a pan with lard or butter if available
4. Place a layer of meat, then a layer of mixture, continuing alternately, ending with meat
5. Add half a cup of water and half a cup of vinegar with salt
6. Place in the oven or over two fires
