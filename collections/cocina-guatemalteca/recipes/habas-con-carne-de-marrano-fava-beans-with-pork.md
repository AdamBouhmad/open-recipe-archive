## Ingredients

- Fava beans
- Pork meat, cooked with salt
- Pork rib or loin
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped onion
- One tablespoon flour
- Pepper powder
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the fava beans as previously described, taking care they come out very whole
2. Have pork meat (rib or loin) cooked with salt
3. Fry peeled tomato with chopped onion
4. Add the meat and fava beans to this and one tablespoon of flour dissolved in the same broth
5. Serve with pepper powder and chopped parsley
