## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cooked in little water with salt
- Butter, half a pound for three pounds of potatoes
- Milk, half a cup
- Salt, one quarter of a teaspoon
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in very little water with salt
2. Peel with great care, removing all dark spots
3. Grind in a meat grinder which should be very clean
4. For three pounds of potatoes, if fine puree is desired, add half a pound of butter, half a cup of milk and one quarter of a teaspoon of very white salt
5. Mix everything very well
6. If desired, serve just like this with chopped parsley
7. If not, place on the stove to brown, but be careful to leave it very firm so it does not spread
