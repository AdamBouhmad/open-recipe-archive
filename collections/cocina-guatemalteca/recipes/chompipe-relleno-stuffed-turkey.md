## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Pork meat (5 libras for large turkey)
- Salt
- Castilian pepper
- Cinnamon powder
- Raw eggs
- Bread flour
- Ham (half libra)
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Leeks
- Parsley
- Beef feet

## Directions

1. Hang the turkey and cut its tongue or throat
2. Once dead, place in a pan and pour a jug of boiling water, turning quickly so it does not boil over
3. Pluck and clean well, then burn some straw or twigs to remove all small feathers
4. Carefully cut the wings and legs without breaking them, cut the head and split the skin along the backbone
5. Carefully remove the meat leaving only the bones
6. Once well deboned, remove most of the fat and the top part that has no meat, cover with breast pieces trimmed from thick areas
7. Grind five libras of pork meat, passing it twice through the grinder until very fine
8. Once ground, add half an ounce of salt, a quarter ounce of Castilian pepper, half an ounce of cinnamon powder (if desired), four or five raw eggs and a spoonful of bread flour
9. Mix well and stuff the turkey
10. Cut half a libra of ham into long strips and insert inside the meat
11. Sew closed with thick thread the openings of the legs and wings
12. Wrap in a long cloth, sewing it very well and very tightly to press it
13. Place in a pan with water, salt, bay leaves, a sprig of thyme, a bunch of leeks, a bunch of parsley and two well-cleaned beef feet
14. Let boil four hours over very even heat
15. After this time place in a press while still very hot
16. The next day serve as desired
