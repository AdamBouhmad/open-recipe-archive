## Ingredients

- a loin of pork
- salt
- lemon
- pepper
- strips of bacon
- black sausage
- capers
- chopped parsley
- onion
- powdered pepper

## Directions

1. Pound a pork loin and leave in brine with salt, lemon, and pepper
2. Spread on a table and cover with strips of bacon, crumbled black sausage, salt, capers, chopped parsley, onion, and powdered pepper
3. Roll up, wrap in cloth, and cook with salt and aromatic herbs
4. Once cooked, place in a press to serve cold, sliced
