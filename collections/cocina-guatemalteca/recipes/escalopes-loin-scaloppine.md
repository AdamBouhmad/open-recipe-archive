## Ingredients

- loin
- salt
- pepper powder
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- cooked peas
- pickles
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into slices, not too thin, and flatten with a mallet
2. Season with salt and pepper powder
3. Coat in beaten egg and then in bread crumbs
4. Place on a baking sheet in the oven
5. When golden, garnish with cooked peas
6. Serve with pickles and mushrooms
