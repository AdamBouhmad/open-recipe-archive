## Ingredients

- meat
- lard or oil
- tomato, peeled and in pieces
- garlic, sliced thin
- parsley
- pepper powder
- water, a little
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut meat slices as for steak
2. Heat lard or oil and add the meat with peeled tomato cut in pieces, thinly sliced garlic, parsley, pepper powder, a little water, and salt
