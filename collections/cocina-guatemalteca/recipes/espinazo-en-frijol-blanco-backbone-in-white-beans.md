## Ingredients

- white beans
- water
- salt
- head of garlic
- split backbone
- three or four roasted tomatoes
- two onion heads
- four roasted garlic cloves
- one dried chile
- oregano
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- a small piece of achiote
- one soaked French roll

## Directions

1. Cook the beans in cold water, and when they have boiled for two hours remove the water and add fresh clean water
2. Add salt and a head of garlic
3. When more than cooked add the split backbone
4. Grind three or four roasted tomatoes, two onion heads, four roasted garlic cloves, one dried chile, oregano, cumin, Castilian pepper, a small piece of achiote and one soaked French roll
5. Thin with a little water or the same broth, stirring so it does not stick
6. Serve not too thin
