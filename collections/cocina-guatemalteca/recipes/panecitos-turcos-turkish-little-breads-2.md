## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- one tablespoon of Royal baking powder
- four eggs
- one small cup of sugar
- half a cup of milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the four eggs as for nougat, beating the yolks with one small cup of sugar
2. Mix all well with the flour and half a cup of milk
3. Butter some papers very well and place them on pewter plates
4. Put two tablespoons of the batter on these and place in a hot oven
5. When cool, place cream or whatever is desired on top and stack one on top of another to reach the desired height
