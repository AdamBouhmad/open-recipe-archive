## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- half a pound of peeled and ground almonds (dry)
- half a pound of sifted sugar
- pieces of candied fruit: lemon, chilacayote, orange, sweet potato, figs

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites to stiff peaks and the six yolks separately
2. When well beaten, combine them
3. Add half a pound of peeled and ground almonds (dry) and half a pound of sifted sugar
4. Mix everything well
5. Add pieces of candied fruit such as lemon, chilacayote, orange, sweet potato, and figs
6. Mix well, pour into a mold coated with butter
7. Place in a not-very-hot oven
