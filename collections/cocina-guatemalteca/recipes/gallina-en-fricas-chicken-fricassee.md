## Ingredients

- butter
- chopped onion
- one cup milk
- one tablespoon flour or cornstarch
- nutmeg
- capers
- chicken or hen

## Directions

1. Fry the chicken or hen in butter with chopped onion
2. When it begins to brown, add one cup of milk
3. Add one tablespoon of flour or cornstarch
4. Add a little nutmeg and some capers
5. Cook until done
