## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- butter
- tomato
- onion
- bread flour
- water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes with salt
2. Peel and slice them and fry in a pan with very little butter, peeled tomato and chopped onion
3. Dissolve a teaspoon of bread flour in a little water
4. Add it to the mixture with water so a thin sauce remains
5. Put the potatoes in this
6. Serve hot with chopped parsley
