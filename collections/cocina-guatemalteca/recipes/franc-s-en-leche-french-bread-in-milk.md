## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- water
- four tablespoons of yeast
- one glass of milk
- one teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Put one pound of flour with the necessary water to make it smooth, well mixed with four tablespoons of yeast
2. Let it sit all day
3. In the evening dissolve this with one glass of milk, one teaspoon of salt and the necessary flour to make a compact dough
4. Put the dough in molds and the next day put in a very hot oven
