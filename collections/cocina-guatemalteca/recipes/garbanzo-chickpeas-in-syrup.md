## Ingredients

- one pound of chickpeas
- water
- sifted ash
- one pound of sugar clarified
- panela
- cinnamon strip

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of chickpeas overnight
2. The next day put a pot on the fire with one liter of water and one cup of well-sifted ash
3. Add the chickpeas
4. When the skin comes off lower the heat, after a quarter hour wash and return to the fire with one liter of cold water
5. When cooked remove
6. Have clarified one pound of sugar, if making with panela it should be quite white; clarify half a cup
7. When both are cold mix and put on the fire, which should be moderate
8. When the chickpeas begin to reach medium heat add a cinnamon strip
9. Do not forget that the honey and chickpeas must be cold, otherwise they turn out hard
