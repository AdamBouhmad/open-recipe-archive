## Ingredients

- Cleaned tongue
- Long strips of ham
- Garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper powder
- Water
- Bay leaves
- Oregano powder
- Vinegar or sherry

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well
2. Prepare ham strips with finely chopped garlic, salt, and black pepper powder
3. Stuff the tongue by inserting the ham strips so they come out the other side, keeping them even
4. Place the tongue in a pot with sufficient water, salt, two bay leaves, oregano powder, plenty of garlic, and a cup of vinegar or sherry (if using sherry, do not add oregano)
5. Cook until tender
