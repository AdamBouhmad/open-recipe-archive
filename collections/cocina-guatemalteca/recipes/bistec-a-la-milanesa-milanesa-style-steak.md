## Ingredients

- tenderized meat slices
- salt
- pepper powder
- one egg
- white bread powder

## Directions

1. Make slices from tenderized meat, not too thin, and season with salt and a little pepper powder
2. Beat one egg (white and yolk) with salt
3. Dip the meat in the egg and then coat with white bread powder
4. Fry in very hot butter over low heat
5. Serve with lettuces and hot sauce
