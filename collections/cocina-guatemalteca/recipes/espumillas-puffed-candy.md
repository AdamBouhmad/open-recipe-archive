## Ingredients

- egg whites
- sugar syrup
- pearl sugar (gragea)

## Directions

1. Beat exactly as the previous recipe, same quantity and everything
2. Moisten some sheet metal leaves and make the espumillas round or long as desired
3. Put pearl sugar on top and put in the oven for ten minutes
