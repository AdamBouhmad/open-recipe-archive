## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil
- salt
- ham
- slices of sausage
- slices of onion
- two cloves of garlic
- fifteen fried almonds
- two roasted and ground tomatoes
- water
- dry sherry
- a piece of French bread fried and ground
- ten raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and fry them in oil with salt
2. When they take on color, add the chopped ham, slices of sausage, slices of onion, two finely chopped cloves of garlic, and fifteen fried almonds
3. Add two roasted and ground tomatoes
4. Cover with water, salt, and a glass of dry sherry
5. When it is half-cooked, add a piece of French bread fried and ground
6. Add ten raisins if desired
