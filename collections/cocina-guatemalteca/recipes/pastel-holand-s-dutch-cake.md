## Ingredients

- Apples (one pound, well cored)
- Sugar (half a pound)
- Pitted raisins (two ounces)
- Figs and candied lemons (if available)
- Flour (one pound)
- Butter (half a pound)
- Sugar (four ounces)

## Directions

1. Peel one pound of apples, well cored, and split them lengthwise.
2. Add half a pound of sugar, two ounces of pitted raisins, figs and candied lemons if available, one pound of flour, and half a pound of butter.
3. Mix very well with the hand without kneading, with four ounces of sugar well mixed.
4. Butter a mold and put a thin layer of this dough in it; bake briefly in the oven without browning it.
5. On top of this place the fruit and cover with the rest of the dough but sprinkled, not evenly.
