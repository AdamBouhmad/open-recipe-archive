## Ingredients

- 15 chilacayotes
- 15 figs
- 10 oranges
- 15 coconut candies
- candied lemons (if available)
- 3 egg yolk breads (at ten centavos each)
- butter or lard for greasing
- raisins, pitted
- 1 liter of milk cooked with cream
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 ounces sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla or 1 glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Chop about 15 chilacayotes, 15 figs, 10 oranges, 15 coconut candies and if available some candied lemons; chop everything very finely
2. Chop three egg yolk breads
3. Place in a mold greased with lard, alternating layers: a layer of chopped bread, a layer of sweets with pitted raisins, another layer of bread, and continue alternating the layers, ensuring the last layer is bread
4. In 1 liter of milk cooked with cream, add 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks, 4 ounces of sugar, and 1 tablespoon of vanilla or 1 glass of sherry
5. Beat very well and pour this mixture over the cake
6. This should be done at night to put it in the oven the next day, which will take two hours to bake
