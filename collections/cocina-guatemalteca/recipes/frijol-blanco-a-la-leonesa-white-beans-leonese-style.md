## Ingredients

- White beans, cooked
- Large white chicharrones
- Salt
- Butter
- Pickled vegetables, chopped
- Chorizo negro slices
- Cooked ham pieces
- Oregano powder
- Two roasted tomatoes
- Thyme sprig
- One small teaspoon vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. Cook separately some large white chicharrones with salt
3. Line a pan with butter and in it layer white beans, a layer of chicharrones mixed with chopped pickled vegetables, slices of black chorizo, pieces of cooked ham, and oregano powder, then another layer of white beans, taking care that this is the last
4. Grind two roasted tomatoes with a sprig of thyme, dissolve with one small teaspoon of vinegar and a little water seasoned with salt
5. Cover the beans with this, place on two fires so it comes to a boil
