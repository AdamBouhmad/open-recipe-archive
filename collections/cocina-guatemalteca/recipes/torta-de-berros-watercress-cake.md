## Ingredients

- piece of pork
- watercress
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- pepper powder
- two eggs
- bread flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Partially cook a piece of pork, chop it very finely
2. Chop a small amount of watercress
3. Mix the pork and watercress together
4. Add tomato, onion, garlic, and pepper powder
5. Add two eggs and a little bread flour
6. Fry in hot lard
