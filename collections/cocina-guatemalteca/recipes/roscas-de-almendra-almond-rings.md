## Ingredients

- half a pound of peeled almonds
- six ounces of butter
- two ounces of lard
- six egg yolks
- flour

## Directions

1. Grind the peeled almonds in a machine
2. Mix with butter, lard, and egg yolks
3. Add enough flour to form rings
4. Place on trays and bake in the oven
