## Ingredients

- Roasted tomato
- Onion stalks
- Parsley
- Mint leaves
- Cooked egg yolks
- Cumin powder
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Saffron
- Broth from boiled meat
- Noodles
- Cheese powder
- Green beans
- Peas
- Chayote
- Parsley sprig
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Mince a portion of roasted tomato, some onion stalks, parsley and mint leaves
2. Add three cooked egg yolks with a little cumin powder, cinnamon, clove and saffron, all in small quantities
3. Add broth from boiled meat making a thin sauce
4. Add noodles according to the number of people eating
5. Add cheese powder, green beans, peas and chayote, all minced into small pieces
6. Add a sprig of parsley
7. Cover and let boil
8. If desired, add toasted bread and serve
