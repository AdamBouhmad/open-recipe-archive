## Ingredients

- tomato sauce
- garlic
- onion
- cumin
- salt
- cooked beef tongue
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare a tomato sauce with plenty of garlic, onion, cumin, and salt
2. Fry with sliced cooked beef tongue and already fried mushrooms
3. Mix the sauce with the mushrooms and tongue
