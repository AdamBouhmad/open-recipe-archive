## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut in half with the thick skin still attached
2. Season with salt and a little vinegar
3. Leave for a quarter hour
4. Then roast on a grill over very low heat
5. Serve with a chimol sauce
