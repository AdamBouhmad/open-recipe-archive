## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- salt
- a few drops of lemon
- half a pound of butter
- 4 ounces of cooked ham, chopped
- half a pound of Parmesan cheese
- 1 glass of white wine
- 1 cup of cream
- 6 whole eggs, well beaten
- butter for greasing the mold
- butter for the sauce
- oil for the sauce
- onion
- roasted garlic
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup milk
- sufficient mushrooms
- salt for the sauce

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of macaroni in salted water with a few drops of lemon
2. Mix the cooked macaroni with half a pound of butter, 4 ounces of chopped cooked ham, half a pound of Parmesan cheese, 1 glass of white wine, 1 cup of cream, and 6 well-beaten whole eggs, mixing everything well
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold and bake in the oven for 10 minutes
4. For the sauce: fry butter, oil, onion, and roasted garlic; add 1 tablespoon of flour dissolved in 1 cup of milk, sufficient mushrooms, and salt
5. Serve the pudding with this sauce
