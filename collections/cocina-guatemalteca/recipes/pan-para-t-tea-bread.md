## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half a large cup of yeast
- half a pound of lard
- half a pound of sugar
- half a teaspoon of salt
- ten eggs

## Directions

1. Put in a dish one pound of flour, half a large cup of yeast, half a pound of lard, half a pound of sugar, half a teaspoon of salt and ten eggs
2. Knead for half an hour
3. Put in greased molds, let it rise
4. After twelve hours when it has risen, put in a hot oven
