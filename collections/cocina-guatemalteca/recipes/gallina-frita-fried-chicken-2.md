## Ingredients

- one chicken
- pork loin
- cooked ham
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Debone the chicken as with the others
2. Chop a little pork loin with a piece of cooked ham cut into long strips, add pepper powder, oregano powder and one teaspoon of salt
3. Mix all this well with the meat and fill the chicken with it
4. Wrap it in cloth and cook with water, salt and plenty of oregano powder
5. After two hours, press it and serve cold
