## Ingredients

- venado (venison)
- salt
- little feet, chopped
- masa (corn dough), very fine
- roasted tomatoes
- Castilian peppers
- chile
- achiote (annatto)
- epazote branch
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook the shredded venison with salt and chopped little feet.
2. Grind a little very fine masa with roasted tomatoes, Castilian peppers, chile, and a small piece of achiote.
3. Dissolve this with the same cooking broth.
4. Fry an epazote branch in lard.
5. Place the venison and feet with their well-fried seasoning into this.
