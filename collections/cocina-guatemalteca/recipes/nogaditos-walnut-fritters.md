## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 5 eggs
- a pinch of salt
- a little warm water if needed
- lard for frying
- 2 pounds of sugar
- dragées

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with five eggs and a pinch of salt
2. If too stiff, add a little warm water, knead very well until it forms blisters
3. Let rest half an hour
4. Then stretch with the hands, cut small pieces the size of a finger and fry them in hot lard
5. Clarify two pounds of sugar and when at very stiff peaks beat very well
6. Add the nogaditos to this, form them into small balls and place dragées on top
