## Ingredients

- Loin with rib attached
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Two eggs
- White bread powder
- Lard
- Lettuce
- Mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut chops leaving one third bone attached
2. Pound with a mallet to flatten but not make thin
3. Rub with salt and pepper powder
4. One hour before cooking, beat two eggs with salt for twelve chops
5. Coat chops in the beaten egg
6. Roll in white bread powder
7. Fry in hot lard over low heat
8. Serve on tender lettuce leaves with mashed potatoes
