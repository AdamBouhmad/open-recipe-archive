## Ingredients

- broth
- fried seasoning
- egg yolks
- nutmeg
- potatoes
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare a broth as before with fried seasoning and three cooked and ground egg yolks with a little nutmeg
2. Cook four ounces of well-peeled potatoes, grind them, and dissolve with a teaspoon of butter
3. Mix well, form balls wrapped in flour
4. Drop them into the broth to give it a boil
5. Serve with chopped parsley
