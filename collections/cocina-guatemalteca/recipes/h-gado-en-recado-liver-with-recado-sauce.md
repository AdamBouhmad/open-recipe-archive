## Ingredients

- achiote
- Castilian pepper
- two roasted tomatoes
- chile
- lard
- epazote branch

## Directions

1. Grind a very fine paste with achiote, some Castilian peppers, two roasted tomatoes and chile
2. Put a pan on the fire with lard and a branch of epazote, then the recado and the liver cut into pieces
3. It can also be made grinding only Chiapa pepper, but without epazote or Castilian pepper
