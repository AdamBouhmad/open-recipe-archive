## Ingredients

- sliced peeled cucumber
- slices of cooked lobster
- slices of cooked potato
- beets
- cooked fresh fish
- salt
- capers
- peppercorns
- bell peppers
- mayonnaise
- peeled tomatoes without seeds

## Directions

1. Arrange layers in a salad bowl: sliced peeled cucumber, slices of cooked lobster, slices of cooked potato, a layer of beets, and a layer of cooked fish, all cooked with salt
2. If desired, add capers and peppercorns and bell peppers
3. Cover with a good mayonnaise
4. Garnish with slices of peeled tomato without seeds
