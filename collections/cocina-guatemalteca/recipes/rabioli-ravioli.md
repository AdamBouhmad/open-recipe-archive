## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- three eggs
- salt
- butter
- cooked and chopped brain
- cooked and chopped spinach
- finely chopped chicken
- ground pork and spinach (alternative filling)
- one pound beef
- ripe and finely chopped tomato
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Knead one pound flour with three eggs and a bit of salt
2. After kneading well, fill small molds with the following filling: heat butter in a pan and fry the cooked and chopped brain, cooked and chopped spinach, and finely chopped chicken
3. Alternatively, the filling can be made with finely chopped pork and spinach
4. After filling, seal well and poach
5. Place on a platter and cover with tomato sauce made as follows: cut one pound beef into small pieces and fry in lard until all the juices are released
6. When well fried, add plenty of ripe and finely chopped tomato
7. Let it boil a few times and use this to cover the ravioli, being careful to remove the meat
8. Sprinkle grated cheese on top
9. Place in the oven to brown and serve hot or cold
