## Ingredients

- cooked pig's feet
- flour
- egg
- lard
- sesame seeds
- a little pepitoria
- roasted tomatillo
- one pepper
- a small piece of cinnamon
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Dry the cooked feet and coat in flour
2. Beat the egg, first the white then the yolk
3. Wrap the feet and fry in hot lard
4. When golden, remove and put in the oven to drain all the fat
5. Grind a little sesame seeds, a little pepitoria and roasted tomatillo with one pepper, a small piece of cinnamon and two cloves
6. Thin with a little water
7. Fry and add the feet for a light boil
8. Serve hot
