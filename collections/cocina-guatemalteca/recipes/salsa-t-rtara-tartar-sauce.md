## Ingredients

- nine egg yolks
- three cooked egg yolks
- two tablespoons of oil
- drops of lemon
- five or six onion heads
- capers
- olives
- cooked egg white

## Directions

1. Beat nine egg yolks until they are very stiff
2. Add three cooked egg yolks, well ground or broken with a mortar
3. Continue beating the mayonnaise, adding the oil drop by drop along with drops of lemon
4. When it is well thick, add five or six finely chopped onion heads, capers, olives, and the cooked egg white cut into small pieces
5. This sauce is served with fish and potato salad
