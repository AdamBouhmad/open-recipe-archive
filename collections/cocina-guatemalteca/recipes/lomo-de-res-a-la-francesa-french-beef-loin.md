## Ingredients

- beef loin
- garlic, finely chopped
- salt
- butter
- potatoes, whole and peeled

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef loin and mix with finely chopped garlic seasoned with salt
2. Tie well with hemp twine
3. Place in a baking pan with a little butter, salt to taste, and whole peeled potatoes
4. Put in the oven
5. Take care to baste it with its juice
6. When cooked, place on a plate decorated with lettuce and serve hot
