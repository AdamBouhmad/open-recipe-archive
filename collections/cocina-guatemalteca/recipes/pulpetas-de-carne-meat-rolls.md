## Ingredients

- meat
- pork loin, in strips
- white ham, in strips
- salt
- vinegar
- wine
- lemon juice
- nutmeg powder
- sherry wine
- egg
- onion, chopped
- lard
- red wine or sherry
- water, sufficient
- tomato
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut meat into slices and soften with a mallet
2. Cut strips of pork loin and white ham
3. Season with salt, vinegar, wine, lemon juice, nutmeg powder, sherry wine, egg, and chopped onion
4. Fill the meat slices with this mixture and sew closed with thread
5. Place in a little lard and when it takes on golden color, add red wine or sherry and sufficient water
6. Add tomato, pepper, clove, and cinnamon
7. When cooked, strain the sauce through a filter and serve hot
