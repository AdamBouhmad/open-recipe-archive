## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- 6 eggs
- a little salt
- half a teaspoon of vanilla
- 1 tablespoon of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Place half a pound of flour in a dish
2. Add the six eggs one by one
3. Mix well
4. Add a little salt
5. Add half a teaspoon of vanilla
6. Add one tablespoon of Royal baking powder
