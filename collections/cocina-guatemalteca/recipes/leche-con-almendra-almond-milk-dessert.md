## Ingredients

- 4 glasses milk
- 12 ounces white sugar
- 3 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- cinnamon powder for garnish
- chopped almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Place milk, sugar, and ground almonds in a saucepan
2. Stir over very low heat until it reaches the consistency of a dessert
3. Decorate with cinnamon powder and chopped almonds
