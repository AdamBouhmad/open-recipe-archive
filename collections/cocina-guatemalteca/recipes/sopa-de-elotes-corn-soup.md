## Ingredients

- good broth
- one fat hen or two chickens
- one brain, well cleaned
- corn
- four or five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare good broth, add one fat hen or two chickens and one well-cleaned brain.
2. When cooked, strain the broth.
3. Buy one or two cans of corn, depending on the quantity being made, let it boil for half an hour, and add the chicken and brain cut into cubes.
4. Before serving, beat four or five egg yolks well in the soup tureen, pour the soup, and serve hot.
