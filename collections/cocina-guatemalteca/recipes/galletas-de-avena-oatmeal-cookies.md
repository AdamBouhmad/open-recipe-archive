## Ingredients

- butter, one tablespoon melted
- sugar, one cup
- eggs, two
- oatmeal, two and a half cups
- Royal baking powder, two teaspoons
- salt, half teaspoon
- vanilla, one teaspoon

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the butter, add the eggs one by one, beating well after each egg
2. Add the oatmeal, Royal, salt and vanilla
3. Mix everything well and drop by spoonfuls onto a greased baking dish, leaving them separated so they have room to spread
4. Bake in a moderate oven for ten minutes
