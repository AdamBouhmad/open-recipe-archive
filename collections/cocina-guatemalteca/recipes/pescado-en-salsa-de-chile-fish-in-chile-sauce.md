## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- flour
- oil
- chile de pasa (dried chile)
- onion
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, season with salt.
2. Coat in flour and fry in oil.
3. When fried, remove the fish.
4. In the same lard, fry two seeded and soaked dried chiles, ground with a large onion head.
5. Dissolve this mixture in a little water and one tablespoon of vinegar.
6. Bathe the fish with this sauce.
