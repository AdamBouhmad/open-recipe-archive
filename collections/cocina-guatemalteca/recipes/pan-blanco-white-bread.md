## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- a little salt
- one teaspoon of Royal
- one tablespoon of lard or butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Put in a dish one cup of flour, a little salt, one teaspoon of Royal, one tablespoon of lard or butter and the necessary milk to make a smooth dough
2. Put in a greased mold in a hot oven
