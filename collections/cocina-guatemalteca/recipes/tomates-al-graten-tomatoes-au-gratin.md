## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- parsley
- garlic
- bread powder
- salt
- pepper powder
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes in half and extract the juice and seeds, being careful not to bruise them
2. Prepare parsley and garlic finely chopped, mix with bread powder and use this to fill the tomato halves
3. Add salt to taste and a little pepper powder
4. Place the tomatoes in a dish or pan, put sufficient oil on top and place in a not very hot oven to prevent them from browning too much
