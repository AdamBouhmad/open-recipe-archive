## Ingredients

- two liters of milk
- two pounds of sugar
- two coconuts, grated with their water
- eight egg yolks
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Put milk and sugar on the fire
2. When it reaches medium-soft ball stage, mix in the two grated coconuts with their water
3. Put on the fire until it is very thick
4. When it is fried, mix in eight well-beaten egg yolks
5. Put on a platter, decorate with raisins and almonds
6. Place in the oven to brown
