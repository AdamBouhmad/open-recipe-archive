## Ingredients

- two glasses of water (about half a liter)
- a few grains of salt
- half a teaspoon of lard
- half a pound of sifted flour
- nine eggs (if small), or eight eggs (if large)
- sufficient lard for frying
- white honey
- water
- dill flowers

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan on the fire with two glasses of water or about half a liter with a few grains of salt
2. When it comes to a boil, add half a teaspoon of lard
3. Remove from the fire and add half a pound of well-sifted flour
4. Return to the fire without stopping stirring
5. When it no longer sticks to the hand, remove it and add the eggs one by one, nine if they are small, eight if they are large
6. Once everything is well mixed, with a small spoon drop the buñuelos into a skillet with sufficient lard that is boiling
7. Prepare some white honey with water so it is not so thick
8. Serve the buñuelos with this honey and adorn with dill flowers
