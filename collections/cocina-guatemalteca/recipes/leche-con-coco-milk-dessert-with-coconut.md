## Ingredients

- 1 pound white sugar
- 4 glasses milk
- 1 coconut, well ground
- cinnamon strips
- 1 orange leaf
- 4 ounces peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Place sugar, milk, ground coconut, cinnamon strips, orange leaf, and peeled and ground almonds in a saucepan
2. Peel almonds with boiling water
3. Place over heat until it reaches proper consistency
4. When serving, remove the cinnamon and orange leaf
