## Ingredients

- 1 coconut, ground
- 2 ounces peeled almonds
- 1 ounce peeled walnuts
- 1 pound sugar clarified to half point
- 6 egg yolks
- butter
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Grind 1 coconut, 2 ounces of peeled almonds, and 1 ounce of walnuts peeled with hot water
2. Have clarified 1 pound of sugar to half point
3. Add all the ground mixture to it
4. Put on the fire until it thickens
5. Remove and when cold, add 6 egg yolks
6. Return to the fire until it reaches the point of before (ante)
7. Grease a mold with butter and bread powder
8. Pour the mixture into it and put in the oven for 10 minutes
9. Insert a knife and if it comes out clean, it is ready to serve
