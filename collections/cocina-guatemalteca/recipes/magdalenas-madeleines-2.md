## Ingredients

- Six egg whites
- Six egg yolks
- Five ounces of sugar
- Zest of one lemon
- Six ounces of sifted flour
- One ounce of melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat the six egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Mix the egg yolks with five ounces of sugar and the lemon zest
3. Add the six ounces of sifted flour and lastly one ounce of melted butter
4. Place in greased paper boxes in the oven
