## Ingredients

- Onion stalks
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the tender onion stalks.
2. Blanch them lightly.
3. Squeeze them very well so they have no water.
4. Mix them in beaten egg and fry as in the previous recipe.
