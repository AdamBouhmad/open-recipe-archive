## Ingredients

- capon
- livers
- bread
- cream
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- three egg yolks
- butter
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Clean the capon well and debone it
2. Chop the livers and with all the meat pieces that remain, put a piece of bread in a dish with half a cup of cream with finely chopped parsley, onions and mushrooms, salt and pepper
3. Pass all this through lard
4. Dissolve three raw egg yolks and use this to stuff the capon
5. Spread well with butter and well covered with bread powder, place in the oven to cook
