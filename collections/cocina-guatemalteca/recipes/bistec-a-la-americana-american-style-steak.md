## Ingredients

- tenderized, round pieces of meat
- sour orange juice
- many onions sliced very thin
- butter, oil or buttermilk
- fried potatoes or soufflés
- tender lettuce

## Directions

1. Prepare tenderized, round pieces of meat; if they are from the side leave the bone
2. Add sour orange juice and many very thinly sliced onions and fry in butter, oil or buttermilk
3. Serve with two turns along with fried potatoes or soufflés and tender lettuce
