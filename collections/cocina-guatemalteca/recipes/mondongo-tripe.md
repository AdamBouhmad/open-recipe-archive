## Ingredients

- beef tripe
- salt
- a little lard
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- achiote
- a little flour
- a little water
- cooked chickpeas (for Spanish style variation)

## Directions

1. After the tripe is well cooked, put a little lard in a pan with a little salt and heat it.
2. Prepare a fried mixture as follows: after peeling the tomato, chop it very finely with onion and parsley that have been chopped beforehand.
3. Take a small piece of achiote and dissolve a little of it in the same pan to give it color.
4. Add the tripe cut into long pieces and add a little flour dissolved in a little water.
5. For Spanish-style mondongo, prepare it the same way but add cooked chickpeas.
