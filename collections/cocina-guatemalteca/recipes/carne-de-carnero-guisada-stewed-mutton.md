## Ingredients

- mutton
- water
- salt
- lard
- flour
- broth or water
- pepper
- clove
- parsley
- garlic
- small onions
- egg yolks
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well and cut into pieces
2. Place in a pot with a little water and salt; when it comes to a boil, remove and wash in cold water and drain well
3. Heat a saucepan with lard on the fire
4. Add the meat with salt; when it takes on color, add one or two tablespoons of flour according to the amount of meat and stir so it does not burn
5. Add a little broth or water and season with pepper, clove, parsley, garlic and small crushed onions
6. When cooked, remove all the herbs
7. Dissolve three egg yolks in the remaining sauce
8. When serving, add a few drops of lemon
