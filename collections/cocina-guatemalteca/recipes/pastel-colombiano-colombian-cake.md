## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 4 ounces butter
- 4 ounces powdered sugar
- 6 egg whites
- 5 ounces salpor or bread flour
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg yolks with 4 ounces of butter and 4 ounces of powdered sugar
2. When well beaten, mix in the 6 egg whites with 5 ounces of salpor or bread flour
3. Add 1 teaspoon of Royal
4. Place in a mold greased with lard and into the oven
