## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Water
- Salt
- Cooked meat, half beef and half pork
- Peeled and chopped tomato
- Onion
- Parsley
- One teaspoon of vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Butter

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage and cook in water with salt
2. Chop some cooked meat (half beef and half pork); fry with peeled and chopped tomato, onion and parsley, one teaspoon of vinegar, pepper powder
3. Butter a pan, place a layer of cabbage and a layer of meat, alternate them, making sure the last one is cabbage
4. Add two tablespoons of melted butter and place in the oven or over two fires
