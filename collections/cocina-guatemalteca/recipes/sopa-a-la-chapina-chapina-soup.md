## Ingredients

- Broth
- Pepper powder
- Chopped parsley
- Ground meat
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Cinnamon
- Castilian pepper
- Whole egg
- Egg yolk
- Rice (one ounce)
- Eggs
- Flour
- Salt
- Lard

## Directions

1. After preparing the broth well, strain it and add pepper powder and chopped parsley
2. Make small meatballs with the well-ground meat, adding as spices: saffron, cumin, cinnamon and Castilian pepper
3. Mix all this with the meat, adding one whole egg and one yolk
4. In this proportion grind one ounce of well-soaked and dried rice
5. Beat two eggs, first the whites and then the yolks
6. Mix in flour and a little salt
7. Have a pan with well-heated lard ready
8. Drop the egg mixture well mixed with flour by spoonfuls making very small fritters
9. Remove them golden
