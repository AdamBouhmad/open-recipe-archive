## Ingredients

- izote flower
- salt
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the seed from the flower
2. Cook it with salt
3. When done, remove and squeeze to release all the water
4. Mix with beaten egg, seasoning with salt
5. Fry in hot butter
