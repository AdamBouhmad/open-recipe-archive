## Ingredients

- beef broth
- egg yolks
- mustard powder
- butter
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve two egg yolks in a cup of broth, a tablespoon of mustard powder, a tablespoon of melted butter, two tablespoons of vinegar
2. If desired, add a bit of sugar
