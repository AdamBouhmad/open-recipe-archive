## Ingredients

- cooked miltomate (tomatillos)
- garlic clove
- oregano

## Directions

1. Make like the previous hilachas, with the only difference being that you grind only cooked miltomate, a garlic clove, and a little oregano, without grinding bread or tortilla into it
