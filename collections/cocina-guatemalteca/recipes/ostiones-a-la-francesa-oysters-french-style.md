## Ingredients

- oysters
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- oyster cooking broth
- bread powder
- Castilian pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning the oysters well, cook them.
2. Place a pan over heat with two or three tablespoons of oil.
3. Add a peeled tomato, onion and chopped parsley.
4. Add a little of the broth in which the oysters were cooked.
5. Add one tablespoon of bread powder ground with some Castilian peppers.
6. Add the oysters for a light boil and serve.
