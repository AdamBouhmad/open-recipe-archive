## Ingredients

- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 cup of water
- 1 cup of white honey
- 4 egg whites
- lemon zest (optional)
- ground cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. Put sugar, water, and white honey together
2. Cook until it reaches the ribbon stage
3. Remove and let cool
4. While the honey cools, beat 4 egg whites for the same amount of time it takes to cool the honey
5. When the honey is cool, pour it onto the beaten egg whites while continuing to beat with a wooden spatula
6. Optional: add lemon zest and ground cinnamon on top
