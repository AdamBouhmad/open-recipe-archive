## Ingredients

- plenty of peeled garlic, minced
- chorizo
- parsley
- tomato
- spearmint
- 1 green chile
- pepper powder
- salt
- oregano powder
- 2 onion heads
- 2 bay leaves
- breadcrumb powder
- a small amount of dried cheese powder
- 1 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince plenty of peeled garlic, chorizo, parsley, tomato, spearmint and 1 green chile
2. Season with sufficient pepper powder, salt and plenty of oregano powder
3. Lard the leg with this mixture after rubbing it on all sides
4. Place it in a pan with 2 onion heads, 2 bay leaves, pepper powder, breadcrumb powder, a small amount of dried cheese powder and 1 cup of vinegar
5. Put it in the oven for two hours, basting it with its juices frequently so it does not dry out
