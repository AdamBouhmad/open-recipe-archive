## Ingredients

- Green guaque chiles
- Zucchini
- Carrot
- Tender green beans
- Potatoes
- Tender peas
- Salt
- One teaspoon of lard
- Peeled tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- A little vinegar
- One tablespoon of bread powder ground with Castilian pepper
- A small piece of cinnamon
- Two cloves
- Beaten egg
- Hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as in the first chile recipe
2. Chop zucchini, carrot, tender green beans, potatoes, and tender peas
3. Cook everything with salt
4. Put a pan on the fire with one teaspoon of lard
5. Add peeled, chopped tomato, garlic, and chopped onion
6. When fried, add all the vegetables with a little vinegar, salt, one tablespoon of bread powder ground with Castilian pepper, a small piece of cinnamon, and about two cloves
7. Fill the chiles with this mixture
8. Wrap them in beaten egg
9. Fry in lard and serve like the others
