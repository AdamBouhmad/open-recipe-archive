## Ingredients

- pineapple
- flesh of one coconut
- coconut water
- three pounds of clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and grind pineapple with the flesh of one coconut
2. Dissolve everything with the coconut water and have three pounds of sugar clarified at the right point
3. Add the coconut to let it reach the consistency of paste
4. Decorate
