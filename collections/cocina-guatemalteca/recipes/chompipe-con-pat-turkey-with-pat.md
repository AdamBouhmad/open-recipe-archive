## Ingredients

- five pounds of meat
- half an ounce of salt
- a quarter ounce of Castilian pepper
- four eggs
- one tablespoon of flour
- one jar of paté
- one deboned turkey

## Directions

1. Grind five pounds of meat and add half an ounce of salt, a quarter ounce of Castilian pepper, four eggs and one tablespoon of flour
2. Have ready a jar of paté and a deboned turkey
3. Layer the meat and paté alternately, one layer of meat and one of paté, continuing until finished
4. Sew closed with thick thread, wrap in cloth, cook and press as with the previous recipes
