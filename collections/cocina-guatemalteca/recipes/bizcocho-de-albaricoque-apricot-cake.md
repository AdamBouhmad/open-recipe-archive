## Ingredients

- eggs (2)
- milk (1 cup)
- butter (2.5 teaspoons)
- sugar (1 cup)
- flour (1 cup)
- Royal baking powder (1 teaspoon)
- vanilla essence (1 teaspoon)
- apricots with whipped cream (for filling)

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar.
2. Add the flour mixed with Royal baking powder and hot milk mixed with flavoring.
3. Beat very well.
4. Pour into the oven in layers.
5. When cooked, layer in a serving platter: one layer of cake, one layer of apricots with whipped cream, another layer of cake, and finally a layer of apricots and sweetened cream on top.
