## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound butter
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 4 eggs
- 0.5 pound flour, sifted
- 1 glass of milk
- 0.5 pound flour
- 0.5 glass of sherry
- 1 teaspoon Royal baking powder
- 0.5 pound raisins, figs, chilacayote and candied lemons, chopped

## Directions

1. To 0.5 pound of butter add 0.5 pound of sugar, 4 eggs (white and yolk) and beat very well
2. Then mix in 0.5 pound of sifted flour, beat very well
3. Add 1 glass of milk, then add another 0.5 pound of flour with 0.5 glass of sherry
4. When everything is well mixed, add 1 teaspoon of Royal, 0.5 pound of chopped raisins, figs, chilacayote and candied lemons
5. Pour everything into a mold greased with lard and place in the oven very hot
