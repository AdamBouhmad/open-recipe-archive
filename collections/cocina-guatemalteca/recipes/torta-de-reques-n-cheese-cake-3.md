## Ingredients

- one liter of milk
- a small piece of rennet tablet or half a teaspoon of rennet
- one ounce of almonds
- one ounce of sugar
- two ounces of requesón (curd cheese)
- three eggs
- two ounces of melted butter
- one eighth of an ounce of cinnamon powder
- half a pound of clarified sugar
- half a teaspoon of vanilla

## Directions

1. Heat one liter of milk over the fire and add a small piece of rennet tablet or half a teaspoon of rennet
2. When the requesón forms, remove and squeeze very well in a clean cloth
3. Grind one ounce of almonds and one ounce of sugar
4. Beat three eggs, first the whites and then the yolks
5. Mix all the ground ingredients with the eggs, two ounces of melted butter, and one eighth of an ounce of cinnamon powder
6. Coat a mold with butter
7. Pour in the mixture and place in the oven for twenty minutes
8. Insert a knife; if it comes out clean it is done
9. Remove and place in another mold
10. Have ready half a pound of clarified sugar with half a teaspoon of vanilla
11. Cover the cake and put back in the oven
