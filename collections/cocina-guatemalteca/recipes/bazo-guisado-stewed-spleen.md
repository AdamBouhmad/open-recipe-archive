## Ingredients

- spleen
- salt
- miltomate, cooked or roasted
- garlic clove
- oregano
- dry chile
- piece of French bread, soaked
- lard

## Directions

1. Salt the spleen and roast it in the oven or grill it.
2. Then cook it in salted water.
3. Grind a bit of cooked or roasted miltomate with a garlic clove, a little oregano, a dry chile, and a piece of soaked French bread.
4. Dissolve this mixture in the water in which the spleen was cooked.
5. Fry with a little lard and add the spleen cut into pieces of desired size.
6. Serve hot.
