## Ingredients

- Green fava beans, peeled
- Salt
- Two cooked tomatoes
- Garlic clove
- Oregano
- Small onion head, roasted
- Achiote, hot
- French bread, soaked
- One tablespoon lard
- Pork loin or rib, cooked (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled green fava beans with salt
2. When cooked, remove them
3. Grind two cooked tomatoes, one garlic clove, a little oregano, a roasted small onion head, a hot piece of achiote, and a piece of soaked French bread
4. Dissolve this in the broth in which the fava beans cooked and fry in one tablespoon of lard
5. Add the cooked fava beans to this sauce; serve alone if desired, or with pieces of cooked pork loin or rib
