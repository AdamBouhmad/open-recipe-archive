## Ingredients

- good broth
- all kinds of finely chopped vegetables
- fried seasoning
- eggs (amount depends on soup quantity)
- sardine
- vinegar
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Prepare very good broth and add all kinds of finely chopped vegetables and fried seasoning
2. Beat the necessary eggs, being careful to beat the white and yolk separately; add the yolk to the white when it is stiff
3. Add a little sardine and make small fritters
4. Just before serving, add the fritters to the broth
5. Add a little vinegar and pepper powder
6. Serve hot
