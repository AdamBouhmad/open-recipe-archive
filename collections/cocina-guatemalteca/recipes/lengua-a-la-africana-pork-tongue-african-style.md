## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- long strips of ham
- Castilian pepper powder
- salt
- olive oil
- bay leaves
- parsley
- two pimientos morrones
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and lard it with long strips of ham that go through to the other side
2. Arrange the ham strips neatly for good appearance
3. Rub the ham strips with Castilian pepper powder and salt
4. Fry the tongue in a tablespoon of oil and when golden add salt, two bay leaves, parsley, two diced pimientos morrones, a cup of water, and half a cup of vinegar
5. Let it cook until done and serve hot
