## Ingredients

- apple or quince (2 pounds)
- sugar (1 pound)
- water (minimal, used in cooking)

## Directions

1. Cook the apple or quince with a small amount of water.
2. Remove the core and seeds from the fruit.
3. When cooled, strain the fruit without any water.
4. For 2 pounds of fruit, use 1 pound of sugar.
5. Place on fire, adding only the water in which the fruit was cooked.
6. After 1 hour of cooking, let it boil.
7. Pour into molds of desired shape and let cool.
8. The same method is used for guava paste.
