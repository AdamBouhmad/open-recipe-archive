## Ingredients

- a piece of meat
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- raw tomato
- bay leaf
- a sprig of thyme
- onion stems
- a small jar of mushrooms with its juice
- 1 cup red wine
- 2 tablespoons flour
- a piece of burnt sugar

## Directions

1. The day before, place a piece of meat in brine with salt, pepper, vinegar, raw tomato, bay leaf, a sprig of thyme, and onion stems
2. The next day, place over heat; when half cooked, add a small jar of mushrooms with its juice, 1 cup of red wine mixed with 2 tablespoons of flour and a piece of burnt sugar
3. Cook in an oven if available, or over two fires if not
