## Ingredients

- pig blood
- salt
- spearmint
- onion
- green chile
- oregano powder
- pepper powder
- lard
- intestines
- soap
- lemon
- water

## Directions

1. At the moment of slaughtering, add salt to the blood and stir very well
2. Chop plenty of spearmint, onion, green chile, oregano powder, and pepper powder
3. Chop plenty of lard
4. Mix everything well together
5. Wash intestines very well with plenty of soap and lemon
6. Fill the intestines with the mixture, not completely full to prevent bursting
7. Place in a little boiling water to parboil
8. After boiling ten minutes, remove them
9. Eat roasted or fried with tomato chirmol and cooked potatoes
