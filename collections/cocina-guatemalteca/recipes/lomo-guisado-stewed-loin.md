## Ingredients

- Loin
- Ham
- Garlic cloves
- Pepper powder
- Butter or oil
- Water
- Vinegar
- French bread
- Tomato
- Onion
- Pulque or vinegar

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with ham, garlic cloves, and pepper powder
2. Brown in butter or oil
3. When it takes on color, add water and vinegar to cover the meat
4. Fry a piece of French bread, tomato, and onion; grind these and dissolve in a little pulque (or use vinegar if pulque is not available)
5. Bring to a boil with the meat
6. Serve as preferred, either in slices or whole
