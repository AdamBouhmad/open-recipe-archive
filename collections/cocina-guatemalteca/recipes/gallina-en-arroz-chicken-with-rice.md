## Ingredients

- one chicken
- salt
- rice
- saffron water
- chayote (güisquil)
- carrot
- potatoes
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- oregano powder

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken well and cook it with salt
2. When cooked, remove the chicken and in the broth add rice according to the number of people, well washed but not soaked as it will fall apart
3. Add a little saffron water, chayote, carrot, potatoes cut into small pieces, a fried mixture of peeled and chopped tomato, garlic clove and onion
4. Place over very gentle heat without stirring so it does not fall apart
5. Just before serving, add the chicken in pieces and oregano powder
