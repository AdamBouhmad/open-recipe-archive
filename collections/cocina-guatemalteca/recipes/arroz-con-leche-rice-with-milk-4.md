## Ingredients

- 0.5 pounds rice
- 1 glass water
- 2 glasses cooked milk
- salt
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 2 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Wash 0.5 pounds of rice
2. Put it with 1 glass of water on very gentle fire without stirring the rice, only move the pot
3. When the water is consumed, add 2 glasses of cooked milk
4. Add some grains of salt and 1 cinnamon stick
5. When it has boiled sufficiently, add 2 ounces of sugar
6. Serve cold or hot
