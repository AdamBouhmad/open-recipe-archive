## Ingredients

- salmon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- parsley
- nutmeg
- capers
- cooked tomato
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a serving dish, place the salmon with a little butter, salt, pepper powder, onion, chopped parsley, a little nutmeg, some capers and cooked ground tomato.
2. Mix everything well and place on the salmon with lemon juice to taste.
