## Ingredients

- Water (two glasses or half a liter)
- Salt
- Lard (half a teaspoonful)
- Flour (half a pound, well sifted)
- Eggs (eight to nine, depending on size)
- Lard (sufficient for frying)

## Directions

1. Put a saucepan on the heat with two glasses of water (or half a liter) and a pinch of salt.
2. When it boils, add half a teaspoonful of lard, remove from the heat and add half a pound of well-sifted flour.
3. Put back on the heat without stopping stirring it.
4. When it no longer sticks to the hand, remove it and add eggs one by one, eight if they are large, nine if they are small.
5. Mix everything well with a small spoon and drop the fritters one by one into a pan with sufficient lard.
