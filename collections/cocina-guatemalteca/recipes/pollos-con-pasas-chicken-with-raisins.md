## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil or butter
- 8 ground raisins
- pepper
- 2 cloves
- water
- 2 onion heads, chopped

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the chickens well
2. Fry them in oil or butter
3. When they turn color, add about 8 ground raisins mixed with pepper and about 2 cloves
4. Dissolve with a little water
5. In the chicken fat, fry 2 chopped onion heads
6. Put all the ground mixture in this
7. Add the chicken and let it boil 2 or 3 times
8. Serve hot
