## Ingredients

- Young, tender squabs, plucked and cleaned
- Butter
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 2 cloves of garlic
- Salt
- 1 sprig of thyme
- 1 tablespoon of bread powder

## Directions

1. Place the plucked and cleaned squabs in a dish with butter, a sprig of parsley, two cloves of garlic, salt, a sprig of thyme and a tablespoon of bread powder
2. Cover with water
3. Let them boil for ten minutes
4. To serve, remove all the vegetables
