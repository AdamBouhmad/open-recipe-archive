## Ingredients

- crabs
- 1 garlic clove
- Castilian peppers
- oregano
- cumin seeds
- 2 roasted tomatoes
- 4 raw tomatoes
- guaque chile, cooked or dried chile with a small piece of achiote
- French bread, soaked
- lard
- crab cooking broth

## Directions

1. Clean the crabs and cook them as the previous recipe
2. Grind the small claws raw with one garlic clove, some Castilian peppers, a little oregano, some cumin seeds, two roasted tomatoes, four raw tomatoes, and one cooked guaque chile; if not available, grind dried chile with a small piece of achiote, and a soaked French bread
3. Dissolve all this with a little of the broth in which the crabs were cooked
4. Fry in a little hot lard and in this put the crabs to give a few boils and serve hot
