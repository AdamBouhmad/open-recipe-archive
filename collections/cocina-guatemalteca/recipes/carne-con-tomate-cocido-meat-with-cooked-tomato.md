## Ingredients

- meat for slicing
- butter
- peeled tomatoes
- parsley
- garlic cloves, roasted
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Heat butter
2. Add chopped peeled tomatoes to the meat with a little parsley and a few roasted garlic cloves
3. When it has boiled, remove the parsley and garlic
4. Serve hot
