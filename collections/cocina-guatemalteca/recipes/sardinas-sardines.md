## Ingredients

- three green guaque chiles, roasted and peeled
- salt
- lemon juice
- two or three onions
- vinegar
- pepper powder
- sardines with oil
- pickles in mustard
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Roast and peel three green guaque chiles
2. Cut them into very thin strips and place with salt and lemon juice
3. Slice two or three onions very thinly and place in this same juice for three hours
4. Remove and place in vinegar with salt and a little pepper powder
5. Arrange alternating layers of this mixture and sardines with their oil on a platter
6. Top with a layer of pickles in mustard
7. Cover the edge of the platter with fried bread slices
