## Ingredients

- Cleaned squabs
- Salt
- Powder of oregano
- 1 glass of white wine
- Onion, chopped
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 sprig of thyme
- Parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. After the squabs are well cleaned, rub them inside and out with salt and powder of oregano
2. Place them in a dish with a glass of white wine, chopped onion, 2 bay leaves and a sprig of thyme
3. Put in a hot oven, basting them every five minutes
4. After one hour, cover the squabs with plenty of Parmesan cheese powder until they are golden
5. Serve as desired
