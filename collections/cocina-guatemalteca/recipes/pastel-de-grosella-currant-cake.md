## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- one quarter teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- one cup of currant (canned)
- three quarters cup of sour cream
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one quarter teaspoon of nutmeg powder

## Directions

1. Mix everything very well
2. Put in a buttered mold
3. Place in the oven for half an hour
