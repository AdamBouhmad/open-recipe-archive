## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Salt
- Lettuce leaves
- Ham slices, from York or domestic, well cooked
- Fried potato slices, preferably soufflé

## Directions

1. Fry or break the eggs in hot lard, either in an egg dish or in a small pan, put the pan on the fire with one tablespoon of lard.
2. When well hot, break the egg, making sure it stays very whole, add a little salt on the yolk and with a spoon baste with very hot lard until it is covered with a small white film that forms.
3. If fried in an egg dish, remove with a spoon, and if not, with a skimmer so they come out whole and well white, place them on the platter with lettuce leaves, slices of ham, whether from York or domestic and very well cooked, and slices of well fried potato, so much better if they are soufflés.
