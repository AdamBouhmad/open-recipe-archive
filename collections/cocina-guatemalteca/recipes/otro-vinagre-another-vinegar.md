## Ingredients

- flour (1 pound)
- yeast (1 cup)
- water (1 cantaro, a large jug)
- panela (1 piece, added after 5 days)

## Directions

1. Mix 1 pound of flour with 1 cup of yeast.
2. Wrap very tightly in thin cloth.
3. Place in a jug of water and cover.
4. After 5 days, add 1 piece of panela.
5. After 15 days, stir without touching it.
6. If it is already very strong, strain and it is ready to use; if not, leave it several more days until it is strong enough.
