## Ingredients

- loin
- one-quarter ounce of salt
- garlic clove
- broth from the cooked meat
- flour
- achiote
- parsley
- cooked potato slices

## Directions

1. Season the loin with one-quarter ounce of salt and a garlic clove
2. When well fried, add broth from the cooked meat
3. Mix a tablespoon of flour with achiote coloring
4. Place the loin with chopped parsley and cooked potato slices in the sauce
