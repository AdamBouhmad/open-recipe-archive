## Ingredients

- bread, sliced
- oil or butter
- ground tomato
- sweet pepper or dried pepper
- eggs
- bacon, pieces

## Directions

1. Fry slices of bread in oil or butter
2. In the same fat that remains fry ground tomato with sweet or dried pepper
3. Cover the bread with this sauce and place a fried egg on top with pieces of well-fried bacon
