## Ingredients

- pickles, very fresh
- fine oil
- salt
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- fresh cheese or dry cheese powder
- green chile

## Directions

1. Chop pickles finely
2. Mix with fine oil, salt, pepper powder, and oregano powder
3. Cut a small cap off a French roll and remove all the crumb
4. Soak the roll with one spoonful of oil
5. Fill with the chopped mixture
6. Top with a slice of fresh cheese or one spoonful of dry cheese powder
7. Place half a green chile on top
8. Cover with the cap that was removed
9. Serve
