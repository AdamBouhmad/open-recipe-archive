## Ingredients

- Shrimp
- Potatoes, peeled and cut
- Shrimp shells
- Castilian peppercorns
- Two roasted tomatoes
- One dried chile
- A small piece of annatto
- A little finely ground masa
- Water
- One tablespoon of hot lard

## Directions

1. Soak the shrimp with hot water and peel them.
2. Cook them with peeled potatoes that have been cut.
3. Grind the shrimp shells with some Castilian peppercorns, two roasted tomatoes, one dried chile, a small piece of annatto, and a little finely ground masa.
4. Mix this with a little water.
5. Fry in one tablespoon of hot lard.
6. Add the shrimp and potatoes to this mixture, ensuring it is neither too thin nor too thick.
