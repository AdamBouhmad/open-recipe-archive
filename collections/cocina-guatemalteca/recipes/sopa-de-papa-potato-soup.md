## Ingredients

- potato
- good broth
- fried seasoning
- sliced fried bread
- grated hard cheese powder
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the potato
2. Grind it in a meat grinder
3. Dissolve in very good broth
4. Add fried seasoning
5. Serve with slices of fried bread, hard cheese powder, and chopped parsley
