## Ingredients

- one turkey
- one Frenchman
- vinegar
- pepper powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Kill one day before
2. Soak a Frenchman in vinegar
3. Rub the turkey inside and outside with this, pepper powder and butter
4. Place in the oven as the previous recipe
