## Ingredients

- 3 ounces peeled almonds
- 1 glass milk
- 4 ounces sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 ounces butter
- Leche Nevada (snowy milk)

## Directions

1. Grind 3 ounces of peeled almonds
2. Dissolve them in 1 glass of milk
3. Add 4 ounces of sugar
4. Put on the fire to reach the point
5. Lower the heat and when cool, mix in 4 egg yolks and 2 ounces of butter
6. Grease a dish with butter
7. Put a layer of this mixture and a layer of Leche Nevada (snowy milk) and cover with another layer
8. Put in the oven to brown
