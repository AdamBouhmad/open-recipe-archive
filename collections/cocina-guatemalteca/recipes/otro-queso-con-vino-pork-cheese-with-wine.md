## Ingredients

- pig's head
- red wine
- thyme sprig
- bay leaves
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Clean the head well.
2. Chop it raw.
3. Place in a dish with a bottle of red wine, a thyme sprig, two bay leaves and a little pepper powder.
4. Let it cook well for six hours.
5. When well cooked, remove the bay leaves and thyme.
6. Press the rest as with the other cheese.
