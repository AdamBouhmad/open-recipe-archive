## Ingredients

- butter
- plenty of garlic
- one tablespoon of flour
- one cup of milk
- one can of oysters with their brine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry plenty of garlic in butter
2. When fried, remove it and in that same butter add one tablespoon of flour without letting it brown
3. Add one cup of milk without making it too thick
4. As soon as it boils, add one can of oysters with all their brine
5. Serve with lemon juice
