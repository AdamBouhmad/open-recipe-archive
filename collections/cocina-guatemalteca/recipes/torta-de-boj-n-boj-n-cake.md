## Ingredients

- bojón
- salt
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove all the skin and thin center stalk from the bojón
2. Chop it
3. Cook it with salt
4. When cooked, drain all the water
5. Mix with beaten egg
6. Form the cake
7. Fry like the previous ones
