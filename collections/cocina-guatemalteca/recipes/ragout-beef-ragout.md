## Ingredients

- good piece of loin
- butter
- flour
- salt
- thyme
- parsley
- bay leaf
- water
- carrot slices
- potato slices

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of loin and slice it
2. Fry the slices in a pan with butter
3. Sprinkle with a little flour and salt to taste
4. Make a bouquet of thyme, parsley, and bay leaf
5. Add sufficient water and the bouquet
6. Add slices of carrot and potatoes
7. Place on very low heat until everything is cooked
8. Remove the bouquet of herbs before serving
9. Serve on a plate garnished with lettuce and serve hot
