## Ingredients

- white beans
- tomato
- onion
- achiote
- flour
- bretones (broccoli rabe)
- black chorizo
- ham

## Directions

1. Cook the white beans well
2. Fry them with a fried mixture made of chopped tomato and onion
3. Add a bit of achiote coloring
4. Add a bit of flour dissolved in water
5. Add the bretones that have been chopped and cooked beforehand
6. Add chopped black chorizo and pieces of ham
7. Let it boil a few times and serve hot
