## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), in pieces
- salt
- miltomate, raw
- handful of green chiles
- 1 garlic clove
- Castilian peppers

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey the same way as the previous recipe.
2. Grind sufficient raw miltomate according to the amount of meat, with a handful of green chiles, garlic clove, and Castilian peppers.
3. Fry this mixture, dissolving it with the same broth.
4. Add the turkey pieces.
5. Serve hot.
