## Ingredients

- Half pound of butter
- Four ounces of ground sugar
- Three egg yolks
- Half pound of sifted flour
- Half cup of milk
- One teaspoon of Royal

## Directions

1. Beat half pound of butter with four ounces of ground sugar and three egg yolks
2. After ten minutes of beating, add half pound of sifted flour, half cup of milk, and one teaspoon of Royal
3. Mix well
4. Pour into a buttered mold
5. Put in a hot oven
