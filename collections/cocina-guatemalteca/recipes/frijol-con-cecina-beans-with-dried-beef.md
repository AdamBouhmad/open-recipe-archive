## Ingredients

- one pound of beans
- one pound of dried beef
- two spoonfuls of lard
- about twenty miltomates, raw and ground
- a few Castilian peppers
- a small piece of dough
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the beans
2. At the first boil add one pound of dried beef
3. When both things are cooked, add two spoonfuls of lard
4. Add about twenty raw and ground miltomates with a few Castilian peppers
5. Add a small piece of dough, dissolve with a little water
6. Let it boil for another quarter hour
