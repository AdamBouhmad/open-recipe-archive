## Ingredients

- jocotes (30)
- sugar (1 pound) or white panela (0.25 pound)

## Directions

1. Put a pot of water on the fire.
2. When boiling, add the jocotes that have been diced beforehand.
3. When they are partially cooked, transfer them to clarified honey or syrup.
4. For 30 jocotes, use 1 pound of sugar; if using panela, use one-quarter that amount, but it must be white.
