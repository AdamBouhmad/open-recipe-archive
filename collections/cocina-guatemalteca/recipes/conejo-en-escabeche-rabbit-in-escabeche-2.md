## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- pieces of ham
- pepper powder
- one and a half cups oil
- thyme
- bay leaf
- one cup water
- half cup vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. After cleaning the rabbit well, lard it with pieces of ham and pepper powder
2. Place over heat with one and a half cups of oil, thyme, and bay leaf
3. When it stops releasing juice, add one cup of water and half cup of vinegar with salt
4. Let it brown very slowly
