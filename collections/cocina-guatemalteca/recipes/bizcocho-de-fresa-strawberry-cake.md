## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- four tablespoons of butter
- one egg
- one cup of flour
- one tablespoon of Royal baking powder
- a little salt
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of vanilla
- one teaspoon of cream
- one teaspoon of strawberries

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar together.
2. Add the beaten egg.
3. Add the flour and half of the milk.
4. Add the Royal baking powder, vanilla, and the rest of the milk.
5. Place in the oven in a very tall mold.
6. When cool, split in half and cover with strawberries mashed with sugar and cream on top.
7. Place the other layer on top and more strawberries on top.
