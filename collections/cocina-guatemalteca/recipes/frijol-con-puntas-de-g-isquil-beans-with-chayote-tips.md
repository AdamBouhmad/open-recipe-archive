## Ingredients

- beans
- one bunch of chayote tips
- one tablespoon of lard
- one tablespoon of flour
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. When the beans are more than cooked, take one bunch of chayote tips
3. Remove all the thin strings and peel them very well
4. Chop them and add to the beans with one tablespoon of lard
5. Dissolve one tablespoon of flour in a little water
6. Let it boil until cooked
7. Stir the beans so they burst and do not come out with the shell
