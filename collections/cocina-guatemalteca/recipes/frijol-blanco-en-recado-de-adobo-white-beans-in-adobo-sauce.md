## Ingredients

- White beans
- Two roasted tomatoes
- Garlic clove
- Oregano
- Roasted onion
- Achiote (optional for heat)
- French bread, soaked
- Water

## Directions

1. Cook the beans as previously described
2. Grind two roasted tomatoes with one garlic clove, a little oregano and a roasted onion, with a little heat if desired, a piece of achiote and a piece of soaked French bread
3. Dissolve everything with water
4. Add the beans when the mixture comes to a boil and serve hot
