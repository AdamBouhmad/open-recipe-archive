## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- 4 or 5 Castilian peppers
- 2 roasted tomatoes
- a small piece of achiote
- dried chile (optional)
- French bread, soaked and ground or a small piece of very finely ground corn dough
- a sprig of epazote or cumin seeds

## Directions

1. Clean the crabs very well and remove all the small claws, leaving them to cook with salt, and keep the large claws on the crab
2. Grind the small claws with four or five Castilian peppers, two roasted tomatoes, a small piece of achiote and if desired a dried chile
3. Thicken with soaked and ground French bread or with a small piece of very finely ground corn dough
4. Dissolve the sauce in the broth in which the claws were cooked, add a sprig of epazote and if epazote is not ground, grind some cumin seeds instead
