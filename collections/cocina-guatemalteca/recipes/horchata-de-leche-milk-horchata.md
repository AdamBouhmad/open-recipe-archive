## Ingredients

- Half a pound of pepitoria, peeled like for marzipan
- Four ounces of peeled almond
- Four ounces of melon seed, soaked in hot water
- Sugar to taste
- Four liters of water
- Four liters of milk

## Directions

1. Peel half a pound of pepitoria like for marzipan and grind very fine
2. Grind four ounces of peeled almond
3. Grind four ounces of melon seed soaked in hot water
4. Strain with a little clean water
5. Add sugar to taste, four liters of water and four liters of milk
6. Alternatively, it can be made by grinding four ounces of soaked rice, half a pound of pepitoria and half an ounce of cinnamon, straining it only with sugar and water
