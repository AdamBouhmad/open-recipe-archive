## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Water
- Butter equal in weight to the dough
- One egg white
- Cooked brain
- Butter for frying
- Pepper powder
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with a little very cold water until the dough is soft
2. Let rest one hour
3. Weigh the dough; add an equal amount of butter
4. Roll out with a roller and spread butter on it
5. Close the dough very well, roll out two or three times and rest ten minutes
6. Roll out again and cut layers with the rim of a jar
7. Place first layers on a baking sheet, brush with egg white
8. Cover with another layer that has been cut in the middle with a smaller mold
9. Place in a very hot oven
10. Prepare filling with cooked brain fried in butter with pepper powder and chopped parsley
11. Fill the shells and cover with the baked lids
