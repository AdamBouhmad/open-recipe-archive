## Ingredients

- Twenty-four egg yolks
- Two pounds of very white sugar at medium point
- One egg white for clarifying
- A few drops of lemon
- One stick of cinnamon
- Raisins and chopped almonds for garnish
- Nougat

## Directions

1. Beat the twenty-four egg yolks until they are very stiff
2. Pour into a greased pan and place in the oven
3. Insert a stick; if it comes out clean, it is done
4. Pour onto a very clean cloth and leave covered until the next day
5. Remove the bottom skin
6. Cut into the desired shape, preferably large quarters made into sponge cake
7. Clarify two pounds of very white sugar at medium point with one egg white and a few drops of lemon
8. Boil the eggs in this syrup for half an hour, because if they boil longer they turn black
9. Just before removing them, add a stick of cinnamon
10. Coat with the syrup after removing the cinnamon and garnish with raisins and chopped almonds
11. If desired, let all the syrup drain very well and arrange in the platter in alternating layers of nougat and egg, finishing with nougat and covered with cinnamon powder
