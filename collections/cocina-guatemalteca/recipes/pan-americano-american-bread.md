## Ingredients

- butter or lard (0.5 pound)
- sugar (6 ounces)
- eggs (9)
- flour (1 pound)

## Directions

1. Work 0.5 pound of butter or lard for half an hour with 6 ounces of sugar.
2. Beat 9 eggs; mix the yolks with the butter and 1 pound of flour.
