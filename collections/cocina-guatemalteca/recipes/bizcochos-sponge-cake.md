## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 ounce chocolate powder
- 4 ounces sifted bread flour

## Directions

1. Beat the six egg whites as if for nougat
2. Mix in the six yolks with one ounce of chocolate powder and four ounces of sifted bread flour
3. Stir together and place in a mold greased with lard in the oven
