## Ingredients

- eggplants
- tomato
- onion
- chorizo
- ham
- flour
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the eggplants
2. Stew them as in the previous lentil recipe
3. Alternatively, they can be made in oil and vinegar
