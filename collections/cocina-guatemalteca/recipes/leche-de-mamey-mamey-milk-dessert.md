## Ingredients

- 3 glasses milk
- 1 pound sugar
- 12 ounces peeled and ground almonds
- 1 mamey, ground and strained
- almonds and raisins for garnish

## Directions

1. Place sugar, peeled and ground almonds, and ground and strained mamey in three glasses of milk
2. Place over heat until it reaches proper consistency
3. Decorate with almonds and raisins
