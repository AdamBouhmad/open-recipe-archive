## Ingredients

- one fat hen
- eight cloves
- two bay leaves
- three onions browned in butter
- potatoes
- small amount of peas
- carrots
- green beans
- chayote squash
- potato
- parsley
- tomato
- minced onion
- flour or white bread without sweetening
- dry sherry (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean a fat hen well and simmer it over low heat for three hours
2. Add eight cloves, two bay leaves, and three onions browned in butter
3. When the hen is tender, remove it and add enough peeled potatoes to the broth, leaving them until they break down
4. Add a small amount of peas, carrots, green beans, chayote squash, and potato, all cut into very small pieces
5. Debone the hen and mince the meat until it is like ground meat
6. Make a small seasoning mixture of onion and peeled and finely chopped tomato
7. Add the hen meat with a little flour or a little white bread without sweetening
8. Mix very well with a small spoon and form small meatballs by taking them in small quantities and dropping them into the broth
9. As soon as it boils, remove from heat and serve hot
10. If desired, add a small glass of dry sherry
