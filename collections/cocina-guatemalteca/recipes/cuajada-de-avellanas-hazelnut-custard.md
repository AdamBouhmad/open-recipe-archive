## Ingredients

- One pound of hazelnuts
- Half pound of peeled and toasted almonds
- One pound of sugar
- Nine eggs
- One teaspoon of cinnamon powder
- Half pound of candied chilacayote, chopped

## Directions

1. Grind the hazelnuts and almonds in the machine
2. Add the sugar, eggs, cinnamon powder, and chopped candied chilacayote
3. Mix everything well
4. Pour into a greased mold
5. Place in the oven
