## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- salt
- a little white wine or sherry
- juice of one lemon
- bay leaf
- thyme
- pepper
- chopped garlic
- one tablespoon oil
- one tablespoon butter
- white bread crumbs

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place in a pan with salt, a little white wine or sherry, lemon juice, bay leaf, thyme, pepper, and chopped garlic
2. Once cooked, remove and fry in one tablespoon of oil and one of butter
3. Coat with white bread crumbs and let brown
