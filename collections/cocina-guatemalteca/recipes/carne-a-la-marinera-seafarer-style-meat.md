## Ingredients

- Meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Butter
- Large onions
- Red wine
- Bay leaf
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Cook the meat with salt, pepper, parsley, and oregano
2. When cooked, cut into slices and arrange in a well-spread skillet
3. Prepare a sauce by browning large onions in butter
4. When the onions have taken on color, add a little red wine, salt, pepper, a bay leaf, and a sprig of thyme
5. Bathe the meat with this sauce and place over very low heat
6. Serve hot
