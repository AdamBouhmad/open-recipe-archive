## Ingredients

- Cooked tongue
- Lard or butter
- Peeled and chopped tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Pineapple
- Black pepper powder
- Butter
- Breadcrumb powder
- Pickled vegetables
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the well-cooked tongue and fry it
2. When golden in color, remove and set aside
3. In the same lard, fry the peeled and chopped tomato, onion, garlic, finely chopped pineapple, and black pepper powder
4. Butter a casserole dish and layer alternately: tongue, fried sauce, breadcrumb powder, and pickled vegetables, ensuring the last layer is breadcrumbs
5. Pour a cup of equal parts water and vinegar over the layers
6. Place over two fires to bring to a boil
