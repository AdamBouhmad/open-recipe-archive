## Ingredients

- a small loin
- salt
- pepper powder
- a little vinegar
- flour
- butter
- ripe tomato
- tender onion stalks
- garlic head
- parsley
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. After cleaning the loin well, rub it with salt and pepper powder mixed with a little vinegar, pricking it very well with a fork
2. Let it sit for an hour
3. Then coat it in flour and fry it in butter
4. When browned, place it in a saucepan
5. Cook some ripe tomato, a bunch of tender onion stalks, a garlic head, and a bunch of parsley; strain and cover the meat with this
6. Place on very low heat and serve hot, adorning it with lettuce leaves
