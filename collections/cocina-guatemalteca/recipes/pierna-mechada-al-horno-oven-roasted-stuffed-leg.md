## Ingredients

- parsley
- spearmint
- 1 green chile
- pepper powder
- oregano powder
- salt
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 cup red wine
- butter
- breadcrumb powder
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mince parsley, spearmint and 1 green chile
2. Season with pepper powder, plenty of oregano powder and salt
3. Lard the leg on all sides with this mixture and rub with plenty of salt
4. Coat with 1 cup of vinegar and 1 cup of red wine
5. Coat generously with butter and sprinkle with breadcrumb powder, pepper, cloves and cinnamon
6. Put in the oven for two hours
7. Serve with salad
