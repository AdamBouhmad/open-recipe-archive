## Ingredients

- miltomate
- cooked chile zambo
- ten or twelve Chiapa peppercorns
- water
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast a little miltomate, grind with a cooked chile zambo and about ten or twelve Chiapa peppercorns
2. dissolve with a little water, one spoonful of vinegar, salt and serve cold
