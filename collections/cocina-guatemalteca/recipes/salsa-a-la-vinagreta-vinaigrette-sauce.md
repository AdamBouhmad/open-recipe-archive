## Ingredients

- parsley
- vinegar
- mustard powder
- fine oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind sufficient parsley, add strong vinegar, two spoonfuls of mustard powder and three spoonfuls of fine oil, well seasoned with salt
2. serve with any meat
