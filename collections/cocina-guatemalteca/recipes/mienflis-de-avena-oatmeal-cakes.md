## Ingredients

- one cup of quick oats
- one cup of sour milk or curdled milk
- one egg
- one half cup of brown sugar
- one cup of wheat flour
- one half teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of Royal baking powder
- one half teaspoon of baking soda
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak the oats in the milk for one hour
2. Add the egg and beat well
3. Then add the sugar and the flour sifted with the Royal baking powder and baking soda and the melted butter
4. Mix well
5. Butter the small molds and place them in a hot oven for ten to twenty minutes
