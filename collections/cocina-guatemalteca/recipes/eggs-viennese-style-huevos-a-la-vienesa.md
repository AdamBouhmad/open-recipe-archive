## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Bread
- Butter
- Lettuce leaves
- Parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in oil, ensuring they come out very whole.
2. Add salt and pepper powder to them.
3. Remove them with a skimmer with great care.
4. Place them on slices of bread browned in butter.
5. Arrange them on a platter on tender lettuce leaves.
6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese powder on top.
