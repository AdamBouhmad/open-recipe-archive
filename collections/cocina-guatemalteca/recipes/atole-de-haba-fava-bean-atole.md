## Ingredients

- fava bean powder
- milk or water
- cinnamon stick
- optionally: one tablespoon of coffee essence
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the fava bean powder, if desired with milk, if not with water
2. Add a small stick of cinnamon
3. When made only with water, one tablespoon of coffee essence and sugar to taste can be added
