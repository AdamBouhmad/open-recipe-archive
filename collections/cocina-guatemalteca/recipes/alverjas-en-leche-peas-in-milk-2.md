## Ingredients

- peas, well washed
- salt
- a little baking soda
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- milk
- a little ground nutmeg powder
- Castilian pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the peas well washed in boiling water with salt and a little baking soda
2. Fry one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour
3. When fried, gradually add milk until a thin sauce forms
4. Add a little ground nutmeg powder and Castilian pepper
5. Add the peas to this
6. Serve hot
