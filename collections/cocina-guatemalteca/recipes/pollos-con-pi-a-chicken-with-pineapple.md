## Ingredients

- chickens
- onion heads, peeled
- ground pineapple
- sausage
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- water
- 1 small glass of vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well
2. Put them in a casserole with peeled onion heads, ground pineapple and sausage
3. Add 1 tablespoon of butter
4. When half fried, add a little water and 1 small glass of vinegar
5. Let it cook until the liquid is consumed
