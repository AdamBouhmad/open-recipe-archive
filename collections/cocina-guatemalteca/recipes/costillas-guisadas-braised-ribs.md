## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Lard or butter
- Garlic clove
- Tomatoes
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Lemon juice
- Bread powder
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Cut ribs to desired size
2. Fry in a pot with lard or butter, a clove of garlic, two ground tomatoes mixed with a little water, salt, pepper powder, clove, and three tablespoons of vinegar
3. Let simmer for two hours
4. Remove ribs and add lemon juice, bread powder, and cheese
5. Fry in a little lard
6. Serve as desired
