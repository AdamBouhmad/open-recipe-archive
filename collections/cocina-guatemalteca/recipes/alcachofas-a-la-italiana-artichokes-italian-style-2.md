## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Manteca (lard), one tablespoon
- Onions, two large, chopped
- Mushrooms, abundant, chopped
- Meat juice, one cup
- White wine, one glass
- Salt
- Parsley, a bit, chopped
- Bay leaves, two
- Pepper powder

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes with water and salt.
2. In a pan, put one tablespoon of manteca with two large chopped onions.
3. When fried, add abundant chopped mushrooms, one cup of meat juice, one glass of white wine, salt, a bit of chopped parsley, two bay leaves and pepper powder.
4. Let simmer until it thickens and bathe the artichokes with this sauce.
