## Ingredients

- oil
- two to three roasted and ground tomatoes
- two garlic cloves
- oregano powder
- two to three cooked onions, shredded
- salt
- one tablespoon vinegar
- french bread, fried
- water
- fish, whole or in pieces

## Directions

1. Fry two to three roasted and ground tomatoes in oil with two garlic cloves.
2. Add oregano powder, two to three cooked shredded onions, salt, one tablespoon of vinegar with a piece of fried french bread dissolved in a little water.
3. Add the whole or cut fish to this preparation.
