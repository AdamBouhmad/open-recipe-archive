## Ingredients

- rice or corn dough
- green or guaque chile
- tomato
- miltomate

## Directions

1. If made of rice, proceed as with the previous tamales
2. If made of corn, the same, except when simmering the dough do not add saffron
3. Make the sauce by grinding green or guaque chile, plenty of cooked tomato and miltomate
4. Fry and mix as with the others
