## Ingredients

- 9 eggs
- juice of 4 sweet oranges
- 0.5 pound of sugar

## Directions

1. In nine eggs add the juice of four sweet oranges and half a pound of sugar
2. Beat for a quarter hour
3. Pour into a mold and let set for one hour until it sets
4. Place in a water bath, cover with a sheet and place hot coals on top to brown it, or place in a hot oven
