## Ingredients

- miltomate
- tomato
- guache chile
- lard
- cooked tongue
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook a little miltomate, tomato and soaked guache chile.
2. Fry it in a little lard and dissolve with water.
3. Add the well-cooked tongue, either in slices or whole, with potatoes.
4. Serve hot, or if for travel, serve cold.
