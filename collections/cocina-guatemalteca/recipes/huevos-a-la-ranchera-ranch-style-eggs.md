## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard
- Peeled tomato
- Onion
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. They can be made fried in lard, making sure they come out very white; fry a peeled and chopped tomato, with very thin slices of onion; season with salt and one tablespoon of water; cover the eggs with this to serve, serve them hot.
2. They can also be made by frying peeled and chopped tomato, with onion, add salt, one or two tablespoons of water, in this drop the eggs making sure the boiling is even, so they come out whole: serve to the taste of the person, if desired tender or well cooked.
