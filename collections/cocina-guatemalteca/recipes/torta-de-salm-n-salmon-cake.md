## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- salt
- pepper powder
- 1 tin salmon, without bones
- 1/2 cup milk
- lard
- puree or peas for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs with salt and pepper powder to taste
2. When well beaten, add one tin of salmon (without bones) and one-half cup of milk
3. Mix everything well and fry in hot lard and when golden, fold and decorate with puree or peas around the edge
