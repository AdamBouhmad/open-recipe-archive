## Ingredients

- oysters
- oil
- garlic
- onion
- bay leaves
- oregano powder
- Castilian pepper
- clove
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cooking the oysters, fry in oil.
2. Remove the oysters.
3. In the same oil fry chopped garlic and onions and add two bay leaves, oregano powder, Castilian pepper and a little clove.
4. If the oil is not sufficient, add more with one tablespoon of vinegar.
5. Let boil and when reduced, remove the bay leaves and onion.
6. Bathe the oysters with this sauce to serve.
