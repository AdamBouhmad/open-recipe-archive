## Ingredients

- meat
- lard
- parsley, ground
- Castilian pepper, two small
- chile, a small piece
- water
- vinegar, a little
- potatoes, peeled

## Directions

1. Pierce a piece of meat with a fork and place in hot lard
2. Add a little ground parsley with two small Castilian peppers and a small piece of chile
3. Cover the meat with water and a little vinegar
4. When half-cooked, add peeled potatoes
5. Serve hot
