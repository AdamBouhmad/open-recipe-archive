## Ingredients

- Bread slices
- Oil
- Lard
- Hen, well cooked
- Four egg yolks, hard-cooked
- Two roasted tomatoes
- Eight Castilian peppercorns
- Three cloves
- A small piece of cinnamon
- Red wine (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry bread slices in oil and lard, and place in the soup tureen
2. Remove the breast from the well-cooked hen and grind it together with four hard-cooked egg yolks, two roasted tomatoes, eight Castilian peppercorns, three cloves and a small piece of cinnamon
3. Pour everything into the broth to boil once and cover the bread slices with this mixture
4. Add a little red wine if desired
