## Ingredients

- 1 turkey, deboned
- ground meat for filling
- salt
- Castilian pepper powder
- nutmeg
- parsley, chopped
- caul fat (membrane from sheep's belly)
- 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
- lard
- water and salt

## Directions

1. Debone the turkey and cut into 2-inch square pieces.
2. Prepare a filling of ground meat seasoned with salt, Castilian pepper powder, nutmeg, and chopped parsley.
3. Fill the turkey pieces with this mixture.
4. Prepare caul fat in pieces and wrap the turkey pieces in it, securing with a stitch.
5. Place in a pan with lard and the sliced carrots.
6. Add a little water with salt.
7. Let simmer until cooked through.
