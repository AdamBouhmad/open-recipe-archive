## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cooked with salt and peeled
- Butter
- Bread powder, ground
- Castilian pepper
- Cheese powder, plenty
- Milk, half a cup

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes with salt and peel them
2. When cold, slice them
3. Spread a pan with butter
4. Place a layer of potatoes, another of ground bread powder with Castilian pepper and mixed with plenty of cheese powder, another of potatoes and so on, alternating
5. Cover with half a cup of milk and place in the oven or over two fires
