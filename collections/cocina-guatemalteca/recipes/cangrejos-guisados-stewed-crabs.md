## Ingredients

- Crabs
- Salt
- Four raw tomatoes
- One garlic clove
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Castilian peppers
- Achiote
- Cooked guaque chile
- French bread soaked
- Lard

## Directions

1. After cleaning the crabs well, cook them with only the claws, with salt
2. Grind all the small legs with four raw tomatoes, one garlic clove, a little oregano, a little cumin, some Castilian peppers, a piece of achiote, a cooked guaque chile and a piece of soaked French bread
3. Mix with the same broth, fry in one teaspoon of lard and place the crabs in this to serve hot
