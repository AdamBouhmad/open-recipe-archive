## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), in pieces of desired size
- salt
- 6 to 8 pasilla chiles, seeded
- 6 ounces of peeled and fried almonds
- 4 ounces of toasted sesame seeds
- 1 small piece of nutmeg
- 4 chocolate tablets with cinnamon
- lard for frying
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey in pieces with salt.
2. Fry the pasilla chiles in lard and grind them with peeled and fried almonds, toasted sesame seeds, nutmeg, and chocolate tablets with cinnamon.
3. Dissolve this mixture with the same broth and fry.
4. If desired sweet, add sugar to taste.
5. Serve hot.
