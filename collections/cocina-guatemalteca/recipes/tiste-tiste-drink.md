## Ingredients

- Two pounds of salpor
- One ounce of cinnamon
- Three ounces of cacao
- Two small cloths of very fine achiote
- Three pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Toast two pounds of salpor
2. Grind it, sift in a strainer and once sifted, grind again with one ounce of cinnamon, three ounces of cacao, two small cloths of very fine achiote and three pounds of sugar
3. To serve, put one large spoon in a pitcher of water and beat well so that when poured into a glass it makes foam
