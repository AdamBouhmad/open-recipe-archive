## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- ham
- pepper powder
- garlic
- onion
- salt
- vinegar
- butter
- thyme
- bay leaves
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Lard the leg with ham, pepper powder, garlic and onion with salt
2. Rub with vinegar, salt and one tablespoon of butter
3. Add chopped onion, thyme and bay leaves
4. Place in the oven with plenty of bread powder on top
5. Baste frequently while cooking
