## Ingredients

- five and one quarter ounces of flour
- one egg
- half cup of milk
- water if milk is not available
- a pinch of salt
- sugar
- oil, lard or butter

## Directions

1. Mix very slowly so it does not become lumpy
2. Have a skillet with hot oil, lard or butter
3. Fry the size desired
4. Put sugar on top and burn with a hot iron
