## Ingredients

- one turkey with wings and legs
- salt
- butter
- four pounds of pork meat
- one tablespoon of lard
- four peeled and chopped tomatoes
- four onions
- two garlic cloves
- one small glass of vinegar
- one glass of sherry
- a little ground breadcrumb powder
- eight cloves
- eight Castilian peppers
- one small piece of cinnamon
- one tablespoon of sugar (optional)
- two carrots
- one güisquil or half if large
- ten to fifteen tender green beans
- four large potatoes
- one cup of tender peas
- one ounce of raisins
- ten to twelve peeled and halved almonds
- capers
- olives
- four bay leaves
- cloves
- Castilian peppers
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Debone the turkey leaving the wings and legs
2. Rub with salt and butter and let sit for one hour
3. Parboil four pounds of pork meat with salt, remove and chop finely
4. Place a pan over the fire with one tablespoon of lard, four peeled and chopped tomatoes, four onions and two garlic cloves
5. Add the meat with a small glass of vinegar, one glass of sherry, a little ground breadcrumb powder, eight cloves, eight Castilian peppers and one small piece of cinnamon
6. Add one tablespoon of sugar if you wish it sweet
7. Remove when half cooked
8. Have ready two carrots, chopped and cooked, half a güisquil (or whole if small), ten to fifteen tender green beans, four large potatoes and one cup of tender peas
9. Mix everything with the meat, adding one ounce of raisins, ten to twelve peeled and halved almonds, capers and olives
10. When well mixed, sew the turkey closed with thick thread
11. Rub the turkey with salt and butter, place four bay leaves, some cloves and Castilian peppers on it
12. Place in the oven for three hours, basting with its juices every fifteen minutes
13. Serve hot, garnishing with green lettuce
