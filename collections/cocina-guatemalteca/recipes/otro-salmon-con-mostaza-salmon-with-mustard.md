## Ingredients

- one or two spoonfuls French mustard
- vinegar
- abundant oil
- pepper powder
- salt
- salmon
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Place one or two spoonfuls of French mustard in a platter with a little vinegar and abundant oil, pepper powder and salt
2. Place the salmon in this mixture, mixing with great care
3. Serve with minced parsley on top
