## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- five eggs
- five ounces of sugar
- one pound of flour
- one teaspoon of Royal

## Directions

1. Beat half a pound of butter for a quarter of an hour
2. Beat five eggs as for nougat and when well beaten mix the yolk with the white
3. Mix with the butter five ounces of sugar and one pound of flour
4. Stir everything well with one teaspoon of Royal
5. Put in greased baking dishes in the oven with sprinkles on top
