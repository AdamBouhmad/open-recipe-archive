## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- flour
- pepper powder
- cinnamon
- lard
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- olive oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish well, season with salt.
2. Coat in flour mixed with pepper powder and cinnamon.
3. Fry in very hot lard until golden.
4. Remove the fish and place on a platter.
5. Bathe with one cup of vinegar heated with two bay leaves.
6. Add pepper powder and half a cup of fine olive oil with salt.
7. Cover and let rest until the following day.
8. Serve with very thin onion slices and peeled lemon slices.
