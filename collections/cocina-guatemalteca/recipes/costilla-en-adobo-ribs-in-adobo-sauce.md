## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Salt
- Cooked or roasted tomato
- Garlic clove
- Oregano
- Achiote
- Dried chile
- French bread
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with salt
2. Grind cooked or roasted tomato with a garlic clove, a little oregano, a small piece of achiote, a dried chile, and soaked French bread
3. Dissolve this mixture in the broth in which the meat cooked
4. Add the ribs and let them come to a boil two or three times
5. Serve hot with slices of cooked potatoes
