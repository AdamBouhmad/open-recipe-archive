## Ingredients

- Nine ounces of powdered sugar
- Nine ounces of flour
- Nine ounces of butter
- One teaspoon of vanilla
- Four whole eggs

## Directions

1. Place powdered sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a copper pot
2. Place over gentle heat or double boiler
3. Beat the contents of the pot until it reaches a foamy appearance and has doubled in volume
4. Add sifted flour carefully, adding melted butter
5. Pour this batter into a mold buttered and dusted with flour
6. Put in a hot oven to bake quickly
7. Pour some of the batter into small fluted molds, similarly buttered and floured
8. Place on a bed of ash and put in the oven
9. When baked, place the cake on a platter with the small molds around it
