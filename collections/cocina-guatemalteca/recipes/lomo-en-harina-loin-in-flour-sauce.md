## Ingredients

- Loin or tender meat
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- One teaspoon of butter or lard
- Tomatoes
- Onions
- Slices of chorizo
- Ham
- One sprig of thyme
- One bay leaf
- One tablespoon of cold flour

## Directions

1. Make slices of loin or tender meat and place in a pot with water, salt, pepper powder, and butter or lard
2. Grind raw tomatoes and onions and add to the meat
3. Add slices of chorizo, ham, a sprig of thyme, and a bay leaf
4. When it has reduced somewhat, add one tablespoon of cold flour
5. Place over two fires so it cooks very slowly, ensuring even heat
