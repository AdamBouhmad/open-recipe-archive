## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- pepper powder
- vinegar
- butter
- onion
- red bell peppers
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Well wash the liver and cut it into thin strips
2. Rub it with salt, pepper powder and a little vinegar
3. Let it sit in this brine for half an hour
4. Place a pan on the fire with butter
5. Fry thinly sliced onion and as soon as it is fried, add the liver
6. Then add red bell peppers cut lengthwise
7. When it has taken on flavor, add one tablespoon of flour dissolved in half a cup of water
8. Let it boil for ten minutes
9. Serve hot
