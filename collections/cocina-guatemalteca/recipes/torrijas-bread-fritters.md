## Ingredients

- bread sliced into rounds
- two eggs
- lard for frying
- one pound of clarified sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- ground or chopped almonds

## Directions

1. In two small bowls beat two eggs, first the white and then the yolk
2. Wrap the bread slices in this
3. Fry in hot lard
4. Wash and dry with a clean and damp cloth
5. Have ready one pound of clarified sugar
6. Put the torrijas in this with cinnamon sticks and when they heat, remove them
7. Place on a platter and decorate them with almonds
