## Ingredients

- meat, in pieces
- lard
- tomatoes, raw, chopped
- tomatoes, roasted, chopped
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- garlic, crushed
- pimentón (if available)
- water, sufficient

## Directions

1. Fry meat pieces in lard
2. Add chopped raw tomatoes and roasted tomatoes, pepper, clove, cinnamon, and crushed garlic (add pimentón if available)
3. Add sufficient water to cook
4. Simmer until cooked
