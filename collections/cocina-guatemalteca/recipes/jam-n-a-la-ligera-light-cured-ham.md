## Ingredients

- 1 ham leg
- water
- 2 pounds salt
- 1 pound saltpeter
- juice of 12 sweet oranges
- juice of 10 sour oranges
- 1 ounce Castilian pepper
- 1/2 ounce cloves
- 2 pounds ice
- bay leaves
- parsley
- orange leaves

## Directions

1. Prick the leg with a fork as in the previous recipe and cover with water, 2 pounds of salt, 1 pound of saltpeter, the juice of 12 sweet oranges and 10 sour oranges, 1 ounce of Castilian pepper and 1/2 ounce of cloves
2. Keep it in this brine for 5 or 6 days, pressing it every night
3. The next day return it to the same brine with 2 pounds of ice
4. After this time, put it to cook in the same brine with bay leaves, parsley and orange leaves
5. After four hours, remove it, turning it over every hour
