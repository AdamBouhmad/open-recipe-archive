## Ingredients

- whole green beans
- salt
- beaten egg
- hot lard
- cooked tomato sauce
- onion
- chile

## Directions

1. Cook the whole green beans with salt
2. Remove and dry with a clean cloth
3. Wrap in beaten egg
4. Fry in hot lard
5. Cover with a sauce made of cooked tomato, onion and chile, mashed and seasoned with salt
6. Add this sauce to the green beans
