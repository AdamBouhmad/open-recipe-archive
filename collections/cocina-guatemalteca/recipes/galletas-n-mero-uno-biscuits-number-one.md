## Ingredients

- 9 ounces of flour
- 6 ounces of washed and melted butter
- 4 ounces of powdered sugar
- 1 ounce of cinnamon powder
- 0.5 bottle of cooked milk
- 2 eggs beaten as for sponge cake with one additional yolk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together
2. Let rest for two hours
3. Then drop by teaspoonfuls into a pastry machine that has been preheated with alcohol
