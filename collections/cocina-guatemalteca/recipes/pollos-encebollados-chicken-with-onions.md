## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- pepper
- half a cup of oil
- half a cup of vinegar
- half a cup of white wine or sherry
- 4 raw tomatoes
- 3 heads of onion
- 3 bay leaves

## Directions

1. After washing the chickens, add salt and pepper
2. Place in a pot and add half a cup of oil, half a cup of vinegar, half a cup of white wine or sherry, 4 raw tomatoes, 3 heads of onion, and 3 bay leaves
3. Cook over low heat
