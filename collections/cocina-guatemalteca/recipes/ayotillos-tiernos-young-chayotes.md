## Ingredients

- young chayotes
- salt
- dried chile soaked and seeded
- oregano
- garlic clove
- three cooked tomatoes
- one teaspoon lard

## Directions

1. If small, cook whole with salt; if not, chop them
2. Grind a soaked and seeded dried chile with a little oregano, one garlic clove, three cooked tomatoes
3. Fry in one teaspoon of lard
4. Add the chayote to this
