## Ingredients

- marquesote (sponge cake)
- fresh ripe strawberries
- one glass of whipped cream with sugar

## Directions

1. Break the marquesote and place it on a flat plate
2. Cover it completely with whole ripe strawberries
3. On top pour a glass of cream beaten with sugar
4. Cover with the remaining marquesote
5. Cover with more cream and adorn with ripe strawberries
