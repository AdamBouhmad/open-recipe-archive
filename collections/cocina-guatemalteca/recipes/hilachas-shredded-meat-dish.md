## Ingredients

- cutaxte (a cut of meat)
- salt
- tomato, ground
- dried chile
- onion with stalk
- manteca (lard)
- water
- french bread or white bread
- cold tortillas
- achiote (annatto)
- cooked potatoes, sliced

## Directions

1. Put the cutaxte to cook; after four or five hours of boiling (add salt before cooking), it will be well done
2. Slice it lengthwise, making thin slices, and fry in very hot manteca
3. Grind together a little tomato, a dried chile, and an onion with its stalk, all cooked
4. Remove the hilachas and in the same manteca fry this mixture, thickening it with a little water, salt, and two tortillas soaked in cold water
5. If desired, add a bit of achiote coloring and sliced cooked potatoes with plenty of manteca
6. Serve hot
