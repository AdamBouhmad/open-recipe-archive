## Ingredients

- milk, sweetened to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon bread flour
- a little sherry

## Directions

1. Heat a little sweetened milk
2. Add 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of bread flour so it reaches a regular consistency
3. When it thickens, add a little sherry
