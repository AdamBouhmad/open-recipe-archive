## Ingredients

- Oil
- Four large peeled tomatoes
- Red pepper strips, very thin
- Onion slices
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Cooked cod
- One glass of oil and vinegar
- Canned fried vegetables, optional

## Directions

1. Place a baking dish with a little oil, four large peeled tomatoes cut into pieces, very thin strips of red pepper, onion slices, salt, and pepper powder.
2. Oil a frying pan with one tablespoon of oil.
3. Layer alternately the chopped vegetables and cooked cod that has been prepared as in previous recipes, ensuring the top layer is sauce.
4. Moisten with one glass of oil and vinegar mixture.
5. If desired and available, add a can of fried vegetables.
6. Cook over two fires until it boils.
