## Ingredients

- sheep head
- tomato
- miltomate
- garlic
- oregano
- cumin
- chile
- achiote
- French roll

## Directions

1. Cook the head
2. Grind cooked tomato and miltomate with garlic, oregano, cumin, chile, a small piece of achiote, and a soaked French roll
3. Thin the mixture with the cooking broth
4. Fry the sauce
5. Add the head cut into pieces with the tongue cut into pieces
