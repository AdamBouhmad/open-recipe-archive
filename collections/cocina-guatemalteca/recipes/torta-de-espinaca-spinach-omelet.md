## Ingredients

- spinach
- eggs
- tomato
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach well, remove the stems and chop very fine.
2. Beat eggs according to the quantity desired.
3. Add the spinach to the beaten eggs and fry the omelet in a hot pan.
4. Make a fried seasoning of tomato and onion.
5. Give the omelet a quick boil in this seasoning.
