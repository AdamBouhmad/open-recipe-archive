## Ingredients

- carrot
- chayote
- green beans
- potatoes
- bicarbonate of soda
- peas
- tender beans
- salt
- chicken or tender hen
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper powder
- parsley
- one large tomato
- onion
- French mustard mayonnaise
- hard egg
- red bell peppers
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Chop carrot, chayote, green beans, potatoes in slices; cook everything separately, adding a little bicarbonate of soda to the greens
2. also cook peas and tender beans, all with salt, and likewise a clean chicken with salt
3. if there is no chicken, use a tender hen; when cooked, chop into very whole pieces
4. prepare on a platter one cup of vinegar, one of oil, pepper powder, chopped parsley, one large peeled and seeded tomato, one peeled and chopped onion
5. in this place the vegetables well mixed with the chicken, season with salt and cover with a mayonnaise made with French mustard
6. decorate with hard egg, cut lengthwise or in slices, long pieces of red bell peppers, olives and capers
