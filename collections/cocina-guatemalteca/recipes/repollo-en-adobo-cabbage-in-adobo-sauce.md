## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Three roasted tomatoes
- One roasted onion head
- One garlic clove
- A small piece of achiote
- One dry chile
- A piece of French bread, soaked
- One teaspoon of lard

## Directions

1. Cook the chopped cabbage with salt
2. Grind three roasted tomatoes, one roasted onion head, one garlic clove, a small piece of achiote, one dry chile, and a piece of soaked French bread
3. Dissolve this mixture with a little water
4. Season with salt
5. Fry in one teaspoon of lard
6. Add the cabbage to this mixture, making sure it is not too sparse
