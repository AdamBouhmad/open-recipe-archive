## Ingredients

- acetic acid (0.5 ounce)
- water (1 liter)

## Directions

1. Buy 0.5 ounce of acetic acid.
2. Add 1 liter of water.
3. Use carefully as it is very strong.
4. This is the purest vinegar and should be used.
