## Ingredients

- vegetables
- three eggs
- three cooked egg yolks
- one raw egg yolk
- melted butter
- one cup of milk
- salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Chop the vegetables and cook separately so they come out green
2. Prepare a sauce as follows: cook three eggs, three cooked egg yolks and one raw yolk, break them up very well and gradually add melted butter
3. Mix very well, add one cup of milk, a little salt and mix in the vegetables which should be very well cooked
4. If there is cream, add one cup
