## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- milk, as hot as possible
- lard for frying
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of flour, add half a teaspoonful of salt and moisten with a little of the hottest milk possible, stirring so it does not form lumps, leaving it like dough for making fritters
2. Place in a long tin can that has a small hole opened in the bottom, add the dough and press with a wooden pin so the dough comes out and falls into very hot lard
3. Cut and while still hot, sprinkle powdered sugar on top
