## Ingredients

- two or three peeled tomatoes
- garlic clove
- onion
- one teaspoon oil or lard
- pepper powder
- clove powder
- half cup water
- salt
- chayote
- cheese slices

## Directions

1. Chop two or three peeled tomatoes, one garlic clove and onion
2. Fry in one teaspoon of oil or lard
3. Add pepper powder and clove powder, half a cup of water and a little salt
4. Add the chopped chayote to this; if small put whole
5. When cooked, serve with plenty of cheese slices
