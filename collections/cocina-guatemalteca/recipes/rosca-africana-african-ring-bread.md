## Ingredients

- Four ounces of butter
- Two eggs
- Two ounces of sugar
- Four ounces of well-sifted flour
- Juice of two lemons
- Half glass of milk
- One and a half ounces of yeast
- Four ounces of currants
- Two ounces of raisins
- Sugar and chopped almond for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until it becomes a paste
2. Add the two eggs, two ounces of sugar, and four ounces of well-sifted flour, the lemon juice, half glass of milk, and one and a half ounces of yeast
3. Knead very well for half an hour
4. Add the currants and raisins
5. Place in a very clean cloth, tie, and leave overnight in a container with water
6. The next day, remove and form the ring
7. Top with sugar and chopped almond
8. Place in the oven
