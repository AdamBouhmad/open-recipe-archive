## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- half a cup of sugar
- 6 tablespoons of cold water
- half a cup of flour
- half a teaspoon of Royal baking powder
- quarter teaspoon of salt
- cream or jelly of desired flavor
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Separate the eggs
2. Beat yolks until thick
3. Add sugar without stopping beating
4. Add water without stopping moving
5. Sift the dry ingredients together
6. Add to the yolks and fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Line a pan with waxed paper
8. Pour the batter thinly into the mold
9. Bake in a moderate oven for fifteen minutes
10. Remove from mold and place on a damp cloth dusted with powdered sugar
11. Remove paper and spread with cream or jelly of desired flavor
12. Roll without letting it cool
13. Place on a cold cloth in a platter
14. Dust with powdered sugar; everything must be done lightly because if it cools the pastry will crack when rolling
