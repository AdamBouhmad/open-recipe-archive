## Ingredients

- 3 cups of sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 1 pound of peeled and ground almonds (dry)
- butter for coating
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Place three cups of sugar and three egg whites in a vessel
2. Beat until stiff
3. Add one pound of peeled and ground almonds (dry)
4. Coat tin leaves with butter
5. Drop spoonfuls of this paste onto the leaves
6. Before placing the dough, dust the tin with bread flour
