## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sifted sugar
- four egg yolks
- a small glass of coffee essence
- ladyfinger biscuits (chiqueadores)

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, mix in the sifted sugar
2. When it becomes cream, add the egg yolks and beat gently
3. Gradually add the coffee essence
4. In a dish, cover the bottom with ladyfingers
5. Add a layer of cream and another of ladyfingers, alternating
6. Ensure the last layer is ladyfingers
7. Leave in a cold place
8. The next day, place the dish in hot water to turn out easily
