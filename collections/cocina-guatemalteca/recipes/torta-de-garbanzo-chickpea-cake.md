## Ingredients

- One pound of chickpeas
- One eighth ounce of cinnamon powder
- Sugar to taste
- Three eggs
- Butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of chickpeas; the following day peel them
2. Add one eighth ounce of cinnamon powder, sugar to taste, and three eggs
3. Mix everything well
4. Place in a pan greased with butter
5. Bake in the oven
