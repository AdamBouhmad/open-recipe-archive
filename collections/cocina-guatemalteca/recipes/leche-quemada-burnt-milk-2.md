## Ingredients

- 2 glasses milk
- 1 ounce cornstarch or 1 ounce soaked and ground rice
- 3 egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks or orange peel or 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put 1 ounce of cornstarch or 1 ounce of soaked, well-washed, ground and strained rice in 2 glasses of milk with 3 egg yolks
2. Put on the fire with some cinnamon sticks or an orange peel, or 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
3. When it has boiled sufficiently, pour into a serving platter
4. Once cold, sprinkle with powdered sugar
5. With a hot iron or band, brown it
