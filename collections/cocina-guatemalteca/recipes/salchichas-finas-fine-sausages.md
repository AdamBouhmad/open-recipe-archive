## Ingredients

- 6 pounds meat
- 1/2 ounce salt
- 1/4 ounce clove
- 1/4 ounce cinnamon
- 1/4 ounce Castilian pepper
- 1 small glass generous wine
- 1 small glass vinegar
- thin clean intestines
- lemon

## Directions

1. Grind 6 pounds of meat very finely
2. Add 1/2 ounce salt, 1/4 ounce clove, 1/4 ounce cinnamon, 1/4 ounce Castilian pepper, 1 small glass generous wine, and 1 small glass vinegar
3. Let sit in this infusion for three days
4. Wash thin, clean intestines with lemon
5. Fill the intestines with the meat mixture and tie them to the desired length
6. Place in the open air to dry
7. Serve roasted or fried with rice soup
