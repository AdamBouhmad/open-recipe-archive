## Ingredients

- mustard
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the mustard with water, salt, vinegar, oil and about four well-cooked and ground egg yolks
2. If desired, add a bit of sugar
