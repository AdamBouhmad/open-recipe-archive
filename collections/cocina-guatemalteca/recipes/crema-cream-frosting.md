## Ingredients

- Half pound of lump sugar
- One teaspoon of vanilla
- Half pound of strawberries passed through a sieve
- Three egg whites
- Half pound of melted butter
- Whole ripe strawberries for decoration

## Directions

1. Place sugar, vanilla, and sieved strawberries in a copper pot
2. When sugar is melted, let it reach the caramel stage
3. Beat three egg whites until stiff peaks form
4. Lower the caramel and gradually mix the beaten egg whites with the caramel
5. When cold, mix in half pound of melted butter while continuing to beat
6. Cover the cake with this cream
7. Decorate on all sides with whole ripe strawberries
