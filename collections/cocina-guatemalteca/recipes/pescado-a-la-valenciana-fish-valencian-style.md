## Ingredients

- butter
- tomato, peeled
- parsley
- garlic
- onion
- green chile
- olives
- capers
- clove powder
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- fish, clean
- bread powder
- one cup water

## Directions

1. Coat a dish well with butter.
2. Add peeled tomato, parsley, garlic, onion, green chile, olives, and capers, all chopped.
3. Mix in clove powder, cinnamon, and pepper in small quantity, well mixed with salt.
4. Place the clean fish on top of all this.
5. Cover with the same sauce, a little bread powder, and one cup of water.
6. Place in the oven or over two fires.
