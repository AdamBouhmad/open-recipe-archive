## Ingredients

- meat
- oregano
- pepper, ground
- garlic
- salt
- pepper powder
- vinegar
- tomato, chopped
- onion, chopped
- parsley
- bay leaves, two
- lard, one tablespoon
- red or white wine, one glass

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well
2. Coat with oregano, ground pepper, garlic, salt, pepper powder, and vinegar
3. Let infuse for two hours
4. In a pot, place chopped tomato and onion
5. Place the meat on top
6. Add more chopped tomato with parsley, two bay leaves, one tablespoon lard, and one glass red or white wine
