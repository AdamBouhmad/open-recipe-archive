## Ingredients

- egg whites
- sugar syrup (from previous recipe)
- fresh butter or butter mixed with quince paste for filling

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites as in the previous recipe, making sure the syrup is very overcooked
2. When cold, place a damp piece of paper on a thin board and put a teaspoon of nougat on it
3. Put in the oven; if desired remove white, if not let them take on color, leave for five minutes
4. Then remove and with great delicacy join one with another as a cap to form true meringues
5. They can also be filled before joining with fresh butter or mixed with quince paste, and once filled, joined together
