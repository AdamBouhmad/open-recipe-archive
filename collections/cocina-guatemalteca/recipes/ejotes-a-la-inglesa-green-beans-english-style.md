## Ingredients

- green beans
- salt to taste
- melted butter
- parsley, finely chopped
- lemon juice
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Cook the green beans with salt to taste, making sure they stay green.
2. While still a little hot, toss with a little melted butter, very finely chopped parsley, lemon juice and pepper powder.
