## Ingredients

- paprika
- ham in small pieces
- 15 cloves of garlic
- 3 roasted tomatoes
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- French bread

## Directions

1. Place paprika and ham pieces in a soup tureen
2. Grind 15 cloves of garlic and 3 roasted tomatoes together
3. Add water, vinegar, and salt to the ground mixture
4. Cut French bread into small pieces
5. Mix everything together and serve
