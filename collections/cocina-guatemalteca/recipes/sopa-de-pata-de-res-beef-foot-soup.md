## Ingredients

- One beef foot
- Three ounces of well-cooked pork loin
- Two eggs
- Bread powder
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook one beef foot until well done
2. Chop it with three ounces of well-cooked pork loin
3. Mix in two eggs (yolk and white) and a little bread powder
4. Fry in small cakes or balls as desired
5. Have the broth prepared as in the previous recipes and add the cakes to boil once
6. Serve with chopped parsley
