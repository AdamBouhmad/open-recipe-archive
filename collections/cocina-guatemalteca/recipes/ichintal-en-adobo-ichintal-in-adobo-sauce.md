## Ingredients

- ichintal, well washed
- salt
- about two cooked tomatoes
- one head of roasted onion
- a little oregano
- one clove of garlic
- a small piece of achiote
- one dry cooked chile
- a piece of French bread, soaked
- one tablespoon of lard

## Directions

1. Peel the ichintal and chop into small pieces
2. Cook with salt
3. Grind about two cooked tomatoes, one roasted onion head, a little oregano, one clove of garlic, a small piece of achiote, one dry cooked chile, and a piece of soaked French bread
4. Dissolve with the same broth
5. Fry in one tablespoon of lard
6. Add the pieces of ichintal to this
