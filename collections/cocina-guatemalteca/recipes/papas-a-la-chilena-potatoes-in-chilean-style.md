## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cooked with salt
- Butter, one spoon
- Garlic and parsley, very finely chopped
- Oil, one spoon

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes with salt
2. When cooked and cold, peel and cut into slices
3. Spread a pan with a spoon of butter
4. Chop some garlic and parsley very fine
5. Fry in a spoon of oil
6. Place a layer of potatoes and another of this fried mixture and so alternating the layers
7. Place in the oven or over two fires
