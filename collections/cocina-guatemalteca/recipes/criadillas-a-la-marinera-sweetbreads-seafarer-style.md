## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper powder
- bread
- oil
- lettuce
- potatoes
- cheese
- sardines
- egg

## Directions

1. After cooking well and removing the thick skin, cut lengthwise or into slices as desired
2. Place in a dish with a little vinegar, salt and pepper powder
3. Leave for a quarter hour
4. Fry very thin slices of bread in oil and when golden remove and place on green lettuce leaves on a platter
5. Add a layer of sweetbreads, a layer of cooked potato slices with salt bathed in the sweetbread infusion
6. Cover with pieces of fresh cheese or powder of dry cheese with sardines and pieces of hard egg
