## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- one tablespoon oil or lard
- one eighthth of an ounce salt
- one egg (beaten)
- one tablespoon sweet wine
- brain with salt, pepper powder, and chopped parsley (for filling)
- plenty of lard (for frying)
- tender lettuce leaves
- roasted and ground tomato sauce
- ten capers
- salt
- one teaspoonful oil

## Directions

1. Mix everything very well
2. Roll out with a rolling pin two or three times, spreading lard on each fold
3. On the final rolling, cut out small circles with the rim of a glass
4. Prepare a filling of brain with salt, pepper powder, and chopped parsley
5. Fill the circles with this mixture
6. Fold and seal the edges very well
7. Heat plenty of lard in a frying pan until very hot
8. Fry the pastries until golden
9. Place on a platter on tender lettuce leaves
10. Serve alone if desired, or with a sauce made from roasted and ground tomato mixed with about ten capers; season with salt and fry in one teaspoonful of oil
