## Ingredients

- one pound well-clarified sugar
- six egg yolks
- four ounces butter
- one cup pineapple juice

## Directions

1. Put one pound of well-clarified sugar on the heat; when it reaches caramel point, remove from heat and when cool, mix in six egg yolks with four ounces of butter.
2. Return to heat without stopping to stir, add one cup of pineapple juice, and when thickened, put on a plate and serve.
