## Ingredients

- meat (loin or ribs)
- tomato
- miltomate
- garlic head
- onion head
- a little burned sugar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces
2. Fry on low heat without browning
3. Add tomato, miltomate, a garlic head, and an onion head
4. Grind well with a little burned sugar
5. Cover with water and salt until cooked
