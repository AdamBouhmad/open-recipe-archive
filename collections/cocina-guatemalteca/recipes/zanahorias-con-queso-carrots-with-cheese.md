## Ingredients

- carrots
- salt
- one tablespoon butter
- cheese powder
- two roasted tomatoes
- parsley
- half cup milk

## Directions

1. Cook the carrots with salt, peel them, and cut into pieces or slices
2. Place one tablespoon of butter in a pan
3. Layer carrots and cheese powder alternately, ensuring the last layer is cheese
4. Cover with two roasted tomatoes and chopped parsley
5. Pour half a cup of milk over everything
6. Let it simmer slowly over low heat
