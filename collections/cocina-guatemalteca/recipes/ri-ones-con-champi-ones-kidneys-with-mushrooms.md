## Ingredients

- kidneys
- butter
- parsley
- garlic
- mushrooms
- pepper powder
- salt
- flour
- white wine or dry sherry

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys and fry them in butter
2. Add parsley, garlic, finely chopped mushrooms, pepper powder and salt
3. When everything is well fried, add one teaspoon of flour dissolved in white wine or dry sherry
4. Put them on the fire without letting them boil much because they become tough
