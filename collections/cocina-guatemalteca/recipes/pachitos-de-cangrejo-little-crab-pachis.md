## Ingredients

- corn (maíz)
- salt
- twelve ounces lard
- crab legs
- one cup cooked miltomate
- a little oregano
- a little cumin
- a small piece of achiote
- dried chile, cooked
- güisquil, chopped
- carrot, chopped
- green beans (ejotes), chopped
- potatoes, chopped
- one cup water
- whole crabs, well washed

## Directions

1. Grind the corn as for the other tamales.
2. Add salt and twelve ounces of lard.
3. Remove the thin pointed ends of the small crab legs and grind the rest with one cup of cooked miltomate, a little oregano, a little cumin, a small piece of achiote, and dried chile cooked to taste.
4. Chop güisquil, carrot, green beans, and potatoes and mix everything well together.
5. Add one cup of water.
6. Place one tablespoon of this mixture on a corn husk with a well-washed whole crab and its two large claws.
7. Wrap and cook.
