## Ingredients

- loin
- salt
- butter
- bread crumbs
- potato puree

## Directions

1. Rub the loin with salt and butter
2. Coat with bread crumbs and bake in the oven
3. Baste with its own juice to prevent it from drying out
4. Serve with mashed potatoes
