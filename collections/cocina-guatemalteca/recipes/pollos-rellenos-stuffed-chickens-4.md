## Ingredients

- 3 wide dried chiles
- 1 garlic clove
- a small piece of cinnamon
- 1 pound of pork
- tomato
- onion
- 1 teaspoon of vinegar
- salt
- butter
- 2 or 3 tablespoons of vinegar
- 2 bay leaves
- thyme

## Directions

1. Remove seeds from 3 wide dried chiles
2. Toast and grind them with 1 garlic clove and a small piece of cinnamon
3. Parboil 1 pound of pork and chop it
4. Put in a pan with tomato and peeled and chopped onion
5. Add the ground chile mixed with 1 teaspoon of vinegar seasoned with salt
6. Mix everything
7. Stuff raw chickens that have been previously rubbed with salt and butter with this mixture
8. Place them in a dish with 2 or 3 tablespoons of vinegar, 2 bay leaves, thyme and chopped onion
9. Take to the oven, basting them with their own juice so they don't dry out
