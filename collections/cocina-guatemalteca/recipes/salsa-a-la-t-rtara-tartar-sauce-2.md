## Ingredients

- four raw egg yolks
- half a glass of oil
- salt to taste
- a few drops of vinegar
- parsley, finely chopped
- one tablespoon English or French mustard

## Directions

1. Beat four raw egg yolks.
2. Gradually add half a glass of oil.
3. Season with salt to taste.
4. Add a few drops of vinegar and finely chopped parsley.
5. When well beaten, mix in one tablespoon of English or French mustard.
