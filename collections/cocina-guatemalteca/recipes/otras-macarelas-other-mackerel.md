## Ingredients

- three or four roasted tomatoes
- three or four red peppers
- hot oil
- salt
- one teaspoon vinegar
- mackerel

## Directions

1. Grind three or four roasted tomatoes with three or four red peppers
2. Fry this in hot oil and season with salt and one teaspoon vinegar
3. Serve the mackerel with this sauce, hot or cold as preferred
