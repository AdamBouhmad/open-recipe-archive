## Ingredients

- Pieces of meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ground garlic
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Onion
- Ham
- Green chile
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Sour orange juice
- Flour
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of meat and season with salt, pepper, ground garlic, and a bit of vinegar
2. Let sit overnight
3. The next day, fry the slices or pieces in lard with onion, ham, and finely chopped green chile and roasted, ground tomatoes
4. Dissolve in water and add enough to cover the meat, along with two bay leaves, a sprig of thyme, the juice of a sour orange, and a tablespoon of flour browned in lard or butter
5. Cook over moderate heat for about two hours
6. Serve with potatoes cooked plain
