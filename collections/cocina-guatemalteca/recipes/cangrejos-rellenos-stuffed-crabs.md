## Ingredients

- Crabs
- Salt
- Butter
- Peeled and chopped tomato
- Onion
- Peas
- Potatoes
- String beans
- Carrot
- Pepper powder
- White bread powder
- Vinegar
- Egg
- Lard

## Directions

1. After cooking the crabs well with salt, remove all the small legs, lift the shell and remove everything inside and everything in the claws
2. Fry all this in one tablespoon of butter with peeled and chopped tomato and onion, mix peas, potatoes, string beans, carrot, cooked and chopped
3. Add pepper powder, one teaspoon of white bread powder, one teaspoon of vinegar and salt
4. Fill the crabs with this, place the little cover on, wrap in beaten egg, fry in hot lard and serve with fried recado
