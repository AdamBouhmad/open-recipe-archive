## Ingredients

- onion
- tomato
- basil leaf
- mutton head
- broth
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- flour
- butter
- sherry

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped onion, tomato, and a basil leaf
2. When well fried, add the well-cooked mutton head cut into pieces
3. When it has taken on a golden color, add the broth in which the head was cooked with fried seasoning, pepper powder, and a tablespoon of flour fried in butter
4. Add a little sherry
5. Serve with toasted bread
