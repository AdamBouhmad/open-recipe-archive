## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- 3 pounds good quality meat
- butter
- onion
- salt
- powdered pepper
- Parmesan or Gruyère cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni in boiling salted water; if possible leave them whole; when cooked, remove them carefully so they do not break
2. Prepare the sauce: for one pound of macaroni, pound three pounds of very good meat in a meat grinder to extract all the juice
3. Fry the meat in hot butter where onion, very finely chopped, has already been fried
4. If there is no meat grinder, slice the meat and pound it well, then fry it
5. When it has taken on a golden color, fry the onion
6. Cover with water, add a little salt and a little powdered pepper
7. Let it boil for two hours
8. Remove the meat and cover the macaroni with this juice, sprinkling with Parmesan or Gruyère cheese
