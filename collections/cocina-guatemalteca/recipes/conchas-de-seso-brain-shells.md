## Ingredients

- butter
- garlic
- onion
- flour
- milk
- pepper powder
- one brain
- parsley
- oysters with their broth
- Parmesan cheese powder
- bread powder

## Directions

1. Fry butter, garlic and chopped onion
2. When it begins to brown, add a little flour and incorporate a little milk
3. Add pepper powder, chopped brain and parsley and oysters with their broth
4. Fill the shells, sprinkle Parmesan cheese powder and bread powder on top and put in the oven for half an hour
