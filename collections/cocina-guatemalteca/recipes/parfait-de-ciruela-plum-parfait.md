## Ingredients

- 6 ounces plum pulp
- half a glass of cream
- 1 egg
- three-quarters ounce of sugar
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Pass the cooked plums through a strainer
2. Put the sugar with the plum over the fire until it forms a ball
3. Beat until cold
4. Beat the cream and mix it with the previous mixture, as well as the well-beaten egg and lemon juice
5. Put in a mold for four or five hours in ice
