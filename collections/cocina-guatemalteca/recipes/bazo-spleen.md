## Ingredients

- spleen
- salt
- tomatillo
- garlic
- oregano
- dried chile
- French bread
- butter

## Directions

1. After washing the spleen well, split it in half, salt it, place it in the oven or roast it on a grill
2. Then put it to cook in salted water
3. Grind some cooked or toasted tomatillo, one clove of garlic, a little oregano, a dried chile and a piece of soaked French bread
4. Dissolve this in the water in which the spleen cooked, fry it with a little butter and add the spleen cut into pieces of the desired size
5. Serve hot
