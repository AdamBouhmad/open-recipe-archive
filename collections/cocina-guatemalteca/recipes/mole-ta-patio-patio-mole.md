## Ingredients

- lard
- chile de pasa, twenty, seeded
- almonds, three ounces
- sesame seeds
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- French spice, well fried
- salt
- chicken, turkey, or pork, cooked in pieces

## Directions

1. Toast in lard twenty seeded red chiles
2. Fry three ounces of almonds, a little sesame, cloves, pepper, cinnamon, and well-fried French spice
3. Grind all of this, add salt to taste
4. Place in a pan with lard to fry
5. Add the previously cooked pieces of chicken, turkey, or pork
