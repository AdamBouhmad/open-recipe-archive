## Ingredients

- lentils
- water
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- chorizo
- ham
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils in water with salt
2. Prepare a fried mixture of chopped tomato and onion
3. Fry the lentils with this mixture, adding chorizo and cooked ham and a bit of flour dissolved in water
4. Let it boil a few times and serve hot
