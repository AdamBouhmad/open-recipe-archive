## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- lemon juice
- egg
- oil
- dried mulato chile or pasilla chile
- vinegar
- parsley
- celery
- hierbabuena (spearmint)
- cilantro
- onions
- garlic

## Directions

1. Slice the fish after cleaning it well.
2. Season with salt and lemon juice and let sit ten minutes.
3. Pass through water and then through beaten egg with salt.
4. Fry in oil.
5. Place the fried slices on a serving platter.
6. Toast dried mulato or pasilla chile, grind it, and dissolve in one cup of vinegar, one of oil, and a little water.
7. Chop parsley, celery, spearmint, cilantro, onions, and garlic.
8. Cover the fish with this mixture and place over low heat.
