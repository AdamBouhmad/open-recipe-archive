## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/3 cup milk
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper powder
- 1 cup asparagus tips
- egg whites
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Make a sauce with one tablespoon of butter and one-third of a cup of milk
2. Beat four egg yolks (keeping the whites separate); once the yolks are beaten, mix in the cold sauce with salt, a little pepper powder and one cup of asparagus tips
3. When everything is well mixed, fold in the beaten egg whites and pour into a pan that is already on the fire with hot lard or butter
