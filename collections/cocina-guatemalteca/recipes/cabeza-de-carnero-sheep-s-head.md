## Ingredients

- sheep head
- salt
- chickpeas
- leek bundle
- garlic head
- chayote
- cabbage
- carrot
- turnip
- kohlrabi
- potatoes
- ham piece
- chorizo piece
- French roll
- cumin

## Directions

1. Clean the head well and cut in half
2. Cook with salt, plenty of chickpeas, a bundle of leeks, a head of garlic, chayote, cabbage, carrot, turnip, kohlrabi, potatoes, a piece of ham, and a piece of chorizo
3. Grind a French roll with a little cumin and add to the pot
4. Use the broth for soups and serve the rest as a potage
