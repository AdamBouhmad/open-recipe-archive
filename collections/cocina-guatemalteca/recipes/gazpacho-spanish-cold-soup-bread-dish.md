## Ingredients

- slices of French bread
- garlic
- lard or butter
- belly strips, well cooked
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper powder
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry very thin slices of French bread
2. Spread with ground garlic
3. Grease a skillet with lard or butter
4. Place a layer of French bread, then a layer of very thin strips of cooked belly, and so on successively, making sure the last layer is French bread
5. Bathe with a little thin vinegar, salt, pepper powder, and oil
6. Cover and place on very low heat or put in the oven
