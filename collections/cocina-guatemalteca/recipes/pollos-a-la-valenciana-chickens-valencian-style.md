## Ingredients

- chickens
- oil or butter
- salt
- six to eight heads of onion
- two ounces of raw ham
- two cloves of garlic
- two black chorizos
- bay leaf
- saffron water
- water
- pimentón
- rice
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens well and fry them in oil or butter with salt
2. Add six to eight chopped heads of onion, two ounces of raw ham, two cloves of garlic and two chopped black chorizos, a bay leaf, a little saffron water and clear water until the chickens are covered
3. When cooked, remove the chickens and add a teaspoon of pimentón to the same broth
4. If there are two chickens, wash a pound of rice and fry it well, then add to the broth in which the chickens were cooked, cover so it comes out fluffy, ensuring the water only covers the rice; if there is more, remove some to prevent the rice from becoming mushy
5. Let boil fifteen minutes
6. Keep the chickens spread with butter in the oven
7. As soon as the rice is ready, place the chicken on the platter with the rice, ham and chorizo around it
