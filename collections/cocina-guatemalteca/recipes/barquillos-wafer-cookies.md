## Ingredients

- Half a liter of milk
- Half a pound of sugar
- One pound of sifted bread flour
- Two ounces of melted butter
- Half an ounce of vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Put half a liter of milk with half a pound of sugar in a bowl and beat until no granules remain
2. Add one pound of sifted bread flour and beat so it does not become lumpy
3. Add two ounces of melted butter
4. If too thick, add a little more milk and half an ounce of vanilla essence
5. Heat the wafer iron over charcoal fire
6. Pour in one large spoonful of batter; if the iron is large, three or four wafers can be made at once
7. Close the wafer iron carefully so they do not spread too much
8. When golden, roll them in a tube or stick made for this purpose
9. Remove and place in a location where they will not break
