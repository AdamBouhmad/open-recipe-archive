## Ingredients

- green beans
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- garlic clove
- one tablespoon ground breadcrumb powder
- Castilian peppercorns
- two cloves
- piece of cinnamon
- piece of achiote
- one cup hot water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the green beans and cook as before
2. Fry tomato, onion and one garlic clove
3. Once fried, add the green beans
4. Add one tablespoon of ground breadcrumb powder with some Castilian peppercorns, two cloves, a piece of cinnamon and a piece of achiote
5. Add one cup of hot water
6. Just before serving add one teaspoon of vinegar
