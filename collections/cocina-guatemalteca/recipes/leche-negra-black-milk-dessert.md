## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds sugar
- 1 pound zapote, strained
- 2 ounces pitted raisins
- 1 small glass sweet sherry wine
- 12 ounces sugar
- 3 glasses milk
- 2 ounces unpeeled toasted almonds, ground
- 0.25 ounce cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify 1.5 pounds of sugar and place over heat until it reaches proper point
2. Add strained zapote with pitted raisins and one small glass of sweet sherry wine
3. Cook over low heat for half an hour
4. In another vessel, combine 12 ounces sugar, 3 glasses milk, ground unpeeled toasted almonds with cinnamon
5. Mix everything well and place over fire
6. When it has boiled for a quarter hour, mix in the zapote and bring to proper consistency
