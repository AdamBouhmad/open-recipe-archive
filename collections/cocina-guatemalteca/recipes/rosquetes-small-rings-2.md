## Ingredients

- one-half pound of flour
- two eggs
- one-half teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of Royal
- sugar powder
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix one-half pound of flour with two eggs, one-half teaspoon of salt, and one teaspoon of Royal
2. Knead until very fine
3. Round with the palm of the hand and cut rings of desired size
4. Heat a small amount of lard and fry the rings
5. Remove and while still hot, dust with well-sifted sugar powder
