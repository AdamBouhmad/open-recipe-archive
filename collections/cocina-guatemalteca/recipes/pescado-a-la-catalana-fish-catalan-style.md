## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- lard
- oil
- three large onion heads, sliced thin
- one small bunch of parsley, finely chopped
- two large tomatoes, peeled and finely chopped
- three roasted and ground tomatoes
- one can of peppers, finely chopped
- one tablespoon of capers
- one dried chile, seeded (optional for heat)
- a little water
- pepper powder
- one teaspoon of vinegar
- salt to taste
- olives
- one pepper, sliced lengthwise

## Directions

1. After the fish is well cleaned, coat it in flour and fry it in sufficient hot lard.
2. If the fish is very large, cut it into pieces.
3. When well fried, remove it.
4. In a pan with sufficient oil, fry three large onion heads sliced very thin, one small bunch of finely chopped parsley, two large peeled and finely chopped tomatoes, three roasted and ground tomatoes, one can of finely chopped peppers, and one tablespoon of capers.
5. If desired for spice, grind one seeded dried chile and add it.
6. Fry everything well in oil.
7. Add a little water, making sure it is neither too thin nor too thick.
8. Add a little pepper powder, one teaspoon of vinegar and salt to taste.
9. Place the fish on a platter and coat well with this sauce.
10. Serve hot; it can be simmered for a few minutes in a baking dish or if desired, also placed in the oven.
11. When placing on the platter, be careful not to break it.
12. Pour the sauce over it and garnish with olives, capers and one pepper sliced lengthwise.
