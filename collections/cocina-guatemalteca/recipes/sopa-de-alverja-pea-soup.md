## Ingredients

- sufficient peas
- broth
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cook sufficient peas
2. When well cooked, grind and strain like a purée
3. Drop into broth that has been prepared beforehand
4. Add fried seasoning and a little pepper powder
5. Serve with fried bread
