## Ingredients

- pork tongue
- raw miltomate
- garlic clove
- cumin
- pepper
- dried chile
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the tongue and when it has browned, grind a little raw miltomate with a garlic clove, a little cumin, pepper, and a cooked dried chile
2. Dissolve in a cup of water and add to the tongue
3. Let it cook until done
