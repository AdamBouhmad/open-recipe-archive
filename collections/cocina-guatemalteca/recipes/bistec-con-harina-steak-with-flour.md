## Ingredients

- meat slices
- butter
- tomato
- parsley
- onion, chopped with tender stems
- one tablespoon flour
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the meat slices as before
2. In the same butter add tomato, parsley, chopped onion with tender stems
3. Once fried, add one tablespoon of flour, give it two turns and add water until the meat cooks
4. Let it boil for ten minutes
