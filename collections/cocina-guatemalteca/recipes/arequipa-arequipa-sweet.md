## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of sugar
- one and one-half ounces of well-sifted rice flour
- two ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- one liter of milk
- one-half liter of milk
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify one and one-half pounds of sugar with very little water
2. Lower the heat and add one and one-half ounces of well-sifted rice flour, two ounces of peeled and ground almonds in their dry state, and one liter of milk
3. Place all over fire, leaving the almonds aside
4. When it is about to reach the proper stage, add the almonds dissolved in one-half liter of milk
5. When it reaches the soft-ball stage (punto de ante), pour into a platter
6. Decorate with cinnamon
