## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of butter
- juice of 3 large lemons or juice of 3 oranges
- 6 tablespoons of sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- baked pastry crust or American pie dough
- 6 egg whites
- 3 tablespoons of powdered sugar
- zest of 3 fruits of whichever was used for the cream

## Directions

1. Beat 4 ounces of butter with the juice of 3 lemons or 3 oranges
2. Place over heat, adding one at a time 6 tablespoons of sugar and 6 egg yolks alternately, stirring constantly
3. Continue until thickened
4. Use a pre-baked pastry crust or pie dough mold
5. Pour the mixture into the crust
6. Beat the 6 egg whites as if for nougat with 3 tablespoons of powdered sugar and the zest of 3 fruits
7. Place lightly in the oven
