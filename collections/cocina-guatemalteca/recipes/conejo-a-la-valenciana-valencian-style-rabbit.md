## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- ham
- one glass sherry or red wine
- garlic cloves
- pepper powder
- bay leaves
- thyme
- two orange leaves
- mushrooms (if available)
- water

## Directions

1. After cleaning the rabbit well, lard it with ham
2. Once browned, add one glass of sherry or red wine, peeled garlic cloves, pepper powder, bay leaves, thyme, two orange leaves, and chopped mushrooms if available
3. Cover with water and let boil
4. When cooked, serve with the strained sauce
