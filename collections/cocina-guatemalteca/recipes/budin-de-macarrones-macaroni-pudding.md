## Ingredients

- one pound of macaroni
- salt
- drops of lemon
- half a pound of butter
- four ounces of cooked diced ham
- half a pound of parmesan cheese
- one glass of white wine
- one cup of cream
- six whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of macaroni in salted water with a few drops of lemon
2. Mix with half a pound of butter, four ounces of cooked diced ham, half a pound of parmesan cheese, one glass of white wine, one cup of cream, and six whole beaten eggs
3. Mix everything well and pour into a greased mold
4. Put in the oven for ten minutes
