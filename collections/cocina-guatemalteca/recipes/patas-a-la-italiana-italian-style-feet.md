## Ingredients

- Feet
- salt
- butter
- white bread, ground
- black pepper
- clove
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Cook the feet with salt
2. When they are well cooked, butter a platter and put a layer of ground white bread with pepper and clove
3. Place a layer of chopped feet on top, then a layer of grated Parmesan cheese, then another of feet and another of bread, alternating in this way, making sure the last layer is cheese
4. Sprinkle with a tablespoon of lard and butter and put in the oven for about ten minutes
