## Ingredients

- Cooked tongue
- Butter or lard
- Cooked potatoes
- Salt
- Peeled tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Broth from cooking the tongue
- Black pepper powder
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Slice the well-cooked tongue
2. Place in a serving dish with butter or lard
3. Layer with sliced cooked potatoes seasoned with salt
4. Make a sauce with peeled tomato, onion, garlic, and chopped parsley, adding some of the broth in which the tongue was cooked
5. Layer alternately with tongue and the sauce, ensuring the last layer is potatoes
6. Add black pepper powder, chopped olives, and chopped capers to the sauce
7. Place over two fires to bring to a boil two or three times
8. Serve hot
