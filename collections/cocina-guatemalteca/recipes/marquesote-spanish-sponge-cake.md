## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- four ounces of sugar
- six ounces of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form
2. When they are well set, mix in the egg yolks and the sugar, ground and sifted
3. Then add the sifted flour
4. Put in small greased molds and place in the oven
