## Ingredients

- Loin
- Ground garlic
- Pepper powder
- Clove
- Salt
- Ham
- Water
- Vinegar
- Onion
- Celery
- Butter
- Avocado
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Oregano powder
- Green chile
- Onion
- Oil
- Pickles
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Tenderize the loin and add ground garlic, pepper powder, clove, and salt
2. Place plenty of ham along the edge
3. Roll the loin up very well, tie it, and put to cook with water, vinegar, onion, celery, and butter
4. When cooked, serve with guacamole made as follows: chop the avocado very well, add salt, pepper powder, oregano powder, green chile, chopped onion, oil, and finely chopped pickles
5. Mix very well, add plenty of cheese on top, and serve
