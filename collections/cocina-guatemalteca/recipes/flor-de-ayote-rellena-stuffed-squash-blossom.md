## Ingredients

- cooked pork meat
- tomato
- onion
- breadcrumbs
- Castilian pepper
- vinegar
- salt
- egg
- hot lard
- fried tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Chop a little cooked pork meat
2. Fry with tomato and onion and add its dust of bread with Castilian pepper, a teaspoon of vinegar and salt
3. Fill the squash blossom with this mixture
4. Wrap in egg and fry in hot lard
5. Serve with fried tomato sauce
