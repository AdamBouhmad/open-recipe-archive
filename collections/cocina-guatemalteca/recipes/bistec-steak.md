## Ingredients

- good quality meat sliced
- butter
- orange juice or a little vinegar
- pepper powder
- onion sliced
- raw tomato
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make slices from good meat and fry in little butter after tenderizing, adding orange juice or a little vinegar and pepper powder
2. Have butter or buttter prepared in a pan
3. Some like it well cooked and others with only two turns
4. Serve with sliced onion, raw tomato and minced parsley
