## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Dried chile
- Tomates
- Onion
- Hierbabuena (mint)
- Sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Split the sweetbreads in half with the thick skin still on
2. Sprinkle with salt and a little vinegar and let sit for a quarter hour
3. Roast on a grill over very low heat
4. For the chirmol sauce: toast a dried chile in hot coals until golden and not burnt, remove the seeds, crush it dry with salt, then gradually add hot water so no skin remains
5. Once well dissolved, add two roasted and peeled tomatoes, a good amount of minced onion, hierbabuena, and sour orange juice
6. Serve the sweetbreads with the chirmol
