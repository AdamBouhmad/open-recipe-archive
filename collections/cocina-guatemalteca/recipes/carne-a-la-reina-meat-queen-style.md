## Ingredients

- onion, chopped
- tomato, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- meat, pierced with fork and coated with lard or butter
- bay leaves, two
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- white wine or vinegar, one cup

## Directions

1. Chop onion, tomato, and parsley
2. In a pot, place a layer of this mixture
3. On top place the meat pierced with a fork and coated with lard or butter
4. Add two bay leaves, pepper, clove, cinnamon, and one cup white wine or vinegar
5. Cook over low heat
6. Remove all spices before serving
