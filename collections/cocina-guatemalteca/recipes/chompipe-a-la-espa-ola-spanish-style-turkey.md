## Ingredients

- 1 chompipe (turkey), cleaned
- half butter and half lard
- 3 carrots, sliced lengthwise
- 4 bay leaves
- 6 to 8 onion heads, peeled and quartered
- a little Castilian pepper powder
- 1 cup of saffron water
- 1 tablespoon of flour, fried in the same butter where the turkey was fried
- 1 to 2 cups of water

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and cut into pieces of desired size.
2. Heat a pan with half butter and half lard.
3. When the turkey pieces begin to turn yellow, remove them and place in a pan.
4. Once all is well fried, add the carrots sliced lengthwise, bay leaves, peeled and quartered onion heads, Castilian pepper powder, saffron water, and flour fried in the same butter.
5. Add 1 to 2 cups of water until the meat is covered.
6. If the carrots are cooked before the turkey, remove them as well as the onions.
7. Serve hot.
