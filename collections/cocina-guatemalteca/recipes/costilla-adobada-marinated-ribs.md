## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Salt
- Sour orange juice
- Tomatillo
- Garlic cloves
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Castilian peppers
- Dried chile
- Achiote
- Vinegar
- Lard

## Directions

1. Season ribs with one ounce of salt and sour orange juice
2. Let sit overnight in this brine
3. The next day, grind a little tomatillo with two garlic cloves, oregano, cumin, Castilian peppers, a dried chile, and a small piece of achiote
4. Dilute with two tablespoons of vinegar and salt
5. Fry in a little lard
6. Rub the fried mixture on the ribs
7. Place in the sun for one or two days to eat roasted or in chimol
