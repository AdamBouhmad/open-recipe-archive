## Ingredients

- one and a half cups of brown sugar
- two large tablespoons of butter
- two cups of sifted flour
- four cooked potatoes, well mashed
- three tablespoons of cognac
- pieces of prune and walnuts
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Mix everything well
2. Pour into a well-greased dish
3. Put in the oven
