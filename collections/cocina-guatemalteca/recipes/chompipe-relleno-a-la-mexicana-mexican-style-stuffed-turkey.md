## Ingredients

- one cleaned turkey
- ten apples
- half pound of butter
- half pound of peeled almonds
- half pound of prunes
- half pound of raisins
- one glass of sherry wine
- a little ground bread
- Castilian pepper
- a small piece of nutmeg
- sugar (optional)
- two bay leaves
- two onion heads

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey well and debone it, leaving only the wings and legs
2. Peel ten apples and remove the seeds very well, cut them into small pieces and give them a light boil
3. Melt half a pound of butter and in this place the apples with half a pound of peeled and halved almonds, half a pound of prunes and half a pound of raisins, one glass of sherry wine, a little ground bread with Castilian pepper and a small piece of nutmeg, and if desired a little sugar
4. When well mixed, stuff the turkey, sew it closed with thick thread
5. Place in a pan, rub with butter and salt, place two bay leaves and two onion heads on it
6. Place in a gentle oven for three hours, basting with its juices so that it comes out juicy
