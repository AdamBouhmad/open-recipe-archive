## Ingredients

- plums, two and a half pounds
- sugar, two and a half pounds
- egg yolks, eight
- vanilla, one teaspoon
- milk, cooked, one liter
- gelatin sheets, thirty
- heavy cream, half glass
- egg whites, three, beaten

## Directions

1. Soak the plums in tea water the night before
2. The following day cook them in the same water until they become like a puree, leaving some whole for decoration
3. In a pot place the sugar with the vanilla and mix the yolks with a wooden spoon
4. Add cold cooked milk and gelatin sheets washed in cold water
5. Continue stirring in a water bath until it thickens
6. Remove and continue stirring a little until it is somewhat cold
7. Then add the plum puree, cream and beaten egg whites
8. Wet a mold with water, place the contents in it
9. Place the mold in ice until it hardens
10. Unmold and decorate with the plums, also with Chantilly cream
