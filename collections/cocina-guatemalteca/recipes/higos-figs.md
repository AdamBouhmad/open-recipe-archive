## Ingredients

- figs
- water
- sifted ash or bicarbonate of soda
- one and a half pounds of sugar clarified per twenty-five figs
- high-point honey

## Directions

1. Put in cold water and peel with a piece of glass or on a grater
2. Make three cuts down the middle
3. Blanch in a little water with about six tablespoons of sifted ash, or if not, in water with a teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda
4. When blanched, remove and wash very well in two or three waters
5. For twenty-five figs clarify one and a half pounds of sugar
6. When cold and strained, add the figs
7. Put on the fire until they heat through
8. If desired serve in syrup; if not, remove from the honey and when dry, coat with high-point honey
