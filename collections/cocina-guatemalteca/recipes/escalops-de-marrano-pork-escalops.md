## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- sweet bell pepper
- onion
- garlic
- vinegar
- avocados
- oil
- lettuce
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the pork loin well and season with salt, sweet bell pepper, onion, garlic and vinegar, covering the meat with this mixture.
2. Place in a hot oven or over high heat to brown.
3. Once cooled, slice into rounds.
4. Grind some avocados and mix with oil, vinegar, salt and finely chopped lettuce.
5. Place the salad on the plate, top with the meat and garnish with potato slices.
