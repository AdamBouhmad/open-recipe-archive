## Ingredients

- Pork ribs
- Carrot
- Güisquil
- Tender green beans
- Potatoes
- Peas
- Tomato
- Onion
- Chorizo
- Hard-boiled egg
- Capers
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Bread powder
- Pepper
- Clove
- Paper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook ribs as in the previous recipe
2. Chop carrot, güisquil, tender green beans, potatoes, and peas, all cooked
3. Fry with tomato, onion, small slices of chorizo, pieces of hard-boiled egg, capers, two tablespoons of vinegar, salt, bread powder mixed with pepper and clove
4. Place bone on paper as before
5. Add a spoonful of this vegetable mixture
6. Fold the paper and place on a platter in the oven as before
