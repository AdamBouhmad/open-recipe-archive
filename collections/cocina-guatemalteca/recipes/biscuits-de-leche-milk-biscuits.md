## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- salt
- four tablespoons Royal baking powder
- a pinch of bicarbonate of soda
- five tablespoons butter
- one cup curdled milk
- butter for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix the bicarbonate of soda with the butter until it resembles coarse breadcrumbs.
2. Place on a kneading board and knead for forty seconds.
3. Cut biscuits, rolling them out with a rolling pin, and place on a greased baking sheet.
4. Brush with butter and bake in the oven.
