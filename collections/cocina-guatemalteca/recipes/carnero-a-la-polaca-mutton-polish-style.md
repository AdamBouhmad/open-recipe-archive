## Ingredients

- mutton
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- bay leaves
- onion
- vinegar
- pepper powder
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Fry the meat cut into pieces with salt, garlic, parsley, two bay leaves, two onions divided, two cups of vinegar and pepper powder
2. When ready to serve, add one tablespoon of flour fried in butter
