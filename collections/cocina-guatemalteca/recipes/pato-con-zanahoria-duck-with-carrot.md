## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of butter
- duck
- salt
- four peeled and cut carrots
- one spoonful of flour fried in butter
- water
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Fry well-cleaned and cut duck in four spoonfuls of butter with salt
2. When it takes a golden color, add four peeled and cut carrots and one spoonful of flour fried in butter
3. Cover with water and a little pepper powder
4. Let cook and serve hot
