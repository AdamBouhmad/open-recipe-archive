## Ingredients

- One liter of milk
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Bring one liter of milk to a boil with half an ounce of cinnamon and twelve ounces of sugar
2. When it has boiled, remove from heat and let cool
3. Strain and put in the ice cream maker to set like the previous recipe
4. If desired, serve with a tablespoon of cinnamon snow on top
