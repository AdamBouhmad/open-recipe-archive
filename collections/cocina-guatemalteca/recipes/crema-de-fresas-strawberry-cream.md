## Ingredients

- heavy cream (0.5 liter)
- powdered sugar (2 cups)
- strawberries (0.25 pound, lightly mashed)
- strawberries for serving (halved)
- muslin strip (for covering)
- ice and salt (for freezing)

## Directions

1. Whip 0.5 liter of cream until stiff.
2. Add 2 cups of powdered sugar and 0.25 pound of lightly mashed strawberries.
3. Place everything in a covered mold with a strip of muslin to prevent ice from penetrating it.
4. Place the mold in a bowl with ice and salt for 4 hours.
5. To serve, unmold and fill the cavity with halved strawberries mixed with whipped cream.
6. Decorate with leaves and serve with biscuits.
