## Ingredients

- broth from a hen or plump chickens
- rice, well fried in oil or butter
- fried seasoning
- pepper powder
- saffron water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. In the broth from a hen or plump chickens, drop the rice that has been well fried in oil or butter
2. Remove the hen and leave the chicken pieces
3. Add fried seasoning, pepper powder, and saffron water
4. Just before serving, add a little vinegar
5. Cook over very low heat without stirring so that the rice comes out whole
6. Serve hot
