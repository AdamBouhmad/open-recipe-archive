## Ingredients

- beans
- salt
- one onion
- two spoonfuls of lard
- a small piece of dough, well ground
- a few Castilian peppers
- water
- hot pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Soak in water the night before
2. The next day place over fire with salt
3. When more than cooked, chop one onion (head and stalk)
4. Add two spoonfuls of lard
5. Add a small piece of dough well ground with a few Castilian peppers
6. Dissolve with a little water
7. Add to the beans seasoned with salt and a little hot pepper
8. Serve hot
