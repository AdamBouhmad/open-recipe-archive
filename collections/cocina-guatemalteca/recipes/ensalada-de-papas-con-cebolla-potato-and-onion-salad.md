## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- Three or four onion heads

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well and cook them with salt
2. Prepare in a bowl one cup of oil, one of vinegar, pepper powder and three or four onion heads sliced very thinly
3. Once the potatoes are cooked, peel them and slice them, trying to keep them as hot as possible
4. Mix everything well, cover it and let it rest a few minutes before serving immediately
