## Ingredients

- green beans
- water
- salt
- half cup water
- half cup vinegar
- pepper powder
- two tablespoons oil
- thin onion slices
- peeled and seeded tomato slices

## Directions

1. Cook the green beans as before, taking care to boil for only a quarter hour so they do not turn black
2. Place on the platter
3. Mix half a cup of water and half a cup of vinegar, pepper powder, salt and two tablespoons of oil
4. Bathe the green beans with this mixture
5. Add very thin onion slices, peeled and seeded tomato slices
