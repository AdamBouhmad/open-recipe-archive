## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- bicarbonate of soda
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- milk
- nutmeg powder
- Castilian pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the well-washed peas in boiling water with salt and a little bicarbonate of soda
2. Fry one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour in a pan
3. Once fried, gradually add milk until a thin sauce forms
4. Add a little nutmeg powder and Castilian pepper
5. Add the peas to the sauce and serve hot
