## Ingredients

- green guaque chile
- one garlic clove
- three roasted tomatoes
- oregano
- peppercorns
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the green guaque chile
2. once cooked, grind it with one garlic clove, three roasted tomatoes, a little oregano, and some peppercorns
3. dissolve this with a little vinegar, water and salt
4. add some capers
