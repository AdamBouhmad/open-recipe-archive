## Ingredients

- half a liter of milk
- 4 ounces of flour or cornstarch
- 4 ounces of butter
- juice from a can of asparagus tips
- 4 ounces of grated Parmesan cheese
- eggs (two per person)

## Directions

1. Make a white sauce with the milk, flour or cornstarch, and butter
2. Put some sauce on a tray or platter
3. Place asparagus tips on top
4. Cover with the remaining sauce and Parmesan cheese
5. Place whole or split eggs in the sauce
6. Brown in the oven or over two fires
