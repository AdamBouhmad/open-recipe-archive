## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water and salt
- Ham, cooked, sufficient amount
- Bread powder
- Pepper powder
- Salt
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook in water with salt.
2. Chop sufficient cooked ham.
3. Mix with bread powder, pepper powder, salt and lemon juice.
4. Fill the artichokes with this mixture, tie them and fry lightly.
