## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- six eggs
- a little salt
- half a teaspoon of vanilla
- one tablespoon of Royal baking powder

## Directions

1. Place half a pound of flour in a bowl
2. Add the eggs one by one, mixing well
3. Add salt, vanilla, and Royal baking powder and mix
