## Ingredients

- Four ounces of flour
- Two ounces of butter
- Two ounces of grated cheese
- Two eggs
- Salt
- Broth

## Directions

1. Mix four ounces of flour, two ounces of butter, two ounces of grated cheese and two eggs with a few grains of salt
2. Knead very well and stretch with a rolling pin
3. Dry some very thin small tortillas, roll them up and let them fall into a broth prepared as the others
