## Ingredients

- Good broth
- All kinds of vegetables
- Mushrooms or champignons
- Chopped parsley
- Pepper powder
- Thyme branch
- Clove
- Bay leaf
- Sausage slices

## Directions

1. Prepare a good broth as before and mince all kinds of vegetables
2. If there are mushrooms, mince them well, and if not use champignons
3. Add chopped parsley and pepper powder
4. Separately, cook a thyme branch, a little clove and a bay leaf
5. Strain this water well and add it to the soup
6. Place some slices of well-cleaned sausage in the tureen
7. Pour the soup and serve very hot
