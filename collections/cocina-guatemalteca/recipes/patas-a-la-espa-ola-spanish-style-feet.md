## Ingredients

- Feet
- half pound of chickpeas
- two heads of garlic
- two onions
- bunch of parsley
- mint
- piece of ham
- peppercorns
- three cloves
- piece of cinnamon
- two or three cumin seeds
- piece of ginger
- piece of nutmeg
- oil
- teaspoon of paprika powder
- broth
- slices of chorizo

## Directions

1. Cook the feet with about half a pound of chickpeas, two heads of garlic, two onions, a bunch of parsley, mint and a piece of ham
2. When everything is well cooked, grind some peppercorns, about three cloves, a piece of cinnamon, about two or three cumin seeds, a piece of ginger and a piece of nutmeg
3. Fry this in a little hot oil with a teaspoon of paprika powder
4. Add a little of the same broth and put in the feet with the chickpeas, slices of chorizo, the ham divided up, and serve hot
