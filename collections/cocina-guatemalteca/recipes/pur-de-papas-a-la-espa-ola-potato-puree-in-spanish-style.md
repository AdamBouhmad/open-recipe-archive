## Ingredients

- Cooked potatoes
- Butter, one spoon
- Meat juice, two spoons
- Cheese powder, two ounces
- Parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked potatoes
2. Fry in a spoon of butter, two spoons of meat juice and two ounces of cheese powder
3. Serve with parsley sprigs, giving it the desired shape
