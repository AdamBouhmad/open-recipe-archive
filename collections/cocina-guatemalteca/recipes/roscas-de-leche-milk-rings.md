## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 tablespoon of yeast
- 10 ounces of flour
- 8 egg yolks beaten
- 1.5 pounds of powdered sugar
- 1/8 ounce of cinnamon powder
- 0.5 glass of milk
- a little orange blossom water
- a small pinch of salt

## Directions

1. In the morning, put five eggs, one tablespoon of yeast, and ten ounces of flour in a bowl and mix very well
2. In the afternoon, when it has risen well, add eight well-beaten egg yolks, one and a half pounds of powdered sugar, one eighth of an ounce of cinnamon powder, half a glass of milk, a little orange blossom water and a small pinch of salt
3. Let it rest until four o'clock in the morning of the next day
4. Form the rings and place them in a hot oven
