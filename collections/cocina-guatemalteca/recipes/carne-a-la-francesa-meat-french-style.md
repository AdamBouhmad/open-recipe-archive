## Ingredients

- lomo, posta negra o badilla (loin, black brisket or shoulder cut)
- manteca (lard)
- nabos (turnips)
- zanahorias (carrots)
- perejil (parsley)
- polvo de pimienta (ground pepper)
- clavo (clove)
- canela (cinnamon)
- vino de Jerez (Jerez wine)
- agua (water)

## Directions

1. Fry loin, black brisket or shoulder cut in lard
2. Add two turnips, two carrots, chopped parsley, ground pepper, clove, cinnamon, a glass of Jerez wine and water
