## Ingredients

- 4 pasilla chiles
- 2 French peppers, halved
- miltomate
- 1 ounce of sesame seeds
- 1 ounce of pumpkin seeds
- lard
- 1 small piece of edible clove
- 1 small piece of cinnamon
- Castilian peppers
- bread
- water
- raw turkey pieces

## Directions

1. Toast the pasilla chiles in lard, along with French peppers, miltomate, sesame seeds, and pumpkin seeds.
2. Grind all this with the chiles, edible clove, cinnamon, and Castilian peppers.
3. Last, grind the bread so it does not stick to the stone.
4. Dissolve with a little water and fry with 2 to 3 tablespoons of lard.
5. Add raw turkey pieces and sufficient water to allow them to simmer.
6. Cook over even heat for three hours.
