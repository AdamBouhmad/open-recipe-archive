## Ingredients

- flour (1 pound)
- egg yolks (2)
- egg white (1)
- water (as needed)
- butter, melted (0.5 pound)

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 2 egg yolks, 1 egg white, and enough water to make a soft dough.
2. Sprinkle flour on a work table and roll out the dough with a rolling pin.
3. Have 0.5 pound of butter melted and ready.
4. Spread some of the melted butter on the dough and fold the four corners together.
5. Roll out again with the rolling pin and add more butter.
6. Repeat this operation 3 or 4 times total.
7. This pastry can be filled with many different things.
