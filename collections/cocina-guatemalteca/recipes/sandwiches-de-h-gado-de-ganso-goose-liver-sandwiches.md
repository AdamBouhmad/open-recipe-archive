## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- goose liver
- cheese (butter cheese, Swiss, or Flanders)
- French mustard (optional)

## Directions

1. Spread bread slices with butter
2. Cover with a layer of goose liver
3. Alternatively, use a thin slice of butter cheese, Swiss cheese, or Flanders cheese instead of butter
4. Or spread with French mustard if desired
