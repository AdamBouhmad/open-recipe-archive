## Ingredients

- meat
- lard, melted
- salt
- ham
- parsley
- garlic
- almond, fried in oil
- vinegar or lemon juice, a little
- pepper powder
- clove
- cinnamon
- raw egg
- water
- potato salad or tomato sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Take a piece of meat and slice it into thin rounds
2. Mash the slices very well and coat with melted lard and salt
3. Chop ham, parsley, garlic, and fried almond
4. Add a little vinegar or lemon juice, pepper powder, clove, and cinnamon
5. Mix this filling with a raw egg
6. Fill the meat slices with this mixture, roll them, and sew them closed
7. Fry in lard or oil until they take color
8. Cover with water and cook until done
9. Serve with potato salad or tomato sauce
