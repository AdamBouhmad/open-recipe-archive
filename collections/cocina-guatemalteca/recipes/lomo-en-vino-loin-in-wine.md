## Ingredients

- lomo (loin)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- sal (salt)
- manteca (lard)
- agua caliente (hot water)
- vino tinto (red wine)

## Directions

1. Clean and pound the loin
2. Keep in vinegar and salt overnight
3. The next day, fry in very hot lard
4. When golden, add a little hot water and after a moment the same quantity of vinegar
5. When consumed, add the same quantity of red wine
