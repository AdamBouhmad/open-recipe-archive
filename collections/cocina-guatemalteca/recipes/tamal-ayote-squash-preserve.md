## Ingredients

- coastal squash
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- a small teaspoon of lime lye
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the coastal squash very well
2. Place it in water for half an hour with a small teaspoon of lime lye
3. Clarify one and a half pounds of sugar
4. Add the squash cut as desired
5. Allow the syrup to reach a very high point and let the fruit dry
