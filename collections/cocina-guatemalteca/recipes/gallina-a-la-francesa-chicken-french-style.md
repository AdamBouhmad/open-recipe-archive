## Ingredients

- one chicken
- oil
- lard or butter
- ham
- tomato
- garlic
- parsley
- saffron water
- water
- pickled vegetables

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken well, brown it in oil, lard or butter
2. When it has taken on a golden color, remove it and chop ham very finely, tomato, peeled garlic and chopped parsley, and fry it in the oil left from the chicken
3. Add a little saffron water and water until the chicken is covered
4. When cooked, serve with pickled vegetables
