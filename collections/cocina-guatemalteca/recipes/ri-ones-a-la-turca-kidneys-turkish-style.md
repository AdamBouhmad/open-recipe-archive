## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- half liter clear broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- one beaten egg
- butter
- six to eight lamb kidneys
- pepper powder
- flour
- bacon fat
- one cup broth
- one glass sherry
- lemon drops

## Directions

1. Fry the rice and add half a liter of very clear broth, salt, and a little nutmeg
2. Cook over very low heat
3. When done, allow to cool
4. Add one beaten egg to a mold lined with butter and pour in the rice
5. Place in a moderate oven or in a bain-marie
6. Clean six to eight lamb kidneys, cut into pieces, and season with salt, pepper powder, and flour
7. Fry pieces of fat bacon in butter and add the kidneys
8. Add one cup of broth and one glass of sherry
9. Let boil for just a few minutes as they become tough easily
10. Place the rice on a plate and make a hollow in the center
11. Place the kidneys in the hollow, add a few drops of lemon, and serve hot
