## Ingredients

- apples
- apricot
- one pound of sugar
- two ounces of melted butter
- three eggs
- a little butter
- half a pound of flour
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Cook a little apple with apricot, strain and mix with one pound of sugar and two ounces of melted butter
2. Take a round pan, place a glass in the middle and arrange the fruit all around
3. Make a dough with three eggs, a little butter, half a pound of flour and a pinch of salt
4. Place in the oven and when browned, flip it over
5. Serve cold or hot, as desired
