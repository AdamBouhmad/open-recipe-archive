## Ingredients

- cabbage
- tomato
- onion
- 1 clove garlic
- hen broth
- cinnamon stick
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig parsley
- rice
- hen pieces
- 3 ounces shrimp
- pepper
- saffron water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Parboil some cabbage, and once parboiled, have the fried seasoning of tomato, onion and one clove of garlic ready
2. In this seasoning, add the cabbage and chicken broth that will have been cooked beforehand with a cinnamon stick, a bay leaf and a sprig of parsley
3. Add all of this to the rice, which should already be fried as in the previous recipes
4. Add the hen pieces and about three ounces of well-cleaned shrimp, with pepper powder and a little saffron water, cover the rice and let it reduce over very low heat
5. Serve with chopped parsley
