## Ingredients

- calf brains, cooked
- eggs (two)
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. After cooking the brains, let them cool and then dry with a very clean cloth
2. Beat two eggs, first the whites until well stiffened, then beat in the yolks
3. Add salt and a little flour
4. Wrap the brain slices in this mixture and fry in manteca
