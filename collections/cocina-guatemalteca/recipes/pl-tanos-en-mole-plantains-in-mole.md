## Ingredients

- Plantains
- Lard
- Half ounce of pepitoria
- Half ounce of sesame
- One guaque or dried chile
- Three sweet breads
- Two ripe tomatoes
- Five cloves
- Castilian peppers
- Cinnamon
- Achiote
- Water
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry the plantains cut in pieces in lard
2. Toast half ounce of pepitoria, half ounce of sesame, one guaque or dried chile, three sweet breads and two ripe tomatoes
3. Grind with about five cloves, some Castilian peppers, a piece of cinnamon and a piece of achiote
4. Mix well ground with water, fry in one tablespoon of lard, place the plantains in this without boiling too much so they don't look bad, adding salt and sugar to taste
