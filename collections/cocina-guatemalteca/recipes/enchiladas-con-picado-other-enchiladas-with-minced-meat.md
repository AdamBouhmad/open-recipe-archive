## Ingredients

- Pork meat, partially cooked
- Peeled tomato
- Chopped onion
- Pepper powder
- Half a teaspoon of vinegar
- Salt
- Chopped pickled vegetables
- Tortillas
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a minced mixture of partially cooked pork meat, fry it halfway with peeled and chopped tomato and onion, add pepper powder and half a teaspoon of vinegar, season with salt, add chopped pickled vegetables.
2. Cover half of the tortilla with this, fold it to wrap it in egg as in the previous recipe, serving it with the same sauce.
