## Ingredients

- Artichokes, well cleaned
- Salt
- Pork, partially cooked, ground
- Ham, cooked, in small pieces
- Manteca or mantequilla, one tablespoon
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic, peeled and chopped
- Hard-boiled egg yolks, chopped
- Pepper powder
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Saffron water, one teaspoon
- Bread powder, one teaspoon
- Oil, one tablespoon
- Bay leaves, two
- Castilian pepper
- Ham pieces

## Directions

1. Remove the top leaves from well-cleaned artichokes and cook with salt.
2. Once cooked, remove and let all water drain completely.
3. Dry with a cloth.
4. Have prepared a filling of partially cooked pork, cooked ham in small pieces.
5. Fry this in one tablespoon of manteca or mantequilla with tomato, onion and peeled and chopped garlic, chopped hard-boiled egg yolks, pepper powder, cinnamon and clove, one teaspoon of saffron water and one teaspoon of bread powder.
6. Fill the artichokes with this, tie with hemp twine, making sure the filling does not spill out.
7. Place in a cooking pot with one tablespoon of oil, salt, two bay leaves, some Castilian pepper and ham pieces.
8. Place in the oven and when serving, remove the spices.
