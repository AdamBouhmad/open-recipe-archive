## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 0.5 pound sugar
- 0.5 pound butter or lard
- 1 pound bread flour, sifted
- zest of 1 lemon or 1 sweet orange

## Directions

1. Beat 8 eggs, the whites like for nougat and the yolks with 0.5 pound of sugar and 0.5 pound of butter or lard
2. When well beaten, mix them together
3. Mix in 1 pound of well-sifted bread flour and the zest of 1 lemon or 1 sweet orange
4. Pour into a mold greased with lard and into the oven hot
