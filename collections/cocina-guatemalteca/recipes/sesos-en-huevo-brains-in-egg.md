## Ingredients

- brains
- eggs
- salt
- flour
- tomato
- onion
- water
- achiote or flour

## Directions

1. Cook the brains and let them cool, then dry them with a clean cloth.
2. Beat two eggs, first the whites until stiff, then mix in the yolks.
3. Add salt and a little flour to the beaten eggs.
4. Wrap the brain slices in the egg mixture and fry them in hot lard.
5. Fry a peeled and chopped tomato in a little lard, the same as the onion.
6. When fried, add water and breadcrumb powder with a bit of achiote or a little flour dissolved in water, making a thin sauce.
7. Season the sauce with salt and a little pepper.
8. Cover the brains with this sauce and serve hot.
