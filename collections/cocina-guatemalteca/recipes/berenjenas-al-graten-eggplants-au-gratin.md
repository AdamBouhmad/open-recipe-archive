## Ingredients

- eggplants
- salt
- lard
- bread crumbs
- milk
- butter
- chopped onion
- bacon in small pieces
- salt and pepper powder
- two eggs
- parmesan cheese powder

## Directions

1. Cut the eggplants into slices or lengthwise
2. Place them in brine and one hour before cooking brown them in lard
3. Soak a little bread crumb in milk and put it in butter with chopped onion and bacon in small pieces, salt and pepper powder, letting it fry for a while
4. Then add the eggplants sprinkling them with parmesan cheese powder
5. Beat two eggs, cover with this and place in the oven or over two fires
