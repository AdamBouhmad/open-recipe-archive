## Ingredients

- one chicken
- butter
- salt
- lemon juice
- parsley
- pepper powder
- bay leaves
- oil
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken well, place it on a spit and coat inside and out with melted butter and salt
2. Place near a very gentle fire
3. Prepare some melted lard or butter with lemon juice and bathe the chicken constantly with this, turning it at the same time
4. When it has taken on a medium color, place it in a serving dish with one tablespoon of butter, chopped parsley, pepper powder, salt and two bay leaves, the fat that has accumulated with garlic, one cup of oil and enough water to cover the chicken
5. Let it cook; when serving, remove all the spices and in the remaining broth, dissolve about two egg yolks which should give a light boil and with this cover the chicken
