## Ingredients

- one turkey
- ham
- pepper powder
- garlic
- salt
- onion
- butter
- breadcrumb powder
- two tablespoons of Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Kill the turkey as the others do, well washed
2. Stud it on all sides with ham, pepper powder, garlic, salt and onion, all chopped finely
3. Rub well on all sides with butter and salt
4. Place in a pan with breadcrumb powder and two tablespoons of Parmesan cheese on top
5. Put in the oven for three hours as the previous recipes, basting with its juices to prevent it from drying out
