## Ingredients

- Two plantains
- Cinnamon
- Refried beans
- Sugar
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cook two plantains in steam; when already fried grind with a piece of cinnamon
2. Make small tortillas of the desired size, fill with well-refried and sweetened beans
3. Fold the tortilla and fry in hot lard; serve with powdered sugar on top
