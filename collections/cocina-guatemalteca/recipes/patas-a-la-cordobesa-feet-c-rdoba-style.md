## Ingredients

- feet
- cloth
- flour
- beaten egg
- lard
- vinegar
- dry sherry
- sesame seeds, toasted
- Castilian pepper
- clove
- tomato
- cinnamon
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the feet.
2. Dry them very well with a clean cloth.
3. Coat with flour and then in beaten egg.
4. Fry in hot lard and then leave on very low heat.
5. In the same pan, leave a little lard, add a little vinegar and one large tablespoon of dry sherry.
6. Grind half an ounce of toasted sesame with about four Castilian peppercorns, two cloves and a tomato, with a little cinnamon.
7. Dissolve with a little water and bring to a boil with the rest.
8. Baste the feet with this sauce.
9. Serve hot.
