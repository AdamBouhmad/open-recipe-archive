## Ingredients

- Cleaned ducks
- 1/4 ounce of saltpeter
- 1 glass of water
- 1 tablespoon of salt
- Onion
- Garlic, crushed
- Lard or butter
- Broth
- Powder of pepper
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Onion
- Leeks
- 1 sprig of mint
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 1 glass of white wine

## Directions

1. After the ducks are well cleaned, prepare 1/4 ounce of saltpeter in a glass of water with a tablespoon of salt
2. Bathe the duck inside and out with this mixture
3. Place it in a dish with crushed onion and garlic
4. Leave it in this infusion overnight
5. The next day, remove it and fry it in lard or butter
6. When it takes color, add the rest of the infusion and a little broth, powder of pepper, thyme, laurel, onion, leeks, a sprig of mint, a sprig of parsley and a glass of white wine
7. Let it boil for about two hours
8. Place it on a platter and serve with the same sauce strained
