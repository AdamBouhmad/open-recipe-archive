## Ingredients

- peaches
- apricots
- plums
- pears
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, apricots, plums, and pears, remove the pits
2. Place in a jar alternating layers of fruit and white sugar until the jar is full
3. Place in a bain-marie for three hours
4. Allow to cool before removing the jars to prevent them from breaking
