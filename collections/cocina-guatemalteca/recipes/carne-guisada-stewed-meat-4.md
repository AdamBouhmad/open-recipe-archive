## Ingredients

- meat
- oil
- parsley
- hazelnuts, peeled, eight
- pepper
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- onion
- garlic, minced

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of meat in oil
2. When it takes color, add peeled and ground hazelnuts and pepper
3. Before adding this, add peeled and chopped tomato with onion and finely minced garlic
4. Cover with water and let it reduce
