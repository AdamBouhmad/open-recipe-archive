## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Manteca (lard), one tablespoon
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic, one clove
- Water, one cup
- Bread powder, ground
- Achiote (annatto) for color
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Flour, one teaspoon (optional), dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower as in previous recipes.
2. Fry in one tablespoon of manteca, tomato, onion and one clove of garlic.
3. Add the cauliflower with one cup of water, ground bread powder with achiote color and pepper.
4. If not using achiote, use one teaspoon of flour dissolved in water.
5. Serve hot.
