## Ingredients

- beef tail
- chicken
- fat hen or gallina gorda
- sheep head
- green peas
- green beans
- chayote (güisquil)
- carrots
- potatoes
- fried seasoning (recado frito)
- two tablespoons flour browned in butter
- chopped parsley
- pepper powder
- two or more cans of oysters

## Directions

1. Prepare a broth with beef tail, chicken or fat hen, and a sheep head
2. Boil all of this for three hours
3. Strain the broth and put it back on the fire
4. Add tender green peas, green beans, chayote, carrots and potatoes, all finely chopped
5. When cooked, add fried seasoning, two tablespoons of flour browned in butter, chopped parsley, pepper powder and two or more cans of oysters according to taste
