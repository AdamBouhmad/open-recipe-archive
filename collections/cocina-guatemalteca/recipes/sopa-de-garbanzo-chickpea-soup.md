## Ingredients

- beef broth
- chickpeas
- pepper powder
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Bring beef broth to a boil
2. Drop in the chickpea and let it boil for ten minutes
3. Remove and place in cold water over strong and steady heat
4. When well cooked, grind it
5. Add very good broth, pepper powder, and chopped parsley
6. Serve hot
