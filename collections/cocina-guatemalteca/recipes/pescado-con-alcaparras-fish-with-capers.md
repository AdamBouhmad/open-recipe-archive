## Ingredients

- onion
- oil
- chile de pasa (dried chile)
- lard
- oregano
- capers
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry onions cut into quarters in oil.
2. When fried, grind a seeded and soaked dried chile and two dried chiles toasted in lard with a little oregano and ten or twelve capers.
3. Dissolve with a little water and one tablespoon of vinegar.
