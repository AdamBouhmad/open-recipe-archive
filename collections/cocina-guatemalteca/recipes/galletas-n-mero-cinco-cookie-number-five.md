## Ingredients

- Half pound of flour
- Four ounces of sugar
- Three eggs
- Quarter of a glass of milk
- Half teaspoon of Royal
- One quarter ounce of cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Mix everything well by spoonfuls
2. Place on buttered baking sheets
3. Put in the oven
4. If desired, place a raisin in the middle of each
