## Ingredients

- oil
- two tender chicken pieces
- tomato, peeled and chopped
- four ounces of pork loin slices
- four ounces of fresh pork ribs, cut
- three sausages, cut
- one large jar of piquillo peppers
- one glass of water
- one-eighth ounce of saffron
- two pounds of rice
- snails
- tender peas
- potato slices
- carrot
- tender green beans, cut
- salt
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Put sufficient oil in a pan and add pieces of two tender chickens
2. When they take color, add peeled and chopped tomato, four ounces of pork loin slices, four ounces of fresh cut pork ribs, three cut sausages, and one large jar of piquillo peppers
3. Add one glass of water in which one-eighth ounce of saffron has been dissolved
4. When this has boiled for half an hour, add two pounds of rice that has been fried in three spoonfuls of oil until golden but not burned
5. Add snails, tender peas, potato slices, carrot, tender cut green beans, salt, and a little pepper powder
6. Cover with one liter of water
7. Cover and let cook on very gentle heat
