## Ingredients

- yuca
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Peel and clean the yuca well
2. Cook it with salt
3. When done, grind it
4. Mix in eggs like the potato cakes
5. Season with salt
6. Form the small cakes
7. Fry them in butter to serve dry
