## Ingredients

- Large or small potatoes
- Salt
- Cooked tomato
- Guaque or dry chile
- Garlic clove
- Oregano
- Achiote
- Small onion head, roasted
- French bread, soaked
- Water

## Directions

1. If potatoes are large, peel and cook them chopped; if small, peel and cook them whole with salt
2. Grind cooked tomato with a guaque or dry chile, a garlic clove, a little oregano, a piece of achiote, a roasted small onion head, and a piece of soaked French bread
3. Dissolve this mixture with a little water and fry it
4. Add the potatoes to this sauce and serve hot
