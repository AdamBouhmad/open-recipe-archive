## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- one tablespoon oil or butter
- three garlic cloves
- two Castilian peppers
- two cloves
- a small piece of fried French bread
- water
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. If fresh, cook the chopped asparagus with salt; if canned, simply chop it
2. Fry three garlic cloves in one tablespoon of oil or butter until golden, then remove
3. Add the asparagus
4. Grind the garlic with two Castilian peppers, two cloves, and a small piece of fried French bread
5. Dissolve this with half a cup of water and one tablespoon of vinegar
6. Add this mixture to the asparagus, let it come to a light boil, and serve
