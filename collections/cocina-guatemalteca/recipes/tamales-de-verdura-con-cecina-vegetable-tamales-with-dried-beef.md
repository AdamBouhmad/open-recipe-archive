## Ingredients

- carrot, chopped
- güisquil, chopped
- potatoes, chopped
- buxmayes, chopped
- pacayas, chopped
- green beans (ejotes), chopped
- roasted tomatoes
- two dried chile peppers (chiles de pasa)
- salt
- a small piece of achiote
- pieces of dried beef (cecina), more than parboiled
- cooked masa

## Directions

1. Chop carrot, güisquil, potatoes, buxmayes, pacayas, and green beans.
2. Grind roasted tomatoes with two dried chile peppers.
3. Season this with salt and grind in a small piece of achiote.
4. Mix with the vegetables and add pieces of well-parboiled dried beef.
5. Have some masa cooked as for red tamales ready.
6. Place one tablespoon of this masa on a corn husk, in the middle place a piece of dried beef with vegetables and sauce.
7. Wrap and cook like the others.
