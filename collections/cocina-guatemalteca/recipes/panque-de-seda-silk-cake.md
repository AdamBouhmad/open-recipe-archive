## Ingredients

- 0.5 pound butter
- 0.5 pound powdered sugar
- 10 egg yolks
- 0.5 pound sifted cornstarch
- 10 egg whites
- 1 small glass of rum
- 1 ounce pitted raisins

## Directions

1. Beat 0.5 pound of butter with 0.5 pound of powdered sugar and 10 egg yolks
2. When well beaten, add 0.5 pound of sifted cornstarch
3. When well worked, add the 10 egg whites, 1 small glass of rum, 1 ounce of pitted raisins
4. Place in a mold greased with lard and into the oven
