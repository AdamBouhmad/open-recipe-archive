## Ingredients

- chicken
- oil or butter
- white wine
- parsley
- salt
- pepper powder
- butter
- cheese powder

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken well and place in a dish to brown in oil or butter
2. When it takes on color, add a glass of white wine, a sprig of parsley, salt and pepper powder
3. After boiling fifteen minutes, place in a dish in the oven
4. Add butter and cover with cheese powder until it browns
