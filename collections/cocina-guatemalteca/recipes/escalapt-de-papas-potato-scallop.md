## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- cheese
- milk
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the potatoes and slice them.
2. Butter a pan, put a layer of potato and a layer of cheese cut into pieces and small pieces of butter, alternating layers with the last one being potato.
3. Cover with equal parts water and milk, add salt to taste, and bake in the oven.
