## Ingredients

- pig's head
- salt
- pepper powder
- dried thyme powder
- parsley, chopped
- bay leaf powder
- dry sherry
- lemon juice
- lemon zest
- nutmeg powder

## Directions

1. Cut the head in half.
2. Remove the brains and debone very carefully, keeping the skin very intact.
3. Chop all the meat.
4. Cut the ears carefully so as not to tear the skin.
5. Chop some sweetbreads.
6. Mix with pepper powder, dried thyme powder, chopped parsley, bay leaf powder and salt.
7. When well mixed, add a glass of dry sherry, the juice of a lemon, the grated zest and a little nutmeg powder.
8. When well mixed, place on the skin and sew it closed with thread.
