## Ingredients

- sour cream
- cucumber kvass
- beet greens from several beets
- 1 tablespoon of vinegar
- water
- several eggs
- 1 fresh cucumber
- 1 pickled cucumber
- cold roasted veal
- chives
- dill
- salt and pepper to taste
- ice

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of sour cream and cucumber kvass
2. Cook beet greens from several beets in water with 1 tablespoon of vinegar and chop
3. Add to the sour cream mixture and blend
4. Add several hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
5. Add 1 fresh and 1 pickled cucumber cut into cubes
6. Add some finely chopped cold roasted veal
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Finally, chop some chives and dill and add to the soup
9. Place on ice where it should stay until serving
10. Serve when thoroughly chilled
