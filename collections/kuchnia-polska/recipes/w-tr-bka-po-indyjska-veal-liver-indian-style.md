## Ingredients

- one spoonful of fat
- one onion, sliced
- one spoonful of chopped fresh parsley
- two or three spoonfuls of finely chopped smoked bacon
- veal liver
- flour mixed with butter
- half cup of boiling water
- juice of half a lemon
- one teaspoonful of curry powder
- one teaspoonful of finely chopped sorrel

## Directions

1. Brown one spoonful of fat on a pan, add one onion sliced, one spoonful of chopped fresh parsley and two or three spoonfuls of finely chopped smoked bacon.
2. Cut the veal liver into pieces the size of a finger.
3. When the fat on the pan begins to boil, add the liver to the pan and fry until well browned, turning frequently.
4. Remove the liver from the pan and place on a hot platter, keeping it warm in the oven.
5. Strain the remaining sauce through a sieve, rinse the pan with hot water and pour the strained sauce back in and place on heat.
6. Add one spoonful of flour mixed with one spoonful of butter and cook, stirring, until the sauce forms a smooth brown mass.
7. Then add half cup of boiling water, juice of half a lemon, one teaspoonful of curry powder and one teaspoonful of finely chopped sorrel.
