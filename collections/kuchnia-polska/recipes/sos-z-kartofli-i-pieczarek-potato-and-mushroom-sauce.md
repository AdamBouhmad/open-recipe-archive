## Ingredients

- several peeled potatoes
- broth or bouillon
- 1 large tablespoon dried mushrooms
- butter
- flour
- salt
- parsley or dill (optional when serving with meat)

## Directions

1. Slice or cut potatoes into strips and cover with broth or bouillon and cook
2. Scald and braise 1 large tablespoon dried mushrooms in butter
3. Add to the cooked potatoes
4. Season with a heaping teaspoon of butter mixed with a little flour, stir and pour over meat
5. When serving with meat, add some chopped parsley or dill
