## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of pork tenderloin or loin
- fat
- half pound of soft veal without bones and sinews
- water
- salt and pepper to taste
- one clove of garlic
- thick intestine

## Directions

1. Cut the pork tenderloin into fine pieces along with fat, first in strips, then into cubes.
2. Pass half pound of soft veal through a meat grinder.
3. Mix together with the chopped pork.
4. Add several tablespoons of water, salt and pepper to taste, and one clove of garlic, crushed in a mortar.
5. After thoroughly mixing, stuff the thick intestine with this mixture.
6. Let it rest for an hour.
7. Arrange the prepared sausage at the bottom of a pot and cover with enough water to barely cover it.
8. Cook on high heat, frequently turning, until all water evaporates, which should not take longer than twenty minutes.
9. After the water has evaporated, add a small amount of butter and place in a hot oven to brown.
