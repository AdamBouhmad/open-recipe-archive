## Ingredients

- a large piece of ribs
- salt
- pepper (for seasoning ribs)
- three tablespoons grated breadcrumbs or bread
- one onion finely minced
- one small apple minced
- one-half teaspoon salt (for stuffing)
- one-quarter teaspoon ground pepper
- one-quarter teaspoon powdered sage
- two tablespoons beef suet, preferably from the kidneys
- one-half quart water

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of ribs and wipe with salt and pepper
2. Break in the middle across the ribs
3. Prepare the stuffing: mix three tablespoons of grated breadcrumbs or bread, one finely minced onion, one small minced apple, one-half teaspoon salt, one-quarter teaspoon ground pepper, one-quarter teaspoon powdered sage, and two tablespoons of beef suet
4. Mix everything well
5. Place the stuffing on the inner side of the ribs
6. Roll the ribs and tie with twine or sew
7. Place in a baking pan, pour in one-half quart of water and roast in a moderately hot oven until well browned
8. Baste frequently during roasting
