## Ingredients

- leftover meat, poultry, or fish, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon cream
- lemon juice
- mustard, a little
- bread, rye or white

## Directions

1. Chop any leftover meat, poultry, or fish very finely
2. Add salt and pepper to taste
3. Mix in one tablespoon butter and one tablespoon cream
4. Mix well
5. Add lemon juice
6. Add a little mustard
7. Spread butter on rye or white bread
8. Spread the mixture on the bread
