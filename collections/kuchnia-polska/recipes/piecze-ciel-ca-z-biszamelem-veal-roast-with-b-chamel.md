## Ingredients

- veal roast
- béchamel sauce
- Parmesan cheese
- young butter
- flour
- sour cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Roast the meat as usual. When it is almost done, cut it deeply and interlard with béchamel sauce mixed with Parmesan.
2. Pour the same béchamel sauce over the top and place in the oven for five to ten minutes until it browns.
3. To prepare the béchamel: Take a quarter pound of young butter, bring to a boil with one tablespoon flour, mix in a half cup of sour cream, cook down to half.
4. Then season with three egg yolks beaten previously with one tablespoon of cold sour cream, and then add a quarter pound of grated Parmesan.
