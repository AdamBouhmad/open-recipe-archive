## Ingredients

- half quart grated roll
- one large cup hot milk
- four egg yolks
- salt to taste
- quarter pound bacon, finely chopped
- two egg whites
- one large cup wheat flour

## Directions

1. Pour one large cup of hot milk over half quart of grated roll and let sit for a few minutes
2. Add four egg yolks and season with salt to taste
3. Fry quarter pound of finely chopped bacon until crispy, cool it and add to the batter along with the cracklings
4. Add two beaten egg whites while gradually sprinkling in one large cup wheat flour
5. Mix carefully, turn onto a table and shape into dumplings the size of a walnut, dusting with flour so they don't stick
6. Cook in a large amount of water in a large pot
7. When they float to the surface and puff up well, remove with a slotted spoon onto a serving plate and drizzle with rendered bacon
