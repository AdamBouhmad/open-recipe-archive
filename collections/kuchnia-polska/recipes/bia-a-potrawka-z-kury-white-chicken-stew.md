## Ingredients

- chicken, cut into pieces
- water
- quarter pound of salted pork fat, not smoked
- tablespoon of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- fresh parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into about fifteen pieces and place in a pot.
2. Cover with enough water to barely cover the meat.
3. Add a quarter pound of salted pork fat (not smoked), cut into strips.
4. Cover tightly and braise on low heat until tender; the water should completely evaporate.
5. In a pan, melt a tablespoon of butter, add two tablespoons of flour and fry briefly, stirring with a spoon, being careful not to brown the flour.
6. Add one and a half to two cups of milk to the flour mixture and bring to a boil.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Pour over the meat arranged on a serving dish.
9. Finish by sprinkling chopped fresh parsley on top.
