## Ingredients

- 1 cup chicken meat, finely chopped or ground
- 1/4 cup blanched almonds
- sour cream
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Chop or grind very finely the chicken meat with the blanched almonds
2. Add sour cream and mix into a smooth mass
3. Spread on buttered bread and cover with another slice
4. Bread should be sliced very thinly
