## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 glass strong bouillon
- capers
- lemon juice
- 1 small glass white wine
- 1 teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
2. Dilute with 1 glass strong bouillon
3. Add capers
4. Press in lemon juice to taste
5. Add 1 small glass white wine and 1 teaspoon sugar
6. Boil and use
