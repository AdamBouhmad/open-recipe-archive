## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- four egg yolks
- half tablespoon clarified butter
- salt to taste
- half quart milk
- half quart water
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks with the butter and add salt
2. Add the flour and then the milk mixed with water
3. Make a thin batter that will pour
4. Finally beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into the batter
5. Heat a pan and grease it with a feather dipped in clarified butter or with a piece of lard rind
6. Pour enough batter onto the pan to cover it completely, working quickly
7. Tilt and rotate the pan so the batter spreads evenly, keeping the pan on high heat
8. When the pancake is cooked on one side, run a knife around the edge and flip it briefly to the other side
9. Place on a plate
