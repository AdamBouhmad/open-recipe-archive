## Ingredients

- one pound lean pork
- a pinch of powdered sage leaves
- one-half teaspoon salt
- black pepper
- flour for coating
- egg for coating
- fat for frying
- flour (for sauce)
- one-half cup milk
- salt and pepper (for sauce)

## Directions

1. Mince very finely one pound of lean pork
2. Add a pinch of powdered sage leaves, one-half teaspoon of salt and some ground black pepper
3. Mix into a smooth mass and form small oval patties
4. Fry on hot fat until well browned on both sides
5. For the sauce: to the fat in which the croquettes were fried, add one tablespoon of flour and mix well while holding the pan over the heat
6. Add one-half cup of milk, salt and pepper to taste
7. Pour this sauce over the croquettes
