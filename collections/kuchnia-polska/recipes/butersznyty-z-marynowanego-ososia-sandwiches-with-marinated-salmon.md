## Ingredients

- 1 cup marinated salmon
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- white bread

## Directions

1. Remove bones from the salmon
2. Cook with the milk
3. Add salt and pepper to taste
4. Rub together butter and flour into a smooth mass and thicken the simmering fish with this mixture
5. Toast several slices of white bread and spread with butter
6. Place the hot fish mixture on the toast
