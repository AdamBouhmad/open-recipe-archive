## Ingredients

- four eggs
- flour, about one pound

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs and knead them with as much flour as they will take, about one pound, and the dough should be as hard as possible.
2. Roll out as thinly as possible and leave on the work surface for half an hour or longer to dry.
3. Then roll it into a log and cut very thinly.
4. Scatter the cut pasta loosely on the work surface for at least an hour.
5. Shortly before serving the soup or broth, bring water to a boil in a pot, add some salt and drop in the pasta.
6. When it floats to the surface, it is a sign that it is cooked.
7. Pour it onto a colander, and when the water drains, put it in a bowl and pour broth over it.
8. It can also be cooked in broth and will be even better than cooked in water, but then the broth will not be as clear.
