## Ingredients

- one medium onion
- butter
- two cups marinated tomatoes or fresh tomatoes
- one teaspoon curry powder
- two pounds calf meat, cut into pieces
- salt and pepper to taste
- three and a half cups boiling water
- flour
- rice

## Directions

1. Cut one medium onion into slices and place in a pot with one tablespoon of butter and brown it
2. Add two cups of marinated tomatoes or the same amount of fresh ones, one teaspoon of curry powder and two pounds of calf meat cut into pieces
3. Add salt and pepper to taste
4. Pour three and a half cups of boiling water and place on fire
5. Cook covered on low heat for one and a half hours
6. Then thicken with one tablespoon of flour mixed with a little water
7. Cook rice until thick
8. Pour the ragout onto a large serving platter and arrange rice around it
