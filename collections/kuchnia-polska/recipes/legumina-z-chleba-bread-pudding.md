## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- peel of one lemon
- one quart of milk
- half a quart of grated bread
- two tablespoons of sugar
- butter for greasing
- two egg whites
- four tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks well and add to them the grated peel of one lemon.
2. Into one quart of milk sprinkle half a quart of grated bread, mix and add the beaten yolks and two tablespoons of sugar.
3. After mixing, pour into a tin mold greased with butter and place in a moderately hot oven.
4. Remove from the oven as soon as the mass thickens well, taking care not to keep it in the oven too long, as water will appear.
5. After removing from the oven, pile thick foam from two egg whites beaten with four tablespoons of sugar on top, put back into the oven and remove immediately when the whites brown on top.
