## Ingredients

- one large cauliflower
- two quarts salted water
- one egg yolk
- one quarter teaspoon salt
- three tablespoons oil
- one tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice
- one quarter teaspoon dry mustard

## Directions

1. Cook one large cauliflower in two quarts salted water until tender, which should take about half an hour or slightly longer
2. Drain water and place cauliflower in a cool place
3. When completely cool, break into small pieces and arrange nicely in the center of a large salad bowl
4. In a small bowl, beat one egg yolk, add one quarter teaspoon salt and mix; add drop by drop, while continuously stirring, three tablespoons oil and one tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice, and one quarter teaspoon dry mustard; mix until smooth
5. Pour this sauce over the cauliflower
6. Alternatively, French salad dressing or mayonnaise sauce may be used instead
