## Ingredients

- lamb back, or other lamb meat
- onion slices
- vinegar
- pepper
- English herbs
- bay leaves
- juniper berries
- salt
- lard
- butter
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. For this dish, the back of lamb works best, that is the upper part on both sides of the spine, although other parts of meat can also be used for this purpose.
2. Clean the meat of all veins and trim all fat so that only meat is visible everywhere.
3. Place the prepared meat in a flat vessel; cover entirely with onion slices, pour with vinegar boiled with pepper, English herbs, bay leaves and a little juniper berries and place in a cool cellar for three to four days.
4. Then remove from vinegar, rub slightly with juniper berries, salt, lard with thinly sliced lard and let rest for an hour.
5. Then place in a roasting pan, baste with butter at first with a little vinegar, then sprinkle with flour, and shortly before serving flavor generously with sour cream.
