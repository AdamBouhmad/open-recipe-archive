## Ingredients

- melons (cantaloupe)
- three pounds of sugar
- half a quart of white vinegar
- cinnamon sticks
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel melons (cantaloupe), remove seeds and pulp and cut into long pieces
2. For seven pounds of prepared melons, take three pounds of sugar and half a quart of white vinegar
3. Put vinegar with sugar on fire, add a few cinnamon sticks and a few cloves
4. Cook for ten or fifteen minutes
5. Place the sliced melons and cook until transparent, which will take a few hours
6. Fill jars and seal tightly
