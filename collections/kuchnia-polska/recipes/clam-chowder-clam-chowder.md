## Ingredients

- fifteen clams with shells
- one quart of boiling water
- one quarter pound of salted bacon
- four large potatoes
- three large onions
- salt and pepper to taste
- one quart of milk

## Directions

1. Thoroughly wash fifteen clams with their shells using a brush, then place in a pot, pour one quart of boiling water and cook until the shells open
2. Remove from heat, take the clams out of the shells and chop into small pieces
3. Chop finely one quarter pound of salted bacon, cut four large potatoes into thin slices and three large onions
4. Place the bacon in a separate pot and brown it slightly on low heat without browning much, then add the strained water in which the clams were cooked, the chopped potatoes, onion, salt and pepper to taste and cook on very low heat for one hour
5. Then add one quart of milk and the chopped clams, cook for another fifteen minutes and serve on the table
