## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- vegetables and roots for cooking
- gray sauce with raisins
- Italian pasta or potatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the beef tongue as usual with vegetables and roots
2. When completely tender, remove and peel the skin
3. Slice diagonally
4. Arrange on a platter
5. Pour with gray sauce with raisins
6. Surround with Italian pasta or potatoes
