## Ingredients

- One pound of fresh lean beef, chopped fine
- Two quarts of cold water
- Several celery stalks cut into half-inch pieces
- One onion, sliced
- Half cup of washed rice
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop fine one pound of fresh lean beef
2. Pour in two quarts of cold water and cook on very low heat for two hours
3. Cut several celery stalks into half-inch pieces
4. Add one sliced onion, half cup of washed rice, salt and pepper to taste
5. Cook another two hours; at this time there should be a good quart of soup (liquid only)
6. Add one cup of good milk and strain
7. Serve
8. A few tablespoons of cooked rice and also cream can be added at serving
