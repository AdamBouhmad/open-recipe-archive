## Ingredients

- ripe and perfectly healthy berries
- one quart syrup made from sugar and water (for each quart of juice)

## Directions

1. Select ripe and completely healthy berries, place in a piece of clean cloth or bag and crush
2. Press out all the juice into a prepared vessel
3. For each quart of this juice, take one quart of regular syrup made from sugar and water
4. Mix and cook on very low heat for one hour
5. Then pour into bottles and seal hermetically with corks
