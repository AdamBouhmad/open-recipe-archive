## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- quarter teaspoon salt
- six egg yolks
- one cup sugar
- juice and zest of one lemon
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Sift one cup of flour with quarter teaspoon of salt
2. Beat six egg yolks, add one cup of sugar, adding slowly, juice and grated zest of one lemon, and mix well
3. Beat the egg whites into very stiff foam and add as well
4. Add the flour, mix, place in a form and bake for about fifty minutes
5. Do not open the oven door for the first fifteen minutes
