## Ingredients

- 3 cups rhubarb stalks, chopped into small pieces
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- pie crust dough

## Directions

1. Line a pie plate with dough and sprinkle with sugar mixed with flour, using half of the amount
2. Place chopped rhubarb on top
3. Sprinkle with the remaining flour and sugar mixture
4. Scatter butter, broken into small pieces, on top
5. Cover with a second layer of rolled-out dough and seal the edges firmly
6. Bake in a moderately hot oven for approximately three quarters of an hour
