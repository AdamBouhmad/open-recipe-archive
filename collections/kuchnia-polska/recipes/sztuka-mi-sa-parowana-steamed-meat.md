## Ingredients

- 3 to 4 pounds middle cross-cut meat
- young bacon, thick pieces
- butter
- vegetables: parsley, carrots, celeriac, celery, cauliflower, potatoes, and other vegetables as available

## Directions

1. Take a piece of middle cross-cut meat, remove the bones, beat well, and lard it with thick pieces of young bacon.
2. Salt it and let it sit for 1/2 hour.
3. For 3 to 4 pounds of meat, place 1 tablespoon of butter in a pot, lay the meat on it.
4. On top sprinkle a large amount of finely shredded vegetables: parsley, carrots, celeriac, celery, cauliflower, potatoes and any other vegetables available.
5. Place 1/2 tablespoon of butter on top, cover, and place this pot in another pot with boiling water, adding water continuously as it evaporates.
6. Cook in this manner for 5 hours without disturbing the meat.
7. Then place on a platter and pour the sauce with vegetables over it; very little sauce should remain.
