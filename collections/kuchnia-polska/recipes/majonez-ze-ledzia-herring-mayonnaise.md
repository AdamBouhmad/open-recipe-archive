## Ingredients

- one large fat herring
- two or three large potatoes
- one egg yolk
- two tablespoons oil
- mustard
- salt
- mushrooms
- gherkins
- capers

## Directions

1. Soak one large fat herring in cold water for eighteen hours, then clean, remove skin and all bones and slice thinly
2. Cook two or three large potatoes, peel and slice thinly
3. Beat one egg yolk with two tablespoons of oil, a little mustard and salt
4. On a small plate, layer herring, then potatoes, alternating until a small cake is formed
5. Cover the entire cake with mayonnaise so it is completely covered
6. Decorate with mushrooms, gherkins, capers, etc.
7. Serve for breakfast
