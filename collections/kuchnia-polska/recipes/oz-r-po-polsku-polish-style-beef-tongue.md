## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- vegetables
- roots
- gray sauce
- raisins
- Italian pasta or potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook beef tongue as usual with vegetables and roots until completely tender
2. Remove, peel off the skin, and slice diagonally
3. Arrange on a platter and pour gray sauce with raisins over it
4. Arrange Italian pasta or potatoes around it
