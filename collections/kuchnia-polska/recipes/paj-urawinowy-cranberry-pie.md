## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups cranberries
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon flour
- lemon extract for flavoring
- 2 egg whites
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Carefully sort, wash and chop the cranberries
2. Add sugar, one egg, two tablespoons water, a teaspoon of flour and lemon extract for flavor, mix everything together
3. Bake with bottom crust only
4. Cover with meringue made from two egg whites beaten with a teaspoon of powdered sugar
