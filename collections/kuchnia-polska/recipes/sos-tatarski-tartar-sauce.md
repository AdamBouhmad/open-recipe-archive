## Ingredients

- one egg yolk
- one teaspoon French mustard
- salt
- ten hard-boiled eggs
- one pound olive oil
- one lemon
- or two tablespoons strong vinegar
- ten small pickles
- ten marinated mushrooms
- or capers and chives

## Directions

1. Mix one egg yolk with one teaspoon of French mustard and salt in a bowl.
2. Remove yolks from ten hard-boiled eggs, strain through a sieve, add to the mixed yolk and blend.
3. Take one pound of olive oil and pour slowly into the yolks, constantly stirring with a wooden spoon until it thickens.
4. Then squeeze juice from one lemon, or pour in about two tablespoons of strong vinegar.
5. Chop finely ten small pickles and ten marinated mushrooms, add to the sauce and mix.
6. Instead of mushrooms and pickles, capers and chives can be used; the sauce will be different but also good.
