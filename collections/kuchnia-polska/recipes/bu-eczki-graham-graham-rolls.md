## Ingredients

- 3 cups graham flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 well-beaten egg
- 0.5 cup sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- sweet milk as needed

## Directions

1. Mix 3 cups graham flour, 3 teaspoons baking powder, 1 tablespoon melted butter, 1 well-beaten egg, 0.5 cup sugar, and 0.5 teaspoon salt.
2. Add enough sweet milk to make fairly thin dough.
3. Pour into small round forms called 'gem pans' and bake in a hot oven.
