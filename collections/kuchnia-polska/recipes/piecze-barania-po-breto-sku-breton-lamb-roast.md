## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- garlic
- butter or oil
- salt
- pepper
- mixed vegetables (various cut vegetables)
- white beans, cooked sour

## Directions

1. Beat the lamb shoulder strongly and rub garlic around it. Brush with butter or oil, salt and pepper, and let it rest for an hour and a half.
2. After this time, place on a roasting pan, pour fat over it and put in a very hot oven to brown as quickly as possible, but so that the roast does not release its juices.
3. After browning, add various chopped vegetables, pour butter over and put in the oven. Bake only enough so that it does not cook completely inside, and it will be more juicy.
4. Serve with white beans cooked sour.
