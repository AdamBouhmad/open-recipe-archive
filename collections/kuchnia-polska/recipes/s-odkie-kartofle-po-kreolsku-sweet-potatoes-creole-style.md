## Ingredients

- six firm sweet potatoes
- salt
- butter
- one egg
- one cup grated bread crumbs or white bread

## Directions

1. Peel and cut six firm sweet potatoes in half lengthwise
2. Place in a deep baking form
3. Sprinkle half a teaspoon salt and add one tablespoon butter
4. Beat one egg and mix with one cup of grated bread crumbs or white bread
5. Cover the potatoes arranged in the form with this mixture
6. Bake in a moderately hot oven for one hour
