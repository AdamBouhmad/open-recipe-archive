## Ingredients

- six medium tart apples
- a heaping cup of sugar
- half a cup of water
- half a lemon sliced

## Directions

1. Peel and cut six medium tart apples into quarters
2. Bring a heaping cup of sugar and half a cup of water to boil, and when the sugar is completely dissolved, add the cut apples and half a lemon sliced into pieces
3. Cook until the apples are soft, being careful not to crush them, but the quarters should remain whole
