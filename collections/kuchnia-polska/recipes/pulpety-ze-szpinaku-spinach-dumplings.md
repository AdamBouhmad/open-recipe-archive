## Ingredients

- some spinach
- grated roll in equal amount to the spinach
- salt to taste
- a pinch of regular pepper and red pepper
- egg white

## Directions

1. Cook some spinach in salted water, chop it very finely and add the same amount of grated roll
2. Season well with salt and add a pinch of regular pepper and red pepper
3. Add enough egg white to dampen the mass well, then let it stand for fifteen minutes
4. Make balls the size of a hazelnut and cook them in soup
5. Cook for five minutes
6. Serve three or four dumplings per soup bowl
