## Ingredients

- half cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- half cup of milk
- half teaspoon of salt
- two egg yolks
- two cups of flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- two egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream half cup of butter with one cup of sugar
2. Add half cup of milk and half teaspoon of salt
3. Add two egg yolks
4. Mix in two cups of flour with two teaspoons of baking powder sifted together
5. Mix well and then add the egg whites beaten to foam, mixing only lightly
6. Divide the batter in half and bake in a moderately hot oven on two plates
7. After removing from the oven, spread some filling on one layer, place the other layer on top, and frost the entire cake
