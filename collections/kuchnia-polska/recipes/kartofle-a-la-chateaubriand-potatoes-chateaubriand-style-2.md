## Ingredients

- 1 quart young potatoes, no larger than a walnut
- 3 ounces butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrub a quart of young potatoes, which should not be larger than a walnut.
2. Pour into a pan, add 3 ounces of butter and braise covered on low heat until soft, which will take about twenty minutes.
3. Shake the pan from time to time so they do not burn.
4. Arrange on a serving dish, sprinkle with salt and chopped fresh dill and serve.
