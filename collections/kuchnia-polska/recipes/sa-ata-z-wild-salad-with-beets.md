## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- red beets
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- or mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Wash lettuce leaves and dry them, place in a salad bowl
2. Cook a couple of red beets, peel and slice them
3. Cut the slices into pretty stars, hearts and similar shapes
4. Arrange in the salad bowl so that the beets are in the middle and lettuce around them
5. Pour oil with vinegar and pepper, or mayonnaise sauce over everything
