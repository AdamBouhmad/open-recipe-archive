## Ingredients

- Yellow part of orange peel
- 3 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 egg white
- 1 small cup sugar

## Directions

1. Rub the yellow part of orange peel
2. Pour over three teaspoons of lemon juice and let sit for half an hour
3. Strain through cloth and squeeze out juice
4. For every teaspoon of juice obtained, take one egg white and one small cup of sugar
5. Mix everything well and beat strongly for five minutes
6. Spread on cake with a knife dipped in warm water
