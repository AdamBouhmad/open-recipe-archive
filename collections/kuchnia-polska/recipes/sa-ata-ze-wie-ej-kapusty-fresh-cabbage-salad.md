## Ingredients

- white or red cabbage
- boiling water
- vinegar
- oil
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Shred white or red cabbage as finely as possible
2. Plunge into boiling water, and as soon as it boils, immediately drain the water and squeeze the cabbage
3. Place in a salad bowl and pour with vinegar; let sit half an hour
4. Drain the vinegar, then pour the cabbage with the following sauce: for one small head of cabbage take two tablespoons oil, one tablespoon vinegar and half tablespoon sugar
