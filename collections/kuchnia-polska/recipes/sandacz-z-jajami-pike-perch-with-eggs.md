## Ingredients

- sandacz (pike-perch)
- salt
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- onion
- eggs
- fresh parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the pike-perch, salt it and let it sit in salt for an hour.
2. Cook a stock from miscellaneous vegetables, onion, etc. and let it cool.
3. Remove the fish from salt, cut it into rings, place in a pan or dish, pour the cooled vegetable stock over it, cook on high heat until tender.
4. Carefully remove the fish and arrange on a platter, placing one ring next to another to form the whole fish.
5. Cook a couple of eggs hard, chop them, add plenty of chopped fresh parsley and toss onto melted butter, which should not be browned.
6. When it has warmed slightly, pour this sauce over the fish arranged on the platter.
