## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- milk or water
- 1 quart boiled water
- 5 or 6 ounces sugar
- 1/2 cup rice
- cinnamon (optional)
- sugar (optional)
- raisins (optional)
- ice

## Directions

1. Scald 1/2 pound sweet almonds and 5 bitter almonds, peel them of their skin, chop very finely, then pound in a mortar, moistening with milk or water so the oil does not release.
2. Pour 1 quart of boiled water into the almonds ground into a paste, mix well and strain through a fine sieve or cloth.
3. Pound the remaining pulp again, pour a little of the same liquid and strain through the sieve again.
4. Add 5 or 6 ounces of sugar and mix everything well together.
5. Separately cook 1/2 cup of rice in milk or water - it can be made with cinnamon, sugar and raisins - put in a bowl and pour the soup over it, then place on ice to cool.
6. Note: This soup can also be served hot.
