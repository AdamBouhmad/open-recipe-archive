## Ingredients

- four quarts of raspberries
- two quarts of currant juice
- sugar equal in weight to berries and juice combined

## Directions

1. Put four quarts of raspberries and two quarts of currant juice on fire and cook for three minutes
2. Add sugar equal in weight to the berries and juice and cook for an additional twenty minutes
3. Fill jars and seal tightly
