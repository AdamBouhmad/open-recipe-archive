## Ingredients

- beef kidneys
- water
- butter or fresh lard
- onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Parboil kidneys in water, then remove and devein
2. Slice the kidneys
3. Melt some butter or fresh lard in a pan, add the sliced kidneys
4. Sprinkle with chopped onion and braise over good heat, pouring water from time to time
5. When almost cooked through, add salt and continue braising until completely tender
6. Sprinkle with some grated bread, bring to a boil, and serve
