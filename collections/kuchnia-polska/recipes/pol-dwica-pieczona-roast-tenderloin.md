## Ingredients

- tenderloin
- lard for larding
- butter

## Directions

1. Lard and stuff the tenderloin with lard
2. Place it on a baking dish with hot butter
3. At first, brown it on strong heat over an open flame, and when it becomes browned, place it in a hot oven
4. Various vegetables can be served with the tenderloin, though small potatoes fried are the best
