## Ingredients

- three heaping tablespoons grated chocolate
- small amount of water
- one quart hot milk
- half cream

## Directions

1. Beat three heaping tablespoons of grated chocolate with a small amount of water and mix to a smooth paste
2. Add one quart of hot milk mixed half with cream
3. Cook in a double boiler for five minutes
4. Pour into cups and top with some foam from beaten egg whites
