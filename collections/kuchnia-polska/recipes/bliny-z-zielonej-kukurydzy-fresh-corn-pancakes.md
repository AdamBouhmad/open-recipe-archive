## Ingredients

- corn kernels from fresh corn cobs or canned corn
- two well-beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- fat or lard for the pan

## Directions

1. Scrape kernels from fresh corn cobs with a sharp knife, or use canned corn.
2. To a quarter of such a mass, add two well-beaten eggs, salt, and pepper.
3. Stir into smooth batter, place spoonfuls on a lard-greased pan, and fry until golden on low heat.
