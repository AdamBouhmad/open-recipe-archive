## Ingredients

- tripe, cleaned
- water
- beef broth
- vegetables
- butter
- celery and parsley, thinly chopped

## Directions

1. Carefully clean the tripe, cover with water and bring to a boil, discard the water
2. Add fresh water and cook the tripe until tender
3. After cooking, slice thinly like pasta and cover with warm broth previously cooked on beef
4. Add salt and vegetables and cook until well set
5. Separately braise thinly sliced celery and parsley in butter, and when tender add to the cooking tripe
6. Once the tripe thickens, remove from heat and cool
7. After cooling, take a wide shallow pot or stone jar or earthenware vessel and layer in the following order: a layer of tripe, a layer of fresh unsalted
