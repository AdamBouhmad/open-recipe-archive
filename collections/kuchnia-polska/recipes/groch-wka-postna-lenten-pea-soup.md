## Ingredients

- half quart of peas
- soft water
- pearl barley
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread sliced and fried in butter

## Directions

1. For four people, take half quart of peas and pour a quart of soft water over them
2. Bring to boil and then drain the water
3. Rinse the peas carefully and pour fresh hot water over them
4. Cook until the peas are completely soft
5. Strain through a sieve and add separately cooked pearl barley
6. Mix the barley with a spoonful of good butter, salt and pepper to taste
7. Line a bowl with bread slices fried in butter and pour the soup over them
