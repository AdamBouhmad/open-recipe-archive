## Ingredients

- broth made on beef bones with celery, onion, parsley, carrot, leeks and turnips
- one quart of lentils
- fat from the broth
- croutons

## Directions

1. Cook broth on beef bones with celery, onion, parsley, carrot, leeks and turnips
2. Dry lentils well on a baking sheet and brown them
3. Crush the lentils in a mortar, mix with fat collected from the broth into a smooth paste
4. Strain through a sieve and mix with broth
5. Serve to the table with croutons
