## Ingredients

- one spoon butter
- one spoon fat
- two egg yolks
- one whole egg
- one and a half spoon flour

## Directions

1. Beat one spoon butter and one spoon fat in a bowl until almost white.
2. Pour in two egg yolks one by one and continue beating until thick.
3. Then add one whole egg and stir constantly in one direction until thick.
4. Take one and a half spoon flour and add very slowly while beating.
5. When flour is well mixed, place dumplings with a spoon on broth, cover with a lid so they rise, then serve.
