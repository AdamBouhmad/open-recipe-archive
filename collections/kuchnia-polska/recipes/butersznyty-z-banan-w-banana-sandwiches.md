## Ingredients

- white bread, sliced very thinly, day-old
- mayonnaise sauce
- bananas, sliced lengthwise into thin slices

## Directions

1. Slice white bread very thinly (bread must be day-old)
2. Spread one slice thickly with mayonnaise sauce
3. Place a few thin lengthwise banana slices on top
4. Cover with another slice of day-old bread
