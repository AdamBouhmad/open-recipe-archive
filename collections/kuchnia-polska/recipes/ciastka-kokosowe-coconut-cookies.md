## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- three cups of sugar
- eight beaten eggs
- one pound of shredded coconut
- a little salt
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- two and a half cups of flour

## Directions

1. Make dough from all ingredients
2. Drop by teaspoonful onto a greased baking sheet
3. Bake
