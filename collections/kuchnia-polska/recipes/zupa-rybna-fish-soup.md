## Ingredients

- fish (carp, pike, or perch)
- vegetables (carrots, onions)
- butter
- dumplings (kluski krajane)
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook a large amount of chopped vegetables and onions
2. Place the fish on this base, taking care that it does not fall apart
3. Cook the fish for 15 to 25 minutes depending on size
4. When the fish feels soft when touched with a fork, remove it
5. Strain the soup, add a spoonful of butter and cook cut dumplings in the same broth
6. Before serving, thicken with sour cream
7. If the fish is large, you can cut off the head and place it in the pot with the vegetables for a more flavorful soup
