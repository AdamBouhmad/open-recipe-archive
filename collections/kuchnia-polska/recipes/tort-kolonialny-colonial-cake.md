## Ingredients

- one cup butter
- one and a half cups sugar
- three egg yolks
- one teaspoon vanilla extract
- a little nutmeg
- one cup flour
- one and a half teaspoons baking powder
- half cup milk
- three egg whites
- jam or preserves for filling
- one cup raisins without seeds
- quarter cup sweet almonds
- some grated coconut
- one egg white for the filling
- white icing made from sugar, butter, and milk

## Directions

1. Cream one cup butter, gradually add one and a half cups sugar
2. Add three well-beaten egg yolks and one teaspoon vanilla extract and a little nutmeg
3. Sift one cup flour with one and a half teaspoons baking powder and add to the butter
4. Simultaneously pour in half cup milk and mix well
5. Fold in stiff meringue made from three egg whites
6. Divide batter in half and bake two cakes
7. After removing from oven, spread one with jam or preserves
8. Prepare filling by finely chopping one cup raisins without seeds, quarter cup sweet almonds, and some grated coconut; mix with stiff meringue made from one egg white
9. Place filling on top of the first cake
10. Place second cake on top and cover with white icing made from sugar, butter, and milk
