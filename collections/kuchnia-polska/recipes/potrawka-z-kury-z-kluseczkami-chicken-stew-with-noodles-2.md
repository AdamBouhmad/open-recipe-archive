## Ingredients

- fat young chicken
- water
- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- flour dough for noodles
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as usual and place it in a pot so that it fits completely.
2. Pour enough water to cover the chicken and add mixed vegetables.
3. Cook covered on low heat for about two hours until tender.
4. Make noodles the day before, rolling them very thin and cutting them diagonally; dry them on a sieve.
5. Remove the chicken from the broth and cut into pieces.
6. Place the meat in the center of a platter, sprinkle with chopped green parsley.
7. Cook the noodles in the chicken broth without removing the fat.
8. Arrange the cooked noodles around the meat and pour the remaining broth over everything.
9. Note: Add enough water before cooking so that no additional water needs to be added during cooking. Remove the meat and strain the broth before returning it to the pot and adding the noodles.
