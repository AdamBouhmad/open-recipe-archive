## Ingredients

- one head of cabbage
- one tablespoon of butter
- one onion, sliced thin
- vegetable broth made from mixed vegetables
- salt and pepper to taste
- separately cooked rice
- one clove of garlic
- some grated cheese

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage finely and place in a pot with one tablespoon of butter and thinly sliced onion
2. Braise covered for about twenty minutes
3. Separately prepare a broth from mixed vegetables and use it to deglaze the cabbage
4. Add salt and pepper to taste and place on the fire
5. Add separately cooked rice, a clove of garlic, and some grated cheese
6. Bring to a boil and serve
