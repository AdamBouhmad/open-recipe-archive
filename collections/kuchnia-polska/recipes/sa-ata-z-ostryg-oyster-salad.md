## Ingredients

- half quart of oysters with their juice
- the same amount of chopped celery
- salt and pepper to taste
- mayonnaise sauce
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Pour half a quart of oysters with their juice into a pan, place on fire and cook until they plump up
2. Drain all the liquid and chop the oysters
3. Mix with the same amount of chopped celery, add salt and pepper to taste
4. Pour mayonnaise sauce over it and serve at table
5. Instead of celery, you can also use chopped lettuce
