## Ingredients

- beef bone
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1/2 cup rice soaked in water until softened
- 3 tablespoons oatmeal
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cover beef bone with 2 quarts of cold water and cook on low heat for 3 hours
2. Half an hour before serving, add 1/2 cup of rice soaked in water until softened, 3 tablespoons of oatmeal, salt and pepper to taste
3. Cook for another half hour and serve
