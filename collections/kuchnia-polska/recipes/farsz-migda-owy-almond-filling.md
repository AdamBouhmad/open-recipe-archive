## Ingredients

- two egg whites
- two cups of sugar
- one teaspoon of vanilla extract
- two cups of finely chopped blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Beat strongly two egg whites with two cups of sugar
2. Add one teaspoon of vanilla extract and two cups of finely chopped previously blanched almonds
3. Mix everything into a smooth mass
