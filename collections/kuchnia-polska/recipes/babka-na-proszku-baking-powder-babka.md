## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one cup sugar
- one cup flour
- one teaspoon vanilla extract
- one teaspoon baking powder
- half cup boiled milk

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs perfectly with one cup of sugar
2. Add one cup of flour, one teaspoon of vanilla extract, one teaspoon of baking powder, and finally half cup of boiled milk
3. Fill the form and bake in a moderately hot oven
