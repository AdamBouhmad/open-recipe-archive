## Ingredients

- one egg
- two heaping tablespoons sugar
- one large tablespoon butter
- one cup sour milk
- one cup corn meal
- one cup wheat flour
- one teaspoon soda
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Beat one egg with two heaping tablespoons of sugar and one large tablespoon of butter
2. Add one cup of sour milk, one cup of corn meal, one cup of wheat flour, one teaspoon of soda, and salt to taste
3. Mix well and bake
