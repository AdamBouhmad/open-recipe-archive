## Ingredients

- one gallon of cherries
- one quart of water
- two pounds of sugar per gallon of liquid

## Directions

1. Crush one gallon of cherries without damaging the pits
2. Add one quart of water and set in a cool place for a whole day
3. Then strain and add two pounds of sugar per gallon of liquid
4. Set again in a cool place for a day
5. Then pour into a barrel or jug, close the opening loosely and set in a cool place for fermentation, which should last from three to six months
6. When fermentation is complete, pour into bottles and cork
