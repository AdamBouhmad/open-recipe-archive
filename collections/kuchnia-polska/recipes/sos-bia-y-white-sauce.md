## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon raw butter
- broth or fish stock
- sour cream
- green dill, chopped

## Directions

1. Mix 1 tablespoon flour with 1 tablespoon raw butter until white
2. Dilute with broth or fish stock
3. Add sour cream and chopped green dill
4. Boil and pour over meat or fish
