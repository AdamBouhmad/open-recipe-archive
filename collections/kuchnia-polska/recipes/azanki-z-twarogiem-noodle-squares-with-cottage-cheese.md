## Ingredients

- half quart of flour
- one egg
- half pound or more of fresh cottage cheese
- three egg yolks
- one cup of sour cream
- one tablespoon of sugar
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Knead half quart of flour and one egg into stiff dough
2. Roll out thinly
3. Cut first into strips, then into square noodle squares
4. Scatter on work surface to dry slightly
5. Cook in salted water
6. Rub half pound or more of fresh cottage cheese with three egg yolks and one cup of sour cream and add one tablespoon of sugar
7. Grease a baking dish with butter and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
8. Place a layer of noodle squares on the bottom
9. Add cottage cheese, then noodles and cottage cheese again until all ingredients are used
10. Place in a hot oven for one hour
11. When well baked and browned, transfer to a serving platter and serve
