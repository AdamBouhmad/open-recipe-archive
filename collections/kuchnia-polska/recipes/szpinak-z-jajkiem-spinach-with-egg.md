## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- salt to taste
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Rinse spinach thoroughly in several waters.
2. Place in a pot and pour a large amount of salted boiling water over it.
3. Cook for twenty-five minutes.
4. Drain and squeeze out the water, chop the spinach very fine.
5. Return to the pot and add 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon vinegar and salt to taste.
6. Place on heat and stir; cook until a smooth mass is obtained.
7. Lay on a hot plate and garnish with hard-boiled eggs, cut into quarters or slices.
