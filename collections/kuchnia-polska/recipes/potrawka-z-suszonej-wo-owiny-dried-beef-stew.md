## Ingredients

- one half pound dried beef
- one teaspoon butter
- one teaspoon flour
- two small cups milk
- chopped fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the dried beef into thin slices
2. Tear into small pieces and chop finely
3. Melt one teaspoon of butter in a pan
4. Fry with one teaspoon of flour until the flour browns
5. Pour two small cups of milk onto the pan
6. Stir and bring to a boil
7. Add the chopped meat to the boiling milk
8. Stew on low heat until everything thickens slightly
9. Add chopped fresh parsley
10. Serve on the table
11. This stew is served for breakfast in American homes
