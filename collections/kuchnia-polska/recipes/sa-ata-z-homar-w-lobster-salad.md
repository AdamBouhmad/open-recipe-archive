## Ingredients

- one cup of lobster meat
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- a large head of lettuce
- half cup of mayonnaise sauce
- olives, capers, etc.

## Directions

1. Take one cup of lobster meat, break it into small pieces and pour oil with vinegar and pepper over it, as with chicken salad
2. Tear a large head of lettuce into small pieces an hour after preparing the lobster meat and mix together
3. Add half a cup of mayonnaise sauce, mix everything together and arrange on a salad bowl lined with whole lettuce leaves
4. You can garnish the top with olives, capers, etc.
5. Salads from other crustaceans are prepared in the same way
