## Ingredients

- medium-sized ham
- cold water
- cloves
- bay leaves
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash a medium-sized ham in cold water.
2. Place in a pot, cover with cold water.
3. Add six cloves and a couple of bay leaves.
4. Place over heat and cook fifteen minutes per pound of ham.
5. Cook over low heat and skim.
6. After cooking, remove from heat and let cool without removing from the water.
7. When completely cold, remove the skin from the ham.
8. Brush with egg.
9. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
10. Place on a roasting pan in a hot oven for fifteen to twenty minutes.
