## Ingredients

- Beef broth with vegetables
- White bread
- Parmesan or grated Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Cook broth in the usual way on beef with vegetables
2. Cut white bread into thin square slices
3. Moisten with broth
4. Place in a bowl, sprinkle with parmesan or grated Swiss cheese and put in the oven
5. When browned, remove from the oven, pour broth over it and serve
