## Ingredients

- herring
- one large onion
- butter
- one bread roll
- one egg yolk
- pepper to taste
- flour
- egg
- water

## Directions

1. Soak herring in cold water for twenty-four hours, clean, remove all bones and chop into a mass
2. For one pound of herring mass, peel and grate one large onion and fry it in a pan with butter
3. Soak one bread roll in milk and squeeze it out, add one egg yolk and pepper to taste
4. Place all ingredients into a bowl and mix until the mass becomes white and fluffy
5. Turn out onto a floured surface and shape into oblong cutlets
6. Dip the cutlets in egg beaten with water and fry in clarified butter
7. Serve with brown caper or gherkin sauce
