## Ingredients

- one cup cooked ham, finely chopped
- three eggs
- butter
- grated mustard, a little
- red pepper
- bread, rye or white

## Directions

1. Chop cooked ham very finely into a cup measure
2. Hard-boil three eggs for fifteen minutes
3. Chop the hard-boiled eggs finely
4. Mix the chopped eggs with the chopped ham
5. Add a little grated mustard and red pepper
6. Spread butter on bread slices
7. Spread the ham and egg mixture on the bread
8. Cover with another slice of bread
