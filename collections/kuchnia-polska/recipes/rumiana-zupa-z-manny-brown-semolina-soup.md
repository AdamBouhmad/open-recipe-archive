## Ingredients

- veal bone
- two quarts water
- sliced carrot
- onion
- several celery stalks
- parsley, some amount
- salt
- several peppercorns
- one egg
- a pinch of salt
- three tablespoons semolina
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Place a veal bone in two quarts of water, and when it comes to a boil, remove the scum and add sliced carrot, onion, several celery stalks, some parsley and salt, as well as several peppercorns.
2. Cook until it reduces to nearly half.
3. Break one egg with a pinch of salt and gradually add three tablespoons of semolina to it, continually beating until a thick dough forms.
4. In a pan dissolve one tablespoon of butter and place the semolina by the spoonful on it and fry: when it browns on one side, turn it over to the other.
5. When both sides have been browned in this way, add to the soup that has been removed from the heat and let it sit for fifteen minutes, then place on heat and cook for fifteen minutes, then serve.
