## Ingredients

- cauliflower heads
- salt
- water
- small amount of baking soda
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- brown butter sauce

## Directions

1. In a large pot, bring water to boil.
2. Add peeled and cleaned cauliflower and cook for a few minutes.
3. Pour off the water, add fresh boiling salted water and cook until tender, which should take about twenty minutes.
4. To ease cooking, add a small amount of baking soda and 1 teaspoon sugar to the water.
5. Lay on a serving platter and pour brown butter sauce over it.
