## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- chopped fresh parsley
- milk

## Directions

1. peel raw potatoes and cut into very small cubes
2. butter a baking dish and spread a layer of potatoes on the bottom, sprinkle with salt, pepper, chopped onion and chopped fresh parsley, and scatter some small pieces of butter
3. add a second similar layer and continue until the dish is full
4. pour milk over everything, though it should not completely cover the potatoes
5. place in a moderately hot oven for forty to fifty minutes
6. if the potatoes dry out too much during baking, add a little more milk
7. for a quart of cut potatoes use two tablespoons of butter, one teaspoon of salt, one tablespoon of chopped onion and half a tablespoon of parsley
