## Ingredients

- minced zrazy
- cabbage
- fresh lard slices
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare minced zrazy as described below and braise so they are half-cooked
2. Braise some cabbage separately, either fresh or sour
3. Coat each roll thickly with cabbage and then cover with slices of fresh lard so the entire roll is covered
4. Arrange the prepared rolls in a pan so they do not touch each other
5. Add a little broth and place in a hot oven without covering the pan
6. When the lard is well browned, the rolls are done
7. Transfer to a serving platter and pour some strong broth or meat stock over them
