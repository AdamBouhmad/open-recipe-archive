## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound box of sardines
- 2 eggs
- mustard
- 1 teaspoon grated horseradish
- white bread

## Directions

1. Remove sardines from the box, skin and bone them, and chop the meat finely
2. Hard boil two eggs and chop finely
3. Add mustard and grated horseradish
4. Mix with the sardines
5. Spread on thin slices of white bread, cover with another slice, and arrange on a platter or tray
