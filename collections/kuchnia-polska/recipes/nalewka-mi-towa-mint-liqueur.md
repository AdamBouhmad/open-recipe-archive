## Ingredients

- half a quart of thick syrup made with sugar in pieces and very little water
- one quart of strong spirits
- one-eighth ounce of mint oil

## Directions

1. Make half a quart of thick syrup using sugar in pieces and very little water
2. Pour one quart of strong spirits into the syrup and add one-eighth ounce of mint oil and mix well
