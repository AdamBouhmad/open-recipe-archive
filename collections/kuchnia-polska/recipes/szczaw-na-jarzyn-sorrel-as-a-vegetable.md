## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- butter
- sour cream
- flour
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and carefully rinse the sorrel leaves, cook in water and chop very finely.
2. Melt plenty of butter in a pan, add sorrel and braise briefly.
3. Beat thick sour cream with some flour and season the sorrel with it.
4. Add salt to taste and a little sugar if too sour.
5. Serve with veal cutlets or a piece of meat.
6. Alternatively, braise sorrel in butter raw without previous cooking.
