## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter
- two tablespoons grated cheese
- four to six eggs
- salt
- pepper
- one or two tablespoons cream
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Spread one tablespoon of butter on the bottom of a baking dish or cake form and on top sprinkle two tablespoons of grated cheese, spreading it evenly over the entire dish
2. Carefully break four to six eggs on top, taking care not to break the yolks
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and pour one or two tablespoons of cream, then sprinkle a fairly thick layer of grated cheese on top
4. Place in a slow oven for fifteen to twenty minutes and serve
