## Ingredients

- any cold roasted meat, finely chopped
- half quarter-weight of chopped meat in grated bread
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Chop finely any cold roasted meat
2. For each portion, use small cup of grated bread, salt and pepper to taste, mix well
3. Divide this mass into as many portions as there are people, place on small saucers
4. Into each break one egg, add half teaspoon butter and place in oven
5. When egg begins to set, sprinkle slightly with grated bread
6. Remove from oven and serve immediately
