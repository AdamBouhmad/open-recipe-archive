## Ingredients

- one cup of cream
- one tablespoon of flour
- one large tablespoon of butter
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- a few teaspoons of cream
- salt and red pepper
- about thirty oysters

## Directions

1. Steam one cup of cream in a double boiler
2. Mix one tablespoon of flour with one large tablespoon of butter and add to the cream
3. Rub three hard-boiled egg yolks to a smooth paste with a few teaspoons of cream and add to the sauce
4. Mix everything well, keeping it over heat, then add salt to taste and some red pepper
5. Cook about thirty oysters in their own liquid until the edges curl
6. Drain the oysters and add them to the sauce
7. Serve immediately on a platter
