## Ingredients

- one quart grated roll
- half quart hot milk
- three egg yolks
- half tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon chopped ham or cracklings
- salt to taste
- one roll, diced and fried in butter
- egg whites
- clarified butter
- eight ripe sweet pears
- sugar
- cinnamon
- anise
- one cup sour cream
- one tablespoon flour
- hot smoked meat

## Directions

1. Pour half quart of hot milk over one quart of grated roll and let sit until the roll softens
2. Add three egg yolks, half tablespoon butter, one tablespoon chopped ham or cracklings, salt to taste, one diced roll fried in butter and beaten egg whites
3. Mix everything and fry in clarified butter
4. Dust the table with flour, moisten hands in flour and shape the mixture into round dumplings slightly larger than a walnut
5. Cook in salted water
6. Take eight ripe sweet pears, peel and cut each in half, remove the seeds
7. Place in a pot and add just enough water to barely cover them
8. Add sugar, cinnamon and a pinch of anise and cook until soft
9. Thicken with one cup sour cream mixed with one tablespoon flour, bring to a boil
10. Pour this sauce over the dumplings arranged on a serving plate
11. Arrange slices of hot boiled smoked meat around them and serve
