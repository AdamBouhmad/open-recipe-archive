## Ingredients

- pike or zander
- mixed vegetables and roots
- butter
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook a stock from mixed vegetables and roots and cool
2. Clean the fish and cut into pieces
3. Cover with the stock and cook until tender
4. Remove the fish and make a white sauce from the remaining stock, adding butter, flour and a few tablespoons of sour cream
5. Alternatively, serve with parsley sauce poured over the arranged fish on a platter
