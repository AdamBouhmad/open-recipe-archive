## Ingredients

- young celery, finely chopped
- mayonnaise sauce
- butter
- bread, sliced very thinly

## Directions

1. Chop young celery finely
2. Mix with mayonnaise sauce
3. Spread butter on very thinly sliced bread
4. Spread the celery and mayonnaise mixture on the bread
5. Cover with another slice of bread
