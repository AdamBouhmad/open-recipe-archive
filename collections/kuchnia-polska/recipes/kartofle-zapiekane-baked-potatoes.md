## Ingredients

- several large potatoes
- water
- two tablespoons butter
- one and a half tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one cup hot milk
- two beaten eggs
- grated breadcrumbs or white bread

## Directions

1. Peel several large potatoes, cover with water in a pot and boil for half an hour
2. Drain the water and mash the potatoes to a smooth paste
3. For each quart of potatoes take two tablespoons of butter, one and a half tablespoons of finely chopped green parsley, salt and pepper to taste and mix well with the potatoes
4. Add one cup of hot milk, pouring slowly and stirring constantly
5. At the end add two well-beaten eggs
6. Grease a baking form with butter and sprinkle fairly thickly with grated breadcrumbs or white bread
7. Fill with the potato mixture and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour
8. After removing from the oven let stand a few minutes, then loosen around the edges with a knife and turn out onto a hot serving dish or plate
9. These potatoes look best baked in an oval form shaped like a serving dish
