## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- a few tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch fresh mushrooms, then slice them thinly
2. Blanch once more in cold water, place in a saucepan, add butter and braise under a cover, stirring frequently to prevent burning
3. When soft, salt to taste, pour in a few tablespoons of sour cream, braise a bit longer and serve at the table
