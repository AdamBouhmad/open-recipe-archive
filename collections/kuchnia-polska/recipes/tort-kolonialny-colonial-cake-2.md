## Ingredients

- one cup butter
- one and a half cups sugar
- three egg yolks
- one teaspoon vanilla extract
- pinch of nutmeg
- one cup flour
- one and a half teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat one cup of butter until creamy, add one and a half cups of sugar, adding slowly, then three well-beaten egg yolks and one teaspoon of vanilla extract, as well as a pinch of nutmeg
