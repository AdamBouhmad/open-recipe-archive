## Ingredients

- six eggs
- cold water
- lettuce leaves
- half cup vinegar
- two tablespoons grated bread
- one teaspoon mustard
- one teaspoon celery seed
- one tablespoon butter
- pepper
- or two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- one tablespoon finely chopped pickled cucumbers
- salt
- a little grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cook six eggs hard and plunge into cold water
2. When completely cool, peel and slice very thinly, best sliced lengthwise
3. Line a salad bowl with lettuce leaves and arrange the sliced eggs on top
4. For the first sauce option: mix together half cup vinegar, two tablespoons grated bread, one teaspoon mustard, one teaspoon celery seed, one tablespoon butter and some pepper
5. For the second sauce option: melt two tablespoons butter, add one tablespoon lemon juice, one tablespoon finely chopped pickled cucumbers, salt and pepper to taste and a little grated lemon zest; mix and pour
6. Pour one of the sauces over the eggs
