## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked meat left over from broth from the previous day
- 1/4 pound fresh beef suet from the kidney
- 1/4 pound grated roll
- one baked and finely chopped onion
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat and 1/4 pound fresh beef suet from the kidney very finely
2. Add 1/4 pound grated roll, one baked and finely chopped onion, salt and pepper to taste
3. Mix everything thoroughly in a bowl, then lay out on a board sprinkled with flour
4. Work this mass into round rolls, flatten them a little with your hand and place them in boiling water in a deep pan or shallow pot, and cook covered
5. After cooking, place on a colander and when they have drained from water, cut them diagonally with a knife into one-inch pieces
6. Place them in a bowl and pour borscht over them
