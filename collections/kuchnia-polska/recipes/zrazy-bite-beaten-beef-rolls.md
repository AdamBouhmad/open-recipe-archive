## Ingredients

- beef for zrazy
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- onion
- mushrooms
- water
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices and beat well
2. Season with salt and pepper, coat each in flour and place in a pan with hot butter
3. When browned, add onion cut into slices and some mushrooms
4. Add a little water and braise until the sauce thickens, then add one tablespoon of sour cream and serve
