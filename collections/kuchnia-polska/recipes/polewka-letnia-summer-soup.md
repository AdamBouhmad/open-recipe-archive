## Ingredients

- two large potatoes
- hot water
- two tomatoes
- one onion
- a handful of green corn kernels scraped with a knife
- two quarts of cold water
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- flour (optional, for thickening)

## Directions

1. Peel and slice two large potatoes
2. Pour hot water over them and boil for no more than ten minutes, then strain the water
3. Chop two tomatoes and one onion finely, add a handful of green corn kernels scraped with a knife
4. Place along with the potatoes in a pot and pour two quarts of cold water over them
5. Boil until everything is soft, then press through a sieve
6. Pour the soup back into the pot, add one tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste and serve
7. For a thicker soup, mix a small amount of flour with cold water and add to the pot
