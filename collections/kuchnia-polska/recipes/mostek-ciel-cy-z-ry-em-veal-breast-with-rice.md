## Ingredients

- veal breast
- rice
- veal broth
- butter
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Drain the meat and cover the rice with veal broth, add a tablespoon of butter and braise until the rice is soft.
2. Just before serving, place the breast in the rice and heat through.
3. Serve on the table arranging the breast in the center and rice around it.
4. Can be poured with bread roll browned in butter and grated.
