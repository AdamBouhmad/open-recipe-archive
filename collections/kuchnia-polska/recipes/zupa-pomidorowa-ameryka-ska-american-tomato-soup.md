## Ingredients

- one can of marinated tomatoes
- half a quart of liquid from the tomatoes
- one teaspoon of salt
- some pepper
- half a teaspoon of baking soda
- half a quart of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Pour the can of marinated tomatoes into a pot and cook until soft
2. Strain through a fine sieve so that seeds and skins remain on the sieve
3. To half a quart of this liquid add one teaspoon of salt, some pepper, and half a teaspoon of baking soda
4. Pour back into the pot and place on the stove
5. In another pot, bring half a quart of milk to a boil
6. Separately, melt one tablespoon of butter, add one tablespoon of flour, and fry for about three minutes
7. Pour this mixture slowly into the milk while constantly stirring
8. Bring to a boil and pour into the tomatoes
9. Strain the soup once more before serving, bring to a boil, and serve immediately
10. Can be served with croutons
