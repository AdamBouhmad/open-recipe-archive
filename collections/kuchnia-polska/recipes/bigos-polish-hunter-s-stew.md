## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage, shredded
- salt
- 2 white onions, minced
- a few sour apples
- fat cooked beef, sliced
- cold roast
- raw pork
- various meats
- butter
- broth or bouillon
- 1 tablespoon butter mixed with 1 tablespoon flour
- cooked sausage, sliced

## Directions

1. Shred the cabbage, salt it well, and press out the liquid firmly.
2. Mince the onions and peel and chop the sour apples.
3. Slice the cooked beef and prepare various other meats.
4. Mix the cabbage with the onion.
5. In a pan, layer as follows: butter on the bottom, then cabbage, apples, pork, other meats, then butter again, repeating until all ingredients are used.
6. Pour broth or bouillon over everything.
7. Cover and braise for two hours or longer over low heat, shaking the pan frequently to prevent sticking.
8. When the bigos is nearly done, thicken with a tablespoon of butter mixed with a tablespoon of flour, stir well, bring to a boil a few more times, and serve.
9. Traditionally, cooked sausage sliced into rounds is placed on top when serving.
10. Bigos tastes even better when reheated the next day.
11. Fresh cabbage can be replaced with sauerkraut, but the sauerkraut should first be covered with cold water and brought to a boil, then the water drained, and about a quarter pound of bacon cut into cubes placed on the bottom of the pan instead of butter.
