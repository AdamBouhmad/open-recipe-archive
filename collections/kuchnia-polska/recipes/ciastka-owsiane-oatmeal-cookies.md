## Ingredients

- two and a half cups of oatmeal
- one cup of sugar
- two eggs
- two tablespoons of melted butter
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- a little salt
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Mix oatmeal, sugar, two eggs, two tablespoons of melted butter, one teaspoon of baking powder, a little salt and vanilla extract into dough
2. Drop small spoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet two inches apart
3. Shape into pyramids with fingers
4. Bake
