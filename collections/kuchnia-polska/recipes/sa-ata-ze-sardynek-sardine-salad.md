## Ingredients

- one small can of sardines
- a large handful of lettuce
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- six tablespoons of oil
- two tablespoons of vinegar
- one small teaspoon of salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take one small can of sardines, remove the fish, rinse off the oil and carefully remove all the bones
2. Wash and tear a large handful of lettuce into small pieces, place in a salad bowl and mix with the sardines
3. Chop finely four hard-boiled egg yolks and also place in the salad bowl
4. Mix six tablespoons of oil, two tablespoons of vinegar, one small teaspoon of salt and some pepper together and pour this sauce over the salad
5. Mix everything together well and serve at table
