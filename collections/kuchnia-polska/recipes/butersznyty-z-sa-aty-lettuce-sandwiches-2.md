## Ingredients

- bread, sliced thinly
- butter
- small lettuce leaves
- one egg yolk, raw or hard-boiled
- vinegar
- olive oil
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Slice bread thinly and spread with butter
2. Wash small lettuce leaves carefully
3. Place lettuce on the bread
4. Pour sauce over the lettuce
5. Cover with another slice of bread
6. For the sauce: mash one egg yolk (raw or hard-boiled) with vinegar and olive oil
7. Add salt and a pinch of white pepper to the sauce
