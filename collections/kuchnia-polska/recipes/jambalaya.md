## Ingredients

- half pound of rice
- cold water
- half pound of sausage
- half pound of ham
- tablespoon of lard
- onion
- small red pepper pod
- several medium tomatoes
- several sprigs of parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Rinse half pound of rice and soak in cold water for one hour.
2. Cut half pound of sausage and half pound of ham into one-inch pieces.
3. Add to a pot with a tablespoon of lard, onion sliced into thin pieces, a small red pepper pod, several medium tomatoes, and several sprigs of parsley.
4. Fry for several minutes on low heat.
5. Add the rice, pour in half a quart of water, and braise on low heat under a cover for half an hour.
