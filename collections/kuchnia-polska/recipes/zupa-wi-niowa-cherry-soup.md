## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe cherries
- cinnamon stick
- a few cloves
- water
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- cold water
- ice
- small fried bread cubes (grzaneczki)
- sour cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Pit 1 quart of ripe cherries, put them with the pits in a pot, add a piece of cinnamon and a few cloves. Pour enough water to barely cover the cherries and cook for several minutes.
2. Strain through a colander and add 1/2 pound of sugar and mix.
3. Next, mix 1/2 tablespoon of flour with cold water, bring everything together to a boil and place on ice to cool.
4. When completely cooled, serve on the table with small fried bread cubes.
5. Note: Some also thicken this soup with sour cream.
