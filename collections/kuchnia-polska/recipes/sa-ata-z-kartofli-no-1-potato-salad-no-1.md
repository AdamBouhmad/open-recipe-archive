## Ingredients

- one quart potatoes
- one raw egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- one quarter pound oil
- vinegar
- finely chopped chives
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook one quart potatoes unpeeled, cool, peel and slice thinly
2. Thoroughly mix one raw egg yolk with salt and white pepper
3. Slowly add one quarter pound oil while continuously stirring; when the mixture thickens, add vinegar to taste
4. Add finely chopped chives and parsley
5. Pour this sauce over the potatoes in a salad bowl, mix gently and let sit undisturbed for several hours so the potatoes absorb the dressing, then serve
