## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked and mashed squash
- 0.75 cup sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 1.5 cups sweet milk
- 0.5 cake of compressed yeast

## Directions

1. Take 1 cup of cooked and mashed squash and add 0.75 cup sugar, 0.5 cup butter, 1.5 cups sweet milk, and 0.5 cake of compressed yeast.
2. Set to rise, then bake as usual.
3. To have these rolls for dinner, the dough should be started in the morning.
