## Ingredients

- half quart milk
- half quart water
- three or four heaping tablespoons chocolate
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix half quart milk with the same amount of water and bring to a boil
2. Grate three or four heaping tablespoons of chocolate, add to the boiling milk and cook for fifteen minutes, skimming off the foam that rises to the surface
3. Serve with sugar and cream
