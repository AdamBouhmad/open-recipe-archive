## Ingredients

- four cooking apples
- sugar
- one egg
- cup milk
- cup flour
- one teaspoon baking powder
- salt
- fat or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Peel and core four cooking apples to remove all seeds, then slice them crosswise into round slices and sprinkle lightly with sugar
2. Beat one egg, add cup of milk and cup of flour sifted with one teaspoon baking powder to mix well, and a pinch of salt; mix everything together and beat hard for about three minutes, then put the sliced apples into this batter
3. Heat plenty of lard or fat in a baking dish, and when the lard is very hot, take batter by large spoon with an apple slice and drop into the lard
4. Fry for about ten minutes until browned and serve hot
