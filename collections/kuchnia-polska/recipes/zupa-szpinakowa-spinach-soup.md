## Ingredients

- one pound of spinach
- water
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- one quart of any broth
- two egg yolks
- a few drops of lemon juice
- small croutons of white bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. Separate and carefully wash one pound of spinach, pour a small amount of water in a pot and cook until soft, then strain through a sieve
2. Mix one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour in a pot, add salt and pepper to taste, pour one quart of any broth and bring to a boil
3. When it boils, remove from heat and add to the soup two well-beaten egg yolks mixed with a few drops of lemon juice
4. Serve on the table with small croutons of white bread fried in butter
