## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- two egg whites for foam
- two additional egg whites
- flour
- butter
- milk
- water
- salt
- quarter pound almonds
- quarter pound sugar
- melted butter
- quarter pound chocolate
- regular sugar

## Directions

1. Make pancake batter as usual but using six egg yolks instead of four, and making foam from two egg whites instead of four, mixing with the batter and reserving the remaining two egg whites
2. Should make about twenty pancakes
3. After frying, cut pancakes first into strips, then into small cubes
4. Grate quarter pound almonds, add quarter pound sugar and a few tablespoons melted butter, mix with the chopped pancakes, then arrange on a large metal plate or platter
5. Grate quarter pound chocolate, beat the remaining four egg whites into stiff foam and mix with the chocolate, then pour this mixture over the arranged pancakes on the platter
6. Sprinkle with regular sugar on top and place in a hot oven but with heat only from above
7. After twenty minutes serve to the table
8. Instead of the almond mixture, may use baked apples pressed through a sieve; the apple mixture should then be beaten with sugar and arranged on the chopped pancakes, then baked in the oven as above
