## Ingredients

- a couple tablespoons chopped meat
- pepper
- salt
- one teaspoon finely chopped onion
- a bit of fat or butter
- six eggs
- toast from white bread

## Directions

1. Take a couple tablespoons of chopped meat seasoned with pepper and salt as for sausage, add one teaspoon of finely chopped onion and fry in a pan with a bit of fat or butter
2. Beat six eggs and add to the meat, place the pan on low heat and fry, constantly stirring, until the eggs are set
3. Place on a hot serving dish and serve on toast made from white bread
