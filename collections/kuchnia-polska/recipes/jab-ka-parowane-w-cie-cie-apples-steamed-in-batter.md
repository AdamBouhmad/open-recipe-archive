## Ingredients

- cooking apples
- flour
- baking powder
- lard or butter
- salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into hazelnut-sized pieces enough cooking apples to make a quart
2. Sift three cups flour with two teaspoons baking powder and then mix with two tablespoons lard or butter, add a bit of salt and enough cold water
