## Ingredients

- some rye flour
- warm water
- fresh beet greens from young beets
- dill
- chives
- salt to taste
- rye kvass
- sour cream and flour

## Directions

1. Take some rye flour, pour over with warm water and set in a warm place for forty-eight hours for fermentation
2. Cook fresh beet greens from young beets in fresh water with dill and chives, adding salt to taste
3. When everything is well cooked, take this cooked mass and dilute with rye kvass, thicken with sour cream and flour, and serve
