## Ingredients

- large piece of rib meat
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Rub the rolled meat with salt, pepper, and a little mustard
2. Place in a hot oven
3. During roasting, baste frequently with the drippings in the pan
4. One hour before it is cooked, surround the meat with peeled potatoes and roast together
5. The sauce can be thickened with flour or served as is
