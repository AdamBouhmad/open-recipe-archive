## Ingredients

- three eggs
- two and a half cups sweet milk
- two and a half cups white corn meal
- two teaspoons baking powder
- one teaspoon salt
- half cup wheat flour
- one tablespoon melted lard

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs well and add two and a half cups of sweet milk
2. Sift together to mix well two and a half cups of white corn meal with two teaspoons of baking powder, one teaspoon of salt, and half cup of wheat flour
3. Add one tablespoon of melted lard and mix
4. Pour the batter into a form and bake in a moderately hot oven
5. Melt the lard in the form in which it is to be baked
