## Ingredients

- quarter pound white bread
- butter
- flour
- water
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Break quarter pound white bread into small pieces and fry in a pan with a large spoonful of butter
2. Add a grated spoonful of flour and continue frying, constantly stirring, until the flour is well browned
3. Bring a quart of water to boil and pour gradually into the pan, stirring quickly
4. Then add half quart of milk
5. Salt and add pepper to taste
6. Just before serving, add two well beaten egg yolks
