## Ingredients

- good beef broth
- a small head of cabbage
- a few beets
- butter
- three or four small tomatoes (or a few larger ones)
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook good beef broth
2. Separately, prepare a small head of cabbage by first scalding it with boiling water and pouring off that water, then cook it along with a few beets
3. Cut the cabbage into quarters and the beets into thin slices and add to the broth
4. Braise three or four small tomatoes (or a few larger ones) in butter, press through a sieve to separate the seeds and skins, and add to the soup
5. Cook everything for half an hour
6. Before serving, pour a few spoons of good sour cream into the dish and pour the soup over it while stirring simultaneously so the cream blends with the soup; then take the meat from the broth, cut into small cubes and add to the dish
