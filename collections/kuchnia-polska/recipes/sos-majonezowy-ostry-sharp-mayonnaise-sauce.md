## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- half teaspoon salt
- a pinch of red pepper
- one teaspoon dry mustard
- half cup best olive oil
- three tablespoons vinegar
- half lemon

## Directions

1. Beat two egg yolks into a bowl; adding while mixing half teaspoon of salt, a pinch of red pepper and one teaspoon of dry mustard.
2. Mix well, then add half a cup of best olive oil, pouring slowly and constantly mixing.
3. Lastly pour in three tablespoons of vinegar and juice from half a lemon.
4. All these ingredients should be added one by one and well mixed each time.
5. This sauce is used mainly for salads.
