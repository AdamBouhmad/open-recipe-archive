## Ingredients

- various vegetables like pasta
- duck, cut into pieces
- broth
- sour apples
- two large onions
- butter
- flour
- caramel

## Directions

1. Shred various vegetables like pasta, then blanch with boiling water, drain this water, then add fresh water and bring to a boil, then add the duck cut into pieces and cook
2. When the meat is almost cooked, peel and slice several sour apples and two large onions, cover with broth from the duck and braise under a cover in a separate pan until the apples and onions soften
3. When the duck is completely tender, remove it from the pan with the broth, in that same pan brown one spoon of butter with one spoon of flour, mix with the broth, color with caramel; strain the cooked apples with onion through a sieve and add to this sauce, mix well and pour over the duck arranged on a platter
4. If the sauce is too sour, add a little sugar
