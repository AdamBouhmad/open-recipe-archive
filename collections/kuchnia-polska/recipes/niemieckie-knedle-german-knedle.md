## Ingredients

- quarter pound fresh lard
- couple of onions
- two rolls

## Directions

1. Cut a quarter pound fresh lard into small cubes, chop a couple of onions finely and fry together with the lard so the lard is only half cooked.
2. Chop two rolls finely, add to the lard, mix well and continue frying, stirring constantly.
