## Ingredients

- a piece of calf's liver
- a piece of bacon
- one large onion
- previously cooked broth
- a little flour and butter

## Directions

1. Clean a piece of calf's liver of membrane and chop finely, chop bacon into cubes and one large onion, place everything in a pot and stew for about twenty minutes covered
2. Remove and chop everything very finely, pour with previously cooked broth and cook for half an hour
3. Just before serving, fry a little flour with butter and thicken the soup with it
4. Serve to the table with croutons of rolls or white bread
