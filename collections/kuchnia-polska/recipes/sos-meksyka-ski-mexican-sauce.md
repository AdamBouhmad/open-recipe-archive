## Ingredients

- one large onion
- one tablespoon butter
- two tomatoes
- one pod red pepper
- one pod green pepper
- one clove garlic
- half teaspoon celery seed
- quarter teaspoon ground black pepper
- one teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Chop a large onion into thin slices and fry in one tablespoon butter until lightly browned.
2. Then add: two tomatoes peeled and chopped into thin slices, one pod of red pepper and one pod of green pepper, chopped finely with the seeds, and stew everything under cover until soft.
3. Then add one finely chopped clove of garlic, half teaspoon of celery seed, quarter teaspoon of ground black pepper and one teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce.
4. Mix and pour over meat.
5. This sauce is used for beefsteaks and other fried meat.
