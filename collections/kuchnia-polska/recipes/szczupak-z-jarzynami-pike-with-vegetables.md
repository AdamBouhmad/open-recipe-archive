## Ingredients

- parsley roots
- celery roots
- carrot
- large onion
- pike
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Take a couple of thick parsley roots, celery roots, one carrot, one large onion and chop everything very finely, like pasta
2. Scald with boiling water
3. Clean and cut the pike into rings
4. Put one tablespoon butter on the bottom of a saucepan
5. Layer chopped vegetables, then a layer of fish, sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Continue alternating vegetables and fish, finishing with a piece of butter on top
7. Cover tightly and stew on low heat, shaking the pan, until the fish is tender
8. Remove the fish carefully and arrange on a platter
9. Arrange the vegetables around it
