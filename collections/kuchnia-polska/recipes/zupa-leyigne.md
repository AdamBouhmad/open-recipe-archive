## Ingredients

- two carrots
- one onion
- salted water
- one and one-half quarts prepared broth
- half a cup cream
- four egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut two carrots and one onion into thin slices and cook in salted water.
2. Strain and add to one and one-half quarts of previously prepared broth, from which all the fat has been removed.
3. Whip half a cup of cream with four egg yolks.
4. Remove the soup from the heat, add salt and pepper to taste, then pour in the egg yolks, mix well and serve.
