## Ingredients

- small red beets
- horseradish, grated
- sugar
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Take small red beets and cook them until tender
2. Cool and peel them
3. Slice into thin slices
4. Layer the slices in a jar, sprinkling grated horseradish between layers
5. Add a little sugar
6. Finally pour light vinegar that has been boiled with salt and cooled over everything
7. Place in a cool location
8. Can be used after three days
