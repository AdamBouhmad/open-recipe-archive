## Ingredients

- chuck steak
- salt
- butter
- grated horseradish
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the chuck steak into cutlets and remove the thick bones, leaving only a small piece of rib on each
2. Beat lightly and shape into cutlet form, keeping the fat on them
3. Salt and place on a pan with hot butter
4. Start frying on high heat to quickly brown
5. After browning one side, flip to the other side and brown it equally, then finish frying on low heat
6. When serving, arrange the cutlets with grated horseradish and potatoes, preferably fried in the French style, or fried together with the cutlets in the pan
