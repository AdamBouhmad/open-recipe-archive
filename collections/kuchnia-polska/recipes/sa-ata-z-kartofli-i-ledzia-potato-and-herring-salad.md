## Ingredients

- potatoes in their skins
- water
- one good herring
- one medium onion
- two tablespoons oil
- one tablespoon strong vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in their skins as usual for potato salad, drain water and let cool
2. When completely cold, slice or dice
3. For one quart of diced potatoes, take one good herring, clean, wash thoroughly, peel off skin and bones and dice along with the milt
4. Grate on a grater or chop very finely one medium onion
5. Mix everything together and add two tablespoons oil, one tablespoon strong vinegar and pepper to taste
