## Ingredients

- one cup pickled tomatoes
- a pinch of baking soda
- one teaspoon butter
- three eggs
- salt
- pepper
- toast from white bread

## Directions

1. Take one cup of pickled tomatoes, add a pinch of baking soda and one teaspoon of butter, pour into a saucepan and bring to a boil
2. Then break three whole eggs into the tomatoes and cook briefly, stirring gently, until the eggs are set
3. Salt to taste, add a bit of pepper and serve at the table with toast, or arrange on toast made from large slices of white bread spread with butter
