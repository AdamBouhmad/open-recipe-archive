## Ingredients

- two pounds lean beef without bone, or two and a half to three pounds with bone
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- half cup carrots
- half cup turnips
- one medium onion
- two cups potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe meat with damp cloth and cut into one-inch cubes
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and small amount of flour
3. If meat has fat, trim it with knife and render in pan, then add meat and fry on strong heat until slightly browned
4. Place everything in pot, cover with quart of water and cook one and a half hours on low heat
5. Cut half cup carrots and half cup turnips into half-inch cubes, slice one medium onion and add to meat, cook another half hour
6. Cut two cups potatoes into quarter-inch thick slices and cook separately in salted water until halfway cooked, then add to stew fifteen minutes before serving
7. Thicken stew with two spoonfuls of flour mixed previously in cold water, or omit if you prefer thinner sauce
