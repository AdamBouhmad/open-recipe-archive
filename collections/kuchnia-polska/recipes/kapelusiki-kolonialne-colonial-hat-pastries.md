## Ingredients

- one cup of currants without seeds
- one citron
- one cup of sugar
- one beaten egg
- a pinch of salt
- flour
- lard
- cold water
- sweetened milk

## Directions

1. Chop finely one cup of currants without seeds; squeeze juice from one citron and grate the peel on a grater; add one cup of sugar, one beaten egg, a pinch of salt; mix everything well
2. Make a medium dough from flour, lard and cold water, roll out into thin circles like pasta, about six inches in diameter
3. Place a spoonful or more of the prepared currant mixture on each circle and fold the dough from three sides, giving it the shape of a triangular hat; seal the edges tightly
4. Brush on top with sweetened milk and place in the oven for fifteen to twenty minutes
