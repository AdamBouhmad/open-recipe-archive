## Ingredients

- half a pound of fruit preserves
- one quart of water
- a couple of pieces of cinnamon
- two tablespoons of sugar
- quarter cup of sour cream
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Pour half a pound of fruit preserves with one quart of water, add a couple of pieces of cinnamon and two tablespoons of sugar, and place on fire
2. When the preserves cook well, thicken with a quarter cup of sour cream mixed with a little flour
3. Strain through a sieve and serve to the table
4. As an addition to this soup, fried croutons from white bread or French dumplings are served
