## Ingredients

- pike or sandacz fish
- salt
- butter, melted
- lemon juice
- vegetables, various chopped
- pepper, a few grains
- flour
- egg yolks, a few beaten
- fish balls (from the Soup Additions section)

## Directions

1. Clean pike or sandacz, fillet, carefully remove all bones, and cut the meat into neat flat pieces about two inches long.
2. Salt the pieces and let them rest in salt for one hour.
3. After one hour, remove from salt, place on a baking sheet, pour generously with melted butter, sprinkle with lemon juice and place over low heat so it slowly fries without browning.
4. Cover the removed head, fins and bones with water, add miscellaneous chopped vegetables and cook well, then strain.
5. Add some butter in which the fish was stewed, enough flour to create a sauce of medium thickness, squeeze some lemon juice, add a few beaten egg yolks and mix everything well with the remainder of the sauce in which the fish was stewed.
6. Finally add several fish balls (see the Soup Additions section).
7. Arrange the fish on a platter and pour the sauce over it, placing a piece of heated French pastry on top.
8. To serve this dish more elegantly, the fish can be interspersed with stewed mushrooms, and with crayfish necks and claws.
