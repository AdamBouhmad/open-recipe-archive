## Ingredients

- lamb cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- vegetables
- white onion sauce à la Soubisse

## Directions

1. Debone the cutlets, leaving however some fat on them. Then beat each one, salt it, dust with flour and fry in browned butter on strong heat.
2. Serve with various vegetables, and the best sauce for lamb cutlets is white onion sauce à la Soubisse, which should be made thick and poured around the cutlets, not on them.
