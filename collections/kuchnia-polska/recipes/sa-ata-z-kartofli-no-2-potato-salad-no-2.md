## Ingredients

- one quart unpeeled potatoes
- water
- one quarter pound fatty smoked bacon
- one onion
- half cup hot water
- half cup hot vinegar
- one large teaspoon salt
- black pepper
- two tablespoons oil

## Directions

1. Cook one quart unpeeled potatoes, drain water and place briefly on hot stove to dry
2. Peel and cut into thin slices while hot
3. Cut one quarter pound fatty smoked bacon into small cubes and fry on low heat
4. Finely chop one onion and add half cup hot water, half cup hot vinegar and one large teaspoon salt; mix in a bowl and add some black pepper
5. When the bacon is well fried and the cracklings browned, add two tablespoons oil, mix and carefully pour into the bowl with vinegar and water, stirring gently to avoid splattering; mix together
6. Pour this over the potatoes arranged in a salad bowl, mix everything and serve immediately
