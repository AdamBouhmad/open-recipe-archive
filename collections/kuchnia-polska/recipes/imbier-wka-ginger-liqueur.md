## Ingredients

- one quart of boiled and cooled water
- one quart of strong spirits
- one-quarter pound of sugar
- one tablespoon of dried and finely chopped orange zest
- one-quarter pound of ground ginger
- burnt sugar for color

## Directions

1. Pour one quart of strong spirits into one quart of boiled and cooled water
2. Add one-quarter pound of sugar, one tablespoon of dried and finely chopped orange zest, and one-quarter pound of ground ginger; mix well and place in a cool place for two days
3. Then strain or filter, add burnt sugar for color, pour into bottles and cork
