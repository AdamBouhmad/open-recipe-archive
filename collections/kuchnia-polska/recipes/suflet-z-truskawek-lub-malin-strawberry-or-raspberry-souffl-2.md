## Ingredients

- half a pound of strawberries or raspberries, completely ripe and healthy
- half a pound of sugar
- two egg whites
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Press half a pound of strawberries or raspberries through a fine sieve, which must be completely ripe and healthy; sprinkle in half a pound of sugar, add two egg whites and beat in a cool place until white
2. Separately beat a stiff meringue from four egg whites and mix with the berry mass, arrange on a baking sheet, sprinkle with sugar and place in a very slow oven for half an hour; the oven must be only moderately warm, as in a hot oven the soufflé will fall and become a cake: this condition must be observed when making all soufflés
3. When the soufflé has risen well, remove and serve at table, giving sweet cream separately
4. Proportions for five persons
