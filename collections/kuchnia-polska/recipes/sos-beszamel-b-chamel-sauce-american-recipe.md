## Ingredients

- one and a half tablespoons of flour
- two tablespoons of butter
- one small cup of boiling water
- one bay leaf
- several sprigs of parsley
- one sprig of thyme
- one small piece of carrot
- half an onion
- a bit of nutmeg
- several peppercorns
- salt
- several tablespoons of broth
- one cup of sour cream

## Directions

1. Brown one and a half tablespoons of flour with two tablespoons of butter in a roux
2. Then pour in one small cup of boiling water
3. Tie together one bay leaf, several sprigs of parsley, and one sprig of thyme and place in the sauce
4. Also add one small piece of carrot, half an onion, a bit of nutmeg, and several peppercorns
5. Salt and pour in several tablespoons of broth
6. Cook everything over low heat for one hour
7. Then strain, add one cup of sour cream, boil, and serve
