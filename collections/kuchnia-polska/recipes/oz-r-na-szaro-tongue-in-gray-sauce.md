## Ingredients

- one cleaned beef tongue
- water
- various vegetables
- a few bay leaves
- about a dozen peppercorns
- one tablespoon sugar
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one half cup wine
- one tablespoon vinegar
- lemon zest
- blanched and sliced almonds

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned tongue in a pan
2. Cover with water
3. Add various vegetables, bay leaves, and peppercorns
4. Boil until tender
5. Immediately remove the skin
6. Slice diagonally
7. Moisten one tablespoon sugar with water and brown in a pan
8. Add one tablespoon butter
9. Add one tablespoon flour, previously cooked
10. Mix with the sugar and brown together
11. Distribute with broth from cooking the tongue
12. Add one half cup wine, one tablespoon vinegar, and some lemon zest
13. Strain and add blanched and sliced almonds
14. Pour this sauce over the tongue arranged in a pan
15. Bring to a boil and serve on the table
