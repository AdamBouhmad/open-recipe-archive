## Ingredients

- half quart buckwheat flour
- one spoonful wheat flour
- salt
- hot water
- fresh milk

## Directions

1. Take half quart buckwheat flour and one spoonful wheat flour, salt it
2. Scald in a bowl with several spoonfuls of hot water and stir until all flour is moistened
3. Bring a quart or more of fresh milk to boil in a pot
4. Quickly place oblong dumplings on a spoon onto the boiling milk
5. When they float to the surface, serve on table
