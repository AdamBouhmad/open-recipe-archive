## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Various vegetables and greens
- Water
- Flour
- Butter
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- Cauliflower and other vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken as usual and cut into quarters
2. Place in a pot, add various greens and vegetables, pour with water and boil until tender, which should take half an hour from the time it comes to a boil
3. When tender, work together one spoonful of flour with one spoonful of butter, dilute with broth from the chicken, add some finely chopped green parsley and boil until it thickens
4. Then add chopped cauliflower etc., warm together with the chicken and arrange on a serving platter
