## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- one cup of sugar
- two eggs
- four tablespoons of milk
- one cup of finely chopped peanuts
- one and a half cups of flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- one teaspoon of lemon juice
- split peanuts for topping

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add two eggs
3. Add four tablespoons of milk
4. Add one cup of finely chopped peanuts
5. Sift together one and a half cups of flour with two teaspoons of baking powder and mix in
6. Add one teaspoon of lemon juice
7. Make dough and drop by teaspoonful onto a greased baking sheet in the form of cookies
8. Place one split peanut on top of each cookie
9. Bake in the oven for twelve to fifteen minutes
