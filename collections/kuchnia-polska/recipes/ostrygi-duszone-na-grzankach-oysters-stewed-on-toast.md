## Ingredients

- half a quart of oysters with their liquid
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of grated bread
- half a teaspoon of salt
- a little pepper
- a pinch of red pepper
- one teaspoon of lemon juice
- thin slices of white bread
- butter for toast
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Pour half a quart of oysters with their liquid into a saucepan and place over heat
2. As soon as they come to a boil, add one tablespoon of butter, one tablespoon of grated bread, one half teaspoon of salt, a little pepper, a pinch of red pepper and one teaspoon of lemon juice
3. Mix everything and bring to a boil; when boiled for one minute, remove from heat
4. Make toast from thin slices of white bread, butter them and arrange the cooked oysters on top
5. Arrange on a platter, surrounding with lemon slices
