## Ingredients

- spinach leaves
- water
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter per 1/2 quart spinach
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup milk
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Sort, clean roots from spinach leaves and wash in several waters.
2. Place in a small pot, pour a small amount of water and cook until tender.
3. Drain on a colander; when water drains, chop very fine, season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. For every 1/2 quart of chopped spinach, take 1 tablespoon butter and 1/2 tablespoon flour.
5. Place in a pan and make a roux, not allowing it to brown.
6. When the flour is made into a smooth paste, add the spinach to the pan and fry for five minutes, stirring with a spoon.
7. Add 1/2 cup milk and cook for another three minutes.
8. Lay on a hot plate.
9. Cook a couple of eggs hard, cut in slices and garnish the spinach on top.
