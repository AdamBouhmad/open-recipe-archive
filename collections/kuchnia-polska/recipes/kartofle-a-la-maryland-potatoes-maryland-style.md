## Ingredients

- 6 medium potatoes
- cold salted water
- milk
- butter
- salt
- chopped fresh parsley
- flour (if needed)

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into thin slices six medium potatoes.
2. Cover with cold salted water and let sit for fifteen minutes.
3. Drain the potatoes well through a colander.
4. Place them in a small pan and add enough milk to cover the potatoes.
5. Place on low heat and cook covered until completely soft.
6. Add some butter, salt to taste and finely chopped fresh parsley and serve.
7. If the potatoes look watery after cooking, they can be thickened slightly with a bit of flour.
