## Ingredients

- two cups dried apples
- one cup molasses
- one cup sugar
- one cup butter
- one cup sweet milk
- one cup raisins
- one cup currants
- two eggs
- four cups flour
- one heaping teaspoon soda
- one teaspoon cinnamon
- one teaspoon mace
- one teaspoon nutmeg
- one teaspoon ginger

## Directions

1. Soak two cups dried apples in water overnight
2. The next morning, chop them finely and add one cup molasses
3. Cook for one hour, then add one cup sugar, one cup butter, one cup sweet milk, one cup raisins, one cup currants, and two eggs
4. Add four cups flour, one heaping teaspoon soda, one teaspoon cinnamon, one teaspoon mace, one teaspoon nutmeg, and one teaspoon ginger
