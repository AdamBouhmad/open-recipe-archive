## Ingredients

- a quarter pound of rice
- hot water
- cold water
- butter, about a quarter tablespoon

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over a quarter pound of rice in a pan and place on heat
2. As soon as it boils, immediately remove from heat and drain the water
3. Pour cold water over it, add a little butter (about a quarter tablespoon), and cook until the rice is soft
4. Stir frequently to prevent sticking
5. It is best to cook in a double boiler as there is no danger of sticking
6. When cooked, place in a bowl and pour soup over it
7. Whoever likes it can sprinkle grated Parmesan or other cheese on the rice
