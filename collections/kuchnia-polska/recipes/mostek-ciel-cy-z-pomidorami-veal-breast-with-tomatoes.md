## Ingredients

- veal breast
- marinated tomatoes
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After washing the veal breast, dry it with a damp cloth and place it in a roasting pan and put in a very hot oven to quickly brown the meat on top.
2. Remove from the oven, add salt and pepper and cover with a quart of marinated tomatoes, then put back in the oven until the meat is completely cooked.
3. Place on a platter along with the tomatoes and serve immediately on the table.
