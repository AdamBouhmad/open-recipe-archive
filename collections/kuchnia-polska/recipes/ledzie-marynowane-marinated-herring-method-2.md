## Ingredients

- herring
- water
- onion
- lemon
- herring roe
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak herring as above, clean and wipe dry
2. Arrange in a jar interspersed with sliced onion and lemon slices, peeled of skin and pith
3. Chop herring roe very finely, mix with oil and then dilute with vinegar
4. Pour this sauce over the arranged herring
