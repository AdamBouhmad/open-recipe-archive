## Ingredients

- one pound of chopped beef
- one small carrot
- one small turnip
- three small onions
- one cup of tomatoes
- a clean cloth
- one celery stalk
- a few sprigs of parsley
- a few cloves
- four peppercorns
- a little marjoram
- one half tablespoon of salt
- one half teaspoon of sugar
- a little ground pepper
- one and one quarter quarts of cold water

## Directions

1. Chop finely each of the vegetables: one small carrot, one small turnip, three small onions, and one cup of tomatoes
2. Tie together in a piece of clean cloth: one celery stalk, a few sprigs of parsley, a few cloves, four peppercorns and a little marjoram
3. Mix together on a saucer: one half tablespoon of salt, one half teaspoon of sugar and a little ground pepper
4. Take a clay pot with a lid, place meat at the bottom, then salt and pepper, then arrange the vegetables in layers on top, finally pour one and one quarter quarts of cold water
5. Cover tightly and place in a moderate oven to cook slowly for five hours
6. This soup is best prepared a couple of hours before dinner and reheated when serving
