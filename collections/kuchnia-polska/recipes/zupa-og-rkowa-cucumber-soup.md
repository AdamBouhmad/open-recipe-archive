## Ingredients

- three large pickled cucumbers
- flour
- butter
- mixed vegetable broth
- one quarter quart of sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel three large pickled cucumbers and slice them
2. Dust with flour and fry in a pot with butter
3. Separately prepare a broth from mixed vegetables available on hand
4. When the cucumbers are well browned, pour this broth over them and boil together for about half an hour
5. Strain the soup, add one quarter quart of sour cream and serve
