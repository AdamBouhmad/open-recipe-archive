## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound walnuts
- mayonnaise sauce
- butter
- bread
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Rub walnuts into a mass
2. Mix with mayonnaise sauce, stirring until a thick mass is obtained
3. Spread on lettuce leaves and place on buttered bread
