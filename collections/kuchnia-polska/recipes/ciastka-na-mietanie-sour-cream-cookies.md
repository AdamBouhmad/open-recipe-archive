## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- one cup of sour cream
- three eggs
- one teaspoon of soda
- wheat flour
- salt

## Directions

1. First cream butter with sugar
2. Then add egg yolks and sour cream, beaten together
3. At the end add the foam from the egg whites, a little salt and enough wheat flour to make a soft dough
4. Roll out to half an inch thickness
5. Cut out round cookies
6. Bake on a baking sheet
