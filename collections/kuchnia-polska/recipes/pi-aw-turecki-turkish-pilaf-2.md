## Ingredients

- one pound of rinsed rice
- water
- fatty lamb breast or other meat
- butter
- small onions

## Directions

1. Pour water over one pound of rinsed rice and cook for ten minutes, then throw it onto a sieve so the water drains well.
2. Chop fatty lamb breast or other meat into pieces the size of cutlets, salt it and place a layer of meat in a braising pan on melted butter, then a layer of rice, then meat again and so on until everything is used, interspersing these layers with small onions that have been previously blanched with boiling water.
3. Place the meat and rice arranged in this way on low heat and cover tightly with a lid so that as little steam escapes as possible.
4. After fifteen minutes check the pan and, if necessary, pour in a tablespoon of cold water so it does not burn. Repeat this watering every few or several minutes until the rice is completely tender, which will take about two hours.
