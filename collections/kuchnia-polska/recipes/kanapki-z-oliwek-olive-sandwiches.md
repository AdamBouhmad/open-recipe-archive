## Ingredients

- rye bread, sliced thinly
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- olives, pitted and finely chopped
- celery, finely chopped, equal amount to olives
- small pickled cucumber, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- one quarter teaspoon mustard
- optional: meat or sausage slices

## Directions

1. Slice rye bread thinly
2. Spread with butter
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Remove pits from olives
5. Chop olives finely
6. Mix with an equal amount of finely chopped celery
7. Add one finely chopped small pickled cucumber
8. Add salt, pepper, and one quarter teaspoon mustard
9. Mix well
10. Spread on buttered bread
11. Cover with another slice of bread
12. Optionally, place a slice of meat or sausage on the bread before adding the olive mixture
