## Ingredients

- chicken
- vegetables for broth
- 1/4 pound cooked rice, very soft
- 1 tablespoon good butter
- 2 egg yolks
- broth from chicken
- 1/2 glass Rhine wine (optional)
- small croutons fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cook broth from chicken in the usual way, adding vegetables, but cook longer so the meat separates completely from the bones.
2. Take the white meat and meat from the thighs, chop finely, discarding the skin.
3. Separately prepare 1/4 pound of rice cooked until very soft.
4. Take some meat and rice and pound together in a mortar into a paste, then pass through a sieve, pouring broth over it.
5. Take 1 tablespoon of good butter, beat it with two egg yolks and mix into the soup that has been removed from the heat.
6. Do not cook further, but serve immediately to the table.
7. Some add 1/2 glass of Rhine wine before serving, which is poured into the soup and heated together.
8. Serve with small croutons fried in butter.
