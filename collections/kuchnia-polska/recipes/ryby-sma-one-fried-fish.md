## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- beaten egg
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Salt the fish an hour before frying.
2. Wipe off excess moisture with clean blotting paper.
3. Dip in beaten egg and fry in hot butter or fat, using plenty of fat.
4. Instead of butter, fat can be used equally well.
5. Serve the fried fish dry, without pouring butter on it, sprinkled only with finely chopped green parsley.
