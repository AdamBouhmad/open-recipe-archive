## Ingredients

- young green beans
- water
- salt to taste
- butter
- a little flour
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Remove the strings from young green beans and chop finely, throw into a pan with boiling water, of which there should be plenty; salt and cook on high heat for ten minutes, then throw into a colander and rinse with cold water to cool.
2. Brown a little butter with a pinch of flour on white, add the beans, sprinkle with pepper, heat and serve.
