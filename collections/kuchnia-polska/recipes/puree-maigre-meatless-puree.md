## Ingredients

- one onion
- handful of white cabbage
- water
- half a cup of rice
- one small turnip
- one carrot
- a couple of tablespoons of celery
- one tablespoon of green parsley
- one quart of cold water
- one cup of tomatoes from a can or fresh
- pinch of baking soda
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- one cup of good milk

## Directions

1. Peel and finely chop one onion, finely chop a handful of white cabbage, pour water over them and cook for twenty minutes, then drain the water
2. Rinse half a cup of rice and soak in cold water for two hours
3. Peel and grate one small turnip and one carrot on a grater
4. Chop a couple of tablespoons of celery and one tablespoon of green parsley
5. Pour all of the above with one quart of cold water, place on fire and cook for one hour from when it comes to a boil
6. Take one cup of tomatoes from a can, or the same amount of fresh ones, add to the soup and cook for another half hour
7. Strain everything through a fine sieve or colander
8. Pour back into the pot, place on the fire, add a pinch of baking soda, mix one tablespoon of flour with one tablespoon of butter and bring to a boil
9. Add salt and pepper to taste, and after five minutes remove from heat, add one cup of good milk, and serve to the table
