## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- salt
- two eggs
- half a tablespoon butter
- nutmeg, grated
- one teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- beaten egg
- grated bread crumbs
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Boil sweet potatoes in salted water until tender
2. Drain the water and mash them into a mass
3. Beat two eggs and add to the potatoes
4. Add half a tablespoon butter, salt to taste, and a pinch of grated nutmeg
5. Add one teaspoon of chopped fresh parsley and mix everything
6. Place in a saucepan on low heat
7. Stir constantly until the mass begins to pull away from the sides of the saucepan
8. Transfer to a board and place in a cool place
9. When completely cooled, form into rolls three inches long and one inch thick
10. Dip each in beaten egg and then in grated bread crumbs
11. Fry in hot fat
