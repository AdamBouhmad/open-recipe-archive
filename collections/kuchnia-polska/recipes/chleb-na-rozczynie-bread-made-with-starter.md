## Ingredients

- half tablet of pressed yeast
- half quart of lukewarm water
- one quart of flour
- half quart of water
- two teaspoons of salt
- two tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of lard
- two quarts of flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve half tablet of pressed yeast in half quart of lukewarm water
2. Add one quart of flour and mix to a uniform mass
3. Make this starter late in the evening and place in a warm location
4. The next morning, add half quart of water, two teaspoons of salt, two tablespoons of sugar, two tablespoons of lard and two quarts of flour
5. Mix everything thoroughly and place in a warm location for the dough to rise
6. After rising, knead the dough, then proceed as with white bread above, but the oven should have medium temperature
7. Both the starter and dough should have a temperature of 80 to 84 degrees Fahrenheit
