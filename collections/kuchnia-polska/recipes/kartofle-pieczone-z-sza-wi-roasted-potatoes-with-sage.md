## Ingredients

- small potatoes
- sage, sprinkled
- salt
- pepper
- one large onion
- butter
- milk or broth, half a cup

## Directions

1. Peel only small potatoes to cover the bottom of a baking dish
2. Arrange them in this manner, then sprinkle with grated sage using about half a teaspoon
3. Add half a teaspoon of salt and some pepper
4. Slice one large onion into thin slices and cover the prepared potatoes with them
5. Place a tablespoon of butter in small pieces on top
6. Pour over everything half a cup of milk or broth
7. Bake in a moderately hot oven, which will take about forty minutes
