## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of rhubarb stalks
- one pound of sugar
- two or three bitter almonds blanched and cut in half
- one lemon cut into small pieces

## Directions

1. Peel the outer fibers and cut into one-inch pieces one and a half pounds of rhubarb stalks, add one pound of sugar, two or three bitter almonds blanched and cut in half and one lemon cut into small pieces
2. Put all this in a pot and cook until the mass is thick
3. This excellent marmalade can be kept for a long time without spoiling
