## Ingredients

- one quart of strong brandy
- one cup of orange juice
- half a pound of sugar in pieces
- zest from three oranges (yellow part only)

## Directions

1. Take one quart of strong brandy and add one cup of orange juice, half a pound of sugar in pieces and zest from three oranges; use only the yellow part of the zest, scraped with a sharp knife, discard the white part
2. Pour everything into a jar or bottle and cork it tightly, then place in a cool place for three days, stirring several times each day
3. After three days strain the liquid, add more sugar if not sweet enough, pour into bottles and cork
