## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef, finely chopped
- 1 carrot, finely sliced
- 1 small rutabaga, finely sliced
- 1 onion, finely sliced
- 1 clove
- 1/2 bay leaf
- 1 quart water
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon tomato sauce (optional)
- burnt sugar for color (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop finely 1 pound of lean beef; slice finely 1 carrot, 1 small rutabaga and 1 onion, also add 1 clove and 1/2 bay leaf
2. Cover vegetables with 1 quart of water and cook for 20 minutes, then strain
3. Pour the strained liquid back into the pot, add the meat and cook on low heat for 1 hour with salt and pepper to taste
4. You can also add 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce and burnt sugar for color
5. Serve on the table with the meat
