## Ingredients

- veal meat
- egg
- cold water
- flour
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into thin large slices and pound each one quite thin, giving it the shape of a cutlet.
2. Salt and dip in egg beaten with a spoonful of cold water and a spoonful of flour; the layer of this batter should be very thin, but the entire cutlet must be covered with it.
3. Heat a spoonful of butter on a pan or griddle, place the cutlet on it and fry until browned on one side.
4. Turn over to the other side, brown similarly and immediately serve to the table.
