## Ingredients

- one-quarter cup butter
- one cup sugar
- one egg, beaten
- one-half cup milk
- one-quarter teaspoon salt
- one and one-half cups flour
- one heaping teaspoon baking powder
- small pieces of butter for topping
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Cream one-quarter cup butter with one cup sugar
2. Add one beaten egg
3. Add one-half cup milk
4. Add one-quarter teaspoon salt and one and one-half cups flour sifted several times with one heaping teaspoon baking powder
5. Mix into a smooth dough
6. Pour into shallow pans to no more than one inch depth
7. Scatter small pieces of butter on top
8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
9. Bake in a moderately hot oven
