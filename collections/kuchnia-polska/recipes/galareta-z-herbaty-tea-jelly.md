## Ingredients

- half a box of gelatin
- water
- very strong tea
- one cup of sugar for every half quart of tea
- cream and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a box of gelatin in just enough water to barely cover it
2. Make very strong tea, add one cup of sugar per half quart of tea, and when dissolved mix with the gelatin
3. Strain and pour into small molds, which should be placed on ice
4. Serve very cold with cream and sugar on the side
