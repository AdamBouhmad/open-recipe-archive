## Ingredients

- one onion
- one small carrot
- a piece of ham
- bay leaf
- several peppercorns
- celery stalk
- butter
- two tablespoons vinegar
- some broth
- sour cream (optional, for wild sauce variation)

## Directions

1. Chop finely one onion, one small carrot, and a piece of ham. Add a bay leaf, several peppercorns, and a celery stalk.
2. Brown all of this in butter. Then add two tablespoons of vinegar and some broth and stew on low heat until tender.
3. Press through a sieve and pour into the sauce that remained on the roasting pan from the lamb roast and bring to a boil once more.
4. This sauce is called sharp. To make wild sauce, add several tablespoons of sour cream to it.
