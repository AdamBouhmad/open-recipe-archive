## Ingredients

- two pounds veal bones
- two quarts cold water
- half a cup pearl barley
- one cup hot cream
- salt and pepper to taste
- meat patties as garnish

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of veal bones, cover with two quarts of cold water and cook on very low heat for three hours.
2. Collect the scum and strain.
3. Rinse half a cup of pearl barley in cold water and pour into a pot and cover with the prepared broth, then cover and cook on low heat until the barley softens.
4. Remove one-third of the barley and place in another pot, pass the rest through a sieve and pour everything onto the whole barley.
5. Add one cup of hot cream, add salt and pepper to taste and serve at table.
6. Meat patties are served as a garnish to this soup.
7. Instead of veal bones, one can use bones left from a roasted chicken or turkey.
