## Ingredients

- large pike
- salt
- white pepper
- young bacon
- cotton thread

## Directions

1. Clean the large pike carefully.
2. Salt and sprinkle it lightly with white pepper.
3. Cut young bacon into broad but very thin slices.
4. Wrap the entire pike with these bacon slices.
5. Tie with cotton thread so the bacon lies smoothly on the fish.
6. Place the prepared pike in a roasting pan.
7. Roast in a moderately hot oven, basting frequently with the drippings from the fish.
