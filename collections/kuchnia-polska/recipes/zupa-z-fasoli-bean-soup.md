## Ingredients

- White beans
- A piece of ham
- A piece of smoked bacon
- One onion
- A piece of cabbage head
- Celery
- Leeks
- Several fresh tomatoes
- Salt and pepper
- A handful of cooked rice

## Directions

1. Take white beans; if very dry, soak in water for several hours
2. Put a piece of ham and a piece of smoked bacon together with the beans in a pot and cook almost until tender
3. Add one sliced onion, a piece of sliced cabbage head, celery, leeks, and several fresh tomatoes, salt and pepper to taste
4. Cook further one hour
5. Before serving, add a handful of cooked rice
6. Meatballs made from beef fat are the best addition to this soup
