## Ingredients

- lean pork meat
- soaked bread roll
- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Chop the pork meat finely.
2. Add soaked bread roll.
3. For every two pounds of meat, add one egg yolk, salt and pepper to taste.
4. Mix everything thoroughly.
5. Form flat cutlets from the mixture.
6. Coat with bread crumbs.
7. Fry in hot butter or lard.
