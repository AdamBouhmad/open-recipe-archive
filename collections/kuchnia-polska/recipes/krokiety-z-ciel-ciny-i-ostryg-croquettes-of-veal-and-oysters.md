## Ingredients

- One cup raw oysters
- One cup cold roasted or cooked veal
- One spoonful of butter
- Three spoonfuls of grated bread or bread crumbs
- Two egg yolks
- One spoonful of grated onion
- Salt and pepper
- Egg for dipping
- Grated bread for coating
- Hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the veal and oysters very finely
2. Add pepper and salt to taste, then the egg yolks, butter, bread, and onion
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Form into oblong small cutlets from this mass
5. Dip in beaten egg, coat in grated bread, and fry on hot fat until golden
