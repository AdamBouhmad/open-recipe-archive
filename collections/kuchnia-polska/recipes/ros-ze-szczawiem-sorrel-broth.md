## Ingredients

- beef for broth
- handful of sorrel leaves
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook strong broth on beef
2. Pour off half a quart of this broth into a separate pot and cook a handful of sorrel leaves in it
3. Pour the broth and sorrel leaves into a bowl and mix
4. Cook eggs in their shells in water, one per person, arrange on plates and then pour soup onto each plate
