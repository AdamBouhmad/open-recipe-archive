## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- crayfish tails and claws
- parsley

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the eel, salt it, cut it into rings and cook in stock made from vegetables, prepared previously and cooled.
2. When the fish is completely tender, remove it; clarify the remaining stock and place in a cold place to cool.
3. When the aspic begins to set, pour a few tablespoons of it onto a small plate, arrange the cooked crayfish tails and claws on it, intersperse with sprigs of parsley, arrange the fish on top and cover with the remaining aspic, then leave undisturbed until it sets.
