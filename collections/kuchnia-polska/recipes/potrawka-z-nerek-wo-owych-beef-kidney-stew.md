## Ingredients

- one beef kidney
- salted water
- one onion, finely chopped or grated
- pinch of sage
- one cup boiling water
- one tablespoon cornstarch
- salt and pepper to taste
- two hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Chop one beef kidney into small pieces, place in a pan, cover with salted water, and cook for half an hour
2. Drain the kidneys on a colander
3. Return to the pan and add one finely chopped or grated onion, a pinch of sage, and one cup of boiling water
4. Bring to a boil, then thicken with one tablespoon of cornstarch
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and cook a few minutes more
6. Before serving, chop two hard-boiled eggs and stir into the stew
