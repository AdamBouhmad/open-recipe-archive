## Ingredients

- one cup finely chopped cooked ham
- two hard-boiled eggs
- one cup grated rolls or white bread
- one tablespoon melted butter
- one cup milk mixed with two tablespoons cornstarch
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Take one cup of finely chopped cooked ham, two hard-boiled eggs, one cup of grated rolls or white bread, one tablespoon of melted butter, one cup of milk mixed with two tablespoons of cornstarch, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Mix the grated bread and butter well.
3. Chop the whites finely.
4. Rub the yolks into a smooth paste.
5. Take a small baking dish and on the bottom spread some of the ham, then bread, whites, and finally yolks until the dish is full.
6. Pour the milk with cornstarch over everything.
7. Place in the oven for half an hour and serve hot.
