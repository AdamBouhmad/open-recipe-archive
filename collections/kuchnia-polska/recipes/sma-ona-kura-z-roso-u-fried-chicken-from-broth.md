## Ingredients

- cooked chicken from broth
- one egg
- grated bread
- hot fat for frying
- broth
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. After cooking the broth with the chicken, remove the meat onto a colander so it dries completely.
2. Take one piece at a time, dip it in beaten egg and then in grated bread.
3. Fry on hot fat for about ten minutes until browned, watching carefully so as not to burn it.
4. Place on a platter and pour over a sauce made from broth, butter and flour.
