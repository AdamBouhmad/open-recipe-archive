## Ingredients

- sauerkraut
- smoked meat (wędzonka)
- water
- butter
- flour
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Finely chop a sufficient amount of sauerkraut
2. Place in a pot with a piece of smoked meat
3. Cover with water and cook until the meat is tender
4. Remove the meat, cut into cubes and return to the soup
5. Fry some butter with flour and add it
6. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve
