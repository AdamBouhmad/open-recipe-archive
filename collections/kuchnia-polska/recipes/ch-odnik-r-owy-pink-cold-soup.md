## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cucumber brine
- water (if too sour)
- chopped dill
- a few red beets
- 2 pickled cucumbers
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- several crayfish tails
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Take 1 quart of cucumber brine and add water if it is too sour
2. Add chopped dill and boil, then remove from heat
3. Cook a few red beets separately, peel, and cut into thin strips
4. Add to the warm brine so it absorbs color and sweetness
5. Peel and slice 2 pickled cucumbers
6. Cut 3 hard-boiled eggs into quarters
7. Add several crayfish tails
8. Beat 1 cup of sour cream well
9. Combine all ingredients
