## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Various vegetables
- Salt
- Rice, quarter pound for four persons
- Hot water
- Veal broth
- Butter
- Grated bread

## Directions

1. Scald the veal breast, then cook until tender with various vegetables and sufficient salt
2. For four persons, take a quarter pound of rice, scald with hot water, bring to a boil, drain the water, and cover the rice with veal broth
3. Add a spoonful of butter and braise until the rice is tender
4. Just before serving, place the breast in the rice and heat through
5. Serve with the breast in the center and rice around it
6. Can be garnished with bread crumbs browned in butter
