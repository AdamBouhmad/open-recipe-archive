## Ingredients

- 1 quart small white onions
- water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 quart hot milk
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Peel 1 quart of small white onions, place in a pot and cover with boiling water.
2. Cook until almost tender, then add 1 teaspoon salt, cook a few minutes longer and drain on a colander.
3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a pot, thicken with 1/2 tablespoon flour and fry for a few minutes, being careful not to brown the flour.
4. Pour in 1/2 quart hot milk, add salt and pepper to taste and bring to boil for about two minutes.
5. Lay hot onions on a plate and pour this sauce over them.
