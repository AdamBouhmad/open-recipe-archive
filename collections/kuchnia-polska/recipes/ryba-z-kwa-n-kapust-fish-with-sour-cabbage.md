## Ingredients

- fish (tench preferred, or carp)
- sour cabbage
- butter
- flour
- onion
- sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. For this dish, tench is best, but since tench is not found in America, any other fish can be used, even carp.
2. Cook the sour cabbage after rinsing it thoroughly in cold water so it is not too sour.
3. Season with butter mixed with flour and roasted and grated onion.
4. Cut the fish into small pieces, not thick slices, remove all bones, salt and set aside for an hour.
5. Place the fish in the cabbage, pour in sour cream, mix and braise on low heat under a cover until tender, being careful not to let it burn.
