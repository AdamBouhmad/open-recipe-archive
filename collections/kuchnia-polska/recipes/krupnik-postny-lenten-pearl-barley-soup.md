## Ingredients

- pearl barley
- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- butter
- salt
- two egg yolks
- sweet sour cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. For four people, cook a quarter-quart of fine pearl barley with whole mixed vegetables so it can be discarded
2. Add a spoonful of butter and salt to taste
3. When the barley is cooked, beat two egg yolks in a bowl with a cup of sweet sour cream, add finely chopped parsley and then pour the soup over it, constantly stirring
