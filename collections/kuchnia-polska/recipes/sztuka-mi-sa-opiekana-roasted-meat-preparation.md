## Ingredients

- meat cooked until tender in broth
- horseradish
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- broth
- salt to taste
- two egg yolks
- sugar
- optional: béchamel sauce or butter and flour sauce with broth
- optional: grated cheese or parmesan

## Directions

1. Remove cooked meat from broth and cut into long pieces, arrange on a metal or ceramic plate
2. Grate several spoonfuls of horseradish on a grater, pour boiling water over it and place in a pot
3. Add two spoonfuls of sour cream, juice from half a lemon, several spoonfuls of broth and salt to taste
4. Bring to boil, then beat two egg yolks and pour into the horseradish
5. Immediately remove from heat, add one teaspoon of sugar and pour this sauce over the meat
6. Place in oven to brown well and serve at table
7. Optional: instead of horseradish sauce, use béchamel sauce or sauce made from butter and flour with addition of several spoonfuls of broth
8. Some prefer to sprinkle with grated cheese or parmesan and then place in oven for browning
