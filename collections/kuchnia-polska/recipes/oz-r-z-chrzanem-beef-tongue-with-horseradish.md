## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- vegetables
- horseradish, grated
- butter
- sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook beef tongue with vegetables as usual
2. Remove, peel off the skin, and slice thinly
3. Arrange on a platter
4. Take a handful of grated horseradish and brown it with butter
5. Add sour cream, bring to a boil, and pour this sauce over the tongue arranged on the platter
