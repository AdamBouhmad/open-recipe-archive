## Ingredients

- oxtail, cut into 8 to 10 pieces
- cold water
- vegetables as available
- pearl barley
- garlic sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into 8 to 10 pieces and cover with cold water and cook.
2. When the scum appears, collect it carefully, and continue until it stops appearing.
3. Add all available vegetables and sprinkle in barley and cook for three hours on low heat.
4. When the meat is cooked, remove it and add separately in garlic sauce, and pour the soup into a dish.
