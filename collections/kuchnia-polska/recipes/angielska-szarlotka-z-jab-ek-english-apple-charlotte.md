## Ingredients

- sour apples
- two-thirds cup sugar
- one quarter cup water
- butter
- salt
- grated lemon zest
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut sour apples into very thin slices and fill a deep baking pan or any vessel with straight sides to the brim
2. Sprinkle two-thirds cup sugar
3. Add one quarter cup water, a little butter, a pinch of salt and some grated lemon zest
4. Mix some flour with lard and water to make a stiff dough
5. Roll out on a table to the size of the pan with apples
6. Cover the pan and seal the edges
7. To help the dough hold better, wet the edges of the pan with cold water
8. Bake in the oven for half an hour
9. Serve hot with sugar and cream
