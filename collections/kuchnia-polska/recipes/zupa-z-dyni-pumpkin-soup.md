## Ingredients

- half quart yellow pumpkin
- celery stalks
- butter
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- white bread

## Directions

1. Peel and finely chop half quart yellow pumpkin and a couple of celery stalks
2. Melt a spoonful of butter in a pot, add the pumpkin and celery and fry until lightly browned
3. Pour a quart of hot water, salt and pepper, then cook until everything is soft enough to strain through a sieve
4. After straining, pour the soup back into the pot and place on fire
5. Separately make a roux in a small pot with a large half spoonful of flour and the same amount of butter and brown into a uniform mass, pour into the soup and mix well and cook for about ten minutes
6. Line a bowl with small croutons of white bread and pour the soup over them
7. These croutons should be made from stale bread, cut into small cubes and fried in butter in a pan
