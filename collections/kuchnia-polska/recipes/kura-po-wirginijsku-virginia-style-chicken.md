## Ingredients

- medium chicken, three to four pounds
- water
- potatoes (one per person)
- fresh corn kernels, scraped from cobs with a sharp knife
- white beans
- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- rice, cooked

## Directions

1. Clean and quarter the chicken.
2. Place in a small amount of water and cook for fifteen minutes.
3. Add potatoes (one per person), a handful of fresh corn kernels scraped from the cob, a handful of white beans, three tomatoes, a teaspoon of salt, and a quarter teaspoon of pepper.
4. Cook on low heat under a cover for one and a half hours.
5. Serve on a deep platter with the vegetables and sauce; serve cooked rice separately.
