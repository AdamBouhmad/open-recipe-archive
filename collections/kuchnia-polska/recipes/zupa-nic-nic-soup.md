## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon fine sugar
- 1 quart unboiled milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons cold milk
- cinnamon stick
- handful of raisins without seeds
- ice

## Directions

1. Whip 3 egg whites into a foam, add 1 tablespoon of fine sugar and mix.
2. Bring 1 quart of unboiled milk to a boil, take the foam of egg whites with a spoon and place on the boiling milk like dumplings. When cooked, remove with a slotted spoon and place in a bowl.
3. Mix 1/2 cup of sugar with 3 egg yolks almost to whiteness, add 2 tablespoons of cold milk. Then pour hot milk onto this mixture, stirring continuously.
4. Add a couple of pieces of cinnamon and a handful of raisins without seeds and pour everything into the bowl and place on ice to cool.
