## Ingredients

- crabapples
- water
- three-quarters pound sugar per pound of apples
- three lemons per pound of apples

## Directions

1. From nice crabapples, remove the core along with the seeds
2. Pour enough water to barely cover and cook until soft
3. Strain through a thin strainer and set aside the liquid
4. Pour a small amount of water over the apples and cook
5. Push through a colander
6. Pour the liquid from the cores into the apple mass
7. Place everything on the heat and cook until the mass is thick
8. Add three-quarters pound sugar per pound of apples and three lemons
9. Mix and cook for forty-five minutes more
10. Fill jars or glasses and seal with paraffin
