## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds cleaned tripe
- salted water
- small cup well-rinsed rice
- two sliced onions
- fat or lard
- hot water
- one teaspoon curry powder

## Directions

1. Pour salted water over the cleaned tripe and cook for ten minutes from the time it comes to a boil. Pour out the water, add fresh clean water, salt and cook for fifteen minutes.
2. Remove the tripe and to the remaining broth add a small cup of well-rinsed rice and cook until tender.
3. Fry two sliced onions in fat until golden brown, add the tripe, pour hot water just to cover it barely and cook briefly.
4. Add one teaspoon of curry powder, mix well, then arrange the tripe on a platter and surround it with the cooked rice.
