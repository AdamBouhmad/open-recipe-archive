## Ingredients

- one quart of shelled fresh green peas
- one tablespoon butter
- salt to taste
- water
- one tablespoon butter
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the shelled green peas, which must be very young and fresh, into a two-quart pan, add one tablespoon of butter, salt to taste, fill with water and braise covered on low heat for half an hour.
2. When soft, drain the water, add another tablespoon of butter, a little sugar and serve immediately on the table.
