## Ingredients

- one cup seedless raisins
- one lemon
- one cup sugar
- one egg, beaten
- a pinch of salt
- flour, lard, and cold water for dough

## Directions

1. Chop finely one cup seedless raisins
2. Squeeze juice from one lemon and grate the zest
3. Add one cup sugar, one beaten egg, and a pinch of salt and mix well
4. Make a medium dough with flour, lard, and cold water
5. Roll into thin circles about six inches in diameter
6. Place a spoon or more of the raisin filling on each circle
7. Fold the dough from three sides to form a triangular hat shape
8. Seal the edges tightly
9. Brush the top with sweetened milk and place in the oven
