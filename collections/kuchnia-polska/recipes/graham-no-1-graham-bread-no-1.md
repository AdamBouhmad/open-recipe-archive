## Ingredients

- two cups of graham flour
- one cup of wheat flour
- three tablespoons of sugar
- one teaspoon of salt
- one egg
- two teaspoons of soda dissolved in two cups of sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix the graham flour, wheat flour, sugar, salt, egg and soda dissolved in sour milk into dough
2. Bake for one hour in a moderate oven
