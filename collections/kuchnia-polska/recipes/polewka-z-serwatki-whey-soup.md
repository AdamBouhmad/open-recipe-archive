## Ingredients

- 1 quart of fresh whey
- a few tablespoons of wholemeal flour
- sour cream
- salt to taste
- croutons

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart of fresh whey
2. Mix wholemeal flour with sour cream
3. Slowly add the cream mixture to the whey, constantly stirring
4. Add salt to taste
5. Serve with croutons
