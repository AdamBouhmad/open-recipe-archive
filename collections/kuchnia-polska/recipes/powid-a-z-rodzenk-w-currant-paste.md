## Ingredients

- one large pound of currants without seeds
- one cup of water
- half a pound of sugar
- half of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Carefully sort and wash one large pound of currants without seeds
2. Chop them, add one cup of water, half a pound of sugar and half of grated nutmeg
3. Mix everything and boil until it breaks down into a thick mass
4. Fill glasses and seal tightly
