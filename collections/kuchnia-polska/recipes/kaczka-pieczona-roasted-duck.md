## Ingredients

- duck, older and well-fattened
- apples
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Very young and lean ducks are not suitable for roasting, as they have very little meat, so it is best to take older and well-fattened ducks
2. Prepare and clean, then stuff with apples and marjoram and roast exactly the same as goose
