## Ingredients

- eel
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- egg
- water
- bread crumbs
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. After skinning and cleaning the eel, cut it into rings, pour stock made from miscellaneous vegetables over it and cook until it is half cooked.
2. Then remove it, dip in egg beaten with water, coat with bread crumbs mixed with flour and fry in hot butter.
3. The best sauce for eel is caper sauce.
