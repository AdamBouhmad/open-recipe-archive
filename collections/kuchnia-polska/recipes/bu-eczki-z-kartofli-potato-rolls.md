## Ingredients

- three medium potatoes
- one cup of boiled milk
- one tablespoon of lard
- one teaspoon of butter
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one teaspoon of salt
- one tablet of yeast
- warm water
- two well-beaten eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook three medium potatoes and mash into a puree, then add one cup of boiled milk, one tablespoon of lard, one teaspoon of butter, one tablespoon of sugar and one teaspoon of salt and set to cool
2. When cooled to lukewarm temperature, add one tablet of yeast dissolved in warm water, two well-beaten eggs and enough flour to make a rather thin dough
3. Set in a warm place for one hour to rise, then add more flour and knead properly
4. Set again to allow the dough to rise; when well risen, spread on a work surface, roll out and cut small round rolls
5. Dip the rolls in melted butter and arrange on a baking sheet one inch apart from each other
6. Cover again and set in a warm place until well risen, then place in a hot oven for ten minutes
