## Ingredients

- ground meat
- butter
- broth
- juice from half a lemon
- one teaspoon sugar
- onion, sliced or chopped
- grated bread or flour mixed with butter

## Directions

1. Prepare meat the same way as for ground roast and mix well
2. Form into round meatballs and flatten slightly
3. Brown in a pan in hot butter over high heat
4. Place in a pot, add enough broth to not quite cover the meatballs
5. Add one tablespoon of butter, juice from half a lemon, one teaspoon sugar, some sliced or chopped onion, and a few tablespoons of grated bread
6. Braise covered for fifteen to twenty minutes
7. The quality of the meatballs can be improved by adding capers
