## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup syrup
- 0.5 cup lard
- 1 egg
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- graham flour as needed

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 cup syrup, 0.5 cup lard, 1 egg, 1 cup sour milk, and 1 teaspoon soda with enough graham flour to make fairly thin dough.
2. Mix well, pour into forms, and bake.
