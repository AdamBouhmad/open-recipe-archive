## Ingredients

- 1 cup plus a bit cold cooked or fried fish, crumbled
- 1 cup hot softly cooked rice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- a pinch of red pepper
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- equal amount of butter
- 2 well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Crumble cold cooked or fried fish to make 1 cup with a bit extra
2. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to 1 cup of hot softly cooked rice and mix well
3. Mix with the fish, add salt, pepper and a pinch of red pepper to taste
4. Bring 1 cup of milk to a boil and thicken with 1 teaspoon of flour mixed with the same amount of butter
5. Pour into the fish and rice mixture and place on heat
6. As soon as it boils, pour in 2 well-beaten eggs and immediately remove from heat and serve at table
7. The eggs may be omitted and it will be good without them
