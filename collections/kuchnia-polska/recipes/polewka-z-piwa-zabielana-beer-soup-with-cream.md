## Ingredients

- one quart of beer
- piece of rye bread crust
- quarter cup of sour cream
- four egg yolks
- one tablespoon of cold beer
- one tablespoon of fine sugar
- bread cut into cubes and dried
- curd cheese
- a couple of tablespoons of sugar with cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil under a cover one quart of beer with a piece of rye bread crust
2. Separately, beat in a pot a quarter cup of sour cream with four egg yolks, one tablespoon of cold beer, and one tablespoon of fine sugar
3. When the beer boils, pour it slowly, constantly stirring, into the pot with the sour cream and egg yolks
4. Place on fire so that it becomes warm, but does not boil
5. Pour into a bowl in which bread cut into cubes and dried, curd cheese, and a couple of tablespoons of sugar with cinnamon are placed first
6. If sour cream is lacking, one can make it with egg yolks alone, but then take more, namely six yolks for four persons
