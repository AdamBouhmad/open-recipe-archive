## Ingredients

- dried mushrooms
- couple of onions
- butter
- 2 egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- several large potatoes
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook dried mushrooms until soft and chop very finely.
2. Chop a couple of onions and sauté in butter, mix with the mushrooms.
3. Add two egg yolks, breadcrumbs, salt and pepper to taste and mix everything into a smooth mass.
4. Peel several large potatoes and place in the oven for a few or a dozen minutes, just long enough for them to lose their rawness.
5. Cut off the top slice from each potato, hollow out with a spoon and fill the hollow with the mushroom mass.
6. Cover with the cut slice, arrange on a sheet, pour butter over and bake in a moderate oven.
7. Just before serving, make a roux with flour and butter, thin it with the liquid from the cooked mushrooms and the sauce, and pour over the potatoes.
