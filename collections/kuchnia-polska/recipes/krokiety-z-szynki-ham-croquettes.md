## Ingredients

- two large boiled potatoes
- one cup finely minced cooked ham
- one tablespoon butter
- a pinch of red pepper
- one cup grated breadcrumbs
- two eggs
- grated breadcrumbs (for coating)
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mash two large boiled potatoes while hot
2. Add one cup of finely minced cooked ham, one tablespoon of butter, a pinch of red pepper, one cup of grated breadcrumbs, and two eggs
3. Mix everything well with the potatoes
4. When the mixture cools slightly, form small oval patties
5. Coat with grated breadcrumbs, dip in beaten egg, then coat again in grated breadcrumbs
6. Fry in a pan filled with hot lard for two to three minutes no longer
7. Drain on paper
