## Ingredients

- three cups of rhubarb stalks, cut into small pieces
- one tablespoon of flour
- one cup of sugar
- one teaspoon of butter
- pie dough
- sugar

## Directions

1. Line a sheet with dough and sprinkle with sugar mixed with flour, using half the amount
