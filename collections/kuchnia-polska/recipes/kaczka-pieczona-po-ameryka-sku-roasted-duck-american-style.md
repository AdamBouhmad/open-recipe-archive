## Ingredients

- young duck
- half a quart grated white bread, preferably stale
- half a teaspoon salt
- quarter teaspoon pepper
- half a spoon chopped fresh parsley
- one teaspoon chopped onion
- half a teaspoon powdered sage
- one-third cup butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean the young duck, clean it and wipe the inside with salt, then make the following filling: Take half a quart of grated white bread, preferably stale; add half a teaspoon of salt, quarter teaspoon of pepper, half a spoon of chopped fresh parsley, one teaspoon of chopped onion, half a teaspoon of powdered sage and one-third cup of butter; mix all this properly and stuff the duck with this filling
2. Then wipe the top with salt and then with butter, and finally sprinkle with flour to form a coating from the butter and flour; place in the oven
