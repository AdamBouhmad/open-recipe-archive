## Ingredients

- beef or veal broth
- rye kvass to taste
- beet greens with dill
- 1/2 quart of sour cream
- 1/2 quart of broth
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- cucumbers cut into cubes
- some crayfish tails
- ice

## Directions

1. Cook a strong beef or veal broth and strain it
2. Add rye kvass until the flavor is vinegary
3. Cook some beet greens with dill separately, chop finely, and place in a bowl
4. Pour 1/2 quart of sour cream over them
5. Mix together
6. Slowly pour in 1/2 quart of broth, constantly stirring
7. Add 3 hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters, cucumbers cut into cubes, and some crayfish tails
8. Place the bowl on ice
9. When thoroughly chilled, serve on a plate with a piece of ice
10. Instead of rye kvass, you can use cucumber brine boiled briefly with broth, or sorrel juice
