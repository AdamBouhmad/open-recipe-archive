## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup sugar
- one and a half cups sifted flour
- one heaping teaspoon baking powder
- salt
- half quart milk
- two eggs
- half cup flour
- half cup sugar
- one tablespoon butter
- vanilla or lemon extract

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs and add one cup sugar
2. Add one and a half cups sifted flour mixed with one heaping teaspoon baking powder and a pinch of salt
3. Mix everything together into dough
4. Divide dough into two halves and pour onto two baking pans
5. Bake in the oven until the cakes brown slightly and take on a dark straw color
6. Remove and cool
7. Bring half quart milk to a boil
8. Beat two eggs and add to them half cup flour and half cup sugar, well mixed
9. Mix everything well and pour into the milk as soon as it comes to a boil
10. Add one tablespoon butter and a little salt and cook on low heat until it thickens
11. Split each previously baked cake in half with a sharp knife
12. Spread cooled cream on the bottom half and cover with the top half
13. Place in a cold spot to cool well
14. It is good to add one teaspoon vanilla or lemon extract to the cream right after cooking
