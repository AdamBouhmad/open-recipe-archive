## Ingredients

- three tablespoons flour
- half a teaspoon baking powder
- salt
- three large cooked potatoes
- two beaten eggs
- one tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Sift together three tablespoons flour, half a teaspoon baking powder, and salt to mix well
2. Grate three large cooked potatoes on a grater
3. Add the grated potatoes to the flour
4. Add two beaten eggs and one tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley
5. Mix everything into a dough
6. Take small spoonfuls and fry in hot fat, using plenty of fat in the pan
7. After frying, place on paper to absorb the fat
