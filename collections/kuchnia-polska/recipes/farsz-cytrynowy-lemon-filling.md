## Ingredients

- one large citron
- one cup of sugar
- two eggs
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Take the grated peel and juice from one large citron, add one cup of sugar, two eggs and one tablespoon of butter
2. Pour into a small pot, place the pot in a pot of boiling water and cook until the mass is thick, then cool
