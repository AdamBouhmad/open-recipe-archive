## Ingredients

- thin bacon slices
- various vegetables and roots
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- lamb meat
- water
- vinegar
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a braising pan with thin bacon slices. Then place various chopped vegetables, roots, a bay leaf, salt and pepper to taste, and on top place lamb meat.
2. Pour water halfway up, add a tablespoon of vinegar and stew on low heat until tender.
3. After cooking, remove the meat and arrange on a serving dish. Thicken the sauce with flour, thin with broth, bring to a boil once, strain and pour over the meat.
