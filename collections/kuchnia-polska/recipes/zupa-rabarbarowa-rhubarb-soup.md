## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of rhubarb stalks
- one and a half quarts of water
- half a pound of sugar
- zest of half a lemon
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- one spoonful of cornstarch
- cold water
- dumplings or cooked rice

## Directions

1. Finely chop one and a half pounds of rhubarb stalks
2. Pour one and a half quarts of water over them and cook until the rhubarb completely falls apart
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Add half a pound of sugar, zest from half a lemon, some cinnamon and nutmeg, and one spoonful of cornstarch mixed in cold water
5. Bring to a boil and serve with dumplings or cooked rice
6. This soup can also be served cold like a fruit soup with bread cubes
