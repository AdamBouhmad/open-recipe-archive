## Ingredients

- beef marrow from bones
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped green parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- red pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon onion juice
- white bread

## Directions

1. Remove marrow from beef bones and cut into inch pieces
2. Cook in a quart of salted water for 1.5 to 2 minutes
3. Place finely chopped parsley, lemon juice, salt, red pepper, and onion juice in a hot small bowl
4. Remove cooked marrow with a slotted spoon and place in the bowl with the additions and mix well
5. Have toast made from white bread and spread the marrow mixture on the hot toast immediately
6. Serve immediately as these sandwiches are only good when hot
