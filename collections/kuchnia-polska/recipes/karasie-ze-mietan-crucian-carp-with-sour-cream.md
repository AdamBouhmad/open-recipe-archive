## Ingredients

- crucian carp
- salt
- mixed vegetables
- butter
- flour
- sour cream
- lemon
- sugar
- dill
- potatoes
- noodles

## Directions

1. Clean and salt the crucian carp an hour before cooking.
2. Prepare a broth from various vegetables, strain and cool it; there should be just enough broth to barely cover the fish.
3. After removing the fish from salt, place it in a pot, pour the broth with vegetables over it and cook on high heat until tender, about fifteen minutes.
4. In a separate pot, mix a spoonful of butter with a spoonful of flour, add about three spoonfuls of sour cream, squeeze in about a quarter of a lemon, add a little sugar and some chopped dill.
5. Dilute with the broth from the fish, bring to a boil together and then pour this sauce over the fish arranged on a serving dish.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes and small noodles cooked in salted water and dressed with butter.
