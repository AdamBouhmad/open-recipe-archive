## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- three hard-cooked eggs
- one large onion
- salt
- pepper
- one egg yolk
- one cup vinegar
- two thirds cup sour cream

## Directions

1. Chop cooked potatoes fairly coarsely
2. For one quart of potatoes, hard cook three eggs and chop them; finely chop or grate on a grater one large onion
3. Layer in a salad bowl: a layer of potatoes mixed with sauce, then a layer of chopped eggs mixed with onion, add salt and pepper to taste, then another layer of potatoes, and so on until all ingredients are used
4. For the sauce: beat one egg well and mix with one cup vinegar; place on heat and bring to a boil a couple of times, stirring; remove to cool
5. When completely cool, beat two thirds cup sour cream and mix with the vinegar
6. Use this sauce for the layered salad
