## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- two tablespoons cornmeal
- water
- one and a half cups sugar
- three egg yolks
- juice of three oranges
- grated zest of one orange
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. Bring one quart milk to a boil
2. Add two tablespoons cornmeal mixed with a little water
3. Add one and a half cups sugar and three beaten egg yolks
4. Cook for three minutes
5. Squeeze juice from three oranges and grate zest from one, then add
6. Mix well and place in a baking pan
7. Cover with thick meringue made from three egg whites
8. Put in the oven to brown
