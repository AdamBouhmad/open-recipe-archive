## Ingredients

- pike or sandacz
- various vegetables
- onion
- salt
- gelatin (one ounce for a fish weighing six to eight pounds)

## Directions

1. Prepare a court-bouillon (smak) from various vegetables and onion, then cool it.
2. Clean a pike or sandacz and place it in a long pan.
3. Pour the cold court-bouillon over it.
4. Cook until tender.
5. Remove and arrange on a platter.
6. Strain the remaining court-bouillon through dense cloth and cool it.
7. For a pike weighing six to eight pounds, take one ounce of gelatin.
8. Pour one quarter of the cold court-bouillon over the gelatin.
9. When it softens completely, drain off this liquid and place it on the fire.
10. When it is well heated, add the gelatin which will dissolve.
