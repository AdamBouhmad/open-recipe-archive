## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- eggs
- grated bread
- grated parmesan
- fat or butter for frying
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Remove cooked beef from broth and let cool slightly
2. Cut into even slices
3. Beat eggs, add some grated bread and grated parmesan, mix to fairly thin consistency
4. Dip sliced meat in this mixture, coat with grated bread mixed with parmesan
5. Fry in hot fat or butter until well browned on both sides
6. Arrange on platter and pour caper sauce over
