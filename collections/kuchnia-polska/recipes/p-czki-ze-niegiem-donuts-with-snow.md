## Ingredients

- one cup milk
- two cups flour
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one cup dry clean snow
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix one cup milk with two cups flour to make a thick smooth dough
2. Add one-half teaspoon salt and one cup dry clean snow and mix quickly with the dough
3. Heat plenty of lard in a pan until very hot
4. Using a spoon, drop dough into the hot lard and fry until the donuts brown well
5. Keep the bowl of dough in a cold place while frying
6. The snow makes the donuts very puffy, light, and better than any other method
