## Ingredients

- fat young chicken
- water
- mixed vegetables
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a fat young chicken as usual and place it in a pot large enough to contain it whole.
2. Add enough water to cover the chicken and add mixed vegetables.
3. Cook covered for about two hours on low heat until tender.
4. Prepare noodles (kluseczki), preferably the day before, by rolling them very thin and cutting diagonally.
5. Dry them on a sieve.
6. Cook the noodles in the chicken broth without skimming the fat.
7. After removing the chicken, cut it into pieces.
