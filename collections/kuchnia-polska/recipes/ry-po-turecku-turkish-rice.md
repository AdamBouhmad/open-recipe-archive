## Ingredients

- three cups strong beef broth
- three tomatoes
- one cup coarse rice
- one teaspoon salt
- one quarter teaspoon pepper
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Pour three cups of strong beef broth into a saucepan and bring to a boil
2. Chop three tomatoes into small pieces, add to the broth and cook for five minutes, then strain and pour the broth back into the saucepan
3. Rinse one cup of coarse rice and spread on a table or paper to dry, then add to the broth and cook on low heat until all the broth is absorbed
4. Add one teaspoon salt, one quarter teaspoon pepper and one tablespoon butter, mix well and serve at the table
