## Ingredients

- 1 quart beer
- 1/4 pound sugar
- small glass beer
- juice of 1 lemon
- handful of raisins without seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon powdered cinnamon
- a few whole pieces of cinnamon
- handful of grated rye bread
- 1 small glass red wine or 1 glass arak
- small handful bread cut into small cubes
- ice (optional)

## Directions

1. For four people, take 1 quart of beer. Put 1/4 pound of sugar in a bowl, pour a small glass of beer, squeeze the juice of 1 lemon.
2. Scald a handful of raisins without seeds with water, rinse, pour off the water and throw the raisins into the bowl. Also add 1/4 teaspoon of powdered cinnamon and a few pieces of whole cinnamon, a handful of grated rye bread and 1 small glass of red wine, or instead of wine 1 glass of arak.
3. Mix all this well in the bowl and add the rest of the beer, then place on ice.
4. Dry in an oven a handful of bread cut into small cubes and just before serving, throw into the bowl.
5. Note: To make the kalteszal colder, you can place a piece of ice in the bowl.
