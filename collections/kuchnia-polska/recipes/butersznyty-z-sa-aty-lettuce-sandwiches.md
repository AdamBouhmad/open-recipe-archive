## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- small lettuce leaves
- sauce made from egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut bread into very thin slices and spread with butter
2. Rinse small lettuce leaves carefully
3. Place on bread, pour sauce over, and cover with another slice of bread
