## Ingredients

- several dried mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook dried mushrooms until tender
2. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
3. Dilute with the cooking liquid from the mushrooms
4. Add vinegar to taste and finely chopped mushrooms
5. Boil and serve
