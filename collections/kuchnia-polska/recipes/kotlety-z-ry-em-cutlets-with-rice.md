## Ingredients

- cold roasted meat of any kind, one large onion, total two cups
- one cup cold cooked rice
- salt and pepper to taste
- egg for binding
- one beaten egg for coating
- grated bread or breadcrumbs for coating
- butter for frying
- tomato sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Chop cold roasted meat with one large onion until you have two cups total
2. Mix with one cup cold cooked rice
3. Add salt and pepper to taste
4. Add enough egg to make a fairly thick mixture
5. Place in a cold location for half an hour
6. Spread on a work surface and flatten with a rolling pin
7. Cut with a knife into cutlet shapes
8. Dip each cutlet in beaten egg
9. Coat with grated bread or breadcrumbs
10. Fry until well browned
11. Serve on the table with tomato sauce
