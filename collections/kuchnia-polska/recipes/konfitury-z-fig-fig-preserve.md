## Ingredients

- one pound of figs
- one cup of water
- half pound of sugar
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop the figs finely and place in a pot.
2. Pour one cup of water over the figs, add half pound of sugar and a little grated nutmeg.
3. Cook on a low fire until the mixture becomes thick.
4. Pour into a dish wetted with water and let cool.
5. Transfer to a tightly sealed jar for storage.
