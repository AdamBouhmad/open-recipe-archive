## Ingredients

- four bananas
- one tablespoon fine sugar
- two large oranges

## Directions

1. Peel four bananas and cut into pieces one inch long, arrange in a glass bowl, sprinkle with one tablespoon of fine sugar and then pour over with juice squeezed from two large oranges; set in a cold place for several hours.
2. It is best to prepare them in the evening for use the next day.
