## Ingredients

- dozen or so asparagus spears
- salted water
- four or five eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook a dozen or so asparagus spears in salted water until tender, about fifteen minutes
2. Cut the tender ends into small pieces
3. Beat four or five eggs and pour onto a frying pan in which one tablespoon of butter has been melted and place on low heat
4. When the eggs set, arrange the asparagus on top
5. Fold the omelet in half so that the asparagus are in the middle, and serve immediately
