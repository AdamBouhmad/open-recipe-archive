## Ingredients

- a few pounds of beef bones
- water
- several peppercorns
- a few bay leaves
- a few beets
- sour cream
- flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Pour water over a few pounds of beef bones, add several peppercorns and a few bay leaves, and cook for an hour and a half
2. Chop a few beets very finely, add to the broth and cook further until the beets are completely soft
3. Season with a cup of sour cream mixed with a small amount of flour, add vinegar to taste, bring to a boil and serve
4. This soup can be made equally well with smoked meat or ham
