## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- one large tablespoon of butter
- juice from four medium-sized oranges
- juice from one lemon
- peel of one orange
- sugar
- four egg whites
- sugar for beating

## Directions

1. Blanch and cook half a pound of rice until soft, with the addition of one large tablespoon of butter, then set aside to cool.
2. When completely cooled, squeeze into the rice the juice from four medium-sized oranges and one lemon.
3. Rub the peel of one orange against sugar, then pound it and add it, also add another quarter pound of sugar or more, depending on the sweetness of the oranges, then mix everything thoroughly.
4. Arrange on a platter or tin plate, pour foam from four egg whites beaten with sugar and bake in a gentle oven so that heat comes from above only.
5. When it browns well on top, serve to the table.
6. This proportion is for eight people.
