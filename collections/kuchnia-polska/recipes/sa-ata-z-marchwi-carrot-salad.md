## Ingredients

- about three carrots
- one boiled egg yolk
- one tablespoon of butter
- a few tablespoons of vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cook about three carrots in a large quantity of water until tender; when half cooked, drain the water and add fresh water
2. After cooking, remove them from water and cool completely
3. Chop into half-inch pieces and place in a salad bowl lined with lettuce leaves
4. Rub one cooked egg yolk through a sieve with one tablespoon of butter, add a few tablespoons of vinegar, salt and pepper to taste and pour this sauce over the carrots
