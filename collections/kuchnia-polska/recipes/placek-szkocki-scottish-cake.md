## Ingredients

- two cups sugar
- one cup butter
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Cream two cups sugar with one cup butter
2. Add enough flour as the butter will take and mix into a thick dough
3. Turn out onto a work surface and roll to one-half inch thickness
4. Cut with a knife into small squares
5. Prick each square with a fork
6. Bake in a moderately hot oven
