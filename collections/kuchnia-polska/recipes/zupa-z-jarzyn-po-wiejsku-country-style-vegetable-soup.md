## Ingredients

- carrots
- onions
- celery
- parsley
- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- cold water
- potatoes
- flour
- butter
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Take carrots, onions, celery, parsley and tomatoes, chop these vegetables
2. Add salt and pepper to taste, and also a bay leaf
3. Pour cold water over them, place on fire and cook twenty minutes from the moment of boiling
4. After twenty minutes, add finely chopped potatoes, cover and cook another twenty minutes on low heat
5. Then bind with flour browned in butter, sprinkle with finely chopped fresh parsley and serve
