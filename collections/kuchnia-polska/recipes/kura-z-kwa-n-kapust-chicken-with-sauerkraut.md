## Ingredients

- small young chicken
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- boiled potatoes
- slices of smoked bacon
- one quart of sauerkraut
- cold water

## Directions

1. Clean the small young chicken and dry the inside, then sprinkle the outside with salt, pepper and a good amount of flour.
2. Pound boiled potatoes into a mass and while hot stuff the chicken with them.
3. Take a deep pan with a tight cover (clay dishes are best), place a few slices of smoked bacon on the bottom, put the chicken on top and again a few slices of bacon on top.
4. Take one quart of sauerkraut, which must be fresh and not too sour, rinse it with cold water and place it on the chicken so that it is completely covered with cabbage.
5. Place in a moderately hot oven, pour in one cup of water and bake for three hours, watching carefully so it does not dry out, so water should be added from time to time.
6. When serving, place the chicken in the center of the platter and surround it with sauerkraut.
