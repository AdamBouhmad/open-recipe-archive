## Ingredients

- half quart of pickled tomatoes
- a few slices of onion
- half cupful of water
- a pinch of baking soda
- half quart of milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- one teaspoon of butter
- two tablespoons of soft cooked rice
- white bread
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Boil slowly for fifteen to twenty minutes: half quart of pickled tomatoes, a few slices of onion, and half cupful of water
2. Press through a colander and pour back into the pot
3. Add a pinch of baking soda
4. Bring half a quart of milk to a boil and pour gradually into the tomatoes, stirring constantly
5. Add salt and pepper to taste, one teaspoon of butter and two tablespoons of soft cooked rice
6. Boil for five minutes, then serve with croutons prepared as follows: chop a piece of white bread into half-inch cubes and fry in a pan with one teaspoon of hot foaming butter until all croutons are evenly browned
