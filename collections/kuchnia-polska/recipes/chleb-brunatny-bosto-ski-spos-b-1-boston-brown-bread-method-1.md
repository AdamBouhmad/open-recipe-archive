## Ingredients

- two cups of rye flour
- one cup of corn meal
- one teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of soda
- two cups of sour milk
- one-third cup of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix together two cups of rye flour, one cup of corn meal, one teaspoon of salt and one teaspoon of soda
2. Add two cups of sour milk and one-third cup of molasses
3. Mix into smooth dough, then cook on steam in a butter-greased form for three hours
4. Place in a hot oven for twenty minutes to brown
