## Ingredients

- thirty small crayfish
- salted water
- small amount of dill
- butter
- various vegetables for stock
- flour
- salt
- chopped dill
- rice
- filling

## Directions

1. Cook thirty small crayfish in salted water with a small amount of dill
2. Peel the tails and set aside, and also clean a dozen shells for later stuffing; the rest together with the shells, pound into a mass in a mortar
3. Place this mass with a piece of butter into a pot and stew for about twenty minutes
4. Have separately prepared a stock made from various vegetables
5. As soon as the crayfish mass takes on a red color, add a couple of tablespoons of flour, salt, and chopped dill
6. Add the vegetable stock, bring to a boil for about five minutes, then strain through a sieve and pour into a bowl in which crayfish shells are found, stuffed with the following filling: Mix cooked rice with butter and chopped dill into a thick mass, fry well over low heat without browning, and use this to fill the crayfish shells
