## Ingredients

- noodle squares made as above
- one medium head of cabbage
- one large white onion
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary noodle squares in salted water as described above
2. Peel cabbage of outer leaves, cut into quarters and parboil one medium head of cabbage and one large white onion in water
3. Chop both the cabbage and onion and stew in butter until tender
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Mix while hot with the cooked noodle squares
6. Grease a baking dish or mold generously with butter
7. Fill with the mixture of noodles and cabbage
8. Place in a moderately hot oven until it bakes and browns nicely
9. Transfer to a serving platter or bowl and serve on the table
