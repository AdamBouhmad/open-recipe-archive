## Ingredients

- three egg yolks
- one quart milk
- one-half cup melted butter
- two teaspoons baking powder
- flour as needed
- egg whites beaten to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Beat three egg yolks with one quart milk, one-half cup melted butter, two teaspoons baking powder, and enough flour to make a thin batter
2. Fold in egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
3. Bake on a well-greased waffle iron
