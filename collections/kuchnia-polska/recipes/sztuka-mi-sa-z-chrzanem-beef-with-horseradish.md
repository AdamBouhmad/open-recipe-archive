## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- horseradish sauce
- grated bread
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut cooked beef into slices and arrange on a platter
2. Pour horseradish sauce over it
3. Sprinkle lightly with grated bread, drizzle with melted butter
4. Place in oven for ten minutes
5. When browned on top, remove and serve immediately
