## Ingredients

- eggs
- mayonnaise sauce (thicker and more salted than that used for fish)
- one green plum, cut in half

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs for at least five minutes.
2. Plunge into cold water.
3. Once completely cooled, shell and cut each egg in half with a sharp knife.
4. Prepare a mayonnaise sauce, thicker and more salted than that used for fish.
5. Dip each egg half in the mayonnaise sauce.
6. Arrange neatly on a plate with a halved green plum in the center.
7. Serve for breakfast or as a snack.
