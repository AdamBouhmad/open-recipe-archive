## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- vinegar
- parsley root
- celery stalks
- pickled cucumbers
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- English herbs
- red wine
- cucumber pickle

## Directions

1. Clean the carp, salt it and pour strong vinegar over it.
2. For a three-pound fish, take two large parsley roots, a couple of celery stalks, two pickled cucumbers, chop everything into small pieces and place together with the carp in a pot.
3. Add a bay leaf, several peppercorns and English herbs.
4. Mix red wine half and half with the brine from the cucumbers, pour over the carp and place on low heat to cook slowly covered.
5. When the fish is tender, remove it, arrange on a serving dish and pour the sauce remaining in the pot over it.
6. The carp should be cooked whole; some cook it with the scales, claiming that cooked this way it is more delicious.
