## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped green parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat 3 tablespoons butter into cream in a mortar
2. Add 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped green parsley, 1/2 teaspoon salt and some pepper
3. Mix everything well
4. This sauce is used for fish, best boiled fish, in the following way: Place the boiled fish on a platter and spread this sauce thickly over the top
5. Serve immediately
