## Ingredients

- three large herring
- small potatoes
- chives

## Directions

1. Soak, clean and slice three large herring into rings
2. Cook small potatoes, peel and slice them
3. On a wide dish, arrange alternating rows of herring and potatoes so that potatoes are at the edges
4. Arrange rows in this order: potatoes, herring, potatoes, herring, potatoes, herring, potatoes - four rows of potatoes and three rows of herring
5. Arrange potato slices so they overlap, arrange herring rings next to each other to form a complete row
6. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle potatoes with finely chopped chives
