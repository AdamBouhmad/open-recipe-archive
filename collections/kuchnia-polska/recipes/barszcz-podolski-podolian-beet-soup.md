## Ingredients

- beef meat, bones, or smoked meat
- a few onions
- bay leaf
- several peppercorns
- a few beets
- half a head of cabbage
- sour cream
- flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Set meat, bones or smoked meat as for broth, add a few onions, a bay leaf, and several peppercorns, and cook for a good hour
2. Chop a few beets and cut not very finely half a head of cabbage, add to the soup and cook as long as the cabbage and beets are completely soft
3. Season with a cup of sour cream mixed with a small amount of flour, add vinegar to taste and serve
