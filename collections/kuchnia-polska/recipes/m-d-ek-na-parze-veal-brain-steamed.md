## Ingredients

- veal brain, one pound
- cold water
- salt
- three eggs
- butter, one teaspoon
- milk, one tablespoon
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of veal brain for twenty minutes in cold water, remove and peel off the membrane, then place in salted boiling water and cook for ten minutes.
2. After cooking remove and place again in cold water, and when completely cooled, break into small pieces.
3. Beat well three eggs and mix with the brain, also add one teaspoon of butter, one tablespoon of milk, salt and pepper to taste, then place on heat and braise until the eggs are well set.
