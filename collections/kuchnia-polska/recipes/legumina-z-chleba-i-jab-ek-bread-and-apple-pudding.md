## Ingredients

- half a small loaf of white bread
- butter
- several sour apples
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut half a small loaf of white bread into thin slices and spread thinly with butter
2. Peel, remove seeds, and cut several sour apples into small pieces
3. Take a baking tin and line it with bread
4. Place a layer of apples on top, then sprinkle with sugar
5. Continue layering until the pan is full
6. Put into the oven and bake until the apples are completely soft
7. Serve usually with cream separately
