## Ingredients

- half a quart of milk
- half a cup of sugar
- three heaping tablespoons of chocolate
- one heaping tablespoon of cornstarch
- cold water
- cream flavored with vanilla or vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Heat half a quart of milk in a double boiler, then add half a cup of sugar.
2. Grate three heaping tablespoons of chocolate, mix with one heaping tablespoon of cornstarch, and dissolve in a little cold water into a smooth paste.
3. Pour this mixture into the boiling milk and cook together for five minutes.
4. Pour into a tin mold (or can be left in the pot) and place on ice.
5. When very cold, run a knife around the edges and place the mold in hot water for half a minute so the mixture pulls away from the bottom.
6. Turn out onto a glass serving dish.
7. Serve with cream flavored with vanilla or vanilla extract, which can be poured around the cream in the dish or served separately.
