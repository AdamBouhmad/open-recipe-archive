## Ingredients

- veal liver
- milk
- lard bacon
- butter
- vinegar
- one large apple
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in milk, remove the membrane and lard it as described above; sprinkle with vinegar and let it rest for an hour.
2. Melt a spoonful of butter in a pan, and when it begins to brown, salt the liver, place it in the butter and braise until the liver browns.
3. Then grate one large apple and add it to the liver, along with a teaspoonful of sugar, and continue braising, watching carefully so it does not burn, as the whole liver would burn.
4. As soon as it is tender, serve to the table.
