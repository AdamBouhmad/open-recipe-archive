## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- vegetables
- vinegar
- spices
- juniper berries
- one onion
- one small carrot
- ham piece
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- celery stalk
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Marinate lamb loin for four days, adding to the marinade in addition to vegetables, vinegar and spices, also some juniper berries.
2. After removing from the marinade, roast the roast as usual, slice into thin slices, arrange on a serving platter and pour with the following sauce:
3. Finely chop one onion, one small carrot, a piece of ham, add a bay leaf, several peppercorns and a celery stalk; brown all this in butter, then add two tablespoons vinegar, some broth and braise on low heat until tender.
4. Then strain through a sieve, pour into the sauce that remained on the roasting pan from the lamb roast and boil once more.
5. This sauce is called sharp; to have a wild sauce, a few tablespoons of sour cream should be added to it.
