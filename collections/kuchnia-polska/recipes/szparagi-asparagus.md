## Ingredients

- asparagus
- butter
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus and break off the tough ends
2. Tie them in bundles of eight or ten pieces
3. Throw them into a large pot with hot water
4. When it boils, drain the water and pour in fresh hot water
5. Cook until tender
6. Remove, untie, arrange on a serving dish
7. Pour the tips with butter browned with bread roll
8. Serve good melted butter separately on the side
9. Important: do not overcook the asparagus as it will absorb water and become unpalatable
10. Avoid using wilted asparagus, though it can be refreshed by burying it in earth for two feet; when removed after fifteen or twenty hours it will be fresh again
