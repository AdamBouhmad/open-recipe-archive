## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup finely chopped cooked ham
- 2 small tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon chopped green parsley
- white bread

## Directions

1. Mix the ham, butter, and chopped parsley into a smooth mass
2. Cut white bread into thin slices of any shape
3. Fry lightly in butter until golden
4. Spread the ham mixture on the fried bread
