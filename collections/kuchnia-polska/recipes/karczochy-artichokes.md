## Ingredients

- water
- butter
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil, then drain this water
2. Pour in fresh hot water and cook until tender
3. Serve hot with melted butter, or serve cold with vinegar and oil
