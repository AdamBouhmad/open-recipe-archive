## Ingredients

- tench, pike or pike-perch
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion
- mushrooms
- Italian pasta
- water

## Directions

1. For this dish, tench, pike or pike-perch is best. After cleaning the fish, cut it into pieces, remove all bones, salt and pepper it at least an hour before use.
2. Place a spoonful of butter on the bottom of a pot.
3. On top, place thinly sliced onion that has been previously scalded with boiling water, a few cooked mushrooms cut into thin slices.
4. Place the fish on top, then add Italian pasta, broken into small pieces and cooked in salted water.
5. Add a little more butter on top.
6. Cover tightly and braise for no more than half an hour, frequently shaking the pot.
7. Instead of pasta, chopped potatoes can be used, and the taste will be different but equally good.
