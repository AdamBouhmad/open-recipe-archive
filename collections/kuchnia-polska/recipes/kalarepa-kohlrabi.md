## Ingredients

- young kohlrabi
- water
- butter
- butter
- flour
- salt to taste
- a little sugar
- fresh chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut young kohlrabi into slices, cover with water and bring to a boil, then drain the water, add a little butter and braise until tender, then bring to a boil a little butter with flour on white, add salt to taste, a little sugar and fresh chopped parsley and serve on the table.
