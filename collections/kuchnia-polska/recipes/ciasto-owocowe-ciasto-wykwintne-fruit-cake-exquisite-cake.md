## Ingredients

- one pound butter
- one pound and quarter dark brown sugar
- one pound flour
- one pound citron
- half pound orange peel candied in sugar
- half pound lemon peel candied in sugar
- six pounds raisins without seeds
- two pounds currants
- three tablespoons cinnamon
- two tablespoons mace
- two tablespoons nutmeg
- one tablespoon Jamaican ginger (allspice)
- one teaspoon cloves
- one cup molasses
- half quart brandy
- twelve eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare and bake according to the recipe for White Fruit Cake above
2. This proportion is for an enormous wedding cake weighing several pounds
