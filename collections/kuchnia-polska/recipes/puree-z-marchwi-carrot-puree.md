## Ingredients

- peeled and cooked carrots
- milk
- salt
- flour
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pass peeled and cooked-until-tender carrots through a sieve.
2. For half a quart of this puree take a cup of milk and some salt.
3. Mix and place on the fire; when it begins to boil, add two tablespoons of flour mixed with a small amount of cold milk.
4. Cook for several minutes, constantly stirring.
5. At the end add a teaspoon of butter and a pinch of pepper and serve.
