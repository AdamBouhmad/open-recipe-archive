## Ingredients

- half a quarter of cold boiled potatoes
- half a cup of grated cheese
- one cup white sauce
- half a cup grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut into half-inch cubes half a quarter of cold potatoes boiled in salted water
2. Pour into a flat dish, preferably a clay or earthenware bowl
3. Sprinkle half a cup of grated cheese on top
4. Cover with one cup of white sauce
5. Sprinkle half a cup of grated breadcrumbs on top
6. Place in a moderately hot oven and bake until the breadcrumbs are well browned
7. For the white sauce: boil one cup of milk in a double boiler; in another pan melt one tablespoon of butter until it begins to foam, then add one tablespoon of cornstarch and mix well, then pour one third cup of the boiled milk into the butter and mix to a smooth paste; add the remaining milk, stirring constantly until a smooth mass without lumps is obtained; season the sauce as desired with onion juice, parsley, mushrooms, curry powder and any other seasonings desired
