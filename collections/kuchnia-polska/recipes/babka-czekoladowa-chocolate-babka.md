## Ingredients

- one cup brown sugar
- one cup grated chocolate
- one cup milk
- one cup sugar
- half cup butter
- two eggs
- half cup sweet milk
- one teaspoon soda
- two cups flour
- one teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Boil together one cup of brown sugar, one cup of grated chocolate, and one cup of milk, then cool
2. Separately, beat one cup of sugar with half cup of butter, add two eggs, half cup of sweet milk, one teaspoon of soda, two cups of flour, and one teaspoon of vanilla extract
3. Mix, then add the cooled milk with sugar and chocolate, mix everything again
4. Fill the form and bake
