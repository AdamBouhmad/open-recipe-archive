## Ingredients

- cooked tripe
- salt
- pepper
- two eggs
- two tablespoons flour
- one cup milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the tripe until tender and cut into pieces about three inches long and wide. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Beat two eggs in a bowl, add two tablespoons flour, one cup milk and a pinch of salt to create a thin batter.
3. Dip the cut tripe pieces in this batter and fry in butter in a pan until golden brown.
4. Serve immediately at the table.
