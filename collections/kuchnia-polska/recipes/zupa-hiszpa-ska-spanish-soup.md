## Ingredients

- 1 pound white beans
- 1 small head white cabbage
- smoked meat (wędzonka)
- 1 red pepper pod
- salt to taste
- lard (smalec)
- 1 onion
- 1 clove garlic

## Directions

1. Soak 1 pound of white beans overnight
2. The next day, finely chop a small head of white cabbage, some smoked meat, and one red pepper pod
3. Add to the beans and cook for one hour
4. Add salt to taste
5. Melt some lard in a pan and fry one finely chopped onion
6. Add slowly to the soup while constantly stirring
7. Add one crushed clove of garlic, bring to a boil and serve
8. Garlic can be omitted
