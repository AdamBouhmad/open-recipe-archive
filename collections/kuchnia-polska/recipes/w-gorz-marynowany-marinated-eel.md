## Ingredients

- eel
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- roots and herbs
- white wine
- vinegar
- parsley
- chives
- dill
- vinegar and oil

## Directions

1. Cook stock from miscellaneous vegetables and roots, then strain and pour over the eel cut into pieces and arranged in a pan; there should be only enough stock to barely cover the fish.
2. Place on heat and cook until completely tender; during cooking add one glass of white wine and half a glass of vinegar.
3. Arrange the cooked eel in a stone jar and cover with the stock.
4. When serving at the table, sprinkle with chopped parsley, chives or dill and pour the sauce over it, and serve vinegar and oil on the side.
