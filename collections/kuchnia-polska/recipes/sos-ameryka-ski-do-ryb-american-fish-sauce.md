## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- one cup of water
- one tablespoon of flour
- cold water
- two eggs
- salt
- pepper
- two hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of milk with one cup of water and boil
2. Then add one tablespoon of flour previously dissolved in a small amount of cold water
3. Beat two eggs and also add to the sauce
4. Salt and pepper to taste and remove from heat
5. Slice two hard-boiled eggs thinly
6. Arrange the boiled fish on a platter, sprinkle with the sliced eggs, and then pour the sauce over it
