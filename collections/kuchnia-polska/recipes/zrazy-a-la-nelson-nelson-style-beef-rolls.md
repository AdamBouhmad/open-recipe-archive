## Ingredients

- 1 pound sirloin, thinly sliced
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion, blanched and thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Beat the beef slices well and season with salt and pepper.
2. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a wide pan until browned.
3. Layer the beef slices, then sprinkle with the blanched onion slices.
4. Repeat with another layer of beef and onion, then add a bit more butter on top.
5. Cover tightly and braise, shaking the pan frequently.
6. Use fairly high heat, as the main requirement is to braise for no longer than twenty minutes, after which they should be tender.
7. If desired, partially boiled potatoes cut into slices can be braised with the rolls.
8. The addition of mushrooms braised together with the meat makes the dish considerably better.
