## Ingredients

- half a quart of sour cream
- two tablespoons of sugar
- a small piece of vanilla or some vanilla extract
- six egg yolks
- two tablespoons of sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil half a quart of sour cream with two tablespoons of sugar and vanilla or vanilla extract
2. Beat six egg yolks and two tablespoons of sour cream together strongly
3. Add to the boiled sour cream and warm together over very low heat, constantly stirring, until it begins to thicken
4. This sauce is used for cold legumes
