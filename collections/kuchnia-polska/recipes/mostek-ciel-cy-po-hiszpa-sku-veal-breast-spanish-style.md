## Ingredients

- veal breast, about three pounds
- white bread, one pound
- marinated tomatoes, half a quart
- onion, one tablespoon finely chopped
- celery, one tablespoon chopped
- salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Take a veal breast weighing about three pounds and make a pocket between the ribs and meat for stuffing.
2. Remove all crust from a pound of white bread and crumble and soak the center in half a quart of marinated tomatoes.
3. Add one tablespoon of finely chopped onion, one tablespoon of chopped celery, salt to taste and a bit of red pepper; mix everything well.
4. Stuff the veal with this stuffing and roast in a hot oven.
5. Roasting should take two hours.
