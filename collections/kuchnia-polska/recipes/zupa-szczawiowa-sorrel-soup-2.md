## Ingredients

- broth made on beef
- sorrel, enough to make the soup sour
- butter
- sour cream or sour cream mixed with flour
- soft-boiled eggs in shells (optional)
- hard-boiled eggs (optional)
- stuffed eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook broth on beef in ordinary manner
2. Chop enough sorrel to make the soup sour to taste
3. Stew the sorrel first in butter, then thin it with broth
4. Season with sour cream, either fried or mixed with flour
5. Bring to a boil and serve on the table
6. The best additions to this soup are soft-boiled eggs in shells, which can be put in the soup and stirred in it, hard-boiled eggs, or stuffed eggs
