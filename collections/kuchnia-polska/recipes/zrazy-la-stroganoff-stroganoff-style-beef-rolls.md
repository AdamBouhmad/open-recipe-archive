## Ingredients

- tenderloin or tender beef
- salt
- butter
- flour
- onion
- sour cream
- paprika
- soy sauce
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices, salt and fry in butter
2. Put a piece of butter in a pan, add a little flour and finely minced onion and brown
3. Pour in a few tablespoons sour cream, add a little paprika and one tablespoon soy sauce
4. Arrange the fried rolls on toast slices on a platter and pour the above sauce over them
5. Be careful not to over-fry the rolls
