## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- one medium onion, finely chopped
- previously cooked broth
- one spoon of flour
- water
- salt and pepper to taste
- stale bread

## Directions

1. Heat one spoon of butter in a pan and add to it one medium onion, finely chopped, then fry, stirring constantly, so as not to burn, until it is well browned
2. Add to previously cooked broth and cook for fifteen minutes
3. Mix one spoon of flour with water so smoothly that there are no lumps, pour into the soup and cook for another five minutes
4. Add salt and pepper to taste; chop stale bread into small cubes, brown them well in an oven, pour into a bowl, pour the soup and serve immediately
