## Ingredients

- leftover cold roasted veal
- lettuce leaves
- French sauce or mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Take leftover cold roasted veal and carefully remove all fat and browned skin
2. Chop the white meat into small cubes and arrange on a salad bowl lined with lettuce leaves
3. Pour French sauce made of oil, vinegar, salt and pepper, or mayonnaise sauce over it
