## Ingredients

- salad greens
- one egg
- one tablespoon of strong vinegar
- two tablespoons of oil
- half teaspoon of sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Sort and wash the salad greens carefully
2. Drain of water and place in a napkin, then holding by the four corners, shake the napkin vigorously so all moisture remains on it
3. Hard-boil one egg and remove the yolk into a small bowl
4. Add one tablespoon of strong vinegar, two tablespoons of oil, half teaspoon of sugar and some salt
5. Mix this thoroughly with a fork
6. Pour this sauce over the salad, mix well and let stand quietly for ten minutes
7. Then serve on the table, making sure all salad leaves are covered with sauce
