## Ingredients

- sour apples with skin
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut sour apples with skin, remove seeds, cover with enough water to barely cover them
2. Cook until completely softened
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add some sugar and serve
5. If the sauce is too thin, add a little water
6. This sauce is served with schnitzel, pork or lamb cutlets, ducks and geese
