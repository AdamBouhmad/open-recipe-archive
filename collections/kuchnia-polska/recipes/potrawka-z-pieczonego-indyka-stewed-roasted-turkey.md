## Ingredients

- leftover roasted turkey
- butter
- one cup broth or bouillon
- half a lemon
- pepper
- powdered ginger
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Slice the remaining roasted turkey into pieces
2. Place in a saucepan with one spoon of butter, one cup of broth or bouillon, squeeze half a lemon, add a little pepper and some powdered ginger, bring to a strong boil
3. Then add the meat and a handful of grated bread, heat well and serve
