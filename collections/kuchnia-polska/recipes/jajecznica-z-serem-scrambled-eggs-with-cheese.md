## Ingredients

- cheese
- one teaspoon strong mustard
- one cup milk
- one teaspoon salt
- a bit of red pepper
- five eggs

## Directions

1. Slice cheese thinly and line the bottom and sides of a baking dish with it
2. Mix in a cup of milk one teaspoon of strong mustard with one teaspoon salt and a bit of red pepper, and pour half of this mixture over the arranged cheese
3. Carefully break five eggs so as not to disturb the yolks and pour over the cheese, then pour the rest of the milk mixture on top
4. Place in the oven for about ten minutes; when the eggs are set, remove and serve immediately at the table
