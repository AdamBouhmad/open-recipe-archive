## Ingredients

- one small can of sardines in oil
- celery stalks
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- one cup of milk
- half tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Take one small can of sardines in oil, remove the fish and lay them on blotting paper, turning them so that all the oil is absorbed
2. Chop the celery stalks into cubes and take twice as much as the amount of sardines
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and pour vinegar over it
4. Arrange the prepared celery in the middle of a salad bowl, preferably glass, in the form of a pyramid, and arrange the sardines around the celery
5. Heat one cup of milk, season it with half a tablespoon of flour, pepper and salt, remove from heat and cool
