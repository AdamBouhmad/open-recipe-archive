## Ingredients

- roasted chicken from previous day (or boiled chicken)
- chicken meat, skinned and boned
- raw ham, cut into small pieces with fat
- butter
- chopped green parsley
- pepper
- cooked rice
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the chicken meat very finely.
2. For each cup of chicken meat, take half a cup of raw ham cut into small pieces with fat.
3. Fry the ham with a spoon of butter for a few minutes.
4. Add the chopped chicken meat, a teaspoon of chopped green parsley, and some pepper.
5. When everything is well mixed, add two large cups of cooked rice.
6. Keep on heat for a moment while stirring with a spoon until well heated.
7. Transfer to a platter and sprinkle with a small amount of lemon juice.
