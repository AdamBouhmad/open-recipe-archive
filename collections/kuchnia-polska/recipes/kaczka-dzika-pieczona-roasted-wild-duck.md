## Ingredients

- 1 wild duck
- vinegar
- roots
- butter

## Directions

1. To make the wild duck tender and soft, soak it for a couple of hours in vinegar that has been boiled with roots and then cooled
2. Roast, basting with butter, like any other game
3. You can also lard it with bacon
