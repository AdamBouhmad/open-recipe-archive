## Ingredients

- mutton drippings and skin
- lean mutton meat
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs
- cooked Italian pasta
- tomato sauce (catsup)

## Directions

1. Trim all fat and browned skin from leftover roasted mutton.
2. Cut the lean meat into thin slices and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Butter a small roasting pan or cake form.
4. Place a layer of bread crumbs on the bottom.
5. Add a layer of meat and a layer of cooked Italian pasta cut into one-inch pieces.
6. Pour one tablespoon of tomato sauce (catsup) until the dish is filled.
7. Sprinkle everything with bread crumbs to a depth of at least one quarter-inch.
8. Pour two tablespoons of melted butter over top.
9. Place in a fairly hot oven for half an hour.
