## Ingredients

- calf liver
- one roll soaked in milk for one hour
- three eggs
- one dry roll grated finely
- one large onion, parboiled and finely chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Peel the calf liver of membranes, carefully remove veins, chop very finely and pass through a sieve.
2. Soak one roll in milk for one hour, beat in three eggs, add one dry roll grated finely, and one large parboiled onion finely chopped, and salt and pepper to taste.
3. Mix everything properly with the liver and form oblong dumplings by hand.
4. Cook in a wide pot, remove with a slotted spoon, place carefully on a serving platter and pour with browned butter with onion.
5. Such dumplings can also be added to broth.
