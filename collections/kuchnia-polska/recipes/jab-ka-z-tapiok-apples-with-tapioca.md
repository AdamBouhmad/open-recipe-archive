## Ingredients

- one cup pearl tapioca
- cold water
- six medium apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of pearl tapioca in cold water overnight.
2. The next morning, peel and cut into quarters six medium apples and place in a butter-greased baking dish, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and then pour the tapioca on top, sprinkle again with sugar and scatter a few small pieces of butter on top, pour a little water and place in a moderately hot oven for one hour.
3. Serve with sugar, and you can also serve it with sour cream.
