## Ingredients

- one pound Italian pasta
- salt
- one quarter pound cooked ham
- butter
- sour cream
- cheese

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of Italian pasta in salted water until tender.
2. Drain the water and place the pasta in a serving bowl.
3. Cut or chop one quarter pound of cooked ham into small pieces.
4. Add one tablespoon of butter and three tablespoons of thick sour cream.
5. Add a couple of tablespoons of grated cheese.
6. Mix and place the entire mixture in a buttered cake form or roasting pan.
7. Place in the oven for twenty minutes.
