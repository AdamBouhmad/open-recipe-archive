## Ingredients

- Strong beef broth
- Caramel (burnt sugar)
- One whole egg
- One egg yolk
- Two tablespoons of cream
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook strong broth on beef and strain
2. Color with caramel (burnt sugar)
3. Beat one whole egg and one yolk with two tablespoons of cream
4. Add salt and a pinch of nutmeg
5. Pour into a mold or cup greased with butter
6. Place the mold in a bain-marie with hot water
7. Cook in the bain-marie
8. After cooking and cooling, turn out of the mold
9. Cut into cubes or slices
10. Place in a bowl and pour broth over it
11. Meatballs can also be added to this soup
