## Ingredients

- mealy potatoes
- salted water
- cold water
- one cup milk
- salt
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cook mealy potatoes in salted water
2. Cover the potatoes with cold water, never hot
3. After cooking drain the water and place the potatoes on the heat for a few minutes to dry
4. Then rub them to a paste
5. For each quart of such potatoes heat one cup of milk with salt and one tablespoon of butter
6. When the milk is hot, but should not boil, pour it over the potatoes and beat strongly with a spoon for at least three minutes until the potatoes become well white
7. Then serve at table
8. The method above is American; the European method differs in that instead of milk sour cream is used, not heated, and the pot with potatoes during beating is placed in another pot filled with hot water
