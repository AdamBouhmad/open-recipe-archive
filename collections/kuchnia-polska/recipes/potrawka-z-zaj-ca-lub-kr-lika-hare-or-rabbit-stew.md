## Ingredients

- 1 hare or rabbit
- water
- salt
- pepper
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Quarter the hare or rabbit and cook until soft in enough water to barely cover the meat, with the addition of salt, pepper, and one onion sliced into pieces
2. When the meat is soft, place on a platter; to the sauce remaining in the pot add 1 cup of milk with 1 tablespoon of butter, bring to a boil for about three minutes, and pour this sauce over the hare on the platter
