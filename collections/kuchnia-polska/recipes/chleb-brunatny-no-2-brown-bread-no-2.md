## Ingredients

- half a cup of wheat flour
- one and a half cups of graham flour
- one cup of corn meal
- two teaspoons of salt
- two teaspoons of soda
- one cup of sour milk
- one cup of sweet milk
- one cup of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix together the wheat flour, graham flour, corn meal, salt and soda
2. Add one cup of sour milk, one cup of sweet milk and one cup of molasses
3. Mix into dough, cook on steam for two and a half hours
4. Place in a hot oven for fifteen to twenty minutes to brown
