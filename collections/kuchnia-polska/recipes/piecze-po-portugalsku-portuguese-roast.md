## Ingredients

- a piece of tender beef
- various vegetables, cut into cubes
- several potatoes
- a few sliced tomatoes
- some green peas
- broth

## Directions

1. Brown the piece of tender beef on all sides in a roasting pan
2. Add various vegetables cut into cubes, several potatoes, a few sliced tomatoes, and some green peas
3. Pour broth over and braise on low heat until tender
4. When the meat is tender, remove from the pan, slice it, arrange on a platter and pour the remaining sauce with vegetables over it
