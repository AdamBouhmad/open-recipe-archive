## Ingredients

- boiled and cooled potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut boiled and cooled potatoes into thin slices
2. Pour into a shallow pot
3. Add some butter, salt and pepper to taste
4. Cover with enough milk to barely cover them
5. Cook on low heat until the potatoes have absorbed all the milk, then serve
