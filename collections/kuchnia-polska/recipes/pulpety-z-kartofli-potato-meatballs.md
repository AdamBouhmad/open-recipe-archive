## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes boiled in salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- one beaten egg yolk
- fresh parsley, finely chopped
- beaten egg
- grated bread crumbs
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Mash cooked potatoes that were boiled in salted water while they are still hot
2. For every half-quart of potatoes, add one tablespoon butter, half a teaspoon salt, some pepper, and some celery salt
3. Mix everything well and set aside to cool
4. When the mass has cooled, add one beaten egg yolk and mix well in a bowl
5. Add one teaspoon of finely chopped parsley and mix
6. Form the mass into balls or small sticks
7. Dip in beaten egg
8. Coat with grated bread crumbs and fry in hot fat
9. Fry for only one minute
10. Arrange on a serving dish in the shape of a pyramid and serve immediately
