## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- one tablespoon sugar
- quarter teaspoon salt
- one cup milk
- three beaten eggs
- butter for frying
- jam or preserves for filling
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour with one tablespoon of sugar and a quarter teaspoon of salt.
2. Slowly add one cup of milk and three beaten eggs, then beat vigorously for at least five minutes.
3. Fry on hot buttered pan.
4. Spread generously with jam or preserves, roll into a tube, sprinkle with sugar, and serve immediately.
