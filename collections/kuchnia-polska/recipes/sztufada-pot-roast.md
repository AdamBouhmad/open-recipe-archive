## Ingredients

- 4 to 6 pounds roasting meat
- bacon, coarsely chopped
- butter
- carrots, chopped
- onions, chopped
- black peppercorns
- bay leaves
- 1 glass white wine or a little clear vodka

## Directions

1. Lard a 4 to 6 pound piece of roasting meat generously with coarsely chopped bacon.
2. Place it in a pot with hot melted butter.
3. Turn the meat on all sides over high heat to brown it well on all sides.
4. When well browned, add chopped carrots, onions, a few peppercorns, and a few bay leaves.
5. Add 1 glass of white wine or a little clear vodka.
6. Cover and braise on very low heat for 3 to 4 hours; the heat must be so low that the meat barely simmers.
7. Before serving, dust the roast with flour, turn it a few times in the sauce to thicken it, then place on a platter and pour the strained sauce over it.
