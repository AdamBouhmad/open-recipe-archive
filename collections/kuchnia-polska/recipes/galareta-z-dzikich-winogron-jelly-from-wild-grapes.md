## Ingredients

- wild grapes
- sugar in the proportion of half a quart per quart of juice

## Directions

1. Gather grapes when they begin to ripen
2. Crush and cook them into a mass, place in a bag and hang it overnight over a bowl so all the juice drips out
3. The next day, press out the remaining juice, pour it into a pot and boil for half an hour, frequently skimming
4. Add half a quart of sugar for each quart of juice and boil until thick
5. Pour into glasses or jars
