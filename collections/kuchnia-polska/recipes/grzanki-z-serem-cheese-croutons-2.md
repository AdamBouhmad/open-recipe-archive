## Ingredients

- bread or bread roll without crust
- butter
- grated cheese (green, Swiss or Parmesan)

## Directions

1. Cut bread or bread roll without crust into small, very thin slices
2. Spread each slice well with butter and sprinkle with grated cheese (green, Swiss or Parmesan)
3. Place in a moderately hot oven for about ten minutes
4. When the croutons are well dried, remove and serve with soup
