## Ingredients

- calf's head with skin
- beef loin, finely chopped
- wheat flour
- onions
- various vegetables, finely chopped
- clove
- salt and pepper to taste
- vinegar
- nutmeg flower
- parsley sprigs
- tartar sauce or vinegar with olive oil

## Directions

1. After cleaning the calf's head, which should have skin, split it into two halves
2. Remove the brain, tongue and bones
3. Cut each half into three parts so that the ear forms a square
4. Place in a large pot, pour full of water and cook for twenty minutes on high heat, constantly skimming
5. Drain the water and rinse with cold water several times
6. In the same pot melt half pound of finely chopped beef loin on low heat so it does not fry
7. Add half pound of wheat flour, add several onions and various finely chopped vegetables, one clove, salt and pepper to taste, one tablespoon of vinegar and a little nutmeg flower
8. Mix all this together and place on fire, and when it comes to a boil, throw in the cut calf's head
9. Cover with paper so it does not blacken and cook on low heat for two hours
10. When the meat is completely tender, remove and arrange on a serving platter in the following manner: Place the tongue cut lengthwise in half in the center; place the brains separately cooked and laced in a nicely cleaned skull; arrange the ears at the corners of the platter, and cover everything with sprigs of green parsley and pour with tartar sauce or vinegar with olive oil
