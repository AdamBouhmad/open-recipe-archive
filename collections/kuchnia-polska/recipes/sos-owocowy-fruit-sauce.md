## Ingredients

- one glass of any fruit juice
- half a glass of red wine
- one tablespoon of potato starch or cornstarch
- half a glass of water

## Directions

1. Take one glass of any fruit juice and half a glass of red wine
2. Add one tablespoon of potato starch or cornstarch dissolved in half a glass of water
3. Mix well and boil over low heat, constantly stirring
4. This sauce is for legumes
