## Ingredients

- butter
- onion, finely chopped
- pepper
- green parsley, chopped
- green bell pepper, chopped
- six eggs

## Directions

1. This dish is best prepared in a small earthenware dish, but a pot can also be used
2. Melt one tablespoon of butter, add finely chopped onion, some pepper, one teaspoon chopped green parsley and one tablespoon chopped green bell pepper
3. Fry this mixture on low heat for a few minutes
4. Then carefully crack six eggs so as not to damage the yolks
5. As soon as the eggs are fried on one side, flip them to the other side
6. Serve on the table in the same dish in which the eggs were fried
