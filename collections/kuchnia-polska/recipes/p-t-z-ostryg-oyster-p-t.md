## Ingredients

- half a quart of oysters
- one cup of milk
- one cup of cold milk
- one cup of grated bread
- one tablespoon of butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Bring half a quart of oysters to a boil with one cup of milk
2. Mix one cup of cold milk with one cup of grated bread
3. Pour this mixture into the oysters as soon as they come to a boil
4. Stir and cook for two minutes
5. Remove from heat and add one tablespoon of butter and some pepper
6. Pour into a buttered cake mold and bake in the oven until well browned
