## Ingredients

- 1/2 quart milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon corn starch
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Heat 1/2 quart milk
2. Beat 1 egg with 1/2 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon corn starch and add to the milk
3. Cook for a while, constantly stirring, until thickened
4. Add vanilla extract to taste
5. This sauce is used for legumes
