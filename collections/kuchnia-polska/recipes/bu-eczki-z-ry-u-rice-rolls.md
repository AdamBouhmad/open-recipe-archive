## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- pinch of salt
- 0.5 cup softly cooked cold rice
- 1 heaping tablespoon butter
- 1 beaten egg

## Directions

1. Mix 1.5 cups wheat flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 1 teaspoon sugar, and a pinch of salt and sift through a sieve to mix well.
2. Take 0.5 cup of softly cooked cold rice and mix with 1 heaping tablespoon of butter (previously melted on the fire).
3. Add 1 beaten egg and mix all with the sifted flour into smooth dough.
4. Shape into small rolls and bake in the oven, preferably in small metal forms, for about half an hour.
