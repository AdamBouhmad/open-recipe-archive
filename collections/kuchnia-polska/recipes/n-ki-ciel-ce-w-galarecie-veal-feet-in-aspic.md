## Ingredients

- veal feet
- water
- vegetables
- beef fat
- pepper
- salt
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Carefully clean the veal feet, remove the thick bones, cover with water in a pot and bring to a boil, then immediately drain the water.
2. Rinse the feet, place back in the pot, cover with soft warm water, add various vegetables, a piece of beef fat and pepper, then cook with constant skimming for three to four hours; do not add salt as they will blacken.
3. After cooking remove the feet, salt the remaining broth and clarify with whites beaten with water, which should be poured into the broth and well brought to a boil.
4. Cut the removed feet into small pieces, salt them, arrange in a mold or salad bowl and cover with the clarified strained broth, stir and place in a cold place until set.
5. Serve with vinegar and oil. It is best to take four veal feet and two pork feet, as aspic made from only veal feet is not as tasty.
