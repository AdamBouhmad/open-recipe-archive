## Ingredients

- a pair of large apples
- jelly, preferably currant

## Directions

1. Wash a pair of large apples and cut them in half lengthwise, but do not peel; hollow out the center of each half with a teaspoon so as to remove all the seeds and create a round hollow in the center.
2. Fill this hollow with some jelly, preferably currant, and place in a moderately hot oven.
3. Serve with sugar and sour cream.
