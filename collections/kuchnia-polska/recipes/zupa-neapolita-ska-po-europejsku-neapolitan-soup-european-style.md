## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of parmesan cheese
- one cup of sour cream or sweet cream
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon of cold water
- one quart of good previously cooked broth
- Italian pasta

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour and one tablespoon of parmesan cheese, stirring until a uniform mass is obtained.
2. Dissolve this mixture with one cup of sour cream; if sour cream is unavailable, use sweet cream.
3. Bring to a boil, remove from heat and thicken with two egg yolks beaten with one tablespoon of cold water.
4. Add one quart of good previously cooked broth.
5. Separately have cooked Italian pasta broken into small pieces, place it in a bowl and pour the soup over it.
