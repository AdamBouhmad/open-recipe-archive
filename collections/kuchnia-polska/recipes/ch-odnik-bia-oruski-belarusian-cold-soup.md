## Ingredients

- several red beets
- 3 eggs
- 1 four-inch fresh cucumber
- 1 tablespoon of chopped chives
- 1 tablespoon of chopped dill
- 1 cup of sour cream
- 2 tablespoons of vinegar
- 1 quart of cold water
- salt and pepper to taste
- crayfish tails (optional)
- ice

## Directions

1. Cook several red beets, peel, and chop finely
2. Hard-boil 3 eggs and cut each into 8 pieces
3. Chop 1 four-inch fresh cucumber into small cubes
4. Take 1 tablespoon of chopped chives and 1 tablespoon of chopped dill
5. Combine with 1 cup of sour cream and 2 tablespoons of vinegar
6. Add 1 quart of cold water and mix everything well
7. Add salt and pepper to taste
8. Place on ice where it should stay until serving
9. When serving, place a piece of ice the size of an egg on each plate and pour the soup over it
10. The soup becomes more delicious with the addition of crayfish tails
11. If chives are not available, use finely chopped onion
