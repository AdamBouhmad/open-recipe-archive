## Ingredients

- sirloin, stew meat, or rump, cut into thick square pieces
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion, thinly sliced
- potatoes

## Directions

1. The best meat for rump steak is sirloin, stew meat, or rump.
2. Cut the meat into thick square pieces.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Fry in hot butter in a pan without over-cooking.
5. Toward the end of frying, add plenty of thinly sliced onion and fry it together with the meat.
6. When serving, surround with potatoes.
