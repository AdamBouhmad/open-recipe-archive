## Ingredients

- cooked cold potatoes
- melted butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut cooked cold potatoes into cubes.
2. Dip them in melted butter and then lightly coat in flour.
3. Arrange them on a sheet or baking dish and place in a hot oven for fifteen minutes.
4. Serve immediately.
