## Ingredients

- couple of dozens of crayfish
- salt and pepper
- a tablespoon of raw butter
- a tablespoon of grated bread roll
- chopped parsley
- nutmeg
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook a couple of dozens of crayfish with salt and pepper
2. When cool, remove meat from the necks and legs and chop finely
3. Mix with a tablespoon of raw butter, a tablespoon of grated bread roll
4. Add some chopped parsley, nutmeg, and salt to taste, and beat in two egg yolks
5. Mix everything into a smooth mass and fill the emptied and cleaned crayfish shells with this stuffing
6. Cook the prepared shells in crayfish soup, or they can be lightly fried in butter
