## Ingredients

- one quart of asparagus
- water
- milk
- butter
- flour
- bread cubes (grzanki)

## Directions

1. Chop a quart of asparagus into one-inch pieces
2. Pour one quart of water over them and cook until completely soft
3. Strain through a colander and return to the water in which they cooked
4. Heat half a quart of milk and whisk in a spoonful of butter mixed with a spoonful of flour
5. Bring to a boil and pour into the asparagus, then bring everything to a boil and pour into a serving bowl lined with bread cubes cut into small pieces
