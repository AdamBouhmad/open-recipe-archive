## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- water
- butter
- grated cheese, preferably Parmesan
- béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Cook cauliflower in salted water as described above.
2. Remove from water and place in another pot in which melt a little butter and braise slightly on low heat.
3. Sprinkle the entire cauliflower from all sides with grated cheese, preferably Parmesan.
4. Pour béchamel sauce over it, sprinkle again with cheese, pour good butter over everything.
5. Place in a hot oven to brown.
6. Serve with the pot.
7. Best baked in a deep serving dish or nice porcelain bowl.
