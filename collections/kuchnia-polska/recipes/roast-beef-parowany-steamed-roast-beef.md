## Ingredients

- piece of cross-cut beef with two ribs
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the bones, roll the meat, and tie with twine
2. Rinse with boiling water
3. Place the meat in a roasting pan of such height that the meat almost reaches the top
4. Pour boiling water one inch deep
5. Cover tightly and place in a moderately hot oven for two and a half to three hours
6. After one hour of cooking, sprinkle with about one teaspoon of salt
7. When the meat is cooked, remove the cover and leave in the hot oven for about ten minutes
8. To make the sauce: remove the meat from the pan, add one and a half tablespoons of flour to the remaining sauce and brown on the fire, then serve separately or pour over the meat
