## Ingredients

- one pound minced leftover veal roast
- one large herring, skinned and boned
- six medium potatoes
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- several tablespoons broth or strong beef broth
- a few tablespoons sour cream
- salt and pepper to taste
- one large roasted onion, grated
- two eggs
- grated roll

## Directions

1. Mince very finely the leftover veal roast, preferably using a meat grinder.
2. For one pound of such meat, take one large herring, skin and bone it, and crush in a mortar into a paste.
3. Cook six medium potatoes and grate them.
4. Boil two tablespoons butter with one tablespoon wheat flour, dilute with several tablespoons broth or strong beef broth and add a few tablespoons sour cream.
5. Season everything with salt and pepper to taste, add one large roasted onion grated, beat in two eggs and mix until a uniform mass forms, which however should not be thick.
6. Grease a baking dish or pan with butter, sprinkle with grated roll, place all the mass, sprinkle the top also with roll and place in the oven for one hour and ten minutes.
7. Then turn out onto a serving platter and place in a cool place to cool.
8. Serve cold with vinegar and oil as an appetizer.
