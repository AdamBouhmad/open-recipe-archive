## Ingredients

- half a cup rice
- salted water
- six eggs
- butter
- half a cup broth
- salt
- red pepper
- white bread for toast, buttered
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Rinse half a cup of rice and cook until tender in a large amount of salted water and drain
2. Hard-boil six eggs; remove the yolks and chop them
3. Put two tablespoons of butter in a pot, add half a cup of broth, bring to a boil and then add the rice and yolks, salt to taste and a little red pepper
4. Place on heat to warm the entire mixture, then arrange on a serving platter lined with buttered white bread toast, or spread the mixture on the toast and then arrange on the platter
5. Garnish on top with egg whites cut into strips or slices
