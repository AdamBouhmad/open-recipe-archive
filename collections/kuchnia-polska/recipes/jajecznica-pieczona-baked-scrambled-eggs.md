## Ingredients

- butter
- eight eggs
- salt
- pepper
- three tablespoons cream
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Grease a baking dish with butter and break eight eggs into it
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, pour in three tablespoons of cream and one tablespoon of butter, scattering it in small pieces over the entire surface
3. Place in a moderately hot oven for twenty minutes and serve immediately at the table
