## Ingredients

- one cup of flour
- half a cup of lard
- salt
- half a cup of very cold water
- one tablespoon of flour
- one cup of milk
- one teaspoon of lemon juice
- a pinch of nutmeg
- one small cup of oysters

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of flour, one half cup of lard, salt and one half cup of very cold water to make a dough
2. Roll out fairly thin and line small tin molds called patty pans
3. Mix one tablespoon of flour with one cup of milk; dissolve the flour in a little cold milk and heat the rest
4. Add the heated milk to the flour mixture and bring to a boil
5. Add one teaspoon of lemon juice and a pinch of nutmeg, then add one small cup of oysters
6. Salt to taste and bring to a boil once
7. Remove from heat and place four oysters in each mold lined with dough
8. Cover the top with the same dough and bake in a hot oven
