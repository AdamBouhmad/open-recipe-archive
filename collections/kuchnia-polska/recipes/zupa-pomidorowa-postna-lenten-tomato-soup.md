## Ingredients

- one quart of tomatoes
- one quart of water
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- half quart of milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- white bread for croutons (optional)

## Directions

1. Press one quart of tomatoes through a colander so as to separate the skins and seeds, which should be discarded
2. Add one quart of water and boil
3. Mix one tablespoon of flour with one tablespoon of butter and half a quart of milk
4. Bring to a boil and pour into the soup
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve on the table
6. This soup can be served with croutons made from white bread
