## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- two teaspoons baking powder
- half cup lard
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- cooked cranberries

## Directions

1. Sift two cups flour with two teaspoons baking powder and mix in half cup lard
2. Add enough milk to form a soft dough
3. Divide dough in half and roll each into a round cake, using as little flour as possible
4. Place one cake on a baking sheet or tin plate and brush with butter
5. Place second cake on top and put in a hot oven
6. When the cakes are baked and well browned, remove from oven and set aside for five minutes
7. Separate the cakes and place a layer of cranberries cooked with sugar on the bottom cake
8. Place the second cake on top, cover it with a thinner layer of cranberries, and serve hot
