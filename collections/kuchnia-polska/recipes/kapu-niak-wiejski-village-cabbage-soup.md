## Ingredients

- fresh cabbage
- 1/2 pound lard (słonina)
- 3 onions
- broth made from bones and one pig's ear
- salt, pepper and vinegar to taste

## Directions

1. Finely chop fresh cabbage and let it lie on a board for half an hour or longer
2. Cut 1/2 pound of lard into small cubes, place in a pot and render
3. Then add three chopped onions to the lard and fry until they turn glassy, being careful not to brown them
4. Place the cabbage in the same pot, cover and braise on low heat until the cabbage begins to shine, stirring frequently to prevent it from burning
5. Have broth prepared on bones and one pig's ear
6. Dilute the cabbage with this broth and bring to a boil together
7. Cut the cooked ear into cubes, add salt, pepper and vinegar to taste and serve
