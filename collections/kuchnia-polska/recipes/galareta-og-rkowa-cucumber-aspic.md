## Ingredients

- four not-too-ripe cucumbers
- one quart water
- one teaspoon salt
- small half teaspoon white pepper
- small package gelatin
- one cup cold water
- one cucumber for garnish
- lettuce leaves
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel four cucumbers and cut into pieces
2. Pour one quart of water over them and add one teaspoon of salt and small half teaspoon of white pepper
3. Cook until the cucumbers completely disintegrate
4. Soak a small package of gelatin in one cup of cold water
5. When the cucumbers are completely cooked, pour in the gelatin and stir until completely dissolved
6. Strain everything and place in a cold location
7. When almost completely cooled, peel and slice one cucumber thinly
8. Line a mold or bowl (which should first be moistened with cold water) with these thin slices
9. Pour the liquid into the mold and place in a cold location for several hours until completely set
10. Turn the aspic out onto a plate lined with lettuce leaves and pour French sauce made from oil and vinegar over it, or serve as a side dish to fish or cold meat
