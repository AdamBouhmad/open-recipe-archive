## Ingredients

- 1 pound tender beef, preferably sirloin, cut into walnut-sized pieces
- salt
- pepper
- 2 small whole onions
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 large cup sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into pieces the size of a walnut and beat each one very thoroughly.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add the two small whole onions, flour, and mix everything well together.
4. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a pan or small dish until well browned.
5. Add the prepared rolls and fry over high heat, stirring with a spoon so the meat does not clump together but remains in separate pieces.
6. When the meat loses its red color, pour in a large cup of sweet sour cream.
7. Simmer for about two minutes and serve.
