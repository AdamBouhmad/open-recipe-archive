## Ingredients

- veal brain
- cold water
- salted water with vinegar and roots
- egg
- bread roll
- butter
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the brain in cold water for half an hour or an hour, then remove the membranes and blanch in salted water with vinegar and roots.
2. After boiling immediately remove and cut into slices, shaping them like cutlets, dredge each in grated bread, dip in beaten egg, coat with grated bread and fry in butter until browned.
3. When browned, place in a hot oven for a few minutes and then arrange on a platter and garnish with lemon slices and sprigs of fresh parsley.
