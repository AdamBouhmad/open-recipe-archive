## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- cooking liquid from dried mushrooms
- cucumber vinegar
- pickled cucumbers, diced
- 1 teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
2. Dilute with cooking liquid from dried mushrooms
3. Add some cucumber vinegar, pickled cucumbers cut into small dice and 1 teaspoon sugar
4. Boil and serve
5. Cucumber sauce is used for meat dishes and herring and potato cutlets
