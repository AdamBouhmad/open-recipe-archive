## Ingredients

- five or six egg yolks
- quarter pound of fine sugar
- half a quarter of white wine

## Directions

1. Beat five or six egg yolks strongly with quarter pound of fine sugar
2. Pour in half a quarter of white wine
3. Place over low heat and continue beating with a whisk until thickened, but do not allow to boil
