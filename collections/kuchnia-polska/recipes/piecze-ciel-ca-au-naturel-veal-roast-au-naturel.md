## Ingredients

- Veal
- Salt
- Carrots, sliced
- Onion, sliced
- Celery, sliced
- Water
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Lemon
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the veal and arrange it in a roasting pan
2. Add sliced carrots, onion, and celery
3. Roast until tender, basting initially with water, then with the drippings that accumulate in the pan
4. When nearly done, mix a piece of butter with flour, place in the roasting pan, dilute with broth, and season with lemon
5. Strain the sauce and pour it over the veal arranged on a platter
