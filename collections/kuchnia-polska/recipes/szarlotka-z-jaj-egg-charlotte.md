## Ingredients

- five hard-boiled eggs
- grated rolls
- butter
- milk
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Hard boil five eggs and cut into thin slices.
2. On the bottom of a cake form, spread a layer of grated bread.
3. Then arrange a layer of sliced eggs, salt, pepper to taste, and a few small pieces of butter.
4. Repeat with another layer of bread and eggs and so on until the form is full.
5. Sprinkle grated bread on top and pour over one cup of milk.
6. Bake in a slow oven until everything is well browned.
