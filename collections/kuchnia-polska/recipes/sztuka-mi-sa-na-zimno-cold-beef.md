## Ingredients

- thick piece of cross-cut beef
- bacon, sliced thick and sprinkled with salt and pepper
- water
- two veal feet
- vegetables for broth
- salt
- caramelized sugar

## Directions

1. Lard thick piece of cross-cut beef with bacon sliced into fairly thick strips and sprinkled with salt and pepper
2. Cover with cold water and cook like broth
3. After cooking, remove meat from broth, place in another pot, cover and let cool
4. Separately cook two veal feet with vegetables, clarify, color with caramelized sugar and chill to aspic
5. When meat is completely cold, slice thinly and surround with veal foot aspic cut into cubes
6. Serve separately with oil and vinegar
