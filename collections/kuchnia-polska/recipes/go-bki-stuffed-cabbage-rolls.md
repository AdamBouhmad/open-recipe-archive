## Ingredients

- 2/3 pound beef and 1/3 pound pork (or all beef)
- 1 pound minced meat total
- 1 cup grated bread or white bread
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 medium onions, finely minced and browned in butter
- 1 whole egg
- fresh cabbage leaves
- butter for frying
- water

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 pound of finely minced meat, 1 cup of grated bread or white bread, salt and pepper to taste, 2 medium onions finely minced and browned in butter, and 1 whole egg.
2. Mix everything into a smooth mass.
3. Form into oblong cutlets and wrap each one in a fresh cabbage leaf and tie with string or pin with a toothpick.
4. Scald the cabbage leaves in boiling water before use so they are soft and do not break when wrapping.
5. Melt a piece of butter in a wide pan and arrange the cabbage rolls in it.
6. Fry them initially on high heat to brown them slightly.
7. Then add a little water and braise on low heat until the cabbage is completely soft.
