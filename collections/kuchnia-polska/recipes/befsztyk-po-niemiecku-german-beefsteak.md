## Ingredients

- a piece of tender beef for beefsteak
- olive oil
- salt
- butter
- onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Smear a piece of tender beef on both sides with olive oil and lightly salt
2. Immediately throw it onto a skillet with browned butter and sliced onion
3. The onion should be placed on the butter just before laying the meat on it
