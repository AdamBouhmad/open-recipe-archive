## Ingredients

- chicken, about four pounds
- chicken liver
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken weighing about four pounds into six pieces and flatten each piece, shaping them like cutlets.
2. Take the liver and pound it into a paste with a spoon of milk, a teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of pepper.
3. Beat one egg and prepare remaining ingredients for breading.
