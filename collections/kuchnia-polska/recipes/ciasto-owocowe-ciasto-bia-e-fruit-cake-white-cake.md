## Ingredients

- one cup butter
- two cups sugar
- one pound figs
- one pound dates
- one pound almonds
- one pound raisins
- half pound citron
- one pound walnuts
- two and a half cups flour
- two and a half teaspoons baking powder
- seven egg whites

## Directions

1. Cream one cup butter with two cups sugar
2. Chop figs, dates, almonds, raisins, citron, and walnuts separately, not too finely, then mix lightly with a spoon
3. Dust the chopped fruit and nuts with some of the flour
4. Sift the remaining flour with two and a half teaspoons baking powder
5. Add the flour mixture to the creamed butter and sugar, then add the fruit, and finally fold in seven egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
6. Line two square forms with buttered paper and bake in a slow oven for one and a half hours
