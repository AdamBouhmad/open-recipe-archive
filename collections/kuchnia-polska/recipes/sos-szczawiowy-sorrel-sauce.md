## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- salted water
- butter
- strong broth or bouillon
- two tablespoons sour cream
- one tablespoon flour
- one teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Carefully peel, wash and chop sorrel leaves.
2. Blanch them in salted water and then stew in butter, moistening with a little strong broth or bouillon.
3. Strain through a sieve, add two tablespoons of sour cream beaten with one tablespoon flour.
4. Add one teaspoon of sugar, bring to a boil and pour over meat.
5. Serve with a piece of meat or veal cutlets.
