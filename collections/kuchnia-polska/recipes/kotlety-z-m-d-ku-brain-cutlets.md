## Ingredients

- veal brain
- cold water
- salted water
- vinegar, one tablespoon
- egg
- bread roll
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak veal brain for an hour in cold water, then carefully remove all veins and membranes.
2. Cover the deveined brain with salted water and add one tablespoon of vinegar, place on heat and bring to a boil, then immediately drain the water.
3. Cut the brain into flat round pieces, dip them in beaten egg, coat with grated bread and fry in clarified butter until both sides are browned.
