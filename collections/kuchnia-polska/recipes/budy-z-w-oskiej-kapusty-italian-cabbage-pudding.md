## Ingredients

- 1 medium head Italian cabbage
- 1/4 pound cooked ham, chopped fine
- 1 large roasted onion, chopped
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 small cup grated bread
- 4 whole eggs
- salt and pepper to taste
- butter and grated bread for the mold

## Directions

1. Grate a medium head of Italian cabbage, pour water in a pot, bring to boil.
2. Pour off the water and squeeze the cabbage well of water.
3. Chop fine 1/4 pound cooked ham, chop one large roasted onion.
4. Add 1/4 pound butter, 1 small cup grated bread, 4 whole eggs, salt and pepper to taste.
5. Mix everything well with the cabbage.
6. Butter and sprinkle grated bread in a deep baking dish.
7. Fill with the mixture and place in a pot with boiling water, with 2 inches of water after placing the form.
8. Cover the form and cook for about an hour, adding hot water to the pot as it evaporates.
9. When completely tender, serve as a vegetable.
