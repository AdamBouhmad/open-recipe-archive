## Ingredients

- small quarter of veal
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon
- young bacon
- vinegar
- good sour cream

## Directions

1. Butterfly the small quarter of veal lengthwise so the bones are loosened. After removing the bones, pound the meat well and season with salt and a bit of nutmeg.
2. Sprinkle inside with lemon, using one large or one and a half medium lemons. Over the lemon, lay finely sliced young bacon to cover the entire inner surface.
3. Roll tightly into a log and tie with twine.
4. Place the rolled roast in a salad bowl or dish, sprinkle with good vinegar and place in a cold pantry for two days. Every day it should be turned several times.
5. After two days, remove from the dish, salt well and let it rest in salt for about three hours.
6. Then place on a roasting pan and bake in a moderately hot oven, basting frequently with butter. At the end of roasting, season with one tablespoon of good sour cream to form a creamy sauce.
7. Serve sliced.
