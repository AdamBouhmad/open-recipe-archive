## Ingredients

- one spoon melted butter
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites
- half quart wheat semolina

## Directions

1. Beat one spoon melted butter in a bowl to a cream, adding four egg yolks one by one.
2. Then pour in four egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, mix well and add, pouring slowly and constantly stirring, half a quart of wheat semolina.
3. Mix until the semolina swells and the dough becomes thick and set aside for about half an hour.
4. When the semolina swells well, place dumplings from this dough with a spoon on simmering broth, each time dipping the spoon in clean water before taking dough.
5. Cover the broth so they rise, then serve on the table.
