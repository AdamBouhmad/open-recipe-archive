## Ingredients

- two cups of grated bread or crumbs
- half a teaspoon of salt
- a pinch of pepper
- two cups of oysters
- one and one half cups of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- grated bread for topping
- small pieces of butter

## Directions

1. Take two cups of grated bread or crumbs, add one half teaspoon of salt and a pinch of pepper
2. Pour in two cups of oysters and mix well
3. Add one and one half cups of milk and one tablespoon of butter and mix everything thoroughly
4. Pour into a cake mold, sprinkle grated bread on top
5. Place small pieces of butter on top and bake in the oven for one hour
