## Ingredients

- three cups of flour
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- one teaspoon of salt
- one cup of lard
- ice water
- small pieces of butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Sift three cups of flour with one teaspoon of baking powder and one teaspoon of salt; add one cup of lard and mix with a knife, then add enough ice water so that the dough is thick
2. Turn onto a work surface and roll out to the thickness of an inch, then scatter small pieces of butter on top and sprinkle with a pinch of flour, fold the dough and roll out again; repeat this several times
