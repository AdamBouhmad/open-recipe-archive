## Ingredients

- one quart fresh unskimmed milk
- half cup fresh buttermilk
- four pieces white sugar or one tablespoon granulated sugar
- yeast (optional substitute for buttermilk)

## Directions

1. Pour half cup of fresh buttermilk into one quart of fresh unskimmed milk and add four pieces of white sugar or one tablespoon of granulated sugar
2. Pour into a pitcher and stir until all the sugar dissolves
3. Place in a warm place for ten hours
4. After ten hours, when the milk thickens, mix it well so it becomes a uniform liquid, which is best accomplished by pouring from one pitcher to another several dozen times
5. Pour into clean bottles, cork and tie the cork with wire or string
6. Place in a warm place for twenty-four hours
7. Before use, shake the bottle well several times
8. Instead of buttermilk, yeast can be used and the kumiss will have a slightly different taste
