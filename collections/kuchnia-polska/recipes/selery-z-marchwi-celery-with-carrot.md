## Ingredients

- carrots sliced thin
- celery cut into small cubes in equal quantity to carrots
- salted water
- half quart sweet milk
- one teaspoon cornstarch
- cold milk
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Slice carrots thinly and cut equal quantity of celery into small cubes
2. Cook the carrots to tenderness in salted water, then drain the water
3. Cook the celery in another pot
4. When both celery and carrot are tender, drain the water and mix both vegetables
5. Add half quart of sweet milk
6. Mix one teaspoon cornstarch in a small amount of cold milk and add
7. Add salt and pepper to taste and one tablespoon butter
8. Mix everything, place on fire, boil once and serve on the table
