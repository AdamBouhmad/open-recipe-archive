## Ingredients

- pike or other fish
- broth
- burnt sugar for yellow color (optional)
- red gelatin for pink color (optional)
- pickles, miscellaneous
- mushrooms
- capers
- olives
- green cooked peas
- carrots, cooked and cut into cubes and stars

## Directions

1. Clean and cook pike or other fish as above, taking care not to overcook it.
2. Strain the broth through cloth and clarify.
3. For yellow colored aspic, add some burnt sugar; for pink, add some red gelatin.
4. Take one quarter of this broth and pour into a mold, distributing evenly throughout the mold.
5. Carefully pick the cooked fish of all bones and cut into small pieces.
6. Add miscellaneous pickles, mushrooms, capers, olives, green cooked peas, cooked carrots cut into nice cubes and stars.
7. Mix everything lightly with the fish.
8. When the aspic in the mold begins to thicken, arrange the fish with pickles and vegetables on the aspic and cover with the rest of the broth, trying to keep the fish in the center and the aspic around it.
9. Chill until set.
10. When firm, turn the mold onto a salad bowl and serve at table with vinegar and oil.
