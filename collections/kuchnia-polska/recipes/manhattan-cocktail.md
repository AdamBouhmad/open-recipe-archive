## Ingredients

- one-third quart of vodka
- one-third quart of Italian vermouth wine
- one-third quart of water
- one and a half liqueur glasses of Peyschaud or Angostura Bitters
- one liqueur glass of syrup

## Directions

1. Mix well, pour into a bottle and cork
