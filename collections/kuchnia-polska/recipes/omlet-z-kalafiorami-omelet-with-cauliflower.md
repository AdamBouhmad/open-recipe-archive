## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- four eggs
- small teaspoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Boil cauliflower in salted water until tender
2. Drain and break into small pieces
3. Beat four eggs with a small teaspoon of cornstarch
4. Add the cauliflower and fry in a pan until the eggs set
5. Fold in half and serve on the table
