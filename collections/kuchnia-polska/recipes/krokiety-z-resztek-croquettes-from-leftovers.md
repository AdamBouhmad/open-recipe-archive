## Ingredients

- various leftovers of meat from several days
- cooked potatoes or cooked rice from the previous day, at a ratio of one part rice or potatoes to two parts meat
- one beaten egg
- one tablespoon grated bread or breadcrumbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- one tablespoon tomato sauce
- cold milk if needed
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Take various meat leftovers
2. Remove meat from bones and chop finely
3. Add cooked potatoes or cooked rice, using one part rice or potatoes for every two parts meat
4. Add one beaten egg
5. Add one tablespoon grated bread or breadcrumbs
6. Add salt, pepper, and one tablespoon tomato sauce
7. If the mixture is too dry, pour in some cold milk
8. Form small round cutlets from the mixture
9. Dip each cutlet first in beaten egg, then in grated breadcrumbs
10. Fry in hot lard until well browned
11. Sauce can be made by adding a little flour to the lard in which the croquettes were fried
