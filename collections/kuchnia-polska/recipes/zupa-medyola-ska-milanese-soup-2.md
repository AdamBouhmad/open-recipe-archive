## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- one onion
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- a few tablespoons of parmesan or grated Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Rinse half a pound of rice and cook it.
2. Chop one onion finely and fry it in butter in a pan.
3. When the onion becomes golden, add the rice to it, mix with the previously cooked broth, put on the fire and cook until the rice softens so much that it can be pressed through a sieve.
4. After pressing through the sieve, mix again with the soup, add salt and pepper to taste and serve at table, adding a few tablespoons of parmesan or grated Swiss cheese.
