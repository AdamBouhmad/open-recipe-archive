## Ingredients

- four pounds beef
- two quarts water
- four cloves
- piece of cinnamon
- salt
- pepper
- small amount of vegetables
- flour
- fresh parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cook four pounds beef in two quarts water with four cloves, piece of cinnamon, salt, pepper and small amount of vegetables
2. When meat is tender, place on platter
3. Thicken remaining broth in pot with flour mixed previously in cold water
4. Add finely chopped fresh parsley
5. Pour sauce over meat on platter and serve immediately
