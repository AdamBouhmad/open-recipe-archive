## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lamb from the neck
- 2 quarts cold water
- 2 carrots
- 1 rutabaga
- 1 or 2 onions
- 2 tablespoons pearl barley
- salt and pepper to taste
- parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Soak 3 pounds lamb from the neck in cold water for 1 hour, then remove, trim all fat, and cut meat into small pieces
2. Place meat in a pot, cover with 2 quarts of cold water and bring to boil
3. Skim well, then cook for 1.5 hours
4. Cut 2 carrots, 1 rutabaga, 1 or 2 onions into pieces and add to the soup along with 2 tablespoons of pearl barley, salt and pepper to taste
5. Continue cooking for 3 more hours
6. Serve sprinkled with finely chopped parsley
