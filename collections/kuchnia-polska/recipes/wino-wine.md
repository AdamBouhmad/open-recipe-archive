## Ingredients

- completely ripe grapes
- no additives mentioned in complete portion

## Directions

1. Grapes should be completely ripe, undamaged, unspoiled and completely fresh
2. Sort carefully, crush and press out the juice; when crushing, be careful not to crush the seeds, as they would give the wine a bitter taste
3. Pour the pressed juice into an open vessel and place at a temperature between 58 to 68 degrees Fahrenheit for fermentation
4. The higher the temperature, the faster the fermentation, but be careful not to let it turn to vinegar, which can happen if the temperature is too high
5. Fermentation lasts from three to seven days, and when it stops, which can be recognized by the foam ceasing to be released, the liquid should be strained into another vessel immediately, doing this carefully so all the sediment remains on the bottom
