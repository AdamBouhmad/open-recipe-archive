## Ingredients

- one egg
- one tablespoon butter
- four tablespoons sour cream (may be canned)
- water
- one and a half cups flour
- one cup sugar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat one egg in a cup, add one tablespoon butter, four tablespoons sour cream, and fill with water
2. Sift together one and a half cups flour, one cup sugar, and a pinch of salt
3. Add the egg mixture to the dry ingredients, beat well for at least five minutes
4. Fill a form with the batter and bake
