## Ingredients

- one pound of walnuts in their shells
- eight egg yolks
- half a pound of sugar
- stiffly beaten egg whites from the remaining whites
- two ounces of potato starch
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the walnuts from their shells and pound them in a mortar into a paste.
2. Whip eight egg yolks with half a pound of sugar in a bowl.
3. Add the walnuts gradually and continue to grind for another half hour.
4. When the entire mass is well ground, beat a stiff foam from the remaining egg whites and add this foam one spoonful at a time.
5. Also add two ounces of potato starch, sprinkling it in by small spoonfuls at a time and mixing.
6. When everything is mixed this way, place it in a mold greased with butter for baking and put it into a moderately hot oven for forty to forty-five minutes.
7. After removing from the oven, cover with preserves or fruit jelly and serve on a platter or serving dish.
