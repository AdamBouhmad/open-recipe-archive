## Ingredients

- one spoon fresh butter
- four egg yolks
- half spoon fat or broth fat
- four egg whites
- half quart flour

## Directions

1. Beat one spoon of fresh butter in a bowl, then add four egg yolks one by one, stirring constantly in one direction.
2. Take half a spoon of fat or broth fat, cool it well and pour into the yolks.
3. Beat the remaining four egg whites into stiff peaks and mix with the yolks, taking one spoon of foam at a time.
4. Sift half a quart of flour and add it very slowly to the beaten eggs, mixing together.
5. Take the dough with a spoon and place on simmering broth, cover, and when they rise, remove with a spoon and place in a bowl.
6. Pour some broth first into the bowl, then after placing the dumplings, pour the rest of the broth through a sieve.
7. Handle the dumplings very carefully with the spoon so they do not fall apart.
