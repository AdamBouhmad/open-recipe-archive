## Ingredients

- squash, peeled and cut into pieces
- one cup cooked squash puree
- one cup hot milk
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon sugar
- half teaspoon salt
- one well-beaten egg
- one cup flour
- one teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Peel, cut into pieces, and cook squash until soft, then pass through a sieve.
2. Take one cup of this puree, place in a bowl, and pour one cup of hot milk over it.
3. Add one tablespoon of butter, one tablespoon of sugar, and half a teaspoon of salt.
4. Set in a cool place.
5. When completely cooled, add one well-beaten egg and one cup of flour sifted with one teaspoon of baking powder.
6. Mix well and fry on a pan until nicely browned on both sides.
