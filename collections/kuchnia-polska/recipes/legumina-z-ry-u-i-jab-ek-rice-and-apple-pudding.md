## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- water
- butter
- quarter pound of sugar
- eight firm apples
- preserves or currants with sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of rice and cook it in water, then add butter, and finally add a quarter pound of sugar and mix well.
2. Peel eight firm apples and hollow out the center so as to remove all the seeds, then place them in a mold, pour a small amount of water and cook, but not until soft, only halfway.
3. Fill the hollows in the apples with preserves or currants with sugar and cinnamon.
4. Grease a mold or form with butter, spread a layer of rice on the bottom, arrange the prepared apples on the rice, cover with the rest of the rice, sprinkle with a little sugar mixed with cinnamon and put into a moderately hot oven for half an hour.
5. Alternatively, instead of hollowing out, peel the apples, cut them into slices raw and then layer them between the rice, sprinkling with sugar mixed with cinnamon, with the addition of currants and butter.
6. This proportion is for six people.
