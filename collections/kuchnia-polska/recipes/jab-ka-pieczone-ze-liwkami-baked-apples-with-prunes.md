## Ingredients

- sour apples of medium size
- cooked prunes
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cold sour cream

## Directions

1. Take a sufficient number of sour apples of medium size, hollow out the core to remove all the seeds and fill these hollows with cooked prunes, and add in each one teaspoon of sugar and some cinnamon and place in the oven where they should bake slowly.
2. Serve at table with cold sour cream.
