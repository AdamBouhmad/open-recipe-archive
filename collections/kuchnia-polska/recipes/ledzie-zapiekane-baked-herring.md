## Ingredients

- 4 fine and fatty herring
- several cold cooked potatoes
- butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 teaspoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak 4 fine and fatty herring in cold water overnight
2. The next day split lengthwise along the back, remove the skin, take out the bones and cut the flesh into inch-sized pieces
3. Cut several cold cooked potatoes into slices
4. Butter a metal dish or baking form
5. Place a layer of sliced potatoes on the bottom, then herring and so forth until the dish is filled, with potatoes on top
6. Beat 2 eggs, mix with 2 cups of milk and pour over the herring and potatoes
7. Sprinkle 1 cup of breadcrumbs mixed with 1 teaspoon of melted butter on top
8. Place in the oven for 40 or 45 minutes and serve hot at table
