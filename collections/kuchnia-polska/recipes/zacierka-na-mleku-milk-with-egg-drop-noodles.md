## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 tablespoons of flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart of milk
2. Beat 2 whole eggs with 4 tablespoons of flour and enough cold water to obtain thin batter
3. Make a cone from paper and pour the batter through it onto the boiling milk
