## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 tablespoon cornmeal (com meal)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (preferably brown)
- 1 heaping tablespoon wheat flour
- 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs well, add 1 tablespoon melted butter, 1 tablespoon cornmeal, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 1 heaping tablespoon wheat flour mixed with 0.5 teaspoon baking powder and 1 cup milk.
2. Mix everything well, place in small buttered metal forms, and bake in a hot oven.
