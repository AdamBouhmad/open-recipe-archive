## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- one pound sugar
- one tablespoon anise seed
- flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs with one pound sugar for one-half hour, beating always in one direction only
2. Add one tablespoon anise seed and enough flour to make a medium-thick dough
3. Turn out onto a work surface and roll thin
4. Cut out small round cakes
5. Place in a hot oven until browned
