## Ingredients

- several bananas
- one lemon
- three tablespoons sugar
- one tablespoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in half lengthwise several bananas, place them in a baking pan and sprinkle with the following syrup: squeeze the juice from one lemon, add three tablespoons of sugar and one tablespoon of melted butter and mix well.
2. With this syrup you should only dampen the bananas and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour, but during this time you must baste them every few minutes with the syrup until it is all used.
