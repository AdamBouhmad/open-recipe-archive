## Ingredients

- 3 medium onions
- cold water
- 1 heaping tablespoon fat, preferably beef tallow
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 teaspoon beef extract
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Pour cold water over 3 medium onions and bring to a boil.
2. When they have boiled for five minutes, remove and chop finely.
3. Grate 1 heaping tablespoon of fat, preferably beef tallow, and fry for about ten minutes, constantly stirring.
4. Pour in 1 quart of boiling water, add 1 teaspoon of beef extract, salt and pepper to taste.
5. Cook on low heat for half an hour, then strain.
6. When serving, whiten with 3 tablespoons of sour cream.
