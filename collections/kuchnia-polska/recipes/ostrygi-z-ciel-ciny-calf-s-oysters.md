## Ingredients

- calf meat from the leg
- olive oil
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- hot fat
- salad leaves
- tartar sauce
- salad

## Directions

1. Take calf meat from the leg and cut out small pieces in the shape of oysters
2. Take each piece of meat prepared in this manner individually
3. Dip in olive oil, then in breadcrumbs, then in beaten egg and again in breadcrumbs
4. Throw into hot fat and fry until well browned
5. Arrange on a serving platter lined with salad leaves
6. Serve separately with tartar sauce and salad
