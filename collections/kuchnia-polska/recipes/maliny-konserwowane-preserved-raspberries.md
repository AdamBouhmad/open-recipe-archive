## Ingredients

- sixteen quarts of raspberries
- eleven pounds of sugar
- two quarts of water

## Directions

1. Fill jars with berries and arrange in a large steamer
2. Cook on steam for half an hour
3. Make a syrup from sugar and water and cook it for five minutes
4. Uncover the steamer and immediately pour the syrup over the berries
5. Jars should be full
