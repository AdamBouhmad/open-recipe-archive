## Ingredients

- leftover cooked chicken from previous day
- rice, cooked
- butter or fat
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove meat from bones and chop into small pieces.
2. For each cup of meat, take one cup of cooked rice and mash together into a paste.
3. Place the chicken mixture in a pan with a large spoon of butter or fat and brown it.
4. Sprinkle a spoon of dry flour and fry until the flour is lightly browned.
5. Pour in two quarts of boiling water and add the rice.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and simmer over low heat until everything is cooked enough to pass through a fine sieve.
7. Press through a fine sieve.
8. Pour back into the pan and bring to a boil, adding a little boiling water if the soup is too thick.
