## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- broth or fish stock
- one large teaspoon of English mustard
- vinegar
- sugar
- several slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour
2. Dissolve in broth or fish stock
3. Add one large teaspoon of English mustard, some vinegar, and sugar
4. Add several slices of lemon
5. Boil together and serve
6. Used for fish and also for sausage
