## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes and cut raw into thin slices and place in cold water for half an hour.
2. Drain the water, place the potatoes in a pan, salt and pepper them.
3. Pour enough milk to not completely cover the potatoes and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour.
4. Before serving, add half a spoon of good butter.
