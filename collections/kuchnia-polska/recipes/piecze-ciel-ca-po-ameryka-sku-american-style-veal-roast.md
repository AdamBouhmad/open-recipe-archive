## Ingredients

- piece of veal from the leg
- bread or bread crumbs
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- bacon
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal from the leg and remove the bone.
2. Soak one cup of grated bread or bread crumbs in a little water, add one tablespoon of butter, a quarter teaspoon of salt, some pepper and a bit of powdered thyme. Mix into a paste and fill with this stuffing the place where the bone was.
3. Roll the meat and tie with twine. Rub the top with salt, pepper and flour.
4. Place in a roasting pan, put a couple of bacon slices on top, add water and roast in a moderately hot oven.
5. Baste frequently to avoid burning; for this purpose the meat can also be covered with paper.
6. Roast for about two hours.
