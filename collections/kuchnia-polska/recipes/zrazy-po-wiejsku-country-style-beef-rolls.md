## Ingredients

- tender beef
- salt
- pepper
- lard slices
- bay leaves
- water
- grated rye bread

## Directions

1. Cut tender beef into thin slices, beat, season with salt and pepper and arrange in a pan
2. Layer with thin slices of lard and bay leaves
3. Add a small amount of water and braise on low heat until the meat is tender, then add grated rye bread so the sauce is thick
4. These rolls are usually served with potato pancakes
