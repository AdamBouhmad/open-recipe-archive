## Ingredients

- butter
- grated bread or breadcrumbs
- rhubarb stalks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grease a deep baking dish with butter and on the bottom spread a layer of grated bread or breadcrumbs about an inch thick
2. Peel the outer fibers of the rhubarb stalks, cut into small pieces and spread a layer of the same thickness on the grated bread and sprinkle generously with sugar, then alternately add layers of grated bread and rhubarb with sugar until the dish is full, with grated bread on top
