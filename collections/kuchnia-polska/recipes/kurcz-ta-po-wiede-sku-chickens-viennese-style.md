## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- flour
- one egg
- grated bread
- hot fat for frying
- parsley sprigs
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean the young chickens as usual, cut them into quarters, salt them and sprinkle lightly with flour.
2. Dip each quarter in beaten egg, coat with grated bread and fry in hot fat, of which there should be enough to completely cover the chickens.
3. After frying, garnish with parsley sprigs and lemon slices and serve.
