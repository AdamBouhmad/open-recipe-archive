## Ingredients

- half cup butter
- one cup water
- one cup flour
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Bring half a cup of butter and one cup of water to a boil in a pot and slowly add one cup of flour to the boiling liquid, stirring at the same time so a uniform mass forms.
2. Remove from heat and cool well, then add two eggs and mix properly.
3. From this dough dumplings can be made in three ways: 1) Take with a spoon and place dumplings on simmering soup, then cover so they rise; 2) make small round balls with fingers and fry them in hot fat, of which there must be much in the pot; 3) fill a buttered baking tin with dough to half full, place in a pot with hot water and cook in steam, cool, cut with a knife into cubes and place in a bowl before filling it with soup.
