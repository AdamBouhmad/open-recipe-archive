## Ingredients

- half pound sugar
- half quart water
- lemon peels
- lemon juice (to taste)
- mineral or regular water
- red sour wine (optional)
- thin lemon slices for serving

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from half pound sugar and half quart water, add several lemon peels and let it sit for one hour
2. Remove the peels, add lemon juice to taste
3. Add mineral or regular water and serve
4. Red sour wine can be added and the lemonade will be pink with a slightly different flavor
5. When serving, place a thin lemon slice in each glass
