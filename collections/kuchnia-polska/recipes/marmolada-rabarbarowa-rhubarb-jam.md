## Ingredients

- three pounds rhubarb stalks
- two pounds sugar
- two lemons, sliced into small pieces
- one ounce bitter almonds, blanched and split in half

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in a pot
2. Cook until thick
3. Fill glasses and store
