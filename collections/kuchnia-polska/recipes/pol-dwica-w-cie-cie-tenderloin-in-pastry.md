## Ingredients

- tenderloin
- salt
- French pastry dough
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove sinews and clean the tenderloin of fat
2. Salt it and let it rest for one hour
3. Place in a very hot oven and bake for half an hour
4. Make French pastry and roll it thin
5. Cool the tenderloin so only the surface cools slightly while the inside remains hot
6. Wrap it entirely in pastry so no meat is visible anywhere
7. Place in a hot oven and when the pastry is well browned, the meat is also done
8. The tenderloin baked this way will be red inside
