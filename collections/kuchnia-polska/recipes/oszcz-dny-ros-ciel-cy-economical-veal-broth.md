## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal from the neck
- water
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 1/2 cup rinsed rice
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 tablespoons water
- salt and pepper to taste
- small croutons

## Directions

1. Cover 2 pounds of veal from the neck with water and cook until the meat is tender
2. Remove meat from bones and set aside, place bones back in the broth and cook for about 2 more hours on low heat, then strain and pour back into the pot
3. Add 1 medium sliced onion, 1/2 cup of rinsed rice and 1 tablespoon of flour previously mixed in 2 tablespoons of water
4. Place on fire and cook until rice is done
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve with small croutons
6. From the meat that was removed from the bones, cook a separate stew separately, thus you have two dishes at once
