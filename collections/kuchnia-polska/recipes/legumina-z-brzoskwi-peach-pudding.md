## Ingredients

- three cups of milk
- two tablespoons of cornmeal
- cold water
- one tablespoon of butter
- three egg yolks
- half a cup of sugar
- canned peaches
- two egg whites
- three teaspoons of fine sugar
- peach syrup

## Directions

1. Bring three cups of milk to a boil and immediately add two tablespoons of cornmeal mixed with a little cold water, then cook for a short time until it thickens slightly.
2. Remove from heat, add one tablespoon of butter and three egg yolks beaten well with half a cup of sugar.
3. Drain the syrup from a tin of marinated peaches, place them on a baking sheet for baking pastries and pour the previously cooked mass over them.
4. Then put into a hot oven for ten minutes.
5. After baking, remove from the oven and cover the top with foam from two egg whites beaten stiffly with three teaspoons of fine sugar and put back in the oven to brown.
6. Serve to the table with peach syrup, to which add sugar if it is not sweet enough.
