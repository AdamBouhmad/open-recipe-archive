## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups boiled milk
- 1 cake yeast
- 3 cups wheat flour
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Put 1 tablespoon sugar, 0.5 teaspoon salt, and 2 tablespoons butter in a bowl and pour over 2 cups boiled milk and mix.
2. When cooled to lukewarm temperature, add 1 cake of yeast, crumbled into small pieces, and stir until the yeast dissolves completely.
3. Add 3 cups wheat flour, beat well, and set in a warm place.
4. When the dough rises well, add 2 well-beaten eggs and 1 cup flour, mix, and let rise again.
5. Fill forms with dough and place in a hot oven for twenty to twenty-five minutes.
