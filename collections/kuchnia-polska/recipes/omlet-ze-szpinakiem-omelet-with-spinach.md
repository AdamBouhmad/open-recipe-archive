## Ingredients

- spinach leaves
- water
- salt
- sour cream
- four eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Carefully clean spinach leaves of roots and rinse in several waters
2. Plunge into a pot of boiling water and cook, then pour onto a sieve so all water drains
3. Chop finely, season with salt and add a little sweet sour cream
4. Beat four eggs, beating them only for one minute
5. Pour onto a frying pan with hot butter, stir a few times with a fork and fry until the eggs set
6. Then place the hot spinach on top, arranging it lengthwise
7. Fold the omelet in half so the spinach is in the middle, and serve on the table
