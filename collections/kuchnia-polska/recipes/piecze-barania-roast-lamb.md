## Ingredients

- lamb hind quarter
- vinegar
- garlic cloves
- salt

## Directions

1. Take the hind quarter for roast, clean of excess fat, veins and membranes, then beat fairly strongly.
2. Scald with vinegar, lard with garlic cloves, salt and let rest for about an hour.
3. Then place in a roasting pan and roast in a medium oven, basting with the liquid sauce from the pan.
4. Shortly before serving, sprinkle with flour and brown.
