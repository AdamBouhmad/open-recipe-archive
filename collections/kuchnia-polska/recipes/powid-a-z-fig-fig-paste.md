## Ingredients

- one pound of peeled and carefully washed figs
- one cup of water
- half a pound of sugar
- half of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop very finely one pound of peeled and carefully washed figs
2. Add one cup of water, half a pound of sugar and half of grated nutmeg
3. Place in a saucepan and cook to a thick mass
4. Fill small glasses, seal tightly and store
