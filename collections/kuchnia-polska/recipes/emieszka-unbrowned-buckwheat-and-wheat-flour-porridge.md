## Ingredients

- half quart of buckwheat flour
- half quart of wheat flour
- one quart of milk
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Make in the same way as Prażucha, with the difference that for Łemieszka use raw flour instead of browned flour
2. Pour fried bacon over it
