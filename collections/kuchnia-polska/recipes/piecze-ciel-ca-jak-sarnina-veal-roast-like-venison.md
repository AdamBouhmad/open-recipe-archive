## Ingredients

- Veal
- Vinegar
- Juniper berries, crushed
- Young bacon
- Butter
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Scald a nice piece of veal with light vinegar, having first rubbed and dusted it with crushed juniper berries
2. Place in a dish and let it stand in a cold place for two to three days
3. Remove from the dish, rinse in the same vinegar, lard with young bacon, and roast in a medium oven as you would any roast, basting frequently with butter
4. At the end of roasting, season with sour cream
