## Ingredients

- 1 herring
- 1 large onion
- butter
- 1 bread roll
- milk
- 1 egg yolk
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Soak herring in cold water for 24 hours, clean, remove all bones and chop into a mass
2. For 1 pound of herring mass, peel and grate 1 large onion and fry it in a pan with butter
3. Soak 1 bread roll in milk and squeeze it out
4. Add 1 egg yolk and pepper to taste
5. Put everything in a mortar and mix until the mass becomes white and fluffy
