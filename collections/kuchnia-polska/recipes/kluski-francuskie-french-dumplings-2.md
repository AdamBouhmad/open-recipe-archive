## Ingredients

- half tablespoon fat
- one tablespoon good butter
- two egg yolks
- one whole egg
- one heaping tablespoon sifted flour
- two tablespoons good jam
- sugar
- two tablespoons sour cream
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix fat and butter in a bowl until it turns white
2. Add two egg yolks one at a time and beat until it thickens while stirring in one direction
3. Add one whole egg and beat a bit more
4. Very slowly add one heaping tablespoon of sifted flour while simultaneously stirring until a thick uniform mass forms
5. Scoop the prepared batter with a spoon and place in a large pot of boiling water
6. When they float to the surface, carefully remove with a spoon onto a hot serving plate
7. Press two tablespoons of good jam through a sieve
8. Mix with sugar and two tablespoons sour cream
9. Add some ground cinnamon and mix thoroughly
10. Pour in three more tablespoons of sour cream and mix lightly
11. Pour this sauce over the dumplings on the serving plate
