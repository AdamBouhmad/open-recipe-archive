## Ingredients

- tenderloin
- lard for larding
- hot butter
- various vegetables
- potatoes fried raw
- Italian pasta
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Remove sinews and lard the tenderloin
2. Place on a roasting pan with hot butter
3. Initially sear on high heat over an open flame until browned
4. When browned, place in a hot oven
5. Serve with various vegetables, preferably raw fried potatoes, Italian pasta, and mushrooms
