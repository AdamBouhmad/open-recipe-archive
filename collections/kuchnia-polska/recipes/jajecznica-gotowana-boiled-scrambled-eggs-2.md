## Ingredients

- five or six eggs
- salted water
- smoked bacon, thinly sliced

## Directions

1. Beat five or six eggs until uniform
2. Pour them into a pot of boiling salted water and cook for two minutes, stirring with a spoon
3. Pour everything onto a colander to drain the water
4. Arrange the scrambled eggs on a serving platter
5. Garnish with thin slices of fried smoked bacon
6. Use plenty of water in the pot, at least two quarts for six eggs
