## Ingredients

- thin slice of meat (preferably first-cut or cutlet)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 onion, parboiled or roasted
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut a thin slice of meat (the best is first-cut or cutlet) and beat it strongly into a round slice the size of a plate and salt it.
2. Put 1/2 tablespoon of butter into a pan, and when it is well browned, place the meat in it and fry on very high heat for 2 minutes on one side.
3. Turn to the other side.
4. Have a parboiled or roasted onion ready, and when the meat is turned to the other side, place this onion on the edge of the pan and fry it so that it just softens well but does not brown.
5. The entire frying should not last longer than 5 minutes, as the quality of schnitzel depends on quick frying.
