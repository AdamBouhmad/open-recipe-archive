## Ingredients

- six medium raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- four tablespoons hot sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into quarter-inch thick slices six medium raw potatoes
2. Place in a baking pan or tin
3. Place several pieces of butter, salt and pepper to taste on top
4. Place in a hot oven; when they brown well
5. Transfer to a serving dish and pour over with four tablespoons of hot sour cream
