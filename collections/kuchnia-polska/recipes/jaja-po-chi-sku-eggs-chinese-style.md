## Ingredients

- four eggs
- four large slices of bread
- fresh parsley, chopped
- salt and pepper
- thick tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Hard boil four eggs for at least fifteen minutes.
2. Place in cold water and shell.
3. Trim the whites around with a knife so as not to disturb the yolks.
4. Cut the whites into thin strips using scissors.
5. Make toast from four large slices of bread.
6. Place the toast on a hot platter.
7. On each piece of toast, arrange the cut whites in the form of a nest.
8. Place a whole yolk in the center of each nest.
9. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley around it to form a green circle.
10. Salt and pepper to taste.
11. Pour plenty of thick tomato sauce around each nest over the toast.
12. Serve. This dish looks very attractive due to the contrast of four colors.
