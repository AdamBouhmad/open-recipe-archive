## Ingredients

- two large cooked potatoes
- one onion
- two spoonfuls tapioca
- a few grains allspice
- cold water
- hot milk
- butter
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Pass two large cooked potatoes through a sieve
2. Chop very finely or mince one onion
3. Put the above in a pot, add two spoonfuls of tapioca and a few grains of allspice, pour three half-quarts of cold water over everything and cook until the onion is soft
4. Then add half quart of hot milk, one spoonful of butter, salt and white pepper to taste
5. Bring to boil once more and serve
6. This proportion is for five people
