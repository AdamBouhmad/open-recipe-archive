## Ingredients

- any cold beef, which can be roast or cooked beef
- a considerable amount of grated onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- a couple of tablespoons of broth or any soup
- one tablespoon of flour
- one egg
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Chop any cold beef finely, add a considerable amount of grated onion, salt and pepper to taste
2. Add a couple of tablespoons of broth or any soup, place on heat, thicken with one tablespoon of flour and cook for five minutes
3. Remove from heat and when it cools, make balls the size of a walnut, dip them in egg and fry in hot lard until brown
4. Then add them to soup
