## Ingredients

- piece of beef suitable for roasting
- salt
- butter
- water
- onion
- fresh grated horseradish
- butter
- grated bread roll
- sugar
- salt and pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon
- twine

## Directions

1. Beat a piece of beef well, salt it, and sear in a roasting pan in hot butter
2. When it is well browned all around, pour water over it, add some onion, and braise under cover on low heat
3. When completely tender, remove from the pan and cut lengthwise through the center to three-quarters of the meat's thickness and open it
4. Line both edges of the cut with thin slices of lemon, peeled of skin and seeds
5. Prepare the filling: take three tablespoons of freshly grated horseradish, two tablespoons of butter, one tablespoon of grated bread roll, one teaspoon of sugar, salt and pepper to taste, and two egg yolks; mix everything into a smooth mass
6. Place the filling between the lemon slices in the cut
7. Tie the meat with twine, place back in the pan, and braise for half an hour or forty minutes
8. Thicken the sauce with flour and sour cream, simmer together briefly, and serve
