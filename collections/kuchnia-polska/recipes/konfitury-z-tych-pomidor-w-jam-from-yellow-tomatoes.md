## Ingredients

- small yellow tomatoes
- sugar equal in weight to the tomatoes
- lemon

## Directions

1. Scald the small yellow tomatoes with boiling water and peel them
2. Take sugar equal in weight to the tomatoes
3. Place the peeled tomatoes in a saucepan and boil for about half an hour until the mass is thick
4. For each quart of tomatoes, take one lemon, cut into small pieces, mix with sugar with the addition of the smallest amount of water and let it stand during the cooking of the tomatoes
5. When the tomatoes are cooked, add the lemon syrup to them
6. Boil once, then remove from the fire
7. Fill jars and store
