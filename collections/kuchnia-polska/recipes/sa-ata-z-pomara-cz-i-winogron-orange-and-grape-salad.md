## Ingredients

- three oranges
- half pound of large white grapes (preferably Malaga)
- half cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel three oranges, remove seeds and chop into cubes
2. Rinse half a pound of large white grapes (the best are called Malaga), cut each in half and remove the seeds
3. Add half a cup of sugar and mix everything well
4. This salad is usually served in orange skins, peeled from the oranges and cut in half
