## Ingredients

- piece of beef suitable for braising
- lard, cut into thick pieces
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- water
- onion
- dried mushrooms
- peppercorns
- flour or grated bread
- tomatoes (optional)

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with fairly thick pieces of lard sprinkled with pepper
2. Salt and let sit for one or two hours
3. Melt a tablespoon of butter in a roasting pan
4. When it is brown, place the meat and on high heat brown it all around without covering
5. The pan should be such a size that the meat fits snugly
6. Pour water over the meat, add sliced onion, a few dried mushrooms, and a few peppercorns
7. Braise under cover on very low heat for two to four hours depending on the size of the roast
8. Be careful that there is never too much water, otherwise the meat will boil rather than braise, and will be neither as juicy nor as tasty
9. Shortly before serving, a few tomatoes can be added and braised together
10. After removing the meat, thicken the remaining sauce with a little flour or grated bread
11. Tomatoes can be omitted and the sauce thickened with flour only
12. Serve separately any vegetables, preferably braised in butter
