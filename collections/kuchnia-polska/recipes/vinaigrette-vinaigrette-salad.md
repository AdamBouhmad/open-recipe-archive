## Ingredients

- cooked fish or herring
- potatoes
- beets
- hard-boiled eggs
- gherkins
- pickled cucumbers
- marinated mushrooms
- olive oil
- strong vinegar
- egg yolks
- sugar
- mustard
- capers
- red cabbage

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes and slice thinly
2. Cook beets and slice thinly
3. Add a couple of hard-boiled eggs, a couple of gherkins, pickled cucumbers and marinated mushrooms, all sliced
4. Add any cooked fish, cut into small pieces and mix together
5. Add a couple of tablespoons of olive oil and one tablespoon of strong vinegar
6. Arrange on a platter and garnish nicely on top and around with sliced eggs, beets, cucumbers, capers, red cabbage, etc.
7. Separately, rub four egg yolks with four tablespoons of olive oil, add two tablespoons of vinegar, a little sugar and a touch of mustard
8. Pour this sauce over the vinaigrette arranged on the platter
