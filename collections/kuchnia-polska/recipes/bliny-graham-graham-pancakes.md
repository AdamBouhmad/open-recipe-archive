## Ingredients

- two cups boiled milk
- one teaspoon sugar
- one teaspoon salt
- half a cake of yeast, finely crumbled
- one cup graham flour
- one cup wheat flour
- quarter teaspoon soda dissolved in two tablespoons of warm milk

## Directions

1. Pour one teaspoon of sugar and one teaspoon of salt into two cups of boiled milk, stir, and set in a cool place.
2. When cooled to lukewarm temperature, add half a finely crumbled cake of yeast and stir until it dissolves.
3. Then add one cup of graham flour and one cup of wheat flour, beat well, and set overnight in a cool place.
4. The next morning, add a quarter teaspoon of soda dissolved in two tablespoons of warm milk, and fry on a hot pan as usual for pancakes.
