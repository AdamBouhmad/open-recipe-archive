## Ingredients

- three whole eggs
- one cup whole milk
- salt to taste
- two spoons grated cheese, preferably Parmesan
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Beat three whole eggs in a bowl, add one cup whole milk, salt to taste and two spoons grated cheese, preferably Parmesan, and mix everything properly.
2. Pour into a buttered baking tin, place the tin in a pot with hot water, which should reach higher than half the height of the tin, place on heat and cook about twenty minutes.
3. When cooked, which can be known by placing a toothpick in the center of the tin—if the dough does not smear on the toothpick it is done—remove the tin and turn it over on the work surface and leave to cool.
4. Then cut with a knife into long dumplings, place in a bowl and cover with broth.
