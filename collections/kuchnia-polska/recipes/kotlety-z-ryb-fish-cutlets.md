## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- milk or water
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- olive oil
- cooked or fried fish
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Brown a spoonful of butter with a spoonful of flour in a pot, add two-thirds of a cup of milk, or water if milk is lacking, and bring to a boil once, then add salt and pepper to taste and remove from heat.
2. Beat in two egg yolks.
3. Pour onto an olive oil-greased plate and mix in about a cup of cold cooked or fried fish, broken into small pieces.
4. Place in a cold place.
5. When completely cooled, turn out onto a work surface and form the mixture into cork-shaped pieces, dip in grated breadcrumbs, then in beaten egg, and again in breadcrumbs.
6. Fry until golden in hot fat.
7. It is best to fry them in a wire basket immersed in boiling fat.
8. The breadcrumbs must be grated very finely.
