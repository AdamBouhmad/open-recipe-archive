## Ingredients

- cabbage head
- butter
- onion
- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- salt
- pepper
- rice
- garlic clove
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Chop finely a cabbage head, put into a pot with a spoonful of butter and thinly sliced onion, and stew covered for about twenty minutes
2. Separately cook a broth from mixed vegetables, use it to thin the cabbage
3. Add salt and pepper to taste and place on fire
4. Then add separately cooked rice, also a clove of garlic and a bit of grated cheese, bring to boil and serve
