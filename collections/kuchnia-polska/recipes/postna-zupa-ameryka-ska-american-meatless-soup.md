## Ingredients

- three medium carrots
- same amount of turnips
- three onions
- sufficient amount of butter
- handful of finely chopped celery
- salt and pepper to taste
- two cloves
- some parsley
- pinch of grated nutmeg
- three quarts of water

## Directions

1. Peel and finely chop three medium carrots, the same amount of turnips, and three onions
2. Fry these vegetables in a pan with sufficient butter until everything is nicely browned
3. Toward the end of frying, also add a handful of finely chopped celery
4. Put into a pot and add salt and pepper to taste, as well as two cloves, some parsley, and a pinch of grated nutmeg
5. Pour in three quarts of water and cook over a low fire for three hours
6. Serve to the table
