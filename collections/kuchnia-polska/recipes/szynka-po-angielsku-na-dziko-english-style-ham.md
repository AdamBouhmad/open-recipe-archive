## Ingredients

- eight thin slices of cooked ham
- mustard
- red pepper
- one tablespoon butter
- three or four tablespoons red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Spread each slice of thinly cut cooked ham thinly with mustard
2. Sprinkle with red pepper
3. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a pan
4. Add three or four tablespoons of red currant jelly
5. Bring to a boil together
6. Arrange the ham slices in the pan and fry until slightly browned
