## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes
- salted water
- butter
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Boil six large potatoes in salted water until soft.
2. Drain the water and leave the pan on the fire for a moment so the potatoes dry.
3. Mash with a masher until smooth.
4. Add butter, half a cup of cream, some salt and white pepper.
5. Beat with a spoon until the potatoes are white.
6. Arrange them on a small metal plate in the form of a pyramid.
7. Place a piece of butter on top and put in a hot oven.
8. When the top forms a crust and browns well, remove from the oven and serve. Can be served on a serving dish or with the plate in which they were baked.
