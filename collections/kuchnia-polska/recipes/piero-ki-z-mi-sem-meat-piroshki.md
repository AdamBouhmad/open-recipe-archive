## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked beef, minced
- 1/4 pound young previously cooked bacon, minced
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 onions, finely minced (preferably roasted)
- mushrooms, minced (if available)

## Directions

1. Mince together the cooked beef and the quarter pound of young previously cooked bacon.
2. Place a tablespoon of butter in a pan and when it melts, add the two finely minced onions (preferably roasted) and fry briefly.
3. Remove from heat and mix with the minced meat.
4. If mushrooms are available, mince a few and add them along with some of the sauce in which they were cooked.
