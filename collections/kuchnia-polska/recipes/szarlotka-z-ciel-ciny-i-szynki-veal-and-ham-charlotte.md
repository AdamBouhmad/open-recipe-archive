## Ingredients

- cold veal from a roast or stew, minced finely to make one and a half cups
- one cup of cooked ham, minced finely
- one small onion, grated
- salt and pepper to taste
- some celery salt
- one egg
- half cup of water
- flour
- lard
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mince finely one and a half cups of cold veal and one cup of cooked ham.
2. Mix them together and add one small onion grated on a grater, salt and pepper to taste, some celery salt, one egg and half cup of water.
3. Mix everything into a smooth mass.
4. Make soft dough from flour, lard and cold water, place on work surface and roll out fairly thinly.
5. Fill a metal plate with the meat mixture, cover the top with the rolled out dough, seal the edges and bake in a moderate oven.
