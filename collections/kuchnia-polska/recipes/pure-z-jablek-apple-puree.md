## Ingredients

- several sour apples
- water
- sugar to taste
- one egg white for every six apples
- cream for serving

## Directions

1. Wash several sour apples carefully, add a little water in a pot and cook until completely soft
2. Drain the water and press the apples through a sieve
3. Add sugar to taste to the resulting mass and mix well, beat one egg white to stiff peaks for every six apples and also mix with the apple mass, then place on ice and leave until serving time
4. Serve cold with cream
