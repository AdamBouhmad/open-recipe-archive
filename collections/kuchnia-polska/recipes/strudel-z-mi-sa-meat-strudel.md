## Ingredients

- 1 large slice tender beef, beaten very thin
- salt
- pepper
- thin slices of young bacon
- roasted onion
- butter
- grated bread roll
- 1 tablespoon butter
- broth
- 1 tablespoon white wine

## Directions

1. Beat a large slice of tender beef as thin as possible.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Line the entire piece with thin slices of young bacon.
4. Grate roasted onion, add butter and grated bread roll, and mix into a paste.
5. Cover the meat with this filling.
6. Roll up and tie with twine.
7. Brown a tablespoon of butter in a wide pan or on a griddle.
8. Place the meat inside and braise on low heat until well browned.
9. Then add broth and a tablespoon of white wine and braise until tender.
