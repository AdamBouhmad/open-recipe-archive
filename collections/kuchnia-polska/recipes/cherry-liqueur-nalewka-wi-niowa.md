## Ingredients

- one quart of ripe cherries
- one quart of strong brandy
- half a pound of sugar or more to taste

## Directions

1. Crush one quart of ripe cherries without damaging the pits
2. Pour one quart of strong brandy over them and set in a cool place for three days
3. After this time, strain and add half a pound of sugar or more to taste
4. When the sugar dissolves, pour into bottles and cork well
