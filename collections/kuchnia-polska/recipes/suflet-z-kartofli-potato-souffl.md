## Ingredients

- two cups grated cooked potatoes
- three tablespoons sour cream
- two egg yolks
- half a teaspoon salt
- two egg whites
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Place two cups of grated cooked potatoes in a saucepan
2. Add three tablespoons of sour cream, two egg yolks, and half a teaspoon of salt
3. Place on fire and stir continuously with a spoon
4. When the entire mass is well heated, remove from heat
5. Add two egg whites beaten into a foam and mix lightly
6. Transfer everything to a buttered baking mold
7. Bake in a hot oven until browned
