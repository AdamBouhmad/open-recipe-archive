## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds finely chopped beef
- salt and pepper to taste
- one chopped onion
- one beaten egg
- half a cup of semolina
- salted water
- half a cup of marinated tomatoes
- a little red pepper
- one tablespoon flour mixed in cold milk

## Directions

1. Salt and pepper the finely chopped beef to taste, add one chopped onion, one beaten egg, and half a cup of semolina
2. Mix everything into a mass and form into balls the size of a hen's egg
3. Braise in a pot with a small amount of salted water
4. Once cooked, remove the meatballs and arrange on a hot platter
5. To the remaining sauce in the pot add half a cup of marinated tomatoes, a little red pepper, and one tablespoon of flour mixed in cold milk
6. Bring to a boil and pour over the meatballs on the platter
