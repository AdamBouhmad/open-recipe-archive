## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- half pound sugar
- five eggs
- one glass wine or brandy
- half glass rose water
- half pound flour
- quarter teaspoon salt
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Wash half pound butter carefully and squeeze out all water
2. Cream the butter in a bowl, then add half pound sugar and beat until completely white and smooth
3. Beat in one egg and mix until uniform; repeat with remaining four eggs, beating four to five minutes after each addition
4. When the whole mixture resembles thick yellow cream, add one glass wine or brandy and half glass rose water
5. Mix well and add half pound dried, sifted, and slightly warmed flour mixed with quarter teaspoon salt
6. Line a form smoothly with buttered paper and pour in batter
7. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top and place in a very slow oven for one and a half hours
8. Cover the form with cardboard or thick paper at first and bake for one hour, then remove cover and let brown for the last half hour
9. If turning the form in the oven, do so very carefully so as not to jar the cake
