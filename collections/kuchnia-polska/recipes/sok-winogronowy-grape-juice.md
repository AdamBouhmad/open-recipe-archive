## Ingredients

- ten pounds grapes
- water

## Directions

1. Remove ten pounds of grapes from stems, place in an enameled pot, add some water and cook until they fall apart
2. Strain through thick cloth, return the juice to the pot
3. Add three pounds of sugar, then bring to a boil
4. Pour into bottles and seal tightly with corks
