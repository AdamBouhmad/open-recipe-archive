## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup grated roll or white bread
- 1/2 cup milk
- one egg white
- two tablespoons of butter
- some chopped parsley
- a lot of pepper
- 2/3 cup cooked ham

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 cup grated roll or white bread in 1/2 cup milk into a mass
2. Remove from heat and add one egg white beaten into a stiff foam, two tablespoons of butter, some chopped parsley and a lot of pepper
3. Chop 2/3 cup cooked ham finely, mix with the previously made mass and beat or chop everything into a mass
4. Press this mass through a fine sieve
5. Make oblong dumplings from this mass and cook them in soup
