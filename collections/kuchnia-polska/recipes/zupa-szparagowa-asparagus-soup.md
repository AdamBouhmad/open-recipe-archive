## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salted boiling water
- broth made on beef bones
- butter and flour, or sour cream or sour milk
- asparagus for garnish

## Directions

1. Chop asparagus into pieces one inch long, place in a pot and cover with salted boiling water
2. Cook for a few minutes to remove the bitterness, then remove and place in previously cooked broth made on beef bones
3. Cook until the asparagus is soft
4. When the asparagus is well cooked, strain it through a colander
5. Season with butter mixed with flour, or with one quarter sour cream or sour milk
6. Separately cook some fine asparagus in salted water, chop it and place in a bowl, then pour the soup over it
7. If the soup is without sour cream, beat two egg yolks in the bowl and pour hot soup over them
