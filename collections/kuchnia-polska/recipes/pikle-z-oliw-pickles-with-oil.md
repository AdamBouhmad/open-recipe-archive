## Ingredients

- one hundred medium cucumbers
- one and a half quarts of white onions
- three ounces of ground white pepper
- three ounces of white mustard seeds
- one ounce of celery seeds
- two cups of good oil
- a small piece of alum
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice cucumbers into equal-sized pieces without peeling
2. Salt them and let them stand for three hours
3. Drain the salted water that has collected; if cucumbers are too salty, rinse with cold water
4. Slice the white onions into equal-sized pieces
5. Add ground white pepper, white mustard seeds, and celery seeds
6. Mix in good oil
7. Add a small piece of alum dissolved in warm water
8. Cover with cold vinegar and seal the jars hermetically
