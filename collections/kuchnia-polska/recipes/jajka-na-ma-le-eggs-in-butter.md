## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Melt some butter in a pan, and when it is well heated, drop eggs into it, holding just above the pan so they don't break, and fry until the whites are set; the yolks should remain soft
2. To fry eggs better, it is recommended to place them in warm water for a few minutes before frying
