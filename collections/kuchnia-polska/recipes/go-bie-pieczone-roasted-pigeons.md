## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- flour
- butter
- bacon
- flour
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and dress the pigeons, then salt them and sprinkle with flour, then place in a roasting pan and roast, basting with butter, as you would with chicken
2. You can also cover them with thin slices of young bacon, tie them, and roast
3. Thicken the sauce with flour and butter, and you can also add sour cream
