## Ingredients

- steak, cut fairly thick
- potatoes, peeled and sliced into half-inch slices
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the steak in the center of a baking dish
2. Arrange peeled and sliced potatoes around it
3. Salt the potatoes and sprinkle with pepper
4. Place a tablespoon of butter, broken into small pieces, on top
5. Do not salt the meat yet
6. Put in the oven, making sure it is not too hot, and bake for twenty minutes to one hour depending on the amount of meat and whether you want it well-cooked or not
7. Salt the steak only after baking, when serving on the table
8. Even lower quality meat prepared this way will taste good
