## Ingredients

- veal
- salt
- carrots
- onions
- celery
- water
- butter
- flour
- broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Arrange the rinsed and salted veal in a roasting pan. Add some carrots, onions and celery sliced into pieces and roast until tender.
2. Pour with water at first, then with the drippings from the pan.
3. When serving, mix a piece of butter with flour, place in the roasting pan, mix with broth and at the end season with lemon.
