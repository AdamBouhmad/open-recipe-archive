## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Flour
- Butter
- Raw tomatoes (2 large tomatoes per chicken)
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as usual for roasting and lightly dust with flour
2. Smear with raw butter and place in a hot oven for no longer than ten minutes, then remove and let cool
3. Stew several raw tomatoes in a pot, calculating two large tomatoes for each chicken, then pass through a sieve so that all the seeds and skins remain on it
4. Cut the cooled chicken in half, place in a pot, pour over the sieved tomatoes, place good butter on top with a little paprika and stew under a cover until tender
5. Arrange on a serving platter, pour over the sauce which must be thick, and serve at table
