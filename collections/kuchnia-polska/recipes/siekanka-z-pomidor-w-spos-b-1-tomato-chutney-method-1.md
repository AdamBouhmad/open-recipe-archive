## Ingredients

- a bunch of green tomatoes
- two pounds of common raisins
- one pound of currants
- five pounds of brown sugar
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- one tablespoon of cloves
- three nutmegs
- quarter cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop a bunch of green tomatoes
2. Add two pounds of common raisins, one pound of currants, five pounds of brown sugar
3. Add cinnamon, cloves, and nutmegs, which must be ground
4. Cook for about three hours, and when almost soft, add quarter cup of vinegar
5. Fill jars and seal
