## Ingredients

- four large cucumbers
- boiling water
- one and a half cups sour cream
- half tablespoon butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel four large cucumbers and cut into slices half an inch thick
2. Place in a pot and cover with just enough boiling water
3. Cook for fifteen minutes or until tender, but they should not fall apart
4. Drain the water
5. In a separate pot pour one and a half cups sour cream, half tablespoon butter, salt and pepper to taste
6. Place on fire and when it just boils, throw in the cooked cucumbers
7. Boil a few times to heat the cucumbers thoroughly
8. Serve on the table
9. These prepared cucumbers can also be served on bread slices, like asparagus
