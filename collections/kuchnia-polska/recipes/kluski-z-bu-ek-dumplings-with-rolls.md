## Ingredients

- several spoons butter
- finely grated roll

## Directions

1. Beat several spoons butter in a bowl into foam, then add finely grated roll until a fairly thick dough forms when mixed.
2. Make small dumplings from this dough and cook them in broth.
3. When they float to the surface, cover and simmer a moment, they will be fluffier and more crisp, then serve with broth on the table.
