## Ingredients

- half a quart of milk
- quarter pound of clarified butter
- two ounces of sugar
- one cup of potato starch or half a quart of wheat flour
- eight egg yolks
- one and a half tablespoons of sugar
- a little nutmeg
- salt to taste
- hot water
- fruit juice, preferably raspberry

## Directions

1. Pour half a quart of milk into a pot, add quarter pound of clarified butter and two ounces of sugar, and heat until well boiling.
2. Add one cup of potato starch or half a quart of wheat flour, pouring gradually and stirring constantly.
3. Cook until the dough pulls away from the sides of the pot, then remove from heat and let cool.
4. When completely cool, slowly beat in eight egg yolks one by one with a spoon.
5. Add one and a half tablespoons of sugar, a little nutmeg, and salt to taste.
6. After mixing well, pour into a mold greased with butter and place the mold in a pot filled with hot water to a depth of one and a half to two inches.
7. Cook on a strong fire for one to two hours, adding hot water as it evaporates.
8. Alternatively, bake in a hot oven for about half an hour.
9. Serve with fruit juice, preferably raspberry.
