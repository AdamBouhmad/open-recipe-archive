## Ingredients

- one cup of molasses
- one cup of brown sugar
- one cup of well-salted lard or lard mixed half with butter
- two well-beaten eggs
- half cup of cold tea
- one teaspoon of soda dissolved in the tea
- half teaspoon of ginger
- half teaspoon of cinnamon
- flour

## Directions

1. Combine molasses, brown sugar and lard or lard mixture
2. Add two well-beaten eggs
3. Add half cup of cold tea with one teaspoon of soda dissolved in it
4. Add half teaspoon of ginger and half teaspoon of cinnamon
5. Add enough flour to obtain soft dough
6. Make small round cookies
7. Bake in a moderately hot oven
