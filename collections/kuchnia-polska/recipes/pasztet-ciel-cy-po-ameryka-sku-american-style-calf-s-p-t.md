## Ingredients

- one pound fresh calf meat, finely chopped
- half pound pork, finely chopped
- two beaten eggs
- two tablespoons grated breadcrumbs or bread
- one large onion, finely chopped
- one teaspoon chopped parsley
- half cup sour cream or milk
- salt to taste
- red pepper
- bacon slices
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Chop very finely one pound of fresh calf meat and half pound of pork
2. Add two beaten eggs, two tablespoons of grated breadcrumbs or bread, one large finely chopped onion, one teaspoon of chopped parsley, half cup of sour cream or milk, salt to taste and a little red pepper
3. Mix everything well
4. Fill a loaf pan with this mixture
5. Place a few slices of bacon and a bay leaf on top
6. Place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour
