## Ingredients

- four large soaked herring
- one large onion
- herring roe
- one tablespoon butter
- one egg
- pepper
- egg
- water
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Soak four large herring for a full day, then wipe dry with paper
2. Clean the herring inside without removing the skin
3. Chop one large onion finely, add finely chopped herring roe, one tablespoon butter, one egg and pepper
4. Mix all ingredients well
5. Stuff the herring with this filling
6. Dip in egg beaten with water and coat in grated bread
7. Fry in hot clarified butter
