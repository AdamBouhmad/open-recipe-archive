## Ingredients

- four eggs
- half a pound cooked or fried cold fish, deboned and crumbled
- salt
- pepper
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs, add to them half a pound of any cooked or fried cold fish, deboned and crumbled into small pieces
2. Mix well and add salt and pepper to taste, and some chopped green parsley
3. Melt half a tablespoon of butter in a frying pan, pour in the eggs with fish
4. Fry on low heat until the entire mass sets
5. Fold in half, place on a hot serving platter and serve
