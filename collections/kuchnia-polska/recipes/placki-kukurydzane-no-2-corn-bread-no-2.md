## Ingredients

- half quart sour milk
- three eggs
- half teaspoon salt
- one cup corn meal
- one teaspoon soda
- hot water
- half cup butter

## Directions

1. Take half quart of sour milk, three well beaten eggs, half teaspoon of salt, one cup of corn meal, one teaspoon of soda dissolved in a small amount of hot water, and half cup of butter
2. Melt the butter in the form in which it is to be baked
3. Mix into smooth dough, pour into the form and bake
