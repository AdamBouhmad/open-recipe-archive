## Ingredients

- one whole egg
- three spoons milk
- three spoons flour
- quarter pound butter

## Directions

1. Beat one whole egg, add three spoons milk and three spoons flour and mix everything well.
2. Melt a quarter pound butter in a pot, pour in the above dough, place on very low heat and stir with a spoon.
3. When the dough pulls away from the pot, remove from heat and cool.
4. Then add one egg, salt, mix well with a spoon soaked in hot water, take the dough with a spoon and place on simmering soup and cook until it floats to the surface, then serve.
