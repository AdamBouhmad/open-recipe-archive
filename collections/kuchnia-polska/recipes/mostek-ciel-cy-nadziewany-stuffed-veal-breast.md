## Ingredients

- Thick veal breast
- Cold water
- Salt
- Young bacon, quarter pound
- One large onion
- Veal liver, half pound
- Pepper
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Nutmeg
- Two whole eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Soak the thick breast in cold water for an hour, then rinse and salt it
2. Chop a quarter pound of young bacon into a mass, grate one large onion, and fry together with the bacon without allowing it to brown
3. Remove the crusts from two bread rolls and soak them in sweet milk
4. Chop veal liver very finely, add pepper, salt, chopped fresh parsley, nutmeg, two whole eggs, and a spoonful of butter
5. Mix everything thoroughly
6. Stuff the breast under the membrane with this filling and roast first in a hot oven, then in a moderately hot oven, basting continuously with butter
7. Do not add water
