## Ingredients

- one pound of dried fruits
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- some fruit juice
- corn flour
- cold water
- small dumplings or croutons

## Directions

1. Rinse one pound of dried fruits properly, pour water over them and cook until soft
2. Press through a sieve, add sugar, cinnamon and some fruit juice
3. Add a sufficient amount of water
4. Thicken with corn flour mixed in cold water
5. Bring to a boil and serve with small dumplings or croutons
