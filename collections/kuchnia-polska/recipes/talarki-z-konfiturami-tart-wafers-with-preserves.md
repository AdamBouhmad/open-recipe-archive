## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- one tablespoon lard
- cold water as needed
- thick preserves, jelly, or jam

## Directions

1. Mix one cup flour well with one tablespoon lard and add enough cold water to make a medium dough
2. Turn out onto a work surface and roll thin
3. Cut into strips about three inches wide and six to eight inches long
4. Spread the strips thickly with thick preserves, jelly, or jam
5. Roll up lengthwise into a log and seal the outer edge well
6. Place on a baking sheet and bake in a hot oven
7. After removing from the oven, cut the entire log into slices and serve
