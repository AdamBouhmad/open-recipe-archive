## Ingredients

- one pound dates
- cold water
- two cups milk
- three eggs
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Rinse one pound dates in warm water
2. Place in a saucepan and add just enough cold water to barely cover them
3. Let stand overnight
4. Next day, place on the fire and cook until completely soft
5. Remove the pits
6. Add two cups milk, three eggs, a little salt and grated nutmeg
7. Mix everything into a smooth paste
8. Make dough from flour, lard and cold water
9. Line a baking pan with this dough, rolled out quite thin
10. Fill with the date mixture and bake
11. The proportion above is for two large charlottes
