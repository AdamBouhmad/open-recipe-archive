## Ingredients

- one and a half cups of grated bread
- two ounces of suet
- half a pound of flour
- two ounces of finely chopped almonds
- two pounds of finely chopped beef suet
- two grated nutmegs
- two pounds of common raisins
- two pounds of currants
- juice and grated zest of one lemon
- two pounds of sugar
- one teaspoon of salt
- two ounces of candied lemon peel fried in sugar
- sixteen eggs
- milk as needed to make a thick mass

## Directions

1. Mix the above ingredients in the order given and set in a cool place overnight
2. The next morning place in greased molds and steam for at least twelve hours
3. Before serving steam for another two hours
4. When serving pour two spoonfuls of brandy over it and ignite
5. Serve with the following sauce: cream half a cup of butter, add one cup of sugar, beat for fifteen minutes, then beat in two eggs and whip to a foam, just before serving add a quarter cup of hot water, beat strongly again, add two glasses of wine and half a teaspoon of vanilla extract
6. This pudding can be kept for a very long time without spoiling
