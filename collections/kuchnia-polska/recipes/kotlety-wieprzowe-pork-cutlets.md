## Ingredients

- pork cutlets
- salt
- egg
- water
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Beat the cutlets well, giving them a neat shape.
2. Salt them.
3. Dip in beaten egg mixed with water.
4. Coat with bread crumbs.
5. Fry in hot butter or lard.
6. Alternatively, instead of dipping in egg, simply salt and coat with flour.
