## Ingredients

- rutabaga
- salt
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one cup hot milk
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Peel and cut rutabaga into half-inch cubes and cook until tender in salted water, then drain the water.
2. Blend raw one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour, dilute slowly by pouring one cup of hot milk, add salt and pepper to taste.
3. Pour this sauce over the rutabaga, mix well and serve on the table.
