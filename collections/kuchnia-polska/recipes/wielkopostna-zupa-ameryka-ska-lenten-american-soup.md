## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- 2 large potatoes
- 2 onions
- 2 quarts of cold water
- 2 tablespoons of fine tapioca
- a little marjoram
- salt to taste
- 2 small cups of milk
- 1/2 tablespoon of flour
- 1/2 tablespoon of butter
- small croutons

## Directions

1. Pour 2 quarts of cold water over 3 tomatoes, 2 large potatoes, and 2 onions
2. Cook until everything is soft
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Add 2 tablespoons of fine tapioca, a little marjoram, and salt to taste
5. Cook for another 20 minutes
6. Separately boil 2 small cups of milk
7. Mix 1/2 tablespoon of flour well with 1/2 tablespoon of butter
8. Add this mixture to the milk
9. Pour into the soup, boil again, and serve with small croutons
