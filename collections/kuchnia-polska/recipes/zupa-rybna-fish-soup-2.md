## Ingredients

- assorted vegetables and onion
- fish

## Directions

1. Fish soup is best made from crucians or pike, though in America they most often use perch
2. Cook a lot of chopped vegetables and onion, and on this stock place the fish, making sure it does not fall apart; fish cooks for 15 to 25 minutes, depending on size
3. When you feel by touching with a fork that the fish is already soft, remove the fish, strain the soup, add one tablespoon of butter, and on this same stock cook small dumplings
4. When serving, thicken with sour cream
5. If the fish is large, you can cut off the head and put it in the pot along with the vegetables
