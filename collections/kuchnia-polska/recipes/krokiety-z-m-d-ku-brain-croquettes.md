## Ingredients

- veal brain
- salted water
- butter, one tablespoon
- flour, one tablespoon
- two egg yolks
- salt and white pepper
- bread roll
- egg whites
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Carefully clean the veal brain so no veins or membranes remain and blanch in salted water.
2. Boil one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour, beat in two egg yolks, add the blanched brain and mash everything into a smooth mass with salt and white pepper added.
3. Spread this mass on a work surface sprinkled with grated bread and form into long small rolls.
4. Beat the remaining whites to a stiff foam and dip the croquettes in it and coat with grated and sifted bread; repeat this several times until the croquettes are evenly coated in bread.
5. Then drop them into hot fat in a pan and fry until evenly browned on all sides.
6. Serve on a platter lined with a napkin and garnish with sprigs of fresh parsley.
