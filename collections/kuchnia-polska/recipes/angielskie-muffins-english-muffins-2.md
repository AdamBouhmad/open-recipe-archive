## Ingredients

- one and a half cups lukewarm water
- one teaspoon salt
- two-thirds tablet of pressed yeast
- three tablespoons lukewarm water
- four cups wheat flour

## Directions

1. Pour one and a half cups of lukewarm water into a bowl, add one teaspoon of salt
2. Dissolve two-thirds of a tablet of pressed yeast in three tablespoons of lukewarm water, then add to the bowl
3. Slowly add, while constantly stirring, four cups of wheat flour
4. Beat perfectly and place in a warm place so the dough rises, which should take five hours
5. When well risen, turn out onto a work surface and form round cakes, less than half an inch thick and two and a half inches wide
6. Arrange these on a warm buttered pan or baking sheet, place on a warm stove so they slowly rise, which will take about twenty minutes
7. Then move the pan to a hotter place and bake, turning frequently, which should take fifteen minutes
8. Brush with butter while hot and serve immediately
