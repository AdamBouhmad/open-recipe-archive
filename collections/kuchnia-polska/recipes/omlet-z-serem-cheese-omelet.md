## Ingredients

- five egg yolks
- five tablespoons milk
- half a small teaspoon salt
- two pinches pepper
- remaining egg whites
- butter
- half a cup cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Beat five egg yolks well, add five tablespoons milk, half a small teaspoon salt and two pinches pepper
2. Beat the remaining egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently mix with the yolks
3. Butter a large frying pan well, heat it thoroughly and pour in the eggs
4. Cover with a lid and fry on low heat until the bottom is well browned
5. Then put in the oven for a minute to dry the omelet slightly on top
6. After removing from the oven, grate half a cup of cheese and sprinkle on top of the omelet
7. Fold the omelet in half and serve immediately
