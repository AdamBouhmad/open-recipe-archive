## Ingredients

- beef meat with bone, preferably with marrow
- small amount of water
- a few beets
- parsley
- carrot
- sugar
- a whole onion
- a few dried mushrooms
- a few small red beets
- beet kvass

## Directions

1. Take beef meat with bone, preferably with marrow, and pour a small amount of water over it, then cook like regular broth
2. Separately, lightly sauté a few beets, parsley, and carrot, sprinkle with sugar, and when they release juice, add to the simmering broth along with one whole onion and a few dried mushrooms
3. Cook a few small red beets separately, then peel them, chop finely, and pour beet kvass over them; set in a warm place and let stand for an hour or slightly longer
4. When the broth is cooked, pour in the kvass mixture, bring to a boil, and immediately remove from heat; do not cook longer as it becomes bitter and unpleasant
5. Before serving, strain the soup so all additions remain in the strainer, then pour into a dish and add a handful of previously cooked and chopped red beets
6. Serve with uszka, sliced cooked kielbasa, eggs in their shells carefully added to the plate, or even cooked potatoes
