## Ingredients

- beef broth with vegetables
- flour, two spoonfuls
- eggs, two whole
- cold water

## Directions

1. Prepare broth from beef meat with various vegetables, strain it, pour it back into the pot, and place over heat. Beat two spoonfuls of flour with two whole eggs and cold water to make a thin batter. Form a paper cone and pour the batter through it onto the simmering broth.
