## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- two medium onions, finely chopped
- one clove of garlic
- one tablespoon of finely chopped cooked ham
- one tablespoon of flour
- one large cup of canned tomatoes
- salt
- red pepper
- one tablespoon of sugar
- two tablespoons of vinegar

## Directions

1. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a pan and add two medium finely chopped onions and one clove of garlic
2. Fry over low heat until the onion slightly browns
3. Then add one tablespoon of finely chopped cooked ham and one tablespoon of flour and fry a few minutes longer
4. Add one large cup of canned tomatoes (about half the contents of a regular can) along with their juice
5. Add salt and red pepper to taste, one tablespoon of sugar, and two tablespoons of vinegar
6. Mix everything together and cook for fifteen minutes
7. Then pour the sauce over meat
