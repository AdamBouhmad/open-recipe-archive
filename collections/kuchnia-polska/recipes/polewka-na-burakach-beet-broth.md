## Ingredients

- several red beets
- half a quart of milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of flour
- half a cup of sour cream

## Directions

1. Grate several red beets on a grater and strain so as to extract all the juice, which pour into a pan and bring to a boil
2. Separately bring half a quart of milk to a boil and pour into the beet juice, also add salt and pepper to taste
3. Brown one spoon of butter with one spoon of flour in a pan and add gradually to the soup, stirring constantly, bring to a boil and serve
4. The soup will be more tasty by adding half a cup of sour cream
