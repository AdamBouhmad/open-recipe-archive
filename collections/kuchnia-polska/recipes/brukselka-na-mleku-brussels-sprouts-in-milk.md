## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- water
- salt
- milk
- butter
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Brussels sprouts are small cabbages with heads barely the size of a walnut growing around a tall stalk.
2. Peel and blanch the Brussels sprout heads, throw into boiling water for five minutes, then turn out onto a sieve and drain.
3. When all the water has drained away, place Brussels sprouts in a pan, pour warm milk, salt and cook until tender.
4. Then drain the excess milk, pour the cabbage with butter mixed with a browned roll and serve.
