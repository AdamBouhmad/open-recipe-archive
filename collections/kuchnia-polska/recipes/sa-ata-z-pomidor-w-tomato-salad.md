## Ingredients

- four large tomatoes
- two medium cucumbers
- lettuce leaves
- chives, finely chopped
- fresh parsley
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over the tomatoes, then peel off the skin
2. Slice or cut the tomatoes into pieces
3. Peel and slice the two cucumbers
4. Mix the tomatoes and cucumbers together
5. Arrange on a plate lined with lettuce leaves
6. Sprinkle finely chopped chives and fresh parsley over top
7. Pour French sauce made from oil, vinegar, salt and pepper over everything
