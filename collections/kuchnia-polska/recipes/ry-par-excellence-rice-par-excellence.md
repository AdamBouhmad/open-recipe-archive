## Ingredients

- one cup rice
- three and a half cups salted water
- butter
- cream or syrup

## Directions

1. Rinse one cup rice in several waters
2. Pour into a saucepan and add three and a half cups salted water
3. Cook for twenty minutes, counting from when it comes to a boil
4. Strain the rice through a colander
5. Grease the saucepan with butter
6. When water has drained from the rice, return the rice to the saucepan
7. Place one tablespoon of butter on top
8. Cover with a lid and place in a warm spot
9. When the butter melts, serve to the table
10. Serve with cream or syrup
