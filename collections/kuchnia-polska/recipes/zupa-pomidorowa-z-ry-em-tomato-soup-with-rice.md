## Ingredients

- two large cupfuls of pickled or fresh tomatoes
- half cupful of rice
- two onions
- one carrot
- three tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one quart of cold water
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the onion and carrot finely and fry in a pot over low heat for twenty minutes
2. Pour the tomatoes and one quart of cold water into the pot and bring to a boil
3. Remove the carrot and onion from the butter and add to the boiling tomatoes
4. To the remaining butter in the pot, add two tablespoons of flour and brown to a smooth paste
5. Pour the soup over this and place on the fire, cooking slowly for half an hour
6. When all vegetables are soft, press through a sieve so that only seeds and skin remain, then discard these
7. Rinse the rice, add to the soup along with salt and pepper to taste
8. Cook over low heat for one hour, stirring from time to time so the rice does not burn
9. Serve directly from the fire
