## Ingredients

- salad greens
- one cup of thick sour cream
- a few tablespoons of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Sort, wash and drain the salad as above
2. Prepare the dressing as follows: Take one cup of thick sour cream, a few tablespoons of vinegar, salt and pepper to taste, and a little sugar
3. Beat all of this in a small bowl
4. Pour this sauce over the salad and mix well
