## Ingredients

- large pike or sandacz fish
- salt
- various vegetables
- onion
- roots
- flour
- unsalted butter
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- mushrooms (if available)

## Directions

1. Clean and salt the large fish an hour before use.
2. Prepare a court-bouillon (smak) from various vegetables, onion and roots.
3. Cool the court-bouillon and pour it over the fish.
4. Cook until the fish is tender, being careful not to overcook it.
5. In another pan, make a roux by frying one spoon of flour with one spoon of unsalted butter.
6. Dilute the roux with a quarter of the court-bouillon from the cooked fish.
7. Add two spoons of sour cream and some lemon juice to the sauce.
8. If available, add several sliced mushrooms.
9. Place the cooked fish in this sauce, bring to a boil and serve at table.
