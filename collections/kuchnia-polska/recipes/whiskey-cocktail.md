## Ingredients

- two-thirds quart of ordinary vodka
- one-third quart of water
- one liqueur glass of Peyschaud or Angostura Bitters
- two liqueur glasses of syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as Brandy Cocktail, using ordinary vodka instead of brandy
