## Ingredients

- one egg yolk
- one tablespoon olive oil
- three tablespoons grated horseradish
- two tablespoons sour cream
- juice from one grated raw carrot
- one teaspoon strong vinegar
- salt
- white pepper
- strong cold broth or bouillon

## Directions

1. Mix one egg yolk with one tablespoon olive oil.
2. Add three tablespoons of grated horseradish, two tablespoons of sour cream, juice squeezed from one grated raw carrot, one teaspoon of strong vinegar, salt and white pepper to taste.
3. Mix everything into a uniform mass, dilute with strong cold broth or bouillon and strain through a fine sieve.
4. If the sauce is too sharp, it can be tempered after straining by adding one teaspoon of fine sugar.
5. This sauce is used for cold meat, suckling pig or fish.
