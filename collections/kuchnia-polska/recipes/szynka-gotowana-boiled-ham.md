## Ingredients

- ham with skin
- water
- vegetables
- pepper
- bay leaves
- green peas
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Select ham from young pigs that is not too fatty.
2. Always cook ham with its skin on, as ham cooked without skin will be tasteless.
3. Before cooking, wash the ham well in water and soak in cold water for four hours.
4. Place in a pot, cover with fresh cold water.
5. Add some vegetables, pepper, and a couple of bay leaves.
6. Cook until tender, which will take three to five hours depending on size; it is done when the meat separates easily from the bone.
7. If the ham is to be served hot: remove the skin after cooking, separate the meat from the bone, slice the meat and arrange the slices back around the bone on a platter; serve with green peas and horseradish.
8. If the ham is to be used cold, leave it in the cooking water until completely cooled.
9. To carve: with a sharp knife break out the lower bone, then remove the round bone next to it; remove the skin and carve from the side where the bone was; when you reach the thick leg bone, turn the ham over and carve from the other side.
