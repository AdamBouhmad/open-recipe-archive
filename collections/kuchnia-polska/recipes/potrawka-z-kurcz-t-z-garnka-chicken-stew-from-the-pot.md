## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Various vegetables and greens
- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Green parsley, chopped
- Flour
- Cooked vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean the chicken as usual, cut into quarters and place in a pot, preferably earthenware with a wide bottom
2. Add various vegetables and greens, one spoonful of butter, a few small mushrooms, add salt to taste, cover with a lid and stew very slowly, preferably not on fire but in a hot oven
3. After half an hour throw in chopped green parsley, shake the pot several times and stew further until tender, which altogether should take about an hour
4. Finally thicken the sauce with flour, bring to a boil, then pour out the chicken and arrange on a platter and pour over the sauce, and sprinkle on top with finely chopped cooked vegetables
