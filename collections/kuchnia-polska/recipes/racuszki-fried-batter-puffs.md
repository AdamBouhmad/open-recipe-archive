## Ingredients

- half quart flour
- cup sour cream
- two whole eggs
- salt
- pound of lard or fat for frying
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Sift half quart flour, add cup of sour cream, two whole eggs and a bit of salt and beat everything well, beating for at least twenty minutes
2. Heat a pound of fat in a baking dish or deep pan; the fat must be hot enough that a piece of bread crust thrown in sizzles immediately
3. As soon as the fat is sufficiently heated, take batter by spoon and pour onto the fat in the form of small pancakes
4. When they brown, remove with a fork, drain well from the fat and place on paper so the remaining fat soaks in, then arrange on a platter and serve to the table
5. Serve separately with berry or fruit juice
