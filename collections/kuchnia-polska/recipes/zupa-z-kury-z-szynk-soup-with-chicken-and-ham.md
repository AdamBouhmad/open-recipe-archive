## Ingredients

- one chicken, quartered
- half pound ham
- two onions
- three quarts water
- half a cup rice
- two cups good milk
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Quarter the chicken and place in a pot with a half pound piece of ham, add two onions and cover with three quarts of water.
2. Cook on low heat until one-third of the water has evaporated and the meat pulls away from the bones.
3. Then add half a cup of rice.
4. Cook until the rice is soft and then remove the meat.
5. Enrich the soup with two cups of good milk mixed with a little flour, then serve at table.
