## Ingredients

- beef broth with vegetables
- sauerkraut
- butter
- buckwheat groats

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary broth with vegetables
2. Squeeze sauerkraut firmly of salt and juice, chop finely
3. Place in a pot with butter
4. Add some broth and cook until tender
5. Place the cooked cabbage in a dish and dilute with broth
6. Serve with fried buckwheat groats prepared as follows: Cook coarse buckwheat groats to tenderness, pour onto a board, roll it flat and let it cool
7. Once the groats have cooled well, cut it with a knife into long pieces and fry in a pan in butter or young lard
