## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter mixed half with lard
- one cup of sour milk
- one teaspoon of soda
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- some extract for flavoring
- flour

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients with enough flour so that the dough can be rolled
2. Roll out to half an inch thickness
3. Bake on a baking sheet
