## Ingredients

- five cups of whole wheat flour
- two cups of warm water
- one tablet of pressed yeast
- two tablespoons of molasses
- half teaspoon of salt
- one tablespoon of butter or lard

## Directions

1. Mix five cups of whole wheat flour, two cups of warm water, one tablet of pressed yeast, two tablespoons of molasses, half teaspoon of salt, and one tablespoon of butter or lard into a dough stiff enough to knead
2. Turn onto a floured surface and knead for about fifteen minutes
3. Cover and place in a warm location to rise
4. Knead again, divide into loaves, let rise a second time
5. Bake in a medium oven for about forty-five minutes
6. This bread can be varied by adding raisins, chopped walnuts, a portion of sifted flour, or similar additions
