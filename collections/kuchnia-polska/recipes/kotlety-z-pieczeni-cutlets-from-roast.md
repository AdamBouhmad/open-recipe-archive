## Ingredients

- Leftover roasted veal, preferably with kidney
- One bread roll, soaked
- One whole egg
- One egg yolk
- Half a spoonful of butter
- Salt and pepper
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the leftover roasted veal very finely
2. For every pound of chopped meat, take one soaked bread roll, one whole egg, one egg yolk, half a spoonful of butter, salt, and pepper to taste
3. Mix everything carefully
4. Form into thin round cutlets and fry in butter until browned
