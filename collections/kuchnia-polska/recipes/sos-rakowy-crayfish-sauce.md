## Ingredients

- cooked crayfish shells
- butter
- flour
- good raw butter
- salt
- white pepper
- strong broth or bouillon
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pound cooked crayfish shells in a mortar, place in a small pot, add butter and stew on gentle heat.
2. Crayfish butter will form on the surface, which should be carefully collected with a spoon.
3. Two tablespoons of this butter boil on gentle heat with one tablespoon flour.
4. Add one tablespoon of good raw butter, salt and white pepper to taste.
5. Dilute with one-quarter portion of strong broth or bouillon, place on gentle heat and cook until the sauce is sufficiently thick.
6. Beat two egg yolks with one tablespoon sweet cream and season the sauce, which should previously be removed from heat so that the yolks do not curdle.
