## Ingredients

- beets
- butter or lard
- one tablespoon flour
- boiled vinegar to taste
- a few tablespoons of sour cream
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Trim beets, scald with boiling water, cover with soft water and cook until tender.
2. When completely soft, peel them and cut into thin strips.
3. Place plenty of butter or lard in a pan, and when it heats up, add one tablespoon of flour and bring to a boil together, being careful not to brown it.
4. Salt, add boiled vinegar to taste, add the beets, pour in a few tablespoons of sour cream, cover tightly and braise on low heat for half an hour, then serve on the table.
5. Alternatively, instead of cutting into strips, beets can be grated, but then they should be braised for only about fifteen minutes.
