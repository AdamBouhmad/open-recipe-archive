## Ingredients

- white bread or rolls without crust
- butter
- grated cheese - green, Swiss, or Parmesan

## Directions

1. Cut the bread or rolls without crust into small, very thin slices
2. Spread each slice with good butter and sprinkle with grated cheese - green, Swiss, or Parmesan
3. Place in a moderately hot oven for about ten minutes
4. When the croutons are well dried, remove and serve with soup
