## Ingredients

- fresh sausages
- water
- butter
- vinegar
- flour
- fat
- caramel for brown color
- pickled gherkins, finely chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Arrange fresh sausages in a pot and cover with enough water to just cover them.
2. Cook for twenty minutes, being careful not to let them burn.
3. Add a piece of butter and fry until browned.
4. Then pour in several tablespoons of water and vinegar.
5. Sprinkle in some flour mixed with fat.
6. Bring together to a boil and serve.
7. For a brown sauce, add a tablespoon of caramel.
8. If desired, finely chopped pickled gherkins can be added to the sauce; in that case, remove the sausage while making the sauce.
