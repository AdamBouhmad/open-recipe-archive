## Ingredients

- one pound of lean cooked beef
- a quarter pound of cooked bacon
- a tablespoon of butter
- half a tablespoon of chopped roasted onion
- a few cooked mushrooms, finely chopped
- a couple of tablespoons of water in which the mushrooms were cooked
- a couple of tablespoons of grated bread roll
- one or two egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste
- half a quart of flour
- one egg
- water

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked lean beef and cooked bacon together very finely, almost into a mass
2. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a pan and add half a tablespoon of chopped roasted onion, then remove from heat
3. Place the chopped meat in the pan with butter, add a few finely chopped cooked mushrooms
4. Pour in a couple of tablespoons of the water in which the mushrooms were cooked and add a couple of tablespoons of grated bread roll
5. Add one or two egg yolks and salt and pepper to taste, mix well
6. Make a soft dough from half a quart of flour, one egg and water
7. Roll out fairly thinly and cut into square pieces, but cut diagonally so that when folded in half they form an even triangle
8. Place filling on the dough, fold the two opposite sharp corners together, seal firmly
9. Drop into salted boiling water and cook
10. This proportion is for eight people
11. For fasting soup uszka, make them the same way but use mushrooms and butter instead of meat and bacon
