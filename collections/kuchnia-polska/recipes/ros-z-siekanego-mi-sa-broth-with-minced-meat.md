## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon good butter
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 2 pounds lean beef, finely chopped
- 1 quart cold water
- 1 egg white, well beaten
- 1 eggshell
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- a few cloves
- a pinch of nutmeg
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pot, add sliced onion of medium size and braise on low heat under a lid until the onion lightly browns, stirring from time to time
2. Add 2 pounds of finely chopped lean beef to the browned onion, pour in 1 quart of cold water, cover with a lid and cook on very low heat for 3 hours, then strain and pour back into the pot
3. Add 1 well-beaten egg white, 1 eggshell, 1 tablespoon of cold water, a few cloves, a pinch of nutmeg, salt and pepper to taste
4. Cook for 5 minutes, then strain again and serve
