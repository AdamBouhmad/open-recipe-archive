## Ingredients

- Veal, sliced thin
- Rye bread
- Onion, roasted and grated
- Salt and pepper
- English herbs, powdered
- Butter

## Directions

1. Slice the veal into small thin pieces
2. Grate some rye bread, add roasted onion grated on a grater, pepper and salt to taste, and some powdered English herbs
3. Arrange the zrazy in a pot on hot butter, sprinkling with the bread mixture, cover tightly, and place in a hot oven for half an hour
4. Serve from the pot to the table
5. Alternatively, the zrazy can be braised on low heat, but they will not be as good
6. Potatoes cut into quarters can be braised together with the meat
