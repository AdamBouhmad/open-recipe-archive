## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter
- two tablespoons cream or milk
- four eggs
- four sardines, cleaned of bones and skin, crumbled
- one half tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Heat one tablespoon of butter with two tablespoons of cream or milk in a pan, and when it boils, break in four eggs and stir with a spoon over heat until the eggs just begin to set
2. Then add four cleaned and crumbled sardines and fry for a few more minutes
3. Pour in one half tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce and serve immediately at the table
