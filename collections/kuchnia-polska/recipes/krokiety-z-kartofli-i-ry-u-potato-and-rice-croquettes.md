## Ingredients

- one cup cold cooked potatoes
- one cup cold cooked rice
- salt and pepper to taste
- celery salt, optional
- half a cup milk
- flour
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Grate one cup of cold cooked potatoes into a mass
2. Add one cup of cold cooked rice
3. Season with salt, pepper, and celery salt if desired
4. Pour in half a cup of milk and mix everything into a smooth mass
5. Transfer to a board and form into small oblong patties
6. Coat in flour and fry in hot fat
