## Ingredients

- pike
- salt and pepper
- horseradish root
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the pike as usual
2. Cut into rings, sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Grate a good amount of horseradish root and scald with boiling water
4. Put one tablespoon butter in a saucepan, then add the chopped fish
5. Sprinkle with horseradish and cover with sour cream
6. Cover and stew on low heat until tender, which should take at most half an hour
7. During cooking, shake the pan frequently to prevent burning
