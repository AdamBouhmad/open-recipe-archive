## Ingredients

- white or red cabbage
- vinegar
- two tablespoons of oil
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- half tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Shred the cabbage as finely as possible
2. Throw it into boiling water, and as soon as it comes to a boil, immediately drain the water and squeeze out the cabbage
3. Place in a salad bowl and pour vinegar over it and let it sit for half an hour
4. Then drain the vinegar
5. For a small head of cabbage, take two tablespoons of oil, one tablespoon of vinegar and half a tablespoon of sugar; mix well and pour over the cabbage
6. Prepare the above sauce an hour before serving the salad; after pouring the sauce, serve immediately at table
7. American version: Heat half a cup of vinegar with one tablespoon of butter in a double boiler; beat one egg yolk with one tablespoon of flour and one tablespoon of sugar, add two tablespoons of sour cream, pour it all into the vinegar and cook stirring until it becomes a smooth mass; beat the egg white, mix with the sauce and immediately pour over the finely shredded and salted cabbage arranged in a salad bowl
8. Or instead of the above sauce, if you want to prepare the salad quickly, pour French sauce made only of vinegar, oil and pepper
