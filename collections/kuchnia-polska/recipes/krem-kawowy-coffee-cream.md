## Ingredients

- half a quart of cream
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- two cups of strong coffee
- eight gelatin leaves

## Directions

1. Beat half a quart of cream with half a pound of powdered sugar into stiff meringue
2. Cook two cups of strong coffee and dissolve eight gelatin leaves in it; when the coffee cools, pour it into the beaten cream, mix thoroughly and pour into a mold rinsed with water and sprinkled with sugar and set on ice until serving
3. Tea cream is made in the same way, taking strong tea essence instead of coffee
