## Ingredients

- sauerkraut
- butter
- dried mushrooms
- water
- onion

## Directions

1. Squeeze the sauerkraut out of its liquid and place in a pan in which you have previously dissolved a tablespoon of butter.
2. Cook a few dried mushrooms in a small amount of water, pour the liquid into the cabbage, and finely chop the mushrooms and add them as well.
3. Also add some finely chopped onion.
4. Mix everything and braise until completely tender.
5. On serving season with butter browned with flour, bring to a boil and serve.
6. In the same way cabbage can be prepared with lard or smoked meat.
