## Ingredients

- veal liver
- milk
- lard bacon
- butter
- sour cream
- vinegar
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the veal liver in milk for an hour, then remove the membrane and lard it with thinly sliced bacon.
2. Place in the oven. At the beginning of roasting, baste with butter, then subsequently with sour cream with the addition of a spoonful of vinegar.
3. Toward the end of cooking, dust with flour and salt.
