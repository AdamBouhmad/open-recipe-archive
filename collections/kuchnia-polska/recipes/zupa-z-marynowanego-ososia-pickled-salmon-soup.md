## Ingredients

- one small can of pickled salmon
- one quart of water
- a few chopped onions
- one quart of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- a few stalks of celery (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a small can of pickled salmon, remove bones and skin so that one large cupful of meat remains
2. Pour one quart of water over the meat and add a few chopped onions
3. Boil slowly for fifteen to twenty minutes, then press everything through a sieve
4. Bring one quart of milk to a boil and thicken it with one tablespoon of butter rubbed with one tablespoon of flour
5. Pour into the soup, season with salt and pepper to taste and serve
6. If desired, add a few stalks of celery when cooking the soup
