## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound fresh lard
- a couple of onions
- two rolls
- a few tablespoons of broth
- two whole eggs
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the fresh lard into small cubes and finely chop a couple of onions and fry them together with the lard so that the lard is only half fried
2. Chop two rolls finely, add to the lard, mix well and continue frying, stirring constantly, until the bread is dry
3. Pour in a few tablespoons of broth, beat in two whole eggs, add salt and pepper to taste and mix everything into a thick mass
4. Make balls the size of a walnut and cook them in broth or soup
