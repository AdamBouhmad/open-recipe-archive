## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- one egg
- water as needed
- cheese
- butter
- egg yolk
- sugar
- chives, finely chopped (optional)
- raisins (optional)
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Knead a not-very-stiff dough using one quart of flour, one egg, and as much water as needed.
2. Roll out the dough and place small heaps of cheese on it, which should be well-mixed with butter, egg yolk, and sugar.
3. Cover with dough and cut out small pierogi using a glass or small cup.
4. Drop into boiling water in a large pot, and when they float to the surface, remove with a slotted spoon and arrange on a serving platter.
5. Pour melted butter over them and serve, passing sour cream separately on the side.
6. If desired, finely chopped chives or raisins can be added to the cheese.
