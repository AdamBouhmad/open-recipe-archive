## Ingredients

- one cup cooked rice
- half tablespoon sugar
- one tablespoon melted butter
- one cup milk
- half tablet of pressed yeast
- wheat flour as needed

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of cooked rice, half tablespoon of sugar, and one tablespoon of melted butter perfectly
2. Boil one cup of milk and cool to lukewarm temperature, then dissolve half a tablet of pressed yeast in it
3. Pour into the rice and add enough wheat flour to obtain fairly thick dough
4. Place in a warm place
5. When well risen, add three beaten eggs, mix, and fill molds halfway
6. When it has risen well again, place in a hot oven for ten minutes
7. The molds must be well buttered
