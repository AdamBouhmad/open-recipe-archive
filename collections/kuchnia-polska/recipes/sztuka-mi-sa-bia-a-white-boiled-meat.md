## Ingredients

- cross-cut meat
- vegetables (carrots, cabbage, celeriac)
- small potatoes
- butter
- flour
- fresh parsley or dill
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook broth with vegetables as usual.
2. When the meat is almost done, remove it from the broth and place it in another pot.
3. Pour a small amount of broth (with all fat removed) over it.
4. Add a large variety of finely cut vegetables and braise covered on low heat for 3/4 hour.
5. Prepare potatoes by blanching a few, cutting them into slices, putting them in a pot, covering with fat broth, and cooking.
6. Then add a little butter mixed with a spoonful of flour and chopped fresh parsley or dill.
7. Serve the meat on a platter with various vegetables cooked in broth (such as cabbage cut into quarters, carrots, celeriac) and the potatoes prepared as above, pouring the sauce over it.
