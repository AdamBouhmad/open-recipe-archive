## Ingredients

- lamb caul (szlarl)
- poultry liver, or calf liver if unavailable
- bread crumbs
- milk
- egg yolks
- nutmeg
- orange flower
- egg whites
- salt
- bacon
- sweet almonds
- French wine
- sugar
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean the caul thoroughly.
2. Prepare the stuffing: use poultry livers, or if unavailable, calf liver with membranes removed and finely chopped, then pressed through a sieve to remove veins and large pieces.
3. Grate a couple of bread rolls and add two cups of milk, two egg yolks, a little nutmeg, a touch of orange flower, meringue made from two egg whites, salt to taste, and some bacon cut into small cubes.
4. Mix everything carefully.
5. Stuff the caul with this mixture, sew it, wrap in cheesecloth, place in a pot and cover with cold water.
6. Add whatever vegetables are on hand.
7. Cover and cook for two hours.
8. When serving, pour the following sauce over it: grate ten sweet almonds, add two egg yolks, one glass of French wine, and a little sugar; mix and bring to a boil.
