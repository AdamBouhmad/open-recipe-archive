## Ingredients

- one cup cooked rice
- one egg
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon sugar
- half teaspoon salt
- pinch of nutmeg
- half cup milk
- beaten egg for coating
- grated bread for coating
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Take one cup of cooked rice, one egg, one tablespoon butter, one tablespoon sugar, half teaspoon salt and a pinch of nutmeg
2. Boil half cup of milk, add the rice, sugar, salt and nutmeg
3. Boil once more, then add the beaten egg, mix and place in a cool place
4. When completely cooled, form small cutlets
5. Dip in beaten egg and then in grated bread
6. Fry until golden in hot fat
