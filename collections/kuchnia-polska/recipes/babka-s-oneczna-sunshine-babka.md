## Ingredients

- eleven egg whites
- three-quarters cup sugar
- six egg yolks
- one cup flour
- one teaspoon cream of tartar
- one teaspoon orange extract

## Directions

1. Beat eleven egg whites into foam, then slowly add three-quarters cup of sugar and add six egg yolks beaten until white
2. Add again three-quarters cup of sugar and mix
3. Sift one cup of flour with one teaspoon of cream of tartar and add, as well as one teaspoon of orange extract
4. Bake for about fifty-five minutes in a metal form
