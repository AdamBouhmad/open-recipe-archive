## Ingredients

- one quart of strong brandy
- one cup of orange juice
- half a pound of sugar in pieces
- zest from three oranges

## Directions

1. Take one quart of strong brandy and add one cup of orange juice
2. Add half a pound of sugar in pieces and zest from three oranges, using only the yellow part of the peel, scraped with a sharp knife, discarding the white part
3. Pour all this into a jug or jar and cork tightly
4. Set in a cool place for three days, stirring a few times each day
5. After three days strain the liquid and add more sugar if not sweet enough
6. Pour into bottles and cork
