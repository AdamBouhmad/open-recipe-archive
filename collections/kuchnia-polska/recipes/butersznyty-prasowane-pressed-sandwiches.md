## Ingredients

- large loaf of bread
- cooked ham, chopped
- roasted chicken, chopped
- walnuts, chopped
- mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from a large loaf of bread
2. Slice the bread into thin slices
3. Chop cooked ham, roasted chicken, and walnuts
4. Take one slice of bread and layer chopped ham on it
5. Pour mayonnaise sauce over the ham
6. Add a layer of chopped chicken on top
7. Sprinkle a layer of chopped walnuts
8. Place another slice of bread on top
9. Repeat the layers with ham, mayonnaise, chicken, and walnuts until all ingredients are used
10. Top with a final slice of bread
11. Wrap everything in damp cloth
12. Place a heavy weight on top
13. Let it sit for six to eight hours
14. Remove from press and cut crosswise into slices like bread
15. Serve as an appetizer
