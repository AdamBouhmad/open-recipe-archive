## Ingredients

- carrots
- celery
- kohlrabi
- cauliflower
- cabbage
- parsley
- green peas
- asparagus
- butter
- sugar
- water
- onions
- bread cubes (grzanki)

## Directions

1. Finely chop a large quantity of various vegetables (carrots, celery, kohlrabi, cauliflower, cabbage, parsley, green peas, asparagus, etc.)
2. Place a spoonful of good butter in a pot and add a couple of teaspoonfuls of sugar
3. Add the vegetables and fry covered on low heat, frequently shaking the pot, so the vegetables only brown without burning
4. When everything is nicely browned, add as much water as desired for the amount of soup
5. Add two onions baked in the oven, place on heat and cook until the vegetables fall apart
6. Strain the soup and serve with bread cubes fried in butter
7. Alternatively, serve with stuffed crab shells or thick pasta cooked separately and placed in the bowl before pouring the soup
