## Ingredients

- six medium potatoes
- six eggs
- one tablespoon butter
- two tablespoons sour cream or milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and boil six medium potatoes.
2. Drain the water and let cool.
3. Hard boil six eggs for fifteen minutes.
4. Plunge into cold water and let cool.
5. Chop the eggs and potatoes together, not too finely.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Place in a small pan with one tablespoon of butter and two tablespoons of sour cream or milk.
8. Put on the fire, stir slowly, and when it comes to a boil, serve immediately to the table.
