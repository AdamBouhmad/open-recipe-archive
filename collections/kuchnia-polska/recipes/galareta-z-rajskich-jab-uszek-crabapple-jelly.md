## Ingredients

- crabapples
- water
- sugar, measure for measure with the juice

## Directions

1. Chop crabapples, add a little water and cook until soft
2. Strain the juice and cook until clear
3. Add sugar in the same measure as the juice
4. Cook slowly for twenty minutes
5. Pour into jars or glasses and seal tightly
