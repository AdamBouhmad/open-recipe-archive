## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- one cup sugar
- one and one-half teaspoons baking powder
- three eggs
- six tablespoons hot water
- powdered sugar for dusting
- marmalade or fruit jelly

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly one cup flour, one cup sugar, and one and one-half teaspoons baking powder
2. Beat three eggs and mix with the flour mixture
3. Finally, pour in six tablespoons hot water and beat everything thoroughly into a smooth batter
4. Pour into a flat pan to a depth of only one-quarter inch
5. Bake in a slow oven
6. After baking, turn out onto a sheet of paper sprinkled with powdered sugar
7. Take marmalade or fruit jelly, thin it with a fork, and spread thickly on the baked cake
8. Roll up into a log, wrap in cloth, and place in a cold spot
9. Must be rolled while hot, otherwise the cake will crack
