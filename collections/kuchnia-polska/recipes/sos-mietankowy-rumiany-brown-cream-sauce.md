## Ingredients

- sour cream
- sugar
- vanilla
- one tablespoon of burnt sugar
- six egg yolks
- two tablespoons of cold sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil sour cream with sugar and vanilla as described above
2. Pour in one tablespoon of burnt sugar
3. Beat six egg yolks with two tablespoons of cold sour cream and add to the sauce
4. Then warm the sauce slowly, constantly stirring, until it thickens
5. Cool and serve
6. This sauce prepared this way has both a different color and taste
