## Ingredients

- half pound of flour
- three eggs
- salted water
- half pound of ham
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of sour cream
- butter for greasing the pot
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Make stiff dough from half pound of flour and three eggs, roll out thinly and cut into pasta pieces.
2. Cook in salted water and strain through a colander.
3. Cut half pound of ham into thin strips and place in a bowl with the pasta, add one tablespoon of butter and one tablespoon of sour cream, mix well, and place in a pot greased with butter and sprinkled with grated bread.
4. Put in the oven and when well browned, turn out onto a serving platter and serve at table.
