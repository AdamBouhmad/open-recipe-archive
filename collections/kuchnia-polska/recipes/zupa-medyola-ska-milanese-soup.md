## Ingredients

- half a pound rice
- one onion
- butter
- prepared broth
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse half a pound of rice and cook.
2. Chop one onion finely and fry in butter in a pan.
3. When the onion browns, add the rice to it, mix with the previously prepared broth, place on heat and cook until the rice softens enough that it can be passed through a sieve.
4. After passing through, mix again with the soup, add salt and pepper to taste and serve at table.
