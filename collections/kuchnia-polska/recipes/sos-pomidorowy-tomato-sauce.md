## Ingredients

- six medium tomatoes
- butter
- broth or bouillon
- one teaspoon sugar
- one teaspoon butter
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Chop six medium tomatoes and stew them in butter, moistening with broth or bouillon.
2. Cook until they break down into a paste, then strain through a sieve so that all the seeds remain in it.
3. Then add one teaspoon of sugar.
4. Make a white roux separately with one teaspoon of butter and a little flour, add to the tomatoes, mix well and dilute with strong broth or bouillon to the right consistency.
5. Finally bring to a boil and pour over meat.
