## Ingredients

- half cup butter
- one cup sugar
- two-thirds cup cold water
- two cups flour
- one teaspoon baking powder
- four egg whites
- fresh geranium leaves

## Directions

1. Cream half cup butter with one cup sugar
2. Slowly add two-thirds cup cold water while stirring constantly
3. Add two cups flour sifted with one teaspoon baking powder
4. Beat four egg whites into stiff meringue and gently fold into batter
5. Line a form with buttered paper and place several fresh geranium leaves on the bottom
6. Pour batter over the leaves and place in a moderately hot oven
7. After baking, remove cake and clean off the leaves
8. The geranium leaves impart an unusual flavor and aroma to the cake
