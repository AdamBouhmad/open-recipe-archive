## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water in which asparagus was cooked
- two tablespoons flour
- two tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread slices (optional)

## Directions

1. After washing the asparagus, break it into one-inch pieces (do not cut)
2. Discard the ends that do not break easily in the fingers as they are not good
3. Throw into boiling water and cook for fifteen minutes
4. Remove and arrange on a serving dish
5. For the sauce: take one cup of water in which the asparagus was cooked
6. Brown two tablespoons flour with two tablespoons butter
7. Add to the water and boil
8. Add salt and pepper to taste
9. Pour the sauce over the asparagus
10. Some arrange the dish with bread slices first, then place the asparagus on them and pour the sauce over
