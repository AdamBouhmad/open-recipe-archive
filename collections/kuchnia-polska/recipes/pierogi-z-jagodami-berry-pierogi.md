## Ingredients

- flour
- egg
- water
- berries
- sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Knead flour with egg and water into a stiff dough.
2. Roll out very thinly on a work surface and place small heaps of berries on it.
3. Sprinkle the berries with sugar, wrap the dough around them, and seal the edges well by first brushing them with egg white so they do not come apart during cooking.
4. Leave on the work surface for half an hour so the berries release their juices.
5. Boil a large pot of water and drop the pierogi into it, cooking until they float to the surface, then carefully remove with a slotted spoon and arrange on a serving platter.
6. Serve with sugar and sour cream.
7. If made with cherries, remove the pits and drain excess juice first.
