## Ingredients

- 1 quart of boiling water
- 1/4 pound of white sago
- 1 bottle of white or red sour wine
- 3/4 pound of sugar
- cinnamon in pieces
- lemon peel
- several cloves
- several slices of lemon without seeds

## Directions

1. Pour 1 quart of boiling water into a pot
2. Add 1/4 pound of white sago and cook, constantly stirring, until soft
3. Pour in 1 bottle of white or red sour wine
4. Add 3/4 pound of sugar, some cinnamon in pieces, lemon peel, and several cloves
5. Boil everything together
6. Pour into a bowl into which you have first placed several slices of lemon without seeds
7. This proportion is for eight people
