## Ingredients

- one quart of raspberries
- juice of one whole lemon
- quarter pound of powdered sugar
- six gelatin leaves, soaked previously in cold water
- half a cup of hot milk
- half a quart of cream
- quarter pound of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Press one quart of raspberries through a sieve, add juice from one whole lemon and quarter pound of powdered sugar
2. To six gelatin leaves, soaked previously in cold water, pour half a cup of hot milk and stir until the gelatin completely dissolves, then pour into the raspberry juice and mix
3. Beat half a quart of cream with quarter pound of powdered sugar and then slowly pour the raspberry juice with gelatin into this cream, stirring continuously
4. Beat with a whisk as long as the cream begins to thicken, then pour into a mold rinsed and sprinkled with sugar and set on ice to set
5. Serve at table with small cakes
6. In a similar way one can prepare cream using any other berries
