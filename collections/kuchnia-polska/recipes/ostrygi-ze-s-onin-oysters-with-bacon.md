## Ingredients

- large oysters
- smoked bacon
- salt and pepper
- wooden toothpicks or skewers

## Directions

1. Choose large oysters, salt and pepper them
2. Cut smoked bacon into thin, long slices
3. Wrap each oyster in a slice of bacon and secure with a wooden toothpick or skewer
4. Heat an empty skillet over high heat until very hot
5. Place wrapped oysters in the skillet and fry for about two minutes, shaking constantly to avoid burning
6. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley and serve
