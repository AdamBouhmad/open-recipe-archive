## Ingredients

- four large potatoes
- one onion
- a couple of celery stalks
- a couple of sprigs of parsley
- two quarts of water
- salt and pepper to taste
- one tablespoon of flour
- two heaping tablespoons of good butter
- one cup of good milk

## Directions

1. Cook four large potatoes, one onion, a couple of celery stalks, and a couple of sprigs of parsley in two quarts of water
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Pour back into a pot, add salt and pepper to taste, and one tablespoon of flour mixed with two heaping tablespoons of good butter
4. Pour one cup of good milk into a bowl first, then pour the soup into it
