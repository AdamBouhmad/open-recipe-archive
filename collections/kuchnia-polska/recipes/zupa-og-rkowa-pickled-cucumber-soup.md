## Ingredients

- three large pickled cucumbers
- flour
- butter
- previously cooked ordinary broth
- one quarter of sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel three large pickled cucumbers, cut into slices, sprinkle with flour and fry in a small pot with butter.
2. When the cucumbers are well fried, cover them with previously cooked ordinary broth, put on the fire and cook for at least half an hour.
3. Then strain the soup, season with one quarter of sour cream and serve.
