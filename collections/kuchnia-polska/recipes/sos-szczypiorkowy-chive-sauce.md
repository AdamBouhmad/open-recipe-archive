## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- good broth
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- handful of green chives, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
2. Dilute with good broth
3. Add 1 tablespoon vinegar and a handful of finely chopped green chives
4. Boil and serve
5. Used for meat dishes
