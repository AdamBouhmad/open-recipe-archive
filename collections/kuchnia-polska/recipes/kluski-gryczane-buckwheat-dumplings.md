## Ingredients

- one pound buckwheat flour
- salt
- warm water

## Directions

1. Take one pound buckwheat flour and add salt and enough warm water so the batter is quite thin
2. Mix well and drop large dumplings with a spoon onto boiling water
3. When they float to the surface, place on a serving plate and drizzle with rendered bacon
