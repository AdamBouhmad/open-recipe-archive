## Ingredients

- two quarts chicken broth
- finely chopped celery
- one cup green corn, scraped with a knife
- a pinch of red pepper
- one cup cream or milk

## Directions

1. Take two quarts of chicken broth and add finely chopped celery, one cup of green corn scraped with a knife and a pinch of red pepper.
2. Cook everything for forty minutes, then add one cup of cream or milk and serve.
