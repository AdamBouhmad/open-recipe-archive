## Ingredients

- minced meat prepared as for pieczeń siekana
- bacon
- half a quart coarse buckwheat groats
- water
- flour
- sour cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as described in the pieczeń siekana recipe above
2. Fry half a quart coarse buckwheat groats with bacon, then let cool
3. Dampen a tablecloth with water, spread the meat on it in a thin round slice, arrange the buckwheat lengthwise in the center, roll into an oblong loaf, dust or coat with flour and place on a hot buttered skillet
4. Brown on high heat, then place the skillet in a very hot oven for half an hour or slightly longer
5. Before serving, thicken the sauce with flour and sour cream
