## Ingredients

- kohlrabi cut into equal cubes
- carrots cut into equal cubes
- water
- green peas fresh or canned
- green beans in pods cut into half-inch pieces
- one tablespoon butter
- one teaspoon flour
- triangular bread slices
- sour cream with egg yolk
- butter for frying
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut equal quantities of kohlrabi and carrots into cubes
2. Cover with water, boil, drain the water and pour in fresh hot water
3. Cook until tender
4. Separately cook green peas, fresh or canned
5. When tender, drain and add to the carrots and kohlrabi
6. Similarly cook separately green beans in pods cut into half-inch pieces and also add to the vegetables
7. Season everything with one tablespoon butter browned with one teaspoon flour
8. Mix, boil and arrange on a serving dish
9. Around it arrange triangular bread slices dipped in sour cream with egg yolk, fried in butter and sprinkled with a small amount of sugar
