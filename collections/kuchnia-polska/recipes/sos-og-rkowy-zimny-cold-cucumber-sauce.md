## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers, not very ripe
- 1 onion
- 1/2 teaspoon grated horseradish
- white mustard seeds
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Grate 3 large cucumbers on a grater and place the mass on a colander to let excess juice drain
2. Chop 1 onion very finely
3. Add 1/2 teaspoon grated horseradish and some white mustard seeds
4. Mix everything together and add enough good vinegar to make the sauce rather thin
5. This sauce, kept in a cool place, can be stored for a longer time without spoiling
