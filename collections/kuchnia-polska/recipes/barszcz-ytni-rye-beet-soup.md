## Ingredients

- a few spoons of rye flour
- warm water
- a few red beets
- a small head of cabbage
- hearty broth
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Pour a few spoons of rye flour with warm water and set in a warm place for fermentation, which should take forty-eight hours
2. Separately, cook a few red beets and a small head of cabbage; chop the beets finely and cut the cabbage into larger pieces
3. Have a hearty broth ready, mix it half and half with the rye ferment, bring to a boil together, season with sour cream if desired, and pour into a dish in which cabbage and beets were previously placed
4. Instead of rye flour, one can use ground barley with equally good results
