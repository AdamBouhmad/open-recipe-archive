## Ingredients

- dried mushrooms
- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- butter
- fine groat mixed with egg

## Directions

1. Cook the broth from dried mushrooms with mixed vegetables
2. When the mushrooms are completely soft, strain
3. Salt the broth
4. Add butter
5. Sprinkle in fine groat mixed with egg
