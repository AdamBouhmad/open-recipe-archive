## Ingredients

- large oysters
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- bread
- fat or lard
- paper
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Drain and wipe large oysters almost dry
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and coat in flour, then dip in beaten egg, coat in grated bread
3. Fry in a deep pan filled with hot fat or lard
4. When done, remove and place on paper to absorb excess fat
5. Arrange on a napkin or plate, decorate with parsley sprigs and lemon slices
6. Serve immediately
