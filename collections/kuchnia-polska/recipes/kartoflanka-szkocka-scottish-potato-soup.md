## Ingredients

- lamb broth made from scraps and bones
- various finely chopped vegetables
- few grains of pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 quart of potatoes, cut up
- lard (słonina)
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- fresh dill
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook broth on lamb meat—can be scraps and bones
2. After proper skimming, add plenty of various finely chopped vegetables, a few grains of pepper, two bay leaves and 1 quart of cut potatoes
3. Cook until potatoes are tender
4. Fry some lard cut into small cubes in a pan, fry one onion in it, thicken with 1 tablespoon of flour
5. Add chopped dill, add this to the soup, bring to a boil and serve
