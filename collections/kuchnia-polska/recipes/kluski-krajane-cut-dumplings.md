## Ingredients

- half quart flour
- one egg
- water

## Directions

1. Mix half quart flour with one egg and enough water so the dough is not too stiff
2. Roll out on a table
3. Cut into one and a half inch strips
4. Lay the strips one on top of another, sprinkling flour between them so they don't stick together
5. Cut into dumplings of any desired width
6. Drop the cut dumplings into boiling salted water and cook until they float to the surface
7. Then drain them, place on a serving plate and drizzle with hot melted butter or rendered young bacon
