## Ingredients

- Guinea fowl
- Lard, thinly sliced
- Fat or butter

## Directions

1. Pluck and prepare like a chicken, then lard the breast with thinly sliced lard and roast in a hot oven, basting frequently with fat or butter
2. After placing on a serving platter pour over with browned butter
3. For them to be tender, guinea fowl should hang for a week in winter after being killed, and three days in summer
