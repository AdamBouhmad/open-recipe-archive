## Ingredients

- several boiled and cooled potatoes
- butter
- hard boiled eggs
- one tablespoon butter
- one and a half tablespoons flour
- salt
- pepper
- a few tablespoons boiling water
- vinegar, same amount as water
- green parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into fairly thick slices several boiled and cooled potatoes
2. Fry them in butter in a pan until they brown well on both sides
3. Arrange them on a hot serving dish
4. Place a thin slice of hard-boiled egg on each potato slice (two eggs are sufficient for four people)
5. For the sauce: melt one tablespoon of butter in a pan and add one and a half tablespoons of flour, pouring very slowly and stirring constantly
6. Add salt and pepper to taste
7. Then pour in a few tablespoons of boiling water and the same amount of vinegar
8. Boil for a few minutes
9. Sprinkle with chopped green parsley and pour over the hot potatoes
