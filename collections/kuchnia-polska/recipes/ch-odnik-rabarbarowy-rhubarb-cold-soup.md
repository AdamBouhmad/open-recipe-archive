## Ingredients

- a few rhubarb stalks
- 1 quart of water
- several cooked red beets
- 1 large pickled cucumber
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- a few tablespoons of crayfish meat
- sour cream
- chopped dill
- salt and pepper to taste
- ice

## Directions

1. Chop a few rhubarb stalks and cook in 1 quart of water for about 1/2 hour
2. Strain the water into a bowl
3. Add several cooked red beets, finely chopped
4. Add 1 large pickled cucumber cut into cubes
5. Add 3 hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters
6. Add a few tablespoons of crayfish meat and sour cream
7. Add chopped dill, salt, and pepper to taste
8. Place on ice
9. Serve when very cold; you can also add a piece of ice to the bowl just before serving
