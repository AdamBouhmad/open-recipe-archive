## Ingredients

- five medium tart apples
- half a cup of sugar
- half a teaspoon of cinnamon
- nutmeg
- one teaspoon of butter
- rolled out pie dough

## Directions

1. Peel five medium tart apples, cut each into eight pieces and remove the seeds
2. Line a baking sheet with rolled out dough, add to the apples half a cup of sugar, half a teaspoon of cinnamon and a pinch of grated nutmeg, mix and arrange on the sheet, scatter one teaspoon of butter, broken into small pieces on top, cover with another layer of rolled out dough, seal the edges firmly and place in a moderately hot oven for forty to forty-five minutes
