## Ingredients

- beef, preferably tenderloin
- mustard
- pepper
- salt
- capers
- chopped onion
- pickles
- lettuce leaves
- raw egg yolk

## Directions

1. Scrape a piece of beef, preferably tenderloin
2. Add mustard, pepper and salt to taste, and a few capers
3. Mix well and place on a platter, forming it into a neat shape
4. Around it, arrange chopped onion, pickles, lettuce leaves, etc.
5. On top of the meat, make a small hollow and place one raw egg yolk in it
