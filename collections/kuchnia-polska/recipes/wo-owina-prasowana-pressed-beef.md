## Ingredients

- beef cooked in broth until meat falls from bones
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove meat from hot broth, trim all fat and cut into small pieces
2. Add salt and pepper to taste, melted butter and mix with cup of broth
3. Mix everything well and place in pot or bowl, cover with lid smaller than pot so it enters inside
4. Place weight of several pounds on top, preferably a pressing iron, and place in cold location
5. When well cooled, slice with sharp knife and serve
