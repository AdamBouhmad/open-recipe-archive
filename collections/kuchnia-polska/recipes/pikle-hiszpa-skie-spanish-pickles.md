## Ingredients

- twelve cucumbers
- one large head of cabbage
- twelve onions
- twelve mango fruits (six red and six green)
- one pound of brown sugar
- celery seeds
- mustard seeds
- one gallon of vinegar
- yellow ginger (turmeric powder)

## Directions

1. Chop twelve cucumbers finely and salt overnight
2. Chop one large head of cabbage
3. Chop twelve onions and twelve mango fruits (six red and six green) finely and salt
4. Let stand for five hours, then press out the salt properly
5. Add one pound of brown sugar, celery seeds, and mustard seeds
6. Cover with one gallon of vinegar
7. Add yellow ginger (turmeric powder)
8. Put on fire and when the entire mixture cooks well, fill jars and seal tightly
