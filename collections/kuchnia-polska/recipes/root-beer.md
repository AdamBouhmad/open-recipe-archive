## Ingredients

- five quarts of water
- one and a half cups of thick molasses
- one ounce of sassafras bark
- one ounce of wintergreen bark
- one ounce of sarsaparilla root
- half a cup of yeast
- two gallons of water

## Directions

1. Boil five quarts of water, add one and a half cups of thick molasses and stir until dissolved
2. Set aside for three hours
3. After this time add one ounce each of sassafras bark, wintergreen bark, and sarsaparilla root, all ground into powder
4. Then add half a cup of yeast, add two gallons of water and set at room temperature for twelve hours for fermentation
5. When fermentation is complete, pour the clear liquid into bottles and cork tightly
