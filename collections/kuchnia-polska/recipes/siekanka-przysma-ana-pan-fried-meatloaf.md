## Ingredients

- leftover roasted meat from the previous day or several types of various meats
- salt and pepper to taste
- celery stalk with leaves
- cooked potatoes, mashed, equal in quantity to the meat
- butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Remove all fat from the meat
2. Chop the lean meat finely
3. Add salt and pepper to taste
4. Add celery stalk with leaves
5. Add mashed cooked potatoes equal in quantity to the meat
6. Mix everything well
7. Spread on a work surface
8. Form thin cutlets or one flat patty from the mixture
9. Fry in butter or lard in a pan until both sides are well browned
