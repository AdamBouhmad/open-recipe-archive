## Ingredients

- six medium potatoes
- water
- one small teaspoon salt
- one and a half tablespoons butter
- half a cup hot milk
- ground pepper
- one tablespoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Peel and cook six medium potatoes
2. Drain the water and mash the potatoes to a paste
3. Then beat with a spoon until it becomes almost foamy
4. Add one small teaspoon of salt, one and a half tablespoons of butter, half a cup of hot milk and some ground pepper
5. Mix everything well
6. Place on a pan in which one tablespoon of butter has been melted first
7. Spread the potato mixture evenly over the whole pan, cover with a lid and fry on very low heat or in a hot oven for about fifteen minutes until it browns nicely on the bottom
8. Then fold the omelet in half and immediately transfer to a hot serving dish and serve
