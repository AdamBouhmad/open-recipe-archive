## Ingredients

- one quart shelled green peas
- water
- beef broth, cooked previously
- butter
- flour
- egg yolks
- small bread cubes
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Pour water over one quart of shelled peas and cook until tender.
2. Press through a fine sieve and dilute with previously cooked beef broth.
3. After stirring well, thicken the soup with a spoon of butter rubbed with half a spoon of flour and bring to a boil.
4. Remove from heat, beat two egg yolks and mix into the soup.
5. Pour into a serving bowl.
6. Serve separately with small bread cubes fried in butter.
