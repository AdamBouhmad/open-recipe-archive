## Ingredients

- one cup of cold boiled potatoes
- half cup of celery
- some finely shredded raw cabbage
- some cooked green beans
- a small chopped onion
- lettuce leaves
- two tablespoons of oil
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- half teaspoon of salt
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Slice one cup of cold boiled potatoes
2. Chop half a cup of celery into cubes
3. Add some finely shredded raw cabbage, some cooked green beans and a small chopped onion
4. Mix together and place into a salad bowl lined with lettuce leaves
5. Mix two tablespoons of oil, one tablespoon of vinegar, half a teaspoon of salt and pepper to taste together and pour this sauce over the salad
