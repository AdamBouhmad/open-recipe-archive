## Ingredients

- 1 cup cheese, grated
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- red pepper to taste
- white bread

## Directions

1. Grate the cheese and add salt and red pepper
2. Cut white bread into round slices
3. Sprinkle each slice with cheese, salt, and pepper
4. Place on a baking sheet and put in a moderately hot oven
5. When the cheese melts, remove from the oven and serve immediately
