## Ingredients

- several carrots
- butter
- broth, cooked previously
- sour cream
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- bread cubes for croutons

## Directions

1. Scrape several carrots, chop finely and braise in butter for several minutes.
2. Pour in previously cooked broth and cook until tender.
3. Strain the broth and press the remaining carrot through a sieve.
4. Mix with sour cream beaten with egg yolks, then add to the broth.
5. Mix well, bring to a boil and serve at table with croutons.
6. Alternatively, this soup may be thickened with cooked rice instead of sour cream.
