## Ingredients

- green string beans
- salt
- water
- one teaspoon grated onion
- one teaspoon lemon juice
- one teaspoon salt
- one teaspoon fine sugar
- two pinches pepper
- two pinches dry mustard
- one tablespoon oil

## Directions

1. Cook green string beans in salted water
2. Drain and set aside to cool
3. When completely cold, arrange on a salad bowl
4. For the sauce: mix together one teaspoon grated onion, one teaspoon lemon juice, one teaspoon salt, one teaspoon fine sugar, two pinches pepper, two pinches dry mustard and one tablespoon oil
5. Pour the sauce over the beans
