## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- one cup tomato liquid from cooked and strained tomatoes
- one cup strong broth
- salt
- pepper
- one large onion, grated or finely chopped
- one cup well-rinsed rice
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes and then press through a sieve
2. For every cup of strained tomato liquid, take one cup of strong broth, add plenty of salt and pepper and one large grated or finely chopped onion
3. Bring to a boil and then add one cup of well-rinsed rice
4. Cook further, stirring frequently with a fork, until the rice absorbs all the liquid
5. Add one tablespoon butter and mix, then cook in steam for twenty minutes
6. This rice is served as a vegetable dish
