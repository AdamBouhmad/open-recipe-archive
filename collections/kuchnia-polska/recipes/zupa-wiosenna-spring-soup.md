## Ingredients

- one onion
- a handful of lettuce
- a bit of parsley
- one cup of pickled green peas
- one half quart of water
- one quart of previously cooked broth
- three egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop finely one onion, a handful of lettuce and a bit of parsley and place in a pot with one cup of pickled green peas, pour one half quart of water and cook until everything is soft, then salt and pepper to taste
2. Strain the water into another dish and pour one third of this liquid into one quart of previously cooked broth; in the remaining two thirds beat three egg yolks and quickly bring to a boil
3. Add together with the vegetables to the rest of the soup and serve on the table
