## Ingredients

- duck
- salt
- brown sauce, caper sauce, or gherkin sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean the duck, then salt it and place in a hot oven and leave only until it browns on top
2. After roasting, slice into pieces, place in a saucepan, cover with brown sauce, caper sauce, or gherkin sauce, add the sauce that dripped during roasting and braise over low heat until tender
3. When serving, arrange the meat on a platter and cover with sauce
