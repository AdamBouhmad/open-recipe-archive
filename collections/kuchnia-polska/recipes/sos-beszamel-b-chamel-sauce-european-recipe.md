## Ingredients

- quarter pound of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- two tablespoons of broth
- half a quart of sour cream, previously boiled
- four egg yolks
- one tablespoon of sour cream
- a bit of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry quarter pound of butter with one tablespoon of flour until browned
2. Dissolve in two tablespoons of broth and then slowly pour in half a quart of previously boiled sour cream
3. Cook until the sauce thickens well, constantly stirring so it does not burn
4. Remove from heat and cool
5. When completely cooled, temper with four egg yolks beaten with one tablespoon of sour cream
6. Add a bit of nutmeg
