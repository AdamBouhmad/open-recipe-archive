## Ingredients

- two pounds lean beef, preferably first-cut cross-rib
- salt
- pepper
- one bun soaked in milk and squeezed out
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon butter
- egg whites, beaten stiff
- flour
- butter for frying
- one cup sour cream
- half a tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of lean beef, preferably first-cut cross-rib, and grind it in a meat grinder
2. Add salt and pepper to taste, and one bun soaked in milk and squeezed out
3. Grind again, beat in two egg yolks, one tablespoon of butter and egg whites beaten stiff
4. After mixing, form nine round flat patties from this mixture
5. Dredge them in flour and fry them in butter on low heat without browning them too much
6. Mix one cup of sour cream with half a tablespoon of flour, pour it over the patties in the pan when they are almost done and fry together with the sour cream for a few minutes
7. Serve these patties with potatoes fried in the French style, or with regular fried potatoes
