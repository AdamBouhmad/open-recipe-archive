## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted flour
- quarter of heated milk
- three beaten egg yolks
- one tablet of yeast
- two tablespoons of melted butter
- salt
- three egg whites
- fruit juices or clarified butter

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of sifted flour with a quarter of heated milk, add three beaten egg yolks, a tablet of yeast, two tablespoons of melted butter, a pinch of salt and meringue from three egg whites
2. Beat everything well for fifteen minutes, then place the dough in a buttered mold and set in a warm place to rise
3. When the dough has risen well, place the mold with it in a pot with boiling water and cook for one hour
4. After cooking, turn onto a platter or salad bowl and serve at table with fruit juices or clarified butter
