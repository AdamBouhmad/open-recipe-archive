## Ingredients

- one bushel of tomatoes
- half quart of salt
- half tablespoon of cloves
- one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice)
- one-sixth teaspoon of red pepper (cayenne)

## Directions

1. Add half quart of salt to one bushel of tomatoes and cook until completely soft, then press through loose sieve
2. To the obtained mass add half tablespoon of cloves, one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice), one-sixth teaspoon of red pepper (cayenne), pour into a pot and cook until only ten quarts remain
3. Pour into bottles while hot, pour some melted paraffin on top and then cork
