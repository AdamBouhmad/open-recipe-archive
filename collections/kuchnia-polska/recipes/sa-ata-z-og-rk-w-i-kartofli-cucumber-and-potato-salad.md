## Ingredients

- two large cucumbers
- a couple of potatoes
- half teaspoon of salt
- two pinches of pepper
- three tablespoons of oil
- quarter teaspoon of onion juice
- one tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel two large cucumbers and plunge into cold water for an hour
2. Chop the cucumbers into fairly thin slices and also chop a couple of potatoes in a similar way; the potatoes should be half the amount of the cucumbers
3. Mix together and pour the following sauce over them: Mix half a teaspoon of salt, two pinches of pepper, three tablespoons of oil, a quarter teaspoon of onion juice and one tablespoon of vinegar well together, taking all these ingredients separately in the order given above; pour the oil very slowly, constantly stirring
