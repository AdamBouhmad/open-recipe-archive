## Ingredients

- soaked herring
- egg
- water
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the soaked herring inside, keeping the skin, split it open and remove the backbone
2. Wipe dry with paper, dip in egg beaten with water, coat with flour and fry in hot butter
3. Fry first skin-side down, then turn over to brown both sides
4. When the herring is almost fried, add two eggs per herring in the same butter
5. Serve as soon as the egg whites set, typically for breakfast
