## Ingredients

- cleaned and cooked tripe
- water
- smoked bacon
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- flour
- beef broth or water
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the tripe in water. When cooled, cut into square pieces about one inch in size.
2. For every pound of such tripe, fry fat from a quarter pound of smoked bacon in a pan. Season the tripe with salt and pepper to taste, coat with flour and fry in this fat until well browned.
3. Remove from the fat and place on a hot platter.
4. To the remaining fat in the pan add one teaspoon of flour, a few tablespoons of broth or water, and a few finely chopped mushrooms if available. Place on heat and stir until it comes to a boil.
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and pour this sauce over the tripe arranged on the platter, which must be very hot.
