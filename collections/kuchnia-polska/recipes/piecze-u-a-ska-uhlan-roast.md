## Ingredients

- meat
- salt and pepper to taste
- vegetables
- cold water
- three tablespoons horseradish
- butter
- one tablespoon sugar
- two egg yolks
- grated bread
- fresh parsley or dill, finely chopped
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Salt and pepper the meat to taste, brown it on all sides with vegetables, cover with cold water and braise on low heat under a lid
2. When the meat is half-braised, remove it from the pan and cut deeply along or diagonally
3. Prepare the stuffing: mix three tablespoons horseradish, some butter, one tablespoon sugar, two egg yolks, and enough grated bread to make a fairly thick mass, add salt, pepper and finely chopped fresh parsley or dill, mix well
4. Layer the meat with this stuffing
5. Place the prepared roast in a clean roasting pan
6. In the pan where the meat was braised, put one tablespoon butter and one tablespoon flour, brown them, then dilute with sour cream, bring to a boil, strain, pour this sauce over the roast and braise on low heat until tender, being careful that the stuffing does not leak out
