## Ingredients

- 1 hare
- smoked bacon, thinly sliced
- water or broth
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- 1 bay leaf
- pepper grains
- 1 onion
- flour
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the hare well, quarter it, cover with thin slices of smoked bacon, and fry in a pan on high heat so that it browns on top
2. Remove from pan and place in a pot, add about 1/2 quart of water or broth, salt and pepper to taste, add one bay leaf, a few peppercorns, and one whole onion, and braise covered on low heat until the meat is soft
3. Remove the meat to a platter, add some flour and butter to the pot and make a fairly thick sauce, bring to a boil, and pour over the hare, and around it arrange lemon slices
