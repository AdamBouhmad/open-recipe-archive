## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe blueberries
- water
- 1/4 pound sugar
- cinnamon stick
- a few tablespoons cold water
- 1 small teaspoon wheat flour
- handful of choice blueberries
- ice
- small fried bread cubes (grzaneczki)
- white bread
- small noodles (kluseczki) (optional)
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pour 1 quart of ripe blueberries with just enough water to soak them, then cook until they burst. Strain through a sieve and let the liquid settle.
2. When all the sediment settles to the bottom, pour the clear juice into a pot, add 1/4 pound of sugar and a piece of cinnamon.
3. Mix a few tablespoons of cold water with a small teaspoon of wheat flour, thicken the soup and place on heat.
4. As soon as it begins to boil, add a handful of choice blueberries, cook for a few minutes, then place on ice.
5. As a side serve fried small bread cubes from white bread, or separately cooked small noodles rinsed with cold water.
6. Note: This soup can also be served hot.
