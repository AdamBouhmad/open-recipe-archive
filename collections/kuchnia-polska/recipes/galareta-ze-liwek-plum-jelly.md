## Ingredients

- plums not overly ripe
- sugar equal in weight to the juice, or less if the plums are sweet

## Directions

1. Take plums not overly ripe, sort them, pour into a pot, place on the fire and crush the plums so as to extract all the juice from them
2. Pour all the mass into a bag and press out the juice, then strain it several more times so that it is clear
3. For one pound of juice obtained this way, add one pound of sugar, or less if the plums are sweet
4. First place the sugar in the oven and heat it well, being careful not to burn it
5. Pour the pressed juice back into the pot and boil for about twenty minutes, then add the hot sugar and stir until it dissolves completely
6. Pour into jars or glasses and place in the sun until the jelly sets
