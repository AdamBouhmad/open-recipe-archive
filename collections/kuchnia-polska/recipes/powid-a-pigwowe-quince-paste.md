## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar in the proportion of one and a half quarts per quart of grated mass

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the quinces on a grater
2. Take one and a half quarts of sugar for each quart of grated mass
3. First make a syrup from the sugar with the addition of the smallest amount of boiling water
4. Then pour in the grated quinces and boil on a strong fire until the mass thickens
5. For six quarts of this paste, one bunch of quinces is required
