## Ingredients

- one large piece of veal loin
- salt and pepper
- lard for larding
- one tablespoon butter
- various vegetables
- broth

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of veal loin, remove bones, remove veins, salt and pepper, then lard with lard and let it rest for about half an hour.
2. Place one tablespoon butter and various vegetables in a pan with the meat; braise on low heat, basting with broth, until tender.
3. Then remove the meat, add one tablespoon flour to the pan and brown with vegetables.
4. Finally strain and pour this sauce over the meat arranged on a serving platter.
5. The platter is usually surrounded with potato puree.
