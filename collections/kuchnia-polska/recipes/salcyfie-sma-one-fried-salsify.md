## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- egg
- salt
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Take a couple of salsify roots, scrape them, cut into small pieces and cook in water until tender.
2. Mash into a paste and add one well-beaten egg, some salt, and a tablespoon of flour.
3. Add enough milk to make the batter fairly thin.
4. Take the batter with a spoon, place on a pan and fry like pancakes.
