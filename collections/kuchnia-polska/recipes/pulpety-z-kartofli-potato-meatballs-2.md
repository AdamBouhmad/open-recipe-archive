## Ingredients

- several cold cooked potatoes
- a tablespoon of fresh parsley
- two egg yolks and one egg white
- grated bread crumbs
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Grate the cold cooked potatoes into a mass
2. For half a quarter of potatoes, add a tablespoon of fresh parsley, two egg yolks and one egg white
3. Beat into a smooth mass
4. Shape small balls from the mixture
5. Roll in grated bread crumbs
6. Fry until browned in hot fat
7. Place in a bowl and pour soup over them
