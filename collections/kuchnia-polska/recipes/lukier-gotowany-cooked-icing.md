## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup water
- 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cook sugar, water, and cream of tartar over low heat until a small amount of the mixture dropped in cold water forms a soft ball
2. Beat one egg well
3. Pour the sugar mixture into the egg in the thinnest stream possible, stirring constantly
4. Spread on cake with a knife dipped in warm water
5. Chopped almonds or nuts may be added to this icing
