## Ingredients

- several mushrooms
- a couple of onions
- various vegetables to taste
- several red beets
- beet kvass
- half a spoon of flour
- half a quarter litre of good sour cream
- cooked beets

## Directions

1. Cook several mushrooms, a couple of onions and various vegetables to taste
2. Separately cook or bake several red beets
3. Chop them finely and pour over with beet kvass, then set in a warm place for an hour or somewhat longer
4. Pour this kvass over the taste from the cooked vegetables
5. Mix half a spoon of flour with half a quarter litre of good sour cream and thicken the beet soup with it, pouring gradually and stirring constantly
6. Finally bring to a boil and pour into a bowl to which a couple of spoonfuls of cooked beets have been added
