## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal (com meal)
- 1.5 cups wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- 0.5 pint milk

## Directions

1. Mix together cornmeal, wheat flour, and baking powder and sift through a sieve several times to mix well.
2. Cream 2 tablespoons of butter with 3 tablespoons of sugar, then add 3 well-beaten eggs.
3. Add the flour mixture and then pour in 0.5 pint of milk, mixing into smooth dough.
4. Fill small metal forms with the dough and bake in a hot oven.
5. Small metal forms called 'muffin tins' are best for this purpose, as larger forms will not rise as well or bake evenly.
