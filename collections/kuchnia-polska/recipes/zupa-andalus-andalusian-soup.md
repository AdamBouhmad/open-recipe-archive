## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- 3 onions
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 clove garlic
- English spices
- beef broth
- piece of ham
- fresh dill
- eggs in their shells

## Directions

1. Place tomatoes, onions, bay leaf, garlic clove, and English spices in a clay pot.
2. Stew under a lid until soft, then press through a sieve.
3. Have beef broth ready and mix with the sieved mass and bring to a boil.
4. Slice ham thinly and fry it in a pan with fresh dill.
5. Place in a serving bowl and pour the soup over it.
6. Serve this soup with eggs in their shells.
