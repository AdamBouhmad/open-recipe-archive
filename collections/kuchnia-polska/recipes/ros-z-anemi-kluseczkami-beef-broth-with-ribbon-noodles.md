## Ingredients

- beef for broth
- various vegetables
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 whole eggs
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cook broth on beef with addition of various vegetables, strain it, pour back into the pot and place on fire
2. Beat 2 tablespoons flour with 2 whole eggs and cold water to make a thin batter
3. Make a paper cone and pour the batter through it into the boiling broth
