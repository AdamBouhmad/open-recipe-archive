## Ingredients

- eleven egg whites
- one teaspoon cream of tartar
- one and a half cups sugar
- one cup flour
- quarter teaspoon salt
- one teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Beat eleven egg whites into foam, add one teaspoon of cream of tartar and beat further until the foam is stiff
2. Slowly add one and a half cups of sugar, one cup of flour sifted with quarter teaspoon of salt and mix
3. Add one teaspoon of vanilla extract, place in a form and put in the oven for forty-five to fifty minutes
4. After baking, turn the form onto a plate and let the babka fall out by itself
