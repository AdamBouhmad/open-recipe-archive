## Ingredients

- Young fat turkey, weighing ten to twelve pounds
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Two dozen chestnuts
- Turkey liver
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Onion
- Pepper
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Choose a young fat turkey weighing ten to twelve pounds
2. After plucking, singe the down by holding the turkey over a sheet of lit paper; carefully draw out so as not to tear the innards
3. Wash carefully in four waters; add one teaspoon of baking soda to the third water
4. Wipe dry both inside and outside, then rub inside with a little salt and stuff with the following stuffing: Roast two dozen chestnuts and peel them
5. Crush eight of these chestnuts and the turkey liver in a mortar into a paste, add some very finely chopped parsley, onion, salt and pepper, and two egg yolks
