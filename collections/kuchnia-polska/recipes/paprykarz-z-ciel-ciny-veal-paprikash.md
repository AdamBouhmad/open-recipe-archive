## Ingredients

- two medium onions
- butter
- veal meat
- flour
- paprika to taste
- several spoonfuls of marinated tomatoes
- some broth
- sour cream (as substitute for tomatoes)

## Directions

1. Chop two medium onions finely and brown them with butter in a pan.
2. Cut the veal meat into cubes, dust with flour, add to the browned onions, salt and add paprika to taste.
3. Add several spoonfuls of marinated tomatoes and some broth.
4. Stew on low heat until the meat is tender.
5. If desired, sour cream may be used instead of tomatoes.
6. The paprikash may be served alone or with pyza (see 'Dishes with Flour and Eggs').
