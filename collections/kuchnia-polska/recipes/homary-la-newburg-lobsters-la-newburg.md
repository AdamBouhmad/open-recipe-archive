## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- half a quart of milk
- one small can of lobsters
- hot toast

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon of flour with one tablespoon of butter in a saucepan
2. Add one half quart of milk and cook until it thickens
3. Take one small can of lobsters, remove the meat and crumble it fairly finely
4. Add it to the milk and cook no longer than two minutes
5. Pour onto a platter lined with hot toast and serve
6. Note: if fresh lobsters are used instead of canned, the dish will be significantly better
