## Ingredients

- Tender veal
- Beef marrow
- Salt and pepper
- Bread crumb soaked
- Egg yolk
- Water
- Clarified butter

## Directions

1. Select a tender piece of veal, remove sinews thoroughly, and chop finely
2. For every half pound of meat, take a quarter pound of beef marrow
3. Mix the marrow with the meat and pound together in a mortar, carefully removing any remaining sinews
4. After pounding into a mass, salt it, add a large spoonful of soaked bread crumb, mix, and form into thin small cutlets
5. Brush with egg yolk beaten with a little water, sprinkle with grated bread, and fry on clarified butter
6. Pour broth as sauce and garnish with lemon slices
