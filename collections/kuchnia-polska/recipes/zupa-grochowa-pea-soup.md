## Ingredients

- Half quart of peas
- One quart of soft water
- One pound of overripe pork, or several pork tails, or smoked meat
- One tablespoon of butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Salt and pepper
- Marjoram

## Directions

1. Pour half quart of peas in one quart of soft water and bring to a boil
2. Strain this water, rinse the peas thoroughly and pour fresh hot water over them, as peas soaked in cold water will not be so soft
3. Separately cook with one pound of overripe pork, or several pork tails, or use smoked meat instead
4. When the peas are completely soft, strain the water and pass the peas through a sieve, pouring broth from the cooking meat over them
5. Make a roux with one tablespoon of butter and one tablespoon of flour
6. Cut the pork into pieces and add to the soup
7. Bring together to a boil and pour into a bowl into which first place croutons fried in butter and a pinch of marjoram
