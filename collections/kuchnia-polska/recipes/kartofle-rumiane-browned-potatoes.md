## Ingredients

- medium potatoes
- cold water
- salted water
- hot fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel a few medium potatoes and leave in cold water for one hour.
2. Drain this water, pour new water, salt it and cook the potatoes whole until almost cooked.
3. Drain the water and when the potatoes dry a little, throw them into hot fat and fry like doughnuts until they brown well on top.
4. Arrange on a serving dish and sprinkle with salt.
