## Ingredients

- 1 pound small fish (smelts)
- best quality olive oil
- 1 onion
- bay leaf pieces
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup cold vinegar
- 2 cloves
- a pinch of sugar
- a piece of red pepper

## Directions

1. Clean 1 pound of small fish (smelts) and fry in a pan using best quality olive oil instead of butter until browned
2. Cool completely
3. Arrange in a salad bowl and sprinkle with finely chopped onion
4. Add broken bay leaf pieces, salt and pepper to taste
5. Pour 1 cup of cold vinegar that has been previously boiled with 2 cloves, a pinch of sugar and a piece of red pepper
6. Place in a cool location for 24 hours
7. Then pour a little olive oil into the vinegar and serve
