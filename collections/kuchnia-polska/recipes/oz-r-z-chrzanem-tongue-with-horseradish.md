## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- vegetables for cooking
- grated horseradish
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook the beef tongue with vegetables as usual
2. Remove and peel off the skin
3. Slice thinly
4. Arrange on a platter
5. Take a handful of grated horseradish
6. Brown with butter
7. Season with sour cream
8. Bring to a boil
9. Pour this sauce over the tongue arranged on the platter
