## Ingredients

- rutabaga
- water
- salt
- salt pork or pork
- butter
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Carefully peel rutabaga and cover with cold water
2. Bring to a boil and drain the water
3. Pour in fresh warm water
4. Add a piece of salt pork or pork, salt to taste, and cook until tender
5. When completely tender, dice the rutabaga and the pork
6. Brown one tablespoon butter with one tablespoon flour
7. Dilute with the broth in which the rutabaga was cooked
8. Add the rutabaga and pork
9. Add a little sugar and stew on low heat for a while longer
10. Serve at table
