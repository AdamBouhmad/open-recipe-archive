## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- butter
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickens as usual and salt them.
2. Heat one tablespoon of butter in a pan, but do not brown it, then put the chickens in and brown them on all sides on very high heat.
3. Then place with the pan in the oven, add a bit more butter, cover with a lid and bake for half an hour.
4. When they are almost baked, put grated bread on a plate and roll the chickens in it, put them back in the pan and continue baking, turning frequently so the bread lightly browns.
