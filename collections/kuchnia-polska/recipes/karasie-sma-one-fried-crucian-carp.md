## Ingredients

- crucian carp
- salt
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and salt the fish an hour before; after removing from salt, dry it carefully with paper, dip in egg beaten with water and then coat all around with grated breadcrumbs.
2. Put a piece of butter on a frying pan, and when it browns, place the fish on it and fry until golden on both sides.
3. Then pour a couple of spoonfuls of sour cream onto the pan, fry a moment longer, transfer to a serving dish and pour the sauce over it.
