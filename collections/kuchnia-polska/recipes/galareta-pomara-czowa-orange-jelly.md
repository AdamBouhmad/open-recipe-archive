## Ingredients

- one quarter box of gelatin
- one quarter cup of cold water
- half a cup of boiling water
- one cup of orange juice
- half a cup of lemon juice
- half a cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak a quarter box of gelatin in a quarter cup of cold water and let it soak for about half an hour
2. Pour half a cup of boiling water into the gelatin and stir until almost completely dissolved, then add one cup of orange juice, half a cup of lemon juice and half a cup of sugar
3. Strain through cloth, pour into a mold and place in a cool place for several hours
