## Ingredients

- pork loin, weighing at least four pounds
- salt
- marjoram
- apples

## Directions

1. Select a piece weighing at least four pounds, as smaller pieces will dry out too much in the oven and taste poorly.
2. Rub the pork loin with salt and a little marjoram.
3. Place in a medium oven and roast without basting.
4. Near the end of cooking, add a couple of peeled apples cut into quarters.
5. If the pork loin is from a young pig with skin, score the skin in a crosshatch pattern and roast as above.
