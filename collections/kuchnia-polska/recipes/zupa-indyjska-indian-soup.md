## Ingredients

- 1 medium onion
- butter
- twice the amount of veal as onion, cut into cubes
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- lemon zest
- beef broth
- 1 large sour apple
- salt and pepper to taste
- separately cooked rice

## Directions

1. Finely chop one medium onion and fry it slightly in butter
2. Add twice the amount of veal cut into cubes as there is onion, and 1 tablespoon of flour
3. Fry everything together
4. Add 1 teaspoon of curry powder and some grated lemon zest for flavor
5. Dilute all of this with previously cooked beef broth
6. Add one large sour apple cut into slices
7. Add salt and pepper to taste
8. Add separately cooked rice
9. Bring everything to a boil once more and serve
