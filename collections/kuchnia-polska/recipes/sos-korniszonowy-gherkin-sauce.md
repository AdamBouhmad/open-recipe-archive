## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- bouillon or strong broth
- gherkins, sliced
- broth for cooking gherkins
- caramelized sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour and dilute with bouillon or strong broth, keeping on heat and stirring constantly
2. In a separate pot, cook gherkins sliced in broth
3. When tender, pour with the broth into the sauce
4. Add some caramelized sugar for color and some vinegar if the sauce is not sufficiently sour
5. Pour over meat
6. Gherkin sauce is served with meat dishes and cutlets
