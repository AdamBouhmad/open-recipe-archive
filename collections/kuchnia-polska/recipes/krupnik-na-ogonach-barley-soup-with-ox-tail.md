## Ingredients

- 1 ox tail
- cold water
- vegetables
- barley groats
- garlic sauce

## Directions

1. Cut one ox tail into eight to ten pieces
2. Cover with cold water and cook
3. When the scum stops appearing, carefully collect it
4. Add all kinds of vegetables that are on hand
5. Add barley groats and cook for three hours on low heat
6. When the meat is cooked, remove it and serve separately in garlic sauce
7. Pour the soup into a dish
