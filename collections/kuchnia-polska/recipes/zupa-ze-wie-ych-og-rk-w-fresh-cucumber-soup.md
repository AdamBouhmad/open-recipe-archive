## Ingredients

- three medium cucumbers
- two onions
- half a quart of water
- half a quart of veal or chicken broth
- half a quart of milk
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt
- pepper
- small croutons

## Directions

1. Cut three medium cucumbers and two onions into thin slices and cover with half a quart of water and half a quart of veal or chicken broth.
2. Cook on a low fire for one hour, then press everything through a sieve, pour back into the pot and place on a hot stove.
3. Boil half a quart of milk and add to it one tablespoon of flour rubbed with one tablespoon of butter, mix well and pour into the soup, also add salt and pepper to taste.
4. Cook for about five minutes and serve with small croutons.
