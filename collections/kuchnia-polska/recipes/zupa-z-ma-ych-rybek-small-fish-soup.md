## Ingredients

- small fish (preferably carp)
- vegetables (various)
- flour
- butter
- sour cream
- dumplings (kluski)
- cooked groats (kaszka)

## Directions

1. Cook a broth with various vegetables
2. Clean the fish, preferably carp, discarding the roe, and place in the broth
3. Cook until the fish completely falls apart
4. Strain everything through a colander
5. Make a roux of half a spoonful of flour with a spoonful of butter and thicken the soup
6. Sour cream may also be added
7. Place dumplings or cooked groats (chilled and cut into cubes) in the serving bowl before pouring the soup
