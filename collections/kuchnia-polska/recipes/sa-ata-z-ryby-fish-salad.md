## Ingredients

- any cooked or fried fish
- vinegar
- water
- roots and herbs for boiling
- mayonnaise sauce or tartare sauce
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Take any cooked or fried fish, carefully peel off skin and bones, break into small pieces
2. Pour over cooled boiled vinegar with roots for one hour
3. After one hour, drain from vinegar, arrange on a salad bowl and pour with mayonnaise or tartare sauce, and arrange lettuce leaves around it
