## Ingredients

- one tablet of yeast
- half a quart of warm water
- one quart of rye flour
- half a quart of warm water
- half a quart of sifted rye flour
- one and a half quarts of wheat flour
- two teaspoons of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve one tablet of yeast in half a quart of warm water, add one quart of rye flour and set in a warm place
2. When it has risen so that it begins to fall, add half a quart of warm water, half a quart of sifted rye flour, one and a half quarts of wheat flour and two teaspoons of salt, mix everything together and let it stand for about twenty minutes
3. Divide into loaves and let them rise in forms by one-third of their volume, which will take about 45 minutes
4. Bake in a moderately hot oven; when removing from the oven, brush the top with water
