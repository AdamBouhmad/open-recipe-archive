## Ingredients

- six tart apples
- sugar
- nutmeg
- butter
- six tablespoons fine sugar
- two tablespoons milk
- half a teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Peel six tart apples and hollow them out to remove all the seeds.
2. Fill the hollow in each apple with one teaspoon of sugar with a pinch of nutmeg and a small amount of butter, then place in the oven.
3. When the apples are almost baked, remove from the oven and set in a cold place.
4. Mix six tablespoons of fine sugar with two tablespoons of milk, add half a teaspoon of vanilla extract and beat well.
5. When the apples are completely cooled, coat them around with this frosting and place in a hot oven for one minute to lightly brown the frosting.
6. These apples can be served with sour cream or without.
