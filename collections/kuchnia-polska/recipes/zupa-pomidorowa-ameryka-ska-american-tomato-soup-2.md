## Ingredients

- one can of pickled tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- half teaspoon of baking soda

## Directions

1. Pour a can of pickled tomatoes into a pot and boil until soft
2. Press through a fine sieve so that only seeds and skins remain on the sieve
3. For half a quart of this liquid, add one teaspoon of salt, some pepper, and half a teaspoon of baking soda
4. Pour back into the pot and place on the stove
