## Ingredients

- green grapes, just beginning to ripen
- one cup of water per eight pounds of grapes
- sugar, measure for measure with the juice

## Directions

1. Select grapes that are just beginning to ripen, sort them, and wash carefully
2. Add one cup of water per eight pounds of grapes and cook until completely soft
3. Crush and strain all the juice through thin cloth, without pressing
4. Pour the obtained juice back into a pot, bring to a boil and skim
5. Add sugar in the same measure as the juice
6. Cook for twenty minutes
7. Pour into glasses or jars and seal tightly; best to seal with paraffin
