## Ingredients

- four bread rolls
- one quart of hot water
- two spoons of butter
- salt to taste
- half a quarter litre of sour cream or milk
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop four bread rolls, pour over with one quart of hot water, add two spoons of butter and salt to taste, bring all this to a boil together and then pass through a sieve
2. Thicken the soup with half a quarter litre of sour cream or milk in which two egg yolks have been beaten
