## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- three-quarters pound sugar per pound of peaches
- one cup water per pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel ripe peaches, remove pits, and chop into small pieces
2. For each pound of chopped peaches, take three-quarters pound of sugar
3. For each pound of sugar, pour in one cup of water and bring to a boil
4. Skim the surface
5. Add the peaches and cook on high heat until soft
6. Mash into pulp and continue cooking until the mixture is thick
7. Pour into jars or glasses, seal tightly and store
