## Ingredients

- tender veal, thinly sliced
- salt and pepper
- butter
- lemon
- grated roll

## Directions

1. Slice tender veal thinly, beat lightly and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Melt a piece of butter in a pan without letting it brown, add the rolls to the butter and fry until they brown slightly.
3. Squeeze a little lemon juice, sprinkle with grated roll, cover with a lid and braise on low heat for about five minutes.
