## Ingredients

- sardines or good herring
- half a pound of good butter

## Directions

1. clean the sardines or herring carefully, wash them, remove the bones and pound them to a paste in a mortar
2. take half a pound of good butter for half a pound of sardines or herring
3. pound together in a mortar
4. press through a fine sieve and serve
5. chopped fresh parsley or chives may be added if desired
6. this butter may be kept in a cool place for several weeks
