## Ingredients

- ten pounds of ripe tomatoes
- four pounds of sugar
- one quart of vinegar
- one tablespoon of ground cinnamon
- one tablespoon of ground cloves

## Directions

1. Scald ten pounds of ripe tomatoes with boiling water, peel and cut into slices, then add four pounds of sugar, one quart of vinegar, one tablespoon of ground cinnamon and one tablespoon of ground cloves
2. Place everything on fire and cook until thick, stirring frequently, then fill jars and close tightly
