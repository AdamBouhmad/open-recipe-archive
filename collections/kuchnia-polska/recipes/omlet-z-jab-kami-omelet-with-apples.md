## Ingredients

- six large apples
- one cup sugar
- one teaspoon butter
- one cup grated bread
- three eggs
- butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Cook six large apples until soft and press through a sieve
2. While hot, add one cup sugar and one teaspoon butter, mix well and cool
3. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a frying pan, add one cup grated bread and fry until golden
4. Beat three eggs, add to the cooled apple mixture and mix well
5. Butter a baking pan, sprinkle the fried bread on the bottom and sides
6. Add the apple mixture on top, then more grated bread on the top and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour
7. This omelet is usually served cold with sugar and sour cream, but can also be served hot
