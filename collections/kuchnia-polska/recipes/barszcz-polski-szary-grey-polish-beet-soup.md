## Ingredients

- pork bones
- a piece of smoked meat
- beet kvass
- a few onions
- a few dried mushrooms
- a clove of garlic
- ginger
- marjoram
- several peppercorns
- flour
- fresh dill

## Directions

1. Take pork bones and a piece of smoked meat, pour beet kvass over them and cook
2. After skimming, add a few onions, a few dried mushrooms, a clove of garlic, some ginger and marjoram, and several peppercorns, then continue cooking until the meat is tender
3. Strain the soup into another pot, thicken with flour, bring to a boil and serve in a dish or on plates with the meat from the soup cut into cubes
4. Sprinkle with chopped fresh dill on top
