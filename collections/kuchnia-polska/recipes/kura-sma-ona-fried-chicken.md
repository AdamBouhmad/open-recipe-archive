## Ingredients

- chicken weighing about four pounds
- chicken liver
- one tablespoon milk
- one teaspoon salt
- pinch of pepper
- one egg
- grated bread
- fat for frying
- thin slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Divide the chicken into six pieces and flatten each piece, giving it the shape of a cutlet.
2. Take the liver and pound it into a paste with one tablespoon of milk, one teaspoon of salt and a pinch of pepper.
3. Beat one egg, dip the quartered chicken in it, then spread the liver paste on it, dip in egg again, and then coat with grated bread.
4. Fry until well browned in a pan or deep skillet filled with fat.
5. Place on a platter and arrange thin slices of lemon around it.
