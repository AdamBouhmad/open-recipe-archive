## Ingredients

- large pike or zander
- mixed vegetables
- onion
- roots
- flour
- unsalted butter
- fish stock

## Directions

1. Clean and salt the large fish one hour before use
2. Cook a stock from mixed vegetables, onion and roots, cool and cover the fish with it
3. Boil until the fish is tender, being careful not to overcook
4. In another pan, make a roux with one tablespoon flour and one tablespoon unsalted butter
5. Thin with a quarter of the stock from cooking the fish
