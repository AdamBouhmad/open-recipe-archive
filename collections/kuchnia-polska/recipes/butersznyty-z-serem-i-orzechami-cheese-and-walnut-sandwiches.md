## Ingredients

- Swiss cheese, grated
- walnuts, finely chopped, equal amount to cheese
- salt
- red pepper
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Grate Swiss cheese
2. Mix with an equal amount of finely chopped walnuts
3. Add salt and red pepper to taste
4. Mix well
5. Spread butter on bread
6. Spread the cheese and walnut mixture on the bread
