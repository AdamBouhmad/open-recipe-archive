## Ingredients

- cold cooked beef (roast or boiled beef)
- grated onion, a generous amount
- salt and pepper to taste
- a couple of tablespoons of broth or soup
- a tablespoon of flour
- egg
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mince the cold cooked beef into a mass
2. Add a generous amount of grated onion, salt and pepper to taste
3. Add a couple of tablespoons of broth or soup, place on heat
4. Thicken with a tablespoon of flour and cook for five minutes
5. Remove from heat and let cool
6. Shape into balls the size of an Italian walnut
7. Dip in egg and fry in hot lard until browned
8. Serve in soup
