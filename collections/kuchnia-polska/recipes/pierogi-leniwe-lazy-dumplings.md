## Ingredients

- one pound fresh cottage cheese
- four egg yolks
- one tablespoon butter
- salt to taste
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one egg white
- hot butter
- browned roll

## Directions

1. Carefully press one pound of fresh cottage cheese to remove whey and mix in a bowl with four egg yolks and one tablespoon butter
2. Add salt to taste and one tablespoon wheat flour and mix well
3. Add the foam from one egg white and mix lightly
4. Turn onto a table thickly dusted with flour
5. Roll into a long cylinder, notch with a knife and cut into diagonal pieces
6. Cook in a very large pot full of boiling water
7. When they float to the surface, remove with a slotted spoon and arrange on a platter
8. Drizzle with hot melted butter with browned roll and serve
