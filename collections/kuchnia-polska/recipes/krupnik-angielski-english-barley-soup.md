## Ingredients

- beef broth
- vegetables as customary
- pearl barley
- burnt sugar for coloring
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare beef broth with vegetables added as usual.
2. Toward the end, add barley and cook until it is soft.
3. Before serving, color with burnt sugar, add finely chopped parsley and serve.
