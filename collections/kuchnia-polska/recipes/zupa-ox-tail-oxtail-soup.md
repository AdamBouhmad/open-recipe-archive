## Ingredients

- one beef oxtail
- flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- several peppercorns
- two whole onions
- one carrot
- one turnip
- two stalks of celery
- parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Chop beef oxtail into inch-long pieces, dredge each in flour and place in a pot.
2. Add one teaspoon of salt, several peppercorns, two whole chopped onions, one carrot, one turnip, two stalks of celery and parsley, and cover with water.
3. Cook on a low fire for three hours.
4. Remove the meat, skim all the fat from the soup and then strain it well.
5. Carefully cut the meat from the bones, chop into small pieces, place in a bowl and pour the hot soup over it.
