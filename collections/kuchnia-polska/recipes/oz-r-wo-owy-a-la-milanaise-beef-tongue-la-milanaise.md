## Ingredients

- fresh beef tongue
- salt
- vegetables for broth
- one large onion
- butter
- one cup rice
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- broth from cooking the tongue
- salt and pepper to taste
- one cup grated Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. Cook fresh beef tongue in salted water with vegetables as for broth, about one and a half hours
2. Remove, peel off the skin, and arrange on a platter
3. Surround with hot rice prepared as follows: chop a large onion and brown it in butter until golden
4. Add one large cup of rice and a few finely chopped mushrooms
5. Bring to a boil for two minutes, then add one quart of broth from cooking the tongue
6. Stir and cook for fifteen minutes, adding more broth if needed, and cook another fifteen minutes, about half an hour total
7. At the end add salt and pepper to taste and one cup of grated Swiss cheese, stir and arrange around the tongue
