## Ingredients

- five cups of cooked broth
- salt
- pepper
- one egg
- flour
- parmesan cheese
- parsley

## Directions

1. Have five cups of cooked broth seasoned with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Knead one egg with enough flour, to which a pinch of salt has been added, to create a dough of medium thickness.
3. Make balls the size of a thimble from this dough and drop them into the boiling broth ten minutes before serving.
4. When serving, sprinkle one tablespoon of parmesan and one teaspoon of finely chopped parsley.
