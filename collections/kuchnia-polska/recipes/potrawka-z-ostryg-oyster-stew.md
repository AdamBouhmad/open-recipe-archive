## Ingredients

- one quart oysters
- water
- butter
- crackers
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Drain one quart of oysters through a colander, catching the sauce in a bowl
2. Add half a cup of water to the sauce, bring to boil and strain
3. Add one tablespoon butter and one heaping tablespoon crushed crackers, salt and pepper to taste
4. Place on fire and when it boils, add the oysters and simmer until the edges begin to curl
5. Add half a cup of boiled cream and serve immediately
