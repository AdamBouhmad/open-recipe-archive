## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- broth or fish stock
- 1 large teaspoon English mustard
- vinegar
- sugar
- a few slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
2. Dilute with broth or fish stock
3. Add 1 large teaspoon English mustard, some vinegar, sugar, and a few slices of lemon
4. Boil together and serve
5. Use for fish and also for sausage
