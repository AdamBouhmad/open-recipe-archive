## Ingredients

- three ounces flour
- one ounce butter
- half teaspoon salt
- pinch of red pepper
- half cup water
- three ounces grated cheese
- one egg yolk
- one egg white
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Rub together in a bowl three ounces of flour with one ounce of butter, with the addition of half a teaspoon of salt and a pinch of red pepper.
2. Slowly pour in half a cup of water, stirring constantly, then add three ounces of grated cheese mixed with one egg yolk.
3. Beat the egg white to stiff peaks and mix with the dough at the very end.
4. Take spoonfuls and drop into hot fat, which must be abundant, and fry until golden, the same way as regular fritters.
