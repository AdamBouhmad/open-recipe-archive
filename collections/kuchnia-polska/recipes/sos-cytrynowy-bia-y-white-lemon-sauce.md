## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of clarified butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- poultry broth or sweet sour cream
- juice from half a lemon
- a bit of sugar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Beat one tablespoon of clarified butter with one tablespoon of flour until white
2. Dissolve in poultry broth or sweet sour cream
3. Squeeze in juice from half a lemon
4. Add a bit of sugar and pour out
5. To have a more substantial sauce with a yellow color, before serving pour in two beaten egg yolks, warm, and serve
6. This sauce is used for poultry, veal, and fish
