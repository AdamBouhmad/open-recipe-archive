## Ingredients

- pork cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Season the prepared cutlets with salt and pepper.
2. Coat with flour and fry in butter over high heat just until lightly browned.
3. Arrange the browned cutlets in a pot, placing onion slices between layers.
4. Cover and braise over low heat until tender.
5. Serve with mashed potatoes, either arranged around them or served separately.
