## Ingredients

- two cups grated potato mass
- two beaten eggs
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- half teaspoon salt
- butter for frying
- pinch of pepper

## Directions

1. Grate potatoes on a grater.
2. To two cups of potato mass add two beaten eggs, one tablespoon of wheat flour, half a teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of pepper.
3. Beat everything well and fry in butter.
4. It is recommended to peel potatoes in the evening and place them in cold water, where they should remain all night, and grate them only the next day, just before use.
