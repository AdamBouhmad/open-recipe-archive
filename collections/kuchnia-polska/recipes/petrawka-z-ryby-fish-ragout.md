## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- milk
- 1 small teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- a pinch of red pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon powdered ginger (optional)
- 1 large cup cold fried or cooked fish, crumbled into pieces the size of a hazelnut
- parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Place 1 tablespoon of butter in a saucepan and brown it slightly
2. Add 1 tablespoon of flour, brown together and add enough milk to form a rather thin sauce
3. Add 1 small teaspoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of pepper, a pinch of red pepper, a little nutmeg and 1 teaspoon of powdered ginger if desired
4. Mix everything well, bring to a boil and add 1 large cup of cold fried or cooked fish, crumbled into pieces the size of a hazelnut
5. Place on heat and cook for 10 minutes
6. Arrange on a hot serving platter lined with parsley leaves and serve at table
