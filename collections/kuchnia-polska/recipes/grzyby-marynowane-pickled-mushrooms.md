## Ingredients

- young fresh mushrooms
- a pinch of mace
- one teaspoon of ground pepper
- salt to taste
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Select the youngest and freshly gathered mushrooms
2. For one quart of mushrooms, take a pinch of mace, one teaspoon of ground pepper and salt to taste
3. Put mushrooms in a saucepan, sprinkle with salt, pepper and mace
4. Put on fire and hold on the fire, constantly shaking the saucepan, until all the juice that the mushrooms initially release evaporates
5. Pour in enough vinegar to barely cover them
6. Boil once, remove from fire and fill jars, which should then be sealed tightly
7. To preserve for a longer time, keep them in a dry and cool place
