## Ingredients

- several tart apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- one cup of water
- half cup of rye flour
- half cup of cornmeal
- a pinch of salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the seeds, and slice them.
2. In a deep baking dish, layer apple slices on the bottom and sprinkle generously with sugar mixed with a little cinnamon.
3. Alternate layers of apples and sugar until the dish is nearly full, then pour one cup of water over.
4. Mix half cup of rye flour and half cup of cornmeal with a pinch of salt and enough boiling water to make a thick dough.
5. Cover the apples tightly with this dough and place in a slow oven for four hours.
6. Remove from the oven, break the crust in several places with a fork, cover with a plate, and bake for another two hours.
7. Remove and let cool.
8. Serve with cream and sugar; should be prepared in the evening for use the next day.
