## Ingredients

- two pounds lean raw beef, finely chopped
- one teaspoon salt
- quarter teaspoon pepper
- half teaspoon sage
- two spoonfuls melted butter
- two heaping spoonfuls grated bread
- two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Chop two pounds lean raw beef very finely
2. Add one teaspoon salt, quarter teaspoon pepper, half teaspoon sage and two spoonfuls melted butter
3. Grate bread very finely and take two heaping spoonfuls, add two beaten eggs
4. Mix everything well and form into one long loaf and place in oven
