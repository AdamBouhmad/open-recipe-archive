## Ingredients

- one quarter of flour
- one egg
- water
- one small head of fresh cabbage
- salted water
- several dried mushrooms
- one herring
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of chopped onion
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make dough from one quarter pound of flour, one egg, and enough water so it is not too stiff, and roll out thinly on a work surface.
2. Shred a small head of fresh cabbage and cook in salted water, then drain and chop finely.
3. Cook several dried mushrooms separately until tender and chop finely, and also chop very finely one well-soaked herring that has been cleaned of skin and bones.
4. Fry one tablespoon of butter with the same amount of chopped onion, then add the cabbage and braise until completely tender, then add the mushrooms and herring, mix everything well, and add pepper to taste.
5. Place heaps of this mixture on the rolled out dough, cut out small pierogi with a glass, seal the edges carefully, and cook in boiling water like any other pierogi.
6. Serve on a platter poured with melted butter.
