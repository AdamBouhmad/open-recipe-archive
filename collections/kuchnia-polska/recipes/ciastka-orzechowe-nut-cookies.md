## Ingredients

- four cups of flour
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- one cup of chopped nuts
- one teaspoon of baking powder
- extract for flavoring

## Directions

1. Make dough from all ingredients, adding some extract for flavoring
2. Drop onto baking sheet by teaspoonful
3. Place in oven until they brown well
