## Ingredients

- one quart of oysters
- half a cup of water
- one tablespoon of butter
- one heaping tablespoon of crumbled crackers
- salt and pepper to taste
- half a cup of boiled cream

## Directions

1. Drain one quart of oysters in a colander, collecting the liquid in a bowl
2. Add half a cup of water to the oyster liquid, bring to a boil and strain
3. Add one tablespoon of butter and one heaping tablespoon of crumbled crackers, salt and pepper to taste
4. Place over heat and when it comes to a boil, add the oysters and cook until the edges begin to curl
5. Pour in half a cup of boiled cream and serve immediately
