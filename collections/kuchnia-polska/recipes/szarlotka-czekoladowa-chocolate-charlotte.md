## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- baking powder
- salt
- one heaping tablespoon lard
- very cold water
- two cups milk
- one cup sugar
- butter the size of a walnut
- four egg yolks
- three tablespoons cornmeal
- cold milk
- vanilla extract
- whipped sweetened cream

## Directions

1. To one cup flour add a pinch of baking powder, a pinch of salt and one heaping tablespoon lard
2. Mix everything perfectly, then add just enough very cold water to make a stiff dough
3. Divide the dough into two halves and roll each out to the size of a large plate
4. Line the bottom and sides of a deep baking pan with this dough and bake in a hot oven
5. Heat two cups milk
6. Add one cup sugar and a piece of butter the size of a walnut
7. Beat four egg yolks and add to the milk
8. Mix three tablespoons cornmeal in cold milk and pour into the pan with hot milk
9. Cook until it thickens, then add one teaspoon vanilla extract
10. Fill the baked dough in the pans with this mixture
11. Spread sweetened whipped cream on top and serve
