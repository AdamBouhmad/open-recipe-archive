## Ingredients

- one quart of cranberries
- one cup of water
- sugar measured equal to the amount of juice obtained

## Directions

1. Put one quart of cranberries in a pot, add one cup of water and cook until completely soft, then pour into a strainer made of fine cloth and hang over a bowl overnight
2. The next morning add as much sugar as there is juice, cook for fifteen minutes from the moment of boiling, skim, and when the liquid begins to thicken on a spoon, pour into a mold and place in a cool place so the jelly sets
