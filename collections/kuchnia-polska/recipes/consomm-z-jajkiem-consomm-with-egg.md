## Ingredients

- One beef bone
- One carrot, sliced
- Two onions
- One turnip
- Several celery stalks
- Several red pepper pods
- Two cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Egg whites and shells from three eggs
- Four quarts of water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Pour in four quarts of water and place on fire
3. When it comes to a boil, add salt
4. Cook three hours on very low heat
5. Strain
6. Serve with one fried egg per person
