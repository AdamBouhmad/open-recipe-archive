## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut lamb meat from the shoulder into slices at least as thick as a finger, beat lightly, sprinkle with salt and pepper and fry in hot butter, being careful not to overfly.
2. When serving, pour hot butter over it and separately serve potatoes and salad, or some other vegetable.
