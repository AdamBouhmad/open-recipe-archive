## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound meat
- a piece of beef suet the size of a chicken egg, preferably from the kidney
- roll soaked in water
- salt and pepper to taste
- chopped green parsley
- a pinch of nutmeg
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Chop any tender meat very finely
2. Take a piece of beef suet the size of a chicken egg, preferably from the kidney, chop it very finely and then beat it into a mass
3. Add the roll soaked in water, salt and pepper to taste, chopped green parsley and a pinch of nutmeg, and beat in two eggs
4. Mix everything well, dust your hands with flour and make small balls the size of a walnut from this mass
5. Cook them in water
6. These dumplings are usually served with borscht
