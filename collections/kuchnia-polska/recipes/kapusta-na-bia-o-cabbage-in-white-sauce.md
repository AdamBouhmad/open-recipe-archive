## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- cream or milk
- butter
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop cabbage fairly finely, place in a pan, salt and pour water.
2. Cook for about fifteen minutes, then drain the water and finish cooking in fresh water.
3. When cabbage is tender, pour in a cup of cream or milk, add half a tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste.
4. Season with a teaspoon of flour mixed first in a small amount of cold water.
5. Bring to a boil once more and serve.
