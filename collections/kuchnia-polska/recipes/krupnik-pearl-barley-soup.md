## Ingredients

- beef bones and meat scraps
- vegetables
- 1 cup pearl or barley groats
- butter
- beef broth

## Directions

1. It is most appropriate to serve krupnik on the day when there is roast beef for dinner, as all pieces of meat unsuitable for roasting can be used for krupnik
2. Cook broth as usual from bones and meat scraps
3. Cook finely chopped vegetables separately
4. Cook 1 cup of barley or pearl groats separately with the addition of a few tablespoons of fat from the broth
5. When the groats are sufficiently softened and separate, add 1 tablespoon of butter, mix and dilute with cooked broth
6. To the cooked vegetables add some fresh parsley, place everything in a dish and cover with krupnik
7. Remember that an important condition for good krupnik is cooking the groats separately, because if you sprinkle the broth with groats immediately, the krupnik will be white and sticky
