## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1/2 pound grated cheese
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- bread

## Directions

1. Rub the hard-boiled egg yolks into a mass in a small bowl
2. Add in order, mixing well after each addition: salt, pepper, mustard, grated cheese, and vinegar
3. Mix well and spread on bread
