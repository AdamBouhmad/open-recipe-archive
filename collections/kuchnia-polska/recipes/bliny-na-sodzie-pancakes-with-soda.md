## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- half a teaspoon salt
- one teaspoon soda
- two cups sour milk
- two beaten eggs
- two tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Sift the flour two or three times with half a teaspoon of salt and one teaspoon of soda.
2. Slowly add two cups of sour milk, two beaten eggs, and two tablespoons of melted butter.
3. Beat well before adding the butter, then mix with the butter and fry on a hot pan.
