## Ingredients

- 6 medium potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup hot milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 quart milk
- 1 slice onion
- 1/2 quart previously cooked and finely crumbled stockfish

## Directions

1. Cook 6 medium potatoes and grate them well
2. Add 1 tablespoon of butter while hot, 1 cup of hot milk, salt and pepper to taste
3. Beat the mixture well and spread it on a serving platter so that there is only a thin layer of potatoes in the middle of the platter but considerably more around the edges, forming a wall around the platter
4. Bring 1/2 quart of milk to a boil with one slice of onion
5. Mix 1 tablespoon of butter with 1 tablespoon of flour, then add 1 tablespoon of hot milk, mix properly and pour into the boiling milk
6. Add 1/2 quart of previously cooked and finely crumbled stockfish to this milk, stir and cook for 10 minutes from the moment it boils
7. Pour onto the platter lined with potatoes
8. The potato walls must be high enough that the fish with milk cannot spill over them
