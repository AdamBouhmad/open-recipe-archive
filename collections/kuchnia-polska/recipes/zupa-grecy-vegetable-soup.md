## Ingredients

- two carrots
- one potato
- one turnip
- one quart of water
- one spoon of finely chopped onion
- a little celery salt
- salt and pepper to taste
- half a spoon of butter
- one cup of milk
- one spoon of flour
- cold water
- small croutons

## Directions

1. Peel two carrots, one potato and one turnip and chop into small cubes, pour over with one quart of water and cook until the vegetables are completely soft
2. Remove the vegetables and pass through a sieve, then put the mashed mass back into the water in which it was cooked, add one spoon of finely chopped onion, a little celery salt, salt and pepper to taste, half a spoon of butter and one cup of milk, and then cook for five to ten minutes
3. Finally mix one spoon of flour with cold water, pour into the soup and bring to a boil again
4. Serve with small croutons fried in butter
