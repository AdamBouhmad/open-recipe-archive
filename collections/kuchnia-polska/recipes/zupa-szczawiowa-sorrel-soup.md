## Ingredients

- vegetables (onions, celery, leeks, parsley, carrots, and other vegetables)
- sorrel
- butter
- sour cream
- flour
- egg yolks
- hard-boiled eggs or stuffed eggs

## Directions

1. Cook a broth with various vegetables such as onions, celery, leeks, parsley, carrots and whatever vegetables are on hand
2. Separately, stew a sufficient amount of sorrel in butter, and when completely soft, thin it with the vegetable broth
3. Strain through a fine sieve
4. Take one cup of sour cream and one spoonful of flour to thicken the soup
5. In a bowl, beat four egg yolks with four spoonfuls of cooled vegetable broth, then pour the hot soup into this, constantly stirring with a spoon
6. The above proportion is for four people
7. Serve with hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters, stuffed eggs, or eggs cooked in their shells, which can be placed in the soup and cooked in it
