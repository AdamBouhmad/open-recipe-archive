## Ingredients

- veal liver
- lard bacon
- flour
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the liver, lard it with bacon, cut into thick slices and dust with flour.
2. Place a spoonful of butter in a pan, brown it slightly and add the liver, layering with sliced onion, place a little butter on top, cover and braise for about twenty minutes on strong heat.
3. When the liver is half braised, salt it, add a little pepper, shake the pan and then finish braising.
