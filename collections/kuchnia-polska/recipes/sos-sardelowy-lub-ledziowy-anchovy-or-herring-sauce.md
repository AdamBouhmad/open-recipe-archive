## Ingredients

- sardines or herring
- chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- strong broth or bouillon

## Directions

1. Clean, carefully peel the sardines or herring of bones and skin and chop very finely
2. Add some chopped onion and mix well
3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a pan, add the sardine or herring mixture and fry
4. In another pan, brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour
5. Dilute with strong broth or bouillon to desired thickness, mix everything together, boil and pour over meat
