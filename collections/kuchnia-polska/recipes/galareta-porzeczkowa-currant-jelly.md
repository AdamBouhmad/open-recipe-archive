## Ingredients

- currants not overly ripe
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Take currants not overly ripe, carefully sort and pour into an enameled pot
2. Place on the fire and crush the berries so as to extract all the juice from them, then strain through cloth
3. Repeat this straining several times until the juice is completely clear
4. For each pound of this juice, take one pound of sugar
5. First place the sugar in the oven and heat it well, not allowing it to burn
6. Pour the strained juice into the pot and let it boil for about twenty minutes, then add the hot sugar and stir until it dissolves completely
7. Pour into jars or glasses, seal tightly and place in the sun until the jelly sets
