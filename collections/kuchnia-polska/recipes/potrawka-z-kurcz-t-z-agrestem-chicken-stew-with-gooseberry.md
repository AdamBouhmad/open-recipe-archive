## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Vegetables and greens
- Water
- Unripe gooseberry (half a quart per two chickens), removed from stems
- Sugar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken stew as above, but do not let it cook until very tender
2. For two chickens throw in half a quart of unripe gooseberry removed from stems and boil until tender
3. Then add one spoonful of sugar, place in one spoonful of butter and boil a little longer until the sauce thickens
4. Flour is not needed
