## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold cooked fish (preferably stockfish)
- 1 cup cold cooked Italian pasta, broken into inch-sized pieces
- butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- milk to cover the mixture
- breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup of cold cooked fish (preferably stockfish) with 1 cup of cold cooked Italian pasta broken into inch-sized pieces
2. Butter a baking dish or mold
3. Place the fish and pasta mixture in the dish
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, as well as 1/2 tablespoon of butter and enough milk to moisten the entire mixture
5. Sprinkle a layer of breadcrumbs on top, then 3 tablespoons of grated cheese
6. Place in the oven and bake until well browned
7. Remove from oven and serve immediately at table
