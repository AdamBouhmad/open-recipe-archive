## Ingredients

- steak, cut thick
- four bananas
- butter
- salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the thickly cut steak on strong heat
2. Peel four bananas and slice each lengthwise into three parts
3. Place them on a pan with hot butter and fry for five minutes
4. Place the steak on a platter and cover the top with the fried bananas
5. Pour melted butter over it, to which add a little salt and a pinch of red pepper
6. Serve on the table
