## Ingredients

- cleaned piglet, cut into pieces
- head of piglet
- tablespoon of flour
- tablespoon of butter
- several tablespoons of vinegar
- caramel for color
- large raisins without seeds
- sweet almonds, sliced
- water
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the piglet and cut it into pieces; split the head across, leaving the brain untouched in the upper part.
2. Place in a pot, cover with water and cook until tender.
3. Remove the meat from the pot.
4. In the pot, brown a tablespoon of flour and a tablespoon of butter together.
5. Add several tablespoons of vinegar, a small amount of caramel for color, and if desired, large raisins without seeds and sliced sweet almonds.
6. Boil everything together, then add the meat.
7. Add the broth remaining from cooking the piglet and bring to a boil.
8. Arrange on a serving dish, garnish with lemon slices, pour sauce over it and serve.
