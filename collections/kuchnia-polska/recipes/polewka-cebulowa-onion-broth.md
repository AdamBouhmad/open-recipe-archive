## Ingredients

- four onions, finely chopped
- one large spoon of butter
- one and a half quarts of water
- salt and pepper to taste
- a handful of parsley
- one quart of milk
- one large spoon of cornmeal
- one spoon of cold water
- three spoons of butter

## Directions

1. Chop four onions finely and fry in one large spoon of butter, being careful however not to brown the onion, then add one and a half quarts of water, salt and pepper to taste and cook for an hour, together with a handful of parsley
2. Strain and add one quart of milk, beaten with one large spoon of cornmeal mixed in one spoon of cold water
3. Cook for about ten more minutes, and just before serving add three spoons of butter
