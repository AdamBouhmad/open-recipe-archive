## Ingredients

- one quart of good milk
- half a quarter litre of cold water
- one onion
- a couple of cooked and mashed potatoes
- one spoon of butter
- two spoons of tapioca previously soaked in a small amount of milk
- salt and pepper
- celery
- one beaten egg

## Directions

1. Add half a quarter litre of cold water to one quart of good milk, add one onion and a couple of cooked and mashed potatoes, as well as one spoon of butter and bring all this to a boil
2. When it boils, add two spoons of previously soaked tapioca in a small amount of milk, salt and pepper and boil for a few minutes
3. The quality of the soup can be improved by adding celery and one beaten egg
