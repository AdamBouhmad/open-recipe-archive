## Ingredients

- rutabaga
- butter
- milk or broth
- grated bread
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook rutabaga in water until almost tender, then cut into thin slices.
2. Grease a metal plate or shallow cake pan with butter and arrange the rutabaga.
3. Pour a little milk or broth over it, sprinkle lightly with grated bread on top, add salt and pepper to taste and bake in a moderate oven until nicely browned.
4. Transfer to a hot serving platter and serve on the table.
