## Ingredients

- two oranges
- four bananas
- one cup of sugar
- half a box of gelatin
- water
- cream whipped with sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the oranges and bananas thinly into a serving dish.
2. Add one cup of sugar and mix.
3. Dissolve half a box of gelatin in water and pour over the arranged fruit.
4. Place in a cold place for several hours until the jelly sets.
5. When completely set, cut into large pieces and top each with a dollop of cream whipped with sugar.
6. Instead of oranges and bananas, other fruits and berries may be used.
