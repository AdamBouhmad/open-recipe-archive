## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- water
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Quarter a head of white cabbage, pour water in a pan and cook for fifteen minutes.
2. Drain all the water and pour fresh boiling water in which to continue cooking the cabbage until tender.
3. Turn out onto a sieve and let cool.
4. When completely cold, chop finely, add two beaten eggs, a tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste and three tablespoons of cream.
5. Mix well and place in a buttered baking mold and set in the oven.
6. When well browned, remove from the oven and serve immediately, best of all in the mold.
