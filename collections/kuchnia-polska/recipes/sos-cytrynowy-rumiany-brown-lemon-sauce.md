## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- half a glass of strong broth or poultry broth
- juice from half a lemon
- good half a teaspoon of burnt sugar
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour
2. Dissolve in half a glass of strong broth or poultry broth
3. Squeeze in juice from half a lemon
4. Add good half a teaspoon of burnt sugar for color
5. Boil, strain through a sieve, and pour over meat on which lemon slices have been previously placed, with the seeds removed
