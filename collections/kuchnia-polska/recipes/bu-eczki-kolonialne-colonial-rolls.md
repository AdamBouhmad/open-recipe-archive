## Ingredients

- 0.5 quart sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- pinch of salt
- 1 quart flour
- 1 cake of compressed yeast

## Directions

1. Heat 0.5 quart of sweet milk enough to dissolve 1 tablespoon of butter in it.
2. After the butter dissolves, add 1 tablespoon of sugar and a pinch of salt and cool slightly.
3. When the milk reaches lukewarm temperature, add 1 quart of flour and 1 cake of compressed yeast.
4. Set in a warm place for the dough to rise.
5. When the dough becomes light, knead it a little and let it rise again.
6. Shape into small rolls, brush the top with egg white, and bake in a moderately hot oven.
