## Ingredients

- bread or bread roll
- butter, preferably unsalted

## Directions

1. Remove the crust from bread or a bread roll and cut the center into small cubes
2. Throw into melted butter in a pan, stir and fry until slightly browned
3. Alternatively, instead of frying on the stove, place in a hot oven for ten minutes until browned and dried
4. For fruit soups, dip the croutons first in cream, then fry in a pan, and before serving sprinkle with sugar and serve with the soup
