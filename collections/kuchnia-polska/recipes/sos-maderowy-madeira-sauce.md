## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- one quart of strong broth
- one glass of Madeira wine
- marinated truffles, mushrooms, or olives

## Directions

1. Brown half a pound of butter with two tablespoons of flour
2. Dissolve in one quart of strong broth
3. Add one glass of Madeira wine
4. Mix thoroughly and cook covered over low heat until the sauce is thick
5. Then strain through a sieve, holding the roux in a bowl with hot water
6. To such sauce one usually adds sliced marinated truffles, mushrooms, or olives
7. Used for sirloin, veal cutlets, etc.
8. The above proportion is for ten persons
