## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- sweet cream, as needed for dough consistency
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt and baking powder, add enough sweet cream to make a thick dough and mix quickly
2. Roll out the dough and line a pie plate with perforated bottom, then bake
3. Mix cornstarch with sugar and pour in boiling water, stirring constantly
4. Place on heat and simmer slowly for three minutes
5. Remove from heat, add melted butter, lemon juice and zest, then two well-beaten egg yolks
6. Fill the baked crust with this mixture and bake in a moderately hot oven
7. Beat two egg whites with a teaspoon of powdered sugar until stiff, spread on the baked pie and return briefly to the oven to brown
