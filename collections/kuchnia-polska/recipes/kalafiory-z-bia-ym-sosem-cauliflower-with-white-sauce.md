## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower
- salt
- water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 1/2 cups cold water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon cold water

## Directions

1. Place a large cauliflower stem side up in a pot, cover with cold salted water and cook for an hour.
2. After cooking, drain and place on a hot serving platter.
3. In a pan, make a roux with 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon flour.
4. Gradually add 1 1/2 cups cold water and cook on low heat, constantly stirring, until a smooth sauce forms.
5. Add 1/4 teaspoon salt and some pepper.
6. Remove from heat and add 1 egg yolk well beaten, mixed with 1 tablespoon cold water.
7. Mix and pour over the cauliflower.
8. Serve immediately.
