## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef
- 1/4 pound smoked lard (słonina)
- water
- various vegetables
- macaroni or noodles (łazankami)
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Pour water over 1 pound of beef and 1/4 pound of smoked lard and cook
2. After skimming, add a generous amount of various vegetables that are on hand
3. Cook until vegetables and meat are completely tender
4. Strain the broth and pour into a dish lined with slices of the smoked lard removed from the soup and cut up, and with macaroni or noodles
5. Whole boiled potatoes can also be served with this soup
6. Serve the meat separately, dressed with vegetables cooked in the soup
