## Ingredients

- Two whole eggs
- Quarter teaspoon of salt
- Butter
- Fat for greasing

## Directions

1. Beat two whole eggs for five minutes
2. Add quarter teaspoon of salt and beat three minutes longer
3. Pour the beaten eggs into a cup greased with butter
4. Place the cup in a bain-marie with warm water
5. Cover with paper greased with fat
6. Place in a moderately hot oven for about half an hour
7. Test with a knife; when the mass no longer sticks to the knife, the eggs are cooked
8. Remove from the oven and place in a cold location
9. When properly cooled, turn out of the cup and cut into cubes
10. Place in a bowl and pour hot broth over it
