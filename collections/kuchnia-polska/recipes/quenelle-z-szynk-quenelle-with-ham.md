## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups finely minced cooked ham
- one and one-half cups cooked potatoes sliced thin
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one-half quarter of cream
- pepper
- two eggs
- grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mince the ham very finely and slice the cooked potatoes thinly
2. Fill a baking mold alternating layers of potatoes and ham
3. Sprinkle the ham lightly with pepper
4. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a pan, mix in one tablespoon of flour
5. Add one-half quarter of cream very slowly while stirring constantly, and a pinch of pepper
6. Bring to a boil and remove immediately
7. Beat two eggs and mix first with two tablespoons of the sauce, then pour the eggs into the sauce, mix and pour over the layered ham and potatoes in the mold
8. Sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs on top and place in a hot oven for about fifteen minutes
9. This dish is usually served for breakfast
