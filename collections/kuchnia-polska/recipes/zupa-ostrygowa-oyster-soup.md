## Ingredients

- one quart of oysters
- milk
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Pour a quart of oysters into a colander and strain all the liquid into a bowl
2. When all the liquid has drained, rinse the oysters with cold water, being careful that no shell pieces remain
3. Pour the drained oyster liquid into a pot, bring to a boil, and strain after skimming
4. Bring a quart of milk to a boil and thicken slightly with flour mixed with butter
5. Add the oysters to the strained oyster liquid and cook until they puff up and their edges begin to curl
6. Then add the milk, mix together, bring to a boil and serve
