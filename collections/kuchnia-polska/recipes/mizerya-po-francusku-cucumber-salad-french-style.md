## Ingredients

- pair of large cucumbers
- one tablespoon vinegar
- two tablespoons oil
- half teaspoon salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Peel a pair of large cucumbers and place them in cold water (preferably with ice) for one hour
2. Remove and slice into thin slices
3. Place in a bowl
4. Mix together one tablespoon of vinegar, two tablespoons of oil, half teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of red pepper
5. Pour this sauce over the cucumber slices
