## Ingredients

- lamb cutlets
- white onion sauce à la Soubisse

## Directions

1. Make these cutlets in a similar way as beaten cutlets, except that they should be fried on strong heat and very briefly, so that the meat inside is still pink.
2. Serve separately with white onion sauce, called Soubisse.
