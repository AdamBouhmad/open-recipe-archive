## Ingredients

- young and fat goose
- salt
- apples, quartered
- marjoram
- potatoes, optional

## Directions

1. For a good roasted goose, it must necessarily be young and fat
2. After preparing and cleaning the goose, rub it with salt outside and inside
3. Stuff with apples cut into quarters, place sprigs of marjoram between the apples, sew shut, place in a roasting pan and put in the oven
4. During roasting, frequently baste with the sauce that drips into the pan
5. Before serving, remove the apples, sprinkle with a little sugar, then pour the sauce over the goose
6. Instead of apples, the goose can be stuffed with potatoes cut into pieces
