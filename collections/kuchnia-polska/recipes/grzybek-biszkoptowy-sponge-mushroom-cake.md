## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- one-eighth pound of powdered sugar
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- grated zest of half a lemon
- four egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Take four egg yolks, one-eighth pound of powdered sugar, two tablespoons of wheat flour and grated zest of half a lemon; beat properly then add four egg whites, beaten into stiff meringue, and mix lightly, then pour into a buttered mold and place in a hot oven for fifteen minutes
2. Serve at table with fruit juice, preserves or marmalade
