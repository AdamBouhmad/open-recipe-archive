## Ingredients

- half quart sweet cream
- two tablespoons sugar
- a small piece of vanilla or a little vanilla extract
- six egg yolks
- two tablespoons sweet cream

## Directions

1. Bring to a boil together half quart of sweet cream, two tablespoons of sugar and a small piece of vanilla or a little vanilla extract.
2. Beat six egg yolks and two tablespoons of sweet cream vigorously, add to the boiled cream and heat together until it comes to a boil.
