## Ingredients

- one quart of dried peas
- soft water
- one tablespoon butter
- one blanched onion
- a little parsley
- one tablespoon good butter
- a quarter tablespoon flour
- one cup sweet cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Pour one quart of ordinary dried peas into a pan, cover with soft water and bring to a boil. After skimming, drain the water, rinse the peas in fresh water and then cover with another hot water and cook until completely soft.
2. During cooking add one tablespoon of butter, one blanched onion and a little parsley.
3. When the peas are well cooked, mash them with a roller, then press through a fine sieve; if too thick, add some hot water.
4. Place one tablespoon of good butter in a pan and brown lightly with a quarter tablespoon of flour, and when it thickens, dilute with one cup of sweet cream, add to the pressed peas, mix well, heat on hot fire and serve.
