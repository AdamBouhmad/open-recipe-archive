## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds good beef
- quarter pound kidney fat
- half a large soaked and wrung-out roll
- salt and pepper to taste
- onion
- water
- flour
- butter
- meat broth from cooked bones or sour cream

## Directions

1. Mince one and a half pounds good beef with a quarter pound kidney fat
2. Add half a large soaked and wrung-out roll, salt and pepper to taste, and finely minced onion
3. Mix everything into a paste and form into elongated rolls
4. Moisten with water and coat with flour and drop onto browned butter
5. When browned on both sides, add meat broth from cooked bones or two tablespoons sour cream
6. Braise covered for fifteen minutes
