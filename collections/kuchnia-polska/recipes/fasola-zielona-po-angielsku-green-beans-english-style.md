## Ingredients

- young green beans
- water
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the strings from young beans, throw into a large pan with boiling water and cook until tender, then throw into a colander and rinse with cold water until completely cool, then spread on a napkin to dry.
2. Pour a few tablespoons of water into a flat pan and add one tablespoon of fresh butter, and when the butter melts, add the beans and cook quickly, shaking the pan, until the water evaporates.
3. Transfer the beans to a salad dish and place slices of good butter on top.
