## Ingredients

- one pound beef liver
- one large tablespoon butter
- one cup milk
- one teaspoon cornstarch
- one half teaspoon salt
- a pinch of red pepper
- one teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- one teaspoon sharp tomato sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the beef liver into half-inch cubes
2. Melt a large tablespoon of butter in a pan
3. Add the liver and fry until well browned
4. Heat one cup of milk
5. Dissolve one teaspoon of cornstarch in a small amount of water
6. Add the cornstarch mixture to the hot milk
7. Add one half teaspoon salt, a pinch of red pepper, and one teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
8. Cook until the mixture becomes smooth
9. Pour the sauce over the liver arranged on a platter
10. If desired, add one teaspoon of sharp tomato sauce
