## Ingredients

- Veal cutlets cut from the bones
- Salt
- Egg
- Cold water
- Grated bread crumbs, fine and sifted
- Butter, clarified if possible

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets from the bones so they have a good shape
2. Beat and salt each cutlet
3. Dip each in egg beaten with a spoonful of cold water
4. Coat in fine grated bread crumbs that have been sifted
5. Put a piece of butter on a pan, preferably clarified, heat strongly, then add the cutlets
6. Fry on low heat so they do not brown too much
7. After browning on one side, turn to the other side
8. When cooked, place on a platter and pour the remaining butter from the pan over them
