## Ingredients

- one pound of ripe peaches
- one quart of strong spirit
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Crush one pound of ripe peaches without damaging the pits
2. Then place with the pits and pour one quart of strong spirit over them and let stand in a cool place for a whole day
3. Then strain and add one pound of sugar, pour into bottles and cork
4. Can be colored with burnt sugar
