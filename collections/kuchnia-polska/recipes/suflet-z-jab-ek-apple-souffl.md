## Ingredients

- several tart apples
- two small cups of apple marmalade
- half a pound of sugar
- two egg yolks
- six egg whites
- sugar
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Bake several tart apples and press through a colander, then cool
2. When completely cold, take two small cups of this marmalade, add half a pound of sugar and two egg yolks and beat with a spatula in a bowl until white; this should be done in a cool place
3. Beat meringue from six egg whites, carefully mix with the marmalade, arrange on a baking sheet in the shape of a pyramid, sprinkle with sugar and chopped almonds and place in a slow oven for half an hour
4. Proportions for six persons
