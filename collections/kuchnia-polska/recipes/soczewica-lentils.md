## Ingredients

- one half quart of lentils
- water
- one tablespoon butter
- one half tablespoon flour
- a little vinegar
- burned sugar
- fried sausages or slices of smoked meat

## Directions

1. Cover half a quart of lentils with water, bring to a boil, drain this water, rinse the lentils, cover with other hot water and cook until tender.
2. When completely soft, bring to a boil on white one tablespoon of butter with half a tablespoon of flour and add, also add a little vinegar and burned sugar, mix well and serve on the table, arranging fried sausages or slices of smoked meat around it, which can be cooked in the lentils.
