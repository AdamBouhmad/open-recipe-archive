## Ingredients

- beef or cross-cut meat
- salt
- two roasted and grated onions
- one tablespoon butter
- half a cup grated bread
- pepper to taste
- butter for pan
- roasted onion
- broth
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into large but very thin slices and beat them
2. Salt and let rest about fifteen minutes
3. Mix two roasted and grated onions, one tablespoon butter, half a cup grated bread, salt and pepper to taste into a smooth paste
4. Spread this paste on the slices and roll them tightly so they do not unroll
5. Melt a piece of butter in a pan and add the rolled slices, plus roasted onion
6. Braise covered on low heat
7. When browned, add a little broth and some grated bread
8. Braise until tender, which should take about forty-five minutes
