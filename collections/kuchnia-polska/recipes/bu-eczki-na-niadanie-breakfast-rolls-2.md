## Ingredients

- one cup of corn meal
- one and a half cups of wheat flour
- two teaspoons of baking powder
- two tablespoons of butter
- flour for kneading

## Directions

1. Mix together one cup of corn meal, one and a half cups of wheat flour and two teaspoons of baking powder and sift the whole thing several times through a sieve to mix thoroughly
2. Rub two tablespoons of butter together
