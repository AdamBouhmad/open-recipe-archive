## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter
- one tablespoon of grated green cheese, finely grated
- one small teaspoon of French mustard
- one small glass of good rum

## Directions

1. mix with a wooden spoon into a uniform mass
2. arrange nicely on a butter dish and serve to the table
