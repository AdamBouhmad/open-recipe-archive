## Ingredients

- three egg yolks
- salt
- half a pound of oil
- two tablespoons of good vinegar
- one teaspoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three egg yolks in a bowl, salt them, and stir with a wooden spoon for twenty minutes
2. When thickened, add half a pound of oil very slowly, preferably drop by drop, constantly stirring with the spoon
3. When thickened, add about two tablespoons of good vinegar and one teaspoon of sugar
4. Keep in a cool place until time of use
